Title: Provisional Organization Outline of the Beijing Municipal People's Government
Author: 
Date: 1949-11-23
Page: 4

[Beijing News] The second session of the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference on the 22nd unanimously adopted the "Provisional Organization Outline of the Beijing Municipal People's Government." The full text is as follows:

Article 1: The Beijing Municipal People's Government is directly under the State Council of the Central People's Government. Before the Central People's Government promulgates the municipal government organization regulations, it shall be organized according to this outline.

Article 2: The Beijing Municipal People's Government Committee shall be composed of one mayor, two deputy mayors, and ten members elected by the People's Representative Conference from all walks of life. A secretary-general shall be elected by the members from among themselves and submitted to the Central People's Government for appointment. The term of office is temporarily set at one year, but they may be re-elected. When leaving office for any reason, they may be re-elected according to law.

Article 3: The Mayor presides over the meetings of the Municipal People's Government Committee and the administrative meetings of the Municipal People's Government, and leads the work of the Municipal People's Government. The Deputy Mayor and the Secretary-General assist the Mayor in performing his duties.
    The Mayor may, as needed, request the Municipal People's Government Committee to approve the appointment of several Deputy Secretaries-General.

Article 4: The Beijing Municipal People's Government Committee implements democratic centralism.
    The Beijing Municipal People's Government Committee shall hold meetings twice a month, convened by the Mayor. The Mayor may convene meetings earlier or later as needed, or upon the proposal of more than one-third of the members.

Article 5: The Beijing Municipal People's Government may establish bureaus, institutes, divisions, and various committees and other institutions as needed. Their organizational regulations shall be determined separately.
    The establishment, merger, and abolition of various bureaus, institutes, divisions, committees, and other equivalent institutions, as well as the appointment and removal of their principal responsible persons, shall be approved by the Municipal People's Government Committee and submitted by the Mayor to the State Council of the Central People's Government for approval or appointment and removal.

Article 6: During the period of military control, the Beijing Municipal People's Government shall take unified steps with the Beijing Military Control Commission in consolidating revolutionary order, suppressing counter-revolutionary activities, and implementing land reform.

Article 7: This organizational outline shall be implemented after being approved by the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference and submitted to the Central People's Government for approval.

### 北京市人民政府暂行组织大纲

1949-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京市人民政府暂行组织大纲
    （北京市第二届各界人民代表会议一九四九年十一月二十二日通过）
    【北京讯】北京市第二届各界人民代表大会二十二日的会议上，出席代表一致通过了“北京市人民政府暂行组织大纲”，全文如下：
    第一条　北京市人民政府直属中央人民政府政务院，在中央人民政府未颁布市政府组织条例前，根据本大纲组织之。
    第二条　北京市人民政府委员会由各界人民代表会议选举市长一人、副市长二人、委员十人、由委员中互选秘书长一人，呈请中央人民政府加委组成之。其任期暂定为一年，但得连选连任，因故离职时得依法补选之。
    第三条　市长主持市人民政府委员会会议和市人民政府行政会议、并领导市人民政府工作。副市长秘书长协助市长执行职务。
    市长根据需要，得提请市人民政府委员会通过，任命副秘书长若干人。
    第四条　北京市人民政府委员会实行民主集中制。
    北京市人民政府委员会会议，每月举行二次，由市长负责召集之。市长根据需要，或经三分之一以上委员提议，得提前或延期召集之。
    第五条　北京市人民政府得根据需要，设立局、院、处和各种委员会及其他机构。其组织规程另定之。
    各局、院、处、委员会和其他同级机构的设立与裁并，及其主要负责人员的任免，须经市人民政府委员会通过，由市长呈请中央人民政府政务院批准或任免之。
    第六条　在军事管制时期，北京市人民政府在巩固革命秩序、镇压反革命活动、实行土地改革等方面，须与北京市军事管制委员会采取统一步骤。
    第七条　本组织大纲经北京市各界人民代表会议通过，呈请中央人民政府批准后实施之。
