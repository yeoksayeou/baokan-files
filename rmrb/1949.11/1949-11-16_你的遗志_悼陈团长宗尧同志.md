Title: Your Will - In Memory of Comrade Chen Zongyao, Regiment Commander
Author: Peng Fang
Date: 1949-11-16
Page: 6

　　Your Will
    In Memory of Comrade Chen Zongyao, Regiment Commander
    Peng Fang
    How can I forget, beneath the Mei Mountain,
    On the banks of the Ban River, that moonlit night,
    Due to excessive blood loss on the battlefield,
    Your face, so pale,
    Had lost the strength to speak;
    But you gave the only precious thing you possessed,
    That weapon you seized from the enemy,
    Your lifelong companion in battle—
    Your beloved Browning,
    Silently to Commander Wang,
    As a farewell memento.
　
    Through blurred tears,
    I couldn't clearly see your face;
    But I saw
    General Wang Zhen, overcome with grief and anger,
    Holding that Browning,
    Tightly in his hand;
    As if about to shoot it at the enemy!
　
    Respected Comrade Zongyao:
    I deeply understand
    This is your will—
    Your strong fighting heart!
    You wanted us to use our weapons,
    For the suffering Chinese people,
    To resolutely wipe out the Kuomintang reactionaries!
    Under the banner of Mao Zedong,
    To build a new people's nation.
    　　　　　　　　　　　First draft, June 1945, after the battle of Xiaomei in Hunan

### 你的遗志  悼陈团长宗尧同志
篷放
1949-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　你的遗志
    悼陈团长宗尧同志
    篷放
    怎能忘记眉山下
    斑江岸上的月夜里，
    由于战场上血流过多，
    面色惨白的你，
    已失掉了讲话的力气；
    但把身边唯一珍贵的东西，
    你那从敌人手中夺来的，
    战斗了终身的武器——
    你心爱的卜朗宁，
    默默地交给王司令，
    作为永别的纪念品。
　
    在泪水模糊中，
    我看不清你的遗容；
    但是我看见了
    悲忿已极的王震将军，
    把那支卜朗宁，
    紧紧地握在手里；
    仿佛就要向仇敌射去！
　
    敬爱的同志宗尧：
    我已深深地知道
    这就是你的意志——
    你那坚强的战斗的心！
    要我们用自己的武器，
    为受难的中国人民，
    把国民党反动派坚决扫清！
    在毛泽东旗帜下，
    建设起人民的新国家。
    　　　　　　　　　　　一九四五，六，于湖南小眉战斗后草稿
