Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Issues Statement Regarding the Flight of the Kuomintang Reactionary Bandits Abroad to Vietnam and Other Places: Our Government Reserves the Right to Intervene Regarding the Fleeing Bandit Troops; Countries Harboring Bandit Troops Must Bear All Consequences
Author:
Date: 1949-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 29th: Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of our Central People's Government issued a statement today regarding the flight of the Kuomintang reactionary bandits abroad to Vietnam and other places. This statement has been sent by the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in the form of a letter to the former diplomatic personnel of relevant countries in China for forwarding to their respective governments. The full text of the statement is as follows:

As the People's Liberation Army of the People's Republic of China is currently and rapidly continuing to eliminate the remnant reactionary bandit troops of the Kuomintang and liberate the vast areas of South and Southwest China, it is increasingly approaching our country's southwestern border. The Kuomintang reactionary bandit gang, headed by Chiang Kai-shek, Li Zongren, Bai Chongxi, Yu Hanmou, and others, are turning their hopes towards Vietnam and other places bordering China, attempting to use these places as havens for their defeated troops and preparing them as bases to await the opportunity to stage a comeback. To this end, I, on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, solemnly point out to the French government and the governments of countries bordering China: It is the unwavering policy of our government to completely eliminate all reactionary armed forces of the Kuomintang. No matter where the defeated Kuomintang reactionary troops flee, the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China reserves the right to intervene in this matter, and the government of any country harboring the Kuomintang reactionary armed forces must be responsible for this fact and bear all the consequences arising therefrom.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China  Zhou Enlai
   　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 29, 1949, in Beijing

### 就国民党反动匪帮逃往国外越南等地　周恩来外长发表声明  我政府保有权利过问窜逃匪军  容留匪军各国须承担一切后果

1949-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　就国民党反动匪帮逃往国外越南等地
  　周恩来外长发表声明
    我政府保有权利过问窜逃匪军  容留匪军各国须承担一切后果
    【新华社北京二十九日电】我中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长本日就国民党反动匪帮逃往国外越南等地一事发表声明。这一声明已由外交部办公厅以公函送交有关各国在华前外交人员转送各该国政府。声明全文如下：
    中华人民共和国人民解放军在目前迅速地继续消灾国民党反动残余匪军、解放了我华南与西南广大地区的情况下，正日益接近我国的西南国境。以蒋介石、李宗仁、白崇禧、余汉谋等为首的国民党反动匪帮，正在把他们的希望转向越南及其他和中国国境毗连的地方，企图把这些地方作为他们的残兵败将的逋逃所，并准备把它们作为等待时机以便卷土重来的基地。为此，我代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府向法国政府及和中国国境毗连的各国政府郑重指出：彻底消灭国民党所有反动武装力量，乃我政府不可动摇的政策。不管战败了的国民党反动军队，逃到什么地方，我中华人民共和国中央人民政府都保有权利过问这一事实，而容留国民党反动武装力量的任何国家的政府必须对此事实负责，并承担由此产生的一切后果。
　　　　　　　　　中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长  周恩来
   　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十一月二十九日于北京
