Title: People in Belgium, Denmark, Norway, Australia, Canada and Other Countries Hold Celebrations and Demonstrations, Firmly Advocating a Policy of Friendship with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-11-10
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 9th. People in countries such as Belgium, Denmark, Norway, Australia, and Canada have held gatherings or demonstrations to celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution.

　　In Belgium, the Belgian Communist Party presided over a large demonstration in Brussels on the 7th to commemorate the Great October Revolution, halting traffic in the capital for six hours.

　　In Copenhagen, the capital of Denmark, the Danish Communist Party held an open-air celebration to commemorate the October Revolution Day.

　　In Norway, the Norwegian Communist Party held a solemn memorial meeting in Oslo. The meeting was presided over by Lovlien, Chairman of the Norwegian Communist Party, and a resolution was passed to send a congratulatory telegram to Marshal Stalin.

　　In Australia, celebrations were held in major cities. On the 6th, a mass meeting was held in Sydney. Speakers from various public organizations and trade unions spoke at the meeting about the achievements of the Soviet Union, emphasizing that socialism is the road to progress and peace. The Australia-Soviet House in Melbourne held a grand commemorative meeting. The Soviet Life photo exhibition, sponsored by the Australia-Soviet Association in Perth, opened on the 8th.

　　In Canada, the National Committee of the Canada-Soviet Friendship Association, in commemoration of the great October Socialist Revolution, especially urged all Canadians to demand that the government change its policy and implement a policy of friendship with the Soviet Union. The committee will hold a Soviet Friends Conference in Toronto on November 26th. The Montreal Canada-Soviet Friendship Association held a celebration on the 7th.

### 比丹挪澳加等国人民　举行庆祝会及示威　坚主实行对苏友好政策

1949-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　比丹挪澳加等国人民
  　举行庆祝会及示威
  　坚主实行对苏友好政策
    【新华社北京九日电】比、丹、挪、澳、加等国的人民，都举行了庆祝十月革命三十二周年的集会或示威。
    在比利时，比利时共产党于七日在布鲁塞尔主持了纪念伟大十月革命的大示威，比京交通为之停顿六小时。
    在丹麦首都哥本哈根，丹麦共产党举行了露天庆祝大会，纪念十月革命节。
    在挪威，挪威共产党在奥斯陆举行了庄严的纪念会，会议由挪共主席洛夫林主持，会议决议致电斯大林大元帅致贺。
    在澳大利亚，各主要城市都举行了庆祝会。六日，悉尼举行了群众大会，许多公共团体和工会的演说者在大会上谈到苏联的成就，并着重指出社会主义是走向进步与和平的大道。墨尔本澳苏之家举行了盛大纪念会。伯斯澳苏协会主办的苏联生活照片展览会于八日开幕。
    在加拿大，加苏友好协会全国委员会为纪念伟大的十月社会主义革命，特敦促加拿大全体人民要求政府改变其政策，实行对苏友好的政策。委员会将于十一月二十六日在多伦多举行苏联之友大会。蒙特利尔加苏友好协会于七日曾举行庆祝会。
