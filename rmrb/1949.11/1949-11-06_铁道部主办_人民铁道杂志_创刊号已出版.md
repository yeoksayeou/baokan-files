Title: Ministry of Railways Sponsors, People's Railway Magazine, Inaugural Issue Published
Author:
Date: 1949-11-06
Page: 4

　　Ministry of Railways Sponsors
　　People's Railway Magazine
　　Inaugural Issue Published
    [Beijing News] In order to improve the professional knowledge of railway employees, exchange railway technology, and systematically introduce advanced Soviet experience, the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government has published the People's Railway Magazine, with the inaugural issue published on November 1st. The content includes Minister Teng Daiyuan's "Opening Remarks," Peng Min's "Preface to the Summary of the New Huai River Bridge Project," "Railway Transportation in the Soviet Union's New Five-Year Plan," and other specialized articles on railway operations and technology.

### 铁道部主办　人民铁道杂志　创刊号已出版

1949-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　铁道部主办
  　人民铁道杂志
  　创刊号已出版
    【北京讯】中央人民政府铁道部为了提高铁路员工的业务知识，交流铁道技术，并有计划地介绍苏联先进经验起见，特出刊人民铁道杂志，创刊号已于十一月一日出版。内容有滕代远部长的“发刊词”，彭敏的“淮河大桥新建工程总结序言”，“苏联新五年计划中的铁路运输”以及其他有关铁路业务和技术上的专门文字多篇。
