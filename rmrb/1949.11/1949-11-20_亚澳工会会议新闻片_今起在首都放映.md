Title: Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference Newsreel to be Shown in the Capital Today
Author: Fang Jingmin
Date: 1949-11-20
Page: 4

Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference Newsreel
　　To be Shown in the Capital Today
    [Our Newspaper] The newsreel supplement "Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference," produced by the Beijing Film Studio, was completed a few days ago and is scheduled to be shown in the capital city at the National, Dahua, Liberation, Beiyang, Tongle, and China theaters starting today (the 20th). At the same time, ten copies will be printed and distributed to the Northeast and East China regions for screening.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Fang Jingmin)

### 亚澳工会会议新闻片　今起在首都放映
方景民
1949-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　亚澳工会会议新闻片
  　今起在首都放映
    【本报讯】北京电影制片厂摄制之简报号外“亚澳工会会议”新闻片，业于日前完成，定今（二十）日起在京市国民、大华、解放、北洋、同乐、中国六院放映。同时洗印十个考贝，分发东北、华东各地区放映。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（方景民）
