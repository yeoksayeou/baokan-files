Title: Chairman Mao's Reply Telegram: Thanking the Iranian Labor Party for Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1949-11-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Chinese Communist Party today sent a telegram replying to the Central Committee of the Iranian Labor Party with congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:

Dear Comrades of the Central Committee of the Iranian Labor Party:

I extend my deep gratitude for your congratulations.

The Chinese people respectfully observe the struggle of the Iranian Labor Party and the Iranian people against imperialism and domestic reactionaries, and firmly believe that this struggle will achieve a victorious outcome.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 毛主席复电　感谢伊朗劳动党祝贺

1949-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席复电
  　感谢伊朗劳动党祝贺
    【新华社北京十九日电】中国共产党毛泽东主席本日电复伊朗劳动党中央委员会祝贺。电文如下：伊朗劳动党中央委员会亲爱的同志们：
    对于你们的祝贺谨致深切的感谢。
    中国人民以敬意注视着伊朗劳动党和伊朗人民反抗帝国主义和本国反动派的奋斗，并坚信这一奋斗将获得胜利的结果。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东
