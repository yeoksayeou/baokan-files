Title: Xinhua Radio Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-11-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua Radio
    Tonight's Highlights
    [This newspaper dispatch] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast the full text of a speech by Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, at 19:15 in Indonesian, entitled "Welcoming the Asian Women's Representative Conference." The youth program at 18:15 will broadcast: "Recording the Gifts from International Friends Brought Back by the Chinese Democratic Youth Delegation." At 19:15, there will be an introduction to India, one of the countries participating in the Asian and Australian Trade Union Conference and the Asian Women's Representative Conference. At 19:00, a lecture on common sense in natural science: "How Does Blood Circulate?" At 18:30, New Folk Art Time, a solo string performance "Good Husband and Wife," performed by Cao Baolu, accompanied by Shen Deyuan. At 22:00, the new Peking Opera "Two Ingots of Silver" will be broadcast, performed by the North China Peking Opera Research Society.

    Also, on the second channel, at 17:15, the women's program will discuss "How to Correct Several Bad Habits in Children." At 17:45, the workers' program: a speech by Fu Congmin from the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau Broadcasting Group, entitled "From the Beijing Telecommunications Hospital to Our Prevention of Plague." The original script is a collective creation by Zhang Hongrui, Cui Yuying, Xue Dazhong, and Liu Changyi from the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau Broadcasting Group. At 20:00, Voice of the Municipality: The Beijing Telecommunications Bureau will broadcast "On the Issue of Refunding the Phone Installation Fees for Users Whose Phones Have Been Disconnected."

### 新华电台  今晚要目

1949-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    新华电台
    今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十九点十五分，用印尼话播送中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅九号在该台广播演讲词：“迎接亚洲妇女代表会议”的全文。十八点十五分青年节目，播送：“记中国民主青年代表团带回来的国际朋友的礼物”。十九点十五分介绍参加亚洲、澳洲工会会议、和亚洲妇女代表会议的国家之一——印度。十九点自然科学常识讲座：“血液怎样循环？”十八点三十分新曲艺时间，单弦：“好夫妻”，曹宝禄演唱，沈德元伴奏。二十二点播送新京剧“两锭银子”，由华北京剧研究社演唱。
    又第二台十七时十五分妇女时间，谈谈“怎样纠正小孩子的几种坏习惯”。十七时四十五分职工时间：由本台北京电信局广播小组傅聪敏讲话，题目是“由北京电信医院谈到我们的预防鼠疫”。原稿是本台北京电信局广播小组张宏瑞、崔玉英、薛大中、刘厂一的集体创作。二十时市政之声：由北京电信局播送“关于退还已经撤过电话用户押机费的问题”。
