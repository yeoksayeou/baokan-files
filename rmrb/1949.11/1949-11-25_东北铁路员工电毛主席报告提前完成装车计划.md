Title: Northeast Railway Workers Telegraph Chairman Mao Reporting Early Completion of Loading Plan
Author:
Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 24th: With the active efforts of 180,000 railway workers in Northeast China, the Northeast Railway has completed the annual railway transportation loading plan for 1949 ahead of schedule on the 17th of this month. The Northeast Railway Administration telegraphed Chairman Mao and Minister of Railways Teng Daiyuan on the 18th, on behalf of all railway workers in Northeast China, with great excitement, to report their achievements and their determination to complete even greater tasks. The telegram stated:

To Minister Teng Daiyuan of the Ministry of Railways, and forwarded to the beloved Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government:

We report to you that under your leadership, all employees of the Northeast Railway have completed the annual loading plan on November 17th, and have started loading beyond the plan from the 18th. All employees of the railway will continue their efforts without arrogance or impetuosity, and prepare to meet even greater tasks.

Yu Guangsheng, Director of the Northeast Railway Administration
Deputy Directors Liu Juying, Chen Tan, Su Mei November 18th

### 东北铁路员工电毛主席报告提前完成装车计划

1949-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北铁路员工电毛主席报告提前完成装车计划
    【新华社沈阳二十四日电】在全东北十八万铁路员工的积极努力下，东北铁路已于本月十七日提前完成了一九四九年全年的铁路运输的装车计划。东北铁路总局特于十八日代表全东北铁路员工，以极兴奋的心情致电毛主席和铁道部滕代远部长，报告他们的成绩和争取完成更大的任务的决心。电文称：
    铁道部滕部长并转中央人民政府主席亲爱的毛泽东同志：
    我们向您报告：东北铁路全路员工在您领导之下，已于十一月十七日完成了全年的装车计划，并由十八日起已开始计划以外的装车。全路员工当再接再厉，勿骄勿躁，迎接更巨大的任务。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　东北铁路总局长余光生
    　　　　　　　　副总局长刘巨英、陈坦、苏梅　十一月十八日
