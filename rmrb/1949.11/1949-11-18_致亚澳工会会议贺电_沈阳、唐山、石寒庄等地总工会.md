Title: Greetings to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference from the General Trade Unions of Shenyang, Tangshan, Shijiazhuang, and Other Places

Author:
Date: 1949-11-18
Page: 4

　　Greetings to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference
　　General Trade Unions of Shenyang, Tangshan, Shijiazhuang, and Other Places
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th] The general trade unions of Shenyang, Tangshan, Shijiazhuang and other places have sent telegrams to congratulate the opening of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. The telegram from the Shenyang Municipal Federation of Trade Unions stated: "The Asian and Australian Trade Union Conference of the World Federation of Trade Unions is being held in the capital of the new China, which is an honor for the Chinese people, especially the Chinese working class. More than 200,000 workers in Shenyang are celebrating the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference with practical actions of creating new production records."
　　The telegram from the Tangshan Federation of Trade Unions stated: "The convening of this conference will enable the millions of workers in Asia and Australia to unite more closely, smash all the reactionary conspiracies of imperialism and its lackeys, and promote the national liberation movement on the Asian continent to move forward more victoriously."
　　The telegram from the Shijiazhuang Federation of Trade Unions stated: "This conference will further demonstrate the great strength of the working classes of Asia and Australia uniting together."

### 致亚澳工会会议贺电　沈阳、唐山、石寒庄等地总工会

1949-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　致亚澳工会会议贺电
  　沈阳、唐山、石寒庄等地总工会
    【新华社北京十六日电】沈阳、唐山，石家庄等地总工会纷纷电贺亚澳工会会议开幕。沈阳市总工会的贺电说：世界工联亚洲、澳洲工会会议在新中国人民首都召开，这是中国人民特别是中国工人阶级的光荣。沈市二十余万职工，正以创造生产新纪录运动的实际行动来庆祝亚澳工会会议。
    唐山总工会的贺电说：这一会议的召开将使亚澳千百万工人阶级更紧密地团结起来，粉碎帝国主义及其走狗们的一切反动阴谋，促使亚洲大陆民族解放运动更胜利地前进。
    石家庄总工会贺电说：这个会议将更进一步显示出亚澳两洲工人阶级进一步团结起来的伟大力量。
