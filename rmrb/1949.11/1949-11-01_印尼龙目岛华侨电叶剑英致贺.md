Title: Overseas Chinese in Lombok Island, Indonesia Send Telegram to Ye Jianying to Congratulate
Author:
Date: 1949-11-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 31st: The Chinese General Association and other organizations in Lombok Island, Indonesia, sent a telegram to Chairman Ye Jianying of the Guangdong People's Government to congratulate him. The telegram is as follows:

To Mr. Ye Jianying, Chairman of the Guangdong People's Government: On the occasion of the founding of the new Chinese People's Government, overseas Chinese are delighted to hear that you have been appointed to an important position in the provincial government. Overseas Chinese compatriots are all celebrating that the right person has been chosen. At the beginning of this new administration, our association, on behalf of all Chinese compatriots on this island, is willing to work hard for the construction of our hometown. We send this telegram to congratulate you and extend our highest respect.

　　Chinese General Association of Lombok Island, Indonesia, Chinese General Chamber of Commerce
　　October 23rd

### 印尼龙目岛华侨电叶剑英致贺

1949-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　印尼龙目岛华侨电叶剑英致贺
    【新华社广州三十一日电】印度尼西亚龙目岛中华总会等团体，致电广东人民政府叶剑英主席祝贺。电文如下：
    广东人民政府叶剑英先生钧鉴：际兹新中国人民政府宣告成立之时，海外华侨欣闻先生荣任省府重任，海外侨胞，咸庆得人，当此新政伊始，本会谨代表本岛全体同侨，愿为家乡建设而努力，谨电祝贺，并致崇高敬礼。
　　印尼龙目岛中华总会、中华商务总会
　　十月二十三日
