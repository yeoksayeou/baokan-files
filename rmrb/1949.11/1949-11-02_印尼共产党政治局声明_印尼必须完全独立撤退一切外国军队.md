Title: Indonesian Communist Party Politburo Statement: Indonesia Must Be Fully Independent, All Foreign Troops Must Withdraw

Author:

Date: 1949-11-02
Page: 3

　　Indonesian Communist Party Politburo Statement
  　Indonesia Must Be Fully Independent, All Foreign Troops Must Withdraw
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st] According to a dispatch from Antara News Agency in Yogyakarta on October 9th, the Indonesian Communist Party Politburo published a statement in the "Nation" newspaper, insisting that Indonesia must be fully independent and the Republic of Indonesia must not become a semi-colony. The Indonesian Communist Party Politburo also put forward three necessary conditions: (1) the withdrawal of all foreign troops from Indonesia; (2) no granting of military or naval bases to foreign countries; (3) no foreign interference in Indonesia's political, economic, and financial affairs.

### 印尼共产党政治局声明　印尼必须完全独立撤退一切外国军队

1949-11-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　印尼共产党政治局声明
  　印尼必须完全独立撤退一切外国军队
    【新华社北京一日电】据塔斯社十月九日引安普通讯社日惹讯：印尼共产党政治局在民族报上发表声明，坚持印度尼西亚必须完全独立，印尼共和国不得成为半殖民地。印尼共产党政治局并提出三个必要条件：（一）自印尼撤退一切外国军队；（二）不得给予外国以军事或海军基地；（三）外国不得干涉印尼的政治、经济和财政。
