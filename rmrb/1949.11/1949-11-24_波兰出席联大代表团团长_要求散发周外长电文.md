Title: Head of Polish Delegation to UN General Assembly Requests Distribution of Foreign Minister Zhou's Telegram
Author:
Date: 1949-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 22nd: The Polish News Agency reports that Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, previously sent a telegram to the President of the United Nations General Assembly, Romulo, and the Secretary-General of the United Nations, Lie, requesting the revocation of the rights of the Kuomintang representatives to represent China in the United Nations. However, the President of the United Nations General Assembly and the Secretary-General of the United Nations considered the two telegrams as "neither a notification from a member state of the United Nations nor from a government." Therefore, the head of the Polish delegation, Wierblowski, sent a letter to Lie, proposing that Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's telegram be distributed as an official document to all member states of the United Nations.

### 波兰出席联大代表团团长　要求散发周外长电文

1949-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　波兰出席联大代表团团长
　　要求散发周外长电文
    【新华社北京二十三日电】波兰通讯社华沙二十二日讯：中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来，前致电联合国大会主席罗慕洛与联合国组织秘书长赖伊，要求取消国民党代表在联合国组织中代表中国的权利。但该两电为联合国大会主席与联合国组织秘书长认为“既不是来自联合国会员国，也不是来自政府方面的通知”。因此，波兰代表团团长维尔布洛夫斯基致函赖伊，提议把周恩来外长的电文当作官方文件散发给联合国的一切会员国。
