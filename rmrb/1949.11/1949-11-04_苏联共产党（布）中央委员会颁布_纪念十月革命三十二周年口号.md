Title: Slogans Issued by the Central Committee of the Communist Party (Bolshevik) of the Soviet Union in Commemoration of the Thirty-Second Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1949-11-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 1st: The Central Committee of the Communist Party (Bolshevik) of the Soviet Union has issued the following slogans to commemorate the thirty-second anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution:

　　(1) Celebrate the thirty-second anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution!

　　(2) Fraternal greetings to the people of all countries who are fighting for the victory of democracy and socialism!

　　(3) Fraternal greetings to the people of Poland, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, and Albania who have embarked on the road of building socialism!

　　(4) Fraternal greetings to the great Chinese people who have won freedom and the independence of their country and established a people's democratic state! Long live the People's Republic of China!

　　(5) May a united, independent, democratic, and peaceful Germany endure and prosper!

　　(6) Long live the united Korean People's Democratic Republic!

　　(7) Working people of all countries: Defending peace is the cause of all nations and peoples of the world! Expose the aggressive plans of the instigators of a new war! Unite all forces to fight for lasting peace and the security of all countries! Greetings to all fighters who defend the cause of peace and oppose the instigators of a new war!

　　(8) Long live the friendship between the people of the United States and Great Britain and the people of the Soviet Union in the struggle to defend peace and oppose the instigators of a new war!

　　(9) Greetings to the Soviet Armed Forces who gloriously defend the freedom and independence of our motherland! Soviet soldiers: Vigilantly defend the peace that has been won and the constructive labor of our people!

　　(10) Long live the Soviet border guards who vigilantly defend the sacred and inviolable borders of our motherland!

　　(11) Glory to the Soviet heroes and Heroes of Socialist Labor! They are the best sons and daughters of our motherland.

　　(12) Soviet workers, peasants, and intellectuals: Further expand the socialist emulation drive to complete the post-war Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule!

　　(13) Soviet working people: Work for the further progress of industry, transportation, and agricultural technology! Adopt new technologies and perfect production techniques! Tirelessly increase labor productivity - this is the basis for further reducing consumer goods prices!

　　(14) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians: Tirelessly improve product quality! Produce more high-quality goods!

　　(15) Soviet working people: Work for the maximum savings and over-planned socialist accumulation in all sectors of the national economy! Improve the utilization of working capital and reduce production costs!

　　(16) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians: Work for better utilization of equipment! Save materials, fuel, and electricity!

　　(17) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the coal mining industry: Work for the complete mechanization of the entire coal mining process! Restore and build new mines as quickly as possible to supply our national economy with more coal!

　　(18) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the petroleum industry: Increase the speed of production and refining! Drill oil wells and develop new oil fields at a faster pace! Produce more oil and petroleum products for the socialist economy!

　　(19) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the metallurgical industry: Ensure new progress in the production of hardware, maximize the utilization of human and material resources in production! Learn from the experience of outstanding metallurgical experts! Supply the country with more pig iron, steel, rolled metal, and non-ferrous metals!

　　(20) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of power plants: Increase the output of electricity! Open new power stations as quickly as possible and ensure a constant supply of electricity to the national economy!

　　(21) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of machine manufacturing enterprises: Equip the national economy with advanced technology! Establish new production units as quickly as possible! Supply more machines to industry, transportation, and agriculture!

　　(22) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the automobile and tractor industry: Strive to increase the production of automobiles and tractors!

　　(23) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the chemical industry: Work to adopt new and progressive technologies! Increase the production of mineral fertilizers! Supply the national economy with more chemical products!

　　(24) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the shipbuilding industry: Master new shipbuilding technologies! Build new ships faster! Let us build a strong fleet for the Soviet Union!

　　(25) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians engaged in construction work: Rapidly complete the construction of new enterprises, homes, cultural and service institutions, improve the mechanization of construction work, reduce costs, and improve the quality of construction work!

　　(26) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the building materials industry: Increase the production of cement, bricks, glass, roofing, and other materials! Establish new production units as quickly as possible!

　　(27) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the timber and paper industry: Strive to increase the felling and transportation of timber! Implement mechanization on a larger scale in the timber industry! Dedicate more timber and paper to the country!

　　(28) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of light industry: Supply the people with more textiles, shoes, clothing, knitted goods, and other items! Work to improve the quality of products and increase the variety!

　　(29) Workers of the food, meat, cheese, and fish food industries: Increase the production of food and improve its quality! Supply the people with more sugar, butter, meat, cheese, fish, and other products!

　　(30) Workers in local industries and production cooperatives: Produce more goods using local raw materials! Improve the quality of consumer goods! Improve the daily services to the working people!

　　(31) Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians engaged in public utilities and housing construction: Increase space and improve the quality of construction of houses and cultural institutions! Work to improve towns and workers' residential areas!

　　(32) Railway workers: Further increase the speed of building railways! Increase freight volume! Increase the number of train services! Reduce freight costs! Improve service to passengers! Ensure exemplary railway work under severe cold conditions!

　　(33) Workers of ocean and inland river ships: Increase freight volume! Increase the number of voyages! Transport goods for the national economy as quickly as possible! Let us prepare for the 1950 navigation season in an exemplary manner!

　　(34) Agricultural workers: Greatly increase the achievements of socialist farming and animal husbandry! Produce sufficient grain for the people and sufficient raw materials for light industry!

　　(35) Greetings to the male and female farmers of collective farms, workers of machine and tractor stations, and male and female workers of state farms who have gloriously fulfilled their obligations to the state!

　　(36) Male and female farmers of collective farms, workers of machine and tractor stations, and male and female workers of state farms, agricultural experts: Apply the achievements of progressive agricultural science to practical farming on a large scale! Expand the ranks of outstanding agriculturalists, livestock breeders, holders of orders, and Heroes of Socialist Labor!

　　(37) Male and female farmers of collective farms, male and female workers of state farms, livestock experts, and veterinarians: Fully develop the public raising of livestock! Increase the number of livestock while simultaneously improving the productivity of livestock products!

　　(38) Male and female farmers of collective farms: Increase the collective wealth of collective farms, strengthen labor discipline, and strictly abide by the articles of association of agricultural cooperatives - the basic law of collective farm life!

　　(39) Agricultural workers, tractor drivers, combine drivers, machinists of machine and tractor stations and state farms: Make better use of agricultural machinery and increase labor productivity! Work for the progress of electrification of farming and animal husbandry!

　　(40) Workers in agriculture and forestry: Work to complete and over-complete the plan for planting forest shelterbelts in the grassland and forest-grassland zones! Adopt crop and pasture rotation systems at maximum speed, and develop the construction of ponds and reservoirs on a larger scale! Guarantee bumper harvests every year!

　　(41) Workers of Soviet trade organizations, cooperatives, and public supply agencies: Expand Soviet urban and rural trade by all effective means! Improve service to Soviet consumers!

　　(42) Staff members of state organs: Improve the work of Soviet administrative organs, strengthen state discipline, and pay attention to the demands and needs of the working people!

　　(43) Soviet scientific workers: Enrich our motherland's science and technology with new research, discoveries, and inventions! Consolidate the link between science and production!

　　(44) Workers in literature, art, and film: Raise the level of your skills! Create new works of art with lofty ideological content, worthy of the great Soviet people!

　　(45) Male and female teachers in education: Arm students with solid knowledge based on science, and educate our youth in the spirit of Soviet patriotism! Cultivate knowledgeable and cultured citizens of socialist society and active fighters for communism!

　　(46) Medical workers: Improve the quality of people's medical work! Work for exemplary work in medical and health institutions!

　　(47) The entire nation expresses its condolences to the disabled veterans of the patriotic war, and to the families of the heroic Soviet fighters who contributed their lives for the freedom and independence of our motherland!

　　(48) Soviet trade unions: Develop socialist emulation more widely, strive for the early completion of the post-war Five-Year Plan! Promote the experience of production reformers! Persistently care about improving the material and cultural level of staff and workers! Long live the Soviet trade unions - the school of communism!

　　(49) Soviet women: Work for the further progress of our motherland's economy and culture! Long live Soviet women - active builders of communism!

　　(50) Long live the Lenin and Stalin Communist Youth League! It is the advanced team of young builders of communism, a reliable helper and reserve force of the Bolshevik Party!

　　(51) Soviet young men and women: Tirelessly master advanced science, technology, and culture! Be strong and brave, ready to overcome any difficulties! Work selflessly for the benefit of our motherland!

　　(52) Young Pioneers and elementary school students: Master knowledge and prepare to become resolute fighters for the cause of Lenin and Stalin!

　　(53) Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members: Stand at the forefront of the battle, fight for the early completion of the post-war Five-Year Plan, for new and powerful progress in economy and culture, and for the greater power of the Soviet state!

　　(54) Long live our great Soviet motherland, the friendship of all nationalities in our country and a glorious and powerful fortress!

　　(55) Long live the great Bolshevik Party, the party of Lenin and Stalin - the long-tested vanguard of the Soviet people, the inspirer and organizer of our victories!

　　(56) Under the banner of Lenin, under the leadership of Stalin, advance towards the victory of communism!

### 苏联共产党（布）中央委员会颁布　纪念十月革命三十二周年口号

1949-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联共产党（布）中央委员会颁布
  　纪念十月革命三十二周年口号
    【新华社北京三日电】塔斯社莫斯科一日电：苏联共产党（布）中央委员会颁布纪念伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年的口号如下：
    （一）庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年！
    （二）向为民主与社会主义的胜利而奋斗的各国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    （三）向已进入建设社会主义之道路的波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、阿尔巴尼亚的人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    （四）向赢得了自由和他们国家的独立、并建立了人民民主国家的伟大的中国人民致兄弟的敬礼！中华人民共和国万岁！
    （五）祝统一、独立、民主、和平的德国长存与繁荣！
    （六）统一的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国万岁！
    （七）一切国家的劳动人民们：保卫和平是全世界一切民族人民的事业！揭穿新战争挑拨者的侵略计划！团结一切力量，为持久和平与各国安全而斗争！向保卫和平事业、反对新战争挑拨者的全体斗士致敬！
    （八）美、英两国人民与苏联人民在保卫和平、反对新战争挑拨者之斗争中的友谊万岁！
    （九）向光荣地保卫我们祖国之自由与独立的苏联武装部队致敬！苏军战士们：警醒地捍卫业已赢得的和平与我国人民的建设性的劳动！
    （十）警醒地保卫着我们祖国神圣不可侵犯的边疆的苏联边防军万岁！
    （十一）光荣归于苏联英雄们与社会主义劳动英雄们！他们是我们祖国的最优秀的儿女。
    （十二）苏联工人、农民和知识分子们：为提前完成战后五年计划而进一步扩大社会主义竞赛！
    （十三）苏联劳动人民们：为工业、运输业与农业技术的进一步进展而工作！采行新的技术，使生产技术臻于完善！不倦地提高劳动生产率——这是消费品价格进一步减低的基础！
    （十四）男女工人、工程师与技师们：不倦地改进产品质量！生产更多的优良货物！
    （十五）苏联劳动人民们：为国民经济一切部门的最大节省与超过计划的社会主义积累而工作！改进对流动资金的利用，减低生产成本！
    （十六）男女工人、工程师与技师们：为更好地利用设备而工作！节省材料、燃料与电力！
    （十七）煤矿工业的男女工人、工程师与技师们：为采煤的全部过程的完全机械化而工作！尽速恢复并建造新矿场，以更多的煤供应我国的国民经济！
    （十八）石油工业的男女工人、工程师与技师们：提高生产与炼油的速度！以更高的速度开凿油井和开发新油田！为社会主义经济生产更多的石油和石油产品！
    （十九）冶金工业的男女工人、工程师与技师们：保证五金生产上的新的进展，最大限度地利用生产上的人力与物力！吸取卓越的冶金专家们的经验！供给国家更多的铣铁、钢、辗金属及非铁金属！
    （二十）电力站的男女工人、工程师与技师们：增加电力的产量！尽速开办新的动力站，保证以源源不断的电力供应国民经济！
    （二十一）机器制造企业的男女工人、工程师与技师们：以进步的技术装备国民经济！尽速建立新的生产单位！以更多的机器供给工业、运输业与农业！

    （二十三）化学工业的男女工人、工程师与技师们：为采行新的进步的技术而工作！增加矿物肥料的生产！以更多的化学产品供应国民经济！
