Title: Head of Soviet Mission to Germany, Pushkin, Presents Credentials; Head of German Mission to the Soviet Union, Abel, Arrives in Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 5th: TASS, Berlin, November 4th: Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, received Pushkin, head of the Soviet Provisional Government's Diplomatic Mission to the German Democratic Republic, on November 4th. Foreign Minister Dodinger was also present. Pushkin presented President Pieck with a letter from Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, appointing Pushkin as head of the Soviet Provisional Government's Diplomatic Mission to the German Democratic Republic.
    TASS, Moscow, November 4th: Abel, head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Government, arrived in Moscow on the 4th.

### 苏驻德使团团长　普式金呈递任命书　德驻苏使团团长阿贝尔抵苏

1949-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏驻德使团团长
  　普式金呈递任命书
  　德驻苏使团团长阿贝尔抵苏
    【新华社北京五日电】塔斯社柏林四日讯：德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克于十一月四日接见苏联驻德意志民主共和国临时政府外交使团团长普式金，外交部部长窦丁格尔亦在座。普式金向皮克总统呈递了苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克关于任命普式金为苏联驻德意志民主共和国临时政府外交使团团长的信件。
    又据塔斯社莫斯科四日电，德意志民主共和国驻苏联政府外交使团团长阿贝尔，已于四日抵莫斯科。
