Author:
Date: 1949-11-28
Page: 3

　　Xinhua Radio Tonight's Highlights
    [Our Newspaper] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station, starting from today (the 28th), will temporarily change the revolving program sponsored by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation every Monday and Thursday, and the non-fixed program at 19:15 every day, to special broadcast programs on the Asian Women's Representative Conference. Today (the 28th) at 18:15, during the women's revolving program: Hu De Ming, member of the Executive Committee of the Vietnam Women's National Salvation Association and member of the preparatory committee of the Asian Women's Representative Conference, will broadcast a speech in Vietnamese, entitled: "The Armed Struggle of Vietnamese Women is a Struggle for Peace." The non-fixed program at 19:15 will rebroadcast the above speech in Mandarin. At 19:00, a lecture on common sense of natural science, entitled: "A Casual Discussion on the Endocrine System," written by Cai Qiao, Director of the Department of Physiology, Medical College of Nanjing University. At 18:30, the first literary and art program will feature a Mongolian song: the broadcast of the song record sent by the Mongolian delegation of the Asian-Australian Federation of Trade Unions to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. At 22:00, the second literary and art program will be the same as the first literary and art program.
    [Another News] On the Second Station, at 17:15, the children's program will feature children from the Second Affiliated Elementary School of the Normal University telling "The Story of Chicken Feather Letter" and singing songs. At 17:45, during the workers' program, Xiao Ming, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, will speak on the topic "Workers of the Whole City Continue to Work Hard and Resolutely Support the Implementation of the Resolutions of the Second Session of the Beijing Municipal People's Representatives Conference." At 18:00, a music program: the People's Art Troupe will perform "Army Songs." At 20:00, the Voice of the City will broadcast a "Comprehensive Report on the Cleaning Campaign Launched by the Beijing Municipal Epidemic Prevention Committee" from the News Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

### 新华电台今晚要目

1949-11-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　新华电台今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台，从今（二十八）天起每周星期一、四将中华全国民主妇女联合会主办的轮回节目，和每天十九点十五分不固定节目，暂时改为亚洲妇女代表会议的专题广播节目。今（二十八）天十八点十五分妇女轮回节目时间：由越南妇女救国联合会执行委员会委员、亚洲妇女代表会议筹备委员胡德明用越南话广播演讲，题目是：“越南妇女的武装斗争是为和平而斗争”，十九点十五分不固定节目，用国语重播上述讲词。十九点自然科学常识讲座、题目是：“内分泌丛谈”，撰稿人是南京大学医学院生理系主任蔡翘。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目蒙古歌曲：就是放送亚澳工会会议蒙古代表团送给中华全国总工会的歌曲唱片。二十二点第二次文艺节目和第一次文艺节目一样。
    【又讯】第二台十七点十五分儿童时间，由师大第二附小小朋友讲“鸡毛信的故事”及唱歌。十七点四十五分职工时间，京市总工会筹委会主任萧明讲话，题目是“全市工人继续努力，坚决拥护执行北京市第二届各界人民代表会议决议。”十八点音乐节目：人民文工团演唱“部队歌曲”。二十点市政之声，播送北京市人民政府新闻处“关于北京市防疫委员会所发动的清洁运动的综合报导”。
