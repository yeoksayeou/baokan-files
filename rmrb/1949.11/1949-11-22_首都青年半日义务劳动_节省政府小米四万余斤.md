Title: Capital Youth Half-Day Voluntary Labor Saves the Government Over 40,000 Catties of Millet
Author: Yi Yan
Date: 1949-11-22
Page: 4

　　Capital Youth Half-Day Voluntary Labor
　　Saves the Government Over 40,000 Catties of Millet
    [Beijing News] The capital's youth half-day voluntary labor to commemorate World Youth Day and World Student Week continued at the original site on the 20th. In the morning, 2,590 workers from 13 units including the Ministry of Railways, Water Company, Telecommunications Bureau, 70th Ordnance Factory, Construction Bureau Engineering Corps, Machine Factory, First Telecommunications Repair Shop, North China Electric Power Company, Clothing Factory, and Public Transport Company, and Women's Handicraft Factory participated, completing 1,294 cubic meters of roadbed leveling. In the afternoon, 2,089 cadres from 26 units including various units under the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Municipal Cultural Committee, the Suburban Committee, and the Experimental Peking Opera Troupe, completed 1,200 cubic meters of the project. The winners of today's competition in the morning include the 70th Ordnance Factory, Tram Company, Public Transport Company, and Telecommunications Bureau. The winners in the afternoon include the Construction Bureau, Tax Bureau, Education Bureau, and Civil Affairs Bureau. In total, six half-day labor sessions mobilized 27,169 people, completing a total of 7,684 cubic meters of roadbed leveling, saving the government approximately 40,000 catties of millet.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Yi Yan)

### 首都青年半日义务劳动　节省政府小米四万余斤
已彦
1949-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　首都青年半日义务劳动
　　节省政府小米四万余斤
    【北京讯】为纪念世界青年日及世界学生周的首都青年半日义务劳动，廿日继续在原地进行，上午参加的有铁道部、自来水公司、电信局、七十兵工厂、建设局工程总队、机器厂、第一电讯修配所、华北电业公司、被服厂、公共汽车公司、妇女手工厂等十三单位的工人两千五百九十人，完成一千二百九十四立方公尺平垫路基的工程，下午由北京市人民政府所属各单位及中苏友好协会、中国共产党北京市委员会、市总工会、市文委、郊委、实验平剧团等二十六个单位的干部，两千零八十九人，完成一千二百立方公尺的工程，今天竞赛得奖的上午计有七十兵工厂、电车公司、公共汽车公司、电信局四个单位。下午计有建设局、税务局、教育局、民政局四个单位。总计前后六次半日劳动，共发动二万七千一百六十九人，共完成七千六百八十四立方公尺平垫路基的工程，为政府节省约四万余斤小米。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（已彦）
