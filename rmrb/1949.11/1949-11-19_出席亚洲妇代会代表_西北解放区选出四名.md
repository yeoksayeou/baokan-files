Title: Representatives to the Asian Women's Congress Elected; Four from Northwest Liberated Areas

Author:
Date: 1949-11-19
Page: 3

　　Representatives to the Asian Women's Congress
  　Four Elected from Northwest Liberated Areas
    [Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 17th] The Preparatory Committee of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Democratic Women's Federation convened a meeting of Northwest democratic women's organizations on the 14th to elect four representatives to the Asian Women's Representative Conference. The elected representatives are: Zhang Zifang, representative of the People's Political Consultative Conference and director of the Preparatory Committee of the Border Region Democratic Women's Federation; Cao Guanqun, director of the Preparatory Committee of the Xi'an Women's Federation; Wang Cunyi, teacher at the Experimental Elementary School, chairman of the Xi'an YMCA, and chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Xi'an Primary and Secondary School Female Teachers' Representative Conference; and Li Fuqing, deputy director of the Women's Department of the Northwest General Branch of the China Democratic League. The Northwest region has a quota of six representatives to the conference, and the remaining two will be elected separately in Xinjiang and Lanzhou.

### 出席亚洲妇代会代表　西北解放区选出四名

1949-11-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　出席亚洲妇代会代表
  　西北解放区选出四名
    【新华社西安十七日电】陕甘宁边区民主妇女联合会筹委会于十四日召集西北各民主妇女团体选举出席亚洲妇女代表会议的四名代表，当选者为：人民政协代表、边区民主妇女联合会筹委会主任张子芳，西安市妇联筹委会主任曹冠群，实验小学教员、中华基督教西安市青年会理事长、西安市中小学女教师代表会筹委会主任委员王存义和民盟西北总支部妇女部副部长李馥清。按西北区出席会议的代表名额为六名，尚有两名将由新疆和兰州两地分别选出。
