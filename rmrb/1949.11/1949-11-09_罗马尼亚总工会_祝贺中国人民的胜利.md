Title: Romanian General Confederation of Labor Congratulates the Victory of the Chinese People
Author: 
Date: 1949-11-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 8th. The Chairman of the Central Committee of the Romanian General Confederation of Labor, Apostol, sent a telegram to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on the occasion of the anniversary of the October Revolution. The telegram is as follows:

All-China Federation of Trade Unions,

    On the occasion of the thirty-second anniversary of the great October Revolution, the Central Committee of the Romanian People's Republic General Confederation of Labor, on behalf of the workers, staff, and engineers in the organization, sends you brotherly and combative greetings. The path of the heroic Chinese people—the historic victory over Chiang Kai-shek's corrupt and reactionary regime and its American masters—continues to be illuminated by the light of the October Socialist Revolution. For thirty-two years, the October Revolution has been guiding the entire working class forward. The working people of the Romanian People's Republic sincerely congratulate you on your new victories in the struggle to develop and consolidate the young People's Republic of China.

    Long live the thirty-second anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution!
    Long live the Chinese people's struggle for peace, democracy, and socialism!
    Long live the world socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, guided by the respected and beloved genius teacher and friend of peace-loving people, Stalin!

    Apostol, Chairman of the Central Committee, Romanian People's Republic General Confederation of Labor

### 罗马尼亚总工会　祝贺中国人民的胜利

1949-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚总工会
  　祝贺中国人民的胜利
    【新华社布拉格八日电】罗马尼亚总工会中央委员会主席阿波斯托尔于十月革命节电贺我中华全国总工会。电文如下：中华全国总工会鉴：
    值此伟大的十月革命三十二周年纪念日，罗马尼亚人民共和国总工会中央委员会代表组织在工会中的工人、职员与工程师向你们致兄弟的战斗敬礼。英勇的中国人民的道路——对于蒋介石腐败反动政权及其美国主子的具有历史意义的胜利——被十月社会主义革命的光芒继续不断地照耀着。三十二年以来，十月革命即在引导着整个工人阶级前进。罗马尼亚人民共和国劳动人民，衷诚地祝贺你们在发展与巩固年轻的中华人民共和国的斗争中取得新的胜利。
    伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年万岁！
    中国人民争取和平、民主与社会主义的斗争万岁！
    以爱好和平的人民的可敬爱的天才导师和朋友斯大林指导下的苏联为首的世界社会主义阵线万岁！
    罗马尼亚人民共和国总工会　中央委员会主席　阿波斯托尔
