Title: Sayan and Others, Coming to China to Preside Over the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference, Pass Through Shenyang and Come to Beijing; Warmly Welcomed Along the Way Through Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1949-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 14th: Louis Saillant, General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU), and WFTU Vice-Presidents Subka (Czechoslovakia), Toledano (Latin America), Lerrrip (France), Broeksz (Netherlands), Piña (Cuba), Abdoulaye (Africa), and a delegation of more than 20 others who are coming to China to preside over the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference, arrived in Harbin by special train at 5:57 PM on the 13th. Feng Zhongyun, Chairman of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government, Vice-Chairmen Li Fanwu and Li Yanlu, Zhang Ce, Secretary of the CCP Provincial Committee, Zhang Xiuzhu, Chairman of the Harbin Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and representatives from various agencies, factories, and organizations, totaling more than 20,000 people, welcomed them at the station. After a short rest upon disembarking, Sayan and others participated in a welcoming rally in front of the station. When Sayan and his party stepped out of the station, the crowd enthusiastically shouted and applauded for several minutes. Sayan and others also frequently waved their hats and hands to express their gratitude. At the welcoming rally, after Zhang Xiuzhu, Chairman of the Harbin Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a welcoming speech, Louis Saillant spoke. He first paid his respects on behalf of the 72 million members of the World Federation of Trade Unions to the Chinese people, the People's Liberation Army, and Chairman Mao Zedong, the leader of the people. He emphasized that the working class around the world pays tribute to the newly founded People's Republic of China, because this historic event has strengthened the forces of the democratic camp. Subsequently, speeches were given by Soloviev, a representative of the Soviet Trade Unions, Toledano, Chairman of the Latin American Trade Unions, and Ali Madena, Chairman of the Indonesian Trade Unions. Although it was raining heavily at the time, the enthusiasm of the masses participating in the welcoming rally did not diminish. At 10:00 PM that evening, Sayan and his party left Harbin by special train for Beijing via Shenyang. Furthermore, when Sayan and his party passed through Ang'angxi Station in Heilongjiang Province, they were warmly welcomed by representatives from all sectors of the Party, government, military, and people of Heilongjiang Province and Qiqihar City.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 14th: Louis Saillant, General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU), and Vice-Presidents Lerrrip (France), Toledano (Mexico), Piña (Black Cuban), Broeksz (Netherlands), Abdoulaye (Africa), Subka (Czechoslovakia), and representatives from the eight member republics in the Asian part of the Soviet Union attending the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference, totaling more than 20 people, arrived in Shenyang at 1:00 PM today. Gao Gang, Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, Vice-Chairmen Li Fuchun and Lin Feng, Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Weizhen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, Zhu Qiwen, Mayor of Shenyang, and more than 3,000 workers from various enterprises went to the station to welcome them. Sayan and others alighted from the train amidst music and shook hands with the welcomers. The welcoming meeting was immediately held in front of the station square. After the Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions delivered a welcoming speech, Sayan gave a speech saying: "We see Shenyang as a new city, and it represents the victory of the working class struggle. I, on behalf of the 72 million workers of the world, pay tribute to the people of this city." He concluded by shouting slogans such as "Long live Mao Zedong." Speeches were also given by WFTU Vice-President and General Secretary of the French Trade Union Confederation Lerrrip, Bessierov, one of the heads of the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic Trade Union, and Piña, General Secretary of the Cuban Trade Union. After the meeting, Sayan and his party, accompanied by the welcomers, went to the Cultural Hotel for a tea party and lunch; then, at 4:30 PM, they left by train for Beijing.

### 来华主持亚澳工会会议  赛扬等过沈来京  过东北时沿途受到热烈欢迎

1949-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    来华主持亚澳工会会议
    赛扬等过沈来京
    过东北时沿途受到热烈欢迎
    【新华社哈尔滨十四日电】来华主持亚澳工会会议的世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬和世界工联各副主席——苏布卡（捷）、托列达诺（拉丁美洲）、勒里普（法）、勃罗克萨尔（荷兰）、皮尼亚（古巴）、阿勃杜拉（非洲）等一行二十余人，于十三日下午五时五十七分专车抵达哈尔滨。松江省人民政府主席冯仲云，副主席李范五、李延禄，中共省委书记张策，哈尔滨市总工会主席张修竹以及各机关、工厂、团体代表两万余人在车站欢迎。赛扬等下车稍事休息后，即参加站前欢迎大会。当赛扬等步出车站时，群众热情地高呼、鼓掌达数分钟之久。赛扬等亦频频挥帽、招手，以示谢意。欢迎大会在哈市总工会张修竹致欢迎词后，即由路易·赛扬讲话。他首先代表世界工会联合会七千二百万会员向中国人民、人民解放军和人民领袖毛泽东主席致敬。他并着重说明，全世界工人阶级都向新创立的中华人民共和国致敬，因为这个历史上的大事变，增强了民主阵营的力量。继由苏联工会代表索洛维夫、拉丁美洲工会主席托列达诺、印尼工会主席阿里·马德诺那先后讲话。当时虽然下着大雨，但参加欢迎大会的群众，热情始终不减。当晚十时，赛扬等一行专车离哈经沈赴京。又赛扬等一行路经黑龙江省昂昂溪车站时，曾经受黑龙江省和齐齐哈尔市党、政、军、民各界代表的热烈欢迎。
    【新华社沈阳十四日电】来华主持亚澳工会会议的世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬及副主席勒里普（法）、托列达诺（墨西哥）、皮尼亚（古巴黑人）、勃罗克萨尔（荷兰）、阿勃杜拉（非洲）、苏布卡（捷克）等以及出席亚澳工会会议的苏联在亚洲部分的八个加盟共和国的代表等二十余人，今日下午一时抵沈。前往车站欢迎的有东北人民政府主席高岗、副主席李富春、林枫，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，东北总工会主席张维桢，沈阳市长朱其文等及各企业工人三千余人。赛扬等在乐声中下车，与欢迎者握手。欢迎会当即在车站广场前举行。东北总工会主席致欢迎词后，赛扬发表演讲说：“我们看到沈阳这个新的城市，它表现了工人阶级斗争的胜利。我代表七千二百万世界职工向这个城市的人民致敬。”他最后高呼“毛泽东万岁”等口号。继续讲话的有世界工联副主席、法国职工会总书记勒里普，苏联卡萨克斯坦加盟共和国职工会负责人之一别西耶洛夫，古巴职工会总书记皮尼亚三人。会后，赛扬等一行在欢迎者陪同下赴文化宾馆举行茶话会，并进午餐；然后于下午四时半乘车赴京。
