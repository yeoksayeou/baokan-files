Title: Rewarding Works with Outstanding Contributions to Peace; The Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace Decides to Establish International Peace Prizes

Author:
Date: 1949-11-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd: TASS, Rome, 2nd: The Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace decided at its current session to establish three types of international peace prizes, each with a value of five million francs per year. The prizes will be awarded to works, films, and other artistic creations that have made the most outstanding contributions to consolidating peace among nations. The initial selection of winning works may be conducted by national judging committees established in various countries with the consent of the Standing Committee, while the final judging will be conducted by a judging committee designated by the Standing Committee before January 1st each year.

### 奖励对和平有卓越贡献的著作　和大常设委会决议设立国际和平奖金

1949-11-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　奖励对和平有卓越贡献的著作
  　和大常设委会决议设立国际和平奖金
    【新华社北京三日电】塔斯社罗马二日讯：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会在本届会议中决定设立三种国际和平奖金，每种奖金的金额为每年五百万法郎。奖金将授予对巩固各民族间的和平有最卓越贡献的著作、电影及其他艺术作品。获奖作品的初选，可由经和大常委会的同意而在各国设立的全国性的评判委员会主持，而最后的评判则将由常设委员会所指定的评判委员会在每年一月一日以前举行。
