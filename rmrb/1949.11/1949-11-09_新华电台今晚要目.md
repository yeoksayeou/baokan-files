Title: Xinhua Radio Tonight's Program Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-11-09
Page: 4

　　【This Newspaper News】Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station tonight, 19:15, Asian Women's Representative Conference special broadcast speech program: Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, will speak on "Welcoming the Asian Women's Representative Conference." 18:15 Youth Program: Qu Tangliang, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, will speak on "Commemorating World Youth Day, Unite International Youth to Defend Peace." At 20:15, an introduction to the Turkmen Soviet Socialist Republic, one of the countries participating in the Asian and Australian Trade Union Conference and the Asian Women's Representative Conference. At 19:00, a lecture on common sense in natural science, broadcasting "How are body wastes eliminated?" 18:30, First Arts and Entertainment Program, New Folk Art – Xihe Dagu "Sister-in-law Ye Rowing a Boat Across the River". 22:00, Second Arts and Entertainment Program, broadcast of "Hong Niangzi".
    Also, on the second channel at 17:15, Children's Time: Mr. Sun Jingxiu continues to tell the "Story of Paul." 17:45, Workers' Time: Comrade Shi Yu of the Private Industry Workers Working Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee will report on the work of the trade union to the city's private industry workers. 20:00, Municipal Voice: Broadcasting the Beijing Municipal Epidemic Prevention Committee's appeal to all citizens on preventing plague.

### 新华电台今晚要目

1949-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　新华电台今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十九点十五分亚洲妇女代表会议专题广播演讲节目：由中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅讲：“迎接亚洲妇女代表会议”。十八点十五分青年节目：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会联络部副部长区棠亮讲：“纪念世界青年日团结国际青年保卫和平”。二十点十五分，介绍参加亚洲、澳洲工会会议、亚洲妇女代表会议的国家之一——土尔克明苏维埃社会主义共和国。十九点自然科学常识讲座，播送：“身体废物怎么排除？”十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，新曲艺——西河大鼓“叶大嫂摇船渡江”。二十二点第二次文艺节目，播送“红娘子”。
    又第二台十七时十五分儿童时间：由孙敬修先生继续讲“保尔的故事”。十七时四十五分职工时间：本市职工总会筹委会私营产业工人工作委员会石羽同志，向全市私营产业工人报告工会工作情况。二十时市政之声：播送北京市防疫委员会关于预防鼠疫给全体市民的号召。
