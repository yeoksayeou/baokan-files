Title: Eighth Government Affairs Council Meeting Discusses Draft of the General Organizational Rules for People's Representative Conferences at All Levels
Author:
Date: 1949-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 28th—The Central People's Government Government Affairs Council held its eighth meeting today, discussing the draft of the general organizational rules for people's representative conferences in cities, counties, and provinces. These three drafts will be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for discussion after consultation with relevant parties. Today's meeting also discussed and approved the "Provisional Measures of the Central People's Government Government Affairs Council Regarding the Appointment and Removal of Personnel," which will be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 第八次政务会议　讨论各界人民代表会议组织通则草案

1949-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　第八次政务会议
  　讨论各界人民代表会议组织通则草案
    【新华社北京二十八日电】中央人民政府政务院今天举行第八次政务会议，讨论了市的、县的和省的各界人民代表会议的组织通则草案，这三个草案将于与有关各方协议后提请中央人民政府委员会讨论。今天的会议并且讨论通过了“中央人民政府政务院关于任免工作人员的暂行办法”，准备提请中央人民政府委员会批准。
