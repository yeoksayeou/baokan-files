Title: Asian Women's Congress Preparatory Committee Holds Special Radio Lectures; To Be Rebroadcast in Various Languages; Xinhua Second Station Adds Plague Prevention Programs
Author:
Date: 1949-11-09
Page: 4

The Preparatory Committee for the Asian Women's Congress
　　Holds Special Radio Lectures
　　To Be Rebroadcast in Various Languages
　　Xinhua Second Station Adds Plague Prevention Programs
　　[Report from this newspaper] The Asian Women's Representative Conference, decided to be convened by the International Democratic Women's Federation, will be held on December 6th in Beijing, the capital of New China. The conference preparatory committee has decided to hold special radio lectures related to the Asian Women's Representative Conference on the Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station, starting from the ninth of this month. The time will be from 19:15 to 19:30 every Wednesday to Saturday. In addition to the speakers broadcasting in their native languages, the radio station will also rebroadcast in Mandarin, English, Indonesian, and other languages at the same time from Thursday to Saturday, respectively. The order of the broadcast programs is as follows:
　　First week: November 9th, Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation: "Welcoming the Asian Women's Representative Conference" (Mandarin). November 10th (Cantonese). November 11th (English), November 12th (Indonesian).
　　Second week: November 16th, Marosova, representative sent by the International Democratic Women's Federation to participate in the preparatory work of the Asian Women's Representative Conference: "Soviet Women and Asian Women Fight Side by Side for World Peace" (Russian). November 17th (Mandarin), November 18th (Cantonese), November 19th (English).
　　Third week: November 23rd, Simonne Bertrand, representative sent by the International Democratic Women's Federation to participate in the preparatory work of the Asian Women's Representative Conference: "Democratic Women of Imperialist Countries Fight Against Those Who Oppress Asia" (French). November 24th (Mandarin), November 25th (Cantonese), November 26th (English).
　　[Another dispatch] The Propaganda Department of the Central Epidemic Prevention Committee, in order to further promote plague prevention, has specially consulted with the Second Station of Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station, and from today until November 14th, a special program on plague prevention will be broadcast from 13:30 to 14:00 every day, discussing various issues related to plague prevention. Wavelength 447.4 meters, 670 kilocycles. Today, the key issues of plague prevention released by the Central Epidemic Prevention Committee and the general public's understanding of plague prevention will be broadcast.

### 亚洲妇代会议筹委会　举办专题广播讲演　并将用各国语言重播  新华二台增防治鼠疫节目

1949-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　亚洲妇代会议筹委会
  　举办专题广播讲演
  　并将用各国语言重播
    新华二台增防治鼠疫节目
    【本报讯】国际民主妇女联合会决定召开的亚洲妇女代表会议，将于十二月六日在新中国的首都北京举行。该会议筹委会特决定自本月九日起，在北京新华广播电台举办有关亚洲妇女代表会议的专题广播演讲，时间是每星期三至六，十九点十五分至十九点三十分。除演讲者用其本国语言广播外，电台并将在星期四至六同一时间，分别用国语、英文、印尼语等重播。广播节目次序如下：
    第一周：十一月九日，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅：“迎接亚洲妇女代表会议”（国语）。十一月十日（广东话）。十一日（英语），十二日（印尼语）。
    第二周：十一月十六日，国际民主妇联派来参加亚洲妇女代表会议筹备工作代表玛洛索娃：“苏联妇女和亚洲妇女为世界和平而并肩奋斗”（俄语）。十一月十七日（国语），十八日（广东话），十九日（英语）。
    第三周：十一月二十三日，国际民主妇联派来参加亚洲妇女代表会议筹备工作代表西门贝特朗：“帝国主义国家的民主妇女向压迫亚洲的人们作斗争”（法语）。十一月二十四日（国语），二十五日（广东话），二十六日（英语）。
    【又讯】中央防疫委员会宣传处为进一步宣传防治鼠疫，特商同北京新华广播电台第二台，从今天起到十一月十四日每天十三点三十分至十四点特辟防治鼠疫特别节目，讲述有关防治鼠疫各种问题。波长四百四十七点四公尺，六七○千周。今天播送中央防疫委员会发布之防治鼠疫的几个关键问题和一般群众对于防治鼠疫应有的认识。
