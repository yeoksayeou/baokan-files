Title: Youth League and Youth Federation Send Congratulatory Message to World Federation of Democratic Youth
Author: Feng Wenbin, Liao Chengzhi
Date: 1949-11-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th.
Youth League and Youth Federation
Send Congratulatory Message to World Federation of Democratic Youth

    The Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth jointly sent a telegram to the World Federation of Democratic Youth, congratulating them on World Youth Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To the World Federation of Democratic Youth:
    Chinese youth warmly celebrate World Youth Day and support all the efforts of the World Federation of Democratic Youth to strive for world peace.
    Chinese New Democratic Youth League Feng Wenbin
    All-China Federation of Democratic Youth Liao Chengzhi
                November 5th (Attached picture)
    Speech by the Chief Delegate of the Korean Delegation (Photographed by this newspaper)

### 青年团及青联  电贺世界民主青联
冯文彬  廖承志
1949-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团及青联
    电贺世界民主青联
    【新华社北京十二日电】中国新民主主义青年团和中华全国民主青年联合总会，联合致电世界民主青年联盟，祝贺世界青年日。电文如下：
    世界民主青年联盟：
    中国青年热烈庆祝世界青年日，拥护世界民主青年联盟对争取世界和平的一切努力。
    中国新民主主义青年团  冯文彬
    中华全国民主青年联合总会  廖承志
                十一月五日（附图片）
