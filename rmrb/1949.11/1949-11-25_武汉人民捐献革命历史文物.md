Title: Wuhan People Donate Revolutionary Historical Relics
Author:
Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 2
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 24th: In response to the call of the Propaganda Department of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, people from all walks of life here enthusiastically donated various revolutionary historical relics. Among the relics received are short-term revolutionary war bonds issued by the Provisional Central Government of the Chinese Soviet Republic on July 1, 1932, serial notes issued by the Sichuan-Shaanxi-Shanxi Soviet Government, silver coins cast by the Workers and Peasants Bank of the Soviet Government of Henan, Hubei, and Anhui Provinces, and photos of the Red Army Martyrs Memorial Tower in Ruijin Central Soviet Area, Jiangxi. The photo of the memorial tower has the inscription by Commander-in-Chief Zhu, "The workers and peasants of the world will forever remember that this is the relic of the blood of the martyrs," and the handwriting is still clearly distinguishable. There are also portraits and letters of martyrs from the Land Revolution period. After these relics are collected, they will be sent to Beijing for display in the museum.

### 武汉人民捐献革命历史文物

1949-11-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　武汉人民捐献革命历史文物
    【新华社汉口二十四日电】此间各界人民响应中共武汉市委宣传部的号召，踊跃捐献各种革命历史文物。现已收到的文物中，有中华苏维埃共和国临时中央政府一九三二年七月一日发行的革命战争短期公债券、川陕晋省苏维埃政府发行的串票、鄂豫省苏维埃政府工农银行铸制的银元，和江西瑞金中央苏区红军烈士纪念塔照片等多种，纪念塔照片上有朱总司令的题字“世界工农群众永远地记着这是先烈血的遗迹”，字迹仍清晰可辨。其他尚有土地革命时期先烈的遗像、遗书等物。这些文物等收集齐备后将送北京交博物馆陈列。
