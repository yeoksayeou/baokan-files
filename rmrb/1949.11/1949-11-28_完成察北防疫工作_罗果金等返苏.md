Title: Luo Guojin and Others Return to Soviet Union After Completing Epidemic Prevention Work in Chabei
Author:
Date: 1949-11-28
Page: 1

　　Having Completed Epidemic Prevention Work in Chabei
　　Luo Guojin and Others Return to Soviet Union
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 27th] Dr. Luo Guojin (formerly translated as Laksen), a Soviet medical expert invited by the Central Epidemic Prevention Committee to work in China and complete plague prevention and treatment in Chabei, along with three associate professors from Moscow Medical College, Shagdotovsky, Belosetseva, and Mosinkova, as well as Suvalova, a laboratory technician from the Moscow Epidemic Prevention Station who transported three million doses of vaccine to Beijing from the Soviet Union, departed Beijing at 9:00 PM on the 26th to return to the Soviet Union. Zhou Enlai, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of Deputy Director Yan Baohang, and Dong Biwu, Director of the Central Epidemic Prevention Committee, on behalf of Zhu Lian, Li Dequan, Minister of the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government, and Vice Minister He Cheng, as well as representatives from various health departments and mass organizations, went to the station to see them off.

### 完成察北防疫工作　罗果金等返苏

1949-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　完成察北防疫工作
  　罗果金等返苏
    【新华社北京二十七日电】应中央防疫委员会的邀请来我国并在察北完成防治鼠疫工作的苏联医学专家罗果金（前译拉克森）博士和莫斯科医科大学三位副教授沙格多夫斯基、白洛塞杰娃、莫辛柯娃，以及自苏运送三百万份疫苗来京的莫斯科防疫站化验员苏瓦洛娃等，已于二十六日晚九时离京返苏。中央人民政府外交部周部长代表办公厅副主任阎宝航、中央防疫委员会董必武主任代表朱琏、中央人民政府卫生部李德全部长、贺诚副部长，以及各卫生部门、群众团体的代表等，均到车站欢送。
