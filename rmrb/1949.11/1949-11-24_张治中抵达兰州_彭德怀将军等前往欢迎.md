Title: Zhang Zhizhong Arrives in Lanzhou; General Peng Dehuai and Others Welcome Him
Author:
Date: 1949-11-24
Page: 1

　　Zhang Zhizhong Arrives in Lanzhou
　　General Peng Dehuai and Others Welcome Him
    [Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 23rd] Zhang Zhizhong, a member of the Central People's Government, arrived in Lanzhou at 4:00 PM on the 22nd on official business. Xing Zhaotang, a representative of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, arrived with him. Those who went to welcome him included Peng Dehuai, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission and Commander of the First Field Army; He Long, Commander of the Northwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Xi Zhongxun, Political Commissar; Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Commander of the First Field Army and Director of the Lanzhou Military Control Commission; Wu Hongbin, Mayor of the Lanzhou People's Government, and more than 40 of General Zhang's relatives in Lanzhou. The Lanzhou Military Control Commission hosted a banquet.

### 张治中抵达兰州　彭德怀将军等前往欢迎

1949-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　张治中抵达兰州
　　彭德怀将军等前往欢迎
    【新华社兰州二十三日电】中央人民政府委员张治中于二十二日下午四时因公抵达兰州。同来者有人民政协代表邢肇棠。前往欢迎的有中国人民革命军事委员会副主席兼第一野战军司令员彭德怀，中国人民解放军西北军区司令员贺龙，政治委员习仲勋，第一野战军副司令员兼兰州市军管会主任张宗逊，兰州市人民政府市长吴鸿宾及张将军留兰亲属等共四十余人。兰州市军管会设宴招待。
