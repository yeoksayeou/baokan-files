Title: Soviet Writers' Association Hosts Reception for Chinese Delegation; Ding Ling, Zhao Shuli, and Others Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 1

　　Soviet Writers' Association Hosts Reception for Chinese Delegation
　　Ding Ling, Zhao Shuli, and Others Invited to Attend
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 23rd: TASS Moscow, November 22nd: Fadeyev, General Secretary of the Soviet Writers' Association, hosted a banquet to entertain the Chinese delegation that participated in the celebrations of the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution. Among those attending the banquet were prominent Chinese writers Ding Ling, Zhao Shuli, Ding Xielin, and Li Yong, the driver of the "Mao Zedong" locomotive. Writer Gorbatov extended greetings to the delegation on behalf of the Soviet Writers' Association. Fadeyev congratulated the Chinese writers, actors, and public figures on their greater achievements in their creative work for the benefit of the Chinese people. He presented Ding Ling and Zhao Shuli with the Russian editions of "The Sun Shines Over the Sanggan River" and "Changes in Li Village". The reception was filled with an extremely cordial atmosphere.

### 苏联作家协会欢宴我代表团　丁玲赵树理等应邀出席

1949-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联作家协会欢宴我代表团
　　丁玲赵树理等应邀出席
    【新华社北京二十三日电】据塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：苏联作家协会总书记法捷耶夫设宴招待中国参加十月革命节三十二周年纪念典礼的代表团。出席宴会的有中国著名作家丁玲、赵树理、丁燮林、毛泽东号铁路机车司机李永等人。作家郭尔巴托夫代表苏联作家协会向代表团致敬。法捷耶夫祝贺中国的作家、演员及公众领袖在他们为中国人民的福利而进行的创造性工作中取得更多的成就。他把在莫斯科以俄文出版的“太阳照在桑乾河上”及“李家庄的变迁”两书送给丁玲和赵树理。招待会上洋溢着极恳挚的气氛。
