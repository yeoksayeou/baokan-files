Title: Tsinghua University Holds Ethnic Minority Cultural Relics Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1949-11-02
Page: 4

　　Tsinghua University Holds
  　Ethnic Minority Cultural Relics Exhibition
    [This newspaper dispatch] To welcome the liberation of the vast border regions and introduce the cultures of Taiwan, Tibet, and various ethnic minorities in the Southwest, Tsinghua University is holding a joint exhibition of cultural relics related to Tibet and ethnic minorities in the Southwest, collected in recent years, along with cultural relics of the Gaoshan people of Taiwan, recently donated by Dr. Ding Huikang of the Hongqiao Sanatorium in Shanghai. The exhibition will be held at the National College of Art, Shuaifu Garden, from November 5th to 7th (i.e., Saturday to Monday), from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. People from all walks of life, organizations, institutions, and schools are welcome to visit. Admission is free.

### 清华大学举办　少数民族文物展览

1949-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　清华大学举办
  　少数民族文物展览
    【本报讯】为迎接广大边疆地区的解放，介绍台湾、西藏及西南各少数民族的文化，清华大学特将近年来所采集的有关西藏及西南少数民族的文物，并最近上海虹桥疗养院丁惠康医师所捐赠的台湾高山族文物，联合举行展览。展览会地址在帅府园国立艺术专科学校，展览日期为十一月五日至七日（即星期六至星期一）上午九时至下午五时。欢迎各界人士及团体机关学校参观，不收门票。
