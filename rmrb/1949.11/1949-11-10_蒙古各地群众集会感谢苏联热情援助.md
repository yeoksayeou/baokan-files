Title: Mass Rallies in Mongolia Thank the Soviet Union for its Warm Assistance
Author:
Date: 1949-11-10
Page: 3
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 9th: TASS, Ulan Bator, November 8th: The working people of the Mongolian People's Republic are joyously celebrating the 32nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. Streets and squares are decorated with flags, streamers and banners, with numerous portraits of Lenin, Stalin, members of the Politburo of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and leaders of the Mongolian People's Republic. Thousands upon thousands of working people and columns of the Ulan Bator garrison gathered in the square named after Sukhe-Bator and its adjacent streets. The First Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Suran Yaabu, delivered a speech at the mass rally, expressing on behalf of the free and independent Mongolian people their heartfelt gratitude to the friendly Soviet people and the great Stalin for their selfless assistance to the continuous progress and prosperity of the Mongolian People's Republic. After the meeting, a festive demonstration of the working people of the capital was held. Mass rallies commemorating the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution were also held in all regions of the country.

### 蒙古各地群众集会感谢苏联热情援助

1949-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　蒙古各地群众集会感谢苏联热情援助
    【新华社北京九日电】塔斯社乌兰巴托尔八日讯：蒙古人民共和国的劳动人民，欢欣鼓舞地庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年。街道和广场装饰着旗帜、长幡与标语牌，列宁、斯大林、苏联共产党政治局各委员和蒙古人民共和国各领袖的许多画像。以苏赫——巴托尔命名的广场及其邻近的街道，聚集了千千万万的劳动人民和乌兰巴托尔警卫部队的行列。蒙古人民共和国第一副总理苏兰雅布在群众大会上发表演说，他代表自由独立的蒙古人民，向友好的苏联人民和伟大的斯大林表示衷心感激，他们对蒙古人民共和国的不断进步与繁荣给予了大公无私的援助。会后举行首都劳动人民的节日示威游行。全国各区也都纷纷举行了纪念十月革命三十二周年的群众大会。
