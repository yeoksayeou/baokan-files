Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Xinhua Radio Station Two to Hold Russian Broadcast Lectures, Seeking Opinions from All Sectors of the City
Author:
Date: 1949-11-03
Page: 1

　　Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Xinhua Radio Station Two
    Will Hold Russian Broadcast Lectures Seeking Opinions from All Sectors of the City
    [This newspaper's report] The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Beijing Xinhua Radio Station Two are preparing to jointly hold Russian broadcast lectures in the near future, starting with pronunciation. Most of the new words will be taken from workers' literacy textbooks. This is the first time it has been done in this city. We hope that citizens from all walks of life will provide opinions on various issues such as how many times a week, how long each time, and what time to schedule it, so as to meet the needs of the majority of listeners. Please send your opinions to Beijing Xinhua Radio Station Two, No. 3 West Chang'an Street.

### 北京中苏友协与新华二台  将举办广播俄文讲座向各界市民征求意见

1949-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京中苏友协与新华二台
    将举办广播俄文讲座向各界市民征求意见
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会和北京新华广播电台第二台准备在最近联合举办广播俄文讲座，从发音教起，生字多半采自工人识字课本，这在本市还是初办，希望各界市民对于每周几次，每次多长时间，按排在什么时候等各项问题，多多提出意见，以便适合多数听众需要，意见请寄交西长安街三号北京新华广播电台第二台。
