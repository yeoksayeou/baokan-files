Title: Trade Unions in Fujian and Other Places Hold Production Celebration Meetings
Author:
Date: 1949-11-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 21st: Trade union organizations in Fujian, Shandong, Jinan, Zhangjiakou, and other places have sent telegrams to congratulate the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference. The units sending congratulatory telegrams include: the Preparatory Committee of the Fujian Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Shandong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Jinan Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Preparatory Committee of the Zhangjiakou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Shijiazhuang Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the First Congress of Trade Union Members of Xuzhou City, the Songjiang Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Benxi Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Subei District Federation of Trade Unions, and the Nanjing Branch of the Shanghai Railway Trade Union. In their congratulatory telegrams, trade union groups in various places unanimously stated that the convening of the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference in our capital, Beijing, is an honor for the Chinese working class and the broad masses of the people. At the same time, they expressed their determination to celebrate the success of the conference with practical actions such as striving to increase production, improve quality, and do a good job in trade union work.

### 福建等地工会提高生产祝贺大会

1949-11-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　福建等地工会提高生产祝贺大会
    【新华社北京二十一日电】福建、山东、济南、张家口等地工会组织，纷纷驰电祝贺亚澳工会会议。发出贺电的单位包括：福建省总工会筹备委员会，山东省总工会、济南市总工会、张家口市总工会筹备委员会、石家庄市总工会、徐州市第一届工会会员代表大会、松江省总工会、本溪市总工会、苏北区总工会和上海区铁路工会南京分会。各地职工团体在贺电中，一致认为亚澳工会会议能在我国首都北京召开，是中国工人阶级和广大人民的光荣，同时表示决以努力生产，提高质量，作好工会工作等实际行动，来庆祝大会的成功。
