Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang Holds Central Plenary Session, Elects Chairman and Standing Committee Members
Author:
Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th—The Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a plenary session at 10:00 a.m. on the 22nd of this month. Over 40 central committee members attended, including Li Jishen, He Xiangning, Tan Pingshan, Chen Mingshu, Cai Tingkai, Jiang Guangnai, Shao Lizi, Chen Shaoxian, Li Renren, Zhu Yunshan, Wang Kunlun, Yu Xinqing, Li Minxin, and Yu Zhenying. The meeting elected Li Jishen, He Xiangning, Tan Pingshan, Chen Mingshu, Cai Tingkai, Shao Lizi, and He Guiyan as the presidium of the plenary session, Chen Cisheng as the secretary-general, and Wu Maosun, Lü Jiyi, and Li Junlong as the deputy secretaries-general. Li Jishen served as the executive chairman of the meeting. After discussing the proposals submitted by the Democratic Factions of the Kuomintang Meeting for implementation, the meeting elected Li Jishen as the chairman. Li Jishen, He Xiangning, Tan Pingshan, Chen Mingshu, Cai Tingkai, Jiang Guangnai, Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, Shao Lizi, Liu Yazi, Zhu Yunshan, Chen Shaoxian, Chen Qiyuan, Mei Gongbin, Wang Kunlun, Guo Chuntao, Xu Baoju, Ning Wu, He Guiyan, Gan Zhenying, and Li Shizhang were elected as 21 standing committee members, with Mei Gongbin as the secretary-general, Zhu Yunshan as the head of the organization department, and Xu Baoju as the head of the propaganda department. The meeting concluded in the afternoon.

### 民革举行中央全会　选出主席及常务委员

1949-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　民革举行中央全会
　　选出主席及常务委员
    【新华社北京二十四日电】中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会，于本月二十二日上午十时举行全体会议。出席中央委员李济深、何香凝、谭平山、陈铭枢、蔡廷锴、蒋光鼐、邵力子、陈劭先、李任仁、朱蕴山、王昆仑、余心清、李民欣、于振瀛等四十余人。会议公推李济深、何香凝、谭平山、陈铭枢、蔡廷锴、邵力子、贺贵严为全会主席团，陈此生为秘书长，吴茂荪、吕集义、李俊龙为副秘书长。由李济深任本次会议执行主席。会议在讨论国民党民主派会议交下执行之各案后，选举李济深为主席。李济深、何香凝、谭平山、陈铭枢、蔡廷锴、蒋光鼐、程潜、张治中、邵力子、柳亚子、朱蕴山、陈劭先、陈其瑗、梅龚彬、王昆仑、郭春涛、许宝驹、寗武、贺贵严、干振瀛、李世璋等二十一人为常务委员，梅龚彬为秘书长，朱蕴山为组织部长，许宝驹为宣传部长。会议于下午闭幕。
