Title: All-India Trade Union Congress Cables Guo Moruo Inviting Him to Attend the All-India Peace Conference; Guo Moruo Replies with Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1949-11-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 22nd: The All-India Trade Union Congress recently cabled Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, inviting him to attend the All-India Peace Conference and requesting a congratulatory message for the conference. Guo Moruo replied yesterday expressing his gratitude and sent a congratulatory telegram to the All-India Peace Conference. The three telegrams are published below:

	(1) Telegram from the All-India Trade Union Congress to Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace:

	The All-India Peace Conference has decided to open in Calcutta on November 24th. We cordially invite you to participate as a representative with brotherly status. If you cannot come, please send a telegram to the All-India Peace Conference, and we hope you can give us a congratulatory message.

						All-India Trade Union Congress

	(2) Guo Moruo's Reply to the All-India Trade Union Congress:

	Thank you for the invitation on behalf of the All-India Peace Conference. However, due to the lack of diplomatic relations between China and India, I am unable to attend this conference, which we would like to attend very much. I regret this. A separate congratulatory message will be forwarded.

				Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace

	(3) Congratulatory Telegram to the All-India Peace Conference:

	On behalf of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, I extend my warmest congratulations to you and ask you to convey brotherly greetings to the peace-loving working people of India. We are confident that, with the concerted efforts of peace-loving people around the world, we will certainly be able to stop the warmongers' conspiracy to provoke a new war. With this confidence, the convening of the All-India Peace Conference is of great significance. We wish the conference success.

			Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace

### 全印职工大会电郭沫若邀请出席全印和平大会　郭沫若复电致贺

1949-11-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　全印职工大会电郭沫若邀请出席全印和平大会
　　郭沫若复电致贺
    【新华社北京二十二日电】全印职工大会于日前致电中国保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，邀请他出席全印和平大会，并希望给大会以贺电。郭沫若已于昨日复电表示感谢，并致电全印和平大会祝贺。兹将三电发表如下：
    　　　　（一）全印职工大会来电中国保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若：
    全印和平大会决定于十一月二十四日在加尔各答开幕，热忱地请你以亲如弟兄的代表资格参加。假使不能来，请电全印和平大会，并希望你能给我们贺电。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　全印职工大会
    　　　　（二）郭沫若复电全印职工大会：
    感谢你们代表全印和平大会的邀请。但是，由于中印两国间还没有外交的关系，使我不能参加这个我们愿意参加的大会，我很遗憾。另贺电乞转达。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若
    　　　　（三）致全印和平大会贺电全印和平大会：
    我代表中国保卫世界和平委员会，向你们热烈地致贺，并请转致全印爱好和平的劳动人民以兄弟的敬礼。我们确信，在全世界爱好和平人民的一致努力之下，一定能制止战争贩子们挑拨新战争的阴谋。在这样的信心下，全印和平大会的召开是有重大意义的。预祝大会成功。 　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若
