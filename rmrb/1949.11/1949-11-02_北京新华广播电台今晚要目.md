Title: Tonight's Program Highlights from Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-11-02
Page: 4

　　【This Newspaper Reported】Tonight's special anti-plague program on Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station at 19:00: Continuing broadcast of the second lecture on how to eliminate plague, "Specific Issues in Plague Prevention," and the third lecture, "General Knowledge the Masses Should Have Regarding Plague Prevention." The youth program at 18:15 will broadcast the China Youth's twenty-third issue editorial, "Learning Mutual Aid Must Be Based on Self-Study," and the twenty-fourth issue editorial, "Middle School Curriculum Should Not Be Too Heavy." The first arts and entertainment program at 18:30 will feature a new folk art form: local "Luozihu" opera "Liang Shiying Sacrifices Himself to Kill the Enemy," performed by the Joint Association of Folk Artists from Hebei, Shandong, and Henan. The second arts and entertainment program at 22:00 will feature military music.
    Also, the second station's children's hour at 17:15: Mr. Sun Jingxiu will continue to tell "The Story of Paul."
    The workers' hour at 17:45: The Broadcasting Group of the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau of this station will report on: "The Production Situation of the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau After Implementing Democratic Wage Evaluation." At 20:00, the Voice of the Municipality will broadcast the Beijing Municipal People's Government Education Bureau's "Introduction to the Work of the Municipal People's Education Center and Reading Rooms."

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十九点防治鼠疫特别节目：继续播送怎样扑灭鼠疫第二讲“鼠疫预防的几个具体问题”，和第三讲“一般群众对于防治鼠疫应有的常识”。十八点十五分青年节目，播送中国青年第二十三期的社论“学习互助要以自学为基础”，和第二十四期的社论“中学功课不宜太重”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，新曲艺：地方落子“梁世英舍身杀敌”，由冀鲁豫民间艺人联合会演唱。二十二点第二次文艺节目—军乐。
    又第二台十七时十五分儿童时间：由孙敬修先生继续讲“保尔的故事”。
    十七时四十五分职工时间：由本台北京电信局广播小组报导：“北京电信局实行民主评议工资以后的生产情况”。二十时市政之声播送北京市人民政府教育局“关于本市人民教育馆和书报阅览室的工作介绍”。
