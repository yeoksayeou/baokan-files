Title: Xinhua Bookstore, Sanlian Bookstore, Guangzhou Branch Opens

Author:

Date: 1949-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua Bookstore
Sanlian Bookstore
Guangzhou Branch Opens
    [Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 16th] The Guangzhou Xinhua Bookstore and the "Life, Reading, New Knowledge Sanlian Bookstore" Guangzhou branch simultaneously opened in Guangzhou. The Xinhua Bookstore currently has tens of thousands of copies of Chairman Mao's important works and books on relevant policies, women's issues, literature, and art. Approximately one million copies of various books shipped from Hankou to Guangzhou will also arrive soon. The Sanlian Bookstore Guangzhou branch will offer more than 300 kinds of books and periodicals to the market.

### 新华书店　三联书店　广州分店开业

1949-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　新华书店
  　三联书店
  　广州分店开业
    【新华社广州十六日电】广州新华书店及“生活、读书、新知三联书店”广州分店，同时在广州开业。新华书店现有毛主席重要著作及有关政策方针、妇女问题、文艺等方面书籍十几万册，由汉口运穗的各种书籍约百余万册，最近亦将抵达。三联书店广州分店则以三百多种书刊应市。
