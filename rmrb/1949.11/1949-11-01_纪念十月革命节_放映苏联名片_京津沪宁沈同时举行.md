Title: Commemorating the October Revolution Day: Screening of Famous Soviet Films Simultaneously Held in Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, and Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1949-11-01
Page: 4

　　Commemorating the October Revolution Day
  　Screening of Famous Soviet Films
  　Simultaneously Held in Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, and Shenyang
    [North China News] November 7th marks the 32nd anniversary of the Soviet Socialist Revolution. To celebrate this auspicious occasion, the Central Film Bureau has arranged a widespread screening of Soviet films. Starting from November 1st, it will be held simultaneously in major cities such as the capital, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, and Shenyang. The Propaganda Department of the bureau has also edited a "Introduction to Soviet Films," which will be available in major bookstores and cinemas starting today. The content includes articles by Guo Moruo, Lu Dingyi, Mao Dun, Tian Han, An E, Ai Qing, Babo, Wang Yaping, Lü Jian, Shi Dongshan, Wu Zuguang, Cai Chusheng, and others, as well as an article by the head of the Soviet delegation and famous director Gerasimov, "How 'The Young Guard' Was Filmed." The films being screened in Beijing and Tianjin include "The Young Guard," "The Life of Lenin," "Lenin in October," "Lenin in 1918," "Moscow the Capital," "Revolutionary Heroes," "Leningrad," and other films.
  　　　　　　　　　　　(Wire, Propaganda, No. 3)

### 纪念十月革命节　放映苏联名片　京津沪宁沈同时举行

1949-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　纪念十月革命节
  　放映苏联名片
  　京津沪宁沈同时举行
    【华北消息】十一月七日为苏联社会主义革命三十二周年纪念。为庆祝这一佳节，中央电影管理局特布置了一次苏联电影的普遍放映。自十一月一日起，在首都、天津、上海、南京、沈阳等大都市同时举行。该局宣传科并编辑了一本“苏联电影介绍”，自今日起在各大书店及影院出售，内容载有郭沫若、陆定一、茅盾、田汉、安娥、艾青、巴波、王亚平、吕剑、史东山、吴祖光、蔡楚生等人的文章，及苏联代表团团员名导演格拉西莫夫的“青年近卫军是怎样摄制的”一文。在京、津放映者有“青年近卫军”、“列宁的生平”、“列宁在十月”、“列宁在一九一八年”、“首都莫斯科”、“革命英雄”、“列宁格勒”等影片。
  　　　　　　　　　　　（电、宣、第三号）
