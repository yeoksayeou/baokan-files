Title: Chairman Mao Replies to Former National Resources Commission Employees in Hong Kong, Encouraging Them to Uphold Patriotic Stance and Protect National Property
Author:
Date: 1949-11-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 19th: Chairman Mao today sent a telegram replying to the employees of the former Kuomintang government's National Resources Commission's Hong Kong Trade Office and Foreign Trade Office, stating:

To the comrades of the former National Resources Commission's Hong Kong Trade Office and Foreign Trade Office:

Your telegram of the 14th has been received, and I am very pleased. I hope you will unite as one, uphold your patriotic stance, and protect national property, awaiting the takeover by the Central People's Government.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mao Zedong, November 19th

### 毛主席电复前资委会驻港员工　勉坚持爱国立场保护祖国财产

1949-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席电复前资委会驻港员工
  　勉坚持爱国立场保护祖国财产
    【新华社北京十九日电】毛主席本日电复前国民党政府资源委员会驻香港员工称：
    前资源委员会香港贸易处国外贸易事务所员工同志们：
    十四日电悉，甚为欣慰。即望团结一致，坚持爱国立场，保护祖国财产，以待中央人民政府的接收。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　毛泽东　十一月十九日
