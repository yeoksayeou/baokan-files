The First Engineering Division of the Railway Engineering Corps Completes the Wukong River Bridge Day and Night
　　Ensuring the Early Opening of the Baoding-Shijiazhuang Section of the Beijing-Hankou Railway
　　[This newspaper dispatch] The Wukong River Bridge is the most difficult bridge to repair on the 131-kilometer-long Baoding-Shijiazhuang section of the Beijing-Hankou Railway, with a total length of 450 meters. More than 1,200 employees of the First Engineering Division of the Railway Engineering Corps officially started construction in mid-October. With the close cooperation and assistance of the local Party, government, military, and people, as well as the Railway Bureau, it was fully completed on November 3. By this point, the entire Beijing-Hankou Railway from the people's capital, Beijing, to Shijiazhuang, was open to traffic. (Attached pictures)

### 铁道兵团第一工程处昼夜抢修　悟空河大桥如期完成  保证了京汉路保石段提前通车

1949-11-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　铁道兵团第一工程处昼夜抢修
  　悟空河大桥如期完成
    保证了京汉路保石段提前通车
    【本报讯】悟空河大桥在一百三十一公里长的京汉路保石段上是最难修复的一座桥，全长四百五十公尺。铁道兵团第一工程处一千二百余员工，在十月中旬正式施工，在当地党政军民和路局的密切配合协助下，至十一月三日全部竣工。至此，人民的首都北京到石家庄的京汉路全线通车了。（附图片）
