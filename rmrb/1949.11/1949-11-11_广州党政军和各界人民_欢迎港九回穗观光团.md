Title: Guangzhou Party, Government, Military, and People of All Circles Welcome Hong Kong and Kowloon Sightseeing Delegation to Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1949-11-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 10th. The Hong Kong and Kowloon delegation, composed of people from all walks of life, has been warmly welcomed by the Guangzhou Party, government, military, and people of all circles since arriving in Guangzhou on the 6th of this month. On the afternoon of the 7th, the Preparatory Office of the Guangzhou Federation of Trade Unions first hosted a banquet for all the representatives of the delegation. On the morning of the 8th, the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, the Preparatory Office of the Federation of Trade Unions, the New Democratic Youth League, the Student Union, the Women's Federation, and the South China Cultural Troupe jointly held a welcoming meeting. On the evening of the same day, the Guangdong Provincial People's Government and a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army held a meeting to welcome the Hong Kong and Kowloon sightseeing delegation, members of the provincial government who came to the province to take office, and democratic figures who arrived in Guangzhou. Ye Jianying, Fang Fang, Li Zhangda, Chen Geng, Deng Hua and others attended. At the meeting, the sightseeing delegation presented flags to the people's leader Chairman Mao, a banner reading "Savior of the People," which was accepted by Ye Jianying on behalf of Chairman Mao; a banner to the Guangdong Provincial People's Government reading "Light of Baiyue," which was accepted by Fang Fang on behalf of the government; and two banners to the commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army who liberated Guangzhou, which were accepted by Chen Geng and Deng Hua respectively. At the meeting, Chairman Ye Jianying delivered a speech, calling on compatriots in Hong Kong and overseas to continue their efforts to assist the People's Government in building a new Guangzhou, a new Guangdong, and a new China. Chairman Ye Jianying's speech was met with warm applause from all the representatives of Hong Kong and Kowloon. Zhang Zhennan, the leader of the delegation, said in his reply: "We must bring the new atmosphere of Guangzhou after liberation to all 1.8 million compatriots in Hong Kong."

### 广州党政军和各界人民  欢迎港九回穗观光团

1949-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    广州党政军和各界人民
    欢迎港九回穗观光团
    【新华社广州十日电】港九各界组成的回穗观光团，自本月六日到达广州后，受到广州党政军和各界人民的热烈欢迎。七日下午，广州总工会筹备处首先欢宴该团全体代表。八日上午，中国共产党广州市委会、广州市人民政府、总工会筹备处、新民主主义青年团、学生联合会、妇女联合会、华南文工团等联合举行欢迎大会。同日晚，广东省人民政府及人民解放军某部，开会欢迎港九各界观光团及来省就职的省府委员及抵穗的民主人士。叶剑英、方方、李章达、陈赓、邓华等均出席。会上由观光团举行献旗礼，献给人民领袖毛主席的锦旗“人民救星”，由叶剑英代表接受；献给广东省人民政府的锦旗“百粤之光”，由方方代表接受；献给解放广州的人民解放军指战员们的锦旗两面，分别由陈赓、邓华代表接受。会上叶剑英主席致词，他号召香港和海外的侨胞们，继续努力，协助人民政府建设新广州、新广东和新中国，叶剑英主席的致词获得港九全体代表热烈的鼓掌。该团总领队张振南在致答词中说：“我们一定要把广州解放后的新生气象，带给全港一百八十万同胞。”
