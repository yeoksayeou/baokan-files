Title: Celebrating the Founding of the People's Republic of China, Vietnamese People and Overseas Chinese Hold Grand Rally; Over 20,000 People Cheer "Long Live Chairman Mao! Long Live President Ho!" Viet Minh Calls on People to Emulate the Chinese People and Prepare for General Counter-Offensive

Author: 
Date: 1949-11-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th: News from Vietnam: The Vietnamese people warmly celebrate the founding of the People's Republic of China. On October 25th, the Viet Minh Headquarters and the General Association of the Vietnam National United Front held a grand rally in Thai Nguyen City, the wartime capital of Vietnam, to celebrate the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. A few days prior, the streets were adorned with giant slogans such as "Unite the Chinese and Vietnamese People," "Down with International Imperialism," "Long Live Chairman Mao," and "Long Live President Ho." The rally commenced at 7:00 PM. The square where the rally was held was brightly lit, with large portraits of Chairman Mao and President Ho Chi Minh prominently displayed. Attendees included Pu Xuanjin, Minister Without Portfolio of Vietnam; Dr. Wu Tingcong, Minister of Veterans Affairs; General Zhu Wenjin, Commander of the Viet Bac War Zone; Huang Guoyue, Secretary-General of the Viet Minh; Nguyen Van Nhan, Secretary-General of the Vietnam National United Front; Shu La, representative of the Preparatory Committee of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association; overseas Chinese representatives Zhang Jianmin and Mei Zhannan; representatives from various political, military, and civilian sectors; and over 20,000 Vietnamese and Chinese people. The rally orchestra first played the Vietnamese national anthem, followed by the national anthem of the People's Republic of China. The Vietnamese masses shouted "Long Live the People's Republic of China!" "Long Live Chairman Mao!" The overseas Chinese masses shouted "Long Live the Democratic Republic of Vietnam!" "Long Live President Ho!" "Long Live Sino-Vietnamese Unity!" These slogans echoed for a long time. At the rally, Huang Guoyue, Secretary-General of the Viet Minh, pointed out in his speech that the victory of the Chinese people is the victory of all oppressed nations in the world. He called on the Vietnamese people to emulate the Chinese people and actively prepare for a general counter-offensive. Nguyen Van Nhan, representative of the Viet Lien Association, pointed out in his speech that the failure of US imperialism in China is the failure of international imperialism. Shu La, representative of the Preparatory Committee of the Viet-Hoa Friendship Association, analyzed the common struggle of the Chinese and Vietnamese people against oppression throughout history and called on the Chinese and Vietnamese people to unite and defeat international imperialism. The rally closed at 10:00 PM amidst enthusiastic cheers and the singing of "Ode to Mao Zedong" and "Long Live Ho Chi Minh." After the meeting, the overseas Chinese political work team performed plays and Yangko dances, which lasted until 2:00 AM.

### 庆祝中华人民共和国成立  越人民与华侨盛大集会  二万余人欢呼毛主席万岁胡主席万岁  越盟号召向中国人民看齐准备总反攻

1949-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国成立
    越人民与华侨盛大集会
    二万余人欢呼毛主席万岁胡主席万岁
    越盟号召向中国人民看齐准备总反攻
    【新华社北京十二日电】越南消息：越南人民热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立。十月二十五日越盟总部和越南国民联合会总会在越南抗战首都太原市举行盛大集会，庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立。几天前，街头便悬挂了“中越人民团结起来”、“打倒国际帝国主义”、“毛主席万岁”、“胡主席万岁”等巨型标语。大会于下午七时开幕。集会的广场上灯火辉煌，高悬着毛主席和胡志明主席的巨像。到会者有越南不管部部长浦春津、荣誉军人部长武庭颂博士、越北战区司令朱文尽将军、越盟总书记黄国越、越南国民联合会总书记阮文仁、越华亲善会筹备会代表舒拉、华侨代表张建民、梅展南、各政军民代表及越华群众二万余人。大会乐队首奏越南国歌，继奏中华人民共和国国歌。越南群众高呼“中华人民共和国万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”华侨群众高呼“越南民主共和国万岁！”“胡主席万岁！”“华越团结万岁！”等口号声经久不息。大会上越盟总书记黄国越在讲话中指出，中国人民的胜利即是全世界各被压迫民族的胜利。他号召越南人民向中国人民看齐，积极准备总反攻。越联会代表阮文仁在讲话中指出，美帝国主义在中国的失败，即是国际帝国主义的失败。越华亲善会筹备会代表舒拉分析了历史上中、越人民反对压迫的共同斗争，并号召中越人民团结，击败国际帝国主义。大会于十时在热烈欢呼和高唱“毛泽东颂”、“胡志明万岁”的歌声中闭幕。会后由华侨政工队演出话剧及秧歌，至深夜二时始毕。
