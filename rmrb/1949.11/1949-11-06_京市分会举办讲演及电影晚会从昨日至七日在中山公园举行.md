Author:
Date: 1949-11-06
Page: 4

　　Beijing Branch Holds Lectures and Movie Nights at Zhongshan Park from Yesterday to the 7th

[Report from this newspaper] The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has scheduled lectures and movie nights at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall at 6:30 PM daily from the 5th to the 7th to celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution. The first evening event was held yesterday, attended by approximately fifty students and office workers. Qian Junrui delivered a lecture explaining the world significance of the Soviet October Revolution, recounting the great and friendly assistance the Soviet Union gave to the Chinese people after the October Revolution, and emphasizing the importance of consolidating and developing Sino-Soviet friendship. The audience repeatedly shouted slogans such as "Long live the October Revolution!" "Long live the Soviet Union!" "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Stalin!" The evening concluded with a screening of the Soviet Union's 1949 May Day parade documentary. The crowd's enthusiasm remained high throughout, with applause erupting frequently during the lecture and film viewing.

### 京市分会举办讲演及电影晚会从昨日至七日在中山公园举行

1949-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市分会举办讲演及电影晚会从昨日至七日在中山公园举行
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会定于五日至七日每日下午六时半在中山公园音乐堂举行庆祝十月革命节三十二周年讲演及电影晚会，昨第一次晚会，到学生和机关职员约五十人。由钱俊瑞讲演，说明苏联十月革命的世界意义，叙述了苏联在十月革命后给与中国人民伟大友好的帮助，指出了巩固和发展中苏友谊的重要。全场不断高呼“十月革命万岁！”“苏联万岁！”“中华人民共和国万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”“斯大林万岁！”等口号。最后放映苏联一九四九年五一节大检阅纪录片。群众情绪始终高涨，在听讲和观电影时，不时发出掌声。
