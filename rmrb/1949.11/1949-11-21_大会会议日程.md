　　Conference Meeting Schedule
    [Beijing News] The Second Session of the People's Representatives Conference of All Circles in Beijing passed the following meeting schedule at yesterday's (20th) plenary session:
    　　　　20th (Sunday) Afternoon (Meeting begins at 2:00 PM)
    One, Announce the opening of the meeting.
    Two, Elect the Presidium.
    Three, The Chairman delivers the opening speech.
    Four, The Presidium announces the Rules of Procedure for the Second Session of the People's Representatives Conference of All Circles in Beijing.
    Five, Approve the Organizational Regulations for the Second Session of the People's Representatives Conference of All Circles in Beijing.
    Six, Government Work Report.
    Seven, Approve the list of members of the Government Work Report Review Committee.
    Eight, Approve the members of the Proposal Review Committee.
    　　　　21st (Monday) Afternoon (Meeting begins at 2:00 PM)
    One, Discuss the Government Work Report.
    Two, Approve the Organizational Outline of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.
    Three, Discuss the proposals from the Consultative Committee and the Municipal Government.
    　　　　22nd (Tuesday) Morning (Meeting begins at 9:00 AM)
    One, Elect the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and members of the Consultative Committee of this session of the People's Representatives Conference of All Circles.
    Two, Approve the Election Rules for the Mayor, Deputy Mayors, and members of the Beijing Municipal Government Committee.
    Three, Elect election supervisors and ballot counters.
    Four, Elect the Mayor, Deputy Mayors, and members of the Beijing Municipal Government Committee.
    Five, Discuss the report on the results of the Proposal Review Committee's review.
    　　　　22nd (Tuesday) Afternoon (Meeting begins at 2:00 PM)
    One, Announce the election results.
    Two, Approve various telegrams and the Conference Declaration.
    Three, Deliver the closing speech.
    Four, Take a photograph.
    Five, Announce the closing of the meeting.

### 大会会议日程

1949-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　大会会议日程
    【北京讯】北京市第二届各界人民代表会议在昨（二十）日全体会议中，通过会议日程如下：
    　　　　二十日（星期日）下午（二时开会）
    一、宣布开会。
    二、选举主席团。
    三、主席致开幕词。
    四、主席团宣布北京市第二届各界人民代表会议议事规则。
    五、通过北京市第二届各界人民代表会议组织条例。
    六、政府工作报告。
    七、通过政府工作报告审查委员会委员名单。
    八、通过提案审查委员会委员。
    　　　　二十一日（星期一）下午（二时开会）
    一、讨论政府工作报告。
    二、通过北京市人民政府组织大纲。
    三、讨论协商委员会及市政府提案。
    　　　　二十二日（星期二）上午（九时开会）
    一、选举本届各界人民代表会议协商委员会主席、副主席及委员。
    二、通过北京市市长、副市长及市政府委员会委员选举规则。
    三、选举选举监督及监票人。
    四、选举北京市市长、副市长及市政府委员会委员。
    五、讨论提案审查委员会审查结果报告。
    　　　　二十二日（星期二）下午（二时开会）
    一、宣布选举结果。
    二、通过各项通电暨大会宣言。
    三、致闭幕词。
    四、摄影。
    五、宣布闭会。
