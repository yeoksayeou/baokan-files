Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation Sends Letter to Chairman Mao to Express Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1949-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th. The Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation departed from Harbin and returned to their country on the 2nd. Before leaving, they wrote a letter to Chairman Mao to express their gratitude. The letter stated:
    "To Chairman Mao of the People's Republic of China:
    As our Soviet cultural workers delegation departs from the People's Republic of China, we once again express our profound and sincere gratitude for the warm and friendly reception from various Chinese organizations and the Chinese people.
    Each of us, upon returning to our homeland, will exert all our strength to further consolidate the friendship between our two great nations.
    We respectfully wish you good health and new successes in your work for the happiness of the Chinese people and the happiness of all mankind."

### 苏文化代表团函毛主席致谢

1949-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏文化代表团函毛主席致谢
    【新华社北京十五日电】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团，已于二日离哈返国，离别时，曾写信给毛主席，表示谢意。信称：
    “中华人民共和国毛主席：
    我们苏联文化工作者代表团离开中华人民共和国时，对于中国各团体和中国人民对我们的热烈友谊的招待，再致深厚而诚挚的感谢。
    我们每个人回到自己的祖国后，要尽一切力量，来更加巩固我们两大民族的友谊。
    敬祝您的身体健康和为中国人民谋幸福、为全人类谋幸福的工作之新的成功。”
