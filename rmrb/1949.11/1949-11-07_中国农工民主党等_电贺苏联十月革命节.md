Title: China Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, etc. Congratulate the Soviet Union on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1949-11-07
Page: 4

China Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, etc.
Congratulate the Soviet Union on the October Revolution Anniversary

[Reported by this newspaper] The China Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin, congratulating him on the anniversary of the October Revolution. The telegram reads as follows:

Marshal Stalin:
We warmly celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution and respectfully extend our highest regards to you. The October Revolution is the lighthouse for the liberation of the oppressed classes and oppressed people all over the world. Under the wise leadership of the great Lenin and yourself, the October Revolution inaugurated a brilliant new history for mankind.

The brilliance of the October Revolution shines all over the world. The Chinese people's liberation movement, illuminated by the light of the October Revolution, has achieved a comprehensive victory. The liberated Chinese people are resolutely following the great Soviet Union and learning from the Soviet Union.

We respectfully congratulate the Soviet Union's socialist construction under your leadership, which is developing day by day, and the ever-growing strength of the forces defending world peace, led by the Soviet Union.

Chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the China Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, Zhang Bojun
Chairman of the Supervisory Committee, Peng Zemin

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 6th: Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, sent a telegram to the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, congratulating them on the Soviet October Revolution Anniversary. The telegram reads as follows:

Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee:
We, the Chinese youth, celebrate the great October Revolution Day with unparalleled enthusiasm. On behalf of the 4.2 million organized members and the broad masses of democratic youth, we extend our highest brotherly salute to you.

Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Liao Chengzhi
November 5, 1949

Beijing News: The full text of the congratulatory telegram sent by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's celebration of the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution to Soviet Marshal Stalin is as follows:

"Marshal Stalin: The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association warmly celebrates the 32nd anniversary of the great socialist October Revolution. Under your leadership, the Soviet people have made incomparably brilliant contributions to all mankind in the glorious cause of opposing world fascism and in the glorious cause of building socialism.

We warmly support Sino-Soviet friendship, deeply thank the Soviet state and people for their significant assistance to our revolution and construction, and warmly celebrate your health and the happiness of the Soviet people.

Support the Soviet Union, the leader of the world peace and democracy camp!

Long live Stalin, the leader and teacher of progressive mankind all over the world!

Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Celebrating the 32nd Anniversary of the October Revolution
November 6th

### 中国农工民主党等　电贺苏联十月革命节

1949-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国农工民主党等
  　电贺苏联十月革命节
    【本报讯】中国农工民主党致电斯大林大元帅，祝贺十月革命节，电文如下：斯大林大元帅：
    我们热烈的庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年，并敬向阁下致崇高的敬礼。十月革命是全世界被压迫阶级和被压迫人民解放的灯塔，十月革命在伟大的列宁和阁下的睿智的领导下，开创了人类的灿烂的新历史。
    十月革命的光辉照耀着全世界，中国人民解放运动就是在十月革命的光辉照耀下，取得了全面的胜利，解放了的中国人民是坚决地追随着伟大的苏联，向苏联学习。
    我们敬贺在阁下领导下的苏联社会主义建设，日益发展和以苏联为首的保卫世界和平力量日益强大。
    中国农工民主党中央执行委员会主席章伯钧监察委员会主席彭泽民
    【新华社北京六日电】中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志致电苏联青年反法西斯委员会，祝贺苏联十月革命节。电文如下：
    苏联青年反法西斯委员会：
    我们中国青年用无比的热忱来庆祝伟大的十月革命节日。谨代表四百二十万有组织的会员及广大的民主青年向你们致崇高的兄弟的敬礼。
    中华全国民主青年联合总会主席　廖承志
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十一月五日
    【北京讯】北京市中苏友好协会庆祝十月革命三十二周年纪念大会向苏联斯大林大元帅发出的贺电全文如下：“斯大林大元帅：北京市中苏友好协会热烈地庆祝伟大的社会主义十月革命三十二周年，在您领导下的苏联人民在反对世界法西斯的光荣事业中，在建设社会主义的光荣事业中，对全人类作了无比光辉的贡献。
    我们热烈的拥护中苏友好，深切的感谢苏联国家和人民对我们革命与建设事业的重大援助，热烈的庆祝您的健康和苏联人民的幸福。
    拥护世界和平民主阵营的领导者苏联！
    全世界进步人类的领袖和导师斯大林万岁！
    北京市中苏友好协会庆祝十月革命三十二周年纪念大会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月六日
