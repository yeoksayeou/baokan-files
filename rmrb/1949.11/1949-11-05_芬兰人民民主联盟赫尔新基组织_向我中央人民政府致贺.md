Title: Finnish People's Democratic League Helsinki Organization Sends Congratulations to Our Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1949-11-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 4th: A letter from Helsinki, dated October 21st, states that at a meeting hosted by the Helsinki local organization of the Finnish People's Democratic League, many workers and progressive intellectuals listened to reports on the heroic struggle of the Chinese people and the founding of the People's Republic of China. The meeting passed a letter of congratulations to the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and Chairman Mao Zedong, stating: "Your great victory encourages workers all over the world to fight more vigorously for peace, democracy, and socialism." The letter of congratulations was signed by Aino Sastamoinen, Chairman of the Helsinki Organization of the Finnish People's Democratic League.

### 芬兰人民民主联盟赫尔新基组织　向我中央人民政府致贺

1949-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　芬兰人民民主联盟赫尔新基组织
  　向我中央人民政府致贺
    【新华社布拉格四日电】赫尔新基十月二十一日来信称：在赫尔新基的芬兰人民民主联盟地方组织主持的会议上，许多工人与进步的知识分子倾听了关于中国人民的英勇斗争与中华人民共和国成立的报告。会议通过了一封向中华人民共和国中央人民政府与毛泽东主席致贺的信，内称：“你们的伟大胜利鼓舞着全世界的工人们更有力地为和平、民主与社会主义而斗争。”贺信由芬兰人民民主联盟赫尔新基组织的主席艾伊诺·萨斯塔摩伊宁签署。
