Title: The Soviet Union Supports Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement; Vyshinsky Denounces the Chiang Kai-shek Gangster Tsiang Tingfu at the UN General Assembly, Denies the Kuomintang Delegation as Representatives of China
Author:
Date: 1949-11-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 26th: TASS, New York, November 25th, reported that Soviet delegation leader Vyshinsky, in his speech at the UN General Assembly plenary session on November 23 regarding the prohibition of atomic weapons and the control of atomic energy, mentioned the shameless lies of Chiang Kai-shek gangster representative Tsiang Tingfu, saying: "I do not intend to argue with this gentleman (referring to Tsiang Tingfu), primarily for the simple reason that it is not known whom he represents. As everyone knows, Mr. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, notified Mr. Romulo, Chairman of the UN General Assembly, on November 15, that the delegation led by Tsiang Tingfu can no longer represent China and has no right to speak on behalf of the Chinese people at the United Nations. The Soviet delegation supports this statement and does not consider the Kuomintang delegation to be representatives of China." (Note: Tsiang Tingfu slandered the Soviet Union in the Special Political Committee of the UN General Assembly on the 22nd for deliberately delaying the establishment of an agreement.)

### 苏联支持周外长声明  维辛斯基在联大痛斥蒋匪廷黻　否认国民党代表团为中国代表

1949-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联支持周外长声明
    维辛斯基在联大痛斥蒋匪廷黻
  　否认国民党代表团为中国代表
    【新华社北京二十六日电】据塔斯社纽约二十五日消息，苏联代表团团长维辛斯基二十三日在联合国大会全体会议上关于禁用原子武器与管制原子能问题的发言中，提到国民党匪帮代表蒋廷黻的无耻谰言时说：“我不打算和这位先生（指蒋廷黻）进行争论，首先是因为这样一个简单的理由，即：不知道他究竟是代表谁。大家知道，中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来先生在十一月十五日曾通知联合国大会主席罗慕洛先生说：蒋廷黻所领导的代表团已经不能代表中国，而且也无权代表中国人民在联合国发言。苏联代表团支持这一声明，并且不认为国民党代表团是中国的代表。”（按蒋廷黻在二十二日的联合国大会特别政治委员会上曾诬蔑苏联故意拖延协议的成立）
