Title: Simonov Arrives in Changsha; Cheng Qian and 3,000 Others Go to the Station to Welcome Him
Author:
Date: 1949-11-07
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 6th. The famous Soviet writer Simonov and his party arrived in Changsha on the morning of the 6th. Cheng Qian, a member of the Central People's Government, Xiao Jingguang, Commander of the Hunan Military Region and Commander of the 12th Army of the Fourth Field Army, Huang Kecheng, Secretary of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and more than 3,000 representatives from various organs, troops, and people's organizations went to the station to welcome them. After General Xiao Jingguang delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the people of Hunan, Simonov said in his speech: "I am very grateful to the people of Changsha for their welcome to me. I do not regard this welcome as a welcome to me personally, but as a welcome to all the people of the Soviet Union. The Soviet Union was a friend of the Chinese people during the war, and is also a friend of the Chinese people during this happy period; it was a friend in the past, it is a friend now, and it will definitely be a friend in the future." Finally, Simonov shouted in Chinese: "Long live Sino-Soviet friendship!" "Long live Mao Zedong!" "Long live Stalin!" In the afternoon, all circles in Changsha hosted a banquet to welcome them and held an evening party.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 5th. After the famous Soviet writer Simonov arrived in Hankou, he was warmly received and welcomed by people from all walks of life in Wuhan. The Central China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee convened a grand welcome meeting of more than 1,000 people on the afternoon of the 4th. After Deng Zihui delivered a welcome speech, he invited Simonov to give a speech. He said: The friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union has always been very deep and very solid. The Soviet people have long been watching and caring about the struggle of the Chinese people. Chinese comrades say that the struggle of the Soviet Union has inspired the Chinese revolution; I want to say that the revolutionary struggle of the Chinese people also often inspires the Soviet people. Then he introduced the situation of the Soviet people in restoring war wounds and engaging in new construction in the four years after the war. He also expressed that he would do his best to introduce the heroic struggle of the Chinese people's revolutionary victory to the Soviet people. In the evening, people from all walks of life held a banquet. Eight Soviet engineers, Vasarov and Chikhonov, who were invited to China to help repair the Wuchang-Changsha section of the Yuehan Railway, had just returned from Changsha and were also invited to participate.

### 西蒙诺夫抵长沙　程潜等三千人赴车站欢迎

1949-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　西蒙诺夫抵长沙
  　程潜等三千人赴车站欢迎
    【新华社长沙六日电】苏联名作家西蒙诺夫等一行，六日晨到达长沙。中央人民政府委员程潜、湖南军区司令员兼第四野战军第十二兵团司令员萧劲光、中共湖南省委书记黄克诚等及各机关、部队、人民团体代表三千余人都到车站欢迎。萧劲光将军代表湖南人民致欢迎词后，西蒙诺夫在致词中说：“我很感激长沙人民对我的欢迎，这种欢迎我不当作是对我个人的欢迎，而是当作对全苏联人民的欢迎。苏联过去在战争期间是中国人民的朋友，在现在幸福的期间也是中国人民的朋友；过去是朋友，现在是朋友，将来也一定是朋友。”最后西蒙诺夫用中国话高呼：“中苏友好万岁！”“毛泽东万岁！”“斯大林万岁！”下午，长沙各界设宴欢迎并举行晚会。
    【新华社汉口五日电】苏联名作家西蒙诺夫抵汉后，受到武汉市各界人民热情的接待和欢迎。中共中央华中局于四日下午召集一个一千余人的盛大欢迎会。邓子恢致欢迎词后，即请西蒙诺夫演讲，他说：中苏两国人民的友谊一向是很深刻而且很巩固的。苏联人民长期地注视和关心中国人民的斗争。中国的同志说苏联的斗争鼓舞着中国革命；我要说，中国人民革命斗争也经常鼓舞着苏联人民。接着他介绍了苏联人民在战后四年中恢复战争创伤和从事新的建设的情况。他并表示要尽最大努力把中国人民革命胜利的英勇斗争介绍给苏联人民。晚上各界举行欢宴。应邀来华帮助修复粤汉路武（昌）长（沙）段的苏联工程师瓦沙罗夫、奇霍诺夫八人刚从长沙回来，亦应邀参加。
