Title: The Song of the Chinese and Soviet People
Author: Ke Zhongping
Date: 1949-11-13
Page: 5

The Song of the Chinese and Soviet People
Ke Zhongping
We, the people of China and the Soviet Union,
You are willing to pull out the thorn in my eye,
I am willing to wash away the hatred in your heart,
We love each other so dearly!
★
We, the people of China and the Soviet Union,
Our countries are two,
Our hearts are one,
We wholeheartedly love independence, freedom, and peace.
★
Come! Come! Come!
For us, for the freedom and peace of the people of the world,
Under the banner of Stalin and Mao Zedong,
Let us sing together and advance together:
The territory of our two great nations, China and the Soviet Union,
The wind cannot blow to the end in a few days,
The people of our two great nations, China and the Soviet Union,
Outnumber the stars in the sky;
Our Red Army, created by Lenin and Stalin,
Has the ability to protect the people of the whole world,
Our People's Liberation Army, created by Mao Zedong and Zhu De,
Has the ability to determine the fate of the new China's progress.
The ocean is only so wide and so deep,
The strength of our two great nations, China and the Soviet Union, is infinite,
We can use infinite strength,
To fight for freedom and for the peace of the people of the world.
The people's Earth is one,
The heart of the people's Earth is a big red heart,
We can use our red heart to unite the people of the world,
We can turn the Earth into a peaceful and beautiful big family!
        (Written in Yan'an in 1945, reorganized in Beijing in 1949 – the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution)

### 中苏人民的歌
柯仲平
1949-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏人民的歌
    柯仲平
    我们中苏两国的人民，
    你肯为我拔掉眼中钉，
    我肯为你洗掉心头恨，
    我们是这样的相爱相亲！
  ★
    我们中苏两国的人民，
    我们的国是两个国，
    我们的心是一条心，
    我们一心爱独立，爱自由和平。
  ★
    来！来！来！
    为我们，为世界人民的自由和平，
    我们在斯大林、毛泽东的旗帜下，
    一同唱，一同前进：
    我们中苏两大国的国土，
    大风跑几天都跑不尽，
    我们中苏两大国的人民，
    多过那满天的星星；
    我们列宁斯大林创造的红军，
    有本领保护全世界的人民，
    我们毛泽东朱德创造的人民解放军，
    有能力决定新中国前进的命运。
    海洋只有那么广，那么深，
    我们中苏两大国的力量无穷无尽，
    我们能够拿无穷无尽的力量，
    为自由，为世界人民的和平斗争。
    人民地球是一个，
    人民地球的心是颗大红心，
    我们能够拿红心团结全世界的人民，
    我们能够把地球变成一个和平而美好的大家庭！
            （一九四五年作在延安，一九四九年——十月革命三十
      二周年重新整理在北京）
