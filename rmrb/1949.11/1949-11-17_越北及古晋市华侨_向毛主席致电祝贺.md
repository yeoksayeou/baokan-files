Title: Overseas Chinese in Northern Vietnam and Kuching Send Telegrams to Chairman Mao to Express Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1949-11-17
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th. Overseas Chinese organizations in Northern Vietnam sent a telegram to Chairman Mao, expressing their support for the Central People's Government. The telegram reads as follows: Respected Chairman Mao:
　　Hearing the news of the founding of the People's Government of the motherland, the overseas Chinese in Northern Vietnam are all overjoyed and encouraged. We have suffered from the exploitation and oppression of the French imperialists and the Kuomintang reactionaries. We clearly see that only an independent and democratic motherland can protect overseas Chinese, and only when the country where we reside is liberated can our legitimate rights and interests be guaranteed. Therefore, we are determined to thoroughly support the People's Government of the motherland under your leadership and actively participate in the liberation struggle of the Vietnamese people.
　　Salute to the liberation of the Chinese and Vietnamese people!
　　Zhang Jianmin, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Council of Thai Nguyen, Northern Vietnam; Mei Zhannan, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Council of Bac Giang; Zheng Lincheng, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Council of Bac Can; Deng Ying, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Council of Lang Son.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th. Overseas Chinese in Kuching, British Sarawak, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao on the 12th, expressing their warm support for the Central People's Government. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Chairman Mao and members of the Government Committee of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    We sincerely congratulate you on the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and guarantee our most enthusiastic support.
    Kuching Hakka Association, Chinese Times, Chinese Women's Association, Overseas Chinese Youth Association, Overseas Chinese Dock Workers' Union, Overseas Chinese Coffee Shop Employees' Union, respectfully.

### 越北及古晋市华侨　向毛主席致电祝贺

1949-11-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　越北及古晋市华侨
  　向毛主席致电祝贺
    【新华社北京十六日电】越南北部各华侨团体致电毛主席，表示拥护中央人民政府。电文如下：敬爱的毛主席：
    欣闻祖国人民政府成立，我们越北华侨无不欢欣鼓舞。我们曾受尽了法帝国主义者和国民党反动派的剥削压迫，我们看清了只有独立民主的祖国才能保护海外侨胞，只有我们所居留的国家得到解放，我们切身合法权益才有保障，因此我们誓愿彻底拥护你所领导的祖国人民政府，并积极参加越南人民的解放斗争。
    中越人民解放敬礼！
    越北太原华侨理事会理事长张建民，北江华侨理事会理事长梅展南，北浒华侨理事会理事长郑林成，谅山华侨理事会理事长邓瀛。
    【新华社北京十六日电】英属萨拉瓦克古晋市华侨于十二日致电毛主席，表示热烈拥护中央人民政府。电文如下：
    中国北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛主席暨政府委员钧鉴：
    我们竭诚向你们祝贺中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立，并保证予以最热烈的拥护。
    古晋客家协会、中华时报、中华妇女协会、海外华侨青年协会、华侨码头工人工会、华侨咖啡馆员工联合会叩。
