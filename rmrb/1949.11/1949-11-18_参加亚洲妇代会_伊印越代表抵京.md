Title: Representatives from Iran, India, and Vietnam Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Asian Women's Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-11-18
Page: 4

　　Attending the Asian Women's Conference
  　Representatives from Iran, India, and Vietnam Arrive in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th] Masin Faroughi, representative from Iran, Amita Devi, representative from India, and Ho Thi Minh, representative from Vietnam, arrived in Beijing by car at 7:10 this morning to attend the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian Women's Conference. More than 20 people, including all the representatives of the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian Women's Conference and representatives of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, went to the station to welcome them.

### 参加亚洲妇代会　伊印越代表抵京

1949-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　参加亚洲妇代会
  　伊印越代表抵京
    【新华社北京十七日电】参加亚洲妇女代表会议国际筹备委员会的伊朗代表玛辛·法洛琪、印度代表阿米塔·台维、越南代表胡德明三人于今日上午七时一刻乘车抵达北京。前往车站欢迎者有亚洲妇女会议国际筹委会的全体代表和中华全国民主妇女联合会代表等二十余人。
