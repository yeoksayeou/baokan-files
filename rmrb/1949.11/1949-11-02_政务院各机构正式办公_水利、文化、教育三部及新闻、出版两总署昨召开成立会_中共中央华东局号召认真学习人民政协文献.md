Title: The State Administrative Council's various agencies officially begin work. The Ministry of Water Resources, Ministry of Culture, and Ministry of Education, as well as the General Administration of News and the General Administration of Publications, held their founding meetings yesterday. The CCP Central Committee's East China Bureau calls for earnest study of the documents of the People's Political Consultative Conference.
Author:
Date: 1949-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 1st: The various ministries, commissions, academies, administrations, and banks of the Central People's Government's State Administrative Council officially began work today. The Ministry of Water Resources, Ministry of Culture, Ministry of Education, General Administration of News, and General Administration of Publications all held their founding meetings today.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 1st: The Propaganda Department of the CCP Central Committee's East China Bureau has issued instructions calling on all Party members and cadres in the East China region to earnestly study the three important documents of the Political Consultative Conference -- "The Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference," "The Organizational Law of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference," and "The Organizational Law of the Central People's Government" -- as well as Chairman Mao's opening speech and Comrade Liu Shaoqi's speech at the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and to make these documents the focus of winter political education. The instructions emphasized that when studying these documents, one should grasp the spirit and essence of the documents well. Systematic study should be made of the issues related to the current situation and tasks, the issue of the people's democratic dictatorship, the issue of the people's democratic united front, and other basic policy issues, and at the same time, Mao Zedong's "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," the spirit of the Second Plenary Session of the CCP, and the relevant editorials of the Beijing People's Daily and Shanghai Liberation Daily should also be studied in connection with this. Regarding learning and education methods, the instructions stipulate that in addition to class discussions, special reports should be organized, disseminated, and necessary examinations should be held.

### 政务院各机构正式办公  水利、文化、教育三部及新闻、出版两总署昨召开成立会  中共中央华东局号召认真学习人民政协文献

1949-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院各机构正式办公
    水利、文化、教育三部及新闻、出版两总署昨召开成立会
    中共中央华东局号召认真学习人民政协文献
    【新华社北京一日电】中央人民政府政务院各部、会、院、署、行已于今日开始正式办公。水利部、文化部、教育部、新闻总署、出版总署均于今日召开成立会议。
    【新华社上海一日电】中共中央华东局宣传部顷发布指示，号召华东地区全体党员和干部，认真学习政治协商会议的三个重要文献——“中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领”、“中国人民政治协商会议组织法”、“中央人民政府组织法”以及中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议上毛主席的开幕词和刘少奇同志的讲话等，并把这些文件作为冬季政治教育的重心。指示着重指出：在学习这些文件时，应很好掌握文件的精神和实质。对于其中有关目前形势和任务的问题、人民民主专政问题、人民民主统一战线问题，及其他各项基本政策问题，均应作系统研究，并须同时联系研究毛主席的“论人民民主专政”、中共二中全会的精神及北京人民日报、上海解放日报与此有关的社论。在学习和教育方法上，指示规定除上课讨论外，还应组织专门报告、传达并举行必要的考试。
