Title: Moscow People Cheerfully Prepare to Celebrate the October Revolution Anniversary; Central Department Store Sees a 500,000 Ruble Increase in Daily Trade; Thousands of People Move into New Homes on the Revolutionary Holiday
Author:
Date: 1949-11-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th: Moscow, 5th—The residents of the Soviet capital, Moscow, are enthusiastically preparing to celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution. Business in the shops has been booming recently. The central department store alone receives more than 100,000 customers daily. Sales of woolen fabrics, silk fabrics, leather shoes, and ready-made garments have greatly increased compared to usual. It is easy to identify those customers celebrating two holidays (the October Revolution anniversary and the joy of moving into a new home). Thousands of Muscovites have received housing in newly completed apartment buildings, and they are buying new furniture, carpets, radio receivers, and more. Food stores are crowded with people until late at night. People are buying large quantities of fruits from Georgia, Armenia, Azerbaijan, and Moldavia, as well as pork and sausages from Ukraine, canned goods from Latvia, and various famous wines from Transcaucasia and Central Asia. All thirty-two markets in Moscow are doing good business, with collective farmers from the Moscow region selling tons of meat, vegetables, poultry, dairy products, etc., there every day.

### 莫斯科人民欢腾鼓舞　准备庆祝十月革命节  中央商店贸易日增五十万卢布成千人民于革命节日迁入新居

1949-11-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科人民欢腾鼓舞
  　准备庆祝十月革命节
    中央商店贸易日增五十万卢布成千人民于革命节日迁入新居
    【新华社北京六日电】莫斯科五日讯：苏联首都莫斯科的居民正热烈准备庆祝十月革命三十二周年纪念。近日商店的生意大为兴隆，只中央百货商店一处，每天就有十万以上的顾客。毛织物、丝织物、皮鞋及成衣等的售货量较平日大增，中央百货商店每日贸易额比平日增加五十万卢布。在顾客中很容易看出来那些庆祝两个节日（十月革命纪念日和迁入新居的喜日）的买主。数以千计的莫斯科人在新建筑完成的公寓中得到了住所，他们纷纷购置新的家具、地毯、无线电收音机等。食品店直到深夜还挤满了人。人们大量购买从齐治亚、亚美尼亚、阿塞拜疆和摩尔达维亚运来的水果和乌克兰的猪肉、腊肠，拉脱维亚的罐头食品，外高加索与中央亚细亚的各种名酒。所有莫斯科的三十二个市场生意都很好，每天都有莫斯科州的集体农民们在那里售卖数以百吨计的肉、菜蔬、鸡鸭、乳品等。
