Title: Xinhua Radio Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-11-21
Page: 4
　　Xinhua Radio Tonight's Highlights
    [News from this newspaper] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station, tonight (21st) 18:15 Women's Program: "Introducing the female workers in the state-owned enterprises of Andong City". 19:00 Natural Science Knowledge Lecture: The author is Zhai Yusheng from the Department of Geology, National Peking University, and the topic is "Talking about Mercury". 18:30 First Literary Program: Broadcasting "Workers' Songs". 22:00 Second Literary Program: The Inner Mongolia Art Troupe will perform "Mongolian Music". In addition, the station's special broadcast program on the Asian Women's Representatives Conference, week three, will begin on the 23rd, with the time remaining from 19:15 to 19:30 every day. On the 23rd, Simonne Bertrand, member of the Colonial Countries Women's Committee of the International Democratic Women's Federation, Director of the French Women's Council, and member of the Preparatory Committee of the Asian Women's Representatives Conference, will broadcast in French, with the topic: "Democratic women in imperialist countries fight for the oppressed people of Asia". On the 24th, the full text of this speech will be rebroadcast in Mandarin, on the 25th in Cantonese, on the 26th in English, and on the 27th in Japanese.
    [Also News] The Second Station, 17:45 Workers' Time: Comprehensive report on the enthusiastic welcome of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference held in Beijing by the workers of this city. 20:00, broadcast the important documents and resolutions of the Second Session of the People's Representatives Conference of All Circles in Beijing.

### 新华电台今晚要目

1949-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　新华电台今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台，今（二十一）日十八点十五分妇女节目：“介绍安东市省营企业中的女工们”。十九点自然科学常识讲座：撰稿人是国立北京大学地质系翟裕生，题目是“谈水银”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目：播送“工人歌曲”。二十二点第二次文艺节目，由内蒙古文艺工作团演播“蒙古音乐”。另外，该台关于亚洲妇女代表会议专题广播节目第三周，从二十三日开始，时间仍为每天十九点十五分到三十分。二十三日由国际民主妇联殖民地国家妇女委员会委员、法国妇女理事会理事、亚洲妇女代表会议筹备委员西门贝特朗用法语播讲，题目是：“帝国主义国家的民主妇女向压迫亚洲的人们作斗争”。二十四日用国语重播这个讲词的全文，二十五日用广州话，二十六日用英语，二十七日用日语重播。
    【又讯】第二台十七时四十五分职工时间：本市工人热烈欢迎亚澳工会会议在北京召开的综合报导。二十时，播送北京市第二届各界人民代表会议的重要文件和决议。
