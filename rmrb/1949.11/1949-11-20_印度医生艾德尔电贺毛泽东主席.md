Title: Indian Doctor Atal Congratulates Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1949-11-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 19th: Indian doctor Atal (also known as Andre Hua), who served in our liberated areas with a medical team during the War of Resistance Against Japan, sent a telegram from Bombay to congratulate Chairman Mao. The telegram reads:

To Chairman Mao Zedong, Beijing:

Your glorious victory will not only change the face of China but also the face of the entire world. My medical team and I extend our celebration and congratulations to you.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Atal, November 1st

### 印度医生艾德尔电贺毛泽东主席

1949-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度医生艾德尔电贺毛泽东主席
    【新华社北京十九日电】抗战期间曾偕医疗队来我解放区服务的印度医生艾德尔（即安德华），自孟买电贺毛主席，电文如下：北京毛泽东主席：
    你的光荣胜利，不仅要改变中国的面目，连整个世界的面目都要改变。我和我的医疗队向你敬致欢庆与祝贺。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　艾德尔　十一月一日
