Title: Xinhua Radio Tonight's Program

Author: 
Date: 1949-11-29
Page: 3

　　Xinhua Radio Tonight's Program
　　[This newspaper dispatch] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast the following tonight (29th): 18:15, Workers' Time: Broadcast "Wage Reduction at China Textile Factory No. 9". 19:00, Natural Science Knowledge Lecture, title: "Male Reproductive Function", written by Cai Qiao, Director of the Physiology Department of the Medical College of Nanjing University. 19:15, English broadcast of the full text of the speech given yesterday (28th) by Vietnamese women's representative Ho Duc Minh on the station, titled: "The Armed Struggle of Vietnamese Women is a Struggle for Peace". 18:30, First Literary Program: New Quyi: Leting Drum "Lanqiao Hate". 22:00, Second Literary Program: Mongolian song: Recording sent by the Mongolian delegation to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
　　[Another dispatch] Second Station, 17:15, Women's Time: Broadcast an excerpt of Zhang Xiaomei's report on "Summary of Women's Work in Beijing Since Liberation and Future Work Guidelines". 17:45, Youth Time: Zhang Ding from the Affiliated Women's Middle School of the Normal University and Sun Molin from Beiman Women's Middle School will talk about their thoughts on participating in the Second All-Circles People's Representative Conference. 18:00, New Song Practice: Continue to teach "Railway Workers' Song". 20:00, Voice of the City: Broadcast the "Notice from the Beijing Municipal People's Government Taxation Bureau on the Upcoming Unified Collection of Income Tax on Profit-Making Enterprises and Summer Business Tax for the First Half of 1949". 20:15, Listener Service: The Beijing Municipal Library "introduces the purchasing and cataloging work of the Beijing Municipal Library."

### 新华电台今晚要目

1949-11-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　新华电台今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今（二十九）晚十八点十五分职工时间：播送“中纺九厂的减低工资”。十九点自然科学常识讲座题目是：“男性生殖作用”，撰稿人是南京大学医学院生理系主任蔡翘。十九点十五分英语播送越南妇女代表胡德明昨（二十八）天在该台广播演讲的全文，题目是：“越南妇女的武装斗争是为和平而斗争”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目：新曲艺：乐亭大鼓“蓝桥恨”。二十二点第二次文艺节目：蒙古歌曲：播亚澳工会会议蒙古代表团送给中华全国总工会的录音片。
    【又讯】第二台十七点十五分妇女时间：播送张晓梅关于“北京市解放以来妇女工作总结及今后工作方针”报告的摘要。十七点四十五分青年时间：由师大女附中张鼎、贝满女中孙墨林谈她们参加第二届各界人民代表会议的感想。十八点新歌练习：继续教唱“铁路工人歌”。二十点市政之声：播送北京市人民政府税务局“将要开始统一征收一九四九年上半年营利事业所得税和夏季营业税的通知”。二十点十五分听众服务：由北京市立图书馆“介绍北京市立图书馆的采购和编类工作。”
