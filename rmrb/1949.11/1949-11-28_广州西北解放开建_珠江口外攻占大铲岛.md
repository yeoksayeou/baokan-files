Title: Northwest Guangzhou Liberated and Being Developed; Dachan Island Captured Outside the Pearl River Estuary
Author:
Date: 1949-11-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, South China Front, 26th: A delayed report: A unit of the People's Liberation Army on the Guangdong front liberated the county seat of Kaijian on the border of Guangdong and Guangxi provinces on the 5th of this month. A unit of local bandit forces surrendered to us. On the 6th, the Liberation Army also annihilated the bandit "Anti-Communist National Salvation Army" of Luoding County in Fengchuan County, capturing over a hundred bandits including the bandit division commander Mo Man. In the above two battles, our army captured a total of nine machine guns and 230 rifles and short guns.
　　Xinhua News Agency, South China Front, 26th: (Delayed Report) Dachan Island, belonging to Bao'an County, Guangdong Province, outside the Pearl River Estuary, was liberated on the 16th. A unit of the Liberation Army landed on the island on the 16th and quickly eliminated the remaining bandits on the island. A total of 170 officers and soldiers, including Xu Da, a major general and senior staff officer of the Bandit 21st Army Headquarters, were captured. One heavy machine gun, one light machine gun, fifty rifles, twenty short guns, one grenade launcher, seven wooden boats, and a quantity of gasoline and other materials were seized.
　　Xinhua News Agency, South China Front, 26th: A certain unit of the Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, in the bandit suppression battles in Zengcheng, Foshan, Conghua and other areas of Guangdong from October 21st to this month's 21st, annihilated a total of 21 groups of fleeing Kuomintang troops and local bandits. They captured alive Deng E, commander of the 1st Column of the Yuezhong Garrison District, Zhou Huaiguo, chief of staff, Yang Yan, commander of the Guangzhou Security Column, Huang Chaozao, battalion commander, and 2,225 other officers and soldiers; they seized 10 artillery pieces, 32 grenade launchers, 10 heavy machine guns, 93 light machine guns, 24 submachine guns, 4,414 rifles and short guns, and a quantity of other military supplies.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 26th: The People's Liberation Army garrisoning Guangzhou discovered a large number of weapons hidden by the Kuomintang bandit army when they fled Guangzhou in Hengsha, 30 miles southeast of Guangzhou, on the 17th of this month. The inventory includes: 30 artillery pieces, 11 anti-aircraft machine guns, 2 anti-tank guns, 3 82mm mortars, 2 flat trajectory guns, 2 infantry guns, 1 heavy machine gun, 8 light machine guns, 88 rifles, and a quantity of other ammunition and supplies.

### 广州西北解放开建　珠江口外攻占大铲岛

1949-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　广州西北解放开建
  　珠江口外攻占大铲岛
    【新华社华南前线二十六日电】迟到消息：广东前线解放军一部，于本月五日解放粤桂边境的开建县城。匪地方武装一部向我投诚。六日解放军又于封川县境消灭匪罗定县“反共救国军”，俘匪师长莫满以下百余人。以上两次战斗中，我军共缴机枪九挺、长短枪二百三十支。
    【新华社华南前线二十六日电】（迟到消息）珠江口外广东宝安县属之大铲岛已于十六日解放。解放军一部于十六日登陆该岛，迅速肃清该岛残匪。共计俘匪二十一兵团部少将高级参谋徐达等以下官兵一百七十人，缴获重机枪一挺、轻机枪一挺、步枪五十支、短枪二十支、掷弹筒一个、木船七艘、汽油等物资一部。
    【新华社华南前线二十六日电】人民解放军第四野战军某部，在十月二十一日至本月二十一日的一个月中，在广东增城、佛山、从化等地区的剿匪战斗中，共歼灭国民党军逃匪及地方土匪二十一股，生俘匪粤中师管区第一纵队司令邓鄂、参谋长周怀国，匪广州保安纵队总队长杨衍再、大队长黄潮藻等以下官兵二二二五名；缴获机关炮十门、掷弹筒三十二个，重机枪十挺，轻机枪九十三挺，冲锋枪二十四支，长短枪四四一四支及其他军用物资一部。
    【新华社广州二十六日电】警备广州的人民解放军本月十七日在广州东南三十里的横沙搜获国民党匪军由广州逃窜时隐藏的大批武器，计有：机关炮三十门，高射机关炮十一门，战防炮二门，八二迫击炮三门，平射炮二门，步兵炮二门，重机枪一挺，轻机枪八挺，步枪八十八支，其他弹药物资一部。
