Title: International Wood and Construction Industry Trade Department Congratulates the Chinese Working Class; Vietnamese Women's Federation Sends Telegram to Congratulate Chinese Women on Participating in the Government; All-China Women's Federation Chairman Cai Chang Sends Reply of Thanks
Author:
Date: 1949-11-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th: This office has recently received a letter from the General Secretary of the Executive Bureau of the International Wood, Construction Industry and Building Materials Industry Trade Department, affiliated with the World Federation of Trade Unions, from Helsinki, the capital of Finland, congratulating the Chinese workers and Chinese trade union organizations. It also congratulated the Chinese people and the Chinese people's army and its leader, Chairman Mao Zedong, on the victorious establishment of the People's Republic of China. The Executive Bureau respectfully wishes the Chinese people great success in the process of building a new China, which has now become a powerful and decisive force in the world's democratic and peaceful camp.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th: Le Thi Xuyen, President of the Vietnam Women's Union, sent a telegram to Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, congratulating the Chinese women representatives on participating in the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. Cai Chang also sent a reply of thanks. Le Thi Xuyen's congratulatory telegram said: "We are delighted to hear that two representatives from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation have participated in the newly established Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The Vietnam Women's Union, on behalf of all Vietnamese women, extends our congratulations. The participation of Chinese women representatives in this government, which was established after a long and arduous struggle to defeat the reactionaries, is a glorious victory for Chinese women. In the common cause of realizing world peace and democracy, Vietnamese women will always stand with Chinese women." Cai Chang's reply said: "I represent all members of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation in thanking you for your congratulations. And I extend my respects to the heroic and fighting Vietnamese sisters! Let us unite closely and strive for world peace."

### 国际木材、建筑业工会部　向中国工人阶级祝贺  越妇联电贺中国妇女参加政府我全国妇联蔡畅主席复电致谢

1949-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际木材、建筑业工会部
  　向中国工人阶级祝贺
    越妇联电贺中国妇女参加政府我全国妇联蔡畅主席复电致谢
    【新华社布拉格四日电】此间顷收到世界工会联合会所属国际木材、建筑工业及建筑材料业工会部执行局总书记自芬兰京城赫尔辛基来函，祝贺中国工人和中国工会组织，并向胜利地创立中华人民共和国的中国人民和中国人民的军队及其领袖毛泽东主席致贺称：执行局敬祝中国人民在建设新中国过程中的绝大成功，这个新中国现在已经成为世界民主和平阵营的强大和有决定性的生力军。
    【新华社北京五日电】越南妇女联合会会长黎化钏，致电中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，祝贺中国妇女代表参加中华人民共和国中央政府。蔡畅亦复电致谢。黎化钏的贺电说：“欣闻中华全国民主妇女联合会有两位代表参加了新成立的中华人民共和国中央政府，越南妇联代表越南全体妇女谨致贺意。中国妇女代表参加在这个经过长期艰苦奋斗，打败反动派之后，才获得成立的政府中，是中国妇女的光荣胜利。在为实现世界和平民主的共同事业中，越南妇女永远和中国妇女站在一起。”蔡畅的复电说：“仅代表中华全国民主妇女联合会全体会员感谢你们的祝贺。并向英勇奋斗的越南姊妹们致敬！让我们紧密团结，争取世界和平。”
