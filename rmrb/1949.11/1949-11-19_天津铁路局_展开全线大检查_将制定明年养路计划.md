Title: Tianjin Railway Bureau Launches Full-Line Inspection; Will Formulate Next Year's Track Maintenance Plan
Author: Liu Lin
Date: 1949-11-19
Page: 2

Tianjin Railway Bureau
Launches Full-Line Inspection
Will Formulate Next Year's Track Maintenance Plan

[Our Newspaper's Report] The Tianjin Railway Bureau, in order to summarize the work of 1949 and formulate the track maintenance plan for 1950, the Bureau Affairs Meeting decided that inspection teams from various departments of the bureau would be dispatched to the field to carry out a comprehensive inspection of the entire line. Before departure, the inspection teams should also formulate inspection outlines. After conducting detailed inspections with a focus on key points at each site, each department will convene a full-line meeting separately, and the Railway Bureau will convene a full-line commendation and award conference to summarize this year's work and convey and deploy the construction guidelines and work plans for 1950. Director Guo pointed out at the meeting that implementing a full-line comprehensive inspection and regularly convening commendation and award conferences is very important work. We must inspect good and bad examples, work progress, and the implementation of policies and guidelines from the bottom up, and link them to the formulation of next year's work plan and guidelines, in order to identify next year's direction of struggle.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Liu Lin)

### 天津铁路局  展开全线大检查  将制定明年养路计划
柳林
1949-11-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　天津铁路局
    展开全线大检查
    将制定明年养路计划
    【本报讯】天津铁路局为总结一九四九年工作，制定一九五○年养路计划，局务会议决定路局各处派出检查组分赴现场，展开全路总检查工作。检查组在出发前并应制定检查提纲，在各现场进行有重点的详细检查后，由各处分别召开全线会议，路局召开全路评功发奖大会，以总结今年工作，传达布置一九五○年建设方针、工作计划。郭局长在会议上指出，实行全线总检查，并定期召开庆功给奖大会，是很重要的工作，我们要由下而上的检查好坏典型，工作进度，对政策方针的执行，并联系规定明年工作计划方针，以便找出明年奋斗方针。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（柳林）
