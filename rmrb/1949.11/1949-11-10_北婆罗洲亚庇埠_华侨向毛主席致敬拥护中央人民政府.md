Title: Overseas Chinese in Kota Kinabalu, North Borneo, Salute Chairman Mao and Support the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1949-11-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th: Overseas Chinese in Kota Kinabalu, North Borneo, the Kota Kinabalu Chung Hwa School, and the Kota Kinabalu Students' Association sent a telegram to Chairman Mao, expressing their enthusiastic support for the Central People's Government. The telegram reads as follows:

To Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing: We are delighted to hear of the establishment of the Central People's Government of the Motherland. We pledge our enthusiastic support and extend our highest respects.

Respectfully,
One hundred and twenty-six Overseas Chinese in Kota Kinabalu, British North Borneo, Kota Kinabalu Chung Hwa School, and Kota Kinabalu Students' Association

October 12th

### 北婆罗洲亚庇埠　华侨向毛主席致敬拥护中央人民政府

1949-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　北婆罗洲亚庇埠
  　华侨向毛主席致敬拥护中央人民政府
    【新华社北京八日电】北婆罗洲亚庇埠华侨、亚庇中华学校、亚庇学友会致电毛主席，表示热诚拥护中央人民政府。电文如下：
    北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛主席勋鉴：欢悉祖国中央人民政府成立，我们誓以热诚拥护，并谨致崇高敬礼。
    　英属北婆罗洲亚庇埠华侨一百二十六名、亚庇中华学校、亚庇学友会同叩
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十月十二日
