Title: Nearly 50,000 Tons of Grain Arrive in Beijing-Tianjin Area
Author:
Date: 1949-11-26
Page: 1

　　Nearly 50,000 Tons of Grain Arrive in Beijing-Tianjin Area
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 25th] From the 16th to the 24th of this month, within eight days, the amount of grain transported into the interior via the Beining Railway reached 1,319 train cars, approximately 39,570 tons. The amount of grain transported into the Beijing-Tianjin area via the Beijing-Suiyuan Railway reached 290 train cars, approximately 8,700 tons. The total for both lines is 48,270 tons. The majority was sorghum rice and millet, followed by rice and wheat.

### 近五万吨粮食运抵京津地区

1949-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　近五万吨粮食运抵京津地区
    【新华社北京二十五日电】自本月十六日至二十四日八天内，经由北宁线运入关内的粮食达一千三百一十九辆车，约合三万九千五百七十吨；经由京绥线运入京津地区的粮食达二百九十辆车，约合八千七百吨。两项共计四万八千二百七十吨。其中以高粱米和小米为主，大米、小麦次之。
