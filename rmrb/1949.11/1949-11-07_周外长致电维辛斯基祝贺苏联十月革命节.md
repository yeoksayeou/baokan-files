Title: Foreign Minister Zhou Sends Telegram to Vyshinsky to Congratulate Soviet October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1949-11-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 6th: Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government sent a telegram to Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky on the 5th, congratulating him on the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution. The telegram reads as follows: To His Excellency Mr. Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

　　On the occasion of the 32nd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution in your country, I would like to extend my heartfelt congratulations to the government and people of your country. Since the October Revolution, the Soviet government and people have always been the most sincere and reliable friends of the Chinese people. I believe that in the future, in the common struggle for lasting world peace and people's democracy, the friendship between China and the Soviet Union will be further consolidated and developed.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Beijing, November 5, 1949

### 周外长致电维辛斯基祝贺苏联十月革命节

1949-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　周外长致电维辛斯基祝贺苏联十月革命节
    【新华社北京六日电】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长五日致电苏联外交部部长维辛斯基，祝贺十月革命三十二周年。电文如下：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟外交部部长维辛斯基先生阁下：
    欣逢贵国伟大的十月社会主义革命节第三十二周年，我谨向贵国政府及人民致衷心的祝贺。十月革命以来，苏联政府及人民始终是中国人民的最忠诚可靠的朋友。我相信，今后在共同为世界持久和平与人民民主的斗争中，中苏两国之友谊将更加巩固和发展。
    　　　　　　　　中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十一月五日于北京
