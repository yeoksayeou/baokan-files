Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts Banquet for Personnel of Two Aviation Companies, Calls for Adherence to Self-Reliance
Author:
Date: 1949-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government's State Council hosted a banquet this afternoon for all the personnel of the China National Aviation Corporation (CNAC) and the Central Air Transport Corporation (CATC) who had returned to the mainland after their uprising, and delivered a speech. Those attending the banquet included Liu Jingyi, General Manager of CNAC; Chen Zhuolin, General Manager of CATC; Cha Yiping, former Deputy General Manager of CATC; as well as pilots, radio operators, managers, and technicians from both companies, totaling forty people. Accompanying them were Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Zhang Bojun, Minister of Communications; Li Kenong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Liu Yalou, Commander of the People's Liberation Army Air Force.

In his speech, Premier Zhou expressed his warmest welcome to the staff of the two companies for their uprising and return.

Premier Zhou said: "We welcome the uprising of the two companies because this is the starting point of the infinitely promising Chinese People's Civil Aviation industry." Premier Zhou called on all personnel of the two companies to work hard to overcome the difficulties in their path forward, adhere to self-reliance, and not harbor any illusions about the imperialists.

After Premier Zhou's speech, Liu Jingyi and Chen Zhuolin, General Managers of the two companies, and Zhang Yi, Captain of CNAC, spoke successively, expressing their determination to follow Premier Zhou's instructions and serve the people diligently under the leadership of Chairman Mao. Finally, Minister of Communications Zhang Bojun spoke, encouraging all personnel of the two companies, based on his own experience, to firmly trust in the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Communist Party, and hoping that everyone would humbly learn the Communist Party's theories, ideology, and working methods.

### 周恩来总理　欢宴两航空公司人员　号召坚持自力更生

1949-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理
  　欢宴两航空公司人员
  　号召坚持自力更生
    【新华社北京十五日电】中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来今午欢宴到京的中国航空公司和中央航空公司的全体起义人员，并发表讲话。出席欢宴者有中国航空公司总经理刘敬宜、中央航空公司总经理陈卓林、中央航空公司前任副总经理查夷平、和两公司的驾驶人员、电台人员、经理人员和技工等共四十人，出席作陪者有人民革命军事委员会代理总参谋长聂荣臻、交通部长章伯钧、外交部副部长李克农、人民解放军空军司令刘亚楼。
    周总理在讲话中对两公司员工的起义归来，表示无限欢迎。
    周总理说：我们欢迎两公司的起义，因为这是具有无限前途的中国人民民航事业的起点。周总理号召两公司的全体人员努力克服前进途中的困难，坚持自力更生，而不要对帝国主义者存任何幻想。
    周总理讲话后，两公司总经理刘敬宜、陈卓林、中国航空公司正驾驶张镒相继讲话，表示一定遵照周总理的指示，在毛主席的领导下努力为人民服务。最后交通部部长章伯钧讲话，他以他自己的经验，勉励两公司全体人员坚决信赖毛主席和共产党的领导，并希望大家虚心学习共产党的理论、思想和工作方法。
