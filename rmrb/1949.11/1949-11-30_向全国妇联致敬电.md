Title: Telegram of Respect to the All-China Women's Federation
Author:
Date: 1949-11-30
Page: 1

To Chairperson Cai Chang, Vice-Chairpersons Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, and Xu Guangping of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation: From November 26th to 29th, on the eve of the Asian Women's Representative Conference, we, 371 representatives elected from all walks of life in the capital, held the first women's representative conference in history, summarized the women's work in the nine months since liberation, and determined the future work guidelines and specific tasks. We elected the first executive committee and formally established the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation. We unanimously believe that our bright and happy life today is entirely due to the victory of the people's democratic revolution led by Chairman Mao; and our work has achieved preliminary results today because of the correct resolutions of the All-China Women's Congress this spring and your constant leadership and care. Therefore, we would like to extend our highest and cordial respect to you. In the future, we will continue to implement the resolutions of the All-China Women's Congress, work hard, create experience, and unite the sisters of Beijing more widely and deeply, and strive for the great construction of the new China, the new Beijing, and the cause of our own liberation. With respect, and wishing you good health.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Beijing First Women's Representative Conference
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 29th

### 向全国妇联致敬电

1949-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　向全国妇联致敬电
    全国民主妇联蔡畅主席，邓颖超、李德全、许广平副主席：十一月二十六日——二十九日，正当亚洲妇代会的前夕，我们三百七十一名从首都各阶层各行业选举出来的代表举行了有史以来第一次妇女代表大会，总结了解放九个月来的妇女工作，确定了今后的工作方针和具体任务。选举了第一届执行委员，正式成立了北京市民主妇女联合会。我们一致认为，我们所以有今天的光明幸福的生活，完全是由于毛主席领导的人民民主革命已取得了胜利；而我们的工作所以有今天的初步成绩，则是由于有了今春全国妇代大会的正确的决议和你们的经常领导与关怀。为此，我们谨向你们致崇高的亲切的敬礼。今后，我们将继续贯彻全国妇代大会的决议，努力工作、创造经验，更广泛更深入地团结北京的姊妹们，为新中国、新北京的伟大建设和我们自身的解放事业而奋斗。此致敬礼，并祝健康
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　北京市第一次妇女代表大会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月二十九日
