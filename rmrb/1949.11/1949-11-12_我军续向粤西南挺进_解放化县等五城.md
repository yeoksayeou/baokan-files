Title: Our Army Continues to Advance into Southwestern Guangdong, Liberating Five Cities Including Hua County
Author:
Date: 1949-11-12
Page: 1

    Our Army Continues to Advance into Southwestern Guangdong
    Liberating Five Cities Including Hua County
    [Xinhua News Agency, South China Front, November 11th] Belated News: A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army continues to advance into the southwestern region of Guangdong Province, liberating the two county seats of Hua County and Maoming on the 2nd of this month, Xinyi on the 3rd, and Lianjiang on the 4th. Another unit of the People's Liberation Army liberated the county seat of Deqing on the 3rd.

### 我军续向粤西南挺进  解放化县等五城

1949-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我军续向粤西南挺进
    解放化县等五城
    【新华社华南前线十一日电】迟到消息：人民解放军某部继续向广东西南地区挺进，于本月二日解放化县、茂名两座县城，三日解放信宜，四日解放濂江。解放军另部于三日解放德庆县城。
