Title: General Managers of China National Aviation Corporation and Central Air Transport Corporation Take Office; Send Telegrams Encouraging Employees in All Regions to Uphold Patriotic Stance
Author:
Date: 1949-11-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th: General Manager Liu Jingyi of China National Aviation Corporation and General Manager Chen Zhuolin of Central Air Transport Corporation took office in Beijing yesterday after receiving appointments from the State Council of the Central People's Government. The two General Managers, Liu and Chen, sent a letter to Premier Zhou Enlai on the same day reporting their assumption of office. The full text of the letter is as follows:

To Premier Zhou of the State Council:

Respectfully, Jingyi and Zhuolin, in compliance with the appointment of November 12th, have taken office in Beijing today and notified all staff at all stations under our jurisdiction. From now on, under the leadership of the great Chairman Mao, we will humbly learn and work hard to build the civil aviation industry of the new China. We also request guidance from leaders at all levels and all advanced comrades at any time.

　　　　　　　　　　General Manager of China National Aviation Corporation, Liu Jingyi
　　　　　　　　　　General Manager of Central Air Transport Corporation, Chen Zhuolin
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 14, 1949

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th: General Manager Liu Jingyi of China National Aviation Corporation and General Manager Chen Zhuolin of Central Air Transport Corporation yesterday sent a telegram to employees of the two companies in various regions, encouraging them to adhere to their posts, protect assets, and await further instructions from the Central People's Government. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To all staff at all stations of China National Aviation Corporation and Central Air Transport Corporation in the liberated areas of the People's Republic of China, the Hong Kong region, and the areas awaiting liberation: Jingyi and Zhuolin, in compliance with the appointment of November 12th by Premier Zhou of the State Council of the Central People's Government, hereby announce our assumption of office in Beijing, the capital of the people, by telegram today. We are following the government's orders to plan for resumption and opening of air routes. We hope that all staff stationed in Hong Kong and areas awaiting liberation will unite, heighten vigilance, thwart the conspiracies of the reactionaries, be responsible for protecting all assets, and await further instructions; and we hope that you will adhere to a patriotic stance, strive for progress, and work together under the leadership of the great Chairman Mao to build the people's aviation industry of the new China.

　　　　　　　　　　　General Manager of China National Aviation Corporation, Liu Jingyi
　　　　　　　　　　　General Manager of Central Air Transport Corporation, Chen Zhuolin
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 14, 1949

### 中航央航两总经理就职　电勉各地员工坚守爱国立场

1949-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　中航央航两总经理就职
  　电勉各地员工坚守爱国立场
    【新华社北京十五日电】中国航空公司总经理刘敬宜、中央航空公司总经理陈卓林自接奉中央人民政府政务院任命后，已于昨日在京就职。刘、陈两总经理曾于当日致函周恩来总理报告就职。该函全文如下：政务院周总理钧鉴：
    敬宜、卓林遵奉十一月十二日的任命谨于本日在京就职，并通知了所属各站台的全体员工们。今后我们一定要在伟大的毛主席领导之下，为建设新中国的民航事业而虚心学习，努力工作。并请各级主管长官和一切先进同志们，随时指导我们。
    　　　　　　　　　　中国航空公司总经理　　刘敬宜
    　　　　　　　　　　中央航空公司总经理　　陈卓林
    　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十一月十四日
    【新华社北京十五日电】中国航空公司总经理刘敬宜、中央航空公司总经理陈卓林昨日通电各地该两公司员工，勉励他们谨守岗位，保护资产听候中央人民政府进一步指示。该电全文如下：
    中华人民共和国解放区、香港地区、及待解放区中国航空公司中央航空公司的各站台全体员工们：敬宜、卓林遵奉中央人民政府政务院周总理十一月十二日的任命，谨于本日在人民的首都——北京通电就职，我们正秉从着政府的命令筹划复员和通航的工作，希留驻香港及待解放地区的全体员工，团结一致，提高警惕，粉碎反动派的阴谋，负责保护一切资产，听候进一步的指示；并盼坚持爱国立场努力进步，同心合力，在伟大的毛主席领导之下，为建设新中国人民航空事业而奋斗。
    　　　　　　　　　　　中国航空公司总经理　　刘敬宜
    　　　　　　　　　　　中央航空公司总经理　　陈卓林
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十一月十四日
