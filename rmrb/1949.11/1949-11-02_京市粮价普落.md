Title: Beijing Grain Prices Generally Fall
Author:
Date: 1949-11-02
Page: 2

　　Beijing Grain Prices Generally Fall
    [Beijing News] Grain prices in Beijing generally fell yesterday. The rise in grain prices that began on the 28th of last month has begun to stabilize. Another sixteen train cars of grain from the Northeast successively arrived in Beijing yesterday (the 1st). At the same time, a large quantity of goods was shipped from Shanghai, totaling 1,437 large boxes.

### 京市粮价普落

1949-11-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　京市粮价普落
    【北京讯】京市粮价昨日普落，自上月二十八日开始之粮价涨风已趋平稳，东北粮食昨（一）日又有十六车陆续运抵北京。同时，并自上海运来大批百货，计一千四百三十七大箱。
