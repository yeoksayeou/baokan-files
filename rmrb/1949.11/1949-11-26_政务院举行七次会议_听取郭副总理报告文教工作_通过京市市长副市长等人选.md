Title: Government Administration Council Holds Seventh Meeting; Premier Guo Reports on Cultural and Educational Work; Approves Candidates for Mayor and Deputy Mayors of Beijing

Author:
Date: 1949-11-26
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 25th: The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its seventh meeting at 5:00 PM today. It listened to Vice Premier Guo Moruo's report on the work of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee, and approved the candidates for Mayor, Deputy Mayors, and committee members of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, elected by the Second Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference, to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for appointment.

### 政务院举行七次会议　听取郭副总理报告文教工作  通过京市市长副市长等人选

1949-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行七次会议
  　听取郭副总理报告文教工作
    通过京市市长副市长等人选
    【新华社北京二十五日电】中央人民政府政务院今天下午五时举行第七次政务会议，听取郭沫若副总理关于政务院文化教育委员会工作的报告，并通过了北京市第二届各界人民代表会议选出的北京市人民政府市长、副市长和委员人选，提请中央人民政府委员会任命。
