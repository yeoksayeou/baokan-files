Title: First Batch of Disease Prevention Films Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-11-02
Page: 4

　　First Batch of Disease Prevention Films Arrives in Beijing
    [Beijing News] The first batch of new copies of disease prevention films for screening in various parts of North China arrived in Beijing yesterday from the Northeast. In order to strengthen disease prevention publicity, the Central Disease Prevention Committee has entrusted the North China Film Company (85 Beichizi, Beijing) and its Tianjin office to handle the screening matters for various agencies, organizations, schools, and enterprises in Beijing and Tianjin.

### 防疫影片首批抵京

1949-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　防疫影片首批抵京
    【北京讯】供应华北各地放映的防疫影片新拷贝，第一批昨已由东北运抵北京。为加强防疫宣传，中央防疫委员会已委托华北影片公司（北京北池子八十五号）及该公司天津办事处办理京津两地各机关、团体、学校、企业等放映事宜。
