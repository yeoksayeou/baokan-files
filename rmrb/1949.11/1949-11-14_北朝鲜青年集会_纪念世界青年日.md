Title: North Korean Youth Gather to Commemorate World Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1949-11-14
Page: 3

    North Korean Youth Gather
    to Commemorate World Youth Day
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 13th] North Korean youth in various regions held widespread gatherings on the 9th and 10th to commemorate World Youth Day. The meeting in Pyongyang was held at the Mudanfeng Theater. More than 800 youths from all walks of life, mainly labor youth, participated. The conference began with a report by Hyun Jung-min, Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League. He elaborated on the struggle of democratic youth around the world for lasting world peace and democratic freedom, as well as the tasks that Korean youth should undertake to commemorate World Youth Day. The conference also adopted congratulatory telegrams to Boesson, Chairman of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and Voinova, Chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee.

### 北朝鲜青年集会  纪念世界青年日

1949-11-14
第3版()
专栏：

    北朝鲜青年集会
    纪念世界青年日
    【新华社平壤十三日电】北朝鲜各地青年于九、十两日普遍集会纪念世界青年日。平壤的会议在牡丹峰剧场举行。参加者有以劳动青年为主体的各界青年八百余人。大会首由北朝鲜民主青年中央委员会主席玄正民作报告。他阐述了全世界民主青年为世界的持久和平及民主自由所进行的斗争，以及朝鲜青年纪念世界青年日应有的任务。大会并通过致世界民主青年联盟主席鲍埃逊和苏联青年反法西斯委员会主席伏依诺娃的贺电。
