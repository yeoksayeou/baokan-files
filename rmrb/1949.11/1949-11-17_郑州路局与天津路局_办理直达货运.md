Title: Zhengzhou Railway Bureau and Tianjin Railway Bureau Handle Direct Freight Transport
Author:
Date: 1949-11-17
Page: 2

　　Zhengzhou Railway Bureau and Tianjin Railway Bureau
  　Handle Direct Freight Transport
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 16th] In order to strengthen the exchange of goods between regions, the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau has reached an agreement with the Tianjin Railway Bureau to handle direct freight transportation to and from all stations under the Tianjin Railway Bureau. Starting from the 11th of this month, freight from the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau will directly reach stations under the jurisdiction of the Tianjin Railway Bureau, including Beijing, Tianjin, Baoding, Tangshan, Shanhaiguan, Zhangjiakou, Datong, Gui Sui, and Baotou. In addition, direct freight transport is also being handled with Qingdao, Lianyungang, and Yuxikou in Anhui, which are under the jurisdiction of the Jinan Railway Bureau.

### 郑州路局与天津路局　办理直达货运

1949-11-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　郑州路局与天津路局
  　办理直达货运
    【新华社开封十六日电】为加强各地区间的物资交流，郑州铁路局已与天津路局洽妥，办理与天津路局各站间的货物直达运输。自本月十一日起，郑州路局的货运即直达津局辖内之北京、天津、保定、唐山、山海关、张家口、大同、归绥、包头等站。此外，并与济南路局所辖的青岛、连云港、和安徽裕溪口等站，办理直达货运。
