Title: Great October Revolution Anniversary: Gromyko Hosts Foreign Guests; Soviet Embassies in Various Countries Hold Receptions
Author:
Date: 1949-11-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 9th: TASS, Moscow: On the occasion of the 32nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Gromyko hosted a reception in Moscow for the diplomatic missions of various countries. Among those present at the reception were the heads and personnel of various embassies and legations in Moscow, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Choibalsan, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Heo Heon, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Luvsan, Maidar, and Sharav, Chairman of the Provisional People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Dieckmann, General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Saillant, Head of the Belgian Trade Delegation, Seynaeve, Director of the Swedish National Trade Commission, Modee, Chairman of the World Peace Congress Standing Committee, Joliot-Curie, President of the Latin American Labor Federation, Toledano, Secretary-General of the Belgian-Soviet Friendship Association, San Gil, and Secretary-General of the Italian-Soviet Association, Berti, Vice President of the French-Soviet Association, Grenier, and others.

Soviet attendees at the reception included Soviet Minister of Foreign Trade Menshikov, Minister of Railways Beshchev, Minister of Finance Zverev, Minister of Higher Education Kaftanov, Minister of Cinematography Bolshakov, Akopov, and Minister of Meat and Dairy Industry Smirnov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation Vlasov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Gorkin, Soviet Marshals Budyonny, Sokolovsky, and Govorov, Marshal of Artillery Voronov, Lieutenant General of the Air Force Zhavronkov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Lavrentiev, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union Kumykin and Serdechick, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions Kuznetsov, General of the Army Shtemenko, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences Vavilov, Academician Lysenko, senior officials of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade, and the Soviet Armed Forces, scientists, writers, artists, actors, and representatives of Soviet and foreign news media, etc.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 9th: Compiled from TASS reports: On the occasion of the 32nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, the Soviet Ambassadors, Ministers, Chargé d'Affaires, and Senior Commissioners stationed in Czechoslovakia, Poland, Bulgaria, Hungary, Romania, Albania, Mongolia, the United States, Britain, Finland, the Netherlands, Australia, Syria, and other countries, all held grand receptions. Heads of state, ministers, leaders of communist and workers' parties, diplomatic missions, and representatives from cultural, scientific, and mass organizations of various people's democratic countries all attended to offer congratulations. At the reception held at the Soviet Embassy in Washington, more than 1,400 guests attended, including prominent representatives from the American scientific and cultural circles, social celebrities, diplomatic missions from various countries, and many representatives from the business community. Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky also came from New York to participate.

### 伟大十月革命纪念日  葛罗米柯招待外宾　苏驻各国外交使节举行招待会

1949-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　伟大十月革命纪念日
    葛罗米柯招待外宾
  　苏驻各国外交使节举行招待会
    【新华社北京九日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年纪念日，苏联副外长葛罗米柯在莫斯科招待各国外交使团。出席招待会的，有驻莫斯科各大使馆与公使馆的首长与人员，蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席却伊巴桑，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民议会议长许宪，蒙古人民共和国部长会议副主席鲁布桑、梅达尔、沙拉普，德意志民主共和国临时人民议会议长狄克曼，世界工联总书记赛扬，比利时贸易代表团团长赛纳物，瑞典国家贸易委员会主任摩地，世界拥护和平大会常设委员会主席约里奥——居里，拉丁美洲劳工联合会主席托列达诺，比苏友好协会秘书长桑日，意苏协会秘书长贝尔蒂，法苏协会副会长格勒尼埃等。
    苏联出席招待会的，有苏联对外贸易部部长孟希科夫，铁道部部长别什切夫，财政部部长兹维列夫，高等教育部部长卡夫坦诺夫，电影事业部部长鲍尔沙科夫、阿科波夫和肉乳工业部部长斯米尔诺夫，俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席伏拉索夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金，苏联元帅布琼尼、索科洛夫斯基和戈伏罗夫，炮兵元帅伏罗诺夫，空军中将热沃隆科夫，苏联外交部副部长拉夫伦捷夫，苏联对外贸易部副部长库米金和塞尔杰奇克，全苏工会中央理事会主席库兹涅佐夫，陆军上将施蒂门柯、苏联科学院主席瓦维洛夫，科学院院士李森科，苏联外交部、苏联对外贸易部、苏联武装部队部的高级官员，科学家、作家、艺术家、演员以及苏联与外国新闻界的代表等。
    【新华社北京九日电】综合塔斯社消息：在伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年纪念日，苏联驻捷、波、保、匈、罗、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古、美、英、芬、荷、澳、叙利亚等国的大使、公使、代办及驻奥高级专员、均举行了盛大招待会。各人民民主国家的政府元首、部长、共产党与工人党的领袖、各外交使团、各国文化界科学界及群众团体的代表，都出席致贺。在华盛顿苏联大使馆的招待会中，到有来宾一千四百余人，其中有美国科学界、文化界的著名代表、社会名流、各国外交使团，及许多企业界的代表。苏外长维辛斯基亦自纽约前来参加。
