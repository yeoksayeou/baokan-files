Title: Jinghan Railway's Beijing-Shijiazhuang Section Officially Opened to Traffic Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-11-19
Page: 1
　
　　Jinghan Railway's Beijing-Shijiazhuang Section Officially Opened to Traffic Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th] The Beijing-Shijiazhuang section of the Jinghan Railway officially resumed traffic today. The first mixed train departed from Beijing at 6:20 this morning. The Beijing-Shijiazhuang section of the railway, which had been interrupted for more than three years, has now been restored. The Beijing-Shijiazhuang section is 276 kilometers long. After five months of dedicated repairs by all the staff of the First Engineering Office of the Fourth Detachment of the Railway Corps, it was completely repaired on November 3. Currently, there is one pair of ordinary passenger trains and one pair of mixed trains running between Beijing and Shijiazhuang daily. There is also one pair of mixed trains running between Beijing and Baoding.

### 京汉路京石段昨日正式通车

1949-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　京汉路京石段昨日正式通车
    【新华社北京十八日电】京汉铁路北京、石家庄段，已于今日正式恢复通车，首次混合列车已于今晨六时二十分自北京开出，中断了三年多的京、石段铁路，至此乃告恢复。京石段全长二百七十六公里，经铁道兵团第四支队第一工程处全体员工五个月来的努力抢修，卒于十一月三日全部修复。现京石段每日对开普通客车一对，混合列车一对。京保间对开混合列车一对。
