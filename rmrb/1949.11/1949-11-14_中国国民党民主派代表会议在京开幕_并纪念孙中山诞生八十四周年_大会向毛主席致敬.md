Title: Chinese Kuomintang Democratic Faction Holds Representative Conference in Beijing and Commemorates Sun Yat-sen's 84th Birthday; The Conference Pays Respects to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1949-11-14
Page: 1

    [This report]
    Chinese Kuomintang Democratic Faction Holds Representative Conference in Beijing
    and Commemorates Sun Yat-sen's 84th Birthday
    The Conference Pays Respects to Chairman Mao
    [This newspaper reports] The Chinese Kuomintang Democratic Faction held an opening ceremony in Beijing on November 12th, simultaneously commemorating Mr. Sun Yat-sen's 84th birthday. During the memorial meeting, Li Jishen presided over the ceremony, and He Xiangning reported on Mr. Sun's revolutionary history, encouraging comrades to carry forward Mr. Sun's revolutionary spirit and advance under the leadership of Chairman Mao. Subsequently, the conference passed a resolution to pay respects to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all commanders and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army, and to send a telegram of respect to Madam Sun Yat-sen, Soong Ching-ling.

### 中国国民党民主派代表会议在京开幕  并纪念孙中山诞生八十四周年  大会向毛主席致敬

1949-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中国国民党民主派代表会议在京开幕
    并纪念孙中山诞生八十四周年
    大会向毛主席致敬
    【本报讯】十一月十二日中国国民党民主派代表会议在京举行开幕典礼，同时举行孙中山先生诞生八十四周年纪念。在纪念会中，由李济深主持纪念仪式，由何香凝报告孙先生革命历史，勉励同志们发挥孙先生的革命精神，在毛主席领导下向前迈进。继由大会通过向毛主席朱总司令及人民解放军全体指战员致敬，并电孙夫人宋庆龄先生致敬。
