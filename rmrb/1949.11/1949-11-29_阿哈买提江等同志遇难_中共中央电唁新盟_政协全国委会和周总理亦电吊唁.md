Title: Comrade Ahemaitijiang and Others Die in Accident; CPC Central Committee Sends Condolences to New Alliance; CPPCC National Committee and Premier Zhou Also Send Condolences

Author:

Date: 1949-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy Defence Alliance to express condolences for Comrade Ahemaitijiang and others. The original telegram is as follows: To the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy Defence Alliance:

Comrade Ahemaitijiang, Chairman of the Central Committee of the New Alliance, Comrade Ishaq Beg, Member of the Central Organizing Committee of the New Alliance, Comrade Abudukelimu, Member of the Central Committee of the New Alliance and Director of the Intelligence Department of the New Alliance, Comrade Dalielhan, Member of the Central Committee of the New Alliance, and Comrade Luo Zhi, President of the Xinjiang Branch of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Association, have unfortunately died in an accident. This is a great loss to the revolutionary cause in Xinjiang and China. We express our deep condolences.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 28th

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy Defence Alliance, expressing condolences for the death of Ahemaitijiang and others, the first group of CPPCC representatives from Xinjiang. The telegram reads as follows: To the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy Defence Alliance:

Having read your obituary, we were shocked to learn of the deaths of five comrades, Ahemaitijiang, Ishaq Beg, Abudukelimu, Dalielhan, and Luo Zhi, the first group of CPPCC representatives from the people of Xinjiang. This is a great loss. This committee extends its deep condolences and firmly believes that you will carry on the legacy of the five comrades, Ahemaitijiang, and unite the people of Xinjiang to support the Central People's Government and strive to build a new democratic China.

  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 26th

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government today sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy Defence Alliance, expressing condolences for the death of Comrade Ahemaitijiang, Chairman of the Alliance and Vice Chairman of Xinjiang Province, and others. The telegram reads as follows: To the Comrades of the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy Defence Alliance:

Comrade Ahemaitijiang, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy Defence Alliance and Vice Chairman of Xinjiang Province, Comrade Ishaq Beg, Commander-in-Chief of the National Army in the Ili, Ashan, and Tacheng regions of Xinjiang Province, Comrade Abudukelimu, Member of the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy Defence Alliance, Comrade Dalielhan, Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the National Army, and Comrade Luo Zhi of the Xinjiang Sino-Soviet Cultural Association, were representing the people of Xinjiang at the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. Unfortunately, they died in a plane crash en route in September 1949. This is indeed a significant loss for the revolutionary cause of the people of Xinjiang and the people of China. I hereby send this telegram to express my deep condolences, and I especially hope that in our joint efforts, we will continue and carry forward the legacy of the martyrs.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 28, 1949

### 阿哈买提江等同志遇难　中共中央电唁新盟  政协全国委会和周总理亦电吊唁

1949-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　阿哈买提江等同志遇难
  　中共中央电唁新盟
    政协全国委会和周总理亦电吊唁
    【新华社北京二十八日电】中国共产党中央委员会顷致电新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员会吊唁阿哈买提江等同志。原电如下：新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员会：
    新盟中央委员会主席阿哈买提江同志，新盟中央组织委员会委员伊斯哈克伯克同志，新盟中央委员兼新盟情报处长阿不都克里木同志，新盟中央委员达列尔汉同志，中苏文化协会新疆分会会长罗志同志不幸遇难，是新疆和中国革命事业的巨大损失，谨致深沉的哀悼。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国共产党中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月二十八日
    【新华社北京廿八日电】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会电新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员会，吊唁新疆人民第一批政协代表阿哈买提江等的遇难，电文如下：新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员会礼鉴：
    阅贵会讣文，惊悉新疆人民第一批政协代表阿哈买提江、伊斯哈克伯克、阿不都克里木、达列尔汉、罗志等五同志遇难，这是一个巨大的损失，本会敬致沉痛的哀悼，深信贵会将继阿哈买提江等五同志的遗志，团结新疆人民拥护中央人民政府，为建设新民主主义的新中国而奋斗。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月二十六日
    【新华社北京二十八日电】中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理今日致电新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员会，吊唁该会主席新疆省副主席阿哈买提江同志等的遇难，电文如下：新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员会的同志们：
    新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员会主席新疆省副主席阿哈买提江同志，新疆省伊犁、阿山、塔城三区民族军总指挥伊斯哈克伯克同志，新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员阿不都克里木同志，民族军副总指挥达列尔汉同志和新疆中苏文化协会的罗志同志代表新疆人民出席中国人民政治协商会议，不幸竟于一九四九年九月因飞机失事，中途遇难牺牲。这对于新疆人民和中国人民的革命事业，实是一个重大的损失。谨此电唁，表示深切的哀悼，尤望在我们共同的努力中，继续并发扬先烈的遗志。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十一月二十八日
