Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Bureau Presents Banners to North China Special Forces, etc.
Author:
Date: 1949-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 29th: The Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) presented banners to the Special Forces of the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Beijing People's Public Security Forces on the 28th. Attending the ceremony were Louis Saillant, Secretary of the WFTU Executive Bureau; Kuznetzov, Vice-Chairman, representing Solovyov, Lerrrip, Torridano, Pinia, Liu Shaoqi, Abdullah, Broksal, and Subka; Li Chengrui, Political Commissar of the Special Forces; combat heroes from the Special Forces and the People's Public Security Forces; and representatives from various countries attending the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference, totaling over a hundred people. The banner presentation ceremony began at 6:00 PM, chaired by Saillant. He delivered a speech stating: "Today's banner presentation symbolizes the closer unity between the working class of the world and the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The Chinese People's Liberation Army is an armed force that defends world peace." Political Commissar Li Chengrui received the banners on behalf of the Special Forces and the People's Public Security Forces and delivered a reply, saying: "The Chinese People's Liberation Army is an army led by the Chinese working class. It has the glorious tradition of proletarian internationalism. We must also learn deeply from our international friends, promote the spirit of internationalism, and resolutely fight to defend the interests of the world's working class and people." Finally, Political Commissar Li introduced the combat heroes to the members of the WFTU Executive Bureau. When the leaders of the workers' movements from various countries shook hands with the combat heroes, the entire audience responded with enthusiastic applause.

### 世界工联执行局向华北特种兵部队等献旗

1949-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界工联执行局向华北特种兵部队等献旗
    【新华社北京二十九日电】世界工联执行局二十八日向中国人民解放军华北军区特种兵部队和北京市人民公安部队献旗。参加典礼的有世界工联执行局书记路易·赛扬，副主席库兹涅佐夫代表索洛维耶夫、勒里普、托里达诺、皮尼亚、刘少奇、阿勃杜拉、勃罗克萨尔、苏布卡，特种兵部队政治委员李呈瑞，特种兵部队和人民公安部队战斗英雄，以及亚澳工会代表会议各国代表等共百余人。六时整献旗典礼开始，由赛扬担任主席，他致词说：今天的献旗象征着全世界工人阶级和中国人民解放军的更紧密的团结。中国人民解放军是保卫世界和平的武装。李呈瑞政委代表特种兵部队和人民公安部队受旗并致答词，他说：中国人民解放军是中国工人阶级领导的军队，它有着工人阶级的国际主义的光荣传统，我们还要向国际友人深入学习，发扬国际主义精神，为保卫世界工人阶级和人民的利益而坚决奋斗。最后，由李政委向世界工联执行局各委员介绍战斗英雄。当各国工人运动领袖和战斗英雄握手时，全场报以热烈掌声。
