Title: Establishment of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Southern Jiangsu Branch
Author:
Date: 1949-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 2nd. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Southern Jiangsu Branch, was established on the 1st. More than 2,000 members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association from party, government, military, and people's organizations in the Southern Jiangsu region and Wuxi city attended the inaugural meeting that day. The meeting opened with the solemn national anthems of China and the Soviet Union. Guan Wenwei first reported on the significance of the establishment of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, followed by Li Fanying, a member of the Chinese workers' delegation attending the World Federation of Trade Unions Congress, who reported on the lofty friendship of the Soviet Union and the new democratic countries of Eastern Europe towards the Chinese people. The meeting elected Chen Pixian, Guan Wenwei and other thirty-seven people as directors of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Southern Jiangsu Branch, Liu Jiping as president, and Gu Feng, Chen Yicheng, Hong Shimin, Zhang Yunwen, Zhou Meichu, and Zhu Shujian as vice presidents.

### 中苏友好协会苏南分会成立

1949-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会苏南分会成立
    【新华社上海二日电】中苏友好协会苏南分会已于一日成立。是日参加成立大会的有苏南区及无锡市党、政、军机关及人民团体的中苏友好协会会员二千余人。大会在庄严的中苏两国国歌声中开幕，首由管文蔚报告中苏友好协会成立的意义，继由出席世界工联大会的中国工人代表团团员李凡英报告苏联和东欧新民主主义国家对中国人民的崇高友谊。大会选出陈丕显、管文蔚等三十七人为中苏友好协会苏南分会理事，刘季平为会长，顾风、陈一诚、洪式民、张允温、周美初、朱叔建等六人为副会长。
