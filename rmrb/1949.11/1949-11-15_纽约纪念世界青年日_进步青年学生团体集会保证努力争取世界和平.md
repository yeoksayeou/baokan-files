Title: New York Commemorates World Youth Day; Progressive Youth and Student Groups Gather to Pledge Efforts to Win World Peace
Author:
Date: 1949-11-15
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th: TASS, New York: To commemorate World Youth Day, more than a thousand representatives from progressive youth and student groups and labor unions in the United States held a mass rally in New York on the 11th of this month, pledging to wholeheartedly redouble their efforts to fight for world peace. They warmly welcomed the American delegation returning from the Budapest Youth Festival. When the delegation reported that the Soviet representatives expressed their keen desire to establish closer friendship with progressive American youth and pledged to spare no effort to fight for peace, democracy, and the independence of all nations, the audience responded with cheers. Tillman, a Black youth leader and one of the heads of the American delegation to the Budapest Youth Festival, said: "We have seen the love of youth around the world for the Soviet Union, seeing it as a liberator and as the savior of humanity. Therefore, we are determined to strive for the unity of youth around the world through the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Union of Students, until fascism, exploitation, poverty, and imperialism are eradicated and peace is guaranteed."

### 纽约纪念世界青年日  进步青年学生团体集会保证努力争取世界和平

1949-11-15
第3版()
专栏：

    纽约纪念世界青年日
    进步青年学生团体集会保证努力争取世界和平
    【新华社北京十四日电】塔斯社纽约讯：为了纪念世界青年日，美国进步的青年学生团体和工会的一千余名代表，于本月十一日在纽约举行群众大会，并在会上保证全心全意地加倍努力为世界和平而奋斗。他们热烈欢迎从布达佩斯青年联欢大会归来的美国代表团。当代表团报告苏联代表表示他们切望与美国进步青年建立更密切的友谊，并保证将不遗余力地为和平民主及各民族独立而斗争时，听众报以欢呼。参加布达佩斯青年联欢大会的美国代表团团长之一、黑人青年领袖蒂尔曼称：“我们看到全世界青年对苏联的热爱，他们把苏联当作解放者，当作人类的救星。因此，我们决心经过世界民主青年联盟及世界学生联合会，来争取全世界青年的团结，直至法西斯主义、剥削、贫困和帝国主义绝迹及和平得到保证为止。”
