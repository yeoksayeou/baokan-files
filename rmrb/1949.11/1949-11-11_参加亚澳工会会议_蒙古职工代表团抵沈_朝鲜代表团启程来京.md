Title: Mongolian Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Shenyang to Attend Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference; Korean Delegation Departs for Beijing

Author:
Date: 1949-11-11
Page: 1

    Participating in the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference
    Mongolian Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Shenyang
    Korean Delegation Departs for Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 10th] The trade union delegation from the Mongolian People's Republic, participating in the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference, arrived in Shenyang at 8:30 AM on the 10th from Harbin, accompanied by Rao Bin, the Mayor of Harbin. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, who had arrived in Shenyang from Beijing, Zhang Weizhen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, Zhu Qiwu, Mayor of Shenyang, Tang Yunchao, Director of the Northeast Labor Bureau, Xu Zhi of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and over one thousand workers from various agencies and factories. The delegation consists of seven members, with Adili Bish, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Trade Unions of the Mongolian People's Republic, as the chief delegate. Upon disembarking, the Mongolian delegation was immediately greeted with applause and cheers from all welcomers, and received flowers from children. After Xu Zhi, Vice Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of all workers in the city, Adili Bish gave a brief reply, saying: "Our trade union delegation from the Mongolian People's Republic, on behalf of all the Mongolian people, extends the most respectful salute to the great Chinese people! The Chinese people have achieved a great victory, and the Mongolian people warmly welcome this victory, because this great victory of the Chinese people is of great importance to the security of the world. The Chinese people, who have already achieved victory, have also made great achievements on the labor front, and will surely achieve even greater achievements in the future!
    Long live Comrade Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people!
    Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian people!
    Long live Stalin, the great leader of the world's people!"
    After visiting the Beiling Mausoleum, the museum, and the Forbidden City in Shenyang, the Mongolian delegation departed by special train for Beijing at 8 PM.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th] The Korean delegation to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference, consisting of eighteen members led by Choi Kyung-duk, Chairman of the General Federation of Trade Unions of North Korea, departed from here today by special train for Beijing.

### 参加亚澳工会会议  蒙古职工代表团抵沈  朝鲜代表团启程来京

1949-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    参加亚澳工会会议
    蒙古职工代表团抵沈
    朝鲜代表团启程来京
    【新华社沈阳十日电】蒙古人民共和国参加亚洲、澳洲工会会议的职工代表团，十日上午八时半由哈尔滨市长饶斌陪同自哈抵沈。赴车站欢迎者有方自京抵沈的全国总工会副主席刘宁一、东北总工会主席张维桢、沈市市长朱其文、东北劳动总局长唐韵超、市总工会徐志等及各机关、工厂的职工一千余人。该团由七人组成，首席代表为蒙古人民共和国全国职工会中央主席阿德利比什。蒙古代表团下车后当即受到全体欢迎者的鼓掌欢呼，并接受儿童献花。市总工会副主席徐志代表全市工友向代表团致欢迎词后，阿德利比什即致简短答词称：“我们蒙古人民共和国的职工代表团，代表全蒙古人民，向伟大的中国人民致最敬礼！中国人民已经获得了伟大的胜利，蒙古人民非常欢迎这一胜利，因为中国人民的这一伟大的胜利，对全世界的安全有很大关系。已经获得了胜利的中国人民，在劳动战线上也有了伟大的成绩，将来必然会得到更大的成绩！
    中国人民领袖毛泽东同志万岁！
    中蒙人民友好万岁！
    世界人类伟大领袖斯大林万岁！”
    蒙古代表团在沈参观了北陵、博物馆及故宫后，于晚八时专车赴京。
    【新华社平壤十日电】参加亚澳工会会议的朝鲜代表团一行十八人，由首席代表北朝鲜职业总同盟主席崔ｊīｎｇ＠①德率领，于今日自此间乘专车赴北京。
