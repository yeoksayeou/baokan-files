Title: Overseas Chaozhou and Shantou People in Hong Kong Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1949-11-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 11th: Chaozhou and Shantou people residing in Hong Kong sent a telegram to the Central People's Government, the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, and the Shantou Military Control Commission on October 30th, congratulating the establishment of the Central People's Government and the liberation of various places in South China by the People's Liberation Army. The telegram stated: We thank the wise Chairman Mao, the Chinese Communist Party, and the People's Liberation Army for their continuous struggle to seek freedom, democracy, peace, and a happy life for all of China. From now on, we vow to follow the leadership of Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Chairman Ye Jianying, and Director Lin Meinan, to implement the Common Program, carry the revolution through to the end, and strive for the construction of a new South China and a new Chaozhou and Shantou.

### 旅港潮汕籍人士  电贺中央人民政府

1949-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    旅港潮汕籍人士
    电贺中央人民政府
    【新华社北京十一日电】旅居香港的潮汕籍人士于十月三十日致电中央人民政府、广东省人民政府暨汕头市军管会，祝贺中央人民政府成立及人民解放军解放华南各地。电文称：我们感谢英明的毛主席、中国共产党、人民解放军为求全中国得到自由、民主、和平、幸福的生活而不断奋斗。今后我们誓在毛主席、朱总司令、叶主席剑英、林主任美南领导之下，执行共同纲领，将革命进行到底，为建设新华南、新潮汕而努力奋斗。
