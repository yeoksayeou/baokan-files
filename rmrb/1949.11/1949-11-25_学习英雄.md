Title: Learning from a Hero
Author: Xin Er
Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 6

　　Learning from a Hero
　　Xin Er
    Platoon Leader Li Dexin,
    Determined to learn culture;
    "I'm thirty-six and illiterate,
    With my eyes open, I'm like a blind man.
    Three months of rectification time,
    Seize the opportunity to learn."
　
    Quit smoking and save money,
    Buy pen, ink, paper first;
    Although the allowance is small, save it slowly,
    In three months, bought a Hero brand pen.
　
    Figure out the learning method,
    A journey of ten thousand li starts with the first step.
　
    Cooking squad, ten brothers,
    First, recognize the names;
    Hand grenade, carbine,
    Name list, nailed on the wall;
    Recite in your mouth, remember in your heart,
    Every stroke must be careful.
　
    Barley porridge, rice,
    Cook on one side, recite on the other;
    Stove, pot, knife, board, scallions, chives, garlic,
    Seven days of learning, can recite them all completely.
　
    Write on the ash when burning the fire,
    Write on the ground in the toilet,
    Write while reciting,
    Review it again before going to bed at night.
　
    Recognize fifty in seven days,
    A hundred and something in half a month;
    Can write wall newspaper稿 in a little over a month,
    The whole company praises him highly.
　
    Not complacent, not arrogant;
    The minimum goal has not yet been reached:
    "Want to read the Liberation Daily in half a year."
    Shanghai, November 16th, 1949

### 学习英雄
辛而
1949-11-25
第6版()
专栏：

　　学习英雄
　　辛而
    炊事班长李德新，
    学习文化下决心；
    “咱三十六岁不识字，
    睁着两眼象瞎子。
    三月整军时间长，
    抓紧机会去学习。”
　
    戒了香烟省下钱，
    笔墨纸张先买全；
    津贴虽少慢慢集，
    三月买了枝金星笔。
　
    学习方法弄清楚，
    万里长征第一步。
　
    炊事班，十弟兄，
    名和姓，先认清；
    手榴弹，卡宾枪，
    名称表，钉上墙；
    嘴里念，心里记，
    一撇一划要仔细。
　
    大麦羹，稻米饭，
    一面煮，一面念；
    炉、锅、刀、板，葱、韭、大蒜，
    七天学习，完全会念。
　
    烧火时灰上写，
    厕所里地上写，
    一面写一面念，
    晚上睡觉温一遍。
　
    七天认五十，
    半月一百几；
    一个多月能写墙报稿，
    全连数他呱呱叫。
　
    不自满，不骄傲；
    最低目标还没到：
    “半年要看解放报。”
    一九四九，十一，十六于上海
