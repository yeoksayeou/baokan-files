Title: Soviet Diplomats in Korea, France, Italy, and Other Countries Hold Receptions to Celebrate the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1949-11-11
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th. On the 32nd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the Soviet ambassadors, ministers, and chargés d'affaires in Korea, France, India, Italy, Canada, Belgium, Egypt, Iran, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, and other countries held grand receptions. Government officials, diplomatic corps, scientists, writers, journalists, and representatives of mass organizations from various countries attended to offer congratulations. At the reception in the Soviet Embassy in Korea, those present included Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Vice Premiers Park Heon-young, Kim Chaek, and Hong Myung-hee; members of the Presidium of the People's Assembly; representatives of the Workers' Party and mass organizations; scientific and cultural workers; and representatives of the Northeast Business Delegation. The venue was filled with a festive atmosphere, and attendees raised their glasses in Russian, Korean, and Chinese, cheering "Long live Stalin!" "Long live Kim Il-sung!" "Long live Mao Zedong!" and other toasts. Among those attending the reception at the Soviet Embassy in France were French Communist Party General Secretary Maurice Thorez and Secretaries Jacques Duclos, Auguste Lecœur, and Marcel Servin, as well as French General Confederation of Labour General Secretary Benoît Frachon. Those attending the reception in Italy included Italian Communist Party leaders Palmiro Togliatti, Luigi Longo, Pietro Secchia, and Giancarlo Pajetta, as well as Italian Socialist Party Secretary Pietro Nenni. Among those attending the reception in Belgium was Belgian Communist Party Central Committee General Secretary Julien Lahaut.

### 苏联驻朝法意等国外交官员  举行招待会庆祝十月革命节

1949-11-11
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联驻朝法意等国外交官员
    举行招待会庆祝十月革命节
    【新华社北京十日电】在伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年纪念日，苏联驻朝鲜、法、印、意、加、比、埃及、伊朗、瑞典、挪威、丹麦等国的大使、公使、代办，均举行了盛大的招待会。各国的政府官员、外交使团、科学家、作家、新闻界、群众团体的代表，都出席致贺。在朝鲜苏联大使馆的招待会中，有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国总理金日成、副总理朴宪永、金策、洪命熹和人民议会主席团委员、劳动党及群众团体以及科学与文化工作者和东北商务代表团代表等，会场充满了节日的欢悦气氛，与会者以俄语、朝鲜语和华语举杯欢呼着“斯大林万岁！”“金日成万岁！”“毛泽东万岁！”等祝词。在苏联驻法大使馆的招待会中，出席者有法共总书记多列士和书记杜克洛、马尔梯、摩维以及法国总工会总书记佛拉商等。在意大利参加招待会的，有意共领袖托里亚蒂、谢嘉、斯科西马罗及意大利社会党书记南尼等。在比利时参加招待会的，有比共中委会总书记拉尔曼等。
