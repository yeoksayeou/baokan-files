Title: US Economic Situation Will Continue to Decline Next Year - US Bureau of Agricultural Economics Forecasts
Author:
Date: 1949-11-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 2nd: TASS, New York dispatch: The US Department of Agriculture's Bureau of Agricultural Economics published a report at the end of October, predicting that US economic activity will continue to decline, and this will continue into 1950. Employment will further decrease, and the situation of farmers will further deteriorate. The Bureau's assessment of the economic situation has always been more realistic than the economic forecasts made by other departments of the US government.

### 美国明年经济情况仍将继续衰退下去　美农业经济局预测

1949-11-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　美国明年经济情况仍将继续衰退下去
  　美农业经济局预测
    【新华社北京二日电】塔斯社纽约讯：美国农业部农业经济局于十月底发表报告，预言美国经济活动将继续衰落，一九五零年仍将如此，就业人数将进一步减少，农民境况将益形恶化。该局所估计的经济情况，一向比较美国政府其他部门所作的经济预测实在些。
