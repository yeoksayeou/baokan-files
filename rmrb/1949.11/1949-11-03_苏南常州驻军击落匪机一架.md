Title: PLA Garrison in Southern Jiangsu's Changzhou Downs Bandit Plane

Author:
Date: 1949-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 2nd. On October 29th, a unit of the People's Liberation Army shot down a Kuomintang plane in Changzhou County, Jiangsu Province. The plane caught fire and was destroyed after landing, and the eight crew members, including pilot Jie Renbo, Dong Hualan, and telegraph operator Zhou Shaonan, were captured alive. The plane belonged to the 104th Squadron of the 16th Group of the bandit air force. On the morning of the 29th, it flew from Dinghai to Shanghai to harass the area and was shot down by the People's Liberation Army when it attempted to continue harassing Changzhou.

### 苏南常州驻军击落匪机一架

1949-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏南常州驻军击落匪机一架
    【新华社南京二日电】十月二十九日人民解放军某部在江苏常州县境击落国民党飞机一架。该机落地后即起火烧毁，驾驶员解仁博、董华岚及报务员周绍南等八人均被生俘。该机系属匪空军第十六大队一零四中队，二十九日上午自定海飞至上海窜扰后，企图继续窜扰常州时被人民解放军击落。
