Title: Calcutta Municipal Employees' Strike Ends in Victory

Author:

Date: 1949-11-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 1st: A Reuters Calcutta dispatch reports: The strike of over 20,000 municipal employees in Calcutta, which began on October 21st to demand higher wages, ended victoriously on the 30th. The Calcutta authorities have agreed to give low-paid workers twelve months of temporary relief; not to dismiss any of the striking workers; not to pay wages for the strike period, but to allow the striking workers certain other forms of subsidies.

### 加城市政雇员罢工胜利结束

1949-11-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　加城市政雇员罢工胜利结束
    【新华社北京一日电】据路透社加尔各答消息：加尔各答两万余名市政机关雇员为要求增加工资而于十月二十一日举行的罢工，已于三十日胜利结束。加尔各答当局已同意给低薪工人十二个月的临时救济；不解雇任何一个罢工的工人；罢工期间不付工资，但允许给予罢工工人某些其他形式的津贴。
