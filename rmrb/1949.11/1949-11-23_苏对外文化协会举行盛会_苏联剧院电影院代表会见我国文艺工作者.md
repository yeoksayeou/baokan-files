Title: Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Holds Grand Gathering; Representatives of Soviet Theaters and Cinemas Meet with Chinese Literary and Art Workers
Author:
Date: 1949-11-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 21st: TASS, Moscow: The Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a grand gathering of the Department of History and Film Art on the 19th. Representatives of Soviet theaters and cinemas met with Chinese cultural and artistic workers who are visiting Moscow. Chinese writer Cao Yu and actress Bai Yang introduced how China's progressive writers and artists actively participated in the Chinese people's liberation struggle and established a new life. The Chinese People's Government and the Communist Party pay attention to the development of culture and art. The victory of the Chinese people has opened a new page in the history of Chinese people's drama culture.

Cao Yu also talked about the development of amateur art activities in the new China's urban and rural areas. More than two million people have now participated in various amateur art groups.

After the meeting, the Chinese guests presented the Chinese translations of Gorky's "The Lower Depths", "Selected Works of Chekhov", Stanislavsky's "An Actor Prepares", and Danchenko's "Memoirs" to the Soviet Gorky Art Theatre as gifts from the Chinese National Academy of Drama.

### 苏对外文化协会举行盛会　苏联剧院电影院代表会见我国文艺工作者

1949-11-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏对外文化协会举行盛会
　　苏联剧院电影院代表会见我国文艺工作者
    【新华社北京二十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联对外文化关系协会十九日举行了历史与电影艺术部的盛会，会上苏联剧院与电影院的代表们会见了正在访问莫斯科的中国文化艺术工作者。中国作家曹禺和女演员白杨介绍了中国的进步作家和艺术工作者如何积极参加中国人民的解放斗争并建立新的生活。中国人民政府和共产党对文化与艺术发展给予的注意，中国人民的胜利已揭开了中国人民戏剧文化历史的新页。
    曹禺并谈到新中国城乡业余艺术活动的发展，现已有两百多万人参加了各种业余艺术团体。
    会后，中国来宾把高尔基的“夜店”、“柴霍甫选集”、斯丹尼斯拉夫斯基的“演员自我修养”、丹钦科的“回忆录”等中文译本赠给苏联高尔基艺术剧院，作为中国国立戏剧学院的赠礼。
