Title: US-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Grand Banquet to Celebrate October Revolution Day Urges Efforts to Win Peace and Friendship with the Soviet Union Vyshinsky Elaborates on Soviet Peaceful Policy
Author:
Date: 1949-11-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th: TASS, New York, 14th: On November 10th, the National Council of American-Soviet Friendship held a banquet at the Waldorf Astoria Hotel to celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution and the 16th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the United States and the Soviet Union (the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and the United States began on November 16, 1933). One thousand people attended the banquet. Speakers unanimously urged intensified efforts to fight for peace and emphasized the necessity of American-Soviet friendship.
  After the chairman of the National Council of American-Soviet Friendship, Mr. Kingsbury, made a brief opening speech, the first speaker was the writer Lamont. He urged the American people to strive to abolish the "Cold War" policy and restore President Roosevelt's policy of friendship and cooperation with the Soviet Union. Lamont said: "The most urgent issue at present is to achieve understanding between the United States and the Soviet Union and international peace." When mentioning the issue of atomic energy, Lamont urged the United States to concentrate its efforts on developing atomic energy for peaceful purposes, rather than on developing atomic bombs. He pointed out that the Soviet Union is developing the peaceful uses of atomic energy.

### 美苏友协盛大宴会庆祝十月革命节　敦促争取和平对苏友好  维辛斯基阐述苏联和平政策

1949-11-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　美苏友协盛大宴会庆祝十月革命节
  　敦促争取和平对苏友好     
    维辛斯基阐述苏联和平政策
    【新华社北京十六日电】塔斯社纽约十四日讯：十一月十日，美苏友好协会全国委员会在华多尔夫·亚斯托利亚饭店举行宴会，庆祝伟大的社会主义十月革命三十二周年和美苏建立外交关系十六周年（按苏美建立外交关系始于一九三三年十一月十六日）。赴宴者有一千人。演说者一致敦促加紧为和平而斗争，并强调美苏友好的必要性。
    在美苏友好协会全国委员会主席金斯贝莱发表简短的开会词后，第一个演说的是作家拉蒙特。他敦促美国人民力争废除“冷战”政策，恢复罗斯福总统的对苏友好合作政策。拉蒙特说：“目前最迫切的问题是获致美苏之间的谅解和国际的和平。”当提及原子能问题时，拉蒙特敦促美国集中力量，发展用于和平事业上的原子能，而不要集中力量，来发展原子弹。他指出苏联正在发展原子能的和平用途。
