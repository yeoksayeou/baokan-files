Title: Nanjing is Actively Preparing to Commemorate the Anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution

Author:
Date: 1949-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, November 2nd: Nanjing is actively preparing to commemorate the anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution. On November 1st, the Propaganda Department of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party issued a notice stipulating that November 7th to 14th will be the propaganda week for commemorating the 32nd anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution. During the propaganda week, various celebration and propaganda activities will be used to introduce the Soviet Union and call on people to learn from the Soviet Union. The Nanjing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is now actively organizing commemorative meetings, lectures, exhibitions, performances, etc. The Preparatory Committee of the Nanjing Municipal Democratic Youth Federation is also preparing to launch in-depth propaganda, combining the celebration of the October Revolution with the activities of World Youth Day and Student Week. The Nanjing Art Troupe is rushing to rehearse the famous play "The Russian Question" by the famous Soviet writer Simonov, which is scheduled to be performed on the October Revolution Day. Newspapers and magazines here plan to publish commemorative supplements or special issues, and the radio station will also broadcast special programs. Literary and art groups are preparing to screen Soviet films and organize Soviet music evenings.

### 苏联十月革命节将临南京正积极筹备纪念

1949-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联十月革命节将临南京正积极筹备纪念
    【新华社南京二日电】南京市正在积极筹备纪念苏联十月革命节。十一月一日，中共南京市委宣传部发出通知，规定从七日到十四日为纪念苏联十月革命节第三十二周年的宣传周。在宣传周内要通过各项庆祝及宣传活动介绍苏联，号召向苏联学习。中苏友好协会南京分会现正积极组织纪念会、讲座、展览、演出等。市民主青联筹委会亦正准备展开深入宣传，将庆祝十月革命节与世界青年日、学生周的活动结合进行。南京文工团正赶排苏联名作家西蒙诺夫的名剧“俄罗斯问题”，定于十月革命节公演。此间报纸，杂志则拟出纪念专刊或特辑，广播台也将播送特别节目，文艺团体正在筹备放映苏联影片，组织苏联音乐晚会。
