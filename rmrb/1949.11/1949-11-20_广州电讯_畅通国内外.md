Title: Guangzhou Telecommunications: Open to Domestic and International Communication
Author:
Date: 1949-11-20
Page: 2

　　Guangzhou Telecommunications
  　Open to Domestic and International Communication
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 19th – Thanks to the efforts of the staff of the Guangzhou Telecommunications Bureau, telegraphs and long-distance telephone lines between Guangzhou and various liberated cities within the country, as well as telegraphs and telephone lines between Guangzhou and Hong Kong and Macau, have been successively restored. Now, the bureau has also completed coordination with the Shanghai International Radio Station and the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau, and has begun receiving international telegrams since the 16th. In addition to all the original communication circuits of the former Shanghai International Radio Station being open, new circuits opened via Shanghai and Beijing can also be used to communicate with countries such as Czechoslovakia, North Korea, and Mongolia.

### 广州电讯　畅通国内外

1949-11-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　广州电讯
  　畅通国内外
    【新华社广州十九日电】在广州电讯局员工的努力下，广州与国内解放区各埠间的电报、长途电话，以及与港澳之间的电报、电话均已先后恢复畅通。现该局又与上海国际电台和北京电讯局联络就绪，于十六日起收受国际电报。所有前上海国际电台原有通报各电路，均已畅通外，并可经由上海和北京新辟的电路，与捷克、朝鲜、蒙古等国通报。
