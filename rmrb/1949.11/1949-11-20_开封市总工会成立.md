Title: Kaifeng City Federation of Trade Unions Established
Author:
Date: 1949-11-20
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 19th: The Kaifeng City Federation of Trade Unions was declared established at the city's first Workers' Representative Congress. The Representative Congress was held from the 10th to the 15th of this month. Two hundred and sixty-eight representatives from various trade unions attended the meeting. The congress elected 54 executive committee members of the Federation of Trade Unions, including Wu Xuanxuan, Deputy Secretary of the Kaifeng Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and mutually elected Wu Xuanxuan as Chairman of the Federation of Trade Unions.

### 开封市总工会成立

1949-11-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　开封市总工会成立
    【新华社开封十九日电】开封市总工会在本市首届工人代表大会上宣告成立。代表大会系于本月十日至十五日举行。到会各业工会代表二六八人，大会选出中共开封市委副书记武镟声等五十四人为总工会执行委员，并互推武镟声为总工会主席。
