Title: Our Country's Special Envoy and Plenipotentiary Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Wang Jiaxiang, Arrives in Moscow; Gromyko and Others Greet Him at the Train Station
Author:
Date: 1949-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 1st—TASS, Moscow: Mr. Wang Jiaxiang, Special Envoy and Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the People's Republic of China, and staff members of the embassy, arrived in the Soviet capital on October 31st. They were welcomed at the Yaroslavsky Railway Station by Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Gromyko, Chairman of the Moscow Soviet Popov, Major General Artemyev, Moscow Commander Lieutenant General Sinilov, Lieutenant General Slavin, and officials from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Their arrival was also welcomed by Mr. Ge Baoquan, Acting Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China, and heads of embassies and legations of several countries stationed in Moscow.

### 我国驻苏特命全权大使　王稼祥抵达莫斯科  葛罗米柯等赴车站欢迎

1949-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国驻苏特命全权大使
  　王稼祥抵达莫斯科
    葛罗米柯等赴车站欢迎
    【新华社北京一日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：中华人民共和国特命全权大使王稼祥先生和大使馆人员，已于十月三十一日抵达苏京。他们在雅罗斯拉夫斯基火车站受到苏联副外长葛罗米柯、莫斯科苏维埃主席波波夫、阿尔杰米也夫准上将、莫斯科司令西尼洛夫中将、斯拉温中将以及苏联外交部的官员们的欢迎。
    他们的抵达，也受到了中华人民共和国代理代办戈宝权先生和若干国家驻莫斯科的大使馆与公使馆首长的欢迎。
