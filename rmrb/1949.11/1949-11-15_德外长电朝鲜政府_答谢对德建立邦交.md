Title: German Foreign Minister Cables North Korean Government, Thanking Them for Establishing Diplomatic Relations with Germany
Author:
Date: 1949-11-15
Page: 3

    German Foreign Minister Cables North Korean Government
    Thanking Them for Establishing Diplomatic Relations with Germany
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th] TASS, Berlin: The Press Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic released a reply cable on the 12th from Foreign Minister Dellinger to the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The cable expresses gratitude to the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea for its decision to establish diplomatic relations with the German Democratic Republic. The cable also stated that the Provisional Government of the German Democratic Republic will do its utmost to promote friendly relations between the peoples of the two countries.

### 德外长电朝鲜政府  答谢对德建立邦交

1949-11-15
第3版()
专栏：

    德外长电朝鲜政府
    答谢对德建立邦交
    【新华社北京十四日电】塔斯社柏林讯：德意志民主共和国外交部新闻处，十二日发表外交部长窦丁格尔致朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府复电一件。该电对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府决定与德意志民主共和国建立外交关系，表示感谢。电文并称：德意志民主共和国临时政府将尽最大努力促进两国人民间的友好关系。
