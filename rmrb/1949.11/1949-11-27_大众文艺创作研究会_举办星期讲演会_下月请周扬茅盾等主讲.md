Title: Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association to Hold Sunday Lectures; Zhou Yang, Mao Dun and Others to Speak Next Month
Author:
Date: 1949-11-27
Page: 3

　　Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association
　　Holding Sunday Lectures
　　Zhou Yang, Mao Dun and Others to Speak Next Month
　　[This Newspaper's News] The Beijing Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association, in order to improve members' creative skills and research interests, and to help members strengthen their learning, is holding a Mass Literature and Art Sunday Lecture series. Writers and experts in the literary and art circles have been invited to give thematic reports at the Jiefang Cinema in Dengshikou from 9:30 to 11:30 every Sunday morning, starting December 4th. In addition to issuing hearing certificates to members, who will be admitted upon presentation of the certificate, it is also planned to issue 500 auditing certificates. Those who wish to audit can register at the Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association at 15 Xiagongfu, Wangfujing, from the 26th to the 30th of this month. The speakers invited for December include Mr. Zhou Yang, Mr. Mao Dun, Mr. Li Bozhao, and Mr. Zhang Geng. The content will be announced later.

### 大众文艺创作研究会　举办星期讲演会　下月请周扬茅盾等主讲

1949-11-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　大众文艺创作研究会
  　举办星期讲演会
  　下月请周扬茅盾等主讲
    【本报讯】京市大众文艺创作研究会，为了提高会员创作技术及研究兴趣，并帮助会员加强学习，特举办大众文艺星期讲演会，聘请文艺界作家及专家，于十二月四日起每星期天上午九时半至十一时半，在灯市口解放电影院作专题报告。除发给会员听讲证，届时凭证入场听讲外，并拟发旁听证五百名，凡愿旁听者可于本月二十六日至三十日到王府井霞公府十五号大众文艺创作研究会报名，十二月份聘请的报告人有周扬、茅盾、李伯钊、张庚诸先生，内容临时通知。
