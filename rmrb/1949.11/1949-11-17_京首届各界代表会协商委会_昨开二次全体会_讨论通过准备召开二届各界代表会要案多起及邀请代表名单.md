　　Beijing's First All-Circles Representative Conference Consultative Committee
　　Holds Second Plenary Meeting Yesterday
    Discusses and Approves Several Important Cases in Preparation for Convening the Second All-Circles Representative Conference and the List of Invited Representatives
    [Beijing News] The Consultative Committee of the First All-Circles People's Representative Conference of Beijing Municipality held its second plenary meeting yesterday (16th). The meeting was presided over by Peng Zhen, Chairman of the Consultative Committee. The meeting discussed and approved the drafts of the "Organizational Regulations of the Second All-Circles People's Representative Conference of Beijing Municipality" and the "Provisional Organizational Outline of the People's Government of Beijing Municipality" to be submitted to the Second All-Circles People's Representative Conference. At the meeting, Qian Duansheng, Vice Chairman of the Consultative Committee, reported on the discussion of the "Finance and Taxation" group, and it was decided to form two additional groups, "Income Tax and Business Tax for Profit-Making Enterprises" and "Progressive Agricultural Tax," to further study the financial and taxation proposals drafted by the Municipal Government in detail. Other issues such as providing relief to unemployed workers and establishing spare-time supplementary schools will be discussed at the next plenary meeting of the Consultative Committee. The meeting also discussed and approved the list of representatives invited by the Municipal Government and decided to convene the Second All-Circles People's Representative Conference of Beijing Municipality in the near future.

### 京首届各界代表会协商委会　昨开二次全体会  讨论通过准备召开二届各界代表会要案多起及邀请代表名单

1949-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　京首届各界代表会协商委会
　  昨开二次全体会
    讨论通过准备召开二届各界代表会要案多起及邀请代表名单
    【北京讯】北京市第一届各界人民代表会议协商委员会昨（十六）日举行第二次全体会议。由协商委员会主席彭真主持，会议讨论并通过了准备向第二届各界人民代表会议提出的“北京市第二届各界人民代表会议组织条例”和“北京市人民政府暂行组织大纲”两草案，会上由协商委员会副主席钱端升报告“财政税收”小组讨论情形，并决定按市府所拟财政税收提案，另组“营利事业所得税和营业税”和“农业累进税”等两小组，再加以详细研究。其他关于救济失业员工和开办业余补习学校等问题，定于下次协商委员会全体会议中再行讨论。会议并讨论与通过了市政府所提邀请代表名单，并决定于最近期间召开北京市第二届各界人民代表会议。
