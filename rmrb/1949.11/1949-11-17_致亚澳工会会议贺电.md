Title: Telegrams of Congratulations to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-11-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 16th. The Preparatory Committee of the Northwest Federation of Trade Unions and the Preparatory Committee of the Xi'an Municipal Federation of Trade Unions today sent a telegram of congratulations to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. The telegram reads: We celebrate the opening of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference in Beijing, the capital of the Chinese people, with great excitement and joy. This is an unprecedented great unity of Asian and Australian worker brothers, which will greatly strengthen the forces of world peace and democracy, promote the development of the Eastern people's revolutionary movement, and encourage the working class of the People's Republic of China to bravely and resolutely build a new China. On behalf of all the staff and workers in Northwest China, we extend our warm congratulations and support to the conference, and call on the workers in the Northwest region to organize quickly in order to complete the great tasks entrusted to us by the motherland and better implement the resolutions of the Asian-Australian Conference.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th. The Preparatory Committee of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions today sent a telegram of congratulations on the opening of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. The telegram reads:
　　It is an honor for the Chinese working class that labor movement leaders from all over the world have come to our capital to hold the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. We, one million workers in Shanghai, extend our brotherly greetings and congratulations to you.
　　The convening of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference signifies the unprecedented great unity of the Asian and Australian working classes. This unity will be increasingly consolidated and expanded. This conference also shows the failure of the imperialists' conspiracy to split the world labor movement. We workers in Shanghai resolutely support the World Federation of Trade Unions and the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. We are prepared to thoroughly implement the resolutions of the conference.
　　Finally, we sincerely hope that after the successful completion of the conference, the delegates will be able to visit Shanghai, which will be of great help to the labor movement in Shanghai. We warmly welcome and await you.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th. The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, the Australian Seamen's Union, and the Victorian Branch of the Australian Ship Painters and Dockers Union, respectively, sent telegrams of congratulations to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference, which opened in Beijing. The telegrams are as follows:
　　(1) Telegram from the Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth: Beijing World Federation of Trade Unions:
　　We send you brotherly and militant greetings! We wish the conference complete success and believe that it will contribute to achieving lasting world peace and realizing the demands of the Asian and Australian working masses. The celebration of the February 21st Youth Day, which struggles against the colonial system, will show that the progressive youth of the world supports the resolutions of the conference.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth
　　(2) Telegram from the Australian Seamen's Union: Beijing World Federation of Trade Unions:
　　The Australian Seamen's Union extends brotherly greetings to the conference! The World Federation of Trade Unions is the beacon of hope for militant workers in capitalist countries.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Burrd, Melbourne, Australia
　　(3) Telegram from the Victorian Branch of the Australian Ship Painters and Dockers Union: Chairman of the Beijing World Federation of Trade Unions:
　　The Victorian Branch of the Ship Painters and Dockers Union extends its congratulations and pledges its full support and unity in order to improve the living standards of the Pacific countries.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Secretary Doyle, Melbourne, Australia

### 致亚澳工会会议贺电

1949-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　致亚澳工会会议贺电
  　西北及西安市总工会筹委员
    【新华社西安十六日电】西北总工会筹委会、西安市总工会筹委会于本日电贺亚澳工会会议，电文称：我们以极大的兴奋和愉快的心情，庆祝亚澳工会会议在中国人民首都北京开幕。这是亚澳工人兄弟空前的大团结，她将大大增强世界和平民主力量，推动东方人民革命运动的发展，鼓励中华人民共和国的工人阶级勇敢坚韧地建设新中国。我们代表中国西北区的全体职工，谨向会议致热烈的庆贺和拥护，并号召西北区的工人迅速组织起来，以完成祖国所赋予的伟大任务，更好地执行亚澳会议的决议。
  　上海总工会筹委会
    【新华社北京十六日电】上海总工会筹备委员会，今日电贺亚澳工会会议开幕，电文称：
    世界各国的工运领袖到我国的首都来开亚澳工会会议，这是我们中国工人阶级的光荣，我们上海一百万工人特向你们致以兄弟的敬礼和祝贺。
    亚澳工会会议的召开，表示亚澳工人阶级的空前大团结。这一团结将日益巩固，日益扩大。这一会议更表明了帝国主义分裂世界工运阴谋的失败。我们上海的工人坚决拥护世界工联和亚澳工会会议。我们准备彻底执行大会的决议。
    最后，我们竭诚希望在大会胜利完成以后，代表们能够光临上海，这对上海的职工运动将有很大的帮助，我们热烈地欢迎与等待着。
    世界民主青年理事会秘书处、澳洲海员及造船业工会
    【新华社北京十六日电】世界民主青年理事会秘书处，澳洲海员工会和澳洲漆船工人、造船工人联合会维多利亚分会等，分别电贺在北京开幕的亚澳工会会议。电文如下：
    （一）世界民主青年理事会秘书处的贺电：北京世界工联：
    谨向你们致兄弟般的战斗的敬礼！祝会议全部胜利，并相信会议定能对世界持久和平的获得以及亚澳工人群众要求的实现有所贡献。为了反对殖民地制度而斗争的二月二十一日青年日的庆祝中，将要显示出世界进步青年是拥护会议的决议的。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　世界民主青年理事会秘书处
    （二）澳洲海员工会的贺电：北京世界工联：
    澳洲海员工会向大会致兄弟的敬礼！世界工联是资本主义国家中战斗工人希望所寄的灯塔。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　伯尔德发自澳大利亚墨尔钵
    （三）澳洲漆船工人、造船工人联合会维多利亚分会贺电：北京世界工联主席：
    漆船工人、造船工人联合会维多利亚分会谨致庆贺，并誓将全力支持，合力团结，以求提高太平洋各国之生活标准。
     　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　书记道尔发自澳大利亚墨尔钵
