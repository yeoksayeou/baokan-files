Title: Hualian Jinglian Charity Games for Disaster Relief
Author:
Date: 1949-11-03
Page: 4

　　Hualian Jinglian Charity Games for Disaster Relief
    [Beijing News] The "Charity Games for Disaster Relief" jointly organized by the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation and the Municipal Education Bureau will be held at the Fu Jen Catholic University stadium at 3:00 pm today (the 3rd). "Hualian" and "Jinglian" will hold a basketball game. At that time, there will also be an accompanying game between the Normal University basketball team and the "Rainbow Team" (women's team). Tickets are priced at 400 yuan.

### 华联京联救灾义赛

1949-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　华联京联救灾义赛
    【北京讯】全国体育总会筹委会和市教育局联合举办的“救灾义赛”，定于今（三）日下午三时在辅仁大学体育场举行，由“华联”与“京联”举行篮球赛，届时，并有师大篮球队对“虹队”（女）的陪赛。票价为四百元。
