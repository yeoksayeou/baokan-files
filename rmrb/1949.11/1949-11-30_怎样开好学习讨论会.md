Title: How to Conduct Effective Learning Discussion Meetings
Author:
Date: 1949-11-30
Page: 3

From the learning experiences of the Shaanxi Suide Dazhong (Masses) Newspaper Agency, Xinhua Branch, Rehe Masses Daily, Shijiazhuang Education Bureau, Shijiazhuang Literary and Art Work Committee, etc., to conduct good learning discussion meetings, the following should be done:

Before holding a learning discussion meeting, make thorough preparations: organize everyone to talk and chat casually, and everyone should write a speech outline; further check the speech outlines and designate speakers. The discussion questions should be relevant to the level of most attendees, and should also be common problems that everyone has. General minor problems and individual words and sentences can be solved through individual conversations, casual chats during meals, or by assigning someone to provide written answers and posting them on the wall newspaper, so as not to take up discussion meeting time.

　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　　×

The chairman of the discussion meeting plays an important role. Therefore, the chairman cannot merely preside over the opening and closing of the meeting, or who speaks on what issues. They must make more preparations than everyone else, be good at grasping the center of the discussion and everyone's emotions, and promptly raise the points of contention in the issue, avoid delving into trivial issues, wasting time, and slackening learning enthusiasm.

　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　　×

Responsible comrades should personally participate in the discussion meeting, pay attention to enlightening everyone on possible doubts and unclear issues, find contradictions, clarify the key to the issue, bring out all the questions in everyone's minds, in order to grasp everyone's level of understanding, guide them step by step to go deeper, and solve the problem.

　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　　×

In some units' learning discussion meetings, the following situation has occurred: some newly joined personnel have the idea of "not daring to raise questions" and "being afraid of making mistakes," making discussion difficult to unfold. The learning committee then encouraged everyone to speak, while also correcting the tendency of some comrades to like to "label people." As a result, everyone dared to speak and argue, and the issue could be deeply investigated.

　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　　×

The discussion must reach a clear conclusion in order to enhance everyone's interest. To this end, the Shijiazhuang City Learning Committee has notified the various Learning Committee branches to promptly send the unresolved issues in the discussion to the City Learning Committee, so that the City Learning Committee can promptly grasp the situation, organize discussions, and make various disputes get a clear resolution.

　　　　　　　　　　　　×　　×　　×

Without in-depth learning, there can be no in-depth discussion. Therefore, to ensure that the discussion meeting is well conducted, it is necessary to organize a good thorough reading and careful study of the documents. At a minimum, the important parts must be thoroughly read, grasping the center of the document, and understanding the spirit and essence of the document, so that the actual problems can be well-connected and discussed. This is a lesson learned by the Suide Dazhong Newspaper Agency and the Xinhua Branch in their study. When studying "On New Democracy," they did not study the key points of each section, and the discussion was only rambling and far-fetched. They originally wanted to "make the study deeper," but as a result, they deviated from the center and could not figure out the beginning and end.

### 怎样开好学习讨论会

1949-11-30
第3版()
专栏：各地学习集锦

　　怎样开好学习讨论会
    根据陕北绥德大众报社与新华支社、热河群众日报社、石家庄市教育局、石市文艺工作委员会等处的学习经验，要搞好学习讨论会，应该：
    在举行学习讨论会之前，先作充分准备：组织大家交谈、漫谈，每人都写出发言提纲；进一步检查发言提纲，推定发言人。讨论的问题，应切合于大多数到会人的水平，而且又须是大家存在的普遍问题，对于一般小问题与个别词句，或则个别交谈解决，或则在吃饭时漫谈，或则指定专人作书面解答，分布在墙报上，以免占去讨论会时间。
    　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　　×
    讨论会的主席，对讨论会起重要作用。所以当主席不能仅仅是主持开会、散会、会上问题谁发言，他必须比大家作更多的准备，善于掌握讨论中心及大家的情绪，及时提出问题的争论点，避免钻枝节问题，浪费时间，松懈学习情绪。
    　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　　×
    负责同志亲自参加讨论会，注意启发大家可能怀疑的与不明确的问题，找出矛盾，明确问题的关键，把大家心眼里的问题都兜出来，以便掌握大家的认识程度，步步引导深入，解决问题。
    　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　　×
    有的单位学习讨论会上曾发生过这样的情况：一部分新参加工作的人员，存在着“有问题不敢提”、“怕犯错误”的思想，讨论不易展开，学委会便一面鼓励大家发言，同时纠正某些同志爱“扣帽子”的偏向。这一来，大家敢说话、敢争论了，问题乃能深究下去。
    　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　　　×
    讨论必须得出明确的结论，才能提高大家的兴趣。为此，石家庄市学委会曾通知各学委分会，及时将讨论中争论不决的问题汇送市学委会，以便市学委会及时掌握情况，组织讨论，使各种争论得到明确解决。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　×　　×　　×
    没有深入的学习，不可能有深入的讨论，因此，要保证搞好讨论会。必须好好地组织对文件的熟读、精读，最低限度要将重要地方熟读，抓住文件中心，了解文件的精神实质，然后才能很好地联系实际问题去展开讨论。这是绥德大众报社与新华支社学习中的一个教训。他们在学习新民主主义论时，没有研究每节的重点所在，讨论时便只有漫无边际的发言，扯得很远，原想把学习“搞深入一点”，结果脱离了中心，弄不出个头绪来。
