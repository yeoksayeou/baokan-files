Title: Beijing Federation of Trade Unions Fourth District Office Holds Workers' Clinic; Over a Hundred Doctors and Nurses Volunteer for Medical Services
Author:
Date: 1949-11-08
Page: 4

　　Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Fourth District Office
  　Holds Workers' Clinic
  　Over a Hundred Doctors and Nurses Volunteer for Medical Services
    [Beijing News] The workers' clinic jointly established by the Fourth District Office of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee and the Fourth District District Public Office Preparatory Committee was officially established on the 2nd of this month. The clinic was established to implement the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference. After two months of active preparation, the Zhonghe Hospital Trade Union warmly responded and sponsored the clinic. As many as 110 doctors and nurses volunteered to participate in the free medical service. Among them, Huang Dayou, Liu Gengnian and others generously donated various valuable medicines and equipment. Subsequently, with the assistance of construction workers, employees of Fuxing Hardware Store, the Yanjing Paper Mill Trade Union, and other parties, it has developed into a small-scale hospital. It includes internal medicine, surgery, obstetrics and gynecology, ophthalmology, otolaryngology and other departments, and has a laboratory. The clinic is open from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM every day. The address is Yangshi Street, Xisi.

### 北京市总工会四区办事处　举办工人诊疗所　百余医生护士义务诊病

1949-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京市总工会四区办事处
  　举办工人诊疗所
  　百余医生护士义务诊病
    【北京讯】北京市总工会筹委会四区办事处和四区区公所筹办的工人诊疗所，已于本月二日正式成立。该所是为执行京市各界代表会议而设立的，经过两个月的积极筹备，首由中和医院工会热烈响应和赞助，医生护士等自动报名参加义务诊病的，已达一百一十人，内并有黄大有、刘赓年等人慷慨捐助贵重药品和器材多种，继由建筑业工人、复兴五金号店员、燕京造纸厂工会等多方协助，已具小型医院规模，内分内、外、妇产、眼、耳鼻喉等科，并设有化验室，每日下午三时至五时门诊，地址在西四羊市大街。
