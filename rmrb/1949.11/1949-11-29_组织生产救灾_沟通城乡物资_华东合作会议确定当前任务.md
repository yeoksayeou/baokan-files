Title: Organize Production and Disaster Relief, Connect Urban and Rural Materials: East China Cooperation Conference Determines Current Tasks
Author:
Date: 1949-11-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 27th: The first East China District Cooperative Work Conference concluded on the 15th of this month. More than 50 representatives from cooperatives in Zhejiang Province, Southern Jiangsu, Northern Jiangsu, Southern Anhui Province, Bohai, Jiaodong, and Central and Southern Shandong regions of Shandong Province, and Nanjing, Shanghai, Qingdao, Jinan, and Weifang cities attended the conference. The conference conducted detailed discussions on the direction, tasks, operations, and community affairs of the cooperatives. The conference unanimously agreed that cooperatives should focus on organizing production for disaster relief and facilitating the exchange of materials between urban and rural areas in the near future. To strengthen the exchange of materials between urban and rural areas, urban and rural cooperatives should be developed in a focused and appropriate manner. Regarding the business and community affairs of the cooperatives, the conference adopted draft versions of eight documents, including the "East China District Cooperative Provisional Organizational Measures." These eight drafts will be taken back to various regions by the representatives for pilot testing, and after successful testing, they will be organized, approved, and promulgated by the government. At the meeting, cooperative workers from various regions also presented reports on various typical cooperatives to exchange experiences. After the meeting, representatives from various regions also negotiated and signed some business contracts for exchanging urban and rural materials with the Shanghai Municipal Supply and Marketing Cooperative.

### 组织生产救灾　沟通城乡物资　华东合作会议确定当前任务

1949-11-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　组织生产救灾　沟通城乡物资
  　华东合作会议确定当前任务
    【新华社上海二十七日电】华东区第一次合作社工作会议已于本月十五日结束。出席这次会议的有浙江省、江苏省的苏南、苏北，安徽省的皖南，山东省的渤海、胶东、鲁中南等区和南京、上海、青岛、济南、潍坊等市合作社的代表五十余人。会议对合作社的方针、任务、业务、社务等都作了详尽的讨论。会议一致认为合作社最近时期应着重组织生产救灾和城乡物资交流的工作。为加强城乡物资交流，城乡合作社应作有重点地适当地发展。关于合作社的业务和社务问题，会上通过了“华东区合作社暂行组织办法”等八个文件的草案。这八个草案将由各代表带回各地先作重点试验，待试验成功后再由政府加以整理批准公布施行。会上，各地区的合作社工作者并曾作各种典型合作社的报告，借以交流经验。会后，各地区代表又分别与上海市供销合作总社洽订了一些交流城乡物资的业务合同。
