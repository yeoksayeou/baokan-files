Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Issues Slogans Commemorating the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1949-11-05
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 2nd: The Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party has recently issued slogans to commemorate the 32nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, extending heartfelt greetings to the people of all nations who are fighting for the victory of democracy and socialism, for peace and the friendship of all nationalities, and against the instigators of a new war. The commemorative slogans state: "The entire nation expresses its gratitude to the Soviet people and the great Stalin for their fraternal and selfless assistance in our country's revolution and economic and cultural development; long live the inviolable brotherly friendship between the Mongolian and Soviet people!" The slogans also stipulate the most important economic and political tasks facing the working people of the Mongolian People's Republic on the road to the victorious realization of the five-year plan for developing the country's national economy and culture.

### 蒙古人民革命党发布纪念十月革命节口号

1949-11-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民革命党发布纪念十月革命节口号
    【新华社北京三日电】塔斯社乌兰巴托二日讯：蒙古人民共和国人民革命党顷发布纪念伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年的口号，对为民主与社会主义的胜利、为和平与各民族人民的友谊、反对新战争挑拨者而斗争的各民族人民致衷心的敬礼。纪念口号中提出：“举国感谢苏联人民及伟大的斯大林在我国革命及经济与文化发展中的兄弟般的与大公无私的援助；蒙古人民与苏联人民不可侵犯的兄弟般的友谊万岁！”口号并规定了蒙古人民共和国劳动人民在胜利实现发展该国国民经济与文化的五年计划的道路上所面临的最重要的经济与政治任务。
