Title: Congratulations on the 32nd Anniversary of the October Revolution in the Soviet Union: Chairman Mao Sends a Congratulatory Telegram to Marshal Stalin; Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Send Separate Congratulatory Telegrams

Author: 
Date: 1949-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 5th. Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin congratulating him on the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution, stating:

Marshal Stalin:

On the occasion of the 32nd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, I respectfully extend my warm congratulations to you. I wish the socialist construction of the Soviet Union ever greater development, the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union unparalleled strength, and the people of China and the Soviet Union eternal friendship and cooperation.

　　　　　　　　　　　 Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 5, 1949

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 5th. Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Vice Presidents Soong Ching Ling, Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Zhang Lan, and Huang Yanpei, today sent a telegram to Professor Tizov, Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, congratulating him on the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution. The telegram stated:

Professor Tizov, Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries:

The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association warmly celebrates the 32nd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, and warmly celebrates the health of Marshal Stalin and the happiness of the Soviet people. The Chinese people feel particularly excited when celebrating this great festival this year, because we have victoriously established our own country, and the Soviet Union was the first to establish friendly and cooperative diplomatic relations with our country, and gave important assistance to our construction work. May the people of China and the Soviet Union cooperate forever in defending world peace and building their own happy lives to achieve even greater victories.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Liu Shaoqi
Vice Presidents Soong Ching Ling, Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Zhang Lan, Huang Yanpei
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 5, 1949

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 5th. Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, today sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin, celebrating the Soviet October Revolution Day. The telegram reads as follows:

Marshal Stalin: On this anniversary of the October Revolution, I, together with the liberated Chinese people, extend to you our warmest congratulations. Like Sun Yat-sen, we believe that the October Revolution was the birth of hope for mankind. Sun Yat-sen and Chairman Mao understood that only the Soviet Union truly desired the liberation of the Chinese people. Our current victory is based on this understanding. The close cooperation between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union, which Sun Yat-sen dreamed of, has now become a reality. The arrival of the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific delegation in China is a manifestation of this cooperation. We thank the delegation for giving us many benefits and helping us to resolutely follow the example pioneered by the Soviet Union in building the country and improving the people's living standards and cultural standards. Long live the friendship, unity, and cooperation between the Chinese and Soviet people!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Soong Ching Ling

### 祝贺苏联十月革命三十二周年　毛主席电贺斯大林大元帅  中苏友协、宋庆龄副主席分电致贺

1949-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺苏联十月革命三十二周年
  　毛主席电贺斯大林大元帅
    中苏友协、宋庆龄副主席分电致贺
    【新华社北京五日电】毛泽东主席本日致电斯大林大元帅祝贺十月革命三十二周年电称：斯大林大元帅：
    值此伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年之际，我敬向阁下致热烈的祝贺。祝苏联的社会主义建设日益发展，祝苏联为首脑的世界和平民主阵营无比强大，祝中苏两国人民永远友好合作。
    　　　　　　　　　　　中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十一月五日
    【新华社北京五日电】中苏友好协会会长刘少奇，副会长宋庆龄、吴玉章、沈钧儒、李济深、郭沫若、张澜、黄炎培，本日致电苏联对外文化协会主席琴尼索夫教授，祝贺十月革命三十二周年。电称：苏联封外文化协会主席琴尼索夫教授：
    中苏友好协会热烈地庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年，热烈地庆祝斯大林大元帅的健康和苏联人民的幸福。中国人民在庆祝今年的这个伟大节日的时候特别感觉兴奋，因为我们已经胜利地建立了自己的国家，而苏联则首先与我国建立了友好合作的外交关系，并给了我们的建设工作以重要的援助。愿中苏两国人民在保卫世界和平与建设自己的幸福生活中永远合作，以取得更加伟大的胜利。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中苏友好协会会长　刘少奇
　副会长　宋庆龄、吴玉章、沈钧儒、李济深、郭沫若、张澜、黄炎培 　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十一月五日
    【新华社上海五日电】中央人民政府副主席宋庆龄本日致电斯大林大元帅，庆贺苏联十月革命节。电文如下：
    斯大林大元帅：在这个十月革命纪念日，我和解放了的中国人民，一致向您致以最热烈的祝贺。和孙中山一样，我们认为十月革命是人类希望底诞生。孙中山和毛主席了解，只有苏联是真正渴望中国人民获得解放的。我们现在的胜利，就是基于这样的认识。孙中山所梦寐以求的中苏两大民族底紧密合作，现在成为事实了。苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团的来到中国，是这种合作的表现。我们感谢代表团给我们很多的益处，帮助了我们怎样在建设国家，提高人民生活水准和文化水准中，坚决地追随苏联所首创的榜样。中苏人民友好、团结、合作万岁！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　宋庆龄
