Title: Government Administration Council Passes Appointment: Qin Tianzhen Appointed Mayor of Guiyang City
Author:
Date: 1949-11-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th—The sixth meeting of the Government Administration Council held today passed the appointment of Qin Tianzhen as Mayor of the People's Government of Guiyang City.

### 政务会议通过任命　秦天贞任贵阳市长

1949-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务会议通过任命
  　秦天贞任贵阳市长
    【新华社北京十八日电】今天举行的政务院第六次政务会议，通过任命秦天贞为贵阳市人民政府市长。
