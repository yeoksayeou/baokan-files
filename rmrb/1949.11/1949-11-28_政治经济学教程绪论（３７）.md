    Political Economy: Research Object, Tasks, and Characteristics
  　Chapter Three: The Tasks and Significance of Political Economy and the Chinese Revolution
    Section Three: Marxist-Leninist Political Economy and the Chinese Revolution

    In summary, Marxist-Leninist political economy is not only a weapon of knowledge and a weapon of criticism, but also a weapon of struggle and a weapon of construction, essential for all revolutionary work, and must be possessed by every revolutionary fighter and every revolutionary work cadre. In our daily revolutionary work, possessing and mastering it can enable us to recognize and understand the nature of society, and the nature, tasks, targets, and driving forces of the revolution determined by it. It can enable us not only to understand and grasp the Party's lines, principles, and policies, and correctly implement them, but also to have the knowledge, ability, and methods to easily combine with practice. When difficulties arise in our work, it can give us the knowledge, ability, methods, and courage to solve these difficulties based on the connection with practical situations. It can help our cadres engaged in practical work to summarize experience, generalize, systematize, organize, and rationalize practical work experience, and elevate it to the height of theory. Therefore, Marxist-Leninist political economy is indispensable scientific theoretical knowledge and a weapon of revolutionary struggle for our revolutionary military, political, economic, mass, cultural education, Party affairs, as well as foreign affairs, judicial, public security, and other entire revolutionary work, and all types of revolutionary work cadres.

### 政治经济学教程绪论（３７）

1949-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    政治经济学教程绪论（３７）
  　政治经济学的研究对象任务及其特点
  　第三章　政治经济学的任务意义与中国革命
    第三节　马列主义政治经济学与中国革命
    总而言之，马列主义政治经济学不仅是认识的武器，批判的武器，而且是斗争的武器，建设的武器，为一切革命工作所必需，为每一革命战士，每一革命工作干部所必须具备，在日常的革命工作中，我们具备了它掌握了它，可以使我们认识与了解社会的性质，与由此而决定的革命的性质，任务，对象，动力，可以使我们不但能理解与掌握党的路线方针政策，并能正确地执行它，而且可以有知识有能力有方法，便于与实践结合，当工作发生困难的时候，可以使我们有知识有能力有方法有勇气，根据与实践情况的联系，去解决困难；它可以帮助我们实际工作的干部，能够总结经验，把实际工作的经验概括起来，系统化，条理化，原则化，提高到理论的高度。所以马列主义政治经济学对于我们革命的军事、政治、经济、群众、文化教育、党务以及外交司法，公安等等整个革命工作，所有各种革命工作干部，都是不可缺少的科学理论知识与革命斗争武器。
