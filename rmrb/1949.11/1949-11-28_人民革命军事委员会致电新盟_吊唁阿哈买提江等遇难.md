Title: People's Revolutionary Military Commission Sends Condolences to New League on the Death of Akhmetjan Kasimi and Others

Author:

Date: 1949-11-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 27th. The People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy League, expressing condolences on the death of Akhmetjan Kasimi, Vice Chairman of Xinjiang Province, and others. The original telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy League, dear comrades:

Comrade Akhmetjan Kasimi, Vice Chairman of Xinjiang Province, Comrade Ishaq Beg, Commander-in-Chief of the National Army of the three districts of Ili, Ashan, and Tacheng in Xinjiang Province, Comrade Abduk Kerim, Member of the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy League, Comrade Dalelkhan, Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the National Army, and Comrade Luo Zhi of the Xinjiang China-Soviet Cultural Association, unfortunately died in a plane crash on their way to Beijing. This is a huge loss for the people of Xinjiang and the people of China. We express our deep condolences to you.

The People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, November 26th.

### 人民革命军事委员会致电新盟　吊唁阿哈买提江等遇难

1949-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民革命军事委员会致电新盟
  　吊唁阿哈买提江等遇难
    【新华社北京二十七日电】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会致电新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员会，吊唁新疆省副主席阿哈买提江等的遇难。原电如下：
    新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员会亲爱的同志们：
    新疆省副主席阿哈买提江同志，新疆省伊犁、阿山、塔城三区民族军总指挥伊斯哈克伯克同志，新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员阿不都克里木同志，民族军副总指挥达列尔汉同志，和新疆中苏文化协会的罗志同志不幸在来京途中因飞机失事遇难，这是新疆人民和中国人民的巨大损失。敬向你们致沉痛的哀悼。
    中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会　十一月二十六日
