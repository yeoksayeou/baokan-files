Title: Second Beijing Opera Training Class to Hold Opening Ceremony Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-11-30
Page: 3

Second Beijing Opera Training Class to Hold Opening Ceremony Tomorrow

[Beijing News] The opening ceremony of the second Beijing opera training class in this city is scheduled to be held at 9:00 AM on December 1st at the Democratic Theater. A total of more than 1,300 people have registered for this class. The model students from the first class will also participate in continued learning, and Shang Xiaoyun, Xun Huisheng, Tan Fuying, Xiao Cuihua and others have also registered.

### 京戏曲界二届讲习班　明日举行开学典礼

1949-11-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　京戏曲界二届讲习班
  　明日举行开学典礼
    【北京讯】本市戏曲界第二届讲习班开学典礼，定于十二月一日上午九时在民主剧场举行。这次报名者共计一千三百余人。第一届模范学员亦均参加继续学习，尚小云、荀慧生、谭富英、筱翠花等也都已报名。
