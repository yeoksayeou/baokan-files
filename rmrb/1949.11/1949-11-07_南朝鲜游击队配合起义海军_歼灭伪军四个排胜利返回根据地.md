Title: South Korean Guerrillas, Coordinating with Uprising Navy, Annihilate Four Platoons of Puppet Troops and Return Victoriously to Base Area
Author:
Date: 1949-11-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th—The People's Guerrilla force that entered Jinju, Gyeongsangnam-do on October 27th, together with the uprising naval forces, after routing the reinforcing enemy troops, annihilated four platoons of the 3rd company of the 22nd battalion of the puppet "National Defense Army" in the Michen district of the mountainous area northeast of Jinju. They seized a number of weapons, including armored vehicles, mortars, and light and heavy machine guns, and also destroyed the puppet police station in the area. The People's Guerrillas and the uprising Navy have now victoriously returned to the Jiri Mountain base area.

### 南朝鲜游击队配合起义海军　歼灭伪军四个排胜利返回根据地

1949-11-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜游击队配合起义海军
  　歼灭伪军四个排胜利返回根据地
    【新华社平壤六日电】十月二十七日攻入庆尚南道晋州市的人民游击队与起义的海军部队，在击溃增援敌军之后，复于晋州东北山岳地带的美川区歼灭伪“国防军”第二十二营三连的四个排，缴获装甲车、迫击炮、轻重机枪等武器一部，并将该区伪敬察署捣毁。现人民游击队和起义的海军，已胜利返回智异山根据地。
