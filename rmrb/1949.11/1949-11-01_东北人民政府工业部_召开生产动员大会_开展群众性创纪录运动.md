Title: Northeast People's Government Industry Department Convenes Production Mobilization Conference to Launch Mass Emulation Drive to Break Records
Author:
Date: 1949-11-01
Page: 2

　　Northeast People's Government Industry Department
  　Convenes Production Mobilization Conference
  　Launches Mass Emulation Drive to Break Records
    [Shenyang News] Launching a mass emulation drive to break records, continuing to implement economic accounting, and initiating a high tide of economic construction are the current central tasks of industrial and mining enterprises in Northeast China. In order to unify thinking and pace, and to complete this glorious political task, the Industry Department of the Northeast People's Government specially convened a mobilization conference on the 10th of last month for cadres and activists from the Party, the Youth League, the administration, and the trade unions of its subordinate bureaus, companies, and factories in Shenyang. A total of 45 units and more than 1,100 people attended. At the meeting, they listened to reports from Comrade Li Fuchun and Minister Wang Heshou and Deputy Minister Lü Dong of the Industry Department, and decided to conduct in-depth communication in various factories to mobilize the broad masses of workers and staff, unleash new labor enthusiasm, and strive to break existing production quotas, increase production, and reduce costs.

### 东北人民政府工业部　召开生产动员大会　开展群众性创纪录运动

1949-11-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北人民政府工业部
  　召开生产动员大会
  　开展群众性创纪录运动
    【沈阳讯】开展群众性的创纪录运动，继续贯彻经济核算制，掀起经济建设的高潮，这是东北各工矿企业当前的中心工作。为了统一思想与步调，完成这一光荣的政治任务，东北人民政府工业部特于上月十日，召集所属各局各公司及沈阳各工厂党、团、行政、工会干部及积极分子的动员大会，出席共四十五个单位，一千一百余人。会上听取了李富春同志及工业部部长王鹤寿、副部长吕东的报告，并决定在各厂作深入传达，以动员广大职工，发挥新的劳动热情，为打破现有生产定额，提高生产，减低成本而奋斗。
