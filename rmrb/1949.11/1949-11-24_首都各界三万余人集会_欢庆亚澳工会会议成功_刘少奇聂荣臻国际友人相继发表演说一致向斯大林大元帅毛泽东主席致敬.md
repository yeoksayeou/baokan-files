Title: Over 30,000 People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Gather to Celebrate the Success of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference; Liu Shaoqi, Nie Rongzhen, and International Friends Deliver Speeches One After Another, Unanimously Paying Respects to Marshal Stalin and Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1949-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 23rd: Over 30,000 organized masses from all walks of life in the capital gathered in front of the Hall of Supreme Harmony in the Forbidden City this morning to hold a grand meeting celebrating the successful conclusion of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. The masses who went to the meeting held high fighting banners such as "Salute to the World Federation of Trade Unions," "Workers of Asia and Australia Unite," "Struggle to Defend World Peace and Democracy," and "Workers of All Countries, Unite!" Members of the Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions and representatives from various countries entered the venue amidst thunderous applause from the masses present. At 10:30 AM, the celebration officially began, with the military band playing the Chinese National Anthem and The Internationale. The congress elected twelve people to the presidium, including Liu Shaoqi, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Nie Rongzhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal Government; Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; and Xiao Ming, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Trade Union. Xiao Ming, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Trade Union, delivered the opening speech on behalf of the presidium. Liu Shaoqi, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Nie Rongzhen, Mayor of Beijing, delivered speeches successively. In his brief opening speech, Xiao Ming earnestly pointed out that the working class and people from all walks of life in Beijing are holding this grand celebration with great excitement and joy to celebrate the success of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference held in Beijing. This Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference marks the great unity of the working class of Asia and Australia and the world working class, and marks the victory and development of the struggle of the working people of the world against imperialism and its running dogs.

　　International friends attending the celebration included World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Saillant, World Federation of Trade Unions Vice-Chairman Kuznetsov representing Solovyov, Toledano, Abdullah, as well as Iranian representative Shad, Indonesian representative Mazono, Indian representative Sherfanka, Vietnamese representative Liu Defu, Korean representative Choi Kyung-duk, and Mongolian representative Adili Bish, who all delivered speeches amidst warm applause. They unanimously pointed out that the success of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference is a serious blow to British and American imperialism, and the forces of world peace and democracy will be greatly strengthened as a result. The working class of the world, especially the working class of Asia and Australia, will be further united through this conference, and will therefore more fully realize that their strength is far greater than that of the enemy. In their speeches, the representatives of various countries all specially mentioned that the people of all countries are not isolated in their struggle against imperialism and for world peace and democracy. The socialist Soviet Union is a strong fortress of world peace, and the people's democratic countries all over the world, especially the People's Republic of China, which has already achieved great victory in the battle against American imperialism and its running dog, the Kuomintang reactionaries, will be a powerful force in winning world peace and democracy. Therefore, the trade union representatives from various countries all expressed their sincere respect to the great teacher of mankind, Marshal Stalin, and the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong.

　　The congress passed a congratulatory telegram to the Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions to celebrate the success of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. Before the adjournment, the congress also presented a banner to the World Federation of Trade Unions and flowers to the members of the Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions.

　　Tonight, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions hosted a banquet for the members of the Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions and trade union representatives from Asian and Australian countries.

### 首都各界三万余人集会　欢庆亚澳工会会议成功  刘少奇聂荣臻国际友人相继发表演说一致向斯大林大元帅毛泽东主席致敬

1949-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　首都各界三万余人集会
　　欢庆亚澳工会会议成功
　　刘少奇聂荣臻国际友人相继发表演说一致向斯大林大元帅毛泽东主席致敬
    【新华社北京二十三日电】首都各界三万余有组织的群众，今日上午在故宫太和殿前，举行盛大集会，欢庆亚洲澳洲工会会议的胜利成功。前往开会的群众高举着“向世界工联致敬”、“亚澳各国工人阶级团结起来”、“为保卫世界和平民主而斗争”、“全世界无产阶级团结起来”等战斗的旗帜。世界工联执行局的委员们和各国代表在到会群众山洪般的鼓掌声中进入会场。十时半，庆祝大会正式开始，军乐队奏起中国国歌和国际歌。大会推选中华全国总工会名誉主席刘少奇、中国共产党北京市委会书记彭真、北京市人民政府市长聂荣臻、中华全国总工会副主席李立三、中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬、北京市职工总会筹委会主任萧明等十二人为主席团。北京市职工总会筹委会主任萧明代表主席团致开会词，中华全国总工会名誉主席刘少奇和北京市市长聂荣臻先后发表演说。萧明在简短的开会词中恳切指出：北京的工人阶级和各界人民以极大的兴奋和愉快的心情来开这个盛大的庆祝会，庆祝在北京召开的亚澳工会会议的成功。这个亚澳工会会议，标志着亚澳工人阶级和世界工人阶级的大团结，标志着全世界劳动人民反帝国主义及其走狗的斗争的胜利与发展。
    参加庆祝大会的国际友人世界工联总书记赛扬、世界工联副主席库兹涅佐夫代表索罗维耶夫、托里达诺、阿勃杜拉及伊朗代表沙德、印尼代表马佐诺、印度代表谢尔凡卡、越南代表刘德福、朝鲜代表崔璟德、蒙古代表阿德利比什等均在热烈的掌声中发表演说。他们一致指出：亚澳工会会议的成功，对于英美帝国主义是一个严重的打击，全世界和平民主力量将因此而大为加强，全世界的工人阶级，特别是亚洲和澳洲的工人阶级，将通过这个大会而进一步团结起来，并因此而更加认识到自己的力量远较敌人的为强大。各国代表在演说中都特别提到各国人民在反对帝国主义和争取世界和平民主的斗争中不是狐立的。社会主义的苏联是世界和平力量的强大堡垒，全世界各人民民主国家，特别是在反对美帝国主义及其走狗国民党反动派战斗中已经取得伟大胜利的中华人民共和国，将是赢得世界和平民主的强大力量。因此，来自各个国家的工会代表们，都表示向世界人类伟大导师斯大林大元帅及中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东主席致诚挚的敬意。
    大会通过了为庆祝亚澳工会会议成功而给世界工联执行局的贺电。在散会前，大会并向世界工联献旗，向世界工联执行局各委员献花。
    今日晚，中华全国总工会欢宴世界工会联合会执行局各委员及亚澳各国工会代表。
