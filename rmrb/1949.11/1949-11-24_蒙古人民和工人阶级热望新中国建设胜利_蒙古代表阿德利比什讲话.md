Title: Mongolian People and Working Class Eager for New China's Construction Victory - Speech by Mongolian Representative Adili Bish
Author:
Date: 1949-11-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd: Speech by Mongolian representative Adili Bish at the Beijing People's Conference celebrating the success of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference, the full text is as follows:

　　On behalf of the people of the Mongolian People's Republic, I extend my heartfelt and warm greetings to the working people of Beijing and the entire Chinese people! At the same time, on behalf of the Mongolian people, I extend my warmest greetings to the Chinese working class and the Chinese Communist Party and its great leader, Comrade Mao Zedong.

　　The victory of the Chinese people over the imperialists and their running dogs, the Kuomintang reactionaries, is a good example for the oppressed people of Asian and Australian countries in their struggle for freedom and liberation, and a great and good example for the people of colonial and semi-colonial countries to learn from. The victory of the Chinese people is of great significance to the liberation struggle of the oppressed people all over the world. People all over the world warmly praise the great victory of the Chinese people. The people of the Mongolian People's Republic are overjoyed by your victory. Your victory has further encouraged us to work hard to build our own motherland. The Mongolian people and the working class hope that under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao, the Chinese people will achieve great victories in construction in the shortest possible time, and at the same time hope that the Chinese people will thoroughly eliminate the remaining forces of the enemy and complete their great historical mission.

　　Long live the great friendship and unity between the Chinese and Mongolian people!

　　Long live the People's Republic of China!

　　Long live the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao!

　　Long live Stalin, the great teacher of the working people of the world!

### 蒙古人民和工人阶级热望新中国建设胜利　蒙古代表阿德利比什讲话

1949-11-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民和工人阶级热望新中国建设胜利
　　蒙古代表阿德利比什讲话
    【新华社北京二十三日电】蒙古代表阿德利比什在北京各界人民庆祝亚澳工会会议成功大会上的讲话，全文如下：
    我代表蒙古人民共和国人民向北京的劳动人民和全中国人民致以衷心的热烈的敬礼！同时代表蒙古人民向中国工人阶级和中国共产党及其伟大的领袖毛泽东同志致最热烈的敬礼。
    中国人民战胜了帝国主义者及其走狗国民党反动派，这对于亚澳各国被压迫人民为争取自由解放的斗争，是一个很好的榜样，是殖民地和半殖民地的人民要学习的伟大的很好的榜样。中国人民的胜利，对于全世界被压迫人民的解放斗争，有着重大的意义。全世界人民都热烈地赞扬中国人民的伟大胜利。蒙古人民共和国的人民因为你们的胜利而欢欣鼓舞。你们的胜利更鼓舞了我们去努力建设我们自己的祖国。蒙古的人民和工人阶级，希望中国人民在英明的毛主席领导之下，在最短期内，在建设上得到伟大胜利，同时希望中国人民根本肃清敌人的残余势力，完成伟大的历史使命。
    中国与蒙古人民的伟大友谊团结万岁！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席万岁！
    全世界劳动人民的伟大导师斯大林万岁！
