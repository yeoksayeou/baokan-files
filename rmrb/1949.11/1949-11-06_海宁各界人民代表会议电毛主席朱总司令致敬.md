Title: People's Representatives from All Circles in Haining Send Respects to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu
Author:
Date: 1949-11-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th: The People's Representatives Conference from all circles in Haining County, Zhejiang Province, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu expressing their respects. The telegram reads as follows:

Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu:

Thanks to your wise and correct leadership and the heroic and skillful fighting of the People's Liberation Army, with the support of the broad masses of the Chinese people, the People's Liberation Army liberated Haining, liberated Zhejiang, and liberated vast areas of China. Our 320,000 people in Haining County express infinite excitement and joy. Today, our 185 representatives from all circles in Haining are happily united to discuss the major issues of building a new Haining together. We resolutely support all the policy programs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, actively support the People's Liberation Army, thoroughly eliminate the Kuomintang remnant bandits, and liberate all of China. Finally, we, on behalf of all the people of Haining County, extend our highest revolutionary salute to you.

　　　　　　　　　　The People's Representatives Conference from all circles in Haining County kowtows
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 1st

### 海宁各界人民代表会议电毛主席朱总司令致敬

1949-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　海宁各界人民代表会议电毛主席朱总司令致敬
    【新华社北京五日电】浙江省海宁县各界人民代表会议致电毛主席、朱总司令致敬，电文如下：毛主席朱总司令：
    中国人民解放战争由于你们的英明正确领导，人民解放军的英勇善战，在中国广大人民的支援下解放了海宁，解放了浙江，解放了中国广大的地方。我们海宁县三十二万人民表示无限的兴奋和欢跃。今天我们海宁一百八十五个各界代表很愉快地团结在一起，共同商讨建设新海宁的大事。我们坚决的拥护中华人民共和国中央人民政府的一切施政纲领，并且积极地支援人民解放军，彻底消灭国民党残余匪帮，解放全中国。最后我们谨代表海宁县全体人民，向你们致以崇高的革命敬礼。
    　　　　　　　　浙江省海宁县各界人民代表会议叩
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月一日
