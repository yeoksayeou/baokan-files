Title: How is Bolshevik Leadership in Trade Union Work Achieved?
Author:
Date: 1949-11-30
Page: 4

　　*Labor Daily* Publishes Editorial
  　How is Bolshevik Leadership in Trade Union Work Achieved?
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 28th] TASS, Moscow: *Labor Daily* published an editorial on the 22nd entitled "Mastering Bolshevik Leadership," stating: Bolshevik trade union activity means the Bolshevik principles and determination of trade union cadres, and their skills in overcoming difficulties; it means comprehensively carrying out criticism and self-criticism, placing all of people's work under the supervision of the masses, bravely exposing shortcomings in practical work, finding their causes, and taking all methods to overcome them. Bolshevik principle means not tolerating deviations from the Communist Party's ideology and policies.

### 劳动报发表社论　怎样才是工会工作中布尔什维克式的领导？

1949-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　劳动报发表社论
  　怎样才是工会工作中布尔什维克式的领导？
    【新华社北京二十八日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：劳动报二十二日以“掌握布尔什维克式的领导”为题发表社论称：布尔什维克式的工会活动，意味着工会干部的布尔什维克原则性和坚决性，以及他们在克服困难上的技能；意味着全面地开展批评与自我批评，把人们的一切工作置于群众的监督之下，勇敢地暴露实际工作中的缺点，找出它们的原因并采取一切方法加以克服。布尔什维克的原则性，意味着不容忍脱离共产党思想与政策的偏向。
