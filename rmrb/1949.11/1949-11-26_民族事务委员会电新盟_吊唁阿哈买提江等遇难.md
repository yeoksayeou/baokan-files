The State Ethnic Affairs Commission Sends Condolences to Xinmeng on the Death of Ahemaitijiang and Others

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 25th -- The State Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy League, expressing condolences for the death of Ahemaitijiang, Vice Chairman of Xinjiang Province, and others. The original telegram is as follows:

Dear Comrades of the Central Committee of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy League:

The unfortunate death of Vice Chairman Ahemaitijiang, and Comrades Yisihake Burke, Abudukelimu, Dalieerhan, and Luo Zhi, who bravely fought for the cause of the liberation of the people of Xinjiang, is a great loss to the people of Xinjiang and the people of China. We extend our deepest condolences to you.

　　　　　　　　　　　State Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government
　　　　　　　　　　　　November 25th

### 民族事务委员会电新盟　吊唁阿哈买提江等遇难

1949-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　民族事务委员会电新盟
  　吊唁阿哈买提江等遇难
    【新华社北京二十五日电】中央人民政府民族事务委员会致电新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员会，吊唁新疆省副主席阿哈买提江等的遇难。原电如下：
    新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员会亲爱的同志们：
    为新疆人民解放事业英勇奋斗的阿哈买提江副主席及伊斯哈克伯克、阿不都克里木、达列尔汉、罗志诸同志的不幸遇难，是新疆人民和中国人民的一个巨大损失。敬向你们致沉痛的哀悼。
    　　　　　　　　　　　中央人民政府民族事务委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月二十五日
