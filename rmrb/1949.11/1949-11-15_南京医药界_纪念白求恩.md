Title: Nanjing Medical Community Commemorates Bethune
Author:
Date: 1949-11-15
Page: 4

    Nanjing Medical Community
    Commemorates Bethune
    [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 13th] More than one hundred Chinese and Western medicine doctors and medical workers in Nanjing gathered at the Health Bureau at 2:00 PM on the 12th to commemorate the tenth anniversary of the death of international friend and Canadian doctor Norman Bethune. Song Chao, Deputy Director of the Health Bureau, called on the Nanjing medical community to learn from Dr. Bethune's great internationalist spirit, strengthen political study, improve work style, and enhance professional skills. Today, all newspapers published Dr. Bethune's biography and commemorative texts. The Xinhua Daily also published Chairman Mao's article "Learn from Comrade Bethune," written ten years ago.

### 南京医药界  纪念白求恩

1949-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    南京医药界
    纪念白求恩
    【新华社南京十三日电】南京市中西医师及医务工作人员百余人，十二日下午二时在卫生局集会纪念国际友人加拿大医师诺尔曼·白求恩逝世十周年。卫生局宋潮副局长在会上号召南京医药界学习白求恩医师伟大的国际主义精神，加强政治学习，改进工作作风，提高业务水平。今天各报都载有白求恩医师的简历及纪念文字，新华日报并发表了毛主席在十年前所写“学习白求恩”一文。
