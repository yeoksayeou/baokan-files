Title: Professor Dragunov Completes Chinese Grammar Work
Author:
Date: 1949-11-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th: Leningrad dispatch: Professor Alexander Dragunov of the Institute of Oriental Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has completed a work on the grammar of modern Chinese, which cites many examples from the modern national language of the Chinese people and the pamphlets and slogans of the People's Liberation Army. This book will serve as teaching material for Soviet institutions of higher education.

### 德拉古诺夫教授  完成中文文法著作

1949-11-12
第3版()
专栏：

    德拉古诺夫教授
    完成中文文法著作
    【新华社北京十日电】列宁格勒讯：苏联科学院东方学研究所的亚历山大·德拉古诺夫教授业已完成一种关于现代中国语文文法的著作，该书从中国人民现代国语和人民解放军的小册子、标语宣言中引用许多实例。此书将作为苏联高等教育机关的教材。
