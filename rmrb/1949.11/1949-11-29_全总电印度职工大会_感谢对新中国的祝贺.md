Title: ACFTU Sends Telegram to All-India Trade Union Congress, Thanking Them for Congratulating New China
Author:
Date: 1949-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 27th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the All-India Trade Union Congress to thank them for congratulating the founding of the People's Republic of China. The full text is as follows:

To the All-India Trade Union Congress and all the laboring brothers in India:

We are immensely honored and excited to receive your congratulatory telegram on the founding of the People's Republic of China. This congratulation comes from a large country with a population of over 300 million. The combined population of our two countries is nearly half of the world's population. The unity and struggle of the working classes and laboring people of China and India play a decisive role in safeguarding lasting world peace and people's democracy. On behalf of the Chinese working class and laboring people, we extend our highest respect and warmest gratitude to the All-India working class and laboring people who are bravely and arduously fighting against British and American imperialism and their agents.

Long live the All-India Trade Union Congress!

Long live the unity of the Chinese and Indian working classes!

Long live the victory of the Indian working class and laboring people's struggle for liberation!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 26th

### 全总电印度职工大会　感谢对新中国的祝贺

1949-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总电印度职工大会
  　感谢对新中国的祝贺
    【新华社北京二十七日电】中华全国总工会为全印度职工大会对中华人民共和国诞生的祝贺，复电致谢，全文如下：全印职工大会和全印度的劳动弟兄们：
    我们收到你们庆贺中华人民共和国诞生的贺电，感到无限的光荣与兴奋。因为这个祝贺是从一个拥有三万万以上人口的大国发来的，我们这两个国家的人口加起来就将近全世界人口的半数。中印两国工人阶级与劳动人民的团结与斗争，对于保卫世界持久和平与人民民主有着决定的作用。我们谨代表中国工人阶级与劳动人民向正在艰苦地英勇地进行着反对英美帝国主义及其代理人的斗争的全印工人阶级与劳动人民表示崇高的敬礼与热烈的感谢。
    全印职工大会万岁！
    中印工人阶级团结万岁！
    印度工人阶级及劳动人民争取解放斗争的胜利万岁！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国总工会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月二十六日
