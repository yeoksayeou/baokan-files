Title: Mankind Uses Atomic Energy to Transform Nature for the First Time: Soviet Union Diverts the Ob and Yenisei Rivers to Turn Central Asian Deserts into Fertile Fields
Author:
Date: 1949-11-19
Page: 3

　　Mankind Uses Atomic Energy to Transform Nature for the First Time
　　Soviet Union Diverts the Ob and Yenisei Rivers to Turn Central Asian Deserts into Fertile Fields
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th: TASS reports: The Soviet Union has begun, for the first time in human history, to use atomic energy for the peaceful construction work of benefiting mankind. Vyshinsky recently announced at the United Nations Political Committee that the Soviet Union is, with the help of atomic energy, "leveling mountains, irrigating deserts, and cleaving through jungles... spreading life, joy, prosperity, and happiness to the wilderness where humans have rarely set foot for thousands of years." A recent special article in the Soviet Literary Gazette announced that the Soviet Union is implementing such a great plan to transform nature, which is: to divert the two longest rivers in the world—the Siberian Ob River and Yenisei River—thereby turning 30 million hectares of barren desert land in Central Asia into fertile cotton fields, vineyards, and gardens, the abundance of which will be beyond all dreams. At the same time, the hydropower locks built along these two new river channels will be able to generate 82 billion kilowatt-hours of cheap electricity annually, which can supply all the electricity for the heavy industrial area of ​​southern Ural. The greatest difficulty in realizing this plan is to excavate new river channels through the Kazakh mountains. This project would be impossible without the most modern technology in science. The Soviet Union is now carrying out this project with the help of atomic energy. The creator of this huge plan is the engineer Davydov.

### 人类第一次用原子能改造自然　苏联将鄂毕叶尼塞两河改道使中央亚细亚沙漠变为肥田

1949-11-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　人类第一次用原子能改造自然
  　苏联将鄂毕叶尼塞两河改道使中央亚细亚沙漠变为肥田
    【新华社北京十八日电】据电通社讯：苏联已开始在人类历史上第一次把原子能用于为人类造福的和平建设事业。维辛斯基最近曾在联合国政治委员会上宣布说：苏联正在原子能帮助之下“削平山岳，灌溉沙漠，劈开丛林……把生命、快乐、繁荣与幸福散布于数千年人迹不到的荒野”。最近苏联文学报发表的一篇专文，宣布了苏联正在实行这样一个改造自然的伟大计划，这个计划就是：使全世界最长河流中的两条河流——西伯利亚的鄂毕河与叶尼塞河改道，从而使中亚细亚三千万公顷不毛之地的沙漠变为肥沃的棉田、葡萄园和花园，其收成之丰硕将是从来所梦想不到的。同时，沿着这两条新河道建立的动力水闸，每年将能发出八百二十亿千瓦时的廉价电力，可以供给乌拉尔南部重工业区的全部电力。实现这一计划的最大困难，是要穿越哈萨山脉开凿新河道。除非使用科学上最新式的技术，这一工程将是不可能的。苏联现正借原子能之助，进行着这一工程。这个巨大计划的创造者是工程师达维多夫。
