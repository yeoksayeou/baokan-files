Title: Marshal Stalin Cables Chairman Mao, Thanking Him for Friendly Greetings on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1949-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 26th: Chairman Mao Zedong sent a congratulatory telegram to Marshal Stalin on the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution. A reply telegram has now been received from Marshal Stalin. The full text of the reply telegram is as follows:

To Chairman Mao Zedong, Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

I sincerely thank you for your friendly greetings and heartfelt wishes on the 32nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Joseph Stalin

### 斯大林大元帅电复毛主席　感谢对十月革命节的友谊祝贺

1949-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　斯大林大元帅电复毛主席
  　感谢对十月革命节的友谊祝贺
    【新华社北京二十六日电】毛泽东主席于十月革命三十二周年纪念时曾致电斯大林大元帅祝贺，现已接到斯大林大元帅的复电。复电全文如下：北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席：
    衷心感谢阁下对于伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年纪念的友谊的祝贺和新切的愿望。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　约瑟夫·斯大林
