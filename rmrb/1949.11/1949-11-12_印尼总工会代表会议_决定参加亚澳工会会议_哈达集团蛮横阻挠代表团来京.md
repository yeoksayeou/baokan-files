Title: Indonesian Central Labor Union Congress Decides to Participate in the Asian-Australasian Labor Union Conference; Hatta Group Barracks Viciously Obstructs Delegation from Coming to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-11-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 11th: TASS, The Hague, 9th: It has been learned here that the Hatta Group is doing everything possible to obstruct the delegation of the Indonesian Central Labor Union, which announced its willingness to participate in the Asian-Australasian Labor Union Conference, from going to Beijing.

According to a United Press report from Yogyakarta on October 31st, the Indonesian Central Labor Union (a member of the World Federation of Trade Unions) decided at its general congress that it would try to send representatives to the Asian-Australasian Labor Union Conference to be held in Beijing in mid-November. The conference also passed a resolution appealing to the WFTU to support Indonesia's struggle for independence by demanding the release of political prisoners, the withdrawal of Dutch troops in Indonesia, and the passage of legislation guaranteeing the rights of Indonesian trade unions. The resolution expressed welcome to the WFTU to send representatives to Indonesia to investigate the life of labor, and to strengthen the links between the Indonesian Central Labor Union and trade unions in other countries.

### 印尼总工会代表会议  决定参加亚澳工会会议  哈达集团蛮横阻挠代表团来京

1949-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼总工会代表会议
    决定参加亚澳工会会议
    哈达集团蛮横阻挠代表团来京
    【新华社北京十一日电】塔斯社海牙九日讯：此间获悉，哈达集团尽其一切可能阻挠宣布愿意参加亚澳工会会议的印尼总工会代表团前往北京。
    另据合众社日惹十月三十一日电称，印尼总工会（世界工会联合会会员）在全体代表会议上决定，它将设法派遣代表参加本月中旬在北京召开的亚澳工会会议。该会并通过决议，吁请世界工联以要求释放政治犯、要求撤退在印尼的荷兰军队以及要求通过保障印尼工会权利法案等，来支持印尼争取独立的斗争。决议表示欢迎世界工联派遣代表赴印尼调查劳工生活，并加强印尼总工会与世界各国工会之间的联系。
