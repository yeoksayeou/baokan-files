Title: Wuzhou Southeast Liberated Fengchuan; 2,000 Remnant Bandits in Western Hunan Surrender to Our Army; Annihilation of the Xiangnan Column Headquarters of Bandits West of Hengyang
Author:
Date: 1949-11-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, South China Front, 6th: A unit of the South China People's Liberation Army liberated Fengchuan County Town, 90 *li* southeast of Wuzhou, on the 5th, capturing more than 150 enemy security troops.

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 6th: The remaining Kuomintang bandit troops in western Hunan have surrendered to the People's Liberation Army with their weapons. Wang Yuanhua, deputy commander of the bandit Provisional Second Army formerly stationed in the Yongshun area, Xiao Yangzhi, deputy commander of the Provisional 5th Division, and the remnants of the bandit 208th Division, and Peng Yao, commander of the 3rd detachment of the bandit Zhijiang Garrison Command, with a total of more than 800 men, surrendered to the People's Liberation Army on the 24th and 26th of last month, respectively. More than 1,000 men from the bandit Provisional Division of the Provisional 2nd Army in the Chenxi area also surrendered to the Liberation Army at the end of last month, led by Division Commander Bai Yusheng. The above surrendered troops brought with them a total of 5 six-zero mortars, 4 mortars, more than 90 light and heavy machine guns, and more than 1,000 rifles. In addition, 109 bandits, including the head of the Huitong Self-Defense Force, also surrendered their weapons on the 4th of this month.

Furthermore, a unit of the Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army annihilated the headquarters of the bandit Hunan Provincial National Salvation Army Xiangnan Column in Tuziqiao, west of Hengyang, on the 22nd of last month while pursuing and suppressing bandits who were wandering in the area. Dozens of bandits were captured, including the bandit Major General Commander and concurrently Hengshan County Chief Zuo Dajian, and the bandit Battalion Commander Guan Zuolin, and one radio station was seized.

### 梧州东南解放封川　湘西残匪二千人向我军投诚　衡阳西歼匪湘南纵队司令部

1949-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　梧州东南解放封川
  　湘西残匪二千人向我军投诚
  　衡阳西歼匪湘南纵队司令部
    【新华社华南前线六日电】华南人民解放军某部于五日解放梧州东南九十华里之封川县城，俘敌保安团一百五十余名。
    【新华社汉口六日电】残留湘西的国民党匪军纷纷携械向人民解放军投诚。原驻永顺地区的匪暂编第二军副军长汪援华、暂五师副师长萧仰之及匪二○八师残部、匪芷江警备司令部三支队长彭耀以下共八百余人，分别于上月二十四、二十六两日向人民解放军投诚。辰溪地区匪暂二军暂编师千余人亦于上月底在师长白玉盛率领下向解放军投诚。以上各投诚部队共携来六零炮五门，迫击炮四门，轻重机枪九十余挺，步枪千余支。另匪会同自卫队长以下一百零九人亦于本月四日缴械投降。
    又人民解放军第四野战军某部于上月二十二日在衡阳以西兔子桥追剿流窜该地的匪湖南省救国军湘南纵队，将匪司令部一举扑灭。俘匪少将司令兼衡山县长左大笺、匪大队长关作霖等数十名，缴获电台一部。
