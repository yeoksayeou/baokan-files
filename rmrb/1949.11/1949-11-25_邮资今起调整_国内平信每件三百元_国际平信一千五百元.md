Title: Postage Adjusted Today: Domestic Regular Mail 300 Yuan Per Piece, International Regular Mail 1,500 Yuan

Author:

Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 2

　　Postage Adjusted Today
　　Domestic Regular Mail 300 Yuan Per Piece
　　International Regular Mail 1,500 Yuan
    [Beijing News] The Beijing Postal Administration has been instructed to adjust domestic and international postage rates from today (the 25th) as follows:
    (A) Domestic Postage:
    (1) General Rates – Letters: 150 yuan for every 20 grams (6 mace by the market scale) for local mail, 300 yuan for domestic inter-provincial mail; Postcards: 100 yuan for local mail, 150 yuan for domestic inter-provincial mail; Newspaper articles: 150 yuan for every 20 grams for local mail, 250 yuan for domestic inter-provincial mail.
    (2) Special Rates – Registered mail: 900 yuan extra per piece in addition to the regular rate; Double-registered mail: 1,800 yuan extra per piece in addition to the regular rate; Priority mail: 300 yuan extra per piece in addition to the regular rate; Express registered mail: 1,200 yuan extra per piece in addition to the regular rate; Return receipt fee: 900 yuan per receipt; Inquiry fee: 1,200 yuan per inquiry; Poste restante fee: 300 yuan per piece; Change of address and mail recall application fee: 1,200 yuan per application.
    (B) International Postage:
    (1) General Rates – Letters: 1,500 yuan for every 20 grams (6 mace by the market scale); Postcards: 750 yuan.
    (2) Special Rates – Registered mail: 4,500 yuan extra per piece in addition to the regular rate; Double-registered mail: 9,000 yuan extra; Priority mail: 1,500 yuan extra; Express registered mail: 6,000 yuan extra; Return receipt: 4,500 yuan per receipt; Inquiry fee: 6,000 yuan per inquiry.

### 邮资今起调整　国内平信每件三百元　国际平信一千五百元

1949-11-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　邮资今起调整
　　国内平信每件三百元
　　国际平信一千五百元
    【京市讯】北京邮政管理局顷奉令自今日（二十五日）起将国内及国际邮资调整如下：
    （甲）国内邮资：
    （一）一般资费——信函每重二十公分（市秤六钱），本埠者一五○元，国内互寄者三○○元；明信片本埠者一○○元，国内互寄者一五○元；新闻稿件每重二十公分，本埠者一五○元，国内互寄者二五○元。
    （二）特种资费——单挂号除普通资费外每件另加九○○元，双挂号除普通资费外每件另加一，八○○元，平快信除普通资费外每件另加三○○元，快递挂号除普通资费外每件另加一，二○○元，回执费每个九○○元，查询费每次一，二○○元，存局候领费每件三○○元，更改地址与撤回邮件申请费每件一，二○○元。
    （乙）国际邮资：
    （一）一般资费——信函每重二十公分（市秤六钱）一，五○○元，明信片七五○元。
    （二）特种资费——单挂号除普通资费外，每件另加四，五○○元，双挂号另加九，○○○元，平快费另加一，五○○元，快递挂号另加六，○○○元，回执每个四，五○○元，查询费每次六，○○○元。
