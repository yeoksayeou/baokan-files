Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, List of Council Members Determined, Qian Junrui Appointed as Secretary-General
Author:
Date: 1949-11-11
Page: 1

    Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters
    List of Council Members Determined
    Qian Junrui Appointed as Secretary-General
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] According to its charter, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters has designated one Secretary-General, seven Deputy Secretaries-General, and twenty-nine Council Members, as nominated by the President and Vice-Presidents. The list of Council Members is as follows:
Secretary-General: Qian Junrui
Deputy Secretaries-General: Xiao San, Li Dehui, Shen Zhiyuan, Yan Baohang, Lai Yali, Luo Shuzhang, Ding Xiu
Council Members: Cao Yu, Huan Xiang, Cao Jinghua, Fan Hong, Xu Guangping, Zhang Qinqiu, Zhang Zhongshi, Ding Zan, Ding Xielin, Wang Zhixiang, Wang Kunlun, Xin Zhichao, Chen Jiakang, Qian Sanqiang, Li Bozhao, Cao Mengjun, Fu Xuewen, Hu Jibang, Guang Weiran, Jin Shan, Fei Xiaotong, Fei Qing, Li Shaopeng, Weng Dujian, Shu Yi, Pan Defeng, Pei Wenzhong, Wu Qingyou, Dai Ailian
