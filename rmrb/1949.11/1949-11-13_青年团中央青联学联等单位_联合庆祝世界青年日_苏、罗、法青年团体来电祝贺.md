Title: Youth League Central Committee, All-China Youth Federation, All-China Student Federation and other units jointly celebrate World Youth Day; Soviet, Romanian and French youth organizations send congratulatory messages

Author:
Date: 1949-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th. The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, the All-China Student Federation, the Youth League Central Committee School, the China Youth Art Theatre and other units jointly held a conference today to celebrate World Youth Day and World Student Week. Over 900 people attended the conference. Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Democratic Youth Federation, delivered the opening speech. He pointed out: To celebrate World Youth Day and World Student Week, it is necessary to strengthen the education of internationalism and new patriotism. Then Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, spoke. He emphasized that we must care about the lives and struggles of democratic youth around the world and support them with practical work and actions. At the same time, we must redouble our efforts in various construction projects in the New China to celebrate World Youth Day and World Student Week.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th. The Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, the Romanian Labor Youth League, and the National Committee of the French Republican Youth Federation sent telegrams to the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League on World Youth Day (this month's 10th) to express their congratulations. The texts of the three telegrams are as follows:

(1) Congratulatory telegram from the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee

To the China New Democratic Youth League: On the occasion of World Youth Day, the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee extends warm fraternal greetings to you, our dear friends. Soviet youth are sincerely excited about your historic victory over domestic and foreign reactionary forces and your great success in establishing the People's Republic of China. The historic victory won by the great Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and its Chairman Mao Zedong has made a tremendous contribution to the cause of further consolidating the democratic peace and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union. Soviet youth firmly believe that millions of young men and women in People's Democratic China will tirelessly develop ties of friendship and cooperation with progressive youth all over the world, united in the ranks of the World Federation of Democratic Youth. From Soviet youth, you have the most sincere and reliable friends.

Long live the unbreakable friendship between Chinese and Soviet youth!
      Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee
      November 11th

(2) Congratulatory telegram from the Romanian Labor Youth League

On the occasion of World Youth Day, we, in the name of the Romanian People's Republic Labor Youth League, extend our comradely respects to you and wish the heroic youth of the People's Republic of China, who are actively fighting for the victory of democracy, peace, and socialism, success in their struggles.
      Florescu
      November 10th

(3) Congratulatory telegram from the National Committee of the French Republican Youth Federation:

To the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League:

On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, we warmly congratulate you on the victory of people's democracy. French youth will continue to fight gloriously and bravely for peace.
      National Committee of the French Republican Youth Federation
      Paris, October 11th

### 青年团中央青联学联等单位  联合庆祝世界青年日  苏、罗、法青年团体来电祝贺

1949-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团中央青联学联等单位
    联合庆祝世界青年日
    苏、罗、法青年团体来电祝贺
    【新华社北京十二日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会，中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会、青年团中央团校、中国青年艺术剧院等单位于今日联合举行庆祝世界青年日与世界学生周大会，到会九百余人。民主青联主席廖承志致开会词。他指出：庆祝世界青年日与世界学生周，要加强国际主义和新爱国主义的教育。接着青年团中央书记冯文彬讲话。他着重指出我们要关心世界民主青年的生活和斗争，并以实际工作和行动来支援他们，同时要在新中国的各项建设工作中加倍努力，来庆祝世界青年日与世界学生周。
    【新华社北京十二日电】苏联青年反法西斯委员会、罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟及法国共和青年联合会全国委员会，于世界青年日（本月十日），都致电中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会，表示祝贺。三电电文如下：
    （一）苏联青年反法西斯委员会的贺电
    中国新民主主义青年团：适逢世界青年日，苏联青年反法西斯委员会向你们，亲爱的朋友们，致以热烈的兄弟的敬礼。苏联青年为你们对于国内外反动势力的历史性胜利，为你们建立中华人民共和国的伟大成功而感到衷心兴奋。伟大的中国人民在中国共产党及其主席毛泽东领导下所获得的历史性胜利，对于进一步巩固以苏联为首的民主和平和社会主义阵营的事业有着巨大的贡献。苏联青年坚信人民民主中国的千百万男女青年，将与团结在世界民主青年联盟队伍中的全世界进步青年，不倦地展开友谊与合作的联系。从苏联青年那里，你们有最真诚的、最可靠的朋友。
    中苏青年坚不可摧的友谊巩固万岁！
              苏联青年反法西斯委员会
              十一月十一日
    （二）罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟的贺电
    适值世界青年日，我们以罗马亚尼人民共和国劳动青年联盟的名义，谨向你们致同志的敬意，并祝为民主和平和社会主义的胜利而积极斗争的中华人民共和国的英勇青年战斗成功。
          弗洛雷斯古
          十一月十日
    （三）法国共和青年联合会全国委员会的贺电：
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会：
    趁世界民主青年联盟成立纪念日的时候，我们向你们热烈地祝贺人民民主的胜利。法国青年将坚持光荣地和勇敢地为和平而斗争。
            法国共和青年联合会全国委员会
            巴黎  十月十一日
