Title: Czechoslovak-German-Polish Youth Delegation Visits Soviet Union Upon Invitation; Participated in October Revolution Day Red Square Parade
Author:
Date: 1949-11-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th: TASS reports that youth delegations from many people's democracies have arrived in Moscow at the invitation of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee. Currently in Moscow are a delegation of outstanding workers from the Czechoslovak Youth League led by the League's General Secretary, Krutina; a delegation of the German Democratic Republic's youth led by the Free German Youth League Secretary, Brasch; and a delegation of the Polish Republic's youth led by the Polish Youth League Chairman, Matwin. A delegation of Chinese youth from the People's Republic of China, led by Xiao Hua, a member of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China, who returned after attending the Second World Democratic Youth Conference, is also in Moscow.

	On the 7th, youth delegations from various people's democracies participated in the Red Square parade of the Soviet Army forces and the grand march of the working people of the Soviet capital celebrating the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution. On the 8th, the youth delegations participated in many mass celebration meetings commemorating the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution. Currently, the youth delegations from various people's democracies are studying the Soviet youth movement and admiring the scenery of Moscow and the historical sites of the great October Revolution.

### 捷德波青年代表团  应邀赴苏参观  曾参加十月革命节红场大检阅

1949-11-12
第3版()
专栏：

    捷德波青年代表团
    应邀赴苏参观
    曾参加十月革命节红场大检阅
    【新华社北京十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：许多人民民主国家的青年代表团，应苏联青年反法西斯委员会之邀，纷纷抵达莫斯科。目前在莫斯科的有捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟总书记克鲁提纳率领的该联盟的优秀工人的代表团，自由德国青年联盟书记布拉许率领的德意志民主共和国青年代表团，波兰青年联盟主席马特温率领的波兰共和国青年代表团。由中国新民主主义青年团中央委员萧华率领，出席世界民主青年第二届大会后返国的中华人民共和国青年代表一行，现在也在莫斯科。
    七日，来自各人民民主国家的青年代表团，参加了庆祝十月革命三十二周年的苏联陆军部队的红场检阅，及苏联首都劳动人民的大游行。八日，各青年代表团参加了纪念十月革命三十二周年的许多群众庆祝大会。现各人民民主国家的青年代表团，正在研究苏联青年运动，并瞻仰莫斯科的风光和伟大的十月革命的史迹。
