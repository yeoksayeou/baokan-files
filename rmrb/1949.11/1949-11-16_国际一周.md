Title: International Week
Author:
Date: 1949-11-16
Page: 3

The past week has been a week of universal celebration of the 32nd anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution. This year, the people of the world are celebrating this great festival with particularly joyful and encouraging hearts, because the day of liberation for all mankind, illuminated by the light of the October Revolution, is fast approaching. Therefore, this year's October Revolution Day has become a carnival day for progressive humanity all over the world to celebrate victory, a demonstration day against warmongers, and a combat day to strengthen friendship with the Soviet Union.

### 国际一周

1949-11-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际一周
    过去一周，是普天同庆苏联十月革命三十二周年的一周。今年，全世界人民以特别欢欣鼓舞的心情来纪念这个伟大的节日，因为为十月革命的光芒所普照的全人类，它们的翻身日子是快要到来了。因此，今年的十月革命节，变成了全世界进步人类庆祝胜利的狂欢日，变成了反对战争贩子的示威日，变成了加强对苏友谊的战斗日。
