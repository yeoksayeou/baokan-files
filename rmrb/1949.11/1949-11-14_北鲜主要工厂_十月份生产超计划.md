Title: North Korean Major Factories Exceed October Production Plans
Author:
Date: 1949-11-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 11th: Major factories and mines in North Korea exceeded their production plans in October. The Hungnam Fertilizer Factory completed 121% of its ammonium sulfate production plan, 116.5% of natural electrodes, and 101.3% of soap, with a production speed increase of 111.5% compared to September. The Suncheon Chemical Factory in South Pyongan Province completed 127.3% of its coal nitrogen production plan in mid-October. The Sinuiju Textile Factory in North Pyongan Province has completed 339.6% of its production plan. The Hwanghae Iron Works and the Seongjin Steel Works in North Hamgyong Province completed 120.9% and 185% of their pig iron and special steel production, respectively. In addition, coal mines in Aoji in North Hamgyong Province, Dansan in South Hamgyong Province, and the Bukjung Machine Factory in North Pyongan Province, among many other factories and mines, completed their entire year's production plans in October.

### 北鲜主要工厂  十月份生产超计划

1949-11-14
第3版()
专栏：

    北鲜主要工厂
    十月份生产超计划
    【新华社平壤十一日电】北朝鲜各主要工厂矿山在十月份纷纷超过生产计划。兴南肥料工厂完成了硫酸铵生产计划的百分之一百二十一，天然电极百分之一百一十六点五，肥皂百分之一百零一点三，生产速度提高到九月份的百分之一百一十一点五。平安南道顺川郡化学工厂，十月中完成石炭窒素生产计划的百分之一百二十七点三。平安北道新义州纺织工厂，已完成生产计划的百分之三百三十九点六。黄海制铁所和咸镜北道的城津制钢所，完成了铣铁和特殊钢的生产百分之一百二十点九和百分之一百八十五。此外，咸镜北道阿吾地、咸镜南道端山等地的煤矿，和平安北道北中机制作所等许多工厂矿山，在十月份即完成了今年全年的生产计划。
