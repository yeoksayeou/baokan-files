Title: All-China Students' Federation Sends Telegram to International Students' Union to Congratulate World Student Week
Author:
Date: 1949-11-17
Page: 4

　　All-China Students' Federation Sends Telegram to International Students' Union
  　Congratulating World Student Week
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th -- Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, today sent a telegram to the International Students' Union congratulating World Student Week on November 17th. The text of the telegram is as follows: International Students' Union:
    Chinese students warmly celebrate World Student Week and support the International Students' Union in its struggle for peace, national independence, and the democratization of education.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 16, 1949, Beijing

### 全国学联电国际学联　祝贺世界学生周

1949-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国学联电国际学联
  　祝贺世界学生周
    【新华社北京十六日电】中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定，今日致电国际学生联合会祝贺十一月十七日的世界学生周，电文如下：国际学生联合会：
    中国学生热烈庆祝世界学生周，拥护国际学联为争取和平、民族独立、教育民主化而斗争。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十一月十六日　北京
