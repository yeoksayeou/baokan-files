Title: North Korean People Enthusiastically Prepare to Celebrate Stalin's Seventieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1949-11-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 27th. The people of North Korea are enthusiastically preparing to celebrate Stalin's seventieth birthday on December 21st. The preparatory work is being directed by the Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front and the Korean-Soviet Cultural Association. Throughout North Korea, there will be widespread introductions to the life, work, and writings of the great Stalin, the genius leader of the working people of the world. Factories, rural areas, the people's army, and schools will all hold lectures and symposiums on Stalin. Libraries everywhere will hold photo exhibitions of Stalin. Movie theaters will screen films such as "The Defense of Stalingrad" and "The Vow." The Korean-Soviet Cultural Association will publish various publications about Stalin. On December 20th, the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front will convene a celebration meeting in the capital of the Republic to celebrate Stalin's seventieth birthday.

### 庆祝斯大林七十寿辰  北朝鲜人民热烈筹备

1949-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝斯大林七十寿辰
    北朝鲜人民热烈筹备
    【新华社平壤二十七日电】北朝鲜人民正热烈筹备庆祝十二月二十一日斯大林的七十寿辰。筹备工作由朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线和朝苏文化协会主持。北朝鲜各地将广泛介绍全世界劳动人民的天才领袖伟大的斯大林的生活事业和著作。工厂、农村、人民、军队和学校，都将举行关于斯大林的讲演会和座谈会。各地图书馆将举办斯大林的照片展览会。各电影院将放映“斯大林格勒保卫战”、“宣誓”等影片。朝苏文化协会将发行关于斯大林的各种出版物。十二月二十日，祖国战线中央委员会将在共和国首都召开斯大林七十寿辰的庆祝大会。
