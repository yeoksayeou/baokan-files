Title: Nanyuan Live Ammunition Drill: Citizens Should Not Be Alarmed by the Sound
Author:
Date: 1949-11-20
Page: 4

　　Nanyuan Live Ammunition Drill
  　Citizens Should Not Be Alarmed by the Sound
    [This Newspaper] The Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army has scheduled a continuous live ammunition drill at Nanyuan Airport for ten days, from November 21st to 30th. The drill will be postponed in case of rain. All government agencies and citizens are requested not to be alarmed by the sound.

### 南苑实弹演习　市民闻声勿惊

1949-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　南苑实弹演习
  　市民闻声勿惊
    【本报讯】中国人民解放军京津卫戍区司令部定于十一月二十一日至三十日，在南苑机场连续举行实弹演习十天，天雨顺延，望各机关及市民闻声勿惊。
