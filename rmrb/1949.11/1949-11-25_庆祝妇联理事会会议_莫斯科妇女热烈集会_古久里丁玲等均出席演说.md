Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 3

　　Celebrating the Executive Council Meeting of the Women's Federation
    Moscow Women Enthusiastically Gather
　　Gu Jiu Li, Ding Ling, and Others Attend and Speak

    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd dispatch] Moscow News: Women of Moscow City and Moscow Oblast gathered on the 21st at the Hall of Columns of the Moscow Trade Unions to celebrate the convocation of the Executive Council meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation. Present were representatives of women's organizations from thirty-six countries attending the Federation Council meeting, female Stakhanovite workers from various enterprises in Moscow, advanced collective farmers, students, and workers in literature, art, and science. The meeting was opened by Popova, Vice-Chairman of the Women's International Democratic Federation and Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee. Then, the Secretary-General of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Vaillant-Couturier, spoke, conveying to the heroic Soviet women the gratitude and love of women from all over the world. She said: "We solemnly swear to devote all our efforts, energy, and will to fight side by side with the Soviet women to ensure the security of peace." Ding Ling, the representative of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and a female writer, discussed at the meeting the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples, and called on all democratic forces to unite in the struggle against the warmongers. The audience also listened with great interest to the speeches of women representing Albania, Spain, the United States, Britain, Democratic Germany, Argentina, and other countries. Finally, the congress passed with great enthusiasm a telegram of tribute to Comrade Stalin, the leader of the Soviet people.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th dispatch] TASS Moscow News: The Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee hosted a reception on the 22nd for the representatives attending the Executive Council meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation. Among those present were Vaillant-Couturier, Secretary-General of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Vice-Chairmen Dolores Ibárruri, Yuriina, Pragrova, and all the representatives attending the Executive Council meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation. On the Soviet side, those attending the reception included Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee and Vice-Chairman of the Women's International Democratic Federation, female scientists, Stakhanovite workers from various enterprises in Moscow, female writers, and famous women in the art world. After the reception, a grand concert was held with the participation of Moscow's best actors. The reception was spent in a warm and intimate atmosphere.

### 庆祝妇联理事会会议  莫斯科妇女热烈集会　古久里丁玲等均出席演说

1949-11-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　庆祝妇联理事会会议
    莫斯科妇女热烈集会
　　古久里丁玲等均出席演说
    【新华社北京二十三日电】莫斯科讯：莫斯科市和莫斯科州的妇女们，二十一日在莫斯科工会圆柱大厅集会，庆祝国际民主妇联理事会会议的召开。到会的有出席妇联理事会会议的三十六国妇女组织的代表、莫斯科各企业的女斯塔哈诺夫运动者、先进的集体农民、学生，及文学、艺术与科学工作者。会议由国际民主妇联副主席、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃致开幕词。接着由国际民主妇联总书记瓦伦特——古久里发言，她向英勇的苏联妇女转达全世界妇女的感激与热爱。她说：“我们庄严地宣誓：要竭尽我们一切的努力、精力和意志去为保证和平的安全而与苏联妇女们并肩斗争。”中华人民共和国民主妇女联合会的代表、女作家丁玲，在会上论述了中国人民与苏联人民之间的友谊，并号召一切民主力量在反对战争贩子的斗争中团结一致。会众也非常感兴趣地聆听了代表阿尔巴尼亚、西班牙、美国、英国、民主德国、阿根廷及其他国家的妇女们的演说。最后，大会以巨大的热情通过了向苏联人民领袖斯大林同志的致敬电。
    【新华社北京二十四日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联妇女反法西斯委员会于廿二日招待出席国际民主妇联理事会的代表。与会者有国际民主妇联总书记瓦伦特——古久里、副主席多洛列斯·伊巴露丽、尤里尼亚、普拉格洛娃及出席国际民主妇联理事会的全体代表。苏联方面出席招待会的有苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席和国际民主妇联副主席波波娃、女科学家、来自莫斯科各企业的斯塔哈诺夫工作者、女作家和艺术界的妇女著名人物。在招待会之后又举行了由莫斯科最优秀的演员参加的盛大音乐会。招待会在热烈亲密的气氛中渡过。
