Title: Polish Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Hua Luoji Arrives in Beijing to Prepare for the Establishment of the Polish Embassy Site

Author:
Date: 1949-11-24
Page: 1

　　Polish Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Hua Luoji Arrives in Beijing
　　Preparing for the Establishment of the Polish Embassy Site
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd – Mr. Hua Luoji, Chargé d'Affaires of the Polish Embassy to the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing from Nanjing under the orders of the government of the People's Republic of Poland to prepare for the establishment of the Polish Embassy site. Chargé d'Affaires Hua Luoji left Nanjing on November 21st and arrived in Beijing at 9:30 AM on the 23rd. Those who went to the station to welcome him included Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department. Accompanying Chargé d'Affaires Hua Luoji were Madame Hua and Ms. Kang Bin, the Chinese secretary.

### 波使馆代办华罗奇抵京　筹设波兰大使馆馆址

1949-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　波使馆代办华罗奇抵京
　　筹设波兰大使馆馆址
    【新华社北京二十三日电】波兰驻中华人民共和国代办华罗奇先生，奉波兰人民共和国政府之命，由南京前来北京筹设波兰大使馆馆址事宜。华罗奇代办于十一月二十一日离宁，于二十三日上午九时半抵京。前往车站欢迎者有我外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，交际处处长王倬如等。随华罗奇代办同来者有华代办夫人及中文秘书康彬女士。
