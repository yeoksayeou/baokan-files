Title: Celebrating World Youth Day, All-Soviet Youth Enthusiastically Gather, Resolutely Opposing American and British Warmongers
Author:
Date: 1949-11-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th: A comprehensive TASS dispatch reports: November 10th was World Youth Day, and also the fourth anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Democratic Youth. On this day, the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee specially convened an enlarged meeting. Young men and women workers, collective farmers, and students in Moscow, Leningrad, the Ukrainian capital Kiev, the Belarusian capital Minsk, the capitals of various republics, and major cities held mass rallies and seminars to celebrate.

At the enlarged meeting of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee held in Moscow, Chairman Zimasov of the committee delivered a report. In the discussion, attendees enthusiastically endorsed the activities of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Union of Students, and expressed the determination of Soviet youth to unite closely with democratic youth around the world to fight against American and British warmongers. The meeting sent telegrams of respect to the World Federation of Democratic Youth and democratic youth organizations in various countries. Factories, institutions, and schools in various districts of Moscow held celebration rallies. At a seminar in the Central House of Art Workers, young people from Moscow and youth representatives from Poland, Romania, Albania, and Republican Spain, who attended the Second Congress of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, gathered together joyfully.

### 庆祝世界青年日  全苏青年热烈集会  坚决反对美英战争贩子

1949-11-13
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝世界青年日
    全苏青年热烈集会
    坚决反对美英战争贩子
    【新华社北京十二日电】综合塔斯社消息：十一月十日是世界青年日，同时也是世界民主青年联盟成立的四周年纪念日。在这一天，苏联青年反法西斯委员会特别召开了扩大会议。莫斯科、列宁格勒、乌克兰首都基辅、白俄罗斯首都明斯克、各共和国首都及各大城市的青年男女工人、集体农民和学生们纷纷举行群众大会和座谈会等，进行庆祝。
    在莫斯科召开的苏联青年反法西斯委员会扩大会议，听取了该委员会主席齐马索夫的报告。在讨论中，到会者热烈赞同世界民主青联和世界学联的活动，并表明苏联青年和全世界民主青年紧密团结为反对美英战争贩子而斗争的决心。会议向世界民主青联和各国民主青年团体通电致敬。莫斯科各区的工厂、机关、学校都举行了庆祝大会。在艺术工作者中央大厦的座谈会中，莫斯科的青年们和出席世界民主青年第二届代表大会的波兰、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚和共和西班牙的青年代表们都欢聚一堂。
