Title: Soviet Scholars Publish Works Studying the Chinese Revolution: Voitingky Completes "A Short History of China"
Author:
Date: 1949-11-08
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 7th: Soviet Oriental scholars have published many works on the Chinese Revolution. Doctor of History Voitingky has completed the first volume of "A Short History of China," divided into two volumes. This volume covers the period from the Xinhai Revolution of 1911 to the Second World War. The second volume describes the historical events that have taken place in China over the past decade. In his work, Voitingky emphasized the role of the Chinese Communist Party in uniting China and democratizing it, as well as the role of the victorious operations of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Army.

　　Soviet Sinologist Pozdneeva, in her lengthy essay on the outstanding Chinese writer Lu Xun, vividly depicts Lu Xun's role in creating new Chinese literature. At the same time, based on the historical materials and statements of this great Chinese writer, she appropriately portrays Lu Xun as a loyal friend of the Soviet Union. Chikanov's paper describes the land reform in 1947 that gave land to Chinese peasants in the liberated areas. Many graduate students and doctoral candidates at the Soviet Academy of Sciences are writing papers on China. The young Sinologist Klivtsov obtained a Master of Science degree with his thesis "Confucianism Serves the Chinese Reactionaries," and Gainshin also obtained a master's degree with a thesis on the Chinese economy. Simanovskaya obtained a Doctor of Science degree with a thesis on "Research on the Chinese Revolution of 1911."

### 苏联学者发表著作  研究中国革命问题　沃伊丁斯基完成“中国历史简编”

1949-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联学者发表著作
    研究中国革命问题
  　沃伊丁斯基完成“中国历史简编”
    【新华社北京七日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联的东方学者们发表了许多关于中国革命的著作。历史学博士沃伊丁斯基已完成分为上下两卷的“中国历史简编”的第一卷。该卷内容自一九一一年的辛亥革命开始，至第二次世界大战为止。第二卷则叙述过去十年内中国发生的历史性事件。沃伊丁斯基在他的著作中着重说明了中国共产党在统一中国并使其民主化的过程中所起的作用，以及中国人民革命军的胜利作战所起的作用。
    苏联女汉学家波兹德聂耶娃在她所写关于中国杰出作家鲁迅的长篇论文中，生动地描绘了鲁迅在创造中国新文学方面所起的作用。同时她根据这位中国伟大作家的历史资料和言论，恰当地描绘鲁迅是苏联的忠实友人。契卡诺夫的论文描写一九四七年使解放区的中国农民获得了田地的土地改革，苏联科学院的许多研究生和准备博士论文的人们，正在写作关于中国的论文。年青的汉学家克利夫卓夫以“儒学服务于中国反动派”的论文，获得了科学硕士学位，加恩辛也以关于中国经济的论文，获得了硕士学位。西马诺夫斯卡亚以关于“一九一一年中国革命的研究”论文，获得了科学博士学位。
