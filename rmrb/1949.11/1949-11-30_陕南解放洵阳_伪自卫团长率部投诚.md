Title: Southern Shaanxi Liberates Xunyang; Puppet Self-Defense Regiment Commander Leads Troops to Surrender
Author:
Date: 1949-11-30
Page: 1

　　Southern Shaanxi Liberates Xunyang
　　Puppet Self-Defense Regiment Commander Leads Troops to Surrender
    [Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 29th] The People's Liberation Army of Southern Shaanxi liberated Xunyang County on the northern bank of the Han River, northeast of Ankang, at 5:00 AM on the 25th. Bandit troops fled westward. Duan Xiping, the commander of the puppet self-defense regiment of the county, led his troops to surrender to the People's Liberation Army.

### 陕南解放洵阳　伪自卫团长率部投诚

1949-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕南解放洵阳
  　伪自卫团长率部投诚
    【新华社西安二十九日电】陕南人民解放军二十五日五时解放安康东北汉水北岸的洵阳县城，匪军向西逃窜。该县伪自卫团团长段西屏率部向解放军投诚。
