Title: Kiselyov Appointed Soviet Ambassador to Hungary
Author:
Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 3

　　Kiselyov Appointed Soviet Ambassador to Hungary
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd] TASS, Moscow, 19th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has appointed Yevgeny Kiselyov as the Plenipotentiary and Extraordinary Ambassador of the Soviet Union to the Hungarian People's Republic. The former Soviet Ambassador to Hungary, Tyshkov, has been assigned to another post and has been relieved of his duties.

### 基赛列夫任苏驻匈大使

1949-11-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　基赛列夫任苏驻匈大使
    【新华社北京二十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科十九日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团顷任命叶夫金尼·基赛列夫为苏联驻匈牙利人民共和国特命全权大使。苏联前任驻匈大使蒂希科夫另有任用，已解除职务。
