Title: New Knowledge Symposium to Hold a Forum on Current Price Issues on the 17th
Author: Zheng Zhong
Date: 1949-11-16
Page: 2

　　New Knowledge Symposium
  　Holding a Forum on Current Price Issues on the 17th
    [This newspaper's report] The New Knowledge Symposium is specifically inviting experts such as Zhang Naiqi, Yao Yilin, and Di Chaobai to discuss the current price issue at 7:00 PM on the 17th. The location is the auditorium of the European and American Alumni Association. Furthermore, the 33rd symposium of the association will still be held as usual at 7:00 PM on the 18th to continue discussing "Labor-Capital Relations", and responsible comrades from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Municipal Labor Bureau are invited to participate in guidance. Director He Guanqing of the First Municipal Health Office will give a lecture on "Plague and Rats" before the meeting.
                            　　　　　　　　　　　　(Zheng Zhong)

### 新知识座谈会　定十七日座谈目前物价问题
郑重
1949-11-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会
  　定十七日座谈目前物价问题
    【本报讯】新知识座谈会特定于十七日下午七时邀请章乃器、姚依林、狄超白诸专家座谈目前物价问题，地点在欧美同学会礼堂。又该会第三十三次座谈会仍照例于十八日下午七时举行，继续讨论“劳资问题”，并请全国总工会及市劳动局负责同志参加指导，本市第一卫生事务所何观清所长，将于会前讲述“鼠疫与鼠”。
                            　　　　　　　　　　　　（郑重）
