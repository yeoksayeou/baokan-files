Title: Trade Union of Cultural and Educational Workers Established in Tangshan
Author: You Min
Date: 1949-11-23
Page: 2

　　Tangshan Cultural and Educational Workers Establish Trade Union
　　[This newspaper Tangshan News] The cultural and educational workers of Tangshan City held their first representative meeting on the 17th and 18th of this month to officially establish a trade union for cultural and educational workers. Eighty representatives attended the meeting, representing more than 1,900 members from seven branches in the city, including education, news, drama, film, and medicine. Feng Bin, Tang Yu, and fifteen others were elected as members of the trade union committee.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(You Min)

### 唐山文教工作者成立工会
幼民
1949-11-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　唐山文教工作者成立工会
    【本报唐山讯】唐山市文化教育工作者于本月十七、十八两日举行第一次代表大会，正式成立文化教育工作者工会。出席代表八十人，代表该市教育、新闻、戏剧、电影、医药等七个分会的一千九百余会员。当选出冯彬、唐余等十五人为工会委员。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（幼民）
