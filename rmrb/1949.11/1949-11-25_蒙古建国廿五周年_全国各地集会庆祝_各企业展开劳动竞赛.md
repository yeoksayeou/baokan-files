Title: Mongolia's 25th Anniversary of Founding: Celebrations Held Across the Country; Labor Emulation Campaigns Unfold in Various Enterprises
Author:
Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 3

　　Mongolia's 25th Anniversary of Founding
　　Celebrations Held Across the Country
　　Labor Emulation Campaigns Unfold in Various Enterprises

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th] TASS, Ulaanbaatar, 22nd: The 26th marks the 25th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Mongolia's First Great Khural (Supreme People's Assembly) and the promulgation of its first constitution. The working people of Mongolia are preparing for the anniversary in a warm and enthusiastic atmosphere. Lectures, symposiums, reports, and get-together rallies are being organized everywhere in the country. A grand exhibition was held in Ulaanbaatar to showcase the achievements of the People's Republic of Mongolia over the past 25 years. Among them, a special Soviet-Mongolian Friendship Exhibition Hall was set up to showcase the substantial assistance that the Soviet people and Stalin consistently provided to the Mongolian people. The labor emulation campaign to complete this year's plan ahead of schedule, held to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the People's Republic, is in full swing in enterprises across the country.

### 蒙古建国廿五周年　全国各地集会庆祝　各企业展开劳动竞赛

1949-11-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　蒙古建国廿五周年
　　全国各地集会庆祝
　　各企业展开劳动竞赛
    【新华社北京二十四日电】塔斯社乌兰巴托尔二十二日讯：二十六日是蒙古人民共和国第一届大呼拉尔（最高人民会议）成立和颁布第一个宪法的二十五周年纪念日。蒙古劳动人民正在热情洋溢的气氛中筹备纪念。全国各地纷纷筹办讲演会、座谈会、报告和联欢大会等。在乌兰巴托尔举行了一个盛大的展览会，展示二十五年中蒙古人民共和国的成就。其中，专辟了一间苏蒙友谊陈列室，表现了苏联人民和斯大林经常给予蒙古人民的大量援助。为庆祝人民共和国二十五周年纪念而举行的提早完成今年计划的劳动竞赛，正在全国各企业中如火如荼地开展着。
