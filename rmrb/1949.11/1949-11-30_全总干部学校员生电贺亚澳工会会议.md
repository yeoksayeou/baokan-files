Title: Cadres and Students of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cadre School Send a Telegram to Congratulate the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-11-30
Page: 1

To the Chairman of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference, and to all the fellow delegates:
	
	The convocation of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference in Beijing demonstrates the unprecedented great unity of the Asian and Australian working classes. All our students and staff will dedicate ourselves to studying and implementing the resolutions of the conference, and enhancing our understanding of internationalism, as a gift to the conference. We extend our congratulations and wish you good health!
	
	All Cadres, Students and Staff of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cadre School

### 全总干部学校员生电贺亚澳工会会议
中华全国总工会干部学位全体学员职工
1949-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总干部学校员生电贺亚澳工会会议亚洲、澳洲工会会议主席团结转全体代表同志们：
    亚澳工会会议在北京的召开，显示着亚澳工人阶级的空前大团结。我们全校学员及职工，决以学习并执行大会的决议，提高自己的国际主义的认识，作为对大会的献礼。谨此祝贺，并祝健康！
    中华全国总工会干部学校全体学员职工
