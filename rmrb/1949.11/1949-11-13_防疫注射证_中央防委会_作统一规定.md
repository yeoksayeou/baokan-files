Title: Epidemic Prevention Vaccination Certificate: Central Epidemic Prevention Committee Makes Uniform Provisions
Author:
Date: 1949-11-13
Page: 4

    Epidemic Prevention Vaccination Certificate
    Central Epidemic Prevention Committee
    Makes Uniform Provisions
    [Beijing News] The Central Epidemic Prevention Committee has recently stipulated that, with the exception of the four formats of epidemic prevention vaccination certificates issued by the Central Epidemic Prevention Committee, the Beijing Municipal Health Bureau, the Ministry of Railways Health Bureau, and the North China Military Region Health Department, all others shall be discontinued.

### 防疫注射证  中央防委会  作统一规定

1949-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    防疫注射证
    中央防委会
    作统一规定
    【北京讯】中央防疫委员会顷规定除中央防疫委员会、北京市卫生局、铁道部卫生局、华北军区卫生部所发布四种格式的防疫注射证外，其他均一律停止使用。
