Title: Indonesian Bangka Labor Federation Sends Congratulations to the Central People's Government of China; Chairman Mao Sends Telegram in Return, Expressing Warm Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1949-11-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 25th: The Indonesian Bangka Labor Federation sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong on the 21st of this month, congratulating the establishment of the Central People's Government of China. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Chairman Mao Zedong of the New China in Beijing:

    The announcement of the establishment of your new government marks the liberation of workers and peasants, and all workers in the world share the same glory. Congratulations on your great success, and please accept our greetings and cordial respect.

    Indonesian Bangka Labor Federation
    November 21st

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 25th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China replied to the congratulatory telegram from the Indonesian Bangka Labor Federation on the 24th of this month. The telegram reads as follows:
    To the Indonesian Bangka Labor Federation:

    Warmly thank you for your congratulations. Hope that the Indonesian people, after a long struggle, can also win the independence and freedom of their own motherland.

    Mao Zedong, November 24th

### 印尼邦加劳工联合会　电贺我中央人民政府　毛主席复电热忱感谢

1949-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　印尼邦加劳工联合会
  　电贺我中央人民政府
  　毛主席复电热忱感谢
    【新华社北京二十五日电】印尼邦加劳工联合会于本月二十一日致电毛泽东主席，祝贺中国中央人民政府成立，电文如下：北京新中国主席毛泽东先生：
    你的新政府的宣告成立，标志着工人与农民的解放，世界全体工人都享有同样的光荣。向你的伟大成功致贺，并请接受我们的问候和亲切的敬意。
    印尼邦加劳工联合会
    十一月二十一日
    【新华社北京二十五日电】中国中央人民政府毛泽东主席于本月二十四日电复印尼邦加劳工联合会的祝贺。电文如下：印尼邦加劳工联合会：
    热忱地感谢你们的祝贺。希望印尼人民在长期的奋斗以后，能够同样赢得自己祖国的独立和自由。
    毛泽东　　十一月二十四日
