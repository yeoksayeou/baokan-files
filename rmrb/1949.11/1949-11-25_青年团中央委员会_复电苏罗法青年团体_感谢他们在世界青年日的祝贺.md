Title: Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Replies to Youth Groups in the Soviet Union and Other Countries, Thanking Them for Their Congratulations on World Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 23rd:

        Central Committee of the Communist Youth League
    Replies to Youth Groups in the Soviet Union and Other Countries
　　Thanking Them for Their Congratulations on World Youth Day
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 23rd] The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League recently sent telegrams to the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, the Romanian Labor Youth Union, and the National Committee of the French Republican Youth Federation, thanking them for their congratulations on World Youth Day. The full text of the three telegrams is as follows:

    　　　　I. Reply to the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist League
    To the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee:
    We were very excited to receive your congratulatory telegram to Chinese youth on World Youth Day. On behalf of the Chinese youth, we extend our highest brotherly greetings to the Soviet youth.

    The Chinese youth will strive to learn from the glorious example of the Soviet youth to build our young People's Republic of China, and together with the world's democratic youth led by the Soviet Union, will strive for a better future for mankind. Long live the unity and friendship between Chinese and Soviet youth!
    　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 17th

    　　　　II. Reply to the Romanian Labor Youth Union
    To the Romanian Labor Youth Union:
    Thank you for your congratulatory telegram to us on World Youth Day. Chinese youth extend warm revolutionary greetings to you and are willing to work together with Romania for peace and democracy.
    　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 17th

    　　　　III. Reply to the National Committee of the French Republican Youth Federation
    To the National Committee of the French Republican Youth Federation:
    Chinese youth thank you for your friendly congratulations, extend our warm and high respects to you, and wish the French youth success in their great cause of bravely fighting for peace and democracy.
    　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 17th

### 青年团中央委员会  复电苏罗法青年团体　感谢他们在世界青年日的祝贺

1949-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央委员会
    复电苏罗法青年团体
　　感谢他们在世界青年日的祝贺
    【新华社北京二十三日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会顷电复苏联青年反法西斯委员会、罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟、法国共和青年联合会，感谢他们在世界青年日的祝贺。三电全文如下：
    　　　　一、复苏联青年反法西斯联盟电苏联青年反法西斯委员会：
    我们很兴奋地接到你们在世界青年日给中国青年的贺电，谨代表中国青年向苏联青年致崇高的兄弟敬礼。
    中国青年将努力学习苏联青年的光辉的榜样来建设我们年轻的中华人民共和国，并与以苏联为首的世界民主青年共同为争取人类的美好将来而奋斗。中苏青年团结友谊万岁！
    　　　　　　　　　　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月十七日
    　　　　二、复罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟电罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟：
    感谢你们在世界青年日给我们的贺电。中国青年向你们致热诚的革命敬礼，并愿与罗马尼亚共同为和平民主而奋斗。
    　　　　　　　　　　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月十七日
    　　　　三、复法国共和青年联合会全国委员会电法国共和青年联合会全国委员会：
    中国青年感谢你们友谊的祝贺，向你们致热烈崇高的敬礼，并祝法国青年英勇地为和平民主而斗争的伟大事业成功。
    　　　　　　　　　　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月十七日
