Title: Opening of Amateur School for the Fifth District Cleaning Team in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1949-11-30
Page: 3

　　[Beijing News] The opening ceremony of the amateur school for workers of the Fifth District Cleaning Team of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Health Bureau was held on the 28th at the Hebei Provincial High School. Study time is scheduled from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM daily. The curriculum includes both cultural and political classes. All students are divided into three classes: literacy, elementary, and advanced. A life and work review meeting is held every Sunday. Teachers are provided voluntarily by teachers from Gulou East Gongjie Elementary School and New China Women's Vocational School.

### 京市五区清洁队业余学校开学

1949-11-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　京市五区清洁队业余学校开学
    【北京讯】北京市人民政府卫生局第五区清洁队工人业余学校，二十八日在河北省立中学举行开学典礼。学习时间规定为每日下午六点至八点，学习内容分文化课及政治课两种，全体学员共分文化识字、初级、高等三班，每星期日举行生活工作检讨会，教员是由鼓楼东公街小学及新中国女子职业学校教员义务担任。
