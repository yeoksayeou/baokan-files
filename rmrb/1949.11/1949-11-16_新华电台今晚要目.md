　　Xinhua Radio Tonight's Highlights
    [This newspaper] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station's 19:15 tonight is the second week of the special broadcast on the Asian Women's Representative Conference, with a speech by Talamo Marosova, member of the Asian and Philippine Women's Issues Committee of the International Democratic Women's Federation and member of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee: "Soviet Women and Asian Women Fighting Side by Side for World Peace." At 20:15, an introduction to Siam, one of the countries participating in the Asian and Australian Trade Union Conference and the Asian Women's Representative Conference. At 18:15, the youth program will broadcast two articles from the 26th issue of Chinese Youth: (1) "Do Not Regard the Young Pioneers as a Narrow Organization for Children"; (2) "Children Should Not Conduct Identity Checks." At 19:00, popular science common sense talks about: "Man and Salt." At 18:30, new folk art, broadcasting two sections of "Women Transporting Grain."

### 新华电台今晚要目

1949-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　新华电台今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十九点十五分是关于亚洲妇女代表会议专题广播的第二周，由国际民主妇联亚、菲妇女问题委员会委员，苏联妇女反法西斯委员会委员塔拉玛·玛洛索娃演讲：“苏联妇女和亚洲妇女为世界和平而并肩奋斗”。二十点十五分介绍参加亚洲、澳洲工会会议，和亚洲妇女代表会议的国家之一——暹逻。十八点十五分青年节目，播送中国青年第二十六期上的两篇文章（一）“不要把少年儿童队当作一个少年儿童的狭隘组织”；（二）“儿童不要进行查身份”。十九点自然科学常识讲痤：“人和盐”。十八点三十分新曲艺，播送“女运粮”二段。
