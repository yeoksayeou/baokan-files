Title: Soviet Embassy Military Attaché Dulewen Pays Visit to Acting Chief of General Staff Nie; Soviet Consul General in Beijing Visits Mayor Nie
Author:
Date: 1949-11-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th -- Soviet Embassy Military Attaché, Colonel Dulewen, accompanied by the embassy's First Adjutant, Xie Mingfu, and translator Morozov, paid a visit to Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, at 11:00 AM on the 16th.
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th -- Soviet Consul General in Beijing, Xie Liejin, leading a group of four personnel from the Soviet Consulate General in Beijing, paid their first visit to Nie Rongzhen, Mayor of Beijing, at 10:00 AM on the 16th. Present were Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of Beijing, Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal Government, and others. It is understood that before the establishment of the Soviet Consulate in Zhangjiakou, the Soviet Consulate General in Beijing will concurrently handle matters related to Soviet citizens in Zhangjiakou.

### 苏大使馆武官杜乐文  昨拜会聂代总参谋长　苏驻京总领事拜会聂市长

1949-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏大使馆武官杜乐文
    昨拜会聂代总参谋长
  　苏驻京总领事拜会聂市长
    【新华社北京十六日电】苏联驻中华人民共和国大使馆上校武官杜乐文率领大使馆首席副官谢明夫及翻译官莫罗佐夫于十六日上午十一时拜会中国人民革命军事委员会代理总参谋长聂荣臻。
    【新华社北京十六日电】苏联驻京总领事谢列金率领苏联驻京总领事馆人员一行四人，于十六日上午十时首次拜会北京市市长聂荣臻。在座者有北京市副市长张友渔，北京市政府秘书长薛子正等。按苏联驻京总领事馆在张家口苏领事馆未成立以前，将兼理张家口苏侨有关事务。
