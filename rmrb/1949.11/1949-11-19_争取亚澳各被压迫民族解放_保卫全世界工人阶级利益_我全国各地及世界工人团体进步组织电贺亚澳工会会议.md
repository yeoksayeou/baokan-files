Title: Strive for the Liberation of Oppressed Nationalities in Asia and Australia, Defend the Interests of the Working Class Worldwide. Workers' Organizations and Progressive Organizations from Across China and the World Send Congratulatory Messages to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference.
Author:
Date: 1949-11-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th. The welcome news of the arrival of leaders of the World Federation of Trade Unions in Beijing and the opening of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference has stirred enthusiastic welcome and congratulations among the Chinese working class and the broad masses of the people. Preparatory committees of trade unions in Tianjin, Taiyuan, Kaifeng, Hangzhou, and provinces and cities such as Hebei, Henan, Hunan, and Zhejiang have sent telegrams to congratulate the opening of the conference and its success.

		Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee
	The congratulatory telegram from the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee stated: We, the workers of Tianjin, warmly support this conference and are prepared to thoroughly implement all the resolutions of the conference. We will unite even better, make the greatest efforts for the production and construction of New China and the eradication of counter-revolutionary remnants in our country, consolidate our New China, and support the struggles of democratic trade unions in Asia, Australia, and other countries, contributing all our strength to the cause of lasting world peace. The congratulatory telegram from the Hebei Provincial Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee stated: This committee calls on all employees in the province to actively organize, strengthen our ranks, resolutely accept the resolutions and tasks assigned by the conference, and support the liberation struggles of workers in various countries with practical actions to restore and develop production.

		The Second Shanxi Provincial Workers' Congress
	The Second Shanxi Provincial Workers' Congress, currently in session in Taiyuan, expressed in its congratulatory telegram to Louis Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and members of the Executive Bureau, its willingness to unite with workers around the world under the leadership of the World Federation of Trade Unions and fight to the end to defend world peace and democracy. The Second Shanxi Provincial Workers' Congress also entrusted the Soviet delegation attending the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference to convey its greetings to Stalin, the teacher of the world proletariat.

		Henan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee
	The congratulatory telegram from the Henan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee stated: We are determined to organize and unite the working class of Henan under the leadership of the World Federation of Trade Unions and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, unite with the working classes of all countries in the world, oppose the aggressive conspiracies of warmongers, defend world peace, strive for the liberation of oppressed nationalities in Asia and Australia, and fight to the end to defend the interests of the working class worldwide. The first membership representative congress of the Kaifeng Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, which is currently in session, also sent a telegram in the name of the congress to congratulate the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. In addition, the Henan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Henan Provincial People's Government, the Henan Military Region Command of the People's Liberation Army, the Political Department, the Henan Provincial Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the Henan Provincial Preparatory Committee of the Democratic Youth Federation, the Henan Provincial Preparatory Committee of the Democratic Women's Federation, and other organizations also sent telegrams to congratulate the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference.

		Hunan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee
	The congratulatory telegram from the Hunan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee stated: The opening of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference is an exchange of combat experience between workers' brothers in the two continents, and a great unity to defeat the common enemy. Hunan workers hereby express their high determination to increase production as a gift to the conference.

		Zhejiang Provincial Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee
	The congratulatory telegram from the Zhejiang Provincial and Hangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee stated: We hereby respectfully present to the conference four silk portraits of the great working-class revolutionary leaders Lenin, Stalin, Mao Zedong, and Zhu De, woven by the workers themselves with heartfelt love, to express the enthusiastic support of hundreds of thousands of workers in the province and city for the conference, and to wish the conference success.

		All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles
	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th. The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles recently sent a telegram congratulating the opening of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. The telegram reads as follows: Presidium of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference:

	We send you our comradely greetings! And warmly congratulate you on the victory of your conference. This conference of yours demonstrates the success of the unprecedented great unity of the working class in Asia and Australia and will determine the fate of the complete liberation of the working class in Asia and Australia. Let us fight shoulder to shoulder to overthrow the colonial system, to overthrow imperialism, to defend lasting world peace, and to strive for people's democracy.
	
All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Chairman Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairmen Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, November 17, 1949

		Northeast Federation of Trade Unions
	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th. The Northeast Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram on the 16th to congratulate the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference and welcome leaders of the World Federation of Trade Unions and delegations of workers to visit the Northeast. The telegram stated:

	We, on behalf of 2.5 million employees in the Northeast, hereby celebrate the grand convening of the Asian and Australian Trade Union Conference in our capital with the new achievements of our ongoing record-breaking movement and the labor accomplishments of the working masses, and warmly celebrate the success of the conference. We eagerly hope that Comrade Louis Saillant and other leaders of the World Federation of Trade Unions and delegations of workers will visit the Northeast as soon as possible to guide the Northeast labor movement so that our trade union work can be improved as a result.

		People from All Walks of Life in Tianjin
	Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 18th. The news of the opening of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference has been met with support and congratulations from the people of Tianjin. Newspapers in Tianjin have all published the news of the conference's opening in prominent positions and have devoted considerable space to introducing the activities of the Asian-Australian Trade Unions. The Tianjin Industrial Exhibition, which has been open for half a month, has decided to extend its closing date to the 4th of next month in order to receive representatives from various countries attending the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference for visits. The Industrial Exhibition is currently mobilizing various units to prepare English explanations for various exhibits to facilitate understanding by international friends. The broad masses of employees in Tianjin are particularly excited about the opening of the conference. Many employees of the Tianjin Railway Bureau jumped for joy upon hearing the news of the conference's opening. Guan Peihou, a worker at the Telecommunications Bureau, said: This conference has invigorated the mood of our working class. In the future, we must work harder on the production front to quickly build a new and prosperous New China, and thus more forcefully support the liberation movement of oppressed peoples around the world. The Tianjin Federation of Industrial Trade Unions has scheduled a singing performance on the 20th to celebrate the opening of the conference, to strengthen internationalist education for workers through cultural activities.

In addition, the Tianjin Federation of Industrial Trade Unions, the Tianjin Textile Industry Trade Union, the Tianjin Handicraft Industry Trade Union, the Tianjin Seamen's Trade Union, the Tianjin Store Employees' Trade Union, and the Tianjin Democratic Women's Federation, and other organizations have all sent telegrams one after another to celebrate the opening of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th. The Organizing Committee of the Chemical Industry Branch of the World Federation of Trade Unions, the Australian Railway Union, and the Central Committee of Bulgarian Trade Unions have recently sent telegrams to congratulate the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. The telegrams are as follows:

(1) Congratulatory telegram from the Organizing Committee of the Chemical Industry Branch of the World Federation of Trade Unions: Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference:

The Organizing Committee of the Chemical Industry Branch of the World Federation of Trade Unions congratulates the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference and invites chemical industry trade unions from Asian and Australian countries to participate in the founding conference of the Chemical Industry Branch to be held in Budapest on March 12.
	Chairman: Gedi
	Secretary: Leidwo
	November 14

(2) Congratulatory telegram from the Australian Railway Union: Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference:

The Australian Railway Union hereby sends brotherly greetings and heartfelt congratulations to the conference, and wishes the conference success!
	Secretary: Chaboer
	November 16

(3) Congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of Bulgarian Trade Unions: Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference:

The Central Committee of Bulgarian Trade Unions, on behalf of all employees in Bulgaria, sends combat greetings to the representatives of Asian and Australian countries participating in the first Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference convened by the World Federation of Trade Unions! And through you, extend condolences to the heroic working class of Asian and Australian countries and the People's Republic of China.

The workers of Bulgaria firmly believe that the representative conference will adopt various resolutions and formulate various concrete programs to consolidate the struggle of the working people of Asian countries against imperialist aggression and the instigation of new wars, to consolidate their struggle against cruel exploitation and forced labor, to defend the vital interests of the working people, and to emulate the example of the heroic Chinese people and the Chinese working class, to strive for their democratic rights and freedoms in these countries, and to strive for world peace and democracy. The Chinese people and the Chinese working class, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, have achieved victory through their many years of tenacious struggle against American and British imperialists and their agents, and have established the People's Republic of China. At this moment, Bulgarian workers wholeheartedly hope that their Asian and Australian brothers will successfully complete the work of the representative conference. This conference will further consolidate the friendship, cooperation, and solidarity of not only the Asian and Australian working classes but also the working classes of the world led by the World Federation of Trade Unions. The unification of divided trade unions and the undermining of the unity of the working class in these countries are not allowed.

Long live the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference!
Long live the great unity of workers around the world!
Long live the World Federation of Trade Unions!
Chairman of the Central Committee of Bulgarian Trade Unions, Leiko Damyanov
November 15

		Secretariat of the World Student Union
	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th. The Secretariat of the World Student Union sent a telegram congratulating the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. The telegram reads as follows: Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference:

	On behalf of the World Student Union, we send our congratulations to the conference and guarantee that 3.5 million students will unite to jointly strive for national independence and peace. We believe that the conference is of great significance and will strengthen the unity of the trade union movement under the banner of the World Federation of Trade Unions.

Secretariat of the World Student Union

### 争取亚澳各被压迫民族解放　保卫全世界工人阶级利益  我全国各地及世界工人团体进步组织电贺亚澳工会会议

1949-11-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　争取亚澳各被压迫民族解放
  　保卫全世界工人阶级利益         
    我全国各地及世界工人团体进步组织电贺亚澳工会会议
    【新华社北京十八日电】世界工联领导人员到达北京和亚澳工会会议开幕的喜讯，在中国工人阶级和广大人民中间激起热烈的欢迎和祝贺。天津、太原、开封、杭州和河北、河南、湖南、浙江等省市的总工会筹备委员会，纷纷驰电祝贺会议的开幕和会议的成功。
    　　　　天津总工会筹委会
    天津总工会筹委会的贺电说：我们天津工人热烈拥护这个大会，并准备彻底执行大会的一切决议。我们将更好的团结起来，为新中国的生产建设和肃清本国的反革命残余，巩固我们的新中国尽最大的努力；并支持亚洲澳洲及其他国家的民主工会的斗争，为世界的持久和平贡献出我们所有的力量。河北省总工会筹委会的贺电说：本会号召全省职工积极组织起来，壮大自己的队伍，坚决接受大会决议与付给的任务，以恢复与发展生产的实际行动支援各国工人的解放斗争。
    　　　　山西省二届工人代表大会
    正在太原市开会的山西省第二届工人代表大会在致世界工联总书记路易·赛扬和执行局委员的贺电中，表示愿在世界工联的领导下和全世界的工人兄弟团结一致，为保卫世界和平民主而斗争到底。山西省第二届工人代表大会并托出席亚澳工会会议的苏联代表团转达向全世界无产阶级的导师斯大林的致敬电。
    　　　　河南省总工会筹委会
    河南省总工会筹委会的贺电说：我们坚决在世界工联和中华全国总工会领导下，把全河南工人阶级组织和团结起来，与世界各国工人阶级团结一致，反对战争贩子的侵略阴谋，保卫世界和平，争取亚澳各被压迫民族的解放，为保卫全世界工人阶级的利益而奋斗到底。正在开会的开封市总工会首届会员代表大会，也以大会名义致电亚澳工会会议祝贺。此外，中共河南省委员会、河南省人民政府、人民解放军河南省军区司令部、政治部、中国新民主主义青年团河南省工作委员会、河南省民主青年联合会筹委会、河南省民主妇女联合会筹委会等机关团体，也纷纷致电亚澳工会会议祝贺。
    　　　　湖南省总工会筹委会
    湖南省总工会筹委会的贺电说：亚澳两洲工会会议的开幕，是两洲工人兄弟战斗经验的交流，是战胜共同敌人的大团结。湖南工人谨以高度的决心来增加生产，以作为向会议的献礼。
    　　　　浙江省总工会筹委会
    浙江省和杭州市总工会筹委会的贺电说：我们谨向会议敬献工人们自己以衷心的爱戴所织成的伟大工人阶级革命领袖列宁、斯大林、毛泽东、朱德绸织像四幅，借以表示全省、市数十万工人对于会议的热烈拥护与支持，并祝会议成功。
    　　　　中华全国文联
    【新华社北京十八日电】中华全国文学艺术界联合会顷电贺亚澳工会会议开幕，电文如下：亚洲澳洲工会会议主席团：
    谨向你们致同志的敬礼！并热烈祝贺你们会议的胜利。你们这一会议，说明了亚澳两洲工人阶级空前大团结的成功，并将决定亚澳两洲工人阶级彻底解放的命运。让我们为打倒殖民地制度，为打倒帝国主义，为保卫世界持久和平及争取人民民主而并肩奋斗。
    中华全国文学艺术界联合会主席郭沫若副主席茅盾周扬一九四九年十一月十七日
    　　　　东北总工会
    【新华社北京十八日电】东北总工会十六日电贺亚澳工会会议，并欢迎世界工联领导同志和各职工代表团到东北参观。电称：
    我们谨代表东北二百五十万职工以我们正在开展着的创纪录运动的新成果和工人群众的劳动业迹，庆贺盛大的亚洲、澳洲工会会议在我们首都的召开，并热烈地庆祝大会的成功。我们殷切地盼望路易·赛扬等世界工联各位领导同志和各职工代表团早日莅临东北参观，指导东北工运，使我们工会工作能够借此提高一步。
    　　　　天津各界人民
    【新华社天津十八日电】亚澳工会会议开幕的消息传来，天津市人民纷纷表示拥护和祝贺。天津各报都以显著地位发表会议开幕消息，并以绝大篇幅介绍亚澳工会活动情况。开幕已历半月的天津市工业展览会，决定延期至下月四日闭幕，以便招待出席亚澳工会会议的各国代表来津参观。现在工业展览会正在发动各单位准备各种展览品的英文说明，便利国际友人的了解。天津广大职工对于会议的开幕，尤其兴奋万状。天津铁路局很多职工听到会议开幕的消息后，高兴得跳起来。电信局工人关培厚说：这个会议振奋了我们工人阶级的心情。我们今后要在生产战线上更加努力生产以便迅速地建立一个崭新的繁荣的新中国，进而更有力地支援世界各被压迫民族的解放运动。天津产业工会联合会为庆祝大会的开幕，已定于二十日举办歌咏表演会，通过文艺活动来加强对于工人的国际主义教育。
    又，天津市产业工会联合会、天津市纺织业工会、天津市手工业工会、天津市海员工会、天津市店员工会和天津市民主妇女联合会等各团体，为庆祝亚澳工会会议开幕，都先后驰电祝贺。

    （一）世界工联化学工业产业部组织委员会贺电：亚洲澳洲工会会议：

    保加利亚的工人们坚信：代表会议将通过各种决议，制定各种具体方案来巩固亚洲各国劳动人民反对帝国主义侵略及新战争挑拨的斗争，巩固他们为反对残酷剥削、奴役劳动，为保卫劳动人民的切身利益，以及仿效英勇的中国人民及中国工人阶级的范例，为争取他们在这些国家内的民主权利及自由、并争取世界和平与民主的斗争。中国人民及中国工人阶级，在中国共产党的领导下，以其多年顽强的反对美英帝国主义者及其代理人的斗争，获得了胜利，建立了中华人民共和国。值此，保加利亚工人衷心地希望自己亚澳的弟兄们胜利地完成代表会议的工作。这次会议将进一步巩固不仅是亚澳工人阶级，而且是以世界工联为首的全世界工人阶级的友谊合作及职工的团结。分裂工会的统一及破坏这些国家工人阶级的团结是不允许的。
