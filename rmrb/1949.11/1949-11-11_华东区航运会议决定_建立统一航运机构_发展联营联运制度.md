Title: East China Navigation Conference Decides to Establish Unified Shipping Agency, Develop Joint Operation and Transportation System
Author:
Date: 1949-11-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 10th -- The East China Shipping Affairs Bureau's East China Shipping Affairs Conference, lasting six days, concluded here on the 7th. Attending the conference were representatives from shipping agencies in Fujian, Zhejiang, Northern Jiangsu, Southern Jiangsu, Northern Anhui, Southern Anhui, Shanghai, Nanjing, Qingdao, Zhenjiang, and other regions, as well as representatives from the Shanghai Seamen's Union, shipping companies, and the Planning Department of the East China Financial and Economic Committee, totaling 75 people. Based on the current needs of shipping business development and the specific conditions of the region, the conference decided that the main business content of the East China Shipping Affairs Bureau should be: managing shipping affairs, maritime administration, ports, waterways, and shipbuilding. The conference also passed the following resolutions: (1) Under the principle of unified leadership and decentralized management, unify maritime administrative leadership, establish a unified shipping organization, regulations, and systems for the East China region, and eliminate the phenomenon of independent operations. (2) Manage and transform all existing public and private shipping industries. Based on different conditions and needs in various regions, develop businesses in a planned and focused manner, and dredge rivers and allocate routes in a step-by-step manner. Also, unify the shipping industry's accounting system and tax system to reduce shipping costs. (3) Strengthen the cultivation and training of sailors and boatmen, and improve their technical level. (4) Public shipping industries should lead private shipping industries. On the basis of self-reliance, the government should provide appropriate assistance and necessary management to private shipping industries. (5) Develop a joint operation and transportation system to improve transportation efficiency, so as to gradually move towards a planned shipping industry.

### 华东区航运会议决定  建立统一航运机构  发展联营联运制度

1949-11-11
第2版()
专栏：

    华东区航运会议决定
    建立统一航运机构
    发展联营联运制度
    【新华社上海十日电】华东区航务管理局在此间召开的华东区航务会议，历时六天，已于七日结束。出席会议的有福建、浙江、苏北、苏南、皖北、皖南、上海、南京、青岛、镇江等地的航运机关，和上海海员工会、轮船业、华东区财政经济委员会计划部等单位的代表共七十五人。会议根据目前航运业务发展的需要与本区具体情况，决定华东区航务管理局的主要业务内容应为：管理航务、航政、港埠、河道及建造船舶等工作。会议并通过如下决议：（一）在统一领导分区管理的方针下，统一航政领导，建立华东区统一的航运机构、法规与制度，消除各自为政的分割现象。（二）管理和改造现有的一切公私营航业。根据各地不同的条件和需要，有计划有重点地发展业务，有步骤地疏浚河流和分配航线。并统一航运业会计制度，统一税收制度，以减低航运成本。（三）加强海员和水手的培养与训练，提高海员和水手的技术水平。（四）公营航业领导私营航业，在自力更生的基础上，由政府给予私营航业以适当帮助和必要的管理。（五）发展联营联运制度，提高运输效能，以便逐步走上计划性的航运事业。
