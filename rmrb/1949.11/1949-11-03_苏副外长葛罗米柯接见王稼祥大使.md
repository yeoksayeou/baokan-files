Title: Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Gromyko Receives Ambassador Wang Jiaxiang
Author: 
Date: 1949-11-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow Dispatch: TASS, Moscow—On November 1st, Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Gromyko received Wang Jiaxiang, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, to discuss Ambassador Wang's upcoming presentation of his credentials to Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

### 苏副外长葛罗米柯接见王稼祥大使

1949-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏副外长葛罗米柯接见王稼祥大使
    【新华社北京二日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：十一月一日，苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯，接见中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使王稼祥，商谈王大使即将向苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克呈递国书事。
