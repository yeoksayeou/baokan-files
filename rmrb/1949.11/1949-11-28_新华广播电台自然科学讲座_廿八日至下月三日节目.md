Title: Xinhua Broadcasting Station Natural Science Lectures: Programs from the 28th to the 3rd of Next Month
Author:
Date: 1949-11-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 26th. The programs for the natural science common knowledge lectures on the Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station from November 28th to December 3rd are as follows:

　　November 28th (Monday): A Brief Discussion of the Endocrine System
　　November 29th (Tuesday): Male Reproductive Function
　　November 30th (Wednesday): Female Reproductive Function
　　December 1st (Thursday): Growth, Aging, Disease, and Death
　　(The above four articles are all written by Cai Qiao, Director of the Department of Physiology, Nanjing University Medical College)
　　December 2nd (Friday): Sulfa Drugs (Li Xiuqin, Peking University Hospital)
　　December 3rd (Saturday): Streptomycin (Mu Kuijin, Peking University Hospital)

### 新华广播电台自然科学讲座　廿八日至下月三日节目

1949-11-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　新华广播电台自然科学讲座
  　廿八日至下月三日节目
    【新华社北京二十六日电】北京新华广播电台自然科学常识讲座自十一月二十八日起至十二月三日的节目如下：
    十一月二十八日（星期一）内分泌丛谈
    十一月二十九日（星期二）男性生殖作用
    十一月三十日（星期三）女性生殖作用
    十二月一日（星期四）生长衰老病死
    （以上四篇均由南京大学医学院生理系主任蔡翘撰稿）
    十二月二日（星期五）磺剂（北大医院李秀琴）
    十二月三日（星期六）链霉素（北大医院穆魁津）
