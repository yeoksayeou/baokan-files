Title: Changsha Holds First Women's Congress

Author:

Date: 1949-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 31st—The first Changsha Municipal Women's Congress was held on the 28th and 29th of this month. 135 delegates attended, of whom female workers accounted for more than one-quarter. The congress determined that the central task of women's work in Changsha in the future is to organize women to participate in economic, political power, cultural and educational construction, with the work of female workers as the focus of current work. The congress elected 21 preparatory committee members and established the preparatory committee of the Changsha Democratic Women's Federation. During the meeting, Wang Shoudao, Deputy Director of the Changsha Military Control Commission, and Cao Ying of the Changsha Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered speeches in person.

### 长沙市召开首届妇代会

1949-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　长沙市召开首届妇代会
    【新华社长沙卅一日电】长沙市首届各界妇女代表会议于本月二十八、二十九两日举行，出席代表一百三十五人，其中女工代表占四分之一以上。大会确定了今后长沙市妇女工作的中心任务是：组织妇女参加经济、政权、文化教育建设，而以女工工作作为目前工作的重点。大会选出筹备委员二十一人，成立了长沙市民主妇女联合会筹备委员会。会议期间，长沙市军管会副主任王首道及中共长沙市委曹瑛等都亲临讲话。
