Title: Soviet Writers Delegation Visits Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1949-11-21
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Lahore, 17th (TASS) – The Pakistan Progressive Writers Association warmly welcomed the Soviet writers' delegation led by Tikhonov. Crowds of peasants, workers, and intellectuals stood in the streets to welcome the Soviet writers upon their arrival here. At the welcoming meeting, Pakistani writers expressed their hope to develop and strengthen cultural relations between Pakistan and the Soviet Union. In his reply, the head of the Soviet writers' delegation, Tikhonov, told the audience how Soviet culture and art have flourished.

On November 16th, the Soviet writers' delegation led by Tikhonov visited historical sites and places of interest in Lahore.

### 苏作家代表团访问巴基斯坦

1949-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏作家代表团访问巴基斯坦
    【新华社北京二十日电】塔斯社拉合尔十七日讯，巴基斯坦进步作家协会热烈欢迎吉洪诺夫所率领的苏联作家代表团。成群的农民、工人和知识分子们站在街上欢迎苏联作家抵达此间。巴基斯坦作家在欢迎大会上，表示希望发展与加强巴苏两国文化关系。苏联作家代表团团长吉洪诺夫在致答词时，向听众讲述苏联的文化和艺术是如何繁荣起来的。
    十一月十六日，吉洪诺夫所率领的苏联作家代表团在拉合尔访问该地历史古迹及名胜。
