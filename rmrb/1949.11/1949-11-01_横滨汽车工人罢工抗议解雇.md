Title: Yokohama Auto Workers Strike to Protest Layoffs
Author:
Date: 1949-11-01
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 31st - AFP, Tokyo dispatch: 15,000 workers from the three major automobile manufacturing companies in Yokohama, including "Nissan" and "Toyota," went on strike on the 28th to protest the management's plan to massively lay off more than 3,500 workers and freeze wages at their current levels. On the morning of the same day, the unions of the three major companies held a joint mass rally in Kawasaki, near Yokohama, and finally held a demonstration to protest the "capitalist offensive."

### 横滨汽车工人罢工抗议解雇

1949-11-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　横滨汽车工人罢工抗议解雇
    【新华社北京三十一日电】据法新社东京讯：横滨的“日产”、“丰田”等三大汽车制造公司的工人一万五千名，于二十八日举行罢工，以抗议资方之大规模解雇三千五百余工人，并将工资冻结在目前水平的计划。同日晨，三大公司的工会于横滨附近的川崎举行联合群众大会，最后并举行示威，抗议“资本家的攻势”。
