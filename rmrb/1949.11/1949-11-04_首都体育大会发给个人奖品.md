Title: Awards Given to Individuals at the Capital Sports Meet
Author:
Date: 1949-11-04
Page: 4

　　【Our Newspaper's Dispatch】Following the conclusion of the Capital People's Sports Meet on October 24th, the group awards for all performances and competitions were distributed on the spot. There remained several individual prizes, including books and diaries, which, after discussion by the Organizing Committee, were decided to be awarded to athletes with excellent performance. All relevant units have been notified to inform the award recipients to collect them from the Statistics Section of the Municipal Education Bureau.

### 首都体育大会发给个人奖品

1949-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　首都体育大会发给个人奖品
    【本报讯】首都人民体育大会于十月二十四日闭幕后，所有表演及各项竞赛节目之团体奖已当场发出，尚余书籍、日记本等多种奖品，经大会讨论，决定分奖成绩优良的运动员。现已通知各单位转知受奖人员，到本市教育局统计股领取。
