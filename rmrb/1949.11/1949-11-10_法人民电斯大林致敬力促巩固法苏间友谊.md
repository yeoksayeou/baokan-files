Title: French People Send Telegram to Stalin Expressing Respect and Promoting Consolidation of Franco-Soviet Friendship
Author:
Date: 1949-11-10
Page: 3

TASS, Paris: The French working people warmly commemorated the 32nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The celebration meeting held by the Franco-Soviet Friendship Association took place on the evening of the 6th in the Pleyel Hall in Paris. The hall was packed with people. Amidst the prolonged applause of the audience, General Petit, on behalf of the friends of the Soviet Union, paid tribute to France's great ally – the Soviet Union and its leader, Marshal Stalin. The Chairman of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Boyer, and the President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Madame Gordon, both spoke at the meeting. The meeting enthusiastically adopted a telegram to the leader of the world labor movement, Stalin, expressing respect, and unanimously passed a resolution to promote the consolidation of Franco-Soviet friendship.

In other cities in France, many gatherings were also held to celebrate the October Revolution. At a celebration meeting of 15,000 people in Tarbes (Hautes-Pyrénées), Thorez delivered a speech. The participants of the meeting unanimously adopted a resolution expressing warm tribute to the Soviet people and their leader Stalin.

### 法人民电斯大林致敬力促巩固法苏间友谊

1949-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　法人民电斯大林致敬力促巩固法苏间友谊
    【新华社北京九日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：法国劳动人民热烈地纪念伟大的十月社会主义革命三十二周年。法苏友好协会所举办的庆祝大会，于六日晚在巴黎泼莱耶尔大厅举行。厅中挤满了人。在听众的历久不息的鼓掌中，伯蒂将军代表苏联的朋友向法国的伟大盟国——苏联及其领袖斯大林大元帅致敬。国际民主青联主席鲍埃逊和国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人，都在大会上讲了话。大会极其热烈地通过向世界劳工运动领袖斯大林致敬电，并一致通过决议，力促巩固法苏友谊。
    在法国的其他城市中，也举行了许多庆祝十月革命的集会。在塔尔勃（上比利牛斯省）的一万五千人的庆祝大会上，多列士曾发表演说。大会参加者一致通过热烈向苏联人民及其领袖斯大林致敬的决议。
