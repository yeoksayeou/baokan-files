Title: Epidemic Prevention Committee, Ministry of Health, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Others Hold Grand Gathering to See Off Soviet Anti-Epidemic Personnel; Dong Biwu Delivers Speech Thanking Stalin and the Soviet Government
Author:
Date: 1949-11-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd – In order to celebrate and thank Dr. Raksin, a Soviet medical expert who completed the plague prevention and control work in Chahar Province and is about to return to his country, and the Soviet anti-epidemic personnel led by Maisky, the Central Anti-Epidemic Committee, the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters jointly held a grand farewell meeting at 3:00 p.m. today. More than 500 representatives from 67 units, including the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and the Beijing Municipal People's Government, attended the farewell meeting. Dong Biwu, Chairman of the Central Anti-Epidemic Committee, delivered the opening speech. He introduced the work enthusiasm and achievements of the Soviet experts and anti-epidemic personnel, and pointed out that this great revolutionary spirit and combat actions are worthy of the Chinese people's learning. Chairman Dong also thanked Marshal Stalin and the Soviet government on behalf of the Central Anti-Epidemic Committee for their concern and help for China's plague prevention and control work. Huang Shaohong, a member of the Central Anti-Epidemic Committee, He Cheng, a member of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission and Minister of Health, Qian Junrui, a representative of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, Khokholova, the first squad leader of the anti-epidemic team, and Maisky, the chief representative of the anti-epidemic staff, then spoke. During the farewell meeting, Chairman Dong Biwu and Minister of Health Li Dequan, on behalf of the Central Anti-Epidemic Committee, awarded flags, commemorative medals, and gifts to Soviet experts and anti-epidemic personnel respectively. In addition, representatives from ten agencies and organizations, including the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Anti-Epidemic Committee, presented flags and flowers, expressing the Chinese people's heartfelt gratitude and love for the friendly Soviet Union. The meeting ended at 5:30 p.m., and a soiree was held in the evening to entertain Soviet experts and all members of the anti-epidemic team.

### 防疫委会卫生部外交部等盛大集会　欢送苏联防疫工作人员  董必武致词感谢斯大林及苏联政府

1949-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　防疫委会卫生部外交部等盛大集会
　　欢送苏联防疫工作人员     
    董必武致词感谢斯大林及苏联政府
    【新华社北京二十二日电】为了庆祝和感谢完成了察北防治鼠疫工作并即将回国的苏联医学专家拉克森博士和以马意斯基为首的苏联防疫工作人员，中央防疫委员会、中央人民政府卫生部、中央人民政府外交部和中苏友好协会总会今天下午三时特联合举行盛大的欢送会。参加欢送会的有中华全国民主妇女联合会、北京市人民政府等六十七个单位的代表五百余人。中央防疫委员会主任委员董必武致开会词。他介绍了苏联专家和防疫工作人员的工作热情和成绩，指出这种伟大的革命精神和战斗动作是值得中国人民学习的。董主任委员并且代表中央防疫委员会感谢斯大林大元帅以及苏联政府对于中国防治鼠疫工作的关心和帮助。接着讲话的有中央防疫委员会委员黄绍竑，中国人民革命军事委员会委员卫生部部长贺诚，中苏友好协会总会代表钱俊瑞，防疫队第一分队长霍荷洛娃，防疫工作人员总代表马意斯基。在欢送会中，董必武主任委员和卫生部李德全部长代表中央防疫委员会分别向苏联专家和防疫工作人员授旗授纪念章和授礼物。此外，并有中苏友好协会总会、北京市防疫委员会等十个机关团体代表献旗献花，衷心表示中国人民对友邦苏联的感谢和热爱。大会在下午五时半结束，晚间举行晚会招待苏联专家和防疫队全体队员。
