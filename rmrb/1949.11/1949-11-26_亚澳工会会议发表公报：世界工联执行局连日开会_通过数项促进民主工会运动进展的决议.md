Title: Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference Issues Communiqué: World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Bureau Holds Consecutive Meetings, Adopts Several Resolutions to Promote Progress of Democratic Trade Union Movement
Author:
Date: 1949-11-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 25th: The Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference today issued the following communiqué:

　　The Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions held a meeting in Beijing on November 24th and 25th. The Executive Bureau discussed the following issues:

　　1. Regarding the activities of international solidarity of Trade Unions (Industrial Departments of the World Federation of Trade Unions);

　　2. Regarding the activities of the World Federation of Trade Unions in the United Nations Economic and Social Council;

　　3. Regarding the summary of the International Day for the Struggle for Peace;

　　4. Regarding the final accounts of the World Federation of Trade Unions and the budget for 1950.

　　Resolutions have been passed on these issues, which will promote further development of the democratic trade union movement. The Executive Bureau agreed to the final accounts of the World Federation of Trade Unions and approved the budget for 1950.

　　Meanwhile, the various committees of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference held meetings. Tonight, the committee elected by the General Assembly will review the draft of the General Assembly's letter to the working people of capitalist countries. The committee will continue to prepare for the work of the General Assembly's resolutions.

　　In the past few days, members of various delegations to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference visited factories and cultural institutions in the Chinese capital, and held gatherings in many factories.

　　The Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions will continue to meet at the All-China Federation of Trade Unions at 11:00 am tomorrow.

### 亚澳工会会议发表公报：世界工联执行局连日开会  通过数项促进民主工会运动进展的决议

1949-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　亚澳工会会议发表公报：世界工联执行局连日开会
    通过数项促进民主工会运动进展的决议
    【新华社北京二十五日电】亚澳工会会议今日发表公报如下：
    十一月二十四、二十五日世界工联执行局在北京开会。执行局讨论了下列问题：
    一、关于职工会（世界工联产业部）的国际团结的活动；
    二、关于世界工联在联合国经济社会理事会中的活动；
    三、关于国际争取和平斗争日进行的总结；
    四、关于世界工联的决算和一九五○年的预算。
    在这些问题上已通过了决议，这些决议将促进民主工会运动进一步的发展。执行局同意世界工联的决算并批准了一九五○年的预算。
    同时，亚澳工会会议的各委员会举行了会议。今晚由大会选举的委员会将审查大会告资本主义国家劳动人民书的草稿。该委员会将继续准备大会决议的工作。
    这几天来，亚澳工会会议各代表团团员参观了中国首都的工厂和文化机关，在许多工厂中举行了集会。
    世界工联执行局将于明日十一时在中华全国总工会继续开会。
