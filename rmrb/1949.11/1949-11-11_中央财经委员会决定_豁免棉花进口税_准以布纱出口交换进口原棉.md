Title: Central Financial and Economic Commission Decides to Exempt Cotton Import Tax and Allow Export of Cloth and Yarn in Exchange for Imported Raw Cotton
Author:
Date: 1949-11-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th. To accommodate the production needs of some private yarn factories, the Central People's Government's Financial and Economic Commission has recently decided that private manufacturers applying to import raw cotton will be allowed to exchange it for exports of domestically produced cotton cloth and cotton yarn. Furthermore, in order to encourage the use of self-provided foreign exchange for imports and to safeguard the interests of manufacturers in the exchange process, all cotton import taxes will be exempted.

### 中央财经委员会决定  豁免棉花进口税  准以布纱出口交换进口原棉

1949-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中央财经委员会决定
    豁免棉花进口税
    准以布纱出口交换进口原棉
    【新华社北京十日电】为照顾部分私营纱厂的生产需要，中央人民政府财政经济委员会顷决定：今后凡私营厂商申请进口原棉，准以国产棉布棉纱出口交换；并为奖励自备外汇进口及照顾厂商在交换中的利益起见，棉花进口税一律予以豁免。
