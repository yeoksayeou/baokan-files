Title: Beijing's First Women's Congress Scheduled to Open Tomorrow
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 4

Beijing’s First Women’s Congress Scheduled to Open Tomorrow

[Our Newspaper] Beijing: The first Beijing Women's Congress is scheduled to officially open at 2:00 PM tomorrow (the 26th) in the auditorium of the Sino-French University. The main tasks of the congress are to summarize the women's work in Beijing since liberation, determine the future direction and tasks of the women's movement in Beijing, and exchange work experiences of women in various positions. The congress will adopt the charter of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation and elect the first executive committee, and also elect six representatives from Beijing to attend the Asian Women's Representative Conference. All three hundred and seventy-one delegates attending the congress have reported in. The delegates include female workers, female students, housewives, female teachers, women workers, childcare workers, medical workers, literary and drama workers, etc. The meeting is scheduled to last four days.
                   　　　　　　　　(Bai Sheng)

### 京市首次妇代大会定明日揭幕
柏生
1949-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市首次妇代大会定明日揭幕
    【本报讯】北京市第一次妇女代表大会定于明（二十六）日下午二时在中法大学礼堂正式揭幕。大会的主要任务是总结北京市解放以来的妇女工作和确定今后北京妇运方针与任务，并交流各个岗位妇女的工作经验。大会将通过北京市民主妇女联合会的章程及选举第一届执委，同时并选出北京市出席亚洲妇女代表会议的六名代表。出席大会的三百七十一名代表已全部报到，代表成份包括女工、女学生、家庭劳动妇女、女教员、妇女工作者、儿童保育工作者、医务工作者、文艺戏剧工作者等。会期规定四日。
                   　　　　　　　　（柏生）
