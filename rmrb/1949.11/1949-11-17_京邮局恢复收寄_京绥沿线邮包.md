Title: Beijing Post Office Resumes Accepting and Sending Parcel Post Along the Beijing-Suiyuan Railway Line
Author:
Date: 1949-11-17
Page: 2

　　Beijing Post Office Resumes Accepting and Sending
  　Parcel Post Along the Beijing-Suiyuan Railway Line
    [Beijing News] The Beijing-Suiyuan Railway has resumed traffic on the 16th. The Beijing Postal Administration and its branches will resume accepting and sending all kinds of parcels weighing up to 30 kilograms to various places along the Beijing-Suiyuan Railway line from the same date.

### 京邮局恢复收寄　京绥沿线邮包

1949-11-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　京邮局恢复收寄
  　京绥沿线邮包
    【北京讯】京绥铁路已自十六日起恢复通车，北京邮政管理局及所属各支局于同日起恢复收寄京绥铁路沿线各地三十公斤以内各种包裹。
