Title: Ethnic Minority Cultural Relics Exhibition Opens Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-11-05
Page: 4

　　Ethnic Minority Cultural Relics
  　Exhibition Opens Yesterday
    [Beijing News] The National Tsinghua University held a preview exhibition of cultural relics from Taiwan, Tibet, and ethnic minorities in the Southwest at the auditorium of the National Art College in Beijing yesterday (the 4th). Attendees included Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Ulanhu, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Government Administration Council; Committee Member Wang Zaitian; Soviet Ambassador to China Roshchin; Xie Xuehong, Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League; Wu Julongzang, a Yenching University anthropologist who was one of the first to study the culture of the Gaoshan people in Taiwan; as well as Jian Bozan, Ai Qing, Xu Beihong, and many others. The exhibits in this exhibition were collected by Tsinghua University in the Southwest during the Anti-Japanese War, and donated to Tsinghua University by Mr. Ding Huikang after being transported from Shanghai after many years of collection. The exhibition includes life photos, clothing, shoes and hats, utensils, firearms and weapons, Buddha statues, musical instruments, extremely precious totem plates, ancestor door panels for men and women, and exquisite women's embroidery. The exhibition will last for three days, from today to the 7th, and all sectors are welcome to visit.

### 少数民族文物昨起展览

1949-11-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　少数民族文物
  　昨起展览
    【北京讯】国立清华大学于昨（四）日在北京国立艺专礼堂举行台湾、西藏、西南少数民族文物预展。到场参观者有中央人民政府副主席李济深、政务院民族事务委员会副主任委员乌兰夫、委员王再天、苏联驻华大使罗申、台湾民主自治同盟主席谢雪红、燕大人类学家最早研究台湾高山族文化的乌居龙藏，及翦伯赞、艾青、徐悲鸿等多人。此次展览会之展览品，为清华大学于抗战期间在西南搜集及丁惠康先生多年收藏现由上海运来捐赠清华大学的。展览内容有生活照片、服饰、鞋帽、器皿、枪支武器、佛像、乐器、极珍贵的图腾版、男女祖先门板及妇女精致刺绣等多种。展览会自今日起至七日止，共展览三天，欢迎各界参观。
