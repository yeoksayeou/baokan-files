Title: Our Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, Wang Jiaxiang, Presents Credentials; Shvernik Receives Him at the Kremlin
Author:
Date: 1949-11-05
Page: 1

　　Our Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union,
    Wang Jiaxiang, Presents Credentials
  　Shvernik Receives Him at the Kremlin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th] TASS, Moscow, 3rd: The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Shvernik, received Mr. Wang Jiaxiang, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, at the Kremlin on November 3rd, to accept his credentials. Ambassador Wang Jiaxiang was accompanied by Counselor of the Embassy, Mr. Zeng Yongquan, Mr. Ge Baoquan, Mr. Xu Jiepan, and Military Attaché of the Embassy, Lieutenant General Bian Zhangwu, and Secretary of the Embassy, Mr. Zhang Guan. Ambassador Wang introduced them to Shvernik one by one. Those participating in the ceremony included Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Lavrentiev, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Gorkin, Acting Director of the First Far Eastern Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Kerdyukov, and Acting Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Bushuyev.
    After the presentation of credentials, Shvernik spoke with Mr. Wang Jiaxiang, with Lavrentiev also present.

### 我驻苏特命全权大使  王稼祥呈递国书　什维尔尼克在克里姆林宫接见

1949-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　我驻苏特命全权大使
    王稼祥呈递国书
  　什维尔尼克在克里姆林宫接见
    【新华社北京四日电】塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克于十一月三日在克里姆林宫接见呈递国书的中华人民共和国驻苏特命全权大使王稼祥先生。王稼祥大使率领大使馆参赞曾涌泉先生、戈宝权先生、徐介藩先生和大使馆武官边章五中将、大使馆秘书张观先生，王大使并一一向什维尔尼克介绍。参加典礼者有苏联副外长拉夫伦捷夫、苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金、苏联外交部第一远东司代理司长克尔久柯夫、苏联外交部交际司代理司长布舒也夫。
    呈递国书后，什维尔尼克曾与王稼祥先生谈话，拉夫伦捷夫亦在座。
