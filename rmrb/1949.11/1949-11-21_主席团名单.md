　　Presidium List
    [Beijing News] The second session of the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference yesterday (the 20th) unanimously approved the list of 29 members of the Presidium proposed by the first session of the People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee. The complete list is as follows (in order of the number of strokes in the surnames):
    Wang Wenbin, Wang Fu, Gu Qizong, Zhu Changjiang, Yu Xinqing, Ma Yuhuai, Liang Sicheng, Xu Beihong, Chai Zemin, Weng Dujian, Chen Mingde, Zhang Youyu, Zhang Zhixiang, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Xiaomei, Xu Deheng, Peng Zhen, Fu Huating, Yang Bozhen, Wen Jiasi, Liu Ren, Liu Yifeng, Liu Tong'en, Li Jinxi, Qian Duansheng, Xue Chengye, Han Zhuoru, Nie Rongzhen, Xiao Ming
    The conference also approved Xue Zizheng as Secretary-General and Cui Yueli, Zhang Wensong, Cao Zhilin, Guo Zechen, Liao Mosha, and Sun Fuling as Deputy Secretaries-General.
    The first meeting of the Presidium elected Peng Zhen, Nie Rongzhen, Yu Xinqing, Wen Jiasi, Han Zhuoru, Xu Deheng, Zhang Xiruo, Xiao Ming, Zhang Xiaomei, Yang Bozhen, and Fu Huating as the eleven members of the Standing Presidium.

### 主席团名单

1949-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　主席团名单
    【北京讯】北京市第二届各界人民代表会议昨（二十）日会议一致通过由第一届各界人民代表会议协商委员会提出的主席团二十九人，全部名单录后：（以姓氏笔划多少为序）
    王文斌、王甫、古奇踪、朱长江、余心清、马玉槐、梁思成、徐悲鸿、柴泽民、翁独健、陈铭德、张友渔、张致祥、张奚若、张晓梅、许德珩、彭真、傅华亭、杨伯箴、闻家驷、刘仁、刘一峰、刘同恩、黎锦熙、钱端升、薛成业、韩卓儒、聂荣臻、萧明
    并由大会通过，以薛子正为秘书长、崔月犁、张文松、曹志麟、郭则忱、廖沫沙、孙孚凌等六人为副秘书长。
    主席团第一次会议推选彭真、聂荣臻、余心清、闻家驷、韩卓儒、许德珩、张奚若、萧明、张晓梅、杨伯箴、傅华亭等十一人为常务主席。
