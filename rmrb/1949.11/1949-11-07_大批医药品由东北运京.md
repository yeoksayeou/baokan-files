Title: Large Quantity of Medicines Transported from Northeast to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-11-07
Page: 4

　　Large Quantity of Medicines Transported from Northeast to Beijing
　　[Beijing News] A large quantity of medicines and medical equipment, totaling fifty-six boxes, arrived in Beijing yesterday morning (the 6th), transported from the Northeast. This includes vaccines and serums for plague, typhoid, diphtheria, cholera, and dysentery, as well as equipment for epidemic prevention, treatment, testing, and dissection.

### 大批医药品由东北运京

1949-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　大批医药品由东北运京
    【北京讯】东北运来大批药品及医务器材共五十六箱，昨（六日）晨已抵京，其中包括鼠疫、伤寒、白喉、霍乱、赤痢等疫苗及血清以及防疫、治疗化验、解剖等器材。
