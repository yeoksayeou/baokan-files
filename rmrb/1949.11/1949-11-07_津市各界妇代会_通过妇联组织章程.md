Title: Tianjin Municipal All-Circles Women's Congress Passes Federation of Women's Organizations Charter
Author:
Date: 1949-11-07
Page: 4

　　Tianjin Municipal All-Circles Women's Congress
　　Passes Federation of Women's Organizations Charter
　　[Our newspaper's Tianjin dispatch] The first Tianjin Municipal All-Circles Women's Congress continued its session on the 6th, with 484 representatives attending. After heated discussions, all representatives passed the "Tianjin Municipal Democratic Women's Federation Organizational Charter." All representatives unanimously expressed their willingness to, under the leadership of the Women's Federation, strive to improve their political, cultural, and technical skills, and struggle for the construction of a people's Tianjin. In the afternoon, the election method was approved, and it is scheduled to close on the 7th after the election of the executive committee.

### 津市各界妇代会　通过妇联组织章程

1949-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　津市各界妇代会
  　通过妇联组织章程
    【本报记者天津专电】津市首届各界妇女代表大会于六日继续开会，到代表四百八十四人。经热烈讨论后，全体代表通过“天津市民主妇女联合会组织章程”，全体代表一致表示：愿在妇联领导下，努力提高政治、文化、技术、为建设人民的天津市而奋斗。下午通过选举法，预定于七日选举执委后闭幕。
