Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-11-16
Page: 2

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
  　Adjusted Starting Yesterday
    [Shanhaiguan News] The joint office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank in Shanhaiguan has changed its exchange rate, effective yesterday (the 15th), to 1 RMB equaling 20 Northeast Yuan.

### 山海关货币牌价　昨日起调整

1949-11-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币牌价
  　昨日起调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行。东北银行山海关联合办事处自昨（十五）日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券二十元。
