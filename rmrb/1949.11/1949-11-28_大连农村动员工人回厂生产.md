Title: Dalian Rural Areas Mobilize Workers to Return to Factories for Production
Author:
Date: 1949-11-28
Page: 2

The Dalian County accepted the opinions of the People's Representative Conference and mobilized rural technical workers to return to factories for production. Up to now, more than 440 workers have been mobilized to return to factories for production in various districts. According to county-wide statistics, there are a total of 722 technical workers. In addition to those who have already returned to factories, 282 are still waiting to be transferred to factories.

### 大连农村动员工人回厂生产

1949-11-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　大连农村动员工人回厂生产
    【本报讯】大连县接受人民代表会议的意见，动员农村技术工人回厂生产，到目前止各区动员回厂生产的已有四百四十余名。据全县统计，共有七百二十二名技术工人，除目前已回到工厂者外，尚有二百八十二名正待转入工厂。
