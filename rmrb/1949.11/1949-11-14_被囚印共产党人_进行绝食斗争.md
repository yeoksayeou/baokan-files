Title: Imprisoned Indian Communists Wage Hunger Strike

Author:

Date: 1949-11-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th -- According to a United Press report from Allahabad on the 8th: Communist prisoners in certain jails of the Central Provinces of India are staging a hunger strike to protest the abuse they are suffering in the prisons. More than 200 people are participating in the hunger strike. Furthermore, according to an Agence France-Presse dispatch from New Delhi on October 28th: The number of Communists currently detained in prisons in various provinces of India (excluding former princely states) has reached over 2,500, of whom only 119 have been tried.

### 被囚印共产党人  进行绝食斗争

1949-11-14
第3版()
专栏：

    被囚印共产党人
    进行绝食斗争
    【新华社北京十日电】据合众社阿拉哈巴八日讯：被囚在印度中央省某些监狱中的共产党人，正在进行绝食斗争，以抗议监狱中对他们的虐待。参加绝食斗争的有二百余人。又据法新社新德里十月二十八日消息：目前被拘在印度各省（前王公土邦不在内）监狱中的共产党人，已达二千五百余人，其中只有一百十九人经过审讯。
