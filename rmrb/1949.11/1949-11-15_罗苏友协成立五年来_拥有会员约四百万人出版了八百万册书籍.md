Title: The Romanian-Soviet Friendship Association Celebrates its Fifth Anniversary: Approximately Four Million Members, Eight Million Books Published
Author:
Date: 1949-11-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest: TASS reports that the Romanian-Soviet Friendship Association celebrated its fifth anniversary on the 12th. Over the past five years, the Association has made significant contributions to familiarizing the Romanian working people with Soviet life. It has become the largest mass organization in Romania, boasting approximately four million members and 17,230 branches throughout the country. The Association's large printing house, "Cartea Rusa" (meaning "Russian Book"), had published 676 kinds of Soviet books in Romanian, Hungarian, and other languages, totaling eight million copies, by November 1st of this year, supplying 84,000 libraries of trade unions and cultural centers. The exhibitions, discussion meetings, and evening gatherings organized by the Association were held approximately 200,000 times during the period from February to September alone.

### 罗苏友协成立五年来  拥有会员约四百万人出版了八百万册书籍

1949-11-15
第3版()
专栏：

    罗苏友协成立五年来
    拥有会员约四百万人出版了八百万册书籍
    【新华社北京十三日电】塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：十二日是罗苏友好协会成立五周年纪念日。五年以来，协会在使罗马尼亚劳动人民熟识苏联生活上贡献极大。它已成为罗马尼亚最大的群众性组织，拥有会员约四百万人，有一万七千二百三十个分会分布在全国各地。协会的大印刷所“卡提亚·罗沙”（意为“俄罗斯之书”），至今年十一月一日止，已经用罗马尼亚文、匈牙利文及其他文字出版了六百七十六种苏联书籍，共达八百万册，供给了工会和文化之家的八万四千个图书馆。协会举办的展览会、讨论会和晚会等，仅在本年二月至九月期间，就举行了约二十万次。
