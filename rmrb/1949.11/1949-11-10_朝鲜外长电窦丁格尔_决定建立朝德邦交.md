Title: Korean Foreign Minister Cables Dürrenger – Decision to Establish Korean-German Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1949-11-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th. The Democratic People's Republic of Korea has decided to establish diplomatic relations with the German Democratic Republic. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Park Hon-yong, telegraphed the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Dürrenger, on the 7th, stating: The government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea heartily congratulates the establishment of the German Democratic Republic and the provisional government. The establishment of the peace-loving and democratic German Democratic Republic is a great victory for the people of the world in their struggle for peace and democracy. The government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has decided to establish diplomatic relations with the German Democratic Republic. The government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is deeply convinced: The establishment of diplomatic relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the German Democratic Republic will enhance the friendship between our two peoples and will promote the consolidation of peace throughout the world. I hope you will accept my sincere respect.

### 朝鲜外长电窦丁格尔　决定建立朝德邦交

1949-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜外长电窦丁格尔
  　决定建立朝德邦交
    【新华社平壤九日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国决定与德意志民主共和国建立外交关系，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外交部部长朴宪永七日致电德意志民主共和国外交部部长窦丁格尔称：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府衷心庆贺德意志民主共和国和临时政府的成立。爱好和平民主的德意志民主共和国的成立，是全世界人民为和平民主而斗争的巨大胜利。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府，已决定和德意志民主共和国间建立外交关系。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府深信：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和德意志民主共和国建立外交关系，将增进我们两国人民间的友谊，并会促进全世界的和平更加巩固。希望阁下接受我的衷心敬意。
