Title: With the Consent of Our Central People's Government, the Soviet Union Establishes a Consulate General in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1949-11-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd: With the consent of our Central People's Government, the Soviet Embassy in China has established a Consulate General in Tianjin, and appointed Mr. Gidov, the former Acting Soviet Consul General in Tianjin, as Acting Consul General. After receiving the order, Mr. Gidov, accompanied by his secretary, paid a formal visit to Mayor Huang Jing of Tianjin at the Tianjin Municipal Government at 9:00 AM on the 2nd to discuss matters related to the establishment of the consulate. Mayor Huang Jing, Deputy Mayor Liu Xiufeng, Secretary-General Wu Yannong, and Director and Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office, Zhang Wenjin and Yang Gongsu, received them. The meeting was very cordial. Mr. Gidov and his party left after a conversation of about half an hour. The Soviet Consulate General in Tianjin officially opened on the 2nd.

### 征得我中央人民政府同意　苏在津设总领事馆

1949-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　征得我中央人民政府同意
  　苏在津设总领事馆
    【新华社北京三日电】苏联驻华大使馆征得我中央人民政府同意，在天津设立总领事馆，并任命原苏联驻津代理总领事吉多福为代理总领事。吉多福接奉命令后，于二日上午九时偕秘书赴津市府见黄敬市长，作正式拜会，并商谈设馆事宜。当由市长黄敬、副市长刘秀峰偕同秘书长吴砚农与外侨事务处正副处长章文晋、杨公素等接见，双方晤谈极为融洽。吉多福等于谈约半小时后辞去。苏联驻津总领事馆当即于二日正式开馆。
