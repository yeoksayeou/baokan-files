Title: Representatives to Asian Women's Conference Elected in Jiangxi Province and Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan and Other Cities
Author:
Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 1

　　Jiangxi Province and Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan and Other Cities
　　Representatives to Asian Women's Conference Elected
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd — Representatives to the Asian Women's Conference from Jiangxi, Nanjing, Wuhan and other places have been elected one after another. The representative from Jiangxi Province is Wei Xiuying, Secretary of the Women's Work Committee of the CPC Jiangxi Provincial Committee. She left Ganzhou on the 20th of this month to come to Beijing. At a meeting of women representatives from all walks of life convened by the preparatory committee of the Nanjing Municipal Democratic Women's Federation on the 20th of this month, Chen Xiuliang, director of the preparatory committee of the Nanjing Municipal Women's Federation, and Tao Bin, executive committee member and deputy director of the student union of Nanjing University, were selected as representatives of the city to attend the Asian Women's Conference. The representatives from Wuhan were elected by the executive committee of the Wuhan Municipal Democratic Women's Federation on the 15th of this month. The elected were: the official representative, Qi Yuande, director of the Wuhan Municipal Women's Federation; and the alternate representative, He Ming, director of the Women Workers' Department of the Women's Federation. They will come to Beijing at the end of this month. Exhibits from Wuhan women to the Asian Women's Conference have been delivered to Beijing. The exhibits include photos of women's lives and work in Wuhan, as well as various exquisite embroideries.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 20th — The preparatory committee of the Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation held its fourth preparatory committee meeting on the 18th of this month, electing six representatives from Shanghai to attend the Asian Women's Conference. Those elected were: Zhang Yun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation; Zhou Xingmei, representative of female workers; Su Shuyuan, representative of female students; Ying Renzhen, representative of women and children welfare workers; Min Shufen, representative of the industrial sector; and Hu Xiufeng, director of the Shanghai Federation of Housewives.

### 江西省及沪宁汉等市　亚洲妇代会代表选出

1949-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　江西省及沪宁汉等市
　　亚洲妇代会代表选出
    【新华社北京二十三日电】江西、南京、武汉等地出席亚洲妇女代表会议的代表，已先后选出。江西省的代表为中共江西省委妇女工作委员会书记危秀英，她已于本月二十日离赣来京。南京市民主妇联筹委会在本月廿日召集的各界妇女代表会议上，选派该市妇联筹委会主任陈修良和南京大学学生会执委兼副主任陶彬为该市出席亚洲妇代会代表。武汉市的代表，于本月十五日由该市民主妇联执委会选出。当选者有正式代表武汉市妇联会主任戚元德、候补代表妇联女工部部长何铭，她们将于本月底来京。武汉市妇女赠给亚洲妇代会的展览品已送达北京。展览品中有武汉妇女生活和工作照片及精美刺绣等多种。
    【新华社上海二十日电】上海民主妇女联合会筹委会本月十八日举行第四次筹委会，选举上海出席亚洲妇女代表会议的六位代表。当选的为：上海民主妇联筹委会主任章蕴，女工代表周杏妹，女学生代表苏淑媛，妇女儿童福利工作者代表应仁珍，产业界代表闵淑芬，上海家庭妇女联合会主任胡绣枫。
