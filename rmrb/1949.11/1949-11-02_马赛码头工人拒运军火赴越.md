Title: Marseilles Dockworkers Refuse to Ship Arms to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1949-11-02
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st – French dockworkers are actively supporting the Vietnamese people's liberation war for national independence. According to Agence France-Presse's Marseilles dispatch, the Marseilles dockworkers, affiliated with the French General Confederation of Labour (CGT), decided on October 30th to refuse to load munitions and weapons destined for Vietnam to massacre the Vietnamese people, starting from November 2nd. The Dockworkers' Union affiliated with the CGT (with 35,000 members) has also called on other unions to take joint action to support the Vietnamese people's liberation war.

### 马赛码头工人拒运军火赴越

1949-11-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　马赛码头工人拒运军火赴越
    【新华社北京一日电】法国码头工人积极支援越南人民争取民族独立的解放战争。据法新社马赛消息称，法国总工会所属的马赛码头工人，十月三十日决定从十一月二日起，拒绝装运军火与武器赴越南，屠杀越南人民。总工会所属之码头工人工会（拥有会员三万五千人）亦号召其他工会采取共同行动，支援越南人民的解放战争。
