Title: Xinhua Radio Station Tonight's Program Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-11-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua Radio Station
    Tonight's Program Highlights
    [This newspaper reports] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will, at 20:15 today, introduce one of the countries participating in the Asian and Australasian Trade Union Conference and the Asian Women's Representative Conference: Korea. At 19:15, the special broadcast on the Asian Women's Representative Conference will broadcast in English the full text of the broadcast speech by Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, on the 9th, titled "Welcoming the Asian Women's Representative Conference". At 18:15, the workers' program will broadcast: (1) "Establishing a national industrial union is the central link in organizing trade unions at present," an editorial from the Workers' Daily; (2) "Introduction to the Soviet Shop Employees' Union." At 19:00, a lecture on general knowledge of natural science: "The Morphology and Function of Blood." At 18:30, the entertainment program will feature a new form of storytelling—Qinshu "The Transformation of Shi Yaozong".

### 新华电台  今晚要目

1949-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

    新华电台
    今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天二十点十五分，介绍参加亚洲、澳洲工会会议，和亚洲妇女代表会议的国家之一——朝鲜。十九点十五分关于亚洲妇女代表会议专题广播时间，今天用英语播送中华全国民主妇联主席蔡畅九号在该台广播词“迎接亚洲妇女代表会议”全文。十八点十五分职工节目，播送（一）工人日报社论：“建立全国性的产业工会是目前组织工会的中心环节”；（二）“介绍苏联的店员工会”。十九点自然科学常识讲座：“血液的形态和功能”。十八点三十分文艺节目，新曲艺——琴书“史耀宗转变”。
