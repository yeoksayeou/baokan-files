Title: Marshal Rokossovsky Appointed Polish Minister of National Defense with the Approval of the Soviet Government to Return to the Motherland to Serve
Author:
Date: 1949-11-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th (TASS): Polish President Bierut, considering Soviet Marshal Rokossovsky's Polish nationality and his prestige among the Polish people, specially requested the Soviet government to transfer Marshal Rokossovsky to the Polish government for service in the Polish army. The Soviet government, based on the friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Poland, and because Marshal Rokossovsky indicated that this matter could be entirely decided by the Soviet government, has agreed to President Bierut's request, and in accordance with the instructions of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, relieved Marshal Rokossovsky of his duties in the Soviet army and sent him back to the Polish government for assignment.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th: According to a report by the Polish News Agency on the 7th, Polish Minister of National Defense, Marshal Zymierski, requested to be relieved of his duties, which has been approved by the Polish government. The Polish government has decided to appoint Rokossovsky as Marshal of Poland and to succeed him as Minister of National Defense of the Polish government.

The letters from Polish President Bierut and Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz to Marshal Zymierski regarding his request to resign are as follows:

To the Minister of National Defense, Marshal of Poland, Zymierski: In accordance with Article 45 of the Constitution of March 17, 1921, and Article 13 of the Law on the National System and the Powers of the Supreme Organs of the Polish Republic of February 19, 1947, I grant your request and relieve you of your position as Minister of National Defense. I would like to express my gratitude for your contributions to the establishment of the Polish army, in the struggle for the liberation of Poland, and in cultivating cadres for the glorious People's Polish Army. I am confident that in the new and important positions in the country, you will also serve the People's Poland with all your knowledge and experience.

President Bolesław Bierut
Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz (Signed)

The appointment order of President Bierut and Chairman Cyrankiewicz appointing Rokossovsky as Minister of National Defense states:

Citizen Marshal of Poland, Konstantin Rokossovsky: In accordance with Article 45 of the Constitution of March 17, 1921, and Article 13 of the Law on the National System and the Powers of the Supreme Organs of the Polish Republic of February 19, 1947, I appoint you as Minister of National Defense.

President Bolesław Bierut
Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz (Signed)

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 7th: Polish News Agency, Warsaw, 7th: Polish Marshal Zymierski issued an order stating: I have been entrusted by the Polish President with a new national position. Marshal Rokossovsky, a great soldier who was educated at the glorious Stalinist command school, has been appointed Minister of National Defense by the Polish President. What our people are proud of is that this Warsaw worker in Poland has become one of the best military leaders in the war against Hitler, enjoying the glory of the victories of Stalingrad and Kursk. He will be recorded in the history of the Polish people as the leader of the Soviet army that liberated Polish territory. The Polish army is connected with Marshal Konstantin Rokossovsky by a particularly strong bond, which was generated in the war under his leadership, which is a common bond against the Hitler bandits. The Kosciuszko Division and the Polish First Army experienced the glorious path of war on the front led by Marshal Rokossovsky. Today, this great Pole has taken on the responsible position of Minister of National Defense of Poland. Generals, officers and soldiers of the Army and Navy! I am convinced that under such excellent command, together with the people of the whole country, you will further strengthen the defense capabilities of our motherland.

### 经苏政府同意回到祖国服务　罗科索夫斯基元帅荣任波兰国防部长

1949-11-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　经苏政府同意回到祖国服务
  　罗科索夫斯基元帅荣任波兰国防部长
    【新华社北京八日电】塔斯社莫斯科六日讯：波兰总统贝鲁特考虑到苏联罗科索夫斯基元帅的波兰国籍及他在波兰人民中的声望。特请求苏联政府将罗科索夫斯基元帅归由波兰政府调遣，以便他可以在波兰军队中服务。苏联政府本苏联与波兰的友谊关系，并因罗科索夫斯基元帅表示这件事情可以完全由苏联政府决定，已同意贝鲁特总统的请求，依照苏联最高苏维埃主席团的指令，解除罗科索夫斯基元帅在苏维埃军队中的职务，并将他送归波兰政府调遣。
    【新华社北京八日电】据波兰通讯社七日讯：波兰政府国防部长齐默尔斯基元帅自请解除职务，已经波政府批准。波政府已决定任命罗科索夫斯基为波兰元帅，并继任波兰政府之国防部长。
    波总统贝鲁特、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹复齐默尔斯基元帅自请辞职的函件如下：
    国防部部长波兰元帅齐默尔斯基：根据一九二一年三月十七日的宪法第四十五条，按照一九四七年二月十九日关于国家制度和波兰共和国最高机关之权能的法律的第十三条，我答应你的请求，并解除你的国防部部长的职位。对于你在建立波兰军队，在波兰解放的斗争中，在为全国之光荣的人民波兰军队培养干部上的功绩，谨致谢意。我确信：在国家的新的重要工作岗位上，你也会用全部知识与经验为人民的波兰服务。
    　　　　　　　　　总统　波列斯劳·贝鲁特
    　　　　　　　　　部长会议主席　约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹（签字）
    波总统贝鲁特、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹任命罗科索夫斯基为国防部长的任命状称：
    波兰元帅康士坦丁·罗科索夫斯基公民：根据一九二一年三月十七日宪法第四十五条，按照一九四七年二月十九日关于国家制度和波兰共和国最高机关之权能的法律的第十三条，我任命你为国防部部长。
    　　　　　　　　　总统　波列斯劳·贝鲁特
    　　　　　　　　　部长会议主席　约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹（签字）
    【新华社北京八日电】波兰通讯社华沙七日讯：波兰元帅齐默尔斯基发布命令称：我已被波兰总统付托以新的国家职位。曾受教于光荣的斯大林主义的司令官学校的伟大军人罗科索夫斯基元帅，已被波兰总统任命为国防部长。我国人民所感骄傲的就是：这位波兰的华沙工人，已成为反对希特勒战争中最优秀的军事领袖之一，享有斯大林格勒、库尔斯克胜利的光荣。他将被作为解放波兰国土的苏军的领袖而记载在波兰人民的历史上，波兰陆军以特别强固的纽带与康士坦丁·罗科索夫斯基元帅联系着，这一纽带产生于在他领导下的战争中，这是反对希特勒匪帮的共同纽带。科斯西乌兹科师和波兰第一军，在罗科索夫斯基元帅所领导的战线中经历了光辉的战争的道路。今天，这一伟大的波兰人，已担负起波兰国防部长的负责职位。陆海军将军们、军官们和士兵们！我深信：在如此优秀的指挥下，与全国人民在一起，你们将更进一步地加强我们祖国的防卫力量。
