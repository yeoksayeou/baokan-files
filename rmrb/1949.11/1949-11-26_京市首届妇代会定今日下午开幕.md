Title: Beijing's First Women's Congress to Open This Afternoon

Author:

Date: 1949-11-26
Page: 1

　　Beijing's First Women's Congress to Open This Afternoon
    [Beijing News] The first Beijing Women's Congress, marking the great unity of women in the capital, is scheduled to open grandly at 2:00 PM this afternoon (the 26th) in the auditorium of the Sino-French University in Donghuangchenggen. Honorary Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, He Xiangning, Vice Chairman Li Dequan, Mayor of Beijing Nie Rongzhen, Central People's Government Supervisory Committee member Zhang Xiuyan, and Soviet representative of the Asian Women's Representative Conference Preparatory Committee, Malosova, will all attend and speak. The congress will hold a preparatory meeting at 9:00 AM, which will include a report on the preparations for the first Beijing Women's Congress, as well as the election of the congress presidium, the adoption of the meeting agenda and rules of procedure, and the election of members of the proposal review committee. The congress has received many banners of congratulations from the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Beijing Student Federation, and the Beijing Youth Federation, and many other organizations and nurseries will present flowers to the congress.

### 京市首届妇代会定今日下午开幕

1949-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市首届妇代会定今日下午开幕
    【北京讯】标志着首都妇女大团结的北京市第一次妇女代表大会，定于今（二十六）日下午二时在东黄城根中法大学礼堂隆重揭幕。全国民主妇女联合会名誉主席何香凝，副主席李德全，北京市市长聂荣臻，中央人民政府监察委员张秀岩，以及亚洲妇女代表会议筹备委员苏联代表玛洛索娃等均将莅会讲话。大会将在上午九时举行准备会议，会上除有北京市第一次妇女代表大会筹备经过的报告外，并进行推选大会主席团，通过会议日程和议事规则，选举提案审查委员会委员等。大会已收到市总工会、市学联及市青联等团体的贺幛多面，另有许多团体和托儿所将向大会献花。
