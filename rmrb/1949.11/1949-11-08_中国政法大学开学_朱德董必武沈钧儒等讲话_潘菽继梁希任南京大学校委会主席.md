Title: China University of Political Science and Law Opens; Zhu De, Dong Biwu, Shen Junru and Others Speak; Pan Shu Succeeds Liang Xi as Chairman of Nanjing University's School Affairs Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-11-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 7th: China University of Political Science and Law held its opening ceremony at 9:00 AM today. Over 1,500 faculty, staff, and students of the university attended, along with many guests from various circles. Besides Xie Juezai, the president of the university, those who spoke at the opening ceremony included Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Wu Yuzhang, President of North China University; Shi Liang, Minister of Justice; and Soviet legal expert Sudarikov.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 7th: Liang Xi, Chairman of the School Affairs Committee of Nanjing University, has been transferred to serve as Minister of Forestry and Land Reclamation of the Central Government. The Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government has decided that Pan Shu, the Academic Dean of the university, will succeed him in the vacated position.

### 中国政法大学开学　朱德董必武沈钧儒等讲话  潘菽继梁希任南京大学校委会主席

1949-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国政法大学开学
  　朱德董必武沈钧儒等讲话
    潘菽继梁希任南京大学校委会主席
    【新华社北京七日电】中国政法大学于本日上午九时举行开学典礼，到该校员生一千五百余人及各界来宾多人。在开学典礼中讲话的除该校校长谢觉哉外，有中央人民政府副主席朱德，政务院副总理董必武，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，华北大学校长吴玉章，司法部部长史良，和苏联法学专家苏达里可夫。
    【新华社北京七日电】南京大学校务委员会主席梁希调任中央林垦部长，遗职经中央人民政府教育部决定由该校教务长潘菽继任。
