Title: All-China Students' Federation Sends Telegram of Congratulations to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-11-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd. The All-China Students' Federation sent a telegram of congratulations to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. The text of the telegram is as follows:

　　To All Representatives of the Asian and Australian Trade Union Conference:

　　As your conference is victoriously convened in the capital of New China - Beijing, we, on behalf of the Chinese students, extend our highest respect to you.

　　The convening of the Asian and Australian Trade Union Conference will strengthen the unity of the working class in Asia, Australia, and the world, and will make a significant contribution to the workers' movements and national liberation movements in Asia and Australia.

　　The Chinese students, who have already gained freedom and liberation, hereby assure the conference: Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and our leader, Chairman Mao Zedong, we will closely unite with the people of Asia and Australia, and strive to oppose imperialist aggression, fight for the true independence and democracy of the people of the two continents, and defend world peace.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Students' Federation November 22nd

### 中华全国学生联合会电贺亚澳工会会议

1949-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国学生联合会电贺亚澳工会会议
    【新华社北京二十三日电】中华全国学生联合会电贺亚澳工会会议。电文如下：
    亚洲、澳洲工会会议全体代表们：
    当你们的会议在新中国首都——北京胜利地召开之际，我们谨代表中国学生向你们致崇高的敬礼。
    亚澳工会会议的召开，将加强亚澳两洲及全世界工人阶级的团结，将对亚澳两洲的工人运动及民族解放运动作重大的贡献。
    已经获得了自由和解放的中国学生谨向大会保证：我们在中国共产党及我们全体人民的领袖毛泽东主席的领导下，和亚澳两洲人民紧密团结，为反对帝国主义侵略、争取两洲人民真正的独立民主和保卫世界持久和平而奋斗。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国学生联合会　十一月二十二日
