Title: Tonight's Highlights on Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-11-04
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
　　Tonight's Highlights
　　[This newspaper dispatch] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station's special plague prevention program at 19:00 tonight: (1) Dr. Zhang Zhenyi from the Temple of Heaven Epidemic Prevention Office will discuss the issue of plague vaccination; (2) Huang Xiangzhen, Dean of the Beijing Municipal Health Experimental Institute, will discuss the symptoms and treatment of plague. At 18:15, the Workers' Program will introduce the working class of the Mongolian People's Republic, one of the countries participating in the Asian and Australian Trade Union Conference. At 18:30, the first literary program, a new folk art form: "Heroine Sun Naiying Crossing the River" performed by MeiHua Dagu singer Sun Yanqin. At 22:00, the second literary program, "Workers' Chorus" performed by the Northeast Lu Yi Music Troupe.
　　Additionally, the second channel will have Children's Time at 17:15: songs sung by children from the Ninth District Central National Primary School of this city. At 17:45, Youth Time: a speech by Comrade Gao Qi from the Beijing Fu Ren Girls' High School Broadcasting Group, titled: "How I Organize Women's Literacy Classes." At 18:00, a music program featuring Soviet music.

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台
  　今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十九点防治鼠疫特别节目：（一）天坛防疫处张振宜大夫谈注射鼠疫预防针问题；（二）北京市卫生实验院黄祥祯院长谈鼠疫的症状和治疗方法。十八点十五分职工节目，介绍参加亚、澳工会会议国家之一——蒙古人民共和国的工人阶级。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，新曲艺：梅花大鼓“渡江女英雄孙乃英”，孙艳琴演唱。二十二点第二次文艺节目，由东北鲁艺音工团演唱“工人大合唱”。
    又第二台十七时十五分儿童时间：由本市第九区中心国民小学小朋友们唱歌。十七时四十五分青年时间：由本市辅仁女中广播小组高奇同学讲话，题目是：“我怎样搞妇女识字班”。十八时音乐节目：苏联音乐。
