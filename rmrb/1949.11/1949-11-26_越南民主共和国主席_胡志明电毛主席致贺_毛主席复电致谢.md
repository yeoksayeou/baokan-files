Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Sends Congratulations to Chairman Mao; Chairman Mao Sends Reply of Thanks
Author:
Date: 1949-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 25th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong on the 15th of this month to congratulate him on the founding of the People's Republic of China. Chairman Mao has replied today to express his thanks. The exchange of telegrams is as follows:
(I) Telegram from Chairman Ho Chi Minh:
To Chairman Mao of the People's Republic of China:

	Hearing the happy news of the establishment of the government of the People's Republic of China, I, on behalf of the Vietnamese people and the Vietnamese government, extend my congratulations to Your Excellency and the people and government of your country. China and Vietnam have thousands of years of fraternal relations. In the future, this relationship will become even closer in order to jointly develop the freedom and happiness of our two nations, and to safeguard democracy and lasting peace in the world. I respectfully wish you political well-being.

Chairman of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh
November 15

(II) Chairman Mao Zedong's reply telegram:
To President Ho of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

	Deeply grateful for your telegram of congratulations on the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. China and Vietnam are both at the forefront of the anti-imperialist struggle. With the victorious development of the liberation struggles of the two peoples, the friendship between our two nations will surely become closer. I respectfully wish the Chinese and Vietnamese people a consolidated unity, and wish the independent and democratic cause of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam complete success.

Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
November 25, 1949

### 越南民主共和国主席　胡志明电毛主席致贺　毛主席复电致谢

1949-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南民主共和国主席
  　胡志明电毛主席致贺
  　毛主席复电致谢
    【新华社北京二十五日电】越南民主共和国主席胡志明于本月十五日致电毛泽东主席，祝贺中华人民共和国成立。毛主席已于本日复电致谢。来往电文如下：
    （一）胡志明主席来电中华人民共和国毛主席钧鉴：
    欣闻中华人民共和国政府成立，谨代表越南人民和越南政府向阁下及贵国人民和政府致贺。中国与越南有几千年历史的兄弟关系，今后这关系必更加密切，以共同发展我两民族的自由和幸福，并保卫世界的民主与永久和平。恭颂政安
    越南民主共和国主席　胡志明
    十一月十五日
    （二）毛泽东主席答复电越南民主共和国胡主席：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立，承来电祝贺，至深感激。中越两国同处反帝国主义斗争前线，随着两国人民解放斗争的胜利发展，两民族的友谊必将日臻亲密。敬祝中越人民巩固团结，并祝越南民主共和国独立民主事业彻底成功。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东
    一九四九年十一月二十五日
