Title: Preparatory Committee of Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce Established
Author:
Date: 1949-11-16
Page: 2

　　Preparatory Committee of Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce Established
    [Beijing News] The Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce held its inaugural meeting at the Municipal Chamber of Commerce yesterday (the 15th) at 2:00 PM. Sixty-five members of the preparatory committee attended. Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and Cheng Hongyi, Director of the Municipal Government's Bureau of Commerce, were present to provide guidance. Liu Yifeng of the Municipal Chamber of Commerce served as the temporary chairman. Sun Fuling of the Municipal Industrial Association reported on the preparatory process, followed by speeches from Deputy Mayor Zhang and Director Cheng. Deputy Mayor Zhang said: Industry and commerce cannot be separated and should be closely integrated. Under the policy of considering both public and private interests and benefiting both labor and capital, efforts should be made to develop production and prosper the economy. He pointed out that the tasks of the Federation of Industry and Commerce are: 1. Unite the industrial and commercial circles of Beijing; 2. Represent the interests of Beijing's industrial and commercial circles, and obey the interests of all the people; 3. Strive to develop production. In his speech, Director Cheng explained that the Preparatory Committee of the Federation of Industry and Commerce was established by industrialists and businessmen through long-term deliberation, multiple consultations, and research, with the assistance of the government. He pointed out: The industrial and commercial circles should be well organized, and should restore and develop production in a more planned way, carry out urban and rural trade and import and export trade. At the same time, he hoped that all industrial and commercial organizations in the city would establish a new style. After Director Cheng's speech, the meeting continued to adopt the draft articles of association of the Federation of Industry and Commerce, and elected Fu Huating, Pu Jiexiu, Liu Yifeng, Han Songshang, Ling Qijun, Shi Jinkui, Yin Yukun, Hou Rulin, Wang Xiangchen, Sun Xueling, Tang Shaoyuan, Le Songsheng, and Zhao Yizhi as standing committee members, with Fu Huating as chairman and Pu Jiexiu, Liu Yifeng, Han Songshang, and Ling Qijun as vice chairmen. At the same time, 52 representatives from the industrial and commercial circles were elected to attend the Second Beijing People's Representative Conference. In the free speech session, Hou Rulin, Yin Yukun, Pu Jiexiu and others unanimously believed that in the future, the industrial and commercial circles of Beijing must closely cooperate to develop the people's capital into a production city. The meeting adjourned at 5:30 PM. The first meeting of the Standing Committee is scheduled to be held this morning (the 16th).

### 京工商业联合会筹备委员会成立

1949-11-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　京工商业联合会筹备委员会成立
    【北京讯】北京市工商业联合会筹备委员会于昨（十五）日下午二时在市商会举行成立会。出席筹备委员六十五人，北京市张友渔副市长，市政府商业局程宏毅局长均莅会指导。市商业会刘一峰担任临时主席，市工业会孙孚凌报告筹备经过后，即请张副市长和程局长讲话，张副市长说：工业和商业是不能分开的，应密切结合起来，在公私兼顾、劳资两利的政策下，努力发展生产，繁荣经济。他指出工商业联合会的任务为：一、团结北京市工商业界；二、代表北京工商界的利益，并服从全体人民的利益；三、努力发展生产。程局长讲话中说明工商业联合会筹委会是工商业人士经过长期酝酿，多次的协商研究，在政府协助下建立起来的。他指出：工商业界应很好的组织起来，要更有计划地恢复与发展生产，开展城乡贸易及出入口贸易，同时希望本市各工商业团体要树立新的作风。程局长讲话后，会议继续通过工商业联合会简章草案，并推举付华亭、浦洁修、刘一峰、韩诵裳、凌其峻、石金奎、殷玉昆、侯儒林、王向宸、孙学陵、汤绍远、乐松生、赵宜之十三人为常务委员，付华亭为主任委员、浦洁修、刘一峰、韩诵裳、凌其峻为副主任委员。同时，并推选出席北京市第二届各界人民代表会议的工商界代表五十二人。自由讲话中侯儒林、殷玉昆、浦洁修等一致认为今后京市工商界必需密切联系，把人民首都发展成为生产城市。至五时半散会。常委会第一次会议定今（十六）日上午举行。
