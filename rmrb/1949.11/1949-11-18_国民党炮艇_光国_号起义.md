Title: Kuomintang Gunboat "Guangguo" Uprising
Author:
Date: 1949-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th – The Kuomintang Shantou Naval Patrol Office's "Guangguo" gunboat gloriously revolted at 4:00 AM on the 9th in the Nan'ao sea area, led by Deputy Captain Wu Gaoyuan. It has now safely arrived in a certain liberated area and was warmly welcomed by the military and civilians there.

### 国民党炮艇　“光国”号起义

1949-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　国民党炮艇
  　“光国”号起义
    【新华社北京十六日电】国民党汕头海军巡防处“光国”号炮艇九日上午四时在副艇长吴高远率领下于南澳海面光荣起义，现已安抵解放区某地。受到该地军民的热烈欢迎。
