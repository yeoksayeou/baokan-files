Title: Battle to Liberate Guangxi Opens
Author:
Date: 1949-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, South China Front, 23rd — The People's Liberation Army liberated Guilin City, the capital of Guangxi Province, at 2:30 PM on the 22nd. When our troops entered the city, the citizens lined the streets to welcome them with great enthusiasm. Before liberating Guilin, our troops first liberated Lingchuan City, northwest of Guilin, at 8:00 AM on the 22nd. While pursuing fleeing bandits from Lingchuan towards Guilin, over 1,000 remnants of the enemy's Provisional 1st Division were forced to surrender.

### 解放广西战役揭幕

1949-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放广西战役揭幕
    【新华社华南前线二十三日电】人民解放军于二十二日下午二时三十分解放广西省会桂林市。当我军开入城内时，市民夹道欢迎，情绪热烈。在解放桂林前，我军先于二十二日上午八时解放了桂林西北的灵川城。由灵川向桂林追击逃匪途中，敌暂一师残部一千余人被迫投降。
