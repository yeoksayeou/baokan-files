Title: Tonight's Program Highlights from Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-11-05
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
  　Tonight's Program Highlights
    [News from our newspaper] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast a special program on plague prevention at 7:00 PM today: (1) Hu Zhengxiang, Director of the Pathology Department of Peking Union Medical College Hospital, will discuss the pathology of plague; (2) Zhang Xiaoqian, Director of the Department of Internal Medicine of Peking Union Medical College Hospital, will discuss the different types of plague and their interrelationships. The youth program at 6:15 PM will broadcast a piece of correspondence: "How the Communist Youth League branch of the Shenyang Rubber Factory uses League Day to solve the practical problems of its members." The first literary program at 6:30 PM will feature a new form of folk art: "Yang Faguei Slams His Son," performed by Guan Dejun, Guan Jinfeng, and Quan Feng. The second literary program at 10:00 PM will feature the Beijing Youth Cultural Troupe singing "Workers' Suite" and "Long Live the Working Class," etc.
    Also, on the second channel, the women's program at 5:15 PM will broadcast an introduction by the preparatory committee of the Municipal Women's Federation about the first Beijing Women's Congress, entitled "Welcoming the First Beijing Women's Congress." The workers' program at 5:40 PM will feature Comrade Wang Zhi from the Beijing Telegraph Office of the Tianjin Railway Bureau "talking about the learning situation and experience of the Telegraph Office." The Municipal Voice at 8:00 PM will (1) broadcast the first and second announcements of the Beijing Epidemic Prevention Committee, slogans for plague prevention, and basic knowledge of epidemic prevention masks; (2) broadcast the publicity slogans of the Beijing Municipal Public Security Bureau on preventing fires in winter for citizens. At 8:15 PM, the listener service will answer the questions asked by Li Zhao and Wang Shiwen: "What is the October Revolution Day?"

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-11-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台
  　今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十九点防治鼠疫特别节目：（一）协和医院病理科主任胡正祥谈鼠疫的病理；（二）协和医院内科主任张孝骞谈鼠疫的几种病型和相互关系。十八点十五分青年节目，播送一篇通讯：“沈阳橡胶厂团支部怎样利用团日解决团员切身问题”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，新曲艺：莲花落“杨发贵摔子”，关德俊、关金凤、全凤演唱。二十二点第二次文艺节目，由北京市青年文工团演唱“工人组歌”、“工人阶级万岁”等。
    又第二台十七时十五分妇女时间：播送市妇联筹委会关于北京市妇女第一次代表大会的介绍，题目是“迎接北京市妇女第一次代表大会”。十七时四十分职工时间：由天津铁路局北京电报所王智同志“谈谈电报所的学习情况和经验”。二十时市政之声：（一）播送北京市防疫委员会第一号、第二号通告，预防鼠疫口号和防疫用口罩的常识。（二）播送北京市人民政府公安局关于市民冬季防止火灾的宣传标语。二十时十五分听众服务回答李昭、王世文两位所问的“什么叫作十月革命节”。
