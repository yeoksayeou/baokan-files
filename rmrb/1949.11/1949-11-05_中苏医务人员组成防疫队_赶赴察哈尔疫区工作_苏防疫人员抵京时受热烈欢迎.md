Title: Chinese and Soviet Medical Personnel Form Epidemic Prevention Team to Work in the Chahar Epidemic Area; Soviet Epidemic Prevention Personnel Receive Warm Welcome Upon Arrival in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-11-05
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th. A epidemic prevention team composed of 49 Chinese medical personnel and 33 Soviet medical personnel invited from Northeast China arrived in Beijing and departed for the Chahar epidemic area to work at 9:00 AM today. Li Zhizhong, director of the Epidemic Prevention Department of the Central Epidemic Prevention Committee, and Dr. Rakson and three other medical experts who newly arrived in Beijing from the Soviet Union yesterday afternoon also traveled in the same vehicle to lead the epidemic prevention team in its work.

　　The Soviet medical personnel in the epidemic prevention team are headed by the medical scientist Maisky. They arrived in Beijing by train from Northeast China at 4:00 AM this morning, staying in Beijing for only five hours. When the team arrived in Beijing, Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the Central People's Government, He Cheng, Minister of Health of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Yan Baohang, representative of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Zhu Lian, representing Dong Biwu, director of the Central Epidemic Prevention Committee, Zhang Wenqi, representing Nie Rongzhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Song Jie, director of the Health Bureau of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, went to the station to welcome them.

### 中苏医务人员组成防疫队　赶赴察哈尔疫区工作  苏防疫人员抵京时受热烈欢迎

1949-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏医务人员组成防疫队
  　赶赴察哈尔疫区工作
    苏防疫人员抵京时受热烈欢迎
    【新华社北京四日电】我国医务人员四十九人和被邀由东北来京的苏联医务人员三十三人所组成的防疫队，已于今日上午九时赶赴察哈尔疫区工作。中央防疫委员会防疫处主任李志中，和昨日下午新从苏联到达北京的拉克森博士等四医学专家，亦同车前往领导防疫队进行工作。
    防疫队中的苏联医务人员以医学家马意斯基为队长。他们系于今晨四时从东北乘车抵达北京，在北京只停留了五小时。该队抵京时，我国中央人民政府卫生部部长李德全、中国人民革命军事委员会卫生部部长贺诚、中央人民政府外交部代表阎宝航、中央防疫委员会董必武主任代表朱琏、北京市人民政府聂荣臻市长代表张文奇、中央人民政府铁道部卫生局局长宋杰等曾赴车站欢迎。
