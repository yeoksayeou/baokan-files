Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Moves Fifth Broadcast Time Earlier
Author:
Date: 1949-11-28
Page: 1

Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Moves Fifth Broadcast Time Earlier
    [This newspaper] The Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will move the fifth broadcast time forward by thirty minutes starting from December 1st. The program schedule is as follows: Fifth broadcast time (17:00-23:30)
    17:00-17:15　Opening music
    17:15-17:30　Announcing the evening programs
    17:30-17:45　Rotating programs: Staff (Tuesdays, Fridays, Sundays); Youth (Wednesdays, Saturdays); Women (Mondays, Thursdays)
    17:45-18:00　Japanese News
    18:00-18:30　First Arts and Literature Program (New Folk Art Time)
    18:30-18:45　Lectures on Social Science or Natural Science Knowledge
    18:45-19:00　Miscellaneous Music
    19:00-19:15　Amoy Dialect (Xiamen) News
    19:15-19:30　Chaozhou Dialect News
    19:30-19:45　Cantonese News
    19:45-20:00　Beijing Opera
    20:00-20:20　Third Mandarin News Broadcast
    20:20-20:30　International Current Events
    20:30-20:45　Commentary or Comprehensive Report
    20:45-21:00　Speech or Communication
    20:00-21:30　Second Arts and Literature Program
    21:30-22:00　English News
    22:00-22:20　Fourth Mandarin News Broadcast
    22:20-22:30　Tomorrow’s Program Preview, Time Signal, Closing Music
    22:30-23:30　Recorded News
