Title: Japanese Democratic Women's Council Sends Congratulatory Telegram to New China
Author:
Date: 1949-11-09
Page: 1

　　Japanese Democratic Women's Council
　　Sends Congratulatory Telegram to New China
　　[Our Newspaper] According to the Akahata newspaper of the Japanese Communist Party: The committee of the Japanese Democratic Women's Council recently sent a telegram to the All-China Democratic Women's Federation congratulating the founding of the People's Republic of China. The telegram stated: "Japanese women wholeheartedly welcome the founding of the People's Republic of China and extend our congratulations. We deeply understand that women can only achieve true liberation when they are liberated from imperialist oppression. Your victory gives us great confidence and courage." It also stated: "We are waiting with great enthusiasm for the Asian Women's Representative Conference to be held in China."

### 日民主妇女协议会　电贺新中国

1949-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　日民主妇女协议会
  　电贺新中国
    【本报讯】据日共赤旗报载：日本民主妇女协议会委员会顷致电中华全国民主妇女联合会祝贺中华人民共和国的成立。该电略称：“日本妇女对中华人民共和国的成立衷心欢迎，并致贺意。我们深知只有从帝国主义压迫下解放出来时妇女才能得到真正的解放。你们的胜利给予我们很大的信心与勇气。”并称：“对于行将在中国召开的亚洲妇女代表会议，我们以高度的热情在等待着。”
