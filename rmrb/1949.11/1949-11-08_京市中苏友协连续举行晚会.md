Title: Beijing Municipal China-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Consecutive Evening Parties
Author:
Date: 1949-11-08
Page: 4
　　
    [Beijing News] The Beijing Municipal China-Soviet Friendship Association, in celebration of the great festival of the Soviet October Revolution, has held consecutive evening parties for three days (the 5th, 6th, and 7th) at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall. Approximately 15,000 people, including workers, students, soldiers, and personnel from various organizations and groups, participated. During the three evening parties, Qian Junrui, a cadre from the All-China China-Soviet Friendship Association, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Xiao Ming, Vice President of the Beijing Municipal China-Soviet Friendship Association, were invited to give speeches. At the same time, Soviet films such as "May Day Parade in 1949," "The Seven-Colored Flower," and "The Cuckoo and the Eagle" were screened.

### 京市中苏友协连续举行晚会

1949-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市中苏友协连续举行晚会
    【北京讯】北京市中苏友好协会，为庆祝苏联十月革命的伟大节日，三天（五、六、七日）来在中山公园音乐堂连续举行晚会，参加者有工人、学生、部队、各机关团体约一万五千余人。三天晚会中，分别请中苏友好协会总会干事钱俊瑞、全国总工会副主席刘宁一和北京市中苏友好协会副会长萧明作讲演，同时并放映苏联影片：“一九四九年五一大检阅”、“七彩花”、“杜鹃与老鹰”等。
