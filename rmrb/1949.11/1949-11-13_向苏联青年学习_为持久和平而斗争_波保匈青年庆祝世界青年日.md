Title: Learn from Soviet Youth, Fight for Lasting Peace - Polish, Bulgarian, and Hungarian Youth Celebrate World Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1949-11-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th: Youth from Poland, Bulgaria, and Hungary enthusiastically celebrated World Youth Day on the 10th.

	The Polish Youth League held symposiums on the activities and tasks of the World Federation of Democratic Youth in all its organizations in schools, factories, and villages.

	Various meetings and ceremonies were held in Warsaw, Krakow, and other places. Many groups of the Polish Youth League also held exhibitions displaying the work achievements of the Soviet Komsomol and the youth of people's democratic countries. Morawski, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish Youth League, pointed out in an interview with reporters: The main task of World Youth Day is to further mobilize the younger generation to fight for lasting peace. He said: "The slogan of the recent National Congress of Polish Youth Patriots held in Warsaw was: All youth participate in the struggle for peace and work for the motherland." Polish youth also celebrated World Youth Day under the same slogan.

	The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Dimitrov Democratic Youth League organized a celebration meeting. Toshkov, a member of the Central Committee of the League, emphasized in his report that the selfless struggle of Soviet youth for peace and for the construction of communism is an example for the younger generation all over the world. The meeting passed telegrams of salute to Stalin, Kolarov, the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, and the International Union of Students.

	All organizations of the Hungarian People's Youth League widely held celebration meetings. The leading organ of the League also sent telegrams to the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, the All-China Youth Federation, the central leading organ of "Free German Youth," the Albanian Labor Youth League, and the leading organ of the World Federation of Democratic Youth in Paris to express congratulations.

### 向苏联青年学习  为持久和平而斗争  波保匈青年庆祝世界青年日

1949-11-13
第3版()
专栏：

    向苏联青年学习
    为持久和平而斗争
    波保匈青年庆祝世界青年日
    【新华社北京十二日电】波、保、匈三国青年于十日热烈庆祝世界青年日。
    波兰青年联盟在学校、工厂、乡村所有的组织，都举行了关于世界民主青联的活动与任务的座谈会。
    华沙、克拉科等地，都举行了各种会议和仪式。许多波兰青年联盟的团体并举行展览会，展览苏联共产主义青年团和人民民主国家青年的工作成绩。波兰青年联盟中央委员会书记摩拉夫斯基在接见记者时指出：世界青年日的主要任务，就是更进一步地动员青年一代去为持久和平而斗争。他说：“最近在华沙举行的波兰青年爱国者全国代表会议的口号是：全体青年参加和平斗争，为祖国而工作。”波兰青年也在同样的口号下庆祝世界青年日。
    保加利亚季米特洛夫民主青年联盟中央委员会组织了庆祝大会。该联盟的中央委员托什科夫在会上作报告时着重指出：苏联青年为和平及为建设共产主义而进行的忘我斗争，是全世界年青一代的榜样。大会通过向斯大林、科拉罗夫、世界拥护和平大会常设委员会、苏联青年反法西斯委员会及世界学联致敬电。
    匈牙利青年人民联盟的一切组织，都广泛地举行了庆祝大会。该联盟的领导机关并致电苏联青年反法西斯委员会、中华全国民主青年联合会、“自由德国青年”的中央领导机关、阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟，和在巴黎的世界民主青年联盟的领导机关表示贺忱。
