Title: Notice from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Regarding the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-11-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 6th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions issued the following notice today regarding the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference: To all trade union organizations and workers and staff members across the country:

The World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) has decided to convene a meeting of its Executive Bureau in Beijing, the capital of China, on November 11th this year to discuss the work of the world's working class in the struggle to defend world peace and democracy, as well as issues such as the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference. Leaders of the WFTU and trade union leaders from various countries will soon be arriving in Beijing for the meeting. This is an event of great significance for the world labor movement, and especially for our Chinese labor movement. At the same time, the WFTU has also decided to convene the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference in Beijing from November 16th to 26th. This is a conference of great unity for the working classes of Asia and Australia, and a conference of great unity between the workers of Asia and Australia and the world's working class. This conference will be held under the direct leadership of the WFTU Executive Bureau, will hear reports from WFTU Secretary-General Louis Saillant on the world labor movement, as well as reports from trade unions in various Asian and Australian countries, and will adopt various resolutions and establish appropriate institutions to more effectively develop the labor movements in various Asian and Australian countries. This conference will proceed under the general goals of opposing imperialism, opposing feudalism, opposing wars of aggression, opposing splitters of the world labor movement, defending world lasting peace, striving for national independence for oppressed countries, defending trade union democratic rights, and defending the unity of the world labor movement, and will enable the exchange of production and construction experiences of the working classes of those countries that have already been liberated. It is a great honor for us Chinese working class that such a grand international workers' conference can be held in our Chinese capital as a result of the victory of the Chinese people's revolution, and it will be of great help to the work of our Chinese labor movement. In the traditional spirit of proletarian internationalism of the Chinese working class, we express our sincere welcome to these two conferences.

To this end, the Standing Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has specifically decided to select and dispatch 58 worker and staff representatives to attend this conference. The principles for selecting these representatives, the allocation of representative quotas, and other matters requiring attention are hereby notified as follows:

(1) The selection of representatives to attend the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference shall be based on industrial trade unions as the primary consideration, and local trade unions as the secondary consideration.

(2) The specific allocation of representative quotas is as follows:

(A) Eight representatives from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

(B) A total of thirty-four representatives from various industrial trade unions, including: four from the Metalworkers' Union Working Committee, five from the Railway Workers' Union Preparatory Committee, five from the Textile Workers' Union Working Committee, five from the Seamen's Union Working Committee, three from the Postal Workers' Union Preparatory Committee, three from the Telecommunications Workers' Union Preparatory Committee, two from the Food Workers' Union Working Committee, and three from the Cultural and Educational Publishing Workers' Union Working Committee.

(C) A total of sixteen representatives from local trade unions, including: two from Shanghai, two from the Northeast, two from Tianjin, two from Beijing, two from Taiyuan, two from Jinan, two from Xi'an, two from Nanjing, and one from Wuhan.

(3) The list of representatives from various industrial and regional trade unions attending the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference has been approved by the Standing Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and has been separately notified to each trade union, and they must report to Beijing by November 13th.

(4) In order to strengthen internationalist education, trade unions in all regions and factories should conduct universal and in-depth education among workers and staff members on the significance of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference and the role of the World Federation of Trade Unions in the world labor movement, and hold mass meetings of workers in each factory to congratulate the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference, express the close fraternal friendship between the Chinese working class and the world's working class, express support for the WFTU, express resolute implementation of the WFTU's resolutions, and unite with the working classes of Asian countries and the working class of the whole world to oppose imperialist aggression and oppression, and to fight to the end to defend world peace and democracy.

All-China Federation of Trade Unions, November 6th.

### 中华全国总工会　关于亚洲澳洲工会会议的通知

1949-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会
  　关于亚洲澳洲工会会议的通知
    【新华社北京六日电】中华全国总工会今日发出关于亚洲澳洲工会会议的通知如下：全国各工会组织和工人职员们：
    世界工会联合会决定，今年十一月十一日在中国首都北京召开执行局会议，讨论世界工人阶级为保卫世界和平民主而斗争的工作及亚洲澳洲工会会议等问题。现在世界工联的领袖及各国工会的领袖很快就要来到北京开会了。这是对于世界工人运动，特别是对于我们中国工人运动有重大意义的事件。同时，世界工联又决定于十一月十六日至二十六日，在北京召开亚洲澳洲工会会议，这是一个亚洲、澳洲工人阶级大团结的会议，并且是亚洲、澳洲工人与世界工人阶级大团结的会议。这个会议，将在世界工联执行局的直接领导之下举行，听取世界工联总书记路易·赛扬关于世界工人运动的报告，以及亚洲、澳洲各国工会的报告，并将通过各种决议，建立适当的机构，以便更有力地开展亚洲、澳洲各国的工人运动。这个会议将在反对帝国主义、反对封建主义、反对侵略战争、反对世界工人运动的分裂者、为保卫世界持久和平、争取被压迫国家的民族独立、捍卫工会民主权利与捍卫世界工运统一的总目标下进行，并使那些业已解放的国家的工人阶级获得生产建设经验的交流。这样盛大的国际工人会议能在我们中国首都来开，这是由于中国人民革命获得了胜利的结果，这对于我们中国工人阶级是莫大的光荣，对于我们中国工人运动的工作会有很大的帮助。我们本着中国工人阶级国际主义的传统精神，对这两次会议表示竭诚的欢迎。
    为此，中华全国总工会常务委员会特决定选派五十八名职工代表出席这个会议。兹将派选这些代表的原则及代表名额的分配与其他应注意事项通知如下：
    （一）出席亚洲澳洲工会会议的代表的选派，原则上以产业工会为主，地方工会为次。
    （二）代表名额的具体分配如下：
    （甲）中华全国总工会的代表八名。
    （乙）各产业工会的代表共三十四名，计：五金工会工作委员会四名，铁路总工会筹备委员会五名，纺织工会工作委员会五名，海员工会工作委员会五名，邮政总工会筹备委员会三名，电讯总工会筹备委员会三名，食品工会工作委员会二名，文教出版工会工作委员会三名。
    （丙）各地方工会代表共十六名，计：上海二名，东北二名，天津二名，北京二名，太原二名，济南二名，西安二名，南京二名，武汉一名。
    （三）各产业各地区工会出席亚洲澳洲工会会议的代表名单已由全国总工会常委会通过，分别通知各工会，务须于十一月十三日前抵京报到。
    （四）为加强国际主义教育，各地区工会、各工厂工会，应将亚洲澳洲工会会议的意义，与世界工会联合会在世界工人运动中的作用，向工人职员群众，进行普遍深入的教育，并在各工厂中分别召开工人群众的会议，祝贺亚洲澳洲工会会议，表示中国工人阶级与世界工人阶级的亲密的兄弟友谊，表示对世界工联的拥护，表示坚决执行世界工联的决议，与亚洲各国工人阶级及全世界工人阶级团结一致，反对帝国主义的侵略和压迫，为保卫世界和平民主而奋斗到底。
　　　　　　　　　中华全国总工会　十一月六日
