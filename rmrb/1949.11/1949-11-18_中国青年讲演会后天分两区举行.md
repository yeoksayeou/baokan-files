Title: China Youth Lecture Hall to be Held in Two Districts the Day After Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-11-18
Page: 4

　　【Beijing News】Since the 13th session, the China Youth Lecture Hall has been held in separate districts. The 14th lecture is scheduled for 9:00 am this Sunday, with Hu Sheng lecturing on "Study Problems" at the male affiliated high school of the Normal University; at 7:00 pm, Peng Qingzhao of the Cultural and Educational Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions will lecture at the Tianqiao Theater outside Qianmen on "Workers of the World are One Family."

### 中国青年讲演会后天分两区举行

1949-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国青年讲演会后天分两区举行
    【北京讯】中国青年讲演会，自第十三次起已分区举行。第十四次讲演会，决定于本星期日上午九时请胡绳在师大男附中讲“学习问题”；下午七时请中华全国总工会文教部彭庆昭至前门外天桥剧院讲“天下工人是一家”。
