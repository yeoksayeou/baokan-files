Title: Beijing Municipal Grain Corporation: Sufficient Grain Supply for Citizens; 26 Million Jin of Grain Transported in Eight Days; 56 Additional Grain Retail Stores Established
Author: Jin Yu
Date: 1949-11-26
Page: 1

　　Beijing Municipal Grain Corporation
　　Sufficient Grain Supply for Citizens
　　26 Million Jin of Grain Transported in Eight Days; 56 Additional Grain Retail Stores Established

    [Report from this newspaper] To implement the Beijing Municipal People's Government's measures to guarantee food grain supply, the Beijing Municipal Grain Corporation, in addition to accelerating and smoothing grain sources, transported more than 26.8 million jin of various food grains from the Northeast and Northwest from the 16th to the 24th (eight days). Furthermore, it added 56 retail stores within three days, completing 65% of the planned task of establishing 100 new retail stores. The company previously had 12 business offices and 23 retail stores distributed throughout the city to handle direct retail business. To strengthen the retail network and fully supply the food grain needs of citizens, starting from the 21st of this month, the company consulted with the Municipal Supply and Marketing Cooperative and entrusted the following organizations affiliated with the cooperative to undertake retail sales: Beijing Clothing General Factory Cooperative (Chaonei South Xiaojie), First Cooperative (Tiejiaoying), Second Cooperative (South Xiaojie Caijia Courtyard),搬运工人合作社 (West Willow Tree Well),搬运工人分社 (West Jingji Road), Third District Tenth Street Cooperative (Xiang'er Hutong), Eleventh Street Cooperative (Dongsi Third Alley), Sixteenth Street Cooperative (Beixinqiao Bao'en Temple), First Sales Department of the Municipal General Cooperative (Bingmasi Shui Dayuan), Second Sales Department (South Xiaojie Middle), Third Sales Department (South Xiaojie South Entrance), Fourth Sales Department (Yangquan Hutong), First Central Cooperative (Yangfang Hutong), Second Central Cooperative (Denei Guozishi), Third Central Cooperative (Yandaixiejie), Fourth Central Cooperative (Niangniangmiao), Fifth Central Cooperative (Nanluoguxiang), Fifth Street Cooperative (Jingshan Avenue), Thirteenth, Fourth, and Fifth Street Cooperatives (Xiyinsi Street), Rongxian Hutong, Damochang, West Liulichang Third搬运工人合作社, Tricycle Cooperative (Fahua Temple), Printing Factory Cooperative (Baizhifang Chongxiao Temple), Tricycle Worker Cooperative (Xuanwai Baiyian), and the cooperative's First Branch (Pen Hutong), Second Branch (Guanganmen Nei), Sixth Street Cooperative (Yongdingmen Nei), Third Street Cooperative (Taipingqiao), totaling 29 organizations. From the 11th to the 25th of this month (14 days), these 64 retail units sold a total of 3,499,711 jin of various food grains, serving over 344,240 purchasers. During this period of price fluctuations, when unscrupulous merchants seized the opportunity to raise prices and refuse sales, these retail outlets ensured the grain supply for the broad masses of working people.

    Addresses of New Retail Stores

    While channeling grain sources, the company mobilized a large part of its strength to establish a large number of retail stores. Within three days (from the 23rd to the 25th), it successively established 56 retail stores at: Tizi Hutong, Yangweiba Hutong, West Biaobeihu Hutong, Gongyuan East Street, Baofang Hutong, Maojiawan Hutong, Chongnei Zhugangxiang, Qian Chaomian Hutong (belonging to the First Business Office), Yazi Temple, South Gouyan, Pi Ku Hutong, Guiche Hutong (belonging to the Second Business Office), Dongsi Fourth Alley, Beixin Kai Lu, Wangfuma Hutong (belonging to the Third Business Office), Da Huoyaoju, Da Huaishu, Xiao Yu Huanggge, West Zhimen Huoyaoju (belonging to the Fourth Business Office), Gulou Dongsigong Street, North Luoguxiang, Shuiche Hutong (belonging to the Fifth Business Office), Pudu Temple, West Huamen Street, Zhantan Temple (belonging to the Sixth Business Office), Chongnei Avenue, Anfu Hutong (belonging to the Seventh Business Office), East Willow Tree Well Daci Alley, North Wu Lao Hutong (belonging to the Eighth Business Office), West Heyean, Ji Jia Keng, South Xinhua Street South Entrance, West Liulichang (belonging to the Ninth Business Office), Maweimao Hutong, South Hetao, Mile Temple, Shatushan Houjie, Dongsi Kuaoyu, Xizhaosi, Long'an Temple, Xia Tangdao, Nan Gangzi Street (belonging to the Tenth Business Office), Shaoxing Guildhall, Mishi Hutong, Lao Juntang, Guangnei Avenue, Shuru Hutong, Sanmiao Street, Lao Qianggen, Shalanhutong (belonging to the Eleventh Business Office), Ya'er Hutong, Hufang Road, South Heng Street, South Xiawazi, Chenghuang Temple, Taoshikou, Xiaoshi Caishen Temple, Luban Guan, Chunshuyuan (belonging to the Twelfth Business Office). These stores began selling grain successively from the 24th. There are plans to establish another 44 retail stores, and the company is actively looking for housing to build them. After the business offices increased retail stores, the supply range has increased. On the 25th, the Fourth Business Office sold more than 30,000 jin of grain in one day, and more than 5,000 people purchased grain, but there was no congestion. Sales were completed within the scheduled business hours (9:00 AM to 4:30 PM).

    In addition, starting yesterday (the 25th), the company began piloting a method of supplying flour to the Beijing pasta industry merchants on a daily basis. The purpose of implementing this method is to ensure that the pasta industry merchants in the city have the flour they need every day so that citizens can purchase flatbreads, steamed buns, and other flour-based foods at fair prices. The company specially printed (Grain Purchase Booklets), which will be distributed to the pasta shops by the Pasta Business Association. After the booklets are filled out and reviewed, the flour will be purchased from the grain company in groups.
    (Jin Yu)

### 京市粮食分公司　充分供应市民粮食　八天运来粮食二千六百万斤  并增设粮食零售店五十六处
靳羽

第1版()
专栏：

　　京市粮食分公司
  　充分供应市民粮食
  　八天运来粮食二千六百万斤  并增设粮食零售店五十六处
    【本报讯】京市粮食分公司为贯彻北京市人民政府保证食粮供应之措施，除加紧顺畅粮源，由十六日至二十四日八天中自东北、西北调运到各种食粮二千六百八十余万斤外，并于三天内增设了五十六个零售店，完成预计增设零售店一百个任务的百分之六十五。该公司为办理直接零售业务曾有十二个营业处，二十三个零售店分布市内各处。本月二十一日起为加强零售机构，充分供应市民所需食粮，又与市供销合作总社商妥，委托该社所属：北京被服总厂合作社（朝内南小街）、第一合作社（铁匠营）、第二合作社（南小街蔡家大院）、搬运工人合作社（西柳树井）、搬运工人分社（西京畿道）、三区第十街合作社（香饵胡同）、第十一街合作社（东四三条）、十六街合作社（北新桥报恩寺）、市总社第一代销部（兵马司水大院）、第二代销部（南小街中间）、第三代销部（南小街南口）、第四代销部（羊泉胡同）、第一中心社（羊坊胡同）、第二中心社（德内果子市）、第三中心社（烟袋斜街）、第四中心社（娘娘庙）、第五中心社（南锣鼓巷）、第五街社（景山大街）、第十三、四、五街合作社（惜阴司街）、绒线胡同、打磨厂、西琉璃厂三搬运工人合作社、三轮车合作社（法华寺）、印刷厂合作社（白纸坊崇效寺）、三轮车工人合作社（宣外白衣庵）及该社第一分社（盆胡同）、第二分社（广安门内）、第六街社（永定门内）、第三街社（太平桥）等二十九个机构担负代销业务。自本月十一日至二十五日十四天中，此六十四个零售单位，共售出各种食粮三百四十九万九千七百十一斤，获得购买此项粮食者计三十四万四千二百四十余人。在此次物价波动，奸商乘机抬价、拒售过程中，通过这些零售机构，保证了广大劳苦市民的粮食供应。
    新增零售店地址
    该公司在疏导粮源同时，动员大部力量增办大批零售店，三日内（二十三日至二十五日）陆续在：王府井大街梯子胡同、羊尾巴胡同、西裱褙胡同、贡院东街、报房胡同、毛家湾胡同、崇内竹杆巷、前炒面胡同（以上属第一营业处），鸭子庙、南沟沿、皮库胡同、轨车胡同（属第二营业处）东四四条、北新开路、汪驸马胡同（属第三营业处），大火药局、大槐树、小玉皇阁、西直门火药局（属第四营业处），鼓楼东四公街、北锣鼓巷、水车胡同（属第五营业处），普渡寺、西华门大街、旃坛寺、（属第六营业处），崇内大街、安福胡同（属第七营业处），东柳树井大慈巷、北五老胡同（属第八营业处），西河沿、激家坑、南新华街南口、西琉璃厂（属第九营业处），马尾帽胡同、南河套、弥勒庵、沙土山后街、东四块玉、夕照寺、隆安寺、下唐刀、南岗子街（属第十营业处），绍兴会馆、米市胡同、老君堂、广内大街、输入胡同、三庙街、老墙根、纱栏胡同（以上属第十一营业处），鸦儿胡同、虎坊路、南横街、南下洼子，城隍庙、桃市口、晓市财神庙、鲁班观、椿树院（以上属第十二营业处），增设了五十六个零售店，于二十四日起陆续开始售卖。并计划再建立四十四个零售店，现正寻找房舍积极筹建中。各营业处增加零售店后，供应范围增大。二十五日第四营业处虽然一日间卖出粮食三万多斤，购买粮食的有五千多人，但并无拥挤现象，能在预定营业时间内（上午九时至下午四时半）售卖完毕。
    此外该公司于昨日（二十五日）起开始试行对京市面食业各商逐日供给面粉办法。施行此办法目的在保证本市面食业各商每日所需面粉，使市民能以公道价格购得面制烙饼、馒头等食品。该公司特印制之（购粮册），由面食商业公会代为分发各面食店，经填报审核，后以组为单位向粮食公司购买面粉。
