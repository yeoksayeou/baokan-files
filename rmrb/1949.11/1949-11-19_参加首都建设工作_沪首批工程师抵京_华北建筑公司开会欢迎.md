Title: Shanghai's First Batch of Engineers Arrive in Beijing to Participate in Capital Construction; North China Construction Company Holds Meeting to Welcome Them
Author: Cheng Jiang
Date: 1949-11-19
Page: 1

　　Participating in Capital Construction Work
  　Shanghai's First Batch of Engineers Arrive in Beijing
  　North China Construction Company Holds Meeting to Welcome Them
    [Reported by this newspaper] The first batch of sixty construction engineers, out of the ninety construction engineers hired by the North China Construction Company from Shanghai, arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 16th. The construction company held a meeting to welcome them. The remaining thirty will come to Beijing successively. Among the first batch of sixty engineers is Chief Engineer Zhuang Jun. Zhuang is a graduate of Tsinghua University and a returned student from the United States, where he obtained a doctorate in architectural engineering. In the past, he presided over the construction of two major projects: Tsinghua Garden in Beijing and JinCheng Bank in Shanghai. Another Deputy Chief Engineer, Wu Jingxiang, is a returned student from France, where he obtained a master's degree in architectural engineering. They are both excited and confident about participating in the work of the North China Construction Company and personally participating in the construction of the people's capital.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Cheng Jiang)

### 参加首都建设工作　沪首批工程师抵京　华北建筑公司开会欢迎
程江
1949-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加首都建设工作
  　沪首批工程师抵京
  　华北建筑公司开会欢迎
    【本报讯】华北建筑公司由上海聘请之九十位建筑工程师，首批六十位已于十六日晨抵京，建筑公司并开会欢迎。其余三十位亦将陆续来京。首批六十位工程师中，有庄俊总工程师，庄系清华大学毕业，美国留学生，曾获得美国建筑工程博士学位，过去曾主持建筑北京清华园，上海金城银行两大工程。另一位副总工程师吴景祥，系法国留学生，曾获法国建筑工程硕士学位。他们对这次参加华北建筑公司的工作，亲自参加人民首都的建设事业，都感到兴奋并抱有很大信心。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（程江）
