Title: Celebrating the October Revolution with Outstanding Achievements: All-Soviet People's Competition Increases Production
Author:
Date: 1949-11-08
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow dispatch, 7th: The Soviet people are celebrating the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution with outstanding achievements. More than 90 percent of the working people have participated in the socialist competition held to celebrate this festival. Hundreds of large and small factories in Moscow have completed this year's plan and the five-year plan ahead of schedule in terms of output. More departments in enterprises are turning to collective Stakhanovite work. Leningrad initiated a movement this year in which scientific workers cooperated with workers in industry to improve technical levels, and achieved great results. The Leningrad Heavy Machinery Manufacturing Enterprise alone manufactured 850 new types of machines, machine tools, tools and instruments this year. In Kiev, 5,000 workers completed the output of the five-year plan ahead of schedule, and more than 140 enterprises have reached the production level of 1950. Many large and small factories in Kharkov, Riga, Vilnius, Minsk and other cities have completed the five-year plan.

### 用卓越成就庆祝十月革命节　全苏人民竞赛增产

1949-11-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　用卓越成就庆祝十月革命节
  　全苏人民竞赛增产
    【新华社北京七日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联人民以卓越的成就来庆祝十月革命三十二周年纪念日。百分之九十以上的劳动人民都参加了为庆祝这节日而举行的社会主义竞赛。莫斯科数百个大小工厂已在产量上提前完成了今年的计划和五年计划。企业中更多的工作部门正转入集体的斯塔哈诺夫式的工作。列宁格勒今年发起了一个科学工作者与工人在工业上合作以提高技术水平的运动，获得了巨大的成绩。仅列宁格勒的重型机械制造企业，今年就制造了八百五十种新型的机器、工作母机、工具与仪器。在基辅，五千工人提前完成了五年计划的产量，一百四十多个企业已达到一九五○年的生产水平。哈尔科夫、里加、维尔那、明斯克及其他城市的许多大小工厂，均已完成了五年计划。
