　　International Association of Democratic Lawyers Fourth Congress Opens
    Delegations from Soviet Union, Belarus, Poland, Italy, and other countries participate
    【Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 29th】The Fourth Congress of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers opened in Rome on the 28th. The agenda of the congress includes the following four items: (1) On the issue of compliance with international obligations as a prerequisite for peaceful cooperation among nations; (2) On the issue of the practical application of human rights principles by judicial institutions; (3) On the issue of the struggle for independent and equal national rights and democratic freedoms in colonies, semi-colonies, and dependent countries; (4) On the issue of the struggle to prohibit the use of atomic energy for military purposes. Delegations from the Soviet Union, Belarus, Poland, Italy, and other countries are participating in this congress.

### 国际民主法律工作者第四次代表大会揭幕  苏白俄罗斯波意等代表团参加

1949-11-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际民主法律工作者第四次代表大会揭幕
    苏白俄罗斯波意等代表团参加
    【新华社北京二十九日电】罗马讯：国际民主法律工作者第四次代表大会，已于二十八日在罗马开幕。大会议程共有以下四项：（一）关于遵守作为各民族间和平合作所必需之先决条件的国际义务问题；（二）关于司法机构实际应用人权原则问题；（三）关于在殖民地、半殖民地与附属国家为独立、平等民族权利与民主自由的斗争问题；（四）关于为禁止把原子能用于军事目的的斗争问题。参加此次大会的有苏联、白俄罗斯、波兰及意大利等国代表团。
