Title: The Strength of the Human Peace Camp Stands With Us - Speech by Iranian Representative Shad

Author:

Date: 1949-11-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd—The full text of the speech by Iranian representative Ali Mahmoud Shad at the Beijing People's Congress celebrating the successful Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference is as follows:

　　I, on behalf of the Iranian trade unions, the Iranian workers, and the entire Iranian people, pay my respects to the workers of Beijing, and through you, to the heroic Chinese people and the victorious Chinese proletariat. Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and its leader Comrade Mao Zedong, the victory of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the founding of the People's Republic of China is a serious blow to our common enemy—American and British imperialism. We Iranian workers, farmers, Iranian women and youth are as happy as you are, because we believe that your victory is our victory. The imperialists are afraid of the glorious victory of the Chinese people, so they are trying to establish a new fascist system in Iran, India, Iraq, Turkey, and other Middle Eastern and Far Eastern countries, abolishing all freedoms.

　　Recently, Nehru of India and King Mohammad Reza of Iran went to Washington and London respectively. While we are meeting here, they are listening to instructions from Churchill and the masters of Wall Street in the United States to suppress the working class, suppress all freedom movements, and prepare for a new war.

　　Reactionary capitalists are concentrating in London and Washington, but working people are meeting in Beijing and Moscow. Our strength is growing, and the strength of all the human peace camp stands with us.

　　The sun rises in the East, Eastern Europe is shining with the bright stars of the new democratic countries. Under the leadership of the Soviet Union, we will be able to achieve victory, because our struggle is correct, because we demand the progress and happiness of working people and humanity.

　　Long live the heroic Chinese people and the Chinese working class!
　　Long live the World Federation of Trade Unions!
　　Long live the vanguard of the Chinese people, the Chinese Communist Party and its leader Chairman Mao!
　　Long live Comrade Stalin, the bastion of peace and democracy and the leader of the Soviet Union and the world's working people!

### 人类和平阵营的力量都和我们站在一起　伊朗代表沙德讲话

1949-11-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　人类和平阵营的力量都和我们站在一起
　　伊朗代表沙德讲话
    【新华社北京二十三日电】伊朗代表阿里·麻莫德·沙德在北京各界人民庆祝亚澳工会会议成功大会上的讲话全文如下：
    我代表伊朗工会、伊朗劳动者、伊朗全体人民向北京市劳动者致敬，同时通过你们，向全体英勇的中国人民和胜利的中国无产阶级致敬。在中国共产党及其领袖毛泽东同志领导之下，中国人民解放军的胜利，以及中华人民共和国的成立，对于我们共同敌人——美英帝国主义，是一个严重的打击。我们伊朗的工人、农民，我们伊朗的妇女和青年，也同你们一样地高兴，因为我们认为你们的胜利就是我们的胜利。帝国主义者害怕中国人民的辉煌胜利，所以他们企图在伊朗、印度、伊拉克、土耳其、以及其他中东国家、远东国家建立一个新的法西斯制度，废除一切自由。
    最近印度的尼赫鲁、伊朗王穆罕默瑞扎曾分别到华盛顿、伦敦，当我们在这里开会的时候，他们正在听取邱吉尔和美国华尔街的主人的指示去镇压工人阶级，镇压一切自由的运动，并且准备一个新的战争。
    反动的资本家们正集中在伦敦和华盛顿两地，但是劳动人民在北京、莫斯科开会。我们的力量日见增强，所有人类和平的阵营的力量，都和我们站在一起。
    太阳从东方升起，东欧闪烁着新民主主义国家的光明的星，在苏联的领导之下，我们将能获得胜利，因为我们的斗争是正确的，因为我们要求劳动人民及人类的进步和幸福。
    英勇的中国人民及中国工人阶级万岁！
    世界工联万岁！
    中国人民的先锋、中国共产党及其领袖毛主席万岁！
    和平民主堡垒和苏联、全世界劳动者的领袖斯大林同志万岁！
