Title: Yugoslav Capital Belgrade Actually Becomes Center of American Imperialist Espionage Conspiracy
Author:
Date: 1949-11-04
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd: TASS Paris dispatch: According to reliable sources, a conference of American ambassadors to European countries discussed the issue of adopting special propaganda measures in various Eastern European people's democracies. The propaganda center is being established in Belgrade and is directed by the American embassy in Belgrade. American authorities are appropriating $10 million as funds for underground activities in various people's democracies. The espionage center organization in Belgrade will send intelligence personnel specially trained by the American Federal Bureau of Investigation to American embassies in various people's democracies to serve as diplomatic positions such as counselors. These people have been selected from the intelligence personnel originally sent to various people's democracies. They can speak the languages of those countries and will use false names to carry out new work in the future.

### 南斯拉夫首都贝尔格莱德　竟成美帝间谍阴谋中心

1949-11-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫首都贝尔格莱德
  　竟成美帝间谍阴谋中心
    【新华社北京三日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：据可靠消息，美国驻欧洲国家大使的会议讨论了在东欧各人民民主国家采取特别宣传措施的问题，宣传中心正在贝尔格莱德建立，由美国驻贝尔格莱德大使馆指挥。美国当局拨款一千万美元作为在各人民民主国家进行地下活动的经费。在贝尔格莱德的间谍中心组织，将把美国联邦调查局特别训练出来的谍报人员，派到各人民民主国家的美国大使馆中担任参赞之类的外交职位。这些人都是从原来派赴各人民民主国家的谍报人员中遴选出来的，他们能操各该国家的语言，今后将改用假名去进行新的工作。
