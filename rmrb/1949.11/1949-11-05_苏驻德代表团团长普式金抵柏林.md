Title: Soviet Representative to Germany, Mr. Pushkin, Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1949-11-05
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 3rd: TASS Berlin, November 3rd—Mr. Pushkin, Head of the Soviet diplomatic mission to the Provisional Government of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Berlin on the 2nd. Greeting him at the airport were the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Didinger, the Mayor of Greater Berlin, Ebert, officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Central News Bureau, as well as representatives from various parties and organizations.

### 苏驻德代表团团长普式金抵柏林

1949-11-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏驻德代表团团长普式金抵柏林
    【新华社北京四日电】塔斯社柏林三日讯：苏联驻德意志民主共和国临时政府外交代表团团长普式金二日到达柏林。在机场欢迎的有德意志民主共和国外长窦丁格尔、大柏林市长艾柏特、外交部和中央新闻局的官员，以及各党派和团体的代表。
