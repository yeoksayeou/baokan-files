Title: Athens Bandits, Ignoring Condemnation from Democratic Public Opinion, Continue to Persecute Greek Patriots; World Federation of Students and Other Groups Issue Strong Protests
Author:
Date: 1949-11-01
Page: 3

　　Athens Bandits, Ignoring Condemnation from Democratic Public Opinion,
  　Continue to Persecute Greek Patriots
    World Federation of Students and Other Groups Issue Strong Protests
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 31st] TASS, Athens, 28th: While the US-UK bloc in the Political Committee of the UN General Assembly stubbornly refuses to adopt the Soviet proposal to stop the execution of Greek patriots, the bloody terror of the Athens royalist fascists has not ceased. According to news from Athens: Two days ago, the Piraeus Military Tribunal sentenced eight people—four of whom were women—to death on the grounds that they joined the Communist Party and refused to abandon their political claims.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 31st] Polish Press Agency, Prague, 29th: The Secretary-General of the World Federation of Students and the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Student Union have sent telegrams to UN General Assembly President Romulo and UN Secretary-General Lie, protesting the Athens government's execution of Greek patriots.

### 雅典匪徒不顾民主舆论谴责　竟继续残害希爱国者  世界学联等团体严重抗议

1949-11-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　雅典匪徒不顾民主舆论谴责
  　竟继续残害希爱国者
    世界学联等团体严重抗议
    【新华社北京三十一日电】塔斯社雅典二十八日讯：当美英集团在联合国大会的政治委员会上顽强地拒绝采纳苏联关于停止处决希腊爱国者的建议时，雅典保皇法西斯血腥的恐怖迄未停止。据雅典消息：两天以前，比里犹斯军事法庭曾判处八人——其中四人为妇女——死刑，理由是他们加入共产党并拒绝放弃他们的政治主张。
    【新华社北京三十一日电】波兰通讯社布拉格二十九日讯：世界学联总书记及捷克斯洛伐克学生联盟中央委员会，顷分别致电联大主席罗慕洛及联合国秘书长莱氏，抗议雅典政府处决希腊爱国者。
