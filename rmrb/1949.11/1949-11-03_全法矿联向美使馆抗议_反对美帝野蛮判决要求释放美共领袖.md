Title: French National Miners' Federation Protests to U.S. Embassy, Opposes U.S. Imperialist Brutal Verdict, and Demands Release of American Communist Leaders
Author:
Date: 1949-11-03
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd. TASS, Paris: The Executive Committee of the French Miners' Federation has lodged a strong protest with the U.S. Embassy in Paris against the verdict on the eleven American Communist leaders. The Executive Committee demands their immediate release. L'Humanité reports that the French people will continue to strongly protest this barbaric verdict. Numerous letters of protest are arriving daily at the U.S. Embassy in France from all over France.

### 全法矿联向美使馆抗议　反对美帝野蛮判决要求释放美共领袖

1949-11-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　全法矿联向美使馆抗议
  　反对美帝野蛮判决要求释放美共领袖
    【新华社北京二日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：法国矿工联盟执委会顷向巴黎美国大使馆强硬抗议对美共十一领袖的判决。执委会要求立即释放他们。人道报称：法国人民将继续强力抗议这一野蛮判决。法国全国各地每天有许多抗议书已到达美国驻法大使馆。
