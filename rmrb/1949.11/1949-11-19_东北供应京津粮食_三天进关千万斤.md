Title: Northeast Supplies Grain to Beijing and Tianjin: Ten Million Jin Enter Customs in Three Days
Author:
Date: 1949-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th – To supply food to the people of Beijing and Tianjin, the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government has consulted with the Ministry of Railways to transport large quantities of grain from the Northeast into customs continuously. Since the 15th, 167 train cars of coarse grains from the Northeast, totaling ten million jin, have entered customs every day.

### 东北供应京津粮食　三天进关千万斤

1949-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北供应京津粮食
  　三天进关千万斤
    【新华社北京十八日电】为供应京津一带民食，中央人民政府贸易部已和铁道部商定，将东北大批粮食源源运入关内。自十五日以来，每天进关的东北杂粮有一百六十七车皮，共达一千万斤。
