Title: Shanxi, Hebei, and Six Other Provinces and Cities Establish Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Organizations; General Council Establishes Division of Labor for Institutions
Author:
Date: 1949-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th: Amidst the nationwide celebrations of the October Revolution Day, various localities have successively established branches and preparatory committees of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The Shanxi and Hebei branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association were both established on the 7th, with Lai Ruoyu, Deputy Secretary of the Shanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Yang Xiufeng, Chairman of the Hebei Provincial People's Government, elected as presidents of the respective branches. The preparatory committee for the Ningxia branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association was also formed on the 6th during a symposium held in Yinchuan by people from all walks of life to celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution. The Hebei branch already has 19,661 members. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association organizations in Taiyuan and Jinan were both established on the 7th. The former has established 151 branches and sub-branches with 45,000 members, while the latter has established 39 branches and sub-branches with over 60,000 members in two months. At the same time, the preparatory committee for the Fuzhou branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association was also established.

The General Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association convened its first council meeting at 9:00 AM on the 13th, presided over by Secretary-General Qian Junrui, to discuss the organization and division of labor of the council. The following institutions were established by resolution: 1. Secretariat: with Lai Yali as Director and Shu Yi [@] as Deputy Director; 2. Organization Department: with Xin Zhichao as Director and Ding Xiu and Cao Mengjun as Deputy Directors; 3. Research and Publication Department: with Zhang Zhongshi as Director and Shen Zhiyuan and Cao Jinghua as Deputy Directors; 4. Service Department: with Cao Yu as Director and Pan Defeng and Jin Shan as Deputy Directors; 5. Liaison Department: with Xiao San as Director and Li Dequan and Hu Jibang as Deputy Directors; 6. Library and Archives: with Wang Zhixiang as Director and Shu Yi [@] concurrently as Deputy Director.

### 山西河北等六省市  中苏友协组织建立  总会干事会确定机构分工

1949-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    山西河北等六省市
    中苏友协组织建立
    总会干事会确定机构分工
    【本报综合消息】全国欢庆十月革命节声中，各地纷纷成立中苏友好协会分支会及筹备会。山西、河北中苏友好协会分会均于七日成立，中共山西省委副书记赖若愚、河北省人民政府主席杨秀峰分别当选为分会会长；中苏友好协会宁夏分会筹备会亦于六日在银川各界庆祝苏联十月革命三十二周年座谈会中产生。河北分会现已拥有会员一万九千六百六十一人。太原、济南两市的中苏友好协会组织均于七日成立，前者已建立支分会一百五十一个，拥有会员四万五千人；后者在两个月中已建立了三十九个支会与支分会会员达六万余人。与此同时，福州市亦成立了中苏友好协会分会筹备会。
    【新华社北京十三日电】中苏友好协会总会于十三日上午九时召集第一次干事会会议，由总干事钱俊瑞主持，讨论干事会的组织与分工。经决议设以下各机构：一、秘书处：以赖亚力为主任，舒翼＠为副主任；二、组织部：以辛志超为主任，丁秀、曹孟君为副主任；三、研究出版部：以张仲实为主任，沈志远、曹靖华为副主任；四、服务部：以曹禺为主任、潘德枫、金山为副主任；五、联络部：以萧三为主任，李德全、胡济邦为副主任；六、图书资料室：以王之相为主任，舒翼＠兼副主任。
