Title: Yuehan Railway Wuchang-Changsha Section Opens to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1949-11-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, November 2nd: The Yueyang-Miluo section of the Yuehan Railway was repaired and opened to traffic at the end of October. Thus, the 474-kilometer railway line of the Yuehan Railway, from Wuchang in the north to Songting Station south of Changsha, has been completely repaired. The first passenger-freight mixed train between Wuchang and Changsha departed from each location on the morning of the 1st. From the 1st, one passenger train and one mixed train will run between the two cities daily. The original planned opening date for the Wuchang-Changsha line was November 7th. Due to the enthusiastic labor competition launched by the railway construction workers, the entire project was completed ahead of schedule.

### 粤汉路武昌长沙段通车

1949-11-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　粤汉路武昌长沙段通车
    【新华社汉口二日电】粤汉铁路岳阳至汨罗段已于十月底修复通车。至此，粤汉铁路北起武昌，南至长沙以南的宋亭车站四百七十四公里的铁路线已完全修复。武昌和长沙间对开的首次客货混合列车，已于一日晨分别开出。从一日起，两地间每日对开客车及混合列车各一次。武昌长沙间原定在十一月七日通车，由于修路员工们展开了热烈的劳动竞赛，全部工程提前完成。
