Title: Iranian Labor Party and Women's and Youth Organizations Send Congratulations on the Birth of New China; Vietnamese Youth Federation Sends Congratulations to the All-China Youth Federation
Author:
Date: 1949-11-17
Page: 3

　　Iranian Labor Party and Women's and Youth Organizations
    Send Congratulations on the Birth of New China
  　Vietnamese Youth Federation Sends Congratulations to the All-China Youth Federation
    [Comprehensive News Report from Our Newspaper] The Central Committee of the Iranian Labor Party sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, congratulating the founding of the People's Republic of China. At the same time, the Iranian Democratic Women's Association sent a telegram congratulating the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the Iranian Democratic Youth Central Committee sent a telegram congratulating the Chinese Progressive Youth. The congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Iranian Labor Party stated that the victory of the Chinese people is one of the greatest victories of mankind in the struggle for its own liberation, and is a brilliant example for Asia, Iran, and other oppressed nations in their struggle for their own liberation. The congratulatory telegram from the Iranian Democratic Women's Association stated: We heard the news of the establishment of the People's Republic of China with great joy. We Iranian women believe that your victory is also our victory. Dear sisters, we have gained more assistance from your victory to continue fighting against the reactionaries and British and American imperialism at home. The congratulatory telegram from the Iranian Democratic Youth Central Committee stated: We are following the glorious victories won by the Chinese People's Liberation Army with great excitement, and we also revel in every heroic achievement of the Chinese soldiers. Undoubtedly, the great historical victory of the Chinese people plays an important role in the world's struggle for lasting peace and in the cause of liberation of all oppressed nations.
    [Another dispatch] The Central Executive Committee of the Vietnamese Youth League sent a telegram to the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, congratulating the founding of New China.

### 伊朗劳动党及妇女青年团体  电贺新中国诞生　越南青联电我国青联致贺

1949-11-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　伊朗劳动党及妇女青年团体
    电贺新中国诞生
  　越南青联电我国青联致贺
    【本报综合报导】伊朗劳动党中央委员会致电中国共产党中央委员会，祝贺中华人民共和国成立。同时，伊朗民主妇女协会电贺中华全国民主妇女联合会，伊朗民主青年中央委员会电贺中国进步青年。伊朗劳动党中央委员会贺电中，认为中国人民的胜利是人类在争取自身解放的斗争中的一个最伟大的胜利，对于亚洲和伊朗及其他各被压迫民族，是他们在争取自身解放的斗争中的辉煌的榜样。伊朗民主妇女协会贺电则称：我们怀着巨大的喜悦，听到中华人民共和国建立的消息。我们伊朗妇女，认为你们的胜利，也是我们的胜利。亲爱的姐妹们，我们从你们的胜利中，获得了更多的继续与国内的反动派及英美帝国主义作斗争的援助。伊朗民主青年中央委员会贺电中说：我们怀着非常激动的心情，注意着中国人民解放军所获得的辉煌的胜利，并且还因为中国战士们的每一个英勇的战绩而狂欢。无疑地，中国人民伟大的历史胜利，在全世界争取持久和平的斗争中和在各被压迫民族的解放事业中是起着重大作用的。
    【又讯】越南青年联盟中央执行委员会致电我国民主青联，祝贺新中国的成立。
