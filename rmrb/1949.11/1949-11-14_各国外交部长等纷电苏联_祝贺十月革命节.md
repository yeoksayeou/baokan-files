Title: Foreign Ministers and Others Send Telegrams to the Soviet Union Congratulating on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1949-11-14
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th - On the 32nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, the Soviet government, the Central Committee of the CPSU, and the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs continued to receive congratulatory telegrams from Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China; Puk, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Romanian People's Republic; Kalle, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Hungarian People's Republic; Poptomov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Bulgarian People's Republic; Lezhiski, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Polish Republic; Dodinger, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic; Park Heon-young, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Count Sforza, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Italy; Wang Jiaxiang, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union; Rastovicka, Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to the Soviet Union; Sambrowitz, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Republic to the Soviet Union; Idamjab, Special Envoy of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Soviet Union; McIntosh, Secretary of the New Zealand Ministry of Foreign Affairs (representing the Prime Minister); Watkins, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Canada to the Soviet Union (representing Canadian Foreign Minister Pearson); the Finnish People's Democratic League; Yugoslav revolutionary exiles; the Italian-Soviet Association; Pashov, Chairman of the Bulgarian Slavic Committee; and Basikowski, Chairman, and Chakin, Secretary-General, of the Polish Democratic Party.

### 各国外交部长等纷电苏联  祝贺十月革命节

1949-11-14
第3版()
专栏：

    各国外交部长等纷电苏联
    祝贺十月革命节
    【新华社北京十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：在十月社会主义革命三十二周年纪念日，苏联政府、联共中央及苏联外交部继续收到中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来，罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长波克，匈牙利人民共和国外交部长卡莱，保加利亚人民共和国外交部长波普托莫夫，波兰共和国外交部副部长列兹齐斯基，德意志民主共和国外交部长窦丁格尔、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外交部长朴宪永，意大利外交部长史佛卓，中华人民共和国驻苏大使王稼祥，捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻苏大使拉斯托维茨卡，波兰共和国驻苏代理代办萨姆布劳维兹，蒙古人民共和国驻苏特使伊达姆热布，新西兰外交部秘书姆辛托斯（代表总理），加拿大驻苏代理代办华特金斯（代表加拿大外交部长皮尔逊），芬兰人民民主联盟，南斯拉夫革命流亡者，意苏协会，保加利亚斯拉夫委员会主席波绍夫，以及波兰民主党主席巴西科夫斯基和总书记查金等发来的贺电。
