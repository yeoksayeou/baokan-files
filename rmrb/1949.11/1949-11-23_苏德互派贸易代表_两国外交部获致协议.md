Title: Soviet-German Exchange of Trade Representatives; Agreement Reached by Foreign Ministries of Both Countries
Author:
Date: 1949-11-23
Page: 3

　　Soviet-German Exchange of Trade Representatives
　　Agreement Reached by Foreign Ministries of Both Countries
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 21st] TASS, Berlin: The press office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic issued a communique stating that after exchanging letters on November 19th at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic reached an agreement to allow the Soviet Union to establish a trade delegation in the German Democratic Republic, with its office in Berlin. Simultaneously, the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic has appointed Gustav Sobottka as the Commercial Counselor to the diplomatic mission in the Soviet Union.

### 苏德互派贸易代表　两国外交部获致协议

1949-11-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏德互派贸易代表
　　两国外交部获致协议
    【新华社北京二十一日电】塔斯社柏林讯：德意志民主共和国外交部新闻处发表公报称：德苏两国于十一月十九日在外交部交换公函后，获致协议，同意苏联在德意志民主共和国设立贸易代表团，其办事处设于柏林。同时，德意志民主共和国部长会议已任命格斯塔夫·索波特基亚为驻苏外交使团商务参赞。
