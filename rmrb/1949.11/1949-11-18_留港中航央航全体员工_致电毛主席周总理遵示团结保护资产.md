Title: All Employees of China Aviation and Central Aviation Remaining in Hong Kong Telegraph Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou, Acknowledging Instructions to Unite and Protect Assets
Author:
Date: 1949-11-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 17th: All employees of China National Aviation Corporation (CNAC) in Hong Kong telegraphed Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou on the 14th, expressing their compliance with instructions to do their best to protect the company's assets. The telegram reads as follows:

To Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou of the Central People's Government:

We have received your telegram of November 12th with respect. We are deeply gratified that you have accepted the request of our colleagues and appointed Liu Jingyi as the General Manager of our company. From now on, all employees pledge to unite as one under the leadership of the People's Government and General Manager Liu, stand firm, do our best to protect the company's assets, and await further instructions. We hereby reply by telegram.

　　　　　　　　　All Employees of China National Aviation Corporation
　　　　　　 פון　　　　　　　　November 14th

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 17th: All employees of Central Air Transport Corporation (CATC) now in Hong Kong telegraphed Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou on the 14th, expressing their compliance with instructions to unite, stand firm at their posts, and protect the company's property remaining in Hong Kong. The telegram reads as follows:

To Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou of the Central People's Government:

We have received your telegram of November 13th with respect. Since our uprising, all of our employees have quickly resumed work, united as one, stood firm at our posts, and protected the company's property remaining in Hong Kong. From now on, we pledge to intensify our studies under your leadership and strive to build the people's aviation industry of the new China.

　　　　　　　　　　　All Employees of Central Air Transport Corporation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 14th

### 留港中航央航全体员工　致电毛主席周总理遵示团结保护资产

1949-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　留港中航央航全体员工
  　致电毛主席周总理遵示团结保护资产
    【新华社北京十七日电】香港中国航空公司全体员工十四日致电毛主席、周总理，表示遵示尽力保护公司资产。电文如下：中央人民政府毛主席周总理钧鉴：
    奉十一月十二日钧电，敬悉。接受本公司同人请求，并任命刘敬宜为本公司总经理，至深欣慰。今后全体员工誓在人民政府及刘总经理领导下，团结一致，坚定立场，尽力保护公司资产，静待指示。谨此电复。
    　　　　　　　　　中国航空公司全体员工
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月十四日
    【新华社北京十七日电】现在香港中央航空公司全体员工十四日致电毛主席、周总理，表示遵示团结一致，坚守岗位，保卫公司留在香港的财产。电文如下：中央人民政府毛主席、周总理钧鉴：
    十一月十三日电敬悉。我们全体员工自起义后已迅速恢复了工作，并团结一致，坚守岗位，保卫公司留在香港的财产。今后誓在钧座领导下，加紧学习，努力建设新中国的人民航空事业。
    　　　　　　　　　　　中央航空公司全体员工
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月十四日
