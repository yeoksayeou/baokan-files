　　Xinhua Radio Tonight's Program Highlights
    [This newspaper dispatch] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station's 19:15 special broadcast time regarding the Asian Women's Representative Conference will today broadcast in Cantonese the speech given yesterday by Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, on this station: "Welcoming the Asian Women's Representative Conference." At 20:15, an introduction to Vietnam, one of the countries participating in the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference and the Asian Women's Representative Conference. 18:30, first arts and entertainment program, new folk art – Tiepian Dagu [iron plate ballad] "Wei Bingyi Returns Home" and "Envoy from Jiangnan". 22:00, second arts and entertainment program: musical program celebrating International Student Week.
    Also, on the second channel, at 17:40, employee time, a speech by Xiao Ming, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, entitled: "Workers of the Entire City of Beijing, Let Us Commemorate the Great Soviet October Socialist Revolution Day with Our Practical Actions." At 20:00, Voice of the Municipality, broadcasting the "Methods for Catching Rats" issued by the Beijing Municipal Epidemic Prevention Committee.

### 新华电台今晚要目

1949-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　新华电台今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台十九点十五分关于亚洲妇女代表会议专题广播时间，今天用广东话播送中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅昨天在该台广播的演讲词：“迎接亚洲妇女代表会议”。二十点十五分介绍参加亚洲、澳洲工会会议和亚洲妇女代表会议的国家之一——越南。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，新曲艺——铁片大鼓“魏炳义回家”和“江南使者”。二十二点第二次文艺节目：庆祝国际学生周音乐节目。
    又第二台十七时四十分职工时间，本市职工总会筹委会主任萧明讲话，题目是：“全北京市的工人同志们，以我们的实际行动来纪念伟大的苏联十月社会主义革命节”。二十时市政之声，播送北京市防疫委员会发布的“捕老鼠的方法”。
