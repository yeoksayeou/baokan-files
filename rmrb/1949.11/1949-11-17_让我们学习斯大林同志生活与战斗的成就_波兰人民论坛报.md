Title: Let Us Study the Achievements of Comrade Stalin's Life and Struggle - Tribune of the Polish People
Author:
Date: 1949-11-17
Page: 3

　　Let Us Study the Achievements of Comrade Stalin's Life and Struggle
  　Tribune of the Polish People
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th: The Polish News Agency reports: The Polish people are enthusiastically preparing to celebrate Comrade Stalin's 70th birthday on December 21st. The Politburo of the Polish United Workers' Party has issued a decision. The People's Tribune published a special article entitled "Let Us Study the Achievements of Comrade Stalin's Life and Struggle," elaborating: "This day is a great festival for the Polish working masses, the working class, and the United Workers' Party. Stalin's name has long been the banner of struggle and symbol of victory for hundreds of millions of working people around the world. According to a Telecommunications Agency report on the 7th: Polish political parties and organizations have established a celebration committee, with President Bierut as chairman of the committee.
    According to a Telecommunications Agency report from Sofia: The production competition held between Bulgarian workers and enterprises to celebrate Stalin's 70th birthday has achieved remarkable production records. In the city of Burgas, many enterprises celebrated by completing their production plans ahead of schedule. Farmers also participated in the competition, with farmers in Shumen County announcing a ten-day blitz labor campaign, during which they would collectively work in the fields and complete the sowing plan.

### 让我们学习斯大林同志生活与战斗的成就　波兰人民论坛报

1949-11-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　让我们学习斯大林同志生活与战斗的成就
  　波兰人民论坛报
    【新华社北京十六日电】波兰通讯社讯：波兰人民热烈筹备庆祝十二月二十一日斯大林七十寿辰。波统一工人党政治局已发布决定，人民论坛报以“让我们学习斯大林同志生活与战斗的成就”为题，发表专文，加以论述称：“这一天对波兰劳动群众、工人阶级和统一工人党是一个伟大的节日。斯大林的名字许久以来便是全世界亿万劳动人民斗争的旗帜和胜利的象征。据电通社七日讯：波兰各政党团体已成立了庆祝委员会，贝鲁特总统为委员会主席。
    据电通社索非亚讯：保加利亚工人与企业间为庆祝斯大林七十寿辰举行的生产竞赛，已获得引人注目的生产纪录。在布尔加斯城，许多企业以提前完成生产计划来表示庆祝。农民们也参加了竞赛，邵门县的农民们宣布进行十天突击劳动，在这十天中他们将集体到田中工作，并完成播种计划。
