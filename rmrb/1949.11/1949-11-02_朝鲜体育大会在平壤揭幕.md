Title: Korean National Sports Meet Opens in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1949-11-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 1st. The Korean National Sports Meet opened at 10:00 AM on October 30th simultaneously at six locations including the Moranbong Stadium and the Normal University Stadium in Pyongyang. More than 3,000 athletes, including workers, farmers, soldiers, students, and cadres from various organizations, participated in the sports competitions. The performances and competitions included bayonet drills by the Korean People's Army, group exercises, and various ball games and track and field events. The Deputy Minister of Education of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in his opening address, called on all sports workers to strive to establish a new direction for people's sports through this conference.

### 朝鲜体育大会在平壤揭幕

1949-11-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜体育大会在平壤揭幕
    【新华社平壤一日电】朝鲜全国体育大会于十月三十日上午十时在平壤牡丹峰运动场、师范大学运动场等六处同时开幕。参加体育竞赛的有工人、农民、士兵、学生和各机关干部的选手三千余名。表演与竞赛的项目有朝鲜人民军的刺枪、团体操及各种球赛与田赛径。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府教育部副部长在致开幕词中，号召全体体育工作者通过这次大会，为树立人民体育的新方向而斗争。
