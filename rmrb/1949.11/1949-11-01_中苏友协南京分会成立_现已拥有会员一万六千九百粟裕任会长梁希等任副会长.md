Title: Nanjing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Established; Now Has 16,900 Members; Su Yu as President, Liang Xi and Others as Vice Presidents
Author:
Date: 1949-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, October 31st: The Nanjing branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held its inaugural meeting at 10:00 am today, with over 400 member representatives in attendance. General Su Yu, Deputy Director of the Nanjing Military Control Commission, Liang Xi, Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Forestry and Land Reclamation, Tang Liang, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, and Mr. Jabhou, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, attended the meeting. First, Chen Qi Wu, the person in charge of the preparatory committee, reported on the preparatory process, stating that since the Nanjing representatives' conference approved the preparation for the Nanjing branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, after more than a month of preparation, it now has 16,927 members. Subsequently, Su Yu, Liang Xi, Tang Liang, Ke Qingshi and other 22 people were elected as the presidium, and Jabhou was also invited to join the presidium. Then, General Su Yu delivered a speech. After recounting the tremendous spiritual and moral assistance of the Soviet Union to the Chinese revolution, General Su Yu pointed out that the great victory of the Chinese people is inseparable from the deep friendship of the Soviet Union towards China, and we must cherish and strive to consolidate this immortal friendship between China and the Soviet Union. We must strengthen the education of the combination of internationalism and patriotism, and we must learn the great spirit of internationalist friendship of the great Soviet people under the education of Lenin and Stalin, a spirit of high selflessness and unconditional assistance to the Chinese people. General Su Yu also said: In order to build an independent, peaceful, democratic, unified, and prosperous New China, we must learn from the experience of the Soviet revolution, learn from the experience of Soviet economic construction and cultural construction, and diligently "take Russia as our teacher," without the slightest complacency or empiricism.

After General Su Yu finished speaking, Soviet friend Jabhou, as well as Tang Liang, Liang Xi, Yin Zanxun of the Nanjing branch of the Association of Scientific Workers, Gao Jun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Federation of Trade Unions, Chen Minzhi, representative of democratic parties, Wang Zhenyu, representative of farmers, and others spoke successively. After praising the deep friendship between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union, Jabhou, on behalf of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, expressed his guarantee to help the Chinese people culturally. Others who spoke all praised the traditional friendship of the Soviet people towards China and were determined to learn from the Soviet Union and establish the idea of firmly standing in the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union. Minister Liang Xi pointed out in his speech that the tasks of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association are two: first, to consolidate world peace, and second, to communicate the culture between China and the Soviet Union. Director Gao Jun emphasized the success of Soviet construction and called on everyone to learn from Soviet construction. After the meeting passed the constitution of the branch and sent a telegram of tribute to Chairman Mao and Marshal Stalin, Su Yu was elected as the president of the branch, Liang Xi, Tao Menghe, Tang Liang, and Ke Qingshi were elected as vice presidents, and Yin Zanxun and other 131 people were elected as directors. At this point, the Nanjing branch was declared established.

### 中苏友协南京分会成立  现已拥有会员一万六千九百粟裕任会长梁希等任副会长

1949-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协南京分会成立
    现已拥有会员一万六千九百粟裕任会长梁希等任副会长
    【新华社南京三十一日电】中苏友好协会南京分会于今日上午十时举行成立大会，共到会员代表四百余人。南京市军事管制委员会副主任粟裕将军，中央人民政府林垦部梁希部长，人民解放军第三野战军政治部唐亮副主任及苏联对外文化协会代表贾布候等均出席会议。首由筹备会负责人陈其五报告筹备经过，说明自南京各界代表会议通过筹备中苏友好协会南京分会以来，经月余筹备，现已拥有会员一万六千九百二十七人。接着通过粟裕、梁希、唐亮、柯庆施等二十二人为主席团，贾布候亦被邀参加主席团。继由粟裕将军致词。粟裕将军于历述苏联在精神上、道义上对中国革命的巨大援助之后，指出中国人民的伟大胜利是与苏联对中国的深厚友谊分不开的，我们必须十分珍贵和努力巩固中苏两国这种永远不朽的友谊。我们就必须加强国际主义和爱国主义结合的教育，我们必须学习在列宁斯大林教养之下的伟大的苏联人民那种高度的大公无私、无条件的帮助中国人民的伟大的国际主义的友爱精神。粟裕将军又说：为要建设一个独立、和平、民主、统一、富强的新中国，我们就必须学习苏联革命的经验，学习苏联经济建设、文化建设的经验、要勤勤恳恳地“以俄为师”，不能有丝毫的骄傲自满情绪，也不能有经验主义。
    粟裕将军讲毕，苏联友人贾布候，以及唐亮、梁希和自然科学工作者协会南京分会尹赞勋、总工会筹备委员会主任高骏、民主党派代表陈敏之、农民代表王震宇等相继讲话。贾布候于盛赞中苏两大民族的深厚友谊之后，代表苏联对外文协表示保证在文化上帮助中国人民。其他讲话的都盛赞苏联人民对中国的传统友谊，决心要向苏联学习，树立坚决站在以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营的思想。梁希部长讲话中指出中苏友好协会的任务有二：第一是巩固世界和平，第二是沟通中苏两国文化。高骏主任则着重说明苏联的建设成功，号召大家学习苏联的建设。大会通过分会会章和向毛主席及斯大林大元帅致敬电后，选出粟裕为分会会长，梁希、陶孟和、唐亮、柯庆施四人为副会长，尹赞勋等一百三十一人为理事。至此，南京分会乃告成立。
