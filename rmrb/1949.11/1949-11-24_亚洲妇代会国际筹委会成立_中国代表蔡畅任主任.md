Title: Asian Women's Conference International Preparatory Committee Established; Chinese Representative Cai Chang Appointed Director

Author:
Date: 1949-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd – The International Preparatory Committee for the Asian Women's Representative Conference was formally established today, the 23rd, and held its first meeting. Representatives from eight countries, including China, Kyrgyzstan, Korea, Vietnam, Iran, India, Burma, and the Philippines, as well as representatives from two organizations, the Women's International Democratic Federation and the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, a total of ten units, attended. The meeting was chaired by Cai Chang, the representative from China. She began by saying: According to the resolution of the 8th Executive Committee of the Women's International Democratic Federation, the International Preparatory Committee for the Asian Women's Representative Conference should be composed of fourteen units. Today, ten units are present, so I propose that the meeting decide to formally establish the International Preparatory Committee. This was unanimously approved by all attending representatives, and Cai Chang was elected as the Director of the International Preparatory Committee, and Lu Cui as the Secretary-General. Cai Chang then gave a brief work report on behalf of the working committee. The meeting decided that the four sub-committees originally under the working committee would continue their work, with the original heads remaining unchanged, and the newly arrived preparatory representatives would join the respective committees. The names and heads of each committee are as follows: (1) Editorial and Review Committee, Zhang Qinqiu (Chinese representative); (2) Organization Committee, Yelemeeva (representative of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee); (3) Propaganda Committee, Simone Betrand (representative of the Women's International Democratic Federation); (4) Exhibition Committee, Li Dequan (Chinese representative).

### 亚洲妇代会国际筹委会成立　中国代表蔡畅任主任

1949-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　亚洲妇代会国际筹委会成立
　　中国代表蔡畅任主任
    【新华社北京二十三日电】亚洲妇女代表会议国际筹备委员会于二十三日正式宣布成立，举行第一次会议。出席者有中国、吉尔吉斯、朝鲜、越南、伊朗、印度、缅甸、菲律宾等八个国家的代表及国际民主妇联、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会两个组织的代表共十个单位。会议由中国代表蔡畅担任主席。她首先说：根据国际民主妇联第八次执行委员会决议，亚洲妇女代表会议国际筹委会应由十四个单位共同组成，今天出席的已有十个单位，故提请会议决定正式成立国际筹备委员会。当获得全体出席代表一致通过，并推定蔡畅为国际筹委会主任，陆璀为秘书长。蔡畅继代表工作委员会作简单的工作报告。会议通过决定原属工作委员会的四个小委员会继续进行工作，原负责人不变，新到之筹备代表分别加入各委员会。各委员会名称及负责者为：（一）编审委员会，张琴秋（中国代表）；（二）组织委员会，耶列梅耶娃（苏联妇女反法西斯委员会代表）；（三）宣传委员会，西门贝特朗（国际民主妇联代表）；（四）展览委员会，李德全（中国代表）。
