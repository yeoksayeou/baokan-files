Title: Beijing's First All-Circles Representative Conference Consultative Committee Holds Fourth Plenary Session Yesterday; Approves Draft Agenda for the Second Conference; Completes Preparatory Work for the Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-11-20
Page: 4

　　Beijing's First All-Circles Representative Conference Consultative Committee
  　Holds Fourth Plenary Session Yesterday
    Approves Draft Agenda for the Second Conference
  　Completes Preparatory Work for the Conference
    [Beijing News] The Second Beijing All-Circles People's Representative Conference is scheduled to officially open at 2:00 PM this afternoon (20th) at Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park. The First All-Circles People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee held its fourth plenary session at 3:00 PM yesterday (19th). Each group separately reported on the five financial and tax proposals that the Municipal Government prepared to submit to this All-Circles People's Representative Conference. After discussion, the proposals were unanimously approved. The meeting also approved the draft agenda for this People's Representative Conference, completing the preparatory work for the conference. The main agenda of the Second All-Circles People's Representative Conference is as follows: 1. Exercise the functions and powers of the People's Representative Conference, discuss and approve the "Organizational Regulations of the Second Beijing All-Circles People's Representative Conference," the "Provisional Organizational Outline of the Beijing Municipal People's Government," and the "Election Rules for the Mayor, Deputy Mayors, and Members of the Beijing Municipal Government Committee," and elect the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and Members of the Second Beijing All-Circles People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, as well as the Mayor, Deputy Mayors, and Members of the Beijing Municipal Government Committee. 2. Discuss and approve the five financial and tax proposals submitted by the Municipal Government. 3. Discuss and resolve issues such as worker layoffs and relief, and the establishment of large-scale amateur schools. This conference will also hear and review the work report of the Municipal Government, discuss how to further restore and develop production and municipal construction, and discuss how to severely punish unscrupulous merchants and stabilize prices to safeguard the people's livelihood.

### 京首届各界代表会协商委会　昨开第四次全体会议  通过二届大会议程草案　完成大会各项准备工作

1949-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　京首届各界代表会协商委会
  　昨开第四次全体会议
    通过二届大会议程草案
  　完成大会各项准备工作
    【北京讯】北京市第二届各界人民代表会议定今（二十）日下午二时在中山公园中山堂正式开幕。第一届各界人民代表会议协商委员会于昨（十九）日下午三时举行第四次全体会议，由各小组分别报告市政府准备向本届各界人民代表会议提出的五个财政税收提案，讨论结果，经全体一致通过；会议并通过本届人民代表会议议事日程的草案，完成了本届大会的准备工作。第二届各界人民代表会议的主要议程为：一、行使人民代表大会的职权，讨论与通过北京市第二届各界人民代表会议组织条例、北京市人民政府暂行组织大纲和北京市市长、副市长及市政府委员会委员选举规则，选举北京市第二届各界人民代表会议协商委员会主席、副主席及委员，北京市市长、副市长及市政府委员会委员。二、讨论与通过市政府所提出的五项财政税收提案。三、讨论与解决工人解雇与救济问题和举办大规模的业余学校等问题。本届大会并将听取与审查市政府的工作报告，讨论如何进一步恢复与发展生产及市政建设等工作，并讨论如何严惩奸商，稳定物价，以保障人民的生活等问题。
