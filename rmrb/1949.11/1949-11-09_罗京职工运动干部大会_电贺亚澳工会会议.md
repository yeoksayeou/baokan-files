Title: Luo Jing Workers' Movement Cadre Conference Cables Congratulations to Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-11-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th: The Workers' Movement Cadre Conference in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, passed a congratulatory telegram to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference on October 29th, stating: We extend our congratulations to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference held in Beijing, the capital of free China, and wish the conference success in organizing support for workers in their struggle against the bloody exploitation of American, British, and Dutch colonialists! Long live the struggle of workers in colonies and dependent countries to win national independence, democratic freedom, and oppose exploitation and imperialist enslavement! Long live the glorious struggle of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for the prosperity of the young People's Republic of China! Long live the heroic Soviet workers, the vanguard of peace, democracy, and progress! Long live the World Federation of Trade Unions for strengthening the united struggle of the international working class and striving for the victory of the people in the cause of freedom, peace, and opposition to the instigation of a new war by American and British imperialist elements!
　　The congratulatory telegram was issued with the signature of Apostol, Chairman of the Presidium of the Conference.

### 罗京职工运动干部大会　电贺亚澳工会会议

1949-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　罗京职工运动干部大会
  　电贺亚澳工会会议
    【新华社布拉格七日电】罗马尼亚京城布加勒斯特的职工运动干部大会，于十月二十九日通过致亚澳工会会议的贺电称：我们谨向在自由中国首都北京举行的亚澳工会会议致贺，并祝大会组织支持工人们反对美英荷殖民者的血腥剥削的工作成功！殖民地和附属国职工，为争取民族独立、民主自由、反对剥削和帝国主义奴役的斗争万岁！中华全国总工会为年青的中华人民共和国的繁荣而进行的光辉斗争万岁！和平民主进步的先锋——英勇的苏联工人万岁！世界工联为加强团结国际工人阶级的联合斗争，争取自由、和平、反对美英帝国主义分子挑拨新战争的人民的胜利万岁！
    贺电由大会主席团主席阿波司多尔署名发出。
