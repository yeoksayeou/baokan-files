Title: People's Political Consultative Conference Representatives Return to Various Locations from Beijing; Local People Hold Rallies to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1949-11-01
Page: 4

　　People's Political Consultative Conference Representatives Return to Various Locations from Beijing
  　Local People Hold Rallies to Welcome Them
    Shanghai
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 31st] The people of Shanghai, with excited and enthusiastic hearts, welcomed the representatives of the People's Political Consultative Conference and the returning Chinese delegation from the World Federation of Trade Unions, who had just returned from Beijing. On the 29th of this month, nearly ten thousand people from all walks of life in Shanghai held a welcome rally at Yiyuan Garden. When Rao Shushi, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, accompanied more than sixty representatives of the People's Political Consultative Conference into the venue, the entire audience erupted in warm applause. The welcome meeting began with a speech by Rao Shushi, followed by Chen Yi, Chief Representative of the East China Region and Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, who reported on the great significance of the People's Political Consultative Conference and the establishment of the Central People's Government. Mayor Chen pointed out: "The task of us Shanghai citizens is to faithfully obey the leadership of the Central Government, to strive for the realization of the Common Program, and to strive for the construction of a new Shanghai for the people." Subsequently, Chen Shutong, Ma Yinchu, Liu Xiao, Lin Qiangyun, Zhu Junxin, Chen Zhenzhong, Chen Wangdao, Sheng Pihua, Wang Yunsheng, Chen Yisheng, Kui Yanfang, Shi Fuliang and others spoke in succession. Liu Xiao, the chief representative of the Shanghai People's Organizations, said: "In this People's Political Consultative Conference, only 'Shanghai People's Organizations' formed a unit as a single city. This is the glory of the people of Shanghai and the result of the years of cruel struggle against imperialism and bureaucratic capitalism." Lin Qiangyun, one of the representatives returning from the World Federation of Trade Unions, paid tribute to the representatives of the People's Political Consultative Conference and the leaders of the people of Shanghai. He said: "Due to the victory of the Chinese people, the delegation received a warm welcome from the people of various countries abroad. Workers in the Soviet Union, various new democratic countries, and capitalist countries unanimously believe that the victory of the Chinese people is of great help to the people of the world, and they also believe that the Chinese people, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the leader of the Chinese people, Mao Zedong, will surely achieve greater success."
    Kaifeng
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, October 31st] Representatives of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Wu Zhipu, Ji Wenfu, Wang Guohua, Liu Jixue, etc., have returned to Kaifeng. On the 15th of this month, more than 5,000 people from various directly subordinate agencies and all walks of life in Bian City held a welcome rally at Henan University, inviting the representatives to give various reports on the People's Political Consultative Conference. Chairman Wu Zhipu emphasized that the People's Political Consultative Conference is the victorious crystallization of the Chinese people's arduous struggle for nearly a hundred years, especially in the past thirty years. He called on the 30 million people of Henan to unite and resolutely and thoroughly implement all the resolutions of the People's Political Consultative Conference. Ji Wenfu, representative of the Central China Region and professor at Henan University, pointed out the unity, harmony, and democratic style of the CPPCC conference in his speech. Liu Jixue, representative of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, praised the sincere and humble style of the Chinese Communist Party in leading the conference. Farmers' representatives Wang Guohua, Cao Tie, etc., successively spoke about their feelings about participating in the CPPCC conference.
    Xi'an
    [Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, October 30th] A portion of the representatives and alternate representatives from various parts of Northwest China who attended the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing - Jiang Longji, Du Yanqing, Wu Hongbin, Wang Yuefeng, Fang Wenli, Liu Yuhou, Wang Debiao, Li Delin, Jin Hanwen, An Wenqin, Jin Shanshou and others, eleven people in total, returned to Xi'an at 2:30 p.m. on the 25th. Representatives from the Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government and other agencies were dispatched to welcome them at the Chang'an Railway Station.
    Korean Overseas Chinese
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 30th] Representatives of overseas Chinese from various parts of North Korea held a conference at the Pyongyang Municipal Art Theater at 10:00 a.m. on the 28th to welcome Zhao Lingde, a representative of the People's Political Consultative Conference who had just returned from Beijing. More than 100 people, including representatives of the North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation and local overseas Chinese representatives and guests, participated in the conference. Korean friends, Kim Chang-jun, Secretary-General of the Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front, Park Jeong-ae, Chairman of the Democratic Women's League, and Kang Jin-geon, Chairman of the Farmers' League, were also invited to attend the meeting. At the meeting, Representative Zhao Lingde reported on the process and grand occasion of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. In his report, he first pointed out that it was the glory of all 40,000 overseas Chinese in North Korea to have representatives attend the first plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in the people's capital, Beijing, and pay tribute to and present a flag to the most respected leader, Chairman Mao, and Commander-in-Chief Zhu. He said excitedly: "We are now new overseas Chinese, the overseas Chinese of the People's Republic of China, no longer orphans overseas!" After reporting in detail the basic spirit of the three major documents of the CPPCC and the moving scenes of the founding ceremony of the People's Republic of China, Representative Zhao Lingde called on all overseas Chinese to actively participate in the democratic construction of Korea, defend the fruits of the people's victory, and thoroughly smash the US imperialist conspiracy of aggression. The guests who spoke included Wen Shizhen, the chief representative of the Northeast Commercial Delegation, and overseas Chinese representatives Cui Dianfang and Wang Xinyi.

### 人民政协代表自京返回各地　当地人民集会欢迎

1949-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　人民政协代表自京返回各地
  　当地人民集会欢迎
    上海
    【新华社上海三十一日电】上海人民以兴奋热烈的心情欢迎甫自北京归来的人民政治协商会议代表及出席世界工联归国的中国代表团。本月二十九日，上海各界近万人在逸园举行欢迎大会。当中共上海市委书记饶漱石、上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年陪同六十余位人民政协代表进入会场时，全场响起热烈的掌声。欢迎会首由饶漱石致词，接着由华东区首席代表、上海市人民政府市长陈毅报告政协会及中央人民政府成立的伟大意义。陈市长指出：“我们上海市民的任务便是忠实地服从中央政府的领导，为实现共同纳领而奋斗，为建设人民新上海而奋斗”。接着，陈叔通、马寅初、刘晓、林锵云、朱俊欣、陈震中、陈望道、盛丕华、王芸生、陈已生、蒉延芳、施复亮等相继讲话。上海人民团体首席代表刘晓说：“在这次人民政协中，单独一个城市成立一个单位的只有‘上海人民团体’一个，这是上海人民的光荣，是上海人民多年来反帝反官僚资本的残酷斗争所获得的。”出席世界工联归国的代表之一，林锵云，对人民政协代表及领导上海人民的首长们致敬。他说：“由于中国人民的胜利，代表团在国外受到各国人民热烈的欢迎。苏联、各新民主主义国家及资本主义国家里的工人们，一致认为中国人民的胜利对全世界人民有莫大的帮助，并且也相信中国人民在中国共产党及中国人民领袖毛泽东领导之下，一定会获得更大的成功”。
    开封
    【新华社开封三十一日电】出席中国人民政治协商会议的代表吴芝圃、嵇文甫、王国华、刘积学等已返抵开封。本月十五日，汴市各直属机关及各界人民五千余人，假河南大学举行欢迎大会，邀请各代表作有关人民政协的各种报告。吴芝圃主席着重说明人民政协是中国人民近百年来，特别是近三十年来艰苦奋斗的胜利结晶。他号召全河南三千万人民团结起来，坚决彻底执行人民政协的全部决议。华中区代表、河南大学教授嵇文甫讲话中指出了政协会议的团结和谐和民主作风。中国国民党革命委员会代表刘积学盛赞领导会议的中国共产党的诚恳谦虚作风。农民代表王国华、曹铁等相继讲述了自己参加政协会议的感想。
    西安
    【新华社西安三十日电】西北各地赴京出席中国人民政治协商会议的一部分代表及候补代表——江隆基、杜延庆、吴鸿宾、王悦丰、房文礼、刘玉厚、王德彪、李德林、金汉文、安文钦、金山寿等十一人已于二十五日下午二时半返抵西安。中共中央西北局、陕甘宁边区政府等机关均派有代表在长安车站欢迎。
    朝鲜华侨
    【新华社平壤三十日电】北朝鲜各地华侨代表于二十八日上午十时，在平壤市立艺术剧场举行大会，欢迎新自北京归来的出席人民政治协商会议代表赵令德。大会参加者有北朝鲜华侨联合总会和各地华侨代表及来宾共百余人。朝鲜友人、朝鲜祖国统一民主战线书记局长金昌俊、民主妇女同盟主席朴正爱、农民同盟主席姜镇乾等亦应邀与会。会上由赵令德代表报告中国人民政协开会的经过及其盛况。他在报告中首先指出，北朝鲜全体四万余侨胞能有代表在人民的首都北京出席中国人民政协第一届全体会议，并向最敬爱的领袖毛主席和朱总司令致敬、献旗，是全体侨胞的光荣。他兴奋地说：“我们现在是新华侨了，是中华人民共和国的华侨了，不再是海外孤儿了！”赵令德代表在详尽报告了政协三大文献的基本精神和中华人民共和国开国盛典的动人场面之后，号召全体侨胞积极参加朝鲜的民主建设，保卫人民的胜利成果，彻底粉碎美国帝国主义的侵略阴谋。来宾讲话的有东北商业代表团首席代表文士桢，华侨代表崔殿芳和王辛逸等人。
