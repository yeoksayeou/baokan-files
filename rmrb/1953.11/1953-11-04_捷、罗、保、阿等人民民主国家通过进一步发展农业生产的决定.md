Title: People's Democratic Countries such as Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria and Albania Pass Decisions to Further Develop Agricultural Production
Author:
Date: 1953-11-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 3rd: The government of the Czechoslovak Republic promulgated a decision on October 26th regarding the provision of financial assistance to unified agricultural cooperatives and individual farmers. This assistance includes: lowering the interest rates on past loans of unified agricultural cooperatives and extending the terms for their installment payments and interest payments; granting new loans to unified agricultural cooperatives engaged in crop production and livestock breeding; and granting loans to individual farmers. The decision also stipulates that special rewards will be given to unified agricultural cooperatives that achieve production targets. Individual farmers can apply for short-term loans to purchase fertilizers, seeds, and pay for services from machine tractor stations. They can also apply for long-term loans to improve soil and purchase breeding livestock.
    On October 31st, "Red Right Newspaper" published an editorial pointing out: The purpose of this decision is to continuously increase the yield per unit area of crops and the productivity of livestock products. These financial assistance measures will help improve agricultural production, thereby improving the supply of food and industrial raw materials.
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: According to TASS, Bucharest, 28th: Bucharest newspapers published the decision of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic and the Central Committee of the Workers' Party on improving the methods of ordering and purchasing livestock and livestock products. The purpose of this decision is to improve the supply of livestock products to residents and the supply of raw materials to industry, and to encourage the development of animal husbandry. The decision stipulates granting collective farms and working farmers the right to purchase industrial products at reduced prices, and establishes other preferential measures to encourage the development of animal husbandry.
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: According to "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" Issue 42, 1953: The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party recently held a plenary session. The meeting discussed and unanimously approved a resolution on measures to further develop agriculture.
    The resolution pointed out that Bulgaria's total agricultural output has exceeded the pre-war level and is continuing to increase. Cooperation in rural areas is constantly consolidating and developing. The average yield of grain and economic crops is increasing every year. The main task in agriculture at present is to further increase crop yields and the productivity of animal husbandry products. The resolution stipulates a number of important measures to strive for a further upsurge in agriculture and animal husbandry in the coming years. The resolution pays special attention to the development of animal husbandry.
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: According to "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" Issue 42, 1953: The Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania and the Political Bureau of the Labor Party recently adopted various resolutions on the development of agriculture. These resolutions stipulate the adoption of various measures to improve the welfare of members of agricultural production cooperatives and individual farmers, improve the food supply to working farmers in urban and rural areas, encourage working farmers to increase the output of agricultural and livestock products, further develop agricultural production, and enable the country to reserve sufficient food and industrial raw materials. The Albanian government and the Central Committee of the Labor Party also called for expanding the exchange of materials between urban and rural areas, and for the masses of farmers to participate in socialist construction.

### 捷、罗、保、阿等人民民主国家通过进一步发展农业生产的决定

1953-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

    捷、罗、保、阿等人民民主国家通过进一步发展农业生产的决定
    【新华社布拉格三日电】捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府在十月二十六日颁布了关于给予统一农业合作社和个体农民以财政援助的决定。这些援助包括：降低统一农业合作社过去贷款的利率，延长其分期还款和缴付利息的期限；对进行农作物生产并进行牲畜饲养的统一农业合作社给予新的贷款；给予个体农民以贷款。决定并规定对能达到生产目标的统一农业合作社给予特别的奖励。个体农民可以申请短期贷款，以购买肥料、种子和支付给机器拖拉机站的报酬等。他们也可申请长期贷款以改良土壤和购买种畜等。
    十月三十一日，“红色权利报”发表社论指出：这个决定的目的在于不断提高农作物的单位面积产量和牲畜的产品生产率。这些财政援助将有助于提高农业生产，从而改进食粮和工业原料的供应。
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社布加勒斯特二十八日讯：布加勒斯特各报刊载了罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议和工人党中央委员会关于改进定购和采购牲畜和牲畜产品的办法的决议。这个决议的目的在于改善对居民的牲畜产品供应和对工业的原料供应，并鼓励饲畜业的发展。决议中规定给予集体农庄和劳动农民以按减低的价格购买工业品的权利，并定出了其他鼓励发展饲畜业的优惠办法。
    【新华社三日讯】据“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”一九五三年第四十二期报道：保加利亚共产党中央委员会最近举行了全体会议。会议讨论并一致通过了关于进一步发展农业的措施的决议。
    决议指出，保加利亚的农业总产量已超过战前的水平，并且还在继续增加。农村中的合作化正在不断地巩固和发展。谷物和经济作物的平均收获量每年都在增加。当前农业中的主要任务是进一步增加农作物收获量和饲畜业的产品生产率。决议中规定了若干重要措施，争取农业和饲畜业在未来几年中的进一步高涨。决议特别注意饲畜业的发展。
    【新华社三日讯】据“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”一九五三年第四十二期报道：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议和劳动党政治局最近通过了关于发展农业的各项决议。这些决议规定采取各种措施，来增进农业生产合作社社员和个体农民的福利，改善对城乡劳动农民的粮食供应，鼓励劳动农民增加农产品和畜产品的产量，进一步发展农业生产，使国家能够储备充足的粮食和工业原料。阿尔巴尼亚政府和劳动党中央并号召扩展城乡物资交流，使农民群众参加到社会主义建设工作中去。
