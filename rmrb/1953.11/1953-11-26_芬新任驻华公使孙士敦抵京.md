Title: New Finnish Envoy to China, Sundström, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-11-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 25th.
    The new Envoy of the Republic of Finland to our country, Sundström, arrived in Beijing by train on the 25th.
    Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, was at the station to welcome him.
    Also present at the station to welcome him were foreign envoys and embassy staff in China. The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Republic of Finland in China, Brownstein, and others were also present to welcome him.

### 芬新任驻华公使孙士敦抵京

1953-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    芬新任驻华公使孙士敦抵京
    【新华社二十五日讯】芬兰共和国新任驻我国公使孙士敦在二十五日乘车抵京。
    前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如。
    前往车站欢迎的还有外国驻华使节和使馆人员。芬兰共和国驻华公使馆临时代办勃朗斯坦特等也前往欢迎。
