Title: Democratic National Construction Association General Committee Holds Plenary Session: Mobilizing Members to Unite Industrialists and Businessmen to Implement the National General Line
Author:
Date: 1953-11-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 9th—The Plenary Session of the General Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association (CDNCA) opened in Beijing on the 9th. The central task of the meeting is to mobilize all members of the CDNCA to fully recognize the great significance of realizing the national general line for the transition period, and to actively implement the national general line with practical actions.

Ninety-one members attended, and one hundred and seven people attended as observers. Those invited to attend the conference included Yu Yifu, Deputy Director of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Luo Longji, Vice Chairman of the China Democratic League; Wang Shao'ao, Vice Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy and Director Zhao Puchu; Peng Zemin, Chairman of the Central Supervisory Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Chen Shutong, representative of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Member Representative Conference; Jin Dingxin, representative of the China Zhi Gong Party; Chen Bingji, representative of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Headquarters; Wu Lijian and Jiao Qi, representatives of the United Front Work Department of the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and others. Members of the CDNCA attending the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Member Representative Conference and members of the Beijing CDNCA Branch also participated in the conference.

Huang Yanpei, Chairman of the CDNCA, delivered an opening speech entitled "Mobilize All Members, Unite Private Industrialists and Businessmen under the Leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, and Strive to Implement the National General Line for the Transition Period." He said that our country has entered a new period of planned construction, and the country has announced the general line for the transition period, and has specifically put forward the basic tasks of the first five-year construction plan. Therefore, all members must be mobilized to express their loyal support for the national general line with practical actions. He said that in order to adapt to the requirements of the national general line, the future work policy and central task of the CDNCA are: to cultivate and improve the members of the association who are industrialists and businessmen to become the backbone of the industrial and commercial circles through the transformation of private industry and commerce, increased production and frugality, and various patriotic movements; to make them first truly patriotic and law-abiding, actively improve production and management, study hard, sincerely accept socialist transformation, and play a promoting role in the industrial and commercial circles.

Li Jishen, Luo Longji, Wang Shao'ao, Peng Zemin, and Chen Shutong all addressed the conference. They said that it is glorious to strive to implement the general line. They hoped that the members of the CDNCA would take the lead, strive to unite private industrialists and businessmen, accept the leadership of the state-owned economy, accept the supervision of the working masses, oppose tax evasion and shoddy workmanship, study hard, and fight for the implementation of the national general line!

### 民建总会委员会全体会议开幕  动员会员团结工商业者执行国家总路线

1953-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    民建总会委员会全体会议开幕
    动员会员团结工商业者执行国家总路线
    【新华社九日讯】中国民主建国会总会委员会全体会议九日在北京开幕。会议中心任务是：动员民主建国会全体会员充分认识实现国家过渡时期总路线的重大意义，并积极地用实际行动来贯彻执行国家总路线。
    出席委员九十一人，列席一百零七人。应邀参加大会的，有中国共产党中央统战部副部长于毅夫、中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深、中国民主同盟副主席罗隆基、中国民主促进会副主席王绍鏊和理事赵朴初、中国农工民主党中央监察委员会主席彭泽民、中华全国工商业联合会会员代表大会代表陈叔通、中国致公党代表金鼎新、台湾民主自治同盟总部代表陈炳基、中共中央华北局统战部代表吴力践、焦琦等。出席中华全国工商业联合会会员代表大会的中国民主建国会会员和北京市民主建国会分会委员，都参加了大会。
    中国民主建国会主任委员黄炎培作了“全体会员，动员起来，在中国共产党的领导下，团结私营工商业者，为贯彻国家过渡时期的总路线而奋斗”的开幕词。他说，我们国家已进入一个有计划建设的新时期，国家已经宣布了过渡时期的总路线，并且具体地提出了第一个五年建设计划的基本任务。因此，全体会员必须动员起来，以实际行动来表示对国家总路线的忠诚拥护。他说，为了适应国家总路线的要求，中国民主建国会今后的工作方针和中心任务是：通过私营工商业的改造工作、增产节约和各种爱国运动，培养和提高本会工商业者的会员成为工商界中的骨干分子；使他们首先做到真正爱国守法，积极改善生产经营，努力学习，真诚接受社会主义改造，并在工商界中起推动作用。
    李济深、罗隆基、王绍鏊、彭泽民、陈叔通都向大会致词。他们说，为贯彻总路线而奋斗是光荣的。他们希望中国民主建国会会员积极起带头作用，努力团结私营工商业者，接受国营经济领导，接受工人群众的监督，反对偷税漏税和偷工减料，努力学习，为贯彻执行国家总路线而斗争！
