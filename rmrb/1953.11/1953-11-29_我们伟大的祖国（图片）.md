Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-11-29
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Under the leadership of the People's Government, Mongolian herdsmen in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region are carrying out various tasks to protect livestock through the winter safely. This is the flag of the livestock mutual aid team in Inner Mongolia—members of the Huhele Tai Livestock Mutual Aid Team of Bayan Hada Sumu (District), Chen Barag Banner, Eastern Administrative Region. They are using grass cutters to harvest forage on the grasslands for livestock to survive the winter.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    内蒙古自治区的蒙族牧民，在人民政府领导下正进行保护牲畜安全过冬的各项工作。这是内蒙古畜牧业互助组的旗帜——东部行政区陈巴尔虎旗巴颜哈达苏木（区）胡和勒泰畜牧业互助组的组员。他们正用打草机在草原上收割牲畜过冬用的草料。
