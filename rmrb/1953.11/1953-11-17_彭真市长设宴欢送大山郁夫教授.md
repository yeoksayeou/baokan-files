Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Professor Oyama Ikuo
Author:
Date: 1953-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 16th—Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 16th to bid farewell to Oyama Ikuo, the distinguished Japanese public figure, who is about to leave our country.

    Those attending the banquet included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; Wu Yaozong, Shen Yanbing, Xu Deheng, Liao Chengzhi, Luo Longji, and Xiao San, Standing Committee Members; Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Zhang Bojun and Cai Tingkai, Council Members of the World Peace Council; and figures from the cultural and educational circles, including Wang Xuewen, Ai Siqi, Jin Zhonghua, Zhou Peiyuan, Hu Xikui, Sun Xiaocun, Liang Sicheng, Hua Luogeng, Tang Yongtong, Jiang Nanxiang, and Qian Weichang.

    Also present at the banquet were: Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee; Kameta Togo and Van Thulerry, Deputy Secretaries-General; and Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for World Peace, among others.

    Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General, also attended the banquet.

    The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 彭真市长设宴欢送大山郁夫教授

1953-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真市长设宴欢送大山郁夫教授
   【新华社十六日讯】北京市人民政府市长彭真十六日晚设宴欢送即将离开我国的日本杰出的公众领袖大山郁夫。
    出席宴会的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，副主席陈叔通，秘书长刘贯一，常务委员吴耀宗、沈雁冰、许德珩、廖承志、罗隆基、萧三；中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会主席张奚若；世界和平理事会理事章伯钧、蔡廷锴；文化教育界人士王学文、艾思奇、金仲华、周培源、胡锡奎、孙晓村、梁思成、华罗庚、汤用彤、蒋南翔、钱伟长等人。
    出席宴会的还有：亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书长刘宁一，副秘书长龟田东伍、万徒勒里；越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探等人。
    出席宴会的还有：北京市人民政府副市长张友渔，秘书长薛子正。
    宴会在友好空气中进行。
