Our great motherland
Cork is the raw material for making cork, and it has great uses in industry. A large amount of cork has begun to be harvested in the Dabie Mountains of Anhui Province, and more than 800 tons of cork have been shipped to Shanghai and other places, which is welcomed by industrial users. This is a large amount of cork produced in Huoshan County, Anhui Province, which is being transported to various places by wooden rafts.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    栓皮是制造软木的原料，在工业上有很大的用途。安徽省大别山区已开始大量采剥，并已有八百多吨栓皮运往上海等地，受到工业用户的欢迎。这是安徽省霍山县出产的大量栓皮，它们正由木簰运往各地。
