Title: Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum to Open on the 17th

Author: 
Date: 1953-11-16
Page: 4

　　Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum to Open on the 17th
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th] TASS Moscow reported on the 14th: Moscow newspapers reported on the 14th that the Mausoleum of V.I. Lenin and J.V. Stalin will be open on November 17, 1953. The opening hours of the Mausoleum are: Tuesday - from 12:00 to 15:00, Wednesday, Thursday, Saturday - from 17:00 to 20:00, Sunday - from 12:00 to 17:00.
    The procedures for the working people to pay respects at the Mausoleum are temporarily stipulated as follows: The working people of Moscow City should obtain a ticket indicating the date of the visit from the Executive Committee of the District People's Representative Soviet, and enter in the prescribed order. People from other cities and states who come to Moscow City on official business, vacation or sightseeing should organize their visits through relevant ministries, competent authorities, and social organizations.

### 列宁和斯大林陵墓定十七日开放

1953-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　列宁和斯大林陵墓定十七日开放
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：十四日莫斯科各报报道：乌·伊·列宁和约·维·斯大林的陵墓将于一九五三年十一月十七日开放。陵墓开放时间为：星期二——从十二时至十五时，星期三、星期四、星期六——从十七时至二十时，星期日——从十二时至十七时。
    劳动人民谒陵的手续暂时规定如下：莫斯科市的劳动人民，要通过区劳动人民代表苏维埃执行委员会领取注明谒陵日期的谒陵券，按规定次序进谒。其他城市和各州因公以及休假或是游览而来到莫斯科市的人们，通过有关各部、主管机关和社会团体组织谒陵。
