Title: I am Departing for Korea
Author: Tie Yifu Jiang·Aili Youfu
Date: 1953-11-01
Page: 6

　　I am Departing for Korea
    Tie Yifu Jiang·Aili Youfu
    Editor's Note: Tie Yifu Jiang·Aili Youfu is a young poet of the Xinjiang Uyghur nationality. He is participating in the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea to express their sympathy and appreciation. This poem was written before he left the country.
       ＊  ＊  ＊
    I am departing towards the East,
    Along the boundless rivers and mountains of the motherland.
    Deep in my heart, emotions surge,
    Thinking of the destination, I am infinitely excited.
    Knowledgeable friends wave to me,
    Seeing me off with joy;
    The motherland looks kindly into my eyes, saying:
    "Keep my love deep in your heart."
    "Do you know," friends say,
    "How glorious this journey of yours is!
    The great motherland is the pillar of Eastern happiness,
    It is this great nation,
    Trusting you, entrusting you with a sacred mission;
    You are about to embark on this long journey, you are so fortunate,
    Do not spare your strength.
    Shed tears of joy,
    The destination you will reach is a sacred place;
    There you have your heroic comrades,
    And your compatriots—dear brothers.
    Go, go see the people of Korea,
    Meet with friends, embrace them,
    This is a powerful encouragement to friends,
    A powerful demonstration to the enemy.
    You are about to depart, raise your glass,
    Let us drink the cup of happiness dry!
    Go, go express the people's will,
    Go express the leader's loving care!"
    Farewell, I am departing for Korea,
    I hold the people's faith in my heart;
    Constantly, repeatedly, I savor
    The sweet words of my friends.
    (Translated by Kelimu·Hejieyefu)

### 我向朝鲜出发
铁依甫江·艾里尤夫
1953-11-01
第6版()
专栏：

　　我向朝鲜出发
    铁依甫江·艾里尤夫
    编者按：铁依甫江·艾里尤夫是新疆维吾尔族的年轻诗人，此次参加中国人民赴朝慰问团前往朝鲜，这首诗就是他在出国之前写的。
       ＊  ＊  ＊
    我向着东方出发，
    沿着祖国无边无际的河山。
    心底的深处涌流着感情，
    一想到目的地，我无限兴奋。
    知己的朋友们向我挥手，
    满怀着欢喜给我送行；
    祖国亲切地望着我的眼睛，说：
    “把我的慈爱深藏在你心中。”
    “你知道吗，”朋友们说，
    “你这次的远行够多么光荣！
    伟大的祖国是东方幸福的支柱，
    就是这个伟大的国家，
    确信着你，把神圣的事情向你委托；
    你就要踏上这个远程，你真幸福，
    千万别吝惜你的力量。
    你流下快乐的眼泪吧，
    你将到达的目的地是神圣的地方；
    在那里有你的英勇的战友，
    还有你的同胞——亲爱的兄弟。
    你去吧，去见朝鲜的人民，
    和朋友们见面，和他们拥抱，
    这对朋友是有力的鼓励，
    对敌人是强大的示威。
    你就要出发啦，举起你的杯来，
    让我们把幸福的酒杯喝干！
    你去吧，去表达人民的心意，
    去表达领袖的慈爱！”
    再见吧，我向着朝鲜出发，
    我把人民的信念怀抱在我的心里；
    不断地，反复地，我咀嚼着
    朋友们的甘美的言语。
    （克里木·赫捷耶夫译）
