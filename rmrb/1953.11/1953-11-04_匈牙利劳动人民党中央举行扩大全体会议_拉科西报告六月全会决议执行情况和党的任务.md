Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Central Committee Holds Enlarged Plenary Session; Rakosi Reports on Implementation of June Plenary Session Resolutions and Party Tasks
Author:
Date: 1953-11-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 2nd: TASS reports: The Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party held an enlarged plenary session on October 31st. Mátyás Rákosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee, delivered a report on the implementation of the Central Committee's June plenary session resolutions and the Party's future tasks. After a full discussion, the enlarged plenary session adopted a resolution on the report.

    The Central Committee decided to convene the Third Congress of the Hungarian Working People's Party in April next year.

    The enlarged plenary session elected Antal Apró as a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee.

### 匈牙利劳动人民党中央举行扩大全体会议  拉科西报告六月全会决议执行情况和党的任务

1953-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利劳动人民党中央举行扩大全体会议
    拉科西报告六月全会决议执行情况和党的任务
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会于十月三十一日举行扩大全体会议。中央委员会第一书记马加斯·拉科西在会上作了关于中央委员会六月全体会议决议的执行情况和党的今后任务的报告。扩大全体会议经过充分的讨论以后，就这个报告通过了一项决议。
    中央委员会决定在明年四月召开匈牙利劳动人民党第三次代表大会。
    扩大全体会议选举安·阿普罗为中央委员会政治局委员。
