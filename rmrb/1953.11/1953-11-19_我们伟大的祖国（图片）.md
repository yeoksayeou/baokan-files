Author:
Date: 1953-11-19
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Both public and private ships sailing on the Yangtze River have overfulfilled their transportation plans for the past three quarters and are now striving to complete their annual transportation and increased production and conservation plans. This is the "Hua 205" steamship of the Yangtze River Shipping Administration, docked at the Hankou wharf. It is loading carriages needed by the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    航行在长江上的公、私船舶，都超额完成了过去三个季度的运输计划，现在正为完成全年的运输和增产节约计划而奋斗。这是停泊在汉口码头的长江航运管理局华二○五号轮船。它正在装运成渝铁路所需要的车厢。
