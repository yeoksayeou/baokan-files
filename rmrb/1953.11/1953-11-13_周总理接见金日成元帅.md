Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Marshal Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1953-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council received Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on the evening of the 12th. Also present at the reception were Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and He Yangtian, Secretary to the Premier. Present at the reception were Li Kenong and Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Gan Yetao, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of our country's Embassy in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 周总理接见金日成元帅

1953-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见金日成元帅
    【新华社十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理在十二日晚接见朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅。受接见的还有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁外务相南日及首相秘书何仰天。接见时在座的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长李克农、章汉夫和我国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆临时代办甘野陶。
