Title: Moscow and Pyongyang Newspapers Publish News of Chairman Mao Meeting with Korean Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-11-16
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 15th:
    Moscow newspapers on the 15th published news of Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government meeting with the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. At the same time, the newspapers also published Premier Zhou Enlai's welcoming speech at the banquet in honor of the delegation.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 15th:
    Pyongyang newspapers on the 15th prominently published on the front page the news of Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government meeting with the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Premier Kim Il-sung, and the news of Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai hosting a banquet to welcome the Korean government delegation.
    At the same time, the newspapers also published the Beijing "People's Daily" editorial welcoming the Korean government delegation.

### 莫斯科和平壤报纸  刊载毛主席接见朝鲜政府代表团的消息

1953-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科和平壤报纸
    刊载毛主席接见朝鲜政府代表团的消息
    【新华社莫斯科十五日电】莫斯科各报十五日刊载了我中央人民政府毛泽东主席接见朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团的消息。同时，各报还刊载了周恩来总理在招待代表团的宴会上的欢迎词。
    【新华社平壤十五日电】平壤各报十五日都在一版首要地位刊载我中央人民政府毛泽东主席接见金日成首相率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团的消息和周恩来总理兼外交部长设宴欢迎朝鲜政府代表团的消息。
    同时，各报并刊登了北京《人民日报》关于欢迎朝鲜政府代表团的社论。
