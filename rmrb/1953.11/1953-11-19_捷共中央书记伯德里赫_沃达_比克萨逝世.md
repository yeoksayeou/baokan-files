Title: Death of Bedřich Voda-Biksa, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1953-11-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 17th: TASS reports: The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party announced: Bedřich Voda-Biksa, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, has passed away.

### 捷共中央书记伯德里赫·沃达——比克萨逝世

1953-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共中央书记伯德里赫·沃达——比克萨逝世
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社布拉格十七日讯：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会宣布：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会书记伯德里赫·沃达——比克萨逝世。
