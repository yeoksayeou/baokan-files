Title: Guillén Departs Beijing for Cuba
Author:
Date: 1953-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: Mr. Guillén, the Cuban representative who was invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and other five organizations to come to our country to participate in the Commemoration of Four World Cultural Celebrities, left Beijing by plane on the morning of October 31, via Moscow, to return to his country. Among those who went to the airport to see him off were Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Ai Qing, Tu Changwang, Shen Qiyi and many other people from literary, artistic and scientific circles.

### 纪廉已离北京返古巴

1953-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    纪廉已离北京返古巴
    据新华社讯：应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等五团体邀请前来我国参加纪念世界四位文化名人大会的古巴代表纪廉先生在十月三十一日晨乘机离北京取道莫斯科返国。前往机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会联络部副部长唐明照以及文艺界、科学界人士艾青、涂长望、沈其益等多人。
