Title: Shvernik Hosts Grand Reception for Foreign Trade Union Observer Delegations

Author:
Date: 1953-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 9th: According to TASS, Nikolai Shvernik, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, held a grand reception on the 8th in the Column Hall of the Trade Union Building in Moscow, to entertain foreign trade union delegations invited to the Soviet Union to attend the October Revolution Day celebrations.

Present at the reception were representatives and trade union activists of the working class from: the People's Republic of China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, Romania, Albania, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the German Democratic Republic, the United Kingdom, France, Italy, Finland, Belgium, Austria, Indonesia, Tunisia, Algeria, Madagascar, Syria, Lebanon, Cameroon, India, Burma, Vietnam, Japan, Australia, the Union of South Africa, Canada, Chile, Venezuela, Bolivia, Costa Rica, Spain, Argentina, Guatemala, Panama, Mexico, Cuba, and the Dominican Republic, as well as a delegation from the World Federation of Trade Unions.

Also present as hosts were: leaders of various Soviet trade unions, Moscow industrial innovators, scientists, and artists, as well as representatives of Soviet social organizations and the press.

Shvernik addressed the gathering. He warmly congratulated the foreign friends on their achievements in the struggle for the vital interests of the working people and for world peace.

Louis Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions, also spoke at the meeting.

The reception proceeded in an atmosphere of further consolidating the unity and warm friendship of the working people of all countries.

### 什维尔尼克盛会招待各国工会观礼代表团

1953-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    什维尔尼克盛会招待各国工会观礼代表团
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：全苏工会中央理事会主席尼·什维尔尼克，八日在莫斯科工会大厦圆柱大厅举行盛大的招待会，招待应邀到苏联参加十月革命节观礼的外国工会代表团。
    出席招待会的有：中华人民共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、英国、法国、意大利、芬兰、比利时、奥地利、印度尼西亚、突尼斯、阿尔及利亚、马达加斯加、叙利亚、黎巴嫩、喀麦隆、印度、缅甸、越南、日本、澳大利亚、南非联邦、加拿大、智利、委内瑞拉、玻利维亚、哥斯达黎加、西班牙、阿根廷、危地马拉、巴拿马、墨西哥、古巴和多米尼加共和国的工人阶级代表与工会活动家，以及世界工会联合会代表团。
    出席作陪的有：苏联各工会的领导人、莫斯科工业革新者、科学家和艺术工作者，以及苏联社会团体和新闻界代表。
    什维尔尼克在会上致辞。他热烈祝贺外国友人在为劳动人民的切身利益、为世界和平的斗争中获得成就。
    世界工联总书记路易·赛扬也在会上讲了话。
    招待会在进一步巩固世界各国劳动人民的团结和热烈友好的气氛中进行。
