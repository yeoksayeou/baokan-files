Title: U.S. Aircraft Intrudes into Our Area's Airspace Four More Times
Author:
Date: 1953-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, November 11th: At the meeting of secretaries-general of both sides of the Military Armistice Commission on the 11th, the Korean-Chinese side raised the issue of the U.S. side's military aircraft violating the armistice agreement and intruding into our area's airspace four times, requesting the U.S. side to pay attention.

　　At approximately 14:50 on November 5th, two U.S. military single-engine aircraft intruded into our area's airspace north of the military demarcation line near Sogeori, southwest of Kumhwa, from the south, and then flew south. At approximately 9:50 on November 6th, a U.S. military single-engine aircraft intruded into the airspace over Bongsu-dong and other areas in our demilitarized zone northwest of Cheorwon from the southeast, and then intruded into the airspace over Mabang-ri and other areas in our area before flying south. At approximately 14:15 on November 8th, two U.S. military single-engine aircraft intruded into the airspace over Bonghwahyeon and other areas in our area southeast of Songneung from the southeast, and then flew south. On the same day, at approximately 16:15, a U.S. military single-engine aircraft intruded into our area's airspace north of the military demarcation line near Sogeori, southwest of Kumhwa, from the southwest, and then flew back to the area controlled by the U.S. side.

### 美机又四次侵入我方地区上空

1953-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美机又四次侵入我方地区上空
    【新华社开城十一日电】十一日军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议上，朝中方面提出美方军用飞机四次违反停战协定、侵入我方地区上空的事件，要求美方予以注意。
    十一月五日十四时五十分左右，美方军用单引擎飞机两架，由南方侵入金城西南三巨里附近军事分界线以北我方地区上空，又向南飞去。十一月六日九时五十分左右，美方军用单引擎飞机一架，由东南方侵入铁原西北我方非军事区内的凤树洞等地上空，然后又侵入我方地区内的马放里等地上空后，向南飞去。十一月八日十四时十五分左右，美方军用单引擎飞机两架，由东南方侵入朔宁东南我方地区的烽火岘等地上空后，向南飞去。同日十六时十五分左右，美方军用单引擎飞机一架，由西南方侵入金城西南三巨里附近军事分界线以北我方地区上空，然后飞回美方控制地区。
