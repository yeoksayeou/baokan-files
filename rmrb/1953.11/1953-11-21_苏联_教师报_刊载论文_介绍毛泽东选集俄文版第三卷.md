Title: Soviet "Teacher's Newspaper" Publishes Article Introducing the Russian Edition of Mao Zedong's Selected Works, Volume III
Author:
Date: 1953-11-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 19th: The Soviet "Teacher's Newspaper" published an article by P. Sevostyanov on the 18th, introducing the Russian edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," Volume III. The author introduced the historical background and main content of the works included in Volume III of the Russian edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong." He said: In his works from May 1939 to May 1941, Comrade Mao Zedong, based on Marxist-Leninist theory, made a theoretical analysis of the political line, strategy, and tactics of the Chinese Communist Party during this period, stipulated the path to fight and triumph over imperialism and feudalism, and comprehensively studied the issue of building a new democratic China. The author pointed out that the works in Volume III of the Russian edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" are a brilliant example of Comrade Mao Zedong's creative application of Marxism-Leninism to the specific historical environment of China and the integration of Marxist-Leninist theory with the practice of the Chinese revolution. These works prove the vitality of Marxist-Leninist thought.

### 苏联“教师报”刊载论文  介绍毛泽东选集俄文版第三卷

1953-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联“教师报”刊载论文  
    介绍毛泽东选集俄文版第三
    【新华社莫斯科十九日电】苏联“教师报”十八日发表了普·谢沃斯季安诺夫的一篇论文，介绍“毛泽东选集”俄文版第三卷。作者介绍了“毛泽东选集”俄文版第三卷所包括的著作的时代背景和主要内容。他说：毛泽东同志在一九三九年五月到一九四一年五月这一时期的著作中，根据马克思列宁主义的学说，对中国共产党在这个时期的政治路线、战略和策略作了理论上的分析，规定了对帝国主义和封建主义作斗争和取得胜利的道路，并全面地研究了建设新民主主义中国的问题。作者指出：“毛泽东选集”俄文版第三卷中的著作，是毛泽东同志创造性地应用马克思列宁主义到中国具体历史环境中、并把马克思列宁主义理论与中国革命实践结合起来的光辉范例。这些著作证明了马克思列宁主义思想的生命力。
