Title: Remains of Anti-Japanese Martyrs Transported Back to the Motherland; Escort Delegation and Overseas Chinese Escort Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: The third batch of 203 remains of anti-Japanese martyrs who sacrificed in Japan were transported back to China on the Japanese ship "Xing'an Maru" on the morning of the 3rd. A grand welcoming ceremony was held at Xingang Pier. Wu Yannong, Chairman of the Tianjin Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, delivered a speech. He expressed his deep condolences to the anti-Japanese martyrs and thanked the overseas Chinese in Japan and Japanese peace-loving organizations for overcoming the obstruction of the reactionary Yoshida government and sending the remains of the martyrs back to China. Akatsu Masuzo, head of the escort delegation, said in his speech that he will continue to overcome difficulties to escort the remains of anti-Japanese martyrs back to China, and expressed his respect for the Chinese people's enthusiastic assistance in helping Japanese expatriates return to China and providing relief to the Japanese flood-stricken areas. After the welcoming ceremony, the remains of the martyrs were transported by special vehicle to the Tianjin No. 1 Cemetery for temporary storage.

Xinhua News Agency Report: A total of 37 people from the "Chinese Martyrs' Remains Escort Delegation" and the "Third Batch of Returning Overseas Chinese Escort Delegation," who escorted the remains of anti-Japanese martyrs who sacrificed in Japan for the third time, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 3rd. Liao Chengzhi and others were at the station to greet them.

### 抗日烈士遗骨一批运回祖国  护送团和华侨护送代表团来京

1953-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    抗日烈士遗骨一批运回祖国
    护送团和华侨护送代表团来京
    据新华社讯：我国牺牲在日本的抗日烈士遗骨第三批二百零三具，于三日上午由日本轮船“兴安丸”运送回国。迎灵仪式在新港码头隆重举行。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会天津市分会主席吴砚农讲话。他向抗日烈士表示沉痛哀悼，并向冲破吉田反动政府阻挠、将烈士遗骨送回中国的旅日华侨及日本爱好和平团体人士致以谢意。遗骨护送团团长赤津益造在讲话中表示，将继续克服困难护送抗日烈士遗骨回国，并对中国人民热诚协助日侨回国、救济日本水灾区人民表示敬意。迎灵仪式结束后，烈士遗骨即由专车运往天津市第一公墓停放。
    据新华社讯：第三次护送我国在日本牺牲的抗日烈士遗骨的“中国人殉难者遗骨护送团”和“第三批归国华侨护送代表团”共三十七人，于三日晚到达北京。到车站迎接的有廖承志等多人。
