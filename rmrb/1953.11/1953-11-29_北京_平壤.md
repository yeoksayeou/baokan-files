Title: Beijing - Pyongyang
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1953-11-29
Page: 6

    Beijing - Pyongyang
    Tian Jian
    In the east of this world,
    Huai Ren Tang is brightly lit.
    Huai Ren Tang's myriad lights,
    Illuminate the east of the world.
    Chairman Mao and Premier Kim,
    Meet in one place.
    A pair of giant hands clasped tightly,
    Like a bridge.
    This is an immortal bridge,
    Spanning above the mighty currents.
    It is built on the Yalu River,
    And also built in our hearts.
    How many heroic soldiers,
    Come and go on the bridge;
    From Pyongyang to Beijing,
    From Beijing rushing to Pyongyang.
    Pyongyang - Beijing, Beijing - Pyongyang,
    Two places, one ideal.
    This ideal is already a giant wave,
    Surging and flowing in the east!

### 北京——平壤
田间
1953-11-29
第6版()
专栏：

    北京——平壤
    田间
    在这世界的东方，
    怀仁堂灯火辉煌。
    怀仁堂万盏灯火，
    照耀世界的东方。
    毛主席和金首相，
    相会在一个地方。
    一双巨手紧紧相握，
    好像是一座桥梁。
    这是不朽的桥梁，
    横跨在巨流之上。
    它搭在鸭绿江上，
    也搭在我们心上。
    多少英勇的战士，
    在桥上来来往往；
    从平壤来到北京，
    从北京奔向平壤。
    平壤—北京，北京—平壤，
    两个地方，一个理想。
    这理想已是巨波，
    汹涌奔流在东方！
