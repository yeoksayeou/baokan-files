Title: World Peace Council Vienna Meeting Opens
Author:
Date: 1953-11-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, November 24th: TASS reports that the World Peace Council meeting opened on November 23rd at the Palais Trautson in Vienna. In addition to the World Peace Council members, many guests from various countries attended the meeting.
    The presidium of the conference consisted of Joliot-Curie, Jean Laffite, Mao Dun, Isabelle Blume, Eugenie Cotton, Pietro Nenni, Tsuru Sukeji, Walter Friedrich, Ilya Ehrenburg, Nikolai Tikhonov… and other prominent figures in the international peace movement.
    Professor Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, first invited Brandweiner, Chairman of the Austrian Peace Council, to speak, who congratulated the meeting on behalf of Austria’s peace-loving progressive forces.
    Subsequently, Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, delivered a long speech (the text of the speech can be found on page four of today's newspaper).
    After Joliot-Curie's speech, Secretary-General of the World Peace Council, Laffite, presented the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council's suggestions regarding the conference agenda and the establishment of various committees.
    The meeting adopted the following agenda:
    (1) What actions should be taken to eliminate international differences and conflicts through universally acceptable agreements (resolving the Korean issue and the German issue, banning hydrogen bombs and all weapons of mass destruction, reducing armaments, etc.).
    (2) Suggest holding a global conference to defend peace. The meeting decided to establish three committees: (1) a Political Committee, responsible for discussing various proposals related to item one of the agenda; (2) an Organizational Committee, responsible for discussing various proposals related to item two of the agenda; (3) a Cultural Committee, responsible for discussing various cultural activities measures formulated by the World Peace Council for 1954.

### 世界和平理事会维也纳会议开幕

1953-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会维也纳会议开幕
    据新华社讯：塔斯社维也纳二十四日电：世界和平理事会会议十一月二十三日在此间特利公园大厦开幕。参加会议的除了世界和平理事会理事以外，还有各国来的许多来宾。
    大会主席团由约里奥—居里、让·拉斐德、茅盾、伊莎贝丽·布伦姆、欧仁妮·戈登、彼特罗·南尼、大山郁夫、华尔特·腓特烈、伊利亚·爱伦堡、尼古拉·吉洪诺夫……以及国际和平运动中的其它杰出人物组成。
    世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里教授首先请奥地利和平理事会主席布兰德魏纳发言，他代表奥地利的爱好和平的进步力量向会议祝贺。
    接着，世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里发表了长篇演说（演词见今日本报第四版）。
    约里奥—居里发表演说以后，世界和平理事会总书记拉斐德代表世界和平理事会常务委员会提出关于会议议程和建立各种委员会的建议。
    会议通过议程如下：
    （一）应采取怎样的行动，以便通过大家都能接受的协议，消除国际分歧和冲突（解决朝鲜问题和德国问题，禁止氢弹及一切大规模毁灭性武器，裁减军备等等）。
    （二）建议举行保卫和平的世界性大会。会议决定成立三个委员会：（一）政治委员会，负责讨论有关议程第一项的各种建议；（二）组织委员会，负责讨论有关议程第二项的各种建议；（三）文化委员会，负责讨论世界和平理事会制订的一九五四年的各种文化活动措施。
