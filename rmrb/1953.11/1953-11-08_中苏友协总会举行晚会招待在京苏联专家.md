Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Holds Evening Reception for Soviet Experts in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-11-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 7th: To celebrate the 36th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters held an evening reception for Soviet experts in Beijing at Huairen Hall on the 7th. The Central Experimental Opera House presented many wonderful programs, including "Sleeve Dance," "Guzheng Solo," "Lotus Lantern," and Ukrainian folk songs. The evening ended with a majestic rendition of the selection "The Storming of Berlin."

### 中苏友协总会举行晚会招待在京苏联专家

1953-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会举行晚会招待在京苏联专家
    【新华社七日讯】为了庆祝十月社会主义革命三十六周年，中苏友好协会总会于七日在怀仁堂举行晚会，招待在京的苏联专家。晚会由中央实验歌剧院演出“袖舞”、“古筝独奏”、“荷花灯”和乌克兰民歌等许多精彩节目。晚会最后在雄壮的“攻克柏林”选曲的歌声中结束。
