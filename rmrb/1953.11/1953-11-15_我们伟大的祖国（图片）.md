    Our Great Motherland
    Harbin is the largest city in the Songhua River basin. It has a population of 1,050,000 and is a hub connecting southern and northern Manchuria. It has now transformed from a predominantly commercial consumption city into an industrial production city. This is a corner of Harbin Wharf. A large amount of materials from various places are gathered and distributed here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    哈尔滨是松花江流域最大的一个城市。它拥有一百零五万人口，又是联系南、北满的枢纽。现在它已经由过去以商业为主的消费城市，变为工业生产的城市了。这是哈尔滨码头的一角。各地来的大量物资都在这里集散。
