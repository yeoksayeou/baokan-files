Title: Bulgarian Cultural Delegation Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1953-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: The Bulgarian cultural delegation, invited to visit our country according to the 1953 executive plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 31st, led by Marin Dozhev, Deputy Minister of Education of Bulgaria.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government; Dong Chuncai, Deputy Minister of Education; Ding Ling, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association; Cao Yu, Director of the Chinese Writers Association; Qian Sanqiang, Director of the Institute of Modern Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Zou Difan, Director of the Office of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council’s Committee of Cultural and Educational Affairs, and others.

Also present to welcome them were Petkov, the Bulgarian Ambassador to China, and embassy staff, as well as Daskalov and other members of the Bulgarian cultural delegation who had arrived in Beijing earlier.

The list of the delegation is as follows:
　　Head of the delegation: Marin Dozhev (Deputy Minister of Education of Bulgaria)
　　Members: Emil Manov (Vice Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Science, Arts and Culture, writer)
　　Asen Hadjiolov (Academician of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, histologist)
　　Veselin Petkov (Director of the Medical Education Administration of the Bulgarian Ministry of Health and Social Welfare)
　　Orlin Vassilev (Writer)
　　Nikola Lankov (Writer)
　　Hristo Daskalov (Academician of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, agricultural scientist, who had arrived in Beijing earlier)

### 保加利亚文化代表团昨天到达北京

1953-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚文化代表团昨天到达北京
    【新华社三十一日讯】根据中保文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划应邀来我国访问的保加利亚文化代表团一行六人，在团长保加利亚教育部副部长马林·多泽夫率领下，于三十一日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林，教育部副部长董纯才，中国作家协会副主席丁玲、理事曹禺，中国科学院近代物理研究所所长钱三强，外交部交际处副处长王拓，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局办公室主任邹荻帆等。
    前往欢迎的还有：保加利亚驻我国大使彼得科夫和使馆人员，以及先期到京的保加利亚文化代表团团员达斯卡洛夫等。
    代表团名单如下：团长：马林·多泽夫（保加利亚教育部副部长）团员：埃米尔·曼诺夫（保加利亚科学艺术文化委员会副主席、作家）
    阿森·哈吉奥洛夫（保加利亚科学院院士、组织学家）
    韦塞林·彼特科夫（保加利亚卫生与社会福利部医学教育管理局局长）
    奥尔林·瓦西列夫（作家）
    尼古拉·朗科夫（作家）
    赫里斯多·达斯卡洛夫（保加利亚科学院院士、农业科学家，已先期到京）
