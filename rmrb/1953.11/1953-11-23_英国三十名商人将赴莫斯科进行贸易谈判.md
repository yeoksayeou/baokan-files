Title: Thirty British Businessmen to Go to Moscow for Trade Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1953-11-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, November 18th: TASS, London, November 18th: "News Chronicle" prominently published a report from its correspondent stating that thirty British businessmen will depart for Moscow on November 22nd to conduct trade negotiations. The newspaper said that many of them are managers of important companies.

### 英国三十名商人将赴莫斯科进行贸易谈判

1953-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    英国三十名商人将赴莫斯科进行贸易谈判
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社伦敦十八日讯：“新闻纪事报”以显著地位刊载该报记者的一条消息说，有三十名英国商人将于十一月二十二日启程赴莫斯科去进行贸易谈判。该报说，他们之中有许多人是一些重要公司的经理。
