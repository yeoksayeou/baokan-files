Our Great Motherland
The state-owned Shanghai Wire Factory successfully trial-produced Soviet-style sulfur-free rubber insulated wire. This insulated wire is not only durable but also safe, and can save a significant amount of materials in manufacturing. This is a scene of workers and technicians inspecting the new product on a high-voltage test vehicle.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营上海电线厂试制苏联型无硫橡皮绝缘线成功。这种绝缘线不仅耐用而且安全，在制造中又能节省大量材料。这是工人和技术人员正在高压试验车上检验新产品的情形。
