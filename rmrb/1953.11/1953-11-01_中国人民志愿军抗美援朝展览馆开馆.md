Title: Opening of the Exhibition Hall of the Chinese People's Volunteers in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang: The Exhibition Hall of the Chinese People's Volunteers in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, sponsored by the leading organ of the Chinese People's Volunteers, opened on October 29th. General He Long, head of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, cut the ribbon at the opening ceremony.

General Yang Dezhi, Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, delivered a speech at the beginning of the ribbon-cutting ceremony. He said: The exhibition hall is to publicize the great support of the people of the motherland and the people of Korea to the Chinese People's Volunteers, and the great victory achieved by the Chinese People's Volunteers fighting side by side with the heroic Korean People's Army under this support.

Amidst the majestic battle song of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General He Long, head of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, cut the ribbon for the opening of the exhibition hall.

After the ribbon-cutting, General He Long, head of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, deputy heads of the delegation, and representatives of the general delegation and the first sub-delegation visited the exhibition hall in the company of General Yang Dezhi, Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and General Zhang Nansheng, Deputy Director of the Political Department.

### 中国人民志愿军抗美援朝展览馆开馆

1953-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军抗美援朝展览馆开馆
    【新华社平壤电】中国人民志愿军领导机关主办的中国人民志愿军抗美援朝展览馆，十月二十九日开馆，中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团长贺龙将军在开馆时剪彩。
    中国人民志愿军副司令员杨得志将军在剪彩仪式开始时致词，他说：展览馆是为了宣扬祖国人民、朝鲜人民对中国人民志愿军的巨大支援，和中国人民志愿军在这种支援下，与英雄的朝鲜人民军并肩作战所取得的伟大胜利。
    在雄壮的中国人民志愿军战歌声中，中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团长贺龙将军为展览馆的开馆剪彩。
    剪彩后，中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团长贺龙将军、各副总团长以及总团和第一总分团代表在中国人民志愿军副司令员杨得志将军、政治部副主任张南生将军陪同下进行参观。
