Title: Anti-Japanese Martyrs' Remains Escort Delegation Returns to Japan
Author:
Date: 1953-11-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: The "Chinese War Dead Remains Escort Delegation," which was the third delegation sent by Japan to escort the remains of Chinese anti-Japanese martyrs who died in Japan back to China, led by delegation head Masuzo Akatsu, deputy head Hirotsugu Kawahashi, and twelve delegation members in total, left Beijing on the afternoon of November 9th to return to Japan.

Those who came to the station to see them off included: Liao Chengzhi; Zhao Anbo, advisor to the Red Cross Society of China; Ni Peijun, deputy secretary-general; Zhang Liwen, director of the office; Lin Zhong, deputy director of the office; Tang Mingzhao, deputy director of the Liaison Department of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Guo Peng, deputy secretary-general of the Buddhist Association of China; Fang Ming, vice chairman of the National Committee of the China Educational Trade Union; and Gao Buqing, director of the office of the China Relief Administration, among others.

### 抗日烈士遗骨护送团返日

1953-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    抗日烈士遗骨护送团返日
   【新华社九日讯】第三次由日本护送我国在日本牺牲的抗日烈士遗骨回国的“中国人殉难者遗骨护送团”团长赤津益造、副团长河桥弘宗和团员共十二人，于十一月九日下午离北京回日本。
    到车站欢送的有：廖承志，中国红十字总会顾问赵安博、副秘书长倪裴君、办公室主任张立文、办公室副主任林仲，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会联络部副部长唐明照，中国佛教协会副秘书长郭朋，中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明和中国人民救济总会办公室主任高步青等多人。
