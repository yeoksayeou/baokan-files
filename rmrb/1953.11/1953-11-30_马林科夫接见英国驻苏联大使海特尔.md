Title: Malenkov Receives British Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Hayter
Author:
Date: 1953-11-30
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 29th) TASS, Moscow: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, G.M. Malenkov, received the British Ambassador to the Soviet Union, William Hayter, on the 28th.

V.M. Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, was present at the reception.

### 马林科夫接见英国驻苏联大使海特尔

1953-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    马林科夫接见英国驻苏联大使海特尔
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫于二十八日接见英国驻苏联大使威廉·海特尔。
    接见时在座的有苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫。
