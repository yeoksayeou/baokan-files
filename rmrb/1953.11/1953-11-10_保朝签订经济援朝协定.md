Title: Bulgaria and North Korea Sign Economic Aid Agreement
Author:
Date: 1953-11-10
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 9th: According to the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency, the governments of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea signed an agreement in Sofia on the 3rd regarding economic and technical assistance to Korea for the restoration of its national economy. According to this agreement, Bulgaria will supply Korea with equipment, raw materials, building materials, etc. in 1954 and 1955, and will provide technical assistance in Korea's reconstruction efforts.

### 保朝签订经济援朝协定

1953-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    保朝签订经济援朝协定
   【新华社布拉格九日电】据保加利亚通讯社消息：保加利亚人民共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府，于三日在索非亚签订了关于在经济和技术上帮助朝鲜恢复国民经济的协定。根据这个协定，保加利亚将在一九五四年到一九五五年内，供给朝鲜以设备、原料、建筑器材等，并将在朝鲜的恢复工作中给予技术上的援助。
