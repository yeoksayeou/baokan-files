Title: Regarding the Issue of the Political Conference, Bilateral Administrative Advisor Meetings Continue
Author:
Date: 1953-11-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 9th. The delegations from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China participating in the bilateral talks on the issue of the political conference issued the following communique:

　　On November 9th, the bilateral talks on the issue of the political conference continued to hold administrative advisory meetings.

### 关于政治会议问题　双方行政性顾问会议继续举行

1953-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    关于政治会议问题
    双方行政性顾问会议继续举行
    【新华社开城九日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国与中华人民共和国参加关于政治会议问题双方会谈的代表团发表公报如下：
    十一月九日关于政治会议问题的双方会谈继续举行行政性的顾问会议。
