Title: Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the North Korean Cabinet, Leads Delegation to Visit Our Country. The Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea Appoints Choi Il as Ambassador to Our Country.
Author:
Date: 1953-11-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th – The Government Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet and leader of the Korean people, left Pyongyang for Beijing at 12:00 noon on the 10th by special train, upon the invitation of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. Gan Yetao, Chargé d'Affaires of our embassy in North Korea, accompanied the delegation to Beijing.

    The delegation members include: Park Jung-ai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; Hong Myung-hui, Vice Premier of the Cabinet; Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Jung Joon-taek, Chairman of the State Planning Commission; Yoon Gong-heum, Minister of Finance; Kim Hoi-il, Minister of Railways; and Joo Hwang-seop, Minister of Urban Construction. There are also many accompanying personnel.

    At the Pyongyang station to welcome and send off the government delegation were: Kim Il, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; Choi Yong-kun, Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of National Defense; Choi Chang-ik, Jung Il-yong, and Park Eui-wan, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet; ministers of the Cabinet; senior generals of the Korean People's Army; heads of democratic parties and people's organizations; responsible persons of the Pyongyang Municipal Party and government organs; and representatives from all walks of life.

    Also present to see them off were: Soviet Ambassador to North Korea, S. Suzdalev, and diplomatic envoys from our country and other People's Democratic countries stationed in North Korea.

    General He Long, head of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, other responsible persons, and members of the art troupe also went to see them off.

    Before boarding the train, the delegation headed by Marshal Kim Il-sung warmly shook hands with those who came to see them off and accepted flowers presented by the members of our art troupe to North Korea.

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th – According to the Korean Central News Agency: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued decrees on the 5th, appointing Choi Il as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the People's Republic of China, and relieving him of his original post as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the People's Republic of Poland; and appointed Heo Kuk Bong as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of North Korea to the People's Republic of Poland.

### 朝鲜内阁首相金日成元帅  率领代表团来我国访问  朝鲜最高人民会议任命崔一为驻我国大使

1953-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜内阁首相金日成元帅
    率领代表团来我国访问
    朝鲜最高人民会议任命崔一为驻我国大使
    【新华社平壤十日电】应中华人民共和国中央人民政府的邀请访问中国的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府访问中国代表团一行，在朝鲜人民领袖内阁首相金日成元帅的率领下，已于十日中午十二时乘专车由平壤启程赴北京。我国驻朝鲜大使馆代办甘野陶陪同前往北京。
    代表团人员包括：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁副首相洪命熹，外务相南日，国家计划委员会委员长郑准泽，财政相尹公钦，铁道相金会一，都市建设相朱晃爕。另有随员多人。
    到平壤车站欢送政府代表团的有：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长金一，内阁副首相兼民族保卫相崔庸健，内阁副首相崔昌益、郑一龙、朴义琓，内阁各相，朝鲜人民军高级将领，各民主政党、人民团体与平壤市党、政机关负责人及各界代表等。
    前往欢送的还有：苏联驻朝鲜大使苏兹达列夫和我国及各人民民主国家驻朝鲜的外交使节等。
    中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团长贺龙将军和其他负责人及文艺工作团团员们也前往欢送。
    以金日成元帅为首的代表团一行在上车之前和欢送者热烈握手，并接受了我赴朝慰问团文艺工作团团员们的献花。
    【新华社平壤十日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社报道：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会五日分别发布政令，任命崔一为朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使，解除他原任的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国特命全权大使的职务；并任命许国凤为朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国特命全权大使。
