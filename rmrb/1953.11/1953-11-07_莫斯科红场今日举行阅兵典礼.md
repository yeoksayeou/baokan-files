Title: Military Parade to be Held in Moscow's Red Square Today
Author: 
Date: 1953-11-07
Page: 4

    Moscow Military Parade to be Held in Moscow's Red Square Today
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 6th. TASS Moscow, November 5th: Moscow Garrison Commander, Moscow Military District Commander, Army General Moskalenko, issued the following order regarding the review to be held in Moscow on November 7th:
    November 7, 1953, is a holiday for the working people, the 36th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. On that day, at 10:00 a.m., a review of the Moscow Garrison troops will be held in Red Square. The troops will be reviewed by the Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin. The Chief of the Review will be Army General Moskalenko.
    Salutes will be fired during the review.

### 莫斯科红场今日举行阅兵典礼

1953-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科红场今日举行阅兵典礼
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：莫斯科卫戍司令、莫斯科军区司令、陆军大将莫斯卡连科，发布关于十一月七日莫斯科举行检阅的命令如下：
    一九五三年十一月七日，是劳动人民的节日，是伟大的十月社会主义革命三十六周年纪念日。当日上午十时，红场上举行莫斯科卫戍部队的检阅。由苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅尼·亚·布尔加宁检阅部队，检阅总指挥是陆军大将莫斯卡连科。
    检阅时，将鸣放礼炮。
