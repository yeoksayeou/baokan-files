Title: The Third Group of Returned Overseas Chinese from Japan Arrives in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1953-11-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 3rd: The third group of 1,004 returned Overseas Chinese from Japan arrived in Tianjin on the afternoon of the 3rd. Over a thousand people from all walks of life in Tianjin held a welcome rally at the train station.
    Zhuang Xiquan, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, spoke at the meeting. He extended a warm welcome and cordial greetings to the Overseas Chinese who had overcome numerous difficulties and finally returned to the warm family of the motherland. He said: Under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao, the motherland, which Overseas Chinese abroad long for, is becoming increasingly prosperous and strong. The Overseas Chinese in Japan have a strong motherland to rely on, and the people of the motherland are constantly concerned about the legitimate rights and interests of the Overseas Chinese. The vast number of Overseas Chinese are also increasingly united around the motherland. Wang Xiaoyi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Tianjin Municipal People's Government, also delivered a welcoming speech at the meeting. Chen Yifang, a representative of the Overseas Chinese from Japan, gave a reply. He said: The Overseas Chinese are very excited after returning to the motherland, and the returned Overseas Chinese will actively participate in the construction of the motherland.

### 第三批回国旅日华侨到天津

1953-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    第三批回国旅日华侨到天津
    【新华社天津三日电】第三批回国的旅日华侨一千零四人已于三日下午到达天津。天津市各界人民一千多人在车站上举行了欢迎大会。
    中央人民政府华侨事务委员会副主任委员庄希泉在会上讲话。他向冲破了重重困难而终于回到祖国温暖大家庭来的侨胞们，表示热烈欢迎和亲切慰问。他说：国外华侨所向往的祖国，在伟大领袖毛主席领导下，正日益繁荣和富强。旅日侨胞有强大祖国为依靠，祖国人民时时刻刻在关怀着华侨的正当权益，广大华侨也日益团结在祖国周围。天津市人民政府副秘书长王笑一也在会上致词欢迎。旅日华侨代表陈义方致答词。他说：华侨回到祖国后十分兴奋，回国华侨一定积极参加祖国建设事业。
