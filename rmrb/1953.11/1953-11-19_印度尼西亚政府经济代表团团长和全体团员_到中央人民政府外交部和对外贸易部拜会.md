Title: Indonesian Government Economic Delegation Leader and All Members Visit the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1953-11-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The head of the Indonesian Government Economic Delegation, Asmawin, and all members, accompanied by the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Mononutu, and Counselor Isa Matari, visited Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Ye Jizhuang, Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, on the morning of the 17th.
    When Deputy Minister Zhang Hanfu received the delegation, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, were present.
    When Minister Ye Jizhuang received the delegation, Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau, Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department, and Li Yingji, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, were present.
    On the evening of the 17th, Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, held a banquet to welcome the Indonesian Government Economic Delegation.
    The head of the Indonesian Government Economic Delegation, Asmawin, and all members attended the banquet.
    Also present at the banquet were Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu, Counselor Isa Matari, and many members of the embassy.
    On the Chinese side, those present included: Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Li Yingji, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation.

### 印度尼西亚政府经济代表团团长和全体团员  到中央人民政府外交部和对外贸易部拜会

1953-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府经济代表团团长和全体团员
    到中央人民政府外交部和对外贸易部拜会
   【新华社十八日讯】印度尼西亚政府经济代表团团长阿斯玛温及全体团员在印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图、参赞伊沙·玛蒂陪同下于十七日上午先后拜会了我中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫和对外贸易部部长叶季壮。
    章汉夫副部长接见该代表团时在座的有：外交部亚洲司司长陈家康，交际处副处长王拓。
    叶季壮部长接见该代表团时在座的有：对外贸易部副部长雷任民，第三局局长卢绪章，交际处处长杨勉及中国进出口公司副经理李应吉。
    十七日晚，对外贸易部副部长雷任民举行宴会，欢迎印度尼西亚政府经济代表团。
    印度尼西亚政府经济代表团团长阿斯玛温及全体团员出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有：印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图、参赞伊沙·玛蒂及使馆馆员多人。
    中国方面出席作陪的有：对外贸易部第三局局长卢绪章，外交部亚洲司副司长何英，对外贸易部交际处处长杨勉，外交部交际处副处长王拓，中国进出口公司副经理李应吉等人。
