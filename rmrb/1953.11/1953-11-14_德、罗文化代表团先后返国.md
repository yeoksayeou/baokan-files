Title: German and Romanian Cultural Delegations Return Home
Author:
Date: 1953-11-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: The Scientific and Cultural Exploration Delegation from the German Democratic Republic, having visited various locations in China at our invitation, returned to Beijing on the 8th. Six members, including F. Erpenbeck, left Beijing by plane on the 11th to return to their country.

Those seeing them off at the airport included: Zhang Zhaoqian, Deputy Director of the Institute of Botany of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Ouyang Shanzun, Director of the Beijing People's Art Theater; Wu Tong, Section Chief of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee; and Hua Junwu and Wang Ganchang from the capital's scientific and artistic circles.

Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 10th to bid farewell to all representatives of the Scientific and Cultural Exploration Delegation from the German Democratic Republic.

Xinhua News Agency, November 12th: The Romanian Cultural Delegation, invited to China to attend the National Day celebrations and visit, returned to Beijing on the 8th after visiting Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Xiangtan, and Wuhan. On the 12th, the delegation, led by its leader, Francha, left Beijing by plane to return to their country.

Those seeing them off at the airport included: Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and many representatives from the capital's cultural and artistic circles, including Yang Shuo, Zhang Wengang, Huang Zhenxiang, and Zou Difan.

Also present to see them off were: Guo Zuowen, the Romanian Ambassador to China, and embassy staff.

Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 10th to bid farewell to all representatives of the Romanian Cultural Delegation.

### 德、罗文化代表团先后返国

1953-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    德、罗文化代表团先后返国
    【新华社十一日讯】应邀来我国访问的德意志民主共和国科学文化考察团在各地访问完毕后，已于八日回到北京。团员弗·艾尔彭白克等六人于十一日乘飞机离京返国。
    前往机场送行的有：中国科学院植物研究所副所长张肇骞，北京人民艺术剧院院长欧阳山尊，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局科长吴佟，和首都科学艺术界华君武、王淦昌等。
    我中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，曾于十日晚设宴欢送德意志民主共和国科学文化考察团的全体代表。
    【新华社十二日讯】应邀到我国参加国庆节观礼和访问的罗马尼亚文化代表团，在天津、南京、上海、杭州、广州、湘潭、武汉等地访问后，已于八日回到北京。十二日，代表团在团长弗朗查率领下，乘飞机离京返国。
    前往机场欢送的有：中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，对外文化联络事务局副局长陈忠经，外交部交际处副处长王拓，以及首都文化艺术界代表杨朔、张文纲、黄祯祥、邹荻帆等多人。
    前往欢送的还有：罗马尼亚驻华大使郭佐文和使馆人员。
    我中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，曾于十日晚设宴欢送罗马尼亚文化代表团的全体代表。
