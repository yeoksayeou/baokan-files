Title: Volunteer Army Observation Delegation Returns to North Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 10th. The Chinese People's Volunteer Army National Day observation delegation, having completed its visit and reports in Shenyang, returned to North Korea on the 10th. After returning to Shenyang from Lushun-Dalian on the 1st, the delegation successively visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall, the May 3rd Factory, and the Gaokan Village Agricultural Production Cooperative, among other places, and went to Fushun to visit the Open-Pit Mine, the Longfeng Mine, and the Petroleum Factory. The delegation also reported on their heroic and exemplary deeds to approximately 70,000 people from all walks of life in Shenyang.

### 志愿军观礼代表团返回朝鲜

1953-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

     志愿军观礼代表团返回朝鲜
    【新华社沈阳十日电】中国人民志愿军国庆节归国观礼代表团，结束了在沈阳的参观与报告，于十日返回朝鲜，代表团自一日由旅大返回沈阳后，先后参观了东北工业陈列馆、五三工厂、高坎村农业生产合作社等处，并去抚顺参观了露天矿、龙凤矿和石油厂。代表团还向沈阳市各界人士约七万人，报告了他们的英雄模范事迹。
