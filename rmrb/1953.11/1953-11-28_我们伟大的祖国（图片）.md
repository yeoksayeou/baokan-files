    Our Great Motherland
    This year's key electric power basic construction projects in our country—the new power plants in Xi'an, Zhengzhou, and Dihua—are being built quickly and well with the help of Soviet experts. This is the scene of Soviet steam turbine expert Gidarenko personally guiding workers in installing machinery at the Zhengzhou Power Plant.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国今年电业基本建设重点工程——西安、郑州、迪化三座新发电厂，在苏联专家帮助下，建设得又快又好。这是苏联汽机专家吉大伦科正在亲手指导工人安装郑州发电厂机器的情形。
