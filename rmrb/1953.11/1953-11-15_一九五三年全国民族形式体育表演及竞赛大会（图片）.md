Title: National Ethnic Sports Performance and Competition Assembly, 1953 (Photos)
Author: Li Yu, Zhang Li
Date: 1953-11-15
Page: 5

    National Ethnic Sports Performance and Competition Assembly, 1953
    PLA athlete Zhu Hongquan breaks the national record in middleweight weightlifting (305 kg) and wins the championship.
    PLA athletes perform equestrian obstacle course competition - "Jumping through the fire circle".
    Light riding performance competition by athletes from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.
    Song Fulu, an athlete from North China, performs stone lock competition (result: 280 Jin).
    Ma Ruilan, an athlete from North China, performs archery competition.
    Korean female athletes from Northeast China, Li Yishun and others, perform folk sport "seesaw."
    Dai athletes from Southwest China, Diao Zhengzhong and Diao Liangyou, perform folk sport "Elephant Foot Drum Dance."
    Jin Shoukang and other five athletes from East China perform folk sport "Tiao Diao".
    The National Ethnic Sports Performance and Competition Assembly of 1953 grandly opened on the morning of the 8th of this month and successfully closed on the afternoon of the 12th at the Tianjin No. 2 People's Stadium. Over 120,000 people from all walks of life in Tianjin visited. This is the scene of the audience applauding enthusiastically when they saw the wonderful performances of the athletes.
    Gao Jinrong, an athlete from the China Railway Sports Association, performs folk sport "pellet shooting."
    Siti Ke, a Uygur athlete from Northwest China, performs folk sport "rope walking."
    Special invitation to athletes from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region to perform wrestling.
    Special invitation performance: Li Wenzhen sword dance.
    Lan Suzhen, an athlete from Southwest China, performs "Mianquan."
    Hao Xueru, an athlete from North China, performs "Large Iron Shovel."
    Song Bingkun (11 years old), an athlete from North China, performs "Liuhequan."
    Wang Jurong and Gao Junzhu, athletes from East China, perform "Dual Swords."
    Liu Yuhua, an athlete from Central South China, performs "Chaquan."
    Zhang Wancheng, an athlete from Northeast China, performs "Sunflower Spear."
    The athletes participating in the conference exchanged experiences with each other during breaks. This is Zhang Wancheng, an athlete from Northeast China, explaining the movements of the lotus leaf knife.
　　This issue's pictorial photography: Our reporter Li Yu, Zhang Li

### 一九五三年全国民族形式体育表演及竞赛大会（图片）
李棫  张力
1953-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    一九五三年全国民族形式体育表演及竞赛大会
    解放军选手朱鸿全中量级举重（三百零五公斤）打破全国纪录，获得冠军。
    解放军运动员乘马障碍超越表演竞赛——“钻火圈”。
    内蒙古自治区运动员轻乘表演竞赛。
    华北区选手宋福禄石锁竞赛表演（成绩二百八十市斤）。
    华北区选手马瑞兰步射竞赛表演。
    东北区朝鲜族女运动员李乙顺等四人表演民间体育“跳板”。
    西南区傣族运动员刁正中和刁良友表演民间体育“象脚鼓舞”。
    华东区运动员金寿康等五人表演民间体育“调吊”。
    一九五三年全国民族形式体育表演及竞赛大会，于本月八日上午在天津市第二人民体育场隆重开幕，十二日下午胜利闭幕。前往参观的天津市各界人民共十二万余人。这是观众看到运动员表演精彩节目时，热烈鼓掌的情形。
    中国火车头体育协会运动员高金荣表演民间体育“弹丸”。
    西北区维吾尔族运动员司梯克表演民间体育“踩绳”。
    特邀内蒙古自治区运动员表演摔跤。
    特邀表演：李文贞舞剑。
    西南区运动员蓝素贞表演“绵拳”。
    华北区运动员郝学儒表演“大铁锹”。
    华北区运动员松秉堃（十一岁）表演“六合拳”。
    华东区运动员王菊蓉和高君珠表演“对剑”。
    中南区运动员刘玉华表演“查拳”。
    东北区运动员张万成表演“葵花枪”。
    参加大会的运动员们在休息时间相互交流经验。这是东北区运动员张万成讲解荷叶刀的动作。
