Title: Kashmir Premier Opposes Turning Kashmir into an American Base
Author:
Date: 1953-11-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 24th. While public opinion in Pakistan, India, and Kashmir is expressing deep concern about the proposed US-Pakistan military alliance, Kashmir Premier Bakshi Ghulam Mohammad, in a speech to cadres of the Jammu and Kashmir National Conference Party in Handwara (Kashmir) on the 16th, said that the people of Kashmir would never tolerate the entire state of Kashmir, especially Gilgit, being turned into a war base by imperialist countries. He said that the people of the state of Kashmir, who sacrificed their excellent sons and daughters to obtain freedom, will never allow Kashmir to be turned into a war base by those countries that are threatening the freedom and unity of Asia.

### 克什米尔总理反对把克什米尔变为美国基地

1953-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    克什米尔总理反对把克什米尔变为美国基地
    【新华社新德里二十四日电】在巴基斯坦、印度和克什米尔的公众舆论对于拟议中的美、巴军事同盟表示深切忧虑的同时，克什米尔总理巴克希·吴拉姆·穆罕默德于十六日在汉德瓦拉（克什米尔）对查谟与克什米尔国民会议党干部发表的演说里说：克什米尔人民绝不能容忍整个克什米尔邦尤其是吉尔吉特被帝国主义国家变成战争基地。他说，为了获得自由而牺牲了优秀子弟的克什米尔邦的人民，绝不容许克什米尔被那些正在威胁亚洲的自由与团结的国家变成战争基地。
