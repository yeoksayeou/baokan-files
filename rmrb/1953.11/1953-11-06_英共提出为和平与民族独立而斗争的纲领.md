Title: CPGB Proposes Program for Struggle for Peace and National Independence
Author:
Date: 1953-11-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, November 3rd: TASS reports from London that a mass meeting was held in Liverpool, England, where Harry Pollitt, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, delivered a speech. Pollitt put forward a program for how the Communist Party of Great Britain should unite all British working people to fight for peace and national independence. According to the "Daily Worker" newspaper, Pollitt put forward this program as the basis for uniting all trade union movement participants, all peace advocates, and all true patriots.

The program calls for: Struggle for higher wages, unemployment benefits, and pensions; struggle for lower prices; struggle for the construction of housing, schools, and improved healthcare; reject increases in rent and taxes; struggle for the abolition of American military occupation of Britain; struggle for the abolition of restrictions on British trade with foreign countries; express solidarity with the people of Malaya, Kenya, British Guiana, and all other colonial countries; struggle for Britain's independence from the United States, for the independence and freedom of all countries in the British Empire; and struggle for universal disarmament.

### 英共提出为和平与民族独立而斗争的纲领

1953-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    英共提出为和平与民族独立而斗争的纲领
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社伦敦三日讯：英国利物浦举行了一个群众集会，英国共产党总书记哈里·波立特在会上发表了演说。波立特就英国共产党如何团结英国全体劳动人民一致为和平与民族独立而斗争提出了一项纲领。据“工人日报”报道，波立特提出这个纲领作为团结所有工会运动参加者、所有和平拥护者和一切真正爱国人士的基础。
    纲领号召：为争取提高工资、失业补助金和养老金而斗争；为减低物价而斗争；为争取建筑住宅、学校和改进保健工作而斗争；拒绝提高房租和赋税；为取消美国对英国的军事占领而斗争；为取消对英国同外国进行贸易的限制而斗争；声援马来亚、怯尼亚、英属圭亚那和其他所有殖民地国家的人民；为英国摆脱美国而独立、为英帝国各国的独立和自由而斗争；为普遍裁军而斗争。
