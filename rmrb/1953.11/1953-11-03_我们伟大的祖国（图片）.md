Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1953-11-03
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The seamless steel tube plant project of Anshan Iron and Steel Company has been successfully completed ahead of schedule and was fully handed over to the production department on October 31. This plant was designed with the help of a large number of Soviet experts using advanced technology and built with first-class machinery and equipment. This is a scene of Soviet experts observing the operation of the piercing mill on the operating table of the seamless steel tube plant.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍山钢铁公司无缝钢管厂工程已胜利提前完工，并于十月三十一日全部移交生产部门。这座厂是由苏联派了大批专家，以先进的技术帮助设计，并且以头等的机械设备来建设成功的。这是苏联专家在无缝钢管厂操作台上观察穿孔机运转的情形。
