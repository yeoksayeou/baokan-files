Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1953-11-02
Page: 1

Our great motherland
The construction work for this year on the Qinghai-Tibet Highway was successfully completed in early October. Now, the road construction troops are actively launching winter construction. This is the road construction troops starting new labor on a new construction site. This winter, they will extend the construction line to the area south of the Bayan Har Mountains.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    青藏公路今年度的修筑工程，已在十月上旬胜利完成。现在，筑路部队正积极展开冬季施工。这是筑路部队在新工地上开始了新的劳动。他们将在今年冬季把施工线伸延到巴颜喀喇山以南的地方。
