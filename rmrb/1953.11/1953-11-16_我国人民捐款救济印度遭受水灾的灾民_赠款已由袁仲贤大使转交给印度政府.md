Title: Our People Donate Funds to Aid Indian Flood Victims; Grant Handed Over to Indian Government by Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian

Author:

Date: 1953-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 15th. Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, on behalf of the Chinese Red Cross Society and the China People's Relief Administration, presented a check for 287,356 rupees (equivalent to 1.5 billion RMB) to Indian Prime Minister Nehru in New Delhi on the 11th, as part of the Indian Prime Minister's National Relief Fund.

Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian also handed over letters from the Chinese Red Cross Society and the China People's Relief Administration to Prime Minister Nehru. These letters expressed the deep concern of the Chinese people for the severe floods in Bihar Province and the Godavari River region, and stated that they were very happy to donate this sum of money. The letters also requested that the Chinese people's deep sympathy and sincere concern be conveyed to the flood victims.

In response to this donation, Prime Minister Nehru wrote a letter to Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian, stating: "I hope you can convey my gratitude to the Chinese Red Cross Society and the China People's Relief Administration for this generous donation, which will be greatly appreciated by the people concerned. While forwarding the donation, I would also like to convey the Chinese people's sympathy for their suffering." Prime Minister Nehru concluded: "Floods have also occurred in nearby areas and some other regions. I believe that your generous donation can also be used for all flood-affected areas."

### 我国人民捐款救济印度遭受水灾的灾民  赠款已由袁仲贤大使转交给印度政府

1953-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国人民捐款救济印度遭受水灾的灾民
    赠款已由袁仲贤大使转交给印度政府
    【新华社新德里十五日电】中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤于十一日在新德里代表中国红十字会和中国人民救济总会，以一张二十八万七千三百五十六卢比（合十五亿元人民币）的支票赠给印度总理尼赫鲁，作为印度总理的国家救济基金。
    袁仲贤大使同时将中国红十字会和中国人民救济总会的信件交给尼赫鲁总理。这些信中表达了中国人民对于比哈尔省和哥达维利河地区的严重水灾的深切关怀，并表示他们非常乐于捐赠这笔款项。信中还要求向遭受水灾的灾民，转达中国人民深切的同情和真挚的关怀。
    尼赫鲁总理为答谢这次捐赠，写了一封信给袁仲贤大使，信中说：“希望你能向中国红十字会和中国人民救济总会转达我对这次慷慨捐赠的谢意，有关的人民将非常感激这种捐赠。在转送赠款的同时，我也要向他们转达中国人民对他们所受灾难的同情。”尼赫鲁总理最后写道：“该区附近地区和其他一些地区也发生了水灾。我相信你们慷慨的赠款，也可以用于所有遭受水灾的地区。”
