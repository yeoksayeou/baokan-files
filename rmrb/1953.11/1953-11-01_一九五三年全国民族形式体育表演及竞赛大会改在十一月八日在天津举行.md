Title: National Ethnic Sports Performance and Competition Conference of 1953 Rescheduled to November 8th in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1953-11-01
Page: 3

Cultural Briefs

    The All-China Sports Federation has rescheduled the "National Ethnic Sports Performance and Competition Conference of 1953," originally scheduled to be held in Beijing from November 4th to 8th, to be held in Tianjin from November 8th to 12th. The conference preparatory committee has been officially established in Tianjin, with Wu De, the mayor of the Tianjin Municipal People's Government, as the chairman. The preparatory committee is actively carrying out various preparatory tasks. The North China and Southwest China delegations participating in the conference have already arrived in Tianjin, and delegations from other units will arrive in Tianjin for registration successively.

### 一九五三年全国民族形式体育表演及竞赛大会改在十一月八日在天津举行

1953-11-01
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    一九五三年全国民族形式体育表演及竞赛大会改在十一月八日在天津举行
    中华全国体育总会原定十一月四日到八日在北京举行的“一九五三年全国民族形式体育表演及竞赛大会”，已改在十一月八日到十二日在天津举行。大会筹备委员会已在天津正式成立，由天津市人民政府市长吴德任主任委员。筹备委员会正积极进行各项筹备工作。参加大会的华北区、西南区代表队都已到达天津，其他各单位代表队将陆续前往天津报到。
