Title: Indian and Pakistani Newspapers Publish Confessions of American Air Force POWs Regarding Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1953-11-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 29th: The latest issue of the "New Age" weekly published a summary of the confessions of nineteen American Air Force personnel regarding germ warfare under the title "America Dropped Germ Bombs in Korea; Here is New Evidence; Nineteen Airmen Accuse the US Government." These included the confessions of Captain John Osborne, Lieutenant Colonel Andre Evans Jr., Lieutenant Colonel Walker Mahurin, and jet pilot Chalice Eugene Stoll.

Prior to this, the "Pakistan Times" on November 18th published confessions of Lieutenant Roger Wallen, Lieutenant Winston Sommerson, and Lieutenant William Fownes under the title "Experiences of Witnesses to Germ Bomb Attacks; Confessions of Three American Pilots." They confessed the details of conducting germ warfare and provided a large amount of corroborating material. The report pointed out that they were captured at different times, and they described in detail the tactical development of American germ warfare. From this, one can see the decline of the germ warfare plan.

On November 23rd, the "Pakistan Times" continued to discuss and quote the germ warfare confessions of three American Air Force colonels.

### 印、巴报刊登载美国空军战俘的细菌战供词

1953-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    印、巴报刊登载美国空军战俘的细菌战供词
    【新华社新德里二十九日电】最近一期的“新世纪”周报以“美国在朝鲜投过细菌弹；下面是新的证据；十九名飞行人员指控美国政府”为题，发表了十九名美国空军人员关于细菌战的供词的摘要，其中包括约翰·奥斯本上尉、小安德烈·爱文斯上校、瓦克·马胡林上校和喷气机驾驶员却利斯·尤金·史托尔的供词。
    在此以前，“巴基斯坦时报”在十一月十八日曾以“细菌弹袭击的见证人的经历；三个美国驾驶员的供词”为题，报道了罗吉·华伦中尉、温斯顿·萨梦逊中尉和威廉·福恩斯中尉的供词。他们供述了进行细菌战的经过，提供了大量可以互相印证的材料。报道指出，他们在不同的时候被俘，他们详细叙述了美国细菌战的战术发展。从这里面可以看出，细菌战计划的每况愈下。
    十一月二十三日，“巴基斯坦时报”继续谈到并引用了三个美国空军上校的细菌战供词。
