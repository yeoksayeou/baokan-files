    Our Great Motherland
    After the autumn harvest, grain departments in various regions are relying on cooperatives to do a good job in regulation, transportation, and supply, in order to ensure the grain needs of cities, industrial and mining areas, and economic crop areas. This is Hankou, the largest grain distribution center in Hubei Province. Porters are transporting grain here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    秋收以后，各地粮食部门正依靠合作社做好调剂、运输和供应工作，以保证城市、工矿、经济作物地区的粮食需要。这是湖北省最大的粮食集散地——汉口。搬运工人正在这里运送粮食。
