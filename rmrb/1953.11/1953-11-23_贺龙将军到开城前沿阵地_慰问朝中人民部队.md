Title: General He Long Visits the Kaesong Frontline to Console Korean and Chinese People's Forces
Author:
Date: 1953-11-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, November 22nd: General He Long, head of the Chinese People's Third Delegation to Korea, along with deputy heads Xing Xiping, Zhu Xuefan, Chen Yi, Wu Han, Liu Zhiming, Kang Keqing, Mei Lanfang, Lao She, Wang Weizhou, Zhou Xinfang, and more than twenty others, visited and consoled the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers who were guarding the Kaesong frontline on the morning of the 19th.

　　When General He Long and others ascended the mountain, the soldiers came out of their positions to greet them. After General He Long and others shook hands with the soldiers to express their regards, the soldiers led them to inspect the positions and recounted the battles between our army and the enemy during the war.

　　The soldiers guarding the positions felt very honored and excited by the care shown by the people of the motherland and by General He Long and others personally visiting the frontline to console them and inspect their positions. They unanimously stated: We will resolutely implement the armistice agreement, vigilantly guard our positions, and fight to the end for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue.

### 贺龙将军到开城前沿阵地  慰问朝中人民部队

1953-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙将军到开城前沿阵地
    慰问朝中人民部队
    【新华社开城二十二日电】中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团长贺龙将军，副总团长邢西萍、朱学范、陈沂、吴晗、刘芝明、康克清、梅兰芳、老舍、王维舟、周信芳等一行二十多人，于十九日上午慰问了守卫在开城前沿阵地上的朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军。
    当贺龙将军等登上山头时，战士们都从阵地里出来迎接。贺龙将军等与战士们一一握手慰问后，战士们即带领他们参观阵地，并讲述了战争期间我军与敌军战斗的情形。
    守卫在阵地上的战士们，对于祖国人民对他们的关怀和贺龙将军等亲自到前沿阵地来慰问他们，参观他们的阵地，感到十分光荣和兴奋。他们一致表示：坚决执行停战协定，警惕地守卫阵地，为和平解决朝鲜问题奋斗到底。
