Title: Unite All Ethnic Groups in Burma to Oppose Imperialism: Establishment of the Burmese National United Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-11-21
Page: 4

    Unite All Ethnic Groups in Burma to Oppose Imperialism
    Establishment of the Burmese National United Committee

    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th dispatch from Yangon] News from Yangon: The Burmese National United Committee, with one of its main objectives being the unity of all ethnic groups in Burma, was established in Yangon on November 10th. Thakin Kodaw Hmaing was elected as chairman. On the 12th, the Burmese National United Committee issued a statement condemning the conspiracies of American imperialism to divide the various ethnic groups in Burma and its various acts of aggression against Burma. The statement called on all ethnic groups in Burma to unite and resist the external crisis of aggression.

### 团结全缅各民族反对帝国主义  缅甸民族联合委员会成立

1953-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    团结全缅各民族反对帝国主义
    缅甸民族联合委员会成立
    【新华社二十日讯】仰光消息：以团结缅甸各民族为主要目的之一的缅甸民族联合委员会于十一月十日在仰光成立，德钦哥都迈被选为主席。十二日，缅甸民族联合委员会发表文告，谴责美帝国主义分化缅甸各民族的阴谋和对缅甸的各种侵略活动。文告号召缅甸各民族团结起来，抵御外来的侵略危机。
