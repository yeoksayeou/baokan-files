Title: Polish Peace Defense Committee and other groups pass resolution to fully support Soviet proposals to consolidate peace
Author:
Date: 1953-11-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 14th. TASS reported from Warsaw on the 14th: The Presidium of the Polish Peace Defense Committee and the Committee for Peaceful Resolution of the German Question held an enlarged meeting in Warsaw. The meeting adopted a resolution supporting the efforts of the world's people to resolve international issues, especially the German question, through consultation, in order to consolidate peace.

　　The resolution said that the people of all countries hope to see the unification of Germany on a democratic and peaceful basis in accordance with the provisions of the Potsdam Agreement. The people of all countries warmly support the Soviet Union's continuous efforts to resolve the German question and believe that the constructive proposals put forward by the Soviet Union are a way to consolidate peace in Europe.

　　The Polish people sincerely support the Soviet Union's peace policy, which is closely related to Poland's security and independence.

　　The Polish people are linked with the German Democratic Republic in friendship and brotherly cooperation, and they express their full support for all peace-loving democratic forces in Germany who are fighting for the unification of their country, for democracy and for peace.

### 波兰保卫和平委员会等团体通过决议  全力支持苏联巩固和平的建议

1953-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰保卫和平委员会等团体通过决议
    全力支持苏联巩固和平的建议
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社华沙十四日讯：波兰保卫和平委员会主席团与争取和平解决德国问题委员会在华沙举行了扩大会议。会议通过一项决议，支持世界人民争取以协商方式解决国际问题，尤其是德国问题，以巩固和平的努力。
    决议说，各国人民希望看到按照波茨坦协定的规定，在民主与和平的基础上统一德国。各国人民热烈拥护苏联不断努力争取解决德国问题，并认为苏联所提出的建设性的建议是巩固欧洲和平的途径。
    波兰人民热诚拥护苏联的和平政策，这种政策与波兰的安全与独立是密切相关的。
    波兰人民与德意志民主共和国在友谊与兄弟般的合作中联结在一起，他们表示全力支持正在为祖国的统一、为民主与和平而斗争的全德爱好和平的民主力量。
