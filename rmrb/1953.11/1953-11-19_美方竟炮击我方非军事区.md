Title: The U.S. Side Actually Shelled Our Demilitarized Zone
Author:
Date: 1953-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 18th -- Chief Korean-Chinese side member of the Military Armistice Commission, General Li Sang-cho, today raised the issue of the U.S. side shelling the area near Guihu Cave, southeast of Shuoning, within our demilitarized zone north of the military demarcation line, in violation of the armistice agreement, and requested that the Fourth Joint Observer Team be dispatched for investigation. The U.S. side's chief member, Lacey, expressed his agreement. The two sides' secretaries-general met after the Military Armistice Commission meeting and decided that the Fourth Joint Observer Team would meet at 2:00 PM on the 19th.

### 美方竟炮击我方非军事区

1953-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    美方竟炮击我方非军事区
   【新华社开城十八日电】军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员李相朝中将，今天提出美方炮击军事分界线以北我方非军事区内朔宁东南的桂湖洞附近地区的违反停战协定事件，要求派遣第四联合观察小组前往调查。美方首席委员拉西表示同意。双方秘书长在军事停战委员会会议以后开会，决定第四联合观察小组在十九日下午二时会晤。
