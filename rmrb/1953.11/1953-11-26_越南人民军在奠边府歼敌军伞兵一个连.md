Title: Vietnamese People's Army Annihilates an Enemy Paratrooper Company in Dien Bien Phu
Author:
Date: 1953-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th: According to a Vietnam News Agency report on the 24th, the Vietnamese People's Army annihilated an enemy paratrooper company in Dien Bien Phu and actively fought in the provinces of Nam Dinh in North Vietnam and in various parts of Central Vietnam, achieving new victories.

According to preliminary reports on the 23rd, the Vietnamese People's Army annihilated an enemy company in the first engagement with enemy paratroopers airdropped in Dien Bien Phu.

Another report stated: On November 16th, the Vietnamese People's Army launched an attack on an enemy battalion twelve kilometers southwest of Nam Dinh city in North Vietnam, annihilating more than one enemy company, capturing 75 prisoners, and seizing 79 machine guns and rifles. Prior to this, on the 11th, local forces of the People's Army in Nam Dinh Province launched a counter-sweep operation in the southern part of Bui Chu area, annihilating an enemy platoon.

At the same time, at the beginning of this month, the Vietnamese People's Army countered the enemy's "sweep" operations in Trieu Phong of Quang Tri Province and Quang Dien of Thua Thien Province in Central Vietnam, achieving victories and annihilating two enemy platoons. The People's Army also killed more than 100 enemy soldiers while fighting in Khanh Hoa Province.

### 越南人民军在奠边府歼敌军伞兵一个连

1953-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军在奠边府歼敌军伞兵一个连
    【新华社二十五日讯】据越南通讯社二十四日讯：越南人民军在奠边府歼灭敌军伞兵部队一个连，并在北越南定省和中越各地积极作战，取得新的胜利。
    据二十三日初步消息说，越南人民军在第一次和空投在奠边府的敌军伞兵部队交战时，歼灭敌军一个连。
    另一消息说：十一月十六日，越南人民军在北越南定省省会西南十二公里处向敌军的一个营发动进攻，歼灭敌军一个多连，俘掳七十五人，缴获机枪、步枪七十九挺（支）。在这以前，十一日南定省人民军地方部队在裴朱南部地区发动反扫荡战，歼灭了敌军一排。
    同时，越南人民军在本月初对敌军在中越广治省潮丰和承天省广甸的“扫荡战”进行反击，取得胜利，歼灭敌军两排。人民军并曾在庆和省作战时击毙敌军一百多名。
