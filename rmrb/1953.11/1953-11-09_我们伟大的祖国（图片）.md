Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-11-09

    Our Great Motherland
    A large number of outstanding talents have emerged in the construction of Tanggu New Port. Their selfless labor will make Tanggu New Port a beautiful garden. This is Pei Wenxian, the first female driver of a dredger. She is driving a "suction" dredger on the ocean.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在塘沽新港的建设中涌现了大批优秀的人材。他们的忘我劳动将使塘沽新港成为一座美丽的花园。这是第一个驾驶挖泥船的女驾驶员——裴文献。她正在海洋上驾驶着“吸扬式”的挖泥船。
