Title: Sino-Albanian Sign Protocol on Scientific and Technological Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1953-11-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. "Pravda" of the Soviet Union reported on the 9th that the Soviet-Albanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee recently held a meeting in Moscow and signed a protocol on scientific and technological cooperation. According to the protocol, the Soviet Union will provide the People's Republic of Albania with various kinds of scientific and technological assistance, including: supplying Albania with design data for the establishment of medical research institutes, technical process specifications for the production of certain light industry and food industry products, and working rules for the production of asphalt, management of power stations, and railway transportation. The Soviet Union will also provide teaching plans, teaching outlines, and textbooks for Albania to train cadres in the timber industry, wood processing industry, light industry, and food industry. In addition, the Soviet Union will send experts to Albania to provide on-site technical assistance in organizing the production of building materials and managing automobile transportation, and to impart the Soviet Union's experience in cultivating citrus fruits to Albania. The Soviet Union will help Albanian experts understand the advanced experience in the work of certain agricultural departments in the Soviet Union, and introduce to Albanian experts the work of scientific research departments in the Soviet oil industry.
    According to the principle of scientific and technological cooperation between the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries, the Soviet Union's scientific and technological assistance to Albania is free of charge.

### 苏阿签订科学技术合作议定书

1953-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏阿签订科学技术合作议定书
   【新华社十八日讯】苏联“真理报”九日报道，苏阿（阿尔巴尼亚）科学技术合作委员会最近在莫斯科举行了会议，并签订了科学技术合作议定书。根据议定书，苏联给予阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国以各种科学技术上的帮助，办法是：供给阿尔巴尼亚以成立医学研究所的设计资料，生产轻工业和食品工业某些产品的技术过程说明书，生产沥青、管理电力站和铁道运输等的工作细则。苏联还为阿尔巴尼亚培养木材业、木材加工业、轻工业和食品工业的干部提供教学计划、教学大纲和教科书。此外，苏联将派专家到阿尔巴尼亚，在组织建筑材料的生产、管理汽车运输方面给予现场的技术帮助，把苏联在培育橘类水果方面的经验传授给阿尔巴尼亚。苏联将帮助阿尔巴尼亚专家了解苏联某些农业部门工作中的先进经验，并向阿尔巴尼亚专家介绍苏联石油工业中科学研究部门的工作情况。
    根据苏联和各人民民主国家进行科学技术合作的原则，苏联对阿尔巴尼亚的科学技术帮助都是无偿的。
