Title: On the Question of the Political Conference, Both Sides' Advisors Continue Administrative Meetings
Author:
Date: 1953-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 10th. The delegations from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China participating in the talks on the question of the Political Conference issued the following communiqué:

    On November 10th, the talks on the question of the Political Conference continued with administrative meetings of advisors from both sides.

### 关于政治会议问题  双方顾问继续开行政性

1953-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    关于政治会议问题
    双方顾问继续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国与中华人民共和国参加关于政治会议问题双方会谈的代表团发表公报如下：
    十一月十日关于政治会议问题的双方会谈继续举行行政性的顾问会议。
