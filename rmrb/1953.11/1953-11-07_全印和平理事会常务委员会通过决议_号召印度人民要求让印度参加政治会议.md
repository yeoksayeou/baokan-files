Title: Resolution Passed by the Standing Committee of the All-India Peace Council Calls on the Indian People to Demand India's Participation in Political Conferences
Author:
Date: 1953-11-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 6th: The Secretariat of the World Peace Council issued a communique stating that the Standing Committee of the All-India Peace Council passed a resolution at its recent meeting, calling on the Indian people to "unanimously demand that India participate in political conferences and to ensure that the affairs of the Asian people are not decided without the participation of the Asian people."

The resolution stated: "For the sake of world peace, India should fully support all efforts genuinely aimed at resolving international disputes through consultation, and should constantly denounce and expose those forces that are now trying to delay and obstruct peaceful negotiations between the great powers." The resolution also opposed the use of Indian territory or resources for war and demanded the prohibition of weapons of mass destruction.

### 全印和平理事会常务委员会通过决议  号召印度人民要求让印度参加政治会议

1953-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    全印和平理事会常务委员会通过决议
    号召印度人民要求让印度参加政治会议
    【新华社布拉格六日电】世界和平理事会书记处发表公报说：全印和平理事会常务委员会在最近举行的会议中通过决议，号召印度人民“一致要求让印度参加政治会议，并且要求保证不在没有亚洲人民的参与的情况下决定亚洲人民的事务。”
    决议说：“为了世界和平，印度应全力支持真正为协商解决国际争端而作的一切努力，并且不断地控诉和揭露现在正尽力拖延和阻挠大国间和平谈判的那些势力。”决议并反对利用印度领土或资源进行战争，要求禁止大规模毁灭的武器。
