Title: Chinese Trade Union and Women's Delegation Visit and Tour the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1953-11-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 11th: The Chinese trade union delegation, invited to the Soviet Union to participate in the October Revolution Day celebrations, traveled to Stalinabad by plane on the 10th, after participating in the military parade and holiday parade held in Red Square, Moscow, on the 7th. They are preparing to visit and tour the Tajik Republic.
    Before participating in the holiday celebrations, the delegation visited Leningrad. They visited the historical sites of the October Revolution in Leningrad and various famous factories, and investigated the life of the local workers and the activities of the trade union organizations.
    Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: According to TASS, Moscow: The Ministry of Education of the Russian Federation held a reception in Moscow on the 10th to entertain the Chinese Women's Delegation, which was invited to participate in the October Revolution Day celebrations. The Deputy Minister of Education of the Russian Federation, Dubrovinna, introduced the Soviet education system and its achievements and school activities to the guests at the meeting. Yang Zhihua, the deputy head of the Chinese Women's Delegation, spoke at the reception, praising the excellent Soviet education system.
    At the end of the reception, a film about education in China was shown.

### 我国工会和妇女代表团  在苏联进行参观访问等活动

1953-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我国工会和妇女代表团
    在苏联进行参观访问等活动
    【新华社莫斯科十一日电】应邀到苏联参加十月革命节观礼的中国工会代表团，在七日参加了莫斯科红场举行的阅兵典礼和节日游行观礼后，已于十日乘飞机前往斯大林纳巴德，准备在塔吉克共和国进行参观访问。
    代表团在参加节日观礼前，曾访问列宁格勒。他们参观了列宁格勒的十月革命历史胜迹和各个著名的工厂，并考察了当地工人的生活和工会组织的活动。
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：俄罗斯联邦教育部于十日在莫斯科举行招待会，招待应邀参加十月革命节观礼的中国妇女代表团。俄罗斯联邦教育部副部长杜伯洛维娜在会上向来宾介绍了苏联的教育制度及其成就和学校的活动。中国妇女代表团副团长杨之华在招待会上讲话，盛赞苏联良好的教育制度。
    招待会最后放映了关于中国的教育影片。
