    Our Great Motherland
    The Anshan Iron and Steel Works' Cast Pipe Plant has produced a large number of iron pipes to supply the needs of basic construction in various regions. This is a collection of finished iron pipes of various specifications. They are being transported out of the factory and distributed to various places.

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍钢铸管厂生产了大批铁管，供应各地基本建设的需要。这是已经制好的各种不同规格的铁管。它们正被载运出厂分送到各地去。
