Title: Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The Vietnamese Trade Union delegation, on its way home after attending the Third World Trade Union Congress and passing through our country, arrived in Beijing on the 27th.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others.

The list of the Vietnamese Trade Union delegation:
　　Nguyen Cong Hoa (Head of the delegation) Standing Committee Member of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor
　　Nguyen Van Diep (Deputy Head of the delegation) Executive Committee Member of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, Secretary of the Viet Bac Inter-Zone Trade Union Federation
　　Nguyen Thi Tieu Member of the Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, National Model Worker
　　Trinh Xuan Phai National Model Worker
　　Phi Huy Dap National Model Worker
　　Do Van Quyen Agronomy Engineer, National Model Worker
　　Tran Anh Lien Executive Committee Member of the Hong Gai District Trade Union
　　Pham Hong Standing Committee Member of the Fourth Inter-Zone Trade Union Federation of Vietnam
　　Nguyen Minh Staff Member of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor
　　Tran Duc Staff Member of the International Liaison Department of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor
　　Representative of the Farmers' Association

### 越南工会代表团到达北京
