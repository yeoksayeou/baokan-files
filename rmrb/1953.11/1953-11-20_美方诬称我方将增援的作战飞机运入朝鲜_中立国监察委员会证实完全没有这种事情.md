Title: The U.S. Side Falsely Accuses Our Side of Transporting Reinforcement Warplanes into Korea; Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Confirms This is Completely Untrue
Author:
Date: 1953-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, November 19th: On October 12th, the U.S. side falsely accused the Korean-Chinese side of transporting reinforcement warplanes, packed in crates, from Northeast China to the Uiju airfield, and requested the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission to dispatch a mobile inspection team to the Uiju airfield for investigation.

After conducting an investigation at the Uiju airfield from October 14th to 26th, the Fourth Mobile Neutral Nations Inspection Team submitted an investigation report to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission. The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission has reviewed and approved the investigation report, and all parties unanimously believe that this U.S. accusation is completely groundless.

### 美方诬称我方将增援的作战飞机运入朝鲜  中立国监察委员会证实完全没有这种事情

1953-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    美方诬称我方将增援的作战飞机运入朝鲜
    中立国监察委员会证实完全没有这种事情
    【新华社开城十九日电】十月十二日，美方曾诬称朝中方面将增援的作战飞机自中国东北装箱运入义州机场，要求中立国监察委员会派遣机动视察小组前往义州机场进行调查。
    第四机动中立国视察小组于十月十四至二十六日在义州机场进行调查后，已向中立国监察委员会提出调查报告，中立国监察委员会并已对该调查报告作了审定，均一致认为美方这一诬控是毫无根据的。
