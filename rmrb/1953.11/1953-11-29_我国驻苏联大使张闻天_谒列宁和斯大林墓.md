Title: China's Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, Visits the Tombs of Lenin and Stalin
Author:
Date: 1953-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 27th -- Zhang Wentian, China's Ambassador to the Soviet Union, led all the staff of the embassy on the afternoon of the 26th to pay respects to the recently opened Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum and presented wreaths. He was accompanied by Ledovsky, Deputy Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and personnel from the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 我国驻苏联大使张闻天  谒列宁和斯大林墓

1953-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我国驻苏联大使张闻天
    谒列宁和斯大林墓
    【新华社莫斯科二十七日电】我国驻苏联大使张闻天于二十六日下午率领大使馆全体工作人员，拜谒最近开放的列宁和斯大林陵墓，并献花圈致敬。陪同前往的有苏联外交部远东司副司长列多夫斯基和苏联外交部交际司的人员等。
