Title: Hungary's Sales Volume Increases Greatly After Retail Price Reduction of Daily Necessities
Author:
Date: 1953-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 16th: According to TASS, Hungarian trade volume has greatly increased since the implementation of retail price reductions for food and daily necessities in early September. In September of this year, the turnover of state-owned company retail trade increased by 23.7% compared to the same period last year, and cooperatives increased by 23.1%. Clothes, radio receivers, bicycles, furniture, sewing machines, etc., are selling particularly well. For example, the sales volume of sewing machines in September this year increased by 64% compared to the same period last year, and bicycles increased by more than 63.4%. The number of small agricultural machinery and tools purchased by working farmers has increased one and a half times compared to the same period last year. Their demand for building materials has also greatly increased. In order to better meet the growing needs of residents for commodities, Hungary's trade network is constantly expanding, and the variety of commodities is also increasing. Recently, many department stores and specialty stores have been opened in Budapest and major cities. Many stores specializing in serving miners have also been established in coal mining areas.

### 匈牙利减低日用品零售价格后销售额大增

1953-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利减低日用品零售价格后销售额大增
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利自九月初实行减低食品和日用品零售价格以来，贸易额已大大增加了。今年九月，国营公司零售贸易的流通量，比去年同期增加了百分之二十三点七，合作社增加了百分之二十三点一。衣服、无线电收音机、自行车、家具、缝纫机等特别畅销。例如，今年九月缝纫机的销售量比去年同期增加了百分之六十四，自行车增加了百分之六十三点四强。劳动农民购买小型农业机械和农具的数量，比去年同期增加了一倍半。他们对于建筑材料的需求量也大大增加了。为了更好地满足居民对商品的不断增长的需要，匈牙利的贸易网正在不断扩大，商品种类也在不断增加。最近布达佩斯和各大城市已开设了许多百货商店和专业商店。煤矿地区中还设立了许多专门为矿工服务的商店。
