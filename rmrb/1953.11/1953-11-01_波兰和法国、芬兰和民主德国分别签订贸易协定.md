Title: Poland and France, Finland and East Germany Sign Trade Agreements Respectively
Author:
Date: 1953-11-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw: Poland and France signed a one-year trade agreement in Warsaw on October 29, according to the Polish Press Agency.

According to this agreement, Poland will import fertilizers, iron ore, hardware products, medicines, chemicals, fruits and spices, etc. from France.

France will import coal, timber, pottery and metal products, etc. from Poland.
Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, October 31st: Finland and the German Democratic Republic signed a new trade agreement for 1954 in Helsinki. According to the agreement, Finland will supply the German Democratic Republic with goods worth 3 billion marks and will import goods worth 3.8 billion marks from the German Democratic Republic.

Finland will mainly export wood products and cheese to the German Democratic Republic. It will import chemical products, machinery, automobiles, etc. from the German Democratic Republic.

### 波兰和法国、芬兰和民主德国分别签订贸易协定

1953-11-01
第3版()
专栏：

    波兰和法国、芬兰和民主德国分别签订贸易协定
    【新华社三十一日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰和法国于十月二十九日在华沙签订了为期一年的贸易协定。
    根据这一协定，波兰将从法国输入肥料、铁矿砂、五金产品、药品、化学品、水果和香料等。
    法国将从波兰输入煤、木材、陶器和金属品等。
    【新华社讯】塔斯社赫尔辛基三十一日讯：芬兰和德意志民主共和国在赫尔辛基签订了一九五四年的新贸易协定。根据协定，芬兰将供应德意志民主共和国以价值三十亿马克的货物，并将从德意志民主共和国输入价值三十八亿马克的货物。
    芬兰将向德意志民主共和国输出的，主要是木器工业的产品和乳酪。将从德意志民主共和国输入的是化学药品、机器、汽车等。
