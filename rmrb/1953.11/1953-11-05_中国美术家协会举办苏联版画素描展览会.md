Title: China Artists Association to Hold Soviet Print and Sketch Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1953-11-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:

    To celebrate the 36th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the China Artists Association is scheduled to hold the "Soviet Print and Sketch Exhibition" at Yilantang in Beihai Park starting on the 6th of this month. The content includes more than 70 pieces of Soviet modern woodcuts, linocuts, copperplate engravings, lithographs, and sketch illustrations. Among them are works depicting the deeds of the leaders of the October Revolution, the Soviet Patriotic War and the situation of post-war recovery and construction, as well as various subjects depicting the scenery of Soviet cities and collective farms. The prints on display are all printed with relative refinement. The exhibition will last until the 12th of this month, for a total of one week.

### 中国美术家协会举办苏联版画素描展览会

1953-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中国美术家协会举办苏联版画素描展览会
    【新华社讯】为了庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十六周年，中国美术家协会定本月六日起在北海漪澜堂举办“苏联版画、素描展览会”。内容包括苏联现代木刻、麻胶版刻、铜版画、石版画和素描插图等作品七十多件。其中有描写十月革命领袖事迹的，有描写苏联卫国战争和战后恢复和建设情况的，也有描写苏联城市和集体农庄的风景等各方面的题材。这次陈列的画片印刷都较精致。展览日期到本月十二日止，共一星期。
