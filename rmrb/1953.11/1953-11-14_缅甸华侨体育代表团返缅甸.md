Title: Burmese Chinese Sports Delegation Returns to Burma
Author:
Date: 1953-11-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The Burmese Chinese Sports Delegation, which returned to China for sightseeing in late July of this year and stayed in the motherland for more than three months, returned to Burma via Hong Kong from Guangzhou on November 12th.

During their return to China, the Burmese Chinese Sports Delegation participated in the National Day observation ceremony in Beijing this year, visited the "1953 National Track and Field, Gymnastics, and Cycling Games," and held basketball and volleyball exhibition matches. The delegation also visited Tianjin, Nanjing, Wuxi, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Shenyang, Fushun, and other cities, and held friendly matches with local basketball and volleyball teams. All members of the delegation also returned to their hometowns for sightseeing in mid-October.

### 缅甸华侨体育代表团返缅甸

1953-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸华侨体育代表团返缅甸
    【新华社十三日讯】今年七月下旬回国观光的缅甸华侨体育代表团在祖国逗留了三个多月，已于十一月十二日由广州取道香港返缅甸。
    缅甸华侨体育代表团回国期间，曾在北京参加了今年的国庆节观礼、参观了“一九五三年全国田径、体操、自行车运动大会”，并举行了篮排球表演赛，代表团还访问了天津、南京、无锡、上海、杭州、沈阳、抚顺等城市。和各地篮排球队作了友谊比赛。代表团全体团员并曾在十月中旬分别回原籍观光。
