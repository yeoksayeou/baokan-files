Title: Vietnamese Women Actively Engage in Agricultural Production
Author:
Date: 1953-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    Vietnamese women in the Nghệ An province of Central Vietnam, in order to support the front lines, are voluntarily replacing their husbands and sons who have joined the army to engage in production in the rear, and have achieved great success in competitions. During the short-term blitz competition movement from Ho Chi Minh's birthday on May 19th to the anniversary of the Vietnamese August Revolution on August 19th, more than 17,500 women learned to use plows and harrows. At the same time, they reclaimed 1,400 hectares of rice paddies, 6 hectares of sweet potato fields, and 200 hectares of vegetable gardens.

### 越南妇女积极进行农业生产

1953-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    越南妇女积极进行农业生产
    据新华社讯：中越义安省的妇女为了支援前线，纷纷在后方自动地代替参军的丈夫和儿子进行生产，并在竞赛中取得了很大成绩。在从五月十九日胡志明主席诞辰那天起到八月十九日越南八月革命纪念日为止的短期突击竞赛运动中，就有一万七千五百多名妇女学会了使用犁、耙。同时，她们还开了一千四百公顷稻田、六公顷白薯田和二百公顷菜地。
