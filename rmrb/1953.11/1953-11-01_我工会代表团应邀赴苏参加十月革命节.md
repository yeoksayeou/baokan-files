Title: Our Trade Union Delegation Invited to USSR to Participate in October Revolution Celebration

Author:
Date: 1953-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st:

    The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, at the invitation of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, has dispatched a Chinese Trade Union Delegation to the Soviet Union to attend the celebration of the 36th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. Part of the delegation has already left for Moscow. The remaining members departed by plane on the 31st. The list of the Chinese Trade Union Delegation is as follows:
    Head of Delegation: Kang Yonghe (Member of the Presidium of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Director of the North China Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions)
    Members: Yang Chao (Executive Member of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Deputy Director of the Southwest Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions)
      Ma Chungu (Executive Member of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Director of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Labor Bureau)
  Zhao Tiefu (Deputy Director of the Central South China Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions)
  Zhang Fanghai (Vice Chairman of the Northwest Federation of Trade Unions)
  Fan Xiaofeng (Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Textile Trade Union)
  Zhou Rong (Technician at Anshan Iron and Steel Company)
  Ren Leiyuan (Staff Member of the Agricultural, Forestry and Water Conservancy Trade Union Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions) The above eight are already in Moscow.
  Zhang Zhaomei (Chairman of the Trade Union Federation of Songjiang Province)
  Fan Yong (Railway Labor Model)
  Guo Xiuyun (Postal and Telecommunications Labor Model)
  Wei Yuxi (Labor Model)

### 我工会代表团应邀赴苏参加十月革命节

1953-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团应邀赴苏参加十月革命节
    【新华社三十一日讯】中华全国总工会应全苏工会中央理事会的邀请，派遣中国工会代表团前往苏联参加伟大的十月社会主义革命三十六周年庆祝典礼观礼。代表团一部分人员已赴莫斯科。一部分人员于三十一日乘飞机启程。中国工会代表团名单如下：团长：康永和（中华全国总工会主席团委员，中华全国总工会华北工作委员会主任）团员：杨超（中华全国总工会执行委员，中华全国总工会西南办事处副主任）
      马纯古（中华全国总工会执行委员，上海市人民政府劳动局局长）
  赵铁夫（中华全国总工会中南区工作委员会副主任）
  张方海（西北总工会副主席）
  范小凤（中国纺织工会上海市委员会副主席）
  周镕（鞍山钢铁公司技术人员）
  任雷远（中华全国总工会农林水利工会工作委员会工作人员）以上八人已在莫斯科
  张兆美（松江省工会联合会主席）
  范永（铁路劳动模范）
  郭秀云（邮电劳动模范）
  韦玉玺（劳动模范）
