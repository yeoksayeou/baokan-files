Title: Ikio Oyama Returns to Beijing After Visiting Various Places
Author:
Date: 1953-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Professor Ikio Oyama, Chairman of the Japan Peace Supporters Committee and winner of the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize, his wife, and his secretary, Tokusaburo Tan, accompanied by Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, returned to Beijing on the evening of the 1st after visiting Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, and Wuhan.

Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Director of the Organization Department, were at the station to greet them.

Togo Kameda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and Japanese peace advocate Ganemon Nakamura were also at the station to welcome them.

### 大山郁夫在各地参观后返京

1953-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    大山郁夫在各地参观后返京
    【新华社二日讯】日本拥护和平委员会主席、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人大山郁夫教授和他的夫人以及秘书淡德三郎，在中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一陪同下赴天津、南京、上海、杭州、广州和武汉参观访问后，已于一日晚返抵北京。
    前往车站迎接的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会联络部副部长唐明照和组织部副部长王明远等。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍、日本和平人士中村玩右卫门也往车站欢迎。
