Title: Pyongyang Mass Rally Welcomes Japanese People's Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-11-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th.—More than 800 people from all walks of life in Pyongyang held a welcome rally on the 7th to welcome the Japanese People's Delegation to Celebrate the Korean Armistice and Peace, headed by Ikuo Oyama. Those present included: Han Seol-ya, Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Support of Peace and Vice-Chairman, Park Jeong-ae, winner of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace," Kim Cheon-hae, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland of Korea, and other members, responsible persons and representatives of the Korean Workers' Party, various democratic parties, and people's organizations, officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army, and representatives of people from all walks of life in Pyongyang.
    Han Seol-ya delivered a welcome speech at the meeting, saying: The Korean people support and echo the Japanese people's struggle against the American imperialists and to defend freedom and national independence, and firmly believe that the Japanese people will achieve great victories and make significant contributions to the cause of peace in Asia and the world.
    Afterwards, the Korean National Committee for the Support of Peace and the Japanese People's Delegation to Celebrate the Korean Armistice and Peace presented each other with banners.
    Ikuo Oyama delivered a reply speech at the end. After thanking the Korean people for their warm welcome and kind reception, he said: The Japanese people are jubilant for the victory of the Korean people. Since the realization of the Korean armistice, 360 places in Japan have held mass celebration gatherings, and the movement to defend peace is also developing rapidly. The Japanese people will further strengthen their friendship and solidarity with the Korean people and fight to defend peace in Asia and the world in the future.
    After the meeting, the Korean People's Army Ensemble performed brilliant programs.

### 平壤各界集会欢迎日本人民友好代表团

1953-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤各界集会欢迎日本人民友好代表团
   【新华社平壤八日电】平壤各界八百多人在七日举行欢迎大会，欢迎以大山郁夫为首的日本人民祝贺朝鲜停战和平友好代表团。到会的有：朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员长韩雪野及副委员长、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖者朴正爱，朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会议长团议长金天海及其他委员，朝鲜劳动党、各民主政党、人民团体负责人和代表，朝鲜人民军将士及平壤市各界人民代表等。
    韩雪野在会上致欢迎词，他说：朝鲜人民支持和声援日本人民反对美帝国主义者及保卫自由和民族独立的斗争，并确信日本人民必将取得巨大的胜利，对亚洲与世界和平的事业作出重大的贡献。
    接着，朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会和日本人民祝贺朝鲜停战和平友好代表团互赠了锦旗。
    大山郁夫最后致答词。他在感谢朝鲜人民的热烈欢迎和亲切的接待之后说：日本人民为朝鲜人民的胜利而欢欣鼓舞。朝鲜停战实现以后，日本有三百六十个地方举行了群众庆祝集会，保卫和平的运动也在迅速发展中。日本人民今后将进一步加强与朝鲜人民的友谊团结，为保卫亚洲与世界和平而斗争。
    大会结束后，由朝鲜人民军协奏团演出精采节目。
