Title: Czechoslovak People Expand Donation Campaign to Aid Korea: Total Donations Exceed 1.6 Million Crowns
Author:
Date: 1953-11-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 31st: The working people of Czechoslovakia continue to expand their donation campaign to aid Korea and have achieved preliminary success. According to a report in Prague's "Labor Daily" on October 30th, since the campaign officially began on September 28th, the country had donated more than 1,624,000 crowns (Czech currency) by October 26th, equivalent to the total value of 1.2 million catties of ordinary bread in Prague.

### 捷人民扩展援朝捐献运动  捐款总数已达一百六十多万克朗

1953-11-01
第3版()
专栏：

    捷人民扩展援朝捐献运动
    捐款总数已达一百六十多万克朗
    【新华社布拉格三十一日电】捷克斯洛伐克的劳动人民继续扩展援助朝鲜捐献运动，并且获得了初步成就。根据布拉格“劳动报”十月三十日刊载的消息，自从这个运动在九月二十八日正式开始以来，到十月二十六日，捷克斯洛伐克全国已经捐献了一百六十二万四千多克朗（捷币），相当于布拉格当地一百二十万斤普通面包的总值。
