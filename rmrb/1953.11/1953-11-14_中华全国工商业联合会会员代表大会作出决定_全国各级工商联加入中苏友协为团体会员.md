Title: All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Membership Representative Assembly Makes Decision: All Levels of Industry and Commerce Federations Nationwide Join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as Group Members
Author:
Date: 1953-11-14
Page: 2

    The All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Membership Representative Assembly unanimously adopted the proposal of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee regarding all levels of industry and commerce federations nationwide joining the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as group members, and made the following decision:
    Strengthening the friendly cooperation between the people of China and the Soviet Union and learning from the Soviet Union is the common aspiration of the people and industrialists and business people nationwide; it is also a common political task of all levels of industry and commerce federations. It is hereby decided: The All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce and all levels of industry and commerce federations shall join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as group members, maintain close contact with the local Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations, and, in conjunction with the current movement for increased production and thrift and the first Five-Year Plan for economic construction, widely promote the experience of Soviet socialist construction and advanced science and technology among industrial and commercial circles and cadres of industry and commerce federations, in order to further strengthen and consolidate the unbreakable fraternal friendship and sincere cooperation between the people of China and the Soviet Union, thereby promoting the overall task of gradually realizing socialist industrialization and socialist transformation in our country.
                                    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中华全国工商业联合会会员代表大会作出决定  全国各级工商联加入中苏友协为团体会员

1953-11-14
第2版()
专栏：

    中华全国工商业联合会会员代表大会作出决定
    全国各级工商联加入中苏友协为团体会员
    中华全国工商业联合会会员代表大会一致通过中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会关于全国各级工商业联合会加入中苏友好协会为团体会员的提议，作出决定如下：
    加强中苏两国人民的友好合作和学习苏联，是全国人民和全国工商业者的一致愿望；同时亦为各级工商业联合会共同的政治任务。兹决定：中华全国工商业联合会及各级工商业联合会均加入中苏友好协会为团体会员，并与当地中苏友好协会密切联系，结合当前的增产节约运动和第一个五年经济建设计划，经常地在工商界及工商联干部中，广泛展开学习苏联社会主义建设的经验和先进的科学技术，进一步加强和巩固中苏两国人民之间牢不可破的兄弟友谊和真挚的合作，借以促进我国逐步实现社会主义工业化和社会主义改造的总任务。
                                    （新华社）
