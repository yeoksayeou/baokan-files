Title: Hungarian Artist Gergely Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-11-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd: Hungarian young artist Vencel Gergely, specializing in plastic arts, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 2nd, invited to China under the 1953 executive plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Vencel Gergely specializes in charcoal drawings, black chalk drawings, and pencil drawings. Due to his outstanding organizational work and artistic achievements, he was awarded the "Medal for Socialist Culture" on April 4th this year (Hungarian Liberation Day).

### 匈牙利艺术家格尔盖依到京

1953-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利艺术家格尔盖依到京
    【新华社二日讯】根据中匈文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划应邀来我国的匈牙利青年造型艺术家文泽·格尔盖依在二日乘飞机到达北京。
    文泽·格尔盖依专长于炭画、黑粉笔画、铅笔画等。由于他卓越的组织工作和艺术成就，他在今年四月四日（匈牙利解放日）获得“为社会主义的文化”奖章。
