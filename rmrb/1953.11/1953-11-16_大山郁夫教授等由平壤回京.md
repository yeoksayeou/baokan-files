Title: Professor Oyama Ikuo and Others Return to Beijing from Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1953-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Japanese People's Delegation for Congratulations on the Korean Armistice and Peace and Friendship, headed by Professor Oyama Ikuo, returned to Beijing by train on the morning of the 14th. Liu Guanqi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department, and many others welcomed them at the station.

### 大山郁夫教授等由平壤回京

1953-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　大山郁夫教授等由平壤回京
    【新华社十四日讯】以大山郁夫教授为首的日本人民祝贺朝鲜停战和平友好代表团，在十四日上午乘车返抵北京。到车站欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一、联络部副部长唐明照等多人。
