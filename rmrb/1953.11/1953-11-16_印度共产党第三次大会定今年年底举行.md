Title: Communist Party of India Third Congress Scheduled for End of This Year
Author:
Date: 1953-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 14th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of India held a meeting in Delhi from November 5th to 9th.

　　The meeting focused on preparations for the Third Congress of the Communist Party of India. The meeting decided to move the venue of the Congress from Hyderabad to Madurai in Madras Province.

　　The Party's Third Congress will be held from December 27, 1953, to January 3, 1954. The Party Congress will discuss the Indian political situation and the resolutions submitted to the Congress by the Central Committee. The Party Congress will discuss revisions to the CPI Party Constitution.

　　The CPI Central Committee meeting also discussed the political situation in Travancore-Cochin and the recent mass movements carried out in West Bengal, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Travancore-Cochin, Tamil Nadu, Rajasthan and other provinces (states). The meeting passed a resolution supporting the struggle of Lucknow students for academic freedom.

### 印度共产党第三次大会定今年年底举行

1953-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度共产党第三次大会定今年年底举行
    【新华社新德里十四日电】印度共产党中央委员会自十一月五日至九日在德里举行会议。
    会议着重讨论了印度共产党第三次代表大会的筹备工作。会议决定将大会地点从海得拉巴移至马德拉斯省的马都拉。
    党第三次代表大会将于一九五三年十二月二十七日至一九五四年一月三日举行。党代表大会将讨论印度政治形势并讨论中央委员会向大会提出的决议。党代表大会将讨论印共党章的修改。
    印共中央委员会会议还讨论了特拉凡哥尔—柯钦的政治形势和最近在西孟加拉、北方省、比哈尔、特拉凡哥尔—柯钦、泰米尔纳德、拉贾斯坦等省（邦）开展的群众运动。会议通过了支持勒克瑙学生争取学院自由的斗争的决议。
