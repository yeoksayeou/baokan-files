    Our Great Motherland
    China's synthetic petroleum industry is developing rapidly. The Northeast Petroleum Plant is the largest synthetic petroleum plant in our country. In addition to producing large quantities of crude oil, diesel, gasoline, and paraffin wax, it can also obtain by-products needed for industry and agriculture. This is the ammonia recovery cooling tower in the crude oil section of the plant.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国的人造石油工业正在迅速地发展。东北石油厂是我国规模最大的一座人造石油厂。它除了生产大量的原油、柴油、汽油、石蜡以外，还可以得到工业和农业上所需的副产品。这是该厂原油部的阿摩尼亚回收冷却塔。
