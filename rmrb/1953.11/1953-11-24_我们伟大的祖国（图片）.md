    Our Great Motherland
    The Liming Collective Farm in Yanji County, Yanbian Korean Autonomous Region, Jilin Province, has achieved its first bumper harvest since its establishment. This farm member, after a day's work, comes to the nursery to pick up her child, showing the joy of an increasingly better life and a bountiful harvest.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    吉林省延边朝鲜族自治区延吉县黎明集体农庄获得建庄后第一年的大丰收。这位庄员在一天的劳动完了以后，到托儿所来带领她的小孩，表现出生活日益美好和丰收的喜悦。
