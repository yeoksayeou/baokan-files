Title: Leaders of Various People's Democratic Countries Send Telegrams to Soviet Party and Government Leaders to Congratulate the 36th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution; Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party Holds Meeting to Celebrate the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1953-11-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th (TASS dispatch): The leaders of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Mongolian People's Republic, the Eastern European People's Democratic Countries, and the German Democratic Republic, have sent telegrams to Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, congratulating them on the 36th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

    Those who sent congratulatory telegrams to Voroshilov were Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea; the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic; Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic; Zápotocký, President of the Czechoslovak Republic; Dobi, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic; Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria; Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic; Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the Albanian People's Republic; and Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic.

    Those who sent congratulatory telegrams to Malenkov were Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic; Beirut, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic; Nagy, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic; Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria; Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic; Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Albanian People's Republic; and Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic.

    In their congratulatory telegrams, the leaders of these countries emphasized the international significance of the Great October Revolution, expressed that the glorious achievements of the Soviet Union in building communism have greatly encouraged the people of their respective countries, and wished the Soviet Union new victories in its continued struggle to build communism and defend world peace.
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 8th (Deutsche Nachrichten-Agentur dispatch): The Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party and the leadership of the party's Greater Berlin district held a meeting at the Friedrichstadt-Palast in Berlin on the 6th to celebrate the 36th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. More than three thousand people attended the celebration.

    When Wilhelm Pieck, Otto Grotewohl, and other members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party, as well as Max Reimann, Chairman of the Communist Party of Germany, walked onto the stage, the participants enthusiastically cheered them. When the diplomatic envoys, headed by F. S. Semyonov, the Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Germany, arrived, the audience also cheered warmly.

    After the national anthems of the Soviet Union and Germany were played, Karl Schirdewan, a member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party, spoke. He highly praised the many contributions made by the Soviet Union to defending world peace and believed that the Soviet Union is the most sincere friend of the German people. When Schirdewan spoke about the achievements made by the Soviet Union on the road to building communism and the great assistance of the Soviet Union to the German nation, his speech was repeatedly interrupted by the cheers of the audience. At the end of the speech, the participants gave stormy cheers for the glorious Soviet Communist Party, the powerful Soviet Union, and the German Socialist Unity Party.

    Finally, a brilliant performance was presented.

### 各人民民主国家领袖致电苏联党和政府领袖  祝贺十月社会主义革命三十六周年  德国统一社会党中央举行庆祝十月革命节大会

1953-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    各人民民主国家领袖致电苏联党和政府领袖
    祝贺十月社会主义革命三十六周年
    德国统一社会党中央举行庆祝十月革命节大会
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科七日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国、蒙古人民共和国、东欧各人民民主国家、德意志民主共和国的领袖，分别致电苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和苏联部长会议主席马林科夫，祝贺伟大的十月社会主义革命三十六周年。
    向伏罗希洛夫致贺电的有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉、蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团，波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席团主席萨瓦茨基，捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基，匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比，保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席达米扬诺夫，罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席格罗查，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希，德意志民主共和国总统皮克。
    向马林科夫致贺电的有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成，越南民主共和国主席胡志明，蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，波兰人民共和国部长会议主席贝鲁特，匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席纳吉，保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席契尔文科夫，罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席乔治乌—德治，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席霍查，德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥。
    这些国家的领袖在贺电中强调伟大的十月革命的国际意义，表示苏联共产主义建设的光辉成就给予各该国人民以极大的鼓舞，并祝苏联在继续进行共产主义建设和保卫世界和平的斗争中获得新的胜利。
   【新华社八日讯】据德意志通讯社柏林讯：德国统一社会党中央委员会和党的大柏林区领导机构，六日在柏林腓特烈宫举行庆祝十月社会主义革命三十六周年大会。参加庆祝大会的有三千多人。
    当以威廉·皮克、奥托·格罗提渥为首的德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员和德国共产党主席马克斯·雷曼走上主席台时，与会者向他们热烈欢呼。在以苏联驻德特命全权大使弗·斯·谢麦诺夫为首的外交使节到会时，全场也热烈欢呼。
    在演奏了苏联和德国国歌后，德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员卡尔·希德万讲话。他盛赞苏联对保卫世界和平所作的许多贡献。并认为苏联是德国人民最真挚的朋友。当希德万谈到苏联在建设共产主义的道路上所获得的成就和苏联对于德意志民族的伟大援助时，他的讲话一再被听众的欢呼所打断。演讲结束时，与会者为光荣的苏联共产党、强大的苏联和德国统一社会党发出了暴风雨般的欢呼。
    最后演出了精采节目。
