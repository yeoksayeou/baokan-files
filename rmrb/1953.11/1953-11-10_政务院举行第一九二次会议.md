Title: Government Administration Council Holds 192nd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-11-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The 192nd session of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 5th. The meeting listened to the explanation made by Liao Luyan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, on the measures for land requisition for national construction, and adopted the "Measures of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government for Land Requisition for National Construction." The meeting also adopted various appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行第一九二次会议

1953-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一九二次会议
   【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百九十二次政务会议在五日下午举行。会议听取了中央人民政府政务院廖鲁言副秘书长所作的关于国家建设征用土地办法的说明后，通过了“中央人民政府政务院关于国家建设征用土地办法”。会议并通过了各项任免案。
