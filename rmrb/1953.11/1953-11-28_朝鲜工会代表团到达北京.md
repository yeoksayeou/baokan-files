Title: Korean Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-11-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The Korean trade union delegation, returning home after attending the Third World Trade Union Congress and passing through our country, arrived in Beijing on the 26th.

Li Jiebo, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Fang Ming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, went to the airport to welcome them.

The list of the Korean trade union delegation:
　　Xu Hui (Head of the delegation), Acting Chairman of the Korean Vocational Trade Union League
　　Cha Longyun, Staff Member of the International Department of the Korean Vocational Trade Union League
　　Cui Chengjunn, Deputy Chairman of the Primary Organization of Factory No. 65, Labor Hero
　　Han Changnan, Member of the Central Committee of the Korean Vocational Trade Union League, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Labor News"

### 朝鲜工会代表团到达北京

1953-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜工会代表团到达北京
    【新华社二十六日讯】出席世界工会第三次代表大会后返国途中路过我国的朝鲜工会代表团在二十六日抵达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会书记李颉伯和中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长方明。
