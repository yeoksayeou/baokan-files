Title: Chinese People's Relief Administration Guangdong Branch Allocates Funds to Aid Disaster-Stricken Compatriots in Hong Kong and Kowloon

Author:
Date: 1953-11-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency Report: From the end of last year to recently, major fires have occurred successively in Shek Kip Mei Village, Ho Man Tin Village, Man Heung Yuen, Kowloon Walled City, Tai Kok Tsui, Wo Tsai Village, Aberdeen squatter area, Ho Man Tin New Village, and Pak Tin Village in Kowloon and Hong Kong. Nearly 4,000 wooden houses were destroyed, affecting more than 16,000 disaster victims. The Guangdong Branch of the Chinese People's Relief Administration has allocated a total of RMB 1.33 billion in the past ten months to provide relief to the disaster-stricken compatriots, and has written letters to express condolences to them.

### 中国人民救济总会广东省分会  拨款救济香港九龙受灾同胞

1953-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民救济总会广东省分会
    拨款救济香港九龙受灾同胞
    据新华社讯：从去年底到最近，九龙石硖尾村、何文田村、万香园、九龙城砦、大角咀、窝仔村、香港仔木屋区、何文田新村及白田村先后发生了大火，被烧毁的木屋近四千间，灾民达一万六千多人。中国人民救济总会广东省分会在今年十个月内共拨款人民币一十三亿三千万元，救济受灾同胞，并写信慰问他们。
