Title: Government Administration Council Holds its 193rd Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1953-11-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: The 193rd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 11th. The meeting heard and approved the summary report on the movement to implement the Marriage Law, given by Liu Jingfan, Deputy Director of the Central Committee for the Implementation of the Marriage Law. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行第一九三次会议

1953-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一九三次会议
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百九十三次政务会议在十一日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央贯彻婚姻法运动委员会刘景范副主任所作的关于贯彻婚姻法运动的总结报告。会议并通过了各项任免案。
