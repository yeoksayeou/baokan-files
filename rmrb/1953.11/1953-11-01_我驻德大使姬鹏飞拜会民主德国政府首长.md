Title: My Ambassador to Germany, Ji Pengfei, calls on the leaders of the Government of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1953-11-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 31st: After the diplomatic mission of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic was changed to an embassy, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Ji Pengfei paid separate calls on Johannes Dieckmann, Chairman of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, and the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the German Democratic Republic, V.S. Semyonov, on October 28th. On October 30th, he paid courtesy visits to Deputy Prime Minister Paul Scholz and Minister of Foreign and Domestic Trade Kurt Gregor of the German Democratic Republic.

### 我驻德大使姬鹏飞拜会民主德国政府首长

1953-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻德大使姬鹏飞拜会民主德国政府首长
   【新华社柏林三十一日电】中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国外交使团改为大使馆后，特命全权大使姬鹏飞于十月二十八日分别拜会德意志民主共和国人民议院主席约翰尼斯·狄克曼、总理奥托·格罗提渥及苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使弗·斯·谢麦诺夫，并于十月三十日前往德意志民主共和国副总理保罗·舒尔茨及对外与国内贸易部部长库尔特·格里哥尔处作礼节性的拜会。
