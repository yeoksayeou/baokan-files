Our Great Motherland
The state-owned Lutai Farm successfully tested a reaper-binder for harvesting rice. This reaper-binder can harvest eighteen mu of rice per hour, and only two people are needed to operate the entire machine. This reaper-binder is smoothly harvesting rice in the paddy fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　国营芦台农场使用割晒机试割水稻成功。这种割晒机每小时可收割水稻十八亩，只要两个人就可操作全部机器。这部割晒机正顺利地在稻田里收割水稻。
