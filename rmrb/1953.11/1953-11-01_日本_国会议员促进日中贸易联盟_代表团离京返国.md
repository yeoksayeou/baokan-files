Title: Delegation of the "Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade" of Japan Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1953-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st:

    The delegation of the "Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade" of Japan, headed by Ikeda Masanosuke, and including Vice Heads Eto Natsuo, Hoashi Kei, Nakamura Koichi, and all delegation members, left Beijing this morning, October 31st, to return home.
    Present to see them off were Ji Chao-ting, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Xie Xiao'nai, Director of the Liaison Department, and Lu Xuzhang, Ni Weiting, Shu Ziqing, and other representatives who participated in the trade negotiations.
    Japanese peace activists Kameda Togo and Nakamura Ganemon were also present to see them off.
    During their stay in Beijing, the delegation of the "Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade" of Japan attended our country's 1953 National Day celebrations and visited the Guanting Reservoir project on the Yongding River, as well as farms and schools.

### 日本“国会议员促进日中贸易联盟”代表团离京返国

1953-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    日本“国会议员促进日中贸易联盟”代表团离京返国
   【新华社三十一日讯】日本“国会议员促进日中贸易联盟”代表团团长池田正之辅，副团长江藤夏雄、帆足计、中村高一和全体团员，已于三十一日晨离北京返国。
    前往欢送的有中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎，联络部部长谢筱迺和参加这次贸易谈判的代表卢绪章、倪蔚庭、舒自清等。
    前往欢送的还有日本和平人士龟田东伍和中村玩右卫门。
    日本“国会议员促进日中贸易联盟”代表团在京期间，曾参加我国一九五三年国庆典礼，并参观了永定河官厅水库工程和农场、学校等。
