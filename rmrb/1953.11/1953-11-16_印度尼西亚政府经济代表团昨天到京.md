Title: Indonesian Government Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1953-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: The Indonesian Government Economic Delegation, a group of seven led by Head of Delegation Asmawin, arrived in Beijing yesterday evening via Guangzhou and Hankou.

Those present at the station to welcome them included: Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government; Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau; Yang Mian, Director of the Reception Department; Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Reception Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Li Yingji, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and others.

Also present at the station to welcome them were: Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu, Counselor Isa, Marty, and many embassy staff members.

The list of the Indonesian Government Economic Delegation is as follows:

　　Head of Delegation: Asmawin, with the rank of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary, Director of the Foreign Economic Liaison Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Economy
　　Members: Fejie Shalin
　　　　　　Batilazhawani
　　　　　　Sunazo
　　　　　　Hasuno
　　　　　　Dawulan
　　　　　　Xu Mianqing, Delegation Translator

### 印度尼西亚政府经济代表团昨天到京

1953-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚政府经济代表团昨天到京
    【新华社十五日讯】印度尼西亚政府经济代表团一行七人，在团长阿斯玛温率领下，经广州、汉口于十五日晚抵达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民，第三局局长卢绪章，交际处处长杨勉，外交部交际处副处长王拓，中国进出口公司副经理李应吉等人。
    到车站欢迎的还有：印度尼西亚驻华大使馆大使莫诺努图，参赞伊沙·玛蒂及使馆馆员等多人。
    印度尼西亚政府经济代表团名单如下：
    团长    阿斯玛温  特命全权大使衔，印度尼西亚经济部国外经济联络署署长
    团员    费节·沙林
    巴蒂拉渣哇尼
    苏纳佐
    哈苏诺
    达务兰尼
    许绵清  代表团翻译
