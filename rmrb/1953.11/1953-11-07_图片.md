Date: 1953-11-07

    Demobilized Red Army soldier Long Ge (right) and middle peasant Wa Ao (center) visit a collective farm in an advanced area of the Soviet Union. They are looking at the income ledgers of each member of the village. From these specific figures, they realize the superiority of the collective system.
