Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Hosts Japanese Trade Union Delegation and Farmer Representatives
Author:
Date: 1953-11-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman, held a reception on the evening of the 26th to host the Japanese Trade Union Delegation and Japanese Farmer Representatives who were invited to visit our country.

Present as guests were Li Jiebo, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Chen Yu, Secretary-General of the Asia-Australia Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions; Fang Ming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Jin Ziming and others.

In his speech, Lai Ruoyu first extended a warm welcome to the Japanese Trade Union Delegation and Japanese Farmer Representatives. He then said: The Chinese working class and the people of the whole country are concerned about and support the Japanese people's struggle for independence, peace, democracy, and freedom, and are very sympathetic to the current situation of the Japanese people. He said: The militaristic Japan of the past brought great disasters to Asia and China. Therefore, an independent, peaceful, democratic, and free Japan is of great importance to the peace of China and Asia. Finally, he described the achievements of China in the four years since liberation, and expressed that the Chinese working class hopes and believes that the Japanese people's movement for independence, peace, democracy, and freedom will surely be victorious.

Next, Nakamura Mitsuo, head of the Japanese Trade Union Delegation, and Kubota Yutaka, a Japanese farmer representative, delivered speeches in reply. Nakamura Mitsuo said: We are very happy to visit China this time. On behalf of all Japanese workers, we would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to all Chinese workers. This visit will enable us to learn from the experience of the Chinese working class's great revolutionary victory and see the development and growth of the Chinese working class. He expressed that they will convey these to all Japanese workers and peasants, and believes that this will promote the friendly unity between Chinese and Japanese workers. In his reply, Kubota Yutaka first expressed his gratitude for the invitation from the Chinese people, and said that he would learn from the experience of the worker-peasant alliance in the victory of the Chinese revolution, so that Japanese farmers can strive for the victory of the Japanese national liberation struggle under the leadership of the Japanese working class.

Comrade Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, also spoke at the reception.

The reception was filled with a friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 全国总工会招待日工会代表团和农民代表

1953-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    全国总工会招待日工会代表团和农民代表
    【新华社二十六日讯】中华全国总工会主席赖若愚、副主席刘宁一在二十六日晚举行招待会，招待应邀来我国访问的日本工会代表团和日本农民代表。
    出席作陪的有中华全国总工会书记李颉伯，世界工会联合会亚澳联络局秘书长陈宇，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长方明、金子明等人。
    赖若愚在致词中首先对日本工会代表团和日本农民代表表示热烈的欢迎。接着他说：中国工人阶级和全国人民对日本人民争取独立、和平、民主、自由的斗争是关怀和支持的，对日本人民现在的处境是十分同情的。他说：过去的军国主义的日本曾给亚洲和中国带来了很大的灾难，因此一个独立、和平、民主、自由的日本，对中国和亚洲的和平是有重要关系的。最后他叙述了中国解放后四年来的成就，并表示中国工人阶级希望并相信日本人民争取独立、和平、民主、自由的运动一定可以得到胜利。
    接着，日本工会代表团团长中村满夫和日本农民代表久保田丰致答词。中村满夫说：这次来访问中国，感到非常高兴，我们用全日本工人的名义，向全中国工人致以衷心感谢。这次访问，会使我们学到中国工人阶级伟大革命胜利的经验和看到中国工人阶级发展壮大的情况。他表示他们将把这些传达给全日本的工人和农民，并相信这将促进中、日两国工人的友好团结。久保田丰致答词中首先对中国人民的邀请表示谢意，并说要学习中国革命胜利中工农联盟的经验，使日本农民在日本工人阶级领导下，争取日本民族解放斗争的胜利。
    中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一同志也在招待会上讲了话。
    招待会始终洋溢着友好的气氛。
