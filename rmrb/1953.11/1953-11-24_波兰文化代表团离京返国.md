Title: Polish Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1953-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 21st: After visiting Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Xiangtan, Wuhan, Shenyang, Fushun and other places, the Polish Cultural Delegation returned to Beijing on the 13th. On the 21st, the head of the delegation, Sokolsky, and the members, a party of eight, left Beijing by plane to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee of Culture and Education, Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Fang Ming, Zhang Zhaoqian, Chen Baichen, Zou Difan, Guo Naian and other figures from the capital's cultural, artistic, educational and scientific circles.

Also present to see them off was Gula, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China.

Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 18th to bid farewell to all the representatives of the delegation.

### 波兰文化代表团离京返国

1953-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰文化代表团离京返国
    【新华社二十一日讯】波兰文化代表团在天津、南京、上海、杭州、广州、湘潭、武汉、沈阳、抚顺等地访问完毕后，于十三日回到北京。二十一日代表团团长索科尔斯基和团员等一行八人乘飞机离京返国。
    前往机场欢送的有：中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局副局长陈忠经，外交部交际处副处长王拓，以及首都文化、艺术、教育、科学界人士方明、张肇骞、陈白尘、邹荻帆、郭乃安等。
    前往欢送的还有波兰驻我国大使馆临时代办古拉等。
    我中央人民政府文化部副部长周扬十八日晚曾设宴欢送代表团全体代表。
