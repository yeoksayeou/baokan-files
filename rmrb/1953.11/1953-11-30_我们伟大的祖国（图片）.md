    Our Great Motherland
    This is a corner of the Shixian Paper Mill, a state-owned enterprise in Jilin Province. The paper used for printing the Complete Works of Stalin is produced here. Currently, workers are studying advanced Soviet methods to further improve the quality of paper used for the Complete Works of Stalin.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是吉林省国营石岘造纸厂的一角。印刷斯大林全集所需的纸张，就是在这里生产的。现在，工人们正学习苏联的先进方法，为进一步提高斯大林全集用纸的质量而努力。
