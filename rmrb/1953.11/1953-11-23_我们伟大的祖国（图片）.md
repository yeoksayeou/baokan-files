Our Great Motherland
　　Along the Chengyu Railway line after the autumn harvest, a prosperous new atmosphere has emerged. In Jianyang County, the main cotton-producing area of Sichuan Province, the yield of more than 440,000 mu of cotton fields has generally increased by 15%. Cotton farmers have been selling cotton to the state to support industrial construction. This is the cotton piled up at Jianyang Station, waiting to be shipped out.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    秋后的成渝铁路沿线，出现了一片繁荣的新气象。四川省主要产棉区——简阳县的四十四万余亩棉田，普遍增产百分之十五。棉农们纷纷将棉花卖给国家，支援工业建设。这是堆在简阳车站上等待外运的棉花。
