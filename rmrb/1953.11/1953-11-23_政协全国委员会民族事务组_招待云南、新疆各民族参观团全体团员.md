Title: The National Committee of the CPPCC's Ethnic Affairs Group Hosts All Members of the Visiting Delegations of Various Ethnic Groups from Yunnan and Xinjiang

Author:
Date: 1953-11-23
Page: 1

    The National Committee of the CPPCC's Ethnic Affairs Group
    Hosts All Members of the Visiting Delegations of Various Ethnic Groups from Yunnan and Xinjiang
   [Xinhua News Agency, 22nd] The Ethnic Affairs Group of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a reception on the afternoon of the 21st to host all members of the visiting delegations of various ethnic groups from Yunnan and Xinjiang.
    The reception was presided over by Gan Chunlei, Deputy Head of the Ethnic Affairs Group of the National Committee of the CPPCC. At the meeting, Zhu Zao Guan, Member of the National Committee of the CPPCC and Head of the Ethnic Affairs Group, spoke first. He expressed a warm welcome on behalf of the Ethnic Affairs Group of the National Committee of the CPPCC to the two visiting delegations for coming to the capital for a visit. He then said: Ethnic regional autonomy is the basic policy for resolving China's ethnic issues. The implementation of the policy of ethnic regional autonomy has enhanced the patriotism of the people of all ethnic groups and strengthened the unity between and within all ethnic groups. He expressed his hope that all brotherly ethnic groups will steadily develop their political, economic, and cultural construction undertakings in the future under possible conditions, and unite more closely to jointly build the great motherland.
    Nusreti (Uyghur), Head of the Xinjiang Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, and Si Hongsheng (Dai) and Si Nashan (Jingpo), representatives of the Yunnan Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, spoke at the meeting. They recounted the initial improvements in the lives of the people of all ethnic minorities in Xinjiang and Yunnan under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao after liberation, and stated that after this visit, they would definitely convey what they had learned to the local people. They also unanimously expressed that in the future, they would encourage the people of all ethnic minorities to contribute all their strength to participate in the construction of the motherland.

### 政协全国委员会民族事务组  招待云南、新疆各民族参观团全体团员

1953-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会民族事务组
    招待云南、新疆各民族参观团全体团员
   【新华社二十二日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会民族事务组，在二十一日下午举行招待会，招待云南、新疆各民族参观团的全体团员。
    招待会由政协全国委员会民族事务组副组长甘春雷主持。会上，首先由政协全国委员会委员、民族事务组组长朱早观讲话。他代表政协全国委员会民族事务组对两参观团来到首都参观，表示热烈欢迎。他接着说：民族区域自治是解决我国民族问题的基本政策，民族区域自治政策推行后，增强了各族人民的爱国主义精神，加强了各民族间和各民族内部的团结。他希望各兄弟民族今后在可能的条件下，稳步发展自己的政治、经济和文化建设事业，并且更加紧密地团结起来，共同建设伟大的祖国。
    新疆各民族参观团团长努斯热提（维族），云南各民族参观团代表思鸿升（傣族）、思纳山（景颇族）先后在会上讲话。他们讲述了新疆、云南各少数民族人民解放后在中国共产党和毛主席领导下生活已得到初步改善的情景，表示这次参观后，一定把参观中得到的东西传达给当地人民。他们还一致表示，今后要鼓励各少数民族人民贡献出自己的全部力量，参加祖国的建设事业。
