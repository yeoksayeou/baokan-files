Title: International Volleyball Federation Holds Congress; Accepts All-China Sports Federation as Formal Member
Author:
Date: 1953-11-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 18th: According to TASS reports, the Fourth Congress of the International Volleyball Federation was held in Bucharest, Romania, from the 14th to the 16th. Representatives from twenty countries attended the congress.

    The Congress approved the All-China Sports Federation's application to join the International Volleyball Federation as a formal member. It also approved the admission of volleyball organizations from Austria, India, Egypt, and Spain.

    The Congress decided to hold the European Men's and Women's Volleyball Championships in Bucharest in 1955. It was also decided that if volleyball is not included in the program of the 16th Olympic Games, the World Volleyball Championships would be held in Paris in 1956.

    The Congress accepted the Soviet delegation's proposal to revise the volleyball rules based on Soviet rules.

### 国际排球联合会举行代表大会  接受我体育总会为正式会员

1953-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    国际排球联合会举行代表大会
    接受我体育总会为正式会员
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社消息：国际排球联合会第四届代表大会于十四日到十六日在罗马尼亚布加勒斯特举行。参加大会的有二十个国家的代表。
    大会批准中华全国体育总会加入国际排球联合会为正式会员，同时并批准奥地利、印度、埃及和西班牙等国的排球组织加入该会。
    大会决定在一九五五年在布加勒斯特举行欧洲男子与女子排球冠军赛，同时并决定如果第十六届奥林匹克运动会项目中没有排球赛的话，一九五六年将在巴黎举行世界排球冠军赛。
    大会接受了苏联代表团的建议，决定根据苏联规则来整理排球规则。
