Title: 政务院 Holds 194th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-11-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — The 194th meeting of the Central People's Government Council was held on the afternoon of the 19th. The meeting heard a statement on the grain issue by Zhang Naiqi, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Food, and then adopted the decision on grain work. The meeting also approved various appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第一九四次会议

1953-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一九四次会议
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百九十四次政务会议在十九日下午举行。会议听了中央人民政府粮食部章乃器部长所作的关于粮食问题的说明后，通过了关于粮食工作的决定。会议并通过了各项任免案。
