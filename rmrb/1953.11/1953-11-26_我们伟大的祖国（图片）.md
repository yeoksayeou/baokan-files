    Our Great Motherland
    Our country's newly built mechanized and automated large-scale steel rolling mill is smoothly undergoing trial operation and acceptance work. Production personnel are working around the clock to practice operating techniques. This is the central cooling platform of the large-scale steel rolling mill. The rolled steel rails and structural steel (I-beams, channel steel, angle steel) must be cooled here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国新建的机械化自动化的大型轧钢厂，正在顺利地进行试车和验收工作，生产人员日夜加紧练习操作技术。这是大型轧钢厂的中央冷却台。轧成的钢轨型钢（工字钢、槽钢、角钢），都要在这里冷却。
