Title: Malayan National Liberation Army Achieves Victory in Attack in Central Johor

Author:

Date: 1953-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, 25th: The Malayan National Liberation Army forces in central Johor attacked Yong Peng on the morning of the 22nd, and entered a "new village" in the area. Before actively withdrawing, the National Liberation Army forces captured all the weapons of the puppet defense corps stationed in the "new village."

### 马来亚民族解放军在柔佛州中部出击获得胜利

1953-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    马来亚民族解放军在柔佛州中部出击获得胜利
    【新华社二十五日讯】新加坡消息：在柔佛州中部的马来亚民族解放军部队二十二日晨向永平出击，攻进了该地区的一个“新村”。在主动撤出之前，民族解放军部队缴获了驻守该“新村”的伪保卫团的全部武器。
