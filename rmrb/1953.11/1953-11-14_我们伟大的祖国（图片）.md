Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-11-14
Page: 1

Our great motherland

The 1953 National Ethnic Sports Performance and Competition Conference has concluded victoriously. The conference reviewed the achievements of ethnic sports activities in recent years and laid a good foundation for sorting out and promoting ethnic sports in the future. This is a picture of athlete Zhang Zhibin (eight years old) participating in the conference, performing the Ba Fang Dao.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    一九五三年全国民族形式体育表演及竞赛大会胜利闭幕了。大会检阅了几年来民族形式体育运动的成就，并为今后整理和推广民族形式体育打下良好的基础。这是参加大会的运动员张植滨（八岁），表演八方刀的情形。
