International Briefs

　　Criminal's Confession
　　Syngman Rhee's representative to the United States, Yim Pyong-jik, said during a television broadcast on October 19th, in response to an interviewer, that the United States supported South Korea from the beginning of the war for the "unification of Korea." Yim Pyong-jik said: "Of course, this was the main objective, otherwise why fight? The main and sole purpose of launching this war and fighting with us was to unify Korea."

　　In response to another interviewer, Yim Pyong-jik said: "...We started this war, and if we are to end it, we must achieve our goal."

　　Yim Pyong-jik not only revealed the relationship between master and lackey, but also unintentionally admitted that the United States and Syngman Rhee were the culprits who launched the war of aggression against Korea.

　　Sinful City
　　United Press reporter William Miller sent a report about Chitose, Hokkaido, Japan (Chitose is a small city in Hokkaido next to the US Air Force base and the US First Cavalry Division camp, with an area of only two square miles).

　　Miller began by saying: "The Japanese call this city 'the most sinful city in the world,' and American soldiers have nicknamed it 'Whorehouse'."

　　Miller described the city as follows: "Along the muddy streets are five hundred and sixty-four brothels, sixty-six 'beer halls,' eight 'restaurants' and 'dance halls,' and hundreds of smaller 'businesses'." "Here, drugs can be found everywhere. Heroin, morphine, and any drug you need can be bought for less than a dollar a shot or dose."

　　The sinful soldiers of the most sinful country in the world created "the most sinful city in the world."

### 国际简讯

1953-11-20
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    罪犯的供状
    李承晚驻美国代表林炳稷十月十九日在作电视广播时回答一个访问者说：美国在“统一朝鲜”的战争开始时就是支持南朝鲜的。林炳稷说：“当然，这是主要的目的，不然为什么要打呢？发动这场战争，并与我们一起打仗，其主要的和唯一的目的就是统一朝鲜。”
    林炳稷在答复另一个访问者时说：“……我们发动了这场战争，如果我们要结束它的话，就必须要实现我们的目的。”
    林炳稷不仅道出了主子与走狗的关系，而且无意间承认了美国和李承晚是发动侵朝战争的罪魁。
      罪恶的城市
    合众社记者威廉·米勒发出了一篇关于日本北海道千岁市（千岁是位于北海道的美国空军基地和美军第一骑兵师营地旁边的一个小城市，面积只有两平方英里）的一篇报道。
    米勒一开头就说：“日本人把这个城市叫做‘世界上最罪恶的城市’，美国兵给这个城市起了个浑名叫‘淫窟’。”
    米勒描写这个城市说：“泥泞的街道旁有五百六十四家妓院，六十六家‘啤酒馆’，八家‘餐馆’和‘跳舞厅’，还有几百家较小的‘营业所’。”“在这里，到处都可以找到毒品。海洛英和吗啡等一切你所需要的毒品，不到一美元就可买一针或一服。”
    世界上最罪恶的国家的罪恶的士兵造成了“世界上最罪恶的城市”。
