Title: Bulgarian Academy of Sciences Confers Honorary Academician Title on Guo Moruo
Author:
Date: 1953-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The ceremony at which the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences conferred the title of Honorary Academician on Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, was held in Beijing on the 1st. Attending the ceremony were: Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Petkov, and embassy staff; the Bulgarian cultural delegation headed by Marin Dozev; and Bulgarian journalist Topincharov, who was invited to visit China. Also present were: Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, and Wu Youxun; Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government Chen Zhongjing; Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture Zhao Feng; Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Tuo; and figures from the capital's scientific, cultural, and educational circles, including Chu Tunan, Fan Wenlan, Hua Luogeng, Zheng Zhenduo, Qian Sanqiang, Chen Yuan, Fu Zhongsun, Tu Changwang, Qian Weichang, Zhou Peiyuan, Ye Qisun, Cao Richang, Zeng Xianpu, Lin Rong, and Shen Qiyi, among others.

    The awarding ceremony was presided over by Petkov, the Bulgarian Ambassador to China. In his speech, he pointed out: The awarding of the title of Honorary Academician of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences to Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, is a major event in Sino-Bulgarian cultural relations. It is a new contribution to the cause of consolidating cultural exchanges between Bulgaria and the great Chinese people and to the cause of developing and consolidating the mutual ties and cultural cooperation between the Academies of Sciences and scientific workers of the two countries. Then, He. Daskalov, representative of the Bulgarian cultural delegation and academician of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, presented Guo Moruo with the Honorary Academician certificate on behalf of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences and delivered a speech. Finally, Guo Moruo delivered a reply. (The full text of the speeches can be found on page four of today's newspaper)

### 保加利亚科学院授予郭沫若名誉院士学位

1953-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚科学院授予郭沫若名誉院士学位
    据新华社讯：保加利亚科学院授予中国科学院院长郭沫若以名誉院士学位的仪式，于一日在北京举行。参加仪式的有：保加利亚驻我国大使彼得科夫和使馆人员，以马林·多泽夫为首的保加利亚文化代表团和应邀到我国访问的保加利亚记者托平查罗夫。参加仪式的还有：中国科学院副院长陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局副局长陈忠经，文化部办公厅主任赵沨，外交部交际处副处长王拓，以及首都科学、文化、教育界人士楚图南、范文澜、华罗庚、郑振铎、钱三强、陈垣、傅忠荪、涂长望、钱伟长、周培源、叶企孙、曹日昌、曾宪朴、林鎔、沈其益等多人。
    授予学位的仪式由保加利亚驻我国大使彼得科夫主持。他在致词中指出：以保加利亚科学院名誉院士学位授予中国科学院郭沫若院长，是中保文化关系上的一件大事，它在巩固保加利亚与伟大中国人民之间的文化交流的事业中，在发展和巩固保中两国科学院及科学工作者的互相联系及文化合作的事业中，是一个新的贡献。接着，保加利亚文化代表团代表、保加利亚科学院院士赫·达斯卡洛夫，代表保加利亚科学院以名誉院士证书授予郭沫若并致词。最后，郭沫若致答词。（致词全文都见今日本报第四版）
