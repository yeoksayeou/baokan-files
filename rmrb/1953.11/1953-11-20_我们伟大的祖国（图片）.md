Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1953-11-20
Page: 1

    The preliminary survey work for the Chengdu-Kunming Railway is being intensified. In order to select a reasonable and economical route, the surveying personnel are launching a red flag competition, striving to complete all preliminary survey work by the end of the year. This is a scene of the surveying personnel conducting surveys on the banks of the Jinsha River.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    成（都）昆（明）铁路的初测工作正在加紧进行。勘测人员为了选择出一条合理的、经济的线路，正展开红旗竞赛，争取全部初测工作在年底结束。这是勘测人员在金沙江边进行勘测的情形。
