Title: Newsreel Film of the Korean Government Delegation's Visit to Our Country Begins Screening
Author:
Date: 1953-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The newsreel film "Marshal Kim Il-sung Leads the Korean Government Delegation to Beijing," produced by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, will be released in Beijing and throughout the country starting on the 19th. This newsreel film includes the arrival of the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Marshal Kim Il-sung, in Beijing, and the welcome at the station by Premier of the State Administrative Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Zhou Enlai, and other leaders, as well as Premier Zhou Enlai's banquet at Huairen Hall in Beijing on November 13th to entertain the Korean government delegation.

### 朝鲜政府代表团访问我国的新闻特辑影片开始放映

1953-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团访问我国的新闻特辑影片开始放映
    【新华社十八日讯】中央新闻记录电影制片厂摄制的“金日成元帅率领朝鲜政府代表团到北京”新闻特辑影片，十九日起将陆续在北京和全国各地上映。在这个新闻特辑影片中有金日成元帅率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团到达北京，和我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来等首长到车站欢迎的情形，以及周恩来总理十一月十三日在北京怀仁堂设宴招待朝鲜政府代表团的情形。
