Title: Malenkov's Reply to Chairman Mao Thanking Him for Congratulations on the 36th Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author: Ge·Mǎlínkēfū
Date: 1953-11-28
Page: 1

    Thanking Him for Congratulations on the 36th Anniversary of the October Revolution
    Malenkov's Reply to Chairman Mao

    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

    Please accept my heartfelt gratitude for your friendly congratulations on the 36th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, addressed to you, Comrade Chairman, and through you, to the Government of the People's Republic of China. The increasingly consolidated fraternal friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and China is a powerful force for safeguarding peace and security in the Far East and the world.

    G. Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union
    Moscow, November 21, 1953

### 感谢对十月革命三十六周年的祝贺  马林科夫致毛主席复电
格·马林科夫
1953-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢对十月革命三十六周年的祝贺
    马林科夫致毛主席复电中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    在伟大十月社会主义革命三十六周年纪念时，承致友谊的祝贺，谨向您，主席同志，并通过您，向中华人民共和国政府表示我衷心的感谢。苏中两国人民日益巩固的兄弟般的友谊，是保卫远东和世界和平与安全的强大力量。
    苏联部长会议主席格·马林科夫
    一九五三年十一月二十一日于莫斯科
