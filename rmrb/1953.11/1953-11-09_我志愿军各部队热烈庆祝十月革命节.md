Title: Our Chinese People's Volunteer Army Units Warmly Celebrate the October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1953-11-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th. Chinese People's Volunteer Army (CPVA) units warmly celebrated the 36th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution Day on November 7th.

    At the celebration meeting held by the leadership organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, Li Da, Chief of Staff of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, gave a report. After describing the great significance of the October Socialist Revolution and the great achievements of the Soviet Union in building a communist society, Li Da called on all commanders and fighters of the CPVA to learn from the Soviet army's high degree of patriotism and internationalism, their high degree of organization, discipline, and revolutionary vigilance, and to learn from the advanced military science of the Soviet Union. Li Da also called on all commanders and fighters of the CPVA to strive for the thorough implementation of the terms of the Korean Armistice Agreement and for the peaceful resolution of the Korean issue.
    Various units and organs of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army also held celebration meetings on the 7th. The CPVA's central command organ also invited Wu Kejian, head of the Fourth General Sub-Group of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, and Jin Zhonghua, deputy head, to deliver reports at the celebration meeting on the great achievements of the friendship and unity between China and the Soviet Union and the communist construction of the Soviet Union. Some units spent two days extensively studying documents related to the October Revolution Day; some companies also held lectures to celebrate the October Revolution Day. Many heroes and meritorious individuals wrote articles and expressed their feelings, thanking the Soviet Union for its selfless assistance to our country. Huang Jiafu, a first-class hero and special-class meritorious individual of the CPVA who attended the Fourth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, said: "The Soviet Union's great and selfless assistance to our country will accelerate the process of our country's socialist industrialization. The Soviet Union has always been safeguarding world peace. Our ability to achieve the great victory of the Korean armistice is inseparable from the efforts of the Soviet Union." He expressed his determination to learn from the Soviet army's patriotism and internationalism, and to fight to the end to defend the motherland and defend peace.

### 我志愿军各部队热烈庆祝十月革命节

1953-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    我志愿军各部队热烈庆祝十月革命节
   【新华社平壤八日电】中国人民志愿军各部队在十一月七日热烈庆祝十月社会主义革命节三十六周年。
    在中国人民志愿军领导机关举行的庆祝大会上，中国人民志愿军参谋长李达作了报告。李达在叙述了十月社会主义革命的伟大意义以及苏联在建设共产主义社会中的伟大成就后，号召志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员学习苏联军队高度的爱国主义和国际主义精神，学习苏联军队高度的组织性、纪律性和革命警惕性，学习苏联先进的军事科学；李达并号召志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员为朝鲜停战协定条款的彻底实施和为和平解决朝鲜问题而奋斗。
    中国人民志愿军的各部队、各机关也都在七日举行了庆祝会。志愿军中线指挥机关并邀请中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团第四总分团团长吴克坚、副团长金仲华在庆祝会上分别作了中苏两国友好团结和苏联共产主义建设的伟大成就的报告。有的部队以两天时间广泛地学习了有关十月革命节的文件；有些连队还举行了庆祝十月革命节的讲演会。很多英雄和功臣写文章和发表感想，感谢苏联对我国大公无私的援助。曾经出席第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的志愿军一级英雄、特等功臣黄家富说：“苏联对我国伟大无私的援助，将加速我国社会主义工业化的进程。苏联一直是维护世界和平的。我们能够获得朝鲜停战的伟大胜利，是和苏联的努力分不开的”。他表示要学习苏联军队的爱国主义和国际主义精神，为保卫祖国、保卫和平而奋斗到底。
