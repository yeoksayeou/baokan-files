Title: Czechoslovak Trade Union Chairman Kliment Passes Away, Lai Ruoyu Sends Condolences to Czechoslovak Trade Union

Author:
Date: 1953-11-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 29th -- Gustav Kliment, Chairman of the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions, passed away on the 22nd. Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram to the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions, expressing condolences. The telegram reads as follows:

To the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions:

We are shocked and saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Gu. Kliment, Chairman of the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions. This is a great loss to the Czechoslovak trade union movement and a great misfortune for the world workers' movement. On behalf of the Chinese working class, please accept our deepest condolences to you and all the workers of Czechoslovakia.

Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
October 28th

### 捷工会主席克利门特逝世  赖若愚致电捷工会表示吊唁

1953-11-01
第3版()
专栏：

    捷工会主席克利门特逝世
    赖若愚致电捷工会表示吊唁
    【新华社二十九日讯】捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会主席古斯塔夫·克利门特于二十二日逝世。我中华全国总工会主席赖若愚致电捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会，表示吊唁。电文如下：捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会：
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会主席古·克利门特同志不幸逝世。这是捷克斯洛伐克工会运动的一大损失，也是世界工人运动的很大不幸。谨代表中国工人阶级向你们和捷克斯洛伐克全体工人致以深切的吊唁。
    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚
    十月二十八日
