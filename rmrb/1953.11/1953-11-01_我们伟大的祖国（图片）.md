    Our Great Motherland
    The Central People's Broadcasting Station begins broadcasting children's programs every afternoon at 5:40 PM. Children all over the country can hear many stories, learn a lot of knowledge, and receive patriotic education on the radio. These children are attentively listening to a story.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中央人民广播电台在每天下午五时四十分开始播送少年儿童节目。全国各地少年儿童，都可以在收音机里听到许多故事，懂得许多知识，受到爱国主义的教育。这几位小朋友正在聚精会神地听故事。
