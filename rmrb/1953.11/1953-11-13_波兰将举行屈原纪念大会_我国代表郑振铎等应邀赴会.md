Title: Poland to Hold Qu Yuan Memorial Meeting; Chinese Representatives Zheng Zhenduo and Others Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1953-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Polish Committee for the Defense of Peace is scheduled to hold a memorial meeting for the great Chinese poet Qu Yuan in Warsaw on November 16th. Zheng Zhenduo and Zhu Shilun, the two Chinese representatives invited to attend the meeting, left Beijing for Poland on November 12th.

### 波兰将举行屈原纪念大会  我国代表郑振铎等应邀赴会

1953-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰将举行屈原纪念大会
    我国代表郑振铎等应邀赴会
    【新华社十二日讯】波兰保卫和平委员会定于十一月十六日在华沙举行我国伟大诗人屈原纪念大会，应邀前往参加大会的我国代表郑振铎、朱世纶二人已于十一月十二日离京赴波。
