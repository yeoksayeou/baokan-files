Title: Korean Government Delegation Passes Through Andong City on Return Trip Home
Author:
Date: 1953-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, November 26th. The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Premier Kim Il-sung, Marshal of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, left China via Andong on the 26th to return home after visiting China. Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Reception Department, accompanied the delegation to the border. Xu Zhe, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, also accompanied them.

At the Andong Railway Station, seeing off Marshal Kim Il-sung and the delegation were: Li Tao, Chairman of the Liaodong Provincial People's Government; Gao Yang, Secretary of the Liaodong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Wang Ping, Deputy Political Commissar of the Liaodong Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Chen Beichen, Mayor of Andong City People's Government, and more than 400 people from all walks of life.

The flags of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China were hung at the station. The band played the national anthems of Korea and China.

Before leaving Chinese territory, Marshal Kim Il-sung and members of the delegation reviewed the guard of honor and accepted flowers presented by the Young Pioneers.

When the delegation passed through Tianjin and Shenyang on their way back home, local Party, government, and military leaders and people from all walks of life went to the stations to see them off. Those seeing them off in Tianjin included: Wu De, Mayor of Tianjin; Huang Huoqing, Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; and Liao Dinglin, representative of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Tianjin. Those seeing them off in Shenyang included: Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee and First Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee; Zhang Xiushan, Second Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Zhang Mingyuan, Vice Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee and Third Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Wang Jinxiang and Gu Zhuoxin, Vice Chairmen of the Northeast Administrative Committee; and Wu Xinquan, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The Consuls General and Vice Consuls of the Soviet Union in Tianjin and Shenyang, as well as representatives from the Polish Consulate in Tianjin and the Shenyang Liaison Office of the Korean Embassy in China, also went to the stations to see them off. Marshal Kim Il-sung and members of the delegation visited the Tianjin No. 2 State-owned Cotton Textile Factory and the Tianjin State-owned Paper Mill when they passed through Tianjin.

### 朝鲜政府代表团过安东市返国

1953-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团过安东市返国
    【新华社安东二十六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府访问中国代表团在二十六日离开我国国境安东返国。陪送到国境的有：中央人民政府外交部办公厅主任王炳南、交际处副处长沈平。陪行的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办徐哲。
    在安东车站上欢送金日成元帅和代表团的有：辽东省人民政府主席李涛，中共辽东省委员会书记高扬，中国人民解放军辽东军区副政治委员王屏，安东市人民政府市长陈北辰等和各界群众四百多人。
    车站上悬挂着朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国旗和中华人民共和国国旗。乐队奏起朝中两国的国歌。
    金日成元帅和代表团团员在离开我国国境前，检阅了仪仗队，并接受了少年先锋队队员们的献花。
    当代表团在返国途中经过天津和沈阳时，当地党政军首长和各界群众曾到车站欢送。在天津欢送的有：天津市人民政府市长吴德，中共天津市委员会书记黄火青，中国人民解放军驻津部队代表廖鼎琳。在沈阳欢送的有：东北行政委员会副主席、中共中央东北局第一副书记林枫，东北行政委员会副主席高崇民，中共中央东北局第二副书记张秀山，东北行政委员会副主席、中共中央东北局第三副书记张明远，东北行政委员会副主席汪金祥、顾卓新，中国人民解放军东北军区副参谋长吴信泉等。苏联驻天津、沈阳两地的总领事和副领事以及波兰驻天津领事馆、朝鲜驻华大使馆沈阳联络所的代表也到车站欢送。金日成元帅和代表团团员们在路过天津时，曾参观了国营天津第二棉纺织厂和国营天津造纸总厂。
