Title: Japanese People's Delegation Leaves Pyongyang for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-11-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 13th. The Japanese People's Delegation to Celebrate the Korean Armistice and Peace and Friendship, headed by Professor Ikuo Oyama, left Pyongyang by train on the morning of the 12th for Beijing. They were accompanied to Sinuiju by Li Il-gyeop, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Korean Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Li Myeon-sang, representative of Korean cultural and artistic workers.

Those who went to Pyongyang Railway Station to see them off included: Han Seol-ya, Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace; Kim Cheon-hae, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Fatherland Unification Democratic Front; heads of various democratic political parties, government organs, and people's organizations; Jeong Yeon-pyo, Chairman of the Pyongyang City People's Committee; and representatives from all walks of life.

During their six days in Pyongyang, the delegation was warmly received by people from all walks of life in Korea. At the same time, the delegation conveyed to the people of Korea the congratulations of the Japanese people on the victory and achievements of the Korean people.

### 日本人民代表团离平壤来京

1953-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    日本人民代表团离平壤来京
    【新华社平壤十三日电】以大山郁夫教授为首的日本人民祝贺朝鲜停战和平友好代表团一行，已于十二日上午乘车离开平壤，前往北京，由朝鲜外务省仪礼部副部长李日爕，朝鲜文化艺术工作者代表李冕相等陪送到新义州。
    前往平壤车站欢送的有：朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员长韩雪野，祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会议长团议长金天海，及各民主政党、政府机关和人民团体负责人，平壤市人民委员会委员长郑然彪和各界人民代表等。
    代表团在平壤的六天中，曾受到朝鲜各界人士亲切的接待。同时，代表团向朝鲜各界人士传达了日本人民对朝鲜人民的胜利和成就的祝贺。
