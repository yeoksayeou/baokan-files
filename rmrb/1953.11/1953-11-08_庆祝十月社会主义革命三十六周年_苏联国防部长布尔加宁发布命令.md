Title: Celebrating the 36th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution: Order Issued by Soviet Defense Minister Bulganin
Author: Ni. Ya. Bulganin
Date: 1953-11-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 7th: TASS Moscow, November 7th: Soviet Defense Minister, Marshal of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin, issued an order on the 36th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The full text is as follows:

Comrades soldiers of the Army and Navy! Comrades warrant officers and non-commissioned officers! Comrades officers and generals!

Today, the Soviet people and their glorious armed forces are celebrating the 36th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution together.

The Soviet working people, united around the Communist Party and the government, are greeting their National Day with new achievements in the implementation of the communist construction plan. Thanks to the selfless labor of the Soviet people, the socialist economy is developing continuously, the goals of the Fifth Five-Year Plan are being surpassed, and Soviet science and culture are thriving.

Our people have begun with great enthusiasm to implement the resolutions of the Party and the government on further rapid development of agriculture and ensuring a rapid increase in the production of daily necessities. The implementation of these resolutions will further improve the welfare of the working class, collective farmers, and Soviet intellectuals.

In the past year, the Soviet Union and the entire democratic camp have achieved significant achievements in the struggle for peace and the security of all nations. At the same time, the Soviet people are vigilant against the threat of aggression from the imperialists, and are unremittingly paying attention to doing their best to strengthen the defense of the socialist motherland.

Our armed forces are infinitely loyal to their people, the Communist Party and the Soviet government. They are vigilantly defending the great achievements of the October Revolution, and are unswervingly improving their military technology.

Comrades soldiers of the Army and Navy! Comrades warrant officers and non-commissioned officers! Comrades officers and generals!

On this great 36th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, I salute you and congratulate you. I wish you new achievements in improving your military and political cultivation, mastering modern military technology and weapons, and increasing your military and political knowledge.

In commemoration of the National Day, I order:

Today—November 7th, in Moscow, the capital of our motherland, in the capitals of the Union Republics, in Kaliningrad, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok, and in the hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, twenty artillery salvos shall be fired.

Long live our socialist motherland!

Long live the heroic Soviet people and their heroic Army and Navy!

Long live the Soviet government!

Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union!

Soviet Defense Minister, Marshal of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin

### 庆祝十月社会主义革命三十六周年  苏联国防部长布尔加宁发布命令
尼·亚·布尔加宁
1953-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月社会主义革命三十六周年
    苏联国防部长布尔加宁发布命令
      【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日讯：苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅尼·亚·布尔加宁，在伟大的十月社会主义革命三十六周年纪念日发布命令，全文如下：
    陆军和海军士兵同志们！尉官和士官同志们！校官与将军同志们！
    今天，苏联人民和他们光荣的武装部队一同庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十六周年。
    团结在共产党和政府周围的苏联劳动人民，以实行共产主义建设计划所获得的新成就来迎接他们的国庆节。由于苏联人民的忘我的劳动，社会主义经济正在不断发展，第五个五年计划的目标正在被超过，苏维埃科学和文化正在欣欣向荣。
    我们的人民已经以高涨的热情开始实行党和政府的关于进一步迅速发展农业以及保证日用品产量的迅速增加的决议。这些决议的实施将进一步增进工人阶级、集体农民和苏维埃知识分子的福利。
    在过去一年中，苏联和整个民主阵营在争取和平和各国人民安全的事业的斗争中，获得了重大的成就。同时，苏联人民警惕着来自帝国主义者的侵略威胁，正毫不松懈地注意尽力加强社会主义祖国的国防。
    我国的武装部队对他们的人民、共产党和苏维埃政府是无限忠诚的，他们正在警惕地保卫着十月革命的伟大成果，正在坚持不渝地提高他们的军事技术。
    陆军和海军士兵同志们！尉官和士官同志们！校官与将军同志们！
    在这伟大的十月社会主义革命三十六周年纪念日，我向你们致敬并祝贺你们。我祝你们在提高你们的军事和政治修养、掌握现代军事技术和武器、增进你们的军事和政治知识方面获得新的成就。
    为了纪念国庆节，我命令：
    今天——十一月七日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科，在各加盟共和国的首都，在加里宁格勒、罗夫、伯力、海参崴，在列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、塞瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨这几个英雄城市鸣放礼炮二十响。
    我们的社会主义祖国万岁！
    英雄的苏联人民和他们英雄的陆军和海军万岁！
    苏维埃政府万岁！
    苏联共产党万岁！
    苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅尼·亚·布尔加宁
