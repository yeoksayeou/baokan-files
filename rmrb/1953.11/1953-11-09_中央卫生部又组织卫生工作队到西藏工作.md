Author:
Date: 1953-11-09
Page: 3
Cultural News Briefs

    The Central Ministry of Health Organizes Another Health Work Team to Work in Tibet
    The Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government has organized another ethnic health work team to work in Tibet. The work team consists of forty-four people, including surgeons, pediatricians, nurses, midwives, and pharmacists. Among them are well-known physicians who have worked in hospitals for many years, as well as students who have just graduated from school. The team leader, Gao Lun, is the attending surgeon of Tianjin Municipal General Hospital. He has participated in the Korean War Volunteer Medical Team twice, and this time he volunteered to join the ethnic health work team. The team departed from Chongqing on November 3 for Lhasa.

### 中央卫生部又组织卫生工作队到西藏工作

1953-11-09
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    中央卫生部又组织卫生工作队到西藏工作
    中央人民政府卫生部又组织了一个民族卫生工作队，到西藏工作。工作队由外科和小儿科医师、护士、助产士和药剂师等四十四人组成。其中有在医院工作多年的著名医师，也有刚从学校毕业的学生。队长高伦是天津市市立总医院的外科主治医生，曾两次参加抗美援朝志愿医疗队，这次又志愿参加了民族卫生工作队。该队已在十一月三日由重庆出发前往拉萨。
