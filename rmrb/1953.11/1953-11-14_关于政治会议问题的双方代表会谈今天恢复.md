Title: Two Sides' Talks on the Political Conference Issue Resume Today
Author: 
Date: 1953-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 13th - The delegations from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China participating in the two sides' talks on the political conference issue issued a communique as follows:

    On November 13th, the two sides' talks on the political conference issue continued with an administrative advisory meeting. The advisory meeting concluded its work today. The talks between the two sides' representatives will resume tomorrow.

### 关于政治会议问题的双方代表会谈今天恢复

1953-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    关于政治会议问题的双方代表会谈今天恢复
    【新华社开城十三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国与中华人民共和国参加关于政治会议问题双方会谈的代表团发表公报如下：
    十一月十三日，关于政治会议问题的双方会谈继续举行行政性的顾问会议。顾问会议已于今日结束其工作。双方代表间的会谈将于明日恢复。
