Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-11-22
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    With the help of the state, the Xinghuo Collective Farm in Huachuan County, Songjiang Province, has achieved a 40% mechanization rate in its total annual labor, resulting in an unprecedented bumper harvest of 202 hectares of paddy rice, with an average yield of over 12,000 catties of rice per hectare (equivalent to 15 mu). On November 9th, the farm held a meeting of its members and decided to sell 1.62 million catties of rice to the state to support national construction. The picture shows the farmers threshing grain with a threshing machine and selecting the best grain to sell to the state.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1953-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    松江省桦川县星火集体农庄在国家的帮助下，机械化作业已达到全年总劳动量的百分之四十，使二百零二垧水稻获得空前的丰收，每垧（合十五市亩）稻谷的产量平均达一万二千多斤。农庄在十一月九日召开了庄员大会，决定卖给国家一百六十二万斤稻谷，支援国家建设。这是庄员们正在用脱谷机脱谷，挑选最好的粮食卖给国家。
