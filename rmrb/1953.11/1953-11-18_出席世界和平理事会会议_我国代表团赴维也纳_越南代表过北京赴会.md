Title: Attending the World Peace Council Meeting, Our Delegation Leaves for Vienna; Vietnamese Representatives Pass Through Beijing to Attend the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The World Peace Council meeting is scheduled to be held in Vienna from November 23rd to 28th. The Chinese delegation attending the meeting, led by the head of the delegation, Mao Dun, and the deputy head of the delegation, Liao Chengzhi, left Beijing by plane on the 17th to attend the meeting. The list of the delegation is as follows:

Thirteen members of the World Peace Council: Mao Dun, Zhang Bojun, Cai Tingkai, Xiao San, Liao Chengzhi, Wu Yaozong, Guo Fang, Liu Guanyi, Meng Yongqian, Jin Zhonghua, Lu Cui, Li Yimang (already abroad), Liu Changsheng (already abroad). Seven specially invited representatives: Jiang Guangnai, Bai Xiqing, Zheng Zhenduo (already abroad), Guo Zechen, Zhu Shilun (already abroad), Tang Mingzhao, Zheng Senyu (already abroad).

[Another Dispatch] The Vietnamese representatives, Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and Nguyen Xuan Thuy, Secretary-General, who passed through Beijing to Vienna to attend the World Peace Council meeting, left Beijing by plane on the 17th to attend the meeting. Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee and Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and Zhou Liang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and others saw them off at the airport.

### 出席世界和平理事会会议  我国代表团赴维也纳  越南代表过北京赴会

1953-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会会议
    我国代表团赴维也纳
    越南代表过北京赴会
    据新华社讯：世界和平理事会会议定于十一月二十三日到二十八日在维也纳举行，出席会议的中国代表团，在团长茅盾和副团长廖承志率领下，在十七日乘飞机离北京赴会。代表团名单如下：
    世界和平理事会理事十三人：茅盾、章伯钧、蔡廷锴、萧三、廖承志、吴耀宗、郭芳、刘贯一、孟用潜、金仲华、陆璀、李一氓（已在国外）、刘长胜（已在国外）。特邀代表七人：蒋光鼐、白希清、郑振铎（已在国外）、郭则沉、朱世纶（已在国外）、唐明照、郑森禹（已在国外）。
    【又讯】路过北京到维也纳去参加世界和平理事会会议的越南代表，越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探、总书记阮春水二人，在十七日乘飞机离北京赴会。到机场送行的有亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘宁一和越南驻我国大使馆临时代办周亮等。
