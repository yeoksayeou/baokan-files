Title: Soviet Ambassador Kuznetsov Holds Reception; Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Others, and Foreign Envoys Attend

Author: 
Date: 1953-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th: Soviet Ambassador to China, Kuznetsov, held a grand reception in Beijing on November 7th to celebrate the 36th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

Among those attending the reception were: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government of China Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Gao Gang, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Deng Xiaoping, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Deputy Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Gao Kelin, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang, Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, and heads of other ministries, commissions, administrations, and banks, Vice Mayor of Beijing Zhang Youyu, and others.

Also attending the reception were: Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Yuzhang, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Lai Ruoyu, Vice President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Zhang Yun, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth Liao Chengzhi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League Hu Yaobang, and heads of various democratic parties and other people's organizations, figures from cultural and scientific circles, combat heroes, and labor models.

Diplomatic envoys of various countries stationed in China and the Bulgarian cultural delegation visiting China also attended.

Ambassador Kuznetsov delivered a speech at the reception. He pointed out: Under the leadership of the Communist Party, the Soviet people have achieved great successes in further developing industry and agriculture in 1953. He said: The Soviet people are engaged in creative labor while doing everything possible to defend and consolidate the cause of peace throughout the world. The Soviet people believe that there are no international disputes that cannot be resolved peacefully through negotiations with the countries concerned. The Soviet government resolutely and consistently pursues a policy of peace, which aims at easing international tensions, consolidating friendship with the great Chinese people and the people of people's democracies, and expanding cooperation with all countries.

Vice Chairman Zhu De then delivered a speech. He said: The Soviet Union has always been an inspiration to the revolutionary cause of the Chinese people and the most loyal friend of the Chinese people, and it has always been the hope of hundreds of millions of working people around the world. The brilliant achievements of the Soviet people in building communism are increasingly strengthening the confidence of the people of the world in their struggle for a better future. The consistent peace policy pursued by the Soviet Union and its sincere friendship with the people of all countries have made the people of the world realize that the Soviet Union is the most powerful bastion for maintaining world peace and human justice.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the consolidation and growth of the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, the growing unbreakable friendship between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union, the great Soviet people who are striving to realize the highest ideal of all mankind—a communist society, and the Chinese people who are striving for a victorious transition to socialism, the increasingly strong world democratic camp headed by the Soviet Union, and the health of Chairman Malenkov, the leader of the Soviet people, and Chairman Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people.

The atmosphere of the reception was extremely warm and friendly.

### 苏联大使库兹涅佐夫举行招待会  朱德、刘少奇副主席等及各国使节到会

1953-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联大使库兹涅佐夫举行招待会
    朱德、刘少奇副主席等及各国使节到会
    【新华社七日讯】苏联驻我国特命全权大使库兹涅佐夫于十一月七日在北京举行盛大招待会，庆祝十月社会主义革命三十六周年。
    出席招待会的有：我中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深、高岗、秘书长林伯渠，政务院副总理董必武、陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，最高人民检察署副检察长高克林，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，外交部副部长王稼祥、章汉夫、伍修权和其他部、会、署、行负责人，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等。
    出席招待会的还有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴，中华全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记胡耀邦及各民主党派和其他人民团体的负责人，文化和科学界人士，战斗英雄，劳动模范等。
    到会的还有各国驻我国外交使节及来我国访问的保加利亚文化代表团。
    库兹涅佐夫大使在会上致辞。他指出：在共产党领导下，苏联人民一九五三年在进一步发展工农业方面已获得了巨大的成就。他说：苏联人民从事创造性的劳动，同时作到所能作的一切，以保卫和巩固全世界和平事业。苏联人民认为没有不可能用和平方法与有关各国以谈判的方法来解决的国际纷争。苏联政府坚决而一贯地实行爱好和平的政策，这个政策旨在缓和国际紧张局势，巩固与伟大的中国人民和人民民主国家人民的友谊，扩大与各国的合作。
    朱德副主席继起致辞。他说：苏联一直是中国人民革命事业的鼓舞者和中国人民最忠诚的朋友，也一直是全世界亿万劳动人民的希望。苏联人民在建设共产主义事业中的光辉灿烂的成就，日益增强着全世界人民为争取美好的未来而斗争的信心。苏联一贯奉行的和平政策以及对各国人民的真诚友谊，使全世界人民都认识到：苏联是维护世界和平与人类正义的最强大的堡垒。
    席间，宾主频频为以苏联为首的和平民主阵营的巩固和壮大、为中苏两大国之间日益增长的牢不可破的友谊、为正在为实现全人类的最高理想——共产主义社会而努力的伟大的苏联人民和正在为胜利过渡到社会主义而奋斗的中国人民、为以苏联为首的世界民主阵营日益强固、为苏联人民的领袖马林科夫主席和中国人民的领袖毛泽东主席的健康干杯。
    招待会的气氛极为热烈和友好。
