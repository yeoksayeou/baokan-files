Title: The US Forcibly Detains Our Personnel Who Mistakenly Entered the Joint Security Area. General Li Xiangchao Strongly Protests the US Violation of the Agreement
Author:
Date: 1953-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 23rd - At the Military Armistice Commission meeting on the 21st, General Li Xiangchao, Chief Commissioner of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, lodged a strong protest against the forcible detention of three personnel from the Korean-Chinese side by the US military police. The full text of General Li Xiangchao's protest is as follows:

"On November 19th, three of our personnel who mistakenly entered the Joint Security Area were forcibly detained by your security personnel. Your forcible detention of our personnel is a serious violation of the agreement, and you are even attempting to fabricate malicious slanders based on this serious violation of the agreement. Therefore, I lodge a strong protest against your barbarous act of forcibly detaining our personnel by force in the Joint Security Area, which is a serious violation of the agreement.

    You must fully bear the serious consequences arising from your forcible detention of our personnel in the Joint Security Area."

### 美方强行扣留误入共同警卫区的我方人员  李相朝将军强硬抗议美方违反协议的行为

1953-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    美方强行扣留误入共同警卫区的我方人员
    李相朝将军强硬抗议美方违反协议的行为
    【新华社开城二十三日电】军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员李相朝将军，在军事停战委员会二十一日会议上就美方军事警察强行扣留朝中方面三名人员一事提出强硬抗议。李相朝将军的抗议全文如下：
   “于十一月十九日误入共同警卫区的我方人员三名已被你方警卫人员强行扣留。你方强行扣留我方人员，是严重违反协议的行为，而你方竟企图根据这一严重违反协议的行为捏造恶毒的诽谤。因此，我就你方蛮横地在共同警卫区以武力扣留我方人员的严重违反协议的行为，提出强硬抗议。
    你方必须完全承担由于你方在共同警卫区强行扣留我方人员而引起的严重后果。”
