Title: State Council Approves Technical Design Plan for Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge
Author:
Date: 1955-07-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th

    The State Council approved the technical design plan for the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge on July 18th. At the same time, the construction schedule and total budget for the bridge were also approved.

    Prior to this, in late May to early June, the Ministry of Railways gathered renowned bridge experts and experienced bridge construction engineers from across the country to hold a technical design review meeting for the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge. The meeting conducted a thorough review of the bridge's technical design, construction schedule, and total budget, and finally submitted the plan to the State Council for approval.

### 国务院批准武汉长江大桥技术设计方案

1955-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院批准武汉长江大桥技术设计方案
    新华社二十日讯　国务院在七月十八日批准了武汉长江大桥技术设计方案，同时批准了大桥的施工进度计划和总预算。
    在此之前，铁道部曾在五月下旬到六月初集中国内著名的桥梁专家和有经验的桥梁建筑工程师，举行武汉长江大桥技术设计审查会议，就大桥的技术设计、施工进度计划和总预算进行了缜密的审查，最后作出方案呈送国务院批准。
