Title: Chinese Art Troupe Presents Gifts to the University of Paris
Author:
Date: 1955-07-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 30th. The Chinese art troupe, currently in Paris to participate in the Second International Drama Festival, has presented the University of Paris with a set of ancient Chinese opera books and a variety of other publications.

　　The presentation ceremony took place at the University of Paris at 5 p.m. on the 29th. Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese art troupe, personally presented the gifts to Sarrailh, the president of the University of Paris. Those attending the ceremony also included Professors Delachaux and Mayoux of the University of Paris, who had previously visited China, as well as the well-known sinologist Paul Demiéville, and dozens of others.

　　Zhao Feng, deputy head of the Chinese art troupe, gave a speech on classical Chinese opera and cultural life. Sarrailh, the president of the University of Paris, delivered a speech of thanks. He believed that the precious books presented by the Chinese art troupe would help develop cultural exchanges between China and France. Professor Mayoux spoke highly of the greatness of traditional Chinese art. Finally, Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese art troupe, reiterated the desire to promote Sino-French cultural exchanges.

　　The presentation ceremony ended in a friendly atmosphere.

### 我国艺术团向巴黎大学赠礼

1955-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国艺术团向巴黎大学赠礼
    新华社布拉格三十日电　现在巴黎参加第二届国际戏剧节的中国艺术团把古本戏曲丛书一部和其他出版物多种赠送给巴黎大学。
    赠送仪式二十九日下午五时在巴黎大学举行。中国艺术团团长张致祥把礼品当面交给巴黎大学校长沙拉伊尔，参加仪式的还有曾经访问过中国的巴黎大学教授德莱希、麦尤和著名汉学家戴密维等数十人。
    中国艺术团副团长赵沨作了关于中国古典戏曲和文化生活的讲话。巴黎大学校长沙拉伊尔致谢词。他认为中国艺术团所赠名贵典籍，将有助于发展中法两国的文化交流。麦尤教授发言盛赞中国传统艺术的伟大。最后，中国艺术团团长张致祥再次表示促进中法文化交流的愿望。
    赠送仪式在友好的气氛中结束。
