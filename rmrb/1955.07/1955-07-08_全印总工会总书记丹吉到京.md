Title: AITUC General Secretary Dange Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-07-08
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, July 7th. S.A. Dange, General Secretary of the All India Trade Union Congress (AITUC) and Vice-Chairman of the World Federation of Trade Unions, who attended the World Peace Conference in Helsinki, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 7th for a visit to China at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Accompanying him were his daughter and All India Trade Union Congress Assistant Secretary MacDonagh. Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Secretariat Secretaries Li Jiebo and Dong Xin, and Director of the International Liaison Department Ma Chungu went to the airport to welcome them.
    Representatives from India, Indonesia, the Soviet Union and Japan of the Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau were also present to welcome them.

### 全印总工会总书记丹吉到京

1955-07-08
第4版()
专栏：

    全印总工会总书记丹吉到京
    新华社七日讯　出席赫尔辛基世界和平大会的全印总工会总书记、世界工联副主席斯·阿·丹吉，应中华全国总工会的邀请前来中国访问，在七日下午乘飞机到达北京。同来的还有他的女儿和全印总工会助理书记麦克冬。前往机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，书记处书记李颉伯、董昕，国际联络部部长马纯古。
    前往欢迎的还有亚澳工会联络局印度、印度尼西亚、苏联和日本的代表。
