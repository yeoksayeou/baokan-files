Title: US, Britain, and France Announce Delegation Lists in Preparation for the Four-Power Summit
Author:
Date: 1955-07-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 15th. The governments of the United States, Britain, and France have each announced the lists of delegations participating in the Four-Power Summit, which is scheduled to open in Geneva on July 18th.

　　U.S. President Dwight D. Eisenhower will lead the U.S. delegation. Other members of the U.S. delegation include: Secretary of State John Foster Dulles, Special Assistant to the President for National Security and National Security Council Affairs Dillon Anderson, U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union Charles Bohlen, Presidential Press Secretary James Hagerty, State Department Counselor Douglas MacArthur II, Assistant Secretary of State for European Affairs Merchant, U.S. Ambassador to Austria Llewellyn Thompson, and others.

　　British Prime Minister Anthony Eden will lead the British delegation, accompanied by Foreign Secretary Harold Macmillan. The delegation's formal advisors include: Cabinet Secretary Sir Norman Brook, Permanent Under-Secretary of the Foreign Office Sir Ivone Kirkpatrick, Deputy Secretary of the Cabinet Sir Nevile Butler, British Ambassador to the Soviet Union Sir William Hayter, and others.

　　The French delegation will be led by Prime Minister Edgar Faure, assisted by his diplomatic advisor Armand Bérard and his Secretary-General Jacques Duhamel. Foreign Minister Antoine Pinay will also go to Geneva, assisted by Henri Iriysou, Jean Laloy, and others. In addition, those attending the meeting include the French Ambassador to the Soviet Union Louis Joxe, and others.

### 准备参加四国政府首脑会议  美英法三国公布代表团名单

1955-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

    准备参加四国政府首脑会议
    美英法三国公布代表团名单
    新华社十五日讯　美、英、法三国政府已经分别宣布了参加七月十八日将在日内瓦开幕的四大国政府首脑会议的代表团名单。
    美国总统艾森豪威尔将率领美国代表团。美国代表团的其他团员有：国务卿杜勒斯，关于国家安全和国家安全委员会事务的总统特别助理狄龙·安德逊，美国驻苏联大使波伦，总统新闻秘书詹姆斯·哈格蒂，国务院顾问道格拉斯·麦克阿瑟第二，负责欧洲事务的助理国务卿麦钱特，美国驻奥地利大使劳埃林·汤普逊等。
    英国首相艾登将率领英国代表团，陪同他的是外交大臣麦克米伦。代表团的正式顾问有：内阁秘书诺曼·布鲁克爵士，外交部常务次官伊冯·柯克帕特里克爵士，内阁副秘书奈维尔·布朗约翰爵士，英国驻苏联大使威廉·海特尔爵士等。
    法国代表团将由富尔总理率领，协助他的有他的外交顾问阿蒙·毕拉尔和他的行政秘书长雅克·杜阿梅。外交部长比内也将到日内瓦去，协助他的有亨利·伊里苏，让·拉洛瓦等。此外，参加会议的人还有法国驻苏联大使路易·约克斯等。
