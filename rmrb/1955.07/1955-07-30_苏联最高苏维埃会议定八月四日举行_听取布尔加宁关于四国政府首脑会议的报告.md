Title: Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to Convene on August 4th; To Hear Bulganin’s Report on the Four-Power Summit Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-07-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 28th, TASS Dispatch: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union announces: In view of the conclusion of the Geneva Conference of the Heads of Government of the Soviet Union, the United States, the United Kingdom, and France, the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union has submitted to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union a report on the participation of the Soviet government delegation in the aforementioned conference.

The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has decided to convene a session of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on August 4, 1955, in order to hear a report by the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin, on the results of the Geneva Four-Power Summit Conference.

The order for the convening of the session of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has been published.

### 苏联最高苏维埃会议定八月四日举行  听取布尔加宁关于四国政府首脑会议的报告

1955-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃会议定八月四日举行
    听取布尔加宁关于四国政府首脑会议的报告
    新华社二十九日讯　塔斯社莫斯科二十八日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团公告：鉴于苏联、美国、英国和法国四国政府首脑日内瓦会议已结束，苏联部长会议特向苏联最高苏维埃提出了苏联政府代表团参加上述会议的报告。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定在一九五五年八月四日召开苏联最高苏维埃会议，以便听取苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁关于日内瓦四国政府首脑会议结果的报告。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团召开会议的命令已经公布。
