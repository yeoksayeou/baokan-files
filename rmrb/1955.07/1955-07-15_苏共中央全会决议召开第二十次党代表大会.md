Title: Resolution of the CPSU Central Committee Plenum to Convene the 20th Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-07-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 14th: According to TASS, the Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, held from July 4th to 12th, passed a resolution to convene the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on February 14, 1956.

    The agenda of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union will include the following items:

    (1) Summary report of the CPSU Central Committee - Report by Comrade Khrushchev, Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee.

    (2) Summary report of the CPSU Central Auditing Commission - Report by Comrade Moskatov, Chairman of the Auditing Commission.

    (3) The 20th CPSU Congress's instructions on the Sixth Five-Year Plan (1956-1960) - Report by Comrade Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

    (4) Election of the central organs of the Party.

    The number of delegates to the 20th Congress of the CPSU is as follows: one delegate with voting rights shall be elected for every 5,000 party members; one delegate with speaking rights shall be elected for every 5,000 candidate members.

    Party conferences in states and border regions and congresses of the Communist Parties of the Union Republics, which elect delegates, are scheduled to be held from December 1955 to the first half of January 1956.

### 苏共中央全会决议召开第二十次党代表大会

1955-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央全会决议召开第二十次党代表大会
    新华社十四日讯据塔斯社莫斯科十四日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会七月四日到十二日举行的全体会议通过决议，决定在一九五六年二月十四日召开苏联共产党第二十次代表大会。
    苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的议程将有下列数项：
    （一）苏共中央委员会总结报告——报告人是苏共中央书记赫鲁晓夫同志。
    （二）苏共中央检查委员会总结报告——报告人是检查委员会主席莫斯卡托夫同志。
    （三）苏共第二十次代表大会对第六个五年计划（一九五六—一九六零年）的指示——报告人是苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁同志。
    （四）党中央机构的选举。
    出席苏共第二十次代表大会的代表名额如下：每五千名党员中选出一名有表决权的代表；每五千名候补党员中选出一名有发言权的代表。
    产生代表的州和边疆区的党代表会议和加盟共和国共产党代表大会，规定在一九五五年十二月到一九五六年一月上半月中举行。
