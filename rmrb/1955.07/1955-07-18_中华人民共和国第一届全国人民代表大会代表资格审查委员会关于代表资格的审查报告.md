Title: Report on the Examination of the Qualifications of Deputies by the Credentials Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1955-07-18
Page: 1

    The credentials of fourteen deputies to the National People's Congress had been terminated due to deaths between the first and second sessions. Three deputies have been elected to fill vacancies. Chen Shufang was elected to fill the vacancy after the death of Deputy Jia Peizhi elected from Sichuan Province. Xiong Yingdong was elected to fill the vacancy after the death of Deputy Li Xiangjiu elected from Xi'an City. Yi Xiangsu was elected to fill the vacancy after the death of Deputy Dong Chun elected from Hunan Province. The Credentials Committee has examined the qualifications of Deputies Chen Shufang, Xiong Yingdong, and Yi Xiangsu based on their election certificates and other relevant materials. The results of the examination indicate that their qualifications as deputies should be confirmed as valid. This report is now submitted to the Congress for consideration and approval.

### 中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会代表资格审查委员会关于代表资格的审查报告

1955-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会
    代表资格审查委员会关于代表资格的审查报告
    全国人民代表大会代表在第一次会议到第二次会议期间先后逝世十四人，已经补选三人。四川省选出的贾培之代表逝世后，补选了陈书舫。西安市选出的李象九代表逝世后，补选了熊应栋。湖南省选出的董纯代表逝世后，补选了易湘苏。代表资格审查委员会根据代表当选证书和其他有关材料，就陈书舫、熊应栋、易湘苏代表的资格进行了审查。审查结果，认为应该确认他们的代表资格有效。现在向大会提出报告，请予审议通过。
