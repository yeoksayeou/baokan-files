Title: Sudanese Jurist Ibrahim Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 14th – Ali Muhammad Ibrahim, a Sudanese jurist invited by the China Political Science and Law Association to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 13th.

### 苏丹法学家易卜拉欣到达北京

1955-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏丹法学家易卜拉欣到达北京
    新华社十四日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的苏丹法学家阿里·穆罕默德·易卜拉欣，已在十三日下午乘飞机到达北京。
