Title: Ngo Dinh Diem Clique Openly Opposes General Elections
Author:
Date: 1955-07-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 18th: Saigon News: The Ngo Dinh Diem regime has begun a week-long "anti-communist movement" to "condemn the Geneva Accords" and "support the policies of the Ngo Dinh Diem government."
    On the 17th, the Ngo Dinh Diem clique organized a "demonstration" in Saigon, openly clamoring against the Geneva Accords and general elections, as the beginning of this "anti-communist movement."
    The parade organized by the Ngo Dinh Diem authorities also shouted slogans attacking the International Commission when passing in front of the hotel where the personnel of the Vietnam International Commission resided.
    According to an Associated Press report, the Ngo Dinh Diem government is "taking severe measures" to suppress those who demand the peaceful reunification of the country through general elections. Detention camps are being established to imprison hundreds or thousands of people.

### 吴庭艳集团公开反对普选

1955-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    吴庭艳集团公开反对普选
    新华社河内十八日电　西贡消息：吴庭艳政权已经开始发动一个星期的“反共运动”，来“谴责日内瓦协定”和“支持吴庭艳政府的政策”。
    十七日，吴庭艳集团在西贡组织了一次“示威游行”，公开叫嚷反对日内瓦协定和普选，作为这个“反共运动”的开端。吴庭艳官方所组织的这个游行队伍在经过越南国际委员会人员居住的旅馆前，还呼喊攻击国际委员会的口号。
    据美联社消息指出，吴庭艳政府正“采取严峻的措施”，来镇压要求通过普选实现国家和平统一的人。拘留营正在建立中，以准备监禁成百或成千的人。
