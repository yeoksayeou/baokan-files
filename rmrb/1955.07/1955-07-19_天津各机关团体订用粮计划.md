Title: Tianjin Various Agencies and Organizations' Grain Ordering Plan
Author:
Date: 1955-07-19
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    Tianjin Municipal agencies, organizations, schools, factories, and other units are implementing a grain pre-budget and final accounting system in July. More than 1,100 units with a collective mess of thirty people or more have submitted their July grain purchase plans to the Grain Bureau. According to statistics from the Grain Bureau, these units' July grain purchase plans are reduced by more than 920,000 jin compared to the actual grain consumption in May. The grain consumption plans of each unit were formulated after careful calculation under the leadership of the responsible comrades of the unit.

### 天津各机关团体订用粮计划

1955-07-19
第2版()
专栏：

    天津各机关团体订用粮计划
    天津市机关、团体、学校、工厂等七月份实行用粮预决算制度。集体入伙人数在三十人以上的一千一百多个单位已把七月份的购粮计划送交粮食局。据粮食局统计，这些单位的七月份购粮计划比五月份实际用粮减少九十二万多斤。各单位的用粮计划是在本单位负责同志领导下，经过认真计算后订出的。　（新华社）
