Title: U Nu-U.S. Government Summit Concludes
Author:
Date: 1955-07-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 3rd. The summit between the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and U.S. President Dwight D. Eisenhower and Secretary of State John Foster Dulles has concluded. A joint statement issued by U Nu and Eisenhower on the 2nd stated that they "discussed many matters of common concern and exchanged views on current international problems." The joint statement also said that they "studied the issues of peace and security in Asia."
The joint statement specifically mentioned that "they frankly discussed the complex economic problems arising from the large rice surpluses available for export in both Burma—one of the world's major rice exporting countries—and the United States."
On the 1st, U Nu reiterated Burma's independent policy of non-alignment with military blocs at a luncheon at the National Press Club in the United States.

### 吴努同美政府首脑会谈结束

1955-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　吴努同美政府首脑会谈结束
    新华社三日讯　华盛顿消息：缅甸联邦总理吴努同美国总统艾森豪威尔和国务卿杜勒斯的会谈已经结束。吴努和艾森豪威尔二日发表的联合声明说，他们“讨论了许多共同有关的问题，并且对当前国际问题交换了意见”。联合声明还说，他们
  “研究了亚洲和平和安全的问题”。
    联合声明特别提到，“他们坦率地讨论了由于缅甸——世界主要大米出口国家之一——和美国国内都存在大量可以出口的大米剩余而引起的复杂的经济问题”。
    吴努一日在美国全国新闻俱乐部的午餐宴会上重申缅甸奉行不参加军事集团的独立政策。
