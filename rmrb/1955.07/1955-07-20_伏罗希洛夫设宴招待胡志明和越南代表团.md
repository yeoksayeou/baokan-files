Title: Voroshilov Hosts Banquet for Ho Chi Minh and Vietnamese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th - According to TASS, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, held a banquet in the Grand Kremlin Palace on the 18th to entertain Ho Chi Minh, President and Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and members of the Vietnamese government delegation who were visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet government.

The Vietnamese attendees at the banquet included President Ho Chi Minh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party Trường Chinh, Minister of Finance Lê Văn Hiến, Minister of Industry and Commerce Phan Anh, Minister of Education Nguyễn Văn Huyên, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry Nghiêm Xuân Yêm, Deputy Minister of the President's Office Nguyễn Duy Trinh, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ứng Văn Khiêm, Deputy Minister of Health Phạm Ngọc Thạch, and Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, Nguyễn Lương Bằng.

The Soviet attendees at the banquet included: Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Suslov, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union Pospelov and Shepilov, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the USSR Zavenyagin, Kucherenko, Lobanov, Malyshev, Tevosyan, and Khrunichev, Deputy Chairmen of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Arushanyan and Tarasov, ministers of various ministries of the USSR, Marshal of the Soviet Union Vasilevsky, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Pegov, the Soviet Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Lavrishchev, and officials from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also attending the reception was Liu Xiao, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union.

President Voroshilov and President Ho Chi Minh exchanged speeches at the banquet.

The banquet took place in a very sincere and friendly atmosphere.

### 伏罗希洛夫设宴招待胡志明和越南代表团

1955-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫设宴招待胡志明和越南代表团
    新华社十九日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫于十八日在大克里姆林宫举行宴会，招待应苏联政府邀请前来苏联访问的越南民主共和国主席兼总理胡志明和越南政府代表团人员。
    越南方面出席宴会的有胡志明主席、越南劳动党总书记长征、财政部长黎文献、工商部长潘英、教育部长阮文煊、农林部长严春庵、主席府副部长阮维祯、外交部副部长雍文谦、医济部副部长范玉石和越南民主共和国驻苏联大使阮良朋。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、苏斯洛夫，苏联共产党中央委员会书记波斯伯洛夫、谢皮洛夫，苏联部长会议副主席查文尼亚金、库切连科、洛巴诺夫、马利歇夫、捷沃西安、赫鲁尼切夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席阿鲁夏尼扬和塔拉索夫，苏联各部部长，苏联元帅华西列夫斯基，苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫，苏联驻越南民主共和国大使拉夫里谢夫以及苏联外交部负责人员等。
    出席招待会的还有中华人民共和国驻苏联大使刘晓。
    伏罗希洛夫主席和胡志明主席在宴会上相互致词。
    宴会在十分真诚和友好的气氛中进行。
