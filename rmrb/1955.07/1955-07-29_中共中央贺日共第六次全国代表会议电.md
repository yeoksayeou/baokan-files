Title: CCP Central Committee's Congratulatory Telegram to the Sixth National Congress of the Japanese Communist Party
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-29
Page: 1

    CCP Central Committee's Congratulatory Telegram to the Sixth National Congress of the Japanese Communist Party
    To the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party and to all comrades of the Sixth National Congress of the Japanese Communist Party!
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends its heartfelt, warm, and fraternal congratulations to the Sixth National Congress of the Japanese Communist Party.
    We believe that the convocation of the Sixth National Congress of the Japanese Communist Party will further strengthen the unity of the entire party, strengthen the ties between the party and the masses, consolidate and expand the national democratic united front, and achieve significant accomplishments in the struggle for independence, democracy, peace, and the protection of the people's livelihood.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
            July 28, 1955

### 中共中央贺日共第六次全国代表会议电

1955-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央贺日共第六次全国代表会议电
    日本共产党中央委员会并转日本共产党第六次全国代表会议全体同志们！
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向日本共产党第六次全国代表会议致以衷心的、热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    我们相信日本共产党第六次全国代表会议的召开，将进一步加强全党的团结、密切党与群众的关系，巩固和扩大民族民主统一战线，并在争取独立、民主、和平及保卫人民生活的斗争中，获得重大的成就。
    中国共产党中央委员会
            一九五五年七月二十八日
