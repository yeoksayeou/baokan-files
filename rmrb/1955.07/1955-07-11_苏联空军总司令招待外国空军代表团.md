Title: Soviet Air Force Commander-in-Chief Hosts Foreign Air Force Delegations
Author:
Date: 1955-07-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to TASS, the Soviet Air Force Commander-in-Chief, Air Chief Marshal Zhigarev, held a reception on the 8th to host foreign air force delegations who came to the Soviet Union to participate in Aviation Day.

    The heads and members of the air force delegations from China, India, Yugoslavia, Poland, Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, North Korea, and Romania, as well as members of the Czechoslovak Air Force delegation, attended the reception.

    Military attachés of the embassies of India, China, North Korea, Poland, Romania, Yugoslavia, and Czechoslovakia in the Soviet Union were also present at the reception.

    The Soviet side was represented by Soviet Marshals Sokolovsky and Biryuzov, senior generals of the Soviet Army, responsible persons from the Soviet Ministry of Defense and the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and members of the Soviet press.

    The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 苏联空军总司令招待外国空军代表团

1955-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联空军总司令招待外国空军代表团
    新华社九日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联空军总司令、空军主帅日加列夫在八日举行招待会，招待到苏联参加航空节的外国空军代表团。
    出席招待会的有中国、印度、南斯拉夫、波兰、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、朝鲜和罗马尼亚等国空军代表团的团长和团员，以及捷克斯洛伐克空军代表团的团员。
    出席招待会的还有印度、中国、朝鲜、波兰、罗马尼亚、南斯拉夫和捷克斯洛伐克等国驻苏联大使馆武官。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有：苏联元帅索科罗夫斯基、比留佐夫、苏军高级将领，苏联国防部和苏联外交部的负责人以及苏联新闻界人士。
    招待会在热烈、友好的气氛中进行。
