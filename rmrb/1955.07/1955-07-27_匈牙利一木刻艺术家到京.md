Title: Hungarian Woodcut Artist Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 26th. József Domján, a Hungarian woodcut artist who is visiting China according to the 1955 implementation plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing on July 24th.

### 匈牙利一木刻艺术家到京

1955-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利一木刻艺术家到京
    新华社二十六日讯　根据中匈文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来我国访问的匈牙利木刻艺术家多米扬·约瑟夫，在七月二十四日抵京。
