Title: Bulganin Hosts Banquets Separately for American and British Delegations
Author:
Date: 1955-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 21st TASS: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, hosted a banquet on the 21st to entertain the American delegation attending the Four-Power Summit Conference.

Those attending the banquet included Dulles, Anderson, Bohlen, MacArthur, Merchant, and Thompson.

The Soviet side attending the banquet included Khrushchev, Molotov, Zhukov, Gromyko, Zarubin, and Ilyichev.

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd According to TASS, Geneva: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, hosted a banquet on the 22nd to entertain the British delegation attending the Four-Power Summit Conference.

Those attending the banquet included: Eden, Macmillan, Brooke, Kirkpatrick, Hayter, and Cassia.

The Soviet side attending the banquet included: Khrushchev, Molotov, Zhukov, Gromyko, Malik, and Ilyichev.

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd, TASS, Geneva: Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov received Ambassador M. Magistrati, Director of the Political Department of the Italian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who was in Geneva for the Four-Power Summit Conference on the 22nd. (Attached picture)

The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, hosted a banquet on July 20th to entertain the American, French, and British delegations attending the Four-Power Summit Conference. The picture shows Chairman Bulganin (third from the right) talking with the heads of the delegations. From the right, the first is French Foreign Minister Pinay, the second is Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov, the fifth is British Prime Minister Eden, and the sixth is US Secretary of State Dulles. TASS Moscow fax (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 布尔加宁设宴分别招待美国和英国代表团

1955-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁设宴分别招待美国和英国代表团
    新华社二十二日讯　塔斯社日内瓦二十一日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁二十一日设宴招待出席四国政府首脑会议的美国代表团。
    出席宴会的有杜勒斯、安德逊、波伦、小麦克阿瑟、麦钱特、汤普逊。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有赫鲁晓夫、莫洛托夫、朱可夫、葛罗米柯、查鲁宾、伊利切夫。
    新华社二十三日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁二十二日设宴招待参加四国政府首脑会议的英国代表团。
    出席宴会的有：艾登、麦克米伦、布鲁克、柯克帕特里克、海特尔和卡西亚。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：赫鲁晓夫、莫洛托夫、朱可夫、葛罗米柯、马立克和伊利切夫。
    新华社二十三日讯　塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫二十二日接见了由于举行四国政府首脑会议而在日内瓦的意大利外交部政治司司长马·马吉斯特拉蒂大使。（附图片）
    苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁七月二十日设宴招待参加四国政府首脑会议的美国、法国和英国代表团。图为布尔加宁团长（右第三人）和各国代表们晤谈。右起，第一人是法国外交部长比内，第二人是苏联外交部长莫洛托夫，第五人是英国首相艾登，第六人是美国国务卿杜勒斯。塔斯社莫斯科传真稿（新华社发）
