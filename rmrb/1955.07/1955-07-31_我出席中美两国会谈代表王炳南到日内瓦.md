Title: My Representative to the Sino-American Talks, Wang Bingnan, Arrives in Geneva
Author:
Date: 1955-07-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 30th. Wang Bingnan, representative of the People's Republic of China to the Sino-American ambassadorial talks and Chinese Ambassador to Poland, arrived in Geneva on the evening of the 29th from Warsaw.

At the train station, Ambassador Wang Bingnan made a statement to dozens of journalists from various countries, saying: "I am very pleased to come to Geneva to participate in the Sino-American talks. A ceasefire in Indochina was achieved in Geneva last July. This July, the summit meeting of the four major powers was also just held here. I congratulate Geneva for its significant contribution to the cause of peace."

Wang Bingnan also said: "In order to ease and eliminate the tense situation in the Taiwan area, Premier Zhou Enlai of our country proposed at the Bandung Conference as early as April this year that the Chinese and American governments should sit down for negotiations. This time, the ambassadorial talks between the two countries, as long as both sides have the common desire and sincerity to solve the problem, we believe that results can be achieved."

The Sino-American ambassadorial talks are scheduled to begin on August 1st.

### 我出席中美两国会谈代表王炳南到日内瓦

1955-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    我出席中美两国会谈代表王炳南到日内瓦
    新华社日内瓦三十日电　中华人民共和国出席中美两国大使级会谈的代表、中国驻波兰大使王炳南二十九日晚上从华沙抵达日内瓦。
    王炳南大使在车站上向数十名各国新闻记者发表谈话说：“我很高兴到日内瓦来参加中美会谈。去年七月在日内瓦曾取得了印度支那的停战。今年七月又在这里刚举行过四大国政府首脑会议。我祝贺日内瓦对和平事业所作的重大贡献。”
    王炳南又说：“为了和缓和消除台湾地区的紧张局势，早在今年四月我国周恩来总理就在万隆会议上提出中美两国政府坐下来谈判。这次两国大使级的会谈，只要双方具有解决问题的共同愿望和诚意，我们相信是能够取得结果的。”
    中美两国大使级会谈定于八月一日开始举行。
