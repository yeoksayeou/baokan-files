Title: Member of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Czechoslovak Minister of Labor, Nosík, Dies
Author:
Date: 1955-07-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 27th: Václav Nosík, Member of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and Minister of Labor of the Czechoslovak Republic, passed away on July 22nd.

On the 26th, the working people of Czechoslovakia bid a final farewell to Nosík with deep sorrow. Czechoslovak Republic President Zápotocký, on behalf of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, the government, and all working people, delivered a report on Nosík's life and achievements at the farewell ceremony.

Nosík was one of the founders of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and had served as a member of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the Minister of Labor of Czechoslovakia for many years.

### 捷共中央委员、捷劳动部长  诺西克逝世

1955-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共中央委员、捷劳动部长
    诺西克逝世
    新华社布拉格二十七日电　捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会委员、捷克斯洛伐克共和国劳动部部长瓦茨拉夫·诺西克，在七月二十二日逝世。
    二十六日，捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民怀着悲痛的心情向诺西克最后告别。捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基代表捷克斯洛伐克共产党、政府和全体劳动人民，在告别仪式上作了关于诺西克生平事迹的报告。
    诺西克是捷克斯洛伐克共产党的建立人之一，多年来一直担任捷共中央委员和捷克斯洛伐克劳动部长的职务。
