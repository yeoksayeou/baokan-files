Title: Nehru Visits Various Places in Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1955-07-07
Page: 4
　  Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, July 5th (TASS): According to the "Borba" newspaper, Indian Prime Minister Nehru, accompanied by Yugoslav President Tito, visited the "Zenica" Metallurgical Combine and the "Jablanica" Hydroelectric Power Station in Sarajevo on the morning of the 3rd.

　  In Sarajevo, Pucar, Chairman of the People's Committee of Bosnia and Herzegovina, hosted a banquet for Nehru.

　  Nehru and Tito traveled by car to Mostar in the afternoon, and then to Dubrovnik. From there, they took the "Jadranka" speedboat to Split.

### 尼赫鲁在南斯拉夫各地参观

1955-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

　  尼赫鲁在南斯拉夫各地参观
    新华社五日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德讯：据“战斗报”报道，印度总理尼赫鲁三日上午在南斯拉夫总统铁托陪同下，参观了塞拉热窝的“塞尼查”冶金联合厂和“雅布拉尼查”水电站。
    在塞拉热窝，波斯尼亚—黑塞哥维纳人民委员会主席普卡尔设宴招待尼赫鲁。
    尼赫鲁和铁托在下午乘汽车前往摩斯塔，随后到杜布罗夫尼克，他们从那里乘“雅德朗卡”快艇前往斯普利特。
