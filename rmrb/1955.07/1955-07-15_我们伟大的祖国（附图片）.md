Author:
Date: 1955-07-15
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In Wuqia County, Xinjiang Province, during the lambing and lamb-rearing period, the Kirgiz herdsmen organized temporary mutual aid teams, allocating manpower to wolf hunting and sideline production. The herdsmen who practice collective living and mutual assistance drive their flocks to the grasslands early in the morning.

### 我们伟大的祖国（附图片）

1955-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆省乌恰县柯尔克孜族牧民在接羔、育羔期间组织了临时互助组，抽出人力从事打狼和副业生产。实行集居互助的牧民们，一清早就把羊群赶到草原上去。
