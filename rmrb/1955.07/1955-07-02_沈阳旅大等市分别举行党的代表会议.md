Title: Party Representative Meetings Held in Shenyang, Lüda and Other Cities Respectively
Author:
Date: 1955-07-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 1st - The cities of Shenyang, Lüda, Fushun, Benxi, and others in Liaoning Province have recently held representative meetings of the Communist Party of China.

The representatives attending the meetings in each city unanimously endorsed the various reports and resolutions of the Party's National Representative Conference and expressed great indignation at the criminal acts of the Gao Gang-Rao Shushi anti-Party alliance.

The Party representative meetings in each city fully promoted the spirit of democracy, carried out criticism and self-criticism, and conducted a strict examination of the leadership and work of Party organizations at all levels. The representatives attending the Shenyang City meeting examined and criticized the criminal activities of the Gao Gang anti-Party clique, reviewed the widespread complacency, and the serious damage that this complacency had brought to various tasks. Lüda City exposed the influence of the Gao Gang anti-Party clique on the Lüda Party organization, focusing on criticizing certain existing phenomena of disunity. Representatives from Fushun and Benxi also examined and criticized the impact of the criminal activities of the Gao Gang anti-Party clique on individual departments and individuals.

The Party representative meetings in each city believe that in order to guarantee the realization of the state's first Five-Year Plan and the various tasks for 1955, Party organizations at all levels must continue to eliminate the pernicious influence of the Gao Gang-Rao Shushi anti-Party alliance, enhance revolutionary vigilance, further strengthen the Party's ideological and organizational construction, enhance Party unity, strengthen the Party's political and ideological leadership in various tasks, give play to the role of Party organizations, and ensure the correct implementation and thorough implementation of the Party's policies.

The Party representative meetings in each city unanimously supported the policy determined by the Party Central Committee to focus on the development of heavy industry as the key to economic construction. Shenyang, Lüda, Fushun, and Benxi are important heavy industrial cities in our country, and therefore it is even more important to increase production with the greatest effort, practice strict economy, and ensure the comprehensive overfulfillment of the national economic construction plan. The Party representative meetings in each city all believe that it is necessary to further publicize the great significance of increasing production and practicing economy, opposing waste, to the working class and the broad masses of the people, so that the movement to increase production, practice economy, and oppose waste is carried out more deeply. The CPC Shenyang City Representative Meeting also proposed that in the future it is necessary to establish the idea of comprehensively completing the plan and the spirit of increasing production and practicing economy among all cadres and workers, earnestly learn from the Soviet Union, implement expert suggestions, earnestly establish the management thinking of socialist enterprises, strengthen technical management, and improve the technical and management level of enterprises.

### 沈阳旅大等市分别举行党的代表会议

1955-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　沈阳旅大等市分别举行党的代表会议
    新华社沈阳一日电　辽宁省沈阳、旅大、抚顺、本溪等市最近先后举行了中国共产党的代表会议。
    各城市出席会议的代表，一致拥护党的全国代表会议的各项报告和决议，对高岗、饶漱石反党联盟的罪恶行为表示极大的愤慨。
    各市的党代表会议充分发扬了民主精神，开展了批评和自我批评，对各级党组织的领导和工作进行了严格的检查。沈阳市到会代表对高岗反党宗派的罪恶活动进行了检查和批判，检讨了普遍存在的自满情绪，以及这种自满情绪给各种工作带来的严重损害。旅大市揭露了高岗反党宗派对旅大党组织的影响，着重批判了当前存在的某些不团结现象。抚顺和本溪市的代表们，也检查、批判了高岗反党宗派的罪恶活动给个别部门和个人的影响。
    各市党的代表会议认为：为了保证实现国家第一个五年计划和一九五五年的各项任务，各级党的组织必须继续肃清高岗、饶漱石反党联盟的罪恶影响，提高革命警惕，进一步加强党的思想建设和组织建设，增强党的团结，加强党在各项工作中的政治思想领导，发挥党组织的作用，保证党的方针政策的正确执行和贯彻。
    各市党的代表会议一致拥护党中央确定的以发展重工业为经济建设重点的方针。沈阳、旅大、抚顺、本溪是我国重要的重工业城市，因此更要以最大的努力增加生产，厉行节约，保证国家经济建设计划全面地超额完成。各市党的代表会议都认为：要进一步向工人阶级和广大人民宣传增产节约、反对浪费的重大意义，使增加生产，厉行节约，反对浪费的运动更深入的开展。中共沈阳市代表会议还提出今后要在全体干部和工人中树立全面完成计划的思想和增产节约的精神，认真学习苏联，贯彻专家建议，切实地树立起社会主义企业的管理思想，加强技术管理，提高企业的技术水平和管理水平。
