Title: Prime Minister U Nu to Visit the Soviet Union This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-07-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, July 26th, TASS: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu said at a press conference on the 26th that he intends to visit the Soviet Union this year.

### 吴努总理将在今年内访苏

1955-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努总理将在今年内访苏
    新华社二十七日讯　塔斯社仰光二十六日讯：缅甸总理吴努二十六日在记者招待会上说，他打算在今年访问苏联。
