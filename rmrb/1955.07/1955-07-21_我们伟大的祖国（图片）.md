Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-07-21
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The main bridge project of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge has officially started. All the pipe piles for the No. 1 and No. 2 piers of the main bridge have been driven, and the construction of the No. 8 pier has also begun. A tugboat is towing the coffer dam of the No. 8 pier to the Yangtze River Bridge construction site, preparing to sink it.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    武汉长江大桥正桥工程已正式开工，正桥的一、二号桥墩管桩已全部打好，八号桥墩工程也已开始施工。拖轮正将八号桥墩的围囹拖向长江大桥工地，准备下沉。
