Title: French Ambassador to the Soviet Union Holds National Day Reception; Bulganin, Kaganovich and Others Attend
Author:
Date: 1955-07-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 15th: French Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Louis Joxe, held a reception on the 14th to celebrate the French National Day.
    Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Molotov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, and Khrushchev attended the reception. Also present were Shepilov, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Soviet Ministers Kabanov and Mikhailov, Soviet Marshals, as well as heads of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of Foreign Trade, Ministry of Defense, the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and representatives of the press.
    Also attending the reception were diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union.

### 法驻苏大使举行国庆招待会  布尔加宁卡冈诺维奇等出席

1955-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

    法驻苏大使举行国庆招待会
    布尔加宁卡冈诺维奇等出席
    新华社十五日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：法国驻苏联大使路易·约克斯在十四日举行招待会，庆祝法国国庆节。
    布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、莫洛托夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛和赫鲁晓夫出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有苏联最高苏维埃民族院外交委员会主席谢皮洛夫，苏联部长卡巴诺夫和米哈伊诺夫，苏联元帅，以及苏联外交部、对外贸易部、国防部、苏联对外文化协会负责人和新闻界代表。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻苏联的外交使节。
