Title: Second Session of the National People's Congress Begins Group Discussions on Draft Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1955-07-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th—The Second Session of the First National People's Congress began group discussions today on the Draft First Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy of the People's Republic of China.

　　The State Council of the People's Republic of China dispatched a large number of responsible staff members to the representative groups to provide consultation and answer questions for the representatives during the representative group discussions. The number of these staff members totaled more than two hundred and eighty.

　　Today, each representative group began discussing the preamble part of the Draft Five-Year Plan, the task part of the First Five-Year Plan, and the investment allocation and production target part of the First Five-Year Plan. The representatives' enthusiasm for discussion was very high. In each group meeting venue, the representatives carefully read the documents and made speeches despite the intense heat. During the discussion, the responsible staff members of the State Council who attended the meeting gave detailed answers to the questions raised by the representatives.

### 全国人民代表大会第二次会议　开始分组讨论五年计划草案

1955-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会第二次会议
　　开始分组讨论五年计划草案
    新华社七日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议今日开始分组讨论中华人民共和国发展国民经济的第一个五年计划草案。
    在代表小组进行讨论时，中华人民共和国国务院派出大批负责工作人员到各代表小组备代表们咨询和解答问题。这批工作人员为数共达二百八十多人。
    各代表小组今天对五年计划草案的绪言部分、第一个五年计划的任务部分、第一个五年计划的投资分配和生产指标部分开始进行讨论。代表们讨论的情绪非常热烈。在各个小组会场里，代表们冒着酷暑认真地阅读文件和发言。在讨论中，到会的国务院负责工作人员对代表们提出的问题都作了详尽的解答。
