Title: Sixty-Six Countries to Participate in International Conference on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-07-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 15th—The United Nations Office in Geneva announced that sixty-six countries have accepted invitations to participate the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy, which will be held in Geneva from August 8th to August 20th. These include Albania, Austria, Burma, Bulgaria, the United States, France, the United Kingdom, Hungary, India, Indonesia, Japan, Poland, the Federal Republic of Germany, Byelorussia, Ukraine, Romania, Switzerland, the Soviet Union, Yugoslavia, etc.
　　The governments of these countries that accepted the invitations have received abstracts of 1,084 scientific reports submitted to the conference for study by thirty-three countries and the World Health Organization, the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations, the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization, and the United Nations itself.
　　The preparatory work for the conference is being carried out under the guidance of seventeen representatives from various countries, headed by Professor Whitman (USA), Secretary-General of the International Committee for the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy, and his assistant V. Vavilov (USSR).

### 六十六国将参加和平利用原子能国际会议

1955-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

    六十六国将参加和平利用原子能国际会议
    新华社十五日讯　塔斯社日内瓦十四日讯：日内瓦联合国办事处宣布，六十六个国家已经接受邀请参加将于八月八日到八月二十日在日内瓦举行的和平利用原子能国际会议，其中有阿尔巴尼亚、奥地利、缅甸、保加利亚、美国、法国、英国、匈牙利、印度、印度尼西亚、日本、波兰、德意志联邦共和国、白俄罗斯、乌克兰、罗马尼亚、瑞士、苏联、南斯拉夫等国。
    接受邀请的这些国家政府已经收到了三十三个国家和世界卫生组织、联合国粮食及农业组织、联合国教育、科学及文化组织以及联合国组织本身提交会议研究的一千零八十四项科学报告的提要。
    会议的筹备工作在以和平利用原子能国际委员会秘书长惠特曼教授（美国）和他的助手维·瓦维洛夫（苏联）为首的各国十七名代表的指导下进行。
