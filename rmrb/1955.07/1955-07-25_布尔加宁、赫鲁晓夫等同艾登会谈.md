Title: Bulganin, Khrushchev, and Others Meet with Eden

Author: 
Date: 1955-07-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 24th: TASS, Geneva, July 24th: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Molotov, held talks with British Prime Minister Eden on the evening of the 23rd.

The British side also included British Foreign Secretary Macmillan, British Ambassador to the Soviet Union Hayter, and Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, Cassia.

The Soviet side also included Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom, Malik.

### 布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫等同艾登会谈

1955-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫等同艾登会谈
    新华社二十四日讯　塔斯社日内瓦二十四日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫和苏联外交部长莫洛托夫在二十三日晚间同英国首相艾登举行会谈。
    英国方面参加会谈的还有英国外交大臣麦克米伦、英国驻苏联大使海特尔和外交部次官卡西亚。
    苏联方面参加会谈的还有苏联驻英国大使马立克。
