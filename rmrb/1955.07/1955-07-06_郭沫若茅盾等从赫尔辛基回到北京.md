Title: Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, and Others Return to Beijing from Helsinki
Author:
Date: 1955-07-06
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 5th: Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, Mao Dun, head of the Chinese delegation to the Helsinki World Peace Conference, deputy heads Chen Shutong and Liao Chengzhi, Secretary-General Liu Ningyi, and a portion of the delegation members returned to Beijing by plane on the morning of the 5th.

### 郭沫若茅盾等从赫尔辛基回到北京

1955-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若茅盾等从赫尔辛基回到北京
    新华社五日讯　出席赫尔辛基世界和平大会的世界和平理事会副主席郭沫若、中国代表团团长茅盾、副团长陈叔通、廖承志、秘书长刘宁一和一部分团员在五日上午乘飞机回到北京。
