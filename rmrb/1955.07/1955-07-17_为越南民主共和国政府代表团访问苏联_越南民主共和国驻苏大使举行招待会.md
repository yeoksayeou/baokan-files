Title: The Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union Hosts Reception for the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's Visit to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th: According to TASS Moscow, Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, held a reception on the 15th for the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam headed by President Ho Chi Minh's visit to the Soviet Union.

    Among those present at the reception were Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and members of the delegation: Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Labor Party, Le Van Hien, Minister of Finance, Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce, Nguyen Van Huyên, Minister of Education, Nghiem Xuan Yem, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry, Nguyen Duy Trinh, Deputy Minister of the President's Office, Ung Van Khiem, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Pham Ngoc Thach, Deputy Minister of Medical and Social Affairs.

    The Soviet side was represented by Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Khrushchev, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Kucherenko, Lobanov and Tevosyan, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation Tarasov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation Puzanov, Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the Soviet Union Baibakov, Soviet ministers, marshals of the Soviet Union, senior officials from various ministries, representatives of the Soviet public, and Soviet and foreign newspaper reporters.

    Foreign envoys to the Soviet Union present at the reception included the ambassadors of Burma, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, India, Czechoslovakia, Mongolia, Albania, Yugoslavia, Indonesia, Hungary, the People's Republic of China, and Finland, the ministers of Ethiopia, Syria, and Lebanon, and the chargés d'affaires ad interim of Iran, Egypt, Afghanistan, the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, Romania, and Poland, and the counselor of the French Embassy.

    At the reception, Ambassador Nguyen Luong Bang and Chairman Bulganin exchanged speeches.

    The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 为越南民主共和国政府代表团访问苏联  越南民主共和国驻苏大使举行招待会

1955-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    为越南民主共和国政府代表团访问苏联
    越南民主共和国驻苏大使举行招待会
    新华社十六日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：越南民主共和国驻苏联特命全权大使阮良朋为以胡志明主席为首的越南民主共和国政府代表团访问苏联，于十五日举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有越南民主共和国主席胡志明和代表团团员：越南劳动党总书记长征、财政部长黎文献、工商部长潘英、教育部长阮文煊、农林部长严春庵、主席府副部长阮维祯、外交部副部长雍文谦和医济部副部长范玉石。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、赫鲁晓夫、苏联部长会议副主席库切连科、洛巴诺夫和捷沃西安、俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫、俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席普札诺夫、苏联国家计划委员会主席巴伊巴科夫、苏联部长、苏联元帅、一些部的高级官员、苏联公众代表、苏联和外国报纸的记者。
    出席招待会的外国驻苏使节有：缅甸、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、印度、捷克斯洛伐克、蒙古、阿尔巴尼亚、南斯拉夫、印度尼西亚、匈牙利、中华人民共和国、芬兰等国的大使，埃塞俄比亚、叙利亚、黎巴嫩等国的公使，伊朗、埃及、阿富汗、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、波兰等国的临时代办和法国大使馆的参赞。
    在招待会上，阮良朋大使和布尔加宁主席相互致词。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
