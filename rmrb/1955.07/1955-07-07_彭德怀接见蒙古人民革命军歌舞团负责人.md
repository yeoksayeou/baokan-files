Title: Peng Dehuai Receives Leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-07-07
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, received Major General Damdin Hù, leader and head of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Troupe, and Deputy Heads Colonel Qīndí and Lieutenant Colonel Nàmùjílā Dào'ěrdjí, on the afternoon of the 6th.
    Present at the reception were Huang Kecheng, Vice Minister of National Defense; Su Yu, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense; and Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 彭德怀接见蒙古人民革命军歌舞团负责人

1955-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　彭德怀接见蒙古人民革命军歌舞团负责人
    新华社六日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀，在六日下午接见了蒙古人民革命军歌舞团领队兼团长达姆丁呼少将，副团长钦迪上校、那木吉拉道尔吉中校。
    接见时在座的，有国防部副部长黄克诚，中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕，总政治部副主任萧华，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣，中国人民解放军总政治部文化部部长陈沂。
