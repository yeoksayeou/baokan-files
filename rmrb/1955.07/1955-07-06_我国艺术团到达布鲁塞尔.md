Title: Chinese Art Troupe Arrives in Brussels
Author:
Date: 1955-07-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 5th - Brussels News: After staying in Paris for more than a month, the Chinese Art Troupe arrived in Brussels from Paris on the 3rd for a performance tour.
	On the 4th, the Mayor of Brussels, Mr. Van den Eynde, received the head of the Chinese Art Troupe, Zhang Zhixiang, and the main performers of the troupe. He welcomed the art troupe's performance in Brussels and hoped that this performance would enable the Belgian people to further understand China and thus promote the friendship between the two peoples.
	Zhang Zhixiang expressed his heartfelt thanks to the Mayor for his friendly reception, and congratulated the growing friendship and increasing cultural exchange between the two peoples. Zhang Zhixiang also presented gifts to Mayor Van den Eynde.

### 我国艺术团到达布鲁塞尔

1955-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国艺术团到达布鲁塞尔
    新华社布拉格五日电 布鲁塞尔消息：中国艺术团在巴黎逗留了一个多月之后，已经在三日从巴黎到达布鲁塞尔作访问演出。
    四日，布鲁塞尔市市长格洛克接见了中国艺术团团长张致祥和艺术团的主要演员。他对艺术团到布鲁塞尔演出表示欢迎，并且希望这次演出会使比利时人民进一步了解中国，从而促进两国人民的友谊。
    张致祥对市长的友好接待表示衷心感谢，并且祝贺两国人民的友谊日益发展，文化交流日益增进。张致祥并且向格洛克市长赠送礼物。
