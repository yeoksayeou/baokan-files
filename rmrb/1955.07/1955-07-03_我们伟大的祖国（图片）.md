Author:
Date: 1955-07-03
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Many higher primary school and junior high school graduates in our country are serving the socialist construction cause of the country. The three female electric locomotive drivers at the Lingshi Shaft of Shuangyashan are all higher primary school graduates. They mastered the technology of driving electric locomotives after studying at the Beipiao Coal Mine for four months. This is a picture of them happily coming out of the coal mine.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国许多高小、初中毕业生正在为国家的社会主义建设事业服务。双鸭山岭西竖井的三位女电机车司机都是高小毕业生，她们在北票煤矿学习了四个月就掌握了开电机车的技术。这是她们愉快地走出煤井的情形。
