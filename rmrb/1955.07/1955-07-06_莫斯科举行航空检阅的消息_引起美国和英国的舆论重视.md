Title: The News of the Moscow Air Show Arouses Attention from American and British Public Opinion
Author:
Date: 1955-07-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 5th: The news that Moscow held an air show on Soviet Aviation Day has aroused great attention in American and British public opinion.

　　New York newspapers prominently published reports on the Moscow air show and the opinions of former US Air Force Secretary Symington on the 5th. The "New York Herald Tribune" published a United Press International dispatch in a headline position, saying: "During this largest display of strength in Soviet aviation history, two new types of fighter jets and long-range jet bombers flew over Moscow." A UPI Washington dispatch said that the US Department of Defense might issue a comment after studying the relevant news. Former US Air Force Secretary Symington said: "It is now clear that we have lost some of the air supremacy we previously thought belonged to us." The UPI dispatch described the air show as "the largest demonstration in the history of the Soviet Air Force." It said: "This sensational demonstration is a warning that in many aspects of modern aircraft design, Russia may have caught up with, or perhaps surpassed, the United States."
　　
　　The "New York Times" published a report from its Moscow correspondent, Daniel, in a headline position. This report described the air show in detail and pointed out that the performance of a jet airliner "seems to prove that the Soviet Union has greatly surpassed the United States in the design and production of jet airliners."
　　
　　British newspapers also paid close attention to the various types of aircraft appearing in the air show, especially jet aircraft, when reporting on the Moscow air show. A reporter for the London "News Chronicle" pointed out that the appearance of so many types of aircraft in the air show surprised foreign spectators.
　　
　　Jean Brelot, the chief accountant of the International Aviation Federation who participated in the air show, said to a "Daily Mail" reporter by telephone: "I have never seen such a majestic air power display."

### 莫斯科举行航空检阅的消息　引起美国和英国的舆论重视

1955-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科举行航空检阅的消息
  　引起美国和英国的舆论重视
    新华社五日讯　　据塔斯社报道：莫斯科在苏联航空节那天举行航空检阅的消息，引起了美国和英国舆论界的重视。
    纽约各报五日以显著地位刊载了关于莫斯科举行航空检阅的报道和前美国空军部长薛明顿的意见。“纽约先驱论坛报”以头栏地位刊载合众社的消息说：“在这次苏联航空史上最大一次显示力量的时候，莫斯科上空飞过的有两种新型的战斗机和远程喷气式轰炸机。”合众社华盛顿消息说，美国国防部要等研究了有关消息后也许会发表评论。前美国空军部长薛明顿说：“现在很明显，我们已经丧失了过去认为属于我们的空中霸权的一部分。”合众社的消息把这次航空检阅说成是“苏联空军史上最大的示威”。它说：“这次耸动听闻的示威是一个警告：在现代飞机的设计的许多方面，俄国可能已经赶上了、或许已经超过了美国。”
      “纽约时报”以头栏地位刊载了它的驻莫斯科记者丹尼尔的报道。这篇报道详细地描写了检阅的情况，并且指出，一种喷气式客机的表演“看来证明，在喷气式客机的设计和生产方面，苏联已经大大地超过了美国”。
    英国报纸在报道关于莫斯科举行航空检阅的消息时，也非常注意检阅中出现的各种类型的飞机，特别是喷气式飞机。伦敦“新闻纪事报”的记者指出，在检阅中，出现了这样多类型的飞机，这使外国观众感到惊奇。
    曾参加这次检阅的国际航空联合会总会计让·布列利奥在电话中对“每日邮报”的记者说：“我从来没有看到过这样威武的航空力量的检阅”。
