Title: Peng Dehuai Sends Telegram Congratulating Railway Corps on Completion of Lizhan Railway
Author:
Date: 1955-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 6th. Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Peng Dehuai, sent a telegram on the 4th to Wang Zhen, Commander of the Railway Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and all officers and soldiers, congratulating them on the completion of the Lizhan Railway. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

Commander Wang Zhen and all comrades of the Railway Corps:

In the arduous work of building the Lizhan Railway, all comrades of the Railway Corps displayed a high degree of patriotic labor enthusiasm and creativity. With the active participation and support of the people across the country, especially the people of South China, and the enthusiastic assistance of Soviet experts, the construction task was finally completed six months ahead of schedule, providing favorable conditions for consolidating our national defense and enlivening our foreign trade. I hereby send a telegram of congratulations, and hope that all comrades will continue their efforts, guard against arrogance and impatience, and continue to work hard to complete the glorious task of building the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway.

　　　　　　　　　　　　Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 4, 1955

Xinhua News Agency, July 6th. The Lizhan Railway Inauguration Celebration Conference telegraphed Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai on July 1st, reporting the official opening of the Lizhan Railway. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

Minister Peng of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China:

The Railway Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, acting on the orders of the government, united with the people of South China and built the Litang-Zhanjiang Railway with a spirit of courageous labor. Encouraged by the people of the whole country carrying out socialist construction and the struggle to liberate Taiwan, they completed the construction task half a year ahead of schedule and officially opened to traffic on the 34th anniversary of the birth of the great Party. As we celebrate the opening with joy and excitement, we would like to extend our warm congratulations and our highest respect to you.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Lizhan Railway Inauguration Celebration Conference
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 1, 1955

### 彭德怀电贺铁道兵修通黎湛铁路

1955-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　彭德怀电贺铁道兵修通黎湛铁路
    新华社六日讯　中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀四日打电报给中国人民解放军铁道兵司令员王震和全体指战员，祝贺黎湛铁路修建完成。电报全文如下：王震司令员并转铁道兵全体同志：
    在建筑黎湛铁路的艰苦工作中，铁道兵全体同志发扬了高度的爱国主义劳动热情和创造精神，在全国人民特别是华南人民的积极参加支援和苏联专家的热忱帮助下，终于提前六个月完成了施工任务，对巩固我国国防、活跃我国对外贸易提供了有利条件。特电祝贺，并望全体同志再接再厉，戒骄戒躁，为完成建筑鹰潭至厦门铁路的光荣任务而继续努力。
                      国防部长彭德怀
                            一九五五年七月四日
    新华社六日讯　黎湛铁路通车庆祝大会七月一日
　　打电报给国防部部长彭德怀报告黎湛铁路正式通车。电报全文如下：中华人民共和国国防部彭部长：
    中国人民解放军铁道兵执行政府的命令，与华南人民团结一致，以奋勇劳动的精神修建黎塘到湛江的铁路，在全国人民进行社会主义建设和一定要解放台湾的斗争的鼓舞下，提前半年完成了施工任务，于伟大的党的诞生三十四周年纪念日正式通车。当我们以欢欣鼓舞的心情庆祝通车的时候，谨向您致以热烈的祝贺并致以崇高的敬意。
                黎湛铁路通车庆祝大会
                        一九五五年七月一日
