Title: Mongolian Military Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Shanghai and Performs
Author:
Date: 1955-07-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai: The Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Troupe, headed by Major General Dunjrub Damdin Hoshuu, arrived in Shanghai on the morning of the 18th. More than 900 people, including representatives from the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, Shanghai's literary and art workers, and people from all walks of life, greeted the song and dance troupe at the Shanghai Railway Station. A grand welcoming ceremony was held in the station square by people from all walks of life in Shanghai. Yang Jin, Director of the Cultural Department of the Political Department of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Zhong Wangyang, Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Culture, delivered welcome speeches on behalf of the troops stationed in Shanghai and the people of Shanghai.

    Major General Damdin Hoshuu delivered a reply.

    On the evening of the 18th, Major General Damdin Hoshuu and all members of the troupe attended a welcome banquet hosted by Liao Rongbiao, Deputy Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Liu Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai.

    That evening, Major General Damdin Hoshuu and all members of the troupe also attended a reception and watched the Peking Opera "Shou Guan Sheng," "Picking up the Jade Bracelet," and an acrobatic performance.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 20th: On the evening of the 19th, the Shanghai Cultural Square, which can accommodate more than 14,000 people, was packed with crowds who forgot the midsummer heat to watch the performance of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Troupe.

    The song and dance troupe performed a total of sixteen programs. These songs and dances with distinct national characteristics were warmly praised by the audience.

    The following attended the performance on the 19th: Chen Pixian, Second Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Wei Wenbo, Deputy Secretary; Liao Rongbiao, Deputy Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Xu Jianguo, Liu Jiping, and Song Richang, Deputy Mayors of Shanghai; literary and art workers from the troops and Shanghai; and people from all walks of life.

    Consuls and consulate staff from various countries stationed in Shanghai also attended the performance.

### 蒙军歌舞团到达上海并举行演出

1955-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙军歌舞团到达上海并举行演出
    据新华社上海电　以敦儒布·达姆丁呼少将为首的蒙古人民革命军歌舞团，十八日上午到达上海。在上海车站迎候歌舞团的有中国人民解放军驻沪部队和上海市文艺工作者及各界人士等九百多人。上海各界人士在车站广场上举行了盛大的欢迎仪式。中国人民解放军上海警备政治部文化部部长杨进和上海市文化局副局长锺望阳代表驻沪部队和上海市人民致欢迎词。
    达姆丁呼少将致答词。
    十八日晚，达姆丁呼少将和全体团员出席了中国人民解放军上海警备区副司令员廖容标、上海市副市长刘季平的欢迎宴会。
    当晚，达姆丁呼少将和全体团员还出席招待晚会，观看了京剧“收关胜”“拾玉镯”和杂技表演。
    据新华社上海二十日电　十九日晚上，可容一万四千多人的上海文化广场挤得密密层层，人们忘记了盛夏的酷热来观看蒙古人民革命军歌舞团的表演。
    歌舞团一共演出了十六个节目，这些富有鲜明的民族色彩的歌舞得到观众热烈的赞赏。
    出席十九日表演会的有：中共上海市委员会第二书记陈丕显、副书记魏文伯，中国人民解放军上海警备区副司令员廖容标，上海市副市长许建国、刘季平、宋日昌、部队和上海市的文艺工作者及各界人民。
    各国驻上海领事及领事馆人员也出席了表演会。
