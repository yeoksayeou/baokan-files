Title: After Attending the World Mothers' Congress, Some Representatives of Our Delegation Returned to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th. After attending the World Mothers' Congress, Li Dequan, the head of the Chinese delegation, and most of the representatives of the delegation returned to Beijing by plane in succession from the 21st to the 24th. The remaining representatives will return to Beijing by train at the end of the month.

### 出席世界母亲大会后  我代表团部分代表回到北京

1955-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界母亲大会后
    我代表团部分代表回到北京
    新华社二十五日讯　出席世界母亲大会的中国代表团团长李德全和代表团的大部分代表已在二十一日到二十四日陆续乘飞机回到北京。其余代表将乘火车在月底回到北京。
