Title: Vice Premier He Long Presents Gifts to the Polish State Council
Author:
Date: 1955-07-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 29th: He Long, head of the Chinese government delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council, who was invited to attend the closing ceremony of the 10th anniversary of the founding of People's Poland, recently presented gifts to the State Council of the People's Republic of Poland. The gifts included a carved lacquer vase and a pair of ivory carved boxes with the story of Mulan engraved on them.

The Polish State Council decided to hand over these gifts to the Warsaw National Museum for exhibition.

### 贺龙副总理向波兰国务委员会赠礼

1955-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理向波兰国务委员会赠礼
    新华社华沙二十九日电　应邀参加人民波兰成立十周年纪念年闭幕典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长、国务院副总理贺龙日前向波兰人民共和国国务委员会赠送了礼品，礼品中有一个雕漆花瓶，一对象牙雕盒，上面刻着花木兰的故事。
    波兰国务委员会决定把这些礼品交给华沙国家博物馆展览。
