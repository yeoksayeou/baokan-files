Title: Indonesian President Sukarno Departs for Mecca on Pilgrimage
Author:
Date: 1955-07-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 18th - Indonesian President Sukarno left Jakarta on the morning of the 18th to go on a pilgrimage to Mecca, Saudi Arabia. Accompanying him on the pilgrimage were Deputy Prime Minister Zainal Arifin, Minister of Religious Affairs K.H. Masjkur, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Abdulgani, and Commander of the 4th Military Region, Baron, who served as the president's military attache.

Those who went to the airport to see him off included Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo and cabinet ministers, Army Chief of Staff Bambang Utojo, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Indonesia.

President Sukarno delivered a short speech at the airport. He said that due to recent domestic problems, his long-planned pilgrimage had been delayed by a week. He said that now he could leave with peace of mind. Sukarno said, "The current domestic situation allows me to leave. This is due to the spirit of mutual understanding, tolerance, and willingness to negotiate among all of us." He hoped that during his absence, everyone would jointly maintain Indonesia's peace and security.

President Sukarno is scheduled to return home on August 5th.

### 印度尼西亚总统苏加诺启程赴麦加朝圣

1955-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总统苏加诺启程赴麦加朝圣
    新华社雅加达十八日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在十八日上午离开雅加达前往沙特阿拉伯的麦加朝圣。随同前往朝圣的有副总理蔡纳·阿里芬、宗教部长凯伊·马斯古、外交部秘书长阿卜杜加尼以及担任总统随从武官的第四军区司令巴伦。
    前往机场欢送的有印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约和内阁各部部长、陆军参谋长班邦·乌多约以及各国驻印度尼西亚外交使节。
    苏加诺总统在机场发表了简短谈话。他说，由于最近的国内问题，他的计划已久的朝圣延迟了一星期。他说，现在他能够安心地离开了。苏加诺说，“目前的国内局势允许我离开。这是由于在我们大家之间存在着互相接近、容忍和愿意协商的精神。”他希望在他离国期间，大家共同维护印度尼西亚的平静和安全。
    苏加诺总统预定在八月五日回国。
