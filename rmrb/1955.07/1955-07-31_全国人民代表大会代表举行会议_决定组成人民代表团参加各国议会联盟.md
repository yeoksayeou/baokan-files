Title: National People's Congress Deputies Hold Meeting Deciding to Form People's Delegation to Participate in Inter-Parliamentary Union

Author:

Date: 1955-07-31
Page: 2

The one thousand and ninety deputies attending the Second Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, currently being held in Beijing, held a meeting on the morning of the 30th. The meeting studied the statutes of the Inter-Parliamentary Union and decided to form a "People's Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Inter-Parliamentary Union" to join the Inter-Parliamentary Union.

The entire body of representatives present unanimously elected a presidium composed of fifteen people, including Song Qingling, Dong Biwu, Lin Boqu, Peng Zhen, Lin Feng, Li Jishen, Cheng Qian, Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Huang Yanpei, Li Zhuchen, Ma Yinchu, Chen Jiageng, Saifuding, and Liao Chengzhi.

Lin Boqu, on behalf of the meeting, introduced the organization and work of the Inter-Parliamentary Union and the preparations for the establishment of the "People's Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Inter-Parliamentary Union."

The meeting adopted the resolution on the establishment of the People's Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Inter-Parliamentary Union and the charter of the People's Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Inter-Parliamentary Union.

The meeting elected the Executive Committee of the People's Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Inter-Parliamentary Union. The results of the election were: Peng Zhen as Chairman of the People's Delegation, Cheng Qian, Lin Feng, Saifuding, Chen Jiageng, Zhang Bojun, Ma Yinchu, Li Zhuchen, Liao Chengzhi and eight others as Vice-Chairmen, Wu Kejian as Secretary-General, Wang Yunsheng, Shi Liang, Zhu Xuefan, Wu Yuzhang, Wu Yifang, Song Qingling, Li Yimang, Li Siguang, Li Chunqing, Li Xuefeng, Li Weihan, Li Dequan, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Shen Yanbing, Xing Xiping, Zhou Jianren, Zhou Gengsheng, Qu Wu, Lin Boqu, Shao Lizi, Hou Debang, Hu Yuzhi, Hu Yaobang, Ulanhu, Ma Xulun, Gao Chongmin, Qu Tangliang, Zhang Zhizhong, Zhang Wentian, Zhang Nanxian, Zhang Yun, Xu Guangping, Xu Deheng, Guo Moruo, Chen Shaoxian, Chen Qiyou, Chen Shutong, Chen Qiyuan, Chen Shaokuan, Fu Zuoyi, Xiraojiacuo, Peng Zemin, Huang Yanpei, Huang Shaohong, Huang Qixiang, Dapusheng, Rong Yiren, Xiong Kewu, Zhao Puchu, Liu Changsheng, Liu Ningyi, Pan Shu, Cai Chang, Jiang Guangnai, Jiang Nanxiang, Deng Yingchao, Lai Ruoyu, Qian Duansheng, Burhan, Luo Longji and sixty-one others (listed in order of the number of strokes in their surnames) as members of the Executive Committee.

Note: The Inter-Parliamentary Union is an international organization of parliamentarians from various countries. It was established in 1889. On June 29th and July 2nd of this year, the Soviet Union, Ukraine, and Belarus successively decided to join the Inter-Parliamentary Union. On July 24th, one month before the 44th conference of the Union was to be held on August 25th, Peng Zhen, Secretary-General of the Second Session of the First National People's Congress of our country, sent telegrams to Lord Stansgate, Chairman of the Inter-Parliamentary Union, Mr. Blonay, Secretary-General, and Mr. Fagerholm, Chairman of the Organizing Committee of the 44th conference of the Inter-Parliamentary Union, notifying them that the deputies of the National People's Congress of our country would form a People's Delegation to participate in the Inter-Parliamentary Union and would send representatives to attend the 44th conference of the Union. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国人民代表大会代表举行会议  决定组成人民代表团参加各国议会联盟

1955-07-31
第2版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会代表举行会议
    决定组成人民代表团参加各国议会联盟
    出席正在北京举行的中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议的一千零九十名代表，三十日上午举行会议。会议研究了各国议会联盟章程，决定组成“中华人民共和国参加各国议会联盟的人民代表团”加入各国议会联盟。
    到会的全体代表一致选出由宋庆龄、董必武、林伯渠、彭真、林枫、李济深、程潜、沈钧儒、章伯钧、黄炎培、李烛尘、马寅初、陈嘉庚、赛福鼎、廖承志等十五人组成会议的主席团。
    林伯渠代表向会议介绍了各国议会联盟的组织和工作情况以及“中华人民共和国参加各国议会联盟的人民代表团”筹备成立的经过。
    会议通过了关于成立中华人民共和国参加各国议会联盟的人民代表团的决议和中华人民共和国参加各国议会联盟的人民代表团章程。
    会议选出了中华人民共和国参加各国议会联盟的人民代表团执行委员会。选举的结果是：彭真为人民代表团主席，程潜、林枫、赛福鼎、陈嘉庚、章伯钧、马寅初、李烛尘、廖承志等八人为副主席，吴克坚为秘书长，王芸生、史良、朱学范、吴玉章、吴贻芳、宋庆龄、李一氓、李四光、李纯青、李雪峰、李维汉、李德全、李济深、沈钧儒、沈雁冰、邢西萍、周建人、周鲠生、屈武、林伯渠、邵力子、侯德榜、胡愈之、胡耀邦、乌兰夫、马叙伦、高崇民、区棠亮、张治中、张闻天、张难先、章蕴、许广平、许德珩、郭沫若、陈劭先、陈其尤、陈叔通、陈其瑗、陈绍宽、傅作义、喜饶嘉错、彭泽民、黄炎培、黄绍竑、黄琪翔、达浦生、荣毅仁、熊克武、赵朴初、刘长胜、刘宁一、潘菽、蔡畅、蒋光鼐、蒋南翔、邓颖超、赖若愚、钱端升、鲍尔汉、罗隆基等六十一人（按姓名笔划排列）为执行委员会委员。
    按：各国议会联盟是各国议会议员之间的国际组织。一八八九年成立。今年六月二十九日和七月二日，苏联和乌克兰、白俄罗斯先后决定参加各国议会联盟。七月二十四日，即在联盟第四十四届大会将在八月二十五日举行的一个月前，我国第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议秘书长彭真曾分别致电各国议会联盟主席斯坦斯盖特子爵、秘书长布隆内先生和各国议会联盟第四十四届大会组织委员会主席法格霍姆先生，通知我国全国人民代表大会代表将组成人民代表团，参加各国议会联盟，并将选派代表出席联盟第四十四届大会。（新华社）
