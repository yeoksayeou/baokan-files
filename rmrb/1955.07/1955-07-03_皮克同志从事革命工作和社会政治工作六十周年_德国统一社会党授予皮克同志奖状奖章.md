Author:
Date: 1955-07-03
Page: 4

　　Comrade Pieck's Sixtieth Anniversary of Engaging in Revolutionary Work and Social and Political Work
　　The Socialist Unity Party of Germany Awards Comrade Pieck with Certificate and Medal
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 2nd (TASS dispatch from Berlin, July 1st): July 1st marks the sixtieth anniversary of the outstanding activist of the German workers' movement, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and President of the German Democratic Republic, Wilhelm Pieck, engaging in revolutionary work and social and political work. On this day, the members, alternate members, and secretaries of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany presented Pieck with a certificate from the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. The certificate pointed out Pieck's outstanding contributions to the development of the German revolutionary movement as a loyal student of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, and as a comrade-in-arms of August Bebel, Karl Liebknecht, Rosa Luxemburg, and Ernst Thälmann.
    While awarding the certificate, the Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, awarded Pieck the medal for the oldest party member.
    In his congratulatory message, Grotewohl emphasized Pieck's contributions to the German working class and to all the German people, and wished him good health, longevity, and fruitful work. Pieck warmly thanked him for the congratulations he received on the sixtieth anniversary of his engagement in revolutionary work and social and political work.

### 皮克同志从事革命工作和社会政治工作六十周年　德国统一社会党授予皮克同志奖状奖章

1955-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　皮克同志从事革命工作和社会政治工作六十周年
　　德国统一社会党授予皮克同志奖状奖章
    新华社二日讯　塔斯社柏林一日讯：七月一日是德国工人运动杰出的活动家之一、德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员、德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克从事革命工作和社会政治工作的六十周年纪念日。在这个日子，德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局的委员们、候补委员们和中央委员会书记们把德国统一社会党中央委员会的奖状授予皮克。奖状指出了皮克作为马克思、恩格斯、列宁和斯大林的忠实学生和奥古斯特·倍倍尔、卡尔·李卜克内西、罗萨·卢森堡、恩斯特·台尔曼的战友在发展德国革命运动中的卓越功绩。
    在授予奖状的同时，德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥代表德国统一社会党中央委员会，授予皮克以最老的党员的奖章。
    格罗提渥在致贺词时强调指出，皮克对德国工人阶级和对全体德国人民的功绩，并且祝他健康长寿，工作富有成果。皮克热烈感谢在他从事革命工作和社会政治工作六十周年时对他的祝贺。
