Title: At the Lao Bilateral Military Conference, the Lao Royal Government Demanded Border Demarcation to Encroach on Sam Neua Province
Author:
Date: 1955-07-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 30th: A Vietnam News Agency dispatch from Laos states that the Lao bilateral military conference has not achieved any results in resolving the military conflict in Sam Neua Province due to the lack of sincerity on the part of the Lao Royal Government.

At the meeting on July 23, the Lao Royal Government delegation stubbornly demanded that the Nam Sam River be used as the boundary to divide Sam Neua Province into two zones, with the Lao Royal Army occupying the area west of the Nam Sam River.

Reports indicate that the Lao Royal side is continuing to strengthen its military forces around Muong Son and is also air-dropping many bandits from Xieng Khouang and other places into the Sang Duea area of Sam Neua Province, which they are demanding to occupy.

### 在老挝双方军事会议上  老挝王国政府要求划界侵占桑怒省

1955-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    在老挝双方军事会议上
    老挝王国政府要求划界侵占桑怒省
    新华社河内三十日电　越南通讯社消息：据老挝来的消息，由于老挝王国政府方面缺少诚意，老挝双方军事会议对解决桑怒省中的军事冲突问题还没有取得任何结果。
    在七月二十三日的会议上，老挝王国政府代表团硬要求以南桑江为界把桑怒省划分成两区，并且由老挝王国军队占领南桑江以西地区。
    有消息说，老挝王国方面正继续加强芒奔周围的军事力量，又从川圹等地把许多土匪空投到他们要求占领的桑怒省的桑杜区。
