Title: Korean and Chinese Delegation Holds Evening Party to Commemorate the Second Anniversary of the Korean Armistice

Author:

Date: 1955-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 27th (delayed dispatch from Kaesong by the Korean Central News Agency): The Korean-Chinese side delegation to the Military Armistice Commission held an evening party on July 26th to commemorate the second anniversary of the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement.

Invited to the evening party were: Polish Minister Mr. Morskie, member of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission; Czechoslovak Chief Member General Taus; Swiss Minister Mr. Stucki; Swedish Member General Malin; and other personnel of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission.

Also attending the evening party were General Zheng Guolu, Senior Member of the Korean-Chinese side to the Military Armistice Commission, and other representatives and staff members of the Korean-Chinese side delegation.

The evening party proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 朝中代表团举行晚会纪念朝鲜停战两周年

1955-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    朝中代表团举行晚会纪念朝鲜停战两周年
    新华社平壤二十七日电　朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯：军事停战委员会朝中方面代表团在七月二十六日举行了晚会，纪念朝鲜停战协定签订两周年。
    应邀参加晚会的有：中立国监察委员会波兰委员莫尔斯基公使；捷克斯洛伐克首席委员陶什将军；瑞士委员斯图基公使；瑞典委员马林将军；以及中立国监察委员会的其他人员。
    参加晚会的还有军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员郑国禄将军和朝中方面代表团其他代表和工作人员。
    晚会在友好空气中进行。
