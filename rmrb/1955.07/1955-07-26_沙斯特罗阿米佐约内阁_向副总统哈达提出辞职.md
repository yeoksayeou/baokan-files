Title: Sastroamidjojo Cabinet Resigns to Vice President Hatta
Author:
Date: 1955-07-26
Page: 4

　　Sastroamidjojo Cabinet
　　Resigns to Vice President Hatta
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 25th: Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo returned his letter of mandate to Vice President Hatta on the evening of the 24th.
　　Indonesian newspapers have widely commented on the new situation after the resignation of the Sastroamidjojo cabinet. The "Eastern Star" newspaper on the 23rd demanded that the new cabinet to be established must be based on the principles of parliamentary democracy.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约内阁  向副总统哈达提出辞职

1955-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    沙斯特罗阿米佐约内阁
    向副总统哈达提出辞职
    新华社雅加达二十五日电　印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约已在二十四日晚间把委任状送还给副总统哈达。
    印度尼西亚报纸广泛地评论了沙斯特罗阿米佐约内阁辞职以后的新形势。二十三日的“东星报”要求将要成立的新内阁必须建立在国会民主的原则的基础上。
