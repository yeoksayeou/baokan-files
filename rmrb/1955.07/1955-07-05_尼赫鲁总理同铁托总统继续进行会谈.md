Title: Prime Minister Nehru and President Tito Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-07-05
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, July 3rd: According to TASS, on July 2nd, Indian Prime Minister Nehru visited the Ivo Lola Ribar factory near Belgrade.

　　Afterwards, Nehru continued his talks with Tito.

　　On the afternoon of the 2nd, Nehru held a banquet for the Yugoslav President, attended by senior Yugoslav officials and Nehru's entourage.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, July 3rd: According to TASS, Indian Prime Minister Nehru held a reception on the evening of the 2nd, attended by Yugoslav President Tito, Yugoslav state activists, foreign diplomatic envoys, representatives of social organizations, and workers in science, culture, and the arts.

　　After the reception, Prime Minister Nehru and President Tito traveled to Sarajevo by special train.

### 尼赫鲁总理同铁托总统继续进行会谈

1955-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　尼赫鲁总理同铁托总统继续进行会谈
    新华社三日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德三日讯：七月二日，印度总理尼赫鲁参观了贝尔格莱德附近的伊沃·洛拉·利巴尔工厂。
    随后，尼赫鲁继续同铁托进行了会谈。
    二日下午，尼赫鲁为南斯拉夫总统举行了宴会，出席宴会的有南斯拉夫高级官员和尼赫鲁的随行人员。
    新华社四日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德三日讯：印度总理尼赫鲁在二日晚上举行了招待会，出席招待会的有南斯拉夫总统铁托，南斯拉夫的国家活动家，各国外交使节，社会团体的代表以及科学、文化和艺术工作者。
    招待会后，尼赫鲁总理和铁托总统乘专车前往塞拉热窝。
