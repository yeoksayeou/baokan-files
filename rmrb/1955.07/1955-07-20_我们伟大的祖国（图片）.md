Author: 
Date: 1955-07-20
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The "Report on the Comprehensive Plan for Radically Eliminating the Yellow River's Water Disasters and Developing the Yellow River's Water Conservancy," made by Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Zihui, has inspired the enthusiasm of the young teachers of the Water Energy Utilization Teaching and Research Group of the Department of Hydraulic Engineering of Tsinghua University. They are observing the water energy utilization map and studying the cascade development plan of the Yellow River's main stream.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国务院副总理邓子恢所作的“关于根治黄河水害和开发黄河水利的综合规划的报告”，鼓舞了清华大学水利系水能利用教研组青年教师们的工作热情。他们正在观察水能利用地图，研究黄河干流阶梯开发计划。
