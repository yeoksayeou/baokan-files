Title: Vietnam National United Front National Committee, Labor Party, etc. Issue Joint Statement Supporting Government's Note on Holding Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 22nd: According to the Vietnam News Agency on July 22nd: The Vietnam National United Front issued a statement on July 21st, supporting the note from the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the South Vietnamese authorities on July 19th. This note suggested that the other party send representatives to hold a consultative conference with representatives of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, starting from July 20th.

The Vietnam National United Front, in its statement, refuted Ngo Dinh Diem's slander that there was no freedom in North Vietnam by listing facts, and pointed out that the South Vietnamese authorities were attempting to use this as a cover for their conspiracy to not hold consultative conferences and to oppose national unification.

The statement also condemned Ngo Dinh Diem's attempt to evade the obligations of the Geneva Accords. The statement pointed out that the South Vietnamese authorities are the successors to the French government in the South and are obligated to fulfill the Geneva Accords.

The statement said: "The National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, various political parties, and people's organizations warmly applaud and fully support the note of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on July 19th, and at the same time urge the South Vietnamese authorities to immediately send representatives to consult with representatives of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the issue of organizing general elections to unify the country."

The statement expressed the hope that governments concerned with the implementation of the Geneva Accords will continue to make greater efforts to guarantee the implementation of the Geneva Accords for common peace.

The statement was signed by: the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, the Vietnam Labor Party, the Vietnam Democratic Party, the Vietnam Socialist Party, and various Vietnamese people's organizations.

### 越南国民联合战线全国委员会和劳动党等发表联合声明  支持政府关于举行协商会议的照会

1955-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国民联合战线全国委员会和劳动党等发表联合声明
    支持政府关于举行协商会议的照会
    新华社河内二十二日电　据越南通讯社二十二日讯：越南国民联合战线在七月二十一日发表声明，支持越南民主共和国政府七月十九日给越南南方当局的照会，这个照会建议对方派出代表同越南民主共和国政府代表从七月二十日起举行协商会议。
    越南国民联合战线在声明中列举事实驳斥了吴庭艳所谓越南北方没有自由的诬蔑，并且指出，越南南方当局企图借此掩饰它的不举行协商会议和反对国家统一的阴谋。
    声明也谴责吴庭艳企图逃避日内瓦协议的义务。声明指出，越南南方当局是在南方的法国政府的继承者，负有履行日内瓦协议的义务。
    声明说：“越南国民联合战线全国委员会、各政党和人民团体热烈欢呼和全力支持越南民主共和国政府七月十九日的照会，同时规劝越南南方当局立即派遣代表同越南民主共和国政府代表就组织普选来统一国家的问题进行会商。”
    声明表示希望对于履行日内瓦协议有关的政府为了共同的和平，在保证日内瓦协议的执行上继续作更大的努力。
    在声明上签名的有：越南国民联合战线全国委员会、越南劳动党、越南民主党、越南社会党以及越南各人民团体。
