Title: Li Xiannian's Report Scheduled to be Broadcast Today
Author:
Date: 1955-07-09
Page: 1

　　Li Xiannian's Report Scheduled to be Broadcast Today
    This newspaper reports: The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the "Report on the 1954 National Final Accounts and the 1955 National Budget" made by Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Finance, on behalf of the State Council at the Second Session of the First National People's Congress, starting at 7:30 AM on the 9th. This report will be rebroadcast at 7:30 AM on the 12th.

### 李先念的报告定于今天广播

1955-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　李先念的报告定于今天广播
    本报讯　中央人民广播电台将在九日上午七点三十分开始，播送国务院副总理、财政部长李先念在第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议上代表国务院所作的“关于一九五四年国家决算和一九五五年国家预算的报告”。这个报告在十二日上午七点三十分重播。
