Title: Seventy Deputies of the Albanian People's Assembly Meet to Decide on Joining the Inter-Parliamentary Union and Establishing a National Parliamentary Group
Author:
Date: 1955-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana Dispatch: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, seventy deputies of the Albanian People's Assembly convened in Tirana on the 15th, deciding to establish a national parliamentary group and to join the Inter-Parliamentary Union.

    The meeting also approved the charter of the Albanian National Parliamentary Group, elected the temporary standing committee of the parliamentary group, and representatives to the council of the Inter-Parliamentary Union. Deputy Faja Pacha Rhami was elected as the chairman of the National Parliamentary Group, and Deputy Ki Harito was elected as the vice-chairman. Deputies Bei Shtura and Sa Bahori were elected as representatives to the council of the Inter-Parliamentary Union.

    Finally, the meeting passed a letter to all deputies of the People's Assembly, inviting them to join the National Parliamentary Group.

    The temporary standing committee will notify the Inter-Parliamentary Union of the establishment of the Albanian National Parliamentary Group and its participation in the Inter-Parliamentary Union.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会的七十位议员集会  决定参加各国议会联盟并成立全国议员团

1955-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会的七十位议员集会
    决定参加各国议会联盟并成立全国议员团
    新华社十六日讯　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民议会的七十位议员十五日在地拉那举行会议，决定成立全国议员团并且参加各国议会联盟。
    会议还通过了阿尔巴尼亚全国议员团的章程，选举了议员团的临时常务委员会和出席各国议会联盟理事会的代表。议员法·帕奇拉米当选为全国议员团主席，议员基·哈里托当选为副主席。议员贝·什图拉和萨·巴霍里当选为参加各国议会联盟理事会的代表。
    最后，会议通过了一个告人民议会全体议员书，邀请他们参加全国议员团。
    临时常务委员会将把成立阿尔巴尼亚全国议员团及其参加各国议会联盟的事情通知各国议会联盟。
