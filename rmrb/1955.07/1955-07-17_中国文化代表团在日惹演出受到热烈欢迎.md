Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation's Performances in Yogyakarta Receive Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1955-07-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Yogyakarta, July 16th: The Chinese Cultural Delegation held three performances in Yogyakarta, a renowned cultural center of Indonesia, on July 11th, 12th, and 14th, receiving a warm welcome from the people.

　　The first two performances were held in a former palace in the city, attended by the Mayor of Yogyakarta and other officials, as well as many prominent figures from the Indonesian cultural circles and representatives of overseas Chinese, totaling about four thousand people.

　　Approximately 50,000 people watched the open-air performance held at "Southern Square" on the evening of the 14th. Many of them entered the square five hours before the performance began. Many audience members also came from other cities such as Semarang, Solo, and Magelang.

　　The programs performed by the Chinese Cultural Delegation were appreciated by the audience. Peking Opera "The Tale of the White Snake," "Yandang Mountain," "San Cha Kou," and "Xinjiang Plate Dance," "Lotus Dance," "Red Silk Dance," "Lion Dance," and "Tea Picking and Butterfly Catching Dance" were especially warmly welcomed.

　　Many prominent figures in Yogyakarta praised the performances of the Chinese Cultural Delegation. The Mayor of Yogyakarta, Purwokususumo, said that these performances could serve as valuable lessons for Indonesia in seeking new directions for cultural and artistic development. The performances of the Chinese Cultural Delegation are inspiring in solving the problem of how to preserve the essence of cultural heritage.

　　The famous painter Kusnadi said that Peking Opera and Chinese music are amazing. Peking Opera is very dramatic. Although Chinese musical instruments are simple in structure, they can play such rich, beautiful, and harmonious music.

### 中国文化代表团在日惹演出受到热烈欢迎

1955-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中国文化代表团在日惹演出受到热烈欢迎
    新华社日惹十六日电　七月十一日、十二日和十四日，中国文化代表团在日惹举行了三次演出，受到印度尼西亚这个著名文化中心的人民的热烈欢迎。
    头两次演出是在城里的一个故宫中举行的，观看演出的有日惹市长和其他官员，以及许多著名的印度尼西亚文化界人士和华侨代表等约四千人。
    观看十四日晚上在“南部广场”举行的露天演出的约有五万人。他们之中许多人在演出开始之前五小时就进入广场了。还有许多观众来自三宝垄、梭罗、马吉冷等其他城市。
    中国文化代表团表演的节目都受到观众的赞赏。京剧“白蛇传”、“雁荡山”、“三岔口”和
  “新疆盘舞”、“荷花舞”、“红绸舞”、“狮舞”和“采茶扑蝶舞”等受到了特别热烈的欢迎。
    日惹许多著名人士赞扬中国文化代表团的演出。日惹市长普沃库苏莫说，这些演出可以作为印度尼西亚在文化和艺术方面寻求新的发展方向的宝贵借镜。中国文化代表团的演出，在解决怎样保存文化遗产中的这个精华的问题方面，是有启发作用的。
    著名画家库斯纳迪说，京剧和中国音乐是惊人的。京剧是非常富有戏剧性的。中国乐器虽然构造简单，却能够奏出那么丰富、美妙和和谐的音乐来。
