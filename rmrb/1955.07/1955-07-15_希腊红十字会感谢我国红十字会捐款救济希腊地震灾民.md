Title: Greek Red Cross Thanks Chinese Red Cross for Donation to Aid Greek Earthquake Victims
Author:
Date: 1955-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 14th: On July 2nd, the Chinese Red Cross received a letter dated June 21st from the Greek Red Cross, expressing once again its most heartfelt thanks for the Chinese Red Cross's donation to aid Greek earthquake victims, considering it a touching expression of sympathy for the Greek victims.

The League of Red Cross Societies also sent a letter to the Chinese Red Cross on June 9th, expressing its warmest gratitude for the donation to the Greek Red Cross.

The Chinese Red Cross donated 20,000 Swiss francs on June 8th to aid residents of Volos, Greece, who were affected by the earthquake.

### 希腊红十字会感谢我国红十字会捐款救济希腊地震灾民

1955-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    希腊红十字会感谢我国红十字会捐款救济希腊地震灾民
    新华社十四日讯  七月二日，中国红十字会接到希腊红十字会六月二十一日的来信，对中国红十字会捐款救济希腊地震灾民，再次表示最衷心的谢意，认为这是对希腊灾民的一种令人感动的同情的表示。
    红十字会协会也在六月九日致函中国红十字会，对于捐助希腊红十字会一举，表示最热烈的感谢。
    中国红十字会在六月八日曾捐赠两万瑞士法郎，救济希腊瓦洛斯城因发生地震而遭灾的居民。
