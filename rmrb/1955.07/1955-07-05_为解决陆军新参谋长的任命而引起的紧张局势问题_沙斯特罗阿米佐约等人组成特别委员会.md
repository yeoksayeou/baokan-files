Title: Sastroamidjojo and Others Form Special Committee to Resolve Tensions Arising from Appointment of New Army Chief of Staff
Author:
Date: 1955-07-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 4th: The Indonesian Cabinet held a meeting lasting three and a half hours on the evening of July 2nd to discuss the tensions arising from the appointment of a new Army Chief of Staff.

Indonesian Minister of Information, Dubin, issued a statement after the meeting stating that the Indonesian Cabinet has established a special committee to resolve the current tensions. This committee will be composed of Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo, Deputy Prime Minister Arifin, Minister of Defense Kusumah Sumantri, and Minister of Information Dubin.

Dubin said that the Indonesian government will resolutely resolve this issue in accordance with democratic parliamentary principles and the Provisional Constitution of the Republic of Indonesia.

### 为解决陆军新参谋长的任命而引起的紧张局势问题　沙斯特罗阿米佐约等人组成特别委员会

1955-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　为解决陆军新参谋长的任命而引起的紧张局势问题
　　沙斯特罗阿米佐约等人组成特别委员会
    新华社雅加达四日电　印度尼西亚内阁在七月二日晚间举行了历时三小时半的会议，讨论由于陆军新参谋长的任命而引起的紧张局势问题。
    印度尼西亚情报部长杜斌在会后发表声明说，印度尼西亚内阁已经成立了一个特别委员会来解决目前的紧张局势问题。这个委员会将由总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约、副总理阿里芬、国防部长库素马素曼特里和情报部长杜斌组成。
    杜斌说，印度尼西亚政府将坚决根据民主的国会原则和印度尼西亚共和国临时宪法来解决这个问题。
