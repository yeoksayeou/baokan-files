Title: Polish Embassy Holds Press Conference to Commemorate National Day of Rebirth
Author:
Date: 1955-07-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 16th. The Polish Embassy in China held a press conference in Beijing on the morning of July 16th to commemorate the 11th anniversary of the Polish National Day of Rebirth on July 22nd.

At the press conference, the Polish Chargé d'affaires ad interim, Majman, introduced the brilliant achievements of the Polish people in the great cause of socialist construction. Majman and others also answered many questions raised by reporters.

More than 30 reporters from various newspapers and magazines attended the press conference.

### 波兰大使馆为纪念复兴节举行记者招待会

1955-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰大使馆为纪念复兴节举行记者招待会
    新华社十六日讯　七月二十二日是波兰国家复兴节十一周年纪念日。波兰人民共和国驻华大使馆十六日上午在北京举行记者招待会。
    招待会上，波兰临时代办马伊曼介绍了波兰人民在社会主义建设的伟大事业中所取得的辉煌成就。马伊曼等并回答了记者所提出的许多问题。
    出席招待会的，有各报刊杂志的记者三十多人。
