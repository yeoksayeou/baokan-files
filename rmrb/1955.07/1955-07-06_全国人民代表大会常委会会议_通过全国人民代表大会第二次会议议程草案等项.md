Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Passes Draft Agenda for the Second Session of the National People's Congress, and Other Items
Author:
Date: 1955-07-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 5th --

　  The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 19th meeting on July 5th.

　  The meeting discussed and approved the draft agenda for the Second Session of the First National People's Congress, the draft list of candidates for the Presidium and Secretary-General, and the draft list of candidates for the Chairman and members of the Proposals Examination Committee.

　  The meeting also approved the work report to be submitted to the Second Session of the National People's Congress.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会会议  通过全国人民代表大会第二次会议议程草案等项

1955-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　  全国人民代表大会常委会会议
    通过全国人民代表大会第二次会议议程草案等项
    新华社五日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在七月五日举行第十九次会议。
    会议讨论并通过了为第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议拟订的会议议程草案、主席团和秘书长人选草案、提案审查委员会主任委员和委员人选草案。
    会议还通过了将要向全国人民代表大会第二次会议提出的工作报告。
