Title: Albania Proposes Restoring Diplomatic Relations with Greece
Author:
Date: 1955-07-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 3rd: The Albanian Telegraph Agency reported from Tirana on the 2nd that the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Albania, Behar Shtylla, sent a telegram to the Secretary-General of the United Nations, Dag Hammarskjöld, regarding the issue of restoring diplomatic relations between Albania and Greece.
    The telegram stated that the Government of the People's Republic of Albania has consistently adhered to a policy of strengthening peace and security among the Balkan countries and among the European countries. In the spirit of this policy, and guided by the principles of equality, mutual respect for territorial integrity and sovereignty, non-interference in internal affairs, and mutually beneficial economic and cultural cooperation, it hopes to establish normal diplomatic relations with Greece. The establishment of such relations between the People's Republic of Albania and Greece will be in the best interests of the peoples of both countries, and in the interests of peace and security in the Balkans and in Europe.
    The telegram concluded by stating that, therefore, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of Albania, I request you, Mr. Secretary-General, to convey to the Greek Government the proposal of the Government of the People's Republic of Albania regarding the establishment of normal diplomatic and good-neighborly relations between Albania and Greece. The Government of the People's Republic of Albania hopes to know the Greek Government's views on this proposal.

### 阿尔巴尼亚建议恢复同希腊的外交关系

1955-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚建议恢复同希腊的外交关系
    新华社三日讯　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那二日讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部长贝·什图拉就恢复阿尔巴尼亚同希腊的外交关系问题致电联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德。
    电报说，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府一向坚持不渝地执行加强巴尔干各国之间和欧洲各国之间的和平和安全的政策。本着这种政策的精神，并在平等、互相尊重领土完整和主权、互不干涉内政、进行互惠的经济和文化合作的原则的指导下，它希望同希腊建立正常的外交关系。在阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国和希腊之间建立这种关系，将符合于阿希两国人民的切身利益，符合于巴尔干和欧洲的和平和安全的利益。
    电报最后说，因此我奉阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府的命令，请求您，秘书长先生，把阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府关于建立阿希两国正常的外交和睦邻关系的建议，转告给希腊政府。阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府希望知道希腊政府对这个建议的看法。
