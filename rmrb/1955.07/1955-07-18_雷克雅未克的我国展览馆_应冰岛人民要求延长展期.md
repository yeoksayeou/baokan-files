Title: China's Exhibition Hall in Reykjavik Extends Exhibition Period at the Request of the Icelandic People
Author:
Date: 1955-07-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 17th: Reykjavik News: The Chinese Exhibition Hall at the Reykjavik Exhibition in Iceland has decided to extend the exhibition period by seven days, until July 24th, in response to the request of the Icelandic people.
    Since the opening of the Chinese Exhibition Hall on July 5th, the number of visitors has been very enthusiastic; visitors have expressed great interest in the various exhibits in the Chinese Pavilion.

### 雷克雅未克的我国展览馆  应冰岛人民要求延长展期

1955-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    雷克雅未克的我国展览馆
    应冰岛人民要求延长展期
    新华社布拉格十七日电　雷克雅未克消息：冰岛雷克雅未克博览会中的中国展览馆应冰岛人民的要求，决定把展出的日期延长七天，到七月二十四日结束。
    自从七月五日中国展览馆开幕以来，参观的人十分踊跃；参观者对中国馆的各项展品表示了巨大的兴趣。
