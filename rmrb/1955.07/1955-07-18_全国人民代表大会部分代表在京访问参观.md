Title: Some Deputies to the National People's Congress Visit Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th. Some deputies attending the Second Session of the First National People's Congress visited the state-owned Beijing Southern Suburbs Farm and the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory on the 17th.

Today is Sunday, and the Second Session of the First National People's Congress is adjourned for one day. Some deputies attending this session visited the state-owned Beijing Southern Suburbs Farm at 9 am. Guo Ziqing, the farm director, introduced the farm's general situation to the deputies. Then, they visited the farm's tractor depot. Many labor models and ethnic minority representatives among the deputies were very interested in the various agricultural machines and new-style farm tools displayed here. The deputies watched the farm workers making silage. The deputies also successively watched the growth of crops such as cotton in the fields; watched various breeds of dairy cows in the dairy farm; and watched rice at the Heyi Work Station. They also made a special visit to the newly built farm employee dormitory.

In the afternoon, some deputies visited the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory. From the introduction by Song Ting, the director of the factory's preparatory office, they learned about the factory's construction process, scale, and equipment. Then, the deputies visited the seven workshops according to the entire production process. Some of the deputies also visited the factory's living area. After seeing this new-style cotton textile factory built with our country's own complete set of equipment, the deputies were very excited about the development of the textile industry in our country's socialist construction.

On the 15th, some deputies also took advantage of the gaps in the meeting to visit the "Liberate Taiwan Exhibition."

On the evenings of the 16th and 17th, the deputies watched the drama "People Who Plant Oranges" performed by the China Youth Art Theater.

### 全国人民代表大会部分代表在京访问参观

1955-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会部分代表在京访问参观
    新华社十七日讯　出席第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议的部分代表在十七日访问和参观了国营北京南郊农场和国营北京第二棉纺织厂。
    今天是星期日，第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议休会一天。出席这次会议的部分代表在上午九时多前往国营北京南郊农场访问。农场场长郭子清向代表们介绍了农场概况。接着，他们参观了农场的拖拉机库。代表中的许多劳动模范和少数民族代表对这里陈列的各种农业机器和新式农具都感到很大兴趣。代表们观看了农场工人制作青贮饲料的情况。代表们还先后在农田里观看棉花等作物的生长情况；在奶牛场观看了各种品种的奶牛；在和义作业站观看了水稻。他们并特地参观了新近建成的农场职工宿舍。
    在下午，部分代表访问和参观了国营北京第二棉纺织厂。他们从这个工厂的筹建处处长宋汀的介绍中，得知工厂筹建经过、工厂规模和设备情况。然后，代表们按全部生产程序，参观了七个车间。其中一部分代表还参观了工厂的生活区。代表们在看到了这个由我国自己的全套设备建设起来的新式棉纺织厂以后，对我国社会主义建设中纺织工业的发展，感到非常兴奋。
    在十五日，部分代表还曾利用会议空隙参观了“解放台湾展览会”。
    十六日晚和十七日晚，代表们观看了中国青年艺术剧院演出的话剧“种橘的人们”。
