Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev to Visit Britain Next Spring
Author:
Date: 1955-07-29
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 27th: TASS reported that during the Geneva Conference, the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union discussed the importance of consolidating relations between their two countries by maintaining the personal contacts they had established in Geneva. Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Nikolai A. Bulganin, and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Nikita S. Khrushchev, have accepted Sir Anthony Eden's invitation to visit the United Kingdom next spring.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫将在明春访问英国

1955-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫将在明春访问英国
    新华社二十八日讯　塔斯社莫斯科二十七日讯：在日内瓦会议期间，联合王国首相和苏联部长会议主席讨论了关于通过保持他们在日内瓦已经建立起来的个人接触的方法，来巩固他们两国之间的关系的重要性的问题。苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫已经接受了安·艾登爵士关于在明年春天访问联合王国的邀请。
