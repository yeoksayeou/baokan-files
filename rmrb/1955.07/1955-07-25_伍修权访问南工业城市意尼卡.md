Title: Wu Xiuquan Visits Southern Industrial City of Zenica
Author:
Date: 1955-07-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 22nd: According to the Yugoslav News Agency, Wu Xiuquan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Yugoslavia, accompanied by Begović, Director of "Borba" newspaper, visited Zenica, an industrial city in the Republic of Bosnia and Herzegovina, on the 21st. Ambassador Wu Xiuquan visited various departments of Yugoslavia's largest steel factory and held talks with factory representatives, local people's representatives, and government officials.

Prior to this, Ambassador Wu Xiuquan visited the industrial area of Tuzla. After leaving Zenica, he will also visit the Jablanica power station.

### 伍修权访问南工业城市意尼卡

1955-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    伍修权访问南工业城市意尼卡
    新华社布拉格二十二日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社消息：中华人民共和国驻南斯拉夫特命全权大使伍修权，在“战斗报”社长贝戈维奇陪同下，二十一日访问了波斯尼亚—黑塞哥维那共和国的工业城市意尼卡。伍修权大使参观了南斯拉夫最大的钢铁工厂的各个不同部门，并且同工厂代表、当地人民代表和政府官员进行了谈话。
    在这以前，伍修权大使参观了图斯拉的工业地区。在离开意尼卡后，他还要去参观雅布拉尼查的电力站。
