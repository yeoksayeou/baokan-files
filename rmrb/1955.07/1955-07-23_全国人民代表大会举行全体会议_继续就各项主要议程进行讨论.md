Title: National People's Congress Holds Plenary Meeting, Continues Discussions on Major Agenda Items
Author:
Date: 1955-07-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd—The First National People's Congress, Second Session, continued its meeting at 3:00 PM today to discuss various major agenda items.

    The representatives and heads of various departments of state who spoke at today's meeting were: Dong Biwu, Shen Junru, Zhang Xiruo, Ye Shengtao, Shu Tong, Zhang Qi, Jin Shuliang, He Xiangning, Xirao Jiacuo, Guo Dihuo, Wang Juqian, Yu Deyuan, Zhang Dingcheng, Ma Tengai, Zhu Xuefan, An Wenqin, and others, totaling sixteen people.

    The executive chairmen of today's meeting were: Luo Ronghuan, Lai Ruoyu, Shu Sheyu, Xiong Kewu, Lin Qiaozhi, and Han Wangchen.

    Today's meeting concluded at 7:10 PM. The general discussion will continue on the 23rd.

### 全国人民代表大会举行全体会议  继续就各项主要议程进行讨论

1955-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会举行全体会议
    继续就各项主要议程进行讨论
    据新华社二十二日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议二十二日下午三时继续举行会议，讨论各项主要议程。
    在今天会上先后发言的代表和国家机关各部门负责人有：董必武、沈钧儒、张奚若、叶圣陶、舒同、张祺、靳树梁、何香凝、喜饶嘉错、郭棣活、汪菊潜、喻德渊、张鼎丞、马腾霭、朱学范、安文钦等十六人。
    今天会议的执行主席是：罗荣桓、赖若愚、舒舍予、熊克武、林巧稚、韩望尘。
    今天的会议进行到七时十分结束。二十三日继续进行大会讨论。
