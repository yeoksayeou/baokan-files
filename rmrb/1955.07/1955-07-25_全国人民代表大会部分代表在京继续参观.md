Title: Some National People's Congress Deputies Continue Visits in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th. Some deputies attending the Second Session of the First National People's Congress continued their visits in Beijing on the 24th – Sunday.

    In the morning, some of the labor models and technical workers among the deputies visited the boiler-locomotive performance hosted by the Third Ministry of Machine Building Industry; another group of deputies visited the ceramic kiln of the Central Academy of Fine Arts. In the afternoon, many deputies went to Nan苑 (Nanyuan) to visit the Red Star Collective Farm.

    Today, some deputies also visited the “Fengtai-Shacheng Railway Model Exhibition” hosted by the Ministry of Railways.

    In the evening, the deputies watched the豫剧 (Yu Opera) "花木兰" (Hua Mulan), starring National People's Congress Deputy 常香玉 (Chang Xiangyu), and wonderful performances by the Fujian Minnan Opera Experimental Troupe and the Jiangxi Gan Opera Troupe at the Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai.

### 全国人民代表大会部分代表在京继续参观

1955-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会部分代表在京继续参观
    新华社二十四日讯　出席第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议的部分代表，二十四日——星期日在京继续参观。
    上午，代表中的部分工农业劳动模范和技术工作者参观了第三机械工业部主办的锅驼机表演；另一部分代表参观了中央美术学院的陶瓷窑。下午，许多代表前往南苑参观了红星集体农庄。
    今天，一部分代表还先后参观了铁道部主办的“丰沙线铁路模型展览”。
    晚上，代表们在中南海怀仁堂观看了由全国人民代表大会代表常香玉主演的豫剧“花木兰”和福建省闽南戏实验剧团、江西省赣剧团的精彩演出。
