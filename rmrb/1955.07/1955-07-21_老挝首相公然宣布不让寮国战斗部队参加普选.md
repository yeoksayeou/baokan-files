Title: Lao Prime Minister Publicly Announces Lao Fighting Units Will Not Participate in General Election
Author:
Date: 1955-07-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 20th -- According to a Agence France-Presse dispatch from Vientiane: Lao Kingdom Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith, in a speech on the 19th, publicly declared that Lao Fighting Units would not be allowed to participate in the national general election to be held in December this year. In his speech, Katay Don Sasorith also wantonly attacked the Lao Fighting Units.
　　As everyone knows, since the armistice, the Lao Kingdom government has repeatedly obstructed the Lao Fighting Units from participating in the common life of the nation, according to the Geneva Accords. And the Geneva Accords specifically stipulated that the common life of the nation in Laos included general elections.

### 老挝首相公然宣布不让寮国战斗部队参加普选

1955-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝首相公然宣布不让寮国战斗部队参加普选
    新华社河内二十日电　据法新社万象消息：老挝王国首相克特在十九日发表演说，公然声称不让寮国战斗部队参加将在今年十二月举行的全国普选。克特在演说中还对寮国战斗部队恣意进行攻击。
    大家知道，从停战以来，老挝王国政府一再阻挠寮国战斗部队根据日内瓦协议参加全国的共同生活。而日内瓦协议特别指明老挝全国共同生活是包括普选在内的。
