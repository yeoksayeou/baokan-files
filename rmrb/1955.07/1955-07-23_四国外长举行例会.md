Title: Four Foreign Ministers Hold Regular Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-07-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st, TASS dispatch: The four foreign ministers held a regular meeting on the morning of the 21st, chaired by Molotov.
    In addition to the four foreign ministers, advisors and experts from the four delegations attended the meeting.
    The meeting discussed the instructions that the heads of government would give to the foreign ministers. The foreign ministers unanimously agreed to recommend that the heads of government meet at 3:30 p.m. on the 21st to hear a report on the foreign ministers' meeting on the 21st.
    The four heads of government will begin discussing the third item on the agenda—disarmament—at 4:00 p.m.

### 四国外长举行例会

1955-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    四国外长举行例会
    新华社二十二日讯　塔斯社日内瓦二十一日讯：二十一日上午，四国外长举行例会，由莫洛托夫担任主席。
    出席会议的除四国外长外，还有四国代表团的顾问和专家。
    会议讨论了关于政府首脑将给予外长们的指示的问题。外长们一致同意建议政府首脑在二十一日下午三时三十分开会听取关于二十一日外长会议的报告。
    四国政府首脑在下午四时将着手讨论议程上的第三项——裁军问题。
