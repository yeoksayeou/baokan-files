Title: Li Fuchun's Report Scheduled to Begin Broadcasting Today
Author:
Date: 1955-07-07
Page: 1

    Li Fuchun's Report Scheduled to Begin Broadcasting Today
    This newspaper reports that the Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast continuously the "Report on the First Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy" delivered by Li Fuchun, Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Planning Commission, on behalf of the State Council at the Second Session of the First National People's Congress, starting at 7:30 AM on the 7th and 8th.

### 李富春报告定于今天开始广播

1955-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    李富春报告定于今天开始广播
    本报讯　中央人民广播电台在七日和八日上午从七点三十分开始连续播送国务院副总理、国家计划委员会主任李富春在第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议上代表国务院所作的“关于发展国民经济的第一个五年计划的报告”。
