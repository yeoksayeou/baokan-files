Title: Yugoslavia Appoints Observer to Attend Four-Power Conference
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 16th: According to a Belgrade dispatch from the Yugoslav News Agency, the spokesman for the Yugoslav Secretariat of Foreign Affairs, Draskovic, announced at a press conference on the 15th that the Yugoslav government has appointed Ambassador to France, Bebler, as an observer to the summit conference of the four powers, the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France, to be held in Geneva.

### 南斯拉夫任命出席四国会议的观察员

1955-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫任命出席四国会议的观察员
    新华社布拉格十六日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫外交秘书处发言人德拉斯科维奇十五日在记者招待会上宣布：南斯拉夫政府已经任命驻法大使贝勃勒为将在日内瓦举行的苏、美、英、法四大国首脑会议的观察员。
