Title: Poland, India, and Canada, as Members of the International Commission in Vietnam, Jointly Protest Saigon Authorities' Atrocities in Saigon
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, July 23rd: Canadian Minister of External Affairs Pearson told the House of Commons on the 22nd that the Canadian commissioner, together with the Indian and Polish commissioners of the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam, have jointly protested to the South Vietnamese government regarding the attack on the residences of the Polish and Indian commissioners by rioters.

Pearson said that the International Commission also "expressed the hope that the ringleaders of the incident be arrested, and that steps be taken to prevent such regrettable incidents from happening again."

### 参加越南国际委员会的波、印、加三国委员  联合向越南南方当局抗议西贡暴行事件

1955-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    参加越南国际委员会的波、印、加三国委员
    联合向越南南方当局抗议西贡暴行事件
    新华社二十三日讯　渥太华消息：加拿大外交部长皮尔逊二十二日对众议院说，对于越南国际监督和监察委员会波兰和印度委员居所受到暴徒攻击一事，加拿大委员已经同印度和波兰委员联合向越南南方政府提出抗议。
    皮尔逊说，国际委员会还“表示希望逮捕肇事的魁首，并采取步骤，使这样令人遗憾的事件不致再发生”。
