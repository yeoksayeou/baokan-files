Title: Resolution of the Second Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on Authorizing the Standing Committee to Enact Separate Regulations
Author:
Date: 1955-07-31
Page: 2

    The Second Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China believes that, with the progress of socialist construction and socialist transformation, the state urgently needs to formulate various laws to meet the requirements of national construction and national work. During the adjournment of the National People's Congress, some laws of a partial nature inevitably need to be adopted and implemented by the Standing Committee urgently. For this reason, in accordance with the provisions of Article 31, Item 19 of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, the Standing Committee is authorized to formulate laws of a partial nature, that is, separate regulations, in a timely manner in accordance with the spirit of the Constitution and based on actual needs.

### 中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议关于授权常务委员会制定单行法规的决议  （一九五五年七月三十日第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议通过）

1955-07-31
第2版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议关于授权常务委员会制定单行法规的决议
    （一九五五年七月三十日第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议通过）
    第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议认为，随着社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业的进展，国家急需制定各项法律，以适应国家建设和国家工作的要求。在全国人民代表大会闭会期间，有些部分性质的法律，不可避免地急需常务委员会通过施行。为此，特依照中华人民共和国宪法第三十一条第十九项的规定，授权常务委员会依照宪法的精神、根据实际的需要，适时地制定部分性质的法律，即单行法规。
