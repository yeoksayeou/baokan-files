Title: Chinese Yue Opera Troupe Holds First Performance in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1955-07-04
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 1st – The Chinese Yue Opera Troupe, consisting of over seventy members led by the head of the troupe, Xu Guangping, arrived in Berlin by train on June 30th.

　　Those who went to the station to welcome them included Engel, Director of the German Academy of Arts, Wiese, Secretary-General of the German Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Rentmeister, Director of the Department of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Ministry of Culture, as well as representatives from the People's Theatre and DEFA Film Studio. Wang Yutian, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our country's Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, also went to the station to greet them.

　　A welcoming ceremony was held at the station. Rentmeister, Wiese, and Xu Guangping spoke successively, expressing their hope to further strengthen the friendship between the Chinese and German people through cultural exchange.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 3rd – The Chinese Yue Opera Troupe, which is visiting the German Democratic Republic for performances, gave its first performance of the Yue Opera "The Story of the Western Wing" at the famous People's Theatre in Berlin on the evening of the 2nd.

　　This performance was greatly welcomed by people in German artistic and theatrical circles and by the Berlin audience.

　　The "Neues Deutschland" reported on the evening of the 1st when the Chinese Yue Opera Troupe was in rehearsal. It said on the 3rd that "the performance of Yue Opera in Berlin and the German Democratic Republic is sure to achieve the same success as the recent performance of Peking Opera in Paris."

　　The Chinese Yue Opera Troupe will continue to perform in Berlin for several days and then travel to various parts of the German Democratic Republic for touring performances.

### 中国越剧团在柏林举行首次公演

1955-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国越剧团在柏林举行首次公演
    新华社柏林一日电　中国越剧团一行七十多人在团长许广平率领下，在六月三十日乘火车抵达柏林。
    前往车站欢迎的有德国艺术科学院主任恩格尔、德国对外文化联络协会秘书长维塞、文化部对外文化联络司司长伦特迈斯特以及人民剧院和德发电影制片厂的代表等。我国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆临时代办王雨田也到车站迎接。
    在车站上举行了欢迎会。伦特迈斯特、维塞和许广平先后讲话，表示希望通过文化交流更进一步加强中德两国人民间的友谊。
    新华社柏林三日电　到德意志民主共和国作访问演出的中国越剧团二日晚在柏林著名的人民剧院首次公演越剧“西厢记”。
    这次演出受到德国艺术界、戏剧界人士和柏林观众的极大欢迎。
    “新德意志报”三日在报道中国越剧团一日晚彩排的情形时说，“越剧在柏林和民主德国的演出，定将获得与不久前京剧在巴黎演出时同样的成功”。
    中国越剧团将在柏林继续公演数日，然后赴民主德国各地作旅行演出。
