Title: Soviet Agricultural Workers Delegation Travels to the United States
Author:
Date: 1955-07-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th. According to TASS, a Soviet agricultural workers delegation left Moscow by plane for the United States on the 12th. The delegation is led by Matskevich, First Deputy Minister of the Soviet Ministry of Agriculture.

### 苏联农业工作者代表团前往美国

1955-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联农业工作者代表团前往美国
    新华社十三日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联农业工作者代表团于十二日离莫斯科乘飞机前往美国。代表团由苏联农业部第一副部长马茨凯维奇率领。
