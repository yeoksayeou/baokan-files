Title: Japanese Press and Broadcasting Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-07-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th. The Japanese press and broadcasting delegation, led by head Yokota Minoru, arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 27th. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association. The delegation consists of representatives from nine Japanese newspapers, one news agency, and three broadcasting organizations. Upon the delegation's arrival, Wu Lengxi, Jin Zhonghua, and Wang Yunsheng, vice-presidents of the All-China Journalists Association, personnel from the capital's press circles, and Japanese peace activists in Beijing, Kameta Togo and Nakamura Wan'emon, welcomed them at the station.

In the evening, the delegation was invited to attend a welcome reception hosted by Deng Tuo, president of the All-China Journalists Association. Among those present were Mei Yi, Wu Lengxi, Jin Zhonghua, Wang Yunsheng, vice-presidents of the All-China Journalists Association, heads of Beijing's news and publishing organizations, and Gong Peng, director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

At the reception, delegation leader Yokota Minoru presented gifts from the Japanese press and broadcasting circles to the All-China Journalists Association.

### 日本新闻界广播界访华代表团到京

1955-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    日本新闻界广播界访华代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　以团长横田实为首的日本新闻界广播界访华代表团一行十五人，在二十七日清晨乘火车到达北京。他们是应中国新闻工作者联谊会的邀请来我国访问的。代表团由日本的九家报纸、一家通讯社和三个广播组织的代表组成。代表团到达时，中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长吴冷西、金仲华、王芸生和首都新闻界人士，以及在京的日本和平人士龟田东伍、中村玩右卫门都到车站欢迎。
    晚间，代表团全体人员应邀出席了中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓举行的欢迎酒会。出席作陪的有中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长梅益、吴冷西、金仲华、王芸生，北京各新闻、出版机关负责人，外交部新闻司司长龚澎等人。
    酒会上，横田实团长代表日本新闻界、广播界向中国新闻工作者联谊会赠送了礼品。
