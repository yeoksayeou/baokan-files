Title: Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session; Workers in Factories and Mines Across Poland Greet National Day with Production Achievements
Author:
Date: 1955-07-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 17th - According to the Polish Press Agency, the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party held its fourth plenary session in Warsaw on July 15th and 16th.

    The plenary session heard a report by Z. Nowak, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, on the issue of agricultural development in the Five-Year Plan (1956-1960), and held discussions.

    The plenary session discussed in detail and specifically defined the production tasks proposed by the Second Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party regarding further improvement of the socialist sector of agriculture and the individual peasant economy. The plenary session also discussed ways to increase state assistance to agriculture in the next five years.

    The plenary session decided to establish a committee to be responsible for drafting a resolution on the tasks of the Five-Year Plan for agricultural development.

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 18th - Workers in factories and mines across Poland have already achieved significant results in the labor competition to welcome the National Day on July 22nd.

    The Bolesław Bierut Steel Plant has achieved outstanding results in the competition. In the first half of July, the plant completed 103% of its steelmaking plan, 104% of its ironmaking plan, and 117% of its steel rolling plan.

    In the coal mining industry, the "Gotwald", "Stalinogród", "General Zawadzki" and "Ludwik" coal mines have achieved the best results in the competition. The "Gotwald" and "Stalinogród" coal mines alone overproduced more than 6,000 tons of coal in the first thirteen days of July.

### 波兰统一工人党中央委员会举行全体会议  波兰各地工厂矿山工人以生产成就迎接国庆节

1955-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党中央委员会举行全体会议
    波兰各地工厂矿山工人以生产成就迎接国庆节
    新华社华沙十七日电　据波兰通讯社报道：波兰统一工人党中央委员会于七月十五日和十六日在华沙举行第四次全体会议。
    全体会议听取了波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员泽·诺瓦克所作的关于五年计划（一九五六—一九六○年）中发展农业问题的报告，并展开了讨论。
    全体会议详细讨论并且具体确定了波兰统一工人党第二次代表大会提出的关于进一步提高农业的社会主义部分和个体农民经济的生产任务。全体会议还讨论了在今后五年中增加国家对农业的帮助的办法。
    全体会议决定成立一个委员会，责成它拟定关于发展农业五年计划的任务的决议草案。
    据新华社华沙十八日电　波兰全国各地工厂、矿山的工人在迎接七月二十二日国庆节的劳动竞赛中已经取得了显著的成就。
    波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特钢铁工厂在竞赛中的成绩十分突出。这个工厂七月份上半月炼钢计划完成了百分之一百零三，炼铁计划完成了百分之一百零四，轧钢计划完成了百分之一百一十七。
    在煤矿工业中，“哥特瓦尔德”、“斯大林诺格罗德”、“萨瓦茨基将军”和“卢德维克”等煤矿在竞赛中成绩最好。单是“哥特瓦尔德”和“斯大林诺格罗德”两个煤矿在七月份的头十三天中就超计划生产了六千多吨煤。
