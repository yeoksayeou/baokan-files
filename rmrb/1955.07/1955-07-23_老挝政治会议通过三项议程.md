Title: Lao Political Conference Adopts Three-Item Agenda
Author:
Date: 1955-07-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 22nd (Via Vietnam News Agency, July 22nd): At the Lao political conference on July 18th, the political delegation of the Lao Fighting Units and the political delegation of the Royal Lao Government adopted the conference agenda, which included the following three points:
    I. The issue of free general elections and the unity of all Lao citizens in a common national life;
    II. The issue of guaranteeing various democratic freedoms of the people;
    III. The issue of administrative management in the provinces of Sam Neua and Phong Saly.
    Currently, the two delegations are discussing the issue of free general elections.

### 老挝政治会议通过三项议程

1955-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝政治会议通过三项议程
    新华社河内二十二日电　据越南通讯社二十二日讯：在老挝政治会议七月十八日的会议上，寮国战斗部队政治代表团和老挝王国政府政治代表团通过了会议议程，其中包括下列三点：
    一、自由普选和全体老挝公民团结在全国共同生活中的问题；
    二、保障人民的各种民主自由的问题；
    三、桑怒和丰沙里两省的行政管理问题。
    目前，两个代表团正在讨论自由普选问题。
