Title: Vietnamese Government Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-07-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th - TASS Moscow, 12th: The government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by President Ho Chi Minh, arrived in Moscow at 5:00 PM on the 12th, at the invitation of the Soviet government.

The Soviet Communist Party and government leaders Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Pospelov, Suslov, and others welcomed the delegation at the Central Airport.

Also present to welcome the delegation were deputies of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, ministers of various Soviet ministries, social activists, and representatives of the working people of Moscow. Diplomatic envoys from some countries to the Soviet Union were also at the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 越南政府代表团到达莫斯科

1955-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    越南政府代表团到达莫斯科
    新华社十二日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：应苏联政府的邀请到苏联访问的、以胡志明主席为首的越南民主共和国政府代表团十二日下午五时到达莫斯科。
    在中央飞机场欢迎代表团的有苏联共产党和政府的领导人布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、波斯伯洛夫、苏斯洛夫等。
    前往欢迎代表团的还有苏联最高苏维埃代表、苏联各部部长、社会活动家、莫斯科劳动人民的代表。在机场欢迎代表团的还有一些国家驻苏联的外交使节。
