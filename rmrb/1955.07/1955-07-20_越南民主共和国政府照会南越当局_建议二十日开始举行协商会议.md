Title: Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Sends Note to South Vietnamese Authorities Suggesting Consultative Conference Begin on the 20th
Author:
Date: 1955-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 19th: Vietnam News Agency reported on July 19th: Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, upon the order of Chairman and Premier Ho Chi Minh and on his behalf, sent a note to the Head of State and Premier of the State of Vietnam (South Vietnam) on July 19th. This note has also been sent to the Chairman of the Geneva Conference, the Chairman of the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam, and the French government.

The note recounted Article Seven of the Final Declaration of the Geneva Conference and Article 14(a) of the Agreement for the Cessation of Hostilities in Vietnam, and also recounted the statement of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on June 6th, which stated that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is prepared to hold consultative conferences with the responsible authorities in Southern Vietnam who are representative, in order to prepare for the general elections next year to achieve national unification. The note then pointed out the great importance of holding consultative conferences between the representative responsible authorities of North and South Vietnam as scheduled.

The note concluded by suggesting that the representative responsible authorities of South Vietnam appoint their representatives to hold consultative conferences with the representatives of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, starting from July 20, 1955, as stipulated by the Geneva Accords, at a location on Vietnamese territory agreed upon by both sides, to discuss the issue of holding free general elections throughout Vietnam to unify the country.

### 越南民主共和国政府照会南越当局  建议二十日开始举行协商会议

1955-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国政府照会南越当局
    建议二十日开始举行协商会议
    新华社河内十九日电　越南通讯社十九日讯：越南民主共和国副总理范文同，根据主席兼总理胡志明的命令并且代表他，在七月十九日送给越南国（南越）国长和总理一件照会。这个照会同时也已经送交日内瓦会议的主席、越南国际监督和监察委员会主席以及法国政府。
    照会叙述了日内瓦会议最后宣言第七条和越南停止敌对行动协定第十四条甲款，并且叙述了越南民主共和国政府六月六日的声明，这个声明表示，越南民主共和国政府准备同越南南方有代表性的负责当局举行协商会议，以筹备明年的普选来实现全国的统一。照会接着指出，如期举行越南北方和南方有代表性的负责当局之间的协商会议有巨大的重要性。
    照会最后建议，越南南方有代表性的负责当局任命他们的代表同越南民主共和国政府的代表，从日内瓦协议所规定的一九五五年七月二十日开始，在双方同意的越南领土上的一个地点举行协商会议，讨论在越南全境举行自由普选以统一国家的问题。
