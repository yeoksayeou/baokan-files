Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Visits Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1955-07-06
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, visited Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China at 9 a.m. on the 5th. Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling had a conversation for about thirty minutes. (Attached photo)
    Chairman Ho Chi Minh visits Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. The picture shows Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling shaking hands. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Huaxue photographed

### 胡志明主席拜会宋庆龄副委员长

1955-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席拜会宋庆龄副委员长
　  新华社五日讯　越南民主共和国主席胡志明，在五日上午九时拜会了中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄。胡志明主席和宋庆龄副委员长作了约三十分钟的交谈。（附图片）
