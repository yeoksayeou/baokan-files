Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Arrives in Warsaw to Attend World Youth Festival
Author:
Date: 1955-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 26th: The Chinese youth delegation, comprising over 250 members, including some members of the art troupe, arrived in Warsaw by train on the morning of the 25th to attend the Fifth World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship. The head of the delegation, Luo Yi, the deputy head, Qu Tangliang, and the secretary-general, Wu Xueqian, had already arrived by plane on the morning of the 24th.

The Chinese youth were warmly welcomed at the Warsaw station. Among those welcoming them were Bernini, Chairman of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Starewicz, Chairman of the Polish Festival Preparatory Committee, and representatives of Polish youth.

The band played the World Democratic Youth March and Chinese songs. Shouts of "Peace" and "Friendship" in Polish and Chinese resounded throughout the station again and again.

Bernini, representing the International Preparatory Committee of the Festival and the World Federation of Democratic Youth, extended a warm welcome to the Chinese youth delegation.

Luo Yi, speaking at the station, first paid tribute on behalf of the Chinese youth to the Polish youth and the youth of all countries participating in the festival. He stated that during the festival, the Chinese youth delegation would, together with the youth of all countries, contribute their utmost to the cause of peace and friendship.

Luo Yi's speech was repeatedly interrupted by applause and cheers. Warm and loud songs from Chinese and Polish youth reverberated through the station, and they walked out of the station hand in hand, shoulder to shoulder.

### 参加世界青年联欢节  中国青年代表团到华沙

1955-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    参加世界青年联欢节
    中国青年代表团到华沙
    新华社华沙二十六日电　参加第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的中国青年代表团和一部分艺术团团员共二百五十多人，在二十五日早晨乘火车到达华沙。代表团团长罗毅、副团长区棠亮、秘书长吴学谦已经在二十四日上午乘飞机到达。
    中国青年们在华沙车站受到热情的欢迎。在欢迎者的行列中有世界民主青年联盟主席柏尼尼，波兰联欢节筹备委员会主席斯塔列维奇、和波兰青年的代表。
    乐队奏起世界民主青年进行曲和中国的歌曲。用波兰话和中国语喊出的“和平”和“友谊”的声音一次又一次响遍整个车站。
    柏尼尼代表联欢节国际筹备委员会和世界民主青年联盟对中国青年代表团表示热烈的欢迎。
    罗毅在车站讲话时，首先代表中国青年向波兰青年和参加本届联欢节的各国青年致敬。他表示中国青年代表团在联欢节期间，将同各国青年一道，为和平和友谊的事业贡献出自己最大的力量。
    罗毅的讲话一再被掌声和欢呼声所打断。车站上传出中波青年们热情嘹亮的歌声，他们手拉手肩并肩一起走出车站。
