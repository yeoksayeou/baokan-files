Title: Chilean, Vietnamese and Other Delegations Arrive in Beijing After Attending the World Mothers' Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-07-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 30th. The Chilean delegation of five members, led by Madam Lia Rafayee, president of the delegation, who attended the World Mothers' Congress and were invited to visit China by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 30th. Also arriving on the same plane were one representative from Bolivia and two representatives from Colombia, who attended the World Mothers' Congress and were invited to visit China by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.
    On the same day, the Vietnamese delegation of five members, led by Đoàn Ca Vân Thanh, president of the delegation, who attended the World Mothers' Congress and were passing through China, arrived in Beijing by plane. They will also visit China.

### 出席世界母亲大会后  智利越南等国代表团到京

1955-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    出席世界母亲大会后
    智利越南等国代表团到京
    新华社三十日讯　应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请前来我国参观访问的出席世界母亲大会的智利代表团一行五人，由团长利亚·拉法耶夫人率领在三十日下午乘飞机到达北京。同机到达的还有应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请前来我国参观访问的出席世界母亲大会的玻利维亚代表一人，哥伦比亚代表二人。
    同日，出席世界母亲大会后路经我国的越南代表团一行五人，由团长歌文清率领，乘飞机到达北京。她们也将在我国进行参观访问。
