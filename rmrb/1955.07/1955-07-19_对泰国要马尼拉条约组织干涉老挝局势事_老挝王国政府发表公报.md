Title: Lao Kingdom Government Issues Communiqué Regarding Thailand's Request for SEATO Intervention in Lao Situation
Author:
Date: 1955-07-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 18th. Vientiane Dispatch: The Lao Kingdom Government issued a communiqué on the 17th regarding the Thai government's suggestion to request the Manila Treaty Organization to intervene in the Lao situation.

    The communiqué stated that the Laotian problem will be resolved "within the scope of the Geneva Accords and in accordance with the procedures stipulated by the Geneva Accords." This problem is an "internal affair."

    The spokesman for the Lao Kingdom Government also said that Thailand's actions were neither consulted with the Kingdom Government nor formally notified to it. The Kingdom Government learned about this matter from newspaper reports. The Kingdom Government believes that Thailand's actions are "particularly untimely."

### 对泰国要马尼拉条约组织干涉老挝局势事  老挝王国政府发表公报

1955-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    对泰国要马尼拉条约组织干涉老挝局势事
    老挝王国政府发表公报
    新华社河内十八日电　万象消息：老挝王国政府在十七日就泰国政府要求马尼拉条约组织干涉老挝局势的建议，发表了一项公报。
    公报说：寮国问题将“在日内瓦协定的范围内以及按照日内瓦协定所规定的程序”来解决。这个问题是一个“内政问题”。
    老挝王国政府的发言人还说：泰国的行动既没有和王国政府磋商过，也没有正式地通知它。王国政府是从报纸消息中知道这件事的。王国政府认为泰国的行动“特别不合时宜”。
