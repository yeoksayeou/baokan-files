Title: Composition of the Soviet Delegation to the Four-Power Summit Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-07-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 14th. TASS, Moscow, July 14th: The Soviet Council of Ministers has approved the composition of the Soviet government delegation to the Four-Power Summit Conference in Geneva.

    The composition of the delegation is as follows: Nikolai A. Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR (Head of Delegation), Nikita S. Khrushchev, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Vyacheslav M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Georgy K. Zhukov, Minister of Defense of the USSR, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Andrei A. Gromyko, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR.
    Yakov A. Malik, Sergei A. Vinogradov, Georgy N. Zarubin, Georgy M. Pushkin and Vladimir S. Semyonov have been appointed as advisors to the delegation.

### 苏联出席四大国政府首脑会议代表团组成

1955-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联出席四大国政府首脑会议代表团组成
    新华社十四日讯塔斯社莫斯科十四日讯：苏联部长会议批准了出席日内瓦四大国政府首脑会议的苏联政府代表团。
    代表团组成人员如下：苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁（代表团团长）、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫、苏联国防部长、苏联元帅格·康·朱可夫、苏联外交部第一副部长安·安·葛罗米柯。
    任命雅·亚·马立克、斯·阿·维诺格拉多夫、格·纳·查鲁宾、格·马·普希金和弗·斯·谢苗诺夫为代表团顾问。
