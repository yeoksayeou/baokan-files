Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the German Ambassador König and Accepts a Gift; Our Embassy in Germany Presents a Gift to Premier Grotewohl
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 26th: On the occasion of the first anniversary of the joint communiqué issued by the Premiers of China and Germany, Premier Zhou Enlai received König, the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, on the afternoon of July 26th. He also accepted a bronze statue of a Chinese female worker, cast by a German artist, presented by Ambassador König on behalf of Premier Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic.

    Premier Zhou Enlai greatly appreciated the work of the German artist and asked Ambassador König to convey his gratitude to the government of the German Democratic Republic and to Premier Grotewohl. In the conversation, Premier Zhou Enlai pointed out that the people of China and Germany have supported each other in the struggle to safeguard world peace in the past, and will continue to do so in the future.

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 25th: Wang Yutian, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, presented a huge wool tapestry depicting the scene of the meeting and handshake between the Premiers of China and Germany to Premier Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic on the occasion of the first anniversary of Premier Zhou Enlai's friendly visit to the German Democratic Republic (July 23rd) at the invitation of the latter.

    Premier Grotewohl received Wang Yutian on the 23rd for this purpose. During the meeting, Grotewohl expressed his gratitude to the government of the People's Republic of China to Wang Yutian and emphasized the great historical significance of Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to the German Democratic Republic last year in developing the friendly relations between the people of China and Germany.

    Premier Grotewohl also praised the vividness, realism, and exquisiteness of the tapestry embroidery. He considered it a testament to the outstanding talent of Chinese artisans.

### 周恩来总理接见德大使柯尼希并接受赠礼  我驻德大使馆赠礼给格罗提渥总理

1955-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见德大使柯尼希并接受赠礼
    我驻德大使馆赠礼给格罗提渥总理
    新华社二十六日讯　值此中德两国总理发表会谈公报一周年的时候，周恩来总理在二十六日下午接见了德意志民主共和国驻我国大使柯尼希，并接受柯尼希大使代表德意志民主共和国格罗提渥总理赠送的德国艺术家铸造的一个中国女工的铜像。
    周恩来总理对德国艺术家的作品极为欣赏，并请柯尼希大使向德意志民主共和国政府和格罗提渥总理转致谢意。周恩来总理在谈话中指出中德两国人民在维护世界和平的斗争中，过去是，今后仍然是互相支持的。
    新华社柏林二十五日讯　中国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆临时代办王雨田在周恩来总理应邀来德意志民主共和国进行友好访问的一周年（七月二十三日），向德意志民主共和国格罗提渥总理赠送绣有中德两国总理握手会见时的情景的巨幅毛绣壁毯。
    格罗提渥总理为此在二十三日接见了王雨田。在晤谈中，格罗提渥向王雨田表示对中华人民共和国政府的谢意，并且强调指出周恩来总理去年对德意志民主共和国的访问在发展中德两国人民友好关系方面的重大历史意义。
    格罗提渥总理还赞扬壁毯绣像的生动、逼真和精美。他认为这是中国工艺家卓越才能的证明。
