Title: Ministry of Culture Holds Film Reception to Celebrate Polish National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-07-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 21st. The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China held a film reception on the evening of the 21st, screening the Polish color film "Five Boys from Barska Street" to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the Polish National Day of Rebirth.
    The film depicts the lives of Polish youth and their participation in socialist construction. The film won an international award at the Cannes International Film Festival in France in 1954. The film was warmly praised by the audience today.
    Before the film screening, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, and Majman, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, delivered speeches successively, congratulating the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the people of China and Poland, and congratulating the development and consolidation of cultural exchange between the two countries.
    Those present at the film reception tonight included Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture; Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of Higher Education; Wei Que, Vice Minister of Education; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the State Council; Wang Lanxi, Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture; Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and people from all walks of life in the capital.
    Majman, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, and embassy officials, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, also attended the film reception.

### 我文化部举行电影招待会庆祝波兰国庆节

1955-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我文化部举行电影招待会庆祝波兰国庆节
    新华社二十一日讯　二十一日晚，中华人民共和国文化部举行电影招待会，放映波兰彩色影片“从巴尔斯卡街来的五个孩子”，庆祝波兰国家复兴节十一周年。
    这部影片是描写波兰青年的生活和他们参加社会主义建设的情形。影片曾在一九五四年法国戛纳国际电影节获得了国际奖。今天这部电影放映时受到观众的热烈赞赏。
    在放映电影以前，文化部副部长夏衍和波兰驻华大使馆临时代办马伊曼相继讲话，祝贺中波两国人民牢不可破的友谊和合作事业，祝贺两国文化交流事业的发展和巩固。
    出席今晚电影招待会的，有文化部副部长夏衍，高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，教育部副部长韦悫，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，国务院对外文化联络局副局长陈忠经，文化部电影局局长王阑西，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清，以及首都各界人士。
    波兰驻华大使馆临时代办马伊曼和使馆官员，各国驻华使节和外交官员也出席了电影招待会。
