Title: Soviet Union Celebrates Soviet Sports Festival, Holds Athletic Competitions
Author:
Date: 1955-07-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Throughout the Soviet Union, the Soviet Sports Festival was joyfully celebrated on the 17th. Millions of young men and women showcased their achievements in stadiums and on sports fields.
    The largest stadium in Moscow, the Dynamo Stadium, held a grand celebration on this festival day. The highlight of the program was the friendly match between the Soviet and Chinese men's and women's basketball teams.
    On the 17th, the 2,000-kilometer Moscow-Minsk-Kiev-Kharkov-Moscow bicycle race concluded. The winner of this race was the Central Sports Club of the Soviet Ministry of Defense.
    Large-scale sports competitions were also held in many cities and villages across the country. The sports meet held in Tbilisi, the capital of Georgia, began with car and motorcycle races, and rowing competitions were held on the nearby Kura River. In Frunze, Kirghizia, and Kazakhstan, athletes held traditional football, boxing, and wrestling matches.

### 苏联各地庆祝苏联体育节并举行运动比赛

1955-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联各地庆祝苏联体育节并举行运动比赛
    据新华社讯　苏联全国各地在十七日欢欣地庆祝苏联体育节。千百万青年男女在运动场和体育场上显示了他们的成就。
    莫斯科最大的运动场狄纳莫运动场在这个节日举行了盛大的庆祝会。最精采的节目是苏联和中国的男女篮球队的友谊赛。
    十七日这天，莫斯科—明斯克—基辅—哈尔科夫—莫斯科两千公里的自行车赛结束。在这次比赛中得胜的是苏联国防部中央运动俱乐部。
    全国许多城市和乡村也都举行大规模运动比赛。在格鲁吉亚首都第比利斯举行的运动会以汽车和摩托车赛开始，并且在附近的库拉河上举行了划船比赛。在伏龙芝、吉尔吉斯和哈萨克的运动员们举行了传统的足球、拳击和摔角赛。
