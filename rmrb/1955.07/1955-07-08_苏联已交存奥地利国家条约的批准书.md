Title: The Soviet Union has deposited the Instrument of Ratification for the Austrian State Treaty
Author:
Date: 1955-07-08
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 7th: TASS Moscow, June 6th—The Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs notified the US, British, French and Austrian embassies in Moscow on July 5th: The Soviet instrument of ratification of the Austrian State Treaty (approved by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR) has been deposited in the Soviet government archives.

### 苏联已交存奥地利国家条约的批准书

1955-07-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联已交存奥地利国家条约的批准书
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科六日讯：苏联外交部七月五日通知美国、英国、法国和奥地利驻莫斯科大使馆：奥地利国家条约的苏联批准书（经苏联最高苏维埃主席团批准）已经交给苏联政府档案库保存。
