Title: National People's Congress Holds Group Discussions on Draft Five-Year Plan; Budget Committee Holds Meeting to Begin Reviewing Draft National Budget and Final Accounts

Author:
Date: 1955-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Second Session of the First National People's Congress continued group reading and discussions on the Draft First Five-Year Plan on the 11th. Most of the representative groups today read and discussed the industry and agriculture sections. Some representative groups adopted the method of reading first and then discussing. Today they have finished reading the full text of the Draft First Five-Year Plan and have begun to discuss it chapter by chapter.

Representatives of ethnic minorities such as Mongolians, Tibetans, and Uyghurs in the various representative groups have been reading the Draft First Five-Year Plan translated into Mongolian, Tibetan, and Uyghur in recent days. The meeting is specially equipped with many translators to assist the representatives of various ethnic minorities in reading and discussion.

On the 11th, the Budget Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China held its first meeting and began to review the Draft National Final Accounts for 1954 and the Draft National Budget for 1955. The meeting decided to first conduct group studies of the draft final accounts and draft budget separately. When the meeting was held, responsible personnel from the Ministry of Finance of the People's Republic of China attended to provide consultation.

### 全国人民代表大会分组讨论五年计划草案  预算委员会举行会议开始审查国家预决算草案

1955-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会分组讨论五年计划草案
    预算委员会举行会议开始审查国家预决算草案
    新华社十一日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议十一日继续分组阅读和讨论第一个五年计划草案。大部分代表小组今天阅读和讨论了工业和农业部分。有一部分代表小组采取先阅读后讨论办法，今天已将第一个五年计划草案全文阅读完毕，并开始逐章进行讨论。
    各代表小组中的蒙族、藏族、维吾尔族等少数民族代表，连日来阅读用蒙文、藏文、维吾尔文翻译的第一个五年计划草案。会议专门配备有许多翻译人员帮助各少数民族代表进行阅读和讨论。
    十一日，中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会预算委员会举行第一次会议，开始对一九五四年国家决算草案和一九五五年国家预算草案进行审查，会议决定首先分组对这个决算草案和预算草案分别进行研究。会议举行时，中华人民共和国财政部负责人员到会备咨询。
