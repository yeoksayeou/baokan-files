Title: Joint Communiqué of the Chinese and Vietnamese Governments
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 8th. During the visit of the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by President Ho Chi Minh, to China, the government delegations of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held talks in Beijing from June 27th to July 7th, based on the principles agreed upon by Chairman Mao Zedong and President Ho Chi Minh. Following the talks, a Joint Communiqué of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic Republic was issued. The signing ceremony of the communiqué was grandly held in Beijing at 8 p.m. on July 7th.

The Vietnamese side participating in the signing ceremony were: Ho Chi Minh, President and Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Truong Chinh, Secretary-General of the Vietnam Workers' Party; Le Van Hien, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce; Nguyen Van Huyên, Minister of Education; Nghiem Xuan Yem, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry; Ung Van Khiem, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Nguyen Duy Trinh, Deputy Minister of the Presidential Palace; Pham Ngoc Thach, Deputy Minister of Medical Economy; Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China; Nguyen Van Tran, Head of the Vietnamese Trade Delegation; and Chu Luong, First Secretary of the Vietnamese Embassy in China. The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony were: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Zhu De, Vice-Chairman; Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Yun and Deng Xiaoping, Vice-Premiers of the State Council; Bo Yibo, Director of the State Construction Commission; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways; Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education; Zhang Wentian and Wang Jiaxiang, Vice-Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister; and Luo Guibo, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

President and Prime Minister Ho Chi Minh signed on behalf of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Premier Zhou Enlai signed on behalf of the People's Republic of China.

After the signing, the representatives of both sides warmly shook hands and raised their glasses in congratulation. (Attached photo)

The signing Ceremony of the Joint Communiqué of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of Democratic Republic of Vietnam was held in Beijing. Premier Zhou Enlai (right) signed on behalf of the People's Republic of China, and President and Prime Minister Ho Chi Minh (left) signed on behalf of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 中越两国政府发表联合公报

1955-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　中越两国政府发表联合公报
    新华社八日讯　在胡志明主席率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团访问中国期间，中华人民共和国政府代表团和越南民主共和国政府代表团，根据毛泽东主席和胡志明主席商定的原则，从六月二十七日到七月七日在北京举行了会谈。会谈以后，发表了中华人民共和国政府和越南民主共和国政府的联合公报，公报的签字仪式七月七日下午八时在北京隆重举行。
    越南方面参加签字仪式的有：越南民主共和国主席兼总理胡志明，越南劳动党总书记长征，越南民主共和国政府财政部部长黎文献，工商部部长潘英，教育部部长阮文煊，农林部部长严春庵，外交部副部长雍文谦，主席府副部长阮维祯，医济部副部长范玉石，越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢，越南贸易代表团团长阮文珍，越南驻华大使馆一等秘书周亮。中国方面参加签字仪式的有：中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、陈叔通，国务院副总理陈云、邓小平，国家建设委员会主任薄一波，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，铁道部部长滕代远，高等教育部部长杨秀峰，外交部副部长张闻天、王稼祥，部长助理陈家康，中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波。
    越南民主共和国方面由胡志明主席兼总理签字，中华人民共和国方面由周恩来总理签字。
    签字后，双方代表热烈握手并举杯祝贺。（附图片）
