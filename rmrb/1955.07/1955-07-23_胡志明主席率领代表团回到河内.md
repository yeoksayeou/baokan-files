Title: President Ho Chi Minh Leads Delegation Back to Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1955-07-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, July 22nd: President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh, and the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam he led, who were invited to visit our country, returned to their country from Wuhan by special plane on the morning of the 22nd.

At the airport, President Ho Chi Minh and the members of the delegation repeatedly and cordially shook hands with the well-wishers to say goodbye. The people who came to see them off accompanied the distinguished guests to the front of the plane. President Ho Chi Minh and delegation member, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Truong Chinh, stood at the door of the cabin, smiling and waving. The well-wishers also waved constantly, bidding farewell to the distinguished guests.

Those who went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests included: Liu Zihou, Second Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the CPC and Governor of Hubei Province; Zhang Pinghua, Third Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the CPC and First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the CPC; Chen Zaidao and Tan Furen, heads of the leading organ of the Wuhan Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Song Kanfu, Mayor of Wuhan; Li Erzhong, Third Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the CPC; Yao Guang, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Hubei Provincial People's Committee, and others.

Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department, who accompanied the distinguished guests to Wuhan, also went to the airport to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 22nd: The government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by President Ho Chi Minh, returned to Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, by special plane at 10:00 a.m. on the 22nd, after visiting China and the Soviet Union.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army; Ton Duc Thang, Acting Chairman of the National Assembly; ministers and deputy ministers of various government departments; members of the Political Bureau and Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party; members of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front; heads of various democratic parties and mass organizations; and Vuong Thua Vu, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Military and Administrative Committee, Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Administrative Committee, and representatives of the citizens of Hanoi, totaling more than 300 people.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Sokolov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Xie Shuangqiu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; and diplomatic envoys and officials from Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Korea, and the German Democratic Republic. Also present at the airport to welcome them was a representative of the French government's General Representative to the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

When President Ho Chi Minh and Truong Chinh, members of the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, stepped off the plane, warm applause and the slogan "Long Live President Ho Chi Minh" resounded through the airport.

President Ho Chi Minh and Truong Chinh, accompanied by Pham Van Dong and Vo Nguyen Giap, reviewed the guard of honor and shook hands with the welcomers one by one.

### 胡志明主席率领代表团回到河内

1955-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席率领代表团回到河内
    新华社武汉二十二日电　应邀来我国访问的越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团，二十二日清晨乘专机自武汉返国。
    在机场上，胡志明主席和代表团团员们一再亲切地同欢送者握手道别。欢送的人陪送贵宾们到机前，胡志明主席和代表团团员、越南劳动党总书记长征站在机舱门口含笑挥手致意，欢送者也不断招手，向贵宾们告别。
    到机场欢送贵宾们的有：中共湖北省委员会第二书记、湖北省省长刘子厚，中共湖北省委员会第三书记兼中共武汉市委员会第一书记张平化，中国人民解放军武汉驻军领导机关首长陈再道、谭甫仁，武汉市市长宋侃夫，中共武汉市委员会第三书记李尔重，湖北省人民委员会外事处处长姚广等。
    陪送贵宾们来到武汉的中华人民共和国外交部副部长姬鹏飞、礼宾司司长柯华也到机场欢送。
    新华社河内二十二日电　由胡志明主席率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团在访问中国、苏联以后，已经在二十二日上午十时乘专机回到越南民主共和国首都河内。
    到机场欢迎的有越南民主共和国副总理范文同，国防部长兼越南人民军总司令武元甲，国民大会代理主席孙德胜，政府各部部长和副部长，越南劳动党政治局和中央委员会的委员们，越南国民联合战线全国委员会的委员们，各民主党派和群众团体的负责人，以及河内市军政委员会主席王承武、行政委员会主席陈维兴、河内市市民代表等三百多人。
    到机场欢迎的还有苏联驻越南民主共和国大使馆临时代办索科洛夫、中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使馆临时代办谢爽秋，以及波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、朝鲜、德意志民主共和国的外交使节和外交官员。到机场欢迎的还有法国政府驻越南民主共和国政府总代表的代表。
    当胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府代表团团员长征等步下飞机时，飞机场上响起了热烈的掌声和“胡主席万岁”的口号声。
    胡志明主席和长征在范文同和武元甲陪同下检阅了仪仗队，并和欢迎者一一握手。
