Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Visits and Performs in Chongqing; Chongqing's Vice Mayor Hosts Banquet for Indian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-07-18
Page: 1
	Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, July 16th: On July 16th, the Indian Cultural Delegation, during its first day in Chongqing, visited the Luohan Temple, a Buddhist temple built during the Northern Song Dynasty, and the Chongqing No. 1 Iron and Steel Plant. They also toured the Chongqing city area.
	At Luohan Temple, delegation leader Chanda and his wife, along with other members of the delegation, were warmly received by the host – Master Tai Kong, who had previously visited India, and the monks of the temple. Master Tai Kong accompanied the guests on a tour of the entire temple. The guests carefully toured the iron smelting workshop, the newly built steelmaking workshop, and the rolling mill workshop at the Chongqing No. 1 Iron and Steel Plant.
	While touring the Chongqing city area, the guests spent a long time at the Chaotianmen riverbank, watching with great interest the magnificent river scenery at the confluence of the Yangtze and Jialing Rivers. Afterwards, they visited the streets and shops in the downtown area of Chongqing.
	Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, July 17th: On the evening of July 16th, the Indian Cultural Delegation held a performance in Chongqing. More than 3,500 people in Chongqing watched the outstanding performances of the Indian artists with great excitement. Half an hour before the performance, the theater was full, and many people came from Dadukou, Jiulongpo, and other places by taking the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway train on the afternoon of the 16th.
	The Indian artists performed nine wonderful song and dance programs, each of which was warmly welcomed by the audience. When Indrani Rehman performed Bharatanatyam, India's oldest classical dance, the audience was captivated by her expressive eyes and sincere facial expressions, and her graceful dance was fascinating. At the audience's enthusiastic request, Indrani Rehman re-enacted a section of Bharatanatyam. The audience warmly welcomed the ballet "The Offering," jointly performed by dancers Shirin Vajifdar, Krishna Kutty, and Panikkar. After the performance, the Indian artists took four curtain calls in a row. The Kathak dance performed by young female artist A. Guha was unanimously praised by the audience. Her dance was graceful and moving, and her steps were lithe and rhythmic. After artist D. Biswas sang "Tagore's Anthem," the audience applauded continuously, so he sang the Sino-Indian friendship song in Chinese.
	Before the performance began, Chongqing's Vice Mayor Luo Shigao, on behalf of the people and cultural and artistic workers of the city, welcomed and thanked the Indian artists for performing in Chongqing. Indian Cultural Delegation leader Chanda gave a speech of thanks. After the performance, sixty representatives from Chongqing's literary and art circles rushed onto the stage to present flowers to the Indian artists. Chongqing's Vice Mayors Luo Shigao and Chen Chou, and Deputy Director of Chongqing's Cultural Bureau Pei Dongli went backstage and shook hands with the Indian artists one by one, congratulating them on the success of their performance.
	Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, July 17th: Delegation leader Chanda and his wife, as well as some members of the Indian Cultural Delegation, toured the Beibei Hot Springs scenic area in Chongqing on July 17th. On the way to Beibei Hot Springs, the guests visited the Southwest Normal College in Beibei.
	In the afternoon, the delegation's small ballet troupe performed the beautiful fairy tale ballet "Striving for Friends" for the children of Chongqing. The enthusiastic scenes of the plot strongly attracted more than 2,000 young audience members and were praised by more than 700 Chongqing literary and art workers who attended the viewing.
	Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, July 17th: Chongqing's Vice Mayor Luo Shigao hosted a banquet on the evening of July 17th to entertain all members of the Indian Cultural Delegation, headed by Chanda.
	Indian Ambassador R.K. Nehru, who arrived in Chongqing by plane from Beijing today, was invited to attend the banquet.
	Chongqing's Vice Mayor Chen Chou, Deputy Directors of the Cultural Bureau Pei Dongli and Xiao Qin, and people from Chongqing's cultural and artistic circles and the press also attended the banquet.
	At the beginning of the banquet, Chongqing's Vice Mayor Luo Shigao delivered a speech, in which he expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Indian Cultural Delegation on behalf of the people of Chongqing. He congratulated the delegation on the complete success of its visit and performance in Chongqing, and wished for the friendship and unity of the people of China and India.
	Next, delegation leader Chanda delivered a speech in reply.
	During the banquet, the famous Sichuan Opera actor Jing Hua sang "Stealing the Red Shawl," and Gao Fenglian and Chen Guixian sang the "Weaving Silk" section from "Huaiyin Ji." Some actors from the Chongqing Municipal Art Troupe and others sang Sichuan folk songs. Indian artists also gave wonderful performances at the banquet, and V. K. Lokur sang two Chinese songs in Chinese.

### 印度文化代表团在重庆市参观和表演  重庆市副市长设宴招待印度文化代表团

1955-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团在重庆市参观和表演
    重庆市副市长设宴招待印度文化代表团
    据新华社重庆十六日电　十六日，印度文化代表团在重庆第一天的访问中，参观了北宋年间修建的佛教寺院罗汉寺和重庆第一钢铁厂，并游览了重庆市区。
    在罗汉寺，钱达团长和他的夫人及代表团其他团员们，受到了主人——曾经到过印度的太空法师和寺院的僧众的热情接待。太空法师陪同客人们参观了整个寺院。贵宾们在重庆第一钢铁厂仔细地参观了炼铁车间、新建炼钢车间及轧钢车间。
    在游览重庆市区时，贵宾们在朝天门江岸停留很久，极有兴趣地观看了长江和嘉陵江合流处雄伟的江景。随后，他们参观了重庆市中心区的街道和商店。
    据新华社重庆十七日电　十六日晚，印度文化代表团在重庆市举行了访问演出。重庆市三千五百多人十分兴奋地欣赏了印度艺术家们杰出的表演。演出前半小时，戏场内就坐满了人，很多人是从大渡口、九龙坡等地搭乘十六日下午成渝铁路火车赶来的。
    印度艺术家们演出了九个精采的歌舞节目，每个节目都受到了观众的热烈欢迎。当印·拉赫曼表演印度最古老的古典舞蹈——婆罗多舞时，观众们被她传神的眼睛和面部真挚的表情所吸引，她的优美的舞姿引人入胜。印·拉赫曼在观众的热烈鼓掌要求下，重演了婆罗多舞中的一节。观众热烈欢迎舞蹈家希林·瓦吉弗达尔，克里希纳·库蒂，潘尼迦等人合演的芭蕾舞剧“赏赐”，表演结束后，印度艺术家们连续谢幕四次。年轻的女艺术家阿·古哈表演的卡塔克舞，受到观众的一致赞扬。她的舞姿优美动人，舞步婀娜而有节奏。艺术家德·毕斯瓦斯演唱“泰戈尔的颂歌”后，观众不断鼓掌，他就用中国话唱了印中友好歌。
    重庆市副市长罗士高在表演开始前，代表全市人民和文化艺术工作者欢迎并感谢印度艺术家们在重庆演出。印度文化代表团团长钱达致词感谢。演出结束后，重庆市文艺界代表六十人拥上舞台，向印度艺术家们献花。重庆市副市长罗士高、陈筹，重庆市文化局副局长裴东篱到后台和印度艺术家们一一握手，庆贺他们演出的成功。
    据新华社重庆十七日电　印度文化代表团钱达团长和夫人以及一部分团员十七日游览了重庆北温泉风景区。在去北温泉的途中，客人们曾经参观了北碚西南师范学院。
    代表团的小芭蕾舞剧团下午向重庆市的少年儿童们表演了优美的童话舞剧“争取朋友”，剧情的热烈场面，强烈地吸引着两千多个小观众，并受到参加观摩的重庆市文艺工作者七百多人的赞赏。
    新华社重庆十七日电　重庆市副市长罗士高在十七日晚设宴招待以钱达为首的印度文化代表团全体人员。
    应邀参加宴会的，有今日由北京乘飞机到达重庆的印度大使赖嘉文。
    重庆市副市长陈筹、文化局副局长裴东篱、萧秦和重庆市文化艺术界、新闻界人士也参加了宴会。
    宴会开始时，重庆市副市长罗士高致词，他代表重庆市人民，向印度文化代表团表示衷心的感谢。并祝贺代表团在重庆访问和演出圆满成功，祝贺中印两国人民的友谊和团结。
    接着，钱达团长致词答谢。
    在宴会进行中，川剧著名演员竞华唱了“盗红绡”，高凤莲和陈桂贤合唱了“槐荫记”中的“织绫”一段。重庆市文工团等的部分演员演唱了四川民歌。印度艺术家们也在宴会上作了精采表演，维·克·洛基亚还用中国话唱了两首中国歌曲。
