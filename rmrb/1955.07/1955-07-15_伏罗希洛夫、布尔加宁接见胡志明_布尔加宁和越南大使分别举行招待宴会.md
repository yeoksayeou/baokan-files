Title: Voroshilov and Bulganin Receive Ho Chi Minh; Bulganin and the Vietnamese Ambassador Hold Separate Receptions

Author:
Date: 1955-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 14th.  Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, received Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on the 13th. Accompanying President Ho Chi Minh were Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union. Present at the reception were Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Begov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Lavrischechev, Soviet Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, also received President Ho Chi Minh, Truong Chinh, and Nguyen Luong Bang on the 13th. Molotov and Lavrischechev were also present at the reception.

On the same day, Chairman Bulganin hosted a luncheon in honor of President Ho Chi Minh and the entire delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.  The Soviet side attending the banquet included Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Suslov, Khrushchev, and other leaders of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Government, as well as other heads of departments of the Supreme Soviet and the Soviet Government. Liu Xiao, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, also attended the banquet.

On the same day, Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, held a banquet in honor of the visit of the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union. President Ho Chi Minh and members of the delegation attended the banquet. The Soviet side attending the banquet included leaders of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Government, and other heads of departments of the Supreme Soviet and the Soviet Government. Liu Xiao, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, also attended the banquet.

At 11:30 a.m. on the 13th, President Ho Chi Minh and members of the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam also paid their respects at the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum and laid wreaths. One wreath read: "Dedicated to the immortal V.I. Lenin and J.V. Stalin, respectfully offered by the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam"; another wreath read: "Dedicated to the immortal V.I. Lenin and J.V. Stalin, respectfully offered by Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam."

After the mausoleum visit, President Ho Chi Minh and members of the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam visited the Kremlin. The guests viewed Lenin's former bedroom and office. Members of the delegation also visited the Tsar Bell, the Tsar Cannon, and other historical sites.

In the afternoon of the same day, some members of the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam visited the Stalin Automobile Factory.

### 伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁接见胡志明  布尔加宁和越南大使分别举行招待宴会

1955-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁接见胡志明
    布尔加宁和越南大使分别举行招待宴会
    新华社莫斯科十四日电  苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫十三日接见了越南民主共和国主席胡志明。伴随着胡志明主席的有越南劳动党总书记长征、越南民主共和国驻苏联大使阮良朋。接见时在座的有苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫，还有苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫和苏联驻越南民主共和国大使拉夫里谢夫。
    苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁也在十三日接见了胡志明主席和长征、阮良朋。接见时，莫洛托夫和拉夫里谢夫也在座。
    同日，布尔加宁主席设午宴招待胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府代表团全体人员。苏联方面出席宴会的有苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫等苏联共产党和苏联政府的领导人以及苏联最高苏维埃和苏联政府各部门的其他一些负责人。中华人民共和国驻苏联大使刘晓也出席了宴会。
    同日，越南民主共和国驻苏联大使阮良朋为越南民主共和国政府代表团访问苏联举行了宴会。胡志明主席和代表团人员出席了宴会。苏联方面出席宴会的有苏联共产党和苏联政府的领导人和苏联最高苏维埃和苏联政府各部门的其他一些负责人。中华人民共和国驻苏联大使刘晓也出席了宴会。
    胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府代表团人员还于十三日上午十一时三十分拜谒了列宁—斯大林陵墓，并且献了花圈。一个花圈上写着：“献给不朽的弗·伊·列宁和约·维·斯大林越南民主共和国政府代表团敬献”；另一花圈上写着：“献给不朽的弗·伊·列宁和约·维·斯大林越南民主共和国主席胡志明敬献”。
    在谒陵后，胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府代表团人员随即参观了克里姆林宫。客人们在这里瞻仰了列宁过去的卧室和办公室。代表团人员还参观了钟王、炮王及其他古迹。
    同日下午，越南民主共和国政府代表团的部分团员访问了斯大林汽车工厂。
