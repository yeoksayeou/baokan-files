Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Visits Belgrade
Author:
Date: 1955-07-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade dispatch: Indian Prime Minister Nehru arrived in Belgrade on June 30 for an official visit, according to a TASS report from Belgrade.

Those who went to the airport to welcome Nehru included: Yugoslav President Tito, President of the Federal People's Assembly Pijade, Vice Presidents of the Federal Executive Council Kardelj, Ranković, Vukmanović-Tempo and Kolački.

Nehru delivered a speech at the airport, conveying greetings to the people of Yugoslavia.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁到贝尔格莱德访问

1955-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度总理尼赫鲁到贝尔格莱德访问
    新华社一日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德讯：印度总理尼赫鲁六月三十日到达贝尔格莱德作正式的访问。
    前往机场欢迎尼赫鲁的有：南斯拉夫总统铁托，联邦国民议会议长皮雅杰，联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔、兰科维奇、伏克曼诺维奇—泰波和科拉柯维奇等人。
    尼赫鲁在机场上发表谈话，向南斯拉夫人民致意。
