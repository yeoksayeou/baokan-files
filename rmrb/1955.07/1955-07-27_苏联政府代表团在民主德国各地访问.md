Title: Soviet Government Delegation Visits Various Parts of Democratic Germany
Author:
Date: 1955-07-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 26th: According to TASS, the Soviet government delegation, accompanied by Grotewohl, Matern, and other leaders of the German Democratic Republic government and the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, visited various parts of the German Democratic Republic on the 25th.

	The streets and squares of all the cities and villages through which the Soviet government delegation passed were packed with welcoming crowds. The Soviet and German Democratic Republic flags flew everywhere.

	Representatives of the working people of the Halle district greeted the delegation on the Elbe bridge near Dessau, where a mass rally was held. The attendees listened intently to Khrushchev's speech. Khrushchev briefly described the work of the Geneva Conference and explained the Soviet government's position on the German question. Amidst the storm of applause from the attendees, Khrushchev exclaimed: Long live the great German people! Long live the friendship between Germany and the Soviet Union!

	The Soviet government delegation visited the Plant Research Institute in Bernburg near Halle, where it was warmly welcomed by the institute's staff and nearby residents. Bulganin and Khrushchev learned about the work of the institute and spoke with the institute's leaders and scientific staff.

	The Soviet government delegation also visited one of the largest enterprises in the German Democratic Republic, the Buna Chemical Plant. Khrushchev spoke to the workers. Bulganin and Khrushchev also spoke with the factory's leaders. They inquired with concern about all aspects of the enterprise's life.

	On the same evening, after the Soviet government delegation returned to Berlin, Soviet Ambassador Pushkin held a grand reception for the Soviet government delegation's visit to the German Democratic Republic.

### 苏联政府代表团在民主德国各地访问

1955-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府代表团在民主德国各地访问
    新华社二十六日讯　据塔斯社柏林二十六日讯：苏联政府代表团在德意志民主共和国政府和德国统一社会党领导人格罗提渥、马特恩等陪同下，二十五日在德意志民主共和国各地访问。
    苏联政府代表团所经过的各城市和乡村的街道和广场上都挤满了欢迎的人群。到处飘扬着苏联国旗和德意志民主共和国国旗。
    哈雷区的劳动人民代表在德骚附近的易北河桥上迎接代表团，并且在这里举行了群众大会。到会的人聚精会神地听了赫鲁晓夫的讲话。赫鲁晓夫简短地叙述了日内瓦会议的工作，说明了苏联政府在德国问题上的立场。在到会者暴风雨般的掌声中，赫鲁晓夫高呼：伟大的德国人民万岁！德苏友谊万岁！
    苏联政府代表团参观了哈雷附近勃恩堡的植物研究所，受到了该所工作人员和附近居民的热烈欢迎。布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫了解了研究所的工作，同研究所的领导人和科学工作人员进行了谈话。
    苏联政府代表团还参观了德意志民主共和国最大的企业之一——布纳化学工厂。赫鲁晓夫向工人们讲了话。布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫还同工厂的领导人进行了谈话。他们关心地问到企业生活的各个方面。
    同一天晚上，在苏联政府代表团回到柏林后，苏联大使普希金为苏联政府代表团访问德意志民主共和国举行了盛大的招待会。
