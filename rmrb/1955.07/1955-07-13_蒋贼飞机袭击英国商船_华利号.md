Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes Attack British Merchant Ship "Inch Wells"
Author:
Date: 1955-07-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, July 10th: At 10:05 AM on July 9th, the British merchant ship "Inch Wells", en route from Fuzhou to Shanghai, was attacked by four U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek bandit F-84 jet fighters when it reached the sea east of Meihua Town, Minjiang Estuary. The Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes strafed the "Inch Wells" multiple times and dropped six bombs, one of which landed on the stern of the ship.

### 蒋贼飞机袭击英国商船“华利号”

1955-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

    蒋贼飞机袭击英国商船“华利号”
    新华社福州十日电　九日上午十时零五分，英国商船“华利号”（Inch Wells）在从福州驶往上海的途中，行至闽江口梅花镇以东海面时，遭到四架美造蒋贼F—八四型喷气式飞机的袭击。蒋贼飞机向“华利号”扫射多次，并投弹六枚，其中一枚落于船的尾部。
