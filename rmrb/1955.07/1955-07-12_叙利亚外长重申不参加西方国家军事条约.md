Title: Syrian Foreign Minister Reiterates Not Joining Western Military Treaties
Author:
Date: 1955-07-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 11th: Syrian Foreign Minister Azem, after recently visiting the United States, Britain, and France, reiterated Syria's policy of not joining Western military treaties at a press conference held on the 9th after returning to Damascus. He said that Syria has told Western countries that it is determined to continue to pursue a policy guided by Syria's national interests and is determined not to participate in any military alliances. He said, "There is no question of Syria possibly joining a military alliance at all." Azem said, "However, Syria is interested in cooperating in efforts to maintain world peace."
    Xinhua News Agency: A Syrian parliamentary delegation of twenty people flew from Damascus to Moscow by special plane on the 10th. The delegation was led by Rafik Bahour, Deputy Speaker of the House of Representatives of the Parliament.

### 叙利亚外长重申不参加西方国家军事条约

1955-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚外长重申不参加西方国家军事条约
    新华社十一日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚外交部长阿泽姆最近访问了美国、英国和法国回到大马士革以后在九日举行的一次记者招待会上，重申叙利亚不参加西方国家的军事条约的政策。他说，叙利亚已经告诉西方国家，它决心继续奉行以叙利亚民族利益为指针的政策，并且决心不参加任何军事联盟。他说：“根本说不上叙利亚可能参加军事联盟的问题。”阿泽姆说，“但是，叙利亚对于在维护世界和平的努力方面进行合作感到兴趣。”
    据新华社讯　由二十人组成的叙利亚议会代表团在十日乘专机从大马士革飞往莫斯科。代表团由议会众议院副议长拉菲克·巴胡尔率领。
