Title: Premier Nehru and President Tito Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-07-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, July 2nd: According to TASS, Indian Prime Minister Nehru, who is visiting Belgrade, attended a meeting of the Belgrade City People's Committee on July 1st and received a certificate of honorary citizenship of Belgrade at the meeting.

　　At 11:00 am, Nehru held talks with President Tito.

　　In the afternoon, Nehru attended a luncheon hosted by Yugoslav President Tito in his honor. Nehru and Tito exchanged speeches at the luncheon.

### 尼赫鲁总理和铁托总统举行会谈

1955-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　尼赫鲁总理和铁托总统举行会谈
    新华社二日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德一日讯：在贝尔格莱德访问的印度总理尼赫鲁，七月一日出席了贝尔格莱德市人民委员会的会议，在会上接受了贝尔格莱德荣誉公民的证书。
    上午十一时，尼赫鲁同铁托总统举行了会谈。
    下午，尼赫鲁出席了南斯拉夫总统铁托招待他的午宴。尼赫鲁和铁托在宴会上互相致词。
