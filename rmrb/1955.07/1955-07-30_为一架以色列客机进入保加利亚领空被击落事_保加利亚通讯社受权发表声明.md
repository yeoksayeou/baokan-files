Title: On the Downing of an Israeli Passenger Plane Entering Bulgarian Airspace: Bulgarian News Agency Authorized to Issue Statement
Author:
Date: 1955-07-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, July 28th (TASS dispatch): The Bulgarian News Agency reported: On July 27th, at 7:35 am Bulgarian time, a passenger plane, later identified as Israeli, deviated from its flight path and entered Bulgarian airspace in the region of Tran without prior notification. It flew over the cities of Stankei-Dimitrov and Blagoevgrad, heading south towards the city of Petrich.

Bulgarian air defense forces were unable to identify the nationality of the aircraft and, after issuing multiple warnings, opened fire. As a result, the aircraft was shot down and crashed in the area just north of Petrich.

All personnel on board the aircraft perished. In light of this, the Bulgarian News Agency is authorized to declare: The Bulgarian government and the entire Bulgarian public express their deepest regret for the unfortunate accident that occurred. The Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria has appointed a government commission consisting of Foreign Minister Dr. Niychev, Minister of Internal Affairs Tsankov, Minister of National Defense General Panchevski, Minister of Health and Social Welfare Dr. Kolarov, and Supreme Prosecutor Chobanov, in order to determine the circumstances of the unfortunate accident in more detail.

### 为一架以色列客机进入保加利亚领空被击落事  保加利亚通讯社受权发表声明

1955-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    为一架以色列客机进入保加利亚领空被击落事
    保加利亚通讯社受权发表声明
    新华社二十九日讯　据塔斯社索非亚二十八日讯：保加利亚通讯社报道：七月二十七日，保加利亚时间七时三十五分，有事后获悉为以色列的客机一架离开了自己的航线，在特雷恩市地区不经预先通知进入了保加利亚领空，飞过了斯坦凯—季米特洛夫市和布拉戈耶夫格拉德市，朝南飞向彼得里士市。
    保加利亚的防空部队没有可能辨明飞机所属的国籍，在作了多次警告以后开火，结果，这架飞机被击落，并且在彼得里士市稍北的地区坠毁。
    飞机上全部人员都已毙命。为此，保加利亚通讯社受权声明：保加利亚政府和保加利亚全体公众对于发生的不幸事故表示深深的遗憾。保加利亚人民共和国部长会议任命了由外交部长湼伊切夫博士、内务部长赞科夫、国防部长潘切夫斯基大将、卫生与社会福利部长科拉罗夫博士和最高检察长乔班诺夫组成的政府委员会，以便更详细地确定所发生的不幸事故的情况。
