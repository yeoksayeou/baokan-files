Title: Li-Zhan Railway Officially Opens to Traffic on July 1st; Railway Construction Troops and Representatives from All Circles in South China Hold Celebration Rally
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 1st: The Railway Construction Troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and representatives from all circles in South China held a celebration rally today on the square of the Zhanjiang South Station to mark the opening of the Li-Zhan Railway. Among those attending the celebration rally were Wang Zhen, Commander of the Railway Construction Troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Yin Linping, Head of the South China Delegation from All Circles Celebrating the Opening of the Li-Zhan Railway, Director of the Transportation Department of the CCP Central Committee South China Bureau, and concurrently Vice Governor of Guangdong Province; Zhang Boquan and Xiao Junying, Deputy Heads of the Delegation and Vice Chairmen of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Liu Su, Representative of the People's Liberation Army stationed in the Guangzhou area; Chen Zaili, Vice Governor of Guangxi Province; Yin Shiyan, Representative of the Ministry of Railways and Acting Director of the Liuzhou Railway Bureau; responsible persons from various organs and people's organizations in the Yuexi area of Guangdong Province and Zhanjiang City, as well as local garrison troops and citizens of Zhanjiang City, totaling approximately 10,000 people.

　　At precisely 6:00 AM, the celebration rally commenced. After Zhang Boquan, Secretary of the Zhanjiang Municipal Committee of the CCP, delivered the opening remarks, Wang Zhen, Commander of the Railway Construction Troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, spoke. He said: The completion of this railway provides favorable conditions for enlivening our country's foreign trade. It will also have a very good effect on the cause of socialist construction in the South China region. After this railway opens to traffic, our country's southern coastal defense will also be further consolidated. This also provides favorable conditions for the current struggle to liberate Taiwan and the enemy-occupied islands along the coast, and to smash the pirate activities carried out by the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique under the instigation of American imperialism. He then pointed out: The rapid completion of the Li-Zhan Railway is due to the correct leadership of the Central Government, the active support of the people of the whole country, especially the people of Guangdong and Guangxi, and the selfless labor of the commanders and fighters of the Railway Construction Troops, who rapidly improved their scientific and technical knowledge and operational skills. All of this embodies the improvement of our country's socialist industrial production level and the superiority of our country's socialist construction. Yin Linping, Head of the South China Delegation from All Circles Celebrating the Opening of the Li-Zhan Railway, Director of the Transportation Department of the CCP Central Committee South China Bureau, and concurrently Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, and Chen Zaili, Vice Governor of Guangxi Province, spoke successively. In their speeches, they unanimously expressed warm congratulations and respect to the commanders and fighters of the Railway Construction Troops and the railway construction workers. They also wished the Railway Construction Troops to achieve more and greater successes in future new construction tasks. At the celebration rally, congratulatory telegrams from Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways, to all commanders and fighters of the Railway Construction Troops, and from the troops stationed in the Guangzhou area to all commanders and fighters of the Railway Construction Troops, were also read out.

　　After the celebration rally, nearly ten thousand soldiers and civilians joyfully marched to the station platform. At 7:25 AM, Wang Zhen, Commander of the Railway Construction Troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, issued the order to start the inaugural train on the platform, and personally handed the route permit to driver Li Meichen. Li Meichen and his drivers immediately ran and boarded the locomotive. Then, the whistle of the inaugural train sounded a long blast, and amid the cheers of the soldiers and civilians present, it slowly departed from Zhanjiang South Station, speeding towards Li Tang Station.

### 黎湛铁路七月一日正式通车　铁道兵部队和华南各界代表举行庆祝大会

1955-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　黎湛铁路七月一日正式通车
　　铁道兵部队和华南各界代表举行庆祝大会
    新华社广州一日电　中国人民解放军铁道兵部队和华南各界代表今日在湛江南站广场上举行黎湛铁路通车庆祝大会。参加庆祝大会的有中国人民解放军铁道兵司令员王震、华南各界庆祝黎湛铁路通车代表团团长、中共中央华南分局交通运输部部长兼广东省副省长尹林平，代表团副团长、中国人民政治协商会议广东省委员会副主席张?村、萧隽英，人民解放军驻广州地区部队代表刘甦，广西省副省长陈再励，铁道部代表、柳州铁路管理局代局长尹诗炎，广东省粤西区和湛江市各机关、各人民团体的负责人和当地驻军以及湛江市的市民共约一万人。
    六时正，庆祝大会开始。中共湛江市委员会书记张伯泉致开会词后，中国人民解放军铁道兵司令员王震讲话。他说：这一条铁路的建成，对活跃我国对外贸易提供了有利的条件。它对华南地区的社会主义建设事业也会有很好的作用。这一条铁路通车后，我国南部的海防也更加巩固了。这对目前解放台湾和解放沿海敌占岛屿的斗争，粉碎蒋介石卖国集团在美帝国主义指使下进行的海盗活动，也提供了有利的条件。他接着指出：黎湛铁路的迅速建成，是由于中央的正确领导，全国人民特别是两广人民的积极支援，铁道兵指战员的忘我劳动和迅速提高了科学技术知识和作业技能。这一切都体现了我国社会主义工业生产水平的提高和我国社会主义建设的优越性。华南各界庆祝黎湛铁路通车代表团团长、中共中央华南分局交通运输部部长兼广东省副省长尹林平和广西省副省长陈再励相继讲话。他们在讲话中一致向铁道兵指战员和筑路民工们表示热烈的祝贺和敬意。并预祝铁道兵部队在今后的新的修建任务中取得更多更大的成就。庆祝大会上还宣读了铁道部部长滕代远给铁道兵全体指战员的贺电，和驻广州地区部队给铁道兵全体指战员的贺电。
    庆祝大会结束后，近万名军民欢腾地走向车站站台。七时二十五分，中国人民解放军铁道兵司令员王震在站台上发布典礼列车开车命令，并亲自将路签授予司机长李梅臣。李梅臣和他的司机们当即跑步登上机车。于是，典礼列车的汽笛发出长鸣，在到会军民的欢呼声中徐徐驶出湛江南站，向黎塘车站奔驰前进。
