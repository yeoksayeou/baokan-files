Title: Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Joint Statement by the Prime Ministers of China, India, and Burma

Author:

Date: 1955-07-02
Page: 4

缅中友协主席信波博士谈话
缅中友好协会主席信波博士六月二十八日对记者发表谈话说：“中缅两国总理的联合声明对于亚洲和世界和平都有重大意义，同时对中缅两国的友好关系起了重大的作用，因此它为两国人民所热烈拥护”。信波指出，如果中印和中缅总理联合声明中的和平共处五项原则能为世界各国所遵守，世界就不会受到战争的威胁。
信波博士最后说，“在过去一年中，中缅两国在原有的友好基础上，又在和平共处五项原则指导下更增强了彼此之间的友好与合作。我相信今后这种友好和合作必将更加发展。”
缅中友协致电中缅友协
缅甸缅中友好协会在中缅两国总理发表联合声明一周年的时候致电中国中缅友好协会表示祝贺。电文说，在过去一年中，缅甸联邦和中华人民共和国的友谊有了显著的发展。谨祝你们成功，并愿我们两国的友谊和合作日益发展和巩固。
德里市政委员会主席谈话
德里市政委员会主席阿加瓦尔六月三十日在接见新华社记者时，表示庆贺中印两国总理联合声明发表一周年。
他说：“在印度，没有一个人不抱着极大的崇敬和欢欣的心情来欢迎印度和中国总理的联合声明。自从这个声明发表以来，不只在亚洲、就是在全世界，和平地区是肯定地大大扩大了。”
他在谈到亚非会议时说：“在亚洲和非洲国家中间有了显著的改变和很大的觉醒，对于这一点，两国总理的联合声明是作了重大的贡献的。”
阿加瓦尔说，联合声明以实际而具体的方式表示了争取和平的愿望。
加尔各答各团体庆祝
加尔各答各团体举行公众集会热烈庆祝周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理关于和平共处五项原则的联合声明发表一周年。
印中友好协会西孟加拉省分会在六月二十八日举行集会。会议主席、西孟加拉省立法会议议长沙·库·慕克吉在会上说：“印度和中国两国总理一年前宣布的五项原则是国际事务中的一个里程碑。有着不同政治制度的两个伟大国家——中国和印度——由于履行了和平共处的政策，改进了世界和平的前景。”印中友好协会西孟加拉省分会秘书长巴塔查里雅教授说，联合声明符合世界人民要求挫败美帝国主义者准备原子战争的活动以建立持久和平的强烈愿望。
在同一天，在亚洲团结协会主持的公众集会上，与会者通过决议，对许多国家广泛地支持和接受五项原则表示欢迎。决议欢迎布尔加宁和尼赫鲁发表的联合声明，并且表示相信和平共处的原则指出了在互相谅解和协商的基础上和平解决一切国际问题的道路。　（据新华社讯）

缅中友协主席信波博士谈话
Dr. Htin Wah, Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association, said to reporters on June 28th: "The joint statement by the Prime Ministers of China and Burma is of great significance for peace in Asia and the world, and it has also played a significant role in the friendly relations between China and Burma, and is therefore warmly supported by the people of both countries." Htin Wah pointed out that if the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence in the joint statements of the Prime Ministers of China and India and China and Burma could be observed by all countries in the world, the world would not be threatened by war.
Dr. Htin Wah concluded, "In the past year, on the existing basis of friendship, China and Burma have further enhanced their friendship and cooperation under the guidance of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. I believe that this friendship and cooperation will definitely develop further in the future."
Burma-China Friendship Association Sends Telegram to China-Burma Friendship Association
The Burma-China Friendship Association of Burma sent a telegram to the China-Burma Friendship Association of China to express congratulations on the first anniversary of the joint statement issued by the Prime Ministers of China and Burma. The telegram stated that in the past year, the friendship between the Union of Burma and the People's Republic of China has developed significantly. We wish you success, and may the friendship and cooperation between our two countries grow and consolidate day by day.
Statement by the Chairman of the Delhi Municipal Committee
Agarwal, Chairman of the Delhi Municipal Committee, expressed congratulations on the first anniversary of the joint statement issued by the Prime Ministers of China and India when he met with Xinhua News Agency reporters on June 30th.
He said: "In India, no one does not welcome the joint statement of the Prime Ministers of India and China with great respect and joy. Since the issuance of this statement, not only in Asia, but also in the world, the area of peace has definitely greatly expanded."
Speaking of the Asian-African Conference, he said: "There has been a significant change and great awakening among the countries of Asia and Africa, and the joint statement of the two Prime Ministers has made a significant contribution to this."
Agarwal said that the joint statement expressed the desire to strive for peace in a practical and concrete way.
Celebrations by Various Groups in Calcutta
Various groups in Calcutta held public gatherings to warmly celebrate the first anniversary of the joint statement by Prime Minister Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.
The West Bengal Branch of the India-China Friendship Association held a gathering on June 28th. Sha K. Mukherjee, Chairman of the meeting and Speaker of the West Bengal Legislative Assembly, said at the meeting: "The Five Principles announced by the Prime Ministers of India and China a year ago are a milestone in international affairs. Two great countries with different political systems—China and India—have improved the prospects for world peace by implementing a policy of peaceful coexistence." Professor Bhattacharya, Secretary-General of the West Bengal Branch of the India-China Friendship Association, said that the joint statement conforms to the strong desire of the people of the world to thwart the activities of American imperialists in preparing for an atomic war in order to establish lasting peace.
On the same day, at a public gathering hosted by the Asian Solidarity Association, participants passed a resolution welcoming the widespread support and acceptance of the Five Principles by many countries. The resolution welcomed the joint statement issued by Bulganin and Nehru and expressed the belief that the principles of peaceful coexistence point the way to peacefully resolving all international issues on the basis of mutual understanding and consultation. (According to Xinhua News Agency)
