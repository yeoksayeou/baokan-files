Title: British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Humphrey Trevelyan, Presents Letter of Credence to Foreign Minister Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1955-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th. British Chargé d'Affaires to the People's Republic of China, Humphrey Trevelyan, paid a courtesy call to Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, on the afternoon of the 13th to present his letter of credence. Present at the meeting were Huang Hua, Director of the Department of Western European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 英国驻华代办欧念儒向周外长递交委任书

1955-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    英国驻华代办欧念儒向周外长递交委任书
    新华社十三日讯　英国驻中华人民共和国代办欧念儒在十三日下午拜会中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来递交委任书。在座的有外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华和礼宾司副司长王倬如。
