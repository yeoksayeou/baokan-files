Title: Vietnamese Delegation Visits Atomic Energy Power Station, Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Others Watch Ballet "Swan Lake"
Author:
Date: 1955-07-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 16th, TASS Moscow, July 15th: The Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Chairman and Prime Minister Ho Chi Minh, visited the Atomic Energy Power Station of the Soviet Academy of Sciences on the 15th.
    Accompanying the delegation were Begov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Lavrishchev, Soviet Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Kochetkov, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Xinhua News Agency, July 16th, TASS Moscow: Chairman and Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Ho Chi Minh and members of the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam watched the ballet "Swan Lake" at the Bolshoi Theatre in Moscow on the 15th.
    Accompanying the distinguished guests to the performance were Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, and others.
    The audience welcomed the Vietnamese guests and the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government with prolonged and enthusiastic applause.

### 越南代表团参观原子能电力站  胡志明主席等观看舞剧“天鹅湖”

1955-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    越南代表团参观原子能电力站
    胡志明主席等观看舞剧“天鹅湖”
    新华社十六日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十五日讯：以胡志明主席兼总理为首的越南民主共和国政府代表团在十五日参观了苏联科学院的原子能电力站。
    陪同代表团参观的有苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫、苏联驻越南民主共和国大使拉夫里谢夫和苏联外交部礼宾司副司长科切特科夫。
    新华社十六日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：越南民主共和国主席兼总理胡志明和越南民主共和国政府代表团团员在十五日到莫斯科大剧院观看舞剧“天鹅湖”。
    陪同贵宾们观剧的有伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬等。
    观众以长时间热烈的鼓掌来欢迎越南贵宾和苏联共产党和政府的领导人。
