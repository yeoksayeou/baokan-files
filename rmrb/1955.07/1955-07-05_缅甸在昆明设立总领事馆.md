Title: Burma to Establish Consulate General in Kunming
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-05
Page: 1

　　Burma to Establish Consulate General in Kunming
    Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd: The Chinese government has agreed to the Government of the Union of Burma establishing a Consulate General in Kunming. The Burmese Consul General to Kunming, U Ba Maung, is scheduled to arrive on July 4th.

### 缅甸在昆明设立总领事馆

1955-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　缅甸在昆明设立总领事馆
    新华社三日讯　中国政府已同意缅甸联邦政府在昆明设立总领事馆。缅甸驻昆明总领事吴巴茂订于七月四日到任。
