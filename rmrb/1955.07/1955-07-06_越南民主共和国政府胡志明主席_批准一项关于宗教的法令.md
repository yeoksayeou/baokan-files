Title: President Ho Chi Minh of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Approves Decree on Religion
Author:
Date: 1955-07-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 4th: According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 4th, President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam approved a decree on religion on June 14th.

The decree declares that the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam guarantees the people's freedom of religious belief, and no one may infringe upon this right. Every Vietnamese person has the freedom to believe in a certain religion, and also the freedom not to believe in any religion. Pastors and followers enjoy all the rights of citizens and must also fulfill all the obligations of citizens.

The decree stipulates that pastors are allowed to preach, churches, temples, and pagodas are protected, and every religion may publish books and newspapers in accordance with the law.

The decree stipulates that when land belonging to religious organizations is requisitioned or purchased in the current land reform, the government will reserve a portion of land sufficient to maintain the worship and living expenses of the pastors.

The decree also declares that the government will sanction anyone who abuses religion to undermine peace, unity, independence, and democracy, engages in war propaganda, creates discord among the people, prevents believers from exercising their civil rights, infringes upon the religious freedom and freedom of speech of others, or engages in illegal activities.

### 越南民主共和国政府胡志明主席  批准一项关于宗教的法令

1955-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南民主共和国政府胡志明主席
　  批准一项关于宗教的法令
    新华社河内四日电　据越南通讯社四日讯：越南民主共和国主席胡志明六月十四日批准了一项关于宗教的法令。
    法令中宣布，越南民主共和国政府保证人民的宗教信仰自由，任何人都不能侵犯这种权利。每一个越南人有信仰某种宗教的自由，也有不信仰任何宗教的自由。牧师和教徒享有一切公民权利，也必须履行公民的一切义务。
    法令中规定，允许牧师讲道，保护教堂、庙宇和宝塔，每一种宗教都可以依照法律出版书刊报纸。
    法令规定，在目前的土地改革中征收或征购属于宗教团体的土地时，政府将为它们保留一份足够维持牧师们的礼拜和生活开支的土地。
    法令也宣布，凡滥用宗教来破坏和平、统一、独立和民主，进行战争宣传，在人民中间制造不和，阻止教徒履行公民权利，侵犯他人的信仰自由和言论自由或者进行非法活动的人，政府将予以制裁。
