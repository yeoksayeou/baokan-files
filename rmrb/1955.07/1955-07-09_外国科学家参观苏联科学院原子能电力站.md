Title: Foreign Scientists Visit the Soviet Academy of Sciences Atomic Energy Power Station
Author:
Date: 1955-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to TASS, participants in the Soviet Academy of Sciences conference on the peaceful use of atomic energy are being divided into several groups to visit research institutes and laboratories of various scientific departments in the Soviet Union that are engaged in research on the peaceful use of atomic energy.

　　A group of foreign scientists attending the conference visited the Soviet Academy of Sciences Atomic Energy Power Station. Conference participants also visited the Soviet Academy of Sciences Institute of Nuclear Problems. The scientists visited the world's largest synchrocyclotron with great interest.

### 外国科学家参观苏联科学院原子能电力站

1955-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　外国科学家参观苏联科学院原子能电力站
    新华社八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联科学院和平利用原子能问题会议的参加者正分成许多组访问苏联从事和平利用原子能研究工作的各科学部门的研究所和实验室。
    出席会议的一批外国科学家参观了苏联科学院原子能电力站。会议参加者还参观了苏联科学院核子问题研究所。科学家们以极大的兴趣参观了世界上最大的同步回旋加速器。
