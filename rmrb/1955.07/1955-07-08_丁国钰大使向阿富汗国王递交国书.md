Title: Ambassador Ding Guoyu Presents Credentials to the King of Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1955-07-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, July 7th: Ding Guoyu, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Afghanistan, accompanied by Counselor Zhang Shijie, Cultural Counselor Liang Shangyuan, and Second Secretary Chen Rang, presented his credentials to King Mohammed Zahir Shah of Afghanistan at the Delkosha Palace on the morning of the 5th. When presenting his credentials, Ambassador Ding Guoyu expressed his commitment to working towards developing friendly and cooperative relations between China and Afghanistan and conveyed sincere respect to the Afghan people. The King of Afghanistan expressed his gratitude and affirmed the desire for joint cooperation between the two countries.
    After presenting the credentials, Ambassador Ding Guoyu and others, accompanied by the Chief of Protocol of Afghanistan, went to the tomb of the former King of Afghanistan to lay a wreath.

### 丁国钰大使向阿富汗国王递交国书

1955-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　丁国钰大使向阿富汗国王递交国书
    新华社七日讯　喀布尔消息：中华人民共和国驻阿富汗首任特命全权大使丁国钰偕参赞张世杰、文化参赞梁上苑、二等秘书陈让上，在五日上午到德尔科希宫向阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿递交国书。丁国钰大使在递交国书时表示将为发展中国同阿富汗之间的友好合作关系而努力，并向阿富汗人民致诚挚的敬意。阿富汗国王表示感谢，并表示阿中两国共同合作的愿望。
    在递交国书后，丁国钰大使等在阿富汗礼宾司长的陪同下，前往阿富汗前国王陵墓献花圈。
