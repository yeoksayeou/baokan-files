Title: Soviet Union Aids Vietnam with 20,000 Tons of Rice
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 10th: Vietnam News Agency reports: The Soviet government and people recently decided to donate 20,000 tons of rice to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The Polish ship "Kilinski" carrying the first shipment of 10,000 tons of Soviet aid rice arrived in Ha Long Bay, North Vietnam on July 5th.

### 苏联援助越南两万吨大米

1955-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联援助越南两万吨大米
    新华社河内十日电　越南通讯社报道：苏联政府和人民最近决定赠送越南民主共和国两万吨大米。波兰船“基林斯基号”载着第一批苏联援助的大米一万吨已在七月五日驶抵北越的亚龙湾。
