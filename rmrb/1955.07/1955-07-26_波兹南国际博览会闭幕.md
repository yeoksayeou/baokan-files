Title: Poznań International Fair Closes

Author:
Date: 1955-07-26
Page: 4

    Poznań International Fair Closes
    Xinhua News Agency: The 24th Poznań International Fair closed on the 24th. Over the course of three weeks, the fair received over 1.3 million visitors, including 3,500 from foreign countries. Many trade contracts were signed during the fair, with the value of trade contracts signed by Polish foreign trade companies and foreign companies alone reaching 461 million rubles.
    Speaking at the closing ceremony, the Polish Minister of Foreign Trade, Mr. Dąbrowski, pointed out that the government of the People's Republic of Poland will continue to strive to develop economic and trade cooperation between Poland and countries around the world, and decided to hold the 25th International Fair in Poznań in June 1956.

### 波兹南国际博览会闭幕

1955-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    波兹南国际博览会闭幕
    据新华社讯　第二十四届波兹南国际博览会已在二十四日闭幕。博览会在三个星期中接待了一百三十多万参观者，其中从外国到波兹南博览会来参观的有三千五百人。在博览会期间签订了许多贸易合同，单是波兰对外贸易公司和外国公司签订的贸易合同的价值就达到了四亿六千一百万卢布。
    波兰对外贸易部长董布罗夫斯基在闭幕仪式上讲话时指出，波兰人民共和国政府将继续努力发展波兰同世界各国的经济和贸易合作，并且决定在一九五六年六月在波兹南举行第二十五届国际博览会。
