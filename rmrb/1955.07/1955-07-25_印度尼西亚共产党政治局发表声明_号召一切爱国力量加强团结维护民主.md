Title: Statement by the Politburo of the Communist Party of Indonesia: Calling on All Patriotic Forces to Strengthen Unity and Safeguard Democracy
Author:
Date: 1955-07-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 23rd. TASS, Jakarta, July 23rd: The Politburo of the Communist Party of Indonesia issued a statement in Jakarta on July 23rd. After reviewing the recent developments, the statement called on the people to be vigilant against the danger of establishing a military dictatorship and foreign interference in Indonesia's internal affairs. The Politburo of the Communist Party called on all patriotic forces to strengthen unity and safeguard parliamentary democracy.

### 印度尼西亚共产党政治局发表声明  号召一切爱国力量加强团结维护民主

1955-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党政治局发表声明
    号召一切爱国力量加强团结维护民主
    新华社二十四日讯　塔斯社雅加达二十三日讯：印度尼西亚共产党政治局二十三日在雅加达发表声明。声明在回顾了最近局势的发展后呼吁人民对国内建立军事独裁和外国干涉印度尼西亚内政的危险提高警惕。共产党政治局号召一切爱国力量加强团结，维护议会民主。
