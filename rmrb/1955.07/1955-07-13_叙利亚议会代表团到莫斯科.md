Title: Syrian Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-07-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Syrian parliamentary delegation, visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 11th. Tarasov, Vice-Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, said in his welcoming speech at the airport that your visit to the Soviet Union is a significant event in developing cooperation and friendly relations between our two countries. There is no doubt that direct contact between the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Parliament of the Syrian Republic will help strengthen the friendly ties between our two countries and contribute to the cause of world peace.

Bahhur, head of the Syrian parliamentary delegation and Vice-Speaker of the House of Representatives of the Parliament, said in his reply that we have come here to study the situation together with the Soviet Parliament. We hope that the cooperation between Syria and the Soviet Union will be increasingly consolidated and developed forever. We are fighting for the maintenance of world peace and will fight to the end.

### 叙利亚议会代表团到莫斯科

1955-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚议会代表团到莫斯科
    据新华社讯　应苏联最高苏维埃邀请访问苏联的叙利亚议会代表团在十一日乘飞机到达莫斯科。苏联最高苏维埃副主席兼俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席塔拉索夫在机场致欢迎词说，你们的访问苏联在发展我们两国的合作和友善关系方面是一个意义重大的事件。毫无疑问，苏联最高苏维埃同叙利亚共和国议会进行直接的接触，这将有助于加强我们两国之间的友好联系和有助于世界和平的事业。
    叙利亚议会代表团团长、议会众议院副议长巴胡尔致答词说，我们到这里来，是为了要同苏联议会一道研究局势。我们希望，叙利亚和苏联两国之间的合作将日益巩固和永远发展。我们为维护世界和平而斗争着，并且将斗争到底。
