Title: Chinese Sports Delegation Returns to Beijing from Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1955-07-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, July 15th. Cai Shufan, head of the Chinese sports delegation that participated in the First Czechoslovak National Games, and its members, returned to Beijing by plane on the morning of the 15th.
    Huang Qixiang, the deputy head of the delegation, had already returned to Beijing on July 11th.

### 我国体育代表团由捷回京

1955-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我国体育代表团由捷回京
    新华社十五日讯　参加捷克斯洛伐克第一届全国运动大会的中国体育代表团团长蔡树藩和团员，十五日上午乘飞机回到北京。
    代表团副团长黄琪翔已在七月十一日回京。
