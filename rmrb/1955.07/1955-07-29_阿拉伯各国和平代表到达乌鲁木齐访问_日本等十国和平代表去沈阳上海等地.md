Title: Peace Representatives from Arab Countries Arrive in Urumqi for Visit; Peace Representatives from Japan and Ten Other Countries Go to Shenyang, Shanghai and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1955-07-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, July 28th. A delegation of forty peace representatives from five Arab countries—Syria, Iraq, Lebanon, Jordan, and Sudan—arrived in Urumqi by plane from Lanzhou at 1:00 PM on the 27th, at the invitation of our country. Bao Erhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, and others accompanied them.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport included Nusreti, Chairman of the Urumqi Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Ma Zhongcai, Deputy Mayor of Urumqi; A. Saide; Ma Liangjun, the Grand Imam of the Hui ethnic group; He Jiani Yazi Aji, the Grand Imam of the Uyghur ethnic group, as well as representatives from various people's organizations, business circles, and literary and art circles, totaling more than 120 people. After the representatives disembarked from the plane, they received flowers from the welcomers amidst warm applause.
    At the airport, Nusreti, Chairman of the Urumqi Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, warmly welcomed the peace representatives on behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in Urumqi and expressed heartfelt gratitude for their efforts and contributions to the cause of human peace at the World Peace Conference in Helsinki. Muhammad Hattab, the representative of Lebanon, delivered a speech on behalf of the peace representatives from various countries, expressing his gratitude. He warmly congratulated the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang for their contributions to building their motherland and safeguarding world peace, and expressed his excitement at having the opportunity to visit Xinjiang. His speech was repeatedly interrupted by warm applause from the welcomers. Finally, the peace representatives enthusiastically shouted in their own languages, "Long live world peace!" "Long live the great unity of the people of all ethnic groups in China!" "Long live the friendship between the people of Arab countries and the Chinese people!"
    In the evening, the representatives attended a banquet held by the Urumqi Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace in their honor.
    Xinhua News Agency, July 28th. Peace representatives from ten countries, including Japan, Argentina, Chile, Brazil, Cuba, Colombia, Mexico, Uruguay, Canada, and Australia, who were invited to visit our country, have successively concluded their visits in Beijing. From the 26th to the 28th, they left Beijing in batches to visit Shenyang, Shanghai, and other places.
    When the peace representatives from various countries left Beijing, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General, and Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General, went to see them off.

### 阿拉伯各国和平代表到达乌鲁木齐访问  日本等十国和平代表去沈阳上海等地

1955-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯各国和平代表到达乌鲁木齐访问
    日本等十国和平代表去沈阳上海等地
    新华社乌鲁木齐二十八日电　应邀来我国访问的叙利亚、伊拉克、黎巴嫩、约旦、苏丹五个国家的和平代表一行四十人，二十七日下午一时乘飞机由兰州到达乌鲁木齐市。陪同前来的有中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉等。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会乌鲁木齐市分会主席努斯热提、乌鲁木齐市副市长马仲才、阿·赛德，回族大阿訇马良骏、维吾尔族大阿訇何加尼牙孜阿吉等宗教界人士、各人民团体代表、工商界、文艺界人士共一百二十多人。代表们下机后在热烈的掌声中，接受了欢迎者的献花。
    在机场上，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会乌鲁木齐市分会主席努斯热提，代表乌鲁木齐市各族各界人民热烈欢迎各位和平代表的访问，并对他们在赫尔辛基的世界和平大会上为人类和平事业所作的努力和贡献表示衷心感谢。黎巴嫩代表穆罕默德·哈塔布代表各国和平代表致词答谢，他热烈祝贺新疆各民族人民在建设自己的祖国和保卫世界和平方面所作的贡献，并为有机会到新疆访问感到兴奋。他的讲话不断被欢迎者热烈的掌声打断。各位和平代表最后还用他们自己国家的语言热情地高呼“世界和平万岁！”“中国各民族人民大团结万岁！”“阿拉伯各国人民和中国人民的友谊万岁！”
    晚上，代表们出席了中国人民保卫世界和平委员会乌鲁木齐市分会为他们举行的宴会。
    新华社二十八日讯　应邀来我国访问的日本、阿根廷、智利、巴西、古巴、哥伦比亚、墨西哥、乌拉圭、加拿大、澳大利亚等十个国家的和平代表已陆续结束在北京的参观活动，从二十六日到二十八日分批离北京到沈阳和上海等地参观访问。
    各国和平代表离开北京时，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，秘书长刘贯一、副秘书长吴茂荪等人曾分别前往欢送。
