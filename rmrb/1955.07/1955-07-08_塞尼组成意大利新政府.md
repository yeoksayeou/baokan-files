Title: Segni Forms New Italian Government
Author:
Date: 1955-07-08
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Rome, July 7th: After ten days of efforts to form a cabinet, Christian Democrat Segni, who was mandated to form a government, has formed a new government and was sworn in on the evening of the 6th. The Italian Parliament is scheduled to hold a vote of confidence in the new government on July 13.
    The composition and position distribution of the Segni cabinet are the same as its predecessor, including fourteen Christian Democrats, four Social Democrats, and three Liberals. Many ministers who served in the Scelba government continue to hold their original positions in the new government, including Deputy Prime Minister Saragat, Foreign Minister Martino, Budget Minister Vanoni, and Defense Minister Taviani.

### 塞尼组成意大利新政府

1955-07-08
第4版()
专栏：

　  塞尼组成意大利新政府
    新华社七日讯　罗马消息：受命组阁的天主教民主党人塞尼在经过十天的组阁活动以后，已经组成新政府并且在六日晚宣誓就职。意大利议会定于七月十三日对新政府举行信任投票。
    塞尼内阁的组成成份和职位分配同它的前任一样，包括天主教民主党十四人、社会民主党四人、自由党三人。谢尔巴政府任内的许多部长继续在新政府中留任原职，其中有副总理萨拉盖特、外交部长马蒂诺、预算部长万诺尼、国防部长塔维亚尼等人。
