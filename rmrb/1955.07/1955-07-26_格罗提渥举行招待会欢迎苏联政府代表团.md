Title: Grotewohl Holds Reception to Welcome Soviet Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-07-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 25th (TASS): German Democratic Republic Premier Otto Grotewohl held a reception on the evening of the 24th to welcome the Soviet government delegation visiting the German Democratic Republic.

Among those attending the reception were: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Nikolai Bulganin, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Nikita Khrushchev, Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the German Democratic Republic, G.M. Pushkin, and Commander-in-Chief of the Group of Soviet Forces in Germany, Marshal of the Soviet Union A.A. Grechko.

On the German side, those attending the reception included: Deputy Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Lothar Bolz, Deputy Premiers Nuschke, Loch, and Schultz, Mayor of Greater Berlin, Ebert, members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED), Materne, G. Sidwan, and Oesner, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the SED, ministers of various departments, members of the People's Chamber, leaders of various democratic parties and mass organizations, as well as representatives of social figures and scientific and cultural workers.

Also attending the reception were: diplomatic envoys from various countries, German and foreign journalists.

Grotewohl and Khrushchev spoke at the reception.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 24th (TASS): Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, received the Soviet government delegation that came to Berlin at the invitation of the government of the German Democratic Republic, including Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Nikolai Bulganin, and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Nikita Khrushchev.

Present at the meeting were Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Lothar Bolz, and Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the German Democratic Republic, G.M. Pushkin.

### 格罗提渥举行招待会欢迎苏联政府代表团

1955-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥举行招待会欢迎苏联政府代表团
    新华社二十五日讯　塔斯社柏林二十五日讯：德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥在二十四日晚上举行了招待会，欢迎来到德意志民主共和国的苏联政府代表团。
    出席招待会的有：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫，苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使普希金和驻德苏军总司令、苏联元帅格列奇科。
    德国方面出席招待会的有：副总理兼外交部长博尔茨，副总理努舍克、洛赫和舒尔茨，大柏林市长艾伯特，德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员马特恩、希德万和厄斯纳，德国统一社会党中央委员会书记，各部部长，人民议院议员，各民主党派和群众团体的领导人以及社会人士的代表和科学和文化工作者。
    出席招待会的还有：各国外交使节，德国和外国的新闻记者。
    格罗提渥和赫鲁晓夫在招待会上讲了话。
    招待会在热烈的、友好的气氛中进行。
    新华社二十五日讯　塔斯社柏林二十四日讯：德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥接见了应德意志民主共和国政府邀请而到柏林的苏联政府代表团，代表团中有苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫。
    谈话时在座的有德意志民主共和国外交部长博尔茨和苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使普希金。
