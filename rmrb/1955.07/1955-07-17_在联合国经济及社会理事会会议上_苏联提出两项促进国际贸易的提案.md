Title: At the United Nations Economic and Social Council Meeting, the Soviet Union Proposed Two Proposals to Promote International Trade
Author:
Date: 1955-07-17
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 14th: According to TASS, at the United Nations Economic and Social Council's continued discussion of the world economic situation, Soviet representative Kumykin said in his speech that, in order to promote the development of international trade, the Soviet delegation proposed a proposal requesting governments of all countries to abolish discriminatory restrictions on trade. At the same time, considering the urgent necessity of establishing an international organization that can promote the development of international trade relations, the Soviet delegation put forward a proposal requesting the governments of the United Nations member states to approve as soon as possible the charter of the International Trade Organization signed by 54 countries at the Havana Conference in 1948. Kumykin said that the Soviet Union is ready to participate in this international trade organization.

### 在联合国经济及社会理事会会议上  苏联提出两项促进国际贸易的提案

1955-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    在联合国经济及社会理事会会议上
    苏联提出两项促进国际贸易的提案
    新华社十六日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦十四日讯：在联合国经济及社会理事会继续讨论世界经济局势的过程中，苏联代表库米金在发言里说，为了促进发展国际贸易，苏联代表团提出一项提案，要求各国政府取消贸易上的歧视限制。同时，苏联代表团考虑到建立一个能促进发展国际贸易关系的国际组织的迫切必要性，提出了一个提案，要求联合国会员国政府尽速批准一九四八年由五十四个国家在哈瓦那会议上签订的国际贸易组织的章程。库米金说，苏联准备参加这个国际贸易组织。
