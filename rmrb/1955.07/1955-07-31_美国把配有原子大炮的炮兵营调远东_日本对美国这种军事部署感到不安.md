Title: U.S. Deploys Atomic Cannon-Equipped Artillery Battalion to the Far East; Japan Uneasy About U.S. Military Deployment
Author:
Date: 1955-07-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 30th – Washington: U.S. Army authorities announced on the 28th that the 663rd Field Artillery Battalion and the 5th Field Artillery Detachment, equipped with atomic cannons and rockets capable of carrying nuclear warheads, would be transferred to the Far East. According to a Reuters dispatch from Tokyo, the U.S. Far East Command issued a statement on the 29th stating that the 663rd Field Artillery Battalion had arrived on Okinawa.

Reports indicate that Japan is already uneasy about this U.S. military deployment. Japanese public opinion fears that the U.S. is turning Japan into a base for U.S. atomic cannons.

### 美国把配有原子大炮的炮兵营调远东  日本对美国这种军事部署感到不安

1955-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    美国把配有原子大炮的炮兵营调远东
    日本对美国这种军事部署感到不安
    新华社三十日讯　华盛顿美国陆军当局二十八日宣布把配备有原子大炮和可以装上核子弹头的火箭的第六六三野战炮兵营和第五野战炮兵队调到远东。据路透社东京消息，美国远东陆军司令部二十九日发表声明说，第六六三野战炮兵营已经抵达冲绳岛。
    有消息说，日本已经对美国的这种军事部署感到不安。日本舆论担心美国把日本作为美国原子大炮的基地。
