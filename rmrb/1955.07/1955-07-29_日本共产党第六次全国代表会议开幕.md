Title: Sixth National Congress of the Japanese Communist Party Opens
Author:
Date: 1955-07-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 27th. The Sixth National Congress of the Japanese Communist Party began in Tokyo on the 27th and will last for three days. More than one hundred people, including leaders of the Party's central leading organs and local organizations, attended the meeting.

    Kasuga Masaruichi, Chairman of the Central Guidance Department, reported at the opening session on the Party's work since the Fifth National Congress in 1951 and the basic principles for future work.

### 日本共产党第六次全国代表会议开幕

1955-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    日本共产党第六次全国代表会议开幕
    新华社二十七日讯　东京消息：日本共产党于二十七日在东京开始举行为期三天的第六次全国代表会议。党的中央领导机构和地方组织的负责人共一百多人出席了会议。
    中央指导部主席春日正一在开幕会上报告了自从一九五一年第五次全国代表会议以来党的工作和今后工作的基本方针。
