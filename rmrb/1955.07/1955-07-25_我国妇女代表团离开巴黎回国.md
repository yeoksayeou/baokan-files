Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Leaves Paris for Home

Author:

Date: 1955-07-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 23rd: (Paris dispatch) The Chinese Women's Delegation, led by Cao Mengjun, who were invited to visit France, left Paris for home on the 22nd. The Chinese Women's Delegation arrived in France on the 13th. During their stay in France, in addition to sightseeing, the delegation had extensive contacts with people from all walks of life in France.

On the evening of the 20th, the Executive Bureau of the French Women's Union held a grand reception to entertain the Chinese Women's Delegation, which was presided over by Mrs. Gordon, Chairman of the French Women's Union. Besides the heads of the French Women's Union, the French Young Women's League, and women from other organizations, also present at the reception were Piu and Rocher, members of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party Central Committee, and Lafette, Secretary-General of the World Peace Council, among others.

On the afternoon of the 21st, the French Reception Committee, responsible for receiving the Chinese Women's Delegation, held a press conference. Cao Mengjun, head of the Chinese Women's Delegation, delivered a speech thanking the French people for their warmth and friendship towards the Chinese people and bidding them farewell.

### 我国妇女代表团离开巴黎回国

1955-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我国妇女代表团离开巴黎回国
    新华社柏林二十三日电　巴黎消息：应邀访问法国的中国妇女代表团曹孟君等一行已经在二十二日离巴黎回国。中国妇女代表团是十三日到达法国的，在法国期间除了参观访问外，代表团同法国各阶层人士进行了广泛的接触。
    二十日晚，法兰西妇女联盟执行局举行盛大酒会，招待中国妇女代表团等，酒会由法兰西妇女联盟主席戈登夫人主持，参加酒会的除法兰西妇女联盟、法兰西女青年联盟的负责人及其他团体的妇女外，还有法国共产党中央政治局委员皮佑和罗歇，世界和平理事会总书记拉斐德等人。
    二十一日下午，负责招待中国妇女代表团的法国接待委员会举行记者招待会，中国妇女代表团团长曹孟君致词，感谢法国人民对中国人民的热情和友谊，并且向他们告别。
