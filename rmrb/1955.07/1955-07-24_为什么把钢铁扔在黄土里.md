Title: Why is Steel Thrown into the Yellow Earth?
Author: Wu Lianyou
Date: 1955-07-24
Page: 6

To the Editor:

　　Our grain depot often excavates and transports yellow earth from near some factories (such as the bicycle factory) in Tiexi District, Shenyang, because we need it to level the grain drying yard. However, when using the yellow earth, we found a lot of scrap steel and bricks in it. From 1954 to the present, we have picked up six tons of scrap steel, a box of hacksaw blades, and more than a thousand red bricks from this soil. We are very surprised, why do these factories so generously abandon the state's wealth? Relevant departments should inspect and correct this phenomenon.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shenyang Municipal Grain Bureau First Grain Depot　Wu Lianyou

### 为什么把钢铁扔在黄土里
吴连有
1955-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    为什么把钢铁扔在黄土里编辑同志：
    我们粮库因为要垫粮场，经常从沈阳铁西区一些工厂（如自行车工厂）附近挖运黄土。但是在使用黄土时，我们发现里面有很多废钢铁和砖块。从一九五四年到现在，我们就从这些土中拣到了废钢铁六吨、钢锯条一盒和红砖一千多块。我们很奇怪，这些工厂为什么把国家财富如此大方地随便抛弃了？有关部门应该检查并纠正这种现象。
                沈阳市粮食局第一粮库　吴连有
