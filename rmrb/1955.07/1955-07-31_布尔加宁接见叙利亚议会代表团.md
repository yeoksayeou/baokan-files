Title: Bulganin Receives Syrian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-07-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 30th: According to TASS, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, received the Syrian parliamentary delegation on the 29th, which is currently visiting Moscow at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Molotov, participated in the talks.
    On the same day, the Deputy Chairmen of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Tarasov and Arushanyan, also received the Syrian parliamentary delegation headed by Bahur.

### 布尔加宁接见叙利亚议会代表团

1955-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁接见叙利亚议会代表团
    新华社三十日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁于二十九日接见了应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请目前在莫斯科访问的叙利亚议会代表团。苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫参加了这次交谈。
    同日，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席塔拉索夫和阿鲁善扬也接见了以巴胡尔为首的叙利亚议会代表团。
