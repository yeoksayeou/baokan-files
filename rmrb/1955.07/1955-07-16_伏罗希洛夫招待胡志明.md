Title: Voroshilov Hosts Ho Chi Minh
Author:
Date: 1955-07-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 15th. TASS Moscow, July 14th: The Presidium Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, held a reception at the Grand Kremlin Palace on the 14th to host Ho Chi Minh, President and Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    The national anthems of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Soviet Union were played at the beginning of the reception.

    Those present at the reception were Ho Chi Minh, President and Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Tru'o'ng Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Le Van Hien, Minister of Finance, Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce, Nguyen Van Hu'o'ng, Minister of Education, Nghiem Xuan Yem, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry, Nguyen Duy Trinh, Deputy Minister of the President's Office, Ung Van Khiem, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Pham Ngoc Thach, Deputy Minister of Medical Affairs, and Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union.

    The Soviet side was represented by Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Suslov, Khrushchev, Ponomarenko, Aristov, Pospelov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR Zavenyagin, Kosygin, Kucherenko, Lobanov, Malyshev, Tevosyan, Khrunichev, Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Tarasov, Rashidov, Chubinidze, Ibragimov, Kulatov, Arushanyan, Sareyev, Kuusinen, Paleckis, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Shepilov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation Puzanov, ministers of various ministries of the USSR, marshals of the Soviet Union, responsible persons from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, the Council of Ministers of the USSR, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, the Ministry of Defense of the USSR, and the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the USSR, innovators in production, scientific and cultural workers, Soviet and foreign journalists.

    The guests attending the reception included the ambassadors of Burma, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, India, Czechoslovakia, Argentina, Mongolia, Austria, Albania, the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, Mexico, the Republic of Indonesia, the People's Republic of China, Israel, and Finland, the ministers of Ethiopia, Syria, and Lebanon, the chargés d'affaires ad interim of Iran, Egypt, Afghanistan, the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Sweden, and Poland, and the counselor of the French Embassy.

    Members of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the World Peace Congress, headed by Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Peace Defense Committee, also attended the reception.

    The Syrian parliamentary delegation headed by Ra'uf al-Bahur, Deputy Speaker of the Syrian Parliament, also attended the reception.

    Chairman Voroshilov and President Ho Chi Minh exchanged speeches at the reception.

    The reception was held in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.

    A grand artistic performance was held at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 15th. According to TASS Moscow: Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam he led, visited a Young Pioneer summer camp located in a scenic area near Moscow—Zvenigorod—on the 14th.

    Red flags of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Soviet Union fluttered over the green arches. A red banner read: "Long Live Comrade Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam!" and "Welcome!". Young Pioneers in white shirts and red scarves stood along the tree-lined road leading to the camp, holding flowers to greet the guests. The Young Pioneers warmly saluted President Ho Chi Minh and the members of the Vietnamese government delegation. They presented bouquets and photo albums introducing the life of the summer camp to President Ho, and presented each member of the delegation with a red scarf.

    President Ho Chi Minh thanked the children. He said: I am very happy to see you living so closely, so healthy and happily. I hope that everyone of you will be a good child and study hard.

    President Ho Chi Minh gave a Young Pioneer salute and said in Russian: "Young Pioneers, be ready!" The children replied affectionately in unison: "Always ready!"

    President Ho Chi Minh and the members of the delegation visited the dormitories where the Young Pioneers lived. The children performed amateur artistic programs for the guests. President Ho Chi Minh gave the Young Pioneers a photo album with pictures of him with Vietnamese children. He also gave some other gifts.

    Accompanying President Ho Chi Minh and the Vietnamese government delegation on their visit to the Young Pioneer summer camp were Begov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Shelepin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Komsomol of the USSR, Kairov, Minister of Education of the Russian Federation, and Kochetkov, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the USSR.

    That afternoon, some members of the Vietnamese government delegation visited the All-Union Agricultural Exhibition. Academician Tsitsin, Director-General of the Exhibition, explained to the members of the delegation the history of the exhibition and its significance for the development of Soviet agriculture.

### 伏罗希洛夫招待胡志明

1955-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫招待胡志明
    新华社十五日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十四日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫于十四日在大克里姆林宫举行招待会，招待越南民主共和国主席兼总理胡志明。
    招待会开始时奏越南民主共和国国歌和苏联国歌。
    出席招待会的有越南民主共和国主席兼总理胡志明、越南劳动党总书记长征、财政部长黎文献、工商部长潘英、教育部长阮文煊、农林部长严春庵、主席府副部长阮维祯、外交部副部长雍文谦、医济部副部长范玉石和越南民主共和国驻苏联大使阮良朋。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、波诺马连科、阿里斯托夫、波斯伯洛夫，苏联部长会议副主席查文尼亚金、柯西金、库切连科、洛巴诺夫、马利歇夫、捷沃西安、赫鲁尼切夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席塔拉索夫、拉希多夫、楚比尼泽、伊布拉吉莫夫、库拉托夫、阿鲁夏尼扬、萨雷耶夫、库西宁、帕列茨基斯，苏联最高苏维埃民族院外交委员会主席谢皮洛夫、俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席普扎诺夫，苏联各部部长，苏联元帅，苏联最高苏维埃主席团、苏联部长会议、苏联外交部、苏联国防部、苏联对外贸易部负责人员，生产革新者，科学和文化工作者，苏联和外国记者。
    出席招待会的来宾有：缅甸、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、印度、捷克斯洛伐克、阿根廷、蒙古、奥地利、阿尔巴尼亚、南斯拉夫联邦、墨西哥、印度尼西亚共和国、中华人民共和国、以色列和芬兰等国的大使，埃塞俄比亚、叙利亚、黎巴嫩等国的公使，伊朗、埃及、阿富汗、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、瑞典、波兰等国的临时代办和法国大使馆的参赞。
    以越南保卫和平委员会主席黎廷探为首的出席世界和平大会的越南民主共和国和平代表团人员也出席了招待会。
    叙利亚议会副议长拉·巴胡尔为首的叙利亚议会代表团也出席了招待会。
    伏罗希洛夫主席和胡志明主席在招待会上相互致词。
    招待会在真诚、友好的气氛中进行。
    在招待会上举行了盛大的艺术表演。
    新华社十五日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团在十四日参观了一所设在莫斯科近郊一个风景优美的地方——兹维尼戈罗德的少年先锋队夏令营。
    在绿色的拱门上，飘扬着越南民主共和国和苏联两国的鲜红的国旗。在红色的横幅标语上写着：“越南民主共和国主席胡志明同志万岁！”，“欢迎！”。穿着白衬衫、打着红领巾的少年先锋队员手里拿着鲜花，排列在通往夏令营营地的林荫道上迎接客人。少先队员们热诚地向胡志明主席和越南政府代表团的团员们致敬。他们把花束和介绍夏令营生活的照片册献给胡主席，并给代表团的每个团员都带上了红领巾。
    胡志明主席向孩子们表示感谢。他说：我十分高兴看到你们生活得这样亲密，这样健康和快乐。我希望你们人人都做一个好孩子，好好地学习。
    胡志明主席行了个少年先锋队的敬礼，并且用俄文说：“少年先锋队员们，要准备着！”孩子们亲切地齐声回答：“时刻准备着！”
    胡志明主席和代表团团员参观了少年先锋队员所住的宿舍。孩子们为来宾们演出了业余艺术节目。胡志明主席送给了少先队员一本照片簿，里面的照片是他和越南儿童一起拍的。他还送了一些别的礼物。
    陪同胡志明主席和越南政府代表团在少先队夏令营参观的有苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫、苏联共青团中央委员会书记谢列平、俄罗斯联邦教育部长凯罗夫、苏联外交部礼宾司副司长科切特科夫。
    这一天下午，越南政府代表团部分团员参观了全苏农业展览会。展览会总干事齐津院士向代表团人员讲解了展览会的历史和它对苏联农业发展的意义。
