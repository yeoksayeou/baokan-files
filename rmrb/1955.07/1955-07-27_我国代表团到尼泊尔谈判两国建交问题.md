Title: Chinese Delegation Arrives in Nepal to Negotiate Establishment of Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-07-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 26th: The delegation of the People's Republic of China, headed by Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, arrived in Kathmandu, the capital of the Kingdom of Nepal, on the 25th to negotiate with the government of the Kingdom of Nepal on the establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Nepal.

### 我国代表团到尼泊尔谈判两国建交问题

1955-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我国代表团到尼泊尔谈判两国建交问题
    新华社新德里二十六日电　以中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤为首的中华人民共和国代表团已于二十五日到达尼泊尔首都加德满都，同尼泊尔王国政府就中华人民共和国和尼泊尔王国建立外交关系的问题进行谈判。
