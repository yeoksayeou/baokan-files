Title: Egyptian Foreign Minister Criticizes Turkish-Iraqi Pact
Author:
Date: 1955-07-03
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, 1st. Egyptian Foreign Minister Mahmoud Fawzi said at a press conference in Ottawa, Canada on June 29th that the treaty signed between Turkey and Iraq was "a discordant note in the Arab defense arrangements."

　　Fawzi expressed opposition to military treaties. He said, "We oppose any treaty that does not truly contribute to world peace, and any treaty that constitutes an instrument of foreign domination or colonialism."

### 埃及外交部长抨击土伊条约

1955-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　埃及外交部长抨击土伊条约
    新华社一日讯　渥太华消息：埃及外交部长马茂德·法齐六月二十九日在加拿大渥太华举行的一次记者招待会上说，土耳其和伊拉克签订的条约是“阿拉伯防御安排中一个不和谐的音调”。
    法齐表示反对军事条约。他说：“我们反对任何并不真正有助于世界和平的条约，以及任何构成外国统治或殖民主义的工具的条约。”
