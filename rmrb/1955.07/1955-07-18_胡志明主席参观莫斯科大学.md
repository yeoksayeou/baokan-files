Title: President Ho Chi Minh Visits Moscow University
Author:
Date: 1955-07-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 17th: According to TASS, on the morning of the 16th, the gates of Moscow University on Lenin Hills were exceptionally crowded, with many students, professors, lecturers, and graduate students gathered here. They were waiting to welcome President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam he led.

    When President Ho Chi Minh and the other Vietnamese dignitaries arrived, the hosts of Moscow University, who had been waiting for a long time, shouted "Hurrah!" and burst into thunderous applause.

    Professor Vovchenko, Vice-President of Moscow University, expressed a warm welcome to the guests on behalf of all the teachers and students of the university.

    President Ho Chi Minh and his entourage, accompanied by their hosts, entered this world-renowned institution, engaged in friendly conversations with the university staff, and received two volumes of the history of Moscow University and other gifts from the hosts.

    The guests visited the Moscow University Bicentennial Memorial Hall and the Agricultural Museum, as well as the laboratories and reading rooms of the Geology Department. They then ascended to the 24th floor, overlooking the panorama of Moscow and the new residences in the southwestern district of the Soviet capital.

    The Vietnamese guests then entered the Great Hall of Moscow University, where enthusiastic cheers resounded. President Ho Chi Minh addressed the teachers and students of Moscow University gathered there and accepted flowers presented by the students. President Ho Chi Minh also presented a picture album reflecting the life of the Vietnamese people to the young hosts as a gift to Moscow University.

    The guests visited the students' cultural center and then came to a large classroom, where Vietnamese students studying at Moscow University were gathered. Upon meeting, everyone warmly embraced and kissed each other. President Ho Chi Minh also spoke to the Vietnamese students, encouraging them to study hard for the construction of their motherland and to live in brotherly friendship with students of all nationalities of the Soviet Union.

    Accompanying the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on their visit to Moscow University were Yelyutin, Minister of Higher Education of the Soviet Union, Begov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Lavrishchev, Soviet Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, among others.

### 胡志明主席参观莫斯科大学

1955-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席参观莫斯科大学
    新华社十七日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：十六日上午，莫斯科列宁山上的莫斯科大学的大门前显得异常拥挤，这里聚集着这个大学的许多学生、教授、讲师和研究生等。他们是在等候欢迎前来参观的越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团。
    当胡志明主席和其他越南贵宾们来临的时候，等候了多时的莫斯科大学的主人们高呼“乌拉！”，掌声雷动。
    莫斯科大学副校长伏夫钦科教授代表全校师生向贵宾们表示热烈欢迎。
    胡志明主席和他的随行人员在主人的陪同下走进这所全世界著名的学府，同学校人员进行友谊的交谈，并且接受了主人们赠送的两卷本莫斯科大学史和其他礼品。
    客人们参观了莫斯科大学二百周年纪念馆和农业博物馆，以及地质系的实验室和阅览室。他们然后登上了第二十四层楼，眺望莫斯科全景和苏联首都西南区的新住宅。
    越南贵宾们接着走进莫斯科大学的大礼堂，礼堂内响起了热烈的欢呼声。胡志明主席向聚集在这儿的莫斯科大学的师生们讲了话，并接受了学生们的献花。胡志明主席同时把一本反映越南人民的生活的画册赠送给年轻的主人，作为献给莫斯科大学的礼物。
    贵宾们参观了学生们的文化馆，随后来到一间大教室。在莫斯科大学学习的越南留学生都聚集在这里。大家一见面，就亲热地拥抱在一起，互相接吻。胡志明主席还向越南学生们讲了话，勉励他们为祖国建设而努力学习，并且同苏联各民族的同学像兄弟一样地友爱相处。
    陪同越南民主共和国政府代表团访问莫斯科大学的有苏联高等教育部部长叶留金、苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫，苏联驻越南民主共和国大使拉夫里谢夫等人。
