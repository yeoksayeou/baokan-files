Title: Eisenhower Hosts Banquet for Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 19th: TASS reported from Geneva on July 19th: US President Eisenhower hosted a banquet on July 18th in honor of the Soviet government delegation participating in the Four-Power Summit Conference.

    Those attending the banquet were: N. A. Bulganin (head of the delegation), N. S. Khrushchev, V. M. Molotov, G. K. Zhukov, and A. A. Gromyko.

    Those attending the banquet on the US side were: J. Dulles, C. Bohlen, MacArthur Jr., L. Merchant, J. Hagerty, and L. Thompson.

### 艾森豪威尔宴请苏联代表团

1955-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔宴请苏联代表团
    新华社十九日讯　塔斯社日内瓦十九日讯：美国总统艾森豪威尔七月十八日举行宴会招待参加四大国政府首脑会议的苏联政府代表团。
    出席宴会的有：尼·亚·布尔加宁（代表团团长）、尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、维·米·莫洛托夫、格·康·朱可夫、安·阿·葛罗米柯。
    美国方面出席宴会的有：约·杜勒斯、查·波伦、小麦克阿瑟、利·麦钱特、詹·哈格蒂、劳·汤普逊。
