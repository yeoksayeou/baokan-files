Title: Indonesian Farmers and Trade Union Organizations Support Government Appointment of New Army Chief of Staff
Author:
Date: 1955-07-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta dispatch: The Central Java regional organization of the Indonesian Peasants Front has sent a telegram to the Indonesian cabinet, expressing its support for the government's appointment of the new Army Chief of Staff. The telegram stated that some events surrounding this new appointment "demonstrate tendencies that endanger democracy and encourage military fascism."

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta dispatch: The Indonesian Ministry of Defense Employees Union wrote a letter to the newly appointed Army Chief of Staff, Udojo, congratulating him on taking office and expressing the hope that after taking office, Udojo's efforts to restore unity within the army will achieve satisfactory results.

　　The union also sent letters with similar content to the President, Prime Minister, and Minister of Defense of Indonesia.

### 印度尼西亚农民和工会组织  拥护政府任命新陆军参谋长

1955-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚农民和工会组织
    拥护政府任命新陆军参谋长
    据新华社雅加达电　印度尼西亚农民阵线中爪哇地方组织已致电印度尼西亚内阁，表示拥护政府对于新陆军参谋长的任命。电文说，围绕这次新任命而产生的一些事情“表明了危害民主和鼓励军事法西斯主义的倾向”。
    据新华社雅加达电　印度尼西亚国防部雇员工会写信给新任命的陆军参谋长乌多约，祝贺他就任这个新的职务，并表示希望乌多约在就任这个职务后能使他恢复陆军中间的团结和统一的努力获得满意的结果。
    工会还把内容相似的信送给印度尼西亚总统、总理和国防部长。
