Title: Soviet Government Delegation Leaves Berlin for Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 27th: According to TASS, the Soviet government delegation, which visited the German Democratic Republic at the invitation of the government of the German Democratic Republic, left Berlin for Moscow on the 27th.

Those present at Schönefeld Airport to see them off included: Premier Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic, Deputy Premier and Foreign Minister Bolz of the German Democratic Republic, and other senior government officials, members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and secretaries of the Party Central Committee, and leaders of various democratic parties and mass organizations.

Also present to see off the Soviet government delegation were the Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Pushkin, Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Forces in Germany, Marshal of the Soviet Union Grechko, senior officials of the Soviet Embassy, representatives of the Soviet Military Administration in Germany, and members of various foreign diplomatic missions.

Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin, made a speech at the airport. He said: We visited your cities, industrial enterprises, and agricultural enterprises. During our short stay here, we personally witnessed the great achievements of the working people of the German Democratic Republic in all aspects of political, economic, and cultural life, and saw how the working people of the German Democratic Republic are building a new life with inspiring enthusiasm and confidence in the victory of their cause. I wish the German Democratic Republic further prosperity and achievements in the just struggle for the establishment of a unified, democratic, and peace-loving Germany.

### 苏联政府代表团离柏林回莫斯科

1955-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府代表团离柏林回莫斯科
    新华社二十七日讯　据塔斯社柏林讯：应德意志民主共和国政府的邀请前来德意志民主共和国访问的苏联政府代表团二十七日离柏林回莫斯科。
    前往什涅菲尔德机场欢送的有：德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥、德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨和政府其他高级负责人员，德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局的委员们和党中央委员会的书记们，各民主党派和群众团体的领袖们。
    前往欢送苏联政府代表团的，还有苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使普希金，驻德苏军部队总司令、苏联元帅格列奇科，苏联大使馆高级官员、驻德苏军司令部代表和各国外交使团的人员。
    苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁在机场上发表了谈话。他说：我们访问了你们的城市、工业企业和农业企业。在这里短短的逗留期间，我们亲眼看到德意志民主共和国劳动人民在政治、经济和文化生活各个方面的巨大成就，看到德意志民主共和国劳动人民是怎样以鼓舞的心情和对自己事业必胜的信心在建设着新生活。我祝德意志民主共和国获得进一步的繁荣，在争取建立统一、民主和爱好和平的德国的正义斗争中获得成就。
