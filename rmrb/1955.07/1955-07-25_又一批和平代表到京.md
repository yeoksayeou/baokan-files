Title: Another Group of Peace Representatives Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd: After attending the World Peace Conference in Helsinki, and at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, a delegation from Argentina consisting of 14 people, headed by Vazquez, a delegation from Brazil consisting of 6 people, headed by Falcon, a delegation from Cuba consisting of 2 people, headed by Fernandez, as well as 2 representatives from Colombia, 1 representative from Mexico, and 1 representative from Uruguay, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 22nd for a visit.
    Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Wu Mao-sun, Deputy Secretary-General, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 又一批和平代表到京

1955-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    又一批和平代表到京
    新华社二十三日讯　出席赫尔辛基世界和平大会后，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来华参观访问的，以团长瓦兹盖斯为首的阿根廷代表团十四人、以团长法尔康为首的巴西代表团六人、以团长费尔南德思为首的古巴代表团二人，以及哥伦比亚代表二人、墨西哥代表一人、乌拉圭代表一人，在二十二日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一、副秘书长吴茂荪等都到机场欢迎。
