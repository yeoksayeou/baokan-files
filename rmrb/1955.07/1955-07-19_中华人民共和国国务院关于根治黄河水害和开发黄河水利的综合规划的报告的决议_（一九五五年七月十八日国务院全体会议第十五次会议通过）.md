Resolution of the State Council of the People's Republic of China on the Report on the Comprehensive Plan for Radically Controlling the Yellow River's Disasters and Developing the Yellow River's Water Resources
(Adopted at the 15th Plenary Meeting of the State Council on July 18, 1955)

The 15th Plenary Meeting of the State Council, after hearing the explanations by Li Baohua, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Water Resources, and Liu Lanbo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry, adopted the report on the comprehensive plan for radically controlling the Yellow River's disasters and developing the Yellow River's water resources, and decided that Vice Premier Deng Zihui would represent the State Council to make a report at the Second Session of the First National People's Congress, and requested the Congress to review and approve it.

### 中华人民共和国国务院关于根治黄河水害和开发黄河水利的综合规划的报告的决议  （一九五五年七月十八日国务院全体会议第十五次会议通过）

1955-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国国务院关于根治黄河水害和开发黄河水利的综合规划的报告的决议
    （一九五五年七月十八日国务院全体会议第十五次会议通过）
    国务院全体会议第十五次会议在听取了水利部李葆华副部长和燃料工业部刘澜波副部长的说明后，通过了关于根治黄河水害和开发黄河水利的综合规划的报告，决定由邓子恢副总理代表国务院在第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议上作报告，并请大会审查批准。
