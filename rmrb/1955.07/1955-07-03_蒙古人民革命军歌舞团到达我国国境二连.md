Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Troupe Arrives at Eren, Our Country's Border

Author: 
Date: 1955-07-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 1st. The Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Troupe, invited to China for a performance tour, left Ulaanbaatar for Beijing by train on the afternoon of June 30th, led by General Damdinhuu, the head of the delegation.

Those who saw them off at the station included Chen De Ayush, Director of the Political Department of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army, Choramtse, President of the Mongolian Academy of Arts, Tserendorj, Assistant Minister of the Mongolian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ganbosuren, Director of the Department of Oriental Affairs.

He Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, also went to the station with embassy staff to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, Eren, 2nd. The Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Troupe, invited to visit our country, arrived this morning at Eren, the terminal station on our country's border of the Jining-Ulaanbaatar Railway, led by General Damdinhuu, the head of the delegation.

Wang Dizi, representative of our Ministry of National Defense, Wu Xiling, representative of the Ministry of Culture, and Alingching Sunom, Second Secretary of the Mongolian People's Republic Embassy in China, went to Eren Station to welcome the song and dance troupe.

When the song and dance troupe arrived at Eren, nearly two hundred people, including commanders and fighters of our country's border defense troops and representatives of Mongolian and Han workers, welcomed them at the station. After the members of the song and dance troupe got off the train, they received flowers from the welcoming people amidst warm applause and shook hands cordially with the welcomers one by one.

Then, a welcoming ceremony was held at the station. Wang Dizi delivered a welcoming speech, saying: "On the eve of the 34th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Republic, we are very happy to welcome the artistic envoys of the Mongolian People's Republic at Eren Station, the junction of the newly built railway connecting China and Mongolia." General Damdinhuu delivered a reply speech, saying that the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Troupe was extremely excited and pleased to be able to visit China and see with their own eyes the great Chinese people carrying out creative labor to build their own beautiful lives.

After the welcoming ceremony, the guests boarded a special train and left Eren, with the welcoming crowd constantly waving to them.

### 蒙古人民革命军歌舞团到达我国国境二连

1955-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民革命军歌舞团到达我国国境二连
    新华社一日讯　乌兰巴托讯：应邀来中国作访问演出的蒙古人民革命军歌舞团，在总领队达姆丁呼少将的率领下，已于六月三十日下午乘火车离开乌兰巴托去北京。
    到车站欢送的有蒙古人民革命军政治部主任陈德·阿尤希，蒙古艺术院院长乔热姆茨，蒙古外交部部长助理策林道尔吉和东方司司长冈博苏伦。
    中国驻蒙古大使何英也偕同使馆人员到车站送行。
    新华社二连二日电　应邀来我国访问的蒙古人民革命军歌舞团，在总领队达姆丁呼少将率领下，今天上午到达集宁—乌兰巴托铁路我国国境终点站二连。
    我国国防部代表王地子和文化部代表邬析零以及蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆二等秘书艾林清苏诺木等前往二连车站迎接歌舞团。
    当歌舞团到达二连时，在车站上欢迎的还有我国国境边防部队指挥员、战斗员和蒙汉族职工代表等近二百人。歌舞团人员下车后，在热烈的掌声中接受了欢迎人们的献花，并同欢迎者一一亲切握手。
    接着，在车站上举行了欢迎仪式。王地子致欢迎词说：正当蒙古人民共和国国庆三十四周年的前夕，在连接中蒙两国的新建铁路的接轨点二连车站，来欢迎蒙古人民共和国的艺术使者们，感到十分高兴。达姆丁呼少将致答词，他说，蒙古人民革命军歌舞团能够访问中国，亲眼看到伟大的中国人民为建设自己美好的生活而进行着创造性的劳动，感到无比的兴奋和愉快。
    欢迎仪式结束后，客人们乘上专车离开二连，欢迎人群不断地向他们招手致意。
