Title: Indonesian Vice Premier Receives Head of Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 29th - Indonesian Republic Vice Premier Arifin received the head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation Zheng Zhenduo and deputy head Zhou Erfu on the 29th. The Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, was also present at the meeting.

### 印度尼西亚副总理接见我文化代表团负责人

1955-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚副总理接见我文化代表团负责人
    新华社雅加达二十九日电　印度尼西亚共和国副总理阿里芬在二十九日接见了中国文化代表团团长郑振铎和副团长周而复。陪同接见的有中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇。
