Title: Japanese Peace Activists Ikuo Oyama and Others Invited to Visit China
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th: Japanese public figure, Chairman of the Japan Peace Supporters Committee, winner of the Stalin Prize "For Strengthening International Peace," Ikuo Oyama, his wife, Yaneko Oyama, secretary Michio Iwamura, and another group of thirty-three members of the Japanese Delegation to the World Peace Conference, who were invited by the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace after attending the Helsinki World Peace Conference, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 19th. They received flowers from welcomers upon disembarking.

Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and many others welcomed them at the airport. Also at the airport to welcome them were Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, Togo Kameda, and Japanese peace activist Wan'emon Nakamura. Chun'o Takakuwa, the head of the Japanese Delegation to the World Peace Conference, who arrived in Beijing yesterday, also came to welcome them.

### 日本和平人士大山郁夫等应邀到我国访问

1955-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    日本和平人士大山郁夫等应邀到我国访问
    新华社十九日讯　出席赫尔辛基世界和平大会后应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的日本公众领袖、日本拥护和平委员会主席、“加强国际和平”斯大林奖金得奖人大山郁夫和夫人大山柳子、秘书岩村三千夫和出席世界和平大会日本代表访华团另一部分团员一行三十三人，在十九日下午乘飞机到达北京。他们下机后接受了欢迎者的献花。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一等多人到机场欢迎。到机场欢迎的还有亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍和日本和平人士中村玩右卫门。昨日先行到京的出席世界和平大会日本代表访华团团长高桑纯夫等也前往欢迎。
