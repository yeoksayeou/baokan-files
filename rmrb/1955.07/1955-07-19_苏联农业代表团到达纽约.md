Title: Soviet Agricultural Delegation Arrives in New York
Author:
Date: 1955-07-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 17th (TASS dispatch): The Soviet agricultural delegation, headed by Matskevich, First Deputy Minister of Agriculture of the Soviet Union, arrived in New York on the 16th.

Welcoming the Soviet delegation at the New York airport were Er. Baatz, Assistant Secretary of the U.S. Department of Agriculture, Dr. Strom, Plenipotentiary Representative of the U.S. Secretary of Agriculture, responsible staff members of the Soviet Embassy in the United States, the Soviet Representative to the United Nations, and American and foreign journalists.

### 苏联农业代表团到达纽约

1955-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联农业代表团到达纽约
    新华社十八日讯　塔斯社纽约十七日讯：以苏联农业部第一副部长马茨凯维奇为首的苏联农业代表团于十六日到达纽约。
    在纽约机场上欢迎苏联代表团的有美国农业部助理部长埃尔·巴茨，迎接苏联代表团的美国农业部长全权代表斯特罗姆博士，苏联驻美国大使馆负责工作人员，苏联驻联合国代表以及美国和外国的新闻记者。
