Title: Zheng Guolu, Chief of the Korean-Chinese Side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, Writes a Letter Congratulating the Second Anniversary of the Establishment of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission

Author:

Date: 1955-07-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 29th.  Korean Central News Agency, Kaesong, July 27th:  As the second anniversary of the establishment of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission on August 1st approaches, Major General Zheng Guolu, Chief of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, wrote a letter to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission expressing his heartfelt congratulations to all the commissioners and all personnel.
    Zheng Guolu said in his congratulatory letter, "The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission has helped to consolidate the Korean armistice in the past by fulfilling its responsibility of supervising the implementation of the Korean armistice, and has made a great contribution to the peace of Korea, Asia and the world.
    "I firmly believe that the Commission will successfully fulfill its sacred duties in the future through the joint efforts of its respected commissioners and all personnel."

### 朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员郑国禄  写信祝贺中立国监察委员会成立两周年

1955-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员郑国禄
    写信祝贺中立国监察委员会成立两周年
    新华社平壤二十九日电　朝鲜中央通讯社开城二十七日讯：在八月一日中立国监察委员会成立两周年纪念到来的时候，军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员郑国禄少将写信给中立国监察委员会向各委员和全体人员表示衷心的祝贺。
    郑国禄在贺信中说，“中立国监察委员会由于完成了它监督执行朝鲜停战的职责而在过去有助于巩固朝鲜停战，并且对朝鲜、亚洲和全世界的和平作出了巨大的贡献。
    “我坚信，委员会在将来也将通过它可敬的委员和全体人员的共同努力而顺利地完成它神圣的职责。”
