Title: Japanese Working People Celebrate the 33rd Anniversary of the Founding of the Japanese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1955-07-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th dispatch: Japanese working people across the country enthusiastically held celebrations on July 15th to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Japanese Communist Party.
    Tokyo held a grand "Evening for Peace and Independence" to celebrate the birthday of the Japanese Communist Party. Japanese Communist Party leaders such as Kasuga Shoichi, Shiga Yoshio, Miyamoto Kenji, Matsumoto Saneki, and Hasegawa Hiroshi attended the meeting. More than 25,000 Japanese Communist Party members and workers attended the meeting.
    The meeting opened with the singing of "The Internationale." Kasuga Shoichi, Chairman of the Central Guidance Department of the Japanese Communist Party, delivered a speech at the meeting commemorating the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the party. He emphasized that the Japanese Communist Party must carry out activities among the people and strengthen cooperation with democratic organizations. He called on the people to continue the struggle based on the new program of the Japanese Communist Party.
    The meeting also featured wonderful artistic performances.

### 日本劳动人民庆祝日共成立三十三周年

1955-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    日本劳动人民庆祝日共成立三十三周年
    新华社十六日讯　东京消息：七月十五日是日本共产党成立三十三周年纪念。日本全国各地劳动人民在这一天热烈地举行了庆祝活动。
    东京举行了盛大的“和平与独立晚会”，来庆祝日本共产党的生日。日本共产党领导干部春日正一、志贺义雄、宫本显治、松本三益、长谷川浩等出席了大会。参加大会的日本共产党党员和工人群众共有两万五千人以上。
    大会在“国际歌”声中开幕。日共中央指导部主席春日正一在会上发表了建党三十三周年纪念的演说。他强调日共必须在人民的中间进行活动，并且加强同民主组织的合作。他号召人民根据日共的新纲领继续斗争。
    大会还演出了精彩的文艺节目。
