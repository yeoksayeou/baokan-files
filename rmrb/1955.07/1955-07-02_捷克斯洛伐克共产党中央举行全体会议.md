Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1955-07-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 1st: The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party held a plenary session in Prague on June 29th and 30th.

　　The plenary session heard and discussed the report made by Antonín Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, on measures to develop agricultural production, further build unified agricultural cooperatives, and on the current international situation.

　　After discussion, the Central Committee unanimously approved Novotný's report and made a resolution on further developing unified agricultural cooperatives, ensuring harvesting and autumn field work, and fulfilling the national supply plan, and adopted a resolution on the 1956 planting area and animal husbandry development plan.

　　The plenary session also passed a resolution endorsing the communique issued after the meeting in Bucharest between representatives of the Soviet Union, Romania, Hungary, and Czechoslovakia on the results of the Soviet-Yugoslav talks. The Central Committee welcomed the agreement between the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia and expressed its belief that friendly cooperation beneficial to peace and socialism will also be established between other countries of the socialist camp and Yugoslavia. The Central Committee will take all necessary measures to expand economic and cultural relations between Czechoslovakia and Yugoslavia and to rapidly establish friendly relations between the two countries and their peoples.

### 捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央举行全体会议

1955-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央举行全体会议
    新华社布拉格一日电　捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会于六月二十九日和三十日在布拉格举行了全体会议。
    全体会议听取并且讨论了捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记安·诺沃提尼所做的关于发展农业生产、进一步建设统一农业合作社的措施和关于目前国际形势的报告。
    中央委员会经过讨论后一致批准了诺沃提尼的报告，并且作出了关于进一步发展统一农业合作社、保证收割工作和秋季田间工作以及实现国家供应计划的决议，通过了关于一九五六年播种面积和畜牧业发展计划的决议。
    全体会议并通过决议，赞同关于苏联、罗马尼亚、匈牙利和捷克斯洛伐克的代表就苏联和南斯拉夫的会谈结果在布加勒斯特举行会谈的公报。中央委员会欢迎苏联和南斯拉夫的协议并表示相信社会主义阵营其他国家和南斯拉夫之间也将建立有利于和平和社会主义的友好合作。中央委员会将采取一切必要的措施使捷克斯洛伐克和南斯拉夫的经济和文化关系扩大，使捷南两国和两国人民之间迅速建立友好的关系。
