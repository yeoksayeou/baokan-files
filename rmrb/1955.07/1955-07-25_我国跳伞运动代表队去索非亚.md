Title: China's Parachuting Team Leaves for Sofia
Author:
Date: 1955-07-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: The Chinese parachuting team, participating in the 1955 International Parachuting Friendship Competition to be held in Bulgaria in August, left Beijing for Sofia by plane on the 24th.

    This delegation consists of five parachutists, including one female athlete.

    This is China's first time participating in this international competition.

### 我国跳伞运动代表队去索非亚

1955-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我国跳伞运动代表队去索非亚
    新华社二十四日讯　参加八月间在保加利亚举行的一九五五年国际跳伞友谊比赛的中国跳伞运动代表队，二十四日乘飞机离北京前往索非亚。
    这个代表队共有五名跳伞运动员，其中有一名女运动员。
    我国还是第一次参加这项国际比赛。
