Title: Southern Provinces Begin Harvesting Early Rice

Author:

Date: 1955-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th: Rural areas in Guangdong, Guangxi, Yunnan, Fujian, Hunan, Jiangxi and other provinces have successively begun harvesting early rice. In Guangdong Province, which plants the most early rice, more than 5 million mu out of the province's more than 30 million mu of early rice have been harvested. In Guangxi Province, the counties in the Rongxian and Yongning Special Districts are in the midst of a busy time harvesting early rice. Early rice in the southern part of Fujian Province and the river valleys of Simao and Mengzi Special Districts in Yunnan Province has also begun to be harvested. Most of the early rice in Hunan and Jiangxi provinces is about to ripen, and farmers in Dayu, Nankang, and Qiannan counties in southern Jiangxi Province have also begun harvesting early rice.

Due to the promotion of double-cropping rice this year, Hunan, Jiangxi, Yunnan and other provinces have expanded the early rice harvest area significantly compared to previous years. The area planted with double-cropping rice in Yunnan Province has doubled compared to last year.

The early rice harvest in the aforementioned provinces is generally good this year.

### 南方各省开始收割早稻

1955-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　南方各省开始收割早稻
    据新华社八日讯　广东、广西、云南、福建、湖南、江西等省农村已经先后开始收割早稻。种早稻最多的广东省，全省三千多万亩早稻中已经有五百多万亩收割完毕。广西省南部容县专区和邕宁专区各县收割早稻到了大忙的时候。福建省南部和云南省思茅、蒙自等专区的河谷的早稻也已开始收割。湖南和江西两省的早稻大部即将成熟，江西省南部的大庾、南康、虔南等县的农民，也已经开镰收割早稻。
    湖南、江西、云南等省由于今年推广了双季稻的种植，早稻收获面积比往年扩大了许多。云南全省种双季稻的面积比去年增加了一倍。
    上述各省今年早稻的收成一般都比较好。
