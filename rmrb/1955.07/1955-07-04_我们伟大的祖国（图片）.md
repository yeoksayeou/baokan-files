Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-07-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Workers in various places are actively responding to the Party Central Committee's call to practice strict economy. Workers in the steelmaking department of the Tianjin Steel Plant are actively devising ways to increase the steel output rate. They have erected an iron bar at the furnace opening to prevent excessive steel runoff when removing slag, saving 7.8 tons of steel each month. This is a picture of open hearth furnace workers using the new method to remove slag.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地工人积极响应党中央厉行节约的号召。天津钢厂炼钢部工人积极想办法提高出钢率，他们在炉门口架上一根铁槓使扒钢渣时不致带出过多的钢水，每月能节约七点八吨钢。这是平炉工人采用新方法扒渣的情形。
