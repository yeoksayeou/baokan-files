Title: Li Fuchun Receives Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 5th—Li Fuchun, Vice Premier of the State Council, received on June 30th the Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation led by V.I. Ovsyankin, head of the Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation, and members of the Soviet group of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, who came to China to participate in the second meeting of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee.

Present at the reception were Kong Xiangzhen, Chairman of the Chinese group of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee and Deputy Director of the State Construction Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Zhang Xi, Peng Tao, and Han Zheyuan, Deputy Directors of the State Planning Commission.

### 李富春接见苏联科学技术合作代表团

1955-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　李富春接见苏联科学技术合作代表团
    新华社五日讯　国务院副总理李富春六月三十日接见了来我国参加中苏科学技术合作委员会第二次会议的苏联科学技术合作代表团团长瓦·伊·奥弗西扬金和中苏科学技术合作委员会苏联组委员。
    接见时在座的有中苏科学技术合作委员会中国组主席、中华人民共和国国家建设委员会副主任孔祥祯和国家计划委员会副主任张玺、彭涛和韩哲一。
