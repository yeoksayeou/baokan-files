Our Great Motherland
The Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway Yellow River Bridge is the first large iron bridge built on the Yellow River after the founding of New China. After more than a year of construction, the bridge was completed and opened to traffic on July 1st. This is a scene of decorated locomotives passing over the bridge.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    兰新铁路黄河大桥是新中国成立后在黄河上修筑的第一座大铁桥。这座大桥经过一年多的施工，已在“七一”落成通车。这是扎彩的机车正通过大桥的情形。
