Title: Guo Moruo Fetes Peace Representatives from Japan, Chile, Australia, and Vietnam / China Islamic Association Fetes Peace Representatives from Arab Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-07-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 20th to welcome peace representatives from Japan, Chile, and Australia who were invited to visit China, as well as Vietnamese peace representatives who were returning home after attending the World Peace Conference and passing through China.

Also invited to the banquet were international peace figures currently in Beijing, including Kameda Togo, Nakamura Kan'emon, Huang Fengjiu, Rewi Alley, and Vantourout.

Prominent figures from all walks of life in the capital attended as guests.

Guo Moruo delivered a welcoming speech, saying: "Thanks to the efforts of all of you, the heads of the four major powers are now holding a meeting in Geneva. May the summit of the four major powers thoroughly ease international tensions." Guo Moruo said: "To thoroughly ease international tensions, people all over the world still need to make further efforts to defend peace." Guo Moruo concluded by wishing peace would prevail.

Subsequently, Le Dinh Tham, head of the Vietnamese peace delegation, Golan, head of the Australian delegation, Ikuo Oyama, a prominent Japanese peace figure, Sumio Takakuwa, head of the Japanese peace delegation, and Juan Martínez Campos, head of the Chilean delegation, spoke at the banquet.

After the banquet, the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe performed excellent programs for the peace representatives.

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th: The China Islamic Association held a banquet on the 19th to welcome peace representatives from Syria, Iraq, Lebanon, Jordan, and Sudan who were invited to visit China. After the banquet, a forum was held with the peace representatives from various countries.

Bao Erhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, Yang Jingren, Deputy Director, Zhang Yuzhen, Secretary-General; Bai Shouyi and Ma Songting, Deputy Directors of the Chinese Hui People's Cultural Advancement Association, Gan Chunlei, Secretary-General; and figures from the Islamic community in Beijing, Yang Mingyuan and An Shiwei, attended the banquet and forum to receive their Muslim brothers who had come from afar to visit China.

Bao Erhan delivered a welcoming speech at the banquet. Izzat Husseini Agha thanked them for their welcome on behalf of the peace representatives from various countries.

At the forum, Bao Erhan first cited examples of Xinjiang's economic and cultural development and improvements in people's lives, introduced the happy lives of people of all ethnic groups who believe in Islam in the new China, and explained the love and support of people of all ethnic groups who believe in Islam for the People's Government, the Communist Party, and Chairman Mao Zedong. Bao Erhan then spoke of the long-standing friendship and cultural ties between China and Arab countries, and expressed the hope that this traditional friendship and cultural connection would be rapidly restored and developed.

Syrian peace representative and painter Said Tahsin finally expressed his gratitude and respect to the Chinese people on behalf of all the peace representatives, saying: "The Chinese people have waged unremitting struggles to defend peace and the cooperation of all countries. The situation described today has shattered the rumors of imperialism. As a Muslim, I will bring these facts to Syria and Muslims outside of Syria."

After the forum, the China Islamic Association presented each peace representative with a copy of the Quran published in China.

### 郭沫若欢宴日、智、澳、越等国和平代表  我伊斯兰教协会欢宴阿拉伯各国和平代表

1955-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若欢宴日、智、澳、越等国和平代表
    我伊斯兰教协会欢宴阿拉伯各国和平代表
    据新华社二十日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，二十日晚设宴欢迎应邀来我国访问的日本、智利、澳大利亚的和平代表，和出席世界和平大会后途经中国返国的越南和平代表。
    应邀出席宴会的还有现在北京的各国和平人士龟田东伍、中村翫右卫门、黄凤九、路易·艾黎、万徒勒里。
    出席作陪的有首都各界著名人士。
    郭沫若致欢迎词说：由于诸位努力的结果，四大国首脑现在正在日内瓦举行会议。愿四大国首脑会议把国际紧张局势彻底地缓和下来。郭沫若说：要使得国际紧张局势彻底缓和下来，全世界人民也还需要为保卫和平作更进一步的努力。郭沫若最后祝和平胜利。
    接着，越南和平代表团团长黎廷探、澳大利亚代表团团长高兰、日本著名和平人士大山郁夫、日本和平代表团团长高桑纯夫、智利代表团团长胡安·马丁涅斯·康普斯相继在宴会上讲了话。
    在宴会以后，中国杂技团为和平代表们表演了精采节目。
    据新华社十九日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会在十九日举行宴会，欢迎应邀来我国访问的叙利亚、伊拉克、黎巴嫩、约旦、苏丹五国的和平代表。在宴会以后，并和各国和平代表举行了座谈会。
    中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉，副主任杨静仁，秘书长张玉珍；中国回民文化协进会副主任白寿彝、马松亭，秘书长甘春雷；北京伊斯兰界人士杨明远、安士伟等出席了宴会和座谈会，招待远道来我国访问的穆斯林兄弟。
    鲍尔汉在宴会上致欢迎词。伊扎依·侯赛因·阿加代表各国和平代表感谢对他们的欢迎。
    在座谈会上，鲍尔汉首先举出新疆的经济和文化发展和人民生活改善的事例，介绍了新中国的信仰伊斯兰教的各族人民的幸福生活，说明信仰伊斯兰教的各族人民对人民政府和共产党、毛泽东主席的热爱和拥护。鲍尔汉接着谈到中国和阿拉伯各国悠久的友谊和文化联系，并希望这种传统的友谊和文化联系将迅速地恢复和发展起来。
    叙利亚和平代表、画家萨伊德·塔哈辛最后代表全体和平代表向中国人民表示感谢和敬意，他说：中国人民为保卫和平和各国人民的合作进行了不懈的斗争，今天所讲到的这些情况粉碎了帝国主义的谣言。作为一个穆斯林，我将把这些事实带给叙利亚和叙利亚以外的穆斯林。
    在座谈会后，中国伊斯兰教协会赠送各国和平代表每人一部中国出版的古兰经。
