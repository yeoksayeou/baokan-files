Title: Indonesian Government Minister of Justice Exposes Foreign Elements' Conspiracy to Influence General Election
Author:
Date: 1955-07-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta. According to Antara News Agency, at a meeting of local officials and group representatives held in Manado on June 28, the Indonesian Government Minister of Justice, Zodi Gonggusuomo, said that foreign elements are attempting to influence the upcoming general election and are supporting armed gangs in Indonesia.

　　Zodi Gonggusuomo pointed out that if the terrorists were operating with their own funds, it would have been easy to eliminate them long ago. However, he emphasized that certain foreign countries are supporting them. He said that various signs indicate that foreign elements are trying to influence the general election, wanting to make groups willing to form a pro-Dutch cabinet victorious. He called on the entire country to unite and eliminate these foreign elements' conspiracy activities.

### 印度尼西亚政府司法部长　揭露外国分子阴谋影响大选

1955-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚政府司法部长
  　揭露外国分子阴谋影响大选
    新华社雅加达电　据安塔拉通讯社消息，六月二十八日，在万雅老举行的地方官员和团体代表会议上，印度尼西亚政府司法部长佐迪·龚多古苏莫说，外国分子正在试图影响即将到来的大选，并且正在支持印度尼西亚的武装匪帮。
    佐迪·龚多古苏莫指出，如果恐怖分子是用自己的经费进行活动的话，早就毫不困难地把他们消灭了。但是，他强调指出，有某些外国在支持他们。他说，各种迹象说明，外国分子正在试图影响大选，想要使愿意成立亲荷兰的内阁的集团获得胜利。他号召举国团结一致来消灭这些外国分子的阴谋活动。
