Title: Nehru Returns to New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1955-07-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 13th - Indian Prime Minister Nehru returned to New Delhi by plane on the 13th after visiting the Soviet Union and seven other countries. Indian President Prasad, Vice President Radhakrishnan, ministers of the Indian government, members of parliament, and foreign envoys all went to the airport to welcome him. When Prime Minister Nehru disembarked from the plane, he was warmly cheered by the tens of thousands of people gathered at the airport. The banners held high by the welcoming crowd read: "Long live India-Soviet friendship!"
　　When Prime Minister Nehru returned to Mumbai from Cairo on the 12th, he was also warmly welcomed by tens of thousands of Mumbai citizens.

### 尼赫鲁回到新德里

1955-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁回到新德里
    新华社新德里十三日电　印度总理尼赫鲁在访问苏联和其他七个国家以后，在十三日乘飞机回到新德里。印度总统普拉沙德，副总统拉德哈克里希南，印度政府各部部长，国会议员，以及各国外交使节都到机场欢迎。尼赫鲁总理下飞机时，受到聚集在机场上的成千上万的群众的热烈欢呼。欢迎群众高举着的标语牌上写着：“印度—苏联友好万岁！”
    尼赫鲁总理在十二日从开罗回到孟买时，也受到数以万计孟买市民的热烈欢迎。
