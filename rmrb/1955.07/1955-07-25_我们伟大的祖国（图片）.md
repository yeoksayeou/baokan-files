Our Great Motherland
    The state-owned Zhoushan Fish Meal Factory in Zhejiang Province has started production. This processing plant can process 1,900 to 2,300 dan of fish per day. This is a corner of the fish floss workshop, where technicians and workers are calculating the drying time of the steaming machine.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    浙江省的地方国营舟山鱼粉厂开工生产。这个加工厂每天可加工一千九百到二千三百担鱼货。这是鱼松车间的一角，技术员和工人正在计算蒸干机烘干的时间。
