Title: Our Islamic Hajj Delegation to Mecca, Saudi Arabia
Author:
Date: 1955-07-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 19th. A Chinese Islamic Hajj delegation, composed of nineteen prominent Chinese Ahongs, Mullahs, and devout Muslims, left Kunming by plane on the morning of the 19th to make a pilgrimage to Mecca, Saudi Arabia.
    The delegation members are from Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and provinces such as Xinjiang, Gansu, Yunnan, Shandong, Qinghai, and Hebei, including Hui, Uyghur, and Dongxiang nationalities. It is led by Nur Muhammad Dapusheng, Yusuf Mayu Huai, and Yiming Mahsum, the deputy directors of the China Islamic Association.
    The delegation departed from Beijing on the 15th. Before leaving Beijing, the China Islamic Association held a farewell party. On the day of departure, Burhan, the director of the China Islamic Association, Yang Jingren, the deputy director, Zhang Yuzhen, the secretary-general, and Ma Songting, the deputy director of the China Hui Nationality Cultural Advancement Association, Gan Chunlei, the secretary-general, as well as the Ahongs and followers from various mosques in Beijing, all went to the airport to see them off. The Ahongs also performed "Du'a" at the airport, praying for the delegation's safe arrival in the Holy Land.

### 我伊斯兰教朝觐团去沙特阿拉伯麦加朝圣

1955-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我伊斯兰教朝觐团去沙特阿拉伯麦加朝圣
    新华社昆明十九日电　由十九名中国著名的阿訇、毛拉和虔诚的伊斯兰教徒组成的中国伊斯兰教朝觐团，十九日上午乘飞机离开昆明，前往沙特阿拉伯的麦加朝圣。
    朝觐团的成员来自北京、上海、广州和新疆、甘肃、云南、山东、青海、河北等省，包括回、维吾尔及东乡等民族，由中国伊斯兰教协会副主任努尔穆罕默德·达浦生、优素福·马玉槐及依明·马合苏木率领。
    朝觐团是十五日从北京动身的。离京前，中国伊斯兰教协会曾举行欢送会。动身的那天中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉、副主任杨静仁、秘书长张玉珍和中国回民文化协进会副主任马松亭、秘书长甘春雷以及北京市各清真寺的阿訇和教徒，都前往机场欢送。阿訇们还在机场上做了“都阿依”，祈祷朝觐团平安到达圣地。
