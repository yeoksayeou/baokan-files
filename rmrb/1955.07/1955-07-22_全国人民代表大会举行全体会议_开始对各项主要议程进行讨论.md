Title: National People's Congress Holds Plenary Session, Begins Discussion of Major Agenda Items
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 21st—The Second Session of the First National People's Congress held a plenary session at 3:00 PM on July 21st, and began discussing the major agenda items of this meeting.

    The representatives and heads of state organs who spoke in today's discussion were Guo Moruo, Li Jishen, Meng Tai, Li Daizhang, Liu Bocheng, Chen Yongkang, Li Zhuchen, Chen Yun, Lai Ruoyu, Teng Daiyuan, Cai Chang, Xie Fumin, Xia Yikun, and others, thirteen people in total.

    The executive chairmen of today's meeting were: Shen Junru, Ye Jianying, Zhang Nanxian, Xu Deheng, Yang Zhihua, Wu Yaozong, and Chen Jingyu. The meeting lasted from 3:00 PM to 7:20 PM.

    The meeting will continue on the 22nd.

### 全国人民代表大会举行全体会议  开始对各项主要议程进行讨论

1955-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会举行全体会议
    开始对各项主要议程进行讨论
    新华社二十一日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议二十一日下午三时举行全体会议，开始对本次会议各项主要议程进行讨论。
    在今天讨论中先后发言的代表和国家机关各部门负责人有郭沫若、李济深、孟泰、李大章、刘伯承、陈永康、李烛尘、陈云、赖若愚、滕代远、蔡畅、谢扶民、夏以焜等十三人。
    今天会议的执行主席是：沈钧儒、叶剑英、张难先、许德珩、杨之华、吴耀宗、陈经畬。会议从下午三时进行到七时二十分。
    会议将在二十二日继续举行。
