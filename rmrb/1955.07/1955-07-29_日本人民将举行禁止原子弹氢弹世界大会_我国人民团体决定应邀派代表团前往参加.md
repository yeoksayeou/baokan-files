Title: Japanese People to Hold World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs; Chinese People's Organizations Decide to Send Delegation to Participate
Author:
Date: 1955-07-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 28th: This year, August 6th marks the tenth anniversary of the atomic bombing of Hiroshima, Japan. People from all walks of life in Japan will hold a World Conference against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs in Hiroshima and Tokyo from August 6th to 15th. The preparatory committee of the conference has invited foreign representatives to participate. The China Peace Committee had successively received letters of invitation from the preparatory committee of the conference on June 17th and July 1st. The Chinese People's Relief Administration, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation also received invitations from the Japan National Relief Association, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, and the Federation of Japanese Women's Organizations to attend the Hiroshima Conference and visit Japan. The China Peace Committee and other organizations have agreed to send a delegation to participate. The members of the delegation are as follows: Liu Ningyi (Standing Committee Member of the China Peace Committee, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Member, Standing Committee of the National People's Congress), Cheng Fangwu (Educator, President of Northeast Normal University, Deputy to the National People's Congress), Zhao Puchu (Vice-President of the Buddhist Association of China, Executive Committee Member of the Chinese People's Relief Administration, Deputy to the National People's Congress), Xie Bingxin (female, writer, Member of the National Committee of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Deputy to the National People's Congress), Chen Tiqiang (jurist, Director of the China Political Science and Law Association). Secretary Zheng Senyu, translators Sun Shengquan, An Shuqu.

### 日本人民将举行禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会  我国人民团体决定应邀派代表团前往参加

1955-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    日本人民将举行禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会
    我国人民团体决定应邀派代表团前往参加
    新华社二十八日讯　今年八月六日为日本广岛遭受原子弹轰炸十周年纪念日，日本各界人民将于八月六日至十五日在广岛和东京举行禁止原子弹、氢弹世界大会，该会筹备委员会并邀请外国代表参加。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会曾于六月十七日和七月一日连续接到该会筹备委员会发出的邀请信，中国人民救济总会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会也分别接到日本国民救援会、日本工会总评议会、全日本妇女团体联合会关于参加广岛大会和访问日本的邀请信件。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等各团体已协商决定派遣代表团前往参加。代表团组成人员如下：刘宁一（中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员会委员、中华全国总工会副主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员），成仿吾（教育家、东北师范大学校长、全国人民代表大会代表），赵朴初（中国佛教协会副会长、中国人民救济总会执行委员会委员、全国人民代表大会代表），谢冰心（女，作家、中国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会委员、全国人民代表大会代表），陈体强（法学家、中国政治法律学会理事）。秘书郑森禹，翻译孙盛泉、安淑渠。
