Title: French National Assembly Foreign Affairs Committee to Send Delegation to Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1955-07-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 30th -- The Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs has invited the French National Assembly to send a delegation to visit China. According to an Agence France-Presse report from Paris, the Foreign Affairs Committee of the French National Assembly, after listening to Chairman Daniel Mayer's notification regarding this invitation on the 29th, has passed a recommendation to accept the invitation. The delegation will include several members of the Foreign Affairs Committee. They may come to China at the end of September or the beginning of October this year.

### 法国国民议会外委会将派代表团访问我国

1955-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    法国国民议会外委会将派代表团访问我国
    新华社三十日讯　中国人民外交学会曾经邀请法国国民议会派遣一个代表团来中国访问。据法新社巴黎消息，法国国民议会外交委员会二十九日在听取了主席丹尼埃·麦耶关于这项邀请的通知后，已通过了接受邀请的建议。代表团将包括外交委员会的几个委员。他们或许会在今年九月底或者十月初前来中国。
