The Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Ensemble's Performance in Beijing

The Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Ensemble held a performance in Beijing. Each performance by the Song and Dance Ensemble was warmly welcomed by the audience, and many programs were performed again at the enthusiastic request of the audience. The picture above shows the Song and Dance Ensemble singing "Ode to the Altai Mountains" (Mongolian folk song).

　The photos for the pictorial are taken by reporters Yuan Yiping and Wang Zhiyuan from this newspaper.

### 蒙古人民革命军歌舞团在北京的访问演出（图片）
袁毅平　王志渊
1955-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命军歌舞团在北京的访问演出
    蒙古人民革命军歌舞团在北京举行了访问演出。歌舞团的每一次演出都受到广大观众的热烈欢迎，许多节目在观众们的热情要求下作了再一次的表演。上图是歌舞团正在合唱阿尔泰山颂（蒙古民歌）　
　画刊照片为本报记者　袁毅平　王志渊摄
