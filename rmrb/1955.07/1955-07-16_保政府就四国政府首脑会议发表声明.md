Title: Bulgarian Government Issues Statement on Four-Power Summit
Author:
Date: 1955-07-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, July 14th: According to TASS, the Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria has issued a statement regarding the upcoming Four-Power Summit in Geneva.

The statement says: The Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the peace-loving Bulgarian people welcome the convening of the Four-Power Summit and sincerely hope that this meeting can be conducted in an atmosphere that can lead to positive results in establishing mutual trust and gradually easing international tensions.

The statement said that many events that have occurred recently have created favorable conditions for ending the "Cold War" and gradually resolving outstanding issues through peaceful negotiations.

The Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria supports and is taking measures aimed at easing international tensions, resolving outstanding international disputes through negotiations, and consolidating peace in the Balkans and the world. The Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria fully supports the Belgrade Declaration of the leaders of the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia and will do everything in its power to strengthen friendly relations with the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia. The Bulgarian government will also continue to take peaceful actions aimed at establishing good relations with its southern neighbors, Greece and Turkey. The Bulgarian government hopes that Greece and Turkey will also take the same peaceful actions to continuously consolidate peace in the Balkans.

The statement then says: The Bulgarian people are extremely concerned about resolving the German question on a peaceful and democratic basis, establishing a European collective security system, reducing armaments, banning atomic weapons, and guaranteeing lasting world peace.

The statement also says: The People's Republic of Bulgaria has always unconditionally upheld the principles of the United Nations Charter and fully complies with the requirements for members of the organization, and therefore reiterates its belief that the indisputable right of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to be a member of this organization will finally be realized.

The statement concludes: The Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the entire Bulgarian people hope that the heads of government of the four major powers gathering in Geneva will show sincerity and have a clear understanding of the international situation, so that it will be possible to reach the necessary agreements to end the "Cold War," embark on the path of negotiation, and restore trust between countries.

### 保政府就四国政府首脑会议发表声明

1955-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

    保政府就四国政府首脑会议发表声明
    新华社十五日讯　据塔斯社索非亚十四日讯：保加利亚人民共和国政府就即将在日内瓦举行的四国政府首脑会议发表声明。
    声明说：保加利亚人民共和国政府和爱好和平的保加利亚人民欢迎召开四国政府首脑会议，并且真诚地希望，这次会议能够在可以建立相互信任和在逐步和缓国际紧张局势方面导致良好结果的气氛中进行。
    声明说，最近时期所发生的许多事件，为结束“冷战”以及通过和平谈判的方式逐步解决悬而未决的问题创造了有利的条件。
    保加利亚人民共和国政府支持，并且正在采取目的在于缓和国际紧张局势，以谈判方式解决悬而未决的国际争端以及巩固巴尔干半岛和全世界的和平的措施。保加利亚人民共和国政府完全支持苏联和南斯拉夫的领导人的贝尔格莱德宣言，它将尽自己的一切力量来加强同南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国的友好关系。保加利亚政府并且将继续采取目的在于同南部邻国——希腊和土耳其建立良好关系的和平行动。保加利亚政府希望，希腊和土耳其方面也采取同样的和平行动，以使巴尔干的和平不断巩固。
    声明接着说：保加利亚人民对于在和平民主的基础上解决德国问题、建立欧洲集体安全体系、裁减军备、禁止原子武器和保证世界持久和平的问题表示极大的关心。
    声明又说：保加利亚人民共和国始终无条件地拥护联合国宪章的原则，完全符合对这个组织的成员所提出的要求，因此再一次表示相信保加利亚人民共和国作为这个组织成员的无容争辩的权利终于将会实现。
    声明最后说：保加利亚人民共和国政府和全体保加利亚人民希望聚会在日内瓦的四大国政府首脑表现诚意，并且对国际局势有一个明智的理解，以便有可能达成必要的协议，来结束“冷战”，走上谈判的道路和恢复国与国之间的信任。
