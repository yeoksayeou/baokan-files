    Our Great Motherland
    The Anshan Iron and Steel Construction Company has added a new machine to the tailing dam construction project – a Soviet electric multi-bucket excavator. This excavator can dig 45 cubic meters of earth per hour, and the conveyor belt behind it can also transport the earth to where it is needed within a short distance.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍山钢铁建设公司在一尾矿坝建设工程中增添了一种新机械——苏联的电动多斗挖土机。这种挖土机每小时可挖土方四十五立方公尺，后面的皮带运输机，还可把土方运到短距离内需要的地方去。
