Title: Czechoslovakian National Games Held Grand Parade; Chinese Gymnastics Team's Performance Warmly Welcomed
Author:
Date: 1955-07-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 3rd: The final portion of the first Czechoslovakian National Games is continuing. On the morning of the 3rd, athletes held a grand parade in Prague and were reviewed by Czechoslovak Communist Party and government leaders, headed by President Zapotocky. The parade included Czechoslovak world record holders, more than 130,000 athletes and sports figures, tens of thousands of folk dancers, and people from factories, rural areas, government offices, and schools across the country. The parade also included athlete delegations from the Soviet Union, China, Poland, the German Democratic Republic, Romania, Bulgaria, Hungary, and Mongolia. Amidst continuous cheers and applause, they marched spiritedly on Wenceslas Square and the streets of Prague, or danced joyfully. Those in the parade, together with those lining the sidewalks, shouted: "Long live the Czechoslovak Communist Party!" "Long live the Soviet Union!" "Long live peace!"
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 4th: The Chinese gymnastics team, consisting of more than 150 people, performed group exercises and Chinese folk sports programs—tumbling, pole climbing, and leather straps—at the Strahov Stadium in Prague on the afternoon of the 3rd. This was one of the performance programs of the first Czechoslovakian National Games. The Chinese athletes' performance was warmly welcomed by the audience.
　　On this day, the Strahov Stadium, which can accommodate approximately 200,000 spectators, was packed. Among those present to watch the performance were leaders of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and government headed by President Zapotocky, Chinese Deputy Minister of National Defense Xiao Ke, the Chinese sports delegation headed by Cai Shufan, and Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Cao Ying.

### 捷运动大会举行盛大游行  我国体操队表演得到热烈欢迎

1955-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

　  捷运动大会举行盛大游行
    我国体操队表演得到热烈欢迎
    新华社布拉格三日电　捷克斯洛伐克第一届全国运动大会的最后一部分节目正在继续进行。三日上午，运动员们在布拉格举行了盛大的游行，接受以萨波托斯基总统为首的捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府领导人的检阅。游行行列中有捷克斯洛伐克的世界纪录保持者以及十三万名以上的运动员和体育家、数以万计的民间舞蹈家和来自全国各地工厂、农村、机关和学校的人。在游行队伍中还有苏联、中国、波兰、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利和蒙古的运动员代表队。在连续不绝的欢呼和掌声中，他们在瓦茨拉夫广场上和布拉格的街道上精神抖擞地行进，或者愉快地舞蹈。游行的人同排列在人行道上的人一起高呼：“捷克斯洛伐克共产党万岁！”“苏联万岁！”“和平万岁！”
    新华社布拉格四日电　由一百五十多人组成的中国体操队三日下午在布拉格的斯特拉霍夫运动场表演了团体操和中国的民间体育节目——翻筋斗、爬竿和皮条等。这是捷克斯洛伐克第一届全国运动大会的表演节目之一。中国运动员的表演得到观众的热烈欢迎。
    这一天，能容纳观众约二十万人的斯特拉霍夫运动场挤满了人。到场观看表演的有以萨波托斯基总统为首的捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府的领导人、中华人民共和国国防部副部长萧克、以蔡树藩为首的中国体育代表团和中国驻捷大使曹瑛。
