Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Chongqing for Visit
Author:
Date: 1955-07-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, July 15th. The Indian Cultural Delegation, headed by Chand, arrived in Chongqing by plane at 12:00 noon on the 15th. Luo Shigao and Chen Chou, Deputy Mayors of Chongqing, Pei Dongli, Deputy Director of the Chongqing Municipal Bureau of Culture, and more than 500 people from Chongqing's cultural and artistic circles welcomed the delegation at the airport.

　　The airport was newly decorated with colorful flags, and a red cloth banner with gold characters reading "Welcome to the Indian Cultural Delegation" was hung high on the platform. When the plane carrying the distinguished guests landed at the airport, the welcoming crowd waved bouquets, cheered, and applauded enthusiastically.

　　Chairman Chand and his wife accepted flowers presented by the Young Pioneers; all the distinguished guests also received flowers from the welcomers. During a short rest in the airport lounge, Chairman Chand said, "I have been very well received in every city I have visited in China, just like being at home."

　　Afterwards, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Luo Shigao, Chen Chou, Pei Dongli, and others, took cars to their residence to rest. When the vehicles carrying the distinguished guests passed through the city, they were applauded and cheered by thousands of citizens along the streets.

　　At 7:30 PM, Chand, head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, and Gurubachan Singh, Secretary of the delegation, and Ram Marathe, Stage Supervisor, Director and Program Director of All India Radio, met with Luo Shigao and Chen Chou, Deputy Mayors of Chongqing.

　　At 8:00 PM that evening, Chairman Chand and all members of the delegation attended a Sichuan Opera performance hosted by the Chongqing Municipal Bureau of Culture, and watched performances such as "Niu Gao Tears Up the Decree," "槐荫会" (Huaiyin Meeting), "Wu Song Fights the Tiger," and "Interrogation of Hong" performed by famous Sichuan Opera actors Zhou Qihe, Zeng Ronghua, Jing Hua, and others.

### 印度文化代表团到达重庆访问

1955-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团到达重庆访问
    新华社重庆十五日电　以钱达为首的印度文化代表团在十五日上午十二时乘飞机到达重庆。重庆市副市长罗士高、陈筹、重庆市文化局副局长裴东篱以及重庆市文化艺术界人士五百多人到机场欢迎。
    飞机场被各色彩旗装饰一新，站台上高悬着“欢迎印度文化代表团”的红布金字横幅。当载着贵宾们的飞机在机场上降落时，欢迎的人群挥动着花束热烈欢呼鼓掌。
    钱达团长及夫人接受了少年先锋队员的献花；全体贵宾也受到欢迎者的献花。贵宾们在机场的休息室里作短时间的休息时，钱达团长说：“我到中国每一个城市都受到很好的接待，像在自己家里一样。”
    接着，贵宾们由罗士高、陈筹、裴东篱等陪同乘车到住地休息。贵宾们的车辆经过市区时，受到了沿街几千市民的鼓掌欢呼。
    下午七时半，印度文化代表团团长钱达和代表团秘书古尔巴昌·辛格，舞台监督、全印广播电台主任兼节目指导拉姆·马拉太会见了重庆市副市长罗士高、陈筹。
    当晚八时，钱达团长和代表团全体团员出席了重庆市文化局举办的川剧晚会，观看了川剧著名演员周企何、曾荣华、竞华等演出的“牛皋扯旨”、“槐荫会”、“武松打虎”、“拷红”等节目。
