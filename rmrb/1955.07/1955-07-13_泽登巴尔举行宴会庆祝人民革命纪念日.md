Title: Tsedenbal Holds Banquet to Celebrate Anniversary of the People's Revolution
Author:
Date: 1955-07-13
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 12th. Mongolian People's Republic Premier Tsedenbal held a banquet on the evening of the 11th to celebrate the 34th anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution.
    Among those present at the banquet were Damba, First Secretary of Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of Great People's Hural; Shirindib, First Deputy Premier; and Deputy Premiers Lubsan and Maydar, as well as heads of government departments and people's organizations.
    Also invited to the banquet were He Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia; Hong Dong-chul, Korean Ambassador; Minichi, Hungarian Ambassador; Lokanov, Bulgarian Ambassador; Hoang Van Hoan, Vietnamese Minister; Dasfu, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy; and diplomatic officials from various embassies. The entire Chinese science, education, and art workers delegation, headed by Hou Wailu, which is currently visiting Mongolia, was also invited to the banquet.

### 泽登巴尔举行宴会庆祝人民革命纪念日

1955-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔举行宴会庆祝人民革命纪念日
    新华社十二日讯　乌兰巴托消息：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔在十一日晚间举行宴会，庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十四周年。
    出席宴会的有蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记处第一书记达姆巴，大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布，第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理鲁布桑、迈达尔及政府各部门和各人民团体负责人。
    应邀出席宴会的还有中国驻蒙古大使何英，朝鲜大使洪东哲，匈牙利大使明尼契，保加利亚大使洛卡诺夫，越南公使黄文欢，苏联大使馆临时代办达斯福和各使馆外交官员。以侯外庐为首的现在蒙古访问的中国科学、教育、艺术工作者代表团全体人员也应邀出席了宴会。
