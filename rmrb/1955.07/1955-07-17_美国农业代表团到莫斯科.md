Title: US Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: A US agricultural delegation arrived in Moscow by plane on the 15th. Soviet Deputy Minister of Agriculture Bowen, Professor Nikolaev of the Moscow Timiryazev Agricultural Academy, and US Chargé d'Affaires to the Soviet Union, Warmsley, welcomed the delegation at the airport.

The delegation is composed of farmers, scientists, entrepreneurs, and journalists, and is headed by William Lambert, Dean of the College of Agriculture at the University of Nebraska. During their stay in the Soviet Union, the delegation will visit the Soviet Agricultural Exhibition and will visit various parts of the Soviet Union to study the production conditions of collective farms and state farms, as well as visit industrial enterprises, machine tractor stations, and scientific research institutes. Before leaving for the Soviet Union, the delegation held a press conference in Washington. A representative of the US Department of Agriculture said at the press conference: the agricultural delegation is a "non-official delegation" and its members are not "official representatives of the US government."

### 美国农业代表团到莫斯科

1955-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    美国农业代表团到莫斯科
    据新华社讯　美国农业代表团十五日乘飞机到达莫斯科。苏联农业部副部长博文，莫斯科季米里雅泽夫农学院教授尼古拉耶夫和美国驻苏联代办华姆斯莱前往机场欢迎。
    代表团由农民、科学家、企业家和新闻记者组成，团长是内布拉斯加大学的农学院院长威廉·兰伯特。代表团在苏联期间将参观苏联农业展览会，并且将到苏联各地访问，研究集体农庄和国营农场的生产情况并且参观工业企业、机器拖拉机站和科学研究所。代表团在动身来苏联前曾在华盛顿举行记者招待会。美国农业部代表在记者招待会上致开会词说：农业代表团是“非官方性质的代表团”，它的成员不是“美国政府的官方代表”。
