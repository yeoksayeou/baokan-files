Title: The Friendship Between Chinese and Soviet Children
Author:
Date: 1955-07-02
Page: 3
　　
　　The Friendship Between Chinese and Soviet Children
    On June 29th, a grand children's gathering themed on celebrating Sino-Soviet friendship was held in the "Sokolniki" Culture and Recreation Park in Moscow.
    In the park's open-air theater, the flags of the sixteen Soviet republics and the national emblems of China and the Soviet Union, made of fresh flowers, were displayed.
    More than eight thousand Moscow children participated in the gathering, some of whom specially wore Chinese national costumes. When the representatives of the Chinese Young Pioneers and the staff of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union appeared at the venue, the Moscow children warmly applauded to welcome them. Sulemov, Secretary of the Railway District Committee of the Lenin Communist Youth League, first extended congratulations to the children. The children sang in unison the song "Moscow-Beijing," which praised the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union.
    Zhang Yingwu, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, introduced the life and study of Chinese children to the children, and talked about their love for Soviet children. Nina Zasimova, a girl from Moscow No. 660 Primary School, greeted the guests on behalf of the Young Pioneers of Moscow.
    Finally, a grand performance was held. Moscow artists sang Chinese songs and dances for the children, which were warmly welcomed by the children. After the performance, the children also visited an exhibition of pictures introducing the happy lives of children and young people in New China with great interest, and watched Chinese fairy tales and films about China.
                                (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中苏两国儿童的友谊

1955-07-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　中苏两国儿童的友谊
    六月二十九日，在莫斯科“索科尼基”文化休息公园，举行了盛大的以庆祝中苏友谊为主题的儿童集会。
    在公园的露天剧场里，装饰着苏联十六个加盟共和国的国旗和用鲜花组成的中苏两国的国徽。
    八千多个莫斯科儿童参加了这个集会，他们有的还特地穿上了中国的民族服装。当中国少年先锋队代表和中国驻苏大使馆工作人员在会场上出现时，莫斯科儿童热烈鼓掌欢迎。列宁共产主义青年团铁路区委员会书记苏列莫夫首先向儿童们致贺。儿童们齐声高唱起歌颂中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊的歌曲“莫斯科—北京”。
    中国驻苏大使馆文化参赞张映吾向儿童们介绍了中国儿童的生活和学习情况，谈到了他们对苏联儿童的热爱。莫斯科第六六零小学女生尼娜·扎西莫娃代表莫斯科的少先队员向来宾们问候。
    最后，举行了盛大的表演。莫斯科的艺术家们向儿童们演唱了中国的歌曲和舞蹈，获得了儿童们的热烈欢迎。表演结束后，儿童们又很有兴趣地参观了介绍新中国儿童和青年幸福生活的图片展览，看了中国童话剧和有关中国的电影。
                                （新华社）
