Title: Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Ho Chi Minh Watch Performances by Artists from the Indian Cultural Delegation

Author:

Date: 1955-07-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, who is currently visiting our country, watched a performance by the Indian Cultural Delegation at Huairen Hall tonight.

Also present at the Huairen Hall tonight to watch the performance by the Indian artists were: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, and Li Weihan, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, and Chen Yi, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Zhang Wentian, Zhang Hanfu, and Ji Pengfei, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture; Ding Xilin and Xia Yan, Vice Ministers, as well as heads of various government departments, representatives to the National People's Congress in Beijing, leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and well-known figures from all walks of life.

Members of the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Truong Chinh, Le Van Hien, Phan Anh, Nguyen Van Huyen, Nghiem Xuan Yem, Ung Van Khiem, Nguyen Duy Trinh, and Hoang Van Hoan, attended the performance.

Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and his wife, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China, also attended the performance by the Indian artists.

At 8:00 p.m., Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Ho Chi Minh, and Chand, head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, entered the hall. The entire audience stood up and cheered and applauded enthusiastically, lasting for five minutes.

The thirteen wonderful musical and dance performances presented by the Indian artists tonight were warmly welcomed.

At tonight's performance, Chinese dance workers who are studying Kathakali dance from Krishna Kutty, a member of the Indian Cultural Delegation and an Indian dancer, performed the "Happy Dance." When the performers and their teacher took their bows together, enthusiastic applause resounded throughout the hall again and again.

After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai personally presented flowers to Chand, the head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, and his wife, expressing his gratitude for the excellent performances of the Indian artists. Young Chinese literary and art workers also went on stage to present flowers to the Indian artists to express their thanks. At this moment, a burst of applause echoed in the hall for a long time.

After the curtain call, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi, Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui, Vice Ministers Ding Xilin and Xia Yan, accompanied by Delegation Leader Chand and Ambassador R.K. Nehru, went to the stage and shook hands with the Indian artists. Deepak, the performer of the Punjabi song, was the first to pin a flower on Premier Zhou Enlai's chest. Many Indian artists presented flowers to Premier Zhou Enlai. Premier Zhou Enlai also took a group photo with the friends of the Indian Cultural Delegation.

At the farewell, Premier Zhou Enlai and the Indian artists chanted "Long live Sino-Indian friendship" in Hindi. (Attached photo)
    Chairman Mao Zedong received Chand, the head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, before the performance of the Indian Cultural Delegation. Special correspondent of this newspaper, Hou Bo, photographed it.

### 毛泽东主席和胡志明主席　观看印度文化代表团艺术家表演

1955-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛泽东主席和胡志明主席
　　观看印度文化代表团艺术家表演
    新华社三十日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东和正在我国访问的越南民主共和国主席胡志明今晚在怀仁堂一起观看了印度文化代表团的表演。
    今晚在怀仁堂观看印度艺术家表演的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、黄炎培、李维汉，国务院副总理陈云、邓小平、陈毅，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫、姬鹏飞，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，副部长丁西林、夏衍，以及政府各部门负责人，在北京的全国人民代表大会代表，各民主党派、各人民团体负责人和社会各界著名人士。
    越南民主共和国政府代表团团员长征、黎文献、潘英、阮文煊、严春庵、雍文谦、阮维祯、黄文欢出席观看了表演。
    印度驻华大使赖嘉文和夫人，以及各国驻华使节和外交官员都出席了印度艺术家的表演会。
    八时正，毛泽东主席、胡志明主席和印度文化代表团团长钱达等步入大厅。这时，全场起立热烈欢呼鼓掌，持续达五分钟之久。
    印度艺术家们今晚表演的十三个精采的音乐歌舞节目，都受到了热烈的欢迎。
    在今晚举行的表演会上，向印度文化代表团团员、印度舞蹈家克里希纳·库蒂学习卡达卡利舞的中国舞蹈工作者表演了“快乐舞”。当表演者和他们的教师一同谢幕时，大厅中一再响起了热烈的掌声。
    演出结束后，周恩来总理亲向印度文化代表团团长钱达和他的夫人赠花，对印度艺术家们的卓越表演表示感谢。中国青年文艺工作者也登台向印度艺术家献花致谢。这时，大厅中响起了持久不绝的一片掌声。
    闭幕以后，周恩来总理和陈毅副总理，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞、副部长丁西林、夏衍，由钱达团长和赖嘉文大使陪同到舞台上和印度艺术家们握手。旁遮普之歌的表演者迪帕克第一个把一朵鲜花戴在周恩来总理的胸前。不少印度艺术家把朵朵鲜花献给周恩来总理。周恩来总理并和印度文化代表团的朋友们在一起合影留念。
    送别时，周恩来总理和印度艺术家们一起用印地语欢呼“中印友好万岁”。（附图片）
