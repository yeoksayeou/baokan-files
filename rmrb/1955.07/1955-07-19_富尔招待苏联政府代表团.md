Title: Four Holds Reception for Soviet Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-07-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 18th. TASS, Geneva, July 18th: French Prime Minister Four held a banquet on July 17th to receive the Soviet government delegation attending the Four-Power Heads of Government Conference.
    Those attending the banquet included Bulganin (head of the delegation), Khrushchev, Molotov, Zhukov, and Soviet Ambassador to France Vinogradov.
    On the French side, those attending the banquet included Pinay, Billotte, Doumer, Elissou, Laloy, and Joxe.

### 富尔招待苏联政府代表团

1955-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    富尔招待苏联政府代表团
    新华社十八日讯　塔斯社日内瓦十八日讯：法国总理富尔七月十七日设宴招待参加四国政府首脑会议的苏联政府代表团。
    出席宴会的有布尔加宁（代表团团长）、赫鲁晓夫、莫洛托夫、朱可夫和苏联驻法国大使维诺格拉多夫。
    法国方面出席宴会的有比内、毕拉尔、杜阿梅、伊里苏、拉洛瓦和约克斯。
