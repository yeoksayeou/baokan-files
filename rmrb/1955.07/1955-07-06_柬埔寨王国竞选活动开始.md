Title: Kingdom of Cambodia Election Campaign Begins
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 4th. According to a broadcast from Saigon Radio: The election campaign for the Kingdom of Cambodia's general election is scheduled to begin on the 4th. Four political parties are now prepared to participate in the election. These four parties are the People's Socialist Community, the Democratic Party, the Liberal Party, and the Renovation Party. The general election is scheduled to be held on September 11th.

### 柬埔寨王国竞选活动开始

1955-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

　  柬埔寨王国竞选活动开始
    新华社河内四日电　据西贡电台广播：柬埔寨王国普选的竞选活动定于四日开始。现在已有四个政党准备参加竞选，这四个政党是人民社会同盟、民主党、自由党和复兴党。普选预定在九月十一日举行。
