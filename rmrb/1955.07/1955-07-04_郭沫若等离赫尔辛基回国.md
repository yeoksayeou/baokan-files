Title: Guo Moruo and Others Leave Helsinki for Home
Author:
Date: 1955-07-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, July 3rd: Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council and head of the Chinese delegation to the World Peace Assembly, along with Mao Dun, deputy heads Chen Shutong, Liao Chengzhi, and Secretary-General Liu Ningyi, left by plane at 3 p.m. on the 2nd to return to China. Among those seeing them off at the airport were Yap Wen-pei, Chairman of the Finnish-Chinese Association, and representatives from the Finnish Committee for the Defense of Peace.

Chen Xinren, China's ambassador to Finland, and personnel from the embassy were also at the airport to see them off.

### 郭沫若等离赫尔辛基回国

1955-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若等离赫尔辛基回国
    新华社赫尔辛基三日电　出席世界和平大会的世界和平理事会副主席郭沫若，中国代表团团长茅盾、副团长陈叔通、廖承志和秘书长刘宁一等在二日下午三时乘飞机返国。在机场欢送的有芬中协会主席叶文佩和芬兰保卫和平委员会的代表等。
    我国驻芬大使陈辛仁及大使馆人员也在机场送行。
