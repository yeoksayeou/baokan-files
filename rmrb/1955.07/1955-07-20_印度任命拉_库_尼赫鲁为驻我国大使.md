Title: India Appoints R.K. Nehru as Ambassador to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1955-07-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 19th — The Indian government announced on the 18th that R.K. Nehru, Secretary of External Affairs of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, has been appointed as the Indian Ambassador to the People's Republic of China.

    R.K. Nehru and his wife are expected to depart for Beijing around mid-September.

### 印度任命拉·库·尼赫鲁为驻我国大使

1955-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    印度任命拉·库·尼赫鲁为驻我国大使
    新华社新德里十九日电　印度政府在十八日宣布，印度外交部外事秘书拉·库·尼赫鲁已经被任命为印度驻中华人民共和国大使。
    拉·库·尼赫鲁和他的夫人大约将在九月中旬动身前往北京。
