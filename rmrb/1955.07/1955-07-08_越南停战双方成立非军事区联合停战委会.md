Title: Vietnam Armistice: Both Sides Establish Joint Armistice Committee for Demilitarized Zone
Author:
Date: 1955-07-08
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 6th: Vietnam News Agency reported on the 5th: The delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army and the delegation of the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces in the Central Joint Armistice Commission of Vietnam passed a joint resolution on July 2nd, announcing that the former Joint Armistice Committees of Quang Binh, Quang Tri, and Thua Thien provinces would henceforth become Joint Armistice Committees for the Demilitarized Zone.
　　According to this resolution, the Joint Armistice Committee for the Demilitarized Zone will guarantee the implementation of the various clauses in the Geneva Accords related to the demilitarized zone and will guarantee the implementation of the regulations unanimously agreed upon by the delegations of both sides of the former Joint Armistice Committees of Quang Binh, Quang Tri, and Thua Thien provinces.

### 越南停战双方成立非军事区联合停战委会

1955-07-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南停战双方成立非军事区联合停战委会
    新华社河内六日电　越南通讯社五日讯：越南中央联合停战委员会越南人民军总司令部代表团和法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团在七月二日通过的一个联合决议中宣布：前广平、广治、承天地区联合停战委员会今后要成为非军事区联合停战委员会。
    根据这个决议，非军事区联合停战委员会将保证执行日内瓦协定中和非军事区有关的各项条款，并且保证执行前广平、广治、承天地区联合停战委员会双方代表团所一致同意的规定。
