Title: New British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. Humphrey, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-07-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Mr. Humphrey, the former British Chargé d'Affaires to China, was transferred from Beijing on May 28th. Mr. Humphrey, the new British Chargé d'Affaires to China, arrived in Beijing by train on the 11th. Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the station to welcome him.
Also at the station to welcome him were foreign envoys to China and embassy staff. Staff from the British Chargé d'Affaires Office in China were also there to welcome him.

### 英新任驻华代办欧念儒到京

1955-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    英新任驻华代办欧念儒到京
    新华社十一日讯　英国前任驻我国代办杜维廉已于五月二十八日奉调离京。英国新任驻我国代办欧念儒已于十一日乘车抵京。前往车站欢迎的有我外交部礼宾司副司长王倬如。
    前往车站欢迎的还有外国驻华使节和使馆人员。英国驻华代办处人员也前往欢迎。
