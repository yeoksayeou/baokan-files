Title: US and Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Air Force Summit Concludes
Author:
Date: 1955-07-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The secret meeting between US Far East Air Force Commander Carter, Pacific Air Force Commander Smith, and other US Air Force chiefs and the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, which began in Taipei on the 8th, concluded on the 9th. According to reports, the second day's meeting focused on discussing the issue of strengthening the provocative activities of the US-Chiang Air Force against the Chinese mainland. The meeting submitted new recommendations to the Pentagon in Washington, requesting further strengthening of the US-Chiang Air Force strength in the entire Taiwan area.

### 美国和蒋贼的空军首脑会议结束

1955-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美国和蒋贼的空军首脑会议结束
    新华社十日讯　美国远东空军司令卡特、太平洋空军司令史密斯等美国空军首脑和蒋介石卖国集团八日开始在台北举行的秘密会议，已经在九日结束。据消息透露，第二天的会议集中讨论了加强美蒋空军对中国大陆的挑衅活动问题。会议向华盛顿五角大楼提出了新的建议，要求进一步加强美蒋在整个台湾地区的空军力量。
