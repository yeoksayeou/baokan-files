Title: Members of the Vietnamese Government Delegation Continue Their Visit in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-07-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The members of the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Phan Anh, Nguyen Van Huyen, Nghiem Xuan Yem, and Ung Van Khiem, visited the China Natural Environment and Mineral Resources Exhibition in the morning of the 1st.

The distinguished guests visited the sections of natural environment, ferrous metals, non-ferrous metals, non-metals, coal, and petroleum successively. They were very interested in China's rich mineral resources.

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. On the morning of the 2nd, Nguyen Van Huyen and Nghiem Xuan Yem, members of the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, visited the Renmin University of China.

During the visit, Nguyen Van Huyen and Nghiem Xuan Yem were pleased to meet Vietnamese students studying here and had a brief conversation with them. Nguyen Van Huyen urged the Vietnamese students to study hard, master their skills, and return to build their motherland.

There are more than 50 Vietnamese students studying at the Renmin University of China. They work hard in their studies and achieve excellent results.

The distinguished guests visited the school's exhibition room and library, and then had a discussion with the school's leaders.

Before the visit, Vice-President Nie Zhen gave the distinguished guests a detailed introduction to the situation of this new type of regular university.

In the evening, some members of the delegation, Le Van Hien, Phan Anh, Nguyen Van Huyen, Nghiem Xuan Yem, Ung Van Khiem, Nguyen Duy Trinh, and Pham Ngoc Thach, visited the Summer Palace.

### 越南政府代表团团员继续在京参观访问

1955-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南政府代表团团员继续在京参观访问
    新华社一日讯　越南民主共和国政府代表团团员潘英、阮文煊、严春庵、雍文谦，在一日上午参观了中国自然环境与矿产资源展览。
    贵宾们先后参观了自然环境、黑色金属、有色金属、非金属、煤炭、石油等部分，他们对中国有这样丰富的矿产资源，感到很大兴趣。
    新华社二日讯　越南民主共和国政府代表团团员阮文煊、严春庵二日上午参观了中国人民大学。
    阮文煊和严春庵在参观时，高兴地会见了在这里学习的越南学生，并进行了简短的谈话。阮文煊叮嘱越南学生要努力学习，学好本领回去建设自己的祖国。
    在中国人民大学学习的越南学生共有五十多人，他们在学习上刻苦努力，成绩优良。
    贵宾们参观了这个学校的展览室和图书馆，参观后和学校负责人进行了座谈。
    在参观前，副校长聂真曾向贵宾们详细地介绍了这所新型的正规的大学的情况。
    晚间，代表团部分团员黎文献、潘英、阮文煊、严春庵、雍文谦、阮维祯、范玉石游览了颐和园。
