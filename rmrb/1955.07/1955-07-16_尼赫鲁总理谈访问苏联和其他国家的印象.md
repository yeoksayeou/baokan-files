Title: Prime Minister Nehru Discusses Impressions of Visits to the Soviet Union and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-07-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 15th (TASS dispatch): According to the "Hindustan Standard," Indian Home Minister Pant held an informal reception for Prime Minister Nehru on the 13th. Those attending the reception included Indian President Prasad, Vice President Radhakrishnan, and Indian government officials.

At the reception, Nehru spoke about his impressions of his visits to the Soviet Union and other countries. He particularly emphasized that wherever he went, he found a universal desire for peace.

Nehru said that the friendship shown to him by the Soviet Union was undoubtedly sincere. When talking about Soviet industrialization, Nehru pointed out that there was a high level of mechanization not only in industry but also in agriculture. He emphasized that in the Soviet Union he saw care for the children of workers.

### 尼赫鲁总理谈访问苏联和其他国家的印象

1955-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理谈访问苏联和其他国家的印象
    新华社十五日讯　塔斯社德里十四日讯：据“印度斯坦旗报”报道，印度内政部长潘特在十三日为尼赫鲁总理举行了一次非正式的招待会。出席招待会的有印度总统普拉沙德，副总统拉德哈克里希南和印度政府官员。
    尼赫鲁在招待会上讲到了这次去苏联和其他国家访问的印象。他特别着重指出，他不论到什么地方，都发觉到对和平的普遍愿望。
    尼赫鲁说，苏联对待他的友谊无疑是真诚的。尼赫鲁在谈到苏联的工业化时指出，不仅在工业中，而且在农业中都有着高度的机械化水平。他强调指出，他在苏联看到了对工人的孩子们的关怀。
