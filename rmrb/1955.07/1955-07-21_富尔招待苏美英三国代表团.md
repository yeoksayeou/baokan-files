Title: Faure Hosts Delegation from the Soviet Union, the United States, and the United Kingdom
Author:
Date: 1955-07-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva dispatch from TASS: French Prime Minister Faure hosted a banquet on the 19th to entertain the delegations from the Soviet Union, the United States, and the United Kingdom who were participating in the Four Power Summit Conference.
    Attending the banquet were: the head of the Soviet government delegation, Bulganin, and members Khrushchev, Molotov, Zhukov, and Gromyko.
    Attending the banquet were: the head of the British delegation, Eden, and members Macmillan, Kirkpatrick, and Caccia; members of the American delegation Dulles, Bowie, and Fliegel.
    Representing France at the banquet were: Pinay, Billotte, and Laloy.

### 富尔招待苏美英三国代表团

1955-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    富尔招待苏美英三国代表团
    新华社二十日讯　塔斯社日内瓦讯：法国总理富尔十九日设宴招待参加四国政府首脑会议的苏联、美国和英国代表团。
    出席宴会的有：苏联政府代表团团长布尔加宁，团员赫鲁晓夫、莫洛托夫、朱可夫和葛罗米柯。
    出席宴会的有：英国代表团团长艾登、团员麦克米伦、柯克帕特里克、卡契阿，美国代表团团员杜勒斯、鲍依、弗列格尔。
    法国方面参加宴会的有：比内、毕拉尔、拉洛瓦。
