Title: China Appoints Wang Bingnan as Representative to Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks; U.S. Announces Appointment of Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Johnson, as Representative

Author: 
Date: 1955-07-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: The Government of the People's Republic of China has decided to appoint Wang Bingnan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Poland, as the representative of the People's Republic of China to the ambassadorial talks between China and the United States of America to be held in Geneva.

It is reported that the U.S. State Department announced on July 25 that the U.S. government has decided to appoint U. Alexis Johnson, the United States Ambassador to the Czechoslovak Republic, as the representative of the United States of America to the ambassadorial talks between the United States of America and the People's Republic of China to be held in Geneva.

### 我国委派王炳南为中美两国大使级会谈代表  美国宣布委派驻捷大使约翰逊为会谈代表

1955-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我国委派王炳南为中美两国大使级会谈代表
    美国宣布委派驻捷大使约翰逊为会谈代表
    新华社二十七日讯　中华人民共和国政府决定委派中华人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国大使王炳南为中华人民共和国同美利坚合众国在日内瓦举行大使级会谈的代表。
    据悉，美国国务院七月二十五日宣布，美国政府决定委派美国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使乌·亚厉克西斯·约翰逊为美利坚合众国同中华人民共和国在日内瓦举行大使级会谈的代表。
