Title: President Ho Chi Minh Visits Beijing Machine Manufacturing School
Author:
Date: 1955-07-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 4th: President Ho Chi Minh and Phạm Ngọc Thạch, members of the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, visited the Beijing Machine Manufacturing School on the afternoon of the 4th. This is a secondary technical school with complete facilities and more than 2,000 students.
　　Accompanying President Ho Chi Minh on the visit were Huang Jing, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Luo Guibo, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
　　When President Ho Chi Minh arrived, the entire school lined up on both sides of the road to warmly welcome him. The Vietnamese students studying at this school were particularly happy and excited today. They cheered and applauded non-stop with the Chinese students.
　　President Ho Chi Minh, accompanied by Principal Gao Xijin and others, visited various well-equipped laboratories and watched various complex experimental machines. Finally, he also visited the internship factory, where the students who were interning were operating various huge machines.
　　In a wide courtyard, a dozen Vietnamese students and countless Chinese students surrounded President Ho Chi Minh, they applauded and cheered for a long time, vying to shake hands with President Ho Chi Minh. At the end of the visit, President Ho Chi Minh was once again surrounded by the enthusiastic students gathered in the courtyard, who would not let him leave for a long time. The car had already started, but the students still crowded behind and beside the car, waving goodbye to President Ho Chi Minh.

### 胡志明主席访问北京机器制造学校

1955-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席访问北京机器制造学校
    新华社四日讯　四日中午，胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府代表团团员范玉石访问了北京机器制造学校。这是一所中等技术学校，校内设备完整，有学生两千多人。
    陪同胡志明主席访问的，有第一机械工业部部长黄敬，中国驻越南大使罗贵波，外交部礼宾司司长柯华。
    当胡志明主席到达时，全校学生列队在道路两旁夹道热烈欢迎。在这个学校学习的越南同学今天特别高兴和激动，他们和中国学生在一起不停地欢呼鼓掌。
    胡志明主席由校长高锡金等陪同参观了各个设备完整的实验室，观看了各种复杂的实验机械。最后，并参观了实习工厂，在那里，正在实习的学生们开动了各种庞大的机器。
    在一个宽阔的院落里，十几个越南学生和数不清的中国学生把胡志明主席围住，他们长久地鼓掌欢呼，争相和胡志明主席握手。在参观结束时，胡志明主席再一次被聚集在院子里的热情的同学们包围起来，很久不让他走开。汽车已经开动了，学生们还前拥后挤地跟在汽车后面和两旁，挥手向胡志明主席道别。
