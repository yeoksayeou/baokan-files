Title: Four Foreign Ministers' Meeting Adopts Agenda for Four-Power Summit
Author:
Date: 1955-07-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 20th (TASS): TASS special correspondent reports: On the morning of the 19th, the foreign ministers of the four powers – the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France – participating in the Four-Power Summit held their first meeting.

    Attending the meeting for the Soviet side was Molotov, for the United States, Dulles, for Britain, Macmillan, and for France, Pinay. Also attending the meeting were advisors and experts from the four countries' delegations. The meeting discussed the agenda for the afternoon's summit meeting.

    The foreign ministers unanimously adopted a resolution stipulating that the agenda for the summit meeting on the 19th should include the four issues mentioned in the speeches of all four heads of government at the meeting on the 18th, namely: 1. The German question; 2. The question of European security; 3. The question of disarmament; 4. The question of developing contacts between East and West.

    The foreign ministers also reached an agreement that whether other issues raised in the speeches of the four heads of government at the meeting on the 18th should be included on the agenda would be decided by the heads of government.

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 20th (TASS): On the morning of July 20th, the four foreign ministers held their second meeting, attended by Molotov, Dulles, Macmillan, and Pinay.

    Also attending the meeting were advisors and experts from the four countries' delegations.

    Macmillan chaired the meeting.

    The foreign ministers unanimously decided to recommend that the four heads of government discuss the second item on the agenda – the issue of European security – at the meeting on the 20th.

### 四国外长会议通过四国政府首脑会议议程

1955-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    四国外长会议通过四国政府首脑会议议程
    新华社二十日讯　塔斯社日内瓦十九日讯：塔斯社特派记者报道：十九日上午，四国政府首脑会议的参加者——苏、美、英、法四国外长举行了首次会议。
    苏联方面出席会议的是莫洛托夫、美国方面是杜勒斯、英国方面是麦克米伦、法国方面是比内。出席会议的还有四国代表团的顾问和专家。会议讨论了十九日下午的政府首脑会议的议程问题。
    外长们一致通过一项决议，规定十九日的政府首脑会议的议程应该包括所有四国政府首脑在十八日会议上的发言中所谈到的四个问题，即：一、德国问题；二、欧洲安全问题；三、裁军问题；四、发展东西方之间的接触问题。
    外长们还达成协议：关于四国政府首脑在十八日会议上的发言中所提出的其他问题是否列入议程，将提交政府首脑来决定。
    新华社二十日讯　塔斯社日内瓦二十日讯：七月二十日上午，四国外长举行第二次会议，参加会议的有莫洛托夫、杜勒斯、麦克米伦和比内。
    出席会议的还有四国代表团的顾问和专家。
    会议由麦克米伦担任主席。
    外长们作出了一致的决定，建议四国政府首脑在二十日的会议上讨论议程的第二个问题——欧洲安全的问题。
