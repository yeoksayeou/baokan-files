    Our Great Motherland
    Fuzhou's bodiless lacquerware is a famous handicraft in our country, with a history of more than two hundred years. Now, the lacquerware produced by the Fuzhou Bodiless Lacquerware Company is exquisitely designed, with an increasing variety of types, and the output is also increasing year by year. These two female workers are packaging newly made bodiless teacups.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    福州的脱胎漆器是我国著名的手工艺品，至今已有二百多年的历史。现在福州脱胎漆器公司出产的漆器，式样精巧，种类日益繁多，产量也逐年增加。这两个女工正在包装新制成的脱胎茶杯。
