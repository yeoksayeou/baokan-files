Title: Czechoslovakia and Burma Establish Formal Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-07-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 25th: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency on the 24th, the governments of Czechoslovakia and Burma have agreed to establish formal diplomatic relations between the two countries. According to the agreement, the Czechoslovak Consulate General in Rangoon will become an embassy. Through this agreement, the two governments expressed their hope to strive for the further development of comprehensive cooperation between the two countries.

### 捷克斯洛伐克同缅甸建立正式外交关系

1955-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克同缅甸建立正式外交关系
    新华社布拉格二十五日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社二十四日消息，捷克斯洛伐克和缅甸两国政府已经同意建立两国间的正式外交关系。根据协议，捷克斯洛伐克驻仰光总领事馆将成为大使馆。两国政府通过这个协议表示希望为进一步发展两国间的全面合作而努力。
