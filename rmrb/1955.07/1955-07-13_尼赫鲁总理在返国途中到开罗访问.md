Title: Prime Minister Nehru Visits Cairo on His Way Home

Author:

Date: 1955-07-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 12th: According to Indian press reports, Indian Prime Minister Nehru arrived in Cairo on the 11th for a 24-hour visit on his way home from London.

At Cairo Airport, Nehru said in a speech that everyone, including the Soviets, were looking forward to the upcoming Four Power Summit. He said, "I have found that there is an extremely strong desire everywhere for peace and for easing tensions in order to peacefully resolve outstanding issues."

Nehru stated that he would hold talks with Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser.

In response to a question regarding his views on Pakistan's decision to join the Turkey-Iraq pact, he said that all such treaties "do not help peaceful solutions" and "only create suspicion."

Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser and ministers were at the airport to welcome Nehru.

### 尼赫鲁总理在返国途中到开罗访问

1955-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理在返国途中到开罗访问
    新华社新德里十二日电　据印度新闻处消息，印度总理尼赫鲁在从伦敦返国途中，十一日到达开罗，进行为时二十四小时的访问。
    尼赫鲁在开罗机场发表谈话说，每一个人，包括苏联人在内，都在展望着四大国首脑将要举行的会谈。他说，“我发现，到处都有着对于和平与和缓紧张局势以便和平解决悬而未决的问题的极其强烈的愿望。”
    尼赫鲁表示，他将同埃及总理纳赛尔举行会谈。
    在回答关于他对巴基斯坦决定加入土耳其—伊拉克条约一事的看法的问题时，他说，一切这种条约“都无助于和平解决”而“只会产生怀疑”。
    到机场欢迎尼赫鲁的有埃及总理纳赛尔和部长们。
