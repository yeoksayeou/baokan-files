Title: Mao Zedong's Works Are Published in Large Quantities at Home and Abroad
Author:
Date: 1955-07-01
Page: 1

　  Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Mao Zedong's classic works are being published in large quantities at home and abroad.

　  From October 1951 to the present, the People's Publishing House has printed 9,036,500 copies of the three volumes of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong." In addition, various single editions of Mao Zedong's works, which have been issued in large quantities in the past, are still being published one after another. Since liberation, 1,589,000 copies of "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" published by the state publishing institutions have been issued; 1,300,900 copies of "On Practice" have been issued. The works of Mao Zedong translated into various ethnic minority languages include versions in Mongolian, Tibetan, Uyghur, and Kazakh.

　  According to incomplete statistics, Mao Zedong's works have been published in more than 50 countries. Countries such as the Soviet Union, Poland, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, and North Korea have published "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" in their respective languages. Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, personally translated Mao Zedong's "On Practice" and "On Contradiction" into Vietnamese for publication.

　  The United Kingdom, the United States, India, France, Japan, and Israel have also published English, French, Japanese, and Hebrew versions of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" respectively. Mao Zedong's works have also been translated and published in countries such as Italy, Iran, Burma, the Netherlands, and Chile. In India, Mao Zedong's works have been translated into several languages such as Hindi, Bengali, Urdu, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam.

　  China's Foreign Languages ​​Press has translated and published some of Mao Zedong's works in Russian, English, French, Indonesian, and German, which have been widely distributed abroad and warmly welcomed by readers in various countries.

### 毛泽东的著作在国内外大量发行

1955-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

　  毛泽东的著作在国内外大量发行
    新华社三十日讯　毛泽东的经典著作，在国内外大量发行。
    从一九五一年十月到现在，人民出版社出版的三卷“毛泽东选集”已印行了九百零三万六千五百册。另外，过去已大量发行的毛泽东著作的各种单篇本，现在还在陆续出版。解放以来，由国家出版机构出版的“论人民民主专政”已发行一百五十八万九千册；“实践论”已发行一百三十万零九千册。用各民族文字翻译的毛泽东的著作，已有蒙、藏、维吾尔、哈萨克等文字的版本。
    据不完全的统计，毛泽东的著作已在五十多个国家发行。苏联、波兰、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、朝鲜等国家，已分别以本国文字出版了“毛泽东选集”。越南民主共和国主席胡志明还曾亲自把毛泽东的“实践论”“矛盾论”等译成越南文字出版。
    英国、美国、印度、法国、日本和以色列也分别出版了英文、法文、日文和希伯来文版本的“毛泽东选集”。在意大利、伊朗、缅甸、荷兰、智利等国家也翻译出版了毛泽东的著作。在印度，毛泽东的著作已有印地文、孟加拉文、乌尔都文、马拉提文、泰米尔文、泰鲁固文、马拉雅兰文等好几种文字的译本。
    我国外文出版社以俄、英、法、印度尼西亚和德文翻译出版了毛泽东部分著作，在国外大量发行，受到各国读者热烈欢迎。
