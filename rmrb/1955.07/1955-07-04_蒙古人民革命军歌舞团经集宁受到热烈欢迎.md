Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Troupe warmly welcomed upon arrival in Jining
Author:
Date: 1955-07-04
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jining, 3rd. The Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Troupe, led by Major General Dunruib Damdinhu, arrived in Jining, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, by special train at 4:55 PM on the 3rd, accompanied by Wang Dizi, representative of our Ministry of National Defense, Wu Xiling, representative of the Ministry of Culture, and Alingqing Sonom, Second Secretary of the Mongolian People's Republic Embassy in China, who went to the border to welcome them. They were warmly welcomed by representatives from all walks of life in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.
　　Hafunga (Mongolian), Vice Chairman of the People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Ting Mao, Director of the Political Department of the Inner Mongolia Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Buhe (Mongolian), Deputy Director of the Cultural Bureau of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, were at the station to greet them. Also at the station to welcome them were members of the Inner Mongolia Military Region Cultural Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Inner Mongolia Song and Dance Troupe, as well as local cadres and Young Pioneers, totaling more than 140 people. The special train carrying the song and dance troupe left Jining for Beijing at 5:27 PM.

### 蒙古人民革命军歌舞团经集宁受到热烈欢迎

1955-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民革命军歌舞团经集宁受到热烈欢迎
    新华社集宁三日电　由敦儒布·达姆丁呼少将率领的蒙古人民革命军歌舞团一行由前往国境迎接的我国国防部代表王地子、文化部代表邬析零以及蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆二等秘书艾林清苏诺木等陪同，三日下午四时五十五分乘专车到达内蒙古自治区的集宁，受到内蒙古自治区各界代表的热烈欢迎。
    内蒙古自治区人民委员会副主席哈丰阿（蒙）、中国人民解放军内蒙古军区政治部主任廷楙、内蒙古自治区文化局副局长布和（蒙）等到车站迎接。到车站欢迎的还有中国人民解放军内蒙古军区文工团和内蒙古歌舞剧团的团员以及当地干部和少先队员共一百四十多人。下午五时二十七分歌舞团乘坐的专车离开集宁前往北京。
