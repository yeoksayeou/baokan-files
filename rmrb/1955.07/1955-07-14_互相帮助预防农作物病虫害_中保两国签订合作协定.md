Title: Mutual Assistance in Preventing Crop Diseases and Pests: China and Bulgaria Sign Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 13th: An agreement between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Bulgaria on cooperation in the prevention of crop diseases and insect pests was signed in Sofia on July 11. The agreement stipulates that China and Bulgaria will cooperate in preventing the spread of crop diseases and insect pests, exchange experiences and knowledge in protecting crops, and, when necessary, send experts to each other to provide mutual assistance.

Zhou Zhu'an, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Bulgaria, signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese government, and S. Todorov, Minister of Agriculture of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, signed the agreement on behalf of the Bulgarian government.

During the signing ceremony, Minister Todorov and Ambassador Zhou Zhu'an exchanged congratulatory messages.

### 互相帮助预防农作物病虫害  中保两国签订合作协定

1955-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    互相帮助预防农作物病虫害
    中保两国签订合作协定
    新华社十三日讯　索非亚消息：中华人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国之间的预防农作物病虫害合作协定，于七月十一日在索非亚签订。协定规定，中保两国在防止农作物病虫害的传播方面进行合作，并且互相交换保护农作物的经验和知识，必要时并且互派专家，互相帮助。
    代表中国政府在协定上签字的是中华人民共和国驻保加利亚特命全权大使周竹安，代表保加利亚政府在协定上签字的是保加利亚人民共和国农业部长斯·托多罗夫。
    签字仪式举行时，托多罗夫部长和周竹安大使曾互致祝词。
