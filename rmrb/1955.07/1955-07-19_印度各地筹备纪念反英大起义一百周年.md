Title: Preparations Underway Across India to Commemorate the Centenary of the Anti-British Uprising
Author:
Date: 1955-07-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 17th: Preparations are underway across India to commemorate the centenary of the 1857 Indian Mutiny against British colonial rule.

The "Hindustan Times" Lucknow reported that the preparatory committee established in Lucknow has recommended that the Uttar Pradesh government erect a memorial to the martyrs in Lucknow, as the city was a center of the uprising. The committee also suggested establishing a museum to collect and display books, charts, and pictures about the uprising.

The Compilation Committee for the "History of the Indian Independence Movement," established by the Indian government's Ministry of Education, is collecting materials on the history of India's struggle for freedom, especially the 1857 Mutiny. The committee will complete its work by December 1956.

"People's Publishing House" has planned to publish a collection of essays commemorating the centenary of the Mutiny, which will include scientific analyses by Indian progressive historians of the history of the national liberation struggle.

This great uprising against British colonial rule began on the evening of Sunday, May 10, 1857, in Meerut, forty-five miles from Delhi, and spread throughout northern and central India. The Indian people and Indian soldiers in the British army liberated Delhi from British occupation. This uprising was a strong challenge by the Indian people to British rule in India. The British colonial rulers suppressed the uprising with extreme brutality.

### 印度各地筹备纪念反英大起义一百周年

1955-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    印度各地筹备纪念反英大起义一百周年
    新华社新德里十七日电　印度各地正在筹备纪念反对英国殖民统治的一八五七年印度大起义一百周年。
    “印度斯坦时报”勒克瑙的消息说，勒克瑙成立的筹备委员会已经建议北方省政府在勒克瑙建立阵亡烈士纪念碑，因为这个城市曾经是起义的中心。委员会还建议建立博物馆，收集和陈列关于这次起义的书籍、图表和图片。
    印度政府教育部设立的“印度争取自由运动史”编纂委员会正在收集关于印度争取自由斗争的资料，特别是一八五七年大起义的资料。这个委员会将在一九五六年十二月以前完成它的工作。
    “人民出版社”已计划出版纪念大起义百周年的文集，其中将有印度进步历史学家关于民族解放斗争历史的科学的分析。
    这个反对英国殖民统治的伟大起义是在一八五七年五月十日（星期日）晚上，在离德里四十五英里的密拉特地方开始的，起义扩大到印度的整个北部和中部。印度人民和英国军队中的印度士兵曾把德里从英国占领下解放出来。这次起义是印度人民对英国在印度的统治的强烈挑战。英国殖民统治者用极端残暴的手段才把起义镇压下去。
