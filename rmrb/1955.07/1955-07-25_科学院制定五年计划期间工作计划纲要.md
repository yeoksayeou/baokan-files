Title: Academy of Sciences Formulates Work Plan Outline for the Five-Year Plan Period
Author:
Date: 1955-07-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: The Chinese Academy of Sciences, based on the fundamental tasks of the state's first Five-Year Plan, has formulated a work plan outline for the first Five-Year Plan period.

During this period, the Chinese Academy of Sciences will increase its research institutions by twenty-three and its scientific research personnel by three thousand four hundred. The establishment of research institutions will fully consider the greatest needs of national construction and scientific development, and will change the unreasonable situation where the vast majority of research institutions are distributed in a few large coastal cities. Scientific research institutions will be gradually established in the interior, and scientific research institutions will be systematically brought closer to national production and construction bases, so that scientific and cultural undertakings in the interior can be better developed. New scientific research institutions will first be established in the Northwest and Central South regions.

In addition to general work, the Chinese Academy of Sciences has also formulated eleven key tasks during the first Five-Year Plan period: research on the peaceful use of atomic energy; research in coordination with the construction of new steel bases; petroleum research; earthquake research; investigation and research in coordination with river basin planning and development; investigation and research on tropical plant resources in South China; research on China's natural regionalization and economic regionalization; research on antibiotics; research on various basic theoretical issues in my country's transitional period of national construction; research on my country's modern and contemporary history and modern and contemporary intellectual history; and research on polymer compounds. These key tasks are major scientific issues in national construction, many of which are comprehensive research and comprehensive investigation work. The Chinese Academy of Sciences will soon establish a Comprehensive Investigation Work Committee to be specifically responsible for handling comprehensive major investigation work.

In addition, the Chinese Academy of Sciences will also establish a Scientific Cadre Training Department to strengthen cadre training. Starting this year, a graduate student system will be established, and the work of enrolling graduate students and preparing to send researchers to study in the Soviet Union and the People's Democratic countries will begin soon.

### 科学院制定五年计划期间工作计划纲要

1955-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    科学院制定五年计划期间工作计划纲要
    新华社二十四日讯　中国科学院根据国家第一个五年计划的基本任务，已经制定出第一个五年计划期间的工作计划纲要。
    在这期间，中国科学院的研究机构将增加二十三所，科学研究人员将增加三千四百人。研究机构的设置，将充分考虑到国家建设事业和科学发展的最大需要，并改变过去绝大部分研究机构分布在沿海少数大城市的不合理情况，逐渐在内地建立科学研究工作的基础，有计划地使科学研究机构接近国家生产建设基地，使内地科学文化事业得到更好的发展。首先将在西北和中南地区建立新的科学研究机构。
    中国科学院在第一个五年计划期间，除了一般工作外，还拟定了十一项重点工作：原子能和平利用的研究；配合新钢铁基地建设的研究；石油的研究；地震的研究；配合流域规划与开发的调查研究；华南热带植物资源的调查研究；中国自然区划和经济区划的研究；抗生素的研究；我国过渡时期国家建设中的各种基本理论问题的研究；我国近代、现代史和近代、现代思想史的研究以及高聚化合物的研究。这些重点工作都是国家建设中的重大科学问题，其中很多是综合性的研究和综合性的考察工作。中国科学院不久还要设置一个综合考察工作委员会，专门负责处理综合性的重大考察工作。
    此外，中国科学院还要设置科学干部培养部，加强干部培养工作。从今年开始，将建立研究生制度，并即将开始办理招收研究生的工作和准备派遣研究人员到苏联和人民民主国家学习。
