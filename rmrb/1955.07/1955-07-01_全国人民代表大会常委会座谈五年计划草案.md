Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Symposium on Draft Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1955-07-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Following Premier Zhou Enlai's explanation to the 17th session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (NPC) regarding the draft First Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy of the People's Republic of China, which is to be submitted to the Second Session of the NPC for deliberation and approval, the Standing Committee of the NPC held group discussions on the draft Five-Year Plan from the 27th to the 30th. Members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) in Beijing also participated in the discussions.

During the group discussions, representatives from the State Planning Commission participated and provided explanations and clarifications on the content of the draft Five-Year Plan and the questions raised during the discussions.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会座谈五年计划草案

1955-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常委会座谈五年计划草案
    新华社三十日讯　在全国人民代表大会常务委员会第十七次会议上国务院周恩来总理对将要提请全国人民代表大会第二次会议审定的中华人民共和国发展国民经济的第一个五年计划草案作了说明以后，全国人民代表大会常务委员会于二十七日到三十日分组对五年计划草案进行了座谈。中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在京的常务委员也参加了座谈。
    在分组座谈中，国家计划委员会派人参加，并对五年计划草案的内容和座谈中提出的问题作了解释和说明。
