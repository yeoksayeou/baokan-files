Title: Our Army on the Fujian Front Shoots Down and Damages Three Chiang Kai-shek Planes
Author:
Date: 1955-07-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Our frontline anti-aircraft artillery units in Fujian shot down and damaged three American-made Chiang Kai-shek planes on the 15th and 16th.

　　At 17:30 on the 15th, four American-made Chiang Kai-shek F-84 aircraft intruded into our coastal areas of Fujian to harass. They were intercepted by our anti-aircraft artillery fire over the Minjiang River estuary area, and two of them were immediately damaged.

　　At 10:10 a.m. on the 16th, four American-made Chiang Kai-shek F-84 aircraft intruded into the airspace over Xiamen to harass. Our anti-aircraft artillery units fired fiercely, hitting one of them. This American-made Chiang Kai-shek plane immediately exploded in the air and crashed in a certain area of ​​Wuyu Island. The body of Chiang Kai-shek's plane and the pilot on board were completely destroyed.

### 福建前线我军击落击伤蒋贼飞机三架

1955-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线我军击落击伤蒋贼飞机三架
    新华社十六日讯　我福建前线高射炮部队在十五、十六两日内击落击伤美制蒋贼飞机三架。
    十五日十七时三十分，美制蒋贼F—八四型飞机四架，侵入我福建沿海地区骚扰，它们在闽江口地区上空，遭我高射炮火拦击，其中二架当即被击伤。
    十六日上午十时十分，美制蒋贼F—八四型飞机四架，侵入我厦门上空骚扰，我高射炮部队猛烈射击，击中其中一架。这架美制蒋贼飞机当即在空中爆炸，坠落在浯屿岛某地。蒋贼飞机机身和机上驾驶员都被毁粉碎。
