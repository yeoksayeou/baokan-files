Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Visits Wuhan and Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1955-07-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency report:

    The Indian Cultural Delegation, headed by Mr. Chand, left Chongqing on a special ship at 8:30 AM on the 18th and arrived in Wuhan at 8:00 AM on the 21st. The Indian Ambassador to China, Mr. R.K. Nehru, also arrived with the delegation.
    Over 500 people from all walks of life in Wuhan arrived early in the morning at the No. 4 Steamboat Wharf in Hankou to welcome the distinguished guests. Among those welcoming were Wuhan Mayor Song Kanfu, Deputy Mayor Tang Wuyuan, Director of the Hubei Provincial Cultural Bureau Fang Zhuangyou, Director of the Wuhan Municipal Cultural Bureau Huang Juyi, and heads of provincial and municipal cultural and artistic groups.
    Mr. Chand, the head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, his wife, and members of the delegation, visited the famous Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge project on the afternoon of the 21st. The Indian Ambassador to China, Mr. R.K. Nehru, also joined the visit.
    In the evening, Mr. Chand and all members of the delegation attended a banquet hosted by Wuhan Mayor Song Kanfu.
    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, July 23rd: The Indian Cultural Delegation performed at the Wuhan "People's Auditorium" on the evening of the 22nd, with over 6,000 people in attendance.
    The various performances by the Indian artists were warmly welcomed by the audience, and after each performance, the performers repeatedly took bows amidst thunderous applause.
    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, July 23rd: The Indian Cultural Delegation, headed by Mr. Chand, left Wuhan by special plane on the afternoon of the 23rd.
    Before leaving Wuhan, the Indian Cultural Delegation visited the Gude Temple, Baotong Temple, and the ancient Hongshan Pagoda.
    After completing the visit to the Hongshan Pagoda, the guests, accompanied by Ba Nangang, Deputy Director of the Wuhan Municipal Cultural Bureau, and others, traveled by car to Nanhu Airport. Wuhan Mayor Song Kanfu, Deputy Mayor Tang Wuyuan, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Hubei Provincial People's Committee Yao Guang, Director of the Wuhan Municipal Cultural Bureau Huang Juyi, representatives from all walks of life, and heads of cultural and artistic groups all went to the airport to see them off.
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, July 23rd: At 5:40 PM on the 23rd, the Indian Cultural Delegation arrived in Nanjing.
    Mr. Chand and his wife folded their hands to greet the welcoming people as they stepped off the plane. They accepted flowers from the children of Nanjing and shook hands warmly with Nanjing Mayor Peng Chong and others who came to welcome them.
    At 7:30 PM, Mr. Chand and his wife, the delegation's secretary, Mr. G. Singh, and the delegation's stage supervisor, Dr. R. Malathe, paid a visit to Liu Shunyuan, Deputy Governor of Jiangsu Province, and Peng Chong, Mayor of Nanjing, at the Jiangsu Provincial People's Committee.

### 印度文化代表团访问武汉、南京

1955-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团访问武汉、南京
    据新华社讯　以钱达团长为首的印度文化代表团，在十八日上午八时半乘专轮离开重庆，二十一日上午八时到达武汉。和代表团一起到达的还有印度驻我国大使赖嘉文。
    武汉各界人民五百多人这天一早就到汉口第四轮船码头迎候贵宾。欢迎的人中有武汉市市长宋侃夫，副市长唐午园，湖北省文化局局长方壮猷，武汉市文化局局长黄居易，以及省、市文化艺术团体的负责人等。
    印度文化代表团团长钱达和他的夫人以及代表团人员，二十一日下午参观了著名的武汉长江大桥工程。同去参观的还有印度驻我国大使赖嘉文。
    晚上，钱达团长和全体团员出席了武汉市市长宋侃夫的宴会。
    据新华社武汉二十三日电　印度文化代表团二十二日晚间在武汉“人民会场”演出，有六千多人出席观看。
    印度艺术家们演出的各种节目都博得全场观众的热烈欢迎，每个节目演完后，表演者都在观众的春雷般的掌声中一再谢幕。
    据新华社武汉二十三日电　以钱达为首的印度文化代表团二十三日下午乘专机离开武汉。
    印度文化代表团在离开武汉的前一天和当天，曾先后参观古德寺、宝通寺和古代建筑物——洪山宝塔。
    结束了洪山宝塔的参观后，客人们由武汉市文化局副局长巴南冈等陪同乘车赴南湖机场。武汉市长宋侃夫、副市长唐午园，湖北省人民委员会外事处处长姚广，武汉市文化局局长黄居易，各界代表和文艺团体的负责人等都到机场欢送。
    据新华社南京二十三日电　二十三日下午五时四十分，印度文化代表团到了南京。
    钱达团长和夫人走出飞机时，合掌向欢迎的人们致意。他们接受了南京市少年儿童的献花，并同前来欢迎的南京市市长彭冲等人亲切握手。
    七时半，钱达团长和夫人，代表团秘书古·辛格先生、代表团舞台监督拉·马拉太博士等到江苏省人民委员会拜会江苏省副省长刘顺元，南京市市长彭冲。
