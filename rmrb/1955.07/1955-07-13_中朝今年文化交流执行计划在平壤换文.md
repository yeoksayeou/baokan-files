Title: Sino-Korean Cultural Exchange Implementation Plan for This Year Exchanged in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1955-07-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 12th. The Sino-Korean cultural exchange implementation plan for 1955, formulated according to the Sino-Korean Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement, was exchanged in Pyongyang on June 22nd. Representing their respective governments in the exchange were Pan Zili, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Li Dongjian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 中朝今年文化交流执行计划在平壤换文

1955-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中朝今年文化交流执行计划在平壤换文
    新华社平壤十二日电　根据中朝经济及文化合作协定制定的中朝一九五五年文化交流执行计划已于六月二十二日在平壤换文。代表双方政府换文的是中华人民共和国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国特命全权大使潘自力和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省副相李东建。
