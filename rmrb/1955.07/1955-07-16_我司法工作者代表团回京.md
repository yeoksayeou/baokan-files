Title: Our Delegation of Judicial Workers Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-07-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th July: The Chinese judicial workers delegation, led by Minister of Justice Shi Liang, which visited the Soviet Union, returned to Beijing on the 10th.

The Chinese judicial workers delegation was composed of workers from the Supreme People's Court, the Supreme People's Procuratorate, the Ministry of Justice, and legal education departments. The delegation departed for the Soviet Union on April 10th. During their visit to the Soviet Union, they visited Moscow, the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic, the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic, and Leningrad. They focused on studying the work experience of Soviet judicial administration, adjudication, procuratorial organs at all levels, and legal education departments. In the process of learning from the advanced experience of Soviet judicial work, the delegation received warm receptions and selfless assistance from Soviet Minister of Justice Gorshenin, President of the Soviet Supreme Court Volin, Soviet Procurator General Rudenko, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Union of Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Denisov, and local judicial organs and jurists, which enabled the delegation to gain a great deal in a short period of study.

While in the Soviet Union, the delegation also visited many Soviet industrial enterprises, collective farms, state farms, and some places of scenic and historical interest.

### 我司法工作者代表团回京

1955-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我司法工作者代表团回京
    新华社十五日讯到　苏联访问的由司法部长史良率领的中国司法工作者代表团，已在十日返抵北京。
    中国司法工作者代表团是由最高人民法院、最高人民检察院、司法部和法律教育部门的工作者组成的，代表团于四月十日赴苏联访问。在访苏期间，曾先后在莫斯科、乌兹别克共和国、乌克兰共和国和列宁格勒等地进行访问。他们着重学习了苏联各级司法行政、审判、检察机关和法律教育部门的工作经验。代表团在学习苏联司法工作先进经验的过程中，受到了苏联司法部长戈尔谢宁、苏联最高法院院长沃林、苏联最高检察长鲁金科、苏联对外文化协会理事会主席杰尼索夫和各地司法机关及法学家们的热忱接待和无私的帮助，因而使代表团在短时间的学习中得到了很大的收获。
    代表团在苏联还参观了苏联许多工业企业、集体农庄、国营农场和一些名胜古迹。
