Planting double-cropping rice can increase rice production. The growth period of early and late rice in the field is only about 210 days. Staff members of the Hejialong First Agricultural Production Cooperative in Liling County, Hunan Province, are introducing the experience of increasing production of double-cropping rice to the visiting group organized by the Ministry of Agriculture.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    种植双季稻可以提高水稻产量，早、晚两季稻在田间的生育期仅约二百一十天。湖南省醴陵县何家垅第一农业生产合作社的工作人员，正向农业部组织的参观团介绍双季稻的增产经验。
