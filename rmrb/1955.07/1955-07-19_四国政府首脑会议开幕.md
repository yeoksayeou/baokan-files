Title: Four-Power Summit Opens

Author:

Date: 1955-07-19
Page: 1

    Four-Power Summit Opens
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 18th: TASS, Geneva, July 18th: The Four-Power Summit opened at 10:00 AM Central European Time today in the Assembly Hall of the Palace of Nations.
    The head of the Soviet delegation, N.A. Bulganin, and delegation members N.S. Khrushchev, V.M. Molotov, G.K. Zhukov, and A.A. Gromyko attended the meeting.
    Representing the United States at the meeting were D. Eisenhower, J. Dulles, MacArthur, C. Bohlen, and others.
    The British delegation attending the meeting included A. Eden, H. Macmillan, and Kirkpatrick.
    Representing France at the meeting were E. Faure, A. Pinay, L. Joxe, and others.
    Reporters were allowed to stay in the venue for a short time before the meeting began.
    U.S. President Eisenhower was the first to speak at the first meeting.

### 四国政府首脑会议开幕

1955-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    四国政府首脑会议开幕
    新华社十八日讯　塔斯社日内瓦十八日讯：四国政府首脑会议于今天中欧时间上午十时在国联大厦会议厅开幕。
    苏联代表团团长尼·亚·布尔加宁和代表团团员尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、维·米·莫洛托夫、格·康·朱可夫和安·安·葛罗米柯出席了会议。
    代表美国出席会议的是德·艾森豪威尔、约·杜勒斯、小麦克阿瑟、查·波伦等。
    出席会议的英国代表团中有安·艾登、哈·麦克米伦和柯克帕特里克。
    代表法国出席会议的是埃·富尔、安·比内、路·约克斯等。
    会议开始前曾允许记者们在会场停留一个短时间。
    在第一次会议上首先发言的是美国总统艾森豪威尔。
