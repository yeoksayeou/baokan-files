Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-07-10
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    China's first grain combine harvester has been successfully trial-produced by the Beijing Agricultural Machinery Factory. After field tests, the combine harvester has proven to be highly effective. This is the first time the combine harvester is being used to harvest wheat in the wheat field.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国第一台谷物联合收割机，已经由北京农业机械厂试制成功。联合收割机经过田间试验，证明效能良好。这是联合收割机在麦田里初次试割麦子的情形。
