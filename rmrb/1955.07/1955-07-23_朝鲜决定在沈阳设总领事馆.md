Title: Korea Decides to Establish Consulate General in Shenyang
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-23
Page: 1
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd – The government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, with the consent of our government, has decided to establish a Consulate General in Shenyang and a Consulate in Tumen.

### 朝鲜决定在沈阳设总领事馆

1955-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜决定在沈阳设总领事馆
    新华社二十二日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府经征得我国政府同意后，已决定在沈阳市设立总领事馆，在图们市设立领事馆。
