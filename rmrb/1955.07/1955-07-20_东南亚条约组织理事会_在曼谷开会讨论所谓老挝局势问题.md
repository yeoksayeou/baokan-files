Title: Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Council Meets in Bangkok to Discuss So-Called Laotian Situation

Author: 
Date: 1955-07-20
Page: 4

　　Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Council
　　Meets in Bangkok to Discuss So-Called Laotian Situation
　　Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, July 19th: According to a Reuters dispatch from Bangkok, the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Council held a meeting in Bangkok on the morning of the 18th to discuss the so-called Laotian situation.

### 东南亚条约组织理事会  在曼谷开会讨论所谓老挝局势问题

1955-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    东南亚条约组织理事会
    在曼谷开会讨论所谓老挝局势问题
    新华社十九日讯　据路透社曼谷消息，东南亚条约组织理事会十八日上午在曼谷举行会议，讨论所谓老挝的局势问题。
