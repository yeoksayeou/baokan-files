Title: President Ho Chi Minh and the Vietnamese Delegation Arrive in Beijing En Route Home

Author: 
Date: 1955-07-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th — The government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by President Ho Chi Minh, arrived in Beijing by special plane on the afternoon of the 20th while returning home after visiting the Soviet Union.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, and Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee Deng Xiaoping, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Public Security Luo Ruiqing, Director of the State Construction Commission Bo Yibo, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Railways Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, Minister of Higher Education Yang Xiufeng, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Finance Jin Ming, Vice Minister of Education Dong Chuncai, Vice Minister of Health Cui Yitian, responsible staff members of various departments of the CPC Central Committee Yang Shangkun and Li Chuli, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yu Xinqing, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Dong Yueqian, Director of the Second Asian Department Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Protocol Department Ke Hua, and Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Luo Guibo.
    Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China Hoang Van Hoan and embassy staff, members of the Vietnamese Trade Delegation and the Vietnamese Peace Delegation also welcomed them at the airport.
    Also welcoming them at the airport were Romanian Ambassador Guo Zuowen, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Luo Maijin, and Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Maiman.

### 胡志明主席和越南代表团回国途中到北京

1955-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席和越南代表团回国途中到北京
    新华社二十日讯　由胡志明主席率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团，在访问苏联以后归国途中在二十日下午乘专机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈云、彭德怀、陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，中共中央政治局委员、中共中央秘书长邓小平，国务院秘书长习仲勋，公安部部长罗瑞卿，国家建设委员会主任薄一波，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，铁道部部长滕代远，农业部部长廖鲁言，高等教育部部长杨秀峰，外交部副部长张闻天、姬鹏飞，财政部副部长金明，教育部副部长董纯才，卫生部副部长崔义田，中共中央各部门负责工作人员杨尚昆、李初梨，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长余心清，外交部办公厅主任董越千、第二亚洲司司长章文晋、礼宾司司长柯华，以及中国驻越南大使罗贵波。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢和使馆人员，越南贸易代表团和越南和平代表团人员都到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有罗马尼亚大使郭佐文，蒙古大使奥其尔巴特，苏联临时代办罗迈进，波兰临时代办马伊曼。
