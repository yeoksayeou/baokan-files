Title: Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo Welcomes the Four-Power Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-07-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 19th – According to Antara News Agency, Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo issued a statement on the 18th, welcoming the Four-Power Heads of Government Conference held in Geneva.

Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo said that the Four-Power Heads of Government Conference is a manifestation of the spirit of reconciliation, tolerance, and consultation.

He said that it was this spirit that brought together the government leaders of Asian and African countries in Bandung, proving that it can open new avenues for solving various problems. He said that thanks to this spirit, the Asian-African Conference reached the ten principles for promoting world peace and cooperation.

Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo concluded, "I pray that the same spirit will also emerge at the summit meeting in Geneva and bring results conducive to world peace and cooperation."

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理表示欢迎四国会议

1955-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理表示欢迎四国会议
    新华社雅加达十九日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约在十八日发表谈话，表示欢迎在日内瓦举行的四国政府首脑会议。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理说，四国政府首脑会议是和解、容忍和协商的精神的表现。
    他说，使得亚非国家的政府领袖们在万隆聚集一堂的就是这种精神，它证明能够为解决各种问题打开新的道路。他说，由于这种精神，亚非会议才达成了促进世界和平和合作的十项原则。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理最后说：“我祈祷同样的精神也将在日内瓦的最高级会议上出现，并带来有利于世界和平和合作的结果。”
