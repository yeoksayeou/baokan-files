Title: Lao Fighting Forces Delegation Demands Resumption of Political Conference; Lao Sides' Military Conference Discusses Ceasefire in Sam Neua Province

Author:
Date: 1955-07-07
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 6th -- Vietnam News Agency reports: According to news from Laos, the political delegation of the Lao Fighting Forces, in a letter simultaneously sent to the Royal Lao Government and the International Commission for Laos on July 1st, demanded the resumption of the interrupted political conference. The delegation of the Lao Fighting Forces reiterated its commitment to do everything possible to resume the political conference with the Royal Government side, while pointing out the absurdity of the conditions for resuming the conference put forward by Royal Government Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith.

　　The delegation of the Lao Fighting Forces emphasized that the Royal Lao Government must cease all military offensives and encroachments on the assembly areas of the Lao Fighting Forces. Only in this way can a favorable atmosphere be created for the resumption of the political conference.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 6th -- Vietnam News Agency reports: News from Laos states that the military conference between the military delegations of the Lao Fighting Forces and the Royal Lao Government is underway. This military conference began on June 27th to discuss taking necessary measures to stop the current conflict in Sam Neua province. The news also said that both delegations have agreed to issue a joint statement on the working methods of the conference.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 5th -- According to Vietnam News Agency on the 5th: In the week up to June 27th, the Royal Lao Army in the Muong Phuan area of Sam Neua province launched several offensives against the areas defended by the Lao Fighting Forces. On June 26th, a company of the Royal Army opened fire on the Lao Fighting Forces.

　　News said that the Royal Army in the areas of Na Sang and Na Sala has expanded their activities to neighboring areas. In order to protect the lives and property of the residents and for self-defense, the Lao Fighting Forces were forced to retaliate. As a result, the Royal Army had to retreat.

### 寮国战斗部队代表团要求恢复政治会议　老挝双方军事会议讨论停止在桑怒省的冲突问题

1955-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　寮国战斗部队代表团要求恢复政治会议
　　老挝双方军事会议讨论停止在桑怒省的冲突问题
    新华社河内六日电　越南通讯社讯：据来自老挝的消息说，寮国战斗部队政治代表团在七月一日同时发给老挝王国政府和老挝国际委员会的一封信中，要求恢复中断了的政治会议。寮国战斗部队代表团重申它将尽一切可能来恢复同王国政府方面的政治会议，同时指出了王国政府首相克特所提出的恢复会议的条件的荒谬性。
    寮国战斗部队代表团强调，老挝王国政府必须停止对寮国战斗部队集结区的一切军事进攻和侵犯，只有这样，才可能给恢复政治会议创建有利的气氛。
    新华社河内六日电　越南通讯社报道：从老挝来的消息说，寮国战斗部队和老挝王国政府双方军事代表团之间的军事会议正在进行中。这次军事会议在六月二十七日开始，讨论采取必要措施来停止目前在桑怒省的冲突的问题。消息又说，双方代表团已同意发表一个关于会议的工作方法的联合声明。
    新华社河内五日讯　据越南通讯社五日讯：在到六月二十七日为止的一个星期中，桑怒省芒奔地区的老挝王国军队向寮国战斗部队驻防的地区发动了数次进攻。六月二十六日，有一连王国军队曾向寮国战斗部队开火。
    消息说，那桑和那萨拉地区的王国军队已经把他们的活动扩大到邻近地区。寮国战斗部队为了保护居民的生命财产和自卫，被迫进行还击。结果，王国军队不得不撤退。
