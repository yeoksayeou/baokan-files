Title: Indian Communist Party General Secretary Ghosh Discusses the Indian Communist Party's Opinions on India's Second Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1955-07-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 1st. Indian Communist Party General Secretary Ghosh stated at a press conference on June 29th that the Indian Communist Party welcomes the proposal that the main focus of India's Second Five-Year Plan should be placed on heavy and basic industries.

　　He said that these industries should be primarily state-owned. The need for consumer goods should be met through better utilization of existing industries and the development of household and small-scale industries.

　　Ghosh said, "The Indian Communist Party believes that it is urgently needed to develop heavy and basic industries in India at the fastest possible speed in order to reduce our dependence on foreign countries." This will help create more jobs.

　　He said that the Indian Communist Party will demand the nationalization of the coal mining industry, large farms, and the manganese mining industry. The state should monopolize foreign trade, especially the trade in jute and leather. The Indian Communist Party does not demand the immediate nationalization of banks and insurance companies but advocates taking over British enterprises.

　　Ghosh said, "We will generally support anything that leads to strengthening the national economy and improving the living conditions of the masses."

### 印度共产党总书记高士　谈印共对印度第二个五年计划的意见

1955-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度共产党总书记高士
　　谈印共对印度第二个五年计划的意见
    新华社新德里一日电　印度共产党总书记高士六月二十九日在记者招待会上说，印度共产党欢迎印度第二个五年计划中主要的重点应该放在重工业和基本工业上面的主张。
    他说，这些工业应该主要是国家所有的。对消费品的需要应该通过更好地利用现有工业及发展家庭工业和小型工业的办法来加以满足。
    高士说，“印度共产党认为，以最快的速度在印度发展重工业和基本工业，以减少我们对外国的依赖，这是迫切需要的。”这将有助于使更多的人就业。
    他说，印度共产党将要求把煤矿工业、大农场和锰矿工业国有化。国家应该垄断对外贸易，特别是黄麻和皮革的贸易。印度共产党并不要求立刻把银行和保险公司国有化，但是主张接收英国的企业。
    高士说，“我们将一般地支持任何导向加强国民经济和改善群众生活条件的东西。”
