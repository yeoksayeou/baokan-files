Title: State Council Plenary Session Approves Draft Amendment to China's Military Service Law and Approves Decision on Construction of Zhanjiang Port
Author:
Date: 1955-07-05
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, July 4th -- The 14th Plenary Session of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held on the 4th.

Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai explained the "Military Service Law of the People's Republic of China (Draft Amendment)" at the meeting. This draft amendment was revised based on the "Resolution on the Draft of the Military Service Law of the People's Republic of China" by the Fifth Session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and compiled the opinions of people's committees at all levels and the broad masses of the people. The meeting approved the "Military Service Law of the People's Republic of China (Draft Amendment)" and decided to submit it to the Second Session of the National People's Congress for deliberation.

After discussing the report on the construction of Zhanjiang Port by the Ministry of Communications, the meeting approved the decision on the construction of Zhanjiang Port.

### 国务院全体会议通过我国兵役法修正草案　并通过关于建设湛江港的决定

1955-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　国务院全体会议通过我国兵役法修正草案
　　并通过关于建设湛江港的决定
    新华社四日讯　中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第十四次会议在四日举行。
    副总理兼国防部长彭德怀在会上就“中华人民共和国兵役法（修正草案）作了说明。这个修正草案是根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五次会议关于中华人民共和国兵役法草案的决议，汇集了地方各级人民委员会和广大人民的意见修改而成的。会议通过了“中华人民共和国兵役法（修正草案）”，决定提交全国人民代表大会第二次会议审议。
    会议在讨论了交通部关于建设湛江港的报告后，通过了关于建设湛江港的决定。
