Title: People in Various Parts of the Soviet Union Warmly Celebrate Soviet Navy Day
Author: 
Date: 1955-07-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: People in various parts of the Soviet Union warmly celebrated Soviet Navy Day on July 24th.

On the eve of Navy Day (July 23rd), the Soviet Ministry of Defense, together with the Communist Party organizations, Soviets, and mass organizations of Moscow City and Moscow Oblast, held a celebration meeting at the Central Soviet Army Theater in Moscow. The celebration was presided over by Soviet Admiral Gorshkov. In his opening remarks, he stated that Soviet naval personnel should do everything possible to defend the interests of the socialist motherland. Subsequently, Prokhorov, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Nosenko, Minister of the Soviet Shipbuilding Industry, and others spoke at the meeting. A grand concert was held after the meeting.

On the 24th, a grand water sports event was held at the Khimki Reservoir near Moscow. Hundreds of thousands of spectators attended. A naval review was held at the anchorage. Then, speedboats flying the Soviet flag, the flags of the various Soviet republics, and naval flags sped across the calm water. The audience warmly welcomed the sailing program of single-masted sailboats. The sports event also included yacht racing, sailing demonstrations, water obstacle relay races, rowing and single scull races, and swimming competitions.

Other cities, troops, and rural areas in the Soviet Union also held holiday celebrations, and organized lectures and reports on the glorious history of the Soviet Navy. Cities such as Leningrad, Odessa, Vladivostok, Riga, and Tallinn also held water sports events and naval reviews on the 24th. In the famous hero city of Sevastopol, Bulgarian and Romanian naval fighters, who were on a friendly visit here, celebrated Navy Day with the Soviet people on the 24th.

### 苏联各地人民热烈庆祝苏联海军节

1955-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联各地人民热烈庆祝苏联海军节
    据新华社讯　苏联各地热烈庆祝七月二十四日的苏联海军节。
    在海军节前夕（二十三日）苏联国防部会同莫斯科市和莫斯科州的共产党组织、苏维埃和群众团体在莫斯科中央苏军剧院举行了庆祝会。庆祝会由苏联海军上将戈尔什科夫主持。他在致开幕词时表示，苏联海军军人要尽一切努力来保卫社会主义祖国的利益。接着，苏联共产党莫斯科市委员会书记普罗豪罗夫、苏联造船工业部部长诺森科等在会上讲了话。会后还举行了盛大的音乐会。
    二十四日，在莫斯科附近的希姆基水库举行了盛大的水上运动会。到会观众有数十万人。在停泊场举行了军舰检阅。接着，悬挂着苏联国旗、各加盟共和国国旗和海军军旗的快艇在平静的水面上疾驰而过。观众们对单桅帆船航行的节目表示热烈欢迎。运动会上还举行了游艇圆形航行竞赛、航行表演、水上障碍接力赛、划艇和单人短艇的划行竞赛、游泳比赛。
    苏联其他城市、部队和农村也都举行了节日庆祝会，举办了关于苏联海军的光荣历史的讲座和报告会。列宁格勒、敖德萨、海参崴、里加、塔林等城市在二十四日也举行了水上运动会和军舰检阅。在著名的英雄城市塞瓦斯托波尔，正在这里作友好访问的保加利亚和罗马尼亚的海军战士二十四日同苏联人民一起庆祝海军节。
