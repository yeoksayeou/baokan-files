Title: President Ho Chi Minh to Deliver Radio Address Today
Author:
Date: 1955-07-07
Page: 1
    
    President Ho Chi Minh to Deliver Radio Address Today

    (Dispatch from this newspaper) At the invitation of the Central People's Broadcasting Station, President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will deliver a radio address to Chinese listeners during the joint broadcasting program of local people's radio stations from 20:30 to 21:00 on the 7th.

### 胡志明主席今天发表广播谈话

1955-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席今天发表广播谈话
    本报讯　越南民主共和国主席胡志明，应中央人民广播电台邀请，在七日二十点三十分到二十一点各地人民广播电台联播节目时间，向中国听众广播谈话。
