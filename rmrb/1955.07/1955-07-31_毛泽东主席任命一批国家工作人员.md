Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Appoints a Group of National Workers
Author:
Date: 1955-07-31
Page: 2

Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, in accordance with the decision of the Second Session of the First National People's Congress, appointed Chen Yu as Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry, Liu Lanbo as Minister of the Ministry of Electric Power Industry, Li Juikui as Minister of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, and Yang Yichen as Minister of the Ministry of Agricultural Products Procurement on July 30.

Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China also appointed Zhang Jingwu as Director of the Chairman's Office and Tian Jiaying as Deputy Director.

### 毛泽东主席任命一批国家工作人员

1955-07-31
第2版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席任命一批国家工作人员
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议的决定，在七月三十日任命陈郁为煤炭工业部部长，刘澜波为电力工业部部长，李聚奎为石油工业部部长，杨一辰为农产品采购部部长。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东并任命张经武为主席办公厅主任，田家英为副主任。
