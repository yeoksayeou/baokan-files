Title: Ho Chi Minh and the Vietnamese Delegation Arrive in Novosibirsk
Author:
Date: 1955-07-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Novosibirsk, July 10th: Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam he led, arrived in Novosibirsk on the 10th.

Accompanying the delegation were Begov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Kochetkov, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, and Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union.

Welcoming them at the airport were Shevnin, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Novosibirsk City Soviet; Ulibin, Secretary of the Novosibirsk Oblast Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union; Bakukin, Secretary of the Novosibirsk City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union; and representatives from all walks of life in Novosibirsk.

Shevnin welcomed the distinguished guests on behalf of the city's working people.

President Ho Chi Minh delivered a speech in reply, saying:

Dear Comrades! We are very happy to visit your city, the beautiful capital of Soviet Siberia. Allow me to convey the warmest greetings of the Vietnamese people to you and to thank you for your warm welcome.

Long live the friendship between the Vietnamese and Soviet peoples!

Long live world peace!

President Ho Chi Minh's speech was frequently interrupted by stormy applause.

Elementary school students from Novosibirsk presented flowers to President Ho Chi Minh and the delegation members.

The distinguished guests then left the airport and entered the city.

### 胡志明主席和越南代表团到达新西伯利亚

1955-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席和越南代表团到达新西伯利亚
    新华社十日讯　据塔斯社新西伯利亚讯：越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团在十日到达新西伯利亚。
    陪同代表团的有苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫、苏联外交部礼宾司副司长科切特科夫以及越南民主共和国驻苏大使阮良朋。
    前往机场欢迎的有新西伯利亚市苏维埃执行委员会主席谢夫宁、苏联共产党新西伯利亚州委员会书记乌利宾、苏联共产党新西伯利亚市委员会书记巴库金以及新西伯利亚市各界人民的代表。
    谢夫宁代表全市劳动人民欢迎贵宾。
    胡志明主席致答词说：
    亲爱的同志们！我们访问你们的城市——苏维埃西伯利亚的优美首府，感到非常高兴。让我向你们转达越南人民的最热烈的问候，并且感谢你们给予我们的热诚欢迎。
    越苏两国人民友好万岁！
    世界和平万岁！
    胡志明主席的讲话频频为暴风雨般的掌声所打断。
    新西伯利亚的小学生向胡志明主席和代表团人员献花。
    贵宾们随即离机场入城。
