Our Great Motherland

The state-owned Beijing No. 2 Machine Tool Plant launched a labor emulation drive and overfulfilled the June production target ahead of schedule. Ma Shumin, a milling machine operator and member of the Communist Youth League at this plant, has overfulfilled her production quota for six consecutive months and won the mobile red flag.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营北京第二机床厂展开了劳动竞赛，提前完成六月份的生产任务。这个厂的铣工、青年团员马淑敏已连续六个月超额完成生产任务，获得了流动红旗。
