Title: Czechoslovakia and Romania Establish Organizations for the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-07-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 9th: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency, the government of the Czechoslovak Republic has decided to establish a Nuclear Physics Institute under the guidance of the Government Committee for Research and Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy, in order to rapidly develop research and peaceful use of atomic energy with the assistance of the Soviet Union.

    The main tasks of this institute are: to carry out basic and applied research in nuclear physics, radiochemistry, and nuclear energy, especially to apply the results of the research in power economy, science, industry, agriculture, and medicine; to produce radioactive isotopes and radioactive mixtures widely used in science and national economy; and to train postgraduates in relevant fields.

Xinhua News Agency, July 10th: According to a report by the Romanian News Agency, Bucharest, July 9th: The Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, in order to promote research in nuclear physics and the peaceful use of atomic energy, has established the Nuclear Energy Committee of the Council of Ministers.

    The Nuclear Energy Committee will provide a material foundation for scientific research in nuclear physics and the application of nuclear physics in science and technology, will establish institutions to train necessary professional cadres in this field, and will be responsible for the construction and use of nuclear technology equipment for the peaceful use of atomic energy.

### 捷罗分别设立研究和平利用原子能的组织

1955-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    捷罗分别设立研究和平利用原子能的组织
    新华社布拉格九日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府决定成立一个在研究及和平利用原子能政府委员会指导下的核子物理学研究所，以便迅速发展在苏联援助的基础上进行的研究及和平利用原子能的工作。
    这个研究所的主要任务是：在核子物理学、放射化学和核子能学方面进行基本的和应用的研究工作，特别是把研究的结果用在动力经济以及科学、工业、农业和医学方面；生产在科学和国民经济方面广泛应用的放射性同位素和放射性混合物；训练有关方面的研究生。
    新华社十日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特九日讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议为了促进原子核物理学的研究工作和和平利用原子能，设立了部长会议核子能委员会。
    核子能委员会将为原子核物理学方面的科学研究和原子核物理学在科学技术方面的应用提供物质基础，将建立机构培养这方面的必要的专业干部，并且负责建设和使用在和平利用原子能方面的原子核技术设备。
