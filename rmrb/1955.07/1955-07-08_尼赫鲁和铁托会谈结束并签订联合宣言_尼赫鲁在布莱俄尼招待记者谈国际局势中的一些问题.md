Title: Nehru and Tito End Talks and Sign Joint Declaration; Nehru Holds Reception for Journalists in Brioni to Discuss Issues in the International Situation
Author:
Date: 1955-07-08
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, July 7th, Belgrade Dispatch: According to newspaper reports, Nehru and Tito held talks on Brioni Island on the 6th.

The signing ceremony of the joint declaration between Yugoslavia and India regarding the results of these negotiations took place at 8:15 AM on the 6th.

The new Indo-Yugoslav declaration will be simultaneously published in Belgrade and Delhi on the 8th.

According to newspaper reports, Indian Prime Minister Nehru and his party left Brioni by motorboat for Pula on the morning of the 7th, where they boarded a Yugoslav special plane to Rome. Nehru will stay in Rome for two days before heading to London.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 7th: According to a Yugoslav News Agency report, Indian Prime Minister Nehru held a press conference in Brioni on the 6th, discussing some issues in the international situation.

In answering a question about the Four Power Summit, Nehru expressed his belief that the conference would achieve good results. He also pointed out that the conference
  "should have a general discussion on the Indochina issue and the Taiwan issue because they are the most important issues with far-reaching influence."

Nehru said that the German issue should be resolved through a peace agreement among the countries concerned to unify Germany. He said that the impression he gained during his talks in Moscow was that the Soviet Union hoped for a reasonable solution to the German issue.

Nehru pointed out in his speech that the role of the United Nations would increase as a result of admitting the People's Republic of China and other countries.

Nehru also spoke about his talks with President Tito and his impressions of his visit to Yugoslavia.

### 尼赫鲁和铁托会谈结束并签订联合宣言  尼赫鲁在布莱俄尼招待记者谈国际局势中的一些问题

1955-07-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　尼赫鲁和铁托会谈结束并签订联合宣言
    尼赫鲁在布莱俄尼招待记者谈国际局势中的一些问题
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德讯：据报纸消息，六日，尼赫鲁同铁托在布莱俄尼岛举行了会谈。
    南斯拉夫和印度关于这次谈判结果的联合宣言的签字仪式，已经在六日八时十五分举行。
    新的南印宣言将在八日在贝尔格莱德和德里同时公布。
    据报纸消息，印度总理尼赫鲁一行七日早晨从布莱俄尼乘汽艇去普拉，在那里乘南斯拉夫的专机飞往罗马。尼赫鲁将在罗马逗留两天，然后前往伦敦。
    新华社布拉格七日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社消息：印度总理尼赫鲁六日在布莱俄尼举行记者招待会，谈到国际局势中的一些问题。
    尼赫鲁在回答关于四大国首脑会议问题时，表示相信会议将获得良好结果。他同时指出：会议
  “应该对印度支那问题和台湾问题作一般的讨论，因为它们是最重大的具有深远影响的问题。”
    尼赫鲁说，德国问题应该通过有关国家的和平协议来统一德国的办法加以解决。他说，他在莫斯科进行会谈期间获得的印象是：苏联是希望德国问题得到合理解决的。
    尼赫鲁在谈话中指出，联合国的作用将由于接纳中华人民共和国和其他国家而增加。
    尼赫鲁还谈到他同铁托总统的会谈和他访问南斯拉夫的观感。
