Title: Japanese Communist Party to Hold Sixth National Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 23rd: Masao Kasuga, Chairman of the Central Guidance Department of the Japanese Communist Party, announced at a press conference on the 22nd that the Japanese Communist Party will hold its Sixth National Congress in Tokyo from July 27th to 29th. The meeting will analyze the international situation and discuss the party's policies. The last national congress was held in October 1951.

### 日本共产党将举行第六次全国代表会议

1955-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    日本共产党将举行第六次全国代表会议
    新华社二十三日讯　东京消息：日本共产党中央指导部主席春日正一二十二日在记者招待会上宣布，日本共产党将从七月二十七日至二十九日在东京举行第六次全国代表会议。会议将分析国际形势，并讨论党的政策。上一次全国代表会议是在一九五一年十月召开的。
