Title: Oyama Ikuo and Others Leave Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-07-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Ikuo Oyama, Chairman of the Japan Peace Supporters Committee, who came to China at the invitation of the China Peace Committee, along with his wife, Ryuko Oyama, and secretary, Michio Iwamura, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 30th via Guangzhou to return home.

Professor Golan, head of the Australian peace delegation, also departed Beijing on the same plane.

### 大山郁夫等离京返国

1955-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    大山郁夫等离京返国
    据新华社讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的日本拥护和平委员会主席大山郁夫同夫人大山柳子和秘书岩村三千夫在三十日晨乘飞机离京经广州返国。
    同机离京的还有澳大利亚和平代表团团长戈兰教授。
