Title: The Second Session of the National People's Congress Opens; Meeting Adopts Main Agenda and Begins Hearing Report on the First Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1955-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 5th. The Second Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China opened at 3:00 PM on the afternoon of July 5th at Huairentang Hall in Zhongnanhai, Beijing.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China Soong Ching Ling, Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Luo Ronghuan, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Saifudin, Vice Premiers of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, and Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng attended the opening ceremony. When they ascended the rostrum, the entire audience stood up, and prolonged applause resounded through the hall.

President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Ho Chi Minh and the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam he led were invited to attend today's opening ceremony. All the representatives welcomed the distinguished guests with warm applause.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi declared the meeting open. The band played the national anthem of the People's Republic of China. Subsequently, the meeting unanimously elected a presidium of the session consisting of seventy-nine members and a secretary-general.

The meeting adopted the main agenda of this session as follows:

	1. Decide on the first Five-Year Plan for the development of the national economy of the People's Republic of China.

	2. Examine and approve the 1954 national final accounts and the 1955 national budget.

	3. Enact the Military Service Law of the People's Republic of China.

	4. Adopt the resolution on the plan for governing the Yellow River.

	5. Adopt the work report of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

	6. Hold by-elections for members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

After the opening ceremony, the meeting immediately entered the first item on the agenda, with Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Planning Commission Li Fuchun, representing the State Council, beginning to deliver the "Report on the First Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy."

Today's meeting adjourned at 7:35 PM. At the meeting on the 6th, Li Fuchun will continue to deliver the "Report on the First Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy."

As of the 5th, one thousand one hundred and eighteen representatives to the National People's Congress had reported to the meeting, and one thousand one hundred and five representatives attended today's meeting. Seven heads of government departments attended the meeting as observers, and one hundred and sixty-three people were present as observers.

Twenty-three heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China and twenty-two guests from various countries were invited to attend today's meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, July 5th. Before the Second Session of the First National People's Congress was held, the representatives attending the meeting had exchanged opinions fully in groups on the preparations for the meeting proposed by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

On July 2nd, the representatives to the National People's Congress who had reported to Beijing held a gathering to discuss the preparations for the Second Session of the National People's Congress. After the meeting, the representatives formed thirty-three representative groups according to the electoral units and began to read the draft of the First Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy of the People's Republic of China.

The representatives also exchanged views in groups on the draft agenda of the Second Session of the National People's Congress, the draft list of candidates for the Presidium, and the draft list of candidates for the Secretary-General. Most of the representative groups also held discussions on the inspection work of the representatives. Everyone believed that this inspection was of great help to them in understanding the current important work of the country and fulfilling their responsibilities when discussing the agenda items at this meeting.

### 全国人民代表大会第二次会议开幕　会议通过主要议程并开始听取关于第一个五年计划的报告

1955-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会第二次会议开幕
　　会议通过主要议程并开始听取关于第一个五年计划的报告
    新华社五日讯　中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议五日下午三时在北京中南海怀仁堂开幕。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、副主席朱德，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、林伯渠、李济深、罗荣桓、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通、赛福鼎，中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、李富春、李先念，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，出席了会议的开幕式。当他们登上主席台时，全场起立，大厅里响起了经久不息的掌声。
    越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他所率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团应邀参加了今天的开幕式。全体代表以热烈的掌声欢迎贵宾们的光临。
    刘少奇委员长宣布会议开幕。乐队奏中华人民共和国国歌。接着，会议一致选出了由七十九人组成的本次会议的主席团和秘书长。
    会议通过本次会议的主要议程为下列各项：
    一、决定中华人民共和国发展国民经济的第一个五年计划。
    二、审查和批准一九五四年国家决算和一九五五年国家预算。
    三、制定中华人民共和国兵役法。
    四、通过关于治理黄河规划的决议。
    五、通过全国人民代表大会常务委员会的工作报告。
    六、补选全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员。
    在开幕式举行后，会议即进入第一项议程，由中华人民共和国国务院副总理、国家计划委员会主任李富春代表国务院开始作“关于发展国民经济的第一个五年计划的报告”。
    今天的会议进行到七时三十五分休会。在六日的会议上，李富春将继续作“关于发展国民经济的第一个五年计划的报告”。
    到五日止，向会议报到的全国人民代表大会代表一千一百一十八人，出席今天会议的代表一千一百零五人。列席会议的政府各部门负责人七人，旁听的有一百六十三人。
    应邀参加今天会议的有各国驻华使节和外交官员二十三人，各国来宾二十二人。
    新华社五日讯　在第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议举行前，出席会议的代表曾分组就全国人民代表大会常务委员会提出的关于会议的准备事项充分地交换了意见。
    七月二日，全国人民代表大会在京报到的代表曾举行集会，商谈有关全国人民代表大会第二次会议的准备事项。会后，代表们按照选举单位分别组成三十三个代表小组，并开始阅读中华人民共和国发展国民经济的第一个五年计划草案。
    代表们还曾分组对全国人民代表大会第二次会议议程草案、主席团人选和秘书长人选草案交换了意见。大部分代表小组还对代表视察工作进行了座谈。大家都认为这次视察对于他们了解国家当前重要工作的情况和在这次会议讨论各项议程时履行他们所担负的职责，有很大帮助。（附图片）
