Title: Peng Dehuai Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Albanian People's Army Day
Author:
Date: 1955-07-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 8th. Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Peng Dehuai, sent a telegram on July 8th to Lieutenant General Beqir Balluku, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Albania, warmly congratulating him on Albanian People's Army Day, and wishing the Albanian People's Army new achievements in safeguarding the socialist construction of the Albanian people and maintaining world peace.

### 彭德怀部长电贺阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军节

1955-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　彭德怀部长电贺阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军节
    新华社八日讯　中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀八日致电阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库中将，热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军节，并祝阿尔巴尼亚人民军在保卫阿尔巴尼亚人民的社会主义建设和维护世界和平的事业中获得新的成就。
