Title: Vietnam International Commission Issues Report on Saigon Atrocities; Prime Minister Nehru Condemns Atrocities Against Vietnam International Commission
Author:
Date: 1955-07-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 25th: Vietnam News Agency reported on the 24th that the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam issued a press release on July 21st regarding the atrocities in Saigon on July 20th, stating:

"The International Commission has studied the violent demonstrations that occurred on July 20, 1955, at the Queen's Hotel and Gallieni Hotel, where Commission personnel reside. After studying the detailed circumstances of this event, the International Commission has submitted its report to the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference through the Government of India, the chairman country of the International Commission."

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 25th: Prime Minister Nehru, speaking at a meeting of the Congress Party Standing Committee in New Delhi on the 23rd, said that the recent "outrageous demonstrations" in Saigon "could hinder the work of the International Armistice Supervisory Commission."

Nehru said that these demonstrations against International Commission personnel were organized and deliberate, with the aim of creating obstacles in the path of implementing the Geneva Accords on Indochina.

### 越南国际委员会就西贡暴行事件提出报告  尼赫鲁总理谴责袭击越南国际委员会的暴行

1955-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国际委员会就西贡暴行事件提出报告
    尼赫鲁总理谴责袭击越南国际委员会的暴行
    新华社河内二十五日电　越南通讯社二十四日讯：越南国际监督和监察委员会七月二十一日就西贡七月二十日的暴行事件发表了一个新闻公报说：
    “国际委员会已经研究了一九五五年七月二十日在委员会人员居住的皇后旅馆和加利埃尼旅馆发生的带有暴行的示威。国际委员会在研究了这一事件的详细情况以后，已经通过国际委员会的主席国印度的政府向日内瓦会议的两位主席提出了它的报告。”
    新华社新德里二十五日电　尼赫鲁总理二十三日在新德里举行的国大党常务委员会会议上讲话时说，西贡最近的“蛮横的示威游行”，“很可能会妨碍国际停战监察委员会的工作”。
    尼赫鲁说，这些反对国际委员会人员的示威游行是有组织的，蓄意的，其目的是要在履行关于印度支那的日内瓦协议的道路上制造障碍。
