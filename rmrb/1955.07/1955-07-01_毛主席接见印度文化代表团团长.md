Title: Chairman Mao Receives Head of Indian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-07-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th—Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received A. K. Chanda, the head of the Indian Cultural Delegation and Deputy Minister of the Indian Government's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and his wife, Gu Singh, the delegation's secretary and an official of the Indian Government's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ra. Malate, the delegation's stage supervisor, director of All India Radio, and program director, on the afternoon of the 30th.

Present at the reception were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, and Li Weihan, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, and Chen Yi, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, and Fu Zuoyi, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council; Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture; Ding Xilin and Xia Yan, Vice Ministers; and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Lai Jiawen, the Indian Ambassador to China, and his wife were also present at the reception.

### 毛主席接见印度文化代表团团长

1955-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见印度文化代表团团长
    新华社三十日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席，三十日下午接见了印度文化代表团团长、印度政府外交部副部长阿·库·钱达和夫人，代表团秘书、印度政府外交部官员古·辛格，代表团舞台监督、全印广播电台主任兼节目指导拉·马拉太。
    接见时在座的，有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇、国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、黄炎培、李维汉，国务院副总理陈云、邓小平、陈毅，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻、叶剑英、程潜、张治中、傅作义，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，副部长丁西林、夏衍和外交部副部长章汉夫。
  接见时，印度驻华大使赖嘉文和夫人也在座。
