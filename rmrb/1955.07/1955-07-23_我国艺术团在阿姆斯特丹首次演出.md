Title: Chinese Art Troupe's First Performance in Amsterdam
Author:
Date: 1955-07-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 22nd: Amsterdam dispatch: After concluding its performances in Belgium, the Chinese Art Troupe arrived in the Netherlands on the evening of the 18th for a performance tour.

    On the afternoon of the 19th, the Mayor of Amsterdam, Mr. D'Ailly, received the head of the Chinese Art Troupe, Zhang Zhixiang, and the deputy head, Zhao Feng, as well as the troupe's main performers. D'Ailly, on behalf of the citizens of Amsterdam, welcomed the Chinese Art Troupe and congratulated it on the great achievements it had made in its performances in Europe. He said that the Netherlands and China have a long-standing cultural and trade relationship, and the Chinese Art Troupe's tour of the Netherlands will further promote and develop the friendly relations between the two countries.

    On the evening of the 19th, the Chinese Art Troupe held its premiere performance at the Royal Theatre in Amsterdam. The performance was warmly welcomed by the Amsterdam audience. From five o'clock in the morning, hundreds of people buying tickets waited at the entrance of the theatre, although the theatre stipulated that ticket sales would not begin until ten o'clock. The theatre was packed for the first performance, and the performers were enthusiastically asked to take curtain calls more than forty times. At the end of the performance, the audience stood up and cheered the Chinese artists.

    On the 20th, Amsterdam newspapers published articles commenting on the performance of the Chinese Art Troupe. "Het Volk" said: The Chinese Art Troupe has achieved another great success following its performances in France and Belgium.

    The newspaper stated: The Chinese artists' skills are an inexhaustible treasure of ancient civilization and outstanding art.

    "De Telegraaf" said that the performance of the Chinese Art Troupe is not limited by national boundaries. The pantomime is harmonious and touching, and the martial arts are excellent. This poetic and rare performance demonstrates thousands of years of civilization and also showcases a new realm of art.

### 我国艺术团在阿姆斯特丹首次演出

1955-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团在阿姆斯特丹首次演出
    新华社布拉格二十二日电　阿姆斯特丹消息：中国艺术团结束了在比利时的演出后，已经在十八日晚从比利时抵荷兰作访问演出。
    十九日下午，阿姆斯特丹市长戴利接见了中国艺术团团长张致祥和副团长赵沨以及艺术团的主要演员。戴利代表阿姆斯特丹市民欢迎中国艺术团，并祝贺它在欧洲演出中所获得的巨大成就。他说，荷兰和中国之间有着长期的文化和贸易关系，中国艺术团到荷兰来作巡回演出，将进一步促进和发展两国的友好关系。
    十九日晚间，中国艺术团在阿姆斯特丹皇家剧院举行首次公演。这次演出受到阿姆斯特丹观众的热烈欢迎。从清晨五点钟起，数以百计的买票的人群就等候在剧院门口，虽然剧院规定要到十点钟才开始售票。第一场公演时剧场内座无虚席，演员们被热烈要求谢幕达四十余次。演出结束时，观众起立向中国艺术家们喝彩。
    二十日，阿姆斯特丹各报刊载文章，评论中国艺术团的演出。“人民报”说：中国艺术团继它在法国和比利时的演出之后，又获得了一次巨大的成功。
    这一家报纸说：中国艺术家的演技是古代的文明和卓越的艺术中的一种无穷无尽的财富。
    “电讯报”说，中国艺术团的表演不受国界的限制。哑剧是和谐而感人的，武功是卓越的，这种富有诗意的、罕见的表演，显示了几千年的文明，也展示了艺术的全新的境界。
