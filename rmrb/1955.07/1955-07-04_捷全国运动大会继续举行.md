Title: Czechoslovak National Games Continue
Author:
Date: 1955-07-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency: The Czechoslovak First National Games youth day program concluded on June 26th. During the youth day (June 23rd to 26th), tens of thousands of young athletes participated in various group callisthenics performances every day. On the morning of that day, a grand parade involving over 100,000 young athletes was also held in Wenceslas Square. During the parade, marchers wearing sports clothes of various colors cheered as they walked across the square, receiving the review of Czechoslovak Communist Party and government leaders headed by President Zápotocký.
    The adult day program of the Games began on June 27th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 3rd: The final part of the Czechoslovak First National Games – the adult day program – began on July 2nd. On this day, the Strahov Stadium in Prague, which can hold approximately 200,000 spectators, was packed. Among those present to watch the performances were leaders of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and government headed by President Zápotocký, and heads of sports delegations from the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and many other countries.
    This part of the Games program will run until its conclusion on July 5th. During this period, in addition to the 137,000 athletes performing, tens of thousands of dancers and singers will also participate in the performances.

### 捷全国运动大会继续举行

1955-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷全国运动大会继续举行
    据新华社讯　捷克斯洛伐克第一届全国运动大会青少年日的节目已在六月二十六日结束。在青年少年日（二十三日到二十六日）期间，每天都有数以万计的青少年运动员参加各种团体操表演。这一天上午，瓦茨拉夫广场还举行了由十万多个少年运动员参加的盛大游行。游行时，身穿各色运动服装的游行者欢呼着走过广场，接受以萨波托斯基总统为首的捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府领导人的检阅。
    六月二十七日，运动大会成年日的节目开始。
    据新华社布拉格三日电　捷克斯洛伐克第一届全国运动大会的最后部分——成年日节目在七月二日开始。这一天，布拉格能容观众约二十万人的斯特拉霍夫运动场挤满了人。到场观看表演的有以萨波托斯基总统为首的捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府的领导人，苏联、中华人民共和国和其他许多国家的体育代表团团长等。
    运动大会这一部分的节目将进行到七月五日结束。在这期间，除有十三万七千名运动员表演节目外，另外还有成万名的舞蹈家和歌唱家等参加表演。
