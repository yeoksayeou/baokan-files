Title: Delegations from the United States, Britain, and France Arrive in Geneva
Author:
Date: 1955-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: According to TASS news, U.S. President Eisenhower, Secretary of State Dulles, British Prime Minister Eden, Foreign Secretary Macmillan, French Prime Minister Faure, and Foreign Minister Pinay arrived in Geneva by plane on the 16th, respectively, to attend the four-power summit conference to be held on July 18th.

    French Prime Minister Faure told reporters at Paris airport before departing for Geneva, "The meeting of heads of government and foreign ministers will undoubtedly be a milestone for reconciliation and the establishment of peace." He said that he was leaving to attend the meeting "with confidence of success, and to work to achieve this."

### 美英法三国代表团到日内瓦

1955-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    美英法三国代表团到日内瓦
    新华社十七日讯　据塔斯社讯：美国总统艾森豪威尔，国务卿杜勒斯，英国首相艾登，外交大臣麦克米伦，法国总理富尔，外交部长比内在十六日分别乘飞机到达日内瓦，出席将在七月十八日举行的四大国政府首脑会议。
    法国总理富尔在启程去日内瓦时在巴黎机场向记者发表谈话说，“政府首脑和外交部长会议无疑将是和解和建立和平的里程碑”。他说，他动身去参加会议是“怀着成功的信心的，而且要努力达到这一点”。
