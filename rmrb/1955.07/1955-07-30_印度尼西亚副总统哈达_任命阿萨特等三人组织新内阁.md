Title: Indonesian Vice President Hatta Appoints Assaat and Two Others to Form New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1955-07-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 28th: Indonesian Vice President Hatta concluded his consultations with various political parties on the formation of a new cabinet on the 28th. Over the past three days, he has met with representatives of twenty-one parties with members in the parliament.

Hatta told reporters on the 28th that most parties advocate the establishment of a parliamentary coalition cabinet.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 29th: Indonesian Vice President Hatta appointed Assaat (non-partisan), Wilopo (National Party), and Sukiman (Masyumi Party) on the morning of July 29th to form a new cabinet. Hatta told reporters that the new cabinet would be a parliamentary cabinet. He said that the term of the new cabinet would be limited, and it would be dissolved after the new parliament is elected.

### 印度尼西亚副总统哈达  任命阿萨特等三人组织新内阁

1955-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚副总统哈达
    任命阿萨特等三人组织新内阁
    新华社雅加达二十八日电　印度尼西亚副总统哈达二十八日结束了他就组织新内阁问题同各政党进行的磋商。三天来，他先后接见了在国会中有议员的二十一个党派的代表。
    哈达二十八日对新闻记者们说，大多数党派都主张成立国会联合内阁。
    新华社雅加达二十九日电　印度尼西亚副总统哈达二十九日上午任命阿萨特（无党派）、韦洛坡（国民党）和苏基曼（马斯友美党）三人组织新内阁。哈达对记者们说，新内阁将是一个国会制内阁。他说，新内阁的任期将是有限的，它在新国会选出后即行解散。
