Title: Order of the Ministry of Public Security of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1955-07-26
Page: 3

The "Regulations on the Management of Radio Equipment" was approved by the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on September 25, 1954. It is hereby promulgated, and it is hoped that all localities will comply with it.

Minister Luo Ruiqing
July 23, 1955
