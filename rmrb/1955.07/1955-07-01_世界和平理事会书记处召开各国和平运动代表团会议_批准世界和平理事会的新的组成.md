Title: Secretariat of the World Peace Council Convenes Meeting of Delegations from National Peace Movements; Approves New Composition of World Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1955-07-01
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 30th: TASS reports from Helsinki on the 29th: The Secretariat of the World Peace Council convened a meeting of delegations from national peace movements after the conclusion of the World Peace Assembly. The meeting approved the new composition of the World Peace Council. Candidates for membership in the World Peace Council were nominated by the national delegations.
　　At the invitation of the Secretariat, some individuals who had not participated in the peace movement also attended this meeting.

### 世界和平理事会书记处召开各国和平运动代表团会议　批准世界和平理事会的新的组成

1955-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会书记处召开各国和平运动代表团会议
　　批准世界和平理事会的新的组成
    新华社三十日讯　塔斯社赫尔辛基二十九日讯：世界和平理事会书记处在世界和平大会闭幕后，召开了一次各国和平运动代表团的会议。会议批准了世界和平理事会的新的组成。世界和平理事会理事候选人是由各国代表团提出的。
    根据书记处的邀请，有些没有参加和平运动的人士也出席了这次会议。
