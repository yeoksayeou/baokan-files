Title: Implementation of the Agreement and Exchange of Notes Regarding the Chinese Changchun Railway, etc. The Soviet Union Transfers Property to Our Government, and the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee Completes All Transfer Work

Author:
Date: 1951-01-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th. In accordance with the Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics of February 14, 1950, regarding the Chinese Changchun Railway, Port Arthur, and Dalian, and the exchange of notes on the same day, it was stipulated that the property temporarily administered by the Soviet side or leased by the Soviet side in Dalian, the property obtained by Soviet economic organs in the Northeast from Japanese owners, and all the real estate of the former Beijing Barracks, be transferred without compensation to the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China.

　　To this end, the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee established for this purpose, in 1950, has transferred all the above-mentioned properties to the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The work of the committee has now been fully completed.

### 执行关于中长铁路等协定及换文的规定  苏联移交财产给我政府  中苏联合委员会移交工作全部完成

1951-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　执行关于中长铁路等协定及换文的规定
    苏联移交财产给我政府
    中苏联合委员会移交工作全部完成
    【新华社十八日讯】中华人民共和国与苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟间于一九五零年二月十四日关于中国长春铁路、旅顺口及大连的协定及同日之换文，曾规定将大连市苏联方面临时代管或苏联方面租用之财产，苏联经济机关在东北自日本所有者手中所获得之财产，以及过去北京兵营的全部房产，无偿地移交中华人民共和国中央人民政府。
    为此目的而设立之中苏联合委员会于一九五零年内，已将上述一切财产移交给中华人民共和国中央人民政府，该委员会之工作现已全部完成。
