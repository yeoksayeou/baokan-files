Title: Doctor Yudin Departs Beijing to Return to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1951-01-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 5th: Doctor Yudin, a famous Soviet scholar and philosopher, and his wife, who were invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to lecture in China, left Beijing at 10:00 this afternoon to return to the Soviet Union. Those who went to the station to see them off included Guo Moruo, Nie Rongzhen, Lu Dingyi, Yang Shangkun, Li Lisan, Chen Boda, Hu Qiaomu, Qian Junrui, and the Soviet Ambassador to China, Rosin, and Counselor Fedlin. After coming to China, Doctor Yudin gave academic lectures in Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan, and Xi'an.

### 尤金博士离京返苏

1951-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　尤金博士离京返苏
    【新华社五日讯】应中苏友好协会总会邀请来中国讲学的苏联著名学者、哲学家尤金博士及其夫人于今日下午十时离京返苏。前往车站欢送者有郭沫若、聂荣臻、陆定一、杨尚昆、李立三、陈伯达、胡乔木、钱俊瑞，和苏联驻中国大使罗申、参赞费德林等人。尤金博士来中国后，曾先后在北京、南京、上海、杭州、广州、武汉和西安等地作学术讲演。
