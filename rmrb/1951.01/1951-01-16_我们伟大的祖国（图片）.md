Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-01-16
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This is the Yellow Sea, located between our country and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The great Yellow Sea surrounds the long coastline stretching from the mouth of the Yalu River in the north to the north of the Yangtze River estuary. In a sense, the Chinese People's Volunteers' anti-aggression struggle on the Korean battlefield is a struggle to defend the security of the Yellow Sea.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是我国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国之间的黄海的海面。伟大的黄海，包围着由鸭绿江口以南直至长江口以北的漫长的海岸线。中国人民志愿军在朝鲜战场上的反侵略斗争，在一个意义上说，就是保卫黄海安全的斗争。
