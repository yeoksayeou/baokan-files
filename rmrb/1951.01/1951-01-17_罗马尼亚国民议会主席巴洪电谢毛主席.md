Title: Romanian National Assembly Chairman Parhon Sends Telegram to Thank Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1951-01-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: K.N. Parhon, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong to thank him for his congratulatory telegram on the third anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic. The telegram reads:
To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
On the occasion of the third anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic, I would like to express my sincere thanks to you and the Chinese people for your congratulatory telegram, and to express my highest wishes to you.
Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, Dr. K.N. Parhon

### 罗马尼亚国民议会主席巴洪电谢毛主席

1951-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚国民议会主席巴洪电谢毛主席
    【新华社十六日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席巴洪电谢毛泽东主席电贺罗马尼亚人民共和国成立三周年。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    罗马尼亚人民共和国成立三周年之际，承蒙来电祝贺，谨对您及中国人民致以热诚的谢意，并向您表示我至上的希望。
                             罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团
                                          主席Ｋ·Ｎ·巴洪博士
