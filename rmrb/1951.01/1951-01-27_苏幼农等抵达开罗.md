Title: Su Younong and Others Arrive in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1951-01-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 26th: Cairo dispatch: Su Younong, representative of our country to the joint conference of the Universal Postal Union and the International Air Transport Association, along with advisor Ge Baoquan and attendant Xu Chuanxian, arrived in Cairo, the capital of Egypt, on the 23rd to attend the conference.

### 苏幼农等抵达开罗

1951-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏幼农等抵达开罗
    【新华社布拉格二十六日电】开罗讯：我国出席万国邮政联盟与国际航空运输协会混合会议的代表苏幼农，顾问戈宝权、随员徐传贤等一行，已于二十三日抵达埃及首都开罗出席会议。
