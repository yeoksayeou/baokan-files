Title: Representatives of Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia Hold Meeting to Organize the United Front of Indochinese Nationalities
Author:
Date: 1951-01-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 20th: Representatives of the national united fronts of Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia held a three-day meeting in the second half of November last year to discuss the establishment of a joint national united front of Indochina. Ton Duc Thang, the representative of the Vietnam National United Front, delivered the opening speech. The participating representatives reported on the activities of their respective national united fronts, exchanged experiences of the three countries, discussed the establishment of a joint national united front of Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia, and elected a preparatory committee to carry out the work of organizing this front.

The representative of the Lao National United Front said: "The Joint Front is a common organization leading the people of our three countries in the struggle against the common enemy. Because we convened this meeting, we have won a great victory over the enemy." The representative of the Cambodian National United Front said: "From the geographical and historical conditions of Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia, they cannot be separated. They must unite into a group to resist the aggression of French imperialism. Thanks to the support of the Vietnamese people, the Cambodian people have achieved many victories. The liberated areas of Cambodia continue to expand day by day." Hoang Quoc Viet, Secretary-General of the Viet Minh Front Steering Committee, pointed out in his closing speech that the alliance between Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia is not only a prerequisite for the complete victory of the people of the three Indochinese countries, but also a solid foundation for building a truly democratic, prosperous, and happy Indochinese Federation. This alliance is based on mutual respect and equality, and it will surely achieve the ultimate goal of the people of the three countries.

### 越南、老挝、柬埔寨三国代表举行会议  组织印度支那民族统一阵线

1951-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南、老挝、柬埔寨三国代表举行会议
    组织印度支那民族统一阵线
    【新华社二十六日讯】越南通讯社北越二十日讯：越南、老挝和柬埔寨的民族统一阵线代表曾于去年十一月下半月举行历时三天的会议，讨论建立印度支那联合民族统一阵线问题。越南民族统一阵线代表孙得胜致开会词。与会代表报告了他们的民族统一阵线的活动，互相交换了三国的经验，讨论了建立越南、老挝和柬埔寨联合民族统一阵线问题，并选举了一个筹备委员会以进行组织这个阵线工作。
    老挝民族统一阵线的代表说：“联合阵线是领导我们三国人民进行反对共同敌人的斗争的共同组织。由于我们召开了这个会议，我们已赢得了一个对敌人的伟大胜利。”柬埔寨民族统一阵线代表说：“从越南、老挝和柬埔寨的地理条件和历史条件来看，它们是不能被分离的。它们必须团结成一个集团，以反抗法帝国主义的侵略。由于越南人民的支持，柬埔寨人民已获致很多胜利。柬埔寨的解放区一天一天地继续扩大着。”越盟阵线指导委员会总书记黄国越在闭幕词中指出：越南、老挝和柬埔寨之间的联盟不仅是印度支那三国人民获得完全的胜利的先决条件，而且是建立一个真正民主、繁荣和幸福的印度支那联邦的稳固基础。这个联盟是建立在互尊和平等的基础上的，它一定能够达到三国人民的最终目的。
