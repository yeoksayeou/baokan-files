Title: Indian Poet Edward Says: Asian People Stand on China's Side
Author:
Date: 1951-01-08
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rome, January 5th: TASS reported that the head of the Indian delegation to the Second World Peace Congress and member of the World Peace Council, Indian poet Edward, delivered a speech at the Rome Peace Congress. In reporting on the struggles of the Indian people and the people of Asian countries to defend peace, Edward emphasized that since the people of the world learned about the atrocities committed by the Americans in Korea, the United States has greatly lost its "prestige" among the people of Asian countries. Edward said, "If a war breaks out between the United States and China, the entire Asia will undoubtedly stand on China's side."

### 印度诗人爱德华说：亚洲人民站在中国这一边

1951-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度诗人爱德华说：亚洲人民站在中国这一边
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社罗马五日电：印度出席第二届世界保卫和平大会代表团团长和世界和平理事会理事、印度诗人爱德华，曾在罗马的和平大会上发表演说。爱德华在报告印度人民和亚洲各国人民为保卫和平而进行的斗争时，曾强调指出：自从世界人民知道了美国人在朝鲜的暴行以后，美国在亚洲各国人民中间大大丧失了它的“威信”。爱德华说：“倘使美国与中国之间爆发了战争，整个亚洲无疑地将站在中国一边。”
