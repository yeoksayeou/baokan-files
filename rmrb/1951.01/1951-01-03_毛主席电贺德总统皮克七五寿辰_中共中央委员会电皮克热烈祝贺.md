Title: Chairman Mao Sends Telegram to Congratulate President Pieck on His 75th Birthday; CPC Central Committee Sends Warm Congratulations to Pieck
Author:
Date: 1951-01-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, January 2nd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government sent a telegram of congratulations on the occasion of the 75th birthday of President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic on January 3rd. The telegram reads as follows:
Dear President Wilhelm Pieck:
　　On the occasion of your seventy-fifth birthday, I would like to extend my highest respect and warmest congratulations to you on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and myself.
　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong.
　　Xinhua News Agency, January 2nd: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to congratulate Wilhelm Pieck, Chairman of the German Socialist Unity Party, on his birthday. The telegram reads as follows:
Dear Comrade Wilhelm Pieck:
　　The Communist Party of China warmly congratulates you on your seventy-fifth birthday. We celebrate your health and longevity, and celebrate the rapid democratic unification of the German people under your leadership.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 毛主席电贺德总统皮克七五寿辰   中共中央委员会电皮克热烈祝贺

1951-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席电贺德总统皮克七五寿辰
    中共中央委员会电皮克热烈祝贺
    【新华社二日讯】一月三日为德意志民主共和国皮克总统七十五岁寿辰，我中央人民政府毛泽东主席特致电祝贺。电文如下：亲爱的威廉·皮克总统：
    当您的七十五岁寿辰之际，谨代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府和我个人向您致崇高的敬意和热烈的祝贺。
                              中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席  毛泽东
    【新华社二日讯】中国共产党中央委员会电贺德国统一社会党主席威廉·皮克寿辰。电文如下：亲爱的威廉·皮克同志：
    中国共产党热烈地祝贺您的七十五岁寿辰。庆祝您的健康和长寿，并且庆祝德国人民在您的领导下迅速获得民主的统一。
                                     中国共产党中央委员会
