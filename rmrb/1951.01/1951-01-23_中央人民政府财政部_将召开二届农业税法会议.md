Title: The Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government Will Convene the Second Agricultural Tax Law Conference
Author:
Date: 1951-01-23
Page: 1

　　Central People's Government Ministry of Finance
    Will Convene the Second Agricultural Tax Law Conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, 22nd] The autumn collection work of the national agricultural tax for the year 1950 is about to be completed. The Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government has decided to convene the Second National Agricultural Tax Law Conference in Beijing on February 28th to summarize last year's agricultural tax collection work, revise the various laws and regulations on agricultural tax in the newly and old liberated areas, and study this year's summer collection work. The Ministry of Finance is now actively carrying out preparations for the conference, mainly soliciting opinions on the agricultural tax law from people from all walks of life across the country, especially from the broad masses of farmers.

### 中央人民政府财政部  将召开二届农业税法会议

1951-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府财政部
    将召开二届农业税法会议
    【新华社二十二日讯】一九五零年度全国农业税的秋征工作即将完成。中央人民政府财政部，已决定于二月二十八日在北京召开第二届全国农业税法会议，总结去年的农业税征收工作，修订新、老解放区农业税的各项法规；并研究本年度的夏征工作。现在财政部正积极地进行会议准备工作，主要是向全国各界人士，特别是向广大农民征询对农业税法的意见。
