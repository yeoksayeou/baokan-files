    Our Great Motherland
    This is a type of old-fashioned waterwheel in our country's countryside. Farmers near Lanzhou, Gansu, utilize the rapids in the Yantan area of the upper Yellow River to pile up these many wooden waterwheels, obtaining approximately 200,000 liters of water daily to irrigate their fields and supply drinking water.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是我国农村中旧式水车的一种。甘肃兰州附近的农民们，利用黄河上游雁滩中的急流堆运这许多木制的水车，每日得到约二十万升的水量，来灌溉他们的田园，并且供给饮水。
