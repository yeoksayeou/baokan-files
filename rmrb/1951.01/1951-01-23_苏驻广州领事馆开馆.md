Title: Soviet Consulate in Guangzhou Opens

Author:

Date: 1951-01-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 21st: With the consent of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, the Soviet government has established a consulate in Guangzhou. The consulate was officially established and held an opening ceremony on the 20th. The Soviet Consul in Guangzhou, Ma Luhang, delivered a speech at the opening ceremony, hailing the friendly unity between the Chinese and Soviet peoples. Ye Jianying, the Mayor of Guangzhou, extended warm congratulations on behalf of all the people of Guangzhou on the establishment of the Soviet Consulate in Guangzhou.

### 苏驻广州领事馆开馆

1951-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏驻广州领事馆开馆
    【新华社广州二十一日电】苏联政府在征得我中华人民共和国中央人民政府同意后，在广州市设立领事馆，该馆已于二十日正式成立并举行开馆典礼。苏联驻广州市领事马陆恒在开馆典礼上致词，为中苏两国人民的友好团结欢呼。广州市市长叶剑英代表全广州人民对苏联驻广州市领事馆的成立，表示热烈的祝贺。
