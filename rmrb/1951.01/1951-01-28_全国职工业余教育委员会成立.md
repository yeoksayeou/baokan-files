Title: National Committee for Workers' Spare-Time Education Established
Author:
Date: 1951-01-28
Page: 3

　　National Committee for Workers' Spare-Time Education Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, 26th] In accordance with the State Council of the Central People's Government's "Directive on Developing Workers' Spare-Time Education" and the "Organizational Regulations for Committees at All Levels for Workers' Spare-Time Education" approved and promulgated by the State Council, the National Committee for Workers' Spare-Time Education was formally established on the 24th of this month. The committee is composed of 22 members, including Ma Xulun, Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, Qian Junrui, Vice Minister, Lin Handa, Director of the Department of Social Education, Jiang Ling and Ma Yinqiu, Deputy Directors, Liu Ziju, Minister of Culture and Education of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Feng Suhai and Zhang Xiuzhu, Deputy Ministers, Dong Xin, Minister of Production, Wu Ping, Deputy Director of the Women Workers Department, Dong Chuping, Director of the Information and Research Office of the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government, Yang Shu, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, Hu Naiqiu, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, He Changgong, Vice Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Heavy Industry, Chen Yu, Minister of the Fuel Industry, Wu Jingtian, Vice Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Railways, Shao Quanlin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, and Xue Muqiao, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee. According to the provisions of the Organizational Regulations for Committees at All Levels for Workers' Spare-Time Education, Ma Xulun and Liu Ziju were elected as Chairman and Vice Chairman respectively. Shao Quanlin, Jiang Ling, Zhang Xiuzhu, Dong Chuping, Yang Shu, and Hu Naiqiu were also elected as Standing Committee members, and Jiang Ling and Zhang Xiuzhu were appointed as Secretary-General and Deputy Secretary-General. The committee has two working bodies: a secretariat and the "Learn Culture" magazine. According to the current needs, the committee has determined three main tasks: (1) to inspect and study the work of workers' spare-time education in various regions; (2) to launch the "Learn Culture" bi-monthly magazine in February, targeting workers who know around 1,000 characters; (3) to organize forces to compile and review teaching materials for workers' spare-time schools.

### 全国职工业余教育委员会成立

1951-01-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国职工业余教育委员会成立
    【新华社二十六日讯】根据中央人民政府政务院《关于开展职工业余教育的指示》以及政务院批准发布的《各级职工业余教育委员会组织条例》的规定，全国职工业余教育委员会已于本月二十四日正式成立。委员会由中央人民政府教育部部长马叙伦，副部长钱俊瑞，社会教育司司长林汉达，副司长江凌、马印秋，中华全国总工会文教部部长刘子久，副部长冯宿海、张修竹，生产部部长董昕，女工部副部长吴平，中央人民政府劳动部资料研究室主任董锄平，新民主主义青年团中央宣传部部长杨述，全国民主妇联宣传部副部长胡耐秋，中央人民政府重工业部副部长何长工，燃料工业部部长陈郁，中央人民政府铁道部副部长武竞天、政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长邵荃麟，财政经济委员会秘书长薛暮桥等二十二人组成。根据各级职工业余教育委员会组织条例的规定，由马叙伦、刘子久分别担任正副主任委员，另推选邵荃麟、江凌、张修竹、董锄平、杨述、胡耐秋为常务委员，江凌、张修竹为正副秘书长。委员会下设秘书处及《学文化》杂志社两个工作机构。根据当前需要，委员会确定三项主要任务：（一）检查研究各地职工业余教育工作；（二）于二月间创刊《学文化》半月刊，以识千字上下的职工群众为读者对象；（三）组织力量，编审职工业余学校教材。
