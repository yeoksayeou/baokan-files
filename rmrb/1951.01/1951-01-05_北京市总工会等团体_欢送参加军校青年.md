Title: Beijing Federation of Trade Unions and Other Organizations Celebrate Youth Joining Military Academies
Author:
Date: 1951-01-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd—The Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Central Academy of Drama, the Youth Art Theater, the People's Art Theater, and the Cultural Troupe of the North China People's Revolutionary University held evening parties on the 2nd at the Youth Palace, the August 1st Auditorium, and the Fu Jen Catholic University Auditorium, respectively, to celebrate the youth from Beijing joining military cadre schools. Responsible persons from each organization spoke at the meetings. They expressed warm respect for the patriotic actions of the youth joining military cadre schools and encouraged everyone to complete the glorious tasks assigned by the motherland. At the meeting held at the Youth Palace to celebrate eight hundred young women joining military cadre schools, Yang Yunyu, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, said: "So many young women participating in the national defense construction of the motherland is something that has never happened in Chinese history." She also encouraged the young women to work hard to master military science knowledge and bravely face the upcoming more organized, disciplined, and arduous life.

### 北京市总工会等团体  欢送参加军校青年

1951-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市总工会等团体
    欢送参加军校青年
    【新华社三日讯】北京市总工会、北京市民主妇女联合会、北京市文学艺术界联合会、北京市中苏友好协会和中央戏剧学院、青年艺术剧院、人民艺术剧院、华北人民革命大学文工团等八个团体于二日晚分别在青年宫、八一礼堂、辅仁大学礼堂等处举行晚会，欢送北京参加军事干部学校的青年。会上各团体负责人都讲了话。他们对参加军事干部学校的青年的爱国行动，表示热烈的敬意，并勉励大家完成祖国所给予的光荣任务。在青年宫举行的欢送八百女青年参加军事干部学校的大会上，北京市民主妇女联合会副主席杨蕴玉说：“这么多的女青年参加祖国的国防建设，这是中国历史上从来没有过的事情。”她并勉励女青年们努力学习掌握军事科学知识和勇敢迎接即将到来的更有组织、有纪律和更艰苦的生活。
