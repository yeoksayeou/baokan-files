Title: Ethnic Minorities in Beijing to Hold Parade Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1951-01-29
Page: 1

　　Beijing Ethnic Minorities to Hold Parade Tomorrow
    [By Our Newspaper] In order to continue to deeply promote the movement to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes and defend our country, and to oppose the U.S. imperialist plot to conclude a separate peace treaty with Japan and arm Japan, Beijing's ethnic minorities are scheduled to gather at the Hall of Supreme Harmony in the Forbidden City at 11:00 AM on January 30th and hold a patriotic parade.

### 京市各少数民族定明日游行

1951-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市各少数民族定明日游行
    【本报讯】北京市各少数民族为了继续深入开展抗美援朝保家卫国运动，反对美帝国主义单独对日媾和与武装日本的阴谋，定于一月三十日上午十一时在故宫太和殿集会，并举行爱国大游行。
