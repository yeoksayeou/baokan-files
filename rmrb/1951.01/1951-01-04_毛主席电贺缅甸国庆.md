Title: Chairman Mao Cables Congratulations on Burmese National Day
Author:
Date: 1951-01-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd.
　　Chairman Mao Cables Congratulations on Burmese National Day
    [Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd] January 4th marks the National Day of Burma. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of our country sent a cable to President Sao Shwe Thaik of the Union of Burma on the 2nd to express his congratulations. The text of the cable is as follows:
    To His Excellency Sao Shwe Thaik, President of the Union of Burma:
    On the occasion of Your Excellency's National Day, I, on behalf of the Government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend my congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Burma, and wish that the friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people will grow with each passing day.
                              Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
                                  January 2, 1951, Beijing

### 毛主席电贺缅甸国庆

1951-01-04
第1版()
专栏：社论

　　毛主席电贺缅甸国庆
    【新华社三日讯】一月四日为缅甸国庆纪念。我中央人民政府毛泽东主席于二日致电缅甸联邦总统萧恢塔，表示祝贺。电文如下：缅甸联邦总统萧恢塔先生：
    欣逢贵国国庆，我代表中华人民共和国政府及人民谨向阁下及缅甸人民致庆贺之忱，并祝中缅两国人民友谊与日俱增。
                              中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席  毛泽东
                                  一九五一年一月二日北京
