Title: American Worker's Daily Urges American People to Demand Government Accept My Peaceful Proposals
Author:
Date: 1951-01-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 28th: The New York Worker's Daily published the Chinese Foreign Ministry's response to the Indian Ambassador to Beijing's inquiry under a banner headline on the 23rd. The paper's Lake Success correspondent wrote: This world-shaking action by China comes at a time when the State Department is cracking the whip and urging the United Nations to condemn People's China as the "aggressor" in Korea. In its first-page editorial, the paper urged the American people to express their choice, stating: If you want peace and not war, to live and not die, then telegraph President Truman, your congressmen, and American senators, and tell them to accept China's proposals, to leave Korea, to accept China into the United Nations, and to save the lives of Americans and Koreans.

### 美工人日报敦促美国人民要求政府接受我和平建议

1951-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　美工人日报敦促美国人民要求政府接受我和平建议
    【新华社布拉格二十八日电】二十三日的纽约工人日报在通栏标题下刊载中国外交部对印度驻北京大使询问的答复。该报驻成功湖记者写道：中国这个轰动世界的行动，是在国务院正挥动鞭子吆喝联合国谴责人民中国为朝鲜的“侵略者”的时候来到的。该报在第一版社论中敦促美国人民表示他们的抉择，并称：如果你要和平而不要战争、要活不要死，那末就打电报给杜鲁门总统、给你的众议员和美国的参议员，叫他们接受中国的建议，叫他们离开朝鲜，接纳中国参加联合国，保全美国人和朝鲜人的生命。
