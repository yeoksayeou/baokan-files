　　Ball Game
    The Beijing Municipal Sports Association and the Youth Service Department are jointly sponsoring a basketball exhibition game to celebrate New Year's Day, starting at 1:00 PM on January 1, 1951. There will be three games in total: (1) Sports Association White vs. Bureau of Culture and Education, (2) Trade Union vs. Public Security Bureau, (3) Student Union vs. Sports Association. The ticket office and performance location are both at the Youth Service Department's court, with a ticket price of 1,000 yuan.

### 球赛

1951-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　球赛
    北京市体育分会和青年服务部为庆祝元旦，联合主办篮球表演赛，一九五一年一月一日下午一时开始。共有三场：（一）体联白——文教局，（二）工联——公安局，（三）学联——体联。售票处及表演地点，均在青年服务部球场，票价一千元。
