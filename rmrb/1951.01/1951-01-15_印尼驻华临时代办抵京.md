Title: Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-01-15
Page: 4

　　Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] The Indonesian Republic's Embassy to the People's Republic of China's Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Isa Matari, arrived in Beijing by car this afternoon. Those who went to the station to greet him included Han Xu, section chief of the Liaison Office of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, as well as personnel from the embassies of the Soviet Union, Romania, India, Poland, Hungary, Burma, Bulgaria, and Switzerland, and representatives of the Dutch government negotiations.

### 印尼驻华临时代办抵京

1951-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　印尼驻华临时代办抵京
    【新华社十四日讯】印度尼西亚共和国驻中华人民共和国大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂于今日下午乘车抵京。前往车站迎接者有我外交部交际处科长韩叙，此外尚有苏联、罗马尼亚、印度、波兰、匈牙利、缅甸、保加利亚、瑞士使馆人员及荷兰政府谈判代表等。
