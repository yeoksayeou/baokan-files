Title: Overseas Chinese in France Write Letters to Comfort the Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1951-01-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 14th: The "Qiaolian" semi-monthly, published by the Federation of Overseas Chinese in France on January 7th, stated that 132 overseas Chinese in France, including Chen Renkuan, wrote letters to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the Korean front, offering condolences and respect to the heroic soldiers. The letter, after pointing out that the great righteous deeds and glorious achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army had moved the entire community of overseas Chinese workers, businessmen, and students in France, said: "The desires and ambitions of American imperialism are bottomless, and they will never give up until they suffer complete defeat and destruction; but your bravery and victory have made them tremble before you. As long as you continue to strike them, their complete collapse is beyond doubt. The people of the whole world are on your side."

### 旅法华侨写信慰问志愿军

1951-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　旅法华侨写信慰问志愿军
    【新华社布拉格十四日电】旅法华侨联合会一月七日出版的“侨联”半月刊载称：旅法华侨陈仁宽等一百三十二人曾写信给朝鲜前线的中国人民志愿军，向英勇的战士们慰问和致敬。该信在指出中国人民志愿军伟大的义举和光荣的成就感动了整个旅法华侨工人商人学生以后说：“美国帝国主义的欲望和野心是无底的，不遭到彻底的失败和灭亡决不甘心罢手；但你们的英勇和胜利已使他们在你们面前战栗。只要继续给他们打击，他们全面的崩溃是无疑的。全世界人民是在你们一边。”
