Title: West German Workers Take United Action to Defend Peace and Oppose Rearmament
Author:
Date: 1951-01-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 18th (TASS dispatch): The German News Agency reported that a conference of workers' representatives, including members of the Communist Party, Social Democratic Party, Christian Democratic Party, and non-partisan individuals, was held in Mannheim (American zone). The meeting discussed what measures should be taken to defend peace and to fight against the re-militarization policy carried out by the Bonn "government."

　　The attendees established a committee to be responsible for the unified direction of workers' activities in Mannheim.

　　A similar committee was also established in Bochum (British zone). Workers there held a rally and passed a resolution stating, in part: "Now, when the German nation is in a major crisis, the unity of all working people is urgently needed. Only united action by all workers can restore Germany's unity, guarantee peace, prevent re-militarization, and lead our people to a bright future."

　　In Recklinghausen (British zone), workers also established a united action committee.

### 为保卫和平、反对重新武装  西德工人统一行动

1951-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　为保卫和平、反对重新武装
    西德工人统一行动
    【新华社讯】塔斯社柏林十八日电：德意志通讯社报道：包括共产党党员、社会民主党党员、基督教民主党党员及无党无派人士的工人代表在曼海姆（美占区）举行了会议。会上讨论了应采何种措施以保卫和平并对波恩“政府”所进行的重新军国主义化的政策进行斗争。
    到会者成立了一个委员会以负责统一指挥曼海姆工人的活动。
    在波却姆（英占区）也成立了同样的委员会。该地工人集会通过决议略称：“现在当德国民族处于重大危机中的时候，全体劳动人民的团结是迫切需要的。只有全体工人联合行动，才能恢复德国的统一，保证和平、制止重新军国主义化、引导我国人民走向美好的未来。”
    在勒克灵斯豪森（英占区），工人们也建立了统一行动委员会。
