Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou's Congratulatory Telegram on Indian National Day Prominently Published in Newspapers Across India

Author: 
Date: 1951-01-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 28th. On January 26th, the first anniversary of the founding of the Republic of India, newspapers across India prominently published in full the congratulatory telegrams from Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government to President Prasad and Prime Minister Nehru of India.

### 毛主席周总理贺印国庆电  印全国各地报纸显著刊登

1951-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　毛主席周总理贺印国庆电
    印全国各地报纸显著刊登
    【新华社新德里二十八日电】一月二十六日印度共和国成立一周年纪念日，印度全国各地报纸都在重要地位全文刊登我中央人民政府毛泽东主席与周恩来总理致印度普拉沙德总统与尼赫鲁总理的贺电。
