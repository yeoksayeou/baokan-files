Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Sends Condolences on the Death of Vavilov
Author:
Date: 1951-01-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters sent a telegram on January 27th to express condolences on the death of Academician Vavilov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. The telegram reads as follows: To Deputy President Bardin of the Soviet Academy of Sciences:
　　The death of President Vavilov is a great loss to the world's scientific community. We are deeply saddened by the news. We hereby send our condolences.
              The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters

### 中苏友好协会总会电唁瓦维洛夫逝世

1951-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会总会电唁瓦维洛夫逝世
    【新华社二十七日讯】中苏友好协会总会二十七日致电悼唁苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫逝世。电文如下：苏联科学院巴尔丁副院长：
    瓦维洛夫院长逝世是世界科学界的巨大损失，噩耗传来，不胜哀悼。谨电致唁。
              中苏友好协会总会
