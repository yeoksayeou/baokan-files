Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Leaves Moscow to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1951-01-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 15th: The first group of 25 members of the Chinese Youth Delegation to the Soviet Union, headed by Feng Wenbin, left by train on the 14th to return home after a two-and-a-half-month visit to the Soviet Union. The second group of 50 members, led by Li Chang, left Moscow on the 15th for a half-month visit to the Georgian Republic. On the 13th, the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee held a special banquet. The hosts and guests were extremely happy.

　　The Komsomolskaya Pravda published a full page on the 14th with the impressions of the delegation members on their visit to the Soviet Union and some photos. The newspaper's masthead was printed with the national flags of China and the Soviet Union and the inscription in Chinese and Russian: "We see the future of New China in your achievements." The newspaper wrote in an editorial: The meeting of Soviet and Chinese youth demonstrates the sincere friendship between the two great peoples. We wish our friends new victories in the struggle to build a prosperous and strong democratic China.

　　On the cold night of the 14th, pairs of Chinese and Soviet flags fluttered on the platform of the station, along with slogans written in Russian and Chinese: "Long live Comrade Stalin, the leader of the working people of the world!" "Long live Comrade Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people!" "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples!" The station was crowded with thousands of well-wishers, including Kozmasov, Chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, Semychasny, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, and Zeng Yongquan, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union. Kozmasov delivered a farewell speech at the station, saying: Bonds of friendship connect Soviet and Chinese youth, who are united in a common struggle for world peace. Soviet youth wish Chinese youth new achievements in the struggle for freedom and world peace. Feng Wenbin, head of the Chinese Youth Delegation, delivered a farewell speech. He expressed his gratitude to the Soviet people, Soviet youth, and the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League for their warm reception and brotherly assistance to the Chinese delegation. He emphasized: "The Soviet people, especially the Soviet youth, are moving forward to communism with such confidence and working hard for world peace, which is an example for Chinese youth to learn from. Chinese youth will always be sincere friends of Soviet youth."

### 中国青年代表团离莫斯科返国

1951-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国青年代表团离莫斯科返国
    【新华社莫斯科十五日电】以冯文彬为首的中国青年代表访苏团一部团员二十五人，在苏联进行了两个半月的访问之后，已于十四日乘火车返国。另外一部团员五十人由李昌率领，于十五日离莫斯科前往乔治亚共和国作半月的访问。十三日苏联青年反法西斯委员会特设宴饯行。宾主极尽欢洽。
    共青真理报十四日以整版篇幅刊载了代表团团员的访苏观感和一些照片。该报报头上印有中苏两国国旗和中俄文的题字：“我们从你们的成就中看见了新中国的将来”。该报著论称：苏联的和中国的青年的会晤，表现了两国伟大人民之间的诚挚友谊。我们祝福我们的朋友在建设繁荣强大的民主中国的斗争中，获得新的胜利。
    十四日晚，在寒冷的夜色中，车站的月台上飘扬着一对对的中国和苏联的国旗，并有用俄文和中文并写着的标语：“世界劳动人民领袖斯大林同志万岁！”“中国人民领袖毛泽东同志万岁！”“中苏人民友谊万岁！”车站上拥挤着上千的欢送者，其中有苏联青年反法西斯委员会主席科齐马索夫、苏联共产主义青年团中央委员会书记赛米恰斯尼、中国驻苏大使馆参赞曾涌泉等。科齐马索夫在车站上致欢送词说：友谊的纽带联系着苏联的和中国的青年，他们团结一致，共同为争取世界和平而斗争。苏联青年祝中国青年在争取自由和世界和平的斗争中取得新的成就。中国青年代表团团长冯文彬致告别词。他对苏联人民、苏联青年和苏联共产主义青年团给中国代表团的热情招待和兄弟般的协助表示感谢。他强调说：“苏联人民，尤其是苏联青年，这样信心百倍地朝着共产主义前进，为世界和平而辛勤工作，是中国青年学习的榜样。中国青年永远是苏联青年诚恳的朋友。”
