Title: Personnel from Our Embassy in the Soviet Union Visit Lenin's Mausoleum; Pravda Publishes Chen Boda's Article Commemorating Lenin

Author:
Date: 1951-01-23
Page: 4

　　Personnel from Our Embassy in the Soviet Union Visit Lenin's Mausoleum
    Pravda Publishes Chen Boda's Article Commemorating Lenin
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 21st: Today is the 27th anniversary of Lenin's death. Zeng Yongquan, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, led embassy personnel to visit Lenin's Mausoleum in the morning and laid a wreath before the mausoleum.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 22nd: Pravda published Chen Boda's article commemorating the 27th anniversary of Lenin's death on the 21st: "Lenin is a great friend and teacher of the Chinese people."

### 我驻苏使馆人员谒列宁墓  真理报刊载陈伯达纪念列宁文

1951-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　我驻苏使馆人员谒列宁墓
    真理报刊载陈伯达纪念列宁文
    【新华社莫斯科二十一日电】今日为列宁逝世二十七周年纪念日。中国驻苏联大使馆曾涌泉参赞于上午率大使馆人员谒列宁墓，并在陵前献花圈。
    【新华社莫斯科二十二日电】真理报二十一日刊载了陈伯达纪念列宁逝世二十七周年的文章：“列宁是中国人民伟大的朋友和导师。”
