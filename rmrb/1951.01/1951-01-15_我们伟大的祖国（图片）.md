Author:
Date: 1951-01-15

    Our Great Motherland
    Tianjin is China's second-largest industrial and commercial city. Today marks the second anniversary of its liberation. This is a part of the Tianjin urban area, which is now more prosperous and beautiful than before liberation. The city now has more than 12,000 large and small factories and more than 28,000 stores.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    天津是中国第二个工商业大城市。今天是它解放的两周年。这是解放后较前更为繁盛和美丽的天津市区的一部分。全市现有大小工厂一万二千余家，商店二万八千余户。
