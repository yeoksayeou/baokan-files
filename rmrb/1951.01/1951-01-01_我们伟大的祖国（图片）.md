    Our Great Motherland
    Last year, agricultural production increased significantly throughout the country. This is one of the military products displayed at the Xinjiang Military and Civilian Production Exhibition—a large winter melon. It weighs seventy catties, and a Uyghur girl cannot lift it.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    去年全国各地农业增产得了很大的成绩。这是新疆省军民生产品展览会上陈列的部队产品之一——大冬瓜。它有七十斤重，一个维吾尔族的女孩子抱它不动。
