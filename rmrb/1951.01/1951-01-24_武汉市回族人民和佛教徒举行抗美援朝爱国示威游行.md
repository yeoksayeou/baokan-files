Title: Wuhan Hui People and Buddhists Hold Patriotic Demonstrations and Parades to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea

Author:
Date: 1951-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, January 23rd: More than 4,300 Hui people and Buddhists in Wuhan held separate demonstrations and parades on the 21st of this month to resist US aggression and aid Korea, protecting their homes and defending the country.

　　Over 1,800 Hui people came from Wuchang, Hanyang and other places to participate in the meeting that morning. At the meeting, Chen Jingshe, a Hui compatriot and Deputy Mayor of Wuhan, called on everyone to study hard, unite as one, and do a good job in various tasks. The meeting also passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and the Chinese People's Volunteers. After the meeting, a demonstration parade was held. The parade included an elderly team of more than 200 people, and 99-year-old Ma Fuqian also participated with a cane. He said: "For the sake of patriotism, we Hui people must not lag behind."

　　More than 2,500 Buddhists in the city also held a demonstration meeting on the same day to resist US aggression and aid Korea, protecting their homes and defending the country. The meeting passed a patriotic covenant of Buddhists, and a grand demonstration parade was held afterwards.

### 武汉市回族人民和佛教徒举行抗美援朝爱国示威游行

1951-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　武汉市回族人民和佛教徒举行抗美援朝爱国示威游行
    【新华社汉口二十三日电】武汉市回族人民和佛教徒四千三百余人于本月二十一日分别举行抗美援朝保家卫国示威大游行。
    回族人民一千八百余人，于是日上午从武昌、汉阳等地赶来参加大会。会上，回族同胞、武汉市副市长陈经畬号召大家努力学习，团结一致，做好各种工作。大会并通过向毛主席、中国人民志愿军致敬的电文。会毕，举行示威游行。游行行列中有二百余人组成的老人队，九十九岁高龄的马福谦也扶杖参加，他说：“为了爱国，我们回族人民决不能落后。”
    全市佛教徒二千五百余人，亦于同日举行抗美援朝保家卫国示威大会。会上通过了佛教徒的爱国公约，会后举行了盛大的示威游行。
