Title: Government Administration Council Holds 68th Meeting; Approves Reports from the Ministry of Railways and the Ministry of Textile Industry
Author:
Date: 1951-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 19th: The 68th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held at 4:00 PM on the 19th. The meeting listened to the report of Teng Taiyuan, Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Railways, on the ministry's 1950 work summary and 1951 plan, and the report of Qian Zhiguang, Deputy Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Textile Industry, on the National Textile Conference. After discussion, the meeting approved the above two reports. The meeting also passed several appointment cases and submitted them to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行六十八次会议  批准铁道部和纺织工业部的报告

1951-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行六十八次会议
    批准铁道部和纺织工业部的报告
    【新华社十九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第六十八次政务会议于十九日下午四时举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府铁道部滕代远部长所作的关于该部一九五零年工作总结与一九五一年计划的报告，以及中央人民政府纺织工业部钱之光副部长所作的关于全国纺织会议的报告后，讨论和批准了上述两个报告。会议并通过任命及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
