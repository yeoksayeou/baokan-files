Title: People of Various Countries Grandly Commemorate the 27th Anniversary of Lenin's Death; Envoys from People's Democratic Countries to the Soviet Union Visit Lenin's Mausoleum

Author:

Date: 1951-01-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd: According to TASS, Moscow: On January 21st, the 27th anniversary of Lenin's death, diplomatic envoys from Korea, Germany, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, and Mongolia to the Soviet Union visited Lenin's Mausoleum and laid wreaths.

Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd: Combined TASS reports: The people of Korea, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, Romania, Albania, and Mongolia grandly commemorated the 27th anniversary of Lenin's death. On January 21st, many newspapers in the people's democratic countries published editorials commemorating Lenin's death, and published photos and articles about Lenin's life and deeds.

In the towns and villages of the liberated areas of northern and southern Korea, rallies commemorating Lenin were held on the 20th. Seminars and photo exhibitions on Lenin's life and work were held in military organs, academic organs, and enterprises.

A grand rally commemorating Lenin was held in Berlin on the 21st. Wilhelm Pieck, Chairman of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and other party leaders attended the meeting. Eisler, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party, made a report on Lenin's life and activities, emphasizing that the Socialist Unity Party of Germany is advancing along the road of transforming the party into a Marxist-Leninist party.

Mass rallies and exhibitions on Lenin's life and activities were held in Warsaw, the capital of Poland, and other parts of Poland. The first Czechoslovak Congress for the Defence of Peace is in session, and a ceremony commemorating Lenin was held on the 21st. Commemorative rallies were held across Hungary on the 21st. Radio stations in Budapest, the capital of Hungary, organized special programs, and cinemas screened the famous films "Vladimir Ilyich Lenin" and "Lenin in 1918."

The Bulgarian newspaper "People's Youth" published an article stating: "The course of history has proved the correctness of Lenin's doctrine. When we commemorate Lenin, we express our unwavering loyalty to the great principles of Leninism." Romanian newspapers published many poems dedicated to Lenin. A rally commemorating Lenin was also held in Tirana, the capital of Albania, on the 21st.

Mass rallies commemorating Lenin were held in enterprises and organs throughout the Mongolian People's Republic. In many cities and villages, propagandists gave reports to workers and peasants about Lenin, about the great cooperation between Lenin and Stalin, and about the friendship between the Soviet people and the Mongolian people. The Unen newspaper published an editorial stating: Lenin's immortal ideas live forever and are always victorious.

Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd: Combined TASS reports: The people of France, the Netherlands, Austria, and Finland commemorated the 27th anniversary of Lenin's death on the 20th and 21st. The Seine Provincial Branch of the France-USSR Friendship Association held a commemorative meeting in front of the house where Lenin lived in Paris from 1909 to 1912. Professor Desay gave a speech at the meeting, congratulating the peaceful friendship between the French and Soviet people. The Sunday "L'Humanité" published an editorial commemorating Lenin and his struggle for peace, calling for the study of Lenin's doctrine. Commemorative rallies were held in major cities in the Netherlands. The Dutch Communist Party's newspaper "De Waarheid" published an article discussing the victorious realization of Lenin's ideals and plans in the Soviet Union. At the commemorative meeting held at the Tabor Cinema in Vienna, the capital of Austria, Austrian Communist Party Chairman Koreni delivered a report on Lenin's life and deeds, and emphasized the inevitable victory of communism. Commemorative rallies were held in various workers' residential areas in Vienna, and films such as "Lenin in October" and "The Man with the Gun" were screened.

The commemorative meeting held in Helsinki, the capital of Finland, was hosted by the Finland-USSR Friendship Association. The chairman of the association, Kilpi, gave a speech, pointing out the importance of Leninism to the struggle of working people in various countries for peace and democracy. After the meeting, the Soviet documentary "Vladimir Ilyich Lenin" was screened.

### 各国人民隆重纪念列宁逝世廿七周年  各人民民主国家驻苏使节曾谒列宁墓

1951-01-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　各国人民隆重纪念列宁逝世廿七周年
    各人民民主国家驻苏使节曾谒列宁墓
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：在二十一日列宁逝世二十七周年纪念日，朝鲜、德、波、捷、匈、罗、保、阿尔巴尼亚及蒙古等国驻苏联外交使节均往谒列宁陵墓，敬献花圈。
    【新华社二十三日讯】综合塔斯社消息：朝鲜、德意志民主共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚和蒙古等国人民，隆重纪念列宁逝世二十七周年。在二十一日这一天，各人民民主国家的许多报纸都发表了纪念列宁逝世的社论，刊载了有关列宁生平和事迹的照片和文章。
    在朝鲜北部和南部解放区的各城镇乡村中，二十日都举行了纪念列宁的集会。在军事机关、学术机关和各企业里，都举办了关于列宁生平及其事业的座谈会和照片展览会。
    柏林于二十一日举行了纪念列宁的盛大集会。德国统一社会党主席皮克和党的其他领袖都出席了大会。统一社会党中央委员会书记处书记艾斯勒在会上作了关于列宁生平及活动的报告，着重指出德国统一社会党正在沿着把党改造成一个马克思——列宁主义政党的道路前进。
    波兰首都华沙和波兰各地都举行纪念列宁的群众大会和关于列宁生平及活动的展览会。捷克斯洛伐克第一届保卫和平大会正在开会，二十一日举行了悼念列宁的仪式。匈牙利全国各地在二十一日举行了纪念大会。匈牙利首都布达佩斯的各广播电台都组织了特别节目、各电影院上演了名片“乌拉第米尔·伊里奇·列宁”和“列宁在一九一八年”。
    保加利亚“人民青年”报发表文章称：“历史的进程已证实列宁学说的正确性，我们在纪念列宁的时候表示我们对列宁主义伟大原则的忠实不渝”。罗马尼亚的报纸刊登了许多诗人献给列宁的诗篇。阿尔巴尼亚首都地拉那二十一日也举行了纪念列宁的大会。
    蒙古人民共和国的各企业和各机关都举行了纪念列宁的群众大会。在许多城市和乡村中，宣传员向广大的工人农民作了关于列宁、关于列宁和斯大林的伟大合作以及苏联人民和蒙古人民的友谊的报告。乌嫩报发表社论称：列宁的不朽思想永远活着，永远在胜利。
    【新华社二十三日讯】综合塔斯社消息：法、荷、奥、芬四国人民于二十、二十一日纪念了列宁逝世二十七周年。法苏友好协会塞纳省分会在列宁一九○九——一九一二年在巴黎住过的房子门前举行纪念大会。德赛教授曾在会上发表演说，祝贺法国与苏联人民之间的和平友好。星期日“人道报”发表了纪念列宁及其为和平而斗争的社论，号召学习列宁学说。荷兰各大城市均举行了纪念大会。荷兰共产党机关报“真理报”发表论文，论叙列宁的理想与计划在苏联胜利实现的情形。在奥京维也纳塔鲍尔电影院举行的纪念大会上，奥地利共产党主席柯伦尼发表了关于列宁生平事迹的报告，并着重指出共产主义必然胜利。维也纳各工人住宅区纷纷举行了纪念大会，并放映了《列宁在十月》及《带枪的人》等影片。
    在芬兰首都赫尔辛基举行的纪念会，由芬苏友好协会主持。协会主席基尔比发表演说，指出列宁主义对各国劳动人民争取和平与民主的斗争的重要意义。会后放映了苏联文献纪录片《乌拉第米尔·伊里奇·列宁》。
