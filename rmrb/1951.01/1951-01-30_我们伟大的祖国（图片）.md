    Our Great Motherland
    This is a view of Mount Hua, the Western Mountain of the famous Five Great Mountains of China. Mount Hua is located south of Huayin County, Shaanxi Province. This is one of the scenic spots on the mountain, popularly known as: "Where Lao Tzu Played Chess".

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是我国著名的五岳中的西岳华山的风景。华山在陕西省华阴县以南。这是山中名胜之一，俗传为：“老君奕棋处”。
