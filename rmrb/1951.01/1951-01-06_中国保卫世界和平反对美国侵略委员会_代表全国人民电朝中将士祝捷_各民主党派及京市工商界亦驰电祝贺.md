Title: China Peace Committee, Opposing American Aggression, Sends Telegram to Korean and Chinese Soldiers to Celebrate Victory; Democratic Parties and Beijing Business Community Also Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1951-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 5th—The China Peace Committee, Opposing American Aggression, today sent a telegram to all officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, congratulating them on the liberation of Seoul. The telegram reads as follows:

To all the heroic officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army:

We are delighted to hear of the liberation of Seoul, the capital of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. On behalf of the entire Chinese people, we extend our warm congratulations and lofty respect to you!

Your great victory has not only fundamentally changed the situation in Korea, saving millions of Korean people from the abyss of suffering, but also seriously struck a blow at the US imperialist world aggression plan, giving infinite confidence and courage to peace-loving people all over the world.

We wish you continued progress in liberating the whole of Korea and eliminating all aggressors in Korea!

Long live the great Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army!

Eternal glory to the martyrs of China and Korea who sacrificed themselves for the liberation of Korea!

                                China Peace Committee, Opposing American Aggression
                                                  January 5, 1951

[This newspaper reports] The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Democratic National Construction Association, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the China Zhi Gong Party, and the September 3rd Society have respectively sent telegrams to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, congratulating them on the great victory in the recovery of Seoul. The congratulatory telegram from the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang stated: "The joyful news of the recovery of Seoul has been transmitted, and the entire Chinese people are rejoicing and celebrating. We extend to you our highest congratulations and regards. The people of all Asia, as well as peace-loving people all over the world, are strengthening their confidence and determination to fight for people's democracy and lasting world peace because of your heroic fighting spirit and great victory in severely damaging the enemy." The congratulatory telegram from the China Democratic National Construction Association stated: "This victory is not only a victory for the people of China and Korea, but also a victory for the people of the world." The telegram from the China Association for Promoting Democracy stated: "Your successive brilliant victories have completely shattered the US imperialist aggression plan, proving once again that your bravery and strength are fully capable of defeating the US aggressor army." The telegram from the China Zhi Gong Party stated: "This fully proves the strength of the people's power, and that US imperialism is nothing but a paper tiger." The congratulatory telegram from the September 3rd Society encouraged the Chinese and Korean soldiers, saying: "Glorious soldiers: Forward, forward, more bravely forward, drive the US aggressors who are unrepentant until death into the sea! Let us cheer for you and extend our highest respect."

[Also Reported] The Beijing business community's Peace Committee, Opposing American Aggression, has recently sent a telegram to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to congratulate them on the great victory in the recovery of Seoul. The telegram stated: The Beijing industrialists and businessmen cordially congratulate you on your victory, and are determined to expand and strengthen our practical actions to resist US aggression and aid Korea, and are even more prepared to welcome and celebrate the arrival of your and the Korean People's Army's joint liberation of the whole of Korea!

### 中国保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会  代表全国人民电朝中将士祝捷  各民主党派及京市工商界亦驰电祝贺

1951-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
    代表全国人民电朝中将士祝捷
    各民主党派及京市工商界亦驰电祝贺
    【新华社五日讯】中国保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会，今日致电朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军全体将士，祝贺汉城解放。电文如下：朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的全体英勇将士们：
    欣闻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首都汉城解放，谨代表全中国人民向你们致以热烈的祝贺和崇高的敬意！
    你们所得到的伟大胜利，不但根本转变了朝鲜的形势，挽救了千百万朝鲜人民于水深火热的灾难之中，而且严重地打击了美帝国主义的世界侵略计划，使全世界爱好和平的人类增加了无限的信心和勇气。
    祝贺你们继续前进，解放全朝鲜，消灭一切在朝鲜的侵略者！
    伟大的朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军万岁！
    为朝鲜解放事业而牺牲的中朝两国烈士们永垂不朽！
                                中国保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
                                                  一九五一年一月五日
    【本报讯】中国国民党革命委员会、民主建国会、中国民主促进会、中国致公党及九三学社分别致电朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军，祝贺光复汉城大捷。中国国民党革命委员会的贺电称：“光复汉城，喜讯传来，全中国人民欢欣鼓舞，特向你们致崇高的祝贺与敬礼。全亚洲人民以及全世界爱好和平的人民，也都为你们英勇的战斗精神和重创敌人的伟大胜利而加强了为人民民主和世界持久和平而斗争的信心与决心。”民主建国会的贺电里说：“这一个胜利不仅是中朝两国人民的胜利，而且是全世界人民的胜利。”中国民主促进会电称：“你们接二连三的辉煌胜利，彻底粉碎了美国帝国主义的侵略计划，又一次证明你们的英勇和强大，完全有力量打垮美国侵略军。”中国致公党电称：“这充分证明了人民力量的强大，美帝国主义纸老虎是毫不足惧的。”九三学社的贺电中激励中朝战士说：“光荣的战士们：前进，前进，更英勇地前进，把至死不悟的美国侵略军赶下海去！让我们向你们欢呼并致最崇高的敬礼。”
    【又讯】北京市工商界保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会，顷致电中国人民援朝志愿军祝贺光复汉城大捷。电文称：北京工商业者热诚地向你们祝捷，决心扩大和加强我们的抗美援朝实际行动，更准备迎接和庆祝你们和朝鲜人民军共同解放全朝鲜的最大胜利的到来！
