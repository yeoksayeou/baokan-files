Title: Celebrating the Liberation of Seoul, Resolutely Resist America and Aid Korea: Chongqing Catholics March

Author:
Date: 1951-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 8th. More than 400 priests, monks, and followers from the Zhenyuan Church, Ruose Church, Ren'ai Church, and Sacred Heart Church in Chongqing held a march on the 7th to celebrate the liberation of Seoul. They wore black religious vestments during the march. Before the march, Father Yin Saming, the Bishop of the Wanzhou Diocese of the Catholic Church, who had just arrived in Chongqing from Wanzhou, presided over a High Mass at Ruose Church to pray for peace. The marching crowd set off firecrackers along the way, shouting slogans such as "Long live Chairman Mao," "Long live General Kim Il-sung," and "Resolutely support the just action of resisting America and aiding Korea."

### 庆祝汉城解放坚决抗美援朝  重庆天主教徒游行

1951-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝汉城解放坚决抗美援朝
    重庆天主教徒游行
    【新华社重庆八日电】重庆市天主教真原堂、若瑟堂、仁爱堂、圣心堂等单位的神父、修道士及教徒四百余人于七日举行庆祝汉城解放的游行。他们在游行时穿着黑色的教衣。在游行前曾在若瑟堂请才由万县抵重庆的天主教万县教区主教殷萨明主持作大弥撒，祈祷和平。游行队伍沿途燃放鞭炮，高呼“毛主席万岁”、“金日成将军万岁”和“坚决拥护抗美援朝的正义行动”等口号。
