Title: International and National Trade Union Women's Organizations Exchange New Year's Greetings with All-China Federation of Trade Unions and All-China Women's Federation
Author:
Date: 1951-01-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: On the eve of the New Year, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent New Year's greetings to trade unions in the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Free Germany, Albania, France, Italy, the Latin American Workers' Confederation, and the Anglo-Chinese Friendship Association. They wished them and all the working people of their countries a happy new year and congratulated them on the great achievements of the workers' movement in the past year. The congratulatory message wished and affirmed that the strength of the world peace and democracy camp headed by the great Marshal Stalin would become even stronger. The working class of the whole world will unite closely under the leadership of the World Federation of Trade Unions and achieve greater success in the struggle to defend world peace and oppose wars of aggression in 1951, in the cause of the Soviet people building a communist society, in the cause of building socialist economies in the people's democratic countries, and in the cause of liberating the working class itself and defending the interests of the working class.

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the World Federation of Trade Unions to congratulate them on the New Year. The telegram reads as follows: To Comrade Vittorio, Chairman of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and Comrade Saillant, General Secretary:

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on behalf of the workers of the whole country, extends New Year's greetings to the World Federation of Trade Unions, and wishes the workers of the whole world greater victories in the cause of fighting for lasting world peace and their own liberation under the leadership of the World Federation of Trade Unions in 1951.

All-China Federation of Trade Unions
December 31, 1950

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: On the eve of New Year's Day in 1951, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions successively received New Year's greetings from trade unions in the Soviet Union, Poland, Romania, Albania, Mongolia, and Siam to the working class and people of our country. The International Transport Workers' Union, the Central Management Committee of the Bulgarian Education, Printing and Political Workers' Union, the Central Committee of the Polish Textile Workers' Union, and the Hungarian Agricultural Workers' Union also sent telegrams to our country's Railway Union, Education Union, Textile Union, and the National Laboring Peasants to congratulate them. These telegrams all wished the Chinese working class victory and success in the cause of defending world peace and strengthening the economic construction of New China in 1951.

This newspaper reports: Around New Year's Day in 1951, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation successively received congratulatory messages to Chinese women from Mrs. Gotodan, Chairman of the International Federation of Women, Vice Chairman Ibarruri, Zora Dragoycheva, Chairman of the Bulgarian Democratic Women's Federation, the Romanian Democratic Women's Federation, the Albanian Women's Federation, and the Central Council of Working Women of the Mongolian People's Republic. The telegrams all unanimously congratulated the Chinese people on the great achievements they had made in 1950, and wished all the Chinese people and women greater victories in the cause of fighting for the motherland and for world peace and democracy for the people of the world.

### 国际及各国工会妇女组织与我全总及妇联互贺新年

1951-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际及各国工会妇女组织与我全总及妇联互贺新年
    【新华社三日讯】中华全国总工会在新年前夕分别向苏联、波兰、捷克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、自由德国、阿尔巴尼亚、法国、意大利等国工会及拉丁美洲工人联盟、英中友好协会发出新年贺电。祝贺他们和他们国家的全体劳动人民新年快乐，并祝贺过去一年来工人运动的伟大成就。贺电中预祝并确信以伟大的斯大林大元帅领导的苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营力量将更加强大。全世界的工人阶级将在世界工联领导下紧密团结起来，在一九五一年的保卫世界和平反对侵略战争的斗争中，在苏联人民建设共产主义社会以及在人民民主国家建设社会主义经济的事业中，在解放工人阶级自身以及为保卫工人阶级利益而斗争的事业中，取得更伟大的成就。
    【新华社三日讯】中华全国总工会致电世界工联祝贺新年，电文如下：世界工会联合会主席维多里奥，总书记赛扬同志：
    中华全国总工会代表全国工人向世界工联致新年的祝贺，并祝全世界工人一九五一年将在世界工联领导下为世界持久和平与自己解放而斗争的事业获得更大的胜利。
                                                    中华全国总工会
                                          一九五○年十二月三十一日
    【新华社三日讯】中华全国总工会于一九五一年元旦前夕，先后接到苏联、波兰、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古、暹罗等国职工会向我国工人阶级及全国人民祝贺新年的贺电。陆空运输工人工会国际、保加利亚教育、印刷与政治工作者工人中央管理委员会、波兰纺织工会中央委员会、匈牙利农业工人联合会也分电我国铁路工会、教育工会、纺织工会以及全国劳动农民祝贺。这些电文都预祝中国工人阶级在一九五一年保卫世界和平和加强新中国的经济建设事业中获得胜利与成功。
    【本报讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会于一九五一年元旦前后陆续收到国际妇联主席戈登夫人，副主席伊巴露丽，保加利亚民主妇联主席左拉·德拉哥郁捷娃，罗马尼亚民主妇女联合会，阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会及蒙古人民共和国劳动妇女中央理事会等致中国妇女的贺电。电文中都一致庆贺中国人民在一九五○年所获得的伟大成就，并预祝全中国人民与妇女在为祖国、为全世界人民和平民主而斗争的事业中，取得更大的胜利。
