Title: Xinjiang Industrial Delegation Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-01-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Xinjiang Industrial Delegation, composed of representatives of the Uyghur, Tatar, Han, Sibe, and Hui ethnic groups from Xinjiang Province, arrived in Beijing on the 27th of last month and will soon visit various industrial departments in Beijing. The delegation of nineteen people, led by head Abula Maihesumu and deputy head Aji Hali (both Uyghur), departed from Dihua on November 11th and traveled to the mainland via Lanzhou and Xi'an for a visit.

### 新疆工业访问团来京访问

1951-01-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　新疆工业访问团来京访问
    【新华社四日讯】新疆省维吾尔族、塔塔尔族、汉族、锡伯族、回族代表合组的新疆工业访问团，已于上月二十七日抵京，即将访问北京市各工业部门。该团一行十九人，由团长阿不拉买合苏木、副团长阿吉哈里（均系维族）率领，于十一月十一日由迪化出发，经兰州、西安来内地访问。
