Title: Chongqing, Kunming, Qingdao, and Other Cities Impose Controls on American Enterprises
Author:
Date: 1951-01-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Chongqing, Kunming, Qingdao, and other cities' Military Control Commissions have successively implemented controls on American enterprises in these cities and frozen American deposits in public and private banks. On January 11th, the Chongqing Military Control Commission announced the implementation of controls over the offices of two American oil companies, Mobil and Texaco, and eight film companies, MGM, Warner Bros., Columbia, 20th Century Fox, United Artists, Universal, RKO, and Paramount, and appointed military control commissioners. Public and private banks in the city have also frozen American public and private deposits. Employees of American enterprises in the city warmly support this measure by the government. Hu Yingcen, the Chinese person in charge of Mobil Oil Company, and Wu Qide, the person in charge of Texaco, both expressed their firm support for the decrees of the People's Government and will submit an inventory of the assets and deposits of the two companies within days. Yang Huiqing and other workers at the 20th Century Fox office even actively assisted government personnel in verifying the company's assets in Chongqing.

On December 28th of last year, the Kunming Military Control Commission froze American public and private deposits in various banks in the city and conducted a preliminary inventory of the former U.S. Consulate's buildings and the real estate and warehouses of the American company, Mobil Oil.

On January 5th, the Qingdao Military Control Commission issued a proclamation ordering four American enterprises in the city, Texaco, Mobil, Mellon, and Zmei, to report all their properties in a list.

### 重庆昆明青岛等市对美国企业实施管制

1951-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　重庆昆明青岛等市对美国企业实施管制
    【新华社十六日讯】重庆、昆明、青岛等市军事管制委员会，已先后对在这些城市的美国企业实行管制，并冻结了公私银行中的美国存款。重庆市军事管制委员会于一月十一日宣布对该市美商美孚、德士古两石油公司及米高梅、华纳、哥伦比亚、二十世纪福克斯、联美、环球、雷电华、派拉蒙等八家影片公司在该市的办事处分别实行管制，并任命了军事管制专员。该市各公私营银行也已将美国公私存款予以冻结。该市各美商企业职工们对政府这一措施都热烈拥护，美孚石油公司华籍负责人胡英岑与德士古公司负责人吴其德均表示坚决拥护人民政府法令，日内即将该两公司之资产及存款造具清册呈报。二十世纪福克斯公司办事处的工人杨惠清等更积极协助政府工作人员清查该公司在重庆的资产。
    昆明市军事管制委员会于去年十二月二十八日即对该市各银行所存的美国公私存款实行冻结，并对前美国领事馆房舍及美商美孚石油公司所有房地仓库进行初步清查。
    青岛市军事管制委员会于一月五日发出布告，饬令该市德士古、美孚、美隆、兹美等四家美国企业将所有财产造表呈报。
