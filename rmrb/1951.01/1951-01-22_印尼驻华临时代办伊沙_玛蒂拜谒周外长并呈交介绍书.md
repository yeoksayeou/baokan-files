Title: Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Isa Matari, calls on Foreign Minister Zhou and presents credentials

Author:
Date: 1951-01-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th—The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Republic to China, Isa Matari, called on Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 5 p.m. today and presented his credentials. Also present were: Chen Jiakang, Acting Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 印尼驻华临时代办伊沙·玛蒂拜谒周外长并呈交介绍书

1951-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　印尼驻华临时代办伊沙·玛蒂拜谒周外长并呈交介绍书
    【新华社二十日讯】印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂于今日下午五时赴外交部拜会周恩来部长，并呈交其介绍书。在座者尚有：外交部亚洲司代司长陈家康及交际处处长王倬如。
