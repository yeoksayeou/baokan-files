    Our Great Motherland
    Tiananmen Square in Beijing is one of the great buildings created by the Chinese working people with historical significance and artistic value. The founding ceremony of the People's Republic of China was held here. Its beautiful image constitutes an important part of our national emblem.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京天安门，是中国劳动人民所创造的富有历史意义和艺术价值的伟大建筑物之一。中华人民共和国的开国典礼就在这里举行。它的美丽的形象构成了我国国徽的重要部分。
