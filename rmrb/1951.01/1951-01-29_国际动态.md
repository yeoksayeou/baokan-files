　　International News
  Four Polish Students Arrive in Beijing
　　Four exchange students from the People's Republic of Poland, namely Dongbrovsky, Yankovska, Stun, and Stefansky, arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 25th. Representatives from the Higher Education Department of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, the All-China Student Federation, and the Student Department of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League welcomed them. They were also invited to a banquet by the Ministry of Education that evening. The four Polish students will soon begin their studies at Tsinghua University.
  Vietnam Issues National Bonds
　　The Democratic Republic of Vietnam has decided to issue national bonds in order to gather the vast financial resources dispersed among the people to intensify production, develop industry, assist in the war of liberation, and lay the foundation for a people's democratic economic system. Vietnamese Minister of Finance Le Van Hien issued a statement to all the Vietnamese people on this matter, saying: Completing this plan will expand the current military victories on the northern border front to accelerate preparations for a general counter-offensive. Completing this plan will consolidate national finances and develop the economy for the benefit of all the people.
  Vavilov's Mourning
　　The funeral of Vavilov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, was held in Moscow on the 27th. Thousands of Soviet scientists, doctors, and representatives from various agencies, organizations, and schools paid their last respects to the remains in the Hall of Columns of the Trade Union Building before escorting the coffin to the burial site. The memorial service was held outdoors. Khrushchev, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Secretary of the Moscow City Committee, and Yasnov, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet, delivered eulogies successively. After the meeting, a three-gun salute was fired, and the coffin was slowly lowered into the tomb to the sound of the Soviet national anthem.
  Syrian Students Anti-Imperialist Demonstration
　　Thousands of university and middle school students in Damascus, Syria, held demonstrations to protest the conspiracies of American and British imperialists to drag Syria and other Arab countries into the vortex of a new war. Demonstrators held up banners and shouted slogans: "We demand Syria remain neutral!" "Down with imperialist conspiracies!" "No Western countries are allowed to open bases in Syria!"

### 国际动态

1951-01-29
第4版()
专栏：国际动态

　　国际动态
  波兰四个留学生抵北京
    波兰人民共和国和我国交换的留学生东勃罗夫斯基、扬可夫斯卡、斯敦、斯蒂凡斯基等四人，已于二十五日上午到达北京，中央人民政府教育部高等教育司、中华全国学生联合会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会学生部均有代表前往欢迎。当晚并受到教育部宴会招待。四位波兰留学生即将入清华大学学习。
  越南发行国家公债
    越南民主共和国已决定发行国家公债，以集合分散在人民手中的巨大财源来加紧生产、发展工业，帮助进行解放战争，并为人民民主主义的经济制度奠定基础。越南财政部长黎文献曾就此事向全体越南人民发表声明说：完成这一计划就可扩大目前北部边境战线上的军事胜利，以加速准备总反攻。完成这一计划，就可巩固国家财政，为全体人民的利益发展经济。
  瓦维洛夫的哀荣
    苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫的葬仪于二十七日在莫斯科举行。苏联科学家、医生及各机关、团体、学校的代表数千人在停灵的工会大厦圆柱大厅最后瞻仰遗容后，扶送灵柩前往墓地。追悼大会在露天举行。苏联共产党中央委员会书记及莫斯科市委员会书记赫鲁舍切夫、莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席雅斯诺夫相继致词。会后鸣放礼炮三响，在苏联国歌声中，灵观徐徐降入墓穴。
  叙利亚学生反帝示威
    叙利亚的大马士革成千的大、中学生举行示威游行，抗议美英帝国主义者们企图把叙利亚和其他阿拉伯国家拉入新战争漩涡的阴谋计划。示威群众高举着标语牌，高呼口号：“我们要求叙利亚保持中立！”“打倒帝国主义的阴谋诡计！”“不准西方国家在叙利亚开辟基地！”
