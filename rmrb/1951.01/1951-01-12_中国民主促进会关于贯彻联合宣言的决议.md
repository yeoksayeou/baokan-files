Title: Resolution of the China Association for Promoting Democracy on Implementing the Joint Declaration
Author:
Date: 1951-01-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, January 8th

　　The following is the full text of the "Resolution on How to Implement the Joint Declaration of the Democratic Parties of November 4th" passed by the Second Plenary Session of the Third Central Council of the China Association for Promoting Democracy, held in Beijing from November 23rd to December 1st last year:

　　The Second Plenary Session of the Third Central Council of the China Association for Promoting Democracy heard the political report of Chairman Ma Xulun, unanimously agreed with and supported the spirit of the report; unanimously agreed that: we should mobilize all forces to resolutely support and implement the Joint Declaration of the Democratic Parties of November 4th (1950), to actively assist the fraternal Korean people with voluntary actions, to resolutely fight to the end against the aggression of American imperialism against Korea and China, to defend the sacred territory of our motherland, to liberate Taiwan, and to defend lasting world peace. In order to achieve this task, the following resolutions are made:

　　First, we must strengthen the study of current events and the research of progressive theories, so as to improve ideological understanding, stimulate our patriotism and internationalism, arouse our hatred, contempt, and disdain for American imperialism, and strengthen our confidence in the inevitable victory of opposing aggression and defending peace.

　　Second, we must strengthen the work of propaganda and education, enhance national self-esteem and self-confidence, expose and combat the pro-American ideas of a few people, conduct in-depth persuasion and explanation work on the ideas of worshipping America, fearing America, and seeking temporary ease, and call on the people of the whole country to concentrate all their efforts to fight for the sacred task of resisting America, aiding Korea, defending our homes, and defending our country.

　　Third, we must consolidate internal unity, strengthen close cooperation with friendly parties, and, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, consolidate and expand the people's democratic united front.

　　Fourth, in all our work, we must strengthen unity, strive for progressive activists with mass influence, as the core of our propaganda and education work, to unite, educate the broad masses of people, and expand the mass base for our support of the joint declaration of the democratic parties.

　　Fifth, we must implement the joint declaration of the democratic parties with active actions, and contribute all our possible strength to provide the greatest support to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers in resisting America and aiding Korea. Our comrades should redouble their efforts in their respective work positions to fight for the defense of the motherland and world peace.

### 中国民主促进会关于贯彻联合宣言的决议

1951-01-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国民主促进会关于贯彻联合宣言的决议
    【新华社八日讯】去年十一月二十三日至十二月一日在北京召开的中国民主促进会中央理事会全体会议通过的“关于如何贯彻各民主党派十一月四日联合宣言的决议”，全文如下：
    中国民主促进会第三届中央理事会第二次全体会议，听取了马叙伦主席的政治报告，一致同意并拥护报告的精神；一致认为：我们应动员一切力量，坚决拥护和贯彻十一月四日（一九五○年）各民主党派的联合宣言，以志愿行动积极援助朝鲜人民兄弟，为抗击美帝国主义对朝鲜和中国的侵略，为保卫我们祖国的神圣疆土、解放台湾，为保卫世界持久和平而坚决奋斗到底。为实现这一任务，作如下的决议：
    第一、我们必须加强时事的学习和进步理论的研讨，以提高思想认识，激发我们的爱国主义与国际主义的热情，唤起我们对美帝国主义的仇视、蔑视和鄙视，坚定我们反对侵略、保卫和平必胜的信心。
    第二、我们必须加强宣传教育的工作，提高民族自尊心和自信心，揭露并打击少数人的亲美思想，对崇美、恐美和苟安思想进行深入的说服解释工作，并号召全国人民，集中一切力量为抗美援朝保家卫国的神圣任务而奋斗。
    第三、我们必须巩固内部团结，加强与各友党间的密切合作，在中国共产党领导下，巩固与扩大人民民主统一战线。
    第四、我们必须在一切工作中，加强团结，争取进步的有群众影响的积极分子，作为我们展开宣传教育工作的核心，去团结、教育广大人民，扩大我们拥护各民主党派联合宣言的群众基础。
    第五、我们必须以积极行动来贯彻各民主党派的联合宣言，对朝鲜人民军和中国人民抗美援朝的志愿部队，应贡献我们一切可能的力量，予以最大支援。我们的同志更应在各自的工作岗位上加倍努力，为保卫祖国和世界和平而斗争。
