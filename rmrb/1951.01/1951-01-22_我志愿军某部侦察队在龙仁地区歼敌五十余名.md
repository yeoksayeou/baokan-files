Title: Our Volunteer Army Reconnaissance Detachment Annihilates Over Fifty Enemies in the Yongin Area
Author:
Date: 1951-01-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Our correspondent on the Korean front reports: At 1500 hours on the 18th, a special force company of the U.S. invading army's 3rd Infantry Division, numbering about 150 men, in nine vehicles and accompanied by three tanks, raided the Yongin (Kimnyangjang-ri) area. Our Chinese People's Volunteer Army reconnaissance detachment dealt them a head-on blow. After two hours of fierce fighting, our Chinese People's Volunteer Army achieved the following results: over fifty enemies killed or wounded, one enemy captured, and the remaining enemies fled south in defeat. Seven vehicles, three mortars, five light and heavy machine guns, one radio, and other weapons, ammunition, and military supplies were seized.

### 我志愿军某部侦察队在龙仁地区歼敌五十余名

1951-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　我志愿军某部侦察队在龙仁地区歼敌五十余名
    【新华社二十日讯】本社朝鲜前线记者报道：十八日十五时，美国侵略军步兵第三师特种兵连约一百五十余人，乘汽车九辆，附坦克三辆，向龙仁（金良场里）一带窜扰。我人民志愿军某部侦察队给以迎头痛击，经两小时激战，我人民志愿军所获战果如下：毙、伤敌五十余名，俘敌一名，残敌向南溃逃。缴获汽车七辆、迫击炮三门、轻重机枪五挺、报话机一部及其他武器弹药军用品一部。
