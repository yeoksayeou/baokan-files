Title: Sports Exhibition Opens at the Palace of Culture
Author:
Date: 1951-01-01
Page: 4
　　The Beijing Municipal Sports Association will hold a "Sports Exhibition" at the Three Great Halls of the Working People's Palace of Culture from December 31, 1950, to January 11, 1951. The opening hours are from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm daily. Welcome to visit.

### 体育展览在文化宫揭幕

1951-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　体育展览在文化宫揭幕
    北京市体育分会于一九五○年十二月三十一日至一九五一年一月十一日在劳动人民文化宫三殿举行“体育展览”。每日开放时间，由上午九时至下午五时。欢迎参观。
