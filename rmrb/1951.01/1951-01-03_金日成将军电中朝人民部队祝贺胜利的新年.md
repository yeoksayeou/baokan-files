Title: General Kim Il-sung Sends New Year Greetings to Chinese and Korean People's Forces Congratulating Them on Their Victory
Author:
Date: 1951-01-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 2nd: Pyongyang Radio recently broadcast a New Year's congratulatory telegram from Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers.

General Kim Il-sung's congratulatory telegram to all commanders and fighters of the Korean People's Army said: In 1951—a new year of heroic fighting and new victories—on behalf of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Supreme Command of the Korean People's Army, and myself, I extend warm congratulations to all commanders and fighters of the People's Army who are heroically fighting for the unification, independence, honor, and freedom of the fatherland. I firmly believe that we will win new victories and new achievements in the patriotic liberation war.

General Kim Il-sung's congratulatory telegram to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers said: On behalf of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Supreme Command of the Korean People's Army, and myself, I extend warm congratulations to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers who are heroically assisting Korea and resolutely resisting the aggression of American imperialism. I firmly believe that in the struggle against the common enemy of the Korean and Chinese people, American imperialists, we will achieve greater and greater victories.

### 金日成将军电中朝人民部队祝贺胜利的新年

1951-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　金日成将军
    电中朝人民部队祝贺胜利的新年
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社平壤一日电：平壤电台顷广播朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首相兼人民军总司令金日成将军致朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的新年贺电。
    金日成将军致朝鲜人民军全体指挥员和战斗员的贺电说：在一九五一年——英勇地战斗的和获致新的胜利的新年，我代表朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府、朝鲜人民军总司令部和我本人，向正在为祖国的统一、独立、荣誉和自由而英勇作战的人民军全体指挥员与战斗员，致热烈的祝贺。我坚决相信：我们在爱国的解放战争中，必将赢得新的胜利和新的成就。
    金日成将军致中国人民志愿军的全体指挥员与战斗员的贺电说：我代表朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府、朝鲜人民军总司令部和我本人，向正在英勇地援助朝鲜、果敢地抵抗美帝国主义侵略的中国人民志愿军全体指挥员与战斗员，致热烈的祝贺。我坚决相信：在反对朝鲜人民和中国人民的共同敌人美国帝国主义者的斗争中，我们必将取得愈来愈大的胜利。
