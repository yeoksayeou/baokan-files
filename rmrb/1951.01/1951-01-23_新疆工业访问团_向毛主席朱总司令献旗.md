Title: Xinjiang Industrial Delegation Presents Flag to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author:

Date: 1951-01-23
Page: 1

　　Xinjiang Industrial Delegation
    Presents Flag to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu
    [Xinhua News Agency, 22nd] The Xinjiang Industrial Delegation presented a flag, gifts, and a dedication to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu this afternoon, which were accepted by Secretary-General Lin Boqu of the Central People's Government on their behalf. The gifts included leather clothing, leather suitcases, carpets, and valuable agricultural and pastoral products from Xinjiang. After the presentation, Secretary-General Lin invited all members of the delegation to a tea party to discuss issues related to the development of Xinjiang's industry.
    The delegation, consisting of sixteen members, is composed of representatives from five ethnic groups: Uyghur, Tatar, Han, Xibe, and Hui. They arrived in Beijing at the end of November last year and have visited various factories in the city.

### 新疆工业访问团  向毛主席朱总司令献旗

1951-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　新疆工业访问团
    向毛主席朱总司令献旗
    【新华社二十二日讯】新疆工业访问团今日下午向毛主席和朱总司令献旗、献礼和献词，由中央人民政府林伯渠秘书长代表接受。该团所献礼品中有皮衣、皮箱、地毡及新疆珍贵的农牧业品等。礼毕，由林秘书长邀请该团各团员举行茶会，座谈关于发展新疆工业的问题。
    该访问团共十六人，由维吾尔族、塔塔尔族、汉族、锡伯族和回族等五个民族的代表组成，去年十一月底抵京，曾参观了本市的各工厂。
