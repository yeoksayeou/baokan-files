Title: Soviet Publishing House Publishes Translation of "On Practice"
Author:
Date: 1951-01-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 13th -- TASS reports: Chairman Mao Zedong's work "On Practice" has been published in Russian as a separate volume by the Soviet State Publishing House for Political Literature. The translator is Fedorenko, and the first printing of the separate volume is 50,000 copies.

### 苏出版“实践论”译本

1951-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏出版“实践论”译本
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社讯：毛泽东主席的著作“实践论”已由苏联国立政治读物出版局印成俄文单行本问世，译者为费多林科，单行本第一次印行五万册。
