Title: Germany Celebrates Pieck's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1951-01-05
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, 4th: According to the German Democratic Republic News Agency: Representatives of the people of the German Democratic Republic, government personnel, and foreign guests gathered at the Presidential Residence in Berlin on January 3rd to congratulate President Pieck on his seventy-fifth birthday.

    The members of the Political Bureau of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, led by Grotewohl and Ulbricht, and the Chairman of the German Communist Party, Lehmann, were the first to offer their congratulations to the President.

    Chairman Grotewohl of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany read out the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, which pointed out the President's great contributions in the struggle to defend the vital interests of the German people.

    In his reply, the President pointed out that the work of building the German Democratic Republic has achieved great success, which is the result of the hard work of all the people. We will solve new and important tasks, which is to ensure peace and achieve the unity and independence of Germany. The German people maintain close friendship with the Soviet Union and are able to accomplish these tasks. If the aggressors attempt to launch a new war, the peace-loving German people will stand together with the Soviet Union.

    The Soviet government delegation, composed of Kuznetsov, Pospelov, and Pushkin, head of the Soviet diplomatic mission to the German Democratic Republic, was the first to offer congratulations to President Pieck. Then, the head of the People's Republic of China delegation, Ji Pengfei, and representatives from various people's democracies, as well as representatives from Italy, Spain, Austria, Britain, France, and other countries, successively offered their congratulations to President Pieck.

    Government officials of the German Democratic Republic, led by Grotewohl, and members of the Presidium of the People's Chamber, led by Dr. Dieckmann and Robe Danz, congratulated the President on behalf of the people of the whole country.

    General Chuikov, Chairman of the Soviet Control Commission in Germany, along with Semichastnov, Semenov, and others, congratulated the President on behalf of the Control Commission.

    Jaso Grube conveyed the congratulations of the Evangelical Church to President Pieck.

### 德国欢庆皮克寿辰

1951-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　德国欢庆皮克寿辰
    【新华社四日讯】据德意志民主共和国新闻处报道：德意志民主共和国人民代表、政府人员和外国来宾于一月三日在柏林总统官邸集会，祝贺皮克总统的七十五岁寿辰。
    以格罗提渥和乌布利希为首的德国统一社会党政治局委员和德国共产党主席雷曼首先向总统致贺。
    德国统一社会党主席格罗提渥宣读德国统一社会党中央委员会的贺词，其中指出总统在保卫德国人民的切身利益的斗争中的伟大功勋。
    总统致答词时指出，建设德意志民主共和国的工作已经取得了巨大的成就，这是全体人民努力工作的成果。我们将要解决新的重大任务，这就是必须保证和平与获致德国的统一和独立。德国人民与苏联保持着密切的友谊，是能够完成这些任务的。如果侵略者企图发动新的战争，爱好和平的德国人民将和苏联站在一起。由库兹涅佐夫、波斯伯洛夫与苏联驻德意志民主共和国外交使团团长普式金组成的苏联政府代表团首先向皮克总统致贺。接着，中华人民共和国代表姬鹏飞使团长和各人民民主国家的代表以及来自意大利、西班牙、奥地利、英国、法国及其他国家的代表都相继向皮克总统致贺。
    以格罗提渥为首的德意志民主共和国政府官员、以狄克曼与罗贝丹兹博士为首的人民议会参议会主席团的委员代表全国人民向总统祝贺。
    苏联对德管制委员会主席崔可夫将军偕赛米恰斯特诺夫、塞米诺夫及其他等人代表管制委员会向总统贺寿。
    亚索·格鲁柏向皮克总统转达了福音会的贺意。
