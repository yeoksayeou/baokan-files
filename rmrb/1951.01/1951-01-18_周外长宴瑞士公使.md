Title: Foreign Minister Zhou Hosts Swiss Envoy
Author:
Date: 1951-01-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet at 7:00 PM today for Swiss Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Charles-Edouard de Bavier, as well as the embassy's Counselor, Mr. A. de Steiner, and Second Secretary, Mr. R. Lemaître. Invited to accompany him were Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Director of the General Office of the Foreign Ministry Wang Bingnan, Director of the Central People's Government Bureau of Protocol Yu Xinqing, Director of the European and African Affairs Department of the Foreign Ministry Huan Xiang, Deputy Director Zhang Yue, and Chief of the Reception Office Wang Zhuoru, among others.

Following the presentation of his credentials, the Swiss Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Charles-Edouard de Bavier, paid courtesy calls on Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Li Jishen and Zhang Lan, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council Huang Yanpei, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Minister of Trade Ye Jizhuang, and Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Zhang Youyu, respectively.

### 周外长宴瑞士公使

1951-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　周外长宴瑞士公使
    【新华社十六日讯】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长，于今日下午七时宴请瑞士驻我国特命全权公使任佐立及该使馆参赞施禔纳，二等秘书雷马赛。应邀作陪者有外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部办公厅主任王炳南，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，外交部欧非司司长宦乡，副司长张越，交际处处长王倬如等。
    瑞士驻我国特命全权公使任佐立于呈递国书后，曾分别拜谒我中央人民政府副主席李济深、张澜，政务院副总理黄炎培，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部部长沈雁冰，贸易部部长叶季壮及北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等。
