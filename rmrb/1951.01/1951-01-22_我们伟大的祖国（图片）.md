    Our Great Motherland
    A logging team composed of working people from Northeast China is engaged in winter labor in the vast forest area. They have systematically felled a large amount of poplar, birch, and toon trees to provide materials for various construction projects.

    我们伟大的祖国
    东北劳动人民所组成的伐木队，在广阔的森林区进行着冬季劳动。他们有计划地砍伐了大量的杨木、桦木和椿木，供给各项建筑的材料。
