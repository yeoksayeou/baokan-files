Title: President Pieck's Seventy-Fifth Birthday: German Ambassador to China Holds Reception
Author: 
Date: 1951-01-04
Page: 4

　　President Pieck's Seventy-Fifth Birthday
　　German Ambassador to China Holds Reception
    【Xinhua News Agency, 3rd】 January 3, 1951, was the seventy-fifth birthday of President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic. Mr. Koenig, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China, with the rank of Ambassador, held a birthday reception in Beijing this afternoon at 7 p.m.

    Those invited to attend were Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Vice Premier Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government, Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and heads of other departments of the Central People's Government.

    The foreign diplomatic envoys invited to attend were Soviet Ambassador to China Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Weiskopf, Korean Ambassador to China Li Zhouyuan, Romanian Ambassador to China Rudenko, Polish Ambassador to China Burgin, Mongolian Ambassador to China Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador to China Saffronko, Bulgarian Ambassador to China Petkov, etc.

    The reception proceeded in a warm atmosphere. After the reception, the documentary film of the German Democratic Republic "On the Road to Progress" was shown.

### 皮克总统七十五岁寿辰  德驻华大使举行招待会

1951-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　皮克总统七十五岁寿辰
    德驻华大使举行招待会
    【新华社三日讯】一九五一年一月三日为德意志民主共和国皮克总统七十五岁寿辰，德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希，特于是日下午七时在京举行祝寿招待会。
    应邀与会者有中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，副总理陈云，郭沫若，黄炎培，中央人民政府最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长兼北京市人民政府市长聂荣臻，及中央人民政府其他部门首长。
    各国外交使节应邀出席者有苏联驻华大使罗申，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使魏斯柯普夫，朝鲜驻华大使李周渊，罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科，波兰驻华大使布尔金，蒙古驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗，匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯，保加利亚驻华大使彼得科夫等。
    招待会在热烈气氛中进行。会后并放映德意志民主共和国记录影片“在前进的路上”。
