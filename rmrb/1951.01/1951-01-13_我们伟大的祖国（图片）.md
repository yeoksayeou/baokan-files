Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-01-13
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    After liberation, female workers in the Jingdezhen Porcelain Factory in Jiangxi Province are happily making adobe bricks. Jingdezhen has been the main production area of China's world-famous porcelain since the Song Dynasty, with a history of nearly a thousand years.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    解放后的江西景德镇瓷器厂的女工们正在愉快地制造土坯。景德镇从宋朝以来就是我国驰名世界的瓷器的主要出产地，至今已有近一千年的历史。
