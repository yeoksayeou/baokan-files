Title: Government Administration Council Financial and Economic Committee Issues Order for Unified Purchase of Cotton Yarn
Author:
Date: 1951-01-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 4th. The Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government issued an order on the 4th for the implementation of the unified purchase of cotton yarn. The full text is as follows:

            Order of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council

    For the purpose of rational supply of yarn and cloth, the "Decision on Unified Purchase of Cotton Yarn" is hereby formulated and promulgated, and shall be implemented from the date of issuance. Please comply!

                                                This order
                           Director  Chen Yun  January 4, 1951
            Decision of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council on Unified Purchase of Cotton Yarn

    1. From the date of promulgation of this decision, all cotton yarn spun and cotton cloth woven by public and private yarn mills for their own use shall be purchased by the state-owned China National Cotton Yarn and Cloth Corporation (hereinafter referred to as the Corporation) under a unified purchase system. The existing cotton yarn and cotton cloth of public and private yarn mills shall be registered and shall cease to be sold on the market; they shall be purchased by the Corporation.
    2. When the Corporation implements the unified purchase according to the provisions of the preceding article, the purchase price shall accurately calculate the factory's cost and give appropriate profits.
    3. The processing fees for cotton yarn and cotton cloth that public and private yarn mills process and weave on behalf of the Corporation shall also be appropriate.
    4. The cotton yarn and cotton cloth produced by public and private yarn mills shall meet the finished product standards stipulated by the Corporation and shall not reduce quality.
    5. In order to rationalize the unified purchase price and processing fees, the local government's Bureau of Industry and Commerce shall preside over the matter, with the participation of the Federation of Industry and Commerce and the Federation of Trade Unions, and convene the Corporation and public and private yarn mills to jointly negotiate and propose specific plans, which shall be implemented upon approval by the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government.
    6. The Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government shall, based on the production and needs of cotton yarn or various cotton fabrics, stipulate the allocation and sales, and processing methods of cotton yarn.
    7. Local people's governments must be responsible for managing the yarn and cloth market, cracking down on speculation and hoarding, and assisting the Corporation in making effective allocation and sales. When necessary, the Corporation may establish more retail stores.

### 政务院财政经济委员会发布统购棉纱命令

1951-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院财政经济委员会发布统购棉纱命令
    【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会四日颁令施行棉纱统购，全文如下：
            政务院财政经济委员会令
    为了纱布的合理供应，兹制订“关于统购棉纱的决定”随令发布，并自即日起施行，希即遵照办理为要！
                                                此令
                           主任  陈云  一九五一年一月四日
            政务院财政经济委员会关于统购棉纱的决定
    一、本决定公布之日起，凡公私纱厂自纺部分的棉纱及自织的棉布，均由国营花纱布公司统购。公私纱厂现存棉纱棉布均行登记，停止在市场出售，均由国营花纱布公司承购。
    二、国营花纱布公司按前条之规定实施统购时，其收购价格须确切计算厂方的成本并给以适当利润。
    三、公私纱厂为国营花纱布公司加工代纺、代织的棉纱棉布，其工缴费的规定，亦须适当。
    四、公私纱厂生产的棉纱棉布，均须符合于国营花纱布公司规定的成品标准，不得降低质量。
    五、为使统购价格、工缴费的规定趋于合理，由当地政府的工商局主持，工商联、总工会参加，召集国营花纱布公司及公私纱厂共同协商，提出具体方案，经中央人民政府贸易部批准实施之。
    六、中央人民政府贸易部，应根据棉纱或各种棉织品的生产和需要情况，规定棉纱的分配销售，加工办法。
    七、各地人民政府，对纱布市场必须负责管理，取缔投机囤积，协助国营花纱布公司作有效的分配销售。必要时，国营花纱布公司得增设零售店。
