Title: Shanghai Lu Xun Memorial Hall Established
Author:
Date: 1951-01-11
Page: 3

　　Shanghai Lu Xun Memorial Hall Established
    【Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 9th】 The Shanghai Lu Xun Memorial Hall was officially established on January 7th. Those who visited that day included Rao Shushi, Chen Yi, Pan Hannian, Feng Ding, Chen Wangdao, and writers Feng Xuefeng, Ye Yiqun, Ba Jin, Zhang Jin, Jiang Chunfang, and TASS China Chief Correspondent Rogov. Tang Tao hosted and explained the arrangement of the museum to the visitors. The memorial hall was originally Mr. Lu Xun's former residence at No. 9 and 10, Mainland New Village, Shanyin Road, Shanghai, where he lived before his death. All the furnishings in the memorial hall have been restored to their original state when Mr. Lu Xun lived there, and some of the houses have been converted into exhibition rooms for Mr. Lu Xun's works, manuscripts, and relics.

### 沪鲁迅纪念馆成立

1951-01-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　沪鲁迅纪念馆成立
    【新华社上海九日电】上海鲁迅纪念馆于一月七日正式成立。当日前往参观的有饶漱石、陈毅、潘汉年、冯定、陈望道和作家冯雪峰、叶以群、巴金、章靳以、姜椿芳及塔斯社中国总分社社长罗果夫等。由唐韬招待，并向参观者解说该馆陈设情况。纪念馆原是鲁迅先生生前在上海山阴路大陆新村第九、十号的故居。纪念馆的一切陈设，都恢复了鲁迅先生生前居住时的原状，并将一部分房屋辟为鲁迅先生的著作、手稿、遗物等的陈列室。
