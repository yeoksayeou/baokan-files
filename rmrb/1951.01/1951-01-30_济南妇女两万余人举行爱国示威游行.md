Title: Over 20,000 Jinan Women Hold Patriotic Demonstration and Parade
Author:
Date: 1951-01-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 29th. More than 26,000 women from all walks of life in Jinan City held a demonstration and parade rally on the 28th to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend the country. Participants in the parade included female workers, suburban farmers, female students, female teachers, female cadres from government organizations, and housewives. At the demonstration and parade rally, women from all walks of life unanimously adopted ten covenants, including actively carrying out work to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of soldiers,慰问(expressing sympathy and solicitude to) the Chinese and Korean people's soldiers, and providing relief to Korean refugees.

### 济南妇女两万余人举行爱国示威游行

1951-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　济南妇女两万余人举行爱国示威游行
    【新华社济南二十九日电】济南市各界妇女两万六千余人在二十八日举行抗美援朝保家卫国示威游行大会。参加游行的有女工、郊区农妇、女学生、女教师、机关女干部和家庭妇女等。各界妇女在示威游行大会上一致通过了积极开展拥军优属工作、慰劳中朝人民战士和救济朝鲜难民等十项公约。
