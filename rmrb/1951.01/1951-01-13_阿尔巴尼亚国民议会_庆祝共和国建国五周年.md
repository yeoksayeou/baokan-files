Title: Albanian National Assembly Celebrates the Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of the Republic
Author:
Date: 1951-01-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana: A comprehensive report from TASS: The Albanian National Assembly held a special meeting in Tirana on the 10th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Albania. Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Kabo, summarized Albania's achievements in the five years since its founding, stating that Albania's industrial production has increased to 439 percent of pre-war levels, with the mining industry increasing by 349 percent compared to 1938; state-owned trade exceeded 136 percent of 1946, and the total trade volume more than doubled pre-war levels; land transportation was 204 percent of 1938, and maritime transportation was 345 percent; the socialist component in agriculture has been established, tractor stations have been established, the sown area of industrial crops has been expanded, cotton fields have exceeded 554 percent of pre-war levels, and the country has planted 1,715 hectares of forests since Albania's liberation. The state's expenditure on the development of culture and education has increased 82 times compared to pre-war levels. The number of people enjoying medical treatment in hospitals and clinics is eight times the pre-war level. In 1949, the population increased by 32,416 people.

The meeting continued to discuss the "Law on the Defense of World Peace" proposed by Nushi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Council of the Albanian General Trade Union and other deputies. After a heated discussion, the meeting passed it amidst prolonged cheers. The law stipulates that those who engage in propaganda and incitement for wars of aggression will be sentenced to a maximum of twenty years of imprisonment and forced labor, deprived of their civil rights, and have part or all of their property confiscated.

### 阿尔巴尼亚国民议会  庆祝共和国建国五周年

1951-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚国民议会
    庆祝共和国建国五周年
    【新华社十二日讯】综合塔斯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚国民议会于十日在地拉那举行特别会议，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国建国五周年。部长会议副主席卡博总结阿尔巴尼亚建国五年来的成就称：阿尔巴尼亚工业生产量增加为战前水平的百分之四百三十九，采矿工业较一九三八年增加了百分之三百四十九；国营贸易超过一九四六年的百分之一百三十六，而贸易总额超过战前两倍以上；陆上运输为一九三八年的百分之二百零四，海上运输为百分之三百四十五；农业中的社会主义部分已建立起来，机器拖拉机站已经成立，工业作物的播种面积已经扩大，棉田已超过战前的百分之五百五十四，从阿国解放后到现在国家已种植了一千七百一十五公顷的森林。国家用于发展文化与教育的经费较战前增加了八十二倍。享受医院和诊疗所医疗的人数为战前的八倍。一九四九年人口增加了三万二千四百一十六人。
    会议继续讨论了阿尔巴尼亚总工会中央理事会主席团主席、议员努什和其他议员提出的“保卫世界和平法案”。经过热烈的讨论后，会议在历久不息的欢呼声中予以通过，法案规定：从事侵略战争宣传和鼓动的人，将被处以最高达二十年的徒刑和劳役，并被褫夺公民权利，没收其财产之一部或全部。
