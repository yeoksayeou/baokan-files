Title: Korean Anti-Aircraft Artillery Units Shot Down 500 US Aircraft in Six Months
Author:
Date: 1951-01-07
Page: 1

　　Korean Anti-Aircraft Artillery Units
    Shot Down 500 US Aircraft in Six Months
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th] According to the Korean Central News Agency: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Democratic Republic Army issued a battle report on the 1st, stating that since the beginning of the war, the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units have displayed unparalleled bravery in battles against enemy aircraft at the front and in the rear, achieving brilliant results. Although the enemy aircraft have a numerical advantage, they have suffered increasingly heavy losses. From June 25, 1950, to December 31, a total of 507 enemy aircraft were shot down. These included 53 B-29 bombers, 88 other bombers, 252 fighter planes (including so-called new jet aircraft), 88 attack planes, 14 fighter planes, and 12 reconnaissance planes.

### 朝鲜高射炮部队  半年击落美机五百架

1951-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜高射炮部队
    半年击落美机五百架
    【新华社五日讯】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部一日发表战报称：从战争以来，人民军高射炮部队在前方和后方迎击和驱逐敌人飞机的战斗中，发挥了英勇无比的战斗性，并获得了辉煌战绩。敌机虽然在数量上占据优势，却遭受到日益增加的重大损失。从一九五○年六月二十五日至十二月三十一日，共击落敌机五百零七架。其中包括Ｂ二十九型轰炸机五十三架、其他轰炸机八十八架、战斗机二百五十二架（包括所谓新式的喷气飞机）、攻击机八十八架、驱逐机十四架、侦察机十二架。
