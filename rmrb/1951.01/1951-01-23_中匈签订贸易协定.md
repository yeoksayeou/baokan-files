Title: China and Hungary Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1951-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: After friendly and harmonious negotiations between the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Commercial Delegation of the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic, the "Agreement on the Exchange of Goods and Payments between the People's Republic of China and the Hungarian People's Republic for 1951" was signed in Beijing on January 22nd. According to this agreement, China will export various raw materials and native products; Hungary will export various industrial raw materials and equipment. The signatories were Yao Yilin, Vice Minister of Trade of the Central People's Government on the Chinese side, and Sándor Szirmai, Hungarian Ambassador to China, on the Hungarian side. Those who attended the signing ceremony included Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Sha Qianli, Vice Minister of Trade, and Sándor Haraszti, head of the Hungarian Commercial Delegation, and more than 20 others.

### 中匈签订贸易协定

1951-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　中匈签订贸易协定
    【新华社二十二日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府贸易部与匈牙利人民共和国政府商务代表团经过友好与融洽的谈判，已于一月二十二日在北京签订了一九五一年“中华人民共和国与匈牙利人民共和国货物交换及付款协定”。根据这个协定中国将输出各种原料及土产品；匈牙利将输出各种工业原料及器材。签订协定者，中国方面为中央人民政府贸易部副部长姚依林，匈牙利方面为匈牙利驻中国大使夏法朗柯。参加签字典礼者，有中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，贸易部副部长沙千里、匈牙利商务代表团团长沙鲁辛斯基等二十余人。
