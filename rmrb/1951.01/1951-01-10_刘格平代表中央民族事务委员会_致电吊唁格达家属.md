Title: Liu Geping, on behalf of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission, Sends a Condolence Message to Gedda's Family
Author:
Date: 1951-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 9th: Belated news: Liu Geping, Deputy Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government and Head of the Southwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation of the Central People's Government, sent a condolence message in mid-December of last year on behalf of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government to the family of Gedda Living Buddha, Deputy Chairman of the Xikang Provincial People's Government, and the Baili Lama Temple he presided over, who were tragically murdered by British imperialist elements. The telegram stated: We were shocked to learn that Deputy Chairman Gedda, who worked tirelessly for the liberation cause of the Tibetan people, was poisoned to death by British imperialist elements, causing us immense grief. We hope that the people of all ethnic groups in Xikang Province will strengthen their unity, support the People's Liberation Army to quickly liberate Tibet, drive out imperialist aggressor forces, and avenge Deputy Chairman Gedda. Head Liu Geping also personally led the visiting delegation to Gedda's hometown of Garze and the Baili Lama Temple he presided over to offer condolences. Head Liu Geping and others visited various relics of Deputy Chairman Gedda at Baili Temple and presented the temple with gifts such as tea, silk, badges commemorating Chairman Mao, and a brocade banner inscribed with "Unite the People of All Nationalities of the People's Republic of China" by Chairman Mao himself. Gedda's younger brother and his housekeeper unanimously stated that they would inherit Gedda's spirit of working for peace and dedicate themselves to the revolutionary cause of the motherland forever under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party.

### 刘格平代表中央民族事务委员会  致电吊唁格达家属

1951-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘格平代表中央民族事务委员会
    致电吊唁格达家属
    【新华社重庆九日电】迟到消息：中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员、中央人民政府西南各民族访问团团长刘格平于去年十二月中旬代表中央人民政府民族事务委员会，致电惨遭英帝国主义分子谋害的西康省人民政府副主席格达活佛家属及其主持之白利喇嘛寺吊唁。电文称：惊悉格达副主席为藏族人民解放事业艰险奔走，惨遭英帝国主义分子毒害身死，不胜哀悼。愿西康省各民族人民加强团结，支援人民解放军迅速解放西藏，驱逐帝国主义侵略势力，为格达副主席复仇。刘格平团长并曾亲率访问团赴格达副主席家乡甘孜及其主持之白利喇嘛寺吊唁。刘格平团长等参观格达副主席之白利寺各种遗物，并送给该寺茶叶、绸缎、毛主席纪念章与毛主席亲笔题有“中华人民共和国各民族团结起来”字样的锦旗等礼品。格达副主席之弟及其管家等一致表示：将继承格达为和平事业奔走的精神，永远在中国共产党领导下献身于祖国的革命事业。
