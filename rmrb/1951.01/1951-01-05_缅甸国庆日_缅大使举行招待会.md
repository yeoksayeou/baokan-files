Title: Burmese National Day: Burmese Ambassador Holds Reception
Author: 
Date: 1951-01-05
Page: 4

　　Burmese National Day
　　Burmese Ambassador Holds Reception
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 4th] The 4th of January is Burmese National Day. The Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, U Myint Toon, held a National Day reception this afternoon from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM.
　　Those invited to attend included Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China; Vice Premiers Guo Moruo and Huang Yanpei; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; and leaders of various people's organizations, totaling more than 200 people.
　　Diplomatic envoys from various countries who were invited to attend included Soviet Ambassador to China, N.V. Roshchin; Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Weisskopf; Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko; Indian Ambassador to China, Panikkar; Polish Ambassador to China, Burgin; Swedish Ambassador to China, Amashton; Hungarian Ambassador to China, Saffronko; Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Petkov; Danish Minister to China, Mook; Swiss Legation Counselor to China, Steiner, and government negotiation representatives from Britain, the Netherlands, and Norway.

### 缅甸国庆日  缅大使举行招待会

1951-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸国庆日
    缅大使举行招待会
    【新华社四日讯】一月四日为缅甸国庆日。缅甸联邦驻华大使吴敏登，特于今日下午四时至六时举行国庆招待会。
    应邀与会者有中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，副总理郭沫若、黄炎培，中央人民政府最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长兼北京市人民政府市长聂荣臻，中央人民政府各部门首长及各人民团体负责人等二百余人。
    各国外交使节应邀出席者有苏联驻华大使罗申，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使魏斯柯普夫，罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科，印度驻华大使潘尼迦，波兰驻华大使布尔金，瑞典驻华大使阿马斯顿，匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯，保加利亚驻华大使彼得科夫，丹麦驻华公使穆克，瑞士驻华公使馆参赞施禔纳及英、荷、挪等国政府谈判代表。
