Title: Viet People's Army Fiercely Fights French Troops, Threatening Three Strongholds Near Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1951-01-18
Page: 4
　　
    Viet People's Army Fiercely Fights French Troops
    Threatening Three Strongholds Near Hanoi

    [Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 15th] TASS Paris, 15th: According to newspaper reports: Vietnamese People's Army troops are engaged in fierce battles with French troops north of Hanoi. According to newspaper reports: People's Army troops are threatening three French strongholds. One is Bao Truc, fifty kilometers northwest of Hanoi, and the other two are southwest of Luc Nam (northeast of Hanoi). "Liberation" newspaper says: The Vietnamese People's Army is fighting the enemy along a 120-kilometer front.

    Another report: Vietnamese People's Army guerrillas destroyed four French strongholds in the Red River Delta area, located in the provinces of Ha Dong, Nam Dinh, Thai Binh, and Hai Duong, and drove the enemy out of the Co Tue mountain in Ha Nam province, south of Hanoi. The French stronghold of Thu Lao in Nam Dinh province, south of Hanoi, has also been captured by the People's Army troops.

### 越人民军与法军激战  威胁河内附近三据点

1951-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　越人民军与法军激战
    威胁河内附近三据点
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社巴黎十五日讯：据报纸消息：越南人民军部队正在河内以北与法军部队进行激战。据报纸消息：人民军部队正威胁着三个法军据点。一个是河内西北五十公里的保烛，另两个是在绿南（河内东北）的西南方。《解放报》说：越南人民军正沿着一百二十公里长的战线与敌人作战。
    又讯：越南人民军游击部队在红河三角洲区域摧毁了位于河东、南定、太平与海阳各省的法军据点四处，并将敌军赶出了河内以南河南省的粗腿山。河内以南南定省守劳的法军据点，也已经被人民军部队拔除。
