Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Other Organizations Hold Meeting to Commemorate Lenin
Author:
Date: 1951-01-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. Today is the 27th anniversary of Lenin's death. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association specially held a lecture meeting to commemorate it. Attending the meeting were more than 800 people, including heads of branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association from various organs, groups, factories, and schools in the capital, representatives from various departments of the Central People's Government, various people's organizations, and various newspapers and magazines. At the meeting, Soviet education expert Arsentiev gave a report on Lenin. In his report, he analyzed the historical conditions for the emergence of Leninism, and briefly described Lenin's great contributions to the struggle of the proletariat and all oppressed people around the world, especially his concern for the Chinese people. After the meeting, the film "Lenin in October" was shown. On the same day, the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association jointly held a lecture and film evening. At the meeting, Safronov, the representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, gave a lecture entitled "In the Twenty-Seven Years Since Lenin's Death, the Soviet Union Has Advanced Along Lenin's Path Under Stalin's Leadership."

### 中苏友协总会等单位集会纪念列宁

1951-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协总会等单位集会纪念列宁
    【新华社二十一日讯】今日为列宁逝世二十七周年纪念日，中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会特举行讲演会进行纪念。到会的有首都各机关、团体、工厂、学校的中苏友好协会支会负责人、中央人民政府各部门、各人民团体、各报纸、杂志的代表共八百多人。会上由苏联教育专家阿尔辛节夫作了关于列宁的报告。他在报告中分析了列宁主义产生的历史条件，扼要地叙述了列宁对全世界无产阶级和一切被压迫者的斗争的伟大贡献，特别是对中国人民的关心。会后并放映“列宁在十月”影片。同日，苏联对外文化协会驻中国代表和北京市中苏友好协会联合举办了讲演、电影晚会。会上苏联对外文化协会驻中国代表萨福隆作了题为“列宁逝世后二十七年中苏联在斯大林的领导下按照列宁的道路前进”的讲演。
