Title: Leaders of Germany, Poland and Bulgaria Send Telegrams to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou to Congratulate the New Year
Author:
Date: 1951-01-03
Page: 1

Telegrams from the leaders of Germany, Poland, and Bulgaria to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou, congratulating the New Year

To Mr. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

　　At the beginning of this new year, I wish you, your government, and the entire Chinese people further achievements in building a democratic, independent, and strong China, and express the deep concern of the German Democratic Republic and all peace-loving forces in Germany to you.

　　Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, December 30, 1950, in Berlin

To Mr. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

　　On the occasion of the New Year, I sincerely wish you, Mr. Chairman, personal happiness, and hope that you will have further fruitful achievements for the happiness of the Chinese people and the lasting peace of the world.

　　Bierut, December 30, 1950, in Warsaw

To Mr. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

　　On the occasion of the New Year 1951, Mr. Chairman, please accept my most sincere congratulations to you. I wish you personal happiness and wish the People's Republic of China new success on the road to liberating all Chinese territory and in the struggle for world peace under the leadership of the great Soviet Union.

　　Damianov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria
　　December 31, 1950, in Sofia

To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China:

　　As 1950 turns to 1951, I would like to extend, in the name of the Government of the German Democratic Republic and in my own name, my heartfelt greetings and warmest congratulations to you, and through you to the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. I hope that you will win new and greater achievements in 1951 in rebuilding your country, in the struggle for peace and China's security, and against the American aggressors. I am convinced that the friendly relations between our two nations will further develop and consolidate.

　　Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic, December 30, 1950, in Berlin

To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

　　On the occasion of the New Year, I sincerely wish you personal happiness, and hope that you will achieve further fruitful results in the cause of building a happy people's China in the future, and in the cause of consolidating world peace.

　　Józef Cyrankiewicz, December 30, 1950, in Warsaw

To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

　　As 1950 turns to 1951, I would like to extend to you my friendly congratulations and pay high tribute to the Chinese People's Volunteers in their struggle in Korea. I am convinced that the existing friendly relations between our two nations will be further consolidated in the coming year, and will contribute to strengthening the peace camp under the leadership of the Soviet Union and crushing the war plans of the American and British imperialists.

　　Didinger, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, December 30, 1950, in Berlin

To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

　　On the occasion of the New Year, Minister, I warmly wish you personal happiness, and hope that you will have further success in the struggle for world peace.

　　Z. Moserowski, December 30, 1950, in Warsaw

To Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

　　Please accept my most cordial New Year's greetings, and sincerely hope you are healthy and happy. May the great Chinese people achieve complete victory and prosperity.

　　M. Nechev, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, December 31, 1950, in Sofia

### 德波保三国领袖电毛主席周总理祝贺新年

1951-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　德波保三国领袖电毛主席周总理祝贺新年中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    值此新年开始之际，谨祝您，您的政府和全体中国人民在建设一个民主、独立和强盛的中国中，赢得进一步的成就，并向您表达德意志民主共和国以及全德爱好和平的力量之深切关怀。
      德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克  一九五零年十二月三十日于柏林中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    值此新年之际，谨诚挚地祝贺您，主席先生，个人幸福，并希望您为了中国人民的幸福和全世界的持久和平，有更进一步的丰盛收获。
                                贝鲁特  一九五零年十二月三十日于华沙中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    值此一九五一年新年之际，主席先生，请接受我对您最衷心的祝贺。恭祝您个人幸福，并祝中华人民共和国在解放全中国领土的道路上，及在伟大的苏联领导下，争取世界和平的斗争中，得到新的成功。
                      保加利亚人民共和国国民大会主席团主席达米扬诺夫
                                    一九五零年十二月三十一日于索非亚中华人民共和国总理周恩来先生：
    值此一九五○转向五一年之际，谨以德意志民主共和国政府以及我个人的名义向您，并且通过您向中华人民共和国中央人民政府致最衷心的问候及最热烈的祝贺。希望您们在一九五一年在重建您们的国家，在争取和平及中国的安全，反对美国侵略者的斗争中赢得新的、更进一步的伟大成就，我深信我们两民族间的友谊关系将进一步发展并巩固。
          德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥  一九五零年十二月三十日于柏林中华人民共和国政务院总理周恩来先生：
    值此新年之际，谨诚挚地祝贺您个人幸福，并希望您在建设未来幸福的人民中国的事业中，与巩固全世界和平的事业中，获得更进一步的丰盛的成果。
                    约塞夫·西伦凯维兹  一九五零年十二月三十日于华沙中华人民共和国总理兼外交部长周恩来先生：
    值此一九五零年转向一九五一年之际，我谨向您致友谊的祝贺，并对中国人民志愿军在朝鲜的斗争致崇高的敬礼。我确信，我们两民族间现存的友谊关系在来年中将更进一步巩固，并对加强在苏联领导下的和平阵营以及粉碎美英帝国主义者的战争计划将有所贡献。
      德意志民主共和国外交部长窦丁格尔  一九五零年十二月三十日于柏林中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来先生：
    值此新年之际，部长先生，谨热烈地祝贺您个人幸福，并希望您在争取全世界和平的斗争中有更进一步的成功。
                      Ｚ·莫塞莱夫斯基  一九五零年十二月三十日于华沙中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来：
    请接受我最亲切的新年的祝贺，至诚地希望您健康、幸福。希望伟大的中国人民获得完全的胜利和繁荣。
    保加利亚人民共和国外交部部长Ｍ·奈捷夫  一九五零年十二月三十一日于索非亚
