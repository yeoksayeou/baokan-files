Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Public Bond Winning Numbers on February 1st
Author:
Date: 1951-01-31
Page: 1

　　【Xinhua News Agency, 29th】The Central People's Broadcasting Station is scheduled to broadcast the winning numbers of the first phase of the 1950 People's Victory Parity Unit Public Bonds from 8:15 PM to 8:30 PM on February 1st. Local people's broadcasting stations will simultaneously rebroadcast the information.

### 中央人民广播电台二月一日播送公债中签号码

1951-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台二月一日播送公债中签号码
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央人民广播电台定于二月一日晚八点十五分至八点三十分播送一九五零年第一期人民胜利折实公债中签号码，各地人民广播电台将予同时联播。
