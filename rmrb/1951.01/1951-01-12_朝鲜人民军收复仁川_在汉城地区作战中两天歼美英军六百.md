Title: Korean People's Army Recaptures Incheon; Eliminates 600 American and British Troops in Two Days of Fighting in the Seoul Area
Author:
Date: 1951-01-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, January 11th: Our correspondent on the Seoul front reports: A unit of the Korean People's Army liberated Incheon Port, west of Seoul, on the afternoon of the 8th.
　　TASS, Pyongyang, January 11th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced on the 7th that People's Army units continue to advance on all fronts, crushing enemy resistance.
　　People's Army units operating in the Seoul area inflicted heavy losses on the US 25th Division and the British 27th Brigade on the 3rd and 4th. According to available materials, in these battles, People's Army units killed or wounded more than 500 American and British officers and soldiers, and captured more than 100. The captured spoils of war are as follows: more than ten tanks, more than seventy vehicles, three 155mm howitzers, thirty-three medium machine guns, more than three hundred rifles, and much other equipment and military supplies.

### 朝鲜人民军收复仁川  在汉城地区作战中两天歼美英军六百

1951-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军收复仁川
    在汉城地区作战中两天歼美英军六百
    【新华社十一日讯】本社汉城前线记者报道：朝鲜人民军一部，于八日下午光复汉城以西的仁川港。
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部七日发表战报称：人民军部队继续在各战线上推进，击溃敌军的抵抗。
    在汉城地区作战的人民军部队于三、四两日使美军第二十五师和英军第二十七旅的部队蒙受重大损失。根据现有材料，在这些战斗中，人民军部队毙伤美英军官兵五百名以上，并俘虏一百余名。缴获的战利品如下：坦克十多辆，汽车七十多辆，一五五公厘大炮三门，中型机关枪三十三挺，步枪三百多枝及其他装备和军火甚多。
