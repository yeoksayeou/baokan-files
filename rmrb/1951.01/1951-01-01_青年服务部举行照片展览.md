Date: 1951-01-01

　　Youth Service Department Holds Photo Exhibition
    To celebrate the New Year, the Youth Service Department is holding a photo exhibition starting from New Year's Day. The content is divided into three parts: (1) The growth of the Chinese People's Navy and Air Force; (2) A Record of the Liberation of Hainan Island; (3) The Korean People Fighting for the Glory and Freedom of the Motherland. The dates are: January 1st to 3rd and 6th to 8th. The daily opening hours, except for New Year's Day from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM, are 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM and 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM on other days. The location is at the Youth Service Department (No. 16 East Chang'an Street). All circles are welcome to visit.

### 青年服务部举行照片展览

1951-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　青年服务部举行照片展览
    青年服务部，为庆贺新年，自元旦起，举办照片展览。内容分三部分：（１）中国人民海空军的成长；（２）海南岛解放记；（３）为祖国的光荣与自由而战的朝鲜人民。日期为：一月一日至三日及六日到八日。每日开放时间，除元旦为上午九时至十二时，下午二时至五时外，其他几天为二时至五时，六时半至八时半。地点在青年服务部（东长安街十六号）。欢迎各界参观。
