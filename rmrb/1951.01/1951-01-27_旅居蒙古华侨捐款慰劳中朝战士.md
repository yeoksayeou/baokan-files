Title: Overseas Chinese in Mongolia Donate Funds to Comfort Chinese and Korean Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1951-01-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: Overseas Chinese residing in the Mongolian People's Republic are enthusiastically comforting the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army. The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Opposition to American Aggression recently received a donation of 100,000 Mongolian currency units (equivalent to 664,449,670 yuan in Renminbi) from the Overseas Chinese Committee for Resistance Against America and Aid to Korea in Mongolia, to be forwarded to the Chinese and Korean People's soldiers, along with a letter of sympathy. The letter stated: We, the compatriots of all nationalities and from all walks of life residing in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, resolutely support the anti-aggression war waged by our Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army against the American imperialists. We have enthusiastically launched a campaign to comfort you and have already raised 100,000 Mongolian currency units in donations. The comfort campaign is still continuing. We will work hard in our own positions and pledge to be your support with practical actions.

### 旅居蒙古华侨捐款慰劳中朝战士

1951-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　旅居蒙古华侨捐款慰劳中朝战士
    【新华社二十五日讯】旅居蒙古人民共和国的我国侨胞热烈慰劳我国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会最近收到旅蒙华侨抗美援朝保家卫国委员会委托转交中朝人民战士的慰劳金蒙币十万元（折合人民币六亿六千四百四十九万六千七百元），并附慰问信一封。信上说：我们旅居在蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴特尔市的各民族各界同胞，坚决拥护我国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军对美帝国主义者进行的反侵略战争。我们热烈地展开了慰劳你们的运动，现已募集慰劳金蒙币十万元。慰劳运动仍在继续展开。我们要站在自己岗位上努力工作，誓以实际行动做你们的后盾。
