Our Great Motherland

　　In the sacred movement to resist US aggression and aid Korea to protect our homes and defend our country, our working class has launched a patriotic production competition. This is the scene of intense labor in the Shenyang Machine Factory in Northeast China.

### 我们伟大的祖国

1951-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
    在神圣的抗美援朝保家卫国的运动中，我国工人阶级展开了爱国主义的生产竞赛。这是在东北沈阳机器厂中紧张劳动的情形。
