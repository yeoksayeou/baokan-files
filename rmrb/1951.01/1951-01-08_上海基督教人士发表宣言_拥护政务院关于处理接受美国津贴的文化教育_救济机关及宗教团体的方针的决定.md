Page: 1

Shanghai Christians Issue Declaration
Supporting the Government Administration Council's Decision on Handling Cultural, Educational, and Relief Organizations and Religious Groups Receiving American Subsidies

　　We, workers from various national and local Christian organizations in Shanghai, warmly support the decision of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council on handling cultural, educational, and relief organizations and religious groups receiving American subsidies. We believe this decision fully aligns with the interests of the Chinese people and will powerfully promote the completion of the task of Christian reform in China.

　　The American government blatantly announced on December 16th of last year the freezing of Chinese assets in the United States, and used the method of cutting off remittances to threaten the enterprises of all religious, cultural, and relief groups receiving American subsidies and the livelihoods of their workers. We solemnly declare that as liberated Chinese people, we will absolutely not be threatened by American imperialism in this way. Based on the spirit of patriotism and self-respect, we can not only completely break away from the economic relations with American imperialism as a tool of cultural aggression, but also establish a healthier Christian enterprise that better serves the new Chinese society with our own strength, on the basis of the new Chinese people.

　　American imperialism has consistently been hostile to the Chinese people. After suffering defeat, it became enraged and adopted a more open and blatant policy of aggression. This has aroused resistance and anger not only from the Chinese people, but also from true Christians and those who previously harbored illusions about the United States. In September of last year, we published the "Ways for Chinese Christianity to Strive in the Construction of New China" declaration. In this declaration, we called on Christians across the country to eliminate the influence of imperialism on Christianity for over a century, and to promote the Three-Self Patriotic Movement (self-governance, self-support, self-propagation) in order to create a church completely led by Chinese Christians. In the past two months, due to the expansion of American imperialism's aggressive policies, Christians across the country have more enthusiastically supported this declaration. This has dealt a severe blow to the cultural aggression carried out by American imperialism through Christian activities. American imperialists attempted to put Christian enterprises receiving American subsidies in a difficult situation by cutting off economic sources. This foolish attempt by American imperialism will have the completely opposite result: it will make all Chinese Christians more clearly recognize the face of American imperialism and more firmly stand on the position of patriotism, and strive for the construction of New China.

　　The Central People's Government's policy on handling cultural, relief, and religious organizations receiving American subsidies not only powerfully strikes at the conspiracy of American imperialism, but also fully takes into account the needs of the Christian enterprise. These policies make us more clearly recognize the sincerity of the Central People's Government in respecting religious freedom. We are very grateful to the government for such assistance and sympathy. We will certainly make this assistance and sympathy a strong pillar for the thorough reform of Chinese Christianity.

### 上海基督教人士发表宣言  拥护政务院关于处理接受美国津贴的文化教育  救济机关及宗教团体的方针的决定

1951-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海基督教人士发表宣言
    拥护政务院关于处理接受美国津贴的文化教育
    救济机关及宗教团体的方针的决定
    我们在上海的各全国性及地方性的基督教团体的工作者，热烈地拥护中央人民政府政务院关于处理接受美国津贴的文化教育、救济机关及宗教团体的方针的决定。我们认为这个决定完全符合中国人民的利益，并将有力地促进中国基督教革新的任务的完成。
    美国政府在去年十二月十六日悍然宣布冻结中国在美国的财产，并用断绝汇兑的办法，来威胁所有接受美国津贴的宗教、文化、救济团体的事业和它们的工作人员的生活。我们郑重地宣言，作为解放了的中国人民的我们，是绝对不会受美帝国主义这样的威胁的。我们本着爱国的精神和自尊的心理，不但可以完全脱离美帝作为文化侵略工具的经济关系，并且能够用自己的力量，在新中国人民的基础上，建立更健全的、更能为新中国社会服务的基督教事业。
    美帝一贯地与中国人民为敌，它遭受了失败以后，便恼羞成怒，采取更公开露骨的侵略政策。这不但引起了中国人民的反抗与愤怒，也引起了真正的基督徒与过去对美国存有幻想的人的反抗与愤怒。去年九月我们发表了《中国基督教在新中国建设中努力的途径》这个宣言。在这个宣言里，我们号召全国的基督徒肃清帝国主义一百多年来对基督教的影响，并提倡自治、自养、自传的运动，以促成一个完全由中国基督徒所主持的教会。在过去两个多月中，由于美帝侵略政策的扩展，全国的基督徒对这个宣言更加热烈地拥护。这就使美帝通过基督教活动而进行的文化侵略受到一个严重的打击。美帝国主义者企图以断绝经济来源的办法，使接受美国津贴的基督教事业陷入窘境。美帝这一个愚拙的企图，将会得到一个与此完全相反的结果：它将使中国所有的基督徒更清楚地认识了美帝国主义的面目，更坚决地站在爱国主义的立场，为新中国的建设而奋斗。
    中央人民政府对于接受美国津贴的文化、救济、宗教等团体的处理方针，不但有力地打击了美帝国主义的阴谋，也充分地照顾了基督教事业的需要。这些方针使我们更清楚地认识了中央人民政府尊重宗教信仰自由的诚意。我们非常感激政府对我们这样的援助与同情。我们必定使这些援助与同情成为中国基督教彻底革新的有力支柱。
                上海基督教传道人联谊会主席尤树勋
