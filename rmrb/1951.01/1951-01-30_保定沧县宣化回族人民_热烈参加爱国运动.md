Title: Baoding, Cang County, and Xuanhua Hui People Enthusiastically Participate in the Patriotic Movement
Author:
Date: 1951-01-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 28th:

　　The Hui people in Baoding, Xuanhua, and other places in Hebei Province are enthusiastically participating in the movement to resist U.S. aggression, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend the country. More than 500 Hui people in Baoding City, Hebei Province, held an anti-U.S. patriotic meeting on the 20th of this month. At the meeting, the Hui people unanimously expressed that they would take practical actions of stepping up production and doing various tasks well to resist U.S. aggression, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend the country. The meeting also enthusiastically passed an anti-U.S. patriotic declaration and a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao. In order to unify the leadership of the anti-U.S. patriotic movement of the Hui people in Baoding City, the Hui people in the city have established a preparatory committee for the Committee to Defend World Peace and Oppose U.S. Aggression. Two thousand Hui people in Cang County, Hebei Province, also held an anti-U.S. aggression and aid Korea meeting on the 19th of last month, followed by a large parade. The Ahongs of various mosques, dressed in neat formal attire, marched in formation, shouting slogans such as "Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, Protect Our Homes and Defend the Country" and "Long Live the Unity of the People of All Nationalities" along the way.
    The Hui people of Xuanhua City, Chahar Province, held a city-wide Hui people's anti-U.S. patriotic parade meeting on the 14th of this month. The meeting unanimously passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and a letter to Hui compatriots.

### 保定沧县宣化回族人民  热烈参加爱国运动

1951-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　保定沧县宣化回族人民
    热烈参加爱国运动
    【新华社二十八日讯】华北保定、宣化等地回族人民热烈参加抗美援朝保家卫国运动。河北省保定市回族人民五百余人于本月二十日举行抗美爱国大会。会上，回民们一致表示：要以加紧生产和做好各种工作的实际行动来抗美援朝保家卫国。大会并热烈地通过了抗美爱国宣言和向毛主席致敬的电文。为了统一领导保定市回民的抗美爱国运动，该市回族人民已成立了保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会筹备委员会。河北省沧县的回族人民二千人，亦曾于上月十九日举行抗美援朝大会，会后并进行大游行。各清真寺的阿訇都穿着整齐的礼服，列队前进，沿途高呼“抗美援朝保家卫国”“各民族人民团结万岁”等口号。
    察哈尔省宣化市回族人民于本月十四日举行全市回民抗美爱国游行大会。会上一致通过了向毛主席致敬的电文和告回民同胞书。
