Title: President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Vavilov, Passes Away. President Guo Moruo Sends Condolence Telegram

Author:
Date: 1951-01-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th: TASS Moscow reports: The Soviet Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) announce with deep sorrow that the President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Vavilov, passed away in Moscow on January 25, 1951, due to serious illness, at the age of sixty. Vavilov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Chairman of the All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge, Editor-in-Chief of the Great Soviet Encyclopedia, and scientist who twice won the Stalin Prize, dedicated all his strength and knowledge to Soviet science and to the great cause of communism. The Soviet Council of Ministers has passed a resolution to establish a funeral committee for Vavilov, composed of Academicians Bardin, Oparin, Gorkin, Yasnov, Baladin, Stokerkchin, and Topchiev, with Bardin as the chairman.

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, sent a telegram to the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, expressing condolences for the death of Vavilov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. The telegram reads as follows:

To the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Moscow:

I was shocked to learn of the death of President Vavilov. This is not only a loss of an outstanding leader for the Soviet scientific community, but also a major loss for the world scientific community. I would like to express my condolences on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chinese scientists.

Guo Moruo

### 苏联科学院院长  瓦维洛夫逝世  郭沫若院长致电吊唁

1951-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联科学院院长
    瓦维洛夫逝世
    郭沫若院长致电吊唁
    【新华社二十六日讯】综合塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议与苏联共产党（布）中央委员会以沉痛心情宣布苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫于一九五一年一月二十五日在莫斯科因重病逝世，享年六十岁。苏联科学院院长、苏联最高苏维埃代表、全苏政治与科学知识普及协会主席、伟大的苏联百科全书总编辑、两次荣获斯大林奖金的科学家瓦维洛夫，曾将他的全部力量和知识贡献给苏联科学。贡献给共产主义的伟大事业。苏联部长会议已通过决议成立瓦维洛夫治丧委员会。由科学院院士巴尔丁、奥巴林、高尔金、雅斯诺夫、巴拉丁、斯哥克尔钦及陶普契夫组成，由巴尔丁任主任委员。
    【新华社二十六日讯】中国科学院院长郭沫若致电苏联科学院主席团，吊唁苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫的逝世。电文如下：莫斯科苏联科学院主席团：
    惊悉瓦维洛夫院长病逝，这不仅是苏联科学界失掉了一个他们的优秀的领导者，同时也是世界科学界的重大损失，我谨代表中国科学院和中国科学工作者致哀悼之忱。
                  郭沫若
