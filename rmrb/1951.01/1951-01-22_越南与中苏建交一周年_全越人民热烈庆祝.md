Title: Vietnam and China, Soviet Union Celebrate First Anniversary of Diplomatic Relations, All Vietnamese People Enthusiastically Celebrate
Author:
Date: 1951-01-22
Page: 4
　　
    Vietnam and China, Soviet Union Celebrate First Anniversary of Diplomatic Relations
    All Vietnamese People Enthusiastically Celebrate
    [Xinhua News Agency, January 21st] According to Vietnam News Agency: People across Vietnam enthusiastically celebrated the first anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Vietnam and China, the Soviet Union, and other People's Democratic countries on the 18th. Government agencies, people's organizations, factories, enterprises, and People's Army troops in urban and rural areas of Vietnam solemnly held celebration meetings.
    Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, delivered a speech to the Vietnamese people at home and abroad on the "Voice of Vietnam" radio station on the 18th. He emphasized the great significance of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and China, the Soviet Union, and other People's Democratic countries. Minister Hoang stated: We must thank the democratic peoples for their friendship towards our people, their trust in our people, and their fervent confidence in the final victory of our liberation struggle. After the great victories achieved by the Vietnamese people last year, and after the recent failures of the American imperialists in Korea, we are confident that we will be able to eliminate the French imperialists and American interventionists. Minister Hoang called on the Vietnamese people to redouble their efforts, overcome difficulties, and implement the government's general mobilization order in order to accelerate the preparation for the general counter-offensive.

### 越南与中苏建交一周年  全越人民热烈庆祝

1951-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南与中苏建交一周年
    全越人民热烈庆祝
    【新华社二十一日讯】据越南通讯社消息：越南全国各地人民于十八日热烈庆祝越南与中国、苏联及其他人民民主国家建交一周年纪念。越南各地城乡的政府机关、人民团体、工厂企业及人民军部队等都庄严地举行了庆祝会。
    越南民主共和国政府外交部长黄明鉴曾于十八日在《越南之声》电台向国内外越南人民发表演说，他着重指出越南民主共和国与中、苏及其他人民民主国家建立外交关系的重大意义。黄外长称：我们必须感谢民主人民对我国人民的友谊，对我国人民的信任，以及对我们解放斗争最后胜利的热烈信心。在去年越南人民获得了伟大的胜利之后，在美国帝国主义者最近在朝鲜的失败之后，我们确信我们必能肃清法国帝国主义者和美国干涉主义者。黄外长号召越南人民加倍努力、克服困难、执行政府的总动员令，以便加速完成总反攻的准备工作。
