Title: Indian Communist Party Central Committee Issues Statement Congratulating Chinese and Korean Peoples on Great Victory
Author:
Date: 1951-01-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 7th. Bombay dispatch: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of India recently issued a statement congratulating the heroic Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers on their great victory on the Korean battlefield. The statement declared: "The Central Committee of the Communist Party of India extends revolutionary greetings to the heroic Korean people, under the leadership of the Korean Democratic People's Republic and its leader Kim Il-sung, for shattering the despicable conspiracy of American imperialism to enslave their motherland. The Central Committee pays tribute to the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers who are fighting alongside the Korean People's Army to defend the integrity of their motherland from American imperialist aggression. The Central Committee pays tribute to the governments and peoples of the People's Republic of China, the Soviet Union, and other people's democratic countries, as well as the democratic people elsewhere, who stand united as a shield for the Korean Democratic People's Republic and against American aggression." The statement concluded by calling on all ethnic groups in India to unite in support of the resolutions of the Second World Peace Congress.

### 印共中央发表文告  祝贺中朝人民大捷

1951-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　印共中央发表文告
    祝贺中朝人民大捷
    【新华社新德里七日电】孟买消息：印度共产党中央委员会最近发表文告，向英勇的朝鲜人民军与中国人民志愿军祝贺在朝鲜战场取得的伟大胜利。文告宣称：“印度共产党中央委员会向在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国及其领袖金日成同志领导下，粉碎美国帝国主义奴役其祖国卑鄙阴谋的、英勇的朝鲜人民致革命的敬礼。中央委员会向为保卫其祖国的完整、不受美帝国主义侵犯而与朝鲜人民军并肩作战的英勇的中国人民志愿军致敬。中央委员会向团结一致、为朝鲜民主主义人民共和国后盾和反对美国侵略的中华人民共和国、苏联与其他人民民主国家的政府与人民以及其他地方的民主人民致敬。”文告最后号召印度各族人民团结起来，拥护第二届世界保卫和平大会的决议。
