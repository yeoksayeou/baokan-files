Title: Korean People's Army 19th Anti-Aircraft Artillery Regiment Awarded Title of Korean Democratic People's Republic Guards Unit
Author:
Date: 1951-01-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 25th (TASS): The Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Korean Democratic People's Republic issued an order on January 22, 1951: In recognition of the courageous heroism and victorious battles of the People's Army 19th Anti-Aircraft Artillery Regiment, it is hereby awarded the title of Korean Democratic People's Republic Guards 19th Anti-Aircraft Artillery Regiment.

### 朝鲜人民军第十九高射炮团荣获朝鲜共和国警卫军称号

1951-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军第十九高射炮团荣获朝鲜共和国警卫军称号
    【新华社讯】塔斯社平壤二十五日电：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会一九五一年一月二十二日命令：由于人民军第十九高射炮团勇敢的英雄气概与胜利的战斗，特授予该高射炮团以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国警卫军第十九高射炮团的称号。
