Title: Beijing Women's Demonstration Tomorrow: Oppose America Arming Japan
Author:
Date: 1951-01-27
Page: 1

　　Beijing Women's Demonstration Tomorrow
    Oppose America Arming Japan
    [Our Newspaper] In order to continue to deeply develop the movement to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes and defend our country, celebrate the great victory of the Chinese and Korean people, oppose America arming Japan, and demonstrate against American imperialism, women from all walks of life in Beijing have decided to gather at the Taihe Gate of the Forbidden City at 1:00 PM tomorrow (the 28th) to hold a patriotic demonstration to resist America, aid Korea and oppose the arming of Japan.
      (Beijing Women's Federation Communication Group)

### 京市妇女明日游行  反对美国武装日本

1951-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市妇女明日游行
    反对美国武装日本
    【本报讯】北京市各界妇女为了继续深入开展抗美援朝保家卫国运动，庆祝中朝人民的伟大胜利，反对美国武装日本，并向美帝国主义示威，定明日（二十八日）下午一时在故宫太和门集合，举行抗美援朝反对武装日本爱国大游行。
      （京市妇联通讯组）
