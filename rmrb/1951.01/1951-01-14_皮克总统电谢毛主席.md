Title: President Pieck Sends Telegram Thanking Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1951-01-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, January 13th: President Wilhelm Pieck of the German Democratic Republic sent a telegram thanking Chairman Mao Zedong for his congratulations on his seventy-fifth birthday. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    I sincerely thank you and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China for your congratulations and wonderful gifts on my seventy-fifth birthday. I wish your country further achievements in national construction and the defense of peace. The German Democratic Republic will continue to do everything possible to consolidate the friendship between our two nations.
                            Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic
                                     Berlin, January 10, 1951

### 皮克总统电谢毛主席

1951-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　皮克总统电谢毛主席
    【新华社十三日讯】德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克电谢毛泽东主席对于他七十五寿辰的祝贺，电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    我衷心地感谢您和中华人民共和国中央人民政府在我的七十五岁生日对我的祝贺以及美好的礼物。谨祝贵国在国家建设及保卫和平中获得进一步的成就。德意志民主共和国为了巩固我们两民族间的友谊将继续尽一切努力。
                            德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克
                                     一九五一年一月十日于柏林
