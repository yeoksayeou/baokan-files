Title: Medical Workers from Jiangxi, Changsha and Other Places Organize Volunteer Surgical Teams to Work on the Korean Battlefield
Author:
Date: 1951-01-12
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 7th. The first group of volunteer surgical teams from Jiangxi Province, composed of twelve medical workers, left Nanchang on the 4th. More than 4,000 people from all walks of life in Nanchang held a grand farewell meeting at the station. People who came to see them off threw flowers at the twelve medical warriors. Xu Fansun, the leader of the surgical team and deputy director of surgery at the Affiliated Hospital of Jiangxi Provincial Medical College, thanked the people who came to see them off and promised to dedicate all their efforts to serving the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers and Korean People's Army soldiers after arriving on the Korean battlefield.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 9th. More than 400 medical workers in Changsha enthusiastically responded to the movement to Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect the Homeland, and Defend the Country, and eagerly signed up to organize "Volunteer Medical Surgical Teams to Resist America and Aid Korea." The first batch of twenty-seven people departed on the 5th. They were warmly seen off by medical workers from across the city, people from all walks of life, and their families. Tang Shengzhi, a member of the Hunan Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, and Ling Minyou, president of Xiangya Medical College, both encouraged the entire volunteer surgical team at the farewell meeting to promote patriotism and internationalism.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 9th. The volunteer surgical team composed of teachers and students from Zhongshan University Medical College in Guangzhou departed on the 4th to work on the Korean battlefield. More than 2,000 people from more than 50 units, including medical and health institutions, organizations, and schools across the city, went to the station to warmly see them off, presenting the surgical team with 13 banners and countless flowers.
　　During the movement to Resist America and Aid Korea, more than 200 teachers, students, and staff members of Zhongshan University Medical College had already volunteered to form the Bethune Combat Team, intensifying their studies of military medical and rescue techniques, and preparing to serve the motherland at any time. The aid-Korea surgical team consisted of 16 surgeons and nurses from the Bethune Combat Team, led by the famous surgical expert Professor Huang Rongzeng as the general leader and Professor Cai Jiyuan as the deputy general leader.

### 江西长沙等地医务工作者  组织志愿手术队赴朝鲜战地工作

1951-01-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　江西长沙等地医务工作者
    组织志愿手术队赴朝鲜战地工作
    【新华社南昌七日电】江西省十二位医务工作者志愿组成的首批志愿赴朝手术队已于四日自南昌出发。南昌市各界人民代表四千多人在车站举行了盛大欢送会。欢送的人们纷纷向十二位白衣战士投掷鲜花。手术队队长胥繁荪医师（江西省立医科专门学校附属医院外科副主任）向欢送的人们致谢，并保证到了朝鲜战场后，献出所有的力量，为英勇的我国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军的战士们服务。
    【新华社长沙九日电】长沙市医务工作者四百余人，热烈响应抗美援朝保家卫国运动，竞相报名组织“抗美援朝志愿医疗手术队”，首批二十七人已于五日出发。他们受到全市医务工作者及各界人民和自己家属的热烈欢送。中国保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会湖南分会委员唐生智及湘雅医学院院长凌敏猷等在欢送会上均以发扬爱国主义与国际主义精神鼓舞全体志愿手术队。
    【新华社广州九日电】广州中山大学医学院师生所组成的志愿援朝手术队于四日出发赴朝鲜战地工作。全市医药卫生机关、团体、学校五十多个单位二千余人前往车站热烈欢送，并献给手术队十三面锦旗和无数的鲜花。
    中山大学医学院在抗美援朝运动中，师生、工友二百余人早已志愿组成白求恩战斗队，加紧学习军医救护技术，准备随时为祖国服务。该援朝手术队即系由白求恩战斗队中的外科医生、护士十六人组成，由著名外科专家黄榕增教授为总领队，蔡纪辕教授为副总领队。
