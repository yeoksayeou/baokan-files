Title: Government Administration Council Holds its Sixty-Sixth Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 5th. The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its sixty-sixth meeting at 3:00 PM on the 5th. The meeting heard and approved the report by Xue Muqiao, Director of the Central Bureau of Private Enterprises, on the draft measures for the inventory of public shares and public property in enterprises, and passed several motions for appointments and removals, as well as motions for appointments to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行六十六次会议

1951-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行六十六次会议
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第六十六次政务会议于五日下午三时举行。会议听取和批准了中央私营企业局薛暮桥局长所作的关于企业中公股公产清理办法草案的报告，并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
