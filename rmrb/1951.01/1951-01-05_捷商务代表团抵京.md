Title: Czechoslovakian Commercial Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-01-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, January 4th—To promote trade between China and Czechoslovakia, the Czechoslovak Republic has once again dispatched a commercial delegation headed by Director Gao Haode to China for trade negotiations. The delegation arrived in Beijing by train on January 4th. Those who went to welcome them included Jia Shi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Trade Department of our Ministry of Trade, Han Xu, Section Chief of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Laigele, Secretary of the Czechoslovak Embassy.

### 捷商务代表团抵京

1951-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷商务代表团抵京
    【新华社四日讯】为了增进中捷两国贸易，捷克斯洛伐克共和国再度派遣了由高浩德司长率领之商务代表团来华商谈。该代表团已于一月四日乘车抵京。前往欢迎者有我贸易部国外贸易司副司长贾石，外交部交际处科长韩叙，捷克斯洛伐克大使馆秘书赖格勒等。
