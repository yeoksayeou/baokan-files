Title: Resolutely Implement Self-Governance, Self-Support, and Self-Propagation: Chongqing Christian and Catholic Figures Telegraph Premier Zhou to Endorse the Government Administration Council's Measures to Eliminate American Imperialist Cultural and Economic Aggression

Author:
Date: 1951-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, January 21st: Chongqing Christian and Catholic figures telegraphed Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Guo Moruo of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, warmly endorsing the Government Administration Council's decision on handling cultural, educational, relief organizations, and religious groups receiving American subsidies, and the order to control American property in China and freeze all American public and private deposits in China.

The telegram stated: American imperialists, through controlling religious, educational, cultural, medical, and relief undertakings with huge subsidies, deceive and anesthetize the Chinese people, instill a slave mentality, and attempt to enslave the Chinese people spiritually forever. We, personnel who have received church education and are working in the church, have a deep experience of this vicious conspiracy of imperialism. After liberation, American imperialism still continuously attempted to use these institutions and organizations to carry out its reactionary propaganda and activities, in an attempt to undermine our revolutionary cause and harm the interests of our people. This vicious crime ignites boundless anger in us. We will not allow American imperialist cultural aggression to continue in China, nor will we allow American imperialism to continue to use cultural and educational institutions and religious groups to carry out reactionary activities. This wise decision of your council has shattered the dream of American imperialist cultural aggression, cut off its devilish hand of cultural aggression, and at the same time liberated church groups receiving American subsidies from their shackles. We, the awakened Chinese Christians and Catholics, will, regardless of any difficulties, resolutely, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and you, completely and cleanly sever any relationship with American imperialism, immediately implement self-governance, self-support, and self-propagation, and establish the Chinese people's own church.

The Christian figures who signed the telegram were (in order of signature): Chen Chonggui (President of Chongqing Theological Seminary), Lu Guangting (Bishop of the Chongqing Diocese of the Methodist Church), Li Shoubao (Liaison Officer of the National Association of the YMCA in Beijing), Cao Xiaocang (Secretary of the Chongqing YMCA), Song Jieren (Secretary of the Chongqing YMCA), Zhang Huamin (Secretary of the Chongqing YMCA), Ge Yabo (Secretary of the Chongqing YWCA), Yang Shuyin (Secretary of the National Association of the YWCA), Li Zhaoqi (Secretary of the Chongqing YWCA), Li Guizhen (Director of the Chongqing YWCA), Zhang Daosheng (Pastor of the Chongqing Dajiaxiang Gospel Church of the Methodist Church), Chen Yuanrong (Pastor of the Chongqing Social Church of the Methodist Church), Cui Dongya (Director of the Chongqing YWCA), Li Gongpu (Principal of the Chongqing Qiming Elementary School of the Methodist Church), Yang Guoping (Principal of the Chongqing Guangyi Elementary School of the YMCA), Xiao Wenruo (Pastor of the Chongqing Shizi Church), Lin Tianhe (Pastor of the China Holy Bible Society), Zhu Xuan (China Holy Bible Society), Ma Ke (Consultant of the Chongqing Christian Sacred Work Office), Li Muqun (Pastor of the Chongqing Lutheran Church), Peng Jianxuan (President of Kuanren Hospital), Yan Chongjie (Pastor of the Chongqing Evangelical Lutheran Church), Lin Huayan (Principal of the Chongqing Qiujing Elementary School of the Methodist Church), Zhang Junci (Director of the Chongqing Fuyou Village of the Methodist Church); The Catholic figures were: Chen Guangpu (Father of the Chongqing Ruose Church), Liu Zongjiao (Father of the Chongqing Zhenyuan Church), Wang Xiaoci (Father of the Chongqing Zhenyuan Church), Chen Shuzhong (Father of the Chongqing Ruose Church), Liu Weiyang (Monk of the Chongqing Zhenyuan Church), Liu Renhui (Father of the Chongqing Ruose Church), Yang Lida (Zhenyuan Church), Lu Binqing (Vice President of the Chongqing Ren'ai Church Hospital), Cao Liangren (President of the Chongqing Ren'ai Church Hospital), Tang Heqin (Secretary of the Chongqing Ren'ai Church Hospital), Tong Shaosheng (Catholic), Zhou Zongqiong (Catholic), Yan Baozhang (Catholic).

### 坚决实行自治自养自传  重庆基督教和天主教人士致电周总理拥护政务院  肃清美帝文化经济侵略的措施

1951-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　坚决实行自治自养自传
    重庆基督教和天主教人士致电周总理拥护政务院
    肃清美帝文化经济侵略的措施
    【新华社重庆二十一日电】重庆基督教及天主教人士致电中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理和郭沫若副总理，热烈拥护政务院关于处理接受美国津贴的文化教育、救济机关及宗教团体的方针的决定和管制美国在我国财产及冻结美国在我国一切公私存款的命令。
    电文说：美帝国主义者通过以巨额款项津贴宗教、教育、文化、医疗、救济事业加以控制来进行对中国人民的欺骗、麻醉，灌输奴化思想，以图永远在精神上奴役中国人民。我们身受教会教育，同时在教会工作的人员，对于帝国主义这种恶毒的阴谋，是有深切的体验的。解放以后，美帝国主义仍然不断企图利用这些机关团体进行其反动宣传和活动，冀图破坏我革命事业、损害我人民利益。这种恶毒的罪行，令我们燃起无比的怒火。我们不容许美帝国主义的文化侵略再继续在中国进行，我们也不容许美帝国主义再继续利用文化教育机关和宗教团体来进行反动活动。钧院这一英明决定，粉碎了美帝国主义文化侵略的迷梦，斩断了它文化侵略的魔手，同时使接受美国津贴的教会团体脱离羁绊，获得解放，我们觉醒了的中国基督教徒和天主教徒将不顾任何困难，坚决地在毛主席和你们的领导下，完全的干净的斩断与美帝国主义的任何关系，立即实行自治、自养、自传，建立中国人民自己的教会。
    在电文上签名的基督教人士为（按签名先后为序）：陈崇桂（基督教重庆神学院院长）、陆光廷（基督教卫理公会重庆教区长）、李寿葆（基督教青年会全国协会驻京联络员）、曹小仓（基督教重庆青年会干事）、宋杰人（基督教重庆青年会干事）、张华民（基督教重庆青年会干事）、葛雅波（基督教重庆女青年会干事）、杨树因（基督教女青年会全国协会干事）、黎昭琪（基督教重庆女青年会干事）、李贵真（基督教重庆女青年会董事）、张道生（基督教卫理公会重庆戴家巷福音堂牧师）、陈元荣（基督教卫理公会重庆社交会堂牧师）、崔东亚（基督教重庆女青年会董事）、李恭朴（基督教卫理公会重庆启明小学校长）、杨国屏（基督教公谊会广益小学校长）、萧文若（基督教重庆十字堂牧师）、林天和（基督教中华圣经会牧师）、朱宣（基督教中华圣经会）、马可（基督教重庆基督徒圣工处顾问）、李牧群（基督教重庆路德会牧师）、彭建煊（宽仁医院院长）、燕重孑（基督教重庆信义会牧师）、林华颜（基督教卫理公会重庆求精小学校长）、张君慈（基督教卫理公会重庆福幼村村长）；天主教人士为：陈光普（天主教重庆若瑟堂神父）、刘宗焦（天主教重庆真原堂神父）、王孝慈（天主教重庆真原堂神父）、陈恕忠（天主教重庆若瑟堂神父）、刘维洋（天主教重庆真原堂修士）、刘仁辉（天主教重庆若瑟堂神父）、杨立达（天主教真原堂）、鲁斌卿（天主教重庆仁爱堂医院副院长）、曹良仁（天主教重庆仁爱堂医院院长）、唐鹤琴（天主教重庆仁爱堂医院秘书）、童少生（天主教）、周宗琼（天主教），鄢宝璋（天主教）。
