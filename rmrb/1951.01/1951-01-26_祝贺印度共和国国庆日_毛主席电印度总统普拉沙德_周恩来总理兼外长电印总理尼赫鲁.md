Title: Congratulations on the National Day of the Republic of India Chairman Mao Cables President Prasad of India Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Cables Prime Minister Nehru of India
Author:
Date: 1951-01-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: On January 26th, the National Day of the Republic of India, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the State Council respectively sent cables to President Prasad and Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Nehru of India to express their congratulations. The texts of the telegrams are as follows:

(1) Chairman Mao's telegram to President Prasad of India
To His Excellency Rajendra Prasad, President of the Republic of India:

	On the occasion of your country's National Day, I, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend my congratulations to Your Excellency and the government and people of India, and wish the friendship between the peoples of China and India to grow day by day.
		Chairman Mao Zedong
		Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
			January 24, 1951, Beijing

(2) Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's telegram to Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Nehru of India
To His Excellency Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru, Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Government of the Republic of India:

	On the occasion of your country's National Day, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the government and people of India, and wish the peoples of China and India to work together in the cause of striving for lasting peace in Asia and the world.
	Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
	State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
		January 24, 1951, Beijing

### 祝贺印度共和国国庆日  毛主席电印度总统普拉沙德  周恩来总理兼外长电印总理尼赫鲁

1951-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺印度共和国国庆日
    毛主席电印度总统普拉沙德
    周恩来总理兼外长电印总理尼赫鲁
    【新华社二十五日讯】一月二十六日为印度共和国国庆日，我中央人民政府毛泽东主席与政务院周恩来总理兼外交部部长分别致电该国总统普拉沙德及总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁，表示祝贺。电文如下：
    （一）毛主席致普拉沙德总统电印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德先生：
    欣逢贵国国庆，我代表中华人民共和国政府及人民谨向阁下及印度政府和人民致庆贺之忱，并祝中印两国人民的友谊日益发展。
        中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席  毛泽东
            一九五一年一月二十四日北京
    （二）周恩来总理兼外长致尼赫鲁总理兼外长电印度共和国政府总理兼外交部部长潘迪特·雅瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁先生：
    欣逢贵国国庆，谨向阁下及印度政府和人民致热烈的庆贺，并祝中印两国人民在争取亚洲及世界持久和平的事业上共同努力。
      中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长  周恩来
          一九五一年一月二十四日北京
