Title: Latin American Peace Fighters Collect Over Seven Million Peace Signatures
Author:
Date: 1951-01-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 11th: Peace fighters in Latin American countries, disregarding severe police terror, have collected over seven million signatures for the Stockholm Appeal. According to newspaper reports: before December of last year, the Argentine Committee for the Defense of Peace had collected 1.5 million signatures, Brazil 4 million, Cuba 1 million, Mexico 300,000, Colombia 150,000, Uruguay 100,000, and Venezuela 75,000.

### 拉丁美洲各国和平战士们征集到七百余万和平签名

1951-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　拉丁美洲各国和平战士们征集到七百余万和平签名
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社纽约十一日讯：拉丁美洲各国的和平战士们，不顾严重的警察恐怖，已为斯德哥尔摩宣言征集到七百多万人的签名。据报纸消息：在去年十二月前，阿根廷保卫和平委员会已征集一百五十万人签名，巴西征集到四百万人签名，古巴一百万，墨西哥三十万，哥伦比亚十五万，乌拉圭十万，委内瑞拉七万五千。
