    Our Great Motherland
    This is a corner of the "Great Wall" near Nankou. The Great Wall starts from Shanhaiguan in Northeast China and ends at Jiayuguan in Northwest Gansu, winding over the lofty mountains and ridges, with a length of more than 5,000 li. It is one of the greatest projects in the world that began to be built in the third century BC.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是南口附近的“万里长城”的一角。长城起于我国东北的山海关，止于西北甘肃的嘉峪关，蜿蜒在崇山峻岭之上，长达五千余里。它是从公历纪元前三世纪就开始建筑的世界最伟大的工程之一。
