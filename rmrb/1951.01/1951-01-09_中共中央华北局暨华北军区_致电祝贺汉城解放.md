Title: CCP Central Committee North China Bureau and North China Military Region Send Telegram Congratulating the Liberation of Seoul
Author:
Date: 1951-01-09
Page: 1

　　CCP Central Committee North China Bureau and North China Military Region
    Send Telegram Congratulating the Liberation of Seoul
    [Report from this newspaper] The North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent a telegram on the 5th to all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army, celebrating the victory of the liberation of Seoul. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    Thanks to your heroic and resolute fighting, the capital of Korea, Seoul, has been liberated. This is another great victory for the people of China and Korea and all peace-loving people around the world, and another failure for American imperialism. The victory of the people of China and Korea once again proves that the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army, who are fighting for justice, are powerful and invincible, and that American imperialism is just a paper tiger that can be completely defeated. On behalf of all the soldiers and civilians in North China, we extend our warm congratulations to you - the heroic sons and daughters of the people of China and Korea, and vow to support you with all our strength in the struggle to finally defeat the American imperialist aggressor troops, liberate the whole of Korea, and defend Asian and world peace!

### 中共中央华北局暨华北军区  致电祝贺汉城解放

1951-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央华北局暨华北军区
    致电祝贺汉城解放
    【本报讯】中国共产党中央华北局、中国人民解放军华北军区于五日致电中国人民志愿部队和朝鲜人民军全体将士，庆祝解放汉城的胜利。电文如下：
    由于你们英勇坚决的战斗，朝鲜的首都汉城解放了。这是中朝两国人民和全世界一切爱好和平人民的又一伟大胜利，是美帝国主义的又一次失败。中朝两国人民的胜利，再次证明了为正义而战的中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军是强大无敌的，而美国帝国主义是只纸老虎，是完全可以被打败的。我们谨代表华北全体军民向你们——中朝两国人民的英雄儿女们遥致热烈的祝贺，誓以全力支援你们，为最后击败美帝国主义侵略军，解放全朝鲜，保卫亚洲和世界和平而奋斗！
