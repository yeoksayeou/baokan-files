Title: Gatherings in East China and Shanghai Welcome the Return of World Peace Congress Delegates
Author:
Date: 1951-01-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai: A grand gathering was held on the afternoon of the 3rd in East China and Shanghai to welcome the victorious return of the East China and Shanghai delegates Ma Yinchu, Sheng Pihua, Lu Cui, Jin Zhonghua, Ba Jin, Huang Zongying, Liu Liangmo, Deng Yuzhi, and Lin Sanliang from the Second World Peace Congress Chinese Delegation. Among those present were Liu Changsheng, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Vice Chairmen Xia Yan and Liu Hongsheng, and all members of the committee, as well as Zeng Shan, Vice Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, and representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, military units, factories, institutions, schools, and others, totaling more than two thousand people.
　　At the meeting, Chairman Liu Changsheng delivered a welcoming speech, extending the highest respect and congratulations to the delegates who had made significant contributions to the people's motherland and world peace. Ma Yinchu reported on the brilliant achievements of the Second World Peace Congress. He pointed out that this glorious victory should be attributed to the continuous efforts of peace-loving people around the world. Subsequently, Sheng Pihua, Jin Zhonghua, Lu Cui, Liu Liangmo, and others spoke successively. They unanimously pointed out that we must not wait for peace, but must strive for peace. Therefore, continuing to deeply develop the movement to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend the country is the most correct path to implement the spirit and resolutions of the Second World Peace Congress.

### 华东及上海市各界集会  欢迎世界和大代表归来

1951-01-05
第1版()
专栏：社论

　　华东及上海市各界集会  欢迎世界和大代表归来
    【本报讯】据新华社上海电：华东暨上海各界于三日下午举行盛大集会，欢迎出席第二届世界保卫和平大会中国代表团华东暨上海代表马寅初、盛丕华、陆璀、金仲华、巴金、黄宗英、刘良模、邓裕志、林三良胜利归来。到会的有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会上海分会主席刘长胜、副主席夏衍、刘鸿生和全体委员，华东军政委员会副主席曾山，上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年和各民主党派、人民团体、部队、工厂、机关、学校等代表二千余人。
    会上，刘长胜主席致欢迎词，向胜利归来的对人民祖国和世界和平具有重大贡献的代表们致崇高的敬意和庆贺。马寅初报告了二届世界和大辉煌的成就。他指出：这光辉的胜利，应归功于全世界爱好和平人民的不断努力。接着，盛丕华、金仲华、陆璀、刘良模等相继讲话。他们一致指出：绝不能等待和平，必须去争取和平。因此继续深入开展抗美援朝保家卫国运动，正是贯彻二届世界和大的精神与决议的最正确的道路。
