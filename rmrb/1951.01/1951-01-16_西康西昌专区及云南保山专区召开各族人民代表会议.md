Title: People's Representative Conferences Convened in Xichang Special District, Xikang and Baoshan Special District, Yunnan
Author: 
Date: 1951-01-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 13th: The Xichang Special District of Xikang Province held its first People's Representative Conference of All Ethnic Groups in late December of last year. Three hundred and forty representatives from eight ethnic groups, including Han, Yi, Hui, Lisu, Mosuo, Tibetan, Miao, and Guzong, attended the meeting.

　　The Xichang Special District is located in the southeastern part of Xikang Province. It includes an area of ​​over a thousand li stretching from the Dadu River in the north to the Jinsha River in the south, bordering the Yalong River in the west and Sichuan in the east. It governs counties such as Xichang, Yuexi, Mianning, Zhaojue, Yanyuan, Yanbian, Huili, Dechang, and Ningnan, with a population of approximately 2.5 million, half of whom are ethnic minorities such as the Yi, and the rest are Han.

　　The conference successively listened to and adopted the work report of the Xichang Military Control Commission of the People's Liberation Army over the past nine months, established the All-Ethnic Groups and All-Circles Consultative Committee of the district, and laid the foundation for the district's unified leadership in carrying out various construction tasks in all counties in the future. The conference also formulated a national unity pact to further strengthen unity among ethnic groups and mediate various disputes. At the same time, the conference made a resolution on the future work tasks of the district: politically, in accordance with the national policies in the Common Program, to establish regional autonomy and joint people's governments of all ethnic groups in areas where ethnic groups live in concentrated and mixed communities; militarily, to continue to suppress the remaining bandits and special agents, and stabilize the lives of the people of all ethnic groups; economically, to restore transportation in a planned manner, organize cooperatives and establish trade and production institutions, and find ways to supply the necessities of the people of all ethnic groups and purchase local products; in terms of culture and education, to rectify schools, and formally open ethnic minority cadre schools to train ethnic minority cadres.

　　Finally, the conference unanimously passed telegrams to Chairman Mao and the Chinese People's Volunteers to express respect and condolences, and passed a declaration to resist America, aid Korea, and defend our homeland. In the telegram to Chairman Mao, all representatives expressed the determination of the 2.5 million people of all ethnic groups in the district to resolutely fight for the construction and defense of the motherland. In the declaration to resist America, aid Korea, and defend our homeland, the representatives of all ethnic groups called on the people of all ethnic groups in the district to take practical actions to further strengthen unity, actively pay public grain, and eliminate bandits and special agents to support the Korean front in achieving greater victories.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 13th: The Baoshan Special District in western Yunnan Province convened a People's Representative Conference of All Ethnic Groups in the district in the late last month. More than nine hundred representatives from nineteen ethnic groups, including Bo, Shantou, Benglong, Achang, Kawa, Lisu, Minjia, Miao, Hui, Han, and Yi, from fourteen counties and bureaus (established bureaus) in the district attended the meeting.

　　This conference was convened with the direct help and guidance of the Second Sub-group of the Central People's Government's Southwest Nationalities Visiting Group. The conference successively listened to the reports of the visiting group's leaders, Xia Kangnong and Wang Lianfang, and the Vice Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial People's Government, Zhang Chong (Yi ethnicity), on the current situation and the national policies of the Central People's Government, as well as the work report of Zheng Gang, the secretary of the CPC Baoshan Local Committee, on the work of the district over the past eight months. Fang Keguang, a Dai representative from Luxi, reported to the conference on the experience and impressions of participating in the National Day celebrations in Beijing. The Ethnic Affairs Committee of Baoshan District was established at the meeting, and the district's 1951 ethnic work plan was adopted.

　　After accusing the imperialists and the Kuomintang bandit gangs of various acts of provocation, the ethnic representatives attending the meeting unanimously expressed their gratitude to Chairman Mao for his deep concern for them. The representatives unanimously agreed that the people of all ethnic groups will further strengthen unity in the future, assist the government in suppressing the remaining bandits and special agents, and consolidate the southwestern national defense of the motherland.

### 西康西昌专区及云南保山专区召开各族人民代表会议

1951-01-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　西康西昌专区及云南保山专区召开各族人民代表会议
    【新华社重庆十三日电】西康省西昌专区于去年十二月下旬，举行首届各族各界人民代表会议。到会汉、彝、回、傈僳、摩些、藏、苗、古宗等八个民族的代表三百四十人。
    西昌专区在西康省东南部。包括北起大渡河，南至金沙江，西靠雅龙江，东与四川毗邻的纵横千余里地区。计辖西昌、越西、冕宁、昭觉、盐源、盐边、会理、德昌、宁南等县，人口约二百五十余万，其中半数是彝族等少数民族，余为汉族。
    会议先后听取和通过了人民解放军西昌军管会九个月来的工作报告，成立了该区各族各界协商委员会，奠定了该区今后统一领导全区各县进行各项建设工作的基础。会议并制定民族团结公约，进一步加强民族间的团结，调解各种纠纷。会议同时作出该区今后工作任务的决议：在政治上，根据共同纲领中的民族政策，在各民族聚居及杂居地区成立区域自治和各民族联合的人民政府；在军事上，继续肃清残余土匪、特务，安定各民族人民的生活；在经济上，有计划地恢复交通，组织合作社及建立贸易生产机构，设法供应各民族人民的必需品和收购当地土产；在文化教育方面，整顿学校，并将正式开办少数民族干部学校，培养少数民族干部。
    会议最后一致通过向毛主席和中国人民志愿军致敬与慰问的电文，并通过了抗美援朝保家卫国宣言。全体代表在致毛主席的电中表示了全区二百五十余万各民族人民，坚决为建设和保卫祖国而奋斗的决心。在抗美援朝保家卫国宣言中各民族代表号召全区各民族人民，以进一步加强团结、踊跃缴纳公粮、肃清土匪特务的实际行动，来支援朝鲜前线获得更大的胜利。
    【新华社昆明十三日电】云南省西部保山专区于上月下旬召开全区各民族人民代表会议。出席会议的有该区十四个县、局（设治局）的僰、山头、崩龙、阿昌、卡瓦、傈僳、民家、苗、回、汉、彝等十九个民族的九百余名代表。
    这次会议是在中央人民政府西南各民族访问团第二分团直接帮助与指导下召开的。会议先后听取了访问团夏康农、王连芳正副团长、云南省人民政府张冲（彝族）副主席等关于目前形势与中央人民政府民族政策的报告及中共保山地方委员会书记郑刚关于全区八个月来的工作报告。潞西泰族代表方克光等向大会报告了晋京参加国庆盛典的经过与观感。会上并成立了保山区民族事务委员会，通过该区一九五一年的民族工作计划。
    与会的各民族代表控诉了帝国主义与国民党匪帮的种种挑拨罪行后，一致感激毛主席对他们的深切关怀。代表们一致协议：各民族人民今后将进一步加强团结，协助政府肃清残余土匪特务，巩固祖国西南国防。
