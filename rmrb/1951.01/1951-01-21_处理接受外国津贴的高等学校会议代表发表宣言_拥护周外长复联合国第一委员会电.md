Title: Meeting of Representatives from Higher Education Institutions Handling Foreign Subsidies Issues Issues Declaration Endorsing Foreign Minister Zhou's Telegram to the UN First Committee
Author:
Date: 1951-01-21
Page: 1

　　The representatives of various universities and colleges attending the Conference on Handling Higher Education Institutions Receiving Foreign Subsidies convened by the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, issued a declaration endorsing Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's telegram to the First Committee of the United Nations as follows:

　　At the Conference on Handling Higher Education Institutions Receiving Foreign Subsidies convened by the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, we deeply felt the greatness of our motherland and our Chinese people's strength. We have absolute confidence in accomplishing the glorious task of "finally, thoroughly, permanently, and completely ending the cultural aggression of the Chinese people by American imperialism for more than a hundred years." On the 18th, we read the telegram from Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government to the First Committee of the United Nations, and we felt even more deeply the glory and pride of the Chinese people standing up. All of us attending representatives fully agree with and enthusiastically support Foreign Minister Zhou's firm and reasonable reply to the First Committee of the United Nations: to hold negotiations between the countries concerned on the basis of agreeing to withdraw all foreign troops from Korea and that the Korean people should resolve Korean internal affairs themselves, in order to quickly end the Korean War; the content of the negotiations must include the withdrawal of the United States armed forces from Taiwan and the Taiwan Strait and related Far Eastern issues; the countries participating in the negotiations should include the People's Republic of China, the USSR, the United Kingdom, the United States, France, India, and Egypt, and the legitimate status of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations should be determined from the beginning of the seven-nation conference; the venue for the seven-nation conference should be selected in China. In order to safeguard the peace and happiness of all mankind and to ensure the complete independence of the culture and education of our great motherland, we must strengthen our learning, raise our political consciousness, unite closely, implement patriotic education against imperialism, and thoroughly eliminate the influence of American imperialist cultural aggression. We use such determination and action to support Foreign Minister Zhou's glorious reply.

### 处理接受外国津贴的高等学校会议代表发表宣言  拥护周外长复联合国第一委员会电

1951-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　处理接受外国津贴的高等学校会议代表发表宣言
    拥护周外长复联合国第一委员会电
    【本报讯】处理接受外国津贴的高等学校会议各校代表，发表拥护周外长复联合国第一委员会电宣言如下：
    我们在中央人民政府教育部召开的处理接受外国津贴的高等学校会议上，真切地感觉到我们祖国和我们中国人民力量的伟大，对于“把一百余年来美国帝国主义对中国人民的文化侵略，最后的、彻底的、永远的、全部的加以结束”的光荣任务，有了绝对完成的信心。十八日我们读到中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来复联合国第一委员会电，我们更深地体会到中国人民站起来了的光荣和骄傲。我们全体与会代表完全同意并且热烈拥护周外长给联合国第一委员会的坚决的、合理的答复：在同意从朝鲜撤退一切外国军队及朝鲜内政由朝鲜人民自己解决的基础上举行有关各国的谈判，以迅速结束朝鲜战争；谈判内容，必须包括美国武装力量从台湾及台湾海峡撤退和远东有关问题；举行谈判的国家，应包括中华人民共和国、苏联、英国、美国、法国、印度和埃及七国，中华人民共和国在联合国的合法地位即从举行七国会议起予以确定；七国会议的地点，应选在中国。为了保障全世界人类的和平幸福，为了使我们伟大祖国文化教育的完全独立，我们一定要加强学习，提高政治觉悟，紧密团结起来，贯彻反帝爱国主义的教育，彻底肃清美帝国主义文化侵略的影响。我们以这样的决心和行动来拥护周外长的光荣的答复。
