Title: All Professors and Lecturers of the Peking University College of Science and Engineering Issue Statement Endorsing National Science Associations' Popular Science Appeal, Resolved to Contribute All Strength to Serve National Defense Construction
Author:
Date: 1951-01-10
Page: 3

[Reported by this newspaper] All 182 professors, lecturers, and teaching assistants from the Peking University Colleges of Science and Engineering signed and issued a statement responding to the call from the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies and the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, expressing their willingness to contribute all their strength to serve national defense construction.

The statement begins by saying: The solemn call of the Second World Peace Congress resolution expresses the firm and unwavering confidence of people all over the world: We "must not wait for peace, we must fight for peace!" In order to more forcefully strike at the imperialist aggressors, the National Science Associations and the Popular Science Associations jointly issued a declaration (see this newspaper on December 12, 1950), calling on natural science workers in all fields of science, engineering, agriculture, and medicine across the country to unite and concentrate all their strength on consolidating national defense, and to fight for lasting peace. We resolutely support this declaration and are willing to do everything we can to fight for the consolidation of the motherland's national defense.

The statement then analyzes: The American imperialists have suffered serious blows in the war of aggression against Korea from the heroic Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. However, aggression is the nature of imperialism, and they will never give up. The results of the Truman-Attlee talks have shown their ambition to continue expanding armed aggression. Undoubtedly, only by continuously giving the aggressors greater blows can we smash their conspiracies and achieve our goal of protecting our homes and defending our country, and maintaining peace.

The statement concludes: We natural science workers are willing to take practical actions to implement our determination to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country. At a time when the work on the front lines of national defense and production urgently needs us, we are extremely willing to contribute all our strength to the motherland and serve the people's army, navy, and air force with our scientific and technological knowledge, serving the work of national defense and production construction. We hope that the National Science Associations and the Popular Science Associations can quickly organize natural science workers across the country, and we also hope that a specialized agency can be established to uniformly lead national scientific research work and fully unleash the great strength of natural science workers across the country.

### 北京大学理工学院全体教授讲师  发表声明拥护全国科联科普号召  决心贡献一切力量服务国防建设

1951-01-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京大学理工学院全体教授讲师
    发表声明拥护全国科联科普号召
    决心贡献一切力量服务国防建设
    【本报讯】北京大学理工两学院全体教授讲师助教一百八十二人，签名发表声明，响应中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会的号召，表示要贡献一切力量，为国防建设工作服务。
    声明首先称：第二届世界和大决议的庄严号召，道出了全世界人民的坚定不移的信心：我们“绝不能等待和平，必须争取和平”！为了更有力地打击帝国主义侵略者，全国科联、科普发表了联合宣言（见一九五○年十二月十二日本报），号召全国理工农医各方面的自然科学工作者，团结一致，把一切力量贯注在巩固国防的工作中，为争取持久的和平而斗争。我们坚决拥护这个宣言，愿意尽一切力量为巩固祖国国防而奋斗。
    声明继而分析：美帝国主义者在侵略朝鲜的战争中，已经遭受了英勇的朝鲜人民军与我国人民志愿部队的严重打击，但是，侵略是帝国主义的本质，它们是决不会就此罢休的。杜、艾会谈的结果，就已表明它们打算继续扩大武装侵略的野心。无疑地，只有继续不断地予侵略者们以更大的打击，才能粉碎它们的阴谋，才能达到我们保家卫国、维护和平的目的。
    声明最后表示：我们自然科学工作者愿以实际行动来贯彻我们抗美援朝、保家卫国的决心。在目前国防与生产战线上的工作迫切地需要着我们的时候，我们极愿将所有的力量贡献给祖国，把我们的科学技术知识服务于人民陆军、海军和空军，服务于国防和生产建设的工作。我们希望全国科联、科普能迅速地将全国自然科学工作者组织起来，更希望能成立专门机构，统一领导全国科学研究工作，充分发挥全国自然科学工作者的伟大力量。
