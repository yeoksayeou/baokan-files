Title: Indian Consul-General in Shanghai Hosts Banquet to Celebrate National Day; Many Local Government Leaders Attend

Author: 
Date: 1951-01-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 27th—Mr. Yeye Sen, the Consul-General of the Republic of India in Shanghai, held a banquet at 11:30 a.m. on the 26th to celebrate the first anniversary of the founding of the Republic of India. Among the guests were Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee; Zeng Shan and Ma Yinchu, Vice Chairmen; Pan Hannian and Sheng Pihua, Deputy Mayors of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government; Hu Juewen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee; Huang Hua, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government; heads of departments, bureaus, and offices of the East China Military and Political Committee and the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, as well as Vladimir, Soviet Consul-General in Shanghai; Sli Mak, Czech Consul-General in Shanghai; and Jabonine, Director of the Shanghai Branch of the Soviet Trade Representative Office in China, totaling about one hundred people. During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to the health of the leaders of China and India.

### 印驻沪总领事举宴庆祝国庆  我当地政府首长多人参加

1951-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　印驻沪总领事举宴庆祝国庆
    我当地政府首长多人参加
    【新华社上海二十七日电】印度共和国驻沪总领事雁谒森为庆祝印度共和国成立一周年纪念，特于二十六日上午十一时半举行宴会。到会来宾有华东军政委员会主席饶漱石，副主席曾山、马寅初，上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年，盛丕华，上海市人民代表会议协商委员会副主席胡厥文，上海市人民政府外事处处长黄华，华东军政委员会与上海市人民政府各部、局、处首长，及苏联驻沪总领事符拉基米洛夫，捷克驻沪总领事斯立马克，苏联驻华商务代表处上海分处处长贾伯宁等约百人。席间宾主频为中、印两国元首的健康而举杯。
