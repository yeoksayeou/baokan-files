Title: Chinese Students in the UK Write to Chairman Mao in Response to the Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect the Homeland and Defend the Nation Movement
Author:
Date: 1951-01-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th - London News: The General Association of Chinese Students in the UK wrote to Chairman Mao on December 26th of last year, responding to the Chinese people's Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect the Homeland and Defend the Nation Movement, and paying tribute to the heroic Chinese People's Volunteer Army. The original letter stated: "The powerful voice of the people across the country has reached here. We are determined to accelerate the completion of our studies and return to the motherland as soon as possible to join the ranks of the people of the motherland. We enthusiastically support the just struggle to Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect the Homeland and Defend the Nation. The American imperialists' aggressive attempts will be crushed, as has been demonstrated in the recent victories in the Korean war situation. Here, we excitedly celebrate the liberation of Pyongyang and look forward to greater and final victory."

### 我留英学生写信给毛主席  响应抗美援朝保家卫国运动

1951-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　我留英学生写信给毛主席
    响应抗美援朝保家卫国运动
    【新华社布拉格七日电】伦敦消息：留英中国学生总会于去年十二月二十六日写信给毛主席，响应中国人民抗美援朝保家卫国运动，并向英勇的中国人民志愿军致敬。原函内称：“全国人民强大的呼声已经传到了这里，我们决意加速完成学业，尽早回国，投入祖国人民的阵营。我们热烈地支持抗美援朝保家卫国的正义斗争。美国帝国主义的侵略企图必被粉碎，这已在最近朝鲜战局的胜利中表现出来。在这里，我们兴奋地庆祝平壤的解放，并盼望着更大的和最后的胜利。”
