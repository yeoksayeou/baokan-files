　　Cultural Life Updates
　　New Philosophy Research Association Discusses "On Practice"
　　The "New Philosophy Research Association" held a symposium at 9:00 AM on January 28th to discuss Chairman Mao's "On Practice." Attendees included Ai Siqi, Tang Yongtong, Feng Youlan, Zheng Xin, Jin Yuelin, He Lin, and dozens of others. Ai Siqi served as chairman. The discussion was very active. All members felt that Chairman Mao's creative work should be studied more, and it was decided to hold another symposium to continue the discussion.
　　15,000 Farmers in Ningxia Participate in Winter Studies
　　More than 15,000 farmers in Ningxia Province are participating in winter studies, exceeding the original plan by more than 25%. Winter schools in various locations started one after another in early November last year. The common feature of winter schools in various parts of the province this year is to combine cultural learning, the study of various policies, and patriotic ideological education of resisting America and aiding Korea. In Pingluo, Zhongning, and other counties, the number of students suddenly increased after the number of current affairs courses per week was increased. Whenever there was a current affairs class, many farmers who did not attend winter school also went to listen.
　　"New Movie" Second Issue to Be Published Soon
　　The "New Movie" magazine, edited by the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, will be published on February 1st in its second issue. In order to cooperate with the publicity of the "New Film Exhibition Month" which will begin on February 20th, this issue published a "Special Issue for New Films Exhibition Month Produced by State-Owned Film Studios."

### 文化生活动态

1951-01-30
第3版()
专栏：文化生活动态

　　文化生活动态
    新哲学研究会讨论《实践论》
    “新哲学研究会”于一月二十八日上午九时举行座谈会，讨论毛主席的《实践论》。到会者有艾思奇、汤用彤、冯友兰、郑昕、金岳霖、贺麟等数十人。由艾思奇任主席。发言甚为踊跃。各会员都认为毛主席的这篇创造性的著作，应多加研究，决定以后再召开座谈会继续讨论。
    宁夏一万五千农民入冬学
    宁夏省参加冬学的农民有一万五千余人，超过原计划百分之二十五以上。各地冬学均于去年十一月上旬先后开学。本年度该省各地冬学的共同特点，是把文化学习、各种政策学习与抗美援朝爱国主义的思想教育结合起来。平罗、中宁等县冬学由于把每星期的时事课程增多后，学生便骤然增多。每当上时事课时，很多未上冬学的农民也前往听讲。
    《新电影》第二期即将出版
    中央人民政府文化部电影局所主编的《新电影》杂志，第二期即将于二月一日出刊。该刊为配合将于二月二十日开始举行的“新片展览月”宣传，这一期刊登了“国营电影制片厂出品新片展览月特辑”。
