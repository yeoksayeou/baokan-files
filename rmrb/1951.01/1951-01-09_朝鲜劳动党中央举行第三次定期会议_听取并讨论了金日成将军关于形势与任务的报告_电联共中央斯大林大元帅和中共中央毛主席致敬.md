Title: The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Holds its Third Plenary Meeting, Hearing and Discussing General Kim Il-sung's Report on the Situation and Tasks, and Sends Respectful Greetings to Marshal Stalin of the CPSU Central Committee and Chairman Mao of the CPC Central Committee

Author:

Date: 1951-01-09
Page: 4

　　The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Holds its Third Plenary Meeting
    Hearing and Discussing General Kim Il-sung's Report on the Situation and Tasks, and Sends Respectful Greetings to Marshal Stalin of the CPSU Central Committee and Chairman Mao of the CPC Central Committee
    [Xinhua News Agency, 8th] According to the Korean Central News Agency: The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea convened its third regular meeting from December 21st to 23rd, 1950. The meeting listened to General Kim Il-sung's report on the "Current Situation and Current Tasks" and Park Heon-yeong's report on "Unifying the Labor Organizations of the North and South."
    On the first day of the meeting, after listening to Park Heon-yeong's report on celebrating Marshal Stalin's 71st birthday, it adopted a congratulatory letter to Marshal Stalin in the name of the Third Plenary Meeting of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea.
    After listening to and discussing General Kim Il-sung's report on the "Current Situation and Current Tasks," the meeting unanimously decided to adopt it as the guiding principle for the work of the Workers' Party of Korea.
    The meeting listened to and discussed Park Heon-yeong's report on "Unifying the Labor Organizations of the North and South" and passed relevant resolutions.
    On the last day of the meeting, it passed telegrams of respect to Marshal Stalin of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央举行第三次定期会议  听取并讨论了金日成将军关于形势与任务的报告  电联共中央斯大林大元帅和中共中央毛主席致敬

1951-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜劳动党中央举行第三次定期会议
    听取并讨论了金日成将军关于形势与任务的报告  电联共中央斯大林大元帅和中共中央毛主席致敬
    【新华社八日讯】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会于一九五○年十二月二十一日至二十三日，召开了第三次定期会议。会议听取了金日成将军关于“目前形势和当前任务”的报告，和朴宪永关于“统一南北劳动团体”的报告。
    会议第一日在听取朴宪永关于庆祝斯大林大元帅七十一岁寿诞的报告后，通过了以朝鲜劳动党中央委员会第三次定期会议的名义致斯大林大元帅的贺信。
    会议在听取和讨论了金日成将军关于“目前形势和当前任务”的报告后，一致决定将它作为朝鲜劳动党组织的工作指导方针。
    会议听取和讨论了朴宪永关于“统一南北劳动团体”的报告，通过了有关的决议。
    会议的最后一天，通过了向苏联共产党中央委员会斯大林大元帅和中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席致敬的电文。
