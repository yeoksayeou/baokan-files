Title: CPC Central Committee Chairman Mao Zedong Cables the Italian Communist Party Central Committee: Congratulating the Italian Communist Party on its 30th Anniversary

Author:
Date: 1951-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: January 21st marks the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Italian Communist Party. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, sent a cable to Luigi Longo, Deputy Secretary-General of the Italian Communist Party, and the Italian Communist Party Central Committee to congratulate them. The cable reads as follows:

Comrade Longo and all comrades of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party:

On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Italian Communist Party, please accept our warm congratulations. The Italian Communist Party has achieved great successes in the past struggle against Fascism and in the current struggle against American aggression and war plans, and for a democratic Italy of the people, thus making itself the core of the entire Italian working class and the entire patriotic people of Italy. We firmly believe that your just cause, under the correct leadership of Comrade Togliatti and the Italian Communist Party Central Committee, will surely achieve final victory.

　　　　Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Mao Zedong

### 中共中央毛主席电意共中央  祝贺意共成立三十周年纪念

1951-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央毛主席电意共中央
    祝贺意共成立三十周年纪念
    【新华社二十二日讯】一月二十一日为意大利共产党成立三十周年纪念日。中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东致电意大利共产党副总书记隆哥和意共中央祝贺。电文如下：隆哥同志和意大利共产党中央委员会全体同志们：
    值此意大利共产党成立三十周年之际，谨向你们致以热烈的祝贺。意大利共产党在过去反对法西斯主义的斗争中以及在现在反对美国的侵略和战争计划、争取人民民主的意大利的斗争中，已经得到巨大成绩，因而使自己成为全意大利工人阶级和全意大利爱国人民的核心。我们坚信你们的正义事业在托里亚蒂同志和意共中央的正确领导之下，必定要得到最后的胜利。
            中国共产党中央委员会主席  毛泽东
