Title: People from All Over Have Donated Over 6.3 Billion Yuan in Comfort Money
Author:
Date: 1951-01-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th dispatch: The nationwide movement to express the people's solicitude to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army has achieved great success. According to incomplete statistics reported by local branches of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression to the head office, from November of last year to January 16th of this year, local branches have received a total of 6,323 million yuan in comfort money donated by people from all walks of life, 254,725 comfort letters, 153,606 comfort bags, and 309,259 other comfort items. This number does not include reports not yet submitted to the head office by local branches and a portion of funds and materials that have already been directly shipped.

### 各地人民捐献慰劳金已达六十三亿元以上

1951-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　各地人民捐献慰劳金已达六十三亿元以上
    【新华社十八日讯】全国人民慰劳我国人民志愿军及朝鲜人民军的运动，已获得巨大成绩。据中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会各地分会向总会报告的不完全统计，自去年十一月至今年一月十六日止，各地分会共已收到各界人民捐献的慰劳金六十三亿二千三百余万元，慰问信二十五万四千七百二十五封，慰问袋十五万三千六百零六个，其他慰劳品三十万九千二百五十九件。此数尚不包括各地分会未向总会报告和一部分已经直接运寄的款物在内。
