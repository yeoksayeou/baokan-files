Title: Polish, Vietnamese Trade Union Organizations Send New Year's Greetings to All-China Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1951-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st—Trade union organizations in Poland and Vietnam have sent cables and letters to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, congratulating them on the New Year of 1951. The cables and letters are as follows:

(1) Polish Trade Union's Congratulatory Telegram

To the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

On the occasion of the New Year, we extend our fraternal congratulations and hope for a bright future.

Central Committee of Polish Trade Unions

(2) Vietnamese General Confederation of Labor's New Year Letter

To the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

On this New Year's occasion, the Vietnamese General Confederation of Labor wishes to extend its fraternal and deep affections to the Chinese labor comrades and their admirable leaders, wishing them a wonderful year.

Vietnamese workers are willing to follow the glorious example, fighting shoulder to shoulder with comrades from China and Korea, in order to annihilate the French colonialists, and smash the US imperialist aggressive bloc and thwart their plans to create a new war.

Nguyen Minh, Representative for Foreign Liaison, Vietnamese General Confederation of Labor

### 波兰、越南工会组织  向我全国总工会祝贺新年

1951-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰、越南工会组织
    向我全国总工会祝贺新年
    【新华社三十一日讯】波兰和越南的工会组织分别电函中华全国总工会，祝贺一九五一年新年。电函如下：
            （一）波兰工会的贺电中华全国总工会执行委员会：
    时值新年，谨致以兄弟般的祝贺，并希望前途美好。
                                        波兰工会中央委员会
            （二）越南总工会的贺年信中华全国总工会：
    当此新年之际，越南总工会愿以兄弟般的深情祝贺中国劳动同志及其可钦佩的领袖，获得一个美好的年辰。
    越南工人愿追随光荣的先例，与中朝两国同志并肩斗争，以便歼灭法国殖民者，并粉碎美帝国主义侵略集团和打击他们制造新战争的计划。
                                  越南总工会对外联络代表阮明
