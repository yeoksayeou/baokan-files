List of Appointments and Removals Passed by the Tenth Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee:

　Vice President
　　　　Wu Youxun Currently a member of the East China Military and Political Committee and concurrently Minister of Education.

　Deputy Director
　　　　Wang Shaochun Formerly a Standing Committee Member of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China.

　Ambassador
　　　　Feng Xuan Formerly a member of the Communist Party of China’s Lüda District Committee.

　Ambassador
　　　　Geng Biao Currently Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Sweden and Ambassador to the Kingdom of Denmark.

　　　　List of Deputy Secretaries-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate of the Central People's Government
　Deputy Secretary-General
　　　　Liu Huizhi Formerly Director of the Propaganda Department of the Political Department of the People's Liberation Army.

　　　　Other
　(1) The Ministry of Food Industry of the Central People's Government is abolished, and Minister Yang Lisan and Vice Minister Song Yuhe should be removed from their posts.
　(2) Remove An Ziwen from his concurrent position as a member of the People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government.
