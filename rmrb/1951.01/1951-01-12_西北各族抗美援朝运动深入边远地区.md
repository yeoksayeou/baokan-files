Title: Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Movement Deepens in Remote Areas of Northwest Ethnic Groups
Author:
Date: 1951-01-12
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 10th: The anti-American aggression and aid Korea, defending the home and defending the country movement among the people of all ethnic groups in Northwest China has penetrated into remote areas.

　　In Qinghai Province, Ang Suo Queji and Gangcha, Tu people from Hualong County, and fifteen chieftains from Huanhai and other regions such as Baoan, Angla, and Qianbulu jointly wrote to the Qinghai Daily, stating that in order to defend their homes and the country, they must resolutely eliminate local remnants of bandits and special agents and stabilize social order. More than ten youths and farmers and herdsmen of various ethnic groups such as Mongolian, Tibetan, Hui, and Tu in the province, including Xiawu Cai Rang and Danzhu, also expressed that they would widely publicize current affairs, expose the rumors of bandits and special agents, do a good job in production, raise livestock well, and strive to resist US aggression and aid Korea and defend their homes and the country.

　　Nima (steward of the Xia Luowa Living Buddha), Gong Qing (Grand Lama), Bi Erla and others, Mongolian compatriots from Hefeng County, which is far away on the northern border of Xinjiang, carried twenty silver ingots of condolence money to Dihua, requesting the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government to transfer them to the Chinese and Korean anti-American soldiers.

　　Tibetan people from Nammuna ethnic group and Chao Wu, Nalang Village and other villages in Zhuoni Autonomous Region, Gansu Province, wrote letters to our Chinese People's Volunteer Army soldiers congratulating them on their victory. Huang Zhengqing, a Tibetan from Xiahe, also mobilized Tibetans to write many condolence letters.

　　More than 5,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the Alxa League Autonomous Region of Ningxia Province held a conference on resisting US aggression and aiding Korea and defending their homes and the country in Dingyuan Camp, the capital of the League, on New Year's Day. At the meeting, a telegram was passed to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and General Kim Il-sung, congratulating them on the great victory on the Korean battlefield.

### 西北各族抗美援朝运动深入边远地区

1951-01-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　西北各族抗美援朝运动深入边远地区
    【新华社西安十日电】西北各民族人民抗美援朝保家卫国运动已深入边远地区。
    青海省化隆县土族昂琐却吉和冈察、环海等区保安、昂拉、千卜录等十五名千户联名投书青海日报，表示为了保家卫国，要坚决肃清地方残余土匪特务，安定社会秩序。该省蒙、藏、回、土各民族青年及农牧民下午才让、丹珠等十余人，也表示要广泛宣传时事，揭破匪特谣言，搞好生产，牧好牲畜，为抗美援朝保家卫国而努力。
    远在新疆北部边陲的和丰县蒙胞尼玛（夏律瓦活佛管家）、公庆（大喇嘛）、辟尔拉等携带慰问金元宝二十个赴迪化，请新疆省人民政府转献中朝抗美战士。
    甘肃省卓尼自治区那木拿族和朝勿、纳浪庄等村的藏民，都写信给我人民志愿军战士祝贺胜利。夏河藏族人士黄正清并发动藏胞写了许多慰问信。
    宁夏省阿拉善旗自治区各民族各界人民五千余人于元旦日在该旗首府定远营举行了抗美援朝保家卫国大会。会上通过致电我人民志愿军和金日成将军，祝贺朝鲜战场上的伟大胜利。
