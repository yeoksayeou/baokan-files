　　Cultural Life Trends
　　　　Northeast Education Develops Comprehensively
    Due to the development of production and the rise of the national economy in the Northeast, and the universal demand for improved culture after the workers and peasants turned over, education in the Northeast has achieved a high degree of development. In the first half of last year, the number of primary school students was 4,576,111, completing 100.7 percent of the original plan. According to incomplete statistics from Liaodong, Liaoxi, Jilin, Songjiang, Heilongjiang, Rehe provinces and the suburbs of Shenyang, there are 2,858 village schools run by farmers, with 205,686 students. In the first half of last year, the number of middle school students was 155,748, completing 96 percent of the original plan. In the second half of the year, the number of university students was 20,431, completing 116.63 percent of the original plan. Six worker-peasant rapid middle schools were opened, with 1,550 students, completing 172.22 percent of the original plan. In addition, approximately 1.8 million farmers transitioned from winter school to year-round study last year. The proportion of children from worker and peasant families entering school is gradually increasing.
　　　　Luda Factories Launch a Literacy Campaign
    Luda factories are striving to complete the task of eliminating illiteracy on schedule and have universally convened literacy "award model, challenge meetings," launching a wave of enthusiasm for the literacy movement.
　　　　Shanghai and Nanjing Commemorate the Centenary of the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom
    To commemorate the centenary of the Jintian Uprising of the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom and promote the revolutionary tradition of the Chinese people against imperialism and feudalism, Shanghai held a revolutionary cultural relics exhibition starting on the 20th. In addition, the cultural circles in Nanjing invited more than 20 elders to discuss revolutionary stories of the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom in commemoration of this great revolutionary movement.
　　　　"Qinghai Tibetan Newspaper" Launched
    "Qinghai Tibetan Newspaper," the mouthpiece of more than 400,000 Tibetan and Mongolian people in Qinghai, was launched on the 16th. The Tibetan and Mongolian people in Qinghai generally use the Tibetan language. The publication of the newspaper was warmly welcomed by the Tibetan and Mongolian nationalities in Qinghai. The newspaper is published every ten days, in four-page broadsheet format.
　　　　"Current Affairs Handbook" Issue 7 Published
    "Current Affairs Handbook" Issue 7 was published on the 20th. This issue contains the "Decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on Establishing a Propaganda Network for the Masses of People in the Whole Party" and the "Notice of the Chinese People's Committee for Defending World Peace and Opposing American Aggression on Comforting the Chinese and Korean People's Forces and Aiding Korean Refugees." It also contains the following articles and materials: Liu Shi's "How to Hold a Good Township People's Representative Conference," Yang Bo's "Why Should We Purchase Agricultural Products on Credit?" Wang Fei's "Oppose the United States' Rearmament of Japan," Ai Xue's "How Does the United States Use Religion to Invade China?" He Fang's "What is the US 'Open Door' Policy Towards China?" Li Longmu's "Reading Newspapers is an Important Method of Mobilization," and materials: "A Look at the Great Achievements of Our Country's Construction from the Numbers" and "How Much Rent Do Farmers Across the Country Pay to Landlords Every Year Under the Feudal Land System?"

### 文化生活动态

1951-01-22
第3版()
专栏：文化生活动态

　　文化生活动态
            东北教育全面发展
    由于东北生产发展，国民经济上升，工农翻身后普遍要求提高文化，使东北教育获得高度的发展。去年上半年小学生为四、五七六、一一一名，完成原计划的百分之一百点七。据辽东、辽西、吉林、松江、黑龙江、热河等省及沈阳市郊不完全统计，共有农民兴办的村学二、八五八所，学生二○五、六八六名。去年上半年中学生为一五五、七四八名，完成原计划百分之九六。下半年大学生为二○、四三一名，完成原计划的百分之一一六·六三。开办了六所工农速成中学，学生一、五五○人，完成原计划百分之一七二·二二。其它去年由冬学转入常年学习的农民约有一百八十万人。工农子弟入学的比率日渐增高。
            旅大各厂掀起识字运动热潮
    旅大各厂争取按期完成扫除文盲任务，普遍召开识字“奖模、挑战大会”。掀起识字运动热潮。
            沪宁纪念太平天国一百周年
    为纪念太平天国金田起义一百周年，发扬中国人民反帝反封建的革命传统，上海市于二十日起举行革命文物展览会。又南京文化界为纪念这一伟大的革命运动，曾邀集耆老二十余人，座谈太平天国革命故事。
            《青海藏文报》创刊
    青海四十余万藏、蒙人民喉舌《青海藏文报》于十六日创刊。青海藏蒙人民一般都用藏文，该报出刊后受到青海藏、蒙民族热烈欢迎。该报为十日刊，四开四版。
            《时事手册》第七期出版
    《时事手册》第七期已于二十日出版。这一期登载了中国共产党中央委员会“关于在全党建立对人民群众的宣传网的决定”、中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会“关于慰劳中朝人民部队救济朝鲜难民的通知”。另外还登载了下面的几篇文章和资料：刘栻的《怎样开好乡人民代表会议》、杨波的《为什么要赊购农产品？》王飞的《反对美国重新武装日本》、艾学的《美国是怎样利用宗教侵略中国的？》贺放的《美国的对华“门户开放”政策是怎么一回事？》李龙牧的《读报是重要的鼓动方法》，资料：《从数字来看我国伟大建设成绩》、“在封建土地制度下，全国农民每年向地主缴多少租子？”
