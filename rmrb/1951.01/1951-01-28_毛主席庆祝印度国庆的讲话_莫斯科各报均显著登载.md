Title: Chairman Mao's Speech Celebrating Indian National Day Published Prominently in Moscow Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1951-01-28
Page: 1

　　Chairman Mao's Speech Celebrating Indian National Day
    Published Prominently in Moscow Newspapers
    【Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 27th】 Today, Moscow newspapers all published Chairman Mao Zedong's speech at the reception held by the Indian Ambassador to China in Beijing to celebrate Indian National Day. Pravda and Izvestia both published it in prominent positions.

### 毛主席庆祝印度国庆的讲话  莫斯科各报均显著登载

1951-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席庆祝印度国庆的讲话
    莫斯科各报均显著登载
    【新华社莫斯科二十七日电】今日莫斯科各报都登载毛泽东主席在北京印度驻我国大使举行的印度国庆招待会上的讲话。真理报、消息报均以显著地位刊出。
