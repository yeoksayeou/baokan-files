Title: Chinese Red Cross Forms Medical Team to Prevent and Treat Epidemics for Huai River Workers
Author:
Date: 1951-01-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bengbu, 29th. To cooperate with the government's Huai River project, the Chinese Red Cross has formed a medical and epidemic prevention service team to go to Fengtai, Yingshang, Zhengyangguan and other places to prevent and treat epidemics for the Huai River workers.

　　The medical and prevention service team is led by the Beijing General Association of the Chinese Red Cross, and consists of 137 medical personnel hired from the Xi'an, Jinan, Hankou, Shanghai, Zhenjiang, Nanjing, Qingpu, Wanxian and other local associations. It is divided into five detachments.

### 中国红十字会组成医疗队  为治淮民工防治疫病

1951-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国红十字会组成医疗队
    为治淮民工防治疫病
    【新华社蚌埠二十九日电】中国红十字会为配合政府治淮工程，已组成一个医疗防疫服务大队，开赴凤台、颍上、正阳关等地，为治淮民工预防和医疗疫病。
    该医防服务大队由中国红十字会北京总会领导，系调聘该会西安、济南、汉口、上海、镇江、南京、青浦、万县等地分会医护人员一百三十七人组成，下设五个分队。
