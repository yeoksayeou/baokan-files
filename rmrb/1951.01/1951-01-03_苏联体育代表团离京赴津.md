Title: Soviet Sports Delegation Leaves Beijing for Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1951-01-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 2nd. The Soviet Sports Delegation arrived in Tianjin from Beijing on New Year's Day afternoon. Tianjin Mayor Huang Jing, Deputy Mayor Zhou Shutao, and representatives from the Tianjin Municipal Democratic Youth Federation, the Tianjin Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Tianjin Municipal Student Federation, and the Tianjin Municipal Sports Association went to the station to welcome them.

On the afternoon of the 2nd, the delegation began a friendly match with the Tianjin "Jinlian" team. More than 12,000 guests from various organizations, groups, and Soviet friends stationed in Tianjin came to watch. Before the game began, Mayor Huang Jing delivered a welcoming speech and performed the opening ball ceremony. As a result of the match, the Soviet basketball team defeated the "Jinlian" team.

### 苏联体育代表团离京赴津

1951-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联体育代表团离京赴津
    【新华社天津二日电】苏联体育代表团一行，于元旦日下午由京抵津。天津市市长黄敬、副市长周叔韬及天津市民主青年联合会、中苏友好协会天津分会、天津市学生联合会、天津市体育分会等单位代表均赴站欢迎。
    二日下午，该团开始与天津“津联”队作友谊比赛。各机关、团体来宾与驻津苏联友人到场参观者达一万二千余人。比赛开始前，由黄敬市长致欢迎词，并行开球礼。比赛结果，苏联篮球队胜“津联”队。
