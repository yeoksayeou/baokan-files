Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-01-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Look! These are the heroes of the great Chinese People's Volunteers. On the snowy and icy Korean battlefield, they defeated the American aggressors. They represent the firm will of all the Chinese people to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country, and are making great contributions to defending peace in Asia and the world.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    看！这是伟大的中国人民志愿军的英雄们。他们在冰天雪地的朝鲜战场上，击败了美国侵略者。他们代表着全中国人民抗美援朝、保家卫国的坚强意志，为保卫亚洲和世界的和平正做着伟大的贡献。
