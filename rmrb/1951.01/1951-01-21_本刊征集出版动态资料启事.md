　　Solicitation of Publishing Dynamics Information

    Our publication has added a "Publishing Dynamics" column, which will periodically report, in concise and analytical language, significant activities related to books, magazines, and newspapers both domestically and abroad. We believe that systematically publishing such reports will be beneficial both for current work guidance and for future historical research. However, our publication has limited staff and a narrow range of information. To do this work well, we must rely on the full cooperation of publishing and news workers nationwide. We hope that relevant parties will enthusiastically provide our publication with the information needed for this column, so that it can become a faithful record of the publishing and news work of our time.

### 本刊征集出版动态资料启事

1951-01-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　本刊征集出版动态资料启事
    本刊新增“出版动态”一栏，将按期用简短的有分析的文字，报告全国以及国外的有关书籍、杂志、报纸的有重要意义的活动。我们认为有系统地发表这种报道，无论对于当前的工作指导，或是对于将来的历史研究，都是有益的。但是本刊人手有限，见闻不广，为了把这个工作作好，必须依靠全国的出版工作者、新闻工作者的通力合作。希望有关各方面踊跃地供给本刊以这一栏所需要的资料，使它成为我们时代的出版工作、新闻工作的忠实的纪录。
