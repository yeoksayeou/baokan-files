Title: Ministry of Education and Four Other Units Hold Banquet to See Off Soviet Sports Delegation
Author:
Date: 1951-01-24
Page: 1

　　The Ministry of Education and Four Other Units Hold Banquet to See Off Soviet Sports Delegation
　　[By this newspaper] The Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation held a banquet on the evening of the 23rd to see off the Soviet Sports Delegation. Ma Xulun, Minister of Education, Wei Que, Deputy Minister, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, Liao Chengzhi, Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretaries, Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Zhang Zhongshi, Officer of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director of the Sports Federation Preparatory Committee, Xu Yingchao, and others attended as hosts. Also invited to the banquet were Chen Yi, Minister of the Cultural Department of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Yang Zhihua, Deputy Director of the Women Workers Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Jiebo, Chairman of the National Railway Workers' Union, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Department of the All-China Women's Federation, Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Jabi Cai, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy, and sports workers and athletes from Beijing, totaling more than 200 people.
　　Before the banquet began, Ma Xulun, Minister of Education, delivered a speech, pointing out that the visit of the Soviet Sports Delegation to China had enabled Chinese sports workers and athletes to absorb a great deal of advanced experience. He expressed the hope that Soviet sports workers would continue to provide concrete assistance to China's sports undertakings in the future. Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, pointed out in his farewell speech that developing national sports in China and enabling the people of the country to have healthy bodies is of great significance for China's national defense construction, economic construction, and the safeguarding of world peace. Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director and Secretary-General of the Sports Federation Preparatory Committee, expressed sincere gratitude in his speech for the assistance given to China's sports undertakings by the Soviet Sports Delegation during its visit. Romanov, head of the Soviet Sports Delegation, said in his reply that he was grateful to the broad masses of the Chinese people, sports workers, and athletes for the warm welcome extended to the delegation, and pointed out that this visit not only exchanged the experience of sports workers from both the Soviet Union and China, but also consolidated the friendship between the people, sports workers, and athletes of the two countries.

### 教育部等五单位举行宴会  欢送苏联体育代表团

1951-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　教育部等五单位举行宴会
    欢送苏联体育代表团
    【本报讯】中央人民政府教育部、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、北京市人民政府、中苏友好协会总会、中华全国体育总会筹委会等五单位于二十三日晚举行宴会，欢送苏联体育代表团。教育部部长马叙伦、副部长韦悫，青年团中央书记冯文彬、副书记廖承志、蒋南翔，北京市人民政府秘书长薛子正，中苏友好协会总会干事张仲实，体育总会筹委会副主任荣高棠、徐英超等人出席作陪。应邀出席参加宴会的尚有中国人民革命军事委员会政治部文化部部长陈沂，中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如，全国总工会女工部副部长杨之华，全国铁道总工会主席李颉伯，全国妇联国际部副部长廖梦醒，全国学联主席谢邦定，苏联大使馆一等秘书贾比才和京市体育工作者，运动员等二百余人。
    宴会开始前，教育部部长马叙伦致词，他指出：这次苏联体育代表团来中国访问，使中国体育工作者和运动员们得以吸收了很多的先进经验，他表示希望苏联的体育工作者以后能经常地给中国体育事业以具体帮助。青年团中央书记冯文彬在欢送词中指出：开展中国国民体育，使全国人民具有健康体魄，对中国国防建设事业，经济建设事业和保卫世界和平具有重要的意义。体育总会筹委会副主任兼秘书长荣高棠在讲话中，对苏联体育代表团在访问中给中国体育事业的帮助致以诚挚的谢意。苏联体育代表团团长罗曼诺夫在答词中，首先对中国广大人民、体育工作者和运动员对该团的热烈欢迎表示感谢，并指出：这次访问不仅交流了苏中两国体育工作者的经验，并使苏中两国人民、体育工作者和运动员的友谊更加巩固起来。
