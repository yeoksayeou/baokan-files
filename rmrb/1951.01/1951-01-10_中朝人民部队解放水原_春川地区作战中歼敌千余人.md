Title: Chinese and Korean People's Forces Liberate Suwon; Over 1,000 Enemy Troops Annihilated in Operations in the Chuncheon Area
Author:
Date: 1951-01-10
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Seoul Front, 9th: The Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, in joint operations, liberated Suwon, a railway junction south of Seoul, at 10 PM on January 7th. The American invading army is fleeing south. Suwon is located 30 kilometers south of Seoul. The Korean People's Army first struck a blow against the American invading army here during their counter-offensive in July of last year.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Seoul Front, 9th: The Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, in operations near Chuncheon from December 31st to January 3rd, annihilated the entire two battalions of the 32nd Regiment and part of one battalion of the 31st Regiment of the Li Chengwan (Syngman Rhee) bandit army, totaling over 1,000 men. 586 enemy troops were captured, 441 were killed or wounded, and the following were seized: 2 cannons, 18 rocket launchers, 41 light and heavy machine guns, 352 automatic rifles, 71 carbines, and 2 submachine guns.

### 中朝人民部队解放水原  春川地区作战中歼敌千余人

1951-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　中朝人民部队解放水原
    春川地区作战中歼敌千余人
    【新华社汉城前线九日电】朝鲜人民军与中国人民志愿军联合作战，已于一月七日下午十时解放汉城以南的铁路交叉点水原。美国侵略军向南逃窜。水原在汉城以南三十公里，朝鲜人民军在去年七月间反攻中曾首创美国侵略军于此。
    【新华社汉城前线九日电】朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军于十二月三十一日至一月三日在春川附近作战中，歼灭李承晚匪军三十二团两个营全部及三十一团一个营一部共一千余人。计俘敌五百八十六名，毙伤敌四百四十一名，缴获加农炮二门，火箭筒十八个，轻重机枪四十一挺，自动步枪三百五十二支，卡宾枪七十一支，冲锋枪二支。
