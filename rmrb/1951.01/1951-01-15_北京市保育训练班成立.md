Title: Beijing Nursery Training Class Established
Author: 
Date: 1951-01-15
Page: 3

    Beijing Nursery Training Class Established
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The Beijing Municipal People's Government and the Beijing Municipal Women's Federation have jointly established a nursery training class, responsible for training existing staff in various nurseries and cultivating new nursery staff. This training class was officially established on the 3rd of this month. The first class currently has fifty-three students. Among the newly enrolled students, five are from the Hui ethnic group. These are specially established recommended and tuition-free places by the Beijing Municipal People's Government to assist ethnic minorities in developing maternal and child welfare undertakings. The teachers include Xu Zhuan, director of the Municipal Second Nursery, who has been engaged in childcare work for twenty-one years, and Liu Jinghe, an expert in child physical and mental development from the Institute of Psychology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and others.

### 北京市保育训练班成立

1951-01-15
第3版()
专栏：

    北京市保育训练班成立
    【本报讯】北京市人民政府和北京市民主妇女联合会合办一保育训练班，负责训练现有各托儿所的工作人员，培养新的保育工作人员。这个训练班已在本月三日正式成立。第一期学生现有五十三人。新招收学生中有五人是回族，这是京市人民政府为了协助少数民族开展妇幼福利事业而特设的保送、免费名额。教师有从事儿童保育工作二十一年的市立第二托儿所所长徐篆、中国科学院心理研究所专攻儿童身心发展的专家刘静和等。
