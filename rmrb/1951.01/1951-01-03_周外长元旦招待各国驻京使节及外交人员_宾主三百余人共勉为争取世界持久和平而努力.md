Title: Foreign Minister Zhou Hosts New Year Reception for Heads of Diplomatic Missions and Diplomatic Personnel in Beijing; More Than 300 Guests Pledge Joint Efforts to Strive for Lasting World Peace
Author:
Date: 1951-01-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 2nd:

　　Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government hosted a New Year reception for heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic personnel in Beijing at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 4:00 PM on the 1st, celebrating the New Year together. More than 300 people attended.

　　The heads of diplomatic missions who accepted the invitation to attend included Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Polish Ambassador Burgin, Swedish Ambassador Armashton, German Diplomatic Mission Ambassador-level Head Koenig, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Burmese Ambassador U Myint Toon, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Danish Minister Munk, Swiss Minister Renzo Luli, members of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Tran Mai, as well as Hungarian Commercial Delegation Head Sarusiński, Deputy Head of the Polish Government Delegation Dist, Head of the Czech Cultural Delegation Průšek, and others. In addition, there were representatives from the British, Norwegian, and Dutch governments.

　　At the reception, Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai congratulated the guests on a happy New Year and wished them good health, and encouraged everyone to work together to strive for lasting world peace.

　　The reception proceeded in a warm atmosphere.

### 周外长元旦招待各国驻京使节及外交人员   宾主三百余人共勉为争取世界持久和平而努力

1951-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　周外长元旦招待各国驻京使节及外交人员
    宾主三百余人共勉为争取世界持久和平而努力
    【新华社二日讯】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长于一日下午四时在外交部招待各国驻京使节及外交人员，互庆新年。到会者共三百余人。
    各国使节应邀出席者有苏联大使罗申、捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、朝鲜大使李周渊、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、印度大使潘尼迦、波兰大使布尔金、瑞典大使阿马斯顿、德国外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、缅甸大使吴敏登、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、丹麦公使穆克、瑞士公使任佐立，越南民主共和国政府代表团团员陈梅及匈牙利商务代表团团长沙鲁辛斯基、波兰政府代表团副团长狄士特、捷克文化代表团团长普实克等，此外尚有英国、挪威、荷兰等国政府的谈判代表。
    周恩来部长在会上祝贺来宾新年快乐与健康，并共勉为争取世界的持久和平而努力。
    招待会在热烈的气氛中进行。
