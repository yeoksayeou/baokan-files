Author:
Date: 1951-01-09
Page: 1

　　Notice on Specifying Daily Submission Deadlines
    To ensure that this newspaper begins printing before 4:30 AM every day, so that the vast number of readers in this city and other areas can read the newspaper as early as possible, it is hereby stipulated that: except for particularly important manuscripts designated by the Central People's Government and the CPC Central Committee that must be published the next day and can be submitted until 2:00 AM, all other manuscripts must be submitted by 12:00 midnight every night. If any government organizations, groups, or individuals have manuscripts that they want to be published the next day, please submit them to the editorial department of this newspaper before 12:00 midnight each night.
              People's Daily  January 7th

### 为规定每日截稿时间启事

1951-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　为规定每日截稿时间启事
    为保证本报每日在早晨四时三十分以前开始印刷，以便本市和外埠的广大读者能够尽早读到报纸起见，兹规定：除中央人民政府和中共中央指定须于次日见报的特殊紧要稿件得延至夜二时截稿外，其余稿件一律定于每晚十二时截稿。各机关团体和个人如有要求在次日见报的稿件，务请在每晚十二时以前送至本报编辑部为要。
              人民日报社  一月七日
