Title: Entire Staff of Liaodong No. 4 Silk Weaving Factory Issues Challenge to National Textile Workers, Proposes Six Conditions to Launch Production Competition
Author:
Date: 1951-01-10
Page: 2

　　The entire staff of the Liaodong No. 4 Silk Weaving Factory
    Issues Challenge to National Textile Workers
    Proposes Six Conditions to Launch Production Competition
    [Reported by this newspaper] In order to strengthen the economic power and shatter the imperialist plot of American imperialism, all the staff of the Liaodong Provincial State-run Silkworm Company No. 4 Silk Weaving Factory, under the leadership of national model worker representative Chang Yongfen, issued a friendly patriotic production competition challenge to textile workers across the country on December 21, 1950. The challenge letter put forward six conditions: 1. Guarantee to strengthen the unity of technical personnel, staff, and workers, overcome all difficulties, and persist in production; 2. Guarantee that the output of the first quarter of 1951 will exceed the task by 3%, and the quality will exceed 6%; 3. Guarantee that the cost in the first quarter of 1951 will be reduced by 5% compared with December 1950; 4. Guarantee compliance with labor discipline, with an attendance rate of 97% for the entire factory; in terms of learning, guarantee that more than 80% of the existing employees are organized to participate in spare-time cultural learning, and strive for each person to be able to recognize 180 characters within three months; 5. Do a good job in four-prevention work to protect factory safety and prevent accidents; 6. Promote Chang Yongfen Technical School, with the first batch of apprentices reaching the technical level of (503 silk) within a fixed period of three months, and shortening the thread-breaking time from 3.5 hours to 2 hours.

### 辽东第四织绸厂全体职工  向全国纺织职工提出挑战  提出六项条件发起生产竞赛

1951-01-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　辽东第四织绸厂全体职工
    向全国纺织职工提出挑战
    提出六项条件发起生产竞赛
    【本报讯】东北辽东省营榨蚕公司第四织绸厂，全体职工为了加强经济力量，粉碎美帝侵略阴谋，在全国劳动模范代表常永芬带头领导下，于一九五○年十二月二十一日，向全国纺织职工提出友谊的爱国主义生产竞赛挑战。在挑战书中提出了六项条件：一、保证加强技术人员、职员、工人的团结，克服一切困难，坚持生产；二、一九五一年第一季产量，保证超过任务百分之三，质量保证超过百分之六；三、保证成本在一九五一年第一季比一九五○年十二月份降低百分之五；四、保证遵守劳动纪律，全厂出勤率达到百分之九十七，学习方面，保证现有职工中组织到百分之八十以上参加业余文化学习，并争取在三个月内每个人能认识到一百八十个字；五、作好四防工作，保护工厂安全不发生事故；六、推广常永芬技术学校，第一批徒弟定期三个月（五○三绸子）达到技术水平，捻头时间由三·五小时缩短到二小时。
