　　Provisions on Applications for Withdrawal of Deposits by American Enterprises and Individuals Issued by the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council

　　[Xinhua News Agency, 15th] The Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government Administration Council issued the "Provisions on Applications for Withdrawal of Deposits by American Enterprises and Individuals" on the 14th. The full text is as follows:

　　Provisions of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council on Applications for Withdrawal of Deposits by American Enterprises and Individuals

　　1. There shall be no restrictions on the withdrawal of personal deposits of American overseas Chinese not exceeding 30 million RMB. For deposits exceeding 30 million RMB, 10 million RMB may be withdrawn each month.

　　2. There shall be no restrictions on the withdrawal of deposits of American enterprises not exceeding 500 million RMB. For deposits exceeding 500 million RMB, the local control and examination authorities may approve payment according to their needs.

　　3. When withdrawing original currency deposits in foreign currency, they shall be converted according to the aforementioned regulations based on the foreign exchange rate.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Director Chen Yun
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　January 14, 1951

### 政务院财政经济委员会发布  关于美国企业及个人存款申请动支的规定

1951-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院财政经济委员会发布
    关于美国企业及个人存款申请动支的规定
    【新华社十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会十四日发布关于美国企业及个人存款申请动支的规定。全文如下：
    政务院财政经济委员会关于美国企业及个人存款申请动支的规定
    一、美国侨民之个人存款不超过人民币三千万元者，动支时不予限制。存款超过人民币三千万元者，每月得动支人民币一千万元。
    二、美国企业存款不超过人民币五亿元者，动支时不予限制。存款超过人民币五亿元者，得由当地管制审查机关按其需要核准支付之。
    三、外币的原币存款动支时，按前述规定照外币牌价折算之。
                                                   主任  陈云
                                         一九五一年一月十四日
