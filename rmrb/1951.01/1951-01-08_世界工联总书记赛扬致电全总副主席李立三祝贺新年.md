Title: World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Saillant Sends New Year Greetings to All-China Federation of Trade Unions Vice-Chairman Li Lisan
Author:
Date: 1951-01-08
Page: 4

To Comrade Li Lisan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

　　At the advent of the New Year, I extend brotherly and friendly greetings to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and to you personally. We thank you for your congratulations and are confident that the World Federation of Trade Unions will, in 1951, be able to more strongly express the will of workers worldwide to defend peace. I wish the People's Republic of China and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions new and significant successes in building a new democratic and peaceful country, and achievements in major tasks.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　General Secretary Saillant of the World Federation of Trade Unions
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　January 2nd

### 世界工联总书记赛扬致电全总副主席李立三祝贺新年

1951-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界工联总书记赛扬致电全总副主席李立三祝贺新年中华全国总工会副主席李立三同志：
    时值新年，谨向中华全国总工会及你个人致以兄弟的、友好的敬礼。我们感谢你的祝贺并确信世界工联在一九五一年中能更有力地表现全世界工人保卫和平的意志。敬祝中华人民共和国及中华全国总工会在建设新民主主义的和平工作中获得新的显著的成功和在重大的任务中的成就。
                              世界工联总书记赛扬
                                      一月二日
