Title: Wuhan 40,000 Women Rally and Demonstrate: Expand the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement for the Happiness of Children
Author:
Date: 1951-01-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 15th: Over 40,000 women from all walks of life in Wuhan held a demonstration rally on the 14th to resist America, aid Korea, and defend world peace. Participants included female labor models from production competitions, glorious mothers who encouraged their children to join military cadre schools and military dependents, female workers, female soldiers, female cadres of government agencies, rural women from suburban areas, female doctors, female students, housewives, female artists, and female religious followers. Qi Yuande, Chairman of the Wuhan Municipal Democratic Women's Federation, called on women throughout the city in her speech at the conference to continue to expand and deepen the movement to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country. When Zhang Jing, a female worker representative from the Central and Southern District attending the Second World People's Congress for the Defense of Peace, presented the peace flag given to the Chinese people by the Polish people to the conference, the applause from the audience was prolonged. The conference adopted congratulatory messages to Chairman Mao, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and the Korean People's Army, and also passed a resolution of Wuhan women to strive for lasting world peace. A demonstration parade began immediately after the meeting. When the 40,000-person procession appeared on the snow-covered streets, they were warmly applauded and cheered by the citizens. The procession marched under the guidance of huge portraits of Chairman Mao, Marshal Stalin, and General Kim Il-sung. Sixty-four orphans who had lost their parents in the war, with white doves on their heads, slowly advanced in the procession holding a large red flag that read "For our bright future, we want peace." Female workers carried their products. Housewives held brooms, kitchen knives, scissors, and cradles. They shouted slogans such as "Educate our children to love the motherland" and "For the happiness of our children, we must defend world peace."

### 武汉四万妇女集会示威游行  为孩子们的幸福扩大抗美援朝运动

1951-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　武汉四万妇女集会示威游行
    为孩子们的幸福扩大抗美援朝运动
    【新华社汉口十五日电】武汉市各界妇女四万余人于十四日举行抗美援朝保卫世界和平示威游行大会。参加大会的有生产竞赛中的女劳动模范、鼓励子女参加军事干部学校的光荣母亲和军属们，女工、部队女战士、机关女干部、郊区农妇、女医生、女学生、家庭妇女、女艺人、宗教界女教徒。武汉市民主妇女联合会主席戚元德在大会上的讲话中号召全市妇女继续扩大与深入抗美援朝保家卫国运动。当出席第二届世界人民保卫和平大会的中南区女工代表张静把波兰人民赠给中国人民的和平旗子献给大会时，全场掌声经久不息。大会通过了向毛主席、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军的致敬电，并通过了武汉市妇女争取世界持久和平的决心书。会后即开始示威游行。当四万人的游行行列出现在积雪未消的街头时，受到市民们热烈的鼓掌和欢呼。游行队伍在毛主席、斯大林大元帅和金日成将军的巨幅画像前导下前进。有六十四个在战争中失去了父母的孤儿，他们的头上顶着白鸽，举着“为了我们的美好将来，我们要和平”的大红旗，在队伍中缓缓前进。女工们抬着自己的产品。家庭妇女们手持扫帚、菜刀、剪子和摇篮。她们高呼“要教育子女，热爱祖国”和“为了孩子们的幸福，我们要保卫世界和平”等口号。
