Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold Evening Party Today to Commemorate the 27th Anniversary of Lenin's Death
Author:
Date: 1951-01-21
Page: 1

　　[This Newspaper News] Today (the 21st) marks the 27th anniversary of the death of Lenin, the great teacher of the world's proletariat and oppressed nations. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will hold a lecture and film evening party at the Beijing Hotel at 7 p.m. tonight. Soviet education expert A.N. Arsintsev will be invited to give a report on Lenin in commemoration. After the report, the Soviet film "Lenin in October" will be screened.
　　In addition, on the eve of the anniversary of Lenin's death, the Association compiled twenty sets of pictures about Lenin's life and deeds, which have been distributed to twenty local Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in Beijing, Tianjin, Hebei, and Chahar to hold commemorative exhibitions. In addition, the "Window of the Soviet Union," which is regularly exhibited by the Youth Publishing House in the Dongcheng District and the Xicheng Study Bookstore in Beijing, has also been replaced with pictures of "Lenin's Life and Deeds" in this issue. (Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Correspondence Group)

### 中苏友好协会今举行晚会纪念列宁逝世二十七周年

1951-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会今举行晚会纪念列宁逝世二十七周年
    【本报讯】今日（二十一日）是全世界无产阶级和被压迫民族的伟大导师列宁逝世二十七周年纪念日。中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会特定于今晚七时在北京饭店举行讲演、电影晚会，邀请苏联教育专家Ａ·ｎ·阿尔辛节夫做有关列宁的报告，以志纪念。报告完毕即放映苏联电影“列宁在十月”。
    又，该会于列宁逝世纪念前夕，编制了二十套有关列宁生平事迹的图片，已分发给北京、天津、河北、察哈尔等二十个地方中苏友好协会举行纪念展览。此外，该会在京市东城青年出版社和西城学习书店经常展览的“苏联之窗”，这期也换上了“列宁生平事迹”图片。（中苏友协总会通讯组）
