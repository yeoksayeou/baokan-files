Title: Over 40,000 People of All Ethnic Groups in Xichang Hold Anti-US and Aid-Korea Parade
Author:
Date: 1951-01-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 6th—Over 40,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Xichang, Xikang Province, held an anti-US and aid-Korea demonstration parade on New Year's Day. Participants in the parade included eight ethnic groups: Han, Yi, Hui, Lisu, Mosuo, Tibetan, Miao, and Guzong. Every household in the city hung national flags and posted new Spring Festival couplets with various slogans defending the country and resisting US aggression and aiding Korea. Many propaganda teams carried out costumed street performances. Vast numbers of workers, farmers, women, and militia from the countryside, holding hammers, sickles, and scissors, and carrying various guns, poured into the city from dozens of miles away, forming a torrent that filled every street. In the parade, people of all ethnic groups shouted slogans in different languages such as "People of all ethnic groups unite," "Defend the motherland," "Support the Korean people," and "Defeat the invading army of US imperialism." Torch parades continued in the evening and did not disperse until late at night.

### 西昌各族四万余人举行抗美援朝游行

1951-01-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　西昌各族四万余人
    举行抗美援朝游行
    【新华社重庆六日电】西康省西昌各民族各界人民四万余人于元旦举行抗美援朝示威游行。参加游行的包括汉、彝、回、傈僳、摩些、藏、苗、古宗等八个民族。城内家家户户悬挂着国旗，张贴各种保卫祖国抗美援朝的新对联。许多宣传队在街头进行化装宣传。乡村的广大工人、农民、妇女、自卫队，高举铁锤、镰刀、剪刀，扛着各种枪枝，从数十里外涌进城内，汇成一股洪流，塞满每一条街道。在游行队伍中，各族人民以不同的语言高呼着“各族人民团结起来”、“保卫祖国大家庭”、“支援朝鲜人民”、“打垮美帝国主义的侵略军”等口号。晚上继续举行火炬游行，直到深夜始散。
