Title: Hungarian Workers' Party Central Committee Member Garó Ernő Delivers Speech Praising the Victory of the Chinese and Korean People
Author:
Date: 1951-01-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 1st—Garó Ernő, member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Workers' Party, delivered a speech at a cadre meeting in Budapest, the capital of Hungary, on the afternoon of December 22nd, praising the victory of the Chinese and Korean people on the Korean battlefield. Garó Ernő said: "The victory won by the Korean People's Army with the assistance of the Chinese People's Volunteers has a permanent historical significance for all peace-loving people. This victory will expand the scale of the struggle for freedom of all oppressed nations in the world. This event is of great significance not only as a turning point in the Korean War, but also for the entire world situation."
    Garó Ernő pointed out that this event is an example of mutual assistance in times of adversity among countries in the world peace camp, and a victory for the unity of peaceful forces. This is a major historical event of great significance for the 475 million Chinese people taking concrete actions in the struggle against American imperialism and for peace.
    Garó Ernő finally pointed out the great impact of this victory, saying: "These victories have filled the hearts of all peace defenders with new enthusiasm, consolidated the peace movement, encouraged the people's movement in capitalist countries, and exposed the contradictions within the American ruling clique. Finally, these victories prove that all conspiracies of the imperialists against the Soviet Union and the countries of the peace camp will inevitably fail."

### 匈工人党中委加罗逸演说  赞扬中朝人民的胜利

1951-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　匈工人党中委加罗逸演说
    赞扬中朝人民的胜利
    【新华社布拉格一日电】匈牙利工人党中央委员会委员加罗逸十二月二十二日下午，在匈京布达佩斯的一个干部会上发表演说，赞扬中朝人民在朝鲜战场的胜利。加罗逸说：“朝鲜人民军在中国人民志愿军的援助下获得的胜利，对所有爱好和平的人类说来，永远具有历史性的意义。这个胜利将扩大全世界被压迫的民族争取自由的斗争的规模。这一事件不仅作为朝鲜战争的转捩点说来，有它的伟大意义；而且就整个世界局势说来，也是如此。”
    加罗逸指出，这一事件是世界和平阵营国家间患难相助的范例，是和平力量团结一致的胜利。这是四亿七千五百万中国人民在反对美帝国主义、争取和平的斗争中采取具体行动的有历史意义的重大事件。
    加罗逸最后指出这个胜利的伟大影响说：“这些胜利已经使一切和平保卫者的心中充满了新的热情，使和平运动更加巩固，给予资本主义国家中的人民运动以鼓励，使美国统治集团内部的矛盾表面化。最后，这些胜利证明帝国主义者反对苏联与和平阵营国家的一切阴谋将不可避免地归于失败。”
