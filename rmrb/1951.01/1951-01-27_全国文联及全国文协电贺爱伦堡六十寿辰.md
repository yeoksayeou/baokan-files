Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and All-China Association of Literary Workers Send Congratulations on Ehrenburg's Sixtieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1951-01-27
Page: 4

　　[Report from this newspaper] The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the All-China Association of Literary Workers sent a telegram of congratulations on the sixtieth birthday of the Soviet writer and social activist Ehrenburg on January 27. The telegram stated: "Dear Mr. Ehrenburg: Warmly congratulate you on your sixtieth birthday! As one of the most outstanding writers and a great social activist of our time, your achievements in striving to defend world lasting peace, and your world-renowned works, have inspired the peace-loving people of New China. The Chinese people love your works very much, they have given us the greatest encouragement and great enlightenment; recently your speeches and essays on defending world peace and others have also become models for our work and study. We thank you deeply! And wish you eternal health!"

### 全国文联及全国文协电贺爱伦堡六十寿辰

1951-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国文联及全国文协电贺爱伦堡六十寿辰
    【本报讯】一月二十七日为苏联作家和社会活动家爱伦堡的六十寿辰。中华全国文学艺术界联合会暨中华全国文学工作者协会特致电祝贺。电文称：“亲爱的爱伦堡先生：热烈地祝贺您的六十大寿！作为我们时代一个最卓越的作家和一个伟大的社会活动家，您那努力为保卫世界持久和平而斗争的功绩，您的全世界闻名的著作，鼓舞了爱好和平的新中国人民。中国人民十分爱好您的作品，它们曾经给我们以最大的鼓舞和很大的教益；最近您的关于保卫世界和平以及其他的讲演和论文，也成为我们工作与学习的范例。我们深深地感谢您！并祝您永远健康！”
