Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its Sixty-Ninth Meeting: Listens to the Summary Report of the Central Northwest Visiting Group's Work
Author:
Date: 1951-01-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its sixty-ninth meeting at 3:30 PM on the 26th. The meeting heard and approved the summary report on the work of the Central Northwest Ethnic Visiting Group, presented by Chairman Shen Junru. The meeting also passed several motions regarding appointments and removals, and motions to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval regarding appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第六十九次会议  听取中央西北访问团工作总结报告

1951-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行第六十九次会议
    听取中央西北访问团工作总结报告
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府政务院第六十九次政务会议于二十六日下午三时半举行。会议听取和批准了沈钧儒团长所作的中央西北民族访问团工作总结报告，并通过了任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
