Author:
Date: 1951-01-28
Page: 1

    Photo Caption
    The U.S. imperialist plot to rearm Japan had already been partially implemented even before the warmonger Dulles's recent visit to Japan. These two photos are ironclad proof. The photo on the upper left shows: the "Japanese Police Reserve Corps" at Shinjuku Station in Tokyo. These people are already armed with American weapons. The photo on the upper right shows: the U.S. imperialism is gradually mechanizing the equipment of the "Japanese Police Reserve Corps." This is an armored vehicle of the "Japanese Police Reserve Corps" dispatched in the Shibuya area. They are attempting to suppress demonstrations by Japanese workers.
           (Photos provided by the News Photography Bureau)

### 照片说明（图片）

1951-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    照片说明
    美国帝国主义重新武装日本的阴谋活动，在战贩杜勒斯这一次来到日本以前，已经局部地进行了。这两张照片，就是铁的证据。左上的照片是：在东京新宿车站的“日本警察后备队”，这些人已经用美国武器武装起来了。右上的照片表明：美帝正使“日本警察后备队”的装备逐渐机械化。这是在澀谷地方出动的“日本警察后备队”的装甲车。他们正企图弹压日本工人的示威行动。
           （照片系由新闻摄影局供给）
