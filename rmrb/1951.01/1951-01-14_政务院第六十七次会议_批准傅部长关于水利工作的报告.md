Title: The 67th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approves Minister Fu's Report on Water Conservancy Work
Author:
Date: 1951-01-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, January 12th: The 67th plenary session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held at 3:00 pm on the 12th. The meeting listened to and discussed the "Summary of Water Conservancy Work in 1950 and the Guidelines and Tasks for 1951" report made by Fu Zuoyi, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Water Resources, and approved the report. The meeting also passed several appointment and removal cases and cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院第六十七次会议  批准傅部长关于水利工作的报告

1951-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第六十七次会议
    批准傅部长关于水利工作的报告
    【新华社十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第六十七次政务会议于十二日下午三时举行，会议在听取和讨论了中央人民政府水利部傅作义部长所作的关于一九五零年水利工作总结和一九五一年的方针与任务的报告后，批准了该报告。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
