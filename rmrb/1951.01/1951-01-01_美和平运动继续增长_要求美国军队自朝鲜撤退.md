Title: US Peace Movement Continues to Grow, Demanding Withdrawal of US Troops from Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 27th (Tass): The movement for peace in the United States continues to grow. The San Francisco "People's World" reported that the executive committee of the Fish Cannery Workers Local in San Diego (California) has sent a letter to Truman demanding the withdrawal of US troops from Korea and a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue. The executive committee meeting of the Washington State Pension Association called for a peaceful resolution of world problems. The United Nations American Labor Union delegation submitted a petition to Truman, bearing the signatures of seven hundred members of labor unions affiliated with the AFL and CIO, as well as independent unions, demanding peaceful negotiations to resolve all differences and an end to the Korean War. The delegation meeting of the Fur and Leather Workers Union in Chicago also passed a resolution demanding an end to the war of aggression against Korea, and called for a struggle to increase wages and abolish the Taft-Hartley and McCarran reactionary laws.

### 美和平运动继续增长  要求美国军队自朝鲜撤退

1951-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　美和平运动继续增长
    要求美国军队自朝鲜撤退
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社纽约二十七日讯：美国争取和平的活动继续增长。旧金山“人民世界”报报道称：圣地牙哥（加利福尼亚）的鱼类装罐业工人地方工会执行委员会已致函杜鲁门，要求美国军队自朝鲜撤退，并和平解决朝鲜问题。华盛顿州养老金协会执行委员会会议要求和平解决世界问题。联合国美国工会代表团向杜鲁门提出了请愿书，上有劳联及产联所属工会和独立工会会员七百人的签名，要求和平谈判解决一切分歧，停止朝鲜战争。在芝加哥举行的毛皮及皮革工业工会代表会议也通过决议，要求停止对朝鲜的侵略战争，并号召为增加工资和取消塔夫脱——哈特莱以及麦卡伦反动法律而斗争。
