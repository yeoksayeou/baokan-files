Title: Mayor of Beijing, Nie Rongzhen, and Chairman of the Moscow Soviet, Yasnov, Exchange New Year's Greetings and Wish Each Other New Achievements This Year
Author:
Date: 1951-01-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency

　　Mayor of Beijing, Nie Rongzhen, and Chairman of the Moscow Soviet, Yasnov,
    Exchange New Year's Greetings and Wish Each Other New Achievements This Year
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 31st] On the eve of the New Year, Mayor of Beijing, Nie Rongzhen, sent a telegram to the Chairman of the Moscow Soviet, Yasnov, to congratulate him on New Year's Day 1951. Yasnov has replied to express his gratitude. The texts of the two telegrams are as follows:

            (1) Telegram from Mayor Nie Rongzhen to Mr. Yasnov, Chairman of the Moscow Soviet:
    Mr. Yasnov, Chairman of the Moscow Soviet:
    On the occasion of the joyous New Year's Day of 1951, on behalf of the two million people of Beijing, I extend warm congratulations to you, Mr. Chairman, and to all the people of Moscow, and wish you new success in the great cause of socialist construction.

                              Nie Rongzhen
                        December 27, 1950, in Beijing

            (2) Yasnov's Reply to Mayor Nie Rongzhen of Beijing:
    Please accept my thanks for your kind congratulations and wishes expressed on the eve of the New Year. On behalf of the working people of Moscow, I congratulate you and all the citizens of Beijing on further success in the construction of the People's Republic of China.

                M. Yasnov, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet of Working People's Deputies
                        December 30, 1950, in Moscow

### 北京市长聂荣臻和莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫  互贺新年并预祝今年新成就

1951-01-01
第1版()
专栏：社论

　　北京市长聂荣臻和莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫
    互贺新年并预祝今年新成就
    【新华社三十一日讯】北京市聂荣臻市长于新年前夕致电莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫祝贺一九五一年元旦。雅斯诺夫已复电申谢。两电电文如下：
            （一）聂荣臻市长致雅斯诺夫电莫斯科苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫先生：
    兹逢一九五一年元旦佳节，我代表北京市二百万人民向您，主席先生暨莫斯科全体人民谨致热烈的祝贺，并预祝你们在伟大的社会主义建设事业上新的成功。
                              聂荣臻
                        一九五零年十二月二十七日于北京
            （二）雅斯诺夫复电北京市聂荣臻市长：
    请接受我对您的感谢，感谢您在新年前夕所表示的亲切的祝贺与愿望。谨以莫斯科市劳动人民的名义，祝贺您及全北京市市民，在中华人民共和国的建设当中，获得进一步的成功。
                莫斯科市劳动人民代表苏维埃执行委员会主席Ｍ·雅斯诺夫
                        一九五零年十二月三十日于莫斯科
