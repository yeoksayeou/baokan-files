Title: Fadeyev and Xiao San Travel to Geneva to Attend World Peace Council Executive Bureau Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-01-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 8th. Fadeyev, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council Executive Bureau, and Xiao San, Secretary of the Executive Bureau, flew from Moscow to Prague by special plane on the 7th and will travel from there to Geneva to attend the plenary meeting of the World Peace Council Executive Bureau to be held on the 10th.

### 法捷耶夫、萧三赴日内瓦  出席世界和平理事会执行局会议

1951-01-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　法捷耶夫、萧三赴日内瓦
    出席世界和平理事会执行局会议
    【新华社布拉格八日电】世界和平理事会执行局副主席法捷耶夫、执行局秘书萧三已于七日自莫斯科乘专机飞抵布拉格，经此前往日内瓦出席于十日举行的世界和平理事会执行局全体会议。
