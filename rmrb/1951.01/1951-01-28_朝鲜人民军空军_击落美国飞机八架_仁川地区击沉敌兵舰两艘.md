Title: Korean People's Army Air Force Shoots Down Eight American Planes, Sinks Two Enemy Warships in Incheon Area
Author:
Date: 1951-01-28
Page: 1

　　Korean People's Army Air Force
    Shoots Down Eight American Planes
    Sinks Two Enemy Warships in Incheon Area
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 27th] Pyongyang Dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the 26th that the People's Army Air Force shot down eight American invading army jet fighters and damaged two enemy planes over Sinuiju and Sinanju on the 24th of this month.
    On the 25th of this month, People's Army troops sank two enemy warships in the Incheon area.

### 朝鲜人民军空军  击落美国飞机八架  仁川地区击沉敌兵舰两艘

1951-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军空军
    击落美国飞机八架
    仁川地区击沉敌兵舰两艘
    【新华社二十七日讯】平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十六日发表战报称：人民军空军于本月二十四日在新义州和新安州上空击落美国侵略军喷气式战斗机八架，击伤敌机二架。
    本月二十五日人民军部队在仁川地区击沉敌军兵舰两艘。
