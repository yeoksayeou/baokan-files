Title: French Government Representative to Vietnam, Jean Sainteny, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 25th—Jean Sainteny, the French Government Representative to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and his wife, along with Roger Dousset, the Cultural Attaché of the French Delegation to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs for a visit to China.

### 法国政府驻越南代表  让·圣德尼到北京

1956-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    法国政府驻越南代表
    让·圣德尼到北京
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民外交学会的邀请，来中国访问的法国驻越南民主共和国政府总代表让·圣德尼和夫人，偕同法国驻越南民主共和国总代表团文化专员罗歇·杜塞，在今天下午乘飞机来到京。
