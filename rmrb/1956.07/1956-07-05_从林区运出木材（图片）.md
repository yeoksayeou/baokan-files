Title: Transporting Timber from Forest Areas (Picture)
Author: Zhao Xiang
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 2

    Transporting Timber from Forest Areas
    Workers in the Greater Khingan Range forest area of Inner Mongolia are stepping up logging operations and transporting the felled timber to all parts of the country. This is a train loaded with logs departing from the Tulihe Station.

### 从林区运出木材（图片）
朝襄
1956-07-05
第2版()
专栏：

    从林区运出木材
    内蒙古大兴安岭林区职工，正在加紧采伐，并且把采伐下来的木材运往祖国各地。这是由图里河车站开出的一列满载原木的火车。
                            朝襄摄　（内蒙古日报社稿）
