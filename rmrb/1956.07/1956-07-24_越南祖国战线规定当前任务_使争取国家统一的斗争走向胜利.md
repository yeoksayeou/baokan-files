Title: Vietnam Fatherland Front Stipulates Current Tasks: Striving to Lead the Struggle for National Reunification to Victory
Author:
Date: 1956-07-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 22nd: The expanded meeting of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, which began on the 18th, concluded this afternoon.

    The meeting passed a resolution pointing out that the main tasks of the Vietnamese people and the Vietnam Fatherland Front in the second half of this year are to continue to consolidate North Vietnam, continue the struggle to thoroughly implement the Geneva Accords, and consolidate and expand the National United Front.

    Hoàng Quốc Việt, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, made a concluding speech at the closing meeting. He pointed out that through this discussion, everyone unanimously affirmed that the signing of the Geneva Accords was a great victory for the Vietnamese people. Everyone unanimously recognized that the struggle for national reunification is a long-term, arduous, and complex struggle, but this struggle will surely be victorious.

    Hoàng Quốc Việt also pointed out that this meeting fully promoted the spirit of democracy. Everyone, proceeding from the interests of the nation and the people, sincerely criticized some shortcomings in the work of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Fatherland Front, and put forward positive suggestions for improving future work, which made this meeting a great success.

    Hoàng Quốc Việt said that in group discussions, everyone unanimously agreed with the report made by Tôn Đức Thắng, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, at the opening meeting.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 23rd: Nguyễn Xiển, General Secretary of the Vietnam Socialist Party, announced at the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam Socialist Party held in Hanoi on the 22nd that the Vietnam Socialist Party will, in accordance with President Ho Chi Minh's call, resolutely struggle to realize the Geneva Accords and national reunification, and to complete the glorious cause of national liberation.

    In his report, Nguyễn Xiển expressed the hope that the Vietnam Socialist Party will receive the support and assistance of fraternal parties and organizations and people of all strata in the Vietnam Fatherland Front, so that the Vietnam Socialist Party can complete its tasks.

### 越南祖国战线规定当前任务  使争取国家统一的斗争走向胜利

1956-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

    越南祖国战线规定当前任务
    使争取国家统一的斗争走向胜利
    新华社河内22日电　越南祖国战线中央委员会十八日起举行的扩大会议在今天下午闭幕。
    在闭幕会议上通过了决议，指出越南人民和越南祖国战线今年下半年的主要任务是继续巩固越南北方，继续进行彻底履行日内瓦协定的斗争，巩固和扩大民族统一战线。
    越南祖国战线中央委员会主席团委员黄国越在闭幕会上作了总结发言。他指出，通过这次讨论大家一致肯定了日内瓦协定的签订是越南人民的巨大胜利。大家一致认识到争取国家的统一的斗争是一个长期的艰苦的和复杂的斗争，但是这个斗争一定将获得胜利。
    黄国越还指出，这次会议充分发扬了民主精神，大家从民族和人民利益出发，诚恳地批评了越南劳动党和祖国战线的工作的一些缺点，并且提出了改进今后工作的积极建议，使这次会议得到了很大的成绩。
    黄国越说，在小组讨论中，大家一致同意越南祖国战线中央委员会主席孙德胜在开幕会上所作的报告。
    新华社河内23日电　越南社会党总书记阮阐二十二日在河内举行的庆祝越南社会党成立十周年大会上宣布，越南社会党将按照胡志明主席的号召，坚决为实现日内瓦协定和国家的统一、为完成民族解放的光荣事业而斗争。
    阮阐在报告中表示希望越南社会党将得到越南祖国战线中各兄弟党和各团体以及各阶层人民的支持和帮助，使越南社会党能够完成自己的任务。
