Title: Nehru Says in Congratulatory Telegram to the West Bengal India-China Friendship Association: The Five Principles Have Become a Common International Language
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 29th. Indian Prime Minister Nehru said in a congratulatory telegram on June 27th to the West Bengal India-China Friendship Association, which is celebrating the second anniversary of the joint statement issued by the Prime Ministers of India and China: "The Five Principles have now become a common international language. They have been directly or implicitly accepted by the vast majority of countries in Asia and Europe. Recently, the United Kingdom and France have also accepted these principles in substance."
    The telegram also stated: "I have no doubt that these Five Principles must become the basis of international relations. If this is fully achieved, then the problem of war will no longer exist."

### 尼赫鲁在给西孟加拉印中友协的贺电中说  五项原则已成国际上共同语言

1956-07-01
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁在给西孟加拉印中友协的贺电中说
    五项原则已成国际上共同语言
    据新华社新德里29日电　印度总理尼赫鲁在六月二十七日给正在庆祝印中两国总理发表联合声明两周年的西孟加拉印中友好协会的贺电中说：“五项原则现在已经成为国际上的共同语言。它们已经直接地或者含蓄地为亚洲和欧洲的绝大多数国家所接受。最近，联合王国和法国也在实质上接受了这些原则。”
    贺电中还说：“我毫不怀疑，这五项原则必定会成为国际关系的基础。如果完全做到了这一点，那么战争的问题就不会存在了。”
