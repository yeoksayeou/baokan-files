Title: Eisenhower Resumes Work
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 16th: Eisenhower returned to Washington from his recuperation site in Gettysburg on July 15th. White House Press Secretary Hagerty said that Eisenhower officially resumed work today.

Eisenhower had been recuperating in Gettysburg after undergoing surgery in early June for a localized obstruction of the terminal ileum.

### 艾森豪威尔恢复工作

1956-07-17
第6版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔恢复工作
    新华社16日讯　华盛顿消息：艾森豪威尔已经在七月十五日从他的休养地葛底斯堡回到华盛顿。白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂说，艾森豪威尔从今天起正式恢复工作。
    艾森豪威尔是在六月初患小肠末端局部阻塞病动手术后到葛底斯堡休养的。
