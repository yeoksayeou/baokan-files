Title: Public Health Work Should Be Improved
Author: Huo Jian
Date: 1956-07-15
Page: 4

Recently, citizens of Shenyang often throw dirty water from washing clothes and vegetables onto the streets. Even residents of some model hygiene courtyards have this unsanitary habit. We hope that Shenyang will pay attention to improving public health work. (Huo Jian)

### 应该改善公共卫生工作
霍建
1956-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    应该改善公共卫生工作
    近来沈阳市市民经常把洗衣、洗菜的脏水往街上泼、甚至连一些卫生模范院的居民也有这种不卫生的习惯。我们希望沈阳市注意改善公共卫生工作。　（霍　建）
