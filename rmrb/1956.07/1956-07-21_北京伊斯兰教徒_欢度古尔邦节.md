Title: Beijing Muslims Celebrate Eid al-Adha
Author:
Date: 1956-07-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th: Beijing's Muslims held festive prayer services at eighty mosques throughout the city this morning to celebrate Eid al-Adha.

　　At the Dongsi Mosque, the festive prayer service was presided over by the famous Imam Nur Muhammad Dapusheng. More than 600 people of Hui, Uyghur, Kazakh, and Dongxiang ethnicities participated in the prayer service. Many foreign friends who believe in Islam also attended the service. At the end of the service, everyone shook hands and congratulated each other on the festival.

　　In Niujie, where the Hui people are most concentrated, people driving cattle and sheep for slaughter were seen continuously today. The whole street slaughtered twenty-five cattle and sheep throughout the morning.

　　In the evening, the faculty and students of the China Islamic Institute invited staff members of the China Islamic Association and faculty and students of the Hui People's College to a dinner gathering.

### 北京伊斯兰教徒  欢度古尔邦节

1956-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    北京伊斯兰教徒
    欢度古尔邦节
    新华社20日讯　北京市伊斯兰教徒今天清晨在全市八十个清真寺分别举行节日会礼，庆祝古尔邦节。
    在东四清真寺里，节日会礼由著名阿訇努尔·穆罕默德·达浦生主持，参加会礼的有回、维吾尔、哈萨克、东乡等民族六百多人，许多信仰伊斯兰教的外国朋友们也参加了会礼。会礼结束时，大家都举行了握手礼，互相庆祝节日。
    在回民最集中的牛街，今天不断看见赶着牛羊去宰杀的人们，整个上午全街就宰杀了二十五只牛羊。
    晚上，中国伊斯兰教经学院的师生们邀请中国伊斯兰教协会的工作人员和回民学院的师生共同举行了聚餐。
