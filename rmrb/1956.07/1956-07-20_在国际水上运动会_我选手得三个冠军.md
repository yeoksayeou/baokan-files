Title: Our Athletes Win Three Championships at International Aquatic Games
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 19th: Chinese athletes participating in the Second International Aquatic Games held in Giżycko, Poland, won three championships in various competitions on the 15th.

    In the 50-meter shooting competition, the Chinese team took first place with a total score of 350 rings.

    In the rope crossing river competition, the Chinese team was the first to climb the rope and cross the river. The Polish "Youth Banner" newspaper reported, "Chinese athletes crossed the Krutynia River with amazing skill and speed, without their feet touching the water."

    In the cooking competition, Chinese athletes took first place by preparing delicious meals in forty minutes.

    In the 350 cubic centimeter cylinder capacity motorboat race held on the same day, the Chinese team's total score was fourth.

### 在国际水上运动会  我选手得三个冠军

1956-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    在国际水上运动会
    我选手得三个冠军
    新华社华沙19日电　参加在波兰吉日茨科举行的第二届国际水上运动会的中国选手在十五日的各项比赛中取得了三个冠军。
    在五十公尺射击比赛中，中国队以总成绩三百五十环取得第一名。
    在利用绳索通过河川的比赛中，第一个攀绳通过河流的是中国队。波兰“青年旗帜报”报道说，“中国运动员以奇妙的机巧和速度，脚不沾水渡过了克鲁迪尼亚河”。
    在烹饪比赛中，中国选手以四十分钟作好美味的饭食，而取得了第一名。
    在这一天举行的汽缸容积三百五十立方公分的摩托艇比赛中，中国队的总评分成绩是第四名。
