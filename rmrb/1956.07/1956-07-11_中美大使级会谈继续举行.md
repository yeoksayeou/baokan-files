Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 10th. The 53rd meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks was held at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 a.m. on July 10th. The meeting adjourned at 11:15 a.m. The next meeting is scheduled for Thursday, July 26th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦10日电　中美两国大使级会谈七月十日上午十时在国联大厦举行第五十三次会议，会议在十一时十五分休会。下次会议订在七月二十六日（星期四）举行。
