Title: Busy Yumen Oilfield Station (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-23
Page: 2

    Busy Yumen Oilfield Station (Picture)
    With the laying of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway to Yumen, the Yumen Oilfield Station has begun to show a busy scene. Every day, trains loaded with natural petroleum depart eastward to support the construction of the Yumen Oilfield, and various building materials supporting the construction of the Yumen Oilfield are also continuously transported from Northeast and North China.

                                  Xinhua News Agency reporter Hu Yue photographed

### 繁忙的玉门油矿车站（图片）

1956-07-23
第2版()
专栏：

    繁忙的玉门油矿车站（图片）
    随着兰新铁路铺轨到玉门，王门油矿车站开始出现了繁忙景象。这里每天都有满载天然石油的列车启程东运，支援玉门油矿建设的各种建筑器材也陆续由东北、华北等地运到。　
                                 新华社记者　胡越摄
