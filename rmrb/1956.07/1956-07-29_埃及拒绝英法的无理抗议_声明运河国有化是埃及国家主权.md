Title: Egypt Rejects Unreasonable Protests from Britain and France, Declares Canal Nationalization is Egypt's National Sovereignty
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 6

    Egypt Rejects Unreasonable Protests from Britain and France
    Declares Canal Nationalization is Egypt's National Sovereignty
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 28th: Egypt resolutely rejected the British government's protest to Egypt on July 27 regarding the nationalization of the International Suez Canal Company.
    Ali Sabri, Director of the Political Bureau of the Egyptian Presidential Office, told reporters that Egypt refused to accept the British protest because the nationalization of the International Suez Canal Company was "an action within the scope of Egyptian sovereignty."
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 28th: The Egyptian Ambassador to Paris, Nabi, rejected the protest made by French Foreign Minister Pineau to Egypt regarding Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal.
    Nabi said that the wording of Pineau's protest "is unacceptable and inadmissible." He lodged a strong protest on the spot against the content of Pineau's protest.

### 埃及拒绝英法的无理抗议  声明运河国有化是埃及国家主权

1956-07-29
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及拒绝英法的无理抗议
    声明运河国有化是埃及国家主权
    据新华社28日讯　开罗消息：埃及七月二十七日断然拒绝了英国政府就国际苏彝士运河公司国有化问题向埃及提出的抗议。
    埃及总统府政治局局长阿里·萨布里对记者说，埃及拒绝接受英国的抗议，因为国际苏彝士运河公司的国有化是“埃及主权范围内的一种行动”。
    据新华社28日讯　巴黎消息：埃及驻巴黎大使纳比拒绝法国外交部长比诺就埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有向埃及提出的抗议。
    纳比说：比诺抗议的措词“是不能容许、不能接受的”。他当场对比诺的抗议的内容提出强烈抗议。
