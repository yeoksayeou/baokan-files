Title: Chinese Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Play First Matches in Poland
Author:
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 11th. The Chinese men's and women's basketball teams, invited to visit Poland, played their first friendly matches with Polish athletes in Łódź on July 10th.

    In the match, the Chinese women's team adopted a "fast break" tactic, resulting in a 70-64 victory over the Łódź women's team.

    The men's team match was also intense. As a result, the Chinese men's team lost to the Łódź men's team with a score of 91-95.

    The Chinese men's and women's basketball teams arrived in Poland from Romania on July 7th.

### 我国男女篮球队在波兰作首次比赛

1956-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

    我国男女篮球队在波兰作首次比赛
    新华社华沙11日电　应邀到波兰访问的中国男女篮球队，七月十日在罗兹市同波兰的运动员进行了第一次友谊比赛。
    在比赛中，中国女子队采用了“快攻”战术，结果以七十比六十四战胜罗兹市的女子队。
    男子队的比赛也很激烈。比赛结果，中国男子队以九十一比九十五败于罗兹市的男子队。
    中国男女篮球队是七月七日从罗马尼亚到达波兰的。
