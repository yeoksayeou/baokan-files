Title: An Unpaid Swimming Pool
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 7

Column: Sports News

    An Unpaid Swimming Pool
    (Xinhua News Agency) The newly built "Youth Swimming Pool" in Tianjin has been opened on June 24th. This swimming pool is built in the beautiful Water Park and can accommodate more than 6,000 people swimming at the same time. During the construction of this swimming pool, more than 27,000 young people from factories, government offices, schools, military units, and industrial and commercial circles in Tianjin carried out more than 50,000 hours of voluntary labor here. This swimming pool is free of charge to those who come to swim.

### 一个不收费的游泳场

1956-07-01
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    一个不收费的游泳场
    据新华社讯　天津市新建的“青年游泳场”已在六月二十四日开放。这个游泳场建筑在风景优美的水上公园里，能同时容纳六千多人游泳。在建筑这个游泳场的时候，天津市二万七千多名工厂、机关、学校、部队和工商界青年在这里进行了五万多小时的义务劳动。这个游泳场对前来游泳的人不收费。
