Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Xinjiang Expedition Team Conducts On-the-Spot Surveys in the Altai Mountains and Other Areas
Author:
Date: 1956-07-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, July 24th. The Xinjiang Comprehensive Expedition Team of the Chinese Academy of Sciences left Urumqi on the 23rd for the Altai Mountains and the Manas River Basin. The expedition team will conduct a three-month on-the-spot survey of the water source at the summit of the Altai Mountains and along the banks of the Manas River. They will conduct specific investigations and research on the comprehensive development of agriculture, forestry, and animal husbandry in the two regions, the rational use of land, and natural economic conditions such as topography, soil, and water conservancy. This will provide systematic scientific data for the upcoming comprehensive development of Xinjiang. The expedition team also plans to conduct a survey of the Bogda Peak in the Tianshan Mountains.

The expedition team consists of more than ninety people, divided into four groups: water conservancy, natural conditions, agriculture and animal husbandry, and economy. The members of the expedition team include members of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, experts in soil, plants, insects, grasslands, and economic geography. A large number of personnel from Xinjiang August 1st Agricultural College, the Agriculture Department of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, and the Water Conservancy Bureau also participated in the expedition.

The personnel from the Chinese Academy of Sciences all arrived in Urumqi from Beijing on the 9th. Before departing, the expedition team members conducted surveys on insects and soil in the Nanshan Mountains of Urumqi and Ulap, a suburb of the city.

### 中国科学院新疆考察队  实地踏勘阿尔泰山区等地

1956-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院新疆考察队
    实地踏勘阿尔泰山区等地
    新华社乌鲁木齐24日电　中国科学院新疆综合考察队二十三日离乌鲁木齐市，前往阿尔泰山区和玛纳斯河流域。考察队将在阿尔泰山巅水源和玛纳斯河两岸进行为期三个月的实地踏勘，对两地农业、林业和畜牧业等的综合开发、土地的合理使用以及地形、土壤、水利等自然经济条件作具体的调查和研究，为即将展开的综合开发新疆提供系统的科学资料。考察队还准备到天山山脉博格达板雪峰进行一次踏勘。
    考察队由九十多人组成，分水利、自然条件、农牧、经济四个组。考察队的成员包括中国科学院学部委员，土壤、植物、昆虫、草原和经济地理方面的专家，新疆八一农学院、新疆维吾尔自治区农业厅、水利局等部门也派了大批人员参加考察工作。
    中国科学院人员是九日全部从北京到达乌鲁木齐市的。在出发之前，考察队人员曾到乌鲁木齐南山和市郊乌拉泊进行过昆虫和土壤等方面的考察。
