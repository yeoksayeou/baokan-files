Title: World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs to be Held in Nagasaki in August; Representatives from China, the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, France, and Other Countries Decide to Attend
Author:
Date: 1956-07-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 24th Tokyo Dispatch: According to notifications received by the Preparatory Committee of the World Conference for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, forty representatives from many countries, including China, the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, France, India, and New Zealand, have agreed to participate in the world conference to be held in Japan in August.

The upcoming conference has drawn the attention of peace-loving people from various countries. Many well-known figures in various countries have sent congratulatory letters to the conference's preparatory organization. Representatives from India, Indonesia, Australia, and New Zealand have also written letters expressing their hope that the conference will make specific recommendations regarding "the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen bomb testing in the Pacific."

Student and women's groups in Japan have launched fundraising activities in various regions to raise funds for the conference.

The World Conference will be held from August 9th to 11th in Nagasaki, the second Japanese city to be bombed by the American atomic bomb. Foreign representatives and 3,000 representatives elected from various parts of Japan will jointly discuss the prohibition of atomic weapons, disarmament, the prevention of atomic war, assistance to atomic weapons victims, and the Okinawa issue.

Before the World Conference begins, representatives will participate in the "Peace of the Sea Meeting" held on the Kamakura seaside on August 5th, as well as welcome receptions and peace rallies scheduled to be held in Tokyo.

### 禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会八月在长崎举行  中苏美英法等国代表决定参加

1956-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会八月在长崎举行
    中苏美英法等国代表决定参加
    新华社24日讯　东京消息：据禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会筹备委员会接到的通知，中国、苏联、美国、英国、法国、印度、新西兰等许多国家的四十位代表已经答应参加将在八月在日本举行的这次世界大会。
    即将举行的这次大会已经引起各国爱好和平人士的重视。很多国家的社会著名人士向大会的筹备组织寄去了贺信。印度、印度尼西亚、澳大利亚和新西兰四个国家的代表还写信表示希望大会作出关于“禁止在太平洋进行原子弹和氢弹试验”的具体建议。
    日本的学生、妇女等群众团体已经在各地展开了募捐活动以筹集大会基金。
    世界大会将从八月九日至十一日在日本第二个遭受美国原子弹轰炸的城市——长崎市举行。外国代表和日本各地选出的三千个代表将共同讨论禁止原子武器、裁减军备、防止原子战争、救济原子武器受害者以及冲绳岛问题。
    在世界大会开始以前，代表们将在八月五日参加镰仓海滨举行的“海之和平会”和预定在东京举行的欢迎会与和平集会。
