Title: My First Ambassador to Switzerland Presents Credentials to Chairman Feldmann
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Bern, 16th: Feng Xuan, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Federal Republic of Switzerland, presented his credentials to Feldmann, the Chairman of the Swiss Confederation, on July 13th.

China and Switzerland established diplomatic relations in January 1950. Initially, the diplomatic representatives of the two countries were ministers; in January 1956, by mutual agreement, the level was upgraded to ambassadors.

At the presentation of the credentials, Ambassador Feng Xuan delivered a eulogy, and Swiss Confederation Chairman Feldmann delivered a reply. They congratulated each other on the good relations that already existed between the two countries and expressed their intention to continue to cooperate closely to further develop bilateral relations.

### 我首任驻瑞士大使向费德曼主席递交国书

1956-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    我首任驻瑞士大使向费德曼主席递交国书
    新华社16日讯　伯尔尼消息：中华人民共和国首任驻瑞士联邦特命全权大使冯铉七月十三日向瑞士联邦主席费德曼递交了国书。
    中国同瑞士在1950年1月就建立了外交关系。原先两国的外交代表是公使，1956年1月，经过双方协议升格为大使。
    在递交国书时，冯铉大使致颂词，瑞士联邦主席费德曼致答词。他们祝贺两国之间已经存在良好关系，并且表示将继续密切合作以进一步发展两国关系。
