Title: Luoyang Mining Machinery Plant Under Construction (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1956-07-30
Page: 2

    Luoyang Mining Machinery Plant Under Construction (Picture)
    China's first modern mining machinery manufacturing plant, the Luoyang Mining Machinery Plant, is under intense construction. Once completed, this plant will be able to manufacture more than twenty types of large mining machinery and equipment. The picture shows the comprehensive auxiliary workshop of the plant, which is undergoing comprehensive hoisting. Photo by Zhang Zhuquan (Contribution from Henan Daily)

### 兴建中的洛阳矿山机器厂（图片）

1956-07-30
第2版()
专栏：

    兴建中的洛阳矿山机器厂（图片）
    我国第一座现代化的矿山机械制造工厂——洛阳矿山机器厂，正在紧张施工。这个工厂建成以后，将可制造二十多种大型的矿山机器设备。图为这个厂正在进行综合吊装的综合辅助工场。　　　张祝泉摄（河南日报社稿）
