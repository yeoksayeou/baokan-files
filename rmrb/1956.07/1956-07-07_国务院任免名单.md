Title: State Council Appointments and Removals

Author:

Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - The State Council of the People's Republic of China made a batch of appointments and removals of personnel from state organs and embassies abroad on June 14th. The list is as follows:

    Appointed Zhang Yue and Lin Zhaonan as Counselors of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Arab Republic of Egypt;
    Yang Chunmao, Pan Jiwen, and Xu Shiping as Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Construction Engineering;
    Jin Shuyuan and Zhang Zhao as Assistant Ministers of the Ministry of Forestry;
    Removed Chen Jiakang from the post of Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs;
    Removed Zhang Yue from the post of Deputy Representative of the Commercial Representative Office of the People's Republic of China in the Arab Republic of Egypt;
    Removed Yang Chunmao, Pan Jiwen, and Xu Shiping from the posts of Assistant Ministers of the Ministry of Construction Engineering.

### 国务院任免名单

1956-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国国务院在六月十四日任免了一批国家机关和驻外使馆工作人员，名单如下：
    任命张越、林兆南为中华人民共和国驻埃及共和国大使馆参赞；
    杨春茂、潘纪文、许世平为建筑工程部副部长；
    金树原、张昭为林业部部长助理；
    免去陈家康的外交部部长助理职务；
    免去张越的中华人民共和国驻埃及共和国商务代表处副代表职；
    免去杨春茂、潘纪文、许世平等三人的建筑工程部部长助理职务。
