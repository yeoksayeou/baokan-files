Title: Vietnam Expands Foreign Trade
Author:
Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 2nd. Up to now, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has signed trade agreements with almost all socialist countries. Trade with these countries has increased fivefold in both quantity and value compared to 1954. In addition, Vietnam has signed barter contracts with the French government and the Egyptian government, and has also signed such contracts with companies in the United Kingdom, Switzerland, West Germany, Japan and other countries.

### 越南对外贸易扩大

1956-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    越南对外贸易扩大
    据新华社河内2日电　到现在为止，越南民主共和国政府已经同几乎所有的社会主义国家签订了贸易协定。同这些国家的贸易不论在数量上和价值上都增加到1954年的五倍。此外，越南已经同法国政府和埃及政府签订了换货合同，还同英国、瑞士、西德、日本和其他国家的一些公司签订了这种合同。
