Title: Secretary-General of French "Liberation" Robbe-Binet Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd. Robbe-Binet, Secretary-General of the French newspaper "Liberation", invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, arrived in Beijing on the 21st to participate in the preparatory meeting for the Asian Writers Conference.

### 法国“解放报”秘书长罗伯·比奈到京

1956-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    法国“解放报”秘书长
    罗伯·比奈到京
    新华社23日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的法国“解放报”秘书长罗伯·比奈，已经在二十一日到达北京。参加亚洲作家会议筹备会议
