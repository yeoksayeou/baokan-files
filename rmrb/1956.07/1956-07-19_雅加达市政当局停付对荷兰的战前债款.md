Title: Jakarta Municipality Suspends Payment of Pre-War Debt to the Netherlands
Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 18th -- The Jakarta Municipal authorities, in view of the fact that the Round Table Conference Agreements have been abolished, have decided to suspend payment of pre-war debts and interest owed to the Netherlands under contract.
    Jakarta's acting Mayor Supranoto said: The suspension of payments has taken effect from July 1st this year, and the amount of suspended payments totals 3,271,000盾 (guilders).

### 雅加达市政当局停付对荷兰的战前债款

1956-07-19
第6版()
专栏：

    雅加达市政当局停付对荷兰的战前债款
    据新华社18日讯　雅加达消息：雅加达市政当局鉴于圆桌会议协定已经废除，决定停止支付根据同荷兰的契约所负的战前的债务和利息。
    雅加达市代理市长苏普拉诺托说：停止支付已经从今年七月一日开始生效，停止支付的款项合三百二十七万一千盾。
