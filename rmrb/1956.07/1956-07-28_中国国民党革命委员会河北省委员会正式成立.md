Title: The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Hebei Provincial Committee, Is Officially Established
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 4

    The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang
    Hebei Provincial Committee Officially Established
    Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, 27th. The Second Provincial Party Congress of the Preparatory Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang in Hebei Province, held in Baoding City from the 21st to the 26th, announced the official establishment of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Hebei Provincial Committee.
    At the meeting, Gao Shuxun delivered a work summary report on behalf of the Preparatory Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang in Hebei Province. He pointed out that since the establishment of the Preparatory Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang in Hebei Province four years ago, it has completed various political tasks, and the number of party members has increased more than tenfold compared to 1952.
    Shao Lizi, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, also attended the meeting and delivered a speech. He explained the policy of long-term coexistence and mutual supervision between the Chinese Communist Party and various democratic parties proposed by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The attendees had a serious discussion on this policy and unanimously agreed that the decision of this policy not only shows that the Communist Party has strengthened its unity, trust, and leadership of the democratic parties, but also gives the democratic parties great encouragement, further increasing the responsibility of the democratic parties as assistants to the revolutionary cause.
    The meeting passed a resolution calling for contributions to the liberation of Taiwan. The conference elected 29 members and 4 alternate members, and announced the official establishment of the First Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang in Hebei Province. The 206 party members present also unanimously adopted a letter to the military and political personnel in Taiwan.

### 中国国民党革命委员会河北省委员会正式成立

1956-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    中国国民党革命委员会
    河北省委员会正式成立
    据新华社保定27日电　二十一日到二十六日在保定市举行的中国国民党革命委员会河北省筹备委员会第二次全省党员大会，宣布了民革河北省委员会正式成立。
    大会由高树勋代表民革河北省筹备委员会作了工作总结报告，他指出：民革河北省筹备委员会成立四年以来，完成了各项政治任务，党员人数比1952年增加了十倍多。
    民革中央常务委员邵力子，也到会讲了话，他对中共中央提出的中国共产党和各民主党派长期共存互相监督的方针作了说明。出席会议人员对这一方针作了认真讨论，一致认为这一方针的决定，不仅说明了共产党对民主党派更加强了团结、信赖与领导，也给民主党派以极大的鼓舞，进一步加重了民主党派作为革命事业助手的责任。
    会议通过了决议，要求为解放台湾作出贡献。大会选出了二十九位委员和四位候补委员，宣布了民革河北省第一届委员会正式成立。到会的206名党员，还一致通过了告台湾军政人员书。
