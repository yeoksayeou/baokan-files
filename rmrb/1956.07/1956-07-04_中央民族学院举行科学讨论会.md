Title: Central Institute for Nationalities Holds Scientific Symposium
Author:
Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 7

The Central Institute for Nationalities began a three-day 1956 scientific symposium on July 2nd. At the meeting, seventeen papers on the history, ethnology, and languages of China's minority nationalities will be discussed and studied.

The central issues for discussion at the scientific symposium are: (1) the situation and problems in the historical research of China's minority nationalities, that is, the evaluation of the past research on the history of minority nationalities, and how to compile the history of minority nationalities and study the interrelationships between various ethnic groups in the future; (2) several issues in China's ethnology, namely, the ethnic identification of minority nationalities, the nature of their societies, and the culture and life of each minority nationality.

On the first day of the scientific symposium, the papers presented and discussed at the meeting included: Professor Weng Dujian, Director of the Research Department's paper "On the Situation and Problems of Historical Research of Chinese Minority Nationalities," Vice President Fei Xiaotong and Professor Lin Yaohua's paper "Several Problems in Chinese Ethnology," and Professor Lin Yaohua's report "The Achievements of Soviet Ethnology."

More than 140 people attended the July 2nd scientific symposium, including responsible comrades from relevant units and more than 40 historians, archaeologists, ethnologists, and linguists.
	(Central Institute for Nationalities Journal Editorial Office)

### 中央民族学院举行科学讨论会

1956-07-04
第7版()
专栏：

    中央民族学院举行科学讨论会
    中央民族学院于七月二日起举行为期三天的1956年科学讨论会。会上将讨论、研究十七篇关于我国少数民族历史、民族学和少数民族语言的论文。
    科学讨论会讨论的中心问题是：（一）关于我国少数民族历史研究的情况和问题，即对于过去少数民族历史研究情况的估计，和今后怎样编写少数民族历史和研究各族之间的相互关系等问题；（二）我国民族学上的几个问题，即关于少数民族的族别问题、社会性质问题和各少数民族的文化和生活等问题。
    科学讨论会的第一天，在会上作报告并进行讨论的论文有：研究部主任翁独健教授的论文“关于中国少数民族历史研究的情况和问题”，费孝通副院长、林耀华教授的论文“中国民族学上的几个问题”，林耀华教授的报告“苏联民族学的成就”。
    参加二日科学讨论会的有一百四十余人，其中有各有关单位的负责同志和历史学家、考古学家、民族学家和语言学家等四十多人。
      （中央民族学院院刊编辑室）
