Title: Premier Zhou Sends a Telegram to Express Sympathy to Earthquake Victims in Burma
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: Following the recent series of earthquakes in northern Burma, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to U Ba Swe, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, on July 25th, stating that the Chinese government and people were deeply concerned upon hearing the unfortunate news and expressed their sympathy to the Burmese government and the affected people.

### 周总理致电慰问缅甸地震灾民

1956-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理致电慰问缅甸地震灾民
    新华社27日讯　最近缅甸北部曾接连发生地震，中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在七月二十五日打电报给缅甸联邦总理吴巴瑞说，中国政府和人民非常关切地听到这一不幸的消息，并且向缅甸政府和受灾的人民表示慰问。
