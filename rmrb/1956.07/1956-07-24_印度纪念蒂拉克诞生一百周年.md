Title: India Commemorates the Centennial of Tilak's Birth
Author:
Date: 1956-07-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 23rd. India today commemorated the centennial of the birth of Lokamanya Tilak, "the father of the Indian national liberation movement."

    Indian President Dr. Rajendra Prasad said in a broadcast on All India Radio on the eve of the celebrations: Tilak's name will be recorded in history as one of the foremost builders of the Indian nation. He said: "Tilak was an outstanding figure and a great national leader who not only cherished the ideal of complete independence, but also considered the issue from a revolutionary perspective."

    Nehru said in a commemorative message for Tilak's centennial: Tilak shaped the thinking of two generations of our people, thus influencing millions of Indians.

    Addresses by Congress President Dhebar and other prominent Congress leaders were also delivered on the centennial. All newspapers published special articles about Tilak. To celebrate Tilak's centennial, the Indian government declared July 23rd a holiday and issued commemorative stamps.

### 印度纪念蒂拉克诞生一百周年

1956-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    印度纪念蒂拉克诞生一百周年
    新华社新德里23日电　印度今天纪念了“印度民族解放运动之父”洛克马尼亚·蒂拉克诞生一百周年。
    印度总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德博士在庆祝活动前夕在全印广播电台的广播中说：蒂拉克的名字将作为印度国家最主要的缔造者之一载入史册。他说：“蒂拉克是一位杰出的人物和伟大的民族领袖，他不仅怀有完全独立的理想，而且还曾经从革命的角度来考虑这个问题。”
    尼赫鲁在蒂拉克诞生一百周年的纪念词中说：蒂拉克陶冶了我国两代人民的思想，因而影响了千百万印度人。
    在一百周年纪念日还发表了国大党主席德巴和国大党其他著名领袖的致词。所有报纸都刊载了关于蒂拉克的专文。印度政府为了庆祝蒂拉克的一百周年纪念，宣布在七月二十三日放假一天，并且发行纪念邮票。
