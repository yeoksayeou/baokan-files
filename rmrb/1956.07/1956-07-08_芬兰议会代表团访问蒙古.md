Title: Finnish Parliamentary Delegation Visits Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1956-07-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar Dispatch: A Finnish parliamentary delegation led by Finnish Speaker of Parliament Sukselainen visited Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, on July 6 after visiting China.

    The guests, accompanied by Dugersuren, Chairman of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, and some representatives of the Great People's Hural, visited the Choibalsan United Factory.

    When leaving the factory, Sukselainen wished the people there greater achievements. The Finnish parliamentarians then toured the city.

    A luncheon was held for the guests. Sukselainen spoke at the banquet, expressing his gratitude for the warm and friendly reception. He said that this visit to Mongolia had given them a very good impression, both of the country and its people, and he wished the entire Mongolian people success.

    The delegation left Ulaanbaatar by plane on the same day to return home.

### 芬兰议会代表团访问蒙古

1956-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰议会代表团访问蒙古
    新华社7日讯　塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：由芬兰议会议长苏克舍拉宁率领的芬兰议会代表团，在访问了中国之后，在七月六日访问了蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托。
    来宾们在蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席杜格苏伦和大人民呼拉尔的一些代表的陪同下参观了乔巴山联合工厂。
    苏克舍拉宁在离开工厂的时候祝那里的人们取得更大的成就。随后芬兰议员们游览了这个城市。
    为来宾们举行了午宴。苏克舍拉宁在宴会上讲话，对于这次热烈和友好的接待表示感谢。他说，这次到蒙古来使我们获得了很好的印象。对你们的国家和你们的人民都是这样，我们祝全体蒙古人民取得成就。
    代表团在同一天离开乌兰巴托乘飞机返国。
