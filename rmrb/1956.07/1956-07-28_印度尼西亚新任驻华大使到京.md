Title: New Indonesian Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th. Sukardjo Wirjopranoto, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Indonesia to China, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 27th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department, and Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association. Sani, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia in China, as well as embassy staff, diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries in China also went to welcome him.

### 印度尼西亚新任驻华大使到京

1956-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚新任驻华大使到京
    新华社27日讯　印度尼西亚共和国新任驻华特命全权大使苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托已在二十七日下午到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有外交部礼宾司司长柯华，亚洲司副司长陈叔亮和中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之。印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使馆临时代办萨尼及使馆人员和各国驻华使节及外交官员也前往欢迎。
