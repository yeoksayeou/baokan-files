Title: Bulganin Hosts Sihanouk; Soviet and Cambodian Leaders Exchange Medals
Author:
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th, According to TASS: On July 6th, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, held a reception in the Grand Kremlin Palace for the visit of Cambodian Prince Sihanouk to the Soviet Union. Leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government attended the reception.

Bulganin said in his speech at the reception that the Soviet people have always held a sympathetic attitude towards the Cambodian people's struggle for the independence of their country. The Soviet Union welcomes the policy of non-participation in military blocs and the peaceful policy pursued by the Kingdom of Cambodia. He said that the relations between the Soviet and Cambodian countries will be successfully developed, benefiting the people of both countries.

Sihanouk said in his reply: A common characteristic has established friendship between the people of the small country of Cambodia and the people of the large country of the Soviet Union. This characteristic is that the people of both our countries love peace, justice, and progress. They all hope that international relations will develop along the path of cooperation and peaceful coexistence.

Sihanouk added that the contacts we have established in the Soviet Union show that as long as we steadfastly follow this path, nothing can separate us.

They wished each other happiness for the people of both countries in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th, According to TASS: On July 7th, Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, on behalf of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, awarded the Order of Suvorov, First Class, to Sihanouk, and asked him to transfer it to the King of Cambodia, Norodom Suramarit, in recognition of the King of Cambodia's achievements in establishing and developing friendly relations between the Kingdom of Cambodia and the Soviet Union.

Voroshilov then awarded Sihanouk the Order of Suvorov, First Class.

Sihanouk said at the award ceremony that the Cambodian people will always stand by the Soviet Union in the struggle for peace and international cooperation.

On the same day, Sihanouk, on behalf of the King of Cambodia, Norodom Suramarit, awarded Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the highest Cambodian medal, the Grand Cross of the Royal Order. Sihanouk also handed a letter from Suramarit to Voroshilov.

After accepting the medal, Voroshilov expressed his belief that the friendship between the Soviet people and the Cambodian people will develop and strengthen.

### 布尔加宁招待西哈努克  苏柬领导人互赠勋章

1956-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁招待西哈努克
    苏柬领导人互赠勋章
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社报道：七月六日，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁为柬埔寨西哈努克亲王访问苏联在大克里姆林宫举行了一次招待会。苏联共产党和政府的领导人都出席了这次招待会。
    布尔加宁在招待会上致词说，苏联人民对柬埔寨人民争取自己国家的独立的斗争，一贯抱着同情的态度。苏联欢迎柬埔寨王国所执行的不参加军事集团的政策和和平政策。他说，苏柬两国之间的关系将要获得成功的发展，使两国人民得到好处。
    西哈努克在致答词的时候说：一种共同的特性使小国柬埔寨人民同大国苏联人民建立了友谊，这种特性就是：我们两国人民都热爱和平、正义和进步。他们都希望国际关系沿着合作与和平共处的道路发展。
    西哈努克又说，我们在苏联所建立的接触表明，只要我们坚定不移地走这条道路，就没有东西能够把我们分开。
    他们在友好热烈的气氛中互祝两国人民的幸福。
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，在七月七日代表苏联最高苏维埃主席团把一枚一级苏沃洛夫勋章交给西哈努克，请他转交给柬埔寨国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特，以表彰柬埔寨国王在建立和发展柬埔寨王国和苏联的友好关系上的功绩。
    伏罗希洛夫接着还授给西哈努克一枚一级苏沃洛夫勋章。
    西哈努克在授勋仪式上说，柬埔寨的人民在争取和平和国际合作的斗争中，将始终站在苏联这一边。
    同一天，西哈努克代表柬埔寨国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特，把一枚柬埔寨最高勋章——皇家大十字勋章授给苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫。西哈努克同时把苏拉玛里特的一封信交给了伏罗希洛夫。
    伏罗希洛夫接受勋章后表示，他相信苏联人民同柬埔寨人民之间的友谊将会发展和加强。
