Title: British Labour Party Demands Military Spending Cuts and an End to Conscription
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, London, 20th. The British Labour Party parliamentary group overwhelmingly passed a resolution on the 18th, demanding that conscription be stopped after the batch of recruits serving until 1958 have completed their service. The resolution also calls for cuts in military spending, the amount of which was not specified.

This resolution of the Labour Party parliamentary group will form the basis of the Labour Party's views during the House of Commons debate on defense policy on July 31.

### 英国工党要求削减军费停止征兵

1956-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

    英国工党要求削减军费停止征兵
    新华社20日讯　伦敦消息：英国工党议会党团在十八日以压倒多数通过一个决议，要求到1958年的一批服役者应征服兵役后，即停止征兵。决议还要求削减军费，削减数量没有说明。
    工党议会党团这个决议将成为七月三十一日下院辩论国防政策的时候工党意见的基础。
