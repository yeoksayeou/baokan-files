Title: Chiang Army Deputy Company Commander Nie Zhiwei and Soldier Zhang Dailin Swim Across the Sea and Return One After Another
Author:
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, From the Front: Before dawn on June 30, at a little after 2 o'clock, Nie Zhiwei, a lieutenant and deputy company commander of the Chiang army on Dadan Island, held a wooden stake and swam across the turbulent sea for six hours to Xiamen Island. He surrendered to the Chinese People's Liberation Army and received a warm welcome from the officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army.

Nie Zhiwei, originally named Nie Yuwu, is a lieutenant and deputy company commander of the heavy weapons company of the Third Battalion, 243rd Regiment, 81st Division of the Chiang army. He is from Wugang County, Hunan Province, and is 26 years old this year.

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 4th: At a little after 5 o'clock on the morning of the 3rd, Zhang Dailin, a soldier of the Chiang army on Jinmen Island, swam for six hours with a lifebuoy and arrived at Dadeng Island, Fujian Province. He was warmly received by the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. He is the fourth Chiang army officer or soldier to swim across the sea and return in 22 days.

Zhang Dailin is a sergeant telecommunications operator from the Fifth Company, Second Battalion, 94th Regiment, 32nd Division of the Chiang army. He is 26 years old and from Qingdao City, Shandong Province.

### 蒋军副连长聂智威和士兵张代林  泅水渡海先后归来

1956-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋军副连长聂智威和士兵张代林
    泅水渡海先后归来
    新华社福建前线电　六月三十日天亮前两点多钟，大担岛上的蒋军中尉副连长聂智威持一根木桩，在波涛汹涌的大海上泅水六小时到了厦门岛，向中国人民解放军部队投诚，受到人民解放军官兵的热情接待。
    聂智威原名聂毓吾，是蒋军八十一师二四三团三营重兵器连的中尉副连长，湖南武冈县人，今年二十六岁。
    新华社福建前线4日电　三日早晨五点多钟，金门岛上蒋军士兵张代林，带救生圈一个泅水六小时到达福建省大登岛，受到中国人民解放军部队的热情接待。他是二十二天内第四个泅水渡海归来的蒋军官兵。
    张代林是蒋军三十二师九十四团二营五连中士话务员，二十六岁，山东省青岛市人。
