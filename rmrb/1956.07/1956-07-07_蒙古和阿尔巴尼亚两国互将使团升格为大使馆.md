Title: Mongolia and Albania to Upgrade Missions to Embassies

Author:

Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 4th, Ulaanbaatar dispatch: According to the Mongolian "Truth Daily" report, the government of the Mongolian People's Republic and the government of the People's Republic of Albania have decided to upgrade the Mongolian mission in Tirana and the Albanian mission in Ulaanbaatar to embassies, and will mutually dispatch ambassadors.

### 蒙古和阿尔巴尼亚两国互将使团升格为大使馆

1956-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古和阿尔巴尼亚两国互将使团升格为大使馆
    新华社4日讯　乌兰巴托消息：据蒙古“真理报”报道，蒙古人民共和国政府和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府决定把蒙古驻地拉那使团和阿尔巴尼亚驻乌兰巴托使团升格为大使馆，并且互派大使。
