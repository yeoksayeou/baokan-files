Title: US House of Representatives Rejects School Construction Appropriation
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 8th—The U.S. House of Representatives rejected on July 5th a bill to appropriate $1.6 billion for school construction over the next four years. Newspaper reports emphasize that people had originally hoped that this session of Congress would agree to allocate this much-needed funding for school construction, but the House of Representatives vote has dashed all these hopes.

### 美众院否决拨款修学校

1956-07-09
第6版()
专栏：

    美众院否决拨款修学校
    据新华社8日讯　华盛顿消息：美国众议院在七月五日否决了关于拨款十六亿美元在最近四年内建筑学校的法案。报纸的报道着重指出，人们原来曾经希望本届国会会议会同意拨出这笔十分必要的经费来建筑学校，但是众议院的表决结果使这一切希望全部成了泡影。
