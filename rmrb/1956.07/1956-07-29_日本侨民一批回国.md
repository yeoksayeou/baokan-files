Title: Batch of Japanese Nationals Return Home
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, July 28th: 203 Japanese nationals who voluntarily chose to return to their home country departed this afternoon on the Japanese ship "Kouan Maru." Accompanying this group of Japanese nationals were also thirty-four Japanese women who had married Chinese citizens and their forty-four children, returning home for family visits. They will return to China after their visits.

### 日本侨民一批回国

1956-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    日本侨民一批回国
    据新华社天津28日电　203名自愿回国的日本侨民今天下午乘日本“兴安丸”轮船回国。和这批日侨同船回国的还有回家探亲的三十四名在中国和中国人结婚的日本妇女以及她们的四十四个子女。她们在探亲后还将回到中国。
