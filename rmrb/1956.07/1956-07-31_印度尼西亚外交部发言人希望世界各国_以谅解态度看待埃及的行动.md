Title: Indonesian Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Hopes All Countries in the World Will View Egypt's Actions with Understanding
Author:
Date: 1956-07-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 30th. Indonesian Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Kusumo Uwidado said today that Egypt's decision to nationalize the Suez Canal is merely a continuation of the Egyptian people's struggle to complete their independence and sovereignty.

He said, "We hope that all countries in the world will view this issue with an attitude of understanding towards a driving force with profound reasons, which is to improve the living standards of the Egyptian people. If this understanding exists, all actions that could undermine peace in the Middle East can be avoided."

He also said: "We fully understand Egypt's situation. Under the current circumstances, Egypt, inspired by the ideal of improving the living standards of the Egyptian people, has had no choice but to choose the path of nationalizing the Suez Canal."

### 印度尼西亚外交部发言人希望世界各国  以谅解态度看待埃及的行动

1956-07-31
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外交部发言人希望世界各国
    以谅解态度看待埃及的行动
    新华社雅加达30日电　印度尼西亚外交部发言人库苏莫维达多今天说，埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的决定只不过是埃及人民为完成他们的独立和主权而进行的斗争的继续。
    他说，我们希望世界各国将对一种具有深远原因的推动力抱着谅解态度来看待这个问题，这种推动力就是提高埃及人民的生活水平。如果这种谅解存在的话，一切可能破坏中东和平的行动就都能够得到避免。”
    他还说：“我们充分理解埃及的处境，埃及在目前的情况下，由于希望提高埃及人民的生活水平的理想的鼓舞，因而不得不选择把苏彝士运河收归国有的道路。
