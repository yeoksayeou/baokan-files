Title: Democratic German Government Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from TASS, Moscow. The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl, left Moscow on the morning of the 18th to return home.

Grotewohl spoke before the plane took off. He said that the results of this meeting were a particularly effective support for the German Democratic Republic's struggle to restore German unity. He said that the results of our talks meant that the German Democratic Republic had taken another big step forward on the road to socialist construction, further development of the national economy, and continuous improvement of the people's material well-being. The results of the talks also meant that the first state of workers and peasants in German history had been greatly consolidated.

### 民主德国政府代表团回国

1956-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府代表团回国
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯　以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团十八日上午离莫斯科回国。
    格罗提渥在飞机起飞前讲了话。他说，这次会谈的结果，是对德意志民主共和国争取德国恢复统一斗争的特别有效的支持。他说，我们会谈的结果对德意志民主共和国来说，意味着在社会主义建设，国家经济的进一步发展，以及人民物质福利的不断提高的道路上又前进了一大步，会谈的结果还意味着德国有史以来的第一个工农国家已经大大巩固了。
