Title: Mongolian Cooperative Sports Association Men's Volleyball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Mongolian People's Republic Cooperative Sports Association men's volleyball team, a group of fourteen people, arrived in Beijing this afternoon.

    The volleyball team, led by Ganzha, director of the Mongolian People's Republic Cooperative Sports Association, was warmly welcomed at the airport today by representatives of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China and Chinese volleyball players.

    This volleyball team will hold a friendly match with the Chinese Coal Mine Sports Association men's volleyball team in Beijing on July 7. After visiting Beijing, they will visit Tianjin, Shenyang, and Changchun.

### 蒙古合作社体协男子排球队到京

1956-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古合作社体协男子排球队到京
    据新华社3日讯　蒙古人民共和国合作社体育协会男子排球队一行十四人，今天下午到达北京。
    这个由蒙古人民共和国合作社体育协会主任冈扎率领的排球队，今天在飞机场上受到了中华人民共和国体育运动委员会的代表和中国的排球运动员的热烈欢迎。
    这个排球队将要在七月七日同中国煤矿体育协会男子排球队在京举行友谊比赛。他们在访问北京以后，将到天津、沈阳和长春访问。
