Title: Exploring the Treasures of the Nanling Region
Author:
Date: 1956-07-12
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 10th: A geological work team began geological surveying and mineral prospecting in ten work areas of the Nanling Region from the end of June and the beginning of July. They will spend two and a half years completing the geological mapping of the entire Nanling Region and comprehensively assessing the prospects of various mineral deposits in this area.

    The Nanling Region covers an area of ​​77,000 square kilometers, spanning four provinces: Guangdong, Guangxi, Hunan, and Jiangxi, with most of it in Guangdong. According to existing partial geological data and mineral exploitation, especially according to the information provided by the people in recent years on finding and reporting minerals, the mineral deposits in this region are widely distributed and there are many types of minerals; among them, tungsten, tin, lead, zinc and other non-ferrous metal deposits are the most abundant.

### 探测南岭区域的宝藏

1956-07-12
第2版()
专栏：

    探测南岭区域的宝藏
    新华社广州10日电　一支地质工作的队伍从六月底、七月初开始，在南岭区域的十个工区展开了地质测量和矿产普查。他们将用两年半的时间完成整个南岭区域的地质填图，全面地作出这个区域的各种矿藏的远景评价。
    南岭区域面积七万七千平方公里，地跨广东、广西、湖南、江西四省，其中大部分在广东。根据现有的部分地质资料和矿藏开采情况，特别是根据几年来人民群众找矿、报矿的情况表明，这个区域的矿藏分布很广，矿种很多；其中钨、锡、铅、锌等各种有色金属矿藏最丰富。
