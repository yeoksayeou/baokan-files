Title: Sino-Japanese Publication Exchange Agreement Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Japanese Publication Exchange Delegation, which was invited by the International Bookstore to visit China, signed an agreement with the International Bookstore in Beijing tonight on the exchange of publications between China and Japan.
    This delegation, headed by Shimonaka Yasaburo, arrived in Beijing on June 29th. During their stay in Beijing, they had extensive contacts with Chinese publishing circles. Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, and Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, respectively received all members of the delegation.

### 中日两国出版物交流协议在京签订

1956-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中日两国出版物交流协议在京签订
    新华社11日讯　应国际书店邀请来中国访问的日本出版交流代表团，今晚同国际书店在北京签订了关于中日两国出版物交流的协议。
    这个以下中弥三郎为首的代表团是在六月二十九日到达北京的。在北京期间曾和中国出版界进行了广泛的接触。全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，文化部部长沈雁冰，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南曾分别接见了代表团全体团员。
