Title: The Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China to be held on September 15th
Author:
Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Sixth Plenary Session (Enlarged) of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China decided on October 11, 1955, to convene the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China in the second half of 1956; Now the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has decided that the Eighth National Congress will be held in Beijing on September 15, 1956. The main agenda of this Congress is as follows: 1. The work report of the Party Central Committee; 2. Report on the revision of the Party Constitution; 3. Suggestions on the Second Five-Year Plan for the development of the national economy; 4. Election of the Party Central Committee.

### 中共第八次全国代表大会定九月十五日召开

1956-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中共第八次全国代表大会定九月十五日召开
    新华社6日讯　1955年10月11日中国共产党第七届中央委员会第六次全体会议（扩大）决议，在1956年下半年召开中国共产党第八次全国代表大会；现在中国共产党中央委员会已经决定第八次全国代表大会在1956年9月15日在北京召开。这次代表大会的主要议程如下：1、党的中央委员会的工作报告；2、关于修改党章的报告；3、关于发展国民经济的第二个五年计划的建议；4、选举党的中央委员会。
