Title: Commemorating Dutch Painter Rembrandt
Author:
Date: 1956-07-18
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Teachers at the Central Academy of Fine Arts gathered this evening to discuss the artistic achievements of Rembrandt, one of the world's cultural figures and a great Dutch painter. Academy President Jiang Feng, Vice Presidents Wu Zuoren and Wang Manshuo, and professors spoke at the meeting, introducing Rembrandt's representative works and his artistic techniques.

Three days ago, the Central Academy of Fine Arts held an exhibition of Rembrandt's works, and Vice President Wu Zuoren gave a report to the students on "Rembrandt's Life and Art" to commemorate the 350th anniversary of Rembrandt's birth.

### 纪念荷兰画家伦勃朗

1956-07-18
第7版()
专栏：

    纪念荷兰画家伦勃朗
    据新华社16日讯　中央美术学院教师们今晚集会，座谈世界文化名人之一、荷兰伟大画家伦勃朗的艺术成就。院长江丰和副院长吴作人、王曼硕和教授等都在会上发言，介绍伦勃朗的代表性作品和他的艺术技巧。
    中央美术学院在三天前曾举行了伦勃朗作品图片展览，由副院长吴作人向学生们作了“伦勃朗的生平及艺术”的报告，纪念伦勃朗诞生三百五十周年。
