Title: Philippine House Special Committee Passes Bill Advocating Limiting Scope and Authority of US Bases in Philippines
Author:
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th: According to a United Press International report from Manila on July 6th: A special committee of the Philippine House of Representatives formally passed recommendations on amending the 1947 US-Philippine Military Bases Agreement. These recommendations advocate limiting the scope of US bases in the Philippines and restricting US authority within these bases.

The report says the main contents of the special committee's recommendations are as follows:

	I. Include a clause in the Military Bases Agreement stipulating that if either government wishes to abolish the agreement, it can unilaterally abolish it one year after giving notice.

	II. Reduce the lease term of these bases from ninety-nine years to twenty-five years.

	III. Include a clause in the agreement stipulating that no taxes, fees, or other levies may be imposed on civilians in these bases or reservations unless Philippine law permits it.

	IV. Cancel a clause in the agreement that stipulates that Filipino nationals in these bases or reservations must obtain US consent when exercising their mining rights within these places.

	V. Raise the Philippine flag at all US military bases as a measure that must be taken after the United States recognizes Philippine sovereignty over these bases.

Leaders of the ruling Nationalist Party and the opposition Liberal Party in the House of Representatives immediately guaranteed that a resolution including these recommendations would be passed in the House soon. Speaker Laurel Jr. plans to submit the special committee's recommendations to the House of Representatives for discussion on July 10th.

### 菲众院一个特别委员会通过议案  主张限制美在菲基地的范围和权限

1956-07-09
第6版()
专栏：

    菲众院一个特别委员会通过议案
    主张限制美在菲基地的范围和权限
    新华社8日讯　据合众社马尼拉七月六日消息：菲律宾众议院的一个特别委员会正式通过了关于修改1947年的美菲军事基地协定的建议，这项建议主张限定美国在菲律宾的基地的范围和限制美国在这些基地的权限。
    消息说：这个特别委员会的建议主要内容如下：
    一、在军事基地协定中列入一项条款，规定凡是任何一方政府希望废除这个协定，它可以在发出通知一年以后片面地废除这个协定。
    二、把这些基地的租借期从九十九年减少到二十五年。
    三、在协定中列入一项条款，规定不得对这些基地或保留地的平民征收赋税、手续费或其他税款，除非菲律宾法律允许这样做。
    四、取消协定中的一项条款，这项条款规定这些基地或保留地的菲律宾国民在这些地方以内行使开矿的权利的时候，必需得到美国的同意。
    五、在所有的美国军事基地升起菲律宾国旗，作为美国承认菲律宾对这些基地的主权以后必须采取的措施。
    众议院的执政党国民党和反对党自由党的领袖立即保证很快在众议院通过一项包括这些建议的决议。议长小劳雷尔计划在七月十日把这个特别委员会的建议提交众议院讨论。
