Title: Cannot Buy Suitable Graph Paper
Author: Zhou
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 2

    Reader Opinions

    Cannot Buy Suitable Graph Paper
    Graph paper and graph notebooks are essential items for industrial schools, scientific institutions, and factories. Although the Beijing Cultural and Educational Supplies Company sells a type of graph paper, the grid spacing is too small, and the printing and paper quality are also poor, making it very unsuitable. Beijing is the political, scientific, and cultural center of our country, yet it doesn't even have suitable graph paper and graph paper notebooks for sale. This is really inexcusable. It is hoped that the Beijing Cultural and Educational Supplies Company will organize production.
              (Zhou)

### 买不到适用的方格纸

1956-07-09
第2版()
专栏：读者意见

    买不到适用的方格纸
    方格纸和方格本子，是工业学校、科学机关和工厂必用的东西。北京市文教用品公司虽然有一种方格纸卖，但格子间隔太小，印刷和纸质也很低劣，很不适用。北京是我国政治、科学和文化的中心，竟然没有适用的方格纸和方格纸本子卖，实在说不过去，希望北京市文教用品公司组织生产。
              （周）
