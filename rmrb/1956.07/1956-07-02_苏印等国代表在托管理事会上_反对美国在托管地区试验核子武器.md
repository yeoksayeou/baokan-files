Title: Representatives of the Soviet Union, India, and Other Countries Oppose US Nuclear Weapons Testing in Trust Territories at the Trusteeship Council
Author:
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York Dispatch from TASS—During the United Nations Trusteeship Council's review of the United States' report on the situation in the Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands, Soviet representative G.P. Grubiakov reiterated that the United States' use of trust territories for nuclear weapons testing is intolerable. He stated that conducting such tests violates the provisions of the United Nations Charter and the Trusteeship Agreement, and threatens the lives and welfare of the inhabitants of the trust territories.
    Representatives of India, Burma, and Syria supported the Soviet representative's opinion. They said that their governments fundamentally oppose the testing of atomic and thermonuclear weapons, especially in trust territories.

### 苏印等国代表在托管理事会上  反对美国在托管地区试验核子武器

1956-07-02
第6版()
专栏：

    苏印等国代表在托管理事会上
    反对美国在托管地区试验核子武器
    新华社据塔斯社纽约讯　苏联代表格鲁比雅科夫在联合国托管理事会审议美国托管太平洋岛屿的情况报告时，再次指出，美国利用托管领土进行核子武器试验是不能容忍的，进行这种试验违反了联合国宪章的条款和托管协定，使托管领土的居民的生活和福利遭到威胁。
    印度、缅甸和叙利亚的代表都支持苏联代表的意见。他们说，他们的政府根本反对进行原子武器和热核子武器的试验，尤其是反对在托管领土上进行这种试验。
