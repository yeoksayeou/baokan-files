Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Expresses Hope for Peace
Author:
Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 6th. Pakistani Prime Minister Ali stated in London on the 5th that the struggle for peace is the noblest cause, and Pakistan is united with Britain in this effort.

At a banquet held by the Pakistan Association in his honor, Ali said, "For an underdeveloped country like ours, nothing is more important than peace—long-term, uninterrupted peace." He said it is already clear to mankind that a major war would mean complete and universal destruction.

### 巴基斯坦总理表示希望和平

1956-07-07
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理表示希望和平
    新华社6日讯　伦敦消息：巴基斯坦总理阿里五日在伦敦说，争取和平的斗争是最崇高的事业，巴基斯坦在这种努力中是同英国联合一致的。
    阿里在巴基斯坦协会招待他的宴会上说，“对于我们这样一个不发达的国家来说，再也没有比和平——长期的、不中断的和平——更重要的了。”他说，大战将意味着彻底和普遍的毁灭，这一点对人类已经很明显。
