Title: Hungary and Greece Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, July 20th: According to TASS, the Hungarian envoy to Belgrade and the Greek ambassador to Belgrade signed an agreement on July 19th regarding the establishment of diplomatic relations between Hungary and Greece and the mutual dispatch of ministers.

The agreement also stipulates that after the establishment of diplomatic relations, the two countries will begin negotiations to resolve economic and financial issues.

### 匈希决定建立外交关系

1956-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    匈希决定建立外交关系
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯二十日讯：匈牙利驻贝尔格莱德公使和希腊驻贝尔格莱德大使七月十九日签订了关于匈牙利和希腊两国之间建立外交关系和互派公使级外交代表的协定。
    协定还规定，在建立外交关系以后，两国将开始举行谈判以解决经济和财政问题。
