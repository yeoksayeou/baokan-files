Title: Mikoyan Arrives in Yugoslavia, Tito to Visit Greece
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: According to the Yugoslav News Agency, Soviet First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, arrived in Brioni on the afternoon of July 21st. He was welcomed at Pula Airport by Ranković, Vice President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia.

That evening, President Tito held a reception for him. It is understood that Mikoyan will visit Brioni for two days.

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: TASS Belgrade, July 21st: "Borba" reported that Yugoslav President Tito and his wife, at the invitation of Greek King Paul I and Queen Frederica, will be traveling to Corfu, Greece, in the coming days for a period of time.

### 米高扬到南斯拉夫  铁托将访问希腊

1956-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    米高扬到南斯拉夫  
    铁托将访问希腊
    新华社22日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬七月二十一日下午到达布里俄尼。他在普拉机场上受到了南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席兰科维奇的欢迎。
    当天晚上，铁托总统为他举行了招待会。据悉，米高扬将在布里俄尼访问两天。
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德二十一日讯：“战斗报”报道，南斯拉夫总统铁托和夫人应希腊国王保罗一世和王后弗雷德里卡的邀请，日内将前往希腊的科孚岛逗留一些时候。
