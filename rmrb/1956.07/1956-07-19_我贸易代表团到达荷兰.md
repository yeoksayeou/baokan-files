Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Arrives in the Netherlands
Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 16th - Amsterdam Dispatch: At the invitation of the Netherlands Central Organization for External Economic Affairs, the trade delegation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, led by Yu Kejian, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, arrived in Amsterdam on the 15th. Among those greeting them at the airport was Mr. Ooyevaar, Vice Chairman of the Netherlands Central Organization for External Economic Affairs.

Yu Kejian said in his speech at the airport: The Chinese delegation is very pleased to have the opportunity to visit the Netherlands – a country with a traditional position in international trade and shipping. Sino-Dutch trade has a long history, and the purpose of this visit by the Chinese delegation is to seek, together with Dutch friends, the possibility of further restoring and developing trade relations between China and the Netherlands.

### 我贸易代表团到达荷兰

1956-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团到达荷兰
    据新华社柏林16日电　阿姆斯特丹消息：应荷兰中央对外经济事务组织邀请，中国国际贸易促进委员会贸易访问团在国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长余克坚率领下，于十五日到达阿姆斯特丹。在机场迎接的有荷兰中央对外经济事务组织副主席奥耶瓦尔等。
    余克坚在机场致词说：中国访问团有机会访问荷兰——这个在国际贸易与航运方面有传统地位的国家，感到非常愉快。中荷贸易有着悠久的历史，中国访问团这次访问的目的是希望与荷兰朋友一道寻求进一步恢复和发展中荷间贸易关系的可能性。
