Title: State Council Holds Plenary Meeting Approving Matters Concerning College Enrollment, etc.
Author:
Date: 1956-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th: The 35th plenary meeting of the State Council was held on the afternoon of the 13th.

    After listening to explanations from Minister of Higher Education Yang Xiufeng and Minister of Education Zhang Xiruo, the meeting approved the Higher Education Ministry's request report on the 1956 college enrollment plan and the Education Ministry's request report on the 1956 higher normal school and middle school enrollment issues. The meeting pointed out that to ensure the smooth implementation of the college enrollment plan, it is necessary to seize the basic construction work of schools and take various effective measures to solve the problem of teachers.

    After listening to a statement by Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on several issues in the current lives of workers, the meeting held a discussion and decided to establish a special group responsible for conducting research and proposing specific solutions to the problems.

    After listening to a statement by Fu Qiutao, Secretary-General of the Central Demobilized Construction Committee, the meeting approved in principle the "Measures of the State Council for Handling Relevant Issues of Collective Demobilized Military Personnel."

    After listening to a statement by Minister of Food Zhang Naiqi, the meeting discussed and approved in principle the 1956-1957 national grain revenue and expenditure plan and the State Council's regulations on the unified purchase and sale of grain by agricultural producers' cooperatives.

    After listening to a statement by Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, the meeting approved the "Organizational Rules (Draft) of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region."

    Finally, the meeting approved appointment and removal matters.

### 国务院举行全体会议  批准有关高等学校招生等事项

1956-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    批准有关高等学校招生等事项
    新华社13日讯　国务院全体会议第三十五次会议，在十三日下午举行。
    会议在听了高等教育部长杨秀峰和教育部长张奚若的说明后，批准了高等教育部关于1956年高等学校招生计划问题的请示报告和教育部关于1956年高等师范学校和中学招生问题的请示报告。会议指出，为了保证高等学校招生计划顺利进行，必须抓紧学校基本建设工作，采取各种有效措施解决师资问题。
    会议在听了中华全国总工会主席赖若愚关于当前职工生活中若干问题的说明后，进行了讨论，并且决定成立专门小组负责进行研究，提出解决问题的具体办法。
    会议在听了中央转业建设委员会秘书长傅秋涛的说明后，原则通过了“关于国务院对于集体转业军队有关问题处理办法”。
    会议在听了粮食部长章乃器的说明后，讨论和原则批准了1956——1957年度国家粮食收支计划和国务院关于农业生产合作社粮食统购统销的规定。
    会议在听了民族事务委员会副主任汪锋的说明后，通过了“西藏自治区筹备委员会组织简则（草案）”。
    最后，会议通过了任免事项。
