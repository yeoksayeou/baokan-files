Title: Japanese Shipbuilding Workers Strike Demanding Summer Allowance

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-03
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 2nd: 12,000 shipbuilding workers at the Nagasaki Shipyard of Mitsubishi Shipbuilding Company in Japan began a 48-hour strike on the afternoon of July 1st, demanding the payment of summer allowances.
    The strike was launched immediately after negotiations between the union and management broke down. In the negotiations, the management refused to pay the 28,000 yen summer allowance requested by the workers, and only indicated its willingness to pay 26,500 yen.

### 日本造船工人罢工  要求发给夏季津贴

1956-07-03
第6版()
专栏：

    日本造船工人罢工
    要求发给夏季津贴
    据新华社2日讯　东京消息：日本三菱造船公司长崎造船所的一万二千个造船工人，从七月一日下午开始举行四十八小时罢工，要求发给夏季津贴。
    罢工是在工会和资方的谈判破裂以后立即举行的。在谈判中，资方拒绝根据工人要求发给二万八千日元的夏季津贴，它只表示愿意发给二万六千五百日元。
