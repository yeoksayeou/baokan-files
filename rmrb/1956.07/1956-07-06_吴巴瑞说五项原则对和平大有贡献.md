Title: U Ba Swe Says the Five Principles Make a Great Contribution to Peace
Author:
Date: 1956-07-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, July 5th: Burmese Prime Minister U Ba Swe said on July 3rd, when meeting with local reporters, that the joint statement on the Five Principles had made a significant contribution to easing international tensions and expanding the world's peaceful areas.

When talking about his feelings about the joint statement issued by the Chinese and Burmese Prime Ministers two years ago, U Ba Swe said that countries adhering to a peaceful foreign policy have implemented these five principles. Initially, some countries showed hesitation in accepting these principles, but now, more and more countries are willing to accept them.

### 吴巴瑞说五项原则对和平大有贡献

1956-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    吴巴瑞说五项原则对和平大有贡献
    新华社仰光5日电　缅甸总理吴巴瑞七月三日在接见本地记者时说：关于五项原则的联合声明已经对缓和国际紧张局势和扩大世界和平地区作出了显著的贡献。
    在谈到他对两年前中国总理和缅甸总理发表的联合声明的感想时，吴巴瑞说，坚持和平外交政策的国家都已经实行了这五项原则。最初，有些国家对接受这些原则表现了迟疑不决，但是现在，越来越多的国家都愿意接受这些原则了。
