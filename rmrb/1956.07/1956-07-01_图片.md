    The rapid growth of young scientific workers is inseparable from the cultivation of veteran scientists. Feng Depei (left), a famous Chinese physiologist and director of the Institute of Physiology and Biochemistry of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, is guiding Fan Shifan (right), who has been newly promoted from research intern to assistant researcher, in performing a brain dissection operation on a rabbit. Xinhua News Agency reporter Fan Jie photographed.
