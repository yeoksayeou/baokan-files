Title: The Chinese Medical Association Approves Another Group of Traditional Chinese Medicine Practitioners for Membership

Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The Chinese Medical Association held a meeting of its standing council on the evening of the 16th and approved the membership of 702 traditional Chinese medicine practitioners. Together with the traditional Chinese medicine practitioners previously approved for membership, the Chinese Medical Association has now admitted a total of more than 1,030 traditional Chinese medicine practitioners as members.

Those approved for membership this time are all traditional Chinese medicine practitioners with good knowledge and rich experience from major cities such as Beijing and Tianjin.

### 中华医学会又批准一批中医师入会

1956-07-19
第7版()
专栏：

    中华医学会又批准一批中医师入会
    据新华社17日讯　中华医学会十六日晚举行常务理事会，批准七百零二名中医师入会。连过去批准入会的中医师一起，中华医学会共已吸收了一千零三十多名中医师为会员。
    这次批准入会的都是京、津等各大城市学识好、经验多的中医师。
