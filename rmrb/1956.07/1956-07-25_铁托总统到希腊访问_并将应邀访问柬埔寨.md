Title: President Tito Visits Greece and Will Visit Cambodia at Invitation

Author:

Date: 1956-07-25
Page: 5

    President Tito Visits Greece
    And Will Visit Cambodia at Invitation
    Xinhua News Agency, Corfu, 24th - Reuters reports: Yugoslav President Tito arrived in Corfu, Greece today, beginning a five-day visit to Greece.
    Tito will hold talks with Greek national leaders.
    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 24th - TASS reports from Belgrade on the 23rd: Yugoslav President Tito and his wife left Brioni Island on the training ship "Galeb" on the 22nd for Corfu, Greece, at the invitation of the King and Queen of Greece.
    Xinhua News Agency, 24th - According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency: It was announced in Belgrade on the evening of the 23rd that Yugoslav President Tito had accepted the invitation of the King of Cambodia to visit Cambodia.
    The invitation was extended by Cambodian Prince Sihanouk during his visit to Yugoslavia.

### 铁托总统到希腊访问  并将应邀访问柬埔寨

1956-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    铁托总统到希腊访问
    并将应邀访问柬埔寨
    新华社24日讯　路透社科孚岛消息：南斯拉夫总统铁托今天到达希腊的科孚岛，开始对希腊进行为时五天的访问。
    铁托将同希腊国家领导人进行会谈。
    新华社24日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德二十三日讯：南斯拉夫总统铁托和他的夫人应希腊国王和王后的邀请，在二十二日乘教练舰“加勒布号”离布里俄尼岛前往希腊的科孚岛。
    新华社24日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道：贝尔格莱德二十三日晚间宣布，南斯拉夫总统铁托已经接受柬埔寨国王的邀请将到柬埔寨访问。
    这个邀请是柬埔寨西哈努克亲王在访问南斯拉夫期间提出的。
