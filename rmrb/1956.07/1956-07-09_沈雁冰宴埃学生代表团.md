Title: Shen Yanbing Hosts Banquet for Egyptian Student Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, July 8th—Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the People’s Republic of China, hosted a banquet at noon on the 8th to entertain the Egyptian student delegation.

    Far, representative of the Egyptian Commercial Representative Office in China, and Meng Dawei, deputy representative, were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 沈雁冰宴埃学生代表团

1956-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰宴埃学生代表团
    据新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国文化部部长沈雁冰八日午间举行宴会招待埃及学生代表团。
    埃及驻中国商务代表处代表法尔和副代表孟大维也应邀出席了宴会。
