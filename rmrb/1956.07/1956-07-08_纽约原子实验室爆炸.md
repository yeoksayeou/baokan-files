Title: New York Atomic Laboratory Explosion
Author:
Date: 1956-07-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: According to an Associated Press dispatch from New York: An explosion occurred in an atomic laboratory in New York on July 2nd. The second floor of the laboratory building was destroyed, and approximately twenty-five people were injured. Because the research being conducted in the laboratory was of an extremely secret nature, all reporters except for one photographer were barred from entering the explosion site.

### 纽约原子实验室爆炸

1956-07-08
第6版()
专栏：

    纽约原子实验室爆炸
    新华社7日讯　据美联社纽约消息：七月二日在纽约的原子实验室中发生爆炸事件。实验室大厦的第二层被炸毁，大约有二十五个人受伤。由于在实验室中进行的研究具有极端秘密的性质，除了一个摄影记者以外，所有记者都不能进入爆炸地点。
