Title: Mongolian Agricultural and Pastoral Cooperatives Increasingly Developing and Consolidating
Author:
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 3rd: According to TASS, Mongolian agricultural and pastoral cooperatives are rapidly developing and consolidating. At the beginning of April, there were a total of 439 agricultural and pastoral cooperatives in the republic, twice the number in 1954; by June 20th, there were already 509 agricultural and pastoral cooperatives. Currently, more than 31,000 herding households have joined the cooperatives, accounting for 20% of the total number of herding households in the country. There are more than 73,000 cooperative members, approximately five times the number in November 1954. There are a total of 2,741,470 head of livestock.

### 蒙古农牧业合作社日益发展和巩固

1956-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古农牧业合作社日益发展和巩固
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：蒙古农牧业合作社在迅速发展和巩固。四月初，共和国里共有439个农牧业合作社，即为1954年的两倍；到六月二十日的时候，农牧业合作社已经有五百零九个。现在，加入合作社的有三万一千多户牧民，占全国牧户总数的20％。合作社社员有七万三千多人，约为1954年11月的五倍。牲口共有二百七十四万一千四百七十头。
