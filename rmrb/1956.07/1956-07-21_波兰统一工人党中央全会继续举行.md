Title: Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee Plenum Continues
Author:
Date: 1956-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 20th – According to the Polish Press Agency, the Seventh Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party continued on July 19th.

	At the meeting that day, Cyrankiewicz delivered a report on the guiding principles of the 1956-1960 Five-Year Plan.

	The plenary session also conducted further discussions on the reports of Ochab and Cyrankiewicz.

	The plenary session designated two committees to consider revisions to the draft resolution on the "Political and Economic Situation and the Party's Main Tasks" and the draft on the guiding principles of the Five-Year Plan.

### 波兰统一工人党中央全会继续举行

1956-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党中央全会继续举行
    新华社华沙20日电　据波兰通讯社报道：波兰统一工人党中央委员会第七次全体会议在七月十九日继续举行。
    在这一天的会议上，西伦凯维兹做了关于1956—1960年的五年计划的指导原则的报告。
    全体会议同时对奥哈布和西伦凯维兹的报告作进一步的讨论。
    全体会议指定了两个委员会来考虑修改“政治和经济局势和党的主要任务”这个决议草案和关于五年计划指导原则草案的问题。
