Title: First Friendly Match between Chinese and Mongolian Volleyball Teams
Author:
Date: 1956-07-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: The Mongolian People's Republic Cooperative Sports Association men's volleyball team played its first friendly match in China tonight in Beijing, losing to the Chinese Coal Mine Sports Association men's volleyball team by a score of 0-3. The match was held in the competition hall of the Beijing Gymnasium, with over 4,000 spectators.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Deng Xiaoping and He Long, Minister of the Coal Industry Chen Yu, as well as the Mongolian People's Republic Ambassador to China, Ochirbat, and his wife, watched tonight's match. After the game, Zhou Enlai, He Long, and Ochirbat also went onto the court to shake hands with the volleyball players from both Mongolia and China, and took photos with them.

In the first and second games tonight, the Mongolian team lost 2-15 and 4-15 respectively. The Mongolian team launched a counterattack in the third game, which lasted for half an hour of fierce battle before a winner was decided. They once led 4-0 and 9-4 in this game. After the Coal Mine team worked hard to counterattack and tied the score at 9-9, the game became even more intense. The scoreboard repeatedly showed 11-11, 12-12, and 14-14. The Coal Mine team scored two consecutive points in the decisive final minutes, eventually winning 16-14. In tonight's match, the Mongolian team captain Avirmed and player Dorjguugda's spikes were outstanding. Players Tugor and Moyu also demonstrated good setting and saving skills in organizing the offense. All four of them are national team members of the Mongolian People's Republic. The Coal Mine team, which won the men's volleyball championship at the first National Workers' Games last year, scored the most points with their fierce spikes today.

A simple but grand welcome ceremony was held before the start of tonight's match. Huang Qixiang, Vice Chairman of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Gonza, the leader of the Mongolian People's Republic Cooperative Sports Association men's volleyball team, delivered speeches respectively.

### 中蒙排球队首次友谊赛

1956-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中蒙排球队首次友谊赛
    新华社7日讯　蒙古人民共和国合作社体育协会男子排球队今天晚上在北京举行来中国访问的第一场友谊比赛时，以零比三败给中国煤矿体育协会男子排球队。比赛在北京体育馆的比赛馆中举行，观众有四千多人。
    周恩来总理，邓小平、贺龙副总理，煤炭工业部部长陈郁，以及蒙古人民共和国驻华大使奥其尔巴特和他的夫人都观看了今晚的球赛。周恩来、贺龙和奥其尔巴特在球赛结束以后，还到球场上同蒙、中两国的排球运动员们一一握手，并且同他们在一起照了像。
    蒙古队在今晚的第一局和第二局比赛中，先后以二比十五和四比十五失败。蒙古队在经过半小时激战才分胜负的第三局比赛中，展开了反攻。他们在这一局曾经以四比零和九比四领先。煤矿队努力反攻追到九平以后，比赛显得更激烈了。记分牌上连续出现了十一平、十二平和十四平的字样。煤矿队在有决定意义的最后几分钟连得了两分，最后以十六比十四获胜。在今晚的比赛中，蒙古队的队长阿维尔米特和队员道尔吉居格达尔的扣球很出色。队员图戈尔和莫尤在组织进攻方面，也表现了良好的托球和救球技术。他们四个人都是蒙古人民共和国的国家队员。煤矿队去年曾经获得全国第一届工人体育运动大会的男子排球冠军，他们今天以猛烈的扣球得分最多。
    在今晚的比赛开始以前举行了简单而隆重的欢迎仪式。中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任黄琪翔和蒙古人民共和国合作社体育协会男子排球队领队冈扎先后讲了话。
