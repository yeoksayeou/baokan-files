Title: French Military Leaders Confer, Study New Strategy in Algeria
Author:
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 6

    French Military Leaders Confer
    Study New Strategy in Algeria
    Xinhua News Agency, July 19th: According to a United Press report, French military leaders gathered in Algiers on July 18th to discuss adopting a new strategy in the military suppression operations in Algeria.

### 法国军事首领会商  研究在阿尔及利亚的新战略

1956-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

    法国军事首领会商
    研究在阿尔及利亚的新战略
    据新华社19日讯　据合众社消息，法国军事领袖七月十八日聚集在阿尔及尔，讨论在阿尔及利亚的军事镇压行动中采取新战略的问题。
