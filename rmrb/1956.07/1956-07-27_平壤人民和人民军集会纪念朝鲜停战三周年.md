Title: Pyongyang Residents and People's Army Hold Meetings to Commemorate the Third Anniversary of the Korean Armistice

Author:

Date: 1956-07-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 26th: On the eve of the third anniversary of the Korean Armistice on July 27th, workers, citizens of Pyongyang, and troops of the Korean People's Army guarding the peace frontline have held lectures and seminars to commemorate the third anniversary of the armistice. They all expressed their determination to consolidate the achievements of the Korean Armistice, transform it into a solid peace, realize the peaceful reunification of the fatherland, and build socialism in northern Korea. Soldiers of the People's Army stated that they should be highly vigilant against the Lee Seung-man group, which is expanding its military strength with the support of the United States.

Citizens of the Central District and Southern District of Pyongyang also went to the cemeteries of the martyrs of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers near the suburbs of the city to sweep the tombs.

### 平壤人民和人民军集会纪念朝鲜停战三周年

1956-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    平壤人民和人民军集会纪念朝鲜停战三周年
    新华社平壤26日电　在七月二十七日朝鲜停战三周年前夕，平壤市的工人、市民和守卫在和平防线上的朝鲜人民军部队纷纷举行讲演会和座谈会等来纪念停战三周年。他们都表示要为巩固朝鲜停战的成果并且把它转变为巩固的和平，实现祖国的和平统一和在朝鲜北部建设社会主义而斗争。人民军的将士们表示，对于正在美国支持下扩充军备的李承晚集团要提高警惕。
    平壤市中区和南区的市民还到市郊附近的朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的烈士墓地去扫墓。
