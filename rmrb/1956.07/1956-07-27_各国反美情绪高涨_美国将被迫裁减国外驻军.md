Title: Anti-American Sentiment Rising in Various Countries; United States Will Be Forced to Reduce Overseas Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-07-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 26th dispatch from Washington: International News Service reported that the United States' policy of reducing troops stationed in the Far East and Europe has been confirmed, although the timing of the reduction has not yet been decided.

The report said that the U.S. Department of Defense supports reducing troops stationed abroad, one of the reasons being "the opposition to U.S. troops in the countries where they are stationed."

### 各国反美情绪高涨  美国将被迫裁减国外驻军

1956-07-27
第6版()
专栏：

    各国反美情绪高涨
    美国将被迫裁减国外驻军
    新华社26日讯　国际新闻社华盛顿消息报道说：美国裁减驻远东和欧洲军队的政策已经是肯定了，虽然关于什么时候裁减的问题还没有决定。
    消息说，美国国防部赞同裁减驻国外军队，原因之一是“因为在美国所驻扎的国家中对美军的反对情绪”。
