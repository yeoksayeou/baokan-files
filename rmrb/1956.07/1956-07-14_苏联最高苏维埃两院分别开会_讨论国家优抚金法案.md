Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Holds Separate Meetings of Two Houses to Discuss National Welfare Fund Bill
Author:
Date: 1956-07-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Soviet Supreme Soviet's Council of the Union and Council of Nationalities held separate meetings on the 12th in the Grand Kremlin Palace to discuss the report on the National Welfare Fund Bill presented by Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Bulganin.

Prior to the discussion, the two houses each heard supplementary reports on the Welfare Fund Bill from their respective legislative committees.

Following the supplementary reports, the two houses then separately began discussing the Soviet National Welfare Fund Bill. A total of twenty-two representatives spoke. The speakers suggested passing the Soviet National Welfare Fund Bill, believing that this bill fully reflects the Communist Party and the Soviet government's constant and deep concern for the people's material welfare. In addition, they also offered new supplements and suggestions for this bill. (Attached picture)

### 苏联最高苏维埃两院分别开会  讨论国家优抚金法案

1956-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃两院分别开会
    讨论国家优抚金法案
    据新华社讯　苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院十二日在大克里姆林宫分别举行会议，讨论苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁所作的关于国家优抚金法案的报告。
    在讨论以前，两院会议分别听取了两院的法案委员会所作的关于优抚金法案的补充报告。
    在作了补充报告以后，两院会议随即分别展开对苏联国家优抚金法案的讨论。共有二十二个代表发言。发言的人建议通过苏联国家优抚金法案，认为这个法案充分反映了共产党和苏联政府对人民物质福利的经常的深切关怀。此外，他们对这个法案还提出了新的补充和建议。（附图片）
