Title: Yunnan Opens Minority Nationality Medical Cadre Class
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, July 10th: The minority nationality medical cadre class, which opened in early July, has officially begun classes in Kunming. This minority nationality medical cadre class has two hundred students, mostly incumbent cadres and promising young adults from rural areas of more than ten minority nationalities from places such as the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture, the Dehong Dai-Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture, the Honghe Hani Autonomous Region, and the Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Region in Yunnan.

### 云南开办少数民族医士班

1956-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

    云南开办少数民族医士班
    据新华社昆明10日电　七月初开办的少数民族医士班，已经在昆明正式开学上课。这个少数民族医士班有二百个学员，绝大部分是来自云南西双版纳傣族自治州、德宏傣族景颇族自治州、红河哈尼族自治区、怒江傈僳族自治区等地的十多种少数民族的在职干部和有培养前途的农村青壮年。
