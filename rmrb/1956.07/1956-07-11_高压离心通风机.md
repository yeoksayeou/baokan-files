Title: High-Pressure Centrifugal Fan
Author:
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 2

    New Products

    The Shenyang Fan Factory has manufactured a type of high-pressure centrifugal fan. This machine can be used for blowing air in large blast furnaces and for transporting gas in gas plants; it can also be used for sending gas to smelting and chemical plants. It has a large flow rate and high efficiency. Ordinary fans usually have one air inlet, but this fan has air inlets on both the left and right sides, and can send out 45,000 cubic meters of air or gas per hour, which is twice the flow rate of similar fans. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 高压离心通风机

1956-07-11
第2版()
专栏：新产品

    高压离心通风机
    沈阳扇风机厂制成了一种高压离心通风机。这种机器可以在大型化铁炉上鼓风用，为燃气厂输送瓦斯；也可以为冶炼、化工厂送气。它的流量大、效率高，过去一般通风机都是一面进风，它是从左右两面进风，每小时能送出空气或瓦斯四万五千立方公尺，较同类型通风机的流量大一倍。　　（新华社）
