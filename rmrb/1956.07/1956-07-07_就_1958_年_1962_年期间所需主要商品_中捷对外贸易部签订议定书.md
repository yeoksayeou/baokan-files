Title: China and Czechoslovakia Sign Protocol on Major Commodities Needed During 1958-1962
Author:
Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China and the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Czechoslovak Republic conducted negotiations on several major commodities needed by the two countries from 1958 to 1962, and reached a satisfactory agreement. A protocol between the two foreign trade ministries was signed on July 4th. Kong Yuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, signed on behalf of China; Otto Kožol, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, signed on behalf of Czechoslovakia.
    Among those who attended the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Luo Gengmo, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, and Cao Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia; on the Czechoslovak side were Vaseka, Chargé d'affaires ad interim in China, and Engineer Šmok, Deputy Minister of Heavy Machinery Industry.

### 就1958年—1962年期间所需主要商品  中捷对外贸易部签订议定书

1956-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    就1958年—1962年期间所需主要商品
    中捷对外贸易部签订议定书
    新华社5日讯　中华人民共和国对外贸易部和捷克斯洛伐克共和国对外贸易部就有关两国自1958年到1962年期间所需要的若干主要商品进行了谈判，并且圆满地达成了协议，在七月四日签订了两国对外贸易部之间的议定书。代表中国方面签字的是对外贸易部副部长孔原；代表捷克斯洛伐克方面签字的是对外贸易部第一副部长奥托·科佐尔。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、国家计划委员会副主任骆耕漠、中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛等人；捷克斯洛伐克方面有驻中国的临时代办瓦塞克，重型机器工业部副部长什莫克工程师等人。
