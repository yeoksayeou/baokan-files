Title: Our Athletes Win Another Championship at International Water Sports Event
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 21st — In the pole climbing competition at the Second International Water Sports Games held in Poland, Chinese athletes Yang Xiangxian and Xu Ruqing won first and third place respectively.

　　A total of forty-four athletes from various countries participated in this competition.

　　In the tug-of-war competition, the Chinese team achieved tenth place.

### 在国际水上运动会上我国选手又得一冠军

1956-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    在国际水上运动会上
    我国选手又得一冠军
    新华社华沙21日电　在波兰举行的第二届国际水上运动会攀登系艇杆的比赛中，中国选手杨象贤和徐汝清分别取得了第一名和第三名。
    参加这项比赛的各国选手共有四十四人。
    在拔河比赛中，中国队取得了第十名。
