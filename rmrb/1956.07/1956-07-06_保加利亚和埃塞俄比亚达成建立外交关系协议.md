Title: Bulgaria and Ethiopia Reach Agreement on Establishing Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-07-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 4th: The Bulgarian Telegraph Agency reported on the 3rd that representatives of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the Kingdom of Ethiopia, after holding talks in Cairo, reached an agreement on establishing diplomatic relations and exchanging diplomatic envoys.

### 保加利亚和埃塞俄比亚达成建立外交关系协议

1956-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    保加利亚和埃塞俄比亚达成建立外交关系协议
    新华社布拉格4日电　据保加利亚通讯社三日报道：保加利亚人民共和国代表和埃塞俄比亚王国代表，在开罗举行会谈以后，就建立外交关系和互派外交使节问题达成了协议。
