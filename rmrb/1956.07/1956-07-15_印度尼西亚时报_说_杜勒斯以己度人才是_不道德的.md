Title: "Indonesia Times" Says Dulles' Projection is "Immoral"
Author:
Date: 1956-07-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 13th: The influential English-language daily "Indonesia Times" published an editorial today criticizing Dulles' remarks about Soviet aid as "rude."

The editorial said: "We really feel sorry for the American leaders... Mr. Dulles and Mr. Nixon could not have expected Indonesia, India, and other neutral countries to allow themselves to be intimidated... and that is precisely the real intention of these remarks... This impolite, or rather rude, talk will in turn damage the position and prestige of the United States in these countries."

The editorial concluded, "To judge another person based on one's own fears and suspicions is certainly—to use Dulles' own smug words—immoral."

### “印度尼西亚时报”说  杜勒斯以己度人才是“不道德的”

1956-07-15
第6版()
专栏：

    “印度尼西亚时报”说
    杜勒斯以己度人才是“不道德的”
    据新华社雅加达13日电　有影响的英文日报“印度尼西亚时报”今天发表社论抨击杜勒斯关于苏联援助的谈话是“无礼的”。
    社论说：“我们的确替美国领袖们难过……杜勒斯先生和尼克松先生原不可能指望印度尼西亚、印度和其他中立国家会让自己受到恫吓……而这正是这种谈话的实际用意所在……这种不客气的或者不如说是无礼的谈话反过来一定会败坏美国在这些国家中的地位和声望”。
    社论最后说，“根据自己的恐惧和怀疑来判断另一个人，肯定是——用杜勒斯自己的得意的字眼来说——不道德的。”
