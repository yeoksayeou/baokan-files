Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Other Organizations Congratulate the Japan-China Friendship Association National Convention
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Report: The Japan-China Friendship Association will hold its sixth national convention from July 21st to 23rd at the Chiyoda Public Hall in Tokyo, Japan. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations and the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, sent congratulatory telegrams on the 19th. Their telegrams expressed warm congratulations to the convention and expressed the hope for further development of friendly relations and cultural exchanges between the people of China and Japan.

### 我国对外文协等  祝贺日中友协全国大会

1956-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    我国对外文协等
    祝贺日中友协全国大会
    据新华社讯　日本中国友好协会七月二十一日到二十三日将在日本东京都千代田区公会堂召开第六届全国大会。中国人民对外文化协会和中国人民外交学会会长张奚若分别于十九日致电祝贺。他们的电报向大会表示热烈祝贺，并表示希望中日两国人民的友好关系和文化交流的进一步的发展。
