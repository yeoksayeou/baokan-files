Title: The Soviet Union and Nepal Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 20th: It was announced here today that the governments of the Soviet Union and Nepal have decided to establish diplomatic relations between the countries and to exchange diplomatic representatives at the ambassadorial level.

The two governments expressed their hope that the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and Nepal would help to further expand the friendly cooperation between the two countries and consolidate universal peace.

### 苏联和尼泊尔决定建交

1956-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和尼泊尔决定建交
    新华社莫斯科20日电　这里今天宣布，苏联和尼泊尔两国政府决定建立两国之间的外交关系，互派大使一级的外交代表。
    两国政府表示，希望苏联和尼泊尔之间建立外交关系将有助于进一步扩大两国的友好合作和巩固普遍和平。
