Title: North Korea Captures a Large Turtle
Author:
Date: 1956-07-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 29th. A certain aquatic product enterprise in North Korea recently captured a large sea turtle weighing 130 kilograms on the east coast. The turtle is 1.2 meters long and 80 centimeters wide. According to North Korean experts, this turtle is more than 800 years old and was born during the Goryeo Dynasty era in Korea.
    This large turtle is now being kept by the South Hamgyong Province Pharmaceutical Administration Office.

### 朝鲜捕获一个大龟

1956-07-30
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜捕获一个大龟
    新华社平壤29日电　朝鲜某水产事业所最近在东海岸捕获了一个重一百三十公斤的大海龟。这个海龟长一公尺二十公分，宽八十公分。据朝鲜专家推算，这个龟已有八百多岁，它生于朝鲜的高丽王朝时代。
    这个大龟现在由咸镜南道医药品管理所保管。
