Title: Eden Receives Matskevich
Author:
Date: 1956-07-12
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, London, July 11th: TASS reports: British Prime Minister Eden received Matskevich, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Agriculture, who is currently visiting Britain, on July 10th.
    In the evening, British Minister of Agriculture and Fisheries, D. Heathcoat-Amory, held a banquet to welcome the Soviet agricultural delegation led by Matskevich.
    The delegation arrived in Britain on July 2nd to study British agriculture.

### 艾登接见马茨凯维奇

1956-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

    艾登接见马茨凯维奇
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社伦敦消息：英国首相艾登七月十日接见了正在英国访问的苏联部长会议副主席兼农业部长马茨凯维奇。
    晚间，英国农业与渔业大臣德·赫斯考特—埃莫里举行宴会，欢迎马茨凯维奇率领的苏联农业代表团。
    代表团在七月二日到达英国，他们是来考察英国的农业情况的。
