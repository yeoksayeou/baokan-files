Title: Moscow University Establishes Institute of Oriental Languages
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 21st. Moscow University in the Soviet Union has newly established an Institute of Oriental Languages. It will train specialists who master the languages of Oriental countries and possess a comprehensive and profound understanding of the economies, histories, and cultures of these countries.

    The Department of Historical Linguistics of this institute will begin classes on September 1st. The study period for this department is set at six years, including one year of internship abroad. After preliminary study at the institute, individual students will be sent to institutions of higher education in Oriental countries for further study.

    The graduate students, professors, and instructors of the institute can travel to Oriental countries for research, so that they can better master Oriental languages and enrich their knowledge of the literature, history, and economics of these countries.

    Orientalists from China, India, Indonesia, Vietnam, Burma, and other countries will be invited to the Soviet Union to teach at this institute.

### 莫斯科大学  设立东方语言学院

1956-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    莫斯科大学
    设立东方语言学院
    新华社莫斯科21日电　苏联莫斯科大学新设了一个东方语言学院。它将培养掌握东方国家语言、并且全面和深刻地了解这些国家的经济、历史和文化的专门人才。
    这个学院的历史语言学系将在九月一日开课。这个系的学习期限定为六年，其中包括在国外实习一年。个别学生在学院中经过初步学习以后，将派往东方国家的高等学校学习。
    学院的研究生、教授和教员可以到东方国家去作考察，以便使他们更好地掌握东方语言，充实对这些国家的文学、历史和经济各方面的知识。
    中国、印度、印度尼西亚、越南、缅甸和其他国家的东方学家将被邀请来苏联担任这个学院的教学工作。
