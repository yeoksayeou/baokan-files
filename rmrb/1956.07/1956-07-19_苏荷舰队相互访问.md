Title: Soviet-Dutch Fleets' Mutual Visits
Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, based on TASS reports: The Soviet Baltic Red Banner Fleet, led by Rear Admiral Kotov, set sail from Baltiysk in the Kaliningrad region on July 17 for a friendly visit to Rotterdam, Netherlands.

Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, based on TASS reports: A Dutch fleet consisting of two destroyers, led by Rear Admiral Goslinga, set sail for Leningrad on July 16 for a visit.

### 苏荷舰队相互访问

1956-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

    苏荷舰队相互访问
    新华社据塔斯社报道　由科托夫海军少将率领的苏联波罗的海红旗舰队，七月十七日从加里宁格勒州的波罗的斯克启程前往荷兰的鹿特丹作友好访问。
    新华社据塔斯社海牙讯　由两艘驱逐舰组成的荷兰舰队由戈斯林格斯海军少将率领，七月十六日启程前往列宁格勒访问。
