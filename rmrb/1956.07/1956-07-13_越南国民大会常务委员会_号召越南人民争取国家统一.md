Title: The Standing Committee of the Vietnam National Assembly Calls on the Vietnamese People to Strive for National Reunification
Author:
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 12th: The Standing Committee of the Vietnam Democratic Republic's National Assembly has called on the people of Vietnam to strengthen their unity and respond to President Ho Chi Minh's call on July 6th to wage a resolute struggle for the consolidation of peace and the realization of national reunification on the basis of adhering to the Geneva Accords.

The Standing Committee of the National Assembly issued this call in an open letter to the people of the whole country yesterday. The letter stated that the date for the general election stipulated in the Geneva Accords has arrived, but the general election still cannot be realized, and our beloved motherland is still divided into two parts with different systems.

The Standing Committee of the National Assembly pointed out that the Vietnamese people's struggle for reunification has entered a new stage, and the struggle for independence and reunification will be an arduous and long-term revolutionary struggle. In this struggle, the great strength of the people will surely achieve victory and will surely shatter the conspiracy of American imperialism and its accomplices to split Vietnam.

The Standing Committee of the Vietnam National Assembly also denounced the United States for directing the South Vietnamese regime to reject consultations and general elections, and for turning South Vietnam into a US military base.

### 越南国民大会常务委员会  号召越南人民争取国家统一

1956-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

    越南国民大会常务委员会
    号召越南人民争取国家统一
    新华社河内12日电　越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会号召越南全国人民加强团结，响应胡志明主席七月六日的号召，在遵守日内瓦协定的基础上为巩固和平、实现国家的统一而进行坚决的斗争。
    越南国民大会常务委员会在昨天发表的一封给全国人民的公开信里发出了这个号召。信中说，日内瓦协定所规定的普选日期已经到了，但是普选仍旧不能实现，我们可爱的祖国仍然被分裂为不同制度的两方。
    国民大会常务委员会指出，越南人民争取统一的斗争进入了一个新的阶段，争取独立和统一的斗争将是一个艰苦和长期的革命斗争。在这个斗争中，人民的伟大的力量一定将取得胜利，一定将粉碎美帝国主义和它的帮凶的分裂越南的阴谋。
    越南国民大会常务委员会并且斥责美国指使南越政权拒绝协商和普选，把越南南方变为美国的军事基地的阴谋。
