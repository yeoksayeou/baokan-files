Title: Our Air Force and Air Defense Force Shot Down Two Chiang Kai-shek Planes
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, July 21st: At 3:59 PM on July 20th, four Chiang Kai-shek American-made F-84 jet fighters flew from Taiwan to harass the skies over the Huangqi Peninsula area of Fujian Province. Our People's Air Force aircraft immediately took off to intercept them. An aerial battle ensued over the skies between Sandu Bay and Matsu Island. Our Air Force shot down one Chiang Kai-shek plane, and then the aircraft returned safely.

On the afternoon of July 20th, the anti-aircraft artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's Air Defense Force also injured a Chiang Kai-shek plane with fierce artillery fire over the Dongchong area of Fujian Province.

### 我空军和防空军  击伤蒋机两架

1956-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我空军和防空军
    击伤蒋机两架
    据新华社福建前线21日电　二十日下午三时五十九分蒋军美造F—84型喷气式飞机四架从台湾飞扰福建省黄岐半岛地区上空，人民空军机群立刻起飞迎击，在三都澳和马祖岛之间的上空与蒋机展开空战，我空军击伤蒋机一架后，飞机安全返航。
    二十日下午，中国人民解放军防空军高射炮兵部队又在福建省东冲地区上空，以猛烈炮火打伤蒋机一架。
