Title: Vietnam's "People's Daily" Welcomes Results of Soviet-Cambodian Talks; Praises Cambodia for Setting Example of Peaceful Coexistence
Author:
Date: 1956-07-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 9th: The Vietnamese "People's Daily," in an editorial published today, believes that the positive results of the talks between representatives of the Soviet government and Cambodian Prince Sihanouk are an important victory for the developing spirit of peaceful friendship and international cooperation, and a new victory for the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

The "People's Daily" warmly welcomes the statement clearly put forward in the joint communique issued by the Soviet Union and Cambodia regarding the need to respect the value of the Geneva Accords and fully implement their various provisions. The "People's Daily" said that the Vietnamese people believe that this is a sincere and reasonable suggestion that must be valued by all relevant countries.

The "People's Daily" also praised the Cambodian people and government for setting an example in establishing mutual trust and equal cooperation in all aspects between countries with different political and social systems.

### 越南“人民报”欢迎苏柬会谈的结果  赞扬柬埔寨为和平共处做出了榜样

1956-07-10
第5版()
专栏：

    越南“人民报”欢迎苏柬会谈的结果
    赞扬柬埔寨为和平共处做出了榜样
    新华社河内9日电　越南“人民报”在今天发表的社论中，认为苏联政府代表和柬埔寨西哈努克亲王会谈的良好结果，是正在发展着的和平友谊和国际合作精神的一个重要胜利，是和平共处五项原则的一个新的胜利。
    “人民报”热烈欢迎苏联和柬埔寨发表的联合公报中明确提出的关于必须尊重日内瓦协定的价值和充分履行这个协定的各项条款的声明。“人民报”说，越南人民认为这是真诚而合理的建议，必须得到一切有关的国家的重视。
    “人民报”还赞扬柬埔寨人民和政府已经为不同政治和社会制度的各国之间建立相互信任和各方面的平等合作做出了一个榜样。
