Title: At the International Conference on Public Education, the Soviet Union, Egypt and other countries protest against the attendance of Kuomintang elements

Author:
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 10th - According to a Reuters report from Geneva: At the opening meeting of the 19th International Conference on Public Education held in Geneva on July 9, representatives from the Soviet Union and People's Democratic countries protested the attendance of Kuomintang elements at the meeting. The Egyptian representative supported this view.

The head of the Egyptian delegation, Said Youssef, said at the meeting that Egypt has friendly and cultural ties with the People's Republic of China and does not recognize the right of Kuomintang elements to attend this meeting.

The International Conference on Public Education is jointly held by the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization and the International Bureau of Education and will last for eight days.

### 在国际国民教育会议上  苏联和埃及等国抗议国民党分子出席

1956-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

    在国际国民教育会议上
    苏联和埃及等国抗议国民党分子出席
    新华社10日讯　据路透社日内瓦消息：在七月九日在日内瓦举行的第十九届国际国民教育会议开幕会议上，苏联和人民民主国家代表们对国民党分子出席会议提出抗议。埃及代表支持这个意见。
    埃及代表团团长赛义德·优素福在会议上说，埃及和中华人民共和国有着友好和文化的联系，它不承认国民党分子有权出席这个会议。
    国际国民教育会议是由联合国教育、科学及文化组织和国际教育局联合举行的，会期八天。
