Title: Bulgaria Completes Second Quarter Industrial Output Value Plan

Author:

Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, July 27th. TASS reports: The Central Statistical Bureau of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers has published a communique on the results of the implementation of the national economic development plan for the second quarter of 1956.

The communique stated that the second quarter plan was completed by 101% in terms of total industrial output value. Compared with the same period last year, coal production increased by 6%, oil increased by 109%, electricity increased by 15%, iron ore increased by 114%, steel increased by 55%, steel products increased by 32%, lead increased by 13%, grain harvesting combines increased by 78%, and silk fabrics increased by 41%.

In agriculture, the amount of field work completed by machine tractor stations from the beginning of the year to July 1st increased by 26% compared with the same period last year. In the second quarter, agriculture received 1,412 tractors (each calculated at 15 horsepower), 447 harvesting combines (197 of which were domestically produced) and many other machines.

The turnover of the retail trade network increased by 16% compared with the second quarter of last year.

### 保加利亚完成第二季工业总产值计划

1956-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    保加利亚完成第二季工业总产值计划
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社索非亚二十七日讯：保加利亚部长会议中央统计局发表了1956年第二季度发展国民经济国家计划执行结果的公报。
    公报说，第二季度计划在工业总产值方面完成了101％。同去年同期相比，煤产量增加了6％，石油增加了109％，电力增加了15％，铁矿砂增加了114％，钢增加了55％，钢材增加了32％，铅增加了13％，谷物收获联合机增加了78％，丝织品增加了41％。
    在农业方面，机器拖拉机站从年初到七月一日所完成的田间工作量比去年同期增加了26％。在第二季度中，农业获得了一千四百一十二台拖拉机（每台以十五匹马力计算），四百四十七部收获联合机（其中有一百九十七部是本国制的）和许多其他机器。
    零售贸易网的贸易额比去年第二季度增加了16％。
