Title: Irish Playwright Robinson and British Writer Milne Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-15
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, July 14th. Irish playwright Robinson and British writer Milne, invited by the Chinese Dramatists Association and the Chinese Writers Association to attend the centenary celebration of the world cultural celebrity and Irish writer George Bernard Shaw, arrived in Beijing on the 13th.
    The joint memorial meeting for Norwegian playwright Ibsen and Irish writer George Bernard Shaw, originally scheduled for July 28th, will be held on July 27th instead.

### 爱尔兰剧作家罗宾逊和英国作家米内到京

1956-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

    爱尔兰剧作家罗宾逊和英国作家米内到京
    新华社14日讯　应中国戏剧家协会和中国作家协会邀请，前来参加世界文化名人，爱尔兰作家肖伯纳诞生一百周年纪念会的爱尔兰剧作家罗宾逊和英国作家米内，已在十三日到达北京。
    原定于七月二十八日举行的挪威剧作家易卜生和爱尔兰作家肖伯纳联合纪念会，将改在七月二十七日举行。
