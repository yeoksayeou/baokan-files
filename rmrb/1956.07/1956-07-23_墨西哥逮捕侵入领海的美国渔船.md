Title: Mexico Arrests American Fishing Boats that Invaded Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1956-07-23
Page: 6

    Mexico Arrests American Fishing Boats that Invaded Territorial Waters
    Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, July 21st: According to a United Press report on the 19th, Mexican coastal defense forces arrested four American fishing boats. These four fishing boats were accused of "illegally fishing in Mexican territorial waters."
    A few days ago, Mexican authorities arrested another seven American shrimp fishing boats for the same reason.

### 墨西哥逮捕侵入领海的美国渔船

1956-07-23
第6版()
专栏：

    墨西哥逮捕侵入
    领海的美国渔船
    据新华社21日讯　墨西哥城消息：据合众社十九日报道，墨西哥的海防部队逮捕了四艘美国渔船。这四艘渔船被指责“非法地在墨西哥领海内捕鱼”。
    几天以前，墨西哥当局由于同样的原因逮捕了另外七艘美国的捕虾渔船。
