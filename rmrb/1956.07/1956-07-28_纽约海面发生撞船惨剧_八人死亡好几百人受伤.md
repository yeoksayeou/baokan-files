Title: Ship Collision Tragedy in New York Waters: Eight Dead, Hundreds Injured
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: Boston dispatch: Two giant ocean liners sailing in the Atlantic collided in darkness and thick fog on the morning of July 26th, about 190 miles east of New York. The 12,600-ton Swedish liner "Stockholm" rammed into the starboard side of the 29,100-ton Italian liner "Andrea Doria," resulting in eight deaths, fifty missing, and hundreds injured.

    Nearby ships immediately rushed to the rescue after hearing the distress signals, rescuing a total of 1,691 people from the "Andrea Doria." According to the United Press, this is the largest number of survivors rescued from a maritime disaster in history.

    The "Andrea Doria" remained afloat for approximately eleven hours after the collision before sinking. The "Stockholm" sustained significant damage, but was able to continue to New York on its own.

### 纽约海面发生撞船惨剧  八人死亡好几百人受伤

1956-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    纽约海面发生撞船惨剧
    八人死亡好几百人受伤
    新华社27日讯　波士顿消息：两只航行大西洋的巨大邮船七月二十六日清晨在纽约以东一百九十英里的海面上在黑暗和浓雾中发生冲撞。排水量一万二千六百吨的瑞典邮船“斯德哥尔摩号”冲到排水量二万九千一百吨的意大利邮船“昂地利亚·多利亚号”的右舷上，结果有八人死亡，五十人失踪，好几百人受伤。
    附近的船只在听见呼救信号后立即前往救援，一共从“昂地利亚·多利亚号”船上救出了一千六百九十一人。据合众社说，这是历史上从海上灾难中得救生还的人数最多的一次。
      “昂地利亚·多利亚号”在冲撞后在水面保持了大约十一个小时，然后沉没。“斯德哥尔摩号”损坏很大，然而还能够独自继续开往纽约。
