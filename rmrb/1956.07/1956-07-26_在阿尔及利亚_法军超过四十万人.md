Title: In Algeria, French Troops Exceed 400,000

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 6

    In Algeria
    French Troops Exceed 400,000
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 23rd (TASS): After returning to Paris from Algeria, French Minister of National Defense Maurice Bourgès-Maunoury and Undersecretary of the Army Le Royer told reporters that the number of French armed forces currently in Algeria exceeds 400,000.

### 在阿尔及利亚  法军超过四十万人

1956-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    在阿尔及利亚
    法军超过四十万人
    新华社讯　塔斯社巴黎二十三日电：法国国防部长莫里斯·布歇—莫努里和陆军次长勒荣纳从阿尔及利亚回到巴黎后向记者发表谈话，他们证实，目前在阿尔及利亚的法国武装部队人数超过四十万人。
