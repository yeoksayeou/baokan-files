Title: Yangtze and Huai River Water Levels Stabilize
Author:
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 1

    Yangtze and Huai River Water Levels Stabilize
    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, July 3rd: The water levels in the upper and middle reaches of the Yangtze River have stabilized. The water level from the upper reaches to Shashi has already dropped. At 2:00 PM today, the Shashi water level had fallen below the warning level. In the middle reaches, from Jianli to Wuxue, the rate of water level increase is small.
    Xinhua News Agency, Bengbu, July 4th: There has been no rain in the Huai River basin for the past two days, and the rapid rise in water levels in Xixian two days ago has greatly weakened. Today, the water level in Xixian has dropped more than four meters compared to July 1st; the water level at Wangjiaba has also dropped.

### 长江淮河水势转稳

1956-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    长江淮河水势转稳
    据新华社武汉3日电　长江上、中游水势转稳。从上游到沙市的水位已经下落。今天下午二时，沙市水位已落到警戒水位以下。中游从监利到武穴，水位涨率较小。
    据新华社蚌埠4日电　这两天，淮河流域没有下雨，两天前息县急涨的水势已经大大减弱。今天息县的水位比七月一日回落了四公尺多；王家坝水位也已回落。
