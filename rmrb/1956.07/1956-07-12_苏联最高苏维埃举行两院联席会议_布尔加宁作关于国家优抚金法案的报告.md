Title: USSR Supreme Soviet Holds Joint Session of the Two Chambers; Bulganin Delivers Report on the State Pension Law Bill
Author:
Date: 1956-07-12
Page: 5

    Moscow Dispatch, July 11th: The Fifth Session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the USSR opened at 10:00 AM today in the Kremlin meeting hall.

    The Council of the Union and the Council of Nationalities held their first meetings at 10:00 AM and 1:00 PM respectively.

    After approving the Credentials Committee's report on the supplementary election of several new deputies, both chambers passed the following identical agenda:

    The State Pension Law Bill;

    The request of the Supreme Soviet of the Karelo-Finnish Republic to transform the Karelo-Finnish Soviet Socialist Republic into the Karelian Soviet Socialist Autonomous Republic and to incorporate it into the Russian Federation;

    Regarding the Supreme Soviet of the USSR's appeal to the parliaments of other countries on the issue of disarmament;

    Regarding the Supreme Soviet of the USSR's statement on the Japanese Diet's appeal for the prohibition of nuclear weapons and the cessation of nuclear weapon testing;

    Approval of the decrees of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

    The Supreme Soviet of the USSR held a joint session of the Council of the Union and the Council of Nationalities at 4:00 PM today. The leaders of the Soviet Party and government attended the meeting. The deputies welcomed with applause the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, and the King and Queen of Iran. Subsequently, the deputies listened with great interest to the report by the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, on the State Pension Law Bill. After pointing out the achievements of Soviet construction and a series of measures recently taken to improve the people's livelihood, Bulganin gave a detailed explanation of the pension law bill proposed by the Council of Ministers of the USSR. The two chambers will hold separate meetings to discuss this draft.

### 苏联最高苏维埃举行两院联席会议  布尔加宁作关于国家优抚金法案的报告

1956-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃举行两院联席会议
    布尔加宁作关于国家优抚金法案的报告
    本报莫斯科11日电　苏联最高苏维埃第四届第五次大会今天上午十时在克里姆林宫会议厅开幕。
    联盟院和民族院分别在上午十时和下午一时举行第一次会议。
    两院在批准了资格审查委员会关于补选几位新代表的报告之后，分别通过下列同样议程：
    国家优抚金法案；
    卡累利阿—芬兰共和国最高苏维埃关于将卡累利阿—芬兰苏维埃社会主义共和国改为卡累利阿苏维埃社会主义自治共和国和使它加入俄罗斯联邦的请求；
    关于苏联最高苏维埃就裁军问题致其他国家议会的呼吁书；
    关于苏联最高苏维埃就日本国会关于禁止核子武器和停止核子武器试验的呼吁书的声明；
    批准苏联最高苏维埃主席团的法令。
    苏联最高苏维埃在今天下午四时举行了联邦院和民族院的联席会议，苏联党和政府的领导人员都出席了会议。代表们鼓掌欢迎，欢迎以金日成首相为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团和伊朗国王和王后。接着代表们以极大的兴趣听取了苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁关于国家优抚金法案的报告。布尔加宁在指出了苏联建设的成就和最近时期采取的改善人民生活的一系列措施以后，对于苏联部长会议所提出的优抚金法案作了详细的说明。两院将分别举行会议，讨论这个草案。
