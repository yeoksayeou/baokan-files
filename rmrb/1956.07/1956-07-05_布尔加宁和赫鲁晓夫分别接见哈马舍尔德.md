Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev separately receive Hammarskjöld
Author:
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, July 4th, Moscow Dispatch: Soviet Minister of the Council Bulganin received United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on the 4th.
    Present at the meeting were: Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov, United Nations Deputy Secretary-General Chernyshev, and others.
    Xinhua News Agency, July 4th, Moscow Dispatch: First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union Khrushchev received United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on July 3rd and held talks with him.
    On the same day in the afternoon, Hammarskjöld visited places of historical interest in the Kremlin. In the evening, he went to the Bolshoi Theatre to watch Prokofiev's opera "War and Peace".

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫分别接见哈马舍尔德

1956-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫分别接见哈马舍尔德
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁在四日接见了联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德。
    接见的时候在座的有：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫、联合国副秘书长契尔尼谢夫等。
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫，在七月三日接见了联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，并且同他进行了谈话。
    在同一天下午，哈马舍尔德参观了克里姆林宫里面的名胜古迹。晚间，他到大剧院观看了普罗科菲耶夫的歌剧“战争与和平”。
