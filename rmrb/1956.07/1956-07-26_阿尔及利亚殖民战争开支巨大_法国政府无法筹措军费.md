Title: Algerian Colonial War Incurs Huge Expenses; French Government Unable to Raise Military Expenditure
Author:
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 25th dispatch: The French National Assembly's Finance Committee, after rejecting the government's proposal to increase taxes to offset the costs of military suppression in Algeria, rejected on July 24th a proposal by the Popular Republican Movement to raise money by issuing public bonds.

The result of the vote on the public bond issuance plan was 16 votes against, 12 votes in favor, and 14 abstentions. Communist committee members opposed the plan, while Socialist and Radical Socialist committee members abstained. The Popular Republican Movement and right-wing parties voted in favor. Poujadist members of the committee also voted against it.

An Agence France-Presse dispatch commented on the matter, saying, "This leaves the government and parliament with no way to pay for the expenses of the Algerian (military operations) in the foreseeable future."

France's military operations in Algeria are costing the French government one billion francs per day. Finance Minister Ramadier said: France must obtain approximately 300 billion francs (over 857 million U.S. dollars) more to cover the costs of the Algerian war.

### 阿尔及利亚殖民战争开支巨大  法国政府无法筹措军费

1956-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚殖民战争开支巨大
    法国政府无法筹措军费
    新华社25日讯　巴黎消息：法国国民议会财政委员会在拒绝了政府提出的增加税收来弥补阿尔及利亚军事镇压费用的方案后，又在七月二十四日拒绝了人民共和党提出的用发行公债来筹募这笔钱的建议。
    发行公债方案的投票结果是十六票反对，十二票赞成，十四票弃权。共产党委员反对这个方案，社会党和激进社会党委员弃权。人民共和党和右翼党派投赞成票。委员会中的布热德分子也投票反对。
    法新社消息评论这件事情说，
  “这使得政府和议会在目前可以预见的将来没有办法来支付阿尔及利亚（军事行动费用）的开支”。
    法国在阿尔及利亚的军事行动使得法国政府每天要消耗十亿法郎。财政部长拉马第说：法国必须再得到约三千亿法郎（八亿五千七百余万美元）来作为阿尔及利亚战争的费用。
