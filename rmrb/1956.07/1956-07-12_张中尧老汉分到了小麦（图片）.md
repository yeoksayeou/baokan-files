Title: Old Man Zhang Zhongyao Receives His Share of Wheat (Picture)
Author: Chen Zhiping
Date: 1956-07-12
Page: 3

    Old Man Zhang Zhongyao Receives His Share of Wheat
    The Qunli Agricultural Production Cooperative in Chengguan Town, Huang County, Shandong Province, is distributing wheat. Zhang Zhongyao (left), a 79-year-old single cooperative member and one of the cooperative’s “Five Guarantees” households, received 237 jin of wheat. The picture shows cooperative members delivering the wheat to his home. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Zhiping took the photo.

### 张中尧老汉分到了小麦（图片）
陈之平
1956-07-12
第3版()
专栏：

    张中尧老汉分到了小麦
    山东黄县城关镇群力农业生产合作社正在进行小麦分配。七十九岁的单身老社员、社里的五保户张中尧（左）分到了二百三十七斤小麦。图为社员们把小麦送到他家里。　新华社记者　陈之平摄
