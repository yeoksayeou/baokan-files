Title: Local Organizations of Democratic Parties in Tianjin Newly Recruit Nearly Seven Hundred Members This Year
Author:
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, July 19th. The local organizations in Tianjin of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Democratic League, the China Democratic National Construction Association, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, and the Jiusan Society have newly recruited more than six hundred and ninety members this year.

The Jiusan Society, which established a preparatory organization in Tianjin last November, has admitted more than seventy people this year, including the famous orthopedic expert Fang Xianzhi, urology expert Shi Xi'en, obstetrics and gynecology expert Ke Yingkui, and Nankai University biology professor Gu Changlian and physics professor Shen Shouchun. The Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang has expanded its contacts with people from all walks of life. It has developed twenty members in the first half of this year, including Zhang Xueming, a member of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. Among the seventy-six members developed by the Tianjin Branch of the China Association for Promoting Democracy in the first half of this year, are thermologist Chen Rongti, Nankai University mathematics professor Liu Jinnian, and some primary and secondary school teachers.

During the upsurge of socialist transformation, a large number of industrialists and businessmen applied to join the China Democratic National Construction Association. In the first quarter of this year, the Tianjin Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association admitted more than five hundred private-sector personnel and private-sector agents. Among them are also more than ten family members of industrialists and businessmen, such as Dai Pianying, the wife of Bi Mingqi, vice chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce. Currently, more than seven hundred people are still applying to join the China Democratic National Construction Association.

### 各民主党派在天津的地方组织  今年新发展成员近七百人

1956-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    各民主党派在天津的地方组织
    今年新发展成员近七百人
    新华社天津19日电　中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主同盟、中国民主建国会、中国民主促进会和九三学社等民主党派在天津的地方组织，今年内新发展的成员已达六百九十多人。
    去年十一月间在天津成立筹备组织的九三学社，在今年已接收了七十多人入社，其中有著名的骨科专家方先之、泌尿外科专家施锡恩、妇产科专家柯应夔和南开大学生物系教授顾昌楝、物理系教授沈寿春。中国国民党革命委员会天津市委员会扩大了对社会人士的联系。今年上半年发展了二十人，其中有政协天津市委员会委员张学铭等。中国民主促进会天津市分会在今年上半年发展的七十六名会员中，有热力学家陈荣悌、南开大学数学系教授刘晋年和一部分中小学教师。
    在社会主义改造高潮中，大批工商业者申请加入了中国民主建国会。在今年第一季度里，中国民主建国会天津市分会就吸收了五百多名实职私方人员和私方代理人入会。其中还有天津市工商业联合会副主任委员毕鸣歧的夫人戴翩英等十多名工商业者家属。现在，申请加入中国民主建国会的还有七百多人。
