Title: Anglo-Egyptian Trade Negotiations Held
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 10th: An Egyptian trade delegation, headed by Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce Nusseir, arrived in London on the evening of the 8th. On the 9th, they began trade negotiations with representatives of the British government.

### 英埃举行贸易谈判

1956-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    英埃举行贸易谈判
    据新华社10日讯　伦敦消息：以埃及工商部长努赛尔为首的埃及贸易代表团八日晚到达伦敦。九日，他们开始同英国政府代表举行贸易谈判。
