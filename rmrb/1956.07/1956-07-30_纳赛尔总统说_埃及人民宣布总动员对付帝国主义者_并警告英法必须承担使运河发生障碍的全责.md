Title: President Nasser Says Egyptian People Announce General Mobilization to Deal with Imperialists, and Warns Britain and France Must Bear Full Responsibility for Obstructing the Canal
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 29th, Cairo News: President Gamal Abdel Nasser said when he returned to Cairo from Alexandria on July 28th, "Today, the Egyptian people have taken up arms and announced a general mobilization to deal with the imperialists. This spirit will enable us to win and will prevent the imperialists from defeating us."

Speaking from the window of the Presidential Palace to the thousands of Cairo citizens gathered in the square, Abdel Nasser said that Egypt is stronger today than anyone can imagine. "No country, no nation, no group can intimidate us."

Amidst the ecstatic cheers of the crowd, he continued: "Today I have personally seen the whole of Egypt, the entire nation of Egypt announcing general mobilization. I have seen the Egyptian people taking up arms to defend their rights, their dignity, and their freedom of life." He said: "We are ready to deal with all eventualities. We will repel force with force, attack with attack, and aggression with aggression." We will move forward bravely to realize our freedom and consolidate our sovereignty, until the last drop of blood is shed.

The Egyptian President pointed out that the West’s “fury” at Egypt’s nationalization of the Suez Canal stemmed from the position of "bloodsucking imperialism that violates the rights of all countries and interferes in the affairs of other nations." He said, "I declare to the world that Britain and France want to turn a domestic affair into a political issue. The result of this approach can only obstruct the navigation of the Suez Canal."

The President said: "We will never interfere with the navigation of the Canal. On the contrary, we will provide all conveniences. I seriously warn the imperialist countries that their interference will obstruct the navigation of the Canal. Therefore, I believe that the full responsibility for any obstruction that may occur in the navigation must be borne by Britain and France."

Referring to Britain's protest against the nationalization of the Suez Canal, Abdel Nasser asked: "What right does Britain have to protest? Does it think that Egypt is part of the British kingdom? We have rejected their protests, and we will reject all future protests."

He compared Egypt's move to nationalize the Canal with Britain's move to nationalize the steel and transportation industries. He said that no one interfered with Britain's actions. Why should they interfere with our move to nationalize an Egyptian company? He pointed out that the nationalization of the Suez Canal is purely an act of sovereignty.

He said: "This canal will be returned to Egypt in twelve years. If we take it over now, what is there to be so furious about?" "This clamor exposes Britain's true intentions: it does not intend to hand over the rights to the Canal when its lease expires in 1968."

Abdel Nasser mentioned that Britain had extorted 44% of the Canal's shares from the Egyptian government. He continued, "We will not treat them in the same way. We will compensate them and forget the past."

Criticizing France's rudeness, the President said: "The French Foreign Minister yesterday (July 27) received our ambassador in his office and used vulgar and almost insulting rude language in his conversation with him. Therefore, let Algeria teach this minister a lesson in courtesy!"

### 纳赛尔总统说  埃及人民宣布总动员对付帝国主义者  并警告英法必须承担使运河发生障碍的全责

1956-07-30
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔总统说
    埃及人民宣布总动员对付帝国主义者
    并警告英法必须承担使运河发生障碍的全责
    新华社29日讯　开罗消息：阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统七月二十八日从亚历山大港回到开罗的时候说，“今天，埃及人民拿起武器，宣布总动员来对付帝国主义者。这种精神将使我们能够获胜，将使帝国主义者不能击败我们。”
    阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔在总统府的窗口向聚集在广场上的成千上万开罗市民说，埃及今天比任何人可以想像的都要强大。“没有任何国家、任何民族、任何集团可以吓倒我们”。
    在群众如醉如狂的欢呼声中，他接着说：“今天我亲眼看见了整个埃及，埃及全国宣布总动员。我看见埃及人民拿着武器站起来保卫他们的权利，他们的尊严和生活自由。”他说：“我们已经准备好应付一切不测事件。我们将以武力击退武力，以攻击击退攻击，以进攻击退进攻”。我们将勇往直前实现我们的自由和巩固我们的主权，直到流尽最后一滴血。
    这位埃及总统指出，西方对埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有这件事“大发雷霆”，是从“侵犯各国的权利的吸血的帝国主义和干涉其他民族的事务”的立场出发的。他说，“我向全世界宣布，英国和法国要把一个内政问题变成一个政治问题。这样做法的结果只能使苏彝士运河的通航发生障碍”。
    这位总统说：“我们决不干涉运河的通航。恰恰相反，我们将提供一切便利。我严重警告帝国主义国家，它们的干涉将会使运河的通航发生障碍。因此，我认为，可能使通航发生任何障碍的全部责任都要由英法两国来承当”。
    阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔在谈到英国反对苏彝士运河国有化的抗议的时候问道：“英国有什么权利提出抗议呢？难道它认为埃及是英国王国的一部分吗？我们已经拒绝了他们的抗议，我们将拒绝将来所有的抗议”。
    他把埃及使运河国有化的举动同英国使钢铁业和交通运输业国有化的举动相比。他说，没有人干涉过英国的行动。为什么他们应该干涉我们把一个埃及公司收归国有的举动呢？他指出，苏彝士运河的国有化纯粹是一个主权的行动。
    他说：“这条运河将在十二年以后还给埃及。如果我们现在接收了它，又有什么可大发雷霆的呢”。“这种叫嚣暴露出英国的真正意图：它到1968年租借权满期的时候也是不打算把运河的权利交出来的”。
    阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔提到英国曾经向埃及政府勒索了运河股票的44％。他接着说，“我们不会以同样的方式对待他们。我们将赔偿他们，把过去的事情忘记。”
    这位总统在抨击法国的无礼态度的时候说：“法国外交部长昨天
  （七月二十七日）在他的办公处接见了我们的大使，并且在同他的谈话中使用了下流的措辞，近乎侮辱的粗鲁的措辞。因此让阿尔及利亚来在礼貌方面给这位部长上一课吧！”
