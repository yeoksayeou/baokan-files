Title: British Auto Workers' Strike Expands; Over 10,000 Ford Workers Join the Struggle
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 26th -- The strike of British Motor Corporation (BMC) workers has spread to the British subsidiary of the American Ford Motor Company. 12,000 workers at this company began a strike on July 25th to protest the company's large-scale layoffs. The company issued notices on July 23rd to dismiss more than 2,000 workers.

    At the British Motor Corporation factory in Birmingham, striking workers continued their activities despite the presence of hundreds of police officers guarding the area. They held a mass rally on the afternoon of July 25th, calling on more workers to join the ranks of the strikers. Union leaders also met to discuss measures to halt production in other industrial sectors of all products related to the British Motor Corporation.

    According to statistics, more than 700 additional British Motor Corporation workers joined the strike on the 25th.

### 英国汽车公司工人罢工扩大  福特公司一万多名工人参加斗争

1956-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    英国汽车公司工人罢工扩大
    福特公司一万多名工人参加斗争
    新华社26日讯　伦敦消息：英国汽车公司工人的罢工已经扩大到美国福特汽车公司在英国的子公司。这个公司有一万二千名工人在七月二十五日开始罢工，以抗议公司大批解雇工人。这个公司在七月二十三日发出了解雇两千多个工人的通知书。
    在伯明翰英国汽车公司的工厂，尽管有成百个警察在周围警戒着，罢工工人还是照样进行各种活动。他们在七月二十五日下午召开了群众大会，号召更多的工人参加到罢工者的队伍里来。工会领袖也开了会，研究采取什么措施来使别的工业部门的工人停止生产一切跟英国汽车公司有关的产品。
    据统计，在二十五日，英国汽车公司的工人中又有七百多人参加了罢工。
