Title: Chen Jiakang Arrives in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 5

    Chen Jiakang Arrives in Cairo
    Xinhua News Agency, 19th – Cairo dispatch: China's first ambassador to the Republic of Egypt, Chen Jiakang, arrived in Cairo, the Egyptian capital, by plane today.
    Representatives from the Egyptian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and other countries stationed in Egypt, personnel from the Chinese Commercial Representative Office, and local overseas Chinese were at the airport to welcome him.

### 陈家康到达开罗

1956-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    陈家康到达开罗
    新华社19日讯　开罗消息：中国驻埃及共和国首任大使陈家康在今天乘飞机到达埃及首都开罗。
    到机场欢迎的有埃及外交部的代表、苏联等国驻埃及外交使节、中国商务代表处人员和当地华侨。
