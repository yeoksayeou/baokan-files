Title: Liao Luyan Hosts Banquet for Albanian Agricultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 18th: Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Albanian agricultural delegation. Albanian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Bino, and his wife also attended the banquet.

    Vice Minister of Agriculture Liu Ruilong delivered a welcoming speech. He expressed his gratitude for the friendship the Albanian people brought to the Chinese people and their concern for China's construction endeavors. He wished Albania and China continuous new developments and achievements in socialist agricultural transformation and agricultural production; and expressed his belief that this visit by the delegation would further facilitate the exchange of experiences in agricultural cooperation and agricultural production between the two countries. Liu Ruilong concluded by proposing a toast to the good health of the delegation during their inspection.

    The head of the delegation, Albanian Deputy Minister of Agriculture Ka. Tashko, and the deputy head of the delegation, Albanian Labor Party Central Agricultural Department Deputy Minister V. Pipa, also spoke successively. They expressed their gratitude for the sincere and warm reception from the Chinese people.

    Also participating in tonight's banquet were Chen Zhengren, Deputy Director of the Rural Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Xiandong, Deputy Minister of Agriculture, and the heads from the Head Office of the Agricultural Bank of China, the All-China Federation of Supply & Marketing Cooperatives, and the Central Handicraft Industry Administration Bureau, as well as representatives from the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and the All-China Women's Federation, totaling more than eighty people.

### 廖鲁言欢宴阿尔巴尼亚农业代表团

1956-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    廖鲁言欢宴阿尔巴尼亚农业代表团
    新华社18日讯　农业部长廖鲁言今晚设宴欢迎阿尔巴尼亚农业代表团。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办比诺及夫人也出席了宴会。
    农业部副部长刘瑞龙致欢迎词。他对代表团带来的阿尔巴尼亚人民对中国人民的友谊和对中国建设事业的关怀，表示感谢。他祝阿、中两国在社会主义农业改造和农业生产中不断获得新的发展和成就；并且相信通过代表团的这次访问，能够进一步交流两国在农业合作化和农业生产方面的经验。刘瑞龙最后提议为代表团在考察期间身体健康干杯。
    代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚农业部副部长卡·塔什考，副团长、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央农业部副部长维·皮帕也先后讲话。他们对中国人民的诚恳热情的接待表示谢意。
    参加今晚宴会的，还有中共中央农村工作部副部长陈正人，农业部副部长杨显东和中国农业银行总行、中华全国供销合作总社、中央手工业管理局的负责人，以及青年团中央、全国民主妇女联合会等单位的代表共八十余人。
