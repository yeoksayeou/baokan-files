Title: Sihanouk Arrives in Paris
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 17th: According to a Reuters Paris dispatch, Prince Sihanouk of Cambodia arrived in Paris by plane on the 16th, after visiting Prague and Moscow.

### 西哈努克到巴黎

1956-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

    西哈努克到巴黎
    新华社17日讯　据路透社巴黎消息：柬埔寨西哈努克亲王在访问了布拉格和莫斯科以后，十六日乘飞机到达巴黎。
