Title: Hungary Recognizes the Kingdom of Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1956-07-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, July 6th: TASS reports: The Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic has recognized the Kingdom of Cambodia and has passed a resolution on establishing diplomatic relations with the Kingdom of Cambodia.

### 匈牙利承认柬埔寨王国

1956-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利承认柬埔寨王国
    新华社7日讯　塔斯社布达佩斯六日讯：匈牙利人民共和国主席团已经承认柬埔寨王国，并且通过了关于同柬埔寨王国建立外交关系的决定。
