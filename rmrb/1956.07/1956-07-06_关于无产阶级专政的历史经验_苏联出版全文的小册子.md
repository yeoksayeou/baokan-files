Title: "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" - Full Text Published in Pamphlet Form in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-07-06
Page: 6

Moscow dispatch from our newspaper, July 5th: "Pravda" reports that "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" (People's Daily Editorial) has recently been published by the Pravda Publishing House in 200,000 copies. The pamphlet contains the full text of the article, and the translation has been accurately proofread.
