Title: Indonesian Speaker of Parliament to Visit Our Country at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 21st: Indonesian Speaker of Parliament Sartono, his wife, and several members of parliament will depart from Jakarta for a visit to China on July 29th.

Sartono is visiting China at the invitation of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China.

### 印度尼西亚国会议长应邀将来我国访问

1956-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会议长应邀将来我国访问
    新华社雅加达21日电　印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺和他的夫人以及国会议员数人将在七月二十九日自雅加达动身到中国访问。
    沙多诺是应中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇的邀请而去中国访问的。
