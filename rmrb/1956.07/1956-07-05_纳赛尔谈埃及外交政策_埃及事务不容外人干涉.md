Title: Nasser on Egypt's Foreign Policy: No Foreign Interference in Egyptian Affairs
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 4th: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency yesterday, Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser said in an interview with Czechoslovak journalists that the aim of Egypt's foreign policy is to ensure Egypt's independent international status.

He said, "We hope to cooperate with everyone who extends a hand to us, but we will not allow anyone to interfere in our affairs."

President Abdel Nasser said, "We pursue a policy of positive neutrality, always stand on the side of national freedom, and always defend the principle of being able to make free decisions. We also believe that world peace is the path to a better life, to the happiness and progress of all countries. The main direction of our foreign policy has been clearly expressed at the Bandung Conference; it is based on the principles of the United Nations Charter and the principles adopted by Asian and African countries at the Bandung Conference."

### 纳赛尔谈埃及外交政策  埃及事务不容外人干涉

1956-07-05
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔谈埃及外交政策
    埃及事务不容外人干涉
    新华社布拉格4日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社昨天报道，埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔在对捷克斯洛伐克记者谈话时说，埃及外交政策的目的是要确保埃及的独立国际地位。
    他说，我们希望同一切向我们伸出手来的人合作，但是我们将不允许任何人来干涉我们的事务。
    阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统说，我们执行积极的中立政策，始终站在民族自由这一方面，并且始终捍卫能够自由作出决定的原则。我们还相信世界和平是引向较好的生活、引向一切国家都幸福和进步的道路。我们外交政策的主要方向已经在万隆会议上清楚地表示过；它是以联合国宪章的原则和亚非国家在万隆会议上所通过的原则为基础的。
