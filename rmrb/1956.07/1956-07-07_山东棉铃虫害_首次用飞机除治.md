Title: Shandong Cotton Bollworm Infestation First Time Using Aircraft for Control

Author:
Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Liaocheng, 6th. On the afternoon of the 4th, a Po-2 aircraft from the Civil Aviation Administration flew over the Chengguan District of Xiajin County, Shandong Province, spraying DDT powder on 482 mu of cotton fields. This is the first time Shandong Province has used aircraft to control bollworm pests in cotton.

The aircraft will continue to control bollworm and late-stage pests in cotton in Chengguan, Rendi, Xiangzhaozhuang and other districts of Xiajin County. The area treated will reach 42,000 mu.

### 山东棉铃虫害  首次用飞机除治

1956-07-07
第3版()
专栏：

    山东棉铃虫害
    首次用飞机除治
    据新华社聊城6日电　四日下午，一架民用航空局的波二型飞机，飞翔在山东省夏津县城关区的上空，在四百八十二亩棉田里喷撒了滴滴涕药粉。这是山东省第一次使用飞机除治棉花的铃期虫害。
    这架飞机将继续在夏津县城关、任堤、香赵庄等区除治棉花的铃期和后期虫害，治虫面积将达到四万二千亩。
