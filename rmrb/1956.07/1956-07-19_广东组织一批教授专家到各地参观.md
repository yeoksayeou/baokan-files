Title: Guangdong Organizes a Group of Professors and Experts to Visit Various Places
Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 17th: A summer tour group composed of more than forty professors and experts from universities and colleges departed from Guangzhou today on a special train of the Yuehan Railway. They will visit Wuhan, Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang, Changchun, Anshan, Lushun-Dalian, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places for a five-week tour and visit.
    The head of the tour group is Professor Chen Xujing of Zhongshan University. Nurses and accompanying staff are along the way to take care of them.

### 广东组织一批教授专家到各地参观

1956-07-19
第7版()
专栏：

    广东组织一批教授专家到各地参观
    新华社广州17日电　一个由四十多位高等学校的教授、专家组成的暑期参观团，今天从广州乘粤汉铁路专车北上。他们将先后到武汉、北京、天津、沈阳、长春、鞍山、旅大、上海、杭州等地作为时五个星期的游览和参观。
    参观团的团长由中山大学教授陈序经担任，沿途有护士和随行人员照顾他们。
