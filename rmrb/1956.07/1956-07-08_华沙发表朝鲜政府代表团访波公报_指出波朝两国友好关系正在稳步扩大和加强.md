Title: Warsaw Publishes Communiqué on Visit of North Korean Government Delegation to Poland, Stating Friendly Relations Between Poland and North Korea are Steadily Expanding and Strengthening
Author:
Date: 1956-07-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 7th. Warsaw announced the final communiqué on the visit of the North Korean government delegation to Poland on July 6th.

The communiqué stated that the North Korean government delegation visited Poland from July 2nd to 6th at the invitation of the Polish government. During this time, the delegation visited some industrial enterprises and cultural institutions in Warsaw and learned about the achievements of the Polish people in the economic and cultural fields.

The delegation exchanged views with the Polish government delegation on some issues in a friendly manner. The talks were conducted in an atmosphere of sincerity, mutual respect for sovereignty, and mutual understanding. The talks discussed issues related to further strengthening the mutual relations between the two countries and certain aspects of the international situation related to both Poland and North Korea.

Both delegations expressed satisfaction in affirming that the friendly relations between Poland and North Korea are steadily expanding and strengthening, which is beneficial to the interests of the people of the two countries who are building socialism, as well as to the interests of consolidating peace in Asia and the world.

### 华沙发表朝鲜政府代表团访波公报  指出波朝两国友好关系正在稳步扩大和加强

1956-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    华沙发表朝鲜政府代表团访波公报
    指出波朝两国友好关系正在稳步扩大和加强
    新华社华沙7日电　华沙七月六日发表了朝鲜政府代表团访问波兰的最后公报。
    公报说，朝鲜政府代表团应波兰政府的邀请从七月二日到六日在波兰进行了访问。在这期间，代表团参观了华沙的一些工业企业和文化机关，了解了波兰人民在经济和文化方面的成就。
    代表团同波兰政府代表团就一些问题友好地交换了意见。会谈是在诚挚、相互尊重主权和相互谅解的气氛中进行的。在会谈中讨论了进一步加强两国之间的相互关系问题以及对波兰和朝鲜两国有关的国际局势的某些方面。
    两国代表团满意地肯定，波兰和朝鲜两国的友好关系正在稳步地扩大和加强，这对于正在建设社会主义的两国人民的利益，以及巩固亚洲和全世界和平的利益都是有利的。
