Title: Austrian Socialist Workers' Party Merges with Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1956-07-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 17th: Vienna dispatch: Erwin Schärf, chairman of the Austrian Socialist Workers' Party (left-wing Socialist Party), announced on July 15th that they are merging with the Austrian Communist Party.

The Austrian Socialist Workers' Party was founded in 1950, and it split from the Socialist Party. This party has jointly campaigned with the Communist Party in the parliamentary elections of 1949 and this year.

### 奥社会工人党同共产党合并

1956-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

    奥社会工人党同共产党合并
    新华社柏林17日电　维也纳消息：奥地利社会工人党（左翼社会党）主席埃尔文·谢尔夫在七月十五日宣布，他们正在同奥地利共产党实行合并。
    奥地利社会工人党成立在1950年，它是从社会党中分裂出来的。这个党在1949年和今年的议会选举中都曾和共产党联合进行竞选斗争。
