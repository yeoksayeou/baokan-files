Title: Indian Political Circles Say "Mossadegh Incident" Must Not Be Repeated / Prominent Pakistani Figures Demand Government Support for Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-07-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 29th: Indian news agency PTI reported that political figures in the Indian capital believe that Egypt's decision to nationalize the Suez Canal was a direct result of the withdrawal of British and American aid for the construction of the Aswan Dam. However, some people expressed concern about the possible political consequences.

    Socialist Party leader Lohia, speaking at a Socialist Party meeting in Hyderabad, said that while some might worry that Britain and other relevant countries might send warships to the region, the greater concern should be the economic, diplomatic, and political conspiracies that might follow. Europeans and Americans might try to take the same actions against Egyptian President Abdel Nasser that they took against former Iranian Prime Minister Mossadegh (Mossadegh was overthrown by a military coup for nationalizing the Iranian oil company).

    This Socialist Party member hoped that people around the world, including Europeans and Americans who are fighting for a united, peaceful, and equal world, would issue a strong warning: We will not allow a "Mossadegh Incident" to happen again.

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, July 29th: Pakistan United Press reported that many people in Pakistan welcomed Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal and demanded that the Pakistani government support this action by Egypt.

    Khawaja Muzaffar-ul-Haq, a member of the West Pakistan Legislative Assembly, said in Karachi that this was a "bold and encouraging step" taken by Egypt to use its own resources to complete its national plan. He demanded that the Pakistani government "openly endorse and guarantee full support" to Egypt on this issue.

    Azizullah, General Secretary of the Karachi People's Alliance, said that the Pakistani government should frankly tell Britain that if Britain attempts to send warships to the Suez Canal, they will not only encounter resistance from the Egyptians but also from all freedom-loving countries.

    The Standing Committee of the Lahore Municipal Administration passed a resolution fully supporting this action by Egypt. Those in Lahore who welcomed this action also included: Abdul Gayoom, General Secretary of the Pakistan Republican Party; Iqbal Chafa, Chairman of the Pakistan Christian League; and Maulana Fazal Hussain Dilawar, Chairman of the Islamic Democratic Conference.

### 印度政界表示不许重演“摩萨台事件”  巴基斯坦知名人士要求政府支持埃及

1956-07-31
第5版()
专栏：

    印度政界表示不许重演“摩萨台事件”
    巴基斯坦知名人士要求政府支持埃及
    据新华社新德里29日电　印度报业托辣斯报道，印度首都政界人士认为埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的决定是英美撤回对于建筑阿斯旺水坝的援助的直接结果。但是有些人对可能产生的政治后果表示担心。
    印度社会党领袖洛希亚在海得拉巴举行的社会党的会议上发表演说说，可能有人担心英国和其他有关国家可能派遣军舰到这个地区去，但是更应该担心的是可能随之而来的经济、外交和政治阴谋。欧洲人和美国人可能设法对埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔采取他们过去对前伊朗首相摩萨台所采取的行动
  （摩萨台曾经因为把伊朗石油公司收归国有而被军事叛变搞下台）。
    这位社会党人希望世界各国人民，包括正在为争取单一的和平等的世界而斗争的欧洲人和美国人在内，发出强有力的警告：我们不容许再发生“摩萨台事件”。
    据新华社喀喇蚩29日电　巴基斯坦联合通讯社报道，巴基斯坦许多人士欢迎埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有，并要求巴基斯坦政府支持埃及的这个行动。
    西巴基斯坦立法会议议员哈瓦贾·穆札法鲁尔·哈克在喀喇蚩发表谈话说，这是埃及利用自己的资源来完成它的国家计划而采取的一个“果敢的和令人鼓舞的步骤”。他要求巴基斯坦政府在这个问题上“公开赞成和保证完全支持”埃及。
    喀喇蚩人民联盟总书记阿齐久拉说，巴基斯坦政府应该坦率地对英国说，如果英国企图派遣军舰前往苏彝士运河的话，他们不但将遭到埃及人的抵抗，而且将遭到所有爱好自由的国家的抵抗。
    拉合尔市行政机关常务委员会通过一个决议，完全支持埃及的这个行动。在拉合尔对这个行动表示欢迎的人士还有：巴基斯坦共和党总书记阿卜杜勒·加尧姆、巴基斯坦基督徒联盟主席伊克巴尔·查法、伊斯兰民主大会主席、大毛拉法查尔·侯赛尼·迪拉华。
