Title: Bulganin Issues Statement on Poland's Western Border Silesia Belongs to Poland Forever
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 6

    Bulganin Issues Statement on Poland's Western Border
    Silesia Belongs to Poland Forever
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 27th - According to the Polish Press Agency, after attending Poland's National Day celebrations in Warsaw, Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin and Soviet Defense Minister Zhukov left Warsaw to visit Łódź, Kraków, Stalinogród and other places in Poland, and returned to Warsaw on July 26th.
    On July 25th, while visiting the Stalinogród province, Bulganin and Zhukov issued a statement on the issue of the western border and Silesia through the "Worker's Tribune".
    The statement said: "Citizens of Silesia! We bring you the fraternal greetings of the people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union. We are very happy that justice has triumphed and Silesia has returned to your homeland, Poland. No one can hinder this just act. The western border and Silesia now belong to Poland forever. The guarantee of this is the friendship of our socialist camp countries, the friendship between Poland and the Soviet Union."

### 布尔加宁发表关于波兰西部边界的声明  西里西亚已经永远属于波兰

1956-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁发表关于波兰西部边界的声明
    西里西亚已经永远属于波兰
    新华社华沙27日电　据波兰通讯社报道：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联国防部长朱可夫在华沙参加波兰国庆节后，离开华沙到波兰的罗兹、克拉科夫、斯大林诺格罗德等地访问，七月二十六日回到了华沙。
    布尔加宁和朱可夫七月二十五日访问斯大林诺格罗德省的时候，通过“工人论坛报”发表了关于西部和西里西亚边界问题的声明。
    声明说：“西里西亚的公民们！我们给你们带来了苏联各族人民的兄弟般的问候。我们很高兴，正义获得了胜利，西里西亚回到了你们的祖国波兰。谁也不能够妨碍这个正义的行动。西部边界和西里西亚现在已经永远属于波兰。对于这一点的保证是我们社会主义阵营各国的友谊，波兰和苏联的友谊。”
