Title: Beijing Competition Zone Holds Youth Football Championship
Author: Wang Yikai
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 4

    Beijing Competition Zone
    Holds Youth Football Championship

    (Our newspaper dispatch) The Beijing Competition Zone of the 1956 National Youth Football Championship began this afternoon at 5:00 PM at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium.

    Participating in the competition are representative teams from five cities: Beijing, Tianjin, Qingdao, Jinan, and Baoding. The participating players are all young people between the ages of eighteen and twenty. The champion team of this zonal competition will participate in the second stage of the National Youth Football Championship held in Beijing in late August.
                    (Wang Yikai)

### 北京竞赛区举行青年足球锦标赛
王宜凯
1956-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    北京竞赛区
    举行青年足球锦标赛
    本报讯　1956年全国青年足球锦标赛北京竞赛区比赛大会今日下午五时在先农坛体育场举行。
    参加竞赛的有北京、天津、青岛、济南、保定五城市的代表队，参加比赛的队员都是十八岁到二十岁的青年。这次分区竞赛的冠军队，将参加八月下旬在北京举行的全国青年足球锦标赛第二阶段比赛。
                    （王宜凯）
