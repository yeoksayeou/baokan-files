Title: Central Committee Leaders of the CCP Receive Advanced Workers
Author:
Date: 1956-07-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Kang Sheng, Peng Zhen, Dong Biwu, Lin Boqu, Peng Dehuai, and Deng Xiaoping, received all the representatives attending the National Salt Industry Transportation and Sales Advanced Workers Representative Conference and the First National Supply and Marketing Cooperative Advanced Workers Representative Conference this afternoon.

### 中共中央领导人接见先进工作者

1956-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见先进工作者
    新华社2日讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、朱德、陈云、康生、彭真、董必武、林伯渠、彭德怀、邓小平，在今天下午先后接见了出席全国盐业运销先进工作者代表会议和出席全国供销合作社第一次先进工作者代表会议的全体代表。
