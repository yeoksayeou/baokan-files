Title: There is Plant Life on Mars
Author:
Date: 1956-07-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 29th. Moscow Radio reported that a spectroscopic test conducted by a Soviet scientist has provided further evidence for the view that there is plant life on Mars. The scientists proved in the experiment that the spectrum of the dark spots on the surface of Mars has much in common with the spectrum of plants growing in the northern and mountainous regions of the Earth.

### 火星上有植物

1956-07-30
第5版()
专栏：

    火星上有植物
    据新华社莫斯科29日电　莫斯科广播电台说，一位苏联科学家进行的光谱试验，给火星上有植物的看法提供了又一个佐证。科学家们在试验中证明：火星表面上的黑点的光谱同地球的北部和山区生长的植物的光谱有很大的共同之处。
