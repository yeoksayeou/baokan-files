Title: Albanian Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The first group of nine members of the Albanian People's Republic Agricultural Delegation arrived in Beijing by plane today.
	They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese government to study issues such as agricultural cooperativization. The delegation consists of forty people, headed by K. Tashko, Deputy Minister of Agriculture of Albania. Members of the delegation include V. Pipa, Deputy Director of the Agricultural Department of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party; S. Pirlikati, Deputy Chairman of the Central Consumer Cooperative Alliance; M. Miko, instructor of the Albanian Women's Union; M. Toda and M. Lapo, Party Secretaries of Elbasan and Korca Provinces; and V. Kelimi, special reporter for "Unity" newspaper, the organ of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party. The remaining members of the delegation will arrive in Beijing successively in the next few days.

### 阿尔巴尼亚农业代表团到京

1956-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚农业代表团到京
    据新华社13日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国农业代表团第一批九人，在今天先后乘飞机到达北京。
    他们是应邀前来中国考察农业合作化等问题的。代表团一共有四十人，团长卡·塔什考是阿尔巴尼亚农业部副部长。团员中有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央农业部副部长维·皮帕，中央消费合作社联盟副主席萨·皮里卡蒂，阿尔巴尼亚妇女联盟指导员玛·米柯，爱尔巴桑和科尔察两省的党委书记米·托达和米·拉波，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央机关报“团结”报特派记者维·凯利米等人。尚未到达的团员将在今后几天内陆续到达北京。
