Title: Delegation of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association Visits Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1956-07-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd. A six-member delegation from the China-Indonesia Friendship Association left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 23rd for a month-long friendly visit to Indonesia and to participate in the Indonesian Independence Day celebrations. The delegation is headed by Da Pongsheng, Director of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association and Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association, and Ling Sha, Director of the Association and President of Beijing Normal College, as Deputy Head. Members of the delegation include historians and figures from the Islamic community.

### 中国印度尼西亚友协代表团  去印度尼西亚访问

1956-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

    中国印度尼西亚友协代表团
    去印度尼西亚访问
    新华社23日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会代表团一行六人，二十三日上午乘飞机离北京前往印度尼西亚作为期一个月的友好访问，并且参加印度尼西亚共和国独立日。代表团由中国印度尼西亚友好协会理事、中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生任团长，协会理事、北京师范学院院长凌莎任副团长，团员中有历史学家和伊斯兰教界人士。
