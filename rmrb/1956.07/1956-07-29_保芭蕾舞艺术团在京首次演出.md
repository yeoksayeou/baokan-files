Title: Bulgarian Ballet Troupe's First Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 28th: The Bulgarian Ballet Troupe, in its first performance at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing tonight, presented the brilliant achievements of Bulgarian ballet art with twenty-one classical ballet and Bulgarian dance programs.

The performance began with a trio dance of Prince Siegfried and guests from the first act of the ballet "Swan Lake," followed by excerpts from nine famous ballets such as "Faust." Lili Beron, a Dimitrov Prize winner, and Ko. Yankov performed deeply moving scenes of Prince Siegfried meeting the maiden Odette in "Swan Lake" and Romeo meeting Juliet in "Romeo and Juliet." Arsen Gavrilov, a winner at the Fourth World Youth Festival, portrayed the heroic image of Andrei, the son of Taras Bulba, in the "Taras Bulba" ballet in a performance lasting less than two minutes, and also performed a scene of the people celebrating victory from the ballet "Flames of Paris" with Pan Aincheva.

Several dances created based on the music of famous musicians such as Chopin and Strauss were also performed at the evening's event. "Past Scenes" composed by Bulgarian musician P. Stoyanov and "Idyll and Classical Dance" composed by Le. Bebukov were also performed tonight.

These dances were a great success and won the audience's long and sustained applause.

At the opening ceremony before the performance, Hu Guogang, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Dance Art Research Association, delivered a speech welcoming the Bulgarian Ballet Troupe. After the performance, Zheng Zhenduo and Xia Yan, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, and Takov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, went on stage to congratulate the Bulgarian artists on their successful performance.

### 保芭蕾舞艺术团在京首次演出

1956-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    保芭蕾舞艺术团在京首次演出
    据新华社28日讯　保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术团今晚在北京天桥剧场的首次演出中，以二十一个古典芭蕾舞和保加利亚舞蹈节目，介绍了保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术的光辉成就。
    晚会上的表演以芭蕾舞剧“天鹅湖”第一幕齐格弗里德王子和客人的三人舞开始，接着又表演了“浮士德”等九出著名芭蕾舞剧中的片断。季米特洛夫奖金获得者莉莉·贝朗和科·杨科夫表演了“天鹅湖”中齐格弗里德王子与少女奥杰塔会见和“罗密欧与朱丽叶”中罗密欧与朱丽叶会见时深情动人的场面。第四届世界青年联欢节获奖者阿森·加夫里洛夫在一次不到两分钟的表演中，刻划了“塔拉斯·布尔巴”舞剧中塔拉斯·布尔巴的儿子安德烈的英武豪迈的形象，又与潘·爱恩切娃合演了舞剧“巴黎的火焰”中人民欢庆胜利的一段。
    晚会上还表演了根据著名音乐家萧邦、斯特劳斯等的乐曲创作的几个舞蹈。保加利亚音乐家普·斯达伊诺夫作曲的“过去的景象”，和勒·贝布科夫作曲的“田园诗与古典舞”也在今天的晚会上演出。
    这些舞蹈获得了极大的成功，博得了观众的经久不息的掌声。
    在演出前的开幕式上，中国舞蹈艺术研究会副主席胡果刚致词欢迎保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术团。在演出结束以后，文化部副部长郑振铎、夏衍，对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经和保加利亚驻华大使馆临时代办塔科夫等到台上祝贺保加利亚艺术家演出成功。
