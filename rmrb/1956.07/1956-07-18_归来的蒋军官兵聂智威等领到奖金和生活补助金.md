Title: Returned Chiang Kai-shek Soldiers Nie Zhiwei and Others Receive Bonuses and Living Allowances
Author:
Date: 1956-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, July 17th. Nie Zhiwei and three other Chiang Kai-shek soldiers who returned from Jinmen Island and Dadan Island have recently received bonuses and living allowances from the leading organ of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Fujian Front. Nie Zhiwei received a bonus of 250 yuan, Huang Kunxiong received a living allowance of 50 yuan, and Wang Jialun and Zhang Dailin each received a living allowance of 30 yuan.

After receiving the bonuses and living allowances, Nie Zhiwei and others happily went to the streets to buy things they liked. Nie Zhiwei bought some bodiless lacquerware and Shoushan stone carvings and other crafts, preparing to give them to long-separated relatives and friends.

### 归来的蒋军官兵聂智威等领到奖金和生活补助金

1956-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    归来的蒋军官兵聂智威等领到奖金和生活补助金
    新华社福州17日电　从金门岛、大担岛归来的蒋军官兵聂智威等四人，最近分别领到中国人民解放军福建前线领导机关发给的奖金和生活补助金。聂智威领到奖金二百五十元，黄坤雄领到生活补助金五十元，王家轮、张代林各领到生活补助金三十元。
    聂智威等领到奖金和生活补助金以后，高兴地到街上买自己喜爱的东西，聂智威买了些脱胎漆器和寿山石刻等工艺品，准备送给久别的亲友。
