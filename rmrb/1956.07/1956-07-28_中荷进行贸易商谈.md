Title: China and the Netherlands Conduct Trade Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Amsterdam, 27th. The Chinese International Trade Promotion Committee trade delegation, currently visiting the Netherlands, held specific trade discussions with more than 70 Dutch manufacturers on July 25th, under the auspices of the Amsterdam Chamber of Commerce.

On the same day, all members of the delegation paid a visit to the Mayor of Amsterdam, Mr. D'Ailly. During the visit, both sides exchanged gifts.

### 中荷进行贸易商谈

1956-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    中荷进行贸易商谈
    新华社27日讯　阿姆斯特丹消息：正在荷兰访问的中国国际贸易促进委员会贸易访问团，七月二十五日在阿姆斯特丹商会主持下，同七十多家荷兰厂家进行了有关贸易的具体商谈。
    同一天，访问团全体团员拜访了阿姆斯特丹市长戴利。在拜访时，双方互赠了礼品。
