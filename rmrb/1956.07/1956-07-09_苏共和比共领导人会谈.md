Title: Soviet and Belgian Communist Party Leaders Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 8th: According to TASS, Soviet Communist Party leaders held a meeting on July 7th with Belgian Communist Party leaders who are currently visiting the Soviet Union.

　　Participating in the meeting from the Soviet Communist Party were Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee; Pospelov, Secretary; and Ponomarev, Member. Representing the Belgian Communist Party were Burnelle, General Secretary; Dejace, Central Committee Member; Van Hoorick, and Gilles.

　　During the sincere talks, they discussed issues of interest to both parties, and the meeting was filled with a spirit of mutual understanding and fraternal trust.

### 苏共和比共领导人会谈

1956-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共和比共领导人会谈
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党领导人同正在苏联访问的比利时共产党领导人，在七月七日举行了一次会谈。
    参加这次会谈的，苏联共产党方面有中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫、书记波斯伯洛夫、委员波诺马烈夫，比利时共产党方面有总书记伯内尔、中央委员杰日斯、范胡里克和日利斯。
    他们在真诚的会谈中讨论了两党感兴趣的问题，会谈并且充满相互谅解和兄弟信任的精神。
