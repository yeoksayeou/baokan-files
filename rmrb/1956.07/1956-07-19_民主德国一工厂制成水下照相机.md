Title: East German Factory Produces Underwater Camera
Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 18th. According to the German News Agency, the state-owned Carl Zeiss Jena camera factory in East Germany has produced an underwater camera called "Taxuna."

This camera can capture scenes up to 30 meters deep in the water, which is very helpful for divers and scientists to work underwater and study underwater conditions.

The Dresden Handicraft Factory has recently successfully manufactured a waterproof casing called "Tachtex" designed for this underwater camera. The camera and casing together weigh 1.8 kilograms.

### 民主德国一工厂制成水下照相机

1956-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国一工厂制成水下照相机
    新华社柏林18日电　据德意志通讯社报道：民主德国国营蔡斯·伊康照相机工厂制成了一种名叫“塔克苏纳”的水下照相机。
    这种照相机能够在水中摄取三十公尺深远的景物，对潜水工人和科学家在水下进行工作和研究水下的情况很有帮助。
    德累斯顿手工业工厂为这种水下照相机设计的名叫“塔赤台克斯”的防水套箱不久以前已经制造成功。照相机和套箱共重一点八公斤。
