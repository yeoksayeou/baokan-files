Title: Eisenhower Decides to Run for President Again
Author:
Date: 1956-07-12
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, July 11th dispatch from Gettysburg: While recuperating here after an illness and surgery, U.S. President Eisenhower stated at a meeting with Republican congressional leaders on July 10th that he will run for president again. Following this meeting, Republican Senate leader Knowland announced this at a press conference.
    When a reporter asked Knowland whether Eisenhower had reconsidered his decision to run again in light of his recent illness, Knowland replied, "The fact remains that he will be a candidate."
    White House Press Secretary Hagerty said at a press conference that there is no need to reconsider Nixon's position to run again as a vice-presidential candidate.

### 艾森豪威尔决再竞选总统

1956-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔决再竞选总统
    据新华社11日讯　葛斯底堡消息：患病动手术后在这里休养的美国总统艾森豪威尔七月十日在同共和党国会领袖举行的一次会议上表示，他将再次竞选总统。在这个会议以后，共和党参议院领袖诺兰举行记者招待会宣布了这件事情。
    有记者问诺兰，艾森豪威尔是否曾经根据他最近生病的情况重新考虑过他要再次竞选的决定。诺兰回答说，“事实仍然是他要参加竞选。”
    白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂在记者招待会上说，不需要重新考虑尼克松准备作为副总统候选人再次参加竞选的地位。
