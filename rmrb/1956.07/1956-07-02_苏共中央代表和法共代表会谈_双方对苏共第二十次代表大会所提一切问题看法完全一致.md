Title: CPSU Central Committee Representatives and French Communist Party Representatives Hold Talks: Both Sides Have Completely Identical Views on All Issues Raised at the 20th Congress of the CPSU
Author:
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st Dispatch: According to TASS, representatives of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the delegation of the French Communist Party visiting Moscow held talks on June 28th and 30th.

    During the talks, both sides expressed unanimous views on all issues raised at the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, including the issues of the working class's actions, which are of great significance for the struggle for peace, democracy, and socialism.

    The talks were conducted in a cordial atmosphere in a spirit of sincerity and friendship.

    The representatives of the CPSU Central Committee who participated in the talks were: Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Secretary Pospelov, and Central Committee member Ponomarev; the representatives of the French Communist Party were: Fajon, Rochet, and Servin, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party.

### 苏共中央代表和法共代表会谈  双方对苏共第二十次代表大会所提一切问题看法完全一致

1956-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共中央代表和法共代表会谈
    双方对苏共第二十次代表大会所提一切问题看法完全一致
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会代表和到莫斯科访问的法国共产党代表团，在六月二十八日和三十日举行了会谈。
    在会谈过程中，双方对苏联共产党第二十次代表大会所提出的一切问题表示了一致的看法，这些问题中包括对争取和平、民主和社会主义斗争有非常重大意义的工人阶级的行动的问题。
    会谈是在亲切的气氛中本着真诚友好的精神进行的。
    参加会谈的苏共中央的代表是：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫、书记波斯伯洛夫和中央委员波诺马烈夫；法国共产党的代表是：法国共产党中央委员会政治局委员法戎、罗歇和赛尔文。
