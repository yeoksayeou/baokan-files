Title: First Tanker Train Departs Yumen (Picture)
Author: Hu Yue
Date: 1956-07-06
Page: 2

    First Tanker Train Departs Yumen
    The first train, fully loaded with crude oil, departed from the Yumen Oilfield area on July 1st. With this, the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway is now open to traffic for over 820 kilometers.
                                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Hu Yue

### 首次油槽车开出玉门（图片）
胡越
1956-07-06
第2版()
专栏：

    首次油槽车开出玉门
    满载着原油的第一列火车已在七月一日从玉门油矿区开出。至此，兰新铁路火车已能通行八百二十多公里。
                                    新华社记者  胡越摄
