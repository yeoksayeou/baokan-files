Title: Water Hyacinth - Good Pig Feed (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 3

Water hyacinth is an aquatic plant with high yield and good nutrition, making it a very good pig feed. One mu of pond can produce more than 300,000 jin of water hyacinth a year, enough for the entire green fodder for 150 pigs. This is a picture of female members of the Xinglong Overseas Chinese Collective Farm in Hainan Island collecting water hyacinth planted in the field.

### 水浮莲——养猪的好饲料（图片）

1956-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    水浮莲——养猪的好饲料（图片）
    水浮莲是一种水生植物，产量很高，营养也很好，是一种很好的猪饲料。一亩池塘一年可产三十多万斤水浮莲，足够一百五十只猪的全部青饲料。这是海南岛兴隆华侨集体农场女庄员在采集场里种植的水浮莲。　　　　　　　　　　　　李起龙摄（新华社稿）
