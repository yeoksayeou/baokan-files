Title: Premier Zhou Receives Visiting Japanese Personages
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received 65 people from three Japanese delegations and Japanese film and trade circles visiting China at 6:00 PM on the 28th.

Those received included the Japanese National Railway Workers Friendship Delegation to China headed by Kiyoshi Suzuki, the Japanese Asian Solidarity Cultural Delegation headed by Tatsuzo Ishikawa, the Japanese film industry representatives Nobuko Otowa and Tamotsu Minato, the Japanese Legal Delegation headed by Morio Aoyagi, and the Japanese trade representative Minoru Shiramizu.

Present at the meeting were Guo Moruo, Li Dequan, Mao Dun, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Lei Renmin, Xia Yan, Yang Hansheng, Zhu Qiwen, Chen Yu, Tang Mingzhao, Wu Defeng, Chen Hansheng, Zheng Senyu, and Wang Zhijie, as well as the Japanese personage currently in Beijing, Togo Kameda.

### 周总理接见访华的日本人士

1956-07-01
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见访华的日本人士
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在二十八日下午六时接见了正在中国访问的三个日本代表团和日本电影界、贸易界人士等六十五人。
    受接见的有以铃木清为首的日本国营铁道工人访华友好代表团，以石川达三为首的日本亚洲团结文化代表团，日本电影界代表乙羽信子、凑保，以青柳盛雄为首的日本法学代表团和日本贸易代表白水实。
    接见的时候，在座的有郭沫若、李德全、茅盾、廖承志、刘宁一、雷任民、夏衍、阳翰笙、朱其文、陈宇、唐明照、吴德峰、陈翰笙、郑森禹、王志杰等人，以及现在北京的日本人士龟田东伍。
