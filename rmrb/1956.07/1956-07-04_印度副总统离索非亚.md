Title: Indian Vice President Leaves Sofia
Author:
Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd: According to the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency, Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan, who was invited to Bulgaria for a friendly visit, left Sofia by plane on the morning of July 2nd.

The Indian Vice President arrived in Sofia on June 29th. During his stay in Sofia, he attended a special session of the Bulgarian National Assembly and visited some industrial enterprises and cultural institutions. On June 30th, Radhakrishnan received an honorary doctorate of philosophy from Sofia University.

### 印度副总统离索非亚

1956-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    印度副总统离索非亚
    新华社3日讯　据保加利亚通讯社报道：应邀到保加利亚作友好访问的印度副总统拉德哈克里希南，七月二日上午乘飞机离开索非亚。
    印度副总统是在六月二十九日到达索非亚的。在索非亚期间，他参加了保加利亚国民议会的特别会议，参观了一些工业企业和文化机关等。六月三十日，拉德哈克里希南接受了索非亚大学赠给的荣誉哲学博士证书。
