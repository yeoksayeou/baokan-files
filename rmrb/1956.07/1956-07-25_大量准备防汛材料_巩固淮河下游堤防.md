Title: Mass Preparation of Flood Control Materials to Consolidate the Lower Reaches of the Huai River Embankment
Author: Li Zao
Date: 1956-07-25
Page: 3
    Mass Preparation of Flood Control Materials
    Consolidating the Lower Reaches of the Huai River Embankment

    (Our Dispatch) The Jiangsu Provincial Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters and the Northern Jiangsu Flood Control and Drought Relief Sub-Headquarters are preparing large quantities of flood control materials to protect the main trunk embankments of the lower reaches of the Huai River. The Jiangsu Provincial Department of Transportation has also designated more than 800 employees from the Zhenjiang, Wuxi, and Huaiyin Navigation Management Bureaus under the Jiangsu Provincial Inland River Shipping Company, along with more than 50 ships and more than 500 wooden barges, and organized 50 wooden boat teams to support this major flood control work. Currently, large quantities of stone are being continuously transported to designated locations from Qixia Mountain, Longtan, Jurong, Zhenjiang, Suzhou, Wuxi, Changzhou, Gaozi, Jiangyin, Lianyungang, Chuxian, Yi and Jiulishan and Dahu areas of Xuzhou. This work is expected to be completed before the end of July. (Li Zao)

### 大量准备防汛材料  巩固淮河下游堤防
立早
1956-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

    大量准备防汛材料
    巩固淮河下游堤防
    本报讯　江苏省防汛防旱总指挥部和苏北防汛防旱分指挥部，为防护淮河下游主要干流堤防，正大量准备防汛材料。江苏省交通厅还指定江苏省内河轮船公司所属镇江、无锡、淮阴三个航管局派出职工八百多人，轮船五十多艘、木驳船五百多只，并组织五十个木船队支援这一重大防汛工作。目前，大批石料正从栖霞山、龙潭、句容、镇江、苏州、无锡、常州、高资、江阴、连云港、滁县、?贻和徐州地区的九里山、大湖等地，源源不断地运到指定地点。这一工作预计七月底以前可以全部完成。（立早）
