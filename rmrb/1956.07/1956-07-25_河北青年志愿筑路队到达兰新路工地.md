Title: Hebei Youth Volunteer Road-Building Team Arrives at Lanxin Railway Construction Site
Author:
Date: 1956-07-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The entire contingent of the Hebei Youth Volunteer Road-Building Team arrived at the Lanxin Railway front construction site on the 21st, where they were warmly welcomed by the road-building workers. When they passed through Lanzhou, the railway workers there held a grand welcome meeting for them.

The members of the Youth Volunteer Road-Building Team volunteered to come to the Northwest to participate in the road construction project in order to accelerate the completion of the Lanxin Railway. Among the more than 1,000 members of the team, 70% are Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members. They will be responsible for a portion of the road construction task in the vast Gobi Desert west of Yumen on the Lanxin Railway.

### 河北青年志愿筑路队到达兰新路工地

1956-07-25
第2版()
专栏：

    河北青年志愿筑路队到达兰新路工地
    据新华社讯　河北省青年志愿筑路队全体队员，已在二十一日到达兰新铁路前方工地，受到了筑路工人们的热情欢迎。他们路过兰州的时候，这里的铁路职工们还为他们举行了一个盛大的欢迎会。
    青年志愿筑路队的队员们，是为了加速修通兰新路，自动报名到西北来参加筑路工程的。全队一千多名队员当中，有70％是共产党员和青年团员。他们将担负兰新铁路玉门以西大戈壁上的一部分筑路任务。
