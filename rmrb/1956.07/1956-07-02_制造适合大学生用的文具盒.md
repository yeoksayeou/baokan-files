Title: Manufacturing Pencil Boxes Suitable for College Students
Author: Guo Jun
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 2
Reader Opinions

    Manufacturing Pencil Boxes Suitable for College Students
    The tin-printed pencil boxes currently sold on the market are only suitable for elementary school students. They are not suitable for college students or office workers. I suggest that factories manufacturing pencil boxes design more specifications and styles, not only producing tin-printed ones, but also producing beautiful and inexpensive pencil boxes made of bakelite, artificial glass, and celluloid.
                      (Guo Jun)

### 制造适合大学生用的文具盒
郭钧
1956-07-02
第2版()
专栏：读者意见

    制造适合大学生用的文具盒
    目前市场上卖的洋铁印花的文具盒只适合小学生使用，大学生或职员使用是很不合适的。我建议制造文具盒的工厂多设计几种规格和式样，不只生产洋铁印花的，也要生产胶木的、人造玻璃的和赛璐珞的又漂亮又便宜的文具盒。
                      （郭钧）
