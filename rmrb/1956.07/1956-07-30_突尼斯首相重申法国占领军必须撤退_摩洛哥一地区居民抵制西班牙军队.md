Title: Tunisian Prime Minister Reiterates French Occupation Forces Must Withdraw; Residents of a Region in Morocco Resist Spanish Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-07-30
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, July 29th: TASS Paris, July 28th: According to Agence France-Presse, Tunisian Prime Minister Bourguiba, in his weekly address, reiterated that France must recognize "the true independence of Tunisia, and therefore French armed forces must withdraw." Bourguiba said: "As long as the occupying forces are stationed in Tunisia, it will be impossible for our two countries to implement any cooperation. Tunisia should take charge of its domestic and external defense."
    
    Xinhua News Agency, July 28th: Rabat dispatch: According to "Al Alam" newspaper, local residents resisted the landing of 1,500 Spanish troops in Sidi Ifni, southwestern Morocco.
    
    Sidi Ifni was separated from Spanish Morocco by Spain in 1951. This year, when Spain transferred sovereignty of Spanish Morocco to Morocco, it attempted to retain ownership of Sidi Ifni.

### 突尼斯首相重申法国占领军必须撤退  摩洛哥一地区居民抵制西班牙军队

1956-07-30
第6版()
专栏：

    突尼斯首相重申法国占领军必须撤退
    摩洛哥一地区居民抵制西班牙军队
    新华社29日讯　塔斯社巴黎二十八日讯：据法新社消息，突尼斯首相布尔吉巴在他的每周谈话中再度申明法国必须承认“突尼斯的真正独立，因而法国武装部队必须撤退”。布尔吉巴说：“只要占领军驻在突尼斯，我们两国就不可能实行任何合作。突尼斯应当自己掌握国内防务和对外防务。”
    据新华社28日讯　拉巴特消息：据“旗帜报”报道，西班牙军队一千五百人在摩洛哥西南部的西迪·伊夫尼登陆的时候遭到当地居民的抵制。
    西迪·伊夫尼在1951年被西班牙从西属摩洛哥分割出来。今年西班牙在把西属摩洛哥主权移交给摩洛哥的时候，企图仍然保持对西迪·伊夫尼的所有权。
