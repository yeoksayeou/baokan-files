Title: All-China Women's Federation Second Session Third Executive Committee Decides: National Third Women's Congress to be Held Next Year
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th - The All-China Democratic Women's Federation, at its Second Session, Third Executive Committee meeting held this morning, passed a resolution regarding the convening of the National Third Women's Congress in 1957.

    The meeting also discussed the All-China Women's Federation's work plan for 1956 and heard an introduction to the work situation regarding women in the Tibet region. A total of sixty-eight committee members attended the meeting.

    Luo Qiong and Cao Mengjun, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, gave reports at the meeting respectively on the domestic and international work situation over the past year. The committee members present discussed the relevant work plans for 1956 put forward in the reports. The committee members attached great importance to the issue of further promoting women and children's welfare work mentioned in the report. They proposed that in the future, in mobilizing women in urban and rural areas to participate in various constructions, special attention should be paid to protecting women's health and appropriately resolving issues concerning childcare and welfare. In addition, many committee members also suggested that the Women's Federation should strengthen its work with women of fraternal ethnic groups and female intellectuals in the future.

    At the meeting, Ulan, Executive Committee member and Director of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Democratic Women's Federation, reported on their participation in the central delegation to Lhasa to congratulate the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region. Yaoxi Zeren Zhuoma, Executive Committee member and Director of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Association, introduced the situation of women's work in the Tibet region at the meeting.

### 全国妇联二届三次执委会决定  全国第三次妇女代表大会明年举行

1956-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    全国妇联二届三次执委会决定
    全国第三次妇女代表大会明年举行
    据新华社29日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会在今天上午举行的第二届第三次执行委员会会议上，通过了关于1957年召开全国第三次妇女代表大会的决定。
    这次会议还讨论了全国妇联1956年的工作计划，听了关于西藏地区妇女工作情况的介绍。出席会议的委员共六十八人。
    全国妇联书记处书记罗琼和曹孟君在会上分别就过去一年来国内和国际工作情况作了报告。到会的委员们对于报告中提出的有关1956年工作计划进行了讨论，委员们很重视报告中提到的进一步推进妇女儿童福利工作的问题，她们提出今后在城市和农村发动妇女参加各项建设中，应特别注意保护妇女健康，适当解决儿童照管及福利问题。此外许多委员还建议妇联今后应加强兄弟民族妇女工作及女知识分子的工作。
    会上，执行委员、内蒙古自治区民主妇女联合会主任乌兰汇报了她们参加中央代表团去拉萨祝贺西藏自治区筹备委员会成立的情况。执委、拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会主任尧西·泽仁卓玛在会上介绍了西藏地区妇女工作的情况。
