Title: Fuzhou City Has Tap Water

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, July 1st – The first phase of the Fuzhou Waterworks construction project has been completed. Fuzhou City, which has never had tap water facilities, now has tap water starting today. Once the entire waterworks project is completed, it can supply water to the entire city of Fuzhou. Through a dedicated pipeline and canal, it can also irrigate more than 6,000 mu of farmland in the suburbs that suffer from drought nine years out of ten.

### 福州市有了自来水

1956-07-02
第2版()
专栏：

    福州市有了自来水
    新华社福州1日电　福州自来水厂第一期建设工程完工，从未有过自来水设备的福州市从今天开始有了自来水。这座自来水厂的全部工程完工以后，可以供应福州市全市的用水。通过一条专设的管道和渠道，还可以灌溉市郊六千多亩十年九旱的农田。
