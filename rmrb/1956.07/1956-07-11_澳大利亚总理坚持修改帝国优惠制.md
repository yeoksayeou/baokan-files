Title: Australian Prime Minister Insists on Revising Imperial Preference System
Author:
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 10th: Australian Prime Minister Menzies stated at a press conference in London on the 9th that Australia insists on revising the Ottawa Agreement, which stipulates the "Imperial Preference" system.

Menzies said that by doing so, Australia "can buy more goods from countries other than Britain and also sell more goods to them." Menzies pointed out that West Germany is a country that Australia wants to increase trade with.

### 澳大利亚总理坚持修改帝国优惠制

1956-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚总理坚持修改帝国优惠制
    据新华社10日讯　伦敦消息：澳大利亚总理孟席斯九日在伦敦记者招待会上表示，澳大利亚坚持修改规定“帝国优惠制”的渥太华协定。
    孟席斯说，这样做，澳大利亚“就可以向英国以外的国家购买较多的货物，也可以卖给它们较多的货物”。孟席斯指出，西德是澳大利亚想同它增进贸易的国家。
