Title: Yanbian's Specialty Apple Pears Expected to Have a Bumper Harvest
Author:
Date: 1956-07-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, July 25th: Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture's specialty apple pears, popular at home and abroad, are expected to have a bumper harvest. The fruit trees in the main producing area of ​​the Laotougou State Farm are laden with fruit, and it is estimated that the yield will double compared to last year.

Apple pears are the result of crossing apples and pears. Its shape and taste combine the characteristics of apples and pears. The fruit is large, the taste is sweet and sour, the water content is sufficient, and it is nutritious. It can also be stored for up to a year.

### 延边特产苹果梨可望丰收

1956-07-27
第3版()
专栏：

    延边特产苹果梨可望丰收
    新华社长春25日电　畅销国内外的延边朝鲜族自治州特产苹果梨，可望大丰收。主要产地老头沟国营农场的果树，都结满了果实，预计能比去年增产一倍。
    苹果梨是苹果和梨杂交的结果。它的形状、味道兼有苹果和梨的特点，果实肥大，味道酸甜适口，水分充足，营养丰富，而且能贮藏一年之久。
