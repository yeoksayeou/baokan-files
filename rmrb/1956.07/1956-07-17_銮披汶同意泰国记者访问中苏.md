Title: Phibunsongkhram Agrees to Thai Journalists Visiting China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 16th dispatch: According to Bangkok newspapers, Thai Prime Minister Phibunsongkhram said on July 8th that he agreed to the Thai press accepting the Soviet Union's invitation to visit, provided they go to the Soviet Union purely as journalists, for the sake of visiting, and without any other political purpose. He also said that the government would not oppose Thai journalists going to mainland China either.

### 銮披汶同意泰国记者访问中苏

1956-07-17
第6版()
专栏：

    銮披汶同意泰国记者访问中苏
    据新华社16日讯　据曼谷报纸报道，泰国总理銮披汶七月八日说，他同意泰国新闻界接受苏联的邀请前往访问，如果他们只是以记者身份到苏联，单纯地为了访问而并没有其他政治目的的话。他又说，泰国记者要去中国大陆也一样地不会受到政府的反对。
