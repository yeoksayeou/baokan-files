Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 1

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    The Model Regulations for Advanced Agricultural Producers' Cooperatives, having been adopted by the Third Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on June 30, 1956, are hereby promulgated.
                  Mao Zedong
                  Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                              June 30, 1956

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1956-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    高级农业生产合作社示范章程已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议于1956年6月30日通过，现予公布。
                  中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                              1956年6月30日
