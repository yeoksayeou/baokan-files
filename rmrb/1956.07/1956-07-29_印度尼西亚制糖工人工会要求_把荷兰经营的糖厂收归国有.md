Title: Indonesian Sugar Workers Union Demands Nationalization of Dutch-Operated Sugar Factories
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 28th: Surabaya dispatch: The Executive Council of the Indonesian Sugar Workers Union recently passed a resolution demanding that the government, in implementing the decision to abolish the Round Table Conference Agreement, nationalize all Dutch-operated sugar factories.

    The Fifth Plenary Meeting welcomed the government's decision to rescind the ban on rubber exports to China and expressed the hope that the ban on other strategic materials would also be lifted to promote Indonesia's export industry.

    The meeting also warmly welcomed and supported the proposal to hold an Asian-African Labor Conference, believing that such a conference is in line with the spirit of the Bandung Conference.

### 印度尼西亚制糖工人工会要求  把荷兰经营的糖厂收归国有

1956-07-29
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚制糖工人工会要求
    把荷兰经营的糖厂收归国有
    新华社雅加达28日电　泗水消息：印度尼西亚制糖工人工会执行理事会第五次全体会议最近通过一项决定，要求政府在执行废除圆桌会议协定的决定的时候把所有荷兰经营的制糖厂收归国有。
    第五次全体会议欢迎政府决定撤销不许向中国出口橡胶的禁令，并且希望也解除对其他战略物资的禁令，以便促进印度尼西亚的出口业。
    这个会议也热烈欢迎并且支持关于举行亚非劳工会议的建议，它认为这种会议是符合万隆会议精神的。
