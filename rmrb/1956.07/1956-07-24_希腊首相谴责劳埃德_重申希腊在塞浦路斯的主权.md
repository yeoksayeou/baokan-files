Title: Greek Prime Minister Condemns Lloyd, Reaffirms Greek Sovereignty over Cyprus
Author:
Date: 1956-07-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd, Athens Dispatch: Greek Prime Minister Karamanlis issued a statement on the 21st, condemning British Foreign Secretary Lloyd's speech in the British House of Commons regarding the Cyprus issue.

Karamanlis pointed out that Britain's excuse that it cannot grant Cyprus self-determination for the time being due to Turkish opposition is intolerable. Greece's sovereignty over Cyprus is unchangeable, not only stipulated in international treaties, but also proven by the sentiments of the vast majority of Greek residents.

Karamanlis denounced Turkey's demands on Cyprus as pure "extortion," and he mocked Turkey's threats of "armed conflict." He said that such threats would never change the thinking of the Greeks.

### 希腊首相谴责劳埃德  重申希腊在塞浦路斯的主权

1956-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    希腊首相谴责劳埃德
    重申希腊在塞浦路斯的主权
    新华社22日讯　雅典消息：希腊首相卡拉曼利斯二十一日发表声明，谴责英国外交大臣劳埃德在英国下院关于塞浦路斯问题的发言。
    卡拉曼利斯指出：英国借口由于土耳其反对而暂时不能给予塞浦路斯自决，这是不能容忍的。希腊在塞浦路斯的主权不能变更，不单国际条约上规定了这一点，绝大多数希腊居民的情绪也证明了这一点。
    卡拉曼利斯斥责土耳其对塞浦路斯的要求纯粹是“敲诈”，他嘲笑土耳其有关“武装冲突”的威胁。他说，这种威胁绝不能改变希腊人的思想。
