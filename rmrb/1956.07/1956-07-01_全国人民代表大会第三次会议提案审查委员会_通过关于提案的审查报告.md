Title: The Third Session of the First National People's Congress Proposal Examination Committee Passes the Examination Report on Proposals
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 30th dispatch. The Proposal Examination Committee of the Third Session of the First National People's Congress held its sixth meeting today and passed the examination report on proposals.

After the Proposal Examination Committee completed the examination report on proposals at its third meeting, the Third Session of the First National People's Congress received nineteen more proposals from the representatives. Therefore, the Proposal Examination Committee continued to meet and reviewed these proposals one by one.

After the Proposal Examination Committee put forward examination opinions on all the proposals, it submitted these examination opinions to the various delegation groups for discussion. The representatives generally agreed with these examination opinions, but some representatives raised different opinions on the examination opinions of individual proposals. The Proposal Examination Committee then held a plenary meeting to re-discuss the matter and made necessary revisions to the original examination opinions on individual proposals.

Therefore, the Proposal Examination Committee also made some revisions to the examination report on proposals. The examination report on proposals passed at today's meeting of the Proposal Examination Committee was submitted to the Third Session of the First National People's Congress this afternoon, and the draft examination opinions on proposals passed by the Proposal Examination Committee were submitted to the Congress for deliberation.

### 全国人民代表大会第三次会议提案审查委员会  通过关于提案的审查报告

1956-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会第三次会议提案审查委员会
    通过关于提案的审查报告
    新华社30日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议提案审查委员会今天举行第六次会议，通过关于提案的审查报告。
    在提案审查委员会第三次会议所完成的关于提案的审查报告后，第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议又收到代表提案十九件。提案审查委员会因此继续举行了会议，对这些提案逐案进行了审议。
    提案审查委员会在对所有提案提出了审查意见后，曾将这些审查意见交各代表小组讨论。代表们对这些审查意见基本上表示同意，但也有的代表对个别提案的审查意见，提出不同的意见。提案审查委员会就此又举行全体会议重加讨论，对原来的个别提案审查意见作了必要的修改。
    提案审查委员会因此对提案的审查报告，也作了部分修改。提案审查委员会今天会议上通过的这个关于提案的审查报告，已在今天下午向第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议提出，并且把提案审查委员会通过的提案审查意见草案提请大会审议。
