Title: Tunisian Neo Destour Party Statement Supports the Algerian People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1956-07-08
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, 6th - The Political Bureau of the Tunisian ruling party, Neo Destour, issued a statement yesterday condemning the bloody colonial war being waged in Algeria and expressing the solidarity of the Tunisian people with the Algerian people. The Neo Destour Political Bureau stated that it issued this statement to commemorate the 126th anniversary of the French invasion of Algeria. The statement believes that the Algerian people have the right to choose their form of government and pays tribute to the Algerian people's struggle.

### 突尼斯新宪政党声明  支持阿尔及利亚人民斗争

1956-07-08
第6版()
专栏：

    突尼斯新宪政党声明
    支持阿尔及利亚人民斗争
    新华社6日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯执政党新宪政党政治局昨天发表声明，谴责在阿尔及利亚进行的血腥的殖民战争，并且表示突尼斯人民同阿尔及利亚人民团结一致。新宪政党政治局说，它用这篇声明来纪念法军侵入阿尔及利亚一百二十六周年。这篇声明认为阿尔及利亚人民有权选择他们的政府的形式，并且对阿尔及利亚人民的斗争致敬。
