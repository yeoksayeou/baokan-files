Title: 101 Factories and Mines in Shanxi Province Reach Next Year's Total Output Level
Author:
Date: 1956-07-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, 5th: As of the end of June, 101 local state-owned and joint state-private factories and mines in Shanxi Province had successively reached the 1957 production total value level ahead of schedule. These factories and mines account for more than 55% of the total number of factories and mines under the Shanxi Provincial Department of Industry included in the Five-Year Plan.

Among the 45 major products included in the Five-Year Plan, 24 have already reached the 1957 output level ahead of schedule. Among them, raw coal production exceeded the output stipulated in the last year of the Five-Year Plan by 410,000 tons, cotton yarn production increased by more than 11,000 pieces, and cotton cloth production increased by more than 3,700 meters.

In the first half of this year, the local state-owned and joint state-private industrial system of Shanxi Province extensively carried out advanced producer movements and universally promoted many effective advanced experiences in China, thereby greatly improving equipment utilization and increasing production.

### 山西省101个厂矿  达到明年的总产值水平

1956-07-06
第2版()
专栏：

    山西省101个厂矿
    达到明年的总产值水平
    据新华社太原5日电　截至六月底为止，山西省有一百零一个地方国营和公私合营工厂矿山，已经先后提前达到了1957年的生产总值水平。这些工厂矿山，占山西省工业厅所属列入五年计划工厂矿山总数的55％以上。
    列入五年计划的四十五种主要产品当中，已经有二十四种提前达到了1957年的产量水平。其中：原煤比五年计划规定的最后一年的产量，多生产了四十一万吨，棉纱增产了一万一千多件，棉布增长了三千七百多公尺。
    山西省地方国营工业和公私合营工业系统，今年上半年广泛地开展了先进生产者运动，并且普遍推广了国内许多行之有效的先进经验，因而大大提高了设备的利用率，增加了生产。
