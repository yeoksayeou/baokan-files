Title: Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session; Moghioroș Reports on Socialist Transformation of Agriculture
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-21
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, July 20th, dispatch from Bucharest by TASS: The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party held a plenary session on July 16th and 17th.

    Moghioroș, First Vice-Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers, delivered a report at the meeting on measures to implement the tasks stipulated by the Second Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party regarding the socialist transformation of agriculture. After discussion, the plenary session unanimously adopted the corresponding resolution.

    Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, reported to the meeting on the talks held between the Yugoslav government delegation headed by Tito and the Romanian government delegation.

    The plenary session expressed satisfaction with the results of the talks reflected in the joint statement of the two governments and the communiqué on the relations between the Romanian Workers' Party and the League of Communists of Yugoslavia.

### 罗马尼亚工人党中央举行全会  莫吉奥罗希作关于农业社会主义改造的报告

1956-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚工人党中央举行全会
    莫吉奥罗希作关于农业社会主义改造的报告
    新华社20日讯　塔斯社布加勒斯特十九日讯：罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会在七月十六日和十七日举行了全体会议。
    罗马尼亚部长会议第一副主席莫吉奥罗希在会上做了关于实现罗马尼亚工人党第二次代表大会在农业社会主义改造方面所规定的任务的措施的报告。全体会议经过讨论后一致通过了相应的决议。
    罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治乌—德治向会议作了关于以铁托为首的南斯拉夫政府代表团同罗马尼亚政府代表团举行会谈的报告。
    全体会议对于反映在两国政府联合声明及罗马尼亚工人党和南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟之间关系的公报中的会谈结果表示满意。
