Title: Argentine Legal Workers Delegation Holds Reception to Celebrate Argentine Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1956-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Prominent figures from all walks of life in China today joined Argentine friends in Beijing to celebrate Argentina's National Day—the 1816 Independence Day.

    The host of the reception—Mr. F.M. Pita, head of the Argentine legal workers delegation—said in his speech to the guests that the Argentine legal workers delegation was extremely pleased to have the opportunity today to celebrate the festival of Argentina's formal declaration of independence together with friends from China, Brazil, and Chile. He raised his glass to the happiness of the Chinese and Latin American people, to the development of economic and cultural relations between China and Argentina, and hoped that China and Argentina would establish normal diplomatic relations as soon as possible.

    Qian Duansheng, Vice President of the Chinese Political Science and Law Association, then delivered a speech. He pointed out that in the 140 years since the Argentine people threw off Spanish colonial rule in 1816, they have waged unremitting struggles for independence and freedom, and for a better and happier life. Qian Duansheng asked the delegation to convey the friendly feelings of the Chinese people to the Argentine people after returning to their country, and wished the Argentine people prosperity and happiness.

    Frias of Chile, representing the legal workers delegations of Brazil and Chile, extended congratulations to the Argentine friends.

    Also attending today's reception were Zhang Zhiran and Wu Defeng, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Political Science and Law Association, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, as well as heads of various people's organizations and prominent figures from all walks of life.

### 阿根廷法律工作者代表团举行酒会  庆祝阿根廷独立纪念日

1956-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    阿根廷法律工作者代表团举行酒会
    庆祝阿根廷独立纪念日
    新华社9日讯　中国社会各界著名人士同阿根廷的友人们今天在北京共同庆祝阿根廷国庆日——1816年独立纪念日。
    酒会的主人——阿根廷法律工作者代表团团长弗·姆·皮塔向来宾致词说，阿根廷法律工作者代表团对于今天有机会同中国、巴西和智利的朋友们一起庆祝阿根廷正式宣布独立的这个节日，感到极大的愉快。他举杯祝中国人民和拉丁美洲人民的幸福，祝中阿两国的经济、文化关系的发展，并且希望中阿两国早日建立正常的外交关系。
    中国政治法律学会副会长钱端升接着致词。他指出阿根廷人民在1816年摆脱了西班牙的殖民统治以来的一百四十年中，为了独立和自由，为了美好幸福的生活进行了不懈的斗争。钱端升请代表团回国后向阿根廷人民转达中国人民的友好感情，并且祝阿根廷人民繁荣和幸福。
    智利的弗里亚斯，代表巴西和智利两国的法律工作者代表团，向阿根廷友人们致贺。
    参加今天酒会的，还有中国政治法律学会副会长张志让、吴德峰，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记曹孟君，以及各人民团体负责人和各界著名人士。
