Title: Ambassador Luo Guibo Holds Reception to Celebrate the Signing of the Sino-Vietnamese Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 27th – Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, Luo Guibo, held a reception this evening to celebrate the signing of the 1956 Sino-Vietnamese Trade Agreement and the Protocols on Chinese Assistance to Vietnam. Ambassador Luo Guibo said at the banquet that the signing of the trade agreement and various assistance protocols between China and Vietnam is a major event in the common undertaking of economic upsurge in both countries. Luo Guibo believes that the prospects for economic and trade relations and friendly cooperation between China and Vietnam are extremely bright and splendid.

Deputy Prime Minister Phan Ke Toai of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam delivered a speech at the banquet. He spoke about the long-standing traditional friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese people. He said that the signing of this trade agreement and the protocols proves that the friendship between the two peoples is developing day by day.

### 庆祝中越贸易协定签字  罗贵波大使举行招待会

1956-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝中越贸易协定签字
    罗贵波大使举行招待会
    据新华社河内27日电　中国驻越南大使罗贵波今天晚上举行招待会，庆祝1956年中越两国贸易协定和中国对越南各项援助议定书的签订。罗贵波大使在宴会上说，中越两国间的贸易协定和各项援助议定书的签订，在中越两国经济共同高涨的事业中是一个重大的事件。罗贵波认为，中越两国之间的经济贸易关系和友好合作的远景是极其光辉灿烂的。
    越南民主共和国政府副总理潘继遂在宴会上致辞。他谈到了中越人民悠久的传统友谊。他说，这次贸易协定和议定书的签订证明了两国人民的友谊正在日益发展。
