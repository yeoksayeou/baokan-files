Title: Latex Products Factory Goes Into Production
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 14th - The newly built state-owned No. 11 Rubber Factory in Guangzhou has gone into production. This rubber factory is China's first modern factory for manufacturing latex products.

The factory's main products include surgical gloves for medical use, various balloons for measuring clouds and wind in meteorological work, and nipples for infant feeding.

### 乳胶制品厂投入生产

1956-07-17
第2版()
专栏：

    乳胶制品厂投入生产
    新华社广州14日电　在广州市新建的国营第十一橡胶厂已经投入生产。这座橡胶厂是中国第一座近代化的制造乳胶制品的工厂。
    这个厂的主要产品有医疗上作手术用的手套，气象工作中用以测云、测风的各种气球，和给婴儿喂奶用的奶嘴。
