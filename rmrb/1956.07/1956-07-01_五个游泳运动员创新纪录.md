Title: Five Swimmers Set New Records
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency: In the 1956 Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Chinese People's Liberation Army Four-Unit Swimming Competition held in Shanghai starting on June 29th, the famous athlete Mu Xiangxiong achieved a time of 1 minute 10.4 seconds in the men's 100-meter breaststroke, surpassing his own national record of 1 minute 10.6 seconds set in April of this year.

    Beijing athlete Wang Qiangli and Chinese People's Liberation Army athlete Chen Yunpeng broke the national record of 2 minutes 46.1 seconds in the men's 200-meter butterfly, with times of 2 minutes 43.8 seconds and 2 minutes 45 seconds respectively.

    Fu Cuimei, the national record holder for the women's 100-meter backstroke, and Dai Lihua, the national record holder for the women's 200-meter breaststroke, set new national records of 1 minute 23.2 seconds and 3 minutes 3.6 seconds respectively in this competition.

### 五个游泳运动员创新纪录

1956-07-01
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    五个游泳运动员创新纪录
    据新华社讯　在六月二十九日开始在上海举行的1956年北京、上海、广州和中国人民解放军四单位游泳对抗赛中，著名运动员穆祥雄在男子一百公尺蛙泳比赛中的成绩达到了一分十秒四，超过了今年四月间他所创造的一分十秒六的全国新成绩。
    北京市运动员王强立和中国人民解放军运动员陈云鹏，在男子二百公尺蝶泳比赛中分别以二分四十三秒八和二分四十五秒打破了二分四十六秒一的全国纪录。
    女子一百公尺仰泳全国纪录保持者傅翠美和女子二百公尺蛙泳全国纪录保持者戴丽华，在这次比赛中，分别创造了一分二十三秒二和三分三秒六的全国新纪录。
