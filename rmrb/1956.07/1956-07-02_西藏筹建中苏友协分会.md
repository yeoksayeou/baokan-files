Title: Tibet Preparing to Establish Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Branch
Author:
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 30th. Tibet has begun preparations to establish a branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in Tibet and a branch of the Buddhist Association of China in Tibet. This was decided at the fifth meeting of the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region yesterday.

    After the Standing Committee Chairman, the Dalai Lama, read the "Proposal on the Establishment of the Tibet Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association," the attending Standing Committee members unanimously raised their hands and passed the proposal. They also passed the "Proposal on the Establishment of the Tibet Branch of the Buddhist Association of China" read by the Dalai Lama.

### 西藏筹建中苏友协分会

1956-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

    西藏筹建中苏友协分会
    据新华社拉萨30日电　西藏开始筹备建立中苏友好协会西藏分会和中国佛教协会西藏分会。这是昨天举行的西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员会第五次会议决定的。
    在常务委员会主任委员达赖喇嘛宣读了“关于成立中苏友好协会西藏分会”的议案以后，到会的常务委员一致举手通过了这个议案。他们并通过了达赖喇嘛宣读的“关于成立中国佛教协会西藏分会”议案。
