Title: US and British Government Officials Advocate Continuing Nuclear Weapons Testing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: According to a Washington dispatch from the Associated Press: American officials on the 16th rejected the Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov's proposal that the United States, Britain, and the Soviet Union conclude an agreement to halt nuclear weapons testing. Officials in Washington stated that the United States opposes any plan to stop atomic and hydrogen weapon testing.

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: London dispatch: London officials said on the 16th that Britain intends to test its first hydrogen bomb next year, and therefore it is highly unlikely that it will agree to the Soviet Union's proposal to ban nuclear weapons testing.

### 美英两国政府官员主张继续试验核子武器

1956-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

    美英两国政府官员主张继续试验核子武器
    据新华社17日讯　据美联社华盛顿消息：美国官员十六日拒绝了苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫提出的美、英、苏三国缔结停止核子武器试验协定的建议。华盛顿的官员说，美国反对任何关于停止原子武器和氢武器试验的计划。
    据新华社17日讯　伦敦消息：伦敦官方人士十六日说，英国打算在明年试验它的第一颗氢弹，因此极不可能同意苏联的关于禁止试验核子武器的建议。
