Title: Wu Yu Repeatedly Breaks Three National Records
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency Report: Amateur sports enthusiast Wu Yu broke the 1955 national record in the women's 5,000-meter individual cycling event held in Guangzhou on June 24 with a time of nine minutes and fifty-six seconds.

　　When Wu Yu participated in the first Guangdong Provincial Games this year, she broke the national records for the women's 1,500-meter and 3,000-meter cycling events with times of two minutes, fifty-two and eight-tenths seconds and six minutes and four seconds respectively. At the first Guangzhou Municipal Education Workers' Track and Field and Cycling Games and the 1956 Guangzhou Municipal Track and Field, Gymnastics, and Cycling Games held in May, Wu Yu further improved her times for the women's 1,500-meter and 3,000-meter cycling events to two minutes, forty-three and four-tenths seconds and five minutes, forty-eight and five-tenths seconds.

### 吴郁连破三项全国纪录

1956-07-01
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    吴郁连破三项全国纪录
    据新华社讯　业余体育爱好者吴郁，六月二十四日在广州举行的女子五千公尺自行车单项比赛中，以九分五十六秒的成绩打破了1955年的全国最高纪录。
    吴郁在今年元旦参加广东省第一届体育运动大会时，曾经以二分五十二秒八和六分四秒的成绩分别打破了女子自行车一千五百公尺和三千公尺的全国最高纪录，在五月间举行的广州市第一届教育工作者田径、自行车运动大会和广州市1956年田径、体操、自行车运动大会上，吴郁又把女子自行车一千五百公尺和三千公尺的成绩提高到二分四十三秒四和五分四十八秒五。
