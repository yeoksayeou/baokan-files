Title: Harbin Electric Meter Instrument Factory Starts Production: An Average of One Meter Produced Every Twenty Seconds
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 29th: The basic construction project of the Harbin Electric Meter Instrument Factory has been completed and accepted. At 5:00 PM today, the members of the National Acceptance Committee signed the acceptance certificate with satisfaction.

The acceptance certificate states: The engineering quality of this factory is excellent, the technology is advanced, the technical equipment and production organization meet modern technical requirements, and the quality of civil construction and equipment installation projects also meets quality standards.

The construction project of the Harbin Electric Meter Instrument Factory started in April 1954 and was basically completed in May of this year, lasting two years and one month. During the construction of this factory, due to the adoption of 68% of domestic equipment and the implementation of effective conservation measures, the construction investment was saved by more than 3.04 million yuan compared to the original plan.

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 30th: The modern Harbin Electric Meter Instrument Factory, which can produce an average of one meter every twenty seconds, officially went into production today. This morning and afternoon, ribbon-cutting ceremonies and grand rallies celebrating the start of production were held respectively.

### 哈尔滨电表仪器厂开工生产　平均每二十秒钟能出产一只仪表

1956-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨电表仪器厂开工生产
    平均每二十秒钟能出产一只仪表
    新华社哈尔滨29日电　哈尔滨电表仪器厂基本建设工程验收完毕。今天下午五时，国家验收委员会委员们满意地在验收鉴定书上签了字。
    验收鉴定书上写道：这个厂的工程质量是优等的，工艺是先进的，技术装备和生产组织合乎现代化的技术要求，土木建筑和设备安装工程的质量也符合质量标准。
    哈尔滨电表仪器厂的建设工程，从1954年4月动工到今年五月基本建成，历时两年零一个月。这个厂在建设当中，由于采用了68％的国内设备，并且采取有效的节约措施，使建设投资比原计划节约了三百零四万多元。
    据新华社哈尔滨30日电　平均每二十秒钟能出产一只仪表的现代化哈尔滨电表仪器厂，今天正式投入生产。今天上午和下午，分别举行了开工剪彩仪式和庆祝开工生产的典礼大会。
