Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1956-07-10
Page: 4

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    The Organizational Regulations of the People's Congress and the People's Committee of Jianghua Yao Autonomous County, Hunan Province, have been approved by the 43rd meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on July 9, 1956, and are hereby promulgated.
                 Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
                          July 9, 1956

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1956-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    湖南省江华瑶族自治县人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于1956年7月9日第四十三次会议批准，现予公布。
                 中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                          1956年7月9日
