Title: British Conservative Party Member of Parliament Says North Atlantic Treaty is a Threat
Author:
Date: 1956-07-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 23rd (TASS dispatch): British Conservative Party Member of Parliament Viscount Hinchingbrooke, in a letter to the "Daily Telegraph and Morning Post," stated that "reducing (Britain's) obligations at home and abroad, which are diplomatically superfluous and militarily outdated, is now a matter of urgent necessity." He emphasized that, given the current developments, the North Atlantic Treaty, "as a tool for promoting continental diplomacy," "is now a threat."

### 英国保守党一议员说  北大西洋公约是一种威胁

1956-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

    英国保守党一议员说
    北大西洋公约是一种威胁
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社伦敦二十三日消息：英国保守党议员欣琴布鲁克子爵在写给“每日电讯与晨邮报”的一封信中说，“减少（英国）在国内外所担负的那种在外交方面是多余的、在军事方面是过时的义务，现在已经是迫切需要的事情了。”他着重指出，由于现在局势的发展，“作为推动大陆外交的工具的”北大西洋公约“现在是一种威胁”。
