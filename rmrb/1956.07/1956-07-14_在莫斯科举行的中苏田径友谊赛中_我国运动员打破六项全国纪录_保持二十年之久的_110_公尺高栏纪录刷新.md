Title: Chinese Athletes Break Six National Records at the Sino-Soviet Track and Field Friendship Competition in Moscow; 20-Year-Old 110-Meter Hurdles Record Broken
Author:
Date: 1956-07-14
Page: 1

    Our Newspaper, Moscow, 13th - Today, at the Dynamo Stadium in Moscow, the track and field friendship competition between Chinese athletes and athletes from the Soviet Central Asian republics was held. Our athletes broke six national records here.
    Our athlete Zhou Lianli achieved a time of 15.4 seconds in the 110-meter hurdles, breaking the national record that had been held for 20 years. (The original record was 15.7 seconds, set by Huang Yingjie in 1936). The other five events in which national records were broken are: women's javelin, Wang Yi, with a score of 40.70 meters, breaking the national record of 38.84 meters set by our athlete Zhou Jian on December 25, 1955; men's high jump, Li Dapei, with a score of 1.92 meters, breaking the national record of 1.89 meters and reaching the standard of a Master of Sports of the People's Republic of China; men's 800 meters, Li Zhonglin, with a score of 1 minute 53.8 seconds, breaking the national record of 1 minute 54.1 seconds; women's 400 meters, Jiang Yumin, with a score of 59.2 seconds, breaking the national record of 59.4 seconds; men's 4x100 meter relay, with a score of 43.3 seconds, breaking the national record of 43.5 seconds.

### 在莫斯科举行的中苏田径友谊赛中  我国运动员打破六项全国纪录  保持二十年之久的110公尺高栏纪录刷新

1956-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    在莫斯科举行的中苏田径友谊赛中
    我国运动员打破六项全国纪录
    保持二十年之久的110公尺高栏纪录刷新
    本报莫斯科13日电　今天在莫斯科狄纳摩运动场上举行了我国运动员和苏联中亚各加盟共和国运动员的田径友谊比赛，我国运动员在这里打破了六项全国最高纪录。
    我国运动员周连立一百一十公尺高栏成绩十五秒四，打破了保持二十年之久的全国最高纪录。（原来的纪录为十五秒七，是1936年由黄英杰创造的）。其他打破全国最高纪录的五个项目是：女子标枪王毅，成绩四十公尺七十，打破了1955年12月25日我国运动员周健创造的三十八公尺八四的全国最高纪录；男子跳高李大培成绩一公尺九二，打破了一公尺八九的全国最高纪录，达到中华人民共和国运动健将的标准；男子八百公尺李中林成绩一分五十三秒八，打破了一分五十四秒一的全国最高纪录；女子四百公尺姜玉民成绩五十九秒二，打破了五十九秒四的全国最高纪录；男子四百公尺接力成绩四十三秒三，打破了四十三秒五的全国最高纪录。
