Title: Merger of Two Austrian Youth Organizations
Author:
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 25th: Vienna Dispatch: The Austrian "Socialist Labor Youth League" and the "Free Austrian Youth League" have decided to merge. After holding a meeting, representatives of the two youth organizations passed a resolution on the merger, which stated: "The economic and social conditions of Austrian young workers make it necessary for them to unite and consolidate their forces to fight for their rights. Both the 'Socialist Labor Youth League' and the 'Free Austrian Youth League' believe that their task is to mobilize Austrian youth to strive for a better life and fight for socialism."

### 奥两青年组织合并

1956-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    奥两青年组织合并
    新华社柏林25日电　维也纳消息：奥地利“社会主义劳动青年联盟”和“自由奥地利青年联盟”决定合并。这两个青年组织的代表们在举行会议以后通过了一项关于合并的决议，其中说：“奥地利青年工人的经济和社会状况使他们有必要统一和团结力量，为争取自己的权利而奋斗。‘社会主义劳动青年联盟’和‘自由奥地利青年联盟’都认为，它们的任务是发动奥地利青年为争取美好生活，争取社会主义奋斗。”
