Compilation of Historical Documents on African Publishing
    Recently, Africa published the first volume of a historical document compilation related to East Africa and Central Africa. This book consists of twenty volumes, including documents from the historical period of 1487 to the early 19th century. The chief editor of this book is Portuguese historian Dr. Alexandre Lobato. The publisher is the Central African Archives and Historical Archives of Mozambique. The first volume contains historical documents from the period of 1487-1505. The first volume will be released simultaneously in English and Portuguese versions. (Wan)

### 非洲出版历史文献汇编

1956-07-22
第7版()
专栏：

    非洲出版历史文献汇编
    近来非洲出版了有关东非和中非的历史文献汇编第一卷。这部书共二十卷，包括1487年到十九世纪初的这段历史时期的文献。这部书的主编是葡萄牙史学家亚历山大·洛巴托博士。出版者为莫占必克的中非档案馆和历史档案馆。第一卷收有1487—1505年这段时期的历史文献。第一卷将同时发行英文版和葡萄牙文版。　　　　（万）
