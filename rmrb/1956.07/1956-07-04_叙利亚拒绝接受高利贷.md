Title: Syria Refuses to Accept Usury
Author:
Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 30th (TASS) - According to news from Damascus, the Syrian government has refused a loan from the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development, which is closely linked to American financial groups, because the bank's administrative authorities put forward unacceptable conditions. The news said that the bank demanded excessively high interest rates.

### 叙利亚拒绝接受高利贷

1956-07-04
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚拒绝接受高利贷
    新华社1日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特六月三十日讯：据来自大马士革的消息说，叙利亚政府拒绝了同美国财政集团有密切联系的国际复兴开发银行的贷款，因为银行的行政当局提出了难以接受的条件。消息说，这家银行要求过高的利息。
