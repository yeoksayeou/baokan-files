Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Issues Statement Supporting Premier Zhou's Proposal for Peaceful Resolution of the Taiwan Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-07-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 5th, Bangkok dispatch: Indonesian Foreign Minister Abdulgani said on July 3rd that Indonesia supports Premier Zhou Enlai's proposal for a peaceful resolution of the Taiwan issue.

He said that Premier Zhou Enlai's statement is worthy of attention, and it is a reassuring sign. Indonesia would like to see it become a reality because if a peaceful resolution of the Taiwan issue can be achieved, it will greatly contribute to world peace and ease tensions in the Far East.

Abdulgani made these remarks to the Thai press while accompanying President Sukarno on his way home through Bangkok.

The Indonesian Foreign Minister said that whether a peaceful resolution of the Taiwan issue through negotiations can be achieved depends on the attitude of the Taiwan side. If the Taiwan authorities agree, a peaceful solution to the Taiwan issue through negotiations will be successful. He said that the facts prove that China is committed to world peace, so Indonesia supports its proposition for the peaceful liberation of Taiwan. He believes that the movements of a country with 600 million people naturally have a great impact on the world situation, and China is capable of contributing to world peace.

When talking about China's status in the United Nations, Abdulgani said that Indonesia absolutely supports China's entry into the United Nations. China's hope of entering the United Nations is growing stronger.

### 印度尼西亚外长发表谈话  支持周总理关于和平解决台湾问题建议

1956-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外长发表谈话
    支持周总理关于和平解决台湾问题建议
    新华社5日讯　曼谷消息：印度尼西亚外交部长阿卜杜加尼在七月三日说，印度尼西亚支持周恩来总理关于和平解决台湾问题的建议。
    他说，周恩来总理的发言是值得重视的，这是一种使人感到欣慰的表现，印度尼西亚愿意看到它成为事实，因为如果能实现和平解决台湾问题，将大大有助于世界和平，同时也将和缓远东的紧张局势。
    阿卜杜加尼是随同苏加诺总统在归国途中经过曼谷的时候对泰国报界发表这番谈话的。
    这位印度尼西亚外交部长说，以谈判方式和平解决台湾问题是否能实现，须看台湾方面的态度，如果台湾当局同意，和谈解决台湾问题将是能获致成功的。他说，事实证明，中国正在致力于世界和平，所以印度尼西亚支持它的和平解放台湾的主张。他认为，像这样拥有六亿人民的国家，它的动向对世界局势当然有极大影响，中国是能够为世界和平尽力的。
    阿卜杜加尼在谈到中国在联合国中的地位问题的时候说，印度尼西亚绝对支持中国进入联合国。中国进入联合国的希望正日益浓厚。
