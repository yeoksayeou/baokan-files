Title: Chinese Peking Opera Troupe Returning to Beijing After Visit to Japan
Author:
Date: 1956-07-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 26th: The Chinese Peking Opera Troupe, led by the renowned artists Mei Lanfang and Ouyang Yuqian, has returned to Beijing in batches by train yesterday and today.

The Chinese Peking Opera Troupe's performances in Japan were warmly welcomed by the Japanese people. After all members of the delegation arrived in Tokyo at the end of May, they performed thirty-two shows in major cities such as Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka, Nagoya, Fukuoka, and Yawata, with an audience of over 70,000 people. In addition, more than ten million people watched the troupe's performances on television.

### 中国访日京剧代表团回京

1956-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中国访日京剧代表团回京
    据新华社26日讯　由著名艺术家梅兰芳、欧阳予倩率领的中国访日京剧代表团，已经在昨天和今天分批乘火车回到北京。
    中国京剧团在日本的访问演出受到日本人民的热烈欢迎。代表团人员于五月底全部到达东京以后，先后在东京、京都、大阪、名古屋、福冈、八幡等大城市演出了三十二场，观众达七万多人。此外，还有一千多万人通过电视欣赏了代表团的演出。
