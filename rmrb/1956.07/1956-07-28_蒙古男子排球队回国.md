Title: Mongolian Men's Volleyball Team Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency News: The men's volleyball team of the Mongolian People's Republic's Cooperative Sports Association concluded its visit to China and left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 26th to return home. Since arriving in China on July 3, they have visited Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang, and Changchun. In the seven friendly matches held with Chinese volleyball teams, they won two games.

### 蒙古男子排球队回国

1956-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古男子排球队回国
    新华社讯　蒙古人民共和国合作社体育协会男子排球队结束了在中国的访问，二十六日早晨坐飞机离开北京回国。他们从七月三日到中国以后，先后访问了北京、天津、沈阳和长春。他们在同中国的排球队举行的七场友谊比赛中，胜了两场。
