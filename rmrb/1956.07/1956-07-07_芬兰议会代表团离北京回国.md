Title: Finnish Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The members of the Finnish parliamentary delegation, after concluding their visit to China, left Beijing for home this morning. At Beijing Airport, they sang the Finnish national anthem to pay respect and bid farewell to their Chinese hosts.

The delegation had visited Changchun, Shenyang, Anshan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places. The head of the delegation, the Speaker of the Finnish Parliament, Mr. Sukselainen, said in a speech at the airport: "Thank you for everything you have done for us and for strengthening the friendship between our two countries in the past two weeks."

Before boarding the plane, the distinguished guests accepted flowers presented by Beijing youth. Chairman Liu Shaoqi asked Speaker Sukselainen to convey his greetings to President Kekkonen and other national leaders of Finland. Speaker Sukselainen also asked Chairman Liu Shaoqi to convey his greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong on his behalf.

Those who came to the airport to see them off today included Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, and members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs and other ministers and vice ministers of various ministries, heads of various people's organizations, and well-known figures from all walks of life.

Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Sundström, and Mongolian Ambassador to China, Mr. Ochirbat, also went to the airport to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The full text of the speech by Mr. Sukselainen, the head of the Finnish parliamentary delegation and Speaker of the Finnish Parliament, at Beijing Airport is as follows:

Dear Chinese friends:

I am both sorry and grateful. I am very sorry to have taken up the time of the dear friends’ sweet morning sleep today. Thank you for everything you have done for us and for strengthening the friendship between our two countries in the past two weeks. At the same time, I am grateful that you have given us the opportunity to thank you on such a clear morning.

I believe that the cooperation between the parliaments of Finland and China, which has now begun, will strengthen democratic ways of thinking and promote international mutual understanding. Therefore, we look forward to the day when we can welcome Chinese friends to visit Finland.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi, please allow me to once again express my personal gratitude to you for your initiative in starting cooperation between the two parliaments, and for all your hospitality and kindness. Let us sing our national anthem to pay tribute to you at this moment of departure!

### 芬兰议会代表团离北京回国

1956-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰议会代表团离北京回国
    新华社6日讯　芬兰议会访华代表团团员们今天在北京机场以高唱芬兰国歌，向中国主人致敬和辞别。代表团是在结束了中国的访问后，于今天上午离开北京回国的。
    代表团曾访问过长春、沈阳、鞍山、南京、上海、杭州等地。代表团团长、芬兰议会议长苏克舍拉宁在机场上发表讲话时说：“对你们在过去两星期内，为了我们，并且为了加强贵我两国之间的友谊所作的一切表示感谢。”
    贵宾们在登机前，接受了北京青年们的献花。刘少奇委员长请苏克舍拉宁议长向吉科宁总统以及芬兰的其他国家领导人问候。苏克舍拉宁议长也请刘少奇委员长代为向毛泽东主席致意。
    今天，到机场欢送的还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、彭真、陈叔通和委员们，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，外交部副部长张闻天和其他各部部长、副部长，各人民团体负责人和各界著名人士。
    芬兰驻华大使孙士敦和蒙古驻华大使奥其尔巴特也到机场欢送。
    新华社6日讯　芬兰议会访华代表团团长、芬兰议会议长苏克舍拉宁在北京机场上的讲话，全文如下：亲爱的中国朋友们：
    我既抱歉又感谢。我很抱歉，今天侵占了亲爱的朋友们甜蜜的早上睡眠的时间。对你们在过去两星期内为了我们，并为了加强贵我两国之间的友谊所作的一切表示感谢。同时，我感激你们在这样的清晨还给予我们致谢的机会。
    我相信，芬中两国议会间现在已经开始了的合作，将加强民主的思想方法和增进国际间的相互了解。因此，我们期待着欢迎中国朋友们到芬兰来作客的一天。
    刘少奇委员长，请允许我再一次对您为开始两国议会间的合作所作的倡议，对您的一切盛情和款待表示我个人的谢意。让我们在这离别的时刻唱我们的国歌向你们致敬！
