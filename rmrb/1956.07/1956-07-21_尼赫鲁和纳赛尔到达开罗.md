Title: Nehru and Nasser Arrive in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1956-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 20th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru and Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser arrived in Cairo together by plane this morning from Yugoslavia.

Nehru is scheduled to stay in Cairo for one day and leave Egypt on July 21st to return home. During his stay in Cairo, Nehru will hold talks with Gamal Abdel Nasser.

### 尼赫鲁和纳赛尔到达开罗

1956-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁和纳赛尔到达开罗
    新华社20日讯　开罗消息：印度总理尼赫鲁和埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔今天上午一起乘飞机从南斯拉夫到达开罗。
    尼赫鲁预定在开罗停留一天，在七月二十一日离开埃及回国。在开罗期间，尼赫鲁同阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔将举行会谈。
