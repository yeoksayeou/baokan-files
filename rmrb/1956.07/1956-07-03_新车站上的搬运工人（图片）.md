Title: Stevedores at the New Railway Station (Photo)
Author: Yao Zongyi
Date: 1956-07-03
Page: 2

    Stevedores at the New Railway Station
    The Baoji-Fengxian section of the northern section of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway has begun to handle temporary freight transport. This is the first time stevedores have appeared at Fengzhou Station, loading rice onto trains, ready for transport north.
                                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Yao Zongyi photo

### 新车站上的搬运工人（图片）
姚宗仪
1956-07-03

专栏：

    新车站上的搬运工人
    宝成铁路北段宝鸡——凤县段开始承办临时货运。这是凤州车站第一次出现的搬运工人把大米搬上火车，准备北运。
