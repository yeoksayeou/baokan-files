Title: China and the Soviet Union Sign Supplementary Goods Exchange Protocol for This Year
Author:
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 25th. China and the Soviet Union have successfully concluded negotiations on the mutual supply of goods for 1956, and signed the Sino-Soviet 1956 Supplementary Goods Exchange Protocol in Beijing on the 25th.

    The protocol stipulates that, in addition to the goods mutually supplied as stipulated in the Sino-Soviet 1956 Goods Exchange Protocol signed on December 27, 1955, the Soviet Union will additionally supply China with metal cutting machine tools, drilling equipment, civil engineering machinery, lifting equipment, compressors, pumps, diesel engines, generators, automobiles, agricultural machinery, various instruments, ferrous metals, and other goods needed for China's national economic construction. The People's Republic of China will additionally supply the Soviet Union with sulfur, mercury, caustic soda, soda ash, rice, tea, woolen cloth, woolen textiles, sewn products, leather raw materials, and other industrial raw materials and agricultural and sideline products.

    Jiang Ming, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the protocol on behalf of the Chinese side, and Yelemin, Commercial Representative of the Soviet Embassy in China, signed the protocol on behalf of the Soviet Union.

    Those who attended the signing ceremony from the Chinese side were: Li Zheren, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and East European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and relevant bureau directors of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, etc.

    Those who attended from the Soviet side were: Li Haiqiu, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, and other responsible personnel.

### 中苏签订今年补充换货议定书

1956-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏签订今年补充换货议定书
    新华社25日讯　中苏两国关于1956年相互供应货物的谈判已经顺利结束，二十五日在北京签订了中苏1956年补充换货议定书。
    这个议定书规定，在1955年12月27日所签订的中苏1956年换货议定书所规定的互相供应的货物以外，苏联将补充供给中国金属切削机床、钻探设备、土建机械、起重设备、压缩机、泵、柴油机、发电机、汽车、农业机械、各种仪器、黑色金属及为中国国民经济建设所需要的其他货物，而中华人民共和国将补充供给苏联硫磺、水银、烧碱、纯碱、大米、茶叶、呢绒、毛织品、缝纫制品、皮革原料及其它工业原料和农土产品等。
    代表中国方面在议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长江明，代表苏联在议定书上签字的是苏联驻华大使馆商务代表叶列敏。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长李哲人，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清，及对外贸易部有关局长等。
    苏联方面有：苏联驻华大使馆临时代办李海秋等负责人员。
