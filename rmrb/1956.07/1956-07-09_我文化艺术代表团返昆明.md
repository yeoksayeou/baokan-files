Title: Our Cultural and Art Delegation Returns to Kunming
Author:
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, July 7th: The majority of the members of the People's Republic of China's Cultural and Art Delegation returned to Kunming in batches from the 4th to the 6th, after visiting the Middle East. The delegation departed at the end of February, and successively made friendly visits and performances in five countries including Egypt, Sudan, Ethiopia, Syria, and Lebanon, and signed cultural exchange and cooperation agreements with the governments of Egypt and Syria.

During the visit and performance period, the delegation had friendly conversations with leaders of the above five countries, and had extensive contact with people from all walks of life including literature, art, education, religion, and journalism.

### 我文化艺术代表团返昆明

1956-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我文化艺术代表团返昆明
    据新华社昆明7日电　中华人民共和国文化艺术代表团在中近东地区访问后，大部分人员已经在四日至六日分批返抵昆明。代表团自二月下旬出国，先后在埃及、苏丹、埃塞俄比亚、叙利亚、黎巴嫩等五个国家进行了友好访问和演出，并和埃及、叙利亚两国政府签订了文化交流与合作的协定。
    代表团在访问演出期间，同上述五个国家领导人员进行了友好交谈，并和各国文学、艺术、教育、宗教、新闻等各界人士进行了广泛的接触。
