Title: A certain military factory on Hainan Island manufactures farm tools for farmers
Author: Liu Anning
Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 4

    A certain military factory on Hainan Island
    Manufactures farm tools for farmers
    (Our Newspaper) News: A certain naval ordnance repair factory stationed in the Hainan Island Li and Miao Autonomous Prefecture is using its spare time to manufacture tools for farmers. In a short period of time, it has already completed the task of one thousand sets of cultivators and fifty threshing machines. In addition, it also manufactured two hundred sets of threshing machine parts.
                        (Liu Anning)

### 海南岛某部兵工厂  为农民制造农具
刘安宁
1956-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    海南岛某部兵工厂
    为农民制造农具
    本报讯　驻海南岛黎族苗族自治州的海军某兵工修理厂，利用空隙时间为农民制造工具。在一个短时期内，已经完成了一千架中耕器和五十架打谷机的任务。另外，还制造了二百架打谷机的零件。
                        （刘安宁）
