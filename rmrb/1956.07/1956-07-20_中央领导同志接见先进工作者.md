Title: Central Leaders Receive Advanced Workers

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th: Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Shaoqi, Peng Zhen, and Deng Xiaoping, received all the representatives of the National Conference of Advanced Workers in Rural Finance this afternoon.

### 中央领导同志接见先进工作者

1956-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中央领导同志接见先进工作者
    新华社19日讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、彭真、邓小平，在今天下午接见了全国农村金融先进工作者代表会议的全体代表。
