Title: Dalian Port (Picture)
Author: Jia Chengbin
Date: 1956-07-31
Page: 2

    Dalian Port (Picture)
    Dalian Seaport is one of the main ports in northern China. This year, the volume of goods handled by this port is the highest since liberation. Ships from twenty-two countries now come and go here for peaceful trade. This is the Japanese "Nisshin Maru" loading soybeans, a specialty of the Northeast.
                                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Jia Chengbin photographed

### 大连港（图片）
贾承滨
1956-07-31
第2版()
专栏：

    大连港（图片）
    大连海港是我国北方的主要港口之一，今年这个港吞吐的货物数量是解放后最高的一年。现在有二十二个国家的船只在这里往来，进行和平贸易。这是日本“日进丸”在装运东北特产大豆。
                                        新华社记者　贾承滨摄
