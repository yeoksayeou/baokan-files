Title: Fraternal Parties of Czechoslovakia, Germany, and Vietnam Congratulate the CPC on its 35th Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Executive Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Germany sent congratulatory telegrams to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. In their telegrams, they congratulated the Chinese people on their greater achievements under the leadership of the Communist Party of China in the struggle to build socialism and safeguard Asian and world peace.

### 捷、德、越三国兄弟党  电贺中共成立三十五周年

1956-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    捷、德、越三国兄弟党
    电贺中共成立三十五周年
    新华社4日讯　在中国共产党成立三十五周年的时候，捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会、德国统一社会党中央委员会、越南劳动党执行委员会和德国共产党中央委员会，给中国共产党中央委员会打来了祝贺的电报。它们在电报中祝贺中国人民在中国共产党领导下，在建设社会主义和维护亚洲和世界和平的斗争中获得更多的成就。
