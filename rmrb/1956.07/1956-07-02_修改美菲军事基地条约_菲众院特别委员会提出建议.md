Title: Philippine House Special Committee Proposes Amendments to US-Philippine Military Bases Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 6

    Amending the US-Philippine Military Bases Agreement
    Philippine House Special Committee Proposes Recommendations
    (Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 29th) The Special Committee of the Philippine House of Representatives responsible for re-examining Philippine-US relations passed five recommendations on June 28th to amend the treaty regarding the establishment of US military bases in the Philippines.
    The committee requests: First, the United States must recognize Philippine ownership of the twenty-two bases occupied by US troops in the Philippines; Second, delineate the boundaries of the areas retained as bases; Third, the Philippines has jurisdiction over US military personnel who violate Philippine laws; Fourth, Philippine nationals have the right to exploit mineral resources within the bases and reservations; Fifth, shorten the current 99-year lease term for the bases leased to the United States.

### 修改美菲军事基地条约  菲众院特别委员会提出建议

1956-07-02
第6版()
专栏：

    修改美菲军事基地条约
    菲众院特别委员会提出建议
    据新华社29日讯　马尼拉消息：菲律宾众议院负责重新审查菲律宾和美国关系的特别委员会六月二十八日通过了修改关于美国在菲律宾设立军事基地的条约的五点建议。
    这个委员会要求：第一，美国要承认菲律宾对美国军队在菲律宾所占领的二十二个基地的所有权；第二，划定保留作为基地的地区的界线；第三，菲律宾对违反菲律宾法律的美国军事人员有裁判权；第四，菲律宾国民有权开采在基地和保留地以内的矿物资源；第五，把目前的租给美国的基地的九十九年租期加以缩短。
