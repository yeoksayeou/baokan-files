Title: South and Cambodian Delegations Hold Talks; Sihanouk Expresses Hope to Establish Close and Friendly Relations Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Yugoslav News Agency: The Yugoslav delegation headed by Tito and the Cambodian delegation headed by Sihanouk held political talks on Vanga Island near Brioni on July 20th.

It is reported that the personnel of the two delegations exchanged views on the international situation and discussed measures to promote relations between the two countries. It is believed that the political part of the talks has ended. During Prince Sihanouk's stay in Belgrade, he will hold talks with Federal Executive Council Vice President Vukmanović to discuss expanding economic relations between Yugoslavia and Cambodia.

It is expected that a joint communique on the talks will be issued when Prince Sihanouk concludes his visit to Yugoslavia.

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Yugoslav News Agency: Prince Sihanouk of the Kingdom of Cambodia and his entourage arrived in Brioni on the afternoon of July 19th. They will be visiting Yugoslavia for five days.

Upon arrival in Yugoslavia, Sihanouk said in a statement that the Cambodian delegation had come to Yugoslavia with the desire to establish the closest friendly relations between the two countries. The delegation hopes that the contact established during this visit will become a new link between Asia and Europe, whose cooperation is indispensable for safeguarding peace.

Sihanouk said that his country is pursuing a neutral foreign policy, it has accepted the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and hopes to cooperate with all countries that respect Cambodia's independence and sovereignty - regardless of their government. He said that domestically, we are advancing on the road to socialism. We have democratized our constitutional regime and are making efforts to achieve socialism in order to improve the people's living standards.

Sihanouk concluded by saying that Cambodia is looking for new friends among those countries that share its ideals and understand its ideals, and it attaches great importance to the friendship of the Yugoslav people. He said, we hope that we will develop cooperation that is beneficial to the people of our two countries and ultimately beneficial to the cause of international understanding between our two countries.

The Cambodian guests arrived at Pula Airport near Brioni by plane from Paris.

### 南柬两国代表团举行会谈  西哈努克表示希望建立两国密切友好关系

1956-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    南柬两国代表团举行会谈
    西哈努克表示希望建立两国密切友好关系
    新华社据南斯拉夫通讯社报道　以铁托为首的南斯拉夫代表团和以西哈努克为首的柬埔寨代表团七月二十日下午在布里俄尼附近的万加岛上举行了政治会谈。
    据悉，两国代表团人员就国际局势交换了意见，并且讨论了促进两国关系的措施。据认为，会谈的政治部分已经结束。西哈努克亲王在贝尔格莱德停留期间将同联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇会谈，讨论扩大南斯拉夫和柬埔寨之间的经济关系问题。
    在西哈努克亲王结束对南斯拉夫的访问的时候，可望发表关于会谈的联合公报。
    新华社据南斯拉夫通讯社报道　柬埔寨王国西哈努克亲王和随行人员七月十九日下午到达布里俄尼。他们将在南斯拉夫进行五天的访问。
    西哈努克在到达南斯拉夫的时候发表谈话说，柬埔寨代表团是怀着建立两国间最紧密友好关系的愿望来到南斯拉夫的。代表团希望，将在这次访问中建立起来的接触，将成为亚洲和欧洲间的一个新的环节，它们的合作是为保卫和平所不可缺少的。
    西哈努克说，他的国家正在实行一项中立的外交政策，它已经接受了和平共处五项原则，并且希望同一切尊重柬埔寨的独立和主权的国家——不问其政府如何——进行合作。他说，在国内方面，我们正在走向社会主义的道路上前进。我们已经把我们的立宪政权民主化，并且正在进行努力来实现社会主义，来提高人民的生活水平。
    西哈努克最后说，柬埔寨在同它抱有相同理想的和了解它的理想的那些国家中寻找新朋友，它极为重视南斯拉夫人民的友谊。他说，我们希望，我们将发展有益于我们两国人民和最终有益于我们两国之间的国际谅解事业的合作。
    柬埔寨客人是从巴黎乘飞机到达布里俄尼附近的普拉机场的。
