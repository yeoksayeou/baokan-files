Title: Shepilov Meets with Greek Prime Minister

Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th dispatch from Athens: Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov held talks with Greek Prime Minister Karamanlis on June 29th. Greek Foreign Minister Averoff said after the talks: "The results of the talks are satisfactory."
    According to Reuters, when asked for his opinion on the Cyprus issue, Shepilov said: "Our general principle is to advocate that all peoples obtain freedom and the right to self-determination."
    On the 29th, Shepilov visited the Acropolis in ancient Athens. He will continue to visit several Greek cities.

### 谢皮洛夫同希腊首相会谈

1956-07-01
第5版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫同希腊首相会谈
    新华社30日电　雅典消息：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫六月二十九日同希腊首相卡拉曼利斯举行了会谈。希腊外交大臣阿维罗夫在这次会谈以后说：“会谈结果是令人满意的。”
    据路透社报道，曾有人要求谢皮洛夫就塞浦路斯问题发表意见，谢皮洛夫说：“我们总的原则是主张让一切人民都获得自由和自决的权利。”
    谢皮洛夫在二十九日参观了古代雅典的卫城。他还要继续到几个希腊城市参观。
