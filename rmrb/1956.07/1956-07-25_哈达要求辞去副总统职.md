Title: Hatta Requests to Resign as Vice President
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-25
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 24th. Indonesian Vice President Hatta formally requested yesterday that he be allowed to resign after the Constituent Assembly is established in a few weeks.

### 哈达要求辞去副总统职

1956-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

    哈达要求辞去副总统职
    据新华社雅加达24日电　印度尼西亚副总统哈达昨天正式要求在制宪议会在几个星期内成立以后就让他辞职。
