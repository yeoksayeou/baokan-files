Title: State-owned Coal Mines Exceed Production Targets
Author:
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. By the end of June, state-owned coal mines across the country had exceeded their production targets for the first half of the year by 0.64%. Compared with the same period last year, they produced more than six million tons of raw coal for the state.

    In the first half of this year, workers in various state-owned coal mines, due to their high level of enthusiasm and creativity in the socialist competition, and by earnestly studying and promoting many advanced experiences, greatly improved labor productivity. Compared with the first half of last year, the efficiency of coal miners, underground workers, production workers, and overall efficiency in state-owned coal mines nationwide increased by 14.8-17.16%.

### 国营煤矿超额完成生产任务

1956-07-05
第2版()
专栏：

    国营煤矿超额完成生产任务
    新华社4日讯　到六月底为止，全国国营煤矿超额0.64％完成了今年上半年的生产任务。和去年同期相比较，为国家多生产了六百万吨以上的原煤。
    今年上半年，各个国营煤矿的职工，由于在社会主义竞赛当中发挥了高度的积极性和创造性，并且认真学习和推广了很多先进经验，因而使劳动生产率大大提高。与去年上半年相比较，全国国营煤矿的回采工、井下工、生产工效率，以及全员效率，提高了14.8—17.16％。
