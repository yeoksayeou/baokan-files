Title: 朝蒙 Decides to Expand Economic and Cultural Ties
Author:
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 19th (TASS): Following several days of sincere and friendly talks, the government delegations of the Mongolian People's Republic and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea signed a joint communiqué on July 18th.

The joint communiqué stipulates further expansion of economic and cultural ties between Mongolia and Korea.

The communiqué was signed by Premier Kim Il-sung on behalf of Korea, and by Chairman Tsedenbal of the Council of Ministers on behalf of Mongolia.

The Korean government invited a Mongolian government delegation headed by Chairman Tsedenbal of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic to visit Korea.

The Mongolian government expressed its gratitude and accepted the invitation.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 18th: More than 50,000 working people in the Mongolian capital of Ulaanbaatar held a mass rally at the Labor Stadium on the afternoon of the 18th to welcome the Korean government delegation headed by Premier Kim Il-sung.

Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Minister, Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and other leaders of the Mongolian government and party attended the welcoming rally. Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Mongolia also participated in the rally.

When Premier Kim Il-sung and Chairman Tsedenbal and others ascended the rostrum of the rally, the entire venue erupted in enthusiastic applause. At exactly 4:00 PM, the rally began. The band played the national anthems of Korea and Mongolia. Chairman Sambu and Premier Kim Il-sung delivered speeches at the rally successively.

In the evening, the Korean Ambassador to Mongolia, Hong Dong-chul, held a grand reception for Premier Kim Il-sung's visit to Mongolia.

### 朝蒙决定扩大经济和文化联系

1956-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    朝蒙决定扩大经济和文化联系
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：蒙古人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国两国政府代表团经过了几天恳切和友好的会谈以后，在七月十八日签署了蒙朝联合公报。
    联合公报规定进一步扩大蒙古和朝鲜的经济和文化联系。
    朝鲜方面在公报上签字的是内阁首相金日成，蒙古方面是部长会议主席泽登巴尔。
    朝鲜政府邀请以蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔为首的蒙古政府代表团访问朝鲜。
    蒙古政府表示感谢，并且接受了这一邀请。
    据新华社18日讯　乌兰巴托消息：蒙古首都乌兰巴托五万多劳动人民十八日下午在劳动体育场举行群众大会，欢迎以金日成首相为首的朝鲜政府代表团。
    蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布、部长会议主席泽登巴尔和蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴等蒙古政府和党的领导人都出席了欢迎大会。各国驻蒙古的外交使节也都参加了大会。
    金日成首相和泽登巴尔主席等走上大会主席台的时候，全场响起了一片热烈的鼓掌声。四时正，大会开始。乐队奏朝蒙两国国歌。桑布主席和金日成首相先后在大会上讲了话。
    晚上，朝鲜驻蒙古大使洪东哲为金日成首相访问蒙古举行了盛大的招待会。
