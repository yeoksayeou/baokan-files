Title: Laotian Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma to Visit Our Country
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 30th - This office received a report from the Laotian "Lao Voice" newspaper published on June 15th, stating that the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, Prince Souvanna Phouma, announced at a meeting of the National Assembly of the Kingdom of Laos that he would visit Beijing at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai.

At the meeting, Phouma also specifically talked about the neutral policy of the Kingdom of Laos. He said that his visit to China was also to realize the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, which are the basis of the Kingdom Government's foreign policy.

### 老挝首相富马将访问我国

1956-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    老挝首相富马将访问我国
    新华社河内30日电　此间收到老挝“寮声报”六月十五日发表的一篇文章报道，老挝王国政府首相富马亲王曾经在老挝王国国民议会的一次会议上宣布，他将应周恩来总理的邀请去北京访问。
    富马在会上还特别谈到老挝王国的中立政策，他说，他访问中国也是为了实现作为王国政府对外政策基础的和平共处五项原则。
