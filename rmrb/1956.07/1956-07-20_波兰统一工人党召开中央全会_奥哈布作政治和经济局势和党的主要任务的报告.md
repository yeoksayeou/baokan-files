Title: Polish United Workers' Party Holds Central Plenary Session; Ochab Delivers Report on Political and Economic Situation and Main Tasks of the Party
Author:
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 19th: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, the Seventh Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party opened on July 18th.
    The agenda of this plenary session is as follows: 1. The political and economic situation and the main tasks of the party; 2. Guiding principles for the 1956-1960 Five-Year Plan; 3. Organizational issues.
    The First Secretary of the Central Committee, Edward Ochab, delivered a report on the first item of the agenda. The meeting then began discussions on this report.

### 波兰统一工人党召开中央全会  奥哈布作政治和经济局势和党的主要任务的报告

1956-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党召开中央全会
    奥哈布作政治和经济局势和党的主要任务的报告
    新华社华沙19日电　据波兰通讯社报道，波兰统一工人党中央委员会第七次全体会议在七月十八日开幕。
    这次全体会议的议程有：一、政治和经济局势和党的主要任务；二、关于1956—1960年的五年计划的指导原则；三、组织问题。
    中央委员会第一书记爱·奥哈布就第一项议程作了报告。会议接着就这个报告展开了讨论。
