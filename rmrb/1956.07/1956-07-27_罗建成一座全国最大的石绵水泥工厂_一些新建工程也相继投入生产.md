Title: Romania Builds the Largest Asbestos Cement Factory in the Country; Some New Construction Projects Have Also Been Put into Production
Author:
Date: 1956-07-27
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 26th (via Bucharest dispatch): Romania recently built the largest asbestos cement factory in the country in Becas.

This factory is highly mechanized and automated, and is considered by experts to be one of the most modern factories in the world.

The first production line of this factory—the production line for asbestos cement corrugated sheets—has already begun trial production. This production line will be able to produce approximately 30 million corrugated sheets annually, which is equal to more than half of the total output of all the tile factories currently in Romania. Before long, the factory's asbestos cement pipe production line will also be put into production.

Since the beginning of this year, Romania has continuously achieved successes in basic construction. Not long ago, the Hunedoara steel factory completed a national blast furnace with a volume of 700 cubic meters, the largest in the country. The factory will soon complete a new open-hearth furnace workshop, a roughing mill, and a chemical coking oven.

A new and powerful fractionation unit at the eighth refinery in Romania has recently begun operations. This unit will greatly increase the oil production of the refinery.

### 罗建成一座全国最大的石绵水泥工厂  一些新建工程也相继投入生产

1956-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    罗建成一座全国最大的石绵水泥工厂
    一些新建工程也相继投入生产
    新华社布拉格26日电　布加勒斯特消息：罗马尼亚最近在贝卡兹地方建成了一座全国最大的石绵水泥工厂。
    这个工厂的机械化和自动化的程度很高，被专家们认为是世界上最现代化的工厂之一。
    这个工厂的第一个作业线——生产石绵水泥波状板的作业线已经开始试验生产，这个作业线每年将能够生产约三千万块的波状板，即等于罗马尼亚现有的一切制瓦工厂的总产量的一半以上。再过不久，工厂的石绵水泥管的作业线也将投入生产。
    罗马尼亚今年以来在基本建设方面不断取得成就。不久以前，洪尼多阿拉的钢铁工厂建成了一座容积为七百立方公尺的全国最大的鼓风炉。这个工厂即将建成一个新的平炉车间、一个粗轧车间和一座化学炼焦炉。
    罗马尼亚第八炼油工厂的一套新型的、效力强大的分馏设备最近已经开始使用。这套设备将大大增加这个炼油厂的石油产量。
