Title: Italian and Yugoslav Trade Unions Resume Contact
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd, Rome: According to the Yugoslav News Agency, an Italian General Confederation of Labor (CGIL) trade union delegation recently visited Yugoslavia. The CGIL Secretariat issued a statement on July 19th, expressing satisfaction with the establishment of contacts and the resumption of contact between the two countries' trade unions during the delegation's visit to Yugoslavia. The statement pointed out that the resumption of relations between the two trade union organizations is of great significance for strengthening the friendship between the people of the two countries.

The Italian CGIL will continue to exchange delegations with the Yugoslav Trade Union Confederation to exchange experiences and conduct contact.

### 意南两国工会恢复接触

1956-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    意南两国工会恢复接触
    新华社23日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社罗马讯：意大利总工会组织的一个工会代表团在最近访问了南斯拉夫。意大利总工会书记处在七月十九日发表声明，对代表团在访问期间同南斯拉夫工会联合会建立了联系和恢复了两国工会之间的接触表示满意，声明指出，两国工会组织的关系的恢复，对于加强两国人民的友好具有重大意义。
    意大利总工会将继续同南斯拉夫工会互派代表团，以交换经验和进行接触。
