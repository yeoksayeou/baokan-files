Title: Deepening Friendly Cooperation Between Chinese and Japanese Railway Workers: Representatives of Railway Workers from Both Countries Sign Five Agreements
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

    To Deepen Friendly Cooperation Between Chinese and Japanese Railway Workers
    Representatives of Railway Workers from Both Countries Sign Five Agreements

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the National Committee of the Railway Workers' Union of China and the Japanese National Railway Workers' Friendship Delegation to China signed five agreements in Beijing on the 28th to strengthen the friendship between railway workers of China and Japan.
    Wang Zhijie, Chairman of the National Committee of the Railway Workers' Union of China, and Kiyoshi Suzuki, Head of the Japanese National Railway Workers' Friendship Delegation to China, signed the agreements on behalf of their respective parties.
    The agreements stated: The National Committee of the Railway Workers' Union of China and the Japanese National Railway Workers' Friendship Delegation to China, based on the agreement signed on November 27, 1955, between Mr. Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Mr. Toitaro Fujita, Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, in order to deepen the friendship between railway workers of the People's Republic of China and Japan, and to further concretely develop this friendship, have reached the following agreements through friendly consultations:
    1.  To strive for the normalization of diplomatic relations between the two countries.
    2.  To oppose the testing and use of atomic and hydrogen bombs.
    3.  To strive to deepen the connections, friendship, and strengthen the solidarity of Asian railway workers.
    4.  To exchange delegations regularly.
    5.  To work together to promote the exchange of sports and cultural activities by exchanging photographs, films, organizational publications, paintings, etc., and by facilitating the exchange of sports and cultural figures.

### 加深中日铁路工人友好合作  两国铁路工人代表签订五项协议

1956-07-01
第5版()
专栏：

    加深中日铁路工人友好合作
    两国铁路工人代表签订五项协议
    据新华社讯　中国铁路工会全国委员会同日本国营铁路工人访华友好代表团二十八日在北京签订了加强中日两国铁路工人友好的五项协议。
    中国铁路工会全国委员会主席王志杰，日本国营铁路工人访华友好代表团团长铃木清分别代表双方在协议上签字。
    协议说：中国铁路工会全国委员会与日本国营铁路工人访华友好代表团，根据1955年11月27日中华全国总工会主席赖若愚先生与日本工会总评议会议长藤田藤太郎先生之间所订的协议，为加深中华人民共和国和日本国的铁路工人的友好，并进一步具体地发展这种友好，双方进行友好协商的结果，达成如下协议：
    一、为实现两国邦交正常化而努力。
    二、反对试验和使用原子弹、氢弹。
    三、为加深亚洲铁路工人的联系、友好和加强团结而努力。
    四、定期交换代表团。
    五、共同努力通过交换照片、电影、机关刊物、绘画等，促进体育、文化活动家的来往，以进行体育、文化活动的交流。
