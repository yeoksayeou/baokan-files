Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Minority Language Survey Team compiles Dai, Jingpo, and Chinese comparative dictionaries
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Third Working Team of the Minority Language Survey of the Chinese Academy of Sciences is currently in Yunnan compiling the first Dai-Chinese and Jingpo-Chinese comparative dictionaries for the Dai and Jingpo people of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture.

　　The Dai and Jingpo vocabulary of the Dehong area has been collected and compiled over the past three years. The two dictionaries being compiled will each collect about 5,000 commonly used words, which can meet the needs of reading newspapers and writing letters. Local Dai and Jingpo ethnic minority cadres have also participated in the compilation work. The first drafts of the dictionaries will be completed in August.

　　At present, this working team is also collecting and compiling the Lisu language of the Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Prefecture and investigating the Dai vocabulary of the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture, preparing to compile dictionaries for the Dai and Lisu peoples respectively.

### 中国科学院少数民族语言调查队  编纂傣文、景颇文和汉文对照小词典

1956-07-01
第7版()
专栏：

    中国科学院少数民族语言调查队
    编纂傣文、景颇文和汉文对照小词典
    新华社讯　中国科学院少数民族语言调查第三工作队正在云南为德宏傣族景颇族自治州的傣族、景颇族编纂第一部傣文和景颇文与汉文对照的小词典。
    德宏地区傣语、景颇语的词汇，已经过了三年来的收集和整理。正在编纂中的这两部小词典，将分别收集五千个左右的常用词，可以满足看报和写信的需要。当地傣族、景颇族的民族干部，也参加了编纂工作。小词典的初稿将在八月间完成。
    目前，这个工作队同时还在收集整理怒江傈僳族自治区的傈僳语，调查西双版纳傣族自治州的傣族语汇，准备分别为傣族、傈僳族编纂词典。
