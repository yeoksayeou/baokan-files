Title: Saluting President Nasser and the People of Egypt - Readers Xiong Minsheng et al. Entrust this Newspaper to Forward a Telegram to President Nasser
Author:
Date: 1956-07-30
Page: 6

Saluting President Nasser and the People of Egypt
— Telegram entrusted by readers Xiong Minsheng and others to this newspaper to be forwarded to President Nasser —
To His Excellency President Abdul Nasser of the Republic of Egypt:

We have read your speech at the Alexandria Liberation Square commemorating the fourth anniversary of the Egyptian Revolution and the decree nationalizing the International Suez Canal Company. In the name of ordinary citizens of the People's Republic of China, we extend our heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and, through Your Excellency, to the people of Egypt who have achieved glorious independence.

The Chinese people only achieved independence seven years ago, and therefore we can deeply appreciate the suffering of people living in countries subject to foreign interference, as well as the enthusiasm of the people to fight for complete political and economic independence. In our country after liberation, the government is leading us in various constructions. Especially as electrical workers, we deeply realize the enormous significance of building hydropower stations in improving people's lives. For this reason, we are following the news of the construction of the Aswan Dam in Egypt with great excitement and respect.

The imperialists vainly attempted to use the conspiracy of "aid" to build the dam to influence the integrity and independence of Egypt, but this conspiracy has been seen through and shattered by you and the Egyptian people. We angrily protest the despicable conspiracy of the imperialists and fully support the Egyptian people's struggle to nationalize the Suez Canal Company in order to defend national sovereignty and safeguard national interests, and believe that you will surely win victory with the support of peace-loving people all over the world.

We extend our highest respect to Your Excellency, and wish Your Excellency even greater achievements in leading the Egyptian people to pursue an independent policy, establish a truly independent new country, in the struggle against imperialism and in defense of world peace.

Citizens of the People's Republic of China
Xiong Minsheng, Ma Zhizhong, Bian Menglin and Li Zupei, et al. (eleven people)
July 28, 1956

### 向纳赛尔总统和埃及人民致敬  读者熊敏生等委托本报转致纳赛尔总统的一封电报

1956-07-30
第6版()
专栏：

    向纳赛尔总统和埃及人民致敬
    ——读者熊敏生等委托本报转致纳赛尔总统的一封电报——埃及共和国总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔阁下：
    我们读到了您在亚历山大港解放广场纪念埃及革命四周年大会上的演说和把国际苏彝士运河公司收归国有的法令。我们以中华人民共和国普通公民的名义向阁下并通过阁下向取得了光荣的独立的埃及人民致以衷心的庆贺。
    中国人民只是在七年前才取得了独立，因而我们能深深地体会到在受到外国势力干预的国家里人民生活的痛苦，以及人民为争取政治和经济的彻底独立而奋斗的热情。在解放后的我国，政府正在领导我们进行各项建设。特别是作为电业工作人员来说，我们深深认识到建设水电站对改进人民生活有着多么巨大的意义。正因为如此，我们怀着十分兴奋和尊敬的心情，关怀着埃及阿斯旺水坝建设的消息。
    帝国主义者妄想以“援助”建设水坝的阴谋来影响埃及的完整和独立，但是这个阴谋已经被您和埃及人民所识破和粉碎了。我们愤怒地抗议帝国主义的卑鄙阴谋，并完全支持埃及人民为保卫国家主权、维护民族利益而把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的斗争，相信您们一定会在全世界爱好和平的人民的支持下得到胜利。
    谨向阁下致以崇高的敬意，并祝阁下在领导埃及人民奉行独立政策、建立真正独立的新国家的事业中，在反对帝国主义和保卫世界和平的斗争中，赢得更大的成就。
                                         中华人民共和国公民
                     熊敏生　马致中　边梦麟和李祖培等十一人
                                              1956年7月28日
