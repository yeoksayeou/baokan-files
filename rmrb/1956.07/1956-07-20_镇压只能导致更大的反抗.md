Title: Suppression Can Only Lead to Greater Resistance
Author: Zhong Renwei
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 6

    Suppression Can Only Lead to Greater Resistance
    When the ancient Roman Empire conquered Syracuse, a major city on the island of Sicily, a Roman soldier named Lucius broke into the house of Archimedes, the famous Greek mathematician and physicist. Archimedes said the following to this Roman soldier: "Force against force is a law. When force is applied, there must be an equal force acting against it. The more powerful you are, the more force you need to use; then one day..."
    This fragment of a historical story tells people a truth: It is impossible to use force to suppress the people of other countries.
    Unfortunately, even today, there are still people who do not understand or believe this truth.
    Since the anti-colonial armed uprising in Algeria began in 1954, the French troops stationed in Algeria have increased from 50,000 to 400,000. Despite this, the Algerian people have not been conquered. On the contrary, the armed uprising has developed into a national liberation war of increasing scale, and the Algerian National Liberation Army is growing stronger.
    As Archimedes said: "Force against force." The more severe the suppression by the French colonialists, the more intense the resistance of the Algerian people. The result will inevitably be the complete failure of the colonialists. The end of France's "dirty war" in Indochina is a lesson.
  Southwest University of Political Science and Law

### 镇压只能导致更大的反抗
钟仁伟
1956-07-20
第6版()
专栏：读者论坛

    镇压只能导致更大的反抗
    在古代罗马帝国征服了西西里岛上的一个大城市——西拉库斯时，一个叫路西阿斯的罗马兵闯进了古希腊著名的数学家和物理学家阿基米德的屋子。阿基米德对这个罗马兵说了以下一段话：“力对抗力，这是一条定律。当力起作用的时候，一定有相等的力和它起反作用。你们越是强大，就越是需要用更多的力；那么总有一天……”
    这个历史故事的片断告诉人们一个真理：使用武力去镇压别国人民是不行的。
    遗憾的是，直到今天，还有人不懂得或者不相信这个道理。
    自从1954年阿尔及利亚反殖民主义的武装起义开始以来，法国驻在阿尔及利亚的军队已经由五万人增加到四十万人，尽管如此，阿尔及利亚人民并没有被征服。恰恰相反，武装起义已经发展成为规模越来越大的民族解放战争，阿尔及利亚的民族解放军正在成长壮大。
    正如阿基米德所说的：“力对抗力”。法国殖民主义者的镇压越厉害，阿尔及利亚人民的反抗也就越厉害。其结果必然是殖民主义者的彻底失败。法国在印度支那进行“肮脏战争”的结局，就是一个教训。
  西南政法学院
            钟仁伟
