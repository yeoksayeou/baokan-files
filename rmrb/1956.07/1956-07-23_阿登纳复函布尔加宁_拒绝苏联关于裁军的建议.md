Title: Adenauer Replies to Bulganin, Rejects Soviet Proposals on Disarmament
Author:
Date: 1956-07-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd—Bonn announced on the 21st Adenauer's reply to Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin, which rejected Bulganin's goodwill appeal and proposals made last month concerning disarmament and European security issues.

Adenauer stated in the letter that the kind of proactive reduction of armed forces as seen in the Soviet Union "cannot replace" an international disarmament agreement. He emphasized that disarmament must be carried out with "supervision."

He disagreed with the withdrawal of foreign troops from German territory.

### 阿登纳复函布尔加宁  拒绝苏联关于裁军的建议

1956-07-23
第6版()
专栏：

    阿登纳复函布尔加宁
    拒绝苏联关于裁军的建议
    据新华社22日讯　波恩在二十一日公布了阿登纳给苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁的回信，这封信拒绝了上个月布尔加宁向他提出来的有关裁军以及欧洲安全问题的善意呼吁和建议。
    阿登纳在信里表示，像苏联那样主动的裁减武装部队“并不能代替”国际裁军协定。他强调裁军必须要有“监督”地进行。
    他不同意从德国领土上撤退外国军队。
