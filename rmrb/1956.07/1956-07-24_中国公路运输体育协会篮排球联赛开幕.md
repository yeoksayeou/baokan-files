China Highway Transportation Sports Association Basketball and Volleyball League Opens

(Our report) The China Highway Transportation Sports Association's 1956 Men's and Women's Basketball and Volleyball League opened in the capital on July 21st.

Participating in this conference are twenty-four men's and women's basketball and volleyball teams, a total of 288 male and female athletes. The majority of them were selected from 1,260 male and female athletes through regional leagues. These team members come from places such as the Kangzang and Qingzang Plateaus, the Yunnan border regions, the Inner Mongolian grasslands, and the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, and include seven nationalities: Han, Manchu, Mongolian, Hui, Uygur, Zhuang, and Korean.

### 中国公路运输体育协会篮排球联赛开幕

1956-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中国公路运输体育协会
    篮排球联赛开幕
    本报讯　中国公路运输体育协会1956年男女篮排球联赛大会七月二十一日在首都开幕。
    参加这次大会的有二十四个男女篮排球队，288名男女运动员。他们大多数是通过分区联赛，在1，260个男女运动员中选拔出来的，这些队员有的来自康藏、青藏高原，有的来自云南边疆，也有来自内蒙古草原和新疆维吾尔自治区的，包括汉、满、蒙古、回、维吾尔、僮、朝鲜等七个民族。
