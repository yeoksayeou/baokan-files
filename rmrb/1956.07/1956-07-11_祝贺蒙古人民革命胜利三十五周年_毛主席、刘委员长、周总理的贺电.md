Title: Congratulations on the 35th Anniversary of the Victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution - Congratulatory Telegram from Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 1

To Comrade Zh. Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic:
To Comrade Yu. Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic:
To Comrade D. Adilbish, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic:

    On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution, we, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend our heartfelt and warm congratulations to you, to the government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and to the people of the Mongolian People's Republic.
    On July 11, 1921, the Mongolian people overthrew the reactionary feudal rule and achieved the victory of the people's revolution. Over the past thirty-five years, the Mongolian people have not only consolidated the people's democratic system, but also achieved brilliant accomplishments in the country's economic and cultural construction. These achievements are of great significance for strengthening the forces of peace, democracy, and socialism and consolidating the cause of peace in Asia and the world. The Chinese people are extremely pleased with the achievements of the fraternal Mongolian people.
    We wish the Mongolian people new and greater achievements in peaceful construction.
    We wish the friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian peoples to be further consolidated and developed.
                                  Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
      Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
                  Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
			            July 9, 1956, Beijing

### 祝贺蒙古人民革命胜利三十五周年  毛主席、刘委员长、周总理的贺电

1956-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺蒙古人民革命胜利三十五周年
    毛主席、刘委员长、周总理的贺电蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布同志：蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席尤·泽登巴尔同志：蒙古人民共和国外交部长达·阿吉尔毕希同志：
    当蒙古人民革命胜利三十五周年的时候，我们谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向你们、向蒙古人民共和国政府和人民致衷心的热烈的祝贺。
    1921年7月11日，蒙古人民推翻了反动的封建统治，取得了人民革命的胜利，三十五年来，蒙古人民不仅巩固了人民民主制度，并且在国家的经济和文化建设中获得了辉煌的成就。这种成就，对加强和平、民主、社会主义力量和巩固亚洲和世界和平的事业，都具有重大的意义。中国人民对于兄弟般的蒙古人民的成就感到万分高兴。
    祝蒙古人民在和平建设中取得新的更大的成就。
    祝中蒙两国人民间的友谊更加巩固和发展。
                                  中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
      中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长　刘少奇
                  中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长　周恩来			            1956年7月9日于北京
