Title: Libyan Capital Residents and Students Hold Demonstrations in Support of Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-07-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tripoli, 30th -- Today, more than two thousand Libyan residents and students held a march and demonstration in the Libyan capital of Tripoli, enthusiastically supporting Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal.

　　The demonstrating masses carried images of Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser and various slogans: "The Canal Belongs to Egypt," "Down with Colonialism," "Gamal (Abdel Nasser), We are with You," "Forward, Long Live Egypt."

### 利比亚首都群众和学生  举行示威支持埃及

1956-07-31
第5版()
专栏：

    利比亚首都群众和学生    
    举行示威支持埃及
    新华社30日讯　的黎波里消息：今天，两千多名利比亚群众和学生在利比亚首都的黎波里举行游行示威，热情地支持埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有。
    示威群众携带着埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔的像和各种标语：“运河归于埃及”、“打倒殖民主义”、“加麦尔（阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔），我们和你在一起”、“前进，埃及万岁”。
