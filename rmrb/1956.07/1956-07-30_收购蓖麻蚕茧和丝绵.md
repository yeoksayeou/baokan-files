Title: Purchase of Eri Silkworm Cocoons and Silk Floss
Author:
Date: 1956-07-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th. This year, many regions have focused on promoting Eri silkworm production. Soon, Eri silkworm cocoons from various regions will be on the market. The China Silk Company has entrusted the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives to purchase Eri silkworm cocoons and silk floss in the six provinces of Anhui, Jiangsu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Fujian, and Henan, where Eri silkworm rearing is more prevalent.

### 收购蓖麻蚕茧和丝绵

1956-07-30
第3版()
专栏：

    收购蓖麻蚕茧和丝绵
    新华社27日讯　今年许多地区重点推广了蓖麻蚕生产。不久，各地蓖麻蚕茧就要上市了。中国丝绸公司已经委托中华全国供销合作总社在饲养蓖麻蚕较多的安徽、江苏、广东、广西、福建、河南等六省，收购蓖麻蚕茧和丝绵。
