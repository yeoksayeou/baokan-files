Title: Victims of Poznan Events Buried
Author:
Date: 1956-07-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 2nd. The burial ceremonies for the victims of the Poznan provocation took place on June 30th in various cemeteries in Poznan. A delegation from the Polish United Workers' Party and the government, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz, attended these burial ceremonies.
The Council of State of the Polish People's Republic posthumously awarded the Officers' and Knights' Cross of the Order of Polonia Restituta to those who died in the defense of the people's regime. Wreaths from the victims' families, party organizations, state organs, and the general public were placed in front of the graves of the victims of the provocation.

### 波兹南事件受难者安葬

1956-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

    波兹南事件受难者安葬
    新华社华沙2日电　波兹南挑衅事件中受难者的安葬仪式，六月三十日在波兹南各个墓地举行。以部长会议主席西伦凯维兹为首的波兰统一工人党和政府的代表团，参加了这些安葬仪式。
    波兰人民共和国国务委员会以波兰复兴军官和骑士十字勋章追赠给在保卫人民政权中殉职的人员。在挑衅事件受难者的墓地前摆着受难者的家属、党组织、国家机关和社会公众所献的花圈。
