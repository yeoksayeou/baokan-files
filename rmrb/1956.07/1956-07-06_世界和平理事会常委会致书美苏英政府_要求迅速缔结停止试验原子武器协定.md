Title: World Peace Council Standing Committee Sends Letter to US, Soviet, and British Governments Demanding Speedy Conclusion of Agreement to Cease Atomic Weapons Tests
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 4th: The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council has published the "Letter to the Governments of the United States of America, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, and Great Britain," which it adopted at its meeting held in Paris from June 23rd to 25th. The full text of the letter is as follows:

Recent scientific investigations have shown that nuclear weapon explosion tests cause dust-like, rain-like radioactive fallout throughout the world. Some of this radioactivity (namely Strontium-90) has a very persistent nature; therefore, as time passes, the effects of successive explosions may accumulate. In this way, people are directly or indirectly exposed to this radiation, and the harmful effects of this radiation are far from fully determined. Even if the actual radiation intensity is lower than the level generally believed to be tolerable by humans, it is known that plants, livestock, and fish can transmit concentrated radioactivity. People who suffer the consequences of this radiation will undergo genetic changes, which will bring disease, suffering, and death to several generations.

Even if explosion tests continue at the current rate, the consequences of chronic effects will be inevitable.

If countries possessing nuclear weapons cease explosion tests, they will prove their sincerity in seeking the immediate conclusion of a more perfect agreement on supervised disarmament.

Atomic weapon explosions are easily detected because the radioactivity caused by the explosions will spread across the entire surface of the earth, thus naturally providing the possibility of supervision.

Due to the above circumstances, we urge the governments of the United States of America, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, and Great Britain to conclude, without delay, an agreement on the cessation of all atomic weapon tests and experimental explosions.

### 世界和平理事会常委会致书美苏英政府  要求迅速缔结停止试验原子武器协定

1956-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常委会致书美苏英政府
    要求迅速缔结停止试验原子武器协定
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社巴黎四日讯：世界和平理事会常务委员会发表了它在六月二十三日到二十五日在巴黎举行的会议上通过的“给美利坚合众国、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和大不列颠三国政府的信”，信的全文如下：
    最近的科学调查表明核子武器爆炸试验在全世界引起了尘状的雨点状的放射性灰尘。这种放射性的一部分（即锶—九十的放射性）具有非常持续的性质；因此，随着时间的过去，放射性接连不断的爆炸的结果就可能积聚起来。这样，人们就直接或间接地受到这种放射性的辐射，而这种辐射的危害影响远还不能完全确定。即使真正的辐射强度低于现在一般认为人类能够忍受的水平，大家知道，植物、牲口和鱼类却能传导浓集的放射性。遭到这种辐射后果的人，他在遗传上发生改变，这将给一连好几代人带来疾病、痛苦和死亡。
    即使爆炸试验以目前这样的速度继续进行下去，慢性作用的后果也将是不可避免的。
    拥有核子武器的国家如果停止爆炸试验，就证明它们真诚谋求刻不容缓地缔结更加完善的关于有监督的裁减军备的协定。
    原子武器爆炸是很容易发现的，因为爆炸所引起的放射性会散布到整个地球表面，因而自然提供了监督的可能性。
    由于上述情况，我们敦促美利坚合众国、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和大不列颠三国政府刻不容缓地缔结关于停止各种原子武器试验和实验性爆炸的协定。
