Title: Dalian Song and Dance Troupe Performs in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1956-07-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 9th. The Chinese Dalian Song and Dance Troupe held its premiere performance at the Ulaanbaatar National Music and Drama Theatre on the evening of the 8th.

More than 600 people attended the performance, including leaders of the Mongolian People's Republic government, prominent figures from the cultural and artistic circles, and residents of the capital.

Mongolian audiences showed great interest in the programs performed by the Chinese song and dance troupe. They especially praised the martial arts performance by Chinese classical dance artists. Folk songs from Northeast China and the Butterfly Dance were warmly welcomed by the audience.

After the performance, all the actors of the Ulaanbaatar National Music and Drama Theatre and the artists of the Soviet Ural Folk Song and Dance Troupe presented flowers to the actors of the Chinese Dalian Song and Dance Troupe. The Acting Minister of Culture of Mongolia and the Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations also congratulated the actors of the song and dance troupe.

### 我旅大歌舞团在乌兰巴托公演

1956-07-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我旅大歌舞团在乌兰巴托公演
    新华社9日讯　乌兰巴托消息：中国旅大歌舞团八日晚上在乌兰巴托国家音乐剧院举行了首次公演。
    出席观看这次演出的有蒙古人民共和国政府的领导人和著名的文化艺术界人士和首都居民等共六百余人。
    蒙古观众对中国歌舞团演出的节目感到很大的兴趣。他们特别赞扬中国古典舞蹈艺术家的武术表演。中国东北的民间歌曲、蝴蝶舞等节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    演出结束后，乌兰巴托国家音乐剧院全体演员和苏联的乌拉尔民间歌舞团的艺术家们还向中国旅大歌舞团的演员们献花，蒙古文化部代理部长和对外文化联络局局长等也向歌舞团演员们祝贺。
