Title: Cambodian Prime Minister Khim Tit Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou, Thanking China for Friendly Economic Aid to Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1956-07-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd -- Cambodian Prime Minister Khim Tit's telegram of thanks to Premier Zhou Enlai regarding the signing of the economic aid agreement between China and Cambodia, and Premier Zhou Enlai's reply telegram. The full text is as follows:

	Prime Minister Khim Tit's Telegram of Thanks
	Premier Zhou Enlai:
	My government is very pleased to learn that the economic aid agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Cambodia has been signed. The value of this aid lies precisely in the fact that it is unconditional and unsupervised. The Khmer people take this opportunity to express their deep gratitude to the people of the People's Republic of China. Please accept my highest respect.
                                 Khim Tit, Prime Minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia
                                      June 22, 1956, Phnom Penh

	Premier Zhou Enlai's Reply Telegram
	To His Excellency Khim Tit, Prime Minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia:
	I have received your telegram of June 22nd.
	 The signing of the economic assistance agreement between China and Cambodia not only marks a new development in the friendly relations between China and Cambodia, but also embodies the sincere desire of Asian countries to carry out mutual assistance and cooperation based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the Bandung Spirit. I wish the Royal Government of Cambodia and the Cambodian people success in their cause of independently developing their country's economy. Please accept my highest respect.
                          Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                      July 2, 1956, Beijing

### 柬埔寨首相钦·迪电周总理  感谢我国对柬埔寨的友好经济援助

1956-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨首相钦·迪电周总理
    感谢我国对柬埔寨的友好经济援助
    新华社2日讯　柬埔寨王国首相钦·迪就中柬签订经济援助协定给周恩来总理的感谢电和周恩来总理的复电。全文如下：
    钦·迪首相的感谢电
    周恩来总理：
    我国政府十分高兴地知道中华人民共和国给予柬埔寨王国的经济援助协定已经签订了。这一援助所以有价值，正因它是无条件的和不受监督的。高棉人民借此机会向中华人民共和国人民表示深切的感谢，请阁下接受我最崇高的敬意。
                                柬埔寨王国政府首相钦·迪
                                      1956年6月22日于金边
    周恩来总理的复电
    柬埔寨王国政府首相钦·迪阁下：
    我收到了你六月二十二日的来电。
    中柬两国经济援助协定的签订，不仅标志了中柬两国友好关系的新发展，同时也体现了亚洲国家根据和平共处五项原则和万隆精神进行互助合作的真诚愿望。祝柬埔寨王国政府和柬埔寨人民在独立发展自己国家的经济的事业中获得成就。请阁下接受我最崇高的敬意。
                          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                      1956年7月2日于北京
