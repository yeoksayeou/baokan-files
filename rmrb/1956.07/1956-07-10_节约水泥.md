Saving Cement

    ·Saving Cement·
    Dry Hard Concrete
    Since March, the Third Engineering Division of the North China Directly Subordinate Engineering Company of the Ministry of Construction Engineering has been using dry hard concrete to pave roads. The frost resistance, compactness, and wear resistance of the roads are superior to those paved with plastic concrete. Each cubic meter of concrete can also save more than 70 kilograms of cement. Up to now, more than 300 tons of cement have been saved.
    Using dry hard concrete to pour U-shaped prefabricated components allows for a template turnover every ten minutes. The template utilization rate is 48 times higher than that of steam-cured concrete templates.
    Brick Powder Substitute Cement
    This engineering division used powdered quicklime as the main material to prepare five types of cement substitutes, including brick powder cement, clay cement, fly ash cement, and slag cement. The test results showed that brick powder cement was the best. This cement can also be used to prepare low-grade concrete and mortar.
    Lime Foam Cement
    In the factory roof project, the thermal insulation material used—foam cement—previously required 200 kilograms of #300 cement per cubic meter. The Third Engineering Division's tests have proven that these 200 kilograms of cement can be completely replaced by lime powder. Recently, they have continued their research and plan to use admixtures to replace a portion of the lime, saving the country even more investment. (Gong Zongyun)

### 节约水泥
龚宗允
1956-07-10
第2版()
专栏：节约水泥

    ·节约水泥·
    干硬性混凝土
    建筑工程部华北直属工程公司第三工程处从三月份开始用干硬性混凝土浇灌路面，路面的抗冻性、密实性、耐磨性都超过用塑性混凝土浇灌的。每立方公尺混凝土还可以节省七十多公斤水泥。到现在为止，已节约水泥三百多吨。
    用干硬性混凝土浇灌厶型预制构件，十分钟就可以周转一次模板。模板利用率比蒸汽养护混凝土模板提高48倍。
    砖末代水泥
    这个工程处用粉末生石灰做主要材料，配制了砖末水泥、粘土水泥、烟灰水泥和炉碴水泥等五种水泥代用品。试验结果，以砖末水泥最好。这种水泥还可以配制低标号混凝土和沙浆。
    白灰泡沫水泥
    在厂房屋面工程上使用的隔热材料——泡沫水泥，以往每立方公尺需要300号水泥二百公斤。第三工程处试验证明，这二百公斤水泥完全可以用白灰粉来代替。最近他们还在继续研究，拟用掺合料来代替一部分白灰，为国家节约更多的投资。　　　　　（龚宗允）
