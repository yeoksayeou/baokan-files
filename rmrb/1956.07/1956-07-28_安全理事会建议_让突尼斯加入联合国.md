Title: Security Council Recommends Admission of Tunisia to the United Nations
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 27th, New York: The Security Council unanimously adopted a resolution on July 26th, recommending that the Eleventh United Nations General Assembly admit Tunisia as a member of the United Nations.

### 安全理事会建议  让突尼斯加入联合国

1956-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    安全理事会建议让突尼斯加入联合国
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社纽约消息：安全理事会七月二十六日一致通过了一项决议，建议第十一届联合国大会接纳突尼斯为联合国会员国。
