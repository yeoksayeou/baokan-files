Title: Sukarno Returns to Jakarta After Visiting the United States, Canada, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 3rd: President Sukarno and his entourage returned here by plane this morning after a fifty-day visit abroad.

President Sukarno delivered a brief speech at the airport. He said that wherever he went, he saw that people had a general understanding of Indonesia's struggle. Many people expressed respect for the ideals and goals of Indonesia's struggle.

Sukarno said that during his visit he saw and studied many things, some of which were worth learning, and some which were not. He concluded by pointing out, "All of this requires us to take on greater responsibility and continue to devote ourselves to the great cause, which is to complete our national revolution so that Indonesia can contribute to the happiness of the world."

Many people went to the airport to welcome Sukarno. Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo gave a welcoming speech at the airport, saying that the Indonesian people were constantly paying close attention to the President's travels abroad. They were encouraged by the achievements of the President's visits abroad.

Many Indonesian newspapers today published commentaries affirming the success of President Sukarno's visits to the United States, Canada, Italy, West Germany, and Switzerland.

### 苏加诺访问美加等国后回到雅加达

1956-07-05
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺访问美加等国后回到雅加达
    据新华社雅加达3日电　苏加诺总统和他的随行人员在国外作了五十天的访问之后今天早晨乘飞机回到这里。
    苏加诺在机场发表了简短的讲话。他说，他不论走到哪里，都看到人们对印度尼西亚的斗争有着概括的了解。许多人对印度尼西亚的斗争的理想和目的表示尊敬。
    苏加诺说，他在访问期间看到了、并且研究了许多东西，其中有些是值得学习的，有些则不然。他最后指出，“这一切都要求我们负起更大的责任，继续致力于伟大的事业，就是完成我们的民族革命，好让印度尼西亚能够对世界幸福作出贡献。”
    有许多人到机场欢迎苏加诺。沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理在机场致欢迎词说，印度尼西亚人民经常密切注意总统在外国的旅行。他们因总统在国外访问取得的成就而得到鼓舞。
    印度尼西亚许多报纸今天发表评论肯定苏加诺总统对美国、加拿大、意大利、西德和瑞士的访问是成功的。
