Title: Reception Held by Mongolian Ambassador to Celebrate the 35th Anniversary of the Victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution
Author:
Date: 1956-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 11th: Last night, more than 800 Chinese and foreign guests gathered in the Beijing Hotel to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

At this grand reception, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yun, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairman Shen Junru, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong, and President Dong Biwu, among others, toasted the host of today's reception – Mongolian Ambassador to China, Ochirbat, offering festive congratulations.

Premier Zhou Enlai, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extended warm congratulations to the government and people of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Premier Zhou Enlai proposed a toast to the prosperity of the Mongolian People's Republic, to the fraternal and eternal friendship between the people of China and Mongolia, to world peace and international cooperation, to the health of the leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government and the greater achievements of the Mongolian people in the cause of national construction, and to the health of Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Prior to this, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Ochirbat, said in his brief speech that the Mongolian people, on their national holiday, wished the Chinese people continuous new victories in socialist construction.

Then, the Ambassador raised his glass and invited Chinese and foreign guests to toast the friendship between the people of Mongolia and China, the Chinese people and the government of the People's Republic of China, and the health of Chairman Mao Zedong.

In the brightly lit hall, Mongolian and Chinese songs were played continuously, and the guests engaged in friendly conversations.

Among the Chinese and foreign guests attending the reception were heads of various departments of state organs, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, well-known figures from all walks of life, as well as diplomatic envoys, diplomatic officials, and commercial representatives from various countries to China.

### 庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十五周年  蒙古大使举行招待会

1956-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十五周年
    蒙古大使举行招待会
    新华社11日讯　今天晚间，有八百多位中外人士聚集在北京饭店的大厅里，欢庆蒙古人民革命胜利三十五周年。
    在这个盛大的招待会上，刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理、陈云副总理、彭德怀副总理、邓小平副总理、沈钧儒副委员长、陈叔通副委员长和董必武院长等，都同今天招待会的主人——蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特干杯，作节日的祝贺。
    周恩来总理代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向蒙古人民共和国政府和人民致热烈的祝贺。
    周恩来总理提议为蒙古人民共和国的繁荣，为中蒙两国人民间的兄弟般的永恒的友谊，为世界和平和国际合作，为蒙古人民革命党和政府的领导人的健康和蒙古人民在国家建设事业中取得更大的成就，为蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布的健康而干杯。
    在这以前，蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使奥其尔巴特在他的简短致词中说，蒙古人民在自己的国家节日里，预祝中国人民在社会主义建设中不断获得新的胜利。
    然后，大使举起酒杯，请中外客人为蒙中两国人民的友谊，为中国人民和中华人民共和国政府，为毛泽东主席的健康干杯。
    灯光辉煌的大厅里，不断响着蒙中两国歌曲，宾主在一起进行着友谊的交谈。
    出席招待会的中外客人中，有国家机关各部门的负责人，中国人民解放军的将军们和社会各界著名人士，以及各国驻中国的使节、外交官员和商务代表。
