Title: Bulganin Sends Greetings to the Mexican People
Author:
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 8th: According to TASS, the Mexican magazine "Siempre!" published a letter from the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, to the Mexican people in its anniversary issue in July. On behalf of the Soviet people and in his own name, Bulganin extended greetings to the freedom-loving people of Mexico.

The letter expressed the Soviet Union's desire to develop good relations with Mexico. When talking about the principles of the Soviet Union's policy towards Mexico, Bulganin said that, as with other countries, the Soviet Union proceeds from the principle that countries with different social systems can and should coexist peacefully. The Soviet Union recognizes and respects the right of all countries to independent development.

Bulganin said: Cultural ties between Mexico and the Soviet Union have recently begun to develop, which helps to enhance mutual understanding and friendship between the two peoples. He expressed his belief that this friendly relationship will continue to develop in the future.

### 布尔加宁写信问候墨西哥人民

1956-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁写信问候墨西哥人民
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：墨西哥的“永久”杂志在创刊周年纪念号七月号上刊载了苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁给墨西哥人民的一封问候信，布尔加宁代表苏联人民，并且以他个人名义向爱好自由的墨西哥人民致意。
    这封信表示了苏联要同墨西哥发展良好关系的愿望。布尔加宁在谈到苏联对待墨西哥的政策的原则时说，同对待其他国家一样，苏联是从不同社会制度的国家有可能而且有必要和平共处这个原则出发的。苏联承认并且尊重一切国家的独立发展的权利。
    布尔加宁说：墨西哥和苏联之间的文化联系最近已经开始发展，这有助于增进两国人民的互相了解和友谊。他表示相信这种友好关系今后还会继续发展。
