Title: Continuing Session of the Central Plenary Session of the Polish United Workers' Party: Discussions on Reports by Ochab and Cyrankiewicz
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 21st: According to the Polish Press Agency, the Seventh Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party continued on the 20th. The session continued to discuss the reports by Ochab and Cyrankiewicz. Comrades such as Łánki, Morawski, and Zawadzki spoke during the discussion.

On the 21st, two committees designated by the plenary session will hold meetings. One committee will review the amendments proposed to the draft resolution on "the political and economic situation and the party's main tasks." The other committee will review the amendments proposed to the draft guiding principles of the Five-Year Plan.

### 波兰统一工人党中央全会继续举行  讨论奥哈布和西伦凯维兹报告

1956-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党中央全会继续举行
    讨论奥哈布和西伦凯维兹报告
    新华社华沙21日电　据波兰通讯社报道：波兰统一工人党中央委员会第七次全体会议在二十日继续举行。会议继续讨论奥哈布和西伦凯维兹的报告。在讨论中发言的有兰基、莫拉夫斯基和萨瓦茨基等同志。
    二十一日，全体会议指定的两个委员会将举行会议。一个委员会将审查对“政治和经济局势和党的主要任务”这个决议草案提出的修正案。另一个委员会将审查对五年计划指导原则草案提出的修正案。
