Title: Seven News Agencies Form Joint Reporting Group to Cover Olympic Games

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th, dispatch from Moscow, TASS, July 24th: The world's seven major news agencies have formed a joint reporting group to cover the main achievements of the Olympic Games to be held in Melbourne from November 22nd to December 8th this year. The organization is called "Colla," and its members are Agence France-Presse, Associated Press, Australian Associated Press, International News Service, Reuters, TASS, and United Press. This is the first time that such a joint reporting group has been formed to cover a major event.

### 报道奥林匹克运动会情况  七个通讯社组成联合记者组

1956-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    报道奥林匹克运动会情况
    七个通讯社组成联合记者组
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二十四日讯：世界七大通讯社组成了一个联合记者组，报道将在今年十一月二十二日到十二月八日在墨尔本举行的奥林匹克运动会的主要成绩。这个组织的名称叫做“科拉”，参加的有法新社、美联社、澳大利亚联合通讯社、国际新闻社、路透社、塔斯社和合众社。为了报道一个重大事件而成立这样一个联合记者组这还是第一次。
