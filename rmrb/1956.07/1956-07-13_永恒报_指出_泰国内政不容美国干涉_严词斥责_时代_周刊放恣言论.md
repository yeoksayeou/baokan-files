Title: "Yong Heng Bao" Points Out that Thailand's Internal Affairs Do Not Allow US Interference; Sternly Criticizes "Time" Magazine's Unbridled Remarks
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 11th, Bangkok Dispatch: The "Yong Heng Bao" editorial on July 9th stated: "Which side Thailand will lean towards is Thailand's internal affair, and does not allow Americans to intervene or interfere. Thailand's destiny should be decided by the Thai people themselves, not controlled and judged by the Americans. Any American who wants to control Thailand is an imperialist."

This editorial, entitled "Reply to Time Magazine," was written in response to a recent article published by the American magazine. In this article, "Time" magazine criticized the current US Ambassador to Thailand for not being able to control Thailand.

The editorial pointed out that the United States cannot monopolize Thailand's friendship.

The editorial said that Thailand, like other countries, will establish trade relations with communist countries. The editorial said: "Trade is something that cannot be obstructed, especially since China and Thailand are neighbors, which is a natural law that is difficult to violate." The editorial said that "Time" magazine speaks for those hunter-like Americans who come to Thailand and do all sorts of things, such as sowing discord and causing all sorts of misunderstandings in Thai political circles.

### “永恒报”指出  泰国内政不容美国干涉  严词斥责“时代”周刊放恣言论

1956-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

    “永恒报”指出
    泰国内政不容美国干涉
    严词斥责“时代”周刊放恣言论
    新华社11日讯　曼谷消息：“永恒报”七月九日的社论说：“泰国将倾向那一方面，这是泰国的内政，不容美国人介入或者干涉。泰国的命运应由泰国人自己来决定，而不是由美国人来控制和判断。那一个美国人想要控制泰国，那么那一个美国人就是帝国主义者”。
    这篇题为“答复时代周刊”的社论是针对这家美国杂志最近发表的一篇文章而写的。“时代”周刊在这篇文章中指责现任美国驻泰国大使不能够控制泰国。
    社论指出，美国是不能独占泰国的友谊的。
    社论说，泰国将和其他国家一样同共产党国家建立贸易关系。社论说：“贸易是一件没有什么能够加以阻挠的事，何况中国同泰国又是毗邻，这更是一种难以违反的自然规律”。社论说，“时代“周刊是代表那些像狩猎者一样的美国人说话的，这些美国人来到泰国作出了种种事情，例如进行挑拨离间，使泰国政界中发生各种各样的误会。
