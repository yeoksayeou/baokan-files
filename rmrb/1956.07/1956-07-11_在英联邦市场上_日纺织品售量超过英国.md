Title: In the Commonwealth Market, Japanese Textile Sales Exceed British Sales
Author:
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 10th: British textile business figures point out that last year, Japan sold more textiles to the Commonwealth market than Britain did. This is the first time in history.

Bodman, chairman of the British textile company Bodman-Malden Group, said in his annual report on the 6th that last year Japan exported 450 million square yards of cotton fabric to these traditional British markets, while Britain only sold 409 million square yards. Last year, Japan exported 70.08 million square yards of cotton fabric to Australia, compared with 57.6 million in 1954 and 21.9 million in 1953. This trend of rapid growth in Japan's textile exports can also be seen in other British dominions such as Canada and Ceylon.

The report believes that judging from the situation in the first quarter of this year, the trend of increasing Japanese textile exports will continue to develop this year. In the first three months of this year, Japan exported 363 million square yards of cotton fabric, exceeding the same period last year by 44.2%.

### 在英联邦市场上  日纺织品售量超过英国

1956-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    在英联邦市场上
    日纺织品售量超过英国
    据新华社10日讯　伦敦消息：英国纺织企业界人士指出，去年日本向英联邦市场销出的纺织品数量超过了英国。这在历史上是第一次。
    英国纺织公司博德门—马尔登集团董事长博德门六日发表的年度报告说，去年日本向英国的这些传统市场输出了四亿五千万平方码棉织品，而英国却只销出了四亿零九百万平方码。日本去年向澳大利亚输出的棉织品是七千零八十万平方码，而在1954年是五千七百六十万，1953年是二千一百九十万。日本这种纺织品出口急剧增长的趋势在英国其他自治领加拿大和锡兰等也可以看到。
    报告认为，如果从今年第一季度情况来看，日本纺织品出口增长趋势在今年还要发展。今年头三个月，日本输出了三亿六千三百万平方码棉织品，超过去年同期44.2％。
