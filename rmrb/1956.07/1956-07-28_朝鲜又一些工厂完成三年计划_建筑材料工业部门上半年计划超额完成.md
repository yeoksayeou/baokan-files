Title: Some Factories in North Korea Complete Three-Year Plan; Building Materials Industry Department Overfulfills First Half Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 27th – Several industrial departments in North Korea have completed or overfulfilled their production targets. The building materials industry completed 115% of its total production target for the first half of the year in terms of value. This is a 27% increase compared to the same period last year.

During this period, the building materials industry department produced more than twenty new products and dozens of new standard products on a large scale. At the same time, each worker's labor productivity increased by 12% compared to the same period last year.

In the iron and steel industry, the Chongjin Steel Plant completed its three-year target for granular iron production five months ahead of schedule on July 24th. The output of converters in this plant has doubled compared to before liberation.

The Guicheng Textile Factory, North Korea's second-largest textile enterprise, also completed its three-year output value plan ahead of schedule on July 25th.

### 朝鲜又一些工厂完成三年计划  建筑材料工业部门上半年计划超额完成

1956-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜又一些工厂完成三年计划
    建筑材料工业部门上半年计划超额完成
    新华社平壤27日电　朝鲜又有一些工业部门完成或超额完成了生产指标。建筑材料工业在价值上完成了今年上半年的总的生产指标的115％。这比去年同期增加了27％。
    在这一时期内，建筑材料工业部门大规模生产了二十种以上的新产品和数十种新标准的产品。同时，每个工人的劳动生产率比去年同期提高了12％。
    在钢铁工业方面，清津炼钢厂已经提前五个月在七月二十四日完成了粒铁生产的三年指标。这个厂转化器的生产量比解放前增加了一倍。
    朝鲜第二个最大的纺织企业龟城纺织厂也在七月二十五日提前完成了三年产值计划。
