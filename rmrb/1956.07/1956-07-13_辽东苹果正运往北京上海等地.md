Title: Liaodong Apples are Being Shipped to Beijing, Shanghai and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Report: Farmers in places like Lüda, Jin County, Fu County, and Gaiping have generally begun picking and selling early-ripening apples.

To meet the needs of various regions, the Liaoning Provincial Supply and Marketing Cooperative has signed apple supply contracts with twenty-six provinces and cities. Currently, they are being shipped to Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Harbin, and other places according to the contracts.

### 辽东苹果正运往北京上海等地

1956-07-13
第3版()
专栏：

    辽东苹果正运往北京上海等地
    据新华社讯　旅大、金县、复县、盖平等地农民，已普遍开始采摘和出售早熟苹果。
    为供应各地需要，辽宁省供销合作社已经同二十六个省、市签订了苹果的供应合同。目前正按照合同陆续运往北京、天津、上海、哈尔滨等地。
