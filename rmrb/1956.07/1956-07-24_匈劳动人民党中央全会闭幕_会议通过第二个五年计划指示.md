Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Central Committee Plenary Session Concludes; Meeting Approves Directives for the Second Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-07-24
Page: 5
	Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd, Dispatch from Budapest, July 22nd, TASS: The plenary session of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, which opened on July 18th, has concluded.

	During the discussion on the second item on the agenda—the Political Bureau’s report and the draft resolution on expanding socialist democracy—twenty-four members of the Central Committee spoke.

	The discussion at the meeting proved that the Central Committee was completely united.

	First Secretary of the Central Committee, Ernő Gerő, made a concluding speech.

	At the enlarged meeting held by the Central Committee on July 21st, the Political Bureau's report on the directives for the development of the Hungarian national economy's Second Five-Year Plan was heard.

	The Central Committee approved the directives for the Second Five-Year Plan and appointed a committee to draft the Five-Year Plan based on the directives for the Second Five-Year Plan.

	The Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party also passed a congratulatory telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

### 匈劳动人民党中央全会闭幕  会议通过第二个五年计划指示

1956-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

    匈劳动人民党中央全会闭幕
    会议通过第二个五年计划指示
    新华社23日电　据塔斯社布达佩斯二十二日讯：七月十八日开幕的匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会全体会议已经闭幕。
    在就第二项议程——政治局的报告和关于扩大社会主义民主的决议草案——进行的讨论中，有二十四位中央委员发言。
    会议的讨论证明中央委员会是完全团结一致的。
    中央委员会第一书记埃诺·格罗作了总结发言。
    中央委员会在七月二十一日举行的扩大会议上，听取了政治局关于发展匈牙利国民经济第二个五年计划的指示的报告。
    中央委员会通过了第二个五年计划的指示，并且指定一个委员会根据第二个五年计划的指示来拟制五年计划。
    匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会另外还通过了给苏联共产党中央委员会的致敬电。
