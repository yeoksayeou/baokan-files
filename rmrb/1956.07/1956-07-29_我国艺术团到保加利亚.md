Title: Chinese Art Troupe Arrives in Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 28th: Sofia dispatch: The Chinese Art Troupe, consisting of seventy-five members, arrived in Sofia by train on the evening of July 26th.

### 我国艺术团到保加利亚

1956-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团到保加利亚
    据新华社布拉格28日电　索非亚消息：中国艺术团一行七十五人，在七月二十六日晚乘火车到达索非亚。
