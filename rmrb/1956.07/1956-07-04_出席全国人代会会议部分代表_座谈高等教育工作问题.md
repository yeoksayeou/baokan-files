Title: Some Delegates Attending the National People's Congress Meeting Discuss Issues in Higher Education

Author:

Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency Report: The Ministry of Higher Education invited delegates from universities outside Beijing who were attending the Third Session of the First National People's Congress to a symposium on the 2nd to discuss issues in higher education.

At the meeting, the delegates exchanged opinions on some of the current issues in higher education. Li Da, President of Wuhan University, Chen Wangdao, President of Fudan University, and others reflected on whether the system of rewarding advanced collectives should continue to be implemented in universities, requiring further research. Lu Kanru, Vice President of Shandong University, and others suggested that it is necessary to carefully consider whether it is appropriate to promote the advanced producer movement in universities, especially among professors. Peng Dixian, President of Sichuan University, said that many students in universities are not learning their regular courses well, but in order to complete the tasks of the science groups, a large number of students have been absorbed into these groups, which not only makes the science groups a mere formality, but also increases the burden on students. Wang Yanan, President of Xiamen University, Li Chang, President of Harbin Institute of Technology, and others suggested that various departments directly assign tasks to the schools, which puts the school leadership in a passive position. They requested that the Ministry of Higher Education study or request relevant departments to make unified arrangements.

### 出席全国人代会会议部分代表  座谈高等教育工作问题

1956-07-04
第7版()
专栏：

    出席全国人代会会议部分代表
    座谈高等教育工作问题
    据新华社讯　高等教育部二日邀请出席第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议的北京以外的高等学校代表举行座谈会。
    代表们在会上交换了对目前高等教育工作中一些问题的意见。武汉大学校长李达、复旦大学校长陈望道等反映，目前在高等学校中是否应该继续推行奖励先进集体制度，需要重新研究。山东大学副校长陆侃如等提出，在高等学校中，特别是在教授中推行先进生产者运动是否适宜，需要很好考虑。四川大学校长彭迪先说，现在高等学校中许多学生正课还学不好，但为了完成发展科学小组的任务，吸收了大批学生参加科学小组，这样不仅使科学小组流于形式，而且增加了学生负担。厦门大学校长王亚南、哈尔滨工业大学校长李昌等人提出，各有关部门直接向学校布置工作，使学校领导工作陷于被动。他们要求高等教育部研究或提请有关部门加以统一安排。
