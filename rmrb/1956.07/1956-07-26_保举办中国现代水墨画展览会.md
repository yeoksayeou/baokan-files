Title: Bulgaria Hosts Chinese Modern Ink Painting Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, July 22nd: The city of Burgas in Bulgaria hosted an exhibition of Chinese modern ink paintings. The exhibition displayed fifty-five ink paintings reflecting the construction of New China, as well as many landscape paintings and book illustrations.

### 保举办中国现代水墨画展览会

1956-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    保举办中国现代水墨画展览会
    据新华社讯　保加利亚通讯社索非亚22日讯：保加利亚的布加斯市举办了一个中国现代水墨画展览会。展览会上展出了反映新中国建设的五十五幅水墨画，以及许多风景画和书籍插画等。
