Title: Bulgaria and France Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-07-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th, TASS, Sofia, July 24th: Following negotiations in Paris, the Bulgarian Minister of Foreign Trade, Zhivkov, signed a trade agreement between Bulgaria and France for 1956-1957 on July 21st.

The agreement stipulates that the trade volume between the two countries will increase by 50% compared to 1955. Bulgaria will supply France with tobacco, rose oil, herbs, peppermint oil, and seeds. France will supply Bulgaria with machinery, metals and metal products, chemicals, fertilizers, and other commodities.

### 保法签订换货协定

1956-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    保法签订换货协定
    新华社24日讯　塔斯社索非亚二十四日讯：保加利亚对外贸易部长日夫科夫在巴黎进行谈判后，在七月二十一日签订了1956年—1957年保加利亚和法国换货协定。
    协定规定两国间的换货额比1955年增加50％。保加利亚将供给法国烟草、玫瑰油、药草、薄荷油和种子。法国将供给保加利亚机器、金属及金属制品、化学制品、肥料和其他商品。
