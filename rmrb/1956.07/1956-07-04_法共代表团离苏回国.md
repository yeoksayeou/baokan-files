Title: French Communist Party Delegation Leaves Soviet Union for Home

Author:

Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd - TASS reports: The French Communist Party delegation, composed of French Communist Party Central Committee Political Bureau members Fajon, Rochet, and Servin, left Moscow by plane on the morning of July 2nd to return to Paris.

### 法共代表团离苏回国

1956-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    法共代表团离苏回国
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：由法国共产党中央委员会政治局委员法戎、罗歇和赛尔文三人组成的法国共产党代表团，在七月二日早晨乘飞机离莫斯科返回巴黎。
