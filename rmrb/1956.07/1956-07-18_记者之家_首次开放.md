Title: "Reporter's Home" Opens for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1956-07-18
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, July 16th: The "Reporter's Home," established by the All-China Journalists Association, began its first activities this afternoon in Taijichang, in the central district of Beijing.

    The All-China Journalists Association held a reception there tonight. Beijing journalists and journalists from nineteen countries in Asia, Europe, and the Americas who are currently in Beijing met and had enthusiastic conversations.

    The "Reporter's Home" will regularly hold activities such as journalist study and fellowship in the future.

### “记者之家”首次开放

1956-07-18
第7版()
专栏：

    “记者之家”首次开放
    新华社16日讯　中国新闻工作者联谊会设立的“记者之家”，今天下午在北京市中心区台基厂开始首次活动。
    中国新闻工作者联谊会今晚在那里举行了酒会。北京新闻界人士和现在北京的来自亚洲、欧洲和美洲的十九个国家的记者会见，并且进行了热烈的交谈。
    “记者之家”今后将经常举办记者学习和联谊等活动。
