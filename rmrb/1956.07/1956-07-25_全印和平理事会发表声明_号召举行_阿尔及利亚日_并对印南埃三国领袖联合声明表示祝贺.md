Title: All-India Peace Council Issues Statement Calling for "Algeria Day" and Congratulating the Joint Declaration of the Leaders of India, Egypt, and Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1956-07-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 23rd: The All-India Peace Council issued a statement today calling on the Peace Committees of all provinces, states, and counties, as well as all groups that uphold the Bandung Spirit, to hold "Algeria Day" on July 27th. The All-India Peace Council requests that public gatherings be held in various locations and resolutions be made to demonstrate India's unanimous support for the Algerian people's struggle and to support Indian Prime Minister Nehru's five-point proposal for a peaceful resolution of the Algerian question.

The All-India Peace Council also sent telegrams to Prime Minister Nehru, President Tito, and President Gamal Abdel Nasser, respectively, congratulating them on the joint declaration issued after the Brioni political talks. The All-India Peace Council particularly welcomed the joint statement's proposals regarding disarmament and the cessation of thermonuclear weapons testing. The statement said that such testing is "contrary to international morality."

### 全印和平理事会发表声明  号召举行“阿尔及利亚日”  并对印南埃三国领袖联合声明表示祝贺

1956-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

    全印和平理事会发表声明
    号召举行“阿尔及利亚日”
    并对印南埃三国领袖联合声明表示祝贺
    据新华社新德里23日电　全印和平理事会今天发表声明，号召各省、邦和县的和平委员会以及一切拥护万隆精神的团体在七月二十七日举行“阿尔及利亚日”。全印和平理事会要求在各地召开公众集会，并且作出决议表明印度一致支持阿尔及利亚人民的斗争和拥护印度总理尼赫鲁就和平解决阿尔及利亚问题的五点建议。
    全印和平理事会还就布里俄尼政治会谈发表三国联合声明一事分别致电尼赫鲁总理、铁托总统和阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统表示祝贺。全印和平理事会对于联合声明中关于裁军和停止热核子武器试验的主张特别表示欢迎。声明说，这种试验是“违背国际道德”的。
