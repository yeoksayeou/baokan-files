Troop Day
Sha Ou
The locust trees have just sprouted tender buds, the new green branches like soft palms, holding up sheep-like white clouds. At the foot of the small hill, a yellow dirt road lies, a glass-like stream flowing alongside the road, and a troop crosses the reed marshes on the other side of the river. What warm sunshine! The shadows of the children and the locust trees are printed at the foot of the hill, quietly, so many eyes are smiling, and a Young Pioneer is reciting a poem…

### 队日
沙鸥
1956-07-29
第8版()
专栏：

    队日
    沙鸥槐树刚刚吐出嫩芽，新绿的树枝像柔软的手掌，托住绵羊似的白云。小山坡下，躺着一条黄土路，玻璃似的小河挨着路边流着，河那边有一个中队穿过苇塘。多暖和的阳光呵！孩子们和槐树的影子印在山
    坡下，静静地，多少双眼睛都微笑了，一个红领巾在念一首诗……
