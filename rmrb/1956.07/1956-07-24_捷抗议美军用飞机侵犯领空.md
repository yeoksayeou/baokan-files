Title: Czechoslovakia Protests Against U.S. Military Aircraft Intrusion into Airspace
Author:
Date: 1956-07-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, according to a Czechoslovak News Agency report: The Czechoslovak Foreign Ministry delivered a note to the U.S. Embassy in Czechoslovakia on the 21st, protesting the intrusion of U.S. military aircraft into Czechoslovak airspace.

The note stated that despite repeated protests by the Czechoslovak government to the U.S. government regarding the intrusion of U.S. balloons into Czechoslovak airspace, recent incidents have occurred of U.S. military aircraft intruding into Czechoslovak airspace on March 9, May 4, June 20, July 2, July 9, and July 10.

The note pointed out that these actions by the U.S. Air Force grossly violate the norms of international law and run counter to the efforts of all peoples to ease international tensions and consolidate world peace.

### 捷抗议美军用飞机侵犯领空

1956-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

    捷抗议美军用飞机侵犯领空
    新华社据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道　捷克斯洛伐克外交部二十一日交给美国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆一件照会，抗议美国军用飞机侵犯捷克斯洛伐克领空。
    照会说，尽管捷克斯洛伐克政府已经一再向美国政府抗议美国气球侵犯捷克斯洛伐克领空，但是最近又发生了美国军用飞机在三月九日、五月四日、六月二十日、七月二日、七月九日和七月十日侵犯捷克斯洛伐克领空的事情。
    照会指出，美国空军的这种行动粗暴地违反国际法的准则，并且是同各国人民为缓和国际紧张局势和巩固世界和平所作的努力背道而驰的。
