Title: Moroccan Defense Minister Says French Troops Must Not Infringe on Moroccan Sovereignty
Author:
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 9th: Moroccan Defense Minister Jadir, who came to Paris to participate in the Franco-Moroccan military talks, said at a press conference: "We cannot be both an independent country and a protectorate. The status quo of the French troops (in Morocco) can only be maintained to the extent that it does not infringe upon our independence and our sovereignty."

　　The Franco-Moroccan military talks mainly discussed the status of French troops stationed in Morocco. France wants to continue to maintain the freedom of action of French troops stationed in Morocco.

### 摩洛哥国防大臣说  法军不能侵犯摩洛哥主权

1956-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥国防大臣说
    法军不能侵犯摩洛哥主权
　　据新华社9日讯　巴黎消息：到巴黎来参加法—摩军事谈判的摩洛哥国防大臣杰迪尔在记者招待会上说：“我们不能既是独立国又是被保护国。法军（在摩洛哥）的现状要保持下去，只能以不侵犯我们的独立和我们的主权为限。”
　　这次法—摩军事谈判主要讨论驻摩洛哥法军的地位问题。法国想继续保持驻摩洛哥法军的行动自由。
