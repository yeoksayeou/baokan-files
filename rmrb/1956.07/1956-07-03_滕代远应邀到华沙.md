Title: Teng Daiyuan Arrives in Warsaw at Invitation

Author:

Date: 1956-07-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 2nd. At the invitation of the Polish Ministry of Railways, Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Warsaw today. Polish Minister of Railways, Mr. Szylling, and others welcomed him at the station.

Teng Daiyuan will pay a short visit to Poland.

### 滕代远应邀到华沙

1956-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

    滕代远应邀到华沙
    新华社华沙2日电　中华人民共和国铁道部部长滕代远，应波兰铁道部的邀请今天到达华沙。波兰铁道部长斯柴莱茨基等到车站欢迎。
    滕代远将在波作短期访问。
