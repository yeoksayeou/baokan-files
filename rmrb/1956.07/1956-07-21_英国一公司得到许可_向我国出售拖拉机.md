Title: British Company Obtains License to Sell Tractors to China
Author:
Date: 1956-07-21
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th. According to an Associated Press dispatch from London: The British Massey-Harris-Ferguson Company announced on the 19th that it has obtained a license from the British government to sell one hundred agricultural tractors to China.

### 英国一公司得到许可  向我国出售拖拉机

1956-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    英国一公司得到许可
    向我国出售拖拉机
    新华社20日讯　据美联社伦敦消息：英国马塞—哈里斯—福格逊公司十九日宣布，它已经得到英国政府的许可，向中国出售一百台农业拖拉机。
