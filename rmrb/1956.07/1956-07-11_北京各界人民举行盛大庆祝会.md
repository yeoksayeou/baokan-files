Title: People from All Circles in Beijing Hold Grand Celebration

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th: On the eve of the 35th anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution—on the evening of July 10th, people from all circles in Beijing held a grand celebration.

This celebration was organized by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Shen Junru, and others attended the celebration.

Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun spoke at the celebration (full text on page 5). He extended warm congratulations to the Mongolian people and the government of the Mongolian People's Republic on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese government.

When the host of the celebration, Shen Yanbing, invited the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Ochirbat, to speak, warm applause resounded throughout the venue.

In his speech (full text on page 5), Ochirbat introduced the achievements made by the Mongolian people in various fields over the past thirty-five years to the Chinese people.

When Ochirbat finished his speech, prolonged applause resounded once again throughout the venue.

At the beginning of the celebration, the band played the national anthems of Mongolia and China.

After the celebration, the hosts and guests enjoyed the Peking Opera "Three Mountains" performed by the China Peking Opera Theatre. This Peking Opera is adapted from the opera of the same name from the Mongolian People's Republic.

Among the more than one thousand people who attended the celebration were also heads of government departments, well-known figures from all walks of life in Beijing, as well as foreign envoys, diplomats, and commercial representatives stationed in China.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Peking University held a meeting today to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

Historian Ishzhamts of the Mongolian Science Committee gave a report at the meeting on the achievements of Mongolia in science and culture over the past thirty-five years. He also described the long history of friendly relations between the Chinese and Mongolian peoples.

Hou Wailu, Director of the Second Institute of the Institute of History of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, reported on his impressions of his visit to the Mongolian People's Republic last year.

Nyamasuren, a Mongolian student in the first grade of the Chinese Language and Literature Department, also spoke at the meeting in fluent Mandarin Chinese. There are currently nine Mongolian students studying at Peking University.

### 北京各界人民举行盛大庆祝会

1956-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    北京各界人民举行盛大庆祝会
    据新华社10日讯　在蒙古人民革命胜利三十五周年的前夕——七月十日晚上，北京各界人民举行了一个盛大的庆祝会。
    这个庆祝大会由中华人民共和国文化部举办。周恩来总理、邓小平副总理和沈钧儒副委员长等都出席了庆祝大会。
    国务院秘书长习仲勋在庆祝大会上讲话（全文见第五版）。他代表中国人民和中国政府向蒙古人民和蒙古人民共和国政府致热烈的祝贺。
    当庆祝大会的主持人沈雁冰邀请蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使奥其尔巴特讲话的时候，会场上响起了热烈的掌声。
    奥其尔巴特在他的讲话（全文见第五版）中，向中国人民介绍了三十五年来蒙古人民在各方面所取得的成就。
    当奥其尔巴特讲话结束的时候，会场上再一次响起长久不息的掌声。
    在庆祝大会开始的时候，乐队奏起了蒙中两国国歌。
    庆祝大会以后，宾主一起欣赏了中国京剧院演出的“三座山”。这个京剧剧目，是根据蒙古人民共和国的同名歌剧改编的。
    在参加庆祝大会的一千多人中，还有政府机关的负责人和北京社会各界著名人士以及各国驻华使节、外交官员和商务代表。
    新华社10日讯　北京大学今天集会庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十五周年。
    蒙古科学委员会史学家伊斯扎姆茨在会上作了关于蒙古三十五年来科学文化方面成就的报告。他还叙述了中蒙两国人民友好关系的悠久历史。
    中国科学院历史研究所第二所所长侯外庐在会上报告了他去年访问蒙古人民共和国的观感。
    中国语言文学系一年级的蒙古留学生尼马苏伦也在会上用流利的中国普通话讲了话。目前在北京大学学习的共有九个蒙古留学生。
