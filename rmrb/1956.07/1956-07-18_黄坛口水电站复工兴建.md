Title: Huangtankou Hydropower Station Resumes Construction
Author:
Date: 1956-07-18
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 16th. Construction has resumed on a hydroelectric power station at Huangtankou, downstream of the Wuxi River in Zhejiang Province. Currently, the concrete dam blocking the Wuxi River is already above the water surface. The foundation work for the power station's plant, water intake, and switch station is also underway. It is expected to generate electricity by the end of next year. This hydroelectric power station will provide construction power for the Xin'anjiang hydroelectric power station, which is about to commence construction.

### 黄坛口水电站复工兴建

1956-07-18
第2版()
专栏：

    黄坛口水电站复工兴建
    新华社杭州16日电　浙江省乌溪江下游的黄坛口正在复工兴建一座水力发电站。目前，截断乌溪江江水的混凝土拦河坝已经高出水面，发电站的厂房、进水口、开关站的基础工程，也在紧张进行。预计明年年底就可以发电。这座水力发电站将为即将开工的新安江水力发电站提供施工动力。
