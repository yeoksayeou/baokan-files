Title: Hong Kong Sino-British Society Music Group Hosts Chinese Folk Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, July 7th: The Music Group of the Hong Kong Sino-British Society held a luncheon on the 6th to host the Chinese Folk Art Troupe.

Xu Bigu, President of the Hong Kong Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, and other social figures were also invited to attend the luncheon. Mr. Barraclough, secretary of the Music Group of the Sino-British Society, congratulated the Chinese Folk Art Troupe on its successful performances in Hong Kong on behalf of the host. Ouyang Shan, head of the Chinese Folk Art Troupe, expressed his gratitude for the hospitality of the hosts.

### 香港中英学会音乐组  招待中国民间艺术团

1956-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    香港中英学会音乐组
    招待中国民间艺术团
    据新华社香港7日电　香港中英学会音乐组在六日午间举行宴会招待中国民间艺术团。
    香港中华总商会会长许庇谷等社会名流也应邀出席了午宴。中英学会音乐组秘书伯拉格在席上代表主方祝贺中国民间艺术团在香港演出的成功。中国民间艺术团团长欧阳山对主人们的盛意表示感谢。
