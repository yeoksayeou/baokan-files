Title: Security Council Unanimously Approves Morocco's Admission to the United Nations
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-23
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: New York dispatch: The United Nations Security Council unanimously approved the admission of Morocco to the United Nations on the 20th.
    The Security Council's decision still needs to be approved by the United Nations General Assembly.

### 安全理事会一致通过  接纳摩洛哥加入联合国

1956-07-23
第6版()
专栏：

    安全理事会一致通过
    接纳摩洛哥加入联合国
    新华社21日讯　纽约消息：联合国安全理事会在二十日一致通过接受摩洛哥加入联合国。
    安全理事会的决定还需要经过联合国大会的批准。
