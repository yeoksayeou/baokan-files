    Liang Qihai is a Mistake for Liang Qichen
    A news item published on page 7 of the July 10th People's Daily reported the successful performance of right ventricular incision and dilation surgery by Professor Liang Qihai of Shanghai Second Medical College. After investigation, the professor's name Liang Qihai is a mistake for Liang Qichen. Please correct it. (Liang Qichen)

### 梁其海是梁其琛之误
梁其琛
1956-07-27
第8版()
专栏：读者·作者·编者

    梁其海是梁其琛之误
    七月十日人民日报第七版刊载的一则新闻，报道上海第二医学院梁其海教授等施行右心室切开和扩张手术成功。查教授姓名梁其海系梁其琛之误，请予更正。（梁其琛）
