Title: Pakistan Holds "Algeria Day" in Support of Algerian People's Liberation Struggle
Author:
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, July 1st. People across Pakistan held "Algeria Day" on June 29th. Mass meetings were held in the capital Karachi and almost all major cities, including Lahore, Dhaka, Peshawar, Rawalpindi, and Quetta, to pledge support for the cause of the Algerian people. Prayers were also held in various mosques, wishing their struggle a swift success.

    In Karachi, a large mass meeting was held. This meeting was organized by various party groups. Abdul Rab Nishtar, President of the Pakistan Muslim League, and others spoke at the meeting.

    The meeting passed a resolution condemning French atrocities in Algeria and condemning the recent unhelpful attitude of certain major powers in the United Nations Security Council on the Algerian question.

    The meeting also condemned the North Atlantic Treaty Organization countries for tacitly and actively sponsoring the use of NATO troops and weapons to suppress the liberation movement.

### 支持阿尔及利亚人民解放斗争  巴基斯坦举行“阿尔及利亚日”

1956-07-02
第6版()
专栏：

    支持阿尔及利亚人民解放斗争
    巴基斯坦举行“阿尔及利亚日”
    新华社喀喇蚩1日电　巴基斯坦全国各地人民在六月二十九日举行“阿尔及利亚日”。首都喀喇蚩以及差不多所有各大城市，包括拉合尔、达卡、白沙瓦、拉瓦尔品第和基达都举行了大会，保证支持阿尔及利亚人民的事业。各清真寺还举行了祈祷，祝他们的斗争迅速获得成功。
    在喀喇蚩，举行了人数众多的群众大会。这个大会是各党派团体组织的。巴基斯坦穆斯林联盟（前译回教联盟）主席阿卜杜勒·拉布·尼什塔和其他的人在会上发了言。
    大会通过了决议，谴责法国在阿尔及利亚的暴行，并且谴责了某些大国最近在联合国安全理事会对阿尔及利亚问题采取的无益态度。
    大会还谴责北大西洋公约组织国家默许和积极赞助使用北大西洋公约组织的军队和武器去镇压解放运动。
