Title: CPPCC Standing Committee Holds Meeting to Discuss Learning Issues
Author:
Date: 1956-07-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th—The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held its 25th meeting (enlarged) on July 7th to discuss the issue of organizing political and theoretical studies for democratic figures from all walks of life and industrialists and businessmen.

The meeting approved in principle the "Brief Rules for the Organization of the Learning Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference," the "Regulations on the Learning Methods of the Socialist Academy," and the "Provisional Conditions for Students of the Socialist Academy."

The meeting emphasized that the principle of voluntariness must be implemented when organizing studies for democratic figures from all walks of life and industrialists and businessmen. In terms of learning methods, the principles of freedom, voluntariness, and self-consciousness should be adopted, encouraging everyone to speak their minds and express their ideas fully; and debates on various academic viewpoints should be allowed.

### 政协常委会  开会讨论学习问题

1956-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    政协常委会
    开会讨论学习问题
    据新华社7日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会七日举行第二十五次会议（扩大），讨论组织各界民主人士和工商业者进行政治学习和理论学习问题。
    会议原则通过了“中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会学习委员会组织简则”、“关于社会主义学院学习方法的规定”和“社会主义学院学员的暂定条件”。
    会议强调指出，在组织各界民主人士和工商业者进行学习的时候，必须贯彻自愿的原则；在学习方法上，应该采取自由、自愿、自觉的原则，提倡人尽其言、言尽其意；允许学术上各种观点的争辩。
