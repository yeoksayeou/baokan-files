Title: Nasser Sternly Warns Western Countries: Egypt Will Not Tolerate Foreign Interference in the Nationalization of the Suez Canal; Egyptian Minister of Commerce Announces That No Vessel Will Be Allowed to Pass Through the Canal Unless It Pays Taxes to the New Company
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 28th, Cairo Dispatch: President Gamal Abdel Nasser, upon returning to Cairo today, told the welcoming crowds that Egypt would not heed Western threats.

Fifty thousand people gathered at the station, flooding the streets and blocking traffic. They chanted: "Long live Abdel Nasser, the champion of nationalization!"

The President said: "Any action by Western countries that does not recognize Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal will be considered blatant interference in our country's sovereignty and dignity."

The Egyptian President told the enthusiastic masses: "My strength comes from your confidence, determination, and support."

He said: "Today, the Egyptian people are armed and have declared general mobilization against imperialism. This spirit will enable us to win victory and prevent imperialism from defeating us. We will move forward, realize our freedom, and consolidate our sovereignty."

He told the imperialist countries: Egypt will maintain the freedom of navigation on the Suez Canal. He said, "I believe that any damage to the freedom of navigation on this canal will be the responsibility of Britain and France."

Xinhua News Agency, July 28th, Cairo Dispatch: Egyptian Minister of Commerce Nusseir stated yesterday at a press conference that the nationalization of the International Suez Canal Company is within the scope of Egyptian sovereignty, and Egypt will not allow foreign interference.

Nusseir said: "Neither the Security Council nor the International Court of Justice is qualified to handle such cases." "The Suez Canal Company has been Egyptian since its establishment, and any disputes must be submitted to the Egyptian courts. Only the Egyptian courts are qualified to handle this matter."

Nusseir said: "Any vessel that refuses to pay taxes to the new Suez Management Company's bank will not be allowed to pass through the canal."

He also said that if foreign banks currently holding the assets of the Suez Canal refuse to hand over the assets to Egypt, "we will not pay compensation to the shareholders."

Nusseir said that the entire property of the former Suez Canal Company in Paris, London, and New York has not been frozen, but is transferable. He said, "We will demand the transfer of these assets based on the right of succession."

He said that the new management agency is studying the method of taxation for vessels passing through the canal.

### 纳赛尔正告西方国家  埃及不容外国干涉苏彝士运河国有化  埃商业部长宣布任何船只如不向新公司纳税就不准通过运河

1956-07-29
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔正告西方国家
    埃及不容外国干涉苏彝士运河国有化
    埃商业部长宣布任何船只如不向新公司纳税就不准通过运河
    新华社28日讯　开罗消息：阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统今天回到开罗的时候向欢迎他的群众说，埃及不会去理会西方的威胁。
    聚集在车站的群众有五十万人，他们像潮水般涌到街道上来，以致交通为之阻塞。他们高呼：国有化运动的倡导者阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔万岁！
    这位总统说：“西方国家任何不承认埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的行动将被认为是公开干涉我国的主权和尊严”。
    这位埃及总统对狂热的群众说：“我的力量是从你们的信心、决心和支持中产生的。”
    他说：“今天，埃及人民武装起来并且宣布总动员来反对帝国主义。这种精神将使得我们获得胜利，而不会让帝国主义打败我们。我们将迈步前进，实现我们的自由和巩固我们的主权。”
    他向帝国主义国家说：埃及将保持苏彝士运河航行的自由。他说，
  “我认为，这条运河上航行的自由如果受到任何损害，都将由英国和法国负责。”
    据新华社28日讯　开罗消息：埃及商业部长努赛尔昨天在记者招待会上声明，把国际苏彝士运河公司收归国有是埃及主权范围以内的事情，埃及不容许外来的干涉。
    努赛尔说：“安全理事会也罢，国际法院也罢，都没有资格处理这样的案件。”“苏彝士运河公司自成立之日起就是埃及的，任何争执都必须提交埃及法院，只有埃及法院，才有资格处理这件事。”
    努赛尔说：“任何船只如果拒绝在新的苏彝士管理公司的银行里付给税金，那么就不许它通过运河”。
    他又说，如果现时握有苏彝士运河财产的外国银行拒绝把资财交给埃及，“我们将不付给股东以赔偿金”。
    努赛尔说，前苏彝士运河公司在巴黎、伦敦和纽约的全部财产没有冻结，但是是可以转移的。他说，“我们将根据承继权要求转移这些财产”。
    他说，新的管理机构正在研究通过运河的船只纳税的办法。
