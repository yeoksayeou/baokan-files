Title: Soviet-Egyptian Agreement on Cooperation in Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1956-07-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, July 15th: TASS reported from Moscow on July 14th that the Soviet Union and Egypt had signed an agreement in Cairo on July 12th regarding cooperation in the peaceful use of atomic energy.

According to the agreement, the two governments will cooperate in nuclear physics research and the peaceful use of atomic energy by establishing installations and laboratories, providing equipment and instruments, exchanging experts, sending Egyptian experts to the Soviet Union for advanced studies, and conducting joint research.

The above items will be realized through separate trade contracts to be concluded by both parties.

The conclusion of this agreement is to promote cooperation between the two countries in this area, which has existed since the signing of the contract to build a nuclear physics laboratory in Cairo equipped with a basic particle accelerator with an energy of three million electron volts.

The construction of an experimental atomic reactor with a capacity of two thousand kilowatts, equipped with scientific laboratories, is currently underway.

The construction of the main installations for conducting atomic energy research and its peaceful uses will be completed by mid-1958. These installations will be the largest in the Middle East.

### 苏埃签订和平利用原子能合作协定

1956-07-16
第5版()
专栏：

    苏埃签订和平利用原子能合作协定
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社报道：莫斯科在七月十四日发表了苏联和埃及关于在和平利用原子能方面进行合作的协定的公报，这个协定是七月十二日在开罗签订的。
    按照这个协定，两国政府将通过建立装置和实验室、提供设备和仪器、交换专家、派遣埃及专家到苏联进修和进行共同研究，在核子物理研究和和平利用原子能方面进行合作。
    上述各项目的将通过双方另订的贸易合同来实现。
    缔结这个协定，是为了促进自从签订关于在开罗修建一个装有能量达三百万电子伏特的基本粒子加速器的核子物理实验所的合同以来就存在于两国之间的这方面的合作。
    现在正在动工修建一个有两千瓩的实验性的原子反应堆，它将设有科学实验室。
    关于进行原子能研究和它的和平用途的主要装置的建设工程，将在1958年年中完成，这些装置将是中东最大的。
