Title: Egypt Announces Military Control in Canal Zone, Any Actions Obstructing Transportation Will Be Punished Most Severely
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 28th dispatch from Cairo: It was announced yesterday that Egypt has implemented military control in the Suez Canal Zone.

An official statement issued in Port Said stated that following Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser's nationalization declaration, the Canal Zone was immediately declared an Egyptian military zone.

The statement said, "Any actions aimed at undermining the interests or property of this newly nationalized company, or aimed at obstructing the smooth flow of transportation, will be a military crime and will be subject to the most severe legal penalties."

Navigation on the Suez Canal proceeded as usual on July 27th.

After the announcement of the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company, Egyptian officials immediately took over the company's branch offices in Cairo, Ismailia, Port Said, and Suez on the same day.

The Egyptian flag was raised on the roof of the Port Said branch office, replacing the company's blue flag. An Egyptian soldier guarded the company's safe there.

The newly appointed Egyptian manager of the nationalized company, Mahmoud Younes, said that the takeover went smoothly, the staff were very happy, and work continued as normal.

Xinhua News Agency, July 28th dispatch from Cairo: Egyptian Minister of Commerce Nuseir yesterday appointed three technical committees to study issues related to the nationalization of the International Suez Canal Company. These committees immediately began work.

A spokesperson for the Ministry of Commerce said that the three committees would be responsible for shipping issues, Suez Canal security issues, and technical issues, respectively.

### 埃及宣布在运河区实行军事管制  任何阻挠交通运输的行动将受最严厉处分

1956-07-29
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及宣布在运河区实行军事管制
    任何阻挠交通运输的行动将受最严厉处分
    新华社28日讯　开罗消息：昨天宣布，埃及已经在苏彝士运河区实行军事管制。
    在塞得港发表的一项官方声明说，在埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔发表国有化声明之后，运河区立即被宣布为埃及的军事区。
    声明说，“目的在于破坏这个新近国有化的公司的利益或财产和目的在于阻挠交通运输的顺利进行的任何行动，都将是一种军事罪行并且将受到最严厉的法律处分。”
    苏彝士运河的航运七月二十七日照常进行。
    在宣布苏彝士运河公司国有化以后，埃及官员们当天立即接收了苏彝士运河公司在开罗、伊士美利亚、塞得港和苏彝士的分公司。
    在塞得港分公司的屋顶上升起了埃及国旗，代替了这个公司的蓝色旗子。一个埃及士兵在那里守卫着这个公司的保险箱。
    新近任命的这个国有化公司的埃及经理马哈茂德·尤内斯说，接收工作进行得很顺利，职员们很高兴，工作在正常地继续进行。
    新华社28日讯　开罗消息：埃及商业部长努赛尔昨天指定了三个技术委员会来研究关于国际苏彝士运河公司国有化的问题，这些委员会都立即开始工作了。
    商业部的一位发言人说，三个委员会将分别负责航运问题、苏彝士运河的安全问题和技术问题。
