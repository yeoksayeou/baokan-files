Title: Printed Tussah Silk (Picture)
Author: Zhang Yinghua
Date: 1956-07-08
Page: 2

    Printed Tussah Silk
    The printed Tussah silk successfully trial-produced by the Tianjin Joint State-Private Guangda New Printing and Dyeing Factory has begun production. This silk has bright colors, is beautiful and strong, and is very suitable for women to make summer clothes.
    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhang Yinghua Photo

### 印花柞蚕绸（图片）
张映华
1956-07-08
第2版()
专栏：

    印花柞蚕绸
    天津市公私合营光大新印染厂试制成功的印花柞蚕丝绸已经开始生产。这种丝绸花色鲜艳，美观坚实，很适合妇女们做夏季衣服。
    新华社记者　张映华摄
