Title: Yangtze River Flood Peak Enters Dongting Lake
Author:
Date: 1956-07-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Changde, July 2nd: The second flood peak of the Yangtze River entered Dongting Lake today.

This flood peak came with great force. According to the person in charge of the Changde District Flood Control Headquarters, heavy rains fell continuously in the upper reaches of the Yangtze River from June 24th to 30th, causing the water level of the Yangtze River mainstream to rise sharply. This flood peak traveled for four days and nights to reach the middle reaches of the Yangtze River and entered Dongting Lake through four outlets.

### 长江洪峰入洞庭湖

1956-07-03
第3版()
专栏：

    长江洪峰入洞庭湖
    新华社常德2日电　长江第二次洪峰今天进入洞庭湖。
    这次洪峰来势凶猛。据常德区防汛指挥部负责人说，长江上游地区从六月二十四日到三十日连降暴雨，引起长江干流水位陡涨。这股洪峰经过四昼夜到达长江中游，由四口进入洞庭湖。
