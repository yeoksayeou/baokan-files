Title: Tangshan City Court Presidents Regularly Receive Visiting Masses

Author:

Date: 1956-07-15
Page: 4

Tangshan City Intermediate People's Court and the People's Courts of various districts, in accordance with the spirit of the Fourth Hebei Provincial Judicial Conference, have further strengthened the connection between the courts and the masses, and from July onwards, will implement a system where the presidents directly receive visiting masses. It is stipulated that every Saturday morning will be the President's Reception Day.

### 唐山市法院院长定期接待群众来访

1956-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    唐山市法院院长定期接待群众来访
    本报讯　唐山市中级人民法院和各区人民法院，根据河北省第四届司法会议精神，进一步密切法院和群众的联系，从七月份开始实行由院长直接接待群众来访制度。规定每星期六的上午为院长接待群众日。
