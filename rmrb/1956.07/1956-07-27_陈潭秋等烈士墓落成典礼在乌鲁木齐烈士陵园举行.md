Title: Completion Ceremony for the Tombs of Martyrs Chen Tanqiu and Others Held at the Urumqi Martyrs' Cemetery
Author:
Date: 1956-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, July 25th: This morning, the completion ceremony for the tombs of five revolutionary martyrs, Chen Tanqiu, Mao Zemin, Lin Jilu, Qiao Guozhen, and Wu Maolin, was held at the Yan'erwo Martyrs' Cemetery in the southern suburbs of Urumqi. Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Party of China, Wu Kaizhang, Deputy Secretary, Saifudin, Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, Imonov and Patikhan, Vice Chairmen, General Tao Zhiyue, Deputy Commander of the Xinjiang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, and Ren Gobai, Mayor of Urumqi, attended the ceremony. More than 3,000 representatives from all walks of life also participated in the ceremony.

The remains of the three martyrs, Chen Tanqiu, Mao Zemin, and Lin Jilu, were moved to the cemetery on July 2nd. The remains of the two martyrs, Qiao Guozhen and Wu Maolin, were recently found on a remote hillside in Liudaowan, Urumqi, and were also buried together in this scenic and tranquil martyrs' cemetery.

### 陈潭秋等烈士墓落成典礼在乌鲁木齐烈士陵园举行

1956-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    陈潭秋等烈士墓落成典礼
    在乌鲁木齐烈士陵园举行
    据新华社乌鲁木齐25日电　今天上午，在乌鲁木齐市南郊燕儿窝烈士陵园，举行了陈潭秋、毛泽民、林基路、乔国桢和吴茂林五位革命烈士墓的落成典礼。中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会第一书记王恩茂、副书记武开章，新疆维吾尔自治区主席赛福鼎，副主席伊敏诺夫、帕提汉，人民解放军新疆军区副司令员陶峙岳上将和乌鲁木齐市长任戈白等参加了典礼。参加典礼的还有各界代表共三千多人。
    陈潭秋、毛泽民、林基路三位烈士的遗骨在七月二日移到陵园，乔国桢和吴茂林二位烈士的遗骨是最近在乌鲁木齐六道湾荒僻山坡上找到的，这次也一同安葬在这一风景清幽的烈士陵园里。
