Author:

    Finally, the congress elected the Central Committee. The newly elected Central Committee included all members and alternate members of the previous Central Committee. The distinguished French scientist Frédéric Joliot-Curie was elected as a new member of the Central Committee. The congress elected a total of seventy-five members of the Central Committee.
    The Central Committee then elected the Politburo and the Secretariat. Fourteen people were elected as members of the Politburo, including Thorez, Duclos, Piette, and Fajon, with three alternate members. Thorez was elected General Secretary of the Secretariat, and Duclos, Servin, Duclos, Plaisance, and Villon were elected as secretaries.
    Representatives from the Communist and Workers' Parties of twenty-nine countries participated in the 14th Congress of the French Communist Party, and twenty-four fraternal parties sent congratulatory messages to the congress.
    During the meeting on the morning of the 21st, the congress adopted the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the French Communist Party to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The congratulatory message pointed out that the fraternal unity between the French and Soviet Communist Parties will greatly help to strengthen peace and the friendly relations between the two peoples.

### 多列士当选总书记

1956-07-23
第6版()
专栏：

    多列士当选总书记
    最后，大会选举了中央委员会。新选出的中央委员会包括上届中央委员会的全体委员和候补委员，杰出的法国科学家弗雷德里克·约里奥—居里当选为新的中央委员。代表大会一共选出七十五名中央委员会委员。
    中央委员会又选出了政治局和书记处，当选为政治局委员的有多列士、杜克洛、皮佑、法戎等十四人，候补委员三人。当选为书记处总书记的是多列士，杜克洛、赛尔文、杜科朗、普利桑尼埃、维恩等五人当选为书记。
    参加法国共产党第十四次代表大会的有二十九个国家共产党和工人党的代表，有二十四个兄弟党向大会发了贺电。
    在二十一日上午会议上，大会通过了法国共产党中央委员会致苏联共产党中央委员会的贺电，贺电指出，法国和苏联共产党的兄弟团结，将在很大程度上有助于加强和平和两国人民的友好关系。
