Title: French Communist Party Congress Hears Thorez Report
Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Havre, July 18th (TASS dispatch): The 14th Congress of the French Communist Party opened on July 18th in the Havre City Council Building. Attending the congress were 1,600 delegates and guests from fraternal Communist and Workers' Parties from various countries. Many French and foreign journalists also participated in the congress.
    The agenda of the congress included the following issues:
    Striving for social progress, defending peace, and fighting for France's national dignity (Reporter: Maurice Thorez);
    Municipal Councils serve the working masses (Reporter: Jacques Duclos);
    Election of party leadership.
    At today's meeting, Maurice Thorez, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, delivered a report on the first item on the agenda. It was greeted by stormy applause from the delegates to the congress.

### 法共代表大会听取多列士报告

1956-07-19
第6版()
专栏：

    法共代表大会听取多列士报告
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社哈佛尔讯：法国共产党第十四次代表大会七月十八日在哈佛尔市政委员会大厦开幕。出席代表大会的有一千六百名代表和来自各国兄弟共产党和工人党的来宾。许多法国和外国记者也参加了代表大会。
    代表大会议程有以下几个问题：
    争取社会进步、保卫和平和争取法国民族尊严（报告人莫里斯·多列士）；
    市政委员会为劳动大众服务（报告人雅克·杜克洛）；
    选举党领导机构。
    在今天的会议上，法国共产党中央委员会总书记莫里斯·多列士就第一项议程作了报告。受到代表大会代表们暴风雨般的鼓掌欢迎。
