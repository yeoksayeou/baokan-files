Shanghai Film Studio is busily filming a movie about the life of the Ming Dynasty medical scientist Li Shizhen. This is a photo of the film's director, Shen Fu (fourth from the left), and Zhao Dan (third from the left), who plays Li Shizhen, and Shu Xiuwen (first from the left), who plays a sick woman, studying the plot.
