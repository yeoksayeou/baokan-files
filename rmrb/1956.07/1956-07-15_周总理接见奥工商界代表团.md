Title: Premier Zhou Receives Austrian Business Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 14th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received the Austrian business delegation headed by Haslinger this afternoon.

Those present at the reception were Li Zucheng, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Xiao Fangzhou, Deputy Secretary-General, Kong Yuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Chen Ming, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau, and Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见奥工商界代表团

1956-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见奥工商界代表团
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在今天下午接见了以哈斯林格为首的奥地利工商界代表团。
    接见的时候在座的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席李烛尘、副秘书长萧方洲，对外贸易部副部长孔原、第三局副局长陈明，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华。
