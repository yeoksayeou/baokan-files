Title: Egypt Decides to Sue Britain in the International Court of Justice for Freezing Egyptian Deposits
Author:
Date: 1956-07-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 29th: The Middle East News Agency of Egypt announced on the evening of the 28th that Egypt has decided to submit the matter of Britain freezing Egyptian pound deposits in Britain to the International Court of Justice in The Hague.

The news agency warned that if any bank disregards the Egyptian government's order to freeze the overseas accounts of this nationalized company, the Egyptian government will take this into consideration when compensating the company's shareholders.

### 埃及决定向国际法院  控告英国冻结埃及存款

1956-07-30
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及决定向国际法院
    控告英国冻结埃及存款
    据新华社29日讯　开罗消息：埃及的中东通讯社二十八日晚间宣布，埃及已经决定把英国冻结埃及在英国的英镑存款一事提交海牙国际法院。
    这家通讯社警告说，如果任何银行漠视埃及政府的关于冻结这个收归国有的公司在国外的账目的命令的话，埃及政府将在对公司股东作赔偿的时候考虑到这一点。
