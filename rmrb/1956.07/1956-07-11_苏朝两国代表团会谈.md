Title: Soviet-Korean Delegation Holds Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th: According to TASS, the Soviet government delegation and the Korean government delegation began talks on the 9th in the Moscow Kremlin.

During the talks, the two government delegations exchanged views on further expanding economic and cultural cooperation between the two countries. The two sides agreed to entrust experts to carefully study the issues discussed in the talks and instructed them to make suggestions on these issues.

The Soviet side participating in the talks included: Bulganin, Khrushchev, Brezhnev, Shepilov, etc.; the Korean side included: Kim Il-sung, Park Jung-ae, Nam Il, Ri Jong-ok, etc.

The talks will continue.

### 苏朝两国代表团会谈

1956-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏朝两国代表团会谈
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府代表团和朝鲜政府代表团，九日在莫斯科克里姆林宫开始会谈。
    在会谈中，两国政府代表团就进一步扩大两国之间的经济和文化合作问题交换了意见。双方商妥委托专家来仔细研究在会谈中所谈到的问题，并且责成他们就这些问题提出建议。
    参加会谈的苏联方面有：布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫、勃列日涅夫、谢皮洛夫等；朝鲜方面有：金日成、朴正爱、南日、李钟玉等。
    会谈将继续举行。
