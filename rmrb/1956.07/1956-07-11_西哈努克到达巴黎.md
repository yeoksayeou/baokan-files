Title: Sihanouk Arrives in Paris
Author:
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 10th. Prince Sihanouk of Cambodia arrived in Paris on July 9th by special plane from Moscow.

### 西哈努克到达巴黎

1956-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    西哈努克到达巴黎
    新华社10日讯　巴黎消息：柬埔寨西哈努克亲王七月九日从莫斯科乘专机抵达巴黎。
