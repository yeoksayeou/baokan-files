Title: China Donates 10,000 Kyats to Aid Earthquake Victims in Burma

Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 28th. Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, on July 27th, presented a check for 10,000 Kyats on behalf of the Chinese government to Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio as relief funds for the recent earthquake victims in Burma. He also expressed his condolences to the earthquake victims.

It was previously reported that a series of earthquakes recently occurred in northern Burma, causing casualties and the collapse of houses.

### 我国赠送一万缅元  救济缅甸地震灾民

1956-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我国赠送一万缅元
    救济缅甸地震灾民
    新华社仰光28日电　中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明在七月二十七日代表中国政府把一张一万缅元的支票交给缅甸外交部长藻昆卓作为对缅甸最近的地震受灾者的救济费用，他并且对地震受灾者表示慰问。
    前已报道，最近缅甸北部曾连续发生地震，造成伤亡和房屋倒塌。
