Title: San Marino Decides to Establish Normal Relations with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 5

    San Marino Decides to Establish Normal Relations with the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, San Marino, July 3rd: News from San Marino: The Republic of San Marino, located within Italy, has decided to establish normal relations with the Soviet Union. The country's Grand and General Council passed a bill yesterday agreeing to the Soviet Union sending a consul to the country.
    According to the Associated Press, this is the first time since the founding of San Marino in the fourth century AD that it has accepted a Russian consul.
    San Marino is one of the smallest countries in Europe, with an area of sixty square kilometers and a population of less than fifteen thousand. The Grand and General Council has a total of sixty seats, of which the Communist Party and the left-wing Socialist Party occupy thirty-five seats, making it the only country in Western Europe where left-wing forces have a majority in parliament.

### 圣马力诺决定同苏联建立正常关系

1956-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    圣马力诺决定同苏联建立正常关系
    新华社3日讯　圣马力诺消息：位置在意大利境内的圣马力诺共和国决定同苏联建立正常关系。这个国家的大议会昨天通过一项议案，同意苏联向这个国家派驻领事。
    合众社的消息说，这是圣马力诺从公元四世纪建国以来第一次接受一个俄国领事。
    圣马力诺是欧洲最小的国家之一，它的面积是六十平方公里，人口不到一万五千人。大议会一共有六十个议席，共产党和左翼社会党占三十五席，它是西欧唯一左派力量在议会中占优势的国家。
