Title: Award Ceremony Held for Fujian Frontline Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-07-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 29th - Personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Fujian who have rendered meritorious service in the People's Revolutionary War have recently been awarded the August 1st Medal, the Independence and Freedom Medal, and the Liberation Medal, respectively. Award ceremonies were held in each unit separately. Some units stationed on the coastal defense frontline held small award ceremonies in the woods and on the positions even while the Chiang Kai-shek troops were shelling. After the awards were presented, the recipients also held discussions on how to strive for greater honor in the historical task of defending the motherland's coastal defense and liberating Taiwan. In the evening, each unit held a celebratory evening party.

### 福建前线部队举行授奖典礼

1956-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    福建前线部队
    举行授奖典礼
    新华社福建前线29日电　中国人民解放军驻福建各部队在人民革命战争中有功的人员，最近分别荣获八一奖章、独立自由奖章和解放奖章。授奖仪式分别在各部队举行。有些驻守在海防前线的部队，在蒋军进行炮击时，仍在树林里、阵地上举行了小型授奖典礼。在授奖以后，荣获奖章的人员还对怎样在保卫祖国海防和解放台湾的历史任务中争取更大的荣誉这个问题进行了座谈。晚间各部队都召开了祝贺晚会。
