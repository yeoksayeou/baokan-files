Title: Chairman Mao Cables Lao King to Congratulate Him on His Birthday and Laotian National Day
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 13th—Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to King Sisavang Vong of the Kingdom of Laos on July 12th, congratulating him on his birthday and the national day of the Kingdom of Laos. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows:
His Majesty King Sisavang Vong of the Kingdom of Laos:
    On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Laos, the Chinese people and I extend warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the Laotian people.
                                        Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
                                                        July 12, 1956, in Beijing

### 毛主席致电老挝国王祝贺他的寿辰和老挝国庆

1956-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席致电老挝国王祝贺他的寿辰和老挝国庆
    新华社13日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东七月十二日打电报给老挝王国国王西萨望·汪，祝贺他的寿辰和老挝王国国庆。贺电全文如下：老挝王国国王西萨望·汪陛下：
    当陛下寿辰和老挝王国国庆日的时候，中国人民和我谨向陛下和老挝人民表示热烈的祝贺。
                              中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                        1956年7月12日于北京
