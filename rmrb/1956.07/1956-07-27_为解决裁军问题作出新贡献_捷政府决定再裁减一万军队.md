Title: Making New Contributions to Solving the Disarmament Problem: Czechoslovak Government Decides to Further Reduce Military by 10,000 Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-07-27
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 26th. The Czechoslovak government announced on July 25th that it had decided to further reduce its armed forces by 10,000 personnel.

This is the second troop reduction by Czechoslovakia after reducing its military by 34,000 personnel in the second half of last year.

The Czechoslovak government pointed out in a communique that this measure was taken to contribute to the efforts of all countries in disarmament. In this way, Czechoslovakia's industry, agriculture, and other sectors of the national economy will increase its workforce, thereby helping the development of domestic peaceful construction.

### 为解决裁军问题作出新贡献  捷政府决定再裁减一万军队

1956-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    为解决裁军问题作出新贡献
    捷政府决定再裁减一万军队
    新华社布拉格26日电　捷克斯洛伐克政府在七月二十五日宣布，它已经决定把武装部队再裁减一万人。
    这是捷克斯洛伐克在去年下半年裁军三万四千人以后的第二次裁军。
    捷克斯洛伐克政府在一项公报中指出，采取这个措施是为了在各国裁军的努力方面作出贡献。这样，捷克斯洛伐克的工业、农业和国民经济的其他部门将增加一批人力，从而有助于国内和平建设的发展。
