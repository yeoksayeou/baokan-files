Title: Korean Government Delegation Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1956-07-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 16th. A Korean government delegation, led by Prime Minister Kim Il-sung, arrived in Ulaanbaatar this afternoon for a friendly visit to Mongolia at the invitation of the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural, and more than 200 prominent figures from all walks of life in Ulaanbaatar.

Diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union, China and other countries to Mongolia also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 朝鲜政府代表团到达乌兰巴托

1956-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团到达乌兰巴托
    据新华社16日讯　乌兰巴托消息：应蒙古人民共和国政府邀请到蒙古作友好访问的朝鲜政府代表团在金日成首相率领下，今天下午到达乌兰巴托。
    前往机场欢迎的有蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴、大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布和乌兰巴托市各界著名人士二百多人。
    苏联、中国和其他国家驻蒙古使节也到机场欢迎。
