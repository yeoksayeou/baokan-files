Title: Sihanouk Meets with Bulganin
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. July. TASS reports: After a two-day visit to Leningrad, Cambodian Prince Sihanouk and his entourage returned to Moscow on July 6.

On the same day as his return to the Soviet capital, Prince Sihanouk held talks with Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin and Foreign Minister Shepilov in the Kremlin.

### 西哈努克同布尔加宁会谈

1956-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克同布尔加宁会谈
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社报道：柬埔寨西哈努克亲王和他的随行人员在列宁格勒访问了两天以后，已经于七月六日回到莫斯科。
    在回到苏联首都的当天，西哈努克亲王同苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、外交部长谢皮洛夫在克里姆林宫举行了会谈。
