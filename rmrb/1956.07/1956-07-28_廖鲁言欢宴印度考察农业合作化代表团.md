Title: Liao Luyan Hosts Banquet for Indian Agricultural Cooperativization Study Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Indian Agricultural Cooperativization Study Delegation, headed by R.K. Patil, member of the Legislative Assembly of the Central Provinces of India. Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

Also present at the banquet tonight were Vice Ministers of Agriculture Liu Ruilong, Yang Xiandong, and Cai Ziwei; Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, Deng Chenxi; and more than 30 representatives from departments such as the Rural Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Land Reclamation, the Head Office of the Agricultural Bank of China, and the Central Administration of Handicraft Industry.

### 廖鲁言欢宴印度考察农业合作化代表团

1956-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    廖鲁言欢宴印度考察农业合作化代表团
    新华社27日讯　农业部长廖鲁言今晚设宴欢迎以印度中央省立法会议议员尔·克·帕蒂尔为首的印度来华考察农业合作化代表团。印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和使馆人员也应邀参加了宴会。
    出席今晚宴会作陪的，还有农业部副部长刘瑞龙、杨显东、蔡子伟和中华全国供销合作总社副主任邓辰西以及中共中央农村工作部、外交部、农垦部、中国农业银行总行、中央手工业管理局等部门的代表三十多人。
