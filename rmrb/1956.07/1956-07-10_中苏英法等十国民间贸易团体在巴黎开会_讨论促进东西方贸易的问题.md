Title: Private Trade Groups from China, the Soviet Union, the UK, France, and Ten Other Countries Meet in Paris to Discuss Promoting East-West Trade

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 9th: Private trade groups from ten countries are scheduled to meet in Paris today under the auspices of the International Trade Promotion Committee to discuss ways to promote East-West trade.

International Trade Promotion Committee Secretary-General Chamberland said on July 8th that the meeting will "study what changes should be made in international trade following the recent improvement in the world situation." The meeting will study ways to eliminate "artificial barriers that still hinder international trade." Japanese representative Kumaichi Yamamoto said that representatives from various countries will propose the abolition or relaxation of embargoes at this meeting.

The countries participating in this meeting are Japan, Brazil, Argentina, the Soviet Union, China, Poland, France, Italy, Belgium, and the United Kingdom. The Chinese side attending the meeting is Ji Chao-ding, Vice-Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

The International Trade Promotion Committee is an organization composed of business people and economists from various countries around the world. The committee's headquarters are located in Vienna.

### 中苏英法等十国民间贸易团体在巴黎开会  讨论促进东西方贸易的问题

1956-07-10
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏英法等十国民间贸易团体在巴黎开会
    讨论促进东西方贸易的问题
    新华社9日讯　巴黎消息：在国际贸易促进委员会的主持下，十个国家的民间贸易团体定今天在巴黎举行会议，讨论促进东西方贸易的问题。
    国际贸易促进委员会秘书长香贝朗七月八日说，这次会议将“研究在世界局势最近改善以后在国际贸易方面应该作些什么改变”。会议将研究可以消除“仍在妨碍国际贸易的人为障碍”的办法。日本代表山本熊一说，各国代表在这次会议上将要提出取消或者放松禁运的建议。
    参加这次会议的国家是日本、巴西、阿根廷、苏联、中国、波兰、法国、意大利、比利时和英国。中国方面出席会议的是中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎。
    国际贸易促进委员会是由世界各国工商界人士和经济学者组成的一个机构，委员会会址设在维也纳。
