Author: 
Date: 1956-07-13

Reader, Author, Editor

    Strengthen the Publicity of Sports Activities
    Our newspapers publicize sports activities too little, and should not be limited to publicizing record-breaking for long periods. Newspapers should pay attention to the mass character of sports and vividly reflect the increasingly active grassroots sports activities. (Liang)

### 加强对体育活动的宣传

1956-07-13
第8版()
专栏：读者·作者·编者

    加强对体育活动的宣传
    我们的报纸对于体育活动宣传得太少，而且，不应该长期间只限于宣传打破纪录。报纸要注意到体育的群众性，把日趋活跃的基层体育活动生动的反映出来。（梁）
