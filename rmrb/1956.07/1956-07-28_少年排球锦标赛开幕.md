Title: National Junior Volleyball Tournament Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 4

    Volleyball Tournament for Youngsters Opens
    (Our report) The 1956 National Junior Volleyball Tournament opened yesterday at the Beijing Gymnasium. Huang Qixiang, Deputy Director of the Sports Commission, spoke at the opening ceremony, encouraging the young athletes to learn from each other in the competition and strive to improve their skills. After the opening ceremony, eight matches were held in the competition hall and practice hall, and almost all of the several thousand spectators who came to watch the matches were young people.
    Twenty-seven male and female junior volleyball teams from fourteen units, including Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Chongqing, Xi'an, Fuzhou, Nanjing (only male team), Shenyang, Harbin, Dalian, Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture, and Taishan County of Guangdong Province, are participating in this tournament. The players are all young people aged fourteen to seventeen.

### 少年排球锦标赛开幕

1956-07-28
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    少年排球锦标赛开幕
    本报讯　1956年全国少年排球锦标赛昨天在北京体育馆开幕。体育运动委员会副主任黄琪翔在开幕式上讲话，勉励少年运动员们在比赛中互相学习，努力提高技术。开幕式后，在比赛馆和练习馆分别进行了八场比赛，到场观看比赛的几千名观众，几乎全部是青少年。
    参加这次锦标赛的有北京、上海、天津、广州、武汉、重庆、西安、福州、南京、沈阳、哈尔滨、旅大、延边朝鲜族自治州和广东省台山县等十四个单位的二十七个男女少年排球队（南京只有男子队），队员都是十四岁到十七岁的少年。
