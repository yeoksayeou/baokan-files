Title: East German Prime Minister and Yemeni Crown Prince Sign Joint Communiqué; Both Countries Willing to Develop Friendly Relations and Conclude a Trade and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 1st. According to the Press Office of the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, a communiqué regarding the visit of the Crown Prince of Yemen to the German Democratic Republic has been signed in Berlin. The communiqué points out that both sides agree that all nations have the right to freedom and independence and the right to exist in conditions of peace and security. Both sides declare that the principles of the United Nations Charter and the principles of the Bandung Conference must be observed in international relations.

The communiqué was signed by the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, and the Crown Prince of Yemen, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister, Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr.

The communiqué also points out that during the talks, both countries expressed their willingness to continue developing friendly relations between the people of Yemen and the people of Germany, and to establish normal relations between them at an appropriate time. Prior to this, it is appropriate to continue developing trade and economic relations. To this end, the German Democratic Republic and Yemen have concluded a trade and payment agreement based on the principles of equality and mutual benefit.

### 民主德国总理和也门皇太子签署联合公报  两国愿意发展友好关系  并缔结了一项贸易和支付协定

1956-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国总理和也门皇太子签署联合公报
    两国愿意发展友好关系
    并缔结了一项贸易和支付协定
    新华社柏林1日电　据德意志民主共和国总理新闻处宣布，关于也门皇太子访问德意志民主共和国的公报已经在柏林签字。公报指出，双方一致认为，一切民族都有自由和独立的权利以及在和平和安全条件下生存的权利。双方宣布，在国际关系中必须遵守联合国宪章的原则和万隆会议的原则。
    这份公报是由德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥和也门皇太子、副首相兼外交大臣塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔签字的。
    公报还指出在会谈期间，两国都表示愿意继续发展也门人民和德国人民之间的友好关系，并且在适当时机建立它们之间的正常关系。而在这以前，继续发展贸易和经济关系是适当的。为了这个目的，德意志民主共和国和也门两国在平等互利原则的基础上缔结了一项贸易和支付协定。
