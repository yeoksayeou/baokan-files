Title: Beautifying People's Lives: Cosmetic Factories Busily Produce
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 2

    Beautifying People's Lives
    Cosmetic Factories Busily Produce
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 15th – Due to the increasing need for people to beautify their lives, dozens of cosmetic factories in Shanghai are busier than ever before.
    In the first half of this year, Shanghai's perfume production increased more than sevenfold compared to the same period last year. Currently, among the more than one hundred kinds of perfumes produced in Shanghai, famous brands such as "Meidi" and "Moon" are selling well in major cities across the country. The famous White Rose Cologne, whose production was discontinued for many years by the state-owned Shanghai Soap Factory, has also recently resumed production.
    The production of cosmetics such as face powder, rouge, and lipstick has also greatly increased in the first half of this year. "Moon" brand face powder is available in white, jade, and beige, allowing users to freely choose the color that suits their skin characteristics. This type of face powder contains more oil, which can make the skin delicate and soft. The gorgeously decorated "Huada Baoli" lipstick is the most valuable type of lipstick. This lipstick does not melt in summer or solidify in winter. When applied thinly to the lips, it has a beautiful color and a faint fragrance.
    Among the new products this year is the high-quality "Friendship" brand vanishing cream produced by the "Star" Household Chemicals Manufacturing Factory. The factory's inexpensive, simply packaged "Star" vanishing cream has also increased its production several times this year. The factory is also preparing to trial-produce a shaving soap specially for men in the third quarter of this year.

### 美化人民的生活  化妆品工厂生产繁忙

1956-07-17
第2版()
专栏：

    美化人民的生活
    化妆品工厂生产繁忙
    新华社上海15日电　由于人们美化生活的需要日益增长，上海几十家生产化妆品的工厂空前地忙碌起来了。
    今年上半年上海香水的产量比去年同期增长了七倍多。现在，在上海生产的一百多种香水中，美迪牌和月亮牌等名牌香水，已经畅销到全国各大城市。国营上海制皂厂停产多年的著名白玫瑰香头水，最近也恢复了生产。
    香粉、胭脂、唇膏等化妆品的产量，在今年上半年里也都增长很大。月亮牌香粉有白色、玉色和米色三种，使用者可以根据自己的皮肤特点自由地选择适合的颜色。这种香粉含油脂较多，可使皮肤细腻柔润。装璜华丽的华达宝利唇膏是最名贵的一种唇膏。这种唇膏夏季不溶化，冬季也不凝固，在唇上薄薄的涂上一层，色泽鲜美，并有一种淡淡的香味。
    在今年的新产品中，还有明星家用化学品制造厂生产的高级友谊牌雪花膏。这个工厂生产的物美价廉的简装明星雪花膏，今年的产量也提高了好几倍。这个厂在今年第三季度中，还准备特为男人们试制一种剃须皂。
