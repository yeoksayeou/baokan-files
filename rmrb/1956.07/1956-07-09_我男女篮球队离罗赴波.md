Title: Our Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Depart Romania for Poland
Author:
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 8th: Bucharest dispatch: The Chinese men's and women's basketball teams left Bucharest for Warsaw by plane on July 7th.

    The Chinese men's and women's basketball teams visited and competed in various parts of Romania for more than half a month. They played eight games each with the Romanian men's and women's basketball teams. The results of the Chinese men's team were: four wins, two draws, and two losses. The results of the Chinese women's team were six wins and two losses.

### 我男女篮球队离罗赴波

1956-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我男女篮球队离罗赴波
    新华社布拉格8日电　布加勒斯特消息：中国男女篮球队七月七日乘飞机离布加勒斯特前往华沙。
    中国男女篮球队在罗马尼亚各地进行了半个多月的访问和比赛。他们同罗马尼亚男女篮球队各举行了八次比赛。中国男子队的比赛结果是：胜四次，平两次，败两次。中国女子队的比赛结果是胜六次，败两次。
