Title: US Auto Production Cuts and Poor Sales: First Half Production Down a Quarter from Last Year
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th, New York dispatch: The "Wall Street Journal" reported that US automobile production in the first half of this year decreased by a quarter compared to the same period last year. The production figure was more than 3,192,000 vehicles.

### 美国汽车减产滞销  上半年产量比去年下降四分之一

1956-07-29
第6版()
专栏：

    美国汽车减产滞销
    上半年产量比去年下降四分之一
    据新华社27日讯　纽约消息：“华尔街日报”报道，今年上半年美国汽车生产比去年同期下降了四分之一。生产的数字是三百一十九万二千多辆。
