Title: China and Austria Sign Forty-Seven Trade Contracts
Author:
Date: 1956-07-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 21st - The Austrian business delegation has returned home in batches after visiting China, with the last group of ten leaving Beijing on the morning of the 21st.

During their stay in Beijing, the delegation held extensive trade talks with China's specialized import and export corporations, signing forty-seven trade contracts with a total import and export value of 5,552,000 pounds sterling. This figure greatly exceeds the total trade volume between China and Austria for the entire year of 1955.

The representatives visited factories, farms, universities, tractor stations, and various exhibitions in Beijing, Shenyang, and Anshan. Some representatives also visited Shanghai and Guangzhou.

Chinese economic figures held a forum for the delegation. The head of the delegation, Professor Limburg Bergs, Chairman of the Austrian East-West Trade Association, gave a report on Austria's economic situation.

### 中奥签订四十七个贸易合同

1956-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中奥签订四十七个贸易合同
    新华社21日讯　奥地利工商界代表团在中国访问后已分批返国，最后一批十人在二十一日晨离开北京。
    代表团在北京期间，和中国进出口各专业公司广泛地进行了贸易商谈，签订了四十七个贸易合同，双方进出口总金额为五百五十五万二千英镑，这个数字大大地超过了1955年全年中奥的贸易额。
    代表们在北京、沈阳和鞍山参观了工厂、农场、大学、拖拉机站和各种展览会。部分代表还访问了上海和广州。
    中国经济界人士为代表团举行了座谈会，代表团团员，奥地利东西贸易协会主席林布瑞兹柏格教授曾作了有关奥地利经济情况的报告。
