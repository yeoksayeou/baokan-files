Title: Seven More National Records Broken in Four-Unit Swimming Competition
Author:
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency report: In the four-unit swimming competition held between Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army in 1956, two relay teams and six athletes broke seven national records on June 30th.

Wang Qiangli, the Beijing athlete who broke the national record for the men's 200-meter butterfly on the 29th, set a new national record of 1 minute 6.7 seconds in the men's 100-meter butterfly today.

In the men's 200-meter breaststroke competition, Xu Zhixiang, the national record holder for the men's 200-meter breaststroke, was caught up by Qi Lieyun from Guangzhou in the last 35 meters. Both reached the finish line simultaneously with a time of 2 minutes 41 seconds, breaking Xu Zhixiang's national record of 2 minutes 42 seconds.

In the men's 400-meter freestyle competition, Li Xiqing, an athlete from the Chinese People's Liberation Army, broke Lin Jinzhu's national record with a time of 4 minutes 47.4 seconds.

In the women's 400-meter freestyle competition, Fu Cuimei broke Liang Shumei's national record of 6 minutes 3.8 seconds, set in 1954, with a time of 5 minutes 52.6 seconds. This is the first time that a Chinese female athlete has completed the 400 meters in under six minutes.

In the women's 100-meter breaststroke competition, Dai Lihua clocked 1 minute 25.4 seconds, 2.8 seconds faster than her own national record of 1 minute 28.2 seconds.

The women's 4x100-meter freestyle relay team, consisting of Xiao Lihui, Pan Jialing, Dai Lihua, and Fu Cuimei, completed the 400 meters in 4 minutes 59 seconds. This result is 14.7 seconds faster than the national record of 5 minutes 13.7 seconds.

The men's 4x100-meter medley relay team, consisting of Huang Tansheng, Mu Xiangxiong, Wang Qiangli, and Mu Ruilong, completed the entire distance in 4 minutes 29.8 seconds. This result is 5.2 seconds faster than the national record of 4 minutes 35 seconds.

### 在四单位游泳对抗赛中又有七项全国纪录被打破

1956-07-02
第7版()
专栏：

    在四单位游泳对抗赛中又有七项全国纪录被打破
    据新华社讯　1956年北京、上海、广州和中国人民解放军四单位游泳对抗赛，在六月三十日又有两个接力队和六个运动员打破了七项全国纪录。
    二十九日打破男子二百公尺蝶泳全国纪录的北京市运动员王强立，今天又创造了男子一百公尺蝶泳一分六秒七的全国新成绩。
    在男子二百公尺蛙泳比赛中，男子二百公尺蛙泳全国纪录保持者徐致祥在最后三十五公尺的时候，被广州市的戚烈云追上。两人都以二分四十一秒的成绩同时到达终点。打破了徐致祥保持的二分四十二秒的全国纪录。
    在男子四百公尺自由泳比赛中，中国人民解放军运动员李喜庆，以四分四十七秒四，打破了林锦珠保持的全国纪录。
    女运动员傅翠美在女子四百公尺自由泳比赛中，以五分五十二秒六的成绩打破了梁树妹在1954年创造的六分三秒八的全国纪录。这是我国女运动员第一次在六分钟以内游完四百公尺。
    女子一百公尺蛙泳比赛中戴丽华以一分二十五秒四，比她自己保持的一分二十八秒二的全国纪录快了二秒八。
    以萧黎辉、潘家玲、戴丽华和傅翠美四个女运动员组成的女子四百公尺自由泳接力队，在四分五十九秒中游完了四百公尺。这个成绩比五分十三秒七的全国纪录快了十四秒七。
    由黄谭胜、穆祥雄、王强立和穆瑞龙组成的男子四百公尺混合式接力队，他们以四分二十九秒八的成绩游完了全程。这个成绩比四分三十五秒的全国纪录快了五秒二。
