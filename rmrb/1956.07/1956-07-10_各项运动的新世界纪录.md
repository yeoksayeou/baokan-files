Title: New World Records in Various Sports
Author:
Date: 1956-07-10
Page: 6

·New World Records in Various Sports·
    Athletics: On July 8th, Soviet athlete M. Krivonosov set a new world record in hammer throw in Minsk. His result was 66 meters 38 centimeters, 41 centimeters further than the best result of K. Blair of the United States on July 4th.
    On June 30th, Polish athlete J. Sidlo's javelin throw in Milan, Italy, reached 83 meters 66 centimeters, 10 centimeters further than the recent result of Finnish athlete Nikkinen, while the official world record is 81 meters 75 centimeters.
    On June 30th, American athlete Lou Jones broke the world record in the 400-meter race with a time of 45.2 seconds, while the previous record was 45.4 seconds.
  Swimming: American female athlete S. Mann broke several world records in swimming competitions from July 5th to 7th. She participated in a competition in a 50-meter swimming pool. Her time for the 100-meter butterfly was 1 minute 14.5 seconds (the previous best was 1 minute 15 seconds); her time for the 200-meter butterfly was 2 minutes 44.4 seconds (the previous world record was 2 minutes 48.8 seconds); and her time for the 400-meter individual medley was 5 minutes 52.5 seconds (the previous world record was 6 minutes 6.9 seconds).
    On July 5th, American 16-year-old female student K. Koen also broke a world record. Her time for the 200-meter backstroke (50-meter swimming pool) was 2 minutes 43.8 seconds, 1.8 seconds faster than the original world record.
    Car Racing: At the end of June, Soviet athlete A. Ambrosenkov drove a car with a cylinder capacity of 250 cubic centimeters on the road in Moscow, with an average speed of 200 kilometers 557 meters per hour (calculated after the car starts and travels one kilometer), about 4 kilometers faster than the previous world record.
    On June 29th, French cyclist J. Anquetil broke a world record in cycling: he cycled a distance of 46 kilometers 159 meters in one hour, while the previous record was 45 kilometers 798 meters.
                (Xinhua News Agency, 9th)

### 各项运动的新世界纪录

1956-07-10
第6版()
专栏：

    ·各项运动的新世界纪录·
    田径赛　七月八日，苏联运动员米·克里沃诺索夫在明斯克创造了掷链球的新世界纪录。他的成绩是六十六公尺三十八公分，比美国运动员克·布莱尔在七月四日创造的最好成绩远四十一公分。
    波兰运动员雅·西德洛六月三十日在意大利的米兰掷标枪的成绩是八十三公尺六十六公分，比芬兰运动员尼基宁最近创造的成绩远十公分，而正式的世界纪录是八十一公尺七十五公分。
    美国运动员刘·琼斯六月三十日打破了四百公尺赛跑的世界纪录，成绩是四十五秒二，而以前的纪录是四十五秒四。
  游泳　美国女运动员谢·曼恩从七月五日到七日，接连打破了几项游泳比赛的世界纪录。她是在一个五十公尺的游泳池参加比赛的，她的一百公尺蝶式游泳的成绩是一分十四秒五（以前最好的成绩是一分十五秒）；二百公尺蝶式游泳的成绩是二分四十四秒四（以前的世界纪录是二分四十八秒八）四百公尺个人混合姿式的成绩是五分五十二秒五（以前的世界纪录是六分六秒九）。
    美国一个十六岁的女学生卡·柯恩在七月五日也打破了一项世界纪录，她的二百公尺仰泳（五十公尺的游泳池）的成绩是二分四十三秒八，比原有的世界纪录快一秒八。
    车赛　苏联运动员阿·阿姆布罗先科夫六月底在莫斯科的公路上驾驶气缸容积为二百五十公分的汽车赛跑，每小时的平均速度是二百公里五百五十七公尺（汽车开动后经过一公里开始计算），比以前的世界纪录约快四公里。
    法国自行车运动员雅·安克蒂尔六月二十九日打破了一项自行车竞赛的世界纪录：他在一小时内骑自行车驶完了四十六公里159公尺的距离，以前的纪录是四十五公里798公尺。
                （新华社九日讯）
