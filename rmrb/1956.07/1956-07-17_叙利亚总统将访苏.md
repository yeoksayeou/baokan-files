Title: Syrian President to Visit Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 16th: The Syrian government announced on July 15th that Syrian President Shukri al-Quwatli will visit the Soviet Union in October this year.

Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov extended an invitation from the Soviet government to Quwatli to visit the Soviet Union during his recent visit to the Middle East. Quwatli's visit will take place in the second half of October.

### 叙利亚总统将访苏

1956-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    叙利亚总统将访苏
    新华社16日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚政府七月十五日宣布：叙利亚总统舒克里·库阿特利将在今年十月访问苏联。
    苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫最近访问中东的时候曾代表苏联政府邀请库阿特利访问苏联。库阿特利将在十月下半月成行。
