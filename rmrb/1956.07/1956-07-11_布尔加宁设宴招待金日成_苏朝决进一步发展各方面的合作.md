Title: Bulganin Hosts Banquet for Kim Il-sung; Soviet-Korean Decision to Further Develop Cooperation in All Aspects
Author:
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 10th -- TASS reports: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, held a reception in the Grand Kremlin Palace on July 9th in honor of the Premier of the Korean Cabinet, Kim Il-sung.

    Bulganin said in his address at the banquet: “The friendship between the Soviet Union and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has grown and been consolidated in the struggle to establish an independent, democratic, and socialist system in Korea." He said, "The Korean people are not isolated in their just struggle for national independence and a better life for the Korean people. They have received the warm sympathy and fraternal support of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and all socialist countries."

    Bulganin said that the Korean people still face many difficulties. But we believe that the Korean people are capable of overcoming these difficulties. "Your visit to our country is undoubtedly a new contribution to the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the Soviet and Korean peoples. This visit will help to further develop the cooperation between our two brotherly peoples in all aspects of political, economic, and cultural life."

    Kim Il-sung then gave a reply speech. He said, "The Korean people do not feel isolated, because they have reliable friends and brothers like the great people of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and the people of other people's democratic countries. They regard the unbreakable friendship formed with the people of these countries as an important source of their strength."

    "The working people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea are fully determined to make every effort to consolidate the people's democratic system in North Korea and to unswervingly strive to consolidate peace and achieve the peaceful reunification of Korea on a democratic basis."

    Kim Il-sung said, "Like all peace-loving people in the world, the Korean people warmly support the Soviet government's consistent Leninist policy of peace and warmly support its great efforts in consolidating the cause of world peace."

    "The Korean people are also sincerely happy for the outstanding achievements made by the Soviet people under the leadership of the glorious Communist Party of the Soviet Union in communist construction."

    Members of the Korean government delegation also attended the reception.

    The Soviet side present at the reception included: Voroshilov, Malenkov, Molotov, Pervukhin, Khrushchev, Zhukov, Brezhnev, Shepilov, Furtseva, Belyaev, Pospelov and others.

### 布尔加宁设宴招待金日成  苏朝决进一步发展各方面的合作

1956-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁设宴招待金日成
    苏朝决进一步发展各方面的合作
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁七月九日在大克里姆林宫举行招待会，招待朝鲜内阁首相金日成。
    布尔加宁在宴会上致词说：“苏联和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国之间的友谊，在争取在朝鲜建立独立、民主和社会主义制度的斗争中已经成长和巩固起来了”。他说，“朝鲜人民在他们争取民族独立、争取朝鲜人民美好生活的正义斗争中不是孤立的，他们得到了苏联、中华人民共和国和所有社会主义国家的热烈同情和兄弟的支援。”
    布尔加宁说，朝鲜人民还有不少的困难。但是我们相信朝鲜人民是能够克服这些困难的。“你们来我国访问，无疑地是对苏朝两国永恒的、牢不可破的友谊的新贡献，这次访问将有助于进一步发展我们两国兄弟人民在政治、经济和文化生活各个方面的合作。”
    金日成接着致答词。他说，“朝鲜人民不觉得自己是孤立的，因为他们有苏联、中华人民共和国的伟大人民和其他人民民主国家的人民这样一些可靠的朋友和兄弟。他们把同这些国家人民结成的牢不可破的友谊看成是自己力量的重要源泉。”
    “朝鲜民主主义人民共和国劳动人民抱定充分决心，要全力巩固朝鲜北部的人民民主制度，并且坚定不移地争取巩固和平、争取在民主的基础上和平统一朝鲜。”
    金日成说，“像全世界一切爱好和平的人民一样，朝鲜人民热烈支持苏联政府实行列宁的一贯的和平政策，热烈支持它在巩固世界和平事业中作的巨大努力。”
    “朝鲜人民也为苏联人民在光荣的苏联共产党领导下在共产主义建设方面所获得的杰出成就衷心地感到高兴。”
    朝鲜政府代表团的团员们也出席了这次招待会。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有；伏罗希洛夫、马林科夫、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、赫鲁晓夫、朱可夫、勃列日涅夫、谢皮洛夫、福尔采娃、别利亚耶夫、波斯伯洛夫等人。
