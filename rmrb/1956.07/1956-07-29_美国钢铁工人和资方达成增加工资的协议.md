Title: US Steelworkers and Employers Reach Agreement on Wage Increase
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 28th: The strike of 650,000 steelworkers, which began at midnight on July 1st, will end in a few days, as American steel industry labor and management reached an agreement on a new wage contract on July 27th.

According to company estimates, the strike has already resulted in a loss of nine million tons of steel production, and its impact on industry will extend to the end of this year and even the beginning of next year.

### 美国钢铁工人和资方达成增加工资的协议

1956-07-29
第6版()
专栏：

    美国钢铁工人和资方
    达成增加工资的协议
    据新华社28日讯　纽约消息：从七月一日零时开始的六十五万钢铁工人的罢工将在几天内结束，因为美国钢铁业的劳资双方已经在七月二十七日就新工资合同达成了协议。
    据公司方面估计，罢工已经使得钢铁生产损失了九百万吨，它对工业的影响将延及今年年底甚至明年年初。
