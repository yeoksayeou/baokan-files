Title: Unwilling to Return to Taiwan, Determined to Return to the Mainland: Chiang Army 2nd Lieutenant Returns to Guangzhou from Japan
Author:
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou Dispatch: Chiang Army 2nd Lieutenant Wang Chunguang returned to Guangzhou from Japan via Hong Kong on the 15th, and was warmly received by the leadership of the People's Liberation Army garrison in Guangzhou.

Wang Chunguang was a 2nd Lieutenant and probationary platoon leader in the 3rd Battalion of the Chiang Army's 9th Army Reconnaissance Regiment. Some time ago, he and a group of Chiang Army officers went to South Korea for vacation. After the vacation, while passing through Japan on his way back to Taiwan, he resolved to choose the path of light and found a way to return to the motherland mainland from Japan via Hong Kong.

### 不愿回台湾决心回大陆  蒋军一少尉由日返广州

1956-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    不愿回台湾决心回大陆
    蒋军一少尉由日返广州
    据新华社广州电　蒋军少尉王春光十五日从日本经香港回到广州，受到中国人民解放军驻广州部队领导机关的热情接待。
    王春光是蒋军第九军搜索团三营的少尉见习排长，不久前，他和一批蒋军军官到南朝鲜休假。休假结束后在路过日本准备回台湾的时候，他决心选择光明的道路，设法从日本经香港回到祖国大陆。
