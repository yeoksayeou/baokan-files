Title: Our Army in Fujian Shoots Down One Chiang Kai-shek Plane

Author:

Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, July 8th. At 5:24 PM on July 7th, four Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter planes intruded into the airspace over the Dongshan Island area of Fujian. One of the lead planes had its right wing damaged by the anti-aircraft artillery of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Defense Force.

From July 1st to July 7th, during the week, Chiang Kai-shek's planes made 39 sorties with 114 aircraft intrusions into the coastal areas of Fujian and along the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway.

### 福建我军打伤蒋机一架

1956-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    福建我军打伤蒋机一架
    新华社福建前线8日电　七日下午五时二十四分，蒋军F—84型战斗机四架窜扰福建东山岛地区上空，被中国人民解放军防空军高射炮兵部队击伤其中一架长机的右翼。
    从七月一日到七日一周间，蒋军飞机共有三十九批114架次侵入建建沿海地区和鹰厦铁路沿线。
