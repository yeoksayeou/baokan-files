Title: Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region: All Fourteen Departments, Commissions, and Offices Established
Author:
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 30th - Apé Awang Jinmei, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, announced today that all fourteen departments, commissions, and offices under the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region have been established.

This afternoon, a meeting was held here for the establishment of the Religious Affairs Commission and five offices for Culture and Education, Transportation, Construction, Public Security, and Justice. Apé Awang Jinmei said at the meeting that after the establishment of these institutions, various tasks will be greatly developed. He said: "Although the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region has been established for a short time, its words and actions have proved to be of great benefit to the people and to religion, and thus have gained widespread understanding and support from all strata of society."

The list of cadres above the section level in the various institutions under the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region has been approved and announced by the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region. The vast majority of the staff of these institutions are Tibetan. They come from all parts of Tibet and all walks of life.

### 西藏自治区筹备委员会  十四个厅、委、处全部成立

1956-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

    西藏自治区筹备委员会
    十四个厅、委、处全部成立
    新华社拉萨30日电　西藏自治区筹备委员会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美今天宣布：西藏自治区筹备委员会所属的十四个厅、委、处已经全部成立。
    今天下午，这里曾举行了成立宗教事务委员会和文教、交通、建设、公安、司法等五个处的会议。阿沛·阿旺晋美在会上说，这些机构成立后，各项工作都将大大地开展起来。他说：“西藏自治区筹备委员会成立起来的时间虽然很短，但是它的一言一行都证明了对人民对宗教都有极大的好处，因而获得各阶层广泛的了解和支持。”
    西藏自治区筹备委员会所属各机构科级以上干部的名单已经由西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员会批准公布。这些机构的工作人员绝大多数是藏族。他们来自西藏各地和各个阶层。
