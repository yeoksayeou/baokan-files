Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Postpones Visit to Indonesia Due to Illness
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 16th: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, at the invitation of President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia and the Indonesian government, was scheduled to visit Indonesia from July 18th to 28th this year. On July 10th, Vice Chairman Soong and her entourage left Beijing by plane for Kunming, preparing to fly from Kunming to Jakarta via Rangoon.

    When Vice Chairman Soong left Beijing, those who saw her off at the airport included Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong, Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yun, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, General Chen Geng, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Deng Yingchao and Li Dequan, Secretary-General of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association Ren Bosheng, Secretary-General of the Red Cross Society of China Wu Yunfu, and Deputy Secretaries-General Ni Feijun and Gu Jinxin.

    Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China, Anwar Sani, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy in China, U Ba Maw, also saw her off at the airport.

    After Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling arrived in Kunming, she suddenly felt unwell. After diagnosis by doctors, she was temporarily deemed unfit for long-distance travel. Therefore, she could not go to Indonesia for a visit as scheduled. Regarding the timing of Vice Chairman Soong's visit to Indonesia, it is reported that it will be re-negotiated by the two governments after her recovery.

### 宋庆龄副委员长因病延期访问印度尼西亚

1956-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副委员长因病延期访问印度尼西亚
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会宋庆龄副委员长应印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统和印度尼西亚政府的邀请，确定今年七月十八日到二十八日访问印度尼西亚。七月十日宋副委员长和她的随行人员离开北京飞往昆明，准备由昆明乘飞机经仰光前往雅加达。
    宋副委员长离北京时到机场送行的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通，国务院副总理陈云、秘书长习仲勋、外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，中国人民解放军副总参谋长陈赓大将，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、李德全，中国印度尼西亚友好协会总干事任泊生，中国人民救济总会秘书长伍云甫、副秘书长倪斐君、顾锦心等。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办安瓦·萨尼和缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办吴巴貌也到机场送行。
    宋庆龄副委员长到达昆明后突感身体不适，经医生诊断暂不宜长途旅行，因此已不能如期前往印度尼西亚进行访问。关于宋副委员长访问印度尼西亚的时间，据悉将在她病愈后由双方政府另行商定。
