Title: Burhan Meets with Prime Minister of Saudi Arabia, Both Parties Expressed a Desire to Develop Friendly Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1956-07-30
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Jeddah, July 29th: Burhan, head of the Chinese Islamic Hajj Delegation, and deputy heads Anniwar Hanbaba and Badruddin Jin Fengshan, met with Faisal, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, on July 25th.

    Head of the delegation, Burhan, conveyed Premier Zhou Enlai's greetings and regards to Prime Minister Faisal, and presented him with a gift. During the conversation, Prime Minister Faisal praised Premier Zhou Enlai's contributions at the Bandung Conference and expressed friendly concern for the Chinese Islamic Hajj Delegation.

    That evening, Head of the delegation, Burhan, also visited Mohammed Surur Sabban, the Minister of Finance of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

    Prior to this, Head of the delegation, Burhan, attended a banquet held for the heads of various Hajj delegations.

    On July 19th, Head of the delegation, Burhan, went to the Mina Palace to congratulate His Majesty the King of Saudi Arabia on the Eid al-Adha festival.

    In these contacts, both sides expressed their desire to safeguard world peace and develop friendly cooperation.

### 鲍尔汉拜会沙特阿拉伯首相  宾主一致表示要发展友好合作

1956-07-30
第5版()
专栏：

    鲍尔汉拜会沙特阿拉伯首相
    宾主一致表示要发展友好合作
    新华社吉达29日电　中国伊斯兰教朝觐团团长鲍尔汉、副团长安尼瓦尔·汉巴巴和巴德鲁丁·金凤山在七月二十五日拜会了沙特阿拉伯王国首相费萨尔。
    鲍尔汉团长向费萨尔首相转达了周恩来总理对他的问候和致意，并且向他赠送了礼物。费萨尔首相在谈话中称赞周恩来总理在万隆会议中所作的贡献，并且对中国伊斯兰教朝觐团表示友好的关切。
    当天晚上，鲍尔汉团长还访问了沙特阿拉伯王国财政大臣穆罕默德·苏鲁尔·萨班。
    在这以前，鲍尔汉团长曾参加了为各个朝觐团团长举行的宴会。
    七月十九日，鲍尔汉团长曾到米纳王宫向沙特阿拉伯王国国王陛下祝贺古尔邦节。
    在这些接触中，宾主双方都表示了保卫世界和平和发展友好合作的愿望。
