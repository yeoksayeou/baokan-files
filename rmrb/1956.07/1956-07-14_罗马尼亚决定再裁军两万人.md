Title: Romania Decides to Reduce Armed Forces by Another 20,000

Author:

Date: 1956-07-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, July 12th: TASS reports that the Romanian Council of Ministers, in view of the progress made so far in easing international tensions, and in keeping with its own peace policy, has decided to further reduce its armed forces by 20,000 men before September 1st of this year.

The demobilized personnel will be assigned to jobs in productive construction, and the funds and materials released will be used to develop peaceful national economic construction and other social and cultural facilities.

### 罗马尼亚决定再裁军两万人

1956-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚决定再裁军两万人
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社布加勒斯特十二日讯：罗马尼亚部长会议鉴于在缓和国际紧张局势方面到现在为止所已经取得的成果，为忠于自己的和平政策，特决定在今年九月一日以前再裁减武装部队两万人。
    裁减的人员将被分配到生产建设的岗位上工作，腾出的资金和物资将用来发展和平的国民经济建设和用在其他社会文化设施方面。
