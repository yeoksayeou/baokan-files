Title: Bulgarian Ballet Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th. The Bulgarian Ballet Troupe, led by Lyuben Chakrov, consisting of nineteen members, arrived in Beijing by plane in two groups on the 23rd and 25th. Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture, and many people from Beijing's literary and art circles went to the airport to welcome them.

The Bulgarian Ballet Troupe is visiting China for performances according to the 1956 implementation plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The head of the troupe, Lyuben Chakrov, is the Deputy Director of the Music and Art Department of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of Bulgaria. The troupe is composed of outstanding actors from the Sofia People's Opera House. Honored actor, Dimitrov Prize winner, and ballet expert of the Sofia People's Opera House, Anastas Petrov, serves as the artistic director of the troupe. Famous ballet dancers in the troupe include Dimitrov Prize winner Lili Beron and Bucharest World Youth Festival winner Asen Gavrilov. The troupe will perform excerpts from famous ballets such as "Swan Lake" and "Romeo and Juliet," as well as the ballet "Idyll and Classical Dance" composed by Bulgarian composer Bebukov.

### 保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术团到京

1956-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术团到京
    新华社25日讯　以柳宾·查克罗夫为首的保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术团一行十九人，已在二十三日和二十五日分批乘飞机到达北京。文化部副部长郑振铎和北京文艺界人士多人曾前往机场欢迎。
    保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术团是根据中保文化合作协定1956年执行计划来中国进行访问演出的，艺术团团长柳宾·查克罗夫是保加利亚人民共和国文化部音乐艺术处副处长。艺术团是由索非亚人民歌剧院的优秀演员所组成。功勋演员、季米特洛夫奖金获得者、索非亚人民歌剧院芭蕾舞专家阿纳斯塔斯·彼得罗夫担任艺术团的艺术指导，团员中的著名芭蕾舞演员有季米特洛夫奖金获得者莉莉·贝朗、布加勒斯特世界青年联欢节获奖者阿森·加夫里洛夫等人。艺术团将要表演的有
  “天鹅湖”、“罗密欧与朱丽叶”等著名芭蕾舞剧中的片断，以及保加利亚作曲家贝布科夫创作的芭蕾舞“田园诗和古典舞”等节目。
