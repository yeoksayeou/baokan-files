Title: Mao Zedong Sends Congratulations on Ethiopian National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-07-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd – Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Emperor Haile Selassie I of Ethiopia to congratulate him on the Ethiopian National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
Your Majesty Haile Selassie I:
On the occasion of your country's National Day, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to you and the people of your country on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
Beijing, July 21, 1956

### 毛主席电贺埃塞俄比亚国庆

1956-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺埃塞俄比亚国庆
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东打电报给埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世祝贺埃塞俄比亚国庆。电文如下：海尔·塞拉西一世陛下：
    值此贵国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民，并以我个人的名义向您和贵国人民致热烈的祝贺。
                              中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                        1956年7月21日于北京
