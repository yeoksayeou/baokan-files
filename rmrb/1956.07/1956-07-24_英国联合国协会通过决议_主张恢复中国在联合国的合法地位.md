Title: British UN Association Passes Resolution Advocating Restoration of China's Legitimate Rights in the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1956-07-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd, London dispatch: The British United Nations Association, holding its annual meeting here on July 22nd, passed a resolution advocating the restoration of the legitimate rights of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations. The resolution urged the British government to support this action, stating that to insist that the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Taiwan "can still speak for or undertake obligations on behalf of the vast majority of the Chinese people is unjust, untrue, and even dishonest."

In another resolution, the association called on all countries to cease nuclear weapons testing until the United Nations Scientific Committee on the Effects of Atomic Radiation issues its final report. British scientist Phillips said that since the beginning of nuclear weapons testing, the amount of radioactivity has increased by 30% over Britain and 100% over the United States, and this evidence "is sufficient to justify a ban on further testing."

The association also passed a resolution calling for a ban on any nuclear weapons testing in United Nations trust territories.

### 英国联合国协会通过决议  主张恢复中国在联合国的合法地位

1956-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    英国联合国协会通过决议
    主张恢复中国在联合国的合法地位
    新华社23日讯　伦敦消息：在这里举行年会的英国联合国协会七月二十二日通过决议，主张恢复中华人民共和国在联合国的合法地位。决议要求英国政府支持这个行动，决议说，硬说台湾蒋介石集团“还能够代表中国的绝大多数人说话或者代表他们承担义务，那是不公正的，不真实的，甚至是不诚实的”。
    协会在另一项决议中要求各国在联合国科学委员会对原子能放射性的影响提出最后报告以前，停止进行核子武器试验。英国科学家菲立普斯说，自从核子武器试验开始以来，放射性的数量在英国上空已经增加了30％，在美国上空已经增加了100％，这些证据“足以证明应该禁止进一步的试验”。
    协会还通过一项决议，要求禁止在联合国托管领土上进行任何核子武器试验。
