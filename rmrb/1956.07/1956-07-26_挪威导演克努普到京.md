Title: Norwegian Director Knoop Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd: Norwegian director Knoop arrived in Beijing yesterday at the invitation of the Chinese Dramatists Association and the Chinese Writers Association to participate in the commemoration of the 50th anniversary of the death of Norwegian playwright Henrik Ibsen, a world cultural figure.
    Norwegian director Gaidarine, who was invited to China in early July to assist in directing the play "Nora," will also attend this commemoration as a representative of Norway.

### 挪威导演克努普到京

1956-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    挪威导演克努普到京
    新华社23日讯　应中国戏剧家协会和中国作家协会邀请来中国参加世界文化名人挪威戏剧家易卜生逝世五十周年纪念会的挪威导演克努普，昨天到达北京。
    在七月初应邀来中国协助导演“挪拉”一剧的挪威导演盖达琳，也将作为挪威的代表出席这一次纪念会。
