Title: Thai Authorities Arrest Three More Overseas Chinese
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-31
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th, Bangkok dispatch: According to a report in the "Liberty News" on the 25th, following the arrest of ten Overseas Chinese on the 21st, the Thai Public Security Bureau arrested three more Overseas Chinese in Bangkok, Si Thammarat in southern Thailand, and Lampang in northern Thailand on the 22nd and 24th on the grounds of allegedly violating the "Anti-Communist Regulations."

    Among those arrested, two are columnists for the "Overseas Chinese Masses Weekly" and "Lefeng Weekly."

### 泰国当局又捕三名华侨

1956-07-31
第5版()
专栏：

    泰国当局又捕三名华侨
    新华社30日讯　曼谷消息：据“自由通讯报”二十五日报道，泰国公安局继二十一日拘捕了十名华侨以后，又在二十二日和二十四日以所谓违反“防共条例”的名义在曼谷、泰国南部的西势洛坤和泰国北部的喃邦等地逮捕了三名华侨。
    被逮捕的三人中，有两人是“侨众周报”和“乐风周报”的专栏撰稿人。
