Title: Voroshilov Receives Hammarskjöld
Author:
Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd: TASS reports: Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, received UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on July 3rd.

On the same day, Soviet First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Kuznetsov, also received Hammarskjöld.

Hammarskjöld arrived in Moscow on the evening of July 2nd at the invitation of the Soviet government.

### 伏罗希洛夫接见哈马舍尔德

1956-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫接见哈马舍尔德
    据新华社3日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫在七月三日接见了联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德。
    同日，苏联外交部第一副部长库兹?佐夫也接见了哈马舍尔德。
    哈马舍尔德是应苏联政府的邀请在七月二日晚上到达莫斯科的。
