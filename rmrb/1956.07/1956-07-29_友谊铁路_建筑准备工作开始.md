Title: "Friendship Railway" Construction Preparations Begin
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 1

    "Friendship Railway" Construction Preparations Begin
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th. A construction and installation train recently arrived at Aktogay Station on the Turkestan-Siberia Railway in the Soviet Union, carrying the first group of volunteer workers from the Belorussian Republic participating in the "Friendship Railway" construction project. The construction preparations for the Soviet section of the Lanzhou-Urumqi-Aktogay Railway have thus begun. Near the residential area for railway employees at Aktogay Station, a construction worker village will be built as the base for the subsidiary enterprises of this new construction project.

### “友谊铁路”建筑准备工作开始

1956-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    “友谊铁路”建筑准备工作开始
    据新华社莫斯科28日电　一列建筑安装列车最近到达了苏联土尔克斯坦—西伯利亚铁路干线上的阿克斗卡站，载来了第一批白俄罗斯共和国参加“友谊铁路”建筑工程的志愿工作人员。兰州—乌鲁木齐—阿克斗卡铁路苏联段的建筑准备工作就这样开始了。在阿克斗卡站铁路员工居住区附近，将要建筑一个建筑工人村镇，作为这个新建筑工程的附属企业的基地。
