Title: Provincial Farmers Work Intensively: Henan Completes Late Autumn Crop Planting
Author:
Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 3rd – In the continuous rainy month of June, members of agricultural production cooperatives in Henan Province have already rushed to plant all 82 million mu of late autumn crops across the province.

Henan Province's rainy season this year arrived almost a month earlier than in previous years, with continuous rain in June lasting almost the entire month. Agricultural cooperative members across the province have been engaged in the most intensive activities in June, seizing the intervals between showers to harvest over 72 million mu of wheat, while simultaneously deploying a large labor force to rush plant late autumn crops.

### 全省农民紧张劳动  河南种完晚秋作物

1956-07-04
第3版()
专栏：

    全省农民紧张劳动
    河南种完晚秋作物
    新华社郑州3日电　在阴雨连绵的六月里，河南省农业生产合作社社员们已经把全省八千二百万亩的晚秋作物都抢种上了。
    河南省今年的雨季比往年提早近一个月，六月间绵绵不停的阴雨差不多整整下了一个月。全省的农业社员们在六月里都投入最紧张地活动，在霪雨的间隙抢收了七千二百多万亩小麦，同时又抽出大批劳动力抢种了晚秋作物。
