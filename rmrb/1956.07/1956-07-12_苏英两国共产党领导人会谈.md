Title: Soviet and British Communist Party Leaders Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-07-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th—TASS reports: Soviet Communist Party leaders and British Communist Party leaders, who are currently visiting Moscow, held talks on July 10th.
    Participating in the talks on the Soviet side were: Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee; Pospelov, Secretary; and Ponomarev, Member of the Central Committee. On the British side were: Pollitt, Chairman of the Executive Committee; Gollan, General Secretary; and Ramelson, Member of the Political Committee of the Executive Committee.
    The talks were conducted in a spirit of sincere friendship and solidarity.
    On the same day, the Presidium of the Central Committee of the CPSU hosted a banquet in honor of the leaders of the British Communist Party.

### 苏英两国共产党领导人会谈

1956-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    苏英两国共产党领导人会谈
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党领导人和正在莫斯科访问的英国共产党领导人，在七月十日举行了会谈。
    参加会谈的苏共方面有：中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫、书记波斯伯洛夫、中央委员波诺马烈夫；英共方面有：执行委员会主席波立特、总书记高兰和执行委员会政治委员会委员拉梅尔逊。
    会谈是本着真挚友好和团结的精神进行的。
    同一天，苏共中央委员会主席团设宴招待英国共产党领导人。
