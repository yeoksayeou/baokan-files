Title: Burma to Hold Disarmament Day
Author:
Date: 1956-07-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, July 11th: According to newspaper reports, the Burma Peace Committee recently passed a resolution to hold a "Disarmament and Opposition to Thermonuclear Testing Day" nationwide on August 6th.

The committee also decided to send representatives to participate in the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs to be held in Japan this August.

### 缅甸将举行裁军日

1956-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸将举行裁军日
    新华社仰光11日电　据报纸报道，缅甸保卫世界和平委员会最近通过决定，要在八月六日在全国举行“裁军和反对热核子试验日”。
    这个委员会还决定派遣代表参加将在今年八月在日本举行的反对原子弹和氢弹世界大会。
