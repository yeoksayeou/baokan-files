Title: Korean Delegation Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 3rd. The Korean government delegation headed by Kim Il-sung arrived in Warsaw by plane on July 2nd after leaving Albania.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Polish United Workers' Party and government leaders Cyrankiewicz, Ochab, Zawadzki, and Rokossowski.
    Diplomatic envoys from various people's democratic countries stationed in Poland and Korean students in Poland also welcomed them at the airport.
    Kim Il-sung delivered a brief speech at the airport.
    On the same afternoon, Kim Il-sung and others visited Cyrankiewicz, Ochab, and Zawadzki respectively.

### 朝鲜代表团到华沙

1956-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜代表团到华沙
    据新华社华沙3日电　以金日成为首的朝鲜政府代表团，七月二日离开阿尔巴尼亚乘飞机到达华沙。
    到机场欢迎的有波兰统一工人党和政府的领导人西伦凯维兹、奥哈布、萨瓦茨基和罗科索夫斯基等。
    各人民民主国家的驻波外交使节和朝鲜的留波学生也到机场欢迎。
    金日成在机场上发表了简短的讲话。
    同一天下午，金日成等分别访问了西伦凯维兹、奥哈布和萨瓦茨基。
