New Technology in the Laboratory
The Angang Central Laboratory widely adopts new technologies to analyze steel products. They use spectral analysis to analyze the magnesium content in ductile iron, and can analyze fifteen samples in two hours. In the past, using chemical methods, it took two and a half days to analyze one sample.

　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Limen photo

杨礼门

专栏：

    试验室里的新技术
    鞍钢中央试验室广泛采用新技术分析钢铁产品。他们用光谱分析球墨铸铁中的镁，两小时就能分析十五个试样。过去用化学方法，分析一个试样要用两天半。　　　　　　　　新华社记者　杨礼门摄
