Title: Sino-Polish Children Hold Evening Gala to Celebrate Polish National Revival Day
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th: More than two hundred children from Beijing and Polish children in Beijing gathered at the Beijing Children's Palace this evening to commemorate the 12th anniversary of Polish National Revival Day.

　　Little Chairman Chen Rong said at the beginning of the party: "On behalf of the children of Beijing, I extend warm congratulations and Young Pioneer salute to the Polish children! Let us hold hands and dance to our hearts' content, and let our friendship flow forever like the Yangtze River and the Vistula River."

　　Then, the Chinese children performed songs, accordion solos, and dances such as "Little White Rabbit."

### 纪念波兰国家复兴节  中波儿童开晚会联欢

1956-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念波兰国家复兴节
    中波儿童开晚会联欢
    新华社20日讯　北京市的少年儿童和在北京的波兰少年儿童二百多人，今天晚上在北京市少年宫共同纪念波兰国家复兴节十二周年。
    小主席陈蓉在晚会开始的时候说：“我代表北京市的少年儿童向波兰小朋友致以热烈的祝贺和少先队的敬礼！让我们拉起手来尽情的舞蹈吧，让我们的友谊像长江和维斯拉河那样永远奔流。”
    接着，中国少年儿童们表演了歌唱、手风琴独奏和“小白兔”等舞蹈。
