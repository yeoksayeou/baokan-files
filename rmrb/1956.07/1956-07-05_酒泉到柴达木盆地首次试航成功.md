Title: First Successful Test Flight from Jiuquan to Qaidam Basin
Author:
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 1

Jiuquan, July 3 (By our own correspondent) The first flight from Jiuquan to the Qaidam Basin was successfully test-flown on July 1.

　　At 10:00 a.m., an Ilyushin-12 aircraft took off from Jiuquan, passing through Yumen and Dunhuang, crossing the Altun Mountains, and safely landed at Taldin Airport in the Qaidam Basin at 12:30 p.m. The aircraft safely flew back to Jiuquan along the original route on the afternoon of the 2nd.

### 酒泉到柴达木盆地首次试航成功

1956-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    酒泉到柴达木盆地首次试航成功
    本报酒泉3日电　从酒泉到柴达木盆地的第一次航行已在七月一日试航成功。
    上午十时，一架伊尔式十二型飞机从酒泉起飞，中途经过玉门、敦煌，越过阿尔金山高峰，十二时三十分在柴达木盆地的塔尔丁机场安全着陆。飞机在二日下午沿原来航线安全飞回酒泉。
