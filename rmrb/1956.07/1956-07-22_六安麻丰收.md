Title: Abundant Harvest of Liu'an Hemp
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 3
Six-An hemp harvest

    (This newspaper reports) The famous Anhui Liu'an hemp has achieved a bumper harvest this year. In Liu'an County, farmers' yards and roadsides are filled with harvested hemp stalks, turning from green to snow-white under the sun's exposure. It is estimated that the total area of ​​hemp and jute in the Liu'an area is more than 120,000 mu, and the average yield per mu can be increased from 180 jin last year to 240 jin.

### 六安麻丰收

1956-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    六安麻丰收
    本报讯　著名的安徽六安麻，今年获得丰收。六安县农民屋前屋后，道路两旁，都摆满了已收割的大麻麻秆，使它在太阳的曝晒下，由绿色变得雪白。据估计，六安全专区的大麻和黄麻十二多万亩，每亩的平均产量可由去年的180斤提高到240斤。
