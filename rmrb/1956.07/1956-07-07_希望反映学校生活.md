Title: Hoping to Reflect School Life
Author: Jia Changyou
Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 8

Column: Readers, Authors, Editor

    Hoping to Reflect School Life
    In newspapers, there are very few reports about school life. Teachers—the engineers of human souls—are diligently cultivating the next generation of the motherland, and our next generation is growing. I sincerely hope that after the People's Daily increases its pages, it can appropriately publish some articles reflecting teaching work and learning life. (Jia Changyou)

### 希望反映学校生活
贾长佑
1956-07-07
第8版()
专栏：读者  作者  编者

    希望反映学校生活
    在报纸上，很少有关学校生活的报道。教师——人类灵魂的工程师，在辛勤地培养祖国后一代，我们的后一代在成长着。我衷心地希望人民日报在增加版面之后，能适当地发表一些反映教学工作和学习生活的文章。　（贾长佑）
