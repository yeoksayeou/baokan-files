Title: China Red Cross Remits 10,000 Swiss Francs to the International Committee of the Red Cross
Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 18th. A spokesperson for the Red Cross Society of China headquarters told Xinhua News Agency reporters today that the Red Cross Society of China has decided to donate 10,000 Swiss francs to the International Committee of the Red Cross in Geneva. He said that Hu Lansheng, Secretary-General of the Red Cross Society of China, had notified Mr. Léopold Boissier, Chairman of the International Committee of the Red Cross, of this decision today, and told him that the donation had been remitted from Beijing.

### 我红十字会汇一万瑞士法郎捐给红十字国际委员会

1956-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我红十字会汇一万瑞士法郎捐给红十字国际委员会
    新华社18日讯　中国红十字会总会发言人今天告诉新华社记者说，中国红十字会决定捐款一万瑞士法郎给日内瓦红十字国际委员会。他说，中国红十字会秘书长胡兰生今天已经把这个决定通知了红十字国际委员会主席利·波瓦西埃，并且告诉他上项捐款已经由北京汇出。
