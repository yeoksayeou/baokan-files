Title: Teng Daiyuan Visits Democratic Germany
Author:
Date: 1956-07-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 7th: The Minister of Railways of the People's Republic of China, Teng Daiyuan, arrived in Berlin on the morning of July 6th.

　　After attending the meeting on railway transportation issues between socialist countries in Sofia, Teng Daiyuan visited Democratic Germany at the invitation of the Minister of Transport of Democratic Germany. Prior to this, Teng Daiyuan also visited Poland.

### 滕代远到民主德国访问

1956-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    滕代远到民主德国访问
    新华社柏林7日电　中华人民共和国铁道部长滕代远七月六日上午到达柏林。
    滕代远是在到索非亚参加社会主义国家间的铁路运输问题会议以后，应民主德国交通部长的邀请去民主德国访问的。在这以前，滕代远还访问了波兰。
