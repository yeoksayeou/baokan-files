Title: Czechoslovakia to Have a Bumper Harvest This Year
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 6

    Czechoslovakia
    To Have a Bumper Harvest This Year
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 26th: Czechoslovakia is proceeding smoothly with the harvest despite unfavorable weather.
    By July 25th, the country had harvested 1.05 million hectares of crops, far more than in the same period last year.
    In the eastern Slovak region, harvesting has begun extensively. Harvesting has also begun in the Czech states, where grain maturity is usually later.
    According to the estimate of the Central Action Committee of the Czechoslovak National Front, Czechoslovakia will have a bumper harvest this year.

### 捷克斯洛伐克  今年将获得丰收

1956-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克
    今年将获得丰收
    据新华社布拉格26日电　捷克斯洛伐克尽管天气不好，还是顺利地进行着收割。
    到七月二十五日，全国已经收割了105万公顷的庄稼，比去年同一时期多得多。
    在东部的斯洛伐克地区，收割工作已经普遍展开。捷克各州的收割工作也已经展开，这里谷物成熟期通常要迟一些。
    据捷克斯洛伐克民族阵线中央行动委员会估计，捷克斯洛伐克今年将获得丰收。
