Title: Adding Fuel to the Fire of the Algerian War: The United States Sells France a Batch of Armored Vehicles
Author:
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 6

    Adding Fuel to the Fire of the Algerian War
    The United States Sells France a Batch of Armored Vehicles
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington: According to Agence France-Presse, Washington, fifty American-made armored vehicles were shipped from Galveston, Texas, on June 26th to France, ready to be equipped and used to strengthen the military suppression operations in Algeria. The report said that this is believed to be part of France's order of 250 armored vehicles from the United States, and the remaining armored vehicles will be shipped to France in succession in the next few days.

### 对阿尔及利亚战争火上加油  美国卖给法国一批装甲车

1956-07-02
第6版()
专栏：

    对阿尔及利亚战争火上加油
    美国卖给法国一批装甲车
    据新华社讯　据法新社华盛顿消息：五十辆美国制造的装甲车已经在六月二十六日从得克萨斯州的加尔维斯敦装船运往法国，准备在法国装备后用来加强在阿尔及利亚的军事镇压行动。消息说，据料这是法国向美国订购的二百五十辆装甲车订货中的一部分，其余的装甲车将在几天以后陆续运到法国。
