Title: After Multiple Exchanges of Letters, Phouma Agrees to Meet with Souphanouvong
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 29th. Vientiane Radio broadcast a communiqué today from the Prime Minister's Office of the Kingdom of Laos stating that Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Kingdom Government, has agreed to meet with Prince Souphanouvong, representative of the Lao Fighting Forces, as soon as possible.
    The communiqué mentioned that the purpose of this meeting is to pave the way for new negotiations between the two sides on the Laotian issue.
    Prior to this, Prince Souphanouvong, representative of the Lao Fighting Forces, and Phouma, Prime Minister of the Lao Kingdom Government, had exchanged multiple letters regarding the holding of this meeting.

### 经过多次交换信件  富马同意和苏发努冯会谈

1956-07-01
第6版()
专栏：

    经过多次交换信件
    富马同意和苏发努冯会谈
    新华社河内29日电　万象电台今天广播老挝王国政府首相府的公报说，王国政府首相梭发那·富马亲王已经同意在最近尽快地同寮国战斗部队代表苏发努冯亲王举行会谈。
    公报提到这次会谈的目的是为双方举行关于寮国问题的新的谈判开辟道路。
    在这以前，寮国战斗部队代表苏发努冯亲王和老挝王国政府首相富马已经为举行这次会谈的问题交换过多次信件。
