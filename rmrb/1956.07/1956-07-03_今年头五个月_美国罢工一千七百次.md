Title: U.S. Saw 1,700 Strikes in the First Five Months of This Year
Author:
Date: 1956-07-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch from Washington: A report released by the U.S. Department of Labor on June 28th stated that the strike situation in the United States in the first five months of this year surpassed any corresponding period since 1952.

    The Bureau of Labor Statistics of the Department of Labor said that during the period from January to May, 730,000 workers participated in 1,750 strikes.

### 今年头五个月  美国罢工一千七百次

1956-07-03
第6版()
专栏：

    今年头五个月
    美国罢工一千七百次
    新华社2日讯　华盛顿消息：美国劳工部六月二十八日发表的报告说，美国今年头五个月的罢工情况超过了1952年以来任何同时期的罢工情况。
    劳工部劳工统计局说，在一月到五月这一段时间内，七十三万工人参加了一千七百五十次罢工。
