Title: Mongolia Donates Wheat and Cattle/Sheep to Korea
Author:
Date: 1956-07-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, the government of the Mongolian People's Republic, in order to help the Korean people develop animal husbandry and improve their living standards, has decided to donate 5,000 tons of wheat, 30,000 sheep, and 2,000 head of cattle to Korea.

The wheat will be transported to Korea this year, and the sheep and cattle will be transported next year.

### 蒙古赠送朝鲜小麦和牛羊

1956-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古赠送朝鲜小麦和牛羊
    新华社平壤20日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：蒙古人民共和国政府为了帮助朝鲜人民发展畜牧业和提高生活水平，决定赠送给朝鲜五千吨小麦、三万头羊和两千头母牛。
    小麦在今年内就要运到朝鲜，羊和牛将在明年运到。
