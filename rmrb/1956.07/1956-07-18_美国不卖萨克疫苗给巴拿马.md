Title: The United States Refuses to Sell Salk Vaccine to Panama
Author:
Date: 1956-07-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Panama City: Ricardo Arias's wife, the President of Panama, said on July 11 that Panama intended to purchase 50,000 doses of Salk vaccine from the United States to stop the spread of the polio epidemic currently raging in Panama, but has been unable to obtain permission from the U.S. Public Health Service.

The Salk vaccine is an effective medicine developed in the United States last year to prevent polio.

### 美国不卖萨克疫苗给巴拿马

1956-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

    美国不卖萨克疫苗给巴拿马
    据新华社讯　巴拿马城消息：巴拿马总统理卡多·阿里亚斯的夫人在七月十一日说，巴拿马打算从美国购进五万针萨克疫苗来制止目前正在巴拿马流行的小儿麻痹症的蔓延，但是一直没有能够得到美国公共卫生局的许可。
    萨克疫苗是美国在去年制成的、预防小儿麻痹症的有效药品。
