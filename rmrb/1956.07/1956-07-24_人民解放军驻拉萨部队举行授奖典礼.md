Title: PLA Troops Stationed in Lhasa Hold Award Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1956-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, July 22nd: The People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region troops stationed in Lhasa held an award ceremony in Lhasa on the 21st.

　　Amidst the melodious military music, Lieutenant General Tan Guansan, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region, presented the Independence and Freedom Medal and the Liberation Medal to 416 officers and soldiers on behalf of Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense. These awarded officers and soldiers persevered in the War of Resistance against Japan for a long time for China's independence and freedom; during the War of Liberation, they fought across the north and south of the Yangtze River; and after the peaceful liberation of Tibet, they enthusiastically helped the Tibetan people in their construction.

　　At the forum held after the award ceremony, the awarded officers and soldiers felt extremely honored by the honor given by the Party and the people, and expressed their determination to strive to advance towards a modernized national defense army, continue to help the Tibetan people build a beautiful and happy life, and defend the peaceful construction of the motherland.

　　Among those who attended the award ceremony were Lieutenant General Apei Awang Jinmei, Deputy Commander of the Tibet Military Region, Lieutenant General Duoga Pengcuo Raojie, Major General Fan Ming, Deputy Political Commissar, Major General Li Jue, Deputy Chief of Staff, and Maji Kaimo Suoan Wangdui of the Tibetan local troops.

### 人民解放军驻拉萨部队举行授奖典礼

1956-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    人民解放军驻拉萨部队举行授奖典礼
    新华社拉萨22日电　中国人民解放军西藏军区驻拉萨部队，二十一日在拉萨举行授奖典礼。
    在悠扬的军乐声中，西藏军区政治委员谭冠三中将代表国防部部长彭德怀元帅把独立自由奖章和解放奖章授给416名军官和士兵。这些受奖的军官和士兵为中国的独立自由，长期坚持了抗日战争；在解放战争中转战长江南北，在西藏和平解放以后，又热情地帮助西藏人民进行建设。
    在授奖典礼结束以后举行的座谈会上，受奖的军官和士兵们对党和人民给的荣誉感到无上光荣，都表示要努力向现代化的国防军前进，继续帮助西藏人民建设美好幸福的生活，保卫祖国的和平建设。
    参加这个授奖典礼的有西藏军区副司令员阿沛·阿旺晋美中将和朵喀·彭措饶杰中将、副政治委员范明少将、副参谋长李觉少将以及西藏地方部队马基凯墨·索安旺堆等。
