Title: First Li and Miao Language Research Institution Established
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 4

    The First Li and Miao Language
    Research Institution Established
    Xinhua News Agency, Tongshi, 27th -- The first institution responsible for creating written languages for the Li and Miao ethnic groups – the Guangdong Province Hainan Li and Miao Autonomous Prefecture Li and Miao Language Research Guidance Committee – was established in Tongshi on the 24th. The committee consists of 37 members, the majority of whom are representatives of the Li and Miao ethnic groups. The chairman is Wang Guoxing, the head of the autonomous prefecture, and the vice-chairmen are Chen Side and others, who are the deputy heads of the autonomous prefecture.
    Prior to this, the Hainan branch of the No. 1 Work Team for the Investigation of Minority Languages of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, which is responsible for assisting the guidance committee, arrived in Tongshi, the location of the Autonomous Prefecture People's Committee, on the 15th. It visited Li and Miao cadres from units under the Autonomous Prefecture People's Committee and held separate forums for Li and Miao cadres and Han cadres at the autonomous prefecture level, soliciting their opinions on the creation of written languages for the Li and Miao ethnic groups.

### 第一个黎族苗族语文研究机构成立

1956-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    第一个黎族苗族
    语文研究机构成立
    新华社通什27日电　第一个负责创造黎族、苗族文字的机构——广东省海南黎族苗族自治州黎族、苗族语文研究指导委员会二十四日在通什成立。这个委员会的组成人员共三十七人，绝大部分都是黎族、苗族的代表人物，主任委员是自治州州长王国兴，副主任委员是自治州副州长陈斯德等人。
    在这之前，负责协助指导委员会工作的中国科学院少数民族语言调查第一工作队海南分队在十五日到达自治州人民委员会所在地通什后，曾访问了自治州人民委员会所属单位的黎、苗族干部，并分别召开了自治州一级机关的黎族、苗族干部和汉族干部座谈会，向他们征求对创立黎族、苗族文字的意见。
