Title: Premier Zhou Receives Delegation of Japanese Business Leaders

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. This evening, Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire delegation of 36 Japanese business leaders, headed by Ito Imakazu and Shima Hiroshi, standing members of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and Takami Shigeyoshi, executive director of the Japan-China Export-Import Association, who recently visited Beijing.

Present at the meeting were Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Xiao Fangzhou, Deputy Secretary-General, and Yang Haolu, Director of the Fourth Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

Since arriving in Beijing on July 7, the Japanese business delegation has held trade talks with various import and export corporations and conducted visits. Some members of the delegation will leave Beijing at the end of July to visit Shenyang, Tianjin, Shanghai, and other places.

### 周总理接见日本工商界代表团

1956-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本工商界代表团
    新华社28日讯　今天晚上，周恩来总理接见了以日本国际贸易促进协会常任委员伊藤今朝市、志村宽为首的日本工商界访华代表团全体人员三十六人，和最近来北京访问的日中输出入协会常务理事高见重义。
    接见时在座的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、副秘书长肖方洲、对外贸易部第四局长杨浩庐等人。
    日本工商界访华代表团自从七月七日来到北京以后，曾经和各进、出口公司洽谈贸易，并且进行参观访问。代表团部分人员将在七月底离北京到沈阳、天津、上海等地参观。
