Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-07-27
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 26th: The 54th meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 am on July 26th. The meeting adjourned at 1:05 pm. The next meeting is scheduled for Thursday, August 9th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦26日电　中美两国大使级会谈七月二十六日上午十时在国联大厦举行第五十四次会议。会议在下午一时零五分休会，下次会议定于八月九日（星期四）举行。
