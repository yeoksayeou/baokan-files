Title: Over Fifty Writers to Visit Important Industrial and Mining Areas to Create Works on the Theme of Industrial Construction
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, July 15th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Chinese Writers Association have invited over fifty writers from across the country to form a writers' tour group. They will visit important industrial and mining areas in the Northeast and South in two batches, to broaden the writers' horizons and encourage more writers to create works on the theme of industrial construction.

The first group of the writers' tour group, consisting of twenty-four members including Kong Luosun, Luo Zhinan, Chen Bochui, Chen Canyun, Wang Jingzhi, and Zhou Jiefu, departed from Beijing on the 15th for Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin, Fushun, Anshan, Lushun and Dalian, and other places. The second group of more than twenty people will depart in August for Datong, Taiyuan, Luoyang, Wuhan, Shanghai, and Hangzhou. In addition, in order to strengthen ties with writers and assist them in having more contact with workers, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has recently invited a group of writers to Qingdao for a summer vacation. Writers Zhang Tianyi, Ai Wu, and poet Zang Kejia, among others, who were invited, have already set off on the 15th.

### 五十多位作家去重要工矿区  他们将创作以工业建设为题材的作品

1956-07-17
第7版()
专栏：

    五十多位作家去重要工矿区
    他们将创作以工业建设为题材的作品
    新华社15日讯　中华全国总工会和中国作家协会邀集全国各地五十多位作家组成作家参观团，分两批去东北和南方的重要工矿区参观访问，以扩大作家的生活视野，吸引更多的作家创作以工业建设为题材的作品。
    作家参观团第一批团员孔罗荪、罗稷南、陈伯吹、陈残云、汪静之、周洁夫等二十四人，十五日从北京出发赴沈阳、长春、哈尔滨、抚顺、鞍山、旅大等地。第二批二十多人将在八月间出发，到大同、太原、洛阳、武汉、上海和杭州等地。另外，中华全国总工会为了密切同作家的联系，协助作家更多地和工人接触，最近并邀请了一批作家在夏季到青岛去休养。应邀前往的作家张天翼、艾芜和诗人臧克家等已经在十五日动身。
