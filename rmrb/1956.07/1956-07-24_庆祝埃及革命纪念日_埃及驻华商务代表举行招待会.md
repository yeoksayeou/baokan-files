Title: Egyptian Commercial Representative to China Holds Reception to Celebrate Anniversary of Egyptian Revolution
Author:
Date: 1956-07-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd. Premier Zhou Enlai raised his glass at the reception held this afternoon by the Egyptian Commercial Representative to China, Mr. Faer, in celebration of the anniversary of the Egyptian Revolution, along with Mr. and Mrs. Faer, Indian Ambassador R.K. Nehru and Mrs. Nehru, and Mrs. Popović, wife of the Yugoslav Ambassador, to congratulate the joint statement issued by the three countries of South, Egypt, and India on July 20th.

At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai also met and spoke with diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China who attended the reception.

The reception was held at the Beijing Hotel. The host, Mr. Faer, was busy receiving his more than three hundred Chinese and foreign guests. He went to Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, and Procurator-General Zhang Dingcheng, one after another, and drank toasts with them.

The reception lasted for one and a half hours. Among the Chinese guests were also heads of various departments of state organs and prominent figures from all walks of life in Beijing.

### 庆祝埃及革命纪念日  埃及驻华商务代表举行招待会

1956-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝埃及革命纪念日
    埃及驻华商务代表举行招待会
    新华社23日讯　周恩来总理今天下午在埃及商务代表法尔举行的庆祝埃及革命纪念日的招待会上，同法尔和夫人、印度大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和夫人以及南斯拉夫大使波波维奇的夫人一起干杯，祝贺南、埃、印三国在七月二十日发表的联合声明。
    在这个招待会上，周恩来总理还分别同出席招待会的各国驻华外交官员们会面和谈话。
    招待会是在北京饭店举行的。招待会的主人——法尔先生忙着接待他的三百多位中外客人。他先后走到陈毅副总理、李先念副总理和张鼎丞检察长的面前，同他们一起干了杯。
    招待会进行了一个半小时，在中国客人中，还有国家机关各部门的负责人和北京的各界著名人士。
