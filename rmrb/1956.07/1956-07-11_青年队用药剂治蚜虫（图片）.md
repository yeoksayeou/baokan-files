Title: Youth Team Uses Pesticides to Treat Aphids (Picture)
Author: Yuan Hao
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 3

    Youth Team Uses Pesticides to Treat Aphids
    The Zhanxin Agricultural Production Cooperative in Wurenqiao District, Anguo County, Hebei Province, planted more than 18,000 mu of cotton this year, of which 2,800 mu were infested with aphids. In order to ensure the normal growth of cotton, the youth team actively eliminated and treated aphids. This is a scene of them using pesticides to eliminate aphids.
　　　　Photo by Yuan Hao (Draft from Hebei Daily)

### 青年队用药剂治蚜虫（图片）
袁浩
1956-07-11
第3版()
专栏：

    青年队用药剂治蚜虫
    河北省安国县伍仁桥区崭新农业生产合作社今年种植了一万八千多亩棉花，其中的二千八百亩生了蚜虫。为了保证棉花的正常生长，青年队积极地除治蚜虫。这是他们正在使用药剂除治蚜虫的情形。
　　　　袁　浩摄（河北日报社稿）
