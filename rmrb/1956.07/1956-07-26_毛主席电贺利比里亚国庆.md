Title: Chairman Mao Sends Congratulatory Message on Liberia's National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to Liberian President Tubman to congratulate him on the National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency President Tubman of Liberia, Monrovia, Liberia:

On the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of Liberia, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to the people of your country and to Your Excellency, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name.

                                    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                        July 24, 1956, Beijing

### 毛主席电贺利比里亚国庆

1956-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺利比里亚国庆
    新华社25日讯　毛泽东主席打电报给利比里亚总统杜伯曼祝贺国庆。电文如下：利比里亚蒙罗维亚利比里亚总统杜伯曼阁下：
    值此利比里亚共和国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民并以我个人名义向贵国人民和阁下致热诚的祝贺。
                                    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东
                                        1956年7月24日于北京
