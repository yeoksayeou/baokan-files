Title: A Group of Leading Cadres from Central Government Organs Visit Old Revolutionary Base Areas
Author:
Date: 1956-07-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: According to the instructions of the State Council, the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the People's Republic of China recently organized fourteen leading cadres from central government organs who had worked in the old revolutionary base areas and had close ties with the local people to visit the old revolutionary base areas. Nine of these fourteen leading cadres have already departed in early July, and the remaining five will depart successively in mid-to-late July.

The areas to be visited are the Central Area, the Sichuan-Shaanxi Area, and the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Area during the Second Revolutionary Civil War, and the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region, the Jin-Cha-Ji Border Region, the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Border Region, and the Shandong Area during the Anti-Japanese War, among other old revolutionary base areas. The main tasks of the visit are to understand the recovery and development of production in the old base areas and the long-term plans; what difficulties and problems still exist in developing production, and to solicit the specific opinions of the local people on solving these problems.

### 一批中央机关负责干部到革命老根据地访问

1956-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

    一批中央机关负责干部到革命老根据地访问
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国内务部根据国务院的指示，最近组织了十四个曾经在革命老根据地担任工作并且同当地群众有密切联系的中央机关的负责干部，前往革命老根据地进行访问。这十四个负责干部中有九人已经在七月初出发，其余五人也将在七月中、下旬陆续出发。
    这次访问的地区是第二次国内革命战争时期的中央区、川陕区、陕甘宁区和抗日战争时期的陕甘宁边区、晋察冀边区、晋冀鲁豫边区、山东区等革命老根据地。访问的任务主要是了解老根据地的生产恢复、发展情况和远景规划；在发展生产上还有什么困难问题，同时征求当地人民对解决这些问题的具体意见。
