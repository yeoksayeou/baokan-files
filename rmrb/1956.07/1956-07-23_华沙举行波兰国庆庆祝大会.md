Title: Warsaw Holds Celebration Rally for Polish National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-07-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 22nd: A celebration rally for the 12th anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Republic was held in the conference hall of the Palace of Culture and Science in Warsaw on the evening of July 21st.

Participating in the celebration rally were workers from various enterprises in Warsaw, representatives from party and public organizations, cultural, scientific, and artistic workers, and generals and officers of the Polish army. A total of more than 3,000 people attended the celebration.

Seated on the rostrum were First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Ochab, Chairman of the Council of State Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz, and other leaders of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Polish government. When the Soviet government delegation headed by Bulganin and the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Bolz appeared on the rostrum, everyone warmly applauded them.

Sarzetsky, Chairman of the Presidium of the Warsaw City People's Council, delivered the opening speech.

First Deputy Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers Nowak gave a report on the 12th anniversary of Poland's liberation.

Bulganin and Bolz spoke at the meeting.

### 华沙举行波兰国庆庆祝大会

1956-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    华沙举行波兰国庆庆祝大会
    据新华社华沙22日电　七月二十一日晚间，在华沙文化科学宫的会议大厅举行了波兰人民共和国成立十二周年庆祝大会。
    参加这次庆祝大会的有华沙各企业的工人，党和公众团体的代表，文化、科学和艺术工作者，波兰军队的将军和军官。出席庆祝会的共有三千多人。
    坐在主席台上的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记奥哈布、国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹和波兰统一工人党和波兰政府的其他领导人。当以布尔加宁为首的苏联政府代表团和以博尔茨为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团出现在主席台上的时候，大家热烈鼓掌欢迎他们。
    华沙市人民会议主席团主席萨热茨基致开幕辞。
    波兰部长会议第一副主席诺瓦克在会上作了关于波兰解放十二周年的报告。
    布尔加宁和博尔茨在会上讲了话。
