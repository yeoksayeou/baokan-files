Title: Algerian National Liberation Front Representative Welcomes Joint Statement of South, India and Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-07-23
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 22nd: Belgrade News—During the meeting of the heads of state of Yugoslavia, India, and Egypt, Abbas, the representative of the delegation of the Algerian National Liberation Front who went to Yugoslavia, said on July 21st that the legitimate national aspirations of the Algerian people were taken into consideration in the section of the joint statement of the three countries of South, India, and Egypt regarding Algeria.
　　Abbas said that the delegation of the Algerian National Liberation Front hopes that this statement can make an effective contribution to the restoration of peace in Algeria while respecting the right of the Algerian people to independence.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表  欢迎南印埃联合声明

1956-07-23
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表
    欢迎南印埃联合声明
    新华社布拉格22日电　贝尔格莱德消息：在南斯拉夫、印度、埃及三国首脑会议期间到南斯拉夫去的阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表团代表阿巴斯七月二十一日说，阿尔及利亚人合法的民族愿望在南、印、埃三国联合声明关于阿尔及利亚的一段中得到了考虑。
    阿巴斯说，阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表团希望，这个声明能够在尊重阿尔及利亚人民独立权利的情况下，对恢复阿尔及利亚的和平作出有效贡献。
