Title: Sino-Cambodian Joint Committee to be Established in Beijing and Phnom Penh
Author:
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 12th: Phnom Penh newspapers published a communiqué from the Prime Minister's Office of the Kingdom of Cambodia on the 7th, stating that a joint committee consisting of representatives from China and Cambodia will be established in Beijing and Phnom Penh in early August. This committee will be responsible for implementing the Sino-Cambodian Economic Aid Agreement.

The communiqué stated that the aid provided by China is unconditional, and will also provide the Kingdom with the necessary technical assistance, and help the Kingdom complete the construction of cement plants, plywood factories, paper mills and cotton textile mills.

According to newspaper reports, the Royal Government of Cambodia will use Chinese aid funds for various domestic constructions, including the development of Cambodia's domestic postal and telecommunications services.

### 中柬混合委员会将在北京和金边成立

1956-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中柬混合委员会将在北京和金边成立
    新华社河内12日电　金边报纸七日刊载柬埔寨王国首相府的公报说，一个包括中柬两国代表的混合委员会将在八月初在北京和金边成立。这个委员会将负责实施中柬经济援助协定。
    公报说，中国给予的援助是无条件的，同时将供给王国所需要的技术援助，并且帮助王国完成水泥厂、胶合板厂、造纸厂和棉纺织厂的建设。
    据报纸报道，柬埔寨王国政府将运用中国的援助款项进行各种国内建设，其中包括发展柬埔寨国内的邮电事业。
