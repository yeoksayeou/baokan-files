Title: Tunisia Requests to Join the United Nations
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 20th—The United Nations Secretariat announced on the 18th that Tunisian Prime Minister Bourguiba had formally requested the admission of Tunisia to the United Nations organization.

Morocco had already requested to join the United Nations a few days earlier.

### 突尼斯要求加入联合国

1956-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

    突尼斯要求加入联合国
    新华社20日讯　纽约消息：联合国秘书处十八日宣布，突尼斯总理布尔吉巴正式请求接纳突尼斯加入联合国组织。
    摩洛哥已在几天以前要求加入联合国。
