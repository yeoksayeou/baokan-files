Title: Korea Publishes "Outline of Korean Bibliography"
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 7

    Korea Publishes "Outline of Korean Bibliography"
    Recently, the National Publishing House of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea published the book "Outline of Korean Bibliography." This book was compiled by the Bibliography Department of the Korean National Central Library. It provides a general introduction to major documents from ancient times to the end of the Joseon Dynasty, with detailed explanations and evaluations of their contents.
    The book introduces extant ancient documents, categorized by subject. It analyzes more than 500 ancient books, including representative works in bibliography, lexicography, agronomy, medicine, astronomy, mathematics, economics, history, geography, military science, linguistics, literature, and religion. It also details the social life and the development of science and culture at the time.
    This book is a comprehensive reference book and a comprehensive introduction to Korean culture. Its publication is of great help to the current classical culture research being conducted extensively in Korea. At the same time, it provides many useful materials for re-organizing classical books, correcting distortions made by Japanese imperialists, introducing valuable cultural heritage to the people, and soundly developing Korean national culture.

### 朝鲜出版“朝鲜书志学概要”

1956-07-17
第7版()
专栏：

    朝鲜出版“朝鲜书志学概要”
    最近，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国立出版社出版了“朝鲜书志学概要”一书。这本书是由朝鲜国立中央图书馆书志学部编的。它对于从古代到李朝末期的主要文献，做了概括的介绍，对其内容作了详尽的解释和评价。
    这本书对留传到现在的古文献，分门别类地做了介绍。它分析的古书有五百多本，其中包括书志学、字典编纂、农学、医学、天文、数学、经济、历史、地理、军事、语言、文学、宗教等方面有代表性的著作，并且，详细说明了当时社会生活和科学文化的发展情况。
    这本书是一本综合性的参考书，也是一本全面介绍朝鲜文化的书。它的出版，对今天在朝鲜广泛进行的古典文化研究工作有巨大的帮助，同时对于重新整理古典书籍，纠正被日本帝国主义者歪曲的部分，把宝贵文化遗产介绍给人民，并健全地发展朝鲜民族文化，提供了很多有益的材料。
