Title: Lüda Inspects the Work of Resettling Demobilized Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 4

    Lüda Inspects the Work of Resettling Demobilized Soldiers
    (Our report) The Lüda Municipal People's Committee has decided to conduct a comprehensive and in-depth inspection of the city's resettlement of demobilized soldiers in July.
    The content of this inspection includes many aspects such as the job placement of demobilized soldiers, salary and benefits, training and education, and care for their hardships in life. It requires villages, agricultural cooperatives in rural areas, and factories, mines, enterprises, institutions, organizations, and schools in urban areas to conduct the inspection. It also requires that existing problems be resolved immediately if possible, and those that cannot be resolved immediately should be resolved gradually within a time limit, solving as many problems as possible. Those who have caused serious accidents due to repelling and打击 demobilized soldiers or due to indifference to their hardships in life shall be seriously dealt with according to the circumstances.

### 旅大检查安置复员军人工作

1956-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    旅大检查安置复员军人工作
    本报讯　旅大市人民委员会决定在七月份对全市安置复员军人的情况，进行一次全面深入的检查。
    这次检查的内容，包括对复员军人的工作安置、薪资待遇、培养教育、对他们生活疾苦的照顾等许多方面，要求农村以乡、农业社，城市以厂矿企业、机关、团体、学校为单位进行，并要求在检查中对现存的问题，凡是能够解决的应立即解决，不能立即解决的要限期逐步解决，做到有多少问题解决多少问题。对其中凡是由于排斥打击复员军人以及由于对他们的生活疾苦漠不关心而造成严重事故的，要根据其情节给予严肃处理。
