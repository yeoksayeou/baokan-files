Title: Ulaanbaatar Holds Ceremony Celebrating 35th Anniversary of the Revolution
Author:
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 12th: A grand ceremony celebrating the 35th anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution was solemnly held in Ulaanbaatar at 10:00 AM on July 11th.

Tens of thousands of workers, students, members of livestock production cooperatives, officers and soldiers of the People's Army, Party and government workers, as well as young male and female herders selected from nearby provinces to participate in the horse racing at the Naadam Festival, all dressed in festive attire, participated in the celebration ceremony.

Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, leaders of the People's Army, and the heads of delegations from eleven countries including the Soviet Union, China, Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, Vietnam, the German Democratic Republic, Korea, Poland, Romania, and Czechoslovakia, as well as the representatives of the presidents of India and Burma, stood on the platform of the mausoleum of Sukhbaatar and Choibalsan to review the parade.

Also watching the ceremony on the viewing platform were foreign guests, advanced workers on the industrial, agricultural, animal husbandry, cultural, and scientific fronts of Mongolia, and more than 500 veteran guerrillas who fought side by side with Sukhbaatar and Choibalsan during the most difficult years of the Mongolian people.

The parade was led by students from the Sukhbaatar Military Academy, followed by officers and soldiers of various branches of the heroic and strong Mongolian People's Army, and then the workers' contingent marched forward holding charts of production achievements high.

At 3:00 PM that day, the Naadam Festival began. Wrestling, horse racing, and other programs were held at the festival.

Mongolian state leaders also accompanied the delegations from various countries to visit the Mongolian industrial, livestock, and agricultural exhibition halls in Naadam Square.

That evening, the Mongolian Council of Ministers held a grand reception. More than 650 people attended the reception, including Mongolian Party, government, and military leaders, delegations from various countries, diplomatic envoys from various countries, and heads of Mongolian mass organizations.

### 乌兰巴托举行革命胜利三十五周年庆祝典礼

1956-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

    乌兰巴托举行革命胜利三十五周年庆祝典礼
    新华社12日讯　乌兰巴托消息：蒙古人民革命胜利三十五周年庆祝典礼七月十一日上午十时在乌兰巴托隆重举行。
    数万名工人、学生、畜牧业生产合作社社员 、人民军官兵、党和政府的工作人员以及穿着各色节日服装的从附近各省选派来参加那达慕大会赛马的年青男女牧民，参加了庆祝典礼。
    蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布、部长会议主席泽登巴尔、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴、人民军的领导人员和苏联、中国、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、越南、德意志民主共和国、朝鲜、波兰、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克等十一个国家的代表团团长，以及印度，缅甸两国总统的代表，站在苏赫—巴托尔和乔巴山陵墓阶台上检阅了游行队伍。
    在观礼台上观礼的还有各国外宾，蒙古工业、农牧业和文化科学战线上的先进工作者，在蒙古人民最艰苦的年代里同苏赫—巴托尔和乔巴山并肩作过战的老游击队员等五百多人。
    游行队伍以苏赫—巴托尔军官学校的学生们为前导，接着来的是英勇坚强的蒙古人民军各兵种的官兵，随后是工人的队伍高举着生产成绩图表前进。
    在当天下午三时，那达慕大会开始。大会上举行了摔跤、赛马等节目。
    蒙古国家领导人还陪同各国代表团在那达慕广场参观了蒙古的工业、畜牧业和农业等展览馆。
    当天晚上，蒙古部长会议举行了盛大的招待会。出席招待会的有蒙古党、政、军领导人，各国代表团，各国外交使节以及蒙古群众团体负责人等六百五十多人。
