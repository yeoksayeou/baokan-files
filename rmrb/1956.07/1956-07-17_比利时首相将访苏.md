Title: Belgian Prime Minister to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency: Soviet Ambassador to Belgium, Avilov, some time ago extended an invitation from the Soviet government to Belgian Prime Minister Van Acker and Foreign Minister Spaak, inviting them to visit the Soviet Union at their convenience to exchange views on issues of common interest to Belgium and the Soviet Union. Van Acker and Spaak have accepted the invitation and will come to Moscow before the beginning of October this year.

### 比利时首相将访苏

1956-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    比利时首相将访苏
    据新华社讯　苏联驻比利时大使阿维洛夫在若干时间以前向比利时首相范阿克和外交大臣斯巴克发出苏联政府的邀请，请他们在他们方便的时候访问苏联，就比利时和苏联共同感到兴趣的问题交换意见。范阿克和斯巴克已经接受邀请，在今年十月初以前来莫斯科。
