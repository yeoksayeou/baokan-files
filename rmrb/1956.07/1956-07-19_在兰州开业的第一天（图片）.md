Title: The First Day of Business in Lanzhou (Picture)
Author: Hao Changgeng
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 3

    The First Day of Business in Lanzhou
    On the first day of business for the Xinda Xiang Silk, Satin, Woolen Fabric, and Cotton Cloth Store, which moved from Shanghai to Lanzhou, it received more than 54,000 customers and sold various fabrics worth 21,680 yuan, exceeding more than twice the average daily turnover in Shanghai. Photo by Hao Changgeng (Xinhua News Agency manuscript)

### 在兰州开业的第一天（图片）
郝常耕
1956-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    在兰州开业的第一天
    由上海迁往兰州的信大祥绸缎呢绒棉布庄开业的第一天，接待了五万四千多名顾客，售出各种布匹价值二万一千六百八十元，超过在上海时期的平均每日营业额二倍多。　　　郝常耕摄　（新华社稿）
