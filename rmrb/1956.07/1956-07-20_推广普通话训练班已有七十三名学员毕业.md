Title: Putonghua Training Class Has Graduated Seventy-Three Students
Author:
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th -- The Putonghua Training Class, commissioned by the Chinese Writing Reform Committee and organized by Renmin University of China, held its graduation ceremony today.

The seventy-three students completed courses in phonetics, grammar, dialect investigation, a brief history of Chinese character development, and model essays in just over six months. They are now proficient in recognizing Pinyin letters, reading Pinyin, mastering the tonal characteristics of Beijing pronunciation, and some can even apply the rules of tone sandhi. They will be dispatched to various parts of the country to promote Putonghua.

### 推广普通话训练班已有七十三名学员毕业

1956-07-20
第7版()
专栏：

    推广普通话训练班已有七十三名学员毕业
    据新华社17日讯　中国文字改革委员会委托中国人民大学举办的推广普通话训练班，今天举行毕业典礼。
    七十三个学员在半年多的时间中学完了正音学、语法、方言调查、汉字发展简史、范文等课程，已经能够熟练地认识拼音字母，朗读拼音方法，掌握北京语音的声调特点，有些还能运用变调的规律。他们将分派到全国各地推广普通话。
