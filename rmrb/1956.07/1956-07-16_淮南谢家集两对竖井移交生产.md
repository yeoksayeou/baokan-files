Title: Huainan Xiejiagji Two Pairs of Vertical Shafts Handed Over for Production
Author:
Date: 1956-07-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, July 15th: The newly built Xiejiagji No. 2 and No. 3 vertical shafts of the Huainan Coal Mine held a handover ceremony for production today.

The handover ceremony for production was held in the newly built grand theater in the Xiejiagji area. Zhang Kaifan, Deputy Secretary of the Anhui Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Governor of Anhui Province, cut the ribbon at Xiejiagji No. 2 and No. 3 shafts respectively.

### 淮南谢家集两对竖井移交生产

1956-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    淮南谢家集两对竖井移交生产
    据新华社合肥15日电　淮南煤矿新建谢家集二号和三号竖井今天举行了移交生产仪式。
    移交生产仪式是在谢家集地区新建的大戏院里举行的。中共安徽省委副书记、安徽省副省长张恺帆，先后在谢家集二号和三号井进行了剪彩。
