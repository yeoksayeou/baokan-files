Title: Bulganin Hosts Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th - According to TASS, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, held a reception at the Grand Kremlin Palace on July 7th in honor of the visit of the Premier of the Cabinet of North Korea, Kim Il-sung, to the Soviet Union.

    Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Park Chung-ae, and Foreign Minister Nam Il, also attended the reception.

    Many leaders of the Soviet Party and government were present.

    Bulganin and Kim Il-sung toasted each other.

### 布尔加宁招待金日成

1956-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁招待金日成
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁七月七日在大克里姆林宫为朝鲜内阁首相金日成访问苏联举行了招待会。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、外务相南日等人也都出席了招待会。
    许多苏联党和政府的领导人都来作陪。
    布尔加宁和金日成曾经举杯互相祝贺。
