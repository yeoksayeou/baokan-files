Title: China Supplies Pakistan with 60,000 Tons of Rice and Donates 4,000 Tons of Rice to Help Overcome the East Pakistan Famine
Author:
Date: 1956-07-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, July 14th: China will soon supply Pakistan with 60,000 metric tons of rice at a fair and reasonable price. This batch of rice will be paid for in Pakistani Rupees.

    The rice contract was signed here yesterday afternoon by the Chinese government and the Pakistani government after friendly and harmonious negotiations.

    Geng Biao, the Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, signed on behalf of the Chinese government. Hasnie, Secretary of the Pakistani Ministry of Food and Agriculture, signed on behalf of the President of Pakistan.

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, July 14th: The Pakistani government announced in a communique on July 13th that it had accepted the 4,000 metric tons of rice donated by the Chinese government to Pakistan.

    The Chinese government's decision to donate the rice was to help overcome the famine in East Pakistan. Geng Biao, the Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, informed the Pakistani government of this decision in a letter to Chaudhry, the acting Prime Minister of Pakistan.

### 我国供应巴基斯坦六万吨大米  并赠送四千吨大米帮助克服东巴基斯坦粮荒

1956-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我国供应巴基斯坦六万吨大米
    并赠送四千吨大米帮助克服东巴基斯坦粮荒
    新华社喀喇蚩14日电　中国不久将以公平合理的价格供给巴基斯坦六万米吨大米。这批大米将用巴基斯坦卢比来支付。
    这个大米合同是中国政府和巴基斯坦政府经过友好和融洽的谈判在昨天下午在这里签订的。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚代表中国政府签字。巴基斯坦粮食和农业部秘书哈斯尼代表巴基斯坦总统签字。
    新华社喀喇蚩14日电　巴基斯坦政府在七月十三日的一项公报中宣布，已经接受了中国政府赠给巴基斯坦的四千米吨大米。
    中国政府赠送大米的决定，是为了帮助克服东巴基斯坦的粮荒。中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚在给巴基斯坦代理总理琼德里加的信中，把这个决定通知了巴基斯坦政府。
