Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Visits Lyon, France
Author:
Date: 1956-07-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 6th: A dispatch from Lyon reports that the Chinese Cultural Delegation left Lyon on July 6th for Grenoble, after a four-day visit to Lyon, the third largest city in France. The delegation visited the University of Lyon and the Lyon National School of Music and Drama, and toured the Berliet automobile factory and the Silk Museum, among other places.

### 我国文化代表团访问法国的里昂市

1956-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    我国文化代表团访问法国的里昂市
    据新华社布拉格6日电　里昂消息：中国文化代表团在法国第三大城市里昂访问四天后，在七月六日离开里昂前往格累诺布尔。代表团曾访问了里昂大学和里昂音乐戏剧学院，参观了贝尔利汽车工厂和丝织品博物馆等。
