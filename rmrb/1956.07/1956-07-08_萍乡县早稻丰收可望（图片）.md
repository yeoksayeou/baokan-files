Title: Promising Early Rice Harvest in Pingxiang County (Photo)
Author: Lu Zhiguang
Date: 1956-07-08
Page: 3

    Promising Early Rice Harvest in Pingxiang County
  　The early rice is growing very well in the Xincun Agricultural Production Cooperative of Pingxiang County, Jiangxi Province, with an estimated yield of 650 to 900 catties per mu. This year, the cooperative planted the superior variety "Nante No. 1" and implemented close planting of small seedlings and close planting of single seedlings. The members are inspecting the rice ears in the fields. Photographed by Lu Zhiguang (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 萍乡县早稻丰收可望（图片）
陆之光
1956-07-08
第3版()
专栏：

    萍乡县早稻丰收可望
  　江西省萍乡县新村农业生产合作社的早稻生长得很好，每亩估计可收六百五十斤到九百斤。今年这个社种的都是优良品种“南特号”，并且进行了小株密植和单株密植。社员们正在田间察看稻穗。　　陆之光摄（新华社稿）
