Title: Chen Jiakang Presents Credentials to Egyptian President Nasser; Nasser Says Sino-Egyptian Cooperation Will Surely Strengthen
Author:
Date: 1956-07-27
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 26th: Chen Jiakang, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Egypt, presented his credentials to Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser at 11:00 a.m. on the 22nd.

　　Chen Jiakang and the staff of the embassy and commercial representative office were warmly welcomed by the masses on their way to the Presidential Palace.

　　Before presenting his credentials, Chen Jiakang addressed Gamal Abdel Nasser, saying: "It is with honor and joy that I present to you, Your Excellency the President, the letter of credence from Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, appointing me as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Egypt. I express my warm respect to you."

　　Abdel Nasser replied:
　　"I welcome Your Excellency as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Egypt. I firmly believe that the friendship between Egypt and China will surely develop, and our cooperation will surely be strengthened."

　　After presenting the credentials, Abdel Nasser and Chen Jiakang had a brief conversation. In the conversation, he expressed that Egypt must cooperate with China in friendship and believed deeply that Egypt and China would gradually draw closer together.

　　Chen Jiakang also presented gifts from Chairman Mao Zedong to Abdel Nasser.

### 陈家康向埃及总统递交国书  纳赛尔表示中埃合作一定会加强

1956-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    陈家康向埃及总统递交国书
    纳赛尔表示中埃合作一定会加强
    新华社26日讯　开罗消息：中华人民共和国驻埃及首任特命全权大使陈家康二十二日上午十一时向埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔递交国书。
    在陈家康率领使馆和商务代表处人员赴总统府途中，受到了群众的热烈欢迎。
    陈家康在递交国书前向阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔致辞说：“我怀着荣幸和欢愉的心情向你，尊敬的总统阁下，递交中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命我为中华人民共和国驻埃及共和国特命全权大使的国书。我向你表示热烈的敬意。”
    阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔致答辞说：
  “我欢迎阁下出任中华人民共和国驻埃及共和国的特命全权大使。我深信埃中两国之间的友谊一定会发展，我们之间的合作一定会加强。”
    递交国书后，阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔和陈家康作了短时间的谈话。他在谈话中表示埃及一定要同中国友好合作，并且深信埃中两国将会逐步接近起来。
    陈家康还代表毛泽东主席向阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔赠送了礼物。
