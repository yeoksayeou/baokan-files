Suburban Farmers Rush to Harvest Wheat Day and Night

(Our newspaper's dispatch) During the wheat harvest season, several heavy rains in the suburbs of Beijing brought great difficulties to the harvesting work, and also affected the wheat yield. In order to prevent or reduce the reduction in wheat production this year, the farmers in the suburbs have been rushing to harvest wheat day and night since late June. As of July 2nd, with the exception of some low-lying areas where harvesting is still underway, wheat in most areas has been harvested and brought to the threshing grounds, and is now being threshed urgently to prevent germination.

On the afternoon of the 2nd, the reporter visited the Red Star Collective Farm in Nan'yuan District, which is a relatively low-lying area in the suburbs of Beijing. Combine harvesters were rushing to harvest the last piece of wheat in the wet wheat field. In recent days, four teams of combine harvesters from the agricultural machinery and tractor station have been concentrated here to rush to harvest wheat, and as of the 2nd, the harvest has been completed.

In order to prevent wheat from germinating during the rainy season, cadres from various districts are helping and guiding farmers to protect the wheat stacks that have been harvested and brought to the threshing grounds, and are threshing them day and night. In the Jingxi Mining Area, some agricultural production cooperatives have distributed the harvested wheat ears to members to speed up threshing, in order to prevent them from germinating on the threshing ground; others have distributed the threshed wheat to members to dry it themselves to prevent germination.

At present, suburban farmers are working hard to ensure that this year's wheat harvest is brought home. (Ping) (Attached picture)

### 郊区农民昼夜抢收小麦

1956-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

    郊区农民昼夜抢收小麦
    本报讯　正在麦收的时候，北京郊区连下几场大雨，给麦收工作带来了很大的困难，同时也影响了小麦的产量。为了使今年的小麦不减产或少减产，郊区的农民们从六月下旬起，昼夜抢收小麦。到七月二日止，除部分低洼地区还在抢收外，大部地区的小麦已收割到场，现正紧张脱粒，防止小麦发芽。
    二日下午，记者访问了南苑区红星集体农庄，这是北京郊区比较低洼的地区。康拜因收割机正在潮湿的麦田里，抢收最后一块麦子。近几天来，这里集中了农业机器拖拉机站四个队的康拜因收割机抢收小麦，到二日为止已经收割完毕。
    为了防止小麦在阴雨时发芽，各区干部正帮助并指导农民全力保护收割到场的麦垛同时夜以继日地脱粒。在京西矿区，有的农业生产合作社把收割到场的麦穗分给社员加紧脱粒，以免垛在场上发芽；也有的社把脱粒后的小麦立即分给社员自己晒晾，防止发芽。
    目前，郊区农民正努力争取今年的小麦颗粒还家。（平）（附图片）
    北京郊区的农民在连阴多雨的天气中抢收小麦。这是在一个农业社的麦场里脱粒机正在紧张脱粒。
                            本报记者　高粮摄
