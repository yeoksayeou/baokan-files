Title: Sihanouk Says People's China Should Have Legitimate Status in the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1956-07-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 15th: Prince Sihanouk of Cambodia said at a press conference held in Prague on the afternoon of July 14th, "We believe that for the sake of peace, the People's Republic of China should have a legitimate position in the United Nations, which is also a matter of justice." He said, "From the viewpoint of peace, we believe that not recognizing a country with a population of 600 million is a very serious problem."
    When talking about Cambodia's attitude towards the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Prince Sihanouk said: Cambodia has reached an agreement with South Vietnam on the issue of mutual dispatch of personnel. These personnel are neither diplomatic representatives nor economic representatives, but representatives dispatched by our two countries as a liaison mechanism between our two governments. If North Vietnam is friendly to us, and if North Vietnam respects our neutrality, we will be willing to "establish relations" with it, especially trade relations.
    In response to a reporter's question about compliance with the Geneva Accords, Sihanouk said: "We hope that all international agreements, especially the Geneva Accords, will be observed and implemented."

### 西哈努克说  人民中国在联合国中应有合法地位

1956-07-16
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克说
    人民中国在联合国中应有合法地位
    据新华社布拉格15日电　柬埔寨的西哈努克亲王七月十四日下午在布拉格举行的记者招待会上说，“我们认为为了和平的利益，人民中国应当在联合国组织中有合法的地位，这也是一个正义的问题。”他说，“从和平观点来看，我们认为不承认一个有六亿人口的国家是一个非常严重的问题。”
    在谈到柬埔寨对越南民主共和国的态度的时候，西哈努克亲王说：柬埔寨已经同南越就互派人员的问题达成协议，这种人员不是外交代表也不是经济代表，而是我们两国派出的作为我们两国政府间的一种联络机关的代表。如果北越对我们的关系友好的话，如果北越尊重我们的中立的话，我们将愿意同它“建立关系”、特别是贸易关系。
    西哈努克在答复记者提出的关于遵守日内瓦协定的问题的时候说：“我们希望所有的国际协定，特别是日内瓦协定得到遵守和实行。”
