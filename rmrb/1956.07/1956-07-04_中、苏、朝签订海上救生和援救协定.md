Title: China, Soviet Union, and North Korea Sign Maritime Lifesaving and Rescue Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 3rd: The Soviet Union, China, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's Maritime Lifesaving Cooperation Conference concluded in Moscow on July 3rd.

    The conference, which began on June 25th, concluded after several days of sincere, friendly, and mutually understanding discussions, with the signing of an agreement between the Soviet Union, China, and North Korea regarding maritime lifesaving and rescue of ships and aircraft in distress at sea. The agreement was signed by Frolov, Director of the Maritime Accident Rescue Bureau of the Soviet Union; Zhang Zhiyuan, Director of the Foreign Liaison Bureau of the Ministry of Communications of China; and Hong Yu-pyo, Director of the Maritime Affairs Department of the Ministry of Transportation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 中、苏、朝签订海上救生和援救协定

1956-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中、苏、朝签订海上救生和援救协定
    新华社莫斯科3日电　苏联、中国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国三国海上救生合作会议七月三日在莫斯科闭幕。
    会议从六月二十五日起，经过几天的真诚友好和互相了解的讨论后，签订了苏、中、朝三国间关于海上救生和援救在海上遇难船只和飞机的协定。在协定上签字的，苏联是海难救护局局长弗罗洛夫，中国是交通部对外联络局局长张致远，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国是交通省海事处处长洪裕杓。
