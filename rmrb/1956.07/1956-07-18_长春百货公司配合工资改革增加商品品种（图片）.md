Title: Changchun Department Store Increases Variety of Goods to Support Wage Reform (Picture)
Author: Tian Jianzhi
Date: 1956-07-18
Page: 3

Changchun Department Store Increases Variety of Goods to Support Wage Reform
	In order to ensure the purchasing power needs of employees after the wage reform, the Changchun State-owned Department Store has recently transported a large number of goods from Shanghai, Tianjin, Guangzhou and other places. The picture shows the salespersons of the women's clothing group of the No. 2 Store of Changchun State-owned Department Store sorting out the newly arrived women's new-style clothing.
		Xinhua News Agency reporter Tian Jianzhi

    长春百货公司配合工资改革增加商品品种
