Title: China's Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Conclude Games in Poland
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 20th. China's men's and women's basketball teams held their final games in Poland in Poznan on July 19th.
    As a result of the games, the Chinese women's team defeated the Polish women's team by a score of 56 to 40. The Chinese men's team lost to the Polish national team's second team by a score of 89 to 104.

### 我国男女篮球队结束在波兰的比赛

1956-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    我国男女篮球队
    结束在波兰的比赛
    新华社华沙20日电　中国男女篮球队七月十九日在波兹南举行了访问波兰的最后一次比赛。
    比赛结果，中国女子队以五十六比四十战胜了波兰女子队。中国男子队以八十九比一百零四败于波兰国家代表队第二队。
