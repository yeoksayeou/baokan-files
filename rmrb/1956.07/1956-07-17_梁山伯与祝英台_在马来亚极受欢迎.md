Title: "The Butterfly Lovers" Extremely Popular in Malaya
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 16th dispatch from Kuala Lumpur: The Chinese color film "The Butterfly Lovers" has been officially shown at the Kwong Yik Theater in Kuala Lumpur since July 10th and has been warmly welcomed by audiences. The theater shows the film five times a day, and every show is sold out.

This is the first time that a film from New China has been shown in the Federation of Malaya. When the news of the preview was announced on the 5th, thousands of spectators quickly gathered in front of the theater to wait to buy tickets. Although the theater authorities announced that tickets would not be sold in advance until the 6th, they still stood there until late at night before leaving.

### “梁山伯与祝英台”在马来亚极受欢迎

1956-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    “梁山伯与祝英台”在马来亚极受欢迎
    据新华社16日讯　吉隆坡消息：中国彩色影片“梁山伯与祝英台”七月十日开始在吉隆坡光艺戏院正式公演以来，受到观众热烈欢迎。戏院每天放演五场，场场满座。
    新中国影片在马来亚联合邦上演这还是第一次。当预演的消息在五日发表后，戏院门前很快地便聚集了成千的观众等候买票。虽然戏院当局宣布要到六日才预售戏票，但是他们仍然站立到深夜才离开。
