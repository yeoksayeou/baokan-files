Title: Prominent Figures in Portugal Demand Political Freedom
Author:
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Lisbon, July 12th: Fifty prominent Portuguese figures, including three former Prime Ministers, submitted a memorandum to Portuguese President Lopes on July 11th, requesting the government to implement political amnesty and grant the Portuguese people political and civil liberties.

### 葡萄牙著名人士要求实行政治自由

1956-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

    葡萄牙著名人士要求实行政治自由
    据新华社12日讯　里斯本消息：包括三个前总理在内的五十个葡萄牙著名人士，七月十一日交给葡萄牙总统洛皮什一份备忘录，要求政府实行政治大赦，给葡萄牙人民政治自由和公民自由。
