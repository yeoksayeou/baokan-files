Title: Outstanding Officers and Soldiers on the Fujian Front Join the Party
Author:
Date: 1956-07-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 29th: A number of outstanding young officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army stationed on the Fujian Front have recently joined the glorious ranks of the Chinese Communist Party.

Among these newly admitted Party members, the majority are outstanding members of the New Democratic Youth League. Of the sixty new Party members recently developed by the Party Committee of a certain infantry unit, forty-six were Youth League members. Each of them has made outstanding contributions to the great tasks of defending the motherland and supporting the country's socialist construction, and has become an advanced element in the unit's combat, training, and various other tasks.

Many soldiers who enlisted in 1955 and progressed rapidly have also become glorious Communist Party members. Among the newly admitted officers and soldiers in various units, there are also some outstanding young intellectuals.

### 福建前线优秀官兵入党

1956-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    福建前线优秀官兵入党
    据新华社福建前线29日电  福建前线中国人民解放军部队中的许多优秀青年军官和士兵，最近加入了中国共产党的光荣行列。
    这些入党的官兵中，大部分是新民主主义青年团的优秀团员。中共某步兵部队党委最近发展的一批六十名新党员中，就有四十六名团员。他们每个人都在保卫祖国和支援国家社会主义建设的伟大任务中，做出优异的贡献，成为部队在战斗、练兵和各项工作中的先进分子。
    许多进步很快的1955年应征入伍的士兵，也成为光荣的共产党员了。在各部队新入党的官兵中，还有一些优秀的青年知识分子。
