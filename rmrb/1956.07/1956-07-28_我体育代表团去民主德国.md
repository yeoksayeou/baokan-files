Title: Our Sports Delegation to the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: The Chinese sports delegation, invited to visit the Second German Sports Festival, left Beijing by plane today for the German Democratic Republic. This sports festival is scheduled to begin on August 2nd in Leipzig.

### 我体育代表团去民主德国

1956-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    我体育代表团去民主德国
    据新华社27日讯　应邀参观第二届德国运动大会的中国体育代表团，今天坐飞机离开北京前往德意志民主共和国。这次运动大会定八月二日开始在来比锡举行。
