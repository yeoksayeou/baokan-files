Title: Due to Increasing National Expenditure, West German Prices Soar
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 16th: Bonn dispatch: West German prices are constantly rising. According to statistics from the Duisburg Statistical Bureau, from March 1955 to March 1956, West German food prices rose by 15% to 80%, with potatoes rising by 70%, eggs by 25%, fish by 25%, and sausage prices by 15%.

The joint forecast announced by major West German economic research institutes on July 12 stated that due to the increasing national expenditure, the trend of rising prices in West Germany will develop at an even greater rate.

### 由于国家支出日增  西德物价猛涨

1956-07-17
第6版()
专栏：

    由于国家支出日增
    西德物价猛涨
    据新华社柏林16日电　波恩消息：西德物价不断上涨。根据杜伊斯堡统计局的统计，从1955年3月到1956年3月，西德的食品价格上涨了15％到80％，其中马铃薯上涨了70％，蛋类上涨了25％，鱼类上涨了25％，腊肠价格上涨了15％。
    西德各大经济研究所在七月十二日宣布的联合预测说：由于国家支出日益增加，西德物价上涨的趋势将以更大的速度向前发展。
