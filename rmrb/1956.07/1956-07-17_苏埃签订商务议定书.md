Title: Soviet-Egyptian Sign Trade Protocol
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 16th: Egypt and the Soviet Union signed a joint trade protocol on July 15th for the establishment of a trade office by the Soviet Union in Cairo. The purpose of establishing this office is to develop trade between the two countries.
    The protocol was signed on behalf of their respective governments by Abdel Fattah Hassan, Egyptian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Kiselev, Soviet Ambassador to Egypt. On the 17th, the Soviet Union and Egypt also signed a barter agreement worth one million Egyptian pounds.

### 苏埃签订商务议定书

1956-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    苏埃签订商务议定书
    据新华社16日讯　开罗消息：埃及和苏联七月十五日就苏联在开罗设立一个贸易办事处签订了一项联合商务议定书。设立这个办事处的目的是发展两国间的贸易。
    代表两国政府在这个议定书上签字的是：埃及外交部副部长阿卜杜勒·法塔赫·哈桑和苏联驻埃及大使基谢廖夫。十七日，苏联和埃及还签订了一项一百万埃及镑的换货协定。
