Title: Iranian King Leaves Moscow for Home

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 12th: His Majesty Mohammad Reza Pahlavi, the King of Iran, Queen Soraya Pahlavi, and their entourage, left Moscow by plane this morning to return home.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and his wife, Bulganin, Malenkov, Pervukhin, Zhukov, and Shepilov, as well as other responsible persons of the Soviet government.

The King of Iran gave a brief speech at the airport. He said that we can be good friends and neighbors, jointly follow the path of social progress, live in peace, and achieve our goals. I hope I can very accurately report to my people your feelings and your sincere attitude towards us, and convey to you the same feelings of our people.

### 伊朗国王离莫斯科回国

1956-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

    伊朗国王离莫斯科回国
    新华社莫斯科12日电　伊朗国王穆罕默德·礼萨·巴列维陛下和莎拉雅·巴列维王后及随行人员，今天上午乘飞机离开莫斯科回国。
    到机场欢送的有苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和夫人，布尔加宁、马林科夫、别尔乌辛、朱可夫和谢皮洛夫，以及苏联政府的其他负责人员等。
    伊朗国王在机场上发表了简短的讲话。他说，我们能够成为很好的朋友和邻国，共同遵循着社会进步的道路前进，在和平中生活，并且达到我们的目标。我希望我能够非常确切地向我国人民报告你们的感情和你们对我们的真诚的态度，并且向你们转达我国人民的同样的感情。
