Title: Our Acrobatic Troupe Invited to Visit the South
Author:
Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 5th. At the invitation of the government of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, led by its head Liu Yi, departed from Beijing today to perform a visit in Belgrade, the capital of Yugoslavia. This troupe will also perform in Italy, France, Belgium and other countries.

### 我杂技团应邀赴南

1956-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    我杂技团应邀赴南
    据新华社5日讯　应南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府邀请的中国杂技团今天在团长柳夷率领下，从北京启程赴南斯拉夫首都贝尔格莱德作访问演出。这个团还将赴意大利、法国、比利时等国演出。
