Title: Cyprus Becomes a World of Terror; British Authorities Conduct More Arrests
Author:
Date: 1956-07-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Nicosia, 13th: British colonial authorities have recently conducted large-scale arrests in Cyprus.

On July 11th, British colonial military police suddenly blockaded the main traffic arteries of Nicosia, the capital of Cyprus, searched pedestrians, and arrested about two hundred local residents of Greek descent on the spot.

### 塞浦路斯成恐怖世界  英国当局又大捕人

1956-07-14
第6版()
专栏：

    塞浦路斯成恐怖世界
    英国当局又大捕人
    据新华社13日讯　尼科西亚消息：英国殖民当局最近又在塞浦路斯进行大规模逮捕。
    七月十一日，英国殖民军警突然封锁了塞浦路斯首府尼科西亚市的交通要道，搜查行人，并当场逮捕了约两百个当地的希腊血统居民。
