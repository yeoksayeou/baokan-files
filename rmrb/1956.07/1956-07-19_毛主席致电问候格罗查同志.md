Title: Chairman Mao Sends a Message of Greetings to Comrade Groza
Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Chairman Mao Zedong's message of greetings to Comrade Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic. The full text is as follows:

To the Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, Dear Comrade Petru Groza:

Upon learning of your illness, the Chinese people and I personally send you our sincere greetings with great concern, and wholeheartedly hope that you will recover your health soon.

Mao Zedong, July 18, 1956, in Beijing

### 毛主席致电问候格罗查同志

1956-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席致电问候格罗查同志
    新华社18日讯　毛泽东主席给罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查的问候电。全文如下：罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席亲爱的彼·格罗查同志：
    获悉你患病的消息，中国人民和我本人怀着十分关切的心情，向你谨致诚挚的问候，并且衷心地希望你早日恢复健康。
                            毛泽东　1956年7月18日于北京
