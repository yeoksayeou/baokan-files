Title: US Weekly Admits US-Japan Relations Are Heading Towards Trouble
Author:
Date: 1956-07-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 13th: The associate editor of the American weekly "Saturday Evening Post," Demaree Bess, who will be published tomorrow, wrote: My impression is that US-Japan relations are not currently in danger, but a trend has begun, "This trend may soon make this relationship dangerous." Bess said that the main factors reducing American influence include: Japan's tendency to establish closer relations with China.

### 美国一家周刊承认  美日关系趋向不妙

1956-07-14
第6版()
专栏：

    美国一家周刊承认
    美日关系趋向不妙
    据新华社13日讯　纽约消息：将在明天出版的美国“星期六晚邮报”周刊的副主笔迪马斯·伯斯写道：我的印象是日美关系当前还没有处在危险中，但是一种趋向已经开始，“这种趋向可能很快地使这种关系变得危险起来”。伯斯说，使美国的影响减少的主要因素包括：日本倾向于同中国建立较为密切的关系。
