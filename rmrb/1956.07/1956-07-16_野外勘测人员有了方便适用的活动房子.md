Title: Field Surveyors Have Convenient and Practical Mobile Houses
Author:
Date: 1956-07-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 15th. The Harbin Joint State-Private Operated Songhua River Furniture Factory has recently manufactured forty mobile and durable houses for surveying teams working in the field in Heilongjiang Province. The total weight of this type of mobile house is only two thousand kilograms, and it is assembled from one hundred and sixteen components. When all the components of the entire house are folded, they can be completely transported by a horse-drawn rubber-wheeled cart. The area of the house is thirty-six square meters, which can be used for office work for ten people or accommodation for sixteen people.

### 野外勘测人员有了方便适用的活动房子

1956-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    野外勘测人员有了方便适用的活动房子
    据新华社哈尔滨15日电　哈尔滨市公私合营松花江家俱厂，最近给在黑龙江省野外工作的勘测队员们制造了四十幢移动方便、耐用的活动房子。这种活动房子全重只有两千公斤，是由一百十六块部件组合起来的。把整幢房子的部件折叠起来，用一辆马拉胶皮大车就能全部装走。房子的面积有三十六平方公尺，能供十个人办公或十六个人住宿用。
