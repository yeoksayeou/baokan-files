Title: Teng Daiyuan Returns Home from Berlin
Author:
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 12th: Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the People's Republic of China, concluded his visit to the German Democratic Republic and left Berlin by train this morning to return home. Kramar, Minister of Transportation of the German Democratic Republic, and others were at the station to see him off.

During his six days in the German Democratic Republic, Teng Daiyuan visited more than ten cities, including Berlin, Dresden, Zwickau, and Leipzig, and toured newly built German double-decker train carriages, new-style reinforced concrete sleepers, new-style automatic dispatch consoles, carriage manufacturing plants, and railway administrations. He also spoke with some heads and experts from the Ministry of Transportation of the German Democratic Republic and exchanged experiences.

### 滕代远离柏林回国

1956-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

    滕代远离柏林回国
    新华社柏林12日电　中华人民共和国铁道部部长滕代远结束了在德意志民主共和国的访问，在今天上午乘火车离柏林回国。到车站欢送的有民主德国交通部长克拉麦尔等人。
    滕代远在民主德国的六天中，访问了柏林、德累斯顿、兹维考和来比锡等十多个城市，参观了德国新造的双层火车车厢、新式的钢筋混凝土枕木、新式的自动调度台、车厢制造厂和铁道管理局等；并且同民主德国交通部的一些负责人和专家们谈话，交流了经验。
