Title: Shen Yanbing Hosts a Banquet to Bid Farewell to the Egyptian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 12th. Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing hosted a banquet at noon today to bid farewell to Dr. Shawarbi, head of the Egyptian Cultural Delegation, and his wife, as well as delegation members Mr. Latif and Dr. Charan.

    Egyptian Republic's Commercial Representative to China, Faer, and Deputy Representative Meng Dawei were also invited to attend the banquet.

    Among those present were Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association Da Putsheng, and President of Peking University Ma Yinchu.

    When the Egyptian Cultural Delegation left Beijing this evening, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan and others went to the station to see them off.

### 沈雁冰设宴欢送埃及文化使团

1956-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰设宴欢送埃及文化使团
    新华社12日讯　文化部部长沈雁冰今天中午举行宴会，欢送埃及文化使团团长夏瓦比博士及夫人，团员拉蒂夫先生和查然博士。
    埃及共和国驻华商务代表法尔、副代表孟大维也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的，有文化部副部长夏衍，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，北京大学校长马寅初等人。
    埃及文化使团今晚离开北京时，文化部副部长夏衍等曾到车站欢送。
