Title: Ukrainian National Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 5

Expanding Cultural Exchanges Between Fraternal Countries

    Ukrainian National Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, July 1st: The Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic National Dance Troupe, consisting of eighty members and led by Artistic Director Virsky and Head of the Troupe Pishmenny, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon.

    The art troupe was invited by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China to participate in the closing ceremony of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition held in Wuhan and to tour and perform in various parts of China.

    Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan delivered a welcome speech at the station, saying that the previous visits of Soviet art troupes to China over the past few years have written glorious chapters in the history of friendship and cultural exchange between the Chinese and Soviet people. He believes that this visit of the dance troupe will further enrich the content of cultural exchange between China and the Soviet Union and will further promote the great friendship between the people of the two countries.

    In his reply, the Artistic Director of the dance troupe, Virsky, said that they felt particularly honored to arrive in the capital of China on the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party, and hoped that their visit and performances would help to further enhance the great friendship and cultural exchange between the Chinese and Soviet people.

    The dance troupe will travel to Wuhan by train tonight.

### 乌克兰国家舞蹈团到京

1956-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    扩大兄弟国家间文化交流
    乌克兰国家舞蹈团到京
    据新华社1日讯　苏联乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国国家舞蹈团一行八十人，在艺术指导维尔斯基和团长彼什缅内的率领下，今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    艺术团是应中华人民共和国文化部的邀请，前来参加在武汉举行的苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会闭幕式和在中国各地访问演出的。
    文化部副部长夏衍在车站上致欢迎词说，几年来苏联艺术团体的历次访问中国，都在中苏两国人民友谊和文化交流的历史上写下了光辉的诗篇。他相信舞蹈团这次访问中国，将进一步丰富中苏两国文化交流的内容，并且将进一步促进中苏两国人民的伟大友谊。
    舞蹈团艺术指导维尔斯基在致答词时，对他们在中国共产党建立三十五周年纪念日到达中国的首都感到特别荣幸，并且希望他们的访问演出能够有助于进一步增进中苏两国人民的伟大友谊和文化交流。
    今晚舞蹈团乘火车前往武汉。
