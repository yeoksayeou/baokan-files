Title: The Central Delegation's Third Sub-Delegation Travels Across Mountains and Rivers to Visit the Border Region of Ali, Tibet
Author:
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, July 19th: The Third Sub-Delegation of the Central Delegation arrived in Gar Kunsa, Ali Prefecture, Tibet, on the afternoon of the 16th.

	On that day, monks, laypeople, troops stationed in Ali by the Tibet Military Region, and staff members welcomed the arrival of the Central Delegation on the banks of the Gar River. More than ten miles from Gar Kunsa, at the welcoming tent, Losang Tsering, the Ali Gaben of the Tibetan Local Government, and his deputy, Gala Tseba, presented Hada scarves to Wang Xinggang, the head of the Third Sub-Delegation. After Wang Xinggang and the representatives arrived in Gar Kunsa, amidst the sound of gongs, drums, and Buddhist chants, they also received Hada scarves presented by the headmen of the twelve districts of Ali, representatives of herdsmen, and representatives of the lamas of Zhaxigang Monastery.

	Following this, a welcoming meeting was held by people from all walks of life in Ali. Gala Tseba delivered a welcome speech, saying: The Third Sub-Delegation of the Central Delegation has come to visit the western border region of Ali, Tibet, braving hardships and traveling across thousands of mountains and rivers, which demonstrates the care and love of the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao for our people of Ali. On behalf of the people of all walks of life in Ali, he expressed heartfelt gratitude and respect to the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao. He said: "Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao, and under the direct leadership of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, we will strive for the early establishment of the Tibet Autonomous Region and strive to build a new Tibet and a great socialist motherland."

	Head of the Delegation, Wang Xinggang, extended cordial greetings on behalf of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao to the monks and lay officials and people of the Ali region, the officers and soldiers of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Ali, and the staff members. He hoped that the Tibetan monks and lay officials and people, the People's Liberation Army, and staff members in Ali would unite more closely and work hard to build the border region of the motherland.

	After leaving Lhasa on May 28th, the Third Sub-Delegation of the Central Delegation traveled through the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region to Ali. They visited and comforted the troops along the Kunlun Mountains and were welcomed by the Zongben of Rituzong, the lamas of Lunzhu Quedeng Monastery, and the herdsmen when they arrived in Rituzong, in the northern part of the Ali region.

### 中央代表团第三分团跋山涉水到达西藏边疆阿里地区访问

1956-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    中央代表团第三分团跋山涉水到达西藏边疆阿里地区访问
    新华社拉萨19日电　中央代表团第三分团在十六日下午到达西藏阿里地区噶尔昆沙。
    这天，阿里地区僧俗人民、西藏军区驻阿里部队和工作人员，在噶尔河畔欢迎中央代表团的到来。在距噶尔昆沙十多里的欢迎帐篷前，西藏地方政府阿里噶本洛桑才旺和副噶本加拉色巴向第三分团团长王兴纲献了哈达。王兴纲和代表们到达噶尔昆沙以后，在锣鼓和佛号声中，又接受了阿里十二区的头人、牧民代表和札锡冈寺喇嘛代表们献的哈达。
    接着，阿里各界人民举行了欢迎大会。加拉色巴致欢迎词说：中央代表团第三分团不辞劳苦，跋涉千山万水，来到祖国西部边疆阿里地区访问，说明了中国共产党、中央人民政府和毛主席对我们阿里人民的关怀和爱护。他代表阿里各界人民向中国共产党、中央人民政府和毛主席表示衷心的感谢和敬意。他说：“我们要在中国共产党、中央人民政府和毛主席的领导下，在西藏自治区筹备委员会和达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼的直接领导下，为早日成立西藏自治区而努力，为建设新西藏，建设伟大的社会主义祖国而奋斗”。
    王兴纲团长代表中共中央、中央人民政府和毛主席向阿里地区僧俗官员和人民、中国人民解放军西藏军区驻阿里部队官兵和工作人员致亲切慰问。他希望在阿里的藏族僧俗官员和人民、人民解放军和工作人员更紧密地团结起来，努力建设祖国边疆。
    中央代表团第三分团五月二十八日离开拉萨以后，取道新疆维吾尔自治区来到阿里，曾在昆仑山南北麓慰问了沿途部队，到达阿里地区北部的日土宗时，曾经受到日土宗宗本、伦珠确登寺喇嘛和牧民们的欢迎。
