Title: International Parachuting Championships Begin in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-07-30
Page: 5

Moscow Dispatch from our Newspaper: The opening ceremony of the Third International Parachuting Championships was held at the Tushino Airfield in Moscow on July 29th. The grand opening ceremony commenced precisely at 12:00 noon. On the green lawn, the national flags of Bulgaria, Hungary, Israel, Poland, Romania, the United States, France, Czechoslovakia, Yugoslavia, and the Soviet Union were flying. Opposite the rostrum stood the excellent parachutists from these ten countries. After the opening ceremony, aviation athletes from the Soviet Chkalov Aviation Club performed aviation sports such as gliders, parachuting, model airplanes, and kites. Following this, athletes from various countries held a competition for precise parachuting from a height of 600 meters above the ground, within a circle with a diameter of 150 meters. The entire competition will conclude on August 5th.

Representatives from the People's Republic of China, the United Kingdom, Turkey, Sweden, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Mongolian People's Republic have been sent as observers to attend this competition.

### 国际跳伞运动锦标赛在莫斯科开始

1956-07-30
第5版()
专栏：

    国际跳伞运动锦标赛在莫斯科开始
    本报莫斯科29日电　七月二十九日，在莫斯科的土希诺飞机场举行了第三届国际跳伞运动锦标赛的开幕式。中午十二时整，隆重的开幕式宣告开始。在绿色草坪上，飘扬着保加利亚、匈牙利、以色列、波兰、罗马尼亚、美国、法国、捷克斯洛伐克、南斯拉夫和苏联的国旗。主席台的对面，站着这十个国家的优秀的跳伞运动员。开幕式结束后，苏联的乞格列夫航空俱乐部的航空运动员表演了滑翔机、跳伞、飞机模型、风筝等航空运动。接着，各国运动员举行了距地面六百公尺的、在直径一百五十公尺的圆周内精确跳伞的竞赛。整个竞赛将在八月五日结束。
    派观察员出席这次竞赛的有中华人民共和国、英国、土耳其、瑞典、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国的代表。
