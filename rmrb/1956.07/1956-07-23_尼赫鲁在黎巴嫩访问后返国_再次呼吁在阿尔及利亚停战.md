Title: Nehru Returns Home After Visiting Lebanon, Reiterates Call for Ceasefire in Algeria
Author:
Date: 1956-07-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, July 22nd: Indian Prime Minister Nehru has left Beirut by plane today to return to India after a one-day official visit to Lebanon.

After arriving in Beirut on July 21, Nehru held a series of talks with Lebanese Parliament Speaker Ousséiran, Prime Minister Yafi, and Foreign Minister Lahoud. Sources say they discussed the issues of Algeria and Palestine.

At a luncheon held in his honor by Lebanese Prime Minister Yafi, Nehru said: India recognizes that it is impossible for a country or even a continent to practice isolationism today. No one can live an isolated life now. What every country is most concerned about is that we should avoid war and achieve peace. He also said that a world war is unacceptable today.

Nehru said that India has never sought leadership or interference, but only seeks friendly cooperation in the tasks facing the world.

When talking about the issues of Algeria and Palestine, Nehru said: A solution to the Algerian problem should be sought through direct negotiations based on the recognition of Algeria's freedom. Nehru said that a ceasefire must first be implemented in Algeria, and then a solution should be studied in a spirit of peace, patience, and mutual understanding. He also said that a solution to the Palestinian problem must be sought in the same spirit, because war cannot solve any problem.

Lebanon is the last country Nehru visited in Europe and the Middle East after attending the Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference in London.

### 尼赫鲁在黎巴嫩访问后返国  再次呼吁在阿尔及利亚停战

1956-07-23
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁在黎巴嫩访问后返国
    再次呼吁在阿尔及利亚停战
    新华社22日讯　贝鲁特消息：印度总理尼赫鲁在黎巴嫩进行了为时一天的正式访问后，已经在今天乘飞机离开贝鲁特返回印度。
    尼赫鲁在七月二十一日抵达贝鲁特后，曾经同黎巴嫩国会议长奥塞兰、总理雅非和外交部长拉霍德举行了一系列会谈。消息说，他们讨论了阿尔及利亚和巴勒斯坦问题。
    尼赫鲁在黎巴嫩总理雅非为他举行的午宴上说：印度认识到今天对一个国家或者甚至一个洲来说，都不可能实行孤立主义。现在谁也不能过孤立的生活，每个国家最最关切的是，我们应避免战争，应当实现和平。他还说，世界大战在今天是一件不能容许的事情。
    尼赫鲁说，印度向来不谋求领导和干涉，只谋求在世界面临的任务中实行友好合作。
    尼赫鲁在谈到阿尔及利亚和巴勒斯坦问题的时候说：应当通过直接谈判来寻求一个以承认阿尔及利亚的自由为基础的解决阿尔及利亚问题的办法。尼赫鲁说，在阿尔及利亚，首先必须实行停火，然后本着和平、忍耐和互相谅解的精神来研究出一个解决办法。他还说，必须本着同样的精神来寻求一个解决巴勒斯坦问题的办法，因为战争不能解决任何问题。
    黎巴嫩是尼赫鲁在伦敦参加英联邦总理会议后在欧洲和中东访问的最后一个国家。
