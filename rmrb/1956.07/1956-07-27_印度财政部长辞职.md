Title: Indian Finance Minister Resigns
Author:
Date: 1956-07-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 25th: The President of India approved the resignation of Finance Minister Deshmukh on the 24th. The Ministry of Finance will now be under the responsibility of Prime Minister Nehru.

Deshmukh said in the People's House today that his resignation was in protest against the government's decision to place the city of Bombay outside of Maharashtra State.

### 印度财政部长辞职

1956-07-27
第6版()
专栏：

    印度财政部长辞职
    据新华社新德里25日电　印度总统二十四日批准了财政部长德希穆克的辞职。财政部改由总理尼赫鲁负责。
    德希穆克今天在人民院说，他的辞职是对政府把孟买市划在马哈拉施特拉邦以外表示抗议。
