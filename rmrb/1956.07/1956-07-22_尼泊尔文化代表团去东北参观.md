Title: Nepalese Cultural Delegation Visits Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — The Nepalese cultural delegation to China, led by Mr. Ba. Sharma, Minister of Education, Health, and Local Self-Government of the Kingdom of Nepal, concluded its visit to Beijing and left Beijing by train on the evening of the 20th to visit various parts of Northeast China.

During its stay in Beijing, the delegation had extensive contacts with people from all walks of life in China. They visited the History Museum, the Exhibition of Natural Environment and Resources of the Motherland, and cultural institutions such as Peking University. When the delegation visited Guangji Temple, they were received by Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, and others. While at the Asian Students Sanatorium, Chairman Sharma presented the Nepalese books and records he brought with him to the Nepalese students recuperating there.

The delegation also visited famous historical sites such as the Great Wall and the Ming Tombs, and visited an agricultural production cooperative in the suburbs of Beijing.

### 尼泊尔文化代表团去东北参观

1956-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔文化代表团去东北参观
    据新华社20日讯　尼泊尔王国教育、卫生、地方自治政府大臣巴·夏尔马率领的尼泊尔访华文化代表团，结束了在北京的访问，二十日晚乘火车离北京去东北各地参观。
    代表团在北京期间，同中国各界人士进行了广泛的接触。参观了历史博物馆、祖国自然环境和资源展览会，以及北京大学等文化机关。代表团在参观广济寺时，受到中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初等的接待。夏尔马团长在亚洲学生疗养院时，把他带来的尼泊尔书籍和唱片赠送给住院休养的尼泊尔学生。
    代表团在北京还游览了长城、明十三陵等名胜古迹，访问了北京郊区的一个农业生产合作社。
