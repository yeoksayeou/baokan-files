Title: Yu Men Crude Oil Directly Transported to Shanghai by Train, Freight Costs Reduced by 36% Compared to Before
Author:
Date: 1956-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 14th. The first batch of crude oil directly loaded onto trains from Yu Men and transported eastward arrived at the Shanghai refinery yesterday.

According to the refinery director, the freight cost for this batch of crude oil has already been reduced by 36% compared to when the railway only reached Qingshui not long ago.

### 玉门原油由火车直运上海  运费比以前降低36％

1956-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    玉门原油由火车直运上海
    运费比以前降低36％
    新华社上海14日电　从玉门直接装上火车东运的第一批原油，昨天已运到了上海的炼油厂。
    据炼油厂的厂长说，这批原油的运费，比不久前火车只通到清水的时候，已经降低了36％。
