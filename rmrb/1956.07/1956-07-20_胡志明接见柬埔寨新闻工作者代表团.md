Title: Ho Chi Minh Receives Delegation of Cambodian Journalists
Author:
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 19th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received the entire delegation of Cambodian journalists this morning.

    Sang Nhean, head of the Cambodian journalists' delegation, presented gifts to President Ho Chi Minh on behalf of the Cambodian journalists.

### 胡志明接见柬埔寨新闻工作者代表团

1956-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明接见柬埔寨新闻工作者代表团
    新华社河内19日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明今天早晨接见了柬埔寨新闻工作者代表团全体人员。
    柬埔寨新闻工作者代表团团长桑·年代表柬埔寨新闻工作者向胡志明主席献了礼。
