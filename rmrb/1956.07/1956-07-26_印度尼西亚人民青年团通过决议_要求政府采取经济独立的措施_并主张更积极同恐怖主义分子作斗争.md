Title: Indonesian People's Youth League Passes Resolution Demanding Government Take Measures for Economic Independence and Advocating More Active Struggle Against Terrorists
Author:
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta. The Second National Congress of the Indonesian People's Youth League closed on the 22nd.

The congress passed a resolution demanding that the government thoroughly implement measures to abolish the Round Table Conference agreements, that the government nationalize the enterprises of foreign monopoly companies, especially Dutch companies, and confiscate leased lands that have expired to distribute them to landless and poor peasants. The resolution also called for stopping or at least restricting the remittance of profits earned in Indonesia by foreign monopoly companies, demanding the cancellation of debts to the Netherlands, and suggesting an increase in construction projects as a means of providing greater employment opportunities and achieving economic independence.

In its resolution, the congress advocated a more active struggle against the Islamic State Movement and the Indonesian Islamic Army, as well as other foreign subversive terrorists.

The congress elected a national committee of forty-nine members, headed by First Secretary-General Sukatno.

The congress received a congratulatory telegram from President Sukarno.

### 印度尼西亚人民青年团通过决议  要求政府采取经济独立的措施  并主张更积极同恐怖主义分子作斗争

1956-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚人民青年团通过决议
    要求政府采取经济独立的措施
    并主张更积极同恐怖主义分子作斗争
    据新华社雅加达电　印度尼西亚人民青年团第二次全国代表大会在二十二日闭幕。
    代表大会通过一项决议，要求政府彻底执行废除圆桌会议协定的措施，要求政府把外国垄断公司的企业，特别是荷兰的企业收归国有，并且把已经到期的租借地没收来分配给无地的和贫苦的农民。决议还要求制止从印度尼西亚汇出外国垄断公司在印度尼西亚获得的利润，或至少加以限制，要求取消对荷兰的债务，并且建议增加建设工程，作为提供更大的就业机会和获致经济独立的手段。
    代表大会在它的决议中主张更积极地为反对伊斯兰国运动和印度尼西亚伊斯兰军以及其他外国的颠复性恐怖主义分子而斗争。
    大会选出了以第一总书记苏卡特诺为首的由四十九人组成的全国委员会。
    大会收到了苏加诺总统的贺电。
