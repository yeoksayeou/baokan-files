Title: Record-Breaking Shooter

Author:

Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 15th: Shooter Zhang Hong achieved outstanding results in the Shanghai Municipal Shooting Friendly Competition, which concluded today, while participating in the men's 25-meter rapid-fire silhouette target exhibition.

Zhang Hong scored 587 rings out of 60 shots (with a perfect score of 600 rings). This score not only exceeded the Chinese Master of Sports standard of 575 rings but also surpassed the score achieved by Zhgutov, a Merited Master of Sports from the Soviet Union, when he won first place in this event at the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition in Beijing last November, by five rings. Zhgutov was the 1954 world champion in this event, and his best score was 585 rings.

### 打破全国纪录的射击手

1956-07-17
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    打破全国纪录的射击手
    据新华社上海15日电　射击运动员张鋐在今天结束的上海市射击运动友谊比赛中，参加男子小口径手枪人像靶距离二十五公尺立姿速射表演赛时，创造了优异的成绩。
    张鋐六十发子弹共射中了587环（满分是六百环），这个成绩不但超过了中国运动健将级575环的成绩标准，而且比苏联功勋运动员日古托夫去年十一月在北京参加1955年国际友谊射击竞赛获得这项比赛第一名时所创造的成绩还多了五环。日古托夫是1954年这项比赛的世界冠军，他最好的成绩是585环。
