Title: Sihanouk Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 12th. Prince Norodom Sihanouk, former Prime Minister of Cambodia, and his entourage arrived in Prague by plane at noon on the 11th at the invitation of the Czechoslovak government.

At the airport, Sihanouk was warmly welcomed by Fierlinger, Chairman of the Czechoslovak National Assembly, Deputy Prime Minister Kopecky, Foreign Minister David, and others.

After Sihanouk reviewed the guard of honor, Kopecky delivered a welcoming speech. Sihanouk then gave a reply.

### 西哈努克到布拉格

1956-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克到布拉格
    据新华社布拉格12日电　柬埔寨前首相西哈努克亲王和随行人员应捷克斯洛伐克政府的邀请，在十一日中午乘飞机到达布拉格。
    西哈努克在机场上受到了捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席费林格、副总理柯别茨基、外交部长戴维等人的热烈欢迎。
    在西哈努克检阅了仪仗队以后，柯别茨基致了欢迎词。西哈努克接着致答词。
