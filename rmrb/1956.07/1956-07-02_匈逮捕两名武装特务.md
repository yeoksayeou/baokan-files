Title: Hungary Arrests Two Armed Special Agents
Author:
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 30th: The Hungarian State Security organs, with the assistance of the working people, captured Meize and Patti, armed special agents of the imperialist spy agency. They had previously fled Hungary to foreign countries and illegally crossed the border back into the country in June of this year.

    The two arrested armed special agents attempted to collect secret intelligence on military targets, barracks, and airfields. The criminals' automatic rifles, cameras, false documents, and all equipment for espionage activities have been confiscated.

### 匈逮捕两名武装特务

1956-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    匈逮捕两名武装特务
    据新华社1日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯六月三十日讯：匈牙利国家保安机关在劳动人民的协助下，捕获了帝国主义间谍机关的武装特务麦泽和帕蒂。他们曾经从匈牙利逃往国外，在今年六月间又偷越国境回国。
    被捕的两名武装特务企图收集有关军事目标、兵营和机场的秘密情报。罪犯的自动步枪、照像机、假证件和进行间谍活动的一切用品，都已经被没收。
