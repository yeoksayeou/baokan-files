Title: Nosaka Sanzō Calls for Unity of People's Strength to Jointly Strive for Japan's True Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 6
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 10th: Japanese Communist Party First Secretary Nosaka Sanzō, after being elected as a Senator yesterday, stated in a speech that he would make the greatest effort to abolish all treaties signed between Japan and the United States on an unequal basis, strive to have the American troops withdraw from Japan, and fight for Japan's true independence. He also expressed his intention to normalize relations between Japan and the Soviet Union and China, and to promote disarmament and the banning of atomic weapons.

　　Nosaka said that he would suggest working together with the Socialist Party and the Labor-Farmer Party to achieve these goals. He also emphasized that in order to achieve these goals, the unity of the people's strength is very important.

### 野坂参三号召团结人民力量共同努力争取日本真正独立

1956-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    野坂参三号召团结人民力量共同努力争取日本真正独立
    新华社10日讯　东京消息：日本共产党第一书记野坂参三昨天在当选参议员后发表谈话说，他将作最大的努力来废除日美两国在不平等基础上签订的一切条约，努力使美军撤出日本，争取日本的真正独立。他并且表示要使日本同苏联和中国的关系正常化，促使裁减军备和禁止原子武器。
    野坂说，他将建议同社会党和劳农党共同努力来实现这些目标。他还强调指出，为了实现这些目标，人民力量的团结是很重要的。
