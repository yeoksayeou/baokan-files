Title: Dai Buddhist Visiting Group Returns to Yunnan
Author:
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, July 10th: The Yunnan Province Dai Buddhist Visiting Group has concluded its visiting activities in various locations and will begin returning to various parts of Yunnan in batches starting today to participate in the Dai traditional religious festival - the Closing Door Festival.

The visiting group conducted more than two months of visits in various parts of the country, visiting the Buddhist Association of China, the Yonghe Temple and Guangji Temple in Beijing, and also visited Anshan, Lushun-Dalian, Shenyang, Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places. They returned to Kunming on the 5th. Yesterday evening, the Religious Affairs Office of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee hosted a banquet to send them off.

### 傣族佛教参观团返回云南

1956-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

    傣族佛教参观团返回云南
    新华社昆明10日电　云南省傣族佛教参观团已经结束了在各地的参观访问活动，从今天起分批返回云南各地参加傣族传统的宗教节日——关门节。
    参观团在祖国各地进行了两个多月的参观访问活动，曾访问了中国佛教协会，参观了北京的雍和宫、广济寺，并先后到鞍山、旅大、沈阳、天津、南京、上海、杭州等地参观，在五日回到昆明。昨天晚间，云南省人民委员会宗教事务处设宴为他们送行。
