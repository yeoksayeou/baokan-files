Title: Mobile Supply and Marketing Group Delivers Goods to Homes
Author:
Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 3

    Mobile Supply and Marketing Group Delivers Goods to Homes
    (Reported by this newspaper) Yushe County, Shanxi Province, Dazhai Township is a relatively large township in the county, with more than 1,000 households scattered across 15 natural villages of various sizes. Recently, the local supply and marketing cooperative established two mobile supply and marketing groups, adopting the method of "delivering goods to homes and supplying them in the fields" to meet the needs of cooperative members and reduce their travel time. After the mobile supply and marketing group arrived at the Ouni’ao natural village with goods, cooperative members purchased various necessities worth 178 yuan in just one afternoon. At the same time, the supply and marketing group also purchased scrap copper and waste hemp, giving cooperative members pocket money.

### 流动供销组送货上门

1956-07-04
第3版()
专栏：

    流动供销组送货上门
    本报讯　山西省榆社县大寨乡是全县一个较大的乡，全乡一千多户分散在大小十五个自然村里。当地供销社在最近设立两个流动供销组，采用了“送货上门，田间供应”的办法，来满足社员需要，减少社员往返时间。流动供销组带货到达沤尼凹自然村后，仅一晌午时间，社员就选购了各种需用品价值178元，同时，供销组还收购了废铜、烂麻，使社员们有了零花钱。
