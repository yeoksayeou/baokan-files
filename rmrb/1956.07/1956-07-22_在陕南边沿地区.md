Title: In the Border Areas of Southern Shaanxi
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 4

The southern border area of Shaanxi Province, located between the Qinling and Daba Mountains, is a region with great production potential that has not yet been well developed. The masses have a high enthusiasm for developing production, but there are many difficulties in construction. This particularly requires more concern, help, and more specific leadership from higher-level leading organs. The criticism at the Second Congress of the Communist Party of China in Shaanxi Province regarding the provincial leading organs' past lack of attention to this region, and the demand to strengthen leadership over this kind of border area, is completely correct and very necessary. These three pictures illustrate how the people in this border area are actively developing production and how young people from other places are working hard to help the masses here in construction. (Pictures attached)

陕西汉中地区满山遍野都是棕树，全国闻名的汉中棕箱就是用这里的棕编制成的。这是沔县新光农业生产合作社的社员正在割棕。
    The Hanzhong area of Shaanxi is full of palm trees. The nationally famous Hanzhong palm boxes are woven from the palm here. This is a member of the Xinguang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Mian County harvesting palm.

陈秀娟在浙江黄岩农校毕业后，来到了城固县农业技术推广站工作。她克服了很多困难，协助农业生产合作社推广了新的耕作技术。
    After graduating from Huangyan Agricultural School in Zhejiang, Chen Xiujuan came to work at the Chenggu County Agricultural Technology Extension Station. She overcame many difficulties and assisted the agricultural production cooperative in promoting new cultivation techniques.

在秦岭山谷和大巴山上，有党参、单皮等许多珍贵的药材。这是凤县十里店九星农业生产合作社社员正在挖药材。（以上图片毓秀摄）
    In the Qinling valleys and the Daba Mountains, there are many precious medicinal materials such as dangshen and danpi. This is a member of the Shili Dian Jiuxing Agricultural Production Cooperative in Feng County digging medicinal materials. (Photos above by Yuxiu)

### 在陕南边沿地区

1956-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    在陕南边沿地区
    位于秦岭和大巴山之间的陕西省南部边沿地区，是一块生产潜力很大但还没有很好开发的地区，群众发展生产的热情很高，建设中的困难较多，这就特别需要上级领导机关更多地关怀、帮助和更具体的领导。中共陕西省第二次代表大会上批评了省级领导机关过去不重视这一地区，要求加强对这种边沿地区的领导，是完全正确和十分必要的。这三幅图片说明这个边沿地区的人民如何积极地发展生产和外地的青年如何努力帮助这里的群众进行建设。（附图片）
