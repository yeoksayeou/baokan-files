Title: Nehru Holds Talks with French Prime Minister and Foreign Minister, Emphasizes Immediate Armistice in Algeria
Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 18th, Paris Dispatch: Indian Prime Minister Nehru left West Germany on the morning of the 17th and flew from Hamburg to Paris for a visit.

In Paris, Nehru held talks with French Foreign Minister Pineau. According to Western news agencies, they discussed the Algerian issue, East-West relations, the European situation, and the results of Nehru's visit to West Germany. The Algerian issue held a prominent position.

Sources say that in his talks with Pineau, Nehru emphasized the need to immediately stop the war in Algeria and expressed his hope that a mutually satisfactory solution could be found to peacefully resolve the Algerian issue.

Subsequently, Nehru held talks with French Prime Minister Mollet. In the evening, Nehru held lengthy talks with Mollet and Pineau again.

Nehru was also received by French President Coty and the President of the French National Assembly, Le Troquer.

Xinhua News Agency, July 18th, Paris Dispatch: Indian Prime Minister Nehru made a speech at the meeting of the "Franco-Indian Friendship" Committee of the French Parliament on the 17th. He said that the world is in the midst of political, economic, and social revolutions. One of the major events of this era is the liberation of the Asian people. The African liberation movement is also gaining momentum. All nations hope for independence and to improve their living standards.

Nehru said that the great forces that have been unleashed must be controlled. They can be for good or for ill. He said that India's political revolution was carried out peacefully, and he hopes that the economic and social revolutions will also be peaceful.

He also said that all international problems must be resolved by peaceful means. The atmosphere of mutual suspicion must be eliminated. Peaceful means must be used to create an atmosphere of peace.

### 尼赫鲁同法国总理和外长会谈  强调应立即在阿尔及利亚停战

1956-07-19
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁同法国总理和外长会谈
    强调应立即在阿尔及利亚停战
    新华社18日讯　巴黎消息：印度总理尼赫鲁十七日上午离开西德，从汉堡乘飞机到巴黎访问。
    在巴黎，尼赫鲁同法国外交部长比诺举行了会谈。据西方通讯社报道，他们谈到了阿尔及利亚问题、东西方关系、欧洲局势和尼赫鲁访问西德的结果等问题。其中以阿尔及利亚问题占重要地位。
    有消息说，尼赫鲁在同比诺会谈中强调必须立即停止在阿尔及利亚的战争，并且表示希望能够找到使双方满意的办法来和平解决阿尔及利亚问题。
    随后，尼赫鲁同法国总理摩勒会谈。晚上，尼赫鲁又同摩勒和比诺举行了长时间的会谈。
    尼赫鲁还受到了法国总统戈蒂和法国国民议会议长勒特罗盖的接见。
    据新华社18日讯　巴黎消息：印度总理尼赫鲁十七日在法国议会“法印友好”委员会会议上发表谈话。他说，世界正处在政治、经济和社会革命中。这个时代的重大事件之一，是亚洲人民的解放。非洲的解放运动也正在得势。各民族都希望独立和改善他们的生活水平。
    尼赫鲁说，已经发动起来的伟大力量必须要加以控制。它可以为善也可以为恶。他说，印度的政治革命是和平地进行的，他希望经济革命和社会革命也会是和平的。
    他还说，国际间的一切问题必须用和平方法来解决。互相猜疑的气氛必须消除。必须用和平的手段来建立一种和平气氛。
