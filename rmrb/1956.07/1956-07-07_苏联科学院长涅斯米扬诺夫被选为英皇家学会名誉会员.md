Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences President Nesmeyanov Elected Honorary Fellow of the British Royal Society
Author:
Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, London, July 3rd (TASS dispatch): The President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Nesmeyanov, has been elected an honorary fellow of the British Royal Society.

### 苏联科学院长涅斯米扬诺夫被选为英皇家学会名誉会员

1956-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院长涅斯米扬诺夫被选为英皇家学会名誉会员
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社伦敦三日讯：苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫已经被选为英国皇家学会的名誉会员。
