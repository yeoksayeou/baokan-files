Title: Cessation of Nuclear Weapon Tests is the First Step Towards Disarmament - Menon States India's Views at the Disarmament Committee
Author:
Date: 1956-07-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 14th: Indian representative to the United Nations, Menon, delivered a speech at the United Nations Disarmament Committee meeting on July 12th, calling for a cessation of nuclear weapon testing. Menon said, "There is no need for further testing. These tests are useless adventures."

India is not a member of the United Nations Disarmament Committee. Menon was granted special permission to attend the Disarmament Committee meeting to present India's views on relevant issues.

Menon said that India unreservedly supports the prohibition of all weapons of mass destruction. Menon also stated that he could not agree with the British proposal to limit nuclear weapon testing, because any limitation still retains the power of destruction.

Menon said that the cessation of nuclear weapon testing is the first step towards disarmament. He also suggested the following steps in world disarmament: 1. Fissionable materials should never again be provided for the manufacture of bombs. 2. The United States and the Soviet Union should be willing to dismantle a certain number of weapons and transfer the fissionable materials for peaceful purposes, as a symbolic step towards actively reducing nuclear armaments. 3. There should be some reductions in military budgets.

Menon hoped that countries possessing nuclear weapons could guarantee to the world that they would not supply such weapons to other countries. He also believed that the current negotiations on disarmament issues must be changed in the following two aspects: 1. Direct negotiations between the United States and the Soviet Union. 2. Reorganize the United Nations Disarmament Subcommittee so that it should "have the greatest possible world representation."

### 停止核子武器试验是走向裁军第一步  梅农在裁军委员会上陈述印度的意见

1956-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

    停止核子武器试验是走向裁军第一步
    梅农在裁军委员会上陈述印度的意见
    据新华社14日电　纽约消息：印度驻联合国代表梅农七月十二日在联合国裁军委员会会议上发表演说，要求停止试验核子武器。梅农说：“没有进行进一步试验的必要了。这些试验是毫无用处的冒险。”
    印度不是联合国裁军委员会的委员。梅农是经特别许可出席裁军委员会会议来陈述印度在有关问题上的主张的。
    梅农说，印度没有任何保留地拥护禁止各种大规模毁灭性的武器。梅农还表示他不能同意英国提出的限制试验核子武器的建议，因为任何限制仍旧保留毁灭的力量。
    梅农说，停止核子武器试验是走向裁军的第一个步骤。他还建议在世界裁军方面采取下列步骤：一、绝不应再提供裂变物资来制造炸弹。二、美国和苏联应当愿意拆卸一定数量的武器并把裂变物资转用于和平目的，作为走向积极性裁减核子军备的象征性步骤。三、在军事预算方面应当有一些削减。
    梅农希望拥有核子武器的国家能够向世界保证，不把这种武器供给其他国家。他还认为目前关于裁军问题的谈判必须在下列两个方面进行改变：一、美苏两国直接谈判，二、重新改组联合国裁军小组委员会，使它应当“有尽可能大的世界代表性”。
