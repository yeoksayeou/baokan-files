Title: Rainy Area Shifts to Northeast and Southwest
Author:
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 4th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a weather forecast at 8:00 PM on the 4th: In the past two days, Gansu, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Shanxi, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Shandong, northern Henan, Hebei, Liaoning, Jilin, and Heilongjiang have all experienced widespread rainfall.

Currently, the main rainy areas have shifted to the Northeast and southern Sichuan, and there is still heavy rain in northern Guizhou. From tomorrow to the day after tomorrow, the weather in Shaanxi, Shanxi, Hebei, and Shandong provinces will gradually improve; in some areas of the aforementioned provinces, there may still be short-term light thunderstorms in the evenings of tomorrow and the day after tomorrow.

### 阴雨区移往东北和西南

1956-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    阴雨区移往东北和西南
    据新华社4日电　中央气象台四日下午八时发布天气预报：最近两天，甘肃、陕西、四川、山西、内蒙古自治区、山东、河南北部、河北、辽宁、吉林、黑龙江等地区都先后普遍下了雨。
    目前主要阴雨区已移往东北和四川南部，贵州北部仍有大雨。明天到后天，陕西、山西、河北、山东各省天气都将逐渐转好；上述各省部分地区，明天后天傍晚仍可能有短时间的小雷阵雨。
