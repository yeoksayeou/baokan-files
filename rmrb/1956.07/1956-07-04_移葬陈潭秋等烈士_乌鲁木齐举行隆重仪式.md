Title: Reburial of Martyrs Chen Tanqiu and Others: Grand Ceremony Held in Urumqi
Author:
Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, July 2nd: A grand reburial ceremony was held at the People's Square in the center of Urumqi this morning to move the remains of martyrs such as Chen Tanqiu, one of the founders of the Chinese Communist Party, Mao Zemin, an outstanding member of the Chinese Communist Party, and Lin Jilu, to the Martyrs' Cemetery for burial.

More than 600 people attended the funeral, including Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zakelov, Secretary-General of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region People's Committee, Major General Zhang Xiqin, Deputy Commander of the Xinjiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Ren Gebai, Mayor of Urumqi, and representatives from various organs and organizations. Comrade Wang Enmao and other leaders personally escorted the coffins to the Martyrs' Cemetery.

The Martyrs' Cemetery is built on the bank of the Urumqi River, backing onto the mountains and facing the water. It is surrounded by lush green willows and poplars, making it a very quiet and solemn place.

### 移葬陈潭秋等烈士  乌鲁木齐举行隆重仪式

1956-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

    移葬陈潭秋等烈士
    乌鲁木齐举行隆重仪式
    据新华社乌鲁木齐2日电　今天上午，在乌鲁木齐市中心人民广场举行了隆重的移葬仪式，将中国共产党创始人之一陈潭秋、中国共产党优秀党员毛泽民、林基路等烈士遗骨移往烈士陵园，准备安葬。
    中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会第一书记王恩茂、新疆维吾尔自治区人民委员会秘书长扎克洛夫、中国人民解放军新疆军区副司令员张希钦少将、乌鲁木齐市市长任戈白以及各机关、团体的代表等六百多人参加了送葬。王恩茂等领导同志亲自扶灵到烈士陵园。
    这座烈士陵园修建在乌鲁木齐河畔，傍山依水，四周一片葱郁的翠柳白杨，十分幽静肃穆。
