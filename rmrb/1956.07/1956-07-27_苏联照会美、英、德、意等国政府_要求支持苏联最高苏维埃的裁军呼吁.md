Title: Soviet Union Sends Notes to the Governments of the United States, Britain, Germany, Italy, and Other Countries Requesting Support for the Disarmament Appeal of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-07-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: According to TASS reports: Soviet Ambassador to Washington, Zarubin, and Ambassador to London, Malik, respectively met with U.S. Acting Secretary of State Herbert Hoover Jr. and British Foreign Secretary Lloyd, handing them the appeal to all parliaments of the world regarding the issue of disarmament, passed by the Joint Session of the Two Chambers of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on July 16th, and asked them to pass it on to the U.S. Congress and the British Parliament.

The Soviet Union also delivered the Supreme Soviet's appeal to the parliaments of the German Democratic Republic, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Greece, and other countries through the same channels.

The appeal points out that taking effective measures to stop the arms race is the sacred responsibility of all parliaments.

Along with the appeal, the letter from Lobanov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Latsis, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, to the Speakers of the parliaments of all countries, regarding the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union's letter to the parliaments of all countries on the issue of disarmament, was also delivered to the parliaments of all countries.

### 苏联照会美、英、德、意等国政府  要求支持苏联最高苏维埃的裁军呼吁

1956-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联照会美、英、德、意等国政府
    要求支持苏联最高苏维埃的裁军呼吁
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联驻华盛顿大使查鲁宾和驻伦敦大使马立克分别会见了美国代理国务卿小赫伯特·胡佛和英国外交大臣劳埃德，把苏联最高苏维埃两院联席会议七月十六日通过的关于裁军问题致各国议会的呼吁书交给他们，请他们转交给美国国会和英国议会。
    苏联还通过同样途径把最高苏维埃的呼吁书交给了德意志民主共和国、德意志联邦共和国、意大利、希腊等国的议会。
    呼吁书指出，采取停止扩充军备的有效措施是各国议会的神圣责任。
    和呼吁书一起交给各国议会的还有苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫和苏联最高苏维埃民族院主席拉齐斯就苏联最高苏维埃关于裁军问题致各国议会书致各国议会议长的信件。
