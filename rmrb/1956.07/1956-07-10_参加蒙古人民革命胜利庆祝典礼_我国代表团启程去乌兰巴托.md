Title: Chinese Delegation Departs for Ulaanbaatar to Attend Celebration of the Victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution
Author:
Date: 1956-07-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th. The People's Republic of China delegation, led by Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by plane this morning for Ulaanbaatar to attend the 35th anniversary celebration of the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

Members of the delegation include: Jiang Ming, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; and He Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia. He Ying is currently in Ulaanbaatar.

Those who saw them off at the airport included: Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; and Ochirbat, Mongolian Ambassador to China, and others.

### 参加蒙古人民革命胜利庆祝典礼  我国代表团启程去乌兰巴托

1956-07-10
第5版()
专栏：

    参加蒙古人民革命胜利庆祝典礼
    我国代表团启程去乌兰巴托
    新华社9日讯　由中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念率领的中华人民共和国代表团，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京前往乌兰巴托，参加蒙古人民革命胜利三十五周年庆祝典礼。
    代表团团员有：对外贸易部副部长江明、中国驻蒙古大使何英。何英现在在乌兰巴托。
    到机场送行的有：国务院秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长李哲人和蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特等。
