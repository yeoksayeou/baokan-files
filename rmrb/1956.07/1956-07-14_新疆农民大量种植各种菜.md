Title: Xinjiang Farmers Massively Plant Various Vegetables
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-14
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, 12th. Farmers of all ethnic groups in the areas along the Lanxin Railway, which is about to be built in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, are actively developing vegetable production. Farmers in Hami, Turpan, Changji and other places are starting to open up more vegetable gardens and sow various vegetables such as Chinese cabbage, radish, garlic and green onions; some areas will also focus on establishing greenhouses and hotbeds to expand the production of leek sprouts, garlic sprouts and tomatoes and other fresh winter vegetables.
    More than a dozen counties from Hami to Usu will newly cultivate thousands of mu of vegetable gardens, and from September this year to April next year, they will supply more than 77 million jin of various vegetables to the Lanxin Railway construction site and cities and industrial and mining areas such as Urumqi.

### 新疆农民大量种植各种菜

1956-07-14
第3版()
专栏：

    新疆农民大量种植各种菜
    新华社乌鲁木齐12日电　在新疆维吾尔自治区境内即将修建的兰新铁路的沿线地区，各族农民积极发展蔬菜生产。哈密、吐鲁番、昌吉等地农民，正在着手增辟菜园和播种白菜、萝卜、蒜、葱等各种菜蔬；有些地区还将重点建立温室、温床，扩大生产韭黄、蒜苗和番茄等冬季鲜菜。
    从哈密到乌苏十多个县，即将新辟数千亩菜园，在今年九月到明年四月，将要向兰新铁路工地和乌鲁木齐等城市、工矿区供应各种蔬菜七千七百多万斤。
