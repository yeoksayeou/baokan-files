Title: Da Punsheng Sends a Telegram to Syrian Imam al-Aichmair, Welcoming Syria's Recognition of China
Author:
Date: 1956-07-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The vice chairman of the China Islamic Association, Nur Muhammad Da Punsheng, sent a telegram on the 7th to Syrian Imam Muhammad al-Aichmair, expressing a warm welcome to the Syrian government's recognition of the People's Republic of China. The telegram stated: "Millions of Chinese Muslims warmly welcome your government's friendly decision to recognize the People's Republic of China. On behalf of all Chinese Muslim brothers, I extend warm congratulations to you and all Syrian Muslim brothers and people. I wish the further development of friendship between the two peoples."

### 达浦生致电叙利亚艾契麦尔阿訇  欢迎叙利亚承认中国

1956-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    达浦生致电叙利亚艾契麦尔阿訇
    欢迎叙利亚承认中国
    新华社11日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会副主任努尔·穆罕默德·达浦生七日致电叙利亚穆罕默德·艾契麦尔阿訇，表示热烈欢迎叙利亚政府承认中华人民共和国。电报说：“中国千万穆斯林热烈欢迎贵国政府承认中华人民共和国的友好决定。兹代表中国全体穆斯林兄弟向你和全体叙利亚穆斯林兄弟和人民致热烈的祝贺。预祝两国人民友好的进一步发展。”
