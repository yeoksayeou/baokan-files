Title: Lebanese Parliament Passes Law to Tax Foreign Companies
Author:
Date: 1956-07-04
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 30th, TASS: The Lebanese Parliament unanimously passed a law deciding to tax all foreign companies that have so far enjoyed preferential treatment in taxation.

　　The "Iraq Petroleum Company," whose oil pipeline passes through Lebanese territory, is also among these companies.

### 黎巴嫩国会通过一法案  决定向外国公司征税

1956-07-04
第6版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩国会通过一法案
    决定向外国公司征税
    新华社1日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特六月三十日讯：黎巴嫩国会一致通过了一项法案，决定向所有到现在为止一直在纳税方面享受优待的外国公司征税。
    “伊拉克石油公司”也在这一类公司之列，这个公司的油管穿过黎巴嫩国境。
