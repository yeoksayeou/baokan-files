Title: Chinese National Leaders Send Congratulations on the National Day of the People's Republic of Poland
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st - Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent congratulations on the 12th anniversary of the National Day of the People's Republic of Poland. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of State of the People's Republic of Poland:

To Comrade Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Poland:

To Comrade Adam Rapacki, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Poland:

On the occasion of the 12th anniversary of the National Day of the People's Republic of Poland, please allow us to express our warm congratulations to you, to the government of the People's Republic of Poland, and to the Polish people, on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people.

The Chinese people have always been concerned about the Polish people's struggle to build a better life and consolidate the people's democratic system. We believe that under the leadership of the Polish United Workers' Party and the government of the People's Republic of Poland in the cause of realizing the new five-year plan and safeguarding European and world peace, the Polish people will achieve new successes. We wish the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Polish peoples to develop and consolidate further!

	Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
	Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
	Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
					July 21, 1956, in Beijing

### 我国家领导人员  电贺波兰人民共和国国庆

1956-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我国家领导人员
    电贺波兰人民共和国国庆
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇、中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来电贺波兰人民共和国国庆十二周年。电文如下：波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席亚历山大·萨瓦茨基同志：波兰人民共和国部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹同志：波兰人民共和国外交部长阿达姆·拉帕茨基同志：
    当波兰人民共和国国庆十二周年的时候，请允许我们代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民向你们，向波兰人民共和国政府和波兰人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民一向关怀波兰人民为建设美好生活和巩固人民民主制度而进行的斗争。我们相信，波兰人民在波兰统一工人党和波兰人民共和国政府的领导下，在实现新的五年计划和保障欧洲和世界和平的事业中，将获得新的成就。祝中波两国人民兄弟般的友谊更加发展和巩固！
        中　华　人　民　共　和　国　主　席　毛泽东
        中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长　刘少奇
        中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长　周恩来
                                            1956年7月21日于北京
