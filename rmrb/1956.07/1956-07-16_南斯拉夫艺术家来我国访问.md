Title: Yugoslav Artists Visit China

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 15th -- According to the Sino-Yugoslav Cultural Cooperation Agreement, a group of twenty-one Yugoslav artists have arrived in Beijing by plane in batches on the 14th and 15th to visit and perform in China. Among the Yugoslav artists visiting China this time are five famous singers from the Yugoslav republics of Macedonia, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Slovenia, Serbia, and Croatia; four ballet dancers from the Belgrade Opera House and the Zagreb Opera House; and the Yugoslav Slovenian Octet, a renowned European ensemble of eight male vocalists.

When the Yugoslav artists arrived in Beijing, Vice Ministers of Culture Xia Yan and Zhang Zhixiang, representatives from the Chinese Musicians Association, and musicians, dancers, and singers from Beijing welcomed them.

This evening, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan hosted a banquet to welcome the Yugoslav artists. After the banquet, a Chinese film will be shown for the Yugoslav artists.

### 南斯拉夫艺术家来我国访问

1956-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫艺术家来我国访问
    据新华社15日讯　根据中南文化合作协议来中国访问演出的南斯拉夫艺术家二十一人，已在十四日和十五日分批乘飞机到达北京。在这次来华的南斯拉夫艺术家中间，有来自南斯拉夫的马其顿、波斯尼亚—黑塞哥维那、斯洛文尼亚、塞尔维亚和克罗地亚五个人民共和国的五位著名歌唱家，有来自南斯拉夫的贝尔格莱德歌剧院和萨格勒布歌剧院的四位芭蕾舞演员，有由八位男声歌唱家组成的、驰誉欧洲的南斯拉夫斯洛文尼亚八重唱团。
    南斯拉夫艺术家们到达北京时，文化部副部长夏衍、张致祥，中国音乐家协会的代表和北京的音乐家、舞蹈家、歌唱家等曾前往欢迎。
    今天晚间，文化部副部长夏衍设宴欢迎南斯拉夫艺术家。宴会以后，为南斯拉夫艺术家们放映中国电影。
