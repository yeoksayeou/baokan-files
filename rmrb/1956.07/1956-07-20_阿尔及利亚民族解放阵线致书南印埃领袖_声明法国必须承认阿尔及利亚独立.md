Title: Algerian National Liberation Front Sends Letter to Leaders of Yugoslavia, India, and Egypt, Declaring France Must Recognize Algerian Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, 19th. The "Action Report" weekly published in Tunis carried the main points of the Algerian National Liberation Front's statement to the leaders of Yugoslavia, India, and Egypt who are meeting in Yugoslavia. The statement pointed out that the preconditions for a ceasefire in Algeria are: France recognizes Algeria as an indivisible country, France unconditionally and unreservedly recognizes Algerian independence, releases all Algerians imprisoned, detained, or exiled for patriotic activities, and France recognizes the Algerian government responsible for negotiating the basis for peace.

The statement said that the goal of the Algerian National Liberation Front is to restore the sovereignty of the Algerian people, to fully and freely exercise their sovereignty, and to ensure independence and the indivisibility of Algerian territory in all matters—including diplomacy and defense.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线致书南印埃领袖  声明法国必须承认阿尔及利亚独立

1956-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线致书南印埃领袖
    声明法国必须承认阿尔及利亚独立
    新华社19日讯　突尼斯消息：在突尼斯出版的“行动报”周刊登载了阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线给正在南斯拉夫开会的南斯拉夫、印度、埃及三国领袖的声明书的要点。这个声明书指出，在阿尔及利亚停火的先决条件是：法国承认阿尔及利亚是一个不可分割的国家，法国无条件地和无保留地承认阿尔及利亚的独立，释放一切由于爱国活动而被监禁、拘留或放逐的阿尔及利亚人，法国承认负责谈判和平基础的阿尔及利亚政府。
    这个声明说，阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的目标在于恢复阿尔及利亚人民的主权，充分地而且自由地行使他们的主权，在一切事务上——包括外交和防御——的独立和阿尔及利亚的领土的不可分割性。
