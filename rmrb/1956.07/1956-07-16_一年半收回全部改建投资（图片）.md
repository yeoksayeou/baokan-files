Recover All Reconstruction Investment in One and a Half Years

The Shenyang Pneumatic Tool Factory, after reconstruction, has recovered all of its reconstruction investment within one and a half years of production. By the end of June, the profits and depreciation fees, etc., turned over to the state by this factory had exceeded 1.4% of the total reconstruction investment. This is a corner of the machining workshop. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Zhao photographed.

### 一年半收回全部改建投资（图片）
于肇
1956-07-16
第2版()
专栏：

    一年半收回全部改建投资
    改建后的沈阳风动工具厂，在投入生产一年半中，已经把改建的投资全部收回。到六月底为止，这个厂上缴给国家的利润和折旧费等已经超过了改建投资总额的1.4％。这是机械加工车间的一角。　新华社记者　于　肇摄
