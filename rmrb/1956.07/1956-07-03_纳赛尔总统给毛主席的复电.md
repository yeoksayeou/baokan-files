Title: President Nasser's Reply to Chairman Mao

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-03
Page: 1

To His Excellency Chairman Mao Tse-tung, President of the People's Republic of China:

    I sincerely thank Your Excellency for your kind telegram, expressing good wishes and noble sentiments to me and the Egyptian people in your personal name and on behalf of the Chinese people. For my part, I most sincerely wish Your Excellency happiness and the Chinese people glory and prosperity.

                Your friend, Gamal Abdel Nasser
                          At the Presidential Residence, Cairo, June 29, 1956

### 纳赛尔总统给毛主席的复电

1956-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔总统给毛主席的复电
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    我衷心地感谢阁下盛情来电，以您个人名义并代表中国人民向我和埃及人民表达良好的愿望和高贵的情意。在我这方面，我万分热诚地祝阁下幸福，祝中国人民光荣、昌盛。
                您的朋友　加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔
                          于开罗共和国大厦　1956年6月29日
