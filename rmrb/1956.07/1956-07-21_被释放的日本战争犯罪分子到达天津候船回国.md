Title: Released Japanese War Criminals Arrive in Tianjin to Await Ships Home

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, July 20th: The second group of 328 released Japanese war criminals arrived in Tianjin today by train from Fushun and Taiyuan respectively.

　　The 296 Japanese war criminals arriving from Fushun sang "People of the World are of One Heart" upon alighting from the train at the station.

　　This group of Japanese war criminals will await ships to return to Japan here.

### 被释放的日本战争犯罪分子到达天津候船回国

1956-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    被释放的日本战争犯罪分子到达天津候船回国
    新华社天津20日电　第二批被释放的三百二十八名日本战争犯罪分子今天分别从抚顺和太原乘火车到达天津。
    从抚顺来的二百九十六名日本战争犯罪分子下车后在车站上高唱“全世界人民心一条”的歌曲。
    这批日本战争犯罪分子将在这里候船回日本。
