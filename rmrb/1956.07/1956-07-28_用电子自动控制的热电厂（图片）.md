Title: Thermoelectric Power Plant with Electronic Automatic Control (Picture)
Author: Xue Yin
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 2

    Thermoelectric Power Plant with Electronic Automatic Control (Picture)
    Jilin Thermoelectric Power Plant, China's first high-temperature and high-pressure thermoelectric power plant with electronic automatic control, is under construction. Once completed, this thermoelectric power plant will be one of China's largest power projects for both heating and power generation. This is the first turbine generator put into production.
                                       Xinhua News Agency Reporter Xue Yin

### 用电子自动控制的热电厂（图片）
雪印
1956-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

    用电子自动控制的热电厂（图片）
    我国第一座用电子自动控制的高温高压热电厂——吉林热电厂正在建设中。这座热电厂建成后，将是我国最大的供热、发电两用的动力工程之一。这是投入生产的第一台透平发电机。　
                                       新华社记者　雪印摄
