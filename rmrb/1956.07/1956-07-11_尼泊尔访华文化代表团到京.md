Title: Nepalese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 10th—The 21-member Nepalese cultural delegation to China, led by the Nepalese Kingdom's Minister of Education, Health, and Local Autonomy, Mr. Balchandra Sharma, was warmly welcomed by Chinese government and cultural figures at Beijing Airport today.

　　The silver "Beijing" special plane carrying the delegation landed at 3:45 PM at the airport, which was adorned with Nepalese and Chinese flags and colorful banners. As head of the delegation Sharma and the delegation members appeared at the cabin door, young literary and art workers and students gathered at the airport waved flowers and applauded enthusiastically.

　　Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers Ding Xilin, Zheng Zhenduo, and Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Higher Education Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of Education Lin Handa, Vice Minister of Health Fu Lianzhang, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, among others, welcomed the delegation in front of the plane. Information Officer Guha of the Indian Embassy in China also came to the airport to welcome them. Head of the delegation Sharma and the members accepted the flowers presented by Beijing youth and were introduced to the heads of various people's organizations and figures from the capital's cultural and religious circles who came to welcome them.

　　On behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Shen Yanbing delivered a speech warmly welcoming the Nepalese cultural delegation to China. He said, "China and Nepal are neighbors with contiguous borders, and the friendly ties between the two countries can be traced back 1,500 years. On August 1, 1955, China and Nepal established normal diplomatic relations, and since then, the friendly relations between the two countries have developed on a new basis."

　　Shen Yanbing said: Today, we are very honored to welcome the Nepalese cultural delegation to China in Beijing. We believe that mutual visits between the two countries will greatly enhance the understanding and traditional friendship between the peoples of the two countries.

　　In his speech, head of the delegation Sharma recalled the long-standing friendly relations between China and Nepal, saying that the friendly relations between our two countries have now been established on a new basis. On our first day in China, we are convinced that the ties and friendship between our two countries will be consolidated and developed.

　　As the delegation members passed through the welcoming crowd to board the cars, people threw flowers and petals at them and competed to offer bouquets of flowers to these guests from afar.

　　The Nepalese cultural delegation to China is visiting China at the invitation of the Ministry of Culture. The delegation consists of Nepalese writers, poets, university professors, social welfare workers, journalists, and folk artists, and will visit various parts of China for a month. (Attached picture)

### 尼泊尔访华文化代表团到京

1956-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔访华文化代表团到京
    新华社10日讯　尼泊尔王国教育、卫生、地方自治政府大臣巴·夏尔马率领的尼泊尔访华文化代表团一行二十一人，今天在北京机场受到中国政府和文化界人士的热烈欢迎。
    代表团乘坐的银色“北京”号专机在下午三时四十五分降落在飘扬着尼泊尔、中国国旗和彩色旗帜的飞机场上。当夏尔马团长和代表团团员出现在飞机舱门的时候，聚集在机场的青年文艺工作者和学生们挥舞着鲜花，向他们热烈鼓掌。
    文化部部长沈雁冰，副部长丁西林、郑振铎、夏衍，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，教育部副部长林汉达，卫生部副部长傅连暲，北京市副市长吴晗等都在飞机前面欢迎代表团。印度驻华大使馆新闻专员古哈也到机场欢迎。夏尔马团长和团员们接受了北京青年献给他们的鲜花，并且被介绍同前来欢迎的各人民团体负责人，首都文化界、宗教界人士见面。
    沈雁冰代表中华人民共和国文化部致词热烈欢迎尼泊尔访华文化代表团，他说：“中国、尼泊尔两国国境毗连，两国的友好联系可以追溯到一千五百年以前。1955年8月1日中、尼两国建立了正常的外交关系，从此两国的友好关系又在新的基础上获得发展。
    沈雁冰说：今天，我们非常荣幸地在北京欢迎尼泊尔访华文化代表团。我们相信，两国之间的相互访问，将会大大地增进两国人民之间的了解和传统友谊。
    夏尔马团长在他的讲话中回溯了中国和尼泊尔之间悠久的友好关系，他说，我们两国之间的友好关系现在已经建立在新的基础上。在我们来到中国的头一天，我们就深信我们两国之间的联系和友谊将会巩固和发展起来。
    当代表团人员通过欢迎的人群前往登上汽车的时候，人们向他们抛掷鲜花和花瓣，并且争着把一束束鲜花献给这些远道而来的客人。
    尼泊尔访华文化代表团是应文化部的邀请前来中国进行友好访问的。代表团由尼泊尔的作家、诗人、大学教授、社会福利工作者、新闻工作者和民间艺术家组成，将在中国各地访问一个月。（附图片）
