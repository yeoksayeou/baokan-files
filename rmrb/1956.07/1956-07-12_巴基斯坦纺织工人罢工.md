Title: Pakistani Textile Workers Strike
Author:
Date: 1956-07-12
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 11th—According to the "Pakistan Times," more than seven thousand workers at the "Colony Textile Mills" in Multan have been on strike since July 7th.
    The workers demanded that six months' bonus be paid before July 12th, that wages be paid in full during power outages and shutdowns, and that wages be doubled for overtime work.
    The factory owners agreed to pay them a five-month bonus. The workers' representatives rejected this offer.

### 巴基斯坦纺织工人罢工

1956-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦纺织工人罢工
    新华社喀喇蚩11日电　据“巴基斯坦时报”消息，穆尔坦“殖民地纺织工厂”的七千多工人从七月七日起举行了罢工。
    工人们要求在七月十二日以前发给六个月的奖金，在电力不足而停工的时候工资照付，加班的时候工资加倍。
    工厂的资方同意付给他们五个月的奖金。工人们的代表拒绝了这个建议。
