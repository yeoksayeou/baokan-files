Title: Soviet-Iranian Leaders Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th dispatch: According to TASS, after visiting Stalingrad, Tashkent, Ashkhabad, the Black Sea coast, Zaporozhye, Kiev and Leningrad in the Soviet Union, the Shah of Iran, Mohammad Reza Pahlavi, and Queen Soraya, returned to Moscow on the 10th.
    On the same day, the Shah of Iran held talks with the leaders of the Soviet government in the Kremlin.

### 苏伊两国领导人会谈

1956-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏伊两国领导人会谈
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：伊朗国王穆罕默德·礼萨·巴列维和王后莎拉雅在苏联的斯大林格勒、塔什干、阿什哈巴德、黑海海滨、萨坡罗什、基辅和列宁格勒等地参观访问后，已经在十日回到了莫斯科。
    当天，伊朗国王同苏联政府领导人在克里姆林宫举行了会谈。
