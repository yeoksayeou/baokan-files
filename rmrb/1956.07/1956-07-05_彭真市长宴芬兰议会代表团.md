Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet for Finnish Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-07-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 4th—Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, hosted a banquet at the Beijing Hotel this evening to entertain the Finnish Parliamentary Delegation.

At 7:40 PM, the guests entered the banquet hall, which was filled with a friendly and pleasant atmosphere. Mayor Peng Zhen accompanied the head of the delegation, Mr. Väinö Johannes Sukselainen, to meet people from various circles in the capital who were attending the banquet. The hosts repeatedly proposed toasts to the guests.

Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, had a cordial conversation with Delegation Head Sukselainen. Mr. Sukselainen expressed his hope that the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress would organize a delegation to visit Finland. Chairman Liu Shaoqi said: We are very happy to accept and thank you for your invitation. The head of the Finnish parliamentary delegation happily raised his glass and said, "See you in Finland!"

At the banquet, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai said to the head of the Finnish Parliamentary Delegation: It's a pity that you will be leaving in two days. I really hope you can stay in China a little longer. Mr. Sukselainen said: I am already very happy to have stayed in China for such a long time. Premier Zhou Enlai hoped that the Finnish friends would visit China again in the future. As they concluded their conversation, Premier Zhou Enlai raised his glass and said "Friendship" in Finnish, and then toasted with the guest.

The head and members of the Finnish Parliamentary Delegation had pleasant conversations with Li Jishen, Shen Junru, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, at the banquet. Vice-Mayors of Beijing also toasted with the distinguished guests.

Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Sundström, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

Also attending today's banquet were members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, heads of central state organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations, as well as people from the capital's political and legal, cultural, scientific, educational, artistic, journalistic, and religious circles.

### 彭真市长宴芬兰议会代表团

1956-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真市长宴芬兰议会代表团
    新华社4日讯　今天晚上，北京市市长彭真在北京饭店举行酒会招待芬兰议会代表团。
    七点四十分，客人们步入酒会大厅，大厅里呈现出一片友好愉快的气氛。彭真市长陪着代表团团长维埃诺·约翰奈斯·苏克舍拉宁到大厅各处和参加酒会的首都各界人士见面。主人们不断同客人干杯。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇和苏克舍拉宁团长亲切地交谈。苏克舍拉宁团长希望全国人民代表大会常务委员会组织代表团到芬兰去访问，刘少奇委员长说：我们很高兴地接受和感谢你们的邀请。这位芬兰的议会代表团团长高兴地举起酒杯说“在芬兰再见！”
    在酒会上，国务院总理周恩来对芬兰议会代表团团长说：可惜得很，你们再过两天就要走了，很希望你们能在中国多留一些时候。苏克舍拉宁团长说：在中国留这样长的时间，我已经很高兴了。周恩来总理希望芬兰的朋友们以后再来访问中国。在他们结束谈话的时候，周恩来总理举着酒杯用芬兰话说了声“友谊”，接着和这位客人干了杯。
    芬兰议会访华代表团团长和团员们，在酒会上分别和全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、陈叔通，国务院副总理陈毅欢谈，北京市副市长们也都和贵宾们干了杯。
    应邀参加这个酒会的还有芬兰驻中国大使孙士敦和使馆人员。
    参加今天酒会的还有，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，中央各国家机关、各民主党派、人民团体的负责人，以及首都政法、文化科学教育、艺术、新闻、宗教界人士。
