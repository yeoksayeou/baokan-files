Title: Ceylon Governor-General Approves Sinhala as the Official Language

Author:

Date: 1956-07-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 10th – According to official announcements, the Governor-General of Ceylon, Sir Oliver Goonetilleke, approved on July 7th a bill that stipulates Sinhala as the sole official language of Ceylon.

### 锡兰总督批准以僧伽罗语为国语

1956-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰总督批准以僧伽罗语为国语
    新华社10日讯　科伦坡消息：据官方宣布，锡兰总督古涅狄莱克七月七日批准了一项规定僧伽罗语为锡兰唯一的官方语言的法案。
