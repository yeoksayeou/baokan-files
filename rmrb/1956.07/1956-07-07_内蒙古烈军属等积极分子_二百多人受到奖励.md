Title: Inner Mongolia's Dependents of Martyrs and Military Personnel and Other Activists: More Than Two Hundred People Receive Awards
Author:
Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, July 5th: 252 dependents of martyrs, dependents of military personnel, revolutionary disabled soldiers, and demobilized construction soldiers were rewarded at the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region's conference of socialist construction activists of dependents of martyrs, dependents of military personnel, revolutionary disabled soldiers, and demobilized construction soldiers, which closed on the 4th.

The Liberation Vegetable Garden Agricultural Production Cooperative, led by outstanding revolutionary disabled soldier Qu Zhanquan, has been named an advanced production and high-yield model cooperative in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Baotou City six times in the past four years. The cabbage produced by this cooperative set a record of 9,400 jin per mu. Last year, the cooperative's eighty-four member households had an average income of more than 760 yuan per household, an increase of more than 50% compared to 1953. Suren Bao Lian, a Daur demobilized soldier who received an award, led Daur herdsmen to establish a pastoral production cooperative of thirty-three households on the Hulunbuir Grassland in February this year. This spring, the survival rate during lambing reached 98%.

This conference selected fourteen representatives from the Mongolian, Hui, and Han ethnic groups to attend the National Conference of Socialist Construction Activists of Dependents of Martyrs, Dependents of Military Personnel, Revolutionary Disabled Soldiers, and Demobilized Construction Soldiers.

### 内蒙古烈军属等积极分子  二百多人受到奖励

1956-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    内蒙古烈军属等积极分子
    二百多人受到奖励
    据新华社呼和浩特5日电　252名烈属、军属、革命残废军人和复员建设军人，在四日闭幕的内蒙古自治区烈属、军属、革命残废军人、复员建设军人社会主义建设积极分子大会上受到了奖励。
    受奖的优秀革命残废军人屈占泉领导的解放菜园农业生产合作社，四年来连续六次被评为内蒙古自治区和包头市的增产、丰产模范社。这个社生产的白菜，创造了每亩生产九千四百斤的纪录。全社八十四户社员，去年每户平均收入七百六十多元，比1953年增加50％以上。受奖的达呼尔族复员军人苏保连，今年二月间带动达呼尔牧民在呼伦贝尔草原上建立了一个三十三户的牧业生产合作社。今春全社接羔时，成活率达到了98％。
    这次大会选出十四名蒙古、回、汉族代表，准备出席全国烈属、军属、革命残废军人和复员建设军人社会主义建设积极分子大会。
