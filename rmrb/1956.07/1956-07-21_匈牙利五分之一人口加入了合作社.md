Title: One-Fifth of Hungary's Population Joins Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1956-07-21
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 20th: A Budapest dispatch states that nearly two million people in Hungary have now joined various cooperatives, with one out of every five people in the country being a member of a cooperative.
    Hungary currently has 4,800 agricultural production cooperatives and production cooperative groups. The cultivated land area owned by production cooperatives accounts for 19% of the country's total cultivated land area.
    Hungary's handicraft cooperatives now number nearly 1,500, and their products account for 7.2% of the total industrial products.
    The turnover of consumer cooperatives accounts for 28% of the country's total retail commodity turnover, and there are currently 1,200 such cooperatives.

### 匈牙利五分之一人口加入了合作社

1956-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利五分之一人口加入了合作社
    新华社布拉格20日电　布达佩斯消息：匈牙利现在已经将近有二百万人参加了各种合作社，全国每五个人当中就有一个人是合作社员。
    匈牙利目前有四千八百个农业生产合作社和生产合作组。生产合作社所拥有的耕地面积占全国耕地面积的19％。
    匈牙利的工艺合作社现在将近有一千五百个，它们生产的产品占整个工业品的7.2％。
    消费合作社的商品流转额占全国零售商品流转总额的28％，这种合作社目前有一千二百个。
