Title: Athletes Need White Insoles
Author: Li Zhicheng
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 2

Column: Reader's Opinion

    Athletes Need White Insoles
    Athletes today generally wear white socks, but the sponge soles of the sports shoes sold in the market are all black. After exercising once in these shoes, white socks become black socks. I am very curious, why don't shoe factories change the sponge soles of sports shoes to white?
                    (Li Zhicheng)

### 运动员需要白鞋垫
李志诚
1956-07-02
第2版()
专栏：读者意见

    运动员需要白鞋垫
    现在的运动员一般都穿白色的袜子，但在市场上卖的运动胶鞋的海棉底，都是黑色的。穿这种鞋运动一次，白袜套就变成黑袜套了。我很奇怪，为什么制鞋工厂不把运动鞋的海棉底改为白色的呢？
                    （李志诚）
