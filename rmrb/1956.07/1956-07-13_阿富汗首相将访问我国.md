Title: Afghan Prime Minister to Visit China

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. A communique issued by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China today states that Afghan Prime Minister Mohammed Daoud has accepted the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai and will visit China next year.

The communique was announced simultaneously in the Afghan capital, Kabul, this afternoon.

The full text of the communique is as follows:

Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China has invited Afghan Prime Minister Sardar Mohammed Daoud to visit China as a guest of the Chinese government.

The Afghan Prime Minister gratefully accepted the invitation, which will help strengthen the friendly relations between Afghanistan and China.

It is understood that this visit will take place next year.

### 阿富汗首相将访问我国

1956-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗首相将访问我国
    新华社12日讯　中华人民共和国外交部今天发表的一项公报说，阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德已经接受周恩来总理的邀请，将在明年到中国访问。
    这项公报今天下午在阿富汗首都喀布尔同时宣布。
    公报全文如下：
    中华人民共和国周恩来总理邀请阿富汗首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德作为中国政府的贵宾访问中国。
    阿富汗首相感谢地接受了这一邀请，这一邀请将有助于加强阿富汗和中国之间的友好关系。
    据悉这一访问将在明年进行。
