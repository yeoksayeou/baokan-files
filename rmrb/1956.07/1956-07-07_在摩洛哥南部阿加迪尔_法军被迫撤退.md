Title: French Troops Forced to Withdraw from Agadir, Southern Morocco
Author:
Date: 1956-07-07
Page: 6

    French Troops Forced to Withdraw from Agadir, Southern Morocco
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th dispatch from Rabat: The French authorities have been forced to agree to withdraw troops from Agadir in southern Morocco, and Agadir will be garrisoned by Moroccan troops. Prince Hassan, Chief of Staff of the Moroccan Army, announced France's decision yesterday in Agadir. After French troops recently entered Agadir, residents of the city held strikes and market boycotts in protest.

### 在摩洛哥南部阿加迪尔  法军被迫撤退

1956-07-07
第6版()
专栏：

    在摩洛哥南部阿加迪尔
    法军被迫撤退
    新华社6日讯　拉巴特消息：法国当局被迫同意从摩洛哥南部的阿加迪尔撤出军队，阿加迪尔将由摩洛哥军队驻防。摩洛哥军队参谋长哈萨尼王子昨天在阿加迪尔宣布了法国的这个决定。在法军最近开入阿加迪尔后，这个城市居民就举行罢工和罢市表示抗议。
