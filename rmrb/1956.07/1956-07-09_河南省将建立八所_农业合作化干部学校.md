Title: Henan Province to Establish Eight Agricultural Cooperativization Cadre Schools
Author: Liu Dehou, Quan Qingbai
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 3

Henan Province to Establish Eight Agricultural Cooperativization Cadre Schools

(Our report) Henan Province has decided to convert one junior high school in each of the eight special districts of Kaifeng, Shangqiu, Anyang, Xinxiang, Xuchang, Luoyang, Nanyang, and Xinyang into an agricultural cooperativization cadre school. The agricultural cooperativization cadre schools will have four specializations: management and administration, agriculture, animal husbandry, and financial accounting. The training targets are the directors, chief accountants, and agricultural and animal husbandry technical cadres of advanced cooperatives. These cadres will receive relatively systematic professional training for a certain period of time, four months (for cooperative directors and chief accountants) and half a year (for agricultural and animal husbandry technical cadres). The purpose of the training is to enable these cadres to initially be competent in the work they undertake. After completing this task, these schools will be transformed into one- or two-year agricultural cooperativization cadre schools to continue training leaders and staff of various cooperatives. According to Henan Province's plan, from the second half of 1956 to 1960, it can train 150,000 cadres of various types for advanced agricultural cooperatives.
(Liu Dehou, Quan Qingbai)

### 河南省将建立八所  农业合作化干部学校
刘德厚  权清柏
1956-07-09
第3版()
专栏：

    河南省将建立八所 
    农业合作化干部学校
    本报讯　河南省决定在开封、商丘、安阳、新乡、许昌、洛阳、南阳、信阳八个专区，把初级中学各改一所为农业合作化干部学校。农业合作化干部学校分设经营管理、农业、畜牧业、财务会计等四个专业；训练对象为高级社的主任、总会计和农业、畜牧业技术干部。这些干部将在一定时期内分别受到四个月（社主任、总会计）和半年（农业、畜牧业技术干部）的较系统的业务训练。训练的目的是使这些干部能够初步地胜任他们所担负的工作。这些学校在完成这个任务以后，就要改变成一年或二年制的农业合作化干部学校，继续培训各社的领导人员和工作人员。按照河南省的计划，从1956年下半年至1960年可以训练高级农业合作社各类干部十五万人。
              （刘德厚、权清柏）
