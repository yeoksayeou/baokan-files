Title: Polish Industrial Output Ranks Fifth in Europe
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 21st: Poland's total industrial output has now risen to fifth place in Europe. Calculated per capita, the amount of industrial products available to each Pole today exceeds that of Italy and is on par with France.

During the Six-Year Plan (1949-1955), Poland completed or started construction on a total of 1,250 industrial enterprises. These include iron and steel complexes, chemical complexes, shipyards, light and heavy vehicle factories, coal mining enterprises, machinery manufacturing plants, power plants, cement plants, and aluminum plants.

Currently, Poland produces nearly 100 million tons of coal annually, ranking fifth in the world in coal production (after the United States, the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, and West Germany). Last year, Poland's automotive industry could produce one heavy-duty truck every twelve minutes and one motorcycle every five minutes. Its steel industry's annual output has increased to nearly 4.6 million tons. The shipbuilding industry ranks eighth in the world in terms of capacity. Ship displacement ranks twelfth in the world. Poland's power industry generates more than 13.1 billion kilowatt-hours of electricity annually. As a result, nearly 40% of rural areas in Poland now have electric lights.

### 波工业总产量跃居欧洲第五位

1956-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    波工业总产量跃居欧洲第五位
    新华社华沙21日电　波兰的工业总产量现在已经跃居欧洲第五位。如果按人口平均计算，今天每个波兰人分到的工业产品量已经超过意大利而同法国并驾齐驱。
    在六年计划期间（1949—1955年），波兰已经建成的和已经开工而正在建设的工业企业共有一千二百五十项。其中，有钢铁联合企业、化学联合企业、造船厂、轻重汽车制造厂、采煤企业、机械制造、发电厂、水泥厂、制铝厂等。
    现在，波兰每年能出产将近一亿吨煤，煤产量占世界第五位（仅次于美、苏、英、西德之后）。去年，波兰的汽车工业每十二分钟就能够制造一辆载重汽车，每五分钟制造一辆摩托车。它的钢铁工业的年产量已经将近增加到四百六十万吨。造船工业按容量来说已经占世界第八位。轮船排水量占世界第十二位。波兰的电力工业每年发电量已达131亿多瓩。因此，波兰农村中已有近40％的地区使用电灯。
