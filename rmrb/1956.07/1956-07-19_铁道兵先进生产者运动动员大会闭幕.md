Title: Railway Engineering Corps Concluding Session of Mobilization Conference for Advanced Producers Movement

Author:
Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, July 18th - The Chinese People's Liberation Army Railway Engineering Corps' mobilization conference for the Advanced Producers Movement concluded on the 17th. The conference held enthusiastic discussions on the implementation measures for carrying out the Advanced Producers Movement, the handling of inventions, technological improvements, and rationalization proposals, and safety in production. It also criticized ideological obstacles to carrying out the Advanced Producers Movement. The conference decided to promote 176 advanced experiences and technologies for railway construction in the second half of this year.

    At the closing session, representatives from eight units, including the Yang Lian Di Company, proposed specific targets for safe production, ensuring quality, and cultivating technical experts, guaranteeing that the quality of all projects reaches the "good" standard, and striving for 50% of them to reach the "excellent" standard.

### 铁道兵先进生产者运动动员大会闭幕

1956-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    铁道兵先进生产者运动动员大会闭幕
    据新华社福州18日电　中国人民解放军铁道兵先进生产者运动动员大会已经在十七日闭幕。大会热烈讨论了关于开展先进生产者运动实施办法，关于发明、技术改进、合理化建议的处理办法和安全生产等问题，并且批判了开展先进生产者运动中的思想障碍。大会确定在今年下半年内推行176项修建铁路的先进经验和先进技术。
    在闭幕会上，杨连第连等八个单位的代表，提出了安全生产、保证质量、培养技术能手等的具体指标，保证全部工程质量达到“良好”指标，并且争取其中50％达到“优良”指标。
