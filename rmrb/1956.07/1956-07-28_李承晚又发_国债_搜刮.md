Title: Syngman Rhee Again Issues "National Bonds" to Plunder
Author:
Date: 1956-07-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th - According to the Korean Central News Agency: Seoul newspapers report that the Syngman Rhee government has decided to extract a total of 10.7 billion hwan from the people of South Korea under the guise of "Industrial Reconstruction National Bonds" during the period from July to December this year.

　　Hapdong News Agency said that, up to now, the total amount of money the Syngman Rhee clique has plundered from the people in the name of national debt has reached 53 billion hwan.

### 李承晚又发“国债”搜刮

1956-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    李承晚又发“国债”搜刮
    据新华社平壤26日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：汉城报纸报道，李承晚政府决定在从今年七月到十二月这个期间以“产业复兴国债”的名义，再从南朝鲜人民身上榨取总数达一百零七亿圜的巨款。
    合同通讯社说，到目前为止，李承晚集团借国债的名义从人民身上搜刮的款项总数已经达到五百三十亿圜。
