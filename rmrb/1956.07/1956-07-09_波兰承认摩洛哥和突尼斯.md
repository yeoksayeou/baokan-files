Title: Poland Recognizes Morocco and Tunisia
Author:
Date: 1956-07-09
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 8th. Warsaw newspapers reported on the 7th that the Polish government sent notes to Morocco and Tunisia, stating that it legally recognizes Morocco and Tunisia and is ready to establish diplomatic relations with these two countries.
    The Polish government's note was delivered by the Polish Ambassador to France, Gajewski, to the representatives of Morocco and Tunisia in France.

### 波兰承认摩洛哥和突尼斯

1956-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰承认摩洛哥和突尼斯
    新华社华沙8日电　华沙报纸七日报道，波兰政府照会摩洛哥和突尼斯，表示在法律上承认摩洛哥和突尼斯，并且准备同这两个国家建立外交关系。
    波兰政府的这项照会是由波兰驻法国大使加耶夫斯基交给摩洛哥和突尼斯驻法国的代表的。
