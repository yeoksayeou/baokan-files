Title: Playwright Cao Yu Joins the Party
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, July 21st. The famous playwright Cao Yu recently joined the Chinese Communist Party in Beijing.
    Cao Yu, 46, is a deputy to the National People's Congress, and currently serves as the director of the Beijing People's Art Theater and the vice president of the Central Academy of Drama. Cao Yu has been engaged in dramatic art for more than twenty years. His excellent plays include "Thunderstorm", "Sunrise", "Peking Man" and "Bright Skies".

### 剧作家曹禺入党

1956-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    剧作家曹禺入党
    新华社21日讯　著名剧作家曹禺最近在北京加入了中国共产党。
    四十六岁的曹禺，是全国人民代表大会代表，现任北京人民艺术剧院院长和中央戏剧学院副院长。曹禺从事话剧艺术事业已有二十多年。他所写的优秀的剧本有“雷雨”、“日出”、“北京人”和“明朗的天”等。
