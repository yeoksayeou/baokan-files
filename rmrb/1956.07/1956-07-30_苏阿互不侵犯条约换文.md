Title: Exchange of Notes on the Soviet-Afghan Treaty of Non-Aggression
Author:
Date: 1956-07-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 29th, Kabul Dispatch: Kabul Radio reported on July 28th that the Soviet Union and Afghanistan have formally exchanged the texts of the Soviet-Afghan Treaty of Non-Aggression.

The treaty has been approved by the King of the Kingdom of Afghanistan and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, respectively, after being passed by the Afghan Parliament and the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

### 苏阿互不侵犯条约换文

1956-07-30
第5版()
专栏：

    苏阿互不侵犯条约换文
    据新华社29日讯　喀布尔消息：喀布尔电台七月二十八日报道，苏联和阿富汗已经正式交换了苏联和阿富汗互不侵犯条约的文本。
    这个条约在阿富汗国会和苏联最高苏维埃通过以后，已经分别由阿富汗王国国王和苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席批准。
