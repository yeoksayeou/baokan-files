Title: East German Military and Civilians Rescue Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1956-07-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 25th: The German News Agency reports that continuous heavy rains have caused flooding in some areas of East Germany. The disaster is particularly severe in the Eisenach area, where many roads, bridges, and cowsheds have been destroyed by the floodwaters, with estimated losses of about eight million marks.

The People's Police of East Germany, the National People's Army, and the Soviet troops stationed in Germany are working with local residents to fight the rising floodwaters and are stepping up repairs to the damaged sluices and dikes. At the same time, residents from various places are providing large quantities of supplies to support the disaster-stricken areas.

### 民主德国军民抢救水灾

1956-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国军民抢救水灾
    新华社柏林25日电　据德意志通讯社报道：连绵大雨已经在民主德国的一些地区引起水灾。埃森纳赫一带灾情特别严重，许多道路、桥梁和牛厩被大水冲坏，估计损失大约八百万马克。
    民主德国的人民警察、国家人民军、苏军驻德部队正和当地居民一起同正在上涨的洪水作斗争，加紧修复被冲毁的水闸和堤坝。同时，各地居民正以大批物资来支援受灾地区。
