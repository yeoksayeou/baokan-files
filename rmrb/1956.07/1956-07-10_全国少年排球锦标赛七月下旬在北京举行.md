Title: National Junior Volleyball Championships to be Held in Beijing in Late July
Author:
Date: 1956-07-10
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency Report:

    The 1956 National Major City Junior Volleyball Championships are scheduled to be held in Beijing from July 20th to 30th.

    This is the first national junior volleyball competition held in our country. Twenty-eight male and female volleyball teams from fourteen units will participate in the competition, including four junior male and female volleyball teams from Taishan County, Guangdong Province, and Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture, which are known for their extensive volleyball activities. The other twenty-four junior male and female volleyball teams were selected from twelve cities, including Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Guangzhou, Harbin, Chongqing, and Xi'an. The male and female athletes participating in this championship are all between the ages of fourteen and seventeen.

### 全国少年排球锦标赛七月下旬在北京举行

1956-07-10
第7版()
专栏：

    全国少年排球锦标赛七月下旬在北京举行
    据新华社讯　1956年全国大城市少年排球锦标赛，定于七月二十日到三十日在北京举行。
    这是我国第一次举行的全国性的少年排球比赛。参加比赛的将有十四个单位的二十八个男、女排球队，其中包括来自以广泛开展排球运动闻名的广东省台山县和延边朝鲜族自治州的四个少年男、女排球队。其他的二十四个少年男、女排球队，是由北京、上海、天津、广州、哈尔滨、重庆、西安等十二个城市中选拔出来的。参加这次锦标赛的男、女运动员的年龄都在十四岁到十七周岁之间。
