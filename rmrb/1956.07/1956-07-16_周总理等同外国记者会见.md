Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Foreign Journalists

Author:

Date: 1956-07-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended a reception held by the All-China Journalists Association this afternoon. The reception was held to welcome journalists from eleven countries, including India, Pakistan, Japan, Iran, Syria, Brazil, Chile, Mexico, Peru, Bolivia, and Uruguay, who visited China after attending the Helsinki International Journalists' Meeting.

　　At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai spoke with journalists from Asia, Africa, and Latin America, talking about the friendship between the Chinese people and the people of these countries, and answering questions raised by the journalists.

　　Vice Premier Chen Yi had a long conversation with journalists from Latin American countries, discussing the development of cultural exchanges and trade relations between China and Latin American countries.

　　At the reception, Premier Zhou also took photos with journalists from various countries at their request and inscribed messages for many of them. In his inscriptions, Premier Zhou Enlai expressed the Chinese people's desire to be friendly with the people of all countries.

　　The reception lasted for two and a half hours. At the reception, Deng Tuo, Wu Lengxi, Mei Yi, and Wang Yunsheng, heads of the All-China Journalists Association, Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Chen Kehan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, and the head of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, as well as well-known figures in the capital's journalistic circles, gathered and talked happily with journalists from various countries.

### 周总理等同外国记者会见

1956-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理等同外国记者会见
    新华社15日讯　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理，今天下午出席了中国新闻工作者联谊会举行的酒会。这个酒会是为欢迎参加赫尔辛基国际新闻工作者会见后访华的印度、巴基斯坦、日本、伊朗、叙利亚、巴西、智利、墨西哥、秘鲁、玻利维亚和乌拉圭等十一个国家的记者举行的。
    周恩来总理在酒会上分别同来自亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲各国的记者谈了话，他谈到了中国人民同这些国家人民的友谊，并且回答了各国记者们提出的问题。
    陈毅副总理同来自拉丁美洲各国的记者们作了长时间的谈话，他们谈到了中国同拉丁美洲各国间发展文化交流和贸易关系的问题。
    在酒会上，周总理还应各国记者的请求，分别同他们在一起照了相，并给许多记者题了字。周恩来总理在题字中表达了中国人民愿意同各国人民友好的愿望。
    酒会共进行了两个半小时。在酒会上，中国新闻工作者联谊会的负责人邓拓、吴冷西、梅益、王芸生，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，文化部副部长陈克寒和外交部新闻司负责人以及首都新闻界的著名人士，都同各国记者们欢聚交谈。
