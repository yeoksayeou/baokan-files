Title: Streetlight Should Be Installed Here
Author: Yang Shulan
Date: 1956-07-23
Page: 4
Column: People's Voice

　　Streetlight Should Be Installed Here
　　The area near Heng Hutong in Caochang Upper Sixth Alley, Qianmen District, Beijing, is very dark at night, making it inconvenient for pedestrians to walk. It is even more difficult to pass through here on rainy days. I hope the relevant departments will install a streetlight here.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Yang Shulan

### 这里应该安路灯
杨淑兰
1956-07-23
第4版()
专栏：群众呼声

    这里应该安路灯
    北京市前门区草厂上六条胡同靠近横胡同附近的地方，晚上很黑，行人走路很不方便，遇上雨天通过这里更加困难。希望有关部门在这里安装一盏路灯。　                     杨淑兰
