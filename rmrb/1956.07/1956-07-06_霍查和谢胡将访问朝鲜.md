Title: Hoxha and Shehu to Visit North Korea
Author:
Date: 1956-07-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 5th. According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency on July 4th, Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, and Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, have accepted an invitation to visit North Korea. The date of the visit will be determined later.

The invitation was extended by Premier Kim Il-sung on behalf of the North Korean government during his visit to Albania.

### 霍查和谢胡将访问朝鲜

1956-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    霍查和谢胡将访问朝鲜
    新华社布拉格5日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社七月四日报道，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查和部长会议主席谢胡，已经接受了访问朝鲜的邀请。访问的日期将在以后确定。
    这项邀请是金日成首相在访问阿尔巴尼亚期间代表朝鲜政府提出的。
