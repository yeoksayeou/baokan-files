Title: Hungarian Folk Band Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 1st. The Hungarian folk band, led by Yaroka Sandor and Rusznyak Maria, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

This band is visiting China for performances as part of the 1956 execution plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. It is one of the four famous Hungarian folk bands, composed of nine people including wind instrumentalists, violinists, and singers.

This evening, Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Minister of Culture, hosted a banquet to welcome the Hungarian folk band.

### 匈民间乐队到达北京

1956-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    匈民间乐队到达北京
    据新华社1日讯　由亚若卡·山道尔和鲁斯尼亚克·玛丽率领的匈牙利民间乐队一行十人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    这个乐队是根据中匈文化合作协定1956年执行计划来中国访问演出的，它是匈牙利的四大著名民间乐队之一，由管乐家、提琴家和歌唱家等九人组成。
    今天晚间，文化部副部长郑振铎设宴欢迎匈牙利民间乐队。
