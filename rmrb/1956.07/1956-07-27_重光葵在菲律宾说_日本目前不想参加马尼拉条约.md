Title: Shigemitsu葵 Says in the Philippines that Japan Currently Does Not Want to Join the Manila Treaty

Author:

Date: 1956-07-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 26th—Foreign Minister Shigemitsu葵 of Japan said here today that Japan does not intend to join the Manila Treaty Organization at present.

Shigemitsu was asked about the possibility of Japan joining the Manila Treaty Organization when he passed through Manila on his way to Moscow. In his reply, Shigemitsu also said that the Japanese government has not yet discussed the issue of joining the Manila Treaty Organization.

### 重光葵在菲律宾说  日本目前不想参加马尼拉条约

1956-07-27
第6版()
专栏：

    重光葵在菲律宾说
    日本目前不想参加马尼拉条约
    新华社26日讯　马尼拉消息：日本外务相重光葵今天在这里说，日本在目前不打算参加马尼拉条约组织。
    重光在前往莫斯科途中路过马尼拉的时候被问到日本参加马尼拉条约组织的可能性如何。重光在答复中还说，日本政府也还没有讨论过参加马尼拉条约组织的问题。
