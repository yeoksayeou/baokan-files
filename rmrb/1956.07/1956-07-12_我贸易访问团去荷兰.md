Title: Chinese Trade Delegation to Visit the Netherlands
Author:
Date: 1956-07-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. At the invitation of the Netherlands Central Organization for Foreign Economic Relations, and in response to the visit of the Dutch business trade delegation to China at the end of 1954, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade has formed a trade delegation to visit the Netherlands by plane on the 11th.

The delegation includes representatives from China's import and export corporations and chartering companies, led by Yu Kejian, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade. They will spend two weeks in the Netherlands on visits and exchanges, and exchange views on developing Sino-Dutch trade.

### 我贸易访问团去荷兰

1956-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    我贸易访问团去荷兰
    新华社11日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会应荷兰对外经济关系中央组织的邀请，并回答荷兰工商界贸易访问团1954年底的来华访问，组成了贸易访问团，在十一日乘飞机前往荷兰访问。
    访问团包括中国各进出口公司和租船公司的代表，由中国国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长余克坚率领。他们将在荷兰作两周的参观、访问，并交换开展中荷贸易的意见。
