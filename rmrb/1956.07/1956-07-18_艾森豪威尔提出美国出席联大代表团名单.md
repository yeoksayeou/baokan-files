Title: Eisenhower Submits List of U.S. Delegation to Attend UN General Assembly

Author:
Date: 1956-07-18
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Gettysburg, July 14th: President Eisenhower submitted the list of the U.S. delegation to the 11th session of the United Nations General Assembly to the Senate on July 13th, requesting Senate approval.

　　The head of the delegation is Henry Cabot Lodge Jr., the U.S. Permanent Representative to the United Nations. The delegates are: Senate Republican Leader Knowland, Minnesota Democratic Senator Humphrey, Republican Paul Hoffman, former Director of the Mutual Security Agency and current Chairman of Studebaker-Packard Corporation, and E. Roland Harriman, Chairman of the American Red Cross.

　　The delegation also includes six alternate representatives.

### 艾森豪威尔提出美国出席联大代表团名单

1956-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔提出美国出席联大代表团名单
    新华社14日讯　葛底斯堡消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔七月十三日向参议院提出了出席联合国第十一届大会的美国代表团名单，要求参议院批准。
    代表团团长是美国驻联合国常任代表洛奇。代表是：参议院共和党领袖诺兰，明尼苏达州民主党参议员汉弗来，共和党人、前经合总署署长、现任史蒂倍克汽车公司董事长保罗·霍夫曼，美国红十字会主席班克。
    代表团中还包括有六名候补代表。
