Title: Lippmann Says Dulles' Fallacy on Neutralism Has Plunged the United States into a Very Disgraceful Ending
Author:
Date: 1956-07-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York: American columnist Walter Lippmann published an article in the "New York Herald Tribune" on July 18, criticizing the confused stance on neutralism.

Lippmann said that Dulles' remarks attacking neutralism as "immoral" "offended the non-aligned countries, which are numerous in South Asia, the Middle East, and Europe."

Lippmann believes that this chaotic rhetoric has plunged the United States into a "very disgraceful ending."

Lippmann said: The basic assumption of the Cold War, that the world is divided into two camps, has fallen behind the process of situation development. The world situation has undergone tremendous changes. The most influential developments that have caused this change are the Soviet Union's achievement of equality in nuclear weapons, the success of Soviet industrialization, and the consolidation of the Chinese Communist regime. The combined impact of these developments has promoted neutralism and overturned the idea that countries without nuclear weapons can gain security by joining one or the other of the two military blocs.

Lippmann demanded that American policymakers formulate a different policy for each non-aligned country, rather than "pronouncing a sweeping judgment" on them.

### 李普曼说：杜勒斯对中立主义的谬论使美国陷进非常可耻结局

1956-07-23
第6版()
专栏：

    李普曼说：  
    杜勒斯对中立主义的谬论
    使美国陷进非常可耻结局
    据新华社讯　纽约消息：美国专栏作家李普曼七月十八日在“纽约先驱论坛报”发表文章，抨击对待中立主义的混乱立场。
    李普曼说，杜勒斯攻击中立主义为“不道德的”言论，“触怒了不参加集团的国家，这些国家在南亚、中东和欧洲是为数很多的”。
    李普曼认为，这种混乱不堪的言论，已经使美国陷进了“非常可耻的结局”。
    李普曼说：冷战的基本假设，即世界被分为两个阵营，已经落后于局势发展的进程了。世界局势已经产生了巨大的变化。引起这种变化的发展中最有影响的是苏联在核子方面达到了平等地位，苏联工业化获得成功以及中国共产党政权的巩固。这些发展的联合影响已经促进了中立主义，已经推翻了没有核子武器的国家参加两个军事集团中的一个或另一个就可以得到安全的意见。
    李普曼要求美国的决策人对每一个不参加集团的国家拟定一个不同的政策，而不要对它们“宣布一个一网打尽的判决”。
