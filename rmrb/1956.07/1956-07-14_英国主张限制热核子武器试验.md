Title: Britain Advocates Limiting Thermonuclear Weapon Tests
Author:
Date: 1956-07-14
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, London, 13th—British Prime Minister Eden stated on the 12th that Britain advocates limiting thermonuclear weapon tests.

　　Eden said in response to questions in the House of Commons that Britain hopes to address this issue within the framework of a comprehensive disarmament agreement. However, “as long as our basic defense interests are not compromised, we do not rule out any other methods of discussion that are acceptable to the parties concerned.”

　　The Soviet Union has long proposed that an agreement be reached on a complete ban on nuclear weapon tests before an international agreement is reached on completely prohibiting nuclear weapons and destroying stockpiles of such weapons. However, Western countries have expressed opposition. As a counter-proposal, British Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, Nutting, proposed limiting the testing of nuclear weapons at the United Nations Disarmament Commission meeting on the 10th.

　　On the other hand, Britain has announced that it is preparing to test a hydrogen bomb next year.

### 英国主张限制热核子武器试验

1956-07-14
第6版()
专栏：

    英国主张限制热核子武器试验
    新华社13日讯　伦敦消息：英国首相艾登十二日表示英国主张限制热核子武器试验。
    艾登在下院回答问题的时候说，英国希望在全面裁军协议的范围内处理这个问题。但是，“只要我们的基本防御利益不受损害，我们不排斥有关方面所能接受的其他任何讨论方法”。
    苏联早已建议在就完全禁止核子武器和销毁这种武器的库存达成国际协议之前，先就完全禁止核子武器试验达成协议。但是西方国家表示反对。作为一个反建议，英国外交副大臣纳丁十日在联合国裁军委员会会议上提出了限制试验核子武器的建议。
    另一方面，英国已经宣布，它准备在明年试验氢弹。
