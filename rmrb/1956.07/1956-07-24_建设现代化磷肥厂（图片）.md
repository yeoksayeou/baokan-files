Title: Building a Modern Phosphate Fertilizer Plant (Picture)
Author: Qu Hongchao
Date: 1956-07-24
Page: 2

    Building a Modern Phosphate Fertilizer Plant (Picture)
    The state-owned Nanjing Phosphate Fertilizer Plant is under construction. Originally scheduled to go into production in 1958, the plant will now be put into production in 1957 in order to meet the needs of the countryside for fertilizer. The picture shows a corner of the mechanized construction site. Photo by Qu Hongchao (Nanjing Daily)

### 建设现代化磷肥厂（图片）
曲宏超
1956-07-24
第2版()
专栏：

    建设现代化磷肥厂（图片）
    国营南京磷肥厂正在建设。这个厂原计划在1958年投入生产，为了满足农村对肥料的需求，将提前在1957年投入生产。图为工地上机械化施工的一角。曲宏超摄（南京日报社稿）
