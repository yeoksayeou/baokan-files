At the Soviet Industrial Exhibition

    Utilizing Radiation to Explore Geology
    In exploring for oil and other minerals, the Soviet Union has more commonly applied radioactive methods. This method utilizes the different amounts of naturally radioactive elements contained in various mineral layers. When these elements decompose, they begin to emit radiation, which can be detected by instruments on the ground or in aircraft, thereby determining the best locations for mineral deposits. Recently, titanium and tungsten deposits discovered in the Soviet Union were found using this method.
    Currently, various radioactive isotopes are also widely used in mineral exploration. For example, powerful neutron beams can be used to irradiate boreholes to determine the boundaries between groundwater and oil, because irradiation will cause the sodium in the mineral water to become radioactive, while oil does not contain sodium. This allows the boundaries between water and oil to be determined and the location of oil deposits to be found.
    Similarly, by using radioactive isotope Cobalt gamma rays to irradiate mineral layers along boreholes, the location and thickness of coal mines can be explored.

    Comfortable and High-Speed Sedans
    The comfortable and high-speed seven-seater sedan "ЗИС—111" will be produced by the Moscow Likhachev Automobile Factory in 1957, replacing the existing "ЗИС—110" sedan.
    The "ЗИС—111" sedan's engine has eight cylinders and a total of 210 horsepower, while the "ЗИС—110" has only 140 horsepower. The "ЗИС—111" has a maximum driving speed of 160 kilometers per hour, and its fuel consumption is lower than that of the "ЗИС—110." For example, at a speed of 50 to 60 kilometers per hour, the "ЗИС—111" only requires 20 liters of gasoline per 100 kilometers, while the "ЗИС—110" requires 23 liters.
    The biggest advantage of the new sedan is its smoothness and comfort. The car body does not experience vibrations when turning, stopping, or starting. Even if the sedan is driving on poor roads, the occupants inside will not feel bumpy. The car is equipped with air conditioning equipment to keep the interior of the car always fresh.

    New Machine for Harvesting Corn
    The new type of corn combine harvester "Учку—Three" will be produced by the Rostov Agricultural Machinery Factory this year. When this machine is working, its sharp knives can simultaneously harvest three rows of corn with a spacing of 700 millimeters per row. The corn harvested from the field is then sent to the upper end of the combine, where the machine cuts the corn ears one by one from the stalks and peels off the husks from the corn cobs. The cleaned corn is then discharged from a spout. At the same time, the machine also cuts the corn stalks into sections and discharges them along with the corn husks and other debris from another spout. In this way, the corn harvesting work is completed. This combine harvester only needs one person to operate, and with the third speed, its production is 11.4 hectares per hour. (Ji Qing)

### 在苏联工业展览会上

1956-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    在苏联工业展览会上
    利用放射线勘探地质
    在勘探石油和其他矿产时，苏联已更加普遍地应用放射性方法。这种方法就是利用各种矿层中所包含的不同数量的天然放射性元素。当它们分解时，这些元素开始放射，这就可以从地面上或飞机上的仪器中看出并确定那里是最好的矿产地。最近苏联所发现的钛和钨的矿产地就是运用这种方法发现的。
    目前在勘探矿产地时，还普遍的应用各种放射性同位素。例如可以用强大的中子流照射钻井来确定地下水和石油的界限，因为照射的结果，将使矿泉水中含有的钠放射，而石油中没有钠，这样就可以确定水和石油的界限，并发现石油矿的所在地。
    同样，用放射性同位素钴丙种射线顺着钻孔照射矿层，就可以勘探出煤矿的所在和它的厚度。舒适和高速度的轿车
  舒适和高速度的有七个座位的轿车“3NC—111”，将于1957年由莫斯科利哈乔夫汽车工厂生产，以代替现有的“3NC—110”轿车。
    “3NC—111”轿车的发动机有八个汽缸，共有二百一十匹马力，而“3NC—110”只有一百四十匹马力。“3NC—111”每小时的最高行车速度是一百六十公里，耗油量反比“3NC—110”减少。例如在每小时行走五十到六十公里的速度下，每一百公里“3NC—111”只需二十公升汽油，而“3NC—110”则需二十三公升。
    新型轿车最大的优点是很平稳和舒适。无论是在拐弯或停车及开始行走时，车身不会感到震动，如果轿车在不好的公路上行走，坐在车内的人也不会觉得颠簸。车内装有调节空气的设备，可以使车厢内永远保有新鲜的空气。
    收割玉蜀黍的新机器
    新式的玉蜀黍联合收割机“учку—三式”将在今年由罗斯托夫农业机器制造厂开始生产。这种机器进行工作时，它的锋利的刀子可以同时收割每行距离七百公厘的三行玉蜀黍。在地里收割下来的玉蜀黍，接着就被送上联合机的上端，在那里，机器一面将玉蜀黍一个一个从茎上切下来，并把玉蜀黍棒上的表皮撕去，被收拾干净的玉蜀黍就从一个筒口倾吐出来。同时机器还将玉蜀黍秸切成几节，连同玉蜀黍皮等其他杂物在另一筒口倒出来。这样，玉蜀黍的收割工作就算完成了。这架联合收割机，只要一个人照管，用第三速率转动，每小时生产量是十一点四公顷。　（纪青）
