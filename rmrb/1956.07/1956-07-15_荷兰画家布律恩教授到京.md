Title: Dutch Painter Professor Bruijn Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Dutch painter Professor Jacob Bruijn arrived in Beijing on the 13th, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Artists Association, to participate in the commemoration and exhibition of the 350th anniversary of the birth of the world cultural figure and Dutch painter Rembrandt.

The opening ceremony of the Rembrandt 350th Anniversary Commemoration and Exhibition has been changed from the originally scheduled July 15th to July 20th.

### 荷兰画家布律恩教授到京

1956-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

    荷兰画家布律恩教授到京
    新华社14日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会和中国美术家协会邀请，前来参加世界文化名人、荷兰画家伦勃朗诞生三百五十周年纪念会和展览会的荷兰画家雅各布·布律恩教授，已在十三日到达北京。
    伦勃朗诞生三百五十周年纪念会和展览会开幕式，已由原定的七月十五日改为七月二十日举行。
