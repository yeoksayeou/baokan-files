Title: Soviet Communist Party Leaders Receive Italian Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 11th: According to TASS, a delegation from the Italian Communist Party, consisting of Italian Communist Party Central Committee Secretariat Secretary Pajetta and members of the party's leadership bodies, Nigavi and Pelegrini, recently arrived in Moscow.
　　On July 11th, First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee Khrushchev, CPSU Central Committee Secretary Pospelov, and CPSU Central Committee member Ponomarev met with the Italian Communist Party delegation and held lengthy and warm talks with them.
　　The two sides exchanged views on the policies of the two parties, as well as the situation and existing problems of the workers' movement.
　　The talks were conducted in a sincere atmosphere and in the spirit of genuine friendship and mutual trust between fraternal parties.

### 苏共领导人接见意共代表团

1956-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共领导人接见意共代表团
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十一日讯：由意大利共产党中央委员会书记处书记巴叶塔和党领导机构的委员尼加维莱、贝勒格林尼组成的意大利共产党代表团，最近到达莫斯科。
    七月十一日，苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫、苏共中央书记波斯伯洛夫和苏共中央委员波诺马烈夫接见了意大利共产党代表团，并且同他们进行了长时间的热烈的谈话。
    双方就两党政策以及工人运动的状况和现存问题交换了意见。
    谈话是在诚挚的气氛中本着兄弟党之间的真诚友谊和相互信任的精神进行的。
