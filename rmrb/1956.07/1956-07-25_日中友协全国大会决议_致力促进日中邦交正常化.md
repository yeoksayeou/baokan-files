Title: Japan-China Friendship Association National Convention Resolution: Dedicated to Promoting the Normalization of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-07-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 24th: The Sixth National Convention of the Japan-China Friendship Association, which closed in Tokyo yesterday, unanimously agreed that the main direction and goal of this year's movement should be focused on the issue of normalizing diplomatic relations between Japan and China. At the same time, it is necessary to actively promote economic, cultural, and various exchanges between Japan and China to enhance Japan-China friendship.

    The convention issued a declaration stating that the time for realizing the normalization of diplomatic relations between Japan and China has arrived. In order to achieve this goal, we must concentrate the strength of the people to break through various obstacles, remind public opinion, and continue to work hard and patiently.

    The convention unanimously adopted several resolutions. These resolutions included: urging Japanese Prime Minister Hatoyama and Foreign Minister Shigemitsu to respond to Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai's call and immediately take measures to promote the normalization of diplomatic relations between Japan and China; requesting the Japanese government to actively strive for the complete removal of export restrictions to China; thanking the Chinese government for its lenient treatment of Japanese war criminals and the Chinese Red Cross for helping repatriate Japanese nationals; and requesting the Japanese government to permit Chinese representatives to attend the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs to enter the country.

    The convention elected the leading members of the Japan-China Friendship Association for this year. Chairman Jiichiro Matsumoto, Vice Chairman Yoshitaro Hirano, and others were all retained.

    The convention concluded with the entire assembly singing "Tokyo-Beijing" and chanting "Long live Japan-China friendship!"

### 日中友协全国大会决议  致力促进日中邦交正常化

1956-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    日中友协全国大会决议
    致力促进日中邦交正常化
    新华社24日讯　东京消息：昨天在东京闭幕的日中友好协会第六届全国大会一致认为，今年的主要运动方针和目标要放在日中两国外交关系正常化问题上。同时，要积极推进日中两国的经济、文化和各种交流来增进日中友好。
    大会发表宣言指出，实现日中两国外交关系正常化的时机已经到来，我们为了达成这个目的，要集中国民的力量打破种种障碍，提醒舆论，刻苦耐劳地继续努力。
    大会一致通过了几项决议。这些决议包括：劝告日本首相鸠山和外务相重光葵响应中国周恩来总理的呼吁，立刻采取促进日中两国邦交正常化的措施；要求日本政府积极努力全面撤销对中国的输出限制；感谢中国政府对日本战争犯罪分子的宽大处理和中国红十字会帮助遣返日侨；要求日本政府许可参加第二届禁止原子弹、氢弹世界大会中国代表入境等。
    大会选出了日中友好协会今年度的领导人员，会长松本治一郎、副会长平野义太郎等全体留任。
    大会在全场合唱“东京—北京”的歌声和高呼日中友好万岁声中结束。
