Title: Language Institute Compiles "Linguistic Terminology"
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 7

    The Institute of Linguistics is compiling a book entitled "Linguistic Terminology" in order to eliminate the current confusion in the use of linguistic nouns and terms. The book is intended for those engaged in linguistic teaching, research, and translation. The book is divided into two parts: the first part is Russian-Chinese linguistic terminology, and the second part is Chinese-Russian linguistic terminology. The first draft of the first part has now been completed.

### 语言研究所编纂“语言学术语”

1956-07-17
第7版()
专栏：

    语言研究所编纂“语言学术语”
    为了消除目前语言学名词术语使用上的混乱现象，中国科学院语言研究所正在搜集和整理各方面的有关材料，进行“语言学术语”一书编纂工作。这本书是供语言学的教学、研究和翻译工作者使用的。全书分为两个部分，第一部分是俄汉对照的语言学术语，第二部分是汉俄对照的语言学术语。第一部分的初稿现已完成。
