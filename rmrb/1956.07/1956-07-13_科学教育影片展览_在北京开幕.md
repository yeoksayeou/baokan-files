Title: "Science Education Film Exhibition" Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-07-13
Page: 1

    "Science Education Film Exhibition" Opens in Beijing

    (Our newspaper dispatch) The "Science Education Film Exhibition," jointly sponsored by the Ministry of Culture and the China Association for Science and Technology, opened at the Xinjiekou Cinema in Beijing on the evening of the 12th.

    Ding Xilin, Vice Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology, pointed out in his opening speech that science education films are a most popular and vivid propaganda tool. Therefore, relevant departments are striving to coordinate the screening of science films at each of the 400,000 science lectures held for workers nationwide this year. Wang Lanxi, Director of the Film Bureau, said in his speech that in the past six years, China has produced and dubbed a total of 158 science education films and eleven issues of the magazine film "Science and Technology." From 1954 to 1955, more than 20,000 screenings were held, with over 10 million viewers.

    The second Science Education Film Exhibition will be held in three batches in ten cities including Beijing, Shanghai, and Tianjin, as well as key industrial cities and medium-sized cities in various provinces, each lasting for one week. The new films on display include more than 20 titles covering natural science, agricultural science and technology, and general scientific knowledge.

    More than 800 people from the capital's science and cultural circles attended the opening ceremony, and they watched science education films such as "Prevention and Control of Cotton Aphids," "Day and Night," and the Soviet "Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy."

### “科学教育影片展览”在北京开幕

1956-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    “科学教育影片展览”在北京开幕
    本报讯　文化部和中华全国科学技术普及协会联合举办的“科学教育影片展览”，十二日晚在北京新街口电影院举行开幕式。
    中华全国科学技术普及协会副主席丁西林在开幕式讲话中指出，科学教育电影是一种最通俗生动的宣传工具，因此有关部门争取在今年对全国职工举行的四十万次科学讲演中，每次都能配合放映科学电影。电影事业管理局局长王阑西在讲话中说，六年来我国已拍摄和译制科学教育影片共计158部、“科学与技术”杂志片十一号。1954年到1955年两年内共映出二万多场，观众一千多万人次。
    这次举行的第二次科学教育影片展览，将分三批在北京、上海、天津等十个城市和各省重工业城市、中等城市先后举行一周。展览的新片有自然科学、农业科学技术和一般科学知识等二十多部。
    出席开幕式的首都科学、文化界人士八百多人，他们观看了“防治棉蚜”、“白天黑夜”和苏联的“和平利用原子能”等科学教育影片。
