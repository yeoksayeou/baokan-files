Title: Korean People Mourn the Heroic Souls; Masses from Various Regions Visit the Korean-Chinese Martyrs' Cemetery to Sweep the Tombs

Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 28th. On the third anniversary of the Korean Armistice Agreement, people from all over Korea flocked to the cemeteries of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to pay their respects.

On the 26th, leading officials from the Korean government and the Korean Workers' Party organs stationed in the area where the leading organs of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army were located, along with representatives from local workers, farmers, women's organizations, youth leagues, and elementary school students, totaling more than 300 people, accompanied by representatives from the leading organs of the Volunteer Army, presented wreaths at the Volunteer Army Martyrs' Cemetery. They placed bright bouquets in front of each martyr's tomb and added fresh soil to the graves. The liaison representative of the Korean People's Army stationed at the Volunteer Army leading organs recounted the deeds of the martyrs during their lives to those who came to sweep the tombs. The tomb sweepers also participated in the renovation work of the Martyrs' Cemetery.

The Korean people in areas such as Kaesong and Shangganling also carried out activities such as sweeping tombs and renovating the Martyrs' Cemetery.

### 朝鲜人民吊英魂  各地群众纷纷到朝中烈士陵园扫墓

1956-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民吊英魂
    各地群众纷纷到朝中烈士陵园扫墓
    新华社平壤28日电　在朝鲜停战三周年纪念日，朝鲜各地人民纷纷到朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军烈士陵园致祭。
    二十六日，中国人民志愿军领导机关驻地的朝鲜政府、朝鲜劳动党机关的负责人和当地的工人、农民、妇女团体、青年团体的代表以及小学生等三百多人，在志愿军领导机关代表的陪同下，到志愿军烈士陵园敬献花圈。他们在每个烈士的墓前放置了鲜艳的花束，并且在墓上培了新土。朝鲜人民军驻志愿军领导机关的联络代表向扫墓的人讲述了烈士们的生前事迹。扫墓的人还参加了烈士陵园的修葺工作。
    在开城、上甘岭等地区的朝鲜人民，也进行了扫墓和修葺烈士陵园等活动。
