Title: Popular Mobile Service Vehicle (Picture)
Author: Yan Zhennan
Date: 1956-07-31
Page: 3

    Popular Mobile Service Vehicle (Picture)
    The Huangshi City Trading Company in Hubei Province has a store that has set up a mobile service vehicle, which is often fully loaded with various foods and non-staple foods to do business in the workers' dormitory areas of factories and mines, and is very popular.
    The picture shows family members of employees of the joint state-private Huaxin Cement Plant selecting goods next to the mobile service vehicle.
                                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Yan Zhennan photo

### 受人欢迎的流动服务车（图片）
严震南
1956-07-31
第3版()
专栏：

    受人欢迎的流动服务车（图片）
    湖北省黄石市贸易公司有一个门市部设立了流动服务车，经常满载着各种食品、副食品到工矿的职工宿舍区里作生意，很受人们的欢迎。
    图为公私合营华新水泥厂的职工家属们在流动服务车旁选购货物。
                                      新华社记者　严震南摄
