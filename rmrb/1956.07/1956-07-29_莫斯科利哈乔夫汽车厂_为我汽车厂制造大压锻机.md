Title: Moscow Likhachev Automobile Plant Manufactures Large Forging Press for Our Automobile Plant
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 28th. The Moscow Likhachev Automobile Plant recently created a forging press for China's First Automobile Manufacturing Plant that the Soviet Union had not produced before. Its production capacity is half again as large as similar machines currently used by the factory itself. A special railway flatcar was also built to transport this machine to China.

This unique machine is used to forge the load-bearing main beam of automobiles, and its pressure reaches 3,500 tons. Its height exceeds twelve meters. Its total weight is approximately seven hundred tons.

### 莫斯科利哈乔夫汽车厂  为我汽车厂制造大压锻机

1956-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科利哈乔夫汽车厂 
    为我汽车厂制造大压锻机
    据新华社莫斯科28日电　莫斯科利哈乔夫汽车工厂最近为中国第一汽车制造厂创制了一部苏联以前还没有生产过的压锻机，它的生产能力比这个工厂目前自己使用的同类机器还要大一半。为了把这部机器运送到中国，还建造了一节专门的铁路敞车。
    这部独特的机器是压锻汽车负重的主梁用的，它的压力达到三五○○吨。它的高度超过十二公尺。它的总重量约有七百吨。
