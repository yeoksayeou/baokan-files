Title: Korean Delegation Leaves Soviet Union for Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1956-07-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 13th: The Korean government delegation headed by Premier Kim Il-sung left Moscow by plane for Ulaanbaatar on the morning of July 13th.

### 朝鲜代表团离苏赴蒙

1956-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜代表团离苏赴蒙
    据新华社莫斯科13日电　以金日成首相为首的朝鲜政府代表团，在七月十三日上午乘飞机离开莫斯科前往乌兰巴托。
