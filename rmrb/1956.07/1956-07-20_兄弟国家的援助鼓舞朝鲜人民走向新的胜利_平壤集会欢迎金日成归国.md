Title: Brotherly Countries' Aid Encourages Korean People to March Towards New Victories; Pyongyang Rally Welcomes Kim Il-sung's Return

Author: 
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 5

    Brotherly Countries' Aid Encourages Korean People to March Towards New Victories
    Pyongyang Rally Welcomes Kim Il-sung's Return
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 19th. The Korean government delegation headed by Kim Il-sung returned to Pyongyang by plane this afternoon after visiting the Soviet Union, the German Democratic Republic, and other brotherly countries.
    A welcoming rally attended by more than 60,000 people was held in Kim Il-sung Square. Kim Il-sung spoke at the meeting. He said that the delegation had held talks with the leaders of various brotherly countries, respectively, and reached unanimous agreements on further strengthening friendly unity and promoting economic and cultural cooperation. In particular, the brotherly countries, headed by the Soviet Union, decided to provide new assistance to Korea, which will encourage us to march towards new victories.
    He concluded by saying that we will further strengthen political, economic, and cultural ties with brotherly countries, strengthen friendly unity, expand the achievements of economic construction, and realize the peaceful reunification of the motherland to repay the assistance of the people of brotherly countries to us.

### 兄弟国家的援助鼓舞朝鲜人民走向新的胜利  平壤集会欢迎金日成归国

1956-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家的援助鼓舞朝鲜人民走向新的胜利
    平壤集会欢迎金日成归国
    据新华社平壤19日电　以金日成为首的朝鲜政府代表团，在访问了苏联、德意志民主共和国等兄弟国家后，在今天下午乘飞机回到平壤。
    在金日成广场上举行了有六万多人参加的欢迎大会。金日成在会上讲了话。他说，代表团曾分别同各兄弟国家的领导人进行了会谈，就进一步加强友好团结和促进经济文化合作达成了一致的协议。特别是以苏联为首的各兄弟国家决定给予朝鲜以新的援助，这将鼓舞着我们走向新的胜利。
    他最后说，我们将进一步加强同各兄弟国家的政治、经济和文化联系，加强友好团结，扩大经济建设的成就，实现祖国的和平统一，来回答各兄弟国家人民对我们的援助。
