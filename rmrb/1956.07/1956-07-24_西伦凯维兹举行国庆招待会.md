Title: Cyrankiewicz Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1956-07-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 23rd. Polish People's Republic Premier Cyrankiewicz held a reception on the 22nd to celebrate the Polish National Day.

    Attending the reception were members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, members of the State Council, ministers of various departments, generals of the Polish army, representatives of various political parties and social organizations, representatives of cultural and scientific workers, advanced workers, and representatives of the press.

    The Soviet government delegation headed by Bulganin and the German Democratic Republic government delegation headed by Bolz attended the reception.

    Foreign diplomatic envoys to Poland also attended the reception.

### 西伦凯维兹举行国庆招待会

1956-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹举行国庆招待会
    新华社华沙23日电　波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹二十二日举行招待会，庆祝波兰国庆节。
    出席招待会的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局的委员们，国务委员会的委员们，各部部长，波兰军队的将领，各个政党和社会组织的代表，文化科学工作者的代表，先进工人和新闻界的代表。
    以布尔加宁为首的苏联政府代表团和以博尔茨为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团出席了招待会。
    各国驻波兰的外交使节也出席了招待会。
