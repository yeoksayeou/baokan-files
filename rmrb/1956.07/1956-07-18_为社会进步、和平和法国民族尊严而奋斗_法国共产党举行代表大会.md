Title: French Communist Party Holds Congress to Fight for Social Progress, Peace, and French National Dignity
Author: 
Date: 1956-07-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th, Paris Dispatch: The 14th Congress of the French Communist Party is scheduled to be held in the Atlantic coastal city of Le Havre starting July 18th, lasting for four days.

    The congress will discuss the draft proposal submitted to the congress by the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, which is entitled "The French Communist Party Fighting for Future Social Progress, Peace, and the National Dignity of France." This proposal has been widely discussed among members of the French Communist Party before the congress.

    The Soviet Communist Party delegation headed by Suslov, which was invited to attend the congress, has arrived in Paris.

### 为社会进步、和平和法国民族尊严而奋斗  法国共产党举行代表大会

1956-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

    为社会进步、和平和法国民族尊严而奋斗
    法国共产党举行代表大会
    新华社17日讯　巴黎消息：法国共产党第十四次代表大会定于七月十八日起在大西洋岸城市哈佛尔举行，大会将举行四天。
    代表大会将讨论法共中央向大会提出的提纲草案，这个提纲草案的题目是“在为未来社会进步、和平和法国的民族尊严而奋斗的法国共产党”。在代表大会之前，这个提纲已经在法国共产党党员中广泛讨论。
    应邀参加大会的以苏斯洛夫为首的苏联共产党代表团已经到达巴黎。
