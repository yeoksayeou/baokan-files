Title: Insecticide Doesn't Kill Insects
Author:
Date: 1956-07-22
Page: 2

Since the beginning of summer this year, our county's supply and marketing cooperative and the health center have purchased a large quantity of DDT and BHC insecticides from the Shanxi Provincial Pharmaceutical Company. These drugs were produced by the state-owned North China Agricultural Pest and Disease Drug Factory. The masses were very happy when the drugs arrived and they were quickly sold out.

However, the results of several months of use have proved that the quality of this drug is much worse than what we bought last year. Last year, only two applications were needed to eliminate flies, mosquitoes, and lice. This year, we sprayed the drug several times, and the amount used was more than last year, but it still doesn't kill the insects. Even in places that have just been sprayed, flies are still crawling on the surface. The masses are very dissatisfied with this and have been questioning us, but we have nothing to answer. We request the China Pharmaceutical Company to strictly inspect the quality of these products before stocking them. And investigate the responsibility of the pharmaceutical factory for producing bad drugs.

### 杀虫药杀不死虫

1956-07-22
第2版()
专栏：

    杀虫药杀不死虫
    今年入夏以来，我县供销合作社和卫生院，先后向山西省医药公司，购买了大批的滴滴涕和六六六杀虫药，这些药品都是国营华北农业病虫药制造厂的出品，药品到了以后群众都很高兴，很快就卖光了。
    但是，几个月来使用的结果证明，这种药比起去年买的，质量要差很多，去年买的只要打上两次，就把苍蝇、蚊子、虱子消灭了。今年买的药喷了好几次，分量也比去年用的多，就是杀不死虫，甚至刚喷过药的地方，苍蝇还在上面爬。群众对这件事很不满意，纷纷向我们质问，我们也无话可答。我们要求中国医药公司在进货以前，严格检查这些产品的质量。并且追究制药厂生产坏药的责任。
      山西保德县卫生院　谢新如
