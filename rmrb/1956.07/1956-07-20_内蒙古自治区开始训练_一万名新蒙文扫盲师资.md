Title: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Begins Training 10,000 New Mongolian Literacy Teachers
Author:
Date: 1956-07-20
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, 18th - The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has begun training 10,000 new Mongolian literacy teachers. After this group of literacy teachers is trained, a region-wide new Mongolian literacy campaign will be fully launched in the Autonomous Region. According to plans from relevant authorities, by the end of this year, 200,000 Mongolian farmers and herdsmen throughout the region will participate in learning the new Mongolian script.
    Currently, more than 1,000 language teachers from new Mongolian primary and secondary schools in the Autonomous Region have also been gathered in Hohhot and other locations to study the new Mongolian script. Starting this autumn term, the first grade of primary schools throughout the region will be taught entirely in the new Mongolian script, and secondary schools will also add new Mongolian script classes.

### 内蒙古自治区开始训练  一万名新蒙文扫盲师资

1956-07-20
第7版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区开始训练
    一万名新蒙文扫盲师资
    新华社呼和浩特18日电　内蒙古自治区已经开始训练一万名新蒙文扫盲师资。这批扫盲师资训练出来以后，一个全区性的新蒙文扫盲工作就要在自治区全面展开。根据有关机关的计划，到今年年底，全区将有二十万蒙古族农民和牧民参加学习新蒙文。
    现在自治区一千多新蒙古族小学和中学的语文教师，也已集中在呼和浩特市等地学习新蒙文。今秋新学年开始，全区小学一年级就要完全用新蒙文教学，中等学校也要增添新蒙文课。
