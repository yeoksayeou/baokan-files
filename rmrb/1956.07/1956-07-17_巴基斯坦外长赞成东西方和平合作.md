Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Advocates Peaceful Cooperation Between East and West
Author:
Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna Dispatch: According to a Vienna dispatch from the Associated Press, Pakistani Foreign Minister Choudhry, who was visiting Austria, said in Vienna on July 14 that he supports peaceful cooperation between East and West.

Choudhry made the remarks in reply to a welcoming speech by Austrian Foreign Minister Figl, saying, "I believe that peaceful cooperation between East and West is an important prerequisite for the progress of mankind and the progress of individual countries."

### 巴基斯坦外长赞成东西方和平合作

1956-07-17
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦外长赞成东西方和平合作
    新华社讯　据美联社维也纳消息：到奥地利访问的巴基斯坦外交部长乔德里七月十四日在维也纳说，他赞成东西方和平合作。
    乔德里在奥地利外交部长费格尔致欢迎词后致答词说：“我认为，东西方之间的和平合作是人类进步和各别国家进步的重要先决条件。”
