Title: Czechoslovakia and Cambodia Decide to Exchange Diplomatic Representatives; Joint Communiqué Announces Bilateral Relations Will Be Based on Five Principles

Author:

Date: 1956-07-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 16th. A joint communiqué of Czechoslovakia and Cambodia, released here, announces that the representatives of the two governments have reached an agreement during their talks to exchange diplomatic representatives in the near future. Relations between the two countries will be based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

The joint communiqué was signed in Prague on July 14th. The signatories on behalf of their respective governments were Czechoslovak Premier Viliam Široký and Cambodian Prince Norodom Sihanouk.

The joint communiqué stated that the two countries unanimously believe that the United Nations Organization should become a universal organization, and that all countries that fulfill the conditions stipulated in the UN Charter should become its members, especially the People's Republic of China, which should obtain its legitimate position in the organization; in addition, Japan and other sovereign states should also become members of the United Nations.

The two countries declared that they will respect international agreements, especially the 1954 Geneva Accords.

The two countries demanded that atomic energy be used only for peaceful purposes.

Both sides also agreed that they would soon conclude a trade agreement and a scientific and technological cooperation agreement.

### 捷柬决定互派外交代表  联合会谈公报宣布两国关系将以五项原则为基础

1956-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    捷柬决定互派外交代表
    联合会谈公报宣布两国关系将以五项原则为基础
    新华社布拉格16日电　这里发表的捷克斯洛伐克和柬埔寨的联合会谈公报宣布，两国政府的代表在会谈中已经达成协议，决定不久即将互派外交代表。两国的关系将以和平共处的五项原则为基础。
    这个联合公报是七月十四日在布拉格签字的。代表两国政府在上面签字的是捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基和柬埔寨西哈努克亲王。
    联合公报说，两国一致认为，联合国组织应该成为具有普遍性的组织，所有履行联合国宪章规定的条件的国家都应该成为它的会员国，特别是中华人民共和国，它应该取得它在这个组织中的合法地位；另外日本和其他主权国家也应该成为联合国的会员国。
    两国宣称，它们将尊重国际协议，特别是1954年的日内瓦协议。
    两国要求原子能只用于和平用途。
    双方还一致同意不久将缔结贸易协定和科学技术合作协定。
