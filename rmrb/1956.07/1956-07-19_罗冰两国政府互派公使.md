Title: Romania and Iceland Exchange Ministers

Author:

Date: 1956-07-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 18th: According to a Romanian News Agency report on the 17th, the Romanian government and the Icelandic government, after agreeing to establish diplomatic relations, have appointed ministers to each other.

    The Romanian government has appointed the Romanian Minister to the United Kingdom, Mr. Corcinschi, to concurrently serve as the Minister to Iceland.

    The Icelandic government has appointed the Icelandic Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Mr. Thorsteinsson, to concurrently serve as the Minister to Romania.

### 罗冰两国政府互派公使

1956-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

    罗冰两国政府互派公使
    新华社18日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社十七日报道，罗马尼亚政府和冰岛政府在同意建立外交关系以后，已经互相任命了公使。
    罗马尼亚政府任命罗马尼亚驻英国公使科尔钦斯基兼任驻冰岛公使。
    冰岛政府任命冰岛驻苏联大使索尔斯坦逊兼任驻罗马尼亚公使。
