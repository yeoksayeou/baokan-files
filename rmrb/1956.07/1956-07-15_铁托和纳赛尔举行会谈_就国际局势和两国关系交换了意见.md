Title: Tito and Nasser Hold Talks to Exchange Views on International Situation and Bilateral Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-07-15
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 14th: Belgrade dispatch: Yugoslav President Tito and Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser held talks in Belgrade on the morning of July 13th.

    A communiqué issued after the talks stated that the two sides exchanged views on the international situation and discussed the mutual relations between Yugoslavia and Egypt. During the talks, suggestions were drafted regarding the issues to be discussed when the two presidents hold talks with Indian Prime Minister Nehru. This meeting is scheduled to be held on the island of Brioni on the Adriatic coast on July 19th.

    The communiqué stated that Tito and Nasser will continue to hold talks in the coming days.

    Xinhua News Agency, citing a report by the Yugoslav News Agency: President of the Egyptian Republic, Gamal Abdel Nasser, and his entourage arrived in Belgrade by plane on the afternoon of July 12th. This visit is a return visit for President Tito's visit to Egypt half a year ago. He will stay in Yugoslavia for eight days.

### 铁托和纳赛尔举行会谈  就国际局势和两国关系交换了意见

1956-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

    铁托和纳赛尔举行会谈
    就国际局势和两国关系交换了意见
    据新华社布拉格14日电　贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫总统铁托和埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔，七月十三日上午在贝尔格莱德举行了会谈。
    会谈后发表的公报说，双方就国际局势交换了意见，并且讨论了南斯拉夫和埃及的相互关系。会谈中草拟了关于两位总统即将和印度总理尼赫鲁举行会谈时所要讨论的问题的建议。这次会议定于七月十九日在亚得里亚海滨的布里俄尼岛上举行。
    公报说，铁托和纳赛尔在以后几天中还将继续进行会谈。
    新华社据南斯拉夫通讯社报道　埃及共和国总统加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔和他的随行人员，七月十二日下午乘飞机到达贝尔格莱德。他的这次访问是对铁托总统半年前访问埃及的答谢访问。他将在南斯拉夫逗留八天。
