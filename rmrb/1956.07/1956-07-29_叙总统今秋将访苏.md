Title: Syrian President to Visit Soviet Union This Autumn
Author:
Date: 1956-07-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 27th: Premier al-Asali announced on July 26th that Syrian President Kuwatly will pay a formal visit to the Soviet Union this autumn.

### 叙总统今秋将访苏

1956-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    叙总统今秋将访苏
    新华社27日讯　大马士革消息：阿萨利总理在七月二十六日宣布，叙利亚总统库阿特利将在今年秋天到苏联进行正式的访问。
