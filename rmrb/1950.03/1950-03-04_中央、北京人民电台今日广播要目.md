Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-03-04
Page: 4

[This Newspaper] News: Central People's Broadcasting Station today (4th) 17:30 Youth Time: "Shandong Students' Production and School Building Activities in the Famine." 18:00 First Art and Entertainment Program: Ma Xiaorong sings the second section of the Zhui Zi opera "Blue Bridge Hate." 21:00 Second Art and Entertainment Program: Amateur National Music Research Society performs national music. 18:30 Soviet music and songs "Collective Farm Polka", "Combine Harvester Cuts and Beats", "Spring Dance", "I Walk on the Shore", "Lively Polka." 18:45 New songs: "The Thousand-Year-Old Dead Tree Blooms", "Spring Plowing", "Intensive Farming", "Harvesting", "We Will Never Forget the Communist Party After Emancipation."

[Also News] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station 12:30 Teach singing the song "March 8th Women's Day". 16:30 Voice of the Municipality: (1) Circular of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Labor Bureau on Unified Supplementary Payment and Accounting of Unemployment Relief Funds, (2) Answers to Several Questions in the Trial Implementation Rules of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Labor Bureau on Relieving Unemployed Workers. 17:00 Children's Program: (1) Introducing a children's book "Watch", (2) Singing by children from Dongbajiao Elementary School in the Eighth District. 18:30 Women's Program: Yu Yunfang sings the Dagu "Celebrating the March 8th Women's Day of 1950." 19:00 Joint Lecture for Workers and Youth: Recording of Hu Hua's speech "Several Issues in the New Sino-Soviet Treaty."

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（四日）十七点三十分青年时间：《山东学生在灾荒中的生产建校活动》。十八点第一次文艺节目：马小荣演唱坠子《蓝桥恨》第二段。二十一点第二次文艺节目：由业余国乐研究社演播国乐。十八点三十分苏联音乐和歌曲《集体农场的坡尔加》、《复割机割着打着》、《春天舞》、《我在岸上行走》、《轻快的波里卡》十八点四十五分新歌曲：《千年的枯树开了花》、《春耕》、《精耕细作》、《收割》、《翻身忘不了共产党》。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分教唱《三八妇女节》歌。十六点三十分市政之声：（１）北京市人民政府劳动局关于统一补缴和核算失业救济金办法的通告，（２）北京市人民政府劳动局关于救济失业员工试行细则中几个问题的解答。十七点儿童节目：（一）介绍一本儿童读物《表》，（二）八区东八角小学小朋友们的唱歌。十八点三十分妇女节目：于韵芳演唱大鼓《庆祝一九五○年的三八妇女节》。十九点工人青年联合讲座：胡华讲演《新的中苏条约中的几个问题》的录音。
