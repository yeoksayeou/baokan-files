Title: The Soviet Union Supplies Us with a Large Number of Books; International Bookstore Business Develops Rapidly; In Three Months, 770,000 Soviet Books and Periodicals, and 600,000 Pictures and Newspapers, Were Distributed.
Author:
Date: 1950-03-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th—The International Bookstore, under the General Administration of Publications of the Central People's Government, is developing rapidly. In the past three months, it has distributed a total of 772,446 copies of books and periodicals supplied by the Soviet Union, and 600,252 copies of pictures and newspapers, among which the works of Lenin and Stalin are the most popular, mostly in short supply. The International Bookstore was established in Beijing on December 1st of last year. In the three months up to the end of February this year, the Soviet International Book Company supplied 13 kinds of Chinese versions totaling 393,567 copies; 1,072 kinds of Russian books totaling 200,850 copies; 96 kinds of English, German, and French books totaling 33,729 copies; 100 kinds of portraits, posters, and pictures totaling 140,000 copies; and four kinds of newspapers, including "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy," "Pravda," "Izvestia," and "Red Star," totaling 461,252 copies. There were also eight kinds of magazines in English, Russian, and French, such as "New Times," "Bolshevik," and "USSR in Construction," totaling 136,600 copies. These illustrated magazines have been widely distributed throughout the country through 426 Xinhua Bookstore, Joint Publishing Company branches, and other public and private bookstores in North China, East China, Northwest China, Central and South China, and Southwest China. Among them, the works of Lenin and Stalin, the portraits of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, and the illustrated magazines of Soviet construction are the most popular among the broad masses of readers. In the seven days of Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday alone, 130,000 copies of Stalin's "Problems of Leninism," "Foundations of Leninism," "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks)," and "Brief Biography of Stalin" in Chinese were sold. In order to strengthen the planned nationwide distribution, the International Bookstore plans to establish retail departments in Beijing, Shanghai, and Shenyang before the end of June this year, gradually establish foreign language book and periodical supply departments in Xinhua Bookstores and Joint Publishing Companies in various places, and establish distribution networks throughout the country.

### 苏联供应我大批图书  国际书店业务日益发展  三个月来，经销苏联书刊七十七万册，图片、报纸六十万份。

1950-03-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联供应我大批图书
    国际书店业务日益发展
    三个月来，经销苏联书刊七十七万册，图片、报纸六十万份。
    【新华社北京十三日电】中央人民政府出版总署所属国际书店业务日益发展。三个月来，经销苏联供应的书刊共七十七万二千四百四十六册，图片、报纸六十万零一千二百五十二份，其中以列宁、斯大林著作最为畅销，大都供不应求。国际书店系于去年十二月一日在北京成立，到今年二月底共三个月中，由苏联国际图书公司供应的中文版书籍有十三种共三十九万三千五百六十七册；俄文书籍一千零七十二种共二十万零八千五百五十册；英、德、法文书籍九十六种共三万三千七百二十九册；人像、挂图和画片等一百种共十四万张；《争取持久和平，争取人民民主》报、《真理报》、《消息报》、《红星报》等四种共四十六万一千二百五十二份。英、俄、法文字《新时代》、《布尔什维克》、《苏联建设画报》等杂志八种共十三万六千六百册。这些画刊，通过华北、华东、西北、中南、西南各地区的新华书店、三联书店分支店和其他公私营书店四百二十六家，在全国已普遍发行，其中以列宁、斯大林著作和马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的画像、苏联建设画报等最为广大读者所欢迎。仅在斯大林大元帅七十寿辰的七天中，斯大林所著作的《列宁主义问题》、《列宁主义基础》及《联共（布）党史》、《斯大林传略》等中文版书，即销售了十三万册。为了加强全国性有计划的发行，国际书店计划在今年六月底前，在北京、上海、沈阳三地设立门市部，在各地新华书店、三联书店逐渐创设外文书刊供应部，并在全国各地普遍建立发行网。
