Title: Soviet Peace Committee Delegation Goes to Sweden to Attend Peace Congress Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-03-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 12th: A delegation from the Soviet Peace Committee left Moscow on March 11th for Sweden to attend the third plenary session of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, which will begin on March 16th in Stockholm. The head of the delegation is Fadeyev, and the members include Nesmeyanov, Gagarina, Ehrenburg, Vasilyevskaya, and Sherasin.

### 苏联拥护和平委会代表团赴瑞典参加和大会议

1950-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联拥护和平委会代表团赴瑞典参加和大会议
    【新华社北京十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：苏联拥护和平委员会代表团于三月十一日离莫斯科，前往瑞典，参加三月十六日在瑞京斯德哥尔摩开始举行的世界拥护和平大会常设委员会第三次全体会议。代表团团长为法捷耶夫，团员有涅斯米雅诺夫、嘉嘉里纳、爱伦堡、华西列夫斯卡和谢拉辛。
