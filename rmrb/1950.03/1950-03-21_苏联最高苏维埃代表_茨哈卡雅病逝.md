Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Deputy Tskhakaya Passes Away

Author:
Date: 1950-03-21
Page: 4

　　Soviet Supreme Soviet Deputy
    Tskhakaya Passes Away
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 20th] TASS Moscow, March 20th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union announce with deep sorrow to the Party and all working people that the oldest leader of the Bolshevik Party and the international labor movement, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Comrade Mikhail Grigoryevich Tskhakaya, passed away in Moscow at 1:00 PM on March 19th after a severe illness.
    At the request of the Party, Soviet, and mass organizations of the Georgian Republic, Tskhakaya's funeral will be held in Tbilisi.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表  茨哈卡雅病逝

1950-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃代表
    茨哈卡雅病逝
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：苏联共产党（布）中央委员会与苏联部长会议怀着沉痛的心情通知党与全体劳动人民，布尔什维克党和国际劳工运动最老的领袖、苏联最高苏维埃代表米哈依尔·格里哥里耶维奇·茨哈卡雅同志重病不治，于三月十九日下午一时在莫斯科逝世。
    现应乔治亚共和国的党、苏维埃与群众团体的要求，茨哈卡雅的葬仪将在第比利斯举行。
