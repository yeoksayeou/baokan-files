Title: Soviet Professors Arrive in China for Lectures at Invitation of Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Lectures or Attendance Can Be Arranged with Various Schools, Institutions, etc.
Author:
Date: 1950-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd – At the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, a group of three Soviet professors have arrived in China to give lectures. They are Dr. Nuzhdin, a biologist; Dr. Giselev, a historian; and Ms. Makarova, a Master of Economics. The other four invited Soviet professors will also arrive in China in batches soon. They are expected to stay in China for one and a half to two months. In addition to lecturing in Beijing, Shanghai, and other places, they will also hold seminars with Chinese experts. The schedule of lectures and seminars by the Soviet professors is currently being drawn up by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Schools, institutions, organizations, and factories in Beijing and other cities can contact the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association at No. 2 Yuheqiao, Beijing, to arrange for lectures and register to attend lectures.

### 应中苏友协邀请来我国讲学  苏教授三人抵京  各学校机关等均可接洽讲学或听讲

1950-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　应中苏友协邀请来我国讲学
    苏教授三人抵京       
    各学校机关等均可接洽讲学或听讲
    【新华社北京二日电】应中苏友好协会总会之请来中国讲学的苏联教授，生物学博士努日金、史学博士吉谢列夫及经济学硕士马卡洛娃等一行三人于今日到达北京。被邀请的其他四位苏联教授不久亦将分批来华。他们预计在中国逗留一个半月到两个月，除在北京、上海等地讲学外，并将与中国专家们举行座谈。苏联教授讲学与座谈的日程，正由中苏友好协会总会拟订中。北京及其他城市的学校、机关、团体、工厂均可与北京御河桥二号中苏友好协会总会接洽邀请讲学和报名参加听讲事宜。
