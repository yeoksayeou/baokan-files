　　National Labor Bureau Chiefs Conference Opens
    Will Discuss Solving Issues Such as the General Organizational Rules for Provincial and Municipal Labor Bureaus
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 7th: The National Labor Bureau Chiefs Conference convened by the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government opened today. Over a hundred people attended, including directors of labor bureaus from major administrative regions, provinces, and cities, as well as representatives from labor unions and industrialists and businessmen. Minister Li Lisan pointed out in his opening speech that this conference should use a pragmatic spirit to solve several urgent and solvable problems: general organizational rules for provincial and municipal labor bureaus, provisional labor union law, the issue of establishing labor-management consultation meetings in private enterprises, organizational regulations for labor exchanges and registration methods for unemployed technicians and staff, factory sanitation, employee spare-time education, the relationship between the labor bureau and relevant agencies, and other issues. The conference is expected to last two weeks, and there will be full discussion and research on the above issues. At today's opening ceremony, Vice Premier Dong Biwu of the Government Administration Council attended the meeting and gave a political report.

### 全国劳动局长会议开幕  将讨论解决省市劳动局组织通则等问题

1950-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国劳动局长会议开幕
    将讨论解决省市劳动局组织通则等问题
    【新华社北京七日电】中央人民政府劳动部召集的全国劳动局长会议今日开幕。到各大行政区、省、市劳动局长及工会、工商业家代表一百余人。李立三部长在开幕词中指出，此次会议要以实事求是的精神解决目前急需解决而又可能解决的几个问题：省市劳动局组织通则、工会暂行法、在私营企业中设立劳资协议会议问题、劳动介绍所组织条例及失业技师员工登记办法，工厂清洁卫生、职工业余教育、劳动局与有关机关的关系等问题。会期预计两周，对上述问题将作充分之讨论与研究。今天开幕式上，政务院董必武副总理莅会作政治报告。
