Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1950-03-09
Page: 4

　　[This newspaper reports] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (9th) at 20:20, Zhang Naiqi will speak on: "Comparing Both Sides and Leaning to One Side – The Great Enlightenment of the Sino-Soviet New Treaty." 17:30 Women's program: "Introducing the work done by the Dalian Democratic Women's Federation in the Dalian literacy campaign." 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: "Soviet Science." 18:00 First Literary Program: Northeast Drum "Anti-Japanese Hero Li Zhaolin," "Sino-Soviet Friendship Little Song." 21:00 Second Literary Program: Women's songs. 18:45 New Songs: Huaihai Campaign Suite.

　　[Another news item] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station 12:30 Workers and Youth Joint Lecture: Di Chaobai speaks on "Soviet Union Reducing Prices and Raising People's Living Standards." 16:30 Voice of the City: Beijing People's Court discusses several issues in divorce cases. 17:00 Children's program: Teaching to sing "Little Magpie Announces Good News." 18:45 Listener Services: (1) A brief introduction to the general situation of the Northeast Lu Xun Academy of Literature and Art, (2) Answering the question "What is the area and population of Dalian, and how long is the China Changchun Railway?", (3) Several unclaimed telegrams from the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau. 21:30 Preview of tomorrow's program, Western music. 22:00 Music and Peking Opera, closing music.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（九）日二十点二十分章乃器讲：“两面对照与一面倒——中苏新约的伟大启示”。十七点三十分妇女节目：“介绍旅大民主妇女联合会在旅大识字运动中所做的工作”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：“苏联的科学”。十八点第一次文艺节目：东北大鼓“抗日英雄李兆麟”，“中苏友谊小唱”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：妇女歌曲。十八点四十五分新歌曲：淮海战役组歌。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人青年联合讲座：狄超白讲“苏联减低物价与提高人民生活水平”。十六点三十分市政之声：北京市人民法院谈离婚案件中的几个问题。十七点儿童节目：教唱歌“小喜鹊报喜”。十八点四十五分听众服务：（一）简单介绍东北鲁迅文艺学院的一般情况，（二）回答“旅大面积、人口和中长路有多长”的问题，（三）北京电信局几件招领的电报。二十一点三十分预告明天节目，西乐。二十二点音乐和京剧，终了曲。
