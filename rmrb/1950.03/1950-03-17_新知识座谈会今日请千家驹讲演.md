Title: New Knowledge Forum to Feature Qian Jiaju Lecture Today
Author:
Date: 1950-03-17
Page: 4

[Our Paper's News] The New Knowledge Forum, sponsored by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, is scheduled to hold its 61st meeting at 7:00 PM on the 17th (Friday). The location will remain the European and American Alumni Association Auditorium. This time, Mr. Qian Jiaju, Deputy Director of the Private Enterprise Administration Bureau of the Central Financial and Economic Commission, will lecture on "The Decision of the Government Administration Council on Unifying National Financial and Economic Work." In addition, Mr. Ling Qijun from the association will relay the summary of the Second Session of the Second Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference. Members and friends are encouraged to participate actively.

### 新知识座谈会今日请千家驹讲演

1950-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会今日请千家驹讲演
    【本报讯】民主建国会北京市分会主办的新知识座谈会，定于十七日（星期五）下午七时举行第六十一次会，地点仍在欧美同学会礼堂，这次是请中央财经委员会私营企业管理局副局长千家驹讲《关于政务院统一国家财政经济工作的决定》，并由该会凌其峻传达北京市第二届第二次各界代表会议总结。希望会员、会友们踊跃参加。
