Title: Flames of Fury
Author: Zhāng Mò
Date: 1950-03-16
Page: 6

　　Flames of Fury
    Premiering March 15th at the Dadushi, Qingniangong, Changong, and Tongle theaters.
    "Flames of Fury" depicts the heroic story of the Soviet people engaging in behind-enemy-lines struggles during the Great Patriotic War.

    In 1941, the Hitler bandit gang launched an inhumane war of aggression against the Soviet Union. This beastly fascist crime angered the Soviet people, all the nationalities of the Soviet Union. They joined the Red Army and the guerrillas, contributing their greatest strength to defend the bulwark of world peace and the proletarian motherland.

    After the German invaders occupied the Orsha railway station in Soviet Belarus, the railway employees of Orsha station, under the leadership of Zaslonov, coordinated with the guerrillas to carry out sabotage work, thwarting the enemy's military transportation plan using the Orsha junction. Zaslonov and all the employees then returned to the guerrillas, cooperating with the regular army in a war to thoroughly and cleanly eliminate the German invaders.

    The Soviet heroic figures depicted in "Flames of Fury" are not merely legendary figures; they are a reproduction of typical Soviet people based on reality. Furthermore, thanks to the excellent artistic cultivation of actor Drobnis, who plays Zaslonov, he vividly embodies the inner emotions of the character in every stage of the struggle, leaving a deep impression of this heroic figure in our minds.

    Flames of fury are burning everywhere. Every Soviet person faces a question: "What are you doing? How are you defending the motherland?" In order to sabotage the German fascist military transportation plan, railway worker Surmin sacrificed his life to freeze the station's water tower, completing the task.

    This film profoundly illustrates that the Soviet people will not yield to the enemy and will certainly be victorious.
        (Zhang Mo)

### 愤怒的火焰
张默
1950-03-16
第6版()
专栏：好电影介绍

　　愤怒的火焰
    三月十五日起大都市、青年宫、蟾宫、同乐等影院上映
    《愤怒的火焰》是描写苏维埃人民在卫国战争中从事敌后斗争的英雄故事。
    一九四一年，希特勒匪帮对苏联发动了灭绝人性的侵略战争，这个野兽般的法西斯罪行使得苏联人民，全苏联各个民族愤怒了。他们参加红军、参加游击队，为保卫世界和平堡垒，保卫无产阶级祖国，他们贡献出了最大的力量。
    当德寇侵占苏联白俄罗斯奥尔沙车站后，奥尔沙车站的铁路员工们，在扎斯罗诺夫的领导下，配合着游击队进行破坏工作，粉碎了敌人利用奥尔沙交叉站的军运计划之后，扎斯罗诺夫和所有的员工们又回到游击队配合着正规军，进行着彻底、干净消灭德寇的战争。
    《愤怒的火焰》所表现的苏联英雄人物，并不是单纯的传奇式的人物，这是基于现实基础之上，把典型的苏联人民再现出来的。更由于饰扎斯罗诺夫的演员德罗白里可夫的优秀的艺术修养，他生动地体现了这个角色在斗争过程中每个环节的内在情感，使这一英雄人物在我们头脑中留着很深的印象。
    愤怒的火焰四处燃烧着，每个苏联人的面前，都有一个问题，“你干什么？你怎样保卫祖国？”为了破坏德国法西斯的军运计划，铁路工人苏尔明牺牲了自己的生命设法把车站水塔冻住了，完成了任务。
    本片深刻说明苏联人民对于敌人是不屈服的；而且一定会胜利的。
        （张默）
