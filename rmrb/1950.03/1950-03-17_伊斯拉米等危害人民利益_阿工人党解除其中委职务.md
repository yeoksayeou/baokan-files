Title: Islami and Others Endanger People's Interests; Albanian Workers' Party Relieves Them of Central Committee Positions
Author:
Date: 1950-03-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, March 13th: The Central Committee of the Albanian Workers' Party recently published a communique stating: At its plenary sessions held from February 20th to 22nd and March 6th, 1950, the Central Committee passed a resolution to remove Xhevdet Shijaku and Islami from their positions as members of the Central Committee, to remove Koleci from his position as alternate member of the Central Committee, and to expel Islami from the Party because they engaged in anti-Party and Trotskyist activities that endangered the interests of the people and the People's Republic. The Central Committee plenary session also passed a resolution to remove Ungani from his position as a member of the Central Committee because he committed serious errors and oversights in his work for the state and the Party.

### 伊斯拉米等危害人民利益  阿工人党解除其中委职务

1950-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　伊斯拉米等危害人民利益  阿工人党解除其中委职务
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社地拉那十三日讯：阿尔巴尼亚工人党中央委员会顷发表公报称：中央委员会在其一九五零年二月二十日至二十二日和三月六日举行的全体会议上通过决定，解除西维克及伊斯拉米的中央委员的职务，解除柯列齐的候补中央委员的职务，并开除伊斯拉米的党籍，因为他们从事危害人民和人民共和国利益的反党与托洛斯基派的活动。中央委员会全会并通过决定，解除温加尼的中央委员的职务，因为他在国家与党的工作中，犯了严重的错误与过失。
