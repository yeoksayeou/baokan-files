Title: Jiangxi's Actual Sales of Public Bonds Exceed Original Allotment

Author: 
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 29th. As of the 27th, Jiangxi Province's actual sales of Victory Public Bonds have reached 2,008,245 shares, exceeding the original allotment by 8,245 shares. Some regions that have not completed their payment tasks are continuing to make payments.

### 江西实销公债超过原配销额

1950-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　江西实销公债超过原配销额
    【新华社南昌二十九日电】截至二十七日止，江西省实销胜利公债已达二百万零八千二百四十五分，超过原配销额八千二百四十五分。部分未完成缴款任务地区正继续缴款中。
