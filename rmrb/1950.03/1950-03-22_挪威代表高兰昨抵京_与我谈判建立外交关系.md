Title: Norwegian Representative Kaurin Arrives in Beijing Yesterday to Negotiate the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations with Our Country
Author:
Date: 1950-03-22
Page: 1

　　Norwegian Representative Kaurin Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
    Negotiating the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations with Our Country
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st] The Norwegian government has designated Mr. Kaurin as its representative to negotiate with the Central People's Government of our country on the establishment of diplomatic relations. Mr. Kaurin arrived in Beijing at 7:40 this morning. Han Xu, Section Chief of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to the station to greet him.

### 挪威代表高兰昨抵京  与我谈判建立外交关系

1950-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　挪威代表高兰昨抵京
    与我谈判建立外交关系
    【新华社二十一日讯】为与我国中央人民政府就建立外交关系问题进行谈判，挪威政府已指定高兰先生为谈判代表。高兰先生业于今晨七时四十分抵京。我外交部派交际处科长韩叙赴站迎接。
