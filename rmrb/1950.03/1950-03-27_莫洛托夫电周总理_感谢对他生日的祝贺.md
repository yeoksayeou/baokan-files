Title: Molotov Telegram to Premier Zhou: Thanks for Birthday Greetings
Author:
Date: 1950-03-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Molotov, sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai thanking him for his birthday greetings. The telegram reads:

"To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

Please accept my thanks for your friendly greetings on my birthday. Molotov."

### 莫洛托夫电周总理  感谢对他生日的祝贺

1950-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　莫洛托夫电周总理
    感谢对他生日的祝贺
    【新华社二十六日讯】苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫电谢周恩来总理对其生日的祝贺。电称：
    “中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来先生阁下：
    承你在我的生日给我友谊的祝贺，请接受我的感谢。莫洛托夫。”
