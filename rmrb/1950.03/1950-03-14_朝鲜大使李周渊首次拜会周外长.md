Title: Korean Ambassador Li Zhouyuan Pays First Visit to Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:

Date: 1950-03-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 13th: Li Zhouyuan, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, accompanied by Counselor Cui Ying, visited the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 5:30 this afternoon to pay his first visit to Minister Zhou Enlai since taking office. Also present were Qiao Guanhua, Acting Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 朝鲜大使李周渊首次拜会周外长

1950-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜大使李周渊首次拜会周外长
    【新华社北京十三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使李周渊偕参事崔英于今日下午五时半赴外交部向周恩来部长作莅任以来首次拜会。在座者尚有外交部亚洲司代司长乔冠华、交际处处长王倬如等。
