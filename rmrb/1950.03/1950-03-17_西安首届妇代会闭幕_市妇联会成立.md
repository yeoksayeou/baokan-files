Title: Xi'an First Women's Congress Concludes; Municipal Women's Federation Established
Author:
Date: 1950-03-17
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 15th. The first Xi'an Women's Congress concluded on the 11th of this month. The congress opened on International Women's Day, with 229 representatives from all ethnic groups and all walks of life attending. Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, Zhao Boping, Secretary of the Xi'an Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Zhang Zifang, Director of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Democratic Women's Federation, attended and provided guidance. The congress adopted four basic tasks for the city's women's movement: to carry out united front work in the women's movement; to mobilize women to actively participate in production and construction; to organize the broad masses of women to learn culture and science; and to closely integrate urban and rural women's work. The congress elected 38 executive committee members of the Xi'an Municipal Democratic Women's Federation, including Cao Guanqun, to form the Xi'an Municipal Women's Federation, and also elected 18 representatives to attend the first Northwest Women's Congress and 18 representatives to attend the Xi'an People's Representative Conference.

### 西安首届妇代会闭幕  市妇联会成立

1950-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　西安首届妇代会闭幕
    市妇联会成立
    【新华社西安十五日电】西安市首届妇女代表大会已于本月十一日结束。大会在“三八”节开幕，出席有各族各界妇女代表二百二十九名。西北军政委员会副主席习仲勋、中共西安市委书记赵伯平和陕甘宁边区民主妇女联合会主任张子芳等均到会指导。大会通过了目前该市妇女运动的四项基本任务：开展妇运中的统一战线工作；发动妇女群众积极参加生产建设；组织广大妇女学习文化、科学；密切结合城市和乡村的妇女工作。大会选出了曹冠群等三十八人为西安市民主妇女联合会执行委员，组成西安市妇联会，并选出了出席西北首次妇代大会的代表和出席西安市各界人民代表大会的代表各十八人。
