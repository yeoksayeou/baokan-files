Title: The Fourth People's Education Center Holds Photo Exhibition
Author: Disi Minjiao Guan Tongxun Zu (第四民教馆通讯组)
Date: 1950-03-08
Page: 4

　　The Fourth People's Education Center Holds Photo Exhibition
    [This newspaper] The Fourth People's Education Center in Beijing will hold a photo exhibition starting from the 8th. The photos are produced by the Beijing Film Studio. The content includes 197 pictures of "A Million Soldiers Crossing the Yangtze," the liberation of Nanjing and Shanghai, and the uprising of the Second Fleet of the Kuomintang Navy.
    In addition: The New Year picture exhibition during the Spring Festival in the center was extended to the 7th due to audience requests. The total number of visitors reached more than 60,000. Everyone greatly appreciated this folk art form. Some people also offered suggestions, saying, "There are too few paintings about workers," and they also hope to see pictures about the lives of the Soviet people.
                                   (Communications Group of the Fourth People's Education Center)

### 第四民教馆举办照片展览
第四民教馆通讯组
1950-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　第四民教馆举办照片展览
    【本报讯】京第四民教馆于八日起举办照片展览。照片为北京电影制片厂出品。内容计有《百万雄师下江南》，解放南京、上海，国民党海军第二舰队起义等一百九十七幅。
    又：该馆春节期间的年画展览，因观众要求，延长至七日才结束。观众共达六万余人。大家对这一民间形式的艺术非常喜爱。也有人提出意见说：“关于工人的画太少”，并且还希望展览关于苏联人民的生活的图画。
                                   （第四民教馆通讯组）
