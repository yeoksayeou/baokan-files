Title: Broadcast Programs
Author:
Date: 1950-03-28
Page: 4

　　Broadcast Programs
    [Our Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a special program on Tibet at 22:30 tomorrow (the 29th) and 8:30 the day after tomorrow: (1) The situation of the evening party held by the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government to welcome representatives of Tibetan people from Xikang; (2) An article "Our Thoughts" written by Xikang Tibetan representatives Wang Jia, Cai Liang, and Bo Zhi, to be broadcast by representative Wang Jia.
    [Our Newspaper] Today (March 28th) the Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast international current affairs at 20:20. At 20:30: Feng Youlan: "The Educational Function of Land Reform." At 20:45: Ding Haochuan: "What is Patriotic Behavior?" At 17:30, Employee Time: Preliminary experience of implementing democratic management in state-owned factories in Shijiazhuang City. At 18:30, Natural Science Knowledge Lecture: "How Paper is Made." At 18:00, First Literary and Art Program: Leting Drum "Hundred Birds Paying Homage to the Phoenix." At 21:00, Second Literary and Art Program: The Peking Opera Experimental School of the China Opera Improvement Bureau broadcasts the first part of the new Peking Opera "Yu Zhou Feng." At 18:45, New Songs: Selected songs of the working class marching forward.
    [Furthermore] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station: At 12:30, Workers' Program: Talk and Sing: Gu Rongfu and Yin Fulai jointly perform "Red Flowers and Green Leaves Help Each Other." At 13:00, Youth Program: (1) School News; (2) Recording of a speech by Leo Figuères, representative of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and Secretary-General of the Republican Youth Federation of France, to Chinese youth; (3) A quick-rhythm clapper talk by students of the Second Teachers College: "Digging Air-raid Shelters." At 16:30, Voice of the Municipality: (1) Several matters that citizens should pay attention to regarding the Beijing Municipal Finance Bureau's levy of property tax for the winter of 1949 and the spring of 1950; (2) A proclamation from the Beijing Municipal Taxation Bureau regarding the designated names and addresses of banks and firms acting as agents for collecting property tax. At 17:00, Children's Program: (1) Storytelling "Li Xiaosi Got Measles"; (2) Singing and quick-rhythm clapper talk by children from the Second Attached Elementary School of the Teachers College. At 18:45, Women's Program: A news report "The Heroic Struggle of the Laboring Women of Jiangxi." At 19:00, Russian Language Lecture.

### 广播节目

1950-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台明天（二十九日）二十二点三十分和后天八点三十分，播送对西藏的特别节目：（一）中央人民政府民族事务委员会举行晚会欢迎西康藏民代表的情形；（二）西康藏民代表汪嘉、蔡良、伯志写的一篇文章《我们的感想》，由汪嘉代表播讲。
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（二十八日）二十点二十分国际时事。二十点三十分：冯友兰：《土改的教育功用》。二十点四十五分：丁浩川《什么是爱国主义的行为？》十七点三十分职工时间：石家庄市公营工厂实行管理民主化的初步经验。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：《纸是怎样造成的》。十八点第一次文艺节目：乐亭大鼓《百鸟朝凤》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：中国戏曲改进局京剧实验学校演播新京剧《宇宙锋》第一部分。十八点四十五分新歌曲：工人阶级前进选曲。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台：十二点三十分工人节目：说说唱唱：顾荣甫、尹福来联合演唱《红花绿叶两相帮》。十三点青年节目：（一）学校新闻；（二）世界青年联盟代表，法国共和青年联盟总书记雷奥·斐盖尔对中国青年讲演的录音；（三）师大二院同学快板：《挖防空壕》。十六点三十分市政之声：（一）北京市财政局关于开征一九四九年冬季和一九五零年春季房产税市民应注意的几件事；（二）北京市税务局关于指定代收房产税款行庄名称地址的布告。十七点儿童节目：（一）讲故事《李小四长了麻子》；（二）师大第二附小小朋友们唱歌、说快板。十八点四十五分妇女节目：一篇通讯《英勇斗争的江西劳动妇女们》。十九点俄语讲座。
