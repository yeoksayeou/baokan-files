Title: "One Month of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation in China" Published in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-03-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 13th: The book "One Month of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation in China" has been published in Beijing. This book was edited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters and published by Xinhua Bookstore. The book summarizes the delegation's activities in China: They visited Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, Jinan, Shenyang, Harbin and many villages, factories, and schools; attended a total of twenty-nine mass meetings and gave speeches, with an audience of 523,200 people; gave fifty-seven special lectures and attended symposiums, with an audience of 77,200 people; and performed music and dance a total of twenty-eight times, with an audience of 583,400 people. The full texts of the general speeches, special lectures, talks and papers made by the delegates are collected in this book.

### 《苏联文化工作者代表团在中国一月》在京出版

1950-03-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　《苏联文化工作者代表团在中国一月》在京出版
    【新华社北京十三日电】《苏联文化工作者代表团在中国一月》一书已在北京出版。该书由中苏友好协会总会编辑，由新华书店印行。该书综述了代表团在中国活动的情况：他们访问了北京、天津、上海、南京、济南、沈阳、哈尔滨等城市和许多乡村、工厂、学校；共计出席群众大会二十九次，并发表了讲演，听众有五十二万三千二百余人；作专题讲演及出席座谈会五十七次，听众七万七千二百余人；音乐舞蹈演出共二十八次，观众五十八万三千四百余人。代表团各代表所作的一般讲演、专题讲演、谈话和论文的全文都收集在这本书里。
