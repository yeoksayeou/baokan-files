Title: Dai Wangshu Buried in Wan'an Cemetery, Cultural and Press Circles Gather for Memorial

Author:

Date: 1950-03-08
Page: 4

　　Dai Wangshu Buried in Wan'an Cemetery
    Cultural and Press Circles Gather for Memorial

    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th] The remains of the poet Dai Wangshu were buried today in Wan'an Cemetery in Xiangshan, Beijing. On the 5th, the International News Bureau of the Central People's Government News Administration and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles jointly held a memorial service at the News Administration Auditorium. More than one hundred people from cultural and press circles attended, including Shen Yanbing, Hu Qiaomu, Yang Hansheng, Feng Naichao, Fan Changjiang, Sa Kongliao, Ouyang Yuqian, Lao She, and Sun Fuyuan. Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council Dong Biwu, Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee Guo Moruo, and Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Lu Dingyi all sent wreaths and elegiac couplets.

### 戴望舒安葬万安公墓  文化界新闻界集会追悼

1950-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　戴望舒安葬万安公墓
    文化界新闻界集会追悼
    【新华社北京七日电】诗人戴望舒灵榇已于今日安葬于北京香山万安公墓。五日，中央人民政府新闻总署国际新闻局、全国文学艺术界联合会曾于新闻总署礼堂联合举行追悼会。到会者有沈雁冰、胡乔木、阳翰笙、冯乃超、范长江、萨空了、欧阳予倩、老舍、孙伏园等文化界新闻界人士百余人。政务院副总理董必武、文教委员会主任郭沫若、中共中央宣传部长陆定一等均送有花圈挽联。
