Title: The Third Plenary Session of the World Peace Council Standing Committee Opens; Representatives from Most Countries and International Organizations Attend; Curie Speaks, Pointing Out That World Peace Forces Are Constantly Growing
Author:
Date: 1950-03-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, March 17th: The Third Plenary Session of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Partisans for Peace opened in Stockholm, the capital of Sweden, on March 15th. One hundred and fifty representatives from most countries in the world and international organizations supporting the World Congress of Partisans for Peace have arrived here to attend the meeting.
    The opening ceremony was presided over by Ivar Bahlman, Chairman of the Swedish National Peace Committee. The famous Swedish female writer, Marika Stiernstedt, warmly welcomed all the representatives on behalf of the Swedish National Peace Committee and wished them victory in their noble work for the benefit of mankind.
    She said: Only by actively fighting for peace can the danger of a new world war be avoided.
    Then, the Chairman of the Congress invited Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Partisans for Peace, to speak. All the representatives rose to their feet and greeted this heroic fighter for international peace with stormy cheers. Joliot-Curie said: The forces of peace are constantly growing. All nations of the world do not want war. They demand the prohibition of atomic weapons, the reduction of armaments, and the conclusion of a peace pact between the great powers.
    The first day of the Third Plenary Session of the World Peace Council Standing Committee was then adjourned.

### 和大常委会三次全会开幕  世界大多数国家及国际组织代表出席会议  居里发言指出世界和平力量正在不断成长

1950-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　和大常委会三次全会开幕
    世界大多数国家及国际组织代表出席会议
    居里发言指出世界和平力量正在不断成长
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩讯：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会第三次全体会议，于三月十五日在瑞典京城斯德哥尔摩开幕。世界上大多数国家以及支持世界拥护和平大会的国际组织的一百五十名代表，均已抵达此间，出席会议。
    会议由瑞典全国和平委员会主席伊瓦·巴尔麦主持开幕。著名瑞典女作家马丽加·施林丝特代表瑞典全国和平委员会热诚欢迎全体代表，并祝他们在为人类利益而从事奋斗的崇高工作中获得胜利。
    她说：只有积极为和平而斗争，才可以避免新的世界战争的危险。
    继由大会主席敦请世界拥护和平大会常设委员会主席约里奥——居里发言。代表们全体起立，以暴风雨般的欢呼来迎接这位为国际和平而斗争的英勇斗士。约里奥——居里发言称：和平力量正在不断成长。世界各民族都不要战争，他们要求禁止原子武器、缩减军备，并缔结大国间的和平公约。
    和大常委会第三次全会的第一日会议至此宣告结束。
