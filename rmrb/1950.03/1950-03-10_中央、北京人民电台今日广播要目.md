Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-03-10
Page: 4

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (the 10th) at 20:20: (1) Broadcast an article by Xie Juezai, Minister of the Central People's Government, "We Must Defeat the Famine," (2) A letter from the famous mathematician Professor Hua Luogeng to his friends when he left the United States to return to China. 17:30 Workers' Time: (1) "Every Female Worker Must Improve Her Education and Master Technology," (2) "Study Documents and Review Work." 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: Continue broadcasting "Soviet Science." 18:00 First Literary Program: Gu Rongfu and Yin Fulai perform the card play "A Little Couple Arguing." 21:00 Second Literary Program: The North China Military Region Art Troupe broadcasts the drama "Growing Up in Battle." 18:45 New Songs: "Small Tune of the Beijing-Hankou Railway," "Division of Labor and Cooperation."
    [Also reported] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 12:30 Workers' Program: 1. Cultural Entertainment: The streetcar company's repair factory workers' choir sings. 2. Newsletter Speech: Introducing the recent production situation of the streetcar company's repair factory, "Dividing Labor to Set Plans and Improve Production." 13:00 Youth Program: (1) School News, (2) Newsletter: "The Situation of Shandong Students Participating in Production and School Construction During the Famine," (3) Cultural Entertainment: Young League members from Liulangzhuang in the Sixteenth District sing new songs and play the sheng. 16:30 Municipal Voice: (1) Continue broadcasting the Beijing People's Court's discussion of several issues in divorce cases, (2) Notice from the Beijing Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau regarding the increase in prohibited routes for animal-drawn vehicles and flatbed carts. 18:45 Listener Services: (1) Answering several questions about the Sino-Soviet loan agreement, (2) Admission brochures for the General Studies Department and Music Teacher Training Class of the Northeast Lu Xun Academy of Literature and Art, (3) Introducing the new Soviet film "Heroes Defending the Country."

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（十日）二十点二十分：（一）播送中央人民政府谢觉哉部长的一篇文章“我们一定要战胜灾荒”，（二）名数学家华罗庚教授在离美回国的时候给他朋友们的一封信。十七点三十分职工时间：（一）“每一个女工都要提高文化、掌握技术”，（二）“学习文件、检讨工作”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：继续播送“苏联的科学”。十八点第一次文艺节目：顾荣甫、尹福来演唱牌子戏“小俩口顶嘴”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：华北军区文工团演播话剧“战斗里成长”。十八点四十五分新歌曲：“京汉路小唱”、“分工合作”。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人节目：一、文化娱乐：电车公司修造厂工人歌咏队唱歌。二、通讯讲话：介绍电车公司修造厂最近的生产情形“分工订计划，提高生产”。十三点青年节目：（一）学校新闻，（二）通讯：“山东学生在灾荒中参加生产建校的情形”，（三）文化娱乐：十六区六郎庄青年团员演唱新歌曲和吹笙。十六点三十分市政之声：（１）继续播送北京市人民法院谈离婚案件中的几个问题，（２）北京市人民政府公安局关于增加禁止兽力车和排子车通行路线的通告。十八点四十五分听众服务：（一）回答关于中苏贷款协定中的几个问题，（二）东北鲁迅文艺学院普通部和音乐师资训练班的招生简章，（三）介绍苏联新影片“双雄护国”。
