Title: Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation, Our Delegation Leaves Beijing for Hungary
Author:
Date: 1950-03-14
Page: 1

　　Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation
    Our Delegation Leaves Beijing for Hungary
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th】The members of our delegation to celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of the Liberation of Hungary on April 4th, including Xu Liqun and ten others, left Beijing for Budapest at 9 p.m. today. Those who went to the station to see them off included Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Feikert, Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy.
    The list of the Chinese delegation to celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of the Liberation of Hungary is as follows: Head of the delegation: Bian Zhangwu (Major General Military Attaché of the Embassy in the Soviet Union), Members: Xu Liqun (Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League), Ma Xiaodi (worker), Li Yonglu (worker), Wang Laowu (worker), Feng Zhi (professor), Hua Junwu (cartoonist), Li Guoying (Youth Federation), Bai Yun (Women's Federation), Qu Duyi (Women's Federation).

### 庆祝匈牙利解放五周年  我代表团离京赴匈

1950-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝匈牙利解放五周年
    我代表团离京赴匈
    【新华社北京十三日电】庆祝四月四日匈牙利解放五周年纪念之我国代表团团员许立群等一行十人，已于本日下午九时离京前往布达佩斯。赴车站欢送者有中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬，中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际部副部长廖梦醒，外交部交际处处长王倬如及匈牙利公使馆参事费凯尔特等。
    庆祝匈牙利解放五周年纪念，中国代表团名单如下：团长：边章五（驻苏大使馆中将武官），团员：许立群（北京市青年团工委书记）、马小弟（工人）、李永禄（工人）、王老五（工人）、冯至（教授）、华君武（漫画家）、李国英（青联）、白芸（妇联）、瞿独伊（妇联）。
