Title: Vietnamese French Authorities Continue to Persecute Overseas Chinese
Author:
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 29th: TASS reported on the 28th, citing a Vietnam News Agency dispatch: The French authorities in Vietnam detained hundreds of overseas Chinese in mid-February, accusing them of protesting the French authorities' harboring of defeated Kuomintang army remnants and celebrating the victory of the Chinese people and the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance and various agreements. In February alone, French authorities arrested more than 300 overseas Chinese in the vicinity of Haiphong.

### 越南法国当局继续迫害华侨

1950-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南法国当局继续迫害华侨
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社二十八日引越南通讯社消息：越南法国当局在二月中旬拘禁了数以百计的华侨，罪名是他们抗议法国当局收留被击败的国民党军残余，和欢庆中国人民的胜利及中苏友好同盟互助条约与各项协定的签订。在二月份，法国当局仅在海防附近，即逮捕华侨三百名以上。
