Title: Beijing Department Store Cancels Rest Day, Open as Usual on Mondays
Author: Wei Daben, Liu Xiaofeng
Date: 1950-03-19
Page: 4

　　Beijing Department Store Cancels Rest Day, Open as Usual on Mondays
    [This Newspaper's News] In order to supply the general public with daily merchandise at low prices at any time and reduce intermediate exploitation, the Beijing Branch of the North China Department Store Company has cancelled its rest day and will be open as usual on Mondays.
        (Wei Daben, Liu Xiaofeng)

### 北京百货公司取消休息日星期一照常营业
魏在本　刘晓峰
1950-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京百货公司取消休息日星期一照常营业
    【本报讯】华北百货公司北京分公司为了随时廉价供应广大市民日常百货用品，减少中间剥削，取消休息日，星期一照常营业。
        （魏大本·刘晓峰）
