Title: Youth Service Department Public Affairs Lecture, Lecture 2: Liao Gailong Lectures on "The Inseparable Sino-Soviet Alliance"
Author:
Date: 1950-03-10
Page: 4

　　Youth Service Department Public Affairs Lecture
    Lecture 2: Liao Gailong Lectures on "The Inseparable Sino-Soviet Alliance"
    [Our Newspaper] The Youth Service Department's public affairs lecture, the second lecture will be held at 4 PM on the 11th of this month (Saturday) at No. 19 Nanheyan Street (Soviet Foreign Culture Association Beijing Office). Xinhua News Agency's Liao Gailong will lecture on: "The Inseparable Sino-Soviet Alliance." A film will be shown after the meeting. Friends of the department and students of the department are welcome to attend. Organizations and groups can also contact the department with a letter of introduction to obtain admission tickets.

### 青年服务部大众时事讲座  第二讲：廖盖隆主讲“不可分离的中苏同盟”

1950-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　青年服务部大众时事讲座
    第二讲：廖盖隆主讲“不可分离的中苏同盟”
    【本报讯】青年服务部大众时事讲座第二讲于本月十一日（星期六）下午四时在南河沿十九号（苏联对外文化协会北京办事处）举行。请新华社廖盖隆主讲：“不可分离的中苏同盟”。会后放映电影。欢迎部友及该部学员听讲，机关团体亦可凭介绍信前往该部接洽，领取入座券。
