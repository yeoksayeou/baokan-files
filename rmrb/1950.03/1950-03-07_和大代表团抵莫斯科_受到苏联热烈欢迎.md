Title: World Peace Congress Delegation Arrives in Moscow, Receives Warm Soviet Welcome
Author:
Date: 1950-03-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 6th: TASS, Moscow, March 5th: A delegation from the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace arrived in Moscow on the 5th. The delegation, composed of members of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace and prominent social leaders from France, Italy, Britain, the United States, and other countries, will present an appeal for the defense of peace to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

The delegation members include: Farge, President of the French Association for Freedom and Peace; De La Vequiere, Member of the French National Assembly; Lucien Jayat, Secretary of the French General Confederation of Labor; Darlan, a French social leader; Pedini, Senator of the Italian Upper House and Manager of Avanti!; Platts-Mills, Member of the British Peace Committee and Chairman of the National Council of the British-Soviet Friendship Society; Steel, a famous American progressive journalist; Kent, a famous American artist; Roaida, an Italian women's leader; and Wen Youzhang, Chairman of the Canadian Peace Committee, among others.

Those who went to the Central Airport to welcome them included: Leonov, Representative of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR; Shostakovich, Representative of the Soviet Peace Committee; representatives from the Soviet Slavic Committee, the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Soviet Writers' Union, and Moscow journalists.

The delegation received a warm welcome. Leonov, Representative of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and writer, delivered a welcoming speech. Farge responded, saying: "As we set foot on Moscow soil and shake hands with our Soviet friends, we wish to convey our respect and gratitude to all the Soviet people. Long live Moscow! Long live peace!"

### 和大代表团抵莫斯科  受到苏联热烈欢迎

1950-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　和大代表团抵莫斯科
    受到苏联热烈欢迎
    【新华社北京六日电】塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会代表团于五日抵莫斯科。代表团由世界拥护和平大会常设委员会委员和法、意、英、美诸国著名的社会领袖组成，将向苏联最高苏维埃提出保卫和平的文告。
    代表团团员包括：法国争和平自由协会主席法奇，法国国民议会议员德·拉·魏基利，法国总工会书记吕鲜·雅雅，法国社会领袖达尔朗，意大利上议员与前进报经理裴丁尼，英国和平委员会委员及英苏友好协会全国理事会主席普拉茨——米尔斯，美国著名进步记者斯蒂尔，美国名艺术家肯特，意大利妇女领袖罗爱达以及加拿大和平委员会主席文幼章等人。
    前往中央机场欢迎的有：苏联最高苏维埃代表李昂诺夫，苏联拥护和平委员会代表萧斯塔科维奇，苏联斯拉夫委员会，苏联妇女反法西斯委员会，苏联青年反法西斯委员会，苏联对外文化协会，苏联作家协会及莫斯科新闻界的代表。
    代表团受到热烈的欢迎。苏联最高苏维埃代表、作家李昂诺夫代表致欢迎辞。法奇致答辞称：“在我们踏上莫斯科的土地并和苏联朋友们握手时，我们愿向全体苏联人民转达我们的敬意与感谢。莫斯科万岁！和平万岁！”
