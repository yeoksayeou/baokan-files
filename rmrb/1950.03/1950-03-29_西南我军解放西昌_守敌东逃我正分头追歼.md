Title: Southwestern People's Liberation Army Liberates Xichang; Enemy Troops Flee Eastward, We Are Dividing Forces to Pursue and Annihilate
Author:
Date: 1950-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, March 28th: A unit of the main force of the Southwest Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, which was attacking the remnant bandits in the Xichang area, liberated Xichang city on the 27th. The defending enemy fled eastward, and we are dividing our forces to pursue and annihilate them. On their march to Xichang, the People's Liberation Army strictly observed mass discipline and minority nationality policies, and was warmly welcomed by Yi compatriots in various places along the way. The Yi compatriots cooperated with the People's Liberation Army in intercepting Liu Menglian's troops, remnants of Hu He's bandits, south of Zhaojue.

### 西南我军解放西昌  守敌东逃我正分头追歼

1950-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　西南我军解放西昌
    守敌东逃我正分头追歼
    【新华社重庆二十八日讯】进击西昌地区残匪之人民解放军西南军区某主力部队一部，已于二十七日解放西昌城。守敌向东逃窜，我正分头追歼中。人民解放军向西昌进军途中，严格遵守群众纪律和少数民族政策，受到沿途各地彝族同胞的热烈欢迎。彝族同胞曾配合人民解放军在昭觉以南截击胡贺残匪刘孟廉部。
