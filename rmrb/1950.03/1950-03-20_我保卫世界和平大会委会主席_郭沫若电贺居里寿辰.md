Title: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Sends Congratulations to Joliot-Curie on His Birthday
Author:
Date: 1950-03-20
Page: 1
　　
    Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace,
    Sends Congratulations to Joliot-Curie on His Birthday

    [Xinhua News Agency, 19th] Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, today sent a telegram to Professor Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the Permanent Committee of the World Peace Congress, on his 50th birthday. The telegram reads:
    Professor Joliot-Curie:
    I am delighted to extend my congratulations on the occasion of your 50th birthday, and express my deep respect for your dedicated efforts in the cause of defending world peace.

  Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
      Chairman Guo Moruo
      March 19, 1950

### 我保卫世界和平大会委会主席  郭沫若电贺居里寿辰

1950-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　我保卫世界和平大会委会主席
    郭沫若电贺居里寿辰
    【新华社十九日讯】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会主席郭沫若，本日电贺世界拥护和平大会常设委员会主席约里奥·居里五十寿辰。电文如下：约里奥·居里教授：
    欣逢阁下五十大寿，谨致祝贺，并对阁下在保卫世界和平斗争事业中的献身的努力，致深厚的敬意。
  中国保卫世界和平大会委员会
      主席  郭沫若
      一九五○年三月十九日
