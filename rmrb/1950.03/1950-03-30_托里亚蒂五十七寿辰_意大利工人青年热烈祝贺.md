Title: Togliatti's Fifty-Seventh Birthday: Italian Workers and Youth Extend Warm Congratulations

Author:

Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 27th. The Telecommunications Agency in Rome reported on the 27th that the workers in various regions of Italy extended warm congratulations on the occasion of the 57th birthday of Comrade Togliatti, the General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, on the 26th.

The Communist Youth League's congratulatory message stated: "Comrade Togliatti, we wish you continued guidance in our struggles in the years to come. We extend to you heartfelt congratulations, which are also the congratulations of all democratic youth, for you are their friend, advisor, defender, and reliable guide."

### 托里亚蒂五十七寿辰  意大利工人青年热烈祝贺

1950-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　托里亚蒂五十七寿辰
    意大利工人青年热烈祝贺
    【新华社二十九日讯】电通社罗马二十七日讯：二十六日为意大利共产党总书记托里亚蒂五十七寿辰，意大利各地工人致以热烈的祝贺。
    共产主义青年团的贺电称：“托里亚蒂同志，祝您在未来的岁月中继续指导我们的斗争。谨向您致以发自我们心坎深处的祝贺，这祝贺同时也是所有民主青年的祝贺，您是他们的朋友、忠告人、保卫者与可靠的指导者。”
