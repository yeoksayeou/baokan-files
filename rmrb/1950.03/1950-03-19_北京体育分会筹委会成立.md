Title: Preparatory Committee for Beijing Sports Association Established
Author:
Date: 1950-03-19
Page: 4

　　[Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The preparatory committee for the Beijing branch of the All-China Sports Federation has been established. The committee held its first plenary meeting on the 17th at the Bureau of Culture and Education, attended by thirteen preparatory committee members. At the meeting, Hou Junyan was elected as director, and Zhang Dazhong and Li Fenglou as deputy directors. A secretariat, a member qualification review group, and a proposal research and collation group were established under the committee. The preparatory committee also discussed the method for generating representatives for the inaugural meeting of the branch, as well as the draft of the association's charter. The date of the inaugural meeting is tentatively scheduled for early April.

### 北京体育分会筹委会成立

1950-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京体育分会筹委会成立
    【京市新闻处讯】中华全国体育总会北京市分会筹委会已经成立。该会于十七日在文教局举行第一次全体会议，出席筹委十三人。会上推出侯俊岩为主任，张大中、李凤楼为副主任。下设秘书处，代表资格审查组及提案研究整理组。筹委会并讨论了分会成立大会代表之产生办法，及会章草案。成立大会日期暂定于四月上旬。
