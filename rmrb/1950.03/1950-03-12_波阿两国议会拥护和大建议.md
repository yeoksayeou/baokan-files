Title: Polish and Albanian Parliaments Endorse World Peace Congress Proposals

Author:

Date: 1950-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 11th. TASS reports: The parliaments of Poland and Albania have recently passed resolutions endorsing the peace proposals of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. The Polish parliament unanimously passed a resolution on March 7th, fully endorsing the proposals of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. The resolution specifically called for the immediate cessation of the criminal bombing of free China's towns and villages by Chiang Kai-shek fascist pilots flying American-made bombers, the cessation of interference in the wars against the peoples of Vietnam and Malaya, the cessation of suppression of peace advocates, and the conclusion of a five-power peace pact among the United States, Britain, France, China, and the Soviet Union within the framework of the United Nations. Following the passage of the above resolution, the parliament held a mass rally in front of the parliament building, attended by approximately 30,000 people from Warsaw. The participants also demonstrated in the streets of Warsaw, shouting: "Down with the warmongers!" "Long live world peace!" "Long live Comrade Stalin, the leader of the world peace camp!"

The Albanian National Assembly recently passed a resolution stating: The Albanian people deeply hate the new war instigators – the American and British imperialists and the Belgrade henchmen and Athens royalist fascists who carry out their orders and provoke our borders. The Albanian people stand firmly in the powerful camp of peace and socialism, responding to the enemy's provocations with heroic resistance. The National Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, representing the will and aspirations of the people, unanimously endorses the peace proposals put forward by the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress to all parliaments.

### 波阿两国议会拥护和大建议

1950-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　波阿两国议会拥护和大建议
    【新华社北京十一日电】综合塔斯社消息：波兰与阿尔巴尼亚议会最近分别通过世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的和平建议。波兰议会于三月七日一致通过决议，完全拥护和大常委会的建议。决议特别提出立即停止蒋介石法西斯驾驶员驾驶美造轰炸机对自由中国的城镇与乡村的罪恶轰炸，停止对越南与马来亚人民的干涉战争，停止镇压和平拥护者，并在联合国机构内，缔结美、英、法、中、苏五国和平公约。议会在通过上述决议后，即在议会大厦前举行了约有三万华沙人民参加的群众大会。与会者并在华沙的街上示威，高呼：“打倒战争贩子！”“世界和平万岁！”“世界和平阵营的领袖斯大林同志万岁！”
    阿尔巴尼亚国民议会最近通过决议称：阿尔巴尼亚人民异常憎恨新战争挑拨者——美英帝国主义和执行他们命令向我国边境进行挑衅的贝尔格莱德走狗及雅典保皇法西斯。阿尔巴尼亚人民坚定地站在强大的和平与社会主义阵营中，以英勇的抵抗来答复敌人的挑衅行为。阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国民议会，代表人民的意志与愿望，一致拥护和大常委会向各国议会提出的和平建议。
