Title: Hungarian Envoy Sándor Haraszti Pays First Visit to Foreign Minister Zhou
Author:
Date: 1950-03-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 15th: Sándor Haraszti, Envoy of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, paid his first visit to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 5:00 PM today since assuming his post. Also present were Ke Bonian, Acting Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 匈公使夏法朗柯首次拜会周外长

1950-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈公使夏法朗柯首次拜会周外长
    【新华社十五日讯】匈牙利人民共和国驻我国公使夏法朗柯，于今日下午五时赴外交部向周恩来部长作莅任以来首次拜会，在座者尚有外交部苏联东欧司代司长柯柏年，交际处处长王倬如等。
