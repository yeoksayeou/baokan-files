Title: North Korean Wonsan Overseas Chinese Celebrate the Sino-Soviet Treaty with a Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1950-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 4th: The entire overseas Chinese community in Wonsan, North Korea, held a meeting on February 18th to celebrate the signing of the new Sino-Soviet treaty and sent a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

After the news of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and the other two agreements on February 14th arrived, all of us overseas Chinese were overjoyed and, with the greatest enthusiasm, convened a celebration meeting on February 18th. We unanimously believe that this treaty and the two agreements have consolidated the profound friendship between China and the Soviet Union, not only ensuring peace in the Far East, but also playing a huge role in defending the happiness of mankind in the world. We overseas Chinese firmly support this treaty and will strive for its realization.

### 北朝鲜元山市华侨庆贺中苏订约电毛主席致敬

1950-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜元山市华侨庆贺中苏订约电毛主席致敬
    【新华社北京四日电】北朝鲜元山市全体华侨二月十八日集会庆祝中苏新约的签订，并电毛主席致敬，电文如下：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东钧鉴：二月十四日中苏友好同盟互助条约及其他两个协定签订的消息传来以后，我们华侨莫不欢欣鼓舞，都以最大的热情，在二月十八日召开庆祝大会，一致认为这一条约和两个协定是巩固了中苏深厚的友谊，不但使远东和平有了保障，而且对保卫世界人类幸福也起了巨大作用。我们华侨坚决拥护这一条约，并为其实现而奋斗。
