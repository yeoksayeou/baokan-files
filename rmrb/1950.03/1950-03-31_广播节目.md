　　Broadcast Programs
　　[This newspaper reports] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (March 31st) at 20:20, International Current Affairs. 20:30: (1) News: A certain corps of North China begins large-scale production; (2) Heroic Story: "Commander Chen Reclaims Nanniwan." 17:30 Workers' Program: "The Revolution in the National Coal Mine Management System." 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: "Total Lunar Eclipse on April 3rd." 18:00 First Literary and Art Program: Wang Xiuying sings Jingyun Dagu "Daughter Hero Wang Guixiang." 21:00 Second Literary and Art Program: China Youth Art Theater performs the Yangko Opera "Land Returns Home." 18:45 New Songs.
　　[Another report] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 12:30 Workers' Program: 1. Culture and Entertainment: The 11th District Trade Union Workers' Night School Choir sings; 2. News Speech: Ding Mengdong of the Telecommunications Bureau speaks on "The Beijing Telecommunications Bureau Launches a Production Competition to Celebrate the Success of the First National Congress of the China Telecommunications Workers' Union." 13:00 Youth Program: (1) School News, (2) A letter from the Young Pioneers of the No. 42 Middle School in Gorky City, Soviet Union, to Chinese classmates, (3) Students from the Second Municipal Middle School sing songs. 16:30 Voice of the Municipality: (1) Interim Measures for the Handling of Ownerless Real Estate in Urban Areas by the Beijing Municipal People's Government, (2) Report on Beijing Construction Bureau's Call to Assist in Greening the Capital. 17:00 Children's Program: Telling the story of Wang Xiaosan and the Ascaris. 18:45 Listener Service: (1) Answering questions about loans in Beijing and Dalian, (2) Notice from the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau regarding unclaimed telegrams, (3) Recruitment advertisement from the Inner Mongolia Daily Printing Factory for technical workers, (4) Recruitment advertisement from the No. 1 Branch Factory of the Agricultural Machinery Factory for technicians. 19:00 Russian Language Lecture.

### 广播节目

1950-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（卅一日）二十点二十分国际时事。二十点三十分：（一）通讯：华北某兵团开始进行大生产；（二）英雄故事：“陈团长开荒南泥湾”。十七点三十分职工节目：“全国煤矿管理制度的革命”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：“四月三日月全食”。十八点第一次文艺节目：王秀英演唱京韵大鼓“女儿英雄王桂香”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：中国青年艺术剧院演播秧歌剧“土地还家”。十八点四十五分新歌曲。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人节目：一、文化娱乐：十一区工会工人夜校合唱团唱歌；二、通讯讲话：电信局丁孟冬讲“北京电信局展开生产竞赛庆祝中国电信工会第一次全国代表大会成功”。十三点青年节目：（一）学校新闻，（二）苏联高尔基城第四十二中学的青年团员给中国同学的一封信，（三）市立二中同学演唱歌曲。十六点三十分市政之声：（一）北京市人民政府处理城区无主房地暂行办法，（二）北京市建设局关于号召协助绿化首都工作的报导。十七点儿童节目：讲王小三闹蛔虫的故事。十八点四十五分听众服务：（一）回答北京和大连贷款的问题，（二）北京电信局招领几件电报的通知，（三）内蒙古日报社印刷厂招用技术工人的启事，（四）农业机械厂第一分厂招考技工的启事。十九点俄语讲座。
