Title: World Democratic Youth Federation Welcomes World Youth Week, Calls on Youth Worldwide to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-03-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, March 16th (TASS dispatch): The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth has just issued a letter to peace-loving youth to the press on the upcoming World Youth Week, stating: From March 21st to March 28th, millions of young men and women will declare their determination to do everything in their power to fight the warmongers in order to ensure the happiness and development of the younger generation. The letter emphasizes that it is necessary to follow the example of many European workers who refuse to load, unload, or transport war materials, and to respond to the war plans of the warmongers with concrete actions. The letter concludes by calling on youth worldwide to join the ranks of heroic peace fighters to defend peace.

### 世界民主青联迎接世界青年周  号召全世界青年保卫和平

1950-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界民主青联迎接世界青年周
    号召全世界青年保卫和平
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社巴黎十六日讯：世界民主青年联盟书记处，顷就即将来临的世界青年周向报界发表告青年和平拥护者书称：从三月二十一日到三月二十八日，数以百万计的青年男女将宣布他们的决心，要竭尽全力与战争贩子们进行斗争，以期确保年青一代的幸福与发展。该书强调指出：必须效法许多欧洲工人拒绝起卸或运输战争物资的范例，而以具体行动来回答战争贩子的战争计划。该书最后号召全世界青年，参加英勇的和平斗士的队伍以保卫和平。
