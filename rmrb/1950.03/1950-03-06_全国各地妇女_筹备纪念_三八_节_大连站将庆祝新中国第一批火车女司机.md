Title: Women Across the Country Prepare to Commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day; Dalian Station to Celebrate the First Female Train Drivers of New China
Author:
Date: 1950-03-06
Page: 3

Women Across the Country
    Prepare to Commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day
    Dalian Station to Celebrate the First Female Train Drivers of New China
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 5th: Preparations for commemorating "March 8th" International Women's Day are underway across the country. The Lüda District Democratic Women's Federation will host a gathering on the evening of the 8th for women from China, the Soviet Union, Korea, Japan, and other countries. The first female train drivers of New China, Tian Guiying, Wang Baohong, and Bi Guiying, have now passed their examinations and will drive the "March 8th" locomotive from Dalian to Lüshun on the 8th. To celebrate this historic achievement for Chinese women, a grand ceremony will be held at Dalian Railway Station on the afternoon of the 8th. The main focus for women's commemoration of "March 8th" in various provinces of North China is to promote and mobilize women to participate in large-scale production. The Pingyuan Provincial Women's Federation has called on women in disaster areas to assist men in production for self-relief, introduce methods for surviving the famine, and commend exemplary women in famine relief. In Baotou and Guisui, Suiyuan Province, exhibitions of pictorial films on maternal and child health, women's labor, etc., are also being prepared. In a notice to various localities regarding the commemoration of "March 8th," the Fujian Provincial Women's Work Committee called on women throughout the province to commemorate "March 8th" with practical actions to actively support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan. Women's organizations in other regions such as the Southwest Region, Hubei Province, Sunan, and cities such as Chongqing, Suzhou, and Changzhou are also actively preparing for the arrival of "March 8th."

### 全国各地妇女  筹备纪念“三八”节  大连站将庆祝新中国第一批火车女司机

1950-03-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国各地妇女
    筹备纪念“三八”节
    大连站将庆祝新中国第一批火车女司机
    【新华社北京五日电】纪念“三八”妇女节的筹备工作已在全国各地展开。旅大区民主妇联将于八日晚召集我国和苏联、朝鲜、日本等国的妇女联欢会。新中国的第一批火车女司机田桂英、王宝鸿、毕桂英三人现已考试合格，八日将驾驶“三八”号机车由大连开往旅顺。为庆祝这一中国妇女历史上的创举，八日下午大连火车站将有盛大的典礼举行。华北各省妇女纪念“三八”节的主要内容是宣传动员妇女参加大生产。平原省妇联号召灾区妇女协助男子生产自救，并介绍渡荒办法，表扬渡荒模范妇女。绥远省包头、归绥等地并准备举行妇婴卫生、妇女劳动等图画影片展览。福建省妇女工作委员会在给各地的关于纪念“三八”节的通知中，号召全省妇女以积极支援解放军解放台湾的实际行动来纪念“三八”节。其他如西南区、湖北省、苏南等地区及重庆、苏州、常州等市妇女团体，亦正积极筹备纪念“三八”节日的来临。
