Title: The Fifth Issue of "People's China" Published
Author:
Date: 1950-03-08
Page: 3

　　[This News] The fifth issue of "People's China" has been published. The contents include the English translation of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance and two agreements, Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou's speech, and Xinhua News Agency's editorial "A New Era of Sino-Soviet Friendly Cooperation." Regarding the Sino-Soviet alliance, there is also the editorial "The Sino-Soviet Friendship is Indestructible," Qian Junrui's "Let the Imperialists Fight Before Us," Xiao San's "Five Weeks of the Soviet Cultural Delegation in China," an introduction to the historical facts of the China Changchun Railway, and a chronology of Sino-Soviet relations. There are also detailed comprehensive reports on the atrocities of the Japanese invaders' germ warfare and the Chiang Kai-shek bandits' bombing of Shanghai. This issue has been increased by four pages.

### 《人民中国》第五期出版

1950-03-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　《人民中国》第五期出版
    【本报讯】《人民中国》第五期业已出版。内容包括该社英译的中苏友好同盟互助条约和两个协定，周总理兼外长的演说和新华社社论《中苏友好合作的新时代》。有关中苏盟约的，并有社论《中苏友谊坚不可摧》，钱俊瑞的《让帝国主义者在我们面前战斗吧》，萧三的《苏联文化代表团在中国的五个星期》，中长路史实的介绍和中苏关系的年表等。关于日寇细菌战和蒋匪轰炸上海的暴行，也有详细的综合报导。这一期篇幅增加了四页。
