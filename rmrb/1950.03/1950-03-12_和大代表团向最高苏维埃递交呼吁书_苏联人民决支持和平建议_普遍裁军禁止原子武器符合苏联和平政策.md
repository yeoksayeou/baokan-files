Title: World Peace Congress Delegation Submits Appeal to Supreme Soviet; Soviet People Resolved to Support Peace Proposals; Universal Disarmament and Banning of Atomic Weapons Consistent with Soviet Peace Policy
Author:
Date: 1950-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow dispatch: The delegation of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress submitted the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress to the chairmen of the two chambers of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. On the afternoon of the 8th, at 3 o'clock, the Chairman of the Soviet of the Union, V.V. Parfenov, and the Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, V.V. Kuznetsov, met the delegation of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress in the Kremlin. Representatives from the Soviet Peace Protection Committee, the Soviet Writers' Union, the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, the Soviet Slavic Committee, the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Soviet press also participated. In addition, reporters from foreign newspapers and news agencies attended the meeting.

The Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, Kuznetsov, delivered a brief opening speech, stating that the appeal submitted by the Standing Committee to the parliaments of various countries is a powerful contribution to the cause of safeguarding lasting and solid peace, and has won the most enthusiastic support from all honest people and those governments that are genuinely, not just verbally, committed to consolidating peace.

The head of the World Peace Congress delegation, Mr. Fache, chairman of the French Peace and Freedom Fighters Association, delivered a speech amidst enthusiastic applause. Fache pointed out that if the parliaments of various countries adopt the two main proposals of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress – stopping the arms race and banning atomic weapons – it would be possible to ease the tension in international relations. Fache handed over the World Peace Congress Standing Committee's appeal to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to Kuznetsov.

Delegation members, Platts-Mills from Britain, Roach from the United States, Russo from Italy, and Wen Youzhang from Canada, successively delivered brief speeches.

The Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, Kuznetsov, then gave a speech, expressing heartfelt gratitude for their expression of friendship towards the peace-loving Soviet people, and read the reply from the Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to the delegation of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. The reply emphasized that the proposals for universal disarmament and the banning of atomic weapons put forward by the Standing Committee would be fully understood and supported by the Soviet people, and that these proposals are in line with the Soviet Union's consistent desire to consolidate world peace. The reply also stated that the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress will be submitted to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union for consideration. Kuznetsov handed over the reply to the head of the delegation, Fache, amidst applause from the audience.

### 和大代表团向最高苏维埃递交呼吁书  苏联人民决支持和平建议  普遍裁军禁止原子武器符合苏联和平政策

1950-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　和大代表团向最高苏维埃递交呼吁书
    苏联人民决支持和平建议
    普遍裁军禁止原子武器符合苏联和平政策
    【新华社北京十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会代表团向苏联最高苏维埃两院主席，递交世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的呼吁书。联盟院主席巴非诺夫与民族院主席库兹涅佐夫，于八日下午三时在克里姆林宫接见世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的代表团。苏联拥护和平委员会、苏联作家协会、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会、苏联青年反法西斯委员会、苏联斯拉夫委员会、苏联对外文化协会及苏联报界均派代表多人参加。此外，出席接见会者尚有外国报纸和通讯社的记者。
    苏联民族院主席库兹涅佐夫致简短的开会词说：常设委员会决定派代表团向各国议会提出的呼吁书，是对保障持久与巩固和平事业的有力贡献，并且博得一切正直的人民和那些在实际上而不是口头上致力巩固和平的政府最热烈的支持。
    和大代表团团长、法国和平自由战士协会主席法奇在热烈欢迎声中发表讲演。法奇指出：各国议会采纳世界和大常委会的两点主要建议——停止军备竞赛与禁止原子武器，就有可能缓和国际关系上的紧张局面。法奇将世界和大常委会致苏联最高苏维埃的呼吁书递交库兹涅佐夫。
    代表团员，英国的普拉茨——米尔斯、美国的罗奇、意大利的鲁索、及加拿大的文幼章等人，相继发表简短演说。
    苏联民族院主席库兹涅佐夫继起致词，衷心感谢他们对爱好和平的苏联人民的友谊表示，并宣读苏联最高苏维埃联盟院与民族院致世界拥护和平大会常设委员会代表团的复文。该复文强调称：常设委员会所提普遍裁减军备与禁止原子武器的提议，将获得苏联人民的充分了解与支持，这些提议是符合苏联巩固世界和平这一始终不渝的愿望的。复文又称，世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的呼吁书，将被提交苏联最高苏维埃考虑。库兹涅佐夫在全场掌声中将该复文递交代表团团长法奇。
