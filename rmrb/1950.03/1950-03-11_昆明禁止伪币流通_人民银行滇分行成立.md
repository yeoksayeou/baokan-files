Title: Kunming Bans Circulation of Counterfeit Currency; People's Bank of China Yunnan Branch Established
Author:
Date: 1950-03-11
Page: 2

　　Kunming Bans Circulation of Counterfeit Currency
    People's Bank of China Yunnan Branch Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 10th] The Kunming Military Control Commission announced on the 8th that the Renminbi is the only legal currency. It is stipulated that counterfeit silver dollar notes and local currency are prohibited from circulation from this date. The Yunnan Branch of the People's Bank of China was also officially established here on the 8th.

### 昆明禁止伪币流通  人民银行滇分行成立

1950-03-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　昆明禁止伪币流通
    人民银行滇分行成立
    【新华社昆明十日电】昆明市军管会于八日明令宣布人民币为唯一合法货币。规定自即日起伪银元券及地方币均禁止流通。中国人民银行云南省分行亦于八日在此间正式成立。
