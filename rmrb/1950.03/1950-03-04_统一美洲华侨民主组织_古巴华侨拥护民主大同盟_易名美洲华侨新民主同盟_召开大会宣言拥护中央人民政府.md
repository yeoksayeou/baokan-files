Title: Unified Overseas Chinese Democratic Organizations in the Americas / Cuban Overseas Chinese Alliance for Democracy and Great Unity renamed as the Overseas Chinese New Democratic Alliance in the Americas / Held a Congress and Declared Support for the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1950-03-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st - It has been learned here that the "Cuban Overseas Chinese Alliance for Democracy and Great Unity," with a history of over twenty years, held its Seventh Congress of All-Cuba Representatives from January 18th to 20th this year in Santiago de Cuba. To coordinate with the development of the motherland's revolutionary situation and to unify overseas Chinese democratic organizations in the Americas, the congress resolved to rename the alliance as the "Overseas Chinese New Democratic Alliance in the Americas" and established a preparatory committee. Once preparations are complete, a central executive committee will be established as the highest leading body of the alliance. On the last day of the congress, the alliance's new charter and congress declaration were adopted, and telegrams of respect were sent to Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the People's Liberation Army. The congress declaration stated: We solemnly declare to our compatriots in the motherland, to overseas compatriots, and to the world: We resolutely support the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, led by Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, in building a New Democratic China. We also firmly express our opposition to wars of aggression, and unite with the people of all countries who love democracy and peace, to support lasting world peace. The declaration also stated: Our future guiding principle is to unite and mobilize overseas Chinese democratic organizations in various American countries, wholeheartedly support the People's Republic of China, implement the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and safeguard the interests of overseas Chinese throughout the Americas.

### 统一美洲华侨民主组织  古巴华侨拥护民主大同盟  易名美洲华侨新民主同盟  召开大会宣言拥护中央人民政府

1950-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　统一美洲华侨民主组织
    古巴华侨拥护民主大同盟  易名美洲华侨新民主同盟
    召开大会宣言拥护中央人民政府
    【新华社北京一日电】此间获悉：有二十余年历史的“旅古巴华侨拥护民主大同盟”，于今年一月十八日至二十日，在汕妲古（圣地牙哥）埠召开第七届全古代表大会。为配合祖国革命形势的发展，并进行统一美洲华侨民主组织，大会决议将该盟易名为“美洲华侨新民主同盟”，并产生了筹备委员会，一俟筹备就绪，即成立中央执行委员会，作为该盟最高领导机构。大会最后一日通过该盟新章程及大会宣言，并分电中央人民政府毛主席、朱总司令暨人民解放军致敬。大会宣言称：我们向祖国的同胞，向国外的侨胞，向全世界庄严地宣告：我们坚决拥护中华人民共和国中央人民政府，在毛主席和中国共产党领导下，建设新民主主义的中国。我们并坚决表示反对侵略战争，联合世界各国爱好民主和平的人民，拥护世界永久和平。宣言又称：我们今后的工作指针，是统一和发动美洲各国华侨民主组织，全心全意拥护中华人民共和国，实行人民政协的共同纲领，保卫全美洲华侨的利益。
