Title: Demanding a Comprehensive Peace Treaty and the Establishment of a Democratic Japan: Japanese Communist Party Puts Forward the Slogan of a Democratic National United Front: Opposing War, the Colonization and Militarization of Japan
Author: 
Date: 1950-03-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th: Following the Japanese Communist Party's declaration on the 24th, calling on Japanese democratic classes to unite and establish a democratic national united front, it put forward slogans for the democratic national united front on the 25th. According to reports from Reuters and Kyodo News Agency, Tokyo, on the 27th, the slogans are as follows:

　　1. In accordance with the Potsdam Declaration, conclude a comprehensive peace treaty with Japan; after the treaty is signed, promptly withdraw all occupying forces.

　　2. Oppose war, oppose the colonization and militarization of Japan. Uphold peace, and cooperate with peace-loving peoples of all countries in a friendly manner.

　　3. Oppose colonial slave life. Guarantee basic human rights. Develop peaceful industries without restriction, and conduct trade independently with the Soviet Union, China, and Korea.

　　4. Oppose the revival of Japanese imperialism. Overthrow the Emperor system and the traitorous, reactionary Yoshida Cabinet.

　　5. Unite the united front; all patriotic people should participate in the democratic national united front. Achieve national independence and establish a people's democratic government.

### 要求全面缔和建立民主的日本  日共提出民主民族战线口号  反对战争、反对日本殖民地化和军事基地化

1950-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　要求全面缔和建立民主的日本
    日共提出民主民族战线口号
    反对战争、反对日本殖民地化和军事基地化
    【新华社三十日讯】日本共产党在二十四日发表宣言，号召日本民主阶级联合建立民主民族战线后，复于二十五日提出关于民主民族战线的口号。据路透社、共同社东京二十七日消息，口号内容如下：
    一、根据波茨坦宣言，缔结全面对日和约；和约签订后，迅速撤退一切占领军。
    二、反对战争，反对日本殖民地化和军事基地化。拥护和平，与爱好和平的各国人民友好合作。
    三、反对殖民地奴隶生活。保障基本人权。无限制地发展和平工业，自主地与苏联、中国、朝鲜进行贸易。
    四、反对日本帝国主义复活。打倒天皇制和卖国反动的吉田内阁。
    五、统一联合战线，一切爱国人民参加民主民族战线。完成民族独立，建立人民民主政府。
