Title: Before the Peace Movement, US Imperialism Trembles: Pravda Comments on US Imperialism's Refusal to Allow Entrance to World Peace Congress Delegation

Author:

Date: 1950-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 9th: According to TASS, Moscow, March 8th: “The US State Department Fears Peace,” is the title of a section in an international commentary by Korionov in Pravda. Korionov wrote: The refusal to issue entry visas to the delegation of the Standing Committee of the Peace Congress demonstrates that US imperialists do not want peace, and are also afraid of anything that can promote international cooperation and strengthen and consolidate it.

Korionov pointed out that the main reason why the US rulers reject all proposals aimed at strengthening peace is that US imperialism is trembling in the face of the growing mass movement for peace. Progressive American democratic forces are intensifying their struggle for peace to answer the growing danger of war. New war provocateurs cannot drown out the powerful voices of people from all countries who support the cause of peace.

### 在和平运动面前  美帝感到胆寒  真理报评美帝拒绝和大代表团入境

1950-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　在和平运动面前
    美帝感到胆寒
    真理报评美帝拒绝和大代表团入境
    【新华社北京九日电】据塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：“美国国务院害怕和平”，这是科里奥诺夫在真理报所撰国际述评中一节的标题。科里奥诺夫写道：拒绝签发入境签证给和平大会常设委员会代表团说明美帝国主义者不要和平，也害怕每件能够促进国际合作增强与巩固的事情。
    科里奥诺夫指出美国统治者所以拒绝一切旨在加强和平的建议，主要原因乃是美帝国主义在日益增强的争取和平的群众运动面前感到胆寒。进步的美国民主力量正加紧为和平而斗争来回答增长着的战争危险。新战争挑拨者是掩没不了拥护和平事业的各国人民有力的声音的。
