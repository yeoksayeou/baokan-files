Title: Soviet and Korean Women Actively Prepare to Welcome International Women's Day; Moscow Exhibition of Gifts from Chinese Women to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1950-03-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 6th. TASS, Moscow, March 5th: The Soviet capital is widely preparing to celebrate "March 8th" International Women's Day. Lectures and seminars are being held in enterprises, institutions, educational organizations and clubs to discuss topics such as "Women - a great force in the Soviet state," "The Stalin Constitution and care for mothers and infants," and "Soviet women in the Great Patriotic War." The Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee and the All-Russian Theatrical Society held an evening party at the Actors' Club on the 4th, with a report by Petrova, secretary of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, and an exhibition of gifts from Chinese women to Soviet women. Of particular note was a silk banner sent by women in Shanghai, inscribed: "Dedicated to our sisters in the Soviet Central Asian Republics. The path you have traveled inspires the revolutionary struggles of women throughout Asia."

The Central Writers' Club also held a get-together on the 4th. Moscow's libraries and clubs are preparing exhibitions on Soviet women.

According to the Romanian News Agency: Women throughout Romania are holding enthusiastic gatherings to celebrate "March 8th". Working women in factories and enterprises have pledged at the meetings to complete and exceed their original production plans. The female workers of the "Rosu" factory in Brasov have challenged the female workers of two other factories to a competition. Female workers at a factory in Sighetu Marmației have joined the socialist competition, promising to increase labor productivity by 50 percent.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 5th: North Korean women are actively preparing to welcome "March 8th" International Women's Day. Since February 10th, women's organizations at all levels have held various lectures and seminars. Photo exhibitions introducing the lives of Soviet women and the struggles of women in Asian countries are being held in the capital, provinces, cities, and counties. Local women's organizations have decided to carry out adult education, eliminate illiteracy among women, and mobilize women to actively participate in textile and spring sowing work to commemorate this festival. The Korean Democratic Women's League is scheduled to hold a commemorative meeting in Pyongyang on March 7th, and the League's journal "Korean Women" will publish a special "March 8th" edition.

### 苏罗朝妇女积极筹备  迎接国际妇女节  莫斯科展览我国妇女赠苏礼物

1950-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏罗朝妇女积极筹备
    迎接国际妇女节
    莫斯科展览我国妇女赠苏礼物
    【新华社北京六日电】塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：苏京广泛准备庆祝“三八”国际妇女节。各企业、机关、教育机关和俱乐部正举行讲演和座谈会，讨论“妇女——苏维埃国家中的伟大力量”，“斯大林宪法及对母亲和婴儿福利的关怀”，“伟大爱国战争中的苏联妇女”等问题。苏联妇女反法西斯委员会及全俄戏剧协会于四日在演员俱乐部举办晚会，由苏联妇女反法西斯委员会书记彼特罗娃作报告，并举办了展览会，陈列着新中国妇女送给苏联妇女的礼物。引入注目的是上海妇女所送的一幅锦旗，上面题着：献给我们苏联中亚细亚各加盟共和国的姐妹们。你们所经历的道路鼓舞着全亚洲妇女的革命斗争。
    中央作家俱乐部也于四日举行了联欢晚会。莫斯科各图书馆及俱乐部正筹备关于苏联妇女的展览会。
    另据罗马尼亚通讯社消息：罗全国各地妇女为庆祝“三八”节，正举行热烈集会，工厂与企业中的劳动妇女在会上保证要完成并超过她们原定的生产计划。布拉索夫的“罗舒”工厂的女工已向另外两家工厂的女工提出竞赛挑战。赛给特——马拉姆列施一家工厂的女工参加了社会主义竞赛，保证要提高劳动生产率百分之五十。
    【新华社北京五日电】北朝鲜妇女正积极筹备迎接“三八”国际妇女节。自二月十日起，各级妇女团体已举行了各种讲演会和座谈会。首都与各道、市、郡，现正举办介绍苏联妇女生活与亚洲各国妇女斗争情况的照片展览会。各地妇女团体决定开展成人教育，扫除妇女文盲与动员妇女积极参加纺织和春季播种等工作，以此纪念这一节日。朝鲜民主妇女总同盟订于三月七日在平壤举行纪念大会，该盟机关杂志“朝鲜妇女”将出“三八”特辑。
