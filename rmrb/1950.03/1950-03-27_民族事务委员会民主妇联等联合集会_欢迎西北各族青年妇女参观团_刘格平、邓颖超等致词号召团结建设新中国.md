Title: Nationalities Affairs Commission, Democratic Women's Federation, etc. Jointly Hold Gathering to Welcome Youth and Women's Delegation from Northwest China; Liu Geping, Deng Yingchao, and Others Deliver Speeches Calling for Unity to Build a New China
Author:
Date: 1950-03-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation (ACWF), the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League (CNDYL), the All-China Youth Federation (ACYF), the All-China Students' Federation (ACSF), the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, and the Beijing Working Committee of the Youth League, jointly held a gathering today to welcome the youth and women's delegation from various ethnic groups in Northwest China, which arrived in Beijing yesterday. At the meeting, Liu Geping, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, first delivered the opening speech, in which he extended a warm welcome to the youth and women's delegation from various ethnic groups in Northwest China. He then pointed out that due to the brilliant victory of the Chinese People's Great Revolution, people of all ethnic groups have now fully obtained the possibility of equality, mutual assistance, and unity. In the future, they must unite more closely and, under the leadership of the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao, jointly build a new China for all ethnic groups in accordance with the principles of the Common Program. Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the ACWF, in her speech, besides pointing out that the conditions for all ethnic groups to live together with mutual respect, equality, and friendship already exist, also hoped that all ethnic groups would strengthen unity and friendship and strive to support the war to liberate Tibet and Taiwan. Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, after expressing his welcome to the delegation, stated that the China New Democratic Youth League is an organization of advanced elements of working youth and a progressive organization of youth from all ethnic groups in China. He called on the youth of all ethnic groups in the country to unite firmly under the banner of the Youth League and advance in the direction indicated by Chairman Mao. Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the ACYF, emphasized that in the future, the youth of all ethnic groups in the country should strive to meet the high tide of economic and cultural construction on the basis of thoroughly completing the liberation of all of China. After speeches by Xie Bangding, Chairman of the ACSF, Zhang Xiaomei, Chairman of the Beijing Women's Federation, and Yang Bozhen, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Working Committee of the Youth League, Wang Weiqi, head of the delegation, Roumanna (woman, Tatar), deputy head of the delegation, and Shadier (youth, Uygur) addressed the meeting successively. Roumanna said: Today, we gather in the capital of the new China, which symbolizes the great unity of young men and women of all ethnic groups under the leadership of the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao. With boundless loyalty, we love our leader and government, and we must redouble our efforts to build a new China together with the people of the whole country. In his speech, Shadier expressed that, along the way, seeing the vastness of the motherland and the bravery and diligence of the people, he has increased his confidence a hundredfold in building a new China.

In addition, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League hosted a banquet this afternoon to entertain the youth and women's delegation from various ethnic groups in Northwest China. Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, Liao Chengzhi, Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretaries, Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General, and heads of various departments of the Central Committee of the Youth League attended, accompanied by Xie Bangding, Chairman of the ACSF.

### 民族事务委员会民主妇联等联合集会  欢迎西北各族青年妇女参观团  刘格平、邓颖超等致词号召团结建设新中国

1950-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　民族事务委员会民主妇联等联合集会
    欢迎西北各族青年妇女参观团
    刘格平、邓颖超等致词号召团结建设新中国
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府民族事务委员会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会、北京市民主妇女联合会、青年团北京市工作委员会等单位，今日联合举行集会，欢迎昨日抵京的西北各民族青年、妇女参观团。会上首由民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平致开会词，他首先向西北各族青年、妇女参观团致热烈的欢迎，继即指出：由于中国人民大革命的辉煌胜利，各族人民今天已完全获得了平等互助团结的可能，今后要更加紧密地团结起来，在中央人民政府和毛主席的领导下，依照共同纲领的原则，共同建设各族人民的新中国。全国妇联副主席邓颖超在讲话中除指出各民族互相尊重、平等友爱地生活在一起的条件已经存在外，并希望各民族加强团结友好，努力支援解放西藏、台湾的战争。青年团中央副书记蒋南翔在对参观团表示了欢迎之忱以后，并称：中国新民主主义青年团是劳动青年的先进分子的组织，是中国各族青年的进步组织。他号召全国各民族青年坚强地团结在青年团的旗帜下，依照毛主席指示的方向前进。全国青联主席廖承志强调今后全国各民族青年应在彻底完成全中国解放的基础上，为迎接经济和文化建设的高潮而努力。在全国学联主席谢邦定、北京市妇联主席张晓梅、青年团北京市工委会副书记杨伯箴致词后，参观团团长王维祺、副团长柔曼娜（妇女，塔塔尔族）、沙的尔（青年，维吾尔族）先后向大会讲话。柔曼娜说：今天我们欢聚在新中国的首都，正象征各民族的男女青年在中央人民政府和毛主席领导下的伟大团结。我们以无限忠诚，热爱我们的领袖和政府，一定要加倍努力，和全国人民一起建设新中国。沙的尔在讲话中表示，一路上看到祖国的辽阔广大、人民的勇敢勤劳，对建设新中国，增加百倍信心。
    又讯：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会于今日下午设宴招待西北各民族青年、妇女参观团，青年团中央书记冯文彬、副书记廖承志、蒋南翔，秘书长荣高棠及青年团中央各部负责人出席，并有全国学联主席谢邦定作陪。
