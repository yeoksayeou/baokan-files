Title: Youth Service Department Holds Mass Current Affairs Lecture and New Knowledge Forum Today to Discuss "The Problem of Relieving Unemployed Workers"
Author:
Date: 1950-03-03
Page: 4
The Youth Service Department is holding a mass current affairs lecture to enhance young people's knowledge of current events. The first lecture, "Celebrating the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance," will be held at 2:00 PM on Saturday, March 4th, at No. 19 Nanheyan (Soviet Foreign Cultural Association). Comrade Wang Fei of the International Department of Xinhua News Agency will speak on "The Sino-Soviet Treaty and the Current International Situation." Members of the department and students of the department are welcome to attend. Organizations and groups can also contact the department with a letter of introduction to obtain admission tickets.

Additionally: The 59th New Knowledge Forum of the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association will be held at 7:00 PM on Friday, March 3rd, at the auditorium of the European and American Students Association in Nanheyan. The topic for discussion is "The Problem of Relieving Unemployed Workers." Mr. Ma Guangdou, Director of the Beijing Municipal Labor Bureau, is specially invited to speak and lead the discussion.

### 青年服务部举办大众时事讲座新知识座谈会今讨论“救济失业员工问题”

1950-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　青年服务部举办大众时事讲座新知识座谈会今讨论“救济失业员工问题”
    【北京讯】青年服务部为了增进青年时事知识，特举办大众时事讲座。第一讲为“庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约之签定”于三月四日（星期六）下午二时在南河沿十九号（苏联对外文化协会）举行，请新华总社国际部王飞同志主讲“中苏条约与目前国际形势”，欢迎部友及该部学员听讲，机关团体也可凭介绍信前往接洽领取入座券。
    又讯：民主建国会北京市分会第五十九次新知识座谈会订于三月三日（星期五）下午七时在南河沿欧美同学会礼堂举行，讨论的题目是“救济失业员工问题”，特请北京市劳动局马光斗局长主讲并领导讨论。
