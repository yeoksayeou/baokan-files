Title: Lecture Preview for the Third Lecture on the Soviet Union

Author: 
Date: 1950-03-18
Page: 3

　　Soviet Union Introduction Lecture Third Lecture Preview
　　[Beijing News Agency] The third lecture in the weekly Soviet Union introduction lecture series sponsored by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is scheduled for 6:30 pm on March 19th (this Sunday) at the Beijing Soviet Foreign Cultural Association at 19 Nanheyan Street. Comrade Xie Tao will speak on "A Comparison of Sino-Soviet and Sino-U.S. Relations Over the Past Thirty Years," followed by a film screening. Persons in charge of each branch of the association may attend the lecture with their temporary lecture passes.

### 苏联介绍讲座第三讲预告

1950-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联介绍讲座第三讲预告
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市中苏友好协会所主办的每周苏联介绍讲座，第三讲定于三月十九日（本星期日）下午六时半在南河沿十九号北京苏联对外文化协会举行，由谢涛同志讲“三十年来中苏与中美关系的比较”，会后并放映电影。该会各支会负责人，届时均可持临时听讲证前往听讲。
