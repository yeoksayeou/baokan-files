Title: All-China Women's Federation Directs Women Cadres to Study Xinhua News Agency's March 8th Editorial
Author:
Date: 1950-03-13
Page: 3

　　All-China Women's Federation Directs Women Cadres to Study Xinhua News Agency's March 8th Editorial
    [This Newspaper] The All-China Democratic Women's Federation has decided to use Xinhua News Agency's "March 8th" editorial "Learn Skills, Do a Good Job" and related "March 8th" documents as learning materials for women cadres in various regions. A notice was issued to local women's federations on the 11th. The notice stated: (1) Local women's federations should continue to unite with local trade unions, Communist Youth Leagues, youth federations, and student unions to mobilize women cadres to study diligently, and report their learning experiences and gains to the All-China Women's Federation before April 10th. (2) Quickly summarize the situation and experience of commemorating "March 8th" International Working Women's Day in various regions, and report them before April 10th as well.

### 全国妇联指示妇女干部学习新华社三八社论

1950-03-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国妇联指示妇女干部学习新华社三八社论
    【本报讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会顷决定以新华社“三八”节社论“学会本领，做好工作”及有关“三八”之文件，作为各地妇女干部学习的材料，并于十一日发出给各地妇联的通知。通知略称：（一）各地妇联仍应联合当地工会、青年团、青联、学联，共同发动妇女干部好好学习，并将学习心得及收获于四月十日前向全国妇联汇报。（二）迅速总结各地纪念“三八”国际劳动妇女节的情况及经验，亦于四月十日前汇报。
