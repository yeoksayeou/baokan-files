Title: Let Chinese and Italian Youth Advance Hand in Hand: Italian Communist Youth Federation Holds Congress; Chinese Communist Youth League and All-China Youth Federation Send Congratulatory Messages
Author:
Date: 1950-03-26
Page: 4

　　Let Chinese and Italian Youth Advance Hand in Hand
    Italian Communist Youth Federation Holds Congress; Chinese Communist Youth League and All-China Youth Federation Send Congratulatory Messages
    [Xinhua News Agency, March 25th] The Italian Communist Youth Federation will hold its 12th (the first post-war) National Congress from March 28th to April 2nd. The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth jointly sent a congratulatory telegram. The telegram reads as follows: To the Italian Communist Youth Federation:
    Chinese youth warmly congratulate the success of your congress and express respect for the heroic struggle of the Italian democratic youth. We wish you more victories in the struggle to unite the broad masses of youth to strive for peace and youth rights. Let the youth of our two countries, China and Italy, advance hand in hand in the common struggle for lasting peace and people's democracy.
    Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
    All-China Federation of Democratic Youth
              March 25th

### 让中意两国青年携手前进  意共产主义青联举行大会  我青年团及青联致电祝贺

1950-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　让中意两国青年携手前进
    意共产主义青联举行大会  我青年团及青联致电祝贺
    【新华社二十五日讯】意大利共产主义青年联盟在三月二十八日至四月二日举行第十二届（战后第一届）全国大会。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国民主青年联合会总会特致电祝贺。电文如下：意大利共产主义青年联盟：
    中国青年热烈祝贺你们大会的成功，并对意大利民主青年的英勇斗争表示敬意。祢你们在团结广大青年争取和平与青年权利的斗争中，获得更多的胜利。让我们中意两国青年在争取持久和平与人民民主的共同斗争中携手前进。
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会中华全国民主青年联合会总会
              三月二十五日
