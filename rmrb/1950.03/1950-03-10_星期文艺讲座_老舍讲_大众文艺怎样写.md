Title: Sunday Literary Lecture: Lao She Speaks on "How to Write Popular Literature"
Author:
Date: 1950-03-10
Page: 4

　　Sunday Literary Lecture
    Lao She Speaks on "How to Write Popular Literature"
    [Beijing News] The Sunday Literary Lecture, jointly organized by the Beijing Popular Literature Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Department, will invite Lao She to speak on "How to Write Popular Literature" at 9:30 a.m. this Sunday (the 12th) at the Youth Palace (the old Majestic Theater). After the lecture, Yu Yunfang, Huo Chunshan, and Song Zhicheng will perform the Jingyun Dagu "Flying奪瀘定橋 [Flying Over the Luding Bridge]". Admission is by long-term ticket and temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department on Saturday.

### 星期文艺讲座  老舍讲“大众文艺怎样写”

1950-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    老舍讲“大众文艺怎样写”
    【北京讯】京市大众文艺创作研究会与青年服务部所举办的星期文艺讲座，本星期日（十二日）上午九时半在青年宫（美琪影院旧址）邀请老舍讲“大众文艺怎样写”。会后由于韵芳、霍春山、宋志诚表演京韵大鼓“飞夺泸定桥”。凭长期券及临时券入场，临时券可于星期六到青年服务部领取。
