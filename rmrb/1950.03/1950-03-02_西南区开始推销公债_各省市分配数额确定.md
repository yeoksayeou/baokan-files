Title: Southwest Region Begins Promoting Public Bonds; All Provinces and Cities' Assigned Quotas Determined
Author:
Date: 1950-03-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 1st. The Southwest Region's quota for the first phase of the 1950 People's Victory Indexed Public Bonds is 7 million shares. Starting today, promotion will begin in all provinces and cities within the region. Based on the specific conditions of each province and city, the Southwest Military and Political Committee has allocated the following sales quotas: Chongqing City 3 million shares, Chengdu City 900,000 shares, Yunnan Province 1 million shares, Guizhou City 350,000 shares, Southern Sichuan Administrative Region 800,000 shares, Eastern Sichuan Administrative Region 600,000 shares, Northern Sichuan Administrative Region 350,000 shares, Western Sichuan Administrative Region 450,000 shares, Xikang Province 50,000 shares, totaling 7.5 million shares. Currently, in Chongqing City, people from all walks of life, led by workers, have already pre-purchased nearly 100,000 shares.

### 西南区开始推销公债  各省市分配数额确定

1950-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　西南区开始推销公债
    各省市分配数额确定
    【新华社重庆一日电】一九五零年第一期人民胜利折实公债西南区配额七百万分，今日起在全区各省市开始推销。西南军政委员会根据全区各省市具体情况，分配推销数额如下：重庆市三百万分，成都市九十万分，云南省一百万分，贵州市三十五万分，川南区八十万分，川东区六十万分，川北区三十五万分，川西区四十五万分，西康省五万分，共计七百五十万分。现重庆市各界人民在工人带头下已预购近十万分。
