Title: China National Grain Corporation Officially Established in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-03-05
Page: 2

　　China National Grain Corporation Officially Established in Beijing
    [This Newspaper reported] The Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government convened a meeting in Beijing on January 22nd to establish the China National Grain Corporation, which concluded on February 11th. During the meeting, in addition to deciding on the business plan, financial plan, and other important matters for 1950, it was also decided to establish the China National Grain Corporation in Beijing. The China National Grain Corporation was officially established on March 1st at No. 143 Rongxian Hutong, Beijing, and has begun operations.

### 中国粮食总公司在北京正式成立

1950-03-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国粮食总公司在北京正式成立
    【本报讯】中央人民政府贸易部于一月二十二日在京召开成立中国粮食公司会议，于二月十一日结束。会中除决定了一九五○年之业务计划、财务计划及其他重要事项外，并决定在北京成立中国粮食总公司。现中国粮食总公司已于三月一日在北京绒线胡同一四三号正式成立，并开始工作。
