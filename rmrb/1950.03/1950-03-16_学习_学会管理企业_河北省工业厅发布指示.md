Title: Studying "Learn How to Manage Enterprises": Hebei Provincial Department of Industry Issues Instructions
Author: Zhang Junfeng
Date: 1950-03-16
Page: 3

　　Studying "Learn How to Manage Enterprises"
    Hebei Provincial Department of Industry Issues Instructions
    [This Newspaper Dispatches] In order to strengthen the ideological education of cadres in state-owned factories and mines in the province and cities, and to further rely on the working class to manage production, the Hebei Provincial Department of Industry issued instructions on the 10th of this month to study the People's Daily editorial "Learn How to Manage Enterprises." The instructions pointed out that this document should be studied as the current industrial policy, and the main responsible persons of each factory and mine were instructed to convene a special meeting in coordination with the leading cadres of the trade union to review the work of their units in accordance with the spirit of the document, and to launch criticism and self-criticism. After the study is completed, a summary should be made at the end of the month, and reports should be made level by level, and no carelessness should be allowed.
    (Zhang Junfeng)

### 学习“学会管理企业”  河北省工业厅发布指示
张峻峰
1950-03-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　学习“学会管理企业”
    河北省工业厅发布指示
    【本报讯】河北省工业厅为加强省市各公营厂矿干部思想教育，进一步依靠工人阶级管理生产，特于本月十日发出学习人民日报社论“学会管理企业”的指示。指出应将该文件当作当前工业方针来学习，并责成各厂矿主要负责人，协同工会领导干部，专门召集一次会议，根据文件精神检查本单位工作，展开批评与互相批评。学习完毕，于月底作总结，逐级报告，不得马虎从事。
    （张峻峰）
