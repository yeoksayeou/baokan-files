"Thirty Years of Soviet Cinema"
    ·Edited by the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture·Published by Dazhong Bookstore·
    In order to celebrate the 30th anniversary of Soviet cinema, and to enable Chinese film workers, literary and art workers, and the broad masses of moviegoers to further and more systematically understand the development process and achievements of Soviet cinema, the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government has specially edited this album, "Thirty Years of Soviet Cinema." This collection includes twenty essays such as "Main Development Stages of Soviet Cinema" and "Thirty Years of Soviet Cinema" by Soviet Minister of the Film Industry, Bolshakov, "International Peace is the Motto of Progressive Art" and "On Soviet Cinema" by the famous director Gerasimov, and "The Resolution on Cinema of the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Board of the Soviet Writers' Association," as well as thirty-six copperplate prints of famous Soviet films.

### “苏联电影三十年”

1950-03-26
第6版()
专栏：

　　“苏联电影三十年”
    ·文化部电影局编辑·大众书店印行·
    中央人民政府文化部电影局为了庆祝苏联电影三十年纪念，为了使我国电影工作者、文艺工作者和广大电影观众进一步的、较系统的认识苏联电影的发展过程与所达到的成就，特编辑了这本《苏联电影三十年》专辑。在这个集子里，包括了苏联电影事业部部长波尔沙可夫的《苏联电影主要发展阶段》、《苏联电影三十年》，名导演格拉西莫夫的《国际和平是进步艺术的座右铭》、《论苏联电影》及《苏联作家协会理事会第十二次全会关于电影的决议》等论著共二十篇及苏联著名影片铜图三十六幅。
