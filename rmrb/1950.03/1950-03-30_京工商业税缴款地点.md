Title: Beijing Industrial and Commercial Tax Payment Locations
Author:
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 1

[Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing Municipal Taxation Bureau and the People's Bank of China Beijing Branch have agreed upon the locations for collecting industrial and commercial taxes for the second half of 1949. Except for industrial and commercial households in the suburban areas under the jurisdiction of the Fifth, Sixth, and Seventh Tax Sub-bureaus, which will pay directly to their respective sub-bureaus, all households that receive payment notices from the First, Second, Third, Fourth, and Eighth Tax Sub-bureaus can pay at the following designated locations:

	I. Six designated payment locations for the First Tax Sub-bureau: People's Bank Dongsi Office, 167 Mishidajie; People's Bank Dongsi Branch, 56 Dongsi Beidajie; People's Bank Dongdan Branch, 17 Dongsi Nandajie; People's Bank Dongcheng Office, 89 Wangfujingdajie; Xinhua Bank Dongcheng Office, 88 Wangfujingdajie; Continental Bank Dongcheng Office, 15 Dongsi Beidajie.

	II. Seven designated payment locations for the Second Tax Sub-bureau: People's Bank Xicheng Office, 208 Xidan Beidajie; People's Bank Xidan Branch, 301 Xidan Beidajie; People's Bank Xicheng Office Second Business Office, 190 Xidan Beidajie; People's Bank Xizhimen Dajie Office, 53 Xizhimen Dajie; People's Bank Xi Chang'an Avenue Office, 109 Xi Chang'an Avenue; Jin Cheng Bank Xicheng Office, 64 Xidan Beidajie; Continental Bank Beijing Branch, 20 Xijiaominxiang.

	III. Eight designated payment locations for the Third Tax Sub-bureau: People's Bank Nancheng Office, 52 Chongwenmenwai Dajie; People's Bank Zhushikou Branch, 150 East Zhushikou; Jin Cheng Bank Beijing Branch, 108 Xijiaominxiang; Fu Shun De Qian Zhuang, 95 Xijiaominxiang; Rong Chang Hou Qian Zhuang, 90 Qianwai Langfang Ertiao; Yi Ju Qian Zhuang, 51 Qianwai Damochang; Da De Heng Qian Zhuang, 4 Jinmao Hutong, Chongwenmenwai; Bank of Communications, Dongjiaominxiang.

	IV. Eleven designated payment locations for the Fourth Tax Sub-bureau: People's Bank Qianmen Dajie Office, 11 Qianwai Shijia Hutong; People's Bank Qianmen Branch, 199 Qianwai Dajie; People's Bank Meishi Branch, 37 Meishi Street; People's Bank Xiliushuijing Branch, 50 Xiliushuijing; People's Bank Cooperative Department, 14 Hufangqiao; Xinhua Trust and Savings Bank Beijing Branch, 8 Qianwai Langfang Toutiao; China Industrial Bank Beijing Branch, 87 Xijiaominxiang; Ren Fa Gong Qian Zhuang, 98 Qianwai Guanyin Temple; Bao Sheng Qian Zhuang, 102 Meishi Street; Qian Xing Qian Zhuang, 12 Qianwai Shijia Hutong; Jin Hong Yu Qian Zhuang, 20 Qianwai Dazhalan.

	V. Three designated payment locations for the Eighth Tax Sub-bureau: People's Bank Beicheng Office, 159 Di'anmen Dajie; Jin Cheng Bank Beicheng Office, 100 Diwai Gulouqian Dajie; Ping Yi Qian Zhuang, 42 Di'anmenwai Dajie.

### 京工商业税缴款地点

1950-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　京工商业税缴款地点
    【京市新闻处讯】本市代收一九四九年下半年度工商业税地点已经市税务局与人民银行北京分行议定，除税务第五、六、七等三个分局所管郊区工商业户就地向各分局直接缴纳外，凡由第一、二、三、四、八等五个税务分局发出缴款通知书的各户，可分别向下列各指定地点缴款：
    一、税务第一分局指定缴款地点六处：人民银行东四办事处，米市大街一六七号；人民银行东四分理处，东四北大街五六号；人民银行东单分理处，东四南大街一七号；人民银行东城办事处，王府井大街八九号；新华银行东城办事处，王府井大街八八号；大陆银行东城办事处，东四北大街一五号。
