Title: Central People's Government Ministry of Railways Establishes Organization and Staffing Review and Warehouse Inventory Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-03-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, March 17th: To implement the State Council of the Central People's Government's decision on unified national financial and economic work, the Ministry of Railways has decided to organize an Organization and Staffing Review Committee and a Warehouse Material Inventory Committee to conduct research and review of staffing levels in organizations at all levels and warehouse material inventory work, respectively. The Organization and Staffing Review Committee will consist of Teng Daiyuan, Wu Jingtao, Lin Yi, Wei Hengcang, Ma Jian, Zhu Shaotian, Qian Yinglin, Liu Hansheng, Guo Lu, and other nine people as standing committee members, with Teng Daiyuan as the chairman. The Warehouse Material Inventory Committee will consist of Lai Ping, Yang Zaitian, Zhu Shaotian, Qian Yinglin, Guo Lu, and other fourteen people as members. Teng Daiyuan will be the chairman, and Lü Zhengcao, Wu Jingtao, and Shi Zhiren will be the vice chairmen. Both committees have decided to form and complete preparatory work within this month, and formally begin work in April.

### 中央人民政府铁道部成立编制审定仓库清查委员会

1950-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府铁道部成立编制审定仓库清查委员会
    【新华社十七日讯】为执行中央人民政府政务院关于统一国家财政经济工作的决定，中央铁道部已决定组织编制审定委员会与仓库物资清查委员会，分别进行研究、审查各级编制机构定员与仓库物资清查等工作。编制审定委员会由滕代远、武竞天、林一、魏恒仓、马健、朱劭天、钱应麟、刘汉生、郭鲁等九人为常务委员，滕代远为主任委员。仓库物资清查委员会由赖萍、杨再田、朱劭天、钱应麟、郭鲁等十四人为委员。滕代远为主任委员，吕正操、武竞天、石志仁为副主任委员。两个委员会决定于本月内组成并完成准备工作，四月开始正式工作。
