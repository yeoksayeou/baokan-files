Title: Beijing to Hold Lantern Festival Gala Today
Author:
Date: 1950-03-03
Page: 4

　　【Beijing News】 The Beijing Spring Festival Arts Committee has decided to hold a Lantern Festival gala today (the 3rd). At 12:00 noon, the Central Academy of Drama will perform "Great Song and Dance of Building the Motherland" at Tiananmen Square, and Yangko teams and stilt teams from all districts of the city will participate. At 7:00 p.m., a joint gala will be held at the auditorium of the Ministry of Railways by the Central Academy of Drama, the People's Art Theater, and adult night schools in various districts. In addition to demonstration performances by the Central Academy of Drama and the People's Art Theater, there will be the play "It's You" by the Second District Adult Night School, the play "Cui Guilan's Study" by the Fifth District Adult Night School, the "New Beijing Dance" by female workers of the Ailun Pharmaceutical Factory in the Seventh District, and the "Taiwan Dance" by the Fourth District Adult Night School.

### 京市今日举行元宵大会演

1950-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市今日举行元宵大会演
    【北京讯】京市春节文艺工作委员会，决定于今（三）日举行元宵大会演。中午十二时在天安门广场由中央戏剧学院演出“建设祖国大歌舞”，全市各区秧歌队和高跷队都前往参加。晚上七时在铁道部大礼堂由中央戏剧学院、人民艺术剧院和各区成人夜校等举行联合大会演。除中央戏剧学院和人民艺术剧院示范演出外，并有二区成人夜校的话剧“就是你”，五区成人夜校的话剧“崔桂兰的学习”，七区爱伦制药厂女工的“新北京舞”，四区成人夜校的“台湾舞”等节目。
