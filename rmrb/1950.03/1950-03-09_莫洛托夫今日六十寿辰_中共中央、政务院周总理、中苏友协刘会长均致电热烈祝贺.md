Title: Molotov's Sixtieth Birthday Today: CCP Central Committee, Premier Zhou of the Government Administration Council, and President Liu of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Send Warm Congratulatory Telegrams

Author: 
Date: 1950-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 8th: Molotov's sixtieth birthday falls on March 9th this year. The CCP Central Committee sent a telegram to Molotov and the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) to offer congratulations. The telegram reads:

    Dear Comrade Molotov and all Comrades of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks):
    We extend our warm congratulations on the occasion of Comrade Molotov's sixtieth birthday.
                            The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                            March 8th

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 8th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council sent a telegram to congratulate Molotov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram reads:

    To His Excellency Mr. Molotov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union:
    On the occasion of Your Excellency's sixtieth birthday, I wish you good health and longevity.
            Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
                                      March 8th

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 8th: Liu Shaoqi, President of the National Headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, sent a telegram to congratulate Molotov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram reads:

    To Mr. Molotov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union:
    On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, I extend to you my sincere and warm congratulations.
                              Liu Shaoqi, President of the National Headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
                                      March 8th, 1950

### 莫洛托夫今日六十寿辰  中共中央、政务院周总理、中苏友协刘会长均致电热烈祝贺

1950-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　莫洛托夫今日六十寿辰
    中共中央、政务院周总理、中苏友协刘会长均致电热烈祝贺
    【新华社北京八日电】今年三月九日为莫洛托夫六十寿辰，中共中央致电莫洛托夫和联共（布）中央祝贺，电文如下：亲爱的莫洛托夫同志和苏联共产党（布）中央委员会全体同志们：
    欣逢莫洛托夫同志六十寿辰，谨致热烈的祝贺。
                             中国共产党中央委员会
                                            三月八日
    【新华社北京八日电】政务院周恩来总理致电祝贺苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫六十寿辰，电文如下：苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫先生阁下：
    在阁下六十寿辰之际，谨祝健康和长寿。
            中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理  周恩来
                                      三月八日
    【新华社北京八日电】中苏友好协会总会刘少奇会长，电贺苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫六十寿辰。电文如下：苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫先生：
    欣逢您的六十寿辰，谨向您致挚诚热烈的祝贺。
                              中苏友好协会总会会长  刘少奇
                                      一九五○年三月八日
