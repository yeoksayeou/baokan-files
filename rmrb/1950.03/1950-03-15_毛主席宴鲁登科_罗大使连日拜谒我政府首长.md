Title: Chairman Mao Hosts a Banquet for Rudenko, Ambassador Luo Pays Successive Visits to Our Government Leaders
Author:
Date: 1950-03-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 14th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet at 7 p.m. on the 14th to welcome Rudenko, the first Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, and all members of the embassy. Invited to attend were Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan of the Central People's Government, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council, Acting Chief of General Staff Nie Rongzhen of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Li Kenong and Zhang Hanfu, Director Wang Bingnan, Director of the Bureau of Protocol Yu Xinqing, Director of the Compilation and Translation Bureau Shi Zhe, and Chief of the Reception Division of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Zhuoru.
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 14th: After presenting his credentials, Rudenko, the Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, paid visits to Vice Chairmen Zhu De and Liu Shaoqi of the Central People's Government on the 13th, and to Vice Chairmen Li Jishen and Zhang Lan on the 14th.

### 毛主席宴鲁登科  罗大使连日拜谒我政府首长

1950-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席宴鲁登科
    罗大使连日拜谒我政府首长
    【新华社北京十四日电】中央人民政府毛泽东主席于十四日下午七时欢宴罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国首任大使鲁登科及全体馆员。应邀作陪者有中央人民政府朱德副主席、刘少奇副主席、李济深副主席、张澜副主席，林伯渠秘书长，政务院周恩来总理兼外交部长，人民革命军事委员会聂荣臻代总参谋长，外交部李克农副部长、章汉夫副部长，王炳南主任，中央人民政府典礼局余心清局长，编译局师哲局长，外交部交际处王倬如处长等。
    【新华社北京十四日电】罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使鲁登科于呈递国书后，十三日拜谒我中央人民政府朱德副主席、刘少奇副主席，于十四日拜谒李济深副主席、张澜副主席。
