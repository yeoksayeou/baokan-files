　　Radio Programs
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (March 17th) at 20:30: (1) The people of Hainan Island who have persisted in long-term struggle for eleven years; (2) Talking about the "soldier acting as soldier" movement in the People's Liberation Army. At 17:30, workers' time: "Talking about the work of the shop employee union." At 18:30, Natural Science Lecture: General Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 25, "Electric Shock." At 18:00, the first literary program: Yang Jirei sings Xihe Dagu "Labor Hero Zhang Zifu." At 21:00, the second literary program: The station's broadcasting art troupe will perform the report drama "Pan Xiuzhi." At 18:45, new songs: "Singing Chairman Mao," "Emancipation Ballad," "Support the Frontline," "A Towel."
    [Another dispatch] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 12:30, Workers' Program: (1) Culture and Entertainment: The Beijing Machinery General Factory Workers' Choir sings; (2) Communication Speech: Han Zhenxing, a worker from the Beijing Trolleybus Company, talks about "Guaranteeing Good Performance on the Ring Road." At 13:00, Youth Program: Youth Lecture: Ding Haochuan talks with young friends about family issues. At 16:30, Voice of the Municipality: ① Introducing the Beijing Municipal Construction Bureau's road engineering plan and work situation for this year; ② The Beijing Municipal Land Administration Bureau notifies citizens to be aware of scammers impersonating staff of the Land Administration Bureau to defraud citizens of money. At 18:45, Listener Service: Introducing the history of the "March 18th" Massacre and the "Paris Commune" revolutionary anniversary.

### 广播节目

1950-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（十七日）二十点三十分：（一）坚持了十一年长期战斗的海南岛人民；（二）谈谈人民解放军里的“兵演兵”运动。十七点三十分职工时间：“谈谈店员工会的工作”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：电学常识第二十五讲“触电”。十八点第一次文艺节目：杨吉瑞演唱西河大鼓“劳动英雄张子富”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：该台广播文工团演播报导剧《潘秀芝》。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《歌唱毛主席》、《翻身道情》、《支援前线》、《一条毛巾》。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人节目：（一）文化娱乐：北京机械总厂工人合唱团唱歌，（二）通讯讲话：北京电车公司工人韩振兴讲“保证走好环行路”。十三点青年节目：青年讲座：丁浩川和青年朋友们谈谈家庭问题。十六点三十分市政之声：①介绍北京市建设局本年度道路工程计划和工作情形，②北京市地政局请市民注意，冒充地政局工作人员向市民诈财分子的通知。十八点四十五分听众服务：介绍“三一八”惨案和“巴黎公社”革命纪念日的历史。
