Title: List of Responsible Persons of the Municipal Communist Youth League Working Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-03-05
Page: 4

In addition, the organizational structure and responsible persons of the Municipal Communist Youth League Working Committee have also been slightly adjusted. In addition to Xu Liqun, Yang Bozhen, and Zhang Dazhong continuing to serve as the Secretary and Deputy Secretaries, the current organization and heads of each department are as follows:

　　Director of the Organization Department: Du Ping, Deputy Director: Chen Yiru.

　　Director of the Propaganda Department: Yu Shiguang (Yao Yisha), Deputy Directors: Wang Shigu, Wang Hu, Li Bokang (Lin Kai).

　　Director of the Youth Workers Department: Wang Lvcong (Ji Zhen), Deputy Director: Ding Ke.

　　Director of the University Department: Wang Jialiu, Deputy Director: Zhang Zhigong.

　　Director of the Middle School Department: Pan Liang, Deputy Directors: Wang Daming, Zheng Meirong.

　　Director of the Children's Department: Zhou Shixian, Deputy Director: Niu Jingcun.

　　Secretary of the Suburban Work Committee: Zhang Jinlin, Deputy Secretary: Leng Lin. Secretary of the Directly Subordinate Organs Work Committee: Xi Congzhen, Deputy Secretary: Yang Shiying.

　　Secretary-General: Zhang Dazhong (concurrent), Deputy Secretary-General: Zhang Shuowen.

　　Director of the General Office: Zhang Shuowen (concurrent), Deputy Directors: Zhu Huang, Shen Shanqi.
