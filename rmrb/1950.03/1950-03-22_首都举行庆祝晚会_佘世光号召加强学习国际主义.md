Title: Capital Holds Celebration Evening, She Shiguang Calls for Strengthening the Study of Internationalism
Author:
Date: 1950-03-22
Page: 4
　　
　　Capital Holds Celebration Evening
    She Shiguang Calls for Strengthening the Study of Internationalism

    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st dispatch] The Beijing Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League and the Beijing Student Union held an evening party at the Beijing Theater on the 20th to commemorate "World Youth Week." The attendees included representatives of the Second Beijing Student Congress, members of the Youth League from various schools, and students, totaling over 1,000 people. She Shiguang, Director of the Propaganda Department of the Beijing Working Committee of the Youth League, spoke at the meeting. He said: To commemorate "World Youth Week," Beijing youth must resolutely unite under the banner of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and struggle for people's democracy and lasting peace. He called on everyone to strengthen the study of internationalism, constantly concern themselves with the liberation cause of the youth and people of all countries, especially in Southeast Asia, and work hard in production and study to build a new democratic China.

### 首都举行庆祝晚会  佘世光号召加强学习国际主义

1950-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　首都举行庆祝晚会
    佘世光号召加强学习国际主义
    【新华社二十一日讯】新民主主义青年团北京市工作委员会与北京市学生联合会于二十日晚在北京剧院举行纪念“世界青年周”晚会。到会的有北京市第二届学生代表大会的代表、各校青年团团员和学生共一千余人。会上由青年团北京市工委宣传部部长佘世光讲话。他说：纪念“世界青年周”，北京青年要坚决地团结在世界民主青年联盟的旗帜下，为争取人民民主与持久和平而斗争。他号召大家加强学习国际主义，时刻关心世界各国特别是东南亚青年与人民的解放事业，努力生产和学习，建设新民主主义的中国。
