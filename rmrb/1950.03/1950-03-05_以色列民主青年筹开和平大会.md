Title: Israeli Democratic Youth Prepare to Convene Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-03-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tel Aviv: Democratic youth and students in Israel have begun preparing for a national conference in support of peace, scheduled to convene on March 10th. According to reports, a signature campaign is underway among students in Israeli higher education institutions, signing an appeal to youth calling on Israeli youth to participate in the peace movement. The appeal points out: "War-mongers are attempting to encircle the world with a vicious network of aggressive military bases. They are carrying out a conspiracy to drag Israel into a Near East military bloc, attempting to turn Israel into an imperialist military base." The appeal calls on all Israeli students to "rise up against war and join the peace camp with millions of people."

### 以色列民主青年筹开和平大会

1950-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　以色列民主青年筹开和平大会
    【新华社北京四日电】塔斯社特拉维夫讯：以色列的民主青年学生，已开始筹备定于三月十日召开的全国拥护和平大会。据报载消息：在以色列高等教育机关的学生中，现正进行征集签名运动，为号召以色列青年参加和平运动的告青年书签名。告青年书指出：“战争贩子们正企图利用毒辣的侵略性的军事基地网来包围世界。他们正在进行将以色列拖入近东军事集团的阴谋，企图将以色列变为帝国主义者的军事基地。”告青年书号召所有以色列学生，“奋起反对战争，并参加拥有千百万人民的和平阵营”。
