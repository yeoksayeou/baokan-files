Title: Be Vigilant Against Imperialist Conspiracies: U.S. Imperialism Intensifies Aggressive Activities in the Far East; New Espionage Centers to be Established in Vietnam, Burma, and Other Places

Author:
Date: 1950-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: A Moscow broadcast on the 24th, citing the *Washington Post*, reported that the U.S. State Department has taken measures to strengthen espionage activities in the Far East. The U.S. House Appropriations Committee has approved appropriations to strengthen surveillance posts along the Chinese border. Former U.S. diplomatic officials in China are being reassigned to other positions in Southeast Asian countries.

It is reported that the United States will open thirteen new consulates in the Far East and one in Central Asia, places where there have never been U.S. consular officials before. The locations where the United States will establish new espionage centers are being kept secret. However, there are reports that U.S. intelligence agencies will operate in Indochina, Burma, Nepal, Kashmir, and other countries.

### 警惕帝国主义阴谋  美帝加强侵略远东活动  将在越缅等地建新间谍中心

1950-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　警惕帝国主义阴谋
    美帝加强侵略远东活动  将在越缅等地建新间谍中心
    【新华社二十八日讯】据莫斯科二十四日广播引《华盛顿邮报》消息：美国国务院已采取措施，以加强远东的间谍活动。美众院拨款委员会已批准加强沿中国边境监视哨的拨款。原在中国的美国外交官员，正在转派到东南亚各国担任其他职位。
    据悉，美国将在远东开辟十三个新领事馆，在中亚开辟一个，这些地方以往从未有过美国领事官员。美国将建立新间谍中心的地点，保持着秘密。但是已有消息说，美国间谍机关将在印度支那、缅甸、尼泊尔、克什米尔以及其他各国进行工作。
