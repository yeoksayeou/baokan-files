Title: Vietnam Ha Tay Overseas Chinese Delegation Arrives in Kunming to Comfort the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1950-03-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 17th—A “Overseas Chinese Representative慰问团” (Comfort Delegation), consisting of seventeen members from the Overseas Chinese Students, Youth, Women, and Overseas Chinese Council Representatives from Đồng Văn County, Hà Tây Province, Vietnam, arrived in Kunming on the 7th. The Kunming Military Control Commission and the People's Liberation Army held a welcome party on the 13th, and invited them to visit the影画展览 (Shadow Play Exhibition) held by the army corps and to tour the famous scenic spots in Kunming. The Comfort Delegation extended cordial greetings to the People's Liberation Army. The representatives stated that the victory of the people's revolution in the motherland has greatly inspired the overseas Chinese in Vietnam, and they all eagerly hope to come back and see the newly reborn motherland.

### 越南河阳侨胞慰问团抵昆慰问人民解放军

1950-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南河阳侨胞慰问团抵昆慰问人民解放军
    【新华社昆明十七日电】越南河阳省同文县由华侨学生、青年、妇女及华侨理事分会代表所组织的“华侨代表慰问团”一行十七人，于七日抵昆。昆市军管会和解放军于十三日举行欢迎晚会，并请他们参观该兵团举办的影画展览和游览昆市名胜。慰问团对人民解放军致以亲切的慰问。代表们表示：祖国人民革命的胜利，大大鼓舞了越南侨胞，他们都切盼能回来看看新生了的祖国。
