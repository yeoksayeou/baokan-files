Title: Chongqing Enthusiastically Promotes Public Bonds

Author:

Date: 1950-03-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, March 8th. Since the Southwest Region began promoting the People's Victory Indexed Bonds on the 1st of this month, Chongqing has enthusiastically launched various promotional activities. All sectors, industries, and groups have successively established public bond promotion organizations. Workers continue to take the lead in subscribing. The business community plans to subscribe for 2.5 million shares and is proceeding with subscriptions in three ways: self-subscription, democratic appraisal, and competition. The Women's Federation is calling on female compatriots, who account for half of the city's population, to commemorate the upcoming "March 8th" International Women's Day by subscribing to public bonds. The New Democratic Youth League has also issued a notice calling on all members to carry out publicity and promotion campaigns. All democratic parties have held meetings or issued proclamations calling on the industrial and commercial sectors and wealthy households to actively subscribe, and emphasized that their members should purchase as much as possible according to their own economic conditions.

### 重庆市推销公债热烈展开

1950-03-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　重庆市推销公债热烈展开
    【新华社重庆八日电】西南区本月一日开始推销人民胜利折实公债以来，重庆市热烈展开了各项推销活动。各界、各行业、各团体均已相继成立了推销公债机构。职工继续带头认购。工商界预定认购二百五十万分，正以自认、民主评议、竞赛三种方式进行认购。妇联号召占全市人口半数的妇女同胞，用认购公债来纪念即将到来的“三八”节。新民主主义青年团也发出通知，号召全体团员进行宣传与劝购运动。各民主党派均已召开会议或发表文告，号召工商界及殷实富户踊跃认购，并强调所属成员应各就自己的经济状况尽量购买。
