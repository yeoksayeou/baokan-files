Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Council to Meet in Budapest in May to Discuss Report on International Trade Union Activities and Other Issues
Author:
Date: 1950-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: According to information from the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions: The World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) Council will meet in Budapest, Hungary, on May 10, 1950. A joint meeting of the Administrative Bureaus of the Trade Union Internationals will be held prior to this meeting to select representatives and alternate representatives to attend the WFTU Council.

　　After the WFTU Council meeting closes, the Seafarers and Dockers Trade Union International will convene a meeting of the Administrative Committee to discuss the following issues: 1. Report on the activities of the Trade Union International over the past period; 2. The issue of fighting to defend peace and international workers' solidarity; 3. The issue of contact with the national trade union federations of WFTU member countries and the trade unions of various countries that have withdrawn from the WFTU; 4. The International Labour Office issue; 5. The issue of establishing an International Seafarers and Dockers Club; 6. Report on the 1949 financial report and the 1950 financial budget.

### 世界工联理事会定五月在匈京开会  讨论工会国际活动报告等问题

1950-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界工联理事会
    定五月在匈京开会
    讨论工会国际活动报告等问题
    【新华社二十三日讯】据中华全国总工会国际联络部消息：世界工联理事会将于一九五○年五月十日在匈京布达佩斯开会。各工会国际行政局联席会议将于此会议前召开，选定出席世界工联理事会代表与候补代表。
    在世界工联理事会闭会后，海员码头工人工会国际将召集行政委员会会议，讨论以下各项问题：一、关于过去一个时期工会国际之活动报告；二、关于为保卫和平与国际工人团结而斗争问题；三、与世界工联各会员国总工会和已退出世界工联之各国工会间的联系问题；四、国际劳工局问题；五、创设国际海员码头工人俱乐部问题；六、关于一九四九年度财政报告及一九五○年度财政预算问题。
