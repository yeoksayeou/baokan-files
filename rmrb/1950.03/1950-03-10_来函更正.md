Title: Letter of Correction
Author:
Date: 1950-03-10
Page: 1

　　Letter of Correction
    Regarding the news item "Central People's Government Ministry of Trade Begins Establishing Trade Treasury" published on page one of your newspaper on March 9th, please make the following corrections:
    (1) Line 17: "and dispatched the Director of the Foreign Trade Department" should read "and dispatched the Director of the Domestic Trade Department."
    (2) Second paragraph, fourth line: "At the same time, the Ministry of Trade issued the Ministry of Finance's cash statistical report with Payment Order No. 1" should read "At the same time, the Ministry of Trade issued Order No. 1 for the amounts due to be received by the various specialized companies on March 7th, according to the Ministry of Finance's cash statistical report."
                                      Central People's Government Ministry of Trade

### 来函更正
中央人民政府贸易部
1950-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　来函更正
    三月九日贵报一版刊载的“中央人民政府贸易部开始建立贸易金库”消息，请作如下更正：
    （一）第十七行：“并派国外贸易司司长”，应为“并派国内贸易司司长”。
    （二）第二段第四行：“同时由中央贸易部以第一号支付命令发出贸易部财务处现金统计报告”，应为“同时由中央贸易部以第一号命令发出各专业公司三月七日应领款项。根据贸易部财务处现金统计报告”。
                                      中央人民政府贸易部
