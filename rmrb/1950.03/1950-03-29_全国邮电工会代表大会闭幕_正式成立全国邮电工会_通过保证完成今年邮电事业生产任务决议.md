Title: National Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Congress Concludes; Formal Establishment of the National Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union; Resolution Passed to Guarantee Completion of This Year's Postal and Telecommunications Production Tasks
Author:
Date: 1950-03-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, dated 28th: The first National Congress of the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, which opened in the capital on the 17th of this month, concluded on the 27th. The conference delegates unanimously adopted the decision proposed by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions regarding the unification of the leading bodies of the National Postal Workers' Union and the Telecommunications Workers' Union. They also adopted a resolution to guarantee the fulfillment of the production tasks for the national postal and telecommunications services in 1950, as set forth by the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government. The conference also adopted letters of respect to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

The conference elected Zhu Xuefan, Li Jinghan, Liu Yin, Wang Zigang, and sixty others as members of the National Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, Li Yaotian, Huo Zhaolin, Zhou Boyue, and fifteen others as alternate members, and Yan Xiaofeng, Yang Zaoxin, and nine others as members of the Financial Review Committee, formally establishing the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union.

During the conference, resolutions were passed on the implementation of democratic management in postal services, interim measures on working hours, rest periods, and labor protection, the gradual signing of collective contracts in telecommunications enterprises, the promotion and development of rationalization proposals in postal and telecommunications enterprises, and the strengthening of cultural education for postal and telecommunications workers.

At the closing ceremony on the 27th, Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, and Liu Zijiu, Director of the Culture and Education Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, attended and delivered speeches. Minister Zhu called on postal and telecommunications workers to unite, improve work efficiency, increase revenue and reduce expenditure, lower costs, overcome current difficulties, and complete the 1950 production tasks. Director Liu Zijiu called on everyone to transform the resolutions of this conference into action, to be consistent in word and deed, and strive for greater victories. Delegates from various regions, including Zhu Changcheng and Wang Weibin, stated in their speeches that they would convey the conference resolutions and the leaders' reports to every employee and implement them.

### 全国邮电工会代表大会闭幕  正式成立全国邮电工会  通过保证完成今年邮电事业生产任务决议

1950-03-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国邮电工会代表大会闭幕
    正式成立全国邮电工会
    通过保证完成今年邮电事业生产任务决议
    【新华社二十八日讯】本月十七日在首都召开的中国邮政和电信工会第一次全国代表大会已于二十七日闭幕。会议代表一致通过了中华全国总工会提出的关于统一全国邮政工会和电信工会领导机构的决定，也通过了保证完成中央人民政府邮电部关于一九五○年全国邮电事业的生产任务的决议。会议又通过了向毛主席，朱总司令及中华全国总工会的致敬信。
    会议选出了朱学范、李景韩、刘寅、王子纲等六十人为中国邮电工会全国委员会委员，李尧天、霍兆麟、周伯约等十五人为候补委员，阎晓峰、杨造新等九人为经费审查委员，正式成立中国邮电工会。
    会议期间，曾分别通过了关于邮政实行管理民主化、关于工时工休及劳动保护暂行办法、关于在电信企业中逐步签订集体合同、关于在邮电企业中提倡和开展合理化建议运动以及关于加强邮电职工文化教育等决议。
    在二十七日的大会闭幕式中，邮电部朱学范部长、中华全国总工会文教部长刘子久都出席讲话。朱部长号召邮电工人团结一致，提高工作效率，开源节流，减低成本，克服当前困难，完成一九五○年的生产任务。刘子久部长号召大家要把这次大会的决议变成行动，要言行一致，争取更大的胜利。各地代表朱长城、王维本等讲话中一致表示要把大会决议和首长报告传达给每一个职工，并贯彻执行。
