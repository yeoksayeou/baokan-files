Title: Romanian Cooperative Representatives' Conference Opens, Central Cooperative Affairs Administration Sends Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1950-03-23
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: The first Romanian People's Republic Cooperative Representatives' Conference was held from the 19th to the 23rd of this month. They previously sent a telegram inviting our country to send representatives to attend; however, due to the late notice, our country was unable to send personnel. Meng Yongqian, Director of our Central Cooperative Affairs Administration, sent a telegram on the 18th to express gratitude and congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows:

　　To all comrades of the First Romanian People's Republic Cooperative Representatives' Conference:

　　Thank you sincerely for your invitation. Unfortunately, due to the late notice, we are unable to send anyone to participate in this grand event. On behalf of the 11 million cooperative members of the New China, I extend the warmest fraternal greetings to the Representatives' Conference and all cooperative members of your country, and wish the conference success and a victorious advance on the road of consolidating the worker-peasant alliance and building socialism.

　　Meng Yongqian, Director of the Central Cooperative Affairs Administration of the People's Republic of China
　　March 18, 1950

### 罗马尼亚合作社代表会开幕  中央合作事业管理局电贺

1950-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚合作社代表会开幕
    中央合作事业管理局电贺
    【新华社二十二日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国第一届合作社代表大会于本月十九日至二十三日召开，前曾来电邀请我国派代表出席；惟因时间已迟，我国不克派员参加。我中央合作事业管理局局长孟用潜特于十八日致电感谢并表示祝贺。电文如下：
    罗马尼亚人民共和国第一届合作社代表大会全体同志们：
    衷心感谢你们的邀请，惜时间已迟，不克派人前来躬与盛会，谨代表新中国合作社一千一百万社员，向代表大会和贵国全体合作社社员致最热烈的兄弟敬礼，并祝大会成功，在巩固工农联盟、走向社会主义建设大道上胜利前进。
    中华人民共和国中央合作事业管理局局长孟用潜
      一九五○年三月十八日
