Author:
Date: 1950-03-16
Page: 4

　　Radio Programs
　　[This newspaper reports] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (16th) at 17:30 Women's Time: "Women in Production and Disaster Relief." At 18:30 Natural Science Lectures: General Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 24, "Vacuum Tubes." At 18:00 First Literary Program: Gu Rongfu and Yin Fulai perform the brand-name opera "Chunyun's Divorce." At 21:00 Second Literary Program: The Beijing Opera Experimental School broadcasts the new Beijing Opera: "Mulan Joins the Army," Part 3. At 18:45 New Songs: "Iron Tree Blooms" and "Brother and Sister Reclaim Wasteland."
　　[Another News Item] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 12:30 Workers' Program: Workers' Lecture: Chen Yongwen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions speaks on "Democratization of Factory Management." At 13:00 Youth Program: (1) School News, (2) Talking about Tibet, (3) The 8th Middle School and the Girls' No. 1 Middle School Choir perform new songs. At 16:30 Municipal Voice: Introducing the Beijing Municipal Sanitation Engineering Bureau's sewer project for this year. At 17:00 Children's Program: Soviet fairy tale "The Blue Carpet." At 18:45 Listener Service: (1) Answering questions about the People's Victory Parity Bonds and the Northeast Production and Construction Parity Bonds, (2) Enrollment brochure for the technical cadre training class of the North China Water Conservancy Engineering Bureau. At 19:00 Russian Language Lectures.

### 广播节目

1950-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（十六日）十七点三十分妇女时间：“生产救灾中的妇女”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：电学常识第二十四讲“真空管”。十八点第一次文艺节目：顾荣甫、尹福来演唱牌子戏《春云离婚》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：京剧实验学校演播新京剧：《木兰从军》第三部分。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《铁树开花》、《兄妹开荒》。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人节目：工人讲座：全总陈用文讲“工厂管理民主化问题”。十三点青年节目：（１）学校新闻，（２）谈谈西藏，（３）八中、女一中歌咏团演唱新歌。十六点三十分市政之声：介绍北京市卫生工程局本年度的下水道工程。十七点儿童节目：苏联童话《蓝地毯》。十八点四十五分听众服务：（１）回答关于人民胜利折实公债和东北生产建设折实公债的问题，（２）华北水利工程局技术干部训练班的招生简章。十九点俄语讲座。
