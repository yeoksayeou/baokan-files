Title: Beijing People's Broadcasting Station to Hold Broadcast Lecture Series on "On New Democracy"; First Session to be Held on the 18th Morning
Author:
Date: 1950-03-17
Page: 4

　　Beijing People's Broadcasting Station
    Sets up Broadcast Lecture Series on "On New Democracy"
    First Session to be Held on the 18th Morning
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] In order to answer the difficulties and questions of the universities in Beijing and Tianjin in the political course of studying "On New Democracy," the Higher Education Department of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government has decided to hold a broadcast lecture series on the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station starting on the 18th of this month. The time is from 10:00 AM to 11:30 AM, and the Tianjin People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast simultaneously. The lecture series will be held seven times within this semester, ending in early June. On March 18th, the lecture will be on the historical characteristics of the Chinese revolution. On April 1st and April 15th, the lecture will be on the history of the Chinese New Democratic Revolution, on April 29th, the lecture will be on the main experiences of the Chinese revolution, on May 13th, the lecture will be on the politics of New Democracy, on May 27th, the lecture will be on the economy of New Democracy, on June 3rd, the lecture will be on the culture of New Democracy, on June 10th, the lecture will be on the culture of New Democracy, and on June 17th, the lecture will be on the future of the Chinese revolution. The first lecture will be given by Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government. The wavelength of the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station is 353 meters, 850 kilocycles; 447.4 meters, 670 kilocycles.

### 北京人民广播电台  设广播学习讲座讲《新民主主义论》  第一次于十八日上午举行

1950-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京人民广播电台
    设广播学习讲座讲《新民主主义论》
    第一次于十八日上午举行
    【本报讯】中央人民政府教育部高等教育司为了解答京津各大学在学习《新民主主义论》政治课当中的疑难和问题，决定从本月十八日起，在北京市人民广播电台举办广播学习讲座。时间是上午十时至十一时半，天津人民广播电台同时联播。该讲座在本学期内到六月初旬止，共举办七次。本月十八日讲中国革命的历史特点。四月一日和四月十五日讲中国新民主主义革命史，四月二十九日讲中国革命的主要经验，五月十三日讲新民主主义的政治，五月二十七日讲新民主主义的经济，六月三日讲新民主主义的文化，六月十日讲新民主主义的文化，六月十七日讲中国革命的前途。第一次由中央人民政府教育部副部长钱俊瑞主讲。北京市人民广播电台波长是三五三公尺，八五零千周；四四七·四公尺，六七零千周。
