Title: Domestic Postage Rates Adjusted Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1950-03-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 10th: With the approval of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government has adjusted domestic postage rates starting March 11th as follows: 1. Letters: Ordinary letters, 1,000 yuan per 20 grams; single registered mail, 4,000 yuan; double registered mail, 7,000 yuan; ordinary express mail, 2,000 yuan; registered express mail, 5,000 yuan; postcards, 500 yuan; news manuscripts, 800 yuan. 2. Newspapers and Printed Matter: Except for bulk newspapers, which are adjusted to 200 yuan per kilogram, the existing rates will continue to apply to other ordinary newspapers, coupon newspapers, and various printed matter. 3. Fees for inquiries, redirection, poste restante, and re-issuance will be adjusted proportionally to the original amounts. As for the postage rates in Northeast China, they will remain unchanged.

### 国内邮资今起调整

1950-03-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　国内邮资今起调整
    【新华社北京十日电】中央人民政府邮电部经呈奉政务院财政经济委员会核准，自三月十一日起调整国内邮资如下：一、信函：每重二十公分平信一千元，单挂号四千元，双挂号七千元，平常快递二千元，挂号快递五千元，明信片五百元，新闻稿件八百元。二、新闻纸类及印刷品类：除总包新闻纸调整为每公斤二百元外，其余普通新闻纸、立券新闻纸及各种印刷品类，仍照现行资费收取。三、查询、改寄、存局候领、补领手续费等，均按原数比例调整。至于东北各地邮资仍照原数收取。
