Title: Beijing People's Bank Extends Government Bond Sales Time Today and Tomorrow to Facilitate Purchases by Business and Industry and Local People
Author:
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 4

　　Beijing People's Bank
    Extends Government Bond Sales Time Today and Tomorrow
    To Facilitate Purchases by Business and Industry and Local People

    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] To facilitate the purchase of government bonds by business and industry circles and local people, the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China has extended the government bond sales time for today and tomorrow (30th and 31st). Sales will begin at 8:00 a.m., with no break at noon, and will continue until 5:30 p.m. According to the bank's statistics, the number of government bonds sold has increased significantly in recent days, with 93,056 shares sold on the 27th and 89,020 shares sold on the 28th. As of yesterday (29th), a total of 2,248,868 shares of government bonds have been sold. In order to complete the glorious task of purchasing government bonds on schedule, the government bond promotion associations of Beijing's business and industry circles and local people have convened mobilization meetings in recent days, calling for prompt payment and bond purchases.

### 京市人民银行  今明延长售卖公债时间  便利工商界及地方人士购买

1950-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市人民银行
    今明延长售卖公债时间
    便利工商界及地方人士购买
    【京市新闻处讯】中国人民银行北京分行为便于工商界及地方人士等购买公债，特将今、明（三十、三十一）两日售卖公债时间延长，上午八时开始，中午不休息，下午延至五时半。据该行统计，发售公债数字近日已有显著增加，二十七日售出九万三千零五十六分，二十八日售出八万九千零二十分。截至昨（二十九）日止已共售出公债二百二十四万八千八百六十八分。京市工商界及地方人士的推销公债分会为如期完成购买公债的光荣任务，连日分别召集动员大会，号召迅速缴款购债。
