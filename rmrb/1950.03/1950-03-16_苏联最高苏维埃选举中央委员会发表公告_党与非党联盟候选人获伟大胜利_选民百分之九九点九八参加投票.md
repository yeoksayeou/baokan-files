Title: Announcement by the Central Election Commission of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union: Candidates of the League of Communists and Non-Party People Achieve Great Victory; 99.98 Percent of Voters Participate in the Election
Author:
Date: 1950-03-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 15th, TASS dispatch: The Central Election Commission of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has released an announcement regarding the results of the election held on March 12, 1950.

The announcement states: The Central Election Commission has received detailed results of the Supreme Soviet elections from all district election committees. The final figure for the total number of voters in all electoral districts has been determined as 111,116,373, of whom 111,109,010, or 99.98 percent of all voters, participated in the Supreme Soviet elections.

In all electoral districts for the Union Council, 111,078,377 people, or 99.73 percent of the total number of voters participating in the election, voted for the candidates of the League of Communists and Non-Party People. 300,146 people, or 0.27 percent of the total number of voters participating in the election, voted against the candidates for representatives to the Union Council. According to Article 88 of the "Election Law of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union," 1,487 ballots were deemed invalid.

In all electoral districts for the Council of Nationalities, 111,078,209 voters, or 99.72 percent of the total number of voters participating in the election, voted for the candidates of the League of Communists and Non-Party People. 306,382 people, or 0.28 percent of the total number of voters participating in the election, voted against the candidates for representatives to the Council of Nationalities. According to Article 88 of the "Election Law of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union," 1,619 ballots were deemed invalid.

The total number of voters in all electoral districts within the Autonomous Republics, Autonomous Regions, and National Areas electing to the Council of Nationalities was 8,726,204, of whom 8,723,874, or 99.97 percent of all voters, went to the polls to vote. Within these electoral districts, 8,684,806 people, or 99.55 percent of the total number of voters who went to the polls, voted for the candidates of the League of Communists and Non-Party People. 38,953 voters voted against the candidates for representatives to the Council of Nationalities from the Autonomous Republics, Autonomous Regions, and National Areas. According to Article 88 of the "Election Law of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union," 115 ballots were deemed invalid. According to Article 38 of the "Election Law of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union," after separately reviewing all materials from each electoral district, the Central Election Commission has registered the election of the Supreme Soviet representatives from all 1,316 electoral districts.

### 苏联最高苏维埃选举中央委员会发表公告  党与非党联盟候选人获伟大胜利  选民百分之九九点九八参加投票

1950-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃选举中央委员会发表公告
    党与非党联盟候选人获伟大胜利
    选民百分之九九点九八参加投票
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十五日电：苏联最高苏维埃选举中央选举委员会顷发表关于一九五○年三月十二日选举结果的公告。
    该公告称：中央选举委员会已经从所有区选举委员会得悉苏联最高苏维埃选举的详细结果。所有选区之全体选民人数其最后数字已确定为一亿一千一百一十一万六千三百七十三人，其中有一亿一千一百零九万零十人亦即全体选民之百分之九十九·九八参加了苏联最高苏维埃选举。
    在所有选举联盟院的选区中，有一亿一千零七十八万八千三百七十七人，亦即参加选举的选民总数中有百分之九十九·七三投票选举党与非党联盟的候选人。有三十万零一百四十六人，亦即赴参加选举的选民总数中有百分之○·二七，对联盟院代表候选人投反对票。根据“苏联最高苏维埃选举法”第八十八条，有一千四百八十七张票被认为无效。
    在所有选举民族院的选区中，有一亿一千零七十八万二千零九名选民，即参加选举的选民总数之百分之九十九·七二投票选举党与非党联盟的候选人。有三十万六千三百八十二人亦即参加选举的选民总数中有百分之○·二八对民族院代表候选人投反对票。根据“苏联最高苏维埃选举法”第八十八条，有一千六百一十九张选票被认为无效。
