Title: Central North Vietnam People's Army Liberates French Troops, Killing 7,000 in Eight Months
Author:
Date: 1950-03-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 17th: According to a communique issued yesterday by the Central North Vietnam Military Headquarters, from July 1949 to February 1950, the Vietnam People's Liberation Army fought 2,000 battles in the central North Vietnam region, wiping out 7,000 French troops, liberating 19 French strongholds, destroying 5 French aircraft, 22 trucks, 2 tanks, 1 train, 16 small steamboats, and large quantities of weapons and ammunition. The Liberation Army captured 200 machine guns, 6 mortars, 300 rifles, and 350,000 rounds of ammunition. The troops in the central North Vietnam region also helped local residents harvest 1.2 million hectares of rice fields.

### 北越中部越南解放军  八个月歼法军七千人

1950-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　北越中部越南解放军
    八个月歼法军七千人
    【新华社二十日讯】越南通讯社北越十七日讯：据北越中部地区陆军总部昨日发表公报称：自一九四九年七月至一九五○年二月止，越南人民解放军在北越中部地区作战两千次，歼灭法军七千人，解放法军据点十九处，击毁法机五架，卡车二十二辆，坦克两辆，火车一列，小汽船十六只，和大量的武器弹药。解放军缴获机枪二百挺，迫击炮六门，步枪三百支，和子弹三十五万发。北越中部地区军队并曾帮助当地居民收割了一百二十万公顷的稻田。
