Title: Kim Il-sung Sends Congratulations on Birthday
Author:
Date: 1950-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th. Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, sent a telegram to Vyacheslav Molotov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, on the 8th, congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram stated: "The Korean people extend their infinite gratitude to you for your international support of the interests of the Korean nation and for the tremendous assistance you have given to the Korean people in strengthening Korean-Soviet friendship. We wish you longevity and greater achievements in the cause of happiness for the working people of the great country and all progressive humanity."

### 金日成电贺寿辰

1950-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　金日成电贺寿辰
    【新华社平壤八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议主席金日成，八日致电苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫，祝贺他的六十寿辰。贺电中说：“朝鲜人民对你在国际间拥护朝鲜民族的利益，和你在加强朝苏友好的工作中给予朝鲜人民的巨大援助，致以无限的感谢。祝你万寿无疆，并祝你为伟大国家的劳动人民和一切进步人类的幸福事业，有更大成就。”
