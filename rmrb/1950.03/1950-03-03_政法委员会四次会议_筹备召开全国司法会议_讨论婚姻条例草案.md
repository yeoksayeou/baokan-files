　　Political and Legal Committee Holds Fourth Meeting
    Preparing to Convene National Judicial Conference
    Discussing Draft Marriage Regulations
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st] The Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council convened its fourth meeting on the 28th of this month. Twenty-eight committee members were present, including Peng Zhen, Zhang Xiruo, Chen Shaoyu, Peng Zemin, Wu Yuzhang, Xie Juezai, Shen Junru, Shi Liang, Liu Wang Liming, Liao Chengzhi, Xu Deheng, and Lan Gongwu. Vice Chairman Peng Zhen presided over the meeting. The meeting heard a report from Minister of Justice Shi Liang regarding preparations for convening a national judicial conference and plans to organize inspection teams jointly with the Supreme People's Court, the Supreme People's Procuratorate, and the Legislative Affairs Commission. The meeting also discussed the draft marriage regulations prepared by the Legislative Affairs Commission and will submit them to the Government Administration Council for review and approval after revisions.

### 政法委员会四次会议  筹备召开全国司法会议  讨论婚姻条例草案

1950-03-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　政法委员会四次会议
    筹备召开全国司法会议       
    讨论婚姻条例草案
    【新华社北京一日电】政务院政治法律委员会于本月二十八日召开第四次委员会议，到彭真、张奚若、陈绍禹、彭泽民、吴玉章、谢觉哉、沈钧儒、史良、刘王立明、廖承志、许德珩、蓝公武等委员二十八人，由彭真副主任主持。会议听取了司法部史良部长关于筹备召开全国司法会议及会同最高人民法院、最高人民检察署、法制委员会组织视察组计划，并讨论了法制委员会拟订的婚姻条例草案，将于修正后通过报请政务院审核。
