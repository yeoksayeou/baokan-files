　　Broadcast Programs
    [This Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (25th) 17:30 Youth Time: An address by Leo Figuères, representative of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and Secretary-General of the French Republican Youth Union. 20:20: (1) An article written by economist Zhang Naiqi, "Unity is Strength, Unity is the Solution"; (2) News report: Crushing the US imperialist plot to plunder the Hai Xuan ship. 18:00 First Arts Program: Jingyun Dagu "Twenty Jin of Rice." 21:00 Second Arts Program: Amateur National Music Research Society performance of national music. 18:30 Soviet Songs. 18:45 New Songs.

### 广播节目

1950-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（二十五日）十七时三十分青年时间：世界民主青年联盟代表，法国共和青年联盟总书记雷奥·斐盖尔广播演讲。二十时二十分：（一）经济学家章乃器写的一篇文章《统一就是力量，统一就是办法》；（二）通讯：粉碎美帝国主义劫夺海玄号轮船的阴谋。十八时第一次文艺节目：京韵大鼓“二十斤米”。二十一时第二次文艺节目：业余国乐研究社演播国乐。十八时三十分苏联歌曲。十八时四十五分新歌曲。
