Title: Three Soviet Professors Begin Academic Lectures in China
Author:
Date: 1950-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th. The academic lectures scheduled by the three Soviet professors visiting China began today. Professor Nurzhin, Doctor of Biology, lectured on "The Struggle Between Materialism and Idealism in Biology" at the auditorium of Sino-French University in the morning. The first lecture was given, and the second lecture was given at the same place in the afternoon. Professor Kiselev, Doctor of History, lectured on the first lecture of "Soviet Historical Science and History Teaching" at the auditorium of Fu Jen Catholic University in the afternoon. Associate Professor Makarova, Master of Economics, lectured on "Lenin's and Stalin's Theory on the Transition from Capitalism to Socialism" at Fu Jen Catholic University in the morning, and gave a lecture on the same topic at Tsinghua University in the afternoon. The audience participating in the above lectures included students from the history, economics, and biology departments of other universities in Beijing, all students from Tsinghua University, history and biology teachers from primary and secondary schools, students from Revolution University, Foreign Languages ​​School, Marxist-Leninist College and other schools, and more than 7,500 people from more than 40 units affiliated to the Cultural and Educational Committee, the Financial and Economic Committee, and the Political and Legal Committee. This lecture will continue in Beijing until the 19th. The broad masses of people have expressed great importance and warm welcome to this lecture. Various agencies and organizations have invited the three professors to give lectures. As of the 10th, the People's University, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Central Youth League School, and the North China Military and Political University have been agreed upon.

### 苏联来华三教授开始作学术讲演

1950-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联来华三教授开始作学术讲演
    【新华社北京十日电】苏联来华三教授预定的学术讲演，已于今日开始。生物学博士努日金教授上午在中法大学大礼堂讲：“生物学中唯物论与唯心论的斗争”。第一讲，下午仍在原地讲第二讲。历史学博士吉谢列夫教授下午在辅仁大学大礼堂讲“苏联的历史科学与历史教学”第一讲。经济学硕士马卡洛娃副教授上午在辅仁大学讲“列宁斯大林关于从资本主义到社会主义过渡时期的理论”第一讲，并于下午赴清华大学作同一题目的讲演。参加上述讲演的听众有北京市其他各大学历史系、经济系、生物系、学生和清华大学全体学生，中小学历史和生物教员，革命大学、外国语学校、马列学院等校学生以及文教委员会、财经委员会、政法委员会等所属机关四十余单位，共计七千五百多人。此次讲学在北京将延续至十九日。广大群众对此次讲学都表示十分重视和热烈欢迎。各机关团体已纷纷邀请三教授前往讲演，截至十日止已经商妥的有人民大学、北京市中苏友好协会、中央团校以及华北军政大学等单位。
