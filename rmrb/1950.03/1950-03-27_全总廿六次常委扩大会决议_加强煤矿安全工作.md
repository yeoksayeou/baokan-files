Title: Resolution of the 26th Enlarged Meeting of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Standing Committee: Strengthening Coal Mine Safety Work
Author:
Date: 1950-03-27
Page: 1

　　Resolution of the 26th Enlarged Meeting of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Standing Committee
    Strengthening Coal Mine Safety Work
    【Xinhua News Agency, 26th】The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held its 26th Standing Committee Enlarged Meeting on the 23rd, presided over by Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi. The meeting discussed, revised, and approved the “Draft Resolution on Strengthening Coal Mine Safety Work” proposed by the preparatory committee of the National Coal Mine Trade Union. It agreed to the “Joint Instruction on Strengthening the Implementation of Democratization of Coal Mine Management” jointly issued by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government. The meeting listened to and discussed the report on the preparatory work for the National Trade Union Organizational Work Conference and the opening of the National Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union Representative Conference. Finally, the meeting also decided to establish the National Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union.

### 全总廿六次常委扩大会决议  加强煤矿安全工作

1950-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总廿六次常委扩大会决议
    加强煤矿安全工作
    【新华社二十六日讯】中华全国总工会于二十三日举行第二十六次常务委员会扩大会议，由刘宁一副主席主持。会议讨论、修正和通过了全国煤矿工会筹备委员会提出的“关于加强煤矿安全工作的决议草案”，同意了全国总工会与中央人民政府燃料工业部共同发表的关于“加强贯彻煤矿管理民主化的联合指示”。会议听取并讨论了全国工会组织工作会议的准备工作报告和全国邮电工会代表会议的开会情况。最后，会议并决定成立中国邮电工会全国委员会。
