Title: Beijing Municipal Fourth Girls' Middle School Jointly Commemorates "March 8th"
Author:
Date: 1950-03-07
Page: 4

　　Beijing Municipal Fourth Girls' Middle School Jointly Commemorates "March 8th"
    [Beijing News] Yesterday (the 6th) at 2:00 PM, Beijing Municipal First, Second, Third, and Fourth Girls' Middle Schools jointly held a commemorative meeting for the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day at the auditorium of Girls' Third Middle School. More than 2,000 teachers and students listened to speeches by Shuai Mengqi, Minister of the Women's Federation of China, and Zhang Xiaomei, Chairman of the Beijing Women's Federation. Shuai Mengqi first explained that the current situation is different from previous years, and that the construction of the new China is a long-term and arduous task. She encouraged everyone to study hard, improve their political and cultural levels, and prepare to participate in the construction of the new China. In her speech, Zhang Xiaomei told the students how to correctly handle family relationships and issues related to love and marriage, and expressed her hope that the students would commemorate "March 8th" by studying in depth and eliminating old ideas. Finally, the Bureau of Culture and Education's film team screened three films, including "Today's Soviet Union."
    [Another News] The Beijing "March 8th" Women's Day Preparatory Committee held a recreational evening party at the auditorium of the Ministry of Railways last night (the 6th) to entertain working women and female workers from various districts of the city. Also: The Peking Union Medical College Hospital and the Young Women's Christian Association also held activities yesterday to commemorate "March 8th," and invited Zhang Xiaomei, Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, and Secretary-General Hu Yizai to attend and speak, respectively.

### 京市立四女中联合纪念“三八”

1950-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市立四女中联合纪念“三八”
    【北京讯】北京市立第一、二、三、四女子中学于昨（六）日下午二时在女三中礼堂联合举行“三八”国际劳动妇女节纪念大会。全体师生二千余人听取全国妇联组织部长帅孟奇和北京市妇联主席张晓梅的讲话。帅孟奇首先说明今年形势和往年不同，新中国的建设是长期而艰巨的工作。她鼓励大家努力学习，提高政治文化水平，准备参加新中国的建设工作。张晓梅讲话时告诉同学们如何正确的处理家庭关系和恋爱婚姻问题，并希望同学们能以深入学习和清除旧思想来纪念“三八”。最后由文教局电影队放映“今天的苏联”等三影片。
    【又讯】京市“三八”妇女节筹委会，昨（六）日晚在铁道部大礼堂举行游艺晚会，招待全市各区劳动妇女和女工。又：昨日举行纪念“三八”节活动的还有协和医院及女青年会，分别请北京市民主妇联主席张晓梅及秘书长胡一哉出席讲话。
