Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Natural Science Lectures, Programs from the 26th to the end of the Month
Author:
Date: 1950-03-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: The Central People's Broadcasting Station's Natural Science Lecture program from the 26th to the end of the month is as follows:

　　March 26th: Soviet Great Scientist Komarov
　　March 27th: Spinning and Weaving
　　March 28th: How is Paper Made?
　　March 29th: The Moon
　　March 30th: Lunar Eclipse
　　March 31st: The Total Lunar Eclipse on April 3rd

　　All programs will be broadcast at 18:30 every evening.
