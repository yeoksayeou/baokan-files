Title: Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace Concludes Third Plenary Session, Calling for Banning of Atomic Weapons; Second World Congress of Supporters of Peace to be Held in Italy in the Fourth Quarter of This Year; Establishment of International Peace Prize with Nenni as Chairman of the Judging Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-03-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, 21st - TASS dispatch: The Third Plenary Session of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace concluded on the 19th, after continuing the discussion of the report by Secretary-General Rafael and passing a resolution.

Representatives from France, Australia, Cuba, South Africa, Mexico, Denmark, Venezuela, Mongolia, Albania, Finland, Israel, and the International Democratic Women's Federation spoke during the discussion.

The plenary session then discussed the motion on the prohibition of atomic weapons and the convening of the Second World Congress of Supporters of Peace, and unanimously adopted the following appeal:

We extend this appeal to all social and religious organizations, cultural workers, and all honest people—regardless of their views on the causes of the current international tension, as long as they are concerned about this situation and sincerely wish to rebuild peaceful relations among all nations.

We demand the unconditional prohibition of atomic weapons as weapons of aggression and mass destruction of people, and demand the establishment of strict international control to ensure the implementation of this decision. Any government that is the first to use atomic weapons against any country will be regarded as a war criminal.

We call on all people of goodwill around the world to sign this appeal.

We suggest that all people of goodwill appoint representatives to participate in the Second World Congress of Supporters of Peace, which will be held in Italy in the fourth quarter of 1950.

At its final session, the plenary session passed resolutions on organizational matters and the establishment of the International Peace Prize, and elected the prize judging committee, with Nenni (Italy) as Chairman, Mrs. Gordon (France) and Professor Bernal (England) as Vice-Chairmen, and Boissari (Brazil), Guo Moruo (China), Kent (USA), Vasilevskaya (USSR), and Muzharovsky (Czechoslovakia) as members. After this, the plenary session concluded its work.

### 和大常委三次全会闭幕  呼吁禁用原子武器  今年第四季在意举行二次世界和平大会设立国际和平奖金南尼任评议委会主席

1950-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　和大常委三次全会闭幕
    呼吁禁用原子武器
    今年第四季在意举行二次世界和平大会设立国际和平奖金南尼任评议委会主席
    【新华社二十一日讯】综合塔斯社斯德哥尔摩讯：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会第三次全体会议于十九日在继续讨论常委会总书记拉斐德的报告并通过决议后，宣布闭幕。
    在讨论中发言的有法国、澳大利亚、古巴、南非、墨西哥、丹麦、委内瑞拉、蒙古、阿尔巴尼亚、芬兰、以色列等国的代表及国际民主妇联的代表。
    全会继而讨论了关于禁止原子武器和召开第二次世界拥护和平大会的议案，并一致通过了下列呼吁书：
    我们特向一切社会与宗教团体、文化工作者和所有正直的人们——不论他们对当前国际形势所以紧张的原因持何见解，只要是对这种形势感到忧心并且极愿在各民族间重建和平关系的人们——发出呼吁。
    我们要求无条件地禁止把原子武器当作侵略与大规模毁灭人民的武器，并要求建立严格的国际管制，以确保这一决定的执行。任何第一个使用原子武器来对付任何国家的政府，我们均将认之为战争罪犯。
    我们号召全世界一切善意的人们在这一呼吁书上签字。
    我们建议一切善意的人们指派代表来参加即将于一九五○年第四季在意大利举行的第二次世界拥护和平大会。
    全会的末次会议通过了关于组织问题以及设立国际和平奖金的决议，并选出了奖金评议委员会，主席为南尼（意）副主席为戈登夫人（法）与贝尔纳教授（英），委员有波萨里（巴西）、郭沫若（中）、肯特（美）、华西列夫斯卡雅（苏）、穆加若夫斯基（捷）等人。在此之后，全会即结束工作。
