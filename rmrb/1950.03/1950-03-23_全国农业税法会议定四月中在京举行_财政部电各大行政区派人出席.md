Title: National Agricultural Tax Law Conference Scheduled for Mid-April in Beijing; Ministry of Finance Cables Major Administrative Regions to Send Representatives
Author:
Date: 1950-03-23
Page: 2

　　National Agricultural Tax Law Conference Scheduled for Mid-April in Beijing
　　Ministry of Finance Cables Major Administrative Regions to Send Representatives
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 21st] The National Agricultural Tax Law Conference is scheduled to be held in the capital on April 15th. The Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government has telegraphed the finance departments of the major administrative regions, instructing them to send personnel in charge of agricultural tax policy (limited to two people), and also requesting each major administrative region to select two directors of provincial finance departments who are relatively familiar with agricultural tax work from the provinces under their jurisdiction to attend the conference. Attending personnel are scheduled to begin registration on April 10th.

### 全国农业税法会议定四月中在京举行  财政部电各大行政区派人出席

1950-03-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国农业税法会议定四月中在京举行
    财政部电各大行政区派人出席
    【新华社二十一日讯】全国农业税法会议定期于四月十五日在首都举行。中央人民政府财政部已电知各大行政区财政部，派农业税税政主管人员（限二人），并由各该大行政区在所辖省抽派比较熟悉农业税工作的财政厅长二人，出席会议。出席人员定于四月十日起开始报到。
