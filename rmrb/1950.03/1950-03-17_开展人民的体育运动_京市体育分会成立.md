Title: Developing People's Sports: Beijing Municipal Sports Association Established
Author:
Date: 1950-03-17
Page: 4

　　Developing People's Sports: Beijing Municipal Sports Association Established
    [Our Newspaper] In order to unite sports workers and enthusiasts in the city and better promote mass sports activities for the people of Beijing, the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, in response to the call of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation, jointly initiated the organization of the "Beijing Municipal Sports Association" and held a symposium on the 14th of this month at the Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League. Representatives from the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation, the Bureau of Culture and Education, the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Municipal Education Union, the Public Security Headquarters, the YMCA, and other units, as well as more than ten people from the sports community, including Ma John, Xu Yingchao, and Duan Bingchen, attended. The meeting discussed and approved the plan to establish the Beijing Municipal Sports Association, and nominated more than ten people, including Hou Junyan, Zhang Dazhong, Li Fenglou, Guan Yushan, Duan Bingchen, representatives from the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and representatives from the North China Military Region, to be responsible for the preparatory work for the establishment of the Beijing Municipal Sports Association.
        (Beijing Youth Agency)

### 开展人民的体育运动  京市体育分会成立

1950-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　开展人民的体育运动  京市体育分会成立
    【本报讯】为了团结全市体育工作者及体育爱好者，更好地开展北京市人民大众的体育运动，北京市人民政府文教局和青年团北京市工作委员会，响应中华全国体育总会筹委会的号召，共同发起组织“北京市体育分会”，并于本月十四日在团市工委会召开了座谈会。出席的有体育总会筹委会、文教局、市总工会、团市工委、市教联、公安总队、青年会等单位代表及体育界马约翰、徐英超、段炳辰等十余人。会上讨论并通过成立北京市体育分会的计划，推出侯俊岩、张大中、李凤楼、管玉珊、段炳辰、市总工会代表、华北军区代表等十余人负责成立北京市体育分会的筹备工作。
        （北京青年社）
