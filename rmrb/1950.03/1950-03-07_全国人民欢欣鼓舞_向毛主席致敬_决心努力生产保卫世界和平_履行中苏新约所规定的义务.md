Title: The Whole Nation Rejoices and Pays Tribute to Chairman Mao, Resolves to Work Hard to Produce and Defend World Peace, and Fulfill the Obligations Stipulated in the Sino-Soviet Treaty
Author: 
Date: 1950-03-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 6th: The news of Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou's return from the Soviet Union has aroused great excitement among people in various regions. After hearing the news, schools in Nanjing immediately organized a propaganda team of thousands of people, holding up a huge banner that read "Chairman Mao is Back" and conducting propaganda in the city center. The Henan Provincial Government and the Henan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, representing the people of the entire province, sent telegrams to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou to express their congratulations and condolences. Workers, students, and citizens in Chongqing, Changsha, and other places were all overjoyed after hearing the news.

The commanders and fighters of the Southwest Military Region, the Central South Military Region, and the Fourth Field Army expressed their gratitude and condolences to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou for their hard work for the country. Lin Biao, Commander of the Fourth Field Army, Luo Ronghuan, Political Commissar, Deng Zihui, and Tan Zheng, Deputy Political Commissar, led all commanders and fighters in sending a telegram to Chairman Mao to pay their respects. The telegram stated briefly: We have launched discussions and studies of the Sino-Soviet Treaty throughout the army and are determined to eliminate the remaining bandits on Hainan Island and the bandits in various parts of Central South China under your leadership and the leadership of the Central People's Government. We will work hard to rectify and train troops, improve military, political quality and cultural level, and actively participate in production and construction, so as to better fulfill the obligations stipulated in the treaty in the common cause of opposing aggression and defending peace.

### 全国人民欢欣鼓舞  向毛主席致敬  决心努力生产保卫世界和平  履行中苏新约所规定的义务

1950-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民欢欣鼓舞
    向毛主席致敬
    决心努力生产保卫世界和平
    履行中苏新约所规定的义务
    【新华社北京六日电】毛主席与周总理由苏联返国的消息，引起各地人民的极大兴奋。南京各学校听到消息后立即组织了千人的宣传队，高举着“毛主席回来了”的巨幅标语，在市中心进行宣传。河南省政府及中共河南省委会等代表全省人民，致电毛主席及周总理表示敬贺及慰问。重庆、长沙等地的工人、学生及市民听了消息后，莫不欢欣鼓舞。
    西南军区、中南军区及第四野战军的指战员们纷纷对毛主席及周总理的为国辛劳表示感谢与慰问。第四野战军司令员林彪、政治委员罗荣桓、邓子恢、副政治委员谭政率全体指战员致电毛主席致敬，电文略称：我们已在全军展开对中苏新约的讨论和学习，并决心在你和中央人民政府的领导下，肃清海南岛残匪和中南各地的土匪，努力整训部队，提高军事、政治质量和文化水平，积极参加生产建设，以便在反对侵略、保卫和平的共同事业中，很好地履行条约所规定的义务。
