Title: Yunnan People's Bank Establishes Remittance Services with Beijing, Shanghai, and Hankou
Author:
Date: 1950-03-14
Page: 2

　　Yunnan People's Bank Establishes Remittance Services with Beijing, Shanghai, and Hankou
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 13th] The Yunnan Branch of the People's Bank of China has established remittance services with Beijing, Shanghai, Hankou and other locations.
