Title: MacArthur Intensifies Sinister Measures; Large Numbers of Japanese War Criminals to be Released
Author:
Date: 1950-03-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: MacArthur is stepping up the implementation of his sinister measures to release all Japanese war criminals. According to a Kyodo News Agency dispatch from Tokyo on the 23rd, the so-called "Oath Release Committee" established by MacArthur to release all Japanese war criminals, accepted the "Oath Release Applications" of Japanese Class A war criminal Mamoru Shigemitsu and 66 other war criminals on the morning of the 22nd, and will soon begin to "examine" these applications. Committee Chairman Hagen declared that these first batch of war criminals to be released on oath could be released from Sugamo Prison in about "thirty days."

### 麦克阿瑟加紧罪恶措施  大批日战犯将被释放

1950-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　麦克阿瑟加紧罪恶措施
    大批日战犯将被释放
    【新华社二十五日讯】麦克阿瑟正加紧实施其释放全部日本战犯的罪恶措施。据共同社东京二十三日讯：麦克阿瑟为释放全部日本战犯而设立的所谓“宣誓释放委员会”，二十二日晨受理日本甲级战犯重光葵及其他六十六名战犯的“宣誓释放申请”，并即将着手“审查”这些申请。委员会主席哈根宣称：这些第一批被宣誓释放的战犯，大约在“三十天以内”就可从巢鸭监狱中放出。
