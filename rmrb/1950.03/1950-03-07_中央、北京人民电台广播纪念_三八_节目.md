Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Broadcasts Programs Commemorating "March 8th"
Author:
Date: 1950-03-07
Page: 4

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Broadcasts Programs Commemorating "March 8th"
[This Newspaper News] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a special program commemorating the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day today (7th) at 20:20: (1) Zhao Guilan, a selfless female communist party member, (2) Three female co-drivers on the light oil vehicle. 18:30 Lecture on Natural Sciences: General Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 21, "Sound Films." 18:00 First literary program: Cai Liangui sings the Henan Drum "Female Grain Transporter." 21:00 Second literary program: Peking Opera "Hong Niangzi." 18:45 New songs: "People's Happiness Forever," "All the People of the Country Cheer Together," etc.
[Also News] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, 12:30 Workers' Program: Talk and Sing, Miao Peishi, Standing Committee Member of the Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association, tells the Ping Shu "Double Happiness Comes to the Door." 13:00 Youth Program: (1) School News, (2) Ma Hongfen from Girls' No. 1 Middle School talks about "The Attitude That Female Students Should Have in Commemorating March 8th," (3) Huiwen Mu Zhen Joint Choir sings new songs. 16:30 Voice of the Municipality: The Public Service Group of the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau introduces some common knowledge about inquiring about incoming and outgoing telegrams. 17:00 Children's Program: (1) Current Affairs Speech, (2) A letter from the Young Pioneers of the Fourth Field Army Children's Primary School to the children of Beijing, (3) Introducing the children of Beijing, (4) Singing. 18:45 Women's Program: (1) A brief history of the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day, (2) Li Guoying, a teacher at the Central National Elementary School in the Fifth District, talks about "Introducing Several Female Teachers."

### 中央、北京人民电台广播纪念“三八”节目

1950-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台广播纪念“三八”节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（七日）二十点二十分纪念“三八”国际劳动妇女节特别节目：（一）舍己为公的女共产党员赵桂兰，（二）轻油车上的三个女副司机。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：电学常识第二十一讲“有声电影”。十八点第一次文艺节目：蔡连贵演唱西河大鼓“女运粮”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：京剧“红娘子”。十八点四十五分新歌曲：“人民幸福万万年”、“全国人民齐欢笑”等。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人节目：说说唱唱，大众文艺创作研究会常委苗培时说评书“双喜临门”。十三点青年节目：（一）学校新闻，（二）女一中马鸿芬讲“女同学纪念三八应有的态度”，（三）汇文慕贞联合歌咏队演唱新歌曲。十六点三十分市政之声：北京电信局公众服务组介绍关于查询来去电报的一些常识。十七点儿童节目：（一）时事讲话，（二）四野子弟小学少年儿童队员给北京市小朋友们的信，（三）介绍北京少年儿童，（四）歌咏。十八点四十五分妇女节目：“三八”国际劳动妇女节简史，（二）五区中心国民小学教员李国英讲“介绍几位女教员”。
