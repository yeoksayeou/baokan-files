Title: Youth Art Theater to Stage "Patriots"
Author:
Date: 1950-03-05
Page: 3

　　Youth Art Theater to Stage "Patriots"
　　[This Dispatch] The China Youth Art Theater is scheduled to premiere Song Zhi's new four-act, seven-scene play "Patriots" on March 8th. The play, through the heroic story of Han Guixiang, a young female Communist Party member in the Meng Lianggu battle in the Yimeng Mountain area of Shandong, depicts the new people, new morality, and high spirit of loving the motherland that grew up among the Chinese people during the War of Liberation. The theme is clear and the characters are vivid. The performance location is the Beijing Youth Palace.

### 青年艺术剧院公演“爱国者”

1950-03-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　青年艺术剧院公演“爱国者”
    【本报讯】中国青年艺术剧院定于三月八日首次公演宋之的新著四幕七场话剧“爱国者”。该剧系通过山东沂蒙山区孟良崮战役中一个青年女共产党员韩桂香的英勇故事，表现中国人民在解放战争中生长起来的新人物、新道德以及高度热爱祖国的精神。主题鲜明，人物生动。演出地点在京市青年宫。
