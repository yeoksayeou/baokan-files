Title: British Newspaper Correspondent in Taiwan Reveals: The People of Taiwan Eagerly Await Liberation
Author:
Date: 1950-03-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from London, March 20th: A report from the correspondent of the British newspaper Manchester Guardian in Kaohsiung, Taiwan, reveals that the lives of the people of Taiwan are sinking into increasing desperation under the persecution of the Kuomintang remnant bandits. The correspondent said that the Kuomintang bandit gang had arrested more than 2,000 Taiwanese people and forcibly conscripted 35,000 into the army. The correspondent also stated: "The Kuomintang regime in Taiwan is becoming increasingly unpopular, and if the Chinese Communist Party launches an attack, the people of Taiwan will welcome it with satisfaction."

### 英报驻台记者透露  台湾人民切望解放

1950-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　英报驻台记者透露
    台湾人民切望解放
    【新华社讯】据法新社二十日伦敦讯：英国曼彻斯特卫报驻台湾高雄记者的报道透露，台湾人民在国民党残匪的迫害下，生活正日愈陷入绝境。该记者说，国民党匪帮曾逮捕台湾人两千多名，并强征了三万五千人入伍。该记者并称：“国民党政权在台湾是愈来愈更不得民心了，如果中国共产党发动进攻，台湾人民是会以满意的心情来欢迎的。”
