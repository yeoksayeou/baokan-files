Title: Railway Ministry Youth Culture Train Departs Beijing Yesterday, Will Go to Luoyang, Xi'an and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1950-03-26
Page: 1

Railway Ministry Youth Culture Train
    Departed Beijing Yesterday
    Will Go to Luoyang, Xi'an and Other Places
    [Xinhua News Agency, 25th] The Railway Ministry Youth Culture Train, jointly organized by the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government and the China Youth Art Theater, departed Beijing today. Lü Zhengcao, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, Zhou Weizhi, Director of the Art Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Liao Chengzhi, Director of the China Youth Art Theater, and more than a hundred others went to the station to see them off. The train's first work route will be along the Longhai Railway to Luoyang, Xi'an and other places. The first working period will be about half a year. Accompanying work units also include a photography team from the Beijing Film Studio, and a mobile cultural supply station jointly formed by Xinhua Bookstore, Sanlian Bookstore, Worker Publishing House, and Youth Publishing House and other publishing institutions.

### 铁道部青年文化列车昨日离京出发  将赴洛阳西安等地

1950-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　铁道部青年文化列车
    昨日离京出发
    将赴洛阳西安等地
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府铁道部和中国青年艺术剧院联合组织的铁道部青年文化列车，于今日离京。中央人民政府铁道部副部长吕正操、中央人民政府文化部艺术局局长周巍峙、中国青年艺术剧院院长廖承志等百余人赴站欢送。列车第一次出发工作路线将沿陇海路至洛阳、西安等地。第一次工作时间约为半年，随行工作单位尚有北京电影制片厂摄影队，及新华书店、三联书店和工人出版社、青年出版社等出版机构合组之流动文化供应站。
