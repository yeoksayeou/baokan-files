Title: The People's Supervision Committee Sends Supervisory Team to Zhengzhou to Investigate Embezzlement Case of Water Tower Project
Author:
Date: 1950-03-25
Page: 2

　　People's Supervision Committee Sends Supervisory Team to Zhengzhou to Investigate Embezzlement Case of Water Tower Project
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st] Following the major embezzlement and waste incident in the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau's water tower construction project, the Government Administration Council's People's Supervision Committee attached great importance to it. It specially dispatched Senior Supervisory Commissioner Jiang Kaifeng and three others to form a supervisory team, which, together with the Ministry of Railways' inspection team, departed today for Zhengzhou to investigate.

### 人民监察委员会派监察小组前往郑州调查水塔工程贪污案

1950-03-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民监察委员会派监察小组前往郑州调查水塔工程贪污案
    【新华社二十一日讯】郑州铁路局修建水塔工程发生巨大贪污浪费事件后，政务院人民监察委员会极为重视，特派高级监察专员姜凯风等四人组织监察小组，会同铁道部检查组于今日出发前赴郑州调查。
