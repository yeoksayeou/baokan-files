Title: Letter from Czech School Children to Chinese Mothers
Author:
Date: 1950-03-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 8th: Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, received a letter from Czech school children to Chinese mothers, forwarded through the Czech Embassy in our country. The full text is as follows:
Dear Chinese Mothers:

　　Our teachers have told us everything about mothers all over the world. All mothers love their children, and they work for them. Chinese mothers are also working in this way so that Chinese children can live a happy life. Chinese mothers! We ask you: bring the congratulations of the Czech children in Geneva to all Chinese mothers, who are building a better world just like our mothers. When we grow up, we will work hard so that children and mothers all over the world have more happiness.

       Václav Horobeck, Chairman of the Student Group of A. T. Special Middle School, Prague

### 捷克学校儿童致中国妈妈的信

1950-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷克学校儿童致中国妈妈的信
    【新华社北京八日电】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，收到经由捷克驻我国大使馆转来的捷克学校儿童给中国妈妈的一封信，全文如下：亲爱的中国妈妈：
    我们的老师曾把有关于全世界妈妈们的一切事情告诉过我们。所有的妈妈都热爱着他们的孩子，她们为孩子们工作。中国妈妈们也是这样地工作着，以便使中国儿童可以过着幸福生活。中国妈妈！我们请求你：把几内瓦的捷克儿童的祝贺带给所有的中国妈妈们，她们正像我们的妈妈一样地在建造一个美好的世界。等我们长大时，我们将努力工作，以便使全世界的儿童与母亲有更多的幸福。
       布拉格  Ａ·Ｔ·特别中学学生团体主席华西列夫·荷罗贝克
