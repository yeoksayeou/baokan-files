Beijing Municipal Democratic Women's Federation Sends Congratulatory Telegram to "March 8th" Women Drivers
[Beijing News] The Beijing Municipal Democratic Women's Federation recently sent a telegram to the three women drivers who drove the "March 8th" locomotive out of Dalian on March 8th to congratulate them. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrades Tian Guiying, Wang Baohong, and Bi Guiying of the Dalian Sino-Soviet Railway Bureau Locomotive Depot: We are delighted to hear that you have gloriously become the first group of female train drivers in New China. We hereby send our congratulations and wish you success in your endeavors.

### 京市民主妇联电贺“三八”号女司机

1950-03-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　京市民主妇联电贺“三八”号女司机
    【北京讯】北京市民主妇女联合会顷致电三月八日驾驶“三八”号机车开出大连的三位女司机祝贺。电文如下：
    大连市中长路机务段田桂英、王宝鸿、毕桂英同志：欣闻你们光荣地成为新中国的第一批火车女司机，特电致贺，并祝努力。
