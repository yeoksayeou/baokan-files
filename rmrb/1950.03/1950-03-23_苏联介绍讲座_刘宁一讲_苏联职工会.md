Title: Soviet Introduction Lecture: Liu Ningyi on "Soviet Trade Unions"
Author:
Date: 1950-03-23
Page: 3

　　Soviet Introduction Lecture
    Liu Ningyi on "Soviet Trade Unions"
    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The fourth lecture in the weekly Soviet introduction series, sponsored by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, is scheduled for 6:30 PM on March 26th (this Sunday) at the Beijing Soviet Foreign Cultural Association on Nanheyan. Comrade Liu Ningyi will speak on "Soviet Trade Unions." Responsible persons from all branches of the association may attend the lecture upon presentation of their long-term or temporary lecture passes.

### 苏联介绍讲座  刘宁一讲“苏联职工会”

1950-03-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联介绍讲座
    刘宁一讲“苏联职工会”
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市中苏友好协会主办的每周苏联介绍讲座第四讲定于三月二十六日（本星期日）下午六时半在南河沿北京苏联对外文化协会举行。请刘宁一同志讲“苏联的职工会”。该会各支会负责人届时均可持长期或临时听讲证前往听讲。
