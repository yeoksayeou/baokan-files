Title: Three Soviet Professors Arrive in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1950-03-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 24th—Three Soviet professors, Dr. Nuzhdin (Biology), Dr. Giselyev (History), and Ms. Makarova (Economics), who were invited by the All-China Soviet Friendship Association to lecture in China, arrived in Nanjing on the evening of the 23rd from Tianjin. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Ke Qingshi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's Nanjing Branch, Jiang Weiqing, Deputy Secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Yin Zanhsun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's Nanjing Branch and Director of the Paleontology Room of the Geological Survey Institute, and more than 40 other people. This morning, Professors Nuzhdin and the other two professors first received interviews from the press, and then held special discussions with more than ten experts and professors from Nanjing's cultural, educational, and scientific circles, including Yin Zanhsun, Jin Shanbao, He Changhui, and Yu Jianzhang, on various issues related to biology and history. In the afternoon, Dr. Nuzhdin and Dr. Giselyev, accompanied by Liu Shuzhou, Secretary-General of the Municipal Government, visited the Nanjing Museum, the Cultural Relics Preservation Committee, and the former Presidential Palace of the puppet regime. Tonight, the Nanjing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will host a banquet to welcome them.

### 苏联三教授到达南京

1950-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联三教授到达南京
    【新华社南京二十四日电】应中苏友好协会总会之请来我国讲学之苏联生物学博士努日金、历史学博士吉学列夫及经济谢硕士马卡洛娃一行已于二十三日晚由津抵宁。前往车站欢迎者有中苏友协宁分会副会长柯庆施、中共南京市委会副书记江渭清、中苏友协南京分会副总干事、地质调查所古物室主任尹赞勋等四十余人。努日金等三位教授于今日上午首先接待新闻界之访问，然后与南京文化教育科学界尹赞勋、金善宝、贺昌辉、俞建章等十余专家和教授，就生物、历史学各项问题作专题讨论。下午，努日金与吉谢列夫二博士由市政府秘书长刘述周等陪同参观了南京博物院、文物保管委员会及前伪总统府。今日晚，中苏友协宁分会设宴欢迎。
