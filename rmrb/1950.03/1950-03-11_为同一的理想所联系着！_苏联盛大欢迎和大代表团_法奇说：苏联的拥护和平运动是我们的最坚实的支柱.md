Title: Linked by the Same Ideals! The Soviet Union Grandly Welcomes the Delegation of the World Peace Congress. Faci Says: The Soviet Union's Peace Movement is Our Most Solid Pillar
Author:
Date: 1950-03-11
Page: 4

　　Linked by the Same Ideals!
    The Soviet Union Grandly Welcomes the Delegation of the World Peace Congress
    Faci Says: The Soviet Union's Peace Movement is Our Most Solid Pillar
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th] TASS, Moscow: The Soviet Peace Committee held a conference in Moscow on the 7th to welcome the delegation of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. Surkov, Deputy Secretary-General of the Soviet Writers' Union, and the famous Soviet musician Shostakovich delivered welcoming speeches at the meeting, expressing sincere respect to the distinguished guests who actively participated in the world peace struggle. The head of the delegation, Faci, President of the French Peace and Freedom Fighters Association, received a warm welcome from the conference when he delivered his reply. He said: I believe we are all linked by the same feeling and the same ideal. That is the sense of responsibility to our people and the desire to be worthy citizens of our country. What we see here, what we read in your country's newspapers, and everything reflected in your faces now, once again convinces us that your country is a country governed by the people, for the benefit of the people. Therefore, the Soviet Union's peace movement is our most solid pillar.

### 为同一的理想所联系着！  苏联盛大欢迎和大代表团  法奇说：苏联的拥护和平运动是我们的最坚实的支柱

1950-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　为同一的理想所联系着！
    苏联盛大欢迎和大代表团
    法奇说：苏联的拥护和平运动是我们的最坚实的支柱
    【新华社北京十日电】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联拥护和平委员会七日在莫斯科举行大会，欢迎世界拥护和平大会常设委员会代表团。苏联作家协会副总书记苏尔科夫与著名苏联音乐家萧斯塔科维奇在会上致欢迎词，对积极参加世界拥护和平斗争的贵宾们，表示诚挚的敬意。代表团团长、法国和平自由战士协会主席法奇在继起致答辞时，受到大会热烈的欢迎。他说：我相信我们彼此间都是为同一的感觉和同一的理想所联系着。这就是对我们人民的责任感和我们要成为我们国家中当之无愧的公民的愿望。我们在这里所看到的，我们从贵国的报纸上所读到的，以及现在在你们脸上所反映的一切，再次使我们相信贵国是人民执政的国家，是为人民谋福利的。因此，苏联的拥护和平运动是我们的最坚实的支柱。
