Title: Chairman Mao Receives Soviet Ambassador Roshchin
Author:
Date: 1950-03-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Soviet Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, Roshchin, and the economic counselor of the embassy, Akhpopov, at 11 o'clock tonight. Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai was also present.

### 毛主席接见苏大使罗申

1950-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见苏大使罗申
    【新华社二十七日讯】毛泽东主席今晚十一时接见苏联驻中华人民共和国大使罗申及大使馆经济参赞阿赫波夫。周恩来总理兼外交部长亦在座。
