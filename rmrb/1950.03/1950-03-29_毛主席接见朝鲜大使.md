Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Korean Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1950-03-29
Page: 1

　　Chairman Mao Receives the Korean Ambassador
    [Xinhua News Agency, 28th] Chairman Mao Zedong received Mr. Li Zhouyuan, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, at 11:30 tonight. Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai was present.

### 毛主席接见朝鲜大使

1950-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见朝鲜大使
    【新华社二十八日讯】毛泽东主席本日晚十一时半接见朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使李周渊，在座者有周恩来总理兼外交部长。
