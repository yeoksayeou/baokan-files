　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
　　[This newspaper dispatch] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (March 1st) at 18:45 special program for Taiwanese compatriots: The live recording of yesterday's "February 28th" memorial meeting held in the capital. 20:00 joint broadcast time: (1) Third News, (2) Newsletter: 1. A production army on the frontier of the motherland, 2. Loving the people and rendering meritorious service, 3. The people in the Guangdong-Guangxi border area love the People's Liberation Army. 17:30 Youth Time: "On Carrying Out New Democracy Studies in Schools". 18:30 Natural Science Lectures: Electrical Knowledge Lecture 19 "Electromagnetic Induction and Transformers". 18:00 First literary program: Qinshu "Examining God's Grandma". 21:00 Second literary program: Mongolian music.
　　[Another dispatch] The recording of Peng Zhen's summary report at the Second Session of the Second Municipal People's Representative Conference broadcast by the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station was warmly welcomed by the audience. It will be broadcast again specifically today from 8:00 to 9:30 pm.
　　[Another dispatch] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station 12:30 Workers Program (1) Culture and Entertainment: Xinhua Printing Factory workers choir sings, (2) "How the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau's Line Section workers completed the project of renovating the Fifth Bureau's lines". 13:00 Youth Program: (1) Introducing the content of the newly ordered youth program, (2) Culture and Entertainment: "Workers' Chorus". 16:30 Voice of the Municipality: (1) Several instructions from the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Commerce on the renewal of vendor licenses for 1950, (2) Notice from the Beijing Municipal Air Defense Headquarters to citizens to strengthen air defense work, (3) Notice from the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Public Health to citizens to prevent smallpox, measles, and meningitis. 18:45 Listener Service: Introducing the new film "Boundless Radiance" from the Northeast Film Studio.

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（三月一日）十八点四十五分对台湾同胞的特别节目：昨天在首都举行的“二、二八”纪念会的实况录音。二十点联播时间：（一）第三次新闻，（二）通讯：一、祖国边疆上的一支生产军，二、爱民立功，三、粤桂边境地区人民热爱解放军。十七点三十分青年时间：“论开展学校中的新民主主义学习”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：电学常识第十九讲《电磁感应和变压器》。十八点第一次文艺节目：琴书《考神婆》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：蒙古音乐。
