Title: Kunming Military Control Commission Begins Takeover Work
Author:
Date: 1950-03-10
Page: 1

　　Kunming Military Control Commission Begins Takeover Work
　　【Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 9th】After consultation with the Yunnan People's Provisional Military and Political Committee, the Kunming Military Control Commission began handling the takeover of various units starting on the 6th.

### 昆明军管会开始接管工作

1950-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　昆明军管会开始接管工作
    【新华社昆明九日电】昆明市军事管制委员会经与云南人民临时军政委员会协商后，已于六日起开始办理各单位的接管工作。
