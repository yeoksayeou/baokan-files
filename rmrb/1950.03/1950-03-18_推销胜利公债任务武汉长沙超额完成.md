Title: Wuhan and Changsha Exceed Quotas in Promoting Victory Bonds

Author: 
Date: 1950-03-18
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Wuhan and Changsha have exceeded their quotas for promoting Victory Bonds. As of the 15th, the total number of Victory Bonds purchased and subscribed for by all sectors in Wuhan reached 6,575,773, exceeding the original allocation by more than 75,000. The city is now entering the payment phase. Changsha has now subscribed for 1,153,606 Victory Bonds, exceeding the original quota by more than 50,000, of which half have already been paid for.

### 推销胜利公债任务武汉长沙超额完成

1950-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　推销胜利公债任务武汉长沙超额完成
    【新华社十七日讯】武汉、长沙两市已超额完成公债推销任务。武汉市截至十五日止，各界已购和认购总数共达六百五十七万五千七百七十三分，超过原配销额七万五千余分，现正进入缴款阶段中。长沙市现已认购一百十五万三千六百零六分，超过原配额五万余分，其中半数已经缴款。
