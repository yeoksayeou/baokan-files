Title: Central People's Government Ministry of Communications Minister Zhang Bojun Issues Statement: Chinese Ships Now in Singapore, Hong Kong, and Various Countries' Ports Are All Chinese Property and May Not Be Infringed Upon or Transferred.
Author:
Date: 1950-03-19
Page: 1

　　Central People's Government Ministry of Communications
    Minister Zhang Bojun Issues Statement
    Chinese Ships Now in Singapore, Hong Kong, and Various Countries' Ports Are All Chinese Property and May Not Be Infringed Upon or Transferred.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 18th] Zhang Bojun, Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, recently issued a statement regarding the Chinese ships now in Singapore, Hong Kong, and various countries' ports, all of which are the property of the People's Republic of China. It is absolutely not allowed for anyone to infringe, damage, detain, transfer, or interfere with their actions by any means. The statement is as follows:
    The Hai Xuan vessel which recently revolted and sailed to Singapore, the vessels which revolted in Hong Kong, and the vessels originally belonging to the pseudo-Kuomintang government and Chinese bureaucratic capital in various countries' ports should all belong to the People's Republic of China and be directly administered by the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government. Only our Central People's Government and personnel entrusted by our Central People's Government have the right to dispose of these vessels. It is absolutely not allowed for anyone to infringe, damage, detain, transfer, or interfere with their actions by any means. This sacred property right of our Central People's Government should be respected by the governments of Singapore, Hong Kong, and various countries. If any of the vessels in Singapore, Hong Kong, and various countries' ports are infringed upon, damaged, detained, transferred, or have their actions interfered with, the governments in question must bear full responsibility and this will lead to corresponding consequences. This is hereby declared.

### 中央人民政府交通部  章伯钧部长发表声明  现在新加坡香港及各国港口的我国轮船，均属我国产权，不容侵犯或转移。

1950-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府交通部
    章伯钧部长发表声明
    现在新加坡香港及各国港口的我国轮船，均属我国产权，不容侵犯或转移。
    【新华社十八日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府交通部章伯钧部长顷就现在新加坡、香港以及各国港口的中国轮船，均为中华人民共和国产权，决不容许任何人以任何手段侵犯、损坏、扣留、转移或干涉其行动事，发表声明如下：
    最近起义驶往新加坡的海玄轮和在香港起义的各轮，以及在各国港口原属于伪国民党政府及中国官僚资本所有的各轮，均应为中华人民共和国所有，受中央人民政府交通部直接管辖。各轮只有我中央人民政府和我中央人民政府所委托的人员，才有权处置，决不容许任何人以任何手段侵犯、损坏、扣留、转移或干涉其行动。我中央人民政府的此项神圣的产权，应受到新加坡政府、香港政府和各国政府的尊重。如在新加坡、香港和各国港口的各轮有被侵犯、损坏、扣留、转移或干涉行动情事，则各该政府必须负完全责任，并将引起相应的后果。特此声明。
