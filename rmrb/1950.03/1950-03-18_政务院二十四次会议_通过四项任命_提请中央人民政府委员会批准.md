Title: 24th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approves Four Appointments to be Submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for Approval
Author:
Date: 1950-03-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th -- The 24th meeting of the Government Administration Council approved the following list of appointments to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval:

    I. Eastern Sichuan Administrative Office
    Director: Yan Hongyan, formerly Director of the Political Department of the People's Liberation Army Corps.
    II. Southern Sichuan Administrative Office
    Director: Li Dazhang, formerly Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
    Deputy Directors: Guo Yingqiu, formerly Director of the Political Department of the People's Liberation Army Corps.
    Liu Piyun, formerly Secretary-General of the Department of Transportation of the Northeast Administrative Committee.
    III. Western Sichuan Administrative Office
    Director: Li Jingquan, currently Deputy Political Commissar of the Southwest Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
    Deputy Director: Yan Xiufeng, formerly Deputy Director of the Jinnan Administrative Office.
    IV. Northern Sichuan Administrative Office
    Director: Hu Yaobang, formerly Director of the Political Department of the People's Liberation Army Corps.
    Deputy Director: Qin Zhongfang, formerly Deputy Director of the Jinxi Administrative Office.

### 政务院二十四次会议  通过四项任命  提请中央人民政府委员会批准

1950-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　政务院二十四次会议
    通过四项任命
    提请中央人民政府委员会批准
    【新华社十七日讯】政务院第二十四次政务会议通过提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命的各项名单如下：
    一、川东行署
    主任  阎红彦  曾任人民解放军兵团政治部主任。
    二、川南行署
    主任  李大章  曾任中国共产党中央东北局宣传部副部长。
    副主任  郭影秋  曾任人民解放军军政治部主任。
    刘披云  曾任东北行政委员会交通部秘书长。
    三、川西行署
    主任  李井泉  现任人民解放军西南军区副政治委员。
    副主任  阎秀峰  曾任晋南行署副主任。
    四、川北行署
    主任  胡耀邦  曾任人民解放军兵团政治部主任。
    副主任  秦仲方  曾任晋西北行署副主任。
