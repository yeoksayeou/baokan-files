Title: Conveying the Resolutions of the Second Session of the Second Municipal People's Representative Conference: Beijing Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Holds a General Membership Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-03-07
Page: 4

　　To convey the resolutions of the Second Session of the Second Municipal People's Representative Conference, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a general membership meeting.
    [This newspaper] To mobilize party members to assist the Beijing Municipal People's Government in completing its work tasks for this year, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang convened a general membership meeting at 9:00 AM on the 5th. Over a hundred people attended. First, Xu Baokui, a member of the committee, conveyed Mayor Nie's report on the Beijing Municipal People's Government's work plan for 1950 and Vice Mayor Zhang's report on the estimated revenue and expenditure of Beijing's finances, both given at the Second Session of the Second Municipal People's Representative Conference. Committee member Weng Shiwu spoke about his impressions of the conference, and Cao Zhilin conveyed the summary report of Peng Zhen, secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, after which the meeting adjourned.

### 传达二届二次各界人民代表会议决议  民革北京市分部召开党员大会

1950-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　传达二届二次各界人民代表会议决议
    民革北京市分部召开党员大会
    【本报讯】国民党革命委员会北京市分部为号召党员协助北京市人民政府完成本年度工作任务，于五日上午九时召开党员大会。到会百余人。首由该分部委员许宝骙传达聂市长在北京市二届二次各界人民代表会议上关于北京市人民政府一九五零年度工作计划的报告和张副市长的北京市财政收支概算的报告。由委员翁世五讲述对大会的观感，曹志麟传达中共北京市委书记彭真的总结报告后散会。
