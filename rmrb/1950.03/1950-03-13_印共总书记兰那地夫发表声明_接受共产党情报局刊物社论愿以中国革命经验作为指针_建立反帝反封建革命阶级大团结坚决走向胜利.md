Title: CPI General Secretary Ranadive Issues Statement Accepting the Journal Editorial of the Cominform, Willing to Take the Experience of the Chinese Revolution as a Guide, Establish a Great Revolutionary Class Unity Against Imperialism and Feudalism, and Resolutely Move Towards Victory
Author:
Date: 1950-03-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: TASS, Delhi, 2nd: CPI General Secretary Ranadive issued a statement to the press, saying: "The Communist Party of India hails the editorial published on January 27th by the organ of the Information Bureau of Communist and Workers’ Parties, *For Lasting Peace, For People’s Democracy*, entitled 'The Great Advance in the National Liberation Movement of Colonial and Dependent Countries,' and considers it a great contribution to understanding the problems of the Indian national liberation struggle, and fully accepts the conclusions made in the editorial. The lessons of the victorious Chinese People's Liberation Struggle, in which the Communist Party of China led by Comrade Mao Zedong successfully applied the doctrines of Lenin and Stalin, will be an infallible guide for the Communist Party and the working class of India, which bear the leadership task in the national liberation struggle. Under the leadership of the Communist Party and the working class, unite all classes willing to fight for national independence, and form a people's liberation army when the necessary domestic conditions permit. By mastering these outstanding lessons, the Indian working class, headed by the Communist Party, can lead the national liberation struggle to victory. Striving for the unity of the working class, strengthening the alliance between the working class and all peasants, immediately implementing anti-feudal land reform, and uniting all parties and classes willing to fight for national independence can remove all obstacles hindering the full mobilization of the Indian people's national struggle, which is against British and American imperialists and the big bourgeoisie, feudal princes, and landlords who cooperate with them. The editorial provides the working class, headed by the Communist Party, with an invaluable weapon to carry out its leading task in the national liberation struggle and helps to eliminate all errors and mistakes that hinder the development of the national liberation struggle."

### 印共总书记兰那地夫发表声明  接受共产党情报局刊物社论愿以中国革命经验作为指针  建立反帝反封建革命阶级大团结坚决走向胜利

1950-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　印共总书记兰那地夫发表声明
    接受共产党情报局刊物社论愿以中国革命经验作为指针
    建立反帝反封建革命阶级大团结坚决走向胜利
    【新华社北京十二日电】塔斯社德里二日讯：印共总书记兰那地夫对报界发表声明称：“印度共产党欢呼共产党与工人党情报局机关报《争取持久和平，争取人民民主》在一月二十七日所发表的题为《殖民地与附属国民族解放运动的巨大进展》的社论，认为它对了解印度民族解放斗争的问题是一个伟大的贡献，并且完全接受该社论所作的结论。由毛泽东同志领导的中国共产党，在胜利的中国人民解放斗争中成功地运用了列宁与斯大林的学说，这种胜利的中国人民解放斗争的教训，对于在民族解放斗争中担负领导任务的印度共产党和工人阶级，将是一个绝不会错的指针。在共产党、工人阶级的领导下，团结一切愿为民族独立而斗争的阶级，并在必要的国内条件许可时，组成人民解放军。掌握了这些卓越的教训，就可使以共产党为首的印度工人阶级把民族解放斗争引向胜利。争取工人阶级的团结，加强工人阶级和一切农民间的联盟，立即实行反封建的土地改革，团结一切愿为民族独立而斗争的各党派与各阶级，就可以扫除横在充分发动印度人民民族斗争道路上的一切阻挠和障碍，这个民族斗争是反抗英美帝国主义者以及与他们合作的大资产阶级、封建王公与地主的。社论给予以共产党为首的工人阶级以价值无可衡量的武器，以便在民族解放斗争中执行其领导任务，并有助于消除妨碍民族解放斗争发展的一切错误与过失。”
