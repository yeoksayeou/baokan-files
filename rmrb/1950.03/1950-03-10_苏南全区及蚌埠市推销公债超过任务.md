Title: Public Bond Sales Exceed Target in Southern Jiangsu and Bengbu

Author: 
Date: 1950-03-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 8th. Public bond sales in the entire Southern Jiangsu region and Bengbu City in the Northern Anhui region have both exceeded their targets. By the end of last month, 4.4 million bonds had been subscribed for in the entire Southern Jiangsu region, exceeding the original sales target of 4 million bonds by 10%. All sectors of Bengbu City have subscribed for 360,575 bonds, exceeding the allocated amount by more than 500.

### 苏南全区及蚌埠市推销公债超过任务

1950-03-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏南全区及蚌埠市推销公债超过任务
    【新华社上海八日电】苏南全区和皖北区的蚌埠市推销公债任务都已超额完成。至上月底止，苏南全区已认购公债四百四十万分，超过原定推销任务四百万分的百分之十。蚌埠市各界认购数已达三十六万零五百七十五分，超过配销额五百余分。
