Title: "Free German Youth" Sends Greetings to West German Youth; Plans to Hold All-German Youth Congress in May
Author:
Date: 1950-03-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin dispatch from TASS: The German News Agency reported that the Central Council of the Free German Youth League sent a telegram to the West German Peace Youth Congress, extending greetings to the West German youth for their courageous struggle for peace and German unification. The West German Peace Youth Congress was established in the Ruhr area according to the plan of the All-German Youth Congress. The telegram stated: The All-German Youth Congress, scheduled to be held in May 1950 in Berlin, the capital of Germany, will be another milestone on the road to a better future for our people. Let no one be deluded into thinking that they can stop our advance towards peace.

In the National Front of Democratic Germany's nationwide advance, the younger generation of Germany is at the forefront. The National Front of Democratic Germany is developing, persuading and organizing the people, and tomorrow it will liberate all of Germany. In our just struggle for unity and peace, we are not alone. With us is the powerful and great Soviet Union – the indestructible fortress of peace. With us is Stalin, the standard-bearer of world peace and progress. With us is the victorious People's Republic of China, led by Mao Zedong. With us are the people's democratic countries and peace-loving people all over the world.

This powerful peace front demonstrates new victories every day, strengthening our strength and confidence in completing our historical mission.

### “自由德国青年”电西德青年致敬  定五月开全德青年大会

1950-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　“自由德国青年”电西德青年致敬
    定五月开全德青年大会
    【新华社讯】塔斯社柏林电：德意志通讯社报道：自由德国青年联盟中央理事会致电西德拥护和平青年大会，向西德青年为和平及德国统一而进行的勇敢斗争致敬。西德拥护和平青年大会是按照全德青年大会的计划在鲁尔成立的。电文称：定于一九五○年五月在德国首都柏林举行的全德青年大会，将是在走向我国人民的较好未来的道路上的又一路标。让任何人都不要幻想能够阻止我们向和平前进。
    民主德国全国阵线在全国进军中，德国的年青一代是处在该阵线的前列。民主德国全国阵线是在发展中，它正在说服和组织人民，而明天，它将解放全德国。在我们为统一与和平而进行的正义斗争中，我们不是孤独的。和我们在一起的有强有力的伟大苏联——和平的不可摧毁的堡垒。和我们在一起的有世界的和平与进步的旗手斯大林。和我们在一起的有以毛泽东为首的胜利的中华人民共和国。和我们在一起的有人民民主国家和全世界爱好和平的人民。
    这一强大的和平阵线每天都表现出新的胜利，增强我们对完成我们的历史任务的力量和信心。
