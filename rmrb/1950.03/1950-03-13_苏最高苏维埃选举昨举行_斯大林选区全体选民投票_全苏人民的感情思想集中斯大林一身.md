Title: Supreme Soviet Elections Held Yesterday; All Voters in Stalin Electoral District Cast Ballots; The Feelings and Thoughts of the Entire Soviet People Concentrated in Stalin

Author:

Date: 1950-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: TASS Moscow, 12th dispatch: According to materials from the Stalin Electoral District Election Committee in Moscow, in this electoral district where the leader of the Soviet people, Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin, is running for representative, 100 percent of the voters cast their ballots by noon.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th dispatch: According to TASS Moscow, 12th dispatch: Moscow began electing the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union at exactly six o'clock this morning. In all seventeen electoral districts of the Soviet capital, voters began to fulfill their civic duty -- electing representatives to the highest state power organ of the Soviet state.

Everywhere was filled with a festive atmosphere. At daybreak in Moscow, colored electric lights illuminated the entire city. Soviet national flags fluttered on all buildings. Portraits of the great leader of the Soviet people -- Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin -- could be seen everywhere. On this day, the feelings and thoughts of millions of Soviet people are concentrated in him.

At five minutes to six in the morning, a large group of voters had already gathered outside Polling Station Number One. Although they did not know each other, they all held a common feeling -- ardent Soviet patriotism, and boundless love for their dearest motherland, the Communist Party, and the great Stalin.

The first to cast his ballot into the sealed ballot box was Vladimir Aleksandrov, Stalin Prize winner and foreman of the assembly shop of the light bulb factory.

### 苏最高苏维埃选举昨举行  斯大林选区全体选民投票  全苏人民的感情思想集中斯大林一身

1950-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏最高苏维埃选举昨举行
    斯大林选区全体选民投票
    全苏人民的感情思想集中斯大林一身
    【新华社北京十二日电】塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：根据莫斯科的斯大林选区选举委员会的材料，在苏联人民领袖约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林竞选代表的这一选区，至正午时，百分之百的选民投票。
    【新华社北京十二日电】据塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：莫斯科在今晨六点正开始选举苏联最高苏维埃。在苏联首都所有的十七个选区中，选民们开始执行其公民的责任——选举苏维埃国家最高国家权力机关的代表。
    到处都洋溢着节日的气氛。莫斯科破晓时，彩色的电灯照耀着全市。所有的建筑物上都飘扬着苏联国旗。到处都可以看到苏联人民的伟大领袖——约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林的肖像。在今天，千百万苏联人民的感情和思想，都集中于他一身。
    清晨六点差五分的时候，第一号选举处门外就已聚集着大群的选民。虽然他们并不相识，但他们都抱着一种共同的感情——热烈的苏维埃爱国主义，对他们的最亲爱的祖国、共产党和伟大的斯大林的无限的热爱。
    头一个把选票投入密封的票柜的，是斯大林奖金的得奖人、电灯泡工厂装配间管理员乌拉季米尔、亚历山得罗夫。
