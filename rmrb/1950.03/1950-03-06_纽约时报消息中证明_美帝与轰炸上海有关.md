Title: New York Times Report Proves US Imperialism Was Involved in the Bombing of Shanghai

Author:

Date: 1950-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 5th. A New York Times article dated February 7th, recently received here, regarding the February 6th bombing of Shanghai by the Kuomintang bandit gang, demonstrates the relationship between US imperialism and the Kuomintang bombing. The message stated that *Time* magazine correspondent Wilson Fielder observed the bombing from the air. Fielder said, "The bombing was carried out around the suburbs of the city," and "Fourteen 500-pound bombs were dropped on a large Zhabei power plant." As a matter of practice, American journalists accompany American Air Force personnel.

### 纽约时报消息中证明  美帝与轰炸上海有关

1950-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　纽约时报消息中证明
    美帝与轰炸上海有关
    【新华社北京五日电】此间新近收到的二月七日的纽约时报所刊载的关于二月六日国民党匪帮轰炸上海的一则消息，表明了美帝国主义与国民党轰炸的关系。该消息称，美国《时代》杂志特派员威尔逊·斐尔德（Wilson Fielder）曾随机观察轰炸。斐尔德说：“轰炸时是环绕着市郊进行的”，“有十四枚五百磅的炸弹投在一个很大的闸北发电厂中。”按照惯例，美国记者伴随着美国空军人员的。
