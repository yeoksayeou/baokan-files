Title: Overseas Chinese Groups in Rangoon Celebrate the Sino-Soviet Treaty
Author:
Date: 1950-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th -- According to Agence France-Presse: Overseas Chinese groups in Rangoon, the capital of Burma, held various recreational activities in the Rangoon City Hall on the evening of the 7th to celebrate the Sino-Soviet Treaty. The celebration did not end until midnight.

### 仰光华侨团体庆祝中苏条约

1950-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　仰光华侨团体庆祝中苏条约
    【新华社北京十二日电】据法新社消息：缅甸首府仰光的华侨团体七日晚在仰光市政厅举行各种游艺来庆祝中苏条约。庆祝会直到午夜始散。
