Title: First Meeting of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace Passes This Year's Specific Work Plan; Decides to Strengthen Internationalist Education Among the People and Connections with Peace Committees of Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1950-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: The China Committee for the Defense of World Peace held its first meeting today. Attending the meeting were Liu Ningyi and Shen Yanbing, Vice-Chairmen of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and seventeen members of the Working Committee. The meeting discussed issues such as how to further develop the movement to defend world peace throughout the country, and discussed and approved the "China Committee for the Defense of World Peace 1950 Work Plan." According to this plan, the two main tasks of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace in 1950 are: (1) To report to the people across the country on the growing strength of the world peace camp and various activities of the world peace movement, and to fully expose all war conspiracies and peace-endangering facts of the imperialists in order to strengthen internationalist education; (2) To convey to the world the determination and efforts of the Chinese people to defend world peace, and to strengthen ties with the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress and peace committees of various countries. The plan stipulates the following specific tasks for this year:

(1) Implement various resolutions and calls of the Standing Committee of the Peace Congress.

(2) Regularly exchange information with peace committees of various countries.

(3) Establish branches of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace in major cities to promote and organize mass movements for the defense of world peace in those areas. Candidates for the branches will be nominated by mass organizations in those areas, and a branch secretariat will be organized with two to five staff members to maintain regular contact with the general committee.

(4) Publish a special peace journal once a month, and regularly convene propaganda meetings to systematically assign experts to write articles and deliver broadcasts. At the end of each year, publish a "Compilation for the Defense of Peace" for distribution at home and abroad.

### 我国保卫世界和平大会工委会首次会议  通过今年具体工作计划  决加强全国人民国际主义教育及  与各国保卫和平委员会之间的联系

1950-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国保卫世界和平大会工委会首次会议
    通过今年具体工作计划
    决加强全国人民国际主义教育及
    与各国保卫和平委员会之间的联系
    【新华社十九日讯】中国保卫世界和平大会工作委员会，于本日召开首次会议。出席会议的有中国保卫世界和平大会委员会副主席刘宁一、沈雁冰及工作委员会委员共十七人。会议讨论了如何在全国进一步展开保卫世界和平运动等问题，并讨论和通过了“中国保卫世界和平大会委员会一九五○年工作计划”。根据这一计划，中国保卫世界和平大会委员会一九五○年的两项主要任务是：（一）向全国人民报道世界和平阵营力量壮大和世界保卫和平运动的各种活动，并尽情揭露帝国主义者的一切战争阴谋和危害和平的事实，以加强国际主义的教育；（二）向世界传达中国人民保卫世界和平的决心和努力，并加强与世界拥护和平大会常设委员会及各国保卫和平委员会之间的联系。计划规定了今年度的如下具体工作：
    （一）执行和平大会常委会的各项决议和号召。
    （二）与各国和平委员会经常交换情况。
    （三）在主要城市成立中国保卫世界和平委员会分会，推动和组织各该地保卫世界和平的群众运动。分会人选由各该地群众团体推选，并组织分会秘书处，设工作人员二至五人，经常与总会联系。
    （四）每月刊行和平专刊一次，同时经常召开宣传会议，有计划地分配专人撰著论文和发表广播。在每年度年终时，刊行《保卫和平汇集》，分发国内外。
