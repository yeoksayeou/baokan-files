Title: Northwest Ethnic Youth and Women Delegation Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-03-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 25th. The Northwest Ethnic Youth and Women Delegation, consisting of twenty-seven people led by Head Wang Weiqi and Deputy Heads Sha de Er and Roumanna, arrived in Beijing today. The delegation includes representatives of youth and women from various ethnic groups, including Han, Hui, Mongolian, Tibetan, Uyghur, Uzbek, Kazakh, and Tatar. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, Luo Qiong and Zeng Xianzhi, Deputy Secretaries-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Peng Sike, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Zhang Dazhong, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, Xu Hong, Deputy Director of the Production Department of the Beijing Municipal Democratic Women's Federation, and many others.

### 西北各族青年及妇女参观团昨日抵京

1950-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北各族青年及妇女参观团
    昨日抵京
    【新华社二十五日讯】西北各族青年、妇女参观团一行二十七人，由团长王维祺、副团长沙的尔、柔曼娜率领，于今日抵达北京。参观团包括汉、回、蒙、藏、维吾尔、乌兹别克、哈萨克、塔塔尔等各族青年、妇女的代表。前往车站欢迎的有新民主主义青年团中央委员会秘书长荣高棠、中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长罗琼、曾宪植、中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定、中央人民政府政务院民族事务委员会办公厅副主任朋斯克、青年团北京市工作委员会副书记张大中、北京市民主妇女联合会生产部副部长徐虹等多人。
