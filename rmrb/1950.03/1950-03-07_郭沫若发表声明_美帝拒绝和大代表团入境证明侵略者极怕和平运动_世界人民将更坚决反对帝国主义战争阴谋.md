Title: Guo Moruo Issues Statement: U.S. Rejection of World Peace Congress Delegation's Entry Proves Aggressors are Extremely Afraid of the Peace Movement; People of the World Will More Firmly Oppose Imperialist War Conspiracies
Author:
Date: 1950-03-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 6th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, has issued the following statement regarding the U.S. government's refusal to allow the World Peace Congress delegation to enter the country:

　　We, the Chinese people, express our utmost indignation at the unreasonable action of the U.S. State Department in refusing to issue entry visas to the World Peace Congress delegation.

　　The World Peace Congress delegation's trip to the United States and other countries was to present the World Peace Congress's two peace proposals on arms reduction and the prohibition of atomic weapons to the parliaments of various countries. This action by the World Peace Congress delegation represents the will of peace-loving people all over the world. The reactionary U.S. government openly refused the delegation's entry and openly denounced the delegation's efforts to have the U.S. Congress accept the peace proposals as a "presumptuous" act. This incident has exposed to the world, more clearly than ever, that U.S. imperialism is the enemy of peace.

　　The U.S. State Department's taking of this shameless step proves that the increasingly powerful world peace movement, under the unified leadership of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, has caused extreme panic among the imperialist aggressive camp headed by the United States.

　　The U.S. government is directing its lackeys around the world to obstruct and sabotage the progress of this peace movement by various means. However, the surging peace movement, representing the hundreds of millions of people of the world, can never be stopped by a small handful of warmongers. The U.S. government's actions against the peace movement will have no other effect than to make the people of the world strengthen their peace movement even more and more resolutely oppose the imperialist war conspiracies.

### 郭沫若发表声明  美帝拒绝和大代表团入境证明侵略者极怕和平运动  世界人民将更坚决反对帝国主义战争阴谋

1950-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若发表声明
    美帝拒绝和大代表团入境证明侵略者极怕和平运动
    世界人民将更坚决反对帝国主义战争阴谋
    【新华社北京六日电】中国保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，顷就美国政府拒绝世界拥护和平大会代表团入境一事发表声明如下：
    我们中国人民，对美国国务院拒发世界拥护和平大会代表团入境签证的无理行动，表示极大的愤怒。
    世界拥护和平大会代表团这次到美国和其他各国去，是为了向各国议会递交世界拥护和平大会关于缩减军备和禁止原子武器的两项和平建议。世界拥护和平大会代表团的这个行动，是代表了全世界爱好和平的人民的意志的。反动的美国政府竟公然拒绝这个代表团的入境，并公然责骂代表团打算让美国国会接受和平建议的努力为“狂妄”行动。这一件事，再清楚不过地向全世界暴露出美帝国主义是和平的敌人。
    美国国务院采取这种无耻步骤，证明在世界拥护和平大会常设委员会统一领导下的愈来愈强大的世界和平运动，已使得以美国为首的帝国主义侵略阵营极为恐慌。
    美国政府正在指挥它在全世界的走狗，用各种办法来阻挠和破坏这个和平运动的进行。但是，代表世界亿万人民的汹涌澎湃的和平运动，决不是一小撮战争贩子所能阻止得住的。美国政府的反对和平运动的行为，除了使世界人民更加加强自己的和平运动，更加坚决地反对帝国主义的战争阴谋以外，不会有别的效果。
