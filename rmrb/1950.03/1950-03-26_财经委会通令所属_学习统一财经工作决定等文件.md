Title: Finance and Economics Committee Issues Circular to Subordinates: Study the Decision on Unified Financial and Economic Work and Other Documents
Author:
Date: 1950-03-26
Page: 1

　　Finance and Economics Committee Issues Circular to Subordinates
    Study the Decision on Unified Financial and Economic Work and Other Documents
    [Xinhua News Agency, 25th] The Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council of the Central People's Government, in order to raise the policy level of financial and economic cadres nationwide, strengthen financial and economic work, and implement the decisions of the National Financial Conference, recently issued a circular to its subordinate ministries, administrations, banks, and major administrative regions' Financial and Economic Committees, organizing all financial and economic cadres to conscientiously study: the State Council's "Decision on Unified National Financial and Economic Work" and the Beijing People's Daily editorials "Why Unify National Financial and Economic Work?" and "The Role of Taxation in Our National Work" and other documents. The order stipulates that learning should be linked to practical reality, and leaders at all levels should personally be responsible for organizing and inspecting the learning situation, striving to achieve the desired results.

### 财经委会通令所属  学习统一财经工作决定等文件

1950-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　财经委会通令所属
    学习统一财经工作决定等文件
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会为提高全国财政经济工作干部的政策水平，加强财经工作，以便贯彻执行全国财政会议的决定，特于日前通令所属各部、署、行及各大行政区财政经济委员会组织全体财经干部认真学习：政务院《关于统一国家财政经济工作的决定》及北京人民日报社论《为什么要统一国家财政经济工作》、《税收在我们国家工作中的作用》等文件。命令规定学习中要联系实际，各级领导干部应亲自负责组织与检查学习情况，务期收到应有效果。
