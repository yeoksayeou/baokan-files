Title: Soviet Photo Exhibition at the Qianmen Arrow Tower People's Education Center
Author:
Date: 1950-03-17
Page: 4

　　Qianmen Arrow Tower People's Education Center Exhibits Soviet Photographs
    [Our Newspaper] The Second People's Education Center in the Qianmen Arrow Tower of Beijing began holding a Soviet photo exhibition on the 16th. The content includes Stalin's life, Moscow's shops, the welfare of the Soviet people, the 32nd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution (including the development of Soviet industry and agriculture, and the life of the Soviet people), the Soviet Red Army, etc., a total of 235 photographs. The exhibition is open from 9:30 am to 5:00 pm daily. All circles are welcome to visit. (Second People's Education Center Communications Group)

### 前门箭楼民教馆展览苏联照片

1950-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　前门箭楼民教馆展览苏联照片
    【本报讯】京市前门箭楼第二民众教育馆自十六日开始举行苏联照片展览。内容为斯大林生平、莫斯科的商店、苏联人民的福利、十月社会主义革命三十二周年纪念（内有苏联工业、农业的发展情形、苏联人民的生活）、苏联红军等，共二百三十五幅。展览时间每日上午九时半至下午五时止。欢迎各界参观。（第二民众教育馆通讯组）
