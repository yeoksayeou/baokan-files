Title: Vietnam Women's Federation Cables Support for Saigon Students
Author:
Date: 1950-03-26
Page: 4

　　Vietnam Women's Federation Cables Support
    for Saigon Students
    [Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 25th] Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 24th: Le Thi Xuyen, Chairwoman of the Vietnam Women's Federation, sent a telegram hailing the heroic struggle of Vietnamese students of all levels in Saigon and Dike, who held a march and demonstration on March 19 to protest against American aid to French colonialists. The telegram stated: "You have for the second time in three months (note: the first time was January 9th this year) expressed to the French colonialists and American imperialists the determination of the Vietnamese people, under the leadership of Ho Chi Minh, to fight to the end for national independence and unification. Vietnamese women guarantee to take more active actions to accelerate the liberation of the entire Vietnam and to avenge those young men and women who died in the struggle against the French colonialists."

### 越妇联通电  声援西贡学生

1950-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　越妇联通电
    声援西贡学生
    【新华社二十五日讯】越南通讯社北越二十四日讯：越南妇女联合会主席黎氏钏，通电欢呼西贡与堤岸的越南大中小学生的英勇斗争，他们曾在三月十九日举行游行示威抗议美国对法国殖民者的援助。电文称：“你们在三个月中已第二次（按第一次是今年一月九日）向法国殖民者与美帝国主义者表示了越南人民在胡志明领导下为争取国家独立与统一而斗争到底的决心。越南妇女保证采取更积极的行动，以加紧整个越南的解放，并替那些在反抗法国殖民者的斗争中殉难的青年男女们复仇。”
