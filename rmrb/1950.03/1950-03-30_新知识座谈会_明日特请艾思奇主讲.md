Title: New Knowledge Forum to Feature Ai Siqi as Speaker Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 4

　　New Knowledge Forum
    Tomorrow Special Lecture by Ai Siqi
    [This newspaper dispatch] The New Knowledge Forum, sponsored by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, is scheduled to hold its 63rd meeting at 7:00 PM on the 31st of this month (Friday). Professor Ai Siqi of the Marxist-Leninist Academy will be the special speaker, lecturing on "Dialectical Materialism and Historical Materialism." To accommodate attendees from all walks of life, the meeting address has been changed to the Xiehe Auditorium, Dongdan Santiao Hutong. Members may enter with their notices, and non-members may obtain admission tickets in advance from the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association at 24 Wuliang Daren Hutong (telephone number 5-1070). (Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association)

### 新知识座谈会  明日特请艾思奇主讲

1950-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会
    明日特请艾思奇主讲
    【本报讯】民主建国会北京市分会主办的新知识座谈会，定于本月三十一日（星期五）下午七时举行第六十三次会，这次特请马列学院艾思奇教授讲“辩证唯物主义与历史唯物主义”。该会为照顾各界听讲，已将开会地址改为东单三条胡同协和礼堂。会友凭通知入场，非会友可先期到无量大人胡同二十四号（电话五局一○七○）民建京分会领取入场券。（民建京分会）
