Title: Soviet Economics Master Baodelev Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-03-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 28th:
　　Soviet Finance Institute lecturer and Economics Master Boris Koligoriyevich Baodelev, invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing today to lecture in our country. Master Baodelev is the fourth Soviet professor to come to our country to lecture, following Professors Nuzhin, Giselev, and Makarova. The topics he plans to lecture on are: 1. The Soviet Tax System, 2. The Soviet National Budget, 3. The Soviet National Credit Loans, 4. The Financial Construction of the New European Democracies.

### 苏联经济学硕士包德列夫抵京

1950-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联经济学硕士包德列夫抵京
    【新华社二十八日讯】应中苏友好协会总会邀请来我国讲学的苏联财政学院主任讲师经济学硕士包里士·柯里戈里耶维赤·包德列夫已于今日抵京。包德列夫硕士是继努日金、吉谢列夫、马卡洛娃三教授之后第四位来到我国讲学的苏联教授。他准备讲演的题目有：一、苏联的税收制度，二、苏联的国家预算，三、苏联的国家信用贷款，四、欧洲新民主主义国家的财政建设。
