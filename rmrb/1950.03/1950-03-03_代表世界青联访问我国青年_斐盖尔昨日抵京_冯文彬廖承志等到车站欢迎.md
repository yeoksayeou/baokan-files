　　Representative of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Visits Chinese Youth
    斐盖尔 Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
    冯文彬、廖承志 Welcome Him at the Station

    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd] 雷奥·斐盖尔, representative of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, arrived in the capital this morning. Those who welcomed him at the station included 冯文彬, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; 蒋南翔, Deputy Secretary; 荣高棠, Secretary-General; 陈家康, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department; 廖承志, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; 钱三强, Vice-Chairman; 沙千里, Vice-Chairman; 吴晗, Secretary-General; and 许立群 of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League. 雷奥·斐盖尔 is the Secretary-General of the French Republican Youth League. He is visiting Chinese youth as a representative of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and will stay in China for a period.

### 代表世界青联访问我国青年  斐盖尔昨日抵京  冯文彬廖承志等到车站欢迎

1950-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　代表世界青联访问我国青年
    斐盖尔昨日抵京       
    冯文彬廖承志等到车站欢迎
    【新华社北京二日电】世界民主青年联盟代表雷奥·斐盖尔，于今日晨抵首都，到站欢迎的有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬、副书记蒋南翔、秘书长荣高棠、联络部副部长陈家康、中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志、副主席钱三强、沙千里、秘书长吴晗，以及青年团北京市工委许立群等。雷奥斐盖尔系法国共和青年联盟总书记，此次代表世界民主青年联盟访问中国青年，将在中国逗留一个时期。
