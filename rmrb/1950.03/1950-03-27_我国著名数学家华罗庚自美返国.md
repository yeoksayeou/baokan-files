Title: Famous Chinese Mathematician Hua Luogeng Returns from the United States
Author:
Date: 1950-03-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The world-renowned Chinese mathematician Professor Hua Luogeng returned to Beijing, the capital, from the United States on the 16th of this month and has returned to teach at Tsinghua University. Mr. Hua was invited to lecture at the University of Illinois in the United States in 1946. After returning to Tsinghua University, Mr. Hua was warmly welcomed by the students of the university. On his way back to China, Mr. Hua published an open letter to Chinese students studying in the United States, calling on them to return to China and work hard for the construction and development of the great motherland.

### 我国著名数学家华罗庚自美返国

1950-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国著名数学家华罗庚自美返国
    【新华社二十六日讯】闻名全世界的我国数学家华罗庚教授已于本月十六日自美国返抵首都北京，并已回清华大学任教。华氏系于一九四六年应美国伊利诺大学之聘，前往讲学。华氏回到清华大学以后，受到该校学生的热烈欢迎。华氏在回国途中曾发表一封给中国留美学生的公开信，号召留美学生回国为伟大的祖国建设和发展而努力。
