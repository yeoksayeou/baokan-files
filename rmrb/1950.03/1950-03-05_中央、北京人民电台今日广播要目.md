Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1950-03-05
Page: 4

　　[This News] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (the 5th) at 17:30, Workers' Time: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions' notice on implementing the guiding principle of "Learn to Manage Enterprises." 18:30 Natural Science Lectures: "The Inventor of the Arc Lamp, Yablochkov." 18:00 First Literary Program: "Plum Blossom Tune, A New Trip to Beijing." 21:00 Second Literary Program: Opera "Wang Xiu Alarmed." 20:30 Weekly Literary Program: Six new songs. 18:45 New Songs: "Support Chairman Mao," "Unite and Advance," etc.
　　[Also News] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station 12:30 Workers' Program: (1) Introducing the Workers' Radio Newspaper jointly organized by the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions and the station, (2) Reporting on the workers' activities in the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, Clothing Factory, Mentougou Coal Mine, Northeast Sujiatun Abrasives Factory, and Gansu Yumen Oil Mine. 13:00 Youth Program: Music Appreciation: Western Music (1) Light Cavalry Overture, (2) Turkish March, (3) The Dove, (4) Violin Piece, (5) Rosamunde Ballet Music. 16:30 Western Music.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（五日）十七点三十分职工时间：全总关于贯彻“学会管理企业”指导方针的通知。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：《弧光灯的发明家亚勃罗齐克夫》。十八点第一次文艺节目：梅花调《新上京》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：歌剧《王秀惊》。八点三十分星期文艺节目：六支新歌曲。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《拥护毛主席》、《团结前进》等。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人节目：（一）介绍北京市总工会和该台合办的工人广播报，（二）报导石景山钢铁厂、被服厂、门头沟煤矿、东北苏家屯砂轮厂和甘肃玉门油矿等地的工人动态。十三点青年节目：音乐欣赏：西乐（一）轻骑兵序曲，（二）土耳其进行曲，（三）鸽子，（四）提琴曲，（五）罗萨孟德舞曲。十六点三十分西乐。
