Title: The 25th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approves the List of Staff Members of the Central-South Military and Political Committee and its Subordinate Provincial and Municipal People's Governments

Author:
Date: 1950-03-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, dated 24th: The 25th Government Affairs Conference of the Central People's Government approved the list of staff members of the Central-South Military and Political Committee and its subordinate Provincial and Municipal People's Governments as follows:
  I. Supplementary List of Members of the Central-South Military and Political Committee: 19 members (in order of stroke count of surnames)
     Wang Shusheng: Currently Deputy Commander of the Hubei Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
     Zhu Dixin: Currently Director of the Public Security Bureau of the Wuhan Municipal People's Government.
     Zhou Li: Currently Minister of the Organization Department of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China.
     Hou Lianying: Currently a member of the Preparatory Committee of the Henan Provincial Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang.
     Ni Zhiliang: Currently Deputy Principal of the Central-South Military and Political University and Commander of the Wuhan Garrison.
     Tang Xing: Currently a member of the Hunan Military and Political Committee.
     Xia Zhichen (female): Currently Minister of the Organization Department of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China.
     Xu Lin: Formerly Minister of the Finance Department of the Central Plains Provisional People's Government.
     Zhang Pinghua: Currently Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China.
     Zhang Jinbao (female): Currently a member of the Central-South Federation of Trade Unions, and Deputy Director and Director of the Women Workers Department of the Wuhan Municipal Federation of Trade Unions.
     Zeng Zhi (female): Formerly Deputy Minister of the Industry Department of the Central Plains Provisional People's Government.
     Zeng Zesheng: Currently Army Commander of the Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army.
     Huang Yongsheng: Currently Deputy Commander of the Guangxi Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
     Wan Yi: Currently Commander of the Special Forces Corps of the Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army.
     Lei Jingtian: Currently Vice Chairman of the Guangxi Provincial People's Government.
     Deng Hua: Currently Corps Commander of the Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army.
     Deng Wencui: Military personnel
     Zheng Shaowen: Currently Director of the Political Department of the Hubei Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
    Nie Guoqing: Currently Chairman of the Provisional Working Committee of the Hubei Provincial Branch of the China Democratic League.
      (The above 19 persons are submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval and appointment.)
          (To be continued)
