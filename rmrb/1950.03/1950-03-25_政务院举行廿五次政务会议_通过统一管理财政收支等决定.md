Title: Government Administration Council Holds 25th Meeting, Passes Decisions on Unified Management of Financial Revenue and Expenditure, etc.
Author:
Date: 1950-03-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 24th: The 25th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held this afternoon at 3:30 PM. The meeting discussed and approved decisions on unified management of financial revenue and expenditure for 1950, unified management of national public grain revenue, expenditure, storage and dispatch, the establishment of security work in national financial and economic departments, interim measures for abolishing feudal control systems in local transportation businesses, and changing grain processing standards to increase edible grain. The meeting also heard a general review report by Committee Member Zhang Naiqi, representing the Government Administration Council and the organizational structure review committee of its affiliated units, on the past three months of review work. In addition, the meeting approved several important appointment cases and submitted multiple appointment cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行廿五次政务会议  通过统一管理财政收支等决定

1950-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行廿五次政务会议  通过统一管理财政收支等决定
    【新华社北京二十四日电】中央人民政府政务院第二十五次政务会议于今日下午三时半举行。会议讨论并通过了关于统一管理一九五零年度财政收支的决定、关于统一国家公粮收支保管调度的决定，关于在国家财政经济部门中建立保卫工作的决定，关于废除各地搬运事业中封建把持制度暂行处理办法，关于改变粮食加工标准，增加食用粮食的决定。会议并听取了章乃器委员代表政务院及所属单位机构编制审查委员会三个月来的审查总报告。此外，会议通过了重要任命案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
