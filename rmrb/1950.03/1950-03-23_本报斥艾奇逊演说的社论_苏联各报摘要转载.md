Title: Editorial in This Newspaper Criticizing Acheson's Speech; Excerpts Reprinted by Soviet Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1950-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 21st: Newspapers are reprinting excerpts from the People's Daily editorial entitled "American Lies and Asian Truth," which comments on Secretary of State Acheson's speech on March 15 regarding American policy in Asia.
