　　Beijing Youth Issue 43 Published
    [Our Newspaper] The 43rd issue of "Beijing Youth" was published on the 25th. To commemorate World Youth Week, the journal published an interview with Léo Figuères, representative of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and General Secretary of the Republican Youth Federation of France, entitled "The People and Youth of France Detest War!". The article vividly describes the struggle of the French people and youth against war and for peace. (Publicity Department of the Municipal Committee of the Youth League)

### 北京青年四十三期出版

1950-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京青年四十三期出版
    【本报讯】《北京青年》四十三期已于二十五日出版。为了纪念世界青年周，该刊发表了以“法国人民和青年憎恶战争！”为题的世界民主青年联盟代表、法国共和青年联盟总书记雷奥·斐盖尔的访问记。该文生动地描述了法国人民和青年的反对战争、争取和平的斗争。（青年团市工委宣传部）
