Title: Greek Democratic Youth League Calls on National Youth to Establish Democratic Fortress of Struggle Against US Imperialism and Royal Dictatorship
Author:
Date: 1950-03-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st: Electone News Agency, Prague: The Central Committee of the Greek Democratic Youth League issued a letter to Greek youth on the seventh anniversary of the League's founding (February 23). The letter calls on young workers to fight for increased wages; students to build fortresses of struggle for Greek democracy; young farmers to fight for the guarantee of returning to villages and adequate living conditions; and youth in prisons to continue to hold high the banner of Greek democratic youth.

The letter also calls on all Greek youth to fight to form a pan-Greek democratic front against the dictatorial conspiracy of the Greek royal clique and American imperialists, and to continue the struggle for bread, independence, democracy, and peace.

### 希民主青年团号召全国青年  建立民主的斗争堡垒  反对美帝及皇室独裁

1950-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　希民主青年团号召全国青年
    建立民主的斗争堡垒  反对美帝及皇室独裁
    【新华社北京一日电】电通社布拉格讯：希腊民主青年团中央委员会在该团成立七周年纪念日（二月二十三日）发表告希腊青年书。告青年书号召青年工人为增加工资而斗争；学生们建立争取希腊民主的斗争堡垒；青年农民为争取返回村庄与适当生活条件的保证而斗争；在监狱中的青年继续高举希腊民主青年的旗帜。
    该书并号召全体希腊青年为组成一个反对希腊皇室集团的独裁阴谋及美国帝国主义者的全希腊民主阵线而斗争，并继续争取面包、独立、民主与和平的斗争。
