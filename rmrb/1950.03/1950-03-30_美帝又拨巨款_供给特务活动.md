Title: US Imperialism Allocates Huge Sums for Espionage Activities
Author:
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, dated 28th: According to a Telecommunications Agency dispatch from Washington: The U.S. House Appropriations Committee passed an appropriation of $57 million on the 22nd for the notorious U.S. government espionage agency, the Federal Bureau of Investigation.
　　The official statement declares that 235 FBI plainclothes detectives have been assigned to specifically investigate the American Communist Party and to plot divisive and despicable activities such as creating "red terror."

### 美帝又拨巨款  供给特务活动

1950-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　美帝又拨巨款  供给特务活动
    【新华社二十八日讯】据电通社华盛顿讯：美国众院拨款委员会于二十二日通过五千七百万美元的拨款，作为丑名远扬的美国政府的特务机关——联邦调查局的经费。
    官方宣称：已命二百三十五名联邦调查局的便衣侦探，专门侦察美国共产党，并策划挑拨离间和制造“赤色恐怖”等卑劣活动。
