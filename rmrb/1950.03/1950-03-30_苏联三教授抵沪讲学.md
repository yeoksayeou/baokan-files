Title: Three Soviet Professors Arrive in Shanghai to Lecture
Author:
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 29th—A group of Soviet scholars, including Dr. Nuzhdin in Biology, Dr. Giselev in History, and Master Makarova in Economics, arrived in Shanghai on the evening of the 27th at the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Among those who went to the station to welcome them were Chen Wangdao and Li Zhaohuan, vice presidents of the Shanghai branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Xia Yan, general secretary, and Ye Yiqun, deputy general secretary; Wu Youxun, minister of the Education Department of the East China Military and Political Committee; Shen Tilan, deputy minister; Xu Dixing, deputy director of the East China Financial and Economic Committee; economists Shou Jinwen and Wu Chengxi; scientists Lu Yudao and Zhang Mengwen; historians Zhou Gucheng and Li Yanong; and democratic figures Chen Jianchen and Hu Ziying, totaling more than fifty people.

### 苏联三教授抵沪讲学

1950-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联三教授
    抵沪讲学
    【新华社上海二十九日电】应中苏友好协会总会之请来我国讲学的苏联生物学博士努日金、历史学博士吉谢列夫及经济学硕士马卡洛娃一行已于二十七日晚由宁抵沪。前往车站欢迎者有中苏友协上海分会副会长陈望道、黎照寰，总干事夏衍、副总干事叶以群，华东军政委员会教育部部长吴有训、副部长沈体兰，华东财政经济委员会副主任许涤新，经济学家寿进文、吴承禧，科学家卢于道、张孟闻，历史学家周谷城、李亚农及民主人士陈建晨、胡子婴等五十余人。
