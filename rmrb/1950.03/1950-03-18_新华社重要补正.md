Title: Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1950-03-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
    March 15th dispatch: In the draft of "Government Administration Council Issues Instructions Regarding Spring Ploughing Production," in the fourth item "issuing agricultural loans," the fourteen characters "according to the method stipulated in item one, section five" should be added before it; the word "down" should be added before the fifth item "submitting public grain." (This draft was published on page of this newspaper on the 16th).
