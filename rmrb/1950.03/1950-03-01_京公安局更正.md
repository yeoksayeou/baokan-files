Title: Beijing Public Security Bureau Correction
Author:
Date: 1950-03-01
Page: 4

　　Beijing Public Security Bureau Correction
    In Article 1 of the "Beijing Municipal Vehicle Management Regulations," "all public, private, and commercial vehicles in this city" should read "all public, private, military, and commercial vehicles in this city." In Article 4, "other vehicles of each type" should read "other vehicles of each." (This news item was published on page four of the February 26th edition.)

### 京公安局更正

1950-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　京公安局更正
    京市车辆管理办法第一条中“凡本市公私暨营业用”，应为“凡本市公、私、部队暨营业用”。第四条中“其他车辆每种”，应为“其他车辆每辆”。（此项消息刊二月二十六日本版）
