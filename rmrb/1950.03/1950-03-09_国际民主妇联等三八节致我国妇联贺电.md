Title: All-China Women's Federation Receives Congratulatory Telegrams from International Democratic Women's Federation on International Women's Day

Author:

Date: 1950-03-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation received congratulatory telegrams from Madame Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, the Prague Women's Conference, and Hoxha, President of the Albanian Women's Union, on "March 8th" International Women's Day. The telegrams are as follows:

(1) Congratulatory telegram from Madame Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, to Comrade Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation:

	On the occasion of March 8th International Women's Day, the Women's International Democratic Federation extends its warmest congratulations to the women of China and thanks them for their great contribution to world peace in the glorious liberation struggle of the Chinese people, and gives great hope to oppressed nations and women. And wish the great friend Comrade Cai Chang good health.

					Madame Eugénie Cotton
					President of the Women's International Democratic Federation

(2) Congratulatory telegram from the Prague Women's Conference to the All-China Democratic Women's Federation:

	On the occasion of "March 8th" International Women's Day, the women of Czechoslovakia extend their warmest congratulations to the Chinese women, the warriors of peace.

					Prague Women's Conference
						March 7th

(3) Congratulatory telegram from the Albanian Women's Union to Madame Cai Chang of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation:

	On the occasion of March 8th International Women's Day, the Albanian Women's Union extends warm greetings to the heroic women of China and the Soviet Union. Congratulations on their new success in strengthening the People's Republic of China and consolidating the invincible camp of peace.

					Naxhmize Hoxha
					President of the Albanian Women's Union

### 国际民主妇联等三八节致我国妇联贺电

1950-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联等三八节致我国妇联贺电
    【新华社北京八日电】中华全国民主妇女联合会收到国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人、布拉格妇女会议、阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会主席霍克哈的“三八”节贺电，电文如下：
    （一）国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人贺电中国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅同志：
    值此三八节的机会，国际民主妇联对于中国妇女，致最热烈的祝贺，并感谢她们在中国人民辉煌的解放斗争中，对全世界和平的伟大贡献，同时给与被压迫民族和妇女广大的希望。并祝伟大的友人蔡畅同志健康。
                                       国际民主妇联主席
                                                戈登夫人
    （二）布拉格妇女会议贺电中华全国民主妇女联合会：
    捷克妇女在“三八”节日敬向和平战士的中国妇女致最热烈的祝贺。
                                             布拉格妇女会议
                                                三月七日
    （三）阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会贺电中华全国民主妇女联合会蔡畅夫人：
    阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会借三八节的机会，向中国和苏联英勇的妇女致以热烈的敬礼。祝贺她们在加强中华人民共和国和巩固和平的不可屈服的阵营之事业上，得到新的成功。
                                   阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会主席
                                       纳克丝密热·霍克哈
