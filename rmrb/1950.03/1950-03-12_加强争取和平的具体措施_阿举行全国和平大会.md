　　Strengthening Concrete Measures to Fight for Peace
    Albania Holds National Peace Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th] A comprehensive TASS dispatch: The Albanian National Congress for the Defense of Peace opened in Tirana, the capital of Albania, on the 5th amidst joyous celebrations from people across the country, and successfully concluded on the 8th. The congress elected a Permanent Committee of the All-Albanian Congress for the Defense of Peace, composed of sixty members, and a Presidium of the Permanent Committee composed of fifteen members. Malior Gonomi was elected Chairman of the Presidium, and Alexander, Dimitri Vani, Fiteliti, and Sheheru were elected Vice-Chairmen. The congress unanimously passed a resolution expressing full support for the decisions of the World Peace Congress and formulated concrete measures to strengthen and expand the Albanian movement for peace. The congress passed a letter to the people of the nation, urging the Albanian people to wholeheartedly strive for the realization of the two-year plan for consolidating national independence. The letter also stated: The Albanian people should spare no effort to strengthen the peace camp and, together with the Soviet Union and other people's democratic countries, fight for world peace.

### 加强争取和平的具体措施  阿举行全国和平大会

1950-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　加强争取和平的具体措施
    阿举行全国和平大会
    【新华社北京十一日电】综合塔斯社消息：阿尔巴尼亚全国拥护和平大会，在各地人民欢庆中于五日在阿京地拉那开幕，并已于八日胜利闭幕。大会选出拥有六十名委员的全阿拥护和平大会常设委员会，和由十五名委员组成的常设委员会主席团。马利奥尔·哥诺米被选为主席团主席，亚历山大、狄米万尼、非特列蒂、雪赫鲁被选为副主席，大会一致通过决议，表示完全拥护世界和平大会的决定，并制定加强和扩大阿尔巴尼亚争取和平运动的具体措施。大会通过告全国人民书，敦促阿尔巴尼亚人民全心全意为实现巩固国家独立的两年计划而努力。告人民书并称：阿尔巴尼亚人民应该不遗余力地加强和平阵营，并与苏联及各人民民主国家一同为全世界的和平而斗争。
