Title: Grand Banquet by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Welcomes Three Soviet Professors; Guo Moruo Presides, with Wu Yuzhang and Others Attending

Author:
Date: 1950-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 5th. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters held a banquet at 7:00 PM tonight to welcome Soviet biology professor Dr. Nuzhdin, history professor Dr. Kiselev, and economics associate professor Ms. Makarova. Vice President Guo Moruo presided over the reception. Attendees included Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters Wu Yuzhang, Deputy Directors of the Central People's Government Cultural and Educational Committee Ma Xulun and Lu Dingyi, Secretary-General Hu Qiaomu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Finance and Economics Committee Xue Muqiao, Deputy Minister of Education Qian Junrui, Vice Presidents of the Academy of Sciences Tao Menghe and Zhu Kezhen, Person in Charge of Peking University Tang Yongtong, Person in Charge of Tsinghua University Ye Qisun, as well as university professors and experts Zhou Jianren, Yue Tianyu, Shen Zhiyuan, Chen Zhen, Fan Hong, Jian Bozan, Hou Wailu, Zheng Zhenduo, Xiang Da, Yin Da, Huang Xinmin, and Directors of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters Xiao San, Zhang Zhongshi, and others. Invited guests included Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, Shafanov, and Representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, Jabouhou.

### 中苏友好协会盛大宴会  欢迎苏联三教授  郭沫若主持招待吴玉章等多人参加

1950-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会盛大宴会
    欢迎苏联三教授
    郭沫若主持招待吴玉章等多人参加
    【新华社北京五日电】中苏友好协会总会今晚七时设宴欢迎苏联生物学教授努日金博士、历史学教授吉谢列夫博士、经济学副教授马卡洛娃硕士，由郭沫若副会长主持招待。参加宴会者有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，中央人民政府文教委员会副主任马叙伦、陆定一，秘书长胡乔木，中央人民政府财经委员会秘书长薛暮桥，教育部副部长钱俊瑞，科学院副院长陶孟和、竺可桢，北京大学校务负责人汤用彤，清华大学校务负责人叶企荪以及各大学教授、专家周建人、乐天宇、沈志远、陈桢、樊弘、翦伯赞、侯外庐、郑振铎、向达、尹达、黄新民，中苏友好协会总会理事萧三、张仲实等。应邀作陪者有苏联驻我国大使馆秘书沙夫朗诺夫及苏联对外文化协会驻中国代表贾布侯。
