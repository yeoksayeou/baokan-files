Title: The Sixth Issue of "People's China" Published
Author:
Date: 1950-03-18
Page: 3

　　[Our Newspaper] The sixth issue of the English-language bi-monthly magazine "People's China" was published on the 15th. This issue includes Deng Yingchao's "Chinese Women Help Build a New China," Zhou Xuesheng's "Lushun and Dalian," and "Back to the Sunshine," which vividly depicts the creation of the prostitution system and the transformation of prostitutes through the stories of five prostitutes. In addition, there are Ding Ling's "The Soviet People," Feng Youlan's "I Discovered Marxism-Leninism," and articles such as "Chairman Mao is Back" and "The Yihe River Project."

### 《人民中国》第六期出版

1950-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　《人民中国》第六期出版
    【本报讯】英文半月刊《人民中国》第六期已于十五日出版。本期有邓颖超的《中国妇女帮助建设新中国》、周学声的《旅顺与大连》。《回到阳光下》，用五个妓女的故事，生动地写出娼妓制度的造成及妓女改造的经过。此外，还有丁玲的《苏联人》，冯友兰的《我发见了马列主义》，以及《毛主席回来》、《沂河工程》等文。
