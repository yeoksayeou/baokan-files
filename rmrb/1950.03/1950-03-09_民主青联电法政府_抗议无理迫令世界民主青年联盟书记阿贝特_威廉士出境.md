Title: Democratic Youth Federation Sends Telegram to French Government Protesting the Unreasonable Expulsion of World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretary Abet Williams

Author:
Date: 1950-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 8th: The All-China Federation of Democratic Youth sent a telegram to the French government on the 6th, protesting the French government's unreasonable measure of forcibly deporting Abet Williams (Australian youth representative), Secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, on February 20th. The telegram reads as follows:

To Mr. Pideault of the French Government:

Chinese youth strongly protest the French government's expulsion of Abet Williams, Secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and demand the immediate cancellation of this decision, which violates democratic rights and hinders the world youth movement.

                      Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth
                                     March 6, 1950

### 民主青联电法政府  抗议无理迫令世界民主青年联盟书记阿贝特·威廉士出境

1950-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　民主青联电法政府
    抗议无理迫令世界民主青年联盟书记阿贝特·威廉士出境
    【新华社北京八日电】中华全国民主青年联合总会于六日致电法国政府，抗议法国政府在二月二十日无理迫令世界民主青年联盟书记阿贝特·威廉士（澳洲青年代表）出境的无理措施，电文如下：
    法国政府皮杜尔先生：
    中国青年严重抗议法国政府驱逐世界民主青年联盟书记阿贝特·威廉士出境，并要求立即取消这次侵犯民主权利及妨碍世界青年运动的决定。
                      中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志
                                     一九五○年三月六日
