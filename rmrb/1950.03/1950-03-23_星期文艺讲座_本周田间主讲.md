Title: Sunday Literary Lecture: Tian Jian to Lecture This Week
Author:
Date: 1950-03-23
Page: 3

　　Sunday Literary Lecture
    Tian Jian to Lecture This Week
    [News Dispatch from the Beijing Municipal News Office] The Sunday Literary Lecture, jointly organized by the Mass Literature Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Department, will feature Tian Jian as the main speaker this week. The topic of his lecture is: "How Should Literary Workers Experience Life?" After the lecture, Li Xinghai and Zhou Yintian will perform the Leting Drum performance, "Chunyun's Divorce." The location is the Youth Palace (the former Majestic Theatre), and the time is Sunday (the 26th) at 9:30 AM. Admission is by long-term pass or temporary pass. Temporary passes can be obtained from the Youth Service Department from Saturday.

### 星期文艺讲座  本周田间主讲

1950-03-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    本周田间主讲
    【京市新闻处讯】大众文艺创作研究会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座，本周请田间主讲，讲题为：《文艺工作者如何体验生活？》。会后由李兴海、周荫田演唱乐亭大鼓《春云离婚》。地点在青年宫（美琪旧址），时间是星期日（二十六日）上午九时半。凭长期券或临时券入场，临时券可于星期六至青年服务部领取。
