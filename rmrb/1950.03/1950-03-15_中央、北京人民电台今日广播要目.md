Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1950-03-15
Page: 4

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Schedule
　　[This Dispatch] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (15th) at 20:20: (1) A heroic story that occurred during a naval exercise by the People's Liberation Army on the East China coastal front, "Storm at Sea"; (2) Newsletter "Liberated Peasants Using New Farm Tools". 17:30 Youth Time: "The Struggle of Southern China's Youth Before and After Liberation." 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: General Electrical Knowledge, Lecture 23, "Fluorescent Lamps." 18:00 First Literary and Art Program: Li Xinghai sings the Leting Drum "Catching Demons and Exorcising Evil." 21:00 Second Literary and Art Program: The Experimental School of Peking Opera under the Ministry of Culture's Opera Improvement Bureau broadcasts the second part of the new Peking Opera "Mulan Joins the Army."
　　[Also News] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 12:30 Workers' Program: (1) Cultural Entertainment: The handicrafts workers' night school choir of the Ninth District Federation of Trade Unions sings songs; (2) Newsletter Speech: Chen Yide of the Fengtai Locomotive Depot speaks on "We have completed the plan of safe travel of 200,000 kilometers." 13:00 Youth Program: Central Academy of Drama recites poems: (1) "The Death of Pushkin" by Lermontov, (2) "The Tale of the Priest and His Worker Balda" and "The Tale of the Fisherman and the Goldfish" by Pushkin. 16:30 Voice of the Municipality: (1) The Beijing Municipal Bureau of Commerce's explanation of the two announcements to various industrial and commercial households, (2) Several air defense measures stipulated by the Beijing Municipal Air Defense Committee's Air Defense Command. 18:45 Listener Service: Introducing a Soviet film "Flames of Wrath."

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-03-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（十五日）二十点二十分：（一）华东沿海前线人民解放军在航海演习当中发生的一个英雄故事《海上的风暴》，（二）通讯《翻身农民使用新农具》。十七点三十分青年时间：《解放前后华南青年的斗争》。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：电学常识第二十三讲《萤光灯》。十八点第一次文艺节目：李兴海演唱乐亭大鼓《捉妖拿邪》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：文化部戏曲改进局京剧实验学校演播新京剧《木兰从军》第二部分。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人节目：（一）文化娱乐：九区工会办事处手工业工人夜校工人歌咏队唱歌，（二）通讯讲话：丰台机务段陈以德讲“我们完成了安全走行二十万公里计划”。十三点青年节目：中央戏剧学院朗诵诗：（１）莱蒙托夫作的《普希金之死》，（２）普希金作的《牧师和他的工人巴尔达的故事》、《渔夫和金鱼的故事》。十六点三十分市政之声：（１）京市商业局对各工商户的两件布告的说明，（２）京市防空委员会防空指挥部规定的几项防空办法。十八点四十五分听众服务：介绍一部苏联影片《愤怒的火焰》。
