Title: "Saturday Literary Lecture: This Week's Performance Concert on Folk Art Studies"
Author:
Date: 1950-03-18
Page: 3

【News Dispatch from Beijing】The Saturday Literary Lecture, jointly sponsored by the Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Department, will organize a "Performance Concert on Folk Art Studies" this week. It will be held at the Youth Palace on Sunday (the 19th) at 9:00 a.m. The program is as follows: 1. Zhao Shuli will give an opening speech. 2. "Examining the Witch" (Qinshu), introduced by Zhao Shuli and performed by Guan Xuezeng. 3. "Gao Laozhuang" (Danxian), introduced, performed and sung by Cao Baolu. 4. "New Water Margin" (Ping Shu), introduced and narrated by Lian Runru. 5. "Great Western Chamber" (Jingyun Dagu), introduced by Wang Chun, broadcasting Liu Baoquan's record. 6. "Hundred Birds Paying Homage to the Phoenix" (Paizi Opera), introduced by Wang Yaping and performed by Gu Rongfu and Yin Fulai. 7. "Visiting the Sick" (Fengdiao Dagu), introduced by Wang Chun and performed by Wei Xikui. 8. Zhao Shuli will give a closing speech.

### 星期文艺讲座  本周举行曲艺研究演唱会

1950-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    本周举行曲艺研究演唱会
    【京市新闻处讯】大众文艺创作研究会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座，本周组织“曲艺研究演唱会”。星期日（十九日）上午九时在青年宫举行。节目如下：一、赵树理致开会词。二、考神婆（琴书），由赵树理介绍，关学增演唱。三、高老庄（单弦），由曹宝禄介绍演并唱。四、新水浒（评书），由连润如介绍并讲说。五、大西厢（京韵大鼓），由王春介绍，放送刘宝全唱片。六、百鸟朝凤（牌子戏），由王亚平介绍，顾荣甫尹福来演唱。七、探病（奉调大鼓），由王春介绍，魏喜奎演唱。八、赵树理致闭会词。
