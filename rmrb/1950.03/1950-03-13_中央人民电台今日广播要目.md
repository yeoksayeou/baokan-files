Title: Central People's Radio Station Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1950-03-13
Page: 3

　　[Our Newspaper News] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (March 13th) at 20:20: (1) An article written by Ye Keming, head of the Chinese Land and Air Transport Workers Delegation to the World Land and Air Transport Workers Conference, after returning to China, entitled "The Brilliant Achievements of Soviet Railway Construction." (2) A section from Simonov's new work "Fighting China," the deputy head of the Soviet Cultural, Artistic and Scientific Delegation that visited our country last October, entitled "Meeting General Liu Bocheng." 17:30 Women's Time: "Introduction to a Female Soldier's Autobiography in the Army." 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: "The Great Contributions of Marx, Engels, Lenin and Stalin in the Field of Natural Science." 18:00 First Literary Program: Henan Zhui Zi ballad "Fiancée Persuades Husband to Join the Army." 21:00 Second Literary Program: Soviet Music and Songs. 18:45 New Songs: "Spring Plowing," "Intensive Farming," etc.

### 中央人民电台今日广播要目

1950-03-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（十三日）二十点二十分：（一）参加世界陆空运输工人会议中国陆空运输工人代表团团长叶克明归国后写的一篇文章《苏联铁路建设的辉煌成就》。（二）去年十月间访问我国的苏联文化艺术科学代表团副团长西蒙诺夫的新作《战斗着的中国》里面的一段“会见了刘伯承将军”。十七点三十分妇女时间：《介绍部队中的一位女战士的自述》。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：《马恩列斯在自然科学领域中的伟大贡献》。十八点第一次文艺节目：坠子“未婚妻劝夫参军”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：苏联音乐和歌曲。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《春耕》、《精耕细作》等。
