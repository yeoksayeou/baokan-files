Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-03-06
Page: 4

　　【News from our newspaper】Central People's Broadcasting Station today (6th), 17:30 Women's Time: A summary broadcast of an article written by Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government and Honorary Chairman of the National Democratic Women's Federation, entitled "March 8th Commemoration and Household Women's Production and Construction"; the full text of this article will be broadcast at 20:20. 18:30 Natural Science Lectures: General Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 20, "How Telephones Transmit Sound". 18:00 First Literary Program: Li Xinghai sings Leting Drum "Beating the Yellow Wolf". 21:00 Second Literary Program: Soviet music and songs. 18:45 New songs.
　　[Further News] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, 12:30 Workers' Program: (1) Trade Union Work: Xiao Ming, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, conveys the resolutions of the Second Session of the Second Beijing People's Representative Conference, (2) Worker Services: Answers to questions about the station's new programs for March. 13:00 Youth Program: (1) School News, (2) Current Affairs Talk. 16:30 Municipal Voice: The Beijing Municipal People's Government Construction Bureau talks about "Why Building Management is Necessary".

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（六日）十七点三十分妇女时间：摘要播送中央人民政府副主席、全国民主妇联名誉主席宋庆龄写的一篇文章《“三八”纪念与家庭妇女生产建设》；二十点二十分播送这篇文章的全文。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：电学常识第二十讲《电话怎样传达声音》。十八点第一次文艺节目：李兴海演唱乐亭大鼓《打黄狼》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：苏联音乐和歌曲。十八点四十五分新歌曲。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人节目：（一）工会工作：北京市总工会副主席萧明传达北京市第二届第二次各界人民代表会议的决议，（二）工人服务：解答对该台三月份新节目的问题。十三点青年节目：（一）学校新闻，（二）时事漫谈。十六点三十分市政之声：北京市人民政府建设局谈“为什么要管理建筑”。
