Title: My Delegate to the World Peace Congress, Xiao San, to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1950-03-29
Page: 1

　　My Delegate to the World Peace Congress
    Xiao San to Return Home
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th] Xiao San, the Chinese delegate to the Third Plenary Session of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress held in Sweden, arrived in Moscow from Sweden on the 24th. At the invitation of the Soviet Peace Committee and the Writers' Association, he will make a short stay in Moscow and then return home.

### 我出席和大代表萧三将返国

1950-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　我出席和大代表
    萧三将返国
    【新华社莫斯科二十八日讯】出席在瑞典举行的世界拥护和平大会常委会第三次全体大会的我国代表萧三，已于二十四日自瑞典抵莫斯科。他应苏联拥护和平委员会及作家协会之邀，将在莫斯科作短期勾留，然后返国。
