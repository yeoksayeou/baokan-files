Title: Forthcoming Sunday Literature and Art Lecture
Author:
Date: 1950-03-31
Page: 4
[News from the Beijing Municipal News Office] The Sunday Literature and Art Lecture, jointly sponsored by the Municipal Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Department, will feature Ma Shaobo this week, whose topic will be: "Experiences in Opera Reform." After the lecture, Cao Baolu will perform a single-string piece, "Centipede Ridge." The venue is the Youth Palace, and the time is 9:30 a.m. on Sunday (April 2nd). Admission is by long-term or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department from Saturday.

### 星期文艺讲座预告

1950-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座预告
    【京市新闻处讯】本市大众文艺创作研究会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座，本周由马少波主讲，讲题为：“戏曲改革经验”。会后由曹宝禄演唱单弦，《蜈蚣岭》。会址在青年宫，时间是星期日（四月二日）上午九时半。凭长期券或临时券入场，临时券可于星期六至青年服务部领取。
