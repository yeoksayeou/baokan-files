Title: Shikov Sends Letter to Chairman Liu Shaoqi Thanking for Congratulations on Molotov's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1950-03-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 25th

　　Shikov Sends Letter to Chairman Liu Shaoqi
    Thanking for Congratulations on Molotov's Birthday
    [Xinhua News Agency, March 25th] Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, received a letter from Shikov, Chargé d'Affaires of the Soviet Embassy in China, the full text of which is as follows:

Mr. Chairman,

I have been entrusted by Molotov, and also in my own name, to express my deep gratitude to you for your sincere and warm congratulations on his sixtieth birthday.

I take this opportunity to extend my highest respects to you, Mr. Chairman.

To Mr. Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

Shikov
March 23rd

### 史白夫函刘少奇会长  感谢祝贺莫洛托夫寿辰

1950-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　史白夫函刘少奇会长
    感谢祝贺莫洛托夫寿辰
    【新华社二十五日讯】中苏友好协会总会刘少奇会长收到苏联驻我国大使馆代办史白夫来函一件，全文如下：会长先生
    我受莫洛托夫的委托并以自己的名义对您为他六十寿辰的真诚与热烈的祝贺表示深厚的谢意。
    顺此，向您，会长先生，致崇高的敬意。中苏友好协会总会会长刘少奇先生        史白夫  三月二十三日
