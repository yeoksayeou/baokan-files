Title: Beijing Department Store Branch Increases Wholesale and Retail Department
Author: Yu Zhanxue, Chai Yifang
Date: 1950-03-29
Page: 4

　　Beijing Department Store Branch
    Increases Wholesale and Retail Department
    [Our Newspaper] In order to facilitate the general supply of daily necessities to the vast number of citizens, the Beijing Branch of the North China Department Store Company has added a wholesale outlet at the old Ruichanghe site opposite the Yaowang Temple in Dongxiaoshi, outside Chongwenmen. It is now open for business. In addition, a second outlet is being added at the old site of the Huafu Department Store on Guanyinsi Street, outside Qianmen. Preparations are currently underway, and it is scheduled to open on April 2nd. (Yu Zhanxue, Chai Yifang)

### 北京百货分公司  增设批发零售部
于占学　柴一方
1950-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京百货分公司
    增设批发零售部
    【本报讯】华北百货公司北京分公司为了便利普遍供应广大市民日用百货，特于崇文门外东晓市药王庙对过瑞昌和旧址增设批发门市部，现已开始营业。此外，并于前门外观音寺街华府百货店旧址增设第二门市部，目前正积极筹备，定四月二日开始营业。（于占学、柴一方）
