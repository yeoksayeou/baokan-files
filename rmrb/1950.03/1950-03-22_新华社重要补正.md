Title: Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1950-03-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
    In the draft of the "Government Administration Council's Instructions on Spring Plowing Production" issued on the 15th, the phrase "in accordance with the methods stipulated in item 1, section 5" should be added before item 4, "issuing agricultural loans." (This draft was published in the 16th edition of this paper.)
