Title: The 24th Session of the Government Administration Council Approves Key Points of the Cultural and Educational Work Plan and Passes Instructions on Spring Water Conservancy Projects
Author: 
Date: 1950-03-18
Page: 1

　　The 24th Session of the Government Administration Council
    Approves Key Points of the Cultural and Educational Work Plan and Passes Instructions on Spring Water Conservancy Projects
    [Xinhua News Agency, March 17th] The 24th session of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held at 3:30 PM today. The meeting heard a report from Vice Premier and Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, Guo Moruo, on the 1950 Cultural and Educational Work Plan, and then discussed and approved the key points of this work plan. The meeting then heard a report from Vice Minister of the Ministry of Water Resources, Li Baohua, on spring water conservancy work, and discussed and passed instructions on the 1950 spring water conservancy projects. In addition, the meeting also approved several appointment cases and submitted appointment cases to the Central People's Government for approval.

### 政务院廿四次政务会议  批准文教工作计划要点通过水利春修工程指示

1950-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院廿四次政务会议
    批准文教工作计划要点通过水利春修工程指示
    【新华社十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二十四次政务会议于今日下午三时半举行。会议听取了副总理兼政务院文化教育委员会主任郭沫若关于一九五○年文教工作计划的报告后，讨论和批准了这一工作计划的要点。会议继听取了水利部副部长李葆华关于水利春修工作的报告，并讨论和通过了关于一九五○年水利春修工程的指示。此外，会议并通过任命案和提请中央人民政府批准任命案多项。
