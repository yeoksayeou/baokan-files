Title: Accelerate Industrial Production, Improve Agricultural Economy, Guarantee Completion of This Year's Economic Plan, National Income to Increase by 30% Compared to Last Year
Author:
Date: 1950-03-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, March 6th: The Bulgarian Council of Ministers has passed a resolution on the 1950 (the second year of the Five-Year Plan) National Economic Plan. The resolution pointed out that the completion and exceeding of the 1949 economic plan guaranteed the further consolidation and expansion of the socialist sector in the national economy. The socialist sector already accounts for 60% of the national income, 99% of industrial production, 86% of trade volume, and 10% of agriculture. The primary economic and political task for 1950 is to further comprehensively develop all sectors of the national economy, accelerate industrial production, improve the work of agricultural economic units, increase crop yields and livestock productivity, and vigorously improve the material and cultural standards of the people.

　　The Council of Ministers has approved the following indices for the 1950 National Economic Plan: Compared with 1949, national income will increase by 29.8%, and industrial products will increase by 16.3% (calculated at 1939 prices).

### 加速工业增产改进农业经济  保通过今年经济计划  国民收入将较去年增百分之三十

1950-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　加速工业增产改进农业经济
    保通过今年经济计划
    国民收入将较去年增百分之三十
    【新华社北京十日电】塔斯社索非亚六日讯：保加利亚部长会议业已通过一九五○年（按系五年计划第二年）国民经济计划的决议。决议指出：一九四九年经济计划的完成及超过，保证了国民经济中社会主义部分的进一步巩固与扩大。社会主义部分在国民收入中已占百分之六十，在工业生产中占百分之九十九，在贸易额中占百分之八十六，在农业中占百分之十。一九五○年经济与政治的首要任务，是进一步全面发展国民经济的一切部门，加速工业增产、改进农业经济单位的工作，增加农作物收获量与牲畜生产率，并大力提高人民的物质与文化水平。
    部长会议已批准一九五○年国民经济计划指数如下：与一九四九年比较，国民收入将增加百分之二十九点八，工业产品将增加百分之十六点三（以一九三九年价格计算）。
