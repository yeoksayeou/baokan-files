Author:
Date: 1950-03-18
Page: 4

　　Broadcast Programs
　　[Our Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (March 18th) at 20:30: (1) The changes in the lives of workers at Anshan Iron and Steel Company over the past year, "A good life gained from labor"; (2) A story about a technician inventing a new tool, "A key that helped Li Qingxuan." 17:30 Youth Time: "How our Youth League branch's work has improved." 18:00 First Arts Program: Mei Hua Drum "Envoy of Jiangnan." 21:00 Second Arts Program: Amateur National Music Research Society performs national music. 18:30 Soviet Songs. 18:45 New Songs.
　　[Another Dispatch] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 10:00: "Broadcast Learning Lectures" sponsored by the Higher Education Department of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government: Vice Minister of Education Qian Junrui will speak on "The Historical Characteristics of the Chinese Revolution." 12:30 Workers and Youth Joint Program: Teaching to sing "We are Revolutionary Pioneers." 16:30 Voice of the Municipality: (1) Notice from the Municipal Taxation Bureau on the collection of the 1949 second half-year industrial and commercial tax from the first of next month, (2) Notice from the Beijing Municipal Air Defense Headquarters requesting citizens to strengthen air defense and pay attention to air raid alarm signals, (3) Emergency handling measures for maintaining public order during air raid alarms promulgated by the Beijing Municipal Air Defense Headquarters. 17:00 Children's Program: The story of a Soviet patriotic young hero, Mania. 18:45 Women's Program: Knowledge of maternal and child hygiene "Pay attention to children's hygiene and how to prevent diseases." 19:00 Soviet Music. 19:15 Ding Haochuan's March 17th broadcast recording "Talking about family issues with young friends."

### 广播节目

1950-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（三月十八日）二十点三十分：（一）一年来鞍山钢铁公司工人生活的变化，“从劳动里得来的好生活”；（二）一个技术人员发明新工具的故事“一把钥匙帮了李庆萱的忙”。十七点三十分青年时间：“我们团支部的工作怎样有了进步”。十八点第一次文艺节目：梅花大鼓“江南使者”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：业余国乐研究社演播国乐。十八点三十分苏联歌曲。十八点四十五分新歌曲。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十点中央人民政府教育部高等教育司主办的“广播学习讲座”：由教育部副部长钱俊瑞讲“中国革命的历史特点”。十二点三十分工人青年联合节目：教唱歌“我们是革命先锋”。十六点三十分市政之声：（一）本市税务局从下月一日起开征本市一九四九年下半年度工商业税的通知，（二）北京市防空指挥部请市民加强防空和注意防空警报信号的通知，（三）本市防空指挥部颁布的防空警报期间维持治安紧急处理办法。十七点儿童节目：一个苏联爱国小英雄玛妮的故事。十八点四十五分妇女节目：妇婴卫生常识“注意儿童卫生与如何预防疾病”。十九点苏联音乐。十九点十五分丁浩川十七日广播录音“和青年朋友们谈谈家庭问题”。
