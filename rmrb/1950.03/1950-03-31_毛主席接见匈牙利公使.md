Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Hungarian Envoy
Author:
Date: 1950-03-31
Page: 1

　　Chairman Mao Receives the Hungarian Envoy
    [Xinhua News Agency, 30th] Chairman Mao Zedong received the Hungarian Ambassador to China, Mr. Száfránko, at 11:30 tonight. Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai was present.

### 毛主席接见匈牙利公使

1950-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见匈牙利公使
    【新华社三十日讯】毛泽东主席今晚十一时半接见匈牙利驻我国公使夏法朗柯。在座者有周恩来总理兼外交部长。
