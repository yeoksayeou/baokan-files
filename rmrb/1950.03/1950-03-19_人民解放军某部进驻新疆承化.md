Title: A Unit of the People's Liberation Army Enters Chenghua, Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1950-03-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, 18th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army arrived at Chenghua City, northern Xinjiang, famous for its gold production, on March 1st. They were warmly welcomed by the local people.

### 人民解放军某部进驻新疆承化

1950-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民解放军某部进驻新疆承化
    【新华社迪化十八日电】人民解放军某部，已于三月一日抵达中国西北部边境，进驻新疆北部产金闻名的承化城，受到当地群众的热烈欢迎。
