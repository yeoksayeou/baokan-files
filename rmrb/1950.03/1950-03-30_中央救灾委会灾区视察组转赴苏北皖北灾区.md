Title: Central Disaster Relief Committee Disaster Area Inspection Team Moves to Northern Jiangsu and Northern Anhui Disaster Areas
Author:
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 1

　　Central Disaster Relief Committee Disaster Area Inspection Team
　　Moves to Northern Jiangsu and Northern Anhui Disaster Areas
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 29th] The Central Disaster Relief Committee's Shandong and Northern Jiangsu Disaster Area Inspection Team, after working for six days in the severely affected areas of central and southern Shandong, has now completed its inspection and departed by car this morning for the Northern Jiangsu disaster area. The Plain and Northern Anhui Disaster Area Inspection Team, after inspecting the Plain disaster area, moved to the Northern Anhui disaster area on the 27th.

### 中央救灾委会灾区视察组转赴苏北皖北灾区

1950-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央救灾委会灾区视察组
    转赴苏北皖北灾区
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央救灾委员会山东、苏北灾区视察组在山东鲁中南灾重区工作六天后，现已视察完毕，于今晨乘车赴苏北灾区视察。又平原、皖北灾区视察组在平原灾区视察后，已于二十七日转赴皖北灾区视察。
