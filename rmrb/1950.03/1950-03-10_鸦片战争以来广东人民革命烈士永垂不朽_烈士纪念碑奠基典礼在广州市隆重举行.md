Title: Immortal Glory to Revolutionary Martyrs of Guangdong People Since the Opium War! Groundbreaking Ceremony for Martyrs' Memorial Held Grandly in Guangzhou

Author:

Date: 1950-03-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 9th: The groundbreaking ceremony for the Memorial to the Revolutionary Martyrs of Guangdong People since the Opium War was held on the 6th of this month at the foot of Guanyin Mountain in Guangzhou. A total of 914 representatives from 36 units, including the CCP Central Committee South China Sub-bureau, various democratic parties organizations, Guangdong Military Region, the People's Liberation Army 15th Corps, Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, and various people's organizations, attended this grand ceremony. Beneath the portraits of the martyrs, wreaths sent from all walks of life were piled high. Behind the memorial site stood four national flags, fluttering in the wind. Relatives of the revolutionary martyrs and veterans who participated in the 1927 Guangzhou Uprising marched into the venue in formation. After the national anthem was played in solemn silence by all, Gu Dacun, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, announced the commencement of the groundbreaking ceremony. Chairman Ye Jianying read aloud the foundation-laying proclamation of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, followed by the groundbreaking ceremony. Subsequently, representatives from all walks of life presented wreaths.

### 鸦片战争以来广东人民革命烈士永垂不朽  烈士纪念碑奠基典礼在广州市隆重举行

1950-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　鸦片战争以来广东人民革命烈士永垂不朽
    烈士纪念碑奠基典礼在广州市隆重举行
    【新华社广州九日电】鸦片战争以来广东人民革命烈士纪念碑奠基典礼，于本月六日在广州市观音山麓举行。出席此隆重仪式的有中共中央华南分局、各民主党派组织、广东军区、人民解放军第十五兵团、广东省人民政府、广州市人民政府以及各人民团体等共三十六个单位的代表九百一十四名。烈士遗像下面，堆满各界送来的花圈。在纪念碑址后面，矗立着四面国旗，迎风飘扬。革命烈士的亲属和参加一九二七年广州起义的老战士，均列队走进会场。在全体肃立中奏毕国歌后，广东省人民政府副主席古大存宣布奠基典礼开始，由叶剑英主席高声朗诵广东省人民政府的奠基典礼文告后即举行奠基仪式。继由各界代表呈献花圈。
