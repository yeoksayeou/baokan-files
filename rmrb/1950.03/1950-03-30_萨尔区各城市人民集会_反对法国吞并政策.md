Title: Rallies Held by People in Various Cities of the Saar Region Oppose France's Annexation Policy
Author:
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 27th. TASS, Berlin, 24th: The Saarbrücken dispatch of the National Democratic Party's newspaper "National Daily": Mass protest rallies against the Saar puppet government's approval of the so-called "Saar Agreement" and the French government's implementation of annexation policies have been held in major cities of the Saar region. The people present at the meetings unanimously declared: The people of the Saar will never agree to be annexed. The Saar region belongs to the German people and will always belong to the German people.

### 萨尔区各城市人民集会  反对法国吞并政策

1950-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　萨尔区各城市人民集会
    反对法国吞并政策
    【新华社廿七日讯】塔斯社柏林二十四日讯：国家民主党机关报《国民日开》萨尔布鲁根讯：萨尔人民反对萨尔傀儡政府批准所谓“萨尔协定”和法国政府实行吞并政策的群众抗议大会，已在萨尔区各大城市举行。到会的人们一致声称：萨尔人民决不同意被吞并，萨尔区是德国人民的，而且永远是德国人民的。
