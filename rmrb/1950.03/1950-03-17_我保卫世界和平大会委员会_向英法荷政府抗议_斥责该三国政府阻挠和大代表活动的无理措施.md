Title: China Peace Committee Protests to the Governments of Britain, France and the Netherlands, Condemning the Unreasonable Measures of These Three Governments in Obstructing the Activities of World Peace Congress Delegates
Author:
Date: 1950-03-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, today sent telegrams to the governments of Britain, France, and the Netherlands, protesting the unreasonable measures taken by these countries to obstruct the peace activities of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace delegates. The telegrams are as follows:
(1) Telegram to British Prime Minister Clement Attlee:
    To British Prime Minister Clement Attlee:
    On behalf of the peace-loving Chinese people, I strongly protest against the British government's refusal to allow the Soviet and Hungarian representatives, who are members of the Standing Committee delegation of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, to enter the country. This absurd measure completely violates the desire of the British people for peace.
    Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee
        March 16, 1950
(2) To French Prime Minister Georges Bidault:
    To French Prime Minister Georges Bidault:
    We express our strong protest against the French government's decision to not allow Mr. Korneichuk, a member of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, to continue his stay in France, and demand the immediate revocation of this unreasonable decision that violates the interests of world peace.
    China Peace Committee
        Chairman, Guo Moruo
    March 16, 1950
(3) To Dutch Prime Minister Willem Drees:
    To Dutch Prime Minister Willem Drees:
    On behalf of the peace-loving Chinese people, I strongly protest against the Dutch government's detention and expulsion of the delegation of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. This absurd measure by the Dutch government completely violates the desire of the Dutch people for peace and fully exposes the Dutch government's pursuit of a criminal warmongering policy.
    Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee
    March 16, 1950

### 我保卫世界和平大会委员会  向英法荷政府抗议  斥责该三国政府阻挠和大代表活动的无理措施

1950-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　我保卫世界和平大会委员会
    向英法荷政府抗议
    斥责该三国政府阻挠和大代表活动的无理措施
    【新华社十六日讯】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会主席郭沫若，今日分别致电英、法、荷三国政府，抗议各该国政府阻挠世界拥护和平大会代表从事和平活动的无理措施。电文如下：
  （一）致英国首相艾德礼电：英国首相艾德礼先生：
    我代表爱好和平的中国人民，严重抗议英国政府拒绝世界拥护和平大会常设委员会代表团团员苏联和匈牙利代表之入境。这一悖谬措施完全违反了英国人民要求和平的意愿。
  中国保卫世界和平大会委员会主席  郭沫若
      一九五○年三月十六日
  （二）致法国总理皮杜尔电：法国总理皮杜尔先生：
    我们对法国政府不许世界拥护和平大会常设委员高涅楚克继续停留法国一事，表示严重抗议，并要求立即撤消这一违反世界和平利益的无理决定。
    中国保卫世界和平大会委员会
        主席  郭沫若
    一九五○年三月十六日
  （三）致荷兰总理德里斯电：荷兰政府总理威尔海姆·德里斯先生：
    我代表爱好和平的中国人民，严重抗议荷兰政府拘禁并驱逐世界和平大会常设委员会代表团出境。荷兰政府这一悖谬措施完全违反荷兰人民要求和平的意愿，并完全暴露了荷兰政府的正执行着罪恶的好战政策。
      中国保卫世界和平大会委员会主席  郭沫若
  一九五○年三月十六日
