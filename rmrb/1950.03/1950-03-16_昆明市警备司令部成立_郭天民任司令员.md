Title: Kunming Garrison Command Established, Guo Tianmin Appointed Commander
Author:
Date: 1950-03-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 15th: The Kunming Garrison Command was officially established on the 11th upon order. Guo Tianmin was appointed Commander, Zhu Jiabi and Zhou Xueyi were appointed Deputy Commanders, and Gu Jingsheng and Lei Qiyun were appointed Political Commissar and Deputy Political Commissar respectively.

### 昆明市警备司令部成立  郭天民任司令员

1950-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　昆明市警备司令部成立
    郭天民任司令员
    【新华社昆明十五日电】昆明市警备司令部奉令于十一日正式成立。由郭天民任司令员，朱家璧、周学义任副司令员，谷景生、雷起云任正副政治委员。
