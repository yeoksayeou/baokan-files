Title: Unified Leadership for All Professional Trade Work: Six State-Owned Specialized Companies Established; Managers of Each Company Appointed

Author:

Date: 1950-03-12
Page: 2

　　Unified Leadership for All Professional Trade Work
    Six State-Owned Specialized Companies Established
    Managers of Each Company Appointed
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th] In order to unify the operations of state-owned specialized trade companies in various regions of the country, the China Bristle Company, China Native Produce Company, China Oils and Fats Company, China Import Company, and China Egg Products Company have all been formally established in Tianjin on the 10th of this month upon the order of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government. The China Cotton Yarn and Cloth Company has also been formally established in Beijing on the same day upon order.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th] The State Council of the Central People's Government has appointed Zhou Kangmin as the manager of the China Grain Company, and He Fazhang as the deputy manager; Li Nansheng as the manager of the China Coal Industry Company; Chen Qixiang as the manager of the China Cotton Yarn and Cloth Company; Li Ruxiu as the deputy manager of the China General Merchandise Company; Guo Jinwu as the manager of the China Oils and Fats Company; Zhao Chongde as the deputy manager of the China Bristle Company; Liu Yi as the manager of the China Native Produce Company, and Cai Xinde as the deputy manager; Sun Liji as the manager of the China Egg Products Company, and Song Keqiang as the deputy manager.

### 统一领导各项专业贸易工作  国营六专业公司分别成立  各公司经理人选业已任命

1950-03-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　统一领导各项专业贸易工作
    国营六专业公司分别成立
    各公司经理人选业已任命
    【新华社北京十一日电】为统一领导全国各区国营专业贸易公司的业务，中国猪鬃公司、中国土产公司、中国油脂公司、中国进口公司、中国蛋品公司均已奉中央人民政府贸易部命令于本月十日分别在津正式成立。中国花纱布公司亦奉令于同日在北京正式成立。
    【新华社北京十一日电】中央人民政府政务院任命周康民为中国粮食公司经理，何法章为副经理；李南生为中国煤业公司经理；陈其襄为中国花纱布公司经理；李汝修为中国百货公司副经理；郭今吾为中国油脂公司经理；赵重德为中国猪鬃公司副经理；刘毅为中国土产公司经理，蔡新德为副经理；孙立基为中国蛋品公司经理，宋克强为副经理。
