Title: Northeast Timber to Enter Customs in Large Quantities to Supply Construction Needs in Various Regions
Author: Run Zhang
Date: 1950-03-19
Page: 2

　　Northeast Timber to Enter Customs in Large Quantities to Supply Construction Needs in Various Regions
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] A large quantity of Northeast basswood will be shipped to Tianjin within days. In order to supply the construction needs of various urban and rural industries, the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government signed a timber contract with the Northeast Forestry Bureau, under which Northeast China will allocate various construction timbers to the interior, totaling 80,000 cubic meters of logs. The total value is approximately 86.4 billion RMB. The transportation time for this timber is planned to be 7,000 cubic meters to Beijing and Tianjin in March, and the entire amount will be shipped by the end of October. The operation of the timber will be the responsibility of the Beijing, Tianjin, and other branches of the North China Coal and Iron Company, which will allocate and supply it to various public and private sectors. The first batch of basswood in March is expected to arrive in Tianjin around the 20th of this month.
        (Run Zhang)

### 东北木材即大量入关供应各地建设需要
润章
1950-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北木材即大量入关供应各地建设需要
    【本报讯】东北大批椴木将于日内运津。中央人民政府贸易部为供给各城乡工业等建设需要，曾与东北林务总局签订木材合同，由东北向关内调拨各种建设木材，合原木八万立方米。总值人民币约八六四亿元。此项木材运输时间计划三月份运京津七千立方米，全数于十月底运完。木材之经营均由华北煤铁公司所属京、津等各分公司负责，调拨供给各公私部门。三月份首批椴木预计于本月二十日前后可抵津。
        （润章）
