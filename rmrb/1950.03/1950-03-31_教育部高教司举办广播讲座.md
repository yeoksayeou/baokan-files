Title: Ministry of Education's Higher Education Department Holds Radio Lectures
Author:
Date: 1950-03-31
Page: 3

【Our Newspaper Dispatches】

　　Ministry of Education's Higher Education Department Holds Radio Lectures
    [This Newspaper Report] In order to answer the questions and difficulties encountered by universities in Beijing and Tianjin in studying the political course "On New Democracy," the Higher Education Department of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government is holding regular radio study lectures on the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station. The first lesson, "The Historical Characteristics of the Chinese Revolution," is scheduled for Saturday, April 1st, from 10:00 AM to 11:30 AM, and will be lectured by Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister of the Ministry. The second lesson, "The History of the Chinese New Democratic Revolution," is scheduled for April 1st, 15th, and 29th, and will be broadcast in three parts. The Tianjin People's Broadcasting Station will also broadcast simultaneously. In addition, Comrade Hu Hua of the Renmin University of China will be invited to speak on "The Question of Revolutionary Leadership from the May Fourth Movement to the First Domestic Revolutionary War Period and the Second Domestic Revolutionary War Period."

### 教育部高教司举办广播讲座

1950-03-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　教育部高教司举办广播讲座
    【本报讯】中央人民政府教育部高等教育司为了解答京津各大学在学习“新民主主义论”政治课当中存在的疑难问题，特在北京市人民广播电台举办定期的广播学习讲座。第一课“中国革命的历史特点”，定于四月一日（星期六）上午十时至十一时半，由该部钱俊瑞副部长主讲，第二课：“中国新民主主义革命的历史”，定于四月一日、十五日及二十九日，分三次播讲。天津市人民广播电台同时联播。此外并请由中国人民大学胡华同志讲“五四运动到第一次国内革命战争时期及第二次国内革命战争时期的革命领导权问题”。
