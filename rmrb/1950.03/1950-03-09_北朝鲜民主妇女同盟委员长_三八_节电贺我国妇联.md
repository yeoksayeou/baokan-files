Title: North Korean Democratic Women's League Chairman Sends "March 8th" Congratulatory Telegram to All-China Women's Federation
Author:
Date: 1950-03-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th. Pak Chong-ae, Chairman of the North Korean Democratic Women's League, sent a congratulatory telegram to Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation on "March 8th" International Women's Day. The original telegram is as follows:

Comrade Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Beijing:

On the occasion of the festival of democratic women all over the world - March 8th International Women's Day, I, on behalf of the Korean women, extend warm congratulations to you and, through you, to all the heroic sisters of the People's Republic of China. The Korean women, who are fighting for the unification and independence of their motherland, are convinced that further consolidating the friendship and unity between the women of China and Korea will surely smash the new aggressive conspiracies and dying struggles of American imperialism in Asia, and win a glorious victory in the common struggle for world peace, democracy and freedom. Finally, I wish you and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation under your leadership greater success in the work of building a new China.

Pak Chong-ae, Chairman of the North Korean Democratic Women's League
March 8, 1950

### 北朝鲜民主妇女同盟委员长“三八”节电贺我国妇联

1950-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜民主妇女同盟委员长“三八”节电贺我国妇联
    【新华社北京八日电】北朝鲜民主妇女同盟委员长朴正爱向我国全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅致“三八”节贺电。原电如下：北京中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅同志：
    当全世界民主妇女的节日——三八国际妇女节，我代表朝鲜妇女向您和通过您向英勇的中华人民共和国全体姊妹们致热情的祝贺，为祖国的统一、独立而斗争的全朝鲜妇女，确信更加巩固中朝两国妇女的友谊团结，必定能粉碎美帝在亚洲的新的侵略阴谋与垂死的挣扎，并在为全世界和平民主自由的共同斗争中争取光辉的胜利。最后，谨祝您和您所领导的中华全国民主妇联在建设新中国的工作上有更大的成功。
                             北朝鲜民主妇女同盟委员长朴正爱
                                         一九五○年三月八日
