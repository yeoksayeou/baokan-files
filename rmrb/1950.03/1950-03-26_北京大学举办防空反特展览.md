Title: Peking University Holds Anti-Air Raid and Anti-Spy Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1950-03-26
Page: 3

　　Peking University Holds Anti-Air Raid and Anti-Spy Exhibition
    [This Newspaper's Report] In order to enhance the awareness of anti-air raid and anti-espionage work among teachers and students, the Security Committee of the Peking University School Committee has collected hundreds of types of materials related to anti-air raid and anti-espionage, including various charts, photographs, documents, and weapons related to anti-air raid knowledge and espionage crimes. Starting today (the 26th), an anti-air raid and anti-espionage exhibition will be held in the school library, welcoming all teachers, students, staff, and people from outside the school to visit. The exhibition will last for two days.
              (Peking University Security Committee)

### 北京大学举办防空反特展览

1950-03-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京大学举办防空反特展览
    【本报讯】北京大学校委会安全委员会为了提高师生对防空反特务工作的认识，特收集到防空反特务等资料数百种，内容有关于防空智识、特务罪行的各种图表、照片、证件、凶器等，于今（二十六日）起在该校图书馆举办防空反特展览会，欢迎全校师生职工和校外人士参观。展览期间为两日。
              （北大安全委员会）
