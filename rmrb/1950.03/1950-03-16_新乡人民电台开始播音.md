Title: Xinxiang People's Radio Begins Broadcasting
Author:
Date: 1950-03-16
Page: 3

　　Xinxiang People's Radio Begins Broadcasting
    [Xinxiang Dispatch, 10th] Xinxiang People's Broadcasting Station is now ready and scheduled to begin trial broadcasting on the 10th of this month. The station's wavelength is 300 meters, and the frequency is 1000 kilocycles. The daily broadcasting time is from 19:15 to 23:00.

### 新乡人民电台开始播音

1950-03-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　新乡人民电台开始播音
    【新乡十日讯】新乡人民广播电台现已筹备就绪，定本月十日试播。该台波长三百公尺，周率为一○○○千周。每日播音时间为十九点十五分到二十三点。
