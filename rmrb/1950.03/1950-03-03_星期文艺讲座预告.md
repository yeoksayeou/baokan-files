Title: Sunday Literary Lecture Announcement
Author:
Date: 1950-03-03
Page: 4

[Beijing News] The Sunday Literary Lecture, organized by the Beijing Mass Literary Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Department, will feature Ai Qing this week speaking on "The Question of Old and New Forms." After the lecture, Gu Rongfu and Yin Fulai will perform the "Pai Zi Qu: Celebrating the New Year After Liberation," and Lian Kuoru will discuss "Water Margin." The address remains the same: Soviet Foreign Culture Association, 19 Nanheyan Street. The time is Sunday (the 5th) at 10:00 AM. Admission is by long-term ticket or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department on Saturday.

### 星期文艺讲座预告

1950-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座预告
    【北京讯】京市大众文艺创作研究会与青年服务部所举办的星期文艺讲座，本星期邀请艾青讲“新旧形式问题”。会后有顾荣甫、尹福来表演牌子曲《翻身过春节》，连阔如讲《水浒传》。地址仍在南河沿十九号苏联对外文化协会。时间是星期日（五日）上午十时。凭长期券或临时券入场，临时券可于星期六到青年服务部领取。
