Title: National Forestry Business Conference Opens: Understanding the Situation to Formulate Work Guidelines and Tasks
Author:
Date: 1950-03-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 28th. The Ministry of Forestry and Land Reclamation of the Central People's Government convened the first National Forestry Business Conference in Beijing today. Attendees included representatives from Northeast China, Northwest China, East China, Central and South China, the five provinces of North China, Inner Mongolia, Xinjiang, and other regions engaged in forestry administration and forest utilization. At the meeting, Liang Xi, Minister of the Ministry of Forestry and Land Reclamation, delivered an opening speech stating that the specific content of this conference is:

	First, to understand the forestry situation in various regions, including the investigation of forest land area, forest protection and afforestation, forest utilization, nursery construction, the division of public and private forest rights, etc., to exchange experiences, and to solve the existing problems in the above aspects where possible, while also establishing the guidelines and tasks for current forestry work.

	Second, to discuss how to systematically establish forestry organizations. Appropriate forestry institutions should be established in all major administrative regions, provinces, cities, and counties across the country where there is a need and the conditions are possible, and the leadership relationships of all these institutions should be established.

	Third, based on the actual conditions in different regions, to stipulate the work plans and budget allocation for 1950.

### 全国林业业务会议开幕  了解情况制订工作方针和任务

1950-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国林业业务会议开幕
    了解情况制订工作方针和任务
    【新华社北京二十八日电】中央人民政府林垦部今日在京召开首次全国林业业务会议，出席者有东北区、西北区、华东区、中南区、华北五省、内蒙、新疆等地区从事林业行政及森林利用等工作的代表。会上林垦部梁希部长致开会词称：这次会议的具体内容是：
    一、了解各地林业情况，包括林地面积的调查，护林与造林，森林利用，苗圃建设，公私林权的划分等问题，交流经验，并在可能的条件下解决上述各方面所存在的问题，同时订出当前林业工作的方针和任务。
    二、讨论如何有系统地建立林业组织机构。全国各大行政区、省、市、县凡有需要及可能条件下，应建立适当的林业机构，并确立所有这些机构的领导关系。
    三、根据不同地区的实际情况，规定一九五○年的工作计划和预算的运用。
