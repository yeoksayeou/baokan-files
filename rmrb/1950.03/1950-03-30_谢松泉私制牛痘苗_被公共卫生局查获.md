Title: Xie Songquan's Privately Made Smallpox Vaccine Confiscated by the Public Health Bureau
Author:
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 4

[Beijing News Agency] Xie Songquan, a resident of No. 3 Yuejia Garden, outside Dongbianmen in this city, was found to be privately manufacturing smallpox vaccine and was seized by the Beijing Public Health Bureau. After testing by the bureau, the smallpox vaccine was found to contain too many bacteria and did not meet the regulations. In addition to ordering Xie Songquan to sign a guarantee that he would no longer privately manufacture and sell it, all the manufactured smallpox vaccine was burned.

### 谢松泉私制牛痘苗  被公共卫生局查获

1950-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　谢松泉私制牛痘苗  被公共卫生局查获
    【京市新闻处讯】本市东便门外岳家花园三号住户谢松泉私制牛痘苗，已经京市公共卫生局查获。此牛痘苗经该局化验结果，因杂菌太多，不合规定，现除令谢松泉具结保证以后不再私自制售外，并将已制出的牛痘苗全部烧毁。
