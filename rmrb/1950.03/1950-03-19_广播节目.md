　　Radio Program
    [Our Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (the 19th) at 17:30, Workers' Time: "How to support and implement the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council's decision on unifying national financial and economic work." At 18:30, Natural Science Lecture: "Introduction to Academician Vavilov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences." At 18:00, First Literary and Art Program: Qinshu "Don't Be Superstitious." At 21:00, Second Literary and Art Program: Songs and instrumental music. At 20:30, Weekly Literary and Art Program: The North China Military Region Art Troupe broadcasts the third act of the drama "Growing Up in Battle." At 18:45, New Songs.
    [Another News] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 12:30, Workers' Program: Workers' Dynamics: "The situation of the Fengtai Locomotive Depot, the 70th Ordnance Factory, the Water Company, and the Jixi Coal Mining Area in Northeast China running workers' spare-time schools." At 13:00, Youth Program: Broadcasting Soviet songs. At 13:30, Local Variety Music: (1) Liaoning Drum: "Explosion Hero Dong Cunrui", (2) Local Ballad: "Liang Shiying Sacrifices Himself to Kill the Enemy".

### 广播节目

1950-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（十九日）十七点三十分职工时间：“怎样拥护和执行中央人民政府政务院关于统一国家财政经济工作的决定”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：“介绍苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫”。十八点第一次文艺节目：琴书“别迷信”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：歌曲和器乐曲。八点三十分星期文艺节目：华北军区文工团演播话剧“战斗里成长”第三幕。十八点四十五分新歌曲。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人节目：工人动态：“丰台机务段、七十兵工厂、自来水公司和东北鸡西煤矿区举办工人业余学校的情形。”十三点青年节目：播送苏联歌曲。十三点三十分地方杂曲：（一）辽宁大鼓：“爆炸英雄董存瑞”，（二）地方落子：“梁世英舍身杀敌”。
