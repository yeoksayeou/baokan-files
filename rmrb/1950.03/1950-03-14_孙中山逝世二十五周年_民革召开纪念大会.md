Title: Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Death: KMT Revolutionary Committee Holds Memorial Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-03-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 13th.

　　Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Death:
    KMT Revolutionary Committee Holds Memorial Meeting
    The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a memorial meeting in Beijing yesterday to commemorate the 25th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's death. Over a hundred people attended, including Li Jishen, Cai Tingkai, Chen Mingshu, Jiang Guangnai, Zhu Yunshan, Xu Baoju, Li Shizhang, Shao Lizi, Li Xijiu, Liu Yazi, Chen Shaoxian, Mei Gongbin, and Wang Kunlun. The meeting was chaired by Chairman Li Jishen, who said: "Today we are holding a meeting in Beijing to commemorate the 25th anniversary of Mr. Sun's death. Our emotions are doubly moved. This great man who advocated the Chinese national democratic revolution, after forty years of struggle, although he did not live to see the success of the great revolutionary cause, the great people's liberation cause has finally achieved basic victory under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party." At the meeting, Li Jishen encouraged the members of the committee to "accept Mr. Sun's firm will and fearless spirit towards revolution, and under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, work together with all democratic parties and the people of the country to build an independent, democratic, peaceful, unified, and prosperous new China." Subsequently, Standing Committee member Shao Lizi and Central Committee member Li Xijiu spoke, elaborating in detail on Mr. Sun's revolutionary spirit and the three major policies of alliance with Russia, cooperation with the Communist Party, and assistance to farmers and workers. Finally, Standing Committee member Chen Shaoxian and Wang Kunlun gave reports at the meeting on the conclusion of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, and on land reform, respectively.

### 孙中山逝世二十五周年  民革召开纪念大会

1950-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　孙中山逝世二十五周年
    民革召开纪念大会
    【新华社北京十三日电】中国国民党革命委员会昨日在京召开孙中山先生逝世廿五周年的纪念大会。到李济深、蔡廷锴、陈铭枢、蒋光鼐、朱蕴山、许宝驹、李世璋、邵力子、李锡九、柳亚子、陈劭先、梅龚彬、王昆仑等百余人。会议由李济深主席，他说：“今天我们在北京开会纪念孙先生逝世的二十五周年，情绪加倍感动，这一倡导中国民族民主革命的伟人，经过四十年的奋斗，虽未及亲眼看到革命大业的成功，但是伟大的人民解放事业，终在毛主席和中国共产党的领导下得到基本的胜利。”李济深在会上勖勉该会会员“接受孙先生对革命的坚决意志和大无畏精神，在毛主席和中国共产党的领导下，与全国各民主党派和人民共同努力，以建设独立、民主、和平、统一和富强的新中国”。继由该会常委邵力子、中委李锡九讲话，对于孙先生的革命精神及联俄、联共、扶助农工三大政策，阐述甚详。最后该会常委陈劭先和王昆仑在会上分别作了关于中苏友好同盟互助条约的缔结经过和土地改革的报告。
