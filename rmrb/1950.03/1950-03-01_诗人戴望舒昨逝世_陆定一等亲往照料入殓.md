Title: Poet Dai Wangshu Passed Away Yesterday; Lu Dingyi and Others Personally Took Care of the Funeral Arrangements

Author:

Date: 1950-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 28th: Poet Dai Wangshu passed away at 1:00 PM today due to ineffective treatment of bronchitis. Dai had been hospitalized several times since last autumn for asthma treatment at Peking Union Medical College Hospital and other hospitals, but to no avail. The capital's cultural circles were deeply saddened by Dai's death. During the funeral arrangements, Lu Dingyi, Shen Yanbing, Hu Qiaomu, Zhou Yang, Ai Qing, Qiao Guanhua, Liu Zunqi, and others personally attended to the arrangements. Dai came to the liberated areas in the spring of 1949 and participated in the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers. Later, he worked as a researcher in the Third Department of North China University. After the establishment of the Central People's Government, he was transferred to the International News Bureau of the News Administration to engage in French translation work. Dai was 45 years old this year and a native of Hangzhou, Zhejiang.

### 诗人戴望舒昨逝世  陆定一等亲往照料入殓

1950-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　诗人戴望舒昨逝世
    陆定一等亲往照料入殓
    【新华社北京二十八日电】诗人戴望舒因患气管炎割治无效，于今日下午一时逝世。戴氏自去年秋季以来，因患哮喘曾数次入协和等医院治疗，终未见效。戴氏逝世后，首都文化界闻耗甚为悲悼。入殓时，陆定一、沈雁冰、胡乔木、周扬、艾青、乔冠华、刘尊棋等均亲往照料。戴氏于一九四九年春来解放区，参加全国文艺界代表大会，后在华北大学第三部担任研究工作，中央人民政府成立后，调新闻总署国际新闻局从事法文编译工作。戴氏今年四十五岁，浙江杭州人。
