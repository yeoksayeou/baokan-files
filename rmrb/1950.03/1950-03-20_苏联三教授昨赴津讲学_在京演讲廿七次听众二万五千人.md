Title: Three Soviet Professors Left for Tianjin Yesterday to Give Lectures; Gave 27 Lectures in Beijing, Audience of 25,000
Author:
Date: 1950-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: The three Soviet professors who were invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to give lectures in our country completed their lecturing schedule in Beijing today. This afternoon, they left Beijing by special train for Tianjin to give lectures. From the 10th to the 19th of this month, the three professors gave a total of 27 academic lectures in Beijing, with an audience of over 25,300 people; they also participated in 5 symposiums. Professors, scholars, experts, and academic organizations from Tianjin, Baoding, Taiyuan, Zhangjiakou, and other places also sent representatives to Beijing to attend the lectures. During this period, scholars and experts from the capital visited the three professors many times to exchange opinions on academic issues. Professor Nurzhin visited the Institute of Botany and Zoology of the Academy of Sciences, the Agricultural University, Peking University, Tsinghua University, and other places, and also visited the peasant greenhouses in Zhongding Village and Huangtugang in the southern suburbs. Professor Kiselyov visited the History Museum, the Cultural Relics Bureau, and the "Peking Man" excavation site in Zhoukoudian. The three professors were enthusiastic and active in their lectures, and Master Makarova gave almost two lectures every day. Academic circles in the capital deeply admired the professors' profound knowledge and sincere lectures.

### 苏联三教授昨赴津讲学  在京演讲廿七次听众二万五千人

1950-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联三教授昨赴津讲学
    在京演讲廿七次听众二万五千人
    【新华社十九日讯】应中苏友好协会总会邀请来我国讲学的苏联三位教授，在京讲学日程今日完毕，今日下午，已乘专车离京赴津讲学。自本月十日起至十九日间，三教授在京共作学术讲演二十七次，听众二万五千三百余人；另参加座谈会共五次。天津、保定、太原、张家口等地的教授、学者、专家及学术团体，也都派代表来京听讲。在此期间，我首都学者、专家曾多次访问三位教授，交换学术问题上的意见。努日金教授曾赴科学院动植物研究室、农业大学、北京大学、清华大学等处参观，并参观了南郊中顶村和黄土岗农民温室园艺。吉谢列夫教授曾赴历史博物馆、文物局、“北京人”发掘地——周口店等处参观。三位教授讲学热情积极，马卡洛娃硕士几乎每日讲学两次。首都学术界人士对三教授学识的精邃和讲学的真挚都深致钦佩。
