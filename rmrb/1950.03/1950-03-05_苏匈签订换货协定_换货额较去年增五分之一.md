Title: Sino-Hungarian Sign Trade Agreement; Trade Volume Increases by One-Fifth Compared to Last Year
Author:
Date: 1950-03-05
Page: 4

　　Sino-Hungarian Sign Trade Agreement
    Trade Volume Increases by One-Fifth Compared to Last Year
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 4th] TASS, Moscow: The Soviet Union and Hungary signed a trade agreement in Moscow on the 3rd. The total trade volume between the two countries in 1950 will exceed that of 1949 by more than 20%. The Soviet Union will supply Hungary with cotton, iron ore, coking coal, hardware, industrial equipment, agricultural machinery, and timber. Hungary will supply the Soviet Union with industrial equipment, locomotives, train carriages, barges, fabrics, and other goods. The agreement was signed on behalf of the two governments by Menshikov, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, and Ronai, Minister of Foreign Trade of Hungary.

### 苏匈签订换货协定  换货额较去年增五分之一

1950-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏匈签订换货协定
    换货额较去年增五分之一
    【新华社北京四日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联和匈牙利于三日在莫斯科签订一九五○年换货协定。一九五○年两国换货总量将超过一九四九年的百分之二十以上。苏联将供给匈牙利以棉、铁矿苗、焦煤、五金、工业设备、农业机器、木材。匈牙利将供给苏联工业设备、机车、火车厢、驳船、织物和其他货物。该协定由苏联对外贸易部部长孟希科夫、匈牙利对外贸易部部长罗奈伊代表两国政府签字。
