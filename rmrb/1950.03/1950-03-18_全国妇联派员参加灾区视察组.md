Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends Personnel to Participate in Disaster Area Inspection Group
Author:
Date: 1950-03-18
Page: 2

[Reported by this newspaper] The All-China Democratic Women's Federation, in response to the call of the Central Relief Committee, has dispatched Tao Jing, Deputy Director of the Secretariat, and Zhang Yuying, Deputy Minister of the Production Department, to participate in the Federation's disaster area inspection group to inspect the disaster areas in Shandong and Northern Jiangsu, in order to deeply understand the disaster situation and the status of relief work among the masses. In addition to cooperating with the central tasks of the inspection group, Comrades Tao and Zhang will also conduct in-depth understanding of the situation of local democratic women's federations mobilizing women to participate in production for disaster relief, and report back to the All-China Women's Federation.

### 全国妇联派员参加灾区视察组

1950-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国妇联派员参加灾区视察组
    【本报讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会为响应中央救灾委员会的号召，特派遣秘书处副处长陶静及生产部副部长张育英参加该会的灾区视察组，赴山东及苏北灾区视察，以便深入群众，进一步了解灾情及救灾工作状况。陶、张两同志除配合视察组的中心任务进行工作外，并将深入了解各地民主妇联发动妇女参加生产救灾的情况，汇报全国妇联。
