Title: Changsha Business Community Delegation Arrives in Beijing, Visits Factories for Several Days
Author:
Date: 1950-03-10
Page: 4

[Report from this newspaper] The sixteen-member Northward Visiting Group organized by the Preparatory Committee of the Changsha Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce of Hunan Province arrived in Beijing on the 5th of this month. Starting from the 8th, they began to visit larger-scale factories in the city. They are scheduled to stay in Beijing for more than ten days before going to the Northeast for a visit. The delegation is composed of people from the Changsha business community, including industries such as papermaking, matches, cigarettes, flour, and silk fabric dyeing and weaving. The purpose of this trip is to understand the industrial and commercial situation in the old liberated areas, so as to provide direction for the future development of industry and commerce in Changsha.

### 长沙工商界参观团抵京连日参观工厂

1950-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　长沙工商界参观团抵京连日参观工厂
    【本报讯】湖南长沙市工商联合会筹备会组织北上参观团一行十六人于本月五日抵京。八日起开始参观本市较大规模之工厂。预定在京停留十余日后转赴东北参观。该团为长沙工商界人士组成，包括造纸、火柴、纸烟、面粉、绸布染织等行业。此行目的在了解老解放区工商业情况，以作长沙工商业今后发展的方向。
