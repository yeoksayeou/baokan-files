　　Tongtang Railway

    Question: The January 25th report "National Railway Engineering Conference Determines This Year's Construction Plan" mentioned "Tongtang Line (Datong, Northern Shanxi to Tanggu, Eastern Tianjin)." Which places will this railway pass through? (Wang Ruilong, Second Supplementary Training Division)
    Answer: (1) The planned Tongtang Line for this year refers to the Nanfengtai to Shacheng section.
    (2) The station names for this section of the route are as follows: Nanfengtai, Weimenkou, Sanjiadian, Xiehejian, Qingshuijian, Yanchi, Zhuwo, Yanhecheng, Youzhou, Jiuzhuangkou, Shuiguan, Jiuying, Shacheng.

### 同塘铁路
王瑞龙
1950-03-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　同塘铁路
    问：一月二十五日报载“全国铁路工程会议确定今年修建计划”的消息中，有“同塘线（晋北大同至津东塘沽）”。这条铁路都要经过那些地方？（补训第二师王瑞龙）
    答：（一）今年计划中的同塘线。是指南丰台至沙城一段。
    （二）这一段路线的车站名称如下：南丰台、卫门口、三家店、斜河涧、清水涧、雁翅、朱窝、沿河城、幽州、旧庄窠、水关、九营、沙城。
