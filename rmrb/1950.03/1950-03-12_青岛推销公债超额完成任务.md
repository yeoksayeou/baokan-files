Title: Qingdao Exceeds Quota in Public Bond Sales

Author:

Date: 1950-03-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, March 11th: Qingdao has exceeded its quota of selling 1.52 million public bonds. As of the 10th, subscriptions from all sectors have exceeded 1.6 million, surpassing the original allocation by more than 80,000.

### 青岛推销公债超额完成任务

1950-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　青岛推销公债超额完成任务
    【新华社青岛十一日电】青岛市已超额完成推销一百五十二万份公债的任务。截至十日止，各界认购数已逾一百六十万份，超过原配销额八万份以上。
