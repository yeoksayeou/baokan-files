Title: Beijing Post Office Revises International Postage Rates
Author:
Date: 1950-03-05
Page: 4

　　Beijing Post Office Revises International Postage Rates
    [Beijing News] The Beijing Postal Administration has revised the international mail rates effective today (5th) as follows: (1) Letters: 3,100 yuan for the initial 20 grams, and 1,800 yuan for each additional 20 grams. (2) Postcards: 1,800 yuan for a single card, and 3,600 yuan for a double card. (3) Trade contracts, printed matter, samples: 1,200 yuan for the initial 50 grams, and 600 yuan for each additional 50 grams; the starting amount for trade contracts is 3,100 yuan. (4) Braille documents: 300 yuan per kilogram. (5) Small parcels (only accepted by Shanghai, Tianjin, and Guangzhou post offices): 1,200 yuan per 50 grams, with a starting amount of 6,100 yuan. Small parcel delivery fee is 5,100 yuan per item, and small parcel customs clearance fee is 5,100 yuan per item. (6) Registration fee: 5,100 yuan per item. (7) Priority mail fee: 7,600 yuan per item. (8) Return receipt: 3,800 yuan per item. (9) Inquiry fee: 5,100 yuan per item. (10) Withdrawal and change of address: 5,100 yuan per item.
    [Additional News] International airmail rates have also been adjusted from today: 7,600 yuan per 10 grams for mail to Asian countries, and 12,700 yuan per 10 grams for mail to other continents. The Soviet Union will be charged according to the rates for other continents.

### 北京邮局改订国际邮资

1950-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京邮局改订国际邮资
    【北京讯】北京邮政管理局自今（五）日起改订国际邮件资费如下：（一）信函：起重二十公分三千一百元，续重每二十公分一千八百元。（二）明信片：单一千八百元，双三千六百元。（三）贸易契，印刷物，货样：起重五十公分一千二百元，续重每五十公分六百元，贸易契起算数三千一百元。（四）瞽者文件：每重一公斤三百元。（五）小包邮件（仅上海、天津、广州邮局收寄），每重五十公分一千二百元，起算数六千一百元。小包投递费每件五千一百元，小包验关费每件五千一百元。（六）挂号费每件五千一百元。（七）平快费每件七千六百元。（八）回执每件三千八百元。（九）查询件五千一百元。（十）撤回及更改地址每件五千一百元。
    【又讯】国际航空邮件资费亦自今日起调整为：寄往亚洲各国每十公分人民币七千六百元，其他各洲每十公分人民币一万二千七百元，苏联全境按其他各洲收费。
