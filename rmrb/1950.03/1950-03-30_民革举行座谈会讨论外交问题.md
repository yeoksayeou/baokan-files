Title: The RCCK Holds a Symposium to Discuss Foreign Affairs Issues
Author:
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 1

 The RCCK Holds a Symposium
　　Discussing Foreign Affairs Issues
    [This Newspaper] The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (RCCK) held a symposium on foreign affairs issues at its auditorium at 2:00 PM on the 28th. Attendees included Liu Yazi, Shao Lizi, Chen Mingshu, Chen Shaoxian, Mei Gongbin, Wang Kunlun, Wu Xiufeng, Fu Xuewen, and 25 others, for a total of 33 attendees. Shao Lizi gave a report on "The Current Diplomatic Situation," and Wu Xiufeng reported on "The Recent Political Situation in Southeast Asia." The meeting lasted for three hours. Subsequently, participants raised questions, which were discussed and answered, before the meeting adjourned. The "RCCK" plans to regularly hold such symposia to enhance the content of internal education and learning.

### 民革举行座谈会讨论外交问题

1950-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　民革举行座谈会
    讨论外交问题
    【本报讯】中国国民党革命委员会于二十八日下午二时在该会礼堂举行外交问题座谈会，出席柳亚子、邵力子、陈铭枢、陈劭先、梅龚彬、王昆仑、吴秀峰、傅学文等三十三人，由邵力子报告“目前的外交形势”，吴秀峰报告“最近的东南亚政治情势”，经三小时始毕。继分别由参加人员提出问题，经过讨论解答以后，始行散会。“民革”预定以后要经常不断的举行这样的座谈会，以加强会内教育学习的内容。
