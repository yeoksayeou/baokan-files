Title: Council of Ministers Decides to Increase Sheep Breeding
Author:
Date: 1950-03-29
Page: 4

　　Polish Council of Ministers Decides
    Increase Sheep Breeding
    【Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 28th】 Polish News Agency, Warsaw: The Economic Committee of the Polish Council of Ministers recently passed a resolution to increase sheep breeding and improve the quality and quantity of wool on farms and state farms. These state farms will be used as a base for developing large-scale livestock breeding and as a source of [fine] Merino wool. In order to implement the breeding plan, sheep breeds will be imported from various people's democracies and other countries. During the implementation of the Six-Year Plan, the number of sheep on state farms will increase to 400,000.

### 波部长会议决定  增加绵羊饲养

1950-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　波部长会议决定
    增加绵羊饲养
    【新华社二十八日讯】波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰部长会议经济委员会最近通过决定，增加绵羊饲养并改良农场和国家农场中羊毛的质量和数量。这些国家农场将用作发展大规模牲畜饲养的基地以及螺角羊羊毛的来源。为了实施饲养计划，将从各人民民主国家以及其他国家中输入羊种。在实施六年计划期间，国家农场中的绵羊将增至四十万头。
