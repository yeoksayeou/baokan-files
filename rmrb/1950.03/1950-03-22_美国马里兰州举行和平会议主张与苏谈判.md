Title: Peace Conference Held in Maryland, USA Advocates Negotiations with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1950-03-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, 20th (TASS dispatch): The Worker's Daily reports: A peace conference held in Maryland, USA, passed a resolution calling for an immediate ban on atomic weapons and the immediate commencement of direct talks between the United States and the Soviet Union to resolve outstanding issues. Dr. Morrison, a well-known physicist from Cornell University and a key speaker at the conference, declared that the American people must force their political leaders to "seek a path of agreement rather than division with the Soviet Union."

### 美国马里兰州举行和平会议主张与苏谈判

1950-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国马里兰州举行和平会议主张与苏谈判
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社纽约二十日电引工人日报载：在马里兰州举行的和平会议，通过决议，呼吁立即禁止原子武器和美苏立即开始直接会谈，以解决未决的问题。会上主要发言人、康奈尔大学著名的物理学家摩里逊博士宣称，美国人民必须强制他们的政治领袖“寻求与苏联协议而非分歧的道路”。
