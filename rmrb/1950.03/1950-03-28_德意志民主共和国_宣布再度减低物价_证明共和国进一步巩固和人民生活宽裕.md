Title: German Democratic Republic Announces Further Price Reductions, Proving the Further Consolidation of the Republic and the Prosperity of the People's Lives
Author:
Date: 1950-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin: TASS Berlin dispatch: The Director of the Trade Bureau of the German Democratic Republic, Bend, announced at a press conference on the 25th that the prices of manufactured goods and food in the German Democratic Republic and Berlin stores would be greatly reduced again. The average price reduction is about 30 percent.

The price of bread and other foods has been reduced by 20 to 40 percent, meat and meat products by 25 percent, and fats and eggs by 40 percent. The prices of boots, shoes, textiles and other manufactured goods have also been significantly reduced.

This is the fourth price reduction since the start of commercial trade in November 1948. The new price reduction is clear proof of the further consolidation of the German Democratic Republic, the gradual prosperity of the lives of the people of the Republic, and the increase in the purchasing power of the Deutsche Emission Bank Mark.

### 德意志民主共和国  宣布再度减低物价  证明共和国进一步巩固和人民生活宽裕

1950-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　德意志民主共和国
    宣布再度减低物价
    证明共和国进一步巩固和人民生活宽裕
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：德意志民主共和国贸易局主任本德，二十五日在记者招待会上宣布德意志民主共和国及柏林商店内制成品及食物价格的再度大大减低。这次的减价平均约为百分之三十。
    面包类食物减少百分之二十到四十，肉类及肉类产品百分之二十五，脂肪及蛋类百分之四十。靴鞋、纺织品及其他制成品的价格亦有相当大的减低。
    自从商业贸易开始，即自一九四八年十一月以来，这已是第四次减低物价。新的减价是德意志民主共和国更进一步巩固的明证，是共和国人民生活逐渐宽裕和德意志发行银行马克的购买力增大的明证。
