Title: Shanghai Trade Treasury Established; Deposits from East China Specialized Companies Have All Been Deposited

Author:
Date: 1950-03-12
Page: 2

　　Shanghai Trade Treasury Established
    Deposits from East China Specialized Companies Have All Been Deposited
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 11th] The nine specialized companies under the Trade Department of the East China Financial and Economic Committee and the Shanghai Branch of the People's Bank of China officially established the Shanghai Trade Treasury on the 7th, and began to implement the joint order of the Central Trade Department and the People's Bank to implement the Trade Treasury contract. Deposits from all specialized companies under the East China Trade Department have been deposited in the treasury since the 7th.

### 上海贸易金库成立  华东专业公司存款皆已入库

1950-03-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　上海贸易金库成立
    华东专业公司存款皆已入库
    【新华社上海十一日电】华东区财政经济委员会贸易部所属九个专业公司与中国人民银行上海分行已于七日正式成立上海市贸易金库，开始执行中央贸易部与人民银行实施贸易金库合同的联合命令。华东贸易部所属各专业公司存款，自七日起皆已入库。
