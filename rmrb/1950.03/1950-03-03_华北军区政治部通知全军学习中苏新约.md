Author:
Date: 1950-03-03
Page: 3

　　North China Military Region Political Department Issues Notice to the Entire Army to Study the New Sino-Soviet Treaty
[This Newspaper News] The Political Department of the North China Military Region of the People's Liberation Army issued a notice to its subordinate units on February 24 to conduct a study of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. After briefly describing the significant meaning of the Sino-Soviet Treaty, the notice pointed out: To strengthen the internationalist education of our army, it is decided to conduct a study of the Sino-Soviet Treaty from February 27 to March 5. For study content, cadres should primarily use the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, the Sino-Soviet Agreement on the China Changchun Railway, Lushunkou and Dalian, and the Sino-Soviet Agreement on Loans to China, combined with the Xinhua News Agency editorial "A New Era of Sino-Soviet Friendly Cooperation" and the Pravda editorial "Long Live Soviet-Chinese Friendship" (published in this newspaper on the 17th), and refer to documents related to Sino-Soviet relations published in the People's Daily and the North China People's Liberation Army Daily. Soldiers should use the speech materials on the New Sino-Soviet Treaty in the North China People's Liberation Army Daily as the basic content. Regarding the learning method, the notice pointed out that cadres should combine self-study with discussions, and the responsible comrades of the Party committees at all levels should make reports. During the reports, they should link the army's response to the Sino-Soviet Treaty and analyze and criticize various reactions. After the cadres have completed their study, they should give lessons to the soldiers based on the speech materials published in the North China People's Liberation Army Daily. Any difficult questions encountered during the study should be reported to the Political Department of the Military Region at any time for resolution.

### 华北军区政治部通知全军学习中苏新约

1950-03-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　华北军区政治部通知全军学习中苏新约
    【本报讯】人民解放军华北军区政治部于二月二十四日通知所属部队，进行中苏友好同盟互助条约的学习。通知略述中苏条约的重大意义后，指出：为加强我军的国际主义教育，决定自二月二十七日起至三月五日进行中苏条约的学习。学习内容，干部以中苏友好同盟互助条约，中苏关于中国长春铁路、旅顺口及大连的协定，中苏关于贷款给中国的协定，结合新华社社论“中苏友好合作的新时代”与真理报社论“苏中友谊万岁”（载十七日本报）为主要学习材料，并参阅人民日报和华北解放军报登载的有关中苏关系的文件。战士以华北解放军报有关中苏新约的讲话材料为基本内容。学习方法上，通知指出，干部以自学与讨论结合进行，各级党委的负责同志要作报告，报告时，应联系部队对中苏条约的反应，对各种反应要加以分析批判。干部学完后，参照华北解放军报登载的讲话材料给战士上课。学习中的疑难问题，应随时报告军区政治部，以便解决。
