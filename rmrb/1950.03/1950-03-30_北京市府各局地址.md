Title: Addresses of Various Bureaus of the Beijing Municipal Government
Author: Dong Yi
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 6

Addresses of Various Bureaus of the Beijing Municipal Government
    [Question] Where are the various bureaus under the Beijing Municipal Government located? The people mostly do not know. When there is something to discuss, they do not know where to go. Please publish it in the newspaper to avoid people running around in vain and not finding them.
    (Dong Yi)
    [Answer] The addresses of the various bureaus of the Beijing Municipal People's Government are as follows:
     Bureau of Civil Affairs – No. 8, Nanwei Hutong, North Xisi
     Bureau of Finance – No. 1, Cheng'en Temple, Xuannei
    Bureau of Culture and Education – No. 32, Jia, West Huangchenggen
    Bureau of Public Security – No. 16, Public Security Street
    Bureau of Labor – No. 1, Youqi Zuo
    Bureau of Construction – No. 114, North Street, Xidan
    Bureau of Industry – No. 21, Yangrou Hutong
    Bureau of Commerce – North of Xin Dalu Road, behind the Post Office inside Qianmen
    Bureau of Land Administration – No. 11, Qian Gongyongku, Xinjiekou
    Bureau of Public Health – No. 17, Baojia Street
    Bureau of Sanitary Engineering – No. 27, Yangrou Hutong
    Bureau of Taxation – Xiaocaochang, East Huangchenggen, Shatan
    Bureau of Water Management – Nanwanzi, South Heyean
    Suburban Work Committee – No. 36, Gongjian Hutong

### 北京市府各局地址
董毅
1950-03-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　北京市府各局地址
    【问】北京市政府所属各局皆在何处？人民多不知悉。如有何事接洽，均莫知所之。请在报端公布，以免各处白跑找不到。
    （董毅）
    【答】北京市人民政府各局地址如下：
     民政局——西四北南魏胡同八号
     财政局——宣内承恩寺一号
    文教局——西皇城根甲三十二号
    公安局——公安街十六号
    劳动局——油漆作一号
    建设局——西单北大街一一四号
    工业局——羊肉胡同二十一号
    商业局——前门内邮局后新大陆路北
    地政局——新街口前公用库十一号
    公共卫生局一—鲍家街十七号
    卫生工程局——羊肉胡同二十七号
    税务局——沙滩东黄城根小草厂
    清管局——南河沿南湾子
    郊区工作委员会——恭俭胡同三十六号
