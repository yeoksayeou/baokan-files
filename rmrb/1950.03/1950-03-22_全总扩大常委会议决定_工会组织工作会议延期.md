Title: ACFTU Enlarged Standing Committee Meeting Decides to Postpone Trade Union Organizational Work Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-03-22
Page: 2
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) 22nd Enlarged Standing Committee meeting has decided to postpone the National Trade Union Organizational Work Conference, originally scheduled to be held in Beijing on March 25th, to April 5th. The meeting also stipulated that representatives from various regions should report to Beijing before April 2nd.

### 全总扩大常委会议决定  工会组织工作会议延期

1950-03-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　全总扩大常委会议决定  工会组织工作会议延期
    【新华社讯】原定于三月二十五日在北京召开的全国工会组织工作会议业经中华全国总工会第二十二次常务委员会扩大会议决定延期于四月五日举行。会议并规定各地代表应于四月二日以前到京报到。
