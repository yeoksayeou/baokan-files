Title: Three Soviet Professors Leave Nanjing for Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1950-03-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 27th—Soviet professors, Dr. Nurzhin of Biology, Dr. Giselev of History, and Master Makarova of Economics, who were invited to lecture in our country, have completed their lectures in Nanjing and left for Shanghai by train today. Those who went to the station to see them off included Ke Qingshi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Nanjing Branch, geological expert Yin Zanxun, and more than 40 others. The three professors, Nurzhin, et al., arrived in Nanjing on the evening of the 23rd. In the past few days, they have made extensive contact with the scientific and academic circles in Nanjing, and have given nine special lectures and three symposiums, with an audience of more than 35,000 people. Experts and representatives from Wuhu, Wuxi, Suzhou, and Zhenjiang have also been sent to attend the lectures.

### 苏联三教授离宁赴沪

1950-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联三教授离宁赴沪
    【新华社南京二十七日电】应邀来我国讲学的苏联教授生物学博士努日金、历史学博士吉谢列夫、经济学硕士马卡洛娃，已在宁讲学完毕，于今日乘车离宁赴沪。赴车站欢送者有中苏友好协会南京分会副会长柯庆施、地质学专家尹赞勋等四十余人。努日金等三教授系于二十三日晚抵南京。数日来与南京市科学、学术界作了广泛接触，并先后作专题演讲九次，座谈会三次，听众达三万五千余人；芜湖、无锡、苏州、镇江各地均派有专家代表前来听讲。
