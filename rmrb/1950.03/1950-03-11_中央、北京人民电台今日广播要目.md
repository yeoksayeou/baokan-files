Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-03-11
Page: 4

　　[This newspaper dispatch] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (11th) 17:30 Youth Time: "The Chinese New Democratic Youth League in the Past Year". 18:00 First Literary Program: Guan Dejun, Guan Jinfeng, and Quan Fengchi sing the Lianhualuo "Aunt Li Watching the Fair". 21:00 Second Literary Program: Amateur National Music Research Society broadcasts national music. 18:30 Soviet folk music and songs. 18:45. Broadcasting seven new songs including "Our Leader Mao Zedong" and "Singing the Chinese Communist Party".
　　[Another dispatch] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station 12:30 Joint Worker-Youth Program: Continuing to teach "March 8th Women's Day Song". 16:30 Municipal Voice, the First Hygiene Clinic of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Public Health Bureau broadcasts "BCG Vaccine and Tuberculosis Prevention". 17:00 Children's Program: (1) Current Affairs Talk, (2) Singing. 18:45 Women's Program: Women's Health Knowledge "Several Symptoms of Amenorrhea". 19:00 Di Chaobai's recorded lecture "Soviet Union Reducing Prices and Improving People's Living Standards".

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（十一日）十七点三十分青年时间：“一年来的中国新民主主义青年团”。十八点第一次文艺节目：关德俊、关金凤、全凤池演唱莲花落《李大娘看会》二十一点第二次文艺节目：业余国乐研究社演播国乐。十八点三十分苏联民间音乐和歌曲。十八点四十五分。播送《咱们的领袖毛泽东》、《歌唱中国共产党》等七支新歌曲。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人青年联合节目：继续教唱《三八妇女节歌》。十六点三十分市政之声，北京市人民政府公共卫生局第一卫生所播讲“卡介苗和防痨”。十七点儿童节目：（一）时事讲话，（二）歌咏。十八点四十五分妇女节目：妇女卫生常识“经闭期的几种症候”。十九点狄超白讲演录音“苏联减低物价与提高人民生活水平”。
