Title: Beijing Postal Administration to Accept and Deliver Small Packages on Sundays
Author:
Date: 1950-03-26
Page: 4

　　Beijing Postal Administration
    Accepting and Delivering Small Packages on Sundays
    [This Newspaper's Report] To facilitate the sending and receiving of packages for personnel from government organizations, groups, factories, and schools, the Beijing Postal Administration will, starting today (March 26th), accept, send, and deliver packages every Sunday from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM. However, this service is limited to individual, scattered packages only; large-volume commercial packages will not be handled for the time being. (Social Service Section, Beijing Post Office)

### 京邮政管理局星期收寄小型包裹

1950-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　京邮政管理局
    星期收寄小型包裹
    【本报讯】北京邮政管理局为了便利机关、团体、工厂、学校人员交寄及领取包裹，自今日（三月二十六）起，每逢星期日上午十点至十二点收寄并投递包裹。但仅以个人零星包裹为限，大宗商包暂不办理。（北京邮局社会服务股）
