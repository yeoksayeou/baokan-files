Title: Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Cabled Congratulations on Soviet Army Day; Marshal Stalin Replied with Thanks; Foreign Minister Vyshinsky Replied to Premier Zhou

Author:
Date: 1950-03-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council, sent cables to Marshal Stalin and Foreign Minister Vyshinsky respectively on Soviet Army Day to extend congratulations. Replies have now been received. The texts of the telegrams are as follows:
  (1) Marshal Stalin's Reply to Chairman Mao Zedong: To His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    I express my gratitude for your friendly congratulations on the thirty-second anniversary of the Soviet Army and Navy.
  Stalin
  (2) Marshal Stalin's Reply to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De: To Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:
    Please accept my thanks for your friendly congratulations on the thirty-second anniversary of the Soviet Army and Navy.
  Stalin
  (3) Foreign Minister Vyshinsky's Reply to Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai: To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the Government Administration Council of the People's Republic of China:
    Please accept my sincere thanks for your friendly congratulations on the thirty-second anniversary of the Soviet Army.
        Vyshinsky

### 毛主席朱总司令曾致电庆贺苏军节  斯大林大元帅复电致谢  维辛斯基外长电复周总理

1950-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席朱总司令曾致电庆贺苏军节
    斯大林大元帅复电致谢
    维辛斯基外长电复周总理
    【新华社十五日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席、中国人民解放军朱德总司令、政务院周恩来总理兼外交部部长，曾于苏军节分别致电斯大林大元帅和维辛斯基外长祝贺，现已分别收到复电。各电电文如下：
  （一）斯大林大元帅复毛泽东主席电：中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东阁下：
    当苏联陆军及海军三十二周年纪念时，承致友谊的庆贺，谨此表示谢意。
  斯大林
  （二）斯大林大元帅复朱德总司令电：中国人民解放军朱德总司令：
    当苏联陆军及海军三十二周年纪念时，承致友谊的庆贺，请接受我的谢意。
  斯大林
  （三）维辛斯基外长复周恩来总理兼外交部部长电：中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来阁下：
    当苏军三十二周年纪念时，承致友谊的庆贺，请接受我的真诚的谢意。
        维辛斯基
