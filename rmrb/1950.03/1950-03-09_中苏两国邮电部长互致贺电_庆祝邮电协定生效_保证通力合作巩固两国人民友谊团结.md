Title: China and Soviet Union Post and Telecommunications Ministers Exchange Congratulatory Telegrams Celebrating the Implementation of the Postal and Telecommunications Agreement, Guaranteeing All-Out Cooperation to Consolidate the Friendship and Solidarity of the Two Peoples
Author: 
Date: 1950-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 8th. The postal and telecommunications agreement signed in Moscow on February 7th of this year between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics came into effect on March 1st. Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government of China, and Sersiyev, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Soviet Union, exchanged congratulatory telegrams as follows:

(1) Congratulatory telegram from Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China, to Sersiyev, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Soviet Union:

    To the Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

    Following the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, the communications between our two countries have been further developed from today onwards, which will undoubtedly have a significant impact on the permanent friendship and permanent cooperation between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union.

    On behalf of all the comrades working at the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China, I would like to extend my greetings to you and the comrades in the postal and telecommunications work you lead, and I believe that through our cooperation and efforts, the political, economic, and cultural ties between the two countries can be strengthened, and the friendship and solidarity of the two peoples can be further consolidated.

    I would like to guarantee that the Chinese postal and telecommunications comrades will complete this great task with the greatest effort.

                        Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China

(2) Congratulatory telegram from Sersiyev, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, to Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of China:

    To the Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    I sincerely thank you for the congratulatory telegram you sent me on the day the Sino-Soviet Postal and Telecommunications Agreement came into effect.

    I fully agree with you that these two new agreements will make the friendly cooperation between our two peoples even stronger.

    On behalf of myself and the colleagues of the Soviet Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, I respectfully congratulate you and your colleagues on your success and completion of your tasks.

                               Sersiyev, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Soviet Union

### 中苏两国邮电部长互致贺电  庆祝邮电协定生效  保证通力合作巩固两国人民友谊团结

1950-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏两国邮电部长互致贺电
    庆祝邮电协定生效     
    保证通力合作巩固两国人民友谊团结
    【新华社北京八日电】中华人民共和国与苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟于本年二月七日在莫斯科签订的通邮通电协定，已于本月一日起生效。我中央人民政府邮电部部长朱学范与苏联邮电部部长塞尔西夫，特互致贺电如下：
    （一）中华人民共和国邮电部部长朱学范致苏联邮电部部长塞尔西夫的贺电：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟邮电部部长先生：
    在中苏友好同盟互助条约签订之后，贵我两国间的通信事业，从今天起，也有了进一步的发展，这对于中苏两国人民的永久友好和永久合作，无疑地会有重大的作用。
    我谨代表中华人民共和国邮电部全体工作同志，向您和您所领导的邮电工作同志们致意，并相信经由我们的合作与努力，必可加强两国间政治、经济与文化的联系，而使两国人民的友谊团结，更加巩固起来。
    我愿保证中国邮电工作同志，必以最大的努力，完成这一伟大任务。
                        中华人民共和国邮电部部长朱学范
    （二）苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟邮电部部长塞尔西夫致我国邮电部部长朱学范的贺电：北京中华人民共和国邮电部部长先生：
    我恳切地感谢你在中苏邮电协定实施那天所给我的贺电。
    我完全同意你所说的，这两个新协定将使我们两国人民间的友谊合作更为坚强。
    我以本人名义和苏联邮电部同人的名义敬向你和你的同事们祝贺成功及完成任务。
                               苏联邮电部部长塞尔西夫
