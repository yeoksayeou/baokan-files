Title: Central Relief Committee Inspection Group for Shandong and Northern Jiangsu Disaster Areas Arrives in Heavily Afflicted Areas of Central and Southern Shandong
Author:
Date: 1950-03-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Qufu, 24th. The Central Relief Committee's Shandong and Northern Jiangsu disaster area inspection group, led by Wu Xinyu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, arrived in the most severely affected disaster area of central and southern Shandong on the 23rd. When the inspection group passed through Yanzhou, they listened to a report from Song Youzhun, Deputy Commissioner of the Nishan Special Prefecture of the Central and Southern Shandong heavily afflicted area, on the disaster situation and production relief work in the area. The inspection group has now dispatched seven people to Jibei County (north of Jining, newly established county), which is seriously affected by the disaster, to carry out work.

### 中央救灾委员会山东、苏北灾区视察组抵达鲁中南重灾区

1950-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央救灾委员会山东、苏北灾区视察组抵达鲁中南重灾区
    【新华社曲阜二十四日电】中央人民政府内务部副部长武新宇所率领之中央救灾委员会山东、苏北灾区视察组，已于二十三日到达山东受灾最重的鲁中南区。当视察组途经兖州时，曾听取了鲁中南重灾区尼山专署副专员宋幼准对该地灾情和生产救灾工作的报告。现视察组已派出七人赶赴灾情严重的济北县（济宁以北，新设县）进行工作。
