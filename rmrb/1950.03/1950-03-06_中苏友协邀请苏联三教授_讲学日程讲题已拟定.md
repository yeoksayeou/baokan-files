Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Invites Three Soviet Professors; Lecture Schedule and Topics Finalized
Author:
Date: 1950-03-06
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 5th. At the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the lecture schedule and topics of Dr. Nuzhdin, Professor of Biology from the Soviet Union, Dr. Kiselyov, Professor of History, and Ms. Makarova, Associate Professor of Economics, who have come to China specifically to lecture, have now been finalized. They will lecture for a total of ten days in Beijing from the 10th to the 19th of this month. Dr. Nuzhdin's lecture on the 10th will be entitled "The Struggle Between Materialism and Idealism in Biology," on the 13th, "What is Michurin Biological Science and its Basic Principles," on the 15th, "The Marxist-Leninist Theory of Development and Michurin Biological Science," and on the 17th, "Michurin Science is the Foundation of Modern Biology." Dr. Kiselyov's lectures on the 10th and 13th will be entitled "Soviet Historical Science and History Teaching," and on the 15th and 17th, "Archaeological Research in the Soviet Union." Ms. Makarova's lectures on the 10th, 11th, and 13th will be entitled "Lenin and Stalin's Theory on the Transition from Capitalism to Socialism," and on the 15th, 16th, and 17th, "The Basic Characteristics and Superiority of the Socialist Economic System." After these lectures, there will be discussion sessions based on questions from the audience. After this, they will travel to other cities to lecture.

### 中苏友协邀请苏联三教授  讲学日程讲题已拟定

1950-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协邀请苏联三教授
    讲学日程讲题已拟定
    【新华社北京五日电】应中苏友好协会总会之请专程前来我国讲学的苏联生物学教授努日金博士、历史学教授吉谢列夫博士与经济学副教授马卡洛娃硕士的讲学日程与讲题，现已拟定。自本月十日至十九日在北京共讲十天，努日金十日讲题为《生物学中唯物论与唯心论的斗争》，十三日讲题为《什么是米邱林生物科学及其基本原理》，十五日讲题为《马列主义的发展学说与米邱林生物科学》，十七日讲题为《米邱林学说是现代生物学的基础》，吉谢列夫十日、十三日讲题为《苏联的历史科学与历史教学》，十五日、十七日讲题为《苏联的考古研究》；马卡洛娃十日、十一日、十三日讲题为《列宁、斯大林关于从资本主义到社会主义过渡时期的理论》，十五日、十六日、十七日讲题为《社会主义经济制度的基本特点及其优越性》。这些讲题讲完后，将根据听众所提问题举行座谈会。此后他们即将转往其他城市讲学。
