Title: Second Anniversary of the February Victory: Czechoslovak People Widely Commemorate; February Shock Labor Achieves New Successes
Author:
Date: 1950-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 28th Dispatch from Prague, February 27th by TASS: The Czechoslovak people widely commemorate the second anniversary of the February Victory. The February shock labor carried out by the working people has achieved new successes. The "Tesla" factory in Prague increased labor productivity by 28 percent. The "Zo" factory in Hradec Králové increased labor productivity by 20 percent in February. The "Aero" factory in Prague had already completed 108.6 percent of its February plan by February 25th. Workers in many enterprises in many cities such as Moravska, Ostrava, and Brno have also achieved great successes.

　　The cities and villages of the entire country are filled with a festive atmosphere, with portraits of Stalin and Czechoslovak President Gottwald hanging everywhere. Working people from all over the country gather to commemorate the event, and the color film "New Czechoslovakia" is being screened.

### 二月事件胜利二周年  捷人民广泛纪念  二月突击劳动已获新成就

1950-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　二月事件胜利二周年
    捷人民广泛纪念
    二月突击劳动已获新成就
    【新华社北京二十八日电】塔斯社布拉格二十七日讯：捷克斯洛伐克人民广泛纪念二月事件胜利两周年。劳动人民所进行的二月突击劳动，已获新的成就。布拉格“泰斯拉”工厂提高了劳动生产率百分之二十八，捷拉科维奇的“佐”工厂，劳动生产率在二月提高了百分之二十。布拉格“艾罗”工厂在二月二十五日就已完成了二月份计划的百分之一○八·六。摩拉夫斯卡、俄斯特拉发、布尔诺等许多城市的许多企业的职工，也已获得了巨大的成就。
    全捷的城市和乡村中，洋溢着一片节日气象，到处悬挂着斯大林和捷总统哥特瓦尔德的肖像，各地劳动人民均集会纪念，并上映彩色影片《新捷克斯洛伐克》。
