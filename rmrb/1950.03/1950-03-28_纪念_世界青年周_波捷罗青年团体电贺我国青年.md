Title: Commemorating "World Youth Week": Polish, Czech, and Romanian Youth Organizations Send Greetings to Chinese Youth via Telegram

Author:

Date: 1950-03-28
Page: 3

　　Commemorating "World Youth Week"
    Polish, Czech, and Romanian Youth Organizations Send Greetings to Chinese Youth via Telegram
    [Xinhua News Agency, 27th] The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth received congratulatory telegrams to Chinese youth from the Central Committee of the Polish Youth Union, the Czech Youth Union, and the Central Committee of the Romanian People's Republic Labor Youth Union in commemoration of "World Youth Week." They unanimously celebrated the victory won by the Chinese youth in their heroic struggle, and wished the Chinese youth to successfully complete the construction of a new democratic society under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, and at the same time contribute their strength in the powerful peace camp headed by the Soviet Union, and strive for lasting world peace and people's democracy.

### 纪念“世界青年周”  波捷罗青年团体电贺我国青年

1950-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　纪念“世界青年周”
    波捷罗青年团体电贺我国青年
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合总会接到波兰青年联盟中央委员会、捷克青年联盟和罗马尼亚人民共和国劳动青年联盟中央委员会为纪念“世界青年周”致中国青年的贺电。他们一致庆祝中国青年在英勇斗争中所获得的胜利，并祝中国青年在中国共产党和毛主席领导下胜利完成新民主主义社会的建设事业，同时在以苏联为首的强大和平阵营中贡献出自己的力量，为争取世界持久和平和人民民主而斗争。
