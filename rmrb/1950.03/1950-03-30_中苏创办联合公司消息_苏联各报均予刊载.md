Title: News of the Establishment of Joint Sino-Soviet Companies Published in All Soviet Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 29th: All newspapers published on the 29th the news of the signing of agreements between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China on the establishment of two joint Sino-Soviet stock companies (one an oil company and one a non-ferrous metals company).

### 中苏创办联合公司消息  苏联各报均予刊载

1950-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏创办联合公司消息
    苏联各报均予刊载
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十九日讯：一切报纸均于二十九日刊登苏联与中华人民共和国签订关于创办两个联合的中苏股份公司（一为石油公司，一为有色金属公司）的协定的消息。
