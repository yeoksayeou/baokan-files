Title: Viet Cong Liberation Army Operations in Vietnam for One Month: Captures Five French Military Strongholds
Author:
Date: 1950-03-28
Page: 4

　　Viet Cong Liberation Army Operations in Vietnam for One Month
    Captures Five French Military Strongholds
    [Xinhua News Agency, Viet Cong, 27th] Vietnam News Agency, Viet Cong, 26th: In one month of operations ending February 15, the Viet Cong Liberation Army in the central Vietnamese provinces of Quang Binh and Thua Thien captured five French military strongholds, killing or wounding 650 French soldiers, and seizing 220 rifles, 4 machine guns, 3 mortars, 160 boxes of ammunition, and 2 radio stations.

### 越解放军越中作战一个月  攻克法军五据点

1950-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　越解放军越中作战一个月
    攻克法军五据点
    【新华社二十七日讯】越南通讯社中越二十六日讯：在截至二月十五日为止的一个月作战中，越南解放军在越南中部广平、承天地区攻克五处法军据点，毙伤法军六百五十名，缴获步枪二百二十枝，机枪四挺，迫击炮三门，弹药一百六十箱及电台两部。
