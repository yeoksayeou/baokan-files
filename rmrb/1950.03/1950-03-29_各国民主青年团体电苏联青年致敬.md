Title: Democratic Youth Organizations of Various Countries Send Respects to Soviet Youth
Author:
Date: 1950-03-29
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 28th: During World Youth Week, democratic youth organizations from various countries have sent telegrams to Soviet youth expressing their respect. According to TASS Moscow on the 24th: The All-Union Leninist Young Communist League and the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee have received telegrams from the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Youth League, the Central Committee of the Polish Youth League, and progressive youth organizations from other countries. In addition to warmly saluting the Soviet youth, the telegrams also wished them new achievements in building a communist society and in the struggle of democratic youth around the world to defend world peace.

### 各国民主青年团体电苏联青年致敬

1950-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　各国民主青年团体电苏联青年致敬
    【新华社二十八日讯】在世界青年周中，各国民主青年团体纷电苏联青年表示敬意。据塔斯社莫斯科二十四日讯：苏联列宁共产主义青年团及苏联青年反法西斯委员会，已收到中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟中央委员会、波兰青年联盟中央委员会及其他国家进步青年团体的电文，电文中除向苏联青年热烈致敬外，并祝他们在建设共产主义社会中，在全世界民主青年保卫世界和平的斗争中，获得新的成就。
