Title: Japanese Progressive Groups to Launch Movement to Expedite Treaty Conclusion

Author:
Date: 1950-03-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd, TASS dispatch of the 21st, citing the Japanese Akahata newspaper: The Japan Peace Protection Association and the League for the Defense of Democracy held a joint meeting in Tokyo. The meeting resolved to launch a movement from April 1st to June 30th to expedite the conclusion of a peace treaty with Japan in order to safeguard peace and national independence.

### 日进步团体将发起加速缔结和约运动

1950-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　日进步团体将发起加速缔结和约运动
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社二十一日电引日本赤旗报消息：日本的保卫和平协会和保卫民主同盟在东京举行了联席会议。会上决议自四月一日起至六月三十日止，发动加速缔结对日和约以保卫和平与国家独立的运动。
