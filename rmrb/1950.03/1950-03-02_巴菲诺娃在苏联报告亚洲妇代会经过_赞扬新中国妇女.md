Title: Bafanova Reports on the Asian Women's Conference in the Soviet Union, Praises the Women of New China
Author:
Date: 1950-03-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st: The Presidium of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee held a meeting on February 27th to hear a report by the committee's Vice-Chairman, Bafanova, on the Soviet delegation's participation in the Asian Women's Representatives Conference held in Beijing last year. Bafanova described in detail the work of the conference in Beijing, attended by representatives and guests from twenty-three countries (including representatives from fourteen Asian countries). Bafanova said that the speeches of the delegates at the Beijing conference showed that the democratic women of Asia are united and, together with the people of their countries, are committed to the struggle for peace and democracy, and for culture and progress. In her report, Bafanova emphasized that the speeches of the representatives of New China, which had defeated the reactionary forces and occupied one of the leading positions in the camp of peace supporters headed by the Soviet Union, won a very warm welcome from the audience. Bafanova described with heartfelt gratitude the warm reception given to the Soviet delegation by the Chinese women and other participants.

### 巴菲诺娃在苏联报告亚洲妇代会经过  赞扬新中国妇女

1950-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴菲诺娃在苏联报告亚洲妇代会经过
    赞扬新中国妇女
    【新华社北京一日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席团于二月二十七日举行会议，听取该委员会副主席巴菲诺娃关于苏联代表团去年出席在北京举行的亚洲妇女代表会议的报告。巴菲诺娃详细描述有二十三个国家的代表和来宾（包括亚洲十四个国家的代表）出席的这次会议的工作。巴菲诺娃说，代表们在北京会议上的发言，表明了亚洲的民主妇女团结一致，和她们国家的人民一道，致力于争取和平与民主、争取文化与进步的斗争。巴菲诺娃在其报告中强调称，战胜了反动力量、而且在以苏联为首的和平拥护者阵营里占领导地位之一的新中国的代表们的演讲，博得了听众们非常热烈的欢迎。巴菲诺娃怀着衷心感激之情叙述苏联代表团受到中国妇女及其他与会者热烈招待的情形。
