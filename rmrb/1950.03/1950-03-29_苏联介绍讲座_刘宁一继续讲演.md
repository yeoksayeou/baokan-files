Title: Lecture on the Soviet Union: Liu Ningyi Continues Lecture
Author:
Date: 1950-03-29
Page: 4

　　Lecture on the Soviet Union
    Liu Ningyi Continues Lecture
    [Beijing Municipal News Agency] The fifth lecture in the series of lectures on the Soviet Union organized by the Beijing Municipal Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is scheduled for 6:00 PM on April 2nd (this Sunday) at the Beijing Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, No. 19 Nanheyan. Liu Ningyi will continue to speak on "Soviet Trade Unions." In his previous lecture, Liu Ningyi resolved many ideological issues regarding the understanding of trade unions. In this lecture, he will give a specific introduction. Responsible persons of the association's various organizations and propaganda departments can attend the lecture at that time with their long-term or temporary lecture passes.

### 苏联介绍讲座  刘宁一继续讲演
京市新闻处
1950-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联介绍讲座
    刘宁一继续讲演
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市中苏友好协会举办的苏联介绍讲座第五讲，定于四月二日（本星期日）下午六时在南河沿十九号北京苏联对外文化协会举行，仍请刘宁一讲“苏联的职工会”。刘宁一在上次讲演中，解决了许多关于对职工会认识上的思想问题，在这一次讲演中，则要作具体的介绍。该会各级组织、宣传部门负责人届时均可持长期或临时听讲证前往听讲。
