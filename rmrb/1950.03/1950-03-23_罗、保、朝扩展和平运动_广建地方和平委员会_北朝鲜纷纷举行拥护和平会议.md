Title: Luo, Bao, and Chao Expand Peace Movement; Widely Establish Local Peace Committees; North Korea Holds Meetings in Succession to Support Peace

Author: 
Date: 1950-03-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: Romania, Bulgaria and Korea and other countries are extensively establishing local peace committees. According to a comprehensive TASS report: The Romanian National Peace Committee announced at a meeting held in mid-March that among the 58 districts in Romania, 45 districts had established 13,545 local peace committees, with 182,800 members, including 45,000 workers, 85,000 farmers, agricultural workers, and a large number of intellectuals.

The Bulgarian people, under the call of the National Peace Committee, are establishing peace committees in various towns, villages, and enterprises. In the Kolarovgrad district alone, 32 villages and 36 factories have established peace committees. At the meetings held in various enterprises, the working people expressed their firm will to defend peace and oppose the instigators of new wars.

According to a Pyongyang report: In the three days from March 15th to 17th, Pyongyang City, South Pyongan Province, North Pyongan Province, Kangwon Province, and Hwanghae Province in North Korea held conferences of all circles in support of world peace and established local peace committees. Leaders from workers, farmers, youth, women, and cultural and educational circles spoke enthusiastically at the meeting, unanimously emphasizing that the Korean people should closely link the struggle for the unification and independence of their motherland with the cause of defending world peace.

### 罗、保、朝扩展和平运动  广建地方和平委员会  北朝鲜纷纷举行拥护和平会议

1950-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗、保、朝扩展和平运动
    广建地方和平委员会
    北朝鲜纷纷举行拥护和平会议
    【新华社二十二日讯】罗马尼亚、保加利亚及朝鲜等国正在广泛建立地方和平委员会。综合塔斯社讯：罗马尼亚全国和平委员会在三月中旬举行的会议上宣布：在罗马尼亚的五十八个区中，有四十五个区成立了一万三千五百四十五个地方和平委员会，拥有会员十八万二千八百人，其中包括四万五千名工人，八万五千名农民，农业工人以及大批知识分子。
    保加利亚人民在全国和平委员会的号召下，正在各城镇、乡村与企业中建立和平委员会，仅在科拉罗夫格勒一区内，就有三十二个村庄和三十六个工厂成立了和平委员会。在各企业中举行的会议上，劳动人民表示他们捍卫和平及反对新战争挑拨者的坚决意志。
    另据平壤消息：在三月十五日到十七日的三天中，北朝鲜的平壤市、平安南道、平安北道、江原道和黄海道纷纷举行了各界拥护世界和平会议，成立了地方和平委员会。工人、农民、青年、妇女及文化教育各界领袖在大会上热烈发言，一致强调朝鲜人民应把争取祖国统一独立的斗争与保卫世界和平的事业，密切地联系起来。
