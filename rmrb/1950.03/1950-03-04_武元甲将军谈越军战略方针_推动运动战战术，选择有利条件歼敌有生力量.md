Title: General Vo Nguyen Giap Discusses the Vietnamese Army's Strategic Policy: Promoting Mobile Warfare Tactics, Choosing Favorable Conditions to Annihilate Enemy Forces

Author: 
Date: 1950-03-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam dispatch: General Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense of Vietnam and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Liberation Army, expounded on the strategic policy of the Vietnamese People's Liberation Army at the National Military Conference held at the beginning of this year, stating: "Our new strategy will be to greatly promote mobile warfare tactics. Mobile warfare will have a more prominent position than guerrilla warfare. We should learn positional warfare." To this end, it is necessary to establish and consolidate field armies, develop reserve forces—local armies and militias, train cadres, improve the equipment of the troops, and boldly concentrate forces in order to annihilate the enemy's effective strength under favorable conditions. General Vo Nguyen Giap also emphasized that the Vietnamese resistance movement should maintain the closest relationship with the democratic forces of the world, and that Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia should maintain close relations. General Vo Nguyen Giap finally called for mobilizing all human and material resources to fully support the front lines.

### 武元甲将军谈越军战略方针  推动运动战战术，选择有利条件歼敌有生力量

1950-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　武元甲将军谈越军战略方针
    推动运动战战术，选择有利条件歼敌有生力量
    【新华社北京三日电】越南通讯社北越讯：越南国防部长兼越南人民解放军总司令武元甲将军，在今年年初举行的全国军事会议上，阐述越南人民解放军的战略方针称：“我们的新战略，将是大大地推动运动战战术，运动战将较游击战有更特出的地位。我们应该学习阵地战。”为此目的，必须建立和巩固野战军，发展后备军——地方军和民兵，训练干部，改善部队的装备，并大胆集中兵力，以便在有利的条件下歼灭敌人的有生力量。武元甲将军并强调越南抵抗运动应与全世界民主力量之间保持最密切的关系；越南、老挝、柬埔寨之间应保持密切关系。武元甲将军最后号召动员一切人力物力，全力支援前线。
