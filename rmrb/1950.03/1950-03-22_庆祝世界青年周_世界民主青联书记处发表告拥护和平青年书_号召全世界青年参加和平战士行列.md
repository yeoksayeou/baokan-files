Title: Celebrating World Youth Week: World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretariat Issues Letter to Peace-Loving Youth, Calling on Youth Worldwide to Join the Ranks of Peace Warriors
Author:
Date: 1950-03-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 21st: The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, in celebration of World Youth Week, has issued the following letter to peace-loving youth:

　　From March 21st to March 28th, millions of young men and women will demonstrate their continued loyalty to the sacred cause of peace and the national independence of all peoples, and will fight with all their strength against the warmongers, to ensure peace and strive for a better future for the younger generation.

　　While the American and British imperialists are actively preparing for a Third World War, intensifying the oppression of colonial peoples and the working people in their own countries, and intensifying hatred and propaganda against the Soviet Union, the People's Democracies, and democratic forces worldwide, millions of young workers, peasants, and students, millions of young men and women will say to the warmongers: "This shall not pass!"

　　We, the peace-loving youth of the world, shout: Long live the world youth front fighting for peace! We call on the youth of the world to join the ranks of the heroic fighters for peace, fighters who are able to unite and maintain this unity under the most difficult conditions, to defend peace and obtain a happy future for the younger generation.

　　Young men and women, unite! Advance for lasting peace, for democracy, for the national independence of all peoples, and for a better future.

### 庆祝世界青年周  世界民主青联书记处发表告拥护和平青年书  号召全世界青年参加和平战士行列

1950-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝世界青年周
    世界民主青联书记处发表告拥护和平青年书
    号召全世界青年参加和平战士行列
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十一日讯：世界民主青联书记处为庆祝世界青年周，发表告拥护和平青年书如下：
    从三月二十一日至三月二十八日，千百万男女青年将表明其继续忠于神圣的和平事业与各族人民的民族独立的意志，将以一切力量进行斗争，反对战争贩子，以确保和平及争取年青一代更美好的将来。
    当美英帝国主义者积极准备第三次世界大战，加紧压迫殖民地人民和本国的劳动人民，加紧针对苏联、人民民主国家和全世界民主力量的憎恨及造谣宣传的时候，千百万青年工人、农民、学生，千百万男女青年将向战争贩子说：“不许这样！”
    我们，全世界拥护和平的青年们高呼：为和平而斗争的世界青年阵线万岁！我们号召全世界青年参加争取和平的英勇斗士的行列，而这些英勇斗士是能够在最困难的条件下团结一致及保持此种团结，以保卫和平及为年青一代获得幸福的将来的。
    男女青年们团结起来！为持久和平、为民主、为各族人民的民族独立、为更美将来好的向前迈进。
