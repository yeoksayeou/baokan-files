Title: Natural Science Lectures of the Central People's Broadcasting Station: Program from the 12th to the 17th
Author:
Date: 1950-03-12
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 11th: The program schedule for the Natural Science Lectures of the Central People's Broadcasting Station from the 12th to the 17th is as follows:

    March 12th: Tsiolkovsky, the Pioneer of Jet Aviation (Introduction to Soviet Scientists)
    March 13th: The Great Contributions of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin in the Field of Natural Science
    March 14th: Marx and Higher Mathematics
    March 15th: Fluorescent Lamps (General Electrical Knowledge Lecture 23) (Shen Keqi)
    March 16th: Vacuum Tubes (Same as above, Lecture 24) (Chen Jingming)
    March 17th: Electric Shock (Same as above, Lecture 25) (Wang Shufen) (End of General Electrical Knowledge)
    All programs will be broadcast at 18:30 every evening.

### 中央人民广播电台自然科学讲座  十二日至十七日节目

1950-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台自然科学讲座
    十二日至十七日节目
    【新华社北京十一日电】中央人民广播电台十二日至十七日自然科学讲座节目如下：
    十二日  喷射航空始祖崔克夫斯基（苏联科学家介绍）
    十三日  马恩列斯在自然科学领域中的伟大贡献
    十四日  马克思和高等数学
    十五日  萤光灯（电学常识第二十三讲）（沈克琦）
    十六日  真空管（同上第二十四讲）（陈景明）
    十七日  触电（同上第二十五讲）（王树芬）（电学常识完）
    各节目均在每晚十八点三十分播讲。
