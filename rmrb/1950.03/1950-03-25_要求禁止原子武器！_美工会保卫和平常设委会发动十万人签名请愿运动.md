Title: Demand the Prohibition of Atomic Weapons! United States Trade Union Standing Committee for the Defense of Peace Launches Ten Thousand Signature Petition Drive
Author:
Date: 1950-03-25
Page: 4

　　Demand the Prohibition of Atomic Weapons!
    United States Trade Union Standing Committee for the Defense of Peace Launches Ten Thousand Signature Petition Drive
    [Xinhua News Agency, New York, 24th] According to a TASS dispatch from New York on the 22nd: The struggle of American workers and other progressive elements to strive for peace and oppose warmongers is expanding throughout the country. According to the Worker Daily, the Standing Committee for the Defense of Peace, established at the All-American Trade Union for Peace Congress held in Chicago last October, is currently launching a ten thousand signature petition drive. The petition will be sent to Truman, demanding the prohibition of atomic weapons, an end to the Cold War, resolution of the growing unemployment problem, the abolition of the slave Taft-Hartley Act, and a stop to racial discrimination and the suppression of the people's freedom.

### 要求禁止原子武器！  美工会保卫和平常设委会发动十万人签名请愿运动

1950-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　要求禁止原子武器！
    美工会保卫和平常设委会发动十万人签名请愿运动
    【新华社二十四日讯】据塔斯社纽约二十二日讯：美国工人和其他进步分子争取和平反对战争贩子的斗争正在全国扩大中。据工人日报报道，去年十月在芝加哥举行的全美工会拥护和平大会成立的保卫和平常设委员会，目前正发动一项十万人签名请愿运动。请愿书将送交杜鲁门，要求禁止原子武器，结束冷战，解决日益增多的失业问题，废除奴隶的塔夫脱——哈特莱法，以及停止种族歧视和停止压制人民的自由。
