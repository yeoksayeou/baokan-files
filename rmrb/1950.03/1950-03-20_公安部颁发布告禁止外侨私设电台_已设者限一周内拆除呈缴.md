Title: Ministry of Public Security Issues Proclamation Prohibiting Foreign Nationals from Privately Establishing Radio Stations; Those Already Established Must Be Dismantled and Surrendered Within One Week

Author:
Date: 1950-03-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: The Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, today issued Public Announcement No. 47 regarding the private establishment of radio stations by foreign nationals within Chinese territory. The full text is as follows:

　　Regarding the matter of foreign nationals privately establishing radio stations within Chinese territory, the following measures are hereby stipulated:

　　1. All foreign nationals and their organizations residing within Chinese territory are strictly prohibited from privately establishing radio stations. Those who violate this will be severely punished.

　　2. Foreign nationals who have already installed radio stations or possess equipment similar to radio stations must dismantle them within seven days from the date of this announcement and surrender the radio stations and their ancillary equipment to the local Public Security Bureau.

　　Minister Luo Ruiqing
　　March 20, 1950

### 公安部颁发布告禁止外侨私设电台  已设者限一周内拆除呈缴

1950-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　公安部颁发布告禁止外侨私设电台
    已设者限一周内拆除呈缴
    【新华社二十日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府公安部，本日就外国侨民在中国境内私设电台事颁发公字第四十七号布告，全文如下：
    关于外国侨民在中国境内私设电台一事，特规定以下处理办法：
    一、凡在中国境内居留之外国侨民及其团体，一律不准私设电台，倘有违反，严惩不贷。
    二、外国侨民已装设电台或持有类似电台之设备者，自本布告公布之日起，限七日内拆除，并将电台及其附属设备呈缴当地公安局。
      部长  罗瑞卿
      一九五○年三月二十日
