Title: Beijing Private Junxinghe Money Shop Suffers Excessive Losses Due to Poor Management; Beijing People's Bank Orders Closure for Liquidation
Author:
Date: 1950-03-31
Page: 4

[Beijing News Agency Report] Beijing private Junxinghe Money Shop, due to poor management and excessive losses, has been ordered by the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China to cease operations for liquidation. Since registering and commencing business last year, the money shop's operations have been inappropriate, with weak capital, high expenses, and frequent use of deposits for operational spending. Recently, it has failed to improve, and losses have continued to increase. To protect legitimate social credit and the rights of creditors, the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China has ordered the money shop to cease business operations from the 30th. The money shop has made full preparations for clearing debts and expects to complete liquidation within days.

### 京私营峻兴和钱庄经营不当亏累过多  京人民银行令其停业清理

1950-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　京私营峻兴和钱庄经营不当亏累过多
    京人民银行令其停业清理
    【京市新闻处讯】京市私营峻兴和钱庄经营不当，亏累过多，已由中国人民银行北京分行令其停业清理。该钱庄自去年登记营业后，经营即不适当，资金薄弱，开支庞大，经常以存款挪借开支，近来仍未能加以改善，亏累益多。中国人民银行北京分行为保障社会正当信用及债权人权益，已令其自三十日起停止营业。该钱庄对清偿债务已作充分准备，日内即可清理完竣。
