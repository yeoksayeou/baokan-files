Title: Jiaxing Special District, Zhejiang Province, Overfulfills Public Bond Quota
Author:
Date: 1950-03-01
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 28th. Jiaxing Special District, Zhejiang Province has overfulfilled its public bond sales task. As of February 26th, people from all walks of life in Jiaxing Special District had subscribed to 600,931.4 shares of public bonds, exceeding the original quota by 9,314 shares.

### 浙江嘉兴专区公债超额完成

1950-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　浙江嘉兴专区公债超额完成
    【新华社杭州二十八日电】浙江省嘉兴专区已超额完成推销公债任务。截至二月二十六日止，嘉兴专区各界已认购公债六十万零九千三百十四分，超过原配额九千三百十四分。
