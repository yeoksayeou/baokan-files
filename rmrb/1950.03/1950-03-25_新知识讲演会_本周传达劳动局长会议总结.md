Title: New Knowledge Lecture Series: This Week, Conveying the Summary of the Labor Bureau Directors' Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-03-25
Page: 3

　　New Knowledge Lecture Series
    This Week, Conveying the Summary of the Labor Bureau Directors' Meeting
    [Reported by our newspaper] The New Knowledge Lecture Series, sponsored by the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce, will invite Shi Fuliang, Deputy Minister of Labor, this week on the 26th (Sunday) at 9:00 AM at the Minzhu Theater in Xizhushikou to convey the summary of the Labor Bureau Directors' Meeting and give a lecture on "Labor Policy and Labor-Capital Relations."
    (Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce)

### 新知识讲演会  本周传达劳动局长会议总结

1950-03-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　新知识讲演会
    本周传达劳动局长会议总结
    【本报讯】京市工商联合会筹委会所主办的新知识讲演会，本周请劳动部施复亮副部长于二十六日（星期日）上午九点在西珠市口民主剧院传达劳动局长会议总结，并讲演《劳动政策与劳资关系》。
    （北京市工商业联合会筹备委员会）
