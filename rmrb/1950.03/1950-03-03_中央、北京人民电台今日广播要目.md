Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-03-03
Page: 4
　　
　　[This newspaper report] Central People's Broadcasting Station's joint broadcast program today (3rd) at 20:00: (1) Third Mandarin News; (2) International Current Affairs: Learning from the Soviet Union, Advancing towards the Road of Happy Life for Mankind; (3) Comprehensive Report: The situation of some factories in Shanghai after relocating to the inland; (4) Broadcasting two pieces of correspondence: One, the process of the China Merchants Steam Navigation Company's Hai Xuan ship defecting to the motherland, and two, a get-together of Hong Kong merchant ship students. 17:30 Staff Time: Two notices from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions: One, a notice on postponing the National Congress of Trade Union Organizations, and two, a notice on the relationship between trade union organizations at all levels and the Worker's Daily. 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: Two different lives and destinies of scientists before and after the Soviet October Revolution. 18:00 First Art and Literature Program: Ma Xiaorong sings the Zhui Zi [a type of storytelling performance] "Blue Bridge Regret." 21:00 Second Art and Literature Program: Celebrating the Lantern Festival, broadcasting the Kuaiban [a type of rhythmic storytelling] play "Brother and Sister Visit the Lantern Market."
　　[Also Reported] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station 12:30 Workers' Program: (1) Entertainment, (2) "Introducing the Regular Spare-time School of the Fengtai Engineering Section." 13:00 Youth Program: (1) School News, (2) Correspondence: "The Student Movement in Yunnan before Liberation," (3) Students from the Department of Musical Drama of the Normal University sing five new songs. 16:30 Municipal Voice: Tang Shaoyuan, a representative of the industrial and commercial circles at the Second Session of the Second Beijing People's Representative Conference, speaks on "Talking about the Central Task of the Second Session of the Second Beijing People's Representative Conference." 17:00 Children's Program: Children from Niujie Elementary School in the Eleventh District broadcast (1) a Winter Vacation Life Forum, (2) Singing. 18:45 Listener Service: (1) Answering "How are Soviet films with Chinese dubbing made?" (2) Answering "How much does an ounce actually weigh?" (3) An announcement from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions about recruiting statistical staff.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（三日）二十点联播节目：（一）第三次普通话新闻；（二）国际时事：学习苏联，向着人类美满生活的道路前进；（三）综合报导：上海的一些工厂迁到内地以后的情形；（四）播送两篇通讯：一、招商局海玄号轮船投向祖国的经过，二、香港商船同学们的一个联欢会。十七点三十分职工时间：全总的两项通知：一、关于延期召开全国工会组织会议的通知，二、关于各级工会组织与工人日报关系的通知。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：苏联十月革命前后科学家两种不同的生活和命运。十八点第一次文艺节目：马小荣演唱坠子《蓝桥恨》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：庆祝灯节播送快板剧《兄妹逛灯》。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人节目（一）文娱，（二）“介绍丰台工务段正规业余学校”。十三点青年节目⑴学校新闻，⑵通讯：“解放前云南的学生运动”，⑶师大乐剧系同学演唱五支新歌曲。十六点三十分市政之声：北京市二届二次各界人民代表会议工商界代表汤绍远讲“谈谈北京市第二届第二次各界人民代表会议的中心任务。”十七点儿童节目：十一区牛街小学小朋友们演播（一）寒假生活座谈会，（二）歌咏。十八点四十五分听众服务：⑴回答“华语对白的苏联影片是怎样制成的？”⑵回答“一盎斯实际有多重”，⑶全总招考统计工作人员的启事。
