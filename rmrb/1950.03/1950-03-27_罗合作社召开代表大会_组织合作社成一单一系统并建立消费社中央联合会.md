Title: Romanian Cooperatives Hold Congress; Organize Cooperatives into a Single System and Establish a Central Federation of Consumer Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1950-03-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, March 23rd: The Romanian National Cooperative Congress opened on the 20th. The Congress heard a report by Comrade Alexe, Secretary of the Cooperative Organizing Committee, on the activities of Romanian cooperatives. Alexe stated that the Congress is completing the organization of cooperatives into a single system and is beginning to establish a central federation of Romanian consumer cooperatives. There are now 5,632 consumer cooperatives nationwide, most of which are located in rural areas. These cooperatives unite 4,665,000 members. In 1949, the sales of cooperative goods increased by 280 percent compared to 1948. Rural cooperatives have 3,100 machinery rental stations, equipped with nearly 180,000 pieces of agricultural equipment for farmers' needs. This year, the sales of goods by Romanian cooperatives will increase by 31 percent. The sales of manufactured goods to the people will increase by 72 percent compared to last year.

### 罗合作社召开代表大会  组织合作社成一单一系统并建立消费社中央联合会

1950-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗合作社召开代表大会
    组织合作社成一单一系统并建立消费社中央联合会
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚全国合作社代表大会于二十日开幕。大会听取了合作社组织委员会书记亚历克舍关于罗国合作社活动的报告。亚历克舍称：代表大会正在完成将合作社组织成一个单一的系统，并着手建立一个罗马尼亚消费合作社的中央联合会。现在全国已有了五千六百三十二个消费合作社，大部都设在乡村中。这些合作社团结了四百六十六万五千名社员。一九四九年合作社货物销售额较一九四八年增加了百分之二百八十。乡村合作社已有三千一百个机器租赁站，装备着将近十八万部农业工具，以供农民需用。今年，罗马尼亚合作社货物销售额将增加百分之三十一。售给人民的制成品销售额将较去年增加百分之七十二。
