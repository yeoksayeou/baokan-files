Title: Shanghai Youth Gather to Commemorate World Youth Week
Author:
Date: 1950-03-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 26th. To commemorate World Youth Week, youth from all walks of life in Shanghai held a grand get-together on the 25th. More than a thousand young people from all walks of life attended, and more than a hundred young people from the Soviet Union, Poland, North Korea, New Zealand, Britain, the United States and other countries residing in Shanghai were also invited to participate. Lu Guang, deputy secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, spoke first. He pointed out that the great victory of China's War of Liberation has strengthened and consolidated the world's peace cause; the signing of the Sino-Soviet New Treaty has more powerfully strengthened the strength of the world's peace camp. Finally, he called on Shanghai youth to strengthen unity and completely eliminate the remaining bandits. Soviet youth Feiotrov called on young people all over the world to resolutely unite around the World Federation of Democratic Youth, defend the cause of lasting world peace, and oppose warmongers. Young people from North Korea, Poland, New Zealand, the United States and other countries also spoke one after another, unanimously pointing out that the strong unity of young people all over the world is a powerful force in the democratic peace camp, and together with the people of the world, it is enough to shatter any aggressive conspiracy of the warmongers. Lu Zhenchuan, an overseas Chinese who returned from Southeast Asia, also reported on the heroic struggle of the people of Southeast Asia for national liberation. The young people present repeatedly cheered for Chairman Mao and Marshal Stalin, overflowing with the spirit of internationalist friendship and unity. Finally, young people from various countries performed various cultural programs, each showing the characteristics of their nation.

### 上海各界青年集会  纪念世界青年周

1950-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　上海各界青年集会
    纪念世界青年周
    【新华社上海二十六日电】上海市各界青年为纪念世界青年周，于二十五日举行盛大联欢会。到各界青年千余人，侨居在上海的苏联、波兰、朝鲜、纽西兰、英、美等国青年百余人亦被邀请参加。新民主主义青年团上海市工作委员会副书记鲁光首先讲话。他指出：中国解放战争的伟大胜利，加强和巩固了世界和平事业；中苏新约的签订，更有力地加强了世界和平阵营的力量。最后他号召上海青年加强团结，彻底消灭残余匪帮。苏联青年斐奥特洛夫呼吁全世界青年坚决团结在世界民主青年联盟周围，保卫世界持久和平事业，反对战争煽动者。朝鲜、波兰、纽西兰、美国等国青年也先后发言，一致指出：全世界青年的坚强团结，是民主和平阵营中一支雄壮的力量，与全世界人民一起，足以粉碎战争贩子们的任何侵略阴谋。东南亚归国华侨卢振川并报告东南亚人民争取民族解放的英勇奋斗情形。到会青年一再为毛主席、斯大林大元帅欢呼，洋溢着国际主义的友爱团结的精神。最后各国青年演出各种文娱节目，各自表现了民族的特色。
