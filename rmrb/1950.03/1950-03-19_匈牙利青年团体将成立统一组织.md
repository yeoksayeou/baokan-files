Title: Hungarian Youth Groups to Establish Unified Organization
Author:
Date: 1950-03-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 18th: Telecommunications Agency Budapest News: The Hungarian Youth Organization Committee recently passed a resolution to hold a unified national youth group congress in July to establish a unified organization for Hungarian youth. The committee issued a statement stating: "It is certain that Hungarian youth will respond to this call and establish an organization capable of mobilizing the youth masses to engage in the construction of socialism and intensify the socialist education of youth, in order to accomplish the current historically significant task."

### 匈牙利青年团体将成立统一组织

1950-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利青年团体将成立统一组织
    【新华社十八日讯】电通社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利青年组织委员会顷通过决定：于七月间举行全国青年团体的统一大会，以成立一个匈牙利青年的统一组织。该委员会发表文告称：“确信匈牙利青年必将响应这一号召，成立一个能够动员青年群众从事建设社会主义及加紧青年的社会主义教育的组织，以完成目前具有历史意义的任务。”
