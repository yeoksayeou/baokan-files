Title: USSR, Poland, Czechoslovakia Withdraw from International Children's Fund in Protest of Remnant Bandit Representation
Author:
Date: 1950-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 8th. TASS, New York dispatch: On March 6th, during a meeting of the Executive Board of the International Children's Emergency Fund, the Soviet representative proposed that the Executive Board of the fund expel the representatives of the Kuomintang clique. This motion was seconded by the representatives of Poland and Czechoslovakia. However, the representatives of the American and British majority faction rejected the Soviet motion.

Therefore, the Soviet representative immediately withdrew from the meeting and stated that the Soviet delegation would not recognize the legal validity of any resolutions formulated with the participation of representatives of the Kuomintang clique. The representatives of Poland and Czechoslovakia also left the meeting at the same time.

### 反对残匪代表出席  苏波捷退出国际儿童基金会

1950-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　反对残匪代表出席
    苏波捷退出国际儿童基金会
    【新华社北京八日电】塔斯社纽约讯：三月六日，在国际儿童紧急救济基金会理事会会议席上，苏联代表提议该基金会理事会开除国民党集团的代表。这个动议获得了波兰与捷克斯洛伐克的代表的附议。但是，美英多数派的代表否决了苏联的动议。
    因此，苏联代表随即退出会场，并说明凡有国民党集团代表参加制订的一切决议，苏联代表团均不承认其有合法效力。波兰与捷克斯洛伐克的代表也同时离开会场。
