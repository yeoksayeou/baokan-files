Title: State Council Holds Twenty-Third Meeting; Premier Zhou Reports on the Treaty Negotiations with the Soviet Union; Approves Key Proposals Including Directives on Spring Plowing and Production
Author:
Date: 1950-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 10th: The Central People's Government State Council held its twenty-third political meeting at 3:00 PM today. At the meeting, Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai reported on the process of signing the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance and two related agreements. The meeting listened to the report by Bo Yibo, Vice-Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council, regarding instructions for spring plowing and production, and approved these instructions. The meeting discussed and approved decisions regarding the temporary staffing levels for all levels of people's governments nationwide, the temporary supply standards for all levels of people's governments nationwide for 1950, the decision on unified implementation methods for national state-owned trade, and the decision on the inventory and allocation of national warehouse materials. The meeting revised and approved the work tasks for the Central and Southern China region for 1950 and the organizational regulations for the Central and Southern China Military and Political Committee. To assist and strengthen the leadership work in Pingyuan Province, the meeting decided that the State Council would appoint Wu De (Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government), Luo Yuchuan (Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government), and Wang Congwu (Director of the Organization Department of the North China Bureau of the CCP Central Committee) to form a central delegation to work in the province, with Wu De as the director. In addition, the meeting approved several important appointments.

### 政务院举行二十三次会议  周总理报告中苏缔约经过  并通过春耕生产指示等要案

1950-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行二十三次会议
    周总理报告中苏缔约经过
    并通过春耕生产指示等要案
    【新华社北京十日电】中央人民政府政务院于今日下午三时举行第二十三次政务会议。会上，周恩来总理兼外交部长报告了中苏友好同盟互助条约和两个协定签订的经过。会议听取了政务院财政经济委员会薄一波副主任关于春耕生产指示的报告，并通过了该指示。会议讨论并通过了关于统一全国各级人民政府员额暂行编制，全国各级人民政府一九五○年度暂行供给标准、关于全国国营贸易统一实施办法的决定及关于全国仓库物资清理调配的决定。会议修正并批准了关于中南区一九五○年的工作任务和关于中南军政委员会组织条例。为了协助和加强平原省的领导工作，会议决定由政务院指派吴德（中央人民政府燃料工业部副部长）、罗玉川（中央人民政府农业部副部长）、王从吾（中共中央华北局组织部长）三人组成中央代表团前往该省工作，以吴德为主任。此外，会议通过了重要任命案多起。
