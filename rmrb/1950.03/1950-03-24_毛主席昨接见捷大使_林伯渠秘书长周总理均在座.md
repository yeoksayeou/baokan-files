Title: Chairman Mao Received the Czechoslovak Ambassador Yesterday; Secretary-General Lin Boqu and Premier Zhou Were Present

Author:
Date: 1950-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China received Dr. Weisskopf, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic, and Dr. Adamec, the head of the Czech Trade Delegation, this evening (23rd). Present were Secretary-General Lin Boqu of the Central People's Government and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai.

### 毛主席昨接见捷大使  林伯渠秘书长周总理均在座

1950-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席昨接见捷大使
    林伯渠秘书长周总理均在座
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席于今（二十三日）晚接见捷克斯洛伐克共和国特命全权大使魏斯柯普夫博士及捷克商务代表团团长阿达麦克博士。在座者有中央人民政府林伯渠秘书长，周恩来总理兼外交部长。
