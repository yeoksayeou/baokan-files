Title: Developing Sino-Czech Trade: Czech Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-03-20
Page: 1

　　Developing Sino-Czech Trade
    Czech Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, 19th) In order to develop Sino-Czech trade, the Czechoslovak Republic, with the consent of our Central People's Government, has dispatched a trade delegation led by Dr. Adamek to China for business negotiations. The delegation arrived in Beijing this morning. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Reception Office of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Lin Haiyun, Director of the International Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade, and Mr. Lodek, Secretary of the Czech Embassy.

### 发展中捷两国贸易  捷克商务代表团抵京

1950-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　发展中捷两国贸易
    捷克商务代表团抵京
    【新华社十九日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国为发展中捷两国贸易，于征得我中央人民政府同意后，派遣了由阿达麦克博士率领的商务代表团来华商谈。该团已于今晨抵京。赴站欢迎者有我外交部交际处王倬如处长，贸易部国际贸易司林海云司长，及捷克大使馆罗戴秘书等。
