Title: Decision of the USSR Council of Ministers and the CPSU Central Committee on Another Reduction of State Trade Prices; People Will Profit Over 100 Billion Rubles Annually
Author:
Date: 1950-03-02
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 1st: TASS, Moscow, February 28th: Decision of the USSR Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) on Another Reduction of State Retail Prices of Food and Manufactured Goods.

　　In view of the new achievements in industrial and agricultural production in 1950, the increase in labor productivity, and the reduction in production costs, the Soviet government and the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) consider it possible to once again – for the third time – reduce state retail prices of food and consumer goods on March 1, 1950.

　　The USSR Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) believe that as a result of another price reduction in state trade, the people's profit for the entire year will be no less than 80 billion rubles.

　　Secondly, under the influence of price reductions in state trade, almost similar price reductions will also occur in collective farm markets and cooperative trade. As a result, the people will additionally gain no less than 30 billion rubles during the year.

　　As a result, the benefits to the people from this new price reduction will be no less than 110 billion rubles.

　　 	Ruble Purchasing Power Will Be Greatly Increased, and the Actual Income of Workers and Peasants Will Be Greatly Increased

　　There is no doubt that as a result of this new price reduction: 1. The purchasing power of the ruble will be greatly increased again; 2. The exchange rate of the Soviet ruble against foreign currencies will be further improved; 3. The real wages of workers and staff will be further greatly increased; 4. The situation of people receiving pensions and allowances will be greatly improved, because the amount of pensions and allowances remains unchanged while prices have been reduced; 5. The income of farmers will be further greatly increased, because farmers purchase manufactured goods at reduced prices, greatly reducing their expenses, while the prices at which the state purchases agricultural and livestock products remain unchanged.

### 苏联部长会议及联共中央决定  再次减低国营贸易物价  人民全年将获利千余亿卢布

1950-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联部长会议及联共中央决定
    再次减低国营贸易物价
    人民全年将获利千余亿卢布
    【新华社北京一日电】塔斯社莫斯科二月二十八日电：苏联部长会议及联共（布）中央委员会关于再次减低食品与制成品国家零售价格的决定。
    鉴于一九五○年工农业生产的新成就，劳动生产率的提高与生产成本的减低，苏维埃政府及联共（布）中央委员会认为已有可能于一九五○年三月一日再次——第三次——减低食品及消费制成品的国家零售价格。
    苏联部长会议及联共（布）中央委员会认为：由于国营贸易再次减价的结果，人民在全年所得的利益将不下于八百亿卢布。
    其次，在国营贸易减价的影响下，集体农场市场及合作社贸易也将发生几乎与此相似的减价，因而，人民在一年过程中将额外获得不下于三百亿卢布的利益。
    结果，人民从这次新减价中所获得的利益将不下于一千一百亿卢布。
            卢布购买力将大大提高  工农实际收入大大增加
    毫无疑义，由于这次新减价的结果：一、卢布的购买力又将大大提高了；二、苏联卢布与外币的兑换率将更为提高；三、工人职员的实际工资将进一步大大增加；四、受领养老金及抚恤金的人民的境况将大大改进，因为养老金及抚恤金的数目不变而物价业经减低；五、农民的收入将进一步大大增加，因为农民以减低的价格购买制成品，其费用大为减低，而国家收买农业和牲畜饲养业产品的价格不变。
