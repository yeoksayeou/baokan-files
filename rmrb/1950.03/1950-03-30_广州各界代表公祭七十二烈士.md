Title: Guangzhou Representatives from All Circles Hold Public Memorial for the Seventy-Two Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1950-03-30
Page: 1

　　Guangzhou Representatives from All Circles
    Hold Public Memorial for the Seventy-Two Martyrs
    [Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 29th] Today is the anniversary of the Huanghuagang Seventy-Two Martyrs. Over two hundred people's representatives from all circles in Guangzhou went to the Huanghuagang Martyrs Cemetery to hold a public memorial. Li Zhangda, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Situ Meitang, a committee member, Zhu Guang, Deputy Mayor of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, and Liao Siguan, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, all participated in the memorial ceremony.

### 广州各界代表公祭七十二烈士

1950-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　广州各界代表
    公祭七十二烈士
    【新华社广州二十九日电】今日为黄花岗七十二烈士纪念日，广州各界人民代表二百余人前往黄花岗烈士陵园举行公祭。广东省人民政府副主席李章达、委员司徒美堂，广州市人民政府副市长朱光、市总工会筹备委员会主任廖似光等均参加祭礼。
