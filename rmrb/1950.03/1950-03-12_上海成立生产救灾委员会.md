Title: Shanghai Establishes Production and Disaster Relief Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-03-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 9th. The Shanghai Military Control Commission, in order to effectively carry out production and disaster relief work in the city and the post-bombing emergency rescue work against the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang, has established the Shanghai Production and Disaster Relief Committee in accordance with the principles of the Central People's Government's Administrative Council and the East China Military and Political Committee regarding the organization of production and disaster relief committees at all levels. Yan Huiqing, Cao Manzhi, and Zhao Puchu were appointed as chairman and vice-chairmen of the committee, and Yang Bingru, Xu Jingping, Zhang Yumin, Jiang Xiulin, Chen Yisheng, Wang Yunsheng, Jin Zhonghua, and Li Wenjie and other twelve people were appointed as members. The organization and selection of personnel for the Production and Disaster Relief Committee has been approved by the Third Administrative Conference of the East China Military and Political Committee and reported to the Administrative Council for record. The committee will begin operations from today.

### 上海成立生产救灾委员会

1950-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海成立生产救灾委员会
    【新华社上海九日电】上海市军事管制委员会为了有效地进行本市的生产救灾与反蒋匪帮轰炸的善后急救工作，特遵照中央人民政府政务院与华东军政委员会关于各级生产救灾委员会组织的原则，成立上海市生产救灾委员会。并任命颜惠庆、曹漫之、赵朴初为该会正副主任委员，杨秉儒、徐镜平、张渝民、蒋秀琳、陈已生、王芸生、金仲华、李文杰等十二人为委员。生产救灾委员会的组织及人选业经华东军政委员会第三次行政会议批准，并转报政务院备案。该会自即日起开始办公。
