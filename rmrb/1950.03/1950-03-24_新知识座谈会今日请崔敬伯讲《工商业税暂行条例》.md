Title: New Knowledge Forum Today Features Cui Jingbo on "Provisional Regulations on Industrial and Commercial Tax"
Author:
Date: 1950-03-24
Page: 3

　　New Knowledge Forum
    Today Features Cui Jingbo on "Provisional Regulations on Industrial and Commercial Tax"
    [From Our Newspaper] The Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, to ensure that industrial and commercial circles have a clear understanding of the Provisional Regulations on Industrial and Commercial Tax, has specially invited Cui Jingbo, Director of the Taxation Bureau of the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government, to speak on "Provisional Regulations on Industrial and Commercial Tax" at the New Knowledge Forum at 7:00 PM on March 24th. The location is the auditorium of the American and European Returned Students Association on Nanheyan Street. All members and people from industrial and commercial circles are encouraged to participate.

### 新知识座谈会今日请崔敬伯讲《工商业税暂行条例》

1950-03-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会
    今日请崔敬伯讲《工商业税暂行条例》
    【本报讯】民主建国会北京市分会，为使工商业界对工商业税暂行条例明确了解，特邀请中央人民政府财政部税务总局崔敬伯局长，于三月二十四日下午七时，假新知识座谈会讲：《工商业税暂行条例》。地点在南河沿欧美同学会礼堂。希各会员和工商界踊跃参加。
