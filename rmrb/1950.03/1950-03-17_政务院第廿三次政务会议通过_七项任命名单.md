Title: The 23rd Government Affairs Council Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approves Seven Appointment Lists
Author:
Date: 1950-03-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The 23rd Government Affairs Council Meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council on March 10th approved the following seven lists of appointments to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval:

	I. Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
		Member: Shu Sheyu, currently a member of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles.

	II. Renmin University of China
		President: Wu Yuzhang, current President of North China University.
		Vice President: Hu Xikui, current Vice President of North China People's Revolutionary University.
		Vice President: Cheng Fangwu, current Vice President of North China University.

	III. Northwest University
		President: Hou Wailu, current Director of the History Department of Beijing Normal University.

	IV. Yunnan Provincial Military and Political Committee
		Chairman: Lu Han, of the Yi ethnic group in Yunnan; General of the Uprising.
		Vice Chairman: Song Renqiong, of Hunan; current Deputy Political Commissar of the Southwest Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
		Vice Chairman: Zhou Baozhong, of Yunnan; formerly Chairman of the People's Government of Jilin Province.

	V. Yunnan Provincial People's Government
		Chairman: Chen Geng, of Hunan; current Deputy Commander of the Southwest Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
		Vice Chairman: Zhou Baozhong, of Yunnan; formerly Chairman of the People's Government of Jilin Province.
		Vice Chairman: Zhang Chong, of the Yi ethnic group in Yunnan; formerly Vice Chairman of the People's Government of Songjiang Province.
		Vice Chairman: Yang Wenqing, of Yunnan; former Director of the Department of Civil Affairs of the Kuomintang Yunnan Provincial Government.

	VI. Xikang Provincial People's Government
		Chairman: Liao Zhigao, of Xikang; current Secretary of the Xikang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Political Commissar of the Xikang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
		Vice Chairman: Zhang Weijiong, of Xikang; former Secretary-General of the Kuomintang Xikang Provincial Government.
		Vice Chairman: Lu Ruilin, formerly Political Commissar of a certain army of the People's Liberation Army.

	VII. Shaanxi Provincial People's Government
		Committee Members (in order of stroke count of surname):
		Ma Zijian, of Shaanxi, Hui ethnic group; China Democratic League.
		Chen Yugao, of Shaanxi, from the education sector.
		Xiong Wentao, of Shaanxi, current Principal of Hanzhong Women's Normal School.

### 政务院第廿三次政务会议通过  七项任命名单

1950-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第廿三次政务会议通过
    七项任命名单

    一、中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会
    委员  舒舍予  现任中华全国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会委员。

    校长  吴玉章  现任华北大学校长。
