Title: Soviet Delegation to London Conference to be Led by Shepilov
Author:
Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 12th: TASS, Moscow, August 12th, reports: The Soviet Council of Ministers has determined the members of the Soviet delegation to the conference on the Suez Canal issue to be held in London. The head of the delegation is D. T. Shepilov, and the members are V. S. Semenov and G. Ts. Zaitsev.

### 苏联出席伦敦会议代表团  将由谢皮洛夫率领

1956-08-13
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联出席伦敦会议代表团
    将由谢皮洛夫率领
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：苏联部长会议已经确定了参加在伦敦举行的苏彝士运河问题会议的苏联代表团成员。代表团团长是德·特·谢皮洛夫，团员是符·斯·谢苗诺夫、格·齐·扎伊采夫。
