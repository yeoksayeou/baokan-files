Title: Shepilov Departs London for Return to Homeland
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, London, August 25th: TASS, London, August 25th, reports: The Soviet Minister of Foreign Affairs, Shepilov, who led the Soviet delegation to the London conference on the Suez Canal issue, left London by plane today to return to Moscow. Accompanying him were members of the Soviet delegation Semyonov, Malik, Zaitsev, as well as Soviet advisors and experts.

### 谢皮洛夫离伦敦回国

1956-08-26
第6版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫离伦敦回国
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社伦敦25日讯：率领苏联代表团出席伦敦苏彝士运河问题会议的苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫今天从伦敦乘飞机回莫斯科。同他一起离开伦敦的有苏联代表团团员谢苗诺夫、马立克、札伊采夫以及苏联的顾问和专家。
