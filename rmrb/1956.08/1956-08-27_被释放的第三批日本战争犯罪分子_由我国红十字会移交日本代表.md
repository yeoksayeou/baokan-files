Title: The Third Group of Released Japanese War Criminals Transferred by China Red Cross to Japanese Representatives
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, August 26th: The Chinese Red Cross Society today handed over 354 released Japanese war criminals to representatives of the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the Japan Peace Liaison Council.

    At 9:00 AM, the handover ceremony was held in the Tianjin Hotel lobby. Chinese Red Cross representatives Peng Yan, Ji Feng, and Wu Shifa and Japanese Red Cross Society representative Kiuchi Risaburo, Japan-China Friendship Association representative Yamauchi Kiyoo, and Japan Peace Liaison Council representative Fukushima Shigeji signed the handover certificates. Takagi Takesaburo, representative of the "Kouan Maru" ship, also signed the handover certificate.

    After the ceremony, the representatives of the three Japanese organizations and the representative of the "Kouan Maru" ship met with the released Japanese war criminals at the Huizhong Hotel.

    The head and deputy head of the sixth "Group for Escorting the Ashes of Chinese Martyrs" and some members also attended the handover ceremony and met with the released Japanese war criminals. Sports News

### 被释放的第三批日本战争犯罪分子  由我国红十字会移交日本代表

1956-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

    被释放的第三批日本战争犯罪分子
    由我国红十字会移交日本代表
    新华社天津26日电　中国红十字会今天把三百五十四名被释放的日本战争犯罪分子移交给日本红十字会、日中友好协会和日本和平联络会三团体代表。
    上午九时，移交仪式在天津饭店大厅里举行。中国红十字会代表彭炎、纪锋、吴士法和日本红十字会代表木内利三郎、日中友好协会代表山西清雄、日本和平联络会代表福岛重之在移交证书上签了字。在移交证书上签字的还有“兴安丸”轮船代表高木武三郎。
    仪式结束后，日本三团体代表和“兴安丸”轮船代表到惠中饭店会见了被释放的日本战争犯罪分子。
    第六批“中国殉难烈士骨灰护送团”的团长、副团长和部分团员也参加了移交仪式，并会见了被释放的日本战争犯罪分子。体育新闻
