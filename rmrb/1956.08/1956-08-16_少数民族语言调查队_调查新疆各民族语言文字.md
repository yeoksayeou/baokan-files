Title: Minority Language Survey Team to Investigate Languages and Scripts of Various Ethnic Groups in Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, August 13th: The work team investigating the languages of various ethnic groups in the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region has begun to depart from Urumqi to the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture, the Kizilsu Kirghiz Autonomous Prefecture, Kashgar, the Lop Nur area, and along the Tarim River. The work team will conduct in-depth investigations and research on the development status of the languages and scripts of the Uyghur, Kazakh, Kirghiz, Xibe, Uzbek, Tatar, and Tajik ethnic groups, and classify the dialects and local languages of each ethnic group; assist ethnic groups with incomplete writing systems to further develop them; and help the Tajik people, who are distributed on the Pamir Plateau and have no writing system to date, create a writing system.

The work team will complete the investigation of the current status of the languages and scripts of various ethnic groups in Xinjiang within this year.

### 少数民族语言调查队  调查新疆各民族语言文字

1956-08-16
第7版()
专栏：

    少数民族语言调查队
    调查新疆各民族语言文字
　　据新华社乌鲁木齐13日电　调查新疆维吾尔自治区各民族语言的工作队，开始从乌鲁木齐分赴伊犁哈萨克族自治州、克孜勒苏柯尔克孜族自治州、喀什、罗布泊地区和塔里木河沿岸等地。工作队将深入调查和研究维吾尔、哈萨克、柯尔克孜、锡伯、乌孜别克、塔塔尔和塔吉克等民族语言文字的发展状况，划分各民族的方言和土语；对文字不够完整的民族协助它们作进一步的发展；帮助分布在帕米尔高原上迄今没有文字的塔吉克族人民创造文字。
　　工作队将在今年内把新疆各民族语言文字的现状调查完毕。
