Title: Egyptian Newspapers Welcome Khrushchev's Speech
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 2nd: TASS reports from Cairo on the 2nd that Egyptian newspapers published excerpts of the part of Khrushchev's July 31st speech concerning the international situation with large headlines on the front pages. The newspapers unanimously welcomed Khrushchev's speech regarding the Egyptian government's decision to nationalize the Suez Canal Company, and expressed great satisfaction with the Soviet Union's support for Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal.

### 埃报欢迎赫鲁晓夫演说

1956-08-03
第6版()
专栏：

    埃报欢迎赫鲁晓夫演说
    据新华社2日讯　据塔斯社开罗二日讯：埃及各报以头版大字标题刊登赫鲁晓夫七月三十一日的演说中关于国际形势部分的摘要。各报一致欢迎赫鲁晓夫关于埃及政府将苏彝士运河公司收归国有的决定的讲话，并且极其满意地指出苏联对埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有给予了支持。
