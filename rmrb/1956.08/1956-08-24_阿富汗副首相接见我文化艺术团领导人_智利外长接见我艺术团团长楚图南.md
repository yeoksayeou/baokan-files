Title: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister Receives Leaders of Chinese Cultural and Artistic Troupe; Chilean Foreign Minister Receives Chu Tunan, Head of Chinese Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, August 23rd: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammed Naim received Ma Hanbing, head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, and Song Xiao, deputy head, on August 21st.
    Ma Hanbing expressed gratitude for the invitation to attend the 35th anniversary celebration of Afghanistan's Independence Day and for the warm welcome the delegation received.
    Ma Hanbing also presented gifts to Naim.
    Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Santiago dispatch: Chilean Foreign Minister Osvaldo Sainte-Marie received Chu Tunan, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, on the morning of August 22nd.
    Marie said that the Chinese Art Troupe's visit to Chile was deeply welcomed by the Chilean people.
    Chu Tunan expressed his gratitude to the Chilean government for its support and assistance, and to the Chilean people for their warm welcome.
    In the afternoon, Fernando Alessandri, President of the Chilean Senate, and Julio Durán, President of the House of Representatives, separately received Chu Tunan and Wang Li, the deputy head of the troupe. Both speakers welcomed the art troupe. During the conversations, both sides expressed their hope to promote friendship and cultural exchange between the two countries.

### 阿富汗副首相接见我文化艺术团领导人  智利外长接见我艺术团团长楚图南

1956-08-24
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗副首相接见我文化艺术团领导人
    智利外长接见我艺术团团长楚图南
    据新华社喀布尔23日电　阿富汗副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆在八月二十一日接见了中国文化艺术代表团团长马寒冰和副团长宋肖。
    马寒冰对被邀参加阿富汗独立日三十五周年纪念以及代表团所受到的热烈欢迎表示感谢。
    马寒冰还向纳伊姆赠送礼物。
    据新华社23日讯　圣地亚哥消息：智利外交部长奥斯瓦尔多·萨英特·马里埃八月二十二日上午接见了中国艺术团团长楚图南。
    马里埃说，中国艺术团访问智利，深受智利人民的欢迎。
    楚图南向马里埃表示感谢智利政府的支持、帮助和智利人民的欢迎。
    下午，智利参议院议长费尔南多·亚历山德里和众议院议长胡利奥·杜拉姆分别接见了楚图南和副团长王力。这两位议长都对艺术团表示欢迎。谈话中，双方都表示希望促进两国的友谊和文化交流。
