Title: 朝、蒙体育代表团到京 (Korean and Mongolian Sports Delegations Arrive in Beijing)
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 9th August.

    Korean and Mongolian sports delegations, invited to China to participate in the sports conference of Korea, Mongolia, Vietnam, and China, each consisting of three members, arrived in Beijing today.
    The Korean delegation was led by Kim Hyong-kwon, member of the State Physical Culture and Sports Guidance Committee of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The Mongolian delegation was led by Dashiin Byambaa, Head of the Organization Department of the Committee for Physical Culture and Sports of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic. They were welcomed by representatives of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China upon their arrival.

### 朝、蒙体育代表团到京

1956-08-10
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    朝、蒙体育代表团到京
    新华社9日讯　应邀来中国参加朝、蒙、越、中四国体育会议的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国体育代表团和蒙古人民共和国体育代表团各三人，今天到达北京。
    朝鲜代表团由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁体育指导委员会委员金亨权率领。蒙古代表团由蒙古人民共和国部长会议体育运动委员会组织部部长达希登巴列布率领。他们到达的时候，都受到了中华人民共和国体育运动委员会的代表的欢迎。
