Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting to Discuss This Year's Disaster Situation and Relief Work
Author:
Date: 1956-08-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th August. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its forty-fourth meeting on the 29th.

    The meeting heard a report by Xie Juezai, Minister of the Interior, on the disaster situation and relief work in 1956, a report by Li Baohua, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Water Resources, on the flood control situation in 1956, and a comprehensive explanation of the disaster situation and relief work by Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council, and conducted discussions. The meeting also approved various lists of appointments and removals.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会  议讨论今年灾情和救灾工作

1956-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行会
    议讨论今年灾情和救灾工作
    新华社29日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在29日举行第四十四次会议。
    会议听了内务部部长谢觉哉所作的关于1956年的灾情和救灾工作的报告，水利部副部长李葆华所作的关于1956年防汛情况的报告，国务院副总理李先念就灾情和救灾工作作了综合的说明，并且进行了讨论。会议还通过了各项任免名单。
