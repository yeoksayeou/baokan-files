Title: World Federation of Trade Unions to Hold Council Meeting Next Month to Discuss the Unification of the World Workers' Movement and Other Issues
Author:
Date: 1956-08-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 30th: A communique released by the Secretariat of the World Federation of Trade Unions on August 29th announced that the Eighth Council Meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions will be held in Sofia from September 27th to October 3rd. The following issues will be discussed at this meeting:
    I. Taking effective measures to meet the urgent social and economic demands of workers and developing new forces to help achieve the unity of the world trade union movement. A report will be presented by Louis Saillant, General Secretary of the Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions.
    II. The fraternal solidarity of trade union movements and workers in countries that have gained or are about to gain independence in the struggle against colonialism. A report will be presented by Abdoulaye Diallo, Vice-Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions.
    III. On the International Solidarity Fund.
    IV. On convening the Fourth World Trade Union Congress. A report will be presented by Giuseppe Casadei.
    The communique pointed out that the Eighth Council Meeting will be held in a new situation where international tensions have eased, that is, at a time when fraternal understanding between trade unions that have been divided until now has become evident in the world trade union movement.

### 世界工联下月举行理事会议  将讨论世界工人运动统一等问题

1956-08-31
第5版()
专栏：

    世界工联下月举行理事会议
    将讨论世界工人运动统一等问题
    新华社30日讯　塔斯社布拉格讯：世界工会联合会书记处8月29日发表的公报宣布，世界工会联合会理事会第八次会议将从9月27日到10月3日在索非亚举行。这次会议将讨论下列问题：
    一、采取有效措施来满足工人们的迫切的社会和经济要求以及发展新的力量以便有助于达成世界工会运动的统一。由世界工会联合会执行局总书记路易·赛扬提出报告。
    二、已经取得独立或正要取得独立的国家的工会运动和工人在反对殖民主义的斗争中的兄弟般的团结一致。由世界工会联合会执行局副主席阿卜杜拉·迪亚洛提出报告。
    三、关于国际团结基金问题。
    四、关于召开第四次世界工会代表大会问题。由朱塞佩·卡萨迪提出报告。
    公报指出，理事会第八次会议将在国际紧张局势有了缓和的新的情况下举行，也就是将在迄今一直陷于分裂的工会之间兄弟般的谅解在世界工会运动中变得明显的时候举行。
