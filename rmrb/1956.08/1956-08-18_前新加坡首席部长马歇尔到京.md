Title: Former Singapore Chief Minister Marshall Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th.
David Marshall, Chairman of the Singapore Labour Front, Member of the Legislative Assembly, Advisor to the Singapore Trade Mission to China, and former Chief Minister of Singapore, arrived in Beijing by train tonight. He is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

David Marshall said at the station: "I am very happy to come to Beijing. This visit is something I have long desired.

Your Premier Zhou said about three years ago that in order to promote mutual understanding and international cooperation, it is very important to expand the basis of contact. I wholeheartedly agree with this view.

We Singaporeans are deeply aware of the need for harmony and cooperation between races.

If my visit can make even a slight contribution to this, then it is very valuable."

Thank you for the hospitality you have shown me on my pleasant journey here."

Welcoming him at the station were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Qiao Guanhua, and Luo Longji, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General; Liao Chengzhi, Member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Zhirang, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Association; Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Wang Kunlun, Vice Mayor of Beijing; and people from all walks of life.

### 前新加坡首席部长马歇尔到京

1956-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    前新加坡首席部长马歇尔到京
    新华社17日讯　新加坡劳工阵线主席、立法议会议员、新加坡贸易访华团顾问、前新加坡首席部长戴维·马歇尔今晚乘火车到达北京。他是应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国访问的。
    戴维·马歇尔在车站发表谈话说：“我非常高兴地来到了北京。这个访问是我久已想望的。
    你们的周总理在约三年前曾说过，为了促进相互谅解和国际合作，扩大接触的基础是很重要的。我衷心同意这个意见。
    我们新加坡人深深地意识到需要种族之间的融洽和协作。
    如果我的访问能对这个方面有些微贡献的话，那么这是很有价值的。”
    谢谢你们在我来这里的愉快旅途中所给予我的款待”。
    到车站欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、陈翰笙、乔冠华、罗隆基、秘书长吴茂荪，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员廖承志，中国政治法律学会副会长张志让，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，北京市副市长王昆仑和社会各界人士。
