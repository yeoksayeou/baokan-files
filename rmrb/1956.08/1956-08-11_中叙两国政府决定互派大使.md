Title: China and Syria Decide to Exchange Ambassadors

Author:

Date: 1956-08-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th August. The governments of China and Syria today released a joint communiqué announcing that both sides have decided to exchange ambassadors and establish embassies in each other's countries. The full text of the joint communiqué is as follows:

    The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Syrian Republic, in order to realize the common aspiration of both sides to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic envoys, have decided to exchange ambassadors and establish embassies in each other's countries.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th August. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Syrian Republic, Salah al-Din Bitar, sent a telegram to the Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, Zhou Enlai, stating that the Syrian government has decided to establish diplomatic relations with the Chinese government at the ambassadorial level. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    I gratefully received your telegram of July 4th. The Syrian government has decided to establish diplomatic relations with your government at the ambassadorial level.

                         Salah al-Din Bitar
                         Minister of Foreign Affairs of Syria
                         August 1, 1956, Damascus

### 中叙两国政府决定互派大使

1956-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中叙两国政府决定互派大使
    新华社10日讯　中国和叙利亚两国政府今天发表联合公报，宣布双方决定互派大使并且互设大使馆。联合公报全文如下：
    中华人民共和国政府和叙利亚共和国政府为了实现双方关于建立外交关系和互换外交使节的共同愿望，决定互派大使并且互设大使馆。
    新华社10日讯　叙利亚共和国外交部长赛拉哈丁·比塔尔打电报给周恩来外交部长说，叙利亚政府已经决定同中国政府建立大使级的外交关系。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来阁下：
    感谢地收到了你七月四日的来电。叙利亚政府已经决定同贵国政府建立大使级的外交关系。
                        叙利亚外交部长赛拉哈丁·比塔尔
                                1956年8月1日于大马士革
