Title: Wu Xiuquan Hosts Reception for Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, Football Team, and South Artists
Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 24th: Belgrade Dispatch: Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, Wu Xiuquan, held a reception in Belgrade on the 22nd for the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe and the Chinese Football Team's visit to Yugoslavia, and for Yugoslav artists returning from their visit to China.
    Present at the reception were Janković, Vice-President of the Belgrade City People's Committee, representatives from the Yugoslav State Secretariat for Foreign Affairs and the Association for Foreign Culture, as well as people from Yugoslav cultural, artistic, and sports circles.
    At the reception, Ambassador Wu Xiuquan raised a glass to congratulate the achievements of the Yugoslav artists during their visit and performance in China, and expressed his gratitude for the warm reception and care the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe and Football Team received in Yugoslavia.

### 伍修权举行酒会招待我杂技团、足球队和南艺术家

1956-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

    伍修权举行酒会招待我杂技团、足球队和南艺术家
    据新华社布拉格24日电　贝尔格莱德消息：中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权为中国杂技团和中国足球队访问南斯拉夫以及南斯拉夫艺术家访问中国归来，二十二日在贝尔格莱德举行酒会。
    出席酒会的有贝尔格莱德市人民委员会副主席扬科维奇，南斯拉夫外交国务秘书处和对外文化协会的代表，以及南斯拉夫文化、艺术、体育各界人士。
    伍修权大使在酒会上举杯祝贺南斯拉夫艺术家在中国访问演出所获得的成就，并且对中国杂技团和足球队在南斯拉夫受到的热情招待和关怀表示感谢。
