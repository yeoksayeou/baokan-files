Title: Lao Royal Government and Lao Fighting Units Continue Talks; Agreement Reached on Universal Suffrage and Formation of Coalition Government
Author:
Date: 1956-08-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 8th. Vientiane Radio broadcast at 18:30 today that the delegations of the Royal Lao Government and the Lao Fighting Units continued their talks this morning.

    This meeting discussed the two issues that remained unresolved in the joint communiqué signed by both parties on August 5th: universal suffrage and the formation of a coalition government.

    According to Vientiane Radio, the delegations of both parties have reached the following agreement:

    1.  Hold a universal election with the participation of Lao Fighting Units and former resistance personnel, in a free and secret ballot;

    2.  Establish a coalition government with the participation of Lao Fighting Units and other parties.

    At today's meeting, both sides agreed to send their respective delegations to form political and military committees to discuss the implementation of all matters agreed upon in these talks.

    Prince Souphanouvong, on behalf of the Lao Fighting Units delegation, read out the final draft of the joint communiqué on the issue of universal suffrage and the formation of a coalition government. The representatives carefully discussed every point of the final draft joint communiqué.

    The meeting ended at 12:30. The next meeting is scheduled for 10:00 am on August 9th.

### 老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队继续会谈  普选和成立联合政府问题达成协议

1956-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队继续会谈
    普选和成立联合政府问题达成协议
    新华社河内8日电　据万象电台今天十八时三十分广播：老挝王国政府代表团和寮国战斗部队代表团今天上午继续举行会谈。
    这次会议讨论了八月五日双方签订的联合公报中尚未解决的两个问题——普选和成立联合政府问题。
    据万象电台报道，双方代表团已经达成以下的协议。
    一、按照自由和秘密投票的方式举行有寮国战斗部队和前抗战人员参加的普选；
    二、成立有寮国战斗部队和其他党派参加的联合政府。
    在今天的会议上，双方同意各自派遣代表团成立政治和军事委员会，以讨论实行这次会谈中已经达成协议的各事项。
    苏发努冯亲王代表寮国战斗部队代表团宣读了关于普选和成立联合政府问题的最后联合公报草案。代表们仔细讨论了最后联合公报草案的每一点。
    会议在十二点三十分结束。下次会议定于八月九日十点举行。
