Title: Bulgarian Foreign Minister Neychev Passes Away
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 12th: According to Sofia Radio, Member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Bulgarian Foreign Minister Mincho Neychev passed away on August 11th after a long illness.

### 保外长?伊切夫逝世

1956-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    保外长?伊切夫逝世
    新华社布拉格12日电　据索非亚电台广播：保加利亚共产党中央委员会委员、保加利亚外交部长明乔·涅伊切夫经过长时期的重病后，已经在八月十一日逝世。
