Author:
Date: 1956-08-17

    Workers' Happy Recuperation Life
    Chen Yanfang, a female worker from Qingdao State-Owned Cotton Mill No. 7, who is recuperating at the Qingdao Textile Sanatorium, is picking flowers with a nurse comrade.
                                      Our reporter Gao Liang photographed
    The Fujian Workers' Hot Spring Sanatorium, newly built on the shore of West Lake in Fuzhou, has welcomed advanced producers, engineers, and senior intellectuals for recuperation. This is a scene of recuperators taking a walk.
                        Lin Tong photographed (Xinhua News Agency draft)
    In the capital's beautiful Summer Palace, the Beijing Workers' Sanatorium receives advanced producers and model workers from different work posts. This is them at the Summer Palace swimming pool.
                                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Shi Panqi photographed
    Highway transportation workers from the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, vacationing in Beidaihe, are overlooking the sea view at the beach.
                                    Ding Butian photographed (Xinhua News Agency draft)
    In order to take care of workers with weak health and allow them to get good rest, the Wuxi Municipal Local State-Owned Silk Reeling Factory in Jiangsu Province has established an amateur sanatorium. These are the workers recuperating there.
                             Liu Qi photographed (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 工人们愉快的休养生活（图片）

1956-08-17
第2版()
专栏：

    工人们愉快的休养生活
    在青岛纺织疗养院疗养的青岛国棉七厂女工陈衍芳，正在和护士同志一道采摘鲜花。
                                      本报记者　高粮摄
    在福州西湖湖畔新建了一所福建工人温泉疗养院。来这里疗养的有先进生产者、工程师、高级知识分子。这是疗养员们在散步。
                        林彤摄　（新华社稿）
    在首都风景绮丽的颐和园里，北京市工人休养所接待着来自不同工作岗位上的先进生产者和模范工作者。这是他们在颐和园游泳场。
                                      新华社记者　时盘棋摄
    在北戴河度假的新疆维吾尔自治区公路运输工人在海滨眺望海洋景色。
                                    丁补天摄　（新华社稿）
    江苏省无锡市地方国营缫丝一厂为了照顾身体虚弱的工人，使他们得到很好的休息，举办了一座业余疗养所。这是在疗养的工人。
                             刘琪摄　（新华社稿）
