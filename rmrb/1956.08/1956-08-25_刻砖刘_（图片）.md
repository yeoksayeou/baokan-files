Title: "Brick Carver Liu"
Author: Zhang Yinghua
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 2

    "Brick Carver Liu"
    Liu Fengming, a 67-year-old brick carving artist from Tianjin, has been carving bricks since he was 15 years old. Because of his famous carving skills, people call him "Brick Carver Liu." The above picture shows Liu Fengming carving a brick table lamp base. The picture on the right shows Liu Fengming carving nine lions of different sizes and expressions on a one-square-foot brick. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Yinghua photo

### “刻砖刘”（图片）
张映华
1956-08-25
第2版()
专栏：

    “刻砖刘”
    天津市六十七岁的刻砖老艺人刘凤鸣，从十五岁起就刻砖。由于他的雕艺著名，人们都称他为“刻砖刘”。上图是刘凤鸣在用砖雕刻台灯座。右图是刘凤鸣在一平方尺的砖上刻出大小不同、表情不同的九个狮子。　新华社记者　张映华摄
