Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 9th: The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their 55th meeting at the Palais des Nations in Geneva at 10:00 a.m. on August 9th. The meeting adjourned at 12:20 p.m., and the next meeting is scheduled for August 21st.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-08-10
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦9日电　中美两国大使级会谈八月九日上午十时在日内瓦国联大厦举行第五十五次会议。会议在十二时二十分休会，下次会议定在八月二十一日举行。
