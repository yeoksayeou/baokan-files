Title: Liaoning Allocates Flood Control Materials to Support Flood Control Efforts in Other Provinces
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 10th: Since the end of July, the Liaoning Provincial Flood Control Headquarters and the grain and supply and marketing departments have allocated more than 10,000 tons of flood control and emergency rescue materials to actively support the flood control efforts of the people in areas severely affected by floods and areas where the flood situation is tense, such as Heilongjiang, Anhui, and Hubei provinces.

In order to repair the roadbed on the Changchun-Harbin line that was destroyed by the flood as soon as possible, the Shenyang Railway Administration is allocating more than 250 train cars of cinder and stones to be transported to the road repair sites.

### 辽宁调拨防汛物资  支援外省防汛斗争

1956-08-12
第3版()
专栏：

    辽宁调拨防汛物资
    支援外省防汛斗争
    据新华社沈阳10日电　七月末以来，辽宁省防汛指挥部和粮食、供销部门拨出了一万多吨防汛抢险物资，积极支援黑龙江、安徽、湖北等省汛情紧张和受灾地区人民的防洪斗争。
    沈阳铁路管理局为了早日修复长（春）哈（尔滨）线上被冲毁的路基，调拨的二百五十多车皮炉灰和石头正在向修路地点调运。
