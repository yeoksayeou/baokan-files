Title: Zhang Baoyuan
Author: Zhang Baoyuan
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 3

Since August 7th, continuous heavy rain has fallen in various parts of Heilongjiang Province, causing the water levels of major rivers such as the Songhua River to rise. Tens of thousands of people in Harbin City have been mobilized to intensify the protection of dikes. The picture shows employees of the Harbin Measuring & Cutting Tool Factory using their holidays to dig earth near the San Kong Bridge, load it into trucks, and transport it to the river embankment.

Zhang Baoyuan (New China News Agency Draft)
