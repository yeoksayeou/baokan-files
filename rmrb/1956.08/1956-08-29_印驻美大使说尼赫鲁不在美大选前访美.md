Title: Indian Ambassador to the US Says Nehru Will Not Visit the US Before the US Election
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Los Angeles, August 28th: Indian Ambassador to the United States, Mehta, said on August 26th that Indian Prime Minister Nehru will definitely not visit the United States before the US election in November.

Mehta came to Los Angeles after attending the US Republican Party's national convention in San Francisco as an observer. He said: Mr. Nehru hopes to visit Washington before India's February election.

Mehta said: Mr. Nehru hopes this will be an informal meeting, but the meeting will discuss major world issues, including the issue of communism and India's future relations with the Soviet Union and the United States.

### 印驻美大使说尼赫鲁不在美大选前访美

1956-08-29
第6版()
专栏：

    印驻美大使说尼赫鲁不在美大选前访美
    新华社28日讯　洛杉矶消息：印度驻美国大使梅达8月26日说：印度总理尼赫鲁肯定不会在美国的11月大选以前访问美国。
    梅达在以观察员的身份列席了美国共和党在旧金山举行的全国代表大会以后来到洛杉矶。他说：尼赫鲁先生希望在印度的2月选举以前到华盛顿访问。
    梅达说：尼赫鲁先生希望这是一次非正式的会谈，但是这次会谈将讨论世界上的主要问题，其中包括共产主义问题以及印度将来同苏联和美国的关系问题。
