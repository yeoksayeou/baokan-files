Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Congratulatory Message to the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs
Author:
Date: 1956-08-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, congratulating the conference on its opening and expressing sincere sympathy to the victims of atomic and hydrogen bombs through the conference.
    The telegram said: "The prohibition of atomic and hydrogen bombs is the common aspiration of peace-loving people all over the world. The Chinese working class fully supports your just cause of striving for peace and believes that your just actions will play a significant role in safeguarding peace in Asia and the world."

### 我总工会电贺禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会

1956-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我总工会电贺禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会
    新华社7日讯　中华全国总工会打电报给第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会，祝贺大会召开，并通过大会向原子弹、氢弹的受害者表示亲切的慰问。
    电报说：“禁止原子弹、氢弹是全世界爱好和平人民的共同愿望。中国工人阶级全力支持你们争取和平的正义事业，并相信你们的正义行动将对维护亚洲和世界和平起重大作用。”
