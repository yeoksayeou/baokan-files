Title: Vietnam Ministry of Foreign Affairs Issues Statement, Reserves the Right to Demand War Reparations from Japan
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 25th: The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement this afternoon regarding the issue of Japanese compensation for war losses in Vietnam. The full text is as follows:

According to foreign news agency reports: Recently, the South Vietnamese regime has been in contact with the Japanese government to demand compensation for war losses in Vietnam.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, authorized by the government, hereby issues the following statement on this matter:

During World War II, Japanese troops caused a great deal of damage to the entire Vietnamese people. The issue of the Japanese government compensating Vietnam for damages is a matter concerning the whole of Vietnam.

Therefore, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam still retains its right to demand that the Japanese government compensate for the war damage caused by Japanese troops on Vietnamese soil.

### 越南外交部发表声明  保留要求日本赔偿战争损害的权利

1956-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    越南外交部发表声明
    保留要求日本赔偿战争损害的权利
    新华社河内25日电　越南民主共和国外交部今天下午就日本赔偿越南战争损失问题发表声明。全文如下：
    据外国通讯社消息：最近南越政权已经同日本政府进行一些接触，以要求赔偿越南战争损失。
    越南民主共和国外交部经政府授权，特对此事发表声明如下：
    在第二次世界大战中，日本军队给越南全国人民造成了许多损害。日本政府赔偿越南损害一事，是与整个越南有关的问题。
    因此，越南民主共和国政府在要求日本政府赔偿由日本军队在越南土地上所造成的战争损害中，仍旧保有自己的权利。
