Title: Egyptian President Nasser Orders National Liberation Army to be Commanded by Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 27th, TASS: It is reported that Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser has issued an order stating that the Egyptian National Liberation Army is an organizational part of the national armed forces and is commanded by the Commander-in-Chief of the Egyptian Armed Forces.
    The Egyptian National Liberation Army recruits volunteers from the age of eighteen to fifty, with a stipulated service period of two years, which can be extended for another year if the volunteer agrees.

### 埃及总统纳赛尔下令  民族解放军由武装部队总司令指挥

1956-08-29
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及总统纳赛尔下令
    民族解放军由武装部队总司令指挥
    新华社28日讯　塔斯社开罗27日讯：据悉，埃及总统加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔已经发布命令说，埃及民族解放军是国家武装部队的组织部分，由埃及武装部队总司令指挥。
    埃及民族解放军招收从十八岁到五十岁的志愿人员，服役期限规定为两年，如果志愿兵同意可以再延长一年。
