Title: International Conference on the Suez Canal Issue: Greece Suggests Postponement and Change of Venue
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, August 9th dispatch from Athens: Greek Foreign Minister Averoff delivered a memorandum to the British Chargé d'Affaires in Greece on August 8th, suggesting that the international conference on the Suez Canal issue proposed by Britain, France, and the United States be postponed. It also suggested that the conference be held in a place other than London, in order to conduct calm and unbiased discussions in a "most peaceful atmosphere."

A copy of this memorandum has been sent to the French and American embassies in Greece.

### 对苏彝士运河问题国际会议  希腊建议延期并易地举行

1956-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

    对苏彝士运河问题国际会议
    希腊建议延期并易地举行
    据新华社9日讯　雅典消息：希腊外交大臣阿维罗夫在八日交给英国驻希腊临时代办一个备忘录，建议延期举行英、法、美三国建议的关于苏彝士运河问题的国际会议，并且建议不在伦敦而在另外的地方举行这次会议，以便在一种“最平静的气氛”中进行冷静的、没有成见的讨论。
    这个备忘录的抄本已经分送给法国和美国驻希腊的大使馆。
