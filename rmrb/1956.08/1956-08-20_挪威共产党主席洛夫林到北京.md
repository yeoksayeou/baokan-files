Title: Norwegian Communist Party Chairman Løvlien Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th August: The Chairman of the Norwegian Communist Party, Emil Løvlien, together with his son, Egil, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 19th for a visit and tour of China.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, member of the CPC Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Deputy Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee; Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others.

### 挪威共产党主席洛夫林到北京

1956-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    挪威共产党主席洛夫林到北京
    新华社19日讯　挪威共产党主席埃·洛夫林偕同他的儿子埃格尔在十九日乘飞机到达北京，前来中国访问参观。
    到机场欢迎的有中共中央政治局委员邓小平、中共中央委员王稼祥、中共中央副秘书长杨尚昆、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一等人。
