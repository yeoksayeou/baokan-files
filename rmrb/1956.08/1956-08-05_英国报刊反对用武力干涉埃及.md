Title: British Newspapers Oppose the Use of Force to Intervene in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 3rd -- As the British government flaunts its military might, and threatens to use force if negotiations fail, to intervene in Egypt's decision to nationalize the Suez Canal, some British newspapers are warning that the British government should not take military action to interfere in Egypt's internal affairs.

    The "Manchester Guardian" said that after studying the balance of power between the two sides, if the West decides to use force to reoccupy the canal, it "will be the most costly and bloody thing to do." The newspaper said: Nationalization is an internal affair, and Egypt's implementation of this measure is the "indisputable right" of a sovereign country. Britain has no reason to take military action against Egypt.

    The "News Chronicle" said: "To immediately take unilateral military intervention simply to prevent Nasser from nationalizing the Suez Canal is the worst possible solution." It warned that if force is used, Britain could "forever lose the world's respect and goodwill."

    The "Tribune" weekly, led by Labour's left-wing leader Bevan, said: "The Labour Party must try to prevent Britain from falling into a shooting war" and should oppose the "hysterical" campaign launched against Egypt by the Conservative Party and some Labour members. The weekly pointed out that Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal is the result of the British government's "seventy years of intimidation and deception against the Egyptians."

### 英国报刊反对用武力干涉埃及

1956-08-05
第6版()
专栏：

    英国报刊反对用武力干涉埃及
    新华社3日讯　伦敦消息：正当英国政府在炫耀武力、扬言谈判不成就要动用武力来干涉埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的决定的时候，英国的一些报纸却提出警告：英国政府不应该采取军事行动来干涉埃及内政。
    “曼彻斯特卫报”说，经过双方力量对比研究之后，如果西方决定使用武力重新占领运河，这“将是一个代价最大和流血最多的事情”。这家报纸说：国有化是一个内政问题，埃及实行这个措施是一个主权国家“不可争辩的权利”。英国没有理由对埃及采取军事行动。
    “新闻纪事报”说：“只是为了阻止纳赛尔把苏彝士运河国有化而立即采取单方面的军事干涉，这是一个可能采取的最坏的解决办法。”它警告说：如果使用武力，英国就可能“永远丧失世界的尊敬和善意”。
    工党左派领袖比万的“论坛”周刊说：“工党必须设法使英国不陷入射击战中”，并且应该反对保守党和一些工党分子对埃及发动的“歇斯底里的”运动。这家周刊指出，埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有就是英国政府“七十年来对埃及人进行威吓和欺骗”的结果。
