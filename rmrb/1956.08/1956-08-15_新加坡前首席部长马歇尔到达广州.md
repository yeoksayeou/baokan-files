Title: Former Chief Minister of Singapore, Marshall, Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 14th. David Marshall, Chairman of the Labour Front of Singapore, Member of the Legislative Assembly, Advisor to the Singapore Trade Delegation to China, and former Chief Minister, arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs for a visit to China. Zhao Zheng-yi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, went to Shenzhen to meet him.

    Welcoming Marshall at the Guangzhou Railway Station were Zhong Ming, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, and Rao Zhangfeng and Cao Ruoming, Directors of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs in Guangzhou.

### 新加坡前首席部长马歇尔到达广州

1956-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    新加坡前首席部长马歇尔到达广州
    新华社广州14日电　应中国人民外交学会邀请来中国访问的新加坡劳工阵线主席、立法议会议员、新加坡贸易访华团顾问、前首席部长戴维·马歇尔在今天下午到达广州。中国人民外交学会副秘书长赵政一曾往深圳迎接。
    在广州车站欢迎马歇尔的有广州市副市长钟明、中国人民外交学会在广州理事饶彰风、曹若茗等人。
