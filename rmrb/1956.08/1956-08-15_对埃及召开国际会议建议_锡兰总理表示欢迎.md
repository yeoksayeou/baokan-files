Title: Welcoming Egypt's Suggestion to Convene an International Conference, Ceylon Prime Minister Expresses Support
Author:
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: Colombo Dispatch: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike told reporters today that he welcomes Egyptian President Nasser's decision to convene an international conference on the Suez Canal.
    Bandaranaike said: "President Nasser's statement is welcome because it brings the possibility of a satisfactory and peaceful resolution of the Suez Canal dispute closer. Furthermore, I am unwilling to make any further comments at this stage."

### 对埃及召开国际会议建议  锡兰总理表示欢迎

1956-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

    对埃及召开国际会议建议
    锡兰总理表示欢迎
    据新华社14日讯　科伦坡消息：锡兰总理班达拉奈克今天告诉记者说，他欢迎埃及总统纳赛尔关于召开一个有关苏彝士运河的国际会议的决定。
    班达拉奈克说：“纳赛尔总统的声明是值得欢迎的，因为它使得圆满地、和平地解决苏彝士运河争端的可能性更加接近，此外，我不愿意在现阶段作任何进一步的评论。”
