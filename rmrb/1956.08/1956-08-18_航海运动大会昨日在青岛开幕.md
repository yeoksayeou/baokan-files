    Navigation Sports Meet Opens in Qingdao Yesterday

    (Our dispatch from Qingdao, August 17th) The 1956 Navigation Sports Competition Meeting opened today in Qingdao. The venue for the meeting is located on the beautifully scenic Huiquan Bay.

    Six representative teams from five provinces and cities, including Beijing, Shanghai, Zhejiang, Qingdao, and Guangzhou, are participating in this competition, with a total of 135 male and female athletes. Qinghai, Hebei, Harbin and other provinces and cities sent observing representatives to participate in the conference. In addition, forty-eight amateur sailing enthusiasts from Qingdao Shandong University, Shiye Cotton Mill and other units also participated in the competition activities.

    After a brief opening ceremony at 9:00 a.m., the conference entered the sculling competition. Amidst the rolling waves affected by the weak typhoon, the athletes were full of spirit, rowing their small boats like arrows, cutting through the water and flying towards the finish line. The Beijing team won first place in both the men's and women's sculling competitions.

### 航海运动大会昨日在青岛开幕

1956-08-18
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    航海运动大会昨日在青岛开幕
    本报青岛17日电　1956年航海运动竞赛大会今天在青岛开幕。大会会场设在景色秀丽的汇泉湾海面。
    参加这次竞赛的有北京、上海、浙江、青岛、广州等五个省市的六个代表队，共有男女运动员135人。青海、河北、哈尔滨等省市派了观察代表参加大会。另有青岛山东大学、实业棉织厂等单位的四十八名业余航海爱好者也参加了竞赛活动。
    大会在上午九时举行简短开幕式后进入荡桨竞赛活动，在微弱台风波及下的滚滚波涛中，运动员们精神百倍，摇动得小船像箭一样划破水面飞向终点。男女两组荡桨比赛均由北京队获第一名。
