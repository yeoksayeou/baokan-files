Title: New Forest Area Discovered in Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture
Author:
Date: 1956-08-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, July 31st. Recently, entire forests have been newly discovered within the Yongshun and Luxi counties of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture in Hunan Province. The chest-high diameter of the giant pines is three feet three inches.

According to a preliminary survey by the logging area survey team of the Jishou Branch of the Hunan Forestry Industry Bureau, the timber reserves of pine, fir, nanmu, chou, cha, sandalwood, and cypress trees in the above-mentioned forest area reach more than 700,000 cubic meters, of which more than 400,000 cubic meters can be felled now.

Based on these forest resources, the Jishou Branch of the Hunan Forestry Industry Bureau is carrying out overall design and compiling plans for labor, capital construction, and material supply. This autumn, they will build three valley reservoirs in the Zhenxi forest area of Yongshun County to store water for timber transportation.

### 湘西苗族自治州发现新林区

1956-08-02
第3版()
专栏：

    湘西苗族自治州发现新林区
    新华社长沙31日电　在湖南省湘西苗族自治州永顺和泸溪两县境内，最近新发现整片森林，巨松胸高直径三尺三寸。
    据湖南森林工业局吉首分局伐区调查队初步勘查，上述林区松、杉、楠、椆、檫、檀、柏树木材积蓄量达七十多万立方公尺，其中现在就可以采伐的有四十多万立方公尺。
    湖南森林工业局吉首分局根据这些森林资源，正在进行整体设计、编制劳力、基本建设、物资供应的规划。今年秋后，他们将在永顺县镇溪林区修建三座山谷水库，蓄水运材。
