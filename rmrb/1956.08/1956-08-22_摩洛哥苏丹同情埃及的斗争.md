Title: Moroccan Sultan Sympathizes with Egypt's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, August 21st: On August 20th, Moroccan Sultan Yusuf, in a speech delivered in Rabat to commemorate the third anniversary of his exile by the French authorities in 1953, expressed sympathy for Egypt's struggle.

He said: "We are particularly concerned about Egypt, which is going through a difficult situation. We sincerely hope that this brotherly country will overcome difficulties, safeguard its dignity, and fully establish its sovereignty."

### 摩洛哥苏丹同情埃及的斗争

1956-08-22
第6版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥苏丹同情埃及的斗争
    新华社21日讯　拉巴特消息：八月二十日，摩洛哥苏丹优素福为纪念1953年他被法国当局放逐三周年在拉巴特发表的演说中表示同情埃及的斗争。
    他说：“我们特别关怀正在经历困难处境的埃及。我们衷心愿望这个兄弟国家克服困难，维护自己的尊严和完全建立自己的主权”。
