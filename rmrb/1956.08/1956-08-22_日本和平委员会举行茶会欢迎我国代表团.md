Title: Japan Peace Committee Holds Tea Party to Welcome Chinese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 21st: The entire Chinese delegation attending the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs attended a welcome tea party held by the Japan Peace Committee in Tokyo last night at their invitation. At the meeting, the Chinese representatives held sincere talks with Japanese peace activists.
    On the same morning, Wang Yunsheng, a representative of the Chinese delegation, attended a current affairs research meeting hosted by the Japan Newspaper Association in Tokyo. In the afternoon, he also attended a symposium held by the Japan Cultural Exchange Association.

### 日本和平委员会举行茶会欢迎我国代表团

1956-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    日本和平委员会举行茶会欢迎我国代表团
    新华社21日讯　东京消息：出席第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团全体人员，昨天晚上在东京应邀出席了日本和平委员会举行的欢迎茶会。在会上，中国代表们同日本和平人士进行了恳切的谈话。
    同一天上午，中国代表团代表王芸生在东京出席了日本新闻协会主办的时事研究会。下午，他还出席了日本文化交流协会举行的恳谈会。
