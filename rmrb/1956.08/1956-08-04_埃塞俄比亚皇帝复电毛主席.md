Title: Ethiopian Emperor Replies to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd -- Chairman Mao Zedong received a reply telegram from the Emperor of Ethiopia. The full text is as follows:
To His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong, Beijing:

    I am most grateful for Your Excellency's kind telegram of congratulations on my birthday.

          Haile Selassie, Emperor of Ethiopia, Addis Ababa, 26th

### 埃塞俄比亚皇帝复电毛主席

1956-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    埃塞俄比亚皇帝复电毛主席
    新华社3日讯　毛泽东主席收到了埃塞俄比亚皇帝的复电，全文如下：北京毛泽东主席阁下：
    在我生日的时候，承阁下来电祝贺，我对阁下的盛情甚为感谢。
          海尔·塞拉西皇帝　亚的斯亚贝巴二十六日
