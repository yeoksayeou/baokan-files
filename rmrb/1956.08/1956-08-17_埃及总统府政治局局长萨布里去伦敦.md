Title: Egyptian President's Office Political Bureau Director Sabri Goes to London
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 16th: Ali Sabri, Director of the Political Bureau of the Egyptian President's Office, left Cairo by plane on the evening of August 15th for London.

Sabri said before his departure that he was going to London on the orders of Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser regarding the London conference on the Suez Canal issue. He also said that he would not attend any of the London conference meetings.

### 埃及总统府政治局局长萨布里去伦敦

1956-08-17
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及总统府政治局局长萨布里去伦敦
    新华社16日讯　开罗消息：埃及总统府政治局局长阿里·萨布里八月十五日晚上乘飞机离开开罗前往伦敦。
    萨布里在动身前发表谈话说，他因为关于苏彝士运河问题的伦敦会议的事情，奉埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔之命到伦敦去。他又说，他将不参加伦敦会议的任何一次会议。
