Title: Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Issues Appeal Demanding Cessation of Conspiracies to Undermine Sino-Burmese Friendship
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 9th: The Burma-China Friendship Association issued an appeal on August 8, urging the governments of Burma and China to promptly and appropriately handle the existing issues between the two countries in accordance with the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, in order to stop the conspiratorial activities of certain individuals aimed at undermining Burmese-Chinese friendship.

The appeal expressed regret over the rumors currently spreading in Burma that are affecting Burmese-Chinese friendship. The appeal stated that Burma and China are countries that accept the Five Principles, practice peaceful coexistence, and work together to promote Burmese-Chinese friendship and world peace. Therefore, the issues between the two countries are not difficult to resolve.

The appeal called on the people of Burma and China to be vigilant, pay close attention to conspiracies to undermine Burmese-Chinese friendship, and strive to realize the cause of peaceful coexistence.

### 缅中友好协会发表呼吁书  要求制止破坏缅中友谊的阴谋

1956-08-10
第5版()
专栏：

    缅中友好协会发表呼吁书
    要求制止破坏缅中友谊的阴谋
    新华社仰光9日电　缅中友好协会在八月八日发出一项呼吁书，敦促缅中两国政府尽速按照和平共处五项原则，妥善地处理存在于两国之间的有关问题，以制止某些人士进行破坏缅中友谊的阴谋活动。
    呼吁书对于正在缅甸散播着的影响缅中友谊的谣言表示遗憾。呼吁书说，缅中两国是接受五项原则、实行和平共处，一致努力促进缅中友谊和世界和平的国家。因此，两国之间的有关问题是不难获得解决的。
    呼吁书要求缅中两国人民提高警惕，密切注意破坏缅中友谊的阴谋活动，为实现和平共处的事业而努力。
