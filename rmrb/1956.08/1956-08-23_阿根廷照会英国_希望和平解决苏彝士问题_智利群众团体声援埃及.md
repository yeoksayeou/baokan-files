Title: Argentina Sends Note to Britain Hoping for Peaceful Resolution of the Suez Problem; Chilean Mass Organizations Support Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st Dispatch from London: According to Reuters, the Argentine Ambassador to Britain, Mr. Candioti, delivered a formal note to British Foreign Secretary Lloyd on August 20th, stating Argentina's concern over the Suez Canal issue and its hope for a peaceful resolution to the problem.

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st Dispatch from New York: According to TASS, the Chilean newspaper "El Siglo" published a statement from the Chilean National Peace Committee supporting Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company.

The statement said that Chilean peace fighters believe that the forces unwilling to end the Cold War and attempting to turn Egypt into a potential source of conflict will be defeated. The statement pointed out that the joint efforts of the people of all countries can prevent Western countries from armed intervention in Egypt's internal affairs.

The Chilean National Federation of Coal Miners, with 65,000 members, also wrote to the Egyptian Ambassador to Chile, welcoming Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company.

The National Federation of Coal Miners also wrote to the British Ambassador and the French Ambassador, firmly protesting these countries' policy of military threats against Egypt.

### 阿根廷照会英国  希望和平解决苏彝士问题  智利群众团体声援埃及

1956-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    阿根廷照会英国
    希望和平解决苏彝士问题
    智利群众团体声援埃及
    新华社21日讯　据路透社伦敦讯：阿根廷驻英国大使坎迪奥提八月二十日交给英国外交大臣劳埃德一件正式的照会，声明阿根廷关怀苏彝士运河问题，并且希望和平解决这个问题。
    据新华社21日讯　据塔斯社纽约二十一日讯：智利“世纪报”刊载了智利全国和平委员会支持埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的声明。
    声明说，智利和平战士们认为，永远不愿同意结束冷战并且企图把埃及变成冲突的潜在根源的那种势力将遭到失败。声明指出，各国人民的联合努力能够阻止西方国家对埃及内政进行武装干涉。
    拥有六万五千个会员的智利全国煤矿工人联合会也写信给埃及驻智利大使，欢迎埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有。
    全国煤矿工人联合会同时还写信给英国大使和法国大使，坚决抗议这些国家对埃及采取军事威胁的政策。
