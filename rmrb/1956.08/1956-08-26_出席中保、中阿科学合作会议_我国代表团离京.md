Title: Chinese Delegation Leaves Beijing to Attend Sino-Bulgarian and Sino-Albanian Scientific Cooperation Meetings
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 5

    Attending Sino-Bulgarian, Sino-Albanian Scientific Cooperation Meetings
    Chinese Delegation Leaves Beijing

    (Our Newspaper) A seven-member Chinese delegation, headed by Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, left by plane on August 24 for Sofia to attend the second meeting of the Sino-Bulgarian Scientific and Technical Cooperation Committee to be held in Sofia at the end of August, and then proceeded to Tirana to attend the third meeting of the Sino-Albanian Scientific and Technical Cooperation Committee to be held in Tirana at the end of September.

    Among those who saw them off at the airport were: Albanian Ambassador to China, Dello Balili, First Secretary Bino; Bulgarian Commercial Counselor to China, Konstantinov; Vice Minister of Agriculture, Cai Ziwei, Deputy Director of the General Office, Xi Fengzhou and many others.

### 出席中保、中阿科学合作会议  我国代表团离京

1956-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    出席中保、中阿科学合作会议
    我国代表团离京
    本报讯　参加八月底在索非亚举行的中保科学技术合作委员会第二届会议和九月底在地拉那举行的中阿科学技术合作委员会第三届会议的中国代表团一行七人在团长农业部副部长刘瑞龙率领下于八月二十四日乘飞机前往索非亚，在索非亚会议结束后，再转道去地拉那。
    前往机场送行的有：阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使德略·巴利里，一等秘书比诺；保加利亚驻华商务参赞康士坦丁诺夫；农业部副部长蔡子伟、办公厅副主任席凤州等多人。
