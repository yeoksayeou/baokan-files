Title: Harbin Municipal Party and Government Leading Organs Call on the Whole City to Resolutely Conquer the Flood; Fu Zuoyi Goes to Inspect Flood Control Situation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 3

    Harbin Municipal Party and Government Leading Organs
    Call on the Whole City to Resolutely Conquer the Flood
    Fu Zuoyi Goes to Inspect Flood Control Situation
    Xinhua News Agency, August 15th: Minister of Water Conservancy Fu Zuoyi, accompanied by members of the Technical Committee of the Ministry of Water Conservancy and others, took a train from Beijing this afternoon to Harbin to inspect the flood control situation.
    Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, August 15th: The Harbin Municipal People's Committee and the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party issued orders and emergency notices on the evening of the 13th, calling on the people of the city and all Communist Party members to urgently mobilize to conquer the largest flood and fight to protect the safety of Harbin.
    The order of the Harbin Municipal People's Committee pointed out that the current serious task is to make every effort to strengthen the embankment construction, ensure that the second phase of the flood control project plan is completed rapidly before the peak of the second flood arrives, and at the same time complete the heightening and strengthening of the second defense line. At the same time, all preparations should be made for emergency rescue, and any dangerous situations should be discovered and eliminated in a timely manner. The order requires the people of the city to quickly form flood control teams and rear service teams according to units and districts (streets) under the leadership of the municipal and district flood control headquarters; and do a good job in transferring important equipment, resettling disaster victims, strengthening public security, and protecting state equipment and public facilities, etc., in case of emergencies.
    The emergency notice of the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party to all levels of Party organizations and all Party members stated that Party organizations at all levels must make flood control work the central task at present, and flood control work must be placed first. In the flood control struggle, it is necessary to strengthen the Party's leadership, strengthen political and ideological work, and heighten vigilance, oppose complacency and slackness, and prevent sabotage by counter-revolutionaries and bad elements. In the struggle, it is also necessary to strengthen Party and League building work, strengthen the Party's supervision work, and give full play to the role of Party organizations as fortresses.

### 哈尔滨市党政领导机关  号召全市人民坚决战胜洪水  傅作义前往视察防汛情况

1956-08-17
第3版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨市党政领导机关
    号召全市人民坚决战胜洪水
    傅作义前往视察防汛情况
    新华社15日讯　水利部长傅作义偕水利部技术委员会委员等多人，在今日下午由北京乘火车前往哈尔滨视察防汛情况。
    据新华社哈尔滨15日电　哈尔滨市人民委员会和中共哈尔滨市委员会于十三日晚分别发布命令和紧急通知，号召全市人民，全体共产党员紧急动员起来战胜最大洪水，为保卫哈尔滨的安全而斗争。
    哈尔滨市人民委员会命令指出，当前的严重任务是要全力进行堤防加固工程，保证在第二个洪峰峰顶到达之前，迅速完成第二期防汛工程计划，并同时完成第二道防线的加高加固工程。同时，要做好抢险的一切准备工作，及时发现和消除一切险象。命令要求全市人民在市、区防汛指挥部的领导下，迅速地按单位和地区（街道），分别组成防汛队伍和后方服务队；并做好重要器材转移、救济安置灾民和加强治安、保护国家器材和公共设施等各项工作，以防万一情况发生。
    中共哈尔滨市委员会对全市各级党组织并全体党员的紧急通知说：各级党组织必须把作好防汛工作作为当前的中心工作，必须把防汛工作放在第一位。在防汛斗争中必须加强党的领导，加强政治思想工作，高度提高警惕，反对麻痹松劲思想，防止反革命分子和坏分子的破坏。在斗争中还必须加强建党建团工作，加强党的监察工作，充分发挥党组织的堡垒作用。
