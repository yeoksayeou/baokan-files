Title: Chairman Mao Receives Lao Delegation; Premier Zhou Enlai Begins Talks with Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, and all members of the Lao Royal Government Delegation at Qin Zheng Dian in Zhongnanhai at 5:00 PM today.

Present at the reception were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, and Li Xiannian, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; and Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China, held talks with Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, at 2:50 PM today.

Participating in the talks on the Lao side were Vice Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith, and Loum Insixiengmay, Minister of Finance, National Economy, and Planning.

Participating in the talks on the Chinese side were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; and Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs. (Attached Picture)
Chairman Mao posing for a group photograph with members of the Lao Royal Government Delegation. Front row from right to left: Tao Songsanith, Guo Moruo, Tong Suthivong Norarat, Peng Dehuai, Loum Insixiengmay, Zhou Enlai, Katay Don Sasorith, Mao Zedong, Souvanna Phouma, Zhu De, Mrs. Katay, Chen Yun, Mrs. Insixiengmay, Li Jishen, Mrs. Songsanith, Mrs. Sasorith.

### 毛主席接见老挝代表团  周恩来总理同梭发那·富马首相开始会谈

1956-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见老挝代表团
    周恩来总理同梭发那·富马首相开始会谈
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席，今天下午五时在中南海勤政殿接见了老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王和老挝王国政府代表团全体团员。
    接见的时候在座的，有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、李先念，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长张闻天。
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国总理周恩来同老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王，在今天下午二时五十分举行会谈。
    老挝王国方面参加会谈的，有副首相卡代·敦·萨索里特，财政、国家经济和计划部部长仑·英锡相迈。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有国务院副总理陈毅、对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长张闻天。（附图片）
    毛主席同老挝王国政府代表团人员合影。前排自右至左：陶·宋沙尼特、郭沫若、通·苏蒂冯·诺腊特、彭德怀、仑·英锡相迈、周恩来、卡代·敦·萨索里特、毛泽东、梭发那·富马、朱德、卡代夫人、陈云、英锡相迈夫人、李济深、宋沙尼特夫人、萨索里特夫人。
