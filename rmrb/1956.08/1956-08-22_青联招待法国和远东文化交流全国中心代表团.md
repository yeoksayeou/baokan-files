Title: ACYF Hosts Delegation from French National Center for Cultural Exchange with the Far East
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: The All-China Youth Federation hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the delegation from the French National Center for Cultural Exchange with the Far East.

    Wu Han, Vice Chairman of the ACYF, extended a warm welcome to the French friends who had come from afar. He expressed the hope that through the delegation's visit, the friendship and cultural exchange between the Chinese and French people would be further strengthened.
    Serge Lafitte-Dupont, on behalf of all members of the delegation, expressed his gratitude to the hosts for their hospitality. He proposed a toast to the friendship between the Chinese and French people and the growing cultural exchange.

### 青联招待法国和远东文化交流全国中心代表团

1956-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    青联招待法国和远东文化交流全国中心代表团
    据新华社21日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会今晚设宴欢迎法国和远东文化交流全国中心代表团。
    全国青联副主席吴晗在会上向远道而来的法国朋友表示热烈欢迎。他希望通过代表团的访问，中法两国人民的友谊和文化交流将进一步加强起来。
    塞日·拉斐特—杜邦代表全体团员对主人的招待表示感谢，他提议为中法两国人民的友谊和文化交流的日益加强而干杯。
