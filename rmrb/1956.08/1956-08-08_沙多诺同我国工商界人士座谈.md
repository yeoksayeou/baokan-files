Title: Sadono Holds Talks with Chinese Industrial and Commercial Figures

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: Indonesian Speaker of Parliament Sadono and the visiting members of parliament held a discussion today with the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce on the issue of the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce.

    Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, briefed the guests on the organization of the Federation and its aims. Chinese industrial and commercial figures Li Zhuchen, Sun Fuling, Le Songsheng, Lei Shaoyu, and others used the production situations of their own enterprises before and after joint state-private ownership to explain how and why private industry and commerce are moving towards the path of joint state-private ownership. Speaker Sadono believes that China's adoption of peaceful transformation to gradually transition capitalist industry and commerce to socialism is a wise policy. He said that this method can serve as a good reference for the Indonesian government in formulating policies.

    In the morning, Speaker Sadono and others visited the Central Institute for Nationalities and Peking University. At the Institute for Nationalities, the guests were warmly welcomed by students of various ethnic groups. At Peking University, the guests met with Indonesian students and asked about their studies. Vice-President Jiang Longji said that these Indonesian students are studying very well and speak Chinese well. Speaker Sadono instructed the Indonesian students to study well and complete the required courses. Jiang Longji said that they are the bridge for future cultural exchange between China and Indonesia. The guests also visited the Indonesian students' dormitory, the Oriental Languages ​​Department Library, and the Electronic Laboratory.

    Afterwards, the guests went to the Soviet Exhibition Center, visited the Chinese Daily Industrial Products Quality Exhibition, and at the invitation of Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, had lunch with representatives from Chinese Buddhist, Islamic, Christian, and Catholic circles at Guangji Temple. After the meal, they visited the Dongsi Mosque.

    In recent days, the guests in Beijing have also visited the Beijing No. 2 Cotton Mill and the Beijing Gymnasium, toured the Forbidden City and Yonghe Temple, and watched famous Chinese Peking Opera and song and dance performances.

### 沙多诺同我国工商界人士座谈

1956-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    沙多诺同我国工商界人士座谈
    据新华社7日讯　印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺和同来访问的议员们，今天同中华全国工商业联合会就资本主义工商业社会主义改造问题举行座谈。
    中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通向贵宾们介绍了工商联的组织情况和它的宗旨。中国工商界人士李烛尘、孙孚凌、乐松生、雷绍瑜等，以他们自己企业在合营前后的生产情况来说明私营工商业如何和为什么走向公私合营的道路。沙多诺议长认为，中国采取和平改造的办法使资本主义工商业逐步过渡到社会主义是一个英明的政策，他说，这个办法可以作为印度尼西亚政府规定政策的良好参考。
    上午，沙多诺议长等访问了中央民族学院和北京大学。在民族学院，贵宾们受到各族学生的热烈欢迎。在北京大学，贵宾们会见了印度尼西亚留学生，并问及他们的学习情况。副校长江隆基说，这些印度尼西亚留学生学得很好，中国话说得不错。沙多诺议长嘱咐印度尼西亚留学生要好好学完规定的功课。江隆基说，他们是未来中国和印度尼西亚文化交流的桥梁。贵宾们还参观了印度尼西亚留学生的宿舍、东方语文系图书馆和电子实验室等。
    随后，贵宾们前往苏联展览馆，参观了中国的日用工业品质量展览会，并且应中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初邀请，同中国佛教、伊斯兰教、基督教、天主教界人士在广济寺共进午餐。餐后参观了东四清真寺。
    连日来，贵宾们在北京还参观了北京国棉二厂和北京体育馆，游览了故宫和雍和宫，并且观看了中国著名的京剧和歌舞。
