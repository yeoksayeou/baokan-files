Title: New Cotton Harvested in Turpan Basin
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 3
　
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: New cotton has been harvested in the Turpan Basin's more than 210,000 mu of cotton fields since the end of July. By August 6th, the first district of Turpan County had harvested more than 340,000 jin of new cotton.
    According to field forecasts, cotton fields in the Turpan Basin will generally achieve a bumper harvest this year, with an increase of 10% to 20% compared to the bumper harvest of 1955. The 100,000 mu of cotton fields in Turpan County can produce an average of about 300 jin of seed cotton per mu, which can complete this year's production increase plan.

### 吐鲁番盆地采摘新棉

1956-08-17
第3版()
专栏：

    吐鲁番盆地采摘新棉
    据新华社讯　吐鲁番盆地二十一万多亩棉花，从七月底起开始采摘新棉。吐鲁番县第一区到八月六日已摘三十四万多斤新棉。
    根据田间预测，今年吐鲁番盆地棉田将普遍获得丰收，比丰收的1955年要增产一成到二成。吐鲁番县十万亩棉田平均每亩可产籽棉三百斤左右，可完成今年增产计划。
