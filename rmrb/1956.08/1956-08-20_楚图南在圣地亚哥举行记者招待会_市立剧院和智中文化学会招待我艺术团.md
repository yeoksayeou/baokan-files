Title: Chu Tunan Holds Press Conference in Santiago; Municipal Theater and Chilean-Chinese Cultural Society Host Our Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 19th, Santiago Dispatch: Chu Tunan, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, which is here for a performance tour, held a press conference on the evening of August 17th, where he expressed his respect to the Chilean people. After his speech, Deputy Head Zhao Feng introduced Chinese classical drama to the reporters. They also answered questions raised by the reporters.

Salvati, the organizer of the Chinese Art Troupe's performances in Chile, introduced the leaders of the Chinese Art Troupe to Chilean and foreign journalists. Later, the Municipal Theater and the Chilean-Chinese Cultural Society hosted a banquet for the art troupe. Several hundred celebrities from Chilean society and cultural circles attended the banquet.

### 楚图南在圣地亚哥举行记者招待会  市立剧院和智中文化学会招待我艺术团

1956-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    楚图南在圣地亚哥举行记者招待会
    市立剧院和智中文化学会招待我艺术团
    新华社19日讯　圣地亚哥消息：来这里作访问演出的中国艺术团团长楚图南八月十七日晚间举行了记者招待会，他在记者招待会上向智利人民表示敬意。在他讲完话以后，副团长赵沨向记者们介绍了中国的古典戏剧。他们还回答了记者提出的问题。
    中国艺术团在智利的演出的组织者萨尔瓦提把中国艺术团的领导人介绍给智利新闻记者和外国新闻记者。后来，市立剧院和智利—中国文化学会设宴招待艺术团。参加宴会的有智利社会和文化界名流数百人。
