Title: Xiao Ke Hosts Banquet for Berlin People's Police Band
Author:
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd: General Xiao Ke, Deputy Minister of National Defense, hosted a banquet at the Beijing Hotel at 7:00 PM today to welcome all members of the German Democratic Republic's Berlin People's Police Band, led by conductor Willi Kaufmann.

General Xiao Ke gave a speech at the banquet, wishing the band success in its performances in China. Willi Kaufmann, in his reply, thanked the band for the warm welcome and hospitality it had received in Beijing.

During the banquet, Elfriede Voss, the band's soprano soloist, sang an interlude from the Chinese opera "The White Haired Girl," receiving enthusiastic applause from the audience.

Before the banquet began, General Xiao Ke, Deputy Minister of National Defense, received Willi Kaufmann, the conductor of the German Democratic Republic's Berlin People's Police Band, and Heinz Opitz, the deputy conductor, as well as musicians Helmut Baumann, Elfriede Voss, and Rudi Marx.

### 萧克欢宴柏林人民警察乐团

1956-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    萧克欢宴柏林人民警察乐团
    新华社22日讯　国防部副部长萧克上将今天下午七时在北京饭店举行宴会欢迎由团长维利·考夫曼率领的德意志民主共和国柏林人民警察乐团全体团员。
    萧克上将在宴会上致词，预祝乐团在中国的演出获得成功。维利·考夫曼在答词中感谢乐团在北京受到的热烈欢迎和款待。
    宴会上，乐团女高音独唱家埃尔弗莉德·伏司演唱了中国歌剧“白毛女”的插曲，获得全场热烈鼓掌。
    在宴会开始以前，国防部副部长萧克上将接见了德意志民主共和国柏林人民警察乐团团长维利·考夫曼和副团长海茵茨·奥皮茨，乐团音乐家赫尔姆特·保曼、埃尔弗莉德·伏司、鲁笛·玛尔克斯等人。
