Title: World Volleyball Championship Opens in Paris; Chinese Women's Team Defeats Austrian Team in Preliminary Round
Author:
Date: 1956-08-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 30th: The World Volleyball Championship opened today at the Coubertin Stadium in Paris. This is the third Men's Championship and the second Women's Championship. The competition will end on September 12th.

    Athletes from twenty-four countries are participating in the competition. Finland, Pakistan, Greece, and Mexico, which were originally scheduled to participate, have withdrawn.

    The Chinese men's team is in the same group as Brazil and India; the women's team is in the same group as Poland, Austria, and West Germany.

    In the preliminary round of the women's competition, athletes from the Soviet Union, China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and North Korea achieved very good results.

    The Chinese team defeated Austria three to zero. The scores for the three sets were 15-1, 15-2, and 15-3.

    The Soviet team defeated Luxembourg three to zero (15-1, 15-2, 15-0).

    Poland defeated West Germany three to zero (15-0, 15-0, 15-1).

    Czechoslovakia defeated Belgium three to zero (15-1, 15-1, 15-4).

    The Democratic People's Republic of Korea defeated Brazil three to one (15-6, 12-15, 15-3, 15-8).

### 世界排球锦标赛在巴黎开幕  中国女子队在初赛中胜奥地利队

1956-08-31
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    世界排球锦标赛在巴黎开幕
    中国女子队在初赛中胜奥地利队
    新华社布拉格30日电　巴黎消息：世界排球锦标赛今天在巴黎顾拜旦运动场开幕。这是第三届男子冠军赛和第二届女子冠军赛。比赛将在9月12日结束。
    参加比赛的有来自二十四个国家的运动员们。原来准备参加比赛的芬兰、巴基斯坦、希腊和墨西哥退出了比赛。
    中国男子队同巴西队和印度队分在一组；女子队同波兰、奥地利和西德分在一组。
    女子队在初赛中，苏、中、波、捷、朝等国的运动员获得十分良好的成绩。
    中国队以三比○胜奥地利。三局的比数是十五比一，十五比二，十五比三。
    苏联队以三比○胜卢森堡（十五比一，十五比二，十五比○）。
    波兰以三比○胜西德（十五比○，十五比○，十五比一）。
    捷克斯洛伐克以三比○胜比利时（十五比一，十五比一，十五比四）。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国以三比一胜巴西（十五比六，十二比十五，十五比三，十五比八）。
