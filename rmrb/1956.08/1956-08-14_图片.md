Title: Picture
Author: Mo Zongwei
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 3

The newly built flood discharge gate on the Nanliu River in Yulin County, Guangxi Province, can save thirty-three towns, more than 100,000 people, and 60,000 mu of paddy fields on both sides of the Nanliu River from floods and droughts, bringing happiness to the people. This is the Nanliu River flood discharge gate opening, with floodwaters slowly flowing into the fields via Laopotou. Photo by Mo Zongwei
