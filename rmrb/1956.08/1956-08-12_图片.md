Title: Xiao Feng
Author: Xiao Feng
Date: 1956-08-12
Page: 3

    Four transport planes of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force airdropped more than 7,600 catties of cooked food over Yu杭 and 象山 counties and 南韭山 Island in Zhejiang Province on August 4 to relieve the people affected by the floods caused by the typhoon. This is a photo of loading a large number of relief supplies onto the plane.
      Photo by Xiao Feng (Draft from Zhejiang Daily)

### 图片
萧枫
1956-08-12
第3版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军空军某部的四架运输机，八月四日在浙江省余杭、象山两县和南韭山岛等地上空空投了七千六百多斤熟食品，救济因台风引起洪水而受灾的群众。这是把大批救济物资装上飞机。
      萧枫摄（浙江日报社稿）
