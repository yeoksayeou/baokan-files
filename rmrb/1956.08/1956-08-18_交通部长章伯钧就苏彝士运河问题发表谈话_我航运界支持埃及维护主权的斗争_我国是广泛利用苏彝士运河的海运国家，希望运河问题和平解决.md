Title: Transportation Minister Zhang Bojun Speaks on the Suez Canal Issue; Our Shipping Industry Supports Egypt's Struggle to Safeguard Sovereignty; China is a Maritime Nation That Extensively Utilizes the Suez Canal, Hoping for a Peaceful Resolution to the Canal Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Minister of Transportation of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Bojun, met with a Xinhua News Agency reporter today to speak on the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company and the issue of freedom of navigation on oceanic canals and straits. He first pointed out: "The Suez Canal Company is an Egyptian company, and the Suez Canal is an indivisible part of Egyptian territory. The Egyptian government's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company, restoring the Suez Canal to the property of the Egyptian people, is entirely proper, whether from the perspective of national sovereignty, legal basis, or morality." Minister Zhang Bojun then said: "The Egyptian government has established an independent specialized agency with a separate budget to manage the canal and take over its operation and management. The Egyptian government has also stated that it will allocate a significant portion of the canal's revenue to widen and deepen the waterway. The Egyptian government has repeatedly and solemnly guaranteed the freedom of navigation through this canal. Since July 26th, the Suez Canal has been unobstructed, with more ships passing through than in the same period last year. This clearly demonstrates that the specialized agency established by the Egyptian government is already capable of managing this waterway more effectively, and nationalization has had no impact whatsoever on the freedom of navigation through this waterway."

He said: "The United Kingdom, France, and the United States are attempting to use the so-called 'international nature' of the Suez Canal as a pretext to force the Egyptian government to cancel the nationalization and accept a so-called 'management arrangement under an international system,' that is, permanent internationalization. This is open interference in Egypt's internal affairs and a blatant violation of Egyptian sovereignty. This is completely unreasonable."

Regarding China's relationship with the Suez Canal, Minister Zhang said: "The Suez Canal is one of the world's busiest canals. It connects the Mediterranean Sea and the Red Sea, playing a significant role in developing trade between the East and the West. The trade relations between the People's Republic of China and Egypt and other Arab countries are developing, and economic exchanges with Western countries are also increasing year by year. China is a maritime nation that extensively utilizes the Suez Canal. With the development of the country's economic construction, the importance of the Suez Canal to China will undoubtedly increase day by day. Therefore, we cannot help but express great concern about the peaceful resolution of the Suez Canal issue."

Finally, he said: "The Chinese people and the Chinese shipping industry fully support the just struggle of the Egyptian government and people to safeguard national sovereignty, and believe that this struggle will certainly be victorious."

### 交通部长章伯钧就苏彝士运河问题发表谈话  我航运界支持埃及维护主权的斗争  我国是广泛利用苏彝士运河的海运国家，希望运河问题和平解决

1956-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    交通部长章伯钧就苏彝士运河问题发表谈话
    我航运界支持埃及维护主权的斗争
    我国是广泛利用苏彝士运河的海运国家，希望运河问题和平解决
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国交通部章伯钧部长，今天接见新华社记者，就苏彝士运河公司国有化以及关于通洋运河和海峡的通航自由问题发表了谈话。他首先指出：“苏彝士运河公司是一个埃及公司，苏彝士运河是埃及领土不可分割的一部分。埃及政府把苏彝士运河公司收归国有，使苏彝士运河重新成为埃及人民的财产；不论从国家主权、法律根据或者道义方面来说，这个行动是完全正当的。”章伯钧部长接着说：“埃及政府设立了一个独立的、有单独预算的管理运河的专门机构来接管对运河的经营管理工作。埃及政府还表示将从运河收入中拨出大部分经费用于加宽和加深河道。埃及政府一再郑重保证这条运河的航行自由。七月二十六日后，苏彝士运河畅通无阻，通过的船只比去年同期还有所增加。这就清楚地证明，埃及政府所设立的专门机构已经能够更加有效地管理这条水道，国有化一点也没有影响通过这条水道的航行自由。”
    他说：“英、法、美三国企图用苏彝士运河的所谓‘国际性质’为借口，强使埃及政府取消国有化，接受所谓‘建立在国际制度下的一种经营安排’，即永久国际化，这是对于埃及内政的公开干涉，对于埃及主权的粗暴侵犯。这是完全没有道理的。”
    关于我国同苏彝士运河的关系问题，章部长说：“苏彝士运河是世界上运量最大的运河之一。它沟通了地中海和红海的交通，对发展东西方之间的贸易有很大的作用。中华人民共和国同埃及和其他阿拉伯国家的贸易关系正在开展，同时同西方国家的经济交流也在逐年增加。我国是广泛地利用苏彝士运河的一个海运国家，随着国家经济建设事业的发展，苏彝士运河对我国的重要性必将与日俱增。因此，我们对于苏彝士运河的问题和平解决不能不表示很大的关心。”
    最后，他说：“中国人民和中国航运界完全支持埃及政府和人民为维护国家主权而进行的正义斗争，并且相信这一斗争一定能够得到胜利。”
