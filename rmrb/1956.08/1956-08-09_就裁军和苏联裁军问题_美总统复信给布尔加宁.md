Title: U.S. President Replies to Bulganin on Disarmament and Soviet Disarmament Issues
Author:
Date: 1956-08-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 8th: According to TASS, Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Bulganin, received U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Bohlen, on August 7th.

During the meeting, Bohlen handed the Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers U.S. President Eisenhower's reply to Bulganin's letter on the issues of disarmament and the Soviet Union's reduction of its armed forces.

### 就裁军和苏联裁军问题  美总统复信给布尔加宁

1956-08-09
第5版()
专栏：

    就裁军和苏联裁军问题
    美总统复信给布尔加宁
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁八月七日接见美国驻苏大使波伦。
    在接见的时候，波伦把美国总统艾森豪威尔对布尔加宁关于裁军和苏联裁减自己武装部队的问题的信件所写的回信交给苏联部长会议主席。
