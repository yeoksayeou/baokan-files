Title: Many Casualties in West German Factories
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The West German Trade Union Federation's official publication, "Arbeitswelt" (Labor World) weekly, recently published materials stating that nearly two million casualties occurred in West German factories in 1955, of which 5,640 resulted in death. The reasons for these numerous accidents are: the implementation of the "hurry-up" system and the lack of safety regulations.

### 西德工厂伤亡事故多

1956-08-17
第6版()
专栏：

    西德工厂伤亡事故多
    据新华社讯　西德工会联合会的机关刊物“劳动世界”周刊最近一期发表的材料，西德的工厂在1955年发生了将近二百万起伤亡事故，其中有五千六百四十起造成了死亡。造成这许多事故的原因是：厉行“赶快”制度和缺少安全条例。
