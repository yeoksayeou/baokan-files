Title: Vice Chairman Li Weihan Receives British Parliamentarians from Various Parties
Author:
Date: 1956-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th - Li Weihan, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received British parliamentarians from various parties who are currently visiting China this morning.

Present at the meeting were Zhang Wentian, Standing Committee Member of the National People's Congress Standing Committee and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Cai Tingkai, Standing Committee Member of the National People's Congress Standing Committee, and Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, among others.

### 李维汉副委员长接见英国各党议员

1956-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    李维汉副委员长接见英国各党议员
    新华社27日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉今日上午接见了现在中国访问的英国各党议员。
    接见的时候在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会常务委员、外交部副部长张闻天，全国人民代表大会常务委员会常务委员蔡廷锴，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若等人。
