Title: British All-Party Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 17th: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the British All-Party Parliamentary Delegation.

    The delegation arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation members include Members of Parliament from the House of Commons: Yates, Hill, and Mathew from the Conservative Party; Mikardo, Rankin, Hale, and Stones from the Labour Party; Bowen from the Liberal Party; Members of Parliament from the House of Lords: Baron Windlesham from the Liberal Party; Earl of Lucan from the Labour Party.

    The head of the delegation is rotated among the members. Mathew, the head of the delegation today, thanked the host at tonight's banquet for giving them the opportunity to visit China. He said that the delegation hopes that this visit will enhance understanding of China.

    At the beginning of the banquet, President Zhang Xiruo made a speech saying that there have been many mutual visits between friendly people of China and Britain, and this visit of distinguished guests to our country further increases the opportunity for mutual understanding between our two peoples.

    Present at the banquet were Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Qiao Guanhua, Vice-Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and some directors, as well as celebrities from all walks of life in China. British Chargé d'Affaires O'Neill also attended the banquet.

### 英国各党议员访华团到京

1956-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    英国各党议员访华团到京
    据新华社17日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚设宴欢迎英国各党议员访华团。
    访华团是在今天下午乘飞机到达北京的。访华团成员包括下院议员：保守党的耶兹、希尔、马修，工党的米卡多、兰金、黑尔、斯通兹，自由党的鲍恩；上院议员：自由党的温德耳夏姆男爵，工党的庐肯伯爵。
    访华团团长是由每个成员轮流担任。今天担任团长的马修在今晚宴会上对主人使他们能有机会来中国访问表示感谢。他说，访华团希望这次访问能增进对中国的了解。
    宴会开始时，张奚若会长曾致词说，中英之间友好人士的相互访问已经有了许多次，这次贵宾们来访我国，更增加了我们两国人民之间相互了解的机会。
    出席宴会的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通，中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、陈翰笙、乔冠华，秘书长吴茂荪和部分理事，以及中国各界社会名流。英国代办欧念儒也出席了宴会。
