Title: Dutch and Danish Communist Parties Voice Support for the German Communist Party's Struggle; French Workers Protest the West German Authorities' Fascist Actions
Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: According to a report by the TASS news agency from The Hague on August 23rd, the Dutch newspaper "De Waarheid" reported that the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Netherlands passed a letter to the Dutch people regarding the West German authorities' banning of the German Communist Party, calling on all Dutch people to protest the Bonn authorities' arbitrary actions.

The plenary session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Netherlands was held on August 20th and 21st, during which issues related to the Party Congress to be held from October 5th to 7th were discussed.

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: Copenhagen dispatch: The Central Committee of the Danish Communist Party wrote a letter to the German Communist Party, condemning the West German Federal Constitutional Court for illegally banning the German Communist Party, and guaranteeing to stand in solidarity with the German Communist Party and jointly fight for peace, democracy, and socialism.

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: According to a TASS dispatch from Paris on August 23rd: The General Confederation of Labour of France, representing millions of French workers, protested against the West German authorities' illegal banning of the German Communist Party. It issued a statement saying that the Bonn authorities' actions in banning the German Communist Party, dissolving many anti-fascist organizations, and persecuting trade union members and workers' representatives in factory committees are all aimed at those who oppose arming Germany and opposing the revival of militarism.

### 荷共和丹共声援德共斗争  法国劳动人民抗议西德当局的法西斯行径

1956-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

    荷共和丹共声援德共斗争
    法国劳动人民抗议西德当局的法西斯行径
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社海牙二十三日讯：据荷兰“真理报”报道：荷兰共产党中央委员会全体会议就西德当局禁止德国共产党的问题通过告荷兰人民书，号召全体荷兰人民对波恩当局的专横行为表示抗议。
    荷兰共产党中央委员会全体会议在八月二十日和二十一日举行，会议讨论了同将在十月五日到七日举行的党代表大会有关的问题。
    据新华社24日讯　哥本哈根消息：丹麦共产党中央委员会写信给德国共产党，谴责西德联邦宪法法院非法禁止德国共产党，并且保证同德国共产党团结一致，共同为和平、民主和社会主义而斗争。
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社巴黎二十三日讯：拥有几百万法国劳动者的法国总工会执行局抗议西德当局非法取缔德国共产党。它发表声明说，波恩当局禁止德国共产党、解散许多反法西斯组织以及迫害工会会员和工厂委员会中劳动者的代表等行动都是用来对付那些反对武装德国、反对复活军国主义的人的。
