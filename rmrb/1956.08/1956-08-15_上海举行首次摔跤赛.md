Title: Shanghai Holds First Wrestling Competition
Author:
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 4
Sports News

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 13th: Zhou Songlin, a worker from the Shanghai Electric Tram Company, threw his opponent nine times during the wrestling competition held on the 12th, winning first place in the light heavyweight division. His opponent, Wang Chenglin, also a worker from the Electric Tram Company, is young and strong and a leading member of the Shanghai Electric Tram Company wrestling team. During the match, he also threw Zhou Songlin five times. This tense and exciting match drew constant cheers from the more than 2,000 spectators.
    This wrestling competition was the first held in Shanghai since liberation. A total of 47 athletes participated in this wrestling competition in the flyweight, lightweight, middleweight, light heavyweight, and heavyweight divisions, including 13 electric tram workers.

### 上海举行首次摔跤赛

1956-08-15
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    上海举行首次摔跤赛
    新华社上海13日电　上海市电车公司工人周松霖在十二日举行的摔跤比赛中，把他的对手摔倒了九次，获得次重级第一名。他的对手王成林也是电车公司的工人，年轻力壮，是上海市电车公司摔跤队的一名健将。比赛中，他也曾把周松霖摔倒了五次。这场比赛紧张激烈，引起了二千多观众不断的喝采。
    摔跤比赛是上海解放以后第一次举行的。参加这次摔跤比赛共有次轻、轻量、中量、次重和重量五个级的运动员四十七人，其中有十三个是电车工人。
