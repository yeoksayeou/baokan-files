Title: Yongdeng Cement Plant Begins Installation (Photo)
Author: Hao Changgeng
Date: 1956-08-09
Page: 2

    Yongdeng Cement Plant Begins Installation
    The Yongdeng Cement Plant in Gansu Province, which is under construction, has entered the installation phase. After completion, this factory will be able to produce a large amount of cement for the construction of Gansu and other places, and for the Liujiaxia Hydropower Project. The photo shows workers performing aerial work on the 70-meter-high chimney at the tail of the rotary kiln. Xinhua News Agency reporter Hao Changgeng

### 永登水泥厂开始安装（图片）
郝常耕
1956-08-09
第2版()
专栏：

    永登水泥厂开始安装
    正在建设中的甘肃省永登水泥厂，已经进入安装阶段。这个工厂建成后，能生产大量水泥，供应甘肃等地建设和刘家峡水力枢纽工程使用。这是工人们正在旋窑尾部高达七十公尺的烟囱上，进行高空作业的情形。新华社记者    郝常耕摄
