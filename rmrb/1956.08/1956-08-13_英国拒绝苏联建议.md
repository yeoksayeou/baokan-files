Title: Britain Rejects Soviet Proposal
Author:
Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 12th: According to a Reuters report from London: A British Foreign Office spokesman announced on August 11th that the British government has notified the Soviet government that it cannot accept the Soviet government's proposal regarding the Suez Canal conference.

### 英国拒绝苏联建议

1956-08-13
第5版()
专栏：

    英国拒绝苏联建议
    新华社12日讯　据路透社伦敦讯：英国外交部发言人八月十一日宣布，英国政府已经通知苏联政府，说它不能接受苏联政府提出的关于苏彝士运河会议的建议。
