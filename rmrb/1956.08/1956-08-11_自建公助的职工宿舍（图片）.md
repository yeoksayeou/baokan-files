Title: Self-built, Publicly Assisted Employee Housing (Picture)
Author: Li Jiuling
Date: 1956-08-11
Page: 2

    Self-built, Publicly Assisted Employee Housing
    In order to solve the housing problems of its employees, the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Engineering Bureau adopted the method of self-construction with public assistance to help employees build twelve residential buildings near Jianqiao New Village. More than 100 employee families have already moved into their new homes. By the end of July, the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Engineering Bureau had used this method and other methods to help 1,500 employee families solve their housing problems. The picture shows the newly built employee housing near Jianqiao New Village. (Photo by Li Jiuling, Xinhua News Agency)

### 自建公助的职工宿舍（图片）
李九令
1956-08-11
第2版()
专栏：

    自建公助的职工宿舍
    武汉长江大桥工程局，为了解决职工的住房问题，在建桥新村附近，采取自建公助的办法，帮助职工盖好了十二幢住房。现在已经有一百多户职工搬进了新居。到七月底止，武汉长江大桥工程局采用这种办法和其他办法，已帮助一千五百户职工解决了住房问题。图为建桥新村附近新建的职工住房。　新华社记者　李九令摄
