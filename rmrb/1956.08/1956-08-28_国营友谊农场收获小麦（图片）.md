Author: Zhang Wenli
Date: 1956-08-28
Page: 3

State-Run Friendship Farm Harvests Wheat
The 16,000 hectares of spring wheat, planted for the first time on the newly reclaimed wasteland at the State-Run Friendship Farm last year, are now being harvested intensively. Preliminary estimates indicate an average yield of 1.66 tons per hectare, with the highest reaching 2.5 tons.

    Above: This is the scene of the farm's combine harvesters harvesting wheat.
    Left: This is the winnowing machine at the farm's Third Branch winnowing wheat. It can winnow 20 tons of wheat per hour.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Wenli photo

### 国营友谊农场收获小麦（图片）
张文礼
1956-08-28
第3版()
专栏：

    国营友谊农场收获小麦
    国营友谊农场在去年开垦的生荒地上第一次播种的一万六千公顷春小麦正在紧张收割。据初步估计，每公顷小麦平均可产一点六六吨，最高的可产二点五吨。
    上图：这是农场的联合收割机正在收割小麦的情形。
    左图：这是农场第三分场的扬场机正在扬麦子。它每小时能扬麦子二十吨。
    新华社记者　张文礼摄
