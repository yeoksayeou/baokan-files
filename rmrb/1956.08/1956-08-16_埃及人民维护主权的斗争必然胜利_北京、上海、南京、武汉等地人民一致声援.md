Title: The Egyptian People's Struggle to Safeguard Sovereignty Will Surely Be Victorious. People in Beijing, Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan, and Other Places Unanimously Voice Support

Author:
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 15th - Muslims of all nationalities in Beijing held a symposium this afternoon at the Dongsi Pailou Mosque to support Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company. The symposium was hosted by the China Islamic Association.

Ma Yu Huai, Vice Chairman of the Islamic Association, said at the meeting: The meeting to be held in London tomorrow has no right to make any decisions on the Suez Canal issue. Ma Yu Huai had been to Egypt twice. He said, "When we were in Egypt, we told our Egyptian friends that whether in times of peace or difficulty, Chinese Muslims and all Chinese people will be friends of the Egyptian people." Ahong Wen Sheng San quoted two sentences from the Koran, calling on all Chinese Muslims to support the Egyptian people. Muslim professor Pang Shi Qian praised the unanimous support given to Egypt by Arab countries, including those participating in the Baghdad Pact. He said, "This is a good example of the Bandung spirit."

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 15th - More than 10,000 workers, students, government employees, professors, writers, and women in Shanghai held a grand rally at the Cultural Plaza tonight to support Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company.

The congress issued a telegram stating: "The people of Shanghai firmly stand on the side of the heroic Egyptian people, fully support Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company, and oppose any military threats and conspiracies by Western countries. We warmly applaud the solemn statement by Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser declaring the Egyptian government's refusal to participate in the London conference and his sincere desire to seek a peaceful resolution to international disputes."

Tonight's congress was jointly initiated by fifteen people's organizations in Shanghai. The congress was presided over by Jin Zhonghua, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and a well-known peace activist.

Zhong Min, Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, spoke at the meeting on behalf of more than one million workers in Shanghai, expressing support for Egypt's just struggle. Renowned writer Ba Jin, President of Jiaotong University Peng Kang, member of the China Islamic Association Ma Renbin Ahong, scientist Shen Ke Fei, and renowned artist Zhou Xinfang also spoke at the meeting, all expressing their firm support for the just struggle of the Egyptian people.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, August 15th - More than 2,000 youths from all walks of life in Nanjing gathered on the evening of the 14th to support Egypt's struggle to recover the Suez Canal Company. Cui Pei Du, a representative of the Nanjing Student Union and two other groups, gave a detailed introduction to the progress of the Egyptian people's anti-colonialist struggle at the meeting. The meeting unanimously passed a letter from the youth of Nanjing to the youth of Egypt. The letter stated: "We, the more than two thousand participating youth, represent the two hundred thousand youth of Nanjing in supporting the struggle of the Egyptian people."

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, August 15th - More than 1,000 youths from all walks of life in Wuhan held a rally today to support the just struggle of the Egyptian people to nationalize the Suez Canal Company. The congress passed a telegram of salute to the youth and people of Egypt.

Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, August 15th - The members attending the Second Session of the First Committee of the Qingdao Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Shandong Province yesterday unanimously expressed their support for Egypt's just action in nationalizing the Suez Canal Company. Zeng Chengkui, a member of the Qingdao Municipal Committee of the CPPCC and Deputy Director of the Institute of Oceanography of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, said that a country that has been oppressed for hundreds of years has stood up, which is a major event. The Chinese people fully sympathize with and support Egypt. Zhang Xiaowei, an Associate Researcher at the Institute of Oceanography of the Chinese Academy of Sciences who had been to the Suez Canal, recalled witnessing the tragic lives of the Egyptian people under the cruel exploitation and oppression of imperialism. He was happy for the Egyptian people's triumph today. The committee members expressed great anger at the unreasonable actions of Britain and France in threatening Egypt with force.

### 埃及人民维护主权的斗争必然胜利  北京、上海、南京、武汉等地人民一致声援

1956-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及人民维护主权的斗争必然胜利
    北京、上海、南京、武汉等地人民一致声援
    据新华社15日讯　在北京的各民族穆斯林今天下午在东四牌楼清真寺内举行座谈会，支持埃及将苏彝士运河公司收归国有。座谈会由中国伊斯兰教协会主持。
    伊斯兰教协会副主任马玉槐阿訇在会上说：明天将在伦敦召开的会议无权对苏彝士运河问题作任何决定。马玉槐阿訇曾经到过埃及两次。他说：“在埃及的时候，我们曾告诉埃及朋友们说，不论在和平或困难的时候，中国穆斯林和所有中国人民都将是埃及人民的朋友。”闻省三阿訇引了可兰经中的两句话，号召所有中国穆斯林给埃及人民以支持。穆斯林教授庞士谦赞扬阿拉伯国家，包括参加巴格达条约的国家，给埃及的一致支持。他说：“这是万隆精神的一个很好的范例。”
    据新华社上海15日电　上海的工人、学生、机关工作者、教授、作家、妇女共一万多人，今晚在文化广场举行盛大集会，支持埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有。
    大会发出通电说：“上海市各界人民坚决地站在英雄的埃及人民一边，完全支持埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有，反对西方国家的任何武力威胁和阴谋诡计。我们热烈赞同埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔宣布埃及政府拒绝参加伦敦会议的严正声明和他寻求和平解决国际争端的真诚愿望。”
    今晚的大会是由上海市十五个人民团体联合发起的。主持大会的是中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海市分会副主席、著名和平人士金仲华。
    上海市工会联合会主席钟民在会上代表上海一百多万工人表示支持埃及的正义斗争。著名作家巴金、交通大学校长彭康、中国伊斯兰教协会委员马人斌教长、科学家沈克非、著名艺术家周信芳等都在会上讲了话，他们都表示坚决支持埃及人民的正义斗争。
    据新华社南京15日电　南京市各界青年二千多人在十四日晚集会支援埃及收回苏彝士运河公司的斗争。南京市学生联合会等三个团体的代表崔佩笃在会上详细介绍了埃及人民反殖民主义斗争的进展情况。会上一致通过了南京市青年致埃及青年的一封信。信上说：“我们两千多个与会青年代表着南京市二十万青年支援埃及人民的斗争。”
    据新华社武汉15日电　武汉市各界一千多青年今天举行大会，声援埃及人民把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的正义斗争。大会通过了一封向埃及青年和人民的致敬电。
    据新华社青岛15日电　出席政协山东省青岛市第一届委员会第二次会议的委员昨天一致表示，支持埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的正义行动。青岛市政协委员、中国科学院海洋生物研究室副主任曾呈奎说，一个几百年来受压迫的国家站起来了，这是一件大事。中国人民完全同情和支持埃及。曾经到过苏彝士运河的中国科学院海洋生物研究室副研究员张孝威，回忆了目睹埃及人民在帝国主义残酷剥削压迫下的悲惨生活。他为埃及人民今天的扬眉吐气而高兴。委员们对英、法两国用武力威胁埃及的无理举动都表示极大的愤怒。
