Title: Danish Prime Minister Advocates Resolving the Suez Canal Issue Through Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, August 25th, Copenhagen News: Danish Prime Minister and Foreign Minister H.C. Hansen said on August 24th after returning to Copenhagen from London that there are great differences of opinion on the Suez Canal issue. He emphasized that in modern society, differences must be resolved through negotiations.

Hansen believes that Egypt will agree to hold negotiations on the Suez Canal issue.

### 丹麦首相主张通过谈判  解决苏彝士运河问题

1956-08-26
第6版()
专栏：

    丹麦首相主张通过谈判
    解决苏彝士运河问题
    新华社25日讯　哥本哈根消息：丹麦首相兼外交大臣汉森8月24日从伦敦回到哥本哈根以后发表谈话说：在苏彝士运河问题上，大家的观点存在着很大的分歧。他着重指出：在现代社会中，必须通过谈判解决分歧。
    汉森认为，埃及会同意就苏彝士运河问题举行谈判。
