Title: Chinese Track and Field Athletes Break Five More National Records in Kyiv
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th - Chinese track and field athletes broke five more national records during a friendly competition with Soviet track and field athletes in Kyiv on the 18th.

    In the men's 400-meter intermediate hurdles, Chen Youcai broke Wang Yong'an's 1953 national record of 56.8 seconds with a time of 55.5 seconds.

    Male athlete Zhou Lianli's time in the 200-meter low hurdles was 25.4 seconds, breaking the national record of 25.8 seconds set by Li Yongqing and Yu Zhangyan in 1954.

    In the men's 4x400 meter relay, the Chinese relay team achieved a time of 3 minutes 22.4 seconds, breaking the national record of 3 minutes 28.8 seconds set by the Chinese People's Liberation Army relay team in 1953.

    Female athlete Yang Yumin achieved a long jump of 5 meters 45 centimeters, breaking her own national record of 5 meters 18 centimeters set last year for the fourth time. Yang Yumin's previous best record was 5 meters 40 centimeters. Another female athlete, Wang Jianxia, achieved a long jump of 5 meters 41 centimeters, and she also broke this national record.

    In the women's 4x200 meter relay, the first and second teams of the Chinese relay team achieved times of 1 minute 45 seconds and 1 minute 47.6 seconds respectively, both breaking the national record of 1 minute 55.5 seconds set by the Chinese People's Liberation Army Southwest Military Region relay team in 1952.

### 中国田径运动员在基辅又打破五项全国纪录

1956-08-21
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中国田径运动员在基辅又打破五项全国纪录
    新华社莫斯科20日电　中国田径队运动员十八日在基辅同苏联田径运动员举行友谊比赛的时候，又打破了五项全国纪录。
    在男子四百公尺中栏比赛中，陈有才以55.5秒的成绩打破了王永安在1953年创造的56.8秒的全国纪录。
    男运动员周连立二百公尺低栏的成绩是25.4秒，打破了李永清和俞樟炎两个人在1954年创造的25.8秒的全国纪录。
    在男子四人一千六百公尺接力比赛中，中国接力队的成绩达到了3分22.4秒，打破了中国人民解放军接力队在1953年创造的3分28.8秒的全国纪录。
    女运动员杨玉敏跳远的成绩达到了5公尺45公分，第四次打破了她在去年创造的5公尺18公分的全国纪录。杨玉敏过去最好的成绩是5公尺40公分。另一名女运动员王剑侠的跳远成绩达到了5公尺41公分，她也打破了这项全国纪录。
    在女子四人八百公尺接力比赛中，中国接力队第一队和第二队的成绩分别达到了1分45秒和1分47.6秒，都打破了中国人民解放军西南军区接力队在1952年创造的1分55.5秒的全国纪录。
