Title: Chinese Youth Organizations Send Congratulatory Telegram to World Conference Against Atomic Bombs
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 4th: The All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation today sent a telegram to the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, congratulating them on the opening of the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs. The telegram stated: Chinese youth and students fully support your just demands and sincerely wish you greater contributions to the cause of safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

### 我国青年团体电贺禁止原子弹世界大会

1956-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我国青年团体电贺禁止原子弹世界大会
    据新华社4日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会今天打电报给日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会，祝贺第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的召开。电报说：中国青年和学生全力支持你们的正义要求，并衷心祝贺你们在维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中作出更大的贡献。
