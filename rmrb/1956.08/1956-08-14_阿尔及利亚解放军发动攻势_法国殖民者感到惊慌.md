Title: Algerian Liberation Army Launches Offensive; French Colonialists Alarmed
Author:
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, August 13th: The Algerian National Liberation Army launched a fierce offensive against French colonial troops south of Algiers on August 12th. According to the Associated Press, this is the largest attack launched by the National Liberation Army in twenty-one months.

In this attack, the National Liberation Army's achievements were: twenty French soldiers killed and twenty wounded.

This attack alarmed the French colonialists in Algiers. The French military and police authorities conducted a large-scale search in the city on this day and detained thousands of people.

### 阿尔及利亚解放军发动攻势  法国殖民者感到惊慌

1956-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚解放军发动攻势
    法国殖民者感到惊慌
    新华社13日讯　阿尔及尔消息：阿尔及利亚民族解放军八月十二日在阿尔及尔南部对法国殖民军队展开猛烈的攻势。据合众社说，这是民族解放军二十一个月以来发动的最大的一次攻击。
    在这次攻击中，民族解放军的战果是：法军二十人死亡，二十人受伤。
    这次攻击使得阿尔及尔的法国殖民者感到惊慌。法国军事和警察当局这一天在城内进行了大搜查，并且扣留了数千人。
