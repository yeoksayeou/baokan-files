Title: Fengman Hydropower Station Adds Another Generator
Author:
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 2

    Fengman Hydropower Station
    Adds Another Generator
    Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, August 12th. Another newly installed giant hydroelectric generator at the Fengman Hydropower Station was officially put into operation at 00:00 on the 11th and began generating electricity. With the installation of one more hydroelectric generator, the entire reconstruction plan of this hydropower station will be completed.

    In the past four years, the giant hydroelectric generators installed during the reconstruction of the Fengman Hydropower Station have been put into operation one after another, greatly increasing the power supply in Northeast China. The newly installed hydroelectric generators during the reconstruction of the Fengman Hydropower Station are all the latest equipment supplied by the Soviet Union. The Fengman Hydropower Station has now become a new type of large-scale hydropower station.

### 丰满水电站  又增加一台发电机

1956-08-14
第2版()
专栏：

    丰满水电站
    又增加一台发电机
    新华社长春12日电　丰满水电站又有一台新安装好的巨型水轮发电机，从十一日零时起正式投入运转，开始发电。这个水电站只要再安装一台水轮发电机，整个改建计划就要全部完成了。
    最近四年，丰满水电站在改建中安装的一台台巨型水轮发电机已经陆续投入运行，大大增加了东北地区的电源。丰满水电站在改建中新安装的水轮发电机都是苏联供应的最新设备。现在丰满水电站已经变成了一座新型的大型水电站。
