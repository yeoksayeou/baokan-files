    Preventing Robbery by "Passengers"
    Recently, the West German Taxi Workers Union decided to install a tear gas device in every car. This is to prevent drivers from being robbed by some American passengers.

### 防止“乘客”的抢劫

1956-08-20
第6版()
专栏：国际点滴

    防止“乘客”的抢劫
    最近西德出租汽车工人联合会决定在每辆汽车上装一个催泪瓦斯设备。这是为了防止司机受一些美国乘客的抢劫。
