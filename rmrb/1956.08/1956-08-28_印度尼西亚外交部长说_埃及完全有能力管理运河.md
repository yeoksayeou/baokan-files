Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Says Egypt is Fully Capable of Managing the Canal
Author:
Date: 1956-08-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Port Said, August 27th: Indonesian Foreign Minister Abdulgani said on August 26th, after visiting the port facilities and navigation management center of Ismailia on the Suez Canal, that the visit convinced him that navigation on the Suez Canal is free and that Egypt is fully capable of managing and maintaining the waterway.

　　During his visit, Abdulgani reiterated that he believed the Suez Canal issue could be resolved peacefully.

　　When asked about Indonesia's attitude in the event of a war over the Suez Canal issue, he said, "We should be optimistic rather than negative, and we should actively strive to safeguard world peace."

　　While in Ismailia, Abdulgani had lunch with Mahmoud Younis, Chairman of the Egyptian Suez Canal Authority, and some newly appointed Egyptian officials.

### 印度尼西亚外交部长说  埃及完全有能力管理运河

1956-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外交部长说
    埃及完全有能力管理运河
    新华社27日讯　塞得港消息：印度尼西亚外交部长阿卜杜加尼8月26日在参观了苏彝士运河上的伊士美利亚的港口设施和航行管理中心以后说，这次访问使他确信苏彝士运河的航行是自由的，埃及完全有能力管理和维护这条水道。
    阿卜杜加尼在参观的时候再次表示他认为苏彝士运河问题可以和平解决。
    在被问到一旦在苏彝士运河问题上发生战争，印度尼西亚的态度如何的时候，他说，“我们应当乐观而不消极，我们应当为保卫世界和平积极努力。”
    阿卜杜加尼在伊士美利亚的时候，曾经同埃及苏彝士运河管理委员会主席马哈茂德·尤尼斯和一些新任命的埃及官员共进了午餐。
