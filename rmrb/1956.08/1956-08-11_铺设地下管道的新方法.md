Title: A New Method for Laying Underground Pipelines
Author:
Date: 1956-08-11
Page: 2

    A New Method for Laying Underground Pipelines
    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch) In Qipan Street, Beijing, workers are laying underground drainage pipes with a diameter of 1.6 meters, while streetcars, cars, and pedestrians are passing by as usual.
    This construction method—the double-sided pipe jacking method—that does not require digging up the road surface to lay sewers is being used for the first time in Beijing. This construction method was learned by the Beijing Municipal Water Supply and Sewerage Engineering Bureau from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union.

### 铺设地下管道的新方法

1956-08-11
第2版()
专栏：

    铺设地下管道的新方法
    据新华社讯　在北京棋盘街，工人们正在地下铺设着直径粗一点六公尺的下水管道，而街上的电车、汽车和行人仍像往常一样穿梭往来。
    这种不用挖开路面就能铺设下水道的施工方法——双面顶管的施工方法，在北京还是第一次采用。这种施工方法是北京市上下水道工程局学习来的苏联先进经验。
