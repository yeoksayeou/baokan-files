Title: China's Lüda Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Ulaanbaatar for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th: Ulaanbaatar Dispatch: China's Lüda Song and Dance Troupe has concluded its visit and performances in Mongolia, and left Ulaanbaatar by train for the Soviet Union at 6:30 PM on the 13th.

    The song and dance troupe arrived in Ulaanbaatar on July 7th. During its thirty-plus days in Mongolia, it performed 43 shows at the Ulaanbaatar National Theater, the Naadam Open-Air Theater, and in factories, mines, and four provinces including Selenge Province, receiving a warm welcome and praise from over 37,000 audience members.

    During its time in Mongolia, the song and dance troupe learned some Mongolian songs and dances.

### 我国旅大歌舞团  离乌兰巴托前往苏联

1956-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

    我国旅大歌舞团
    离乌兰巴托前往苏联
    据新华社14日讯　乌兰巴托消息：中国旅大歌舞团已经结束在蒙古的访问演出，在十三日下午六时半乘火车离开乌兰巴托前往苏联。
    歌舞团是在七月七日到达乌兰巴托的。在蒙古的三十多天中，先后在乌兰巴托国家剧院、那达慕露天剧场以及各厂、矿和色楞格省等四个省演出了四十三场，受到了三万七千多观众的热烈欢迎和称赞。
    歌舞团在蒙古期间学会了一些蒙古歌曲和蒙古舞蹈。
