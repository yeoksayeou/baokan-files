Title: Work Hard to Build Strong Bodies, Each Generation Stronger Than the Last (Pictures)
Author: Gao Liang, Liu Changzhong
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 8

    Work Hard to Build Strong Bodies, Each Generation Stronger Than the Last
    The first National Youth Sports Meet, held in Qingdao for five days, successfully concluded on the afternoon of August 16th. The 1,816 young athletes from all regions of the country, including twenty ethnic groups, through this intense, lively, and beautiful competition and performance, not only created many new achievements, but also produced more sports talents, showing that they are promising new forces in the country's sports movement.
          Our reporters Gao Liang and Liu Changzhong took these photos.
    A panoramic view of the closing ceremony of the first National Youth Sports Meet.
    In the girls' junior group shot put final, Xue Yin'xian from the Shandong team won first place with a score of 9.14 meters.
    Wu Ren from the Fujian team cleared 1.74 meters with the scissor technique, winning second place in the boys' junior group high jump final and reaching the standard of a national second-level athlete.
    The kindergarten children performed dumbbell exercises for their older brothers and sisters and also won awards. Look how happy they are!
    During the girls' junior group 80-meter low hurdles race.
    Tian Maijiu from the Shandong team is a "Three Good" student at Qingdao No. 2 Middle School. In the boys' junior group 800-meter final, he led all the way and won first place.
    Li Peiyu and Li Peirong, brothers from Guiyang, Guizhou, and Ma Chunxi and Ma Chunling, sisters from Kaifeng, Henan, met here.
    Zhang Xiufang from the Beijing team won first place in the girls' junior group all-around gymnastics.
    These are the six young athletes who reached the standard of national second-level athletes. From left: Sun Tingwei (Shanghai), Zhang Meixin (Beijing), Wu Ren (Fujian), Tan Zhongwei (Beijing), Quan Bidan (Jilin), Li Jidong (Shandong).
    Young athletes from Yunnan Province at Qingdao First Bathing Beach.
    Hoop dance performance. A commemorative inscription.

### 努力锻炼身体  一代强过一代（图片）
高粮  刘长忠
1956-08-27
第8版()
专栏：

    努力锻炼身体  一代强过一代
    在青岛举行历时五天的全国第一届少年体育运动大会，于八月十六日下午胜利闭幕了。来自全国各个地区，包括二十个民族的一千八百一十六名少年运动员，经过了这次紧张、活泼、优美的比赛和表演，不仅创造出了许多新的成绩，而且涌现出更多的体育运动能手，显示出他们是祖国体育运动中很有希望的新生力量。
          本报记者　高粮、刘长忠摄
    全国第一届少年体育运动大会闭幕式全景。
    少年女子甲组推铅球决赛中，山东队薛荫娴以9.14公尺的成绩取得了第一名。
    福建队吴任以剪式越过了1.74公尺，获得男子少年甲组跳高决赛第二名，达到国家二级运动员的标准。
    幼儿园的孩子们为哥哥、姐姐们表演了哑铃操，又得了奖。看他们是多么的高兴啊！
    女子少年甲组八十公尺低栏比赛途中。
    山东队的田麦久是青岛二中的“三好”学生，在男子少年甲组八百公尺决赛中，他一路领先，取得第一名。
    来自贵州贵阳的李培瑜、李培荣兄弟俩和来自河南开封的马春喜、马春玲姊妹俩，在这里认识了。
    女子少年甲组体操全能，北京队张秀芳获第一名。
    这是达到国家二级运动员标准的六名少年运动员。左起：孙廷慰（上海）、张梅馨（北京）、吴任（福建）、檀中维（北京）、全必丹（吉林）、李季冬（山东）。
    云南省少年运动员们在青岛第一海水浴场。
    藤圈操表演。题字留念。
