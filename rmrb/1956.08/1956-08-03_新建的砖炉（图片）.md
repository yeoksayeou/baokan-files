Title: Newly Built Brick Furnace
Author: Wang Chaozhen
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 2

Newly Built Brick Furnace
The Shanghai No. 1 Steel Plant has built a new brick furnace, which can increase steel production by 200 tons per month. This is the scene of workers installing the chimney of the brick furnace.

\tWang Chaozhen (Shanghai Labor News Agency)

### 新建的砖炉（图片）
王朝桢
1956-08-03
第2版()
专栏：

    新建的砖炉
    上海第一钢厂新建了一座砖炉，每月可增产二百吨钢。这是工人在安装砖炉的烟囱的情形。
                     王朝桢摄　（上海劳动报社稿）
