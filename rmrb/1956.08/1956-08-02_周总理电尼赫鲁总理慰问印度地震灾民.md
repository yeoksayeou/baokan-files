Title: Premier Zhou Sends Condolences to Prime Minister Nehru Regarding Earthquake Victims in India
Author:
Date: 1956-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Recently, an earthquake occurred in Kutch, India. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India on July 28, expressing his deep condolences on behalf of the Chinese government and people to the Indian government and the earthquake victims.

### 周总理电尼赫鲁总理慰问印度地震灾民

1956-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电尼赫鲁总理慰问印度地震灾民
    新华社1日讯　最近印度喀齐邦曾发生地震。中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在七月二十八日打电报给印度共和国总理尼赫鲁，代表中国政府和人民向印度政府和地震受害者表示深切的慰问。
