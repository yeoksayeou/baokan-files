Title: Publish More Books Introducing China's Musical Knowledge
Author: Chun Geng Yu
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 8

Column: From the Readers

    Publish More Books Introducing China's Musical Knowledge
    I am a university student, and if someone asks me: What was the state of ancient Chinese music? I can only blush and shake my head at him.
    Who were the great composers and musicians of ancient China, and what great musical works did they produce? There are simply too few new books on this subject. Here, I want to appeal: music educators, music publishing houses, please give us some knowledge about China's rich national music!
             Chun Geng Yu

### 多出版一些介绍祖国的音乐知识的书
春耕芋
1956-08-05
第8版()
专栏：读者中来

    多出版一些介绍祖国的音乐知识的书
    我是一个大学生，要是有人问我：祖国古代的音乐状况怎样？我只能红着脸、摇摇头对着他。
    祖国古代有那些伟大的作曲家、音乐家，有些什么伟大的音乐作品？这方面新出的书实在太少了。在这里，我要呼吁：音乐教育家、音乐出版机关，请给我们一些关于祖国丰富的民族音乐知识吧！
             春耕芋
