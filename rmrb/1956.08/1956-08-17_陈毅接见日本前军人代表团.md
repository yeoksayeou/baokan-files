Title: Chen Yi Receives Japanese Former Military Officers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Marshal Chen Yi, received the Japanese former military officers delegation on the afternoon of the 16th. The delegation was composed of fifteen people including former Vice Admiral of the Japanese Navy, Masao Kanazawa, former Lieutenant Generals of the Army, Saburo Endo, Kazuma Hori, and Akio Doi. Present at the reception were President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Xiruo, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong and Liao Chengzhi.

### 陈毅接见日本前军人代表团

1956-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见日本前军人代表团
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅元帅十六日下午接见了前日本海军中将金泽正夫，前陆军中将远藤三郎、堀毛一磨和土居明夫等十五人组成的日本前军人访华代表团。接见时在座的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，肖向荣中将和廖承志等人。
