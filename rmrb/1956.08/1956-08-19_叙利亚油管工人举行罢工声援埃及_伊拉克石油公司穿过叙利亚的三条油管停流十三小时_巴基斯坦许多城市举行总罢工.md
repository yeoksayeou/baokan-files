Title: Syrian Oil Pipeline Workers Strike in Support of Egypt; Iraqi Petroleum Company Pipelines Through Syria Halt Flow for Thirteen Hours; General Strike Held in Many Cities in Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th, Damascus Dispatch: Syrian oil pipeline workers of the Iraqi Petroleum Company held a strike on August 16th in support of Egypt.

The workers stopped the flow of oil through three important pipelines of the Iraqi Petroleum Company that run through Syria. According to a company spokesperson, the strike halted work for thirteen hours.

Arab countries all held a general strike on August 16th to protest the Western countries' unilateral convening of the London Conference to discuss the Suez Canal issue. The Iraqi Petroleum Company's pipeline workers went on strike in response to the call for a general strike.

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, August 18th: The entire city of Karachi held a general strike on August 16th to express support for Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal. The general strike was held at the call of the "Karachi Citizens Committee in Support of the Nationalization of the Suez Canal."

All commercial centers in the city were closed, except for restaurants and eateries.

A conference held by various groups passed a resolution guaranteeing the full support of the Pakistani people for the Egyptian people. The resolution warned Western countries that any threat of military action on this issue would be a threat to world peace.

Lahore also held a city-wide strike on the same day. Almost all businesses and shops remained closed until the 17th. The People's League initiated a rally where leaders of various political parties spoke, all expressing support for Egypt's measure to nationalize the Suez Canal.

In other small cities in West Pakistan, comprehensive strikes were also held.

### 叙利亚油管工人举行罢工声援埃及  伊拉克石油公司穿过叙利亚的三条油管停流十三小时  巴基斯坦许多城市举行总罢工

1956-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚油管工人举行罢工声援埃及
    伊拉克石油公司穿过叙利亚的三条油管停流十三小时
    巴基斯坦许多城市举行总罢工
    新华社18日讯　大马士革消息：伊拉克石油公司在叙利亚的输油管工人八月十六日举行了声援埃及的罢工。
    工人们停止让石油流过三条穿过叙利亚的伊拉克石油公司的重要油管。据公司发言人说，这次罢工使工作停顿了十三个小时。
    阿拉伯国家在八月十六日都举行了总罢工，以抗议西方国家片面召开讨论苏彝士运河问题的伦敦会议。伊拉克石油公司输油管工人是为了响应总罢工的号召而举行罢工的。
    新华社喀喇蚩18日电　喀喇蚩全市在八月十六日举行总罢工表示支持埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有。总罢工是在“喀喇蚩市民支持苏彝士运河国有化委员会”的号召下举行的。
    除餐馆和饮食店以外，全城各商业中心一律停业。
    各团体所举行的一个大会通过决议，保证巴基斯坦人民充分支持埃及人民。决议警告西方国家说，在这个问题上以军事行动进行的任何威胁，都是对世界和平的威胁。
    拉合尔也在同一天举行全市罢工。几乎所有企业和商店到十七日都还关闭着。人民联盟发起了一个集会，各政党领袖都在会上讲了话，他们都表示支持埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的措施。
    在西巴基斯坦的其他小城中，也举行了全面罢工。
