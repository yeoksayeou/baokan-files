    Egypt is Advancing
    Egypt is an ancient civilization with a history of over six thousand years.
    Ancient Egypt was once one of the world's superpowers. However, in recent centuries, it suffered invasions from Turkey, France, and Britain. From the late 19th century, Britain occupied Egypt. To oppose the exploitation and oppression of the colonialists and to strive for national independence, the Egyptian people waged a long and heroic struggle. In July 1952, the Egyptian people expelled King Farouk. In September 1953, the Egyptian government announced the permanent abolition of the monarchy and the establishment of the Arab Republic of Egypt.
    In recent years, the Egyptian Republic government has taken some economic reforms, especially making many efforts to free Egypt's economy from foreign control. In its foreign policy, the Egyptian government advocates promoting international peace and cooperation. Because the Egyptian government has pursued an independent and autonomous foreign policy, its international status has been rising, and it plays an active role in international affairs.

    The Egyptian people have drawn up a grand plan to combat the desert, establishing a vast and fertile new province called the Liberation Province in the Libyan Desert adjacent to the southwest of the Nile Delta. Now bulldozers and earthmovers are removing layers of shifting sand and leveling the undulating desert (top left picture). Some model villages in the Liberation Province have already built clean little houses made of cement (top right picture).
    The Suez Canal took ten years for the Egyptian people to dig, claiming the precious lives of 120,000 Egyptians. However, over the long years, the Suez Canal did not bring any happiness to the Egyptian people. Look, these poor, miserable boat trackers on the Suez Canal are a testament to this painful history (top left picture). From 1882 onwards, British troops occupied the Suez Canal (top right picture). However, on June 13, 1956, the Egyptian people finally forced the last British soldier to withdraw from the Suez Canal Zone. Now the Suez Canal has returned to the embrace of Egypt, and it will smoothly create happiness for the Egyptian people (right picture).

    埃及在前进
    埃及是一个文明古国。它有着六千多年的历史。
    古代的埃及曾经是世界强国之一。但是，在近几个世纪中，它先后遭到了土耳其、法国、英国的侵略。从十九世纪末期起，英国就占领了埃及。为了反对殖民者的剥削和压迫，争取民族独立，埃及人民长期地进行了英勇的斗争。1952年7月，埃及人民驱逐了国王法鲁克。1953年9月，埃及政府宣布永远废除君主政体，成立了埃及共和国。
    几年来，埃及共和国政府采取了一些经济方面的改革措施，特别是为了使埃及的经济摆脱外国的控制，作了许多努力。埃及政府在外交政策上，主张促进国际间的和平与合作。由于埃及政府奉行了独立自主的外交政策，它的国际地位日益提高，它在国际事务中起着积极的作用。

    埃及人民拟定了一个宏伟的反沙漠斗争的计划，在毗连尼罗河三角洲西南方的利比亚沙漠上建立了一个幅员广阔、土地肥沃的新省——解放省。现在推土机和铲土机正铲去一层层的流沙，并把起伏不平的沙漠弄平（左上图），解放省的一些模范乡村，已经建立了一幢幢用水泥筑成的洁净的小屋（右上图）。
    苏彝士运河经过埃及人民十年的开凿，夺去了埃及十二万人的宝贵生命。可是，在漫长的岁月里，苏彝士运河并没有给埃及人民带来任何幸福。请看，这些苏彝士运河上的苦难贫穷的纤夫，就是这一部惨痛历史的见证（左上图）。从1882年以后，英国军队一直占领着苏彝士运河（右上图），然而1956年6月13日，埃及人民终于迫使最后一个英国兵撤离了苏彝士运河区，现在苏彝士运河回到了埃及的怀抱，它将畅通无阻地为埃及人民创造幸福（右图）。
