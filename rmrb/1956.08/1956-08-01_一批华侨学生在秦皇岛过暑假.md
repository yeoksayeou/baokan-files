Title: A Group of Overseas Chinese Students Spend Their Summer Vacation in Qinhuangdao
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, 31st - A "Summer Camp" composed of one hundred and fifty-two returned Overseas Chinese students studying in Tangshan City has already opened in the scenic Qinhuangdao, where they will enjoy their summer vacation.

These one hundred and fifty-two returned Overseas Chinese students returned from eleven countries, including Laos, Thailand, Indonesia, India, Japan, and Malaya, between 1950 and 1955, and are currently studying at Tangshan No. 1 Middle School and No. 2 Middle School.

According to the staff of the Summer Camp, in addition to engaging in some cultural and sports activities here, they will also visit factories and historical sites, and visit the Beidaihe seaside.

### 一批华侨学生在秦皇岛过暑假

1956-08-01
第7版()
专栏：

    一批华侨学生在秦皇岛过暑假
    新华社保定31日电　一百五十二名在唐山市学习的归国华侨学生组成的“夏令营”已经在风景秀丽的秦皇岛开营，他们将在这里愉快地渡过暑假。
    这一百五十二名归国华侨学生，是在1950年到1955年从寮国、泰国、印度尼西亚、印度、日本、马来亚等十一个国家回来的，现在都在唐山市第一中学和第二中学学习。
    据夏令营的工作人员说，他们在这里除了进行一些文艺、体育活动外，还要参观工厂和名胜古迹，并且到北戴河海滨去游览。
