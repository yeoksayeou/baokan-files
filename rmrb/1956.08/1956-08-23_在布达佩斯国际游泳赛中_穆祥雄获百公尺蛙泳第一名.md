Title: Mu Xiangxiong Wins First Place in 100-Meter Breaststroke at Budapest International Swimming Competition
Author:
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 21st. Chinese swimmer Mu Xiangxiong won first place in the men's 100-meter breaststroke at the international swimming competition held in Budapest on August 20th, with a time of 1 minute 12 seconds. Two other Chinese athletes, Qi Lieyun and Xu Zhixiang, took fourth and fifth place in the same event respectively. Their times were 1 minute 12.7 seconds and 1 minute 13.4 seconds. Xu Zhixiang's time is equivalent to the second-place time for this event at last year's World Youth Festival.

    In the men's 400-meter freestyle relay race on the same day, the Chinese relay team, composed of Mu Ruilong, Zhang Jiachen, Lin Li, and Lin Jinzhu, achieved fourth place with a time of 4 minutes 0.6 seconds.

    Chinese athlete Li Xiqing achieved fifth place in the men's 1,500-meter freestyle race with a time of 19 minutes 46.3 seconds, breaking his own national record of 20 minutes 13.4 seconds.

    In the women's 100-meter breaststroke race, Chinese athlete Dai Lihua came in sixth place with a time of 1 minute 28.6 seconds.

### 在布达佩斯国际游泳赛中  穆祥雄获百公尺蛙泳第一名

1956-08-23
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    在布达佩斯国际游泳赛中
    穆祥雄获百公尺蛙泳第一名
    新华社布拉格21日电　中国游泳运动员穆祥雄八月二十日在布达佩斯举行的国际游泳比赛中，又以1分12秒的成绩夺得男子一百公尺蛙泳第一名。另外两名中国运动员戚烈云和徐致祥分别取得这项比赛的第四和第五名。他们的成绩是1分12.7秒和1分13.4秒。徐致祥的成绩相当于去年世界青年联欢节这一项的第二名成绩。
    在这一天的男子四百公尺自由泳接力比赛中，由穆瑞龙、张家琛、林立、林锦珠四人组成的中国接力队，以4分0.6秒的成绩取得第四名。
    中国运动员李喜庆取得了男子一千五百公尺自由泳比赛的第五名，成绩是19分46.3秒，打破了他自己保持的20分13.4秒的全国纪录。
    在女子一百公尺蛙泳比赛中，中国运动员戴丽华得第六名，成绩是1分28.6秒。
