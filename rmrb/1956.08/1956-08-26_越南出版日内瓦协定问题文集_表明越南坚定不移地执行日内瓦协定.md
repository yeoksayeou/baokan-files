Title: Vietnam Publishes Collection of Documents on the Geneva Accords, Demonstrating Vietnam's Unwavering Implementation of the Geneva Accords
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 25th. The Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam today published a "Collection of Documents on the Implementation of the Geneva Accords Concerning Vietnam" in Vietnamese, French, and English.

This collection, comprising one hundred and seventy-four pages in the Vietnamese version, systematically compiles the full texts or excerpts of eighty-one documents, statements, pronouncements, and letters published by various countries and the International Commission on Vietnam since the signing of the Geneva Accords.

The collection is divided into five parts. The first part briefly excerpts the main provisions of the Geneva Accords concerning the Vietnam issue, the declarations of the Foreign Ministers of the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, China, and Vietnam, and the statement by US representative Smith at the final session of the Geneva Conference.

The second part collects pronouncements, orders, decrees, communiques, and letters issued by the Democratic Republic of Vietnam since the signing of the Geneva Accords.

The third part collects statements and treaties made by the United States and Ngo Dinh Diem since the Geneva Conference, reports of the International Commission, Nehru's statements, and the contents of the joint communiques of China-Vietnam, Soviet-Vietnam, and Soviet-India.

The fourth part mainly collects correspondence between the Vietnamese Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference.

The fifth part is an "Appendix," which includes the full text of the Geneva Accords and some materials from the International Commission on Vietnam regarding the South Vietnamese regime's reprisals against former resistance members, persecution of the people, and obstruction of the activities of the International Commission on Vietnam.

From the materials collected in this document collection, it can be clearly seen how the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has steadfastly implemented the Geneva Accords and how the United States and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique have been doing everything possible to undermine the realization of the Geneva Accords.

### 越南出版日内瓦协定问题文集  表明越南坚定不移地执行日内瓦协定

1956-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    越南出版日内瓦协定问题文集
    表明越南坚定不移地执行日内瓦协定
    新华社河内25日电　越南民主共和国外交部新闻司今天分别以越、法、英三种文字的版本公布了一部“关于履行有关越南的日内瓦协定问题的文件集”。
    这部按越文版计算有一百七十四页的文件集系统地收集了自从日内瓦协定签订以来有关各国和越南国际委员会公布的八十一件文件、声明、文告和信件等的全文或者摘录。
    文件集共分五部分。第一部分简要地摘引了有关越南问题的日内瓦协定的主要条文和苏英中越四国外交部长的声明以及美国代表史密斯在日内瓦会议最后一次会议上的声明。
    第二部分收集了越南民主共和国方面从日内瓦协定签订以来所发表的文告、命令、法令、公报和信件等。
    第三部分收集了日内瓦会议以来美国和吴庭艳方面的言论、条约和国际委员会的报告、尼赫鲁的言论以及中越、苏越、苏印三个联合公报的内容。
    第四部分主要收集了越南外交部和日内瓦会议两位主席的来往信件。
    第五部分是“附录”，其中包括日内瓦协定的全文和越南国际委员会关于南越政权对前抗战人员的报复、对人民的迫害和阻挠越南国际委员会的活动的一些材料。
    从这本文件集所搜集的材料中，可以清楚地看出，越南民主共和国是如何坚定不移地在执行着日内瓦协定，美国和吴庭艳集团是如何千方百计地在破坏着日内瓦协定的实现。
