The investigation confirmed that the above-mentioned 354 Japanese war criminals from the war of aggression against China openly violated the norms of international law and the principles of humanity during the Japanese imperialist war of aggression against our country, committing crimes of varying degrees against the people of our country. Among them, Kobayashi Takayasu, Ogawa Tsuneo, and 44 other Japanese war criminals from the war of aggression against China, after Japan's surrender, also participated in the Yan Xishan counter-revolutionary group, carrying out the evil activities of reviving Japanese militarism and attempting to establish a base for re-aggression against our country in Shanxi. According to the crimes committed by these criminals, they should have been prosecuted, brought to trial, and given due punishment. However, in view of the changes in the situation and the current situation ten years after Japan's surrender, and in view of the development of friendly relations between the peoples of China and Japan in recent years, and also considering that these criminals have shown good remorse during their detention, or are secondary war criminals, therefore, according to the "Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on the Handling of Japanese War Criminals Detained in the War of Aggression against China" regarding the spirit of lenient policy for Japanese war criminals from the war of aggression against China and the provisions of Article 1, Paragraph 1, this court decides: to leniently treat the third batch of 354 Japanese war criminals from the war of aggression against China detained, exempt them from prosecution, and release them immediately.
