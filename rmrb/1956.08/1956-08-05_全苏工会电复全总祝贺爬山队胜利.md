Title: All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to ACFTU Congratulating Mountaineering Team on Victory
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 4
Sports News

    All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to ACFTU
    Congratulating Mountaineering Team on Victory
    Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd. Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, received a telegram on the 3rd from Grishin, Chairman of the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions, dated the 2nd. The telegram stated: "Thank you for your congratulations to us on the victorious ascent of Muztagh Ata by the Sino-Soviet mountaineering team athletes. We, together with you, are proud of the achievements made. This joint sports event has promoted the further strengthening of the eternal friendship between the peoples of our two countries. With fraternal greetings!"

### 全苏工会电复全总祝贺爬山队胜利

1956-08-05
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    全苏工会电复全总
    祝贺爬山队胜利
    新华社3日讯　中华全国总工会主席赖若愚三日收到全苏工会中央理事会主席格里申二日给他的一份电报。电报中说：“感谢你们就中苏爬山队运动员胜利攀登慕士塔格山向我们表示的祝贺。我们和你们一起，为所取得的成就而自豪。这次联合体育运动措施促进了我们两国人民之间的永恒友谊的进一步加强。致兄弟般的敬礼！”
