Title: China's Islamic Hajj Delegation Meets Yemeni Prince in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 19th: The head of the Chinese Islamic Hajj Delegation, Burhan, accompanied by deputy heads Li Shu and Anwar Hamba, visited Yemeni Prince Abdullah Hussein and his brother on the afternoon of August 17th.

During the meeting, the hosts and guests engaged in a long and friendly conversation. Prince Abdullah expressed the Yemeni government's welcome to the Chinese Islamic Hajj Delegation to visit Yemen.

### 我国伊斯兰教朝觐团在开罗会见也门王子

1956-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

    我国伊斯兰教朝觐团在开罗会见也门王子
    据新华社开罗19日电　中国伊斯兰教朝觐团团长鲍尔汉偕同副团长李恕和安尼瓦尔·汉巴巴八月十七日下午拜访了也门王子阿卜杜拉·侯赛尼和他的弟弟。
    在会见的时候，宾主进行长时间的友好交谈。阿卜杜拉王子表示也门政府欢迎中国伊斯兰教朝觐团到也门进行访问。
