Title: Communist Party of Indonesia Elects New Politburo, Comrade Aidit Re-elected as General Secretary
Author:
Date: 1956-08-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 5th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia announced at a press conference held in Jakarta on August 3rd that the Fourth Enlarged Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia has elected D.N. Aidit to continue serving as General Secretary of the Politburo of the Party Central Committee. The other members of the Politburo are: Deputy General Secretaries Lukman and Njoto, and members Sudisman, Jusuf Adjitorop, and Siswojo.

There were no changes to the members of the Central Committee. Siswojo was elected as a member of the Central Committee to replace the late Central Committee member Bachtaruddin.

### 印度尼西亚共产党选出新的政治局  艾地同志连任总书记

1956-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党选出新的政治局
    艾地同志连任总书记
    新华社雅加达5日电　印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会八月三日在雅加达举行的记者招待会上宣布：印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会第四次扩大全体会议已经选举迪·努·艾地连任党中央政治局总书记。政治局的其他成员是：副总书记鲁克曼和约多，委员为苏蒂斯曼、优素福·阿吉托罗普和西斯沃约。
    中央委员会委员没有更动。西斯沃约被选为中央委员会委员以代替已故的中央委员会委员巴赫塔鲁丁。
