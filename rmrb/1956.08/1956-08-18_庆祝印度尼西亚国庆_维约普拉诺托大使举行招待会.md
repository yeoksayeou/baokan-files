Title: Ambassador Wijopranoto Holds Reception to Celebrate Indonesian National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai attended the reception held by Indonesian Ambassador Wijopranoto tonight to celebrate the National Day of the Republic of Indonesia.

At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai proposed a toast to the prosperity of the Republic of Indonesia and the happiness of its people, to the friendship between Indonesia and China, to the peace of Asia, Africa, and the world, and to the health of President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia.

Previously, Ambassador Wijopranoto, standing on the steps of the beautiful garden, invited his more than two hundred Chinese and foreign guests to toast the friendship between the Republic of Indonesia and the People's Republic of China, to the happiness of the Chinese people, and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and other leaders of the Chinese government.

In the garden, decorated with festive red lanterns, joyful conversations between hosts and guests could be heard. Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, and Vice Premier Chen Yi met and talked with diplomats from various countries stationed in China.

During his conversation with Ambassador Wijopranoto, Premier Zhou Enlai expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome extended by President Sukarno, the Indonesian government, and the Indonesian people to Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling, who is currently visiting Indonesia. Premier Zhou Enlai also informed the ambassador that the Chinese government is preparing to receive President Sukarno for a visit to China.

Premier Zhou Enlai toasted with the Egyptian Commercial Representative to China, Mr. Far, wishing the Egyptian government and people victory and success in their just struggle over the Suez Canal issue. Mr. Far expressed his gratitude for the support of the Chinese government and people to the Egyptian government and people. Premier Zhou Enlai said that justice is on the side of the Egyptian people, and the colonialists' gunboat policy is bound to fail.

The pleasant reception lasted for an hour and a half. Among the Chinese guests were also heads of various government departments and people from all walks of life in Beijing.

### 庆祝印度尼西亚国庆  维约普拉诺托大使举行招待会

1956-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝印度尼西亚国庆
    维约普拉诺托大使举行招待会
    新华社17日讯　朱德副主席、周恩来总理，今晚出席了印度尼西亚大使维约普拉诺托为庆祝印度尼西亚共和国国庆举行的招待会。
    周恩来总理在招待会上提议，为印度尼西亚共和国的繁荣和人民的幸福，为印度尼西亚和中国的友谊，为亚洲、非洲和世界的和平，为印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统的健康干杯。
    这以前，维约普拉诺托大使站在美丽花园的台阶上，请他的二百多名中外客人，为印度尼西亚共和国和中华人民共和国两国间的友谊，为中国人民的幸福，为毛泽东主席和中国政府其他领导者们的健康干杯。
    在悬挂着节日红灯的花园里，不断传出宾主愉快的谈笑声。朱德副主席、周恩来总理、彭真副委员长和陈毅副总理等，都同各国驻华的外交官们会面交谈。
    周恩来总理同维约普拉诺托大使谈话时，对目前正在印度尼西亚访问的宋庆龄副委员长受到苏加诺总统、印度尼西亚政府和人民的热烈欢迎表示感谢。周恩来总理还告诉这位大使说，中国政府正在准备接待苏加诺总统来中国访问。
    周恩来总理同埃及驻中国商务代表法尔一起干了杯，祝埃及政府和人民在苏彝士运河问题的正义斗争中胜利和成功。法尔对中国政府和人民对埃及政府和人民的支持表示感谢。周恩来总理说，正义是在埃及人民这一边，殖民主义者的炮舰政策是一定要失败的。
    愉快的招待会共进行了一个半小时。在中国客人中，还有政府各部门的负责人和北京社会各界人士。
