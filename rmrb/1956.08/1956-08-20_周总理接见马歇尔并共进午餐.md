Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Marshall and Has Lunch Together
Author:
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Premier Zhou Enlai received David Marshall, Chairman of the Labour Front of Singapore and former Chief Minister of Singapore, at 11:30 this morning, and had lunch with him. Present at the meeting were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Vice President Qiao Guanhua, Secretary-General Wu Maosun, Liao Chengzhi, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade. ### 周总理接见马歇尔并共进午餐

1956-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见马歇尔并共进午餐
    新华社19日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天上午十一时半接见了新加坡劳工阵线主席、前新加坡首席部长戴维·马歇尔，并共进午餐。接见的时候，在座的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长乔冠华、秘书长吴茂荪，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员廖承志，对外贸易部副部长李哲人。（附图片）
