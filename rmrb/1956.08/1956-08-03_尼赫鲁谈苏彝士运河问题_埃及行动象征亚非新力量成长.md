Title: Nehru on the Suez Canal Issue: Egyptian Action Symbolizes the Growth of New Asian-African Forces
Author:
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 2nd. Indian Prime Minister Nehru said at a mass rally in Poona on August 1st that Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal was a sign of the gradual weakening of European influence in Asia and Africa.

Nehru said that new forces of freedom are emerging and growing on these two continents. Asian and African countries are breaking free from the shackles of colonialism. He said that India must understand these new forces that are at work, and only then can it correctly formulate its policies.

### 尼赫鲁谈苏彝士运河问题  埃及行动象征亚非新力量成长

1956-08-03
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁谈苏彝士运河问题
    埃及行动象征亚非新力量成长
    新华社新德里2日电　印度总理尼赫鲁八月一日在浦那举行的群众大会上说，埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有这件事，是欧洲在亚洲和非洲的影响逐渐削弱的征象。
    尼赫鲁说，在这两个大洲上，新的自由力量正在出现，并且正在成长起来。亚非国家正在摆脱殖民主义的枷锁。他说，印度必须了解正在发生作用的这些新的力量，只有这样它才能正确地制定自己的政策。
