Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Says Before Attending London Conference: Opposes International Control of the Suez Canal
Author:
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 13th: Indonesian Foreign Minister Abdugani, head of the Indonesian delegation to the London Conference, said today upon his departure for London that Indonesia opposes any international body, including the United Nations, exercising international control over the Suez Canal.

He said at Kemayoran Airport that Indonesia has a clear position in attending the London Conference, and Indonesia's participation in the London Conference does not mean opposing Egypt's sovereign principle of nationalizing the Suez Canal. He said that it is not surprising that Egypt refused to attend the London Conference. In the absence of Egypt's participation, Indonesia will support Egypt's right to take over the Suez Canal.

Abdugani said that Indonesia's task at the conference will be to do its utmost to prevent the use of force.

When asked about the issue of whether the conference would pass resolutions that violate Indonesia's position, he said, "We cannot be forced to accept any decision. We have been assured that the conference cannot make decisions by majority vote. Based on this guarantee, we have expressed our willingness to participate in the conference."

During his stopover in Cairo, Abdugani will discuss every aspect of the Suez Canal issue with President Nasser.

### 印度尼西亚外长出席伦敦会议前表示  反对国际管制苏彝士运河

1956-08-14
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外长出席伦敦会议前表示
    反对国际管制苏彝士运河
    新华社雅加达13日电　参加伦敦会议的印度尼西亚代表团团长、印度尼西亚外交部长阿卜杜加尼今天在启程前往伦敦的时候说，印度尼西亚反对任何国际机构，包括联合国在内对苏彝士运河实行国际管制。
    他在克玛约兰机场说，印度尼西亚具有鲜明的立场去参加伦敦会议，印度尼西亚参加伦敦会议决不意味着反对埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的主权原则。他说，埃及拒绝参加伦敦会议是不奇怪的。在埃及没有参加会议的情况下，印度尼西亚将支持埃及接管苏彝士运河的权利。
    阿卜杜加尼说，印度尼西亚在会议上的任务将是尽最大努力阻止使用武力。
    在谈到如果会议通过了违反印度尼西亚的立场的决议的问题的时候，他说，我们不能被强迫接受任何决定。我们已经得到了保证，会议不能以多数投票作出决定。根据这一保证，我们才表示准备参加会议。
    阿卜杜加尼在途经开罗逗留的时候，将和纳赛尔总统讨论苏彝士运河问题的每一个方面。
