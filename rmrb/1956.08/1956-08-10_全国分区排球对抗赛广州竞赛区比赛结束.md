Title: National Zonal Volleyball Tournament Guangzhou Competition Zone Concludes
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 8th. The Guangzhou competition zone of the 1956 National Zonal Volleyball Tournament concluded on the evening of the 7th.

After two round-robin matches, the White Team from Wuhan Institute of Physical Education won five sets, securing first place. The Red Team from Wuhan Institute of Physical Education won three sets, taking second place.

On the evening of the 7th, approximately two thousand spectators watched the second, decisive match between these two teams.

Both teams are composed primarily of well-known volleyball players from Taishan County.

### 全国分区排球对抗赛广州竞赛区比赛结束

1956-08-10
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    全国分区排球对抗赛广州竞赛区比赛结束
    新华社广州8日电　1956年全国分区排球对抗赛广州竞赛区的比赛已经在七日晚上结束。
    经过循环制的两场比赛以后，武汉体育学院白队赢五局，获得第一名。武汉体育学院红队赢三局，获得第二名。
    七日晚上，约二千名观众观看了这两个队的第二场带有决定性的比赛。
    这两个队都是以著名的台山县排球好手为骨干组成的。
