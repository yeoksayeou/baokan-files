Title: Ambassador Chivu Stoica Holds Reception Celebrating the 12th Anniversary of Romania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd. Romanian Ambassador to China, Chivu Stoica, held a grand reception this evening to celebrate the 12th anniversary of Romania's liberation.

    Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, attended the reception, extending warm and fraternal congratulations to the Romanian government and people on behalf of the Chinese government and people.

    Vice Chairman Zhu De said that the Chinese people highly cherish every achievement made by the fraternal Romanian people on the road of socialist construction and in the cause of defending world peace, because these achievements are also the achievements of our common cause.

    Vice Chairman Zhu De said that the cooperation between the Chinese and Romanian people in politics, economy, and culture is constantly being consolidated and developed. He wished the Romanian people new and greater achievements in socialist construction and in the cause of defending world peace.

    At the beginning of the reception, Ambassador Chivu Stoica, in his speech, expressed the Romanian people's friendship towards the Chinese people. He congratulated the Chinese people on the victories they have achieved in the struggle for a happy life, for cooperation and friendship, and for the precious cause of peace.

    Among those present at the reception this evening were Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, responsible persons from various departments of state organs, people from all walks of life in Beijing, and foreign diplomats in China, totaling more than 700 people.

    In the middle of the reception, artists from the Romanian "Skylark" Folk Dance and Music Troupe performed many famous Romanian musical pieces, which were praised by Chinese and foreign guests.

### 乔洛尤大使举行招待会  庆祝罗马尼亚解放十二周年

1956-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    乔洛尤大使举行招待会
    庆祝罗马尼亚解放十二周年
    新华社23日讯　罗马尼亚驻中国大使乔洛尤今天晚间举行盛大招待会，庆祝罗马尼亚解放十二周年。
    中华人民共和国朱德副主席出席了招待会，他代表中国政府和人民向罗马尼亚政府和人民致热烈的、兄弟的祝贺。
    朱德副主席说，中国人民十分珍视兄弟的罗马尼亚人民在社会主义建设的道路上和在保卫世界和平的事业中所取得的每一个成就，因为这些成就也就是我们共同事业的成就。
    朱德副主席说，中罗两国人民在政治、经济和文化等方面的合作正在不断地巩固和发展。他预祝罗马尼亚人民在社会主义建设和在保卫世界和平的事业中，获得新的更大的成就。
    招待会开始的时候，乔洛尤大使在他的讲话中表达了罗马尼亚人民对中国人民的友谊。他祝贺中国人民在为幸福生活、为合作和友谊、为珍贵的和平事业所进行的斗争中取得的胜利。
    今晚出席招待会的，有副总理贺龙、陈毅，副委员长陈叔通，外交部副部长张闻天和国家机关各部门的负责人、北京社会各界人士，以及各国驻中国的外交官，共七百多人。
    在招待会进行中间，罗马尼亚“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团的艺术家们，演奏了许多著名的罗马尼亚乐曲，受到中外人士的赞赏。
