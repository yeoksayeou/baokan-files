Title: Moroccan Labour Union Demands Withdrawal of French Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, August 20th: The National Committee meeting of the Moroccan Labour Union, which concluded in Casablanca on August 19, demanded the withdrawal of French troops from Morocco and the adoption of effective policies to support the struggle of the Algerian people.

The National Committee of the Moroccan Labour Union criticized the current Bekkai government for failing to improve the living standards of the people after achieving political independence. It demanded the establishment of a "government of action" to formulate Morocco's economic program and implement a New Deal for Moroccan workers.

### 摩洛哥劳工联盟要求法国军队撤退

1956-08-21
第6版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥劳工联盟要求法国军队撤退
    新华社20日讯　拉巴特消息：八月十九日在卡萨布兰卡闭幕的摩洛哥劳工联盟全国委员会会议，要求法国军队从摩洛哥撤退和采取有效政策支持阿尔及利亚人民的斗争。
    摩洛哥劳工联盟全国委员会批评现在的贝凯政府在取得政治独立以后没有能够改善人民的生活水平。它要求成立“行动的政府”，制订摩洛哥的经济纲领，对摩洛哥工人实施新政。
