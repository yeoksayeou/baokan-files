Title: Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Association Holds Third Membership Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th -- The Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Association held its third membership congress yesterday. Zhuang Xiquan, the vice-chairman of the association, reported on the association's work. Zhuang Xiquan said that from November 1953 to the present, for more than two years, the association has organized members to participate in various political activities, and has conducted patriotic education and propaganda on peaceful foreign policy to returned overseas Chinese by organizing various report meetings, forums, get-togethers, and visits to exhibitions. The organization and liaison work have also developed. Up to now, it has developed more than 1,900 members and organized more than 60 "Overseas Chinese Student Friendship Groups" in universities and colleges. The connection with overseas Chinese compatriots has also become increasingly close. In addition, it has also handled some welfare work for overseas Chinese students in Beijing.
    The meeting also approved the revised constitution, changed the name of the association to the Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Federation, and elected the third council of directors.

### 北京归国华侨联谊会举行第三届会员大会

1956-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    北京归国华侨联谊会举行第三届会员大会
    据新华社20日讯　北京归国华侨联谊会昨天举行第三届会员大会，由联谊会副主席庄希泉报告会的工作。庄希泉说，从1953年11月到现在，两年多来联谊会组织会员参加了各项政治活动，并通过组织各种报告会、座谈会、联欢会及参观展览等方式，对归国华侨进行了爱国主义教育及和平外交政策的宣传。组织与联络工作也有发展。到现在已发展会员一千九百多人，在大、中学校组织了六十多个“华侨学生联谊小组”。和国外侨胞的联系也日益密切。另外，还办理了一些在京华侨学生的福利工作。
    会议还通过了修改后的会章，将会的名称改为北京市归国华侨联合会，并选出了第三届理事会理事。
