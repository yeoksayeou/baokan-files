Title: Defend Peace, Defend the Rights of Women and Children: Second Japan Mothers' Congress Opens; Our Delegation Leader Xu Guangping Delivers Speech Hoping for Unity Among Japanese Women
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 27th: The Second Japan Mothers' Congress, attended by more than twenty Japanese national women's organizations and more than a thousand local women's organizations, opened in Tokyo today.

More than 3,000 women representatives from all over Japan attended the congress. Ms. Vaillant-Couturier, representative of the Women's International Democratic Federation attending the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, and Chinese representative Xu Guangping, British representative Ms. Felton, and Soviet representative Ms. Koroleva also attended the congress.

Kawasaki Natsu, executive chairman of the Mothers' Congress, said in her speech at the meeting: Japanese mothers suffering from the tragic disasters of war and atomic and hydrogen bombs can no longer endure it. Now is the time for all Japanese mothers to stand up, concentrate the great power of mothers, and fight for the happiness of mothers and children.

Xu Guangping, head of the Chinese delegation, said in her speech that the convening of the Japan Mothers' Congress demonstrates the determination of Japanese mothers to resolutely fight against war, defend peace, and defend the rights of children and themselves. We believe that under the call of this congress, Japanese women and mothers will further unite extensively and strive for the rights of women and children.

Xu Guangping stated that Chinese women and mothers all over the world will fully support the just actions of Japanese mothers.

Subsequently, representatives from various parts of Japan reported to the congress on the situation of mothers and children in factories, rural areas, fishing villages, and US military bases in Japan and Okinawa.

The Mothers' Congress will last for three days.

### 保卫和平，保卫妇女和儿童的权利  第二届日本母亲大会开幕  我代表团长许广平致词希望日本妇女团结

1956-08-29
第6版()
专栏：

    保卫和平，保卫妇女和儿童的权利
    第二届日本母亲大会开幕
    我代表团长许广平致词希望日本妇女团结
    新华社27日讯　东京消息：有二十几个日本全国性妇女团体和一千多个地方妇女团体参加的第二届日本母亲大会，今天在东京开幕。
    出席大会的有来自日本全国的妇女代表三千多人。出席第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的国际民主妇女联合会代表瓦扬—古久里夫人、中国代表许广平、英国代表费尔顿夫人、苏联代表科罗荷娃夫人也出席了大会。
    母亲大会执行主席河崎夏在会上致词说：在战争和原子弹、氢弹的悲惨祸害下受痛苦的日本的母亲，不能再忍受下去了。现在正是所有的日本母亲应该站起来，集中母亲的伟大力量，为母亲和孩子的幸福而斗争的时候。
    中国代表团团长许广平在会上致词说，日本母亲大会的召开，表现了日本母亲为反对战争、保卫和平、保卫孩子和自己的权利而坚决斗争的意志。我们相信：在这次大会的号召下，日本妇女和母亲们，将进一步广泛地团结起来，为妇女与儿童的权利而努力奋斗。
    许广平表示，中国妇女和全世界的母亲都将全力支持日本母亲的正义行动。
    接着，日本各地代表在大会上做了关于在工厂、农村、渔村和日本国内的美军基地以及冲绳岛中的母亲和孩子的情况的报告。
    母亲大会将举行三天。
