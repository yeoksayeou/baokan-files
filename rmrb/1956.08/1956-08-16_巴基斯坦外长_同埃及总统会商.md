Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Meets with Egyptian President
Author:
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 15th - President Gamal Abdel Nasser met with Pakistani Foreign Minister Chodri for two hours on August 14th.

Chodri and the Pakistani delegation he led to the London conference arrived in Cairo that morning.

After meeting with President Nasser, Chodri said that Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal "is a matter within the indisputable scope of Egypt's sovereignty," and that any solution to this dispute "must safeguard Egypt's sovereignty." He said: "Force cannot reach an agreement."

### 巴基斯坦外长  同埃及总统会商

1956-08-16
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦外长
    同埃及总统会商
    新华社15日讯　开罗消息：阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统八月十四日同巴基斯坦外交部长乔德里会谈了二小时。
    乔德里和他率领的出席伦敦会议的巴基斯坦代表团是在当天早上到达开罗的。
    乔德里在同纳赛尔总统会谈后说，埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有“是埃及的不容争辩的主权范围以内的事”，任何解决这个争执的办法“都必须保障埃及的主权”。他说：“用武力是不能够达成协议的”。
