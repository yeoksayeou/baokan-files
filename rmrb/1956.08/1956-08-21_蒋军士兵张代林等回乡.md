Title: Chiang Kai-shek Soldiers Zhang Dailin and Others Return Home
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 19th -- Chiang Kai-shek soldiers Zhang Dailin and Wang Jialun, who swam back from Jinmen Island, departed from Fuzhou on the afternoon of the 18th to return to their hometowns. The leadership of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Fuzhou issued them travel expenses, production subsidies, as well as clothing, shoes, socks, and daily necessities.

Zhang Dailin is from Licun, Qingdao City, Shandong Province. Wang Jialun's home is in Daishan Village, Dinghai County, Zhejiang Province. Since returning from Jinmen Island, they have both received letters from their families, who are joyfully awaiting their reunion.

During their stay in Fuzhou, they toured the city and visited agricultural production cooperatives. They said that the prosperous scene of the motherland deeply moved them.

### 蒋军士兵张代林等回乡

1956-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    蒋军士兵张代林等回乡
    新华社福州19日电　由金门岛泅水归来的蒋军士兵张代林、王家轮二人，十八日下午由福州启程返乡。中国人民解放军驻福州部队领导机关发给了他们路费、生产补助金，还发了衣服、鞋袜和日用品。
    张代林是山东省青岛市李村人。王家轮家在浙江省定海县岱山村。他们自金门岛归来后，都接到家人的来信，家里人都在欢天喜地地等待着他们回家团聚。
    在福州期间，他们游览了城市，参观了农业生产合作社。他们说，祖国大陆的繁荣景象，使他们很感动。
