Title: Hungarian Agricultural Machinery Manufacturing Becomes a Major Industrial Sector
Author:
Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest Dispatch: Agricultural machinery manufacturing has become a major industrial sector in Hungary. Not long ago, Brazil placed a large order for grain harvesting combines with Hungary. Hungarian combines are also exported to China, Czechoslovakia, Austria, Iran, and other countries.
    During the First Five-Year Plan (1950-1954), as a result of developing agricultural machinery manufacturing, Hungarian machine tractor stations and state farms received more than 13,000 tractors and more than 35,000 other agricultural machines, including thousands of combines. Last year, agricultural machinery manufacturing expanded production and supplied agriculture with another 6,500 tractors.
    Due to the upsurge of the cooperative movement in the Hungarian countryside and the reclamation of large tracts of wasteland, agricultural machinery manufacturing faces the major task of supplying the countryside with a sufficient number of modern agricultural machines. According to the directives of the Second Five-Year Plan (1956-1960), Hungary's allocations for agricultural mechanization and the supply of modern machines to agriculture will be about twice as much as in the First Five-Year Plan. The number of tractors will increase by 19,000 in five years.

### 匈农业机器制造业  成为主要工业部门

1956-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    匈农业机器制造业
    成为主要工业部门
    新华社讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：农业机器制造业已经成为匈牙利的主要工业部门。不久以前，巴西向匈牙利订购了大批的谷物收获联合机。匈牙利的联合机也向中国、捷克斯洛伐克、奥地利、伊朗和其他国家出口。
    在第一个五年计划（1950—1954年）期间，由于发展农业机器制造业的结果，匈牙利各机器拖拉机站和国营农场就得到了一万三千多台拖拉机和三万五千多台其他农业机器，其中包括上千台的联合机。去年农业机器制造业扩大生产，又供给了农业六千五百台拖拉机。
    由于匈牙利农村中合作化运动的高涨和开垦大片荒地，这就向农业机器制造业提出了供应农村足够数量的现代化农业机器的重大任务。根据第二个五年计划（1956—1960年）的指示，匈牙利在农业机械化和供给农业以现代化机器方面的拨款，比第一个五年计划多一倍左右。五年中拖拉机增加的数目将达到一万九千台。
