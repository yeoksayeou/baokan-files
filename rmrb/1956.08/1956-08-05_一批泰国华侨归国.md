Title: A Batch of Overseas Chinese from Thailand Return to China
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shantou, 2nd. A batch of 641 overseas Chinese from Thailand arrived in Shantou at 2 a.m. on the 1st. Among them were 62 distressed overseas Chinese returning to the country. They were forcibly repatriated by the Thai government. In addition, there were 287 overseas Chinese students returning to China for further education.

### 一批泰国华侨归国

1956-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    一批泰国华侨归国
    据新华社汕头2日电　一批泰国华侨641人在一日早晨二时到达汕头。其中有归国难侨62人，他们是被泰国政府强迫遣送回来的，此外还有归国升学的华侨学生287人。
