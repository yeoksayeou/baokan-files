Title: Harbin Water Level Recedes
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, August 20th: This afternoon at 5:00 PM, the water level of the Songhua River in the Harbin area receded to 119.62 meters, but still exceeds the guaranteed water level by 0.32 meters.

Tens of thousands of flood control workers in Harbin City continued to reinforce the dikes today. Through the efforts of over ten thousand construction workers, a riprap revetment has been built on the second dike.

### 哈尔滨水位回落

1956-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨水位回落
    新华社哈尔滨20日电　今天下午五时，哈尔滨地区松花江水位回落到一一九点六二公尺，但是仍旧超过保证水位零点三二公尺。
    哈尔滨市数万防汛大军今天继续加固堤防。经过一万多名建筑工人的努力，第二道堤防已经垒起了块石护坡。
