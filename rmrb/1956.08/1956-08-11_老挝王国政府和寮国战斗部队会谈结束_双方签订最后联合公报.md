Title: Lao Kingdom Government and Lao Fighting Units Conclude Talks; Both Sides Sign Final Joint Communiqué
Author:
Date: 1956-08-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 10th: According to a Vientiane radio broadcast at 18:30 today, the delegation of the Lao Kingdom Government and the delegation of the Lao Fighting Units held their final meeting today and signed the final joint communiqué at 11:30. This is a supplementary communiqué to the first joint communiqué signed by both parties on August 5th.

In the final joint communiqué, the two parties have reached the following agreements:

I. To hold supplementary general elections with the participation of the Lao Fighting Units and former resistance personnel through free and secret ballot;

II. To organize a coalition government with the participation of the Lao Fighting Units.

After the signing of the final joint communiqué, Prince Souvanna Phouma, on behalf of the Kingdom Government delegation, said that the two joint communiqués signed by both parties demonstrated their desire for a prosperous, peaceful, and unified motherland, and demonstrated the trust between the two parties, which is also a concrete manifestation of the patriotic spirit of both parties in the hope of national unification.

Prince Souphanouvong spoke on behalf of the Lao Fighting Units delegation. He believed that the two joint communiqués marked a new stage in Lao history.

Souphanouvong said that the Lao Fighting Units guaranteed to respect and correctly implement all agreed matters and hoped that the Kingdom side would do the same.

At 18:30 today, the Prime Minister of the Lao Kingdom Government, Prince Souvanna Phouma, had already reported the results of the talks between the Kingdom Government and the Lao Fighting Units to the Lao people on Vientiane Radio.

### 老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队会谈结束  双方签订最后联合公报

1956-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队会谈结束
    双方签订最后联合公报
    新华社河内10日电　据万象电台今天十八时三十分广播：老挝王国政府代表团和寮国战斗部队代表团今天举行了最后一次会谈，并且在十一点三十分签订了最后联合公报。这是双方在八月五日签订的第一个联合公报的补充公报。
    在最后联合公报中，双方已经达成以下的协议：
    一、按照自由和秘密投票的方式举行有寮国战斗部队和前抗战人员参加的补充普选；
    二、组织有寮国战斗部队参加的联合政府。
    在最后联合公报签字以后，梭发那·富马亲王代表王国政府代表团讲话说，双方已经签订的两项联合公报表现了双方希望祖国富强、和平、统一的意志，说明了双方之间的信任，这也是双方希望祖国统一的爱国主义精神的具体表现。
    苏发努冯亲王代表寮国战斗部队代表团讲话。他认为两项联合公报已经标志着老挝历史上的一个新阶段。
    苏发努冯说寮国战斗部队保证将尊重和正确实行已经达成协议的各事项，并且希望王国方面也将这样做。
    今天十八时三十分，老挝王国政府首相梭发那·富马亲王已经在万象电台向老挝人民报告王国政府与寮国战斗部队之间会谈的结果。
