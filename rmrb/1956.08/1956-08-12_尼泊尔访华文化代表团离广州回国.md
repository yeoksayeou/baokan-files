Title: Nepalese Cultural Delegation to China Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1956-08-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 11th: The Nepalese Cultural Delegation to China left Guangzhou by train today to return home.

Delegation leader Sharma said to Chen RuTang, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, and Guo QiaoRan, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, who came to see them off: "This visit is just the beginning. More Nepalese people will come to China for visits in the future." Vice Governor Chen RuTang warmly welcomed what Delegation Leader Sharma said and asked him to bring the Chinese people's sincere feelings and friendship to the Nepalese people.

The Nepalese Cultural Delegation to China held a farewell cocktail party in Guangzhou on the 9th, at which Delegation Leader Sharma and Vice Governor Chen RuTang spoke successively.

All members of the delegation were also invited to attend a farewell cocktail party held by Chen RuTang, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, and Guo QiaoRan, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, last night. They also visited scenic spots and historical sites such as Yuexiu Mountain in Guangzhou.

### 尼泊尔访华文化代表团离广州回国

1956-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔访华文化代表团离广州回国
    据新华社广州11日电　尼泊尔访华文化代表团在今天乘火车离广州回国。
    夏尔马团长对前往送行的广东省副省长陈汝棠和广州市副市长郭翘然说：“我们这次来只不过作了一个开端，以后还会有更多的尼泊尔人来中国访问。”陈汝棠副省长对夏尔马团长说的话表示热烈欢迎，并要求他把中国人民真诚的感情和友谊带给尼泊尔人民。
    尼泊尔访华文化代表团九日曾在广州举行告别酒会，在酒会上夏尔马团长和陈汝棠副省长先后讲了话。
    代表团全体团员昨晚还应邀出席了广东省副省长陈汝棠、广州市副市长郭翘然为他们举行的欢送酒会。他们在广州还参观游览了越秀山等名胜古迹。
