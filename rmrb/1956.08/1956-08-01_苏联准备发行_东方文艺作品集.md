Title: Soviet Union to Publish "Collection of Oriental Literature"
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th. TASS Moscow, 29th: The Soviet State Literary Publishing House is preparing to publish a new publication - "Collection of Oriental Literature."

    This publication will feature works translated into Russian for the first time. In the inaugural issue, literary works from China and India occupy a significant portion. Among them are letters from the great Chinese writer Lu Xun, Lu Xun's memoirs, Li Shangyin's Regulated Verses, and poems by other Chinese poets. Also included are literary works from Indonesia and Korea.

### 苏联准备发行“东方文艺作品集”

1956-08-01
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联准备发行“东方文艺作品集”
    据新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二十九日讯：苏联国家文艺书籍出版局正在准备发行一种新的出版物——“东方文艺作品集”。
    这个刊物将刊载第一次译成俄文的作品。在创刊号刊载的东方国家的作品中，中国和印度的文学作品占有相当大的篇幅。其中有中国伟大作家鲁迅的书简、鲁迅回忆录、李商隐的律诗和中国其他诗人的诗。此外还有印度尼西亚和朝鲜的文艺作品。
