Title: Thai Cabinet Decides to Recognize Nationalization of Suez Canal
Author:
Date: 1956-08-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 30th. According to a report by Agence France-Presse, the Thai cabinet decided at its weekly meeting on August 29th that if the International Suez Canal Company is compensated, Thailand will agree that the nationalization of the Suez Canal is legal.

Agence France-Presse also said that Thailand will also insist on the principle of free navigation of the canal and resolve differences of opinion through peaceful means. If a solution cannot be found, it will ask the United Nations for help.

### 泰国内阁决定  将承认苏彝士运河国有化

1956-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

    泰国内阁决定
    将承认苏彝士运河国有化
    新华社30日讯　据法新社报道，泰国内阁8月29日在每周例会上决定，如果国际苏彝士运河公司得到补偿的话，泰国将同意认为苏彝士运河国有化是合法的。
    法新社还说，泰国也将坚持运河自由航行原则，并以和平手段解决分歧意见，如果不能求得解决办法，那就请联合国帮助。
