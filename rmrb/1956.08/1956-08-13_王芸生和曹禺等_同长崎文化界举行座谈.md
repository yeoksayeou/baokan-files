Title: Wang Yunsheng and Cao Yu Hold Talks with Nagasaki Cultural Circles

Author:
Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nagasaki, 12th. Wang Yunsheng, Cao Yu, and Kang Yonghe, members of the Chinese delegation attending the Second World Conference for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, were invited this morning to attend cultural and labor issue symposiums sponsored by Nagasaki City cultural organizations and trade unions, respectively.

　　At the cultural symposium, Cao Yu gave an introduction to the situation in Chinese theatrical circles. At the labor issue symposium, Kang Yonghe answered various questions raised by Nagasaki worker representatives one by one.

### 王芸生和曹禺等  同长崎文化界举行座谈

1956-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    王芸生和曹禺等
    同长崎文化界举行座谈
    新华社12日讯　长崎消息：出席第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团团员王芸生、曹禺和康永和，今天上午应邀分别出席了长崎市文化团体和工会主办的文化恳谈会和劳动问题恳谈会。
    曹禺在文化恳谈会上就中国戏剧界的情况做了介绍。康永和在劳动问题恳谈会上，对长崎工人代表提出的种种问题一一作了答复。
