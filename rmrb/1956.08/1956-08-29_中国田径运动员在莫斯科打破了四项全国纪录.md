Title: Chinese Track and Field Athletes Break Four National Records in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 28th: The Chinese track and field team and the "Dynamo" Association Moscow city track and field team began competitions in Moscow on August 27th. On this day, Chinese athletes broke four national records.

    Chinese athlete Chen Zhengxiu achieved a time of 2 minutes 19.6 seconds in the women's 800-meter race, and another Chinese athlete, Xu Shizhang, achieved a time of 2 minutes 19.8 seconds, both exceeding the national record of 2 minutes 22.5 seconds.

    Shot putter Zheng Renqiang broke the national record twice during the competition. His final result was 14 meters 18 centimeters, exceeding the original national official record by 80 centimeters.

    Zhang Liansheng broke the national official record of 6 meters 93 centimeters, held by Gao Shugui, in the long jump with a result of 6 meters 98 centimeters.

    Female athlete Shi Baozhu achieved a discus throw of 48 meters 29 centimeters, breaking her own national record of 45 meters 92 centimeters once again. Her highest domestic result had previously reached 49 meters 04 centimeters.

### 中国田径运动员在莫斯科打破了四项全国纪录

1956-08-29
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中国田径运动员在莫斯科打破了四项全国纪录
    据新华社莫斯科28日电　中国田径队和“狄纳莫”协会莫斯科市的田径队8月27日开始在莫斯科进行比赛。在这一天中，中国运动员打破了四项全国纪录。
    中国运动员陈正绣在女子八百公尺赛跑中获得了二分十九秒六的成绩，另一名中国运动员徐世璋的成绩是二分十九秒八，都超过了二分二十二秒五的全国纪录。
    铅球运动员郑仁强在比赛中两次刷新了全国纪录。他的最后成绩是十四公尺十八公分，超过了原来的全国正式纪录八十公分。
    张连生的跳远以六公尺九十八公分的成绩打破了高树贵保持的六公尺九十三公分的全国正式纪录。
    女子运动员石宝珠掷铁饼成绩是四十八公尺二十九公分，再次打破了她保持的四十五公尺九十二公分的全国纪录。她在国内的最高成绩曾经达到四十九公尺零四公分。
