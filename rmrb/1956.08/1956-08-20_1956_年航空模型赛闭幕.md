Title: 1956 Aviation Model Competition Concludes
Author:
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The 1956 Aviation Model Competition concluded today after the flying competition of Class III rubber band powered model airplanes and Class I free-flight piston engine model airplanes.

　　The first place winner in the Class III rubber band powered model airplane competition was Tang Yijun, a student from Xi'an Aeronautical Institute. His model airplane achieved a perfect score of 540 seconds in three flights.

　　The first place winner in the Class I free-flight piston engine model airplane competition, Liu Mingdao, is also a student from Xi'an Aeronautical Institute. His model airplane also achieved a perfect score, with a total flight time of 540 seconds for three flights.

　　In the Class II rubber band powered model airplane flying competition held on the 17th, Shou Erkang, a second-year student from Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Technology, also achieved a flight time of 540 seconds in three flights, earning him first place.

　　Over the past four days of competition in five types of model airplanes, the Xi'an Aeronautical Institute team took first place with a total score of 2454 seconds (the highest possible score being 2700 seconds).

### 1956年航空模型赛闭幕

1956-08-20
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    1956年航空模型赛闭幕
    据新华社19日讯　1956年航空模型竞赛大会今天举行了三级橡筋动力模型飞机和一级自由飞活塞式发动机模型飞机的飞行比赛以后，已经全部结束。
    获得三级橡筋动力模型飞机比赛第一名的是西安航空学院学生唐翊军，他的模型飞机三次飞行的总成绩达到了满分——五百四十秒。
    获得一级自由飞活塞式发动机模型飞机比赛第一名的刘明道也是西安航空学院的学生，他的模型飞机也得到了满分——三次飞行时间达到了五百四十秒。
    在十七日举行的二级橡筋动力模型飞机飞行比赛中，北京钢铁工业学院二年级学生寿尔康的模型飞机三次飞行时间也达到了五百四十秒，他得到了第一名。
    四天来，五种模型飞机飞行竞赛的结果，西安航空学院代表队以2454秒（最高分是二千七百秒）的总成绩获得第一名。
