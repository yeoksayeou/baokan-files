Title: Liberian President Replies to Chairman Mao

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd: Liberian President Tubman sent a telegram from Monrovia on July 28th to Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, expressing his gratitude for Chairman Mao's telegram on July 24th congratulating Liberia's National Day.

The telegram said: "I personally express my gratitude to you for the warm and good wishes you have expressed to the government and people of Liberia. I sincerely express my heartfelt wishes to you, and through you to the government and people of your great country, hoping that you will continue to maintain peace and wishing you success and prosperity."

### 利比里亚总统复电毛主席

1956-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    利比里亚总统复电毛主席
    新华社2日讯　利比里亚总统杜伯曼七月二十八日从蒙罗维亚打电报给中华人民共和国主席毛泽东，对毛泽东主席在七月二十四日致电祝贺利比里亚国庆表示感谢。
    这封电报说：“我亲自对你向利比里亚政府和人民表达的盛情和美好的愿望表示感谢。我衷心地向你，并且通过你向你们伟大国家的政府和人民，表示诚恳的愿望，希望你们继续保持和平，并且祝你们成功和繁荣。”
