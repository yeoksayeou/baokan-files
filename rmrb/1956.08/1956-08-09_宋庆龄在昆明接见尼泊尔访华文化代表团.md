Title: Soong Ching Ling Receives Nepalese Cultural Delegation to China in Kunming
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, August 8th. Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the entire Nepalese Cultural Delegation to China, headed by Chairman B. Sharma, at the Cuihu Hotel in Kunming this afternoon.

　　Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling had a cordial conversation with Chairman Sharma. The famous Nepalese poet Tula Dhar presented his collection of poems, Mrs. Joshi presented her necklace, and Miss Shah presented her bracelet to Soong Ching Ling to express their respect.

　　The Nepalese Cultural Delegation to China arrived in Kunming for a visit on the 6th.

### 宋庆龄在昆明接见尼泊尔访华文化代表团

1956-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄在昆明接见尼泊尔访华文化代表团
    新华社昆明8日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄，今天下午在昆明翠湖宾馆接见了以巴·夏尔马团长为首的尼泊尔访华文化代表团来昆明的全体代表。
    宋庆龄副委员长和夏尔马团长亲切交谈。尼泊尔著名诗人屠拉达尔将他的诗集，约西夫人将她的项圈，沙小姐将她的手镯分别赠给宋庆龄，表示敬意。
    尼泊尔访华文化代表团是六日到昆明访问的。
