Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to Egyptian Liberation Conference and Trade Unions Supporting Egypt's Reclamation of the Suez Canal Company
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st Dispatch: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions today sent a telegram to the Egyptian Liberation Conference and the Standing Committee of the Egyptian Republic Workers' Union.

The telegram said: "On behalf of all Chinese workers, please accept our heartfelt congratulations on Egypt's decision to nationalize the Suez Canal Company. We warmly support this heroic and just action and wish the Egyptian workers and all the Egyptian people continuous new victories in the struggle to defend national sovereignty and safeguard national interests."

### 我国总工会致电埃及解放大会和工会  支持埃及收回苏彝士运河公司

1956-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我国总工会致电埃及解放大会和工会
    支持埃及收回苏彝士运河公司
    据新华社31日讯　中华全国总工会今天打电报给埃及解放大会和埃及共和国工人工会常设委员会。
    电报说：“谨代表中国全体工人对埃及关于把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的决定表示衷心的祝贺。我们热烈支持这一英勇的正义行动，并祝埃及工人及全体埃及人民在保卫国家主权，维护民族利益的斗争中不断获得新的胜利。”
