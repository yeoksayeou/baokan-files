Title: Chinese Society of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Medicine Organizes Experts to Investigate Grassland and Superior Livestock Breeds
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Chinese Society of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Medicine and its local branches are organizing experts to conduct investigations and research on grasslands and superior breeds of livestock.

    The content of this investigation includes a grassland survey in the Eastern United Banner of Xilin Gol League in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, studying the rational use of grasslands, and improving grassland management methods to provide a reference for formulating grassland grazing plans. Superior livestock breeds include the Han sheep of Shandong Province, the Shouyang sheep of Shanxi Province, the Ningxiang pig of Hunan Province, and the Qinchuan cattle of Shaanxi Province. In addition, the Society of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Medicine is also organizing a group of experts to conduct key research on livestock parasites in Hailar City in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Wuwei County in Gansu Province to gain experience for future general surveys.

    This investigation and research work was commissioned to the Society of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Medicine by the Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture to improve livestock breeds and rationally utilize grasslands.

### 中国畜牧兽医学会组织专家  调查草原和家畜优良品种

1956-08-01
第3版()
专栏：

    中国畜牧兽医学会组织专家
    调查草原和家畜优良品种
    据新华社讯　中国畜牧兽医学会和各地分会正在组织专家进行关于草原和家畜优良品种的调查研究工作。
    这次调查的内容包括内蒙古自治区锡林郭勒盟东部联合旗的草原调查，研究合理使用草原，改善草原管理方法，供制定草原放牧规划的参考；家畜优良品种有山东省的寒羊，山西省的寿阳羊，湖南省的宁乡猪，陕西省的秦川牛等。此外，畜牧兽医学会还组织一部分专家在内蒙古自治区海拉尔市和甘肃省武威县进行家畜寄生虫的重点研究工作，为将来普遍调查取得经验。
    这项调查研究工作是农业部畜牧兽医总局为了改良家畜品种和合理利用草原委托畜牧兽医学会进行的。
