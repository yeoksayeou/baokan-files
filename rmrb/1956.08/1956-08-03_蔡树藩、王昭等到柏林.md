Title: Cai Shufan, Wang Zhao and Others Arrive in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 1st. Cai Shufan, Deputy Director of the Chinese State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and three others arrived in Berlin by plane on July 31st at the invitation of the National Sports Commission of the German Democratic Republic.

Wang Zhao, Chairman of the Chinese Public Security Sports Association and Deputy Minister of Public Security, and two others, arrived on the same plane at the invitation of the Dynamo Sports Association of the Ministry of Interior of the German Democratic Republic.

That afternoon, they transferred from Berlin to Leipzig to attend the Second All-German Sports Festival, which will be held there from August 2nd to 5th.

### 蔡树藩、王昭等到柏林

1956-08-03
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    蔡树藩、王昭等到柏林
    新华社柏林1日电　中国体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩等四人应民主德国国家体育委员会的邀请，在七月三十一日乘飞机到达柏林。
    中国公安体育协会主席、公安部副部长王昭等三人应民主德国内务部狄纳莫体育协会的邀请也在这一天同机到达。
    他们在当天下午就从柏林转赴来比锡参加八月二日到五日在那里举行的第二届全德体育运动节。
