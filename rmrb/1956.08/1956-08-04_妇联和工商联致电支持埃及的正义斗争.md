Title: Women's Federation and ACFIC Send Telegrams Supporting Egypt's Just Struggle
Author:
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st: The All-China Women's Federation today sent a telegram to Egyptian women, supporting the Egyptian people's just action of nationalizing the Suez Canal Company.
    The telegram said: "To the Egypt Liberation Congress, and our Egyptian sisters: Chinese women resolutely support the Egyptian government and people's just measures to nationalize the Suez Canal Company. This action has made a valuable contribution to safeguarding Egypt's national independence, opposing colonialist aggression, and defending world peace. We extend our heartfelt respect to you and wish you continued success in your heroic struggle and in the cause of building your motherland."
    Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd: The All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce today sent a telegram to the Egyptian Federation of Commerce. The telegram said: We Chinese industrialists and businessmen warmly support Egypt's just action of nationalizing the Suez Canal Company. Please convey to your government and people our heartfelt congratulations and respect.

### 妇联和工商联致电支持埃及的正义斗争

1956-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    妇联和工商联致电支持埃及的正义斗争
    新华社1日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会今天打电报给埃及妇女，支持埃及人民将苏彝士运河公司收归国有的正义行动。
    电报说：“埃及解放大会转埃及姊妹们：中国妇女坚决支持埃及政府和人民把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的正义措施。这一行动对于维护埃及的民族独立、反对殖民主义者的侵略以及保卫世界和平作出了有价值的贡献。我们特向你们表示衷心的敬意，并预祝你们的英勇斗争和建设祖国的事业取得不断胜利。”
    据新华社3日讯　中华全国工商业联合会今天打电报给埃及商业联合会。电报说：我们中国工商业者热烈支持埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的正义行动。请通过你们向你们政府和人民表示我们衷心的祝贺和敬意。
