Title: Icelandic Foreign Minister Issues Statement Demanding Revision of Iceland-U.S. Treaty
Author:
Date: 1956-08-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Reykjavik, 31st -- Icelandic Foreign Minister Guðmundur Í. Guðmundsson issued a statement on the 29th demanding the revision of the Iceland-U.S. treaty signed in 1951, in order to take over the U.S. military base on Icelandic territory.

Guðmundsson said that Iceland itself could maintain this base to cope with any "deterioration" in the international situation.

The Icelandic Foreign Minister's statement was also circulated in Washington on the 30th through the Icelandic Embassy in the United States.

### 冰岛外长发表声明  要求修改冰美条约

1956-08-02
第6版()
专栏：

    冰岛外长发表声明
    要求修改冰美条约
    新华社31日讯　雷克雅未克消息：冰岛外交部长格·格德门森二十九日发表声明，要求修改1951年签订的冰岛—美国条约，以便接管美国在冰岛的领土上的军事基地。
    格德门森说，冰岛自己能够维持这个基地，以应付国际局势万一发生的“恶化”。
    冰岛外交部长的这篇声明，在三十日又通过冰岛驻美国大使馆在华盛顿加以散发。
