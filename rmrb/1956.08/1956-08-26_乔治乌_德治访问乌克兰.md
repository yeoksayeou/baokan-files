Title: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej Visits Ukraine
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kiev, August 25th: According to TASS, Kiev, August 25th, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and Moghioroş, First Deputy Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers, along with a group of agricultural experts, arrived in Kiev on August 23rd for an unofficial visit at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Ukrainian Communist Party and the Ukrainian Council of Ministers.
　　They will visit Ukraine for a few days to learn about the agricultural production of Ukrainian collective farms, machine tractor stations, and state-owned farms.

### 乔治乌—德治访问乌克兰

1956-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    乔治乌—德治访问乌克兰
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社基辅二十五日讯：罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治乌—德治和罗马尼亚部长会议第一副主席莫吉奥罗希以及一批农业专家应乌克兰共产党中央委员会和乌克兰部长会议的邀请，在八月二十三日到达基辅作非正式的访问。
    他们将在乌克兰访问几天，了解乌克兰集体农庄、机器拖拉机站和国营农场的农业生产情况。
