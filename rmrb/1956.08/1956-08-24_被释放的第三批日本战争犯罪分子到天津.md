Title: The Third Group of Released Japanese War Criminals Arrive in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, August 23rd: The third group of 354 released Japanese war criminals arrived in Tianjin by train this afternoon from Fushun.

After they got off the train, they held a farewell ceremony on the platform for the train crew who had accompanied them to Tianjin. They then took cars to a hotel in the city center to rest. They will stay here waiting for a Japanese ship to pick them up and take them back to Japan.

They departed from Fushun last night.

### 被释放的第三批日本战争犯罪分子到天津

1956-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    被释放的第三批日本战争犯罪分子到天津
    据新华社天津23日电　第三批被释放的354名日本战争犯罪分子今天下午从抚顺乘火车到达天津。
    他们下车后，在站台上向送他们到天津来的火车乘务员举行了告别式，随后乘坐汽车到市中心的一家饭店休息。他们将住在这里等候日本轮船接他们回日本。
    他们是昨晚由抚顺出发的。
