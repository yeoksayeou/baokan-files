Title: Hungarian Folk Orchestra Leaves Beijing for Korea
Author:
Date: 1956-08-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: The Hungarian Folk Orchestra left Beijing by train this morning to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

On the evening of the 25th, Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian watched the farewell performance of the Hungarian Folk Orchestra in Beijing and received the leaders of the orchestra, congratulating them on the success of their performances in China.

### 匈牙利民间乐队离京去朝鲜

1956-08-28
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利民间乐队离京去朝鲜
    据新华社27日讯　匈牙利民间乐队今天上午乘火车离开北京去朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。
    25日晚，国务院副总理李先念观看了匈牙利民间乐队在北京举行的告别演出，并且接见了乐队的领导人，祝贺他们在中国演出的成功。
