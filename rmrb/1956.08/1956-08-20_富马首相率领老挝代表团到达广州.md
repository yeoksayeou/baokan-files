Title: Prime Minister Phouma Leads Lao Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 19th: At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, and the government delegation of the Kingdom of Laos he led, arrived in Guangzhou by special plane this afternoon.

The special plane carrying Prince Souvanna Phouma and others landed at Guangzhou's Tianhe Airport at 2:10 PM, where the flags of the Kingdom of Laos and the People's Republic of China were displayed.

After Prince Souvanna Phouma and others disembarked from the plane, Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, Commander of the Guangzhou Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Wang Zhuoru, Acting Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who had come from Beijing to welcome the distinguished guests, stepped forward to shake hands with them. The band played the national anthems of Laos and China.

Accompanied by Governor Tao Zhu and others, Prince Souvanna Phouma reviewed the honor guard lined up at the airport. Prince Souvanna Phouma and members of the delegation accepted flowers presented by children from Guangzhou.

Subsequently, Prince Souvanna Phouma and members of the delegation were introduced to the heads of Party and government organizations of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, generals of various arms of the Guangzhou Garrison, representatives of democratic parties and people's organizations, as well as the Polish Consul in Guangzhou and the Soviet Acting Consul in Guangzhou, and shook hands with them.

Afterwards, Prince Souvanna Phouma delivered a speech to the more than three thousand welcomers.

Then, accompanied by Tao Zhu and others, Prince Souvanna Phouma and others passed through crowds waving bouquets and applauding, and boarded cars to the Yingbin Hotel to rest. Along the way, the welcomers threw colorful paper flowers and streamers at the distinguished guests and scrambled to present them with bunches of fresh flowers.

At 5:00 PM, accompanied by Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, the distinguished guests toured Guangzhou City by car and visited the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall and the Guangzhou Museum located in the Zhenhai Tower on Yuexiu Mountain. Prince Souvanna Phouma and others climbed to the top of the five-story building of this Ming Dynasty architecture on the peak of Yuexiu Mountain, from where they looked out over the city of Guangzhou.

In the evening, Prince Souvanna Phouma and all members of the delegation attended a welcome banquet hosted by Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province. (Attached picture)

### 富马首相率领老挝代表团到达广州

1956-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    富马首相率领老挝代表团到达广州
    据新华社广州19日电　应中华人民共和国政府邀请前来中国访问的老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王和他所率领的老挝王国政府代表团，在今日下午乘专机到达广州。
    梭发那·富马亲王等乘坐的专机在下午二时十分，降落在悬挂着老挝王国和中华人民共和国国旗的广州天河机场。
    梭发那·富马亲王等走下飞机后，在飞机前面欢迎他们的广东省省长陶铸、广州市市长朱光、中国人民解放军广州驻军首长文年生中将和从北京前来广州迎接贵宾们的外交部礼宾司代司长王倬如等人，都上前同贵宾们握手。乐队奏起老挝、中国两国的国歌。
    梭发那·富马亲王由陶铸省长等陪同检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。梭发那·富马亲王和代表团团员接受了广州市儿童的献花。
    接着，梭发那·富马亲王和代表团团员被介绍给前来欢迎的广东省、广州市党政机关的负责人、广州驻军各兵种将领、各民主党派和人民团体的代表以及波兰驻广州的领事和苏联驻广州的代理领事，并且同他们握手。
    随后，梭发那·富马亲王对三千多欢迎者发表了讲话。
    梭发那·富马亲王等接着由陶铸等陪同穿过挥舞着花束、夹道鼓掌欢呼的人群，登车前往迎宾馆休息。途中，欢迎者向贵宾们抛掷五彩缤纷的纸花和彩色纸条，并且争着把一束一束的鲜花献给贵宾们。
    下午五时，贵宾们由广州市市长朱光陪同乘汽车游览了广州市区，参观了中山纪念堂和设在越秀山镇海楼的广州博物馆，梭发那·富马亲王等登上了越秀山顶峰这座明代建筑的五层高楼，从那里凭栏眺望了广州市区。
    晚间，梭发那·富马亲王和代表团全体团员出席了广东省省长陶铸举行的欢迎宴会。（附图片）
