Title: On the Resolutions of the Arab League Political Committee: Iraqi Prime Minister Expresses Approval; Tunisian Government Announces Support for Egypt's Reclamation of the Suez Canal Company
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 15th, TASS dispatch: According to "Al-Barada" newspaper, Iraqi Prime Minister Nuri al-Said, in a conversation with the Syrian ambassador, expressed his approval of the Arab League Political Committee's resolution on fully supporting Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company.

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis dispatch: A spokesman for the Tunisian Foreign Ministry said on August 15th that Tunisia fully supports Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company. He said that the Tunisian government believes that this is "Egypt, an independent and autonomous country, exercising its rights."

The spokesman pointed out that the Egyptian government "has clearly stated its intention to respect the freedom of all users."

He asked Western countries to "abandon all measures that could exacerbate tensions in the Mediterranean and endanger international peace and security."

### 对阿拉伯联盟政委会的决议  伊拉克首相表示赞同  突尼斯政府宣布支持埃及收回运河公司

1956-08-17
第6版()
专栏：

    对阿拉伯联盟政委会的决议
    伊拉克首相表示赞同
    突尼斯政府宣布支持埃及收回运河公司
    新华社16日讯　塔斯社大马士革十五日讯：据“巴拉达报”报道，伊拉克首相努里·赛义德在同叙利亚大使谈话时，表示赞同阿拉伯联盟政治委员会关于完全支持埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的决议。
    新华社16日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯外交部的一个发言人八月十五日说，突尼斯完全支持埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有。他说，突尼斯政府认为，这是“一个独立和自主的国家埃及在行使它的权利。”
    这个发言人指出，埃及政府“已经明确地申明它打算尊重所有使用者的自由”。
    他要求西方国家“放弃能够使地中海的紧张局势加剧和危及国际和平和安全的一切措施”。
