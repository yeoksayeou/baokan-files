Title: Albania Vigorously Carries Out Agricultural Cooperativization Movement; Nearly a Quarter of the Country's Cultivated Land Now Belongs to Cooperatives

Author:

Date: 1956-08-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 6th - According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency: The speed of agricultural cooperativization in Albania has greatly accelerated since the end of last year. There are now 695 agricultural production cooperatives, more than double the number at the end of last year. The land owned by agricultural production cooperatives across the country already accounts for more than 24% of the country's cultivated land area.

    The Second Five-Year Plan, which began this year, stipulates further vigorous development of the cooperativization movement. By 1960, agricultural production cooperatives will increase to 1,800, and the land owned by cooperatives and collective farms will reach more than 85% of the country's cultivated land area.

### 阿尔巴尼亚大力开展农业合作化运动  现在全国耕地将近四分之一是合作社的

1956-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚大力开展农业合作化运动
    现在全国耕地将近四分之一是合作社的
    新华社布拉格6日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：阿尔巴尼亚农业合作化的速度从去年年底以来大为加快。现在已经有六百九十五个农业生产合作社，比去年年底增加了一倍以上。全国的农业生产合作社拥有的土地已经占全国耕地面积的24％以上。
    今年开始的第二个五年计划规定进一步大力发展合作化运动。到1960年，农业生产合作社将增加到一千八百个，合作社和共耕社所拥有的土地将达到全国耕地面积的85％以上。
