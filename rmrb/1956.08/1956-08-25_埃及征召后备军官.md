Title: Egypt Conscripts Reserve Officers
Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, August 24th, Cairo dispatch: Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser held talks on the evening of August 23rd with Minister of the Army and Navy, Commander-in-Chief of the Egyptian Army Abdel Hakim Amer, and Kamal el-Din Hussein, commander of the newly established National Liberation Army.
    Hussein also held a meeting with regional commanders on the same day to discuss organizing national defense.
    On the same day, the Egyptian Army Headquarters issued a communique, conscripting all reserve officers. The communique stated: "All reserve officers serving in government, administrative agencies, commercial firms, and companies who have not yet received individual conscription notices should report to the National Guard Headquarters on the morning of August 25th (Saturday)."

### 埃及征召后备军官

1956-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及征召后备军官
    新华社24日讯　开罗消息：埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔八月二十三日晚间同陆海军部长、埃及陆军总司令阿卜杜勒·哈基姆·阿密尔和新近建立的民族解放军司令卡迈勒丁·侯赛尼举行了会谈。
    侯赛尼当天也曾经同各地方司令官举行会议，讨论组织国防的问题。
    同一天，埃及陆军总部发表一项公报，征召所有后备军官入伍。公报说：“在政府、行政机构、商行和公司服务而还没有接到单独征召通知的所有后备军官，应在八月二十五日（星期六）上午到国民警卫队总部报到。”
