Title: Peaceful Negotiations to Resolve the Suez Canal Issue; World Peace Council Condemns Countries Attempting to Use Violence
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, August 16th: TASS reported the following statement from the Secretariat of the World Peace Council:

    At a time when international tensions are beginning to ease, and there is every right to hope that reason will be used in resolving international disputes instead of violence, the statements made by certain individuals regarding Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal have caused anxiety in world public opinion. The environment in which the crisis is forming, the military measures of certain countries, and the threatening remarks of certain responsible politicians are causing deep concern among peace-loving people. Despite differing views on this issue, no one is willing to revert to the kind of approach that must be completely abandoned in international relations in the current situation.

    On the eve of the London Conference, the World Peace Movement once again condemns the use of violence in resolving international disputes.

    The World Peace Movement does not intend to comment on the nature of the London Conference, nor does it intend to predict the possible outcome of this conference (we hope that it will contribute to a fair and reasonable solution to the problem). The World Peace Movement believes that the problem can only be completely resolved through consultations among the countries concerned. Such a solution should take into account Egypt's complete independence and sovereignty, as well as the legitimate interests of all countries in using the canal.

    The World Peace Movement calls on the public in all countries to support the principles that form the basis of the United Nations Charter, and calls on governments to act consistently in accordance with these principles.

### 和平协商解决苏彝士运河问题  世界和平理事会谴责企图使用暴力的国家

1956-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    和平协商解决苏彝士运河问题
    世界和平理事会谴责企图使用暴力的国家
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社维也纳十六日讯：世界和平理事会书记处发表声明如下：
    当国际紧张局势开始好转，可以有充分权利希望在解决国际争端时诉诸理智而不使用暴力的时候，某些人士就埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有问题发表的言论引起世界舆论的不安。形成危机的环境、某些国家的军事措施以及某些负责政治人物的威吓言论，使热爱和平的人深感忧虑。尽管大家对这个问题的看法不同，但是没有任何人愿意在当前情况下重新采用那种在国际关系中必须彻底摒弃的方式。
    在伦敦会议开会前夕，世界和平运动再一次谴责在解决国际争端时采取暴力手段。
    世界和平运动不打算对伦敦会议的性质有所评价，不打算预言这个会议的可能的结果（我们希望它能有助于公平合理地解决问题）。世界和平运动认为，只有通过有关国家的协商，才能够彻底解决问题。这种解决办法应当考虑到埃及的完全独立和主权以及各国使用运河的合法利益。
    世界和平运动呼吁各国公众支持作为联合国宪章基础的那些原则，呼吁各国政府经常本着这些原则行事。
