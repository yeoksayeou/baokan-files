Title: Nepalese King Receives Chinese Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 28th. On August 27th at 7:30 PM, His Majesty King Mahendra of Nepal received the Chinese Government Delegation headed by Pan Zili, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Nepal, at the Royal Palace. The Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of Nepal were present.

### 尼泊尔国王接见我政府代表团

1956-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王接见我政府代表团
    新华社新德里28日电　8月27日晚七时三十分，尼泊尔国王马亨德拉陛下在皇宫接见了以中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔王国特命全权大使潘自力为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团，在座的有尼泊尔总理和外交部长。
