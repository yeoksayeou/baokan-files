Author:
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 1

Premier Zhou's Letter of Thanks to Premier Viliam Široký

Dear Comrade Viliam Široký:

    Please allow me to thank you, and through you, the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic, for the gift of a complete set of agricultural machinery needed to build a state-owned farm in our country, and for sending seven experts to provide specific guidance on the installation and use of these agricultural machines.

    I would like to inform you that the Chinese government has decided to use this batch of machinery to equip a state-owned farm established in Huanghua County, Cangxian Prefecture, Hebei Province. In order to permanently commemorate your kindness, we will name this farm the "Sino-Czech Friendship Farm."

    The establishment of this farm provides a good opportunity for exchanging advanced experiences in agricultural production between the two countries, and will further develop the friendship between our two peoples.

    Dear Comrade, please allow me to take this opportunity to extend my sincere respect to you.

                Zhou Enlai (signed)
                July 31, 1956, in Beijing

### 周总理给西罗基总理的感谢信

1956-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理给西罗基总理的感谢信亲爱的威·西罗基同志：
    请允许我向你并通过你向捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府致谢，感谢捷克斯洛伐克政府送给我国一套为建设一个国营农场所需的全套农业机器，并派来七位专家，具体指导这些农业机器的安装和使用。
    我愿意告诉你：中国政府已经决定把这批机器用来装备一个设立在河北省沧县专区黄骅县的国营农场。为了永久纪念你们的盛情，我们将把这个农场命名为“中捷友谊农场”。
    这一农场的建立，对于两国间交换农业生产方面的先进经验提供了良好的机会，并且将使我们两国人民间的友谊获得进一步的发展。
    亲爱的同志，请让我借此机会向你致诚挚的敬意。
                周恩来（签署）1956年7月31日于北京
