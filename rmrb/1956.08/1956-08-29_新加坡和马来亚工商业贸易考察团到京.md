Title: Singapore and Malayan Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, August 28th: The Singapore Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation and the Federation of Malaya Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation arrived in Beijing by train this morning. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

    Goh Teck Phuat, head of the Singapore Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation, and Li Yen-nien, head of the Federation of Malaya Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation, and the members of the delegations, shook hands with Li Zhuchen, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce and Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Sha Qianli, Secretary General of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Le Songsheng, Standing Committee Member, who came to the station to welcome them.

    Also at the station to welcome them were people from industrial and commercial circles, returned overseas Chinese, and overseas Chinese students.

    David Marshall, Advisor to the Singapore Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation and former Chief Minister of Singapore, who is currently visiting Beijing, also came to the station to greet them.

### 新加坡和马来亚工商业贸易考察团到京

1956-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    新加坡和马来亚工商业贸易考察团到京
    新华社28日讯　新加坡工商业贸易考察团和马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团，今天早晨乘火车到达北京。他们是应中华全国工商业联合会和中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请来中国访问的。
    新加坡工商业贸易考察团团长高德根、马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团团长李延年和团员们下车后，同前来车站欢迎的中华全国工商业联合会副主任委员、中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席李烛尘，中华全国工商业联合会秘书长沙千里、常务委员乐松生等一一握手。
    到车站欢迎的，还有工商界人士，归国华侨和华侨学生等。
    正在北京访问的新加坡工商业贸易考察团顾问、前新加坡首席部长戴维·马歇尔也到车站迎接。
