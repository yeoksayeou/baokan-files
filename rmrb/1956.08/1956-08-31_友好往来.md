Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-08-31
Page: 5

    Friendly Exchanges
    △The Chinese cultural, educational, and health workers delegation, led by Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Hanoi by train at 4:30 PM on the 28th. The delegation will participate in Vietnam's National Day celebrations and inspect the cultural, educational, and health undertakings of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    △The Chinese Art Troupe, invited to Romania for performance tours, arrived in Bucharest from Bulgaria by train on August 28th.
    △French National Assembly member and former Prime Minister Edgar Faure, at the invitation of the Soviet parliamentary delegation, arrived in Moscow by plane on the evening of August 28th. On the same day, Bulganin and Khrushchev received him and his wife.
    △The Executive Committee of the Bulgarian National Assembly announced that the Bulgarian National Assembly will send a delegation to Yugoslavia for a visit at the end of September.
    △The Japanese workers' delegation, headed by Koichi Sakuma, Vice Chairman of the Japan Council of Public Corporation and National Enterprise Workers' Unions, concluded its visit to China, which lasted more than ten days, and left Guangzhou on the 30th via Hong Kong to return home.

### 友好往来

1956-08-31
第5版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △由中华人民共和国教育部副部长韦悫率领的中国文化教育卫生工作者代表团在28日下午四时半乘火车到达河内。代表团将参加越南的国庆节，并且考察越南民主共和国的文化教育和卫生事业。
    △应邀到罗马尼亚作访问演出的中国艺术团8月28日乘火车从保加利亚到达布加勒斯特。
    △法国国民议会议员、前总理富尔应苏联议员团的邀请，在8月28日晚间搭飞机到达莫斯科。当日，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫接见了他和他的夫人。
    △保加利亚国民议会执行委员会宣布，保加利亚国民议会将在9月底派代表团到南斯拉夫访问。
    △以日本官公厅工会协议会副主席佐久间孝一为首的日本工人代表团一行六人结束了在中国为期十多天的访问，30日离开广州取道香港回国。
