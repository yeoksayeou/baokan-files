Title: Burmese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Visits China
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 17th: At the invitation of Zhang Wentian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Wu Wang, the Burmese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 17th for a visit to our country. Wang Ping, a specialist from the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the airport to greet him. Hao Sibayar, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in China, and Zhou Liang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Embassy in China, also went to the airport to greet him.

Wu Ba Mao, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy in China, also went to the airport to greet him.

### 缅驻苏大使访华

1956-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    缅驻苏大使访华
    据新华社17日讯　应中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天邀请前来我国访问的缅甸驻苏联大使吴旺已在十七日乘飞机到达北京。前往机场迎接的有外交部礼宾司专员王屏。蒙古驻华大使馆临时代办浩斯巴雅尔和越南民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办周亮也前往机场迎接。
    前往机场迎接的还有缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办吴巴茂。
