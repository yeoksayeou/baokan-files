Title: Zedongbal Hosts Banquet for the Signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Technical Assistance Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-08-31
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, August 30th: On the evening of the 29th, at 8 PM, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Zedongbal, held a banquet for the signing of the Sino-Mongolian agreement on economic and technical assistance from China to Mongolia. At the banquet, Zedongbal said: The economic negotiations between the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic, conducted on the basis of unlimited friendship and fraternal heartfelt understanding, have concluded today, and the relevant agreement has been signed. He then said: China, at the request of Mongolia, is providing Mongolia with 160 million rubles in grants, which is a sincere fraternal assistance from the great Chinese people to the working people of Mongolia. He also pointed out that this selfless assistance from the Chinese people is a concrete manifestation of the further development of the new type of relations between the people of the countries of the socialist camp. Zedongbal said: We express our heartfelt gratitude to the great Chinese people and their government and party for providing fraternal assistance.
    He concluded by proposing a toast to the great Chinese people, to the brilliant achievements of the Chinese people, and to the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian people.
    Following this, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, He Ying, delivered a speech, expressing his warm congratulations on the signing of the agreement with excitement. He emphasized that this agreement aims at the further development of friendly relations between China and Mongolia and the common prosperity and upsurge of the two countries' economies. He also said that the signing of this agreement opens up new prospects for the friendly relations of mutual assistance and cooperation between China and Mongolia, and also shows the enhancement of the new type of international relations among the fraternal countries of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union. Finally, he proposed a toast to the prosperity of the Mongolian People's Republic and to the eternal fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian people.
    The Vice-Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers, Zengde, and the head of the Chinese economic delegation to Mongolia, Lu Xuzhang, also spoke at the banquet. They unanimously pointed out that the signing of this agreement marks a new development in the relations of mutual assistance and cooperation between China and Mongolia and the sincere desire of the two countries to seek common economic upsurges.
    The banquet was attended by leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Mongolian government, officials of the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia, members of the Chinese and Mongolian economic delegations, and diplomatic envoys from various countries.

### 为中蒙签订经济和技术援助协定  泽登巴尔举行宴会

1956-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    为中蒙签订经济和技术援助协定
    泽登巴尔举行宴会
    新华社30日讯　乌兰巴托消息：29日晚8时，蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔为中蒙关于中国给予蒙古经济和技术援助协定的签订举行了宴会。泽登巴尔在宴会上说：中华人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国之间在无限友谊和兄弟般的由衷谅解的基础上进行的经济谈判，今天已告结束，并且签订了有关的协定。他接着说：中国应蒙古的请求给予蒙古一亿六千万卢布的无偿援助，这是伟大中国人民给予蒙古劳动人民的真诚的兄弟援助。他并指出：中国人民的这种无私的援助是社会主义阵营各国人民之间的兄弟般的新型关系的进一步发展的具体表现。泽登巴尔说：我们向给予兄弟般援助的伟大中国人民及其政府和党表示衷心的感谢。
    他最后提议为伟大中国人民，为中国人民所获得的光辉成就，为中蒙两国人民之间的兄弟般的牢不可破的友谊干杯。
    接着，中国驻蒙古大使何英致词，他以兴奋的心情对这次协定的签订表示热烈的祝贺。他强调说：这个协定是以中蒙两国友好关系的进一步发展和两国经济的共同繁荣和高涨为目的的。他还说，这项协定的签订为中蒙两国的互助合作的友好关系开拓了新的远景，同时也显示着以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各兄弟国家新型的国际关系的增进。最后他提议为蒙古人民共和国的繁荣，为中蒙两国人民永恒的兄弟般的友谊干杯。
    蒙古部长会议副主席曾德和中国赴蒙经济代表团团长卢绪章也相继在宴会上讲了话。他们一致指出，这个协定的签订标志着中蒙两国互助合作关系的新发展和两国求得共同经济高涨的真诚愿望。
    出席这次宴会的有：蒙古人民革命党和蒙古政府的领导人员，中国驻蒙古大使馆的官员，中蒙两国经济代表团的人员和各国外交使节。
