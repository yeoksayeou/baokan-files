Title: President Sukarno Hosts Banquet for Soong Ching Ling, Soong Ching Ling Visits Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo
Author:
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 15th: President Sukarno held a banquet on August 14th to entertain Song Qingling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China.

Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, who accompanied Song Qingling on her visit to Indonesia, and Huang Zhen, the Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, and his wife were also invited to attend the banquet. The host and guests had a very cordial conversation in a friendly atmosphere.

Before the banquet, Song Qingling presented Sukarno with gifts she brought from China.

In the afternoon, Song Qingling, accompanied by Jakarta Mayor Sudiro, went to the Indonesian Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery. There, she laid a wreath to the sound of mournful music played by a military band.

Song Qingling received a warm welcome from the citizens wherever she went. The Chinese and Indonesian flags were proudly displayed side by side, flying above the roof of the State Palace.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 15th: Indonesian President Sukarno invited Song Qingling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, to dinner on the evening of August 14th.

Also at the dinner were Vice President Hatta and his wife, First Deputy Speaker Arifin and his wife, the wife of Ali Sastroamidjojo, the wife of Foreign Minister Abdulgani, and the wife of the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wijopranoto. The Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, and his wife, Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, and others were also present.

The hosts and guests had a warm conversation during the meal.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 15th: Song Qingling, the Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic China, paid a courtesy visit to Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo at his official residence this morning. Accompanying Song Qingling were the Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, and his wife, and Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China.

### 苏加诺总统设宴招待宋庆龄  宋庆龄拜会沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理

1956-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统设宴招待宋庆龄
    宋庆龄拜会沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理
    新华社雅加达15日电　苏加诺总统八月十四日举行宴会招待中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄。
    随同宋庆龄到印度尼西亚访问的中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏以及中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和夫人也应邀出席了宴会。主宾在友好的空气中十分恳切地交谈。
    在宴会以前，宋庆龄把她从中国带来的礼物赠送给苏加诺。
    下午，宋庆龄由雅加达市长苏迪罗陪同前往印度尼西亚革命烈士墓。这那里，她在军乐队的哀乐声中献了花圈。
    宋庆龄路过的地方，受到了市民的热烈欢迎。中国国旗和印度尼西亚的国旗高高地并列着，飘扬在国家宫的屋顶之上。
    新华社雅加达15日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在八月十四日晚上邀请中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄共进晚餐。
    同进晚餐的有副总统哈达和夫人、第一副议长阿里芬和夫人、阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约的夫人、外交部长阿卜杜加尼的夫人、印度尼西亚驻中国大使维约普拉诺托的夫人。中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和夫人、中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏等。
    主人和客人们在进餐时进行了热诚的交谈。
    新华社雅加达15日电　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄今天上午到沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理的官邸作礼节性的拜会。陪同宋庆龄一同前往的有中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和夫人以及中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏。
