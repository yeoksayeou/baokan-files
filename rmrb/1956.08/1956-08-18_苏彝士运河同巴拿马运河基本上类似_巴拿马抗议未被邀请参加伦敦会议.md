Title: The Suez Canal is Fundamentally Similar to the Panama Canal; Panama Protests Not Being Invited to the London Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, August 17th dispatch: According to a United Press London dispatch: On the eve of the London conference, Panama formally protested against not being invited to participate in the London conference to discuss the Suez Canal issue.

    Panama's Ambassador to the United Kingdom, Arias, said in a conversation after visiting the Permanent Under-Secretary at the British Foreign Office, Kirkpatrick, on the afternoon of the 15th, "Panama believes that it is necessary to participate in this conference discussing the Suez Canal issue." He said: "The Suez Canal is fundamentally similar to the Panama Canal."
    Arias said: "The Panama Canal is built on Panamanian territory. Panama is the owner of the Canal Zone, although it has ceded certain rights of maintenance, operation, sanitation, and protection of this canal to the United States.
    "The Panamanian government expresses its dissatisfaction with not having the opportunity to participate in the meeting held in London. Our attitude is not only because of the similarities between the Suez Canal and the Panama Canal, but also because Panama ranks sixth among the countries using the Suez Canal."

### 苏彝士运河同巴拿马运河基本上类似  巴拿马抗议未被邀请参加伦敦会议

1956-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

    苏彝士运河同巴拿马运河基本上类似
    巴拿马抗议未被邀请参加伦敦会议
    新华社17日讯　据合众社伦敦消息：在伦敦会议前夕，巴拿马对它没有被邀请参加讨论苏彝士运河问题的伦敦会议一事正式提出了抗议。
    巴拿马驻英国大使阿里亚斯十五日下午在访见了英国外交部常务次官柯克帕特里克以后发表的谈话中说，“巴拿马认为它参加这次讨论苏彝士运河问题的会议，是必要的。”他说：“苏彝士运河同巴拿马运河有基本上的类似之处。”
    阿里亚斯说：“巴拿马运河建筑在巴拿马领土内。巴拿马是运河地区的主人，尽管它把这条运河的保养、经营、卫生和保护的某些权利出让给美国了。
    “巴拿马政府表示它对它没有机会参加在伦敦举行的会议这一点感到不满。我们的这种态度不仅是由于苏彝士运河和巴拿马运河之间有类似之处，而且是因为巴拿马在使用苏彝士运河的国家中居第六位。”
