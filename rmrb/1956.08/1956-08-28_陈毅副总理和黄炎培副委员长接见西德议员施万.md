Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Chairman Huang Yanpei Receive West German Parliamentarian Schwan
Author:
Date: 1956-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received Schwan, a member of the Free Democratic Party of the West German Federal Parliament who is currently visiting China, this morning. In the afternoon, Huang Yanpei, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, also received Schwan.

### 陈毅副总理和黄炎培副委员长接见西德议员施万

1956-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理和黄炎培副委员长接见西德议员施万
    新华社27日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天上午接见了现在中国访问的西德联邦议院自由民主党议员施万。下午，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培曾接见了施万。
