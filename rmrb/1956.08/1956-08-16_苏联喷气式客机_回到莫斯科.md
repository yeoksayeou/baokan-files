Title: Soviet Jet Airliner Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 5

    Soviet Jet Airliner
    Returns to Moscow
    (Our Moscow dispatch, 15th) The "TU-104" jet airliner, returning from Beijing, landed at Vnukovo Airport at 7:20 PM Moscow time on the 14th, where it was greeted by a large group of staff from the Soviet Civil Aviation Administration, airport staff, aircraft pilots, and navigators.
    The "TU-104" jet airliner took off from Beijing Airport at 8:05 AM Beijing time on the 14th. The plane stopped in Irkutsk for several hours to test the airport runway's performance. Due to headwinds, the actual flight time for this 6,350-kilometer Beijing-Moscow journey was approximately nine hours.

### 苏联喷气式客机  回到莫斯科

1956-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联喷气式客机
    回到莫斯科
    本报莫斯科15日电　十四日莫斯科时间下午七点二十分，从北京归来的“TY—104”式喷气客机降落在伏努科夫机场，受到一大群苏联民用航空局工作人员、机场工作人员、飞机驾驶员和领航员的欢迎。
    “TY—104”式喷气客机是十四日上午北京时间八点五分从北京机场起飞的。飞机在伊尔库次克停留了若干小时，目的是试验机场跑道的性能。由于逆风的缘故，这一次北京—莫斯科六千三百五十公里的航程的实际飞行时间约九小时。
