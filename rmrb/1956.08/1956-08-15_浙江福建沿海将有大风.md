Title: Strong Winds Expected Along the Coasts of Zhejiang and Fujian

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon warning at 8:00 PM on August 14th: It is expected that by 2:00 PM tomorrow, the center of the typhoon will reach the sea 170 kilometers directly east of Taiwan. There will be force 5-7 winds along the southern coast of Zhejiang. There will be force 6-8 winds along the coast of Fujian.

### 浙江福建沿海将有大风

1956-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    浙江福建沿海将有大风
    据新华社14日讯　八月十四日下午八点，中央气象科学研究所发布台风警报：预计明天下午二时，台风中心将抵达台湾的正东一百七十公里的海面上，浙江南部沿岸有五—七级大风。福建沿岸有六—八级大风。
