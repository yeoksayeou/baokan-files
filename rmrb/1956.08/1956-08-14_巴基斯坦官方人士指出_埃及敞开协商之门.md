Title: Pakistani Officials Point Out: Egypt Opens the Door to Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, August 13th: According to a report by the Pakistan United Press, senior Pakistani officials said on the evening of August 12th that Egyptian President Nasser's statement was encouraging because it kept "the door open" for negotiated settlement of the Suez Canal issue.

Officials in Karachi adopted a cautious attitude and did not express detailed opinions on all aspects of the statement, as it needs to be carefully studied. Foreign Minister Chaudhry said that the cabinet will hold a special meeting today to discuss the Suez Canal issue.

### 巴基斯坦官方人士指出  埃及敞开协商之门

1956-08-14
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦官方人士指出
    埃及敞开协商之门
    新华社喀喇蚩13日电　据巴基斯坦联合通讯社报道，巴基斯坦高级官方人士八月十二日晚上说，埃及总统纳赛尔的声明是令人鼓舞的，因为它使协商解决苏彝士运河问题“继续敞开大门”。
    喀喇蚩官方人士态度谨慎，没有对声明的各方面发表详细意见，因为需要仔细研究。外交部长乔德里说，内阁将在今天举行特别会议讨论苏彝士运河问题。
