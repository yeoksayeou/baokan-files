Title: Increase in Urban People's Savings Deposits
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: According to the latest statistics from the People's Bank of China, urban people's savings deposits in banks had reached more than 1.67 billion yuan by the end of June, an increase of more than 110 million yuan compared to the end of 1955. Currently, various regions are in the process of retroactively issuing the wage differences resulting from the wage reform from April to July, and there is a further upward trend in urban people's savings deposits.

### 城市人民储蓄存款增加

1956-08-01
第3版()
专栏：

    城市人民储蓄存款增加
    据新华社讯　据中国人民银行最近统计，城市人民在银行的储蓄存款，到六月底止，已经达到十六亿七千多万元，比1955年底增加一亿一千多万元。目前各地正在补发四月到七月因工资改革而增加的工资差额，城市人民的储蓄存款又有进一步增长的趋势。
