Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 2

    Lovely Karamay—Urho Oilfield
    Karamay is advancing by leaps and bounds, and the drilling rigs have extended to the scenic Urho area, more than 100 kilometers away from Karamay. In the Karamay-Urho oil area, which spans several thousand square kilometers, comprehensive exploration, deep exploration, and shallow exploration have been fully launched, with plans to basically explore the Karamay-Urho oil area within two to three years.
    Left picture: Installing derricks and mud pipelines using the rapid installation method.
    Bottom picture: The derrick of the first deep exploration well erected in the Urho area.

    Left picture: The engineering units of the Production and Construction Corps are building dormitories for oil workers.

### 可爱的克拉玛依—乌尔禾油区（图片）

1956-08-29
第2版()
专栏：

    可爱的克拉玛依—乌尔禾油区
    克拉玛依在一日千里地前进，钻架已伸向距离克拉玛依一百多公里的风景优美的乌尔禾地区。在克拉玛依—乌尔禾油区几千平方公里的土地上，全面开始了勘探、深探、浅探，计划将在两三年内基本上探清克拉玛依—乌尔禾油区。
