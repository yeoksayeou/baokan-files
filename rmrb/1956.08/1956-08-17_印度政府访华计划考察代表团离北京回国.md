Title: Indian Government Visiting Delegation for Planning and Research Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: The three members of the Indian Government Visiting Delegation for Planning and Research, Titambar Pant, R. Natarajan, and Moni Mukherjee, left Beijing by plane today to return to their country.

The delegation arrived in Beijing on July 12th. During their visit to China, they held separate discussions with the relevant responsible persons of the State Council's Eighth Office and the State Planning Commission, and visited Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang, Anshan, Changchun, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan, and other places.

Premier Zhou Enlai received the delegation on the evening of August 14th and held a ninety-minute conversation with them. Xue Muqiao, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, held a farewell banquet for the delegation.

### 印度政府访华计划考察代表团离北京回国

1956-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    印度政府访华计划考察代表团离北京回国
    据新华社16日讯　印度政府访华计划考察代表团提坦巴尔·潘特，尔·纳塔拉詹和莫尼·慕克吉等三人，今天在北京乘飞机回国。
    代表团是在七月十二日到达北京的。在中国访问期间，曾经和国务院第八办公室和国家计划委员会等单位的有关负责人分别举行座谈；并且在北京、天津、沈阳、鞍山、长春、上海、广州、武汉等地参观访问。
    周恩来总理在八月十四日晚接见了代表团，和他们进行了九十分钟的谈话。国家计划委员会副主任薛暮桥曾为代表团举行送别宴会。
