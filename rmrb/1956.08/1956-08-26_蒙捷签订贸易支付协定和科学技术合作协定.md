Title: Mongolia and Czechoslovakia Sign Trade Payment Agreement and Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 5
　　
Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: The governments of the Mongolian People's Republic and the Czechoslovak Republic signed an agreement on trade payments for 1956-1957, a protocol on non-trade payments, and an agreement on scientific and technological cooperation between Mongolia and Czechoslovakia on August 23rd.

　　The scientific and technological cooperation agreement stipulates the broad application of the advanced scientific and technological achievements of both countries in economic construction, and mutual assistance in this regard. This agreement will be implemented under the most favorable conditions for both countries.

### 蒙捷签订贸易支付协定和科学技术合作协定

1956-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙捷签订贸易支付协定和科学技术合作协定
    据新华社24日讯　蒙古人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国在八月二十三日签订了两国政府关于1956—1957年贸易支付协定，关于非贸易支付议定书以及蒙捷两国关于科学技术合作的协定。
    科学技术合作协定规定，广泛地运用两国在经济建设方面的先进的科学和技术成就，并且在这方面互相帮助。这个协定将以对两国最有利的条件实行。
