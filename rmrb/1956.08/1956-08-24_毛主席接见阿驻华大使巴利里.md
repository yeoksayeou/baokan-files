Title: Chairman Mao Receives Albanian Ambassador to China, Bali Li
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Bali Li, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China, on the afternoon of the 23rd. Accompanying the Ambassador was Bino, First Secretary of the Albanian Embassy in China. Also present at the meeting were Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Li Huichuan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs, and Wang Yutian, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 毛主席接见阿驻华大使巴利里

1956-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见阿驻华大使巴利里
    新华社23日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东，在二十三日下午接见了阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻华大使巴利里。随同受接见的有阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书比诺。接见的时候在座的，有外交部副部长张闻天、苏联东欧司副司长李汇川和礼宾司副司长王雨田。
