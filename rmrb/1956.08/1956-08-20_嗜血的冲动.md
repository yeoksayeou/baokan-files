Title: "A Bloodthirsty Urge"
Author:
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 6

    “A Bloodthirsty Urge”
    An American high-ranking officer in the occupying army in West Germany, Briante, recently murdered a German woman and chopped her body into pieces. When the court interrogated Briante, the American officer shouted, "This is not the first time in my life I have killed a woman. I often have a bloodthirsty urge."

### “嗜血的冲动”

1956-08-20
第6版()
专栏：国际点滴

    “嗜血的冲动”
    美国驻西德占领军的一个高级军官布列安特不久前谋杀了一个德国妇女，并把她的尸体剁成碎块。法院审讯布列安特时，这位美国军官高声嚷道：“这并不是我生平第一次杀死一个女人。我常常有一种嗜血的冲动”。
