Title: Egypt Begins Mobilization Against Imperialist Threat: Government Conscripts Reserve Officers; Millions of Young People Prepare to Enlist
Author:
Date: 1956-08-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 6th, Cairo Dispatch: The Egyptian people have begun mobilizing to guard against the imperialist military threat.

Cairo Radio broadcast that the Egyptian government has ordered reserve officers to report for active duty.

The radio also broadcast an order from the Egyptian Minister of Education, Kamal el-Din Hussein, instructing student camps currently on summer vacation to return.

According to the Egyptian Middle East News Agency, in the face of Western military threats, thousands of Egyptian youths have volunteered for military service. These volunteers began training on August 5th.

Sources say that Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser has returned to his headquarters in Cairo from Alexandria in order to deal with the situation.

Xinhua News Agency, August 6th: According to a Reuter's dispatch from Cairo today, quoting Egyptian official sources, over one million Egyptian youths between the ages of twenty-five and thirty-five have expressed their readiness to take up arms to defend the nationalization of the Suez Canal. National Guard barracks throughout Egypt have begun accepting volunteers for enlistment and providing them with military training.

Egyptian Minister of Education Hussein held talks with National Guard Commander Abdel Fattah Fayed at his office today. A spokesperson said that the meeting discussed how to mobilize youth for armed resistance in order to cope with "the aggressive nations persisting in their unjust attitude towards the nationalization of the Suez Canal."

Xinhua News Agency, August 6th: According to TASS, Cairo, August 5th: Mass rallies and meetings continue to be held throughout Egypt in support of the government's decision to nationalize the Suez Canal Company.

The Egyptian Federation of Petroleum and Chemical Industry Workers' Unions, with 35,000 members, announced its full support for the Egyptian government's actions and condemned foreign interference.

A meeting of the Egyptian National University Academic Council, attended by university presidents and department heads, passed a resolution endorsing the nationalization of the Suez Canal, considering it "the most important step in ensuring national economic independence and breaking free from foreign domination."

Newspapers report that many other trade union organizations, associations, and federations in Egypt, as well as student and youth groups across the country, have also held various gatherings in support of President Nasser's policies.

### 埃及开始动员对付帝国主义威胁  政府征召预备役军官　百万青年准备参军

1956-08-07
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及开始动员对付帝国主义威胁
    政府征召预备役军官　百万青年准备参军
    新华社6日讯　开罗消息：埃及人民开始动员以防备帝国主义的军事威胁。
    开罗电台广播说，埃及政府已经命令预备役军官服现役。
    电台还广播了埃及教育部长卡迈勒丁·候赛尼的命令，指令正在度暑假的学生营回来。
    据埃及中东通讯社报道，在西方的军事威胁下，已经有数以千计的埃及青年志愿要求服兵役。志愿服兵役的青年已经在八月五日开始受训。
    有消息说，埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔从亚历山大港回到开罗后，已经搬到他的总部居住，以便应付局势。
    新华社6日讯　据路透社今天自开罗引述埃及的官方消息说，一百多万年龄在二十五岁到三十五岁之间的埃及青年已经表示准备拿起武器来保卫苏彝士运河国有化。埃及全国各地的国民警卫队军营已经开始接受志愿兵入伍，给他们施行军事训练。
    埃及教育部长侯赛尼今天在办公室同国民警卫队司令阿卜杜勒·法塔·法德举行了会谈。一位发言人说，这次会谈讨论了怎样动员青年来进行武装抵抗的问题，以便应付“侵略国坚持他们对苏彝士运河国有化的不公正的态度”。
    据新华社6日讯　据塔斯社开罗五日消息：埃及各地继续举行群众大会和会议支持政府把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的决定。
    拥有三万五千名会员的埃及石油和化学工业工人工会联合会宣布完全支持埃及政府的行动，谴责外国的干涉。
    有各大学校长和系主任参加的埃及全国大学学术理事会会议通过一项决议，赞同苏彝士运河国有化，认为这是“保证国家经济独立和摆脱外国统治的最重要的步骤”。
    报纸说，埃及其他许多工会组织、协会和联合会，以及全国各个学生和青年团体也举行了各种集会，支持纳赛尔政府的政策。
