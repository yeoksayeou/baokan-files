Title: Soviet Jet Airliner Departs Beijing for Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Soviet TU-104 jet airliner departed Beijing at 8:05 AM Beijing time today, flying back to Moscow.

    This aircraft, after arriving in Beijing, aroused great interest among people from all walks of life in Beijing. From the 10th to the 13th, a total of more than 21,000 people, including Chinese aviation industry staff, scientists, engineers, experts, heads of government departments, cadres, students, workers, and officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army, visited the aircraft. Foreign experts in Beijing and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China also visited.

    Chinese and Soviet civil aviation personnel held a symposium on the 11th. Chernikov, Deputy Director of the Transport Aviation Administration of the Civil Aviation Administration of the Soviet Union, and Voisekhovich, Chief Engineer of Transport Aviation of the Moscow Administration, detailed the performance, characteristics, and maintenance of the TU-104 jet airliner at the meeting. On the 13th, Chinese aviation engineering and technical personnel also learned about some issues related to Soviet civil aviation and the technology of the TU-104 jet airliner from Soviet aviation personnel on the aircraft.

    During their stay in Beijing, the aviation personnel on the TU-104 jet airliner also engaged in sightseeing activities.

    When the airliner left Beijing, responsible persons and employees of the Civil Aviation Administration of China went to the airport to see them off.

### 苏联喷气客机离京飞回莫斯科

1956-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联喷气客机离京飞回莫斯科
    新华社14日讯　苏联TY——104式喷气客机今天上午北京时间八时零五分离开北京飞返莫斯科。
    这架飞机来京后，曾引起了北京各界人士极大兴趣。从十日到十三日，中国航空工业工作人员、科学家、工程师、专家、政府各部门负责人、机关干部、学生、工人、解放军官兵等共二万一千多人前往参观了飞机。前往参观的还有在京的各国专家们和各国驻华外交官员。
    中、苏民用航空人员在十一日举行了座谈会。苏联民用航空总局运输航空管理局飞行副局长切尔尼科夫和莫斯科管理局运输航空总工程师伏依则霍维奇在会上详细介绍了TY——104式喷气客机的性能、特点以及维护等问题。在十三日，中国航空工程技术人员还在飞机上向苏联航空人员了解和学习了关于苏联民用航空方面和TY——104式喷气客机技术方面的一些问题。
    在京期间，TY——104式喷气客机上的航空人员还进行了参观游览活动。
    客机在离开北京时，中国民用航空局负责人和员工们曾到机场欢送。
