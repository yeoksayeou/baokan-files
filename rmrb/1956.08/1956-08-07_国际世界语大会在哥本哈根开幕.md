Title: International Esperanto Congress Opens in Copenhagen
Author:
Date: 1956-08-07
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, August 6th: The International Esperanto Congress opened in Copenhagen on August 5th, with approximately two thousand delegates from forty-three countries attending.

    The All-China Esperanto Association sent representatives to the congress. This is the first time that Chinese representatives have attended this congress in the name of a national organization, and it is also the first time in twenty years that the Soviet Union has attended.

    This congress is a friendly gathering of Esperanto workers from various countries. Academic papers on Esperanto issues submitted by the delegates will be discussed at the congress. The congress is scheduled to last for seven days.

### 国际世界语大会在哥本哈根开幕

1956-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    国际世界语大会在哥本哈根开幕
    新华社6日讯　哥本哈根消息：国际世界语大会八月五日在哥本哈根开幕，参加这次大会的有四十三个国家的大约两千个代表。
    中华全国世界语协会派代表出席了大会。中国的代表以全国性组织的名义出席这个大会，这还是第一次，苏联也是二十年来第一次出席参加这个大会。
    这次大会是各国世界语工作者的一次友谊性聚会，在会上将讨论代表们提出的有关世界语问题的学术论文。大会预定举行七天。
