Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (RCCK) Liaoning Provincial Committee and Shenyang Municipal Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 10th: The Second Congress of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (RCCK) Liaoning Province and the First Congress of the RCCK Shenyang City were jointly held in Shenyang City from the 6th to the 8th. The meetings announced the formal establishment of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Liaoning Provincial Committee and the Shenyang Municipal Committee, and elected Ning Wu and sixteen others as members of the Provincial Committee; Zhou Dafu and sixteen others as members of the Municipal Committee.

The congress approved the 1956-1957 work plan report of the Preparatory Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Liaoning Province and the work summary report of the Preparatory Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Shenyang City.

Ning Wu, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the RCCK, spoke on behalf of the RCCK Central Committee at the meeting, saying: Our future task is to unite more closely around the Chinese Communist Party, continue to consolidate and expand the people's democratic united front, and contribute to socialist construction and the peaceful liberation of Taiwan. Zhang Xuexuan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the CPC, made a report at the meeting. Gong Tianmin and Zhong Junlin delivered congratulatory messages to the congress on behalf of the provincial and municipal CPPCCs and the provincial and municipal democratic parties, respectively.

On the last day of the congress, a letter to the military and political personnel in Taiwan from the Second Congress of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Liaoning Province was adopted.

### 民革辽宁省沈阳市委员会成立

1956-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    民革辽宁省沈阳市委员会成立
    据新华社沈阳10日电　中国国民党革命委员会辽宁省第二次党员大会和沈阳市第一次党员大会自六日到八日在沈阳市联合举行。会上宣布中国国民党革命委员会辽宁省委员会和沈阳市委员会正式成立，并选举宁武等十七人为省委员会委员；周达夫等十七人为市委员会委员。
    大会通过了中国国民党革命委员会辽宁省筹备委员会1956年—1957年工作规划报告和中国国民党革命委员会沈阳市筹备委员会工作总结报告。
    中国国民党革命委员会中央常务委员宁武代表民革中央委员会在会上讲话说：我们今后的任务是更加紧密地团结在中国共产党的周围，继续巩固和扩大人民民主统一战线，为社会主义建设和争取和平解放台湾贡献力量。中共辽宁省委员会统一战线工作部部长张雪轩在会上作了报告。巩天民和钟俊麟分别代表省、市政协和省、市各民主党派向大会致了贺词。
    大会最后一天，通过了中国国民党革命委员会辽宁省第二次党员大会告台湾军政人员书。
