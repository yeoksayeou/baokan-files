Title: Libyan Newspapers Oppose the Internationalization of the Suez Canal
Author:
Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tripoli, August 6th. The Libyan newspaper "Tarabulus al-Gharb" opposes the demands of the three Western powers to place the Suez Canal under international administration. The newspaper wrote: "We do not know what logic the imperialist gentlemen are using to defend their proposal to implement international management of the Suez Canal. Attempting to use the fact that the Suez Canal is an international waterway as the basis for their proposal is laughable. Currently, all countries in the world are closely linked to each other, and all bays, ports, and airports of any country in the world have international significance."
    The newspaper pointed out that the proposal to implement international management of the Suez Canal is nothing more than an attempt to establish control over Egypt.

### 利比亚报纸反对苏彝士运河国际化

1956-08-08
第5版()
专栏：

    利比亚报纸反对苏彝士运河国际化
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社的黎波里六日讯：利比亚“塔拉勃卢斯加布报”反对西方三国关于使苏彝士运河置于国际管理之下的要求。报纸写道：“我们不知道帝国主义大人先生们尽力替自己提出的关于对苏彝士运河实行国际管理的建议作辩护究竟用的是什么逻辑。企图用苏彝士运河是国际水道这一事实来作为自己建议的根据，是令人可笑的。目前，世界一切国家都彼此密切地联系起来了，世界任何国家的一切大海湾、港口和飞机场都具有国际的意义。”
    报纸指出，对苏彝士运河实行国际管理的建议不外是企图对埃及建立控制罢了。
