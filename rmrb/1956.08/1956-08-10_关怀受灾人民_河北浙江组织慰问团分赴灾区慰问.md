Showing Concern for Disaster-stricken People
	Hebei and Zhejiang Organize慰问团 to Visit Disaster Areas

　　Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, 9th. Seven Hebei Province慰问团 departed for disaster areas on the afternoon of August 8th. These seven groups were led by the heads of various departments of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and the directors of the Provincial Department of Agriculture and the Grain Bureau.

　　Ma Guorui and Gu Yunting, Secretaries of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Zhang Chengxian, Deputy Governor, will also visit Baoding, Tianjin, and Handan areas.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 8th. The Typhoon Disaster Area慰问团, composed of people from all walks of life in Zhejiang Province, departed from Hangzhou today.

　　The慰问团 consists of more than 400 people from units such as the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Zhejiang Provincial People's Committee, the Zhejiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Zhejiang Provincial and Hangzhou Municipal local organizations of various democratic parties, and various people's organizations. The head of the团 is Sha Wenhan, Governor of Zhejiang Province.

　　The慰问团 will visit Zhoushan, Ningbo, Yuhang and other areas that have been severely affected by the typhoon disaster, to convey the concern of the Party and the People's Government to the disaster-stricken people in these areas, as well as various emergency measures for disaster relief, prevention, and restoration of production.

　　Twenty medical teams are traveling to the disaster areas with the慰问团.

　　After the慰问团 finishes its work, many cadres will remain to continue helping with the work in the disaster areas. Rescuers fought the flood for more than ten hours and rescued all the trapped farmers.

　　On August 6th, a certain unit stationed in Fengtai dispatched more than 370 people to Shifosi, Daxing County, to cooperate with the local people in repairing the river embankment.

　　The leading organs of the troops stationed in Tianjin decided that all subordinate units should immediately prepare flood prevention teams. They also decided that the military hospital should form two medical teams to go to the flood prevention sites with the troops.

### 关怀受灾人民  河北浙江组织慰问团分赴灾区慰问

1956-08-10
第3版()
专栏：

    关怀受灾人民
    河北浙江组织慰问团分赴灾区慰问
    新华社保定9日电　河北省的七个慰问团八日下午分赴灾区慰问。这七个团分别由中共河北省委各部部长和省农业厅、粮食厅厅长带领。
    中共河北省委书记处书记马国瑞、谷云亭和副省长张承先等负责同志亦将分别到保定、天津、邯郸地区进行慰问。
    新华社杭州8日电　浙江省各界人民组成的台风灾区慰问团，今日从杭州出发。
    慰问团由中共浙江省委、浙江省人民委员会、中国人民解放军浙江军区、各民主党派浙江省和杭州市地方组织以及各人民团体等单位四百多人组成。团长是浙江省省长沙文汉。
    慰问团将到受台风灾害较严重的舟山、宁波、余杭等地区进行慰问，向这些地区的受灾人民传达党和人民政府对他们的关怀，以及有关救灾、防灾和恢复生产的各项紧急措施。
    随同慰问团前往灾区的有二十个医疗队。
    慰问团慰问结束后，将要有许多干部留下继续帮助灾区工作。抢救人员与洪水搏斗了十多小时，全部救出了被困农民。
    驻丰台某部队八月六日出动了三百七十多人到大兴县石佛寺配合当地人民抢修河堤。
    驻天津部队领导机关决定由所属各单位立即编好防汛队伍，还决定由军医院组成两个医疗队，随部队前往防汛地点。
