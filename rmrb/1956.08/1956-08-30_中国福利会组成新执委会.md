Title: China Welfare Institute Forms New Executive Committee
Author:
Date: 1956-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 28th. The China Welfare Institute, founded by Soong Ching Ling, has formed a new Executive Committee. The committee recently held a meeting in Shanghai to discuss work plans for the institute's cultural, health, and other welfare units. The new executive members include: Ke Qingshi, Chen Pixian, Qi Yanming, Li Dequan, Zhang Yun, Xia Yan, Liu Jiping, Jin Zhonghua, and fifteen others.

The China Welfare Institute has a history of eighteen years. Its predecessor was the China Defense League, later renamed the China Welfare Fund, and finally renamed the China Welfare Institute in August 1950. The China Welfare Institute currently has seven institutions: the Children's Theatre Troupe, Children's Times Press, Children's Palace, International Peace Maternity and Child Health Hospital, Kindergarten, Nursery, and China Construction Society.

### 中国福利会组成新执委会

1956-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中国福利会组成新执委会
    新华社上海28日电　由宋庆龄创办的中国福利会已经组成新的执行委员会。这个委员会最近在上海举行会议，讨论了该会所属文化、卫生等福利单位的工作规划。新的执行委员有：柯庆施、陈丕显、齐燕铭、李德全、章蕴、夏衍、刘季平、金仲华等十五人。
    中国福利会已经有十八年的历史，它的前身是保卫中国大同盟，以后改名中国福利基金会，1950年8月才改称为中国福利会。中国福利会现有儿童剧团、儿童时代社、少年宫、国际和平妇幼保健院、幼儿园、托儿所和中国建设社等七个事业单位。
