Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts Grand Banquet to Welcome Lao Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: Premier Zhou Enlai stated at a grand banquet tonight, welcoming the Lao dignitaries, that the Chinese government and people highly respect Laos' declared policy of peace and neutrality, and welcome and appreciate the friendly gestures from the Kingdom of Laos.

In his speech, Premier Zhou first extended a warm welcome to the government delegation of the Kingdom of Laos, led by Prince Souvanna Phouma. He told the guests that China will steadfastly be a good friend and good neighbor of Laos, based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

In his reply, Prince Souvanna Phouma emphasized that the differences in systems between China and Laos should not be an obstacle to understanding and friendship, and reiterated Laos' support for the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. The Prime Minister said, "I hope that the visit of the Lao delegation will create a friendly atmosphere between Laos and China, and contribute to the easing of the world situation we have witnessed."

These speeches by the leaders of the Chinese and Lao governments, expressing their shared desire for mutual friendship and support for the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, sparked a storm of applause from the more than seven hundred Chinese and foreign guests attending the banquet.

Tonight's banquet began at 7:30 PM. Prince Souvanna Phouma and all the distinguished guests of the Lao government delegation, accompanied by Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai, entered the hall. The band played a welcome tune, and Chinese government officials and people from all walks of life gathered in the hall to welcome them with enthusiastic applause. Accompanying the distinguished guests were also Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, and Li Xiannian; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Peng Zhen; Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng; Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Cheng Qian; Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong; and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Prince Souvanna Phouma delivered speeches at tonight's banquet (full text on page four). At the end of their speeches, they respectively proposed toasts to the growing friendly relations between China and Laos, to the unity of the Asian and African peoples and the victory of the Bandung spirit, to world peace, to the prosperity of the Kingdom of Laos, to the health of King Sisavang Vong, to the prosperity of the Chinese people, and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong! As they finished their speeches, the band played the Lao national anthem and the Chinese national anthem respectively.

During the banquet, Mr. Bounlap Nhouyvanisavong, Lieutenant Sounthara Doun Sasorith, Ms. Souphan Doun Sasorith, and Tao Thongdy sang the Lao song "White Frangipani," and Chinese artists also performed Chinese operas. After the banquet, Prince Souvanna Phouma and others, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and others, came to the orchestra and Chinese folk band to enjoy the Chinese musical pieces "Dance of the Golden Snake" and "The Days of Emancipation."

Tonight, the ambassadors of Poland, Czechoslovakia, Pakistan, India, Norway, Afghanistan, Denmark, Romania, and Albania, the chargés d'affaires of the Netherlands and the United Kingdom, the acting chargés d'affaires of Bulgaria, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Hungary, the Soviet Union, Finland, Yugoslavia, Mongolia, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the German Democratic Republic, Burma, Switzerland, and Indonesia, the Egyptian commercial representative, and the head of the Cambodian economic delegation, as well as the Burmese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, who is currently visiting Beijing, attended the banquet.

### 周恩来总理盛宴欢迎老挝代表团

1956-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理盛宴欢迎老挝代表团
    新华社21日讯　周恩来总理今晚在欢迎老挝贵宾们的盛大宴会上说，中国政府和人民十分尊重老挝所宣布的和平中立政策，并且欢迎和感谢来自老挝王国方面的友好表示。
    周总理在他的讲话中，首先表示热烈欢迎以梭发那·富马亲王为首的老挝王国政府代表团。他向来宾们说，中国将坚定不移地在和平共处五项原则的基础上，做老挝的好朋友和好邻居。
    梭发那·富马亲王在他的答词中强调中国和老挝之间制度的不同不应该成为谅解和友谊的障碍，并且重申老挝对于和平共处五项原则的支持。这位首相说，我希望老挝代表团的访问将在老挝和中国两国之间造成友好的气氛，并且对于我们所看到的世界局势的和缓作出贡献。
    中国和老挝两国政府领导人这些表达两国互相友好和支持和平共处五项原则的共同意愿的讲话，激起了参加宴会的七百多位中外来宾的暴风雨般的掌声。
    今晚的宴会在下午七时半举行。梭发那·富马亲王和老挝王国政府代表团的全体贵宾们由朱德副主席、周恩来总理陪同步入大厅。乐队奏起了欢迎曲，聚集在大厅里的中国政府官员和社会各界人士以热烈的鼓掌来欢迎他们。陪同贵宾们的，还有国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席程潜，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，外交部副部长张闻天。
    周恩来总理和梭发那·富马亲王相继在今晚的宴会上讲话（全文见第四版），他们在讲话结束时分别提议为中、老两国友好关系日益发展，为亚非人民的团结和万隆精神的胜利，为世界和平，为老挝王国的繁荣，为老挝国王西萨汪·冯陛下的健康，为中国人民的繁荣，为毛泽东主席的健康干杯！当他们讲话结束时，乐队分别奏老挝国歌和中国国歌。
    宴会进行中，汶拉·努发尼萨冯先生、顺塔拉·敦·萨索里特中尉、苏芳·敦·萨索里特女士和陶·通迪先生唱了老挝歌曲“白兰花”，中国的艺术家们也演唱了中国戏曲。在宴会结束后，梭发那·富马亲王等由周恩来总理等陪同来到管弦乐队和中国民间乐队前面，欣赏了中国乐曲“金蛇狂舞”和“翻身的日子”。
    今晚，波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、巴基斯坦、印度、挪威、阿富汗、丹麦、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚的大使，荷兰和英国的代办，保加利亚、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、匈牙利、苏联、芬兰、南斯拉夫、蒙古、越南民主共和国、德意志民主共和国、缅甸、瑞士和印度尼西亚的临时代办，埃及商务代表和柬埔寨经济代表团团长，以及正在北京访问的缅甸驻苏联大使都出席了宴会。
