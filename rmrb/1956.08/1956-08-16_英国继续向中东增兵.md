Title: Britain Continues to Increase Troops in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Nicosia, August 15th: According to a United Press report from Nicosia, approximately 40 British military transport planes landed at the Nicosia military airport on the evening of the 14th. This marks the largest airlift since Britain began strengthening its presence in the Middle East.

The report also stated that the 20,000-ton ocean liner "New Australia" departed from Southampton on the evening of the 14th, carrying approximately 3,000 troops bound for the Middle East.

### 英国继续向中东增兵

1956-08-16
第6版()
专栏：

    英国继续向中东增兵
    据新华社15日讯　据合众社尼科西亚消息，十四日晚间约有四十架英国军用运输机在尼科西亚军用机场着陆。这是英国开始加强中东实力以来最大的一次空运。
    消息还说，两万吨的远洋航轮“新澳大利亚号”已经载着军队约三千人在十四日晚间离开南安普敦驶往中东。
