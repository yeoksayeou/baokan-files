Title: Souvanna Phouma's Speech at Beijing Airport
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 1

    At Beijing Airport
    Souvanna Phouma's Speech
    Mr. Prime Minister, Your Excellencies, Ladies and Gentlemen:
    The Laotian delegation and I are deeply moved by your warm and friendly welcome. Please accept our sincere thanks.
    I am honored to express to the government and people of the People's Republic of China the friendly sentiments of the government and people of the Kingdom of Laos, as well as our most sincere wishes for the happiness, prosperity, and peace of the Chinese people.
    In accepting your kind invitation, we are very pleased to be able to restore and develop the friendly contacts between us, which you established with the leaders of Laos in Geneva in July 1954 and in Bandung in April 1955. For Laos to pursue a policy of peace, it is necessary and desirable to re-establish relations with our great neighbor, China. Historically, we have had friendly ties with this great neighbor, China.
    Our two countries live side by side, and there are no doubtful disputes between us to harm our relations, so it is normal for us to strive to promote greater mutual understanding.
    Laos is a small country, but its geographical location makes it understand its responsibilities. Laos will never do anything that its neighbors might feel is a threat. Therefore, the Kingdom of Laos pursues a strict policy of neutrality and will not participate in military alliances.
    We hope to devote ourselves, to the best of our ability, to the development of the ideals of friendship, fraternity, and peace. These ideals are particularly embodied in the declaration of the Five Principles, which both the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Laos recognize and uphold.
    It is in this spirit that we have come to you.
    I congratulate and firmly believe that our visit will achieve the purpose of China and Laos gaining mutual understanding on the basis of friendly relations.

### 在北京飞机场上  梭发那·富马的讲话

1956-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    在北京飞机场上
    梭发那·富马的讲话总理先生、各位阁下、女士们、先生们：
    老挝代表团和我本人对于你们这样热烈和友好的欢迎非常感动，请接受我们诚恳的感谢。
    我很荣幸能够向中华人民共和国政府和人民表达老挝王国政府和人民的友好感情，以及我们对中国人民幸福、繁荣与和平的最衷心的祝愿。
    在接受您好意的邀请的时候，我们感到很高兴能够重新恢复并且发展我们之间的友好接触，这些接触是您曾经在1954年7月在日内瓦，以及在1955年4月在万隆的期间与老挝的领袖们建立起来的。对于老挝执行和平的政策路线来说，同我们伟大的邻邦——中国从新建立关系是必要的，也是我们所希望的。在历史上我们曾经同中国这个伟大的邻邦有过友好的联系。
    我们两国相毗为邻地生活着，我们之间也没有任何可疑的纠纷来损害我们的关系，因此我们努力促进更多的相互了解是正常的。
    老挝是一个小的国家，但是它的地理位置使它明白它的责任，老挝决不作任何足以被它的邻国们可能感到是一种威胁的事。因此，老挝王国奉行着严格的中立政策，并且将不参加军事同盟。
    我们希望以我们力量之所及，致力于友好、博爱与和平的理想的发展。这些理想特别体现在五项原则的声明中，而中华人民共和国和老挝王国都是承认和拥护五项原则的。
    我们就是本着这种精神来到你们这里。
    我祝贺，并且坚信我们的访问将达到这样一个目的，就是中国同老挝在友好关系的基础上获得相互了解。
