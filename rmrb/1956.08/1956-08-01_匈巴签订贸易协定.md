Title: Hungary and Pakistan Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, July 31st. Hungary and Pakistan signed a trade agreement in Karachi yesterday afternoon to expand trade between the two countries on the basis of mutual benefit.

The agreement stipulates that Hungary will supply Pakistan with machinery, electrical equipment, motorboats, and equipment for hydroelectric power plants, thermal power plants, and chemical manufacturing plants, etc. Pakistan will export jute, jute products, cotton, wool, and tea, etc.

### 匈巴签订贸易协定

1956-08-01
第5版()
专栏：

    匈巴签订贸易协定
    新华社喀喇蚩31日电　匈牙利同巴基斯坦昨天下午在喀喇蚩签订了一项贸易协定，以便在互利的基础上扩大两国之间的贸易。
    协定规定，匈牙利将给巴基斯坦供应机器、电气设备、汽艇以及水力发电厂、火力发电厂和化学品制造厂的设备等。巴基斯坦将输出黄麻、麻制品、棉花、羊毛和茶叶等。
