Title: Our Track and Field Athletes in the Soviet Union Break Five More National Records
Author:
Date: 1956-08-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 29th: Chinese track and field athletes broke five more national records on the second day of competition against the "Dynamo" Association Moscow track and field team on the 28th.

　　Zhou Lianli won the men's 110-meter hurdles race with a time of 15.4 seconds, breaking the official national record of 15.7 seconds held by Huang Yingjie.

　　In the javelin competition, Ma Changlu's result was 64.16 meters, surpassing the previous national record of 61.11 meters.

　　In the men's high jump competition, Li Dapei won with a jump of 1.90 meters, breaking his own national official record (1.89 meters).

　　Xu Shizhang finished fourth in the women's 400-meter race. Her time was 59.5 seconds, breaking Jiang Yumin's national record of 1 minute.

　　Li Bingcheng's discus throw was 42.32 meters, surpassing the previous national official record of 42.30 meters.

　　As a result of the two-day competition, the "Dynamo" team emerged victorious. It scored a total of 167.5 points, while the Chinese team scored 131.5 points.

### 我国田径运动员在苏联  又打破了五项全国纪录

1956-08-30
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    我国田径运动员在苏联
    又打破了五项全国纪录
    据新华社莫斯科29日电　中国田径运动员28日在同“狄纳莫”协会莫斯科市田径队进行的第二天比赛中又打破了五项全国纪录。
    周连立在男子一百一十公尺高栏赛跑中获胜，成绩是十五秒四，打破了黄英杰所保持的十五秒七的正式全国纪录。
    在标枪比赛中，马长路的成绩是六十四公尺十六公分，原来的全国纪录是六十一公尺十一公分。
    男子跳高比赛中，李大培以一公尺九十公分的成绩获胜，打破了他保持的全国正式纪录（一公尺八十九公分）。
    徐世璋在女子四百公尺赛跑中获得第四名。她的成绩是五十九秒五，打破姜玉民所保持的一分的全国纪录。
    李秉诚的铁饼成绩是四十二公尺三十二公分，原来全国正式纪录是四十二公尺三十公分。
    两天比赛的结果，“狄纳莫”队获得胜利。它一共得到一百六十七点五分，中国队得一百三十一点五分。
