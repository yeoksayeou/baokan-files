Title: Talks Between the CPSU Central Committee and the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada Representatives
Author:
Date: 1956-08-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th—TASS reports: On August 29th, representatives of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held talks with a delegation from the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, which had arrived in Moscow.

    Participating in the talks on the CPSU Central Committee side were: Central Committee First Secretary Khrushchev, Central Committee Secretary Suslov, and Central Committee member Ponomarev; on the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada side were: National Committee General Secretary Tim Buck, National Committee members Morris, Sulzberger, and Ealda.

    The talks took place in an atmosphere of sincere friendship and complete understanding between the two fraternal parties.

### 苏共中央和加拿大劳工进步党代表会谈

1956-08-31
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共中央和加拿大劳工进步党代表会谈
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：8月29日，苏联共产党中央委员会代表同到达莫斯科的加拿大劳工进步党代表团举行会谈。
    参加会谈的苏共中央方面有：中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫、中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫、中央委员波诺马烈夫；加拿大劳工进步党方面有：全国委员会总书记蒂姆·布克，全国委员会委员莫里斯、苏尔斯伯尔克和艾尔达。
    会谈是在两个兄弟党之间真诚友好和完全谅解的气氛中进行的。
