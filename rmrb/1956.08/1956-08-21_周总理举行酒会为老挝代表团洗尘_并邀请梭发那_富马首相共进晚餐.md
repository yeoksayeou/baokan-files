Title: Premier Zhou Holds Reception to Welcome Lao Delegation and Invites Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma to Dinner

Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th: Premier Zhou Enlai held a reception at Ziguang Pavilion in Zhongnanhai this evening to welcome Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, and the government delegation he leads.

At 6:05 PM, the distinguished guests arrived at Ziguang Pavilion. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, and Li Xiannian, along with Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian, greeted them at the entrance. The reception hall erupted in warm applause. Prince Souvanna Phouma and others clasped their hands together to express their gratitude.

Premier Zhou Enlai accompanied the guests around the reception hall, introducing them to Chinese state leaders and representatives from various circles. Afterwards, Premier Zhou Enlai invited the guests to sit down in a refined and beautiful courtyard behind the hall.

During the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma spoke at length about the situations in their respective countries. Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma said that Laos hoped to build its country peacefully. Premier Zhou Enlai also mentioned that China needed a peaceful environment for its construction. He said that only in this way could the country develop.

Premier Zhou Enlai said to Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma: Our two countries are like relatives, and we have had exchanges since very early times. He proposed a toast to the friendship between the two countries and to peace in the Far East.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith met in Bandung more than a year ago, and they sat together and conversed today. Vice Chairmen Li Jishen and Peng Zhen spoke to Member of Parliament Phoumi Vongvichit about the aspects of the People's Congress in China. Vice Chairman Peng Zhen said to the guests, "A distant relative is not as good as a close neighbor," and he expressed hope to further develop the friendly relations between the two countries.

Vice Chairmen Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Chen Shutong, heads of various departments of the Chinese state organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations, representatives from all walks of life, and Chinese and foreign journalists attended tonight's reception.

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, invited Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, to dinner at 7:00 PM on the 20th.

Also invited from the Kingdom of Laos were Vice Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith, and Minister of Finance, National Economy, and Planning, Leuam Insixiengmay.

Accompanying the dinner were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, and Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian.

### 周总理举行酒会为老挝代表团洗尘  并邀请梭发那·富马首相共进晚餐

1956-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理举行酒会为老挝代表团洗尘
    并邀请梭发那·富马首相共进晚餐
    新华社20日讯　周恩来总理今晚在中南海紫光阁举行酒会，为老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王和他所率领的政府代表团洗尘。
    晚上六时零五分，贵宾们来到了紫光阁。周恩来总理和副总理陈云、彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、李先念，外交部副部长张闻天在入口处迎接。这时，客厅响起来了热烈的欢迎掌声。梭发那·富马亲王等合十，表示感谢。
    周恩来总理陪同贵宾们在客厅里环行一周，介绍他们同中国国家领导工作人员以及各界人士一一见面。随后，周恩来总理请宾主到厅后一个雅致而美丽的庭院入座。
    席间，周恩来总理同梭发那·富马首相畅谈了两国的各方面情况。梭发那·富马首相说：老挝希望和平建设自己的国家。周恩来总理也谈到，中国需要和平的环境进行建设。他说，只有这样才能使国家得到发展。
    周恩来总理对梭发那·富马首相说：我们这两个国家是像亲戚一样的国家，很早以来就有了往来。他提议为两国的友谊和远东和平干杯。
    陈毅副总理同卡代·敦·萨索里特副首相一年多以前曾在万隆相识，今天他们坐在一起交谈着。李济深和彭真副委员长向彭·苏发那冯议员谈到了中国的人民代表大会方面的一些情况。彭真副委员长对客人说：“远亲不如近邻”，他希望能进一步发展两国间的友好关系。
    郭沫若、黄炎培和陈叔通副委员长，中国国家机关各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，社会各界人士和中外记者出席了今晚酒会。
    新华社20日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二十日下午七时，邀请老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王共进晚餐。
    老挝王国方面应邀参加晚餐的，还有副首相卡代·敦·萨索里特，财政、国家经济和计划部部长仑·英锡相迈。
    陪同进餐的，有国务院副总理陈云、陈毅、李先念，外交部副部长张闻天。
