Title: West German Nuclear Physicist Hans Kopfermann Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: West German nuclear physicist, Director of the First Physics Institute of the University of Mainz, Dr. Hans Kopfermann, arrived in Beijing by plane at noon today.

Dr. Hans Kopfermann is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 西德核子物理学家汉斯·克隆伯到京

1956-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    西德核子物理学家汉斯·克隆伯到京
    新华社21日讯　西德核子物理学家、美因兹大学第一物理研究所所长汉斯·克隆伯博士，今天中午乘飞机到达北京。
    汉斯·克隆伯博士是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的。
