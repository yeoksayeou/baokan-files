Title: Chairman Mao Receives Comrade Lovlien
Author:
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd - Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received E. Lovlien, Chairman of the Norwegian Communist Party, who was invited to visit China, on the afternoon of the 22nd.

Present at the reception were Zhu De, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, member of the CPC Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. (Attached photo)

Chairman Mao Zedong had a cordial conversation with Comrade Lovlien. Xinhua News Agency reporter Lü Houmin photographed.

### 毛主席接见洛夫林同志

1956-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见洛夫林同志
    新华社22日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东，在二十二日下午接见了应邀来中国访问的挪威共产党主席埃·洛夫林。
    接见的时候在座的有：中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记朱德，中共中央委员王稼祥和中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席和洛夫林同志亲切地谈话。　新华社记者　吕厚民摄
