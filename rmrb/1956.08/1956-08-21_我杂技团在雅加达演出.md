Title: Chinese Acrobatic Troupe Performs in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 20th – The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, currently on a visit and performance tour in Jakarta, gave a performance for the citizens of Jakarta on the evening of the 17th at the invitation of the "Jakarta August 17th Celebration Committee."

The Ikada Sports Hall, the performance venue, was packed, and thousands of spectators continuously applauded the wonderful performances. At the end of the performance, the representative of the Mayor of Jakarta expressed thanks and congratulations to the acrobatic troupe on behalf of all the citizens of Jakarta.

### 我杂技团在雅加达演出

1956-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

    我杂技团在雅加达演出
    新华社雅加达20日电　正在雅加达进行访问演出的中国杂技团应“雅加达八一七庆祝委员会”的邀请，十七日晚上为雅加达市民作了一场表演。
    演出地点伊卡达体育馆挤得满满的，数千观众不断为精采的表演鼓掌。表演结束时，雅加达市长的代表以雅加达全体市民的名义向杂技团致谢和祝贺。
