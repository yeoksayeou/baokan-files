Title: The Soviet Council of Ministers Seeks New Design Plans to Prepare for the Construction of the Palace of Soviets in Memory of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 25th: The Soviet Council of Ministers issued a statement on August 24th, deciding to hold a competition in the Soviet Union to solicit design plans for the Palace of Soviets in memory of Lenin. This construction project was halted when the Soviet Patriotic War began in 1941, and it is now about to be resumed, but the existing design plans were drawn up nearly twenty years ago. Now, the best design plans are being sought.

The announcement of the Soviet Council of Ministers stated that the building in memory of Lenin—the Palace of Soviets—must be an architectural masterpiece and must possess the solemn and simple spirit of a monumental building that fully embodies the lofty principles of Soviet socialist culture. At the same time, the architectural style should be close to the people and easily understood by the people, making it convenient for a large number of people to meet and work inside.

The competition will begin on August 25 this year and end on February 25, 1957. The best entries submitted during the competition will receive prizes, with the first prize being 100,000 rubles.

In addition to holding an open competition for design plans, a closed competition will also be held, commissioning outstanding architects and sculptors to create design plans.

The total area of all rooms in the Palace of Soviets will not exceed 36,000 square meters. This will include: a large hall with 4,600 seats; two halls with 1,500 seats each for the Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet to hold separate meetings; and many other halls and rooms for holding various meetings and carrying out other mass political and cultural activities. Rooms for the presidium, diplomatic corps, and journalists will also be built in the halls.

### 苏联部长会议征求新设计图样  准备建筑纪念列宁的苏维埃宫

1956-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议征求新设计图样
    准备建筑纪念列宁的苏维埃宫
    新华社莫斯科25日电　苏联部长会议在八月二十四日发表公告，决定在苏联举行征求建筑纪念列宁的苏维埃宫的设计图样的竞赛。这个建筑工程在1941年苏联卫国战争开始的时候停顿了，现在即将恢复，但是现有的设计图样还是将近二十年前绘制的。现在要征求最好的设计图样。
    苏联部长会议的公告说，纪念列宁的建筑物——苏维埃宫，必须是建筑学上的杰作，必须具有充分体现苏维埃社会主义文化的崇高原则的纪念性建筑应该具有的那种庄严朴素的气概。同时，在建筑式样方面要接近人民而可以为人民领会，只要使它容纳大量的人在里面开会和工作感到方便。
    竞赛从今年8月25日开始，到1957年2月25日结束。竞赛中提出的最好的一批图样将获得奖金，其中头奖将获得十万卢布。
    除了举行设计图样的公开竞赛外，还要举行不公开的竞赛，委托卓越的建筑师和雕刻家绘制设计图样。
    苏维埃宫的所有房间的总面积将不超过三万六千平方公尺。其中将包括：一个有四千六百个座位的大厅，供苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院分别举行会议使用的各有一千五百个座位的两个大厅，以及其它许多供召开各种会议和进行其他群众政治文化活动的大厅和房间。在大厅中还要建筑主席团、外交团和新闻记者工作的房间。
