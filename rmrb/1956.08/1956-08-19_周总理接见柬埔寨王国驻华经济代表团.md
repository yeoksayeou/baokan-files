Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Economic Delegation from the Kingdom of Cambodia to China
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th -- Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai received the Economic Delegation from the Kingdom of Cambodia to China, led by Yang An, Head of the Delegation, and Nai Warangdan, Deputy Head of the Delegation, as well as delegation members Nie Yun and Yi Ou, at 4:00 PM today.

Present at the reception were Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Yang Haolu, Director of the Fourth Bureau, and Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见柬埔寨王国驻华经济代表团

1956-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见柬埔寨王国驻华经济代表团
    新华社18日讯　国务院总理周恩来今日下午四时接见了柬埔寨王国驻华经济代表团团长杨安、副团长奈瓦朗丹、团员涅云和伊欧。
    接见时在座的，有对外贸易部副部长李哲人、第四局局长杨浩庐和外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮。
