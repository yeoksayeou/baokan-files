Title: Ceylon Government Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 27th: According to TASS, the Ceylon government delegation headed by Corea arrived in Moscow on the evening of August 27th.

The delegation will discuss the establishment of diplomatic, economic, and cultural relations between the two countries with the Soviet government.

### 锡兰政府代表团到莫斯科

1956-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰政府代表团到莫斯科
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：以柯里亚为首的锡兰政府代表团在8月27日晚到达莫斯科。
    代表团将同苏联政府商谈两国建立外交、经济和文化关系的问题。
