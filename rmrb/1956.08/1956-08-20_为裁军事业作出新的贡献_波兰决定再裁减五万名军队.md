Title: Making New Contributions to the Cause of Disarmament: Poland Decides to Further Reduce its Army by 50,000 Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 5

    Making New Contributions to the Cause of Disarmament
    Poland Decides to Further Reduce its Army by 50,000 Troops
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 19th: It was announced here that the government of the Polish People's Republic has decided to reduce its armed forces by another 50,000 men in order to make new contributions to the cause of disarmament and to further the peaceful development of the Polish national economy.
    This is another disarmament following the decision on September 3rd of last year to reduce the armed forces by 47,000 men. As with the last disarmament, personnel released from the armed forces will be cared for by the state and will find work in industry and agriculture.

### 为裁军事业作出新的贡献  波兰决定再裁减五万名军队

1956-08-20
第5版()
专栏：

    为裁军事业作出新的贡献
    波兰决定再裁减五万名军队
    新华社华沙19日电　此间宣布，波兰人民共和国政府决定再一次把武装部队裁减五万人，以便对裁军事业作出新的贡献和使波兰国民经济得到进一步的和平发展。
    这是继去年九月三日决定裁减四万七千人之后的又一次裁军。这次裁军和上次一样，从武装部队中裁减出来的人员将得到国家的照顾，将在工业和农业中找到工作。
