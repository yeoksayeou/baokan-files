    American Prisons Like Hell
    Thirty-six prisoners in the Buford Prison in the United States smashed their own leg bones with large hammers on July 30 to protest the inhumane treatment by the prison authorities.
    These prisoners were all forced to engage in excessive and heavy labor in a quarry near the prison under the scorching heat.

### 美国监狱如地狱

1956-08-02
第6版()
专栏：国际点滴

    美国监狱如地狱
    美国巴福德监狱内的三十六名犯人在七月三十日用大铁锤击碎了他们自己的腿骨，以抗议监狱当局的不人道的待遇。
    这些犯人都被迫在酷暑之下在监狱附近的一个采石矿场中从事过度的笨重的劳动。
