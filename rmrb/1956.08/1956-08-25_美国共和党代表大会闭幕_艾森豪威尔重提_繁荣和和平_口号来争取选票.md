Title: US Republican Convention Concludes: Eisenhower Revives "Prosperity and Peace" Slogan to Win Votes
Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th, San Francisco Dispatch: The Republican National Convention closed on August 23rd after hearing Eisenhower and Nixon deliver speeches accepting the nominations as candidates for President and Vice President.

    Eisenhower revived the slogan of "prosperity and peace" to win votes. He said that the Republican Party guarantees to strive to ensure America's prosperity and world peace. However, on the other hand, Eisenhower also advocated that the United States must maintain military strength and preparedness.

    Even when talking about America's economic future, Eisenhower said, "Our economic strength is demonstrating a rapid and healthy growth capacity, even while supporting this unprecedented military budget. We must keep it growing."

    Eisenhower said that we are now in an era with intercontinental nuclear weapons that can destroy cities. "With these weapons, fighting is not only tragic but also foolish. ... The goal now must be to ensure that such a war never happens."

    Eisenhower believed that the "three necessary conditions" for peace are: first, maintaining the basic need for America's moral, economic, and military strength; second, maintaining the system of military blocs pieced together by the United States; third, "without relaxing domestic and collective defense for a moment," the United States "must actively seek to bridge the huge gap separating us (the United States) from the people under Communist rule."

### 美国共和党代表大会闭幕  艾森豪威尔重提“繁荣和和平”口号来争取选票

1956-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

    美国共和党代表大会闭幕
    艾森豪威尔重提“繁荣和和平”口号来争取选票
    据新华社24日讯　旧金山消息：共和党全国代表大会在八月二十三日听取艾森豪威尔和尼克松发表接受提名为总统和副总统候选人的演说以后闭幕。
    艾森豪威尔重提“繁荣和和平”的口号来争取选票。他说，共和党保证努力争取确保美国的繁荣和世界和平。但是，另一方面，艾森豪威尔又鼓吹美国必须保持军事上的强大和准备。
    就是在谈到美国的经济前途的时候，艾森豪威尔也说“我们的经济力量正表现出一种既迅速又健康的增长能力，甚至在支持这种空前的军事预算时也是如此。我们必须保持它继续增长。”
    艾森豪威尔说，现在是处在拥有可以毁灭城市的洲际核子武器的时代，“有了这些武器，战斗不仅是悲惨的，而且是愚蠢的。……现在的目的必须是务使这种战争根本不会发生。”
    艾森豪威尔认为和平的“三个必要的条件”是：第一，维持美国的道义、经济和军事力量的基本需要；第二，保持美国所拼凑的军事集团体系；第三，“在一刻也不放松国内和集体防务的情况下”，美国“必须积极设法填平把我们（美国）同共产党统治下的人民隔开的巨大的鸿沟”。
