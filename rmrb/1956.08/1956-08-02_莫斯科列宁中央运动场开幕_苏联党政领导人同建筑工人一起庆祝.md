Title: Opening of the Lenin Central Stadium in Moscow, Soviet Party and Government Leaders Celebrate with Construction Workers
Author:
Date: 1956-08-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 1st. The newly built Luzhniki Central Stadium in Moscow was solemnly opened on July 31st. The stadium's construction workers held a celebration rally here.

The leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government, Bulganin, Voroshilov, Malenkov, Molotov, Pervukhin, Khrushchev, Zhukov, Brezhnev, Shepilov, Furtseva, Aristov, Belyaev, and Pospelov, came to celebrate the opening of this large stadium with the construction workers.

The attendees warmly welcomed the long speech delivered by Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party.

Khrushchev announced that the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union had ordered that the Moscow Luzhniki Central Stadium be named after V.I. Lenin. At this moment, the stadium's construction workers and all attendees warmly applauded.

The assembly adopted a letter to all construction workers in Moscow.

After the celebration rally, a sports performance was held. Subsequently, the Chinese football team played a match against the Russian Federation football team at this new stadium.

Our newspaper, Moscow, August 1st dispatch: On the afternoon of the 31st, after the opening ceremony held at the Lenin Central Stadium in Moscow, the Chinese football team and the Russian Federation Republic football team held a friendly match, which aroused great interest among the audience. After the start of the match, the Chinese football team took the lead, taking the initiative and attacking from both the left and right wings. Several times the ball approached the Russian team's goal, but the Chinese team's shots were inaccurate and often intercepted by the Russian team. Later, the Russian team adopted a swift counterattack. The Chinese team defended tightly and did not allow the ball to approach the goal. The first half of the game ended in a tense tug-of-war without a winner. At the beginning of the second half, the Russian team launched a rapid attack, bringing the ball close to the Chinese team's goal. The Chinese team went all out to defend tightly. The Chinese team's goalkeeper saved several dangerous balls with various diving postures, winning a burst of applause from the audience. The Russian team continued to attack and finally scored a goal twenty-five minutes after the start of the second half. As a result, the Russian Federation Republic football team won by a score of one to zero.

### 莫斯科列宁中央运动场开幕  苏联党政领导人同建筑工人一起庆祝

1956-08-02
第5版()
专栏：

    莫斯科列宁中央运动场开幕
    苏联党政领导人同建筑工人一起庆祝
    据新华社莫斯科1日电　莫斯科新建的卢日尼基中央运动场在七月三十一日隆重地开幕了，运动场的建筑工人在这里举行了一个庆祝大会。
    苏联共产党和政府的领导人布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、马林科夫、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、赫鲁晓夫、朱可夫、勃列日涅夫、谢皮洛夫、福尔采娃、阿里斯托夫、别利亚耶夫和波斯伯洛夫，来同建筑工人们一起庆祝这个大运动场的开幕。
    到会的人热烈欢迎苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫在会上发表的长篇演说。
    赫鲁晓夫宣布，苏联最高苏维埃主席团命令，莫斯科卢日尼基中央运动场以弗·伊·列宁的名字命名。这时，运动场的建筑工人和所有到会的人热烈鼓掌欢迎。
  大会通过了告莫斯科全体建筑人员书。
    庆祝大会闭幕后举行了体育表演。随后，中国足球队同俄罗斯联邦足球队在这个新运动场上举行了比赛。
    本报莫斯科1日电　三十一日下午，在莫斯科列宁中央运动场举行开幕大会以后，中国足球队和俄罗斯联邦共和国足球队举行了友谊比赛，这场比赛引起了观众的极大兴趣。比赛开始后，中国足球队占优势，掌握主动，从左右两翼进攻。有几次球逼近俄罗斯队球门，但中国队射球不准，常被俄罗斯队中途截住。后来俄罗斯队采取迅速反攻，中国队防守严密，没有让球逼近球门，前半场的球赛就在紧张的拉锯形势下不分胜负。下半场一开始，俄罗斯队就用快速进攻，把球逼近中国队球门，中国队全力严密防守。中国队守门员以各种卧扑姿势救出了好几个险球，博得了全场一片掌声。俄罗斯队连续进攻，结果在下半场开场二十五分钟以后射进了一球。比赛结果俄罗斯联邦共和国足球队以一比零获胜。
