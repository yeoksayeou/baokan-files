Title: Sukarno's Itinerary for Visits to China, the Soviet Union, and Other Countries Announced
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 21st: It has been announced that Indonesian President Sukarno will arrive in China on September 29th for a two-week visit. He will attend the National Day celebrations of the People's Republic of China on October 1st.

The Indonesian government officially announced President Sukarno's itinerary for visits to the Soviet Union, China, and Eastern European countries on August 20th. He will depart from Jakarta on August 26th and is expected to arrive in Moscow on the afternoon of August 28th, with a one-night stopover in Tehran, the capital of Iran. Sukarno's visit to the Soviet Union will last for two weeks. He will then depart from Moscow on September 12th for Yugoslavia, and after a five-day stay in Yugoslavia, he will travel to Vienna.

Sukarno is scheduled to arrive in Prague on September 22nd. There, he will stay for four days as a state guest of the Czechoslovakian government, and then depart from Prague for Beijing on September 26th.

### 苏加诺访问中苏等国日程宣布

1956-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺访问中苏等国日程宣布
    新华社雅加达21日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺将在九月二十九日到达中国进行为时两星期的访问。他将参加十月一日中华人民共和国的国庆典礼。
    印度尼西亚官方已经在八月二十日宣布了苏加诺总统访问苏联、中国和东欧国家的日程表。他将在八月二十六日从雅加达动身，预料将在八月二十八日下午到达莫斯科，中途将在伊朗首都德黑兰逗留一夜。苏加诺在苏联的访问将需要两个星期。然后他将在九月十二日从莫斯科动身前往南斯拉夫，在南斯拉夫逗留五天以后前往维也纳。
    苏加诺预定要在九月二十二日到达布拉格。在那里，他将以捷克斯洛伐克政府的国宾的身份逗留四天，然后在九月二十六日从布拉格动身前往北京。
