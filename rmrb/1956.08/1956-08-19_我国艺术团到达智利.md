Title: Chinese Art Troupe Arrives in Chile
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, August 18th. The Chinese Art Troupe arrived in Santiago, the capital of Chile, by plane on August 16th. The head of the delegation is Chu Tunan, and the total number of members is eighty-seven. They will perform in several countries in the Americas, with Chile being the first.

More than two hundred people greeted them at the airport, including Senators Allende and Luis King Tricot, Representatives Martinez Campos and Lia Rafaele, the famous poet Neruda, the director of the Drama Department of the University of Chile, Otas, the director of the Experimental Theater, Delabara, the painter Vantureri, as well as representatives from Chilean cultural circles and representatives of overseas Chinese.

The Chinese Art Troupe will begin performing at the Municipal Theater on August 20.

### 我国艺术团到达智利

1956-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团到达智利
    新华社圣地亚哥18日电　中国艺术团在八月十六日乘飞机到达智利首都圣地亚哥。这个代表团的团长是楚图南，全部团员共八十七人。他们将在美洲若干国家演出，智利是其中第一个国家。
    有两百多人在机场迎接，其中有参议员阿伦德和路易斯·金·特里科特、众议员马丁内斯·康普斯和利亚·拉法埃、著名诗人聂鲁达、智利大学戏剧系主任奥塔斯、实验剧院院长德拉巴拉、画家万徒勒里以及智利文化界代表们和华侨代表们。
    中国艺术团将于八月二十日在市立剧院开始演出。
