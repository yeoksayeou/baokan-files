Title: Manufacturing Exquisite Porcelain

Author:

Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 2

    Manufacturing Exquisite Porcelain
    (Our dispatch) Jingdezhen, Jiangxi Province, recently accepted a central government order for a batch of porcelain. This batch of porcelain totals more than 125,000 pieces, including the famous colored glaze, outstanding famille rose, and exquisite carvings.
    In order to ensure the early completion of this order, Jingdezhen specifically established a National Porcelain Production Committee, convened a meeting of porcelain factory directors, studied methods for completing the task, and also selected a group of veteran artisans, blue-and-white workers, and some technicians with special skills, ceramic engineers, etc., to be responsible for the design.
    The artisans, technicians, and engineers responsible for designing and manufacturing the samples of this batch of porcelain have all expressed confidence that this batch of porcelain will have a unique style in terms of shape and color, and that the quality of the long-lost precious colored glaze will reach and exceed the highest level in history.
                    (Jingdezhen Daily)

### 制造精美的磁器

1956-08-16
第2版()
专栏：

    制造精美的磁器
    本报讯　江西省景德镇最近接受了中央定制一批瓷器的任务。这批瓷器共有十二万五千多件，包括有名的颜色釉、出色的粉彩和精致的雕刻。
    为了保证提前完成这批定货，景德镇专门成立了国家用瓷制作委员会，召开了瓷厂厂长会议，研究了完成任务的办法，同时抽调了一批老艺人、青花工人和一些有特种技艺的技术人员、陶瓷工程师等来负责设计。
    负责设计和制造这批瓷器样品的艺人、技术工人和工程师，都表示有信心有把握使这批瓷器在造型、色彩等方面富有独特风格，并且使久已失传的名贵的颜色釉质量达到和超过历史上最高的水平。
                    （景德镇报）
