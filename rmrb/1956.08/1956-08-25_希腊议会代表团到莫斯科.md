Title: Greek Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, August 24th. The Greek parliamentary delegation, invited by the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to visit the Soviet Union, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 23rd. The delegation is led by Lambros Eftaxias, member of the Greek ruling party "National Radical Union" and former Minister of Finance.

### 希腊议会代表团到莫斯科

1956-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

    希腊议会代表团到莫斯科
    据新华社24日讯　应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请访问苏联的希腊议会代表团在二十三日乘飞机到达莫斯科。率领代表团的是希腊执政党“国民激进联盟”盟员、前财政大臣兰布罗斯·伊弗塔西亚斯。
