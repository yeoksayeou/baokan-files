Title: Chinese Representatives Hold Talks with Japanese Figures from All Walks of Life
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Osaka, 16th: Chinese representatives attending the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs held座谈会 with Japanese figures from all walks of life yesterday afternoon.

　　Xu Guangping, head of the delegation, attended a discussion meeting held by the Osaka Women's Group Federation. Wang Yunsheng attended a discussion meeting jointly held by six news organizations, including "Asahi Shimbun," "Mainichi Shimbun," "Yomiuri Shimbun," and Kyodo News Agency. Kang Yonghe and Cao Yu respectively participated in座谈会 held by Osaka's labor unions and theater circles on labor issues and Chinese drama issues.

　　All members of the Chinese delegation were also invited this afternoon to attend a welcome banquet jointly held by the Osaka branch of the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Kansai General Bureau of the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade, the Kansai branch of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations, the Kansai General Bureau of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, and the Kansai Chinese Overseas Chinese Consultation Conference.

### 我国代表同日本各界座谈

1956-08-17
第5版()
专栏：

    我国代表同日本各界座谈
    新华社16日讯　大阪消息：出席第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表在昨天下午分别同日本各界举行座谈会。
    代表团团长许广平出席了大阪妇女团体联合举行的恳谈会，王芸生出席了“朝日新闻”社、“每日新闻”社、“读卖新闻”社和共同通讯社等六个新闻机关联合举行的恳谈会。康永和和曹禺分别参加了大阪的工会和戏剧界举行的劳工问题和中国戏剧问题的座谈会。
    中国代表团全体人员在这天下午还应邀出席了日中友好协会大阪分会、日本国际贸易促进协会关西总局、日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议关西分会、日中文化交流协会关西总局和关西华侨协商会议联合举行的欢迎宴会。
