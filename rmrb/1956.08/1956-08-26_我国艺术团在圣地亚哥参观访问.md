Title: China's Art Troupe Visits Santiago
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, August 25th: The leaders and members of the Chinese Art Troupe visited the Chilean House of Representatives on the morning of August 24th, accompanied by the Speaker of the Chilean House of Representatives. That noon, they also laid a wreath at the monument to Chilean national hero O'Higgins.

　　On August 23rd, the head of the troupe, Chu Tunan, and the deputy head, Wang Li, visited the President of the University of Chile, Juan Gómez. On this day, one thousand people from Chilean theatrical circles met with the art troupe, and Zhao Feng gave them a report on the experience of Chinese theatrical circles. Later, he introduced Chinese classical drama to people from cultural circles in Santiago and students from the University of Chile.

　　On August 22nd, the chairman of the Chile-China Cultural Society, Allende, held a banquet to entertain the art troupe, attended by four hundred people from cultural and other circles.

### 我国艺术团在圣地亚哥参观访问

1956-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团在圣地亚哥参观访问
    新华社25日讯　圣地亚哥消息：中国艺术团的领导人和团员们在八月二十四日上午在智利众议院议长的陪同下参观了智利众议院。这一天中午，他们还在智利民族英雄奥黑金斯的纪念碑前献了花圈。
    八月二十三日，团长楚图南和副团长王力访问了智利大学校长胡安·戈麦斯。这一天，有一千位智利戏剧界人士会见了艺术团，赵沨向他们作了关于中国戏剧界的经验的报告。后来，他又向圣地亚哥文化界人士和智利大学学生介绍了中国古典戏剧。
    八月二十二日，智利—中国文化学会主席阿伦德举行了宴会招待艺术团，出席宴会的有文化界和其他各界的人士四百人。
