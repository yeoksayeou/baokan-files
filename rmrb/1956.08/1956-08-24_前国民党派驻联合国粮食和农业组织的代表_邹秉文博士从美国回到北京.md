Title: Former Kuomintang Representative to the United Nations Food and Agriculture Organization, Dr. Zou Bingwen, Returns to Beijing from the United States
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd - Dr. Zou Bingwen, former Kuomintang representative to the United Nations Food and Agriculture Organization, and his wife returned to Beijing today.

    Dr. Zou Bingwen had resided in the United States for a long time since 1945. This June, he learned about many exciting things in the motherland's construction undertakings from his old friends, and decided to break away from Taiwan and return to the mainland.

    Zou Bingwen studied agricultural science at Cornell University in New York, and later obtained a doctorate from the University of Michigan. He once served as the director of the Department of Agriculture at Zhongnan University in Nanjing.

    Upon his return to Beijing, he was warmly welcomed by representatives of the Ministry of Higher Education and many of his old friends. He saw his two sons and three daughters.

### 前国民党派驻联合国粮食和农业组织的代表  邹秉文博士从美国回到北京

1956-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    前国民党派驻联合国粮食和农业组织的代表
    邹秉文博士从美国回到北京
    新华社23日讯　前国民党派驻联合国粮食和农业组织的代表邹秉文博士和夫人今天回到北京。
    邹秉文博士从1945年以来，长期居住美国。今年六月，他从他的老朋友那里知道了祖国建设事业中许多令人兴奋的事情，便决定和台湾脱离关系，返回祖国大陆。
    邹秉文曾在纽约康奈尔大学农科读书，后来获得密执安大学博士学位。他曾担任南京中南大学农科主任。
    他回到北京时，受到了高等教育部代表和他的许多老朋友的热烈欢迎。他看到了他的两个儿子和三个女儿。
