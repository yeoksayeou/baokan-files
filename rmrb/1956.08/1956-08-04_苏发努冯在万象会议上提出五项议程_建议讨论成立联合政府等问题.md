Title: Souphanouvong Proposes Five-Point Agenda at Vientiane Conference Suggesting Discussions on the Formation of a Coalition Government and Other Issues
Author:
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 3rd: According to a Vientiane radio broadcast reported by the Vietnam News Agency, the meeting between the Lao Fighting Forces delegation and the Royal Lao Government delegation continued at 10:20 a.m. on August 2nd in Vientiane.

Prince Souphanouvong spoke first. After analyzing the international and domestic situations, he proposed the following five-point agenda:

	One, the issue of realizing democratic freedoms.

	Two, the issue of guaranteeing the rights of the Lao Fighting Forces.

	Three, the issue of forming a coalition government.

	Four, the issue of neutrality and implementing the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

	Five, the issue of resolving the two regroupment provinces peacefully.

Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma then explained the Royal Government's views in detail. He promised to study the Lao Fighting Forces' proposals.

At the first meeting held on August 1st, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma proposed three items for the conference agenda. They were:

	One, cessation of all hostilities prior to the conference.

	Two, the issue of restoring Royal Government authority in the two regroupment provinces of Sam Neua and Phong Saly.

	Three, the issue of the Lao Fighting Forces' participation in national common life.

### 苏发努冯在万象会议上提出五项议程  建议讨论成立联合政府等问题

1956-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    苏发努冯在万象会议上提出五项议程
    建议讨论成立联合政府等问题
    新华社河内3日电　越南通讯社据万象电台广播，寮国战斗部队代表团和老挝王国政府代表团之间的会议八月二日上午十时二十分在万象继续举行。
    苏发努冯亲王首先发言。他在分析国际局势和国内局势之后，提出了下列五项议程：
    一、实现民主自由的问题。
    二、保证寮国战斗部队的权利的问题。
    三、成立联合政府的问题。
    四、中立和实施和平共处的五项原则的问题。
    五、以和平方式解决两个重新集结省份的问题。
    梭发那·富马首相接着详细说明了王国政府的看法。他答应研究寮国战斗部队的建议。
    在八月一日举行的第一次会议上，梭发那·富马首相提出了关于会议的三项议程。它们是：
    一、在会议之前停止一切敌对行动。
    二、在桑怒和丰沙里两个集结省份恢复王国政权的问题。
    三、寮国战斗部队参加国家共同生活的问题。
