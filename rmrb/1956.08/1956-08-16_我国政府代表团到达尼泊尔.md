Title: Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Nepal
Author:
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th. New Delhi Dispatch: The Chinese government delegation, headed by Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Nepal, arrived in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, by plane on the afternoon of August 14th, to discuss various issues related to Sino-Nepalese relations with the Nepalese government.
    Welcoming them at the airport were the Nepalese King's Military Secretary, Mala, the Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Nepalese Foreign Ministry, Thapa, and the Director of Protocol, Singh.
    Lieutenant General Rana, the Nepalese Ambassador to China, also came to the airport to welcome them.
    In addition to the head of the delegation, Ambassador Pan Zili, the Chinese government delegation includes representatives Yang Gongsu, Pengcuo Raojie, Du YuYun and consultant Danba.

### 我国政府代表团到达尼泊尔

1956-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

    我国政府代表团到达尼泊尔
    新华社15日讯　新德里消息：以中国驻尼泊尔特命全权大使潘自力为首的中国政府代表团在八月十四日下午乘飞机到达尼泊尔首都加德满都，准备同尼泊尔政府商谈有关中尼两国关系的各项问题。
    在机场上欢迎的有尼泊尔国王军事秘书马拉，尼泊尔外交部外事秘书塔帕和礼宾司司长辛格。
    尼泊尔驻华大使拉纳中将也到机场欢迎。
    中国政府代表团除团长潘自力大使外有代表杨公素、彭措饶杰、杜毓澐和顾问丹巴。
