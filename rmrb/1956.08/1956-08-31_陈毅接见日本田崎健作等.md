Title: Chen Yi Receives Japanese Kenzo Tazakiet al.
Author:
Date: 1956-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council received this morning the entire personnel of the sixth Japanese "Group for Escorting the Remains of Chinese Martyrs," headed by Kenzo Tazaki, as well as Japanese Red Cross representative Risaburo Kiuchi, Japan-China Friendship Association representative Kiyoo Yamanishi, Japan Peace Liaison Committee representative Shigeyuki Fukushima, and thirteen Japanese journalists who came with the "Xingan Maru," totaling eighteen people.

Present at the reception were Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross; Liao Chengzhi, Advisor; Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China; Wang Zizhong, Chairman of the Administrative Affairs of the Yenching Union Theological Seminary; Zheng Senyu, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; and Ma Chungu, Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 陈毅接见日本田崎健作等

1956-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见日本田崎健作等
    新华社30日讯　国务院副总理陈毅在今天上午接见了以田崎健作为首的日本第六批“中国殉难烈士骨灰护送团”全体人员和日本红十字会代表木内利三郎、日中友好协会代表山西清雄、日本和平联络会代表福岛重之等十八人及随“兴安丸”前来的十三位日本记者。
    接见的时候，在座的有中国红十字会会长李德全、顾问廖承志、中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初、燕京协和神学院院务主席王梓仲、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会联络部副部长郑森禹和中华全国总工会国际联络部部长马纯古。
