Title: Soviet and Swedish Fleets Exchange Visits
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

    The Soviet fleet arrived in Gothenburg, Sweden, on the morning of August 3rd for an official visit. Upon the Soviet fleet's arrival, the sailors on board exchanged warm greetings with the residents of Gothenburg.
    
    A Swedish fleet arrived in Riga, Soviet Union, on August 3rd for a friendly visit. This fleet is composed of a light cruiser and two destroyer escorts.

### 苏联和瑞典舰队互访

1956-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和瑞典舰队互访
    据新华社讯　苏联舰队在八月三日早晨到达瑞典的哥德堡作正式访问。在苏联舰队到达的时候，舰队上的水兵们同哥德堡的居民热烈地打招呼。
    一支瑞典舰队在八月三日到达苏联的里加作友好访问。这支舰队由一艘轻巡洋舰和两艘驱击舰组成。
