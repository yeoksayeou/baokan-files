Title: A French Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th—A seven-member delegation from the French National Center for Cultural Exchange with the Far East arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation. The head of the delegation is French medical doctor Serge Lafitte-Dupont.

### 法国一文化代表团到京

1956-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

    法国一文化代表团到京
    据新华社20日讯　法国和远东文化交流全国中心代表团一行七人今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国民主青年联合会邀请来中国进行友好访问的。代表团团长是法国医学博士塞日·拉斐特—杜邦。
