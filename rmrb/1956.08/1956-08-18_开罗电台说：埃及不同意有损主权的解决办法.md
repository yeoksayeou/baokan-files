Title: Cairo Radio Says: Egypt Disagrees with Solutions that Harm Sovereignty
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 17th dispatch: Cairo Radio commentator said on the evening of the 16th, commenting on the London conference, "Egypt will never agree to a solution aimed at depriving it of sovereignty over the Suez Canal."

The commentator said: "Egypt has provided guarantees of freedom of navigation. Egypt has proposed convening a genuine international conference to conclude a treaty recognizing these guarantees. Egypt cannot offer more than that."

The Cairo Radio commentator condemned the bellicose statements made by British and French leaders.

The commentator said: "Let them understand clearly: Egypt will defend itself, and Egypt is not isolated and helpless. Besides the hundreds of millions of people in Asia and Africa, all peace-loving and tranquil people in the world stand on Egypt's side."

The commentator concluded: The Egyptian people solemnly assure the people of all countries in the world that the Egyptian people will be with them in their struggle for independence, peace and prosperity.

### 开罗电台说：埃及不同意有损主权的解决办法

1956-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

    开罗电台说：埃及不同意有损主权的解决办法
    据新华社17日讯　开罗电台评论员十六日晚在评论伦敦会议时说：“埃及决不会同意目的在于剥夺它对苏彝士运河主权的解决办法”。
    评论员说：“埃及已经提供了通航自由的保证。埃及曾经建议召开一个真正的国际会议来缔结一个认可这种保证的条约。埃及不能够提供更多的东西。”
    开罗电台的评论员谴责了英国和法国领导人所发表的好战的声明。
    评论员说：“让他们听明白：埃及将进行自卫，埃及不是孤立无援的。除了成亿的亚、非人民之外，全世界一切爱好和平和宁静的人民都站在埃及这一边。”
    评论员最后说：埃及人民向全世界各国人民郑重保证，在他们为争取独立、和平和繁荣的斗争中埃及人民将和他们在一起。
