Title: Arab Nations Firmly Support Egypt, Syria Suggests Convening an Emergency Meeting of the Arab League, Lebanese Prime Minister Says Opposition to Egypt is an Attack on All Arab Nations
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 31st Dispatch. A United Press International dispatch from Damascus reported on July 30th that Syrian officials said Syria had suggested convening an emergency meeting of the Arab League Political Committee to discuss supporting Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal.

Syrian officials stated that Syria would propose that the meeting take collective measures by Arab countries to support Egypt's actions.

Xinhua News Agency, 31st Dispatch. According to a Reuters report from Beirut on the 30th, Lebanese Prime Minister Abdullah Yafi, commenting on Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal, said that any opposition by Western countries to Egypt's policies is an attack on all Arab nations.

He said that Arab countries will show that they support Egypt in action, not just in words.

Xinhua News Agency, 31st Dispatch. Cairo News: Saudi Arabian King Saud sent a telegram to Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser today, expressing support for Egypt's action in nationalizing the Suez Canal.

King Saud's telegram was delivered to Nasser by the Saudi Arabian Ambassador to Egypt.

Xinhua News Agency, 31st Dispatch. Cairo News: According to Cairo Radio broadcast on July 30th, Saudi Arabian Prime Minister Faisal Saud said that nationalizing the Suez Canal is Egypt's legitimate right.

### 阿拉伯国家坚决支持埃及  叙利亚建议召开阿拉伯联盟紧急会议  黎巴嫩总理说反对埃及就是攻击所有阿拉伯国家

1956-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯国家坚决支持埃及
    叙利亚建议召开阿拉伯联盟紧急会议
    黎巴嫩总理说反对埃及就是攻击所有阿拉伯国家
    新华社31日讯　据合众社从大马士革发出的消息说，叙利亚官方人士七月三十日说：叙利亚已经建议召开阿拉伯联盟政治委员会紧急会议来讨论支持埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的问题。
    叙利亚官方人士说，叙利亚将建议这个会议采取阿拉伯国家的集体措施来支持埃及的行动。
    新华社31日讯　据路透社贝鲁特三十日消息：黎巴嫩总理阿卜杜勒·雅非在评论埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有时说，西方国家反对埃及的任何政策都是对所有阿拉伯国家的一种攻击。
    他说，阿拉伯国家将显示，他们是在行动上而不是在口头上支持埃及。
    新华社31日讯　开罗消息：沙特阿拉伯国王沙特今天打电报给埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔，表示支持埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的行动。
    国王沙特的电报是由沙特阿拉伯驻埃及大使交给纳赛尔的。
    新华社31日讯　开罗消息：据开罗电台七月三十日广播，沙特阿拉伯首相费萨尔·沙特说，把苏彝士运河收归国有是埃及的合法权利。
