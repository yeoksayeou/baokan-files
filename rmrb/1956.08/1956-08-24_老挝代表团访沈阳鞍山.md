Title: Lao Delegation Visits Shenyang and Anshan
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 23rd: The Lao Royal Government Delegation arrived in Shenyang by special plane at noon today. After leaving the airport, the delegation went to Anshan by special car for a visit, returning to Shenyang at 7 pm to attend a welcome banquet hosted jointly by Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, and Zhang Like, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang.
    Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, August 23rd: Prince Souvanna Phouma and the Lao Royal Government Delegation he led visited the Anshan Iron and Steel Company. The distinguished guests visited projects such as the large rolling mill, seamless steel tube plant, and iron-smelting plant. Prince Souvanna Phouma visited the slag outlet of Blast Furnace No. 8 and Blast Furnace No. 7. In the large rolling mill, the Lao Prince stood for a long time in front of the glass window of an operation room, watching the red-hot steel materials coming out of the heating furnace. Prince Souvanna Phouma watched the operation of the piercing machine in the seamless steel tube plant.

### 老挝代表团访沈阳鞍山

1956-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    老挝代表团访沈阳鞍山
    据新华社沈阳23日电　老挝王国政府代表团在今天中午乘专机到达沈阳。代表团离开机场后，即乘专车去鞍山参观，当晚七时返回沈阳，出席了辽宁省省长杜者蘅、沈阳市副市长张力克共同举行的欢迎宴会。
    据新华社鞍山23日电　梭发那·富马亲王和他所率领的老挝王国政府代表团参观了鞍山钢铁公司。贵宾们参观了大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂和炼铁厂等工程。梭发那·富马亲王参观了八号高炉和七号高炉的出渣口。在大型轧钢厂，这位老挝亲王长久地站在一座操纵室的玻璃窗前，观看刚从加热炉里出来的一根根火热的钢材。梭发那·富马亲王在无缝钢管厂观看了穿孔机的操作。
