Title: Henan Islamic Figures Support Egypt's Just Actions
Author:
Date: 1956-08-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, August 11th: Two hundred and ten Islamic figures from more than one hundred counties and cities in Henan Province gathered in Zhengzhou on the 10th for a symposium to support Egypt's just action in nationalizing the Suez Canal Company.

    The symposium was presided over by Dawood Li Jiarong, a member of the China Islamic Association. In his speech, he sternly rebuked the colonialists' interference and threats against Egypt, and expressed his firm support for the heroic struggle of the Egyptian people to safeguard their national interests. Then, Muhammad Bilali Liu Zijie, Imam of the Zhengzhou Railway Station Mosque, Ersa Ma Mingjian, Imam of the Beiguan Mosque in Lushi County, and Gaiximu Du Shangci, Imam of the Chengguan Mosque in Xiangcheng County, spoke. They unanimously expressed their support for the just action of the Egyptian people in nationalizing the Suez Canal.

    In the end, the more than two hundred Islamic figures present unanimously stated: The more than 380,000 Muslims in Henan Province firmly stand on the side of the Egyptian people and resolutely support the just actions of the Egyptian people.

### 河南伊斯兰教人士支持埃及的正义行动

1956-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    河南伊斯兰教人士支持埃及的正义行动
    据新华社郑州11日电　来自河南省一百多个县市的伊斯兰教人士二百一十人，十日在郑州市集会座谈，支持埃及将苏彝士运河公司收归国有的正义行动。
    座谈会由中国伊斯兰教协会委员达乌德·李家荣主持。他在发言中严正地斥责了殖民主义者对埃及的干涉和威胁，表示坚决支持埃及人民为维护民族利益而进行的英勇斗争。接着发言的有郑州市车站清真寺阿訇穆罕默德·比俩里·刘子杰，卢氏县北关清真寺阿訇耳撒·马明鉴，襄城县城关清真寺阿訇戛西木·杜尚慈等人。他们一致表示支持埃及人民把苏彝士运河收归国有的正义行动。
    最后，到会的二百多个伊斯兰教人士一致表示：河南省三十八万多穆斯林坚决站在埃及人民的一边，坚决支持埃及人民的正义行动。
