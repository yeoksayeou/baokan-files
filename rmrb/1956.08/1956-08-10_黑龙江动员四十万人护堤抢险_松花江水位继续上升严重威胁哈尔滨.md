Title: Heilongjiang Mobilizes 400,000 People to Protect Dikes and Rush to Rescue; Songhua River Water Level Continues to Rise, Seriously Threatening Harbin
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 9th - The Songhua River, Mudan River, Lalin River and other rivers have risen again. Heilongjiang Province has mobilized 400,000 people to protect dikes and rush to rescue.

    The water level of the Songhua River in Harbin City reached 119.67 meters at 11:00 AM today, 3 centimeters higher than the highest water level on the morning of August 7th, and is still rising. Four breaches occurred in the Songhua River dikes in Zhaodong, Shuangcheng, and the suburbs of Harbin City, flooding more than 10,000 hectares of farmland and nearly 100 villages.

### 黑龙江动员四十万人护堤抢险  松花江水位继续上升严重威胁哈尔滨

1956-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    黑龙江动员四十万人护堤抢险
    松花江水位继续上升严重威胁哈尔滨
    据新华社哈尔滨9日电　松花江、牡丹江、拉林河等河再次上涨，黑龙江省动员四十万人护堤抢险。
    哈尔滨市松花江江面水位今天上午十一时上涨到一一九点六七公尺，比八月七日晨最高水位高出三公分，还在继续上涨中。肇东、双城和哈尔滨市郊区的松花江堤发生四处决口，淹没一万多公顷农田和近百个村庄。
