Title: Scientific Discussion at Beijing University of Politics and Law
Author: Zhang Xin
Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 7

Academic Trends

    Scientific Discussion at Beijing University of Politics and Law
    The State and Law Theory and History Teaching and Research Office of Beijing University of Politics and Law held a discussion on July 14, discussing whether socialist countries can adopt the same state forms as bourgeois countries, whether socialist countries can inherit certain elements of the law of exploiting countries, and whether we can absorb something from pre-Marxist jurisprudence.

    When discussing whether socialist countries can adopt the same state forms as bourgeois countries, some people believed that socialist countries can adopt the same state forms as bourgeois countries because it is the content, not the form, that determines things, and it is possible to utilize the old forms. Others believed that socialist countries cannot adopt the same forms as bourgeois countries because the class nature of these two types of countries is fundamentally opposed.

    More debate occurred on the issue of legal inheritance: some people believed that different types of law have inheritance, and socialist countries can also inherit certain elements of the law prior to socialism. Of course, the nature of this inheritance is completely different from the inheritance of feudal or slave-owning states' laws by bourgeois states. Some people believed that the issue of inheritance of different types of law only applies to the laws of various exploiting countries; socialist countries cannot inherit the factors of past laws, but only utilize some of them. Some people believed that although socialist countries can inherit certain elements of the old law, our historical conditions determine that we have not inherited the legal system of the Kuomintang reactionary regime. However, this does not preclude us from absorbing some legislative experience from the old regime when drafting the code.

    Regarding the question of whether we can absorb something from pre-Marxist jurisprudence, there was no dissenting opinion at the meeting.

    The above unresolved issues will be further studied and resolved in the future.
            (Zhang Xin)

### 北京政法学院进行科学讨论
张鑫
1956-08-08
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    北京政法学院进行科学讨论
    北京政法学院国家与法的理论和历史教研室于七月十四日举行讨论会，讨论了社会主义国家是否可以采用与资产阶级国家相同的国家形式、社会主义国家能否继承剥削者国家的法的某些因素、我们能不能在马克思主义以前的法学中吸收一些东西等问题。
    在讨论到社会主义国家是否可以采用与资产阶级国家相同的国家形式问题时，有人认为社会主义国家可以采用与资产阶级国家相同的国家形式，因为决定的东西不是形式而是内容，利用旧形式是可以的。有人则认为社会主义国家不能采用与资产阶级国家相同的形式，因为这两种类型国家的阶级本质根本对立。
    关于法的继承性问题发生了较多的争论：有人认为不同类型的法有继承性，社会主义国家也可以继承社会主义以前的法的某些因素。当然这种继承的性质和资产阶级国家继承封建国家或奴隶制国家的法是完全不同的。有人认为不同类型的法的继承性问题只适合于各种剥削者国家的法；社会主义国家不能继承过去的法的因素，只是利用了其中的某些因素。有人认为社会主义国家虽然可以继承旧法中的某些因素，但是我国的历史条件决定我们并没有继承国民党反动政权的法律制度。不过这并不排斥我们在起草法典时可以吸收一些旧政权的立法经验。
    关于能不能在马克思主义以前的法学中吸收一些东西的问题，会上没有否定的意见。
    以上没有得出一致的看法问题，准备以后继续研究解决。
            （张鑫）
