Title: Burmese President and Prime Minister Host Soong Ching Ling; Soong Ching Ling Visits U Nu
Author:
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 13th: Burmese President Ba U hosted a tea party at the Presidential Palace on the afternoon of August 12th to entertain Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling.

Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming and his wife, as well as Soong Ching Ling's entourage, were also invited to attend the tea party.

Also attending the tea party were Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maw and Daw Khin Kyi, the wife of Aung San.

The tea party proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 13th: Burmese Federal Prime Minister U Ba Swe and his wife held a banquet at 1:00 PM on the afternoon of August 12th to entertain Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China.

Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming and his wife, as well as Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, who accompanied Soong Ching Ling on her visit to Indonesia, were invited to attend the banquet.

Burmese Deputy Prime Ministers Sao Khun Kyee and Thakin Tin, Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maw, Daw Khin Kyi, the wife of Aung San, Supreme Court Judge U Myint Thein, and Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Ba Lwin also attended the banquet.

Before the banquet, Prime Minister U Ba Swe had a friendly conversation with Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling for half an hour. Soong Ching Ling expressed her deep concern about the recent earthquake in Upper Burma and expressed sympathy and condolences to the victims.

Soong Ching Ling also presented gifts to U Ba Swe and his wife.

After arriving in Rangoon on the 11th, Soong Ching Ling sent a letter to Prime Minister U Ba Swe expressing condolences to the earthquake victims. The letter included a remittance of 10,000 kyats as a donation to the disaster relief fund.
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 13th: Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, paid a visit to U Nu, the former Prime Minister of Burma and current Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, and his wife at 9:00 AM this morning.

At U Nu's official residence, U Nu and Soong Ching Ling had a friendly and cordial conversation for an hour. U Nu and his wife treated Soong Ching Ling with Burmese snacks. Soong Ching Ling presented U Nu and his wife with special Chinese gifts.

Accompanying Soong Ching Ling on the visit were Ambassador Yao Zhongming and his wife, and Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China.

Also present today were Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maw, Daw Khin Kyi, the wife of Aung San, and Prime Minister's Secretary U Thant.

### 缅甸总统和总理招待宋庆龄  宋庆龄拜访吴努

1956-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸总统和总理招待宋庆龄
    宋庆龄拜访吴努
    新华社仰光13日电　缅甸总统巴宇八月十二日下午在总统府举行茶会招待宋庆龄副委员长。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和夫人以及宋庆龄的随行人员也应邀出席了茶会。
    出席茶会的还有缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和昂山夫人都庆枝。
    茶会在友好的空气中进行。
    新华社仰光13日电　缅甸联邦总理吴巴瑞和夫人八月十二日下午一时举行宴会招待中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和夫人以及陪同宋庆龄前往印度尼西亚访问的全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏应邀出席了宴会。
    缅甸副总理藻昆卓和德钦丁、缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂、昂山夫人都庆枝、最高法院法官吴敏登和外交部常任秘书巴林顿也出席了宴会。
    在宴会前，吴巴瑞总理同宋庆龄副委员长进行了半小时的友好谈话。宋庆龄对于最近在上缅甸发生的地震表示深切的关怀，对灾民表示同情和慰问。
    宋庆龄还给吴巴瑞和夫人赠了礼品。
    宋庆龄在十一日到达仰光以后，曾给吴巴瑞总理发出了一封慰问地震灾民的信。信里附有一万缅元的汇款，作为对灾民救济基金的捐款。
    新华社仰光13日电　中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄今天上午九时拜访了缅甸前总理、现任缅甸反法西斯自由同盟主席吴努和他的夫人。
    在吴努官邸，吴努和宋庆龄进行了一小时友好亲切的谈话。吴努夫妇以缅甸点心招待宋庆龄。宋庆龄以中国特别的礼品赠送给吴努夫妇。
    陪同宋庆龄拜访的有：姚仲明大使夫妇和中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏。
    今天在坐的还有：缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂、昂山夫人都庆枝和总理秘书吴丹。
