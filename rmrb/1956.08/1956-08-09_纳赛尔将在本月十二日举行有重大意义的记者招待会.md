Title: Nasser to Hold a Significant Press Conference on the 12th of This Month
Author:
Date: 1956-08-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th, Cairo dispatch: Egyptian officials announced on August 7th that Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser will hold a press conference on August 12th with "tremendous world significance."

According to a Middle East News Agency report on August 7th, Egypt's response to the invitation from Britain, France, and the United States to attend the London conference will be delivered to the British Embassy before President Nasser's press conference on the Suez Canal crisis at noon on August 12th.

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th, Cairo dispatch: Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser yesterday once again received the Soviet, Indian, and American ambassadors to Egypt. This is Nasser's fifth consecutive day of meetings with Soviet Ambassador Kiselev. Since the announcement in London of the 24-nation conference on the Suez Canal issue, Nasser has been meeting with the Indian Ambassador to Egypt every day.

Nasser also received yesterday the ambassadors to Egypt from African and Arab countries, including Ethiopia, Iraq, Jordan, Sudan, Libya, and Lebanon.

The Egyptian Foreign Ministry yesterday summoned all the ambassadors to Egypt from countries invited to attend the London conference. Western news agencies quoted well-informed sources as saying that Egypt explained to these ambassadors its attitude towards the Western countries' demands for the internationalization of the Suez Canal.

### 纳赛尔将在本月十二日举行有重大意义的记者招待会

1956-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔将在本月十二日举行有重大意义的记者招待会
    据新华社8日讯　开罗消息：埃及官方八月七日宣布，埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔将在八月十二日举行一次具有“巨大的世界意义”的记者招待会。
    据中东通讯社八月七日报道，埃及对英、法、美三国邀请它参加伦敦会议的答复将在纳赛尔总统在八月十二日中午就苏彝士运河危机问题举行记者招待会以前交给英国大使馆。
    据新华社8日讯　开罗消息：埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔昨天再度接见了苏联、印度和美国驻埃及的大使。这是纳赛尔同苏联大使基谢廖夫连续第五天的会见。自从在伦敦宣布召开关于苏彝士运河问题的二十四国会议以来，纳赛尔每天都同印度驻埃及大使会谈。
    纳赛尔昨天还接见了埃塞俄比亚、伊拉克、约旦、苏丹、利比亚和黎巴嫩等非洲和阿拉伯国家的驻埃及使节。
    埃及外交部昨天召见了所有被邀请参加伦敦会议的国家的驻埃及使节。西方通讯社引述消息灵通人士的话说，埃及向这些使节解释了埃及对西方国家提出的关于苏彝士运河国际化的要求的态度。
