Title: Cambodian King Accepts Resignation of Khim Tit Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 9th: Phnom Penh dispatch: Cambodian King Suramarit accepted the resignation of the Khim Tit cabinet on August 8th.

    The King has begun consultations with the speakers of the parliament on forming a new government.

    The Khim Tit cabinet submitted its resignation on July 29th.

    According to Agence France-Presse, the reason for the resignation is that some parliamentarians with import licenses have criticized the government's import and export regulations, believing that they only benefit certain large businesses.

### 柬埔寨国王接受钦·迪内阁辞呈

1956-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国王
    接受钦·迪内阁辞呈
    新华社河内9日电　金边消息：柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特在八月八日接受了钦·迪内阁的辞呈。
    国王已经开始和议会的议长们磋商组织新政府的问题。
    钦·迪内阁是在七月二十九日提出辞职的。
    据法新社报道，辞职的原因是，一些拥有进口执照的议员对政府的进出口条例提出责难，认为它只对某些大的商家有利。
