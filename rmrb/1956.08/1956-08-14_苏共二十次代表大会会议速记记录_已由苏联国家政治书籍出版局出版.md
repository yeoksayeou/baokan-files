Date: 1956-08-14

    Verbatim Record of the 20th Congress of the CPSU Published by the State Political Literature Publishing House of the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th—The State Political Literature Publishing House of the Soviet Union has published the verbatim record of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The complete book is divided into two volumes, including various reports, speeches, and resolutions made at the Congress.
    On page 402 of the second volume of this book, it is written: "The 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a secret meeting on the morning of February 25, 1956. The meeting listened to the report 'On the Cult of Personality and Its Consequences' made by Comrade Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and made a resolution on this report." The content of the report is not included in this book.
    On page 498 of this book, it is written: "On February 25, 1956, the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union unanimously adopted the Party Congress's resolution on the report made by Comrade Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU, on the cult of personality and its consequences. After listening to Comrade Khrushchev's report, the 20th Congress of the CPSU approved the arguments in the report of the Central Committee of the CPSU and entrusted the Central Committee to take measures to ensure the complete overcoming of the cult of personality, which is incompatible with Marxism-Leninism, to eliminate the consequences of the cult of personality in the Party's, state's, and theoretical-ideological work, and to strictly implement the norms of Party life and the principles of collective leadership formulated by the great Lenin."

### 苏共二十次代表大会会议速记记录  已由苏联国家政治书籍出版局出版

1956-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    苏共二十次代表大会会议速记记录
    已由苏联国家政治书籍出版局出版
    新华社莫斯科13日电　苏联国家政治书籍出版局出版了苏联共产党第二十次代表大会会议的速记记录。全书分两册，其中包括在代表大会上作的各项报告、发言和决议。
    在这部书的第二册第402页写道：“苏联共产党第二十次代表大会于1956年2月25日上午举行了秘密会议。会议听取了苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫同志作的‘关于个人崇拜及其后果’的报告，并且就这一报告作了决议。”报告的内容没有载入这本书内。
    在这本书的第498页写道：“苏联共产党第二十次代表大会在1956年2月25日一致通过了党代表大会关于苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫所作的关于个人崇拜及其后果的决议。在听取了赫鲁晓夫同志的报告以后，苏共第二十次代表大会批准了苏共中央报告中的论点，并且委托中央委员会采取措施以保证完全克服与马克思列宁主义不相容的个人崇拜，在党的、国家的和理论思想工作方面消除个人崇拜的后果，严格执行党生活的准则和伟大列宁所制定的集体领导的原则。”
