Title: Albania and Yugoslavia Sign Agreement on Protecting Boundary Markers
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, August 18th—According to a report by the Albanian News Agency: After negotiations, representatives of the Albanian government and the Yugoslav government signed an agreement in Tirana on August 16 concerning the protection and reconstruction of boundary markers between the two countries.

### 阿尔巴尼亚和南斯拉夫  签订保护边界标志协定

1956-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚和南斯拉夫
    签订保护边界标志协定
    新华社18日讯　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：阿尔巴尼亚政府和南斯拉夫政府的代表经过了谈判以后，八月十六日在地拉那签订了一项有关保护和重建两国边界标志的协定。
