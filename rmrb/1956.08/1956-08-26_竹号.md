    Bamboo Horns
    Fu Chou
    A team of Young Pioneer buglers,
    Soared to the peaks of Mount Paektu;
    Like little lions holding their heads high,
    A row of bamboo horns raised to the sky.
    The majestic bugle call crossed mountains and ridges,
    Chasing snowflakes, flying ten thousand miles in the long sky;
    The children brought joy to the earth,
    Ten thousand households received the children's New Year's gift.
    Ten thousand households happily heard this bright bugle call,
    It vividly conveys the message of harvest, happiness, and peace.
    The children used bamboo horns to summarize the year,
    Joyfully welcoming the new year again.
    The peaks of Mount Paektu are quietly turning green,
    Listening attentively as the bugle call sends away the cold.
    The first to see spring is not the harbinger bird,
    But this team of Young Pioneer buglers.

傅仇

专栏：

    竹号
    傅仇
    一队红领巾号手，
    飞上了白头山峰；
    像小狮子高昂起头，
    一排竹号齐举向天空。
    雄壮的号声翻山越岭，
    追着雪花飞奔长空万里；
    孩子们给大地送来了欢乐，
    万户人家领受了孩子的年礼。
    万户人家喜闻这嘹亮的号声，
    它生动的传达了丰收、幸福、和平。
    孩子用竹号作了一年的总结，
    欢欢喜喜地又去迎接新的年辰。
    白头山峰已在悄悄发绿，
    谛听着号声把寒天送走。
    最早看见春天的，不是报春鸟，
    是这一队红领巾号手。
