Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Central Committee Issues Statement, Angrily Condemning West German Authorities' Infringement on Democracy; People of North Korea and Other Countries Voice Support for the German Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, August 26th, from TASS, Budapest, August 26th: The Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party issued a statement, expressing indignation on behalf of all Hungarian people at the West German authorities' illegal ban on the German Communist Party, and condemning this serious infringement on democracy. The statement expresses deep sympathy and solidarity with the German communists.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 25th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, working people in Hamhung City, known as the Korean-German Friendship City, held a mass rally on August 24th, strongly demanding that the Bonn authorities revoke the decision to declare the German Communist Party illegal and immediately restore the legal status of the German Communist Party.

The conference unanimously adopted a letter of encouragement and support in a very warm atmosphere to the West German workers who are fighting against the announcement that the German Communist Party is illegal. The letter said:

The working people of Hamhung City resolutely stand on the side of the German Communist Party, which insists on struggling for the peaceful and democratic reunification of Germany, and on the side of the German working people who are supporting this party. We are deeply convinced that this struggle, carried out in close unity with hundreds of millions of working people around the world, will be victorious.

Xinhua News Agency, Oslo, August 24th: According to "Friheten," the Norwegian Pipeline Workers' Union held a meeting in Oslo on August 23rd, at which a resolution was unanimously adopted protesting the banning of the German Communist Party. The resolution emphasized that the banning of the German Communist Party has aroused the anger of all freedom-loving Norwegians.

### 匈劳动人民党中央发表声明  怒斥西德当局侵犯民主  朝鲜等国人民声援德共

1956-08-27
第6版()
专栏：

    匈劳动人民党中央发表声明
    怒斥西德当局侵犯民主
    朝鲜等国人民声援德共
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯26日讯：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会发表声明，代表全体匈牙利人民对西德当局非法禁止德国共产党一事表示愤慨，并且谴责这一严重侵犯民主的行为。声明对西德共产党人表示深切的同情和声援。
    据新华社平壤25日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：被称为朝—德友好城的咸兴市的劳动人民8月24日举行了一次群众大会，强烈要求波恩当局撤销宣布德国共产党为非法的决定，立即恢复德国共产党的合法地位。
    大会在十分热诚的气氛中一致通过了一封鼓励和支持正在为反对宣布德国共产党非法一举而斗争的西德工人的信件。信上说：
    咸兴市的劳动人民坚决地站在坚持为德国的和平和民主的重新统一而斗争的德国共产党一边，站在正在支持这个党的德国劳动人民一边。我们深信，你们在同全世界亿万劳动人民紧密团结下进行的这个斗争，一定会获得胜利。
    新华社讯　塔斯社奥斯陆24日电：据“自由报”报道，挪威管道铺设工人工会8月23日在奥斯陆举行了会议，会上一致通过决议抗议取缔德国共产党。决议强调指出，取缔德国共产党一事激起了全体热爱自由的挪威人的愤怒。
