Title: Romanian Government Issues Statement: London Conference Has No Right to Make Legal Decisions
Author:
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 12th: The Romanian government issued a statement on the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company, stating that the Egyptian government's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company is Egypt's legitimate right as a sovereign and independent state. The British, French, and American governments' insistence that the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company means Egypt is violating international obligations and threatening the freedom of navigation of the canal is unfounded.

The Romanian government believes that, regardless of its nature, purpose, or members, the 24-nation conference convened in London at the initiative of the British, French, and American governments cannot be considered an international conference with the right to make legal decisions regarding the Suez Canal. The Romanian People's Republic was not invited to participate in the London Conference, and this is unacceptable.

The Romanian government believes that the United Nations is the appropriate body to discuss this issue. However, if certain countries insist on holding a conference on the Suez Canal issue, then in order to give this conference the necessary prestige, the conference should have broad representation. In this regard, the Romanian government agrees with the suggestions made by the Soviet government in its statement of August 9th.

The Romanian government is convinced that any disputes between countries can be resolved peacefully. For the sake of international peace and respect for the independence and sovereignty of all countries, the Romanian government is willing to contribute to the resolution of the Suez Canal issue.

### 罗马尼亚政府发表声明  伦敦会议无权作出合法决定

1956-08-14
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚政府发表声明
    伦敦会议无权作出合法决定
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社布加勒斯特十二日讯：罗马尼亚政府就苏彝士运河公司国有化问题发表声明说，埃及政府把苏彝士运河公司收归国有是埃及作为主权和独立国家的合法权利。英、法、美三国政府硬说苏彝士运河公司国有化就意味着埃及破坏国际义务和威胁运河航行自由，这种说法是没有任何根据的。
    罗马尼亚政府认为，不论从性质、目的和成员来看，由英、法、美三国政府倡议在伦敦召开的二十四国会议都不能够被认为是有权对苏彝士运河作出合法决定的国际会议。罗马尼亚人民共和国没有被邀请参加伦敦会议，这是不能同意的。
    罗马尼亚政府认为，联合国是讨论这个问题的适当的机构。但是，如果某些国家坚持召开苏彝士运河问题会议，那么为了使这个会议具有必要的威信，会议应当具有广泛的代表性，在这方面罗马尼亚政府同意苏联政府在八月九日的声明中所提出的建议。
    罗马尼亚政府确信，各国间的任何争执问题都是可以通过和平方式解决的。为了国际和平和尊重各国独立和主权，罗马尼亚政府愿意对解决苏彝士运河问题作出贡献。
