Title: Egyptian Public Opinion Opposes “International Control” of the Canal
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 4th dispatch: Egyptian newspapers and radio stations are vehemently criticizing the joint communiqué issued by the United Kingdom, France, and the United States demanding the establishment of an "international organization" to control the Suez Canal.

    The "Al-Ahram" newspaper wrote in an editorial that the Suez Canal is an Egyptian enterprise operated by an Egyptian company on Egyptian territory. "Egypt refuses international administration of the Suez Canal and any other form of administration. Egypt has declared that it guarantees freedom of navigation on the canal, and nothing obstructs this freedom." The newspaper emphasized that the economic and military sanctions threatened by Western countries may create "real obstacles to freedom of navigation."
    "Al-Akhbar" said: Britain says it cannot accept any arrangement that places the Suez Canal under the control of one country. "Then what about Gibraltar, Aden, Bab el-Mandeb Strait (the strait at the Aden end of the Red Sea), the Dardanelles Strait, and the Panama Canal? If these waterways were all placed under international control, Egypt would agree to discuss the question of placing the Suez Canal under international control for the benefit of the whole world."

### 埃及舆论反对“国际管制”运河

1956-08-05
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及舆论反对“国际管制”运河
    新华社4日讯　开罗消息：埃及的报纸和广播电台猛烈地抨击英、法、美三国要求成立“国际机构”来管制苏彝士运河的联合公报。
    “金字塔报”在社论中写道，苏彝士运河是在埃及领土上由埃及公司经营的埃及企业。“埃及拒绝对苏彝士运河的国际管理和任何其他的管理。埃及已经宣布，它保证运河的通航自由，并且没有任何东西阻碍这种自由。”这家报纸强调说，西方国家扬言要采取的经济和军事制裁可能造成“对通航自由的真正障碍”。
    “今日消息”说：英国说它不能接受把苏彝士运河置于一个国家控制下的任何安排。“那末直布罗陀、亚丁、巴布厄尔曼得峡（在红海的亚丁那端的海峡）、达达尼尔海峡和巴拿马运河又怎么样呢？如果这些水路都已置于国际管制之下，埃及就答应讨论关于为了整个世界利益而把苏彝士运河置于国际管制下的问题。”
