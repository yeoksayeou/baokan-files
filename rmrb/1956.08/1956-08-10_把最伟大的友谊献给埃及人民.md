Title: Offering the Greatest Friendship to the Egyptian People
Author: Liao Chengzhi, Hu Yaobang, Hu Qili
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 1

    Offering the Greatest Friendship to the Egyptian People
    Liao Chengzhi, Hu Yaobang, Hu Qili
    If someone asks: What are Chinese youth most concerned about in the current international situation? Chinese youth will definitely answer without hesitation: We are most concerned about the great struggle launched by the Egyptian people on the Suez Canal issue. Why? Because this struggle is related to the autonomous rights of Asian and African countries, to world peace, and to the safety and happiness of mankind.
    If someone asks: What attitude do Chinese youth take towards this struggle? Chinese youth will definitely answer unequivocally: We resolutely, completely, and unhesitatingly stand on the side of the Egyptian people. Why? Because Egypt's recovery of the Suez Canal is a sacred struggle to safeguard national sovereignty, a brilliant example of the Arab people realizing the Bandung spirit, and another magnificent march by the Asian and African people to crush colonialism.
    If someone asks: Why do Chinese youth resolutely, completely, and unhesitatingly stand on the side of the Egyptian people? Chinese youth will definitely answer unequivocally: In the face of this great and victorious struggle, anyone who adopts a hesitant, ambiguous position is consciously or unconsciously condoning the rampant bullying of colonialists. All good people have deeply understood from long-term personal experience the following truth: Maintaining peace requires upholding justice, and maintaining peace requires relying on the unbreakable unity of peace-loving people all over the world. Bowing to colonialists who interfere in the internal affairs of other countries will inevitably lead to the destruction of peace; bravely fighting against lawless colonialists will definitely lead to lasting peace.
    The Egyptian people are absolutely right to do so. The merits of the Egyptian people in this struggle will last forever.
    Dear young Egyptian brothers, your great struggle is inspiring the youth of the world to actively take control of their own destiny. They will stand closely with you. We wish you to hold high the sacred banner of safeguarding national independence and defending world peace, and to advance even more heroically. The 120 million Chinese youth who are forever faithful to the Bandung spirit will surely offer you the greatest friendship!

### 把最伟大的友谊献给埃及人民
廖承志、胡耀邦、胡启立
1956-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    把最伟大的友谊献给埃及人民
    廖承志、胡耀邦、胡启立
    如果有人问：中国青年对目前国际局势最关心的是什么？中国青年一定毫不迟疑地回答：我们最关心的是埃及人民在苏彝士运河问题上所展开的巨大的斗争。为什么？因为这个斗争关系到亚非各国人民自主权利，关系到世界的和平，关系到人类的安全和幸福。
    如果有人问：中国青年对这个斗争采取什么态度？中国青年一定毫不含糊地回答：我们坚决地、完全地、毫不犹豫地站在埃及人民这一边。为什么？因为埃及人民收回苏彝士运河是维护国家主权的神圣斗争，是阿拉伯人民实现万隆精神的辉煌范例，是亚非人民摧垮殖民主义的又一次雄伟的进军。
    如果有人问：为什么中国青年坚决地、完全地、毫不犹豫地站在埃及人民这一边？中国青年一定毫不含糊地回答：在这个伟大的胜利斗争面前，谁要采取瞻前顾后、模棱两可的立场，谁就是自觉地或不自觉地纵容了殖民主义者横行霸道。一切善良的人们从长期的切身经历中深深懂得了这样一条真理：维护和平必须伸张正义，维护和平必须依靠全世界爱好和平的人民牢不可破的团结。向干涉他国内政的殖民主义者低头，和平就一定被断送；勇敢地向无法无天的殖民主义者斗争，和平就一定能持久。
    埃及人民完全做得对。埃及人民在这一斗争中的功绩，必将与世长存。
    亲爱的年青的埃及弟兄们，你们的伟大斗争激动着全世界的青年积极地去掌握自己的命运。他们将同你们紧紧地在一起。祝你们高举着维护民族独立和保卫世界和平的神圣大旗，更英勇地前进。永远忠实于万隆精神的一亿二千万中国青年，一定把最伟大的友谊献给你们！
