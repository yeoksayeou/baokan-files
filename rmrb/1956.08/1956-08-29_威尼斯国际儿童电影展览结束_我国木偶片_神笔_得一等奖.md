Title: Venice International Children's Film Exhibition Concludes: Chinese Puppet Film "Magic Brush" Wins First Prize
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 28th: News from Venice: The Documentary Film section of the 1956 Venice International Film Festival in Italy and the Eighth International Children's Film Exhibition concluded on the evening of August 25th. The Chinese puppet film "Magic Brush" and the Soviet children's film "Yalangjia's Fire" won the first prize for art films for children aged eight to twelve at the International Children's Film Exhibition. More than twenty documentaries and ten children's films from countries such as the Soviet Union, the United States, Italy, France, and Czechoslovakia also won awards.

On the evening of the 25th, a grand evening party was held in Venice, screening outstanding documentaries from various countries. The Chinese documentary "Joyful Songs and Dances" was very well received.

Cai Chusheng, the representative of China participating in the Venice International Film Festival, held a reception in Venice on the 25th to entertain the film festival hosts, local literary and art circles, film representatives from various countries, and news reporters from various countries.

### 威尼斯国际儿童电影展览结束  我国木偶片“神笔”得一等奖

1956-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

    威尼斯国际儿童电影展览结束
    我国木偶片“神笔”得一等奖
    新华社布拉格28日电　威尼斯消息：1956年意大利威尼斯国际电影节中的纪录片和第八届国际儿童电影展览在8月25日晚上结束。参加国际儿童电影展览的中国木偶片“神笔”和苏联儿童片“亚浪珈之火”获得了八岁到十二岁儿童文艺影片的一等奖，同时得奖的还有苏联、美国、意大利、法国和捷克斯洛伐克等国的二十多部纪录片和十部儿童片。
    25日晚上威尼斯举行了盛大晚会，放映各国精彩纪录片。我国纪录片“欢乐歌舞”受到很大的欢迎。
    中国参加威尼斯国际电影节代表蔡楚生25日在威尼斯举行酒会，招待电影节主持人、当地文艺界、各国电影代表、各国新闻记者等。
