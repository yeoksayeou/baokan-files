Title: Chiang Kai-shek soldier Meng Shengmin who fled to Burma returns

Author:

Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, August 12th: Meng Shengmin, a radio operator for the Chiang Kai-shek army who fled to Burma and recently returned to the motherland, arrived in Kunming from a border area on the afternoon of the 11th, and was warmly received by the People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming.

Meng Shengmin was inspired to return to the country after secretly listening to the recorded broadcast of Premier Zhou Enlai's report at the Third Session of the First National People's Congress while in the Chiang Kai-shek army that had fled to Burma.

Meng Shengmin will rest in Kunming for some time and go sightseeing.

### 逃缅蒋军孟省民回来了

1956-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    逃缅蒋军孟省民回来了
    新华社昆明12日电　最近回到祖国的逃缅蒋军电台工作人员孟省民，已于十一日下午从边疆某地到达昆明，受到中国人民解放军驻昆明部队的热情接待。
    孟省民是在逃往缅甸的蒋军中偷着收听了周恩来总理在第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议上的报告录音广播后，受到感召而回国的。
    孟省民将在昆明休息一些时候，并进行参观游览。
