Title: Rushing to Plug the Magaozhuang Breach in the Yongding River
Author:
Date: 1956-08-12
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, 11th. To ensure the safety of the Beijing-Shanhaiguan railway, work began today to rush and plug the breach in the Yongding River at Magaozhuang, Daxing County, Hebei Province, caused by rising water.

On the afternoon of the 9th, the Ministry of Water Resources held an emergency meeting for this purpose. With the consent of relevant departments in Beijing, it was decided to divert the Yongding River water into the Xiaoqing River near Lugou Bridge to facilitate rushing to plug the breach without water passing through it.

The work of diverting water from the Magaozhuang breach has also begun.

### 抢堵永定河麻各庄决口

1956-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    抢堵永定河麻各庄决口
    据新华社11日讯　为保障北京—山海关铁路的安全，永定河因涨水而在河北省大兴县麻各庄的决口，今天开始动工抢堵。
    九日下午，水利部曾为此召开紧急会议，经北京市有关方面同意，决定在芦沟桥附近把永定河水导入小清河，以便利在决口处不过水的情况下抢堵。
    麻各庄决口溃水的疏导工作也已经开始。
