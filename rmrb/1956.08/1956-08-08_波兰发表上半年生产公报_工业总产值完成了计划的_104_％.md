Title: Poland Publishes Production Communiqué for the First Half of the Year: Total Industrial Output Value Reached 104% of the Plan

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 7th: The Warsaw newspapers published on August 7th a communiqué from the Polish Central Statistical Bureau regarding the results of the national economic plan execution for the first half of 1956.

The communiqué stated that the total industrial output value plan for the first half of the year was fulfilled by 104%. The total output value of socialist industry increased by 11% compared to the first half of last year.

In the first half of this year, some of the most important industrial products reached the following quantities: 1,739,000 tons of pig iron, 2,461,000 tons of steel, 1,644,000 tons of steel products, 48 million tons of hard coal, 9,412 million kilowatt-hours of electricity, and 272 million meters of cotton fabrics.

According to statistical data from June this year, the total sown area of agriculture increased by 1.2% compared to the same period last year, the number of cattle increased by 5%, and the number of pigs increased by 6%. In the first half of this year, agriculture received 130,900 tons of nitrogen fertilizer, an increase of 21% compared to the same period last year. State-owned farms and machine tractor stations had a total of 59,000 tractors (calculated in 15 horsepower units), and the number of tractors in machine tractor stations increased by 24% compared to the same period last year. By the end of June, there were 425 machine tractor stations, and the amount of work they completed in the first half of the year increased by more than 46% compared to the same period last year.

The number of agricultural production cooperatives was approximately 10,600 at the end of June.

The retail trade plan for the first half of this year was fulfilled by 104%, with a turnover increase of 13% compared to the same period last year. The number of employed people in the national economy increased by approximately 4% compared to the first half of 1955. In the first half of this year, 2 million people had their wages adjusted, resulting in an annual wage increase of 3,502.3 million zlotys (Polish currency). The total value of foreign trade increased by 3% compared to the first half of last year, with exports increasing by 11%.

### 波兰发表上半年生产公报  工业总产值完成了计划的104％

1956-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰发表上半年生产公报
    工业总产值完成了计划的104％
    新华社华沙7日电　华沙报纸八月七日刊载了波兰中央统计局发表的关于1956年上半年国民经济计划执行结果的公报。
    公报说，今年上半年工业总产值计划完成了104％。社会主义工业总产值比去年上半年增加了11％。
    在今年上半年，一些最重要的工业产品已经达到下列数量：生铁一百七十三万九千吨，钢二百四十六万一千吨，钢材一百六十四万四千吨，硬煤四千八百万吨，电力九十四亿一千二百万度，棉织品二亿七千二百万公尺。
    根据今年六月份的统计材料，农业的总播种面积比去年同期增加了1.2％，牛的头数增加了5％，猪的头数增加了6％。今年上半年农业所得到的氮肥有十三万零九百吨，比去年同期增加了21％。国营农场和机器拖拉机站共有拖拉机五万九千架（以十五匹马力计算），机器拖拉机站的拖拉机数目较去年同期增加了24％。到六月底止，机器拖拉机站共有四百二十五个，它们半年来完成的工作比去年同期增加了46％以上。
    农业生产合作社的数目在六月底约有一万零六百个。
    今年上半年的零售贸易计划完成了104％，比去年同期的周转额增加了13％。国民经济中的就业人数比1955年上半年增加了约4％。今年上半年有二百万人调整了工资，他们每年增加的工资有三十五亿零二百三十万兹罗提（波币名）。对外贸易额比去年上半年增加了3％，其中出口额增加了11％。
