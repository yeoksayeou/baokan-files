Title: Laos and Cambodia Decide to Exchange Ministers
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 17th: Vientiane Radio broadcast on the 16th: The Lao Royal Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a communique stating that the Royal Lao Government and the Royal Cambodian Government have decided to further strengthen the friendship between the two countries and establish diplomatic relations between the two countries, and will exchange ministers.

### 老挝和柬埔寨  决定互派公使

1956-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝和柬埔寨
    决定互派公使
    新华社河内17日电　万象电台十六日广播：老挝王国外交部发表公报说，老挝王国政府和柬埔寨王国政府已经决定进一步密切两国之间的友谊和建立两国之间的外交关系，并且互派公使。
