Title: Second National Congress of the China Association for Promoting Democracy Sends Telegram Expressing Strong Support for the Egyptian Government's Statement

Author:

Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: The Second National Congress of the China Association for Promoting Democracy, currently in session, today sent a telegram to the President of the Arab Republic of Egypt, Gamal Abdel Nasser, stating: "We fully support your and the Egyptian people's solemn declaration against the international control of the Suez Canal. Please allow us, on behalf of all members of our association, to extend our highest and warmest respect to you and all the Egyptian people!"

The telegram also stated: The Egyptian government's just action of nationalizing the Suez Canal Company and the heroic struggle of the Egyptian people have won widespread sympathy and support around the world; while the ugly behavior of the colonialists has incurred well-deserved indignation and condemnation. The struggle of the Egyptian people not only exercises the sacred patriotic right, but also defends human dignity and the due justice between nations, and will be a strong contribution to world peace.

### 中国民主促进会第二次全国代表大会  致电支持埃及政府的严正声明

1956-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中国民主促进会第二次全国代表大会
    致电支持埃及政府的严正声明
    据新华社14日讯　正在举行的中国民主促进会第二次全国代表大会今天打电报给埃及共和国总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔说：“我们完全支持您和埃及人民反对国际管制苏彝士运河的庄严声明。请允许我们代表我会全体会员，向您和全体埃及人民致以崇高和亲切的敬意！”
    这份电报中还说：埃及政府把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的正义行动和埃及人民的英勇斗争，博得全世界广泛的同情和支持；而殖民主义者的丑恶行为，已经招致了应得的义愤和谴责。埃及人民的斗争不仅是执行了神圣的爱国权利，并且保卫了人类的尊严和国际间应有的公道，对世界和平也将是一个有力的贡献。
