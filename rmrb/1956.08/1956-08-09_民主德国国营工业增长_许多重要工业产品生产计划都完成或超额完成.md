Title: East German State-Owned Industry Grows; Production Plans for Many Important Industrial Products Completed or Exceeded

Author:

Date: 1956-08-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 8th: The German News Agency published a communique from the Central Statistical Bureau of the German Democratic Republic on August 7th, regarding the implementation of the national economic plan for the second quarter of 1956.

The communique pointed out that the total output and labor productivity of the central state-owned industrial sector increased by 8% compared to the same period last year; the production of local state-owned industry increased by 9%.

The central state-owned industrial plan was 99.3% fulfilled, and the local state-owned industrial plan was 98% fulfilled. However, the production of many important industrial products completed or exceeded the plan, including the production of electricity and gas, the mining of bituminous coal, iron ore, and copper ore, the production of pig iron and steel, the manufacturing of agricultural machinery, the production of synthetic rubber, the production of fabrics, knitwear, and shoes, etc.

In agriculture, the total amount of work completed by machine tractor stations increased by 20% compared to last year. The number of agricultural production cooperatives increased by 499 in the period from May 31, 1955, to June 30, 1956. Currently, agricultural production cooperatives cultivate 23% of the total arable land in the country.

The freight turnover plan was fulfilled by 105%, and the turnover volume increased by 10% compared to the same period last year.

The total value of foreign and domestic trade in the second quarter also increased by 12% compared to the same period last year.

### 民主德国国营工业增长  许多重要工业产品生产计划都完成或超额完成

1956-08-09
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国国营工业增长
    许多重要工业产品生产计划都完成或超额完成
    新华社柏林8日电　德意志通讯社在八月七日发表德意志民主共和国中央统计局关于1956年第二季度国民经济计划执行情况的公报。
    公报指出，中央国营工业部门的生产总量和劳动生产率比去年同期分别提高了8％；地方国营工业的生产增加了9％。
    中央国营工业的计划完成了99.3％，地方国营工业的计划完成了98％。但是，许多重要工业产品的生产都完成或超额完成了计划，其中包括电力和煤气的生产，烟煤、铁矿和铜矿的开采，生铁和钢的生产，农业机器的制造，人造橡胶的生产，布匹、针织品、鞋类的生产等等。
    在农业方面，机器拖拉机站所完成的工作总量比去年增加了20％，农业生产合作社在1955年5月31日到1956年6月30日这一段时间内增加了四百九十九个，现在农业生产合作社耕种的土地占全国耕地面积总额的23％。
    货物运转计划完成了105％，运转量比去年同期提高了10％。
    第二季度的对外和国内贸易总额也比去年同期提高了12％。
