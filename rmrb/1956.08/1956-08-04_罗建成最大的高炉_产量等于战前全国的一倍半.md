Title: Luo Jiancheng's Largest Blast Furnace: Output Equals One and a Half Times the Pre-War National Total
Author:
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 5

    Luo Jiancheng's Largest Blast Furnace
    Output Equals One and a Half Times the Pre-War National Total
    (Dispatch from Bucharest) The largest blast furnace in Romania, the No. 7 blast furnace of the Hunedoara Iron and Steel Plant, has recently been completed. This blast furnace has a capacity of 700 cubic meters, and the iron it produces annually will be equal to one and a half times Romania's total iron production in 1938.
    Hunedoara is Romania's largest metallurgical base. In recent years, large-scale renovations and expansions have been carried out here. During the first Five-Year Plan, the following have been put into production: a blast furnace with a capacity of 400 cubic meters, a thermal power center, the first phase of a coking chemical plant, and a sintering plant. The newly completed No. 7 blast furnace is the first of a series of giant blast furnaces scheduled to be completed during the second Five-Year Plan period (1956-1960).

### 罗建成最大的高炉  产量等于战前全国的一倍半

1956-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    罗建成最大的高炉
    产量等于战前全国的一倍半
    本报讯　布加勒斯特消息：罗马尼亚最大的高炉——洪尼多阿拉黑色冶金工厂的七号高炉最近已经建成。这座高炉的容量为七百立方公尺，它每年生产的铁将等于1938年全罗马尼亚铁产量的一倍半。
    洪尼多阿拉是罗马尼亚最大的冶金基地。近几年来，这里进行了大规模的改建和扩建工作。在第一个五年计划期间已投入生产的有：容量为四百立方公尺的高炉、热电中心、炼焦化学厂的第一期工程以及烧结工厂。新建成的七号高炉是预定在第二个五年计划期间（1956—1960年）建成的一系列巨大高炉的第一个。
