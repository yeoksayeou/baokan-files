Title: Nasser Receives Soviet and Indian Ambassadors Again
Author:
Date: 1956-08-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 5th: Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser met again with Soviet Ambassador Daniel Solod and Indian Ambassador Ali Yavar Jung on August 4th. This was the second time within twelve hours that he had met with the two ambassadors.

### 纳赛尔再接见苏印大使

1956-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔再接见苏印大使
    新华社5日讯　开罗消息：埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔八月四日分别再次会见了苏联大使基谢廖夫和印度大使阿里·雅瓦尔·忠格。这是他在十二小时之内第二次会见这两位大使。
