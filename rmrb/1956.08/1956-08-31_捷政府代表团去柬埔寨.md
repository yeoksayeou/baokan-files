Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation to Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1956-08-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 30th: A Czechoslovak government delegation, headed by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Šimović, left Prague by plane on August 29 for the Kingdom of Cambodia.

### 捷政府代表团去柬埔寨

1956-08-31
第5版()
专栏：

    捷政府代表团去柬埔寨
    据新华社布拉格30日电　以外交部副部长西莫维茨为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团8月29日乘飞机离布拉格前往柬埔寨王国。
