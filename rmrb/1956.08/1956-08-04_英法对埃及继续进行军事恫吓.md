Title: Britain and France Continue Military Intimidation of Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd, London dispatch: The British government is continuing to implement military intimidation in an attempt to achieve its goal of interfering with Egypt's measure of nationalizing the Suez Canal.

August 2nd, the British Queen signed an order to call up army reservists for service. The Ministry of War issued an order on the same day, requiring six infantry regiments to be on alert, ready to act at any time. The British Navy and Air Force are also in a state of mobilization.

Furthermore, French officials announced on the evening of August 2nd that the French Mediterranean Fleet, composed of cruisers, escort ships, aircraft carriers, and other ships, docked in Toulon, had been ordered to prepare to set sail at any time.

### 英法对埃及继续进行军事恫吓

1956-08-04
第6版()
专栏：

    英法对埃及继续进行军事恫吓
    新华社3日讯　伦敦消息：英政府在继续施行军事恫吓，以图达到它干涉埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的措施的目的。
    八月二日，英国女王签署了一项关于征召陆军后备人员服役的命令。陆军部在同一天发布命令，要六个步兵团警戒待命，准备随时行动。英国的海军和空军也处在动员状态中。
    又讯　法国官方人士二日晚宣布，停泊在土伦港的由巡洋舰、护航舰、航空母舰和其他舰只组成的法国地中海舰队已经奉命准备随时出动。
