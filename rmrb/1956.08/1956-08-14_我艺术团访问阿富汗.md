Title: Our Art Troupe Visits Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 13th—At the invitation of the government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, the People's Republic of China Cultural and Art Delegation, led by the head of the delegation, Ma Hanbing, left Kunming by plane this morning for Kabul to conduct a performance tour.
The Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation consists of sixty-nine people. The programs to be performed in Afghanistan this time include folk songs and dances, instrumental performances, and classical song and dance dramas.

### 我艺术团访问阿富汗

1956-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    我艺术团访问阿富汗
    新华社昆明13日电　中华人民共和国文化艺术代表团应阿富汗王国政府的邀请，在团长马寒冰率领下，已于今晨乘飞机离开昆明前往喀布尔进行访问演出。
    中国文化艺术代表团由六十九人组成，这次去阿富汗演出的节目包括民间歌舞、器乐演奏和古典歌舞剧。
