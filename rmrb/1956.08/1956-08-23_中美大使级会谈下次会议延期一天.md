Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Next Meeting Postponed by One Day
Author:
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 22nd. It is understood that the Chinese and American ambassadors have agreed to postpone the fifty-seventh meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks, originally scheduled for September 6th, to September 7th.

### 中美大使级会谈下次会议延期一天

1956-08-23
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈下次会议延期一天
    新华社日内瓦22日电　据悉，中美两国大使已经同意，原定在九月六日举行的中美两国大使会谈第五十七次会议，延期到九月七日举行。
