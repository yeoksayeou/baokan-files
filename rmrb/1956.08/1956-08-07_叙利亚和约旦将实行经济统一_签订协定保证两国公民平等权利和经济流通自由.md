Title: Syria and Jordan to Implement Economic Unification; Agreement Signed Guaranteeing Equal Rights for Citizens and Free Economic Circulation Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-08-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 6th -- Syria and Jordan announced on August 5th that they had signed an agreement to implement a unified economy.

A joint communique issued by representatives of the two countries after five days of talks in Damascus announced that the agreement they signed stipulated the implementation of complete economic unification governing all aspects of the economy.

The communique stated that the agreement guarantees Syrian and Jordanian citizens equal rights and freedoms in terms of work, employment, residence, and property, as well as freedom of movement for people and capital, and the circulation of goods and products.

The communique stated that, according to this agreement, the two countries will form a unified customs area. The agreement will be implemented after ratification by the parliaments of both countries.

### 叙利亚和约旦将实行经济统一  签订协定保证两国公民平等权利和经济流通自由

1956-08-07
第5版()
专栏：

    叙利亚和约旦将实行经济统一
    签订协定保证两国公民平等权利和经济流通自由
    新华社6日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚和约旦八月五日宣布，它们两国签订了实行统一经济的协定。
    两国代表在大马士革举行五天的会谈后发表的一项联合公报宣布，他们签订的协定规定将实行支配经济各方面的完全的经济统一。
    公报说，这个协定保证叙利亚和约旦的公民在工作、就业、居住和财产方面有平等权利和自由，以及人和资本的移动与货物、产品流通的自由。
    公报说，根据这个协定，两国将形成一个统一的关税区。协定将在两国的议会批准后实行。
