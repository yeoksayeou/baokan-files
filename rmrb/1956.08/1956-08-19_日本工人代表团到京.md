Title: Japanese Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: The Japanese workers' delegation, headed by Sakuma Koichi, Vice Chairman of the Japan Government and Public Office Workers' Union Council, arrived in Beijing yesterday after visiting Poland and the Soviet Union, at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 日本工人代表团到京

1956-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

    日本工人代表团到京
    据新华社18日讯　以日本官公厅工会协议会副主席佐久间孝一为首的日本工人代表团一行六人，在波兰、苏联参观后，应中华全国总工会的邀请在昨天到达北京。
