Title: Premier Zhou Sends Condolences on the Passing of Bulgarian Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 15th. Premier Zhou Enlai and concurrently Foreign Minister, on the 14th, sent a telegram to Anton Yugov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, to express condolences on the passing of Mincho Neychev, Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

In the telegram, Premier Zhou Enlai, on behalf of the Chinese government, expressed deep sorrow to the government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and extended sincere sympathy and condolences to the family of Mincho Neychev.

### 周总理电唁保外交部长逝世

1956-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电唁保外交部长逝世
    新华社15日讯　周恩来总理兼外交部长十四日打电报给保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席安·于哥夫，吊唁保加利亚人民共和国外交部长明·涅伊切夫的逝世。
    周恩来总理在电文中代表中国政府向保加利亚人民共和国政府表示沉痛的哀悼，并且向明·涅伊切夫的家属表示深切的同情和慰问。
