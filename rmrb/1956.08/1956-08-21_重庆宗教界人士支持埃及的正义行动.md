Title: Religious Figures in Chongqing Support Egypt's Just Actions
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, August 19th: Forty-six religious figures in Chongqing held a symposium on the 18th, expressing their support for Egypt's just action in nationalizing the Suez Canal Company.

    Ding Qishan, the head of the Islamic Association of Chongqing, stated at the meeting that all Muslims in our city fully support the statement issued by our government on the Suez Canal issue. Imperialists' attempts to interfere in Egypt's internal affairs will be resolutely opposed by the Egyptian people and peace-loving people around the world.

    Representatives from Christian, Catholic, and Buddhist circles also spoke at the meeting to support the just actions of the Egyptian people. Shi Taikong, President of the Chongqing Buddhist Association, Shi Wuyi, Secretary-General of the Buddhist Association, and Shi Biankong, a member of the Buddhist Association Council, exposed the colonialists' conspiracy to use force to interfere in Egypt's internal affairs.

    The symposium concluded with the adoption of a resolution expressing support for the Chinese government's statement on the Suez Canal issue and supporting Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company.

### 重庆宗教界人士支持埃及的正义行动

1956-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    重庆宗教界人士支持埃及的正义行动
    据新华社重庆19日电　重庆市宗教界人士四十六人在十八日举行座谈会，支持埃及将苏彝士运河公司收归国有的正义行动。
    重庆市伊斯兰教教长丁岐山在会上发言说，我们全市伊斯兰教徒完全拥护我国政府就苏彝士运河问题发表的声明。帝国主义者企图干涉埃及内政，必将遭到埃及人民和世界爱好和平人民的坚决反对。
    基督教、天主教和佛教界人士也在会上发言支持埃及人民的正义行动。重庆市佛教协会会长释太空、佛教协会秘书长释晤一、佛教协会理事释遍空等发言揭露殖民主义者企图用武力干涉埃及内政的阴谋。
    座谈会最后通过了一项决议，表示拥护我国政府关于苏彝士运河问题的声明，支持埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有。
