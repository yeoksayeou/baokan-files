Title: Soong Ching Ling Bids Farewell to Indonesia and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 23rd: Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, has concluded her ten-day visit to Indonesia and returned to China by plane this morning.

    This morning at 8:15, Soong Ching Ling went to the Merdeka Palace to bid farewell to President Sukarno. Tens of thousands of people lined the roads leading to Kemayoran Airport, and when Soong Ching Ling's car passed this road at 8:40, people waved to her and said: Goodbye.

    Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his wife went to the airport to see the distinguished guest off. Those who also went to the airport to see her off included Indonesian Speaker of Parliament Sardjono, Second Deputy Speaker Katawena, Jakarta Mayor Sudiro, the wife of Foreign Minister Abdulgani, Professor Priyono, Chairman of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association, other officials, and members of various foreign diplomatic missions. Chinese Ambassador Huang Zhen and his wife, as well as all the staff of the Chinese Embassy, also went to the airport to see her off.

    Soong Ching Ling delivered a brief farewell speech at the airport. She said that in the past ten days, she felt very kind everywhere she went, as if she were at home. She was warmly and sincerely received everywhere.

    Soong Ching Ling said that the warm and sincere hospitality of President Sukarno and the Indonesian government, as well as the warm welcome given to her by the people during her trips to Jakarta, Bandung, Yogyakarta, and Bali, were unforgettable. She assured her hosts that she would definitely bring the heartfelt hospitality and friendship of the Indonesian people to the Chinese people and tell them of the profound impressions she had gained in Indonesia.

    Soong Ching Ling expressed her gratitude for the friendly hospitality of her hosts and wished them victory in their heroic struggle to reclaim West Irian and new achievements in building a prosperous Indonesia.

    Yesterday afternoon, before attending a mass rally held in the stadium, Soong Ching Ling visited Mrs. Sukarno. The guests and hosts had a very friendly conversation.

### 宋庆龄告别印度尼西亚回国

1956-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄告别印度尼西亚回国
    新华社雅加达23日电　中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄已经结束了对印度尼西亚的十天访问，并且在今天上午乘飞机回中国。
    宋庆龄今天上午在八点十五分到独立宫向苏加诺总统辞行。成千上万的人排列在通往克玛约兰机场的道路的两旁，当宋庆龄所乘的汽车在八点四十分驶过这条路的时候，人们都挥手向她说：再见。
    阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和他的夫人到机场为贵宾送行。到机场送行的还有印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺、第二副议长卡塔韦纳塔、雅加达市长苏迪罗、外交部长阿卜杜加尼的夫人、印度尼西亚—中国友好协会主席普里约诺教授和其他官员以及各国外交使团人员。中国大使黄镇和他的夫人以及中国大使馆全体人员也到机场送行。
    宋庆龄在机场发表简短的告别演说。她说，在过去十天中，她在每个地方都感到很亲切，好像在家里一样。她在每个地方都受到了热烈的和诚挚的接待。
    宋庆龄说，苏加诺总统和印度尼西亚政府的热情的和真诚的招待以及她在雅加达、万隆、日惹和峇里旅行期间人民给予她的热烈的欢迎，都是令人难忘的。她向主人们保证，她一定把印度尼西亚人民由衷的款待和友情带给中国人民，并且要告诉他们她在印度尼西亚所获得的深刻印象。
    宋庆龄对主人们的友好招待表示感谢，祝他们在争取收回西伊里安的英勇斗争中取得胜利和在建设一个繁荣的印度尼西亚的工作中获得新的成就。
    昨天下午在参加在体育馆举行的民众集会以前，宋庆龄拜访了苏加诺夫人。宾主进行了非常友好的谈话。
