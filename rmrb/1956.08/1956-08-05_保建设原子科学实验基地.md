Title: Safeguarding the Construction of an Atomic Science Experimental Base
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, August 3rd (TASS): Bulgaria is constructing its first atomic science experimental base.

    On the construction site, the first productive building has begun to be built. A powerful reactor will be installed in this building. The construction of the Physics Institute is proceeding at full speed. This institute will be equipped with the most modern machines and instruments so that Bulgarian scientists can conduct research on the peaceful use of atomic energy here.

### 保建设原子科学实验基地

1956-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    保建设原子科学实验基地
    新华社4日讯　塔斯社索非亚三日讯：保加利亚正在建设第一个原子科学实验基地。
    在建设工地上，第一座生产性建筑已经开始建造。在这座建筑里将安装一个效力强大的反应堆。物理研究所的建筑工程正在全力展开。这个研究所将配备最现代化的机器和仪器，以便保加利亚的科学家们在这里进行和平利用原子能的研究。
