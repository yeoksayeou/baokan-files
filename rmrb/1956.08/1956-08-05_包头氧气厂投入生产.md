Title: Baotou Oxygen Plant Starts Production
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, August 2nd: A newly built oxygen plant in Baotou went into production on the 1st. The oxygen produced has already begun to be supplied to the Baotou industrial base.

　　The Baotou Oxygen Plant was built in March of this year. In the past, the oxygen needed by the Baotou industrial base was supplied by Beijing, Tianjin and other places.

### 包头氧气厂投入生产

1956-08-05
第2版()
专栏：

    包头氧气厂投入生产
    新华社呼和浩特2日电　在包头新建的一座氧气厂一日投入生产；所生产的氧气，已经开始向包头工业基地供应。
    包头氧气厂是今年三月兴建的。过去包头工业基地需要的氧气，都由北京、天津等地供应。
