Title: Three Organizations Including the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Send Telegrams to Congratulate the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs
Author:
Date: 1956-08-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee, and the Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee today sent telegrams to the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, congratulating them on the opening of the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs.

The telegram from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace first expressed respect to the Japanese people for their unremitting struggle to ban atomic and hydrogen bombs, and extended cordial greetings to the victims of atomic bombing and hydrogen bomb testing. The telegram said: "The Chinese people warmly support the just struggle of the Japanese people against atomic weapons and are willing to work with the Japanese people and the people of the world to eliminate the threat of atomic war and ensure world peace."

The telegram from the Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee said: "The holding of the conference shows the strong will of the Japanese people to oppose atomic weapons. Let the people of Asian countries and the people of the world unite and fight together against atomic weapons and defend peace in Asia and the world."

The telegram from the Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee said: "The people of Asia and the Pacific region and the world firmly oppose atomic weapons and support the just struggle of the Japanese people who have personally suffered from atomic bombing and hydrogen bomb testing. We believe that the convening of this conference will greatly contribute to the movement of the people of all countries in the world to demand the prohibition of atomic weapons and the elimination of the threat of atomic war, thereby ensuring peace in Asia and the world."

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等三团体  电贺禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会

1956-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等三团体
    电贺禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会
    新华社5日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国亚洲团结委员会和亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处今天分别发出电报给日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会，祝贺第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的召开。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的电报首先向为禁止原子弹、氢弹而奋斗不懈的日本人民表示敬意，并对于遭受原子弹轰炸和氢弹试验的受害者致以亲切的慰问。电报说：“中国人民热烈支持日本人民为反对原子武器所作的正义斗争，并愿与日本人民和全世界人民一道，为消除原子战争威胁，确保世界和平而共同奋斗。”
    中国亚洲团结委员会的电报说：“大会的举行显示着日本人民反对原子武器的强烈意志。让我们亚洲各国人民和全世界人民团结一致，为反对原子武器、保卫亚洲和世界和平而共同斗争。”
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处的电报说：“亚洲及太平洋区域和全世界的人民，都坚决反对原子武器，并支持曾亲身遭受原子弹轰炸和氢弹试验灾害的日本人民所进行的反对原子武器的正义斗争。我们相信，这次大会的召开，必将对世界各国人民要求禁止原子武器和消除原子战争威胁从而确保亚洲和世界和平的运动作出很大贡献。”
