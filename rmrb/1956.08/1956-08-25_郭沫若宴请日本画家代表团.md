Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Japanese Painter Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th - Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet tonight for the Japanese painter delegation, led by Hogshun Yamaguchi, who are in China to attend the memorial meeting for the 450th anniversary of the death of world cultural figure Sesshu Toyo.

Present as guests were leading members of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, as well as well-known figures from Chinese cultural and artistic circles.

Also attending the banquet were Japanese painters Toshiko Akamatsu, Iri Maruki, and Hidenosuke Omura, advisor to the Japan Independent Film Company. Japanese peace activists in Beijing, Togo Kameda and Kenta Kaneko, were also invited to the banquet.

### 郭沫若宴请日本画家代表团

1956-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若宴请日本画家代表团
    新华社24日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今晚宴请前来我国参加世界文化名人雪舟等杨逝世四百五十周年纪念会的以山口篷春为首的日本画家代表团。
    出席作陪的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民对外文化协会的负责人和我国文化艺术界著名人士等。
    参加宴会的还有日本画家赤松俊子、丸木位里和日本独立影片公司顾问大村英之助。在京的日本和平人士龟田东伍和金子健太也应邀参加了宴会。
