Title: Central Institute for Nationalities Promotes a Batch of Minority Nationality Lecturers
Author:
Date: 1956-08-02
Page: 7

    This newspaper reports:

    The Central Institute for Nationalities recently promoted a group of assistant teachers and teachers from minority nationalities to the position of lecturer. These personnel include members of seven ethnic groups, including Mongolian, Tibetan, Miao, Zhuang, Buyi, and Naxi, totaling nine individuals. These comrades have demonstrated good performance in their studies and work. In particular, several of these comrades did not receive higher education, but under the cultivation and education of the Party, and through diligent study and work, they have greatly improved their scientific and cultural level. Now they are able to independently undertake the teaching work of their respective specialties. (Central Institute for Nationalities Journal Editorial Office)

### 中央民族学院提拔一批少数民族讲师

1956-08-02
第7版()
专栏：

    中央民族学院提拔一批少数民族讲师
    本报讯　最近中央民族学院提拔了一批少数民族出身的助教和教员为讲师。这些人员中包括蒙古、藏、苗、僮、布依、纳西等七个民族成份，共有九位。这些同志在学习和工作上表现良好。特别其中有几位同志，没受过高等学校教育，但几年来在党的培养教育下，经过刻苦学习和工作的锻炼，大大地提高了科学文化水平，现在他们已经能够独立担任专业的教学工作了。　（中央民族学院院刊编辑室）
