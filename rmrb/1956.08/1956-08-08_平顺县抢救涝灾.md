Title: PingShun County Rescues from Flood Disaster
Author:
Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 3

    PingShun County Rescues from Flood Disaster
    (Our newspaper's Taiyuan dispatch, 5th) Shanxi Province’s PingShun County has recently suffered from a serious flood disaster. From the 1st to the 4th, rainfall reached 187 millimeters, the most and heaviest rain in more than ten years. According to incomplete statistics from nine villages including Hongtiguan and Xingcheng in PingShun County, a total of 235 houses collapsed, more than 450 houses had severe leaks and were in danger of collapsing; 200 mu of land were washed away, and 400 mu of crops were submerged.
    After learning of this news, the PingShun County Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the PingShun County People's Committee immediately sent cadres with relief funds to Hongtiguan and Xingcheng townships, which were severely affected by the disaster, to express their condolences. Now the farmers of PingShun County are working hard to help seedlings and rescue from the disaster.

### 平顺县抢救涝灾

1956-08-08
第3版()
专栏：

    平顺县抢救涝灾
    本报太原5日电　山西省平顺县近日遭受了严重的涝灾。一日至四日降雨量达187公厘，这是十多年来最多最猛的一次大雨。仅据平顺县虹梯关和杏城等九个村的不完全统计，一共倒塌房子二百三十五间，有四百五十多间房子漏雨很厉害，有倒塌的危险；冲没土地二百亩，淹没庄稼四百亩。
    中共平顺县委会和平顺县人民委员会知道这个消息后，马上派干部携带救济款，分赴受灾严重的虹梯关和杏城等乡，进行慰问。现在平顺县农民正在紧张地进行扶苗救灾工作。
