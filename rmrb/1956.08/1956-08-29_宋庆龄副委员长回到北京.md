Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Soong Ching Ling, returned to Beijing by special plane this afternoon from Kunming after her visit to Indonesia at the invitation of the Indonesian government. After disembarking, Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling received flowers from children and shook hands with those who came to welcome her.

Those who welcomed her at the airport today included Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lin Boqu, Guo Moruo, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun and Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Cheng Qian and Long Yun, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, and Shi Liang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress in Beijing, as well as heads of various departments of state organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations, and more than a thousand members of the public.

The Ambassador of Burma to China, U Hla Maung, personnel from the Indonesian Embassy in China, and diplomatic envoys, chargés d'affaires, and commercial representatives from other countries also came to the airport to welcome her.

The Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wirjopranoto, who had specially traveled to Kunming to greet Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling, also returned to Beijing on the same plane. (Picture attached)

Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, was warmly welcomed at the airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Qingrui

### 宋庆龄副委员长回到北京

1956-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副委员长回到北京
    新华社28日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄应邀访问了印度尼西亚后，在今天下午由昆明乘专机回到北京。宋庆龄副委员长下飞机后，接受了儿童的献花，并同欢迎的人们一一握手。
    今天到机场欢迎的，有国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、郭沫若、李维汉、陈叔通，国务院副总理陈云、陈毅，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国防委员会副主席程潜、龙云，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、李德全、史良，外交部副部长章汉夫，北京市副市长吴晗，全国人民代表大会常务委员会在北京的委员以及国家机关各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人和一千多群众。
    缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂，印度尼西亚驻中国大使馆人员和其他各国外交使节、代办、商务代表等也到机场欢迎。
    特地前往昆明迎接宋庆龄副委员长的印度尼西亚驻中国大使维约普拉诺托也同机返回北京。（附图片）
    宋庆龄副委员长在机场受到热烈欢迎。　新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
