Title: Tao Menghe Hosts Banquet for Italian Agricultural Biologists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: Tao Menghe, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Italian agricultural biologists delegation headed by Carlo Jucci, member of the Italian Christian Democratic Party and professor of agricultural genetics at the University of Pavia.

At the beginning of the banquet, Tao Menghe, on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, extended a warm welcome to the Italian agricultural scientists delegation. He said that after realizing agricultural cooperation, China is now actively carrying out agricultural scientific research and agricultural technology reform. Therefore, it highly values and welcomes the rich experience in agricultural science brought by Italian friends.

The head of the delegation, Carlo Jucci, expressed his gratitude to the Chinese hosts for their hospitality. He said that they are not only interested in China's experience in agricultural production and new achievements in agricultural scientific research, but also very interested in China's agricultural cooperation and other issues. He believes that strengthening contacts between scientists from various countries can not only exchange scientific experience, but also have great significance in enhancing the unity and cooperation between the people of all countries and in consolidating and defending world peace.

### 陶孟和欢宴意大利农业生物学家代表团

1956-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    陶孟和欢宴意大利农业生物学家代表团
    据新华社21日讯　中国科学院副院长陶孟和今晚设宴，欢迎以意大利天主教民主党党员、巴维亚大学农业遗传学教授卡洛·尤契为首的意大利农业生物学家代表团。
    宴会开始时，陶孟和代表中国科学院和中国对外文化协会对意大利农业科学家代表团表示热烈欢迎。他说，中国在实现了农业合作化以后，现正积极进行农业科学研究工作和农业技术的改革。因此，非常珍视和欢迎意大利朋友们带来的在农业科学方面的丰富经验。
    代表团团长卡洛·尤契对中国主人的盛情接待表示感谢。他说，他们不但对中国的农业生产的经验和农业科学研究工作的新成就感到兴趣，同时对中国的农业合作化问题和其他方面的问题也很感到兴趣。他认为加强各国科学家之间的联系，不仅可以交流科学方面的经验，在增进各国人民之间的团结和合作、对巩固和保卫世界和平都有很大意义。
