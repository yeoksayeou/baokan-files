Title: China's Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Finish Competition in East Germany
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 11th: China's men's and women's volleyball teams have concluded their competition in the German Democratic Republic and flew to Moscow this morning.

　　In the past few days, the Chinese men's and women's volleyball teams continued to play four games with the national men's and women's volleyball teams of the German Democratic Republic. The Chinese team won one game and lost three.

### 我国男女排球队在民主德国比赛结束

1956-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我国男女排球队在民主德国比赛结束
    据新华社柏林11日电　中国男女排球队已经结束了在德意志民主共和国的比赛，在今天早晨飞往莫斯科。
    这几天来，中国男女排球队继续同民主德国的国家男女排球队进行了四场比赛。中国队胜一场，输三场。
