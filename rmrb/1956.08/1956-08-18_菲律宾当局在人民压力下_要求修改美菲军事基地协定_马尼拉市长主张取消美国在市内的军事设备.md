Title: Under Pressure from the People, Philippine Authorities Demand Revision of the US-Philippine Military Base Agreement. Mayor of Manila Advocates for the Removal of US Military Facilities within the City.
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, August 16th: The Philippines formally requested the United States to revise the 1947 US-Philippine Military Base Agreement on August 15th.

To discuss the revision of the US-Philippine Military Base Agreement, the United States and the Philippines began negotiations in Manila on August 11th. The chief Philippine representative to these negotiations, Senator Pelaez, stated at the negotiating meeting on August 15th that the Philippine and US governments should reach a formal understanding that either country could immediately request the termination of the agreement when a base is no longer needed.

Pelaez reiterated at the meeting that the Philippines is friendly to the United States and reaffirmed its rejection of neutralism, but he demanded that the United States "openly express respect for our sovereignty so that no one can doubt our relationship."

In this regard, Pelaez put forward four demands: First, the establishment of a permanent Philippine-US Defense Committee to ensure coordinated development and planning of joint defense; second, consultation on methods for using the bases in wartime; third, the application of Philippine law in US military bases and recognition of Philippine sovereignty over these bases; fourth, the return of the eleven unneeded bases immediately.

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, August 16th: According to the "Manila Chronicle" on August 9th, Manila Mayor Arsenio Lacson requested the removal of US military facilities within Manila on August 7th.

In a letter to Philippine Vice President and Foreign Minister Garcia, Lacson said that Manila is the "heart and nerve center of the entire country," and in the event of war, the US bases here would become "major military targets."

He asked Garcia to note the fact that Manila is surrounded by US bases such as Sangley Point in Cavite, Cubi Point in Zambales, Bataan and Subic, and Clark Air Force Base in Pampanga.

Lacson also pointed out that the United States seems to be expanding its military port facilities within Manila itself. He added that another base is about to be re-established in Las Piñas, Rizal.

Lacson said that it is unimaginable that the people of Manila would not "look at these (US) bases with fear and panic."

### 菲律宾当局在人民压力下  要求修改美菲军事基地协定  马尼拉市长主张取消美国在市内的军事设备

1956-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    菲律宾当局在人民压力下
    要求修改美菲军事基地协定
    马尼拉市长主张取消美国在市内的军事设备
    据新华社16日讯　马尼拉消息：菲律宾在八月十五日正式向美国提出修改1947年签订的美菲军事基地协定的要求。
    为了讨论修改美菲军事基地协定，美国和菲律宾已在八月十一日开始在马尼拉举行谈判。参加这个谈判的菲律宾首席代表、参议员佩拉埃斯在八月十五日的谈判会议上说，菲律宾和美国政府应该达成正式的谅解，那就是在基地一不需要的时候，无论那个国家都可以马上要求终止协定。
    佩拉埃斯在会上重申菲律宾对美国是友好的，并且重申拒绝中立主义，但是他要求美国“能公开表示对我国主权的尊重，使没有人能够怀疑我们的关系”。
    在这方面佩拉埃斯提出四点要求：一、建立一个常设的菲美防御委员会来保证协调地发展和计划共同防御；二、商议关于战时使用基地的办法；三、在美国的军事基地应用菲律宾的法律，并且承认菲律宾对这些基地的主权；四、现在就归还不需要的十一个基地。
    新华社16日讯　马尼拉消息：据八月九日的“马尼拉纪事报”报道，马尼拉市长阿塞尼奥·拉克森在八月七日要求取消美国在马尼拉市内的军事设备。
    拉克森在给菲律宾副总统兼外交部长加西亚的信中说，马尼拉是“整个国家的心脏和神经中枢”，万一发生战争，美国在这里的基地将成为“主要的军事目标”。
    他要求加西亚注意这样的事实：马尼拉是被这样一些美国基地所包围着：甲米地的桑莱岬、三描礼示的库比岬、巴丹和苏比克以及邦邦牙的克拉克空军基地。
    拉克森还指出，就在马尼拉市内，美国似乎也在扩充它的军事港口设备。他又说，不久就要在黎刹的拉示彬迎示重新恢复另一个基地。
    拉克森说，不可想像，马尼拉人民不会“怀着恐惧和惊慌来看这些（美国的）基地”。
