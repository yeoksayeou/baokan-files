Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Three Visiting Delegations
Author:
Date: 1956-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received the entire members of the Tibetan Women's Visiting Delegation, the Northwest Branch Visiting Delegation of the Central School of Political Science and Law Cadres, and the Visiting Delegation of Various Nationalities of Guangxi Province this evening.

During the reception, the Tibetan Women's Visiting Delegation presented Hadas to Chairman Mao Zedong and others.

Also present at the reception were Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Deng Xiaoping and Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Weihan and Chen Shutong, Director of the Chairman's Office Zhang Jingwu, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court Zhang Zhirang, Vice Minister of Justice Zheng Shaowen, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Cai Chang, Vice President Deng Yingchao, and Vice Chairmen of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Wang Feng, Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, and Yang Jingren.

### 毛泽东主席接见三个参观团

1956-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见三个参观团
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席在今天晚间接见了西藏妇女参观团、中央政法干部学校西北分校参观团和广西省各民族参观团的全体人员。
    接见的时候，西藏妇女参观团向毛泽东主席等献了哈达。
    接见的时候在座的，有国务院总理周恩来，副总理邓小平、陈毅、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉、陈叔通，主席办公厅主任张经武，最高人民法院副院长张志让，司法部副部长郑绍文，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，副主席邓颖超，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春、萨空了、杨静仁。
