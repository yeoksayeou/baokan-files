Title: Egypt's Nationwide General Strike Protests London Conference; General Strikes and Business Closures Held in Arab Countries from Tunisia to the Persian Gulf
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, August 16th dispatch from Cairo: The Egyptian people held a 24-hour general strike today to protest the London conference, unilaterally proposed by Britain, France, and the United States, to discuss the Suez Canal issue.
    At the same time, Arab countries from Tunisia to the Persian Gulf also held general strikes today to express support for Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company and to protest the London conference.
    The general strike in Egypt began after midnight last night. Today, except for the Suez Canal, which remains open to navigation as usual, and some public utilities that are not affected, all other industries have ceased work. Factories, shops, banks, cinemas, theaters, etc., are all closed. Even the shoe-shining boys participated in the strike. There are no trams, taxis, or carriages on the streets. Trains are all parked in the stations.
    According to reports from the capitals of various Arab countries, Syria, Lebanon, Jordan, Iraq, and other countries also held general strikes today. Demonstrations were also held in Damascus, Beirut, and Amman. The demonstrators held up placards with slogans such as "Down with Imperialism" and "The Canal Belongs to Egypt."
    Xinhua News Agency, August 16th dispatch from Tunis: Tunisia held a one-hour strike of businesses nationwide today from 9:00 to 10:00 local time, protesting the opening of the international conference on the Suez Canal issue initiated by the three Western powers in London.

### 埃及全国总罢工抗议伦敦会议  从突尼斯到波斯湾的阿拉伯各国都举行总罢工总罢市

1956-08-17
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及全国总罢工抗议伦敦会议
    从突尼斯到波斯湾的阿拉伯各国都举行总罢工总罢市
    据新华社16日讯　开罗消息：埃及全国人民今天举行二十四小时总罢工，抗议英、法、美三国片面建议召开的讨论苏彝士运河问题的伦敦会议。
    同时，从突尼斯直到波斯湾的各阿拉伯国家也在今天举行了总罢工，以表示支持埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有，并且抗议伦敦会议。
    埃及的总罢工是从昨晚午夜后开始的。今天除苏彝士运河仍然保持照常航行和一些公用事业不受影响外，其它一切行业全部停止工作。工厂、商店、银行、电影院、戏院等全部关上了门。擦鞋的孩子们也参加了罢工。街上没有电车，也没有出租汽车和马车。火车都停在车站里。
    据来自各阿拉伯国家首都的消息，叙利亚、黎巴嫩、约旦、伊拉克等国今天都举行了总罢工。在大马士革、贝鲁特和安曼，还举行了示威游行。示威的人高举的标语牌上写着“打倒帝国主义”、“运河属于埃及”等口号。
    据新华社16日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯全国在今天当地时间九点到十点罢市一小时，抗议西方三国发起的讨论苏彝士运河问题的国际会议在伦敦开幕。
