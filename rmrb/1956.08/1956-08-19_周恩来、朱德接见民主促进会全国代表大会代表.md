Title: Zhou Enlai and Zhu De Receive Representatives of the China Association for Promoting Democracy National Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: Zhou Enlai and Zhu De, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received all the representatives attending the Second National Congress of the China Association for Promoting Democracy this afternoon, and held talks with the members of the Presidium of the Congress.

### 周恩来、朱德接见民主促进会全国代表大会代表

1956-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来、朱德接见民主促进会全国代表大会代表
    新华社18日讯　中共中央书记处书记周恩来、朱德，在今天下午接见了出席中国民主促进会第二次全国代表大会的全体代表，并且同大会主席团人员进行了谈话。
