Title: Lao Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma Arrives in Beijing; People of the Capital Warmly Welcome Lao Delegation at Airport
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th – Lao Kingdom Prime Minister Prince Souvanna Phouma stated upon arriving in Beijing today: The Lao Kingdom adheres to a strict policy of neutrality and will not participate in military alliances.

The Prime Minister also said: Laos will never do anything that its neighbors might feel is a threat.

The Lao Kingdom government delegation, headed by Prince Souvanna Phouma, was warmly welcomed today by Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai and over seven thousand people. When Prince Souvanna Phouma made these remarks at the airport, the welcoming crowd applauded for a long time and enthusiastically, expressing their welcome for the Lao Kingdom government's implementation of a neutral policy.

Prince Souvanna Phouma and his delegation are in China as state guests of the Chinese government for a friendly visit. Shortly before coming to China, he met with Prince Souphanouvong, representative of the Lao Patriotic Front, in Vientiane and reached an agreement on the peaceful reunification of the country, and announced that he would pursue a policy of peace and neutrality.

A joyful atmosphere of peace filled the Beijing airport. The welcoming crowd excitedly jumped and shouted friendly greetings to the distinguished guests. Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma was constantly surrounded by flowers and covered with colorful paper scraps, smiling and frequently greeting the enthusiastic crowd with a "sampai."

The special plane carrying Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma and the delegation members arrived in Beijing at 3:30 PM. Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma was the first to disembark. He is fifty-one years old this year and studied engineering in France in his early years. Following him were the members of the delegation. They were warmly welcomed by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Peng Dehuai, He Long, Chen Yi, and Li Xiannian, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, and others.

After the military band played the national anthems of Laos and China, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, reviewed the honor guard lined up at the airport.

Then, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma and the members of the delegation met and shook hands with the heads of various departments of Chinese state organs, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, people from all walks of life, and diplomats from various countries who had come to welcome them.

Today, the ambassadors of Poland, Czechoslovakia, India, Afghanistan, Romania, Indonesia, and Albania, the charge d'affaires of the Netherlands and the United Kingdom, the charge d'affaires ad interim of Bulgaria, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Soviet Union, Finland, Yugoslavia, Mongolia, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the German Democratic Republic, Burma, and Switzerland, the commercial representative of Egypt, and the head of the Cambodian economic delegation all came to the airport to welcome them.

At 4:05 PM, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, boarded a car to go to the guesthouse to rest.

Today, the national flags of Laos and China were hung at the Beijing West Suburb Airport, and a sign spanning the entrance to the airport runway read: "Welcome the Lao Kingdom Government Delegation Headed by His Highness Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Lao Kingdom."

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 20th – Lao Kingdom Prime Minister Prince Souvanna Phouma and the Lao Kingdom government delegation he led left Guangzhou for Beijing by special plane at 8:00 AM this morning. Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, accompanied the distinguished guests to Beijing.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, and responsible persons of the Guangdong Province and Guangzhou municipal party and government organs, representatives of various democratic parties and people's organizations, etc.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, August 20th – Lao Kingdom Prime Minister Prince Souvanna Phouma and the Lao Kingdom government delegation he led, who were invited to visit China, arrived in Wuhan by special plane at 10:40 AM this morning and made a brief stop.

After Prime Minister Prince Souvanna Phouma and the members of the delegation of the Lao Kingdom disembarked from the plane, they were welcomed by Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Li Minghao, Deputy Governor, Wang Kewen, Deputy Mayor of Wuhan, Major General Zhang Guangcai, head of the Wuhan garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Zhou Jie, Vice Chairman of the Hubei Provincial Political Consultative Conference, and others. The hosts accompanied the distinguished guests to the waiting room to rest. Governor Zhang Tixue also had a cordial conversation with Prince Souvanna Phouma.

After the distinguished guests had lunch, they boarded the plane at 12:00 noon, accompanied by Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, to go to Beijing.

### 老挝首相梭发那·富马到京  首都各界人民在机场热烈欢迎老挝代表团

1956-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    老挝首相梭发那·富马到京
    首都各界人民在机场热烈欢迎老挝代表团
    新华社20日讯　老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王今天到达北京时说：老挝王国奉行着严格的中立政策，并且将不参加军事同盟。
    这位首相还说：老挝决不作任何足以被它的邻国们可能感到是一种威胁的事。
    以梭发那·富马亲王为首的老挝王国政府代表团，今天受到了中华人民共和国总理周恩来和七千多群众的欢迎。当梭发那·富马亲王在机场上讲述这番话的时候，欢迎群众长久地、热烈地鼓掌，对老挝王国政府执行中立政策表示欢迎。
    梭发那·富马亲王和他所率领的代表团，是作为中国政府的国宾来中国进行友好访问的。他在来中国以前不久，在万象同寮国战斗部队代表苏发努冯会谈达成了和平统一国家的协议，还宣布将奉行和平中立政策。
    北京机场上充满了和平欢乐的空气。欢迎群众激动地跳跃着，向贵宾们发出友谊的欢呼。梭发那·富马首相一直被鲜花包围着，全身被撒满彩色的纸片，他微笑着向热情的群众频频合十致意。
    梭发那·富马首相和代表团团员乘坐的专机是在下午三时半到达北京的。梭发那·富马首相第一个走下飞机。他今年五十一岁，早年在法国学过工程。在他的后面是代表团团员。他们受到了周恩来总理和副总理彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅、李先念，副委员长李济深、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，外交部副部长张闻天等人的热情欢迎。
    在军乐队奏老挝和中国两国国歌以后，梭发那·富马首相由周恩来总理陪同检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    接着，梭发那·富马首相和代表团团员同前来欢迎的中国国家机关各部门负责人、中国人民解放军的将军们、社会各界人士以及各国的外交官们，一一会见握手。
    今天，波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、印度、阿富汗、罗马尼亚、印度尼西亚和阿尔巴尼亚的大使，荷兰和英国的代办，保加利亚、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、苏联、芬兰、南斯拉夫、蒙古、越南民主共和国、德意志民主共和国、缅甸和瑞士的临时代办，埃及的商务代表以及柬埔寨经济代表团团长都到机场欢迎。
    四点零五分，梭发那·富马首相由周恩来总理陪同登上汽车赴迎宾馆休息。
    今天，北京西郊机场上悬挂着老挝和中国的国旗，横贯机场跑道入口处的一座牌坊上写着：“欢迎以老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王殿下为首的老挝王国政府代表团。”
    新华社广州20日电　老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王和他所率领的老挝王国政府代表团，今晨八时乘专机离开广州赴北京。中华人民共和国外交部礼宾司代司长王倬如陪同贵宾赴北京。
    到机场欢送的有，广东省省长陶铸、广州市市长朱光、中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长文年生中将，以及广东省、广州市党政机关负责人、各民主党派、各人民团体代表等。
    新华社武汉20日电　应邀来中国访问的老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王和他率领的老挝王国政府代表团，今日上午十时四十分乘专机到达武汉后，做了短时间的停留。
    老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王和他率领的代表团人员下飞机后，受到湖北省省长张体学、副省长李明灏、武汉市副市长王克文、中国人民解放军武汉驻军首长张广才少将和湖北省政治协商委员会副主席周杰等的欢迎。主人陪同贵宾们到候机室内休息。张体学省长还和梭发那·富马亲王做了亲切的交谈。
    贵宾们用过午餐以后，在中午十二时由外交部礼宾司代司长王倬如陪同登上飞机赴北京。（附图片）
