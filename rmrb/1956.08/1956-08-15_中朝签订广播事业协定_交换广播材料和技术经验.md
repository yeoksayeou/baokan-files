Title: China and North Korea Sign Broadcasting Agreement to Exchange Broadcasting Materials and Technical Expertise
Author:
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 5

    China and North Korea Sign Broadcasting Agreement
    Exchange Broadcasting Materials and Technical Expertise
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 14th - The Chinese Broadcasting Affairs Bureau and the Korean Central Broadcasting Committee signed a broadcasting cooperation agreement here on the 13th.
    The agreement stipulates the mutual exchange of materials on broadcasting and experience in broadcasting technology.

### 中朝签订广播事业协定  交换广播材料和技术经验

1956-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

    中朝签订广播事业协定
    交换广播材料和技术经验
    新华社平壤14日电　中国广播事业局和朝鲜中央广播委员会十三日在这里签订了一项广播事业合作协定。
    协定规定互相交换关于广播的材料和广播技术方面的经验。
