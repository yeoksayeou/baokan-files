Title: Chinese Volleyball Teams Win Consecutive Victories in East Germany
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 4

    Sports News

    Chinese Volleyball Teams Win Consecutive Victories in East Germany

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 3rd: The Chinese men's and women's volleyball teams played against local volleyball teams in Dresden and Leipzig in the German Democratic Republic. The Chinese teams won every game.

    The results of the game in Dresden on July 31st were: the Chinese women's team defeated the Leipzig Printing Industry Sports Club team three to two. The Chinese men's team defeated the Dresden Technical College Science team three to one. The results of the game in Leipzig on August 1st were: the Chinese women's team defeated the Halle Science Sports Club team three to one, and the Chinese men's team defeated the German Sports College Science Sports Club team three to one.

### 中国排球队在民主德国连日比赛场场得胜

1956-08-05
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中国排球队在民主德国
    连日比赛场场得胜
    据新华社柏林3日电　中国男女排球队在德累斯顿和来比锡同民主德国的地方排球队进行了比赛，中国队每场都得胜。
    七月三十一日在德累斯顿比赛的结果，中国女子队以三比二胜来比锡印刷业运动俱乐部队。中国男子队以三比一胜德累斯顿技术学院科学队。八月一日在来比锡比赛的结果，中国女子队以三比一胜哈雷科学运动俱乐部队，中国男子队以三比一胜德国体育学院科学运动俱乐部队。
