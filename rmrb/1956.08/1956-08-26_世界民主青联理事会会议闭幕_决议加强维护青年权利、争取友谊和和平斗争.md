Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Council Meeting Closes; Resolution to Strengthen Struggle to Defend Youth Rights, Seek Friendship and Peace
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: According to a TASS report: The World Federation of Democratic Youth Council meeting closed on August 23rd in Sofia.

    The meeting unanimously passed a resolution on the activities of youth in defending their own rights and striving for friendship and peace. The resolution stated that the growing cooperation among the youth of all countries and the recent achievements of the people of all countries in the struggle for peace are noted. The Council emphasized that the struggle to ban atomic weapons testing, for disarmament, and to consolidate peace is one of the most important tasks of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.
    Regarding the convening of the Fourth World Youth Congress, the meeting unanimously adopted recommendations on the qualifications for participating in the congress and decided that the congress would be held after the Sixth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship.
    The meeting also unanimously adopted a resolution to celebrate the Day of Solidarity of Youth Against Colonialism and for Peaceful Cooperation every year on April 24th.
    New members of the Executive Committee and Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth were also elected at the meeting.

### 世界民主青联理事会会议闭幕  决议加强维护青年权利、争取友谊和和平斗争

1956-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    世界民主青联理事会会议闭幕
    决议加强维护青年权利、争取友谊和和平斗争
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社报道：世界民主青年联盟理事会会议八月二十三日在索非亚闭幕。
    会议就青年维护自身权利、争取友谊和和平的活动问题一致通过了一项决议。决议说，各国青年正在不断发展的合作和各国人民最近在争取和平的斗争中获得了成就。理事会着重指出，为禁止原子武器的试验、为裁军和巩固和平而斗争，是世界民主青年联盟最重要的任务之一。
    关于召开第四届世界青年大会的问题，会议一致通过了关于参加这次大会的资格的建议，并且决定这次大会将在第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节以后召开。
    会上还一致通过了关于每年四月二十四日庆祝青年反对殖民主义和争取和平合作的团结日的决议。
    这次会议中还选出了世界民主青年联盟执行委员会和书记处的新成员。
