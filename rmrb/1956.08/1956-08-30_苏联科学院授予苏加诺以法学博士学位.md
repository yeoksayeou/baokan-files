Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Awards Sukarno Doctorate of Law

Author:

Date: 1956-08-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 29th: The Department of Economics, Philosophy and Law of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has passed a resolution to award Indonesian President Sukarno the degree of Doctor of Law.

At the general assembly discussing the awarding of the doctorate to Sukarno, Academician Mitin of the Soviet Academy of Sciences gave a report on the significance of President Sukarno's important works and speeches. He said that President Sukarno is a firm and unyielding anti-colonialist fighter, a consistent warrior for freedom and national independence for his motherland, and a prominent statesman and political activist of the Republic of Indonesia. His entire activities over the years, his words, speeches, and papers allow one to understand the various basic stages of the Indonesian people's national liberation struggle.

Academician Mitin pointed out that Sukarno's political speeches and works have a high academic level, he has a profound understanding of philosophy, history, and law, understands the various forms of the people's struggle against oppression, and is familiar with the history of national liberation movements in Europe, the Americas, and Asian countries.

Others who spoke at the meeting supported the proposal to award the degree.

### 苏联科学院授予苏加诺以法学博士学位

1956-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院授予苏加诺以法学博士学位
    据新华社29日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联科学院经济、哲学和法学部通过决议，决定授给印度尼西亚总统苏加诺以法学博士学位。
    在大会讨论授予苏加诺博士学位的时候，苏联科学院米丁院士作了关于苏加诺总统的重要著作和演说的意义的报告。他说，苏加诺总统是坚定顽强的反殖民主义战士，是为祖国争取自由和民族独立的始终如一的战士，是印度尼西亚共和国杰出的国务和政治活动家。他多年来的全部活动、他的言论、演说和论文令人能够了解印度尼西亚人民民族解放斗争的各个基本阶段。
    米丁院士指出，苏加诺的政治演说和作品具有高度的学术水平，他在哲学、历史和法律方面有深湛的认识，了解人民群众反抗压迫的斗争的各种形式，通晓欧洲、美洲和亚洲国家的民族解放运动的历史。
    其他在会上发言的人都支持授予学位的建议。
