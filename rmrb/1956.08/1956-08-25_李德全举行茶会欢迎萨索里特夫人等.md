Title: Li Dequan Holds Tea Party to Welcome Madame Sopsaisana and Others
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: Li Dequan, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, held a tea party this afternoon to welcome the wives of the distinguished guests from the Kingdom of Laos: Madame Khampheng Tounalom, wife of Deputy Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith; Madame Oun Hueun Phounsavath, wife of Minister of Finance, National Economy, and Planning Leuam Insisienmay; Madame Tao Songsanith, wife of Undersecretary of the Ministry of Interior and Social Welfare; Madame Sunthorn Pathammavong, wife of the Chief of the General Staff of the Armed Forces; and Lieutenant Sunthala Don Sasorith.

    Li Dequan, on behalf of the All-China Women's Federation, extended a warm welcome to the distinguished guests. She said, "We hope that the esteemed ladies will convey the deep sentiments of Chinese women to our Lao sisters. We hope to learn more about the lives and work of Lao sisters. We also look forward to meeting and talking with Lao sisters in your beautiful country."

    Madame Khampheng Tounalom, wife of Deputy Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith, said, "We are very grateful for your kind hospitality. We will bring these deep sentiments back to Laos and share our joy with our Lao sisters."

    The Deputy Prime Minister's wife also said, "We hope that our Chinese sisters will come to Laos. We hope that Chinese and Lao sisters will have more contact and more understanding in order to increase our friendship."

    The distinguished guests presented Vice-President Li Dequan with silver flowerpots and "silk ribbons." The All-China Women's Federation presented the distinguished guests with "Chinese Children" picture albums.

    Also present at the tea party were Luo Shuzhang, Vice Minister of Labor; Pu Jiexiu, Vice-Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation; Zhang Qian, wife of Vice Premier Chen Yi; Fu Ya, wife of General Chen Geng; and representatives of women from all walks of life in Beijing.

### 李德全举行茶会欢迎萨索里特夫人等

1956-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

    李德全举行茶会欢迎萨索里特夫人等
    据新华社24日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全今天下午举行茶会，欢迎老挝王国贵宾们的夫人，她们是副首相卡代·敦·萨索里特夫人，财政、国家经济和计划部部长仑·英锡相迈夫人，内务和社会福利部次长陶·宋沙尼特夫人，武装部队总参谋长顺吞·巴探马冯夫人，中尉顺塔拉·敦·萨索里特夫人。
    李德全代表全国妇联向贵宾们表示热烈的欢迎。她说：“希望各位夫人将中国妇女对老挝姐妹们深厚的情意带回给她们，我们希望更多地了解老挝姐妹们的生活和工作情况。我们也期望在你们美丽的国家和老挝姐妹们会晤谈心。”
    副首相卡代·敦·萨索里特夫人说：“我们非常感谢诸位对我们这样好的招待，我们将把这些深厚的情谊带回老挝去，让老挝的姐妹们分享我们的喜悦。
    副首相夫人还说：“希望中国的姐妹们将来到老挝去，希望中国和老挝的姐妹们更多地接触，更多地了解，以增加我们的友谊。”
    贵宾们向李德全副主席赠送了银花盆和“绸带”。全国妇联送给了贵宾们“中国儿童”画册。
    出席茶会的还有，劳动部副部长罗叔章，北京市民主妇女联合会副主席浦洁修，陈毅副总理夫人张茜，陈赓大将夫人傅涯，和北京市各界妇女的代表。
