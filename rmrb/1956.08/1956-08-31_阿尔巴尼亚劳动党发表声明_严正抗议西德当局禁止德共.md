Title: Albanian Labor Party Issues Statement Strongly Protesting West German Authorities' Ban on the German Communist Party
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 30th: According to a report by the Albanian Telegraph Agency on August 28th, the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party issued a statement, on behalf of the entire party and the people of the country, strongly protesting the West German Federal Constitutional Court's verdict banning the German Communist Party.

The statement said that this not only infringed upon the democratic freedoms of the German people and their efforts to strive for a unified and democratic Germany, but also was detrimental to world peace and security.

The statement expressed sympathy for the West German Communist Party and supported the German people's struggle for democracy, freedom, and rights, for a unified and democratic Germany, and for the defense and consolidation of world peace.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党发表声明  严正抗议西德当局禁止德共

1956-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党发表声明
    严正抗议西德当局禁止德共
    据新华社布拉格30日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社28日报道，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会发表声明，代表全党和全国人民严正抗议西德联邦宪法法院关于禁止德国共产党的判决。
    声明说，这不仅侵犯德国人民的民主自由和他们争取一个统一的、民主的德国的努力，同时也是不利于世界和平和安全的。
    声明对西德共产党表示同情，并且支持德国人民进行的争取民主、自由和权利，争取一个统一的、民主的德国，保卫和巩固世界和平的斗争。
