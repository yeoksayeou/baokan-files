Title: Tianjin Water Level Falls
Author:
Date: 1956-08-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 11th. Today the water level of each river in Tianjin has tended to fall. At 6 p.m., the water level at Jintang Bridge in the city was 16 centimeters lower than yesterday, the water level at Xihe Bridge was 15 centimeters lower, and the water level at Beiyang Bridge was 14 centimeters lower. However, the Tianjin Flood Control Headquarters, in view of the fact that the amount of water coming from the upper reaches is still quite large, and the water storage areas such as Guanting Reservoir, Beiyangdian, and Qujiadian have reached saturation, continues to lead the people of the city to remain on alert.

### 天津水位下落

1956-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    天津水位下落
    据新华社天津11日电　今天天津市各河水位已趋下落。下午六时，市内金汤桥的水位比昨日下落十六公分，西河桥的水位下落十五公分，北洋桥的水位下落十四公分。但是，天津市防汛指挥部鉴于上游的来水量还相当大，官厅水库、北洋淀、屈家店等蓄水地区已达饱和状态，仍继续领导全市人民进行戒备。
