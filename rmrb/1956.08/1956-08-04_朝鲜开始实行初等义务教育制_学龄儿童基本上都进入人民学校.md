Title: Korea Begins Implementing Primary Compulsory Education; School-Age Children Basically Enter People's Schools
Author:
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 2nd. North Korea has begun implementing a primary compulsory education system in the new school year starting August 1st.
    School-age children from seven to eleven years old in various regions have basically entered People's Schools. The number of children enrolled this year has increased by more than 41,000 compared to last year. In the new school year, the number of students in People's Schools in various regions has reached 1.5 million, an increase of more than 200,000 compared to the war period and more than 600,000 compared to before liberation.
    In order to implement the primary compulsory education system, the Korean government newly built more than 50 schools and added more than 800 classrooms before the start of the new school year, and provided free textbooks and stationery to a small number of impoverished children.
    In order to cultivate the technical personnel needed for large-scale restoration and construction, the Korean government has newly established ten higher technical specialized schools and forty-five secondary technical specialized schools this year. The number of students enrolled in technical specialized schools this year has doubled compared to last year.
    In the new school year, a new law and medical university has also been established. There are now seventeen universities in North Korea, an increase of two compared to before the war. Among these universities, twelve were restored from pre-war institutions, and five were newly established after the war. The number of university students has increased by approximately 5,000 compared to before the war.

### 朝鲜开始实行初等义务教育制  学龄儿童基本上都进入人民学校

1956-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜开始实行初等义务教育制
    学龄儿童基本上都进入人民学校
    新华社平壤2日电　朝鲜北部在从八月一日开始的新学年里开始实行初等义务教育制。
    各地七岁到十一岁的学龄儿童已经基本上都进了人民学校。今年就学的儿童比去年增加了四万一千多人。在新学年里，各地人民学校的学生达一百五十万人，这比战争时期增加了二十多万人，比解放前增加了六十多万人。
    朝鲜政府为了实施初等义务教育制，在新学年开始前新建了五十多所学校，增加了八百多间教室，并且对少数贫苦的儿童免费供给教科书和文具。
    朝鲜政府为了培养大规模恢复建设所需要的技术人才，在今年新设立了十所高等技术专门学校和四十五所中等技术专门学校，今年技术专门学校招收的学生比去年增加了一倍。
    在新的学年中，还新设立了一所法律医科大学。现在朝鲜北部已经有十七所大学，比战前增加了两所。在这些大学中，有十二所是恢复战前原有的，有五所是在战后新设立的。大学生的数目比战前增加了约五千人。
