Title: Ten Reclamation Stations in Heilongjiang Province Reclaim Over 120,000 Hectares

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 3

    Ten Reclamation Stations in Heilongjiang Province
    Reclaim Over 120,000 Hectares

    (Our Newspaper News) Heilongjiang Province's ten newly established tractor reclamation stations have, according to statistics up to the beginning of August, reclaimed 127,416 hectares of wasteland, of which over 90,000 hectares have been sown with crops such as soybeans, corn, and potatoes. The quality of reclamation is basically good, and the cost of reclamation has been reduced by more than 10% compared to last year.

    Recently, the Heilongjiang Provincial Department of Agriculture's Tractor Station Administration Bureau held a meeting of tractor station chiefs, which decided to complete the annual plan of reclaiming 270,000 hectares of wasteland for immigrants before the freeze-up.

### 黑龙江省十个垦荒站  开荒十二万多公顷

1956-08-20
第3版()
专栏：

    黑龙江省十个垦荒站
    开荒十二万多公顷
    本报讯　黑龙江省今年新建立的十个拖拉机垦荒站，到八月初统计，已经开垦出荒地达127，416公顷，其中有九万多公顷已播种大豆、玉米、马铃薯等作物。开荒质量基本良好，开荒成本较去年降低10％以上。
    最近黑龙江省农业厅拖拉机站管理局召开的拖拉机站站长会议决定，到封冻以前，完成全年为移民开荒二十七万公顷的计划。
