Title: Greece Refuses to Attend London Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Athens, August 12th: The Greek government issued a communique on August 11th, announcing its refusal to participate in the London conference on the Suez Canal issue.

    Political figures in Athens pointed out that Greece's refusal to participate in the conference is due to the sympathy for Egypt among the Greek public, the deterioration of British-Greek relations due to the Cyprus issue, and the Greek government's desire not to lose the support of Arab countries in the United Nations on the Cyprus issue.
    According to Western news agencies, the British and American sides expressed disappointment and regret over the Greek government's decision.

### 希腊不参加伦敦会议

1956-08-13
第5版()
专栏：

    希腊不参加伦敦会议
    新华社12日讯　雅典消息：希腊政府在八月十一日发表公报，宣布拒绝参加关于苏彝士运河问题的伦敦会议。
    雅典政界人士指出，希腊之所以拒绝参加会议，是因为目前在希腊舆论中间有着同情埃及的情绪和由于塞浦路斯问题而引起的英希两国关系的恶化，以及希腊政府不希望丧失阿拉伯国家在联合国中在塞浦路斯问题上对希腊的支持。
    据西方通讯社报道，英国和美国方面对希腊政府的这个决定表示失望和遗憾。
