Title: Laotian Royal Government Delegation Leaves Beijing; Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma and Laotian Delegation Visit and Tour Shanghai
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 26th - Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, and the Lao Royal Government Delegation, left Beijing by special plane on the morning of the 26th. The distinguished guests will visit Shanghai, Wuhan, and Guangzhou before returning home.

Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma delivered a farewell speech at the airport, saying: Your Excellency Premier, ladies and gentlemen:

	On behalf of the Lao Royal Government Delegation, I would like to express my gratitude to everyone today. This visit to China has further strengthened the inherent relationship between China and Laos. I hope that the friendly relations between the two countries will develop even further in the future, and that our two countries will maintain peace.

	Finally, let me once again express my gratitude to the entire Chinese people and pay the highest respect to Chairman Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people.

At 7:40 AM, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma and others, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, arrived at the Xijiao Airport, which was decorated with the national flags of Laos and China. Here, the distinguished guests shook hands with Peng Dehuai, He Long, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Cheng Qian, Long Yun, Zhang Wentian and others who came to see them off.

The band played the national anthems of Laos and China.

Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, reviewed the guard of honor.

Before boarding the plane, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma and others shook hands and bid farewell to the heads of various departments of the Chinese state organs, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations, people from all walks of life in the capital, and envoys, commercial representatives, and heads of economic delegations from various countries to China.

A group of female students in brightly colored costumes presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

As the plane was about to take off, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma, Deputy Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith, and others waved goodbye to the people who came to see them off from the plane window. At this moment, warm applause rang out at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 26th - Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma and the Lao Royal Government Delegation he led toured the Huangpu River today on a warship of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy.

When the distinguished guests arrived at the warship, Chinese naval officers and sailors warmly welcomed them on the deck. Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma reviewed the naval guard of honor. After the ship set sail, the Lao Prime Minister went to the bridge, held the railing, and while looking at the vast river, said to Xu Jianguo, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and Major General Su Qisheng, that the scenery here is very good, and the night view of Shanghai from the river must be even more beautiful. He also said that he has always liked traveling by boat.

On the way back to Shanghai by car, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma and others visited the Yangshupu Power Plant of the Shanghai Electric Power Administration. The Lao Prime Minister, who had studied electrical engineering in his early years, carefully learned about the production situation of the factory during the visit.

Madame Katay Don Sasorith and other members' wives visited the Children's Palace after leaving the warship.

The Lao Royal Government Delegation arrived in Shanghai at nearly 12:00 noon today. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Xu Jianguo, Liu Jiping, and Jin Zhonghua, Deputy Mayors of Shanghai, and 7,000 people from all walks of life. In the evening, Xu Jianguo held a grand banquet at the Cultural Club to welcome the distinguished guests. (See photo)

### 老挝王国政府代表团离京  梭发那·富马首相和老挝代表团到上海参观和游览

1956-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    老挝王国政府代表团离京
    梭发那·富马首相和老挝代表团到上海参观和游览
    新华社26日讯　老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王和老挝王国政府代表团，二十六日晨乘专机离开北京。贵宾们将在上海、武汉、广州参观后返国。
    梭发那·富马首相在机场发表告别讲话说：总理阁下、女士们、先生们：
    我以老挝王国政府代表团的名义，今天向大家表示感谢。我们这一次到中国来访问，使中国和老挝两国固有的关系更加加强起来了。我希望两国之间的友好关系，在未来将更加发展，我们两国之间将保持和平。
    最后，让我再一次向全中国人民表示感谢，向中国人民的领袖毛泽东主席表示最高的敬意。
    七时四十分，梭发那·富马首相等由周恩来总理陪同来到悬挂着老挝和中国两国国旗的西郊机场。在这里，贵宾们同前来送行的彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅、李先念、郭沫若、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通、程潜、龙云、张闻天等一一握手。
    乐队奏老挝和中国两国国歌。
    梭发那·富马首相由周恩来总理陪同检阅了仪仗队。
    在上飞机前，梭发那·富马首相等同前来送行的中国国家机关各部门的负责人、中国人民解放军的高级将领，各民主党派和人民团体的负责人和首都社会各界人士，以及各国驻中国的使节、商务代表和经济代表团团长等握手告别。
    一群穿着鲜艳服装的女学生向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    飞机就要起飞了，梭发那·富马首相、卡代·敦·萨索里特副首相等在机窗口，向送行的人们频频挥手告别。这时，机场上响起热烈的掌声。
    据新华社上海26日电　梭发那·富马首相和他所率领的老挝王国政府代表团，今天乘中国人民解放军海军的军舰游览了黄浦江。
    贵宾们到达军舰时，中国的海军军官和水兵们在甲板上热烈欢迎。梭发那·富马首相检阅了海军仪仗队。开航以后，这位老挝首相登上了舰桥扶着栏杆，一面眺望辽阔的江面，一面和上海市副市长许建国、苏启胜少将说，这里的景色很好，从江面上看上海的夜景一定更美。他还说，他一直就很喜欢乘船旅行。
    梭发那·富马首相等在乘汽车返回上海市区的途中，参观了上海电业管理局杨树浦发电厂。这位早年曾经攻读过电气工程的老挝首相在参观过程中，仔细地了解了这个工厂的生产情况。
    卡代·敦·萨索里特夫人和其他团员的夫人离开军舰以后，参观了少年宫。
    老挝王国政府代表团是在今天中午近十二时到达上海的。贵宾们在机场上受到上海市副市长许建国、刘季平、金仲华和各界人民七千人的热烈欢迎。晚上许建国在文化俱乐部里举行盛大宴会欢迎贵宾。（附图片）
    在飞机场上，周恩来总理和梭发那·富马首相握别。
