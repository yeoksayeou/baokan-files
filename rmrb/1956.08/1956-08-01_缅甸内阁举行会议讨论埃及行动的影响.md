Title: Burmese Cabinet Holds Meeting to Discuss Impact of Actions in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, August 31st, Rangoon dispatch: Burmese Prime Minister U Ba Swe held a meeting with cabinet ministers on July 28th to discuss the possible economic and political impact on Burma of Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal. The meeting passed a resolution, which has been submitted to the ruling Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League for consideration. Its content has not yet been announced.

### 缅甸内阁举行会议讨论埃及行动的影响

1956-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸内阁举行会议讨论埃及行动的影响
    新华社31日讯　仰光迟到消息：缅甸总理吴巴瑞七月二十八日和内阁部长们举行会议，讨论了埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有问题对缅甸可能发生的经济和政治的影响。会议通过了一个决定。这个决定已经提交执政党反法西斯人民自由同盟考虑，它的内容还没有宣布。
