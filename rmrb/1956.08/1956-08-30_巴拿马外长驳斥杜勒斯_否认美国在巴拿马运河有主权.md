Title: Panamanian Foreign Minister Refutes Dulles, Denies US Sovereignty Over Panama Canal
Author:
Date: 1956-08-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Panama City, August 29th: Panamanian Foreign Minister Alberto Boyd issued a special statement on the evening of August 28th refuting remarks made by Dulles at a press conference in Washington on the same day regarding the issue of sovereignty over the Panama Canal.

Boyd stated that he "disagrees" with Dulles' claim that the United States possesses sovereignty over the Panama Canal Zone. The statement pointed out: "What Panama ceded to the United States was strictly certain rights limited to the use of the canal, and nothing else. Regarding the statement that the Panama Canal is a national waterway of the United States, the Foreign Minister (Boyd) pointed out that the Panama Canal cannot become a national canal of the United States because it is built on Panamanian territory."

The statement said: "It is a waterway whose construction was made possible by Panama and the United States through a public treaty, and, in another public treaty, it is stipulated that the two countries have a common vested interest in this project."

### 巴拿马外长驳斥杜勒斯  否认美国在巴拿马运河有主权

1956-08-30
第6版()
专栏：

    巴拿马外长驳斥杜勒斯
    否认美国在巴拿马运河有主权
    据新华社29日讯　巴拿马城消息：巴拿马外交部长艾伯特·博伊德8月28日晚上发表了一个特别声明，驳斥杜勒斯同一天在华盛顿举行的记者招待会上提到巴拿马运河主权问题的时候所说的那番话。
    博伊德表示“不同意”杜勒斯说美国在巴拿马运河地区拥有主权。声明指出：“巴拿马让给美国的，是严格限于运河使用方面的某些权力，而没有其他任何东西。关于巴拿马运河是美国的一条国家水道的说法，外交部长（博伊德）指出，巴拿马运河不能成为美国的一条国家运河，因为它是建筑在巴拿马的领土之上的。”
    声明说：“它是这样的一条水道：它的建筑是由巴拿马和美国通过一个公共条约而使之成为可能的，而且，在另一个公共条约里还规定，在这个工程中，两个国家有着共同的切身利益。”
