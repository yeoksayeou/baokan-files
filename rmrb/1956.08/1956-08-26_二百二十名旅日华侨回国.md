Title: Two Hundred and Twenty Overseas Chinese Return from Japan
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, August 25th: Two hundred and twenty overseas Chinese and their families returned to Tianjin from Japan today aboard the "Xingan Maru" ship.

Among the overseas Chinese returning from Japan today are Dr. Chen Shanyan, a Doctor of Medicine, Dr. Liu Yuanzhang, a Doctor of Economics, and some students studying in Japan. Eleven overseas Chinese compatriots who had been imprisoned in the Japanese "Hamamatsu Detention Center" and suffered other forms of persecution also returned to their motherland.

### 二百二十名旅日华侨回国

1956-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    二百二十名旅日华侨回国
    据新华社天津25日电　二百二十名旅日华侨和他们的眷属今天乘“兴安丸”轮船从日本回到天津。
    在今天回国的旅日华侨中有医学博士陈善言、经济学博士刘源张和一部分留日学生。曾被日本“滨松收容所”关禁过和受过其他迫害的十一名侨胞也返回祖国。
