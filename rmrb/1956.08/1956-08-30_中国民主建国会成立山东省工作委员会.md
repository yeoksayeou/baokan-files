Title: China Democratic National Construction Association Establishes Shandong Provincial Working Committee
Author:
Date: 1956-08-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 29th. The Shandong Provincial Working Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association was formally established in Jinan City on the 27th. The Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association has appointed Miao Hainan as the Director of the Shandong Provincial Working Committee. The Shandong Provincial Working Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association will develop members in small and medium-sized cities such as Yantai and Zibo, and prepare for the establishment of local organizations of the China Democratic National Construction Association.

### 中国民主建国会成立山东省工作委员会

1956-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中国民主建国会成立山东省工作委员会
    新华社济南29日电　中国民主建国会山东省工作委员会于27日在济南市正式成立。中国民主建国会中央委员会指定苗海南为山东省工作委员会主任委员。中国民主建国会山东省工作委员会将在烟台、淄博等中小城市内发展会员，筹建民建地方组织。
