Title: According to the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Our Delegation Visits Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 5

    According to the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement
    Our Delegation Visits Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, August 25th. According to the "Sino-Vietnamese 1956 Cultural Cooperation Plan," the Chinese cultural, educational, and health workers delegation of eight people, led by head of the delegation Wei Que, left Beijing by train today for Vietnam.

### 根据中越文化合作协定  我代表团去越南访问

1956-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    根据中越文化合作协定
    我代表团去越南访问
    新华社25日讯　根据“中越1956年文化合作计划”赴越南访问的中国文化教育卫生工作者代表团一行八人，在韦悫团长的率领下，今天乘火车离北京赴越南。
