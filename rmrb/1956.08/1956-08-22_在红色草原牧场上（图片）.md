Author: Hu Wei
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 3

    On the Red Prairie Pasture
    The Red Prairie State-owned Ranch in Anda County, Heilongjiang Province, is a rich natural grassland pasture with a land area of 151,100 hectares. It is the largest comprehensive demonstration livestock farm in China. This year, in addition to producing more than 5,146,600 kilograms of milk and 23,000 kilograms of wool for the country, this ranch will also breed a large number of high-quality livestock breeds to supply rural needs.
        (Below) A flock of sheep on the pasture.
        (Right) Veterinary staff are carrying out quarantine on Caucasian sheep.
          Xinhua News Agency reporter Hu Wei

### 在红色草原牧场上（图片）
胡伟
1956-08-22
第3版()
专栏：

    在红色草原牧场上
    黑龙江省安达县国营红色草原牧场是一个富饶的天然草原牧场，它有十五万一千公顷的土地面积，是我国最大的综合性的示范畜牧场。今年这个牧场除为国家生产五百一十四万六千多公斤牛奶和二万三千公斤羊毛外，还要繁殖大批优良畜种供应农村需要。
        （下）牧场上的羊群。
        （右）兽医工作人员在对高加索羊进行检疫。
          新华社记者　胡伟摄
