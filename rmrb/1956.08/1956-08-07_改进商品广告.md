Title: Improving Commodity Advertisements
Author: Han Xinming
Date: 1956-08-07
Page: 8

Column: Reader, Author, Editor

    Improving Commodity Advertisements
    Commodity advertisements published in the People's Daily, especially those for daily consumer goods, should, as much as possible, introduce the performance, uses, and methods of using the commodities to consumers, so as to serve the masses. However, the commodity advertisements currently published often only list the names of the commodities, and readers will not find such advertisements interesting or satisfactory. (Han Xinming)

### 改进商品广告
韩新名
1956-08-07
第8版()
专栏：读者·作者·编者

    改进商品广告
    人民日报登载的商品广告，特别是日用消费品的广告，应该尽可能地向消费者介绍商品性能、用途、使用方法等等，达到为群众服务的目的。但是，现在所登的商品广告，往往只是列举商品的名称，读者对这种广告是不会感到兴趣和满足的。　（韩新名）
