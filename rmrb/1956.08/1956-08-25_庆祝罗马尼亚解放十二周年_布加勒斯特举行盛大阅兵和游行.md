Title: Grand Parade and Procession Held in Bucharest to Celebrate the Twelfth Anniversary of Romania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th -- According to a report by the Romanian News Agency: On August 23rd, Bucharest held a grand military parade and mass procession to celebrate the twelfth anniversary of Romania's liberation.

The parade and procession were held in Stalin Square. Standing on the reviewing stand of the square were Romanian Workers' Party and government leaders Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chivu Stoica, and others.

The Minister of the Romanian Armed Forces, Sălăjan, first reviewed the troops lined up on the square, and then went to the reviewing stand to deliver a speech celebrating the festival. In his speech, he congratulated the troops on the festival and talked about Romania's efforts to expand international cooperation, ease international tensions, and consolidate peace. He called on the working people of the whole country to continue to work hard to realize the Second Five-Year Plan.

After the speech, the march-past of the military parade began. Cadets from military academies and teams from various military branches marched past the reviewing stand in neat formations.

Then, the mass procession began. Workers, collective farmers, literary and art workers, children, youth and athletes marched through the square in a mighty stream. They marched forward, holding high flags and charts and cheering.

On the same day, festive parades were also held in Iasi, Stalin City, Ploiesti, Timișoara, Oradea, Hunedoara and other cities in Romania.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚解放十二周年  布加勒斯特举行盛大阅兵和游行

1956-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚解放十二周年
    布加勒斯特举行盛大阅兵和游行
    新华社24日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社报道：八月二十三日，布加勒斯特举行了庆祝罗马尼亚解放十二周年的盛大的阅兵和群众游行。
    阅兵和游行是在斯大林广场举行的。广场的检阅台上站着罗马尼亚工人党和政府领导人乔治乌—德治、斯托伊卡等。
    罗马尼亚武装部队部部长萨拉扬首先检阅了排列在广场上的部队，随后走上检阅台发表庆祝节日的演说。他在演说中向部队祝贺节日，并且谈到了罗马尼亚在为扩大国际合作、和缓国际紧张局势和巩固和平方面所作的努力。他号召全国劳动人民继续为实现第二个五年计划而努力。
    演说结束以后，阅兵的分列式开始。军事学院的学员、各兵种的队伍都以整齐的队形走过了检阅台。
    接着，群众游行开始。工人、集体农民、文艺工作者、儿童、青年和运动员的队伍浩浩荡荡地走过广场。他们高举着旗帜和图表，欢呼着前进。
    同一天，雅西、斯大林城、普罗什蒂、底密索拉、俄拉第亚、洪尼多阿拉和罗马尼亚其他一些城市也举行了节日游行。
