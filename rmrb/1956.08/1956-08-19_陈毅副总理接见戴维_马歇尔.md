Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives David Marshall
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th -- Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received David Marshall, Chairman of the Labour Front of Singapore and former Chief Minister of Singapore, this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Liao Chengzhi, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

### 陈毅副总理接见戴维·马歇尔

1956-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见戴维·马歇尔
    新华社18日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天下午接见了新加坡劳工阵线主席、前新加坡首席部长戴维·马歇尔。
    接见的时候，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员廖承志也在座。
