Title: Voroshilov Departs Finland for Home

Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 26th: Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and his entourage concluded their official visit to Finland today and left Helsinki at 10:00 AM local time to return to the Soviet Union.

President Kekkonen of Finland, members of parliament, and government officials headed by Prime Minister Fagerholm saw him off.

Xinhua News Agency, August 26th: TASS, Helsinki: Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, who is on a state visit to Finland, held a luncheon at the Soviet Embassy in Finland on August 25th to entertain Finnish President Kekkonen. Among those present at the banquet were Kekkonen and former President Paasikivi, Finnish Prime Minister Fagerholm, Foreign Minister Taugren, and other senior Finnish government officials.

On the same day, Kekkonen held a banquet for Voroshilov. The banquet proceeded in the same warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 伏罗希洛夫离芬兰返国

1956-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫离芬兰返国
    据新华社26日讯　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和他的随行人员今天结束了在芬兰的正式访问，在当地时间十时离开赫尔辛基返回苏联。
    芬兰总统吉科宁、议员们、以总理法格霍姆为首的政府人员前往送行。
    据新华社26日讯　据塔斯社赫尔辛基消息：正在芬兰进行国事访问的苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫8月25日在苏联驻芬兰大使馆举行午宴，招待芬兰总统吉科宁。出席宴会的有吉科宁和前任总统巴锡基维，芬兰总理法格霍姆，外交部长陶格伦及芬兰政府的其他高级官员。
    同一天，吉科宁为伏罗希洛夫举行了宴会。宴会在同样的热烈友好的气氛中进行。
