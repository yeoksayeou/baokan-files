Title: Nehru to Visit Saudi Arabia in September
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 26th - It was officially announced today that Indian Prime Minister Nehru will leave New Delhi on September 24 for a short visit to Saudi Arabia. He is scheduled to return to India on September 27.

When Saudi Arabian King Saud Abdul Aziz visited India last winter, he invited Prime Minister Nehru to visit Saudi Arabia. Nehru accepted the invitation at the time, but the date of the visit was not determined.

### 尼赫鲁九月访沙特阿拉伯

1956-08-27
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁九月访沙特阿拉伯
    新华社新德里26日电　官方今天正式宣布，印度总理尼赫鲁将在9月24日离开新德里，前往沙特阿拉伯作短时期的访问。他预定在9月27日回到印度。
    沙特阿拉伯国王沙特·阿卜杜勒·阿齐兹在去年冬天访问印度时，邀请尼赫鲁总理去沙特阿拉伯访问。尼赫鲁当时接受了邀请，但是没有确定访问的日期。
