Title: Ambassador Huang Zhen Holds Reception

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 23rd. Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen and his wife held a reception on the evening of the 22nd for the visit of Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, to Indonesia.

Five hundred distinguished guests attended the reception at the Chinese Embassy.

Ambassador Huang Zhen proposed a toast to the friendship between China and Indonesia, to world peace, and to the health of President Sukarno. Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo proposed a toast to Ms. Song Qingling, the state guest of Indonesia, to the ever-developing friendship between the two countries, to the prosperity of the Chinese people, and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong.

Guests also watched acrobatics performed by the Beijing Acrobatic Troupe and Chinese dances.

### 黄镇大使举行招待宴会

1956-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    黄镇大使举行招待宴会
    据新华社雅加达23日电　中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和夫人二十二日晚间为中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄访问印度尼西亚举行了宴会。
    出席中国大使馆宴会的有五百位贵宾。
    黄镇大使提议为中国和印度尼西亚的友谊、为世界和平、为苏加诺总统的健康干杯。沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理提议为印度尼西亚的国宾宋庆龄女士、为两国之间的永远发展的友谊、为中国人民的繁荣、为毛泽东主席的健康干杯。
    客人们还观看了北京杂技团表演的杂技和中国舞蹈。
