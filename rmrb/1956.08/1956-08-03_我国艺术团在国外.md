Title: Chinese Art Troupes Abroad
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 5
    Chinese Art Troupes Abroad
    Art Troupe Performs for the First Time in Sofia
    The Chinese Art Troupe, which was invited to visit Bulgaria, gave its first performance in Sofia on the evening of July 31. The performances by the art troupe were warmly welcomed by the audience.
    Acrobatic Troupe Holds Grand Performance in Pula
    The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, which is currently visiting Yugoslavia, held a grand performance in Pula on the Adriatic coast on July 29. Fourteen thousand people watched the performance.
    Shanghai Acrobatic Troupe Leaves East Germany for Poland
    Two hundred thousand spectators watched the performances of the Chinese Shanghai Acrobatic Troupe in the German Democratic Republic, which have just concluded. The acrobatic troupe's performances were warmly welcomed by audiences in East Germany. The acrobatic troupe arrived in Wrocław, Poland, on the 2nd and will be visiting and performing in Poland for nearly two months.
        (Xinhua News Agency)

### 我国艺术团在国外

1956-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团在国外
    艺术团在索非亚首次演出
    应邀到保加利亚访问的中国艺术团七月三十一日晚间在索非亚举行首次访问演出。艺术团演出的节目都受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    杂技团在普拉举行盛大公演
    正在南斯拉夫作访问演出的中国杂技团，七月二十九日在亚得里亚海滨的普拉举行了一次盛大的公演，观看这次演出的有一万四千人。
    上海杂技团离民主德国赴波
    有二十万观众观看了刚刚结束的中国上海杂技团在德意志民主共和国的演出。杂技团的演出受到民主德国观众的热烈欢迎。杂技团已在二日到达波兰弗罗茨瓦夫市，将在波兰进行近两个月的访问演出。
        （据新华社讯）
