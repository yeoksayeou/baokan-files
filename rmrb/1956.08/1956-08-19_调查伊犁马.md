Title: Investigating the Ili Horse
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency report: An Ili horse investigation team is conducting surveys in the Xinyuan, Nileke, Zhaosu, and Tekes areas of the Ili River Valley. This investigation aims to provide specific data and improvement suggestions for future fine horse breeding.

The Ili horse is a famous breed of horse in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, produced in the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture, with a current population of approximately 560,000 to 570,000. The Ili horse is tall, strong, highly adaptable, and suitable for both riding and draft work, making it a favorite among the various ethnic groups in Xinjiang. The investigation covers the Ili horse's types, performance, characteristics, and appearance; the natural, historical, and economic conditions that shaped the breed; past and present crossbreeding improvement efforts and their effects; breeding methods and survival rates; major diseases; and farmers' use of and requirements for horses, as well as their horse-raising experiences. The investigation team includes members from the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Animal Husbandry Department, Xinjiang College, August 1st Agricultural College, Xinjiang Academy of Agricultural and Animal Husbandry Sciences, and other units.

### 调查伊犁马

1956-08-19
第3版()
专栏：

    调查伊犁马
    据新华社讯　一支伊犁马调查队正在伊犁河谷的新源、尼勒克、昭苏、特克斯一带进行调查工作。这次调查是为今后繁育良马提供具体的资料和改进意见。
    伊犁马是新疆维吾尔自治区名马的一种，出产于伊犁哈萨克族自治州，现在约有五十六、七万匹。伊犁马体高力大，适应性强，能乘挽兼用，一向就为新疆各族人民喜爱。这次调查的内容包括伊犁马的类型、性能、特点和外貌；形成品种的自然、历史和经济条件；以往和现在的杂交改良情况和效果；繁育方法，繁殖成活率；主要疾病情况以及农民对马匹的使用和要求、养马经验等。参加调查队的有新疆维吾尔自治区畜牧厅、新疆学院、八一农学院、新疆农牧科学研究所等单位。
