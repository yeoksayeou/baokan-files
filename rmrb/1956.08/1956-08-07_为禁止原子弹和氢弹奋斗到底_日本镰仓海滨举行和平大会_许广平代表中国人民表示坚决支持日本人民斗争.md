Title: Struggle to the End to Prohibit Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs: Peace Conference Held on the Beach in Kamakura, Japan. Xu Guangping Represents the Chinese People in Expressing Firm Support for the Japanese People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1956-08-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kamakura, 6th: On the eve of the opening of the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs (August 5th), a grand peace conference was held on the beach in Kamakura, Kanagawa Prefecture.

　　25,000 peace advocates solemnly declared at the conference that if anyone uses atomic or hydrogen weapons in the future, we will consider him an enemy of mankind. The declaration issued by the conference stated: For the sake of our lives, the happiness of our descendants, and the happiness of mankind, we firmly oppose the use of atomic and hydrogen weapons. In order to achieve the goal of prohibiting atomic weapons, hydrogen weapons, and war, we will resolutely fight to the end together!

　　The beach in Kamakura on the 5th was exceptionally lively. Hundreds and thousands of red flags and trade union flags fluttered in the wind, and crowds of people wearing colorful swimsuits densely surrounded the conference venue. The rostrum, temporarily built with wooden frames, was hung with conference slogans such as "Prohibit Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs" and "Reduce Armaments."

　　At 11:50, representatives from the World Peace Council, the International Liaison Committee of Women for Peace and Freedom, the Women's International Democratic Federation, and China, Britain, Romania, Czechoslovakia, Belgium, Poland and other countries arrived at the venue. At this time, thunderous applause and enthusiastic cheers resounded through the venue.

　　Miyamoto, Deputy Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Kamakura Beach Peace Conference, delivered an opening speech welcoming representatives from all over the world who had come from afar. She said that banning nuclear and hydrogen weapons has become the desire of people all over the world. The foreign representatives have brought the wishes of the people of various countries for peace, banning atomic and hydrogen bombs, and opposing war, making this conference a success.

　　Xu Guangping, head of the Chinese delegation, spoke at the conference. She said that if we want peace, we must never allow the devilish hand of war to drop atomic or hydrogen bombs again; if we want peace, we must strive for peace and strive to defend peace. Xu Guangping told the Japanese public that the Chinese delegation came to Japan with the common will of the entire Chinese people to support peace and oppose war. She said that we are concerned about the cause of peace for the common happiness of mankind and the lives of the Japanese people after the war. She emphasized that we are entrusted by the 600 million people of China to express to you: We are loyal companions on the road to peace. For the sake of peace, for the sake of opposing atomic and hydrogen bombs and other weapons of mass destruction, you are not isolated. The 600 million people of China firmly support you and are willing to unite with you to fight together.

　　Also addressing the conference were Mrs. Vaillant-Couturier, representative of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Mrs. Puhlmann, representative of the World Peace Council, Mr. Ribellini, representative of the International Organization of Journalists, Mrs. Felton, representative of Britain, and Mr. Herzik, representative of Czechoslovakia.

　　Youth and women also performed a friendship dance at the conference. Finally, all the participants sang "No More Atomic Weapons," and the loud singing expressed the Japanese people's strong will to defend peace.

　　That afternoon, the Mayor of Kamakura held a garden party at the Kamakura Daibutsu Temple to entertain representatives from various countries.

### 为禁止原子弹和氢弹奋斗到底  日本镰仓海滨举行和平大会  许广平代表中国人民表示坚决支持日本人民斗争

1956-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    为禁止原子弹和氢弹奋斗到底
    日本镰仓海滨举行和平大会
    许广平代表中国人民表示坚决支持日本人民斗争
    新华社6日讯　镰仓消息：在第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会开幕的前夕（八月五日），在神奈川县镰仓市海滨举行了盛大的和平大会。
    两万五千名和平拥护者在大会上庄严地宣布，今后如果有人使用原子武器和氢武器，我们就认为他是人类的公敌。大会发表的宣言说，为了我们的生命以及子孙和人类的幸福，我们在这里坚决表示反对使用原子武器和氢武器。为了实现禁止原子武器、氢武器和禁止战争的目的，我们一同坚决奋斗到底！
    五日的镰仓海滨显得异常的热闹。数以千百计的红旗和工会旗迎风飘扬，穿着各种彩色游泳衣的群众，密密地绕着大会会场。用木架子临时搭成的主席台上悬挂着“禁止原子弹和氢弹”、“裁减军备”的大会标语。
    十一时五十分，世界和平理事会、国际民主妇女争取和平与自由联盟、国际民主妇女联合会以及中国、英国、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、比利时、波兰等代表来到会场。这时，会场响起了雷动般的掌声和热烈的欢呼声。
    镰仓海滨和平大会执行委员会副委员长宫本致开会词表示欢迎远道而来的世界各国代表。她说，禁止核子武器和氢武器已经成为全世界人民的愿望，外国代表带来了各国人民的和平、禁止原子弹和氢弹、反对战争的愿望，使这个大会获得成功。
    中国代表团团长许广平在大会上讲了话。她说，我们要和平，就绝不允许战争的魔手再投原子弹或氢弹；我们要和平，就必须努力争取和平，努力保卫和平。许广平对日本公众表示，中国代表团带着全中国人民拥护和平、反对战争的共同意志来到日本。她说，我们关心人类共同幸福的和平事业和战后日本人民的生活。她强调说，我们受全中国人民的委托，向你们表示：我们同你们是和平旅途上的忠实同伴。为了和平，为了反对原子弹和氢弹以及其他大规模毁灭性武器，你们不是孤立的。中国六万万人民坚决地支持你们，并且愿意同你们团结一起，共同奋斗。
    在大会上致词的还有：国际民主妇女联合会代表瓦扬—古久里夫人、世界和平理事会代表普尔梦夫人、国际新闻工作者协会代表理倍利尼、英国代表费尔顿夫人、捷克斯洛伐克代表赫齐克等。
    在大会上还由青年和妇女表演了联欢舞。最后，全体与会者合唱“不许再使用原子武器”歌，响亮的歌声，唱出日本人民保卫和平的坚强意志。
    当天下午，镰仓市长在镰仓大佛殿举行游园会招待各国代表。
