Title: Egypt Distributes Weapons to Urban and Rural Citizens to Turn Egypt into a Fortress to Cope with Contingencies
Author:
Date: 1956-08-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 11th – “People’s News” reported today: The measures of supplying weapons to urban and rural citizens, which have already been implemented in the Suez Canal area and nearby areas, will soon be extended to other parts of Egypt.

“People’s News” said that according to the current national defense plan being implemented, the entire country will be turned into a fortress to cope with any unforeseen events.

### 埃及发给城乡公民武器  要把埃及变成要塞应付不测

1956-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及发给城乡公民武器
    要把埃及变成要塞应付不测
    新华社11日讯　开罗消息：“人民报”今天报道：已经在苏彝士运河地区和附近地区执行的给城乡公民供应武器的措施，不久将扩展到埃及其他地方。
    “人民报”说，根据目前执行中的国防计划，全国将要变成一个要塞，以应付任何不测事件。
