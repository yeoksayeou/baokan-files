Title: Indonesian Parliament Celebrates Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 17th. The Indonesian Parliament held a special meeting on the evening of August 16th to celebrate the 11th anniversary of independence. President Sukarno, Vice President Hatta, and a large number of diplomatic representatives attended the meeting. Also in attendance was Song Qingling, State Guest of Indonesia and Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China.

### 印度尼西亚国会庆祝独立日

1956-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会庆祝独立日
    据新华社雅加达17日电　印度尼西亚国会在八月十六日晚间举行特别会议，庆祝独立十一周年纪念。苏加诺总统、哈达副总统和大批外交代表们都参加了这次会议。印度尼西亚国宾、中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄也出席了特别会议。
