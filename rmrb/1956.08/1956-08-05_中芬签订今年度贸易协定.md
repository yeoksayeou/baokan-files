Title: China and Finland Sign This Year's Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 1st. After friendly negotiations, the trade agreement for 1956 between the governments of China and Finland was officially signed in Helsinki on July 31st. According to this agreement, the volume of goods exchanged between the two governments in 1956 will increase compared to the previous year.

Representing the Chinese side, the agreement was signed by Lin Haiyun, head of the Chinese government trade delegation and Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade. Representing the Finnish side, the agreement was signed by Oli Kaila, head of the Finnish government delegation and Acting Director of the Commercial Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 中芬签订今年度贸易协定

1956-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    中芬签订今年度贸易协定
    新华社布拉格1日电　中国和芬兰两国政府之间1956年度的贸易协定经过双方代表团友好的谈判，七月三十一日在赫尔辛基正式签字。根据这个协定，两国政府在1956年度交换的货物将比上一年度有所增加。
    代表中国方面在协定上签字的是中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部部长助理林海云，代表芬兰方面签字的是芬兰政府代表团团长、外交部商务司代司长奥利凯拉。
