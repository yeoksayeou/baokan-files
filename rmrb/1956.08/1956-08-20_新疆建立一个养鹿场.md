Title: A Deer Farm Established in Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, August 18th. The Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture is establishing the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region's first deer farm in the lush Kazi Valley of the Tianshan Mountains. This year, the deer farm will raise fifty young deer.

The Ili River basin, where herds of wild deer roam, is a famous producer of precious medicinal materials such as pilose antler and deer antlers. After the deer farm is completed, it will promote deer breeding and facilitate the planned collection of medicinal materials such as pilose antler.

### 新疆建立一个养鹿场

1956-08-20
第3版()
专栏：

    新疆建立一个养鹿场
    新华社乌鲁木齐18日电　伊犁哈萨克族自治州正在天山水草丰盛的卡其山谷里，建立新疆维吾尔自治区的第一个养鹿场。今年这个养鹿场将饲养五十只幼鹿。
    有着成群的野鹿出没的伊犁河流域两岸，是鹿茸、鹿角等珍贵药材的著名产地。养鹿场建成以后，将促进鹿的繁殖，并便于有计划地采集鹿茸等药材。
