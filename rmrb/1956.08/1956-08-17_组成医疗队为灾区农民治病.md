Title: Medical Team Organized to Treat Farmers in Disaster Areas
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, August 14th: A medical team composed of thirty-four traditional Chinese and Western medicine doctors and nurses from Tianjin set off on the 13th and 14th to treat farmers affected by the floods and laborers defending the dikes of the Ziya River and Daqing River in the Tianjin Special District of Hebei Province.

This medical team includes medical personnel from internal medicine, surgery, obstetrics and gynecology, pediatrics, and acupuncture, and they are carrying a large number of Chinese and Western medicines and medical equipment.

### 组成医疗队为灾区农民治病

1956-08-17
第3版()
专栏：

    组成医疗队为灾区农民治病
    新华社天津14日电　由天津市三十四名中西医师和护理人员组成的医疗队，十三、十四两日先后出发到河北省天津专区为遭受水灾的农民和防守在子牙河、大清河河堤上的民工治病。
    这个医疗队包括内科、外科、妇产科、小儿科、针灸科的医务人员，他们携带了大批中西药品和医疗用具。
