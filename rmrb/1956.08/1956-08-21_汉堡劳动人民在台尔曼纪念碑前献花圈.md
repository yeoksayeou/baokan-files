Title: Hamburg Workers Lay Wreaths at Thälmann Memorial

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 6

    Hamburg Workers Lay Wreaths at Thälmann Memorial
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 20th: Hamburg dispatch: On August 18th, the working people of Hamburg, West Germany, laid wreaths at the memorial in front of the former residence of the late German Communist leader Ernst Thälmann, to commemorate the twelfth anniversary of the death of this great son of Hamburg.
    The wreaths placed in front of the memorial attracted great attention from passers-by. People pointed out that this fact proves that any repressive measures against the German Communist Party cannot eliminate its profound influence among the German people.

### 汉堡劳动人民在台尔曼纪念碑前献花圈

1956-08-21
第6版()
专栏：

    汉堡劳动人民在台尔曼纪念碑前献花圈
    新华社柏林20日电　汉堡消息：西德汉堡的劳动人民八月十八日在已故德国共产党领导者台尔曼故居的纪念碑前献花圈，以纪念这位汉堡的伟大儿子逝世十二周年。
    放在纪念碑前的花圈引起了过路行人的很大注意。人们指出，这个事实证明对德国共产党的任何镇压措施都不可能消除它在德国人民中的深刻影响。
