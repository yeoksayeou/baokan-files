Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Burmese Ambassador to the Soviet Union U Ohn Maung
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 21st. Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai received the Burmese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, U Ohn Maung, this afternoon.

    Also present at the meeting was Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian.

### 周总理接见缅驻苏大使吴旺

1956-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见缅驻苏大使吴旺
    新华社21日讯　国务院总理周恩来今日下午接见了缅甸驻苏联大使吴旺。
    接见时在座的有外交部副部长张闻天。
