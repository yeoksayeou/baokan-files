Title: Chinese Practical Art Exhibition Opens in Ljubljana
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 17th: Belgrade dispatch: The Chinese Practical Art Exhibition opened in Ljubljana, Yugoslavia on August 15th. The exhibition displays approximately one thousand kinds of Chinese practical artworks. These artworks are gifts from China to Yugoslavia.

These exhibits were previously displayed in Belgrade and will later be displayed in other cities in Yugoslavia.

### 我国实用艺术展览会  在卢布尔雅那开幕

1956-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

    我国实用艺术展览会
    在卢布尔雅那开幕
    新华社布拉格17日电　贝尔格莱德消息：中国实用艺术展览会八月十五日在南斯拉夫的卢布尔雅那开幕。展览会中陈列了大约一千种中国实用艺术品。这些艺术品是中国赠给南斯拉夫的礼物。
    这些展览品以前曾经在贝尔格莱德展出过，以后还要在南斯拉夫其他城市展出。
