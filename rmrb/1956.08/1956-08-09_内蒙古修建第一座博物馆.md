Title: Inner Mongolia Builds First Museum
Author:
Date: 1956-08-09
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, 7th - Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region's first museum broke ground here yesterday. The building area is 5,000 square meters.

This museum will showcase Inner Mongolia's rich resources in industry, agriculture, animal husbandry, forestry, and water conservancy; the history of the Mongolian people's revolutionary struggles over the past century; the production, life, and customs of various ethnic groups; and the autonomous region's construction achievements and long-term plans, through the display of objects, specimens, models, and paintings.

Engineering and technical personnel from Shandong, Jilin, Heilongjiang, and Hebei provinces, who came to support the construction of the Inner Mongolia Museum, are conducting interior design work. The entire project will be completed by the end of this year. It is expected to officially open on May 1st of next year, the tenth anniversary of the establishment of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

### 内蒙古修建第一座博物馆

1956-08-09
第7版()
专栏：

    内蒙古修建第一座博物馆
    新华社呼和浩特7日电　内蒙古自治区的第一座博物馆昨天在这里动工兴建。建筑面积有五千平方公尺。
    这座博物馆将把内蒙古工业、农业、牧业、林业和水利等丰富的资源，蒙古族人民近百年来革命斗争的历史，各民族人民生产、生活和风俗习惯及自治区建设成就和远景规划等，通过陈列的实物、标本、模型和图画表现出来。
    从山东、吉林、黑龙江、河北等四省前来支援内蒙古博物馆建设的工程技术人员，正进行室内图式设计工作。全部工程将在今年年底完成。明年五月一日内蒙古自治区成立十周年纪念日，可正式开馆。
