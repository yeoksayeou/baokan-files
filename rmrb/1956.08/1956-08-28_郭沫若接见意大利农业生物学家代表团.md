Title: Guo Moruo Receives Italian Agricultural Biologists Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received the entire Italian Agricultural Biologists Delegation this afternoon.

During the meeting, the head of the delegation, Carlo Jucci, professor of agricultural genetics at the University of Pavia, presented President Guo Moruo with his writings from the past year since his visit to China, as well as some classic Italian works. Guo Moruo also presented the delegation and each of its members with recently published "Scientia Sinica" and "Science Bulletin," as well as portraits and biographies of the four great ancient Chinese scientists Zhang Heng, Zu Chongzhi, Monk Yi Xing, and Li Shizhen.

Also present at the meeting were Tao Menghe, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, as well as experts and professors from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Beijing Agricultural University.

### 郭沫若接见意大利农业生物学家代表团

1956-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见意大利农业生物学家代表团
    新华社27日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今日下午接见了意大利农业生物学家代表团全体人员。
    接见的时候，代表团团长、巴维亚大学农业遗传学教授卡洛·尤契将他自去年访问中国以后一年来的著作和意大利的一些经典著作送给了郭沫若院长，郭沫若也将中国最近出版的“中国科学”和“中国科学通讯”等刊物以及中国古代四大科学家张衡、祖冲之、僧一行、李时珍的画像和自传赠送给代表团和代表团的每个团员。
    接见时在座的，还有中国科学院副院长陶孟和以及中国科学院、北京农业大学的专家教授等多人。
