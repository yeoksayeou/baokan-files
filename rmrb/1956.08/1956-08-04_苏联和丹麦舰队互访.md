Title: Soviet and Danish Fleet Visits

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd: A Soviet fleet consisting of a cruiser and two destroyers arrived in Denmark on August 2nd for a friendly visit. The fleet was warmly welcomed by the Danish people upon arrival at the port of Copenhagen.

The Danish fleet, led by Rear Admiral Lundsteen, Commander of the Coastal Fleet, arrived in Leningrad on August 1st for a courtesy visit.

### 苏联和丹麦舰队互访

1956-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和丹麦舰队互访
    据新华社3日讯　由一只巡洋舰和两只驱击舰组成的一支苏联舰队，八月二日到达丹麦作友好访问。在舰队到达哥本哈根港时，受到丹麦人民的热烈欢迎。
    丹麦舰队，在海岸舰队司令隆德斯廷少将的率领下，八月一日到达列宁格勒作礼节访问。
