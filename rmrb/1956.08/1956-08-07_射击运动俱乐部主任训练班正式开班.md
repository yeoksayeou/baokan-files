Title: Shooting Sports Club Directors Training Class Officially Opens

Author:

Date: 1956-08-07
Page: 4

    Shooting Sports Club
    Directors Training Class Officially Opens
    (Xinhua News Agency, 4th) The Shooting Sports Club Directors Training Class, organized by the Central National Defense Sports Club, officially opened on August 2nd.
    The training class is expected to last for forty days. The curriculum includes not only further improving shooting skills but also focusing on the working methods of organizing and leading urban shooting sports.
    The participants in the training class come from thirty-two cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Kunming, Urumqi, and Hohhot. Among these cities, Beijing, Shijiazhuang, Tangshan, Taiyuan, Shenyang, Fuzhou, Nanchang, and Chengdu have formally established shooting sports clubs, and other cities are also actively preparing to establish them. In the past six months, the number of people participating in shooting sports in these cities has been around 20,000, and 7,840 ordinary shooters have been trained, as well as nearly 3,000 amateur instructors.

### 射击运动俱乐部主任训练班正式开班

1956-08-07
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    射击运动俱乐部
    主任训练班正式开班
    据新华社4日讯　中央国防体育俱乐部主办的射击运动俱乐部主任训练班已在八月二日正式开班。
    训练班学习时间预计四十天，学习内容除进一步提高射击技术外，并特别着重组织领导城市射击运动的工作方法。
    参加训练班学习的成员来自北京、上海、昆明、乌鲁木齐、呼和浩特等三十二个城市。这些城市中，北京、石家庄、唐山、太原、沈阳、福州、南昌、成都八市已正式建立了射击运动俱乐部，其它城市也在积极筹备建立。半年来这些城市参加射击运动的人数有两万人左右，已培养出普通射手七千八百四十名，并训练出业余辅导员将近三千人。
