Title: Albanian New Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Albania to China, Comrade Dello Balili, along with his wife and children, arrived in Beijing this afternoon. Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Li Huichuan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs, were present at the airport to welcome them.
    Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries' embassies in China, as well as Mr. Bino, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Albanian People's Republic Embassy in China, and his wife, were also present to welcome them.

### 阿驻华新大使到京

1956-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    阿驻华新大使到京
    新华社17日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国新任驻华特命全权大使德略·巴利里偕夫人子女在今天下午到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有外交部礼宾司副司长马振武，苏联东欧司副司长李汇川。
    各国驻华使节及外交官员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办比诺及夫人，也前往欢迎。
