Title: Lao Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Shenyang and Anshan; Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma Holds Reception; Ministry of Culture Holds Song and Dance Peking Opera Gala to Entertain Lao Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th. Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma and the Lao Royal Government Delegation he leads held a reception in Beijing this evening, attended by over one hundred Chinese and foreign guests.

Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Zhu De and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai attended the reception. They sat with Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma and Deputy Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith, drinking and conversing.

The reception was held on the lawn of the Guest House, shaded by ancient trees and lush green grass. As dusk fell, strings of colored lights illuminated the greenery. Guests gathered in small groups, conversing.

During the reception, the orchestra played many beautiful Lao and Chinese melodies.

Also attending the reception today were Vice Premiers of the State Council Peng Dehuai, He Long, and Chen Yi; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong; Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Cheng Qian; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian; heads of various departments of state organs; generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Envoys, commercial representatives, and heads of economic delegations from various countries stationed in China also attended the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th. Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, and members of the Lao Royal Government Delegation, attended a song and dance Peking opera gala tonight held by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China to welcome them.

Accompanying the distinguished guests to watch the song and dance Peking opera were Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Chen Shutong.

After the performance, the Lao Royal Government Delegation presented flower baskets to the Chinese artists. Premier Zhou Enlai accompanied Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma and Deputy Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith, among others, to the stage to shake hands with the artists and express their gratitude.

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th. Prince Souvanna Phouma and the Lao Royal Government Delegation, accompanied by Wang Zhuoru, Acting Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, returned to Beijing this morning by special plane from Shenyang after visiting Shenyang and Anshan. ### 老挝代表团到沈阳鞍山参观后回京  梭发那·富马首相举行招待会  文化部举行歌舞京剧晚会招待老挝政府代表团

1956-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

    老挝代表团到沈阳鞍山参观后回京
    梭发那·富马首相举行招待会
    文化部举行歌舞京剧晚会招待老挝政府代表团
    新华社24日讯　梭发那·富马首相和他所率领的老挝王国政府代表团，今天傍晚在北京举行了一次有一百多位中外人士参加的招待会。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德和国务院总理周恩来出席了招待会。他们同梭发那·富马首相、卡代·敦·萨索里特副首相围坐在一起饮酒交谈。
    今天的招待会在迎宾馆的古树遮天、绿草如茵的草坪上举行。当夜幕降临的时候，在绿树丛中亮起了一串串彩色电灯。宾主三三五五围聚在一起交谈着。
    席间，管弦乐队演奏了许多老挝和中国的优美乐曲。
    今天，国务院副总理彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通，国防委员会副主席程潜，外交部副部长张闻天，国家机关各部门的负责人，中国人民解放军的将军们和首都社会各界人士都出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国的使节、商务代表和经济代表团团长也出席了招待会。　　新华社24日讯　老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王和他率领的老挝王国政府代表团团员，今晚出席了中华人民共和国文化部为欢迎他们举行的歌舞京剧晚会。
    陪同贵宾们观看歌舞京剧的有，国务院总理周恩来，副总理贺龙、陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通。
    演出结束后，老挝王国政府代表团向中国艺术家赠送花篮，并由周恩来总理陪同梭发那·富马首相和卡代·敦·萨索里特副首相等上台和艺术家们握手致谢。
    据新华社24日讯　梭发那·富马亲王和老挝王国政府代表团由外交部礼宾司代司长王倬如陪同到沈阳和鞍山参观后，在今天上午从沈阳乘专机回到北京。（附图片）
