Title: Souphanouvong Delivers Speech Before Leaving Vientiane, Emphasizing the Vientiane Talks Resulted in a Great Victory for Laos, Guaranteeing the Lao Side Will Sincerely Cooperate with the Kingdom Government
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 15th: According to Vietnam News Agency on the 14th: Prince Souphanouvong delivered a speech on Vientiane Radio on the 12th, conveying the warm congratulations of the Lao Fighting Units to all the Lao people on the achievements of the Vientiane talks.

　　Souphanouvong pointed out that the signing of the two joint communiqués has important historical significance. This is a great victory for Laos, which is in line with the legitimate aspirations of the people across the country. At the same time, the signing of the two joint communiqués also shattered the foreign conspiracies attempting to obstruct our agreement and undermine our national unity.

　　Souphanouvong expressed the consistent desire of the Lao Fighting Units to sincerely cooperate with the Kingdom of Laos government in order to build Laos into a peaceful, independent, democratic, and unified kingdom.

　　Souphanouvong called on the people across the country to fully support the policy of peace and neutrality of the Kingdom of Laos government headed by Prime Minister Phouma. He also expressed the hope that the Lao people will support both sides in implementing the joint communiqués, thereby strictly consolidating peace and independence, and realizing democracy, freedom, and national unity in accordance with the people's wishes.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 15th: According to Vientiane Radio broadcast on the 14th: The Lao Fighting Units delegation led by Prince Souphanouvong already left Vientiane by plane for Sam Neua on August 12th.

### 苏发努冯在离万象前发表演说  强调万象会谈结果是老挝的伟大胜利  保证寮方将同王国政府真诚合作

1956-08-16
第6版()
专栏：

    苏发努冯在离万象前发表演说
    强调万象会谈结果是老挝的伟大胜利
    保证寮方将同王国政府真诚合作
    据新华社河内15日电　据越南通讯社十四日讯：苏发努冯亲王十二日在万象电台发表演说，就万象会谈的成就向全体老挝人民转达了寮国战斗部队的热烈祝贺。
    苏发努冯指出，两项联合公报的签字具有重要的历史意义。这是老挝的一个伟大的胜利，是符合全国人民的正当愿望的。同时，两项联合公报的签字也粉碎了外国企图阻挠我们达成协议以及破坏我们的民族团结的阴谋。
    苏发努冯表示寮国战斗部队要同老挝王国政府真诚合作，以便把老挝建设成一个和平、独立、民主和统一的王国的一贯愿望。
    苏发努冯号召全国人民全力支持以富马首相为首的老挝王国政府的和平中立政策。他还表示希望，老挝人民将支持双方实施联合公报，从而严格按照人民的愿望，巩固和平和独立，实现民主、自由和全国统一。
    据新华社河内15日电　据万象电台十四日广播：由苏发努冯亲王率领的寮国战斗部队代表团已经在八月十二日从万象乘飞机回桑怒。
