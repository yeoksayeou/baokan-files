Title: Soong Ching Ling Arrives in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 14 - Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People' Congress of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Jakarta by plane at 1 a.m. this morning. Soong Ching Ling is visiting Indonesia and attending the Indonesian National Day celebrations at the invitation of President Sukarno and the Indonesian government.

Welcoming her at the airport were Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo and his wife, First Deputy Speaker Arifin, Second Deputy Speaker Katawenata, the wife of Foreign Minister Abdulgani, Director of the Asia-Pacific Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Malamis, Jakarta Mayor Sudiro, Chairman of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association, Professor Prijono, and other senior officials and celebrities. Also welcoming her at the airport were Chinese Ambassador Huang Zhen and his wife, as well as all the staff of the Chinese Embassy.

Accompanied by Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo and his wife, Soong Ching Ling drove to the Independence Palace, where she was welcomed by President Sukarno and Vice President Hatta.

More than 10,000 people waited outside the airport in the dark to welcome Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling. Colorful arches were erected along the route and at intersections. As Soong Ching Ling's car passed by, the welcoming crowds cheered and applauded enthusiastically.

In a speech at the airport, Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling extended the warm greetings and best wishes of the Chinese people to the Indonesian people.

She said: "In those years when you were fighting for national liberation, our hearts were often with you; our sympathy for you was strengthened by our own unyielding struggle for the same goal."

Soong Ching Ling said: "Our struggle today is to protect our achievements, build our country, and safeguard world peace. The Chinese and Indonesian peoples face the same tasks. The Chinese people deeply trust their Indonesian friends and appreciate the many contributions you are making to promote our common cause. We believe that the growth of friendship between us and the expansion of our cooperative efforts are very important for the final and inevitable victory that will belong to us."

Soong Ching Ling finally expressed the hope that her visit would promote contacts and connections between the peoples of China and Indonesia.

### 宋庆龄到达雅加达

1956-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄到达雅加达
    新华社雅加达14日电　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄今晨一时乘飞机到达雅加达。宋庆龄是应苏加诺总统和印度尼西亚政府的邀请到印度尼西亚访问和参加印度尼西亚国庆典礼的。
    在机场欢迎的有沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和他的夫人，第一副议长阿里芬、第二副议长卡塔韦纳塔，外交部长阿卜杜加尼的夫人，外交部亚洲太平洋司司长马拉密斯、雅加达市长苏迪罗、印度尼西亚—中国友好协会主席普里约诺教授和其他高级官员以及社会名流。在机场欢迎的还有中国大使黄镇和他的夫人以及中国大使馆的全体工作人员。
    宋庆龄在沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和他的夫人的陪同下驱车前往独立宫，她在那里受到了苏加诺总统和哈达副总统的欢迎。
    有一万多人在黑夜中等候在机场外面欢迎宋庆龄副委员长。沿途和十字路口扎起了彩色的牌楼。当宋庆龄所乘的汽车驶过的时候，欢迎的人群热烈地欢呼和鼓掌。
    宋庆龄副委员长在机场上发表谈话的时候，向印度尼西亚人民表示了中国人民的热烈的问候和最善良的愿望。
    她说：“在你们为民族而斗争的那些年代里，我们的心常常是同你们在一起的；我们对你们的同情，随着我们自己为了同样的目标所进行的不屈不挠的斗争而更为加强。”
    宋庆龄说：“我们今天的斗争是保护我们的成果，建设我们的国家和维护世界和平。中国人民和印度尼西亚人民面临着同样的任务。中国人民深深地信任他们的印度尼西亚的朋友，并且赞赏你们为了推动我们的共同事业而正在作出的许多贡献。我们认为，我们之间友谊的增长和我们合作努力的扩大，对于必将属于我们的最后和必然的胜利，是很重要的。”
    宋庆龄最后表示希望她的这次访问会促进中国和印度尼西亚两国人民之间的联系和接触。
