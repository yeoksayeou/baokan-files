Title: Indonesian Political Leaders Respond to Sukarno's Proposal, Supporting the Reopening of the Asian-African Conference and Egypt's Struggle. Aidit Advocates for Discussions on How to Oppose the Military Intervention Preparations of Britain, France, and the United States.
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 26th: Many political leaders here have expressed support for President Sukarno's suggestion to convene a second Asian-African conference to support Egypt's struggle to maintain its sovereignty.

President Sukarno expressed this proposal in his speech at the Jakarta celebration of Indonesian Independence Day on August 17th.

Chairman of the Indonesian Islamic Union Party, Jokroaminoto, told the press on August 24th that Dulles' plan to internationalize the Suez Canal at the London conference was, in effect, forcing others to accept collective colonialism, which is worse than any existing colonialism. He said that the Western countries' attempt to internationalize part of Egyptian territory contradicts their proclaimed recognition of Egyptian sovereignty.

The Indonesian Islamic Union Party leader said that, according to Indonesia's anti-colonial policy, it should take a more active initiative than ever before to convene a second Asian-African conference. He said: "We must not let Egypt be isolated and helpless in defending its rights and fighting colonialism. Egypt should receive the support of Asian and African countries." He warned that if Western countries risk a third world war by forcing Egypt to accept their will, "they will encounter not only the resistance of the Arab world but also the resistance of more than half of the countries in the world."

General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, Aidit, issued a statement welcoming President Sukarno's call for a second Asian-African conference to support Egypt's sovereignty, saying it was the correct response to Western provocations. Aidit's statement was published in the "People's Daily" on August 25th.

Aidit said that the only issue to be discussed at the second conference should be how to oppose the military intervention preparations of Britain, France, and the United States. Aidit believes that the location of the second Asian-African conference should be in Cairo, in order to give greater support to Egypt's attitude. He called on all Indonesian parties and mass organizations to determine their positions and actions in order to cope with the danger of aggression against Egypt.

The First Vice Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, Mangunsarkoro, told a reporter from the "Independent Newspaper" today that he hoped to conduct contact to consolidate the solidarity of Asian and African countries. Commenting on the fact that some Asian and African countries supported the Dulles plan at the London conference, he said that Western countries are always trying to undermine the unity of Asian and African countries.

### 印度尼西亚政界领袖响应苏加诺的主张  赞成重开亚非会议支持埃及的斗争  艾地主张会议讨论如何反对英法美的军事干涉准备

1956-08-27
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政界领袖响应苏加诺的主张
    赞成重开亚非会议支持埃及的斗争
    艾地主张会议讨论如何反对英法美的军事干涉准备
    据新华社雅加达26日讯　这里的许多政界领袖表示支持苏加诺总统提出的这个建议，即：召开第二次亚非会议以支持埃及维护主权的斗争。
    苏加诺总统在8月17日雅加达庆祝印度尼西亚国庆大会上发表演说时，表示了这个主张。
    印度尼西亚伊斯兰联盟党主席佐克罗阿米诺托8月24日对报界说，杜勒斯在伦敦会议上提出的把苏彝士运河国际化的计划，实际上是强迫别人接受集体殖民主义，这种殖民主义比现有的任何殖民主义更坏。他说，西方国家企图把部分埃及领土国际化的做法，是同它们宣称承认埃及主权这一点相抵触的。
    这位印度尼西亚伊斯兰联盟党领袖说，根据印度尼西亚的反殖民主义政策，它应当比以前任何时候更为积极地采取主动来召开第二次亚非会议。他说：“我们决不能让埃及在保卫它的权利和同殖民主义斗争的时候孤立无助。埃及应当得到亚非国家的支持。”他警告说，如果西方国家由于强迫埃及接受它们的意志而去冒第三次世界大战的危险，“它们将不仅遇到阿拉伯世界的抵抗，而且将遇到全世界半数以上国家的抵抗”。
    印度尼西亚总书记艾地发表声明，表示欢迎苏加诺总统关于召开第二次亚非会议以支持埃及维护自己的主权的呼吁，他说这是对西方国家挑衅的正确答复，艾地的声明刊载在8月25日的“人民日报”上。
    艾地说，第二次会议所要讨论的唯一的问题应当是如何来反对英国、法国和美国的军事干涉准备。艾地认为，第二次亚非会议的地点应当是在开罗，以便给予埃及的态度以更大的支持。他号召所有的印度尼西亚党派和群众组织决定它们的立场和行动，以便应付埃及遭受侵略的危险。
    印度尼西亚国民党第一副主席曼贡萨科罗今天向“独立报”记者说，表示希望进行接触以巩固亚非国家的团结。他在评论某些亚非国家在伦敦会议上支持了杜勒斯计划这一事实时说，西方国家总是在企图破坏亚非国家的团结。
