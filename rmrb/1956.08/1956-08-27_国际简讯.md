　　International Briefs

　　Vietnamese and Mongolian Legations Elevated
　　Hanoi Dispatch: The governments of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Mongolian People's Republic today morning issued a joint communiqué announcing that, in order to further strengthen the friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and Mongolia, the two governments have decided to elevate their respective legations in Ulaanbaatar and Hanoi to embassies, and to mutually dispatch ambassadors.

　　Soviet Union Repatriates Another Batch of Japanese Citizens
　　Moscow Dispatch: Recently, another 115 Japanese citizens, with the assistance of the Executive Committee of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union, returned to Japan from the Soviet Union aboard the Japanese ship "Kouan Maru".

### 国际简讯

1956-08-27
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    越蒙两国公使馆升格
    河内讯　越南民主共和国政府和蒙古人民共和国政府今天上午发表联合公报宣布，为了进一步加强越蒙两国之间的友谊和合作，两国政府决定把各自设在乌兰巴托和河内的公使馆升格为大使馆，并且互派大使。
    苏联又遣返一批日本公民
    莫斯科讯　最近又有一百一十五名日本公民在苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会执行委员会协助下乘日本“兴安丸”轮船从苏联返回日本。
