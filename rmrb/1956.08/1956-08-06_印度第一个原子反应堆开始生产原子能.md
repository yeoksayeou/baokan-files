Title: India's First Atomic Reactor Begins Producing Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1956-08-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 5th: Dr. Homi Bhabha, Chairman of the Indian Atomic Energy Commission, announced on the evening of August 4th that India's first atomic reactor, located in Trombay, a suburb of Bombay, produced atomic energy for the first time at 3:45 PM Indian Standard Time.

    Indian Prime Minister Nehru, in a telegram congratulating those involved in the work, said that this was "an event of great significance to us."

    Dr. Bhabha said at a press conference that the reactor was built entirely by Indians and was the result of the joint efforts of about fifty scientists and engineers of the Atomic Energy Commission. The completion of this reactor completes the first phase of India's atomic development plan.

    This atomic reactor is of the "swimming pool" type and is the first in a series of reactors used to train personnel for larger and more complex reactors in the future. Bhabha said that the reactor would also be used for experiments and the production of radioactive isotopes for use in agricultural, industrial, and medical research.

    Bhabha said that the reactor cost a total of 2.5 million to 3 million rupees and produces energy equivalent to about 1,000 kilowatts. However, he said, the second reactor will produce 30,000 kilowatts of energy.

### 印度第一个原子反应堆开始生产原子能

1956-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    印度第一个原子反应堆开始生产原子能
    新华社新德里5日电  印度原子能委员会主席霍米·巴巴博士八月四日晚上宣布，印度设在孟买郊区特罗姆贝的第一个原子反应堆在印度标准时间下午三时四十五分第一次生产原子能。
    印度总理尼赫鲁在致电向参加这项工作的人们祝贺的时候说：这是“对我们有重大意义的事件”。
    巴巴博士在记者招待会上说，这个反应堆完全是印度人建筑的，是原子能委员会约五十位科学家和工程师的共同努力的成果，这个反应堆的建成完成了印度第一阶段的原子发展计划。
    这个原子反应堆是“游泳池”式的，是用来为以后更大和更复杂的反应堆训练人员的一系列反应堆中的第一个。巴巴说，这个反应堆也将用来进行试验和生产放射性同位素，以便供农业、工业和医学方面的研究之用。
    巴巴说，这个反应堆共花费二百五十万卢比到三百万卢比，生产的能量约等于一千瓩，但是，他说，第二个反应堆生产的能量将为三万瓩。
