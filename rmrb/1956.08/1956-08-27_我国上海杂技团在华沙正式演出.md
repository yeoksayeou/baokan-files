Title: China's Shanghai Acrobatic Troupe Officially Performs in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 26th - After concluding their performances in Poznan and Szczecin, Poland, the Shanghai Acrobatic Troupe of China began its official performances in Warsaw on August 24th.

Kuryluk, the Polish Minister of Culture and Arts, Nashkovsky, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and leaders from other departments of the Polish government attended the performance that day.

The audience continuously applauded the acrobats' superb and graceful performances, showering particular praise on the performances of the child performers, who were around ten years old.

### 我国上海杂技团在华沙正式演出

1956-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    我国上海杂技团在华沙正式演出
    新华社华沙26日电　中国上海杂技团在结束了在波兰的波兹南和斯才新等地的演出以后，8月24日在华沙开始正式演出。
    波兰文化艺术部长库里卢克、外交部副部长纳希科夫斯基和波兰政府其他各部门的领导人员都观看了这天的演出。
    观众对杂技团演员的高超和优美的表演不断发出响亮的掌声，对十岁左右的儿童演员们的表演更是赞美不绝。
