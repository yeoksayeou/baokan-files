Title: Polish Youth League Central Council Holds Plenary Meeting to Discuss Current Tasks of the Youth League
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 25th. The Central Council of the Polish Youth League held its third plenary meeting from August 18th to 23rd. The main topics of the meeting were the situation among Polish youth and the current tasks of the Youth League.

	Central Council Chairman Jaworska delivered a report on this issue at the meeting. She spoke of the great significance of the Seventh Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party. She said that the main key task stipulated by the Party's Seventh Plenary Session is to fight for the democratization of our national life.

	She said that the Polish Youth League organizations should mobilize the best forces within their ranks and among the general youth to devote themselves to this struggle to fully realize the resolutions of the Seventh Plenary Session.

	Morawski, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, spoke at the meeting. He said that the Party hopes that the Polish Youth League will grow into an important political force, a creative force that should advance together with the Party in the struggle for democratization and for a better life for the working people.

### 波青年联盟中央理事会举行全体会议  讨论青年联盟目前的任务

1956-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    波青年联盟中央理事会举行全体会议
    讨论青年联盟目前的任务
    据新华社华沙25日电　波兰青年联盟中央理事会在八月十八日到二十三日举行了第三次全体会议。这次会议主要是讨论波兰青年中的情况和青年联盟目前的任务。
    中央理事会主席雅沃尔斯卡在会上就这个问题作了报告。她谈到了波兰统一工人党中央委员会第七次全体会议的重大意义。她说，党的第七次全体会议所规定的主要的关键任务就是为我们国家生活的民主化而斗争。
    她说，波兰青年联盟组织应当动员自己队伍中和一般青年中最好的力量投入这场争取完全实现七中全会决议的斗争。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记莫拉夫斯基在会上讲了话。他说，党希望波兰青年联盟成长起来，成为一个重要的政治力量，成为一种创造性的力量，在争取民主化和争取劳动人民的美好生活的斗争中，它应该同党一道前进。
