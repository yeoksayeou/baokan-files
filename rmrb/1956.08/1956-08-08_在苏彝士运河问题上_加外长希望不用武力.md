Title: On the Suez Canal Issue, Canadian Foreign Minister Hopes to Avoid Use of Force
Author:
Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch from Ottawa: Canadian Foreign Minister Pearson said in the House of Commons on the 6th that Canada hopes "not to use any kind of force" in the Suez Canal issue.

Pearson also stated that Canada will support the goals of the proposed Western international conference on the Suez Canal issue.

Pearson said that he does not believe there are policy differences between the British-French side and the American side on the Suez Canal issue. He also said that Canada hopes that the consistency of purpose between Britain and the United States on the Suez Canal issue will overcome the apparent differences in approach.

When asked whether Canada supports the British-French approach or the American approach, Pearson avoided answering the question directly. He said he hoped that "the three governments will be able to cooperate closely" at the international conference to discuss the canal issue.

### 在苏彝士运河问题上  加外长希望不用武力

1956-08-08
第5版()
专栏：

    在苏彝士运河问题上
    加外长希望不用武力
    新华社7日讯　渥太华消息：加拿大外交部长皮尔逊六日在众议院说，加拿大希望在苏彝士运河问题上“无须使用任何种类的武力”。
    皮尔逊还表示，加拿大将支持西方拟议举行的关于苏彝士运河问题的国际会议的目标。
    皮尔逊说，他不认为，在苏彝士运河问题上英法一方和美国另一方之间有政策上的分歧。他还说，加拿大希望，英国和美国在苏彝士运河问题上目的的一致性将克服做法上看来存在的分歧。
    有人问，加拿大是支持英法的做法呢，还是支持美国的做法。皮尔逊避不直接回答这个问题。他说，他希望在讨论运河问题的国际会议上“三国政府将能够密切合作”。
