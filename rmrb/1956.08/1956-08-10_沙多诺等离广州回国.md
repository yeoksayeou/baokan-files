Title: Sartono and Others Leave Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 9th: Sartono, Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament, his wife, and eight other parliamentarians concluded their ten-day visit to China and left Guangzhou by car today to return home.

Xin Zhi-chao, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, accompanied the distinguished guests to Shenzhen.

Among those who went to the Guangzhou Railway Station to see them off were Zhong Ming, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, Feng Bingquan, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and more than 20 representatives of various democratic parties.

At the station, Sartono spoke with those who came to see them off. He repeatedly expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome he received during his visit to China and asked Deputy Mayor Zhong Ming to convey his greetings to the people of Guangzhou.

### 沙多诺等离广州回国

1956-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    沙多诺等离广州回国
    新华社广州9日电　印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺和他的夫人以及国会议员等八人结束了在中国为期十天的访问，今天已经乘车离开广州回国。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长辛志超陪送贵宾到深圳。
    前往广州车站欢送的有广州市副市长钟明，政协广州市委员会副主席冯秉铨以及各民主党派代表二十多人。
    在车站上，沙多诺与送行者谈了话，他对在中国访问期间所受到的热烈欢迎再三表示感谢，并请钟明副市长转达他对广州市人民的问候。
