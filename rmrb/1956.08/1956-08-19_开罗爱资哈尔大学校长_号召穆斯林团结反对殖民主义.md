Title: Al-Azhar University President in Cairo Calls on Muslims to Unite Against Colonialism
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 16th (TASS) - According to Agence France-Presse, the Muslim leader of Egypt and President of Al-Azhar University in Cairo, Abdel Rahman Taj, called on Islamic people to unite and fight against colonialism.
    He said: "Colonial countries threaten the freedom and independence of Muslims." The people of Arab countries and Islamic countries "should unite and retaliate against any aggressive conspiracy."

### 开罗爱资哈尔大学校长  号召穆斯林团结反对殖民主义

1956-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    开罗爱资哈尔大学校长
    号召穆斯林团结反对殖民主义
    新华社讯　塔斯社巴黎十六日电：据法新社报道，埃及穆斯林领袖、开罗爱资哈尔大学校长阿卜杜勒·腊赫曼·塔季号召伊斯兰人民团结起来，为反对殖民主义而斗争。
    他说：“殖民国家威胁穆斯林的自由和独立”，阿拉伯国家和伊斯兰国家的人民“应当团结起来，对任何侵略阴谋给予回击。”
