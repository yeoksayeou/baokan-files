Title: Romanian Sports Commission Chairman and First Group of Men's Basketball Team Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, August 18th—Mr. M. Bodnaras, Chairman of the Sports and Athletics Committee of the Romanian Council of Ministers, and the first group of ten members of the Romanian National Men's Basketball Team, led by Li Teodorescu, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of China.
    They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Chinese basketball players. Also at the airport to welcome them was Nicolae Cioroiu, the Romanian Ambassador to China.
    After the members of the Romanian National Men's Basketball Team got off the plane, the first person to attract attention was Cocos, a player who is two meters and two centimeters tall, four centimeters taller than Ju Fenkan, the tallest Chinese basketball player who went to the airport to welcome them.
    The second group of ten members of the Romanian National Men's Basketball Team will arrive in Beijing in the next few days.
    When the Chinese Men's Basketball Team visited Romania in early July this year, they drew with the Romanian National Men's Basketball Team.
    The first game of the Romanian National Men's Basketball Team in China will be held next week, and the opponent will be the Beijing Men's Basketball Team.

### 罗体委主席和男子篮球队首批人员到京

1956-08-19
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    罗体委主席和男子篮球队首批人员到京
    据新华社18日讯　应邀来中国访问的罗马尼亚部长会议体育运动委员会主席马·波德纳拉斯和由利·特奥多雷斯库率领的罗马尼亚国家男子篮球队第一批人员十人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    他们在飞机场上受到中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠和中国的篮球运动员们的热烈欢迎。到飞机场欢迎他们的还有罗马尼亚驻中国大使尼古拉·乔洛尤。
    罗马尼亚国家男子篮球队队员下飞机以后，首先引人注意的是身高二公尺零二公分的队员科科斯，他比去飞机场欢迎的中国最高的篮球运动员鞠汾康还要高四公分。
    罗马尼亚国家男子篮球队的第二批人员十人将在日内到达北京。
    中国男子篮球队今年七月初在罗马尼亚访问的时候，曾经同罗马尼亚国家男子篮球队打成平局。
    罗马尼亚国家男子篮球队来中国的第一场比赛将在下星期举行，对手是北京市男子篮球队。
