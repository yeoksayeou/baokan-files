Author: Yan Mingxiang

    Shanxi Province Focuses on General Survey of Groundwater

    (Our report) One of the components of Shanxi Province's water conservancy plan is to massively develop underground irrigation water sources. On the basis of 1.62 million mu of well-irrigated land in the province in 1955, this will be increased to 14 million mu by 1962. In order to prepare for this development work, the Shanxi Provincial Water Conservancy Bureau has formed a hydrogeological survey team of eighteen people (in actual work, it will absorb thirty-six people from special districts and counties). Starting in early August, they will carry out a comprehensive survey of groundwater. This survey team will first focus on surveying forty-nine counties within the five large basins of southern Shanxi, central Shanxi, Lu'an, Xinzhou, and Yanbei, where groundwater potential is high, economic conditions are good, and development prospects are great. The survey is intended to initially clarify the suitable area for wells, the quality of water (bitter or fresh), and the source of groundwater. The survey is expected to take six months, ending in February 1957. (Yan Mingxiang)

### 山西省重点普查地下水
阎明祥
1956-08-11
第3版()
专栏：

    山西省重点普查地下水
    本报讯　山西省的水利规划内容的一项是：大量开发地下灌溉水源，使全省井水灌溉地在1955年的162万亩的基础上，到1962年增加到一千四百万亩。山西省水利局为了给这个开发工作作好准备，已组成了一支有十八人的水文地质调查队（在实际工作中要吸收专、县三十六人参加），由八月上旬开始进行地下水全面普查工作。这支调查队伍首先要在地下水潜力大、经济条件好，并且开发前途大的晋南、晋中、潞安、忻县和雁北五个大片盆地内的四十九个县内重点普查。准备通过普查，初步查清宜井面积、水质苦淡、地下水的来源等。这项普查工作的进行时间预计为六个月，到1957年二月结束。　（阎明祥）
