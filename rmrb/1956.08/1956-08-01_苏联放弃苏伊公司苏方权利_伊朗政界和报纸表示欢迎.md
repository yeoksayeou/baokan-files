Title: The Soviet Union Relinquishes its Rights in the Suez Company; Iranian Political Circles and Newspapers Welcome the Move
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, July 30th (TASS): The evening papers of July 29th published the news that the Soviet Union had relinquished its rights in the Soviet-side of the “Khaviar Khourian” Joint Stock Company of the Suez Canal and transferred them to Iran. According to the "World News," Foreign Minister Ardalan said, "After the King's visit to Moscow, the Soviet government took this step, which will have a good impact on relations between the two countries."

### 苏联放弃苏伊公司苏方权利  伊朗政界和报纸表示欢迎

1956-08-01
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联放弃苏伊公司苏方权利
    伊朗政界和报纸表示欢迎
    新华社31日讯　塔斯社德黑兰三十日讯：七月二十九日各晚报刊载苏联放弃苏伊“凯维尔·胡里安”股份公司中苏联方面的权利并把它转让给伊朗的消息。据“世界报”指出，外交大臣阿达兰说，“在国王访问莫斯科之后，苏联政府采取这一步骤，对两国的关系将起良好影响。”
