Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling to Visit Indonesia
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 11th - At the invitation of President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia and the Indonesian government, Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, left Kunming by special plane today for a visit to Jakarta. Sani, Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy in China, accompanied Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling to Jakarta.

Those who saw her off at the airport today included Xie Fuzhi, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Minghui, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, Chen Kang, Head of the Kunming Military Region, and more than one hundred people in charge of various democratic parties, organizations, and groups.

Consul Gao Hongling of Vietnam in Kunming and Miss Tao Kennu of the Consulate General of Burma in Kunming also went to the airport to see her off.

Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling arrived in Kunming on July 10th, and her visit to Indonesia was postponed due to illness. After a short period of recuperation, she has now recovered her health.

### 宋庆龄副委员长赴印度尼西亚

1956-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副委员长赴印度尼西亚
    新华社昆明11日电　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄，应印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺和印度尼西亚政府的邀请，已在今日乘专机离开昆明赴雅加达访问。印度尼西亚驻我国大使馆参赞萨尼，陪同宋庆龄副委员长赴雅加达。
    今天到机场欢送的有中共云南省委第一书记谢富治，云南省副省长刘明辉，昆明军区首长陈康，和各民主党派、机关、团体的负责人 等一百多人。
    越南驻昆明领事高鸿岭，缅甸驻昆明总领事馆陶肯努小姐也到机场欢送。
    宋庆龄副委员长是七月十日到达昆明的，因病延期访问印度尼西亚。经过短期休养后，现在已恢复健康。
