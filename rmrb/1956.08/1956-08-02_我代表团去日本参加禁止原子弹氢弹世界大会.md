Title: Our Delegation Leaves for Japan to Attend the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs
Author:
Date: 1956-08-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 1st—At the invitation of the Japanese side, the members of the Chinese delegation attending the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, to be held in Tokyo and Nagasaki from August 6th to 11th, departed from Beijing for Japan on July 28th and 31st, respectively.
    The head of the delegation is Xu Guangping; the members are Wang Yunsheng, Cao Yu, and Kang Yonghe.

### 我代表团去日本参加禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会

1956-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我代表团去日本参加禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会
    据新华社1日讯　应日本方面邀请参加八月六日到十一日在东京和长崎举行的第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团人员，已经在七月二十八日和三十一日先后由北京动身去日本。
    代表团团长是许广平；团员是王芸生、曹禺、康永和。
