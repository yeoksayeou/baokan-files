Title: Typhoon Center to Reach Near Kaifeng Today
Author:
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon emergency alert at 8:00 PM on August 2nd: At 5:00 PM today, the center of the powerful typhoon was located near Wuhu. The maximum wind force near the typhoon center is above level 12, which is 40 meters per second. Currently, the typhoon center is moving in a northwest direction at a speed of 18-24 kilometers per hour. It is expected that the typhoon center will reach near Kaifeng at 2:00 PM tomorrow. From today to tomorrow, there will be strong winds of level 8-11 in Anhui, Henan, Jiangsu, southern Shandong, the northern part of the East China Sea, and the western part of the Yellow Sea. There will be strong winds of level 7-9 in northern Zhejiang, coastal areas of Zhejiang, and northern coastal areas of Fujian. There will be strong winds of level 6-8 in Hubei Province, Dongting Lake, and Poyang Lake areas. The wind force in the northern part of the Yellow Sea, the Bohai Sea, northern Shandong, and eastern Hebei will also increase to level 6-8. There will be heavy rain in eastern and northern Henan, the entire province of Anhui, western Jiangsu, southeastern Shanxi, Shandong, and the Weiyun River area in southern Hebei, with the heaviest rainfall in the central Huai River basin, where there will be torrential rain.

### 台风中心今天将到开封附近

1956-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    台风中心今天将到开封附近
    据新华社2日讯　中央气象科学研究所八月二日下午八点发布台风紧急警报：今天下午五点，强大台风中心位置在芜湖附近，台风中心附近最大风力在十二级以上，就是每秒四十公尺。目前台风中心正以每小时18—24公里的速度向西北方向移动。预计明天下午二时，台风中心将到达开封附近。今天到明天，安徽、河南、江苏、山东南部、东海北部和黄海西部海面有8—11级大风。浙江北部、浙江沿海及福建北部沿海有7—9级大风。湖北省、洞庭湖和鄱阳湖一带有6—8级大风。黄海北部、渤海海面、山东北部和河北东部等地区风力也将加强到6—8级。河南东部和北部、安徽全省、江苏西部、山西东南部、山东和河北南部卫运河一带有暴雨出现，其中以淮河流域中部雨量最大，将有大暴雨。
