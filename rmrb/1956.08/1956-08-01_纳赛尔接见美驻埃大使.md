Title: Nasser Receives U.S. Ambassador to Egypt
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 31st: Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser received the U.S. Ambassador to Egypt, Mr. Byroade, on July 30th and held talks with him for ninety minutes.

The meeting was held at the request of Gamal Abdel Nasser.

### 纳赛尔接见美驻埃大使

1956-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔接见美驻埃大使
    新华社31日讯　开罗消息：埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔在七月三十日接见了美国驻埃及大使拜娄德，并且同他会谈了九十分钟。
    这次会谈是根据阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔的要求而举行的。
