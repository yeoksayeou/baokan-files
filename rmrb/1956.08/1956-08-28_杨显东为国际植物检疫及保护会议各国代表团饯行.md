Title: Yang Xiandong Hosts Farewell Banquet for Delegations to the International Plant Quarantine and Protection Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-08-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: Yang Xiandong, head of the Chinese delegation to the Eighth International Plant Quarantine and Plant Protection Conference, hosted a simple banquet tonight to bid farewell to the delegations from various countries who are about to return home. At tonight's banquet, the guests and hosts frequently rose to toast the victorious success of the Eighth International Plant Quarantine and Plant Protection Conference, the development of agricultural production and plant protection work in fraternal countries, and the increasing solidarity and friendship between the peoples of all countries.

### 杨显东为国际植物检疫及保护会议各国代表团饯行

1956-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    杨显东为国际植物检疫及保护会议各国代表团饯行
    据新华社27日讯　参加第八届国际植物检疫及植物保护会议的中国代表团团长杨显东今晚举行便宴，为即将返国的各国代表团饯行。在今晚的宴会上，宾主们都频频起立，为第八届国际植物检疫及植物保护会议的胜利成功、为各兄弟国家农业生产和植物保护工作的发展、为各国人民之间日益增进的团结和友谊干杯。
