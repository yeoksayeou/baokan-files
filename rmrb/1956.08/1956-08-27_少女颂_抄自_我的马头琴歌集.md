Title: Ode to a Girl – Copied from "My Morin Khuur Song Collection"
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 7

    Ode to a Girl
    ——Copied from "My Morin Khuur Song Collection"
    Tian Jian
    I.
    Sunrise over the prairie,
    Shining like gold.
    Here is a maiden,
    Rising earlier than the lark.
    She wears a red headscarf,
    She carries a green kettle,
    Running toward a spring,
    People call her: "Lark."
    II.
    "Lark" she comes to the water's edge,
    Like a flame.
    Her face is reflected in the water,
    Splashing water follows,
    When she drinks a mouthful of water,
    She is so happy.
    Listen to the joyous laughter,
    Laughter awakens the prairie.
    III.
    Ah, maiden, I see you,
    Climbing nimbly up the drilling tower,
    Waving from the top of the tower,
    Saying hello to Baiyun Obo. (Note)
    Your hand is a key,
    Opening the door of the prairie.
    Happiness is calling you,
    And you have found happiness.
    IV.
    Ah, maiden, I see you,
    Standing on a high peak,
    White clouds above your head,
    Piles of stone mines beneath your feet,
    The ore of our motherland,
    This is a treasure of the world,
    You, lark of the world,
    Flying over its top.
    V.
    On top of a high peak,
    Your colorful shirt is fluttering,
    Like a rainbow shining on the prairie,
    Shining on the tents where you live,
    The spring water in the kettle is ringing,
    The ore in your hand is shining,
    You, daughter of the motherland,
    Take this place as your second home.
    VI.
    Treating spring water as milk,
    Putting wildflowers on your head,
    A kettle, a core sample,
    Accompanying your thoughts and fantasies,
    Accompanying you climbing the drilling tower,
    Supporting that iron drill rod,
    On the high tower of the prairie,
    Ah, maiden, you are the sun!
          July 1956, sent from the prairie

    [Note] Baiyun Obo is a large mine in the Inner Mongolian prairie. According to research by many experts, its iron ore is judged to be one of the best iron ores in the world.

### 少女颂——抄自“我的马头琴歌集”
田间
1956-08-27
第7版()
专栏：

    少女颂
    ——抄自“我的马头琴歌集”
    田间
    一
    草原上朝霞升起，
    像金子一样闪耀。
    在这里有一位少女，
    比云雀起得还早。
    她披着红的头巾，
    她提着绿的水壶，
    向一道泉水奔跑，
    人们都叫她：“云雀”
    二
    “云雀”她来到水边，
    如同是一支火焰。
    她的脸映在水上，
    水花也跟着飞溅，
    当她喝下一口水，
    她是多么幸福。
    你听那欢乐的笑声，
    笑声唤醒了草原。
    三
    啊，少女，我看见你，
    矫健地攀上钻塔，
    在塔顶上把手一招，
    向白云鄂博问好。（注）
    你的手是一把钥匙。
    把草原的门打开了。
    幸福在呼唤着你，
    你也把幸福找到。
    四
    啊，少女，我看见你，
    站在一座高峰上，
    头上是朵朵白云，
    脚下是垒垒石矿，
    我们祖国的矿石，
    这是世界上的珍宝，
    你呀，人间的云雀，
    在它的头顶上飞着。
    五
    在一座高峰之上，
    你的彩衫在飘扬，
    像一道长虹照着草原，
    照着你们住的篷帐，
    壶里的泉水发响，
    手上的矿石闪亮，
    你呀，祖国的姑娘，
    把这里当做第二家乡。
    六
    把泉水当做奶汁，
    把野花插在头上，
    一把水壶，一块岩芯，
    伴着你思索幻想，
    伴着你攀登钻塔，
    扶起那铁的钻杆，
    在草原高高的塔顶上，
    啊，少女，你是太阳！
          1956年7月，寄自草原
    〔注〕白云鄂博是内蒙草原上的一座大矿山，它的铁矿，据许多专家研究以后，判断为是世界上最好的铁矿之一。
