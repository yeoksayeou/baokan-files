Title: Shepilov Talks with Dulles
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, 16th: TASS reports: Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov met with U.S. Secretary of State Dulles on August 15th and had a lengthy conversation with him.

### 谢皮洛夫同杜勒斯交谈

1956-08-17
第5版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫同杜勒斯交谈
    新华社16日讯　据塔斯社伦敦讯：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫八月十五日会见美国国务卿杜勒斯，并且同他作了长时间的交谈。
