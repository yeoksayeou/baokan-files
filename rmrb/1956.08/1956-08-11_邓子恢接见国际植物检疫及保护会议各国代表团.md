Title: Deng Zihui Receives Delegations from Various Countries to the International Plant Quarantine and Protection Conference
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Zihui received this morning all the members of the delegations from Albania, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union , the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and other countries attending the Eighth International Plant Quarantine and Plant Protection Conference.

### 邓子恢接见国际植物检疫及保护会议各国代表团

1956-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    邓子恢接见国际植物检疫及保护会议各国代表团
    据新华社10日讯　国务院副总理邓子恢今天上午接见了参加第八届国际植物检疫及植物保护会议的阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联、越南民主共和国等国的代表团全体人员。
