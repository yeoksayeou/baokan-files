Title: Greek Parliamentary Delegation Invited to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Athens, August 23rd: TASS reports that the Greek parliamentary delegation, led by former Greek Minister of Finance, Mr. Eftaxias, departed for Moscow on August 22nd. The delegation is visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

### 希腊议会代表团应邀赴苏联访问

1956-08-24
第5版()
专栏：

    希腊议会代表团应邀赴苏联访问
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社雅典讯：由希腊前财政大臣伊弗塔西亚斯领导的希腊议会代表团在八月二十二日启程飞往莫斯科。代表团是应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请，前往苏联访问的。
