Title: Song Qingling Meets with U Nu
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 26th: Song Qingling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, visited U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma and former Prime Minister, at the Chinese Embassy in Burma on the evening of August 24th.

Song Qingling made a short stop in Rangoon on her way back to China after visiting Indonesia.

They had a long and friendly conversation and dined together.

Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, also participated in the conversation and dined with them.

### 宋庆龄曾会晤吴努

1956-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄曾会晤吴努
    据新华社仰光26日电　缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟主席、前总理吴努在8月24日晚上到中国驻缅甸大使馆访问中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄。
    宋庆龄在访问印度尼西亚后回国途中，在仰光作短期的停留。
    他们进行了长时间的友好谈话，并且共进晚餐。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏也参加了谈话，并且和他们一起进晚餐。
