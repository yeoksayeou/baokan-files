Title: Xinjiang Establishes National Song and Dance Troupe and Drama Troupe
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    The Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region's national song and dance troupe and drama troupe were established in Urumqi on the 20th. The song and dance troupe and drama troupe include 190 actors of Uygur, Han, Kazakh, Uzbek, Tatar, and other ethnic groups.

    The song and dance troupe and drama troupe were established on the basis of the original song and dance team and drama team of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Cultural Troupe and the drama team of the Xinjiang Military Region Cultural Troupe of the People's Liberation Army. Among the actors in the song and dance troupe are female vocalist Pasha Kyzy, who won an award at the Warsaw World Youth Festival last year, and the famous Uygur composer Yushanjiang.

### 新疆成立民族歌舞团话剧团

1956-08-24
第7版()
专栏：

    新疆成立民族歌舞团话剧团
    据新华社讯　新疆维吾尔自治区各民族歌舞团和话剧团二十日在乌鲁木齐成立。参加歌舞团和话剧团的有一百九十名维吾尔、汉、哈萨克、乌孜别克、塔塔尔等民族的演员。
    歌舞团和话剧团是在原新疆维吾尔自治区文工团歌舞队、话剧队和人民解放军新疆军区文工团话剧队的基础上建立起来的。歌舞团演员中，有去年在华沙世界青年联欢节中受奖的女歌唱家帕夏·克孜和著名维吾尔族作曲家雨山江等。
