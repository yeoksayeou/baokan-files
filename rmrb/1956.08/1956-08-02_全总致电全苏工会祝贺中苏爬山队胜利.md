Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends a Telegram to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions to Congratulate the Victory of the Sino-Soviet Mountaineering Team
Author:
Date: 1956-08-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions today sent a telegram to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions. The telegram said: Chinese and Soviet mountaineers, closely united and cooperating with the spirit of perseverance to overcome all difficulties, climbed to the summit of Muztagh Ata. We extend our congratulations on the victory of this mountaineering expedition.

### 全总致电全苏工会祝贺中苏爬山队胜利

1956-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    全总致电全苏工会祝贺中苏爬山队胜利
    新华社1日讯　中华全国总工会今天打电报给全苏工会中央理事会。电报中说：中、苏两国爬山运动员亲密地团结合作，以坚韧不拔克服一切困难的精神，攀登到慕士塔格山顶峰。特祝贺这次爬山的胜利。
