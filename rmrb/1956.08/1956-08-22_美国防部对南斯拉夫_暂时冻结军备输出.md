Title: U.S. Department of Defense Temporarily Freezes Arms Exports to Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, August 21st: A U.S. Department of Defense spokesman said on August 18th that the Department of Defense had decided to freeze all exports of jet fighters and other military equipment to Yugoslavia until Eisenhower makes a top-level decision.

### 美国防部对南斯拉夫  暂时冻结军备输出

1956-08-22
第6版()
专栏：

    美国防部对南斯拉夫
    暂时冻结军备输出
    据新华社21日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国防部发言人八月十八日说，在艾森豪威尔未作出最高级的决定以前，国防部已经决定冻结对南斯拉夫的喷气战斗机和其他军事装备的一切输出。
