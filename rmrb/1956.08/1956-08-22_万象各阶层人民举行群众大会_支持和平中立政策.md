Title: Mass Rally of People from All Walks of Life in Vientiane Supports Policy of Peace and Neutrality
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 21st: Vientiane dispatch: On August 17th, a mass rally of nearly 5,000 people from all walks of life was held in Vientiane, the capital of Laos, to support the policy of peace and neutrality. Participants included many government ministers, members of parliament, military officers, and police. The mass rally, convened by the Lao Committee for Supporting the Policy of Peace and Neutrality, took place two days before the departure of the Lao Royal Government delegation led by Prince Souvanna Phouma for a visit to China.

    Mr. Phoui Sananikone, President of the Lao National Assembly, delivered a speech at the rally on the issue of peace and neutrality and on the achievements of the talks between the Royal Government and the delegation of the Lao Fighting Forces. His speech was warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 万象各阶层人民举行群众大会  支持和平中立政策

1956-08-22
第6版()
专栏：

    万象各阶层人民举行群众大会
    支持和平中立政策
    新华社河内21日电　万象消息：老挝首都万象八月十七日举行了一次各阶层人民近五千人参加的支持和平中立政策的群众大会，参加大会的还包括许多政府部长、议员、军官和警察。这个在梭发那·富马亲王率领的老挝王国政府代表团动身前往中国访问之前两天举行的群众大会是由老挝拥护和平中立政策委员会召集的。
    老挝国民大会主席彭·苏发那冯在大会上作了关于和平中立问题和王国政府同寮国战斗部队代表团会谈的成果的演说。他的演说受到听众的热烈欢迎。
