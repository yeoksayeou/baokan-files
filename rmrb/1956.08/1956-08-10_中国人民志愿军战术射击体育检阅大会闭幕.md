Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Tactical Shooting Sports Reviewing Tournament Concludes
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 8th - The Chinese People's Volunteer Army's all-army tactical, shooting, and sports reviewing tournament concluded on the 7th. Representatives from various branches of service and units participating in the tournament successively performed and competed in more than 30 events, and summarized and exchanged experiences through mutual visits and discussions.

At the closing ceremony, General Wang Yunrui awarded twenty-nine banners to the collective units that won in the competition. Twenty-four outstanding shooters and more than thirty athletes who reached the national second and third-level athlete standards also received material rewards.

The conference also presented banners and gifts to the Korean People's Army team, which was invited to observe the conference and participated in friendly matches with the Volunteer Army team.

General Ren Rong made a summary at the meeting, pointing out that through this review, it was seen that the training achievements made by the troops during this summer training period were significant. He called on all officers and soldiers to strive to overcome the shortcomings of dogmatism and formalism in military teaching and further improve the training quality of the troops in the future.

### 中国人民志愿军战术射击体育检阅大会闭幕

1956-08-10
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中国人民志愿军战术射击体育检阅大会闭幕
    新华社平壤8日电　中国人民志愿军全军战术、射击、体育检阅大会在七日结束。参加大会的各兵种、各部队的代表队先后共进行了三十多种项目的表演和比赛，并且以互相访问、座谈等方式总结和交流了经验。
    在闭幕式上，王蕴瑞将军把二十九面锦旗授给在比赛中获得优胜的集体单位。二十四名优秀射手和三十多名达到国家二级和三级运动员标准的运动员也分别获得了物质奖励。
    大会还赠给应邀到会观摩，并且和志愿军球队进行友谊比赛的朝鲜人民军球队以锦旗和礼品。
    任荣将军在会上作了总结，他指出，通过这次检阅，看到了部队在今年夏季训练期间所取得的训练成绩是显著的。他号召全军官兵努力克服在军事教学中的教条主义和形式主义缺点，进一步提高今后部队的训练素质。
