Title: Marshal Peng Dehuai Receives General Phoumi Nosavan

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st - Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, received General Phoumi Nosavan, Chief of General Staff of the Royal Lao Armed Forces, this afternoon. Also received were Mr. Seunsom Nouphanxay, Secretary-General of the Prime Minister's Office, Captain Sakon Sananikone, Director of the Ministry of Defense Office, and Lieutenant Thao Ekkharat, Director of the Armed Forces General Staff Office.

Present at the reception were General Huang Kecheng, Vice Minister of National Defense, General Chen Geng, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang, Deputy Commander of the Air Force, and Lieutenant General Luo Shunchu, Deputy Commander of the Navy.

### 彭德怀元帅接见巴探马冯将军

1956-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀元帅接见巴探马冯将军
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，今天下午接见了老挝王国武装部队总参谋长顺吞·巴探马冯将军。同时被接见的还有首相府秘书长薛苏·纳·香巴萨先生、国防部办公室主任萨昆·萨纳尼空上尉、武装部队总参谋部办公室主任陶·埃卡拉特中尉。
    接见的时候在座的，有国防部副部长黄克诚大将，中国人民解放军副总参谋长陈赓大将、总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将、空军副司令员王秉璋中将、海军副司令员罗舜初中将。
