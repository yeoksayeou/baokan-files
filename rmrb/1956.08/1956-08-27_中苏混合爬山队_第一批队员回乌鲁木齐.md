Title: Sino-Soviet Mixed Mountaineering Team: First Group of Team Members Returns to Urumqi
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 4

Sports News

    Sino-Soviet Mixed Mountaineering Team
    First Group of Team Members Returns to Urumqi
    Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, August 26th - The first group of personnel from the Sino-Soviet mixed mountaineering team that victoriously climbed Muztagh Ata, including team leader Beretsky, deputy team leaders Shi Zhanchun and Kuzmin, and twenty-seven Chinese and Soviet team members, arrived in Urumqi by plane from Kashgar this afternoon. Gao Jinxian, Vice Chairman of the Trade Union Federation of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, Zhao Lingyun, Deputy Director of the Sports Commission of the Autonomous Region, and Li Shubin, Deputy Director of the Sports Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and other responsible personnel welcomed them at the airport.
    This evening, the Autonomous Region's Federation of Trade Unions held a banquet to welcome the mountaineering team members back.
    In addition, the second group of the mountaineering team—fifteen Chinese team members—will arrive in Urumqi tomorrow.

### 中苏混合爬山队  第一批队员回乌鲁木齐

1956-08-27
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中苏混合爬山队
    第一批队员回乌鲁木齐
    据新华社乌鲁木齐26日电　攀登慕士塔格山胜利归来的中、苏混合爬山队第一批人员：队长别列次基，副队长史占春、库兹明和二十七个中、苏队员，今天下午乘飞机从喀什到达乌鲁木齐市。新疆维吾尔自治区的工会联合会副主席高进先，自治区体育运动委员会副主任赵凌云和中华全国总工会体育运动部副部长栗树彬等负责人员都到机场欢迎。
    今天晚上，自治区工会联合会举行宴会，为爬山队队员们洗尘。
    另外，爬山队的第二批人员——十五名中国队员，将在明天到达乌鲁木齐。
