Title: Public Rallies in India and Pakistan Support Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 14th: Public rallies were held in India to support the Egyptian government's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company.

    A mass meeting held in Calcutta on August 11th unanimously passed a resolution calling on other underdeveloped Asian countries to learn from the Egyptian event. The resolution urged the Indian government to take similar bold steps for the welfare and economic development of the nation.

    A mass meeting jointly convened by the Bombay Peace Committee and the Bombay City Youth League in Bombay on August 10th passed a resolution welcoming Prime Minister Nehru's statement on the Suez Canal issue and expressing hope that India would firmly stand with Egypt at the upcoming London conference.

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, August 14th: Political circles and people from all walks of life in Pakistan warmly support Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal.

    Thousands of students held a demonstration in Karachi on August 13th, marching past the Egyptian Embassy in Pakistan, chanting "Long live Nasser" and "The Suez Canal belongs to Egypt."

    Representatives of various political parties, trade unions, social, commercial, and religious organizations held a meeting at the Karachi People's League office on August 11th. The meeting decided to call for a general strike in support of Egypt's position on the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company.

    On the same day, a public gathering convened by the Pakistan Anti-Merger Front in Peshawar passed a resolution condemning the attitude of Western countries towards Egypt and warning that if Western countries take any military action against Egypt, the people of Pakistan will not stand idly by.

    The resolution also called on the people of the country to boycott British and French goods as a protest against their attitude towards Egypt.

    The Free Pakistan Party convened a public gathering in Lahore on August 9th. Speakers at the meeting condemned the war preparations of Western countries and called on the people and government of Pakistan to wholeheartedly support Egypt.

### 印巴两国公众集会支援埃及

1956-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

    印巴两国公众集会支援埃及
    据新华社新德里14日电　印度公众举行集会支持埃及政府把苏彝士运河公司收归国有。
    八月十一日在加尔各答举行的群众大会一致通过决议，要求其他不发达的亚洲国家从埃及的事件中吸取教训。决议要求印度政府为了国家的福利和经济发展而采取类似的果敢步骤。
    孟买和平委员会和孟买市青年联盟八月十日在孟买联合召开的群众大会通过决议，欢迎尼赫鲁总理就苏彝士运河问题所发表的声明，并且希望印度将在即将举行的伦敦会议上坚决站在埃及方面。
    据新华社喀喇蚩14日电　巴基斯坦政界和各地人民热烈支持埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有。
    几千个学生八月十三日在喀喇蚩举行了示威游行，游行队伍通过在埃及驻巴基斯坦大使馆门前高呼“纳赛尔万岁”和“苏彝士运河属于埃及”。
    各政治党派、工会、社会、商业和宗教团体的代表们八月十一日在喀喇蚩人民联盟办事处举行了会议。会议决定号召进行总罢工，以支持埃及在苏彝士运河公司国有化问题上的立场。
    同日，巴基斯坦反合并阵线在白沙瓦召集的一次公众集会通过了一项决议，谴责西方国家对埃及的态度，并且警告说，如果西方国家对埃及采取任何军事行动的话，巴基斯坦人民将不会作旁观者。
    决议还要求全国人民抵制英国和法国的货物，作为表示反对它们对埃及的态度的抗议。
    自由巴基斯坦党八月九日在拉合尔召集了一次公众集会。会上发言的人谴责西方国家的战争准备，要求巴基斯坦人民和政府全心全意地支持埃及。
