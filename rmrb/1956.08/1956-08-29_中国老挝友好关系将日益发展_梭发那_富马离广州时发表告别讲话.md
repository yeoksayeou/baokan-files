Title: Chinese-Lao Friendship Will Grow Steadily: Prince Souvanna Phouma Issues Farewell Speech Upon Leaving Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 28th: Lao Kingdom Prime Minister Prince Souvanna Phouma said at Guangzhou Airport today, upon his departure, that he firmly believes the friendly relations between China and Laos will develop in the future, and hopes that the two countries will join hands to work to maintain peace in Asia and the Far East. His speech was met with thunderous applause from the more than three thousand people who came to see him off.

The Lao Prime Minister also stated that although Laos is a small country, it can also promote mutual understanding between large countries, and Laos will make its efforts in this regard. He expressed his deep gratitude to the Chinese government and people for their warm and friendly hospitality, and asked those seeing him off to convey their highest respect to the great leader of the Chinese people.

Prince Souvanna Phouma and the other distinguished guests arrived at the airport at 7:30 this morning. At the airport, Guangzhou citizens who came to see them off applauded and cheered enthusiastically, throwing colorful paper flowers and streamers. The band played the national anthems of Laos and China. Prince Souvanna Phouma, accompanied by Chen Rutang, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, reviewed the guard of honor lined up at the airport.

After leaving Guangzhou, the delegation of the Lao Kingdom Government will visit Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Tran Cao Thanh, Second Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, accompanied the distinguished guests.

### 中国老挝友好关系将日益发展  梭发那·富马离广州时发表告别讲话

1956-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中国老挝友好关系将日益发展
    梭发那·富马离广州时发表告别讲话
    新华社广州28日电　老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王今天在离开广州机场时说，他深信中国和老挝两国的友好关系在将来一定会得到发展，并且希望两国携起手来，为维护亚洲和远东和平而努力。他的讲话博得了三千多欢送的群众暴风雨般的掌声。
    这位老挝首相还说，老挝虽然是小国，但是它也可以促进大国之间的互相了解，在这方面，老挝将要作出他的努力。他对中国政府和人民的热情友好的招待表示深切的感谢，他请欢送者向中国人民的伟大领袖转达他们的最高敬意。
    梭发那·富马亲王和其他的贵宾是在今天上午七点三十分到达机场的。在机场上，欢送贵宾的广州市民向他们热烈鼓掌欢呼，抛着五色纷飞的纸花和纸条。乐队奏着老挝和中国的国歌。梭发那·富马亲王由广东省副省长陈汝棠等陪同检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    老挝王国政府代表团离开广州后，将到越南民主共和国首都河内访问，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆二等秘书陈高诚陪同贵宾前往。
