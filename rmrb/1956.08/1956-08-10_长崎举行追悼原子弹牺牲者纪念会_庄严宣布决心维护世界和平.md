Title: Nagasaki Holds Memorial Service for Atomic Bomb Victims, Solemnly Announces Determination to Safeguard World Peace
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 9th, Nagasaki: The people of Nagasaki solemnly declared today their determination to safeguard world peace, to commemorate the eleventh anniversary of their city's suffering from the American atomic bomb.

This morning, more than 10,000 people held a peace memorial service in the center of the atomic bomb explosion to mourn the atomic bomb victims. Participants included citizens, families of atomic bomb victims, and representatives from various countries attending the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs.

Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama and leaders of India, Burma, and Ceylon sent telegrams to the memorial service, expressing sympathy to the people of Nagasaki and calling for the prohibition of atomic weapons.

At 11:02 AM—the tragic moment eleven years ago when Nagasaki suffered the American atomic bomb attack—the sirens of ships and trains, and the bells of churches and temples, sounded together, and all participants and approximately 300,000 residents of the city observed a minute of silence to mourn the more than 70,000 atomic bomb victims. At this moment, many doves symbolizing peace flew into the sky.

Finally, the Mayor of Nagasaki, on behalf of the people of Nagasaki, issued a peace declaration. The declaration stated that the people of Nagasaki have been hoping for lasting world peace for eleven years. Today, we reiterate our pledge that we are determined to realize a lasting world peace based on reconciliation and trust.

### 长崎举行追悼原子弹牺牲者纪念会  庄严宣布决心维护世界和平

1956-08-10
第5版()
专栏：

    长崎举行追悼原子弹牺牲者纪念会
    庄严宣布决心维护世界和平
    新华社9日讯　长崎消息：长崎市人民今天庄严宣布决心维护世界和平，以纪念他们的城市遭受美国原子弹轰炸十一周年。
    今天上午，一万多人在原子弹爆炸的中心区举行了追悼原子弹牺牲者的和平纪念会。参加纪念会的有市民、原子弹牺牲者的遗属和出席第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的各国代表。
    日本首相鸠山一郎和印度、缅甸、锡兰等国家领导人向纪念会拍了电报，向长崎人民表示同情，并且呼吁禁止原子武器。
    十一点零二分——长崎市在十一年前的今天遭受美国原子弹轰炸的悲惨时刻，轮船、火车的汽笛声和教堂、寺庙的钟声一齐响起来，全体与会者和全市约三十万居民一齐默哀一分钟，悼念七万多原子弹牺牲者。这时，许多象征和平的鸽子飞向了天空。
    最后，长崎市市长代表长崎市人民发表了一项和平宣言。宣言说，长崎人民十一年来一直在希望世界和平能够成为持久的和平。今天，我们再次保证，我们决心要使基于和解和信任的世界持久和平实现。
