Title: Ho Chi Minh Receives Guangxi Ethnic Delegation

Author:

Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 21st: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received the Chinese Guangxi Province Ethnic Delegation led by Qin Yingji this morning. The delegation was invited to attend the recent founding大会 of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region.

During the meeting, the delegation also presented local specialty gifts from Guangxi to President Ho Chi Minh.

### 胡志明接见我广西民族代表团

1956-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明接见我广西民族代表团
    新华社河内21日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明今天上午接见了由覃应机率领的中国广西省民族代表团。这个代表团是应邀来参加最近举行的越北自治区成立大会的。
    在接见的时候，代表团还将广西的土产礼品赠送给胡志明主席。
