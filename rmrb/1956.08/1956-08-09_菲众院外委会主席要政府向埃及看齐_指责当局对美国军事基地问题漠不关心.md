Title: Philippine House Foreign Affairs Committee Chairman Urges Government to Emulate Egypt, Accuses Authorities of Indifference to US Military Base Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-08-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 7th - Philippine House Foreign Affairs Committee Chairman Miguel Cuenco yesterday criticized the Magsaysay government for its "indifference" to the issue of US military bases in the Philippines. He said that the Egyptian government's nationalization of the Suez Canal was precisely sufficient to illustrate this point.

Cuenco said: "Egyptian patriots and politicians have spent many years studying the complex issue of the Suez Canal." He accused Philippine government leaders of being "careless, impatient, and inattentive when studying our national problems, especially those related to our foreign policy."

The congressman, who has advocated for the abolition of US military bases and the implementation of a policy of peaceful coexistence, said that the boundaries of any US base in the Philippines have not been clearly defined, thus causing many disputes.

### 菲众院外委会主席要政府向埃及看齐  指责当局对美国军事基地问题漠不关心

1956-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    菲众院外委会主席要政府向埃及看齐
    指责当局对美国军事基地问题漠不关心
    新华社7日讯　马尼拉消息：菲律宾众议院外交委员会主席米格尔·库恩柯昨天抨击麦格赛赛政府对美国在菲律宾的军事基地问题“漠不关心”。他说，埃及政府把苏彝士运河收归国有这件事恰恰足以说明这一点。
    库恩柯说：“埃及的爱国分子和政治家们花费了许多年的精力来研究苏彝士运河这个复杂问题”。他指责菲律宾政府的领袖们“在研究我们的国家问题、特别是那些同我们的外交政策有关的问题的时候，是马马虎虎的，既没有耐心，而且也不当心”。
    这位曾经主张取消美国军事基地和实行和平共处政策的国会议员说，美国在菲律宾的任何基地都没有把界线划分清楚，因此引起了许多争执。
