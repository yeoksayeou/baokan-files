Title: Nasser Postpones Visit to Soviet Union

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 11th, TASS Cairo, August 11th: The Middle East News Agency reported that, it is understood that, in light of the current situation, Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser has decided to postpone his visit to the Soviet Union for a period of time. He was originally scheduled to visit Moscow on August 14th.

### 纳赛尔推迟访苏日期

1956-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔推迟访苏日期
    新华社11日讯　塔斯社开罗十一日讯：中东通讯社报道，据悉，埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔鉴于目前的局势，决定把访问苏联的时间推迟了一些。他是原定在八月十四日到莫斯科进行访问的。
