Title: Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma in Beijing (Pictures)
Author: Zou Jiandong, Niu Weiyu
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 6

Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma in Beijing

    Top Left: Chairman Mao Zedong views the gifts presented by Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma.
    Top Center: Premier Zhou Enlai, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma (center), and Ambassador R.K. Nehru (left) at a banquet held by the Indian Ambassador to China.
    Top Right: Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma and others visit Guangji Temple on August 22nd.
    Bottom Left: At the welcoming rally held by people from all walks of life in the capital on August 22nd, Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen presents a banner to Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma on behalf of the people of Beijing.
    Bottom Right: Literary and art workers from Beijing put on a wonderful performance for the distinguished guests at the welcoming rally. After the performance, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Mayor Peng Zhen, thanked the literary and art workers of Beijing and posed for a group photo.
                Xinhua News Agency reporters Zou Jiandong and Niu Weiyu

### 梭发那·富马首相在北京（图片）
邹健东　牛畏予
1956-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    梭发那·富马首相在北京
    上左　毛泽东主席观看梭发那·富马首相所赠的礼品。
    上中　周恩来总理、梭发那·富马首相（中）、拉·库·尼赫鲁大使（左）在印度驻华大使举行的宴会上。
    上右　梭发那·富马首相等八月二十二日在广济寺参观。
    下左　在首都各界人民八月二十二日的欢迎大会上，北京市市长彭真代表北京市人民向梭发那·富马首相献旗。
    下右　北京市的文艺工作者在欢迎大会上为贵宾们作了精彩演出。演出结束后，梭发那·富马首相在周恩来总理、彭真市长陪同下，向北京的文艺工作者致谢并合影留念。
                新华社记者　邹健东　牛畏予摄
