Title: Ceylon Government to Send Delegation to Visit China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, August 2nd: TASS, Delhi, August 2nd: Colombo dispatch: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike said at a press conference on August 1st that a Ceylon government delegation will depart for Moscow and Beijing on August 27th to study the prospects of diplomatic and trade relations between Ceylon and the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China.

The delegation will be led by Claude Corea, Ceylon's High Commissioner in London.

### 锡兰政府将派代表团访问中苏

1956-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰政府将派代表团访问中苏
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社德里二日讯：科伦坡消息，锡兰总理班达拉奈克八月一日在记者招待会上说，锡兰政府代表团将在八月二十七日动身前往莫斯科和北京，研究锡兰同苏联和中华人民共和国的外交关系和贸易关系的前景。
    代表团将由锡兰驻伦敦高级专员克劳德·柯里亚率领。
