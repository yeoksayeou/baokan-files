Title: The Soviet Union and Afghanistan Sign New Economic Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul: Afghanistan and the Soviet Union signed another economic agreement in Kabul on August 14. According to this new agreement, the Soviet Union will help Afghanistan build power stations in Puli Khumri and other cities.

### 苏联同阿富汗签订新的经济协定

1956-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联同阿富汗签订新的经济协定
    新华社讯　喀布尔消息：阿富汗同苏联八月十四日在喀布尔签订了另一项经济协定。根据这项新的协定，苏联将帮助阿富汗在普利胡姆里和其他城市修建发电站。
