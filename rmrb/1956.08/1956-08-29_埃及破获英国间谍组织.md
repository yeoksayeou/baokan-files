Title: Egypt Uncovers British Spy Ring
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th, Cairo dispatch: Egypt has uncovered a British spy ring, as announced by Lieutenant Colonel Abdul Qader Hatem, Director of the Egyptian Intelligence Bureau, on the evening of August 27th.

    He said at a press conference that six spies belonging to this organization were arrested on the afternoon of the 27th. James Swinburne of the Arab News Agency is the head of this "dangerous spy organization serving the British intelligence agency."

    Lieutenant Colonel Hatem said that Swinburne was arrested in his apartment while he was discussing matters with two members of this spy organization.

### 埃及破获英国间谍组织

1956-08-29
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及破获英国间谍组织
    新华社28日讯　开罗消息：埃及破获了一个英国间谍组织，这是埃及情报局主任阿卜杜勒·卡德尔·哈特姆中校在8月27日晚间宣布的。
    他在记者招待会上说，属于这个组织的六个间谍已经在27日下午被捕。阿拉伯通讯社的詹姆斯·斯温布恩是这个“为英国情报机构服务的危险的间谍组织”的首脑。
    哈特姆中校说，斯温布恩是在他的寓所正同这个间谍组织的两个成员商谈时被捕的。
