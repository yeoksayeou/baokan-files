Title: Central Leaders Receive Music Workers
Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 24th. Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Kang Sheng, Lin Boqu, and Central Committee member Chen Yi, received representatives from all over the country who participated in the First National Music Week at 3:30 this afternoon.

### 中央领导同志接见音乐工作者

1956-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中央领导同志接见音乐工作者
    新华社24日讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员周恩来、朱德、陈云、康生、林伯渠，中央委员陈毅，在今天下午三时半接见了参加第一届全国音乐周的全国各地的代表们。
