Title: World Federation of Trade Unions and Women's International Democratic Federation Issue Statements Advocating Negotiated Solution to Suez Canal Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 22nd: The World Federation of Trade Unions issued a statement here on August 20th regarding the Suez Canal issue, saying that the World Federation of Trade Unions expresses the hope of workers worldwide: that the Suez Canal issue will be resolved through free negotiations between the relevant countries, with full respect for the rights of the people and in accordance with the United Nations Charter.

The statement condemned all threats to maintain private interests of a colonial nature and that could endanger peace and friendship between peoples by relying on force.

The World Federation of Trade Unions finally expressed fraternal support to the workers and trade unions of Egypt and Arab countries who have united to oppose any action interfering in the affairs of the Egyptian people and their determination.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 22nd: The Women's International Democratic Federation issued a statement on August 21st regarding the Suez Canal issue.

The Women's International Democratic Federation, representing women and mothers who have a duty to defend the lives of their children, earnestly requests that a final solution be sought through peaceful means, a solution that should respect the rights of Egypt and take into account the interests of countries using the Suez Canal for international trade.

The statement called for such a solution to be sought based on the principles recognized by the United Nations Organization of the rights of peoples and sovereign states.

### 世界工联和国际民主妇联分别发表声明  主张协商解决苏彝士运河问题

1956-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    世界工联和国际民主妇联分别发表声明
    主张协商解决苏彝士运河问题
    据新华社布拉格22日电　世界工会联合会八月二十日在这里就苏彝士运河问题发表声明说，世界工会联合会表达全世界的工人的希望：苏彝士运河问题将由各有关国家间的自由协商，并且充分尊重人民的权利和按照联合国宪章来求得解决。
    声明谴责依靠武力来保持具有殖民主义性质的私人利益的和可以危及和平和各国人民间的友谊的一切威胁。
    世界工会联合会最后对埃及和阿拉伯国家的工人和工会联合起来反对任何干涉埃及人民事务的行动和决心，表示兄弟般的支援。
    据新华社柏林22日电　国际民主妇女联合会八月二十一日发表了关于苏彝士运河问题的声明。
    国际民主妇女联合会代表妇女和有义务保卫她们的儿女的生命的母亲们恳切要求设法用和平手段来寻求最后的解决办法，这种办法应当尊重埃及的权利并且考虑到使用苏彝士运河来进行国际贸易的国家的利益。
    声明要求根据联合国组织承认各国人民和主权国家的权利的原则来寻求这种解决办法。
