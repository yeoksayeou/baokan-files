Title: Yugoslav Artists' Final Performance in Beijing; Vice Chairman Zhu De Receives All the Artists
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The Yugoslav artists gave a brilliant performance for more than 2,000 suburban audience members in Beijing tonight, concluding their month-long visit to China.

    Vice Chairman Zhu De, Vice Minister of Culture Chen Kehan, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations Chen Zhongjing and others watched tonight's performance. Vice Chairman Zhu De received all the Yugoslav artists, shook hands with them on stage after the performance, and congratulated them on the success of the performance.

    Tonight’s performance by the Yugoslav artists began with a solo by soprano Mica Glavasevic and ended with a duet by male singers Aleksandar Marinković and Mirko Jančić. The Slovenian Octet, renowned in Europe, was extremely warmly welcomed by the audience tonight. The interlude dance from the opera "Carmen," performed by Belgrade ballet dancers Mira Sanjina and Dušan Trninić, was re-performed for the audience. After six curtain calls, female singer Vilma Bukovitz sang "March of the Chinese People's Volunteers" twice in Chinese for the audience who applauded enthusiastically for a long time.

    After the performance, the audience presented the Yugoslav artists with flower baskets and bouquets, and threw handfuls of flowers onto the stage.

### 南斯拉夫艺术家在京最后演出  朱德副主席接见全体艺术家

1956-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫艺术家在京最后演出
    朱德副主席接见全体艺术家
    据新华社16日讯　南斯拉夫艺术家们今晚在北京为郊区观众二千多人作了一场精采表演，结束了历时一个月的访华演出。
    朱德副主席，文化部副部长陈克寒，对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经等观看了今晚的表演。朱德副主席接见了南斯拉夫全体艺术家们，并且在演出结束后到舞台上同他们握手，祝贺演出的成功。
    南斯拉夫艺术家们今晚的表演，以女高音歌唱家米查·格拉娃塞维的独唱开始，以男声歌唱家亚历山大·玛林柯维奇和米尔柯·扬契奇的二重唱结束。驰誉欧洲的斯洛文尼亚八重唱团今晚受到观众们极为热烈的欢迎。贝尔格莱德的芭蕾舞家米拉·萨尼娜和杜珊·特尼尼奇合演的歌剧“卡门”中的插舞，为观众重演了一次。女歌唱家维尔玛·布柯维茨在六次谢幕后，为长时间热烈鼓掌的观众两次用中国话演唱了“中国人民志愿军战歌”。
    在演出结束后，观众向南斯拉夫艺术家们赠送了花篮和花束，并且把一把一把的鲜花投上舞台。
