Title: Typhoon Warning
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 1

    Typhoon Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, 31st – The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon warning today:
    It is predicted that at 2:00 PM on August 1st, the center of a strong typhoon in the Pacific Ocean will reach the sea area 120 meters northeast of Taipei, located at 25.7 degrees North latitude and 123 degrees East longitude. Preliminary estimates indicate that between 3:00 AM and 8:00 AM on August 2nd, the typhoon center will make landfall along the coastal area between Shanghai and Fuzhou. The wind force in the landfall area will be level 12, with heavy rainstorms. The typhoon center will continue to move in a west-southwesterly direction. From noon on August 1st to August 2nd, the coastal areas of Zhejiang and Fujian will experience the strongest winds and heavy rainstorms, and urgent precautions must be taken. In eastern Jiangxi and southern Jiangsu, the wind force will also increase to levels 6 and 7 after noon on August 1st.

### 台风警报

1956-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    据新华社31日讯　中央气象台今天发布台风警报：
    预计八月一日下午二时，太平洋上的强台风中心将达到台北的东北120公尺的海面上，就是北纬25.7度、东经123度的地方。初步估计二日早上三点到八点之间，台风中心将在上海到福州之间的沿海地区登陆。登陆地区的风力有十二级，并有大暴雨。台风中心以后将继续向偏西方向移动。八月一日中午以后到二日，浙江和福建沿海风力最强烈，并有暴雨，须作紧急预防。江西东部和江苏南部等地，八月一日午后风力也将增强到六、七级。
