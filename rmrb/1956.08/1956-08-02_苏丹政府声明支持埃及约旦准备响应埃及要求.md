Title: Sudanese Government Declares Support for Egypt; Jordan Prepared to Respond to Egyptian Demands
Author:
Date: 1956-08-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 1st dispatch from Khartoum: The Sudanese Foreign Ministry issued a statement supporting Egypt's action to nationalize the Suez Canal.

    The Foreign Ministry said in a statement, "Egypt, as an autonomous country, has exercised one of its sovereign rights. Egypt has decided to compensate the shareholders and has announced its determination to guarantee the free navigation of the canal, therefore, it has clearly given appropriate consideration to the whole issue. We wish it complete success."

    The statement pointed out that the Suez Canal Company is an Egyptian limited company and is subject to the laws of the Republic of Egypt.

    Xinhua News Agency, August 1st dispatch from Amman: Jordanian Foreign Minister Hadi, on July 31st, issued a statement on the situation formed after Egypt nationalized the Suez Canal, saying that Jordan is prepared to "actively respond to any demands from Egypt" at any time.

### 苏丹政府声明支持埃及约旦准备响应埃及要求

1956-08-02
第6版()
专栏：

    苏丹政府声明支持埃及约旦准备响应埃及要求
　　新华社1日讯　据法新社喀土穆一日消息：苏丹外交部发表声明，支持埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的行动。
    外交部在声明中说，“埃及作为一个自主国家已经行使了它的一项主权。埃及已经决定给予股东以赔偿，并且宣布它决心保证运河的自由航行，因此，显然它已经对整个问题作了适当的考虑。我们祝它完全成功。”
    声明指出，苏彝士运河公司是一个埃及有限公司，受埃及共和国法律的约束。
    新华社1日讯　安曼消息：约旦外交大臣哈迪七月三十一日就埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有后所形成的局势发表谈话说，约旦准备随时“积极响应埃及的任何要求”。
