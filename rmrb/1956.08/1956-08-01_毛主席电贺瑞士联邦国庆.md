Title: Chairman Mao Sends Congratulations on Swiss Confederation National Day
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Markus Feldmann, President of the Swiss Confederation, to congratulate him on the Swiss Confederation National Day on August 1st. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Markus Feldmann, President of the Swiss Confederation:

    On the occasion of the Swiss Confederation National Day, please accept my sincere congratulations. I wish the Swiss Confederation prosperity and the friendly relations between China and Switzerland growing development and consolidation.

                        Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong
                                        July 29, 1956, Beijing

### 毛主席电贺瑞士联邦国庆

1956-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺瑞士联邦国庆
    新华社31日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东打电报给瑞士联邦主席马库斯·费德曼，祝贺八月一日瑞士联邦国庆纪念。电文如下：瑞士联邦主席马库斯·费德曼阁下：
    当瑞士联邦国庆纪念的时候，请接受我诚挚的祝贺，祝瑞士联邦日益繁荣，中国和瑞士之间的友好关系日益发展和巩固。
                中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                       1956年7月29日于北京
