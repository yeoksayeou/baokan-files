Title: Robert Binet Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Robert Binet, Secretary-General of the French newspaper *Libération*, who visited China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, left Beijing for home on the 17th after visiting Beijing, Shenyang, Fushun, Anshan, Changchun, Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuhan, and Guangzhou.
　　On the evening of the 16th, the responsible person of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and people from the press held a banquet to bid farewell to Binet.

### 罗伯·比奈离北京回国

1956-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

    罗伯·比奈离北京回国
    新华社17日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的法国解放报秘书长罗伯·比奈在访问了北京、沈阳、抚顺、鞍山、长春、天津、上海、杭州、武汉、广州等地后，于十七日离北京回国。
    十六日晚，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会负责人和新闻界人士曾设宴欢送比奈。
