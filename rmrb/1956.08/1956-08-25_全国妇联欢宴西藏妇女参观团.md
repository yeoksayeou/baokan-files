Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Tibetan Women's Delegation

Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: The All-China Women's Federation held a banquet this evening to welcome the Tibetan Women's Delegation.

    The head of the Tibetan Women's Delegation, Tangmai·Gongjue Baimu, and the deputy heads, Caisu·Caiyang, and Bangda·Zeren Quji, presented Hada scarves to Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, before the banquet. Deng Yingchao cordially greeted them.

    At the banquet, Deng Yingchao first extended a warm welcome to the sisters from the Tibetan region and congratulated the Tibetan women on the tremendous achievements they have made in the work of building Tibet in recent years.

    Tangmai·Gongjue Baimu, on behalf of the delegation, expressed deep gratitude to the All-China Women's Federation for its warm hospitality. She said that after learning from the experiences of sisters of all ethnic groups in the mainland in participating in construction, they will definitely work with the Tibetan sisters to better participate in the socialist construction of the motherland when they return. She believes that under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, the lives of the Tibetan people will become happier and happier, just like the people of all ethnic groups.

    Amid beautiful music, more than one hundred Tibetan and Han sisters gathered together and chatted happily.

    Those present as escorts tonight included Yang Jingren, Vice-Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Wang Ziru, Vice-Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Women's Federation, Dunwang·Jianzan Zhaba, Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, Lian Huizhu, Acting Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing, and heads of various women's organizations.

### 全国妇联欢宴西藏妇女参观团

1956-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联欢宴西藏妇女参观团
    据新华社24日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会今天晚间举行宴会欢迎西藏妇女参观团。
    西藏妇女参观团团长汤麦·公觉白姆、副团长才苏·才羊、邦达·泽仁曲吉在宴会前向全国妇联副主席邓颖超献了哈达。邓颖超亲切地向她们问候。
    宴会上邓颖超首先向来自西藏地区的姊妹们表示热烈的欢迎，并且祝贺西藏妇女几年来在建设西藏的工作中所取得的巨大成就。
    汤麦·公觉白姆代表参观团对全国妇联的热情招待表示深切感谢，她说这次参观团在内地学习各族姊妹参加建设的经验后，回去一定和西藏姊妹一起更好地参加祖国的社会主义建设。她相信在中国共产党和毛主席的领导下，藏族人民的生活会同各族人民一样越过越幸福。
    在优美的乐声中，参加宴会的一百多位藏、汉族姊妹欢聚一堂，愉快地交谈着。
    今晚出席作陪的有民族事务委员会副主席杨静仁、北京市民主妇女联合会副主席王子如、达赖驻京办事处处长顿旺·坚赞扎巴、班禅驻京办事处代处长联慧珠以及各妇女团体的负责人等。
