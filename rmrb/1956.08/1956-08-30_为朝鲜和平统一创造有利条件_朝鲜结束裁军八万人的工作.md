Title: Creating Favorable Conditions for Peaceful Reunification of Korea: Korea Completes Work of Disarming 80,000 Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-08-30
Page: 5

    To create favorable conditions for the peaceful reunification of Korea
    Korea Completes Work of Disarming 80,000 Troops
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 29th - The Korean Central News Agency was instructed to announce that, as of today, Korea has fully completed the work of disarming 80,000 troops. This work was completed two days ahead of the originally scheduled completion date (August 31st).
    The demobilized soldiers have all been properly arranged for. They have been assigned to factories, enterprises, government agencies, schools, and agricultural production cooperatives in various places to participate in various construction projects according to their wishes and talents. The military expenditure equivalent to the reduction in troops has been allocated to peaceful construction and improving the people's livelihood.
    The Korean Central News Agency said that the measures taken by the Republic's government regarding the reduction of troops are leading to the consolidation of peace and tending towards the armistice, creating favorable conditions for the cause of peaceful reunification of Korea, and are conducive to further easing the tense situation in Asia.

### 为朝鲜和平统一创造有利条件  朝鲜结束裁军八万人的工作

1956-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

    为朝鲜和平统一创造有利条件
    朝鲜结束裁军八万人的工作
    新华社平壤29日电　朝鲜中央通讯社奉命宣布，到今天为止，朝鲜已经全部结束了裁军八万人的工作。这项工作比原先规定的完成日期（8月31日）提前两天完成。
    复员军人都得到了妥善安排。已经按照他们的志愿和才能到各地工厂、企业、机关、学校和农业生产合作社参加各项建设。相当于兵力裁减的那批军事费用拨归和平建设和改善人民生活方面。
    朝鲜中央通讯社说，共和国政府所采取的关于裁减兵力的措施，是导致停战趋向巩固和平，为朝鲜和平统一事业创造有利条件，并且有助于亚洲紧张局势进一步缓和。
