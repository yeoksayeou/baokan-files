Title: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Sends Minority Nationalities Visiting Group
Author:
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, August 1st: The minority nationalities visiting group of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region departed yesterday. The visiting group will visit more than ten minority nationalities in the autonomous region other than Mongolians, including the Oroqen, Solon, Hui, Manchu, and Korean nationalities. The key visiting areas are Hulunbuir League, Jirem League, and Ju Ud League.

The visiting group will, based on the current development of various minority nationalities, understand and resolve existing problems in current production and life, inspect the implementation of the Party's nationalities policy, and investigate and study relevant materials for further economic and cultural development in these minority nationality areas as a basis for formulating long-term plans.

These minority nationalities total more than 119,000 people, accounting for more than 11.4% of the total population of the autonomous region.

### 内蒙古自治区派出少数民族访问团

1956-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区
    派出少数民族访问团
    新华社呼和浩特1日电　内蒙古自治区少数民族访问团已在昨天出发。访问团将访问自治区内除蒙古族以外的鄂伦春、索伦、回、满和朝鲜等十多个少数民族。访问重点地区是呼伦贝尔盟、哲里木盟和昭乌达盟。
    访问团将根据目前各少数民族发展情况，了解和解决当前生产和生活中存在的问题，检查党的民族政策贯彻执行情况，调查研究这些少数民族地区进一步发展经济、文化的有关资料，作为制定长远规划的依据。
    这些少数民族共有十一万九千多人，占自治区总人口的11.4％多。
