Title: North China Vegetables Support Shanghai
Author: Qu Peiren
Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 3

    North China Vegetables Support Shanghai
    (Our Newspaper) This newspaper reports that to ensure that Shanghai residents, affected by the typhoon, can eat fresh vegetables, cities such as Datong, Zhangjiakou, Xuanhua, Tianjin, and Tangshan have already extracted six million catties of fresh vegetables to be transported to Shanghai. These vegetables include tomatoes, peppers, eggplants, cucumbers, and so on. (Qu Peiren)

### 华北蔬菜支援上海
曲培仁
1956-08-13
第3版()
专栏：

    华北蔬菜支援上海
    本报讯　为了使遭受台风袭击后的上海居民能够吃到新鲜蔬菜，大同、张家口、宣化、天津、唐山等城市已经抽出鲜菜六百万斤，运往上海。这些蔬菜中有西红柿、辣椒、茄子、黄瓜等。（曲培仁）
