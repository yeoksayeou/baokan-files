Title: China's Commercial Representative to Syria Calls on Syrian Minister of Economy
Author:
Date: 1956-08-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 6th. Zhang Huazeng, China's Commercial Representative to the Syrian Republic, Deputy Representative Shi Gu, and other staff members of the Commercial Representative Office arrived in Damascus, the Syrian capital, on July 30th.
    On July 31st, Zhang Huazeng and Shi Gu called on Khalil Kallas, the Minister of Economy of the Syrian government, and presented him with a letter of introduction from Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China.

### 我国驻叙利亚商务代表拜会叙经济部部长

1956-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    我国驻叙利亚商务代表拜会叙经济部部长
    新华社6日讯　大马士革消息：中华人民共和国驻叙利亚共和国商务代表张华增、副代表施谷和商务代表处其他工作人员已经在七月三十日抵达叙利亚首都大马士革。
    七月三十一日，张华增、施谷拜会了叙利亚政府经济部部长哈利勒·卡拉斯并且向他递交了中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮给他的介绍信。
