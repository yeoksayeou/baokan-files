Title: Lao Fighting Units and the Royal Lao Government Hold First Meeting in Vientiane; Both Sides Expressed a Desire to Jointly Solve Problems
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 2nd - Vientiane Radio broadcasted today that the delegation of the Royal Lao Government and the delegation of the Lao Fighting Units held their first meeting from 16:20 to 16:40 on August 1st.

　　Prince Souphanouvong, head of the Lao Fighting Units delegation, and Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Royal Lao Government, delivered speeches at the meeting, respectively.

　　Prince Souphanouvong said that although peace has been restored in Laos for the past two years, due to foreign interference and discord, the internecine killings have not stopped. Under such circumstances, we have a responsibility to jointly find ways to make the people happy, consolidate peace, achieve freedom, democracy, and unity, and consolidate national independence on the basis of thoroughly implementing the Geneva Accords. He said that the Lao Fighting Units will sincerely sit down with the Prime Minister and representatives of the Royal Government in a close atmosphere to discuss and solve the issues concerning both parties and the fate of the Lao nation.

　　Souphanouvong said that he hoped the Royal Government delegation would understand the desire of the Lao Fighting Units for peaceful reunification, and believed that the Royal Government delegation, like the Lao Fighting Units, had the same sincerity to make this important meeting a good result to meet the wishes of all the Lao people and the peace-loving people of the world.

　　Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma said in his speech that he hoped the meeting would proceed in a good atmosphere. He said that the Royal Government also has the same goodwill as the Lao Fighting Units delegation, which is to bring benefits and happiness to all the Lao people.

　　Phouma said that the Royal Government would not allow foreign interference in Laos' internal affairs. He hoped that problems would be solved jointly in the future with the help of the International Commission.

　　Phouma suggested “stopping all hostilities before the meeting.” At the same time, he demanded that "the regime of the Royal Government be restored" in the two provinces of Phong Saly and Sam Neua, and suggested that the Lao Fighting Units "return to the common life of the country."

　　Phouma emphasized that the Royal Lao Government would adhere to Laos' neutral path.

　　Prince Souphanouvong and Prime Minister Phouma also introduced the members of their respective delegations at the meeting.

### 寮国战斗部队和老挝王国政府  在万象举行第一次会议  双方表示了共同解决问题的愿望

1956-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    寮国战斗部队和老挝王国政府
    在万象举行第一次会议
    双方表示了共同解决问题的愿望
    新华社河内2日电　据万象电台今天广播：老挝王国政府代表团和寮国战斗部队代表团八月一日十六时二十分到四十分举行了第一次会议。
    寮国战斗部队代表团团长苏发努冯亲王和老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王分别在会上致辞。
    苏发努冯亲王说，过去两年，老挝的和平虽然已经恢复，但是由于外来的干涉和离间，自己人之间的残杀还没有停止。在这种情况下，我们有责任共同设法在彻底履行日内瓦协定的基础上，使人民幸福，并且巩固和平，实现自由、民主和统一，巩固国家的独立。他说，寮国战斗部队将诚挚地在亲密的气氛中与王国政府首相和代表们坐在一起会谈，以便解决有关双方和有关老挝民族命运的问题。
    苏发努冯说，希望王国政府代表团了解寮国战斗部队想和平统一的愿望，相信王国政府代表团也像寮国战斗部队一样，有同样的诚意，使这个重要的会谈获得良好结果，以符合老挝全体人民和世界爱好和平人民的愿望。
    梭发那·富马首相在致辞中希望这次会谈能在良好的气氛中进行。他说，王国政府也有和寮国战斗部队代表团一样的善意，就是给全体老挝人民带来利益和幸福。
    富马说，王国政府不肯让外国干涉老挝内政。他希望今后将在国际委员会的帮助下共同解决问题。
    富马建议“在会议之前停止一切敌对行动”。同时，他要求在丰沙里和桑怒两省“恢复王国政府的政权”，并且建议寮国战斗部队“返回国家的共同生活”。
    富马强调说，老挝王国政府将坚持老挝的中立道路。
    苏发努冯亲王和富马首相在会上还互相介绍了各自的代表团的团员们。
