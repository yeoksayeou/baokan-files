Title: To Counter Imperialist Conspiracies Harming Arab Countries: Syrian "People's Resistance Organization" Established
Author:
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 22nd: A Syrian Army communiqué issued on August 21st announced the formal establishment of the Syrian "People's Resistance Organization."

    Brigadier General Amir Salash has been appointed as commander.

    Salash said that the Syrian People's Resistance Organization is a reserve force for the regular army and is under the jurisdiction of the army.

    Citizens aged eighteen to forty who are not currently serving in the military can join this organization.

    The Army communiqué stated that five recruitment stations are being established in Damascus, and more will be established in other parts of Syria soon.

    The establishment of the "People's Resistance Organization" was announced by the Syrian Ministry of Defense on August 11th. When announcing the establishment of this organization, the Ministry of Defense called on the people to receive military training in order to counter the imperialist conspiracies that harm Arab countries.

### 对付帝国主义危害阿拉伯国家的阴谋  叙利亚“人民抵抗组织”成立

1956-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    对付帝国主义危害阿拉伯国家的阴谋
    叙利亚“人民抵抗组织”成立
    新华社22日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚陆军的公报八月二十一日宣布，叙利亚“人民抵抗组织”已经正式成立。
    阿密尔·沙拉什准将被任命为司令官。
    沙拉什说，叙利亚人民抵抗组织是正规军的后备队，是受陆军管辖的。
    没有轮到服兵役的十八岁到四十岁的公民都可以参加这个组织。
    陆军的公报说，正在大马士革设立五个募兵站，不久还将在叙利亚其他地方设立募兵站。
    成立“人民抵抗组织”是叙利亚国防部在八月十一日宣布的。国防部在宣布成立这个组织时号召人民接受军事训练，以便对付帝国主义危害阿拉伯国家的阴谋。
