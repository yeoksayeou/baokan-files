Title: Lao Delegation Visits the Summer Palace, Factories, and Agricultural Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: The Lao Royal Government Delegation continued their sightseeing tour in Beijing today.

Prince Souvanna Phouma and Deputy Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith, among others, visited the Summer Palace at 5:00 PM today. The distinguished guests toured the Hall of Dispelling Clouds, which is currently under repair, strolled along the Long Corridor, and boated on Kunming Lake.

In the morning, some members of the delegation, accompanied by Chen Weiji, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Textile Industry of the People's Republic of China, and Song Ting, the female director of the No. 2 State Cotton Factory, visited the entire production process, nursery, and workers' dormitories of the No. 2 State Cotton Factory. The distinguished guests expressed their admiration for China's ability to manufacture textile machinery. They also felt that the factory cared about the lives of the workers and did a good job in welfare work.

This afternoon, some members of the delegation also visited the Beijing Zoo and the agricultural production cooperative and printing factory, respectively.

### 老挝代表团游颐和园  并参观工厂和农业社

1956-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    老挝代表团游颐和园
    并参观工厂和农业社
    新华社25日讯　老挝王国政府代表团今天继续在北京参观游览。
    梭发那·富马亲王和卡代·敦·萨索里特副首相等在今天下午五时游览了颐和园。贵宾们参观了正在修理中的排云殿，步过了长廊，并且泛舟昆明湖。
    上午，代表团部分团员在中华人民共和国纺织工业部副部长陈维稷和国棉二厂女厂长宋汀陪同下，参观了国棉二厂整个生产过程、托儿所和工人宿舍。贵宾们对中国能自己制造纺织机械表示钦佩。他们还认为厂方对工人的生活很关心，福利工作作得不错。
    今天下午，代表团部分团员还分别游览了北京动物园，参观了农业生产合作社和印刷厂。
