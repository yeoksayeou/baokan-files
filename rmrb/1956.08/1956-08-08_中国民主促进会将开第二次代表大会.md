Title: China Association for Promoting Democracy to Convene Second National Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 4

    The Second National Congress of the China Association for Promoting Democracy (CAPD) will be held in Beijing starting August 11th. The Third Session of the Fifth Central Committee will be held first, starting August 7th. The main tasks of this congress are: under the new situation of the high tide of socialist revolution in our country, especially after the Chinese Communist Party put forward the policy of long-term coexistence and mutual supervision between the Communist Party and various democratic parties, with the primary role of supervision over the Communist Party, to summarize the work since the First National Congress, further clarify the direction and tasks of serving socialism; to revise the CAPD Charter in accordance with the needs of the development of the situation, and elect a new central leading body.

    More than 270 delegates and observers will participate in the Second National Congress, the vast majority of whom are intellectuals with a certain degree of representation in cultural and educational, medical and health, engineering technology, political and legal, and financial and economic work. Among them are famous scholars, teachers, translators and compilers, and experienced cadres from CAPD organizations at all levels.

### 中国民主促进会将开第二次代表大会

1956-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    中国民主促进会将开第二次代表大会
    本报讯　中国民主促进会第二次全国代表大会，将自八月十一日起在京举行。八月七日起先举行三届五中全会。这次代表大会的主要任务是：在我国社会主义革命高潮的新形势下，特别是在中国共产党提出了共产党和各民主党派长期共存，互相监督，首先是对共产党起监督作用的方针之后，总结第一次全国代表大会以来的工作，进一步明确为社会主义服务的方针、任务；根据形势发展的需要修改中国民主促进会章程，选举新的中央领导机构。
    参加第二次全国代表大会的，有出席、列席代表二百七十余人，绝大多数是文化教育、医药卫生、工程技术、政法、财经等工作岗位上有一定代表性的知识分子。其中有著名的学者、教师、编译工作者和民进各级组织有经验的干部等。
