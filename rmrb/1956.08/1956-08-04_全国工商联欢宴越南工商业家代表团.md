Title: All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Hosts Banquet for Vietnamese Industrial and Business Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd — Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, held a banquet tonight to welcome the Vietnamese industrial and business delegation headed by Bui Duc Mien and deputy head Nguyen Van Loc, who arrived in Beijing on the 2nd.

    Chen Shutong delivered a speech at the banquet, welcoming the Vietnamese industrial and business delegation to China. He hoped that the Vietnamese industrialists and businessmen would start their tour from Beijing, see everything they wanted to see, and offer their opinions on our work.

    Bui Duc Mien then spoke. He said that Vietnamese industrialists and businessmen, under the leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party, have been liberated and are contributing to consolidating peace and striving for the reunification of their country. He hoped that this visit to China would provide some experience that would help Vietnam in its peaceful construction.

### 全国工商联欢宴越南工商业家代表团

1956-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商联欢宴越南工商业家代表团
    据新华社3日讯　中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通今晚举行宴会，欢迎二日到达北京的越南工商业家访华代表团团长裴德棉、副团长阮文禄和全体团员。
    陈叔通在宴会上致词，欢迎越南工商业家访华代表团。他希望越南工商业家们从北京开始他们的参观，看他们所希望看的一切，并且对我们的工作提出意见。
    裴德棉接着讲话。他说，越南工商业家在越南劳动党的领导下获得了解放，正在为巩固越南的和平和争取祖国统一贡献出一份力量。他希望这次访问中国能了解一些有助于越南进行和平建设的经验。
