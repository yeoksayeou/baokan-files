Title: International Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-08-06
Page: 5

    △ Phnom Penh Radio reported on the 3rd: The Cambodian Royal Cabinet has decided to send a cultural troupe to China for a visit in September.
    △ At the invitation of General Vuckovic, Chief of General Staff of the Yugoslav People's Army, General Ne Win, Commander-in-Chief of the Burmese Armed Forces, will visit Yugoslavia for fifteen days in August.
    △ Mr. Schwann, a member of the Free Democratic Party of the West German Federal Parliament, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. He is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 国际交往

1956-08-06
第5版()
专栏：

    ·国际交往·
    △据金边电台三日广播：柬埔寨王国内阁会议已经决定在九月间派一个文工团到中国访问。
    △缅甸武装部队总司令奈温将军应南斯拉夫人民军总参谋长伏奇科维奇上将的邀请，将在八月间前往南斯拉夫进行十五天的访问。
    △西德联邦议院自由民主党议员施万在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他是应中国人民外交学会的邀请前来中国访问的。
