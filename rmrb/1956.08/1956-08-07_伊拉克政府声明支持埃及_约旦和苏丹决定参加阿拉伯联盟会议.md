Title: Iraqi Government Statement Supports Egypt; Jordan and Sudan Decide to Attend Arab League Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-08-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 6th. Baghdad Dispatch: The Iraqi government issued a communique on the evening of August 5th, stating: "The Iraqi government supports Egypt in safeguarding its dignity, sovereignty, and independence, and considers nationalization an undeniable right of an independent nation."

The communique stated that the Iraqi government hopes that all countries will deal wisely with the Suez Canal nationalization issue and resolve the dispute through peaceful means.

This communique was released after a two-hour meeting of the Iraqi cabinet yesterday. At this meeting, Iraqi Prime Minister Nuri al-Said, who had just returned from London that day, reported to the ministers on the Suez Canal issue based on his contacts in London and Paris.

According to Xinhua News Agency, August 6th: Western news agencies report that the governments of Jordan and Sudan announced yesterday that they would participate in the emergency meeting of the Arab League Political Committee. This meeting will discuss measures for Arab countries to support Egypt's recovery of the Suez Canal, and is scheduled to be held in Cairo on August 7th or 8th.

### 伊拉克政府声明支持埃及  约旦和苏丹决定参加阿拉伯联盟会议

1956-08-07
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克政府声明支持埃及
    约旦和苏丹决定参加阿拉伯联盟会议
    新华社6日讯　巴格达消息：“伊拉克政府在八月五日晚上发表公报说：“伊拉克政府支持埃及保证它的尊严、主权和独立，并且认为国有化是独立国家的一种确定不移的权利。”
    公报说，伊拉克政府希望各国将明智地对待苏彝士运河国有化的问题，并且通过和平方式解决争端。
    这个公报是在伊拉克内阁昨天举行两小时的会议后公布的。在这次会议上，当天刚由伦敦回来的伊拉克首相努里·赛义德根据他在伦敦和巴黎的接触，就苏彝士运河问题向大臣们作了报告。
    据新华社6日讯　据西方通讯社消息，约旦和苏丹两国政府昨天已经宣布将参加阿拉伯联盟政治委员会紧急会议。这次会议将讨论阿拉伯国家支持埃及收回苏彝士运河的措施，会议预定在八月七日或八日在开罗举行。
