Title: Bulgarian Artists Perform for PLA Troops Stationed in Beijing and Suburban Audiences
Author:
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd. The Bulgarian Ballet Troupe gave a delightful performance tonight for the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Beijing and audiences in the suburbs of Beijing.

    The Bulgarian ballet artists, who are of a very high standard, performed a total of nineteen programs at the evening party. From the opening trio from "Swan Lake" to the closing "Idyll and Classical Dance", each wonderful program received a warm welcome.

    Tonight, Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yun, Li Xiannian, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Chen Geng, Deputy Director of the General Political Department General Fu Zhong, General Gan Siqi and others attended the performance.

    During the intermission, Vice Premier Chen Yun and others spoke with Lyuben Chakrov, head of the art troupe, and Anastas Petrov, artistic director. At the end of the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yun and others went backstage to meet with the Bulgarian artists.

### 保艺术家为解放军驻京部队和郊区观众演出

1956-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    保艺术家为解放军驻京部队和郊区观众演出
    新华社3日讯　保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术团今天晚间为中国人民解放军驻北京部队和北京郊区观众举行了一次愉快的演出。
    有着很高水平的保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术家们，在晚会上共演出了十九个节目。从开始的“天鹅湖”中的三人舞，到结束时的“田园诗与古典舞”，每一个精采节目的演出都受到了热烈的欢迎。
    今晚，国务院副总理陈云、李先念，中国人民解放军副总参谋长陈赓大将，总政治部副主任傅钟上将、甘泗淇上将等都到场观看表演。
    在演出休息时，陈云副总理等同艺术团团长柳宾·查克罗夫、艺术指导阿纳斯塔斯·彼得罗夫进行了谈话。在演出结束时，陈云副总理等并到后台同保加利亚艺术家会见。
