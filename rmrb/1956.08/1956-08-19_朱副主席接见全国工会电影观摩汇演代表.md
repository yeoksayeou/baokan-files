Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Receives Representatives of the National Trade Union Film Festival and Performance

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Vice Chairman Zhu De received representatives from various regions who are participating in the National Trade Union Film Festival and Performance in Beijing this morning, and took a group photo with them.

    Li Zaiwen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, was also present at the reception.

### 朱副主席接见全国工会电影观摩汇演代表

1956-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席接见全国工会电影观摩汇演代表
    新华社17日讯　朱德副主席今天上午接见了在北京参加全国工会电影观摩汇演的各地的代表，并且同他们在一起照了像。
    接见的时候，中华全国总工会书记处书记栗再温也在场。
