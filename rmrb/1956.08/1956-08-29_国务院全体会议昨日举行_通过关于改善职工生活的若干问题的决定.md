Title: State Council Plenary Session Held Yesterday; Approved Decision on Several Issues Concerning Improving the Lives of Workers and Staff

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th — The 36th Plenary Session of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held yesterday afternoon.

    At today's plenary session, Premier Zhou Enlai gave explanations on various issues in the "State Council's Resolution (Draft) on Improving the National Administrative System." Vice Premier Li Xiannian explained the situation of disasters and relief work across the country this year.

    After discussion, the meeting approved the "State Council's Decision on Several Issues Concerning Improving the Lives of Workers and Staff" and the "State Council's Notification on the Provisional Regulations for the Management and Use of Welfare Funds for State Organ Staff." The meeting also approved the "State Council's Notification on Abolishing the Provisions in the Labor Insurance Regulations Regarding Workers and Staff Being Responsible for the Cost of Expensive Medicines When Seeking Treatment for Illness or Non-Work-Related Injuries." Finally, the meeting approved various lists of appointments and removals.

### 国务院全体会议昨日举行  通过关于改善职工生活的若干问题的决定

1956-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院全体会议昨日举行
    通过关于改善职工生活的若干问题的决定
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第三十六次会议，在二十八日下午举行。
    在今天的全体会议上，周恩来总理就“国务院关于改进国家行政体制的决议（草稿）”中的各项问题作了说明。李先念副总理就今年全国各地受灾情况和救灾工作情况作了说明。
    会议经过讨论，通过了“国务院关于改善职工生活的若干问题的决定”、“国务院关于国家机关工作人员福利费用掌管使用的暂行规定的通知”，并且通过了“国务院关于取消劳动保险条例中关于职工患病或非因工负伤医治时贵重药费由本人自理的规定的通知”。会议在最后通过了各项任免名单。
