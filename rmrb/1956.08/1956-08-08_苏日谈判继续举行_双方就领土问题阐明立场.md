Title: Soviet-Japanese Talks Continue; Both Sides Clarify Positions on Territorial Issues
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th - According to TASS, the third meeting of the Soviet-Japanese talks on the normalization of Soviet-Japanese relations continued on August 6th at the Soviet Foreign Minister's residence. At the meeting, both sides continued to exchange views on the issue of normalizing Soviet-Japanese relations.
    Japanese delegation head, Japanese Foreign Minister Mamoru Shigemitsu, and Soviet delegation head, Soviet Foreign Minister Dmitri Shepilov, both clarified their countries' positions on the territorial issue.
    The next meeting will be held on August 8th.

### 苏日谈判继续举行  双方就领土问题阐明立场

1956-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

    苏日谈判继续举行
    双方就领土问题阐明立场
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：八月六日，苏日谈判继续在苏联外交部长官邸举行第三次会议。双方在会上继续就苏日关系正常化问题交换了意见。
    日本代表团团长、日本外务相重光葵和苏联代表团团长、苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫都阐明了两国对领土问题的立场。
    下次会议在八月八日举行。
