Title: Lebanese and Syrian Prime Ministers Discuss Suez Canal Issue; Sudanese Foreign Minister Agrees with Indian Representative's Proposal at London Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 25th, TASS Beirut, August 24th: According to "Telegraph" reports, Lebanese Prime Minister Abdullah Yafi and Syrian Prime Minister Sabri al-Asali met in Sofar, Lebanon, on August 23rd and held talks on the work summary of the London Conference.

"Telegraph" pointed out that the Lebanese and Syrian prime ministers both condemned the Western countries' proposals on the Suez Canal issue.

Xinhua News Agency, August 25th, Khartoum News: Sudanese Foreign Minister Muhammad Ahmad Mahjoub said in Khartoum on August 24th that Sudan, like Egypt or other Arab countries, would not accept the Western countries' proposal at the London Conference to place the Suez Canal under international control.

Mahjoub said at a press conference that, on the other hand, he agreed with the Indian representative Menon's Indian proposal at the London Conference. The Sudanese Foreign Minister also praised the attitude taken by the Soviet delegation at the meeting.

Mahjoub recently returned from Cairo, where he attended the Arab League Political Committee meeting and discussed some unresolved issues between Egypt and Sudan.

### 黎叙总理会谈苏彝士运河问题  苏丹外长赞同印度代表在伦敦会议上的建议

1956-08-26
第6版()
专栏：

    黎叙总理会谈苏彝士运河问题
    苏丹外长赞同印度代表在伦敦会议上的建议
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特24日讯：据“电讯报”报道，黎巴嫩总理阿卜杜勒·雅非和叙利亚总理萨布里·阿萨利8月23日在黎巴嫩的索法尔市会见，并且就伦敦会议的工作总结进行了会谈。
    “电讯报”指出，黎巴嫩和叙利亚两国总理都谴责西方国家提出的关于苏彝士运河问题的方案。
    新华社25日讯　喀土穆消息：苏丹外交部长穆罕默德·艾哈迈德·马古布8月24日在喀土穆说，苏丹同埃及或者其他阿拉伯国家一样，不会接受西方国家在伦敦会议上提出的把苏彝士运河置于国际控制之下的建议。
    马古布在记者招待会上说，在另一方面，他赞同印度代表梅农在伦敦会议上提出的印度的方案。这位苏丹外交部长还称赞苏联代表团在会上采取的态度。
    马古布最近刚从开罗回来，他在开罗参加了阿拉伯联盟政治委员会会议，并且讨论了一些埃及和苏丹之间还没有解决的问题。
