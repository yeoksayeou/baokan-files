Title: A Hospital Established in Sanmenxia
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 4th. All the medical personnel from the Third Rehabilitation Hospital of Liaoning Province, who are supporting the construction of Sanmenxia, arrived at the construction site in late July. A large number of medical equipment and medicines were also transported to the construction site with the medical personnel.

	This hospital has six departments: internal medicine, surgery, ENT, acupuncture, pediatrics, and obstetrics and gynecology. The hospital is relatively well-equipped, with 250 beds. There are a total of 141 doctors and medical personnel, most of whom have undergone long-term work experience and have a certain level of technical expertise.

	Before the autumn of last year, Sanmenxia only had one clinic. It was expanded into a clinic in early November last year, and now it has developed into a relatively well-equipped hospital.

### 三门峡建立一个医院

1956-08-05
第7版()
专栏：

    三门峡建立一个医院
    新华社郑州4日电　支援三门峡建设的辽宁省第三康复医院的全体医务人员已在七月下旬到达工地，大批医疗设备和药品也随同医务人员运到工地。
    这个医院分内、外、五官、针灸、小儿、妇产等六个科。院内医疗设备比较齐全，有二百五十个床位：共有医师和医务人员一百四十一人，大部经过了长期的工作锻炼，具有一定的技术水平。
    三门峡在去年秋季以前，只有一个医务室，去年十一月初扩大为医务所，现在已发展为设备比较完善的医院。
