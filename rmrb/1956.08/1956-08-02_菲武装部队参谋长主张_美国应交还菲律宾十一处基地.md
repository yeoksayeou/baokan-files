Title: Philippine Armed Forces Chief of Staff Advocates for the United States to Return Eleven Bases to the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1956-08-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, August 1st - The "Manila Chronicle" of July 24th stated that the Chief of Staff of the Philippine Armed Forces, Vargas, advocates that the United States return eleven American military bases in the Philippines.

Vargas is a member of the Philippine group that will negotiate with the United States regarding the revision of the 1947 US-Philippine military base agreement. According to reports, he made this recommendation in a report submitted to the Philippine group.

According to the 1947 US-Philippine military base agreement, the Philippines provides twenty-three military bases for the United States to use for ninety-nine years, of which sixteen are Class A bases that the United States directly continues to use, and seven are Class B bases that require prior notification to the Philippines before use. Among the eleven military bases requested to be returned, five are Class A bases and six are Class B bases.

Vargas also pointed out that the Philippines should insist on demarcating the boundaries of all bases, limiting them to the scope of the United States' "minimum needs," and transferring those unnecessary areas to the Philippines. He suggested first considering transferring Fort McKinley, Cavite Naval Dockyard, Baguio Naval Reservation, and Manila Military Port to the Philippines.

Vargas also suggested that the United States strictly abide by the Philippines' laws concerning water conservancy, forestry, and mineral resources, and move the base boundaries outside of one hundred meters on both sides of national or provincial highways.

### 菲武装部队参谋长主张  美国应交还菲律宾十一处基地

1956-08-02
第6版()
专栏：

    菲武装部队参谋长主张
    美国应交还菲律宾十一处基地
    新华社1日讯　马尼拉消息：七月二十四日的“马尼拉纪事报”说，菲律宾武装部队参谋长瓦加斯主张，要美国交还十一处美国在菲律宾的军事基地。
    瓦加斯是将要同美国方面进行修改1947年签订的美菲军事基地的菲律宾小组的一员。据报道，他在提交菲律宾小组的一项报告中提出了这个建议。
    根据1947年美菲军事基地协定，菲律宾提供二十三处军事基地给美国使用九十九年，其中十六处是让美国直接地继续使用的甲类基地，七处是需要事先通知菲律宾才使用的乙类基地。要求归还的十一处军事基地中，甲类基地占五处，乙类基地占六处。
    瓦加斯还指出，菲律宾应当坚持划定所有的基地的界线，把它们限制到合乎美国“最低限度的需要”的范围之内，并且把那些不需要的地方移交给菲律宾。他建议首先考虑把麦克金兰要塞、甲米地海军船坞、碧瑶海军保留地和马尼拉军用港口等移交给菲律宾。
    瓦加斯还建议要美国方面严格遵守菲律宾的有关水利、森林和矿产资源的法律，并且把基地的地界移出国有或省有公路两侧一百公尺以外。
