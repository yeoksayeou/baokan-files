Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Reception for Speaker Sartono
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 4th -- Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen held a reception at the Beijing Hotel tonight to entertain Speaker Sartono of the Republic of Indonesia, his wife, and the accompanying members of parliament.

    When the guests entered the reception hall, enthusiastic applause erupted. Mayor Peng Zhen and his wife introduced Speaker Sartono and his wife, as well as several members of parliament, to people from all walks of life in the capital who were present. The hosts and guests repeatedly toasted each other in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    When Vice Premier Chen Yun met Speaker Sartono, he said happily, "This is our third meeting." Speaker Sartono nodded repeatedly with a smile. Afterwards, Speaker Sartono also met with Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council. Speaker Sartono expressed his hope that they would have the opportunity to visit Indonesia.

    At the same time, Speaker Sartono also met with members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and members of the Ethnic Affairs, Legislative Affairs, Budget, and Credentials Committees in Beijing, as well as heads of ministries and commissions of the State Council, and members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing.

    Premier Zhou Enlai entered the hall during the reception. Mayor Peng Zhen accompanied Speaker Sartono to meet Premier Zhou. Premier Zhou said to Sartono: "Your time in Beijing this time is too short. You are welcome to bring your whole family to our country for a longer stay next time."

    Mayor Peng Zhen also introduced Speaker Sartono to Liu Ren, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Deputy Mayors of Beijing, and the heads of various bureaus. At that time, the guests asked about the situation of Beijing's municipal construction, industry and commerce, and education, and received satisfactory answers. Speaker Sartono also shook hands and talked happily with university professors and scientists from Beijing. Mayor Peng Zhen's wife invited Speaker Sartono's wife to sit down at a round table, and they talked intimately about Chinese tea and the Beijing opera.

    Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wiyopo Pranoto, and embassy staff were invited to attend the reception.

    Also attending tonight's reception were the heads of various people's organizations, as well as people from the capital's political and legal, religious, news, medical, cultural, educational, and artistic circles.

### 彭真市长酒会招待沙多诺议长

1956-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真市长酒会招待沙多诺议长
    据新华社4日讯　北京市长彭真今晚在北京饭店举行酒会，招待印度尼西亚共和国国会议长沙多诺及夫人和同来的国会议员们。
    当客人步入酒会大厅时，大厅里响起了热烈的掌声。彭真市长和夫人等介绍沙多诺议长及夫人、几位国会议员分别和到会的首都各界人士见面。宾主在热烈友好的气氛中不断干杯。
    陈云副总理见到沙多诺议长时高兴地说：我们这是第三次见面了。沙多诺议长满面笑容的一再点头。接着，沙多诺议长又和全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通和国务院副总理李先念等见了面。沙多诺议长希望他们有机会能到印度尼西亚去访问。
    同时，沙多诺议长又和全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员和民族、法案、预算、代表资格审查等委员会在京委员，国务院各部会负责人，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在京委员等会见。
    周恩来总理在酒会进行中走进大厅。彭真市长陪同沙多诺议长和周总理见了面。周总理对沙多诺说：你这次在北京的时间太短了，欢迎你下次带着全家来我国多住一个时期。
    彭真市长又介绍沙多诺议长和中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会主席刘仁、北京市副市长、各局局长见了面，当时客人问到北京市政建设、工商业、教育等方面的情况，都得到了满意的回答。沙多诺议长还和北京的大学教授、科学家们握手言欢。彭真市长夫人邀请沙多诺议长夫人在一个圆桌旁坐下来，亲密地交谈中国的茶和京剧情形。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托和使馆人员应邀参加了酒会。
    参加今晚酒会的还有各人民团体的负责人，以及首都政法、宗教、新闻、医务、文化、教育、艺术各界人士。
