Title: Britain Rejects Greek Suggestion, Disagrees with Changing the Time and Venue of International Conference; New York Diplomatic Circles Believe that the London Conference Will Fail if the West Does Not Change Course
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-12
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 11th: Athens dispatch: The British Chargé d'Affaires to Greece delivered a note to the Greek government on August 10th, rejecting the Greek request to postpone the international conference on the Suez Canal issue and to hold it in a location other than London.

　　A United Press report said: "This is the first clear indication of the attitude taken by Britain towards the same request made by Russia."

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 11th: The Indian Press Trust issued a dispatch from New York on August 9th stating that diplomatic circles in New York believe that the London conference is destined to fail unless the initiating countries immediately change their approach and actively "consult" with Egypt on the scope and purpose of the conference.

　　Diplomatic circles in New York expressed the above opinions after listening to Indian Prime Minister Nehru's speech in the House of the People on the Suez Canal issue. The report said they also believe that the actions of the United Kingdom, France, and the United States in unilaterally convening the London conference to discuss the Suez Canal issue while setting aside the United Nations were fundamentally wrong.

### 英国拒绝希腊建议  不同意改变国际会议的时间和会址  纽约外交界认为西方如不转圜伦敦会议就要失败

1956-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

    英国拒绝希腊建议
    不同意改变国际会议的时间和会址
    纽约外交界认为西方如不转圜伦敦会议就要失败
    新华社11日讯　雅典消息：英国驻希腊代办八月十日交给希腊政府一个照会，这个照会拒绝了希腊提出的延期召开并且在伦敦以外的地方举行关于苏彝士运河问题的国际会议的要求。
    合众社消息说：“这是英国对于俄国所提出的同样要求所采取的态度的第一个明确的表示。”
    新华社新德里11日电　印度报业托辣斯八月九日从纽约发出的一条消息说，纽约外交界人士认为，伦敦会议注定要失败，除非会议发起国立即改变它们的方针，并且就会议的范围和目的问题主动同埃及“协商”。
    纽约外交界人士在听了印度总理尼赫鲁在人民院发表的关于苏彝士运河问题的演说以后，发表了上面的意见。消息说，他们还认为，英、法、美三国为了片面召开讨论苏彝士运河问题的伦敦会议而撇开联合国的做法，在原则上是错误的。
