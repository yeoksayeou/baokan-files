Title: World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Steering Committee Holds Garden Party to See Off Delegates from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 24th—The Steering Committee of the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs held a garden party at the Prince Hotel in Tokyo last night to warmly see off delegates from various countries.

    Representatives of international organizations such as the World Peace Council and the World Federation of Trade Unions, as well as delegates from China, the Soviet Union, and other countries, were invited to attend the garden party.

    Secretary-General Yasui Kaoru of the Steering Committee of the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs gave a speech at the meeting, expressing his gratitude for the warm assistance of the delegates from various countries. He said that the Nagasaki Conference's declaration and resolutions pointed out the path we should take. In order to realize the prevention of war, disarmament, opposition to the use of atomic and hydrogen bombs, and the defense of world peace, people all over the world must unite closely and move forward.

    Delegates from various countries successively gave speeches at the meeting.

    Xu Guangping, head of the Chinese delegation, said that the success of the World Conference symbolizes the unity of people from all walks of life in Japan, and also symbolizes the unity of people all over the world. She hoped that the spirit of this conference would be carried forward and achieve final victory.

### 禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会实行委员会  举行游园会欢送各国代表

1956-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

    禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会实行委员会
    举行游园会欢送各国代表
    据新华社24日讯　东京消息：第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会实行委员会昨天晚上在东京王子饭店举行游园会，热烈欢送各国代表。
    世界和平理事会和世界工会联合会等国际团体的代表和中国、苏联等各国代表应邀出席了游园会。
    第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会实行委员会秘书长安井郁在会上致词，对各国代表的热诚协助表示感谢。他说，长崎大会的宣言和决议指明了我们应该要走的道路，为了实现阻止战争、裁减军备、反对使用原子弹和氢弹，保卫世界和平，全世界人民必须紧密团结起来努力前进。
    各国代表相继在会上致词。
    中国代表团团长许广平说，世界大会的成功象征着日本各阶层人民的团结，也象征着全世界人民的团结。她希望这个大会的精神能发扬光大，获得最后胜利。
