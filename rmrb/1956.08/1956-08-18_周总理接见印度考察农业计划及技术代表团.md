Title: Premier Zhou Receives Indian Agricultural Planning and Technical Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th — Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received the entire Indian delegation visiting China to study agricultural planning and technology this afternoon. The Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, was also present. Also present at the reception were Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, and others.

### 周总理接见印度考察农业计划及技术代表团

1956-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印度考察农业计划及技术代表团
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今日下午接见了印度来华考察农业计划及技术代表团全体人员。印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁也在座。接见时在座的还有农业部副部长刘瑞龙等多人。
