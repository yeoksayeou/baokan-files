Title: Nepalese Prime Minister to Visit Our Country in September
Author:
Date: 1956-08-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch: The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China issued a communique today stating that at the invitation of Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai, the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Tanka Prasad Acharya, will visit China in late September of this year.

    This communique was simultaneously released today in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal.

    The full text of the communique is as follows:

    Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, has invited Prime Minister Tanka Prasad Acharya of Nepal, on behalf of the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal, to visit China as a distinguished guest of the Chinese government.

    Prime Minister Acharya has replied to Premier Zhou Enlai, expressing his sincere acceptance of this invitation.

    This visit will take place in late September of this year.

### 尼泊尔首相将在九月访问我国

1956-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首相将在九月访问我国
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国外交部今天发表公报说，尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅应中国总理周恩来的邀请，将在今年九月下旬来中国访问。
    这项公报今天在尼泊尔首都加德满都同时发表。
    公报全文如下：
    中华人民共和国周恩来总理代表中华人民共和国政府邀请尼泊尔首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅代表尼泊尔王国政府作为中国政府的贵宾访问中国。
    阿查里雅首相已经复信给周恩来总理，表示热诚地接受这一邀请。
    这一访问将在今年九月下旬进行。
