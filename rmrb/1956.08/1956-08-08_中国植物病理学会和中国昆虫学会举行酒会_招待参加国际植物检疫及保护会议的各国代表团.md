Title: Chinese Society of Plant Pathology and Chinese Entomological Society Hold Reception to Host Delegations from Various Countries Attending the International Plant Quarantine and Protection Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Chinese Society of Plant Pathology and the Chinese Entomological Society held a reception this evening to host all members of the delegations from Albania, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (North Korea), Mongolia, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam (North Vietnam) who are attending the Eighth International Plant Quarantine and Plant Protection Conference.

　　Dai Fanglan, Chairman of the Chinese Society of Plant Pathology, and Chen Shixiang, Chairman of the Chinese Entomological Society, delivered speeches at the reception, extending a warm welcome to the representatives and experts from fraternal countries attending the conference. They expressed their honor and joy that the Eighth International Plant Quarantine and Plant Protection Conference was being held in China and expressed their deep gratitude for the valuable experiences and friendship brought by friends from fraternal countries.

　　During the reception, Chinese and foreign experts held discussions on issues such as plant quarantine and plant protection.

　　More than 150 Chinese plant pathologists and entomologists in Beijing also attended this evening's reception.

　　A dance was held after the reception.

### 中国植物病理学会和中国昆虫学会举行酒会  招待参加国际植物检疫及保护会议的各国代表团

1956-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中国植物病理学会和中国昆虫学会举行酒会
    招待参加国际植物检疫及保护会议的各国代表团
    新华社7日讯　中国植物病理学会和中国昆虫学会今晚举行酒会，招待参加第八届国际植物检疫及植物保护会议的阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联、越南民主共和国等国的代表团全体人员。
    中国植物病理学会理事长戴芳澜和中国昆虫学会理事长陈世骧在会上先后致词，对参加这次会议的各兄弟国家的代表和专家们表示热烈欢迎；对第八届国际植物检疫及植物保护会议在中国召开感到光荣和高兴；并且对各兄弟国家的朋友们带来的宝贵经验和友谊表示深切的谢意。
    酒会进行中，中外专家们就植物检疫及植物保护等问题进行了交谈。
    参加今晚酒会的，还有在北京的中国植物病理和昆虫学家等一百五十余人。
    酒会结束后，还举行了舞会。
