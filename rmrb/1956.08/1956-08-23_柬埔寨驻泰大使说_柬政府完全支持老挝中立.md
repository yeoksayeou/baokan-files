Title: Cambodian Ambassador to Thailand Says Cambodian Government Fully Supports Laos' Neutrality
Author:
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd, Bangkok dispatch: Cambodian Ambassador to Thailand, Shi Jinshan, stated to the Bangkok press on August 14th that as a close neighbor and intimate friend of Laos, the Kingdom of Cambodia's government fully supports Laos in implementing a neutral policy.

Shi Jinshan said that the Kingdom of Cambodia's government believes that Laos implementing a neutral policy will greatly consolidate the national status of Laos.

The Cambodian ambassador also said that the Kingdom of Cambodia's government and people unanimously welcome Laos achieving peaceful reunification.

### 柬埔寨驻泰大使说  柬政府完全支持老挝中立

1956-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨驻泰大使说
    柬政府完全支持老挝中立
    新华社22日讯　曼谷消息：柬埔寨王国驻泰国大使施金山在八月十四日向曼谷报界发表谈话说，作为老挝的近邻和亲密的朋友，柬埔寨王国政府完全支持老挝实行中立政策。
    施金山说，柬埔寨王国政府认为，老挝实行中立政策将很好地使老挝国家地位获得巩固。
    这位柬埔寨大使还说，柬埔寨王国政府和人民一致欢迎老挝获得和平统一。
