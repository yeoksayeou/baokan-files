Title: A Bag of Soil from South Vietnam (Sculpture)
Author: Fan Chunshi
Date: 1956-08-07
Page: 6

    A Bag of Soil from South Vietnam (Sculpture) Made by Pham Xuan Thi of Vietnam
    (Won first prize at the 1955 All-Vietnam Art Exhibition)
    Explanation: After the restoration of peace in Vietnam, the People's Army gathered from South Vietnam to North Vietnam. Before leaving, the soldier's mother gave the soldier a bag of soil and told the soldier not to forget the homeland, and to fight for the unification of the motherland.

### 南越的一包泥土（图片）
范春诗
1956-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    南越的一包泥土（雕塑）  越南  范春诗作
    （曾获1955年全越美术展览会一等奖）
    说明：越南和平恢复后，人民军从南越向越南北部集结，行前战士的母亲给战士一包泥土，并叮咛战士不要忘记故土，要为统一祖国而斗争。
