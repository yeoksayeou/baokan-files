Title: Marshall Meets with Ye Jizhuang to Discuss Trade Issues Between China and Singapore
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd: David Marshall, Chairman of the Singapore Labour Front and advisor to the Singapore trade delegation to China, paid a visit to Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang this morning. During their hour-long conversation, they exchanged views on the prospects for the development of trade between China and Singapore.

### 马歇尔拜会叶季壮谈中国和新加坡贸易问题

1956-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    马歇尔拜会叶季壮谈中国和新加坡贸易问题
    新华社22日讯　新加坡劳工阵线主席、新加坡贸易访华团顾问戴维·马歇尔在今天上午拜会了对外贸易部部长叶季壮。他们在一个小时的谈话中，曾就中国和新加坡的贸易发展前途问题交换了意见。
