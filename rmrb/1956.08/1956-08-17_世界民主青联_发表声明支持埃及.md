Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Issues Statement Supporting Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: TASS, Budapest, August 15th—The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth issued a statement in Budapest on August 15th, supporting the Egyptian government's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company.

### 世界民主青联  发表声明支持埃及

1956-08-17
第6版()
专栏：

    世界民主青联
    发表声明支持埃及
    据新华社16日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯十五日讯：世界民主青年联盟书记处八月十五日在布达佩斯发表声明，支持埃及政府把苏彝士运河公司收归国有。
