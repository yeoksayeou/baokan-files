Title: Demonstrations in Aleppo, Syria Support Egypt's Nationalization of the Suez Canal Company
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:
    In Aleppo, a major city in northern Syria, more than 50,000 people held demonstrations on August 14th in support of Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company.
