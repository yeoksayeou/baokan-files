Title: Two New Highways Open to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1956-08-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Two highways built for the construction of the Xianghongdian Reservoir and Mozitan Reservoir projects – the Liu'an-Xianghongdian Highway and the Foziling-Mozitan Highway – were completed and officially opened to traffic on August 1st and 3rd, respectively.

### 两条新公路通车

1956-08-06
第2版()
专栏：

    两条新公路通车
    据新华社讯　为了兴建响洪甸水库和磨子潭水库工程而修筑的两条公路——六（安）响（洪甸）公路和佛（子岭）磨（子潭）公路，已经分别在八月一日和三日竣工，正式通车。
