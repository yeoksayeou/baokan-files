Title: First Match Held Between Chinese and Yugoslavian Football Teams
Author:
Date: 1956-08-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 10th: A dispatch from Belgrade: The Chinese football team played its first match in Yugoslavia on August 9th in Belgrade. Their opponent was the Yugoslavian national football team.

    In the first half, both sides played intensely and wonderfully, with Yugoslavia taking the lead at one to zero. In the second half, Yugoslavian athletes demonstrated even better technique, and although the Chinese athletes adopted a "fast attack" tactic, they still could not get the ball into the opponent's goal. As a result, the Yugoslavian team won four to zero.

    Approximately 30,000 people watched the match.

### 中国和南斯拉夫足球队举行第一次比赛

1956-08-11
第4版()
专栏：体育消息

    中国和南斯拉夫足球队举行第一次比赛
    新华社布拉格10日电　贝尔格莱德消息：中国足球队八月九日在贝尔格莱德举行了到南斯拉夫以后的第一次比赛。比赛的对手是南斯拉夫国家足球队。
    上半场，双方打得紧张精采，南斯拉夫队以一比零占先。下半场，南斯拉夫运动员更表现了他们优良的技术，虽然中国运动员采取了“快攻”战术，仍然不能把球攻进对方的球门。结果，南斯拉夫队以四比零获胜。
    观看这次比赛的约有三万人。
