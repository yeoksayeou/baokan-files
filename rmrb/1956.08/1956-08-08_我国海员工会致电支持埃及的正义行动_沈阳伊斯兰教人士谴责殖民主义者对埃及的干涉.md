Title: China's Maritime Union Sends Telegram Supporting Egypt's Just Actions; Shenyang Islamic Figures Condemn Colonialist Interference in Egypt
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th - The National Committee of the China Maritime Trade Union today sent a telegram to the Egyptian Canal Workers' Union, congratulating Egypt on nationalizing the Suez Canal Company.
    The telegram said: "We, with great excitement, on behalf of the maritime and port workers of China, congratulate you on nationalizing the Suez Canal Company. Your just actions in defending sovereignty and safeguarding national interests have won the deep sympathy and support of all the Chinese people and shipping workers."
    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 7th - More than seventy Islamic figures in Shenyang held a forum on the 6th to support Egypt's just action in nationalizing the Suez Canal Company.
    The forum was presided over by Ibrahim Zhao Mingzhou, member of the China Islamic Association and Ahong of the North Mosque. Ibrahim Zhao Mingzhou Ahong, Yunus Bai Fengyuan Ahong, and Ai Maitule Wan Habu Li Chuanzhen Ahong, in their speeches, unanimously expressed their support for the heroic struggle of the Egyptian people to safeguard their national interests. In their speeches, they condemned the colonialists' interference in Egypt. Shenyang Islamic figures Yerguodi Yang Xuanqing and Nulundi'ne Tie Zizhang said that the Egyptian people have the courage to nationalize the Suez Canal, which was occupied by imperialism for eighty-six years, and they also have the strength to defeat all the conspiracies of the colonialists.
    The Islamic figures who participated in this forum unanimously stated that the more than 30,000 Islamic believers in Shenyang stand united with the Egyptian people to fight against colonialism and to safeguard world peace.

### 我国海员工会致电支持埃及的正义行动  沈阳伊斯兰教人士谴责殖民主义者对埃及的干涉

1956-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我国海员工会致电支持埃及的正义行动
    沈阳伊斯兰教人士谴责殖民主义者对埃及的干涉
    新华社6日讯　中国海员工会全国委员会今天打电报给埃及的运河工人工会，庆贺埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有。
    电报说：“我们以极为兴奋的心情谨代表中国的水上和港口工人，庆贺你们将苏彝士运河公司收归国有。你们保卫主权、维护民族利益的正义行动，赢得了全体中国人民和航运职工深切的同情和支持。”
    据新华社沈阳7日电　沈阳市伊斯兰教人士七十多人在六日举行座谈会，支持埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的正义行动。
    座谈会由中国伊斯兰教协会委员、北清真寺阿訇伊布拉欣·赵铭周主持。伊布拉欣·赵铭周阿訇、优奴斯·白凤元阿訇、哎埋土乐万哈布·李传贞阿訇等在发言时，一致表示要支持埃及人民为维护民族利益而进行的英勇斗争。他们在发言中谴责了殖民主义者对埃及的干涉。沈阳市伊斯兰教人士耶尔孤的·杨选青和奴伦底呢·铁子章说，埃及人民有勇气把被帝国主义侵占八十六年的苏彝士运河收归国有，也就有力量击败殖民主义者的一切阴谋。
    参加这个座谈会的伊斯兰教人士一致表示，沈阳的三万多伊斯兰教信徒和埃及人民团结一致，为反对殖民主义，为维护世界和平而斗争。
