Title: Tianjin Personages from All Circles Hold a Meeting to Welcome Returned Overseas Chinese from Japan
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, August 26th. Over two thousand people from all circles in Tianjin held a meeting today to welcome returned Overseas Chinese from Japan.

	Tianjin Vice Mayor Zhou Shutao, on behalf of the Tianjin People's Committee and the people of Tianjin, expressed cordial welcome and greetings to the returned Overseas Chinese at the meeting. He said: "Your return adds new strength to the construction of the motherland. We warmly welcome you to participate in the socialist construction of our great motherland." Fei Zhendong, representative of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, expressed heartfelt gratitude in his welcoming speech to the Overseas Chinese organizations in Japan and all those who helped the Overseas Chinese return to China.

	Guo Pingtan, who served as the chairman of the Association of Chinese Students in Japan before returning to China, spoke on behalf of the returning Overseas Chinese at the meeting. He said: "We sincerely thank our compatriots at home for their warm care. We are willing to work hand in hand with our compatriots at home to liberate Taiwan, the territory of our motherland, as soon as possible, and to build a prosperous and strong socialist motherland."

	After the meeting, the Tianjin Peking Opera Troupe performed wonderful Peking Opera programs for the returned Overseas Chinese.

### 天津各界人士举行大会  欢迎旅日回国的侨胞

1956-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

    天津各界人士举行大会
    欢迎旅日回国的侨胞
    新华社天津26日电　天津市各界人士两千多人今天举行大会欢迎旅日回国侨胞。
    天津市副市长周叔弢代表天津市人民委员会和天津市人民在会上向归国侨胞们表示亲切的欢迎和慰问。他说：“你们的归来，给祖国增加了新的建设力量。我们热烈地欢迎你们来参加伟大祖国的社会主义建设。”中华人民共和国华侨事务委员会代表费振东在致欢迎词中对在日本的华侨团体和帮助侨胞回国的一切人士表示衷心的感谢。
    归国前担任留日同学总会主席的郭平坦，在会上代表这次归国华侨讲了话。他说：“我们衷心感谢国内同胞们对我们的热情关怀。我们愿意为早日解放祖国的领土台湾和建设繁荣富强的社会主义祖国，和国内同胞携手共同奋斗。”
    会后，天津市京剧团为归国侨胞们演出了精采的京剧节目。
