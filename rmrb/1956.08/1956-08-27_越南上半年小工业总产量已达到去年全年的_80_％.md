Title: Vietnam's Total Output of Small-Scale Industry in the First Half of the Year Reached 80% of Last Year's Total
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 26th: According to the Vietnam News Agency: In the first half of this year, the total output of small-scale industry and handicrafts in North Vietnam reached 80% of the total output for the entire year of 1955.

This was announced by Deng Yuezhou, Deputy Minister of Commerce, at a recent meeting of representatives from industrial and commercial organizations to review the implementation of the small-scale industry and handicraft plan in North Vietnam.

In the first half of this year, more than 13,600 small-scale industrial and handicraft enterprises were added. Many sectors in small-scale industry and handicrafts, such as weaving, agricultural tool manufacturing, and brick and tile manufacturing, have developed steadily with the help of state-owned trading organizations.

Due to the recovery and development of these sectors, more than 300,000 people have found work. The restoration of more than 30,000 looms alone has provided a means of livelihood for approximately 100,000 people.

### 越南上半年小工业总产量已达到去年全年的80％

1956-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    越南上半年小工业总产量已达到去年全年的80％
    新华社河内26日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南北部在今年前半年，小工业和手工业总产量已经达到了1955年全年产量的80％。
    这是商业部副部长邓曰州在最近召开的一次审查越南北部小工业和手工业计划执行情况的工商业机构代表会议上报告时宣布的。
    今年上半年，小工业和手工业企业增加了一万三千六百多家。小工业和手工业中像织布、农具制造、砖瓦制造等许多部门在国营贸易机关的帮助下得到了稳步的发展。
    由于这些部门的恢复和发展，已经有三十多万人得到了工作。光是恢复了三万多台织布机就使大约有十万人得到了维持生活的工具。
