Title: French Marseille University Sports Club Women's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 4
Sports News

    French Marseille University Sports Club
    Women's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, August 25th—The French Marseille University Sports Club women's basketball team, a group of fifteen people led by team leader Jean Périssol, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of China.
    The French Marseille University Sports Club women's basketball team is one of the best women's basketball teams in France. They have won the French university system women's basketball championship ten times. They will hold friendly matches with Chinese women's basketball teams in Beijing and other cities.

### 法国马赛大学体育俱乐部  女子篮球队到北京

1956-08-26
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    法国马赛大学体育俱乐部
    女子篮球队到北京
    新华社25日讯　应邀来中国访问的法国马赛大学体育俱乐部女子篮球队一行十五人，今天在领队让·佩里索尔率领下坐飞机到北京。
    法国马赛大学体育俱乐部女子篮球队是法国最优秀的女子篮球队之一，她们曾经十次获得法国大学系统女子篮球赛的冠军。她们将在北京和其他城市同中国的女子篮球队举行友谊比赛。
