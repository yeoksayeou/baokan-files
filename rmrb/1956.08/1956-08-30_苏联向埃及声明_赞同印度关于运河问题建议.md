Title: Soviet Union Declares to Egypt: Supports India's Proposal on the Suez Canal Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-08-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 29th: TASS reports: The Soviet Ambassador to Egypt, Mr. Kiselyov, met with President Nasser on August 28th and delivered a statement from the Soviet government to him.

The statement said: After a comprehensive and detailed study of the issues related to the Suez Canal and the materials from the London International Conference held from August 16th to 23rd, 1956, the Soviet government has concluded that the Suez issue should be resolved on the basis of the principles and charter of the United Nations, that is, the sovereignty of Egypt as the full owner, possessor, and administrator of the canal must be respected, while ensuring that all countries using the canal can freely pass through it at any time.

Based on this, and in the interests of consolidating peace and security in the Near East and Middle East regions, the Soviet government has decided to support the proposal on the Suez issue put forward by the government of the Republic of India at the London Conference on August 20th, 1956.

### 苏联向埃及声明  赞同印度关于运河问题建议

1956-08-30
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联向埃及声明
    赞同印度关于运河问题建议
    新华社29日讯　塔斯社开罗讯：苏联驻埃及大使基谢廖夫8月28日拜见了纳赛尔总统，并且把苏联政府的一个声明交给他。
    声明说：苏联政府全面地、详细地研究了同苏彝士运河有关的问题和1956年8月16日到23日举行的伦敦国际会议的材料以后，得出结论，认为，苏彝士问题应当根据联合国的原则和宪章在下列基础上求得解决，即必须尊重作为运河的全权主人、所有者和管理者的埃及的主权，但同时保证使用运河的一切国家在任何时候都可以自由通过运河。
    从这一点出发，并本着巩固近东和中东地区的和平和安全的利益，苏联政府决定赞同印度共和国政府在1956年8月20日伦敦会议上提出的关于苏彝士问题的建议。
