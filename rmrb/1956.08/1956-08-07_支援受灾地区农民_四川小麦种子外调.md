Title: Supporting Farmers in Disaster Areas: Sichuan Transfers Wheat Seeds
Author:
Date: 1956-08-07
Page: 3

    Supporting Farmers in Disaster Areas
    Sichuan Transfers Wheat Seeds
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The first batch of wheat seeds from Sichuan Province was shipped from Chongqing's Yangtze River dock on August 2nd. From now until early September, Sichuan Province will transport more than 200 million jin of wheat seeds to support farmers in Anhui, Jiangsu, and other provinces affected by floods and pests. Among them, more than 80 million jin are of the improved varieties "Nanda 2419" and "Dwarf Lido".

### 支援受灾地区农民  四川小麦种子外调

1956-08-07
第3版()
专栏：

    支援受灾地区农民
    四川小麦种子外调
    新华社讯　八月二日，从重庆市长江码头运出了第一批四川省的小麦种子。从现在到九月上旬，四川省共将运出二亿多斤小麦种子支援安徽、江苏等省受水淹和虫害地区的农民，其中有“南大二四一九”、“矮立多”两种良种八千多万斤。
