Prince Souphanouvong, leader of the Laotian fighting forces, arrived in Vientiane on July 31 to hold negotiations with the Royal Lao Government. After getting off the plane, he warmly embraced Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Royal Lao Government, who was there to greet him.
