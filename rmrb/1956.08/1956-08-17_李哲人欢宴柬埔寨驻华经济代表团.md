Title: Li Zheren Hosts Banquet for Cambodian Economic Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Zheren hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the entire Cambodian Economic Delegation to China. Those attending the banquet included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and responsible individuals from the relevant departments and bureaus of the Ministry of Foreign Trade and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    In the afternoon, the Cambodian Economic Delegation to China, headed by Yang An, paid a courtesy call on Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Zheren.

### 李哲人欢宴柬埔寨驻华经济代表团

1956-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    李哲人欢宴柬埔寨驻华经济代表团
    新华社16日讯　对外贸易部副部长李哲人今晚设宴欢迎柬埔寨驻华经济代表团全体人员。参加宴会的有外交部副部长章汉夫和对外贸易部、外交部各有关司局的负责人。
    下午，以杨安为首的柬埔寨驻华经济代表团拜会了对外贸易部副部长李哲人。
