Title: Vice Premier He Long Receives Romanian Sports Committee Chairman Bodnaras
Author:
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 19th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, He Long, received the Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers' Committee for Physical Culture and Sports, M. Bodnaras, this morning. Vice Directors of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Cai Tingkai, Huang Qixiang, and Rong Gaotang, were also present at the meeting.

### 贺龙副总理接见罗马尼亚体委主席波德纳拉斯

1956-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理接见罗马尼亚体委主席波德纳拉斯
    新华社19日讯　国务院副总理、体育运动委员会主任贺龙今天上午接见了罗马尼亚部长会议体育运动委员会主席马·波德纳拉斯。接见的时候，体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴、黄琪翔和荣高棠也在座。
