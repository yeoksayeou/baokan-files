Title: All-China Sports Federation Vice Chairman Meets Italian Sports Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th—Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, met with all members of the Italian Sports Delegation this afternoon.

    During the meeting, Rong Gaotang gave the guests a relatively detailed introduction to the development of sports in China and answered the questions raised by the guests.

### 中华全国体育总会副主席会见意大利体育代表团

1956-08-10
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中华全国体育总会副主席会见意大利体育代表团
    新华社9日讯　中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠今天下午会见了意大利体育代表团全体人员。
    在会见的时候，荣高棠向客人们比较详细地介绍了中国体育运动的发展情况，并且解答了客人们提出的问题。
