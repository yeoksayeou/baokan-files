Title: Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition Opens in Rotterdam, Netherlands
Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rotterdam, August 24th: The Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition opened on August 22nd at the Rotterdam City Museum in the Netherlands. The opening ceremony was presided over by Rotterdam Mayor van Walsum. In his speech, he said that he was pleased that the Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition could be held in Rotterdam.

More than 500 prominent figures from the Dutch cultural and social circles attended the opening ceremony.

Xie Li, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in the Netherlands, also attended the opening ceremony.

### 我国工艺美术展览会在荷兰鹿特丹开幕

1956-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

    我国工艺美术展览会在荷兰鹿特丹开幕
    新华社24日讯　鹿特丹消息；中国工艺美术展览会八月二十二日在荷兰鹿特丹市博物馆开幕。开幕式由鹿特丹市长瓦尔森主持。他在致词中说，他对中国工艺美术展览能在鹿特丹举行感到高兴。
    参加开幕式的有荷兰著名的文化界人士和社会人士五百余人。
    中国驻荷兰代办谢黎也出席了开幕式。
