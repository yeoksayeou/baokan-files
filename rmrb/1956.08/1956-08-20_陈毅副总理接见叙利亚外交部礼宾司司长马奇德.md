Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Majid, Chief of Protocol of the Syrian Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received Majid, Chief of Protocol of the Syrian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, this afternoon. Zhu Qiwen, Deputy Director of the First Office of the State Council, and He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, were also present at the reception.

### 陈毅副总理接见叙利亚外交部礼宾司司长马奇德

1956-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见叙利亚外交部礼宾司司长马奇德
    新华社19日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天下午接见了叙利亚外交部礼宾司司长马奇德。接见的时候，国务院第一办公室副主任朱其文、外交部副司长何功楷也在座。
