Title: Egyptian Ambassador Visits Indian Foreign Ministry
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 31st. Egyptian Ambassador to India, Mustafa Kamel, visited the Indian Foreign Ministry yesterday.

According to the Press Trust of India, he conveyed to the Indian government the Egyptian government's attitude towards the nationalization of the Suez Canal.

So far, the Indian government has not expressed its opinion on the nationalization of the Suez Canal.

A Foreign Ministry spokesperson said on July 28th that the plan to nationalize the Suez Canal was not mentioned during the meeting between Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser, Yugoslav President Josip Broz Tito, and Indian Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru in Brioni two weeks ago. Nor was it mentioned subsequently during Nehru's visit to Cairo.

### 埃及大使访印度外交部

1956-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及大使访印度外交部
    新华社新德里31日电　埃及驻印度大使穆斯塔法·卡迈尔昨天访问了印度外交部。
    据印度报业托辣斯说，他把埃及政府对于把苏彝士运河收归国有一事的态度转告了印度政府。
    到现在为止，印度官方还没有对苏彝士运河国有化问题发表意见。
    外交部一位发言人七月二十八日说，在两个星期以前在布里俄尼举行的埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔、南斯拉夫总统铁托和印度总理尼赫鲁三人会谈期间，没有提到苏彝士运河国有化的计划。随后在尼赫鲁访问开罗期间也没有提到这件事。
