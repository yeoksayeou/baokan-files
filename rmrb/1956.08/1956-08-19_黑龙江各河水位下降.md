Title: Water Levels Decrease in Heilongjiang Rivers
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 18th - According to reports from hydrological stations in Heilongjiang Province, the water levels of rivers such as the Lalin River, Nen River, Mudan River, and Hulan River continued to decrease today. The water level of the Songhua River in the Harbin area had fallen back to 119.83 meters by the afternoon.
    The people in the areas along the rivers in Heilongjiang Province are continuing to persevere in the flood control struggle, guaranteeing a final victory over the floods.

### 黑龙江各河水位下降

1956-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    黑龙江各河水位下降
    新华社哈尔滨18日电　据黑龙江省各地水文站报告，拉林河、嫩江、牡丹江、呼兰河等江河的水位今天继续下降。哈尔滨地区松花江水位在午后已回落到119.83公尺。
    黑龙江省沿江河地区人民正继续坚持防洪斗争，保证最后战胜洪水。
