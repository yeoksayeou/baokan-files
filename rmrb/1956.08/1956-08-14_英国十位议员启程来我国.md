Title: Ten British MPs Depart for Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, London, 13th: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, ten members of Parliament from the British Conservative, Labour, and Liberal parties departed for China this morning for a visit. They will stay in China for three weeks.

Huan Xiang, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Britain, gave a farewell dinner for these MPs last night.

The MPs include Conservative Party members Hill, Mathew, Yates, Labour Party members Mikardo, Hale, Rankin, Stones, Lord Lucan, and Liberal Party members Bowen and Lord Winterton.

### 英国十位议员启程来我国

1956-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    英国十位议员启程来我国
    新华社伦敦13日电　应中国人民外交学会的邀请，英国保守党、工党和自由党的两院议员十人已经在今天早上启程到中国访问。他们将在中国逗留三个星期。
    中国驻英代办宦乡昨天晚上曾经为这批议员饯行。
    这批议员包括保守党的希尔、马修、耶茨，工党的米卡多、黑尔、兰金、斯通兹、卢肯勋爵和自由党的鲍恩和温特尔夏姆勋爵等人。
