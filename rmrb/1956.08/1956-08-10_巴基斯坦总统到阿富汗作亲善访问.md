Title: Pakistani President Pays Goodwill Visit to Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, August 8th: The Pakistan United Press reported that Pakistani President Mirza and Afghan King Zahir Shah held talks in Kabul on the afternoon of August 7th.

    The talks, held at the King's Palace in Kabul, took place in a pleasant and friendly atmosphere.

    When President Mirza flew to Kabul on August 7th for a goodwill visit to Afghanistan, Afghan King Zahir Shah personally greeted him at Kabul Airport.

    Kabul newspapers published editorials hoping that President Mirza's visit would find a peaceful solution to the differences between Pakistan and Afghanistan.

### 巴基斯坦总统到阿富汗作亲善访问

1956-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总统到阿富汗作亲善访问
    新华社喀喇蚩8日电　据巴基斯坦联合通讯社报道，巴基斯坦总统米尔扎和阿富汗国王查希尔·沙阿八月七日下午在喀布尔举行了会谈。
    在喀布尔国王王宫举行的会谈是在愉快和友好的气氛中进行的。
    当米尔扎总统八月七日飞到喀布尔对阿富汗进行亲善访问的时候，阿富汗国王查希尔·沙阿亲自在喀布尔机场迎接。
    喀布尔的报纸发表社论，希望米尔扎总统的访问将对巴基斯坦和阿富汗两国间的分歧找出一个和平解决的办法。
