Title: Guangzhou Establishes a Manchu Primary School
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 30th - The Guangzhou Municipal Education Bureau has decided to establish the "Guangzhou Manchu Primary School." This school is being rebuilt from the former "Guangzhou Guoguang Primary School." The "Guangzhou Guoguang Primary School" was formerly private, but was changed to public in 1954. This school is located in an area where the Manchu people of Guangzhou live in concentrated communities.

This school has decided to enroll two first-grade classes in the next semester, and more than sixty Manchu students have already been admitted to the school.

### 广州成立一所满族小学

1956-08-01
第7版()
专栏：

    广州成立一所满族小学
    据新华社广州30日电　广州市教育局决定成立“广州市满族小学”。这所学校是由原“广州国光小学”改建的。“广州国光小学”从前是私立，1954年改为公立，这个学校设在广州市满族人民集居的地区。
    这个学校决定在今年下学期招收两班一年级学生，现在已经有六十多名满族学生考上了这个学校。
