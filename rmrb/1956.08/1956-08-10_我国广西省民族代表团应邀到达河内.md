Title: Guangxi Province Ethnic Delegation of China Arrives in Hanoi at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 9th. The ethnic delegation of Guangxi Province, China, invited to attend the founding conference of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region in Vietnam, arrived in Hanoi on the night of the 8th. They were welcomed by more than 200 representatives from the Vietnamese government, mass organizations, and ethnic minorities of Vietnam.

### 我国广西省民族代表团应邀到达河内

1956-08-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我国广西省民族代表团应邀到达河内
    据新华社河内9日电　应邀到越南参加越北自治区成立大会的中国广西省民族代表团已经在八日夜到达河内。他们受到越南政府、群众团体和越南少数民族的代表二百多人的欢迎。
