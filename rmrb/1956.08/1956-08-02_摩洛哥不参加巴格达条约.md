Title: Morocco Will Not Join Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1956-08-02
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st -- According to a Damascus dispatch from the Associated Press: Moroccan Minister of Justice, Abdul Karim Tuwaymi, told reporters in Damascus on July 30th that Morocco has decided not to join the Baghdad Military Pact.

Tuwaymi is currently traveling in Arab countries. He also told reporters that newly independent Morocco supports Algeria's struggle for independence.

### 摩洛哥不参加巴格达条约

1956-08-02
第6版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥不参加巴格达条约
    新华社1日讯　据美联社大马士革消息：摩洛哥司法大臣阿布杜勒克里姆·托韦米七月三十日在大马士革对记者说，摩洛哥已经决定不参加巴格达军事条约。
    托韦米目前正在阿拉伯国家旅行。他还告诉记者，已经获得独立的摩洛哥支持阿尔及利亚为独立而斗争。
