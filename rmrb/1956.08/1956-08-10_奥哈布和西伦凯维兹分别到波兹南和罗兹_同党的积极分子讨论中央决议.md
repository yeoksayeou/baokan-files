Title: Ochab and Cyrankiewicz Go to Poznan and Lodz Respectively: Party Activists Discuss Central Resolutions
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 9th: Party activists meetings were held in many provinces and cities in Poland on August 7th to discuss the recent 7th Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and its resolutions. Members of the Political Bureau and the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party participated in these meetings respectively.

    First Secretary of the Central Committee, Ochab, attended the party activists meeting in Poznan Province. The meeting focused particularly on issues such as the working people's trust in the Party, the activities of the People's Council, the security organs and the militia, and the political and economic tasks facing the Party organizations. Referring to the Poznan incident, the participants in the meeting, in addition to pointing out the need to be vigilant against the conspiracies of the imperialist elements, also pointed out the need to improve the Party's work and strengthen the Party's ties with the masses, listen to the opinions of the masses, and take measures more quickly to solve the practical problems of the masses. Ochab made a concluding speech at the meeting, pointing out the main tasks facing the Poznan Party organization in accordance with the resolutions of the 7th Plenary Session of the Party Central Committee.

    Political Bureau member and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, participated in the party activists meeting in Lodz. On August 7th, he inspected construction work in Lodz and visited the Marchlewski Textile Factory. He had cordial talks with the workers and discussed their work and life. Cyrankiewicz spoke at a rally at the factory about the work being done by the Polish United Workers' Party and the government to improve the living standards of the broad masses of the people more quickly.

### 奥哈布和西伦凯维兹分别到波兹南和罗兹  同党的积极分子讨论中央决议

1956-08-10
第5版()
专栏：

    奥哈布和西伦凯维兹分别到波兹南和罗兹
    同党的积极分子讨论中央决议
    据新华社华沙9日电　波兰许多省、市在八月七日举行了党的积极分子会议，讨论最近举行的波兰统一工人党中央委员会第七次全体会议和它的决议。波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局和中央委员会的委员们分别参加了这些会议。
    中央委员会第一书记奥哈布出席了波兹南省的党积极分子会议。这次会议特别着重讨论了劳动人民对党的信任问题、人民会议的活动、保安机关和民兵以及党组织所面临的政治和经济任务等问题。在谈到波兹南事件的时候，会议参加者除指出对帝国主义分子的阴谋活动要提高警惕以外，还指出必须改进党的工作和加强党同群众的联系，倾听群众的意见和更快地采取措施以解决群众的实际问题。奥哈布在会上作了总结发言，他指出了波兹南党组织根据党中央第七次全体会议的决议所面临的主要任务。
    政治局委员、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹参加了罗兹的党积极分子会议。他在八月七日视察了罗兹的建设工作并且访问了马尔赫列夫斯基纺织厂。他同工人们进行了亲切的谈话并且和他们讨论了工作和工人生活中的问题。西伦凯维兹在这个工厂的集会上谈到了波兰统一工人党和政府为了更快地改善广大人民群众的生活水平所进行的工作。
