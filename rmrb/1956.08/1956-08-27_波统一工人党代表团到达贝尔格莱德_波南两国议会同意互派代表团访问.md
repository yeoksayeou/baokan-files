Title: Polish United Workers' Party Delegation Arrives in Belgrade; Polish and Yugoslav Parliaments Agree to Exchange Delegation Visits
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, August 26th -- According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency: The delegation of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party arrived in Belgrade from Warsaw on the 25th by special plane. The head of the delegation is W. Mazur, member of the Central Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee Secretariat.
    Vukmanovic, member of the Central Executive Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, and others welcomed the delegation at the airport.
    The delegation will stay in Yugoslavia for about a week. During this period, the representatives will hold talks with the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia in Belgrade. In addition, they will also visit some places in Croatia and Slovenia.
    Xinhua News Agency, August 26th -- According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency on the 25th: The Polish Parliament and the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia have agreed to exchange delegation visits.
    The Polish delegation will visit Yugoslavia on September 3rd, and the Yugoslav delegation plans to go to Poland on October 10th.
    A statement issued by the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia stated that the exchange of parliamentary delegations will undoubtedly contribute to the further development of comprehensive cooperation between the peoples of Poland and Yugoslavia.

### 波统一工人党代表团到达贝尔格莱德  波南两国议会同意互派代表团访问

1956-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    波统一工人党代表团到达贝尔格莱德
    波南两国议会同意互派代表团访问
    新华社26日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道：波兰统一工人党中央委员会代表团25日乘专机从华沙到达贝尔格莱德。代表团团长是中央政治局委员、中央委员会书记处书记弗·马茹尔。
    南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央执行委员会委员伏克曼诺维奇等到机场欢迎代表团。
    代表团将在南斯拉夫逗留约一个星期。在这期间，代表们将在贝尔格莱德同南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会进行会谈。另外，他们还将访问克罗地亚和斯洛文尼亚的一些地方。
    新华社26日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社25日报道：波兰国会和南斯拉夫联邦国民议会已经同意互派代表团访问。
    波兰代表团将在9月3日访问南斯拉夫，南斯拉夫代表团计划在10月10日前往波兰。
    南斯拉夫联邦国民议会发表的公告说，互派议会代表团无疑地会有助于波兰和南斯拉夫两国人民之间全面合作的进一步发展。
