Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Speaker Sartono
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 4th: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai received Indonesian Parliament Speaker Sartono and his wife today, as well as the visiting members of parliament, and had lunch with them.

Present at the reception and lunch were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun and Li Xiannian, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Zhen and his wife, Committee Member Liao Chengzhi, Deputy Secretary-General Xin Zhi Chao, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Shu Liang, and Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia Zhong Qingfa.

Indonesian Ambassador to China Wiyopoanoto was also present.

### 周恩来总理接见沙多诺议长

1956-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见沙多诺议长
    新华社4日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天接见了印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺和夫人以及同来访问的国会议员等，并且同他们共进午餐。
    接见和进餐时，在座的有国务院副总理陈云、李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真和夫人，委员廖承志，副秘书长辛志超，外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮，中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆参赞钟庆发。
    印度尼西亚驻中国大使维约普拉诺托也在座。
