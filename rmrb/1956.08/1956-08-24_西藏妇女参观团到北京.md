Title: Tibetan Women's Visiting Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd. More than two hundred women from all walks of life in Beijing warmly welcomed the first women's visiting group from Tibet at the Qianmen Railway Station tonight. The visiting group includes sixty-one women from various regions and strata of Tibet.
    The head of the Tibetan Women's Visiting Group, Tangmai·Gongjue Baimu, and the deputy heads, Caisu·Caiyang and Bangda·Zeren Quji, presented Hada scarves to Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Sakongliao, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Yang Jingren, who came to welcome them. Then, a group of children presented flowers to all members of the visiting group.
    Also present at the station to welcome them were Cao Guanqun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Wang Chunping, Vice-Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, Xiong Shouqi, Vice-President of the Central Institute for Nationalities, Dunwang·Jianzan Zhaba, Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, and Lian Huizhu, Acting Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing, and others.

### 西藏妇女参观团到北京

1956-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏妇女参观团到北京
    新华社23日讯　北京市各界妇女二百多人今晚在前门车站热烈地欢迎来自西藏的第一个妇女参观团。这个参观团包括西藏各地区、各阶层的妇女共六十一人。
    西藏妇女参观团团长汤麦·公觉白姆、副团长才苏·才羊、邦达·泽仁曲吉下车后，向前来欢迎的中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、民族事务委员会副主任萨空了、杨静仁献了哈达。接着，一群少年儿童向参观团全体团员献花。
    到车站欢迎的还有全国民主妇联书记处书记曹冠群，北京市民主妇女联合会副主席王春平，中央民族学院副院长熊寿祺，达赖驻京办事处处长顿旺·坚赞扎巴，班禅驻京办事处代处长联慧珠等人。
