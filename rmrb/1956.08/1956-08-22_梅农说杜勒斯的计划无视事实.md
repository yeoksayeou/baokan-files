Title: Menon Says Dulles' Plan Ignores Facts
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 21st -- According to TASS, London, August 21st: The head of the Indian delegation to the London conference, Menon, said at a press conference today that the Indian delegation believes that the proposal put forward by Dulles yesterday is unacceptable to the Egyptian government. He said that Dulles' plan ignores the facts. The Egyptian government has already nationalized the Suez Canal Company, but Dulles' plan proposes to internationalize something that has already been nationalized. Menon said that it is inconceivable that the Egyptian government would give up its national independence for an international agreement.

### 梅农说杜勒斯的计划无视事实

1956-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    梅农说杜勒斯的计划无视事实
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社伦敦二十一日讯：出席伦敦会议的印度代表团团长梅农今天在记者招待会上说：印度代表团认为，杜勒斯昨天提出的建议是埃及政府所不能接受的。他说，杜勒斯的计划无视事实。埃及政府已经把苏彝士运河公司收归国有，而杜勒斯的计划却建议把已经收归国有的东西国际化。梅农说，不能想像，埃及政府会为了一个国际协定而放弃自己的民族独立。
