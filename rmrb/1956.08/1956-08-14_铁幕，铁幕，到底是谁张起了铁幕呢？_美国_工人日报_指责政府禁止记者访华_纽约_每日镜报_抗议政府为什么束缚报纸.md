Title: "Iron Curtain, Iron Curtain, Who Exactly Has Erected the Iron Curtain?"
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th, New York dispatch: The New York "Daily Worker" commented on the U.S. State Department's ban on American reporters visiting China, saying: "Iron Curtain, Iron Curtain, who exactly has erected the Iron Curtain?"

	The newspaper's August 8th editorial said: "The State Department is not permitting the free exchange of news. Unfortunately for the State Department, it cannot wave a magic wand and make a quarter of the world's population cease to exist."

	The "Daily Worker" said: "The only thing the State Department can do is to keep the American people from knowing the news, while it can continue to maintain an artificial state of tension—and at the same time, the U.S. government wants to pose as the world's standard-bearer of peace."

Xinhua News Agency, 13th, New York dispatch: Even the extreme right-wing Hearst newspaper chain in the United States has criticized the U.S. State Department for banning American reporters from visiting China.

	The New York "Daily Mirror" published an editorial on August 11th entitled "Why Shackle the Press?" protesting to the State Department. It said: "It is meaningless for the State Department to ban American reporters from visiting Communist China."

	The newspaper believes that the State Department should not "on the basis of some strange reasoning" arbitrarily assume that "its actions have earned it a diplomatic victory." It appealed: "Those who decide our foreign policy must now face the fact that Communist China will not disappear simply because we ignore it." (Attached picture)

### “铁幕，铁幕，到底是谁张起了铁幕呢？”  美国“工人日报”指责政府禁止记者访华  纽约“每日镜报”抗议政府为什么束缚报纸

1956-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    “铁幕，铁幕，到底是谁张起了铁幕呢？”
    美国“工人日报”指责政府禁止记者访华
    纽约“每日镜报”抗议政府为什么束缚报纸
    新华社11日讯　纽约消息：纽约“工人日报”在评论美国国务院禁止美国记者访问中国这件事情的时候说：“铁幕，铁幕，到底是谁张起了铁幕呢？”
    这家报纸八月八日的社论说：“国务院不准发展消息的自由交流。对国务院说来很不幸的是：它不能挥舞一下魔杖就使世界上四分之一的人口不再存在。”
    “工人日报”说：“国务院所能做的唯一的事就是不让美国人民知道消息，同时它可以继续保持人为的紧张局面——而就在这个时候，美国政府却想冒充世界的和平旗手。”
    新华社13日讯　纽约消息：连美国极右派的赫斯特系报纸也指责美国国务院禁止美国记者到中国去访问。
    八月十一日的纽约“每日镜报”发表了以“为什么束缚报纸？”为题的社论，向国务院提出抗议。它说：“国务院禁止美国记者访问共产党中国是没有什么意义的。”
    这家报纸认为国务院不应该“根据某种奇怪的推理”而凭空认为“它的行动已经使它获得了外交胜利”。它呼吁说：“决定我们外交政策的人现在是面对下述事实的时候了：共产党中国不会仅仅因为我们不理睬它就会消灭。”（附图片）
