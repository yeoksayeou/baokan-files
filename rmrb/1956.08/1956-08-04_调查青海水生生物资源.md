Title: Investigation of Qinghai Aquatic Biological Resources
Author:
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 3

    Investigating Qinghai Aquatic Biological Resources
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Lake Survey Team of the Institute of Hydrobiology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, will spend approximately two months conducting aquatic biological surveys and research in Qinghai Province. They have decided to focus on Qinghai Lake and also visit Chaka and other places in the Haixi Mongol, Tibetan and Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture to investigate the types of aquatic organisms in the lakes and examine the physical and chemical properties of the lake waters in order to develop and utilize these lakes.
    Qinghai Province has many lakes. The famous Qinghai Lake is China's largest saltwater lake, with an area of approximately 4,200 square kilometers and is rich in scaleless fish.

### 调查青海水生生物资源

1956-08-04
第3版()
专栏：

    调查青海水生生物资源
    据新华社讯　中国科学院水生生物研究所湖泊调查队将以两个月左右的时间，在青海省进行水生生物调查研究工作。他们决定以青海湖为重点，并去海西蒙藏哈萨克族自治州的茶卡等地，调查各湖泊内水生生物的种类，检查各湖泊水的物理、化学性质，以便开发和利用这些湖泊。
    青海省湖泊很多。著名的青海湖是中国最大的咸水湖，面积约有四千二百多平方公里，盛产无鳞鱼。
