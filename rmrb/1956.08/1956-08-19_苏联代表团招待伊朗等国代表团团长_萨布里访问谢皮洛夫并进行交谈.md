Title: Soviet Delegation Hosts Heads of Delegations from Iran and Other Countries; Sabri Visits Shepilov and Holds Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, from TASS: Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov hosted a banquet on August 17 for the heads of delegations from Iran, Pakistan, Ceylon, and Ethiopia who are participating in the London conference discussing the Suez Canal issue.

Those attending the banquet included Iranian Foreign Minister Ardalan, Pakistani Foreign Minister Choudhry, Ethiopian Foreign Minister Habte-Wold, and Ceylon's High Commissioner to the United Kingdom, Corea, and others.

On the same day, the Director of the Political Bureau of the President's Office of the Egyptian Republic, Ali Sabri, visited Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov and held talks with him.

French Foreign Minister Pineau hosted a banquet for Shepilov on the 17th.

### 苏联代表团招待伊朗等国代表团团长  萨布里访问谢皮洛夫并进行交谈

1956-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团招待伊朗等国代表团团长
    萨布里访问谢皮洛夫并进行交谈
    新华社据塔斯社伦敦讯　苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫八月十七日设宴招待参加讨论苏彝士运河问题的伦敦会议的伊朗、巴基斯坦、锡兰和埃塞俄比亚的代表团团长。
    出席宴会的有伊朗外交大臣阿达兰、巴基斯坦外交部长乔德里、埃塞俄比亚外交大臣哈勃特沃德和锡兰驻英国高级专员柯里亚等。
    同一天，埃及共和国总统府政治局长阿里·萨布里访问了苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫，并且和他作了交谈。
    法国外交部长比诺十七日曾经设宴招待谢皮洛夫。
