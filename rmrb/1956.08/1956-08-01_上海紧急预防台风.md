Title: Shanghai Urgently Prevents Typhoon

Author:

Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 31st. Shanghai entered a period of strong typhoon alert this afternoon. At 9:45 PM, the Shanghai Port Harbor Supervision posted signals predicting winds of level 6 to 7 within 24 hours at four signal stations including Wusongkou and Pudong. All ships anchored in Shanghai Port began to sail to safe areas to avoid the storm. The Shanghai Flood Prevention Headquarters convened an emergency meeting for typhoon prevention. According to reports, more than 100,000 people have been mobilized in factories, warehouses, transportation departments, etc. throughout the city to carry out inspections and preventive work overnight. The sea embankment and river embankment repair teams in the East and West Suburbs have decided to start day and night patrols from today.

### 上海紧急预防台风

1956-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    上海紧急预防台风
    据新华社上海31日电　上海市在今天下午已经进入强台风警戒时期。下午九时四十五分，上海港港务监督在吴淞口、浦东等四个信号台上挂起了预示二十四小时内将有风力六级到七级的信号。停泊在上海港内所有的船只，开始驶往安全地区避风。上海市防汛总指挥部召集了预防台风紧急会议。据汇报，全市工厂、仓库、交通部门等已经动员十多万人，连夜进行检查和预防工作。东郊区和西郊区的海塘、江堤修防大队决定从今天起开始日夜值班巡逻。
