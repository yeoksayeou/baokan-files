Title: Cambodian Economic Delegation to China Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th -- The four-member economic delegation from the Kingdom of Cambodia to China arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon. Those who welcomed them at the station included: Lin Haiyun, Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade, Yang Haolu, Director of the Fourth Bureau, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The delegation is led by Yang Anh, Inspector of the Engineering Group of the Ministry of Planning of the Kingdom of Cambodia. They will reside in Beijing for an extended period while the Sino-Cambodian Economic Aid Agreement is implemented.

### 柬埔寨驻华经济代表团到京

1956-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨驻华经济代表团到京
    新华社14日讯　柬埔寨王国驻华经济代表团一行四人今天下午乘火车到达北京。到车站欢迎的有：对外贸易部部长助理林海云，第四局局长杨浩庐和外交部礼宾司副司长马振武。
    代表团由柬埔寨王国计划部工程组督察长杨安率领。在执行中柬经济援助协定期间，他们将长驻北京。
