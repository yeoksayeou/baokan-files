Title: Soong Ching-ling Arrives in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 12th: Soong Ching-ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, and her entourage arrived in Rangoon from Kunming on August 11th on their way to Indonesia.

    Welcoming Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling at the airport were: Burmese Deputy Prime Minister Sao Hkun Hkio, Nu's wife Daw Mya Yee, Aung San's wife Daw Khin Kyi, Minister of Relief and Resettlement Sao Tun Aye, Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung, Supreme Court Judge U Min Thein, and Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Ba Lwin.

    Also welcoming at the airport were: Chinese Ambassador Yao Zhongming and personnel from the Chinese Embassy, Indonesian Chargé d'affaires Suwanto, foreign diplomatic envoys in Rangoon, and representatives of the Chinese community in Rangoon.

### 宋庆龄到仰光

1956-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄到仰光
    新华社仰光12日电　中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄和随行人员在赴印度尼西亚途中于八月十一日从昆明到达仰光。
    到机场欢迎宋庆龄副委员长的有：缅甸副总理藻昆卓、努夫人都妙意、昂山夫人都庆枝、救济和安置部长绍吞埃、缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂、最高法院法官吴敏登和外交部常任秘书巴林顿。
    到机场欢迎的还有：中国大使姚仲明及中国大使馆人员、印度尼西亚代办苏旺多和在仰光的各国外交使节、仰光的华侨代表。
