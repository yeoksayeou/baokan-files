Title: The United States Attempts to Take the Opportunity to Create Tensions; Chief of Naval Operations Shouts that the United States Must Control the Mediterranean; Defense Department Officials Say the Sixth Fleet is on "Constant Alert"

Author:

Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 31st dispatch: Washington news: Yesterday, the testimony of U.S. Chief of Naval Operations Arleigh Burke from a secret Senate hearing not long ago was released here. He emphasized that the United States must find a way to control the Mediterranean.

This admiral said that if the U.S. aircraft carrier task force "cannot operate in the Mediterranean, we will lose the Mediterranean, so for this reason, we had better be able to operate in the Mediterranean."

Arleigh Burke testified before a special committee of the Senate before Egypt announced the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company. He said: If a war occurs, control of the Mediterranean can only be retained in the hands of "free world" countries if the U.S. Sixth Fleet can remain there and be able to fight there.

Xinhua News Agency, 31st dispatch: Washington news: U.S. Department of Defense officials said on July 30th that the U.S. Sixth Fleet in the Mediterranean is on "constant alert."

These officials said: The Sixth Fleet is currently in the western Mediterranean, near ports such as Naples, Genoa, and Algiers. But it can quickly reach Port Said in the eastern Mediterranean, ready to protect American "interests" anywhere and anytime.

### 美国企图乘机制造紧张局势  海军作战部长叫嚣美国必须控制地中海  国防部官员说第六舰队在“随时警戒”着

1956-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    美国企图乘机制造紧张局势
    海军作战部长叫嚣美国必须控制地中海
    国防部官员说第六舰队在“随时警戒”着
    新华社31日讯　华盛顿消息：昨天这里公布了美国海军作战部长阿莱·伯克不久以前在参议院的一次秘密作证会上的证词。他强调指出美国必须设法控制地中海。
    这个海军上将说，如果美国航空母舰特种舰队“不能在地中海活动的话，我们就失掉了地中海，因此，为了这一点我们最好能够在地中海活动。”
    阿莱·伯克是在埃及宣布把苏彝士运河公司收归国有以前在参议院的一个特别委员会作证的。他说：如果发生战争，只有在美国第六舰队能够留在那里而且能够在那里作战的时候，地中海的控制权才可以保留在“自由世界”国家的手里。
    新华社31日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国防部官员七月三十日说，美国在地中海的第六舰队在“随时警戒”着。
    这些官员说：第六舰队目前正在地中海西部，在那不勒斯、热那亚和阿尔及利亚等港口附近。但是它可以很快地到达地中海东部的塞得港，随时准备在任何地方和任何时候保护美国的“利益”。
