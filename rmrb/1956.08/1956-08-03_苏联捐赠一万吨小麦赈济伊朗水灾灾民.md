Title: The Soviet Union Donates 10,000 Tons of Wheat to Relieve Iranian Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd: According to TASS, the Executive Committee of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union has decided to donate 10,000 tons of wheat to relieve residents of Iran who recently suffered from floods.

### 苏联捐赠一万吨小麦赈济伊朗水灾灾民

1956-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联捐赠一万吨小麦赈济伊朗水灾灾民
    据新华社2日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会执行委员会决定，捐赠一万吨小麦来赈济伊朗最近遭受水灾的居民。
