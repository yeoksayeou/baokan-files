Title: Chinese Gymnastics Team to East Germany
Author:
Date: 1956-08-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 27th - The Chinese Gymnastics Team, composed of twenty-two outstanding male and female gymnasts, left Beijing by plane today for the German Democratic Republic.

　　The Chinese Gymnastics Team is visiting the German Democratic Republic at the invitation of the German Democratic Republic to hold friendly competitions with male and female gymnasts from the German Democratic Republic. After their visit to the German Democratic Republic, they will also go to Czechoslovakia to hold friendly competitions with Czechoslovakian gymnasts.

　　Among the Chinese Gymnastics Team's eleven male athletes is Bao Naijian, 19, the 1956 national all-around champion; among the eleven female athletes is Chen Xiaozhang, the 1956 national all-around champion. The gymnastics team also includes a leader, instructor, piano accompanist, doctor, and translator, totaling eleven people.

### 中国体操队去民主德国

1956-08-28
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中国体操队去民主德国
    新华社27日讯　由二十二名优秀男、女体操运动员组成的中国体操队，今天坐飞机离开北京前往德意志民主共和国。
    中国体操队是应邀到德意志民主共和国同德意志民主共和国的男、女体操运动员举行友谊比赛的。他们在访问德意志民主共和国后，还要去捷克斯洛伐克同捷克斯洛伐克的体操运动员举行友谊比赛。
    中国体操队的十一名男运动员当中，包括1956年全能比赛的全国冠军、十九岁的鲍乃健；十一名女运动员当中，也包括1956年全能比赛的全国冠军陈孝彰。体操队中还有领队、指导、钢琴伴奏、医生和翻译共十一人。
