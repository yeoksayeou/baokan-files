Title: Pollitt Issues Statement Condemning Bonn Authorities' Declaration of the German Communist Party as Illegal
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 18th: Harry Pollitt, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain, issued a statement on August 17th, condemning the Bonn authorities for declaring the German Communist Party illegal.

The statement demanded that the British people not ignore this matter, and called on them to lodge widespread protests with the Bonn government.

### 波立特发表声明  斥波恩当局宣布德共非法

1956-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    波立特发表声明
    斥波恩当局宣布德共非法
    据新华社伦敦18日电　英国共产党执行委员会主席波立特八月十七日发表声明，谴责波恩当局宣布德国共产党为非法。
    声明要求英国人民对这件事不要置之不理，并且号召他们向波恩政府提出广泛的抗议。
