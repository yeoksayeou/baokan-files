Title: Protesting the Convening of the London Conference, Egypt Will Halt All Activities for Five Minutes Today
Author:
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 15th (TASS): According to a report from the Middle East News Agency, all work and transportation in Egypt will stop for five minutes at noon on August 16th (10:00 AM Greenwich Mean Time) when the London Conference opens.

The Middle East News Agency said that this five-minute cessation of activity is a protest against the Western countries' attempt to impose imperialist rule on the Suez Canal.

### 抗议伦敦会议的召开  埃及全国今日停止活动五分钟

1956-08-16
第6版()
专栏：

    抗议伦敦会议的召开
    埃及全国今日停止活动五分钟
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社开罗讯：据中东通讯社报道，在八月十六日中午（格林威治时间早晨十点钟）伦敦会议开幕的时候，全埃及一切工作和交通将停止五分钟。
    中东通讯社说，这五分钟的停止活动是表示对西方国家企图把帝国主义统治强加于苏彝士运河的一种抗议。
