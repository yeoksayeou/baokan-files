Title: Yemen Government Decides to Recognize Our Country; Our Government Suggests the Formal Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Mohammed Abdullah Al-Amri, Yemen's Minister of State and Deputy Foreign Minister, visited Chen Jiakang, China's Ambassador to Egypt, in Cairo on the 21st, and handed him a letter addressed to Ambassador Chen Jiakang. The letter stated that he was instructed to convey the following notification to the Chinese government: His Majesty the King of Yemen and his government have decided to recognize the government of the People's Republic of China as the legitimate government of China. In the letter, Al-Amri congratulated the Chinese government on its achievements in safeguarding world peace and national freedom, and wished the Chinese government continued success and the Chinese people continuous progress and prosperity. He also expressed hope to strengthen the firm friendship and good cooperation between the governments and peoples of China and Yemen.

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, wrote a letter on the 22nd to Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Yemen. The full text is as follows:
His Royal Highness Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Yemen:

I am pleased to receive, through Mr. Chen Jiakang, our Ambassador to Egypt, the official notification of August 21st from His Excellency Mohammed Abdullah Al-Amri, Minister of State and Deputy Foreign Minister of your country, that His Majesty the King of Yemen and the Government of the Kingdom of Yemen have decided to recognize the People's Republic of China. ### 也门政府决定承认我国  我国政府建议两国正式建立外交关系

1956-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    也门政府决定承认我国
    我国政府建议两国正式建立外交关系
    新华社23日讯　也门王国国务大臣兼副外交大臣穆罕默德·阿卜杜拉·阿默利二十一日在开罗访问了中国驻埃及大使陈家康，当面递交了他写给陈家康大使的信。信中说他奉命请陈家康大使转达中国政府以下的通知：也门国王陛下和他的政府已经决定承认中华人民共和国政府为中国合法的政府。阿默利在信中对中国政府在维护世界和平和民族自由的道路上所获得的成就表示祝贺，并祝中国政府永远成功，中国人民不断进步和繁荣。他同时希望加强中国，也门两国政府和人民之间的牢固友谊和良好合作。
    新华社23日讯　中华人民共和国周恩来总理兼外交部长二十二日写信给也门王国副首相兼外交大臣塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔。全文如下：也门王国副首相兼外交大臣塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔殿下：
    我高兴地接到我国驻埃及大使陈家康先生转来八月二十一日贵国国务大臣兼副外交大臣穆罕默德·阿卜杜拉·阿默利阁下，关于也门国王陛下和也门王国政府已经决定承认中华人民共和国的正式通知。我代表中华人民共和国政府热烈地欢迎贵国国王陛下和贵国政府这一友好的决定。我深信，这一符合于亚非会议精神的我们两国关系的新发展，将不仅使两国人民之间的友谊得到进一步的发展和巩固，并且将有利于促进亚非国家的友好合作和维护世界和平的共同事业。
    为了实现中、也两国的友好愿望，中国政府建议两国正式建立外交关系并且互派使节。
         中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长　周恩来
            　1956年8月22日于北京
