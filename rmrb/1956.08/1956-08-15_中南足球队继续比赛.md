Title: The Central South Football Team Continues to Compete
Author:
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 13th: Belgrade dispatch: The Chinese football team held a match in Sarajevo, Yugoslavia on the 12th. The opponent was the football team of the People's Republic of Bosnia and Herzegovina. The Chinese football team lost to the opponent with a score of one to six.

### 中南足球队继续比赛

1956-08-15
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中南足球队继续比赛
    新华社布拉格13日电　贝尔格莱德消息：中国足球队十二日在南斯拉夫的萨拉热窝举行了一次比赛。比赛对手是波斯尼亚—黑塞哥维那人民共和国足球队。中国足球队以一比六败于对方。
