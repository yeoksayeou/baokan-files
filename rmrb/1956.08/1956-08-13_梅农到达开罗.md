Title: Menon Arrives in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 5

    Menon Arrives in Cairo
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 12th: V.K. Krishna Menon, the Indian Minister Without Portfolio, arrived in Cairo this morning. Egyptian President Nasser immediately received him.
    Menon is the head of the Indian delegation attending the conference on the Suez Canal issue to be held in London. He passed through Cairo on his way to London.

### 梅农到达开罗

1956-08-13
第5版()
专栏：

    梅农到达开罗
    新华社12日讯　开罗消息：印度不管部部长梅农今天早晨到达开罗。埃及总统纳赛尔立刻接见了他。
    梅农是出席将在伦敦举行的讨论苏彝士运河问题会议的印度代表团团长。他在前往伦敦途中经过开罗。
