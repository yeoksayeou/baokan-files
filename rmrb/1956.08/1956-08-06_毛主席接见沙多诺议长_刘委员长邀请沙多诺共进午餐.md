Title: Chairman Mao Meets Speaker Sartono / Chairman Liu Invites Sartono to Lunch
Author:
Date: 1956-08-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Qinhuangdao, August 5th: Chairman Mao Zedong received Indonesian Speaker of Parliament Sartono and visiting members of parliament at a villa by the sea in Qinhuangdao today.

    Chairman Mao Zedong had a long and friendly conversation with the distinguished guests. Speaker Sartono conveyed President Sukarno's greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong, who expressed his gratitude. When speaking about President Sukarno's speech in the United States, Chairman Mao Zedong said that President Sukarno not only expressed the Indonesian people's desire for independence, autonomy, and peace, but also spoke for the people of Asian and African countries.

    Speaker Sartono, in his talks with Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and other state leaders, expressed the hope that China would support and assist Indonesia in initiating an Asian-African parliamentary conference. During the talks, the relevant Chinese state leaders expressed their agreement and support for Speaker Sartono's initiative.

    Present at the meeting today were Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Guo Moruo and Peng Zhen, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; and Zhang Wentian, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs.

    Anwar Sani, Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy in China, was also present at the meeting.

    Xinhua News Agency, Qinhuangdao, August 5th: Indonesian Speaker of Parliament Sartono and the visiting members of parliament were invited to lunch with Chairman Liu Shaoqi at the Qinhuangdao seaside today.

    Anwar Sani, Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy in China, was also invited.

    Also attending the lunch were Guo Moruo and Peng Zhen, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, members of the Standing Committee recuperating at the seaside, Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Cheng Qian and Zhang Zhizhong, Vice-Chairmen of the National Defense Council, Zhang Wentian, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs, and other ministers from various ministries who were recuperating at the seaside.

    Speaker Sartono enjoyed the picturesque scenery of the Qinhuangdao seaside this morning.

### 毛主席接见沙多诺议长  刘委员长邀请沙多诺共进午餐

1956-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见沙多诺议长
    刘委员长邀请沙多诺共进午餐
    新华社秦皇岛5日电　毛泽东主席今天在秦皇岛海滨一座别墅里接见了印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺和同来访问的国会议员等。
    毛泽东主席同贵宾们进行了长时间的友好谈话。沙多诺议长向毛泽东主席转致了苏加诺总统的问好，毛泽东主席向他表示了谢意。在谈到苏加诺总统在美国演说时，毛泽东主席认为苏加诺总统不仅表达了印度尼西亚人民要求独立、自主、和平的愿望，也代表了亚非国家人民说了话。
    沙多诺议长先后同刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理以及其他国家领导工作人员交谈时，曾经希望中国方面能支持和协助印度尼西亚发起召开亚非国家议会会议。在交谈时，中国的有关国家领导工作人员对沙多诺议长这个倡议，都表示赞同和支持。
    今天接见的时候，在座的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，彭真，外交部副部长张闻天。
    接见的时候，印度尼西亚驻华大使馆参赞安瓦·萨尼也在座。
    新华社秦皇岛5日电　印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺和同来访问的国会议员们，今天中午在秦皇岛海滨应邀和刘少奇委员长共进午餐。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使馆参赞安瓦·萨尼也应邀出席。
    共进午餐的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、彭真和在海滨休养的委员，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席程潜、张治中，外交部副部长张闻天和在海滨休养的其他各部部长。
    沙多诺议长在今天上午观赏了风景如画的秦皇岛海滨风光。
