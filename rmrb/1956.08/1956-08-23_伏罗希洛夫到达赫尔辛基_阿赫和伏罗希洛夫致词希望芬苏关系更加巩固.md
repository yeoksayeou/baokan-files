Title: Voroshilov Arrives in Helsinki; Ahe and Voroshilov Deliver Speeches Hoping for Stronger Finnish-Soviet Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, August 22nd: According to TASS, Helsinki, August 22nd: At the invitation of the President of Finland, the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, and his entourage arrived in Helsinki on August 21st for a visit to Finland.

Finnish President Kekkonen, Speaker of Parliament Sukselainen, Prime Minister Fagerholm, and others went to the station to welcome the distinguished Soviet guests.

Chairman Ahe of the Helsinki Municipal Council delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the citizens of the Finnish capital to Voroshilov at the station. He pointed out the great historical significance of this visit. He said that this visit once again powerfully proves that the good neighborly relations between Finland and the Soviet Union are developing in a good direction. He believes that this visit will further consolidate this relationship and further consolidate the prerequisites for peace and creative cooperation between the peoples of Finland and the Soviet Union and the peoples of all countries in the world.

Voroshilov delivered a reply speech. He said that we are very pleased that our visit will help to expand and consolidate contacts with the Finnish national leaders, which is conducive to further strengthening the good relations between our two good neighboring countries.

The Soviet people attach great importance to and respect this genuine good neighborly relationship; for the benefit of our two countries and for universal peace and security, this relationship has been developing well in recent years.

Voroshilov finally expressed his firm belief that the relations between the Soviet Union and Finland will certainly continue to develop in the future on the basis of genuine friendship and mutual trust.

### 伏罗希洛夫到达赫尔辛基  阿赫和伏罗希洛夫致词希望芬苏关系更加巩固

1956-08-23
第5版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫到达赫尔辛基
    阿赫和伏罗希洛夫致词希望芬苏关系更加巩固
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社赫尔辛基二十二日讯：应芬兰总统的邀请访问芬兰的苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和他的随行人员，在八月二十一日到达赫尔辛基。
    芬兰总统吉科宁、议会议长苏克西莱宁、总理法格霍姆等前往车站欢迎苏联贵宾。
    赫尔辛基市政委员会主席阿赫在车站上代表芬兰首都市民向伏罗希洛夫致欢迎词。他指出这次访问的重大历史意义。他说，这次访问再次有力地证明芬兰和苏联的睦邻关系是朝着良好的方向发展的。相信这次访问将使得这种关系更加巩固，使得和平以及芬苏两国人民之间和世界各国人民之间的创造性合作的前提更加巩固。
    伏罗希洛夫致答词。他说，我们很高兴，我们的访问将有助于扩大和巩固同芬兰国家领导人的接触，这是有利于进一步加强我们两个善邻国家的良好关系的。
    苏联人民十分重视和尊重这种真正的善邻关系；为了我们两国的利益、为了普遍和平和安全，近年来这种关系已经获得良好的发展。
    伏罗希洛夫最后表示坚信苏联和芬兰两国的关系今后一定继续在真正友好和相互信任的基础上获得发展。
