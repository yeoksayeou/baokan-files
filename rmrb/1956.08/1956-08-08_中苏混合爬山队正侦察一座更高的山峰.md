Title: Sino-Soviet Mixed Climbing Team is Reconnoitering a Higher Peak
Author: Yang Ke Xian
Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Muztagh Ata, August 6th (Xinhua reporter Yang Ke Xian): The Sino-Soviet mixed climbing team is conducting various activities centered on scientific research in groups at the foot of Muztagh Ata.

    On the afternoon of the 5th, the first scientific research team of five members set off on horseback from the base camp to the glacier areas on the west and south slopes of Muztagh Ata. Carrying glacier rulers, they studied the geographical conditions of the glaciers there and collected typical rocks from various canyons. After the 6th, two other scientific research teams will go to work on the northeast slope of Muztagh Ata and the west slope of Kongur Shan, 7,719 meters above sea level, north of Muztagh Ata. They will spend about ten days conducting a general study of the glacier conditions in the center of these two large snow mountains on the Pamir Plateau.

    Other Chinese and Soviet team members are conducting preliminary reconnaissance work on Kongur Shan, which is 173 meters higher than Muztagh Ata Peak. The purpose of the reconnaissance is to provide reliable information for future climbing of Kongur Shan. Some team members have already reached Kanchibairsu on the west side of Kongur Shan and established a new base camp for carrying out the above-mentioned activities.

### 中苏混合爬山队正侦察一座更高的山峰
杨克现
1956-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏混合爬山队
    正侦察一座更高的山峰
    据新华社慕士塔格山6日电  新华社记者杨克现报道：中、苏混合爬山队在慕士塔格山下分组进行以科学研究为中心的各种活动。
    五日下午，第一个科学研究小组五名队员骑马从大本营出发，前往慕士塔格山西坡和南坡的冰河地区。他们携带着冰河标尺，研究那里冰河的地理情况，并且搜集各个峡谷里的典型的岩石。六日以后，另外两个科学研究小组要去慕士塔格山东北坡和慕士塔格山北面的拔海7，719公尺的公格尔山西坡工作。他们将花十天左右的时间，对帕米尔高原上的这两个大雪山中心的冰河情况作一轮廓的研究。
    其他中、苏队员正对比慕士塔格峰还高173公尺的公格尔山作初步侦察工作，侦察的目的是给将来攀登公格尔山提供可靠资料。一部分队员已经到达了公格尔山西面的坎其拜尔苏，建立了从事上述活动的新的大本营。
