Title: Czechoslovakian National Women's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: The Czechoslovakian National Women's Basketball Team, a group of twenty-two individuals led by Mudra Vaclav, Chairman of the "Red Star" Sports Association of the Czechoslovakian Ministry of Interior, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 6th and 8th.

They are visiting China and engaging in friendly matches at the invitation of the Chinese government.

The Czechoslovakian National Women's Basketball Team is one of the strongest women's basketball teams in Europe. This June, they finished third in the European Women's Basketball Championship, behind only the Soviet Union and Hungary.

### 捷国家女子篮球队到京

1956-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    捷国家女子篮球队到京
    新华社8日讯　捷克斯洛伐克国家女子篮球队一行二十二人，由捷克斯洛伐克内务部“红星”体育协会主席穆德拉·瓦次拉夫率领，在六日和八日先后坐飞机到达北京。
    他们是应邀来中国访问和进行友谊比赛的。
    捷克斯洛伐克国家女子篮球队是欧洲实力最强的女子篮球队之一。今年六月，她们在欧洲女子篮球锦标赛中，仅次于苏联和匈牙利获得第三名。
