Title: Arab States Representatives Conference Drafts Economic Unification Agreement

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 6
    
    Arab States Representatives Conference
    Drafts Economic Unification Agreement

    Xinhua News Agency, August 24th, Beirut, TASS, August 23rd: The Arab States Representatives Conference concluded in Bhamdoun on August 22nd. The conference discussed the issue of economic unification of Arab countries.

    According to the published communique, the conference finalized a draft agreement on the establishment of economic unification of Arab countries. This draft will be submitted to the Arab League Political Committee for review.

### 阿拉伯国家代表会议  拟订经济统一协定草案

1956-08-27
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯国家代表会议
    拟订经济统一协定草案
    新华社24日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特二十三日讯：阿拉伯国家代表会议八月二十二日在勃哈姆敦闭幕，会议讨论了关于阿拉伯国家的经济统一问题。
    据发表的公报指出，会议最后拟订了关于建立阿拉伯国家经济统一的协定草案，这个草案将提交阿拉伯联盟政治委员会审核。
