Title: Deng Zihui Receives Albanian Agricultural Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd. Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Zihui received the entire Albanian agricultural delegation this morning. Albania's newly appointed Ambassador to China, Delo Balili, and First Secretary Bino were also present. Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan and Vice Minister Liu Ruilong were also present.

### 邓子恢接见阿尔巴尼亚农业代表团

1956-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    邓子恢接见阿尔巴尼亚农业代表团
    据新华社22日讯　国务院副总理邓子恢今日上午接见阿尔巴尼亚农业代表团全体人员。阿尔巴尼亚新任驻华大使德略·巴利里和一等秘书比诺也在座。在座的还有农业部部长廖鲁言、副部长刘瑞龙等。
