Title: Ceylon and Czechoslovakia Sign Economic Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 17th - Ceylon and Czechoslovakia signed a three-year economic cooperation agreement in Colombo on the 16th.

According to this agreement, Ceylon will repay Czechoslovakia for the capital goods supplied to it in installments over several years. Czechoslovakia will make the greatest possible use of these funds to purchase goods from Ceylon. Czechoslovakia will also provide Ceylon with experts, consultants, training equipment and degrees.

### 锡捷签订经济合作协定

1956-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

    锡捷签订经济合作协定
    据新华社新德里17日电　锡兰同捷克斯洛伐克十六日在科伦坡签订一项为期三年的经济合作协定。
    根据这项协定，锡兰将在数年内分期偿付捷克斯洛伐克供给它的资本货物。捷克斯洛伐克将尽量利用这些款项来购买锡兰的货物。捷克斯洛伐克还将向锡兰提供专家、顾问、训练设备和学位。
