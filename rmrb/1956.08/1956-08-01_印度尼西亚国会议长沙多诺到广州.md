Title: Indonesian Parliamentary Speaker Sartono Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 31st: Sartono, Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament, his wife, and eight members of parliament, who are visiting China at the invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi, arrived in Guangzhou today from Hong Kong by special train.

	Xin Zhi Chao, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, went to Shenzhen to greet the distinguished guests.

	Among those who welcomed them at the Guangzhou Railway Station were Zhong Ming, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou; Rao Zhangfeng, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Feng Bingquan, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; and more than 20 representatives from various democratic parties.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, July 31st: Sartono, Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament, stated in Hong Kong on July 30th that the visit of the Indonesian parliamentary delegation to China is of great significance, as it is a concrete manifestation of the spirit of the Bandung Conference and will help to enhance mutual relations between Asian and African countries.

	Sartono, who was invited to visit China, had these words relayed to the press by his secretary, Sunito, while passing through Hong Kong.

	Sunito, while receiving reporters on behalf of Sartono, said that since the Bandung Conference, the people of Asia and Africa have become more united, and the people of Asia and Africa have made new achievements in their efforts to safeguard national sovereignty and integrity.

### 印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺到广州

1956-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺到广州
    新华社广州31日电　应刘少奇委员长邀请来中国访问的印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺和他的夫人以及国会议员等八人，今天从香港乘专车到达广州。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长辛志超曾到深圳迎接贵宾。
    前往广州车站欢迎的有广州市副市长钟明，政协广东省委员会副主席饶彰风，政协广州市委员会副主席冯秉铨，以及各民主党派代表二十多人。
    新华社香港31日电　印度尼西亚议会议长沙多诺七月三十日在香港表示，印度尼西亚议会代表团这次访问中国的意义是很重大的，因为这是万隆会议精神的具体表现形式，有助于增进亚非国家之间的相互关系。
    应邀到中国访问的沙多诺在路过香港的时候，由他的秘书桑尼托向报界转述了这些话。
    桑尼托在代表沙多诺接见记者时说，万隆会议以后，亚非人民更加团结了，亚非人民在维护国家主权完整的努力上有了新的成绩。
