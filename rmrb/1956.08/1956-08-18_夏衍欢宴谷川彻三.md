Title: Xia Yan Hosts Banquet for Tetsuzo Tanikawa
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 16th to welcome Tetsuzo Tanikawa, the head of the former Japanese Asian Solidarity Committee's cultural delegation to various countries, a famous Japanese philosopher, art critic, and Dean of the Faculty of Arts of Hosei University, Japan, who is visiting China.

### 夏衍欢宴谷川彻三

1956-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    夏衍欢宴谷川彻三
    据新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍十六日晚设宴欢迎来中国访问的原任日本亚洲团结委员会访问各国的文化代表团团长、日本名哲学家、美术评论家、日本法政大学文学院院长谷川彻三。
