Title: Delegation to Attend Sino-Czech Scientific and Technological Cooperation Meeting Departs Beijing for Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 5

    Delegation to Attend Sino-Czech Scientific and Technological Cooperation Meeting
    Our Delegation Departs Beijing for Czechoslovakia
    (Our Newspaper) News: The Chinese delegation of eight members, who will attend the fourth meeting of the Sino-Czech Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation in Prague, departed from Beijing for Czechoslovakia by plane on August 9th.

### 出席中捷科学与技术合作会议  我国代表团离京去捷

1956-08-10
第5版()
专栏：

    出席中捷科学与技术合作会议
    我国代表团离京去捷
    本报讯　出席在布拉格举行的中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会第四届会议的中国代表团一行八人，已于八月九日由北京乘飞机去捷克斯洛伐克。
