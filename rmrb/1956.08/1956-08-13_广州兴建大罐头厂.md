Title: Guangzhou to Build a Large Cannery
Author:
Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 11th. Construction of the state-owned Guangdong Cannery began yesterday in the newly developed industrial zone in the eastern suburbs of Guangzhou.

　　This factory will be the largest fruit and vegetable cannery in the country. It will utilize the abundant fruits and vegetables produced in Guangdong to provide domestic and foreign consumers with a large amount of delicious and inexpensive canned food. After completion, the factory will produce twenty-two kinds of canned products, including pineapple, lychee, papaya, kumquat, water chestnut, peas, green beans, cucumbers, tomatoes, fruit juice, and jam, with an annual output of 20,000 tons.

　　The machinery and equipment for this factory have already been ordered from Shanghai and other places; the production workers it needs are also studying in canneries in Shanghai, Shantou, and other places. It is expected that this factory can be completed and put into production by the end of next year.

### 广州兴建大罐头厂

1956-08-13
第2版()
专栏：

    广州兴建大罐头厂
    新华社广州11日电　国营广东罐头厂昨天在广州市东郊新开辟的工业区动工兴建。
    这个厂是全国最大的一个果蔬罐头厂。它将利用广东出产的丰富的水果、蔬菜，为国内外消费者提供大量的味美价廉的罐头食品。这个厂建成以后，将出产菠萝、荔枝、木瓜、金橘、荸荠、豌豆、四季豆、黄瓜、番茄和果子汁、果子酱等二十二种罐头产品，年产量为二万吨。
    这个厂的机器设备已经在上海等地订制；它所需要的生产工人也已经在上海、汕头等地的罐头厂学习。预计这个厂可以在明年年底建成投入生产。
