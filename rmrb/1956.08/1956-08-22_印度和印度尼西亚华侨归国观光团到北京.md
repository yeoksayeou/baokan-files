Title: Overseas Chinese Visiting Groups from India and Indonesia Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 21st. A group of ninety overseas Chinese returning from India and Indonesia arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 20th.

The head of the Overseas Chinese Visiting Group from India is Liang Zizhi and Jiang Fanghan, and the deputy head is Zhang Jing.

The head of the Overseas Chinese Visiting Group from Indonesia is Ye Cunxu, and the deputy heads are Zhang Yunfei, Zhuang Nanhua, and Su Gongjie.

Representatives from the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission and the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, as well as domestic relatives and friends of many of the overseas Chinese in the visiting group, were at the station to welcome the group.

### 印度和印度尼西亚华侨归国观光团到北京

1956-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印度和印度尼西亚华侨归国观光团到北京
    新华社21日讯　印度、印度尼西亚华侨归国观光团一行九十人，在二十日晚乘火车到达北京。
    印度华侨归国观光团的团长是梁子质和蒋芳汉，副团长是张敬。
    印度尼西亚华侨观光团的团长是叶存湑，副团长张云飞、庄南华和苏公杰。
    到车站迎接观光团的有华侨事务委员会和中华全国归国华侨联合会筹备委员会的代表，以及观光团中许多华侨的国内亲友。
