Title: Production of Spinning Frames for Egypt (Picture)
Author: Yang Putao
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency:

    Production of Spinning Frames for Egypt
    The State-owned Shanghai No. 2 Textile Machinery Factory has started assembling ten spinning frames for Egypt. It is expected that this batch of spinning frames will be ready for delivery by late September. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Putao

### 为埃及生产细纱机（图片）
杨溥涛
1956-08-26
第2版()
专栏：

    为埃及生产细纱机
    国营上海第二纺织机械厂为埃及生产的十台细纱机已经开始装配。预计在九月下旬，这批细纱机就可以出厂。新华社记者  杨溥涛摄
