Title: World Council of Churches Calls for a Halt to Atomic Weapons Testing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 5

    World Council of Churches
    Calls for a Halt to Atomic Weapons Testing
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 20th – TASS reports from Berlin on the 19th: The newsletter of the German Evangelical Lutheran Church published the World Council of Churches' appeal to halt the testing of atomic weapons.

    The World Council of Churches also advocates for the establishment of a system of reasonable arms reduction and the peaceful resolution of outstanding issues that threaten peace.

### 普世基督教会协会  呼吁停止原子武器试验

1956-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

    普世基督教会协会
    呼吁停止原子武器试验
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社柏林十九日讯：德国基督教信义福音会的通报登载了普世基督教会协会要求停止试验原子武器的呼吁书。
    普世基督教会协会还主张建立合理地裁减军备的制度，并且主张和平解决对威胁着和平的悬而未决的问题。
