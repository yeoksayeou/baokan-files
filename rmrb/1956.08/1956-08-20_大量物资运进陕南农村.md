Title: Large Quantities of Supplies Transported to Southern Shaanxi Rural Areas
Author:
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency report: In August, the northern section of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway has transported 690 tons of chemical fertilizer to the rural areas of southern Shaanxi. Large quantities of salt, cloth, and general merchandise are also being continuously transported to the rural areas of southern Shaanxi via the Baoji-Fengxian section of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway. At the same time, rice from the Hanzhong Basin is also being transported out via this section of the railway.

Starting from May this year, this section of the railway has been temporarily handling general freight for the urgent needs of southern Shaanxi and other regions. From May to July, a total of 18,923 tons of goods were transported in and out.

### 大量物资运进陕南农村

1956-08-20
第2版()
专栏：

    大量物资运进陕南农村
    据新华社讯　八月份，宝成铁路北段已经为陕南农村运送了六百九十吨化学肥料。大量的食盐、布匹、百货也正源源地经宝成路的宝鸡到凤县段运进陕南农村。同时，汉中盆地的稻米也正经这一段铁路运出来。
    从今年五月开始，这一段铁路就为陕南等地区的急需临时办理一般货运，从五月到七月三个月；运进、运出货物共达18，923吨。
