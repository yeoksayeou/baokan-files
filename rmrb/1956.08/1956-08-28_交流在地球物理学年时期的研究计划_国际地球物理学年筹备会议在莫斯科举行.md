Title: Exchange Research Plans During the International Geophysical Year; International Geophysical Year Preparatory Meeting Held in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-08-28
Page: 5

    Exchange Research Plans During the International Geophysical Year
    International Geophysical Year Preparatory Meeting Held in Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: According to TASS reports, the International Geophysical Year Preparatory Meeting concluded in Moscow on August 25th.

    Representatives from all participating countries reported on their countries' scientists' research plans during the International Geophysical Year.

    Scientists placed great importance on observing solar activity. The Soviet Union will construct a scientific institution to study the impact of solar flares on the ionosphere and the Earth's magnetic field.

    The meeting discussed in detail the establishment of world scientific centers to collect and preserve the observational materials accumulated during the International Geophysical Year. The Soviet Union will be responsible for building and organizing such a scientific center in Novosibirsk.

    During the International Geophysical Year, observation stations in various countries will collect a large amount of rare and valuable materials. The meeting believed that the complete publication of these materials would be of great significance. Many publications will feature maps of observation stations and observation plans and will introduce the history of previous International Geophysical Years.

    Scientists from Bulgaria, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, China, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, and Yugoslavia participated in this preparatory meeting.

### 交流在地球物理学年时期的研究计划  国际地球物理学年筹备会议在莫斯科举行

1956-08-28
第5版()
专栏：

    交流在地球物理学年时期的研究计划
    国际地球物理学年筹备会议在莫斯科举行
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社报道：国际地球物理学年筹备会议8月25日在莫斯科闭幕。
    所有参加这次会议的各国代表都在会上报告了这些国家的科学家们在地球物理学年时期的研究计划。
    科学家们十分重视对太阳运行进行的观测。苏联将建筑一个科学机构，来研究太阳闪光对电离层和地球磁场发生的影响。
    会议详细地讨论了建立一些世界科学中心，来把在地球物理学年中所积累的观测材料收集和保存起来。苏联将负责在新西伯利亚建筑和组织这样一个科学中心。
    在国际地球物理学年期间，各国观测站将收集到大量少见和十分宝贵的材料。会议认为，这些材料的完全公布将有极大的意义。许多出版物将刊载观测站的地图和观测计划，并且将要介绍有关过去几次国际地球物理学年的历史。
    参加这次筹备会议的，有保加利亚、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、中国、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联、捷克斯洛伐克和南斯拉夫的科学家。
