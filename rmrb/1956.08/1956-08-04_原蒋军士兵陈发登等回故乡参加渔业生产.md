Title: Former KMT Soldiers Chen Fadeng and Others Return to Their Hometowns to Participate in Fishery Production
Author:
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 3rd - Former KMT soldiers Chen Fadeng, Wang Changgen, Gan Lianghe, and Huang Yiquan, who returned from Taiwan by small boat, took a public bus from Fuzhou to their hometown of Lianjiang County on the afternoon of the 2nd. The leadership of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Fuzhou issued them sufficient travel expenses, production subsidies, new clothes, shoes, socks, and daily necessities.

Before leaving Fuzhou, Chen Fadeng and others told reporters that they were very grateful to the People's Liberation Army for their warm hospitality. They were all happy to be reunited with their families and prepared to participate in fishery production after returning home.

### 原蒋军士兵陈发登等回故乡参加渔业生产

1956-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    原蒋军士兵陈发登等回故乡参加渔业生产
    新华社福州3日电　由台湾驾小舟归来的原蒋军士兵陈发登、王昌根、甘良和、黄依铨四人，在二日下午由福州乘公共汽车返连江县故乡。中国人民解放军驻福州部队领导机关发给他们足够的路费和生产补助金，以及新的衣服、鞋袜、日用品等。
    陈发登等在离开福州前对记者说，他们非常感谢解放军对他们的热情款待。他们都为即将和家人团聚而高兴，并且准备回家后参加渔业生产。
