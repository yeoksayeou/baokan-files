Title: Indonesian Government Upholds National Dignity and National Interests: Announces Cessation of Repayment of Debt to the Netherlands as Stipulated in the Round Table Conference Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-08-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 5th: The Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced on August 4th that the Indonesian government has decided: to not recognize and no longer repay the Netherlands any debt stipulated by the Round Table Conference Agreement.

The debts owed by Indonesia to other countries (third parties) will continue to be recognized and guaranteed for repayment.

According to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the outstanding debt stipulated by the Round Table Conference Agreement currently amounts to 4,008,100,000 rupiahs, of which 420,000,000 rupiahs are to be repaid to third parties, and 3,661,000,000 rupiahs (approximately $1 billion) are owed to the Netherlands. Among the debts owed to the Netherlands, 3 billion rupiahs constitute the war expenses incurred by the Netherlands in its past military actions against Indonesia.

The Indonesian government's decision is the first powerful action taken to abolish the Round Table Conference Agreement.

### 印度尼西亚政府维护国家尊严和民族利益  宣布不再偿还圆桌会议协定规定的对荷债务

1956-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府维护国家尊严和民族利益
    宣布不再偿还圆桌会议协定规定的对荷债务
    新华社雅加达5日电　印度尼西亚外交部八月四日公布，印度尼西亚政府已经决定：不承认和不再偿还给荷兰以任何圆桌会议协定所规定的债务。
    印度尼西亚对其他国家（第三方）所负的债务将继续受到承认并且保证予以偿还。
    据外交部公布，圆桌会议协定规定的债务到现在为止没有偿还的有四十亿零八千一百万盾，其中需归还第三方的有四亿二千万盾，对荷兰的债务有三十六亿六千一百万盾（约合十亿美元）。在对荷兰的债务中，有三十亿盾是过去荷兰对印度尼西亚作战的战费。
    印度尼西亚政府的这一决定是在实现废除圆桌会议协定方面所采取的第一个有力的行动。
