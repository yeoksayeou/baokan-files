Title: Construction Begins on Huge High-Voltage Transmission Line from Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station to the Urals
Author:
Date: 1956-08-13
Page: 6

    From the Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station to the Urals
    Construction Begins on Huge High-Voltage Transmission Line
    (Dispatch from our Moscow correspondent) Construction has begun on one of the world's largest high-voltage transmission lines, from the Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station to the Urals. The new transmission line will pass through countless forests and rivers, and cross the Ural Mountains, stretching for 1,148 kilometers. Its voltage will reach 400,000 volts. It will become an important link in the high-voltage grid of the European part of the Soviet Union. Powerful substations are being prepared for construction in Bugulma, Zlatoust, Chelyabinsk, and Sverdlovsk, which will receive the current and send electricity to the oil regions of Tatar and Bashkiria and the industrial enterprises of the Urals. Currently, the first batch of mechanics and concrete workers are already working on the line, with concrete workers laying the concrete foundations for the metal poles, and mechanics installing the metal structure of the poles.
    The section of the transmission line from the Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station – Bugulma – Zlatoust will begin transmitting electricity in the fourth quarter of 1957.

### 从古比雪夫水电站到乌拉尔  巨大的高压输电线开始修筑

1956-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    从古比雪夫水电站到乌拉尔
    巨大的高压输电线开始修筑
    本报莫斯科电　世界上最大的高压输电线之一，从古比雪夫水电站到乌拉尔的输电线已开始建筑。新输电线将经过无数的森林和河流，穿过乌拉尔的山脉，长达一千一百四十八公里。它的电压达四十万伏特。它将成为苏联欧洲部分高压网的一个重要环节。在布古尔马、兹拉托乌斯特、车里雅宾斯克和斯维尔德洛夫斯克准备建立强大的变电站，它将接受电流并向塔塔尔及巴什基里亚的石油区和乌拉尔的工业企业送电。目前第一批机械工人和混凝土工人已经在线路上工作，混凝土工人正在铺设金属电杆下的混凝土地基，机械工人在装置电杆的金属结构。
    从古比雪夫水电站——布古尔马——兹拉托乌斯特这一段输电线，将在1957年第四季度开始输电。
