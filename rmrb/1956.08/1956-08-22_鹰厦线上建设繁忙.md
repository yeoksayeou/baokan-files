Title: Busy Construction on the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway Line
Author:
Date: 1956-08-22
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, August 19th: Yong'an, an important mountain city along the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway line, is a hive of construction activity on the eve of the railway's opening to traffic. Many newly built, expanded, and renovated factories are under intense construction. These include a large-scale timber yard, a thermal power plant, a paper mill with an annual output of 3,000 tons of machine-made paper, and a small-scale combined processing plant. According to the person in charge of the relevant departments, this year's investment in Yong'an's various capital construction projects has exceeded the total of the past six years. The construction area of workers' dormitories and housing for the working people, which began construction in the second half of this year, totals more than 1,760 square meters. The reconstruction and expansion projects of many streets are also about to begin.

### 鹰厦线上建设繁忙

1956-08-22
第2版()
专栏：

    鹰厦线上建设繁忙
    新华社福州19日电　鹰厦线上的重要山城——永安，在通车前夕，呈现一片繁忙建设景象。许多新建、扩建和改建的工厂，正在紧张施工。其中有规模宏大的贮木场，有火力发电厂，还有年产三千吨机制纸的造纸厂和小型联合加工厂等。据有关部门负责人说，今年永安各项基本建设投资，已超过过去六年来的总和。今年下半年动工兴建的职工宿舍和劳动人民住宅的建筑面积，共有一千七百六十多平方公尺。许多街道的改建、扩建工程也即将动工。
