Title: The Local Organizations of Five Democratic Parties, Including the RCCK and CDL, Have Developed in Guangzhou

Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 4th: The local organizations in Guangzhou of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Democratic League, the China National Construction Association，the China Association for Promoting Democracy, and the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party have all developed a large number of new members in the first half of this year.

The Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang admitted 38 people to the organization in the first half of the year, including social figures who had relations with the Kuomintang, such as Su Mingyi and Zhong Qianzhou. The Democratic League has admitted more than 80 members in Guangzhou's universities and colleges and among senior intellectuals in the cultural and artistic circles this year. Among them are Li Peiwen, vice president of South China Agricultural College, Shang Chengzuo, professor of Sun Yat-sen University and expert in oracle bone inscriptions, famous Cantonese opera actors Xue Juexian and Tan Shaojia (Liang Shaojia), Chinese painter Lu Zhenhuan, and chess master Chen Songshun. Among the 873 members admitted by the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the China National Democratic Construction Association this year, there are private representatives, private personnel in actual positions, and family members of industrialists and businessmen.

The Guangzhou organizations of the China Association for Promoting Democracy and the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party have also admitted 50 to 60 members each this year.

### 民革民盟等五个民主党派在广州市的地方组织有了发展

1956-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    民革民盟等五个民主党派
    在广州市的地方组织有了发展
    新华社广州4日电　中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主同盟、中国民主建国会、中国民主促进会、中国农工民主党五个民主党派在广州市的地方组织，今年上半年新发展了大批成员。
    中国国民党革命委员会广州市委员会今年上半年吸收了三十八人参加组织，其中有原与国民党有关系的社会人士苏鸣一、钟前舟等。民主同盟今年在广州市各高等学校及文化艺术界的高级知识分子中吸收了八十多个盟员。其中有华南农学院副院长李沛文，中山大学教授、甲骨文专家商承祚，著名粤剧演员薛觉先、谭少佳（靓少佳），国画家卢振寰，象棋名手陈松顺等。中国民主建国会广州市委员会今年吸收的873名会员中，有私方代理人、实职私方人员以及工商业者家属等。
    中国民主促进会和中国农工民主党的广州市组织今年也各吸收了成员五六十人。
