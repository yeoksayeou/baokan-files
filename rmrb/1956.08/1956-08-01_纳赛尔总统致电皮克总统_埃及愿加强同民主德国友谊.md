Title: President Nasser Cables President Pieck: Egypt Willing to Strengthen Friendship with Democratic Germany
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 6
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 31st. Egyptian President Nasser sent a telegram to Democratic German President Pieck on July 30th, thanking him for his congratulations on Egypt's National Day, saying: "I wholeheartedly wish the German people and its government glory and happiness. I believe that the bonds of friendship existing between our two countries will take an even closer form in the years to come. Egypt fervently hopes to make a full contribution to maintaining peace and happiness for all countries."

### 纳赛尔总统致电皮克总统  埃及愿加强同民主德国友谊

1956-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔总统致电皮克总统
    埃及愿加强同民主德国友谊
    新华社柏林31日电　埃及总统纳赛尔在七月三十日打电报给民主德国总统皮克，感谢他对埃及国庆的祝贺说：“我衷心地祝德国人民和它的政府光荣和幸福。我相信，存在于我们两国之间的友谊联系在今后的年月中将采取更加密切的形式。埃及热烈希望对保持所有国家的和平和幸福作出充分的贡献。”
