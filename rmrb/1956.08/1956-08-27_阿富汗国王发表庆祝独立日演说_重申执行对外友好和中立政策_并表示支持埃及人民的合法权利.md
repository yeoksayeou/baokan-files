Title: Afghan King Delivers Independence Day Speech, Reaffirms Policy of Foreign Friendship and Neutrality, and Expresses Support for the Legitimate Rights of the Egyptian People
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, August 26th: According to TASS, Kabul, August 26th: In his Independence Day speech on August 24th, Afghan King Mohammed Zahir Shah said that the Afghan government pursues a policy of expanding and strengthening friendly relations with other countries, and a policy of neutrality.

    Speaking on the Suez Canal issue, he said that Afghanistan supports the legitimate rights of the Egyptian people.

    The King emphasized that the visits of leaders from certain friendly countries to Afghanistan played a significant role in strengthening and expanding Afghanistan's relations with other countries. The visit of Soviet leaders to Afghanistan produced good results in strengthening existing good-neighborly relations.

    When speaking about the main objectives of the Afghan government's foreign and domestic policies, Mohammed Zahir Shah pointed out that Afghanistan is a country that needs development in all aspects. Therefore, the Afghan government consistently supports plans to provide economic assistance to underdeveloped countries and uses legitimate economic sources to develop its own resources.

    The King said that in order for the country to develop most rapidly, Afghanistan accepted a loan of 100 million US dollars from its friendly neighbor, the Soviet Union.

    Zahir Shah also pointed out that his government pursues a policy of protecting human rights, especially protecting the freedom enjoyed by all nations and their right to solve their own destiny.

### 阿富汗国王发表庆祝独立日演说  重申执行对外友好和中立政策  并表示支持埃及人民的合法权利

1956-08-27
第6版()
专栏：

    阿富汗国王发表庆祝独立日演说
    重申执行对外友好和中立政策
    并表示支持埃及人民的合法权利
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社喀布尔26日讯：阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿在8月24日发表的庆祝独立日演说中说，阿富汗政府执行扩大和加强同其他国家友好关系的政策和中立的政策。
    他在讲到苏彝士运河问题的时候说，阿富汗支持埃及人民的合法权利。
    国王着重指出，某些友好国家的领导人访问阿富汗，在加强和扩大阿富汗同其他国家的关系方面起了重大的作用。苏联领导人的访问阿富汗，产生了加强现有善邻关系的良好结果。
    穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿在讲到阿富汗政府的外交政策和国内政策的主要目的的时候指出，阿富汗是一个各方面都需要发展的国家。因而，阿富汗政府一贯支持对落后国家提供经济援助的计划，并且自己利用正当的经济来源来发展自己的资源。
    国王说，为了使国家能够最迅速地发展，阿富汗接受了友好的邻国苏联一亿美元的贷款。
    查希尔·沙阿还指出，他的政府执行保护人权、特别是保护各民族所享有的自由和自己解决自己命运的权利的政策。
