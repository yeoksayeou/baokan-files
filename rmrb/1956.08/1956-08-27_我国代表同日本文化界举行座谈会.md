Title: Chinese Representatives Hold Talks with Japanese Cultural Figures
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 25th: Xu Guangping, head of the Chinese delegation attending the Second World Conference against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, and delegation member Cao Yu, participated in a literary symposium jointly held by the Chinese Literature Research Office of Tokyo University and the Japanese "Oriental Society" on the afternoon of August 24. At the symposium, Xu Guangping and Cao Yu gave detailed answers to many questions raised by Japanese experts in Chinese literature and drama.

Another member of the Chinese delegation, Wang Yunsheng, visited the Kyodo News Agency of Japan on the morning of the same day. In the afternoon of the same day, he also attended a symposium jointly held by the Japanese "China Research Institute" and the Historical Research Association.

On the evening of August 24, delegation member Kang Yonghe was invited to attend a joint welcome dinner party held by the Japan National Railway Workers' Union and the Japan Synthetic Chemical Industry Workers' Union Federation.

### 我国代表同日本文化界举行座谈会

1956-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    我国代表同日本文化界举行座谈会
    新华社25日讯　东京消息：出席第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团团长许广平和团员曹禺在8月24日下午参加了东京大学中国文学研究室和日本“东方学会”联合举行的文艺座谈会。许广平和曹禺在座谈会上对日本研究中国文学、戏剧的专家们所提出的许多问题一一作了详细的答复。
    中国代表团的另一个团员王芸生在同一天上午应邀访问了日本的共同通讯社。当天下午，他还出席了日本“中国研究所”和历史研究会联合举行的座谈会。
    8月24日晚上，代表团团员康永和应邀出席了日本国营铁道工会和日本合成化学产业工会联合会联合举行的欢迎晚餐会。
