Title: Vice President Hatta Receives Dapusin
Author:
Date: 1956-08-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 28th. Indonesian Vice President Hatta received Dapusin, head of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association delegation, and all members of the delegation in his office at 11:00 am today. Professor Priyono and other leaders of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association were present at the reception.

The Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament, Sartono, also received Dapusin and all members of the delegation this morning.

During these friendly meetings, the hosts and guests engaged in very sincere conversations.

### 哈达副总统接见达浦生

1956-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

    哈达副总统接见达浦生
    据新华社雅加达28日电　印度尼西亚副总统哈达今天上午十一时在他的办公室接见了中国—印度尼西亚友好协会代表团团长达浦生和全体团员。接见时在座的有普里约诺教授和印度尼西亚—中国友好协会的其他领导人。
    印度尼西亚议会议长沙多诺也在今天早上接见了达浦生和代表团全体团员。
    在这几次友好会见中，宾主们进行了非常诚挚的交谈。
