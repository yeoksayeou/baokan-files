Sports News

    In the first sports meet of the Chinese Public Security Sports Association, eleven male and female athletes broke three national records in cycling events during the competitions on the 16th and 17th. The left picture shows the seven athletes who broke the national record in the women's 3,000-meter cycling race, from left to right: Zhang Shulan, Zhang Shengzhu, Chen Shuzhen, Hu Meiguang, Shi Xiuying, Zhou Lanjun, and Liang Xiuqi. The right picture shows the athletes who broke the national record in the men's 5,000-meter cycling race, from left to right: Wang Guicai, Quan Shougen, Li Fugui, and Li Yudi. Li Yudi also broke the national record in the men's 10,000-meter cycling race.
          Guo Fengjiu, Wang Xuelin (Photos)

专栏：体育新闻

    中国公安体育协会第一届体育运动大会在十六、十七两天的比赛中，有十一名男女运动员打破了三项自行车比赛的全国纪录。左图是打破女子三千公尺自行车比赛全国纪录的七名运动员，左起张淑兰、张胜竹、陈淑贞、胡梅光、史秀英、周兰军、梁秀琪。右图是打破男子五千公尺自行车比赛全国纪录的运动员，自左起王贵才、权寿根、李福贵、李玉弟。李玉弟又打破了男子一万公尺自行车比赛的全国纪录。
          郭凤久　王学琳摄
