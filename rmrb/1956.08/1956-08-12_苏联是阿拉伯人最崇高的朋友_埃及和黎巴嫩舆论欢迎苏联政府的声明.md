Title: The Soviet Union is the Arab People's Most Noble Friend: Egyptian and Lebanese Public Opinion Welcome the Soviet Government's Statement
Author:
Date: 1956-08-12
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, August 11th: According to TASS, Cairo, August 10th: The Soviet government's statement on the Suez Canal has been received with great satisfaction in Egypt and is considered an extremely important and meaningful document.

　　Cairo newspapers published the Soviet government's statement on the front page under large banner headlines. The newspapers emphasized that the Soviet government believes that the Egyptian government's decision to nationalize the Suez Canal Company was a legitimate action taken in accordance with Egypt's sovereignty.

　　The newspapers fully quoted this part of the statement: The Soviet government opposes the deliberate selection of participating countries for the London conference and suggests inviting Arab countries, the People's Republic of China, and other relevant countries to participate in this conference.

　　"The Republic" newspaper's headline was: "No decisions infringing on Egyptian sovereignty are allowed. Arab countries from Iraq to Morocco should be invited to participate in the conference."

　　"Al-Ahram" newspaper's headline read: "Russia criticizes the London conference and condemns the actions of Britain and France to use force to put pressure on Egypt. Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal is a legitimate action."

　　"Egyptian Daily" newspaper's headline was: "The Soviet Union fully supports Egypt."

　　"Egyptian Progress" pointed out that the Soviet Union "does not allow the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company to be questioned."

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 11th: According to TASS, Beirut, August 10th: "Al-Telegraph" published an editorial commenting on the Soviet government's statement on the Suez Canal issue, saying: "The Soviet response regarding the upcoming London conference is the most powerful action, which not only defends Egypt but also defends all other Arab countries that are defending their independence and fighting against imperialism. We wholeheartedly welcome the Soviet statement."

　　The newspaper continued: We are "deeply convinced that the Soviet Union is the Arab people's most noble friend, and the most reliable country to defend their rights to freedom, sovereignty, and national dignity."

　　The newspaper also welcomed the Soviet government's suggestion to invite more than twenty-two countries to participate in the Suez Canal issue conference.

### 苏联是阿拉伯人最崇高的朋友  埃及和黎巴嫩舆论欢迎苏联政府的声明

1956-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联是阿拉伯人最崇高的朋友
    埃及和黎巴嫩舆论欢迎苏联政府的声明
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社开罗十日讯：苏联政府关于苏彝士运河的声明在埃及受到十分满意的欢迎，这个声明被认为是一个极其重要和有意义的文件。
    开罗各报在第一版用通栏大标题刊登了苏联政府的声明。报纸着重指出，苏联政府认为埃及政府关于苏彝士运河公司国有化的决定是根据埃及的主权作出的合法行动。
    各报完全援引了声明中的这一部分：苏联政府反对别有用心地选择伦敦会议的参加国，并且建议邀请阿拉伯国家、中华人民共和国和其他有关国家参加这个会议。
    “共和国报”的标题是：“不许有任何侵犯埃及主权的决定。应当邀请从伊拉克到摩洛哥这一地区的阿拉伯国家参加这个会议。”
    “金字塔报”的标题写道：“俄国抨击了伦敦会议，并且谴责英国和法国采取武力对埃及施加压力的行动。埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有是合法的行动。”
    “埃及日报”的标题是：“苏联完全支持埃及”。
    “埃及进步报”指出，苏联“不容许苏彝士运河公司国有化的问题遭到怀疑”。
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社贝鲁特十日讯：“电讯报”发表社论评论苏联政府关于苏彝士运河问题的声明说：“苏联关于即将举行的伦敦会议的答复是一个最有力的行动，它不仅保卫埃及，而且保卫正在捍卫自己的独立和同帝国主义进行斗争的其他所有阿拉伯国家。我们衷心欢迎苏联声明。”
    这家报纸接着写道：我们“深信苏联是阿拉伯人最崇高的朋友，是保卫他们对自由、主权和民族尊严的权利的最可靠的国家”。
    这家报纸还欢迎苏联政府声明中关于多邀请二十二个国家参加苏彝士运河问题会议的建议。
