Title: New Joint Ventures to Implement Wage Reform
Author:
Date: 1956-08-09
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    New Joint Ventures to Implement Wage Reform

    Xinhua News Agency News: The National Wage Conference for Joint State-Private Enterprises, convened by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on behalf of the State Council in conjunction with relevant departments, began on August 6th.

    This conference will study the guidelines and policies for wage reform in newly established joint state-private enterprises and enterprises directly transformed from private to state-owned, review reform plans from various regions, and determine indicators for wage increases, among other issues.

    Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a report at today's meeting. In his report, he discussed the guidelines and policies for wage reform in new joint ventures, the wage treatment of private sector personnel in new joint ventures, the unified and reasonable wage system in new joint ventures, and indicators for wage increases.

    More than 180 representatives from various provinces, cities, relevant central departments, and relevant industrial trade unions are participating in this conference.

### 新合营企业将进行工资改革

1956-08-09
第2版()
专栏：

    新合营企业将进行工资改革
    新华社讯　中华全国总工会受国务院委托会同有关部门召开的全国公私合营企业工资会议于八月六日开始举行。
    这次会议将要研究新公私合营企业和由私营直接转为国营的企业的工资改革的方针政策，审查各地的改革方案，确定工资增长的指标等问题。
    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚在今天的会议上作了报告。他在报告中谈了新合营企业工资改革的方针政策、新合营企业中私方人员的工资待遇、新合营企业工资制度的统一合理以及工资增长的指标等问题。
    参加这次会议的有各省、市和中央有关部门及有关产业工会的代表一百八十多人。
