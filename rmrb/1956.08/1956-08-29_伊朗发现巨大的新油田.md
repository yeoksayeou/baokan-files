Title: Iran Discovers Huge New Oil Field
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th: According to an Associated Press dispatch from Tehran: A rich new oil field has been discovered near Qom, approximately 120 kilometers south of the Iranian capital, Tehran. On August 27th, oil gushed from a large oil well to a height of 150 feet, flooding the nearby fields.

It is estimated that the oil well is gushing approximately 80,000 barrels of oil per day (one barrel equals 158.98 liters).

The new oil field will belong to the National Iranian Oil Company. Iranian experts say that this oil field may be as large as Iran's largest oil field, Agha Jari, in the south.

### 伊朗发现巨大的新油田

1956-08-29
第6版()
专栏：

    伊朗发现巨大的新油田
    据新华社28日讯　据美联社德黑兰消息：伊朗首都德黑兰以南约一百二十公里的库姆的附近发现了一个富饶的新油田。8月27日，石油从一座巨大的油井喷到空中达一百五十英尺高，并且把附近的田野都淹没了。
    据估计这个油井每天喷出约八万桶（一桶等于一五八点九八公升）石油。
    这个新油田将属于伊朗国家石油公司。伊朗专家说，这个油田可能像伊朗南部的最大油田阿加—雅里一样大。
