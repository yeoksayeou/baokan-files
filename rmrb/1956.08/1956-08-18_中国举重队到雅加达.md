Title: Chinese Weightlifting Team Arrives in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1956-08-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 17th. The Chinese weightlifting team arrived in Jakarta by plane on the 16th. Mr. Arifin, Chairman of the Indonesian Amateur Weightlifting Association, was at the airport to welcome them.

　　The Chinese weightlifting team is invited to Indonesia to compete with the weightlifting teams of Indonesia, Burma, Malaya and Singapore.

### 中国举重队到雅加达

1956-08-18
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中国举重队到雅加达
    新华社雅加达17日电　中国举重队已经在十六日坐飞机到达雅加达。到机场欢迎的有印度尼西亚业余举重协会主席阿里芬。
    中国举重队是应邀到印度尼西亚同印度尼西亚、缅甸、马来亚和新加坡的举重队进行比赛的。
