Title: Liao Luyan Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Albanian Agricultural Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan hosted a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the Albanian agricultural delegation, which is about to return home. The newly appointed Albanian Ambassador to China, Comrade Zenel Bali and his wife, also attended the banquet.

    At the banquet, Liao Luyan said that the Albanian agricultural delegation's inspection and visit activities in China, the introduction of Albania's agricultural work experience to China's agricultural departments, and the valuable suggestions made by the delegation will be of great help in improving China's agricultural work.

    The head of the delegation, Comrade Ka. Tashko, and the deputy head of the delegation, Comrade Ve. Pipa, also spoke. They said that during this visit, the delegation not only saw the achievements of the Chinese people in construction but also witnessed the profound friendship of the Chinese people towards the Albanian people. All this left them with a deep impression.

    During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to toast the ever-growing friendship between China and Albania and the common prosperity of the two peoples.

    Among those present at the banquet tonight were Vice Minister of Agriculture Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Land Reclamation Zhang Linchi, Deputy Director of the Rural Work Department of the CPC Central Committee Chen Zhengran, and representatives from other departments, totaling forty people.

### 廖鲁言设宴欢送阿尔巴尼亚农业代表团

1956-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    廖鲁言设宴欢送阿尔巴尼亚农业代表团
    新华社23日讯　农业部长廖鲁言今晚举行宴会，欢送即将返国的阿尔巴尼亚农业代表团。阿尔巴尼亚新任驻华大使征略·巴利里和夫人也出席了宴会。
    廖鲁言在宴会上说，阿尔巴尼亚农业代表团在中国的考察访问活动，和向中国农业部门介绍的阿尔巴尼亚的农业工作经验以及代表团提出的宝贵意见，对改进和提高中国农业工作会有很大帮助。
    代表团团长卡·塔什考、副团长维·皮帕也都讲了话。他们说，代表团在这次访问中，不但看到中国人民在建设事业中的成就，而且还看到了中国人民对阿尔巴尼亚人民无比深厚的友谊。这一切给他们留下了深刻的印象。
    席间，宾主们一再起立为中阿两国之间日益发展的友谊、为中阿两国人民的共同繁荣干杯。
    出席今晚宴会作陪的，有农业部副部长刘瑞龙、农垦部副部长张林池和中共中央农村工作部副部长陈正人以及其他部门的代表四十人。
