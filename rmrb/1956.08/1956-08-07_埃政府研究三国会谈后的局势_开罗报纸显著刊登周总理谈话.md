Title: Egyptian Government Studies the Situation After the Tripartite Talks; Cairo Newspapers Prominently Publish Premier Zhou Enlai's Remarks
Author:
Date: 1956-08-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Cairo News: Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser has held a series of meetings with his senior aides to study the situation following the London talks of the United Kingdom, France, and the United States.

    At the same time, Nasser continued consultations on August 5th with the Soviet, Indian, and Yugoslav ambassadors to Egypt. The British Ambassador to Egypt met with the Indian Ambassador after the Indian Ambassador's consultation with Nasser.

    Nasser also received Yemeni Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister, and Foreign Minister Badr on the same day. Badr said upon his arrival in Cairo that Yemen supports Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal.

    Cairo newspapers on August 5th published in large headlines the remarks made by Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai to reporters supporting Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal.

### 埃政府研究三国会谈后的局势  开罗报纸显著刊登周总理谈话

1956-08-07
第5版()
专栏：

    埃政府研究三国会谈后的局势
    开罗报纸显著刊登周总理谈话
    新华社6日讯　开罗消息：埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔同他的高级助手举行了一系列的会议，研究英法美三国伦敦会谈以后的局势。
    同时，纳赛尔在八月五日继续同苏联、印度和南斯拉夫驻埃及的大使进行了商谈。英国驻埃及大使在印度大使同纳赛尔商谈以后会见了印度大使。
    纳赛尔当天还接见了也门皇太子、副首相兼外交大臣巴德尔。巴德尔在到达开罗的时候说，也门支持埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有。
    开罗报纸八月五日用大字标题刊登中国总理周恩来对记者发表的支持埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的谈话。
