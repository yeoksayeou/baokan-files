Title: Menon Arrives in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1956-08-30
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 29th: Indian Minister Without Portfolio Menon arrived in Cairo by plane this morning.
    When a reporter asked Menon whether he would urge Nasser to accept India's proposal, Menon said: "I am not here to urge him to accept any specific proposal. He will make the decision himself."

### 梅农到开罗

1956-08-30
第6版()
专栏：

    梅农到开罗
    据新华社29日讯　开罗消息：印度不管部部长梅农今天上午乘飞机到达开罗。
    记者问梅农，他是否将敦劝纳赛尔接受印度的建议，梅农说：“我并不是到这里来敦劝他接受任何具体建议的。他将自行作出决定。”
