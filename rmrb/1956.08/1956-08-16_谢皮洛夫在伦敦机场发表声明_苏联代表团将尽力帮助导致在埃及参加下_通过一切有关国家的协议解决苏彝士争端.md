Title: Shepilov Makes Statement at London Airport: Soviet Delegation Will Do Its Utmost to Help Resolve the Suez Dispute Through an Agreement of All Relevant Countries with the Participation of Egypt

Author:

Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 15th dispatch from London, via TASS: The Soviet delegation to the Suez Canal Conference, led by Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov, arrived in London on the afternoon of August 14th.

At the airport, Shepilov made a statement to reporters, saying that the Soviet delegation came to London to exchange views on the freedom of navigation of the Suez Canal at a conference attended by a number of countries.

In the current era, international disputes can only be resolved through peaceful negotiations between the countries concerned, guided by the lofty principles of justice and the spirit of the times; this principle and spirit require strict respect for the legitimate rights and interests of all nations, guarantee the complete equality of all countries - no matter how large or small - create an atmosphere of trust between countries, and consolidate world peace and security.

Shepilov said that the Soviet delegation will do its utmost to help find a solution that will lead to the resolution of this dispute through an agreement of all relevant countries, with the necessary participation of the state of Egypt, on the basis of correctly combining the national interests of independent and autonomous Egypt with the legitimate interests of other countries using the Suez Canal.

Xinhua News Agency, August 15th dispatch from London, via TASS: Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov today visited British Foreign Secretary Lloyd.

Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom Malik accompanied Shepilov.

### 谢皮洛夫在伦敦机场发表声明  苏联代表团将尽力帮助导致在埃及参加下  通过一切有关国家的协议解决苏彝士争端

1956-08-16
第6版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫在伦敦机场发表声明
    苏联代表团将尽力帮助导致在埃及参加下
    通过一切有关国家的协议解决苏彝士争端
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社伦敦讯：由苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫率领的出席苏彝士运河会议的苏联代表团已经在八月十四日下午到达伦敦。
    谢皮洛夫在机场向记者们发表声明说，苏联代表团来伦敦为的是在有若干国家参加的一次会议上对苏彝士运河的航行自由问题交换意见。
    在目前的时代，国际争端只能通过有关国家之间的和受崇高正义原则和时代精神指导的和平谈判来解决；这种原则和精神要求严格尊重各民族的正当权利和利益，保证各国——不论其大小如何——的完全平等，在各国之间建立信任的气氛，巩固世界和平和安全。
    谢皮洛夫说，苏联代表团将尽其所能来帮助找出办法，这种办法将导致在埃及国家的当然参加下，在正确地结合独立自主的埃及的民族利益和使用苏彝士运河的其他国家的正当利益的基础上，通过一切有关国家的协议来解决这个争端。
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社伦敦十五日讯：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫今天访问了英国外交大臣劳埃德。
    苏联驻英国大使马立克陪同谢皮洛夫前往。
