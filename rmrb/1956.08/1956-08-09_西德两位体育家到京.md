Title: Two West German Sports Figures Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th. Dr. Otto Peltzer, once a world record holder in middle-distance running, and football coach Alois Münstermann, arrived in Beijing by plane today. These two famous sports figures from the Federal Republic of Germany are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Sports Federation.

Dr. Otto Peltzer, who is fifty-six years old this year, set six world records between 1926 and 1930 in the 440-yard, 500-meter, 800-meter, 880-yard, 1,000-meter, and 1,500-meter races. He visited China in 1929.

### 西德两位体育家到京

1956-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    西德两位体育家到京
    新华社8日讯　一度是中距离赛跑世界纪录创造者的奥托·佩耳策尔博士和足球教练阿洛伊斯·明斯特尔曼，今天坐飞机来到北京。这两位德意志联邦共和国的著名体育家是应中华全国体育总会邀请来中国访问的。
    奥托·佩耳策尔博士今年五十六岁，他在1926年到1930年间，曾经创造过四百四十码、五百公尺、八百公尺、八百八十码、一千公尺和一千五百公尺六项世界纪录。他在1929年曾经到中国访问过。
