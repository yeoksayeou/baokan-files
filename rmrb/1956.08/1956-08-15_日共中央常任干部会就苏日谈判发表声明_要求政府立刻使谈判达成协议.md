Title: CPC Central Standing Committee Issues Statement on Soviet-Japanese Negotiations, Demands Government Immediately Reach Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, August 14th, Tokyo dispatch: The Central Standing Committee of the Japanese Communist Party yesterday issued a statement on the Soviet-Japanese negotiations, demanding that the Japanese government, for the sake of Japan's political and economic interests, immediately reach an agreement to end the state of war.

The Japanese Communist Party, in its statement, demanded that the Hatoyama government abandon its unreasonable territorial claims. The statement pointed out that the San Francisco Peace Treaty had already stipulated that Japan relinquish the Kuril Islands and Sakhalin Island. The Japanese government's territorial claims are illegitimate demands that cannot be accepted internationally.

Soviet-Japanese Negotiation Plenary Session Suspended

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th dispatch: Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov met with Japanese Foreign Minister Mamoru Shigemitsu on the 13th. They discussed procedural issues regarding future negotiations on the normalization of Soviet-Japanese relations. The Minister and the Foreign Minister agreed that a plenary session of the Soviet-Japanese negotiation delegations would not be convened. The subcommittees formed earlier to draft individual treaty clauses will continue their work.

### 日共中央常任干部会就苏日谈判发表声明  要求政府立刻使谈判达成协议

1956-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

    日共中央常任干部会就苏日谈判发表声明
    要求政府立刻使谈判达成协议
    新华社14日讯　东京消息：日本共产党中央常任干部会昨天就苏日谈判发表声明，要求日本政府为了日本的政治和经济利益，立刻使谈判达成协议，以便结束战争状态。
    日本共产党在声明中要求鸠山政府放弃它的无理的领土要求。声明指出，旧金山和约已经规定日本放弃千岛群岛和库页岛。日本政府的领土要求是在国际上不能被接受的不正当的要求。苏日谈判全体会议停开
    据新华社14日讯　苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫在十三日接见了日本外务相重光葵。他们就今后苏日关系正常化谈判进行的程序问题进行了讨论。部长和外务相同意，将不召开苏日谈判代表团的全体会议。以前组成的、拟订和约各别条款的小组委员会将继续工作。
