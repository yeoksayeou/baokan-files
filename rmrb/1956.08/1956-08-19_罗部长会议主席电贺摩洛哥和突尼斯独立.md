Title: Romanian Council of Ministers Chairman Sends Congratulations to Morocco and Tunisia on Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: According to a report by the Romanian News Agency on the 17th, Romanian Council of Ministers Chairman Stoica sent separate telegrams to the Prime Ministers of Morocco and Tunisia, congratulating them on their countries' independence, and expressing readiness to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic envoys with them.

### 罗部长会议主席电贺摩洛哥和突尼斯独立

1956-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

    罗部长会议主席电贺摩洛哥和突尼斯独立
    新华社18日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社十七日报道：罗马尼亚部长会议主席斯托伊卡分别致电摩洛哥总理和突尼斯总理，祝贺这两个国家的独立，并且表示准备同它们建立外交关系和互派外交使节。
