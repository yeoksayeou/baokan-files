Title: Kuwaiti Masses Hold Demonstrations in Support of Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th: Damascus dispatch: The Syrian Socialist Party's "Al-Raya" reported on August 19th that masses in Kuwait, an emirate on the eastern coast of the Persian Gulf, held demonstrations in support of Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal. Demonstrators clashed with local security forces. The situation in Kuwait has become tense.

"Al-Raya" said that seven people were killed and two hundred and fifty-seven were injured in the clashes.

The newspaper also said that the tense situation has been further aggravated by the presence of three British destroyers stationed in the Persian Gulf, attempting to land troops in Kuwait.

### 科威特群众举行示威支持埃及

1956-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

    科威特群众举行示威支持埃及
    新华社20日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚社会党的“旗帜报”在八月十九日报道，波斯湾东岸的酋长国科威特的群众举行示威，支持埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有。示威群众和地方保安部队发生了冲突。科威特的局势已变得紧张了。
    “旗帜报”说，在冲突中有七人死亡，二百五十七人受伤。
    这家报纸还说，由于有三艘英国驱逐舰停泊在波斯湾中，并且企图派兵在科威特登陆，紧张局势已经更为加剧。
