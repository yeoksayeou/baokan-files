Title: Xi'an Youth Rally Supports Egypt's Just Struggle
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, August 18th: More than 500 youths from all ethnic groups and walks of life in Xi'an City gathered on the evening of the 17th to support the Egyptian people's just action of nationalizing the Suez Canal Company.

    Liu Jian, Secretary of the Xi'an Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and Chairman of the Xi'an Municipal Youth Federation, Ma Junying, a young Muslim, and Liu Weiliang, an activist among young industrialists and businessmen, spoke successively. The meeting concluded with the adoption of a telegram to Egyptian youth friends. The telegram stated: "Your struggle is just, and your heroic and decisive actions have been praised by the people of the world. We, the youth of the entire city, firmly support your just struggle."

### 西安市青年集会  支持埃及的正义斗争

1956-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    西安市青年集会
    支持埃及的正义斗争
    据新华社西安18日电　西安市各族各界青年五百多人在十七日晚上集会支持埃及人民把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的正义行动。
    中国新民主主义青年团西安市委员会书记、西安市民主青年联合会主席刘健、伊斯兰教青年满拉马俊英、工商界青年积极分子刘维良先后讲话。会议最后通过了给埃及青年朋友的电报。电报中说：“你们的斗争是正义的，你们英勇果敢的行动已经得到全世界人民的赞扬。我们全市青年坚决支持你们这一正义斗争。”
