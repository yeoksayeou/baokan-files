Title: Typhoon Warning Lifted
Author:
Date: 1956-08-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th — The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon warning cancellation at 12:00 on August 4th: The typhoon that landed in China has now moved to the vicinity of Zhengzhou, Henan. The wind force on land has weakened to about level 4. The typhoon wind force has eliminated its hazard to the interior of China, and the typhoon warning is lifted.

### 台风警报解除

1956-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    台风警报解除
    新华社4日讯　中央气象科学研究所八月四日十二时发布台风解除警报：登陆我国的台风现在移至河南郑州附近，陆上风力已经减弱至四级左右，台风风力对我国内陆的危害性已经消除，台风警报解除。
