Title: U.S. State Department Spokesperson Reiterates Support for "International Control" Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, August 14th: The U.S. State Department issued a statement on August 13th, reiterating U.S. support for the plan agreed upon by the United Kingdom, France, and the United States in London to establish an international control organization for the Suez Canal.

State Department spokesperson Mr. Repp, in a press conference where he read out a formal statement, denied rumors of a division between the United Kingdom and the United States regarding the issue of “international control” of the Suez Canal.

### 美国务院发言人  重申支持“国际管制”计划

1956-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

    美国务院发言人
    重申支持“国际管制”计划
    据新华社14日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务院八月十三日发表声明，重申美国支持英法美三国在伦敦商定的关于设立对苏彝士运河的国际管制机构的计划。
    国务院发言人里普在记者招待会上宣读的一项正式声明否认了英美两国在“国际管制”苏彝士运河的问题上有分歧的传说。
