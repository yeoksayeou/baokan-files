Title: Syrian Prime Minister Criticizes London Conference, Saudi Arabia Warns Western Countries Not to Use Force Against Egypt, Jordanian Foreign Minister Explains Jordan's Support for Egypt to the U.S. Embassy
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd dispatch from Damascus: Syrian Prime Minister al-Asali said on August 22nd that the London Suez Canal issue conference "cannot take decisions that infringe upon Egyptian sovereignty." He said that this conference "is now in its death throes."

Al-Asali told reporters: "The imperialist countries should understand that their threats to use force against Egypt have not weakened Arab morale, but have strengthened it."

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd dispatch from Paris: The Egyptian Embassy in France said in a press release on August 22nd that Saudi Arabian King Saud has decided to express to the U.S. government his support for Egypt's position on the Suez Canal issue.

The press release said that Saudi Arabian officials in Cairo said that King Saud believes that if the West attacks Egypt, he cannot guarantee the safety of American oil facilities in Saudi Arabia.

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd dispatch from Amman: Jordanian Foreign Minister Abdul Hadi formally explained to the U.S. Embassy on August 22nd Jordan's attitude towards any actions taken by Western countries against Egypt regarding the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company.

According to Reuters, citing sources from the Jordanian Foreign Ministry, Jordan supports Egypt's decision to nationalize the Suez Canal Company.

### 叙利亚总理抨击伦敦会议  沙特阿拉伯警告西方国家不要对埃及使用武力  约旦外交大臣向美国大使馆说明约旦支持埃及

1956-08-24
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚总理抨击伦敦会议
    沙特阿拉伯警告西方国家不要对埃及使用武力
    约旦外交大臣向美国大使馆说明约旦支持埃及
    新华社23日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总理阿萨利八月二十二日说，伦敦苏彝士运河问题会议“不能采取侵犯埃及主权的决定”。他说，这个会议“现在正处于垂死状态”。
    阿萨利对新闻记者说：“帝国主义国家应当了解，它们威胁使用武力来对付埃及，并没有削弱阿拉伯的士气，反而增强了它”。
    新华社23日讯　巴黎消息：埃及驻法国大使馆八月二十二日在一份新闻公报中说，沙特阿拉伯国王沙特已经决定要向美国政府表示它在苏彝士运河问题上支持埃及的立场。
    公报说，在开罗的沙特阿拉伯官方人士说，沙特国王认为，如果西方进攻埃及，他将不能保证美国在沙特阿拉伯的石油设施的安全。
    新华社23日讯　安曼消息：约旦外交大臣阿卜杜勒·哈迪八月二十二日正式向美国大使馆说明了约旦对于西方国家在苏彝士运河公司国有化问题上对付埃及的任何行动所持的态度。
    据路透社自约旦外交部人士处获悉，约旦支持埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的决定。
