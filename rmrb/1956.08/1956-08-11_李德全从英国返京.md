Title: Li Dequan Returns to Beijing from Britain
Author:
Date: 1956-08-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Li Dequan, Minister of Health and Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, returned to Beijing by plane on the 7th, together with her entourage, after attending the 13th Congress of the Women's International League for Peace and Freedom in Britain.

### 李德全从英国返京

1956-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    李德全从英国返京
    新华社9日讯　卫生部长、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全在英国参加了国际妇女争取和平与自由联盟第十三次代表大会后，已在七日偕同随行人员乘飞机回到北京。
