Title: Bucharest Rally Celebrates Romanian National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-08-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 23rd—TASS reports from Bucharest: People from all walks of life in Bucharest held a rally on August 22nd to celebrate the 12th anniversary of Romanian National Day.

    In his report at the meeting, Bunaqu, Secretary of the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly, said that August 23rd opened up a path for the Romanian people to a new life and a truly democratic system. The Romanian working class is realizing the tasks set by the Party Congress with soaring labor enthusiasm.

    Bunaqu announced that in the first half of this year, the total output value plan of Romania's socialist industry was completed by 104%. In the first seven months of this year, another 200,000 farming households embarked on the road of socialist cooperation. As of August 1st, there were already 9,672 collective farms and joint farming cooperatives in the country.

    Bunaqu emphasized that Romania is an active factor in the struggle for peace and cooperation among peoples of all countries. The Romanian people regard the cause of consolidating the great socialist family as a sacred cause. Romania is determined to make its own contribution to the struggle for easing international tensions and consolidating peace and socialism.

    Among those who participated in this rally were Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, Chivu Stoica, Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers, and other leaders of the Romanian Workers' Party and the government.

### 布加勒斯特集会庆祝罗马尼亚国庆

1956-08-24
第5版()
专栏：

    布加勒斯特集会庆祝罗马尼亚国庆
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：布加勒斯特各界人士八月二十二日举行了一个集会，庆祝罗马尼亚十二周年国庆。
    罗马尼亚国民议会主席团秘书布纳丘在会上作的报告中说，八月二十三日这一天为罗马尼亚人民开辟了走向新生活和真正民主制度的道路。罗马尼亚工人阶级正以高涨的劳动热情实现着党代表大会所制定的任务。
    布纳丘宣布，今年上半年，罗马尼亚社会主义工业的总产值计划完成了104％。在今年的头七个月中，又有二十万农户走上了社会主义合作化的道路。到八月一日为止，全国已经有九千六百七十二个集体农庄和联合共耕社。
    布纳丘强调指出，罗马尼亚是争取和平和各国人民合作的活动中的一个积极因素。罗马尼亚人民把巩固社会主义国家大家庭的事业看作神圣的事业。罗马尼亚决心对争取缓和国际紧张局势、巩固和平和社会主义的斗争作出自己的贡献。
    参加这次集会的人中，有罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治乌—德治、罗马尼亚部长会议主席斯托伊卡，以及罗马尼亚工人党和政府的其他领导人。
