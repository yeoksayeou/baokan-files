Title: Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region to Establish a Complete Middle School
Author:
Date: 1956-08-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 30th - The establishment of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region will be expanded. On the afternoon of the 27th, the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region approved the plan to expand the establishment of the Preparatory Committee. The Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee also approved the third-quarter work plan of the Animal Husbandry Department of the Preparatory Committee, preliminary opinions on establishing Lhasa Middle School, and draft organizational rules for the Tibet Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Tibet Branch of the Chinese Buddhist Association.

According to the preliminary opinions on establishing Lhasa Middle School, Lhasa Middle School will be a complete middle school with complete facilities and 18 classes.

### 西藏自治区筹委会  将创办一个完全中学

1956-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    西藏自治区筹委会
    将创办一个完全中学
    新华社拉萨30日电　西藏自治区筹备委员会的编制将扩大。二十七日下午，西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员会通过了筹委会扩大编制的方案。筹委会常务委员会同时还通过了筹委会畜牧处第三季度工作计划、关于创办拉萨中学的初步意见、中苏友好协会西藏分会和中国佛教协会西藏分会的组织简则草案等。
    根据创办拉萨中学的初步意见，拉萨中学将是一个设备齐全、具有十八班级的完全中学。
