Title: Israeli Prime Minister Says: Does Not Oppose Suez Canal Nationalization
Author:
Date: 1956-08-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th dispatch: Agence France-Presse reports from Jerusalem: Well-informed sources say that Israeli Prime Minister David Ben-Gurion said that Israel does not oppose Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal, but is only concerned about the freedom of navigation of Israeli ships through this waterway.
    It is reported that Ben-Gurion made these remarks at a meeting of the Israeli Knesset's Foreign Affairs and Defense Committee held in Jerusalem on August 7.

### 以色列总理说：不反对苏彝士运河国有化

1956-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列总理说：
    不反对苏彝士运河国有化
    据新华社8日讯　法新社自耶路撒冷报道：消息灵通人士说，以色列总理本—古里安说，以色列并不反对埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的做法，而只是关切以色列船只通过这条水道的自由航行。
    据报道，本—古里安是在八月七日在耶路撒冷举行的以色列议会外交和国防委员会的会议上说这番话的。
