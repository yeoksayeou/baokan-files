Title: Xiling Gorge Entrance – One of the Three Gorges Poems
Author: Fang Ji
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 8

    Xiling Gorge Entrance
    —One of the Three Gorges Poems
    Fang Ji
    One

    Steep mountain peaks
    Suddenly obscure the dawn's cyan light,
    The river water darkens,
    The ship's bow churns up black waves.
    Yellow Ox—that giant rock standing in the river's heart,
    Like a massive monster
    Rushes head-on…
    The ship deftly dodges to one side.
    The ship seems to have entered an abyss,
    Gorge winds howl, the river boils,
    Morning stars have no time to hide,
    They are hung on those black cliffs.
    The sky covers overhead,
    Like a dim pond;
    Only on the peaks that reach the sky,
    Does the dawn's cyan light reappear.

    Two

    The captain walks into the wheelhouse,
    Looks at the sky and says: It’s going to be light soon.
    Indeed, turning past the facing mountain peak,
    The dawn shines into the wheelhouse.
    The river surges, stirring up silver flowers,
    Morning mist disperses from the river’s surface,
    The mountain peaks lift their veils,
    The sun shines into Xiling Gorge.
    The sun shines on the red flag,
    The red flag flutters like the morning glow;
    The pontoon stands in the heart of the great river,
    The drilling rig towers like a pagoda.
    The steam whistle sounds, the steam whistle asks:
    —What are you doing here?
    The drilling rig sounds, the drilling rig answers:
    —I am going to develop the Xiling Gorge.

    Three

    Here will be built a dam to block the river,
    Built will be a ship lock that allows vessels to climb mountains,
    Rows of turbines will be buried underground,
    The angry river water will spark electricity.
    The Yangtze River will submit to human control,
    Floods will be retained in the Three Gorges;
    High mountains will become a sea,
    Cities will be built below Goddess Peak.
    Now, here, drilling rigs are opening the way,
    Hydrology teams are surveying,
    Aerial survey planes are flying,
    Scientists are contemplating.
    Tomorrow, this place will become a construction site,
    Beginning the world’s greatest project;
    To conquer the Yangtze River, tens of thousands of
    Brave people will come to the Three Gorges!

### 西陵峡口——三峡组诗之一
方纪
1956-08-16
第8版()
专栏：

    西陵峡口
    ——三峡组诗之一
    方纪
    一
    陡峭的山峰
    突然遮蔽了黎明的青光，
    江水暗淡了，
    船头激起黑色的波浪。
    黄牛——那矗立江心的巨石，
    像一个庞大的怪物
    迎面扑来……
    船只又灵巧地闪在一旁。
    船只像驶进了深渊，
    峡风呼哨，江水沸腾，
    晨星来不及隐去，
    被悬挂在那黑色的峭壁上。
    天空复盖在头上，
    像一个昏暗的池塘；
    只在那高与天齐的峰顶，
    又闪现了黎明的青光。
    二
    船长走进驾驶台来，
    望望天空说：天要亮了。
    果然，转过迎面的山峰，
    曙光照进驾驶台上。
    江水横流，激起银花，
    晨雾从江面散去，
    山峰揭去了头纱，
    太阳照进了西陵峡。
    太阳照在红旗上，
    红旗飘飘像朝霞；
    趸船矗立大江心，
    钻机高耸像宝塔。
    汽笛响了，汽笛问道：
    ——你在这里做什么？
    钻机响了，钻机回答：
    ——我要开发西陵峡。
    三
    这里将筑起拦江大坝，
    筑起那使船只爬山的船闸，
    成排的涡轮机埋伏在地下，
    愤怒的江水会发出火花。
    长江将服从人的支配，
    洪水被拦蓄在三峡；
    高山会变成大海，
    城市将建筑在神女峰下。
    现在，这里是钻机在开路，
    水文队在测量，
    航测机在飞行，
    科学家们在思索。
    明天，这里会变成工地，
    开始那世界上最伟大的工程；
    为了征服长江，成千上万的
    勇敢的人将来到三峡！
