Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Returns to Kunming; Burmese First Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein Holds Banquet to See Off Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, August 26th:  Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's  Congress  of the People' Republic of China, who was invited by the Indonesian government and President Sukarno for a friendly visit to Indonesia, returned to Kunming by special plane at noon today from Yangon.

After Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling deplaned, she was warmly welcomed by approximately one thousand representatives from all ethnic groups and circles in Yunnan Province and Kunming City.

Among those welcoming her at the airport were Yu Yichuan, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Ma Jikong, and other responsible persons from Party, government organs, and people's organizations.

The Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wiyopo Pranoto, also came specifically from Beijing to Kunming to welcome Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, August 26th: Burmese First Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein hosted a banquet on the evening of the 25th to bid farewell to Soong Ching Ling.

Before the banquet concluded, Soong Ching Ling presented a batch of Chinese animals to the Burmese government to the Burmese First Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein as a thank you for the rare Burmese animals the Burmese government gifted her when she visited Burma earlier this year. Soong Ching Ling’s gifts included a pair of sika deer, a pair of red-crowned cranes, and one hundred goldfish.

Soong Ching Ling expressed her warm wishes for the further consolidation and development of friendship between China and Burma. Soong Ching Ling thanked the Burmese government for the warm hospitality it extended to her when she passed through Yangon on her way from China to the Republic of Indonesia and back.

U Kyaw Nyein delivered a reply: "I earnestly hope that this exchange of gifts between our two countries will strengthen our friendship and help remove any obstacles that may exist on the road."

Also attending the banquet were: Daw Khin Kyi, wife of Aung San, U Myint Thein, a justice of the Supreme Court of Burma, and U Ba Lwin, Permanent Secretary of the Burmese Foreign Ministry, and others.

Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming and Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires in Burma Suwondo, as well as diplomatic envoys from other countries, attended the banquet.

### 宋庆龄副委员长回到昆明  缅甸第一副总理藻昆卓举行宴会欢送宋庆龄

1956-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副委员长回到昆明
    缅甸第一副总理藻昆卓举行宴会欢送宋庆龄
    据新华社昆明26日电　应印度尼西亚政府和苏加诺总统邀请赴印度尼西亚作友好访问的中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄，今天中午乘专机由仰光回到昆明。
    宋庆龄副委员长下机后受到云南省和昆明市各族各界代表约一千人的热烈欢迎。
    在机场上欢迎的，有中共云南省委书记于一川、马继孔等党、政府机关、人民团体的负责人。
    印度尼西亚驻我国大使维约普拉诺托还特地从北京来到昆明，到机场欢迎宋庆龄副委员长。
    据新华社仰光26日电　缅甸第一副总理藻昆卓25日晚举行宴会欢送宋庆龄。
    在宴会结束前，宋庆龄把她赠给缅甸政府的一批中国动物交给缅甸第一副总理藻昆卓，作为对今年初她访问缅甸时缅甸政府赠送缅甸稀有动物的答谢。宋庆龄的礼物包括一对梅花鹿，一对丹顶鹤和一百尾金鱼。
    宋庆龄表示她热烈地祝中国和缅甸之间的友谊进一步巩固和发展。宋庆龄感谢缅甸政府在她从中国到印度尼西亚共和国去和回国的途中经过仰光的时候所给予她的热情的招待。
    藻昆卓致答辞：“我热切希望我们两国之间这种礼物的交换将加强我们的友谊和帮助扫除道路上所可能存在的一切障碍”。
    参加宴会的还有：昂山夫人、缅甸最高法院法官吴敏登、缅甸外交部常任秘书巴林顿等。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和印度尼西亚驻缅甸大使馆临时代办苏旺多，以及其他国家外交使节都出席了这次宴会。
