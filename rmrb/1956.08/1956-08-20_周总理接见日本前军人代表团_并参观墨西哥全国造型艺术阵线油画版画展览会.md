Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Delegation of Former Japanese Soldiers and Visits Exhibition of Oil Paintings and Prints by the Mexican National Front of Plastic Arts
Author:
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 19th: Premier Zhou Enlai received all members of the Japanese former soldiers delegation to China on the afternoon of the 19th.

Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, General Chen Geng, Li Dequan, Cai Tingkai, Liu Fei, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Liao Chengzhi, and Qiao Guanhua, among others.

Xinhua News Agency, August 19th: Premier Zhou Enlai visited the Exhibition of Oil Paintings and Prints by the Mexican National Front of Plastic Arts this afternoon.

Mexican painter Aguirre Ignacio explained the content of the oil paintings to Premier Zhou Enlai in detail. He is one of the authors of the exhibited oil paintings and prints.

Premier Zhou Enlai carefully appreciated these oil paintings and prints and expressed his gratitude for the exhibition. He said that this would enable the Chinese people to learn more about Mexico. He hoped that the Mexican artists, who have a tradition of struggle, would work hard for world peace.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai in viewing the Mexican oil paintings and prints were Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations Chen Zhongjing, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhao Yimin, and figures from the capital's art circles.

### 周总理接见日本前军人代表团  并参观墨西哥全国造型艺术阵线油画版画展览会

1956-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本前军人代表团
    并参观墨西哥全国造型艺术阵线油画版画展览会
    新华社19日讯　周恩来总理在十九日下午接见了日本前军人访华代表团的全体人员。
    接见的时候在座的，有张奚若、陈赓大将、李德全、蔡廷锴、刘斐、萧向荣中将、廖承志和乔冠华等。
    新华社19日讯　周恩来总理今天下午参观了墨西哥全国造型艺术阵线油画、版画展览会。
    墨西哥画家阿吉瑞·伊格纳西奥向周恩来总理详细介绍了油画的内容。他是展出的油画和版画的作者之一。
    周恩来总理仔细地欣赏了这些油画和版画，并对这次油画和版画的展出表示感谢。他说，这样可以使中国人民更多地了解墨西哥。他希望有着斗争传统的墨西哥的艺术家们为世界和平而努力。
    陪同周总理观看墨西哥油画和版画的有文化部副部长夏衍，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏，以及首都美术界人士。
