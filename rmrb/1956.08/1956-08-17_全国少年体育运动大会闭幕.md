Title: National Junior Sports Meet Closes

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-17
Page: 4

Sports News:

    National Junior Sports Meet Closes
    (Our Qingdao dispatch, 16th) The first National Junior Sports Meet, after five days of intense performances and competitions, announced its closing at 4 PM today.
    During the competition and performances at this sports meet, the young athletes demonstrated a spirit of courage, tenacity, and solidarity, achieving excellent results. Among the 1,732 young athletes officially participating in the competition, 702 reached the standard of junior-level athletes. Among them, 278 reached the national third-level athlete standard, and six reached the national second-level athlete standard.
    The young athletes demonstrated that they are the most promising new force in the nation's sports movement. This meet laid a good foundation for carrying out diverse sports activities in every corner of the country. At the closing of the meet, the organizing committee encouraged the young sports athletes to humbly learn, guard against arrogance and impetuosity, continue their efforts, and, in accordance with Chairman Mao's instructions, become "three-good" students who are "good in health, good in studies, and good in work," preparing to become outstanding athletes in the future and further improve the nation's physical fitness.

### 全国少年体育运动大会闭幕

1956-08-17
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    全国少年体育运动大会闭幕
    本报青岛16日电　全国第一届少年体育运动大会经过五天的紧张表演、比赛，在今天下午四时宣布闭会。
    在这次运动会的竞赛与表演中，少年运动员们都表现了勇敢顽强和团结友爱的精神，取得了良好成绩。在正式参加比赛的1，732名少年运动员中，出现了702个达到少年级运动员标准的运动员。其中有278人达到国家三级运动员的标准，并且有六人达到了国家二级运动员的标准。
    少年运动员们显示出他们是祖国体育运动中最有希望的新生力量。大会为开展全国各个角落里的多种多样的体育活动打下了良好的基础。大会闭幕的时候，大会组织委员会勉励少年体育运动员们虚心学习、戒骄戒躁、再接再励，按照毛主席的指示做个“身体好、学习好、工作好”的三好学生，准备将来当一名出色的运动员，把祖国的国民体质提高一步。
