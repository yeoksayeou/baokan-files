Title: China Sells 60,000 Tons of Rice to India at a Fair Price
Author:
Date: 1956-08-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th—China and India have reached an agreement on rice negotiations, which was signed in Beijing today.

According to this agreement, China will sell 60,000 tons of rice to India at a fair and reasonable price, and will complete the shipment before the end of November this year.

Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, signed the agreement on behalf of China, and R.K. Nehru, the Indian Ambassador to China, signed on behalf of India.

### 我国以公平价格售给印度六万吨大米

1956-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

    我国以公平价格售给印度六万吨大米
    新华社28日讯　中国和印度的大米谈判已经达成协议，今天在北京签字。
    按照这个协议，中国将以公平合理的价格售给印度六万吨大米，并且在今年十一月底以前运完。
    中国方面在协议上签字的是对外贸易部副部长李哲人，印度方面签字的是印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁。
