Title: American Civil Liberties Union Points Out U.S. State Department Violates Freedom of the Press
Author:
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York dispatch: The American Civil Liberties Union, in a letter sent to Secretary of State Dulles on the 9th, criticized the State Department's practice of prohibiting American journalists from visiting China as "raising two serious civil rights issues: freedom of the press and freedom of movement."

### 美国民权保障联盟指出  美国务院违反新闻自由

1956-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    美国民权保障联盟指出  美国务院违反新闻自由
    据新华社讯　纽约消息：美国民权保障联盟在九日寄信给国务卿杜勒斯，指责国务院禁止美国记者访问中国的做法“引起了两个严重的民权问题：新闻自由和行动自由”。
