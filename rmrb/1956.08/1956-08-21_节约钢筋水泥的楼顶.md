Title: Roofs That Save Steel and Cement
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 2

    Roofs That Save Steel and Cement
    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch) The civil engineering department of the Kailuan Design Institute is designing a three-story building, the Tangshan Guest House of the Kailuan Coal Mine, whose roof will use brick arches instead of reinforced concrete. Preliminary calculations show that each floor's roof can save seven tons of steel and eleven tons of cement.

### 节约钢筋水泥的楼顶

1956-08-21
第2版()
专栏：

    节约钢筋水泥的楼顶
    据新华社讯　开滦设计分院土木科正在设计的一座三层楼——开滦煤矿唐山招待所，楼顶将用砖砌的拱代替钢筋混凝土。初步计算，一层楼顶可以节省钢筋七吨，水泥十一吨。
