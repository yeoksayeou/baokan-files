Title: Closing of the Sports Work Conference of Korea, Mongolia, Vietnam, and China
Author:
Date: 1956-08-21
Page: 4

    Sports News

    Closing of the Sports Work Conference of Korea, Mongolia, Vietnam, and China
    (Our newspaper's dispatch) The Sports Work Conference of Korea, Mongolia, Vietnam, and China recently closed in Beijing. Representatives from the sports organizations of the four countries discussed issues related to sports exchanges among the four countries in 1957; introduced the development of sports in their respective countries; and exchanged opinions on how to develop mass sports activities, improve sports technical levels, and strengthen international sports activities.
    During their stay in Beijing, the delegates from Korea, Mongolia, and Vietnam visited Beijing's sports facilities and toured scenic spots. They have now left Beijing to visit various parts of the country.

### 朝蒙越中四国体育工作会议闭幕

1956-08-21
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    朝蒙越中四国体育工作会议闭幕
    本报讯　朝、蒙、越、中四国体育工作会议最近在北京闭幕。四国体育机构的代表们在这次会议上商谈了1957年四国间体育来往的问题；互相介绍了各国体育运动的发展情况；交换了如何开展群众性体育运动、提高运动技术水平和加强国际体育活动的意见。
    朝、蒙、越三国代表团人员在北京期间，曾参观了北京的体育设备和游览了名胜，现已离京到各地访问。
