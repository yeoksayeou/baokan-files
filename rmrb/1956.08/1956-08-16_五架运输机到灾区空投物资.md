Title: Five Transport Planes Airdrop Supplies to Disaster Area
Author:
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, August 14th. A transport unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force dispatched five more planes this morning to airdrop rescue equipment and food over the flood-stricken areas. Among them, two planes dropped two thousand automobile inner tubes for life-saving and thirty thousand catties of grain over Linqing and Guantao in Shandong Province; the other three planes dropped fifteen hundred automobile inner tubes in the area north of Xian County, south of the Ziya River, east of the Fuyang River, and near the Shaheqiao bridge in Hebei Province.

### 五架运输机到灾区空投物资

1956-08-16
第3版()
专栏：

    五架运输机到灾区空投物资
    新华社14日讯　中国人民解放军空军某运输部队，今天上午又派出五架飞机到被水淹地区的上空，投下救生器材和粮食。其中，两架在山东省临清、馆陶上空，投下二千只救生用的汽车轮内胎和三万斤粮食；另外三架在河北省献县以北、子牙河以南、滏阳河以东和沙河桥附近地区投下一千五百只汽车轮内胎。
