Title: Egyptian Canal Workers Donate Wages to Aswan Dam Fund

Author:

Date: 1956-08-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 1st: According to yesterday's newspapers, all Egyptian workers working on the Suez Canal will donate one day's wages to the national fund for the construction of the Aswan High Dam.

The Suez Canal Workers' Union sent a delegation on July 30 to congratulate President Abdel Nasser on the nationalization of the Suez Canal and to report to him the gift they had decided to present.

### 埃及运河工人捐献工资作为修阿斯旺水坝基金

1956-08-02
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及运河工人捐献工资作为修阿斯旺水坝基金
    新华社1日讯　开罗消息：据昨天的报纸报道，在苏彝士运河工作的全体埃及工人将捐出他们一天的工资作为修建阿斯旺高水坝的国家基金。
    运河工人工会在七月三十日派遣了一个代表团去向阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统祝贺苏彝士运河收归国有，并且向他报告他们所决定赠送的礼物。
