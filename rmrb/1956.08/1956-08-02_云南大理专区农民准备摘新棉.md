Title: Farmers in Dali Prefecture, Yunnan Province, Prepare to Pick New Cotton
Author:
Date: 1956-08-02
Page: 3

    Farmers in Dali Prefecture, Yunnan Province, Prepare to Pick New Cotton
    (Our dispatch from Kunming) The more than 100,000 mu of cotton in Dali Prefecture, Yunnan Province, will be ready for picking in early August. Members of various agricultural cooperatives are preparing cloth bags and drying mats to welcome the coming harvest. The cotton in the entire Dali Prefecture is growing very well this year. The cooperative members say that they have not seen such good cotton in nearly ten years. According to estimates from various agricultural cooperatives, the average yield of ginned cotton per mu can reach more than 50 jin, an increase of about 100% compared to last year.

### 云南大理专区农民准备摘新棉

1956-08-02
第3版()
专栏：

    云南大理专区农民准备摘新棉
    本报昆明电　云南省大理专区的十万多亩棉花，八月初即将采摘。各农业社的社员们正在准备布袋和晒帘，迎接即将到来的丰收。今年整个大理专区的棉花长得都很好，社员们说，近十年来从没有过这样好的棉花。据各农业社估计，一般每亩皮棉产量都可达到五十斤以上，比去年增产一倍左右。
