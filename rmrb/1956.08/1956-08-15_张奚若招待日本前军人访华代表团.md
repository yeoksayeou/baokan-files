Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Japanese Former Military Personnel Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1956-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight for the visiting Japanese Former Military Personnel Delegation.

    In his welcoming speech, Zhang Xiruo said that the Chinese people are willing to maintain lasting peaceful and friendly relations with Japan and to strengthen economic and cultural exchanges, because this is beneficial to the people of both countries.

    Former Japanese Lieutenant General Saburo Endo, in his reply, expressed his gratitude for the delegation's visit to China. He said that we will truthfully report the real situation in China to the Japanese people, which will help promote friendship between the people of China and Japan.

    Among those present at the banquet were Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Qiao Guanhua, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, as well as Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Fei, Zheng Dongguo, Hou Jingru, Weng Wenhao, and Cao Zhongshu.

### 张奚若招待日本前军人访华代表团

1956-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    张奚若招待日本前军人访华代表团
    新华社14日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚设宴招待日本前军人访华代表团。
    张奚若在会上致欢迎词说，中国人民愿意和日本永远和平友好、加强经济和文化的来往。因为这样，对我们两国人民都是有利的。
    前日本陆军中将远藤三郎在答词中对这次代表团能来中国访问表示感谢。他说，我们将把中国的真实情况真实地向日本人民报告，这将有助于中日两国人民亲善。
    出席今天宴会作陪的有中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、陈翰笙、乔冠华，以及萧向荣中将，廖承志，刘斐，郑洞国，侯镜如，翁文灏，曹中枢等人。
