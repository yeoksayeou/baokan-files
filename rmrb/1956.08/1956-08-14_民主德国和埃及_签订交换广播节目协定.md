Title: East Germany and Egypt Sign Agreement on Exchange of Radio Programs
Author:
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 13th: The East German radio station announced on the 10th that East Germany has signed an agreement with Egypt on the exchange of radio programs and radio content.

### 民主德国和埃及  签订交换广播节目协定

1956-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    民主德国和埃及
    签订交换广播节目协定
    新华社柏林13日电　民主德国的广播电台十日宣布，民主德国已经和埃及签订了一项关于交换广播节目和广播内容的协定。
