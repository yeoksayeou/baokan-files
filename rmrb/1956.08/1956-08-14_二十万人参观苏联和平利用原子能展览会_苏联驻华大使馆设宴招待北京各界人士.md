Title: 200,000 People Visit the Soviet Exhibition on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy; Soviet Embassy in China Hosts Banquet for People from All Walks of Life in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-08-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 13th. The Soviet Exhibition on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy and Technology will close on the 15th. The Soviet Embassy in China held a banquet this evening to entertain people from all walks of life in Beijing, including scientists.

    Li Haiqiu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Golikov, Curator of the Soviet Exhibition on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy and Technology, spoke at the banquet successively. In their speeches, they announced that nearly 200,000 Chinese scientists and people had visited the exhibition in the nearly two months since its opening. They expressed their gratitude to the Chinese government and the Chinese staff who participated in the exhibition.

    Pei Lisheng, Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, spoke on behalf of President Guo Moruo, saying that the exhibition had played a huge role, not only helping China's scientific community and the general public further understand the Soviet Union's brilliant achievements in the peaceful use of atomic energy, but also promoting the strengthening of research on the peaceful use of atomic energy in China's scientific community. He expressed his heartfelt thanks to the Soviet government, Soviet scientists, and the Soviet people for their selfless assistance to the Chinese people.

    More than 300 people from various government departments and the scientific community attended the banquet.

### 二十万人参观苏联和平利用原子能展览会  苏联驻华大使馆设宴招待北京各界人士

1956-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    二十万人参观苏联和平利用原子能展览会
    苏联驻华大使馆设宴招待北京各界人士
    新华社13日讯　苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会将在十五日闭幕。苏联驻中国大使馆今天晚间举行宴会，招待中国科学等各界人士。
    苏联驻华大使馆临时代办李海秋、苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会馆长戈利科夫先后在宴会上讲话。他们在讲话中宣布这个展览会开幕近两个月来，参观的已经有二十万中国科学家和中国人民。他们对中国政府和参加展览会工作的中国工作人员的帮助表示感谢。
    中国科学院秘书长裴丽生代表郭沫若院长在讲话中说，这次展览会起了巨大的作用，它不仅帮助我国科学界和广大人民进一步了解苏联在和平利用原子能方面的辉煌成就，并且促进了我国科学界加强对和平利用原子能的研究。他对苏联政府、苏联科学家和苏联人民对中国人民的这种无私帮助表示衷心的感谢。
    出席这次宴会的，有政府各部门和科学界人士共三百多人。
