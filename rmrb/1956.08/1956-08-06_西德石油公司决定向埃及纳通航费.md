Title: West German Oil Companies Decide to Pay Suez Canal Tolls to Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-08-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hamburg, 5th. The West German "Esso" and "Hapag" oil company headquarters in Hamburg announced that their oil tankers will pay tolls directly to the Egyptian government through the companies' agents in Egypt.

### 西德石油公司决定向埃及纳通航费

1956-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    西德石油公司决定向埃及纳通航费
    新华社5日讯　汉堡消息：在汉堡的西德“埃索”和“合牌”石油公司总部宣布，它们的油船将通过这两家公司在埃及的代理人直接向埃及政府缴纳通航费。
