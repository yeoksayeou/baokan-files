Title: South Vietnamese Government Admits to Invading My Nansha Islands
Author:
Date: 1956-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 30th. According to a United Press International dispatch from Saigon on the 30th, the South Vietnamese government today officially confirmed that it dispatched naval forces on August 22nd to invade China's Nansha Islands and illegally raised the South Vietnamese flag there.

A South Vietnamese government spokesman told a UPI reporter that the landing by South Vietnamese naval escort ships was to "maintain" the South Vietnamese government's claim to the Nansha Islands.

### 南越政府承认侵占我南沙群岛的行为

1956-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    南越政府承认侵占我南沙群岛的行为
    新华社河内30日电　据合众社30日西贡消息，南越政府今天正式证实，它在8月22日派遣了海军部队侵占中国的南沙群岛，并且在那里非法升起南越的旗子。
    一个南越政府的发言人告诉合众社记者说，南越海军护航船的登陆是为了“维护”南越政府对南沙群岛的要求。
