Title: Czechoslovak Government Presents China with Complete Set of Farm Equipment; Presentation Ceremony Held in Cang County, Hebei Province; Farm Using Equipment Named Sino-Czech Friendship Farm
Author:
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 1

　　Cang County, August 2nd (By Our Own Correspondent) A ceremony for the Czechoslovak Republic’s donation of a complete set of farm machinery to China was held this morning in Cangzhen, the seat of Cang County Prefecture, Hebei Province.

　　The batch of agricultural machinery donated to China by the Czechoslovak Republic, including thirty-four types of more than six hundred and seventy pieces of agricultural machinery, was gifted to China in January of this year by Czechoslovak Republic Premier Viliam Široký and Czechoslovak Communist Party First Secretary Antonín Novotný during Vice Chairman Zhu De's visit to the Czechoslovak Republic. In order to commemorate the friendship of the government and people of the Czechoslovak Republic, China's State Council has decided to name the state-owned mechanical farm in Huanghua County, which will use this machinery, the "Sino-Czech Friendship Farm." These machines have been transported to Cang County, and under the guidance of seven agricultural machinery experts sent by the Czechoslovak Republic, the installation work has been completed.

　　Vice Governor of Hebei Province, Yan Dakai, spoke first at the presentation ceremony held today. First, on behalf of the Hebei Provincial People's Committee and the people of the entire province, he expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Czechoslovak government and people for this internationalist and selfless assistance to China. He said that the people of Hebei Province feel extremely honored and excited that the "Sino-Czech Friendship Farm" is established within Hebei Province. The Hebei Provincial People's Committee has already made a decision to fully support the "Sino-Czech Friendship Farm." He called on the farm workers to use practical actions in running the farm well to repay the Czechoslovak government and people for their care and help to China.

　　Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Republic's Embassy in China, A. Vašek, spoke amidst warm applause. In his speech, he emphasized that they were very pleased with the sincere friendship that had already been established between the people of China and Czechoslovakia. He believes that the donation of these agricultural machines and the establishment of the "Sino-Czech Friendship Farm" will promote the continuous development and consolidation of the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Czechoslovakia. He wished the Chinese working people victory in mastering new scientific and technological work.

　　Finally, on behalf of the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic, A. Vašek, amid thunderous applause, presented the list of agricultural machinery and equipment donated to China to Cai Ziwei, Vice Minister of Agriculture of China, who received the gift.

　　After receiving the gift, Cai Ziwei, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, expressed his heartfelt thanks to the Czechoslovak Government and extended a warm welcome to the Czechoslovak experts who came to China to guide the installation and use of the machinery. He said that through the establishment of the "Sino-Czech Friendship Farm," the friendship between the people of China and Czechoslovakia will be further consolidated and developed.

　　He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, read the letter of thanks from Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council to Premier Viliam Široký of the Czechoslovak Republic.

　　Nadrnasky, the head of the Czechoslovak agricultural machinery expert group, and Liu Zhong, the director of the "Sino-Czech Friendship Farm," then spoke at the meeting.

　　More than a thousand people attended today's presentation ceremony.

　　In the evening, Vice Governor of Hebei Province, Yan Dakai, hosted a banquet to entertain A. Vašek, the Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Republic in China, and the experts.

### 捷政府赠送我国全套农场设备  受礼大会在河北省沧县举行  使用设备的农场命名为中捷友谊农场

1956-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    捷政府赠送我国全套农场设备
    受礼大会在河北省沧县举行
    使用设备的农场命名为中捷友谊农场
    本报沧县2日电　捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府赠送我国全套农场机器设备受礼大会，今日上午在河北省沧县专区驻地沧镇举行。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国赠送我国的这批包括三十四种六百七十多件农业机器，是在今年一月间朱德副主席访问捷克斯洛伐克共和国时，由捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理威·西罗基和捷克斯洛伐克共产党第一书记诺沃提尼赠送给我国的。为了纪念捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府和人民的友情，我国国务院决定将使用这批机器设备的黄骅县的国营机械农场命名为“中捷友谊农场”。这些机器已运到沧县，并在捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府派来的七位农业机械专家的指导下，完成了安装工作。
    河北省副省长阎达开在今天举行的受礼大会上首先讲话。他首先代表河北省人民委员会和全省人民，衷心感谢捷克斯洛伐克政府和人民对我国的这种国际主义的无私援助。他说，河北省人民以“中捷友谊农场”建立在河北省境内，感到极大的光荣和兴奋。河北省人民委员会已经作了决定，要对“中捷友谊农场”充分予以支持。他号召农场职工用办好农场的实际行动，来答谢捷克斯洛伐克政府和人民对我国的关怀和帮助。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办安·瓦赛克在热烈的掌声中讲话。他在讲话中强调说，他们为中、捷两国人民已经建立起的忠诚友谊，感到十分愉快。他相信赠送这些农业机器以及“中捷友谊农场”的建立，将会促进中、捷两国人民兄弟般的友谊得到不断的发展和巩固。他祝中国劳动人民在掌握新的科学技术的工作中获得胜利。
    最后，安·瓦赛克代表捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府在暴风雨般的掌声中，将赠送我国的农业机器设备清册，送交给受礼的我国农业部副部长蔡子伟。
    蔡子伟在受礼后，代表中华人民共和国政府向捷克斯洛伐克政府表示衷心的感谢，并向来中国指导安装和使用机器的捷克斯洛伐克专家们，表示热烈的欢迎。他说，通过“中捷友谊农场”的建立，中捷两国人民的友谊，将得到进一步的巩固和发展。
    外交部部长助理何伟，宣读了国务院周恩来总理给捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理威·西罗基的感谢信。
    捷克斯洛伐克农业机器专家组长那德尔那斯基和“中捷友谊农场”场长刘忠等接着在会上讲了话。
    参加今天受礼大会的有一千多人。
    当晚，河北省副省长阎达开设宴招待捷克斯洛伐克驻中国临时代办安·瓦赛克和专家。
