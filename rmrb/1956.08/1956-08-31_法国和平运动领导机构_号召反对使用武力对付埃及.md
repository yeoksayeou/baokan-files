Title: French Peace Movement Leadership Calls for Opposition to the Use of Force Against Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-08-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 30th, TASS, Paris: The leadership of the French Peace Movement issued a statement on the Suez issue, saying that France, already caught in the "Algerian tragedy," cannot and should not embark on the path of war. The use of force cannot justly resolve the Suez Canal dispute; using force is a blatant violation of the United Nations Charter, which France solemnly signed, and would create the danger of conflict for the whole world.

The statement said: At the London conference, the vast majority of attendees expressed opposition to the use of force, advocating for a negotiated solution. The parties concerned should continue negotiations to seek a peaceful solution endorsed by the United Nations, and this solution must respect Egypt's sovereignty and ensure that all countries, without exception, can freely pass through the canal.

The French Peace Movement leadership calls on every French person, all organizations and groups fighting for peace, to demand that the government, members of parliament, mayors, provincial councilors, and municipal councilors reject the idea of using force on the Suez issue.

### 法国和平运动领导机构  号召反对使用武力对付埃及

1956-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

    法国和平运动领导机构
    号召反对使用武力对付埃及
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社巴黎讯：法国和平运动领导机构发表关于苏彝士问题的声明说，正陷于“阿尔及利亚悲剧”中的法国，现在不能也不应该踏上战争的道路。用武力是不能公正解决苏彝士运河问题的争执的，使用武力就是公然破坏法国曾经庄严地签字的联合国宪章，并且会为全世界造成冲突的危险。
    声明说：在伦敦会议上，绝大多数到会者都表示反对使用武力，主张通过协商解决问题。有关各方应该继续进行谈判，寻求为联合国赞同的和平解决，而且这个解决办法要尊重埃及的主权，保证一切国家无例外地能够自由地通过运河。
    法国和平运动领导机构号召每个法国人，一切为和平而斗争的组织和团体，要求政府、议会议员、市长、省议员和市议员拒绝在苏彝士问题上使用武力的主张。
