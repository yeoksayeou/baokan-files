Title: Hu Yuzhi Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Speaker Sartono and Welcome Ambassador Viyopranoto

Author:

Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to Indonesian Speaker of Parliament Sartono and his wife, along with the visiting parliamentarians, who are about to leave Beijing to return home. He also welcomed the newly appointed Indonesian Ambassador to China, Viyopranoto.

At the banquet, Hu Yuzhi said: "We are very pleased to see the increasing exchanges between our two peoples, and the two peoples are as close as brothers. In particular, Speaker Sartono's visit has established direct contact between the leaders of our two parliaments, which is of great help to enhancing the friendship and understanding between the Chinese and Indonesian peoples, and is also beneficial to promoting the Bandung spirit and peaceful cooperation among the Asian and African peoples." He said, "Speaker Sartono has brought the Indonesian people's friendly feelings towards China, and we believe he will take the Chinese people's friendly feelings towards Indonesia back with him."

In welcoming Viyopranoto's appointment as Ambassador to China, Hu Yuzhi said: "We have been friends for a long time. I remember that in 1955, on the day the China-Indonesia Friendship Association was established, you and Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo attended our inaugural meeting and gave strong support to our work."

Speaker Sartono expressed his reluctance to part at the meeting. He said that as soon as they set foot on Chinese soil, they felt the deep friendship and affection of the Chinese people towards them. From the Chinese leaders to the receptionists, they were treated very well. He expressed his gratitude for this. He also said that he hoped that their visit would make the friendship between Indonesia and China even closer.

Sartono said that they would take the beautiful memories of China back to Indonesia, and take back to Indonesia the meetings with Chinese leaders, as well as the deep impression that Chinese leaders are deeply supported by the Chinese people, and that Chinese leaders are leading the people towards prosperity and happiness.

Ambassador Viyopranoto also spoke at the meeting. He said that the friendship between China and Indonesia will last forever. He proposed a toast to this profound friendship.

Among those present at the banquet today were Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and some members, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ma Yuhuai, Vice President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, and people from all walks of life in China who had visited Indonesia.

### 胡愈之设宴欢送沙多诺议长  同时欢迎维约普拉诺托大使

1956-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    胡愈之设宴欢送沙多诺议长
    同时欢迎维约普拉诺托大使
    新华社7日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之今晚举行宴会，欢送行将离北京回国的印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺和夫人以及同来访问的议员们，欢迎新近到任的印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托。
    在宴会上，胡愈之说：“我们很高兴，看到我们两国之间人民的来往，一天比一天增加，两国人民正像兄弟一般地亲密无间。特别是沙多诺议长这次来访，使我们两国议会的领袖们之间建立了直接的接触，这一件事对于中国印度尼西亚两国人民之间友谊和谅解的增进，有很大的帮助，对于万隆精神的发扬和亚非人民的和平合作也是有好处的”。他说，“沙多诺议长把印度尼西亚人民对中国的友好情感带了来，我们相信他一定会把中国人民对印度尼西亚人民的友好情感带回去。”
    胡愈之在为欢迎维约普拉诺托荣任驻中国大使时说：“我们早就是相识的朋友了。记得在1955年，我们中国印度尼西亚友好协会成立的那一天，你和阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理参加了我们的成立大会，给我们的工作以大力支持。”
    沙多诺议长在会上讲话时向大家表示惜别。他说，当他们一踏上中国的国土，就感到中国人民对他们的深厚友谊和感情，从中国领袖到招待员都对他们款待很好。他对此表示感谢。他还说，希望他们这次访问，能使印度尼西亚和中国两国的友谊更加密切起来。
    沙多诺说，他们将把在中国的美好回忆带回印度尼西亚去，把同中国领袖们的会见，以及中国领袖们深得中国人民拥护，中国领袖们把人民引向繁荣幸福的道路等深刻印象带回印度尼西亚去。
    维约普拉诺托大使也在会上讲了话。他说中国同印度尼西亚的友谊将永远存在下去。他提议为这深远的友谊干杯。
    今天出席宴会的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通和部分委员，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长马玉槐以及曾经访问过印度尼西亚的中国各界人士。
