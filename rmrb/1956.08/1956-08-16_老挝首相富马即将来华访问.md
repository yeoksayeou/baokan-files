Title: Lao Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma to Visit China Soon
Author:
Date: 1956-08-16
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China today issued the following communique:

    At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, will lead a delegation of the Government of the Kingdom of Laos to China on August 20 for a friendly visit.

### 老挝首相富马即将来华访问

1956-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    老挝首相富马即将来华访问
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国外交部今日发表公报如下：
    应中华人民共和国政府的邀请，老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王将率领老挝王国政府代表团于八月二十日来中国进行友好访问。
