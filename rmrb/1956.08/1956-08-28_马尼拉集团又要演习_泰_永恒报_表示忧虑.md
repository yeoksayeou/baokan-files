Title: Manila Pact Group to Hold Exercises Again; Thai "Eternal Daily" Expresses Concern
Author:
Date: 1956-08-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th, Bangkok Dispatch: The "Eternal Daily" published an editorial on the 26th, expressing concern about the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization's upcoming military exercises in the South China Sea. The editorial said that due to the tensions caused by the Suez Canal issue, the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization "should avoid creating more acute tensions."
    The newspaper's editorial called on the Thai government to help avoid misunderstandings. The editorial said that most Asian countries are now pursuing a policy of neutrality.
    The Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Secretariat recently announced that military exercises will be held at the end of September.

### 马尼拉集团又要演习  泰“永恒报”表示忧虑

1956-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

    马尼拉集团又要演习
    泰“永恒报”表示忧虑
    新华社27日讯　曼谷消息：“永恒报”26日发表社论，对即将在南中国海举行的东南亚条约组织的军事演习表示忧虑。社论说，由于苏彝士运河问题而存在的紧张局势，东南亚条约组织“应该避免制造更剧烈的紧张局势”。
    这家报纸的社论要求泰国政府协助避免发生误解，社论说，大多数亚洲国家现在都奉行中立政策。
    东南亚条约组织秘书处最近宣布，军事演习将在9月底举行。
