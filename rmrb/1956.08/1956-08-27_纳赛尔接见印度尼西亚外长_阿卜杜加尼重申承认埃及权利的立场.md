Title: Nasser Receives Indonesian Foreign Minister, Abdoelgani Reaffirms Position of Recognizing Egypt's Rights
Author:
Date: 1956-08-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 26th: Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser received Indonesian Foreign Minister Abdoelgani in Cairo on August 25th after his arrival from London. After his talks with President Nasser, Abdoelgani told reporters that he had explained the position of the Indonesian delegation to President Nasser.

He said that Indonesia recognizes Egypt's right to nationalize the Suez Canal and believes that the Egyptian government can guarantee the freedom of navigation through the Suez Canal.

The Indonesian Foreign Minister also said that he is optimistic about the possibility of a peaceful resolution to the Suez Canal issue.

Abdoelgani arrived in Cairo on the evening of the 24th. He is scheduled to visit the Suez Canal and then leave Cairo on the 27th to return to Jakarta.

On the same day that President Nasser received the Indonesian Foreign Minister, he also received the Indian Ambassador to Egypt, Ali Yavar Jung. The ambassador handed Nasser a copy of the proposals made by the Indian delegation at the London conference.

### 纳赛尔接见印度尼西亚外长  阿卜杜加尼重申承认埃及权利的立场

1956-08-27
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔接见印度尼西亚外长
    阿卜杜加尼重申承认埃及权利的立场
    新华社26日讯　开罗消息：埃及总统加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔在8月25日接见了从伦敦到达开罗的印度尼西亚外交部长阿卜杜加尼。在同纳赛尔谈话结束以后，阿卜杜加尼对新闻记者说，他向纳赛尔总统说明了印度尼西亚代表团的立场。
    他说，印度尼西亚承认埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有的权利，并且相信埃及政府能够保证苏彝士运河的通航自由。
    印度尼西亚外交部长还说，他对苏彝士运河问题和平解决的可能性抱乐观态度。
    阿卜杜加尼是在24日晚间到达开罗，他预定在访问苏彝士运河后在27日离开开罗回雅加达。
    纳赛尔总统在接见印度尼西亚外交部长的同一天，还接见了印度驻埃及大使阿里·雅瓦尔·忠格。这位大使把印度代表团在伦敦会议上提出的建议的副本交给了纳赛尔。
