Title: Thailand Agrees to Cambodia Importing Goods to China Through Thailand
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, August 18th: According to a report in the "Honor Newspaper" on the 14th, the Thai government has formally agreed to Cambodia importing goods destined for China or other socialist countries through Thailand. The Thai and Cambodian governments have also signed an agreement on the transshipment of goods.

Thai Foreign Minister Narathip told the newspaper's reporter that the agreement shows that Thailand will provide Cambodia with convenience so that Cambodia can use Bangkok's port to import aid materials from China and import goods purchased from China.

Narathip said that Thailand is happy to do so because Thailand has no specific regulations prohibiting the transshipment of goods from China or other communist countries through Thailand.

### 泰国同意柬埔寨经泰国输入中国物资

1956-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

    泰国同意柬埔寨经泰国输入中国物资
    新华社18日讯　曼谷消息：据“荣誉报”十四日报道，泰国政府已经正式同意柬埔寨经过泰国输入中国或其他社会主义国家的货物，泰、柬两国政府并且已经就货物转运问题签订了一项协定。
    泰国外交部长那拉底对这家报纸的记者说，这项协定表明，泰国将给予柬埔寨以方便，以便柬埔寨利用曼谷港口输入中国的援助物资和输入向中国购买的商品。
    那拉底说，泰国所以乐意这样做，是因为泰国并没有特别规定禁止中国或其他共产党国家的货物经过泰国转运。
