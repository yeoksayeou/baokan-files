Title: Beijing Welcomes Distinguished Guests from Laos; People from All Circles Hold Welcome Rally at Beijing Gymnasium; Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma Expresses Warm Wish that the Friendship Between China and Laos Will Forever Be Maintained and Developed
Author:
Date: 1956-08-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd: Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, Prince Souvanna Phouma, and the government delegation of the Kingdom of Laos he leads, received a banner presented by Mayor Peng Zhen on behalf of the people of Beijing at a welcome rally held this afternoon by 8,000 people from all circles in Beijing. The golden characters embroidered on this red satin banner read: "Please convey the friendship of the people of Beijing to the esteemed people of Laos; We wish the friendship between China and Laos will develop day by day; We wish Laos national prosperity and the people happiness."

In his speech before presenting the banner, Peng Zhen, on behalf of all the citizens of Beijing, expressed heartfelt gratitude and a warm welcome to the distinguished guests for their friendly visit. He said, we congratulate the Kingdom of Laos on its independence, unity, and the great solidarity of its people, and we congratulate the growing and enduring friendship between our two countries. We firmly believe that through your visit, the friendly relations between our two countries will gain new development and will lay a new foundation for strengthening economic and cultural cooperation between the two countries.

Prince Souvanna Phouma responded to the welcome from the people of Beijing with a warm speech. He said that the care shown everywhere in your beautiful capital to the Lao delegation proves that the long-standing friendship between the people of China and Laos is not just empty words. This friendship, built from history and from the bottom of our hearts, should be preserved and developed forever for the happiness of us all. The Prince then said that we sincerely seek the friendship and affection of all neighboring countries, and we are proud to have received such friendship and affection here. During his speech, the venue frequently erupted in storms of applause.

The welcome rally was held in the competition hall of the Beijing Gymnasium. The venue was decorated with the national flags of Laos and China, sixteen red flags, and colorful flags adorned with doves of peace and golden olive branches. The huge slogan in gold letters on a red background facing the rostrum read "Welcome the Government Delegation of the Kingdom of Laos Headed by His Highness Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma of Laos", "Long Live the Friendship Between the People of China and Laos", "Long Live Asian-African and World Peace". The stands and aisles were crowded with representatives from all circles of Beijing, their colorful summer clothes forming a magnificent pattern in the venue.

Prince Souvanna Phouma and all the distinguished members of the delegation, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Mayor Peng Zhen, entered the venue at 2:30 PM and ascended to the rostrum. The entire venue stood up to welcome the distinguished guests, shouting "Welcome" amidst rhythmic applause, and a group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to the guests. The applause, cheers, and the music of welcome continued for a long time.

Also on the rostrum with the distinguished guests were Vice Premier He Long, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Chairman of the Beijing Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Liu Ren, Vice Mayors of Beijing Wu Han, Xue Zizheng, Le Songsheng, and heads of various people's organizations in Beijing.

After the band played the Laotian national anthem and the Chinese national anthem, Mayor Peng Zhen and Prince Souvanna Phouma spoke at the rally (full text on page 4). Subsequently, Mayor Peng Zhen, on behalf of the people of Beijing, presented banners and gifts to the distinguished guests.

Literary and artistic workers from Beijing then performed music, dance, and acrobatic programs for the distinguished guests. At the end of the performance, the distinguished guests stepped down from the rostrum and were immediately surrounded by the crowd. The venue cheered "Peace," "Friendship" in unison. Prince Souvanna Phouma smiled and put his hands together to express his gratitude, and Deputy Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith also waved frequently to acknowledge the greetings. As the distinguished guests left the venue, more than 10,000 arms waved in the air, amid applause and cheers.

After the rally, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma and some members of the delegation visited the Temple of Heaven and the Temple of Agriculture. This morning, the distinguished guests visited the Yonghe Temple and Guangji Temple, famous temples in Beijing. (Attached picture)

### 北京欢迎来自老挝的贵宾  各界人民在北京体育馆举行欢迎大会  梭发那·富马首相热情表示愿中老两国友谊永远保持和发展

1956-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    北京欢迎来自老挝的贵宾
    各界人民在北京体育馆举行欢迎大会
    梭发那·富马首相热情表示愿中老两国友谊永远保持和发展
    据新华社22日讯　老挝王国首相梭发那·富马亲王和他所率领的老挝王国政府代表团，今天下午在北京市各界人民八千人举行的欢迎大会上，接受了彭真市长代表北京人民献赠的锦旗。绣在这面红缎锦旗上面的金字写道：“请你们把北京人民的友谊转达给尊敬的老挝人民；祝中老两国之间的友谊日益发展；祝老挝国家繁荣，人民幸福。”
    彭真在赠旗以前的讲话中，代表北京市全体市民对贵宾们的友好访问表示衷心的感谢和热烈的欢迎。他说，我们祝贺老挝王国的独立统一和全国人民大团结，祝贺我们两国之间的持久友谊日益发展。我们深信，通过你们的访问，我们两国的友好关系将获得新的发展，并且会为加强两国的经济和文化合作奠立新的基础。
    梭发那·富马亲王以热情洋溢的讲话来答谢北京市人民对他们的欢迎。他说，你们美丽的首都到处对老挝代表团表现的关怀，证明中老两国人民的悠久友谊不是一句空话。这种从历史中和心坎里所建立的友谊，为了我们大家的幸福应当永远保存并且加以发展。这位亲王接着说，我们诚恳地要求一切邻国的友谊和情感，我们因为在这里得到了这种友谊和情感而感到骄傲。在他讲话的时候，会场内不时发出一阵阵暴风雨般的掌声。
    欢迎大会在北京体育馆比赛馆举行。大会会场内悬挂着老挝、中国的国旗，装饰着十六面红旗和缀有和平鸽、金色橄榄枝的彩旗。正对着主席台的巨幅红底金字标语上面，写着“欢迎以老挝首相梭发那·富马亲王殿下为首的老挝王国政府代表团”、“中老两国人民的友谊万岁”、“亚非和世界和平万岁”。四周看台上和走道上挤满了北京市各界人民的代表，他们穿着的五颜六色的夏装，在会场组成了瑰丽的图案。
    梭发那·富马亲王和代表团全体贵宾们和周恩来总理由彭真市长陪同，在下午二时三十分进入会场，登上主席台。场内全体起立欢迎贵宾，在有节奏的掌声中高呼“欢迎”，一队少先队员向贵宾们献了花。掌声、欢呼声和欢迎曲的音乐声持续了很长的时间。
    同贵宾们一起在主席台上的，有贺龙副总理，陈毅副总理，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会主席刘仁，北京市副市长吴晗、薛子正、乐松生和北京市各人民团体负责人。
    乐队奏老挝国歌和中国国歌后，彭真市长和梭发那·富马亲王在会上讲了话（全文见第四版）。随后，彭真市长代表北京市人民向贵宾们赠送了锦旗和礼品。
    北京市的文艺工作者接着为贵宾们表演了音乐、舞蹈和杂技节目。演出结束的时候，贵宾们走下主席台，立刻被人群包围起来，会场内齐声向他们欢呼“和平”、“友谊”。梭发那·富马亲王微笑着双手合十表示答谢，卡代·敦·萨索里特副首相也频频挥手致意。贵宾们离开会场时，场内一万多只手臂挥动，一片掌声和欢呼声。
    在大会结束以后，梭发那·富马首相和部分团员去游览了天坛、先农坛。今天上午，贵宾们参观了北京著名寺院——雍和宫和广济寺。（附图片）
