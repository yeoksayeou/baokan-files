Title: Afghan Foreign Minister's Statement: Egypt's Actions Comply with International Law
Author:
Date: 1956-08-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, July 7th: Afghan Foreign Minister Naim Khan stated to the Eastern News Agency on August 2nd that the Egyptian government's nationalization of the Suez Canal was a legitimate action of a sovereign state exercising its sovereignty. This right is in accordance with international law and should not be opposed in any way.

Naim Khan said that major powers should carefully consider the important issues of small countries and should not resort to force or threats.

### 阿富汗外交大臣声明  埃及的行动符合国际法

1956-08-08
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗外交大臣声明
    埃及的行动符合国际法
    据新华社7日讯　喀布尔消息：阿富汗外交大臣纳伊姆汗八月二日向东方通讯社发表的声明说，埃及政府把苏彝士运河收归国有是主权国家行使主权的合法行动，这种权利符合国际法，不应当在任何方面遭到反对。
    纳伊姆汗说，如今大国对小国在重要的问题上应当慎重考虑，不应当诉诸武力或威胁。
