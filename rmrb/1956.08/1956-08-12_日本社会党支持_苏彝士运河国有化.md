Title: Japanese Socialist Party Supports Suez Canal Nationalization
Author:
Date: 1956-08-12
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th, Tokyo dispatch: The Japanese Socialist Party issued a statement today, fully supporting the Egyptian government's nationalization of the Suez Canal. The statement said that this is a legitimate action by Egypt to exercise its sovereignty to resist colonialism.
    The Japanese Socialist Party also expressed opposition to military action taken by certain Western countries against Egypt, and opposed "international co-management" of the Suez Canal because "this is a violation of Egyptian sovereignty."
    The Socialist Party hopes that countries participating in the London conference will respect Egyptian sovereignty, and it also hopes that Egypt and relevant foreign governments will reach an understanding as soon as possible to ensure continued freedom of navigation.

### 日本社会党支持  苏彝士运河国有化

1956-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

    日本社会党支持
    苏彝士运河国有化
    新华社11日讯　东京消息：日本社会党今天发表声明，完全支持埃及政府把苏彝士运河收归国有。声明说，这是埃及合法地行使它的主权以抵抗殖民主义的行动。
    日本社会党还表示反对某些西方国家对埃及采取军事行动，并且反对“国际共管”苏彝士运河，因为“这是侵犯埃及的主权”。
    社会党希望参加伦敦会议的国家尊重埃及的主权，它还表示希望埃及和有关外国政府在保证继续自由通航方面早日达成谅解。
