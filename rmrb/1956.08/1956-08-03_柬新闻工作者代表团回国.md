Title: Cambodian Journalists Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-08-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 2nd: The Cambodian journalists delegation left Hanoi by plane this morning to return home.

Before boarding the plane, the head of the delegation, Sang Nian, bid farewell to the Vietnamese journalists, saying that the people of Vietnam and Cambodia are friends based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and that is why they can have such friendship. We will strive for the happiness of the people of both countries.

### 柬新闻工作者代表团回国

1956-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    柬新闻工作者代表团回国
    据新华社河内2日电　柬埔寨新闻工作者代表团已经在今天早晨乘飞机离开河内回国。
    代表团团长桑·年在上飞机之前向越南的新闻工作者告别说，越柬两国人民是以五项原则为基础的和平共处的朋友，所以能有这样的友谊。我们将为两国人民的幸福而努力奋斗。
