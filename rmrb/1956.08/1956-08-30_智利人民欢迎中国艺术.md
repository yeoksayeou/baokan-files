Title: Chilean People Welcome Chinese Art
Author:
Date: 1956-08-30
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, August 29th: The Chinese Art Troupe concluded its performances in Santiago, Chile, on August 26th. A total of nine performances were given, and every show was sold out.

　　The art troupe held a farewell reception on the evening of the 27th, with over two hundred people from Chilean social and cultural circles invited to attend. The guests warmly congratulated the art troupe on the success of their performances, and said that the interest these performances aroused in Chile was rarely seen.

### 智利人民欢迎中国艺术

1956-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

    智利人民欢迎中国艺术
    新华社29日讯　圣地亚哥消息：中国艺术团8月26日结束了在智利圣地亚哥的演出。演出一共有九场，场场都满座。
    艺术团在27日晚上举行了临别招待会，应邀出席的有智利社会和文化界人士两百多人。客人们热烈祝贺艺术团演出的成就，并且说这些演出在智利引起的兴趣是很少见的。
