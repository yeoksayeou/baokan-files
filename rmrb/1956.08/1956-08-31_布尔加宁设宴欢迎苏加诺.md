Title: Bulganin Hosts Banquet in Honor of Sukarno

Author:

Date: 1956-08-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 30th: TASS reports that Soviet Premier Bulganin hosted a banquet today in the Grand Kremlin to welcome Indonesian President Sukarno, who is visiting the Soviet Union at his invitation.

Attending the banquet were: President Sukarno, Indonesian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Bala, First Deputy Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament Arifin, Second Deputy Speaker Katawenata, Director of the Presidential Office Pringgodido, Governor of South Sumatra Danuamozo, and other members of the President's entourage.

The Soviet side was represented by: Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, Suslov, Khrushchev, Brezhnev, Shepilov, Aristov, Belyaev, Pospelov, and others.

Bulganin and President Sukarno exchanged speeches at the banquet.

The banquet took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, August 30th: On August 29th, Indonesian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Bala held a grand reception for President Sukarno's visit to the Soviet Union. All members of President Sukarno's entourage attended the reception. Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Voroshilov and other Soviet state leaders attended the reception.

### 布尔加宁设宴欢迎苏加诺

1956-08-31
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁设宴欢迎苏加诺
    新华社30日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁今天在大克里姆林宫设宴欢迎应邀到苏联访问的印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺。
    出席宴会的有：苏加诺总统、印度尼西亚驻苏联大使巴拉、印度尼西亚国会第一副议长阿里芬、第二副议长卡塔韦纳塔、总统办公厅主任普林果迪多、南苏门答腊省省长达努阿莫佐和总统的其他随行人员。
    苏联方面出席作陪的有：伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、勃列日涅夫、谢皮洛夫、阿里斯托夫、别利亚耶夫、波斯伯洛夫等人。
    布尔加宁和苏加诺总统在宴会上互相致词。
    宴会是在热情和友好的气氛中进行的。
    据新华社30日讯　8月29日，印度尼西亚驻苏联大使巴拉为苏加诺总统访苏举行盛大招待会。苏加诺总统的全体随行人员出席了这次招待会。苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和其他苏联国家领导人出席了这次招待会。
