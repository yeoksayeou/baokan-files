Title: Auto Factory Receives Batch of Korean Intern Workers
Author:
Date: 1956-08-20
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency report: The First Automobile Works recently received eighty-nine intern workers from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
    These workers have been assigned to fourteen production workshops within the First Automobile Works to participate in twenty-five types of automotive manufacturing specialties. They are scheduled to intern here for one year.
    These intern workers are all orphans who lost their parents during the American imperialist war of aggression against Korea. Before coming to the First Automobile Works for their internship, they had studied at Chinese schools until graduating from junior high school.

### 汽车厂接待一批朝鲜实习工人

1956-08-20
第2版()
专栏：

    汽车厂接待一批朝鲜实习工人
    据新华社讯　第一汽车制造厂最近接待了八十九名朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的实习工人。
    这批工人已经分别分配到第一汽车制造厂的十四个生产车间内进行着二十五种汽车制造专业的实习。他们预定要在这里实习一年。
    这批实习工人都是在美国帝国主义侵朝战争期间失去了父母的孤儿。他们在来到第一汽车制造厂实习前，曾在中国的学校内念到了初中毕业。
