Title: Soong Ching-ling Arrives in Rangoon En Route to China
Author:
Date: 1956-08-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 24th: Soong Ching-ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the NationalPeople's Congress of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Rangoon at 5:00 PM on August 23rd. She was passing through here on her way back to China after concluding her visit to Indonesia.

Those who went to the airport to welcome her included Burmese Deputy Prime Minister Zaw Khun Cho, Daw Mya Yee, wife of former Prime Minister U Nu, and Daw Khin Kyi, wife of Aung San.

Yao Zhongming, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and Suwanto, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in Burma, were also at the airport to welcome her.

### 宋庆龄回国途中抵达仰光

1956-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄回国途中抵达仰光
    新华社仰光24日电　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄在八月二十三日下午五时到达仰光。她是在结束了对印度尼西亚的访问以后，在回国途中经过这里的。
    前往机场欢迎的有缅甸副总理藻昆卓、前总理吴努的夫人都妙意、昂山夫人都庆枝等。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和印度尼西亚驻缅甸大使馆临时代办苏旺多也都在机场欢迎。
