Title: Cairo Radio Reaffirms: Absolutely No Foreign Management of the Suez Canal Allowed. Egyptian Cabinet Drafting Response to Dulles Plan

Author:

Date: 1956-08-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th Dispatch from Cairo: Cairo Radio announced again on the evening of August 26th that Egypt will absolutely not permit foreign management of the Suez Canal.

It said that, on the other hand, Egypt "is willing to provide any guarantees people want for the free passage through this canal."

Commenting on the Western countries' appointment of a so-called "Five-Nation Committee" to submit the Dulles Plan to Egypt, a Cairo Radio commentator said, "How can one believe in the sincerity of this proposal when France and Britain are frantically preparing to attack Egypt and impose their will on Egypt?"

The radio commentator concluded, "All Arab nations, all African and Asian nations, and most Western peoples favor a peaceful resolution to this issue. Imperialists are warned that a conspiratorial attack on Egypt will cost them dearly, perhaps even making it impossible for them to maintain a foothold in the Middle East."

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th Dispatch from Cairo: The Egyptian Cabinet held a special meeting on the afternoon of August 26th to discuss the situation after the London Conference.

The meeting, presided over by President Abdel Nasser, was convened after Sabri, Director of the Political Bureau of the Presidential Palace, returned to Cairo from London.

It is reported that the meeting drafted a response to the Dulles Plan submitted to Egypt by the "Five-Nation Suez Canal Committee."

An Associated Press correspondent in Cairo reported on August 25th that Egyptian sources insist that the Egyptian government cannot and will not compromise on the issues of Egyptian sovereignty and the right to control the Suez Canal.

### 开罗电台再次宣布  决不容许外国人管理苏彝士运河  埃及内阁在草拟对杜勒斯计划的答复

1956-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

    开罗电台再次宣布
    决不容许外国人管理苏彝士运河
    埃及内阁在草拟对杜勒斯计划的答复
    新华社27日讯　开罗消息：开罗电台在8月26日晚上的广播中再次宣布，埃及决不容许外国人管理苏彝士运河。
    它说，另一方面，埃及“愿意提供人们所希望的自由通过这条运河的任何保证”。
    开罗电台评论员在评论西方国家指派所谓“五国委员会”把杜勒斯计划提交埃及的这个做法时说：“当法国和英国正在疯狂地进行准备以便进攻埃及并把它们的意志强加于埃及的时候，人们怎么能够相信这个建议的诚意呢？”
    电台评论员最后说：“一切阿拉伯国家、一切非洲和亚洲的国家的人民和大多数的西方人民都赞成和平解决这个问题。警告帝国主义者们，对于埃及进行一次阴谋的攻击将使它们付出很大的代价，也许甚至使他们在中东站不住脚。”
    新华社27日讯　开罗消息：埃及内阁8月26日下午举行了特别会议，讨论伦敦会议以后的局势。
    会议是在总统府政治局局长萨布里从伦敦回到开罗以后召开的，由总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔主持。
    据报道，会议起草了对“五国苏彝士运河委员会”提交埃及的杜勒斯计划的答复。
    美联社驻开罗记者8月25日说，埃及人士坚持认为，埃及政府不可能也不会在埃及主权和控制苏彝士运河的权利问题上妥协。
