Title: Xinjiang Ethnic Groups Form Delegation to Visit Kazakh People in Qinghai
Author:
Date: 1956-08-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Xining, August 18th. A delegation of 31 people from various ethnic groups in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, including Kazakh, Uyghur, Han, Kyrgyz, and Xibe, departed from Xining yesterday to visit the Arltun Quq Kazakh Autonomous Region and the Kazakh people in Qinghai.

The delegation arrived in Xining from Lanzhou on August 14th. While in Gansu, they visited the Kazakh people in the Aksay Kazakh Autonomous County. This visit to the Arltun Quq Kazakh Autonomous Region is to express the concern of the people of all ethnic groups in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region for the Kazakh people living there and to strengthen the ties between the people of the two regions. The delegation brought silk, tea, sugar, cloth, and books and periodicals in Kazakh and Uyghur languages, which they plan to present to the Kazakh people there. The accompanying song and dance troupe and film projection team will also perform ethnic songs and dances and screen films such as the Kazakh-language "Hassen and Gamila."

### 新疆各民族组成访问团  访问住在青海的哈萨克族人民

1956-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

    新疆各民族组成访问团
    访问住在青海的哈萨克族人民
    新华社西宁18日电　由哈萨克、维吾尔、汉、柯尔克孜、锡伯等民族组成的新疆维吾尔自治区访问团一行三十一人，昨天从西宁出发前往阿尔顿曲克哈萨克族自治区，访问青海哈萨克族人民。
    访问团是在八月十四日从兰州到达西宁的。在甘肃时曾经访问了阿克塞哈萨克族自治县的哈萨克族人民。这次去阿尔顿曲克哈萨克族自治区访问，是为了表达新疆维吾尔自治区各族人民对居住在那里的哈萨克族人民的关怀，增强两地人民之间的联系。访问团携带了丝绸、茶、糖、布匹等物品和哈萨克、维吾尔文字的书刊，准备赠送给那里的哈萨克族人民。随访问团同行的文工队和电影放映队并将在那里演出民族歌舞和哈萨克语“哈森与加米拉”等影片。
