Title: Soong Ching-ling Sends Telegram of Thanks to Indonesian Leaders
Author:
Date: 1956-08-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, August 25th: Soong Ching-ling telegraphed Indonesian President Sukarno on the afternoon of August 24th, expressing warm and deep gratitude for the hospitality she received and the various arrangements made for her during her visit to Indonesia. The following is the full text of her telegram:

To His Excellency Dr. Sukarno, President of Jakarta:

I arrived safely in Yangon at 6:15 PM on August 23rd. Please allow me to express my warm and deep gratitude for Your Excellency's friendly hospitality and thoughtful arrangements. These hospitality and arrangements enabled me not only to attend the 11th National Day celebrations of the Republic of Indonesia, but also to visit many beautiful cities and meet many prominent people from all walks of life.

The enthusiasm and determination shown by the hardworking and brave Indonesian people in their cause of building a new Indonesia have deeply impressed me. This makes me feel even more deeply the great value of this unforgettable visit and our sincere friendship. I will convey my pleasant impressions and the friendly feelings of the Indonesian people to the broad masses of the Chinese people, so that friendship between the Chinese and Indonesian people will continue to consolidate and develop day by day.

Soong Ching-ling
In Yangon
August 24, 1956

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, August 25th: Soong Ching-ling telegraphed Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo in Yangon on the afternoon of August 24th, expressing her gratitude for the warm reception from the Indonesian government. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo of Jakarta:

I express my heartfelt gratitude for Your Excellency's warm reception and kind assistance, which made this visit unforgettable for me. Your beautiful country and your enthusiastic people have left me with a deep impression during my visit. Through my contact with prominent people from all walks of life, mutual understanding has been further increased, and I am confident that this situation will further strengthen the friendly exchanges and cultural and economic exchanges between our two peoples. I would like to wish the Republic of Indonesia new achievements in various constructions, and wish that the friendship between the Chinese and Indonesian people will grow day by day.

Soong Ching-ling
August 24, 1956, in Yangon

### 宋庆龄电谢印度尼西亚领导人

1956-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄电谢印度尼西亚领导人
    新华社仰光25日电　宋庆龄在八月二十四日下午打电报给印度尼西亚总统苏加诺，对她在访问印度尼西亚期间所受到的款待和为她作的各种安排表示热烈而深切的感谢。下面是她的电报的全文：雅加达总统苏加诺博士阁下：
    我在八月二十三日下午六时十五分平安到达仰光。请允许我对阁下的友好款待和周到的安排表示热烈而深切的感谢。这些款待和安排使我能够不仅参加了印度尼西亚共和国第十一届国庆典礼，并且还访问了许多美丽的城市和会见了各界许多著名人士。
    勤劳而勇敢的印度尼西亚人民在他们建设新的印度尼西亚的事业中所表现的热情和决心给了我深刻的印象。这使我更深切地感到这次难忘的访问和我们的真诚友谊的巨大价值。我将把我的愉快的印象和印度尼西亚人民的友好感情传达给广大的中国人民群众，以便中国人民和印度尼西亚人民间的友谊一天天地继续巩固和发展。
            宋庆龄　于仰光
                1956年8月24日
    新华社仰光25日电　宋庆龄八月二十四日下午在仰光打电报给印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约，对印度尼西亚政府的热烈接待表示感谢。电文如下：雅加达沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理阁下：
    我对阁下的热烈接待和善意协助表示衷心的感谢，这种接待和协助使得我这次访问成为难以忘怀的。你们美丽的国家和你们热情的人民已经使得我在访问中获得深刻的印象。通过我同各界著名人士的接触，已经进一步增加了相互的了解，我确信，这种情况将进一步加强我们两国人民之间的友好来往以及文化和经济的交流。我谨祝印度尼西亚共和国在各种建设方面获得新的成就，谨祝中国人民和印度尼西亚人民之间的友谊与日俱增。
                      宋庆龄
          1956年8月24日于仰光
