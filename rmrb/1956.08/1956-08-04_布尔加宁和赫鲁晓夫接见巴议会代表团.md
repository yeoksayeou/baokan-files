Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Receive Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-08-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd—Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the Pakistani National Assembly delegation on August 2nd, and held friendly and frank talks with them.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫接见巴议会代表团

1956-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫接见巴议会代表团
    据新华社3日讯　苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏共中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫八月二日接见了巴基斯坦国民议会代表团，并且同他们进行了友好和坦率的谈话。
