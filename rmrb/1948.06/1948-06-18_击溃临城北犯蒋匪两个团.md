Title: Defeat of Two Regiments of Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Invading North from Lincheng
Author:
Date: 1948-06-18
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 16th: The People's Liberation Army on the Jinpu Front routed two regiments of the Chiang army's 37th Brigade that invaded north from Lincheng on the 6th, killing, wounding, and capturing 481 enemies. On the evening of the 5th, two regiments of the 37th Brigade of the 77th Division of the enemy's Reorganized 33rd Army invaded north from Lincheng and advanced to Wanghezhuang and Yangzhuang, about 30 miles northeast of Lincheng, on the 6th. A unit of our army immediately launched a courageous attack. After five hours of fierce fighting, the enemy was completely routed by our army. A total of 252 enemies were captured, 229 were killed or wounded, and three mortars and sixty guns, twenty-one light and heavy machine guns, more than 130 rifles, and more than 30,000 rounds of ammunition were seized. The remaining enemies fled in panic. Another unit of our army raided a regiment of the enemy's Reorganized 73rd Division near Yanzhuang, near Longshan Station, east of Jinan, on the night of the 5th, capturing 50-odd guns each and seizing six light machine guns.

### 击溃临城北犯蒋匪两个团

1948-06-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　击溃临城北犯蒋匪两个团
    【新华社华东前线十六日电】津浦前线解放军，六日击溃由临城北犯之蒋军三十七旅两个团，毙伤俘敌四百八十一人。五日晚，敌整三十三军整七十七师三十七旅的两个团，由临城北犯，六日进至临城东北三十里之望河庄、羊庄一带，我军一部当即奋勇出击，经五小时激战，敌全部被我击溃，计俘敌二百五十二人，毙伤二百二十九人，缴获迫击炮六零炮三门，轻重机枪廿一挺，步枪一百三十多支，子弹三万余发，残敌狼狈逃窜。另部我军，于五日夜袭济南以东龙山车站附近颜庄一带之敌整七十三师之一个团，俘获敌人枪各五十余，缴轻机枪六挺。
