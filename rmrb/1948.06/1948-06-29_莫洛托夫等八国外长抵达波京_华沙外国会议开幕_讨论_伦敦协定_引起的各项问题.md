Title: Molotov and Eight Other Foreign Ministers Arrive in Warsaw; Warsaw Foreign Ministers' Conference Opens; Discussions on Various Issues Arising from the "London Agreements"
Author:
Date: 1948-06-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, June 26th: TASS reports: Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov arrived in the Polish capital on the 23rd. Hungarian Foreign Minister Molnar, Yugoslav Foreign Minister Simić, Czechoslovak Foreign Minister Clementis, Bulgarian Foreign Minister Kolarov, Romanian Foreign Minister Pauker, and Albanian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Hoxha also arrived in Warsaw one after another on the same day. Polish Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz, Foreign Minister Modzelewski, and personnel from various embassies were at the airport to welcome them.
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, June 27th: TASS reports: The foreign ministers of the Soviet Union, Albania, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Yugoslavia, Poland, Romania, and Hungary convened a conference in the Polish capital, Warsaw, on June 23rd. This conference was initiated by the Soviet and Polish governments, and its task is to discuss the various issues arising from the unilateral decisions made on the German question at the London Six-Power Conference which concluded in early June. Participating in this conference were the foreign ministers of eight countries: Molotov (Soviet Union), Hoxha (Albania), Kolarov (Bulgaria), Clementis (Czechoslovakia), Simić (Yugoslavia), Modzelewski (Poland), Pauker (Romania), and Molnar (Hungary). The Polish Foreign Minister delivered the opening speech at the meeting. The conference elected the Polish Foreign Minister as the chairman of the first session.

### 莫洛托夫等八国外长抵达波京　华沙外国会议开幕  讨论“伦敦协定”引起的各项问题

1948-06-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　莫洛托夫等八国外长抵达波京
  　华沙外国会议开幕
    讨论“伦敦协定”引起的各项问题
    【新华社陕北二十六日电】塔斯社报导：苏联外长莫洛托夫于二十三日抵达波兰首都。匈牙利外长莫纳尔，南斯拉夫外长西米区，捷克外长克里门蒂斯，保加利亚外长科拉罗夫，罗马尼亚外长波克，阿尔巴尼亚总理兼外长霍查，亦均于同日先后抵达华沙。波总理西伦凯维兹，外长摩捷列夫斯基及各国大使馆人员均曾至机场欢迎。
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】塔斯社报导：苏联、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、捷克、南斯拉夫、波兰、罗马尼亚及匈牙利外长，于六月二十三日在波兰首都华沙召开会议。此一会议系由苏联及波兰政府发起，其任务为讨论六月初闭幕的伦敦六国会议对德国问题片面决定所引起的各项问题。参加此一会议者为八国外长莫洛托夫（苏）、霍查（阿）、科拉罗夫（保）、克里门蒂斯（捷）、西米区（南）、摩捷列夫斯基（波）、波克（罗）及莫纳尔（匈）。波外长在会上首致开幕词。大会选举波外长为第一次会议的主席。
