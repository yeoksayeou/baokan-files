Title: Battle of Longshan in Zou County: 3,600 Bandits Annihilated
Author:
Date: 1948-06-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 20th. The East China People's Liberation Army Shandong Corps Headquarters has recently announced the results of the capture of Zou County on the 15th, and the comprehensive results of the operations in the Longshan area on the outskirts of Jinan from the 12th to the 15th as follows: (1) In the Battle of Zou County, the entire defending enemy Shandong Provincial Security Third Brigade Second Regiment, and the Zou County "People's Self-Defense Corps" and other units were completely annihilated, totaling 2,309 men, including 1,959 captured alive, and the following were seized: four rocket launchers and small cannons, 31 heavy and light machine guns, 15 submachine guns, 1,913 rifles and other guns, and more than 70,000 rounds of ammunition of various types. (2) In the Battle of Longshan area, parts of the Chiang Kai-shek army's 73rd Division and the 213th Brigade of the 3rd Division were killed, wounded, or captured, totaling 1,350 men. 23 light and heavy machine guns, 5 submachine guns, more than 300 rifles and other guns, and 30,000 rounds of ammunition were seized, and one armored vehicle was destroyed.

### 邹县龙山之役  歼匪三千六百

1948-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　邹县龙山之役
    歼匪三千六百
    【新华社华东前线二十日电】华东解放军山东兵团司令部，顷公布十五日攻克邹县战果，及十二日至十五日济南外围龙山地区作战之综合战果如下：（一）邹县之役，全歼守敌山东省保安第三旅第二团，及邹县“人民自卫总队”等部共二千三百零九名，内生俘一千九百五十九名，缴获火箭筒及小炮四门，经重机枪三十一挺，冲锋枪十五枝，长短枪一千九百一十三枝，各种子弹七万余发。（二）龙山地区之战，毙伤俘蒋军整七三师、整三师之二一三旅等各一部，共一千三百五十名，缴获轻重机枪二十三挺，冲锋枪五枝，长短枪三百余枝，子弹三万发，击毁装甲车一辆。
