Title: Lushan Village Cadres Lead Textile Production
Author: Liu Haigen
Date: 1948-06-03
Page: 1

　　Lushan Village Cadres Lead Textile Production
    [Anyang News] Lushan Village cadres are actively organizing the masses of the whole village to engage in production. For the first time, village head Yang Heshan helped the women's textile group, and in half a month they spun a total of 110 jin of cotton, producing 80 zhang and 1 chi of cloth. After deducting the cost and cotton fluffing wages, the net profit was more than 120,000 yuan, and each person could receive a dividend of 4,000 yuan. Now the whole village has established a production committee, which is divided into four sections: agriculture, textiles, transportation, and carrying, to lead the work separately.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Liu Haigen)

### 鹿山村干领导纺织
刘海根
1948-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　鹿山村干领导纺织
    【安阳消息】鹿山村干部积极组织全村群众进行生产，第一次由村长杨合山帮助妇女纺织组，半个月共纺花一百一十斤，出布八十丈零一尺。除还本和弹花工资外，净赚大洋十二万余元，每人可分红四千元。现在全村成立起生产委员会，内分农业、纺织、运输、担挑等四股，分头领导。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（刘海根）
