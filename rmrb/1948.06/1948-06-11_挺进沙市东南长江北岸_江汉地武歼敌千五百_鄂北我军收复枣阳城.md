Title: Advancing to the Southeast of Shashi and the North Bank of the Yangtze River: Jianghan Local Armed Forces Annihilate 1,500 Enemies; Our Army in Northern Hubei Recovers Zaoyang City
Author:
Date: 1948-06-11
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 8th. A unit of the Jianghan Military Region's local armed forces annihilated an entire enemy regiment and another battalion, totaling over 1,500 enemy troops killed, wounded, or captured, in the Pujiguan area, southeast of Shashi and on the north bank of the Yangtze River. On the 26th of last month, we began an attack on the defending enemy of the 28th Division's rear guard brigade – the 192nd Brigade's 1st Regiment (minus one battalion) – and by the afternoon of the 30th, we had completely annihilated the enemy. When our regiment annihilated the enemy defending Pujiguan, the 2nd and 3rd Regiments of the brigade, each with one battalion led by the 3rd Regiment Commander, went to reinforce from Dama River in Jianli. They were intercepted by us in the southeast of Pujiguan. After two hours of fierce fighting, we completely annihilated the enemy. In total, we annihilated the 192nd Brigade's 1st Regiment (minus one battalion) and one battalion each from the 2nd and 3rd Regiments, capturing 1,217 officers and soldiers, including the 1st Regiment Commander Xu Chengxuan, the 2nd Regiment Deputy Commander Huang Can, and the 3rd Battalion Commander Huang Guangfu. We killed or wounded over 300, including the 2nd Battalion Commander of the 2nd Regiment, Xu Shiyue. We seized 6 mortars, 9 sixty-millimeter guns, 8 grenade launchers, 12 heavy machine guns, 54 light machine guns, over 570 rifles and carbines, 37 pistols, 270 rounds of various artillery shells, over 60,700 rounds of ammunition, over 900 bombs, and other military supplies.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 8th. A unit of the Jianghan Military Region's Liberation Army recovered Zaoyang City on the 1st of this month. The defending enemy abandoned the city and fled.

### 挺进沙市东南长江北岸　江汉地武歼敌千五百　鄂北我军收复枣阳城

1948-06-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　挺进沙市东南长江北岸
  　江汉地武歼敌千五百
  　鄂北我军收复枣阳城
    【新华社中原八日电】我江汉某分区地方武装一部，于沙市东南长江北岸之普济观地区，歼敌一个整团又一个营，共毙伤俘敌一千五百余名。上月二十六日，我开始向普济观守敌二八师的后调旅——一九二旅第一团（缺一个营）发动攻击，至三十日下午，将该敌全部歼灭。当我团歼普济观守敌时，该旅二团、三团各以一个营由三团团长率领，自监利大马河前往增援，中途被我阻击于普济观东南地区，经两小时激战，全歼该敌。以上共歼敌一九二旅一团（缺一个营）及二团、三团各一个营，俘敌一团团长徐承轩、二团副团长黄灿、三营营长黄光复等以下官兵一千二百十七名，毙伤二团二营营长徐师岳以下三百余名。缴获迫击炮六门、六○炮九门、掷弹筒八个、重机枪十二挺、轻机枪五十四挺、步马枪五百七十余支、手枪三十七支、各种炮弹二百七十发、子弹六万零七百余发、炸弹九百余个、及其他军用物资一部。
    【新华社中原前线八日电】江汉军区解放军一部，本月一日收复枣阳城，守敌弃城逃窜。
