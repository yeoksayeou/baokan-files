Title: CCP Central Committee Sends Congratulations on the Great Victory in the Southern Front
Author:
Date: 1948-06-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 16th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China recently sent a telegram to the Central Plains and East China People's Liberation Army, congratulating them on the great victory in the Southern Front. The telegram is as follows:

To Comrades Liu Bocheng, Deng Xiaoping, Chen Yi, Su Yu, Li Xiannian, Deng Zihui, and all comrades of the Central Plains and East China People's Liberation Army:

Congratulations on your recent victories in annihilating the enemy in western and eastern Wan and Jianghan for three consecutive times, and liberating vast areas of Henan, Hubei, and Shaanxi. We hope that you will continue to work hard and fight to eliminate the Chiang enemy and liberate all the people of the Central Plains.

　　　Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, June 12, 1948

### 中共中央委员会　电贺南线大捷

1948-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央委员会
  　电贺南线大捷
    【新华社陕北十六日电】中共中央顷致电中原及华东解放军，祝贺南线大捷。电文如下：
    刘伯承、邓小平、陈毅、粟裕、李先念、邓子恢诸同志，及中原和华东人民解放军全体同志们：
    庆祝你们最近在宛西、宛东和江汉连续三次歼敌，并解放豫鄂陕广大地区的胜利。尚望继续努力，为消灭蒋敌，解放全中原人民而战。
  　　　中共中央委员会　　一九四八年六月十二日
