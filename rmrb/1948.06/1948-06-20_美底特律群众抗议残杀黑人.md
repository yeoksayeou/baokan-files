Title: US Detroit Residents Protest the Killing of Black People
Author:
Date: 1948-06-20
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th. TASS, 12th, quoted the U.S. Worker’s Daily, Detroit, as reporting: 5,000 people here participated in the funeral of fifteen-year-old black youth, Molai. Molai was murdered by the police on June 4th. After the funeral, 4,000 people held a protest demonstration, demanding the replacement of the city’s police chief. Another report: Black lawyer Hill has been elected as a member of the Richmond City Council, the capital of Virginia. Despite the reactionary forces launching a shameful racial discrimination campaign, Hill, supported by all black organizations and white progressive groups, was elected with 9,000 votes. This is the second black person in this century to be elected to the city council.

### 美底特律群众抗议残杀黑人

1948-06-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　美底特律群众抗议残杀黑人
    【新华社陕北十七日电】塔斯社十二日电引美国工人日报底特律消息称：此间五千群众参加十五岁黑人摩莱的出殡典礼。摩莱于六月四日为警察所残杀，丧礼举行以后，有四千群众举行抗议示威，要求撤换该市警察局长。又讯：黑人律师希尔已当选为弗吉尼亚州首府里契蒙市议会的议员。尽管反动派曾发动可耻的种族歧视运动，但为所有黑人团体及白人进步团体支持的希尔，仍以获得九千票而当选。此为该市在本世纪内被选入议会的第二个黑人。
