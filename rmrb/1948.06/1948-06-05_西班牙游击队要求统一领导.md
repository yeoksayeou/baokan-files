Title: Spanish Guerrillas Demand Unified Leadership
Author:
Date: 1948-06-05
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 1st. TASS, Paris: L'Humanité published on the 26th of last month that the Spanish United Guerrillas, active in the Levante and Aragon regions, recently issued a letter to anti-Franco figures. L'Humanité said: This declaration is extremely important. The declaration said: The guerrillas believe that the time is ripe for uniting millions of patriotic people to engage in the struggle for independence and democracy. The Spanish people demand the establishment of a Central Resistance Committee to lead the Spanish struggle. To this end, the guerrillas of the Levante and Aragon regions propose convening a representative conference of all Spanish resistance movements.

### 西班牙游击队要求统一领导

1948-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　西班牙游击队要求统一领导
    【新华社陕北一日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：人道报于上月二十六日载称：在列凡特及阿拉冈地区活动的西班牙联合游击队，最近发表一告反佛朗哥人士书。人道报说：这一宣言异常重要。该宣言说：游击队相信团结千千万万爱国志士，以从事争取独立与民主之斗争，这一事业的时机业已成熟，西班牙人民要求成立中央抵抗委员会，以领导西班牙的斗争。列凡特与阿拉冈地区游击队为此目的，特提议召开西班牙一切抵抗运动团体的代表会议。
