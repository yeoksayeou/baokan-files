Title:剿匪 in Henan, Shaanxi, and Hubei Regions Eliminates Over 500 Local Bandits and Chiang Remnants
Author:
Date: 1948-06-18
Page: 2
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Yu, Shaan, and E, 15th - The armed forces of the Yu, Shaan, and E Military Region, from May 20th to June 5th (a period of half a month), engaged in operations in the area west of Xixiakou County (newly established county) in western Henan, killing and wounding over 70 local bandits and Chiang remnants. They captured 189 enemy soldiers, seized 10 light machine guns, 217 rifles and short guns, and a portion of other military supplies.

Furthermore, the local armed forces of the 1st, 3rd, and 4th military sub-districts of Yu, Shaan, and E also achieved several victories in bandit suppression, killing, wounding, and capturing nearly 300 enemy soldiers. A breakdown of the achievements of each sub-district is as follows: (1) A unit of local armed forces of the 1st sub-district (formerly the 5th sub-district of the Taiyue region, encompassing the six counties of Luoyang, Mengjin, Xin'an, Mianchi, Yiyang, and Luoning), in coordination with the Shan (County) Mian (Chi) Brigade, surrounded the villages of Nanbeiyang in one day, killing nine local bandits and Chiang remnants, capturing 27 enemy soldiers including a battalion commander, and seizing one light machine gun and over ten rifles. On the same day, another unit of our local armed forces attacked the troops of local bandit Qin Shengfu who were invading Yangcun in Mianchi, capturing over 30 enemy soldiers alive, and seizing one light machine gun and over ten rifles. On the 3rd, a unit of our local armed forces ambushed Fanjiapotou, capturing Zhu Jingwu, the commander of the Third General Column of the Xi'an Pacification Headquarters' Bandit Suppression in Western Henan, along with Tang Zifu, a major staff officer of the Pacification Headquarters, Zhang Douyi, a battalion commander, and 92 other enemy soldiers. They killed and wounded 45 enemy soldiers and seized one light machine gun and 71 rifles and short guns. (2) A unit of local armed forces of the 3rd sub-district (encompassing the five counties of Songxian, Yiyang, and Yichuan), jointly attacked the bandit troops of Gao Maozhai on the 3rd, killing and wounding over 20 enemy soldiers, capturing 16 alive, and seizing 20 rifles. (3) The local armed forces of the 4th sub-district (formerly the 5th sub-district, encompassing the seven counties of Linru, Baofeng, Dengfeng, and Jiaxian) engaged in bandit suppression in the area of Wudingling in Dengfeng from May 29th to June 2nd, capturing Wang Yaodong, the chief of the Dengfeng puppet police station, and 42 others, and seizing two light machine guns and 43 rifles.

### 豫陕鄂各地区剿匪　歼灭土蒋五百余

1948-06-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　豫陕鄂各地区剿匪
  　歼灭土蒋五百余
    【新华社豫陕鄂十五日电】豫陕鄂军区武装一部，五月二十日至六月五日半月中，在豫西西峡口县（新设县）以西地区作战毙伤土蒋七十余名，俘敌一百八十九名，缴获轻机枪十挺，长短枪二百一十七支，及其他军用品一部。
    又豫陕鄂一、三、四各军分区地方武装，于剿匪中亦获得若干胜利，毙伤俘敌近三百名。兹分述各分区战果如下：（一）一分区（原太岳五分区，辖洛阳、孟津、新安、渑池、宜阳、洛宁等六县）地方武装一部，一日配合陕（县）渑（池）大队包围南北阳村，毙土蒋九名，俘大队长以下二十七名，缴轻机枪一挺，步枪十余支。同日我地武另一部，打击进犯渑池杨村的土蒋秦生富部、生俘三十余名，缴获轻机枪一挺，步枪十余支。三日我某部地方武装夹击樊家坡头，俘蒋匪西安绥署豫西剿匪第三总队长朱经武，及绥署少校参谋唐子馥，大队长张斗义等以下九十二名，毙伤敌四十五名，缴获轻机枪一挺，长短枪七十一支。（二）三分区（辖嵩县、伊阳、伊川等五县）地方武装一部，三日合击高茂斋匪部，毙伤二十余名，生俘十六名，缴获步枪二十支。（三）四分区（原五分区，辖临汝、宝丰、登封、郏县等七县）地方武装，五月二十九日至六月二日在登封五顶岭一带剿匪，俘登封伪警察局长王耀东以下四十二名，缴获轻机枪两挺，步枪四十三支。
