　　Important Correction
    The May 31st issue of the Jinchaji Daily contained two errors in the document "Decision on Some Issues in the Land Struggle" from 1933: (A) In Chapter 4 (Examples) of the section on reactionary rich peasants, the "four" in "owns 460 dan of land, self-cultivates 80 dan" is a mistake for "one" and should read "owns 160 dan of land." In the chapter on public utilities land, the "经" in "(Explanation) ...have all 须 been according to the degree of need..." is a mistake for "须".

### 重要更正

1948-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　重要更正
    晋察冀日报五月三十一日载一九三三年两个文件中“关于土地斗争中一些问题的决定”这一文中发现有两个错字：（甲）反动富农一章第四节（举例）中“有田四百六十担，自耕八十担”的“四”字系“一”字之误，应为“有田一百六十担”。公共事业田一章中（说明）内“均经按照需要程度”的“经”字系“须”字之误。
