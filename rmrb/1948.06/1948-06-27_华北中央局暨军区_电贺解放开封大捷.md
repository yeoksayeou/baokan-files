Title: North China Central Bureau and Military Region Congratulate on the Great Victory of the Liberation of Kaifeng
Author:
Date: 1948-06-27
Page: 1
　　
　　North China Central Bureau and Military Region
  　Telegraphic Congratulation on the Great Victory of the Liberation of Kaifeng
    [Special Dispatch from This Newspaper] The North China Central Bureau of the Communist Party of China and the Headquarters and Political Department of the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent a special telegram to congratulate the great victory of the liberation of Kaifeng. The original telegram is as follows: Comrades Liu, Deng, Chen, Li, Deng, Song, Zhang, Chen, Tang, and all the heroic commanders and fighters on the Kaifeng front:
    We are delighted to hear of your great victory in liberating Kaifeng, the capital of Henan Province, and annihilating more than 30,000 enemy troops. We hereby send a telegram to congratulate you. We will learn from your invincible spirit of fighting for the liberation of the people of Central Plains, fight for the liberation of the people of North China, and strive to build the North China Liberated Area to support the front line of the People's Liberation War.
  　　　　　　The North China Central Bureau of the CPC
  　　　　　　Headquarters and Political Department of the North China Military Region
    June 24th

### 华北中央局暨军区　电贺解放开封大捷

1948-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北中央局暨军区
  　电贺解放开封大捷
    【本报特讯】中共华北中央局暨中国人民解放军华北军区司令部、政治部特电致贺解放开封的大胜利，原电如下：刘、邓、陈、栗、李、邓、宋、张、陈、唐诸同志并转开封前线全体英勇指战员同志们：
    欣闻你们解放河南省会开封，全歼守敌三万余人的大捷，特电庆贺。我们将学习你们为解放全中原人民而奋战的无敌精神，为解放全华北人民而战，并努力建设华北解放区，支援人民解放战争的前线。
  　　　　　　中共华北中央局
  　　　　　　华北军区司令、政治部
    六月二十四日
