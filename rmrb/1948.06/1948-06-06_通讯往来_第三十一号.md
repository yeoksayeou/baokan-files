Author:
Date: 1948-06-06
Page: 1
Column: Correspondence

　　Correspondence No. 31
    I.  With the current busy farming season, please be responsible for checking and reporting whether there are any instances of abuse of civilian labor affecting the wheat harvest and seedling weeding in various places.
    II. How is production being carried out in the various democratic pilot villages? Please report on this, comrades of the various work teams.
    III. After the correction of deviations, what are the new problems in the production situation in various villages? What are the opinions and demands of various classes?

### 通讯往来　第三十一号

1948-06-06
第1版()
专栏：通讯往来

　　通讯往来　第三十一号
    一、目前农忙，各地有无滥用民力影响麦收锄苗的事情，请负责检查报导。
    二、各民主填补村的生产是如何进行的，请各工作组同志报导。
    三、纠偏以后各村生产情形，有什么新的问题？各阶层有什么意见和要求？
