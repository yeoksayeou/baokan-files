Title: 100,000 Overseas Vietnamese in Siam to Hold Anti-Nguyen Van Xuan Congress; Reduction of Soviet Troops Stationed in North Korea
Author:
Date: 1948-06-20
Page: 2
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 15th: Agence France-Presse, Bangkok dispatch: The head of the Vietnamese news agency in Bangkok, Siam, declared on the 10th that the 600,000 Overseas Vietnamese residing in Siam will never recognize the Nguyen Van Xuan puppet government manufactured by France in Vietnam. He also claimed that Nguyen Van Xuan is French and cannot speak Vietnamese. Many Vietnamese residing in Bangkok will hold an anti-Nguyen Van Xuan Congress on the 13th at the Annam Association in Bangkok.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 15th: TASS, Pyongyang, 10th: The Soviet military command in North Korea recently issued a statement saying that, in accordance with the orders of the Soviet military command, the number of Soviet troops stationed in North Korea has been reduced. Lieutenant General Kolotkov, commander of the Soviet Army in North Korea, and his command have returned to Soviet territory, and the Soviet troops remaining in North Korea are now commanded by Major General Makarov.

### 暹境越侨十万人　将开反阮文春大会　北鲜苏联驻兵减少

1948-06-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　暹境越侨十万人
  　将开反阮文春大会
  　北鲜苏联驻兵减少
    【新华社陕北十五日电】法新社曼谷讯：驻暹罗曼谷的越南通讯社负责人于十日宣称：侨居暹罗六十万越人，决不会承认法国在越南所制造的阮文春傀儡政府。并称，阮文春是法国人，他不会讲越南话。许多侨居曼谷之越人将于十三日在曼谷安南会馆内举行反阮文春大会。
    【新华社陕北十五日电】塔斯社平壤十日电：北鲜苏军司令部顷发表公告称：依照苏联军事统帅部的命令，驻在北鲜的苏军兵力已在减少。北鲜苏军司令柯罗特科夫中将及其司令部已返回苏联领土，停留在北鲜的苏军改由马库罗夫少将统率。
