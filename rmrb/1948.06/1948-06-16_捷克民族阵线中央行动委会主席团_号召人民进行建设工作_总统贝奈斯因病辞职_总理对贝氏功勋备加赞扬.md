Title: Presidium of the Central Action Committee of the Czechoslovak National Front Calls on People to Engage in Construction Work; President Beneš Resigns Due to Illness; Premier Praises Beneš's Meritorious Achievements

Author:
Date: 1948-06-16
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 10th: TASS reports: The Presidium of the Central Action Committee of the Czechoslovak National Front, on the occasion of the victorious conclusion of this general election and the imminent opening of the new parliament, issued a declaration expressing its gratitude to the people who voted for the National Front and calling on the people of the whole country to "engage in construction work happily for the benefit of the republic, to strengthen the republic's strength, and to increase the people's happiness and joy." The declaration pointed out: Never in the history of our republic has any parliament been so unanimously supported as this one. Our republic's democratic development towards socialism has been guaranteed. It is based on the complete unanimity of the vast majority of our people, the close cooperation of all progressive people, and the support of workers, peasants, intellectuals, and artisans. The enemies of the republic who tried to undermine the unity of the people with despicable rumors and slanders have been defeated in the election.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 10th: TASS reports: The Czechoslovak Ministry of the Interior announced on the 5th the post-election results of the National Assembly election, which counted a total of 6,424,734 valid votes for the National Front, accounting for 89.2 percent of the total 7,798,766 valid votes.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th: TASS, Prague: Czech Premier Gottwald announced on the 7th at an emergency cabinet meeting that 64-year-old Czech President Beneš had formally submitted his resignation due to his deteriorating health; Gottwald then gave high praise to Beneš's immortal achievements in creating the republic. After listening to Gottwald's reading of the president's resignation notification, the cabinet approved it and scheduled a special parliamentary session for June 14 to elect a new president. Before the election of the new president, Premier Gottwald will perform the duties of the office in accordance with Article 60 of the 1920 constitution. The Czech government, recognizing Beneš's contributions to the country, decided to continue to grant the president's salary for life and will present the president with an official residence. Beneš had repeatedly requested resignation since May, but the government firmly retained him until now.

### 捷克民族阵线中央行动委会主席团　号召人民进行建设工作  总统贝奈斯因病辞职　总理对贝氏功勋备加赞扬

1948-06-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　捷克民族阵线中央行动委会主席团
  　号召人民进行建设工作
    总统贝奈斯因病辞职
  　总理对贝氏功勋备加赞扬
    【新华社陕北十日电】塔斯社讯：捷克斯拉夫民族阵线中央行动委员会主席团，在此大选胜利结束与新国会即将开幕之际，特发表宣言，向投票选举民族阵线的人民致谢，并号召全国人民“为共和国的利益，为加强共和国的力量，为增进人民的幸福与欢乐高高兴兴地进行建设工作”。宣言指出：我共和国历史上从来没有任何国会象本届国会一样受到如此一致的拥戴，我国走向社会主义的民主发展已得到保证，它是以我们绝大多数人民的完全一致，一切进步人民的密切合作，以及工人农民知识分子与手工业者的支持为基础的。力图以卑鄙谣言与诽谤破坏人民团结的共和国的敌人，已在选举中被击溃了。
    【新华社陕北十日电】塔斯社讯：捷克斯拉夫内政部于五日公布国民议会选举的会后结果计：民族阵线所得有效票共六百四十二万四千七百三十四票，占全部有效票七百七十九万八千七百六十六票的百分之八十九点二。
    【新华社陕北十二日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克总理哥特瓦尔德于七日在内阁紧急会议上，宣布六十四岁的捷克总统贝奈斯已因健康日趋衰弱正式提出辞职；哥氏继对贝奈斯缔造共和国之不朽功勋备致赞扬。内阁在听取哥氏宣读总统辞职通知后已予批准，并定于六月十四日召开国会特别会议，选举新总统。在新总统选出以前，按照一九二零年宪法第六十条之规定，由总理哥特瓦尔德代行职权。捷克政府以贝氏功在国家，决定继续授予总统薪俸终生，并将赠予总统官邸。贝氏自五月以来曾一再提请辞职，以政府坚决挽留始留任迄今。
