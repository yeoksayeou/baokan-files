Title: Central Plains People's Liberation Army Recaptures Xinzheng
Author:
Date: 1948-06-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 18th: A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army recaptured Xinzheng County, south of Zhengzhou, on the 11th. They captured Chen Bingyi, deputy head of the 2nd Regiment of the Provisional 26th Brigade (remnants of the puppet Sun Dianying troops), and over 400 others. They seized three heavy machine guns, twelve light machine guns, two hundred and seven rifles and pistols, forty thousand rounds of ammunition, twenty-one mules and horses, and some other military supplies. We suffered no casualties. In addition, on the 10th, another unit of the People's Liberation Army annihilated a squadron of the Sishui Security Regiment at Mulou, 12 li northeast of Sishui city. They captured 47 personnel and weapons, including a deputy battalion commander, and some other military supplies.

### 中原我军再克新郑

1948-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　中原我军再克新郑
    【新华社中原前线十八日电】解放军某部，十一日再度收复郑州以南之新郑县城，俘敌暂二十六旅（伪孙殿英残部）第二团副团长陈秉义以下四百余名，缴获重机枪三挺，轻机枪十二挺，长短枪二零七支，子弹四万发，骡马二十一匹，其他军用品一部。我无一伤亡。又解放军另部十日在汜水城东北十二里之穆楼消灭敌汜水保安团一个中队，俘大队副以下人枪各四十七，其他军用品一部。
