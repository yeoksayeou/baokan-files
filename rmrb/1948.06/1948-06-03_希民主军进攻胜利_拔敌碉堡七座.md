Title: Greek Democratic Army Achieves Victory: Captures Seven Enemy Bunkers
Author:
Date: 1948-06-03
Page: 2

　　Greek Democratic Army Achieves Victory
  　Captures Seven Enemy Bunkers
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 31st] Free Greece Radio reports: On May 15th, the Greek Democratic Army, with artillery support, victoriously attacked the Royalist Fascist troops in the area near Dra. After repelling five enemy counterattacks, the Democratic Army captured seven bunkers and seized two hilltops. During the battle, three enemy aircraft were destroyed, and over seventy enemy soldiers were killed. On the same day, the Democratic Army attacked the Royalist Fascist garrison in Thessaly. The enemy abandoned a large amount of equipment and fled in panic.

### 希民主军进攻胜利　拔敌碉堡七座

1948-06-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　希民主军进攻胜利
  　拔敌碉堡七座
    【新华社陕北三十一日电】自由希腊电台宣传：五月十五日希民主军在炮兵支持下，胜利进攻德拉附近地区之保皇法西斯军队。民主军在击退敌五次反扑后，拔除碉堡七座，攻占山头两座。战争中击毁敌机三架，毙敌七十余名。同日，民主军在色萨利进攻保皇法西斯守军、敌人遗弃大量装备，狼狈鼠窜。
