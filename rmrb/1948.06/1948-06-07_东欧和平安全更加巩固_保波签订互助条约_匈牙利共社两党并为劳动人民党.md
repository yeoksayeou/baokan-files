Title: Eastern European Peace and Security Further Consolidated; Poland and Bulgaria Sign Mutual Assistance Treaty; Hungarian Communist and Social Democratic Parties Merge into the Hungarian Working People's Party
Author:
Date: 1948-06-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 3rd: TASS, Warsaw Dispatch: Bulgarian Prime Minister Dimitrov arrived in Warsaw on the 28th of last month, leading the Bulgarian delegation. The twenty-year Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance between Poland and Bulgaria was signed on the 29th by Dimitrov and Bulgarian Foreign Minister Kolarov, and Polish Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz and Polish Foreign Minister Modzelewski, representing their respective countries. The treaty guarantees that the two countries will, by all possible means, prevent any form of aggression by Germany or any other country allied with Germany. It stipulates that if either contracting party is subjected to aggression by Germany or any ally of Germany, the other contracting party shall immediately provide military and all possible assistance. Both contracting parties guarantee not to participate in or engage in any alliance or action aimed against the other contracting party. The treaty stipulates that the two countries will cooperate closely on international issues concerning their interests, especially those concerning peace and security, and territorial integrity, and will promote and consolidate economic and cultural relations between the two countries.
    
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 3rd: Budapest Dispatch: The Hungarian Communist Party and the Social Democratic Party announced their merger into the Hungarian Working People's Party at the beginning of last month and promulgated the following program: The current general task is to consolidate people's democracy, improve people's living standards, and move towards socialism. In the political sphere, it calls for the full democratization of government institutions, the consolidation of local self-governing bodies, while guaranteeing the leading role of the central government. It advocates replacing the original loose alliance of parties with a single, popular, mass political organization, the New Independence Front. In the economic sphere, it calls for consolidating and expanding the planned economy, economic cooperation with friendly neighboring democratic countries, increasing labor productivity, expanding industrial and agricultural production, and improving the welfare of workers. In foreign policy, it guarantees peace, defends the victorious achievements of people's democracy, and national independence. The program concludes powerfully: "The Hungarian Working People's Party is striving to build socialism. In this society, there will be no exploiting classes, and individual happiness will be guaranteed by the increase in the general welfare of the country."

### 东欧和平安全更加巩固　保波签订互助条约  匈牙利共社两党并为劳动人民党

1948-06-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　东欧和平安全更加巩固
  　保波签订互助条约
    匈牙利共社两党并为劳动人民党
    【新华社陕北三日电】塔斯社华沙讯：保加利亚总理季米特洛夫于上月二十八日率领保国代表团抵达华沙，波保两国二十年友好合作互助条约已于二十九日由季米特洛夫与保外长科拉罗夫，波总理西伦凯维兹与波外长摩塞莱夫斯基分别代表两国签字。条约保证两国以一切可能方法，防止德国或任何其他与德国联盟之国家的任何形式的侵略，规定缔约国一方如遇德国或德国之任何盟国的侵略时，缔约国另一方应立即给予军事及一切可能的援助。缔约国双方保证不参加或从事一切旨在反对缔约国另一方的联盟或行动。条约规定两国在有关两国利益，尤其是有关和平安全，领土完整诸国际问题上密切合作，并将促进与巩固两国间的经济和文化关系。
    【新华社陕北三日电】布达佩斯讯：匈共与社会民主党，已于上月初旬宣布合并为劳动人民党，并颁布政纲如下：当前总任务为巩固人民民主，提高人民生活标准，和向社会主义迈进。在政治方面，要求政府机构充分民主化，巩固地方自治机构，同时保证中央政权的领导作用。主张以单一的人民的群众性的政治组织新独立阵线，代替原有松驰的各党派联盟。在经济方面，巩固与扩大计划经济，和与各友邻民主国家的经济合作，增加劳动生产率，扩大工农业生产，增进工人的福利。在外交政策方面，保证和平，保卫人民民主的胜利果实和国家独立。政纲最后有力地写道：“匈牙利劳动人民党，正在为建立社会主义而奋斗。在这一社会中，将没有剥削阶级个人的幸福，将因国家普遍福利的增进而获得保证。”
