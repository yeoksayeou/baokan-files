    Wang Rong, Chang Zhen, Liu Zhenji, Nie Bingxian, Niu Dengkui, Sun Li, Cui Fengwu, Lan Tian, Zhang Kaopi, Huo Dong, Chen Minying, Yuan Ming, Wang Wanzhong, Xu Wenliang, Zhou Hanwen, Zhang Anding, Niu Fuwu, Chen Feng, Gao Dai, Jing Shou, Li Qingchi, Zhang Liancheng, Li Zhiqi, Cao Huanzhang, Song Xuhua, Zhu Yicheng, Wang Cunshan, Qi Dongchang, Qin Shubao, Mu Ding, Li Jitang, Xia Qiuchuang, Changzhi County Party Committee Office, Licheng Qiushuyuan Working Group, Qinqi County Town Working Group, Gaoyi Guxingzhuang Working Group Comrades:
    Please read the mailbox in this newspaper on June 9th, and explain the specific situation item by item for common research and judgment on the composition. He Qianyuan, Liu Qianfeng, Shen Xian, Zhao Hongyou Comrades:
    The issue of substitute farming and corvée labor has been forwarded to the relevant authorities for consultation and answers. Song Zhangshi, Wu District, Qizhi County:
    Regarding your child's issue, the Guantao County Government replied that it has been ruled to temporarily be raised by Guo Jinou, and you are allowed to visit and be close to him. When the child is fifteen years old, he can choose voluntarily. Chen Daosheng, Qi District, Zhangnan County Comrade:
    We have forwarded your issue to the county government for investigation and handling. Chen Peien, Nanle County, Ji Lu Yu Comrade:
    Regarding your wife's illness, we have written to the Heping Hospital to inquire. We are looking into foreign breed bees and knitting machines for you and will inform you as soon as there is a result.
