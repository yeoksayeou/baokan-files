　　Communications and Exchanges (No. 29)
　　To Party committees at all levels and in all regions, and to all joint offices:

　　In the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's instructions regarding the land reform work and Party rectification work in 1948, it was mentioned: "It is imperative to assign all cadres engaged in land reform work and Party rectification work to conscientiously study, fully understand, and completely abide by the various important documents promulgated by the Central Committee. No unauthorized modifications are permitted..." What necessary measures and plans do you have in place for cadres to study the Central Committee's various instructions and policies? How is the work progressing? How are you educating cadres and inspecting work based on the spirit of the Central Committee's documents, connecting it with the actual conditions in your area? What are some of the problems and feedback? We hope to receive your report promptly.

　　　　　　　　　　　　This Newspaper's Communications Department

### 通讯往来　（第二十九号）

1948-06-02
第1版()
专栏：通讯往来

　　通讯往来　（第二十九号）
    各地各级党委会，各联合办公室：
    中共中央关于一九四八年土地改革工作和整党工作的指示中提到：“必须将中央颁布的各项重要文件，责成一切从事土改工作和整党工作的干部，认真学习，完全了解，并责成他们全部遵守，不许擅自修改……。”你处对干部学习中央各项指示政策，有些什么必要的措施与计划？进行情况如何？怎样根据中央文件精神联系你们那里实际情况，教育干部，检查工作？有些什么问题和反映？希能迅速给我们报导。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
