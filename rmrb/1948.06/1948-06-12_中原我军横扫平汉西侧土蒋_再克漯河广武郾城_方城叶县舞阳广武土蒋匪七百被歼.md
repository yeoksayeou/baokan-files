Title: Central Plains People's Army Sweeps Away Local Chiang Kai-shek Troops on the West Side of the Pinghan Railway, Recaptures Luohe, Guangwu, and Yancheng; 700 Local Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Eliminated in Fangcheng, Yexian, Wuyang, and Guangwu
Author:
Date: 1948-06-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 9th: A unit of the Central Plains People's Liberation Army, on the 3rd of this month, in a battle near Liwan area west of Yancheng, wiped out 500 members of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit self-defense forces from Fangcheng, Yexian, and Wuyang counties. It captured over 400 enemies, including the commander of the self-defense forces of the three counties, a certain Liu (name to be verified), and the magistrate of Yexian County, Hu Dongcai; dozens were killed or wounded. In the afternoon of the same day, Yancheng was recovered. The defending enemy fled south, and our army pursued the victory, recapturing Luohe on the 4th.

Xinhua News Agency, Luoyang Front, 9th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army, on the evening of June 2nd, once again recovered Guangwu County, northwest of Zhengzhou. It captured alive over 190 enemy soldiers, including the commander of the self-defense force and deputy magistrate of Guangwu County, Zhou Yuxing. One small cannon, three light machine guns, and sixty-five rifles and short guns were seized.

### 中原我军横扫平汉西侧土蒋　再克漯河广武郾城  方城叶县舞阳广武土蒋匪七百被歼

1948-06-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　中原我军横扫平汉西侧土蒋
  　再克漯河广武郾城
    方城叶县舞阳广武土蒋匪七百被歼
    【新华社中原九日电】中原解放军一部，本月三日，在郾城以西李湾附近地区战斗中，歼灭蒋匪方城、叶县、舞阳三县自卫总队五百名。计生俘敌三县自卫总队指挥官刘某（名待查）及叶县县长胡东才以下四百余名，毙伤数十名。同日下午收复郾城，守敌南逃，我乘胜追击，续于四日，再克漯河。
    【新华社洛阳前线九日电】解放军一部，六月二日晚，再次收复郑州西北之广武县城。活捉守敌自卫总队队长兼广武县副县长周玉兴以下一百九十余名。缴小炮一门，轻机枪三挺，长短枪六十五支。
