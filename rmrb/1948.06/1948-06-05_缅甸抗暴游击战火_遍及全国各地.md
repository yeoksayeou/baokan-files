Title: Burmese Anti-Tyranny Guerrilla Warfare Spreads Throughout the Country
Author:
Date: 1948-06-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 1st.  Rangoon dispatch: According to official announcements, the Burmese people's armed resistance against the fascist tyranny of the Free League government is now raging in guerrilla warfare throughout all regions of Burma. This situation has forced Thakin Nu to announce on May 26th that he will resign as Prime Minister on July 20th.  The Associated Press reported that Thakin Nu's "decision has demoralized many political figures in Burma."

### 缅甸抗暴游击战火　遍及全国各地

1948-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　缅甸抗暴游击战火
  　遍及全国各地
    【新华社陕北一日电】仰光消息：据官方公报宣露，缅甸人民武装抵抗自由同盟政府法西斯暴政的游击战火，现遍及缅甸各个地区，此种形势迫使德钦努于上月二十六日宣布他将于七月二十日辞去总理之职。合众社称：德钦努“此一决定，已令缅甸许多政界人士因而丧气”。
