Title: Hai-Tai Line Captured Qutang, Da Xiaobaimi, Jiangyan
Author:
Date: 1948-06-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 16th. A unit of the Central China People's Liberation Army launched an offensive against the Hai (An) Tai (Xian) line on the 7th of this month. By the 8th, it had successively captured Qutang, Da Xiaobaimi on the line, and seized the opportunity to recover Jiangyan. Local armed forces in the area also cooperated in the battle, defeating a regiment of the enemy's 21st Division, which was coming to the aid of Hou (located southeast of Taixian). In total, more than 2,500 enemy troops were annihilated. Detailed battle results are still being investigated.

### 海泰线攻克曲塘大小白米姜堰

1948-06-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　海泰线攻克曲塘大小白米姜堰
    【新华社华东十六日电】华中解放军一部，于本月七日起向海（安）泰（县）线发动攻势，至八日止，先后攻克该线之曲塘、大小白米，并乘胜收复姜堰。该地地方武装，亦配合作战，击溃自堠（在泰县东南）东援之敌整二十一师一个团，以上共歼敌二千五百余名，详细战果，正清查中。
