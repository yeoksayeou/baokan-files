Title: Correspondence

Author:

Date: 1948-06-18
Page: 1

　　To our correspondent comrades and comrades who care for this newspaper:
    With the merging of the Jin-Cha-Ji Daily of the Jin-Cha-Ji area and the People's Daily of the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu area to publish this newspaper, we hope that all the correspondent comrades of the original two newspapers will continue to serve as correspondents for this newspaper, writing articles for this newspaper with the spirit of loving and caring for the Party newspaper, and providing suggestions for improvement at any time. We hope that practical workers from all departments of the Party, government, military, and civilians in the whole region, comrades who love to write, and comrades who care for this newspaper can frequently write articles for this newspaper, provide information, raise questions, or send various materials, or tell us interview leads. Please pay attention to organizing reports on the questions and opinions raised by the masses in various places regarding work and policies, and please be enthusiastic in undertaking to write on behalf of the masses when they request it.
    Please carry out reporting according to the points indicated in the inaugural editorial (serving as the inaugural speech) of this newspaper. The reporting on unity and production mentioned in the article is currently the focus of the reporting task. Please pay close attention to reflecting the recovery and development of agricultural production, the production enthusiasm of people from all social strata (including rich peasants, enlightened gentry, and landlords who have been given land and are willing to engage in production), how various places are entering into production, and organizing mutual aid and labor exchange. Introduce experiences in promoting production and mutual aid and labor exchange. Please also vigorously organize reports on industrial production and urban industry and commerce. Select key points to make specific reports on the actual situation of the recovery and development of industry and handicrafts in various places, and the cooperation between labor and capital and the improvement of production after factories in various places implement new management and operation methods. In addition, focus on systematic and complete typical materials regarding the experience of Party rectification and democracy. Please make regular and continuous reports on the guerrilla warfare in the border areas and the struggle against the enemy that is currently being carried out to protect wheat and grain.

### 通讯往来

1948-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来通讯员同志及爱护本报的同志们：
    晋察冀区晋察冀日报与晋冀鲁豫区人民日报合并出版本报，我们希望原两报的通讯员同志们全部继续充任本报通讯员，一本爱护党报、关心党报的精神，源源为本报写稿，并随时提示改进意见。我们希望全区党政军民各部门的实际工作者、爱好写作的同志及爱护本报的同志们，都能经常给本报写稿、供给情况、提出问题，或寄赐各种材料，或告诉我们采访线索。对各地群众有关工作与政策提出的问题与意见，请能注意组织报导，群众有要求代笔者，务望能热心担承。
    当前报导方针，请根据本报创刊社论（代创刊词）所指出的各点进行报导，文中关于团结生产报导，乃是目前报导任务的重心，请着重注意反映恢复和发展农业生产、各阶层人民（包括富农，开明绅士，以及分了土地后的地主而愿从事生产者）的生产情绪，各地如何进入生产、组织互助变工？介绍生产推进、互助拨工等经验；对工业生产和城市工商业的报导也请大力组织，各地工业、手工业的恢复和发展，各地工厂实行新的管理、经营方法后，劳资合作，提高生产的实际情形，均可选择重点作具体的报导。此外，关于整党、民主的经验，着重系统完整的典型材料；边沿区的游击战争，正在开展着的护麦保粮等对敌斗争，务请作经常的连续的报导。
