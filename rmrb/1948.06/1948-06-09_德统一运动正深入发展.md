Title: German Unification Movement Deepens

Author: 
Date: 1948-06-09
Page: 2

　　German Unification Movement Deepens
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 3rd. The signature campaign for German unification is deepening in the Soviet-occupied zone. TASS reports: As of May 27th, the percentage of residents in the Soviet-occupied zone of Germany who signed in favor of German unification is as follows: Saxony Province accounts for 67 percent of residents, Mecklenburg accounts for 64 percent, and Brandenburg accounts for 50 percent.

### 德统一运动正深入发展

1948-06-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　德统一运动正深入发展
    【新华社陕北三日电】德国人民要求统一的签名运动，在苏占区益趋深入。塔斯社讯：截至五月二十七日止，德境苏占区签名赞成德国统一的人数，比例如下：萨克赤省占居民百分之六十七，梅格森堡占百分之六十四，勃兰登堡占百分之五十。
