Title: National Liberated Areas Trade Union Congress Postponed to July
Author:
Date: 1948-06-14
Page: 2

　　National Liberated Areas Trade Union Congress
  　Postponed to July
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 11th] The National Liberated Areas and Northeast Liberated Areas Trade Union Congress, originally scheduled to open in Harbin on June 10th, has been postponed to July, according to a notice issued by the Congress Preparatory Committee, due to the inability of trade union representatives from various liberated areas within the customs to arrive on time. The opening date will be announced separately once it is determined.

### 全国解放区职工大会　延至七月间举行

1948-06-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国解放区职工大会
  　延至七月间举行
    【新华社东北十一日电】全国解放区与东北解放区职工代表大会，原定六月十日在哈尔滨开幕，现因关内各解放区职工代表不能如期到达，大会筹备处顷特发出通告。宣布大会延至七月间举行开幕，日期俟确定后另行通知。
