Title: French Workers Demand Wage Increase
Author:
Date: 1948-06-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 1st -- Paris dispatch: The annual meeting of the meat workers' union affiliated with the French General Confederation of Labor closed on the 24th of last month. The conference passed a resolution demanding a 20 percent wage increase, and an automatic wage increase every time the cost of living rises by 10 percent. The resolution called for reducing capitalists' profits to suppress prices.

### 法国工人要求增资

1948-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　法国工人要求增资
    【新华社陕北一日电】巴黎讯：法国总工会所属肉类工人工会的年会，于上月廿四日闭幕。大会上通过决议，要求增加工资百分之二十，并在生活费每次上涨百分之十的情况下，工资自动递增。该决议要求以削减资本家利润来抑低物价。
