Title: In Linfen Prisoner Group,阎 Foe Division Commander Xu Qichang Discovered Again
Author:
Date: 1948-06-06
Page: 1

　　Linfen Prisoner Group
  　阎 Foe Division Commander Xu Qichang Discovered Again
    [Jinnan Front, 4th dispatch] The People's Liberation Army, which liberated Linfen, recently discovered Xu Qichang, the Major General Division Commander of the 66th Division of the Kuomintang Yan Xishan bandit troops, in the prisoner group. This is the seventh general-level officer of the enemy army captured in the Linfen campaign.

### 临汾俘虏群中　又查获阎匪师长徐其昌

1948-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　临汾俘虏群中
  　又查获阎匪师长徐其昌
    【晋南前线四日电】我解放临汾之人民解放军，日前于俘虏群中，又查获国民党阎锡山匪部六十六师少将师长徐其昌。此为临汾战役俘获敌军之第七名将级军官。
