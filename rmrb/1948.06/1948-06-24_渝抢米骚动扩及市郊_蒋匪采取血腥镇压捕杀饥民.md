Title: Chongqing Rice Riot Spreads to Suburbs; Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Adopt Bloody Suppression, Hunting Down and Killing Starving People
Author:
Date: 1948-06-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd. According to Reuters and Associated Press reports from Nanjing on the 18th: The Chongqing rice riot that erupted on the 16th had spread from the city proper to Haitangxi and other areas on the South Bank by the 17th. The Chongqing Chiang government has adopted a bloody policy of suppression against the starving people who are seizing grain. Bandit Mayor Yang Sen ordered the police to "execute on the spot" starving people who seize rice. Three of the starving people arrested on the 16th have been brutally murdered, and another 250 starving people have been arrested. According to a TASS dispatch from Shanghai on the 19th: "Despite the measures taken by the police, the Chongqing rice rush continued until the 18th."

### 渝抢米骚动扩及市郊　蒋匪采取血腥镇压捕杀饥民

1948-06-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　渝抢米骚动扩及市郊
  　蒋匪采取血腥镇压捕杀饥民
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】据路透、合众两社十八日自南京报导：十六日爆发的重庆抢米骚动，十七日已由该市市内扩展至海棠溪及南岸其他地区。渝蒋政府对抢粮饥民已采取血腥的镇压政策。匪市长杨森命令警察对抢米饥民“得就地枪决”。十六日被捕饥民中已有三人被惨杀，且续有饥民二百五十人被捕。另据塔斯社上海十九日电称：“尽管警察采取措施，重庆抢米风潮至十八日仍在继续中。”
