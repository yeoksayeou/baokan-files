Anarchism or Socialism?
By Stalin, translated by Cao Baohua

Because this book was written by Stalin at the time to refute anarchists, it is titled this way. In fact, the content of the book is a very accessible reading material explaining revolutionary theory. For comrades who initially want to study theoretical issues, it can be said to be the best introductory textbook. Our motivation for reprinting this book lies in providing this introductory theoretical reader to comrades who want to improve their theoretical level. Do not casually dismiss it just because the title of the book seems far removed from our real life.

### 无政府主义还是社会主义？

1948-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　无政府主义还是社会主义？
    斯大林著·曹葆华译
    因为这本书是斯大林当时为驳斥无政府主义者而写的，所以书名是这样写法。其实书的内容是一本讲解革命理论的最通俗的读物，对于开初想学习理论问题的同志，可说是最好不过的一本入门课本。我们翻印这本书的动机，也就在于拿这册理论入门读本供给一般想提高自己理论水平的同志们看。切不要因为书的题名和我们的现实生活似乎较远，就随便放了过去。
