Title: Qingdao US Troops Violently Beat Our Compatriots
Author:
Date: 1948-03-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th. News from newspapers in the Chiang-controlled areas: On the night of the 22nd of last month, an atrocity occurred in Qingdao where US troops violently beat our compatriots. When Tang Bailin Restaurant on Guanxian Road was preparing to close for the night, four US Marines suddenly barged in, making loud noises and unreasonable demands. They also carried tables and chairs out of the restaurant. When the restaurant worker, Gao Mingxuan, chased after them to dissuade them, he was kicked to the ground by a US soldier. Subsequently, the four US soldiers used clubs and fists to beat him. Gao Mingxuan suffered severe head injuries and fainted on the spot.

### 青岛美军  毒打我同胞

1948-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　青岛美军
    毒打我同胞
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】蒋区报纸消息：上月二十二日夜青岛发生美军毒打我同胞暴行。冠县路汤白林饭店正准备上门休息时，突闯入美海军陆战队士兵四人高声喧吵无理取闹，并将棹椅抬出店外，店伙高明轩追出劝阻时，即被美军一脚踢倒，随后四美军棍棒拳足交加，高明轩头部重伤当场晕蹶。
