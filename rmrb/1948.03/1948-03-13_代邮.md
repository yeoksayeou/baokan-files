Title: Mail Delivery

Author:

Date: 1948-03-13
Page: 1

To Zhang Shaoqing, Commander of the Second Sub-district of the Postal Service:
    Your letter requesting one hundred copies of this newspaper, received on the fifth of this month, did not specify which military region's Second Sub-district you are with, or your location. Therefore, the newspapers cannot be delivered. Please inform us immediately.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Circulation Department, March 6th

### 代邮

1948-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　代邮第二分区司令部张少卿：
    于本月五号收见你的来信，要订本报一百份，但未写明系哪个军区的二分区与你的驻址，报纸无法转递，请即告知是盼！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　发行部三月六号
