Title: Canadian Seamen's Union Resolution: Refuse to Load Aid-Chiang Supplies
Author:
Date: 1948-03-04
Page: 2

　　Canadian Seamen's Union Resolution
  　Refuse to Load Aid-Chiang Supplies
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 2nd. According to Agence France-Presse, Toronto, 29th: The Canadian Seamen's Union Congress, meeting in Toronto, has resolved to oppose the Canadian government's transportation of war materials to Chiang Kai-shek. The union urged all members to refuse to load war materials.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 29th. The Tianjin Daily reported on the 3rd of this month: In order to assist the American imperialists in plundering mineral resources from Hainan Island, the Chiang bandits have decided to establish the Hainan Island Mining Bureau. More than 300,000 tons of iron sand previously stored on the island are being prepared for shipment to Japan in exchange for equipment with the US military headquarters in Japan. The establishment of the Hainan Island fishing base is also being actively carried out by the US and Chiang, and all fishing vessels are UNRRA fishing boats allocated by the US imperialists, and the funding is also the responsibility of the US imperialists.

### 加拿大海员工会决议　拒绝装运援蒋物资

1948-03-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　加拿大海员工会决议
  　拒绝装运援蒋物资
    【新华社陕北二日电】据法新社多伦多二十九日电：在多伦多集会的加拿大海员职工联合会大会，已决议反对加政府运输战争物资给蒋介石。该工会敦促全体会员拒绝装运战争物资。
    【新华社陕北二十九日电】本月三日津报载：蒋匪为协助美帝掠夺海南岛矿产，已决定设立海南岛矿务局，该岛旧存铁砂三十余万吨正拟运日，与日本美军总部交换器材。海南岛渔业基地的建立工作，亦由美蒋积极进行中，所有渔航皆为美帝拨发之联总渔轮，经费亦由美帝负责。
