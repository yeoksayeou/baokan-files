Title: On the Outskirts of Luoyang, Our Army Annihilates 600 Bandits

Author:

Date: 1948-03-28
Page: 3

　　Luoyang Outskirts
  　Our Army Annihilates 600 Bandits
    [Xinhua News Agency, Frontline, Henan-Shaanxi-Hubei, 26th] On the morning of the 20th, a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army, in the Xujiachaizi and Mageta area, 25 li southwest of Luoyang, intercepted the bandit army's 41st Division, 124th Brigade, which was invading from Luoyang. After several hours of fighting, over 500 enemy soldiers were killed or wounded, and over 90 were captured. The annihilated enemy abandoned corpses and wounded soldiers and fled back to Luoyang city in a panic. We seized twelve light and heavy machine guns, one Sten submachine gun, one 60mm mortar, over seventy rifles, and over ten thousand rounds of ammunition.

### 洛阳外围　我军歼匪六百

1948-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　洛阳外围
  　我军歼匪六百
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线二十六日电】解放军某部二十日晨在洛阳西南二十五里之徐家砦、马圪塔地区，阻击由洛阳出犯之匪军四十一师一二四旅经数小时战斗毙伤其五百余名、俘九十余名。歼敌遗弃尸体及伤兵狼狈回窜洛阳城，我缴获轻重机枪十二挺、司登手提式一支、六○炮一门、步枪七十余支、子弹万余发。
