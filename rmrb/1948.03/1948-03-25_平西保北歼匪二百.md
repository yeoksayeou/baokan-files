Title: Wipe Out 200 Bandits in Western and Northern Hebei

Author:

Date: 1948-03-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, March 23rd: Late news: On the 8th of this month, the local armed forces of Western Hebei annihilated most of the more than 200 Chiang-Fu bandit troops who came out of Mentougou to harass the area at Xiaweidian, north of Mentougou. On the 9th, the local armed forces of Northern Hebei repelled more than 400 bandits and puppet troops who invaded, looted, and conscripted soldiers west of Zhuoxian County, and annihilated some of them. On the 11th, another local armed force captured the bandit stronghold of Zhanglicun southeast of Beihedian (south of Dingxing) railway station in one fell swoop. In the above three battles, a total of more than 70 Chiang-Fu bandits were killed or wounded, 166 bandits were captured, one 60mm mortar, ten light machine guns, 120 various guns, and more than 10,000 catties of grain were seized.

### 平西保北歼匪二百

1948-03-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　平西保北歼匪二百
    【新华社晋察冀二十三日电】迟到消息：本月八日平西我地方武装于门头沟北下苇店将由门头沟出扰之蒋傅匪军二百余大部歼灭。九日，保北我地方武装于涿县西击溃出犯抢掠抓丁的匪伪四百余，并歼灭其一部。十一日另部地方武装一举攻克北河店（定兴南）车站东南之张李村匪据点。以上三处战斗，总计毙伤蒋傅匪七十余名、俘匪一百六十六名，缴获六○小炮一门、轻机枪十挺、各种枪一百二十支，粮食万余斤。
