Title: Our Army in Henan, Shaanxi and Hubei Provinces Conquered Luoyang and Recovered Four Cities Including Yuxian and Dengfeng. A Certain Unit Commended Warriors for Forcibly Crossing the Luo River
Author:
Date: 1948-03-23
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Henan, Shaanxi and Hubei Front, 21st: (Delayed News) A unit of the People's Liberation Army successively recovered the three county towns of Yuxian, Dengfeng, and Yichuan southeast of Luoyang on February 21st, March 5th, and 7th. The defending enemy fled upon hearing the news. Furthermore, local armed forces of our Fifth Military Sub-district recovered Xiangcheng on the 15th. The defending enemy abandoned the city and fled eastward.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Henan, Shaanxi and Hubei Front, 21st: A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army issued an order to commend Wu Qinping, the deputy political instructor of the Sixth Company of the Second Battalion of a certain regiment, platoon leader Wu Kaiji, squad leaders Zhai Xiangtian, Cao Wenliang, Wang Youshan, and soldiers Chu Jiashui and Zhang Shunzhong, seven warriors, for forcibly crossing the Luo River and capturing the new city of Yanshi. Platoon leader Wu Kaiji, a combat model who gloriously sacrificed during the crossing, was posthumously recognized as a combat hero. The commendation order pointed out: This kind of heroic, tenacious, and selfless spirit is a manifestation of the generally improved class consciousness after the Three Check-ups and Rectification Movement. Therefore, despite several days of long marches, they disregarded fatigue and cold, eliminated the natural obstacles of deep water and a wide river, and defeated the Chiang bandit troops defending the river. The commendation order called on all commanders and fighters to learn from the heroic struggle, the class conscious spirit of overcoming difficulties, the democratic work style in combat, and the spirit of unity and mutual assistance on the battlefield displayed by the warriors who forcibly crossed the Luo River.

### 豫陕鄂我军攻克洛阳前后　收复禹县登封等四城  某部嘉奖强渡洛河勇士

1948-03-23
第2版()
专栏：

    豫陕鄂我军攻克洛阳前后
  　收复禹县登封等四城
    某部嘉奖强渡洛河勇士
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线二十一日电】（迟到消息）解放军一部于二月二十一日、三月五日、七日先后再度收复洛阳东南之禹县、登封、伊川三座县城，守敌闻风逃窜。又我五分区地方武装十五日收复襄城，守敌弃城东逃。
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线二十一日电】解放军某部颁令嘉奖强渡洛河，攻占偃师新城之某团二营六连副政治指导员吴钦平，排长吴开吉，班长翟相田、曹文良、王友善和战士褚加水、张顺忠等七勇士，并追认强渡中光荣牺牲的战斗模范排长吴开吉为战斗英雄。嘉奖令中指出：此种英勇顽强，奋不顾身的精神，是由于三查整训运动后阶级觉悟普遍提高的表现。因此虽经数日长途行军，仍不顾疲劳，不顾寒冷，排除了水深河宽的天然障碍，击溃河防蒋匪。嘉奖令号召全体指战员学习强渡洛河勇士们英勇奋斗，克服困难的阶级自觉精神，战斗中的民主作风与战场上的团结互助精神。
