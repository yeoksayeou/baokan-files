Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Provoke and Invade Mengjiang, Mongolian Government Protests
Author:
Date: 1948-03-02
Page: 2

　　Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Provoke and Invade Mengjiang
  　Mongolian Government Protests
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th. TASS, Kuren, 25th: The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic published a communique in the Unen newspaper today stating that on January 22nd, seventeen Chinese (referring to Chiang Kai-shek) armed cavalrymen crossed the country's border in the Toaikho, Dolosu, and Barack areas, penetrated 75 kilometers into the country's territory, and attacked the residents of Kiachisomona Village in the Kabku District. 130 farms were attacked. In the two days of looting and killing by the Chinese (Chiang Kai-shek) cavalry, some peaceful residents, both male and female, were killed or injured, one Mongolian border guard was captured, and 1,000 camels and horses were stolen. The communique continued: On January 28th, Chinese (Chiang Kai-shek) soldiers again invaded the country's territory by 15 kilometers, attacking the country's outposts in the Nibuha and Alago areas, killing two Mongolian border guards and seizing their camels, weapons, and personal belongings. The communique stated: The government of the Mongolian People's Republic has protested to the Chinese (Chiang Kai-shek) government for this, demanding that the criminals who sabotaged the border of the Mongolian Republic and attacked peaceful residents and border guards be severely punished, that the captured Mongolian border guards be released immediately, and that the looted property be returned.

### 蒋匪挑衅侵犯蒙疆　蒙古政府提出抗议

1948-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　蒋匪挑衅侵犯蒙疆
  　蒙古政府提出抗议
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】蒙古人民共和国抗议蒋匪政府两度挑衅暴行。塔斯社库伦二十五日讯：蒙古人民共和外交部今日于乌尼恩报发表公报称：中国（指蒋匪）武装骑兵十七名，一月二十二日在托艾何、道洛苏、巴拉克地区越过该国边境，深入该国领土七十五公里后袭击喀卜区基亚奇索莫纳村的居民，农场被袭击者达一百三十个。在中国（蒋匪）骑兵两日的掠夺与杀戮中，男女和平居民死伤若干人，蒙古边防军一名被俘，驼骆与马被抢走一千匹。该公报续称：一月二十八日，中国（蒋匪）士兵又侵入该国领土十五公里，在尼布哈、阿拉戈地区袭击该国岗哨，击毙蒙古边防军两人，并夺去他们的驼骆、武器及私人财物。公报称：蒙古人民共和国政府已为此向中国（蒋）政府提出抗议，要求严惩破坏蒙古共和国边境袭击和平居民与边防军的凶犯，立即释放被俘的蒙古边防军及交还掠去的财物。
