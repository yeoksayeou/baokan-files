　　Handbook on Land Redistribution (Fourth Edition)
    This book is reprinted for the fourth time, incorporating the "Instructions of the CCP Central Committee on Land Reform and Party Consolidation in Old and Semi-Old Areas," the "Instructions of the CCP Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Central Bureau on Land Reform, Party Consolidation and Democratic Movement," the "Typical Experience of Huangjiachuan Subsidy in Suide," and the "Experience of Land Reform in the Old Liberated Area of ​​Pingshan." Also included is a copy of the "Decision of the Central Bureau's Liquidation Committee on Clearing机关 Stores and 机关 Production."
