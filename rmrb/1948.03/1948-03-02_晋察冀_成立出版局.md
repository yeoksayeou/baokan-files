Title: Jin-Cha-Ji Border Region Establishes Publishing Bureau
Author:
Date: 1948-03-02
Page: 1

　　Jin-Cha-Ji Border Region

　　Establishes Publishing Bureau
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji Dispatch] In order to unify and strengthen the publishing and distribution work, the Propaganda Department of the CCP Jin-Cha-Ji Central Bureau has decided to establish the Border Region Publishing Bureau and reorganize and expand the original Jin-Cha-Ji Xinhua Bookstore as the bureau's unified external distribution agency. The director of the bureau will be concurrently held by Zhou Yang, the Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Bureau. Wang Ziye will be appointed as the Director of the Editorial Department, Li Changbin will be appointed as the Minister of Publishing and Distribution and concurrently as the Manager of Xinhua Bookstore, and Wang Chuan will be appointed as the Deputy Minister of Publishing and Distribution.

### 晋察冀　成立出版局

1948-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　晋察冀
  　成立出版局
    【新华社晋察冀电】为统一加强出版与发行事业，中共晋察冀中央局宣传部决定成立边区出版局，并改组与扩大原晋察冀新华书店，作为该局对外统一发行机关。该局局长由中央局宣传部长周扬兼任，另任王子野为编辑部长，李长彬任出版发行部长兼新华书店经理，王钏任出版发行部副部长。
