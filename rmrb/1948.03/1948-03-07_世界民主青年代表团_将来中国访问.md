Title: World Democratic Youth Delegation to Visit China in the Future
Author:
Date: 1948-03-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 5th: Moscow News: The World Federation of Democratic Youth has decided to organize a delegation to visit China, the Middle East, and Latin American countries. The delegation includes representatives from the Soviet Union, Britain, the United States, France, Indonesia, Vietnam, Bulgaria, Poland, and Sweden. The first step will be to visit North Africa and the Middle East in April to understand the situation of young people in these countries. Nina Laguskaya, General Secretary of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, stated that Soviet youth support this move. Nina particularly emphasized that they "also support sending a delegation to visit China," saying, "This will once again prove that the youth and students of all countries support the Chinese democratic youth who are fighting for China's national liberation."

### 世界民主青年代表团　将来中国访问

1948-03-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　世界民主青年代表团
  　将来中国访问
    【新华社陕北五日电】莫斯科讯：世界民主青年联盟决定组织代表团访问中国、中东和拉丁美洲各国。代表团包括苏、英、美、法、印尼、越南、保、波和瑞典的代表。第一步将于四月间访问北非、中东，并了解这些国家的青年状况。苏联青年反法西斯委员会总书记尼娜·拉古斯卡雅表示：苏联青年赞同此举，尼娜特别强调“也赞同派遣代表团访问中国”，她说：“这将再次证明各国的青年和学生，是支持为中国民族解放而斗争的中国民主青年。”
