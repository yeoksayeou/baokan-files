Title: People's Blackboard
Author:
Date: 1948-03-18
Page: 1

　　People's Blackboard
　　Our Northeast People's Liberation Army won a great victory in Sipingjie on the 13th. Our People's Liberation Army south of the Yellow River also captured the famous city of Luoyang on the evening of the same day. In this battle, more than 10,000 Chiang Kai-shek bandit Youth Army 206th Division and other units were annihilated. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to congratulate these two great victories.

### 大众黑板

1948-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　大众黑板
    咱们东北解放军十三号在四平街打了大胜仗，咱们黄河南的解放军，也在这一天晚上，打下了有名的洛阳城，这一仗共消灭蒋匪青年军二○六师等部一万多人。中共中央发电报祝贺这两个地方的大胜利。
