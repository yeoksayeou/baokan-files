Title: Soviet-Bulgarian Treaty of Mutual Assistance to be Signed; Dimitrov Arrives in Soviet Capital
Author:
Date: 1948-03-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 19th: Moscow dispatch: Bulgarian Prime Minister Dimitrov, accompanied by Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Kolarov and others, flew from Sofia to Moscow on the 15th. Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov, Deputy Foreign Ministers Vyshinsky and Zorin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Kruglov, and others warmly welcomed them at the airport. After disembarking, Dimitrov delivered a radio address at the airport, announcing: "The Bulgarian government delegation has arrived in Moscow to conclude a treaty of friendship and mutual assistance with the Soviet government. This treaty will consolidate and develop the indispensable friendship between the two countries. The Bulgarian people believe that the friendship of the people of this powerful socialist country is the primary defender of freedom, democracy, and progress." On the 16th, Stalin received Dimitrov and Kolarov.

### 苏保将缔互助条约　季米特洛夫抵苏京

1948-03-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏保将缔互助条约　季米特洛夫抵苏京
    【新华社陕北十九日电】莫斯科讯：保总理季米特洛夫偕同副总理兼外长科拉罗夫等一行，于十五日乘机由索菲亚飞抵此间，苏外长莫洛托夫、外次维辛斯基和佐林，对外贸易副部长克鲁金诺夫等，均亲赴机场热烈欢迎。季米特洛夫下机后，即在机场发表广播演说，宣布：“保政府代表团到达莫斯科，是前来与苏联政府缔结友好互助条约。这一条约将巩固和发展两国之间不可或缺的友谊。保国人民认为，这个社会主义强大国家人民的友谊，是自由、民主、进步的首要保卫者”。十六日，斯大林接见了季米特洛夫和科拉罗夫。
