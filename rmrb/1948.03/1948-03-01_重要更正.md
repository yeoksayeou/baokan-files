Author:
Date: 1948-03-01
Page: 1

　　Important Correction
　　In the Central Bureau's "February 1" instructions published in this newspaper yesterday (29th), on the second page, second column, seventeenth line, the word "动" (dong) was omitted from the parentheses "脑力劳动的知识分子" (intellectuals engaged in mental labor). It should be added. In the second to last line, "又不至耽误春耕" ("and will not delay spring plowing"), "至" (zhi) should be changed to "致" (zhi). Also, in the article "Chiang Kai-shek Illegally Sells China's New Twenty-One Demands" published in this newspaper on the 26th, in the tenth point, "让美国..." ("allow the United States...") is a mistake for "让美军..." ("allow the American military..."). The British weekly "New National and Ethnic" is a mistake for "New Statesman and Nation". Also, "并有计划有步骤的步步推进..." ("and advance step by step in a planned and methodical way...") is a mistake for "并有待于事实的步步推进..." ("and depend on the step-by-step advancement of facts..."). These corrections are hereby made.

### 重要更正

1948-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　重要更正
    昨（二十九）日本报所登中央局“二一”指示，第二版第二栏第十七行括弧内“脑力劳动的知识分子”，排掉了“动”字应加上。倒数第二行“又不至耽误春耕”，“至”应改为致。又二十六日本报所载“蒋介石盗卖中国的新二十一条”一文中，第十条内“让美国……”系“让美军……”之误。所引英国“新国家与民族”周刊系“新政治家与民族”之误。又“并有计划有步骤的步步推进……”系“并有待于事实的步步推进……”之误。均此更正。
