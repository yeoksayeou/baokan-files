Title: US Imperialism Actively Invading South China: Large-Scale Port and Airfield Construction

Author:

Date: 1948-03-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th: According to a TASS dispatch from Shanghai on the 2nd: US imperialism is actively expanding its invasion base in South China and is planning to build a large port with a capacity of 30,000 tons in Luwang (transliteration), Zhongshan County, Guangdong, and to build a large-scale military airfield in Guangzhou. Shanghai Chiang Kai-shek's official newspapers revealed that the port's construction project will begin soon and is scheduled to be completed this year. It is also said that the United States has obtained the permission of the Portuguese authorities in Macau to cooperate in this plan. It is said that the US imperialists are building this port so that American ships can enter Guangzhou directly without going through Hong Kong.

### 美帝积极侵略华南　大规模筑港筑机场

1948-03-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　美帝积极侵略华南
  　大规模筑港筑机场
    【新华社陕北四日电】据塔斯社上海二日消息：美帝积极扩展华南侵略基地，正计划在广东中山县境内芦旺（译音）建立容量三万吨之大港，并于广州建立大规模之军用机场。上海蒋匪官方报纸透露，港口的建筑工程不久即将开始，预定本年内完成。并说美国已取得澳门葡帝当局在这一计划中与之合作的诺许。据说美帝建筑此一港口，是为了使美国轮船可不经香港直接进入广州。
