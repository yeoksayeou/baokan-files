Title: Generals Liu and Deng Send Telegram Congratulating on the Victory at Yichuan
Author:
Date: 1948-03-09
Page: 2

　　Generals Liu Bocheng and Deng Xiaoping
  　Telegraph Congratulating on the Victory at Yichuan
    Xinhua News Agency, Eyuwan Front, 7th. Generals Liu Bocheng and Deng Xiaoping, upon hearing of the great victory in the Huanglong Mountain area and at Yichuan, led all the commanders and fighters on the front line in sending congratulations. The original telegram read: "Comrades Dehuai, Zongxun, and Shoushan, and to all the commanders and fighters on the Northwest Front: Your great victory in annihilating five brigades of the Chiang bandits at Yichuan has filled our entire army with joy. We send this telegram to celebrate."
    Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 7th. The Northeast Central Bureau of the Communist Party of China, the Headquarters of the Northeast Military Region, and the Northeast Administrative Committee, on March 5th, sent a telegram to General Deputy Commander Peng Dehuai, Zhang Zongxun, and Zhao Shoushan of the Northwest People's Liberation Army, congratulating them on the great victory at Yichuan. The original telegram read: "The news of the great victory in the Huanglong Mountain area and at Yichuan has greatly inspired the military and civilians in the Northeast. We congratulate you on your great victory. We will do our utmost to completely and cleanly eliminate the Chiang bandit army in the Northeast as soon as possible, liberate the entire Northeast, and coordinate with your battle to liberate the Great Northwest."

### 刘邓两将军　电贺宜川大捷

1948-03-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　刘邓两将军
  　电贺宜川大捷
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线七日电】刘伯承、邓小平两将军，闻悉黄龙山区及宜川大捷，率前线全体指战员致贺，原电称：德怀、宗逊、寿山诸同志，并转西北前线全体指战员：你们在宜川消灭蒋匪五个旅的伟大胜利，使我们全军欢腾，特电庆贺。
    【新华社东北七日电】中共东北中央局、东北军区司令部、东北行政委员会，三月五日，电西北人民解放军总副司令彭德怀、张宗逊、赵寿山诸将军庆贺宜川大捷。原电称：黄龙山区及宜川大捷传来，东北军民极为振奋，祝贺你们的伟大胜利。我们将以全力争取早日全部干净消灭东北蒋匪军，解放全东北，配合你们解放大西北的战斗。
