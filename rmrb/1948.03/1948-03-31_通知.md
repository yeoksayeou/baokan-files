Date: 1948-03-31

　　Notice to all parents:
    The Fourth of April Children's Day is approaching. In order to celebrate this festival, our institution is holding a parent-teacher conference. In addition to reporting on the work of the past year, we hope that all parents will prepare their opinions and attend the meeting at that time.
　　　　Sincerely,
    　　　　　　　　Respectfully,
    　　　　　　　　　　　　Experimental Nursery (formerly Welfare Nursery)
