Title: Our Army on the Jiaoji Railway Recaptures Yidu
Author:
Date: 1948-03-27
Page: 2
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 25th: The People's Liberation Army recaptured the city of Yidu on the Jiaoji Railway on the evening of the 21st. The defending enemy abandoned the city and fled east. Our democratic government has entered the city to rebuild order.
    Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 25th: On the 17th, the People's Liberation Army repatriated twenty-six family members of Chiang bandit officers (major and captain level) captured in the Zhoucun battle to their hometowns. Among them were six family members of Chiang bandit officers, including Zhao Xiutang, a major battalion commander of the Chiang bandit 32nd division, and twenty family members of Chiang bandit junior officers, including Wan Jiaxiang, acting section chief of the Chiang bandit 10th supervision department. Before their departure, the People's Liberation Army provided them with sufficient travel expenses, dry rations, and passes.

### 胶济线我军　再克益都

1948-03-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　胶济线我军
  　再克益都
    【新华社华东前线二十五日电】解放军于二十一日晚收复胶济路上的益都城，守敌弃城东逃，我民主政府已进城重建秩序。
    【新华社华东前线二十五日电】十七日解放军在战地遣送周村战斗中被俘的蒋匪校尉级军官眷属二十六名回籍。其中有蒋匪整三十二师少校营长赵秀堂等校官眷属六名及蒋匪第十分监部代理科长万家祥等尉官眷属二十名。行前，解放军均发给充足的路费、干粮及通行证等。
