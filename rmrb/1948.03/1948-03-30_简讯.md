Brief News
To All Parents:
    The April 4th Children's Day is approaching. In order to celebrate this festival, our institute will hold a parent-teacher meeting. In addition to reporting on the work of the past year, we hope that all parents will prepare their opinions and attend the meeting.

    Respectfully,
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Experimental Nursery (formerly Welfare Nursery)
