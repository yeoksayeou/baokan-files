Title: U.S. Strikers Reach 500,000
Author:
Date: 1948-03-24
Page: 2

　　International Week

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd: New York dispatch: The number of strikers across the United States reached over 505,000 on the 19th. The miners' strike has forced the government to order a 25 percent reduction in railway transportation starting yesterday, a ban on anthracite exports next month, and some steel mills have begun to shut down blast furnaces. The strike of 100,000 meatpacking workers has also started to reduce meat supplies. It is reported that the police have intervened in the strike for the first time, arresting workers in Chicago.

### 美国罢工者增达五十万

1948-03-24
第2版()
专栏：国际一周

　　美国罢工者增达五十万
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】纽约讯：美全国罢工人数十九日已达五十万五千余人。矿工罢工已迫使政府下令自昨日起减少铁路运输百分之二十五，下月份禁止无烟煤出口，若干炼钢厂已开始关闭鼓风炉。十万肉类包装工人的罢工，使肉类供应亦开始减少。据悉，罢工中警察已首次实行干涉，在芝加哥逮捕了工人。
