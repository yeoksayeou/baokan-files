Title: Our Army on the Eyuwan Front Liberates Xixian and Yunmeng; Local Armed Forces in Guanzhong Launch a Surprise Attack on Chiang Kai-shek Bandits in @ County
Author:
Date: 1948-03-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Eyuwan Front, 2nd: (1) A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army recaptured Xixian County on the north bank of the Huai River on the morning of the 25th of last month. (2) The Jianghan Military Region's People's Liberation Army liberated Yunmeng County, northwest of Hankou, on the morning of the 22nd, capturing more than 30 Chiang Kai-shek soldiers and officers alive, seizing three light machine guns, more than 40 rifles and horses, and a portion of other military supplies. (3) A unit of the local armed forces of the Eyu Military Region annihilated the Luoshan County Joint Defense Team in Zhongwan, west of Nanxiangdian (southwest of Guangshan), on the morning of the 11th of last month, killing and wounding more than 20 people, capturing more than 20 people including Jin Boru, the head of the Joint Defense Team, seizing two light machine guns and more than 40 rifles.

Xinhua News Agency, Yu-Shaan-E Front, 2nd: A unit of the local armed forces of the Fourth Military Sub-district twice intercepted the invading Chiang Kai-shek 65th Division and the 4th Security Regiment, totaling more than 2,000 people, at Mancuan Pass on the 23rd of last month, killing or wounding more than 300 people, including a battalion commander. Another unit, from mid-month to the 27th, cleared out local Chiang Kai-shek bandits in the Dajiebao, Jinziling, and Shuanghekou areas southeast of Zhen'an, Shaanxi, capturing more than 80 local "Stick Brigade" bandits.

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 2nd: A unit of the local armed forces of the Guanzhong Military Sub-district and the @ County Detachment launched a surprise attack on the puppet @ County Self-Defense Battalion at Qiqiu at dawn on Lunar New Year's Eve (February 9th). After a fierce battle lasting 40 minutes, they killed or wounded 21 enemy soldiers including a company commander, captured 87 enemy soldiers including platoon leader Liu Fugao alive, and seized one Czech-style machine gun and 83 rifles. We suffered no casualties. In addition, the local armed forces and militia guerrillas in Guanzhong also achieved successive victories in the "anti-encirclement and suppression" struggle in January, fighting a total of 47 battles, killing or wounding 114 people, capturing 88 people alive, and seizing one heavy machine gun, 98 long and short guns, and a portion of other military supplies.

### 鄂豫皖前线我军　解放息县云梦两城　关中地武奔袭＠县蒋匪

1948-03-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　鄂豫皖前线我军
  　解放息县云梦两城
  　关中地武奔袭＠县蒋匪
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二日电】（一）解放军某部上月二十五日晨再克淮河北岸的息县城。（二）江汉军区解放军二十二日晨解放汉口西北的云梦县城，活捉蒋匪官兵三十余，缴获轻机枪三挺，步马枪四十余支，其他军用品一部。（三）鄂豫军区地方武装一部，上月十一日晨在南向店（光山西南）以西的中湾，歼灭罗山县联防队，毙伤二十余名，俘联防队长金伯如以下二十余名，缴轻机枪两挺，步枪四十余支。
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线二日电】四分区地方武装一部上月二十三日两度阻击进犯漫川关之蒋匪六十五师及保安第四团各一部共两千余人，先后毙伤其营长以下三百余名。另部于上月中旬至二十七日，在陕南镇安东南之大阶堡、衿子岭、双河口地区清剿土蒋匪，俘获土顽“棒子队”八十余名。
    【新华社西北二日电】关中分区地方武装一部与＠县支队于旧历除夕（二月九日）拂晓奔袭奇阜伪＠县自卫营，激战四十分钟，毙伤敌连长以下二十一名，生俘敌排长刘福高以下八十七名，缴获捷克式机枪一挺，步枪八十三支，我无伤亡。又，关中地方武装及民兵游击队在一月份反“清剿”斗争中亦迭获胜利，共作战四十七次，毙伤一百十四名，活捉八十八名，缴获重机枪一挺，长短枪九十八支及其他军用品一部。
