Title: Chinese Workers' Valiant Struggle: Limitless Admiration from Colombian Labor
Author:
Date: 1948-03-20
Page: 2

　　Our Workers' Valiant Struggle
  　Limitless Admiration from Colombian Labor
    【Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th】Late Dispatch: A resolution from the World Federation of Trade Unions headquarters in Paris, expressing the respect of Colombian workers to Chinese workers, the original text briefly states: "Dear comrades, at the Ninth National Labor Congress held in Cali, we passed a resolution to sincerely salute the glorious people of China, France, and Italy who are valiantly fighting against American imperialism and its international lackeys. Please regard this resolution as a sincere expression of the friendship of Colombian workers. President of the Ninth National Labor Congress, Antonio Ramirez. December 8, 1947."

### 我国工人英勇斗争　哥伦比亚劳工无限敬佩

1948-03-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　我国工人英勇斗争
  　哥伦比亚劳工无限敬佩
    【新华社陕北十七日电】迟到：世界职工联盟巴黎总部转来哥伦比亚工人向中国工人致敬的决议一件，原文略称：“亲爱的同志们，我们在卡里举行的第九届全国劳工大会通过决议，谨向正对美帝及其国际走卒进行英勇斗争的光荣的中国、法国及意大利人民衷心致敬。请将这个决议看作是哥伦比亚工人情谊的忠诚表示。第九届全国劳工大会主席安东尼·拉米瑞兹。一九四七年十二月八日。”
