Title: Our Jinzhong Local Troops Annihilate 300 of Yan's Bandits
Author:
Date: 1948-03-27
Page: 2

　　Our Jinzhong Local Troops
  　Annihilate 300 of Yan's Bandits
    [This Dispatch from Taihang, 23rd] The Taihang Second Military Sub-district troops won a victory in counterattacking the enemy, annihilating more than 140 enemy soldiers. On the 19th, a certain unit of our Second Military Sub-district, combined with two companies of the Taigu Independent Battalion, ambushed the area of Sigua, Xinzhuang, and Wanghui (more than ten li southeast of Taigu). The enemy's special agents discovered it, and at 9:00 AM, all of the 3rd Regiment of the 46th Division of Yan's bandits stationed in Taigu city and two battalions of the 2nd Regiment of the 9th Column, totaling more than 2,000 men, divided into two routes to surround our ambush area. Our troops calmly concealed themselves and did not move. When the enemy entered our ambush circle, our troops immediately charged the enemy's flank. The battle lasted about two and a half hours, killing and wounding more than 140 enemy soldiers, capturing 32 alive, seizing one mortar base, one heavy machine gun, four light machine guns, two small cannons, one grenade launcher, twenty-seven rifles and carbines, twenty-two bayonets, and 1,826 rounds of ammunition.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jin Sui, 25th] A certain unit of the Jinzhong local armed forces, on the 13th, in the Pinglu Lujiayuan area, more than 30 li southwest of Fenyang, routed a part of the 70th Division of Yan's bandit army that was looting grain, capturing more than 120 bandits including a company commander of the 210th Regiment, seizing five light machine guns, thirty-three rifles, and recovering fourteen head of livestock and more than ten thousand jin of grain, which was returned to the masses.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jin Sui, 25th] A part of the local armed forces on the border of Jin, Shaanxi, and Sui, on the 13th, in the Shijia Ge@ area, more than 30 li northwest of Shili Changtan, severely attacked Guan's bandit Zhu Wumei's unit, capturing Deputy Regiment Commander Zuo Yaofeng, Battalion Commander Dai Lun, and 62 others, killing and wounding more than 30 enemy soldiers including Deputy Battalion Commander Hua Haiquan, and seizing five small cannons, two light machine guns, and fifty-four long and short guns.

### 我晋中地方部队　歼灭阎匪三百

1948-03-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　我晋中地方部队
  　歼灭阎匪三百
    【本报太行二十三日电】太行二分区部队反击敌人获胜，歼敌一百四十多名。十九日我二分区某部结合太谷独立营两个连，设伏于四卦、新庄、王惠（太谷东南十余里）一带，被敌人特务发觉，上午九时驻太谷城之阎匪四十六师三团全部及九总队二团两个营共两千余人，便分两路向我伏击地区包围，我部队沉着隐蔽未动，待敌进到我伏击圈内时，我部队即向敌侧翼冲击，战斗约两小时半，毙伤敌一百四十余人，生俘三十二人，缴迫击炮座一个，重机枪一挺，轻机枪四挺，小炮两门，掷弹筒一个，步马枪二十七支，刺刀二十二把，子弹一千八百二十六发。
    【新华社晋绥二十五日电】晋中地方武装某部，十三日在汾阳西南三十余里之平陆芦家垣地区，击溃抢粮阎匪军七十师一部，俘匪二一○团连长以下一百二十余人、缴轻机枪五挺、步枪三十三支、夺回牲口十四头、粮食万余斤退还给群众。
    【新华社晋绥二十五日电】晋陕绥边境地方武装一部，十三日在十里长滩西北三十里之史家圪＠一带，痛击贯匪朱五美部，俘副团长左耀丰，营长戴论以下六十二人，毙伤敌副营长华海全以下三十余人，缴小炮五门，轻机枪两挺，长短枪五十四支。
