Author:
Date: 1948-03-17
Page: 1

　　Mass Blackboard
　　On the 13th, our Northeast People's Liberation Army won another great victory, capturing Sipingjie, the strategic center of the Chiang bandits in the Northeast. The Chiang bandits had constructed modern fortifications and bunkers in this place. We launched a general offensive, and after fighting for twenty-three hours, we completely wiped them out. More than 16,000 Chiang bandit troops defending the city were completely annihilated by us.

### 大众黑板

1948-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　大众黑板
    十三号咱们东北解放军又打了一个大胜仗，把东北蒋匪战略中心——四平街打下来了。蒋匪在这个地方，构筑有现代化的工事堡垒，咱们开始总攻，打了二十三个钟头就把他扫平了，守城蒋匪一万六千多人，被咱歼灭得干干净净。
