Title: Communication Exchange No. 19
Author:
Date: 1948-03-14
Page: 1

　　Communication Exchange
　　No. 19
　　One, to the Hetingpu Working Group in Licheng: Please write to us at any time about your organization of the poor peasant league, the situation of party rectification, and your experiences. Also, please tell us what conditions you based your classification of Hetingpu as a second-type village on, and what standards you used for class division.
　　Two, to the Xincheng Working Team in Shahe: Please provide continuous reports on your work after the public announcement of the branch. Please also write to us about any problems you encounter.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Our Newspaper Liaison Department

### 通讯往来　第十九号

1948-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来
  　第十九号
    一、黎城停河铺工作组：请将你们组织贫农团与整党情形和经验，随时写来。再，你们把停河铺划为第二类型村，系根据那些条件，阶级划分是根据什么标准，望告。
    二、沙河新城工作队：请将你们公开支部后的工作作连续报导，遇到什么问题也请写来。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
