Instructions on Land Reform, Party Consolidation, and the Democratic Movement

　　This book includes four documents guiding the current equal distribution work:
　　One, the CCP Central Committee's instructions on carrying out land reform and party consolidation work in old and semi-old areas.
　　Two, the CCP Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Central Bureau's instructions on land reform, party consolidation, and the democratic movement.
　　Three, the typical experience of Huangjiachuan in Suide in filling gaps and making improvements.
　　Four, the land reform experience in the old liberated area of Pingshan.
　　In order to meet the urgent needs of the current equal distribution work, these four documents are specially printed together in a single volume using large No. 4 font. Those who have previously purchased the "Handbook on Equal Distribution of Land" published by this bookstore urgently need to purchase one copy of this book per person to guide current actions.
　　Distributed by Xinhua Bookstore of North China
