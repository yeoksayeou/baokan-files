Xingtai Baomin Kerosene Factory
    High-Quality Refined Vegetable Kerosene for Sale in Large Quantities

    To our valued customers: Our factory, in order to resist foreign goods and develop production in the liberated areas, has specially refined vegetable kerosene. After repeated scientific experiments, the oil is pure, the light is bright, and it is durable, far superior to imported products. Since our factory's products have been available, they have been praised by all sectors of society as the dawn of the liberated areas. Recently, sales have been increasing daily, and supply cannot meet demand. Our factory, based on the spirit of wholeheartedly serving the people, and to thank our users from all walks of life, is striving for perfection in our products and manufacturing them in large quantities. We are striving for self-sufficiency in the liberated areas, with everyone using our especially low-priced products. If you wish to purchase, please go to No. 7 Daomen, Dongyang Market, Nanguan, Xingtai, to purchase as soon as possible, and don't miss this opportunity.

### 邢台保民煤油制造厂  精制植物煤油大批出售

1948-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　邢台保民煤油制造厂
    精制植物煤油大批出售
    敬启者，敝厂为抵制外货，发展解放区出产起见，特别精制植物煤油，屡经科学实验，油质纯洁，灯光明亮，并且耐用，远胜舶来品。敝厂成货以来，颇蒙各界赞扬，称为解放区之曙光，近来销路日广，供不应求，敝厂依诚心诚意为人民服务之精神，并鉴谢各界用户起见，成品精益求精大量制造，争取解放区自供自给，人人采用特别廉价出售，诸位如有愿购者，请到邢台南关东羊市道门牌七号从速购买，勿失良机。
