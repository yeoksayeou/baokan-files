Title: Mass Blackboard Has a Typo
Author:
Date: 1948-03-12
Page: 1

　　Mass Blackboard Has a Typo
　　Yesterday, in the fifth line of the "Mass Blackboard" column's text in this newspaper's masthead, the word "后" (hòu, after) in the sentence "清明前后分的地" (qīngmíng qiánhòu fēn de dì, land distributed around the Qingming Festival) was a mistake for the word "没" (méi, not). This is hereby corrected.

### 大众黑板有错字

　　大众黑板有错字
