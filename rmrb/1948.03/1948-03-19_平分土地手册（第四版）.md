　　Handbook on Land Redistribution (Fourth Edition)
　　This book is reprinted for the fourth time, incorporating the "Instructions of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on Land Reform and Party Consolidation in Old and Semi-Old Areas," "Instructions of the Central Bureau of the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu of the Communist Party of China on Land Reform, Party Consolidation and Democratic Movement," "Typical Experience of Huangjiachuan Supplement in Suide," and "Land Reform Experience in the Old Liberated Area of Pingshan," four documents. Also included is "Decision of the Central Bureau's Clean-up Committee on Cleaning Up Agency Shops and Agency Production."
