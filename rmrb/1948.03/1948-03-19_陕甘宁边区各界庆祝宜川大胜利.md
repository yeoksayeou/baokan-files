Title: Celebrations of the Great Victory at Yichuan by All Circles in the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region
Author:
Date: 1948-03-19
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 17th: All circles in the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region held a rally of ten thousand people in a certain place on the 10th to celebrate the great victory at Yichuan. Chairman Lin of the Border Government, Comrade Xi Zhongxun of the Northwest Bureau, Deputy Commander Wang of the Joint Defense Army, Vice Chairman Yang Mingxuan of the Border Government, Government Member Fang Wenli, President Li Furen of Yan'an University, Deputy Director Lu Zhiliang of the Border Women's Federation, model military families and women labor hero Liu Jinying, etc., all took the stage to address the meeting successively. They unanimously and excitedly called on the people of the Border Region to unite more closely, concentrate their efforts, win victories on the production front, actively support the front line to liberate the Northwest, rebuild the homes destroyed by the enemy, and restore the happy life they had cultivated for ten years. The conference also passed a telegram to Chairman Mao and all the commanders and fighters at the front to pay their respects.
Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 17th: After our army captured Baishui County on the 10th, it pursued the fleeing enemy and, at dawn on the 11th, completely annihilated the puppet Baishui County Security Police Team and the reactionary armed forces of Xinhua and Longshan townships in the southwest of Pucheng, capturing more than 250 people including the puppet Baishui County Chief Guo Ruisheng, and seizing two light machine guns and 160 rifles.

### 陕甘宁边区各界庆祝宜川大胜利

1948-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　陕甘宁边区各界庆祝宜川大胜利
    【新华社西北十七日电】陕甘宁边区各界于十日在某地举行万人祝捷大会，庆祝宜川大捷。边府林主席、西北局习仲勋同志、联防军王副司令员、边府副主席杨明轩、政府委员房文礼、延大校长李敷仁、边妇联副主任路志亮、模范军属及妇女劳动英雄刘金英等，均先后登台致辞。一致兴奋的号召边区人民更加亲密团结起来，集中力量，在生产战线上打胜仗，积极支援前线解放大西北，重建被敌人破坏的家园，恢复十年经营的幸福生活。大会并通过致电毛主席及前线全体指战员致敬。
    【新华社西北十七日电】我军十日攻克白水县城后，跟踪追击逃敌，十一日拂晓，在蒲城西南地区全歼伪白水县保警队及该县新化、龙山两乡反动武装，计生俘伪白水县长郭瑞生以下二百五十余人，缴获轻机枪两挺、步枪一百六十支。
