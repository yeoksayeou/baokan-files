Title: Local Armed Forces of Hebei-Shandong-Henan Liberate Dong'a and Pingyin

Author:
Date: 1948-03-25
Page: 2

　　Local Armed Forces of Hebei-Shandong-Henan
  　Recapture Dong'a and Pingyin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hebei-Shandong-Henan, 23rd] The enemy's Second Security Brigade and the puppet Sixth Special Office, entrenched in the counties of Dong'a and Pingyin, were forced to flee to Jinan on the 13th and 14th of this month under the siege and attacks of our local armed forces. The two counties were then recovered by us, and our democratic government is now actively carrying out rehabilitation work.

### 冀鲁豫地方武装　收复东阿平阴

1948-03-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀鲁豫地方武装
  　收复东阿平阴
    【新华社冀鲁豫二十三日电】盘踞东阿、平阴两县城之敌保安第二总队及伪第六专署在我地方武装围困打击下，于本月十三、十四两日分别向济南窜撤，该两县城遂为我收复，现我民主政府正在积极进行善后工作。
