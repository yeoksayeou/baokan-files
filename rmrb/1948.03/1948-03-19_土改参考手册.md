　　Land Reform Reference Manual
    The Central Bureau's instruction on February 1st urged comrades leading and participating in land reform not to commit commandism or tailism. Only by learning from the masses while also raising the awareness of the masses can we truly be good "long-term workers" for the masses.

    To enable comrades participating in land reform to follow this instruction from the Central Bureau, to diligently cultivate and learn, and to both connect with the masses and adhere to principles, we have specially compiled this manual for comrades' reference.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　Distributed by Xinhua Bookstore, North China

### 土改参考手册

1948-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　土改参考手册
    二月一日中央局指示里，嘱咐领导与参加土改的同志，既不要犯命令主义，也不要犯尾巴主义，只有既向群众学习，同时还要提高群众的，才能真正为群众作一个好长工。
    为了使参加土改的同志，能按照中央局这一指示，努力修养、学习，既能联系群众，又能坚持原则，我们特辑这一手册，供同志们参考。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　华北新华书店发行
