Title: Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Local Armed Forces and Militia in One Year of Combat: Annihilate 18,000 Chiang Kai-shek Bandits

Author:
Date: 1948-03-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 26th: The headquarters of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia-Jin Sui Joint Defense Command issued the following comprehensive battle results of the local armed forces and militia in the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region over the past year (from March 1947 to March 15, 1948) on March 15th: A. Battle achievements of the local army: 1. 332 combats. 2. 5,565 enemies killed or wounded, 8,951 captured alive, 850 surrendered, totaling 15,366 enemies annihilated. 3. Captured 5,271 rifles and carbines, 3 rifle grenade launchers, 199 light and heavy machine guns, 58 submachine guns, Sten guns, and carbines, 1 mountain gun, 1 rocket launcher, 10 mortars, 16 60mm mortars, and 74 grenade launchers. 4. Captured ammunition: 587,235 rounds of machine gun and rifle ammunition, 1,426 artillery shells, 163 grenades, 8,952 hand grenades, and 14,765 rifle grenades. 5. Captured 336 war horses, 1,495 head of livestock, 3 radio stations, 1 field radio, 29 telephones, 4,070 catties of wire, 7 binoculars, 2,099 shi of grain, and 203 bolts of cloth. 6. Recovered the two cities of Ganquan and Anse, shot down two enemy planes, and destroyed 29 vehicles. B. Battle achievements of the militia: According to incomplete statistics, 947 combats, 900 enemies killed or wounded, 1,623 enemies and secret agents captured, totaling 2,523 enemies annihilated. Captured 380 rifles and carbines, 19 light machine guns, 8 Sten guns and carbines, 124,630 rounds of ammunition, 115 artillery shells, 1,266 hand grenades, 198 rifle grenades, 22 war horses, 49 head of livestock, 1 field radio, 6 telephones, 145 shi of grain, 790 catties of wire, 1 binocular, destroyed 1,048 enemy碉堡, and breached 21 enemy strongholds.

### 陕甘宁地武民兵一年作战　歼灭蒋匪万八千

1948-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　陕甘宁地武民兵一年作战
  　歼灭蒋匪万八千
    【新华社西北二十六日电】陕甘宁晋绥联防军区司令部在三月十五日发布陕甘宁边区一年来（自四七年三月至四八年三月十五）地武民兵的综合战绩如下：甲、地方军战绩：一、作战三三二三次。二、毙伤敌人五五六五名，生俘八九五一名，投诚八五○名，共计歼敌一五三六六名。三、缴获长短枪五二七一支、枪榴筒三个、轻重机枪一九九挺、手提机枪、司登枪、卡宾枪五八支、山炮一门、火箭炮一门、迫击炮十门、六○炮一六门、掷弹筒七四个。四、缴获弹药：机步枪弹五八七二三五发、炮弹一四二六发、掷弹一六三发、手榴弹八九五二颗、枪榴弹一四七六五发。五、缴获战马三三六匹、牲口一四九五头、电台三部、报话机一部、电话机二九部、电线四○七○斤、望远镜七个、粮食二○九九石、布二○三匹。六、收复甘泉、安塞两城，击落敌机两架、击毁汽车二九辆。乙、民兵战绩：据不完全统计，作战九四七次、毙伤敌九○○名，俘敌及特务一六二三名，共计歼敌二五二三名。缴获长短枪三八○支、轻机枪一九挺、司登枪、卡宾枪八支、子弹一二四六三○发、炮弹一一五发、手榴弹一二六六颗、枪榴弹一九八发、战马二二匹、牲口四九头、报话机一部、电话机六部、粮食一四五石、电线七九○斤、望远镜一个、毁敌碉堡一○四八座、破敌城寨二一座。
