Title: Local Armed Forces in Southern Shaanxi Wipe Out Over 1,000 Chiang Bandits; Our Army in the Jianghan Military Region Eliminates Over 700 Enemies

Author:
Date: 1948-03-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Henan, Shaanxi, and Hubei, 28th: From the 20th to the 24th, the local armed forces of the Fourth Sub-district cleared out scattered bandits in the Southern Shaanxi and Northwestern Hubei areas. On the 20th, they routed the 479th Regiment of the 160th Brigade of Chiang's 65th Division in the Shanyang area. In total, over five days, they killed or wounded over 50 bandits, captured 952 bandits below the rank of battalion commander, seized 2 cannons, 40 light and heavy machine guns, 10 grenade launchers, 650 rifles and pistols, over 30,000 rounds of ammunition, and one radio station.

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Hubei, Henan, and Anhui, 27th: Delayed news: A unit of the local armed forces of Jinzhai County (i.e., Lihuang), on the 8th, in the Niushifan and Fotang'ao areas, over 50 li southwest of the county, encircled and annihilated local Chiang bandits. Within three days, they completely wiped out over 200 fleeing bandits, killing or wounding over 60 enemies, capturing over 140 bandit officers and soldiers, and seizing over 100 rifles. In addition, on the 1st, local armed forces of the Hubei-Henan Military Region attacked the Xuweizi stronghold of Chiang bandits north of Popi River (45 li southwest of Guangshan), capturing 12 enemies.

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Hubei, Henan, and Anhui, 28th: Delayed news: A unit of the local armed forces of the Western Anhui Military Region, on the 8th, at Shitouzu, over 40 li north of Lujiang, routed a unit of Chiang's bandit security team, killing, wounding, or capturing over 20 enemies and seizing over 20 rifles. Furthermore, a certain unit of our local armed forces active south of Tongcheng, on the 9th, defeated the Binhai and other two township public offices, capturing 35 personnel including deputy squad leaders, and seizing 28 rifles.

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Hubei, Henan, and Anhui, 28th: A certain unit of the Jianghan Military Region Liberation Army, on the 20th, launched a surprise attack on the 97th Regiment of Chiang's 33rd Brigade, which had infiltrated Yingshan, over 40 li northwest of Yujia Shop. After half an hour of fierce fighting, they killed or wounded over 120 enemies including two company commanders, captured over 400 enemy officers and soldiers, seized 2 mortars, 7 heavy machine guns, 8 grenade launchers, over 190 rifles and pistols, over 600 rounds of various shells, over 92,000 rounds of various bullets, one radio station, and other spoils of war.

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Hubei, Henan, and Anhui, 28th: A certain unit of the Jianghan Liberation Army, on the 15th, near Bian River @, 90 li east of Jianli, routed a unit of the 92nd Brigade of Chiang's 69th Division that had attacked from Jianli City, killing over 30 enemies, capturing over 154 enemy officers and soldiers including a company commander, seizing one 82mm mortar, 12 light and heavy machine guns, 2 grenade launchers, 6 submachine guns, over 70 rifles and carbines, and over 40,000 rounds of ammunition.

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Hubei, Henan, and Anhui, 28th: The Jianghan Military Region Liberation Army's results for February: They liberated the five county towns of Qianjiang, Mianyang, Yingshan, Yunmeng, and Anlu, and annihilated seven squadrons of Chiang's bandit security teams in Anlu, Yunmeng, and other counties, two companies of the First Regiment of Hunan New Security, the Sixth Branch of the Hubei River Police, and seventeen township public offices, totaling 1,671 enemies eliminated, of which 1,421 were captured alive, and 251 Chiang bandits below the rank of Anlu County Chief were killed or wounded. They seized 55 light and heavy machine guns, 1,447 rifles, 2 Sten guns, 348 rounds of various shells, over 200,000 rounds of ammunition, one speedboat, two cars, seven radio stations, and 48 telephones.

### 陕南地武歼蒋匪千余　江汉军区我军歼敌七百多

1948-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　陕南地武歼蒋匪千余
  　江汉军区我军歼敌七百多
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线二十八日电】四分区地方武装二十日至二十四日间在陕南与鄂西北地区清剿散匪，并于二十日在山阳地区击溃蒋匪六十五师一六○旅四七九团，总计五天中毙伤匪五十余名，生俘匪营长以下九百五十二名，缴炮二门，轻重机枪四十挺，掷弹筒十个，长短枪六百五十支，子弹三万余发，电台一部。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十七日电】迟到消息：我金寨县（即立煌）地方武装一部，八日在该县西南五十余里之牛食畈、佛堂坳等地区，围歼土蒋匪，三天内将流窜之二百余名土匪全部歼灭。计毙伤敌六十余，俘匪官兵一百四十余名，缴获步枪百余支。另鄂豫军区地方武装一日袭击泼皮河（光山西南四十五里）以北许围子蒋匪据点，俘敌十二名。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十八日电】迟到消息：皖西军区地方武装一部，八日在庐江以北四十余里之石头咀，击溃蒋匪保安队一部，计毙伤俘敌二十余名，缴步枪二十余支。又活跃于桐城以南的我地方武装某部，九日在盛家桥打垮敌滨海等两个乡公所，俘队附以下三十五名，缴步枪二十八支。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十八日电】江汉军区解放军某部，二十日奔袭窜犯应山西北四十余里余家店的蒋匪三十三旅九七团，激战半小时，毙伤敌连长两名以下一百二十余，俘敌官兵四百余，缴获迫击炮二门，重机枪七挺，掷弹筒八个，长短枪一百九十余支，各种炮弹六百余发，各种子弹九万二千余发，电台一部，其他胜利品一部。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十八日电】江汉解放军某部十五日在监利以东九十里的汴河＠附近，击溃由监利城出犯的蒋匪六九师九二旅一部，计毙敌三十余、俘敌连长以下官兵一百五十四名、缴获八二迫击炮一门、轻重机枪十二挺、掷弹筒两个、手提机枪六支、步马枪七十余支、子弹四万余发。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十八日电】江汉军区解放军二月份战果。计：解放潜江、沔阳、应山、云梦、安陆五座县城，歼灭蒋匪安陆、云梦等县保安队七个中队，湖南新保安第一团两个连，湖北水上警察第六分局及十七个乡公所，共歼敌一六七一名，其中生俘一四二一名，毙伤蒋匪安陆县长以下二百五十一名，缴获轻重机枪五十五挺，步枪一四四七支，司登式两支，各种炮弹三四八发，子弹二十余万发，汽艇一艘，汽车两辆，电台七部，电话机四八部。
