Title: General Zhao Shoushan Appointed Deputy Commander of the Northwest Field Army
Author:
Date: 1948-03-02
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 28th: Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief and Peng Dehuai, Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the People's Liberation Army, recently appointed the famous Northwest General Zhao Shoushan as the Second Deputy Commander of the People's Liberation Army Northwest Field Army. General Zhao is from ＠ County, Shaanxi Province. He was a senior general under General Yang Hucheng, and served as the commander of the former Kuomintang 38th Army and commander-in-chief of the Third Army Group. In order to oppose Chiang Kai-shek's betrayal of the country and elimination of dissidents, and to get rid of the surveillance of secret agents, General Zhao arrived in the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Liberated Area in early July last year after many twists and turns. He arrived in the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region on September 1st of the autumn. After being appointed this time, Zhao immediately rushed to the front line to command operations. The commander of the Northwest Field Army is concurrently held by General Peng Dehuai, Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the People's Liberation Army, and the First Deputy Commander is General Zhang Zongxun.

### 赵寿山将军　荣任西北野战军副司令员

1948-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　赵寿山将军
  　荣任西北野战军副司令员
    【新华社西北二十八日电】人民解放军朱彭总副司令，近任命西北名将赵寿山将军为人民解放军西北野战军第二副司令员。赵将军为陕西＠县人，曾为杨虎城将军高级将领，历任前国民党三十八军军长及第三集团军总司令等职。赵将军为反对蒋贼卖国消灭异己，摆脱特务多方监视，于去年七月初辗转抵达晋冀鲁豫解放区。去秋九月一日抵达陕甘宁边区。赵氏此次受任后即赶赴前线指挥作战。按西北野战军司令员由人民解放军副总司令彭德怀将军兼任，第一副司令员为张宗逊将军。
