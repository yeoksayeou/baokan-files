Title: Ho Chi Minh Calls on Civil Servants to Engage in Production
Author: 
Date: 1948-03-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 25th. According to a broadcast by the Viet Minh Republic government radio station "Voice of Vietnam" a few days ago, President Ho Chi Minh recently issued a call to civil servants in various agencies in the Vietnamese liberated areas, requesting them to launch a production campaign, become self-sufficient, improve their lives, reduce the burden on the people, and support the long-term war of liberation.

### 胡志明号召公务员生产

1948-03-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　胡志明号召公务员生产
    【新华社陕北二十五日电】据越盟共和国政府电台“越南之声”日前广播，胡志明总统近向越南解放区各机关公务人员发出号召，要求他们展开生产运动，自给自足，改善生活，减轻人民负担，以支持长期的解放战争。
