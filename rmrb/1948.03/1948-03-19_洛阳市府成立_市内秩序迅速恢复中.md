Title: Luoyang Municipal Government Established: City Order Rapidly Returning to Normal

Author:

Date: 1948-03-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline, Henan-Shaanxi-Hubei, 17th — The Henan-Shaanxi-Hubei Border Region Administrative Office has appointed Yang Shaoqiao as the Mayor of Luoyang. On the 13th, while the People's Liberation Army was still carrying out the final battle to eliminate the remaining enemies in the city, Mayor Yang led personnel from various municipal departments into the city, announced the government's program, comforted the citizens, and opened the prison to release more than 700 innocent people who had been imprisoned by the Chiang bandits. The municipal bureaus also began work that day. On the 14th, after the establishment of the district offices in the urban area, they immediately began investigating and registering refugees who had suffered from the Chiang disaster, preparing for relief. Upon hearing the news, the people of the city were overjoyed and gathered in the streets to read the municipal government's announcements. Currently, the citizens are assisting the municipal government in investigating and reporting hidden stragglers and Chiang's special agents, rapidly restoring normal order.

### 洛阳市府成立　市内秩序迅速恢复中

1948-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　洛阳市府成立
  　市内秩序迅速恢复中
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线十七日电】豫陕鄂边区行政公署任命杨少桥为洛阳市长。十三日当解放军最后肃清城内残敌的战斗尚在进行时，杨市长即率领所属各机关人员入城，布告施政纲领，抚慰市民，并开狱解放被蒋匪所囚禁的无辜人民七百余人。市府各局亦均于当日开始工作。十四日，城区区公所成立后，即进行调查登记遭受蒋灾的难民，准备救济，全城人民闻讯，欢喜若狂，群集街头，争阅市府布告。现市民正协助市府清查与检举隐藏的散兵及蒋特，迅速恢复正常秩序。
