　　Correspondence (No. 21)
    Nancheng Working Group, Changzhi:
    Please continue to report on the situation of your reorganized team. We hope you can specifically introduce the facts and experiences of combining public propaganda with individual mobilization, and combining party rectification with democracy. Based on the characteristics of villages like Nancheng, we hope you can reflect the masses' conscious process, combined with the working group's self-criticism, to provide experiences and lessons.
    Dongmazhuang Working Group, Wu'an:
    Please continue to organize and send us information about your party rectification.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Our Newspaper's Correspondence Department

### 通讯往来（第廿一号）

1948-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来（第廿一号）长治南呈工作组：
    请把你们整编队伍的情形，继续报导。希具体介绍你们公开宣传与个别发动结合、整党与民主结合进行的事实与经验，并希根据南呈这种类型村的特点，反映出群众的自觉过程，结合工作组的检讨，提供经验教训。武安东马庄工作组：
    请把你们整党的情况，继续整理寄给我们。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
