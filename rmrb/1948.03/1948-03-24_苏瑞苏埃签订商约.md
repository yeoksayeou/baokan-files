Title: Soviet-Swiss Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1948-03-24
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 21st: Moscow Dispatch: The Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade and the Swiss trade delegation concluded a trade agreement and exchange of goods agreement on the 17th, establishing the principles for developing economic relations between the two countries. Switzerland has also agreed to the establishment of a Soviet commercial delegation in Bern. The exchange of goods agreement stipulates that the Soviet Union will exchange grain, timber, and other goods for Swiss generators, electrical equipment, machine tools, and other goods. Furthermore, the Soviet Union recently signed a trade agreement with Egypt in Cairo, stipulating that Egypt will exchange cotton for Soviet grain, timber, and other goods. Both parties agreed to grant each other most-favored-nation status in mutual trade before the conclusion of a general commercial treaty.

### 苏瑞苏埃签订商约

1948-03-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏瑞苏埃签订商约
    【新华社陕北廿一日电】莫斯科讯：苏联对外贸易部与瑞士贸易代表团于十七日缔结商约及换货协定，建立了发展两国经济关系的原则，瑞士并已同意苏联在伯尔尼成立商务代表团。换货协定规定苏联以谷物、木材和其他货物，交换瑞士的发电机、电气装备、机器工具及其他货物。又讯：苏联最近并与埃及在开罗签订了贸易协定，规定埃及以棉花交换苏联的谷物、木材等货物。双方协议：在一般商约缔结之前，在相互贸易中均予对方以最惠国待遇。
