The military family letters broadcast by this station in January and February are mostly addressed to the Third and Sixth Columns, with very few addressed to the First and Second Columns. Therefore, it is hoped that local comrades in the Jinnan and Ji-Yu-Lu regions will mobilize the families of soldiers in the First and Second Columns to write letters to the officers and soldiers at the front.
