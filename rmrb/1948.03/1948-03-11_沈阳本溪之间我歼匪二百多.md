Title: Over 200 Bandits Eliminated Between Shenyang and Benxi
Author: 
Date: 1948-03-11
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 9th. Belated news: A certain unit of the Andong Local Corps launched an attack on the isolated stronghold of Waitou Shandong Station on the central section of the Shenyang-Benxi line on the 20th of last month. After fierce fighting, they completely annihilated the defending enemy, the 3rd Company of the 2nd Regiment of the 1st Brigade of the 207th Division (this company was a special company, with three rifle platoons, one heavy machine gun platoon, one small artillery platoon, and two traffic police platoons). Over 30 enemies were killed or wounded, and over 180 enemies, including a deputy battalion commander, were captured. They seized three heavy machine guns, eleven light machine guns, three 60mm mortars, and over 150 long and short guns. Another local corps began its advance on the Shenyang-Benxi road on the 8th of last month. As of the 23rd, they had controlled the line from Lishudixia in the south to Fubeijiatun in the north, a distance of over 100 li, and had destroyed all the telegraph wires, iron bridges, stone bridges, large and small tunnels, and stations along the line.

### 沈阳本溪之间我歼匪二百多

1948-03-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　沈阳本溪之间我歼匪二百多
    【新华社东北九日电】迟到消息：安东地方兵团某部，上月二十日向沈（阳）本（溪）线中段蒋匪仅有的孤立据点歪头山东站发动攻击，经激战后，将守敌二○七师一旅二团三连（该连系特种连，下设三个步枪排，一个重机枪排，一个小炮排及两个交警排）全部歼灭，毙伤敌三十余名，俘敌副营长以下一百八十名。缴重机枪三挺，轻机枪十一挺，六○炮三门，长短枪一百五十余支。另部地方兵团自上月八日开始向沈（阳）本（溪）路进击，截至二十三日止，共控制该线南自梨树底下，至富北家屯一带，长达百余里，将沿线所有电线、铁桥、石桥、大小车洞及车站等全部破坏。
