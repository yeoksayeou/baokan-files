Title: Finnish Delegation Arrives in Moscow; Soviet-Finnish Negotiations on Mutual Assistance Treaty
Author:
Date: 1948-03-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 27th: Moscow dispatch: The Finnish government delegation arrived in the Soviet Union on the 22nd to negotiate the signing of a mutual assistance treaty between the two countries. The head of the delegation, Finnish Prime Minister Pekkala, was delayed due to illness but arrived in the Soviet capital on the 24th. Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov and Deputy Foreign Minister Zorin warmly welcomed him at the airport. After disembarking, Pekkala delivered a brief speech stating, "I visited Moscow last time during the 30th anniversary of the October Revolution and have a full understanding of the great achievements of the Soviet Union's 30 years of construction. This is due to the highly talented Soviet people and their wise leader, Stalin." On the same day, Pekkala held talks with Molotov.
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 27th: Budapest dispatch: Hungary and Yugoslavia recently signed a one-year trade agreement, with Hungary exchanging light industrial products for timber, oil, and iron ore from Yugoslavia. Hungary also signed a one-year trade agreement with Austria in Vienna, exchanging grain, vegetables, and lignite for timber, manganese, and semi-finished metal products from Austria. The trade volume between the two countries this year will reach five million US dollars, an increase of 1.5 million US dollars compared to last year. Furthermore, a Soviet commercial delegation has arrived in Budapest, and the two countries have begun economic and financial negotiations. According to Warsaw, Romania signed a Romanian-Polish cultural cooperation agreement at the end of last month and agreed to sign a mutual assistance treaty soon. Romania also plans to sign a new trade agreement with Bulgaria, and a Bulgarian commercial delegation has arrived in the Romanian capital for negotiations.

### 芬代表团到达莫斯科　苏芬商订互助条约

1948-03-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　芬代表团到达莫斯科
  　苏芬商订互助条约
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】莫斯科讯：芬兰政府代表团已于二十二日抵苏，谈判两国签订互助条约的事宜，该代表团团长芬总理皮可拉因病迟延行期，亦已于二十四日飞抵苏京，苏外长莫洛托夫，外次佐林等均亲临机场，热烈欢迎。皮氏下机后，发表简短演说称：“我上次在十月革命三十周年时，曾访问莫斯科，对苏联三十年建设的伟大成就具有充分认识，这是具有高度天才的苏联人民及其英明领袖斯大林的功绩。”当天，皮氏即与莫洛托夫举行会谈。
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】布达佩斯讯：匈牙利与南斯拉夫已于日前签订一年换货协定，匈将以轻工业制成品与南交换木材、石油与铁矿砂。匈牙利同时并与奥地利在维也纳签订一年换货协定，匈将以粮食、蔬菜、褐煤与奥交换木材、锰与半制金属品等。今年两国贸易额将达五百万美元，较去年增加一百五十万美元。又苏联商务代表团已抵布达佩斯，两国已开始经济与财政谈判。另据华沙讯：罗马尼亚于上月底与波兰签订罗波文化合作协定，并同意最近即签订互助条约。罗马尼亚最近尚拟与保加利亚签订新商约，保商务代表团已抵罗京洽商中。
