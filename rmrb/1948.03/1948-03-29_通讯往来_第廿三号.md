　　Correspondence

　　No. 23

　　Central Bureau, Jinnan Work Group: Comrades Zhu Xiaocheng, Jiang Dongping, and Huangmei:

　　Please organize typical reports about how you started to mobilize poor peasants and hired laborers, reorganize teams, and launch the democratic party rectification movement after you went to the countryside; in addition to writing yourselves, please also encourage comrades from various groups to write more articles for the newspaper.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Our Newspaper's Liaison Department

### 通讯往来　第廿三号

1948-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来
  　第廿三号中央局冀南工作团：朱效成、江东平、荒煤诸同志：
    请把你们下乡后，如何着手发动贫雇，整编队伍，开展民主整党运动的情况和经验，组织典型报导；除请你们亲自动手写，并望发动各组同志多多给报纸写稿。
    　　　       　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
