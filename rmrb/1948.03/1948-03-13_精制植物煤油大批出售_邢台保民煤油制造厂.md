Author:
Date: 1948-03-13
Page: 1

Refined Vegetable Kerosene for Sale in Large Quantities

　　Xingtai Baomin Kerosene Factory

　　To our esteemed customers, in order to resist foreign goods and develop production in the liberated areas, our factory has specially refined vegetable kerosene. After repeated scientific experiments, the oil is pure and the light is bright and long-lasting, far surpassing imported products. Since our factory's products have been launched, they have been praised by all sectors, hailed as the dawn of the liberated areas. Recently, sales have been increasing daily, and supply cannot meet demand. Our factory, in the spirit of wholeheartedly serving the people and to thank our customers, is striving for excellence in our finished products and manufacturing in large quantities, striving for self-sufficiency in the liberated areas, and offering it for sale at a particularly low price for everyone to use. If you wish to purchase, please come to No. 7, Dongyang City, Daomen, South Gate, Xingtai, to purchase as soon as possible, and do not miss this opportunity.

### 精制植物煤油大批出售　邢台保民煤油制造厂

1948-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　精制植物煤油大批出售
  　邢台保民煤油制造厂
    敬启者，敝厂为抵制外货，发展解放区出产起见，特别精制植物煤油，屡经科学实验，油质纯洁，灯光明亮，并且耐用，远胜舶来品。敝厂成货以来，颇蒙各界赞扬，称为解放区之曙光，近来销路日广，供不应求，敝厂依诚心诚意为人民服务之精神，并答谢各界用户起见，成品精益求精大量制造，争取解放区自供自给，人人采用特别廉价出售，诸位如有愿购者，请到邢台南关东羊市道门牌七号从速购买，勿失良机。
