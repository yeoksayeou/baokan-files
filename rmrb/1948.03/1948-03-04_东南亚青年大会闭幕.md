Title: Southeast Asia Youth Conference Concludes
Author:
Date: 1948-03-04
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 2nd. A dispatch from Calcutta states that the Southeast Asia Youth Conference concluded on the 26th after a seven-day session. Soviet representative Kharamov, in his closing address, emphasized the necessity of unity among the world's democratic youth. Australian representative Ms. Bakon delivered a speech, calling on youths from all countries to support the people of Indonesia and Vietnam in their fight for independence and liberation. The conference unanimously passed a resolution: denouncing the imperialisms of the United States, Britain, the Netherlands, and France, and urging Southeast Asian youth to unite.

### 东南亚青年大会闭幕

1948-03-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　东南亚青年大会闭幕
    【新华社陕北二日电】加尔各答讯，东南亚青年大会开会七日后，已于二十六日闭幕，苏联代表哈拉莫夫在闭幕会上致词时，强调指出世界民主青年团结的必要。澳洲代表巴康女士发表演讲，号召各国青年支援为独立解放而战斗的印尼、越南人民。大会一致通过决议：痛斥美、英、荷、法帝国主义、并敦促东南亚青年团结起来。
