Title: The Chinese Revolution and the Chinese Communist Party by Mao Zedong
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1948-03-22
Page: 1

　   The Chinese Revolution and the Chinese Communist Party
      By Mao Zedong
    This is an article written by Chairman Mao in the past for "The Communist" (the CCP Central Committee's Party journal). From the analysis of Chinese society to the analysis of China's revolutionary movement over the past century, Chairman Mao profoundly and clearly pointed out the targets of the Chinese revolution, the tasks of the Chinese revolution, the driving force of the Chinese revolution, the nature of the Chinese revolution, and the transformation of the future of the Chinese revolution. Chairman Mao said: "This is China's 'national condition'. To understand China's national condition is the basic basis for understanding all revolutionary issues."
    All revolutionary workers and comrades participating in completing the basic task of the Chinese revolution—land reform—must carefully study this work by Chairman Mao.

### 中国革命与中国共产党　毛泽东著

1948-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

　   中国革命与中国共产党
      毛泽东著
    这是毛主席过去在“共产党人”（中共中央党刊）上所写的文章。从对中国社会的分析，到对中国百年来革命运动的分析，毛主席深刻明确的指出了中国革命的对象、中国革命的任务、中国革命的动力，中国革命的性质与中国革命的前途转变。毛主席说：“这是中国的‘国情’，认清中国的国情，乃是认清一切革命问题的基本根据。”
    一切革命工作者，参加完成中国革命基本任务——土地改革的同志，均须仔细研读毛主席这部著作。
