Title: U.S. Imperialism Sends Agricultural Advisory Group to China
Author:
Date: 1948-03-01
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 27th: According to a Tianjin newspaper in mid-February, U.S. imperialism, under the guise of the United Nations Food and Agriculture Organization, is sending a "special advisory group" to China to supervise Chinese agricultural production. The contract has already been signed. The group consists mainly of American agricultural technicians, and is headed by a man named Cummins.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 27th: Washington News: The U.S. State Department revealed in a report to the U.S. Congress on the 24th that the U.S. Maritime Commission approved a loan of $16.65 million to the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang in April of last year for the purpose of purchasing American ships. Of this amount, $14.4 million has already been paid, and another $2.1 million is available for payment at any time. According to reports, in July of last year, the commission sold 18 ships to the Chiang bandits for $6,946,280 to aid Chiang's civil war; on February 18th, the commission approved the sale of 12 ships (some say 22 ships) to the Chiang bandits for $9,948,876, which are expected to be delivered to Chiang for use by the end of March.

### 美帝派农业顾问团来华

1948-03-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　美帝派农业顾问团来华
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】据上月中旬津报载：美帝假借联合国粮食农业组织名义派遣“特别顾问团”来华，监督中国农业生产，合同已经订立。该团团员多为美国农业技术人员，团长名康民斯。
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】华盛顿消息：美国务院二十四日向美国会报告中，曾透露美航务委员会曾于去年四月批准贷给蒋匪一千六百五十万美元，作为购买美舰船之用。其中一千四百四十万元已经付给，另外二百一十万元随时可付给。按去年七月该会为援蒋内战，曾卖给蒋匪十八艘船，价六百九十四万六千二百八十美元；二月十八日该会又批准卖给蒋匪舰只十二艘（一说二十二艘），价为九百九十四万八千八百七十六元，预计三月底可交蒋使用。
