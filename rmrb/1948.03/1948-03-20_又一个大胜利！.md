Title: Another Great Victory!
Author:
Date: 1948-03-20
Page: 1

　　Another Great Victory!
    On the Shandong front, our People's Liberation Army launched a powerful spring offensive on the Jiaoji Railway and achieved a great victory. In two days, over 18,000 Chiang Kai-shek bandits were annihilated, of which over 16,000 were captured alive. In addition, the important towns of Zhoucun and Zhangdian, which are industrial and commercial centers, and the three cities of Zouping, Qidong, and Changshan, as well as many railway stations, were recovered. Now our army's offensive is advancing forward.

### 又一个大胜利！

1948-03-20
第1版()
专栏：大众黑板

　　又一个大胜利！
    华东前线咱们的解放军，在胶济路上发动了强大的春季攻势，得到很大的胜利。两天内消灭蒋匪一万八千多名，其中有一万六千多名是活捉住的。另外还收复了工商业中心周村、张店两个重要大镇，和邹平、齐东、长山三个城，还有许多车站。现在我军攻势正在向前开展。
