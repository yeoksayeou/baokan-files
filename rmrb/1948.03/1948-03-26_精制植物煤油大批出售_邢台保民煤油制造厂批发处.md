　　Refined Vegetable Kerosene for Sale in Bulk

　　Xingtai Baomin Kerosene Factory Wholesale Department

　　To our valued customers: In order to resist foreign goods and develop production in the liberated areas, we have specially refined vegetable kerosene. Through repeated scientific experiments, the oil is pure and the light is bright. It is also durable and far superior to imported products. Since our products have become available, they have been praised by all sectors, and hailed as the dawn of the liberated areas. Recently, sales have been increasing daily, and demand exceeds supply. In the spirit of wholeheartedly serving the people, and to thank all users, we are striving for perfection in our finished products, manufacturing in large quantities, striving for self-sufficiency in the liberated areas, and encouraging everyone to adopt our products. We are offering them for sale at a particularly low price. If you wish to purchase, please come to No. 7 Daomen, Dongyang City, Nanguan, Xingtai to purchase as soon as possible. Do not miss this opportunity.

### 精制植物煤油大批出售　邢台保民煤油制造厂批发处

1948-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　精制植物煤油大批出售
  　邢台保民煤油制造厂批发处
    径启者，敝处为抵制外货，发展解放区出产起见，特别精制植物煤油，屡经科学实验，油质纯洁，灯光明亮，并且耐用，远胜舶来品。敝处成货以来，颇蒙各界赞扬，称为解放区之曙光，近来销路日广，供不应求，敝处依诚心诚意为人民服务之精神，并答谢各界用户起见，成品精益求精，大量制造，争取解放区自供自给，人人采用，特别廉价出售，诸位如有愿购者，请到邢台南关东羊市道门牌七号从速购买，勿失良机。
