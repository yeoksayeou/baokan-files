Title: Red Star Newspaper Comments: Our Northwest Army Grows Stronger Daily
Author:
Date: 1948-03-25
Page: 2

　　Red Star Newspaper Comments
  　Our Northwest Army Grows Stronger Daily
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 23rd] TASS, Moscow, 20th: A military commentator for Red Star Newspaper commented: The situation in Shaanxi Province provides a vivid example of the growing strength of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The military strength of the Northwest People's Liberation Army is continuously growing, its combat experience is becoming increasingly rich, it has victoriously carried out large-scale campaigns, and it has delivered effective blows to the enemy.

### 红星报评论称　西北我军日强

1948-03-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　红星报评论称
  　西北我军日强
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】据塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：红星报军事评论家评称：陕西省的形势，提供了中国人民解放军日益壮大的生动实例。西北人民解放军军力不断增长，作战经验日益丰富，胜利地进行了大规模战役，给了敌人以有效的打击。
