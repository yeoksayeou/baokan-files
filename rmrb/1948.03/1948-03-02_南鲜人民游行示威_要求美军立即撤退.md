Title: South Korean People Demonstrate, Demanding Immediate Withdrawal of US Troops

Author:

Date: 1948-03-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th: Seoul dispatch: Following the anti-American movement earlier this month, the South Korean people held another demonstration on the evening of the 25th, demanding the withdrawal of US troops from Korea. Demonstrators bravely resisted police suppression. During the struggle, the masses attacked seven police stations and destroyed one bridge, resulting in six deaths and sixteen arrests. Another report: Kim Wal-hyun, a former member of the Korean Provisional Assembly currently in the United States, vehemently criticized the US military government in Korea for turning South Korea into a police state. He demanded the immediate withdrawal of US troops from Korea, allowing the Korean people to decide their own fate.

### 南鲜人民游行示威　要求美军立即撤退

1948-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　南鲜人民游行示威
  　要求美军立即撤退
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】汉城讯：南鲜人民继本月初反美运动，二十五日晚再举行示威游行，要求美军撤离朝鲜，示威群众勇敢地反抗警察的镇压。斗争中，群众攻击警察局七处，并毁桥梁一座，死六人，被捕者十六人。又讯：刻在美国的朝鲜临时议会前议员金瓦伦，猛烈抨击朝鲜的美国军政府使南鲜成了警察国家。他要求美军立即撤出朝鲜，让朝鲜人民决定自己的命运。
