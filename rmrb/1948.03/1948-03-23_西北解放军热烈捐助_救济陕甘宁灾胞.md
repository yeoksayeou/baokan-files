Title: Northwest PLA Enthusiastically Donates to Aid Disaster-Stricken Compatriots in Shaanxi, Gansu, and Ningxia
Author:
Date: 1948-03-23
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin Sui, 21st -- The commanders and fighters of the Northwest People's Liberation Army are universally donating clothing to aid the masses in Shaanxi, Gansu, and Ningxia who have suffered from banditry and natural disasters. Soldiers of a certain unit saved a total of 900 catties of grain, more than 300 pieces of clothing, blankets, quilts, and towels, and more than 6.4 million yuan in local currency to aid disaster victims. Another unit, with just one mess unit, donated 30 million yuan and 90 pieces of clothing. A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army advancing towards Jungar Banner in Suiyuan used grain and clothing captured from the bandit Fu to aid impoverished people, assisting 535 households in just eleven days.

### 西北解放军热烈捐助　救济陕甘宁灾胞

1948-03-23
第2版()
专栏：

       西北解放军热烈捐助　救济陕甘宁灾胞
    【新华社晋绥二十一日电】西北解放军指战员普遍捐助衣物救济遭受匪灾和天灾之陕甘宁群众。某部战士共节省粮食九百斤，衣物、毛毯、被子及毛巾等三百余件，本币六百四十余万元救济灾民。另部仅一伙食单位，即捐助三千万元及衣服九十件。向绥远准格尔旗前进之解放军某部，以缴获傅匪之粮食衣物救济贫苦人民，十一天内即救济五百三十五户。
