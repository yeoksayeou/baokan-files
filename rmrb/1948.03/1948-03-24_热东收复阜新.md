　　Northeastern Reaches Recapture Fuxin

    Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 22nd -- The Northeast People's Liberation Army recaptured Fuxin County, an important coal-producing center in eastern Rehe, on the 18th. The enemy's 93rd Army, Provisional 23rd Division headquarters and its two regiments fled towards Yixian on the evening of the 17th. We pursued and annihilated a part of them.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 22nd -- The Northeast Administrative Committee has recently allocated huge sums of money to relieve the citizens of Jilin and Siping after being ravaged by the Chiang bandits, with 200 million yuan (each yuan equivalent to 50 Nationalist currency) for Jilin City and 100 million yuan for Siping City. Another 500 million yuan has been allocated for agricultural loans to help farmers in the new districts near the two cities to engage in production, of which 200 million yuan is for Jilin Province and 300 million yuan is for the provinces of Liaobei and Liaoning.

### 热东收复阜新

1948-03-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　热东收复阜新
    【新华社东北二十二日电】东北解放军十八日收复重要煤产中心热东的阜新县城，守敌九十三军暂二十三师师部及其两个团十七日晚向义县方面逃窜，我追歼其一部。
    【新华社东北二十二日电】东北行政委员会顷拨巨款救济被蒋匪蹂躏后的吉林和四平市民，计吉林市救济费两万万元（每元合蒋币五十元），四平市一万万元。另拨农贷五万万元帮助该两市附近新区各县农民，进行生产，其中属吉林省境者两万万元，属辽北、辽宁两省境者共三万万元。
