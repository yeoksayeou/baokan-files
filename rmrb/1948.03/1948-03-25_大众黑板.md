Date: 1948-03-25
Page: 1

　　People's Blackboard
    Our People's Liberation Army captured Zichuan County town on the Jiaoji Railway in Shandong on the 20th, annihilating nearly 10,000 Chiang bandit troops and capturing two brigadier generals alive. On the morning of the 19th, we captured Yilin, a major commercial town in northern Jiangsu, eliminating more than 6,000 Chiang bandit troops and also capturing a brigadier general alive.

### 大众黑板

1948-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　大众黑板
    咱们解放军二十号在山东胶济路攻克了淄川县城，歼灭蒋匪万把人，活捉了两个旅长。十九号早晨，在苏北攻克商业重镇益林，消灭蒋匪六千多，也活捉住了一个旅长。
