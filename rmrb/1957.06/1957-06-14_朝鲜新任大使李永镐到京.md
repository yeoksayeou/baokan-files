Title: New Korean Ambassador Li Yongho Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 13th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Li Yongho, arrived in Beijing by train tonight. Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the station to welcome him. Kim Gui-nam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and his wife, along with all embassy staff, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, also welcomed him at the station.

### 朝鲜新任大使李永镐到京

1957-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜新任大使李永镐到京
    新华社13日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国新任驻华特命全权大使李永镐今天晚上乘火车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司司长王倬如。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办金贵南夫妇和全体馆员以及各国驻华使节、外交官员也到车站欢迎。
