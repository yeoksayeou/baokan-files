Title: Hungarian Party Congress to be Held the Day After Tomorrow; Main Task is to Further Consolidate the Party and Strengthen Party-Masses Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-06-25
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 24th: According to the "People's Freedom Daily", the National Congress of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party will begin on June 27th. The agenda of the meeting is: 1. Report on the Provisional Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party, rapporteur: Kadar; 2. Report on the revision of the Party Constitution, rapporteur: Marosan; 3. Report on strengthening and enriching the party's leadership organs, rapporteur: Somogyi (Member of the Central Executive Committee of the Party).
    The Provisional Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party held a plenary session on the 22nd to discuss issues related to the holding of the National Congress and various proposals to be submitted to the Congress for discussion.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 24th: The Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, Kiss, said in an article on the 23rd that the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party currently has 350,000 members. The unity of the party has been greatly consolidated. Kiss's article was published in "People's Freedom Daily". The article talked about the main tasks of the upcoming Party Congress. He pointed out that this Congress must further consolidate the Party, strengthen the Party's political influence on the masses, strengthen the Party's ties with the masses, and make the Party truly a leading force of the people. In addition, it is also necessary to wage a firm and thorough struggle against various tendencies that deviate from Marxism-Leninism.

### 匈牙利党代表会议后日举行  主要任务为进一步巩固党密切党群关系

1957-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利党代表会议后日举行
    主要任务为进一步巩固党密切党群关系
    新华社布达佩斯24日电　据“人民自由报”消息，匈牙利社会主义工人党全国代表会议将在6月27日开始举行。会议议程是：一、关于社会主义工人党临时中央委员会的报告，报告人：卡达尔；二、关于修改党章的报告，报告人：马罗山；三、关于加强和充实党的领导机构的报告，报告人：绍莫吉（党的中央执行委员会委员）。
    社会主义工人党临时中央委员会22日举行全会，讨论了同举行全国代表会议有关的问题和将提交代表会议讨论的各项建议。
    据新华社布达佩斯24日电　匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会书记基什23日在一篇文章中说，目前，匈牙利社会主义工人党已经拥有三十五万党员。党的团结已经有了很大的巩固。基什的文章刊登在“人民自由报”上，文章谈到了即将举行的党代表会议的主要任务。他指出，这次代表会议必须进一步巩固党，加强党对群众的政治影响，密切党同群众的联系，使党真正成为人民的领导力量。此外，还必须同脱离马克思列宁主义的各种偏向进行坚决而彻底的斗争。
