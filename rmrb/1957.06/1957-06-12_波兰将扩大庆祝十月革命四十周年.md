Title: Poland to Expand Celebrations for the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1957-06-12
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 11th: Representatives from the Polish United Workers' Party, other democratic parties, youth organizations, trade unions, and other social groups held a meeting on the 10th, deciding to expand celebrations throughout Poland for the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution and to publish books such as "The October Revolution and the Polish People," "Lenin and the October Revolution," and "The October Revolution and the Polish Question."
    In order to celebrate the October Revolution, the meeting also decided to organize various report lectures and literary creations, focusing on the important significance of the October Revolution for Poland, and calling on the people to inherit the traditions of Polish revolutionaries in the October Revolution. A committee for celebrating the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution was also established at the meeting.

### 波兰将扩大庆祝十月革命四十周年

1957-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　波兰将扩大庆祝十月革命四十周年
    新华社华沙11日电  波兰统一工人党、其他民主党派和青年组织、工会等社会团体的代表10日举行会议，决定将在波兰各地扩大庆祝十月革命四十周年和出版“十月革命和波兰人民”、“列宁和十月革命”、“十月革命和波兰问题”等书籍。
    为了庆祝十月革命，会议还决定组织各种报告讲座和文艺创作，着重讲述十月革命对波兰的重要意义，和号召人民继承波兰革命者在十月革命中的传统。会上还成立了庆祝十月革命四十周年委员会。
