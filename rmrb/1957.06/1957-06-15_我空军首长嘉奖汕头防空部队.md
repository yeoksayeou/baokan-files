Title: Air Force Leaders Commend Shantou Air Defense Unit
Author:
Date: 1957-06-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The leaders of the People's Liberation Army Air Force today issued an order commending the air defense unit that shot down two Chiang Kai-shek planes and damaged an invading American plane in the Shantou area on the 12th, and requested that all participating units reward personnel who performed meritorious service in the battle. The order also praised the unit's excellent combat readiness awareness and skilled combat techniques.

### 我空军首长嘉奖汕头防空部队

1957-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　我空军首长嘉奖汕头防空部队
    新华社14日讯　解放军空军首长今天发布命令，嘉奖12日在汕头地区击伤入侵美机一架和击落蒋机两架的防空部队，并要求各参战单位给作战有功的人员以奖励。命令还表扬了这个部队良好的战备观念和熟练的作战技术。
