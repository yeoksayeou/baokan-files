Title: Third National Women's Congress Postponed to September
Author:
Date: 1957-06-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th — The Standing Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, at its 14th meeting held on the 7th, discussed the preparatory matters for convening the Third National Women's Congress and decided to postpone the Congress to September 1957. The meeting also unanimously endorsed the resolution of the recent Executive Committee meeting of the International Women's Federation on the demands for disarmament and opposition to the use of atomic and nuclear weapons. The meeting called on women and mothers across the country to unite more closely with the people of the whole country and the people of the world to strive together for the realization of disarmament, opposition to the use and testing of atomic and nuclear weapons, for the safeguarding of lasting peace in Asia and the world, and for the protection of the happiness of our families and the safety of our children's lives.

### 第三次全国妇女代表大会延到九月举行

1957-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    第三次全国妇女代表大会延到九月举行
    新华社9日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会常务委员会在7日举行的第十四次会议上，讨论了有关召开第三次全国妇女代表大会的准备事项，并决定大会延期到1957年9月召开。这次会议还一致拥护国际妇联最近召开的执行局会议关于要求裁军、反对使用原子武器和核武器的决议。会议号召全国妇女们、母亲们，同全国人民和全世界人民更紧密地团结在一起，为实现裁军、反对使用和试验原子武器、核武器，为保卫亚洲及世界的持久和平、保护我们的家庭幸福和儿童的生命安全而共同奋斗。
