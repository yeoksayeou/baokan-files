Title: U.S. Newspapers Admit Growing Anti-American Sentiment in Canada
Author:
Date: 1957-06-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 12th: The "New York Times" believes that the defeat of the Liberal Party in Canada's election indicates a "hidden anti-American sentiment" exists in Canada.

	The newspaper fears that once the Progressive Conservative Party forms a government, it will adopt a "tougher" approach towards the United States, especially on trade issues.

### 美国报纸承认  加拿大反美倾向加强

1957-06-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国报纸承认
　　加拿大反美倾向加强
    据新华社13日讯　据塔斯社纽约12日讯：“纽约时报”认为加拿大自由党在选举中的失败，表明加拿大存在一种“隐蔽的反美情绪”。
    这家报纸担心，进步保守党一旦组成政府，将会对美国采取“较强硬”的方针，特别是在贸易问题上。
