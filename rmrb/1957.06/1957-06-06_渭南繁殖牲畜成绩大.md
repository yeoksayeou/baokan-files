Title: Great Achievements in Livestock Breeding in Weinan
Author: Yan Chengjie
Date: 1957-06-06
Page: 4

    Weinan Achieves Great Results in Livestock Breeding
    (Our Newspaper News) Weinan County, Shaanxi Province, a well-known livestock-rich county, has achieved significant results in livestock breeding in the first quarter of this year. According to statistics, the county has 10,497 breeding-capable female livestock, of which 3,626 have already been bred (equal to 38% of the breeding task for the entire year of 1956), and more than 2,600 have conceived, with an average conception rate of 72%. Many cooperatives have achieved rates of over 98%. (Yan Chengjie)

### 渭南繁殖牲畜成绩大
严成杰
1957-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    渭南繁殖牲畜成绩大
    本报讯　陕西省著名的牲畜丰产县——渭南县，在今年第一季度内，繁殖牲畜工作又获得了很大成绩。据统计，全县有繁殖能力的母畜一万零四百九十七头，已经配种三千六百二十六头（等于1956年全年配种任务的38％），受胎的在二千六百头以上，平均受胎率达72％，有不少社达到98％以上。（严成杰）
