Title: World Peace Council Holds Group Meetings to Discuss Report on Banning Nuclear Weapons
Author:
Date: 1957-06-16
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 15th. The World Peace Council meeting held committee meetings on the morning of the 14th. The World Peace Council plenary session established two committees, responsible for discussing and drafting reports on the prohibition of atomic and nuclear weapons and on easing world tensions. These two reports are scheduled for general discussion at the plenary session on the 15th. In the afternoon, delegations from various countries held regional gatherings, met with each other, and discussed issues of common concern.

### 世界和平理事会举行分组会议  讨论关于禁止核武器的报告

1957-06-16
第5版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会举行分组会议
    讨论关于禁止核武器的报告
    新华社科伦坡15日电　世界和平理事会会议14日上午举行各委员会会议。世界和平理事会全体会议一共设立了两个委员会，分别负责讨论和起草关于禁止原子核武器和关于缓和世界紧张局势的报告。这两个报告预定在15日举行的全体会议上进行大会讨论。各国代表团在下午分别举行地区性集会，互相会见，讨论共同有关的问题。
