Title: Nehru Reiterates at Swedish Press Conference: China Should Have Its Seat Restored in the United Nations; Accuses Baghdad and Manila Treaty Organizations of Creating Tension
Author:
Date: 1957-06-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th, Stockholm Dispatch: Indian Prime Minister Nehru reiterated at a press conference on June 24th that India supports the admission of the Chinese government to the United Nations because "it is the government exercising sovereignty over its own territory." He said: "Taiwan is not the Chinese government. It is unrealistic for the United Nations not to accept China. If the United Nations does not recognize the current government of China, the Chinese government will have no obligations to the United Nations."

On the issue of disarmament, Nehru advocated a gradual approach. He said that the first step would be to prohibit nuclear testing for a certain period of time, which could draw people's attention to the issue of disarmament. But after that, new steps must be taken, including the supervision of atomic and conventional weapons.

Nehru accused the Baghdad Treaty and the Manila Treaty Organization of creating greater tension through these military alliances.

When reporters asked about the Soviet Union's economic assistance to India, Nehru said that India is currently using a 500 million ruble Soviet loan to purchase factories, machinery, and other industrial equipment.

### 尼赫鲁在瑞典记者招待会上重申  应恢复中国在联合国的地位  指责巴格达和马尼拉条约组织制造紧张局势

1957-06-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　尼赫鲁在瑞典记者招待会上重申
    应恢复中国在联合国的地位
    指责巴格达和马尼拉条约组织制造紧张局势
    据新华社25日讯  斯德哥尔摩消息：印度总理尼赫鲁6月24日在记者招待会上重申印度赞成接纳中国政府进入联合国，因为“它是在它自己的领土上行使主权的政府”。他说：“台湾不是中国政府。联合国不接纳中国是不现实的。如果联合国不承认中国目前的政府，中国政府对联合国也就没有任何义务。”
    在裁军问题上，尼赫鲁主张采用逐步的办法。他说，第一步是规定在某一个时期内禁止原子试验，这样可以使人们思想上注意到裁军问题。但是在这以后还必须采取新步骤，包括监督原子武器和常规武器。
    尼赫鲁指责巴格达条约和马尼拉条约组织，因为这些军事同盟制造更大的紧张。
    当记者问到苏联对印度经济援助的情况时，尼赫鲁说，印度目前正在用五亿卢布的苏联贷款购置工厂、机器和其他工业设备。
