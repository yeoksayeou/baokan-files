Title: Shenyang's Three Machine Tool Factories Increase Production of More Than 4,000 Machine Tools
Author:
Date: 1957-06-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, June 24th: The three factories in Shenyang producing metal cutting machine tools—Shenyang No. 1, No. 2, and No. 3 Machine Tool Factories—have produced, from 1953 to June 15th of this year, more than 4,881 machine tools beyond the targets set in the First Five-Year Plan.

　　The various machine tools produced by these three machine tool factories in the past four years and five and a half months have been supplied to key national construction projects such as the Changchun First Automobile Manufacturing Plant, the Yellow River Sanmenxia project, and the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge.

　　During the First Five-Year Plan period, these three machine tool factories underwent reconstruction or technical transformation, and the production technology level of each factory has greatly improved. After reconstruction, the annual output of Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Factory last year was more than five times that of 1952.

　　During the Five-Year Plan period, these three factories also successfully trial-produced 33 new types of machine tools, exceeding the target set in the Five-Year Plan by seven types.

### 沈阳三个机床厂  增产工作母机四千多台

1957-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    沈阳三个机床厂
    增产工作母机四千多台
    新华社沈阳24日电　沈阳生产金属切削机床的三个工厂——沈阳第一、第二、第三机床厂，从1953年开始到今年6月15日生产出来的工作母机，已经比第一个五年计划规定的指标超出四千八百八十一台。
    这三个机床厂，在过去的四年又五个半月里生产的各种工作母机，供应了长春第一汽车制造厂、黄河三门峡、武汉长江大桥等全国各重点建设工程。
    在第一个五年计划期间，这三个机床厂都进行了改建或技术改造，各厂的生产技术水平都有很大提高。沈阳第一机床厂经过改建以后，去年一年的产量相当于1952年的五倍以上。
    这三个工厂在五年计划期间还试制成功了三十三种新式机床，比五年计划规定的指标多试制了七种。
