　　Laughing Bird
    There is a type of bird in Australasia called the Laughing Bird. It has a long, sharp beak and specializes in eating animals such as snakes, lizards, and field mice. After catching a snake, it first carries the snake to the top of a tree or into the sky, and then throws it down. It throws it down several times like this to kill the snake, and then eats it.
        Xinhua News Agency Draft

1957-06-14

专栏：花絮

　　笑鸟
    澳大利亚洲有一种会笑的鸟，名叫笑鸟。嘴长而尖利，专吃蛇、蜥蜴和田鼠等动物。它捕到一条蛇后，先把蛇衔到树顶上或天空中，扔下来，这样连扔几次，把蛇摔死，然后把它吃掉。
        新华社稿
