Title: Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Book, Holds Reception Celebrating Swedish Flag Day

Author:
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th June. Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Book, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate Swedish Flag Day.

Premier Zhou Enlai, at the reception, extended congratulations on the national day of Sweden on behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people. He proposed a toast to the prosperity of the Kingdom of Sweden and the happiness of its people, to the friendship between the Swedish people and the Chinese people, to world peace, and to the health of His Majesty King Gustaf VI Adolf of the Kingdom of Sweden.

Premier Zhou Enlai also raised his glass to the development of friendship between China and Sweden, to the solidarity of the people of all countries in the world, and to the good health of Swedish Prime Minister Erlander and Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Book.

Ambassador Book, in his address at the beginning of the reception, expressed a warm welcome and gratitude to the Chinese and foreign guests attending the reception. He invited all his guests to toast the friendly relations between the Swedish people and the Chinese people, the happiness and prosperity of the Chinese people, and the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai.

Also present at the reception were heads of various government departments, people from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China. People drank and chatted around a clear pool in the courtyard.

### 瑞典驻华大使布克举行招待会  庆祝瑞典国旗纪念日

1957-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典驻华大使布克举行招待会
    庆祝瑞典国旗纪念日
    新华社6日讯　瑞典驻中国大使布克今天下午举行招待会，庆祝瑞典国旗纪念日。
    周恩来总理在招待会上，代表中国政府和中国人民祝贺瑞典的国庆。他提议为瑞典王国的繁荣和人民的幸福，为瑞典人民和中国人民的友谊，为世界和平，为瑞典王国国王古斯塔夫六世陛下的健康，干杯。
    周恩来总理还举杯，祝中国和瑞典两国友谊的发展，祝世界各国人民的团结，祝瑞典王国首相埃兰德和瑞典驻中国大使布克的身体健康。
    布克大使在招待会开始的时候致词，对出席这个招待会的中外客人们表示热烈欢迎和感谢。他请他的全体客人们为瑞典人民和中国人民的友好关系，为中国人民的幸福和繁荣，为毛泽东主席和周恩来总理的健康干杯。
    出席招待会的还有政府各部门的负责人、社会各界人士和各国驻中国的使节。人们在庭院中一座清彻的水池周围饮酒谈心。
