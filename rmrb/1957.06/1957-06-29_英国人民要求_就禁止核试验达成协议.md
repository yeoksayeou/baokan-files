Title: British People Demand Agreement on Banning Nuclear Tests
Author: 
Date: 1957-06-29
Page: 5

    British People Demand
    Agreement on Banning Nuclear Tests
    Xinhua News Agency, London, June 28th: According to TASS, Labour MP Herbert Butler presented a petition to the House of Commons on the 27th, signed by 9,600 residents of the Hackney district of London, which he represents. The petition calls on the government to immediately sign an international agreement banning the testing of nuclear weapons.
    The day before, residents of the district marched through central London with banners and held a mass rally.

### 英国人民要求  就禁止核试验达成协议

1957-06-29
第5版()
专栏：

    英国人民要求
    就禁止核试验达成协议
    据新华社28日讯　据塔斯社伦敦讯：工党议员赫伯特·巴特勒27日把他所代表的伦敦哈克尼区九千六百名居民签名的一份请愿书交给下院，请愿书要求政府立即签订一项禁止试验核武器的国际协定。
    在这前一天，这个区的居民曾经拿着标语牌在伦敦市区游行，并且举行了群众大会。
