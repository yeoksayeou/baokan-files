Author:
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 4

    Letter of Correction
    In the news item "Prices of Fifteen Important Medicines Reduced" published on the fourth page of the People's Daily on June 1st of this month, "Aureomycin powder" should be "Chloromycetin powder." The price reduction for glucose powder is limited to that for injection use only. The price reduction for calcium gluconate powder is for oral use. We hereby make this correction.
                          Ministry of Commerce

### 来函更正

1957-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    来函更正
    本月1日人民日报第四版所刊“十五种重要药品售价降低”消息中的“金霉素粉”，应为“合霉素粉”。葡萄糖粉降价的，只限于注射用的。葡萄糖酸钙粉降价的，是口服用的。特此更正。
                          商业部
