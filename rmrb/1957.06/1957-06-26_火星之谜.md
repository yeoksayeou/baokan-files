　　The Mystery of Mars
    University of Chicago astronomy professor Dr. Kuiper recently published the results of his observations of Mars during the period in August and September of last year when Mars was closest to Earth. He said that when a person lands on Mars, he will find a primitive state of vegetation, but no canals. He observed some areas on Mars that are green, and he said that these green areas may be due to the existence of plants.

### 火星之谜

1957-06-26
第6版()
专栏：花絮

　　火星之谜
    芝加哥大学天文学教授居培尔博士最近发表他在去年八、九月间当火星距离地球最近的时候观察火星的结果说：当一个人降落到火星上时，他会发现一种原始状态的植物，但并没有运河。他观察到火星上一些地区呈绿色，他说这些绿色的区域，可能是由于有植物存在的原故。
