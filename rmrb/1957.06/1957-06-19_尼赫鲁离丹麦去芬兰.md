Title: Nehru Leaves Denmark for Finland
Author:
Date: 1957-06-19
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, 18th—Indian Prime Minister Nehru left the Danish capital of Copenhagen this morning for Helsinki, the capital of Finland, to continue his tour of Northern Europe.
    Nehru arrived in Copenhagen on the 15th for a visit.

### 尼赫鲁离丹麦去芬兰

1957-06-19
第7版()
专栏：

　　尼赫鲁离丹麦去芬兰
    据新华社18日讯　哥本哈根消息：印度总理尼赫鲁今天早晨离开丹麦首都哥本哈根飞往芬兰首都赫尔辛基，继续他的访问北欧的旅行。
    尼赫鲁是在15日到达哥本哈根访问的。
