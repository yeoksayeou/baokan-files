Title: Ghanaian Prime Minister Says Pan-African Conference Will Be Held in October
Author:
Date: 1957-06-21
Page: 6

　　Ghanaian Prime Minister Says
    Pan-African Conference Will Be Held in October
    Xinhua News Agency, London, June 20th: Ghanaian Prime Minister Nkrumah said in London on the 19th that the main purpose of the Pan-African conference he plans to hold in Accra is to "determine Africa's position so that Africa can play a role in world politics."
    Nkrumah is in London to attend the Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference scheduled to begin on June 26. He said that the Pan-African conference, which will be attended by eight countries, is scheduled to be held in October, and that Morocco and Tunisia have already accepted the invitation.

### 加纳总理说  泛非会议将在十月举行

1957-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　加纳总理说
    泛非会议将在十月举行
    据新华社20日讯　伦敦消息：加纳总理恩克鲁玛19日在伦敦说：他预定在阿克拉举行的泛非会议的主要目的是要“确定非洲的地位，使非洲能在世界政治中发生作用”。
    恩克鲁玛是到伦敦参加定于6月26日开始的英联邦总理会议的。他说，将有八个国家参加的泛非会议定于10月举行，目前摩洛哥和突尼斯已经接受了邀请。
