Title: Yemen Protests British Aircraft Dropping Incendiary Bombs
Author:
Date: 1957-06-22
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, 21st dispatch from London: Yemen protested on the 20th that six British aircraft dropped incendiary bombs on Sukkah, in the Harib region, on the 18th.
　　The statement from the Yemeni embassy in Britain said that the brutal bombing lasted for five hours.

### 也门抗议英机投燃烧弹 

1957-06-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　也门抗议英机投燃烧弹
    新华社21日讯　伦敦消息：也门20日抗议六架英国飞机18日在哈里布区的宿卡尔投掷燃烧弹。
    也门驻英公使馆的声明说，残酷的轰炸延续达五小时之久。
