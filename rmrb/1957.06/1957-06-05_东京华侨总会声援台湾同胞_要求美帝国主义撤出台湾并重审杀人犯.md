Title: Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association Voices Support for Taiwanese Compatriots, Demands that US Imperialism Withdraw from Taiwan and Re-examine Murderers
Author:
Date: 1957-06-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 3rd: The Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association issued a statement today expressing its greatest support and endorsement for the patriotic and righteous actions of Taiwanese compatriots.

　　The statement demands that US imperialism withdraw from Taiwan, promptly re-examine the murderers, and apologize and compensate the victims' families. The statement also demands that the Chiang Kai-shek clique break off its despicable dream of selling out the country and begging for aid, expel US troops, and complete the unification of the motherland as soon as possible.

　　At the Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association's理事会 meeting on May 29th, many people expressed their intense anger at the atrocities of the United States in their speeches. They expressed admiration and support for the righteous actions of Taiwanese compatriots who are in dire straits under the rule of US imperialism and the Chiang Kai-shek clique.

### 东京华侨总会声援台湾同胞  要求美帝国主义撤出台湾并重审杀人犯

1957-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    东京华侨总会声援台湾同胞
    要求美帝国主义撤出台湾并重审杀人犯
    新华社3日讯　东京消息：东京华侨总会今天发表声明，对台湾同胞的爱国正义行动表示最大的支持和拥护。
    声明要求美帝国主义撤出台湾，同时从速重审杀人凶犯，并且向被害者遗族道歉和赔偿。声明还要求蒋介石集团及早打断卖国乞怜的卑鄙恶梦，驱逐美军出境，完成祖国的统一。
    在5月29日举行的东京华侨总会理事会会议上，很多人在发言中表示了他们对美国的暴行的激烈愤怒。他们对处在美帝国主义和蒋介石集团统治下的水深火热中的台湾同胞的义举，表示敬佩和支持。
