Title: Denmark to Study Trade Issues with China
Author:
Date: 1957-06-02
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, June 1st: Danish Minister of Foreign Trade Krag said that the government's economic committee may soon study the issue of a trade agreement between Denmark and China. He told the government gazette "Politiken" on May 31st that Denmark sent an industrial and trade delegation to China last year. At that time, negotiations on such an agreement were scheduled to begin.

### 丹麦将研究对华贸易问题

1957-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    丹麦将研究对华贸易问题
    新华社1日讯　哥本哈根消息：丹麦对外贸易大臣克拉格说，政府经济委员会不久可能研究丹麦和中国的贸易协定问题。他在5月31日向政府机关报“政治报”说，丹麦曾经在去年派遣一个工业和贸易代表团访问过中国。那时就预定要开始举行关于这样一个协定的谈判。
