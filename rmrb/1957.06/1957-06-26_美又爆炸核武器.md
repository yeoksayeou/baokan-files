Title: US Again Explodes Nuclear Weapon
Author:
Date: 1957-06-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 25th: The U.S. government exploded another nuclear weapon at the Nevada test site on the 24th. This is the fifth in the current series of atomic nuclear explosion tests being conducted by the United States.

The nuclear weapon was exploded at an altitude of seven hundred feet. Its power exceeded 20,000 tons of explosives, greater than those dropped on Hiroshima and Nagasaki in Japan during World War II.

### 美又爆炸核武器

1957-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    美又爆炸核武器
    新华社25日讯  华盛顿消息：美国政府24日又在内华达州试验场爆炸了一枚核武器。这是美国目前正在举行的一系列原子核爆炸试验的第五次。
    这枚核武器是在离地七百英尺的高空爆炸的。威力超过两万吨炸药，比第二次世界大战时投掷在日本广岛和长崎的还要大。
