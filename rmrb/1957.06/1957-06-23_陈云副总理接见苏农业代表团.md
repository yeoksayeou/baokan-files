Title: Vice Premier Chen Yun Receives Soviet Agricultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd. Vice Premier Chen Yun received all members of the Soviet delegation to the Chinese Agricultural Exhibition, headed by Soviet Deputy Minister of Agriculture Petrov, this afternoon.

Those present at the reception included: Wang Zhen, Minister of Land Reclamation; and Liu Ruilong, Deputy Minister of Agriculture.

### 陈云副总理接见苏农业代表团

1957-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    陈云副总理接见苏农业代表团
    新华社22日讯　陈云副总理在今天下午接见了以苏联农业部副部长彼得罗夫为首的苏联参观中国农业展览会代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的有：农垦部部长王震，农业部副部长刘瑞龙。
