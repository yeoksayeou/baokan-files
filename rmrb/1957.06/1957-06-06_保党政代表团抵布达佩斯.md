Title: Bulgarian Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Budapest
Author: 
Date: 1957-06-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 5th: The Bulgarian government and party delegation, led by Yugov and Zhivkov, arrived in Budapest by special train this morning.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the station included Kadar, Chairman of the Hungarian Council of Ministers and Chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, and Münnich, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and member of the Central Executive Committee of the Party, among others.

### 保党政代表团抵布达佩斯

1957-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

    保党政代表团抵布达佩斯
    新华社布达佩斯5日电　保加利亚政府和党的代表团在于哥夫和日夫科夫率领下，今天上午乘专车到达布达佩斯。
    到车站欢迎代表团的有匈牙利部长会议主席、匈牙利社会主义工人党中央执行委员会主席卡达尔，部长会议副主席、党中央执行委员会委员明尼赫等人。
