Title: Mongolian Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency: A Mongolian medical delegation for investigating skin and venereal diseases, headed by the Deputy Minister of Health of the Mongolian People's Republic, S. Luvsandagva, arrived in Beijing on the 24th from Hohhot. They are visiting China to implement the 1957 Sino-Mongolian Cultural Agreement.

### 蒙古医学代表团到京

1957-06-26
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古医学代表团到京
    新华社讯  以蒙古人民共和国卫生部副部长斯·拉布佳为首的蒙古考察皮肤性病医学代表团一行九人，已于24日由呼和浩特抵京。他们是为执行1957年中蒙文化协定到中国来访问。
