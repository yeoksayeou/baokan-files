Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting to Discuss Handling the Incident of People Starving to Death Due to Disaster in Pingle, Guangxi
Author:
Date: 1957-06-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 15th: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 73rd meeting this afternoon.

Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai delivered a report at the meeting regarding the handling of the incident of people starving to death due to disaster in Pingle County, Guangxi Province. The meeting discussed this incident and approved the State Council's report.

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress continued its discussion this afternoon on the 1957 National Economic Plan, the 1956 National Final Accounts, and the 1957 National Budget.

Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Economic Commission Bo Yibo, Vice Premier and Minister of Finance Li Xiannian, and Vice Premier Chen Yun, successively addressed the meeting, providing answers and explanations to the questions raised by the committee members.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议　讨论处理广西平乐因灾饿死人事件

1957-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议
　　讨论处理广西平乐因灾饿死人事件
    新华社15日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天下午举行第七十三次会议。
    国务院总理周恩来在会上作了关于处理广西省平乐县因灾饿死人事件的报告。会议就这个事件进行了讨论，并且同意国务院的报告。
    新华社14日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天下午继续讨论了1957年度国民经济计划，1956年国家决算和1957年国家预算问题。
    国务院副总理兼国家经济委员会主任薄一波、副总理兼财政部长李先念、副总理陈云，先后在会上对委员们提出的问题作了解答和说明。
