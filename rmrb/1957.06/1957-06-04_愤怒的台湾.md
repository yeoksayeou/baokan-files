Title: Angry Taiwan
Author: Zou Difan
Date: 1957-06-04
Page: 8

Angry Taiwan
Arise, ye who refuse to be slaves! This is the sound of our national anthem. When the green gauze tent rises, we sing like this, in the anti-Japanese base areas, we sing like this, now, let the song cross the ocean. No matter how long the Seventh Fleet's blockade line is, no matter how many super fortresses are laid down as a net, you rushed through the security cars, military police, and tear gas, the anti-American demonstration crowd is like an ocean. We sing our songs to greet you. Our Taiwan is an unyielding port, your hearts are like the heroic statues of Taiwan. Thousands of troops and horses cannot conquer every heart, the red flag is planted on your heart's island. Hydrogen bombs cannot explode here, missiles will lose their direction here. Fight for the peaceful reunification of the motherland! This sound is thundering in the chests of 600 million people. Invaders get out of China! Let our farthest island sing "Let a hundred flowers bloom" in triumphal songs.

### 愤怒的台湾
邹荻帆
1957-06-04
第8版()
专栏：

    愤怒的台湾
    邹荻帆起来，不愿做奴隶的人们！这是我们的国歌声。当青纱帐起，我们这样歌唱，在抗日根据地上，我们这样歌唱，如今，让歌声横渡海洋。管它第七舰队封锁线多么长，管它超级堡垒布下天罗网，你们冲过警备车、宪兵队、催泪弹，反美示威的人群像海洋。我们把歌声迎着你们唱。我们的台湾是不屈的港，你们的心灵就是台湾英雄像。千军万马攻不下每颗心，红旗扯在你们的心岛上。氢弹在这里不能爆炸，导弹在这里要迷失方向。为和平统一祖国而奋斗！这声音在六万万人胸膛里暴雷般响。侵略者滚出中国去！让我们最远的海岛，在凯歌中唱“百花齐放”。
