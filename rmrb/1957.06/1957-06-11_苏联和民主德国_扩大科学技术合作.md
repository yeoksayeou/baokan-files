Title: Soviet Union and East Germany Expand Scientific and Technological Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-11
Page: 5

    Soviet Union and East Germany
    Expand Scientific and Technological Cooperation
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Soviet-East German Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee recently held a meeting in Berlin to discuss measures to expand scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries, and decided to establish direct links between the scientific research institutions of the two countries. The two countries will exchange technical documents and send experts to each other in four hundred and fifty projects.

### 苏联和民主德国  扩大科学技术合作

1957-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和民主德国
    扩大科学技术合作
    据新华社讯  苏联和民主德国科学技术合作委员会最近在柏林举行的会议上讨论了扩大两国的科学技术合作的措施，决定在两国科学研究机构之间建立直接联系。两国将在四百五十个项目方面交换技术文件和互派专家。
