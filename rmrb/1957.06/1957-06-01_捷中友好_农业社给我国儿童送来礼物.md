Title: "Czechoslovakia-China Friendship" Agricultural Cooperative Sends Gifts to Chinese Children
Author:
Date: 1957-06-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 31st - The "Czechoslovakia-China Friendship" Unified Agricultural Cooperative in Vinarice Village, Czechoslovakia, in order to celebrate the fourth anniversary of the cooperative's establishment and to express friendship to the China-Czechoslovakia Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative in Zhangguozhuang, China, donated three large boxes of children's leather shoes worth 3,000 Czech crowns to the China-Czechoslovakia Friendship Agricultural Cooperative before the International Children's Day on June 1st. This batch of leather shoes has arrived in Beijing.

### “捷中友好”农业社给我国儿童送来礼物

1957-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    “捷中友好”农业社给我国儿童送来礼物
    新华社31日讯　捷克斯洛伐克维纳尔日采村“捷中友好”统一农业合作社为了庆祝该社成立四周年，并表示对中国张郭庄中捷友好农业生产合作社的友谊，在6月1日国际儿童节前夕赠送给中捷友好农业社价值捷币三千克朗的儿童皮鞋三大箱。这批皮鞋已运抵北京。
