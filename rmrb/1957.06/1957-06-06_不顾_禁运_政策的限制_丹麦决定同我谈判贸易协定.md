Title: Defying "Embargo" Restrictions, Denmark Decides to Negotiate Trade Agreement with Us
Author:
Date: 1957-06-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, 5th - A Danish government spokesman said on the evening of the 4th that the Danish government has decided to notify the Chinese government that Denmark is prepared to negotiate a trade agreement between the two countries.

He also said that Denmark will reduce its export restrictions on China to the same level as those on the Soviet Union and Eastern European countries.

### 不顾“禁运”政策的限制  丹麦决定同我谈判贸易协定

1957-06-06
第6版()
专栏：

    不顾“禁运”政策的限制
    丹麦决定同我谈判贸易协定
    新华社5日讯　哥本哈根消息：丹麦政府发言人4日晚上说，丹麦政府已经决定通知中国政府，丹麦准备谈判缔结两国间的贸易协定。
    他还说，丹麦将把它对中国的出口限制降到和对苏联及东欧国家同一水平。
