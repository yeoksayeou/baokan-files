Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Foreign Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-06-27
Page: 6
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai met with the Polish journalist delegation this afternoon. Wu Lengxi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, was present at the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai met with the delegation of the Youth Department of the Japanese Socialist Party this afternoon. Hu Yaobang and Liao Chengzhi were also present at the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai met with the Japanese Okayama Prefecture Peace Delegation this afternoon. Guo Moruo and Liao Chengzhi were also present at the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai met with the Japanese Kyoto Prefecture Academic Delegation and the Japanese Michurin Society Delegation to China this afternoon. Guo Moruo, Pan Zinian, and Liao Chengzhi were present at the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai met with Greek peace activist and International Peace Prize winner Kazantzakis and his wife, and former Greek Ministers of Finance and Agriculture, Avepidi and his wife, this afternoon. Liao Chengzhi and Xu Baoju were present at the meeting.

### 周总理接见外宾

1957-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见外宾
    新华社26日讯  周恩来总理今天下午接见了波兰记者代表团。接见时，在座的有中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长吴冷西。
    新华社26日讯  周恩来总理今天下午接见了日本社会党青年部代表团。接见时，胡耀邦和廖承志也在座。
    新华社26日讯  周恩来总理今天下午接见了日本冈山县和平代表团。接见时，在座的有郭沫若和廖承志。
    新华社26日讯  周恩来总理今天下午接见了日本京都府学术代表团，日本米丘林会访华代表团。接见时在座的有郭沫若、潘梓年、廖承志。
    新华社26日讯  周恩来总理今天下午接见了希腊和平人士、国际和平奖金获得者卡赞亚基斯和夫人，希腊前财政、农业大臣艾威彼蒂和夫人。接见时在座的有廖承志和许宝驹。
