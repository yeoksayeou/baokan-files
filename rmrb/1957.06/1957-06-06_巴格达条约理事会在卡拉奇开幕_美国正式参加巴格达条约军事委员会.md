Title: Baghdad Pact Council Opens in Karachi; United States Formally Joins Baghdad Pact Military Committee
Author:
Date: 1957-06-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency report: According to reports, the Baghdad Pact Council meeting opened in Karachi on the 3rd. The United States has announced at the meeting its participation in the Baghdad Pact Military Committee and the Anti-Subversion Committee.

Participating in the Council meeting were delegations from the five treaty member states of Iran, Iraq, Pakistan, Turkey, and the United Kingdom, as well as a U.S. delegation attending as observers. Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy was elected chairman of the meeting.

U.S. delegation leader Henderson said at the opening meeting that the U.S. participation in the Baghdad Pact Military Committee further demonstrated "our determination to assist the Baghdad Pact countries in resisting the threat of communist aggression against their territories."

On June 4, the Baghdad Pact Council held a top-level meeting. The meeting was attended by the Prime Ministers (or heads of government) of the four Muslim member states of the Baghdad Pact, British Foreign Secretary Lloyd, and U.S. Deputy Assistant Secretary of State Henderson.

It is reported that the meeting discussed the international political situation, particularly the situation in the Middle East.

According to the "Star Evening News," Prime Minister Suhrawardy raised the Kashmir issue at the meeting.

Reports say that Turkish Prime Minister Menderes raised the Cyprus issue at the meeting. Iraqi Prime Minister Said reported on the situation in Jordan and the visit of Saudi Arabian King Saud to Baghdad.

At the same time, the Baghdad Pact Military Committee also held a meeting on June 4. According to newspaper reports, General Twining, head of the U.S. delegation to the Military Committee, hinted on June 3 that the Military Committee had discussed the proposal to establish a joint command. He also hinted that within five years, officers from the Baghdad Pact countries could be trained by the NATO headquarters in Paris. Sources say that Pakistan and Iraq do not favor the proposal to establish a Baghdad Pact joint command in the form of NATO.

On the same day as the Baghdad Pact Military Committee meeting, the U.S. State Department issued a statement expressing support for the "continued progress and development" of the Baghdad Pact.

### 巴格达条约理事会在卡拉奇开幕  美国正式参加巴格达条约军事委员会

1957-06-06
第6版()
专栏：

    巴格达条约理事会在卡拉奇开幕
    美国正式参加巴格达条约军事委员会
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：巴格达条约理事会会议3日上午在卡拉奇开幕。美国已经在会议上宣布参加了巴格达条约军事委员会和反颠覆委员会。
    参加理事会会议的有伊朗、伊拉克、巴基斯坦、土耳其和英国这五个条约成员国的代表团以及以观察员身份出席的美国代表团。巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底当选为会议主席。
    美国代表团团长汉德逊在开幕会议上说，美国参加巴格达条约军事委员会已经进一步表明，“我们决心援助巴格达条约国家抵制它们的领土遭受共产党侵略的威胁”。
    6月4日，巴格达条约理事会举行了最高级会议。参加会议的有巴格达条约四个穆斯林成员国的总理（或者首相），英国外交大臣劳埃德和美国副国务卿帮办汉德逊。
    有消息说，这次会议讨论了国际政治局势，特别是中东局势。
    据“明星晚报”报道，苏拉瓦底总理在会议上提出了克什米尔问题。
    消息说，土耳其总理曼德列斯在会议上提出了塞浦路斯问题。伊拉克首相赛义德在会议上报告了约旦的情况以及沙特阿拉伯国王沙特在巴格达进行的访问。
    与此同时，巴格达条约的军事委员会6月4日也举行了会议。据报纸报道，参加军事委员会的美国代表团团长特文宁将军在6月3日曾经暗示，军事委员会已经讨论了关于成立联合司令部的建议。他还暗示，在五年以内，巴格达条约国家的军官就可以由巴黎北大西洋公约组织总部加以训练。有消息说，巴基斯坦和伊拉克不赞成关于按照北大西洋公约组织的形式成立巴格达条约联合司令部的建议。
    就在巴格达条约的军事委员会会议的当天，美国国务院发表了声明，表示支持巴格达条约的“继续进展和发展”。
