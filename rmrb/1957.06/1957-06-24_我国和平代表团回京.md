Title: China Peace Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: The Chinese delegation to the World Peace Council meeting in Colombo, led by Guo Moruo, returned to Beijing by plane from Kunming this afternoon.

Also arriving in Beijing were the North Korean delegation headed by Han Xueye, the Vietnamese delegation headed by Lê Đình Thám, and the Mongolian delegation headed by Tsegmid. They also came to Beijing after attending the World Peace Council meeting in Colombo.

### 我国和平代表团回京

1957-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和平代表团回京
    新华社23日讯　出席世界和平理事会科伦坡会议的中国代表团在郭沫若率领下，今天下午乘飞机从昆明回到北京。
    同时到达北京的，还有以韩雪野为首的朝鲜代表团，以黎廷探为首的越南代表团，以采格米拉为首的蒙古代表团。他们也是在出席世界和理会科伦坡会议后来到北京的。
