Title: East Germany Local Elections Conclude; National Front Candidates Win
Author:
Date: 1957-06-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 25th - The head of the German Democratic Republic's Election Office announced that in the nationwide elections of approximately 250,000 council members at the county, city, district, township, and town levels on June 23rd, the joint candidates of the National Front received over 99.5% of the votes.

Over twelve million voters participated in the voting, accounting for approximately 97% of the total number of voters.

### 民主德国地方选举结束  全国阵线候选人获胜

1957-06-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　民主德国地方选举结束
    全国阵线候选人获胜
    新华社柏林25日电  德意志民主共和国选举事务所负责人宣布，在6月23日的全国县、市、区、乡、镇各级地方议会总数约二十五万议员选举中，全国阵线联合候选人获得99.5％以上的选票。
    参加这次投票的选民有一千二百多万，约占选民总数的97％。
