Title: Premier Zhou Meets Japanese Medical Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire Japanese medical delegation headed by Yoshio Dohmori today. Present at the meeting were Minister of Public Health and President of the Chinese Red Cross Society Li Dequan, Vice Minister of Public Health and Acting President of the Chinese Medical Association Cui Yitian, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Medical Association Fang Shishan, Lin Qiaozhi, and others. (Photo attached)
Premier Zhou Enlai (second from the right) in conversation with delegation leader Yoshio Dohmori (third from the right), Naotaro Kuji (first from the right), and Zennoshin Hatta (first from the left). Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Chengjian.

### 周总理接见日本医学代表团

1957-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本医学代表团
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理今天接见了以堂森芳夫为首的日本医学代表团全体人员。接见时在座的有卫生部部长、中国红十字会会长李德全，卫生部副部长、中华医学会代会长崔义田，中华医学会副会长方石珊、林巧稚等。（附图片）
    周恩来总理（右起第二人）和团长堂森芳夫（右起第三人）、久慈直太郎（右一）、八田善之进（左一）交谈。新华社记者　於澄建摄
