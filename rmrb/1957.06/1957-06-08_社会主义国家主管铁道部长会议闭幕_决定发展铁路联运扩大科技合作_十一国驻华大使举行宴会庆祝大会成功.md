Title: Meeting of Ministers of Railways of Socialist Countries Concludes: Decision to Develop Railway Intermodal Transportation and Expand Scientific and Technological Cooperation; Ambassadors of Eleven Countries to China Hold Banquet to Celebrate the Success of the Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-06-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th June. The meeting of Ministers of Railways of Socialist Countries, which opened in Beijing on May 27, closed today. At the closing meeting, the ministers of railways from twelve socialist countries signed the conference protocol.

Chinese Minister of Railways Teng Tai Yuan said in his closing speech: This conference has adopted a series of resolutions on railway transportation and organizational cooperation, which will play a significant role in the development of the railway industry in socialist countries. He said that economic cooperation and friendly unity among socialist countries will become closer due to the development of the railway industry.

Bulgarian Minister of Transport and Posts Mihailov said: This conference was very successful because of the spirit of friendly unity among our socialist countries. He said that the resolutions passed by the conference regarding the development of railway transportation will promote better cooperation among socialist fraternal countries. He expressed that after returning home, he would thoroughly implement these resolutions to allow the railway industry of socialist countries to grow rapidly.

Mr. B. Boagači, Director-General of the Yugoslav Railways, who was invited to participate in the conference, also spoke at the meeting. He said that this conference has achieved good results, which will not only play a significant role in the railway work of socialist countries, but also have a positive effect on the economic cooperation of socialist countries. He said that the spirit of friendship and unity at this conference left him with a good impression. Finally, on behalf of the people and railway workers of Yugoslavia, he wished the people and railway workers of all socialist countries greater achievements in their work.

In the afternoon, Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received all members of the railway ministers' delegation attending the conference.

In the evening, the ambassadors and their wives of eleven socialist countries to China jointly held a banquet to entertain all delegations attending the meeting of ministers of railways of socialist countries, celebrating the successful conclusion of the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th June. The meeting of ministers of railways of socialist countries issued the following communique:

The meeting of ministers of railways of the following socialist countries was held in Beijing from May 27 to June 7, 1957:

	People's Republic of Albania
	People's Republic of Bulgaria
	Hungarian People's Republic
	Democratic Republic of Vietnam
	German Democractic Republic
	People's Republic of China
	Democratic People's Republic of North Korea
	Mongolian People's Republic
	Polish People's Republic
	Romanian People's Republic
	Union of Soviet Socialist Republics
	Czechoslovak Republic

The railway delegation of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia participated in the conference as guests.

Comrade Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways of the People's Republic of China, presided over the meeting.

At this session of the meeting of ministers of railways, some amendments to the charter of the Railway Cooperation Organization were discussed and adopted.

The session of the meeting of ministers of railways adopted a series of practically meaningful proposals on further developing international railway intermodal transport, expanding scientific and technological cooperation, and exchanging railway experiences.

The meeting of ministers of railways approved the rules of procedure of its executive body—the Railway Transport Committee—and the work program for 1957-1958, and resolved a series of other organizational issues related to the future activities of the Railway Cooperation Organization.

The meeting of ministers of railways was conducted in an atmosphere of close friendship, unity, and full mutual understanding.

The ministers attending the meeting unanimously believed that the decisions they have adopted will play an important role in successfully solving the technical and economic problems facing the railways of socialist countries.

### 社会主义国家主管铁道部长会议闭幕  决定发展铁路联运扩大科技合作  十一国驻华大使举行宴会庆祝大会成功

1957-06-08
第5版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家主管铁道部长会议闭幕
    决定发展铁路联运扩大科技合作
    十一国驻华大使举行宴会庆祝大会成功
    新华社7日讯　5月27日在北京开幕的社会主义国家主管铁道部长会议，今天闭幕。在闭幕会议上，十二个社会主义国家主管铁道的部长，在会议议定书上签了字。
    中国铁道部长滕代远致闭幕词说：这次会议通过了一系列关于铁路运输和组织合作方面的各项决议，这对发展社会主义国家的铁路事业将起很大作用。他说，社会主义国家的经济合作和友好团结，将由于铁路事业的发展，更加密切。
    保加利亚交通邮电部长米哈依洛夫说：这次会议开得很成功，这是由于我们社会主义国家有友好团结的精神。他说会议通过的有关发展铁路运输的各项决议，将促使社会主义兄弟国家更好地合作。他表示回国以后，要很好地贯彻这些决议，让社会主义国家铁路事业迅速成长起来。
    应邀参加这次会议的南斯拉夫铁路总局长布·包加瓦奇也在会上讲了话。他说，这次会议获得了良好的效果，这不仅对社会主义国家的铁路工作会起很大作用，而且对社会主义国家的经济合作，也将发生良好效果。他说这次会议的友好团结精神，给他留下了良好的印象。最后，他代表南斯拉夫人民和铁路员工，祝社会主义各个国家人民和铁路员工在工作中获得更大成就。
    下午，国务院总理周恩来接见了出席这次会议的铁道部长代表团全体人员。
    入晚，十一个社会主义国家的驻华大使和夫人联合举行宴会，招待参加社会主义国家主管铁道部长会议的所有代表团，庆祝社会主义国家主管铁道部长会议的胜利闭幕。
    新华社7日讯　社会主义国家主管铁道部长会议发表公报如下：
    1957年5月27日至6月7日在北京举行了下列社会主义国家主管铁道部长会议：
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国
    保加利亚人民共和国
    匈牙利人民共和国
    越南民主共和国
    德意志民主共和国
    中华人民共和国
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国
    蒙古人民共和国
    波兰人民共和国
    罗马尼亚人民共和国
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国
    南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国铁路代表团以来宾身分参加了此次会议。
    本届会议是由中华人民共和国铁道部长滕代远同志主持的。
    在本届铁道部长会议上讨论并通过了对铁路合作组织章程的某些修改意见。
    本届铁道部长会议通过了一系列关于进一步发展国际铁路联运，扩大科学技术合作和交流铁路方面经验的具有实际意义的议案。
    铁道部长会议核准了它的执行机关——铁路运输委员会——的办事细则和1957—1958年度的工作纲要，并解决了一系列有关铁路合作组织今后活动的其他组织问题。
    铁道部长会议是在亲密友好、团结和相互充分谅解的气氛中进行的。
    出席会议的各国部长一致深信：他们所通过的各项决议将对顺利解决摆在社会主义各国铁路面前的技术问题和经济问题起重要的作用。
