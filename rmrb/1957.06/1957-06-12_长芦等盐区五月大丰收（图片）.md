Title: Great Harvest in Changlu and Other Salt Production Areas in May (Picture)
Author: Shi Panqi
Date: 1957-06-12
Page: 4

Changlu and Other Salt Production Areas Achieve Great Harvest in May
    The nationally renowned Changlu Hangu Salt Area and Tangda Salt Area are currently in peak salt production season. In May, they overproduced nearly 350,000 tons of sea salt, achieving an unprecedented great harvest. The picture shows a corner of the salt field. Xinhua News Agency reporter Shi Panqi.

　　长芦等盐区五月大丰收
