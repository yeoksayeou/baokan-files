Title: Lin Boqu Receives Australian Labor Party Parliamentary Delegation

Author:
Date: 1957-06-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: Lin Boqu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the Australian Labor Party Parliamentary Delegation headed by Helen today morning. Zhang Xiruo, Wu Kejian, Liu Guanyi, and Wu Maosun were present at the meeting.

### 林伯渠接见澳工党议员访华团

1957-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　林伯渠接见澳工党议员访华团
    新华社24日讯　林伯渠副委员长今天上午接见了以海伦为首的澳大利亚工党议员访华团。接见时，在座的有张奚若、吴克坚、刘贯一、吴茂荪。
