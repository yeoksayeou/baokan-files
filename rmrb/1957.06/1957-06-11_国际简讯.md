　　International Briefs

　　Six Countries Sign Aviation Agreement
　　Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Poland, Romania, and Bulgaria signed an agreement in Budapest on June 8 regarding joint cooperation, mutual dispatch of representatives, and joint operation of air routes between the capitals of these six countries. Prior to this, agreements between aviation traffic administrations of people's democratic countries were bilateral in nature.

　　Vietnam and Mongolia Sign Barter Agreement
　　The signing ceremony for the first barter and payment agreement between Vietnam and Mongolia was held in Hanoi on the afternoon of June 10. According to the provisions of the 1957 barter agreement between the two countries, this year Vietnam will supply Mongolia with timber, dried fruit, Nam Dinh silk and other light industrial products; Mongolia will supply Vietnam with woolen textiles, cream, and dried meat and other animal products.

　　Chinese Youth Delegation Leaves Britain for Home
　　The Chinese youth delegation has already left London on the 9th to return home. The delegation traveled throughout Britain during its one-month visit.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1957-06-11
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
    捷匈等六国签订航空协定
    捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、波兰、罗马尼亚和保加利亚六国6月8日在布达佩斯签订了关于共同合作、互派代表并且共同经营这六个国家首都间的航空线的协定。在这以前，人民民主国家的航空交通管理局之间的协定都是双边性质的。
    越蒙两国签订换货协定
    越南和蒙古之间第一个换货支付协定的签字仪式，10日下午在河内举行。根据两国1957年换货协定的规定，今年越南将供给蒙古木材、干果、南定绸等轻工业品；蒙古将供给越南毛织品、奶油和干肉等畜产品。
    我青年代表团离英返国
    中国青年代表团已经在9日离开伦敦回国。代表团在一个月的访问期间曾经在英国各地旅行。
                            （新华社）
