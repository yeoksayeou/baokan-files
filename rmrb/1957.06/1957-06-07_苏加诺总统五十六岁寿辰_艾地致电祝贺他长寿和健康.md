Title: President Sukarno's Fifty-Sixth Birthday: Aidit Sends Telegram Congratulating Him on Longevity and Health
Author:
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 6th: Today is President Sukarno's fifty-sixth birthday. At 8:00 PM tonight, President Sukarno will receive congratulations from foreign envoys to Indonesia and the public at the Independence Palace.

Aidit, General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, sent a telegram to President Sukarno in the name of one million party members, expressing congratulations. Aidit wished Sukarno "longevity and health, and more contributions to the people, the motherland, democracy, and human peace."

The Jakarta "People's Daily" also published an editorial saying that the Indonesian people are celebrating "the birthday of a leader, a democrat, a patriot." The newspaper praised Sukarno as a resolute opponent of colonialism and imperialism. He has consistently adhered to the ideals of national independence, prosperity, and unity.

President Sukarno was born in Surabaya, East Java, in 1901. He is one of the founders of the Indonesian Nationalist Party and does not currently belong to any party. Sukarno has served as President since the proclamation of Indonesian independence in 1945.

### 苏加诺总统五十六岁寿辰  艾地致电祝贺他长寿和健康

1957-06-07
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统五十六岁寿辰
    艾地致电祝贺他长寿和健康
    新华社雅加达6日电　今天是苏加诺总统的五十六岁寿辰。今晚八点钟苏加诺总统将在独立宫接受各国驻印度尼西亚使节和公众的祝贺。
    印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地以一百万党员的名义打电报给苏加诺总统表示祝贺。艾地祝苏加诺“长寿和健康，并且为人民、祖国、民主和人类和平作更多的贡献”。
    雅加达“人民日报”也发表社论说，印度尼西亚人民是在庆祝“一个领袖、一个民主主义者、一个爱国者的生日”。这家报纸赞扬苏加诺是殖民主义和帝国主义的坚决反对者。他一贯坚持民族独立、繁荣和团结的理想。
    苏加诺总统在1901年生于东爪哇的泗水，是印度尼西亚国民党的创始人之一，现在不属于任何党派。从1945年印度尼西亚共和国宣布独立到现在，苏加诺一直担任总统。
