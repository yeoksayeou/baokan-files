Author:
Date: 1957-06-11
Page: 5

　　The Soviet Union is About to Complete Spring Planting Plan
　　Agricultural Workers in the South Are Actively Preparing for Harvest
    (Our Correspondent in Moscow, 10th) The spring planting plan is about to be completed throughout the Soviet Union. The national sown area has reached 123 million hectares, accounting for more than 98% of this year's planned sown area for state farms and collective farms. The three republics of Kazakhstan, Ukraine, and Moldova have overfulfilled their planting plans. The spring planting work in the western, Ural, and Siberian regions of the Russian Federal Republic has also been completed. Farmers everywhere are now actively replanting seedlings and weeding.
    This year, the sown area of ​​grain, sugar beets and some industrial crops has been expanded in the Soviet Union. For example, the sown area of ​​wheat has increased by 3 million hectares. Now the total output of wheat in the Soviet Union has exceeded that of the United States and Canada, ranking first in the world. The sown area of ​​other grains (excluding corn) has also increased by 1.2 million hectares compared with last year.
    The sown area of ​​sugar beets in the Soviet Union has overfulfilled the plan. The sown area has reached 2,087,000 hectares, an increase of 111,000 hectares compared with last year. 15.4 million hectares of corn were planted, and they are growing well. More than 2 million hectares of cotton were planted. The planting plans for potatoes, flax, and sunflowers are also nearing completion.
    This year, winter wheat is growing well throughout the Soviet Union and has not suffered any disasters. Agricultural workers in Ukraine, the North Caucasus, and the Central Black Soil Region are actively preparing for the harvest.

### 苏联即完成春播计划  南方农业劳动者已在积极准备收割

1957-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联即完成春播计划
　　南方农业劳动者已在积极准备收割
    本报莫斯科10日电  苏联各地春播计划即将完成。全国播种面积已达一亿二千三百万公顷，即占今年全部国营农场、集体农庄播种面积计划的98％以上。哈萨克、乌克兰、摩尔达维亚三个共和国超额完成了播种计划。俄罗斯联邦共和国西部、乌拉尔和西伯利亚的春播工作也已全部结束。现在各地农民积极地补苗、锄草。
    今年苏联谷物、甜菜和部分技术作物的播种面积有了扩大。如小麦的播种面积增加了三百万公顷。现在苏联小麦总产量已超过美国和加拿大，占世界第一位。其他谷物（不包括玉米）播种面积也比去年增加了一百二十万公顷。
    全苏甜菜播种面积超额完成了计划。播种面积已达二百零八万七千公顷，比去年增加了十一万一千公顷。玉米播种了一千五百四十万公顷，长得也很好。棉花播种了二百多万公顷。马铃薯、亚麻、向日葵的播种计划也接近完成。
    今年苏联各地冬麦都长得很好，没有受到什么灾害。乌克兰、北高加索和中央黑土区等地农业劳动者已在积极准备收割。
