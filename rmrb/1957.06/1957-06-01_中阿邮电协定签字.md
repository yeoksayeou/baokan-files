Title: Sino-Albanian Postal and Telecommunications Agreement Signed
Author:
Date: 1957-06-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 31st - The signing ceremony of the Postal and Telecommunications Agreement between the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China and the Ministry of Transportation of the People's Republic of Albania was held in Beijing at 1 p.m. today.

    Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of China, and Jakova, Minister of Transportation of the People's Republic of Albania, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective parties.

    On the Chinese side, those who attended the signing ceremony included Yan Baohang, Director of the Treaty Committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Gu Chunfan, Vice Minister of Posts and Telecommunications; on the Albanian side, there was the Albanian Ambassador to China, Balili, and others. Zhu Xuefan also held a reception banquet to celebrate the signing of the agreement.

### 中阿邮电协定签字

1957-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中阿邮电协定签字
    新华社31日讯　中华人民共和国邮电部和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国交通部邮政和电信协定的签字仪式，今天下午一时在北京举行。
    中国邮电部部长朱学范和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国交通部部长雅科瓦，分别代表双方在协定上签字。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有外交部条约委员会主任阎宝航、邮电部副部长谷春帆等；阿尔巴尼亚方面有阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使巴利里等。朱学范并为庆祝协定的签订举行了招待宴会。
