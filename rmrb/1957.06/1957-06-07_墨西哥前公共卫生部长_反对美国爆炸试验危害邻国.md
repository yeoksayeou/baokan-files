Title: Former Mexican Minister of Public Health Opposes U.S. Nuclear Tests Endangering Neighboring Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, June 6th: Former Mexican Minister of Public Health, Jose Slan, said on June 4th that he will ask all doctors in Mexico to take action to oppose the continued nuclear explosion tests within the United States.

He said that the danger caused by such explosion tests is serious because the winds blowing from north to south are spreading radioactive particles to northern and north-central Mexico.

Slan said that he has asked the Ministry of Defense to establish observation stations in northern Mexico to observe the impact of this radioactive material.

### 墨西哥前公共卫生部长  反对美国爆炸试验危害邻国

1957-06-07
第6版()
专栏：

    墨西哥前公共卫生部长
    反对美国爆炸试验危害邻国
    新华社6日讯　墨西哥城消息：墨西哥前公共卫生部长何塞·斯朗6月4日说，他将要求墨西哥的所有医生都行动起来反对在美国境内继续进行核爆炸试验。
    他说，由于这种爆炸试验而产生的危险是严重的，因为自北朝南的风向正在把放射性微粒散布到墨西哥北部和北中部。
    斯朗说，他已经要求国防部在墨西哥北部建立观测站来观测这种放射性物质的影响。
