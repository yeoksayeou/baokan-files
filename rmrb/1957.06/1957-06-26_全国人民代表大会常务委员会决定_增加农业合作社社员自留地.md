Title: Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress: Increasing Private Plots for Members of Agricultural Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1957-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 25th. Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Increasing Private Plots for Members of Agricultural Production Cooperatives. (Passed at the 76th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on June 25, 1957)

	The 76th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on June 25, 1957, discussed the proposal by Premier of the State Council, Zhou Enlai, on appropriately increasing the private plots for members of agricultural production cooperatives, and decided to make the following supplementary provisions to Article 16 of the Model Regulations for Advanced Agricultural Production Cooperatives: Agricultural production cooperatives may, according to needs and local conditions, allocate a certain amount of land to members for growing pig feed. The amount of such land allocated to each household of members shall be determined according to the number of pigs raised by each household. The total land used by each person for this purpose, together with the land allocated to members for growing vegetables as stipulated in the Model Regulations for Advanced Agricultural Production Cooperatives, shall not exceed 10% of the local average land per person.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会决定  增加农业合作社社员自留地

1957-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常务委员会决定
    增加农业合作社社员自留地
    新华社25日讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于增加农业生产合作社社员自留地的决定。（1957年6月25日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第七十六次会议通过）
    1957年6月25日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第七十六次会议讨论了国务院周恩来总理提出的关于适当增加农业生产合作社社员自留地的议案，决定对高级农业生产合作社示范章程第十六条作如下补充规定：农业生产合作社可以根据需要和当地条件，抽出一定数量的土地分配给社员种植猪饲料。分配给每户社员的这种土地的数量，按照每户社员养猪头数的多少决定。每人使用的这种土地，连同高级农业生产合作社示范章程所规定的分配给社员种植蔬菜的土地，合计不能超过当地每人平均土地数的10％。
