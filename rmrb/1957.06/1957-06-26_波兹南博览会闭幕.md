Title: Poznan Fair Closes

Author:
Date: 1957-06-26
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 24th: The 26th Poznan International Fair, which began on June 9th, closed on the 23rd.
    In addition to Poland, exhibition units from twenty-nine countries participated in this fair. Over the course of fifteen days, a total of 900,000 people visited the fair. Polish trade units concluded trade deals worth over 440 million rubles with foreign countries at this year's Poznan Fair, 60% of which was with socialist countries.

### 波兹南博览会闭幕

1957-06-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　波兹南博览会闭幕
    据新华社华沙24日电  从6月9日开始举行的第二十六届波兹南国际博览会已经在23日闭幕。
    除波兰以外，参加这次博览会的有二十九个国家的展览单位。在十五天里参观博览会的共有九十万人，波兰各贸易单位在今年的波兹南博览会上同外国成交的贸易额达到四亿四千多万卢布，其中有60％是同社会主义国家进行的。
