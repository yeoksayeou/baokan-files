Title: Algerian National Liberation Front Sends Telegrams to Leaders of Soviet Union, United States, India, and Others, Calling for a Halt to French Military Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1957-06-04
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd, Tunis: The representatives of the Algerian "National Liberation Front" in Tunis sent telegrams on the 2nd to Soviet Chairman Voroshilov, U.S. President Eisenhower, Indian Prime Minister Nehru, and Pope Pius XII, requesting them to step in to stop the French military police's "massacre" of Algerian civilians.

    Similar telegrams were also sent to Tunisian Prime Minister Bourguiba and the Sultan of Morocco.

    According to a June 2nd report in "Al-Ahram," Dr. Amin, a leader of the Algerian Liberation Front, has sent telegrams to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, U.S. President Eisenhower, and leaders of many countries, condemning France for launching a war of genocide against the Algerian people.

### 阿民族解放阵线致电苏美印等国领袖  呼吁制止法军暴行

1957-06-04
第6版()
专栏：

    阿民族解放阵线致电苏美印等国领袖
    呼吁制止法军暴行
    据新华社3日讯　突尼斯消息：在突尼斯的阿尔及利亚“民族解放阵线”代表2日打电报给苏联伏罗希洛夫主席、美国艾森豪威尔总统、印度尼赫鲁总理和罗马教皇庇护十二世，要求他们出面制止法国军警对阿尔及利亚平民的“屠杀”。
    同样的电报也打给了突尼斯首相布尔吉巴和摩洛哥苏丹。
    另据“金字塔报”6月2日报道，阿尔及利亚解放阵线的领袖阿明博士已经打电报给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德、美国总统艾森豪威尔和许多国家的领袖们，谴责法国向阿尔及利亚人民发动灭绝种族的战争。
