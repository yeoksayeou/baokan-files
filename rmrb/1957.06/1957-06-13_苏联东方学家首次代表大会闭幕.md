Title: First Congress of Soviet Orientalists Concludes

Author:

Date: 1957-06-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tashkent, June 12th: According to TASS, the First All-Union Conference of Soviet Orientalists concluded on June 11th in Tashkent, the capital of Uzbekistan.

　　The conference heard reports on the state and tasks of Soviet Oriental studies, the international significance of the victory of the Chinese People's Revolution, the disintegration of the imperialist colonial system, the national liberation struggle of the people of Southeast Asia, and the basic situation of the Indian people's liberation struggle. A total of more than 120 large and small reports were heard at the plenary sessions and group meetings.

　　Scholars from China and other People's Democratic countries also participated in the conference. Attendees warmly welcomed the report "Sun Yat-sen and the National Liberation Struggle in Asia" given by Ding Zeliang, Director of the History Department of Northeast People's University of China.

　　The conference passed a resolution formulating specific measures to further develop Soviet Oriental studies in accordance with the spirit of the resolutions of the 20th Congress of the CPSU.

### 苏联东方学家首次代表大会闭幕

1957-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联东方学家首次代表大会闭幕
    新华社12日讯  据塔斯社塔什干讯：苏联东方学家第一次全国代表会议6月11日在乌兹别克首都塔什干闭幕。
    这次会议听取了有关苏联东方学的状况和任务、中国人民革命胜利的国际意义、帝国主义殖民体系的瓦解、东南亚人民民族解放斗争、印度人民解放斗争基本情况等报告。全体会议和分组会议共听了一百二十多个大小报告。
    参加这次会议的还有中国和其他人民民主国家的学者。到会者热烈欢迎中国东北人民大学历史系主任丁则良所作的“孙中山与亚洲民族解放斗争”的报告。
    会议通过决议，拟订了根据苏共第二十次代表大会决议的精神进一步发展苏联东方学的具体措施。
