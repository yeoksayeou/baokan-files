Title: Urging Muslims in Taiwan to Carry the Anti-American Struggle Through to the End: China Islamic Association Issues Letter of Support
Author:
Date: 1957-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st June – The China Islamic Association has issued a letter of support for the patriotic anti-American struggle of the compatriots in Taiwan. The text is as follows:

We, the Muslims of the entire country, feel extreme anger and hatred towards the brutal behavior of the American aggressors in Taiwan. We express our greatest respect for the patriotic struggle of the people of Taiwan. The Muslims of the entire country resolutely support the people of Taiwan with all our strength. American imperialism must get out of Taiwan completely.

"Patriotism is part of Imani (Islamic faith)." We urge Muslims in Taiwan to carry forward the glorious tradition of Islam against aggression and to carry the just struggle through to the end together with the people of Taiwan. We will certainly support you together with the people of the whole country, and we will never give up until Taiwan returns to the embrace of the motherland.

### 吁请在台湾的穆斯林将反美斗争进行到底  中国伊斯兰教协会发出声援书

1957-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    吁请在台湾的穆斯林将反美斗争进行到底
    中国伊斯兰教协会发出声援书
    新华社1日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会对台湾同胞的爱国反美斗争发出声援书。文如下：
    我们全国穆斯林对美国侵略者在台湾的残暴行为感到万分的愤怒和仇恨。对于台湾人民的爱国斗争表示极大的敬意。全国穆斯林坚决以一切力量支援台湾人民。美帝国主义一定要全部滚出台湾去。
    “爱国是伊玛尼（伊斯兰教的信仰）的一部分”。我们吁请在台湾的穆斯林，发扬伊斯兰教反对侵略的光荣传统，和台湾人民一起将正义斗争进行到底。我们一定和全国人民一同支援你们，台湾不回到祖国怀抱，决不罢休。
