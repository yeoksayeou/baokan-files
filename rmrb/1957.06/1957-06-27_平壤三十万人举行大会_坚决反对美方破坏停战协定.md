Title: Pyongyang Holds a Mass Rally of 300,000 People to Resolutely Oppose the US Side's Sabotage of the Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-06-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 26th: Approximately 300,000 workers, farmers, youths, students, and scientific and cultural workers in Pyongyang held a rally today at Kim Il-sung Square in Pyongyang to condemn the US side's plot to bring new types of weapons into South Korea and move the United Nations Command headquarters to Seoul, which would sabotage the Korean Armistice Agreement.

Foreign Minister Nam Il, in his report to the rally, stated that the Korean Armistice Agreement was won with the blood of the Korean people and the Chinese People's Volunteers. The statement issued by the Korean government today reflects the will of all the Korean people. He emphasized that all Korean people resolutely oppose the US side's scheme to sabotage the Armistice Agreement by bringing new types of weapons into South Korea and moving the United Nations Command headquarters to Seoul.

Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Kim Il, Pak Ui-wan, and Chong Chun-taek, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Pak Chong-ae, Pak Kum-chol, Kim Chang-man and other leaders of the Party and government and responsible persons of various social organizations attended the rally.

Following Nam Il's report, Hong Ki-hwang, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Party, and Kim Dal-hyon, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chondoist Chongu Party, spoke successively, unanimously supporting the Workers' Party of Korea and the government's proposal for the peaceful reunification of Korea, and opposing the schemes of the US imperialists to undermine the Korean Armistice Agreement.

Subsequently, representatives of workers, farmers, youth, students, and scientific and cultural workers spoke at the meeting, expressing their determination to support the government's statement and launch a struggle against the US side's sabotage of the Armistice Agreement.

The resolution read out at the end of the meeting expressed support for and endorsement of the government's statement, and called on all Korean people to further unite around the Workers' Party of Korea and the government, thoroughly crush the schemes of the US imperialists, and fight to the end for the peaceful reunification of Korea.

### 平壤三十万人举行大会  坚决反对美方破坏停战协定

1957-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　平壤三十万人举行大会
    坚决反对美方破坏停战协定
    新华社平壤26日电  平壤市的工人、农民、青年、学生和科学文化工作者等约三十万人今天在平壤市金日成广场举行大会，谴责美方破坏朝鲜停战协定要把新型武器运进南朝鲜和把联合国军司令部移到汉城的阴谋。
    外务相南日在大会上作报告说，朝鲜停战协定是朝鲜人民和中国人民志愿军以鲜血争取得来的。朝鲜政府今天发表的声明，反映了全体朝鲜人民的意志。他强调说，全体朝鲜人民坚决反对美方破坏停战协定、把新型武器运入南朝鲜和把联合国军司令部移到汉城的策划。
    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉、内阁副首相金一、朴义琓、郑准洚，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金喆、金昌满等党和政府的领导人和各社会团体负责人都出席了大会。
    南日报告结束后，北朝鲜民主党中央委员会委员长洪基璜和天道教青友党中央委员会委员长金达铉先后发言，一致支持朝鲜劳动党和政府提出的和平统一朝鲜的主张，反对美帝国主义者破坏朝鲜停战协定的策划。
    接着，工人代表、农民代表、青年学生代表和科学文化工作者的代表分别在会上发言，他们纷纷表示，决心拥护政府声明，展开斗争反对美方破坏停战协定。
    大会最后宣读的决议书表示支持和拥护政府的声明，并且号召全体朝鲜人民进一步团结在朝鲜劳动党和政府的周围，彻底粉碎美帝国主义者的阴谋策划，为实现朝鲜的和平统一而斗争到底。
