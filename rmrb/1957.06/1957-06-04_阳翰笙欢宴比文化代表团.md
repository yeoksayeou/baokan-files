Title: Yang Hansheng Hosts Banquet for Belgian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 1st—Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a banquet this evening to welcome the Belgian Cultural Delegation visiting China.
