Title: To Participate in the World Youth Festival, Our Country's Athletic Stars Form Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. A Xinhua News Agency reporter learned today from relevant sources that the Chinese Youth Sports Delegation to the Third International Youth Friendship Games of the Sixth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship has been formed.

　　This delegation of one hundred and sixty people includes one hundred and twenty-three male and female athletes (including forty female athletes). They will participate in the Third International Youth Friendship Games in nine sports: men's and women's track and field, gymnastics, basketball, volleyball, table tennis, and men's weightlifting, swimming, tennis, and soccer. Among the athletes in the delegation are track and field athletes Zheng Fengrong, Cai Yishu, and Tian Zhaozhong, swimming athletes Qi Lieyun, Mu Xiangxiong, and Lin Jinzhu, and weightlifting athletes Chen Jingkai, Xu Honglin, Li Baiyu, and Zhao Qingkui, all of whom have achieved excellent results this year. The delegation also includes volleyball international referee Wang Zhiqiang, gymnastics national referee Jin Yongyan, and Yao Zhizhen (female).

### 参加世界青年联欢节  我国体育健将组成代表团

1957-06-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　参加世界青年联欢节
    我国体育健将组成代表团
    新华社28日讯　新华社记者今天从有关方面获悉：参加第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节第三届国际青年友谊运动会的中国青年体育代表团已经组成。
    这个由一百六十人组成的代表团中，有男女运动员一百二十三名（其中女运动员四十人）。他们将参加第三届国际青年友谊运动会中的男女田径、体操、篮球、排球、乒乓球和男子举重、游泳、网球、足球等九项运动的比赛。代表团的运动员中，有在今年曾经取得优良成绩的田径选手郑凤荣、蔡艺墅、田兆钟，游泳选手戚烈云、穆祥雄、林锦珠，举重选手陈镜开、徐鸿林、李白玉和赵庆奎等人。代表团中还有排球国际裁判王志强，体操国家裁判金永延和姚志贞（女）等三人。
