Title: Lao United Party Chairman Prince Souphanouvong Agrees to Form Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1957-06-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 22nd: Vientiane dispatch: According to Agence France-Presse, Lao United Party Chairman Prince Souphanouvong has accepted the Crown Prince's appointment and agreed to form a new cabinet.

Prince Souphanouvong will not officially become the cabinet-designate until the King approves.

According to Vientiane Radio, after the failure of the National Party Chairman Katay Don Sasorith to form a cabinet, the Crown Prince met with the leader of the Independent Party, Phoui Sananikone, to discuss the possibility of Sananikone forming a cabinet. Sananikone expressed his unwillingness to accept the task of forming a new cabinet. He said that his political platform was consistent with Sasorith's, and Sasorith's platform had not been approved by the National Assembly. Sananikone was the Minister of Defense in Sasorith's cabinet list.

### 老挝联合党主席  彭·苏发那冯同意组阁

1957-06-23
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝联合党主席
    彭·苏发那冯同意组阁
    据新华社河内22日电　万象消息：据法新社报道，老挝联合党主席彭·苏发那冯已经接受王太子的委任，同意组织新内阁。
    彭·苏发那冯要等到国王批准以后才成为正式的组阁人。
    据万象电台广播，在国家党主席卡代·萨索里特组阁失败以后，王太子曾经接见独立党领袖冯·萨纳尼空，商量由萨纳尼空组阁的问题。萨纳尼空表示不愿意接受组织新内阁的任务。他说，他的政纲同萨索里特的政纲是一致的，而萨索里特的政纲并没有能够得到国民议会的同意。萨纳尼空是萨索里特内阁名单中的国防大臣。
