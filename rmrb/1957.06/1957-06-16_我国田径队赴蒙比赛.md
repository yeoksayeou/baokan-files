Title: Our Country's Track and Field Team Leaves for Competition in Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1957-06-16
Page: 5

    Our Country's Track and Field Team Leaves for Competition in Mongolia
    (Our newspaper reports) A Chinese mixed track and field team of eighteen people took the train to the Mongolian People's Republic yesterday morning. They are going to participate in the China, North Korea, and Mongolia trilateral track and field competition held in Mongolia from June 21st to 23rd. Among the athletes are the famous 100-meter runner, 80-meter low hurdle runner, and high jumper, athletic sports masters Liu Jingren, Wen Yunzhen, Li Dapei, and others.

### 我国田径队赴蒙比赛

1957-06-16
第5版()
专栏：

    我国田径队赴蒙比赛
    本报讯　中国田径混合队一行十八人在昨日上午乘火车去蒙古人民共和国，他们是去参加6月21日到23日在蒙古举行的中国、朝鲜、蒙古三国田径比赛的，运动员中有百公尺、八十公尺低栏、跳高的著名运动员、运动健将刘敬仁、文蕴珍、李大培等。
