Title: Nasser Expresses Gratitude and Resentment: The Soviet Union Provided Assistance to Egypt in Times of Crisis; The United States Uses the Eisenhower Doctrine Against the Arabs
Author:
Date: 1957-06-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 20th (TASS): Egyptian President Nasser stated in Cairo to the editor of the "Chicago Daily News" that the "Eisenhower Doctrine" is a new tool used to oppose the national aspirations of the Arab people.

The "Chicago Daily News" published this statement by Nasser. Nasser also told Walter, the editor of the American newspaper, that you great powers despise nationalism and insist that you have power. You can imagine that you have bases. But in reality, there will be twenty secret bases against you.

Nasser expressed his gratitude to the Soviet Union. He said: "We once asked the United States to allow us to buy surplus American wheat, but the United States refused our request, while Russia supplied us with wheat. Europe refused to buy our cotton, but Russia bought it. During the Suez Canal crisis, we lacked oil, and Russia also supplied us with oil. Our people thank Russia because it helped us in times of crisis."

Walters asked Nasser why Egypt could not receive a small American mission to inspect how Egypt used the materials provided by the United States. Thirty-five other countries that have accepted U.S. aid have already done so, so why can't Egypt do the same? Nasser replied: "You can call us an extremely suspicious nation, but we have prejudices against these missions. If you want to contact the people of a country, you should not ignore their history, their traditions, and their prejudices."

Nasser also criticized the Baghdad Pact, believing it to be a manifestation of new colonialism.

Nasser said that Egypt needs time to build and Egypt needs peace.

### 纳赛尔表示恩怨分明  苏联在埃及危急的时刻给予了援助  美国用艾森豪威尔主义反阿拉伯人

1957-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　纳赛尔表示恩怨分明
    苏联在埃及危急的时刻给予了援助  美国用艾森豪威尔主义反阿拉伯人
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社纽约19日讯：埃及总统纳赛尔在开罗对“芝加哥每日新闻”的编辑说，“艾森豪威尔主义”是用来反对阿拉伯民族愿望的新工具。
    “芝加哥每日新闻”刊载了纳赛尔的这番谈话。纳赛尔还对这家美国报纸的编辑华尔特斯说，你们这些大国轻视民族主义，硬说你们有势力。你们可以想像，你们有基地。但是，实际上会有二十个反对你们的秘密基地。
    纳赛尔表示感谢苏联。他说：“我们曾经要求美国让我们购买美国的剩余小麦，美国拒绝了我们的要求，而俄国却供给我们小麦。欧洲拒绝购买我们的棉花，而俄国却买下了它。在苏伊士运河斗争期间我们缺乏石油，俄国也供给了我们石油。我国人民感谢俄国，因为它在危急的时刻援助了我们。”
    华尔特斯问纳赛尔，为什么埃及不能接待人数不多的美国使团去视察埃及如何使用美国所提供的物资。接受美援的其他三十五个国家都已经这样做了，为什么埃及不能这样做。纳赛尔回答说：“你们可以把我们叫作极为多疑的民族，但是，我们是怀有反对这些使团的成见的。如果你们想同某个国家的人民接触，你们就不应该忽视他们的历史，他们的传统和他们的成见。”
    纳赛尔还指责巴格达条约，认为它是新的殖民主义的表现。
    纳赛尔说，埃及需要时间来建设，埃及需要和平。
