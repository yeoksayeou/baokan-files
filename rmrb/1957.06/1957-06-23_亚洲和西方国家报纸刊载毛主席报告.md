Title: Asian and Western Newspapers Publish Chairman Mao's Report
Author:
Date: 1957-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd. Many newspapers in India, Japan, Indonesia, and the Philippines have published Chairman Mao Zedong's speech "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People."

　　Delhi newspapers published lengthy summaries of this speech under large headlines. "National Herald," "Free Press," "Hindustan Times," "The Hindu," "The Times of India," "New India Times," "Indian Express," and "The Statesman" published editorials on this speech. Tokyo newspapers prominently featured detailed reports on this speech. "Mainichi Shimbun" and "Japan Times" also published commentaries. Indonesian newspapers such as "Merdeka," "Republik," "Harian Rakjat," "Pedoman," "Abadi," and "Bandung News" reported on Chairman Mao Zedong's speech. "The Indonesian Observer" and "Social Courier" published commentaries. Some Philippine newspapers also published commentaries on Chairman Mao Zedong's speech.

　　All India Radio, Radio Japan, Kyodo News Agency, and Antara News Agency of Indonesia broadcast or forwarded news and summaries of the speech.

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd. Many newspapers in Western countries have published Chairman Mao Zedong's speech "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People." After the speech was published, all major Western news agencies immediately forwarded detailed summary reports.

　　The American "New York Times" published the full text of the speech on the 19th and published an editorial. Many American newspapers such as "New York Herald Tribune," "New York World-Telegram and the Sun," "Washington Post," "Star," "Oakland Tribune," and "The New Englander" also carried news about this speech, and some newspapers published commentaries. London newspapers published news about this speech on the front page on the 19th. Newspapers such as "The Daily Worker," "The Times," "Daily Express," "Daily Telegraph," and "News Chronicle" published editorials or special articles for commentary. The Paris "People's Paper" was the first to publish a summary of the speech and published a commentary. Other major newspapers such as "Le Monde," "Le Figaro," "Combat," and "Le Parisien Libéré" also published commentaries. Many newspapers in West Germany and West Berlin also published news about this speech. "Die Welt," "Telegraf," and "Der Tagesspiegel" published commentaries.

### 亚洲和西方国家报纸刊载毛主席报告

1957-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　亚洲和西方国家报纸刊载毛主席报告
    新华社22日讯　印度、日本、印度尼西亚和菲律宾的许多报纸刊载了毛泽东主席“关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题”的讲演。
    德里各报都用大字标题发表了这篇讲演的长篇摘要。“国民先驱报”、“自由新闻”、“印度斯坦日报”、“印度教徒报”、“印度时报”、“新印度时报”、“印度快报”、“政治家报”就这篇讲演发表了社论。东京各报都在显著地位刊载了关于这篇讲演的详细报道。“每日新闻”、“日本时报”还发表了评论。印度尼西亚的“独立报”、“共和国报”、“人民日报”、“指南针报”、“永恒报”、“万隆新闻”都报道了毛泽东主席讲演的消息。“印度尼西亚观察家报”和“社会使者报”发表了评论。菲律宾的一些报纸也就毛泽东主席的讲演发表了评论。
    全印电台、日本电台、日本共同通讯社和印度尼西亚安塔拉通讯社都广播或者转发了讲演的消息和摘要。
    新华社22日讯　西方国家的许多报纸刊载了毛泽东主席“关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题”的讲演。在这篇讲演发表后，西方国家的所有大通讯社立即转发了详细摘要消息。
    美国“纽约时报”19日全文刊载了这篇讲演并发表了社论。美国许多报纸如“纽约先驱论坛报”、“纽约世界电讯与太阳报”、“华盛顿邮报”、“明星报”、“奥克兰论坛报”、“英格兰人报”也都刊载了关于这篇讲演的消息，有些报纸发表了评论。伦敦报纸19日都以第一版头条的地位刊载了有关这篇讲演的消息。“工人日报”、“泰晤士报”、“每日快报”、“每日电讯报”、“新闻纪事报”等报纸都发表了社论或专文评论。巴黎“人民报”最先刊载了讲演的摘要并发表评论。其他大报如“世界报”、“费加罗报”、“战斗报”、“解放了的巴黎人报”也都发表了评论。西德和西柏林的许多报纸也都刊载了关于这篇讲演的消息。“世界报”、“电讯报”、“每日镜报”都发表了评论。
