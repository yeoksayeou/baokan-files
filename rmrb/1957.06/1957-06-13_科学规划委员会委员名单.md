Title: List of Members of the Scientific Planning Commission

Author:
Date: 1957-06-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The 48th plenary meeting of the State Council approved the new list of members of the State Council's Scientific Planning Commission.

The Scientific Planning Commission was established in March 1956. Over the past year, the work of the commission has undergone significant changes, and the original organization no longer meets the needs of the actual work, so appropriate adjustments have been made. The number of members has increased from the original thirty-five to one hundred and six. The list is as follows (in order of number of strokes in the surname):

　　Director: Nie Rongzhen
