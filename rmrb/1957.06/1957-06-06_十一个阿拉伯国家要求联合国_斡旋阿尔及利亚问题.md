Title: Eleven Arab Nations Ask the UN to Mediate the Algerian Issue
Author:
Date: 1957-06-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 5th: Eleven Arab nations' delegations to the United Nations jointly submitted a memorandum to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, requesting him to "do his best to mediate" in order to help resolve the Algerian issue.

The memorandum was delivered to Hammarskjöld on the 4th by the representatives of Syria and Lebanon. The delegations of the following eleven countries signed the memorandum: Egypt, Iraq, Jordan, Lebanon, Libya, Morocco, Saudi Arabia, Sudan, Syria, Tunisia, and Yemen.

### 十一个阿拉伯国家要求联合国  斡旋阿尔及利亚问题

1957-06-06
第6版()
专栏：

    十一个阿拉伯国家要求联合国
    斡旋阿尔及利亚问题
    新华社5日讯　纽约消息：十一个阿拉伯国家驻联合国的代表团联名给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德一件备忘录，要求他“尽力斡旋”，以帮助解决阿尔及利亚问题。
    这件备忘录已经在4日由叙利亚和黎巴嫩的代表送交哈马舍尔德。在备忘录上签名的有埃及、伊拉克、约旦、黎巴嫩、利比亚、摩洛哥、沙特阿拉伯、苏丹、叙利亚、突尼斯和也门等十一个国家的代表团。
