Title: NPC Standing Committee Meets, Adopts Work Report, and Decides to Solicit Opinions from People's Representatives on Draft Criminal Law
Author:
Date: 1957-06-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th — The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 77th meeting this afternoon. The meeting discussed and adopted the work report of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. After hearing a statement by Wu Xinyu, Deputy Chairman of the Legislative Proposals Committee, on the "Draft Criminal Law of the People's Republic of China," the meeting held discussions and decided to distribute the draft criminal law to the National People's Congress representatives for their opinions.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会开会  通过工作报告并决定向人民代表征求对刑法草案的意见

1957-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常委会开会
    通过工作报告并决定向人民代表征求对刑法草案的意见
    新华社28日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在今天下午举行第七十七次会议。会议讨论和通过了全国人民代表大会常务委员会的工作报告。会议在听了法案委员会副主任委员武新宇关于“中华人民共和国刑法草案”的说明后，进行了讨论，并且决定将刑法草案发给全国人民代表大会代表征求意见。
