Title: Nehru to Go Abroad for a Month to Attend London Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference; Visit Switzerland, Norway and Other Countries and Meet Egyptian and Syrian Leaders
Author:
Date: 1957-06-14
Page: 6

　　Nehru to Go Abroad for a Month
　　Attend London Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference
　　Visit Switzerland, Norway and Other Countries and Meet Egyptian and Syrian Leaders
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 12th -- Indian Prime Minister Nehru will leave Delhi on June 14 for a month-long trip abroad. During his trip, he will visit Scandinavian countries and attend the Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference in London. He will also meet with Syrian President Kuwatly on his way to Europe and with Egyptian President Nasser on his way back to India.

### 尼赫鲁将出国一月  出席伦敦英联邦总理会议  访瑞挪等国并晤埃叙首脑

1957-06-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　尼赫鲁将出国一月
　　出席伦敦英联邦总理会议
　　访瑞挪等国并晤埃叙首脑
    据新华社新德里12日电　印度总理尼赫鲁将在6月14日离开德里赴国外作一个月旅行。在旅行期间，他将访问斯堪的纳维亚国家，并且参加在伦敦举行的英联邦总理会议。他还将在赴欧途中会见叙利亚总统库阿特利，在返国途中同埃及总统纳赛尔会晤。
