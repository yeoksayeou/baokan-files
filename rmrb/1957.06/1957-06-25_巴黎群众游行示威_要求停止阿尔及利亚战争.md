Title: Paris Masses March in Demonstration Demanding an End to the Algerian War
Author:
Date: 1957-06-25
Page: 6

　　Paris Masses March in Demonstration
    Demanding an End to the Algerian War
    Xinhua News Agency, June 24th, TASS Paris dispatch: On the 22nd, the masses in Paris held a march and demonstration, demanding an end to the war in Algeria and requesting that France establish normal relations with Algeria.
    This march and demonstration was jointly initiated by famous social activists and politicians such as René Capitant, Claude Bourdet, Jean-Paul Sartre, François Mauriac, and organizations such as the "Young Republicans," the "People's Liberation Movement," and the "New Left League."
    The Paris police bureau dispatched police forces to block the marching crowds and arrested several people.

### 巴黎群众游行示威  要求停止阿尔及利亚战争

1957-06-25
第6版()
专栏：

　　巴黎群众游行示威
    要求停止阿尔及利亚战争
    据新华社24日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：巴黎群众22日举行游行示威，要求终止阿尔及利亚的战争，并要求法国同阿尔及利亚建立正常关系。
    这次游行示威是由著名社会活动家和政治家如雷纳·加比唐、克劳德·布尔戴、让·保罗·萨特、弗朗索瓦·莫里亚克等人以及“青年共和党”、“人民解放运动”和“新左翼联盟”等组织共同发起的。
    巴黎警察局出动警察部队拦阻游行队伍，并且逮捕了几个人。
