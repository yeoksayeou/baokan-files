Title: US and Iraq Sign Military Transportation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-06-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, June 11th: Iraq and the United States signed two agreements on the 10th, stipulating that the United States will provide 12.5 million US dollars to improve Iraq's telecommunications, highway, and railway facilities.

This is part of the US plan to develop the transportation infrastructure of the Baghdad Pact countries within the scope of the "Eisenhower Doctrine," which is considered to have military purposes. The United States has also signed similar agreements with other Baghdad Pact countries such as Turkey, Iran, and Pakistan.

### 美国和伊拉克签订军事性交通协定

1957-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国和伊拉克签订军事性交通协定
    据新华社11日讯  巴格达消息：伊拉克和美国10日签订了两项协定，规定美国提供一千二百五十万美元来改善伊拉克的电讯和公路、铁路设施。
    这是美国在“艾森豪威尔主义”范围内发展巴格达条约国家交通设施的计划的一部分，这项计划被认为是有军事目的的。美国同土耳其、伊朗、巴基斯坦这些巴格达条约国家也已经签订了同类的协定。
