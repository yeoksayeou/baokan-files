Title: Breaking Down Sectarian and Caste Concepts: Two Ministers of Kerala Marry

Author: 
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 6

    Breaking Down Sectarian and Caste Concepts
    Kerala Ministers Marry

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 6th - The news of the marriage of two ministers of the Communist-led Kerala State government in India has become a popular topic in India.

    Many newspapers have published reports of the wedding of Labour Minister Thomas and Revenue Minister Gauri on May 30 in Trivandrum, the capital of Kerala. In South India, sectarian and caste concepts are very strong, and marriages are generally limited to men and women of the same religion and caste. Thomas is a Catholic, and Gauri comes from a Hindu family. Their union is seen as a symbol of social progress.

### 打破教派和种姓观念  喀拉拉邦两部长结婚

1957-06-07
第6版()
专栏：

    打破教派和种姓观念
    喀拉拉邦两部长结婚
    新华社新德里6日电　共产党领导的印度喀拉拉邦政府的两位部长结婚的消息已经在印度传为佳话。
    许多报纸刊载了劳工部长托马斯和税收部长高丽5月30日在喀拉拉邦首府特里凡得琅举行婚礼的报道。在南印度，教派和种姓观念非常浓厚，婚姻一般只限于同宗教同种姓的男女。托马斯是一个天主教人，高丽出身于印度教徒家庭。他们的结合被认为是社会进步的一种象征。
