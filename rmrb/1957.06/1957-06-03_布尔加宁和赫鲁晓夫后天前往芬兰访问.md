Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev to Visit Finland the Day After Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1957-06-03
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 1st: TASS reports that Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, have accepted the invitation of the President and Government of Finland and will visit Finland on June 5th.
    They are expected to arrive in Helsinki on June 6th and return to the Soviet Union from Finland on June 13th.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫后天前往芬兰访问

1957-06-03
第3版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫后天前往芬兰访问
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科1日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫已经接受芬兰总统和政府的邀请，将在6月5日前往芬兰访问。
    预计，他们将在6月6日抵达赫尔辛基，在6月13日由芬兰返国。
