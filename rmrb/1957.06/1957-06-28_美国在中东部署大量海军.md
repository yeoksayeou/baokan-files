Title: US Deploys Large Naval Force to the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1957-06-28
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 27th, New York dispatch: "Newsweek" reported on the 25th that, according to senior officials of the US Department of Defense, when the political crisis occurred in Jordan not long ago, two-thirds of the entire US Navy was deployed to the Middle East. Now, more than one-third of the navy remains there.

### 美国在中东部署大量海军

1957-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国在中东部署大量海军
    新华社27日讯  纽约消息：“新闻周刊”25日说，据美国国防部的高级官员透露，在不久前约旦发生政治危机的时候，美国全部海军有三分之二被部署在中东。现在，还有三分之一以上的海军留在那里。
