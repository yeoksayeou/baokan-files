Title: Talks Between Representatives of the Italian and French Communist Parties
Author:
Date: 1957-06-29
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 28th, Moscow dispatch: "L'Humanité" reported that representatives of the French and Italian Communist Parties held talks in Paris on June 21st and 22nd. The delegations exchanged views on the cultural development of France and Italy, the policies implemented by the two parties in this regard, and the active activities of the two parties on the ideological front.
　　The French Communist Party delegation consisted of Garodi, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party, Aragon and Figuères, members of the Central Committee, and Canapa, editor-in-chief of "New Review" magazine. The Italian Communist Party was represented by Alicata, a member of the Party's leadership, and Luporini, Trambatore, and Gutuzo, members of the Central Committee.

### 意共和法共代表团会谈

1957-06-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　意共和法共代表团会谈
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社巴黎讯：“人道报”报道，法国和意大利两国共产党代表团6月21日和22日在巴黎举行了会谈。代表团就法国和意大利的文化发展、两党在这方面所执行的政策以及在思想战线上两党的积极活动等问题交换了意见。
    法国共产党代表团由党中央委员会政治局候补委员加罗迪、中央委员会委员阿拉贡和斐盖尔、“新评论”杂志主编卡纳帕组成。代表意大利共产党参加会谈的有党的领导机构成员阿利卡塔、中央委员会委员路波里尼、特朗巴托雷和古图卓。
