Title: Soviet Union Building Large Number of Giant Thermal Power Plants, Generating Capacity Exceeds One Million Kilowatts
Author:
Date: 1957-06-25
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: TASS reports: While building huge hydroelectric power stations, the Soviet Union is constructing many giant thermal power plants with generating capacities exceeding one million kilowatts.
    The Starobeshevsk State Thermal Power Plant is under construction in the Donets Coal Basin. Its generating capacity, like that of the world's largest Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station already generating power on the Volga River, is 2.1 million kilowatts.
    The South Ural Thermal Power Plant, under construction in the Chelyabinsk Oblast, is also one of the first "million-kilowatt" thermal power plants in the Soviet Union.
    In coal-producing regions such as Ukraine, the Urals, and Siberia, large thermal power plants with generating capacities of 1.2 million kilowatts each are also under construction. Each such thermal power plant will be equipped with eight 150,000-kilowatt turbines. The operation and management of all these thermal power plants will be automated and mechanized. Because they are all built near coal-producing areas and will use low-grade coal as fuel, their electricity production costs will be very low.
    Some of the giant thermal power plants under construction are expected to be completed during this Five-Year Plan period (1956-1960). By the end of 1960, a total of ten new power stations with generating capacities ranging from 600,000 kilowatts to 1.2 million kilowatts will begin generating power throughout the Soviet Union. The electricity produced by thermal power plants nationwide will account for 80% of the total electricity production in the Soviet Union.

### 苏联建设大批巨型热电站  发电能力都超过一百万瓩

1957-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联建设大批巨型热电站
    发电能力都超过一百万瓩
    据新华社23日讯　据塔斯社报道：和建设巨大的水电站同时，苏联正在建设许多发电能力在一百万瓩以上的巨型火力发电站。
    顿巴斯煤区正在建设斯塔罗—贝舍夫斯克国家火力发电站。它的发电能力同伏尔加河上已经发电的世界最大的古比雪夫水电站一样，是二百一十万瓩。
    正在车里雅宾斯克州建设的南乌拉尔火力发电站，也是苏联第一批“百万瓩”火力发电站中的一个。
    在乌克兰、乌拉尔和西伯利亚这些产煤地区，还在建设一些发电能力各为一百二十万瓩的大型火力发电站。每个这样的火力发电站将各安装八台功率为十五万瓩的汽轮机。所有这些火力发电站的操纵和管理工作都是自动化和机械化的。由于都建筑在煤产区附近，而且都将用低级煤作燃料，它们的电力生产成本都将非常低。
    在正在建设的巨型火力发电站中，有一些在这一个五年计划期间（1956—1960年）就可以建成。到1960年年底，全苏联总共将有十个发电能力从六十万瓩到一百二十万瓩的新发电站开始发电。全国火力发电站所生产的电力将占苏联电力总产量的80％。
