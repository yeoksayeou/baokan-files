Title: Typhoon
Author:
Date: 1957-06-26
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: At 20:00 on the 25th, the Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a typhoon warning:

　　At 14:00 today, the center of the typhoon was located in the sea area 100 kilometers northeast of Hengchun, Taiwan, at 22.5 degrees north latitude and 121.4 degrees east longitude. Currently, the typhoon center is moving northward along the east coast of Taiwan at a speed of about 10 nautical miles per hour. It is expected that at 14:00 on the 26th, the typhoon center will reach the sea area about 100 kilometers northeast of Taipei. In the next 36 hours, the wind force in the coastal areas of Zhejiang and southern Jiangsu will increase to level 6 to level 8, the wind force in the southern part of the East China Sea will increase to level 10 to level 12, the wind force in the northern part of the East China Sea will increase to level 8 to level 10, the wind force in the southern part of the Yellow Sea will increase to level 5 to level 7, and there will be heavy rain in eastern Zhejiang and the central and southern parts of Jiangsu.

### 台风

1957-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　台风
    新华社25日讯  25日二十点中央气象科学研究所发布台风警报：
    今天下午二时台风中心位置在台湾恒春东北方一百公里的海面上，即北纬二十二点五度、东经一二一点四度的地方。目前台风中心正以每小时十海里左右的速度沿台湾东海岸向北移动，预计26日十四时台风中心可到达台北东北方一百公里左右的海面上。未来三十六小时内，浙江和江苏南部的沿海地区风力将加强到六级到八级，东海南部的风力将加强到十级到十二级，东海北部的风力将加强到八级到十级，黄海南部的风力将加强到五级到七级，浙江东部、江苏中部南部，将有暴雨出现。
