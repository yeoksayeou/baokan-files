　　Gleanings

　　　　The Kangaroo's Ancestor
    Melbourne stone quarry workers recently unearthed a variety of 150,000-year-old animal bones, including kangaroos, bison, and lions. One kangaroo skeleton was over ten feet tall. According to Mr. Gill, the director of the Melbourne Museum, this discovery is the most complete yet.

### 袋鼠的老祖宗

1957-06-24
第3版()
专栏：花絮

　　袋鼠的老祖宗
    墨尔本石矿工人最近发掘出十五万年的兽骨多种，内有袋鼠、野牛和狮子等动物。有一具袋鼠的骨头竟高至十呎以上。据墨尔本博物院的主任吉尔说，这一次的发现是最完整的。
