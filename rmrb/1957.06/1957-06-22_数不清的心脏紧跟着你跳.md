Title: Countless Hearts Beat Along With You
Author: Fu Shi
Date: 1957-06-22
Page: 8

　　Countless Hearts Beat Along With You
　　Fu Shi
　　Hearing your call, my whole body burns with fervor,
　　Thinking of you by my side, strength comes,
　　Touching your light, receiving your warmth,
　　Millennia of frozen ice also melt instantly.

　　How many years of searching, how much anxiety,
　　Finding you to lead, the revolution is victorious,
　　Learning your thought, walking your path,
　　The people's flag flutters in the wind.

　　Hundreds of millions shout your name, hundreds of millions of families laugh,
　　The name of the Communist Party attracts people,
　　How great a creation, how important an event,
　　In your hands, it's light as a feather.

　　Countless eyes are fixed on you,
　　Countless hearts beat along with you,
　　You steer the helm of the new China steadily,
　　You shoulder the heavy burden of history.

### 数不清的心脏紧跟着你跳
俯拾
1957-06-22
第8版()
专栏：

　　数不清的心脏紧跟着你跳
　　俯拾
　　听到你的号召浑身热火烧，
　　想起你在跟前力量就来了，
　　碰到你的光明得到你的热，
　　千年的冻冰也立刻消。
　　多少年找寻多少人心焦，
　　找到你来领导革命胜利了，
　　学习你的思想走着你的路，
　　人民的旗帜迎风飘。
　　亿万人喊你亿万家欢笑，
　　共产党的名字把人吸住了，
　　多伟大的创造多重大的事，
　　到你的手里是轻飘飘。
　　数不清的眼睛紧向着你瞧，
　　数不清的心脏紧跟着你跳，
　　新中国的船舵你掌握的稳，
　　历史的重担你一肩挑。
