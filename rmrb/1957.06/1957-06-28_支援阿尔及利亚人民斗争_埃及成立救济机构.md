Title: Egypt Establishes Relief Organization to Support Algerian People's Struggle

Author:
Date: 1957-06-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 27th: Egypt established a committee on the 26th to provide relief to victims of the Algerian liberation war. This committee will raise funds for the victims of the Algerian liberation war and express respect for their heroic struggle.

This committee is sponsored by Egyptian President Nasser. The committee members include several government ministers. The agency's term of operation is one year. Egyptian Minister of Social Affairs, Shafei, announced the establishment of the committee, saying that Egypt will hold an Algerian week from July 5th to 15th to raise donations.

### 支援阿尔及利亚人民斗争  埃及成立救济机构

1957-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　支援阿尔及利亚人民斗争
    埃及成立救济机构
    新华社开罗27日电  埃及在26日成立了一个救济阿尔及利亚解放战争受难者委员会，来为阿尔及利亚解放战争中的受难者筹集款项，对他们的英雄斗争表示敬意。
    这个委员会得到埃及总统纳赛尔的赞助。委员会的成员中包括政府的几个部长。这个机构的工作期限是一年。埃及社会事务部长沙菲在宣布这个委员会的成立时说，埃及从7月5日到15日将要举行一个阿尔及利亚周来进行募捐。
