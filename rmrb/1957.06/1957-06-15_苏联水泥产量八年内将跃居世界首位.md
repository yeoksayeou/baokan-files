Title: Soviet Cement Production to Leap to World's First Place Within Eight Years
Author:
Date: 1957-06-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 14th: An article in the Soviet "Industrial Economics Newspaper" believes that Soviet cement production can catch up with and surpass the United States before 1965, and become the world's number one.

The article pointed out that after World War II, cement production in many capitalist countries developed rapidly. From 1950 to 1955, cement production in the United States increased from 38 million tons to 52.4 million tons; West Germany increased from 10.9 million tons to 18.8 million tons; and the United Kingdom increased from 9.9 million tons to 12.7 million tons. However, Soviet cement production increased even faster. During the aforementioned period, the Soviet Union's annual cement production more than doubled, increasing from 10.2 million tons to 22.5 million tons. The Soviet Union's total cement production now ranks second in the world, second only to the United States.

During the new Five-Year Plan (1956-1960), the production capacity of Soviet cement factories will increase to 67 million tons. By 1960, the country's cement production will more than double compared to 1955, reaching 52 million tons. During the Seventh Five-Year Plan (1961-1965), the Soviet Union will be able to surpass the United States in terms of total cement production.

### 苏联水泥产量八年内将跃居世界首位

1957-06-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联水泥产量八年内将跃居世界首位
    新华社莫斯科14日电　苏联“工业经济报”的一篇文章认为，苏联的水泥产量在1965年以前就可以赶上和超过美国，而跃居世界第一位。
    这篇文章指出，在第二次世界大战以后，许多资本主义国家的水泥生产发展得很快。从1950年到1955年，美国的水泥产量由三千八百万吨增加到五千二百四十万吨；西德由一千零九十万吨增加到一千八百八十万吨；英国由九百九十万吨增加到一千二百七十万吨。但是，苏联的水泥生产增加得更快。在上面所说的时期内，苏联的水泥年产量却增加了一倍多，即由一千零二十万吨增加到两千二百五十万吨。苏联的水泥总产量现在占世界第二位，仅次于美国。
    在新五年计划（1956—1960年）期间，苏联的水泥制造厂的生产能力将增加到六千七百万吨，到1960年全国的水泥生产将比1955年增加一倍以上，即达到五千二百万吨。在第七个五年计划（1961—1965年）期间，苏联在水泥总产量方面就可以超过美国。
