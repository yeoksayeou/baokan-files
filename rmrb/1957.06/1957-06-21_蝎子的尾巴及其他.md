Title: The Scorpion's Tail and Other Poems
Author: Li Xue'ao
Date: 1957-06-21
Page: 8

　　The Scorpion's Tail and Other Poems
　　Li Xue'ao
    The Scorpion's Tail
    "I have no tail!
    I have no tail!
    Why don't you give me wings,
    And turn me into a butterfly, to fly to the sky?!"
    In the dark corner of the wall,
    It drags a hook dripping with venom,
    Desperately shouting,
    Crawling! Crawling! Crawling! …
    The Soul of "Southern Overlord"
    The corpse of "Southern Overlord" has long rotted,
    But its soul remains in the world.
    It bites its copper-smelling green teeth,
    Wielding a sword soaked with the blood and sweat of the people:
    "I hate! Hate! Hate!
    Hate you for taking my territory!
    Hate you for seizing my power!
    Hate you Communist Party for doing all kinds of evil!
    Hate you for giving the poor food and clothing!
    I hate! Hate! Hate!"
    It hates the people for taking its heaven!
    Our Answer
    No encroachment on the sacred leadership of the Party!
    No destruction of sacred socialism!
    We speak!
    We speak!!
    We speak with the strongest voice of the class!!!
    Taking advantage of the Party's rectification campaign,
    You want to disrupt our ranks!
    Waving the banner of "patriotism,"
    Shooting poisonous arrows at six hundred million people!
    No! No! No!
    We've seen through your tricks!
    The Communist Party
    —Our minds,
    Socialism
    —Our hearts,
    We are the engines of history,
    Any slander or sabotage is futile,
    Only to be crushed to pieces by our wheels!

### 蝎子的尾巴及其他
李学鳌
1957-06-21
第8版()
专栏：

　　蝎子的尾巴及其他
　　李学鳌
    蝎子的尾巴
    “我没有尾巴！
    我没有尾巴！
    你们为什么不给我插上双翅，
    叫我变为蝴蝶，飞上天哪！？”
    在阴暗的墙脚下，
    它拖着蘸满毒液的钩子，
    拼命地叫喊着，
    爬！爬！爬！……
    “南霸天”的魂灵
    “南霸天”的死尸早已腐烂，
    它的魂灵却留在人间。
    它咬着铜臭熏绿的牙齿，
    挥着饱饮人民血汗的剑：
    “我恨！恨！恨！
    恨你们夺了我的地盘！
    恨你们夺了我的大权！
    恨你们共产党无恶不作！
    恨你们叫穷小子有了吃穿！
    我恨！恨！恨！”
    它恨人民夺了它的天！
    我们的回答
    不准夺神圣的党的领导权！
    不准破坏神圣的社会主义！
    我们发言！
    我们发言！！
    我们用阶级的最强音发言！！！
    乘着党整风的台阶，
    想把我们的队伍搅乱！
    打着“爱国”的旗帜，
    向六亿人民放毒箭！
    不准！不准！不准！
    你满肚子鬼把戏早被我们看穿！
    共产党
    ——我们的头脑，
    社会主义
    ——我们的心肝，
    我们是历史的发动机，
    任何诽谤、破坏都无济于事，
    只能被我们的车轮轧得稀烂！
