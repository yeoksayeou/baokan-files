Title: China-Germany Volleyball Match, Third Game: Joint Team White Victorious
Author:
Date: 1957-06-19
Page: 7

    China-Germany Volleyball Match, Third Game
    Joint Team White Victorious
    (Our newspaper reports) The men's and women's volleyball teams of the German Democratic Republic played a friendly match last night at the Beijing Gymnasium against the men's and women's volleyball teams of the China Joint Team White. As a result, both the White team men and women won three to one.
    Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long shook hands with the athletes of both teams one by one after the game and took a picture together.

### 中德排球赛第三场  联队白队告捷

1957-06-19
第7版()
专栏：

    中德排球赛第三场
    联队白队告捷
    本报讯　德意志民主共和国男、女排球队昨晚在北京体育馆内，对我国联队白队男、女排球队进行友谊比赛。结果白队男、女都以三比一获胜。
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理在比赛完毕后与两队运动员一一握手并在一起照了像。
