Title: Ma John Hosts a Banquet to Bid Farewell to the Indonesian Football Team
Author:
Date: 1957-06-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd: Ma John, Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to all members of the Indonesian National Football Team, as well as the Indian referee Chakraborty and the Burmese linesman U Ba Dan. They will leave Beijing on the 5th to return to their country.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, Indonesian Ambassador to China Wirjopranoto and his wife were invited to attend the banquet.

After the banquet, the guests and hosts exchanged gifts. Kosasih Poerwanegara, the leader of the Indonesian football team, pinned the Indonesian football team emblem on Vice Premier He Long's chest.

Also present at tonight's banquet were the deputy directors of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission, the vice-chairmen of the All-China Sports Federation, as well as the heads of the Chinese Football Association and the China-Indonesia Friendship Association.

Representatives from the Indian and Burmese Embassies in China were also invited to attend the banquet.

The Burmese linesman, Cao Genyuan, did not attend tonight's banquet due to a cold.

### 马约翰设宴欢送印度尼西亚足球队

1957-06-04
第2版()
专栏：

    马约翰设宴欢送印度尼西亚足球队
    新华社3日讯　中华全国体育总会主席马约翰今晚设宴欢送印度尼西亚国家足球队全体人员和印度裁判巧克拉瓦蒂、缅甸巡边员吴巴当。他们将在5日离开北京回国。
    周恩来总理，贺龙副总理以及印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托和他的夫人应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会结束以后，宾主双方互赠了礼品。印度尼西亚足球队领队科沙西·普瓦内加拉把印度尼西亚足球队队徽戴在贺龙副总理的胸前。
    出席今晚宴会的还有体育运动委员会的副主任，中华全国体育总会的副主席以及中国足球协会和中国印度尼西亚友好协会的负责人等。
    应邀出席宴会的还有印度和缅甸驻中国大使馆的代表。
    缅甸巡边员曹根元因为感冒没有参加今晚的宴会。
