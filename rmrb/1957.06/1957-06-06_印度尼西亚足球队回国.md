Title: Indonesian Football Team Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-06-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The entire twenty-two members of the Indonesian National Football Team and Burmese linesmen Cao Genyuan and Wu Badang left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

The leader of the Indonesian National Football Team, Kosasih Poerwanegara, told reporters at the airport that they planned to make a short five- or six-day stopover in Jakarta before going to Yangon, Burma, to prepare for the third match against the Chinese National Football Team.

Chokravarti, the Indian international referee who refereed the June 2nd match, will leave Beijing on the 6th to return home.

### 印度尼西亚足球队回国

1957-06-06
第2版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚足球队回国
    新华社5日讯　印度尼西亚国家足球队全体人员二十二人和缅甸巡边员曹根元、吴巴当今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    印度尼西亚国家足球队领队科沙西·普瓦内加拉在飞机场上对记者说，他们准备回雅加达作五、六天短期逗留后，就去缅甸仰光准备第三次同中国国家足球队比赛。
    担任6月2日比赛裁判的印度国际裁判巧克拉瓦蒂，将在6日离北京回国。
