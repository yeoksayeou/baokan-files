Title: Italian Senate President Merzagora Attempts to Form New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1957-06-19
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, June 16th: Italian President Gronchi on the 15th asked the President of the Senate, Cesare Merzagora, a non-partisan figure close to the Christian Democratic Party, to accept a "tentative" mission to form a new government in order to end the Italian political crisis caused by the resignation of the Zoli cabinet on June 10th.

Merzagora said after meeting with Gronchi that the task he had accepted was limited to exploring the possibility of forming a "center-right" government. He stated that he does not intend to be Prime Minister unless no other suitable candidate can be found.

### 意大利上院议长  梅查戈拉试组新阁

1957-06-19
第7版()
专栏：

    意大利上院议长
    梅查戈拉试组新阁
    新华社16日讯　罗马消息：意大利总统格隆基15日要上议院议长、接近天主教民主党的无党派人士契萨雷·梅查戈拉接受组织新政府的“试探性”任务，以便结束由于佐利内阁在6月10日辞职后而引起的意大利政治危机。
    梅查戈拉在同格隆基会谈之后说，他接受的任务仅限于寻找组织一个“中间派”政府的可能性。他表示，他不打算当总理，除非找不到其他适当的人选。
