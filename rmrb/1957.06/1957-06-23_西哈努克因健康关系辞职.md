Title: Sihanouk Resigns Due to Health Reasons
Author:
Date: 1957-06-23
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 22nd - According to today's report in the weekly "Cambodia Current Situation," Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Norodom Sihanouk has submitted his resignation due to health reasons. King Suramarit accepted Sihanouk's resignation on June 21.

　　According to reports, Sihanouk has been overworked since returning to power in April, and doctors believe he needs to go to France for medical treatment.

　　The weekly did not mention a successor to the premiership.

### 西哈努克因健康关系辞职

1957-06-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克因健康关系辞职
    新华社金边22日电　据“柬埔寨现状”周刊今天报道，柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王以健康关系提出了辞职。国王苏拉玛里特已经在6月21日接受了西哈努克的辞职。
    据报道，西哈努克自从4月间重新执政以来，操劳过度，医生认为他需要到法国去就医。
    周刊没有提及继任首相人选。
