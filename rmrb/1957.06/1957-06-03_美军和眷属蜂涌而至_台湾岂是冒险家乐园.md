Title: US Military and Dependents Swarm In: Is Taiwan a Playground for Adventurers?
Author:
Date: 1957-06-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch: According to an Associated Press report: The American transport ship "General Gaffey" is scheduled to arrive in Keelung Harbor on the 1st, carrying more than 1,500 American military personnel and dependents to Taiwan.

    In order to avoid incidents, American military personnel are still under orders from the US military authorities not to go to any places other than the Chiang Kai-shek group's offices, not to go to bars, restaurants and other entertainment venues, and US soldiers are prohibited from going out after 9 pm.

### 美军和眷属蜂涌而至  台湾岂是冒险家乐园

1957-06-03
第3版()
专栏：

    美军和眷属蜂涌而至
    台湾岂是冒险家乐园
    据新华社2日讯　美联社消息：美国运输舰“加非将军号”定于1日到达基隆港，运载一千五百多名美国军事人员和眷属到台湾。
    为了避免发生事件，美国军事人员仍然奉美军当局的命令不要到除了蒋介石集团机关以外的任何地方去，不要到酒吧间、饭馆和其他娱乐场所去，并且禁止美军士兵在晚上九点以后外出。
