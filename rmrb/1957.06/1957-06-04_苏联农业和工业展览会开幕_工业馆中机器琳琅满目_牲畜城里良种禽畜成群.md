Title: Soviet Agricultural and Industrial Exhibition Opens; Industrial Pavilion Dazzles with Machinery; Livestock City Teems with Fine Breeds of Livestock and Poultry
Author:
Date: 1957-06-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd June - TASS reports: The 1957 Soviet Agricultural and Industrial Exhibition opened on June 2nd at the Soviet Agricultural Exhibition Hall in the northern suburbs of Moscow.

Early that morning, the squares and sidewalks of the exhibition hall were crowded with people coming to visit and attend the opening ceremony of the exhibition. Party and state leaders Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Saburov, Khrushchev and others arrived at the exhibition hall at 10 a.m. They were accompanied by Soviet Minister of Agriculture Matskevich and others to visit the exhibition.

At 12 o'clock, the opening ceremony of the exhibition was held in the collective farm square of the exhibition hall. After the meeting, the exhibition halls were officially opened. Thousands of spectators who came here in the rain, advanced agricultural and industrial workers from all over the country, and foreign agricultural delegations visiting Moscow praised the rich and diverse exhibits in the exhibition halls and the achievements made by the Soviet Union in industry and agriculture in the past year.

A total of forty-six border regions, states, and republics participated in this year's agricultural exhibition due to their outstanding achievements in developing agricultural production. The number of advanced agricultural producers participating in the exhibition has increased from 300,000 last year to 400,000. Exhibits illustrating the achievements of animal husbandry occupy a very prominent place this year. In "Livestock City," a total of more than 25,000 cattle, pigs, horses, sheep and more than 8,000 poultry are on display. Unlike the previous three agricultural exhibitions, this year will be the first time to sell fine breeds of livestock to visitors. This year, a total of 1,500 heads of excellent breeds of cattle, horses, sheep and pigs are planned to be sold.

The exhibits displayed in the exhibition halls of the industrial exhibition include: more than 1,000 machines, more than 25,000 instruments and equipment, and a large number of other exhibits illustrating the achievements of Soviet industry and technological progress. In order to exchange and impart advanced experience in the industrial field, more than 300 scientific and technological conferences, workshops and seminars are also planned to be organized this year. The "Science Pavilion" illustrating the achievements of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has also aroused great interest among the audience. (Attached picture)

### 苏联农业和工业展览会开幕  工业馆中机器琳琅满目　牲畜城里良种禽畜成群

1957-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联农业和工业展览会开幕
    工业馆中机器琳琅满目　牲畜城里良种禽畜成群
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社报道：1957年苏联农业和工业展览会6月2日在莫斯科北郊的苏联农业展览馆开幕。
    这一天清早，展览馆的各个广场和人行道上就挤满了前来参观和参加展览会开幕典礼的人。党和国家的领导人布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫等在上午十时来到展览馆。他们在苏联农业部长马茨凯维奇等人陪同下参观了展览会。
    十二时，在展览馆的集体农庄广场上举行了展览会开幕大会。会后，展览会各展览馆就正式开放。冒雨到这里参观的几千观众、来自全国各地的先进农业和工业劳动者以及正在莫斯科访问的外国农业代表团人员，对各展览馆陈列的丰富多样的展览品，以及过去一年苏联在工业和农业上获得的成就称赞不止。
    总共有四十六个边疆区、州和共和国由于在发展农业生产上成绩卓著而参加了今年的农业展览会。参加展览会的先进农业生产者的人数从去年的三十万增加到四十万。说明畜牧业成就的展览品在今年占非常显著的地位。在“牲畜城中”，共计展出了两万五千多头奶牛、猪、马、羊和约八千多只家禽。和前三次农业展览会不同，今年要头一次向参观者出售良种牲畜。今年，总共准备出售一千五百头优良品种的牛、马、羊和猪。
    在工业展览会的各展览馆中展出的展品计有：一千多台机器、二万五千多台仪器和装备，以及大量其他说明苏联工业成就和技术进步的展品。为了交流和传授工业方面的先进经验，今年还准备组织三百多次科学技术会议、讲习班和座谈会。说明苏联科学院工作成就的“科学馆”也引起观众巨大的兴趣。（附图片）
