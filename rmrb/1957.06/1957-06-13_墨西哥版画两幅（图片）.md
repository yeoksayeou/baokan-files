　　Two Mexican Prints
    These two woodcuts both depict the lives of Mexican working people. The one on the right is titled "Construction Workers," by Fernando Castro Pacheco; the one below is titled "Bloodstains in the Rubber Plantation," by Caro Galecio, which is one of his collection of paintings "South of the Equator."

### 墨西哥版画两幅（图片）

1957-06-13
第8版()
专栏：

　　墨西哥版画两幅
    这两幅木刻，都是表现墨西哥劳动人民生活的。右面一幅题为“建筑工人”，作者弗尔南多·卡斯特罗·巴却柯；下面一幅题为“橡胶园中的血迹”，作者加罗·加莱西奥，这是他的画集“赤道线下”中的一幅。
