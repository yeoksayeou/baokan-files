Title: Eisenhower Recovered
Author:
Date: 1957-06-13
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 12th: It is reported that U.S. President Eisenhower resumed his duties on June 11th. It is said that he has essentially recovered from his illness.

### 艾森豪威尔病愈

1957-06-13
第6版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔病愈
    新华社12日讯  华盛顿消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔已在6月11日恢复办公。据说他的病实际上已经痊愈了。
