Title: Britain Protests Chiang Gang's Shelling of Merchant Ship
Author:
Date: 1957-06-25
Page: 5

　　Britain Protests Chiang Gang's Shelling of Merchant Ship
    Xinhua News Agency, June 24th - Taipei News: The British Consul in Taiwan today lodged a protest with the Chiang Kai-shek group "government," protesting the Chiang army's shelling of the British merchant ship "Cheong Fong," and demanding that measures be taken to prevent similar incidents from happening.
    This British merchant ship was recently attacked by Chiang's army's artillery fire from Kinmen Island while entering and leaving Xiamen port. Under the powerful counterattack and cover of the Chinese People's Liberation Army artillery, the merchant ship safely departed.

### 英国抗议蒋帮炮击商船

1957-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　英国抗议蒋帮炮击商船
    新华社24日讯　台北消息：英国驻台湾领事今日向蒋介石集团“政府”提出抗议，抗议蒋军炮击英国商船“成丰号”，并且要求采取措施防止发生同样事件。
    英国这只商船最近在进出厦门港时却遭到金门岛上蒋军的炮火阻击，在中国人民解放军炮兵的有力还击和掩护下，这只商船才安全离去。
