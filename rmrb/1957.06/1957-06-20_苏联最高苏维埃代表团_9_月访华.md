Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation to Visit China in September
Author:
Date: 1957-06-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 19th. At the invitation of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union will send a delegation of the Supreme Soviet to China in September this year for a friendly visit.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团9月访华

1957-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团9月访华
    新华社19日讯　苏联最高苏维埃应我国全国人民代表大会常务委员会刘少奇委员长的邀请，将在今年9月派遣最高苏维埃代表团来中国进行友好访问。
