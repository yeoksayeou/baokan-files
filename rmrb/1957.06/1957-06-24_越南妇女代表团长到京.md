Title: Head of Vietnamese Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd. Nguyen Thi Thap, Head of the Vietnamese Women's Delegation to China and President of the Vietnam Women's Union, arrived in Beijing by train this evening. Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, welcomed her at the station.

　　Dinh Thi Can, the Deputy Head of the delegation, and nine other members had already arrived in Beijing on the 21st.

　　The delegation will learn about and study women's work in our country, and participate in the Third National Women's Congress of China.

### 越南妇女代表团长到京

1957-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    越南妇女代表团长到京
    新华社23日讯　越南妇女访华代表团团长、越南妇女联合会主席阮氏十在今天晚上乘火车到达北京。中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅到车站欢迎。
    代表团的副团长丁氏谨和团员共九人已经先期在21日到达北京。
    代表团将在我国了解、研究妇女工作，并参加我国第三次妇女代表大会。
