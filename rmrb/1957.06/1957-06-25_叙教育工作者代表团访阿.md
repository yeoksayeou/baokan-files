Title: Syrian Education Workers Delegation Visits Albania
Author:
Date: 1957-06-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Report: A Syrian education workers delegation, after a nine-day visit to Albania at the invitation of the Albanian government, departed Tirana on the 22nd. This is the first delegation from a Middle Eastern country to visit Albania at the invitation of the Albanian government after its recent expressions of strengthening friendship with Middle Eastern countries and improving relations with neighboring countries.

### 叙教育工作者代表团访阿

1957-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　叙教育工作者代表团访阿
    据新华社讯　叙利亚教育工作者代表团应邀在阿尔巴尼亚进行了为时九天的访问后，已经在22日离开地拉那。这是阿尔巴尼亚政府近来表示要加强同中近东国家的友好和改善同邻国的关系后，第一个应邀前来访问的中东国家代表团。
