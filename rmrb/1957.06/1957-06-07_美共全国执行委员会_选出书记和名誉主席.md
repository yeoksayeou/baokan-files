Title: CPUSA National Executive Committee Elects Secretaries and Honorary Chairman
Author:
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 6th: The New York "Daily Worker" published a message on June 4th stating that the National Administrative Committee of the Communist Party of the United States announced on June 3rd that at a meeting held on May 22nd and May 23rd, the CPUSA National Executive Committee elected seven National Secretaries. These seven secretaries will undertake the tasks of the Administrative Committee and are responsible for handling the affairs of the Communist Party during the intervals between the monthly National Executive Committee meetings.

The seven National Secretaries are: Davis (responsible for Black affairs), Dennis (responsible for national affairs), Fine (responsible for labor affairs), Gates (responsible for public affairs), Jackson (responsible for Southern affairs), Hyman Lumer (responsible for education and publication), and Stein (responsible for organization).

Foster was elected Honorary Chairman.

The Administrative Committee was authorized to continue establishing various committees to accelerate the implementation of the decisions of the Sixteenth National Congress.

### 美共全国执行委员会  选出书记和名誉主席

1957-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

    美共全国执行委员会
    选出书记和名誉主席
    新华社6日讯　纽约消息：纽约“工人日报”6月4日刊登消息说，美国共产党全国行政委员会6月3日宣布，美共全国执行委员会在5月22日到5月23日举行的会议上选出了七名全国书记。这七名书记担负起行政委员会的任务，在每月举行一次的全国执行委员会会议间隔期间，负责处理共产党的事务。
    这七名全国书记是：戴维斯（负责黑人事务）、丹尼斯（负责全国事务）、法因（负责劳工事务）、盖茨（负责公共事务）、杰克逊（负责南部事务）、希曼·路默（负责教育和出版）和斯坦因（负责组织）。
    福斯特当选为名誉主席。
    行政委员会受权继续设立各种委员会来加速贯彻执行第十六次全国代表大会的决定。
