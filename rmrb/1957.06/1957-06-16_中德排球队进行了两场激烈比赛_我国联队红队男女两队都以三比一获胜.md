Title: Sino-German Volleyball Teams Held Two Intense Matches; China's United Team Red Team Wins Both Men's and Women's Games Three to One
Author:
Date: 1957-06-16
Page: 5

    Sino-German Volleyball Teams Held Two Intense Matches
    China's United Team Red Team Wins Both Men's and Women's Games Three to One
    (Our Newspaper) News: The Democratic Republic of Germany's men's and women's volleyball teams played a friendly match against China's United Team Red Team men's and women's volleyball teams last night at the Beijing Gymnasium. As a result, both the Chinese men's and women's teams won three to one.
    Although the guest team's female players, Körner and Rheinhardt, had hard and powerful spikes, many were blocked and saved by the Red Team. However, the Red Team's Hua Guiqing and Wang Zishu's fierce spikes, along with the team's close cooperation, enabled the Red Team to win with scores of 15-7, 15-12, 13-15, and 15-10.
    The men's team match dazzled the audience, who were caught up in the action. The fierce spikes, blocks, and saves from both sides earned continuous applause from the audience. The home team achieved victory with diverse tactics such as quick spikes, staggered attacks, and spread-out plays, winning with scores of 15-10, 15-7, 13-15, and 15-7.
    After the match, Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Nie Rongzhen met with the athletes from both sides, shook hands with everyone, and took photos as souvenirs.

### 中德排球队进行了两场激烈比赛  我国联队红队男女两队都以三比一获胜

1957-06-16
第5版()
专栏：

    中德排球队进行了两场激烈比赛
    我国联队红队男女两队都以三比一获胜
    本报讯　德意志民主共和国男、女排球队昨日晚上在北京体育馆内，对中国联队红队男、女排球队进行了一场友谊比赛，结果中国男女两队都以三比一获胜。
    客队女队的柯尔恩和莱茵荷得的扣球虽然硬而有力，但多被红队拦住和救起，而红队的花桂卿、王子淑的猛扣和全队密切的配合，使红队以十五比七、十五比十二、十三比十五和十五比十赢得了胜利。
    男队的比赛，使观众看得眼花缭乱、应接不暇，双方的猛扣、拦网和抢救险球，博得了观众不断的掌声。主队以快扣、梯次和拉开的多样战术，取得了十五比十、十五比七、十三比十五和十五比七的胜利。
    比赛完毕，陈毅、聂荣臻副总理接见了双方运动员，并和大家一一握手和照像留念。
