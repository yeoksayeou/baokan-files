Title: Ambassador Yudin Hosts Reception to Bid Farewell to Soviet Writers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 14th—Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, hosted a reception in Beijing this evening to bid farewell to the visiting Soviet Writers Delegation.

　　Those invited to the reception included Lao She, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association; poets Xiao San and Ai Qing; writers Zhao Shuli, Liu Baiyu, and Chen Baichen; translator Chen Bingyi; and young Uyghur writer Kerimu Hojayev, among others.

　　Ambassador Yudin delivered a speech at the beginning of the reception, and proposed a toast to the unbreakable friendship between the Soviet Union and China and the friendship between the writers of the two countries.

　　The head of the Soviet Writers Delegation, Musrepov, expressed his reluctance to depart in his speech. He deeply thanked his Chinese friends for their hospitality.

　　Lao She also delivered a speech wishing the delegation a safe journey home.

　　During the reception, the Chinese and Soviet writers frequently toasted each other, wishing the literary endeavors of both countries increasing prosperity and flourishing growth.

### 尤金大使举行酒会送别苏联作家代表团

1957-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　尤金大使举行酒会送别苏联作家代表团
    新华社14日讯　苏联驻华大使尤金今晚在北京举行酒会，为访问中国的苏联作家代表团送行。
    应邀参加酒会的有中国作家协会副主席老舍，诗人萧三、艾青，作家赵树理、刘白羽、陈白尘，翻译家陈冰夷和维吾尔族青年作家克里木·赫捷耶夫等人。
    尤金大使在酒会开始时致词，他并提议为苏中两国牢不可破的友谊和两国作家之间的友谊干杯。
    苏联作家代表团团长穆斯雷波夫在讲话中表示了惜别之情。他深深感谢中国朋友的招待。
    老舍也致词祝代表团在归途中一路平安。
    席间，中苏两国作家频频干杯，互祝两国文学事业日益繁荣昌盛。
