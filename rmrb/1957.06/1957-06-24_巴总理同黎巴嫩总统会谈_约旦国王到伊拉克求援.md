Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Meets with Lebanese President; Jordanian King Seeks Aid in Iraq
Author:
Date: 1957-06-24
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: TASS Beirut, June 22nd: According to the "Oriental Daily," Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy, who is on an official visit to Lebanon, held negotiations with Lebanese President Chamoun regarding the relationship between Lebanon and the Baghdad Military Pact.

　　"Telegraph" says that Suhrawardy's visit to Lebanon is a step in activities aimed at expanding the Baghdad Pact.

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: Baghdad News: Jordanian King Hussein arrived in Baghdad, the capital of Iraq, by plane on June 22nd for a three-day unofficial visit.

　　He was accompanied by Jordanian Prime Minister Hashim, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Rifai, Chief of the Royal Court Tarhouni, as well as the Minister of Economy and the Minister of Finance.

　　It is reported that the primary issue in the talks between Hussein and his cousin, Iraqi King Faisal, is a request for financial assistance from Iraq.

　　Sources say that the major parties of the disbanded "National Front" held a secret meeting in Nablus this week, at which they drafted a declaration condemning the government's policies and measures to suppress public opinion.

### 巴总理同黎巴嫩总统会谈  约旦国王到伊拉克求援

1957-06-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　巴总理同黎巴嫩总统会谈
    约旦国王到伊拉克求援
    据新华社23日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特6月22日讯：据“东方报”报道，在黎巴嫩作正式访问的巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底就黎巴嫩同巴格达军事条约的关系问题同黎巴嫩总统夏蒙举行了谈判。
    “电讯报”说，苏拉瓦底的黎巴嫩之行是以扩大巴格达条约的目的的活动的一个步骤。
    据新华社23日讯　巴格达消息：约旦国王侯赛因在6月22日乘飞机到达伊拉克首都巴格达，作为期三天的非正式访问。
    同他一起到达的还有约旦首相哈希姆、副首相兼外交大臣里法伊、宫廷总管塔尔霍尼以及经济大臣和财政大臣。
    据报道，侯赛因和他的堂兄伊拉克国王费萨尔会谈的首要问题是请求伊拉克给予财政援助。
    有消息说，已被解散的“全国阵线”的各主要政党本星期在纳布鲁斯举行了秘密会议，会议拟定了一项宣言，谴责政府的政策以及压制舆论的措施。
