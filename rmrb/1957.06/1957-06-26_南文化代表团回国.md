Title: Yugoslav Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-06-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 24th: The Yugoslav cultural delegation headed by Jože Vilfan Demar left Guangzhou this morning via Hong Kong to return to their country.

### 南文化代表团回国

1957-06-26
第5版()
专栏：

    南文化代表团回国
    据新华社广州24日电  以约·维·德玛尔为首的南斯拉夫文化代表团，今晨离开广州取道香港返国。
