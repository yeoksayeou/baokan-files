Title: Mongolia-China-North Korea-Soviet Athletics Competition Concludes; Our Team Wins Seventeen Championships
Author:
Date: 1957-06-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, June 24th: The Chinese track and field team won seventeen championships in the twenty-four events of the Mongolia, China, North Korea and Soviet Buryat-Mongolian Autonomous Republic track and field competition held in Ulaanbaatar.

The four-nation track and field competition closed on the 23rd. Eight competitions were held before the closing ceremony, and China participated in all of them. The stands around the stadium were packed with spectators. The exciting men's and women's 400-meter relay races received cheers and applause from the audience, and the Chinese team won both events.

A total of twenty-four competitions were held in the four-nation track and field competition over three days. The Soviet Buryat-Mongolian team won six championships. The Mongolian team won the men's 400-meter championship and broke six national records. The North Korean team broke two national records.

### 蒙中朝苏田径赛结束  我队获得十七项冠军

1957-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　蒙中朝苏田径赛结束
    我队获得十七项冠军
    据新华社乌兰巴托24日电　中国田径队在乌兰巴托举行的蒙、中、朝和苏联布利亚特蒙古自治共和国四国田径赛的二十四项比赛中获得了十七项冠军。
    四国田径赛已经在23日闭幕。闭幕前举行了八项比赛，中国全部参加。运动场四周看台上挤满了观众。精采的男女四百公尺接力赛得到观众的喝采和掌声，中国队在这两项比赛中都获得了冠军。
    四国田径赛三天内共举行了二十四项比赛。苏联布利亚特蒙古队得到六项冠军。蒙古队获得男子四百公尺冠军，并且有六项打破国家纪录。朝鲜队有两项打破国家纪录。
