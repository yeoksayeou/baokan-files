Title: Team Member Ding Xingyou Sacrifices His Life
Author: 
Date: 1957-06-18
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Ding Xingyou, a member of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Mountaineering Team and a teaching assistant in meteorology at the Physics Department of Peking University, tragically died on May 28th after being buried by an avalanche during a high-altitude marching exercise on Mount Gongga.

A funeral committee has been formed, consisting of Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Jiang Yinen, Director of the Office of Peking University, Tu Changwang, Director of the Central Meteorological Bureau, Zhao Jiuzhang, Director of the Institute of Geophysics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Lü Jiong, Director of the Agrometeorology Research Office of the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences, and Sun Xiaocun, President of Beijing Agricultural University, to organize memorial services at regular intervals.

### 队员丁行友牺牲

1957-06-18
第7版()
专栏：

    队员丁行友牺牲
    新华社17日讯　中华全国总工会爬山队队员、北京大学物理系气象专业助教丁行友，5月28日在贡嘎山一次高山行军练习中，遭遇雪崩掩埋牺牲。
    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚、北京大学办公室主任蒋荫恩、中央气象局局长涂长望、中国科学院地球物理研究所所长赵九章、中国农业科学院农业气象研究室主任吕烱、北京农业大学校长孙晓村等组成治丧委员会，定期举行追悼。
