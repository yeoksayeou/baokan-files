Title: Australian Labor Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st — The four-member Australian Labor Party delegation, led by Leslie Haylen, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon from Guangzhou. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 澳工党议员访华团到京

1957-06-22
第5版()
专栏：

    澳工党议员访华团到京
    新华社21日讯　以里斯利·海伦为首的澳大利亚工党议员访华团一行四人，在今天下午乘飞机从广州到达北京。他们是应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国访问的。
