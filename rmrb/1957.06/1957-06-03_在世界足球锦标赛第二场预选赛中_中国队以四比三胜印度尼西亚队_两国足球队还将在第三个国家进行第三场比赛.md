Title: In the Second Preliminary Round of the World Football Championship, China Defeats Indonesia Four to Three. The Two National Football Teams Will Play a Third Match in a Third Country
Author:
Date: 1957-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd: The Chinese national football team defeated the Indonesian national football team today in Beijing by a score of four to three.

Now, the Chinese national football team and the Indonesian national football team have each won one match (two points for a win) in the two preliminary rounds of the World Football Championship. According to the regulations of the International Federation of Association Football, the two national football teams will play a third match in a third country.

Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Chen Yi, and Nie Rongzhen, Indonesian Ambassador to China Sukardjo Wirjopranoto, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China watched the match.

Today, the national flags of China and Indonesia were hung high on the rostrum of the Xian Nong Tan Stadium. On the grandstand opposite the rostrum, the national flags of India and Burma were hung to pay tribute to the Indian international referee Waqti and the Burmese linesmen and international referees Cao Genyuan and Wu Badang who officiated at this match.

After the ninety-minute match ended, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, accompanied by Indonesian Ambassador to China Sukardjo Wirjopranoto and Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation Ma John, went to the field to shake hands with the players from both sides and the Indian referee Waqti, as well as the Burmese linesmen Cao Genyuan and Wu Badang, and took photos with them. ### 在世界足球锦标赛第二场预选赛中  中国队以四比三胜印度尼西亚队  两国足球队还将在第三个国家进行第三场比赛

1957-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    在世界足球锦标赛第二场预选赛中
    中国队以四比三胜印度尼西亚队
    两国足球队还将在第三个国家进行第三场比赛
    据新华社2日讯　中国国家足球队今天在北京以四比三战胜了印度尼西亚国家足球队。
    现在，中国国家足球队同印度尼西亚国家足球队在世界足球锦标赛的两场预选赛中，已经各胜一场（胜一场得两分）。根据国际足球联合会规定，两国足球队还将在第三个国家进行第三场比赛。
    朱德副主席，周恩来总理，邓小平、贺龙、陈毅、聂荣臻副总理和印度尼西亚驻中国大使苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托以及各国驻华使节观看了这场比赛。
    今天在先农坛体育场的主席台上高挂着中国和印度尼西亚的国旗。主席台对面的看台上，挂着印度和缅甸的国旗，这是向担任这场比赛的印度国际裁判华克蒂和缅甸巡边员、国际裁判曹根元、吴巴当致敬。
    在历时九十分钟的比赛结束以后，周恩来总理、贺龙、陈毅副总理由印度尼西亚驻中国大使苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托和中华全国体育总会主席马约翰陪同，到球场上同双方队员和担任这场比赛的印度裁判华克蒂，以及缅甸巡边员曹根元和吴巴当一一握手，并且同他们在一起照了像。（附图片）
