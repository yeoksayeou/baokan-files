Title: American Capitalists Seek Amulet, Discuss Promoting "People's Capitalism"
Author:
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Port, Tang Dig State, June 6th: A group meeting composed of twelve people, including American industrialists, labor leaders, editors, and a Yale University professor, recently held a meeting at Yale University to discuss how to promote so-called "people's capitalism."

Yale University Graduate School Dean Synott, the host of this group discussion, said that "people's capitalism" is "an appropriate and attractive term that can help us have a fresh and vivid view of our system."

It is said that the American Advertising Council was the first to use the term "people's capitalism."

### 美国资本家找护身符  商量宣传“人民资本主义”

1957-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

    美国资本家找护身符
    商量宣传“人民资本主义”
    新华社6日讯　唐?狄格州新港消息：由美国工商业家、劳工领袖、编辑和耶鲁大学教授一共十二个人组成的一个小组会，最近在耶鲁大学举行了会议，讨论如何宣传所谓“人民资本主义”问题。
    耶鲁大学研究院院长、这个小组讨论会的主持人西诺特说，“人民资本主义”是“一个适当的和吸引人心的名辞，它能帮助我们对我们的制度有一种新鲜和生动的看法”。
    据说，最初使用“人民资本主义”这个名辞的是美国广告理事会。
