Title: U.S. Military Commander in the Pacific Clamors for the Use of Rockets in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1957-06-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th, Tokyo: U.S. Military Commander in the Pacific, Stump, arrived in Tokyo on the 28th from Taipei.

Stump said at a press conference held before leaving Taipei that he believed the situation in the Taiwan Strait was "gradually changing." If the situation develops to the point where the United States and "Nationalist China" have to jointly fight according to their mutual defense treaty, then the American "Matador" rockets recently deployed in Taiwan "will become a necessary weapon between here and the mainland." As for whether this weapon should be equipped with atomic warheads, Stump said it was up to Eisenhower to decide.

### 美军太平洋区总司令  叫嚣要在台湾使用火箭

1957-06-30
第6版()
专栏：

    美军太平洋区总司令
    叫嚣要在台湾使用火箭
    据新华社29日讯　东京消息：美军太平洋区总司令斯图普在28日从台北到达东京。
    斯图普在离开台北以前举行的记者招待会上说，他认为台湾海峡的局势“逐渐在变化”。如果局势发展到美国和“国民党中国”要根据它们的共同防御条约而去共同作战，那么最近设置在台湾的美国“斗牛士”式火箭“将成为这里和大陆之间的一种必要武器”。至于这种武器是否要配备原子弹头，斯图普说要艾森豪威尔决定。
