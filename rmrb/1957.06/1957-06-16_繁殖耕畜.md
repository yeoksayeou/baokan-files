Title: Breeding Draught Animals
Author: Jing Ying, Xian Zheng
Date: 1957-06-16
Page: 4
    Breeding Draught Animals

    (Our report) The Yihe Agricultural Cooperative in Pingyao County, Shanxi Province, in order to help fraternal cooperatives breed livestock and develop agricultural production, designated several cooperative members with experience in mating to lead male animals to nearby townships and cooperatives to assist in mating. Since the start of this work in early March, they have bred more than two hundred female animals. (Jing Ying, Xian Zheng)

### 繁殖耕畜
京瑛  咸正
1957-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    繁殖耕畜
    本报讯　山西平遥县义和农业社，为了帮助各兄弟社繁殖牲畜，发展农业生产，曾确定社里的几个配种有经验的社员，牵着公畜分赴附近乡、社，帮助配种。自3月初开始这项工作以来，已给二百多头母畜配了种。　（京瑛、咸正）
