Title: Luzon Earthquake Throws Many People from Their Beds
Author:
Date: 1957-06-14
Page: 6

    Luzon Earthquake
    Throws Many People from Their Beds
    Xinhua News Agency, June 13th, Manila dispatch: A violent earthquake occurred in the northern and central parts of Luzon Island in the Philippines in the early morning of June 12th. It is said that its intensity was rare in the past twenty-seven years.
    In South Ilocos Province, the earthquake, which lasted for fifty-two seconds, shook many people from their beds. In some villages, the cracks that opened in the ground were up to twenty meters long, three meters deep, and at least three-tenths of a meter wide. Many government buildings, churches, and civilian houses were damaged.

### 吕宋岛地震  把许多人从床上抛下来

1957-06-14
第6版()
专栏：

    吕宋岛地震
    把许多人从床上抛下来
    新华社13日讯　马尼拉消息：菲律宾的吕宋岛的北部和中部在6月12日清早发生了猛烈地震，据说其剧烈程度为二十七年来所罕见。
    在南依罗戈省，历时五十二秒钟的地震把许多人从床上震下来。在一些村子里，地上震裂出来的沟长达二十公尺、深三公尺、宽起码有十分之三公尺。许多政府机关房屋、教堂和民房被震坏了。
