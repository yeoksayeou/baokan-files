Title: Participate in Voluntary Labor in Factories, Mines and Rural Areas: More Than 20,000 East German Students Sign Up
Author:
Date: 1957-06-12
Page: 5

　　Participate in Voluntary Labor in Factories, Mines and Rural Areas
　　More Than 20,000 East German Students Sign Up
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 4th - According to a report by the German News Agency: Schroeder, the secretary of the Central Council of the Free German Youth League, announced that as of June 1st, more than 25,000 students in East Germany have voluntarily signed up to participate in voluntary labor in mines, factories, and farms.
    At the same time, three hundred foreign students studying at the Dresden University of Technology are also preparing to participate in a week of voluntary labor in East Germany during this year's summer vacation.
    Student representatives from Leipzig have also issued a challenge to all students in East Germany to participate in voluntary labor in factories, mines, and rural areas.

### 到工矿农村参加义务劳动  民主德国两万多学生报名

1957-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　到工矿农村参加义务劳动
　　民主德国两万多学生报名
    新华社柏林4日电  据德意志通讯社报道：自由德国青年联盟中央理事会书记施罗德宣布，截至6月1日，民主德国已经有二万五千名学生自愿到矿场、工厂和农场参加义务劳动。
    同时，在德累斯顿高等技术学校学习的三百名外国留学生也准备在今年暑假在民主德国参加一个星期的义务劳动。
    来比锡的学生代表也为了到工矿和农村参加义务劳动向民主德国的全体学生发出了挑战书。
