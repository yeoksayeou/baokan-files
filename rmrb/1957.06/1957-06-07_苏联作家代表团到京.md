Title: Soviet Writers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: The Soviet Writers Delegation, invited by the Chinese Writers Association, which attended the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Writers Representative Conference in Urumqi in May, arrived in Beijing today, led by the head of the delegation, Musrepov, Chairman of the Writers Association of the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic.

The delegation is composed of seven well-known writers from the Soviet Union's Kazakh, Uzbek, Turkmen, Kyrgyz, and Tajik Union Republics.

### 苏联作家代表团到京

1957-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联作家代表团到京
    据新华社6日讯　应中国作家协会邀请，在5月间到乌鲁木齐参加新疆维吾尔自治区作家代表会议的苏联作家代表团，今日在团长、哈萨克苏维埃社会主义共和国作家协会主席穆斯雷波夫率领下到达北京。
    代表团是由苏联的哈萨克、乌兹别克、土库曼、吉尔吉斯、塔吉克等加盟共和国的七个著名作家组成的。
