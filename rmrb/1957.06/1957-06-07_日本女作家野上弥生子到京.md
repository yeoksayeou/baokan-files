Title: Japanese Woman Writer Yaeko Nogami Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: Japanese writer Yaeko Nogami and her daughter, Mieko Nogami, arrived in Beijing by train on June 6th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 日本女作家野上弥生子到京

1957-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    日本女作家野上弥生子到京
    据新华社6日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的日本作家野上弥生子和她的女儿野上三枝子在6日乘火车到达北京。
