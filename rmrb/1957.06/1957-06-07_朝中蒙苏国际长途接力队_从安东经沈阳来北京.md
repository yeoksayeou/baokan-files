Title: The Korean-Chinese-Mongolian-Soviet International Long-Distance Relay Team Arrives in Beijing from Andong via Shenyang
Author: Wen Tianshen
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 5

This newspaper's Shenyang dispatch on the 6th (reporter Wen Tianshen) reports: The Korean, Chinese, Mongolian, and Soviet International Long-Distance Relay Team, celebrating the Sixth World Youth Festival, arrived in Shenyang, the third city in Northeast China, at 7:00 PM today.

The international long-distance relay team started on May 30th from the Panmunjom Korean Armistice Agreement signing hall by Korean athletes. It will pass through China, Mongolia, and reach the venue of the World Youth Festival—the Lenin Central Stadium in Moscow.

At 4:00 PM on June 5th, Chinese athletes received the relay baton from ten Korean athletes on the banks of the Yalu River. In two days, they have passed through the two cities of Andong and Benxi and four relay points. After receiving the relay baton from Benxi City today, the relay athletes of Shenyang City continued their 15,000-meter relay run through the bustling streets of the city. Two hundred young workers and students also participated in the relay run. Led by fifteen athletes holding torches high, they relayed to the Shenyang No. 1 Lighting Stadium in sections. Along the way, they were warmly welcomed by 300,000 people in Shenyang.

At 8:30 PM, Zhang Hongjun, secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and more than 10,000 young people from all walks of life enthusiastically saw off ten Shenyang motorcycle athletes who will continue the relay run. They will arrive in Jinzhou City at 8:00 PM tomorrow afternoon, and then athletes from Jinzhou and Shanhaiguan will continue to advance towards our capital, Beijing.

### 朝中蒙苏国际长途接力队  从安东经沈阳来北京
文天申
1957-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

    朝中蒙苏国际长途接力队
    从安东经沈阳来北京
    本报沈阳6日电　记者文天申报道：庆祝第六届世界青年联欢节的朝、中、蒙、苏国际长途接力队，今天下午七时来到了中国东北第三个城市沈阳。
    国际长途接力队5月30日由朝鲜运动员自板门店朝鲜停战协定签字大厅起跑。它将经过中国、蒙古，到达世界青年联欢节的会场——莫斯科列宁中央运动场。
    6月5日下午四时，中国运动员在鸭绿江边从十名朝鲜运动员手里接到接力棒后，两天内已经经过了安东、本溪两个市和四个接力点。沈阳市的接力运动员，今天在接到由本溪市传来的接力棒后，继续开始了通过市区热闹大街的一万五千公尺接力跑，同时参加接力跑的还有二百名青年工人和学生，他们在十五个高举着火炬的运动员的带头下，分段接力跑到沈阳市第一灯光球场。途中受到三十万沈阳人民的夹道欢迎。
    晚上八时半，青年团沈阳市委员会书记张鸿钧和一万多名各界青年热烈地欢送了十名继续接力跑的沈阳摩托车运动员，他们将在明天下午八时到达锦州市，然后将由锦州、山海关的运动员继续向我国首都北京前进。
