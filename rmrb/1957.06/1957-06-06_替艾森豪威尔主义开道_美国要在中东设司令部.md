Title: Clearing the Way for the Eisenhower Doctrine: The U.S. to Establish a Command in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1957-06-06
Page: 6

    Clearing the Way for the Eisenhower Doctrine
    The U.S. to Establish a Command in the Middle East
    Xinhua News Agency, June 5th dispatch from Tehran: The "Talaji" weekly reported on the 3rd that the United States will take measures to establish a command in the Middle East. U.S. Admiral Boone, Commander-in-Chief of the Atlantic Eastern and Mediterranean Fleets, has already visited Iran, and a large fleet will be sent to the Persian Gulf, with the headquarters set up in the Persian Gulf region. The U.S. fleet sent to the Persian Gulf will consist of aircraft carriers and other warships equipped with atomic artillery. The "Talaji" weekly emphasized that the establishment of a U.S. command in the Middle East is linked to the purpose of "promoting a new Eisenhower Doctrine in the Middle East." The weekly continued, according to U.S. newspaper reports, a network of radar installations will be established on the northern border of Iran and Iraq, and the U.S. government intends to supply some countries with atomic weapons.

### 替艾森豪威尔主义开道  美国要在中东设司令部

1957-06-06
第6版()
专栏：

    替艾森豪威尔主义开道
    美国要在中东设司令部
    据新华社5日讯  据塔斯社德黑兰讯：“塔拉吉”周刊3日报道，美国将采取措施在中东设立司令部。美国驻大西洋东部和地中海舰队总司令海军上将布恩已经去伊朗访问，一支很大的舰队将派往波斯湾，并且把司令部设在波斯湾地区。派往波斯湾的美国舰队将由配备发射原子炮弹装备的航空母舰和其他军舰组成。“塔拉吉”周刊强调指出，美国在中东设立司令部是同“在中东推行新的艾森豪威尔主义”的目的联系着的。这家周刊继续写道，据美国报纸报道，伊朗和伊拉克的北部边界将建立雷达装置网，美国政府打算供给若干国家原子武器。
