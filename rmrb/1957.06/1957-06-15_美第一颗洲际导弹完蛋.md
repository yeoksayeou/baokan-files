Title: US's First Intercontinental Missile Fails

Author:

Date: 1957-06-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: A message from Florida, USA: The United States Air Force authorities announced that the first "Atlas" intercontinental ballistic missile designed by the United States, with a range of five thousand miles, exploded in mid-flight on June 11th after being launched from the missile test center at Cape Canaveral.
    A United States Air Force spokesperson declined to reveal details of the accident encountered by the missile during its initial flight.

### 美第一颗洲际导弹完蛋

1957-06-15
第6版()
专栏：

    美第一颗洲际导弹完蛋
    据新华社14讯　美国佛罗里达州消息：美国空军当局宣布，美国设计出来的第一颗射程为五千英里的“阿特拉斯式”洲际弹道导弹6月11日从卡纳维拉尔的导弹试验中心发射出去之后，在飞行中途爆炸了。
    美国空军发言人拒绝透露这颗导弹在初步飞行中所遇事故的细节。
