Title: National People's Congress Meeting Opens Today; Standing Committee Yesterday Approved Drafts of the Meeting Agenda and Presidium List; Premier Zhou Enlai to Deliver Government Work Report at the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 76th meeting this afternoon, discussing the agenda of the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress.

The Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress will open as scheduled on the 26th. Premier Zhou Enlai will deliver a government work report after the opening ceremony.

At today's meeting, Secretary-General Peng Zhen explained the preparatory meeting of the Fourth Session of the National People's Congress and the preparations for the Fourth Session. The meeting then adopted the draft agenda of the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress, the draft list of the Presidium and Secretary-General of the Fourth Session, and the draft list of the Director and members of the Proposals Examination Committee of the Fourth Session. The above drafts will be submitted to the Fourth Session of the National People's Congress for approval as recommendations.

After listening to the explanation by Zhou Xing, Vice Minister of Public Security, the meeting adopted the Regulations on the People's Police of the People's Republic of China.

After listening to the explanation by Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, the meeting discussed Premier Zhou Enlai's proposal of the State Council regarding appropriately increasing the private plots of members of agricultural producers' cooperatives, and adopted the decision on increasing the private plots of members of agricultural producers' cooperatives.

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: The State Council held its 53rd plenary meeting today, at which the "Government Work Report" that Premier Zhou Enlai will deliver to the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress was discussed and approved.

### 全国人民代表大会会议今日开幕  常委会昨日通过大会议程和主席团名单等草案  周恩来总理将在会上作政府工作报告

1957-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会会议今日开幕
    常委会昨日通过大会议程和主席团名单等草案
    周恩来总理将在会上作政府工作报告
    新华社25日讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天下午举行第七十六次会议，讨论了第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议议程问题。
    第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议将于26日按期开幕。周恩来总理将在开幕后作政府工作报告。
    在今天的会议上，彭真秘书长就有关全国人民代表大会第四次会议的预备会议和第四次会议各项准备工作的情况，作了说明。会议接着通过了本届全国人民代表大会第四次会议议程草案、第四次会议主席团和秘书长名单草案、第四次会议提案审查委员会主任委员和委员名单草案。上述各项草案将作为建议，提交全国人民代表大会第四次会议通过。
    会议在听取了公安部副部长周兴的说明后，通过了中华人民共和国人民警察条例。
    会议在听取了农业部部长廖鲁言的说明后，讨论了国务院周恩来总理提出的关于适当增加农业生产合作社社员自留地的议案，通过了关于增加农业生产合作社社员自留地的决定。
    新华社25日讯  国务院在今天举行了第五十三次全体会议，会议讨论和通过了周恩来总理将向第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议所作的“政府工作报告”。
