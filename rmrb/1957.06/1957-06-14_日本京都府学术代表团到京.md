Title: Japanese Kyoto Prefecture Academic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-14
Page: 5

    Japanese Kyoto Prefecture Academic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Japanese Kyoto Prefecture Academic Delegation, consisting of nine members led by head of delegation Toshiro Shigesawa, arrived in Beijing this evening at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences for a visit and study tour of our country.

### 日本京都府学术代表团到京

1957-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

    日本京都府学术代表团到京
    新华社13日讯　应中国科学院邀请前来我国参观访问的日本京都府学术代表团一行九人，在团长重泽俊郎率领下，于今晚到达北京。
