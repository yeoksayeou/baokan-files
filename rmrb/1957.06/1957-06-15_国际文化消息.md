　　International Cultural News
　　"Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai" Film Welcomed in Uruguay's Capital
　　The Chinese color film "Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai" was previewed in Montevideo, the capital of Uruguay. The audience included people from industry and commerce, cultural and scientific circles, as well as foreign diplomats and representatives of trade groups. The film was warmly welcomed by them.
　　"Blessing" Film Praised in Moscow
　　China's first color film "Blessing" has been praised by audiences and newspapers in Moscow. "Pravda" wrote in an article entitled "A Major Achievement of Chinese Cinema" on the 12th, that "Blessing" does not only depict the fate of one Xianglin Sao, but the fate of millions of poor peasant women in old China.
　　Soviet Union Creates Largest "Panoramic Film"
　　The Soviet Union has created the world's largest and best "panoramic film" device, with a screen more than twenty-seven meters wide and about eleven meters high. This was stated recently to journalists by Professor Goldovsky, who is responsible for designing this device in the Soviet Union. He also said that the United States has been studying wide-screen films for fifteen years, while the Soviet Union has only studied it for more than a year. The Soviet Union's design of "panoramic film" is completely original and has not adopted the experience of other countries.
　　Chaplin and Robeson Invited to Participate in the Festival
　　Moscow Radio announced on the evening of the 12th that world-renowned film artist Chaplin and black singer Robeson have been invited to Moscow to spend the Sixth World Youth Festival with young people from all over the world.
                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际文化消息

1957-06-15
第5版()
专栏：国际文化消息

　　国际文化消息
    乌拉圭首都欢迎梁祝影片
    中国的彩色影片“梁山伯与祝英台”在乌拉圭首都蒙得维的亚举行预映。观众有各地的工商界人士、文化科学界人士以及外国的外交使节和贸易团体的代表们。影片受到他们的热烈欢迎。
    “祝福”影片在莫斯科获好评
    中国第一部彩色影片“祝福”在莫斯科获得观众和报刊的好评。“真理报”12日在一篇题为“中国电影的重大成就”中写道，“祝福”不只是描写一个祥林嫂的命运，而是千百万旧中国贫农妇女的命运。
    苏联创制最大“全景影片”
    苏联已经创制一种世界最大、最好的“全景电影”装置，它的银幕宽二十七公尺多，高达十一公尺左右。这是苏联负责设计这种装置的戈尔多夫斯基教授最近对新闻记者说的。他还说，美国在宽银幕电影方面已经研究了十五年，而苏联只研究了一年多。苏联在设计“全景电影”时，完全是独创的，并没采取别国的经验。
    卓别林罗伯逊被邀参加联欢节
    莫斯科电台12日晚上宣布，世界著名的电影艺术家卓别林和黑人歌唱家罗伯逊已经被邀请到莫斯科去同全世界青年一起共度第六届世界青年联欢节。
                  （新华社讯）
