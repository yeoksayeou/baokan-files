Title: China Islamic Cultural Association and Islamic Association Jointly Host a Banquet for Soviet Ethnic Minority Writers
Author:
Date: 1957-06-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The China Islamic Cultural Association and the China Islamic Association jointly hosted a banquet today to welcome the Soviet Writers Delegation, composed of writers from various ethnic minorities of the Soviet Union, who are visiting China.

　　Before the banquet, Liu Geping, Director of the China Islamic Cultural Association, and Da Pongsheng, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association, introduced to the distinguished Soviet guests the situation of the ten ethnic groups in China that believe in Islam in terms of ethnic regional autonomy, socialist transformation, culture, and religion.

　　The guests also introduced the economic and cultural construction achievements of various ethnic groups in the Soviet Union that believe in Islam since the October Revolution.

　　After the banquet, the distinguished guests also visited the precious Hui cultural relics exhibited by the China Islamic Cultural Association.

### 我回民文化协进会和伊斯兰教协会  联合宴请苏联少数民族作家

1957-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　我回民文化协进会和伊斯兰教协会
　　联合宴请苏联少数民族作家
    据新华社13日讯　中国回民文化协进会和中国伊斯兰教协会，今天联合设宴招待来我国参观访问的由苏联各少数民族作家组成的苏联作家代表团。
    宴会前，中国回民文化协进会主任刘格平和中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生等，向苏联贵宾们介绍了我国信仰伊斯兰教的十个民族实行民族区域自治、社会主义改造以及文化、宗教方面的情况。
    客人们也介绍了苏联信仰伊斯兰教的各民族在十月革命以后的经济、文化建设方面的成就。
    宴会后，贵宾们还参观了回民文化协进会展出的回族珍贵文物。
