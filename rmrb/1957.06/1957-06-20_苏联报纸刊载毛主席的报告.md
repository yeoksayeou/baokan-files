Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish Chairman Mao's Report
Author:
Date: 1957-06-20
Page: 1

    Soviet Newspapers Publish Chairman Mao's Report
    (Our Moscow dispatch, June 19) Today, "Pravda" published the full text of Chairman Mao Zedong's speech at the Supreme State Conference of China, "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People," across pages three, four, and five. "Izvestia," "Red Star," "Trud," and "Soviet Russia" all published summaries of this speech.

### 苏联报纸刊载毛主席的报告

1957-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联报纸刊载毛主席的报告
    本报莫斯科19日电　今天“真理报”以三、四、五三个版的篇幅全文刊载了毛泽东主席在中国最高国务会议上的讲演：“关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题”。“消息报”、“红星报”、“劳动报”和“苏维埃俄罗斯报”都刊载了这篇讲演的摘要。
