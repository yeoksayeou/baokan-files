Title: Poznań International Fair Opens
Author:
Date: 1957-06-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 9th - The 26th Poznań International Fair officially opened on the 9th. In addition to Poland, a total of thirty countries are participating in this fair.
	The Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, presided over the opening ceremony.

### 波兹南国际博览会开幕

1957-06-10
第3版()
专栏：

    波兹南国际博览会开幕
    新华社华沙9日电　第二十六届波兹南国际博览会9日正式开幕。除波兰以外，共有三十个国家参加这次的博览会。
    波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹主持了开幕仪式。
