Title: Micro-Essays
Author: Yi Sha
Date: 1957-06-26
Page: 8

　　Micro-Essays
　　Yi Sha
    Pearl

　　The pearl
　　Hidden in the seabed
　　Never thought of showing off
　　Only when it dedicates itself to others
　　Does it discover its value

    Rainbow

　　I don't want to become a rainbow
　　To win people's praise and envy
　　Because all its brilliance and splendor
　　Is rain drops bearing the gift of sunshine

    Rose

　　Someone planted a rose
　　He got the flower and the thorns
　　So he believes that everything in the world
　　Is a principle like planting roses
　　As for me, I planted a peony
　　It is no less beautiful than a rose
　　There are more than hundreds and thousands of
　　flowers without thorns in the garden

    Surge

　　Have you ever seen the surge in the sea
　　It makes the entire sea heave with it
　　But it doesn't make a sound
　　People still perceive its enormous strength and confidence
　　You, however, are willing to be a leaping wave
　　Intoxicated by that explosion that roars and commands the wind and clouds
　　The splashing water returns to the sea
　　But you yourself turn into a void

### 微言集
易莎
1957-06-26
第8版()
专栏：

　　微言集
　　易莎
    珍珠
　　藏在海底的珍珠
　　从来没想到要炫耀自己
　　当它把自己献给别人
　　它才发现自己的价值
    虹
　　我不愿化作一条长虹
　　去博得人们的赞美和羡慕
　　因为它全部的灿烂和绚丽
　　是雨珠承受了阳光的赐予
    玫瑰
　　有人种了一株玫瑰
　　他得到花同时又得到刺
　　所以他认定世界上的一切
　　像种玫瑰一样，是一个道理
　　我呢，种了一棵牡丹
　　它并不比玫瑰难看
　　花园里不带刺的花
　　何止几百几千
    涌
　　你是否见过大海里的漫涌
　　它使整个大海都随着自己掀动
　　可是它一点也不露出声色
　　人们同样觉察到它那巨大的力量和信心
　　你却愿意做一朵飞跃的浪花
　　迷醉于那一声咤叱风云的爆炸
　　飞溅的水沫复归到海里
　　你自己却化作一团空虚
