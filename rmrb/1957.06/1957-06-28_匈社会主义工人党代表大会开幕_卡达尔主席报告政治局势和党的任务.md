Title: Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Congress Opens, Chairman Kádár Reports on the Political Situation and Party Tasks
Author:
Date: 1957-06-28
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 27th: The National Congress of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party opened in Budapest this morning.

　　The meeting was presided over by Münnich, a member of the Executive Committee of the Provisional Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party. After delivering the opening address, he passed the list of members of the Credentials Committee and the following agenda: 1. Report of the Provisional Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party; 2. Report on the revision of the Party Constitution; 3. Report on strengthening and replenishing the Party's leadership. The meeting has already begun the first item on the agenda. Kádár, Chairman of the Provisional Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party, delivered a report on the political situation and the tasks of the Party on behalf of the Provisional Central Committee.

### 匈社会主义工人党代表大会开幕  卡达尔主席报告政治局势和党的任务

1957-06-28
第5版()
专栏：

　　匈社会主义工人党代表大会开幕
    卡达尔主席报告政治局势和党的任务
    新华社布达佩斯27日电  匈牙利社会主义工人党全国代表会议今天上午在布达佩斯开幕。
    会议由社会主义工人党临时中央委员会执行委员会委员明尼赫主持。在他致开幕词以后，会议通过了代表资格审查委员会名单和下列会议议程：一、关于社会主义工人党临时中央委员会的报告；二、关于修改党章的报告；三、关于加强和充实党的领导机构的报告。会议已经开始进行第一项议程。社会主义工人党临时中央委员会主席卡达尔在会上作了临时中央委员会关于政治局势和党的任务的报告。
