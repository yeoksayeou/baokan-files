Title: World's Oldest Person Dies: Koloyev Passes Away at 157

Author:

Date: 1957-06-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tbilisi, 9th: According to TASS, Yegor Koloyev, a 157-year-old Soviet man, recently passed away. He was the longest-living person in the world. The old man lived in the village of Yermania on the southern slope of the Caucasus Mountains. In 1812, when Napoleon invaded Russia, he served as a cook's assistant to General Aleksey Yermolov, a hero of the Patriotic War.

According to data collected by the Tbilisi Institute of Labor Hygiene and Occupational Diseases, there are currently more than 20,000 people over the age of 90 in the Georgian Republic. The academy is also publishing a "Longevity Collection," which includes photos and biographical materials of the long-lived elderly people still living in the republic.

### 世界最长命的老人逝世  科罗耶夫享年一百五十七岁

1957-06-10
第3版()
专栏：

    世界最长命的老人逝世
    科罗耶夫享年一百五十七岁
    据新华社9日讯　据塔斯社第比利斯8日讯：一百五十七岁的苏联老人叶戈尔·科罗耶夫最近逝世。他是世界上寿命最长的人。这位老人居住在高加索山主脉南麓的叶尔马尼亚村，他在1812年拿破仑侵犯俄国的时候，作过卫国战争英雄阿列克耶尔莫洛夫将军的司厨助手。
    根据第比利斯劳动卫生和职业病科学院考察团搜集的资料，目前格鲁吉亚共和国共有两万多个九十岁以上的老人。这个科学院还正在出版一部“长寿集”，包括有这个共和国内现在还活着的长寿老人们的照片和传记材料。
