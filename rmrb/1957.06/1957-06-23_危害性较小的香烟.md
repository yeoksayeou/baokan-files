Title: Less Harmful Cigarettes
Author:
Date: 1957-06-23
Page: 5

　　Less Harmful Cigarettes
    Xinhua News Agency: An American doctor, Waddell, recently said at a meeting of the American Cancer Research Society that washing off the wax from fresh tobacco leaves with a special solution can produce less harmful cigarettes. This was reported not long ago by the British "Manchester Guardian". The doctor said that in experiments on mice, he and his assistants found that the wax in tobacco leaves and stems is the main cause of cancer.

### 危害性较小的香烟

1957-06-23
第5版()
专栏：花絮

　　危害性较小的香烟
    美国一个医生韦德尔最近在美国癌研究学会的会议上说，用特制的溶液把新鲜烟叶上的蜡质洗掉，就能制出危害性较小的香烟。这是英国“曼彻斯特卫报”不久前报道的。这个医生说，他和他的助手在对老鼠做的实验中，发现烟叶、烟梗的蜡质是引起癌症的主要原因。
        （新华社）
