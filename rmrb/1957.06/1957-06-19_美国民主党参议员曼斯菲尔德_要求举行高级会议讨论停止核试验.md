Title: US Democratic Senator Mansfield Calls for High-Level Meeting to Discuss Cessation of Nuclear Testing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-19
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 18th—U.S. Democratic Senator Mansfield requested a high-level meeting in the Senate on June 17th to discuss stopping the testing of nuclear weapons with an explosive force greater than one million tons.

Mansfield, during the Senate's discussion on ratifying the international treaty on the peaceful use of atomic energy, said, "If we continue these tests, we are gambling with the future generations of mankind." He said that the current nuclear tests have already contaminated the air to some extent, therefore, "This is certainly a very important issue, worthy of heads of state to explore together ways to reach a limitation or complete cessation of hydrogen bomb tests."

Mansfield also stated that he could not agree that a ban on nuclear weapon testing must be linked to a disarmament agreement. He believes that this "is actually an all-encompassing or all-or-nothing attitude."

The chairman of the Senate Disarmament Subcommittee, Democrat Humphrey, said at the meeting that the United States should hold a meeting with the Soviet Union on the recent Soviet disarmament proposals, "accept their challenge."

Mansfield, Humphrey, and another Democratic Senator, Pastore, also accused the U.S. government at the meeting of damaging the "prestige" of the U.S. representative Stassen in disarmament negotiations, who held a somewhat positive attitude toward reaching a partial disarmament agreement. They pointed out that there had been conflicts between Secretary of State Dulles and Stassen. Stassen has been attacked and blamed, he is the U.S. representative in London, but he does not have the prestige that a person in this position should have.

### 美国民主党参议员曼斯菲尔德  要求举行高级会议讨论停止核试验

1957-06-19
第7版()
专栏：

　　美国民主党参议员曼斯菲尔德
　　要求举行高级会议讨论停止核试验
    新华社18日讯　华盛顿消息：美国民主党议员曼斯菲尔德6月17日在参议院要求举行高级会议，讨论停止试验爆炸力在一百万吨以上的核武器。
    曼斯菲尔德在参议院讨论批准国际和平利用原子能条约问题时说：“如果我们继续进行这些试验的话，我们就是把人类的后代当作赌注。”他说，目前所进行的原子核试验已经在某种程度上毒化了空气，因此，“这肯定是一个很重要的问题，值得各国首脑在一起探求达成限制或完全停止氢弹试验的方法。”
    曼斯菲尔德还表示，他不能同意禁止核武器试验必须与裁军协议联系起来，他认为，这“实际上是一种包罗万象或空无一物的态度”。
    参议院裁军小组委员会主席、民主党人汉弗来在这次会议上说，美国应该同苏联就苏联最近所提出的裁军建议举行会议，“接受他们的挑战”。
    曼斯菲尔德、汉弗来和另一个民主党参议员帕斯托在这次会议上还指责美国政府损害对达成局部裁军协议抱某种积极态度的美国代表史塔生在裁军谈判中的“威信”。他们指出，国务卿杜勒斯和史塔生之间曾经发生冲突。史塔生受到了攻击和责备，他在伦敦是美国的代表，但是，他没有一个处于这种地位的人所应有的威信。
