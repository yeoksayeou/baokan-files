Title: Burmese Union Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Beijing; To Fly to Moscow Today for Visit
Author:
Date: 1957-06-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 9th: The fourteen-member Burmese Union Parliamentary Delegation, led by the Vice-Speaker of the Burmese Union Parliament's Chamber of Nationalities, U De Kin Din Maung, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning for a visit to the Soviet Union at the invitation of Lobanov, Chairman of the Soviet Supreme Soviet of the Union, and Lacis, Chairman of the Soviet Supreme Soviet of Nationalities. Li Jishen, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Deputy Secretaries-General Zhang Su and Liu Guanyi, Qian Weichang, Vice-President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, and Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to the airport to welcome them. Also at the airport to welcome them were Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin and First Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, U Ba Maw.
	The delegation will fly to Moscow on the 10th.

### 缅甸联邦国会代表团到京  今天将乘飞机前往莫斯科访问

1957-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸联邦国会代表团到京
    今天将乘飞机前往莫斯科访问
    据新华社9日讯　应苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫和民族院主席拉齐斯的邀请，前往苏联访问的缅甸联邦国会代表团一行十四人，在缅甸联邦国会民族院副议长德钦登貌率领下，今日上午乘飞机到达北京。中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，副秘书长张苏、刘贯一等，中缅友好协会副会长钱伟长，外交部亚洲司司长章文晋前去机场欢迎。去机场欢迎的，还有苏联驻华大使尤金和缅甸驻华大使馆一等秘书吴巴茂。
    代表团将在十日乘飞机去莫斯科。
