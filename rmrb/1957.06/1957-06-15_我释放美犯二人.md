Title: We Release Two American Prisoners

Author:
Date: 1957-06-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 14th. American prisoners, Zhai Guanghua and He Yongzhi, were released on schedule on the 14th after serving their sentences.

Zhai Guanghua and He Yongzhi violated Chinese law by collecting military, political, and economic intelligence, spreading rumors, and engaging in espionage activities within China. He Yongzhi also concealed a radio receiver and illegally purchased foreign exchange. Both were arrested in Shanghai on June 15, 1953.

### 我释放美犯二人

1957-06-15
第5版()
专栏：

    我释放美犯二人
    新华社上海14日电　美国犯人翟光华和何雍之在14日服刑期满，我主管机关已把他们如期释放。
    翟光华和何雍之因为在我国境内收集军事、政治、经济情报，散布谣言、进行间谍活动而违犯了我国法律；何雍之还隐藏收报机，非法套购外汇。两人都是1953年6月15日在上海被捕的。
