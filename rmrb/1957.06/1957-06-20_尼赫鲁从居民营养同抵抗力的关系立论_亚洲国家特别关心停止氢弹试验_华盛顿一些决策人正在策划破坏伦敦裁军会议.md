Author:
Date: 1957-06-20
Page: 6

　　Nehru Argues from the Relationship Between Resident Nutrition and Resistance
　　Asian Countries Are Particularly Concerned About Stopping Hydrogen Bomb Tests
　　Some Decision-Makers in Washington Are Planning to Disrupt the London Disarmament Conference
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 19th: Copenhagen Dispatch: Indian Prime Minister Nehru said at a press conference held in Copenhagen on the 17th that all of Asia is paying great attention to the developments of the London Disarmament Conference.
　　Nehru emphasized the concern of Asian countries about banning hydrogen bomb tests, saying that most people in Asian countries are malnourished and absorb less calcium, making them more vulnerable to the hazards of radioactive particles than Westerners.
　　Nehru said that his country "is generally not a well-fed country." Bad food provides less protection against strontium-90 than good food.
　　He also said that the Indian government is "very anxious" about the issue of banning hydrogen bomb tests, and both houses of the Indian Parliament have passed resolutions on this issue, calling for an immediate halt to testing or a complete ban on hydrogen bombs.
　　Nehru made these remarks before visiting Finland, Norway, and Sweden. He left Copenhagen for Helsinki early on the 18th and plans to attend the Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference scheduled for June 26th in London after visiting Northern Europe.
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 19th: TASS Moscow Dispatch, June 18th: The "Izvestia" correspondent in New York, commenting on the current "political disputes" in the United States surrounding the U.S. representative to the London talks, Stassen, pointed out: Behind this behind-the-scenes trick lies a dangerous plan, a plan directly intended to disrupt the London disarmament talks. From the newspaper's comments, it can be seen that Washington decision-makers believe that Stassen should use any means to slander Soviet proposals, drive the Disarmament Subcommittee into a dead end, and put the blame for disrupting the London talks on the Soviet Union.

### 尼赫鲁从居民营养同抵抗力的关系立论  亚洲国家特别关心停止氢弹试验  华盛顿一些决策人正在策划破坏伦敦裁军会议

1957-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　尼赫鲁从居民营养同抵抗力的关系立论
　　亚洲国家特别关心停止氢弹试验
　　华盛顿一些决策人正在策划破坏伦敦裁军会议
    新华社19日讯　哥本哈根消息：印度总理尼赫鲁17日在哥本哈根举行的记者招待会上说，整个亚洲正以极大兴趣注意着伦敦裁军会议发展情况。
    尼赫鲁强调指出亚洲国家对禁止氢弹试验的关切，他说，亚洲国家人民大多营养不良，吸收的钙比较少，因此比西方人更容易遭受放射微粒的危害。
    尼赫鲁说，他的国家“总起来说不是一个吃得很好的国家”。不好的食物比良好的食物所提供的抵抗锶—90的保护力量要小。
    他还说，印度政府对禁止氢弹试验问题是“十分焦虑”的，印度议会两院已经在这个问题上通过决议，并且呼吁立即停止试验或者根本禁止氢弹。
    尼赫鲁是在去芬兰、挪威、瑞典访问之前说这些话的。他已经在18日清晨离开哥本哈根飞往赫尔辛基，并且计划在访问北欧之后出席定于6月26日在伦敦举行的英联邦总理会议。
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科18日讯：“消息报”驻纽约记者在评论美国目前围绕出席伦敦谈判的美国代表史塔生而展开的“政治纠纷”时指出：这个幕后把戏隐藏着危险的计划，这种计划是要直接打算破坏伦敦的裁军谈判。从报纸的评论中可以看出，华盛顿决策人的意见是，史塔生应当不惜以任何手段来诽谤苏联的建议，把裁军小组委员会引入绝境，并且把破坏伦敦谈判的罪名加在苏联身上。
