Title: North Atlantic Group Creates Tension, Conducts Atomic War Exercises in the Mediterranean

Author:

Date: 1957-06-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Nicosia, June 26th: The North Atlantic Group's land, sea, and air forces began unprecedented large-scale atomic warfare exercises in the Mediterranean region on June 25th.

Participating in the three-day exercise are jet bombers and fighters from six countries—the United States, Britain, France, Italy, Greece, and Turkey—as well as the U.S. Sixth Fleet, the British and Greek navies, and the Italian, Greek, and Turkish armies. It is reported that the purpose of the exercise is to "test the strength of the North Atlantic Group's southern flank against aerial atomic attacks" and to "check the air defense network from Gibraltar to eastern Turkey."

The exercise will be coordinated by the North Atlantic Group's Southern Air Defense Command near Naples.

### 北大西洋集团制造紧张  在地中海演习原子战

1957-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    北大西洋集团制造紧张
    在地中海演习原子战
    据新华社26日讯  尼科西亚消息：北大西洋集团的陆、海、空军6月25日开始了地中海地区空前规模的原子战争演习。
    参加这次为期三天的演习的有美国、英国、法国、意大利、希腊和土耳其六国的喷气轰炸机和战斗机、美国第六舰队、英国和希腊的海军以及意大利、希腊和土耳其的陆军。据报道：这次演习的目的是要“考验北大西洋集团南翼抵御空中原子攻击的力量”和“检查从直布罗陀到土耳其东部的防空网”。
    演习将由那不勒斯附近的北大西洋集团南部防空司令部统筹安排。
