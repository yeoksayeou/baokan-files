Title: Exhibition of Soviet Art Masterpieces in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 18th: An exhibition of works by eight famous Soviet artists opened today at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace.

The more than 450 works on display include the world-renowned sculptures of the late Soviet People's Artist V.I. Mukhina, satirical drawings and book illustrations by Kukryniksy (Kupriyanov, Krylov, Sokolov), well-known to Chinese art circles and the general public, as well as works with different styles by the famous portrait sculptor Tomsky, the oldest Soviet printmaker Favorsky, the decorator Bimanov, and the fairy tale illustrator Rachov.

Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister of Culture and Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, said at the opening ceremony: This exhibition is a good opportunity for Chinese art circles to learn from Soviet art circles.

### 北京展出苏联美术名作

1957-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

    北京展出苏联美术名作
    据新华社18日讯　苏联八位著名美术家作品展览今天在北京劳动人民文化宫开幕。
    展出的四百五十多件作品中，包括已故的苏联人民艺术家维·伊·穆希娜举世闻名的雕塑，以及为中国美术界和广大人民群众熟悉的库克雷尼克塞（库普利雅诺夫、克雷洛夫、索柯洛夫）的讽刺画和书籍插图，还有著名肖像雕刻家托姆斯基、苏联最老的版画家法沃尔斯基、装璜画家毕曼诺夫、童话插图画家拉奇夫具有不同风格的作品。
    文化部副部长、中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞在开幕式上致词说：这次展览会是中国美术界向苏联美术界学习的一个好机会。
