Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Receive the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai received the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation headed by Mullick Rahman this evening.

Those present at the reception included Vice-Chairman Peng Zhen, Ambassador Geng Biao, and Zhang Bangying, Qu Wu, and Yu Xinqing.

Pakistani Ambassador to China, A.H.B. Ahamed, was also present at the reception.

During the reception, the guests presented gifts to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou. (Photo attached)

Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou receiving the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation. The fourth person from the right is Delegation Leader Rahman.　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Hou Bo.

### 毛主席和周总理接见巴基斯坦议会代表团

1957-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席和周总理接见巴基斯坦议会代表团
　　新华社26日讯  毛泽东主席和周恩来总理今天晚上接见了以穆·拉赫曼为首的巴基斯坦议会代表团。
　　接见的时候，在座的有彭真副委员长、耿飚大使和张邦英、屈武、余心清。
　　接见的时候，巴基斯坦驻中国大使阿哈默德也在座。
　　接见的时候，客人向毛主席和周总理赠送了礼品。（附图片）
　　毛主席和周总理接见巴基斯坦议会代表团。右起第四人是拉赫曼团长。  　　　　　　　　新华社记者  侯波摄
