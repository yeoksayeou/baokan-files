Title: Ho Chi Minh Receives Chinese Buddhist Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 4th: President Ho Chi Minh received the Chinese Buddhist Delegation at the Presidential Palace at 7:00 AM today. Chinese Ambassador Luo Guibo accompanied the delegation.

Present at the meeting were Hoang Quoc Viet, representative of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, and Venerable Thich Tri Do, a prominent Vietnamese monk, among others.

President Ho Chi Minh inquired in detail about the situation of the Buddhist community in China and expressed his hope for more exchanges and sharing of experiences between the two countries, China and Vietnam, given their close proximity.

### 胡志明接见我佛教代表团

1957-06-05
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明接见我佛教代表团
    据新华社河内4日电　胡志明主席今天上午七时在主席府接见了中国佛教代表团。中国大使罗贵波陪同晋见。
    接见时越南祖国战线代表黄国越、越南著名法师释智度等多人在座。
    胡志明主席详细地询问了中国佛教界的情况，并且表示中越两国相隔很近，希望能更多来往，交流经验。
