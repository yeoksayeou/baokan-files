Title: Zhu Kezhen Cables the Special Committee for the International Geophysical Year Protesting its Subservience to the Plot of Creating "Two Chinas"
Author:
Date: 1957-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th: Today, Zhu Kezhen, Chairman of the National Committee of the People's Republic of China for the International Geophysical Year, sent a telegram to Professor Chapman, Chairman of the Special Committee for the International Geophysical Year. In the telegram, Zhu Kezhen stated that the National Committee of the People's Republic of China for the International Geophysical Year lodges a serious protest against the executive bureau of the Special Committee for the International Geophysical Year for arbitrarily changing the name of the National Committee of the People's Republic of China for the International Geophysical Year to the Chinese International Geophysical Year Committee (Beijing), and for recognizing the so-called "Academia Sinica in Taipei" as the Chinese International Geophysical Year Committee (Taipei) to participate in the Special Committee for the International Geophysical Year. The telegram stated: "When the National Committee of the People's Republic of China for the International Geophysical Year joined the Special Committee for the International Geophysical Year in 1955, before the Western Pacific Regional Conference in February 1957, and in my letter to you on April 10th, I repeatedly expressed our unwavering and clear position, which is: Taiwan has been an inseparable part of China since ancient times, and it is only due to foreign interference and obstruction that the current situation in Taiwan has arisen. Therefore, Taiwan should absolutely not be treated as another independent country outside the People's Republic of China to participate in the Special Committee for the International Geophysical Year, alongside the National Committee of the People's Republic of China for the International Geophysical Year, which has already been recognized by the Special Committee for the International Geophysical Year. The National Committee of the People's Republic of China for the International Geophysical Year absolutely cannot agree to this erroneous practice of subordinating international scientific cooperation activities to the plot of creating 'two Chinas' by certain forces internationally. Now, on behalf of the National Committee of the People's Republic of China for the International Geophysical Year, I formally notify you: We firmly demand the revocation of this erroneous decision. Before this decision is corrected, the National Committee of the People's Republic of China for the International Geophysical Year has decided to withdraw from the Special Committee for the International Geophysical Year and all its activities. Please circulate my letter to you of April 10th and this telegram to all national committees of the Special Committee for the International Geophysical Year."

### 竺可桢电国际地球物理年专门委员会  抗议它屈从制造“两个中国”的阴谋

1957-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    竺可桢电国际地球物理年专门委员会
    抗议它屈从制造“两个中国”的阴谋
    据新华社29日讯　今天，中华人民共和国国际地球物理年国家委员会主席竺可桢，打电报给国际地球物理年专门委员会主席查普曼教授。竺可桢在电报中说，中华人民共和国国际地球物理年国家委员会对国际地球物理年专门委员会执行局，擅自将中华人民共和国国际地球物理年国家委员会改成中国国际地球物理年委员会（北京），并承认所谓“台北中国科学院”作为中国国际地球物理年委员会（台北）参加国际地球物理年专门委员会一事，提出严重抗议。电报说：“中华人民共和国国际地球物理年国家委员会曾于1955年参加国际地球物理年专门委员会时和1957年2月西太平洋区域会议前以及我4月10日给您的信中，再三地表达了我们的不可动摇的明确的立场，这就是：台湾自古就是中国不可分割的一部分，仅仅由于外国的干涉和阻挠才形成目前台湾的情况，因之就绝对不应该把台湾当作在中华人民共和国以外的另一个独立国家来参加国际地球物理年专门委员会，同早已被国际地球物理年专门委员会承认的中华人民共和国国际地球物理年国家委员会并列。这种把国际科学合作活动屈从国际上某种势力制造‘两个中国’阴谋的错误作法，中华人民共和国国际地球物理年国家委员会是绝对不能同意的。现在我代表中华人民共和国国际地球物理年国家委员会正式通知您：我们坚决要求撤销这一错误的决定，在这决定未被纠正前，中华人民共和国国际地球物理年国家委员会决定退出国际地球物理年专门委员会和它的一切活动。请将我4月10日给您的信和此电散发给国际地球物理年专门委员会各国家委员会。”
