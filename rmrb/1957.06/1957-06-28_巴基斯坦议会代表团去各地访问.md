Title: Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation Visits Various Places

Author:

Date: 1957-06-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. The Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation headed by Mu. Rahman left Beijing by plane this morning to visit the Northeast, East China, and Northwest regions.

Zhang Bangying, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, accompanied the delegation on the tour.

### 巴基斯坦议会代表团去各地访问

1957-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦议会代表团去各地访问
    新华社27日讯  以穆·拉赫曼为首的巴基斯坦议会代表团今天早晨乘飞机离开北京，去东北、华东、西北各地参观访问。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员张邦英陪同代表团参观。
