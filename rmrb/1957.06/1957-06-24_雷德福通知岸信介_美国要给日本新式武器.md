Title: Radford Notifies Kishi Nobusuke that the U.S. Will Give Japan New Weapons
Author:
Date: 1957-06-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 22nd (TASS): The "Mainichi Shimbun" correspondent in Washington, citing authoritative sources, said that the Chairman of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff, Radford, had notified Japanese Prime Minister Kishi Nobusuke that the U.S. government intends to supply Japan with new weapons.

Radford also said that the United States is prepared to equip its troops in the Far East with atomic weapons.

### 雷德福通知岸信介  美国要给日本新式武器

1957-06-24
第3版()
专栏：

    雷德福通知岸信介
    美国要给日本新式武器
    新华社23日讯　塔斯社东京22日讯：东京“每日新闻”驻华盛顿记者援引权威人士消息说，美国参谋长联席会议主席雷德福曾经通知日本首相岸信介说，美国政府打算供给日本新式武器。
    雷德福还说，美国准备用原子武器装备它在远东的军队。
