Title: French Colonialists' Inhumanity: Using Bacteria to Kill Algerian Children
Author:
Date: 1957-06-27
Page: 6

　　French Colonialists' Inhumanity
    Using Bacteria to Kill Algerian Children
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 26th. According to today's "Al-Ahram" newspaper, France recently used the method of injecting bacteria to kill twenty-two Algerian children. This was announced by the Algerian Liberation Front in a telegram sent to the United Nations and other international organizations. The telegram demanded a stop to this barbaric act that violates international law.
    The telegram said that a French military doctor rounded up many children from eighteen months to five years old in rural areas of Oran Province, near the Moroccan border, and injected them with dangerous bacteria. These children died immediately after being injected.

### 法国殖民者灭绝人性  用细菌杀害阿尔及利亚儿童

1957-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　法国殖民者灭绝人性
    用细菌杀害阿尔及利亚儿童
    新华社开罗26日电  据“金字塔报”今天报道，法国最近用注射细菌的手段杀害了二十二个阿尔及利亚儿童。这是阿尔及利亚解放阵线在拍给联合国和其他国际组织的电报中宣布的。这个电报要求制止这种违反国际法的野蛮行为。
    电报说，一个法国军医在临近摩洛哥边境的瓦兰省的乡村中搜罗了许多从十八个月到五岁的儿童，并且给他们注射了危险的细菌。这些儿童被注射后立即死去。
