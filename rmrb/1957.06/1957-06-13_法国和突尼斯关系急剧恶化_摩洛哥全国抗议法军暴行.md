Title: Franco-Tunisian Relations Acutely Deteriorate; Morocco Protests French Army Brutality Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1957-06-13
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, 11th dispatch from Tunis: The frequent and fairly large-scale armed clashes between French troops and Tunisian soldiers in the past two weeks have caused a sharp deterioration in Franco-Tunisian relations.

　　The Tunisian government sent four notes to the French Ambassador to Tunisia in a single day on the 8th, demanding a swift resolution with France on issues related to the two countries, including the withdrawal of French troops.

　　Massive protest demonstrations were held in many cities in Tunisia on the 9th. The demonstrators demanded that French troops withdraw from Tunisia immediately.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 11th dispatch: A few days ago, French military aircraft strafed Moroccan villages near the Algerian border, killing several civilians and many livestock. This incident has aroused a wave of anger across Morocco.

　　French officials in Rabat admitted that the French Air Force Command was responsible for the incident, and the French side has apologized to the Moroccan government.

### 法国和突尼斯关系急剧恶化  摩洛哥全国抗议法军暴行

1957-06-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　法国和突尼斯关系急剧恶化
　　摩洛哥全国抗议法军暴行
    新华社11日讯  突尼斯消息：法军和突尼斯士兵在过去两周内多次发生规模相当大的武装冲突，使法突两国的关系遽形恶化。
    突尼斯政府在8日一天内就向法国驻突尼斯大使送出了四次照会，要求同法国迅速解决与两国有关的问题，包括撤退法国军队的问题在内。
    9日在突尼斯的许多城市中举行了声势浩大的抗议示威游行。示威群众要求法军立刻撤出突尼斯。
    据新华社11日讯  几天前法国军用飞机扫射靠近阿尔及利亚边境的摩洛哥村庄，打死好几个平民和很多牲畜。这件事已经在摩洛哥全国激起了愤怒的浪潮。
    拉巴特的法国人士承认，这一事件要由法国空军指挥部负责，法国方面已经向摩洛哥政府道歉。
