Title: National People's Congress to Officially Convene on the 26th; Preparatory Meeting to Begin Today
Author:
Date: 1957-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th dispatch: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 75th meeting this afternoon, discussing the agenda of the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress and preparing to submit recommendations to the Congress. The meeting tentatively scheduled the preparatory meeting for the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress to be held from June 20th to 25th, and the official meeting to begin on June 26th.

### 全国人民代表大会26日正式开会  今天开始举行预备会议

1957-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会26日正式开会
    今天开始举行预备会议
    新华社19日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今日下午举行第七十五次会议，讨论了第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议的议程，准备向大会提出建议。会议拟定从6月20日到25日举行第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议的预备会议，从6月26日起正式举行会议。
