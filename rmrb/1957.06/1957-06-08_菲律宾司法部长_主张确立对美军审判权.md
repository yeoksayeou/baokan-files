Title: Philippine Justice Minister Advocates for Establishing Jurisdiction Over U.S. Troops
Author:
Date: 1957-06-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 7th—Philippine Deputy Justice Minister Padilla advocated that the Philippines adopt the same procedures as Japan in the matter of jurisdiction over criminal cases involving American soldiers.

Padilla expressed this view in a radio talk on June 6.

Padilla believes that Japanese courts have the final say in cases of crimes committed by U.S. soldiers against Japanese citizens. This is better than the system proposed in the deadlocked U.S.-Philippine negotiations.

### 菲律宾司法部长  主张确立对美军审判权

1957-06-08
第6版()
专栏：

    菲律宾司法部长
    主张确立对美军审判权
    据新华社7日讯　菲律宾司法部副部长巴雷拉主张菲律宾在关于对美国军人犯罪案件的审判权的问题上采取同日本一样的程序。
    巴雷拉6月6日在一次广播谈话中表达了这种观点。
    巴雷拉认为，日本法院对美国军人对日本人犯罪的案件有最后决定权。这一点同已经形成僵局的美菲谈判中所建议的制度比较起来，要好一些。
