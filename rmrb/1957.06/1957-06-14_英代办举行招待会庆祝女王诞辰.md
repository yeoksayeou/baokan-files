Title: British Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception to Celebrate Queen's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1957-06-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 13th: The British Chargé d'Affaires in China, Mr. Humphrey Trevelyan, held a reception in Beijing this afternoon to celebrate the birthday of Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth II. Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, attended the reception. He extended warm festive greetings to the British government and the British people.

Peng Zhen proposed a toast to the health of Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth II, to the prosperity of the British nation, and to the happiness of the British people.

Chargé d'Affaires Trevelyan also invited his guests to toast the health of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and the happiness of the Chinese people.

Among the guests at the reception were people from all walks of life in China, as well as heads of diplomatic missions and economic delegations from various countries to China, and diplomatic officials.

### 英代办举行招待会庆祝女王诞辰

1957-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　英代办举行招待会庆祝女王诞辰
    新华社13日讯　英国驻中国代办欧念儒今天下午在北京举行招待会，庆祝英国女王陛下伊丽莎白二世诞辰。全国人民代表大会常务委员会彭真副委员长出席了招待会。他向英国政府和英国人民表示热烈的节日祝贺。
    彭真提议为英国女王陛下伊丽莎白二世的健康，为英国国家的繁荣，为英国人民的幸福，干杯。
    欧念儒代办也请他的客人，为中华人民共和国毛泽东主席的健康，为中国人民的幸福干杯。
    招待会的客人中，有中国各方面的人士和各国驻中国的使节、经济代表团团长和外交官员。
