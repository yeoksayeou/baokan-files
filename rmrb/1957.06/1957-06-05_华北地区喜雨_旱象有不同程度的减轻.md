Title: Welcome Rain in North China, Drought Conditions Reduced to Varying Degrees
Author:
Date: 1957-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: Since the 3rd until 2:00 PM on the 4th, the middle and lower reaches of the Yellow River and the upper reaches of the Huai River have successively experienced rainfall, which has alleviated the drought in North China to varying degrees.

A reporter learned from the Central Meteorological Science Research Institute that the rainfall situation is as follows: Rainfall in the southern part of Shaanxi Province and the western part of Shandong Province was about 25 millimeters (10 millimeters equals three "shi fen"), the western part of Hubei Province received 20 to 40 millimeters, the eastern and southern parts of Henan Province received 40 to 90 millimeters, the northwestern part of Henan Province received about 15 millimeters, the northern part of Anhui Province received 30 to 60 millimeters, and the southern part of Shanxi Province received 10 to 20 millimeters. There was also widespread rain in the northern part of Shanxi Province and the provinces of Hebei, Jilin, and Heilongjiang, but the rainfall was not significant. Except for the southwestern part of Heilongjiang Province, which received 10 to 20 millimeters, the rainfall in other areas ranged from 1 to 10 millimeters.

### 华北地区喜雨  旱象有不同程度的减轻

1957-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    华北地区喜雨
    旱象有不同程度的减轻
    新华社4日讯　3日起到4日十四时止，黄河中、下游和淮河上游陆续有雨，对华北地区的旱象有不同程度的减轻。
    记者从中央气象科学研究所获悉这次降雨情况如下：陕西省南部、山东省西部降水量约在二十五毫米左右（十毫米合三市分），湖北省西部二十到四十毫米，河南省东部、南部四十到九十毫米，河南省西北部十五毫米左右，安徽省北部三十到六十毫米，山西省南部十到二十毫米。山西省北部和河北、吉林、黑龙江等省也普遍有雨，但雨量不大，除黑龙江省西南部有十到二十毫米外，都在一到十毫米之间。
