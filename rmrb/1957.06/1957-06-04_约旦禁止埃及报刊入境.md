Title: Jordan Bans Egyptian Newspapers

Author: 
Date: 1957-06-04
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Amman, June 2nd: The Jordanian government issued an order on June 1st prohibiting all Egyptian newspapers and magazines from entering Jordan. The order accused Egyptian newspapers and magazines of attacking Jordan and publishing "false news" about the Jordanian royal family and government.
    The Jordanian military court is continuing to try arrested patriots. An Amman airport employee was sentenced to fifteen years in prison on May 31st.
    The curfew in Amman was lifted on June 1st.

### 约旦禁止埃及报刊入境

1957-06-04
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦禁止埃及报刊入境
    据新华社2日讯　安曼消息：约旦政府6月1日发布命令，禁止所有埃及的报纸和杂志运入约旦。这项命令指责埃及报纸和杂志攻击约旦和刊载有关约旦王室和政府的“虚假消息”。
    约旦军事法庭正在继续审讯被捕的爱国者。有一位安曼机场的职员在5月31日被判处十五年徒刑。
    安曼的宵禁已经在6月1日解除。
