Title: North Korean Ambassador to China Holds Press Conference, Condemns the U.S. for Undermining the Korean Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-06-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 27th. The Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Li Yong-ho, held a press conference in Beijing today, condemning the U.S. for undermining the Korean Armistice Agreement.

　　Ambassador Li Yong-ho read to the more than 30 Chinese and foreign journalists attending the press conference the statement issued yesterday by the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The statement holds the U.S. government fully responsible for all the serious consequences of unilaterally undermining the Korean Armistice Agreement, and advocates for the prompt convening of an international conference with broad participation of relevant countries to promote the peaceful reunification of Korea.

### 朝鲜驻华大使举行记者招待会  斥责美方破坏朝鲜停战协定

1957-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜驻华大使举行记者招待会
    斥责美方破坏朝鲜停战协定
    新华社27日讯  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使李永镐今日在北京举行记者招待会，斥责美方破坏朝鲜停战协定的行为。
    李永镐大使向参加记者招待会的中外记者三十多人宣读了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府昨天所发表的声明。这个声明要美国政府对片面破坏朝鲜停战协定的一切严重的后果负完全的责任，并主张迅速召开有关国家广泛参加的国际会议，以促进朝鲜的和平统一。
