Title: 1956 – A Year of the Great Leap Forward (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1957-06-27
Page: 8

　　1956 – A Year of the Great Leap Forward
"Our socialist construction is proceeding simultaneously with the socialist revolution. In our country's First Five-Year Plan for developing the national economy, we have already correctly planned the steps to combine construction and transformation. And in 1956, with the arrival of the high tide of socialist transformation, our country's socialist construction had a leap-forward development. The scale and speed of the development of economic and cultural and educational undertakings greatly exceeded the first three years of the Five-Year Plan, and some even exceeded the total growth of the first three years." Zhou Enlai: "Government Work Report"

　　Jet airplanes, heavy-duty trucks, and large power generating equipment, and other important new products were successfully manufactured in 1956. Miao Di, Chen Zhi painting

　　The manufacturing of metal-cutting machine tools increased by more than 12,200 units in 1956 compared to 1955. Top picture: Shenyang First Machine Tool Plant manufactured a large number of new-type machine tools for various places last year. This is the main workshop of the machine tool plant manufacturing A62 lathes. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Zhao

　　Steel production increased by a total of 1.5 million tons in the first three years, while it increased by 1.61 million tons in 1956 alone. Top picture: In the Anshan Iron and Steel Works, trains are constantly transporting molten iron into the steelmaking plant and transporting the cast steel ingots out, ensuring timely steel production. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Limen

　　Cotton cloth production increased by a total of 20.5 million bolts in the first three years, while it increased by 43 million bolts in 1956 alone. Top picture: The employees of the state-owned Beijing First Cotton Textile Factory promoted dozens of advanced experiences last year in order to increase the production of yarn and cloth. This is a corner of the weaving workshop of the First Cotton Textile Factory. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Huiru

　　The freight volume of modern transportation tools nationwide increased by a total of 112 million tons in the first three years, while it increased by 93 million tons in 1956 alone.
　　Top picture: The Yingxia Railway was laid to Xiamen at the end of last year. This is a train traveling through high mountains and deep valleys. Ye Dakai (Xinhua News Agency draft)

　　Sugar production increased by a total of 160,000 tons in the first three years, while it increased by nearly 110,000 tons in 1956 alone.
　　Top picture: The Guangxi Guixian Sugar Factory, which went into production in February last year, can produce more than 30,000 tons of high-quality granulated sugar annually. This is a worker packaging white sugar. Tang Qingsen (Xinhua News Agency draft)

　　In 1956, despite serious natural disasters, the total output value of agriculture in our country still increased by 2.74 billion yuan, exceeding the average annual increase in the first three years. Top picture: Last year, members of the New Life Agricultural Production Cooperative in Gucheng County, Hubei Province, welcomed the first bumper harvest after agricultural cooperativization. The cooperative's 2,000 mu of wheat fields yielded an average of 350 jin per mu. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Xinning

　　The total retail sales of commercial institutions increased by a total of 11.3 billion yuan in the first three years, while it increased by 6.2 billion yuan in 1956 alone. Top picture: A corner of the Santai Department Store, newly established last year by the Yumen City Department Store. Xinhua News Agency reporter Hu Yue

　　In 1956, the nation completed nearly 14 billion yuan of basic construction investment, equivalent to one-third of the total investment stipulated in the Five-Year Plan. Top picture: A corner of the large-scale Luoyang Thermal Power Plant construction site. Zhang Zhuquan (Henan Daily draft)

　　In 1956, China's cultural and educational undertakings also had great development. Top picture: In order to meet the needs of the development of secondary education and cultivate a large number of qualified secondary school teachers for the country, Southwest Normal College also held correspondence education in the 1956 academic year. This is an exterior view of the Southwest Normal College campus. Lu Changlin (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 一九五六年——伟大跃进的一年（图片）

1957-06-27
第8版()
专栏：

　　一九五六年——伟大跃进的一年
    “我国的社会主义建设是同社会主义革命同时进行的。在我国发展国民经济的第一个五年计划中，我们已经正确地规划了建设和改造相结合的步骤。而1956年，伴随着社会主义改造的高潮的到来，我国的社会主义建设有了一个跃进的发展，经济事业和文教事业的发展规模和速度，都大大地超过了五年计划的前三年，有些甚至超过了前三年增长的总和。”                周恩来：“政府工作报告”
    喷气式飞机、载重汽车和大型发电设备等重要新产品都是在1956年制造成功的。      苗地  陈志画
    金属切削机床的制造，1956年就比1955年增加了一万二千二百多台。  上图：沈阳第一机床厂，去年为各地制造了大批新型机床，这是机床厂制造一A六二车床的主要车间。
                                 新华社记者  于肇摄
    钢产量前三年共增加了一百五十万吨，而1956年就增加了一百六十一万吨。  上图：在鞍钢炼钢厂里火车正源源不断地把铁水运进炼钢厂，又把铸好的钢锭运出去，保证及时出钢。
                                新华社记者  杨礼门摄
    棉布的产量前三年共增加了二千零五十万匹，而1956年就增加了四千三百万匹。  上图：国营北京第一棉纺织厂的职工为了增产纱布，去年推行了几十种先进经验，这是第一棉纺织厂织布车间的一角。
                          新华社记者  喻惠如摄
    全国现代运输工具前三年的货运量共增加了一亿一千二百万吨，而1956年就增加了九千三百万吨。
    上图：鹰厦铁路去年年底铺轨到厦门，这是穿行在高山深谷之间的列车。  叶大开摄（新华社稿）
    食糖产量前三年共增加了十六万吨，而1956年就增加了将近十一万吨。

    1956年，我国的农业在严重的自然灾害中，总产值仍然增加了二十七亿四千万元，超过了前三年平均每年增加的水平。  上图：去年湖北省谷城县新生活农业生产合作社社员迎接农业合作化后的第一次大丰收。这个社的两千亩麦田，每亩平均收三百五十斤。
                                  新华社记者  刘馨宁摄
    商业机构零售总额前三年共增加了一百一十三亿元，而1956年就增加了六十二亿元。  上图：玉门市百货公司去年新建立的三台门市部的一角。
                                  新华社记者  胡越摄
    1956年全国完成了将近一百四十亿元的基本建设投资额，等于五年计划规定的投资总额的三分之一。  上图：规模巨大的洛阳热电厂工地的一角。              张祝泉摄（河南日报社稿）
