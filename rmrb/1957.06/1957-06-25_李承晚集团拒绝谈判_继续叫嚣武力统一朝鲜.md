Title: Syngman Rhee Clique Refuses Negotiation, Continues to Clamor for Unification of Korea by Force

Author: 
Date: 1957-06-25
Page: 6

    Syngman Rhee Clique Refuses Negotiation
    Continues to Clamor for Unification of Korea by Force
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 24th: Seoul Dispatch: A spokesman for the Syngman Rhee clique yesterday again publicly rejected the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's proposal for peaceful unification of Korea. They refused to negotiate and continued to clamor that "unification can only be achieved by force."
    Premier Kim Il-sung of the Korean Cabinet sternly condemned the United States on the 21st for undermining the armistice agreement, and suggested convening a conference of countries concerned with the peaceful settlement of the Korean question, in order to promote the withdrawal of all foreign troops from Korea and to leave the issue of peaceful unification of Korea to the Korean people themselves to resolve.

### 李承晚集团拒绝谈判  继续叫嚣武力统一朝鲜

1957-06-25
第6版()
专栏：

    李承晚集团拒绝谈判
    继续叫嚣武力统一朝鲜
    据新华社平壤24日电　汉城消息：李承晚集团的发言人昨天再次公开拒绝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国方面关于和平统一朝鲜的建议，他们拒绝谈判，并且继续叫嚣“只有通过武力才能达到统一”。
    朝鲜内阁首相金日成在21日严正谴责了美国破坏停战协定，并且建议召开同和平解决朝鲜问题有关国家的会议，以便促使一切外国军队撤离朝鲜，把和平统一朝鲜的问题交由朝鲜人民自己来解决。
