Title: Chairman Liu Meets with Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi met this afternoon with the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation headed by Mujibur Rahman.

　　Present at the meeting were Vice-Chairmen Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, and Saifudin, as well as Zhang Bangying, Zhang Su, Qu Wu, Yu Xinqing, Liu Guanyi, and Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Geng Biao.

　　Pakistani Ambassador to China, Mr. Ahmed, and his wife were also present. (Picture attached)
　　Chairman Liu Shaoqi meets with the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation. Mujibur Rahman, head of the delegation, is in the middle of the front row. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen.

### 刘委员长接见巴议会代表团

1957-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘委员长接见巴议会代表团
    新华社24日讯　刘少奇委员长今天下午接见了以穆吉布尔·拉赫曼为首的巴基斯坦议会代表团。
    接见时在座的，有林伯渠、李济深、彭真、陈叔通、赛福鼎副委员长，以及张邦英、张苏、屈武、余心清、刘贯一和中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飙。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使阿哈默德和夫人也在座。（附图片）
    刘少奇委员长接见巴基斯坦议会代表团。左排中间是穆·拉赫曼团长。　新华社记者　郑小箴摄
