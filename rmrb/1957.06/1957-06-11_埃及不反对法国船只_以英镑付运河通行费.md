Title: Egypt Does Not Oppose French Ships Paying Suez Canal Tolls in Pounds Sterling
Author:
Date: 1957-06-11
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 10th. An Egyptian Ministry of Finance spokesman said on the 9th that Egypt does not object to accepting free pounds sterling as tolls for French ships using the Suez Canal.
　　It was previously reported that France had made such a request to Egypt through the Swiss Embassy in Cairo.

### 埃及不反对法国船只  以英镑付运河通行费

1957-06-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　埃及不反对法国船只
    以英镑付运河通行费
    新华社开罗10日电  埃及财政部发言人9日说，埃及不反对接受自由英镑作为法国船只使用苏伊士运河的通行费。
    前已报道，法国通过瑞士驻开罗大使馆向埃及提出了这样的要求。
