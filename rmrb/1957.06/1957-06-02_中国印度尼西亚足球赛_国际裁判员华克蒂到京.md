Title: China-Indonesia Football Match: International Referee V. K. Murthy Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-02
Page: 1

    China-Indonesia Football Match
    International Referee V. K. Murthy Arrives in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, June 1st – The famous Indian international football referee, V. K. Murthy, who was appointed by FIFA to referee the second qualifying match of the China-Indonesia World Football Championship, arrived in Beijing this afternoon from Guangzhou by special plane.

### 中国印度尼西亚足球赛  国际裁判员华克蒂到京

1957-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中国印度尼西亚足球赛
    国际裁判员华克蒂到京
    据新华社1日讯　被国际足球协会指派担任中国印度尼西亚世界足球锦标赛第二场预选赛裁判的印度著名足球国际裁判员华克蒂，今天下午由广州乘专机到达北京。
