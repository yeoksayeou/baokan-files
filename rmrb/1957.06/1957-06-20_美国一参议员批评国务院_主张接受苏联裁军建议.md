Title: US Senator Criticizes State Department, Advocates Accepting Soviet Disarmament Proposal
Author:
Date: 1957-06-20
Page: 6

　　US Senator Criticizes State Department
　　Advocates Accepting Soviet Disarmament Proposal
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington: US Democratic Senator Fulbright stated on the 15th that he hoped the US government would accept the Soviet Union's proposal to temporarily halt nuclear weapons testing and criticized the US State Department for its "hesitation" in taking the first step towards disarmament. He pointed out that the Soviet proposal advocates for the establishment of international supervision over the cessation of nuclear weapons testing, which "obviously hits the United States where it hurts."

### 美国一参议员批评国务院  主张接受苏联裁军建议

1957-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国一参议员批评国务院
　　主张接受苏联裁军建议
    据新华社讯　华盛顿消息：美国民主党参议员富布赖特15日表示希望美政府接受苏联关于暂时停止核武器试验的建议，并批评美国国务院在采取裁军的第一步上“犹豫不决”。他指出，苏联建议主张对停止核武器试验建立国际监督，这“显然击中了美国的要害”。
