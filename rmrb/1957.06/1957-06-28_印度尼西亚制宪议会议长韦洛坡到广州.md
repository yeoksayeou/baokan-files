Title: Indonesian Constitutional Assembly Speaker Wilopo Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-06-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 27th: Indonesian Constitutional Assembly Speaker Wilopo, accompanied by his wife and a delegation of ten, arrived here this afternoon at the invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi for a visit to our country.

Accompanying Wilopo were Indonesian Constitutional Assembly First Deputy Speaker Prawoto Mangkusasmito and his wife, among others.

Wang Lifu, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, greeted the guests at Shenzhen. Anwar Sani, Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy in China, also came from Beijing to greet them.

Guangdong Province Governor Tao Zhu hosted a banquet for the guests tonight.

The guests will fly to Beijing tomorrow morning.

### 印度尼西亚制宪议会议长韦洛坡到广州

1957-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚制宪议会议长韦洛坡到广州
    新华社广州27日电  印度尼西亚制宪议会议长韦洛坡偕夫人等一行十人今天下午到达这里，他们是应刘少奇委员长邀请前来我国访问的。
    和韦洛坡同来的还有印度尼西亚制宪议会第一副议长普拉沃托·曼库沙斯米托偕夫人等。
    我人民代表大会常委会办公厅副主任王黎夫到深圳迎接客人，印度尼西亚驻我国大使馆参赞安瓦·萨尼也自北京前来迎接。
    广东省省长陶铸今晚设宴招待客人。
    客人们将在明晨乘飞机到北京。
