Title: Chairman Mao Orders the Promulgation of Organizational Regulations for Four Autonomous Prefectures and Counties; Issues Order Conferring Medals to Meritorious Personnel from the Revolutionary War Period
Author:
Date: 1957-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, based on the decision of the 74th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, today issued an order promulgating the Organizational Regulations for the People's Congress of the Southeast Guizhou Miao and Dong Autonomous Prefecture and the Organizational Regulations for the People's Committee of the Southeast Guizhou Miao and Dong Autonomous Prefecture; promulgating the Organizational Regulations for the People's Congress of the Qiannan Buyi and Miao Autonomous Prefecture and the Organizational Regulations for the People's Committee of the Qiannan Buyi and Miao Autonomous Prefecture; promulgating the Organizational Regulations for the People's Congress and People's Committee of the Dali Bai Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan Province; promulgating the Organizational Regulations for the People's Congress and People's Committee of the Dorbod Mongolian Autonomous County in Heilongjiang Province.

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, based on the decision of the 74th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, today issued an order awarding the First Class August 1st Medal to Yang Chengwu and 46 others, awarding the Second Class August 1st Medal to Ding Sheng and 1,467 others, awarding the Third Class August 1st Medal to Ding Shan and 5,339 others, awarding the First Class Independence and Freedom Medal to Huang Yongsheng and 196 others, awarding the Second Class Independence and Freedom Medal to Ding Benchun and 4,152 others, awarding the Third Class Independence and Freedom Medal to Ding Zhong and 31,098 others, awarding the First Class Liberation Medal to Wang Jian'an and 421 others, awarding the Second Class Liberation Medal to Ding Shicai and 4,932 others, awarding the Third Class Liberation Medal to Diao Zhizhen and 54,879 others.

### 毛主席命令公布四自治州县组织条例  并发布向革命战争时期有功人员授勋命令

1957-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席命令公布四自治州县组织条例
    并发布向革命战争时期有功人员授勋命令
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第七十四次会议的决定，今天发布命令，公布黔东南苗族侗族自治州人民代表大会组织条例和黔东南苗族侗族自治州人民委员会组织条例；公布黔南布依族苗族自治州人民代表大会组织条例和黔南布依族苗族自治州人民委员会组织条例；公布云南省大理白族自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例；公布黑龙江省杜尔伯特蒙古族自治县人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例。
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第七十四次会议的决定，今天发布命令，授予杨成武等四十七人以一级八一勋章、授予丁盛等一千四百六十七人以二级八一勋章、授予丁山等五千三百三十九人以三级八一勋章、授予黄永胜等一百九十六人以一级独立自由勋章、授予丁本淳等四千一百五十二人以二级独立自由勋章、授予丁仲等三万一千零九十八人以三级独立自由勋章、授予王建安等四百二十一人以一级解放勋章、授予丁士采等四千九百三十二人以二级解放勋章、授予刁志真等五万四千八百七十九人以三级解放勋章。
