Title: Preparatory Meeting of the National People's Congress Begins; Agenda for the Meeting Determined; Mao Zedong's Report to be Discussed in Groups
Author:
Date: 1957-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — The preparatory meeting for the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress began this afternoon.

　　The preparatory meeting today was chaired by Chairman Liu Shaoqi.

　　Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Standing Committee, presented to the preparatory meeting the Standing Committee's proposals regarding the agenda and duration of the session.

　　The agenda for this session includes:

　　Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai's report on the work of the government;

　　Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Finance Li Xiannian's report on the 1956 National Final Accounts and the 1957 Draft National Budget;

　　Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Economic Commission Bo Yibo's report on the implementation results of the 1956 National Economic Plan and the Draft 1957 National Economic Plan;

　　Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Zhen's report on the work of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress;

　　President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu's report on the work of the Supreme People's Court;

　　Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng's report on the work of the Supreme People's Procuratorate.

　　In addition, this session will also discuss issues related to the election of deputies to the next National People's Congress.

　　Preparatory meetings will be held from today until June 25. The official opening of this session will be on June 26.

　　The preparatory meeting today approved the above-mentioned proposals of the Standing Committee.

　　After the preparatory meeting today, each delegation held group meetings to elect group leaders and deputy group leaders.

　　During the preparatory meeting, each delegation will discuss Chairman Mao Zedong's report "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People," given at the Eleventh Enlarged Session of the Supreme State Conference on February 27 this year, and will conduct discussions on issues such as the final accounts, budget, and annual plans.

　　As of today, 1,062 deputies have registered.

### 全国人民代表大会预备会议开始举行  确定本次会议议程；并将分组讨论毛主席的报告

1957-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会预备会议开始举行
　　确定本次会议议程；并将分组讨论毛主席的报告
    新华社20日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议预备会议在今天下午开始举行。
    今天的预备会议由刘少奇委员长主持。
    常务委员会副委员长兼秘书长彭真向预备会议提出常务委员会关于议程和会期等问题的建议。
    本次会议议程有：
    国务院总理周恩来关于政府工作的报告；
    国务院副总理兼财政部长李先念关于1956年国家决算和1957年国家预算草案的报告；
    国务院副总理兼国家经济委员会主任薄一波关于1956年度国民经济计划的执行结果和1957年度国民经济计划草案的报告；
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长兼秘书长彭真关于全国人民代表大会常务委员会的工作报告；
    最高人民法院院长董必武关于最高人民法院工作的报告；
    最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞关于最高人民检察院工作的报告。
    此外，本次会议还将讨论有关下届全国人民代表大会代表的选举等问题。
    从今日起到6月25日，举行预备会议。6月26日本次会议正式开幕。
    今天预备会议同意常务委员会的上述建议。
    今天的预备会议结束后，各代表组分别举行了小组会议，推选出组长、副组长。
    预备会议期间，各代表组将讨论毛泽东主席今年2月27日在最高国务会议第十一次扩大会议上“关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题”的报告，并对预决算和年度计划等问题进行座谈。
    截至今天止，报到的代表已有一千零六十二人。
