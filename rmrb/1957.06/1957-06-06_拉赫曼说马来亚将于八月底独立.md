Title: Rahman Says Malaya Will Be Independent at the End of August
Author:
Date: 1957-06-06
Page: 6

    Rahman Says
    Malaya Will Be Independent at the End of August
    Xinhua News Agency, Kuala Lumpur, June 4th: According to reports from Kuala Lumpur, the Chief Minister of the Federation of Malaya, Rahman, said upon returning to Kuala Lumpur from London on June 3rd that he will serve as Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the independent Federation of Malaya.
    Rahman said that he himself will announce the independence of the Federation of Malaya on August 31st.
    When talking about the results of this negotiation in Britain, Rahman said that the negotiation has been successful and that after Malaya becomes independent, it will become a member of the British Commonwealth.

### 拉赫曼说马来亚将于八月底独立

1957-06-06
第6版()
专栏：

    拉赫曼说
    马来亚将于八月底独立
    据新华社4日讯　吉隆坡消息：马来亚联合邦首席部长拉赫曼6月3日从伦敦回到吉隆坡以后说，他将在独立后的马来亚联合邦任总理兼外交部长。
    拉赫曼说，他自己将在8月31日宣告马来亚联合邦独立。
    拉赫曼在谈到这次到英国谈判的结果时说，谈判已经获得成功，将来马来亚独立后，就成为英联邦的一员。
