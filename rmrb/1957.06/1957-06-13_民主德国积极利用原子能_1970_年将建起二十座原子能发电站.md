Title: East Germany Actively Utilizes Atomic Energy: Twenty Atomic Power Stations to be Built by 1970
Author:
Date: 1957-06-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 11th: East Germany plans to build twenty atomic power stations by 1970 to replace the thermal power stations that currently use lignite almost entirely as fuel. In this way, the lignite resources that the German people consider "as precious as gold" can be concentrated for use in the chemical and synthetic materials industries.

This was announced by the Deputy Director of the East German Nuclear Technology Research Bureau at the National Youth Workers' Congress held in Magdeburg the other day.

Currently, East Germany's first atomic power station, also the first in all of Germany, is being designed at an accelerated pace. It is expected to be fully completed before 1960, and its power generation capacity will reach 70,000 kilowatts. As the first step in this construction project, East Germany is installing its first atomic reactor and cyclotron, so that it can begin manufacturing radioactive isotopes before the end of this year.

(Attached picture)

At the Nuclear Physics Institute in Dresden, Alden, one of East Germany's experts in the peaceful use of atomic energy, is conducting research work.

### 民主德国积极利用原子能  1970年将建起二十座原子能发电站

1957-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　民主德国积极利用原子能
　　1970年将建起二十座原子能发电站
    新华社柏林11日电  民主德国计划到1970年建造起二十座原子能发电站，来代替目前几乎全部用褐煤做燃料的火力发电站。这样，德国人民看成
  “像黄金一样珍贵”的褐煤资源就可集中用在化学工业和合成材料工业方面。
    这是民主德国核子技术研究局副局长日前在马格德堡举行的全国青年工人代表大会上宣布的。
    目前民主德国也是全德国的第一座原子能发电站正在加紧设计中。预计在1960年以前全部建成，它的发电能力将达七万瓩。被作为这个建设工程的第一步，民主德国正在装设第一个原子反应堆和回旋加速器，以便在今年年底以前就将开始制造放射性同位素。
　　（附图片）
    在德累斯顿的核子物理研究所里，民主德国和平利用原子能的专家之一阿尔登正在进行研究工作。
