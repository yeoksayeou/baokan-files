Title: Grotewohl Receives Chinese Journalists Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-06-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 3rd -- German Democratic Republic Premier Grotewohl received the entire Chinese Journalists Delegation, which is currently visiting Democratic Germany, at noon today. He had a cordial conversation with them and answered some of their questions. After the conversation, Premier Grotewohl also invited the entire delegation, headed by Wang Yi, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "People's Daily," to have lunch with him.

### 格罗提渥接见  中国新闻工作者代表团

1957-06-05
第5版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥接见
    中国新闻工作者代表团
    新华社柏林3日电　德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥今天中午接见了正在民主德国访问的中国新闻工作者代表团全体人员，同他们进行了亲切的谈话，解答了他们提出的一些问题。谈话以后，格罗提渥总理并请以“人民日报”副总编辑王揖为首的代表团全体人员和他共进午餐。
