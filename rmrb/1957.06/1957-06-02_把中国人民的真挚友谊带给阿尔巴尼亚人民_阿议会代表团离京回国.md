Title: Bringing the Sincere Friendship of the Chinese People to the Albanian People - Albanian Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1957-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st. The People's Assembly Delegation of the People's Republic of Albania left Beijing by plane today to return home.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi went to the airport to see the distinguished guests off.

He said at the airport that the visit of the People's Assembly Delegation of the People's Republic of Albania to China is a major event in Sino-Albanian relations. Although your visit to our country was short, you have made important contributions to further strengthening the friendly unity between the people of China and Albania. Liu Shaoqi expressed his heartfelt thanks to the delegation for this and asked the delegation to bring the sincere friendship and best wishes of the Chinese people to the Albanian people. He said that the Chinese people will always be close brothers and loyal friends of the Albanian people at all times.

Chairman Rita Marko said in his farewell speech, "We are about to leave the People's Republic of China, but we leave behind unforgettable memories. We will keep in our immortal hearts the warmest and brotherly reception given to us everywhere, and we will keep the deep friendship of your hundreds of millions of people for our people."

Chairman Marko waved to the people seeing him off and said in Chinese: "Comrades, goodbye!"

The plane took off at 11:40 a.m.

Also present at the airport today to see off the delegation were Vice-Chairmen Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Vice-Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian, Vice-Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, Bao Erhan, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, China's Ambassador to Albania Luo Shigao, and heads of various government departments, people's organizations, and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee.

Albanian Ambassador to China Balili and his wife were also at the airport to see them off.

### 把中国人民的真挚友谊带给阿尔巴尼亚人民  阿议会代表团离京回国

1957-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    把中国人民的真挚友谊带给阿尔巴尼亚人民
    阿议会代表团离京回国
    新华社1日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团今天乘飞机离北京回国。
    刘少奇委员长到机场为贵宾们送行。
    他在机场上讲话说，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团的访问中国是中阿关系中的一件大事。你们在我国访问的时间虽然很短，但是你们已经为进一步加强中阿两国人民之间的友好团结作出了重要的贡献。刘少奇为此向代表团表示衷心感谢，并且请代表团把中国人民真挚的友谊和美好的祝愿带给阿尔巴尼亚人民。他说，中国人民无论在什么时候都是阿尔巴尼亚人民的亲密兄弟和忠实朋友。
    里塔·马尔科主席致告别词说，我们就要离开中华人民共和国了，但我们留下了不可忘怀的记忆。我们将要在我们的不朽心灵里保持着到处给予我们的最温暖和兄弟般的招待，我们将保持你们数亿人民对我国人民的深深友谊。
    马尔科主席向送行的人们挥着手，用中国话说：“同志们，再见吧！”
    飞机在十一时四十分起飞。
    今天到机场送行的还有副委员长林伯渠、李济深、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，国务院副总理李先念，政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧、包尔汉，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高，政府各部门、各人民团体和北京市人民委员会的负责人等。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使巴利里和夫人也到机场欢送。
