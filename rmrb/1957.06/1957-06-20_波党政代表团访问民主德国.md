Title: Polish Party and Government Delegation Visits Democratic Germany
Author:
Date: 1957-06-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 18th: The delegation of the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee and the Polish People's Republic Government, led by Gomulka and Cyrankiewicz, arrived in Berlin at 8:30 AM (Central European Time) today by special train.

　　Several thousand people gathered at the square in front of the Berlin East Station, which was decorated with an archway displaying the national flags of Germany and Poland, to welcome the distinguished guests. Leaders of the German Socialist Unity Party and the German Democratic Republic government, including Ulbricht, Grotewohl, Dieckmann, Stoph, Bolz, and Nuschke, were present on the platform to welcome the guests.

　　Diplomatic envoys from various countries also came to the station to welcome the delegation.

　　First Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee, Gomulka, delivered a speech over the loudspeaker. He expressed his gratitude for the sincere reception given to the Polish Party and Government delegation, and emphasized that the two working-class parties and the governments of the Polish People's Republic and the German Democratic Republic share common interests in the cause of building socialism and defending European peace.

　　He said that establishing good relations with all the German people is the wish of the Polish people.

　　The delegation of the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee and the Polish People's Republic Government is visiting at the invitation of the German Socialist Unity Party Central Committee and the German Democratic Republic Government for a three-day visit. The delegation will hold talks with the German Socialist Unity Party Central Committee and the German Democratic Republic Government delegation, and will conduct tours and visits in the German Democratic Republic.

　　The party and government delegations of the two countries began talks on the 18th.

### 波党政代表团访问民主德国

1957-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　波党政代表团访问民主德国
    据新华社柏林18日电　以哥穆尔卡和西伦凯维兹为首的波兰统一工人党中央委员会和波兰人民共和国政府代表团今天上午八点半（中欧时间）乘专车到达柏林。
    柏林东车站为此搭起了悬挂着德波两国国旗的拱门，到车站前的广场上来迎候贵宾的有几千人。德国统一社会党和德意志民主共和国政府的领导人乌布利希、格罗提渥、希德万、斯多夫、博尔茨和努舍克等都来到月台欢迎贵宾。
    到车站欢迎的还有各国外交使节。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡在扩音器前发表了演说。他表示感谢对波兰党和政府代表团的真诚接待，并且着重指出，两个工人阶级政党以及波兰人民共和国政府同德意志民主共和国政府在建设社会主义和保卫欧洲和平的事业中是利害与共的。
    他说，同全体德国人民建立良好的关系是波兰人民的愿望。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会和波兰人民共和国政府代表团是应德国统一社会党中央委员会和德意志民主共和国政府的邀请前来进行为期三天的访问的。代表团将同德国统一社会党中央委员会和德意志民主共和国政府代表团举行会谈并将在民主德国进行参观和访问。
    两国党政代表团18日开始举行会谈。
