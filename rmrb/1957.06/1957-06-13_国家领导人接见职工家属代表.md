Title: National Leaders Receive Representatives of Workers' Families
Author:
Date: 1957-06-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai received all the representatives attending the National Conference of Workers' Families this afternoon.

Also present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yun, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen and Huang Yanpei, Honorary Chairman He Xiangning, Chairman Cai Chang, and Vice Chairmen Deng Yingchao and Li Dequan of the All-China Women's Federation, and Vice Chairmen Liu Changsheng and Zhu Xuefan of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 国家领导人接见职工家属代表

1957-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　国家领导人接见职工家属代表
    新华社12日讯  毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理，今天下午接见了出席全国职工家属代表会议的全体代表。
    接见时在座的，有副总理陈云，副委员长李济深、黄炎培，中华全国民主妇女联合会名誉主席何香凝、主席蔡畅、副主席邓颖超、李德全，中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜、朱学范等人。
