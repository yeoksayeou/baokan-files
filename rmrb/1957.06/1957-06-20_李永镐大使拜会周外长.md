Title: Ambassador Li Yong-ho Calls on Foreign Minister Zhou
Author:
Date: 1957-06-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, June 17th—Li Yong-ho, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, called upon Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai this afternoon to discuss matters concerning the presentation of his credentials. Xie Shuangqiu, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, were present at the meeting.

### 李永镐大使拜会周外长

1957-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

    李永镐大使拜会周外长
    据新华社17日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国新任驻华特命全权大使李永镐今天下午拜会我外交部长周恩来，商谈递交国书事宜。拜会时在座的有外交部亚洲司副司长谢爽秋、礼宾司副司长马振武。
