Title: Preliminary Agreement Reached for Third Match Between Chinese and Indonesian Football Teams to be Held in Rangoon

Author:

Date: 1957-06-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd June. A preliminary agreement has been reached for the third qualifying match in the World Football Championship between the Chinese and Indonesian football teams to be held in Rangoon, the capital of Burma, on June 23rd. This was the result of consultations between the two football teams in Beijing today.

According to Huang Zhong, Chairman of the Chinese Football Association, this matter is still subject to notification to FIFA and obtaining the agreement of the Burmese side. China and Indonesia have unanimously agreed to engage Burmese referees and linesmen to officiate the third match.

The Chinese and Indonesian football teams have each won one match in the World Football Championship preliminaries. The team that wins the third match will qualify for the semi-final round of the Asian and African zone of the 1958 World Football Championship. If the third ninety-minute match ends in a tie, and the thirty-minute extension also results in a tie, the team with the most goals scored in the first and second preliminary matches will be awarded the semi-final qualification.

### 中国和印度尼西亚足球队  初步确定在仰光举行第三场比赛

1957-06-04
第2版()
专栏：

    中国和印度尼西亚足球队
    初步确定在仰光举行第三场比赛
    新华社3日讯　中国、印度尼西亚足球队在世界足球锦标赛中的第三场预选赛，已经初步确定6月23日在缅甸首都仰光举行。这是两国足球队今天在北京协商的结果。
    据中国足球协会主席黄中说，这件事还有待通知国际足球联合会和取得缅甸方面的同意。中国和印度尼西亚双方已一致同意聘请缅甸的裁判员和巡边员担任第三场比赛的裁判工作。
    中国、印度尼西亚足球队在世界足球锦标赛的预赛中已经各胜一场。在第三场比赛中获胜的一队将有资格参加1958年世界足球锦标赛亚非区的复赛。如果第三次九十分钟比赛结束双方打成平局，延长三十分钟仍然踢成平局，将由在第一、二场预选赛中得球数目多的队获得复赛权。
