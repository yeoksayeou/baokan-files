Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1957-06-01
Page: 1

	·Friendly Exchanges·
	Maghreb Parliamentary Delegation Returns Home
	The Maghreb (Morocco) National Consultative Assembly delegation to China left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 31st to return home.
	Soviet Consumer Cooperative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
	A Soviet Consumer Cooperative delegation of nine people, headed by Klimov, Chairman of the Central Union of Soviet Consumer Cooperatives, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 30th.
	Mongolian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
	The Mongolian People's Republic Handicraft Cooperative Delegation to China, headed by J. Demchig, member of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Central Council of Handicraft Industry of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 31st.
	Belgian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
	The Belgian cultural delegation of five people, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 31st. The delegation is led by Arthur Wauters, Professor at the University of Brussels and former Belgian Ambassador to the Soviet Union. The members of the delegation include Bouchart, Dean of the Flemish Medical College of Belgium, Robert Mestreau, Professor of Philosophy and Literature at the Saint-Louis University of Brussels, Jacques Huisman, Director of the Belgian National Theatre, and Marcel Poot, Director of the Belgian Royal Conservatory of Music.
	Soviet Dynamo Track and Field Team Arrives in Beijing
	The Soviet Dynamo Track and Field Team of twenty-five people arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 31st. They are visiting China and engaging in friendly competitions at our invitation. On the 4th, the Dynamo team will have its first friendly competition with Chinese track and field athletes in Beijing.
	Japanese Sports Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
	The Japanese Sports Association delegation of six people, invited by the All-China Sports Federation to visit China, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 31st.
			(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1957-06-01
第1版()
专栏：友好往来

   ·友好往来·
    马格里勃议会代表团回国
    马格里勃（摩洛哥）国民协商议会访华代表团31日早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    苏联消费合作社代表团到京
    以苏联消费合作社中央联合会主席克利莫夫为首的苏联消费合作社代表团一行九人，在30日下午乘飞机到达北京。
  蒙古手工业合作社代表团到京
    以蒙古人民革命党中央委员、蒙古人民共和国手工业中央理事会主席济·德木齐格为首的蒙古人民共和国手工业合作社访华代表团，31日下午乘火车到达北京。
    比利时文化代表团到京
    应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的比利时文化代表团一行五人，31日下午乘飞机抵达北京。代表团由布鲁塞尔大学教授、前比利时驻苏联大使阿图尔·华特斯率领，代表团成员有比利时弗莱蒙医学院院长布夏尔，布鲁塞尔圣·路易大学哲学和文学院教授罗伯·梅斯特里厄，比利时国家剧院院长雅克·于斯曼，比利时皇家音乐学院院长马赛·福特。
    苏联狄纳莫田径队到京
    苏联狄纳莫田径队二十五人31日下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应邀来中国访问和进行友谊比赛的。4日，狄纳莫队在京将同中国田径运动员作第一次友谊比赛。
    日本体育协会代表团到京应中华全国体育总会邀请来中国访问的日本体育协会代表团一行六人，31日下午到达北京。
              （据新华社讯）
