Title: The Faure couple held a reception to entertain our national leaders
Author:
Date: 1957-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st. Former French Prime Minister Edgar Faure and his wife held a reception in Beijing today to entertain Chinese national leaders, staff members, and people from all walks of life.

Those present at the reception included Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, and Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo and Vice President, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, ministers from other relevant ministries, as well as well-known figures from all walks of life in Beijing.

The hosts had cordial conversations with these Chinese national leaders and staff members. They talked about the current rectification movement in China, the upcoming National People's Congress, and the current political situation in France. When discussing the embargo issue, Faure said that he hoped the future French government could follow the example of the British government and ease the trade embargo against China.

### 富尔夫妇举行酒会招待我国家领导人

1957-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    富尔夫妇举行酒会招待我国家领导人
    新华社1日讯　法国前总理富尔和夫人今天在北京举行酒会，招待中国国家领导工作人员和各界人士。
    出席酒会的，有周恩来总理，李济深、黄炎培、彭真副委员长，政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若和副会长，外交部副部长章汉夫和其他有关各部部长，以及北京各界著名人士。
    酒会的主人同中国这些国家领导工作人员作了亲切的交谈。他们谈到了中国当前的整风运动，谈到了快要召开的全国人民代表大会，也谈到了法国的目前政局。在谈到禁运问题时，富尔说，他希望将来的法国政府能向英国政府看齐，也放宽对华贸易禁运。
