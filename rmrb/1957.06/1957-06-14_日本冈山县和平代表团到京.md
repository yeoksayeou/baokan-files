Title: Japan Okayama Prefecture Peace Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Japan Okayama Prefecture Peace Delegation, a group of twenty-nine people who were invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit our country, arrived in Beijing today.

### 日本冈山县和平代表团到京

1957-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

    日本冈山县和平代表团到京
    新华社13日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国参观访问的日本冈山县和平代表团一行二十九人，今天到达北京。
