Title: Too Much Meat Consumption Can Damage Appearance

Author:
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 6

Gleanings

    Too Much Meat Consumption Can Damage Appearance
    Vienna - Dr. Hala says that women who eat too much meat may damage their appearance. Generally, people who eat too much pork and lard may develop arteriosclerosis and baldness, and excessive consumption of meat in general may damage the skin.
    Hala made these remarks at the recent Eleventh International Beauty Industry Congress.
      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 食肉过多会损容颜

1957-06-07
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    食肉过多会损容颜
    维也纳一个博士哈拉说，妇女如果食肉过多，可能会损害她的容颜。一般人如果吃太多的猪肉和猪油，可能会引起动脉硬化和秃顶，而一般肉类如果食用过多也可能损坏皮肤。
    哈拉这番话，是在最近举行的第十一届国际美容业代表大会上说的。
      （新华社讯）
