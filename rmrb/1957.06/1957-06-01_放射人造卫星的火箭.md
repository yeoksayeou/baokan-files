    Rocket Launching Artificial Satellite
    The rocket that will launch the American artificial satellite was recently displayed to the public for the first time and will soon be test-fired. This "Vanguard" rocket is seventy-two feet long, and its head is plated with titanium, one of the hardest and most heat-resistant metals. The person in charge of manufacturing the "Vanguard" rocket and launching the artificial satellite told reporters visiting the rocket that the rocket carrying the artificial satellite would be launched around March 1958. This three-stage rocket will continuously send the artificial satellite to an altitude of 300 miles above the earth, and then the artificial satellite will be able to orbit the earth. It will circle the earth every ninety to one hundred minutes.
      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 放射人造卫星的火箭

1957-06-01
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    放射人造卫星的火箭
    美国放射人造卫星的火箭，最近举行了第一次公开展览，不久即将试射。这种“先锋”式火箭全长七十二英尺，它的头部镀着钛，这是最坚硬和最耐热的金属中的一种。制造“先锋”式火箭和发射人造卫星的负责人对参观火箭的新闻记者说，带着人造卫星的火箭将在1958年3月左右发射。这种三段式的火箭将连续把人造卫星送到距离地球三百英里的高空，然后，人造卫星就能够绕着地球飞行。每隔九十到一百分钟，环绕地球一周。
      （新华社）
