Title: Shantou: Our Army Shoots Down and Damages Three Chiang Kai-shek Planes; Fujian Front: Our Army’s Artillery Fiercely Bombards Chiang Kai-shek’s Artillery

Author:

Date: 1957-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 22nd: In the past three days, Chiang Kai-shek's aircraft have continuously harassed the Shantou area, and three of them have been shot down and damaged by our ground fire.

At 11:00 this morning, four Chiang Kai-shek F84 aircraft were harassing the Shantou area when one was damaged by our anti-aircraft artillery. At 14:02 on the 20th, four Chiang Kai-shek F84 aircraft were shot down by our anti-aircraft artillery over the Shantou area. The wreckage of the enemy plane fell into the sea east of Nan'ao Island. In addition, at noon on the 21st, we also damaged one F84 Chiang Kai-shek aircraft.

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 22nd: Yesterday afternoon, Chiang Kai-shek's artillery on Great and Little Kinmen Islands intermittently shelled our forward villages five times. Our artillery forces immediately retaliated forcefully against Chiang Kai-shek's artillery.

In addition, the British merchant ship "Chengfeng," which entered Xiamen Port on the night of the 19th under the cover of our artillery, was attacked by Chiang Kai-shek's artillery near Wuyu Island at 0:15 last night as it left Xiamen Port. Our artillery on Xiamen Island immediately suppressed Chiang Kai-shek's artillery, and the merchant ship left safely.

### 汕头我军击落击伤蒋机三架  福建前线我军炮火猛击蒋军炮兵

1957-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　汕头我军击落击伤蒋机三架
    福建前线我军炮火猛击蒋军炮兵
    新华社广州22日电　近三天来，蒋军飞机连续在汕头地区上空进行骚扰活动，被我军地面炮火先后击落击伤三架。
    今天上午十一时，蒋军F84型飞机四架在汕头地区上空骚扰时，被我高射炮击伤一架。20日十四时零二分，蒋军F84型飞机四架在汕头地区上空被我高射炮击落一架。敌机残骸坠落在南澳岛东面海中。此外，21日中午，我还曾击伤F84型蒋机一架。
    据新华社福建前线22日电　昨天下午大、小金门岛上的蒋军炮兵向我前沿村庄断续轰击五次，我炮兵部队当即给了蒋军炮兵以有力还击。
    另外，19日夜间经我炮兵掩护进入厦门港的英国商船“成丰号”昨夜零时十五分离开厦门港时，又在浯屿附近海面遭到蒋军炮火阻击，我厦门岛上的炮兵当即对蒋军炮兵进行压制后，这条商船安全离开。
