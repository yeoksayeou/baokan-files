Title: Nepalese King Receives Chu Tunan; Kathmandu Rally Welcomes Chinese Friends
Author:
Date: 1957-06-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 17th: Nepalese King Mahendra and Queen received the Chinese Cultural Delegation headed by Chu Tunan at the palace on the afternoon of June 14th.

Also present at the reception were two princes, Prime Minister Acharya, and all cabinet ministers. Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, was also present.

The delegation presented gifts to the King and Queen. King Mahendra spoke cordially with the delegation members, and they discussed matters concerning Chinese script reform, industrial construction, and land reform.

Nepalese Foreign Minister Chuda hosted a buffet reception for the delegation at the Gallery Hall that evening. The King, Queen, Prime Minister, cabinet ministers, senior officials, prominent figures from society, and foreign diplomatic envoys all attended the grand event.

Prime Minister Acharya, in his speech at the reception, welcomed the delegation and emphasized the close ties and cultural exchanges between China and Nepal. Chu Tunan said that the friendship between the Chinese people and the Nepalese people is continuously growing on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

On the morning of June 14th, the Kathmandu municipal authorities held a civic welcoming ceremony to welcome the Chinese Cultural Delegation. Prime Minister Acharya, cabinet ministers, and prominent figures from Nepal all attended the welcoming ceremony.

### 尼泊尔国王接见楚图南  加德满都集会欢迎中国朋友

1957-06-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　尼泊尔国王接见楚图南
    加德满都集会欢迎中国朋友
    据新华社新德里17日电　尼泊尔国王马亨德拉和王后6月14日下午在王宫接见了以楚图南为首的中国文化代表团。
    接见时在座的还有两位亲王、阿查里雅首相和全体内阁大臣。中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力也在座。
    代表团向国王和王后赠送了礼物。马亨德拉国王亲切地和代表团人员谈话，他们谈到了关于中国文字改革、工业建设和土地改革等方面的事情。
    尼泊尔外交大臣丘达当天晚上在回廊大厅举行冷餐会招待代表团。国王、王后、首相、内阁大臣、高级官员、社会知名人士和各国外交使节都出席了这次盛会。
    阿查里雅首相在会上讲话的时候，表示欢迎代表团，并且着重指出中尼两国之间的紧密联系和文化交流。楚图南说，中国人民和尼泊尔人民之间的友谊正在和平共处五项原则的基础上不断增长。
    在14日上午，加德满都市政当局举行市民欢迎会，欢迎中国文化代表团。阿查里雅首相、内阁大臣和尼泊尔的知名人士都参加了这次欢迎会。
