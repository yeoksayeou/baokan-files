Title: Joint Statement by Arab Delegations Attending the Colombo Conference: Calling on the People of Arab Countries to Strengthen Unity and Fight for National Independence Against Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1957-06-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, June 26th, TASS, Cairo, June 25th: According to newspaper reports, the Arab delegations attending the World Peace Council's Colombo Conference issued a joint statement, calling on the people of Arab countries to strengthen unity and fight for national independence against imperialism.

　　The statement said that American imperialism, which is trying to enslave Arab countries, force them to accept colonial rule, and undermine the unity of Arab countries, threatens the freedom and independence of countries in the Middle East and Near East.

　　The statement said that the people of Arab countries hope to live in peace and security. They strive to break free from the shackles of imperialism and safeguard and consolidate their independence so that they can control their own resources to benefit the people and prosper their motherland. The statement pointed out that interference by imperialism in the internal affairs of Arab countries must be stopped.

　　The statement emphasized that the people of Arab countries should "strengthen their ties and cooperation with the world peace movement and the peace forces of all countries" and actively participate in this movement.

　　The statement said, "We, the representatives of Arab countries, call on all peace-loving people and all Arab people, regardless of their political views, to strengthen the unity of our ranks, defend the cause of peace, resist American imperialism, repel attempts by foreign countries to interfere in the internal affairs of Arab countries, and work together with the people of all countries in the world to strive for the cessation of atomic and hydrogen weapon tests."

　　The statement was signed by the representatives of Egypt, Syria, Lebanon, Jordan, Iraq, Sudan, Tunisia, and Morocco.

### 参加科伦坡会议的阿拉伯国家代表团联合声明  号召阿拉伯各国人民加强团结  为反对帝国主义、争取民族独立而斗争

1957-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　参加科伦坡会议的阿拉伯国家代表团联合声明
    号召阿拉伯各国人民加强团结
    为反对帝国主义、争取民族独立而斗争
    新华社26日讯  塔斯社开罗25日讯：据报纸报道，参加世界和平理事会科伦坡会议的阿拉伯国家代表团发表联合声明，号召阿拉伯各国人民加强团结，为反对帝国主义，争取民族独立而斗争。
    声明说，力图奴役阿拉伯国家，并迫使它们接受殖民主义统治和破坏阿拉伯国家团结的美帝国主义威胁着中近东国家的自由和独立。
    声明说，阿拉伯国家的人民希望和平和安全地生活。他们力求摆脱帝国主义的束缚，维护和巩固自己的独立，以便自己支配本国的富源，来造福人民，繁荣祖国。声明指出，必须制止帝国主义对阿拉伯国家内政的干涉。
    声明着重指出，阿拉伯国家人民应当“加强同世界和平运动和各国和平力量的联系和合作”，并且积极参加这个运动。
    声明说，“我们阿拉伯国家的代表号召拥护和平的人们和全体阿拉伯人民，不论政治观点如何，应该加强队伍的团结，捍卫和平事业，反抗美帝国主义，击退外国干涉阿拉伯国家内政的企图，并且同世界各国人民一道，为争取停止原子武器和氢武器试验而奋斗。”
    在声明上签字的有埃及、叙利亚、黎巴嫩、约旦、伊拉克、苏丹、突尼斯和摩洛哥的代表。
