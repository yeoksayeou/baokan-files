Title: Burmese Cooperative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-22
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The Burmese Cooperative Delegation, headed by U Ni Ni, arrived in Beijing by plane from Kunming this afternoon. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of Agriculture. During their stay in China, they will investigate issues related to agricultural cooperatives, supply and marketing cooperatives, and handicraft cooperatives.

### 缅甸合作社考察团到京

1957-06-22
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸合作社考察团到京
    新华社21日讯　以吴尼尼为首的缅甸合作社考察团一行九人于今日下午从昆明乘飞机到达北京。他们是应我国农业部的邀请来中国访问的。在中国期间，他们将考察农业合作社、供销合作社及手工业合作社等方面问题。
