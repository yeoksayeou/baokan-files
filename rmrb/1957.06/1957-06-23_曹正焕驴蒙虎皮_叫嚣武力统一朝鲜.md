Title: Cao Jung-hwan Exposes Himself, Clamoring for Military Unification of Korea
Author:
Date: 1957-06-23
Page: 5

　　Cao Jung-hwan Exposes Himself
    Clamoring for Military Unification of Korea
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 22nd: Seoul dispatch: Syngman Rhee's Foreign Minister Cao Jung-hwan today openly clamored that "only armed action can complete the unification of Korea."
    At a press conference, Cao Jung-hwan expressed dissatisfaction with the US side's plan to undermine the armistice agreement announced yesterday, and raised "objections" to the US statement that the purpose of the armistice agreement was for the peaceful settlement of the Korean question. More frankly than the United States, he pointed out that a peaceful settlement "does not conform to the United Nations' policy of unifying Korea."

### 曹正焕驴蒙虎皮  叫嚣武力统一朝鲜

1957-06-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　曹正焕驴蒙虎皮
    叫嚣武力统一朝鲜
    新华社平壤22日电　汉城消息：李承晚的外务部长官曹正焕今天公然叫嚣“只有武装行动才能完成朝鲜的统一”。
    曹正焕在记者招待会上，对美方昨天发表的破坏停战协定的计划表示不满足，并且对美方声明中关于停战协定的目的是为了朝鲜问题的和平解决的说法提出“异议”。他比美国更坦率地指出，和平解决“不符合联合国统一朝鲜的政策”。
