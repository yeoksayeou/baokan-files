Title: Sino-Japanese Fisheries Agreement Extended for One Year
Author:
Date: 1957-06-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: The China Fisheries Association of the People's Republic of China and the Japan-China Fisheries Council of Japan have agreed to extend the validity of the Yellow Sea and East China Sea Fisheries Agreement for another year, starting from June 13th.

Since May, the China Fisheries Association and the Japan-China Fisheries Council have exchanged documents and completed the procedures for continuing to extend the validity of this agreement for one year on June 4th.

### 中日渔业协定延长一年

1957-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中日渔业协定延长一年
    据新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国中国渔业协会和日本国日中渔业协议会关于黄海、东海渔业协定的有效期限，已由双方同意自6月13日起继续延长一年。
    中国渔业协会和日中渔业协议会双方，5月以来通过交换文件，于6月4日完成了继续延长这个协定的有效期限一年的手续。
