Title: General Su Yu Holds Banquet to See Off Indonesian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-13
Page: 1

　　General Su Yu Hosts Banquet
　　To Bid Farewell to Indonesian Military Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, June 12th. General Su Yu, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to the Indonesian Military Friendship Delegation visiting China. The Indonesian Ambassador to China, Sukardjo Wirjopranoto, and military attachés from various countries to China were also invited to the banquet.
    General Su Yu delivered a farewell speech at the banquet. He first extended his farewell to the Indonesian friends on behalf of the General Staff Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and all officers and soldiers, and wished them a safe journey. He said that the visit of the Indonesian Military Friendship Delegation has further consolidated and developed the friendship between us.
    General Gatot Subroto also spoke at the banquet. He said that he was very grateful for the warm welcome and cordial reception he received during his visit to China. He said that China and Indonesia will always work with other countries to defend lasting world peace.
    Present at the banquet were senior generals such as General Chen Geng, Senior General Zhang Zongxun, Senior General Li Da, Senior General Gan Siqi, Senior General Chen Shiju, Senior General Liu Yalou, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong.
    This afternoon, Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai once again received all members of the military delegation and had a cordial conversation with them.

### 粟裕大将举行宴会  欢送印度尼西亚军事代表团

1957-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　粟裕大将举行宴会
　　欢送印度尼西亚军事代表团
    据新华社12日讯  中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕大将今晚为欢送印度尼西亚军事友好访华代表团举行宴会。印度尼西亚驻中国大使苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托和各国驻中国武官也应邀出席了宴会。
    粟裕大将在宴会上致欢送词。他首先代表中国人民解放军总参谋部和全军官兵向印度尼西亚朋友们送别，并祝他们一路平安。他说，印度尼西亚军事友好访华代表团的访问已经进一步巩固和发展了我们之间的友谊。
    加托特·苏布罗托将军也在宴会上讲了话。他说，他非常感谢在中国访问期间所受到的热烈欢迎和亲切接待。他说，中国和印度尼西亚将永远和其他国家一起来保卫世界持久和平。
    出席宴会作陪的有陈赓大将、张宗逊上将、李达上将、甘泗淇上将、陈士榘上将、刘亚楼上将和萧向荣中将等高级将领。
    今天下午国防部长彭德怀元帅再次接见了军事代表团全体人员，并进行了亲切的交谈。
