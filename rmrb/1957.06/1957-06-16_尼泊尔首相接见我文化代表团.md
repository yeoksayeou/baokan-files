Title: Nepalese Prime Minister Receives Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-16
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 15th: Nepalese Prime Minister Acharya received the Chinese cultural delegation headed by Chu Tunan at the Secretariat Building in Kathmandu on the afternoon of June 13th. They were accompanied by Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal.

### 尼泊尔首相接见我文化代表团

1957-06-16
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首相接见我文化代表团
    据新华社新德里15日电　尼泊尔首相阿查里雅6月13日下午在加德满都秘书处大厦接见了以楚图南为首的中国文化代表团，后者是由中国驻尼大使潘自力陪同进见的。
