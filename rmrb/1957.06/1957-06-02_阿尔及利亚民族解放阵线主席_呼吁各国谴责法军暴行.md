Title: Chairman of the Algerian National Liberation Front Calls on All Countries to Condemn French Military Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1957-06-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st: Dr. Mohammed al-Amin, Chairman of the Algerian National Liberation Front, accused the French army of committing acts of genocide in Algeria at a press conference held in Cairo on May 30th.

He said that since the start of the nationalist resistance thirty-one months ago, approximately 500,000 Algerians have been killed by French troops.

Dr. Amin also denounced various atrocities committed by the French army in Algeria, including: attacking civilians, destroying villages, using gasoline bombs and poison gas bombs, and using barbaric torture methods to force confessions from large numbers of captured Algerians, etc.

Dr. Amin said that 400,000 Algerians have fled to neighboring Tunisia and Morocco to escape French persecution. Dr. Amin appealed to countries around the world to provide assistance to Algerian refugees and condemn the atrocities of the French army.

In other Algerian news: Ada, a former member of the Algerian Constituent Assembly, was assassinated in his hometown near Arrand on May 29th. Ada was a "close friend" of former Deputy Speaker of the Algerian Constituent Assembly and pro-French politician Chékal, who was assassinated in Paris on the 26th of the same month.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线主席  呼吁各国谴责法军暴行

1957-06-02
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线主席
    呼吁各国谴责法军暴行
    据新华社1日讯　阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线主席穆罕默德·阿明博士5月30日在开罗举行的记者招待会上，指责法军在阿尔及利亚进行灭绝种族的罪行。
    他说，自从三十一个月以前开始民族主义的反抗以来，已经有约五十万阿尔及利亚人被法国军队杀害了。
    阿明博士还控诉了法国军队在阿尔及利亚的种种暴行，包括：进攻平民，毁灭村庄，使用汽油弹和毒气弹以及使用野蛮的拷打方法，在大量被围捕的阿尔及利亚人身上进行逼供等等。
    阿明博士说，已经有四十万阿尔及利亚人为了逃避法国人的迫害逃到邻国突尼斯和摩洛哥。阿明博士向世界各国呼吁，要求援助阿尔及利亚难民，谴责法国军队的暴行。
    另据阿尔及利亚消息：前阿尔及利亚制宪议会议员阿达5月29日在阿兰附近的故乡小镇中遇刺身死。阿达是同月26日在巴黎被刺的前阿尔及利亚制宪议会副议长、亲法政客歇卡勒的“密友”。
