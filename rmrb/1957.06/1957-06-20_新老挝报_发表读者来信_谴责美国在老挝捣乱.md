Title: "New Laos Newspaper" Publishes Reader Letter Condemning US Meddling in Laos
Author:
Date: 1957-06-20
Page: 7
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 18th: Vientiane Dispatch: "New Laos Newspaper" published a letter from a group of readers in Vientiane Province on June 12, condemning the intensified conspiratorial activities of the United States in Laos.

The letter stated that the United States is plotting to establish a military base in Xieng Khouang, and is supporting the remnants of the Kuomintang from Taiwan to disturb the peaceful life of the people of Houei Sai Province, where Laos borders Burma and Thailand. The United States is also creating friction in Sam Neua and Phongsaly, interfering in Laotian internal affairs.

The letter revealed that the United States is engaged in bribery activities in order to make Katay Don Sasorith the Prime Minister of Laos.

This group of readers pointed out that wherever American imperialism goes, disturbances occur. They called on the people of the whole country to unite and crush all the conspiracies of American imperialism and its accomplices.

### “新老挝报”发表读者来信  谴责美国在老挝捣乱

1957-06-20
第7版()
专栏：

　　“新老挝报”发表读者来信
　　谴责美国在老挝捣乱
    新华社河内18日电　万象消息：“新老挝报”6月12日发表万象省一群读者的一封来信，谴责美国正在老挝加紧进行的阴谋活动。
    这封信说，美国图谋在川圹建立军事基地，并且扶植台湾国民党残兵败将来扰乱老挝同缅甸、泰国交界处的会晒省人民的和平生活，美国还在桑怒和丰沙里制造磨擦，干涉老挝内政。
    信中揭露美国为使卡代·萨索里特当老挝首相而进行收买活动。
    这一群读者指出，美帝国主义到那里，那里就发生扰乱现象。他们号召全国人民团结一致，粉碎美帝国主义及其帮凶的一切阴谋。
