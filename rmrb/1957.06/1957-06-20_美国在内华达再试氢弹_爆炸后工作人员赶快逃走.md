Title: US Conducts Another Hydrogen Bomb Test in Nevada; Staff Quickly Flee After Explosion
Author:
Date: 1957-06-20
Page: 6

　　US Conducts Another Hydrogen Bomb Test in Nevada
    Staff Quickly Flee After Explosion
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 19th: The US government detonated another nuclear device at the Nevada test site on the 18th. This is the fourth in a series of summer nuclear explosion tests being conducted by the US government. The device was detonated on a balloon 500 feet above the ground, with a yield equivalent to approximately 10,000 tons of explosives. Due to the spread of radioactive particles, all personnel working at the control station at the test site were evacuated after the explosion.

### 美国在内华达再试氢弹  爆炸后工作人员赶快逃走

1957-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国在内华达再试氢弹
    爆炸后工作人员赶快逃走
    新华社19日讯　华盛顿消息：美国政府18日又在内华达州试验场爆炸了一个原子核装置。这是美国政府正在举行的一系列夏季原子核爆炸试验中的第四次。这个装置是在一个离地五百英尺的气球上爆炸的，威力约等于一万吨炸药。由于放射性微粒扩散，在试验场的控制站中工作的人在爆炸后全部撤退。
