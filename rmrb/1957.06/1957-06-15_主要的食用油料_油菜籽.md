　　Rapeseed - A Major Edible Oil Crop

    Rapeseed is one of the major edible oil crops in our country. Its total planting area and total output are second only to peanuts.

    This year, the rapeseed planting area in our country is approximately 35 million mu, the vast majority of which is winter-sown, with only about 7% being spring-sown. Winter-sown rapeseed producing areas are mainly distributed in Sichuan, Hunan, Hubei, Jiangxi, Anhui, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Yunnan and Shaanxi provinces. Spring-sown rapeseed is distributed in Inner Mongolia, Xinjiang, Gansu, Qinghai, Shanxi and Hebei provinces.

    "Victory Rapeseed" is an excellent variety for single-season rice areas. "Seven Star Sword," "Gongchen Bridge Long Pod," and "Rapeseed White" and other excellent varieties are suitable for planting in double-cropping rice areas due to their early maturity.

### 主要的食用油料——油菜籽

1957-06-15
第4版()
专栏：小资料

　　主要的食用油料——油菜籽
    油菜籽是我国主要食用油料的一种。它的种植总面积和总产量仅次于花生。
    今年我国油菜的种植面积约三千五百万亩，其中绝大部分是冬播的，春播的只有7％上下。冬播菜籽产区主要分布在四川、湖南、湖北、江西、安徽、江苏、浙江、福建、广东、广西、贵州、云南和陕西等省。春播菜籽分布在内蒙古、新疆、甘肃、青海、山西和河北等地。
    “胜利油菜”是单季水稻区的一个优良品种。“七星剑”、“拱宸桥长荚”、“油菜白”等优良品种，由于成熟期早，适宜在双季稻区种植。
