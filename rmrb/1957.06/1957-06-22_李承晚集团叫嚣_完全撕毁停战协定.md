Title: Syngman Rhee Clique Clamors for Complete Scrapping of the Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-06-22
Page: 6

　　Syngman Rhee Clique Clamors for
    Complete Scrapping of the Armistice Agreement

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 21st: Seoul dispatch: Syngman Rhee clique spokesman Oh Jae-kyung issued a statement today, openly demanding the scrapping of the entire Korean Armistice Agreement, and expressed welcome for the US's action of shipping new weapons into South Korea in disregard of the provisions of the Armistice Agreement.

    Oh Jae-kyung stated that the Syngman Rhee clique hopes that with the US's "first step" of destroying the Armistice Agreement today, the "inevitable conclusion that the entire Armistice Agreement is invalid" will be reached.

### 李承晚集团叫嚣  完全撕毁停战协定

1957-06-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　李承晚集团叫嚣
    完全撕毁停战协定
    新华社平壤21日电　汉城消息：李承晚集团发言人吴在璟今天发表声明，公然要求撕毁整个朝鲜停战协定，并且对美方不顾停战协定的规定把新武器运进南朝鲜的行为表示欢迎。
    吴在璟表示，李承晚集团希望随着美方今天的破坏停战协定的“第一步”，得出“整个停战协定作废的必然的结论”。
