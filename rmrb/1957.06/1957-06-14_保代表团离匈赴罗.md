Title: Bulgarian Delegation Leaves Hungary for Romania
Author:
Date: 1957-06-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 12th: The Bulgarian government and Communist Party delegation led by Comrades Georgi Traikov and Todor Zhivkov arrived in the Romanian border city of Oradea by special train on the afternoon of June 11th from Hungary.

The delegation will visit Romania for several days at the invitation of the Romanian government and the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party.

### 保代表团离匈赴罗

1957-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

    保代表团离匈赴罗
    据新华社布加勒斯特12日电　由于哥夫和日夫科夫率领的保加利亚政府和共产党代表团11日下午从匈牙利乘专车到达罗马尼亚的边境城市俄拉第亚。
    代表团应罗马尼亚政府和工人党中央委员会的邀请将在罗马尼亚访问几天。
