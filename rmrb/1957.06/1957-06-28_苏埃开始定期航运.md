Title: Suez Begins Regular Shipping Service
Author:
Date: 1957-06-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 27th: A regular passenger and cargo shipping service between Alexandria, Egypt and Odessa, Soviet Union has begun. The first Soviet ship on this route, the "Crimea," arrived in Alexandria on June 25th.

	The "Crimea" set sail from Odessa on June 18th, passing through the ports of Romania, Bulgaria, Turkey, Greece, and Lebanon before arriving in Egypt, a journey of about one week.

### 苏埃开始定期航运

1957-06-28
第5版()
专栏：

    苏埃开始定期航运
    新华社开罗27日电  埃及亚历山大港和苏联奥德萨之间的定期客货轮航运已经开始。行驶这条航线的第一艘苏联轮船“克里米亚号”在6月25日到达了亚历山大港。
    “克里米亚号”是在6月18日从奥德萨启碇的，沿途经过罗马尼亚、保加利亚、土耳其、希腊、黎巴嫩五国的港口而到达埃及，航行时间约一星期。
