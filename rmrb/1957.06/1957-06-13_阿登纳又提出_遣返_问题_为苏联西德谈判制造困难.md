Title: Adenauer Again Raises the "Repatriation" Issue, Creating Difficulties for Soviet-West German Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1957-06-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, June 12th: The West German government released a letter from West German Chancellor Adenauer to the Soviet government on June 11th. In the letter, Adenauer requested that Soviet-West German trade talks be linked to negotiations on the so-called "repatriation of Germans detained in Soviet territory." This request is seen as a tactic to complicate the trade talks.

### 阿登纳又提出“遣返”问题  为苏联西德谈判制造困难

1957-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

    阿登纳又提出“遣返”问题
    为苏联西德谈判制造困难
    据新华社12日讯  波恩消息：西德政府6月11日公布了西德总理阿登纳给苏联政府的信件。阿登纳在信件中要求使苏联西德两国贸易会谈同关于所谓“遣返被拘留在苏联境内的德国人”问题的谈判联系起来。这种要求被认为是要使贸易会谈复杂化的一种做法。
