Title: President Nasser Orders Purchase of British and French Schools in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-06-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 1st – Egyptian President Nasser issued a decree on May 30th, authorizing the Ministry of Education to begin negotiations with the Enemy Property Bureau regarding the purchase of British and French schools in Egypt.

The schools affected by this decree include "Victoria College," run by the British in Cairo and Alexandria, and "Franco-Egyptian Secondary Schools" run by the French in several cities.

### 纳赛尔总统下令  购买英法在埃学校

1957-06-02
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔总统下令
    购买英法在埃学校
    据新华社开罗1日电　埃及总统纳赛尔5月30日颁布法令，授权教育部同敌产局就购买英国和法国在埃及开办的学校一事开始谈判。
    受这项法令影响的包括有英国人在开罗和亚历山大港办的“维多利亚学院”和法国人在几个城市办的“法埃中学”。
