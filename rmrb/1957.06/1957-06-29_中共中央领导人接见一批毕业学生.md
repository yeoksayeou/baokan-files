Title: Central Committee Leaders of the CPC Receive a Group of Graduating Students
Author:
Date: 1957-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice-Chairmen Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, and Chen Yun, and General Secretary Deng Xiaoping of the CPC Central Committee, received graduates from the Central Institute for Nationalities, the Public Security Academy, the Foreign Trade Institute, and cadre schools for banking, cooperatives, finance, and grain this afternoon. At the same time, they also received personnel from the publicity work training class held by the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League.

### 中共中央领导人接见一批毕业学生

1957-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见一批毕业学生
    新华社28日讯　中共中央主席毛泽东，副主席周恩来、朱德、陈云，总书记邓小平，在今天下午分别接见了中央民族学院、公安学院、对外贸易学院和银行、合作社、财政、粮食等干部学校的毕业生。同时，还接见了共青团中央举行的宣传工作讲习会的人员。
