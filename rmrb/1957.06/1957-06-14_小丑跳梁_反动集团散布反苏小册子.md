Title: Clowns Show Their Stuff: Reactionary Groups Distribute Anti-Soviet Pamphlets
Author: 
Date: 1957-06-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, June 13th: Reactionary groups are attempting to sow discord and suspicion among delegates attending the World Peace Council meeting. On June 11th, at the "Oriental Hotel" where the meeting was being held, an uninvited guest distributed slanderous pamphlets entitled "Russia's Acts of Aggression in Hungary." In recent days, certain English-language newspapers have successively published a number of anti-Soviet articles.

### 小丑跳梁  反动集团散布反苏小册子

1957-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

    小丑跳梁
　　反动集团散布反苏小册子
    据新华社13日讯　反动集团企图在参加世界和平理事会会议的代表中间散布不和与怀疑。6月11日在举行会议的“东方大饭店”中，有一位不速之客散布诽谤性小册子，书名是“俄国在匈牙利的侵略行为”。最近几天来，用英文出版的某些报纸，接连发表了一些反苏性的文章。
