Title: Sino-Yugoslav Cultural Cooperation Negotiations Begin
Author:
Date: 1957-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd: Negotiations for the Sino-Yugoslav Cultural Cooperation Agreement began this afternoon in Beijing. Shen Yanbing, head of the Chinese delegation and Minister of Culture, said in his welcoming speech that the people of China and Yugoslavia have a deep historical friendship, and that strengthening cultural exchanges between the two countries is of great significance for consolidating and developing this friendship. Jože Vidmar, head of the Yugoslav delegation, said in his speech that both China and Yugoslavia are socialist countries, and cultural exchanges between socialist countries have special significance, allowing them to understand each other's national characteristics and situations.

The negotiations proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

The Chinese side participating in the negotiations included Xia Yan, member of the delegation and Vice Minister of Culture, and Chen Zhongjing, member of the delegation and Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations. The Yugoslav side included Dejan Marković, member of the delegation, member of the Executive Council of the Serbian People's Republic and Chairman of the Cultural Committee, Ivan Flögl, member of the delegation, Secretary-General of the Yugoslav Federal Commission for Foreign Cultural Relations, and the delegation's secretary, Ristić.

Also participating in the negotiations were Lu Yaowu, Cultural Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Yugoslavia, and Daković, First Secretary of the Yugoslav Embassy in China.

### 中南文化合作谈判开始

1957-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中南文化合作谈判开始
    新华社3日讯　中南文化合作协定的谈判今日下午在北京开始。中国代表团团长、文化部部长沈雁冰在致欢迎词中说，中南两国人民有着深厚的历史友谊，为了巩固和发展这种友谊，加强两国文化交流是有着重要意义的。南斯拉夫代表团团长约·维德玛尔在致词中说，南中两国都是社会主义国家，社会主义国家的文化交流有特别意义，可以相互了解彼此国家的特点和情况。
    谈判在友好的空气中进行。
    参加谈判的中国方面有团员、文化部副部长夏衍，团员、对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经。南斯拉夫方面有团员、塞尔维亚人民共和国执行委员会委员兼文化委员会主席德·马尔科维奇，团员、南斯拉夫联邦对外文化联络委员会秘书长伊·弗罗耳和代表团秘书里斯牛奇。
    参加谈判的还有：中国驻南斯拉夫大使馆文化专员卢耀武和南斯拉夫驻华大使馆一等秘书达科维奇。
