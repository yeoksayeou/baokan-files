Title: China-Soviet Athletics Teams Hold Friendly Match in Beijing; Liu Zheng Breaks National Record in Women's 80m Hurdles

Author:

Date: 1957-06-06
Page: 2

    China-Soviet Athletics Teams Hold Friendly Match in Beijing
    Liu Zheng Breaks National Record in Women's 80m Hurdles
    (Our report) The Chinese Athletics Team and the Soviet "Dynamo" Athletics Team, which is visiting China, held a friendly match yesterday at the Beijing先农坛 (Xiannongtan) Stadium. In the twenty-seven events, Soviet "Dynamo" team athletes won twenty-two championships, and Chinese athletes won six championships (In the women's 100m race, a Chinese athlete and a "Dynamo" team athlete reached the finish line simultaneously and both won the championship).
    Yesterday, due to rain and muddy field conditions, the performance of both teams was affected. However, Chinese athlete Liu Zheng still broke the national record of 11.5 seconds in the 80m hurdles with a time of 11.3 seconds.
    Chinese athletes also achieved championships in six events including men's high jump, pole vault, women's high jump, discus, 100m, and 80m hurdles.
    The Soviet "Dynamo" Athletics Team will travel to Shanghai by train on the 6th for a visit and will also hold a formal competition against the Chinese Athletics Team in Beijing on the 14th of this month.

### 中苏田径队在北京举行友谊赛  刘正打破女子八十公尺低栏全国纪录

1957-06-06
第2版()
专栏：

    中苏田径队在北京举行友谊赛
    刘正打破女子八十公尺低栏全国纪录
    本报讯　中国田径队同来我国访问的苏联“狄纳莫”田径队，昨日在北京先农坛体育场进行了友谊比赛。在二十七个比赛项目中，苏联“狄纳莫”队男女运动员得到了二十二个冠军，我国运动员得到六个冠军（女子一百公尺比赛，我国的一个运动员同“狄纳莫”队一个运动员同时到达终点，都获得冠军）。
    昨天，因天雨场地泥泞，两队运动员的成绩都受到影响，但是中国队的女运动员刘正在八十公尺低栏比赛中，仍然以十一秒三的成绩打破了十一秒五的全国最高纪录。
    我国运动员在比赛中还取得了男子的跳高、撑竿跳高，女子的跳高、铁饼、一百公尺、八十公尺低栏等六项冠军。
    苏联“狄纳莫”田径队将于6日乘车前往上海访问，本月14日还将在北京同我国田径队举行正式对抗赛。
