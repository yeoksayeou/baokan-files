Title: Guatemalan Labor Party Calls on People to Fight Against Armas Government for Selling Out Sovereignty
Author:
Date: 1957-06-21
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 19th. "People's News" published a statement by the Guatemalan Labor Party regarding the situation in Guatemala. The statement said that someone forcibly imposed the U.S. puppet Castillo Armas government on the Guatemalan people, with the aim of making Guatemalans submit to the imperialist military policy, strengthening the plundering of Guatemala's national wealth, and colonizing the country. The statement emphasized that everyone knows that due to the crimes of U.S. imperialism, a traitorous regime is now ruling Guatemala, selling out national sovereignty and wealth, and trampling on the people's democratic freedoms.
　　The Guatemalan Labor Party calls on workers, peasants, students, intellectuals, and all democratic forces in the country to intensify the struggle against the Armas government and the control of Guatemala by U.S. imperialism.

### 危地马拉劳动党号召人民进行斗争  反对阿马斯政府出卖主权

1957-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　危地马拉劳动党号召人民进行斗争
    反对阿马斯政府出卖主权
    新华社19日讯　塔斯社蒙得维的亚19日讯：“人民报”发表了危地马拉劳动党关于危地马拉局势的声明。声明说，有人硬把美国傀儡卡斯蒂略·阿马斯的政府加在危地马拉人民的头上，其目的是使危地马拉人屈从帝国主义的军事政策，加强对危地马拉国家财富的掠夺和使这个国家殖民地化。声明强调说，大家知道，由于美帝国主义的罪恶，一个卖国的政权现在得以统治着危地马拉，它出卖了国家的主权和财富，践踏了人民的民主自由。
    危地马拉劳动党号召工人，农民，学生，知识分子和国内一切民主力量加紧斗争，反对阿马斯政府和美国帝国主义对危地马拉的控制。
