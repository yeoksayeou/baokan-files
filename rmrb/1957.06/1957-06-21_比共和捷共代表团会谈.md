Title: Talks Between the Communist Parties of Belgium and Czechoslovakia

Author:

Date: 1957-06-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 20th: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency, delegations from the Central Committees of the Communist Parties of Czechoslovakia and Belgium began talks in Prague on the 18th.

The head of the Belgian Communist Party Central Committee delegation is Mullan, a member of the Central Political Bureau. The head of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee delegation is Kopecki, a member of the Central Political Bureau.

### 比共和捷共代表团会谈

1957-06-21
第5版()
专栏：

    比共和捷共代表团会谈
    新华社布拉格20日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克和比利时两国共产党中央委员会代表团18日在布拉格开始会谈。
    比共中央代表团团长是中央政治局委员穆蓝，捷共中央代表团团长是中央政治局委员柯别茨基。
