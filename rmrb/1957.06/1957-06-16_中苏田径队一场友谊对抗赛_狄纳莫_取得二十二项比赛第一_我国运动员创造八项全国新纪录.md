Title: Sino-Soviet Track and Field Friendly Match: "Dynamo" Achieves First Place in Twenty-Two Events; Chinese Athletes Set Eight New National Records
Author:
Date: 1957-06-16
Page: 5

A friendly track and field competition was held yesterday at the Xianongtan Stadium in Beijing between the Chinese track and field mixed team and the Soviet "Dynamo" track and field team. The "Dynamo" team won with 53.5 points against 33.5 points. In the twenty-seven events, although Chinese athletes only achieved five first places, they broke eight national records.

Yesterday's women's high jump was particularly tense. When the bar was raised to 1.65 meters, exceeding the national record by four centimeters, everyone held their breath, waiting for this tense moment. Our athlete Zheng Fengrong, with rhythmic steps, ran to the edge of the sandpit and jumped over it. Applause immediately rang out from all sides, and then a Soviet female athlete went up to her and presented her with a bouquet of flowers to congratulate her.

Our athletes achieved five first places in: women's high jump, discus, 100 meters, men's high jump, and 110-meter hurdles.

The eight national records broken were: women's high jump (1.65 meters "new result" - 1.61 meters "national record"), 80-meter low hurdles (11.4 seconds - 11.5 seconds), 800 meters (2 minutes 17.2 seconds - 2 minutes 19.5 seconds), men's high jump (1.96 meters - 1.95 meters), pole vault (4.22 meters - 4.20 meters), hammer throw (50.68 meters - 48.74 meters), 4x100 meter relay (42.9 seconds - 43 seconds), and triple jump (14.95 meters - 14.80 meters). (Attached picture)

蔡艺墅 (Cai Yishu)'s pole vault broke the national record with a score of 4.22 meters.
                Our reporter Yuan Yiping

### 中苏田径队一场友谊对抗赛  “狄纳莫”取得二十二项比赛第一  我国运动员创造八项全国新纪录

1957-06-16
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏田径队一场友谊对抗赛
    “狄纳莫”取得二十二项比赛第一
    我国运动员创造八项全国新纪录
    本报讯　昨天，中国田径混合队与苏联“狄纳莫”田径队，在北京先农坛体育场举行了正式的对抗赛。“狄纳莫”队以五十三点五分对三十三点五分取得胜利。在二十七个比赛项目中，我国运动员虽只取得了五个第一名，但是却有八个项目打破全国纪录。
    昨天的女子跳高特别使人紧张，当横竿升到一公尺六十五公分超过了全国最高纪录四公分时，大家都屏着呼吸，等待着这紧张的一刹那。只见我国运动员郑凤荣踏着有节奏的脚步，跑到沙坑旁一跃而起，跳过去了。鼓掌声顿时从四面响起，这时苏联女运动员走上去向她献了一束鲜花，表示祝贺。
    我国运动员取得五项第一名是：女子的跳高、铁饼、一百公尺，男子的跳高、一百一十公尺高栏。
    打破全国纪录的八个项目是：女子的跳高（1.65公尺“新成绩”——1.61公尺“全国纪录”）、八十公尺低栏（11秒4——11秒5）、八百公尺（2分17秒2——2分19秒5），男子的跳高（1.96公尺——1.95公尺）、撑竿跳高（4.22公尺——4.20公尺）、链球（50.68公尺——48.74公尺）、四百公尺接力跑（42秒9——43秒）和三级跳远（14.95公尺——14.80公尺）。（附图片）
    蔡艺墅的撑竿跳高以四公尺二十二公分的成绩打破全国纪录。
                本报记者　袁毅平摄
