Title: Chiang Kai-shek Continues to Replace Civil and Military Officials
Author:
Date: 1957-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd: Taipei dispatch: Due to the anti-American demonstrations in Taiwan, the Chiang Kai-shek clique continues to replace a number of civil and military personnel.

    Liu Wei, commander of the Chiang army's military police, has been dismissed and replaced by Yin Jun, a lieutenant general of the Chiang army. Xie Dongmin, secretary-general of the Chiang Kai-shek clique's Taiwan Provincial Government, has been dismissed from his post. Liu Guoxian, director of the Chiang Kai-shek clique's Taipei City Police Department, has been dismissed.

### 蒋介石继续撤换文武官员

1957-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋介石继续撤换文武官员
    新华社3日讯　台北消息：蒋介石集团因台湾反美大示威事件，继续撤换文职和军职人员多名。
    蒋军宪兵司令刘炜被撤职，由蒋军中将尹俊接任。蒋介石集团台湾省政府秘书长谢东闵被免职。蒋介石集团台北市警察局长刘国宪被撤职。
