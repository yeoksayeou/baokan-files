　　France Intensifies Military Suppression in Algeria
    Xinhua News Agency, June 11th, Paris Dispatch: French Resident Minister in Algeria, Lacoste, ordered the French army on June 10th to intensify military suppression of the Algerian National Liberation Army.
    Lacoste held a meeting in Paris for several days starting from June 6th. Attending the meeting were General Salan, commander of the French ground forces in Algeria, as well as other military generals and representatives of some local civil administrations. The French government decided to mobilize French troops stationed in Tunisia and Morocco to participate in military operations in Algeria.
    The French army's offensive is being countered by the Algerian National Liberation Army. In the fighting on June 9th, the French army suffered twelve deaths and more than one hundred injuries in various locations.
    At the same time, within France, the French police are continuing to persecute Algerians. On June 7th, the police conducted another search of North Africans residing in Paris, arresting one hundred Algerians. Larger-scale searches occurred in Marseille and Bordeaux.
　　(Attached picture)
　　Algerian refugees who survived under the iron heel of the French army fled to the Tunisian border for refuge.

### 法国在阿尔及利亚加紧进行军事镇压

1957-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　法国在阿尔及利亚加紧进行军事镇压
    新华社11日讯  巴黎消息：法国驻阿尔及利亚驻节部长拉戈斯特6月10日命令法国军队加紧对阿尔及利亚民族解放军进行军事镇压。
    拉戈斯特从6日起在巴黎召开了几天的会议。参加会议的有法国驻阿尔及利亚地面部队司令萨兰将军以及其他军事将领和一些当地民政当局代表。法国政府决定要调集驻在突尼斯和摩洛哥的法军来参加在阿尔及利亚的军事行动。
    法军的进攻正在受到阿尔及利亚民族解放军的反击。在6月9日的战斗中，法军在各地战死十二人，受伤一百多人。
    与此同时，在法国境内，法国警察也在对阿尔及利亚人继续进行迫害。6月7日，警察又对居住在巴黎的北非人进行了一次搜捕，逮捕了一百名阿尔及利亚人。在马赛和波尔多，发生了更大规模的搜捕事件。
