Title: Mr. Chen Tiqiang's Letter
Author: Chen Tiqiang
Date: 1957-06-14
Page: 2

Mr. Chen Tiqiang's Letter

To the Editor:

　　Today's "People's Daily" published a report about Comrade Gong Xiangrui's criticism of my previous speech at the Political Science and Law Association's symposium. Although I disagree with some of Comrade Gong Xiangrui's criticisms, I wholeheartedly welcome these criticisms as a whole. My speech at the symposium was quite lengthy, and inevitably contained many opinions with bourgeois viewpoints. However, I believe it is inappropriate for the "People's Daily" headline to put my name on par with Chu Anping. In fact, I support the Communist Party and socialism, and I am strongly opposed to Chu Anping's anti-Party and anti-socialist remarks. Therefore, I would be grateful if your newspaper would correct today's incorrect headline.

　　Chen Tiqiang

　　Editor's Note: The headline of the news report on the symposium of legal scholars on page 2 of this newspaper on June 13 was inappropriate. We agree with Mr. Chen Tiqiang's opinion.

### 陈体强先生的来信
陈体强
1957-06-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　陈体强先生的来信
　　编辑同志：
    今天“人民日报”登载龚祥瑞同志在政法学会座谈会上批评我前次发言的报道，我对于龚祥瑞同志的批评虽然有个别地方不同意，但整个来讲，对于这些批评我是衷心欢迎的。我在座谈会上发言较长，不免有许多含有资产阶级观点的意见，但“人民日报”标题把我的名字和储安平相提并论，我认为是不恰当的。事实上我是拥护共产党、拥护社会主义的，对于储安平反党、反社会主义的言论我是十分反对的。因此，今天不正确的标题，请你报予以更正为感。
    陈体强
    编者注：本报6月13日第二版报道法学界人士座谈会的新闻标题有欠妥之处。我们同意陈体强先生的意见。
