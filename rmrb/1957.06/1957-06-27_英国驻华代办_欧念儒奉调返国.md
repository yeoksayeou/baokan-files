Title: British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. Humphrey Trevelyan, Recalled Home

Author:

Date: 1957-06-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: The British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. Humphrey Trevelyan, who was recalled home, left Beijing by plane on the 26th via Guangzhou to return to his country. Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the airport to see him off. Diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China and personnel from the British Chargé d'Affaires office also went to the airport to see him off.

### 英国驻华代办  欧念儒奉调返国

1957-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　英国驻华代办
    欧念儒奉调返国
    新华社26日讯  英国驻华代办欧念儒奉调已于26日乘飞机离开北京经广州返国。到机场送行的有外交部礼宾司司长王倬如。各国驻华外交官员和英国代办处人员也到机场送行。
