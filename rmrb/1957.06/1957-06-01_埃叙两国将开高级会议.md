Title: Egypt and Syria to Hold High-Level Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-06-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 30th: The Middle East News Agency reports that Egypt and Syria will hold a high-level meeting. The meeting will study the situation in the Arab world in light of the events in Jordan and examine the current state of the Egypt-Syria Joint Defense Treaty.

### 埃叙两国将开高级会议

1957-06-01
第6版()
专栏：

    埃叙两国将开高级会议
    新华社开罗30日电　中东通讯社消息，埃及和叙利亚两国将举行高级会议。会议将从约旦事件来研究阿拉伯世界的局势，并且检查埃叙共同防御条约目前的状况。
