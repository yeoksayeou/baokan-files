Author:
Date: 1957-06-15
Page: 4

Lhasa Auto Repair Plant Goes Into Production

　　Tibet's first modern auto repair plant, the Lhasa Auto Repair Plant, has officially gone into production. This is one of the modern factories built with the help of the State Council for Tibet's development. In the process of building the factory, the whole country gave it great support in terms of manpower and materials.

### 拉萨汽车修配厂投入生产

1957-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　拉萨汽车修配厂投入生产
    西藏第一座近代化汽车修配厂——拉萨汽车修配厂正式投入生产。这是国务院帮助西藏建设的几个现代化工厂之一。在建厂过程中，全国各地给了它人力物力很大支援。
