Title: Eleanor Roosevelt Requests Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1957-06-27
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 26th: Eleanor Roosevelt, widow of the late President Roosevelt, said in her column "My Day" on June 25th that she has applied to go to China to meet with some officials in the Chinese government. In response to a question from the "New York Post" about American journalists visiting China, Mrs. Roosevelt said she hoped the US State Department would allow all American journalists who wish to visit China to report news from there.

### 罗斯福夫人要求访问中国

1957-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　罗斯福夫人要求访问中国
    据新华社26日讯  纽约消息：美国故总统罗斯福的夫人埃伦诺·罗斯福6月25日在她的专栏“我的日子”中说，她已经申请去中国，以便同中国政府中的一些官员会见。罗斯福夫人在回答“纽约邮报”关于美国记者访华问题时说，她希望美国国务院能允许所有希望访问中国的美国记者到中国采访消息。
