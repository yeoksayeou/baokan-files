From the Yellow Sea

　　Our country has a vast ocean, a long coastline, large fishing grounds, and abundant resources. Seafood is a nutritious supplementary food loved by the broad masses of the people. However, when we buy or eat seafood, not everyone understands the selfless labor of the fishermen at sea to meet the needs of domestic and foreign markets.

　　The photos here introduce the operations of the fishermen of several fishing vessels of the Tianjin Marine Fishing Company.

　　Left photo: Fishing vessels set sail.　          Our reporter Liu Changzhong took the photo.

　　Fishing boats of the Fishery Production Cooperative at sea.

　　Clever seagulls are looking for fish that escaped the net.

　　Work at sea is both tense and pleasant.

　　The work of the radio operator must be error-free.

　　The workers carry boxes of sorted yellow croaker ashore, so that they can be transported to various places.

　　Fishing vessel No. 7 once caught 335 boxes of yellow croaker and prawns in one net. Calculating at 44 catties per box, that's more than 14,700 catties.

### 来自黄海（图片）
刘长忠
1957-06-23
第8版()
专栏：

　　来自黄海
    我国有辽阔的海洋，海岸线长，渔场面积大，资源丰富，而海产品又是广大人民喜爱的、富有营养价值的副食品。但，我们在买或者在吃海产品的时候，并不是每个人都了解捕鱼工人为了满足国内外市场的需要，在海上进行忘我劳动的情况。
    这里的照片，是介绍天津市海洋捕捞公司的几条渔轮工人的作业情况。
    左图：渔轮出海　          本报记者　刘长忠摄
    渔业生产合作社的渔船在海上。
    聪明的海鸥在找漏网之鱼。
    海上的工作，既紧张又愉快。
    报务员的工作是不能出差错的。
    工人们把分类装好的黄鱼，一箱箱地搬上岸，以便运往各地。
    七号渔轮曾经一网捕获三百三十五箱黄鱼和对虾，每箱按四十四斤计算，就是一万四千七百多斤。
