Title: American Scientist Pauling Calls on Colleagues from All Countries to Jointly Demand a Ban on Nuclear Weapons Testing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-11
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 10th: New York dispatch: American Nobel Prize winner and biochemist Pauling told reporters today that he hopes that scientists from all over the world, including those from Britain, France, and the Soviet Union, will jointly issue a declaration calling for a ban on nuclear weapons testing.

　　Pauling is the initiator of the recent movement in which two thousand American scientists jointly called for a ban on nuclear weapons testing. He said that scientists know best the dangers of such tests.

　　Pauling will leave for Europe tomorrow to give a series of lectures. He said that he plans to talk about world peace issues in every lecture.

### 美国科学家保林呼吁各国同业  联名要求禁止核武器试验

1957-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　美国科学家保林呼吁各国同业
　　联名要求禁止核武器试验
    新华社10日讯  纽约消息：美国诺贝尔奖金获得者、生物化学家保林今天对新闻记者说，他希望全世界的科学家，包括英、法、苏等国科学家在内，联名发表宣言，呼吁禁止核武器试验。
    保林是最近美国两千名科学家联合呼吁禁止核武器试验的运动的发起人。他说，科学家是最知道这种试验的危害的。
    保林明天将启程去欧洲作一系列的讲学。他说，他在讲学中每次都准备谈到世界和平问题。
