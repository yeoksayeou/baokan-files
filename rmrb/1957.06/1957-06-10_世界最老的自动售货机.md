Author:

    The World's Oldest Vending Machine

    The world's oldest vending machine is over two thousand years old and is now housed in the British Museum in London. It is an automatic wine dispenser; when people drop a few copper coins into the small hole, the machine automatically fills a cup with wine.

### 世界最老的自动售货机

1957-06-10
第3版()
专栏：花絮

    世界最老的自动售货机
    世界最老的自动售货机已经有两千余年的历史，现在存放在伦敦大英博物馆中。这是一台自动售酒器，当人们在小孔中投进几个铜元时，机器就会自己倒满一杯酒。
