Title: State Council Passes Instruction on Further Developing the Increase Production and Practice Economy Movement
Author:
Date: 1957-06-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 31st - Today's 50th plenary session of the State Council discussed and passed the "State Council's Instruction on Further Developing the Increase Production and Practice Economy Movement."

The meeting also approved the State Council's decision to abolish Chenliu County in Henan Province and incorporate the administrative area of Chenliu County into Kaifeng County.

Finally, the meeting approved appointment and removal matters.

### 国务院通过进一步开展增产节约运动的指示

1957-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院通过进一步开展增产节约运动的指示
    新华社31日讯　今天举行的国务院全体会议第五十次会议，讨论通过了“国务院关于进一步开展增产节约运动的指示”。
    会议还通过了国务院关于撤销河南省陈留县将陈留县所属行政区域划归开封县的决定。
    最后，会议通过了任免事项。
