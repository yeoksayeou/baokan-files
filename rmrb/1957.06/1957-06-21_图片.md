    A meeting was convened by the Italian National Committee for the Development of Relations with China in Milan on June 8 to discuss expanding economic relations between Italy and China. Economists, politicians, and business figures attending the meeting emphasized that Italy must change its policy towards China and establish lasting mutual relations with China in all aspects. The picture shows the interior of the meeting venue.

### 图片

1957-06-21
第5版()
专栏：

    意大利发展同中国关系全国委员会6月8日在米兰召开了一个会议，讨论扩大意大利和中国经济关系问题。参加会议的经济学家、政治家，以及工商界人士强调指出意大利必须改变对中国的政策，要和中国在各方面建立持久的相互关系。图为会议会场内景。　　　　　　
　　　　　（新华社稿）
