Title: Premier Zhou Receives Peace Representatives from Twelve Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th. Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon thirty-one peace representatives from twelve countries including Argentina, Australia, Chile, Colombia, Egypt, Iraq, Japan, Jordan, Portugal, South Africa, Uruguay, and Venezuela who were visiting China after attending the World Peace Council meeting in Colombo. When receiving the representatives, Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, and Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman, were also present.

### 周总理接见十二国和平代表

1957-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见十二国和平代表
    新华社29日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了在出席世界和平理事会科伦坡会议后来中国访问的阿根廷、澳大利亚、智利、哥伦比亚、埃及、伊拉克、日本、约旦、葡萄牙、南非、乌拉圭、委内瑞拉等十二个国家的三十一位和平代表。接见时，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席廖承志也在座。
