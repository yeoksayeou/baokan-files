Confession
　　Fang Cheng
　　Setting: A corner of the U.S. Senate Internal Security Subcommittee hearing room.
　　Characters: Joe—Member of the Subcommittee.
　　      A, B, C, D—Those being interrogated.
　　Joe (to A): Are you a Communist?
　　A: Yes.
　　Joe: A fine of three thousand dollars and three months in prison!
　　Joe (to B): Are you a Communist?
　　B: No.
　　Joe: Lying! A fine of three thousand dollars and three months in prison!
　　Joe (to C): Are you a Communist?
　　C: (Silent)
　　Joe: You are in contempt of court! A fine of three thousand dollars and three months in prison!
　　Joe (to D): Are you a Communist?
　　D: I've already brought three thousand dollars, and told my family I'll see them in three months.

### 供词
方成
1957-06-21
第8版()
专栏：

　　供词
　　方成
　　布置：美国参议院国内安全小组委员会审讯室一角。
　　人物：乔治——小组委员会审讯委员。
　　      爱，皮，西，蒂——被审者
　　乔（问爱）：你是共产党员吗？
　　爱：是。
　　乔：罚款三千元，监禁三个月！
　　乔（问皮）：你是共产党员吗？
　　皮：不是。
　　乔：撒谎！罚款三千元，监禁三个月！
　　乔（问西）：你是共产党员吗？
　　西（沉默不语）
　　乔：你蔑视审讯，罚款三千元，监禁三个月！
　　乔（问蒂）：你是共产党员吗？
　　蒂：我已经带来三千元，并关照家里，三个月以后再见了。
