Title: Wuhan Han Opera Troupe Performs in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-06-29
Page: 7

　　Wuhan Han Opera Troupe Performs in Beijing

　　(Our dispatch) The Wuhan Han Opera Troupe, led by the renowned Han Opera actress Chen Bohua, who won the first prize for actors at the National First Opera Festival, began its reporting performance in Beijing on the 28th. In the premiere performance, Chen Bohua starred in "Universe Seal," the very play that earned her extremely high praise at the First Opera Festival. This performance also received enthusiastic praise from the audience.

　　On the evenings of the 25th and 27th, the Literary and Art Club of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the Drama Club of the Chinese Dramatists Association respectively invited the troupe to perform "Twice Blooming Plum" and "Romance in the Cupboard" for the capital's literary and art circles and drama circles, receiving unanimous praise. Premier Zhou Enlai, along with Comrades Kang Sheng, Deng Zihui, Zhou Yang, Liu Zhiming, and Yang Hansheng, visited the actors backstage after watching the plays, congratulating them on their performances.

### 武汉市汉剧团来京演出

1957-06-29
第7版()
专栏：

　　武汉市汉剧团来京演出
    本报讯　以荣获全国第一届戏曲观摩会演一等演员奖的著名汉剧演员陈伯华为首的武汉市汉剧团，28日起在京汇报公演。首次演出中，由陈伯华主演的“宇宙锋”一剧，正是她在第一届戏曲会演中博得极高评价的剧目。这一次的演出，同样获得观众热烈的赞扬。
    25日和27日两晚，中国文学艺术界联合会的文艺俱乐部和中国戏剧家协会的戏剧俱乐部，曾分别邀请该团为首都文艺界和戏剧界演出“二度梅”和“柜中缘”等剧，受到一致的好评。周恩来总理和康生、邓子恢、周扬、刘芝明、阳翰笙等同志在观剧后，都到后台慰问演员，祝贺他们的演出。
