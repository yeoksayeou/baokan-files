Title: Premier Zhou Receives Belgian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-09
Page: 1

    Premier Zhou Receives Belgian Cultural Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th -- Premier Zhou Enlai received the Belgian Cultural Delegation this afternoon.
    Those present at the reception included Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade.

### 周总理接见比利时文化代表团

1957-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见比利时文化代表团
    新华社8日讯　周恩来总理今日下午接见了比利时文化代表团。
    接见时在座的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙、对外贸易部副部长卢绪章等人。
