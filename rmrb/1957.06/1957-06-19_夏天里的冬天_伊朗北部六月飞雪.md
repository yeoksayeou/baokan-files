Title: Summer in Winter: Snow Falls in Northern Iran in June
Author:
Date: 1957-06-19
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Tehran dispatch: In Azerbaijan Province, Iran, bordering the Soviet Union, in the city of Sain (with a latitude roughly equivalent to Taiyuan, China), residents had already switched to wearing lighter clothing in this early summer season. However, on the 17th, an unprecedented heavy snow suddenly fell. The snow accumulated to a thickness of six inches.

### 夏天里的冬天  伊朗北部六月飞雪

1957-06-19
第7版()
专栏：

    夏天里的冬天
    伊朗北部六月飞雪
    新华社18日讯　德黑兰消息：伊朗和苏联交界处阿塞拜疆省的萨因（纬度约相当于我国太原），正当入夏季节，居民已经穿上单衣。但是，17日忽然纷纷扬扬下了一场前所未见的大雪。积雪厚达六英寸。
