Title: Beijing TV Tower is Under Design, Capable of Broadcasting Color and Black and White Programs
Author:
Date: 1957-06-17
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th - The Moscow Steel Structure Design Institute is designing a coil-rod transmitting tower for the Beijing Television Center, with a tower height of 400 meters.

　　This transmitting tower design was selected by the designers of this design institute after studying several design schemes. This transmitting tower is the most economical and effective, requiring only 1,400 tons of steel during construction.

　　The top of the future Beijing TV Tower will be a 100-meter-long quadrangular prism, with antennas for broadcasting color and two black and white programs installed on top. At a height of 200 meters, there will be a ring of corridors that can accommodate 100 people, from which one can have a panoramic view of the entire city of Beijing.

### 北京电视塔正在设计  可放送彩色和黑白节目

1957-06-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　北京电视塔正在设计
    可放送彩色和黑白节目
    新华社莫斯科16日电　莫斯科钢结构设计院正在为北京电视中心设计卷杆式发射塔，塔高四百公尺。
    这种发射塔的设计是这个设计院的设计人员研究了好几种设计方案以后挑选出的。这种发射塔最经济而且效果最大，建筑时只需要一千四百吨钢材。
    未来的北京电视塔的顶部是长达一百公尺的四角棱柱，上面架设放送彩色和两种黑白节目的天线。在两百公尺高处，将有一圈可以容纳一百人的走廊，从这里可以俯瞰全北京城。
