Title: Former French Prime Minister Faure Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-06-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd—Former French Prime Minister Faure and his wife left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their country.
　　Zhang Xiruo, Wu Maosun, and Yue Songsheng were among those who saw them off at the airport.

### 法前总理富尔回国

1957-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    法前总理富尔回国
    新华社22日讯　法国前总理富尔和夫人今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    张奚若、吴茂荪和乐松生等人都到机场欢送。
