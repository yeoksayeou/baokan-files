Title: Scientific Planning Committee Meeting Begins Today
Author:
Date: 1957-06-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 12th: The fourth enlarged meeting of the State Council's Scientific Planning Committee will be held in Beijing starting on the 13th. This meeting will focus on discussing three issues:

　　I. The 1957 National Science and Technology Research Plan; II. The system of scientific research work; III. A plan to solve the conditions for scientific research work, including books, materials, instruments, and chemical reagents.

　　The meeting is scheduled to last three days. Attending the meeting are: members of the Scientific Planning Committee, cadres from various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions responsible for local scientific administration work – such as heads of local science work committees, heads of local government cultural and educational departments, as well as scientists from certain universities, research institutions, and industrial departments, totaling more than 200 people.

　　Prior to the meeting, representatives from various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions had already held a week-long symposium in Beijing to study the tasks undertaken by local areas in the national science work and how to develop local scientific research work.

### 科学规划委员会会议今起举行

1957-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　科学规划委员会会议今起举行
    新华社12日讯  国务院科学规划委员会第四次扩大会议13日起在北京举行。这次会议将着重讨论三个问题：
    一、1957年全国科学技术研究计划；二、科学研究工作的体制；三、解决科学研究工作条件的方案，包括图书、资料、仪器和化学试剂。
    会议预定开三天。出席会议的是：科学规划委员会委员、各省、市、自治区负责本地科学行政工作的干部——如地方科学工作委员会的负责人，地方政府文教部门的负责人，以及某些高等学校、研究机关和产业部门的科学家，共二百多人。
    在会议举行以前，各省、市、自治区代表已经在北京开了一星期的座谈会，研究了在全国科学工作中地方所负担的任务以及怎样开展地方的科学研究工作等问题。
