Title: Heated Discussions at Budapest Municipal Party Congress; Demands to Eliminate Counter-Revolutionary Remnants
Author:
Date: 1957-06-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 10th: The Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party's Budapest Municipal Party Congress concluded on June 9th. Nine hundred and thirty-two official delegates and two hundred observers attended the two-day meeting.

　　The meeting heard and discussed the report on the work of the Party presented by Mátyás Rákosi, First Secretary of the Budapest Municipal Committee. Twenty-nine people spoke during the discussion. They pointed out the necessity of quickly eliminating counter-revolutionary remnants, severely criticized the shortcomings in the work of Party organizations in various departments, and put forward specific suggestions for improving the work of the Party, economy, ideology, and culture.

　　The delegates' meeting passed relevant resolutions and elected members of the Budapest Municipal Party Committee and ninety-six delegates to the National Party Congress.

　　To date, most of Hungary's counties have held Party Congresses and elected delegates to the National Party Congress.

### 布达佩斯市党代会上发言热烈  要求消灭反革命残余

1957-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　布达佩斯市党代会上发言热烈
　　要求消灭反革命残余
    新华社布达佩斯10日电  匈牙利社会主义工人党布达佩斯市党代表会议在6月9日闭幕。出席这次历时两天的会议的有正式代表九百三十二人，列席代表二百人。
    会议听取和讨论了布达佩斯市委员会第一书记马罗山所作的关于党的工作情况的报告。在讨论中有二十九人发言。他们指出了迅速消灭反革命残余的必要性，严厉地批评了各部门党组织工作中的缺点，并且提出了改进党、经济、思想、文化工作的具体建议。
    代表会议通过了有关决议，并且选举了布达佩斯市党委员会委员和出席全国党代表会议的九十六位代表。
    到现在为止，匈牙利全国大部分的州都已经举行了党代表会议，并且选举了出席全国党代表会议的代表。
