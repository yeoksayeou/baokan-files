Title: Saigon "Zhong Sheng Bao" Reveals: South Vietnam Intensifying Preparations for War

Author:

Date: 1957-06-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 22nd: Vietnam News Agency reported: Saigon's "Zhong Sheng Bao" said on June 12th that the South Vietnamese regime allocated 29,380,000 South Vietnamese Dong this year for airport repair costs. Airports in Phu Bai, Duong Dong, Lien Khang, Buon Ma Thuot, Kontum, and Pleiku will all be repaired.

    The South Vietnamese regime has established "military farms" along strategic highways and at key locations in the Central Highlands of South Vietnam. Recently, more troops have been transferred to these areas to build military bases. Since the end of May this year, an American company has been building a strategic highway between Saigon and Bien Hoa.

### 西贡“钟声报”透露  南越加紧备战活动

1957-06-24
第3版()
专栏：

    西贡“钟声报”透露
    南越加紧备战活动
    据新华社河内22日电　越南通讯社报道：西贡“钟声报”6月12日说，南越政权拨款南越币两千九百三十八万元作为今年修理飞机场的费用。富牌、阳东、联康、邦美蜀、昆嵩和波来古等地的机场都要修理。
    南越政权已经沿着各战略公路和在中越南部高原的枢纽地点建立了“军事农场”。最近以来，有更多的军队奉调到这些地区建筑军事基地。从今年5月底开始，有一家美国公司在西贡和边和之间修筑战略公路。
