Title: Eisenhower Falls Ill
Author:
Date: 1957-06-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Washington dispatch: White House Press Secretary Hagerty announced to reporters on the 10th that President Eisenhower fell ill on the morning of the 10th. He had nausea and vomited two or three times. Doctors advised him to cancel his appointments and rest in bed.

Hagerty said that after examination by heart and intestinal specialists, there was no indication that this digestive abnormality was related to his recent ileitis surgery or heart condition.

### 艾森豪威尔卧病

1957-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔卧病
    新华社11日讯  华盛顿消息：白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂10日向记者宣布，艾森豪威尔总统在10日清晨病倒。他曾经反胃，并且呕吐了两三次。医生建议他取消约会，并且卧床休息。
    哈格蒂说，经心脏和肠科专门医生检查结果，没有迹象表明这次消化上的反常现象同他最近的回肠炎手术或心脏病有关。
