Title: Liu Changsheng Arrives in Belgrade
Author:
Date: 1957-06-26
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Belgrade: Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Belgrade by plane on the morning of June 24th. He is visiting at the invitation of the Yugoslav Trade Union Confederation to attend the first representative conference of the Yugoslav Workers' Council, which opened here on the 25th.

### 刘长胜到达贝尔格莱德

1957-06-26
第5版()
专栏：

    刘长胜到达贝尔格莱德
    据新华社讯  中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜6月24日上午乘飞机抵达贝尔格莱德。他是应南斯拉夫工会联合会的邀请前来参加25日在这里开幕的南斯拉夫工人委员会第一次代表大会的。
