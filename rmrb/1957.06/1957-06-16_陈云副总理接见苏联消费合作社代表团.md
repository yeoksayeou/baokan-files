Title: Vice Premier Chen Yun Receives Soviet Consumer Cooperative Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-06-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Vice Premier Chen Yun received all members of the Soviet Consumer Cooperative Delegation headed by Klimov, Chairman of the Central Union of Soviet Consumer Cooperatives, this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Zhang Qilong, Deng Chenxi, and Luo Jun, Vice Directors of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives.

### 陈云副总理接见苏联消费合作社代表团

1957-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    陈云副总理接见苏联消费合作社代表团
    新华社13日讯　陈云副总理今日下午接见了以苏联消费合作社中央联合社主席克利莫夫为首的苏联消费合作社代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有中华全国供销合作总社副主任张启龙、邓辰西、罗俊等人。
