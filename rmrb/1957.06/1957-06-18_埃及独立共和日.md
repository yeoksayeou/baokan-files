Title: Egyptian Independence Republic Day
Author: Ma Guoyou
Date: 1957-06-18
Page: 6

　　Egyptian Independence Republic Day
　　Four years ago today—June 18, 1953—a new page was turned in the history of Egypt, and the Republic of Egypt was formally established.
　　The Egyptian people have waged a long and heroic struggle to win national independence and get rid of British enslavement and control. After World War II, the Egyptian national liberation movement further surged. The reactionary King Farouk, fearing the patriotic strength of the people, continued to collude with British and American imperialism. Imperialist interference and the rule of the feudal dynasty made the Egyptian political situation turbulent. In the five months of 1952, five governments were replaced.
　　On July 23, 1952, a decisive revolutionary upsurge was set off. The Executive Committee of the "Free Officers Organization," composed of young Egyptian officers headed by Gamal Abdel Nasser, launched a revolution and overthrew the rule of the Farouk feudal dynasty. Subsequently, the Executive Committee of the "Free Officers Organization" was reorganized into the "Revolutionary Command Council." This committee deposed King Farouk, confiscated the vast estates of the court clique, abolished social classes and aristocratic titles, abolished the 1923 Constitution that safeguarded feudal interests, and purged the corrupt state institutions. In September 1952, the new Egyptian government was formed. On February 10 of the following year, the new government announced the provisional constitution. On June 18, 1953, the Republic of Egypt was born.
　　This independent republic, rising in Africa, which is known as the "Dark Continent," has shown its vitality.
　　In order to safeguard national independence and sovereignty, Egypt announced last year that it would nationalize the former Suez Canal Company and withstood the test of war. The Egyptian people defeated the armed aggression of Britain, France, and Israel. The more vicious "Eisenhower Doctrine" also ran into a wall in Egypt. This victory of the Egyptian people is a powerful encouragement to the anti-colonialist movement and a great contribution to the struggle to safeguard world peace.
　　The Republican government held a referendum on June 23, 1956. As a result of the vote, the new draft constitution promulgated on January 16, 1956, was adopted, and Gamal Abdel Nasser was elected as the President of the Republic.
　　The new constitution solemnly declares that the Egyptian people "will use all our efforts and abilities, with our constructive labor, to build a society that can achieve prosperity and in which the Egyptian people can complete the work of eradicating imperialism and its lackeys, eliminating feudalism, eradicating monopoly and capital control over the government, establishing a strong national army, establishing social justice, and establishing a sound democratic life."
　　Now the Egyptian people are working hard to consolidate their independence and have begun the construction of the Five-Year Plan. At the same time, Egypt is not only striving to expand unity and cooperation with Arab countries, but also strengthening friendly relations with the Soviet Union and other socialist countries. On May 16, 1956, Egypt established diplomatic relations with our country. Over the past year, the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Egypt have developed. (Ma Guoyou)

### 埃及独立共和日
马国佑
1957-06-18
第6版()
专栏：国际知识

　　埃及独立共和日
    四年前的今天——1953年6月18日，埃及的历史揭开了新的一页，埃及共和国正式成立了。
    埃及人民为了争取民族独立、摆脱英国的奴役和控制，曾经进行过长期的、英勇的斗争。第二次世界大战后，埃及民族解放运动进一步高涨。反动的国王法鲁克由于惧怕人民的爱国力量，继续同英美帝国主义勾结在一起。帝国主义的干涉和封建王朝的统治，使埃及政局动荡不定，在1952年的五个月中，就更换了五个政府。
    1952年7月23日，决定性的革命风潮掀起了。以加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔为首的埃及青年军官组成的“自由军官组织”执行委员会发动了革命，推翻了法鲁克封建王朝统治。接着“自由军官组织”执行委员会改组为“革命指导委员会”。这个委员会废黜了国王法鲁克，没收了宫廷集团的广大地产，取消了社会等级和贵族称号，废除了维护封建利益的1923年宪法，并清洗了贪污腐败的国家机构。1952年9月，埃及新政府组成。次年2月10日新政府公布了临时宪法。1953年6月18日，埃及共和国诞生了。
    在被称为“黑暗大陆”的非洲崛起的这个独立的共和国，显示了自己的生命力。
    为了维护国家的独立主权，去年埃及宣布把前苏伊士运河公司收归国有，并经受住了战火的考验。埃及人民击败了英、法和以色列的武装侵略。更凶恶的“艾森豪威尔主义”也在埃及碰了壁。埃及人民的这个胜利，是对反殖民主义运动的有力鼓舞，也是对维护世界和平斗争的巨大贡献。
    共和国政府在1956年6月23日举行公民投票。投票结果，通过了1956年1月16日公布的新宪法草案，同时，选出了加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔担任共和国的总统。
    新宪法庄严宣布：埃及人民“将用我们全部努力和能力，以我们的建设性劳动，来建立一个可以实现繁荣和埃及人民可以完成铲除帝国主义和它的仆从、消灭封建主义、铲除垄断以及资本对政府的控制、建立一支强大的国军、建立社会正义、建立健全的民主生活的工作的社会。”
    现在埃及人民正在努力巩固自己的独立，并开始了五年计划的建设。同时，埃及不仅争取扩大同阿拉伯国家的团结和合作，而且还加强了同苏联和其他社会主义国家的友好关系。1956年5月16日埃及同我国建立了外交关系。一年来，中埃两国的友好合作关系获得了发展。　（马国佑）
