Title: Britain Extends Relaxed Trade Regulations to Korea and Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1957-06-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, London, June 1st: A British Board of Trade spokesperson announced on May 31st that Britain's relaxed regulations on trade with China would "automatically" apply to trade with the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 英国放宽贸易的规定适于朝鲜和越南

1957-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    英国放宽贸易的规定适于朝鲜和越南
    新华社1日讯　伦敦消息：英国贸易部发言人5月31日宣布，英国放宽对中国贸易限制的规定，也“自动地”适用于和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和越南民主共和国的贸易。
