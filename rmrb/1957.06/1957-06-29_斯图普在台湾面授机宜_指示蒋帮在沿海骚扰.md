Title: Stup Visits Taiwan to Impart Instructions, Directs Chiang Gang to Harass Coastal Areas
Author: 
Date: 1957-06-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Stup, Commander-in-Chief of the U.S. Pacific Command, held talks with the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Taiwan on June 27th.

According to the Chiang gang's Central News Agency, Stup had "exchanged views" with the Chiang gang's "Minister of National Defense" Yu Dawei and "Chief of Staff" Peng Mengji on "the recent military situation in the Taiwan Strait." Recently, the Chiang Kai-shek clique has been stepping up harassment activities in coastal areas, and Yu Dawei even went to Jinmen on the 26th to cheer up the Chiang troops stationed there.

During his twenty-five hours in Taiwan, Stup also held talks with Chiang Kai-shek on "matters of common concern" and met with U.S. "Ambassador" to Taiwan, Rankin, and Commander of the U.S. "Taiwan Defense Command," Ingersoll.

### 斯图普在台湾面授机宜  指示蒋帮在沿海骚扰

1957-06-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　斯图普在台湾面授机宜
    指示蒋帮在沿海骚扰
    新华社28日讯　美军太平洋区总司令斯图普6月27日在台湾同蒋介石集团会谈。
    据蒋帮中央社说，斯图普曾经“就最近台湾海峡军事情势”同蒋帮“国防部长”俞大维和“参谋总长”彭孟缉“交换意见”。近来蒋介石集团正在沿海地区加紧进行骚扰活动，俞大维还在26日到金门去为那里的蒋军打气。
    斯图普在台湾的二十五小时中，还同蒋介石会谈了“共同有关的问题”，并且会见了美国驻台湾“大使”兰金和美军“协防台湾司令”英格索尔。
