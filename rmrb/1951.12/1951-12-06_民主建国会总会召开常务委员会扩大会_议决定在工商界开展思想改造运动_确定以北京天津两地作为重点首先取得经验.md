Title: Democratic National Construction Association Headquarters Holds Standing Committee Enlarged Meeting; Decides to Launch Ideological Remolding Movement in Industrial and Commercial Circles; Determines Beijing and Tianjin as Key Areas to Gain Experience First
Author:
Date: 1951-12-06
Page: 1

    Democratic National Construction Association Headquarters Holds Standing Committee Enlarged Meeting; Decides to Launch Ideological Remolding Movement in Industrial and Commercial Circles; Determines Beijing and Tianjin as Key Areas to Gain Experience First
    [From Our Newspaper] The Democratic National Construction Association Headquarters held an enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee and decided to carry out ideological remolding movements among members and in industrial and commercial circles in a planned manner.
    In order to support the call of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on promoting the ideological remolding movement, the Democratic National Construction Association Headquarters convened an enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee on November 2. In addition to the committee members in Beijing, the heads of the Democratic National Construction Association from various regions who came to Beijing to attend the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference were also invited to attend. At the meeting, Huang Yanpei, Zhang Naiqi, and Nan Hanchen, conveners of the Standing Committee of the Democratic National Construction Association Headquarters, first gave reports, focusing on the significance of promoting the three major movements of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, increasing production and practicing economy, and ideological remolding. Based on the resolutions of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and in conjunction with the basic work of the Democratic National Construction Association, the meeting discussed methods for promoting this movement, reaching a unanimous understanding.
    The personnel who attended this meeting will go to various parts of the country to report to members, actively arrange various specific tasks, and conscientiously promote the ideological remolding movement.
    [Another Dispatch] In order to improve the work of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea and increasing production and practicing economy in industrial and commercial circles, the Democratic National Construction Association Headquarters has decided to first promote the ideological remolding movement in industrial and commercial circles. It has also decided to use the Beijing and Tianjin branches of the Democratic National Construction Association as key areas to pilot the ideological remolding work in industrial and commercial circles in order to gain experience and promote it to industrial and commercial circles throughout the country. Zhang Naiqi, convener of the Standing Committee of the Democratic National Construction Association Headquarters, gave an enlightening report entitled "Industrial and Commercial Circles and Ideological Remolding" at the "New Century Symposium" hosted by the Tianjin branch on November 25. At the same time, the Beijing branch of the Democratic National Construction Association also convened three symposiums with some Democratic National Construction Association members in industrial and commercial circles to listen to their opinions on the ideological remolding movement as a reference for future ideological remolding movements.

### 民主建国会总会召开常务委员会扩大会  议决定在工商界开展思想改造运动  确定以北京天津两地作为重点首先取得经验

1951-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    民主建国会总会召开常务委员会扩大会
    议决定在工商界开展思想改造运动
    确定以北京天津两地作为重点首先取得经验
   【本报讯】民主建国会总会召开常务委员会扩大会议，决定有步骤地开展会员和工商界思想改造运动。
    民主建国会总会为拥护中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议关于推动思想改造运动的号召，已在十一月二日召开常务委员会扩大会议。参加会议的除在京委员外，这次来京参加人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议的各地民主建国会负责人也被邀请列席。会议上，首先由民主建国会总会常务委员会召集人黄炎培、章乃器、南汉宸作报告，他们着重指出了推行抗美援朝、增产节约和思想改造三大运动的意义。会议又根据人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议的决议，结合民主建国会的基本工作，讨论了推行这一运动的办法，得到了一致的认识。
    参加这次会议的人员会后将分别到全国各地向会员进行传达报告，并积极布置各种具体工作，认真推动思想改造运动。
    【又讯】民主建国会总会为使工商界抗美援朝工作和增产节约工作做得更好，已决定在工商界中首先推行思想改造运动。并决定以民主建国会北京、天津两个分会作为重点，试行工商界的思想改造工作，以便取得经验，推广到全国各地工商界中去。民主建国会总会常务委员会召集人章乃器，十一月二十五日在天津分会主持的“新世纪座谈会”上作了题为“工商界与思想改造”的启发报告。同时，民主建国会北京分会也召集一部分工商界中的民主建国会会员举行了三次座谈会，听取他们对思想改造运动的意见，作为将来进行思想改造运动的参考。
