Title: Results of the Final Day of the National Football Competition
Author:
Date: 1951-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 9th. The results of the four matches on the final day of the 1951 National Football Competition are as follows:

    East China vs. Northwest 6-0 East China wins
    PLA vs. Southwest 5-2 PLA wins
    Central South vs. Railway 1-0 Central South wins
    Northeast vs. North China 2-0 Northeast wins
