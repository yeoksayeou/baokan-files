Title: The American Aggressors Actually Launched Poison Gas Bombs Again; This Crime Must Not Escape the Punishment of Justice
Author:
Date: 1951-12-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 28th—This month, on the 17th, the American aggressors shamelessly violated humanitarian principles and international law, using F80 fighter planes to drop a poison gas bomb on our army's Huangji Mountain position west of Lianchuan. After the bomb landed, it burned, emitting white smoke for thirty minutes. Those poisoned experienced dizziness, vomiting, and a fever throughout their bodies. The American aggressors' repeated and shameless use of poison gas bombs will not escape the righteous punishment of the Korean and Chinese People's Army.

### 美侵略军竟又投掷毒气弹  这种罪行绝不能逃脱正义的惩罚

1951-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    美侵略军竟又投掷毒气弹
    这种罪行绝不能逃脱正义的惩罚 【新华社朝鲜前线二十八日电】本月十七日，美国侵略者又无耻地违反人道和国际公法，用F80型战斗机在我军涟川以西黄鸡山阵地上投下毒气弹一枚。该弹落地后就燃烧，冒白烟达三十分钟。中毒者头晕、呕吐、全身发烧。美国侵略者屡次无耻使用毒气弹的罪行，是不能逃脱朝中人民军队正义的惩罚的。
