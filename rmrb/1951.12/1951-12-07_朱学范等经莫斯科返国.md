Title: Zhu Xuefan and Others Return Home via Moscow
Author:
Date: 1951-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 6th: Zhu Xuefan and other Chinese representatives who attended the World Federation of Trade Unions Council meeting in Berlin passed through Moscow on their way home. They left here by train on the evening of the 5th to return to Beijing; Li Jiebo and others left by plane on the morning of the 6th to return to Beijing.

### 朱学范等经莫斯科返国

1951-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    朱学范等经莫斯科返国
     【新华社莫斯科六日电】出席世界工会联合会理事会柏林会议的中国代表朱学范等在返国途中经莫斯科，五日晚离此乘火车返北京；李颉伯等在六日晨乘飞机返北京。
