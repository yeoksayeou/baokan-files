Title: Pakistani Ambassador to China, Mr. Raza, Held a Reception Yesterday; Vice Chairman Zhu and Others Were Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1951-12-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th: Pakistani Ambassador to China, Mr. Raza, held a reception on the evening of the 28th.

Among those invited to attend were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De and Li Jishen, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council Guo Moruo and Huang Yanpei, Acting Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and heads of various departments of the Central People's Government Peng Zhen, Ma Yinchu, Ma Xulun, Shen Junru, Ye Jizhuang, Shen Yanbing, Peng Zemin, Zhang Xiruo, Xie Juezai, Fu Zuoyi, Zhang Bojun, Luo Ruiqing, Zhu Xuefan, Hu Yuzhi, Liao Chengzhi, He Cheng, Xiao Hua, Yu Xinqing, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Zhang Youyu, totaling more than 300 people.

The foreign envoys present included Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Hungarian Ambassador Sándor, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Burmese Ambassador U Ohn Maung, Swiss Minister Henri Bonjour, Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Isa Matti, Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Daudin, Danish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Meinstorp, diplomatic personnel from the Indian and Swedish embassies, and negotiation representatives from the British and Norwegian governments.

The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 巴基斯坦驻我国大使罗查昨日举行招待会  朱副主席等应邀出席

1951-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦驻我国大使罗查昨日举行招待会
    朱副主席等应邀出席 【新华社二十八日讯】巴基斯坦政府驻我国大使罗查在二十八日晚举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，政务院副总理郭沫若、黄炎培，中国人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，外交部副部长章汉夫和中央人民政府各部门首长彭真、马寅初、马叙伦、沈钧儒、叶季壮、沈雁冰、彭泽民、张奚若、谢觉哉、傅作义、章伯钧、罗瑞卿、朱学范、胡愈之、廖承志、贺诚、萧华、余心清以及北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等共三百多人。
    到会的各国使节有苏联大使罗申、捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、缅甸大使吴拉茂、瑞士公使任佐立、印尼临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、波兰临时代办道定、丹麦临时代办迈思妥、印度和瑞典使馆外交人员及英国、挪威政府的谈判代表等人。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
