Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-12-19
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Ma Shusheng's crew, a famous labor model of the Shanghai Railway Administration, has run for 884 days, achieving a safe mileage of 250,000 kilometers, preventing 22 major and minor operational accidents, and setting a new record for the entire railway. Now Ma Shusheng is driving his locomotive, waving to people to signal that he will continue to advance towards achieving 300,000 safe kilometers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海铁路管理局著名的劳动模范马树生的包乘组，运行八八四天，达到二十五万公里的安全行程，防止了大小运转责任事故二十二件，创全路新纪录。现在马树生正开动他的机车，向人们招手示意，继续为达到三十万安全公里而前进。
