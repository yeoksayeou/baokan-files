Title: Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League Holds Meeting to Accept Gifts from Czechoslovakian Children
Author:
Date: 1951-12-22
Page: 4

    Youth League Beijing Municipal Committee Holds Meeting
    To Accept Gifts from Czechoslovakian Children
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st] The Beijing Municipal Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League held the "Beijing Children's Acceptance Ceremony for Gifts from Czechoslovakian Children" on the 15th. The gifts from the Czechoslovakian children to the Beijing children were a 16mm movie projector and four films introducing Czechoslovakia's industrial construction. These gifts were forwarded by the Czechoslovakian Embassy in China.
    Attending the gift-giving ceremony were Czechoslovak Ambassador Weisskopf and embassy staff, representatives of middle and primary school children from Beijing, Zhang Dazhong, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, representatives from the Youth League Central Committee's Children's Department, and the Beijing Municipal People's Government Bureau of Culture and Education, totaling more than 400 people.

### 青年团北京市委会召开大会  接受捷少年儿童的赠礼

1951-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    青年团北京市委会召开大会
    接受捷少年儿童的赠礼
    【新华社二十一日讯】中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会在十五日举行“北京市少年儿童接受捷克斯洛伐克少年儿童赠礼大会”。捷克斯洛伐克少年儿童送给北京市少年儿童的礼物是一架十六糎的电影机和四部介绍捷克斯洛伐克工业建设的电影片。这些礼物都是由捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆转来的。
    参加赠礼大会的有捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫和大使馆人员，北京市各中、小学少年儿童代表，青年团北京市委员会副书记张大中，青年团中央少年儿童部，北京市人民政府文教局等单位代表四百多人。
