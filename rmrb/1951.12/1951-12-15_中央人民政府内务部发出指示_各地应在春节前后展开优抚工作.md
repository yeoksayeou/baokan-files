Title: Central People's Government Ministry of Internal Affairs Issues Instructions: All Localities Should Launch Support-the-Army Work Around the Spring Festival

Author:
Date: 1951-12-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 12th—The Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government issued instructions on the 11th regarding inspecting and carrying out support-the-army work around the Spring Festival. The instructions stated: Based on the fundamental spirit of Vice Chairman Peng Zhen of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, in his report to the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, regarding continuing to strengthen preferential treatment for the families of revolutionary martyrs and revolutionary servicemen, all levels of people's government should conduct a thorough inspection of support-the-army work before and after the Spring Festival. On the basis of this thorough inspection, all levels of people's government should formulate practical and feasible support-the-army work plans for the coming year. The instructions stated: Around the Spring Festival, all localities should universally launch movements to express sympathy and solicitude to the families of revolutionary martyrs, families of revolutionary servicemen, disabled revolutionary soldiers, and the sick and wounded. The instructions requested that all levels of people's government and civil affairs departments bring the above work to the People's Representatives' Congress for discussion. District and township people's governments should specifically convene People's Representatives' Congresses to discuss the above work.

### 中央人民政府内务部发出指示  各地应在春节前后展开优抚工作

1951-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府内务部发出指示
    各地应在春节前后展开优抚工作
    【新华社十二日讯】中央人民政府内务部十一日发出关于春节前后检查与进行优抚工作的指示。指示说：根据中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席彭真在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议上所作的报告中关于继续加强优待革命烈士家属、革命军人家属的工作的基本精神，各级人民政府应在春节前后深入检查一次优抚工作。在深入检查的基础上，各级人民政府应订出明年的切实可行的优抚工作计划。指示说：春节前后，各地要普遍展开慰劳革命烈士家属、革命军人家属、革命残废军人和伤病员的运动。指示要求各级人民政府和民政部门把上述工作提到人民代表会议上去讨论。区、乡人民政府应专门召开人民代表会议，讨论上述工作。
