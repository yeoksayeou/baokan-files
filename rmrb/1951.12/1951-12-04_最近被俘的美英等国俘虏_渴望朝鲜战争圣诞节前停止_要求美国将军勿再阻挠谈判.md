Title: Recently Captured American and British Prisoners of War Hope the Korean War Will Stop Before Christmas, Request American Generals to Stop Obstructing Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1951-12-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 3rd: Our frontline reporter reports: Recently captured American and British prisoners of war in a certain area on the right bank of the Imjin River, unanimously requested American generals to stop obstructing negotiations and hoped that the Korean War would stop before Christmas. This group of prisoners was captured by the Chinese People's Volunteers during the fighting on the Gaowang Mountain line on the right bank of the Imjin River from November 17th to 24th, including Americans, British, Canadians, and New Zealanders.

    A light machine gunner from the U.S. Army's 3rd Infantry Division said: "I ask that the war stop before Christmas so that I can go home for the holidays. This is not only my personal hope, but also the thought of many soldiers in our unit. Fighting in the bitter cold of winter is becoming more and more difficult. Who wants to fight this endless, bloody war?"

    A sergeant squad leader from the 28th Brigade of the British Commonwealth Division said: "Many British soldiers hope that the Korean War will stop before Christmas. We all want to go back to Britain, especially to go back for Christmas. We will get nothing in Korea except more British soldiers' deaths and bloodshed."

    A private first class from the 25th Canadian Brigade said: "If the Korean War can end before Christmas, that is the hope in the bottom of every Canadian soldier's heart. That way, we can return to Canada soon."

    An observer from the 16th New Zealand Field Artillery Regiment said: "As soon as I see the cold winter in Korea, I think of the scene of our New Zealanders going swimming in the bay on Christmas Day. Stop the Korean War quickly! The sooner the better, let us New Zealanders return to New Zealand! The winter on the Korean battlefield is too terrible, everything is uncomfortable."

### 最近被俘的美英等国俘虏  渴望朝鲜战争圣诞节前停止  要求美国将军勿再阻挠谈判

1951-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    最近被俘的美英等国俘虏
    渴望朝鲜战争圣诞节前停止
    要求美国将军勿再阻挠谈判
   【新华社朝鲜前线三日电】本社前线记者报道：最近在临津江右岸前线某地被俘的美英等国俘虏，
    一致要求美国将军勿再阻挠谈判，并渴望朝鲜战争在圣诞节前停止。这批俘虏是十一月十七日至二十四日在临津江右岸高旺山一线作战中被中国人民志愿军俘掳的，其中有美国人、英国人、加拿大人和新西兰人。
    美军第三师的一个轻机枪射手说：“我要求在圣诞节前停止战争，好回家去过节。这不仅是我个人的希望，我们部队中很多士兵也都是这样想。在酷寒的冬天作战，困难是愈来愈多，谁还愿打这个无止境地流血的战争呢？”
    英军联邦师第二十八旅的一个中士班长说：“很多英国士兵都希望在圣诞节前停止朝鲜战争。我们都想回到英国去，特别是想回去过圣诞节。在朝鲜我们什么也不会得到，有的就是更多的英国士兵的死亡与流血。”
    加拿大第二十五旅的一个上等兵说：“假如圣诞节以前能结束朝鲜战争，那就是我们加拿大士兵每个人心底里的希望。这样我们就可以很快回加拿大去了。”
    新西兰第十六野炮团的一个观察兵说：“我一看到朝鲜寒冷的冬天，我就想到我们新西兰人在圣诞节那天到海湾上去游泳的情景。赶快停止朝鲜的战争吧！愈快愈好，让我们新西兰人回到新西兰去！朝鲜战场的冬天太可怕了，一切都使人难受。”
