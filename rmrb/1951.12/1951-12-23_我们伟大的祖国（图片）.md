Our great motherland
The Shatien pomelo, produced in Shatien Township, Rong County, Guangxi, is a famous specialty of our country. This is the Shatien pomelo on display at the South China Native and Special Products Exchange Exhibition. Each basket contains about six thousand pomelos.
