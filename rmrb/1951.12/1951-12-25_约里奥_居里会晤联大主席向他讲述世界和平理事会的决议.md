Title: Joliot-Curie Meets with UN General Assembly President, Informs Him of World Peace Council Resolutions
Author:
Date: 1951-12-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 22nd: According to TASS, World Peace Council President Joliot-Curie and World Peace Council Executive Bureau member Farge met with UN General Assembly President Nasrollah Entezam on the 19th at the Palais de Chaillot in Paris. Joliot-Curie and Farge informed Entezam of the various issues discussed at the World Peace Council's Second Session held in Vienna in early November.

### 约里奥—居里会晤联大主席向他讲述世界和平理事会的决议

1951-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    约里奥—居里会晤联大主席向他讲述世界和平理事会的决议
    【新华社二十二日讯】据塔斯社巴黎讯：世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里和世界和平理事会执行局委员法奇，十九日在巴黎夏乐宫晤见联合国大会主席纳孚。约里奥—居里和法奇向纳孚讲述了十一月初在维也纳举行的世界和平理事会第二次会议所讨论的各项问题。
