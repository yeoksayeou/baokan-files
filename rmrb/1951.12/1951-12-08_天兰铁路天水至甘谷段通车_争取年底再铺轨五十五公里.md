Title: Tianshui-Gangu Section of the Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway Opens to Traffic; Strive to Lay Another 55 Kilometers of Track by the End of the Year
Author:
Date: 1951-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, December 7th: The Tianshui-Gangu section of the Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway opened to traffic on December 1st. The 65-kilometer section saw the continuous improvement of work efficiency as a result of a patriotic labor emulation campaign initiated by all employees. The daily track-laying speed reached a maximum of 3.3 kilometers, thus completing the year's track-laying plan one month ahead of schedule and enabling the rapid opening of the Tianshui-Gangu section of the railway. To this end, Wang Shitai, Director of the Northwest Railway Trunk Line Engineering Bureau, issued a circular of commendation to all employees on December 1st, and proposed to strive to lay another 55 kilometers of track by the end of the year, under the conditions of increasing production and practicing economy and ensuring project quality.

### 天兰铁路天水至甘谷段通车  争取年底再铺轨五十五公里

1951-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    天兰铁路天水至甘谷段通车
    争取年底再铺轨五十五公里
    【新华社兰州七日电】天（水）兰（州）铁路天水至甘谷段已于十二月一日通车。该段长六十五公里，全体员工发起爱国主义的劳动竞赛的结果，使工作效率不断提高，每日铺轨速度最高时曾达到三点三公里，因而提前一个月完成了全年的铺轨计划，使天水至甘谷段铁路迅速通车。为此，西北铁路干线工程局局长王世泰特于十二月一日通报表扬全体员工，并提出在增产节约和保证工程质量的条件下，争取于年底再铺轨五十五公里。
