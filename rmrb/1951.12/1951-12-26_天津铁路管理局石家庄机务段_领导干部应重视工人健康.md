Title: Tianjin Railway Administration Shijiazhuang Locomotive Depot: Leading Cadres Should Value Workers' Health
Author: Wei Qiming
Date: 1951-12-26
Page: 2
Column: Reader's Letter

    Tianjin Railway Administration Shijiazhuang Locomotive Depot
    Leading Cadres Should Value Workers' Health
    Editor: The leading cadres of the Shijiazhuang Locomotive Depot, under the Tianjin Railway Administration, are solely pursuing efficiency and do not value the health of the workers. From November 1st to 22nd, the freight locomotive crew members worked continuously for more than 24 hours on 36 occasions; more than 27 hours on 11 occasions; more than 30 hours on 3 occasions; more than 35 hours on 2 occasions; and even up to 39 hours. The general crew members of this depot are reflecting one after another: going on like this will kill us! However, the leading cadres in the depot are not thinking of ways to solve the problem, only demanding "eliminate accidents." The Tianjin Railway Administration should quickly inspect and correct this phenomenon.
                                        Wei Qiming

### 天津铁路管理局石家庄机务段  领导干部应重视工人健康
魏其明
1951-12-26
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    天津铁路管理局石家庄机务段
    领导干部应重视工人健康
    编辑同志：天津铁路管理局所属石家庄机务段领导干部，单纯追求效率，不重视工人健康。自十一月一日到二十二日，各货物机车乘务员，连续工作二十四小时以上的有三十六次；二十七小时以上的有十一次；三十小时以上的有三次；三十五小时以上的有两次；甚至有达三十九小时的。这个段的一般乘务员纷纷反映：这样下去要累死了！但段里领导干部也不想办法解决，只是要求“消灭事故”。天津铁路管理局应迅速检查纠正这种现象。
                                        魏其明
