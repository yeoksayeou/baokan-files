Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-12-31
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Defending the borders of our great motherland and crushing imperialist aggression is the sacred duty of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. This is the Wan-ting Iron Bridge on the border between Yunnan Province and Burma. The heroic soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army are guarding the southwestern frontier of the motherland, ready at any time to strike at the plot of American imperialism to continue transporting the Chiang bandit troops from Taiwan to Thailand and northern Burma, taking the opportunity to carry out large-scale sabotage activities along our border.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    保卫我们伟大祖国的边疆，粉碎帝国主义的侵略，是中国人民解放军的神圣任务。这是云南省中缅交界的畹町铁桥。英勇的中国人民解放军战士，正在守卫着祖国的西南边疆，随时准备打击美帝国主义继续由台湾运送蒋匪军到泰国和缅甸北部，向我国边境乘机进行大规模扰乱活动的阴谋。
