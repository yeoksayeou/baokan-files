Author:

    Our Great Motherland
    The Sino-Soviet Petroleum Company jointly established by our country and the Soviet Union in Xinjiang is of great significance to the development of our industry and the economic cooperation between China and the Soviet Union. This is the scene of the staff dispatched by the Xinjiang Sino-Soviet Petroleum Company installing power engines at the mine.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国和苏联在新疆共同创办的石油股份公司，对于我国工业的发展和中苏两国的经济合作有着重大的意义。这是新疆中苏石油股份公司派出的工作人员正在矿地安装动力机的情形。
