Title: Documentary "Resist America, Aid Korea" Released, Warmly Welcomed by People Everywhere
Author:
Date: 1951-12-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th - The people of Beijing, Shanghai, Wuhan and other places warmly welcomed the release of the first part of the film "Resist America, Aid Korea."

The number of tickets reserved in advance by the audience reached more than 200,000 in Beijing by the 29th; more than 640,000 in Shanghai by the 27th; and more than 500,000 in Wuhan by the 24th.

Thirteen people's organizations, including the Shanghai Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association, jointly issued a notice recommending the film "Resist America, Aid Korea" to the citizens. The Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Shanghai Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League separately called on all party members and League members in the city to learn the spirit of patriotism and internationalism from this film. Chen Sisheng, head of the Third Branch of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, and members of the delegation currently in Shanghai, reported on their experiences visiting Korea to the audience of this film in various cinemas.

The propagandists of the Beijing Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association and some staff members of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation also mobilized to organize the audience. Many residents in the streets and alleys were talking about this film, and even old ladies in their fifties and sixties said: "We must see 'Resist America, Aid Korea'!" After seeing the mobile screening team of the China Film Corporation showing this film, the workers of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant were deeply inspired by the heroic image of the Chinese People's Volunteers. They all expressed that they would work harder to promote the increase production and practice economy movement, give full play to the strength of the working class, and support the front.

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, December 28th - The people of Chongqing attach great importance to the film "Resist America, Aid Korea." By the 27th, 190,000 tickets had been reserved in advance, creating an unprecedented record for advance movie ticket sales.

### 纪录片“抗美援朝”上映  受到各地人民的热烈欢迎

1951-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    纪录片“抗美援朝”上映
    受到各地人民的热烈欢迎
    【新华社二十九日讯】北京、上海、武汉等地人民热烈欢迎影片“抗美援朝”第一部的上映。
    预先定票的观众，北京市到二十九日为止已有二十多万人；上海市到二十七日为止已有六十四万人，武汉市到二十四日为止已有五十万人。
    上海市抗美援朝分会等十三个人民团体曾联合发出通知向市民推荐“抗美援朝”影片。中国共产党上海市委员会、中国新民主主义青年团上海市工作委员会曾分别号召该市全体党员、团员从这部影片中学习爱国主义和国际主义精神。中国人民赴朝慰问团第三分团团长陈巳生和现在上海的团员，都在各影院向这一影片的观众报告访问朝鲜的见闻。
    北京市抗美援朝分会的宣传员和北京市民主妇女联合会的部分工作人员也出动组织观众。大街小巷中许多居民都在谈论这部影片，连五、六十岁的老太太们也说：“非要看看‘抗美援朝’不可！”石景山钢铁厂的工人们在看到中国影片经理公司流动放映队放映这个影片后，深为中国人民志愿军的英雄形象所鼓舞，他们都表示要更加努力地搞好增产节约运动，充分发挥工人阶级的力量，支援前方。
    【新华社重庆二十八日电】重庆人民对“抗美援朝”影片极为重视，到二十七日止，预先订票的已有十九万人，创预订电影票的空前纪录。
