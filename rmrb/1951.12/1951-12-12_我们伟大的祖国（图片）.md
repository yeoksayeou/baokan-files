    Our Great Motherland
    The 1951 Weaving Method is an advanced weaving method that concentrates the advantages of the operations of model weaving workers from various places, summarizes the Hao Jianxiu work method, learns from the Soviet Guo Valyov work method, and is formulated after twenty days of performance, observation, measurement, analysis, and research. This is a scene of the Tianjin State-owned Fourth Cotton Textile Factory imparting the 1951 Weaving Method. Sun Xiuzhen, a teacher from the training class, is demonstrating to the students in the workshop.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    一九五一织布工作法是集中各地模范织布工人操作的优点，总结郝建秀工作法，学习苏联的郭瓦廖夫工作法，并经过二十天的表演、观察、测定、分析和研究而制订的先进的织布工作法。这是天津国营第四棉纺织厂传授一九五一织布工作法的情形。训练班教师孙秀贞正在车间中向学员表演。
