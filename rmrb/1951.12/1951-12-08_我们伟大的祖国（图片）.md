    Our Great Motherland
    With the direct assistance of Hao Jianxiu, the promotion of the Hao Jianxiu Work Method in various cotton textile factories in Tianjin has achieved great results. This is a scene of Hao Jianxiu (right) watching female worker Dai Shulan demonstrate the Hao Jianxiu Work Method at the Tianjin Private Hengyuan Yarn Factory.
