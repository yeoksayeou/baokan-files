Title: Government Administration Council Holds 113th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th December - The 113th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 30th. After hearing a report by Wang Zheng, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government, on the signing of the Sino-German Postal and Telecommunications Agreement, the meeting approved the Sino-German Postal Agreement and the Sino-German Telecommunications Agreement. The meeting heard and approved a report by Zeng Zhaolun, Deputy Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, on the national plan for the adjustment of engineering colleges. In addition, the meeting passed several appointment and removal cases and submitted appointment and removal cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一一三次会议

1951-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一一三次会议
    【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百一十三次政务会议于三十日下午举行。会议在听取中央人民政府邮电部王诤副部长所作的关于中德签订邮电协定的报告后，批准了中德邮电协定、中德电信协定。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府教育部曾昭抡副部长所作的关于全国工学院调整计划的报告。此外会议通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
