Title: List and Biographical Notes of the Romanian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1951-12-05
Page: 1

    List and Biographical Notes of the Romanian Cultural Delegation:
    Head of Delegation: 包别斯库·杜列亚努 (Pauka 杜列亚努 Pauker Duliianu), current Minister of Education of the Romanian People's Republic, member of the National Assembly of the People's Republic, professor of Romanian history at the 巴洪 (Bahong) University of Bucharest, and former vice-chairman of the 罗苏 (Luo Su) Friendship Association.
    Members:
    One, 安娜·道玛 (Anna Daoma), current Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Romanian People's Republic, professor of Marxism-Leninism at the Bucharest Academy of Economics and Planning.
    Two, 鲁登科 (Rudenko), current Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to the People's Republic of China.
    Three, 奥雷尔·巴伦加 (Aurel Baranga), writer, whose famous works include the play "The Scoundrels" (already translated into Chinese), currently Secretary of the Drama Department of the Romanian Writers' Association, Secretary of the Bucharest National Theater, member of the Bucharest Municipal People's Committee, and former editor of "Free Romania".

### 罗马尼亚文化代表团名单及简历：

1951-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚文化代表团名单及简历：
    团长：包别斯库·杜列亚努，现任罗马尼亚人民共和国教育部部长、人民共和国国民议会议员，并担任布加勒斯特巴洪大学罗马尼亚历史教授，曾任罗苏友好协会副主席。
    团员：一、安娜·道玛，现任罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部副部长、布加勒斯特经济与计划学院马克思列宁主义教授。
    二、鲁登科，现任罗马尼亚人民共和国驻中华人民共和国大使。
    三、奥雷尔·巴伦加，作家，其著名作品中有剧本“败类”（已译成中文），现任罗马尼亚作家协会戏剧部秘书、布加勒斯特国家戏院秘书、布加勒斯特市人民委员会委员，曾任“自由罗马尼亚”编辑。
