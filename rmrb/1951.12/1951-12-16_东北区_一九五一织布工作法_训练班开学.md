Title: Northeast Region "1951 Weaving Work Method" Training Class Opens
Author: Wei Wu
Date: 1951-12-16
Page: 2

    Northeast Region "1951 Weaving Work Method" Training Class Opens
    The Northeast Textile Administration Bureau and the Northeast Regional Committee of the China Textile Trade Union have established a "1951 Weaving Work Method" training class at the Wafangdian Textile Factory. The training class officially started on December 7th. Worker representatives such as Dong Wenli, who participated in the National Weaving Work Method Conference, and eight others, are serving as instructors for the training class. The trainees include sixty-nine people in total, including weaving workshop directors, technicians, advanced producers, and model workers from nine state-owned cotton textile factories in Northeast China. The training period is expected to be ten days. The learning method will focus on workshop practice, supplemented by lectures, discussions, and demonstrations. In order to ensure the completion of the learning tasks, the trainees have already formulated a patriotic learning pact.
                                    (Wei Wu)

### 东北区“一九五一织布工作法”训练班开学
唯物
1951-12-16
第2版()
专栏：

    东北区“一九五一织布工作法”训练班开学
    东北纺织管理局和中国纺织工会东北区委员会在瓦房店纺织厂举办了“一九五一织布工作法”训练班。十二月七日训练班已正式开课。参加了全国织布工作法会议的工人代表董文礼等九人担任训练班的教员。参加学习的学员有东北九个国营棉纺织厂织布车间主任、技术员、先进生产者和劳动模范等共六十九人。学习期间预计十天。学习方法以车间实习为主，辅以讲课、讨论、表演等。学员们为了保证完成学习任务，已经订出了爱国学习公约。
                                    （唯物）
