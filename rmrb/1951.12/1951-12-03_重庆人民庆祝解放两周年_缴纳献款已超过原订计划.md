Title: Chongqing People Celebrate the Second Anniversary of Liberation; Donation Exceeds Original Plan
Author:
Date: 1951-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, December 2nd: The 30th of November marked the second anniversary of the liberation of Chongqing City. To celebrate their liberation, the people of the city have been enthusiastically donating funds in recent days. Currently, the total amount of donations has reached 69.027799 billion yuan, enough to buy 46 fighter planes, exceeding the original plan to donate 39 fighter planes.

On the evening of November 30th, more than 800 representatives from all walks of life in Chongqing held a celebration meeting. Cao Diqiu, the Mayor of Chongqing Municipal People's Government, reported on the achievements of Chongqing's construction in various fields over the past two years. He said: Over the past two years, the social landscape and people's thinking in Chongqing have undergone fundamental changes. 85% of the city's population has signed patriotic pacts; due to the resolute suppression of counter-revolutionaries, the Kuomintang bandit's spy organizations have suffered heavy blows, and social order has been further stabilized; land reform has been carried out in the suburbs, and hundreds of thousands of farmers have been liberated, and people's democratic power at all levels has been further consolidated. In terms of economic construction, the city's industry and commerce have increased by 12,492 households in the past two years; the output of steel, electricity, cement, cotton cloth, and other industrial products has exceeded the highest levels during the Anti-Japanese War. There has also been significant development in cultural education and municipal construction, with more than 300 spare-time schools in factories alone, and more than 320,000 citizens regularly participating in social education classes.

### 重庆人民庆祝解放两周年  缴纳献款已超过原订计划

1951-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    重庆人民庆祝解放两周年
    缴纳献款已超过原订计划
    【新华社重庆二日电】十一月三十日是重庆市解放两周年纪念日。全市人民为庆祝自己的解放，连日来踊跃缴纳献款。现在，缴款总数已达六百九十亿二千七百九十九万元，可买战斗机四十六架，超过了原订捐献战斗机三十九架的计划。
    在十一月三十日晚上，重庆市各界人民代表八百多人举行了庆祝大会。重庆市人民政府市长曹荻秋在会上报告了两年来重庆市各方面建设的成就。他说：两年来，重庆的社会面貌和人民思想已起了根本的变化。全市已有百分之八十五的人口订了爱国公约；由于坚决镇压了反革命分子，国民党匪帮的特务组织已遭到了沉重的打击，社会秩序已进一步安定；郊区进行了土地改革，数十万农民已得到翻身，各级人民民主政权已进一步巩固。在经济建设方面，两年来全市工商业增加了一万二千四百九十二户；工业生产中钢铁、电力、水泥、棉布等产量，都已超过抗日战争时期的最高水平。在文化教育、市政建设等方面，也有了显著的发展，单是工厂的业余学校就有三百多所，经常参加社教班学习的市民在三十二万人以上。
