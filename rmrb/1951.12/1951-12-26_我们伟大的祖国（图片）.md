    Our Great Motherland
    Bananas, one of Guangdong's specialties, are world-famous. They are most abundantly produced in Zhongshan, Dongguan, Panyu, Shunde, Zengcheng and other counties in Guangdong. This is a display of bananas in the fruit and vegetable hall of the South China Native and Special Products Exchange Exhibition.
