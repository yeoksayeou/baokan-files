Title: Worker Yang Difan and Middle School Teacher Yu Keying Cooperatively Produce Gold Paint for Porcelain
Author: Du Zhihui
Date: 1951-12-26
Page: 6

    Worker Yang Difan and Middle School Teacher Yu Keying
    Cooperatively Produce Gold Paint for Porcelain
    To the Editor: Yang Difan, a porcelain worker from Liling, Hunan, and Yu Keying, a teacher at the First Middle School, have produced gold paint for porcelain through a combination of experience and theory. China has never been able to manufacture gold paint for porcelain decoration, and in the past, we have always used foreign goods, which are very expensive. Porcelain worker Yang Difan has been researching the manufacture of gold paint for more than ten years, but because he lacked theoretical knowledge, he always failed. After the launch of the Resist America, Aid Korea movement, he resolved to manufacture the gold paint himself and stop buying foreign goods. He looked everywhere for someone with chemical theory knowledge to cooperate with. Coincidentally, Yu Keying, a chemistry teacher at the local First Middle School, was also researching the production of gold paint, but he lacked experience. So, the two of them cooperated. They borrowed instruments and some reagents from the school and began experimenting. After three months of research, they finally succeeded. The manufactured gold paint is bright in color, durable, and of the same quality as foreign goods. Now, the Liling Porcelain Enterprise Company is preparing to manufacture a large quantity of gold paint to supply the needs of the entire country.
                                    Du Zhihui

### 工人杨涤藩和中学教员喻科盈  共同合作制成绘瓷金水
都智辉
1951-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

    工人杨涤藩和中学教员喻科盈
    共同合作制成绘瓷金水
    编辑同志：湖南醴陵瓷器工人杨涤藩和第一中学教员喻科盈，在经验和理论相结合的基础上，制成了绘瓷金水。瓷器上绘图用的金水，我国向来不会制造，过去一直采用外货，价值十分昂贵。瓷器工人杨涤藩曾研究制造金水十多年，因为没有理论知识，都失败了。抗美援朝运动开展后，他下决心要把金水制造出来，不再买外货。他到处找懂得化学理论的人合作。恰巧，当地第一中学化学教员喻科盈，也在研究制金水，但他缺乏经验。于是，他们两人就合作了。他们借用了学校中的仪器和一部分药品，开始作实验。经过三个月的研究，终于试验成功了。制造出来的金水，色泽鲜艳，经久耐用，品质和外货相同。现在，醴陵瓷业企业公司正准备制造大量金水，供应全国需要。
                                    都智辉
