Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-12-02

Learning from advanced experience is of great significance to the various construction undertakings of the new China. Su Changyou, a bricklayer team leader and labor model from the Harbin Engineering Office of the Northeast Industrial Department's Construction Engineering Company, learned from the advanced experience of Soviet construction engineering and created the segmented continuous bricklaying method, which improved labor efficiency and quality. This is a depiction of Su Changyou implementing the segmented continuous bricklaying method.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    学习先进经验，对于新中国的各种建设事业都是有重大意义的。东北工业部建设工程公司哈尔滨工程处的瓦工班长、劳动模范苏长有，学习苏联建筑工程的先进经验，创造了分段连续砌砖法，提高了劳动的效率和质量。这是苏长有实行分段连续砌砖法的情形。
