Title: Soviet Pilots and Model Airplane Designers Continuously Set New World Records Astronomers Discover Plants on Mars
Author:
Date: 1951-12-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 24th: Soviet pilots and model airplane designers have set new world records. According to TASS news agency: The International Aeronautical Federation has recently recognized the new world records set by Soviet amateur pilot Golovanov and Soviet glider pilot Samosadova. Soviet Central Aviation Club pilot Golovanov, on August 26th, flying a Yak-11 racing aircraft at an average speed of 442.289 kilometers per hour along the Tushino-Smolensk-Orel-Tushino route, set a world speed record for light aircraft weighing 1,750 to 3,000 kilograms over a distance of 1,000 kilometers. On July 20th this year, Samosadova, piloting an A-9 glider designed by Antonov, took off from Serpukhov and flew 364 kilometers along a predetermined route, landing near Tambov, breaking the world record for long-distance glider flight in the women's category, previously set by Soviet female gliding athlete Prokhorova in 1940.
    The Soviet Union has also set many world records in model airplane design. On July 22nd, a model airplane with a mechanical engine designed by Lyubushkin flew 356 kilometers and 794 meters at an altitude of 4,600 meters, exceeding the official world record set by Soviet model airplane maker Malik by 146 kilometers and 146 meters. In the same month, Bedukhov's model airplane flew in the air for five hours and ten minutes, exceeding the previous record by more than one hour. The "Flying Wing" seaplane model with a mechanical engine designed by Ivanov, flew in the air for 33 minutes and 5 seconds, breaking the official world record for that class of model.

    Xinhua News Agency, December 24th: According to TASS Moscow: Soviet renowned astronomer Professor Tikhov recently published a research report at the Soviet Academy of Sciences. Based on his research conducted at the Kazakh Academy of Sciences, he believes that even though conditions on other planets are different from Earth, organic life may still exist. While photographing Mars, Tikhov discovered that certain parts of Mars are covered with plants. To determine what kind of plants exist on Mars, Professor Tikhov studied many types of plants that grow in high latitudes and near the Arctic, because the natural conditions in these regions are quite similar to the natural conditions on Mars. Professor Tikhov compared the results of this research with the spectrographic photographs of Martian plants and determined that the spectral characteristics of Martian plants are similar to those of plants growing in high latitudes on Earth. He also determined that when the temperature rises, plants begin to emit infrared radiation, which allows them to release excess heat. This characteristic of plants allows us to assume that there are also plants on Venus, because the surface temperature of Venus reaches 80 degrees Celsius. Professor Tikhov said: The Kazakh Academy of Sciences is conducting large-scale research on the question of organic life on other planets. Kazakhstan will build an observatory dedicated to the study of planets in 1952.

### 苏飞行员和飞机模型设计家  连续创造世界新纪录  天文学家发现火星上有植物

1951-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏飞行员和飞机模型设计家
    连续创造世界新纪录
    天文学家发现火星上有植物 【新华社二十四日讯】苏联飞行员和飞机模型设计家又创造世界新纪录。据塔斯社消息：国际航空协会最近已承认苏联业余飞行员戈洛万诺夫和苏联滑翔机驾驶员萨摩萨多娃所创的世界新纪录。苏联中央航空俱乐部驾驶员戈洛万诺夫在八月二十六日乘雅克—十一式竞技飞机以平均每小时四四二点二八九公里的速度沿土希诺—斯摩棱斯克—奥勒尔—土希诺航线飞行，创造了飞行重量为一千七百五十到三千公斤的轻型飞机飞行一千公里的世界速度纪录。今年七月二十日，萨摩萨多娃驾驶安东诺夫所设计的A—九式滑翔机，从塞尔普霍夫起飞沿预定航线飞行三百六十四公里至坦波夫附近着陆，打破了苏联女滑翔运动家普罗霍罗娃在一九四零年创造的女子组长距离滑翔机飞行的世界纪录。
    苏联在飞机模型设计方面也创造了许多世界纪录。七月二十二日，留布什金所设计的装有机械引擎的飞机模型，在四千六百公尺的高空中飞行了三百五十六公里又七百九十四公尺，超过了苏联飞机模型制造者马利克所创的正式世界纪录一百四十六公里又一百四十六公尺。同月，贝都霍夫的飞机模型在空中飞行了五小时十分钟，超过以前的纪录一小时以上。伊万诺夫所设计的装有机械的“飞翼”式水上飞机模型，在空中飞行了三十三分五秒的时间，打破了该级模型的正式世界纪录。
    【新华社二十四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联著名天文学家吉霍夫教授最近在苏联科学院发表研究报告。根据他在哈萨克科学院所进行的研究，认为其他的行星上条件虽和地球不同，也可能有有机生命存在。吉霍夫在摄制火星的照片时，发现火星的某些部分覆盖着植物。为了决定火星上究竟存在着何种植物，吉霍夫教授研究了很多种生长在高纬度和北极附近地区的植物，因为这些地区的自然条件跟火星上的自然条件颇为相似。吉霍夫教授把这种研究的结果跟火星植物的分光照片作一比较，确定了火星上植物的光谱特征跟地球高纬度上生长的植物的光谱特征相似。他同时确定：在温度上升的时候，植物开始放出红外线，这种红外线使它们可能发出过剩的热。植物的这种特性使我们可以假定金星上也有植物，因为金星表面的温度达到零上八十度。吉霍夫教授说：哈萨克科学院正在进行大规模的研究工作，研究其他行星上有机生命的问题。哈萨克将在一九五二年建筑一个专门研究行星的观象台。
