Title: Results of the Fifth Day of the National Football Competition
Author: 
Date: 1951-12-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 6th. The results of the four matches on the fifth day of the 1951 National Football Competition are as follows:
	East China vs. Southwest	6-0	East China wins
	Northeast vs. Central South	9-0	Northeast wins
	Railways vs. Northwest	8-0	Railways wins
	People's Liberation Army vs. North China	2-2	Draw
