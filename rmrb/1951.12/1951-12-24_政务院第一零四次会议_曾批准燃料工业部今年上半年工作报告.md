Title: The 104th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approved the Work Report of the Ministry of Fuel Industry for the First Half of This Year
Author:
Date: 1951-12-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 28th -- The 104th session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 28th. The meeting listened to and approved the report on the work of the Ministry of Fuel Industry in the first half of 1951 made by Minister Chen Yu of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government. The meeting also approved several cases of appointments and removals, as well as cases of appointments to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval. (This news was reissued by Xinhua News Agency on December 22nd)

### 政务院第一零四次会议  曾批准燃料工业部今年上半年工作报告

1951-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院第一零四次会议
    曾批准燃料工业部今年上半年工作报告
    【新华社九月二十八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百零四次政务会议于二十八日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府燃料工业部陈郁部长所作的该部一九五一年上半年工作的报告。会议并通过了任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。（这个消息是新华社在十二月二十二日补发的）
