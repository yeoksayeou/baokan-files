Title: China Peace Committee Holds Meeting to Welcome Delegation Back from World Peace Council; Condemns US Aggression
Author:
Date: 1951-12-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 7th—The China Peace Committee held a meeting on the 7th to welcome the return of the Chinese delegation that attended the World Peace Council meeting in Vienna. Those present included Guo Moruo, head of the delegation, council members Mao Dun, Cai Tingkai, Wu Yaozong, Mei Ru'ao, invited representative Wu Juenong, Secretary-General Liu Guanyi, and all staff members; Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, vice-chairmen of the China Peace Committee; as well as Liu Ningyi, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Wu Maosun, Cui Yueli, Liao Gailong, Tan Tiwu, Meng Lengya, and Xie Bangding. Peng Zhen delivered a welcome speech, saying that the resolutions passed by the World Peace Council at the Vienna meeting are of great significance to the Chinese people's struggle to resist US aggression and aid Korea and to defend world peace. The China Peace Committee passed a resolution on November 16th expressing its support. He expressed his gratitude to the delegation for successfully completing its mission on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Volunteers. Guo Moruo gave a detailed report at the meeting on the achievements of the World Peace Council's meeting in Vienna and the participation of the Chinese delegation. Mao Dun, Wu Yaozong, and Wu Juenong also made some supplementary remarks. The meeting finally decided that the general office of the China Peace Committee and the Beijing branch would convene a meeting of cadres in the coming days, to be addressed by Guo Moruo.

### 中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会举行会议  欢迎出席世界和平理事会代表团回国

1951-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会举行会议
    欢迎出席世界和平理事会代表团回国
    【新华社七日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会在七日举行会议，欢迎出席世界和平理事会维也纳会议的我国代表团回国。出席的有代表团团长郭沫若，理事茅盾、蔡廷锴、吴耀宗、梅汝璈、邀请代表吴觉农，秘书长刘贯一及全体工作人员，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副主席彭真、陈叔通，以及刘宁一、许德珩、许宝驹、吴茂荪、崔月犁、廖盖隆、谭惕吾、孟棱崖、谢邦定等人。彭真致欢迎词说：世界和平理事会维也纳会议通过的各项决议，对于中国人民抗美援朝的斗争和保卫世界和平都有很大的意义。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会已于十一月十六日通过决议表示拥护。他对于代表团代表中国人民及中国人民志愿军胜利完成任务表示感谢。郭沫若在会上详细地报告了世界和平理事会此次在维也纳会议中的成就及我国代表团参加会议的情形。茅盾、吴耀宗、吴觉农又分别作了若干补充。会议最后决定日内由中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会总会和北京市分会召集干部会，由郭沫若传达。
