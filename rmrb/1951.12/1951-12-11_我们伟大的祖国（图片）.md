Author:
Date: 1951-12-11

    Our Great Motherland
    The engineering corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have mastered new technologies and are gradually becoming regularized. This is a scene of engineers practicing coordinated operations. They are pushing howitzers onto "pontoon bridges" made of boats to cross to the other side of the river.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民解放军的工兵部队，已经掌握了新的技术，正在逐渐地走向正规化。这是工兵在演习协同作战的情形。他们正把榴弹炮推上用船只构成的“门桥”，渡到河的对岸去。
