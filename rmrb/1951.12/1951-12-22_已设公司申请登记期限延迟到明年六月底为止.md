Title: Application Deadline for Registration of Established Companies Extended to End of June Next Year
Author:
Date: 1951-12-22
Page: 2
  
    The provision in Article 102, Paragraph 1 of the "Provisional Regulations on Private Enterprises Implementing Measures," which stipulates that the deadline for companies already established before the implementation of the measures to apply for registration should not be later than the end of 1951, has been extended to the end of June 1952 by the decision of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 已设公司申请登记期限延迟到明年六月底为止

1951-12-22
第2版()
专栏：

    已设公司申请登记期限延迟到明年六月底为止
    “私营企业暂行条例施行办法”第一百○二条第一项所规定，在该办法施行前，业经设立的公司申请登记期限，不得迟于一九五一年年底，这项规定经政务院财政经济委员会决定，延至一九五二年六月底为止。（新华社）
