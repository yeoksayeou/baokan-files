Title: British Communist Party Chairman William Gallacher's Seventieth Birthday, Chairman Mao Zedong Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1951-12-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 24th: On December 25th, the Chairman of the British Communist Party, William Gallacher, celebrates his seventieth birthday. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram of congratulations. The telegram reads as follows: Dear Comrade William Gallacher: On the occasion of your seventieth birthday, I, on behalf of the Communist Party of China and myself, extend my heartfelt congratulations to you. And wish you good health.
Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Mao Zedong

### 英共主席威廉·加拉赫七十岁寿辰  毛泽东主席致电祝贺

1951-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    英共主席威廉·加拉赫七十岁寿辰
    毛泽东主席致电祝贺
    【新华社二十四日讯】十二月二十五日是英国共产党主席威廉·加拉赫七十岁寿辰，中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席特致电祝贺。电文如下：亲爱的威廉·加拉赫同志：欣逢您七十寿辰之际，我谨代表中国共产党和我个人，向您致衷心的祝贺。并祝您的身体健康。中国共产党中央委员会主席    毛泽东
