Title: Indian Government Foreign Ministry Hosts Banquet to See Off Chinese Cultural Delegation; Gao Er and Ding Xilin Give Speeches Congratulating the Friendship Between the Chinese and Indian People; Bombay India-China Friendship Association and Other Groups Welcome Members of the Indian Goodwill Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1951-12-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 2nd: After visiting South India, the Chinese Cultural Delegation returned to New Delhi from Madras on November 28th. The Indian Government's Ministry of Foreign Affairs held a grand banquet that evening to see off the Chinese Cultural Delegation. The banquet was hosted by Gao Er, the Indian Government's Minister of Health and Minister of Transportation.

Those attending the banquet included: Menon, Foreign Secretary of the Indian Government's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chopra, Director of Protocol of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other government officials, Saifuddin, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, as well as Bahadur Muhammad, the High Commissioner of Pakistan to India, and more than fifty others. General Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, was also invited to attend.

Gao Er, on behalf of the Indian government, proposed a toast to Chairman Mao Zedong, the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and the Chinese people. Ding Xilin, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, proposed a toast to President Prasad of the Republic of India, the Government of the Republic of India, and the Indian people.

Gao Er said in her speech: "I hope and believe that this is only the first of a series of mutual visits between the people of your great country and the people of our country in the future. The unity between the Chinese and Indian people is unbreakable. This unity has a long history, dating back to ancient times. This ancient past is what we and the people of your great country are proud of, and rightly proud of."

She said: "Now China has stood up. It stands up not only to create its own destiny according to the will of the Chinese people, but also to help bring a better order and a better atmosphere to this world."

Gao Er emphasized: "We must always ensure that we ourselves, as we have done in the past, strive for Sino-Indian friendship."

Ding Xilin, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, then delivered a speech at the meeting, bidding farewell to the Indian government and the Indian people and expressing his gratitude.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere from beginning to end.

On the morning of November 29th, the Chinese Cultural Delegation bid farewell to New Delhi and flew to Benares for a visit. The delegation will also visit Patna and Calcutta along the way, and then fly from Calcutta to Burma for a visit.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 2nd: Bombay news: The Bombay India-China Friendship Association and the All-India Peace Council jointly held a welcome meeting on the afternoon of November 28th to welcome the four members of the Indian Goodwill Delegation to China who recently returned from visiting the People's Republic of China: Karangi, editor-in-chief of the "Lightning" Weekly, Anand, a famous Indian writer and novelist, Abbas, a famous columnist, and Hoot Singh, a journalist.

The four members of the delegation detailed the amazing achievements of the people of New China in all aspects at the meeting. They pointed out that the Chinese people are not only engaged in the work of rebuilding their own country, but also fully aware of their responsibility to establish and maintain world peace. In their speeches, they unanimously expressed unlimited confidence in the friendly cooperation between the Indian and Chinese people.

Abraham, a famous Indian film actor who presided over the welcome meeting, said that the rapid changes that have taken place in China after defeating feudalism and imperialism have amazed the whole world.

At the welcome meeting, students from the local overseas Chinese school performed Yangko dances and sang songs from New China. The choir of the Indian People's Theater Association sang songs specially composed for the welcome meeting.

### 印度政府外交部设宴欢送我文化代表团  高尔和丁西林致词祝贺中印两国人民间的友谊  孟买印中友协等团体欢迎印度亲善访华团团员

1951-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    印度政府外交部设宴欢送我文化代表团
    高尔和丁西林致词祝贺中印两国人民间的友谊
    孟买印中友协等团体欢迎印度亲善访华团团员
    【本报讯】据新华社新德里二日电：中国文化代表团在访问南印度之后，于十一月二十八日从马德拉斯回到新德里。印度政府外交部在当天晚上举行盛大宴会，欢送中国文化代表团。宴会由印度政府卫生部长兼交通部长高尔主持。
    参加宴会的有：印度政府外交部外交秘书梅农、外交部交际司司长乔普拉等政府官员，全印和平理事会主席赛福丁以及巴基斯坦驻印度高级专员巴哈杜·穆罕默德等五十多人。中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤将军也应邀出席。
    高尔代表印度政府提议为毛泽东主席、中华人民共和国中央人民政府和中国人民干杯。中国文化代表团团长丁西林提议为印度共和国总统普拉萨德、印度共和国政府和印度人民干杯。
    高尔在会上致词说：“我希望并且相信，这次仅是伟大的贵国人民与我国人民间未来一系列的互相访问的第一次。中印两国人民间的团结是牢不可破的。这种团结由来已久，远在古代就有了。这个古老的过去是我们和伟大的贵国人民所引为自豪的，也是理当引为自豪的。”
    她说：“现在中国已经站起来了。它站起来，不仅根据中国人民自己的意志来创造他们自己的命运，并且有助于为这个世界带来更好的秩序和更好的气氛。”
    高尔强调说：“我们必须经常保证我们自己像过去所做的那样，为中印友谊而努力。”
    中国文化代表团团长丁西林接着在会上发表谈话，向印度政府和印度人民告别并表示谢意。
    宴会自始至终在友好气氛中进行。
    十一月二十九日晨，中国文化代表团告别新德里，飞往贝拿勒斯访问。代表团沿途并将访问巴特那和加尔各答等地，然后从加尔各答乘飞机到缅甸进行访问。
    【新华社新德里二日电】孟买消息：孟买印中友好协会和全印和平理事会在十一月二十八日下午联合举行欢迎会，欢迎最近访问中华人民共和国后归来的印度亲善访华团的四位团员：“闪电”周刊主编卡朗吉、著名印度作家和小说家安纳德、著名专栏作家阿巴斯及新闻工作者胡特·辛格。
    访华团的四位团员在会上详述新中国人民在各方面的惊人成就。他们指出：中国人民不但正在从事重建自己的国家的工作，并完全知道自己有建立和维护世界和平的责任。他们在演讲中一致对印中人民的友好合作表示无限信心。
    主持这个欢迎会的著名印度电影演员亚伯拉罕说：中国在打败封建主义和帝国主义势力后所发生的迅速的变化，已使全世界惊羡不置。
    在欢迎会上，当地的华侨学校学生表演秧歌舞并演唱新中国的歌曲。印度人民戏剧协会的歌咏队演唱了特别为这次欢迎会而编的歌曲。
