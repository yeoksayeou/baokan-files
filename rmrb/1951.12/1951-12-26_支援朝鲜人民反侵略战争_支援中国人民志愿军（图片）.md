Title: Support the Korean People's War Against Aggression, Support the Chinese People's Volunteers (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-12-26
Page: 5

    Supporting the Korean people's war against aggression and supporting the Chinese People's Volunteers
    The outstanding commanders of the Chinese People's Volunteers are directing the war on the Korean front day and night, overcoming all difficulties, and achieving countless brilliant victories.
    On October 25, 1950, the Chinese people, in order to safeguard world peace, defend the security of the motherland, and help the Korean people in their struggle against aggression, enthusiastically joined the Volunteers. They accepted the entrustment and expectations of the people of the motherland and bravely crossed the Yalu River.
    The Chinese and Korean People's forces marched side by side to strike at the enemy.
    The Chinese and Korean People's forces fought together in the snow to eliminate the enemy.
    Brave Chinese People's Volunteer soldiers, disregarding their own safety, rescued a Korean child from a burning house that had been bombed by enemy planes, handed him over to his mother, and are taking off their cotton clothes to keep the child warm.
    In the battle to liberate Pyongyang, the Chinese and Korean People's forces bravely crossed the river and seized the ferry crossing.
    In the victorious battles, the Chinese and Korean People's forces continuously captured large numbers of prisoners and escorted them to the rear.
    The Chinese and Korean People's forces seized a large number of spoils of war on the battlefield.
    Countless Chinese laborers who volunteered to support the Korean front carried bags of flour to the front line.
    In order to support the Korean front, the Chinese people are widely launching a production increase and donation campaign, and people of all ethnic groups in the remote Northwest Xinjiang Province are also enthusiastically responding to this campaign.
    This is an enemy tank destroyed by the fierce artillery fire of the Chinese and Korean People's forces.
    After more than a year of fighting, the strength of the Chinese and Korean People's forces has grown stronger with each battle.
    The powerful forces of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army are continuing to give the frenzied enemy a serious blow.
    Chinese and Korean motorized units are steadily heading to the front line to eliminate more enemies.
    Powerful Chinese and Korean tank units are victoriously attacking enemy positions.
    Issue 151 This issue's pictorial photos are selected from the first documentary film "Resist America, Aid Korea"

### 支援朝鲜人民反侵略战争  支援中国人民志愿军（图片）

1951-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

    支援朝鲜人民反侵略战争  支援中国人民志愿军
    优秀的中国人民志愿军的指挥员们，日日夜夜地在指挥着朝鲜前线的战事，克服了一切困难，取得了无数次辉煌的胜利。
    一九五○年十月二十五日，中国人民为了保卫世界和平、保卫祖国安全和帮助朝鲜人民进行反侵略的斗争，踊跃地参加了志愿军。他们接受了祖国人民的嘱咐和期望，英勇地踏过了鸭绿江。
    中朝人民部队并肩进军，打击敌人。
    中朝人民部队在雪地里共同作战，消灭敌人。
    勇敢的中国人民志愿军战士，从被敌机滥炸后燃烧着的房子里，奋不顾身地抢救出了朝鲜小孩子，把他交给他的母亲，并且正在脱下自己的棉衣，给孩子御寒。
    中朝人民部队在解放平壤战役中，奋勇地越过河流，抢夺渡口。
    中朝人民部队在胜利的战斗中，不断捉到大批的俘虏，把他们押送到后方去。
    中朝人民部队在战场上缴获了大批的战利品。
    无数志愿支援朝鲜前线的中国民工们，把一袋一袋的面粉背到前线去。
    中国人民为了支援朝鲜前线，正广泛地展开增产捐献运动，远在西北新疆省的各民族人民也热烈响应这个运动。
    这是在中朝人民部队猛烈炮火的攻击下，被摧毁了的敌人坦克。
    经过一年多的战斗，中朝人民部队的力量是愈战愈强了。
    中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军强大的力量，正继续给予疯狂的敌人以严重打击。
    中朝摩托化部队源源驶向前线，去消灭更多的敌人。
    强大的中朝坦克部队正向敌人阵地胜利进攻。
    第一五一期本期画刊照片选自“抗美援朝”第一部文献纪录影片
