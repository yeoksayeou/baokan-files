Title: Six Years Anniversary of the Founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation; China's Women's Federation Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author:

Date: 1951-12-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: The sixth anniversary of the founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation is December 1st. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation has sent a telegram to the Women's International Democratic Federation to express congratulations. The telegram reads as follows: Mrs. Gordon: Mrs. Gujjuri:

	On behalf of all Chinese women, we warmly congratulate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation. Over the past six years, the Women's International Democratic Federation has bravely led women all over the world to unremittingly struggle for the great cause of defending world peace and has achieved brilliant results. We Chinese women will unite even more closely with women all over the world under the banner of the Women's International Democratic Federation, and strive to defend peace and protect the rights of women and children.

	Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Cai Chang
			December 1st

### 国际民主妇联成立六周年　我国妇联致电祝贺

1951-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联成立六周年
　　我国妇联致电祝贺
     【新华社一日讯】十二月一日是国际民主妇女联合会成立六周年纪念日，中华全国民主妇女联合会特致电国际民主妇女联合会，表示祝贺。电文如下：戈登夫人：古久里夫人：
    我们谨代表全中国妇女热烈祝贺国际民主妇联成立六周年纪念日。六年来国际民主妇联英勇地领导全世界妇女为保卫世界和平的伟大事业而不懈地奋斗，获得了辉煌的成绩。我们中国妇女今后将和全世界妇女更加紧密地团结在国际民主妇联的旗帜下，为保卫和平，为保障妇女儿童的权利而奋斗。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会主席    蔡畅
                        十二月一日
