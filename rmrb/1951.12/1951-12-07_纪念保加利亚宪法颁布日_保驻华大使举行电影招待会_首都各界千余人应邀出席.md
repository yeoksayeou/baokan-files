Title: Bulgarian Ambassador to China Hosts Film Reception to Commemorate Bulgarian Constitution Day; Over a Thousand People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1951-12-07
Page: 1
　
    Xinhua News Agency: To commemorate the anniversary of the promulgation of the Constitution of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, Petkov, hosted a reception at the Capital Cinema at 8:30 PM on the afternoon of the 6th, featuring the Bulgarian film "Alarm," to entertain people from all walks of life in the capital.
    Those invited to attend included Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court, Zhang Xiruo, Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and over a thousand people from all walks of life.
    Members of foreign embassies in China were also invited to attend.

### 纪念保加利亚宪法颁布日  保驻华大使举行电影招待会  首都各界千余人应邀出席

1951-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念保加利亚宪法颁布日
    保驻华大使举行电影招待会
    首都各界千余人应邀出席
    【新华社讯】保加利亚人民共和国驻我国大使彼得科夫，为纪念保加利亚人民共和国宪法颁布日，特于六日下午八时半在首都电影院放映保加利亚故事片“警报”，招待首都各界人士。
    应邀出席的有最高人民法院院长沈钧儒、政务院政治法律委员会副主任张奚若、外交部副部长伍修权，以及各界人士一千余人。
    各国驻华使馆人员也应邀出席。
