    Our Great Motherland
    The Mongolian people are skilled at lassoing horses, which is one way they manage their horses. When grazing, they allow the horses to roam freely in the pasture. When they need a horse, they lasso it back. These two Inner Mongolian women are holding lasso poles and are about to lasso a horse.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    蒙古族人民都有熟练的套马本领，这是他们看管马匹的一种办法。他们在放牧时让马匹自由地到草地上去吃草，到需要用马的时候，就将它套了回来。这两位内蒙古妇女拿着套马杆正准备去套马。
