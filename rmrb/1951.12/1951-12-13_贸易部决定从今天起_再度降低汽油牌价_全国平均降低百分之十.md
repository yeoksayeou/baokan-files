Title: Ministry of Trade Decides to Reduce Gasoline Prices Again from Today; National Average Reduction of Ten Percent
Author:
Date: 1951-12-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: In order to reduce the costs of industry and transportation, the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government has decided to reduce gasoline prices again starting from December 13th, with a national average reduction of ten percent. The price of gasoline in Tianjin will decrease from 2,112,000 yuan per bulk barrel to 1,901,000 yuan; in Shanghai, from 2,169,000 yuan to 1,958,000 yuan; and in Hankou, from 2,211,000 yuan to 2,000,000 yuan.

### 贸易部决定从今天起  再度降低汽油牌价  全国平均降低百分之十

1951-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    贸易部决定从今天起
    再度降低汽油牌价
    全国平均降低百分之十
    【新华社十二日讯】中央人民政府贸易部为减低工业和运输业成本，决定自本月十三日起再度降低汽油牌价，全国平均降低百分之十。计天津汽油每散桶由二、一一二、○○○元降至一、九○一、○○○元；上海由二、一六九、○○○元降至一、九五八、○○○元；汉口由二、二一一、○○○元降至二、○○○、○○○元。
