Title: Mao Zedong's "On Practice" Published in Korean

Author:
Date: 1951-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 13th: The Korean Workers' Party Central Publishing House recently published a special collection combining the Korean translation of Chairman Mao Zedong's "On Practice" and the Korean translation of "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship." It has been warmly welcomed by cadres of Korean party and government organs and people from all walks of life. The first edition of 10,000 copies of this book sold out within half a month.

### 毛主席的“实践论”已译成朝鲜文出版

1951-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席的“实践论”已译成朝鲜文出版
    【新华社平壤十三日电】朝鲜劳动党中央出版社最近已将毛泽东主席所著的“实践论”的朝鲜文译本和“论人民民主专政”的朝鲜文译本合并印成专集发行，受到朝鲜党政机关干部和各界人民的热烈欢迎。这本书的第一版一万部，已在半个月内销售一空。
