Title: With Conclusive and Irrefutable Evidence, Ridgway Admits US Aircraft Intruded into Kaesong Neutral Zone
Author:
Date: 1951-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 13th—Due to conclusive and irrefutable evidence, the US side admitted to our side in a letter on the 12th the responsibility for the incident of the US Air Force aircraft violating the agreement on the Kaesong neutral zone and intruding into the Kaesong neutral zone on the morning of the 11th. The US letter is as follows: "To Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army Kim Il-sung, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Peng Dehuai: The investigation results of the air raid on Riryong-ni in the Kaesong area on the morning of December 11 indicate that the air raid was carried out by a United Nations Command aircraft due to negligence in violating current instructions. It has been noted that the attack on the area caused by the pilot's error did not result in any personal injury or material loss. Therefore, the United Nations Command accepts responsibility for violating the agreement on the Kaesong area and is taking appropriate disciplinary action. Commander-in-Chief of the United Nations Command, General of the Army of the United States, Ridgway."

### 由于发证据确凿、无可抵赖  李奇微承认美机侵袭开城中立区罪行

1951-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　由于发证据确凿、无可抵赖
　　李奇微承认美机侵袭开城中立区罪行
　　【新华社开城十三日电】由于证据确凿，无可抵赖，美方已于十二日以函件向我方承认十一日清晨美国空军飞机破坏双方关于开城中立区的协议、侵袭开城中立区事件的责任。美方的函件如下：“朝鲜人民军金日成最高司令官：中国人民志愿军彭德怀司令员：据十二月十一日清晨空袭开城地区丽陵里的调查结果表示，此次空袭系由一架联合国军飞机因疏忽违反了现行指令而进行的。已注意到由于驾驶员错误所产生的对该区的进攻并未造成人员的受伤或物资的损失。因此联合国军接受违反有关开城地区协议的责任，并正采取适当的纪律行动。联合国军总司令美国陆军上将李奇微。”
