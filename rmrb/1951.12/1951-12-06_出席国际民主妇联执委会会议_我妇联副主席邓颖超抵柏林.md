Title: Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Arrives in Berlin to Attend Executive Committee Meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation
Author:
Date: 1951-12-06
Page: 1

    Attending Executive Committee Meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation
    Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Arrives in Berlin

    【Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 5th】Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, arrived in Berlin by train on the 4th to attend the Executive Committee meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation, which is scheduled to open on the 7th. Among those who welcomed her at the station were Hilmer-Plesch, Vice-Chairman of the German Democratic Women's Federation, Mrs. Grotewohl, wife of the Prime Minister, and representatives from the secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation. Wang Yutian, Counselor of the Chinese Diplomatic Mission to Germany, also welcomed her at the station.

### 出席国际民主妇联执委会会议  我妇联副主席邓颖超抵柏林

1951-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    出席国际民主妇联执委会会议
    我妇联副主席邓颖超抵柏林
    【新华社柏林五日电】中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超已在四日乘车抵柏林，出席定于七日开幕的国际民主妇女联合会执行委员会会议。到车站欢迎的有德国民主妇女联合会副主席希尔默—普莱什尔、格罗提渥总理夫人、国际民主妇女联合会书记处的代表等人。我国驻德外交使团参赞王雨田也到车站欢迎。
