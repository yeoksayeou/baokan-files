Title: All Beijing Public Security Personnel Commended

Author:
Date: 1951-12-23
Page: 1

[Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing Municipal People's Government Committee and the Beijing Third All-Circles People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee commended all public security personnel in Beijing at a joint meeting held on the 21st. The full text of the letter of commendation is as follows:

Director Luo, Deputy Directors Feng and Zhang, and all comrades in public security work, all commanders and fighters of the Public Security Corps:

For the past two years, you have correctly and resolutely implemented the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference regarding the suppression of counter-revolutionaries, the Central People's Government's laws and instructions on the suppression of counter-revolutionaries, and the resolutions and instructions of the Beijing All-Circles People's Representative Conference and the Municipal People's Government on the suppression of counter-revolutionaries. You have severely suppressed counter-revolutionaries, consolidated the revolutionary order of the capital, safeguarded the safety of the lives and property of the people of the capital, and enabled the various construction projects of the capital to proceed smoothly. These extremely brilliant achievements are the result of your wholehearted service to the people and your arduous struggle. The people of the capital are very satisfied. Therefore, the Municipal People's Government Committee and the Third Joint Meeting of the Third All-Circles People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee have decided to commend and reward you. You deserve this commendation and reward, but in order to further consolidate the revolutionary order of the capital, it is necessary to continue to be vigilant and strengthen your work. We believe that you will be able to rely on the people of the city to overcome shortcomings in your work and promote strengths, and further eliminate all remnants of counter-revolutionaries cleanly, making the revolutionary order of the capital even more consolidated.

		Beijing Municipal People's Government Committee
		Beijing Third All-Circles People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee

### 北京市全体公安人员荣获表扬

1951-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市全体公安人员荣获表扬
    【北京市新闻处讯】北京市人民政府委员会、北京市第三届各界人民代表会议协商委员会，在二十一日举行的联席会议上，表扬了北京市全体公安工作人员。表扬信的全文如下：罗局长、冯、张副局长暨全体公安工作同志们、公安总队全体指战员同志们：
    两年来，你们正确地坚决执行了中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领关于镇压反革命的方针，中央人民政府关于镇压反革命的法令和指示，以及本市各界人民代表会议和市人民政府关于镇压反革命的决议和指示，严重地镇压了反革命，巩固了首都的革命秩序，保障了首都人民生命财产的安全，并使首都的各项建设能够顺利进行，这些极其辉煌的成就，都是你们全心全意为人民服务艰苦奋斗的结果。首都全体人民甚为满意，因此，市人民政府委员会和市第三届各界人民代表会议协商委员会第三次联席会议决议给予你们以表扬和奖励。你们受到这种表扬和奖励是应当的，但是为了进一步巩固首都的革命秩序，还需要继续地提高警惕，加强工作，我们相信：你们一定能够依靠全市人民克服工作中的缺点和表扬优点，进一步把一切反革命残余肃清得干干净净，使首都的革命秩序更加巩固。
        北京市人民政府委员会
        北京市第三届各界人民代表会议协商委员会
