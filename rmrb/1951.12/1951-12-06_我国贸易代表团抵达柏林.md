Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1951-12-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: Berlin dispatch: The Chinese trade delegation headed by Zhang Huadong arrived in Berlin on November 30th. Günter Gielmanns, State Secretary of the Ministry of Trade of the German Democratic Republic, representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and personnel from the Chinese diplomatic mission in the German Democratic Republic were at the station to welcome them.

### 我国贸易代表团抵达柏林

1951-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我国贸易代表团抵达柏林
    【新华社五日讯】柏林消息：由张化东率领的我国贸易代表团在十一月三十日抵达柏林。德意志民主共和国贸易部国务秘书甘特—吉尔曼斯、外交部代表和我国驻德意志民主共和国外交使团人员均曾赴车站欢迎。
