Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-12-29
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Chen Yongkang, a farmer from Changlou Township, Songjiang County, Southern Jiangsu, has 30 years of experience in rice cultivation methods. After liberation, under the education of patriotism, he worked even harder in production. This year, the highest yield of rice he planted reached 1,433 catties per mu. This is Chen Yongkang and his abundant rice harvest.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    苏南松江县长溇乡农民陈永康，在水稻耕作方法上积有三十年的经验，解放后在爱国主义的教育下，他更加努力生产。今年他所种的水稻产量最高的一亩达一千四百三十三斤，这是陈永康和他丰收的水稻。
