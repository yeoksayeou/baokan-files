Title: Discussing Sino-Czech Trade for Next Year: Our Country's Commercial Delegation Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1951-12-05
Page: 1

    Discussing Sino-Czech Trade for Next Year
    Our Country's Commercial Delegation Arrives in Prague
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th] The Chinese commercial delegation, led by Deputy Head Yi Qintang, arrived in Prague, the capital of the Czechoslovak Republic, on the 1st, to conduct negotiations with the Czechoslovak government regarding Sino-Czech trade for 1952. Those who welcomed them at the station included Junači, Minister of Light Industry of Czechoslovakia; Margolius and Sousek, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Trade; Reiner, Deputy Minister of Heavy Industry; and Karl Bišík, Director of the Chinese Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Tan Xilin, our ambassador to Czechoslovakia, also went to welcome them.

### 商谈明年中捷贸易  我国商务代表团抵布拉格

1951-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    商谈明年中捷贸易
    我国商务代表团抵布拉格
    【新华社布拉格四日电】我国赴捷商务代表团在副团长衣钦堂率领下，已于一日到达捷克斯洛伐克共和国首都布拉格，准备和捷克斯洛伐克政府进行关于一九五二年中捷贸易的商谈。到车站欢迎的有捷轻工业部部长裘那契、对外贸易部副部长玛尔果留斯、苏塞克、重工业部副部长雷纳、外交部中国司司长卡尔比西克等。我驻捷大使谭希林也前往欢迎。
