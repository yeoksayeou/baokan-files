Title: National Football Tournament Concludes; All-China Football Team for 1951 Selected; Northeast Team Wins Championship, East China Team Wins Second Place
Author:
Date: 1951-12-12
Page: 1

    National Football Tournament Concludes
    All-China Football Team for 1951 Selected
    Northeast Team Wins Championship, East China Team Wins Second Place
   [This newspaper dispatch] According to Xinhua News Agency: The 1951 National Football Tournament concluded on the afternoon of the 9th in Tianjin.

    Those attending the closing ceremony included Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation; Huang Jing, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the 1951 National Football Tournament; Zhou Shuta, Deputy Mayor of Tianjin; and responsible individuals from various agencies and people's organizations, as well as 18,000 spectators. International friends, including Soviet Consul General in Tianjin, Matveyev, were also invited to attend.

    Before the closing of the conference, Rong Gaotang delivered a closing speech. He called on all sports workers and athletes to strive for greater achievements in the people's sports cause.

    Finally, Huang Zhong, Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation, announced the list of thirty All-China football players for 1951 selected during this competition, including Wang Zhengwen, Gao Baozheng, and Fang Renqiu, as well as the results of this football tournament: the championship was won by the Northeast Team, and the second place was won by the East China Team. Ma John, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation, presented awards to the champions and runners-up respectively.

    This tournament opened on December 1st and closed on the 9th, lasting nine days. A total of twenty-eight football matches were held, with more than 177,000 spectators attending the matches.

### 全国足球比赛会闭幕  选出一九五一年全国足球选手  东北队获得冠军华东队获亚军

1951-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    全国足球比赛会闭幕
    选出一九五一年全国足球选手
    东北队获得冠军华东队获亚军
   【本报讯】据新华社讯：一九五一年全国足球比赛大会于九日下午在天津闭幕。
    参加闭幕式的有中华全国体育总会筹备委员会副主任荣高棠、一九五一年全国足球比赛大会筹备委员会主任黄敬、天津市副市长周叔弢和各机关、各人民团体负责人及到会观众共一万八千人。苏联驻天津总领事马铁夫等国际友人也应邀参加。
    大会闭幕前，由荣高棠致闭幕词。他号召全体体育工作者和运动员们为争取人民体育事业更大的成就而奋斗。
    最后，中华全国体育总会筹备委员会副秘书长黄中宣布在这次比赛中选出的一九五一年度中华全国足球选手王政文、高保正、方纫秋等三十人的名单，以及这次足球比赛大会的结果：冠军为东北队，亚军为华东队。中华全国体育总会筹备委员会副主任马约翰，分别给冠军和亚军发奖。
    这次大会从十二月一日开幕，九日闭幕，历时九天，共举行了二十八场足球比赛，参观比赛的观众达十七万七千多人。
