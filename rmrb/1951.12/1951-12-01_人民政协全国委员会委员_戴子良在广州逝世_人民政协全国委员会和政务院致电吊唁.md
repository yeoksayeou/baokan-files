Title: CPPCC National Committee Member Dai Ziliang Passes Away in Guangzhou; CPPCC National Committee and Government Administration Council Send Condolences

Author:

Date: 1951-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Dai Ziliang, a member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, passed away at 8:35 AM on November 29th at the Zhongshan University Hospital in Guangzhou due to illness, at the age of sixty. Comrade Dai Ziliang was a native of Haifeng County, Guangdong Province. He was originally engaged in industry and commerce in Malaya and devoted himself to the patriotic and democratic movement of overseas Chinese. He was a Central Committee member of the China Democratic League. He returned to China in 1948 and participated in the CPPCC in 1949, serving as a member of the CPPCC National Committee, a member of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, a member of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee, and a member of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government and Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission. The CPPCC National Committee and the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government have today sent telegrams of condolence to the family of Comrade Dai Ziliang. The Guangdong Provincial People's Government, the Southern General Branch of the China Democratic League, and other relevant organizations have formed a funeral committee. A public memorial service will be held at 9:00 AM on the 30th, and the coffin will be sealed at 3:00 PM in the afternoon.

### 人民政协全国委员会委员  戴子良在广州逝世  人民政协全国委员会和政务院致电吊唁

1951-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会委员
    戴子良在广州逝世
    人民政协全国委员会和政务院致电吊唁
   【新华社三十日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员戴子良，因病在十一月二十九日上午八时三十五分在广州中山大学医院逝世，享年六十岁。戴子良委员是广东省海丰县人，原在马来亚经营工商业，努力于华侨爱国民主运动，为中国民主同盟中央委员，一九四八年回国，一九四九年参加人民政协，为人民政协全国委员会委员，并任中央人民政府华侨事务委员会委员、中南军政委员会委员、广东省人民政府委员兼华侨事务委员会副主任委员。人民政协全国委员会和中央人民政府政务院已于今日致电戴子良委员家属吊唁。广东省人民政府、中国民主同盟南方总支部等有关机关已组织了治丧委员会。三十日上午九时举行公祭，遗体在下午三时大殓。
