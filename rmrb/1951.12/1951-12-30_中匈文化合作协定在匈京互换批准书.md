Title: Exchange of Instruments of Ratification of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement in Budapest
Author:
Date: 1951-12-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th -- The ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement was held in Budapest, capital of the Hungarian People's Republic, on December 22nd. The plenipotentiary representative of our side was Ambassador Huang Zhen, and the plenipotentiary representative of Hungary was Minister of Foreign Affairs Kiss. Others attending the ceremony included Hungarian Minister of Justice Molnár, Deputy Minister of Culture, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Counselor and Secretary of our Embassy in Hungary.

### 中匈文化合作协定在匈京互换批准书

1951-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈文化合作协定在匈京互换批准书
    【新华社二十九日讯】中匈文化合作协定互换批准书仪式，已于十二月二十二日在匈牙利人民共和国首都布达佩斯举行。我方全权代表为黄镇大使，匈牙利全权代表为外交部部长基斯。其他参加仪式者尚有匈牙利司法部部长莫尔那尔、文化部副部长、外交部副部长及我驻匈牙利大使馆参赞与秘书等人。
