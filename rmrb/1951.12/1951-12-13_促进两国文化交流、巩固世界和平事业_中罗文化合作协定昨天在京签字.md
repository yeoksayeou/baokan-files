Title: Promoting Cultural Exchange Between the Two Countries and Consolidating the Cause of World Peace: Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement Signed in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-12-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th:

    Promoting Cultural Exchange Between the Two Countries and Consolidating the Cause of World Peace
    Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement Signed in Beijing Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 12th] The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Romanian People's Republic signed the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement in Beijing on December 12, 1951. The purpose of signing this agreement is to strengthen the friendly relations between the two peoples through close cultural cooperation, promote mutual understanding between the two peoples, and consolidate and develop cooperation in defending the cause of world peace. The agreement stipulates the exchange of construction experiences in various fields such as culture, education, science, and art between China and Romania, and promotes cooperation between cultural, educational, and scientific research institutions of the two countries.
    The negotiations were successfully completed in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The plenipotentiary representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China signing the agreement was Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and the plenipotentiary representative of the Government of the Romanian People's Republic was Bobescu Dulianu, Minister of Education of the Romanian People's Republic.
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 12th] The negotiations on the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement proceeded in a friendly atmosphere, and an agreement has been quickly reached. The signing ceremony was held in Beijing at 3:00 PM on December 12th. In addition to the plenipotentiary representatives of both sides, those who attended the ceremony included Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Ma Xulun, Minister of Education, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xu Yixin, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department, Dong Xibai, Chief Secretary of the Treaty Committee, Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council, Zhao Feng, Counselor of the Cultural and Educational Committee, as well as members of the Romanian cultural delegation, Anna Doma, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ambassador Rudenko, Mr. Aurel Barliga, and all diplomatic officials of the Embassy of the Romanian People's Republic in China. During the ceremony, our plenipotentiary representative, Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, and Romanian plenipotentiary representative, Bobescu Dulianu, Minister of Education of Romania, delivered speeches successively, emphasizing the great contribution of the signing of this agreement to promoting cultural exchange between the Chinese and Romanian people and consolidating the cause of world peace. The ceremony proceeded in a solemn and warm atmosphere throughout.
    Our plenipotentiary representative, Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, and Romanian plenipotentiary representative, Bobescu Dulianu, Minister of Education of Romania, signed the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 促进两国文化交流、巩固世界和平事业  中罗文化合作协定昨天在京签字

1951-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    促进两国文化交流、巩固世界和平事业
    中罗文化合作协定昨天在京签字
    【新华社十二日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府与罗马尼亚人民共和国政府于一九五一年十二月十二日在北京签订了中罗文化合作协定。这一协定签订的目的在于通过密切的文化合作方式，加强两国人民间的友好关系，促进两国人民间的相互了解，巩固与发展在保卫世界和平事业中的合作。协定规定了交流中罗两国文化、教育、科学、艺术等各方面的建设经验，并促进两国文化教育科学研究机构间的合作。
    谈判是在热烈友好的气氛中顺利完成的。协定签字者中华人民共和国中央人民政府全权代表为中华人民共和国中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，罗马尼亚人民共和国政府全权代表为罗马尼亚人民共和国教育部部长包别斯库·杜列亚努。
    【新华社十二日讯】中罗文化合作协定谈判在友好的气氛中进行，业已迅速达成协议，并于十二月十二日下午三时在北京举行签字仪式。参加仪式者除双方全权代表外有中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若，教育部部长马叙伦，外交部副部长伍修权，苏联东欧司副司长徐以新，条约委员会主任秘书董希白，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，文化教育委员会参事赵沨，以及罗马尼亚文化代表团团员、外交部副部长安娜·道玛，鲁登科大使，奥雷尔·巴伦加先生和罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使馆全体外交官员。仪式中我方全权代表沈雁冰部长及罗方全权代表包别斯库·杜列亚努部长先后致辞，着重指出此协定之签订对促进中罗两国人民文化交流及巩固世界和平事业的巨大贡献。仪式始终在隆重热烈气氛中进行。
    我全权代表、中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰和罗马尼亚全权代表、罗教育部部长包别斯库·杜列亚努在中罗文化合作协定上签字。
    新闻摄影局袁苓摄
