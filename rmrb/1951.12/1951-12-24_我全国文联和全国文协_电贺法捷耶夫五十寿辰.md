Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and All-China Association of Literary Workers Telegraph Congratulations to Fadeyev on His Fiftieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1951-12-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd: The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the All-China Association of Literary Workers jointly sent a telegram congratulating the Soviet writer Fadeyev on his fiftieth birthday (December 24th). The telegram reads as follows:
    Dear Comrade Fadeyev,
    With utmost sincerity and heartfelt feelings, we warmly congratulate you on your fiftieth birthday. As an outstanding writer, social activist, and a great organizer and leader of literary and artistic work of our era, you have made tremendous contributions to proletarian literature and left indelible achievements in the cause of defending world peace. All of this inspires the peace-loving Chinese people and increases the strength of the heroic anti-aggression struggle. The close relationship between you and us symbolizes the unshakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. We deeply thank you and wish you greater achievements and eternal health.

### 我全国文联和全国文协  电贺法捷耶夫五十寿辰

1951-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我全国文联和全国文协
    电贺法捷耶夫五十寿辰
     【新华社二十三日讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会暨中华全国文学工作者协会联合电贺苏联作家法捷耶夫五十寿辰（十二月二十四日）。电文如下：亲爱的法捷耶夫同志：
    我们以无比诚恳、真挚的心情，热烈地祝贺你的五十大寿。作为我们时代一个卓越的作家、社会活动家和一个伟大的文艺工作的组织者与领导者，你对无产阶级文学做了巨大贡献，为保卫世界持久和平的事业留下了不可磨灭的功绩，所有这一切都鼓舞着爱好和平的中国人民，增加了英勇的反侵略斗争的力量。你和我们之间的亲密的关系，象征了中苏人民的不可摇撼的友谊。我们深深地感谢你；并祝你获得更大的成就和永远健康。
