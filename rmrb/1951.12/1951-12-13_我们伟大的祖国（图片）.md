    Our Great Motherland
    Strengthening epidemic prevention work is one of the key points of the new China's health construction. This is the situation of epidemic prevention personnel sent by the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government to Inner Mongolia to inspect plague bacteria. In 1947, 13,276 people in Inner Mongolia died of plague; however, in the first nine months of this year, there were only 18 plague patients in the local area, showing the great achievements of epidemic prevention work.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    加强防疫工作是新中国卫生建设事业的重点之一。这是中央人民政府卫生部派到内蒙的防疫人员正在检验鼠疫菌的情形。一九四七年内蒙人民因鼠疫而死亡的曾有一万三千二百七十六人；但今年九个月内的当地鼠疫患者仅有十八人，显示了防疫工作的重大成绩。
