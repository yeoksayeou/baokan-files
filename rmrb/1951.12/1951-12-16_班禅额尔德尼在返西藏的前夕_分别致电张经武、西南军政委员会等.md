Title: Panchen Erdeni Sends Telegrams to Zhang Jingwu, Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, etc. on the Eve of Returning to Tibet

Author:

Date: 1951-12-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, December 15th: On the eve of his departure from Qinghai to return to Tibet, Panchen Erdeni sent telegrams to Zhang Jingwu, the representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet, the Chairman and Vice-Chairmen of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, and the Chairman and Vice-Chairmen of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government. In his telegram of respect to Representative Zhang Jingwu, Panchen stated: After returning to Tibet, he will closely unite with the Dalai Lama and the leaders of various religious sects in Tibet to ensure the early and complete implementation of the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet. The telegram to the Chairman and Vice-Chairmen of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee stated: "The troops entering Tibet have victoriously entered Lhasa and Xigaze, bringing a happy life to the Tibetan people. As Panchen returns to Tibet, I would like to express my respects to you and guarantee that after returning to Tibet, under your direct leadership, I will do my best to assist the troops entering Tibet in consolidating the southwest national defense and building a new Tibet." Panchen also sent a telegram to the Chairman and Vice-Chairmen of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, expressing his gratitude to the Provincial People's Government and the people of all ethnic groups for their care and assistance over the past two years.

### 班禅额尔德尼在返西藏的前夕  分别致电张经武、西南军政委员会等

1951-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼在返西藏的前夕
    分别致电张经武、西南军政委员会等
    【新华社西安十五日电】班禅额尔德尼在离青海返西藏的前夕，分别致电中央人民政府赴藏代表张经武、西南军政委员会主席、副主席，青海省人民政府主席、副主席。在向张经武代表致敬电文中，班禅表示：返藏后要和达赖喇嘛及西藏各教派首领亲密团结，使和平解放西藏的协议早日全部实现。致西南军政委员会主席、副主席电文称：“入藏部队已胜利进驻拉萨和日喀则城，给西藏人民带来幸福的生活，兹值班禅返藏之际，特向您们致敬，并保证返藏以后在您们直接领导下，尽力协助入藏部队巩固西南国防，建设新西藏。”班禅并致电青海省人民政府主席、副主席，对该省人民政府和各族人民两年来的关怀和帮助，表示谢意。
