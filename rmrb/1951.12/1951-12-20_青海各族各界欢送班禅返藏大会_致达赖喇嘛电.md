Title: Qinghai Various Ethnic Groups and Circles Hold Grand Farewell Rally for the Panchen Lama Returning to Tibet - Telegram to the Dalai Lama
Author:
Date: 1951-12-20
Page: 1

    Qinghai Various Ethnic Groups and Circles Hold Grand Farewell Rally for the Panchen Lama Returning to Tibet
    Telegram to the Dalai Lama
    To the Dalai Lama in Lhasa, Tibet:
    The return of Panchen Erdeni to Tibet this time demonstrates the close unity of the Tibetan people under the leadership of the great people's leader, Chairman Mao, and the Central People's Government. On the occasion of Panchen Erdeni's departure from Qinghai, we send this telegram to express our congratulations. We also wish the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni to work together in the future to build a new Tibet.
    The Grand Rally of People from All Ethnic Groups and Circles in Qinghai Province to See Off Panchen Erdeni on His Return to Tibet.
    　Sixteenth

### 青海各族各界欢送班禅返藏大会  致达赖喇嘛电

1951-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    青海各族各界欢送班禅返藏大会
    致达赖喇嘛电西藏拉萨达赖喇嘛鉴：
    班禅额尔德尼先生此次返回西藏，表现了西藏人民在伟大的人民领袖——毛主席和中央人民政府领导下紧密的团结起来了。值此班禅额尔德尼先生离别青海之际，特驰电祝贺。并祝达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼先生今后为建设新西藏而共同努力。
    青海省各族各界人民欢送班禅额尔德尼先生返藏
    大会    　十六日
