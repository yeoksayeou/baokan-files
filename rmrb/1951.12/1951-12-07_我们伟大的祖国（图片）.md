Author:
Date: 1951-12-07

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to develop agricultural production, the People's Government manufactured various new agricultural implements and lent them to farmers at low prices. A promoter from the Anyang Agricultural Extension Station in Pingyuan Province is explaining the use of the light plow to farmers in Datun Village, District 1, Lin County. Many activists are listening attentively to his explanation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    人民政府为了发展农业生产，制造了各式新农具，廉价贷给农民。平原省安阳农业推广站的推广员正在林县一区大屯村向农民讲解轻便步犁的使用法，许多农民积极分子正在热心地听他的讲解。
