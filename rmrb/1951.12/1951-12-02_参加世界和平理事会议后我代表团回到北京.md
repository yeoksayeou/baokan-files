Title: Our Delegation Returns to Beijing After Attending the World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-12-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st Dispatch:

    The delegation of our country attending the World Peace Council Vienna meeting, led by head of delegation Guo Moruo, returned to Beijing by train on the 1st. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Situ Meitang, Shen Junru, Shao Lizi, Hu Qiaomu, Ma Xulun, Zhang Xiruo, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Cai Tingkai, Mei Ru'ao, Yu Xinqing, Wu Han, Li Jihuan, Yang Xiandong, Zhou Yang, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, Yang Hansheng, Yan Baohang, Sha Kefu, Wu Maosun, Cui Yueli and others, more than one hundred people.
    The Vietnamese delegation attending the World Peace Council Vienna meeting also took the same train back to their country via Beijing.

### 参加世界和平理事会议后我代表团回到北京

1951-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    参加世界和平理事会议后我代表团回到北京
    【新华社一日讯】我国出席世界和平理事会维也纳会议代表团，由郭沫若团长率领，在一日乘车返抵北京。前往车站欢迎的有：彭真、陈叔通、司徒美堂、沈钧儒、邵力子、胡乔木、马叙伦、张奚若、许德珩、许宝驹、蔡廷锴、梅汝璈、余心清、吴晗、李济寰、杨显东、周扬、陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训、阳翰笙、阎宝航、沙可夫、吴茂荪、崔月犁等一百多人。
    出席世界和平理事会维也纳会议的越南代表团也同车取道北京回国。
