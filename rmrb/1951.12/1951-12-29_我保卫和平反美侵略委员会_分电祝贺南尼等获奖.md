Title: China Peace Committee Cables Congratulations to Nenni and Others on Awards
Author:
Date: 1951-12-29
Page: 1

    China Peace Committee Cables Congratulations to Nenni and Others on Awards
    [Xinhua News Agency, 26th] The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression has sent cables to the recipients of this year's "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Peace Prize: Italy's Nenni, Japan's Oyama Ikuo, Britain's Mrs. Felton, Germany's Mrs. Seghers, and Brazil's Amado, expressing warm congratulations and wishing them new and great achievements in the struggle to defend world peace, and paying respects to the people of their respective countries.

### 我保卫和平反美侵略委员会  分电祝贺南尼等获奖

1951-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我保卫和平反美侵略委员会
    分电祝贺南尼等获奖 【新华社二十六日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会分别致电本届“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人意大利的南尼、日本的大山郁夫、英国的费尔顿夫人、德国的西格斯夫人、巴西的亚马多，表示热烈祝贺，预祝他们在保卫世界和平的斗争中获得新的伟大的成就，并向各该国人民致敬。
