Title: Romanian Ambassador to China Holds Reception in Beijing to Celebrate the Fourth Anniversary of the Founding of the Romanian People's Republic; Vice-Chairman Zhu, Vice-Premier Dong, and Diplomatic Envoys from Various Countries Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1951-12-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: Romanian Ambassador to China, Mr. Rudenko, held a reception in Beijing at 6:00 PM on the 30th to celebrate the fourth anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic. Invited to attend were: Vice-Chairman of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Vice-Premiers of the Government Administration Council Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Vice-President Zhang Zhizhiang, Deputy Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Lan Gongwu, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government Yu Xinqing, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, and heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and leaders of various people's organizations, including Zhu Xuefan, Li Lisan, Shen Yanbing, Ma Xulun, Li Dequan, Liao Chengzhi, Feng Wenbin, and Vice-Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, totaling more than one hundred people.
    Invited to attend were diplomatic envoys to China from various countries, including: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weisskopf, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic König, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Pakistani Ambassador Rochar, Swiss Minister Renzooli, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Doding, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy Choi Young, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Danish Legation Meistrup, Secretary of the Swedish Embassy Malmer, and personnel from various embassies. The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. After the reception, the Romanian film "In Our Village" was shown.
    Premier of the Government Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Zhou Enlai sent congratulatory telegrams to Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic Groza and Minister of Foreign Affairs Pauk respectively.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚人民共和国成立四周年  罗驻华大使昨在京举行招待会  朱副主席董副总理等和各国使节应邀出席

1951-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚人民共和国成立四周年
    罗驻华大使昨在京举行招待会
    朱副主席董副总理等和各国使节应邀出席
    【新华社三十日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使鲁登科，为庆祝罗马尼亚人民共和国成立四周年纪念，特于三十日下午六时在北京举行招待会。应邀出席的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德，政务院副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒、副院长张志让，最高人民检察署副检察长蓝公武，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，外交部副部长伍修权，以及中央人民政府各部门首长和各人民团体负责人朱学范、李立三、沈雁冰、马叙伦、李德全、廖承志、冯文彬和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等共一百余人。
    应邀出席的各国驻华使节有：苏联大使罗申、捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、巴基斯坦大使罗查、瑞士公使任佐立、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、越南代表团临时代办周亮、朝鲜大使馆临时代办崔英、丹麦公使馆临时代办迈思妥、瑞典大使馆秘书马尔默及各国使馆人员。招待会在友好的气氛中进行。会后并放映罗马尼亚影片“在我们的村子里”。
    中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来曾分别致电罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席格罗查和外交部长波克，表示祝贺。
