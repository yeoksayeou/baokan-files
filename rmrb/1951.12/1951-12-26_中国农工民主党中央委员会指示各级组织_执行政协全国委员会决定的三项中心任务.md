Title: The Central Committee of the China Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Instructs Organizations at All Levels to Implement the Three Central Tasks Decided by the CPPCC National Committee
Author:
Date: 1951-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: The Central Committee of the China Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party issued on the 22nd "The Decision on Implementing the Three Central Tasks Resolved by the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference."

The decision pointed out: The Sixth National Cadre Conference of the Party decided to accept the resolutions of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and will take the three major central tasks "Continue to strengthen the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement; Enhance and promote the Patriotic Production Increase and Economizing Movement; Promote the ideological remoulding movement, and systematically organize the study movement of Mao Zedong Thought which combines Marxism-Leninism with the practice of the Chinese revolution" as the three major central tasks of the Party at present. Regarding the continued strengthening of the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement, the decision pointed out: All levels of organizations must take the seven specific tasks determined by the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in the "Resolution on the Resist America, Aid Korea Work" as our work goals. Regarding advocating and promoting the Patriotic Production Increase and Economizing Movement, the decision pointed out: All levels of organizations should immediately organize Party members in various places to study relevant documents on increasing production and practicing economy, and require Party members to take the lead in the production increase and economizing movement. All levels of organizations should organize economizing inspection committees, combine with the anti-corruption, anti-waste, and anti-bureaucracy campaign, and formulate plans based on actual work. All levels of organizations must use various methods to publicize the positive significance of the production increase and economizing movement to the masses, and help the masses formulate production increase and economizing plans. Regarding launching the ideological remoulding movement, the decision pointed out: All levels of organizations should immediately organize study committees to lead and promote the study movement within the Party. The learning method should be closely combined with one's own thoughts and work, and correctly use the weapon of criticism and self-criticism.

### 中国农工民主党中央委员会指示各级组织  执行政协全国委员会决定的三项中心任务

1951-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中国农工民主党中央委员会指示各级组织
    执行政协全国委员会决定的三项中心任务
    【新华社二十三日讯】中国农工民主党中央委员会在二十二日发出“关于贯彻执行中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议所决议的三项中心任务的决定”。
    决定指出：本党第六次全国干部会议决定接受中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议各项决议，并将三大中心工作“继续加强抗美援朝运动；提高和推动爱国增产节约运动；推动思想改造运动，有系统地组织对于马克思、列宁主义与中国革命实践相结合的毛泽东思想的学习运动”作为本党当前的三大中心任务。关于继续加强抗美援朝运动，决定指出：各级组织必须以中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议“关于抗美援朝工作的决议”所决定的七项具体工作，作为我们的工作目标。关于提倡和推动爱国增产节约运动，决定指出：各级组织应立即组织各地党员学习有关增产节约的文件，并要求党员在增产节约运动中起带头作用。各级组织应组织节约检查委员会，结合反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义，并根据实际工作，订出计划。各级组织必须利用各种方式向群众宣传增产节约运动的积极意义，并帮助群众订立增产节约计划。关于展开思想改造运动，决定指出：各级组织应立即组织学习委员会，领导和推动党内的学习运动。学习方法应密切结合自己的思想和工作，正确地运用批评和自我批评的武器。
