Title: Soviet People Celebrate Stalin Constitution Day; People's Democratic Countries of Eastern Europe Hold Celebratory Rallies; Praising the Stalin Constitution for Inspiring the Anti-Imperialist Struggle of the World's People
Author:
Date: 1951-12-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 7th Dispatch: According to TASS, the Soviet people warmly celebrated December 5th - Stalin Constitution Day. Moscow was decorated in a festive atmosphere, with red banners and flags everywhere. On that day, thousands of Moscow citizens went to clubs and cultural palaces to participate in celebrations. More than 5,000 people visited the Lenin Museum, where original manuscripts and materials related to the Stalin Constitution were particularly noted. More than 4,000 people visited the Soviet Revolution Museum that day. The museum also organized many lectures introducing the Stalin Constitution and praising its creator, the great Stalin. Moscow's theaters and cultural palaces held concerts celebrating the festival, performed by the most outstanding artists.

    Residents of the capitals of the Soviet republics also joyfully celebrated the festival. In Baku, the capital of Azerbaijan, tens of thousands of residents dressed in festive attire crowded the parks and boulevards. Mass rallies were held in cultural palaces and clubs. Baku's excellent football teams held a competition at the Republic Stadium to celebrate the great festival. In Minsk, the capital of Belarus, celebrations and concerts were held on the main streets and squares. In Ashgabat, the capital of Turkmenistan, working people held celebratory forums in cultural palaces and clubs. Employees of the Ashgabat Railway Bureau, staff of the Turkmenistan Civil Aviation Administration, and workers from other enterprises celebrated Stalin Constitution Day by completing this year's production plan ahead of schedule.

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th Dispatch: According to TASS, the people of the German Democratic Republic and the People's Democratic Countries of Eastern Europe warmly celebrated Stalin Constitution Day. Residents of towns and villages in various countries held commemorative meetings and festive cultural activities on the 4th and 5th. At the celebration meeting in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, Czech Minister of Justice Reis gave a report on the historical significance of the Stalin Constitution. In Bucharest, the capital of Romania, cinemas screened several Soviet documentaries and feature films introducing the happy lives of the various ethnic groups of the Soviet Union since the promulgation of the Stalin Constitution. The working people of Bulgaria combined the celebration of Stalin Constitution Day with the anniversary of the promulgation of the Constitution of the People's Republic of Bulgaria (December 4th).

    Communist and Workers' Party newspapers in various countries published editorials and articles celebrating the festival. An editorial in the "Neues Deutschland" of the German Democratic Republic pointed out: While the reactionary rulers of the imperialist countries are destroying the last vestiges of bourgeois democracy, the Stalin Constitution, for the first time, demonstrates true democracy for mankind, equal status for all ethnic groups, and the rights and welfare of working people with material guarantees. Bulgaria's "Rabotnichesko Delo" said: "The Soviet Constitution adopted in 1936 was an event of great significance. It is the first constitution in the world based on the broadest and most thorough socialist democracy. Bulgaria was inspired by the shining example of the Stalin Constitution in its new regime construction." An editorial in Poland's "Trybuna Ludu" said: "On this day, the Polish people, together with the great Soviet people, express their highest respect to Comrade Stalin, the brilliant creator of the Soviet Constitution." "Magyar Nemzet" of Hungary pointed out: The Stalin Constitution is a constitution of peace. Stalin Constitution Day is also a festival for all peace-loving and progressive humanity. Albania's "Zëri i Popullit" said: The Stalin Constitution inspires people all over the world to fight against imperialism and the instigators of new wars.

### 苏联人民庆祝斯大林宪法日  东欧人民民主国家各地人民都举行庆祝大会  赞扬斯大林宪法鼓舞着世界人民的反帝斗争

1951-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联人民庆祝斯大林宪法日
    东欧人民民主国家各地人民都举行庆祝大会
    赞扬斯大林宪法鼓舞着世界人民的反帝斗争
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社消息：苏联人民热烈庆祝十二月五日——斯大林宪法日。在莫斯科，装饰得一片节日气象，到处招展着红色的横幅标语和旗帜。那一天，成千上万的莫斯科市民都到俱乐部和文化宫参加庆祝会。有五千多人参观了列宁博物馆，馆内陈列的各种和斯大林宪法有关的文件的原稿和材料，特别受到人们的注意。苏联革命博物馆当天也有四千多人参观。这个博物馆还组织了许多讲演，介绍斯大林宪法和歌颂它的创造者伟大的斯大林。莫斯科的各剧院和文化宫，都举办了庆祝节日的音乐会，由最卓越的艺术家演出。
    苏联各加盟共和国首都的居民，也欢欣鼓舞地庆祝这个节日。在阿塞拜疆的首都巴库，成千成万的居民穿着节日的礼服，拥塞在公园和林荫大道上。各文化宫和俱乐部内都举行了群众大会。巴库市各个优秀的足球队在共和国体育场举行了比赛大会，以庆祝伟大的节日。在白俄罗斯首都明斯克，各个主要街道和广场上都举行了庆祝会和音乐会。在土库曼首都阿什哈巴德，劳动人民在文化宫和俱乐部举行了庆祝节日的座谈。阿什哈巴德铁路局的员工、土库曼民用航空队管理局的职工，以及其他各企业的工人，都以提前完成今年的生产计划来庆祝斯大林宪法日。
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社消息：德意志民主共和国和东欧人民民主国家的人民，热烈庆祝斯大林宪法日。各国的城镇和乡村的居民，在四、五两日举行了纪念会和庆祝节日的文娱活动。在捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格的庆祝大会上，捷司法部长瑞斯作了关于斯大林宪法的历史意义的报告。罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特的各个电影院，放映了苏联多部记录片和故事片，介绍苏联各族人民在斯大林宪法颁布以来的幸福生活。保加利亚各地的劳动人民把斯大林宪法日和保加利亚人民共和国宪法颁布日（十二月四日）的庆祝会合并举行。
    各国共产党和工人党的报纸都发表了庆祝节日的社论和文章。德意志民主共和国的“新德意志报”的社论指出：当帝国主义国家反动统治者正在毁灭资产阶级民主的最后一点残迹的时候，斯大林宪法却第一次显示出人类的真正民主、各族人民的平等地位，以及得到物质上的保障的劳动人民的权利和福利。保加利亚的“工人事业报”说：“一九三六年通过的苏联宪法是一桩具有重大意义的事件。它是世界上第一部以最广泛、最彻底的社会主义的民主为基础的宪法。保加利亚在从事新的政权建设的时候，得到了斯大林宪法的光辉范例的鼓舞。”波兰的“自由论坛报”的社论说：“在这一天，波兰人民和伟大的苏联人民一道向苏联宪法的天才创造者斯大林同志表示崇高的敬意。”“匈牙利民族报”指出：斯大林宪法是和平的宪法。斯大林宪法日也就是全体爱好和平的进步人类的节日。阿尔巴尼亚的“利尼亚”报说：斯大林宪法鼓舞着全世界人民为反对帝国主义、反对新战争挑拨者而进行斗争。
