Title: The 20th Meeting of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party Passes Resolution on National Liberation Democratic United Front
Author:
Date: 1951-12-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch from Tokyo: The 20th Meeting of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, held on August 20th and 21st, passed a resolution on the National Liberation Democratic United Front. The resolution emphasized the significant importance of the National Liberation Democratic United Front in thoroughly realizing the new program of the Japanese Communist Party—"The Current Demands of the Japanese Communist Party." The resolution explained issues concerning the leadership of the working class, the foundation of the worker-peasant alliance, and the broad unity of all progressive elements within the National Liberation Democratic United Front. The resolution concluded: "The Party firmly believes that among the various tasks the Party must accomplish for the benefit of the people, the greatest task is to unify the Party's will so that the Party can struggle to realize the 'Current Demands,' unify the will of the people throughout the country, and strive to unite the people throughout the country in the National Liberation Democratic United Front."

### 日本共产党中央委员会第二十次会议  通过关于民族解放民主统一战线的决议

1951-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    日本共产党中央委员会第二十次会议
    通过关于民族解放民主统一战线的决议
   【新华社三日讯】东京迟到消息：八月二十日和二十一日举行的日本共产党中央委员会第二十次会议，通过了关于民族解放民主统一战线的决议。决议强调指出民族解放民主统一战线对于彻底实现日本共产党新纲领——“日本共产党的当前要求”的重要意义。决议说明了在民族解放民主统一战线中关于工人阶级的领导、以工农联盟为基础以及广泛团结一切进步分子诸问题。决议最后说：“党深信：党为了人民的利益而必须完成的种种任务之中，最大的任务就是统一党的意志，使党能够为实现‘当前要求’而斗争，统一全国人民的意志，尽力把全国人民团结在民族解放民主统一战线中。”
