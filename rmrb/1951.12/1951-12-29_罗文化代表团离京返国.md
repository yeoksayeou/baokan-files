Title: Romanian Cultural Delegation Departs Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1951-12-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th:

The head of the Romanian Cultural Delegation, Minister of Education Pobescu-Doreanu, and delegation members, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ana Doma and Mr. Aurel Balanga, departed Beijing by plane on December 28th to return home. Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government Shen Yanbing, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council’s Committee of Culture and Education Hong Shen, Deputy Director of the Soviet and East European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Xu Yixin, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Wei Baoshan, and others. Also present were Romanian Ambassador to China Rudenko and all diplomatic officials of the embassy.

During its stay in China, the delegation signed the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement and visited cultural and educational institutions, factories, and suburban rural areas in Beijing, and gave lectures on Romanian culture and education. The delegation also visited and toured Shanghai and Hangzhou, where they were warmly welcomed by our government agencies and people from all walks of life.

### 罗文化代表团离京返国

1951-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    罗文化代表团离京返国
    【新华社二十八日讯】罗马尼亚文化代表团团长、教育部长包别斯库·杜列亚努，团员、外交部副部长安娜·道玛及奥雷尔·巴伦加先生已于十二月二十八日乘飞机离京返国。前往机场送行的有：中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长伍修权，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，外交部苏联东欧司副司长徐以新，交际处副处长魏宝善等。此外尚有罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科及该使馆全体外交官员。
    代表团留华期间除签订了中罗文化合作协定外，还参观了北京的文教机关、工厂和近郊农村，作了有关罗马尼亚文化教育的演讲。代表团并曾前往上海和杭州访问、参观，在各处都受到我政府机关及各界人民的热烈欢迎。
