Our great motherland
    The broadcast exercises organized by the Central People's Broadcasting Station have rapidly attracted the participation of the broad masses. Further promotion of it will greatly improve the health of our people. This is the scene of the students of the Department of Physical Education of National Beijing Normal University doing broadcast exercises.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中央人民广播电台所举办的广播体操已经迅速地吸引着广大群众参加。进一步推广它，将大大改善我国人民的健康状况。这是国立北京师范大学体育系的同学们正在做广播体操的场面。
