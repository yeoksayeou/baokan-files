Title: Northwest Nationalities Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Representative Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1951-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, December 13th: The Northwest Nationalities People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Representative Conference grandly opened here on the 12th. The central topic of the conference is to discuss how to continue to strengthen the work of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea in conjunction with the movement to increase production and practice economy. Attending the conference were 451 representatives from 14 ethnic groups including Han, Tibetan, Hui, Uygur, Kazakh, Mongolian, Uzbek, Russian, Kirgiz, Tatar, Xibe, Dongxiang, Manchu, and Salar.

This conference was convened on the basis of the great achievements made in various tasks in the Northwest Region in the past two years, especially in national unity, resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, land reform, suppression of counter-revolutionaries, and urban democratic reform. Except for Xinjiang Province, all provinces and cities in the Northwest have successively held representative conferences on resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, and summarized the experience of each province and city in resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea. Representatives of all ethnic groups attending the meeting have full confidence in continuing to strengthen the work of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea. On the opening day of the conference, the大会 sent a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao, General Kim Il-sung, and Commander Peng Dehuai of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. On the same evening, the congress received a reply telegram from Chairman Mao, and the representatives of all ethnic groups were extremely excited. When the presidium of the conference read Chairman Mao's reply telegram to the conference in Chinese, Uyghur, Tibetan and other languages ​​the next day, all the representatives of all ethnic groups shouted "Long live Chairman Mao!" in the languages ​​of fourteen ethnic groups!

The telegram of respect from the大会 to Chairman Mao:
Respected Chairman Mao: Today, the Northwest Nationalities People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Representative Conference opened in Xi'an. More than 400 representatives attending the conference extend our highest respect to you and promise to resolutely implement your great call at the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference: "Continue to strengthen the work of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, increase production, and practice strict economy to support the Chinese People's Volunteers," mobilize all forces to further deepen and develop patriotic propaganda and education work against U.S. aggression and aid Korea, and strive for new victories in the cause of building the people's motherland. We wish you good health!
        Northwest Nationalities People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Representative Conference, December 12, 1951

### 西北各民族抗美援朝代表会议开幕

1951-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北各民族抗美援朝代表会议开幕
　　【新华社西安十三日电】西北各民族人民抗美援朝代表会议十二日在此间隆重开幕。会议的中心议题是讨论如何结合增产节约运动，继续加强抗美援朝工作。出席会议的有汉、藏、回、维吾尔、哈萨克、蒙、乌孜别克、俄罗斯、柯尔克斯、塔塔尔、锡伯、东乡、满、撒拉等十四个民族的代表四百五十一人。
    这次会议是在西北区两年来各项工作，特别是在民族团结、抗美援朝、土地改革、镇压反革命和城市民主改革等工作有了很大成绩的基础上召开的。西北各省、市除新疆省外，都已先后举行了抗美援朝代表会议，总结了各省、市抗美援朝工作的经验。到会各民族代表对继续加强抗美援朝工作都具有充分的信心。会议开幕日，大会发出了向毛主席、金日成将军和中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军致敬电。同日晚，大会收到毛主席的复电，各民族代表莫不万分兴奋。当第二天会议主席团用汉、维、藏等族语言向大会宣读毛主席复电时，全场各民族代表用十四种民族的语言高呼“毛主席万岁！”大会给毛主席的致敬电
    敬爱的毛主席：今天，西北各族人民抗美援朝代表会议在西安开幕了。我们出席大会的四百多名代表谨向您致以崇高的敬意，并保证坚决贯彻您在中国人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议上提出的：“继续加强抗美援朝的工作，需要增加生产，厉行节约，以支持中国人民志愿军”的伟大号召，动员一切力量，更加深入和开展抗美援朝爱国主义宣传教育工作，为争取人民祖国建设事业的新的胜利而斗争。祝您身体健康！
        西北各族人民抗美援朝代表会议一九五一年十二月十二日
