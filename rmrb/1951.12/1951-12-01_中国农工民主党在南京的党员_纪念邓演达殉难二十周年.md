Title: Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Members in Nanjing Commemorate the 20th Anniversary of Deng Yanda's Martyrdom
Author:
Date: 1951-12-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 30th. The members of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party in Nanjing commemorated the 20th anniversary of Deng Yanda's martyrdom. On the 29th, they presented wreaths and paid their respects at Deng Yanda's tomb outside Qilinmen in this city. Deng Yanda was the founder of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, and was murdered by the Chiang Kai-shek bandits on November 29, 1931.

### 中国农工民主党在南京的党员  纪念邓演达殉难二十周年

1951-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中国农工民主党在南京的党员
    纪念邓演达殉难二十周年
    【新华社南京三十日电】中国农工民主党在南京市的党员纪念邓演达殉难二十周年，二十九日到本市麒麟门外邓演达墓地献花圈致奠。按邓演达是中国农工民主党的创始人，在一九三一年十一月二十九日被蒋匪介石杀害。
