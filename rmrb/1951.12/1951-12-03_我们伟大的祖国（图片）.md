Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-12-03
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Chinese People's Volunteers are fighting stronger and stronger on the Korean front. In the recent counterattacks and blocking actions, they have continued to annihilate and inflict heavy casualties on the enemy. This is a scene of the soldiers holding a meeting in the trenches after the battle to summarize their experiences. They unanimously passed a battle covenant, guaranteeing to completely eliminate the invading enemy.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军在朝鲜前线愈战愈强，他们在最近的反击战和阻击战中，继续大量地歼灭和杀伤了敌人。这是战斗结束后战士们在战壕里开会总结经验的情形。他们一致通过战斗公约，保证彻底消灭进犯的敌人。
