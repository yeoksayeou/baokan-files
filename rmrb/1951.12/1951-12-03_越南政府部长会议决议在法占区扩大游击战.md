Title: Vietnamese Government Ministerial Conference Resolves to Expand Guerrilla Warfare in French-Occupied Areas
Author:
Date: 1951-12-03
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, November 29th: The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a ministerial conference in mid-November. The meeting was presided over by President Ho Chi Minh.

    After hearing a report on the international situation, the Ministerial Conference heard a report on the Vietnamese military situation in September and October from General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army. General Vo Nguyen Giap pointed out: The urgent tasks of the People's Army and the Vietnamese people at present are: in addition to annihilating the main force of the French aggressor troops on the main front, they should step up guerrilla warfare.

    The conference passed a number of important resolutions, including countermeasures against the French imperialists' plot to "use Vietnamese to fight Vietnamese." The conference emphasized that the Vietnamese people in French-occupied areas, especially in guerrilla areas, should carry out the following three tasks: consolidate and develop people's base areas, conduct propaganda against puppet troops, and expand guerrilla warfare.

    President Ho Chi Minh delivered a speech at the close of the Ministerial Conference, emphatically pointing out the importance of opposing the French imperialists' plot to "use Vietnamese to fight Vietnamese."

### 越南政府部长会议决议在法占区扩大游击战

1951-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    越南政府部长会议决议在法占区扩大游击战
    【新华社一日讯】据越南通讯社北越十一月二十九日讯：越南民主共和国政府在十一月中旬举行部长会议。会议由胡志明主席主持。
    部长会议在听取关于国际形势的报告后，由越南人民军总司令武元甲将军报告九月和十月份的越南军事形势。武元甲将军指出：目前人民军和越南人民的紧急任务是：除在主要战线上歼灭法国侵略军的主力外，应当加紧进行游击战争。
    会议通过了许多重要决议，其中包括对于法帝国主义者“利用越南人打越南人”的阴谋的对策。会议强调指出：在法军占领区尤其是游击区内的越南人民，应进行以下三种工作：巩固和发展人民根据地、对傀儡军队进行宣传和扩大游击战争。
    胡志明主席在部长会议闭幕时发表演说，强调地指出反对法帝国主义者“利用越南人打越南人”的阴谋的重要性。
