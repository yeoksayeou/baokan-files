Title: Panchen Erdeni's Kampos Council Staff Returns to Lhasa. Troops Entering Tibet, Representatives of the Tibetan Local Government, and Monks and Laypeople Welcome Them in the Suburbs. Generals Zhang Guohua and Tan Guansan Host a Banquet to Welcome Them and Celebrate Internal Unity in Tibet.
Author:
Date: 1951-12-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 11th. Over two hundred people, including Ji Jinmei, of the Panchen Erdeni's Kampos Council who left Tibet twenty-eight years ago, returned to Lhasa from Qinghai on the 1st. The People's Liberation Army stationed in Lhasa, representatives of the Tibetan local government, and monks and laypeople of Lhasa all went to the suburbs to welcome them. The Tibetan local government sent Zasa (official title) Awang Jianzan and other officials to present hadas to Ji Jinmei and others. On the 2nd, Generals Zhang Guohua, Commander of the People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet, and Tan Guansan, Political Commissar, hosted a banquet to welcome Ji Jinmei and the other two hundred people. At the banquet, General Zhang Guohua said: "Under the leadership of the great leader of all ethnic groups, Chairman Mao, the Tibetan people are united with all ethnic groups in the country, and internal unity has been achieved in Tibet. The return of the staff of the Panchen Erdeni's Kampos Council to Lhasa is something to be celebrated." Then Ji Jinmei spoke. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude for Chairman Mao's leadership and stated: "In the future, under the leadership of the Central People's Government, I will certainly work hard to quickly and thoroughly implement the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet." Finally, Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, spoke. He pointed out: "As long as the Tibetan people are united and unite with all ethnic groups in the country, we will definitely be able to completely expel the imperialist aggressive forces from Tibet."
The attendees all toasted to Chairman Mao's health, to the eternal unity within Tibet, and to the health of the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni. After the banquet, the troops entering Tibet held an evening party and performed a variety of wonderful programs.

### 班禅行辕堪布会议厅人员返抵拉萨  入藏部队和西藏地方政府代表及僧俗人民到郊外欢迎  张国华谭冠三两将军设宴欢迎并庆贺西藏内部的团结

1951-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅行辕堪布会议厅人员返抵拉萨
    入藏部队和西藏地方政府代表及僧俗人民到郊外欢迎
    张国华谭冠三两将军设宴欢迎并庆贺西藏内部的团结
    【新华社拉萨十一日电】离开西藏二十八年的班禅行辕堪布会议厅计晋美等二百多人，已在一日由青海返抵拉萨。驻在拉萨的人民解放军和西藏地方政府的代表以及拉萨僧俗人民，都到郊外欢迎。西藏地方政府派出札萨（官衔）阿旺坚赞等官员向计晋美等敬献哈达。二日，人民解放军进藏部队司令员张国华、政治委员谭冠三两将军设宴欢迎计晋美等二百多人。张国华将军在宴会上说：“在各族人民伟大领袖毛主席领导下，西藏人民与全国各族人民团结起来了，西藏内部也团结起来了。班禅行辕堪布会议厅的工作人员重新回到拉萨，这是值得庆贺的事情。”接着由计晋美讲话。他衷心地感激毛主席的领导，并表示：今后一定在中央人民政府领导下，为迅速彻底实现和平解放西藏办法的协议而努力。最后由中央人民政府代表张经武讲话。他指出：“只要西藏人民团结一致并和全国各民族团结一致，就一定能彻底把帝国主义侵略势力逐出西藏。”
    到会人员纷纷为毛主席的健康干杯，为西藏内部永远团结干杯，为达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的健康干杯。宴会后，进藏部队并举行晚会，演出精彩节目多种。
