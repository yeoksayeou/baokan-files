Title: Egyptian Peace Advocates Prepare for Near East and North African Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1951-12-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 9th: Preparations are underway in Egypt for the Near East and North African countries' Peace Conference. In order to establish a preparatory committee for the peace advocates' organization in Egypt, it has been decided to hold a mass Peace Defense Conference in Cairo on December 13th, with representatives from all regions of the country participating. According to the "Katib Newspaper," this conference will be an important step in helping to unite millions of farmers, workers, and all Egyptian patriots around the peace movement. The "Katib Newspaper" publishes statements from representatives of all walks of life in each issue, supporting the Near East and North African countries' Peace Conference.

### 埃及和平拥护者筹备近东北非保卫和平大会

1951-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及和平拥护者筹备近东北非保卫和平大会
   【新华社十日讯】塔斯社开罗九日讯：埃及正在进行近东与北非国家保卫和平大会的筹备工作。为了在埃及成立和平拥护者组织的筹备委员会，决定于十二月十三日在开罗举行一个群众性的保卫和平大会，由全国各区派代表参加。据“卡特布报”报道：这个大会将是一个重要的步骤，有助于把数百万的农民、工人以及所有埃及爱国人士都团结在和平运动的周围。“卡特布报”每一期都发表各界代表关于支持近东与北非国家保卫和平大会的声明。
