Title: Beijing Holds Photo Exhibition and Lecture Meeting to Celebrate Bulgarian Constitution Day
Author:
Date: 1951-12-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 8th—To celebrate the Constitution Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria on December 5th, the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council has held a small photo exhibition in the waterside pavilion of Zhongshan Park starting on the 6th of this month. The exhibition includes proportion charts of the industrial development of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, pictures of industrial and agricultural construction, and the people's lives. The Bureau also held a lecture meeting at the International Club on the afternoon of the 8th of this month. At the meeting, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, first explained the significance of the lecture meeting. Liu Qingyang, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, then gave a lecture entitled "Congratulations on the Constitution Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and a Report on its Development and Construction Since its Founding," explaining the important significance of the Constitution Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and praising the great achievements of the People's Republic of Bulgaria in various fields since its founding, as well as the profound friendship between China and Bulgaria. The Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Petkov, gave an impromptu speech, thanking the Chinese people for their friendship and wishing the friendship between the two countries would be further consolidated and developed. After the meeting, the Bulgarian revolutionary war feature film "Alarm" was shown.

### 北京举行图片展览和讲演会  庆祝保加利亚宪法颁布日

1951-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    北京举行图片展览和讲演会
    庆祝保加利亚宪法颁布日
    【新华社八日讯】十二月五日为保加利亚人民共和国宪法颁布日，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局为庆祝这一节日，已于本月六日开始在中山公园水榭举行小型图片展览。展出内容有保加利亚人民共和国工业发展的比例图表、工农业建设及人民生活的图片等。该局并于本月八日下午在国际俱乐部举办讲演会，会上首由对外文化联络事务局局长洪深说明讲演会的意义，继由北京市民主妇女联合会副主席刘清扬主讲“祝贺保加利亚人民共和国宪法颁布日并报告其建国以来之发展与建设情况”，说明保加利亚人民共和国宪法颁布日的重要意义，并盛赞保加利亚人民共和国建国以来各方面的伟大成就及中保两国深厚的友谊。保加利亚驻华大使彼得科夫即席致词，感谢中国人民的友谊，并祝两国的友谊更趋巩固与发展。会后放映保加利亚革命战争故事片“警报”。
