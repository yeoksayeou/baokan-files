    Our Great Motherland
    Preventing and controlling livestock diseases is one of the important measures to restore and develop the economy of pastoral areas. This is the scene of disease prevention personnel sent by the Central People's Government in August this year giving livestock disease prevention injections in the Eastern United Banner of Xilingol League, Inner Mongolia.

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    防治牲畜疫病是恢复和发展畜牧区经济的重要措施之一。这是今年八月中央人民政府派出的防疫人员，在内蒙锡林郭勒盟东部联合旗进行牲畜防疫注射的情形。
