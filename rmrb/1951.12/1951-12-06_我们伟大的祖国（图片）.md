    Our Great Motherland
    The Soviet machine workers' high-speed cutting method is gradually being promoted in our country. It can not only increase production, but also improve product quality. Zhou Yongsheng, deputy chief engineer of the Shanghai Universal Machinery Factory, and worker Jiang Tongsheng are studying the angle of the turning tool so as to adopt the high-speed cutting method like other advanced machine factories.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    苏联机器工人所创造的高速切削法正在我国逐渐推行。它不仅可以提高产量，而且可以提高产品的质量。上海通用机器厂副总工程师周永升和工人蒋同生等正在研究车刀角度，以便和其他先进机器工厂一样地采用高速切削法。
