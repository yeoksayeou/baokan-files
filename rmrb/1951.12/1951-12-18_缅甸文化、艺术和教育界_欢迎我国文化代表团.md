Title: Burmese Cultural, Artistic, and Educational Circles Welcome Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1951-12-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th Dispatch: Rangoon Message: The Chinese Cultural Delegation has been warmly welcomed by people in Burmese cultural, artistic, and educational circles in Rangoon. The delegation has, at the invitation of Burmese fine arts groups, music groups, Rangoon University, the Journalists Association, the Writers Association, and other groups and schools, reported on the situation of culture, art, and education in New China, and exchanged experiences with them. People in Burmese cultural circles highly praised the achievements of New China in cultural construction. Mao Liye, the person in charge of the Burmese Art Gallery, said: "Since China's liberation, there have been amazing achievements in cultural construction. I believe that the people of China and Burma can jointly promote world peace through cultural and artistic exchanges." Yu Zhongming, Vice-Chairman of the Journalists Association, said: "We must learn and study Chinese culture."
    The Chinese Cultural Delegation held a film reception on the 10th. More than 700 people were invited to attend, including playwrights, film actors, musicians, artists, journalists, and social celebrities. Zhang Junxiang, a member of the delegation, introduced the film industry of New China. The Chinese film "The White Haired Girl," which won a special honorary award at the international film festival, was screened at the meeting. Burmese cultural and artistic circles praised the film.
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th Dispatch: Rangoon Message: Yao Zhongming, China's ambassador to Burma, held a grand reception at the embassy on the 12th to welcome the Chinese Cultural Delegation. Invited to attend were senior Burmese government officials, representatives of political parties and mass organizations, people in cultural and artistic circles, writers, social celebrities, overseas Chinese, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma, totaling more than 1,000 people.
    The reception took place in a pleasant and friendly atmosphere. After the meeting, the film "The Great Unity of Chinese Nationalities" was screened.

### 缅甸文化、艺术和教育界  欢迎我国文化代表团

1951-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化、艺术和教育界
    欢迎我国文化代表团
    【新华社十六日讯】仰光消息：中国文化代表团在仰光受到缅甸文化、艺术和教育界人士的热烈欢迎。代表团先后应缅甸的美术团体、音乐团体、仰光大学、报人协会、作家协会等团体和学校的邀请，报告新中国文化、艺术、教育各方面的情况，并和它们交流经验。缅甸文化界人士十分称赞新中国在文化建设上的成就。缅甸美术馆负责人帽力耶说：“中国解放后在文化方面的建设有惊人的成就。我相信中、缅两国人民能通过文化、艺术的交流来共同促进世界和平。”报人协会副主席宇仲明说：“我们要学习和研究中国的文化。”
    中国文化代表团在十日举行电影招待会。应邀到会的有戏剧家、电影演员、音乐家、艺术家、新闻工作者、社会名流等七百多人。代表团团员张骏祥介绍了新中国的电影事业。会上放映曾在国际电影赛会上荣获特别荣誉奖的我国影片“白毛女”。缅甸文化艺术界人士都赞扬这部影片。
    【新华社十六日讯】仰光消息：我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明十二日在大使馆举行盛大招待会，欢迎中国文化代表团。应邀出席的有缅甸政府高级官员、政党及群众团体的代表、文化艺术界人士、作家、社会名流、华侨以及各国驻缅甸的外交使节等共一千多人。
    招待会在愉快友好的气氛中进行，会后放映了“中国民族大团结”影片。
