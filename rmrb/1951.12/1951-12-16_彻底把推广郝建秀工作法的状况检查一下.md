Title: Thoroughly Check the Situation of Promoting the Hao Jianxiu Work Method

Author:
Date: 1951-12-16
Page: 2

Economic Life Brief Commentary

    Thoroughly Check the Situation of Promoting the Hao Jianxiu Work Method

    The state-owned Tianjin No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory took a detour in promoting the Hao Jianxiu Work Method. After inspection and self-criticism, it corrected the erroneous thinking of cadres, strengthened the organizational leadership of the promotion work, and the enthusiasm of the masses increased. The promotion work immediately made progress. This fact once again illustrates the importance of inspection work.

    From the experience of the state-owned Tianjin No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory, the key to promoting the Hao Jianxiu Work Method lies in thoroughly clearing the minds of cadres and strengthening organizational leadership. Workers are willing to learn advanced work methods. In order to learn advanced work methods, workers urgently need cadres to lead them. This is an extremely favorable condition for promoting advanced work methods. However, some cadres have conservative ideas and complacency. They do not promote advanced work methods, or they just perfunctorily arrange them. In this way, advanced work methods cannot be truly promoted.

    We hope that all cotton textile factories will seriously check the situation of promoting the Hao Jianxiu Work Method. In the process of inspection, you can refer to the experience of the Tianjin State-owned No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory, first check the ideological status of cadres; second, check the status of organizational leadership. We believe that thoroughly solving these two problems will definitely make the promotion work successful.

### 彻底把推广郝建秀工作法的状况检查一下

1951-12-16
第2版()
专栏：经济生活简评

    彻底把推广郝建秀工作法的状况检查一下
    国营天津第一棉纺织厂在推广郝建秀工作法中，走了一段弯路。经过检查和检讨，纠正了干部的错误思想，加强了推广工作的组织领导，群众情绪高涨，推广工作立刻有了进步。这一事实，再一次说明了检查工作的重要性。
    从国营天津第一棉纺织厂的经验看来，推广郝建秀工作法的关键，在于彻底打通干部的思想，和加强组织领导。工人是愿意学习先进工作方法的。为了学习先进工作方法，工人迫切需要干部去领导。这本是推广先进工作法的极为有利的条件。但是，有些干部自己有保守思想和自满情绪，他们不去推行先进工作方法，或者只是敷衍塞责地布置一下。这样，先进工作方法当然就不可能真正推广了。
    我们希望所有棉纺织厂，都把推广郝建秀工作法的状况，认真地检查一下。在检查过程中，可以参考天津国营第一棉纺织厂的经验，首先检查干部思想状况；其次，检查组织领导的状况。我们认为：彻底解决了这两个问题，推广工作是一定可以作好的。
