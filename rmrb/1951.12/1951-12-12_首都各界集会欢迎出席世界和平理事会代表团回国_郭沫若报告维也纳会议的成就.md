Title: Capital Circles Hold Rally to Welcome Delegation Returning from World Peace Council Meeting; Guo Moruo Reports on the Achievements of the Vienna Conference
Author:
Date: 1951-12-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Capital circles held a rally at 2:00 PM on the 11th to welcome the Chinese delegation returning from the World Peace Council meeting in Vienna. The rally was jointly hosted by the China Peace Committee and the North China and Beijing branches of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression. Present at the meeting were Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Mao Dun, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Bojun, Ji Chaoding, and more than 1,500 representatives from various people's organizations and government agencies. The meeting was presided over by Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, who delivered the opening address. Chen Shutong said that the solemn resolutions passed by the World Peace Council meeting in Vienna fully met the demands of all the Chinese people and the will of the vast peace-loving people of the world. The Chinese delegation, representing a quarter of the world's population, attended the meeting with the firm belief of our 475 million people in defending peace, which demonstrates to the world our Chinese people's unparalleled enthusiasm for defending world peace, and its significance is particularly significant.

    Guo Moruo, the head of the delegation, gave a detailed report on the situation of participating in the World Peace Council meeting in Vienna and the achievements of the meeting. Following this, Mao Dun reported on the resolutions passed by the Vienna Conference regarding cultural exchanges between countries. Ji Chaoding was also invited to report on the situation of attending the meeting of the preparatory committee of the International Economic Conference held in Copenhagen in October.

### 首都各界集会欢迎出席世界和平理事会代表团回国  郭沫若报告维也纳会议的成就

1951-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界集会欢迎出席世界和平理事会代表团回国
    郭沫若报告维也纳会议的成就
    【新华社十一日讯】首都各界在十一日下午二时举行欢迎中国出席世界和平理事会维也纳会议代表团回国大会。大会由中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会总会及华北总分会、北京市分会联合主持。出席大会的有郭沫若、陈叔通、茅盾、蔡廷锴、章伯钧、冀朝鼎，和各人民团体、各机关等代表一千五百多人。大会由中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副主席陈叔通致开会词。陈叔通说，世界和平理事会维也纳会议通过的庄严的决议，完全符合我们全中国人民的要求和全世界广大爱好和平人民的意志。代表全世界四分之一人口的中国代表团，带了我们全国四亿七千五百万人民保卫和平的坚决信念去参加会议，就是向全世界表示了我们中国人民对保卫世界和平的无比热诚，它的意义是特别重大的。
    代表团团长郭沫若详细报告了参加世界和平理事会维也纳会议的情形和会议的成就。接着由茅盾报告了维也纳会议通过关于各国文化交流问题的决议的情况。冀朝鼎也应邀报告了出席国际经济会议筹备委员会十月间在哥本哈根举行的会议的情形。
