Title: Results of the Fourth Day of the National Football Competition
Author:
Date: 1951-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 5th. The results of the four matches on the fourth day of the 1951 National Football Competition are as follows:
	North China vs. Railway – 3 to 2, North China wins.
	Southwest vs. Northwest – 3 to 1, Southwest wins.
	People's Liberation Army vs. Central South – 5 to 1, People's Liberation Army wins.
	Northeast vs. East China – 2 to 0, Northeast wins.

### 全国足球比赛第四日结果

1951-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    全国足球比赛第四日结果
    【新华社天津五日电】一九五一年全国足球比赛大会第四日四场的比赛结果如下：华北对铁路——三比二，华北胜。西南对西北——三比一，西南胜。解放军对中南——五比一，解放军胜。东北对华东——二比○，东北胜。
