Date: 1951-12-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The completion of the first phase of the Huai River harnessing project has filled the rural areas of the Huai River basin with a new atmosphere. Yangcun Village in Lingbi County, northern Anhui Province, suffered from continuous natural disasters in the past, and the number of draught animals decreased, leaving only five. Now, the number of draught animals has rapidly increased to twenty-eight. After the bumper harvest, farmer Gu Jixiang bought a big yellow ox for 9 million yuan, which the whole family loves.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    根治淮河第一期工程的完成，使淮河流域的农村充满了新气象。皖北灵璧县杨町村过去因连年灾荒，耕畜减少，只剩下五头；现在耕畜已迅速增加到二十八头。农民顾纪香在丰收后以九十万元买了一头大黄牛，全家都喜欢。
