Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Warmly Welcomed in Various Parts of Burma
Author:
Date: 1951-12-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 24th: The Chinese cultural art exhibition and the Chinese films shown by the Chinese Cultural Delegation, currently visiting various parts of Burma, have been warmly welcomed by the broad masses of the Burmese people.

　　The Chinese cultural art exhibition held by the Chinese Cultural Delegation in Rangoon closed on the 15th. During the six days of the exhibition, approximately 80,000 people visited. More than 30,000 people watched screenings of films such as "The White Haired Girl" at the exhibition square. Visitors wrote their opinions in various languages in the visitors' book: "Such cultural and artistic exchanges can not only promote the friendship between the people of China and Burma, but also make a great contribution to safeguarding world peace"; "In the exhibition, I saw the rapid progress of the new China. Today, the new China is the lighthouse of Asia, and I am proud that Asia has such a country"; "This exhibition is of great help to those who do not understand the new China. I want to question the United States and the United Nations why they exclude such a great country from the United Nations"; "I am over sixty years old, and today is the first time I have seen such an exhibition. This is only possible in the Mao Zedong era. I sincerely thank Chairman Mao Zedong. I pay tribute to Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese people."

　　The Chinese Cultural Delegation visited the Southern Shan State on the 14th. Upon arriving in Taunggyi, they were warmly welcomed by more than 2,000 people from all walks of life in Burma and Chinese and Indian expatriates. The delegation visited the Southern Shan State for three days and screened the film "Great Unity of Chinese Nationalities." More than 7,000 people watched the film. Many ethnic minority viewers were very moved after watching the film. They said: "Chairman Mao Zedong treats ethnic minorities so well, leading all ethnic groups in China to unite closely together, which makes us very admire."

　　The Chinese Cultural Delegation arrived in Mandalay for a visit on the 17th. Welcoming them at Mandalay Airport were Mandalay Commissioner U Wan Maung, people from all walks of life in Burma, and more than a thousand Chinese, Indian, and Pakistani expatriates. The welcoming crowds shouted "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong!" and "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people!" The Chinese cultural art exhibition held by the delegation in Mandalay opened on the same day. More than 10,000 people visited that day. People from all over the Mandalay area flocked to visit.

### 我国文化代表团在缅甸各地受到热烈欢迎

1951-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文化代表团在缅甸各地受到热烈欢迎
   【新华社二十四日讯】仰光消息：目前在缅甸各地访问的中国文化代表团所举办的中国文化艺术展览会和代表团所放映的中国影片，受到缅甸广大人民的热烈欢迎。
    中国文化代表团在仰光举办的中国文化艺术展览会已于十五日闭幕。在展览的六天中，观众约达八万人。在展览会广场上看放映“白毛女”等影片的有三万多人。观众们纷纷用各种不同的文字在意见簿上写下他们的意见：“通过这样的文化艺术的交流，不但可以促进中缅两国人民之间的友谊，而且对于保卫世界和平也有很大的贡献”；“在展览会中，我看到新中国正在飞跃地进展，今天新中国是亚洲的灯塔，我为了亚洲有这样的国家而感到骄傲”；“这个展览会对于那些不了解新中国的人们有很大的帮助，我要质问美国和联合国为什么把这样一个伟大的国家拒于联合国之外”；“我活了六十多岁，今天第一次看到这样的展览会。这只有在毛泽东时代才有可能。我衷心地感谢毛泽东主席。谨向毛泽东主席和中国人民致敬”。
    中国文化代表团于十四日前往南掸邦访问，在抵达东枝时，曾受到缅甸各界人士和中、印侨民两千多人的热烈欢迎。代表团在南掸邦访问了三天，并放映“中国民族大团结”影片。看过这部影片的观众达七千多人。许多少数民族的观众看了电影以后，都很感动。他们说：“毛泽东主席对待少数民族这样好，领导中国各民族亲密地团结在一起，使我们非常景仰。”
    中国文化代表团于十七日到达曼德勒访问。在曼德勒机场迎接的，有曼德勒专员宇旺敏以及缅甸各界人士和中国、印度、巴基斯坦侨民千余人。欢迎的群众高呼“毛泽东万岁”，“中缅人民友好万岁。”代表团在曼德勒举办的中国文化艺术展览会在同一天开幕。当天前往参观的就有一万多人。曼德勒附近各地的人民都纷纷前往参观。
