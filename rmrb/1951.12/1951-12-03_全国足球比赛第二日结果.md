Title: Results of the Second Day of National Football Competition
Author:
Date: 1951-12-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 2nd. The results of the four matches on the second day of the 1951 National Football Competition Conference are as follows:

    North China vs. Southwest – 3 to 1, North China won.
    Railways vs. Northeast – 0 to 0, tie.
    People's Liberation Army vs. Northwest – 6 to 0, People's Liberation Army won.
    East China vs. Central South – 2 to 1, East China won.

1951-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    全国足球比赛第二日结果
    【新华社天津二日电】一九五一年全国足球比赛大会第二日四场比赛结果如下：
    华北对西南——三比一，华北胜。
    铁路对东北——○比○，平。
    解放军对西北——六比○，解放军胜。
    华东对中南——二比一，华东胜。
