Title: Romanian Cultural Delegation Visits Guo Moruo and Others; Guo Moruo Hosted a Banquet for the Delegation Last Night
Author:
Date: 1951-12-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 4th:

    After arriving in Beijing on December 2nd, the Romanian Cultural Delegation, led by Head of Delegation Bobescu Duleanu, along with members Anna Doma, Rudenko, and Aurel Barenga, paid separate visits to Guo Moruo, Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's State Council, Minister of Education Ma Xulun, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu. When Guo Moruo received the delegation, those present also included Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, among others. On the evening of the 4th, Guo Moruo hosted a banquet to welcome the Romanian Cultural Delegation. During its stay in China, the delegation will not only negotiate and sign a Sino-Romanian cultural agreement with our cultural negotiation representatives, but also visit our cultural and educational institutions and conduct other activities to promote cultural exchange between the peoples of China and Romania. The delegation will also visit other cities in China and conduct visits.

### 罗马尼亚文化代表团分别拜会郭沫若等  郭沫若昨晚欢宴代表团

1951-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚文化代表团
    分别拜会郭沫若等
    郭沫若昨晚欢宴代表团
    【新华社四日讯】罗马尼亚文化代表团于十二月二日抵京后，该代表团团长包别斯库·杜列亚努偕团员安娜·道玛、鲁登科及奥雷尔·巴伦加曾分别拜会了我中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会主任郭沫若、教育部部长马叙伦和外交部副部长章汉夫。在郭沫若接见代表团时，在座者尚有文化部部长沈雁冰等。四日晚由郭沫若设宴欢迎罗马尼亚文化代表团。该代表团在华期间除将与我方文化谈判代表商谈并签订中罗文化协定外，将参观我国文化教育机构及进行其他为促进中罗两国人民文化交流之活动，代表团并将前往我国其他城市参观并作访问。
