Title: The Ministry of Trade Has Reduced National Edible Oil Prices at the End of November
Author:
Date: 1951-12-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 4th—In order to control seasonal price differences in edible oil, the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government reduced national edible oil prices at the end of November, with an average reduction of 3.39%. For example, in Hankou, the price of raw oil was reduced from 4,900 yuan per jin to 4,700 yuan per jin; in Beijing, the price of raw oil was reduced from 4,800 yuan per jin to 4,630 yuan per jin; and in Taiyuan, the price of raw oil was reduced from 4,600 yuan per jin to 4,360 yuan per jin.

### 贸易部已在十一月底降低全国食油牌价

1951-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    贸易部已在十一月底降低全国食油牌价
    【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府贸易部为掌握食油的季节差价，特于十一月底降低全国食油牌价，平均降低百分之三点三九。计汉口每斤生油由四千九百元降至四千七百元，北京每斤生油由四千八百元降至四千六百三十元，太原每斤生油由四千六百元降至四千三百六十元。
