Title: Northwest Nationalities' People's Conference to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea Sends Telegrams to the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Congratulating Them on Their Close Unity; Representatives of 3,600 Monks from Labrang Monastery in Gansu Also Send Telegrams of Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1951-12-22
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, December 21st) The Northwest Nationalities' People's Conference to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, upon learning the news of Panchen Erdeni's departure from Qinghai to return to Tibet, sent telegrams to the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni on the 19th, congratulating them on their close unity.

(1) Telegram to the Dalai Lama
Dalai Lama:

During our conference, we were delighted to hear that you had sent a telegram urging Mr. Panchen Erdeni to return to Tibet, and the good news that Mr. Panchen Erdeni had set off to return to Tibet. All of our representatives congratulate you and Mr. Panchen Erdeni, under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, for strengthening the close unity within Tibet and among all ethnic groups in the country, thoroughly implementing the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet, eliminating the influence of imperialist aggression, and building a prosperous and happy new Tibet. We send this telegram to congratulate you and wish you good health.

(2) Telegram to Panchen Erdeni
Mr. Panchen Erdeni:

We, more than 400 representatives of people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life from five provinces and one municipality in the Northwest, are very excited to hear the good news that you have set off to return to Tibet during our conference. We firmly support your close unity with the Dalai Lama, and under the wise leadership of the great leader of all ethnic groups, Chairman Mao, we will strive to implement the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet, eliminate the influence of imperialist aggression, consolidate national defense, build a prosperous and happy new Tibet, and enable the Tibetan people to join the people of the whole country in marching towards the bright and victorious road. We send this telegram to congratulate you and wish you a victorious arrival at your destination.

(Xinhua News Agency, Xining, December 21st) As Panchen Erdeni departed from Qinghai to return to Tibet, Jamuyang's financial steward Langzhe and 23 others, representing the 3,600 monks of Labrang Monastery in Gansu Province, sent telegrams to the Dalai Lama and Panchen Master. The telegram reads as follows:

Dalai Buddha Panchen Master

Since liberation, our Labrang Monastery has received unprecedented care. The monastery is protected by the People's Government, and Daji Daxiongzuo has been appointed Deputy County Head of Xiahe County People's Government. All the monks in the monastery were overjoyed to hear the news of Panchen Master's return to Tibet. This is another great happy event following the peaceful liberation of Tibet. On behalf of the 3,600 monks of Labrang Monastery, we sincerely congratulate you: in the future, under the leadership of the great people's leader Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, may the Tibetan people and all fraternal ethnic groups in the country unite more closely and work together to completely expel imperialist forces in Tibet, consolidate the southwest national defense, and build a new Tibet.

We wish you good health.

### 西北各族人民抗美援朝代表会议  致电达赖和班禅祝贺他们的亲密团结  甘肃拉卜楞寺院三千六百僧众的代表亦致电祝贺

1951-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    西北各族人民抗美援朝代表会议
    致电达赖和班禅祝贺他们的亲密团结
    甘肃拉卜楞寺院三千六百僧众的代表亦致电祝贺
    【新华社西安二十一日电】西北各族人民抗美援朝代表会议得知班禅额尔德尼离青海返回西藏的消息以后，十九日分别致电达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼，祝贺他们的亲密团结。
    （一）致达赖喇嘛的电文达赖喇嘛：
    我们在会议进行期间，欣闻你曾来电敦促班禅额尔德尼先生返藏，和班禅额尔德尼先生已动身返藏的喜讯。我们全体代表祝贺你与班禅额尔德尼先生在伟大领袖毛主席与中央人民政府领导下，加强西藏内部与国内各民族之间的亲密团结，彻底实现和平解放西藏办法的协议，驱除帝国主义侵略影响，建设繁荣幸福的新西藏。特电祝贺，并祝健康。
    （二）致班禅额尔德尼的电文班禅额尔德尼先生：
    我们来自西北五省一市的四百多名各族各界人民代表，在会议进行中，欣闻你动身返藏的喜讯，感到非常兴奋。我们坚决拥护你与达赖喇嘛的亲密团结，在各族人民伟大的领袖毛主席的英明领导之下，为执行和平解放西藏办法的协议，驱除帝国主义侵略影响，巩固国防和建设繁荣幸福的新西藏，为使西藏人民和全国人民一道走向光明的胜利的大道而奋斗。谨电致贺，并祝你胜利到达目的地。
    【新华社西宁二十一日电】班禅额尔德尼离青海返西藏之际，加木样财务管家郎哲等二十四人代表甘肃省拉卜楞寺院的三千六百僧众，致电达赖喇嘛和班禅大师，电文如下：达赖佛班禅大师
    解放以来，我们拉卜楞寺得到了前所未有的关怀，寺院受到人民政府的保护，达吉大襄佐荣任夏河县人民政府副县长。全寺僧众听到班禅大师返回西藏的消息后，欢欣鼓舞。这是继西藏和平解放后的又一大喜事。我们代表拉卜楞寺院三千六百僧众诚恳地祝贺您们：今后在伟大人民领袖毛主席和中央人民政府领导下，使西藏人民和国内各兄弟民族更加亲密团结，为彻底驱逐帝国主义在藏势力、巩固祖国西南国防、建设新的西藏而共同努力。
    祝您们身体健康。
