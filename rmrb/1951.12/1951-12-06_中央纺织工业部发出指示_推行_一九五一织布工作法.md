Title: Central Textile Industry Department Issues Instructions to Promote the "1951 Weaving Work Method"
Author:
Date: 1951-12-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

    Central Textile Industry Department Issues Instructions
    to Promote the "1951 Weaving Work Method"
    The Central People's Government Ministry of Textile Industry issued instructions on November 29 regarding the promotion of the "1951 Weaving Work Method." The instructions require the leading organs of the textile industry throughout the country to immediately cooperate with trade union organizations to study this work method and respond to Chairman Mao's call for increased production and frugality. The instructions point out that in promoting this work method, textile administrative departments in various regions should pay attention to continuing to research and improve the weaving work method, effectively organize technical personnel, rely on workers, and conduct measurement and research of new experiences at any time, so as to supplement the operational advantages of weaving workers in various regions into the "1951 Weaving Work Method." In order to smoothly promote the new work method and improve product quality, the instructions require textile administrative departments in various regions to attach importance to improving the work of weaving maintenance, preparation, and spinning workshops. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中央纺织工业部发出指示  推行“一九五一织布工作法”

1951-12-06
第2版()
专栏：

    中央纺织工业部发出指示
    推行“一九五一织布工作法”
    中央人民政府纺织工业部在十一月二十九日发出关于推行“一九五一织布工作法”的指示。指示要求全国各地纺织工业的领导机关立即协同工会组织职工学习这一工作法，响应毛主席增产节约号召。指示指出：在推广这一工作法时，各地纺织行政部门应注意继续研究和提高织布工作法，很好地组织技术人员，依靠工人，随时进行新经验的测定研究工作，以便将各地织布工人的操作优点补充到“一九五一织布工作法”中去。为着顺利推行新工作法和提高产品质量，指示要求各地纺织行政部门重视改进织布保全、准备和细纱等车间的工作。（新华社）
