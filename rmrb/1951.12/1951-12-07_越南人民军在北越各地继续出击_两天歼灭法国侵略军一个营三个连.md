Title: The Vietnamese People's Army Continues to Attack in Various Parts of North Vietnam, Annihilating One Battalion and Three Companies of French Invading Forces in Two Days
Author:
Date: 1951-12-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam: The Vietnamese People's Army continues to fiercely attack French invading forces in various parts of North Vietnam. On the morning of November 24th, the Vietnamese People's Army attacked two strongholds of the French invading forces on Highway 21, six kilometers south of Dutou City in Ha Dong Province. After only eleven minutes of combat, the People's Army annihilated two companies of the French army and seized a large number of weapons and ammunition. On November 25th, the Vietnamese People's Army annihilated a battalion of French invading forces in Xiuwu, 25 kilometers north of He Ping; and annihilated the 14th Assault Company of the French invading forces in Fuliu, 15 kilometers east of Fu An.

### 越南人民军在北越各地继续出击  两天歼灭法国侵略军一个营三个连

1951-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军在北越各地继续出击
    两天歼灭法国侵略军一个营三个连 【新华社五日讯】据越南通讯社北越消息：越南人民军继续在北越各地痛歼法国侵略军。十一月二十四日晨，越南人民军在河东省渡头市以南六公里第二十一号公路上，攻击法国侵略军的两个据点。仅仅经过十一分钟的战斗，人民军就歼灭了法军两个连，并缴获大量武器和弹药。十一月二十五日，越南人民军又在和平以北二十五公里的休武，歼灭了法国侵略军一个营；在福安以东十五公里的富流，歼灭了法国侵略军的第十四突击连。
