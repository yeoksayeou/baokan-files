Response to Readers' Criticisms and Suggestions

    Reply from Beijing Construction Engineering Company
    The Zhoukoudian Lime Factory of Yongmao Company was placed under the leadership of Beijing Construction Engineering Company in September of this year. The Zhoukoudian Lime Factory mining area, in the area outside of Longgushan, was designated by the Fangshan County People's Government in 1949. The Zhoukoudian Lime Factory has ceased mining limestone since September 20th of this year.

### 北京市建筑工程公司的答复

1951-12-19
第2版()
专栏：对人民日报读者批评建议的反应

    北京市建筑工程公司的答复
    永茂公司周口店石灰厂已经在今年九月划归北京市建筑工程公司领导。周口店石灰厂矿区，在龙骨山界外地区，是一九四九年房山县人民政府所指定。周口店石灰厂已在今年九月二十日起停止开采石灰石。
