Title: Government Administration Council Holds 116th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-12-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 21st—The 116th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held this afternoon. The meeting heard and approved the report made by Hu Yuzhi, Director of the Central People's Government Publishing Administration, on the First National Publishing Administration Conference, and the report made by Wang Kunlun, Political Committee Member of the Government Administration Council, on the review of various documents of the First National Publishing Administration Conference. It then passed the "Provisional Measures for the Import of Foreign Printed Matter," the "Instructions on Unifying the Use of Publicly Operated Letterpress Printing Plants' Productivity," and the "Decision on Establishing a National Newspaper and Book Distribution Network," and approved the "Provisional Regulations on the Administration of the Publishing and Distribution Industry of Books and Periodicals," and the "Provisional Measures for the Registration of Periodicals." The meeting also approved several personnel appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第一一六次会议

1951-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一一六次会议
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百一十六次政务会议于二十一日下午举行。会议在听取和批准了中央人民政府出版总署署长胡愈之所作的关于第一届全国出版行政会议的报告和政务院政务委员王昆仑所作的关于第一届全国出版行政会议各项文件审查的报告后通过了“国外印刷品进口暂行办法”、“统一运用公营铅印印刷厂生产力的指示”、“关于建立全国报纸书刊发行网的决定”，批准了“管理书刊出版业发行业暂行条例”、“期刊登记暂行办法”。会议并通过了任免案多起。
