Title: Commemorating the Second Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Issues a Notice on Carrying Out Propaganda Work
Author:
Date: 1951-12-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 19th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters issued a notice on December 18th regarding the propaganda work for the second anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The full text is as follows:

    The second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance is approaching. This association has decided to hold a week-long Sino-Soviet friendship propaganda campaign around this anniversary in various parts of the country in February 1952. The purpose of the propaganda week is to implement Chairman Mao's instruction to "unite well with our Soviet allies," strengthen the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, disseminate the advanced experience of Soviet economic construction, promote the movement of increasing production and practicing economy and ideological remolding among the people of our country, strengthen the understanding of socialism and internationalism among the masses of our people, and further develop the organization of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association on the basis of this propaganda. In order to achieve the above objectives, the content of the propaganda should focus on the following points: 1. Based on the facts of the past two years, publicize the development of the friendly alliance between the people of China and the Soviet Union and the great significance of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, and publicize the great significance of the existence of the Soviet Union to the world. 2. Introduce the achievements of Soviet socialist construction and the happy life of the people, introduce the communist political and spiritual outlook of the Soviet people, and point out that the happy life of the Soviet people today is the goal that the Chinese people will achieve in the future, and eliminate the misunderstandings of some people about socialism. 3. Publicize the advanced experience of the Soviet Union in building socialism and the results of applying these experiences in our country. The propaganda methods can be lectures, broadcasts, forums, oral propaganda, wall newspapers, picture exhibitions, slide shows, publication of pamphlets, articles and propaganda materials in newspapers and magazines, etc. Local branches should immediately begin preparing for the propaganda week for the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. According to the decision of the First National Congress, this propaganda work must be in-depth and specific, and closely integrated with the current needs and the political and ideological situation of the people. The headquarters will compile slides on the specific situation of a certain Soviet factory and the specific situation of a certain collective farm, as well as some written propaganda reference materials, and send them to local branches one after another. Local branches can also compile their own slides and picture exhibitions on Soviet socialist construction and people's lives. The number of such slides and pictures should not be too many, but should be few but refined, and the content should be specific and systematic; the text descriptions should be concise, lively, and clear. All propaganda materials should be subject to responsible review.

### 纪念中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年  中苏友协总会发出开展宣传工作的通知

1951-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年
    中苏友协总会发出开展宣传工作的通知
    【新华社十九日讯】中苏友好协会总会在十八日发出关于中苏友好同盟互助条约两周年宣传工作的通知，全文如下：
    中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年纪念日行将到来。本会决定在一九五二年二月围绕这个纪念在全国各地举行一次为期一周的中苏友好宣传。宣传周的目的是执行毛主席关于“好好地和我们的苏联盟友团结一致”的指示，加强中苏两国人民的友谊，传播苏联经济建设的先进经验，推进我国人民的增产节约和思想改造运动，加强我国人民群众对社会主义和国际主义的认识，并在这些宣传的基础上进一步发展中苏友好协会的组织。为了达到上述目的，宣传的内容应着重以下几点：一、根据两年来的事实，宣传中苏两国人民友好同盟关系的发展和中苏友好同盟互助条约的伟大意义，宣传苏联的存在对于全世界的伟大意义。二、介绍苏联社会主义建设的成就和人民的幸福生活，介绍苏联人民的共产主义的政治的和精神的面貌，指出苏联人民今天的幸福生活，就是将来中国人民所要达到的目标，消除一部分群众对于社会主义的误解。三、宣传苏联建设社会主义的先进经验及这些经验应用于我国的成果。宣传的方式，可采取讲演、广播、座谈、口头宣传、出壁报、办图片展览、放映幻灯电影、出版小册子、在报纸杂志上发表文章和宣传资料等。各地分会应即开始中苏友好同盟互助条约的签订二周年宣传周的准备工作。根据第一次全国代表会议的决定，此项宣传工作一定要深入具体，与目前需要和人民群众政治思想情况密切结合。总会方面将编制有关苏联某一工厂具体情况及某一集体农庄具体情况的幻灯片及一些文字宣传参考材料先后发给各地分会。各地分会也可自行编制有关苏联社会主义建设和人民生活的幻灯片和图片展览等。此种幻灯片和图片，种数不宜求多，要少而精，内容要具体、有系统；文字说明要简洁、生动、明白。所有宣传品都应经过负责的审查。
