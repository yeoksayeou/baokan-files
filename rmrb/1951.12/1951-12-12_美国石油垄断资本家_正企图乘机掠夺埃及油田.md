Title: American Oil Monopoly Capitalists Attempting to Seize the Opportunity to Plunder Egyptian Oil Fields
Author:
Date: 1951-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th—Amidst the rising tide of Egyptian opposition to British imperialism, American monopoly capital is seizing the opportunity to further infiltrate Egypt and plunder its national resources.

According to Agence France-Presse, New York, November 28th: Oil merchant McCarthy of the Texaco Oil Company of America, upon returning to New York from Cairo, stated that he had reached an agreement with the Egyptian National Petroleum Company to develop 2.5 million hectares of oil fields in the Sinai Desert. This oil field is located on the east coast of the Red Sea, extending 25 to 30 miles inland. McCarthy said that if the Egyptian parliament agrees to amend the law prohibiting foreigners from holding a majority of shares in certain Egyptian companies, he intends to purchase 51 percent of the shares and begin "developing" the region's oil resources.

### 美国石油垄断资本家  正企图乘机掠夺埃及油田

1951-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美国石油垄断资本家
    正企图乘机掠夺埃及油田
   【新华社十一日讯】在埃及反对英帝国主义的运动日益高涨声中，美国垄断资本正乘机力图进一步插足埃及，掠夺埃及的国家富源。
    据法新社纽约十一月二十八日电：美国德士古石油公司的油商麦克锡在自开罗返抵纽约时说，他已与埃及国家石油公司达成开采西奈沙漠上的二百五十万公顷油田的协定。那块油田是在红海东岸，向内地延伸二十五至三十英里。麦克锡说，假使埃及议会同意修改关于外国人不许在某些埃及公司拥有大部分股份的法律的话，他打算购买百分之五十一的股票，开始“开发”该区的石油资源。
