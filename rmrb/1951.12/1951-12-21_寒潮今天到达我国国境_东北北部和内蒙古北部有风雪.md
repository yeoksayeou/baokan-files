Title: Cold Wave Arrives in Our Country Today; Wind and Snow in Northeastern and Northern Inner Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1951-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 20th—The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a cold wave message at 10 PM on December 20th, stating that the cold wave will reach our country's border on the morning of December 21st. Northern Northeast (north of Harbin) and Northern Inner Mongolia will be affected by the cold wave on December 21st, with wind and snow.

### 寒潮今天到达我国国境  东北北部和内蒙古北部有风雪

1951-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    寒潮今天到达我国国境
    东北北部和内蒙古北部有风雪
    【新华社二十日讯】中央气象台十二月二十日晚十点发出寒潮消息，寒潮将在二十一日早晨到达我国国境。东北北部哈尔滨以北和内蒙古北部将在二十一日受到寒潮的侵袭，并有风雪。
