Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Sends Letter of Congratulations to Guo Moruo
Author:
Date: 1951-12-28
Page: 1

The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters Writes Letter of Congratulations to Guo Moruo

[This newspaper's dispatch] The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters sent a letter on the 26th to its vice president, Guo Moruo, to congratulate him on winning the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace." The original letter is as follows: Vice President Guo Moruo:

We are delighted to hear that you have won the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace." This is not only your glory, the glory of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, but also the glory of the peace-loving Chinese people. We extend our warmest congratulations to you, and wish you good health and further success in your work of defending Far Eastern and world peace and opposing wars of aggression!
Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters
December 26th

### 中苏友好协会总会写信向郭沫若致贺

1951-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会总会写信向郭沫若致贺
    【本报讯】中苏友好协会总会二十六日写信给该会副会长郭沫若，对他荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金表示祝贺。原信如下：郭沫若副会长：
    欣闻您荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，这不仅是您的光荣，全体中苏友好协会会员的光荣，而且是爱好和平的中国人民的光荣。我们特向您致热烈的祝贺，谨祝您健康，并祝您在保卫远东和世界和平、反对侵略战争的工作中获得进一步的成功！中苏友好协会总会十二月二十六日
