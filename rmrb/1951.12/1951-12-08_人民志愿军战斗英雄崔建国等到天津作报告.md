Title: People's Volunteer Army Combat Hero Cui Jianguo and Others Report to Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1951-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 6th:
    Cui Jianguo, a combat hero of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who participated in the National Day ceremony, and ten others arrived in Tianjin from Beijing on the 6th. Li Zhuchen, Vice Chairman of the Tianjin Anti-American and Aid-Korea Association, and more than 400 representatives from all walks of life went to the station to welcome them. Cui Jianguo and others will report the heroic deeds of the Volunteer Army to people from all walks of life and the troops in Tianjin; then they will go to Hebei, Pingyuan, Inner Mongolia and Chahar to make reports.

### 人民志愿军战斗英雄崔建国等到天津作报告

1951-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    人民志愿军战斗英雄崔建国等到天津作报告
    【新华社天津六日电】曾参加国庆节典礼的中国人民志愿军战斗英雄崔建国等十一人，六日由北京到达天津。前往车站欢迎者有天津市抗美援朝分会副主席李烛尘和各界代表四百多人。崔建国等这次来天津，将向各界人民和部队报告志愿军的英勇事迹；然后分别到河北、平原、内蒙和察哈尔等地作报告。
