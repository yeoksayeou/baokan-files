Title: CPPCC National Committee Participates in Three Great Movements Preparatory Committee; Organizes Land Reform Work Group to Go to Hankou
Author:
Date: 1951-12-13
Page: 1

    The Preparatory Committee for the Three Great Movements of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference recently organized a work group to participate in the land reform work in the Central South Region next spring. The group, led by Yang Hansheng, Qian Jiaju, Tian Han, Hu Sheng, and others, departed for Hankou on the 10th. The entire group consists of 982 people, including working cadres from the Central People's Government, working cadres from the central organs of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and people from all walks of life in large and medium-sized cities such as Beijing, Tianjin, Baoding, Taiyuan, and Shijiazhuang. Before their departure, the group members underwent a short period of study.

### 政协全国委员会参加三大运动筹委会  组织土地改革工作团赴汉口

1951-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会参加三大运动筹委会
    组织土地改革工作团赴汉口
    【新华社十二日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会参加三大运动筹备委员会最近组织的参加中南区明春土地改革工作的工作团，已在十日由阳翰笙、千家驹、田汉、胡绳等率领前往汉口。全团九百八十二人。团员包括中央人民政府的工作干部、各民主党派，各人民团体中央机关的工作干部和北京、天津、保定、太原、石家庄等大中城市的各界人士。团员们在出发前曾进行了短期的学习。
