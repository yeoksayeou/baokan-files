Title: Tikhonov and Nesmeyanov Send Congratulations to Guo Moruo on Winning the Stalin International Peace Prize
Author:
Date: 1951-12-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd:

    Tikhonov and Nesmeyanov
    Respectively Congratulate Guo Moruo on Winning the Stalin International Peace Prize
    The Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, Tikhonov, and the President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Nesmeyanov, have respectively sent congratulatory telegrams to Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, on winning the Stalin International Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations." Tikhonov's congratulatory telegram stated: "The Soviet Peace Committee extends its heartfelt congratulations to you on being awarded the Stalin International Peace Prize for your contribution to promoting international peace. Peace defenders around the world know that you are a tireless fighter for international peace and friendship. They will be delighted to hear that you have won this prize, which is linked to the name of the great peace standard-bearer, Stalin. I wish you good health and new great victories in the great struggle of the people of the world to defend peace." Nesmeyanov's congratulatory telegram stated: "The Soviet Academy of Sciences extends its warmest congratulations to you upon learning that you have been awarded the highest honor, the Stalin International Prize 'For Strengthening Peace Among Nations.' We admire your selfless struggle to safeguard international peace and friendship. We wish you good health and hope that you will achieve further success in your fruitful scientific and social activities."

### 吉洪诺夫和涅斯米扬诺夫  分别电贺郭沫若荣获斯大林国际和平奖金

1951-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    吉洪诺夫和涅斯米扬诺夫
    分别电贺郭沫若荣获斯大林国际和平奖金
    【新华社二十二日讯】苏联拥护和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫和苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫，顷分别电贺我国科学院院长郭沫若荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金。吉洪诺夫的贺电说：“苏联拥护和平委员会对于你因促进国际和平而荣获斯大林国际和平奖金致以衷心的祝贺。全世界和平保卫者都知道你是为国际和平与友谊而奋斗的永不疲劳的战士。他们听到你荣获这个与伟大的和平旗手斯大林的名字连在一起的奖金，将感到欣悦。谨祝你健康和在全世界人民保卫和平的伟大斗争中获得新的伟大的胜利。”涅斯米扬诺夫的贺电说：“欣闻你荣获最高的荣誉的奖金——‘加强国际和平’斯大林国际奖金，苏联科学院谨向你热烈祝贺。我们钦佩你为维护国际和平和友谊而作的忘我斗争。我们祝贺你健康并希望你在有成效的科学和社会活动中获得进一步的成功。”
