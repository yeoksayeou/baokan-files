Title: Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Central Committee Holds Meeting, Decides to Accept Resolutions of Cadre Conference
Author:
Date: 1951-12-14
Page: 1

　　Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party
　　Central Committee Holds Meeting
    Decides to Accept Resolutions of Cadre Conference
    [From Our Newspaper] In order to accept the various resolutions of the Sixth National Cadre Conference of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party and to arrange future work, the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party convened its Sixth First Plenary Session on the 5th. The Central Committee members of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party in Beijing who attended the meeting included Zhang Bojun, Huang Qixiang, Liu Chun, Xu Binru, and others.
    The meeting decided to accept and implement the following five resolutions passed by the Sixth National Cadre Conference: (1) Resolution on the Report on the Transmission of the Resolutions of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Political Report, Party Affairs Report, Party Constitution, and Resolution on Developing the Organization; (2) Accepting and separately processing fourteen proposals from the Sixth National Cadre Conference; (3) Electing Zhang Bojun and Peng Zemin as Chairman and Vice Chairman of the Central Committee respectively; (4) In accordance with the provisions of the Party Constitution, decided to establish the Central Executive Bureau, and elected Zhang Bojun, Peng Zemin, Huang Qixiang, Ji Fang, Liu Chun, Xu Binru, Guo Guanjie, Li Boqiu, Wang Shenlin, Guo Zechen, Yan Xinmin, He Shikun, Zhang Yunchuan, Wang Renxuan, and Li Shihao as fifteen members; (5) Expelling Zhang Juechu, the original alternate Executive Committee member, from the Party, removing Li Rucang, the original Supervisory Committee member, and suspending Zhu Jingtang from his duties as an alternate member of the Central Committee.
    The Central Executive Bureau of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party held its first meeting on the 12th, electing Huang Qixiang as Secretary-General, Ji Fang and Li Boqiu as Deputy Secretaries-General, Wang Shenlin as Minister of Organization, Yang Anping as Deputy Minister, Yan Xinmin as Minister of Propaganda, and Wang Yifan and Ye Yuexiu as Director and Deputy Director of the Secretariat, respectively.

### 中国农工民主党  中央委员会举行会议  决定接受干部会议各项决议

1951-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国农工民主党
　　中央委员会举行会议
    决定接受干部会议各项决议
    【本报讯】中国农工民主党中央委员会为接受中国农工民主党第六次全国干部会议的各项决议，和布置今后工作，在五日召开了第六届第一次中央委员会会议。出席会议的有中国农工民主党在北京的中央委员章伯钧、黄琪翔、刘春、徐彬如等。
    会议决定接受和实行第六次全国干部会议所通过的：（一）关于中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议决议的传达报告决议，政治报告、党务报告、党章、发展组织的决议等五项决议；（二）接受和分别处理第六次全国干部会议提案十四件；（三）推选章伯钧、彭泽民分任中央委员会正副主席；（四）依据党章之规定决定成立中央执行局，并推选章伯钧、彭泽民、黄琪翔、季方、刘春、徐彬如、郭冠杰、李伯球、王深林、郭则沉、严信民、何世琨、张云川、王人旋、李士豪等十五人为委员。（五）开除原候补执行委员张觉初党籍，罢免原监察委员李如苍，停止朱镜堂候补中央委员职权。
    中国农工民主党中央执行局在十二日举行了首次会议，推选黄琪翔为秘书长，季方、李伯球为副秘书长，王深林为组织部长，杨安平为副部长，严信民为宣传部长，王一帆、叶粤秀分任秘书处正副处长。
