Title: National Football Tournament Day 3 Results
Author:
Date: 1951-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 3rd. The results of the four matches on the third day of the 1951 National Football Tournament are as follows:

    Railway vs. Southwest – 5 to 0, Railway wins.
    East China vs. People's Liberation Army – 3 to 1, East China wins.
    Northeast vs. Northwest – 7 to 0, Northeast wins.
    North China vs. Central South – 4 to 1, North China wins.

### 全国足球比赛第三日结果

1951-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    全国足球比赛第三日结果
    【新华社天津三日电】一九五一年全国足球比赛大会第三日四场的比赛结果如下：
    铁路对西南——五比○，铁路胜。
    华东对解放军——三比一，华东胜。
    东北对西北——七比○，东北胜。
    华北对中南——四比一，华北胜。
