    Our Great Motherland
    The Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Xinjiang is actively engaged in production, basically achieving self-sufficiency and saving the country a significant amount of expenditure. This is the bumper wheat harvest of the troops stationed in Xinjiang, with the highest yield per mu reaching 800 jin.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    驻在新疆的中国人民解放军部队，积极地进行生产，基本上已达到自给自足，节省了国家大量的开支。这是驻新疆的部队丰收的小麦，每亩最高产量达八百斤。
