    Our Great Motherland
    Mare's milk is one of the beverages of the people of Inner Mongolia. After a short period of fermentation, it is not only easy to preserve, but also can treat gastrointestinal diseases. This is a scene of the Mongolian people of Beizi Temple in Xilin Gol League, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, collecting and storing mare's milk.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    马奶是内蒙古人民的饮料之一。它经过短期的发酵以后，不但容易保存，并且能治肠胃病。这是内蒙古自治区锡林郭勒盟贝子庙的蒙古族人民正在收储马奶的情形。
