Title: Moscow Newspapers Prominently Congratulate Guo Moruo and Others on Winning Awards
Author:
Date: 1951-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 21st. Moscow newspapers on the 21st—Stalin's 72nd birthday—prominently published on their front pages the decision of the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize Committee on awarding the 1951 prizes. The newspapers also published photos of Guo Moruo and other prize winners, and published editorials to warmly congratulate them. The Pravda editorial said: "Among the winners of the 'Strengthening International Peace' Stalin International Prize is Guo Moruo, the glorious son of the great Chinese people. Peace-loving people know that Guo Moruo is an enthusiastic fighter for peace. He tirelessly exposes the conspiracies of the enemies of peace. Everyone also knows about Guo Moruo's multifaceted activities in the construction of the People's Republic of China and his contributions to the establishment and development of the cultural undertakings of democratic China." The Komsomolskaya Pravda editorial said: "Guo Moruo is the loyal son of the great Chinese people, a great scientist and writer, a social and political activist, and an active participant in the national and international movement to defend peace. Guo Moruo's entire activity is an example of selfless service to the cause of international peace, progress, and freedom."

### 莫斯科各报以显著地位祝贺郭沫若等得奖

1951-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报以显著地位祝贺郭沫若等得奖 【新华社莫斯科二十一日电】莫斯科各报都在二十一日——斯大林七十二寿辰日以首页显著地位，刊载了“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会关于颁发一九五一年度奖金的决定。各报并刊登了郭沫若及其他得奖人的照片，并发表社论向他们热烈祝贺。真理报的社论说：“在‘加强国际和平’斯大林国际奖金的得奖人当中，有伟大的中国人民的光荣儿子郭沫若。爱好和平的人民都知道郭沫若是热情的和平斗士。他不倦地揭露和平的敌人的阴谋。大家也知道郭沫若在建设中华人民共和国中的多方面的活动和他在建立和发展民主中国的文化事业中的功绩。”共青真理报的社论说：“郭沫若是伟大的中国人民的忠实儿子、大科学家和作家、社会和政治活动家、本国和国际保卫和平运动的积极参加者。郭沫若的全部活动，是忘我地服务国际和平、进步和自由事业的榜样。”
