Title: In Commemoration of the Sixteenth Anniversary of the "December 9th" Movement, Capital Youth and Students Launch Various Activities
Author:
Date: 1951-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: Capital youth and students are launching various activities to commemorate the sixteenth anniversary of the "December 9th" Movement. The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League held a meeting in the auditorium of the Central Youth League School on the 2nd of this month to commemorate the sixteenth anniversary of the "December 9th" Movement. More than 1,000 student representatives and Youth League cadres from various middle schools in the capital attended. Zhang Dazhong, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, delivered a report on commemorating the "December 9th" Movement at the meeting.

To commemorate this significant festival and strengthen the ideological education of students combining with workers and peasants, the Beijing Municipal Student Union also organized twenty university students who had interned in factories and rural areas this summer to report their internship experiences to various middle schools. Many university and middle school students in the capital are also preparing to hold various gatherings centered on reviewing patriotic pledges to commemorate the sixteenth anniversary of the "December 9th" Movement.

### 纪念“一二、九”运动十六周年  首都青年学生展开各项活动

1951-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念“一二、九”运动十六周年
    首都青年学生展开各项活动
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：首都青年学生正展开纪念“一二、九”运动十六周年的各项活动。青年团北京市委员会已于本月二日在中央团校礼堂举行了纪念“一二、九”运动十六周年的会议，到有首都各中学学生会和青年团干部一千多人。青年团北京市委员会副书记张大中在会上作了关于纪念“一二、九”运动的报告。
    为纪念这一重大节日并加强学生与工农结合的思想教育，北京市学生联合会还组织了今年暑期到工厂、农村实习过的二十个大学生，分别到各中学报告自己的实习心得。首都许多大、中学校的学生，也正准备以检查爱国公约等为中心，举行各种集会，以纪念“一二、九”运动十六周年。
