Title: Jiaodong Our Army Captures Aoshanwei; Sui-Meng Local Corps Recovers Changhanying
Author:
Date: 1948-04-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, East China, April 4th: Delayed news: The people's armed forces of Jimo (East) County captured Aoshanwei (40 li east of Jimo, on the coast) on the evening of the 20th of last month, killing and wounding part of a company of the Qingdao Security 1st Brigade. The remaining enemies fled in disarray.

Xinhua News Agency, East China, April 4th: The Nanhai Sub-district (administering Jimo, Jiaoxian, Gaomi, and other counties) people's armed forces in Jiaodong captured a Chiang Kai-shek bandit fighter plane at 3:00 PM on the 27th of last month, capturing one pilot and six machine guns. The plane was No. 11 of the 1st detachment of the 27th squadron of the bandit air force's 5th brigade. It was flying from Weixian to Qingdao and was forced to land due to running out of fuel.

Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Sui, April 4th: The Sui-Meng Local Corps recovered Changhanying, a strategic point for Liangcheng, on March 27th. Most of the more than 300 defending troops of Fu bandit Men Shuhuai's unit were annihilated. Our troops also completely defeated the reinforcing troops of Fu bandit's 11th Replenishment Training Division and other units in Yaozi, Northeast Provincial City, 25 li west of Changhanying. In this battle, more than 330 enemy soldiers, including the regimental commander of the 1st Regiment of the 11th Replenishment Training Division, Duan Jintang, were killed or wounded, 96 enemies were captured, and 15 light and heavy machine guns and more than 100 long and short guns were seized.

Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, April 4th: From the 22nd to the 24th of last month, the local armed forces on the northern front of Central Hebei successively killed or captured more than 120 Chiang Kai-shek bandit soldiers who came out to harass us in Yanzhuang Houying east of Baoding, Hongcheng and Mao'erwan east of Xiong County, and the area west of Duliu in Tianjin West. They seized four machine guns and more than 70 rifles and horse rifles, and took advantage of the victory to recover the bandit strongholds of Beizhuotou, Taitou, Huangcha, and Nanbeibazhao villages west of Duliu.

### 胶东我军攻克鳌山卫　绥蒙地方兵团收复厂汉营

1948-04-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　胶东我军攻克鳌山卫
  　绥蒙地方兵团收复厂汉营
    【新华社华东四日电】迟到消息：即（墨）东县人民武装上月二十日晚攻克鳌山卫（即墨东四十里滨海），毙伤匪青岛保安一大队一中队一部，余敌狼狈逃窜。
    【新华社华东四日电】胶东南海分区（辖即墨胶县高密等县）人民武装上月二十七日下午三时缴获蒋匪战斗机一架，俘获驾驶员一名，机枪六挺。该机系匪空军五大队二十七中队一分队之第十一号机，由潍县飞往青岛途中因油尽被迫降落。
    【新华社晋绥四日电】绥蒙地方兵团于三月二十七日收复凉城屏障之厂汉营。守敌傅匪门树槐部三百余人大部被歼，我并在厂汉营西二十五里之东北省城窑子将援敌傅匪补训十一师等部全部打垮，是役共毙伤敌补训十一师一团团长段金堂以下三百三十多名，俘敌九十六名，缴获轻重机枪十五挺，长短枪百余支。
    【新华社晋察冀四日电】冀中北线地方武装上月二十二至二十四日，在保定东之阎庄后营，雄县城东之洪城、毛尔湾及津西独流以西地区，先后毙俘出扰之蒋匪军一百二十余名，缴获机枪四挺，步马枪七十余支，并乘胜收复独流西之匪据点北茁头、台头、黄岔及南北霸召等村。
