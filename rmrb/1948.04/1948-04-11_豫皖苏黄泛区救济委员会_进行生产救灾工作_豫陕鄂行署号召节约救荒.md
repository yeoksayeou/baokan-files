Title: Henan-Anhui-Jiangsu Yellow River Flood Area Relief Committee Conducts Production and Disaster Relief Work; Henan-Shaanxi-Hubei Administrative Office Calls for Frugality in Famine Relief
Author:
Date: 1948-04-11
Page: 2

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Su Lu Yu Wan, 9th] Since its establishment on March 10th, the Henan-Anhui-Jiangsu Liberated Area Yellow River Flood Area Relief Committee has actively led production and disaster relief work. In addition to the emergency relief of 400,000 catties of grain, which has been fully distributed to the disaster-stricken people of Xihua and Fugou, the following work is currently underway: 1. Distributing 30,000 catties of cotton to organize textile production. 2. Allocating 120,000 catties of grain to organize spring plowing and sowing. 3. Distributing a large amount of captured materials from the Pinghan Campaign to organize transportation and sales production. The first batch of 40,000 catties of salt was released at the end of February. 4. Dredging rivers within Xihua County, using work as relief. 5. Distributing 200,000 tree seedlings, calling for a "one person, one tree" campaign. 6. Calling for the large-scale planting of spring vegetables.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Yu-Shaan-E, 6th] The Henan-Shaanxi-Hubei Administrative Office has recently launched a frugality campaign to support the front lines and provide relief for the spring famine. Gao Yunsheng, Director of the Administrative Office, called for "striving to perform meritorious service for the people through frugality." Since late last month, each person in the Administrative Office has been saving five taels of wheat per day, picking wild vegetables every afternoon, and stipulating that all public and private surplus rice coupons must be handed over to the public. Food is prepared daily according to the number of people to avoid waste, the use of livestock is economized, grinding flour and transporting coal are solved independently, and streamlining and reorganization are carried out to save expenses.

### 豫皖苏黄泛区救济委员会　进行生产救灾工作　豫陕鄂行署号召节约救荒

1948-04-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　豫皖苏黄泛区救济委员会
  　进行生产救灾工作
  　豫陕鄂行署号召节约救荒
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖九日电】豫皖苏解放区黄泛区救济委员会于三月十日成立以来，积极领导生产救灾工作，除急赈粮四十万斤，已全部发给西华、扶沟灾民外，目前正进行：一、发放三万斤棉花，组织纺织。二、拨十二万斤粮食组织春耕播种。三、发放平汉战役中大批缴获物资，组织运销生产，首批食盐四万斤，已于二月底放出。四、疏浚西华境内河道，以工代赈。五、发放二十万株树苗，号召一人植一株运动。六、号召大量种植春菜。
    【新华社豫陕鄂六日电】豫陕鄂行署顷发动支援前线和救济春荒的节约运动。行署高芸生主任号召，“争取在节约中为人民立功”。上月下旬以来，行署每人每日已实行节约麦子五两，每日下午都拾野菜，并规定无论公私节余的米票，都须一律交公，每日按人作饭，避免浪费，节省牲口的使用，自行解决磨面驮煤；并进行整理编制，节省开支。
