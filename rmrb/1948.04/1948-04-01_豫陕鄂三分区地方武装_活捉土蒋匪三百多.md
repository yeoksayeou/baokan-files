Title: Local Armed Forces of the Yu-Shaan-E Border Region Capture Over 300 Local Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Alive
Author:
Date: 1948-04-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Yu-Shaan-E Front, 30th: A unit of the local armed forces of the Third Military Sub-district recently launched a surprise attack on Zhongshan Town, located 65 li southwest of Luoning Road, on the 21st. They largely annihilated the 2nd and 5th detachments of the local Chiang Kai-shek bandits. They captured alive Liu Yutang, commander of the 2nd bandit detachment, Zhang Guowei, deputy commander and battalion commander concurrently, and Li Jianzhong, chief of staff of the 5th detachment, along with 333 others. They killed and wounded over 30 bandits, seized three light machine guns, over a hundred long and short guns, and a portion of military supplies. Furthermore, on the 22nd, the local armed forces of the Fifth Military Sub-district captured 41 bandits in Yangzhai, 40 li southeast of Linru.

Xinhua News Agency, E-Yu-Wan Front, 30th: On the 11th, the Jianghan People's Liberation Army routed a battalion of the Chiang Kai-shek bandits' New 17th Brigade in Guchengfan, southwest of Suixian County. They killed and wounded over 30 bandit officers and soldiers, captured 75 enemies including a battalion commander, and seized two mortars, nine light machine guns, 74 long and short guns, and over 10,000 rounds of ammunition. On the same day, another unit of our army routed a battalion of the 490th Regiment of the 164th Brigade of the Chiang Kai-shek bandits' 56th Division in Shijiagang, southeast of Mianyang City. They killed and wounded over 20 enemy officers and soldiers, captured over 80 enemies including Battalion Commander Long Fei, and seized two machine guns, over 70 long and short guns, and many other military supplies. Furthermore, on the 1st, our unit destroyed the bandit township office in Tujiaomiao, Jingmen, capturing 14 enemies and seizing two light machine guns and 17 long and short guns.

### 豫陕鄂三分区地方武装　活捉土蒋匪三百多

1948-04-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　豫陕鄂三分区地方武装
  　活捉土蒋匪三百多
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线三十日电】三分区地方武装一部，二十一日奔袭洛宁路西南六十五里之中山镇，大部歼灭土蒋匪二、五两支队，生俘匪二支队长刘玉堂、副支队长兼大队长张国威，五支队参谋长李剑中以下三百三十三名，毙伤匪三十余名，缴获轻机枪三挺，长短枪百余支，及军用品一部。又：二十二日五分区地方武装在临汝东南四十里杨砦俘匪四十一名。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线三十日电】江汉解放军十一日在随县城西南古城畈，击溃蒋匪新十七旅一个营，毙伤匪官兵三十余名，俘敌营长以下七十五名，缴获迫击炮两门、轻机枪九挺、长短枪七十四支、子弹万余发。同日我军另部在沔阳城东南施家港，击溃蒋匪五十六师一六四旅四九○团一个营，计毙伤敌官兵二十余名、俘营长龙飞以下八十余名，缴获机枪两挺、长短枪七十余支，其他军用品甚多。又我部一日摧垮荆门菟角庙匪乡公所，俘敌十四人，缴轻机枪二挺、长短枪十七支。
