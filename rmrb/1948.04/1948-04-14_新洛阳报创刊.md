Title: New Luoyang Daily Inaugurated
Author:
Date: 1948-04-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline, Yu-Shaan-E, 12th: The "New Luoyang Daily" was inaugurated in Luoyang City on the 9th. The inaugural issue was snapped up by citizens in just half a morning. Furthermore, the Luoyang branch of this agency has also been established and has begun submitting稿 (gao3).

### 新洛阳报创刊

1948-04-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　新洛阳报创刊
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线十二日电】“新洛阳报”九日在洛阳市创刊。创刊号仅半个早晨就被市民争购一空。又本社洛阳支社亦已成立，开始发稿。
