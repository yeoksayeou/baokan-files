Title: Harbin-Jilin Railway Repaired and Open to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1948-04-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 6th. The Harbin-Jilin Railway was officially opened to traffic on the 1st of this month, and the journey takes 20 hours. The Jilin-Meihekou railway line is also being rushed for repairs and is expected to be completed within the month. The direct railway line connecting North and South Manchuria, from Harbin via Jilin and Meihekou, directly to Tonghua, is expected to be open to traffic by the end of this month or the beginning of next month.

### 哈尔滨到吉林铁路修好通车

1948-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　哈尔滨到吉林铁路修好通车
    【新华社东北六日电】哈尔滨至吉林铁路已于本月一日正式通车，二十小时可达。吉林至梅河口线铁路亦正大力赶修中，预定月内竣工。自哈尔滨经吉林、梅河口，直达通化之南、北满直接联系铁路线，本月底或下月初即可通车。
