Title: French Communist Party Demands Dissolution of Fascist Organizations, Establishment of Democratic Coalition Government
Author:
Date: 1948-04-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th. Paris dispatch: The Central Committee of the French Communist Party convened a meeting from the 15th to the 16th of this month and passed important resolutions. Regarding domestic policy, the resolution demands the immediate organization of a democratic coalition government with the participation of the Communist Party, the dissolution of De Gaulle's fascist organization "Rally of the French People," and firmly advocates adjusting wages, lowering prices, reducing military spending, and reorganizing the army. Regarding foreign policy, the resolution advocates abolishing all unequal treaties and agreements signed with imperialist countries, demands the re-establishment of commercial relations with Southeastern Europe, the liberation of colonial peoples, and the termination of the war of aggression against Vietnam.

### 法共要求解散法西斯组织　成立民主联合政府

1948-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　法共要求解散法西斯组织
  　成立民主联合政府
    【新华社陕北二十日电】巴黎讯：法共中央委员会于十五日至十六日在此间召开会议，通过了重要决议，在对内政策方面，决议要求立即组织有共产党参加的民主的联合政府，解散戴高乐的法西斯组织“法国人民联盟”，并坚主调整薪金，降低物价，减少军费及整编军队。在对外政策方面，决议主张废除与帝国主义国家所签订的一切不平等条约与协定，要求与东南欧重建商业关系，解放殖民地人民，并终止对越南的侵略战争。
