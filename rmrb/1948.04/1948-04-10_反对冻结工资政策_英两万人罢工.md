Title: 20,000 British Workers Strike Against Wage Freeze Policy
Author:
Date: 1948-04-10
Page: 2

　　Against the Wage Freeze Policy
  　20,000 British Workers Strike
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 6th: London dispatch: Over 22,000 workers in the British automobile industry went on strike on the 2nd, marking the beginning of the first strike against the government's wage freeze policy.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 5th: Paris dispatch: Thirty-six branches of the Workers' Factory Management Committee recently held meetings here to prepare for a national congress of branch representatives to be held at the Winter Sports Palace on April 4th. According to the newspaper *L'Humanité*, the large number of representatives attending these meetings demonstrates the full determination of the Factory Management Committee, led by the General Confederation of Labour, to carry out daily struggles, defend workers' demands, and strive for democracy and national independence. The meetings mentioned above were met with various obstructions from the factory owners, but thousands of worker representatives from France's important industries still successfully convened the congress.

### 反对冻结工资政策　英两万人罢工

1948-04-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　反对冻结工资政策
  　英两万人罢工
    【新华社陕北六日电】伦敦讯：英汽车工业二万二千余工人于二日罢工，反对政府冻结工资政策的第一次罢工于是开始。
    【新华社陕北五日电】巴黎讯：工人工厂管理委员会三十六个分会，最近在此纷纷举行会议，准备于四月四日在冬季运动场召开全国分会代表大会。人道报说：出席这些会议的代表为数极多，表明总工会领导的工厂管理委员会，有充分决心进行日常斗争，保卫工人要求，争取民主和国家的独立。按上述会议曾受到厂主们的百般阻挠，但数千名代表法国重要工业的工人代表，仍胜利召开了议会。
