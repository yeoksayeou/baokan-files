Date: 1948-04-14
Page: 1

　　A Mirror
    ——An Excerpt from "Stalin on Leadership and Inspection"
    Please consider the second type of worker; I am referring to those who talk emptily, and I might say, those who sincerely talk emptily. (Laughter) They are sincere and loyal to the Soviet regime, but they lack the ability to lead and organize anything. Last year, I spoke with such a comrade. This comrade is very respectable, but he is an incorrigible empty talker, capable of drowning any lively matter in empty rhetoric. Consider this conversation:
    I asked: How is the sowing going in your area?
    He replied: Regarding the sowing, Comrade Stalin, we have mobilized! (Laughter)
    I asked: So, what then?
    He replied: We have directly raised the question! (Laughter)
    I asked: And what happened afterwards?
    He replied: Our work has a turning point, Comrade Stalin, there will soon be a turning point! (Laughter)
    I asked: Exactly how?
    He replied: We are making progress! (Laughter)
    I asked: But exactly how is the sowing going in your area? (Laughter)
    He replied: The sowing in our area is, for the time being, in complete disarray, Comrade Stalin! (Laughter throughout the hall)
    This is the face of the empty talker. They have mobilized, they have directly raised the question, they have both a turning point and progress, yet the matter ultimately remains stalled.

### 一面镜子——节录“斯大林论领导与检查”的一节

1948-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　一面镜子
    ——节录“斯大林论领导与检查”的一节
    请到第二类工作人员，我所指的是一种说空话的人，我可以说，这是些诚恳的说空话的人。（笑声）是诚恳的，是忠于苏维埃政权的人，但没有能力来领导，没有能力来组织什么事情。去年我曾经和这样一个同志谈过话。这个同志是很可尊敬的一个同志，但是他是一个不可救药的说空话的人，他能够把任何一件生动活泼的事情淹没在空谈中去。请看这篇谈话：
    我问：你们那里的播种事情，办得怎样呢？
    他答：关于播种的事情吗？斯大林同志，我们已经动员起来了。（笑声）
    我问：那末，又怎么办呢？
    他答：我们曾经直截了当的提出了问题。（笑声）
    我问：那末，后来又怎么办呢？
    他答：我们的工作有转变，斯大林同志，很快就会有转变。（笑声）
    我问：究竟怎样呢？
    他答：在我们那里有着进步。（笑声）
    我问：可是，究竟你们那里的播种事情办得怎样？（笑声）
    他答：我们那里的播种事情，暂时毫无头绪哩，斯大林同志。（全场大笑）
    这就是说空话人的面目。他们动员起来了，直截了当的提出了问题，他们既有转变又有进步，而事情终究还是停顿着。
