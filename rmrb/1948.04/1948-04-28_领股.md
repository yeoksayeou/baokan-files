Title: Claim Shares

Author:

Date: 1948-04-28
Page: 1

    Notice regarding comrades Niu Zhanying, Cui Zhang, Qiu Shifang, Geng Zhen, Xu Wenqi, Li Zeng, and Guo Xianjun, Gao Zenglu: The principal and interest of your shares in the Qicun Public Coal Mine of Lincheng County were returned to the Gaoyi Industrial and Commercial Bureau last summer and then transferred to us in December. Since you have not inquired about them, please come to claim them before the end of April. If you fail to do so, they will be turned over to the public. This is a special announcement.
        Xingtai City Industrial and Commercial Bureau

    领股
