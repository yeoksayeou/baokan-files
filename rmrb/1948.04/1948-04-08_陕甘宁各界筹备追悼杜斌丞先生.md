Title: Shaanxi, Gansu, Ningxia Circles Prepare to Commemorate Mr. Du Bin-cheng

Author:

Date: 1948-04-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 6th. Mr. Du Bin-cheng, a famous educator in the Northwest, was murdered by the Chiang Kai-shek-Hu Zongnan bandit gang last October. Lin Boqu, Chairman of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary of the CCP Northwest Bureau, Ma Mingfang, Deputy Secretary, Zhao Shoushan, Deputy Commander of the Northwest People's Liberation Army, Li Furen, President of Yan'an University, He Liancheng, Director of the Border Government Education Department, and famous figures from Suide and Mizhi such as Ma Jichuan, Gao Yuting, Wang De'an, Zhang Zheqing, and Ai Bingqing are preparing for a memorial service. The Bin-cheng Library has been established in Mizhi to collect Mr. Du's treasured books, letters, and manuscripts for permanent commemoration. Mr. Du Bin-cheng was from Mizhi, a standing committee member and Northwest Special Commissioner of the China Democratic League. He was a typical representative of the China Democratic League, bravely fighting against the imperialist Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang and the feudal system with integrity.

### 陕甘宁各界筹备追悼杜斌丞先生

1948-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　陕甘宁各界筹备
  　追悼杜斌丞先生
    【新华社西北六日电】西北著名教育家杜斌丞先生，于去年十月横遭蒋胡匪帮凶杀，陕甘宁边区政府主席林伯渠、中共西北局书记习仲勋、副书记马明芳、西北人民解放军副司令赵寿山、延大校长李敷仁、边府教育厅长贺连城、及绥米各界名流马济川、高愉庭、王德安、张哲卿、艾秉卿等正筹备追悼大会。并已在米（脂）成立斌丞图书馆，搜集杜氏生前珍藏之图书、书信、手扎等永留纪念。杜斌丞先生米脂人，为民盟中常委及西北特派员，是民盟中向帝国主义蒋家匪帮及封建制度勇敢战斗有气节的典型代表。
