Title: Our Northwest Army Advances Towards Yongshou County; An Enemy Youth Army Regiment Annihilated; Two Enemy Squadrons Annihilated East of Shashi in Central Hubei
Author:
Date: 1948-04-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, April 21st: The People's Liberation Army, having crossed the Jing River, continues its westward offensive. On the evening of the 18th, a unit of our army completely annihilated the 3rd Regiment of the Chiang bandit Youth Army 203rd Division, which had just been transferred from Xi'an, at Changning Town, 40 li east of Yongshou. At the same time, over 300 local Chiang bandits were also wiped out. Another unit of our army is advancing towards Bin County, a vital point on the Xi'an-Lanzhou highway. More than 100 personnel of the puppet Baojia police and standing self-defense force stationed at Shidian Town, 30 li northeast of the county seat, surrendered to our army on the 18th. We captured one light machine gun and over 90 rifles. At the moment, our army's powerful offensive is continuing to develop.

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, April 21st: A unit of the local armed forces of a sub-district of the Jianghan Military Region besieged the enemy stronghold at Sanyi River, 40 li east of Shashi, on the 7th. After eleven hours of fighting, all the defending local Chiang bandits, two squadrons strong, were annihilated. Over 60 bandits were killed or wounded, and over 60 bandits, including a squadron leader and below, were captured alive. One light machine gun, 78 rifles and various amounts of ammunition were seized.

Xinhua News Agency, East China, April 21st: The coastal local armed forces have launched continuous attacks to protect the spring ploughing. The Donghai Independent Battalion has been continuously clearing out the puppet "Homecoming Corps" on both sides of the Longhai Railway, and has crushed the enemy's and puppet's grain-grabbing "mopping-up" operations in Xinpu, Haizhou and A'hu. The Zhuting (formerly Ganyu) Independent Battalion raided the coastal puppet bandits, wiping out two squads belonging to the 11th Company of the 3rd Brigade of the puppet Salt Police. The Linyi Independent Regiment has been squeezing the enemy and puppets from all directions, and by the 8th, it had recovered important enemy and puppet strongholds such as Tangtou and Taqiao northeast of Linyi, completely eliminating the enemy and puppets in the Tangtou and Hongrui districts. In the above battles, a total of 165 bandits were killed or wounded, and more than 20 people, including Zhang Shaoji, battalion commander of the 83rd Division, and Liu Jinyi, machine gun and artillery company commander, were captured alive.

### 西北我军挺向永寿＠县　匪青年军一个团被歼  鄂中沙市以东歼敌两个中队

1948-04-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　西北我军挺向永寿＠县
  　匪青年军一个团被歼
    鄂中沙市以东歼敌两个中队
    【新华社西北二十一日电】渡越泾河之解放军继续向西进攻。十八日晚我军一部于永寿东四十里之常宁镇，全部歼灭刚由西安调来之蒋匪青年军二○三师第三团，同时并歼灭土蒋匪三百余名。我军另一部正向西（安）兰（州）公路要冲ｂīｎ＠①县城挺进。驻守该城东北三十里世店镇之伪保警及常备自卫队百余人，于十八日向我投降。我缴获轻机枪一挺，步枪九十余支。刻我军强大攻势正继续发展中。
    【新华社中原前线二十一日电】江汉军区某分区地方武装一部，七日围攻沙市以东四十里之三义河敌据点，经十一小时战斗，全歼守敌土蒋匪两个中队，毙伤匪六十余名，生俘匪中队长以下六十余名，缴获轻机枪一挺，长短枪七十八支，弹药一部。
    【新华社华东二十一日电】滨海地方武装连续出击，掩护春耕。东海独立营连日清剿陇海路两侧伪“还乡团”，并粉碎新（浦）海（州）阿（湖）之敌伪抢粮“扫荡”。竹庭（原赣榆）独立营奔袭沿海伪匪，歼伪盐警三大队十一中队所属二个班。临沂独立团纵横挤压敌伪，迄八日止已收复临沂东北汤头、塔桥等匪重要据点，将汤头、洪瑞两区敌伪完全肃清。以上战斗，共毙伤匪一百六十五名，活捉八十三师营长张绍基、机炮连长刘近义等二十余名。
