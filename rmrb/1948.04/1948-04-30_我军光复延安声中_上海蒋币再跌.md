Title: Amidst News of Our Army's Recapture of Yan'an, Shanghai's Kuomintang Currency Plummets Again
Author: 
Date: 1948-04-30
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th—Shanghai dispatch: Amidst the news of the People's Liberation Army's recapture of Yan'an, Shanghai's financial market became even more chaotic. On the 26th, the U.S. dollar soared to 780,000 Kuomintang currency, and on the 27th, it reached a new high of 800,000, an increase of almost three times compared to the highest exchange rate of 220,000 in January. Gold also rebounded to 42.2 million per tael on the 27th, and various securities also rose.

### 我军光复延安声中  上海蒋币再跌

1948-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    我军光复延安声中
    上海蒋币再跌
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】沪讯：在人民解放军光复延安声中，上海金融市场更形混乱。二十六日美钞涨达蒋币七十八万元，二十七日更达八十万元的新高度，较一月份的最高比价二十二万元，上涨几达三倍。黄金每两二十七日亦回涨到四千二百万元，各项证券亦均上升。
