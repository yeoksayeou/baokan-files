Title: Nationwide General Strike of Millions of Workers in Italy at One O'Clock
Author:
Date: 1948-04-17
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 15th. Rome dispatch: A one-hour general strike of millions of workers across Italy, protesting the atrocities of the reactionaries in Sicily, was held from 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM on the 12th. This general strike was decided by the Executive Committee of the Italian General Confederation of Labour at a one-day meeting in Palermo. It demonstrates the strength of the People's Democratic Front among workers and is a powerful response to the killing of 36 labor leaders in Sicily within two years.

### 全意数百万工人总罢工一点钟

1948-04-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　全意数百万工人
  　总罢工一点钟
    【新华社陕北十五日电】罗马讯：抗议反动派在西西里暴行的全意数百万工人一小时总罢工，已于十二日上午十时到十一时举行。这次总罢工为意总工会执委会一日巴勒摩会议所决定，它表示了人民民主阵线在工人中的力量，并且是对两年内杀害西西里三十六名工人领袖的有力回答。
