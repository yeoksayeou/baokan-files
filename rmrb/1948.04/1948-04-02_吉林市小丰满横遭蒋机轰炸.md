Title: Xiaofengman in Jilin City Suffers Bombing by Chiang Kai-shek's Planes
Author:
Date: 1948-04-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 30th. Xiaofengman in Jilin City and the city area have been indiscriminately bombed by American-made Chiang Kai-shek planes, resulting in the deaths and injuries of more than 40 peaceful residents. On the 21st of this month, an American-made B-24 bandit plane intruded into Xiaofengman and dropped heavy and incendiary bombs on the workers' residential area, injuring 21 people and destroying five workshops. The plane then intruded into the Lianhua Pond area of Jilin City and dropped several bombs, massacring 14 of our compatriots and injuring more than 10. Our democratic government is actively providing relief to the victims. When old Mrs. Hu's hand was injured by the bombing, our soldiers sent her to the hospital. She said excitedly, "Children, you must avenge this old woman!"

### 吉林市小丰满横遭蒋机轰炸

1948-04-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　吉林市小丰满横遭蒋机轰炸
    【新华社东北三十日电】吉林市区及小丰满横遭美制蒋机滥施轰炸，死伤和平居民四十余人。本月二十一日美制Ｂ２４匪机一架，先侵入小丰满投重磅炸弹及燃烧弹于工人住宅区，炸伤二十一人，五幢工房亦被烧毁。该机旋又侵入吉市莲花池一带投弹多枚，屠杀我同胞十四人，伤十余人，我民主政府正积极救济被害同胞。当胡家老太太手被炸伤，我军战士送她入医院时，她激动的说：“孩子们可要为我孤老婆子报仇”。
