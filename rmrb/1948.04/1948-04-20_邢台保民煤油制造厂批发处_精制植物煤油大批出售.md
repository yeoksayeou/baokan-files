Recently, sales have been increasing daily, and supply cannot meet demand. In the spirit of serving the people wholeheartedly and to thank all users, our factory is striving for excellence in its products, manufacturing in large quantities, striving for self-sufficiency in the liberated areas, and encouraging everyone to use our kerosene. We are selling at a particularly low price. If you wish to purchase, please come to No. 7, Daomen, Dongyang City, Nankai, Xingtai as soon as possible and do not miss this opportunity.

　　邢台保民煤油制造厂批发处
  　精制植物煤油大批出售
