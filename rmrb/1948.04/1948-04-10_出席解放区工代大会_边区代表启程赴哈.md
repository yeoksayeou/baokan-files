Title: Representatives from the Border Region Set Off for Harbin to Attend the Liberated Area Workers' Congress
Author:
Date: 1948-04-10
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jinji Luyu, 7th: The delegation of eighteen representatives from the Jinji Luyu Liberated Area to attend the Liberated Area Workers' Congress set off on the morning of the 5th. The delegation is led by Li Xian, Standing Committee member of the Border Region Federation of Trade Unions and Director of the Taiyue District Trade Union, and Shen Menghua, Director of the Changzhi City Trade Union in Taihang. The representatives include model worker of military industry Zhen Rongdian, model worker of the clothing factory Zhang Changyin, and worker representatives from various industries such as military industry, railways, post office, printing, coal industry, matches, cigarettes, rural areas, and worker representatives from cities such as Linqing and Xingtai. Before departure, the Federation of Trade Unions held a six-day symposium to study the Xinhua News Agency's "February 7th" editorial, reviewed the past work of the trade union, and the representatives fully exchanged opinions, and prepared various photos, charts and publications. The CPC Jinji Luyu Central Bureau and the Federation of Trade Unions also hosted a banquet for the representatives before their departure.

### 出席解放区工代大会　边区代表启程赴哈

1948-04-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　出席解放区工代大会
  　边区代表启程赴哈
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫七日电】晋冀鲁豫解放区出席解放区工大代表团一行十八人已于五日晨启程。该团由边区总工会常委兼太岳区工会主任李仙，及太行长治市工会主任申梦华率领。代表中有军工劳动英雄甄荣典，被服工厂劳动英雄张长印等，包括军工、铁路、邮政、印刷、煤业、火柴、纸烟、农村等各业工人代表，及临清、邢台等城市的工人代表。出发前曾由总工会召开座谈会六天，学习了新华社的“二七”社论，检讨了过去的工会工作，各代表都充分交换了意见，并准备了各种照片图表与出版物。中共晋冀鲁豫中央局与总工会并为各代表设宴饯行。
