Title: Striding Forward Across the Xi'an-Lanzhou Highway: Northwest We Capture Linyou and Two Other Cities

Author: 
Date: 1948-04-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, Urgent Telegram, 23rd: The People's Liberation Army, striding forward across the Xi'an-Lanzhou Highway, captured the three county towns of Linyou and Fufeng in Shaanxi, and Lingtai in Gansu, on the 21st and 22nd. The defending enemies fled in panic. Among them, over 400 fleeing enemies from the puppet police force of Linyou, and the 1st Battalion of the puppet Shaanxi 9th Administrative Region Security Regiment, were completely routed by our forces south of the city. We captured over 150 enemy soldiers, seized 2 light machine guns, over 100 rifles and carbines, a radio station, over 200 *shi* of grain, and a quantity of ammunition. Currently, our army is vigorously expanding its offensive.

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 23rd: A unit of the Northwest People's Liberation Army captured Yongle Town, located at the Shaanxi-Gansu border north of @ County, on the afternoon of the 21st. The 700-strong bandit troops of Ma Bufang fled in disarray. Furthermore, in the battle to liberate Changwu on the 21st, we captured over 270 local Chiang bandits, including the head of the puppet Public Security Bureau, seized 1 light machine gun, over 280 rifles and carbines, a radio station, 2,000 *shi* of grain, and a quantity of other military supplies.

### 越过西兰公路大踏步挺进  西北我克麟游等三城

1948-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    越过西兰公路大踏步挺进
    西北我克麟游等三城
    【新华社西北二十三日急电】解放军越过西兰公路大踏步向前挺进，二十一、二十二两日连克陕西之麟游、扶风与甘肃之灵台三县城，守敌均惊惶逃窜。其中麟游逃敌伪保警队、伪陕西第九行政区保安团第一大队等部四百余人，在城南被我全部击溃。计生俘敌一百五十余名，缴轻机枪两挺，长短枪百余支，电台一部，粮食二百余石，弹药一部。现我军正猛烈扩张攻势中。
    【新华社西北二十三日电】西北人民解放军一部于二十一日午攻克＠县以北陕甘交界之永乐镇，守敌马步芳匪部七百余狼狈溃逃。又我二十一日解放长武之役，俘伪公安局长以下土蒋匪二百七十余人，缴轻机枪一挺，长短枪二百八十余支，电台一部，粮食两千石及其他军用物资一部。
