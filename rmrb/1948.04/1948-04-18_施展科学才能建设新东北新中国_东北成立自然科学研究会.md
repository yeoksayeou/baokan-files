Title: Exert Scientific Talents to Build a New Northeast and a New China: Northeast Establishes Natural Science Research Association
Author:
Date: 1948-04-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 16th: The Northeast Natural Science Research Association, initiated by more than 80 natural science workers in the Northeast Liberated Areas, including Li Fuchun, Wang Shoudao, Chen Yu, and Shao Shiping, held a preparatory meeting on the 8th of this month in the auditorium of the Political Committee's Economic Committee. Shao Shiping, Chen Kangbai, Bai Xiqing, Wei Zhenwu, Jiang Zemin, Liu Chengdong, and Yang Jin were elected as seven standing committee members, with Shao Shiping and Chen Kangbai as chairman and vice-chairman respectively, and Yang Jin as secretary. It was also decided to initiate the preparation of branch associations or groups in various provinces, cities, mines, factories, and schools to develop membership. In the future, the general association, branch associations, and groups will carry out the following tasks according to specific conditions: organizing scientific lecture meetings; collecting various scientific books, magazines, newspapers, etc., and establishing library reading rooms; holding scientific regular meetings; holding scientific exhibitions; and publishing scientific publications. Before the publication is established, a special column will be opened in the "Northeast Economy" magazine to publish members' scientific theories, technical research, creative inventions, etc.

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 16th: More than 80 initiators of the Northeast Natural Science Research Association recently published an article entitled "The Initiation of the Northeast Natural Science Research Association." The original text is as follows: "At present, the great patriotic liberation war of the Chinese people has achieved an unprecedented victory at a historical turning point. Chiang Kai-shek's defeat is already set, the liberation of the whole country is in sight, and the complete liberation of the Northeast is not far off. Therefore, a new historical task has been put before our natural scientists and scientific workers.

The Northeast Liberated Area has vast rural areas and cities, fertile land and abundant resources, a modern industrial base and huge amounts of electricity, more than 40 million liberated people who have stood up, and the strong leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the democratic government. It is now the time to develop production, prosper the economy, greatly support the war, completely eliminate the rule of Chiang and the US, and build the foundation of a new China. This glorious historical mission requires our natural scientists to participate enthusiastically, bravely shoulder their responsibilities, exert their talents, and serve the people. This is a good opportunity. In view of this, we have initiated the organization of the "Northeast Natural Science Research Association" to facilitate close contact and joint discussion among all natural scientists. Whether they are natural scientists working in office or not, whether they are natural scientists in the liberated areas or not, whether they are Chinese natural scientists or not, or whether they are foreign natural scientists, we sincerely welcome those who are willing to be with the people to build a new Northeast and a new China. Natural scientists who are devoted to the people's cause, unite and organize! Let us strive to build a new China!" ### 施展科学才能建设新东北新中国  东北成立自然科学研究会

1948-04-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　施展科学才能建设新东北新中国
    东北成立自然科学研究会
    【新华社东北十六日电】东北解放区各界自然科学工作者李富春、王首道、陈郁、邵式平等八十余人发起之东北自然科学研究会，于本月八日假政委会经济委员会礼堂成立筹备会，选出邵式平、陈康白、白希清、魏震五、江泽民、刘成栋、杨晋等七同志为常务委员，邵式平、陈康白为正副主任委员，杨晋为秘书。并决定在各省市及矿山、工厂、学校即发起筹备组织分会或小组，发展会员。总会、分会、小组今后将根据具体条件进行以下各种工作：组织科学讲演会；搜集各种科学图书、杂志、报章等，设立图书馆阅览室；召开科学例会；开科学展览会；出版科学刊物，该刊未创办前在“东北经济”杂志上辟专栏，以发表会员之科学理论、技术研究、创作发明等。
    【新华社东北十六日电】东北自然科学研究会发起人八十余人顷发表“东北自然科学研究会的发起”一文。原文如下：“目前中国人民伟大的爱国解放战争已取得了历史转折点的空前胜利，蒋介石的败局已定，全国解放在望，完全解放东北已经是指顾不远的事情了。因此在我们自然科学者及科学工作者的面前，提出了新的历史任务。
    东北解放区有着广大的乡村和城市，有着沃野千里的土地和丰富的资源，有着现代化的工业基础和巨量的电力，有着四千多万解放中的翻身人民，更有着坚强的中国共产党与民主政府的领导，目前正是发展生产，繁荣经济，大量支援战争，完全消灭蒋美的统治，建设新中国基础的时候，这个光荣的历史使命要求我自然科学者们热烈参加，把自己应负的使命勇敢的肩负起来，施展雄才，服务人民，这是良好的机会。同人有鉴于此，特发起组织“东北自然科学研究会”，以便于全体自然科学者密切联系，共同研讨，不论在职工作的自然科学者，也不论不在职的自然科学者；不论解放区的自然科学者，也不论非解放区的自然科学者；不论中国的自然科学者，也不论外国的自然科学者，愿意和人民在一起建设新东北和新中国者，我们都竭诚欢迎。有志于人民事业的自然科学者们，团结起来，组织起来！为建设新中国而奋斗吧！”丁冬放、王首道、王斌、王勋、王文、王金栋、王逢原、王仲、白希清、江泽民、任弼绍、李富春、李逸民、李华、李资平、李汇川、吴正操、吴东、余光生、沈其震、沈毅、何长工、何纯勃、邵式平、武可元、祁竣、吴力永、吴锦、林山、林华、林里夫、周宣城、周建南、孟贵民、屈伯川、洪引、郝希英、夏耘、唐川、唐克、姜怀、韦涵光、陈郁、陈康白、陈先舟、陈凌峰、陈一凡、陈应中、陈剑飞、常乾坤、＠一凡、张克威、张珍、张定一、贺诚、程明升、黄逸峰、黄锡川、孙然、孙新才、孙鸿儒、孙德山、孙仪之、傅大陵、杨作材、杨晋、叶林、阎沛霖、阎顾川、赵濯华、齐铭、葱延、刘向之、刘成栋、刘咸一、刘善本、刘吃夫、钱志道、魏震五、魏兆麟、聂春荣、韩振纪、饶斌、罗叔章、罗日运。
