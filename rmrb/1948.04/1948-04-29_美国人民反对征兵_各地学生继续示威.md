Title: American People Oppose Conscription; Students Continue Demonstrations in Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1948-04-29
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 26th: New York dispatch: "Oppose Truman's conscription and military training" and "Support Wallace's campaign" have become the unanimous calls of the broad masses of the American people. More than 2,400 citizens of Columbus, Ohio, recently enthusiastically participated in a meeting held by the "Columbus Wallace for President Committee." More than 2,000 college students in the city, disregarding the school authorities' order prohibiting Robertson from speaking at the school for Wallace, gathered on the streets outside the school and warmly cheered Robertson, who was speaking from a car. Robertson called on the students to fight against American fascism. On the 18th, the "United Student Committee for Wallace's Election" held a meeting in New York, with more than 700 student representatives from various places attending. The meeting demanded "defeating Truman's conscription plan" and passed the slogan: "We want schools, not barracks; we want books, not bullets."

### 美国人民反对征兵  各地学生继续示威

1948-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    美国人民反对征兵
    各地学生继续示威
    【新华社陕北二十六日电】纽约讯：“反对杜鲁门征兵与军训”，“支持华莱士竞选”，这已成为美国广大人民的一致呼声。俄亥俄州哥伦布城的二千四百余公民，日前热烈参加“哥伦布华莱士竞选总统委员会”所举行的会议。该城二千余大学生不顾学校当局禁止罗伯逊替华莱士在该校讲演的命令，群集校外街上，向车上演说的罗伯逊热诚欢呼。罗氏呼吁学生向美国的法西斯主义进行斗争。十八日，“各大学学生联合会拥护华莱士竞选委员会”在纽约集会，与会者有各地学生代表七百余人。会议要求“击败杜鲁门的征兵计划”；并通过口号说：“我们要学校不要兵营，要书本不要子弹”。
