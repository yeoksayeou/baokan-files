Title: Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1948-04-08
Page: 2

　　Important Correction
    According to a telegram from the General Office: In the congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee to the generals of the Southern Front People's Liberation Army on the great victory in Luoyang (see page one of this newspaper on March 18th), due to a temporary oversight, the names of Generals Chen Shiru and Tang Liang were omitted. We hereby solemnly correct this omission.

### 重要补正

1948-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　重要补正
    据总社来电：三月十六日所发中央致南线人民解放军诸将军庆贺洛阳大捷贺电（见三月十八日本报一版）中，因一时疏忽，漏播陈士渠、唐亮两将军名字，特此郑重补正。
