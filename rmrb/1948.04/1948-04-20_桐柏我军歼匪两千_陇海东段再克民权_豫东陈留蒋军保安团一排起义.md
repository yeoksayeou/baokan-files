Title: Our Army in Tongbai Annihilates 2,000 Bandits; We Recapture Minquan on the Eastern Section of the Longhai Railway; A Platoon of the Chiang Kai-shek Army Security Regiment in Chenliu, Eastern Henan, Uprising

Author: 
Date: 1948-04-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 17th. The People's Liberation Army's Tongbai Military Region troops, in coordination with the local armed forces of the Tenth Military Subdistrict, annihilated more than 2,000 bandits of the pseudo-Biyang Security Regiment on the 14th in the Shaobaisi area, 40 miles northwest of Biyang city, including the capture of more than 1,300 men alive, including the regiment commander Zhang Chengxiang, and killing or wounding more than 500.

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 17th. After capturing the Neihuang and Yejiagang railway stations on the 12th, a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army captured Minquan County and Minquan Railway Station on the night of the 13th, and the defending enemy fled. Now the Chiang Kai-shek bandit's transportation on the eastern section of the Longhai Railway has been interrupted, and our army is expanding its victories.

Xinhua News Agency, Su Lu Yu Wan, 18th. Belated news: A platoon of officers and soldiers, more than 40 men, from the First Battalion of the Sixth Regiment of the Henan Provincial Security Forces of the Chiang Kai-shek army stationed in Chenliu (southeast of Kaifeng) gloriously revolted on the evening of the 27th of last month under the leadership of Platoon Leader Zhang Yunheng and Deputy Platoon Leader Tian Fengcai, carrying five machine guns and 28 rifles. They dug through a hole in the wall and rushed out of the city, fighting with local bandits along the way. They arrived safely in a certain area of the Liberated Area on the 29th and were warmly welcomed by the local army and people.

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 18th. On the 6th, the Suzhong people's armed forces ambushed between Pingchao and Baipu towns on the Tong (Nantong) Ru (Gao) line, intercepting a Chiang Kai-shek bandit vehicle, capturing alive Su Xuecheng, a lieutenant colonel section chief of the military affairs office of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit's 21st Reorganized Division, Wu Datao, director of the 40th station of the United Logistics Headquarters, Chen Huo, a major director of the Taxation Bureau, Zhao Huang, a section chief, and Li Song, an educational commissioner of the bandit Executive Yuan, and other six people, killing and wounding Gu Mianbo, a colonel director of the military affairs office of the bandit's 21st Reorganized Division, and three military doctors, seizing 398 million yuan of Chiang Kai-shek currency. At the same time, the pillboxes and fortifications of the Jiang bandit strongholds of Wanzitou, Sishili, and Renjiakou along the line were destroyed.

### 桐柏我军歼匪两千  陇海东段再克民权　豫东陈留蒋军保安团一排起义

1948-04-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　桐柏我军歼匪两千
    陇海东段再克民权
  　豫东陈留蒋军保安团一排起义
    【新华社中原前线十七日电】解放军桐柏军区部队在十分区地方武装配合下，十四日在泌阳城西北四十里少白寺一带，歼灭伪泌阳保安团两千余名，内活捉团长张成翔以下一千三百余名，毙伤五百余名。
    【新华社中原前线十七日电】解放军某部继十二日攻占内黄、野鸡岗两车站后，又于十三日夜攻占民权县城及民权车站，守敌逃窜。现蒋匪陇海东段交通已告中断，我军正扩大战果中。
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖十八日电】迟到消息：驻守陈留（开封东南）城的蒋军河南省保安六团第一营一个排官兵四十余人，上月二十七日晚在排长张云恒、副排长田奉才率领下，携带机枪五挺、步枪二十八支，光荣起义。他们挖通墙洞冲出城垣，中途并与土蒋匪作战，二十九日安抵解放区某地，受到当地军民热烈欢迎。
    【新华社华东十八日电】苏中人民武装六日在（南）通如（皋）线平潮、白蒲两镇间设伏，截获蒋匪汽车一辆，生俘匪整二十一师军务处中校课长苏学诚、联勤总部第四十兵站主任吴大涛、少校税务局主任陈获、课长赵黄及匪行政院教育专员李宋等六名，毙伤匪整二十一师上校军务处长顾勉伯及军医等三名，缴获蒋币三亿九千八百万元。同时平毁沿线湾子头、四十里、任家口三蒋匪据点之碉堡工事。
