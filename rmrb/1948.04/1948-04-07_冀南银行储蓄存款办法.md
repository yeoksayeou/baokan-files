　　Jinan Bank Savings Deposit Measures
    I. This business is specifically for the savings of institutions, troops, cadres, staff, and employees of public and private enterprises and shops. For convenience, only current savings deposits are offered.
    II. Term: Deposits and withdrawals are limited to one to two times per month. Savings interrupted for more than two months will be treated as ordinary deposits. Savings time is divided into three months or more, six months or more, and one year. Once determined, it cannot be modified.
    III. Interest Rate: Savings deposits accrue monthly compound interest. Deposits over one month will have a provisional monthly interest rate of 3%, over three months 3.5%, over six months 4%, and a full year 4.5%.
    IV. Procedures: Once a savings deposit account is opened, the bank will issue a savings deposit book. Subsequent deposits and withdrawals will be made with the book. If the deposit book is lost, the bank should be notified immediately to report the loss. The bank is not responsible for unauthorized withdrawals that occur before the loss is reported.
    V. Amount: Each deposit must be at least 1,000 yuan and no more than 100,000 yuan. When cadres and staff of institutions and troops open an account with deposits exceeding 100,000 yuan, they must attach a letter of certification from the responsible authority and supervisor. During the savings period, the balance after each withdrawal shall not be less than 50% of the total deposit.
　　Jinan Bank Head Office　April 4th

### 冀南银行储蓄存款办法

1948-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀南银行储蓄存款办法
    一、此项业务专为机关部队、干部、工作人员及公私企业之职员、店员工人等节约储蓄之用，为便利起见，只举办活期储蓄存款。
    二、期限：每月存取以一至二次为限，款项储存中断二月以上者，按普通存款看待。储蓄时间分三个月以上，六个月以上，一年三种，一经确定不得修改。
    三、利率：储蓄存款按月复利计息，存款逾一月者暂定月息三分，逾三月者三分五厘，逾六月者四分，满一年者四分五厘。
    四、手续：储蓄存款一经开户，由本行发给储蓄存款簿，以后凭簿陆续存取，存簿如有遗失，应立即向本行声明挂失，未挂失前发生冒领者，本行不负责任。
    五、额数：每次存入起码金额以一千元以上至十万元为限，机关部队之干部工作人员开户时十万元以上之存款，应附有主管机关及主管人员证明信件。此项存款在储存期限内，每次提取后之余额不得少于存款总数的百分之五十。
    冀南银行总行　四月四日
