Title: Tens of Thousands Demonstrate in Paris to Protest High Prices
Author:
Date: 1948-04-16
Page: 2

　　Protesting High Prices
  　Tens of Thousands Demonstrate in Paris
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 13th: Paris dispatch: Nearly ten thousand workers held a demonstration here on April 8th to celebrate the anniversary of the founding of the Housing Construction Workers' Union and to protest against high prices. At the meeting, Atishaer, one of the leaders of the French General Confederation of Labor, addressed the workers, saying with confidence: "The time for implementing necessary reforms in France for the benefit of the whole country will come soon." After the meeting, the workers held a demonstration, protesting to the Seine County Government and the Employers' Federation about the high cost of living, and demanding an increase in wages.

### 抗议物价高涨　巴黎万人示威

1948-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　抗议物价高涨
  　巴黎万人示威
    【新华社陕北十三日电】巴黎讯：近万工人于四月八日在此间举行示威大会，以庆祝房屋建筑工会成立纪念日及抗议物价之高涨。法总工会领袖之一阿提夏尔向参加大会的工人发表演说，他充满信心地说：“为了全国的利益，在法国实行必要改革的时机，很快就要到来了。”工人于会后举行示威游行，向赛纳县政府及雇主联合会抗议生活费过高，并要求增加工资。
