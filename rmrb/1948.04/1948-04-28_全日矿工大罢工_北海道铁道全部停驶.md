Title: Nationwide Miners' General Strike; Hokkaido Railways Completely Shut Down
Author:
Date: 1948-04-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 25th: Reports from various sources: The All-Japan Coal Miners' Union, with its 75,000 members, launched a 24-hour nationwide general strike on the 21st to insist on wage increase demands. The union had been in negotiations with mine owners for a month, but the latter unreasonably rejected the workers' wage increase demands, and the negotiations broke down on the 20th. Furthermore, according to Agence France-Presse on the 20th: The Hokkaido National Railway Workers' Union is also carrying out a 48-hour strike, and all 41 railway lines on the island have been completely shut down since the 19th.

### 全日矿工大罢工  北海道铁道全部停驶

1948-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

    全日矿工大罢工
    北海道铁道全部停驶
    【新华社陕北二十五日电】各方讯：全日矿工总工会所属七万五千名工人，为坚持增资要求已于二十一日发动二十四小时全国性总罢工。按该工会曾与矿主谈判历时一月，因后者无理拒绝工人增资要求，谈判遂于二十日宣告破裂。另据法新社二十日讯：北海道国营铁道工人工会亦进行四十八小时罢工，该岛四十一条铁路交通线于十九日起全部停驶。
