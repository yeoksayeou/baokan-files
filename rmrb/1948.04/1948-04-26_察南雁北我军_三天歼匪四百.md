Title: Our Army in Southern Chahar and Northern Yanbei Annihilates 400 Bandits in Three Days
Author:
Date: 1948-04-26
Page: 2

    Southern Chahar and Northern Yanbei, Our Army
    Annihilates 400 Bandits in Three Days
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 24th] The Southern Chahar and Northern Yanbei People's Liberation Army annihilated more than 400 Fu-Yan bandit troops in three days. (1) On the 7th, the Southern Chahar People's Liberation Army annihilated parts of the Fu bandit's reorganized 71st Brigade's direct unit and its subordinate 21st Regiment at Hutuo Store, more than 100 *li* southwest of Xuanhua, killing, wounding, and capturing more than 150 bandit officers and soldiers, seizing one 60mm mortar, seven machine guns, more than 100 rifles and submachine guns, and 87 warhorses. (2) On the 9th, our army annihilated parts of the Yan bandit's 20th Security Regiment and the pseudo-Yanggao Security Police Force, which were fleeing from Yanggao to Datong, near Julebao Station east of Datong, killing, wounding, and capturing more than 290 bandit troops, seizing one small cannon, two machine guns, and 84 rifles.

### 察南雁北我军  三天歼匪四百

1948-04-26
第2版()
专栏：

    察南雁北我军
    三天歼匪四百
    【新华社晋察冀二十四日电】察南雁北解放军三日内共歼傅阎匪军四百余人。（一）七日，察南解放军于宣化西南百余里之滹沱店歼傅匪整编七十一旅直属队及其所辖二十一团各一部，毙伤俘匪官兵一百五十余名，缴六○炮一门，机枪七挺，步枪及冲锋枪百余支，战马八十七匹。（二）九日我军于大同东聚乐堡车站附近，歼灭由阳高向大同逃窜之阎匪保安第二十团及伪阳高保警队各一部，共毙伤俘匪二百九十余名，缴小炮一门，机枪二挺，步枪八十四支。
