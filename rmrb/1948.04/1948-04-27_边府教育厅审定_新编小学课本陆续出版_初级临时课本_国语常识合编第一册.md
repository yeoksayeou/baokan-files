Author:
Date: 1948-04-27
Page: 1

Newly Compiled Elementary School Textbooks Approved by the Border Region Government Education Department to be Published Successively
    Elementary Temporary Textbook:
    Combined Textbook of National Language and General Knowledge, Volume 1
    The newly compiled elementary school textbooks, approved by the Border Region Government Education Department, have been successively submitted to this bookstore for publication. Currently, the first volume of the Combined Textbook of National Language and General Knowledge has been printed. When reprinting in various regions, in accordance with last year's agreement, it is hoped that all will reprint according to the printed version of this bookstore without any changes.
            Distributed by Xinhua Bookstore, North China
