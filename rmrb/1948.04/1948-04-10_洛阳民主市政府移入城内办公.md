Title: Luoyang Democratic Municipal Government Moves into the City to Work
Author:
Date: 1948-04-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Yu-Shaan-E, 8th. The Luoyang Democratic Municipal Government, on the morning of the 5th, before the fighting had completely ended, led personnel from the Industry and Commerce, Public Security, and Culture and Education Bureaus into the city to work. Citizens gathered in the streets to welcome them. Mayor Yang Shaoqiao and municipal government personnel respectively expressed their condolences to people of all classes who had suffered again from the disturbances of the Chiang bandits, and carried out investigation and relief work. Currently, the Industry and Commerce and Culture and Education Bureaus are actively assisting in the resumption of business for industry and commerce and the resumption of classes for schools.

### 洛阳民主市政府移入城内办公

1948-04-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　洛阳民主市政府
  　移入城内办公
    【新华社豫陕鄂八日电】洛阳民主市政府五日晨战斗尚未完全结束前，即率工商、公安、文教各局人员，入城办公。市民齐集街头欢迎。市长杨少桥及市府人员均分别慰问再受蒋匪扰害的各阶层人民，并进行调查、救济工作。现工商、文教两局正积极协助工商业复业及学校复课。
