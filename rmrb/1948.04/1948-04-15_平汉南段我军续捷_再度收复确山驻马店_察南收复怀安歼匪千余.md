Title: Our Army Continues to Win Victories in the Southern Section of the Pinghan Railway; Recaptures Queshan and Zhumadian Again; Recaptures Huaian in Southern Chahar, Annihilating Over a Thousand Bandits
Author:
Date: 1948-04-15
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Front Lines of Su, Lu, Yu, and Wan, 13th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army recaptured Zhumadian, an important station on the Pinghan Railway, for the second time on the evening of the 7th. The defending enemy, two companies of the 17th Traffic Police Corps, fled upon hearing the news. The People's Liberation Army took advantage of the victory to advance south and recaptured Queshan County on the 9th.
Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 13th: On the 8th, our army in Southern Chahar annihilated a unit of the 63rd Regiment of the 210th Division of the Provisional 4th Army, over 600 men, in Guhoubao, north of Tianzhen. That evening, we recaptured Huaian County, annihilating all of the defending enemy, over a thousand men. The results of the battle are still being investigated.
Xinhua News Agency, Su, Lu, Yu, and Wan, 13th: Local armed forces of the Fifth Military Sub-district of Yu, Wan, and Su annihilated over 500 enemies in three days. On the 3rd of this month, at Tiangang, 15 li northwest of Yanling City, they blocked the Chiang bandit's 11th Reorganized Division, which was fleeing eastward from Xuchang and Changge, killing over 100 enemies, capturing over 20 enemies, and seizing one light machine gun and three submachine guns. The Chiang bandits who sneaked into Yanling City that day hastily retreated westward that evening. We then took advantage of the victory to move northward on the 5th, surrounding over 400 local Chiang bandits in Beitaixiazhuang, 30 li southwest of Weishi City. All the defending enemies were annihilated. In addition to those killed and wounded, over 300 were captured alive. Detailed battle results are still being investigated.
Xinhua News Agency, Su, Lu, Yu, and Wan, 13th: On March 27th, the local armed forces of the First Military Sub-district of Yu, Wan, and Su launched a surprise attack on Qiuxieyan, 35 li southwest of Shangqiu, annihilating all of the defending Chiang bandit Shangqiu Security Brigade, over 100 men. They seized one light machine gun, two Thompson submachine guns, a total of 74 long and short guns, over 5,800 rounds of ammunition, and 12 mules and horses. In addition, on the night of April 1st, our Taikang local armed forces searched and suppressed the remnants of the local Chiang bandit Guo Xinbo's forces, who were hiding in Taikang City, capturing Wang Laowen, the puppet Cuiqiao Township Chief, and Ren Zongshi, the assassination squad leader, and ten others, and seizing one machine gun and 14 rifles.

### 平汉南段我军续捷　再度收复确山驻马店　察南收复怀安歼匪千余

1948-04-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　平汉南段我军续捷
  　再度收复确山驻马店
  　察南收复怀安歼匪千余
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线十三日电】解放军一部七日晚第二次收复平汉路上重要车站驻马店，守敌交警十七总队两个中队闻风逃窜。解放军乘胜南下，九日复克确山县城。
    【新华社晋察冀十三日电】察南我军八日在天镇北谷后堡歼灭暂四军二一○师六三团一部六百余名，当晚复攻克怀安县城，守敌千余全部歼灭，战果正清查中。
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖十三日电】豫皖苏五分区地方武装，三天内歼敌五百余人。本月三日于鄢陵城西北十五里之田岗，阻击自许昌长葛东窜之蒋匪整十一师，毙敌百余名，俘敌二十余名，缴获轻机枪一挺，冲锋枪三支。该日窜陷鄢陵城之蒋匪，当晚即慌张西撤，我复乘胜于五日挥戈北向，将土著蒋匪四百余人包围于尉氏城西南三十里之北台夏庄，守敌全部就歼，除毙伤外，生俘三百余人，详细战果正清查中。
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖十三日电】豫皖苏一分区地方武装，三月二十七日，奔袭商邱西南三十五里之邱谢岩，守敌蒋匪商邱保安旅百余名全部就歼。缴获轻机枪一挺，汤姆式二支，长短枪共七十四支，子弹五千八百余发，骡马十二匹。另我太康地方武装四月一日夜搜剿在太康城隐蔽活动之土著蒋匪郭鑫波残部，捕获伪崔桥乡乡队长王老文，暗杀团长任宗诗以下十一人，搜出机枪一挺，步枪十四支。
