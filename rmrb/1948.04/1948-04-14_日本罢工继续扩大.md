Title: Strikes in Japan Continue to Expand
Author:
Date: 1948-04-14
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, April 11th: Tokyo dispatch: Local strikes throughout Japan continue to expand. The Tokyo Shibaura Electric Company's union of 23,000 workers, as well as Matsuyama Electric workers and Hokkaido coal miners, defied MacArthur's strike ban and staged a 24-hour strike on the 8th. Miners at the Mitsui Coal Mining Company and other companies in northern Kyushu have also gone on strike. Workers at the Tokyo Transportation Company (the capital's largest private train and bus company) decided to begin a strike on the 11th. The labor unrest has spread to the Tokyo film industry, and 75 leaders of the film industry union have been arrested due to disputes with the factory.

### 日本罢工继续扩大

1948-04-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　日本罢工继续扩大
    【新华社陕北十一日电】东京讯：日本各地地域性的罢工，仍继续扩大中。东京芝浦电气公司工会二万三千工人，及松山电气工人和北海道煤矿工人，蔑视麦克阿瑟的罢工禁令，于八日进行二十四小时罢工。九州北部的三井煤矿公司及其他公司的矿工，也已罢工。东京运输公司（首都最大的私营火车与公共汽车公司）工人决定于十一日开始罢工。工潮已波及东京电影业，电影业工会领袖七十五名曾因与厂方发生纠纷，而被逮捕。
