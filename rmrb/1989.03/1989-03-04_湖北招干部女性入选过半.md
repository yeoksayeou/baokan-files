Title: Over Half of Newly Recruited Officials in Hubei Province Are Women
Author: Fang Zhengjun, Lu Zhibing
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, March 3rd

According to the Hubei Provincial Department of Personnel, out of the 3,268 people recently recruited through the province-wide civil servant examinations, 1,780 are women, accounting for 54% of the total. This result demonstrates the potential of women in competitive environments. Starting from the second half of 1988, the Hubei Provincial Department of Personnel has been conducting open examinations for civil servant positions in the fields of judicial administration, banking, commerce, and taxation, and has stipulated that the selection will be based on examination scores without a predetermined male-to-female ratio.

### 湖北招干部女性入选过半
方政军　鲁知炳
1989-03-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　湖北招干部女性入选过半
    据新华社武汉３月３日电  （记者方政军、通讯员鲁知炳）记者从湖北省人事厅获悉：在刚刚结束的全省招考干部工作中被录取的３２６８人，女性达１７８０人，占录取总数的５４％。这一结果，显示了女性在竞争中的潜力。从１９８８年下半年开始，湖北省人事部门在政法、银行、工商、税务系统公开招考干部，并规定，不定男女比例，按考试成绩择优录取。
