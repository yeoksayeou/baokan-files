Title: Jiang Zemin Appointed Professor at Shanghai Jiao Tong University
Author: Guo Lihua
Date: 1989-03-26
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 24th.

Jiang Zemin, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, was appointed as a professor by his alma mater, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, today. During the appointment ceremony, he delivered an academic report titled "Energy Development Trends and Major Energy-Saving Measures."

### 江泽民被聘为  上海交大教授
郭礼华
1989-03-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　江泽民被聘为        
    上海交大教授
    据新华社上海３月２４日电  （记者郭礼华）中共上海市委书记江泽民今天被母校上海交通大学聘为教授，并在受聘仪式上作了题为《能源发展趋势与主要的节能措施》的学术报告。
