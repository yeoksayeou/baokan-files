Title: China Democratic League Hosts Dinner for Hong Kong and Macau "Two Sessions" Representatives and Committee Members
Author: Meng Xiaoyun
Date: 1989-03-27
Edition: 2

	The China Democratic League (Minzhu Tongmeng) hosted a dinner for representatives and committee members from Hong Kong and Macau attending the "Two Sessions" (National People's Congress and Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference) at the Zhaolong Restaurant this evening. The dinner was held under the names of Fei Xiaotong, the chairman, and Qian Weichang, the vice-chairman, of the China Democratic League Central Committee.

	Fei Xiaotong expressed that the China Democratic League is willing to work together with all the people of the nation, including compatriots from Hong Kong, Macau, and overseas Chinese, to contribute to the revitalization of China and the reunification of the motherland.

### 民盟宴请港澳“两会”代表委员
孟晓云
1989-03-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　民盟宴请港澳“两会”代表委员
    本报北京３月２６日讯  记者孟晓云报道：为了加强联谊，民盟中央今晚以费孝通主席、钱伟长副主席的名义，在兆龙饭店宴请港澳人大代表和政协委员。费孝通表示，民盟愿意和全国人民包括港澳同胞和海外侨胞通力合作，为振兴中华、统一祖国做出贡献。
