Title: Wang Chiji Creates Micro-Engraved Portraits of U.S. Presidents
Author:
Date: 1989-03-09
Edition: 4

A set of micro-engraved portraits of U.S. presidents was recently completed in Suzhou. The artist, Wang Chiji, stated that he will donate this set of works to the American people to express the friendly sentiments of the Chinese people towards the American people.

Wang Chiji is a micro-engraving artist at the Suzhou Materials and Trade Center Advertising Department. He spent over three months of his spare time creating this set of works. The portraits are each engraved on pieces of ivory the size of a watermelon seed.

### 王持纪创作美国历届总统微刻肖像

1989-03-09
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　王持纪创作美国历届总统微刻肖像
    本报讯  一套美国历届总统的微刻肖像近日在苏州创作成功。作者王持纪表示，他将把这套作品赠给美国人民，以表达中国人民对美国人民的友好情谊。
    王持纪是苏州物资贸易中心广告部的微刻艺术家，他用三个多月业余时间创作了这套作品。这些肖像分别刻在４０片瓜子大小的象牙片上。（群）
