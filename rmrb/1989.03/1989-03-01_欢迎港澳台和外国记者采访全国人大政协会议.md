Title: Welcome Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, and Foreign Journalists to Cover the National People's Congress and CPPCC Sessions
Author:
Date: 1989-03-01
Edition: 1

The upcoming Second Session of the Seventh National People's Congress and the Second Session of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference welcome journalists from Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, and foreign countries to cover the events.

Zhang Hugeng, spokesperson for the Office of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Lu Zhichao, spokesperson for the News Office of the CPPCC, told Xinhua reporters that foreign journalists planning to cover these two sessions can apply through the Chinese embassies or consulates abroad or the visa office in Hong Kong to the News Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Journalists from Hong Kong and Macau can apply through the Xinhua News Agency's Hong Kong branch to the Chinese Journalists Association. Journalists from Taiwan can apply through the Xinhua News Agency's Hong Kong branch or Chinese embassies or consulates abroad to the Chinese Journalists Association. Journalists from Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, and foreign countries must provide a letter of authorization from their news organizations or proof of their journalist identity when applying for coverage. The application deadline is March 10.

It is understood that this session of the NPC and CPPCC will establish a press center at the Chinese Journalists Association to receive and arrange coverage for journalists from Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, and foreign countries, and to provide them with session progress updates, news releases, and photo materials.

### 欢迎港澳台和外国记者采访全国人大政协会议

1989-03-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　欢迎港澳台和外国记者采访全国人大政协会议
    新华社北京２月２８日电  即将召开的七届全国人大二次会议和全国政协七届二次会议欢迎香港、澳门、台湾记者和外国记者前来采访。
    全国人大常委会办公厅新闻发言人张虎生、全国政协新闻发言人卢之超在接受本社记者采访时介绍说，凡拟采访这两个大会的外国记者可通过中国驻外使领馆或驻香港签证处向中国外交部新闻司提出申请；港澳记者可通过新华社香港分社向中国记协提出申请；台湾记者可通过新华社香港分社或中国驻外使领馆向中国记协提出申请。港、澳、台记者和外国记者在申请采访时，须持所在新闻机构的委托书或确认本人是新闻记者身份的证明。申请截止日期是３月１０日。
    据悉，这次人大和政协会议将在中国记协设立新闻中心，作为接待、安排港、澳、台和外国记者采访的机构，并向他们提供大会进程消息、新闻稿、图片资料等。
