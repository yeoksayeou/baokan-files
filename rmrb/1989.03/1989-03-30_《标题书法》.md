Title: Calligraphy for Titles
Author: Duan Guihua
Date: 1989-03-30
Edition: 8

Chinese calligraphy is the art of writing. A calligraphy theorist once said: Calligraphy cannot be separated from the content of the text. If one discusses calligraphy without considering the text, it loses the original intention of this traditional classical art and becomes something else. Recently, the Beijing Institute of Physical Education Press published the book "Calligraphy for Titles" (written by Wu Kaiying). This book can be said to further validate this viewpoint in practice: The book takes the highly practical form of calligraphy for titles, seriously exploring how to use calligraphy to enhance the expressiveness and appreciation of articles based on their content and the meaning of the titles.

### 《标题书法》
段桂华
1989-03-30
第8版(副刊)
专栏：书林一叶

　　《标题书法》
    中国书法是文字的艺术。有位书法理论家曾说过：书法不能离开文字的内容，离文字而谈书法，则失去这个传统古典艺术的本意，而成为别的什么艺术。最近，北京体育学院出版社出版的《标题书法》一书（吴开英著），可以说是在实践上进一步论证了这个观点：该书从实用性很强的标题书法这一形式入手，认真探讨了如何根据文章的内容、标题的意蕴而用书法去增强其表现性和观赏性。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（段桂华）
