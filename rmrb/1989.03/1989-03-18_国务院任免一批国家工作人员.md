Title: The State Council Appoints and Dismisses a Batch of State Officials
Author:
Date: 1989-03-18
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 17th.

The State Council appointed and dismissed a batch of state officials from February 5 to March 14, 1989.

Li Peiyao was appointed as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Labor, Hong Zengzeng as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture, Cai Yansong as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Forestry, Wang Yanmou as Director of the State Bureau of Building Materials Industry, and Tang Wuyu as Director of the State Bureau of State-Owned Assets Management.

The State Council agreed to Xiang Zhongyang's resignation as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture.

### 国务院任免一批国家工作人员

1989-03-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　国务院任免一批国家工作人员
    新华社北京３月１７日电  国务院１９８９年２月５日至３月１４日任免一批国家工作人员。
    任命李沛瑶为劳动部副部长，洪绂曾为农业部副部长，蔡延松为林业部副部长，王燕谋为国家建筑材料工业局局长，汤丙午为国家国有资产管理局局长。
    同意相重扬辞去农业部副部长职务。
