Title: Thai Prime Minister to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 1

Thai Prime Minister to Visit China

    New China News Agency, Beijing, March 9 - The Foreign Ministry spokesman announced today that at the invitation of Premier Li Peng, General Chatchai Chunhawan, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Thailand, and his wife will pay an official visit to China from March 14 to 17.

    Li Zhaoxing said at a press conference that this visit by Prime Minister Chatchai Chunhawan is his first official visit to China since taking office, and it is also a return visit to Premier Li Peng's visit to Thailand last year.

    Li Zhaoxing said that the two prime ministers will have an in-depth exchange of views on further strengthening Sino-Thai friendship, developing friendly cooperation between the two countries, and the Cambodian issue.

### 泰国总理即将访华

1989-03-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　泰国总理即将访华
    新华社北京３月９日电  外交部发言人今天在这里宣布，应李鹏总理的邀请，泰王国总理差猜·春哈旺上将偕夫人将于３月１４日至１７日对中国进行正式访问。
    李肇星在新闻发布会上说，差猜·春哈旺总理此次来访是他任总理后对中国的第一次正式访问，也是对李鹏总理去年对泰国访问的回访。
    李肇星说，中泰两国总理将就进一步加强中泰友谊、发展两国的友好合作和柬埔寨问题等广泛、深入地交换意见。
