Title: President Suharto Directs Indonesian Foreign Minister to Prepare for Resumption of Diplomatic Relations with China
Author:
Date: 1989-03-01
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 28th.

Jakarta: Indonesian President Suharto on the 28th directed Foreign Minister Ali Alatas to begin preparations for the resumption of diplomatic relations with China.

Alatas told the press after meeting with Suharto that the timing for the resumption of diplomatic relations between the two countries has not yet been determined, but that the two countries' permanent representatives to the United Nations will begin technical negotiations on the matter.

Suharto and Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met in Tokyo on the 23rd, during which both sides agreed to normalize relations between the two countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, February 27th.

Thai Foreign Minister Siddhi Savetsila said today in Bangkok that the decision by China and Indonesia to take measures to normalize diplomatic relations is a good thing.

He said that Indonesia is the largest country in ASEAN, and China is a major power in Asia. The normalization of relations between the two countries on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the Ten Principles of the Bandung Conference will definitely promote relations between China and countries like Singapore.

He said that the normalization of relations between China and Indonesia will also further strengthen relations between ASEAN and China in various aspects, especially trade between China and ASEAN.

### 苏哈托总统指示印尼外长  为同中国复交作准备

1989-03-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏哈托总统指示印尼外长
    为同中国复交作准备
    新华社北京２月２８日电  雅加达消息：印度尼西亚总统苏哈托２８日指示外交部长阿里·阿拉塔斯开始为恢复印尼同中国的外交关系作准备。
    阿拉塔斯在同苏哈托会见后对新闻界说，两国恢复外交关系的时间还未确定，但两国常驻联合国代表将就恢复关系事开始技术性谈判。
    苏哈托同中国外长钱其琛于２月２３日在东京会晤时，双方同意实现两国关系正常化。
    新华社曼谷２月２７日电　泰国外长西提·沙卫西拉今天在这里对记者说：中国和印尼决定采取措施，实现邦交正常化是件好事。
    他说，印尼是东盟中最大的国家，中国是亚洲大国。两国在和平共处五项原则和万隆会议十项原则基础上实现关系正常化一定会促进中国和新加坡等国的关系。
    他说，中国和印尼实现关系正常化还将进一步密切东盟和中国在各方面的关系，特别是中国和东盟之间的贸易往来。
