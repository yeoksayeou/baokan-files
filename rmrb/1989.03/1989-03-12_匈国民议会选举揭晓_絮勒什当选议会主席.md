Title: Hungarian National Assembly Election Results Announced: Szűrös Elected Chairman
Author:
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 10th.

Hungarian National Assembly Election Results Announced
Szűrös Elected Chairman

Hungarian Party Central Committee Secretary Szűrös Mágash was elected Chairman of the Hungarian National Assembly today in a secret ballot held during the assembly's session.

Szűrös was elected to the Hungarian Party Central Committee in 1978. Since 1983, he has served as a Secretary of the Hungarian Party Central Committee, overseeing foreign affairs. He is also the Chairman of the Hungarian National Assembly's Foreign Affairs Committee.

In his speech after the election, Szűrös pointed out that Hungary's domestic development and foreign policy are now at a new turning point. For many years, Hungary has been considering the direction of its development. He believes that Hungary must establish democracy and move towards the goal of socialism.

This session of the National Assembly began on the 8th and will adjourn tonight, resuming on March 22nd.

### 匈国民议会选举揭晓  絮勒什当选议会主席

1989-03-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　匈国民议会选举揭晓
    絮勒什当选议会主席
    新华社布达佩斯３月１０日电  匈党中央书记絮勒什·马加什今天在匈牙利国民议会进行的无记名投票选举中，当选为匈牙利国民议会主席。
    １９７８年当选为匈党中央委员。从１９８３年起，任匈党中央书记，主管外事工作，他还是匈国会外事委员会主席。
    絮勒什在当选后发表的讲话中指出，现在匈国内发展和外交政策都处在一个新的分界阶段。多年来，匈都在考虑发展方向问题。他认为，匈必须建立民主，必须向社会主义的目标前进。
    这次国会会议是从８日开始举行的，将于今晚休会，３月２２日将继续进行。
