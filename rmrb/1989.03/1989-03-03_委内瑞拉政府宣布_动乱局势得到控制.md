Title: Venezuelan Government Announces Control of Turmoil
Author:
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Caracas, March 1st.

The Venezuelan government today announced that the nationwide turmoil, which lasted for two days, has been brought under control, and called on all workers to return to their posts.

This was announced by Interior Minister Isaías Rodríguez in a nationwide radio and television broadcast this afternoon.

The minister said that the state of emergency and curfew imposed nationwide since last night will continue to be in effect. He emphasized that this means the government will impose severe penalties on those who continue to "create chaos, commit looting, and endanger the peaceful life of the people."

### 委内瑞拉政府宣布  动乱局势得到控制

1989-03-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    委内瑞拉政府宣布
    动乱局势得到控制
    新华社加拉加斯3月1日电　委内瑞拉政府今天宣布，动乱了2天的全国局势已得到控制，并呼吁全体工作人员返回各自的工作岗位。
    这是委内瑞拉内政部长伊萨吉雷1日下午向全国发表广播电视讲话时宣布的。
    这位内政部长说，从2月28日晚在全国实行的紧急状态和宵禁继续有效。他强调，这意味着政府将对那些继续“制造混乱、进行抢劫、危害群众和平生活的人”实行严厉制裁。
