Title: Arafat Visits Pakistan, Benazir Bhutto Says Holding a Middle East Conference is a Guarantee for Peace
Author:
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 3

Arafat Visits Pakistan

Benazir Bhutto Says Holding a Middle East Conference is a Guarantee for Peace

Xinhua News Agency, Islamabad, March 9

Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto said last night that Pakistan will continue to support the Palestinian people in their struggle to establish an independent homeland.

Benazir Bhutto said in a speech at a banquet held in Rawalpindi last night to welcome the Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Palestine Liberation Organization, Yasser Arafat, that the Pakistani people have close relations with the Palestinian people and have always supported their cause.

She pointed out that holding an international Middle East peace conference on an equal basis, including the PLO and all parties, would be the best guarantee for peace in the region.

Arafat said in his speech that holding an international Middle East peace conference on an equal basis, hosted by the United Nations and with the participation of the PLO, would provide the best foundation for achieving peace and ensuring the security of all countries in the region.

He said, "For the establishment of an independent Palestinian state, the Palestinian people's peace initiative is based on the United Nations resolutions on the Palestinian issue and the right of our people to self-determination."

Before the banquet, Benazir Bhutto and Arafat held talks on some regional issues, particularly the Middle East and Afghanistan.

Arafat arrived in Pakistan this morning for a two-day official visit.

### 阿拉法特访问巴基斯坦  贝·布托说召开中东和会是实现和平的保证

1989-03-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　阿拉法特访问巴基斯坦
    贝·布托说召开中东和会是实现和平的保证
    新华社伊斯兰堡３月９日电  巴基斯坦总理贝娜齐尔·布托昨晚说，巴基斯坦将继续支持巴勒斯坦人民争取建立独立家园的斗争。
    贝·布托昨天晚上在拉瓦尔品第为欢迎巴勒斯坦解放组织执委会主席阿拉法特举行的宴会上发表讲话说，巴基斯坦人民同巴勒斯坦人民有着密切关系，并一贯支持他们的事业。
    她指出，在平等基础之上召开包括巴解组织在内所有方面参加的中东和平国际会议，将是实现该地区和平的最好保证。
    阿拉法特在讲话中说，在平等基础上举行由联合国主持并有巴解组织参加的中东和平国际会议，将为这一地区实现和平和确保所有国家的安全提供最好的基础。
    他说：“为了建立独立的巴勒斯坦国，巴勒斯坦人民的和平倡议是以联合国关于巴勒斯坦问题的决议和我国人民享有自决权为基础的。”
    在宴会之前，贝·布托同阿拉法特就一些地区性问题，特别是中东和阿富汗问题，举行了会谈。
    阿拉法特是昨天上午抵达巴基斯坦进行为期两天正式访问的。
    （附图片）
    3月8日，巴解主席阿拉法特在巴基斯坦访问期间，向贝·布托父亲的陵墓敬献花圈。　　　　（新华社发）
