Title: Li Menghua Receives Special Certificate from the International Olympic Committee
Author:
Date: 1989-03-26
Edition: 3

	Li Menghua, chairman of the Chinese Olympic Committee, received a special certificate from the International Olympic Committee chairman Juan Antonio Samaranch at the closing ceremony of the Beijing Sports Management Training Course for Olympic Solidarity Circulating School today.

	Samaranch wrote on the certificate that the special certificate is awarded to those who have cooperated in the Beijing Sports Management Training Course for Olympic Solidarity Circulating School.

	Wei Jizhong, secretary-general of the Chinese Olympic Committee, and Yang Fulu, president of the Beijing Institute of Physical Education, also received the special certificate.

	This is the first time the International Olympic Committee has issued such a special certificate since 1986.

### 李梦华获国际奥委会特别证书

1989-03-26
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　李梦华获国际奥委会特别证书
    新华社北京３月２５日电  在今天结束的北京体育管理人员训练班结业典礼上，中国奥委会主席李梦华接受了国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇签发的特别证书。
    萨马兰奇在证书上说，特别证书是授给举办奥林匹克团结巡回学校体育管理人员训练班中给予合作的人员。
    同时接受特别证书的还有中国奥委会秘书长魏纪中和北京体育学院院长杨福鹿。
    从１９８６年以来，这是国际奥委会第一次颁发这种特别证书。
