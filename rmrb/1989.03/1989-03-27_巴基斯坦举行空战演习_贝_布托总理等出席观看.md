Title: Pakistan Conducts Air Combat Exercise
Author:
Date: 1989-03-27
Edition: 4

Pakistan Conducts Air Combat Exercise
Benazir Bhutto, Prime Minister, and Others Attend

NEW DELHI, March 25 (Xinhua) -- The Pakistan Air Force conducted a large-scale air combat exercise 70 kilometers from Karachi in the skies above Sonewani on March 25.

According to Pakistani military sources, dozens of F-16, A-5, F-6, and Mirage fighter jets participated in this live-fire exercise. These aircraft collectively dropped and launched thousands of bombs, missiles, and rockets during the exercise.

This air combat exercise was held two days after the national day parade in Rawalpindi. Pakistani President Ghulam Ishaq Khan, Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto, and senior military and government officials attended the exercise.

### 巴基斯坦举行空战演习  贝·布托总理等出席观看

1989-03-27
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦举行空战演习        
    贝·布托总理等出席观看
    新华社伊斯兰堡３月２５日电  巴基斯坦空军２５日在距卡拉奇７０公里的松米亚尼上空，举行了一次大规模的飞机实战演习。
    据巴基斯坦军方人士透露，参加这次实战演习的有数十架Ｆ—１６型、Ａ—５型、Ｆ—６型和幻影型等各种型号的战斗机。这些飞机在实战演习中共投掷和发射了数千枚炸弹、导弹和火箭弹。
    这次飞机实战演习是在拉瓦尔品第举行了国庆阅兵式两天后举行的。巴基斯坦总统伊沙克·汗、总理贝·布托以及高级军官和政府高级官员观看了这次空战演习。
