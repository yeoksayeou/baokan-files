Title: Nigerian New Ambassador Presents Credentials to Chairman Yang
Author:
Date: 1989-03-17
Edition: 2

Nigerian New Ambassador Presents Credentials to Chairman Yang

New China News Agency, Beijing, March 15 — Nigerian Federal Republic's new ambassador to China, Emmanuel Nwachukwu Oba, presented his credentials to Chairman Yang Shangkun today at the Great Hall of the People.

Oba arrived in Beijing on March 6.

### 尼日利亚新大使向杨主席递国书

1989-03-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　尼日利亚新大使向杨主席递国书
    新华社北京３月１５日电  尼日利亚联邦共和国新任驻华大使伊曼纽尔·恩瓦楚库·奥巴今天在人民大会堂向国家主席杨尚昆递交国书。
