Title: France Successfully Demolishes 18-Story Building Using Controlled Explosives
Author:
Date: 1989-03-05
Edition: 3

专栏: Picture News

On March 3, French demolition experts used controlled explosives to successfully demolish an 18-story building with 290 apartments in the city of Lille.

### 法国定向爆破18层高大楼（图片）

1989-03-05
