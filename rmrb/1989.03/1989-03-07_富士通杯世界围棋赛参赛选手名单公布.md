Title: List of Participants for the Fujitsu Cup World Go Championship Announced
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-03-07
Edition: 4

People's Daily, Tokyo, March 6th, Reporter Yu Qing:

The organizing committee of the second Fujitsu Cup World Go Championship announced the list of participating players today. The top three players from the previous tournament, Takeo Watanabe, Hideo Imamura, and Nie Weiping, will participate as seeded players. The other players from various countries and regions are as follows.

Japan: Kobayashi Koichi, Cho Chikun, Kato Masao, Ohira Shigeyuki, Honda Kunihisa, Tanro Hisashi, and Yamashiro Hiroshi, all of whom are 9th dan.

China: Ma Xiaochun, Liu Xiaoguang, Qian Yeping, Chen Liren, and Liang Weitang. The first three are 9th dan, and the last two are 7th dan.

South Korea: Cho Hunhyun, Seo Bongsoo (9th dan), Yang Jaeho (6th dan), and Lee Changho (3rd dan).

Taiwan: Wang Mingyuan (8th dan), Chen Changqing (5th dan).

United States: Michael Redmond (4th dan).

Latin America: Fernando Aguilar (6th dan amateur).

Europe: Ronald Sluijter (6th dan amateur).

The oldest participant is 58-year-old Ohira Shigeyuki, and the youngest is 13-year-old Lee Changho from South Korea.

### 富士通杯世界围棋赛参赛选手名单公布
于青
1989-03-07
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　富士通杯世界围棋赛参赛选手名单公布
    本报东京３月６日电  记者于青报道：第二届富士通杯世界围棋赛主办单位今天在此间公布了参赛棋手名单。上届比赛的前三名，武宫正树、林海峰和聂卫平作为种子选手。其他各国和地区的选手如下。
    日本：小林光一、赵治勋、加藤正夫、大平修三、本田邦久、淡路修三、山城宏，全为九段。
    中国：马晓春、刘小光、钱宇平、陈临新、梁伟棠，前三人九段，后二人七段。
    南朝鲜：曹薰铉、徐奉洙九段，梁宰豪六段、李昌镐三段。
    中国台北：王铭琬八段、陈长清五段。
    美国：米恩斯·查四段。
    拉丁美洲：费尔鲁南德·阿基拉鲁业余六段。
    欧洲：罗娜尔德·叙连帕业余六段。
    年龄最大的是５８岁的大平修三，最小的是１３岁的南朝鲜李昌镐。
