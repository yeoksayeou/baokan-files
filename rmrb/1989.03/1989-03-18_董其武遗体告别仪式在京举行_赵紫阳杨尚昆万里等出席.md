Title: Funeral for Dong Qiwu Held in Beijing
Author: Chéng Guānglóng
Date: 1989-03-18
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 17th.

A farewell ceremony for the late Dong Qiwu, former Vice Chairman of the 6th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, was held today at the General Logistics Department of the People's Liberation Army.

Leaders including Zhao Ziyang, Yang Shangkun, and Wan Li, along with over 800 people from various sectors in Beijing, paid their respects to this great patriot, loyal soldier of the Communist Party of China, senior military leader, and outstanding military strategist.

The ceremony was solemn and dignified. Dong Qiwu's body, draped in the flag of the Communist Party of China, lay amidst a sea of fragrant flowers. Surrounding the body were pine trees and wreaths. Wreaths were sent by Deng Xiaoping, Li Peng, Chen Yun, Li Xiannian, Peng Zhen, Deng Yingchao, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Tian Jiyun, Jiang Zemin, Li Ruihuan, Li Ximing, Yang Ruda, Song Ping, Hu Yaobang, Bo Yibo, Wang Shoudao, Wu Xiuquan, Jiang Hua, Li Yimang, Yu Qiuyi, Song Shiwu, Zhang Jinfu, Zhang Aiping, Lu Dingyi, Hu Qiaomu, Geng Biao, Ji Pengfei, Huang Hua, Huang Zhen, Kang Shien, Cheng Zihua, Wei Guoqing, Saifuding Aizizi, Rong Yiren, Ni Zhifu, Fei Xiaotong, Sun Qimeng, Wang Bingqian, Song Jian, Zhou Jiaxuan, Chen Xitong, Chen Junsheng, Pabala Gelengjie, Hu Ziyang, Zhou Peiyuan, Wang Guangying, Ba Jin, Liu Jingji, Qian Weichang, Sun Xiaocun, Su Buqing, Simayi Aimaiti, Lü Zhengcao, Bao Enhan, Mao Yisheng, Wang Feng, as well as the Central Committee of the Communist Party, the Central Advisory Commission, the Central Discipline Inspection Commission, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Supreme People's Court, the Supreme People's Procuratorate, the Ministry of National Defense, the Central Organization Department, the Central United Front Work Department, the central committees of various democratic parties and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, the various general departments of the Military Commission, all major military units, units of the Beijing Military Region where Dong Qiwu previously worked, and various provincial, municipal, and autonomous region party and government leadership organs, including Hejin County, Shanxi Province.

Those who attended the farewell ceremony and sent wreaths included Qiao Shi, Hu Qili, Yao Yilin, Wang Zhen, Li Tieying, Wu Xueqian, Qin Jiwei, Ding Guangen, Song Renqiong, Kui Xionwen, Yan Mingfu, Wen Jiabao, Wang Pian, Liu Lantao, Li Desheng, Yang Dezhi, Xiao Ke, Chen Pishan, Chen Xilian, Duan Junyi, Xi Zhongxun, Peng Chong, Zhu Lirong, Apuo Angjinmei, Zhou Guocheng, Yan Jici, Lei Jieqiong, Wang Hanbin, Wang Fang, Ren Jianxin, Liu Fuzhi, Wang Renmei, Fang Yi, Gu Mu, Yang Jingren, Kang Keqing, Deng Zhaoxiang, Zhao Puji, Qu Wu, Ma Wenrui, Wang Enmao, Qian Xuesen, Hu Qiaomu, Cheng Siyuan, Lu Jiashi, Qian Zhengying, Hong Xuezhi, Liu Huaqing, Yang Chengwu, Chen Zaidao, and others.

During Dong Qiwu's illness, some party, government, and military leaders visited him in the hospital, and Deng Yingchao called to express her concern. After Dong Qiwu's passing, Nie Rongzhen wrote a letter to his children expressing his condolences.

Some of Dong Qiwu's relatives from Taiwan also came to Beijing to attend the funeral.

After the farewell ceremony, Dong Qiwu's body was escorted by family members and officials from the funeral office to the Babaoshan Crematorium for cremation.

### 董其武遗体告别仪式在京举行  赵紫阳杨尚昆万里等出席
程光龙
1989-03-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　董其武遗体告别仪式在京举行
    赵紫阳杨尚昆万里等出席
    新华社北京３月１７日电  （记者程光龙）向六届全国政协副主席董其武同志遗体告别的仪式今天在解放军总后勤部礼堂举行。
    赵紫阳、杨尚昆、万里等党和国家领导人以及首都各界人士８００多人，怀着沉痛的心情向这位伟大的爱国主义者、中国共产党的忠诚战士、中国人民解放军的高级将领和杰出的军事家的遗体告别。
    遗体告别仪式会场庄严肃穆。覆盖着中国共产党党旗的董其武同志的遗体，安卧在散发着缕缕清香的鲜花丛中，四周陈放着松柏和花圈。送花圈的有邓小平、李鹏、陈云、李先念、彭真、邓颖超、徐向前、聂荣臻、田纪云、江泽民、李瑞环、李锡铭、杨汝岱、宋平、胡耀邦、薄一波、王首道、伍修权、江华、李一氓、余秋里、宋时轮、张劲夫、张爱萍、陆定一、胡乔木、耿飚、姬鹏飞、黄华、黄镇、康世恩、程子华、韦国清、赛福鼎·艾则孜、荣毅仁、倪志福、费孝通、孙起孟、王丙乾、宋健、邹家华、李贵鲜、陈希同、陈俊生、帕巴拉·格列朗杰、胡子昂、周培源、王光英、巴金、刘靖基、钱伟长、孙晓村、苏步青、司马义·艾买提、吕正操、包尔汉、茅以升、汪锋，以及中共中央、中央顾问委员会、中央纪律检查委员会、全国人大常委会、国务院、中央军委、全国政协、最高人民法院、最高人民检察院、国防部、中央组织部、中央统战部、各民主党派中央和全国工商联、军委各总部、全军各大单位、董其武生前工作过的北京军区部队和一些省、市、自治区暨山西省河津县等党政军领导机关。
    参加向董其武同志遗体告别仪式并送花圈的有：乔石、胡启立、姚依林、王震、李铁映、吴学谦、秦基伟、丁关根、宋任穷、芮杏文、阎明复、温家宝、王平、刘澜涛、李德生、杨得志、肖克、陈丕显、陈锡联、段君毅、习仲勋、彭冲、朱学范、阿沛·阿旺晋美、周谷城、严济慈、叶飞、廖汉生、陈慕华、雷洁琼、王汉斌、王芳、任建新、刘复之、王任重、方毅、谷牧、杨静仁、康克清、邓兆祥、赵朴初、屈武、马文瑞、王恩茂、钱学森、胡绳、程思远、卢嘉锡、钱正英、洪学智、刘华清、杨成武、陈再道等。
    董其武同志病重期间，部分党、政、军领导同志曾到医院看望，邓颖超同志曾打电话慰问。董其武同志逝世后，聂荣臻同志给其子女写信表示哀悼。
    董其武同志现在台湾的部分亲属也专程来京奔丧。
    遗体告别仪式结束后，董其武同志的遗体由治丧办公室负责同志陪同家属护送到八宝山火化。
