Title: The Mudu Railway Bureau Achieves 2000 Days of Safe Production
Author: Lǐ Qīng Pán Guǎng Tāo
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 2

As of today, the Mudanjiang Railway Bureau has achieved 2001 days without a major train accident, leading all major railway bureaus in the country. This accomplishment has earned them commendations from the Heilongjiang Provincial Government and the Ministry of Railways.

The Mudanjiang Railway Bureau has consistently prioritized safety, implementing stringent measures to ensure the well-being of passengers and the smooth operation of trains. Their dedication to safety has not only prevented accidents but also set a benchmark for other railway bureaus across the nation. The bureau's achievements highlight the importance of safety in the railway industry and serve as an inspiration for others to strive for similar success.

### 牡铁局安全生产二千天
李青　潘广韬
1989-03-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　牡铁局安全生产二千天
    本报哈尔滨三月十三日电  到今天为止，牡丹江铁路分局已经二千零一十二天无行车重大事故，居全国铁路系统各较大分局之首，受到黑龙江省政府和铁道部的表彰。　　　　　　　　　（李青　潘广韬）
