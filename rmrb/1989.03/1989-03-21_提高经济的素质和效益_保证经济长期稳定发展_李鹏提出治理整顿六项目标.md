Title: Improve Economic Quality and Efficiency to Ensure Long-Term Stable Development
Author:
Date: 1989-03-21
Edition: 1

The State Council Premier Li Peng said at the Second Session of the Seventh National People's Congress that starting this year, we need to spend two years or more to strive to achieve the six objectives of the economic rectification and adjustment.

The six objectives are:

1. Eliminate economic overheating and bring the growth rate to a more reasonable level.
2. Curb inflation, making the 1989 price increase significantly lower than in 1988, and further reducing the increase in 1990 and beyond.
3. Reduce the scale of fixed asset investment to match the country's capacity, and control the rapid growth of consumption funds to align with the growth of national income.
4. Gradually alleviate the contradiction between total demand and total supply, achieving a basic balance in finance, credit, materials, and foreign exchange.
5. Seriously adjust the economic structure, increasing the production of major agricultural products such as grain, cotton, and oil, and easing the tight supply of energy, transportation, and raw materials.
6. Establish and improve necessary economic regulations and macro-control and supervision systems, and actively promote the construction of a new socialist commodity economic order.

Only by achieving these objectives can we significantly improve the quality and efficiency of our national economy, ensuring its long-term stable development.

### 提高经济的素质和效益　保证经济长期稳定发展  李鹏提出治理整顿六项目标

1989-03-21
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　提高经济的素质和效益　保证经济长期稳定发展        
    李鹏提出治理整顿六项目标
    新华社北京３月２０日电  国务院总理李鹏在七届全国人大二次会议上提出，从今年起，我们要用两年或者更长一些的时间，努力实现治理整顿所要达到的六项目标。
    这六项目标是：
    一、消除经济过热，把发展速度降到比较合理的水平。
    二、遏制通货膨胀，使１９８９年物价上涨幅度明显低于１９８８年，１９９０年以后的上涨幅度进一步下降。
    三、压缩固定资产投资规模，使它同国力承担的可能相适应；控制消费基金的过快增长，使它同国民收入的增长相适应。
    四、逐步缓解社会总需求大于总供给的矛盾，实现财政、信贷、物资、外汇的基本平衡。
    五、认真调整经济结构，使粮、棉、油等主要农产品的产量有较多增加，使能源、交通、原材料供应的紧张状况有所缓和。
    六、建立健全必要的经济法规以及宏观调控体系和监督体系，积极推进社会主义商品经济新秩序的建设。
    只有实现这些目标，才能使我国经济的素质和效益明显提高，保证国民经济的长期稳定发展。
