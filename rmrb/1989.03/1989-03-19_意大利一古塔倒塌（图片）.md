Title: A Medieval Tower in Italy Collapses
Author:
Date: 1989-03-19
Edition: 4

A medieval tower in Italy collapses

On March 17, a medieval tower built 900 years ago in the city of Pavia, Italy, suddenly collapsed, resulting in three deaths.
