Title: Six Types of Food Will Become Popular in China
Author: Zhang Ming
Date: 1989-03-05
Edition: 8

According to experts in the food industry, six new types of food are expected to become popular in China:

    Longevity Food: Made primarily from soybean protein, with the addition of peanuts, sesame, Polygonum multiflorum, and jujubes. It has the characteristics of low calories, high protein, and easy digestion.

    Artificial Cheese: Made from soybean meal powder after low-temperature solvent defatting, it can be used to make a variety of foods.

    Yogurt: It has the effects of easy digestion, preventing constipation, preventing diarrhea, inhibiting harmful bacteria, anti-cancer, and lowering cholesterol.

    Whey Protein Food: Contains 80% protein and a small amount of lactose, suitable for elderly people to consume.

    Pollen Food: Contains a variety of amino acids, proteins, and rich fats, inorganic salts, vitamins, and has high nutritional value. It is a health food for the elderly, children, patients, and athletes.

    Low-Sodium Food: High-sodium foods can increase vascular resistance, making it easy to cause heart disease and hypertension. Therefore, low-sodium soy sauce, low-sodium tofu, and low-sodium pickled vegetables will become increasingly popular.

    (February 18, China Business News, Zhang Ming)

### 六种食品将在我国流行
张鸣
1989-03-05
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

　　六种食品将在我国流行
    据食品业有关行家分析，我国将流行六种新型食品：
    寿翁食品：以大豆蛋白为主要原料，配以花生米、芝麻、何首乌、大枣等制成。具有低热量、高蛋白、易消化等特点。
    人造奶酪：是经低温溶剂脱脂后的豆粕粉，它可以配制成多种食品。
    酸乳：具有易消化、防便秘、防腹泻、抑制有害细菌、抗癌和降低胆固醇等作用。
    乳清蛋白食品：含有８０％的蛋白质和少量乳糖，适用于老年人食用。
    花粉食品：含有多种氨基酸、蛋白质和丰富的脂肪、无机盐、维生素等营养价值较高，是老幼、病人、运动员的保健食品。
    低盐食品：高盐食品会使血管阻力增加，易引起心脏病、高血压等。因此，低盐酱油、低盐豆腐、低盐咸菜等将日益受到欢迎。
    　　　　　　（２月１８日《中国商报》张鸣文）
