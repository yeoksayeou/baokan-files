Title: Celebrity Bridge Tournament Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-03-13
Edition: 4

The "Changlin Cup" celebrity bridge tournament, divided into red and green groups, was held today at the Great Hall of the People in Beijing. The results were as follows: the red group's south-north champions were won by Wan Li and Rong Le Di; the east-west champions were Wang Hanbin and Wang Daming. In the green group, Nie Weiping and Deng Pu Fang, as partners, won the south-north championship, while Han Boping and Zhang Mingyi won the east-west championship.

### 名人桥牌赛在京举行

1989-03-13
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　名人桥牌赛在京举行
    本报北京三月十二日讯  分红绿两组的“常林杯”名人桥牌赛今天在北京人民大会堂举行。结果红组南北冠军由万里与荣乐弟夺得；东西冠军得主是王汉斌和王大明。在绿组的比赛中，聂卫平与邓朴方搭档获南北组冠军，韩伯平和张明义获东西组冠军。
