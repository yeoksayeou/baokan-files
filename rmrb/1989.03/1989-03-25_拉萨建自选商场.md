Title: Construction of a Self-Service Supermarket in Lhasa
Author: Liu Wei, Lu Xiaofei
Date: 1989-03-25
Edition: 2

Lhasa's first self-service supermarket, the Lhasa Foreign Trade Center Self-Service Supermarket, opened today. This is the first self-service supermarket on the Tibetan Plateau.

The supermarket offers over 300 different products. This morning, more than 2,000 customers visited, with sales reaching over 15,000 yuan.

### 拉萨建自选商场
刘伟　卢小飞
1989-03-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　拉萨建自选商场
    本报拉萨３月２４日电  拉萨外贸中心自选商场今天开业，这是西藏高原第一家自选商场。
    商场有３００余种商品。今天上午，有２０００多顾客流量，收入达１．５万多元。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（刘伟　卢小飞）
