Title: All National People's Congress Delegates Arrive in Beijing
Author: Meng Renquan
Date: 1989-03-18
Edition: 2

All 32 delegations to the Seventh National People's Congress, Second Session, arrived in Beijing on March 17th. In interviews, they expressed their determination to participate in the conference with a sense of responsibility as masters of the country. The photo shows the Sichuan Provincial delegation arriving in Beijing.

		Reporter: Meng Renquan

### 全国人大代表昨天全部抵京（图片）
孟仁泉
1989-03-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    全国人大代表昨天全部抵京
    出席全国人大七届二次会议的32个代表团，3月17日全部到达北京。他们在接受采访时表示，要以主人翁的责任感参加大会。图为四川省代表团抵京。　
                               本报记者  孟仁泉摄
