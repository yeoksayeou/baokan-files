Title: Will This Trip Be Successful?
Author: Chen Jingkai
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 8

	I am ashamed! I am ashamed!

	On February 20, the "Guangming Daily" published an article titled "Will This Trip Be Successful?" written by Chen Jingkai. The article expresses the author's deep sense of shame and regret over the recent events in China. Chen Jingkai reflects on the tumultuous period and the challenges faced by the nation. He acknowledges the mistakes made and the need for introspection and reform. The piece serves as a call for unity and a renewed commitment to the principles that guide the country forward. Chen Jingkai's words resonate with a sense of humility and a determination to learn from the past to build a better future.
