Title: China Hopes for Friendly Relations Among Balkan Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 2

China hopes for friendly relations among Balkan countries.

New China News Agency, Beijing, March 9. Foreign Ministry Spokesman Li Zhaoxing said today that China hopes the Balkan countries can maintain friendly relations, which will be beneficial for the stability and development of the region.

Li Zhaoxing made the above remarks at a press conference this afternoon when an overseas journalist asked about China's views on the "Kosovo incident" in Yugoslavia.

The spokesman said that the Kosovo issue is an internal affair of Yugoslavia. China hopes and believes that Yugoslavia can properly handle its own problems.

### 中国希望巴尔干各国友好相处

1989-03-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　中国希望巴尔干各国友好相处
    新华社北京３月９日电  外交部发言人李肇星今天在这里说，中国希望巴尔干各国友好相处，以利于该地区的安定和发展。
    李肇星是在今天下午的新闻发布会上，当一位外国记者问及中国对南斯拉夫“科索沃事件”的看法时作上述表示的。
    这位发言人说，科索沃问题是南斯拉夫的内政。中国希望并且相信南斯拉夫能够妥善地处理好自己的问题。
