Title: China Wins Championship at Hannover International Fair Gymnastics Competition
Author:
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, March 12th.

The Chinese gymnastics team today demonstrated their skilled and graceful techniques in front of 3,000 spectators at the Hannover Sports Hall, securing the championship at the 2nd Hannover International Fair Gymnastics Competition. The Chinese team consisted of three athletes: Guo Linxian, Fan Di, and Tian Ying (rhythmic gymnastics). The team's final score was 29.15 points. Fan Di scored a perfect 10 on the uneven bars, playing a crucial role in China's victory.

### 中国队获汉诺威国际博览会杯体操赛冠军

1989-03-14
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　中国队获汉诺威国际博览会杯体操赛冠军
    新华社波恩３月１２日电  中国体操队今天在汉诺威体育馆３０００名观众面前表现出娴熟、优美的技巧，夺得了第２届汉诺威国际博览会杯体操赛冠军。中国队由国林显、樊迪和田英（艺术体操）３名选手组成。中国队的最后得分是２９．１５分，其中樊迪在高低杠上获得满分１０分，为中国队的胜利立下头功。
