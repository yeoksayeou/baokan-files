Title: The Fifth Session of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National Committee of the CPPCC Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 1

The fifth session of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) concluded this afternoon at the CPPCC Hall.

Li Xiannian, Chairman of the CPPCC, attended the afternoon session.

Under the chairmanship of Vice Chairman Wang Renzhong, the meeting adopted the agenda and schedule for the second session of the Seventh National Committee of the CPPCC; approved the work report of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National Committee of the CPPCC; approved the list of additional members of the Seventh National Committee of the CPPCC; and approved the report of the Committee on Proposals regarding the handling of proposals since the first session of the Seventh National Committee of the CPPCC.

The Standing Committee also made decisions on the grouping method and the list of group conveners for the second session of the Seventh National Committee of the CPPCC, and the list of attendees.

Vice Chairpersons of the CPPCC, Fang Yi, Gu Mu, Kang Keqing, Deng Zhaoxiang, Zhao Puji, Ma Wenrui, Wang Enmao, Qian Xuesen, Hu Sheng, Sun Xiaocun, Cheng Siyuan, Lu Jiaxi, Qian Zhengying, Su Buqing, and Sima Yiming (Aimaiti), Secretary-General Zhou Shaozhen, attended the meeting.

### 政协常委会第五次会议闭会  通过政协七届二次会议有关事宜

1989-03-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　政协常委会第五次会议闭会
    通过政协七届二次会议有关事宜
    新华社北京３月１４日电  七届全国政协常委会第五次会议今天下午在政协礼堂闭会。
    李先念主席出席了今天下午的会议。
    在王任重副主席的主持下，会议通过了政协七届二次会议的议程和日程；通过了政协第七届全国委员会常务委员会工作报告；通过了政协七届全国委员会委员增补名单；通过了政协提案委员会关于政协七届一次会议以来提案工作情况的报告。
    这次常委会还就政协七届二次会议的分组办法和小组召集人名单，列席会议的人员名单作出了决定。
    全国政协副主席方毅、谷牧、康克清、邓兆祥、赵朴初、马文瑞、王恩茂、钱学森、胡绳、孙晓村、程思远、卢嘉锡、钱正英、苏步青、司马义·艾买提，秘书长周绍铮，出席了今天的会议。
