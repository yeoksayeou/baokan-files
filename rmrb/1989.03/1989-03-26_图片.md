	Pole Vaulting
	(March 8, 1989, China Business News, Tang Shengzhi)

	The image depicts a dynamic scene of a pole vaulting event. The athlete is captured in mid-air, gracefully soaring over the bar. The background features a clear blue sky, adding a sense of openness and freedom to the scene. The artist has skillfully used lines and shading to emphasize the athlete's determination and strength, as well as the tension and excitement of the moment. The work is vivid and full of movement, effectively conveying the spirit of sportsmanship and the pursuit of excellence.
