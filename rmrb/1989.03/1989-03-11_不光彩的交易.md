Title: Shady Dealings
Author: Xu Xianliang
Date: 1989-03-11
Edition: 6

    Shady Dealings

    Protective gear issued by the state to workers is meant for labor protection. Inside the residential area of the Wuhan Steel Company, some unlicensed vendors openly engage in bartering, exchanging protective gear for other items, while some workers have an abundance of protective gear to trade. Why do workers accumulate so much protective gear? Who will stop these transactions? In the picture, a worker is seen exchanging more than ten pairs of long welding gloves for mahjong tiles.

    Wuhan Iron and Steel Company Repair Company, Xu Xianliang

### 不光彩的交易
徐贤亮
1989-03-11
第6版(读者来信)
专栏：耳闻目睹

    不光彩的交易
    国家发给工人的劳保用品是用于劳动保护的。武钢家属住宅区内，一些无证摊贩公然以物易物，换劳保用品，而一些职工却又有那么多的劳保用品去交换。为什么职工竟能积累这许多劳保用品？谁来制止这种交易？图片中这一职工正用十多双焊工长手套换麻将。
                     武汉钢铁公司检修公司　徐贤亮
