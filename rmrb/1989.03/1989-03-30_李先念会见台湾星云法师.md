Title: Li Xiannian Meets with Taiwan's Master Xing Yun
Author: Changzheng
Date: 1989-03-30
Edition: 2

	Li Xiannian hopes for more cultural exchanges across the Taiwan Strait to promote the peaceful reunification of the motherland. Li Xiannian expressed this hope today when he met with Master Xing Yun, the founder of Taiwan's Foguangshan and the Hsi Lai Temple in the United States.

	At 3 PM, Li Xiannian met with Master Xing Yun in the Fujian Hall of the Great Hall of the People. He welcomed Master Xing Yun's visit to the mainland for exchanges and reunions with relatives, and hoped that Master Xing Yun could visit more places.

	During the meeting, Master Xing Yun presented Li Xiannian with a jade "Chinese knot" inscribed with the words "auspicious and fortunate" and some books.

	Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and President of the Buddhist Association of China, Zhao Puxin, was present at the meeting.

### 李先念会见台湾星云法师
长征
1989-03-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　李先念会见台湾星云法师
    据新华社北京３月２９日电  （记者长征）全国政协主席李先念希望海峡两岸有更多的文化交流活动，促进祖国的和平统一。李先念是今天在会见台湾佛光山和美国西来寺创始人、台湾的星云法师时表达这一愿望的。
    下午３时，李先念在人民大会堂福建厅会见了星云法师。他对星云法师来大陆交流、探亲表示欢迎，并希望星云法师能到各地多看看。
    会见时，星云法师向李先念赠送了刻有“吉祥如意”字样的玉制的“中国结”和一些书籍。
    全国政协副主席、中国佛教协会会长赵朴初会见时在座。
