Title: Newly Elected Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference - Ding Guangxun's Biography
Author:
Date: 1989-03-28
Edition: 2

Ding Guangxun, born in September 1915, is from Shanghai. He graduated from Shanghai St. John's University, the Teachers College of Columbia University in New York, and Union Theological Seminary in New York. He holds a Master of Arts and a Doctor of Theology. He currently serves as a member of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress, a member of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the National People's Congress, the President of Nanjing Union Theological Seminary, Vice President of Nanjing University, Vice Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Chairman of the Chinese Christian Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee, President of the China Christian Council, and Chairman of the Board of Directors of the China Aid Foundation.

After 1938, he served as a secretary of the Student Department of the Shanghai Christian Youth Association, a secretary of the Canadian Christian Student Movement, and a secretary of the World Student Christian Federation in Geneva, Switzerland. In September 1951, after returning to China, he served as the General Secretary of the Shanghai Guanghua Society, the President of Nanjing Theological Seminary, the Bishop of the Anglican Diocese of Zhejiang, the Vice President of Nanjing University, the Director of the Institute of Religious Studies, a member, vice chairman, and chairman of the Chinese Christian Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee. He was a representative of the Third, Fourth, and Fifth National People's Congresses, a member of the Standing Committee of the Sixth National People's Congress, a member of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the National People's Congress, a member of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and a standing member of the Fifth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 新增选的全国政协副主席  丁光训简历

1989-03-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　新增选的全国政协副主席         
    丁光训简历
    丁光训，１９１５年９月生，上海人；上海圣约翰大学、美国纽约哥伦比亚大学师范学院、纽约协和神学院毕业；文学硕士、神学博士。他现任第七届全国人大常委会委员、全国人大外事委员会委员，金陵协和神学院院长，南京大学副校长，江苏省政协副主席，中国基督教三自爱国运动委员会主席、中国基督教协会会长，中国爱德基金会董事长。
    １９３８年后，他任上海基督教青年会学生部干事，加拿大基督教学生运动干事，瑞士日内瓦世界基督教学生同盟干事；１９５１年９月回国后，任上海广学会总干事，金陵神学院院长，圣公会浙江教区主教，南京大学副校长兼宗教研究所所长，中国基督教三自爱国运动委员会常委、副主席、主席。他是第三、四、五届全国人大代表，第六届全国人大常委会委员，全国人大外事委员会委员；第三届全国政协委员，第五届全国政协常委。
    （新华社）（附图片）
