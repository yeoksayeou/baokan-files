Title: U.S. Underwater Missile Launch Fails
Author:
Date: 1989-03-24
Edition: 4

The People's Daily, Washington, March 21st.

The U.S. Navy's underwater test launch of a Trident II missile failed today. At 11:20 AM, the nuclear submarine "Tennessee" launched a Trident II missile from the Atlantic Ocean near Cape Canaveral. After the missile emerged from the water and the first-stage rocket ignited, the missile malfunctioned and crashed into the ocean 4 seconds later. The Trident II missile is a new type of intercontinental ballistic missile launched from submarines by the U.S. It has been tested from land before, and today's launch was the first underwater test.

### 美国水下发射导弹失败

1989-03-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国水下发射导弹失败
    本报华盛顿3月21日电  美国海军今天水下试射三叉戟Ⅱ式导弹失败。上午11时20分，“田纳西”号核潜艇在卡纳维拉尔角的大西洋海域发射一枚三叉戟Ⅱ式导弹，当导弹离开水面，第一级火箭点燃后，导弹发生故障，4秒后坠入大洋（见上图）。三叉戟Ⅱ式导弹是美国由潜艇发射的新式洲际导弹，曾从陆地试射过，今天是首次从海下试射。（附图片）
