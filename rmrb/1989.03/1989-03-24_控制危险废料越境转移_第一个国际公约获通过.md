Title: Control of Transboundary Movement of Hazardous Wastes
Author:
Date: 1989-03-24
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Basel, March 22nd.

Representatives from 118 countries, after three days of intense discussions, today adopted the "Convention on the Control of Transboundary Movements of Hazardous Wastes and Their Disposal."

This is the first international convention on hazardous waste issues and the third international convention on environmental protection.

The convention stipulates that the exporting country of hazardous waste must notify the importing country and relevant countries in advance of the quantity and nature of the waste. The establishment of this prior notification system aims to control the illegal transboundary movement of hazardous waste, preventing developing countries, especially African countries, from being harmed by the large-scale transfer of hazardous waste from industrialized countries.

The convention also requires countries to reduce the production of hazardous waste to the lowest possible level, manage hazardous waste in the most environmentally friendly manner, and store and treat hazardous waste locally as much as possible.

The convention calls for developed and developing countries to strengthen various forms of international cooperation in the field of hazardous waste management, including information exchange, training of technical personnel, and technology transfer.

The convention will enter into force 90 days after it is signed by 20 countries.

### 控制危险废料越境转移  第一个国际公约获通过

1989-03-24
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　控制危险废料越境转移        
    第一个国际公约获通过
    据新华社巴塞尔３月２２日电  来自１１８个国家的代表经过３天紧张讨论，今天在这里通过了《控制危险废料越境转移及其处置公约》。
    这是关于危险废料问题的第１个国际公约，也是有关环境保护问题的第３个国际公约。
    公约规定，危险废料出口国必须事先向进口国和有关国家通报废料的数量及性质。建立这种事先通报制度的目的是控制危险废料非法越境转移，使第三世界国家、尤其是非洲国家免受工业化国家大量向外转移危险废料所带来的危害。
    公约还要求各国把危险废料的产量减少到最低限度，用最有利于环境保护的方式来管理危险废料，尽可能就地贮存和处理危险废料。
    公约呼吁发达国家和发展中国家加强在危险废料处理领域里多种形式的国际合作，包括交流情报、培训技术人员、转让技术等。
    公约将在有２０个国家签约后的第９０天开始生效。
