Title: Zheng Lijuan Wins Hong Kong One-Mile Race
Author:
Date: 1989-03-07
Edition: 4

Chinese athlete Zheng Lijuan won the women's division of the Hong Kong's 4th HSBC International Gold One-Mile Invitational on the afternoon of March 5 with a time of 4 minutes and 52 seconds, making it the fourth consecutive year that a Chinese athlete has won the title.

The runner-up in the women's division was Hong Kong athlete Bevis with a time of 5 minutes and 1 second.

### 郑丽娟获港一哩跑赛冠军

1989-03-07
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　郑丽娟获港一哩跑赛冠军
    中国选手郑丽娟５日下午在香港举行的香港第４届中银杯国际金一哩邀请赛女子组的比赛中，以４分５２秒的成绩获得冠军，从而使中国选手连续４届均获冠军。
    获得女子组亚军的是香港选手贝维斯（５分０１秒）。
