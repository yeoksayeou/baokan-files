Title: The People's Armed Police Welcoming Ceremony Artillery Unit Achieves Meritorious Deed
Author: Zhaowenbin Zhaobaoyu
Date: 1989-03-30
Edition: 2

Today, the Beijing Command of the People's Armed Police announced an order, awarding the artillery company of the 11th Regiment, which was responsible for firing the salute during the national welcoming ceremony, a collective second-class meritorious deed. On March 28, Chairman Yang Shangkun inscribed the words: "Diligent training, bringing glory to the nation" for this company.

### 武警迎宾礼炮部队立功
赵文彬　赵宝玉
1989-03-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　武警迎宾礼炮部队立功
    本报北京三月二十九日讯  今天，武警北京总队宣布通令，给在国家迎宾仪式上担负鸣放礼炮任务的十一支队礼炮中队荣记集体二等功。国家主席杨尚昆三月二十八日为这个中队题词：“刻苦训练，为国争光”。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（赵文彬　赵宝玉）
