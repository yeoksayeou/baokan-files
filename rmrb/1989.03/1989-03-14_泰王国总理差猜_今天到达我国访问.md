Title: Thai Prime Minister Chatchai Arrives in China for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency

At the invitation of Premier Li Peng, General Chatchai Chunhawan, Prime Minister of Thailand, and his wife will pay an official visit to China from March 14 to 17.

Chatchai Chunhawan, the Prime Minister, was born on April 5, 1922, in Bangkok, Thailand. He participated in the Second World War. In 1951, he was elected as a member of the National Assembly. After 1957, he entered the diplomatic service, serving as an ambassador and representative to the United Nations Office in Geneva. Since 1972, he has held various positions, including Deputy Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs. In 1976 and 1980, he served as the Minister of Industry in two cabinets. Since February 1978, he has been the Chairman of the Thailand-China Friendship Association. Since August 1986, he has served as the Deputy Prime Minister of Thailand. In August 1988, he was officially appointed by the King as the Prime Minister and also served as the Minister of Defense. Since 1986, he has also been the Chairman of the Thai Nation Party.

Prime Minister Chatchai has made significant contributions and was promoted to the rank of General in 1988.

### 泰王国总理差猜  今天到达我国访问

1989-03-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：人物介绍

　　泰王国总理差猜
    今天到达我国访问
    应李鹏总理的邀请，泰王国总理差猜·春哈旺上将偕夫人于本月１４日至１７日对我国进行正式访问。
    差猜·春哈旺总理１９２２年４月５日生于泰国曼谷。曾参加过第二次世界大战。１９５１年当选为国会议员。１９５７年以后转入外交界，先后担任驻外大使以及驻日内瓦联合国办事处代表。１９７２年后，曾任外交部厅长、副部长和部长。１９７６年和１９８０年曾任两届内阁工业部长。１９７８年２月起当选为泰中友协主席。１９８６年８月起任泰王国副总理，１９８８年８月被国王正式任命为总理，并兼任国防部长。自１９８６年至今，还任泰国民族党主席。
    差猜总理功勋卓著，１９８８年晋升为上将。
    （据新华社）
