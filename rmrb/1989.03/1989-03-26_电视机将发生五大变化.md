Title: TVs to Undergo Five Major Changes
Author: Xiao Yuanzhen
Date: 1989-03-26
Edition: 8

The World Electronics Association recently released a report on practical electronic technology predictions, indicating that within three years, traditional televisions will undergo fundamental changes in five aspects:

1. The number of scan lines on TV screens will increase from the current 625 to 1250. This will double the picture quality and surpass the clarity of current movies.

2. TV screens will be several times larger than they are now. They will be 3 feet high, 4 feet wide, and only 4 inches thick, allowing them to be hung on walls.

3. Flat TVs will no longer be a cumbersome item in the room but will become an indispensable decorative piece.

4. The screens will use transparent crystal glass.

5. By installing a special antenna outside residential areas, viewers will be able to watch programs from dozens of countries and more than a dozen television stations via satellite.

### 电视机将发生五大变化
肖元真
1989-03-26
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

　　电视机将发生五大变化
    世界电子学会最近发布的实用电子技术预测报告表明，３年内传统意义上的电视机将在５个方面发生根本性变化：
    １、电视屏幕将以目前６２５根扫描线增加到１２５０根。这样，画面质量将提高一倍，清晰度将超过目前的电影。
    ２、电视屏幕将比目前增大几倍。将有３英尺高、４英尺宽、仅４英寸厚，可挂在墙上。
    ３、扁平的电视机不再成为房间拥挤的累赘，而变成必不可少的装饰品。
    ４、屏幕将采用透明水晶玻璃。
    ５、在住宅区外装一个特别天线，就可通过卫星收看数十个国家十几个电视台的节目。
　　　　　　　　　　　（３月９日《中国消费者报》　肖元真文）
