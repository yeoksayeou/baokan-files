Title: Gold Mine Discovered in Beijing
Author: Xia Junsheng
Date: 1989-03-31
Edition: 2

Beijing has discovered a gold mine. According to the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Geology and Mineral Resources, a gold mine has recently been discovered in Huairou County, Beijing. The proven gold reserves are 2.3 tons.

### 北京发现金矿
夏俊生
1989-03-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　北京发现金矿
    新华社北京三月三十日电  （记者夏俊生）据北京市地质矿产局透露，北京市新近在怀柔县发现了一个金矿，已探明的黄金储量为二点三吨。
