Title: The Seventh Session of the Second Plenary Meeting of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Concludes
Author: Zhang Heping, He Wei
Date: 1989-03-28
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 27th.

The Seventh Session of the Second Plenary Meeting of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) concluded today in the Great Hall of the People after successfully completing all scheduled agenda items.

The meeting called on the CPPCC to carry forward the glorious tradition of unity and cooperation, striving for the prosperity of the nation and the revitalization of the Chinese people.

Chairman Li Xiannian, Vice-Chairmen Wang Renzhong, Yan Mingfu, Fang Yi, Gu Mu, Yang Jingren, Kang Keqing, Baba La Ge Liangjie, Hu Qiaomu, Wang Guangying, Deng Zhaoxiang, Zhao Puji, Qu Wu, Ma Wenrui, Liu Jingji, Wang Enmao, Qian Xuesen, Qian Weichang, Hu Sheng, Sun Xiaocun, Cheng Siyuan, Lu Jiashi, Qian Zhengying, Su Buqing, and Si Ma Yimaiti, along with Secretary-General Zhou Shaozhen, took their seats in the front row of the presidium. Party and state leaders Zhao Ziyang, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, Wan Li, Qiao Shi, Hu Qili, Yao Yilin, and Wang Zhen attended the meeting to congratulate the successful conclusion of the conference.

Wang Renzhong presided over the meeting, which was attended by 1,687 members.

The meeting adopted a new method of voting by raising hands individually to elect Kuang Ruwei, Vice-Chairman of the China Democratic Revolutionary Party Central Committee, and Ding Guangxin, President of the Chinese Christian Council, as Vice-Chairmen of the National Committee of the CPPCC. Li Kaixin, Vice-Chairman of the China Democratic Revolutionary Party Central Committee, Li Ganliu, Vice-Chairman of the China Democratic Revolutionary Party Central Committee, He Luli, Vice-Chairman of the China Democratic Revolutionary Party Central Committee, Yang Di, Deputy Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Qian Zhongshu, Vice-President of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, and Jie Feng, former Governor of Hebei Province, were elected as Standing Committee Members of the National Committee of the CPPCC.

In his speech at the closing meeting, Li Xiannian said that the Seventh Session of the Second Plenary Meeting of the National Committee of the CPPCC had adhered to the principle of seeking truth from facts, promoted democratic style, and embodied the spirit of unity and struggle.

He pointed out that at present, we must maintain the stability of the economic situation while continuing to ensure political stability and unity.

He emphasized that any actions that harm economic stability and political stability, and any practices that violate the four cardinal principles and the policy of reform and opening up, are not conducive to our cause and do not conform to the fundamental interests of the people of all ethnic groups in the country. They must be resolutely prevented and opposed.

He said that the CPPCC has the duty and responsibility to assist the Party and the government in managing state affairs well.

Before the closing of the meeting, the Political Resolution of the Seventh Session of the Second Plenary Meeting of the National Committee of the CPPCC was adopted.

The resolution stated that the government's work report is in line with reality, and it is hoped that the State Council will further summarize its experience and formulate more complete and specific measures to solve the existing problems, so as to ensure the implementation of the policies and arrangements for rectification and deepening of reform.

The meeting also adopted the resolution on the work report of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the CPPCC and the report on the examination of proposals submitted to the Seventh Session of the Second Plenary Meeting of the National Committee of the CPPCC.

After the closing of the meeting, Li Xiannian and other leaders of the CPPCC warmly met with the newly elected Vice-Chairmen, Standing Committee Members, and Members of the National Committee of the CPPCC in the Hunan Hall and took a group photo with them.

### 全国政协七届二次会议闭幕
张何平　何伟
1989-03-28
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　全国政协七届二次会议闭幕
    本报北京３月２７日讯  记者张何平、何伟报道：全国政协七届二次会议在圆满完成各项预定议程后，今天上午在人民大会堂闭幕。
    会议号召人民政协发扬风雨同舟、和衷共济的光荣传统，共图国家的昌盛和民族的兴旺。
    全国政协主席李先念，副主席王任重、阎明复、方毅、谷牧、杨静仁、康克清、帕巴拉·格列朗杰、胡子昂、王光英、邓兆祥、赵朴初、屈武、马文瑞、刘靖基、王恩茂、钱学森、钱伟长、胡绳、孙晓村、程思远、卢嘉锡、钱正英、苏步青、司马义·艾买提，秘书长周绍铮，在主席台前排就座。赵紫阳、杨尚昆、李鹏、万里、乔石、胡启立、姚依林、王震等党和国家领导人到会，祝贺大会取得圆满成功。
    王任重主持了有１６８７名委员出席的大会。
    会议首次采用逐个举手表决的方式，增选民革中央副主席侯镜如和中国基督教协会会长丁光训为全国政协副主席；增选七届全国政协委员李开信、民革中央副主席李赣骝、民革中央副主席何鲁丽、中共上海市委副书记杨堤、中国社会科学院副院长钱钟书、河北省原省长解峰６人为全国政协常委。
    在今天的闭幕会上，李先念说，全国政协七届二次会议坚持实事求是，发扬民主作风，体现了同心同德、团结奋斗的精神。
    他指出，当前，我们要维护经济局势的稳定，同时继续保持政治上的安定团结。
    他强调，任何损害经济稳定和政治稳定的行为，任何违反四项基本原则和违反改革开放方针的做法，都不利于我们的事业，都不符合全国各族人民的根本利益，必须坚决防止和反对。
    他说，人民政协有义务有责任协助党和政府，把国家的事情办好。
    大会闭幕前，通过了政协七届二次会议政治决议。
    决议认为政府工作报告是符合实际的，希望国务院进一步总结经验，制定更加完整和具体的解决当前存在问题的措施，以保证治理整顿、深化改革方针和部署的贯彻落实。
    会议还通过了政协常委会工作报告的决议和政协七届二次会议提案审查情况的报告。
    大会闭幕后，李先念等政协领导人在湖南厅亲切会见了新增选的全国政协副主席、全国政协常委和委员，并同他们合影留念。
