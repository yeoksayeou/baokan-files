Title: Two Farmers from Gao'an Sacrifice Themselves to Save Others
Author: Lí Bókē
Date: 1989-03-05
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, March 3rd.

Two farmers from Gao'an County, Jiangxi Province, Xie Liangneng from Bajing Township and Jin Changwei from Shitian Township, sacrificed themselves to save others and were recently posthumously honored as revolutionary martyrs by the Jiangxi Provincial People's Government.

### 高安两农民舍己救别人
黎伯基
1989-03-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　高安两农民舍己救别人
    新华社南昌３月３日电  （通讯员黎伯基）江西省高安县八井镇农民谢良能、石脑乡农民金长伟舍己救人，英勇献身，最近同时被江西省人民政府追认为革命烈士。
