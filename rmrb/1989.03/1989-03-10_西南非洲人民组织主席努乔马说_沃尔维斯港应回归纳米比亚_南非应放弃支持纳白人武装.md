Title: SWAPO Leader Nujoma Says Walvis Bay Should Return to Namibia, South Africa Should Abandon Support for White Armed Groups
Author:
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Harare, March 8th.

The Chairman of the South West Africa People's Organization (SWAPO), Nujoma, said today here that the organization's next goal after Namibia's independence is to have Walvis Bay return to Namibia.

Nujoma made the remarks after attending the closing of the summit of the Frontline States in Africa this evening. He said, "We are striving to liberate every inch of Namibia's territory, including Walvis Bay."

Nujoma said that SWAPO will never acknowledge South Africa's claim that Walvis Bay is part of South Africa and not independent Namibia.

Walvis Bay is the only port on Namibia's 1,000-kilometer coastline and is still illegally occupied by South Africa.

Xinhua News Agency, Lusaka, March 8th.

SWAPO Chairman Nujoma urged South Africa to implement UN Security Council Resolution 435 and abandon support for white armed groups in Namibia on March 7th.

According to the Zambia Daily Mail, Nujoma made the remarks during an interview with the newspaper's reporter.

Nujoma pointed out that the ruling party in South Africa is calling on whites in the territories occupied by South Africa to take up arms to resist SWAPO's victory in Namibia and the implementation of UN Security Council Resolution 435.

Nujoma said that the people of Namibia are weary of war because they have suffered the hardships of war for the past 23 years. He said that SWAPO had taken up arms to resist oppression, but according to international peace agreements, no Namibian needs to take up arms again.

### 西南非洲人民组织主席努乔马说  沃尔维斯港应回归纳米比亚  南非应放弃支持纳白人武装

1989-03-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　西南非洲人民组织主席努乔马说
    沃尔维斯港应回归纳米比亚
    南非应放弃支持纳白人武装
    新华社哈博罗内３月８日电  西南非洲人民组织主席努乔马今天在这里说，该组织在纳米比亚实现独立后的下一个目标是使沃尔维斯港回归纳米比亚。
    努乔马是在出席今晚在这里结束的非洲前线国家首脑会议后对记者说上述话的。他说：“我们正在争取解放纳米比亚的每一寸领土，包括沃尔维斯港。”
    努乔马说，西南非洲人民组织将永远不承认南非有关沃尔维斯港是南非的一部分而不属于独立的纳米比亚的说法。
    沃尔维斯港是纳米比亚１０００公里长的海岸线上唯一的港口，迄今仍被南非非法占领着。
    新华社卢萨卡３月８日电　西南非洲人民组织主席努乔马７日在这里敦促南非实施联合国安理会４３５号决议和放弃对纳米比亚白人武装的支持。
    据赞比亚《邮报》今天报道，努乔马是在接受该报记者采访时说上述话的。
    努乔马指出，南非执政党号召在被南非占领的领土的白人拿起武器，抵抗西南非州人民组织在纳米比亚取得的胜利和实施联合国安理会４３５号决议。
    努乔马说，纳米比亚人民厌恶战争，因为在过去的２３年里，他们经历了战争的苦难。他说，西南非洲人民组织也曾拿起武器反抗压迫，但根据国际和平条约，任何纳米比亚人不再需要拿起武器。
