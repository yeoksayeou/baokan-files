Title: Zhao Ziyang and Others Award Enterprise Leaders
Author: Jiang Shaogao
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 3rd.

Today, Party and state leaders Zhao Ziyang, Yao Yilin, Zhang Jinfu, and Zhou Jiaxuan presented the highest honor awards to outstanding figures in the business community at the Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai. Twenty winners of the Second National Outstanding Entrepreneurs and ten enterprises recognized as having excellent management in 1988 received the Golden Cup Award and the Golden Horse Award, respectively.

The entrepreneurs cherish this honor greatly. Wang Dazhong and Deng Shaosheng received the notification while on an overseas trip and rushed back to attend. Liu Guyin, the director of Chongqing Zhongnan Rubber Factory, a woman in her fifties, was deeply moved as she received the Golden Cup Award from Comrade Zhao Ziyang. She told the reporter, "General Secretary Zhao praised me, what luck! I will definitely work harder!"

Before the award ceremony, Zhao Ziyang, Yao Yilin, Zhang Jinfu, and Zhou Jiaxuan met with the award recipients.

### 赵紫阳等向企业家颁奖  廿位优秀企业家获金球杯奖  十个优秀管理企业获金马奖
江绍高
1989-03-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　赵紫阳等向企业家颁奖
    廿位优秀企业家获金球杯奖     
    十个优秀管理企业获金马奖
    本报北京三月三日讯  记者江绍高报道：今天，党和国家领导人赵紫阳、姚依林、张劲夫、邹家华等在中南海怀仁堂向企业界精英颁发最高荣誉奖。二十位第二届全国优秀企业家和十个一九八八年度全国企业管理优秀企业分别领到金球杯奖和金马奖。
    企业家们很珍惜这个荣誉，王大中、邓绍深是在出国途中得到通知后赶回来的。重庆中南橡胶厂厂长刘谷音，一位五十多岁的女同志，她从赵紫阳同志手中接过金球杯奖，心情激动，她告诉记者：总书记表扬我，真幸运！我一定加倍努力！
    授奖前，赵紫阳、姚依林、张劲夫、邹家华等会见了获奖者。
