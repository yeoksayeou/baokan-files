Title: Taiwan Establishes the "China Mainland Research Association"
Author:
Date: 1989-03-16
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 15th.

Taiwan has recently established the "China Mainland Research Association."

This research association is composed of 400-plus Taiwanese government officials, scholars, and experts, including "Director-General of the Executive Yuan's Research, Development, and Evaluation Commission" and "Secretary-General of the Mainland Affairs Council" Ma Ying-jeou, "Director-General of the Government Information Office" Xiao Yuming, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Council Zheng Xiongxu, and President of National Chengchi University Chen Zhishi.

### 台湾成立“中国大陆研究学会”

1989-03-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　台湾成立“中国大陆研究学会”
    据新华社北京３月１５日电  台北消息：台湾“中国大陆研究学会”近日成立。
    这个研究学会由台湾党政人士和学者、专家４００多人组成，其中包括台湾“行政院研究发展考核委员会主任委员”兼“大陆工作会报执行秘书”马英九、“新闻局长”邵玉铭、国民党中央海外工作会主任郑心雄、台湾政治大学校长陈治世等。
