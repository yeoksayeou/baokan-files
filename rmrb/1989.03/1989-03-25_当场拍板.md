Title: Decision Made on the Spot
Author: Zhū Wéiqún
Date: 1989-03-25
Edition: 3

At the Jiangsu delegation, the mayor of Yancheng, Qin Zhaozhen, made a request to the provincial party secretary, Sun Nian, to appoint a science and technology assistant for Yancheng. Sun Nian relayed this request to the president of Nanjing University, Qu Qinyue, who immediately agreed to find a suitable candidate. Since the beginning of last year, Jiangsu has selected personnel from universities to serve as deputy county magistrates for science and technology in 32 counties. Sun Nian said: "This method can help county magistrates organize scientific research, strengthen the relationship between universities and research institutions with grassroots levels, and cultivate talent. Now, the municipal government is also asking for personnel!"

### 当场拍板
朱维群
1989-03-25
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：两会花絮

　　当场拍板
    在江苏代表团，盐城市长秦兆桢向省委副书记孙颔提出一个要求：给盐城配一个市长科技助理。孙颔把这个要求转达给南京大学校长曲钦岳，曲校长当场拍板答应给物色人选。江苏去年初开始，从高校选人，给３２个县配备了科技副县长。孙颔说：“这个办法一可以协助县长组织科研工作，二可以密切高校科研机关与基层的关系，三可以培养人才。现在，市政府也伸手要人啦！”
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　    （朱维群）
