Title: European Conventional Arms Negotiations Commence; Warsaw Pact and NATO Announce Proposals
Author: Xiao Li
Date: 1989-03-08
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, March 6th. (Reporter Xiao Li) The opening ceremony of the European Conventional Arms Negotiations involving 23 countries from the Warsaw Pact and NATO, and the Conference on Security and Co-operation in Europe (CSCE) involving 35 countries, was held this morning at the Hofburg Palace in Vienna. Foreign ministers from 35 countries in Europe, the United States, and Canada attended the opening ceremony. Local East-West figures referred to this gathering as a "new milestone on the road to European peace," with "historical significance."

The host country's Austrian Foreign Minister Alois Mock, in his opening speech, pointed out that the two arms reduction talks about to begin will help curb the global arms race.

Representatives from the Warsaw Pact and NATO announced their respective conventional arms reduction plans during their speeches on the first day of the opening ceremony. Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze presented proposals for reducing conventional arms in Europe on behalf of the Warsaw Pact. British Foreign Secretary Geoffrey Howe, representing NATO, announced NATO's four-point proposals in his speech. (See page three for details)

### 欧洲常规裁军谈判开幕  华约北约宣布各自建议
肖力
1989-03-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　欧洲常规裁军谈判开幕
    华约北约宣布各自建议
    新华社维也纳３月６日电  （记者肖力）由华约和北约两大军事集团２３国参加的欧洲常规军备谈判和由欧安会３５国参加的欧洲建立信任和安全措施谈判的开幕式今天上午在维也纳霍夫堡宫隆重举行。来自欧洲和美国、加拿大的３５国外长出席了开幕式。此间东西方人士把这次聚会称之为“通往欧洲和平道路上的新的里程碑”，具有“历史性的意义”。
    东道国奥地利的外长莫克在开幕式讲话中指出，即将开始的两个裁军谈判将有助于阻止全球性的军备竞赛。
    华约和北约代表在开幕式第一天的发言中分别宣布了各自的常规裁军方案。苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽代表华约提出了裁减欧洲常规军备的建议。英国外交大臣杰弗里·豪代表北约在发言中宣布了北约的四点建议。（详见三版）
