Title: A High-Efficiency Disinfectant Developed
Author:
Date: 1989-03-27
Edition: 2

A new high-efficiency disinfectant, known as Super Iodine, is being promoted and applied across China. This disinfectant, developed by researchers at the Beijing Nutrition Source Research Institute, quickly kills pneumonia viruses and bacteria, making it a powerful weapon against viruses and bacteria.

### 高效消毒剂制成

1989-03-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　高效消毒剂制成
    新华社北京３月２６日电  一种取代碘酒、酒精的新的高效消毒剂——强力碘，正在我国推广应用。
    由北京市营养源研究所科研人员研制成功的这种消毒剂，对肺炎病毒和细菌有快速杀灭作用，成为病毒和病菌的克星。
