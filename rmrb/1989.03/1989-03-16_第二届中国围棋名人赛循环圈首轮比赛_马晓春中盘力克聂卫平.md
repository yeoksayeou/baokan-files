Title: The First Round of the Second Chinese Weiqi Masters Tournament
Author: Wang Qianhai
Date: 1989-03-16
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 15th.

The first round of the second Chinese Weiqi Masters Tournament was held this morning at the Beijing China-Japan Weiqi Association. Ma Xiaochun, a 9th dan player, defeated Nie Weiping, also a 9th dan player, in the middle of the game. Yang Jinhua, a 7th dan player, defeated Yang Hui, also a 7th dan player, in the middle of the game.

Yu Bin, Qian Yiping, Wang Qun, and Cheng Xiaoliu, four players participating in the round-robin tournament, did not participate in today's match due to unforeseen circumstances.

### 第二届中国围棋名人赛循环圈首轮比赛  马晓春中盘力克聂卫平
王前海
1989-03-16
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　第二届中国围棋名人赛循环圈首轮比赛
    马晓春中盘力克聂卫平
    本报北京３月１５日讯  正值富士通杯世界职业围棋锦标赛决赛即将开枰之际，第二届中国围棋名人赛循环圈首轮比赛今天上午在北京中日围棋会馆拉开战幕。马晓春九段中盘胜聂卫平九段，杨晋华七段中盘击败杨晖七段。
    俞斌、钱宇平、王群、程晓流四位参加循环圈比赛的棋手因故没有参加今天的比赛。　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　（王前海）
