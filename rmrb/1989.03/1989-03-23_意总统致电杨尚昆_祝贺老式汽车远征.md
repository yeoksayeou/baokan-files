Title: Italian President Calls Yang Shangkun to Congratulate the Classic Car Expedition
Author:
Date: 1989-03-23
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, March 21st.

Italian President Francesco Cossiga sent a telegram to Chairman Yang Shangkun of the People's Republic of China on the evening of March 21st, expressing warm congratulations on the imminent commencement of the "Beijing to Paris" classic car expedition. The telegram stated: As the Italian classic car expedition team is about to depart from your country, I wish to express, on behalf of the Italian people and myself, my deepest respect and sincere friendship to you.

### 意总统致电杨尚昆  祝贺老式汽车远征

1989-03-23
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　意总统致电杨尚昆        
    祝贺老式汽车远征
    新华社罗马３月２１日电  意大利总统科西加２１日晚打电报给中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆，热烈祝贺“北京—巴黎之路”老式汽车远征活动即将开始。电报说：值此意大利老式汽车远征队正准备从贵国出发之际，我愿以意大利人民和我个人的名义，向阁下表达我深深的敬意和诚挚的友谊。
