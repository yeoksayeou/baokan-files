Title: South Korean Students Protest U.S.-South Korea Military Drills
Author:
Date: 1989-03-19
Edition: 4

On March 17, over 200 students in Seoul, South Korea, staged a demonstration to protest against the joint military exercises conducted by the United States and South Korea.
