Title: The Battle Begins
Author: Zhū Gēnhuá
Date: 1989-03-06
Edition: 7

The world is on the brink of a new technological revolution. The rapid development of science and technology is profoundly changing the way we live, work, and think. This revolution is not only about the advancement of technology but also about the transformation of society. It is a battle for the future, a battle for the soul of humanity.

In this battle, China stands at a critical juncture. We have made significant strides in science and technology, but we are still lagging behind the advanced countries. We must accelerate our pace, catch up, and even surpass the leading nations in key areas. This is not just a matter of national pride but a necessity for our survival and development in the 21st century.

The battle for technological advancement is not just about economic competition. It is about national security, social progress, and the well-being of our people. We must invest heavily in research and development, foster innovation, and cultivate a generation of scientists and engineers who can lead us into the future.

We must also be aware of the challenges and risks that come with this battle. The rapid advancement of technology brings with it ethical, environmental, and social issues that we must address. We must ensure that our technological development is sustainable, equitable, and beneficial to all.

In this battle, we must work together, both within China and with the international community. We must learn from each other, collaborate, and share our knowledge and resources. Only through cooperation and mutual support can we overcome the challenges and achieve our common goals.

The battle has begun. Let us stand united, determined, and resolute. Let us strive for excellence, innovation, and progress. Let us win this battle for the future of our nation and the world.
