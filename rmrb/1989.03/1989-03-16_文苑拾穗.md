	Literary Harvest
	*Expanding the Space for Literary Thought*

	This book includes essays from twenty-seven renowned scholars, both Chinese and foreign, such as Liu Zaifu, Li Zehou, Ye Weilian, and Leo Ou-fan. It offers a comprehensive examination and in-depth exploration of contemporary literature, modern literature, foreign literature, comparative literature, and literary theory. This work provides readers with the latest academic achievements in expanding the space for literary thought. Published by Workers Publishing House.

	*Biographies of Contemporary Chinese Writers*

	He Huoren's *Biographies of Contemporary Chinese Writers* is published by the Cultural and Art Publishing House. This book includes biographies of sixty-seven famous writers from across China, including minority ethnic groups and Taiwan and Hong Kong. It features biographies of the most active middle-aged and young writers such as Wang Meng, Liu Xinhua, Zhang Jie, Chen Rong, Li Yu, Chen Jianfeng, Wang Anyi, Tie Ning, as well as Taiwanese and Hong Kong writers like Bai Xianyong, Chen Yingzhen, and Eileen Chang. The biographies are written by dozens of renowned professors, experts, and scholars from across the country. The book uses a wealth of materials to sketch the unique life and creative paths of each writer, as well as their spiritual development trajectories. Readers can gain insights into the secrets of how these writers became successful.

    　　　　《中国当代名作家小传》
