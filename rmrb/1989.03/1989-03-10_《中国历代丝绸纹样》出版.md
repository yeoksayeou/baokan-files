Title: Publication of "Chinese Silk Patterns Through the Ages"
Author: Wang Wenhui
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 4

The first comprehensive volume of Chinese silk patterns throughout history, "Chinese Silk Patterns Through the Ages," compiled by Miao Liangyun of the Suzhou Silk Institute, has recently been published by the Textile Industry Press. This large-format volume collects and compiles 401 extremely rare and exquisite images of ancient Chinese silk patterns, reflecting the evolution, types, styles, and characteristics of Chinese silk from the Shang and Zhou dynasties to the Ming and Qing dynasties.

### 《中国历代丝绸纹样》出版
王文汇
1989-03-10
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　《中国历代丝绸纹样》出版
    由苏州丝绸工学院缪良云编著的我国第一部大型图册《中国历代丝绸纹样》日前由纺织工业出版社出版。这本大型图册共搜集整理了４０１幅极其珍贵、精美的中国古代丝绸纹样的图片，反映了中国丝绸在上自殷周、下止明清中国各个朝代中的演变、类型、风格和特点。
   　　　　　　　　　　（王文汇）
