Title: Comrade Wang Changhu Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-03-19
Edition: 2

More than 900 people from various circles in Guangdong Province paid their last respects to the late Wang Changhu, former member of the Standing Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Director and Secretary of the Party Group of the Guangdong Provincial Agricultural Reclamation Bureau, at the Guangzhou Funeral Parlor on the morning of March 14. Wreaths were sent by Zhao Ziyang, Wang Zhen, and other comrades.

Wang Changhu passed away on March 7 at the age of 73.

Wang Changhu joined the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in 1930 and the Communist Party of China in 1933. He had served as a divisional political commissar, Director of the Hainan Branch of the South China Reclamation Bureau, Deputy Commander of the Guangzhou Military Region Production and Construction Corps, and Director of the Guangdong Provincial Agricultural Reclamation Bureau, among other positions.

### 王昌虎同志病逝

1989-03-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　王昌虎同志病逝
    新华社广州电  广东省各界人士９００多人３月１４日上午在广州殡仪馆向原中共广东省顾委常委、省农垦总局局长、党组书记王昌虎同志的遗体告别。赵紫阳、王震等同志送了花圈。
    王昌虎是３月７日病逝的，终年７３岁。
    王昌虎１９３０年参加中国工农红军，１９３３年加入中国共产党，曾任师政委、华南垦殖总局海南分局局长、广州军区生产建设兵团副司令员、广东省农垦总局局长等职。
