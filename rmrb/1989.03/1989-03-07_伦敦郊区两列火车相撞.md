Title: Two Trains Collide in London Suburbs
Author: Chen Te'an
Date: 1989-03-07
Edition: 3

Two trains collided in the southern suburbs of London yesterday afternoon, resulting in at least 9 deaths and 94 injuries, with 10 people in critical condition.

Yesterday afternoon, the 1217 train from Southend to Victoria collided with the 1250 train from Horsham to Victoria. Witnesses reported that the 1250 train, which had just left Purley station, was switching from the slow track to the fast track when it collided with the 1217 train, which was traveling on the fast track.

Sir Robert Reid, chairman of British Rail, stated that the cause of the accident is currently under investigation.

### 伦敦郊区两列火车相撞
陈特安
1989-03-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　伦敦郊区两列火车相撞
    本报伦敦３月５日电  记者陈特安报道：昨天下午两列火车在伦敦南郊的普利车站附近相撞，造成至少９人死亡、９４人受伤，其中１０人伤势严重。
    昨天下午，从小汗普顿开往维多利亚的１２１７号列车撞上了从霍舍姆到维多利亚的１２５０号列车。目击者说，已离开普利车站的１２５０号列车正在由慢道转入快道之际，与行驶在快车道上的１２１７号列车相撞。
    英国铁路局主席罗伯特·里德爵士说，现在，正在调查事故原因。
