Title: A Bloom of Spring on the Snow: Wang Jingge Wins a Bronze Medal at the World University Winter Games
Author: Zhang Yuejin Liu Fuping
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, March 8th.

Chinese athlete Wang Jingge today won a bronze medal in the women's 10-kilometer biathlon event at the World University Winter Games.

The biathlon competition was held in the southern Bulgarian mountain city of Bansko. In the afternoon's women's 10-kilometer race, China's Wang Jingge fought hard and achieved a time of 39 minutes and 43.9 seconds, securing third place. This is the best result ever achieved by a Chinese athlete in a major international winter sports event.

Bulgarian athlete Manolova and Soviet athlete Batsovich won the gold and silver medals, respectively.

### 雪上项目一枝报春花　世界大学生冬运会王敬歌获一枚铜牌
张跃进　刘福平
1989-03-10
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　雪上项目一枝报春花
  　世界大学生冬运会王敬歌获一枚铜牌
    新华社索非亚３月８日电  （通讯员张跃进、记者刘福平）中国运动员王敬歌今天在世界大学生冬季运动会冬季两项女子１０公里比赛中获得铜牌。
    冬季两项比赛是在保加利亚南部山城邦斯科进行的。在下午举行的女子１０公里比赛中，我国运动员王敬歌奋力拚搏，以３９分４３秒９的成绩获得第三名。这是我国运动员在世界大型运动会雪上项目的比赛中取得的最好成绩。
    保加利亚运动员玛诺罗娃和苏联运动员巴采维奇分获这项比赛的金牌和银牌。
