Title: Chinese Badminton Team Moves from London to Paris
Author:
Date: 1989-03-25
Edition: 3

Yesterday, the third French Open Badminton Championships officially began. The Chinese men's and women's badminton teams, after completing the All-England Badminton Championships, moved to France to participate in the event.

### 中国羽毛球队由伦敦移师巴黎

1989-03-25
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　中国羽毛球队由伦敦移师巴黎
    本报巴黎３月２３日电  昨天，第三届全法羽毛球公开赛正式举行。中国男女羽毛球队在结束全英羽毛球公开赛后移师法国参赛。
