Title: Seeking Ways to End the Mozambican Civil War: UK Prime Minister Meets Mozambican President
Author:
Date: 1989-03-31
Edition: 4

The British Prime Minister, Margaret Thatcher, held talks with Zimbabwean President Robert Mugabe and Mozambican President Joaquim Chissano on March 29, seeking ways to end the 14-year civil war in Mozambique. The talks were held in the border town of Nyanga, near Mozambique.

Mozambique's president arrived in Zimbabwe on the same day.

In a brief speech, Thatcher revealed the "particularly brutal actions" of the Mozambican rebel forces. She said that increasing aid to the Mozambican government was one of the main topics of their discussion.

Mozambican President Chissano said that efforts to achieve peace in Mozambique had long surpassed national boundaries and should be seen as a struggle for peace and prosperity in the entire southern African region.

### 探求结束莫桑比克内战途径  英首相会晤津莫总统

1989-03-31
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　探求结束莫桑比克内战途径        
    英首相会晤津莫总统
    新华社哈拉雷３月２９日电  正在津巴布韦访问的英国首相玛格丽特·撒切尔夫人２９日同津巴布韦总统穆加贝和莫桑比克总统希萨诺举行会谈，探求结束莫桑比克１４年内战的途径。
    这次三方会谈是在津巴布韦靠近莫桑比克的边境城镇尼扬加举行的。
    莫桑比克总统是２９日抵达津巴布韦的。
    撒切尔夫人在简短的发言中揭露了莫桑比克反政府武装的“特别残暴的行径”。她说，向莫政府增加援助是他们会谈的主要议题之一。
    莫桑比克总统希萨诺说，为实现莫桑比克的和平而作出的努力早已超出了国界，这应被视为是为整个南部非洲的和平和繁荣而斗争。
