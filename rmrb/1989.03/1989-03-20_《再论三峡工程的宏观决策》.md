Title: A Further Discussion on the Macroeconomic Decision-Making of the Three Gorges Project
Author: Tian Fang, Lin Fatang
Date: 1989-03-20
Edition: 6

This book is a continuation of the 1987 publication "A Discussion on the Macroeconomic Decision-Making of the Three Gorges Project." Currently, there are still significant disagreements regarding the macroeconomic decision-making policies and various thematic arguments concerning the Three Gorges Project. Adhering to the principles of democratic and scientific decision-making within the Party, this book compiles the diverse opinions of numerous experts, scholars, and insightful individuals. This is intended to provide a resource for extensive research and consideration by a broad readership and decision-makers.
