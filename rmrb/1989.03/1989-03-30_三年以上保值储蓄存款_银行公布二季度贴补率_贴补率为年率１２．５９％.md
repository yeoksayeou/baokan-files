Title: Three-Year and Above Savings Deposits with Preservation of Value
Author:
Date: 1989-03-30
Edition: 1

The People's Bank of China today announced the supplementary rate for the second quarter of 1989 for long-term savings deposits in renminbi with preservation of value.

According to the price index for retail goods and services compiled by the State Statistical Bureau for the fourth quarter of 1988 and the first quarter of 1989, the supplementary rate for three-year and above term savings deposits for the second quarter of 1989 is 12.59% per annum.

### 三年以上保值储蓄存款  银行公布二季度贴补率  贴补率为年率１２．５９％

1989-03-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　三年以上保值储蓄存款        
    银行公布二季度贴补率        
    贴补率为年率１２．５９％
    据新华社北京３月２９日电  中国人民银行今天公布了１９８９年第二季度人民币长期保值储蓄存款贴补率。
    根据国家统计局统计的１９８８年第四季度和１９８９年第一季度的社会商品零售和服务项目价格总指数计算，１９８９年第二季度３年以上定期储蓄存款的保值贴补率为年率１２．５９％。
