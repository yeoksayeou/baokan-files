Title: Beijing Scholars Gather to Discuss the Issue of New Authoritarianism
Author:
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 6

On February 24, the Beijing Society and Science and Technology Development Research Institute and the "Theory Information Daily" jointly held a seminar on "New Authoritarianism in Current Political Trends." Several dozen young and middle-aged scholars attended and exchanged various views on the issue of "New Authoritarianism."

### 北京学者聚会研讨新权威主义问题

1989-03-03
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：理论简讯

    北京学者聚会研讨新权威主义问题
    本报讯　2月24日，北京社会与科技发展研究所和《理论信息报》联合召开了“当前政治思潮中的‘新权威主义，研讨会”，与会的数十名中青年学者就“新权威主义”问题，广泛地交换了各种不同的看法。
