Title: Fujian's Forest Coverage Ranks First on the Mainland
Author: Dōng Huī
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 2

The Fujian Forestry Photography Exhibition, which opened in Beijing, revealed that the province's forest coverage rate is 43.18%, making it the highest on the mainland (second only to Taiwan). This exhibition, featuring nearly a hundred works, showcases the province's recent efforts in constructing a coastal protective forest system.

### 福建森林覆盖率位居大陆第一名
东辉
1989-03-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　福建森林覆盖率位居大陆第一名
    本报北京３月１３日讯  在北京开幕的福建林业摄影展览会披露，该省森林覆盖率为４３．１８％为大陆之冠（仅次于台湾）。这个展览以近百幅作品，展现了这个省近年来建设沿海防护林体系的场面。
                            （东辉）
