Title: 270,000 Farmers Vacation at Bai Teng Lake
Author: Huang Zhenggen, Zhao Huaqing
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 9th

The Bai Teng Lake Farmers' Holiday Village in Zhuhai, Guangdong Province, hailed as a "domestic first," has captivated both domestic and international tourists with its enchanting allure. From the beginning of last year to the beginning of this year, a total of 800,000 domestic and international tourists have visited the village for vacations. Among them, 270,000 affluent farmers from the Pearl River Delta region of Guangdong have come to enjoy the scenic beauty, generating over 100 million yuan in revenue.

Domestic farmers receive discounted rates for food and accommodation when they come to vacation here.

### ２７万农民到白藤湖度假
黄正根　赵华青
1989-03-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　２７万农民到白藤湖度假
    据新华社广州３月９日电  （记者黄正根、赵华青）被誉为“国内首创”的广东省珠海白藤湖农民度假村，以其神奇的魅力吸引着国内外旅游者。去年年初到今年年初，共有８０万国内外旅游者来此度假，其中来自广东珠江三角洲地区富裕了的农民游客就达２７万人，经营收入上亿元。
    国内农民来这里旅游，食住可以得到低价优惠。
