Title: A Comprehensive View of Spiritual Civilization
Author: Sha Ying
Date: 1989-03-20
Edition: 6

The book "A Comprehensive View of Spiritual Civilization" by Sha Ying, published by Qingdao Publishing House, is divided into three parts: practical issues in spiritual civilization construction, theoretical issues in spiritual civilization construction, and how party members, cadres, and youth can strengthen their own civilization construction. It explores many important issues in spiritual civilization construction. Preface by Yuan Mu and Li Zhuang.
