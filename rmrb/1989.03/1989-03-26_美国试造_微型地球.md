Title: The United States Attempts to Create a "Micro-Earth"
Author: Zhaodongbo
Date: 1989-03-26
Edition: 8

In the desert region of Arizona, USA, there is an independent, self-sustaining ecosystem known as the "super-miniature Earth." This is a new attempt to establish bases on the moon and Mars. The newly constructed "super-miniature Earth" is called the "Biosphere 2 Experimental House." The length, width, and height of this experimental house are all less than 10 meters, with a volume of approximately 480 cubic meters. Inside, plants are abundant, and water and air each form a closed-loop system. The energy source is solar power. The experimental house conducted its first human habitation test in September, with a man living inside for 3 days. Subsequently, researchers conducted a 17-day follow-up investigation of the ecosystem. They continuously measured gases such as carbon monoxide and ammonia, and also conducted precise tests on other trace gases. The results showed 47 types of organic gases, primarily oxygen-containing cyclopentane, a phenomenon not observed in previous unmanned experiments.

The "Biosphere 2" being constructed is more than 330 times larger than the aforementioned "Biosphere 2 Experimental House." The research institution plans to have 8 experimental personnel enter "Biosphere 2" in the early 1990s, where they will grow vegetables and fish. Compared to Earth, the ecosystem of the miniature Earth has a smaller spatial range, faster circulation of water and gases, and a risk of drinking water contaminated with harmful substances. Therefore, farming inside "Biosphere 2" cannot use chemical pesticides. Currently, preparations are being made to release approximately 300 species of insects inside the miniature Earth to explore methods of using natural enemies to control pests.

### 美国试造“微型地球”
赵东波
1989-03-26
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

　　美国试造“微型地球”
    在美国阿里桑那州的沙漠地带，有一座独立的自成生态系统的人造“超微型地球”，这是为在月球及火星建立基地而进行的新尝试。新建的“超微型地球”称为“生物圈２号实验房”。该实验房的长宽高均不足１０米，体积约为４８０立方米，房中植物丛生，水和空气各自形成封闭式的循环系统，能源则接受太阳光等。该实验房９月份进行了初次住人实验，一位男子在实验房中生活了３天，后由研究人员对该生态系统进行了１７天的跟踪调查。他们除了连续测定一氧化碳和氨气等气体外，还对其它微量气体进行了精密检测，结果检测出以氧杂环戊烷为主的４７种有机质气体，这是以前进行的无人实验所没有的现象。
    正在建造的微型地球“生物圈２号”比上述“生物圈２号实验房”大３３０多倍。该研究机构计划在９０年代初让８名实验人员进入“生物圈２号”，并在里面种植蔬菜和钓鱼等。与地球相比，微型地球的生态系统空间范围狭小，水和气体的循环速度快，溶入有害物质的水有被饮用的危险，所以，在“生物圈２号”内种植农作物，不能使用农药等化学药品。目前正准备在微型地球内放养约３００种昆虫，以探索用害虫的天敌来治害虫的办法。
　　　　　　　　　　　（２月２２日《世界科技译报》　赵东波文）
