Title: Dancing in the Morning Frost Adds Vigor
Author: Xǔ Shìjié
Date: 1989-03-21
Edition: 3

    Dancing in the morning frost adds vigor.
    I
    Sweep away the gloom to reveal a blue sky, though there are clouds, they are clear.
    With a calm gaze, observe the ebb and flow of the tides, with an open mind, examine the swirling waters.
    Polluted waters once flowed without roots, and rumors with wings fluttered.
    Spring rain gently moistens all things, and a gentle breeze sweeps away the dust and smoke.

    II
    It is difficult to seek all benefits at once, but if benefits outweigh harms, why complain?
    Success and failure should be evaluated comprehensively, not individually judged by personal bitterness or sweetness.
    Governance should be strict and lenient, and public supervision should ensure clean governance.
    Reform and innovation should bring stability, and dancing in the morning frost adds vigor.

### 起舞晓霜劲倍添
许士杰
1989-03-21
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　起舞晓霜劲倍添
    一
    荡开闷气亮蓝天，虽有浮云却了然。
    冷眼静观潮涨退，平心细察水回旋。
    无根污水曾流淌，有翅谣言也翩跹。
    春雨霏霏润万物，清风习习扫尘烟。
    　　　　二
    万利难求皆得兼，利多害少怎能嫌。
    应从总体评成败，未可个人论苦甜。
    综合宽严安自治，公开监督政当廉。
    布新除旧宜安定，起舞晓霜劲倍添。
    　一九八九年三月二十日
    　　　　　　　　　　　　于北京
