Title: Observing China's Path to Universal Education from the Countryside
Author:
Date: 1989-03-22
Edition: 1

The main challenge in China's push for universal education lies in the countryside, where the difficulties are also concentrated. How can we change the relatively backward state of rural education? Today, the fifth page of this newspaper publishes an article by He Dongchang, Deputy Director of the State Education Commission, proposing that educational reform should start from the countryside, taking a path that combines universal education with vocational and technical education. In cities, an "interactive" mechanism between education and economic and social development can also be established.

### 今日五版发表何东昌文章  从农村看我国普及教育路子

1989-03-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　今日五版发表何东昌文章        
    从农村看我国普及教育路子
    本报讯  我国普及教育的大头在农村，难点也在农村。如何改变农村教育相对落后的状况？本报今日五版发表国家教委副主任何东昌的文章，提出教育改革也要从农村开始，走普及教育与职业技术教育相结合的路子。在城市，也可建立教育与经济社会发展的“互促”机制。
