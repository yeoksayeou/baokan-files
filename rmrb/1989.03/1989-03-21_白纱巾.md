Title: White Silk Scarf
Author: Liu Binfu
Date: 1989-03-21
Edition: 8

In Chengdu, I met a girl from Lhasa.
When the buds first bloom,
There is a layer of snow on the branches.
The peak of Mount Qomolangma holds a glow of dawn.
A red sail rises from the river's flowers.
In the dawn of the central basin,
The first tide of the Eastern Sea is just heard,
The messenger of the sun city of the western plateau
Has already announced the arrival of the spring morning.

The white silk scarf
Is a khata offered to the sun.

### 白纱巾
刘斌夫
1989-03-21
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　白纱巾
    刘斌夫
    在成都，遇见一位从拉萨来的姑娘
    当蓓蕾初绽的时候
    枝头上堆了一场雪
    珠峰上托起一轮霞光
    江花里升起一桅红帆
    在大盆地中央的曦微里
    刚听到东海的初潮
    西部高原太阳城的使者
    已告知春晨的来临
    白色纱巾
    是呈献给太阳的哈达
