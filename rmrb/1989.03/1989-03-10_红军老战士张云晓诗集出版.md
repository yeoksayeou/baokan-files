Title: Poetry Collection by Red Army Veteran Zhang Yunxiao Published
Author:
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 4

The poetry collection "A Red Army Soldier's Poems" by Zhang Yunxiao, a retired military cadre, has been well-received by young soldiers. Zhang Yunxiao, who enlisted in the Red Army in 1933, has long been engaged in propaganda and cultural work. Since his retirement in 1983, he has created numerous poems with themes of the Long March and contemporary life. Soldiers have said that reading Zhang Yunxiao's poems allows them to be imbued with the spirit of revolutionary tradition.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 红军老战士张云晓诗集出版

1989-03-10
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　红军老战士张云晓诗集出版
    军队离休干部张云晓的诗集《一个红军战士的诗》，受到年轻战士的喜爱。张云晓是１９３３年入伍的红军老战士，长期从事宣传、文化工作。他１９８３年离休后，以长征与现实生活为题材，创作了大量诗歌。战士们说，读了张云晓的诗，能够从中受到革命传统的熏陶。
    　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
