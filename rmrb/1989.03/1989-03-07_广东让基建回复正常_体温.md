Title: Guangdong Allows Construction to Return to Normal "Temperature"
Author: Liang Zhaoming
Date: 1989-03-07
Edition: 2

Guangdong's fixed asset investment scale this year will be reduced by 57.8% compared to last year. Currently, various regions in the province are actively implementing this reduction task, aiming to quickly restore the previously overheated construction scale to a normal "temperature."

It is understood that from last August to the present, the province has successively halted or slowed down 1,341 projects, reducing the total investment by 10.05 billion yuan. Among these, 203 projects are for buildings and facilities, with a reduced investment of 2.75 billion yuan.

### 广东让基建回复正常“体温”
梁兆明
1989-03-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　广东让基建回复正常“体温”
    本报广州３月６日电  记者梁兆明报道：广东省今年固定资产投资规模将比去年压减５７．８％。现在，全省各地正按着这个压缩任务加紧行动，使原来过热的基建规模，尽快回复正常的“体温”。
    据了解，去年８月至今，该省已先后３次共停缓建１３４１个项目，压缩总投资１００．５亿元。其中，楼堂馆所项目２０３个，压减投资２７．５亿元。
