Title: Zhao Zhihua Elected Governor of Shandong Province
Author:
Date: 1989-03-08
Edition: 1

	Zhao Zhihua, deputy secretary of the Shandong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, was elected governor of Shandong Province on March 5 at the second session of the seventh Shandong Provincial People's Congress.

### 赵志浩当选山东省省长

1989-03-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　赵志浩当选山东省省长
    本报济南电  中共山东省委副书记赵志浩，３月５日在山东省七届人大二次会议上被选为山东省省长。
