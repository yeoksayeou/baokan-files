Title: Global Drug Cultivation Areas Rapidly Expanding
Author: Cang Lide
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 3

The global areas used for cultivating opium and cocaine crops have rapidly expanded. The international war against drug production "obviously has not won."

This is according to a report submitted by U.S. Secretary of State James Baker to Congress today. The report, which covers foreign drug trafficking for the year, states that in 1988, the global area used for opium cultivation surged from 4,700 to 7,500 acres in the previous year to 6,000 to 8,100 acres. The area for coca cultivation increased from 520,000 acres in 1987 to 560,000 acres last year.

### 世界毒品种植面积急剧扩大
仓立德
1989-03-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　世界毒品种植面积急剧扩大
    本报华盛顿３月１日电  记者仓立德报道：１９８８年世界种植鸦片和可卡因作物的面积急剧增长，反对毒品生产的国际战“显然没有取胜”。美国国务卿贝克今天向国会提交的一份报告作出上述表示。
    这份有关外国运输毒品的年度报告称，去年世界鸦片作物种植面积由前年的４７００—７５００余英亩跃升至６０００—８１００余英亩；古柯面积由１９８７年的５２万余英亩扩大到去年的５６万余英亩。
