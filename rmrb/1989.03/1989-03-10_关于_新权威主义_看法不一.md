Title: Divergent Views on "New Authoritarianism"
Author:
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 1

The People's Daily reported that on March 6, the fifth page of the domestic section (politics and law) of the newspaper's "Youth Hot Topics" column, which aims to promote discussion and debate, published a short article titled "An Analysis of 'New Authoritarianism.'" The article presented the author's views on "new authoritarianism." Today, the fifth page of the same column published another article titled "The Necessity of 'New Authority,'" which presents a different perspective.

### 关于“新权威主义”看法不一

1989-03-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　关于“新权威主义”看法不一
    本报讯  本报３月６日五版国内专页（政治·法律）上旨在开展讨论和争鸣的有奖征文“青年热门话题”专栏，发表了题为《“新权威主义”析》的短文，陈述了作者对“新权威主义”的一些意见。今天五版该专栏又刊登了《“新权威”不可少》一文，提出不同的看法。
