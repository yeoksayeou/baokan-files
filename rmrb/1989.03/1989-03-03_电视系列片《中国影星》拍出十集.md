Title: Television Series "Chinese Film Stars" Produces Ten Episodes

Author:

Date: 1989-03-03

Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency

A large-scale television series titled "Chinese Film Stars," which reflects the achievements of famous film artists in our country, has completed its first ten episodes, produced by Heilongjiang Television Station.
