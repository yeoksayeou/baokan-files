Title: The Old Elm Tree
Author: Yang Jianmin
Date: 1989-03-30
Edition: 8

    The Old Elm Tree
    Yang Jianmin

    Standing outside the fence
    Endlessly ruminating
    Derogatory terms
    Elm knots
    The years have left their marks
    Wrinkles all over, holding bitter memories
    Once with skin and leaves
    Nourished a China in disaster
    As history repeatedly strips it bare
    It stands still with tears of pain
    Allowing its bloodied flesh to sustain a nation
    A stubborn life
    Year after year, it gives to people
    Handfuls of elm seeds
    Life never exhausts its bitterness
    Finally, it struggles
    To stand upright
    Passing on its spirit to countless descendants

### 老榆树
杨建民
1989-03-30
第8版(副刊)
专栏：大地

　　老榆树
    杨建民
    站在栅栏外面
    不停地反刍
    贬义词
    榆木疙瘩
    隆起岁月的印戳
    满身皱纹凝集着冷酷的记忆
    曾用皮肤和叶子
    喂养灾难的中国
    当不断重演的历史剥光衣服
    它眼含苦泪一动不动
    让带血的皮肉延续一个民族
    倔强的生命
    年年岁岁向人们
    施舍大把大把榆钱
    生活总花不完它的辛酸
    终于挣扎着
    站立起来
    把精神传给万代子孙
