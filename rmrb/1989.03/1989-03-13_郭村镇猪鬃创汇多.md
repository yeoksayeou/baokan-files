Title: Guo Village Town's Pig Bristle Exports Surge
Author: Lin Zhen Ke Ming Yu Quan
Date: 1989-03-13
Edition: 2

	Guo Village Town's pig bristle enterprises, run by over 1,400 local farmers, have formed a significant group of export-oriented businesses. Last year, the town exported over 40,000 boxes of pig bristle, accounting for one-sixth of the nation's total pig bristle exports. The foreign trade procurement value reached 44 million yuan, earning 11.87 million US dollars in foreign exchange.

		(Translated by Lin Zhen, Ke Ming, Yu Quan)

### 郭村镇猪鬃创汇多
琳珍　克明　玉泉
1989-03-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　郭村镇猪鬃创汇多
    本报讯  江苏江都县郭村镇１４００多家农民兴办的猪鬃企业如今形成了一个猪鬃创汇企业群。去年该镇出口猪鬃４万多箱，占全国猪鬃出口总量的１／６，外贸收购额４４００万元，创汇１１８７万美元。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（琳珍　克明　玉泉）
