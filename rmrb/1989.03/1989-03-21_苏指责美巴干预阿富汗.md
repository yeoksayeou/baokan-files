Title: Soviet Union Accuses the US and Pakistan of Interfering in Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1989-03-21
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 20th.

The Soviet Union's *Pravda* today published an article expressing "serious concern" over the recent situation in Afghanistan.

The article, written by Glukhov, accuses the United States and Pakistan of "recently escalating their interference in Afghan affairs." The article argues that after the withdrawal of Soviet troops from Afghanistan, there has been no power vacuum. The article states, "The recent battle below Jalalabad is proof of this."

The article reveals that the United States provides $300 million worth of weapons to the Afghan anti-government forces annually.

### 苏指责美巴干预阿富汗

1989-03-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏指责美巴干预阿富汗
    新华社莫斯科３月２０日电  苏联《真理报》今天发表署名文章，对阿富汗最近出现的局势表示“严重不安”。
    这篇由格卢霍夫撰写的文章，指责美国和巴基斯坦“最近加剧了对阿富汗事务的干预”。文章认为，苏军撤出阿富汗以后，阿富汗并没有出现真空。文章说，“前不久贾拉拉巴德城下之战便是证明”。
    文章透露，美国每年向阿富汗反政府军提供３亿美元的武器。
