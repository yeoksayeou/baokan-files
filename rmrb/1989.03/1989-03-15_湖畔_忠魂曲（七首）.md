Title: Odes to the Loyal Souls of the Lakeside (Seven Poems)
Author: Wāng Jìngzhī
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 8

	Odes to the Loyal Souls of the Lakeside (Seven Poems)
		Wāng Jìngzhī

		Yīng Xiū Rén

		With a heart of red loyalty,
		He vowed to face fire and boiling water.
		Crushed bones and shattered body are of no concern,
		Falling from the building to awaken the souls of ten thousand people.

		Róu Shí

		Softness is the spirit, hardness is the stone,
		The soul of the Party is hard, the stone is soft.
		The flowers of Longhua are stained with loyal blood,
		The flowers shine with a red heart that will last forever.

		Pān Mò Huá

		Fasting to fight against the enemy's anger,
		Who can disrespect such righteousness?
		Thousands of bowls of spicy soup are not feared,
		How can one bow down to drink sweet dew!

		Féng Xuě Fēng

		Grasslands and snow mountains temper the loyal bones,
		Concentration camps forge true gold.
		The snow on the peak of Mount Qomolangma,
		Is the purest and most loyal heart!

		Wèi Jīn Zhī

		Four evil spirits trample on human rights,
		Driven into the pen of snakes and ghosts.
		The body is destroyed and the back is bent like a shrimp,
		The strong and brave heart never bends!

		Xiè Dàn Rú

		Where in China is there no bloodstain?
		The earth is filled with loyal souls.
		In the family, there is no fear of hidden thunder,
		A loyal heart protects the Party's heart.

		General Praise

		The heart is filled with ideals for a long time,
		Purifying the soul like crystal.
		Completely selfless and pure,
		The loyal heart fills the sky and earth!


	Note 1: The six comrades are my classmates and good friends, intimate friends, and are all members of the Lakeside Poetry Society. Except for Xiè Dàn Rú, they are all Communist Party members.
	Note 2: “Hidden Thunder” refers to Qū Qiūbái and Yáng Zhī Huá, who changed their names and hid in Xiè Dàn Rú's home for two years during the White Terror period.

### “湖畔”忠魂曲（七首）
汪静之
1989-03-15
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　“湖畔”忠魂曲（七首）
    汪静之
    应修人
    赤诚一片最忠贞，
    赴火蹈汤早许心。
    粉骨碎身何足惜，
    坠楼促醒万民魂。
    　　　　柔石
    刚是精神柔是石，
    党人魂硬石头柔。
    龙华花染忠肝血，
    花耀丹心万古留。
    　　　　潘漠华
    绝食斗争对敌怒，
    懔然正气谁能侮？
    辣汤不怕灌千壶，
    岂肯低头饮甘露！
    　　　　冯雪峰
    草地雪山锻忠骨，
    集中营窟炼真金。
    珠穆朗玛峰头雪，
    至纯至洁是贞心！
    　　　　魏金枝
    四凶魑魅践人权，
    驱入蛇神牛鬼栏。
    躯体摧残虾背曲，
    坚强赤胆未曾弯！
    　　　　谢旦如
    何处神州无血痕？
    阴森遍地是忠魂。
    家中不惧惊雷隐，
    一片忠诚护党心。
    　　　　总赞
    胸中理想久氤氲，
    净化灵魂似水晶。
    彻底无私至纯粹，
    充天塞地是忠心！
    注一：六位同志是我的同窗好友，知己至交，都是湖畔诗社社员。除谢旦如外，又都是共产党员。注二：“惊雷隐”指瞿秋白、杨之华夫妇白色恐怖时期在谢旦如家中隐姓改名住了两年。
