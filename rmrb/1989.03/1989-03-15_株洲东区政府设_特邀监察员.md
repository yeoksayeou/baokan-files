Title: The Dong District Government of Zhuzhou Establishes "Special Invited Inspectors"
Author: Wú Xīnghuá
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 2

The Dong District Government of Zhuzhou City in Hunan Province has appointed six members from democratic parties as "special invited inspectors."

The "special invited inspectors" are recommended and vetted by the respective democratic parties. The Dong District Government has defined the responsibilities of the "special invited inspectors" as follows: to regularly or irregularly report to the district government and the district supervision bureau on the performance of government agencies and their staff in executing official duties, laws, and regulations; to provide timely information on the integrity of government agencies, enterprises, and their staff; to participate in the investigation of major cases and cases involving members of democratic parties; and to participate in discussions on the classification and handling of some important cases.

### 株洲东区政府设“特邀监察员”
吴兴华
1989-03-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　株洲东区政府设“特邀监察员”
    本报长沙３月１４日电  记者吴兴华报道：３月１１日，湖南省株洲市东区人民政府聘请６名民主党派成员，担任区政府的“特邀监察员”。
    “特邀监察员”由各民主党派推荐、审查。东区政府确定的“特邀监察员”的职责是：定期或不定期向区政府和区监察局反映政府机关及其工作人员执行公务、执行法律和法规等情况；随时提供政府机关和企事业单位及其工作人员廉政情况的信息，参与重大案件以及涉及到民主党派成员的案件的查处；参与一些重要案件的定性处理的讨论等。
