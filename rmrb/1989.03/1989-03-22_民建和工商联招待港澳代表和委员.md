Title: The China Democratic National Construction Association and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Host Representatives and Members from Hong Kong and Macau

Author: Xiaodu

Date: 1989-03-22

Edition: 2

The China Democratic National Construction Association and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce held a banquet today to host representatives from Hong Kong and Macau attending the Second Session of the Seventh National People's Congress and members from Hong Kong and Macau attending the Second Session of the Seventh Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Rong Yiren gave a speech and toast at the banquet.

### 民建和工商联招待港澳代表和委员
晓渡
1989-03-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　民建和工商联招待港澳代表和委员
    本报北京３月２１日讯  记者晓渡报道：中国民主建国会和中华全国工商业联合会今天举行宴会，招待出席全国人大七届二次会议的港澳地区代表和全国政协七届二次会议的港澳地区委员。荣毅仁在宴会上致辞祝酒。
