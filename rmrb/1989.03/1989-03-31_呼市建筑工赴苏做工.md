Title: Construction Workers from Hohhot Head to the Soviet Union for Work
Author: Ào Tēng
Date: 1989-03-31
Edition: 2

Hohhot Construction Workers Head to the Soviet Union for Work

HOHHOT, March 29 (Xinhua) — Today, the first construction company of Hohhot City sent off 1,242 workers to the Soviet Union to undertake a series of engineering projects. These projects are located in Khabarovsk, Komsomolsk-on-Amur, and Amur City in the Soviet Union.

Prior to this, Hohhot City had already sent 400 people to Chita in the Soviet Union to undertake the construction of a cold storage facility. Soon, two more batches of construction workers will also head to the Soviet Union. It is estimated that this year, Hohhot City will send 3,500 to 4,000 people to the Soviet Union to participate in construction projects.

### 呼市建筑工赴苏做工
傲腾
1989-03-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　呼市建筑工赴苏做工
    本报呼和浩特３月２９日电  记者傲腾报道：今天，呼和浩特市第一建筑工程公司欢送１２４２名工人赴苏联承担一批工程项目。这些工程分布在苏联的哈巴罗夫斯克、共青城、阿莫尔城。
    在此之前，呼和浩特市已有４００人赴苏联赤塔承担了修建冷库的工程。不久还将有两批建筑工人赴苏。预计今年呼和浩特市有３５００—４０００人去苏联参加土建施工。
