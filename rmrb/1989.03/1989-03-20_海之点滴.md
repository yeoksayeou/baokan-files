Title: Droplets of the Sea
Author: Lú Lí
Date: 1989-03-20
Edition: 8

I
Sea, oh sea,
Azure sea, ink-black sea,
Give me a droplet,
And I can write a poem.

II
In Beidaihe,
I walked along the beach,
Couldn't find a single seashell,
But picked up a piece of sunset.

III
Bluebell flowers bloom on the mountain,
Like blue stars,
They are eyes full of affection,
They are the essence of the sea's soul.

IV
On the sandy beach,
Clothes of various colors are scattered,
People are all naked in the water,
It's hard to tell who is the officer, general,
Commoner, monk…

V
From the sparkling water,
Laughter bubbles up,
The vast sea drowns all human hierarchies,
The boundless sky treats all equally.

VI
She lies on the golden sand,
Letting the wild waves kiss her ankles,
She seems to be wearing a glass dress,
Lost in the cradle of sunlight.

VII
In the world of fame and fortune,
People's eyes become like beans,
In the sea water,
People find the pure heart of a child.

VIII
I've traveled to the ends of the earth,
Crossed mountains and rivers,
Once sought a laurel wreath,
But picked up a piece of sunset.

### 海之点滴
鲁藜
1989-03-20
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　海之点滴
    鲁藜
    一
    海啊，海啊
    湛蓝的海，墨水般的海
    给我一滴
    我就能写一首诗
    　　　　二
    在北戴河
    我走遍海滩
    找不到一枚彩贝
    却拾到一片晚霞
    　　　　三
    蓝铃花开在山上
    像蓝色星星
    是深情的眼睛
    是海之魂的结晶
    　　　　四
    沙滩上
    乱堆着各色各样服装
    人们都赤身在水中
    分不清谁是长官，将军，
    　平民，和尚……
    　　　　五
    从珠光闪烁的水中
    不时溅出晶莹的欢笑声
    博大的海淹没人间一切
    　等级概念
    高邃的苍穹一视同仁
    　　　　六
    她仰卧在金沙上
    让颠狂浪花吻她的足踝
    她好象穿着玻璃衫
    陶醉在阳光的摇篮里
    　　　　七
    人在名利场
    变得目光如豆
    人在海水中
    又找到珍珠的童心
    　　　　八
    我走遍天涯海角
    跋涉千山万水
    曾想寻找一顶桂冠
    却拾到一片晚霞
　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年３月８日
