Title: The Fifth China-Japan Go Match Lineup Announced
Author: Zhang Shu
Date: 1989-03-17
Edition: 3

The lineup for the fifth China-Japan Go match has been finalized. Both sides have eight players each.

As in the previous match, the captains remain the same: Nie Weiping leads the Chinese team, and Fuji Communications Cup World Champion Takeyama Mikio leads the Japanese team. The Japanese team has made some adjustments, including sending nearly 70-year-old veteran Sakata Eio as the vice-captain, as well as sending Shi Tianfeng, known for his clear logic and nicknamed "Big Computer," and the pillar of the Kansai Go Association, Yuan Tianyou. This year's Japanese lineup is stronger than the previous one. The lineups for both sides are as follows:

Chinese Team:
Nie Weiping (Go Saint)
Ma Xiaochun (9th Dan)
Liu Xiaoguang (Meijin, Tianyuan)
Jiang Zhujiu (9th Dan)
Qian Yuping (9th Dan)
Yu Bin (8th Dan)
Zhang Wendi (6th Dan)
Yang Shihai (3rd Dan)

Japanese Team:
Takeyama Mikio (World Champion)
Sakata Eio (Honorary Honinbo)
Shi Tianfeng (9th Dan)
Yamashiro Hiroshi (9th Dan)
Ohirata Shuzo (9th Dan)
Hane Yosei (9th Dan)
Yuan Tianyou (9th Dan)
Yida Jiki (7th Dan)
