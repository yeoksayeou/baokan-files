Title: A Book Titled "Chinese People's View of the Federal Republic of Germany" is Published
Author:
Date: 1989-03-24
Edition: 2

The book "Chinese People's View of the Federal Republic of Germany," written by more than ten Chinese journalists who have visited the Federal Republic of Germany, is soon to be published by Shanxi People's Publishing House. Chancellor Helmut Kohl of the Federal Republic of Germany wrote a foreword for the book titled "Wish More Chinese People to Know German Culture."

### 《中国人看联邦德国》成书出版

1989-03-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　《中国人看联邦德国》成书出版
    本报讯  由曾经访问过联邦德国的十几位中国新闻工作者撰写的《中国人看联邦德国》一书，即将由山西人民出版社出版。联邦德国总理科尔为该书撰写了题为《愿更多的中国人了解德国文化》的前言。
