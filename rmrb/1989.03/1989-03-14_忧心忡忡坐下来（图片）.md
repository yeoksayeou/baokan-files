Title: Worriedly Sitting Down
Author: Zhan Yusun
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 5

The photograph captures a moment of deep concern and anxiety. The subject, seated, appears to be burdened by heavy thoughts, reflecting the tumultuous times of 1989. The image, taken by Zhan Yusun, is a poignant representation of the emotional state of many individuals during this period. The composition and the expression on the subject's face convey a sense of unease and apprehension, making it a powerful piece of documentary photography. This photograph was submitted to the news photography competition, highlighting the photographer's ability to capture the essence of human emotion in a single frame.
