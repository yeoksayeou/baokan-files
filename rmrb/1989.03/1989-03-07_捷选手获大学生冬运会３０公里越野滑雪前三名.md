Title: Czechoslovakian Athletes Secure Top Three Spots in 30km Cross-Country Skiing at Winter Universiade
Author: Wáng Jīn Bǎi
Date: 1989-03-07
Edition: 4

Czechoslovakian athletes secured the top three spots in the men's 30km freestyle cross-country skiing event at the 14th Winter Universiade held today in Vitosha Mountain, south of Sofia.

Václav Aleš of Czechoslovakia completed the course in 1 hour, 22 minutes, and 12.9 seconds, claiming the gold medal.

The second and third-place finishers, Luboš Pítek and Jan Martinek, crossed the finish line 2.7 seconds and 6 seconds behind the gold medalist, respectively.

### 捷选手获大学生冬运会３０公里越野滑雪前三名
王金柏
1989-03-07
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　捷选手获大学生冬运会３０公里越野滑雪前三名
    新华社索非亚３月５日电  （记者王金柏）第十四届世界大学生冬季运动会今天在索非亚南面的维托沙山举行男子３０公里自由式越野滑雪赛，捷克斯洛伐克选手囊括了这项比赛的金、银、铜牌。
    捷选手瓦·阿莱什用１小时２２分１２秒９的时间滑完全程，获第一名。
    获第二、三名的利·皮泰尔和扬·马尔廷，到达终点的时间分别比金牌获得者慢了２秒７和６秒。
