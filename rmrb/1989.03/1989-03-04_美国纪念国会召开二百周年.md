Title: The United States Marks the 200th Anniversary of the Opening of Congress
Author:
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 3

The 101st Congress of the United States held a joint session today to celebrate the 200th anniversary of the convening of the first U.S. Congress. Approximately one-quarter of the members attended the joint session and participated in the unveiling ceremony for the commemorative stamp and coin marking the 200th anniversary.

The first U.S. Congress was convened on March 4, 1789, in New York.

### 美国纪念国会召开二百周年

1989-03-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国纪念国会召开二百周年
    本报华盛顿３月２日电  美国第１０１届国会今天举行两院联席会议，庆祝美国第一届国会召开两百周年。大约１／４议员参加了联席会，并出席了两百周年纪念邮票和纪念币的揭幕仪式。
    美国第一届国会是１７８９年３月４日在纽约召开的。
