Mongolian Doctor Xurijigan Leads Research on "Test-Tube Sheep" Birth

The first "test-tube sheep" in China was born at 1:59 a.m. today in Hohhot. This major scientific achievement, which is on par with international standards, was accomplished by a research team led by Xurijigan, a Mongolian veterinarian and doctor of veterinary medicine who is the director of the Experimental Animal Research Center at Inner Mongolia University. After more than two years of effort, this breakthrough marks a new stage in China's life science research.

On March 9, 1984, while studying in Japan, Xurijigan collaborated with Japanese researchers to cultivate the world's first "test-tube goat," which garnered attention from peers around the world. Today, Xurijigan has made new advancements in this high-tech research field. Compared to five years ago, this achievement is more challenging and has a higher success rate.

Today, reporters visited the center and saw the recently born test-tube black sheep lamb.

This lamb weighs 5.3 kilograms and is sturdy and healthy. Currently, there are 9 more ewes that are pregnant, and if there are no complications, it is expected that by the end of April, a small group of "test-tube sheep" will be born.

### 蒙古族博士旭日干主持研究  “试管绵羊”降生
傲腾　陆永龙
1989-03-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　蒙古族博士旭日干主持研究
   “试管绵羊”降生
    本报呼和浩特３月１０日电  我国第一胎“试管绵羊”今日凌晨１点５９分在呼和浩特降生。这项具有国际水平的重大科研成果，是由内蒙古大学实验动物研究中心主任、蒙古族兽医学博士旭日干主持的科研小组经过两年多努力获得的。它标志我国这项生命科学研究进入一个新的发展阶段。
    １９８４年３月９日，旭日干在日本进修期间，与日方合作，培育出世界上第一胎“试管山羊”，引起了各国同行关注。今天，旭日干在这项高技术研究领域中又取得新的进展。与５年前相比，具有难度大、成功率高的特点。
    今天，记者在这个中心看到了出世不久的试管黑头羊羔。
    这只小羊羔体重５．３公斤，结实健壮。现在，还有９只母羊正处于妊娠之中，没有意外情况，预计在４月底将出生一小群“试管绵羊”。
