Title: Rao Re-elected as General Secretary of the CPI
Author:
Date: 1989-03-17
Edition: 4

Rao Re-elected as General Secretary of the CPI

New Delhi, March 14 (Xinhua) — R. Rajaram Rao was re-elected as the General Secretary of the Communist Party of India (CPI) yesterday, marking his fourth consecutive term in the position.

R. Rajaram Rao was elected during the CPI National Committee meeting held in Kolkata yesterday. The meeting also elected a nine-member Central Secretariat of the CPI.

### 拉奥再次当选印共总书记

1989-03-17
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　拉奥再次当选印共总书记
    新华社新德里３月１４日电  印度共产党总书记拉·拉奥昨天再次当选，这是他第四次连任印共总书记。
    拉·拉奥是在昨天在加尔各答举行的印共全国委员会会议上当选的。这次会议选举了由９人组成的印共中央书记处。
