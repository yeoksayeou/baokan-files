Title: Li Tieying Inspects Education in Shandong
Author: Yang Jianye, Yuan Wenzhong
Date: 1989-03-09
Edition: 2

    Li Tieying, a member of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China Central Committee and a State Councilor, and also the Minister of the State Education Commission, inspected educational work in Pingdu County and Weifang City, Shandong Province on March 7.

    In Pingdu County, Li Tieying inspected the county teacher training center, vocational education center, and county vocational secondary school, as well as the central primary and secondary schools in Mingcun Town and Xiangdian Township. In Weifang City, he inspected the central primary and secondary schools in Weizhou Town, Changyi County, and the First Middle School of Weifang City. Li Tieying listened to reports from the relevant school leaders, inquired about the learning and living conditions of teachers and students, encouraged school leaders and teachers to do a good job in education, and urged students to strive to learn scientific and cultural knowledge and labor skills for the rejuvenation of China.

### 李铁映在鲁考察教育
杨建业　袁文忠
1989-03-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　李铁映在鲁考察教育
    据新华社济南３月８日电  （中国教育报记者杨建业、新华社记者袁文忠）中共中央政治局委员、国务委员兼国家教委主任李铁映３月７日到山东省平度县、潍坊市考察教育工作。
    李铁映在平度县农村先后考察了县师资培训中心、职业教育中心、县职业中专，以及明村镇、香店乡中心中学、小学等；在潍坊市，考察了昌邑县围子镇中心小学、初中和市第一中学。李铁映听取了有关学校领导的汇报，询问了教师、学生的学习和生活情况，鼓励学校领导、教师办好教育，勉励学生为振兴中华而努力学习科学文化知识和劳动技能。
