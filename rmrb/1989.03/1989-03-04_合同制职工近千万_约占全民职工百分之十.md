Title: Nearly 10 Million Contract Workers, Making Up About 10% of State Employees
Author: Wei Xiaoxia
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 1

The number of contract workers in China is steadily increasing. Currently, there are over 9.9 million contract workers in state-owned enterprises, making up about 10% of the total state-owned enterprise workforce.

According to the National Bureau of Statistics, provinces such as Hebei, Liaoning, and Qinghai have made significant progress in fully implementing the labor contract system and related reforms in wages and benefits for contract workers last year.

### 合同制职工近千万  约占全民职工百分之十
魏晓霞
1989-03-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　合同制职工近千万
    约占全民职工百分之十
    新华社北京３月３日电  （通讯员魏晓霞）我国合同制职工队伍稳步发展，目前全民所有制单位有合同制职工９９０多万人，占全民所有制职工人数的１０％左右。
    据国家统计局介绍，河北、辽宁、青海等地去年全面推行劳动合同制与合同制工人的工资福利的配套改革，取得明显进展。
