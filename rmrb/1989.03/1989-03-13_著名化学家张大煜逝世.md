Title: Renowned Chemist Zhang Dayu Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-03-13
Edition: 2

Renowned Chemist Zhang Dayu Passes Away

	New China News Agency, Beijing, March 10 — Member of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Senior Researcher, and one of the principal founders of the field of catalysis in China, Communist Party of China member Professor Zhang Dayu passed away at 2:30 AM on February 20, 1989, at Beijing Hospital due to prolonged illness. He was 83 years old.

	Professor Zhang Dayu was an outstanding chemist in China. He made indelible contributions to the development of China's fuel chemistry, catalysis, and colloid chemistry, as well as to the cultivation of scientific talent in China. He was highly respected in the scientific community.

	Before his passing, Professor Zhang Dayu served as an advisor to the Chinese Academy of Sciences Photochemistry Institute and concurrently as an advisor to the Dalian Institute of Chemical Physics. He was elected as a representative of the First, Second, and Third National People's Congress, a member of the Fifth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and a member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League.

### 著名化学家张大煜逝世

1989-03-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　著名化学家张大煜逝世
    新华社北京３月１０日电  中国科学院学部委员、一级研究员、我国催化学科的主要奠基人，中共党员张大煜教授因病久治无效，于１９８９年２月２０日凌晨２时３０分在北京医院逝世，终年８３岁。
    张大煜教授是我国杰出的化学家，他为我国的燃料化学、催化及胶体化学等科学事业的发展、为培养我国的科学人才做出了不可磨灭的贡献，在科学界享有崇高的威望。
    张大煜教授生前系中国科学院感光化学所顾问并兼大连化学物理所顾问；曾当选为全国人民代表大会第一、二、三届代表，全国政协第五届委员、中国民盟中央委员。
