Title: Villagers in Sri Lanka Massacred
Author:
Date: 1989-03-02
Edition: 3

In the early hours of February 28, 42 Sinhalese villagers were hacked to death with knives in the eastern border region of the North Central Province of Sri Lanka by a group of unidentified individuals. Among the victims were eight women and 19 children.

According to reports, the assailants are suspected to be key members of the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam (LTTE) in Sri Lanka.

Following the incident, Sri Lankan security forces rushed to the scene. However, they stepped on a landmine en route, resulting in one injury.

### 斯里兰卡一批村民遭屠杀

1989-03-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    斯里兰卡一批村民遭屠杀
    二月二十八日凌晨，斯里兰卡北中央省的东部边界地区，有四十二名僧伽罗族村民被一伙不明身份的人用刀砍死。遇难者中有八名妇女，十九名儿童。
    据报道，杀手被怀疑是斯里兰卡泰米尔伊拉姆猛虎解放组织（猛虎组织）的骨干人物。
    事件发生后，斯里兰卡治安部队赶赴现场。然而，部队在途中又踩上地雷，有一人受伤。　　　　　（据新华社）
