Title: The World University Winter Games Opens
Author: Wang Jinbo
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 4

The 14th World University Winter Games opened in the Bulgarian capital of Sofia.

The grand opening ceremony was held at 18:00 this evening at the Winter Palace Sports Hall, the main venue for this event, located on the outskirts of Sofia. G. Yordanov, a candidate member of the Politburo of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and P. Nebiolo, president of the International University Sports Federation, attended the opening ceremony. Yordanov, on behalf of the Chairman of the State Council of Bulgaria, T. Zhivkov, extended a warm welcome to more than 1,000 ice and snow athletes from around the world, wishing them excellent results in the competitions and hoping to deepen the understanding and friendship among university students from around the world.

According to materials provided by the organizing committee of the Games, ice and snow sports powerhouses such as the United States, the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, and Japan have sent teams of 129, 101, 85, and 76 athletes respectively to compete in the eight events of this Winter Games. Among them, many have won medals at the Calgary Winter Olympics.

Chinese athletes will participate in four events: short track speed skating, figure skating, biathlon, and cross-country skiing.

Ice events will be held in Sofia. The much-anticipated ice hockey matches will start tomorrow. Snow events will be held in Vidisha Mountain in the south of Sofia and the slopes of the Bansko Plateau, 150 kilometers from Sofia.

### 世界大学生冬运会揭幕
王金柏
1989-03-04
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　世界大学生冬运会揭幕
    新华社索非亚３月２日电  （记者王金柏）第１４届世界大学生冬季运动会在保加利亚首都索非亚揭幕。
    隆重的开幕式于今晚１８时在本届运动会的中心场地索非亚市郊的冬宫体育馆举行。保共中央政治局候补委员格·约尔丹诺夫和国际大学生体联主席普·内比奥罗出席了开幕式。约尔丹诺夫代表保加利亚国务委员会主席托·日夫科夫向来自世界各地的１０００多名冰雪运动员表示热烈欢迎，预祝他们在比赛中取得优异成绩，增进世界大学生之间的了解和友谊。
    据赛会组委会提供的材料，冰雪运动强国美国、苏联、捷克斯洛伐克以及日本分别派出了１２９人、１０１人、８５人和７６人的强大队伍，参加本届冬运会８个项目的争夺。其中不少人曾在卡尔加里冬季奥运会上获得过奖牌。
    中国选手参加短跑道速滑、花样滑冰、冬季两项和越野滑雪４项比赛。
    冰上项目的比赛将在索非亚市举行。引人注目的冰球赛将于明天率先打响。雪上项目的比赛将分别在索非亚南部的维多沙山和离索非亚１５０公里的班斯科高原坡地举行。
