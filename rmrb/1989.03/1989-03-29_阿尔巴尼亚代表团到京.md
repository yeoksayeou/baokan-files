Title: Albanian Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-03-29
Edition: 2

Albanian Delegation Arrives in Beijing

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 28th: Deputy Foreign Minister Muhamedi Kaprani led an Albanian delegation that arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon, beginning a five-day visit to China.

Kaprani told reporters at the airport that he is visiting China to develop relations between the two countries.

This is the first political delegation sent by the Albanian government to China since the late 1970s, when relations between the two countries cooled. The delegation includes Konstantin Hoxha, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Tian Zengpei went to the airport this afternoon to welcome the Albanian delegation. In the evening, he held a welcome banquet for the guests at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

### 阿尔巴尼亚代表团到京

1989-03-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚代表团到京
    据新华社北京３月２８日电  穆哈梅蒂·卡普拉尼副外长率领的阿尔巴尼亚代表团今天下午乘飞机到达北京，开始对中国进行为期５天的访问。
    卡普拉尼在机场对记者说，他是为发展阿中两国关系而来中国访问的。
    这是自７０年代后期中阿两国关系冷漠以来阿尔巴尼亚政府向中国派出的第一个政治性质的代表团。代表团团员中有外贸部第一副部长康斯坦丁·霍查。
    中国外交部副部长田曾佩下午去机场迎接阿尔巴尼亚代表团。晚上，他在钓鱼台国宾馆为客人举行欢迎宴会。
