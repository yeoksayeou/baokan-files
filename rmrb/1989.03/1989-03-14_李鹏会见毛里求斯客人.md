Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 1

Li Peng Meets with Mauritanian Guests

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 13 – Premier Li Peng met with Mauritian Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Sitanah Rashminaredou and his wife this afternoon at the Zhongnanhai Purple Light Hall.

Both sides expressed satisfaction with the development of friendly cooperative relations between China and Mauritius, and hoped to continue efforts to develop this relationship.

Rashminaredou expressed appreciation for China's stance on issues such as the Indian Ocean peace zone, support for Namibian independence, and opposition to South African apartheid.

Li Peng said, "We are pleased to see that relevant parties have reached an agreement on the Namibian issue, and we hope there will be no reversals. The international community should continue to urge South Africa to completely change its apartheid policies."

### 李鹏会见毛里求斯客人

1989-03-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏会见毛里求斯客人
    据新华社北京３月１３日电  国务院总理李鹏今天下午在中南海紫光阁会见毛里求斯副总理兼财政部长西塔纳·拉什米纳雷杜和夫人一行。
    双方对中毛两国友好合作关系的发展表示满意，并希望继续努力发展这种关系。
    拉什米纳雷杜对中国在印度洋和平区、支持纳米比亚独立、反对南非种族主义等问题上所持的立场表示赞赏。
    李鹏说，我们高兴地看到有关各方就纳米比亚问题达成了协议，并希望不要出现反复，国际社会应继续敦促南非彻底改变种族主义政策。
