Title: The Battle of Beirut Continues
Author:
Date: 1989-03-27
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, March 25th.

The fighting between the Christian Lebanese government forces and the pro-Syrian Lebanese Muslim militias in the eastern and western districts of Beirut continued today.

According to reports, during the overnight shelling on the 24th, 7 people were killed and 43 were injured, bringing the total number of casualties since March 14th to over 370.

Since 4 PM today, the shelling near the demarcation line between the eastern and western districts of Beirut has been particularly intense, with the sound of cannons shaking the entire city.

### 贝鲁特炮战仍在继续

1989-03-27
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　贝鲁特炮战仍在继续
    新华社贝鲁特３月２５日电  黎巴嫩基督教政府军同亲叙利亚的黎巴嫩穆斯林民兵在贝鲁特东区和西区之间的炮击战今天仍在继续。
    据报道，在２４日的通宵炮击战中，有７人被炸死，４３人受伤，从而使３月１４日以来的伤亡人数达３７０多人。
    从今天下午４时起，贝鲁特东、西区分界线附近地区的炮击战尤为激烈，炮声震撼全城。
