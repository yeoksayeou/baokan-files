Title: Guerrillas and Government Forces Continue to Clash; Battle for Jalalabad Intensifies
Author:
Date: 1989-03-19
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Islamabad, March 18th.

Guerrillas and government forces in Afghanistan continued to clash fiercely in the eastern city of Jalalabad yesterday.

The Afghan News Agency reported that government aircraft are continuing to bomb the guerrillas heavily, causing significant casualties among them.

According to a news bulletin from the guerrillas, the guerrillas shot down three government fighter jets and one helicopter at Jalalabad Airport.

Another report from the Afghan News Center stated that in the Behsud area near Jalalabad, 23 guerrillas were killed and 45 others were wounded in the battle to attack government outposts. The report said that the guerrillas have seized eight government outposts in the Behsud area.

### 游击队和政府军继续激战  贾拉拉巴德战事日趋紧张

1989-03-19
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　游击队和政府军继续激战  贾拉拉巴德战事日趋紧张
    新华社伊斯兰堡３月１８日电  阿富汗游击队与政府军昨天在阿富汗东部重镇贾拉拉巴德继续展开激战。
    阿富汗新闻社说政府军的飞机正继续对游击队进行猛烈轰炸并使游击队遭到重大伤亡。
    据游击队方面发表的新闻公报说，游击队在贾拉拉巴德机场击落３架政府军战斗机和１架直升机。
    另据阿富汗新闻中心报道，在贾拉拉巴德附近的贝赫苏德地区，游击队在进攻政府军哨所的战斗中，有２３人被打死，另外４５人被打伤。报道说，游击队方面已经占领了８个政府军设在贝赫苏德地区的哨所。
