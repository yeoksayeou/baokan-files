Author:
Date: 1989-03-09
Edition: 3

Uzbek Supreme Soviet Chairman Replaced

	MOSCOW, March 7 (Xinhua) -- The Soviet newspaper Pravda reported today that Habibullayev was relieved of his post as Chairman of the Presidium of the Uzbek Supreme Soviet on March 6. His position was taken over by Ibragimov, former permanent representative of Uzbekistan to the USSR Council of Ministers. According to the March 6 extraordinary session of the Uzbek Supreme Soviet, Habibullayev had close ties with former Uzbek leaders who were convicted of embezzlement and bribery, and had arranged favorable positions for their relatives.

### 乌兹别克最高苏维埃主席易人

1989-03-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　乌兹别克最高苏维埃主席易人
    新华社莫斯科３月７日电  苏联《真理报》今天报道，哈比布拉耶夫６日被解除乌兹别克最高苏维埃主席团主席职务，这一职务由前乌兹别克常驻苏联部长会议代表易卜拉欣莫夫继任。据３月６日举行的乌兹别克最高苏维埃非常会议揭露，哈比布拉耶夫同犯有贪污受贿罪的乌兹别克共和国前领导人关系密切，并为他们的亲属安排肥缺。
