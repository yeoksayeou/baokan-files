Title: The 80 Teams of the Beijing Fengtai District Foodstuff Company's Youth League Committee Continue to Serve the Community
Author: Tán Zhìshēng
Date: 1989-03-24
Edition: 5

Over the past decade, the 80 Learn-from-Lei-Feng small teams of the Beijing Fengtai District Foodstuff Company's Youth League Committee have persisted in going from street to street, delivering goods to residents' doorsteps or to hospital wards. Look, the delivery vehicle of the small team is setting off again. Photographed by Tan Zhisheng.
