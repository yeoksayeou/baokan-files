Title: Daqiu Village Designated as a Science and Technology Experiment Village
Author:
Date: 1989-03-31
Edition: 2

The State Science and Technology Commission recently decided to designate Daqiu Village in Jixian County, Tianjin Municipality, as a key demonstration village for the "Spark" science and technology program aimed at the modernization and industrialization of rural areas.

### 大邱庄定为科技试验村

1989-03-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　大邱庄定为科技试验村
    国家科委最近决定将天津市静海县大邱庄列为农村企业化、农村现代化“星火”科技示范试验重点。　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
