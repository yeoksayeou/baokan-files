Title: Various Circles Bid Farewell to the Body of Zhu Liangcai
Author:
Date: 1989-03-16
Edition: 1

Today's second page published the news of the farewell ceremony for the body of Zhu Liangcai held in Beijing. Comrades Yang Shangkun, Li Xiannian, Qiao Shi, Yao Yilin, and Wang Zhen attended the ceremony. Additionally, the second page also published a letter from Marshal Nie to Zhu Liangcai's wife.

Furthermore, today's sixth page published an article introducing the life of Comrade Zhu Liangcai.

### 各界向朱良才遗体告别

1989-03-16
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　各界向朱良才遗体告别
    本报讯  今日二版刊登了朱良才遗体告别仪式在京举行的消息，杨尚昆、李先念、乔石、姚依林和王震等同志参加了告别仪式。同时刊登在二版的还有聂帅致朱良才夫人的信。
    另外，今日六版还刊登了介绍朱良才同志生平的文章。
