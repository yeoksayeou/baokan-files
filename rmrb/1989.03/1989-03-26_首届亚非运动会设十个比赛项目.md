Title: The First Asian-African Games to Feature Ten Competitions
Author:
Date: 1989-03-26
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 21st.

The first Asian-African Games will be held in November 1991 in the Indian capital, New Delhi, with competitions in ten events.

The first Asian-African Games will include athletics, football, field hockey, basketball, handball, volleyball, wrestling, judo, weightlifting, and tennis.

This decision was made at the Asian-African Sports Coordination Committee meeting held in Kuwait on the 19th of this month.

Indian Olympic Association Secretary-General Lintel Singh, who participated in the meeting, returned here today and told reporters that 1,600 athletes and 400 officials will participate in this Asian-African Games.

### 首届亚非运动会设十个比赛项目

1989-03-26
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　首届亚非运动会设十个比赛项目
    新华社新德里３月２１日电  首届亚非运动会定于１９９１年（前误发为今年）１１月在印度首都新德里举行，届时将进行１０个项目的角ss逐。
    首届亚非运动会设田径、足球、曲棍球、篮球、手球、排球、摔跤、柔道、举重和网球等项目。
    上述决定是本月１９日在科威特举行的亚非体育协调委员会上作出的。
    参加这次会议的印度奥林匹克协会秘书长伦提尔·辛格今天回到这里并对记者说，将有１６００名运动员和４００名官员参加这届亚非运动会。
