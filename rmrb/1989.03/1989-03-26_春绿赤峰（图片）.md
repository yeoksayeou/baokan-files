Title: Spring Greenery in Chifeng
Author:
Date: 1989-03-26
Edition: 2

    For over 30 years, the city of Chifeng in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has consistently focused on urban beautification and greening efforts. The greening area has increased from 4.26% in the early days of the founding of the nation to 28.6%. The image shows the greening scene at Hongqi Square. Photographed by Saqila.
