Title: International Marathon Race in Hainan
Author: Jiang Enyu
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 4

Athletes from China, Japan, the United States, West Germany, and Macao, totaling 152 participants, took part in an international marathon race held in February in Hainan Province. The event was captured by New China News Agency reporter Jiang Enyu.
