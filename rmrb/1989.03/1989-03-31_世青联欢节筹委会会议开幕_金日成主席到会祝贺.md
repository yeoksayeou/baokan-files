Title: The Preparatory Committee Meeting for the 13th World Youth Festival Opens
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-03-31
Edition: 4

The preparatory committee meeting for the 13th World Youth Festival opened today at the Mansudae Assembly Hall in Pyongyang. Chairman Kim Il-sung attended the meeting to offer congratulations and delivered a speech.

Kim Il-sung said that the 13th World Youth Festival held in Pyongyang has received high attention from the Party and the state of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the entire nation has been mobilized to prepare for the event. The DPRK will warmly welcome and entertain all guests.

Kim Il-sung pointed out that the youth movement in the DPRK is an inseparable part of the world youth movement. The youth of the DPRK will fulfill their duties in the struggle for "anti-imperialism, independence, anti-war, and peace" and will strive to strengthen the world youth movement.

Kim Il-sung hoped that progressive youth around the world would overcome differences in ideology, social systems, and racial backgrounds, unite closely, support each other, and cooperate closely for common causes. Attending today's opening ceremony were 378 representatives from 134 countries and 18 international youth organizations.

### 世青联欢节筹委会会议开幕  金日成主席到会祝贺
徐宝康
1989-03-31
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　世青联欢节筹委会会议开幕        
    金日成主席到会祝贺
    本报平壤３月３０日电  记者徐宝康报道：第十三届世界青年联欢节国际筹委会第四次会议今天在平壤万寿台议事厅举行开幕式。金日成主席到会表示祝贺，并发表讲话。
    金日成说，第十三届世界青年联欢节在平壤举行，受到了朝鲜党和国家的高度重视，并动员全民投入了筹备工作。朝鲜将热烈欢迎和款待所有客人。
    金日成指出，朝鲜青年运动，是世界青年运动不可分割的一部分。朝鲜青年将在“反帝、自主，反战、和平”的斗争中，尽到自己的责任，并为加强世界青年运动竭尽一切努力。
    金日成期望世界进步青年超越思想信仰、社会制度、种族肤色上的差别，紧密团结起来，为共同的事业，相互支持，紧密合作。参加今天开幕式的有１３４个国家和１８个国际青年组织的３７８名代表。
