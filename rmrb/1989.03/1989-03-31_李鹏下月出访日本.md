Title: Li Peng to Visit Japan Next Month
Author:
Date: 1989-03-31
Edition: 1

	The Ministry of Foreign Affairs spokesperson announced today that, at the invitation of the Japanese government, Premier Li Peng will pay an official friendly visit to Japan from April 12 to 16.

### 李鹏下月出访日本

1989-03-31
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏下月出访日本
    新华社北京３月３０日电外交部发言人今天在这里宣布，应日本政府邀请，国务院总理李鹏将于４月１２日至１６日对日本进行正式友好访问。
