Title: UK Supports a Representative Afghan Government, Pakistan Rejects Kabul's Accusations, Soviets Believe Jalalabad May Face More Intense Attacks
Author:
Date: 1989-03-30
Edition: 4

British Foreign Secretary Geoffrey Howe, currently visiting Islamabad, stated today that the UK supports the establishment of a truly representative government in Afghanistan.

Howe expressed this stance to the press after concluding his two-day visit to Pakistan.

Howe also met with the leader of the Afghan Seven Party Alliance's interim government, Mujaddidi. Mujaddidi requested that the UK recognize his government.

Pakistan today rejected accusations from the Kabul regime that it had interfered in Afghanistan's internal affairs and violated the Geneva Accords.

A spokesperson for Pakistan's permanent mission to the United Nations told Xinhua that the accusations made by Kabul regime leader Najibullah in a letter to UN Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar were part of Kabul's propaganda campaign and were "groundless."

The Chief of the General Staff of the Soviet Armed Forces, General Chervov, wrote in today's *Pravda* that Afghan anti-government forces might launch a new and more intense attack on Jalalabad.

### 英支持有代表性的阿政府  巴基斯坦拒绝喀布尔指责  苏认为贾拉拉巴德可能遭更猛烈进攻

1989-03-30
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　英支持有代表性的阿政府        
    巴基斯坦拒绝喀布尔指责     
    苏认为贾拉拉巴德可能遭更猛烈进攻
    新华社伊斯兰堡３月２８日电  正在这里访问的英国外交大臣杰弗里·豪今天说，英国支持建立一个有真正代表性的阿富汗政府。
    杰弗里·豪在结束对巴基斯坦进行的两天访问后对新闻界表示了英国的这一立场。
    杰弗里·豪还会见了阿富汗７党联盟临时政府领导人穆贾迪迪。穆贾迪迪要求英国承认他的政府。
    新华社联合国３月２８日电　巴基斯坦今天拒绝了喀布尔政权对它干涉阿富汗内政，违反日内瓦协议的指控。
    巴基斯坦常驻联合国代表团发言人向新华社记者说，喀布尔政权领导人纳吉布拉在致联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔的信中对巴基斯坦的谴责，是喀布尔政权宣传战的一部分，是“毫无根据”的。
    新华社莫斯科３月２８日电苏军总参谋部局长切尔沃夫上将今天在《真理报》撰文认为，阿富汗反政府军有可能对贾拉拉巴德发动新的更为猛烈的进攻。
