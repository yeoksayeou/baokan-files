Title: Railway Cargo Theft Keeps Insurance Companies Busy
Author: Wáng Yīng  Xiè Débiāo
Date: 1989-03-08
Edition: 6

Some enterprises in our city have reported that a significant number of goods they shipped from other places have been stolen during railway transportation. In 1988, our city's insurance company handled 420 such cases, with a total loss amounting to over 260,000 yuan.

The head of our city's insurance company stated that in 1988, Zhangye Commodity Station alone had 200 incidents of goods being stolen during railway consignment. For instance, on August 25, 1988, 14 bales of acrylic fabric were shipped from Guangzhou to Zhangye, with 8 bales stolen en route, resulting in a loss of 13,900 yuan. In November of the same year, 5 Japanese cameras were shipped from Hangzhou, with 4 stolen en route, causing a loss of 5,360 yuan.

We urge relevant departments to take measures, enforce the law to crack down on criminal elements, and protect the interests of the consignors!

		Zhangye, Gansu Province
		Wáng Yīng  Xiè Débiāo

### 铁路运输货物丢失  可忙坏了保险公司
王英　谢德彪
1989-03-08
第6版(读者来信)
专栏：

　　铁路运输货物丢失  可忙坏了保险公司
    我市一些企业反映，他们从外地发回的货物，不少在铁路运输途中被盗。１９８８年，我市保险公司代理勘查这类案件４２０起，查出货损金额达２６万多元。
    我市保险公司负责人说，１９８８年，仅张掖百货站在铁路托运的货物，被盗事件就发生２００起。例如，１９８８年８月２５日，这个百货站由广州发往张掖的１４件腈纶布料，其中有８件在途中被盗，损失１．３９万元。同年１１月，这个百货站从杭州发回的５架日本照相机，其中４架途中被盗，损失５３６０元。呼吁有关部门采取措施，依法打击不法分子，保护货主利益！
　　　　　　　　　　　　　甘肃张掖市　王英　谢德彪
