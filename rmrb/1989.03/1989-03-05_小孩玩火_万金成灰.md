Title: Children Playing with Fire Reduces Wealth to Ashes
Author: He Yongshun
Date: 1989-03-05
Edition: 4

A private entrepreneur from Jing County, Anhui Province, who deals in food and clothing sales, Deng, wrapped 40,000 yuan in cash in paper and hid it inside a pair of rubber boots under his bed. Recently, his two young grandsons were playing with fire at home, accidentally starting a fire that resulted in the 40,000 yuan being completely burned to ashes.

### 小孩玩火　万金成灰
何永顺
1989-03-05
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：

　　小孩玩火　万金成灰
    据新华社合肥３月４日电  （通讯员何永顺）安徽泾县一位从事饮食和服装销售的个体户邓某，将４万元现金用纸包好，藏在家里的胶鞋内，放在床底下。前不久，他的两个小孙子在家玩火，不慎酿成火灾，致使４万元巨款全被烧成灰烬。
