Title: Huixi Ink Factory Designs New Stamps
Author: Lù Yàn
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 4

	To welcome the 1990 Beijing 11th Asian Games, the Huixi Ink Factory in She County, Anhui, has designed a set of two unique stamp inks. Each stamp features the mascot and emblem of the Asian Games. The factory has also designed a set of "Japanese Stamp Collection Treasure Ink." This set includes four stamp inks featuring Japanese ukiyo-e prints. The colors, framing, and craftsmanship are exquisite, making them both collectible and suitable for gifting as mementos.

### 徽歙墨厂设计新邮票墨
陆燕
1989-03-04
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　徽歙墨厂设计新邮票墨
    为迎接１９９０年北京第十一届亚运会，安徽歙县的徽歙墨厂设计出一套两块别致的邮票墨，上边分别刻有亚运会吉祥物和会徽。该厂同时还设计出套装“日本邮票集锦宝墨”。内装四块刻有日本浮世绘的邮票墨，颜色、装帧、制作精美，既可欣赏收藏，又可馈赠纪念。　　　
　　　　　　　（陆燕）
