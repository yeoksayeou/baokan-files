Title: New Period National Excellent Prose (Collection) and Miscellaneous Prose (Collection) Announced
Author:
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 1

The China Writers Association organized the evaluation of outstanding prose and miscellaneous prose collections of the new period. After serious, earnest, and lively discussions by the committee, the winning entries were determined through secret ballots. There are 7 honorable mentions, 24 outstanding prose collections, and 10 outstanding miscellaneous prose collections.

The following works received the National Excellent Prose and Miscellaneous Prose Collection Honorable Mention:

* "Random Thoughts" (Ba Jin)
* "Summer Yan's Miscellaneous Essays" (Summer Yan)
* "Liao Mi Sha Collection, Volume 2" (Liao Mi Sha)
* "Selected Essays of Ke Ling" (Ke Ling)
* "Selected Essays of Sun Li" (Sun Li)
* "Cloud Dream Reminiscences" (Chen Bai Chen)
* "Memories of Beijing" (Xiao Gan)

The following works received the National Excellent Prose Collection Award (listed in order of votes received):

* "Six Records of the May Seventh Cadres School" (Yang Rong)
* "A Piece of Longing" (Jiang De Ming)
* "Autumn Water" (Yuan Ying)
* "Traces of Love" (Jia Pingwa)
* "Footprints of the Past" (Huang Shang)
* "The Past Is Not Like Smoke" (Huang Qiu Yun)
* "The Pearl Parrot" (Feng Jian Cai)
* "Selected Essays of Yan Wen Jing" (Yan Wen Jing)
* "Arts and Literature Miscellany" (Wu Tai Chang)
* "Tea House in the Water Village" (Yang Yu Yi)
* "Selected Essays of Qin Mai" (Qin Mai)
* "Heart Writing Collection" (Hui Yu Yu)
* "Ding Xiang Jie" (Zong Pu)
* "Kites Hanging on the Tree Tops" (Tian Ye)
* "Wildfire in the Wilderness" (Huang Mo)
* "Green Snow" (Qin Wen Yu)
* "A Female College Student's Diary" (Cao Ming Hua)
* "Di Zi's Diary" (Zeng Zhuo)
* "Jade on the Lotus" (Yi Ming Zhu)
* "Poet's Soul" (Zhao Li Hong)
* "A Nameless Star" (Chen Hui Ying)
* "Fragrant Grass Collection" (Liu Bai Yu)
* "Carefree Tree" (He Gu)
* "Fruit of Fondness" (Xue Er Kang)

The following works received the National Excellent Miscellaneous Prose Collection Award (listed in order of votes received):

* "One Hundred Pieces of Joy and Sorrow" (Shao Yan Xiang)
* "Picking Jade by the Lake" (Mu Hui)
* "Spicy Collection" (Shu Zhan)

* "Each Leading the Fashion for a Few Years" (Chen Xiao Chuan)

### 新时期全国优秀散文（集）杂文（集）评出  巴金《随想录》等７部作品获荣誉奖  ２４部散文（集）１０部杂文（集）榜上有名

1989-03-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　新时期全国优秀散文（集）杂文（集）评出
    巴金《随想录》等７部作品获荣誉奖  ２４部散文（集）１０部杂文（集）榜上有名
    本报讯  中国作家协会主办的新时期全国优秀散文（集）杂文（集）评奖，经过评委会严肃认真热烈的讨论，以无记名投票、确定获奖篇目，其中获荣誉奖７部，优秀散文（集）２４部，优秀杂文（集）１０部。
    获全国优秀散文（集）杂文（集）荣誉奖的有：《随想录》（巴金）、《夏衍杂文随笔集》（夏衍）、《廖沫沙文集·第二卷》（廖沫沙）、《柯灵散文选》（柯灵）、《孙犁散文选》（孙犁）、《云梦断忆》（陈白尘）、《北京城杂忆》（肖乾）。
    获全国优秀散文（集）奖的有：（按得票多少为序）《干校六记》（杨绛）、《相思一片》（姜德明）、《秋水》（袁鹰）、《爱的踪迹》（贾平凹）、《过去的足迹》（黄裳）、《往事并不如烟》（黄秋耘）、《珍珠鸟》（冯骥才）、《严文井散文选》（严文井）、《艺文轶话》（吴泰昌）、《水乡茶居》（杨羽仪）、《秦牧散文选》（秦牧）、《写心集》（惠浴宇）、《丁香结》（宗璞）、《挂在树梢上的风筝》（田野）、《荒野中的地火》（荒煤）、《绿雪》（秦文玉）、《一个女大学生的手记》（曹明华）、《听笛人手记》（曾卓）、《荷上珠小集》（忆明珠）、《诗魂》（赵丽宏）、《无名的星》（陈慧瑛）、《芳草集》（刘白羽）、《无忧树》（和谷）、《留恋果》（薛尔康）。
