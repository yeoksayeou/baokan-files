Title: University Engineering Physics Question Bank Completed
Author: Huang Chuanfang
Date: 1989-03-27
Edition: 2

University Engineering Physics Question Bank System Passes Technical Review

BEIJING, March 26 (Xinhua) — The "University Engineering Physics Course Question Bank System" has recently passed a technical review in Beijing.

The question bank contains 3,388 carefully reviewed questions and is managed using a computer system. It can quickly generate standard test papers for evaluation and self-assessment papers for teaching effectiveness, based on different requirements. It also provides answers, solutions, scoring standards, and other relevant information. This system was jointly developed by six universities, including Tsinghua University and South China University of Technology.

### 高校工科物理试题库建成
黄传芳
1989-03-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　高校工科物理试题库建成
    本报北京３月２６日讯  “高等学校工科大学物理课程试题库系统”日前在京通过技术鉴定。
    试题库储存３３８８道经过严格审查的试题，并用计算机进行科学管理，可根据不同的要求，迅速生成供评估用的标准试卷，以及供自测教学效果用的试卷。同时提供答案、题解、评分标准等有关指标信息。这是由清华大学、华南理工大学等６所高校联合研制的。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（黄传芳）
