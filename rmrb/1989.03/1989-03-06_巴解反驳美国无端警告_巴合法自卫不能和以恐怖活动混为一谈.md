Title: PLO Refutes U.S. Groundless Warning: Palestinian Self-Defense Cannot Be Confused with Terrorism
Author:
Date: 1989-03-06
Edition: 3

The PLO refutes U.S. groundless warning: Palestinian self-defense cannot be confused with terrorism

TUNIS, March 4 (Xinhua) -- The Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) today issued a statement expressing surprise at the warning issued by the U.S. State Department spokesman, Richard Boucher, yesterday, accusing the PLO of attacks in southern Lebanon.

The statement said that Israel attacks southern Lebanon every day, and recently bombed a school south of Beirut, killing at least 20 students. The statement pointed out that the serious situation in southern Lebanon was mentioned in the indirect talks between Palestinian and Israeli leaders to implement the ceasefire agreement in southern Lebanon that took effect in 1982, but Israel has violated this agreement.

The statement criticized the U.S. for putting unilateral pressure on the PLO while Israel continues its aggression and occupation of southern Lebanon. The statement hopes that the U.S. government will not, like the Israelis, confuse the legitimate right of Palestinians to self-defense with terrorist acts that the PLO strongly condemns.

### 巴解反驳美国无端警告  巴合法自卫不能和以恐怖活动混为一谈

1989-03-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　巴解反驳美国无端警告
    巴合法自卫不能和以恐怖活动混为一谈
    新华社突尼斯３月４日电  据巴勒斯坦通讯社报道，巴解组织发言人今天在这里发表一项声明说，巴解组织对美国国务院发言人雷德曼昨天向它发出的声称巴勒斯坦袭击黎巴嫩南部的警告表示惊讶。
    声明说，以色列每天对黎巴嫩南部进行袭击，最近袭击了贝鲁特以南的一所学校，至少造成２０名学生死亡。声明指出，黎巴嫩南部的严重局势在巴勒斯坦领导人同以色列领导人的间接谈判中已提到，以便执行１９８２年生效的黎巴嫩南部的停火协议，但以色列破坏了这项协议。
    声明指责美国在以色列不结束对黎巴嫩南部的侵略和占领的情况下单方面对巴解组织施加压力。声明希望美国政府不要像以色列人那样，把巴勒斯坦人进行自卫的合法权利同巴解组织断然谴责的恐怖行动混为一谈。
