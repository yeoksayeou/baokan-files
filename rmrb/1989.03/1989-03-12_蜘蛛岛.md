Title: Spider Island
Author:
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 7

The Solomon Islands are scattered across the vast South Pacific Ocean, like gleaming pearls scattered on a jade plate. Among these numerous islands, there is a picturesque small island known as Spider Island. The island is covered with large spiders everywhere. The local people use branches to build frames. The spiders work diligently for several days, weaving silver-white, shiny webs on the frames. It is said that these webs can be used to catch fish and are very sturdy and durable.

### 蜘蛛岛

1989-03-12
第7版(国际副刊)
专栏：

　　蜘蛛岛
    所罗门群岛分散在茫茫南太平洋上，宛如撒在翡翠玉盘上的晶莹剔透的珍珠。在这片星罗棋布的海岛中，有一个风景如画的小岛——蜘蛛岛。岛上漫山遍野都是大蜘蛛，当地人用树枝搭起一个个架子。蜘蛛辛勤地劳动几天，就在架子上织好一张张银白色、闪闪发亮的网。据说这种网可用来捕鱼，非常结实耐用。
