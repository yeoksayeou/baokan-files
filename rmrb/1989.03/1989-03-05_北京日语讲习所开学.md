Title: Beijing Japanese Language Training School Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-03-05
Edition: 2

Beijing Japanese Language Training School Opens

	The opening ceremony of the Beijing Japanese Language Training School was held in Beijing yesterday. Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Noboru sent a basket of flowers to express his congratulations.

	One hundred and twenty-five young people who passed the entrance examination attended the ceremony at the Graduate School of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences along with representatives from the Zhou Enlai Memorial Association from Japan. The first session of the training class has set up advanced, intermediate, and beginner classes.

### 北京日语讲习所开学

1989-03-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　北京日语讲习所开学
    本报北京３月４日讯  北京日语讲习所开学典礼昨天在京举行。日本首相竹下登送来花篮以示祝贺。
    经过考试入学的１２５名青年，昨天在中国社会科学院研究生院新闻系同来自日本的周恩来纪念会代表团一起举行了挂牌仪式。第一期讲习班开设高、中、初三个班。
