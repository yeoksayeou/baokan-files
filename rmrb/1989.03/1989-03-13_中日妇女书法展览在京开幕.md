Title: The Second Sino-Japanese Women's Calligraphy Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author: Cai Xianglin
Date: 1989-03-13
Edition: 4

The second Sino-Japanese Women's Calligraphy Exchange Exhibition opened on the morning of March 9 at the National Art Museum of China.

The Japanese delegation of female calligraphers, led by the honorary chairman Mr. Tanaka Toko and vice-chairman Ms. Tanaka Chikuen, both renowned Japanese calligraphers, specially came from Japan to attend the opening ceremony.

This exhibition is jointly organized by the Chinese Calligraphers Association, the Japan Calligraphy Association, and the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association. It features 149 works from the Chinese side and 258 works from the Japanese side. After the exhibition in Beijing, it will travel to Guizhou and Japan.

### 中日妇女书法展览在京开幕
蔡祥麟
1989-03-13
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　中日妇女书法展览在京开幕
    第二次中日妇女书法交流展览３月９日上午在中国美术馆开幕。
    以日本著名书法家田中冻云先生为名誉团长，日本著名女书法家田中竹园为副团长的日本妇女书法家代表团特地从日本赶来参加开幕式。
    此次展览由中国书法家协会、日本书道院、日中文化交流协会主办。共展出中方作品１４９件，日方作品２５８件；在京展出后，将赴贵州、日本展出。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（蔡祥麟）
