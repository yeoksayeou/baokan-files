Title: Civilian Product Value of Military-Industrial Enterprises in Guizhou Exceeds Ten Billion
Author: Pan Di Du
Date: 1989-03-06
Edition: 2

    The military-industrial enterprises in Guizhou Province are now thriving like dragons and tigers emerging from the mountains. Last year, the total civilian product value of the entire system reached 1.26 billion yuan, an increase of 45.3% compared to the previous year.

### 贵州省军工企业民品产值逾十亿
潘帝都
1989-03-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　贵州省军工企业民品产值逾十亿
    本报贵阳３月５日电  记者潘帝都报道：贵州军工企业如今已龙腾虎跃般地出山。去年，全系统民品总产值达１２．６亿元，比上年增长４５．３％。
