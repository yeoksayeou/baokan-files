Title: Liu Shoubin Breaks World Record in 56kg Snatch
Author:
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 1

Chinese athlete Liu Shoubin set a new world record in the 56kg snatch with a lift of 134.5kg on March 1 at the Friendship Cup International Weightlifting Invitational held in Kemerovo, Soviet Union. The previous record was 134kg, held by Chinese athlete He Yingqiang.

During yesterday's competition, Liu Shoubin secured the first place in the 56kg overall standings. Another Chinese athlete, Qiu Yuanfu, won the gold medal in the 52kg overall standings.

Athletes from 11 countries participated in this cup competition.

### 56公斤级抓举134.5公斤  刘寿斌破世界纪录

1989-03-03
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    56公斤级抓举134.5公斤
    刘寿斌破世界纪录
    新华社莫斯科3月2日电　中国运动员刘寿斌3月1日在苏联克麦罗沃举行的友谊杯国际举重邀请赛上，以134.5公斤的成绩，打破了56公斤级抓举的世界纪录。原纪录为134公斤，是中国选手何英强保持的。
    在昨天进行的比赛中，刘寿斌夺得了56公斤级总成绩第一名，另一名中国运动员邱远富获得了52公斤级总成绩冠军。
    有11个国家的运动员参加这次杯赛。
