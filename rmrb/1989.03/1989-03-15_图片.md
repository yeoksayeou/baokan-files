Title: Peru's Fish Meal Production Leads the World
Author: Liu Zhongyang
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 7

In 1988, Peru's fish meal production reached 1.05 million tons, making it the world's largest producer. The image shows a large conveyor belt transferring anchovies from fishing boats to the factory's fish storage area.
