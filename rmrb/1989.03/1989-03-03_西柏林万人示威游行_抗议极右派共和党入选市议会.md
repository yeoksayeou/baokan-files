Title: Mass Protest in West Berlin Against the Far-Right Republican Party's Election to the City Council
Author: Wáng Yí
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 3

On the eve of the West Berlin City Council's meeting, 10,000 people from various sectors of West Berlin braved the rain to hold a protest march yesterday evening, protesting against the far-right Republican Party's election to the City Council.

This protest march was initiated by the West Berlin Trade Union Federation, with participants including members of the Social Democratic Party and the Green Party, among other organizations. West Berlin Social Democratic Party leader Momper and others participated in the march.

### 西柏林万人示威游行  抗议极右派共和党入选市议会
王怡
1989-03-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    西柏林万人示威游行
    抗议极右派共和党入选市议会
    本报柏林3月2日电　在西柏林市议会开会前夕，西柏林各界群众一万人昨晚冒雨举行示威游行，抗议极右派共和党入选市议会。
    这次示威游行是由西柏林工会联合会发起的，参加者包括社会民主党和绿党等组织成员。西柏林社民党领导人莫姆佩尔等参加了游行。（王怡）
