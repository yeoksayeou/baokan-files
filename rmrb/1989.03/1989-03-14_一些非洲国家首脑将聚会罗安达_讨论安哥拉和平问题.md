Title: Some African Heads of State to Meet in Luanda to Discuss Angola's Peace Issue
Author:
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 3

Some African heads of state will gather in Luanda to discuss Angola's peace issue.

	New York, March 12 — According to a report from the Angolan News Agency, Angola's Minister of Justice, Fransa Vandunem, revealed on the 10th that the heads of state of several African countries will soon hold a summit in Luanda, the capital of Angola, to discuss the implementation of the second phase of the Angolan government's plan to resolve domestic issues.

	Vandunem said that the heads of state of Angola, Congo, Gabon, Zaire, Zambia, and Zimbabwe will attend this summit. However, he denied that the South African leader will participate.

	On March 1, Angola's President Dos Santos announced in the Angolan People's Assembly that the Angolan government's second phase peace plan to resolve domestic issues includes seven points: South Africa and the United States stop interfering in Angola's internal affairs, maintain Angola's one-party system, take active political and military measures to end the war, implement amnesty, mobilize all Angolans to participate in national reconstruction, handle the issue of (UNITA leader) Savimbi in a special way, and the international community supports Angola's national reconstruction plan.

### 一些非洲国家首脑将聚会罗安达  讨论安哥拉和平问题

1989-03-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　一些非洲国家首脑将聚会罗安达
    讨论安哥拉和平问题
    新华社罗安达３月１２日电  据安哥拉通讯社报道，安哥拉司法部长弗兰萨·范迪嫩１０日透露，一些非洲国家领导人即将在安哥拉首都罗安达举行首脑会议，讨论实施安哥拉政府制订的解决国内问题的第二阶段计划。
    范迪嫩说，安哥拉、刚果、加蓬、扎伊尔、赞比亚和津巴布韦的国家元首将参加这次首脑会议。但他否认南非首脑将与会。
    安哥拉总统多斯桑托斯３月１日在安哥拉人民议会会议上宣布，安哥拉政府解决国内问题的第二阶段和平计划包括七点内容：南非和美国停止干涉安内政，坚持安哥拉的一党制，为结束战争采取积极的政治和军事措施，实行大赦，动员所有安哥拉人参与国家复兴工作，以特殊方式处理（安盟首领）萨文比的问题，国际社会支持安国家复兴计划。
