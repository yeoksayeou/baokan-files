Title: At the Invitation of the Governments of Five West Asian Countries, Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Leaves Beijing for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 3rd.

At the invitation of the governments of Iran, Turkey, Iraq, Kuwait, and the United Arab Emirates, Vice Premier Tian Jiyun left Beijing this morning for official friendly visits to the aforementioned countries.

Tian Jiyun will hold talks with the leaders of these countries on bilateral relations and economic and trade cooperation.

State Council Secretary-General Luo Gan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Huaiyuan, and the ambassadors of the five countries to China saw him off at the airport.

### 应西亚五国政府邀请  田纪云昨离京出访

1989-03-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　应西亚五国政府邀请
    田纪云昨离京出访
    新华社北京３月３日电  应伊朗、土耳其、伊拉克、科威特和阿拉伯联合酋长国政府的邀请，国务院副总理田纪云今天上午离京对上述国家进行正式友好访问。
    田纪云将同这些国家的领导人就双边关系和经贸合作等问题进行交谈。
    国务院秘书长罗干、外交部副部长齐怀远以及５国驻华使节到机场送行。
