Title: Japanese Injured in the "3·16" Traffic Accident Return Home
Author: Peng Shaoyang
Date: 1989-03-22
Edition: 2

The 12 Japanese tourists injured in the "3·16" bus rollover accident in Jiange County, Sichuan Province, departed from Xi'an by plane this afternoon to return home.

The body of the deceased, Yumi Eiko, a student from Seishin Women's University in Japan, was escorted by her parents, uncle, and sister and flown back to Japan this morning.

### “３·１６”交通事故日方伤员回国
彭少阳
1989-03-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　“３·１６”交通事故日方伤员回国
    新华社西安３月２１日电  （记者彭少阳）在四川省剑阁县发生的“３·１６”翻车事故中受伤的１２名日本游客，今天下午乘飞机离开西安回国。
    这次事故中的死者日本圣心女子大学女学生舆美惠子的遗体，今天上午已由她的父母、叔叔和姐姐护送，乘飞机回国。
