Title: Like a Dream
Author: Liu Shahe, Ding Cong
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 8

Like a Dream

Liu Shahe's Poetry
Ding Cong's Illustration

	Faking drunkenness, I dive into the water to catch the moon and come ashore.
	On the moon's crater, I see a Tang Dynasty scholar's cap waving at me and calling out:
	"Big news! Li Bai speed-learning class!
	Correspondence course for 50 yuan! In-person instruction for 500 yuan!"
