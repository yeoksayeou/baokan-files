Title: Renowned Automobile Engineer Jiang Zemin Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-03-05
Edition: 2

Renowned automobile engineer Jiang Zemin passes away

New China News Agency, Beijing, March 4th: A distinguished member of the Communist Party of China, a loyal and steadfast communist warrior tested by time, former member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and renowned automobile engineer Jiang Zemin passed away due to illness in Beijing on February 11, 1989, at the age of 86.

A farewell ceremony for Jiang Zemin was held today at the Great Hall of the Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery in Babaoshan. Yang Shangkun, Yao Yilin, Song Renqiong, Wu Xiuquan, Duan Jigui, Song Jian, Zhou Jiaxuan, and several hundred friends of Jiang Zemin attended the ceremony. Nie Rongzhen also sent a representative to attend the ceremony.

Leaders who sent wreaths included Deng Xiaoping, Li Peng, Chen Yun, Wan Li, Peng Zhen, Deng Yingchao, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Wang Zhen, Jiang Zemin, Song Ping, Hu Yaobang, Bo Yibo, Yu Quli, Zhang Jinfu, Chen Muhua, Wang Zhaoguo, Fang Yi, Gu Mu, Yang Jingren, Zhou Peiyuan, Ma Wenrui, and Qian Zhengying. The Central Office of the Communist Party, the State Council Office, the Organization Department of the Central Committee, the Office of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Ministry of Machinery and Electronics, the Sichuan Provincial Party Committee and Government, and the Jiangjin County Party Committee and Government sent wreaths. The Soviet Embassy in China also sent a wreath.

Jiang Zemin was born in 1903 in Jiangjin County, Sichuan Province, into a poor family. In 1920, he went to Europe to work and study. During his studies abroad, Jiang Zemin joined the Chinese Socialist Youth League in 1923 and transferred to the Communist Party of China in 1925. From 1926 to 1941, Jiang Zemin worked and studied in the Soviet Union for over a decade, contributing significantly to the socialist construction and development of Sino-Soviet friendship. During the most arduous period of the War of Resistance Against Japan, he returned to Yan'an and served as the Director of the Technical Department and Secretary-General of the Military Industry Bureau of the Central Military Commission, making important contributions to the development of military industry and border region construction.

During the War of Liberation, Jiang Zemin continued to work in the military industry, making significant contributions to supporting the War of Liberation and the construction of national defense industry.

After liberation, Jiang Zemin devoted all his efforts to the construction and development of the automobile industry, becoming one of the founders of China's automobile industry. He held various positions, including Deputy Minister of the First Ministry of Machinery, Member of the Sino-Soviet Technical Cooperation Commission, Deputy General Manager of the Automobile Industry Company of the First Ministry of Machinery, Chairman and Honorary Chairman of the Chinese Society of Mechanical Engineering. During the Cultural Revolution, Jiang Zemin was falsely accused and persecuted, but he upheld the spirit of seeking truth from facts and resolutely fought against the counterrevolutionary actions of the "Gang of Four." In 1975, after resuming work, he served as an advisor to the First Ministry of Machinery and a member of the Fifth and Sixth National Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

In his later years, he continued to care about national affairs, the development and reform of the machinery industry and the automobile industry, and actively proposed suggestions.

### 著名汽车工程专家江泽民逝世

1989-03-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　著名汽车工程专家江泽民逝世
    新华社北京３月４日电  中国共产党优秀党员、久经考验的忠诚的共产主义战士、原全国政协委员、著名汽车工程专家江泽民同志因病医治无效，于１９８９年２月１１日在北京逝世，终年８６岁。
    向江泽民同志遗体告别仪式今天在八宝山革命公墓大礼堂举行。杨尚昆、姚依林、宋任穷、伍修权、段君毅、宋健、邹家华以及江泽民同志生前友好数百人参加了告别仪式。聂荣臻也派专人代表他参加了告别仪式。
    送花圈的领导人有邓小平、李鹏、陈云、万里、彭真、邓颖超、徐向前、聂荣臻、王震、江泽民、宋平、胡耀邦、薄一波、余秋里、张劲夫、陈慕华、王丙乾、方毅、谷牧、杨静仁、周培源、马文瑞、钱正英。中共中央办公厅、国务院办公厅、中共中央组织部、全国政协办公厅、机电工业部、四川省委省政府暨江津县委县政府送了花圈。苏联驻华大使馆也送了花圈。
    江泽民同志１９０３年生于四川省江津县一个贫寒家庭，１９２０年赴欧勤工俭学。在留学期间，江泽民于１９２３年加入中国社会主义青年团、１９２５年转入中国共产党。１９２６年至１９４１年，江泽民先后在苏联工作、学习了十几年，为苏联社会主义建设和发展中苏友谊做了大量的工作。在抗日战争最艰苦的时期，他回到延安，担任中央军委后勤部军事工业局技术处长、秘书长，为发展兵工生产和边区建设做出了重要贡献。
    解放战争时期，江泽民继续在兵工战线工作，为支援解放战争和国防工业建设做了大量工作。
    解放后，江泽民为汽车工业的建设和发展倾注了全部心血，是我国汽车工业的奠基人之一。他先后历任一机部部长助理、中苏技术合作委员会委员、一机部汽车工业公司副总经理、中国机械工程学会理事长及名誉理事长。“文革”期间，江泽民身受诬陷和迫害，但他坚持实事求是精神，旗帜鲜明地同“四人帮”的倒行逆施进行了坚决的斗争。１９７５年，江泽民恢复工作后，任一机部顾问、全国政协第五、第六届委员。
    他在晚年，仍然关心国家大事，关心机械行业和汽车工业的发展与改革，积极提出建议。（附图片）
