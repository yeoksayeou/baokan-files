Title: De Cuellar Meets with Baker
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-03-25
Edition: 4

De Cuellar Meets with Baker

	People's Daily, United Nations, March 23 - Correspondent Liu Kai Chen reports: United Nations Secretary-General Perez de Cuellar met with U.S. Secretary of State James Baker in Washington this morning to discuss a series of international issues. After the meeting, de Cuellar told the press that the talks were "extremely important and beneficial."

	De Cuellar and Baker discussed issues related to the Middle East, Central America, Afghanistan, and the financial problems of the United Nations. Regarding the $494 million in UN dues owed by the United States, de Cuellar said he was pleased that Baker had assured him that he would pay special attention to this issue.

### 德奎利亚尔会晤贝克
刘开宸
1989-03-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　德奎利亚尔会晤贝克
    本报联合国３月２３日电  记者刘开宸报道：联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天上午在华盛顿与美国国务卿贝克就一系列国际问题进行了会谈。德奎利亚尔在会谈后对新闻界表示，这次会谈是“极为重要和有益的”。
    德奎利亚尔与贝克讨论了中东、中美洲、阿富汗和联合国财政问题。关于美国拖欠联合国会费４．９４亿美元，德奎利亚尔说，他高兴的是贝克已保证要特别关心此问题。
