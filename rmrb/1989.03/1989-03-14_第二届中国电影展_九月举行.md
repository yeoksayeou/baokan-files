Title: The Second China Film Festival to be Held in September
Author: Zhong Ying
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 4

	To allow more Chinese films to enter the global film market, the China Film Import and Export Corporation will hold the "Second China Film Festival" on the eve of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China (September 16-22).

	The "First China Film Festival" held in September 1987 sparked a strong response both domestically and internationally, achieving good social and economic benefits. This year's festival will mainly showcase outstanding feature films, documentaries, animated films, and educational films produced since the first festival, totaling more than 50 films. Additionally, 2 to 3 excellent films commemorating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic will be specially screened. The festival will invite film distributors and representatives from the film industry from various countries and regions, including Hong Kong and Taiwan, to participate in various activities.

### “第二届中国电影展”九月举行
钟颖
1989-03-14
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　“第二届中国电影展”九月举行
    本报讯  为使更多的中国电影进入世界电影市场，中国电影输出输入公司将于建国四十周年前夕（９月１６日至２２日）举办“第二届中国电影展”。
    １９８７年９月举行的“第一届中国电影展”在国内外引起了强烈反响，收到了良好的社会效益和经济效益，本届影展将主要展销自上届影展以来生产的优秀故事片、纪录片、美术片、科教片共计５０余部，并将特别献映２至３部纪念建国４０周年的优秀影片，影展将邀请世界各国和港、台地区的影片发行商及电影界代表参加各项活动。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（钟颖）
