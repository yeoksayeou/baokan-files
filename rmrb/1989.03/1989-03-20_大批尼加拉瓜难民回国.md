Title: Large Number of Nicaraguan Refugees Return Home
Author:
Date: 1989-03-20
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Managua, March 18th.

A large number of Nicaraguan refugees have returned home. According to materials provided by the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees, over the past three years, 25,000 Nicaraguans who had fled abroad have returned to their homeland.

In 1988, a total of 8,527 Nicaraguans returned home. Since the beginning of this year, 551 people have already returned. Currently, Nicaragua is the country in Central America with the highest number of returning refugees.

According to unofficial sources, Nicaragua has requested $150 million in aid from the United Nations to assist in the resettlement of returning refugees.

### 大批尼加拉瓜难民回国

1989-03-20
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　大批尼加拉瓜难民回国
    据新华社马那瓜３月１８日电  据联合国难民事务高级专员公署提供的材料，近３年来，已有２．５万名流亡在国外的尼加拉瓜人回到了国内。
    １９８８年，共有８５２７名尼加拉瓜人回国。今年年初以来已有５５１人回国。尼加拉瓜目前是中美洲难民回国人数最多的一个国家。
    据非官方消息说，尼加拉瓜已要求联合国提供１．５亿美元援助，用于安置回国难民。
