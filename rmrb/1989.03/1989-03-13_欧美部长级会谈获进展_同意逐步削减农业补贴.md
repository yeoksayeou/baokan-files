Title: Progress Made in Euro-American Ministerial Talks on Reducing Agricultural Subsidies
Author: Zhuge Canglin
Date: 1989-03-13
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, March 11th. (Reporter Zhuge Canglin) The ministerial-level talks between the European Community and the United States on reducing agricultural subsidies have made progress.

The European Commission's Commissioner for External Relations, Frans Andriessen, and the U.S. President's Special Trade Representative, Carla Hills, said after their two-day meeting that ended on the 11th that the talks would continue within the framework of expert groups.

A spokesman for the European Commission revealed that the U.S. position had softened, agreeing to discuss the implementation of "gradual and irreversible" reductions in agricultural subsidies.

### 欧美部长级会谈获进展  同意逐步削减农业补贴
诸葛仓麟
1989-03-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　欧美部长级会谈获进展  同意逐步削减农业补贴
    据新华社布鲁塞尔３月１１日电  （记者诸葛仓麟）欧洲共同体和美国举行的关于削减农业补贴的部长级会谈取得了进展。
    欧共体委员会负责对外关系的委员弗朗斯·安德里森和美国总统贸易特别代表卡拉·希尔斯女士在两天的会晤在１１日结束后对报界说，会谈将在专家小组范围内继续进行。
    欧共体委员会发言人透露，美国的立场有所松动，同意讨论对农业补贴实行“逐步的和不可逆转的”削减。
