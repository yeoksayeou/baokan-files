Title: Today's Miao Girl
Author: Bai Chun Xiao
Date: 1989-03-13
Edition: 5

Reform and opening up have gradually led the once impoverished and backward Miao people of Yunnan's Wenshan Zhuang and Miao Autonomous Prefecture to a life of prosperity. Look! These two Miao girls are captivated by the dazzling array of goods.

### 今日苗家女（图片）
白春晓
1989-03-13
第5版(国内专页（政治·法律）)

    今日苗家女
    改革开放，使昔日贫穷落后的云南文山壮族苗族自治州的苗族群众生活逐步走向富裕。瞧！这两个苗家少女在五光十色的商品面前流连忘返。
