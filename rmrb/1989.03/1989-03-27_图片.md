Title: U.S. Successfully Launches "Star Wars" Satellite
Author:
Date: 1989-03-27
Edition: 4

On March 24, the U.S. military successfully launched a man-made satellite from the Cape Canaveral Space Center in Florida. The satellite is intended for "Star Wars" experiments and carries a laser radar, seven cameras, and an infrared image analysis device.
