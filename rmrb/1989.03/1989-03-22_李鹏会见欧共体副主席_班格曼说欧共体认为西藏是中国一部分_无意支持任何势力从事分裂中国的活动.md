Title: Li Peng Meets with Vice-President of the European Community Bangemann
Author: Zhou Shuchun
Date: 1989-03-22
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 21st.

Premier Li Peng today met with Vice-President of the European Community Commission Martin Bangemann. Li Peng said that China hopes that the friendly cooperative relationship between China and the European Community countries can develop long-term and amicably.

Li Peng noted that in recent years, China's economic and technological relations with the European Community countries have developed further, and both sides have reached agreements on some major cooperation projects and are implementing these projects.

When discussing the Tibet issue, Li Peng introduced Tibet's history, current situation, and the Chinese government's consistent policy on this issue. He said, "We respect the Tibetan people's national sentiments, religious beliefs, and living habits," hoping that all ethnic groups, including the Tibetan people, can live harmoniously within the Chinese nation's big family and develop together.

Bangemann said that the European Community and its member countries' governments are very familiar with the Chinese government's stance on the Tibet issue and the efforts and achievements made by China in building Tibet.

He said, "The European Community and its member countries all consider Tibet to be a part of Chinese territory. 'We have no intention of supporting any political or religious forces in their activities to split China.'"

### 李鹏会见欧共体副主席  班格曼说欧共体认为西藏是中国一部分  无意支持任何势力从事分裂中国的活动
周树春
1989-03-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏会见欧共体副主席        
    班格曼说欧共体认为西藏是中国一部分        
    无意支持任何势力从事分裂中国的活动
    新华社北京３月２１日电  （记者周树春）国务院总理李鹏今天在会见欧洲共同体委员会副主席马丁·班格曼时说，中国希望与欧共体国家之间的友好合作关系能够长期、友好地发展下去。
    李鹏总理说，近年来中国与欧共体各国的经济技术关系有进一步发展，双方就一些重大合作项目达成了协议，并正在实施这些项目。
    在谈到西藏问题时，李鹏介绍了西藏的历史、现状以及中国政府对这一问题的一贯政策。他说，“我们尊重藏族人民的民族感情、宗教信仰和生活习惯”，希望包括藏族人民在内的各族人民在中华民族的大家庭中和睦相处，共同发展。
    班格曼表示，欧洲共同体及其成员国政府十分了解中国政府在西藏问题上的立场及其为建设西藏所做出的努力和取得的成就。
    他说，欧共体及其成员国都认为，西藏是中国领土的一部分，“我们无意支持任何政治或宗教势力从事分裂中国的活动。”
