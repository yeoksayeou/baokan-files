Title: Montazeri No Longer to Succeed Khomeini
Author:
Date: 1989-03-30
Edition: 1

Montazeri No Longer to Succeed Khomeini

According to Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, March 28: Iran's religious leader Khomeini today accepted Montazeri's request to no longer be his successor.

According to Tehran Radio, Montazeri made this statement in a letter to Khomeini on March 26, and at the same time, he thanked Khomeini for his guidance.

The Iranian government has not yet disclosed what mistake Montazeri made. However, the differences between him and Khomeini have been publicly known since February 11. At that time, Montazeri publicly criticized Iran's policy mistakes over the past 10 years.

### 蒙塔泽里不再当霍梅尼接班人

1989-03-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　蒙塔泽里不再当霍梅尼接班人
    据新华社德黑兰３月２８日电  伊朗宗教领袖霍梅尼今天接受了蒙塔泽里提出的，不再当霍梅尼的接班人的请求。
    据德黑兰电台广播，蒙塔泽里是２６日在给霍梅尼的一封信中作上述表示的，同时他还感谢霍梅尼对他的教导。
    伊朗官方迄今未透露蒙塔泽里犯了什么错误。但他同霍梅尼之间的分歧自２月１１日以来已公开化。当时，蒙塔泽里公开批评了伊朗在过去１０年里的政策失误。
