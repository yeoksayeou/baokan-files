Title: Deputy Manager of a Henan Oilfield Company Illegally Trades Gold Jewelry
Author: Yang Yongde Chen Baozhong
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 4

The People's Procuratorate of Anyang County, Henan Province, in collaboration with the Henan Oilfield Exploration Bureau, recently cracked a case of armed gold jewelry trafficking.

In September 1988, Fang Yulin, the deputy manager of the Henan Oilfield Public Security Bureau's Multi-Business Company, colluded with Deng Xinghe, an unemployed individual from Yun County, Hubei Province. Under the pretense of organizing gold jewelry supplies for the Henan Oilfield Industrial and Commercial Bank, they carried out armed trafficking of gold jewelry. From October 3 to 26, 1988, Fang Yulin and Deng Xinghe, along with others, carried bank drafts totaling 1.35 million yuan. They conspired with some lawless elements in Shenzhen to purchase and resell gold jewelry weighing 11,250 grams from owners Cai and Shi, among others. In this case, the criminal Fang Yulin obtained 257.17 grams of gold jewelry, worth over 30,000 yuan. The case is currently under trial.

### 河南油田某公司副经理倒卖黄金饰品
杨永德　陈保栓
1989-03-12
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：

　　河南油田某公司副经理倒卖黄金饰品
    本报讯  河南省安阳县人民检察院会同河南油田勘探局，最近破获一起持枪倒卖黄金饰品案。
    １９８８年９月，河南油田公安局多种经营公司副经理方玉林勾结湖北郧县无业人员邓兴合，以替河南油田工商银行组织黄金饰品货源为由，持枪武装押运，进行倒买倒卖黄金饰品犯罪活动。１９８８年１０月３日至２６日，方玉林、邓兴合等人自带汇票１３５万元，勾结深圳一些不法分子，从货主蔡某、施某等人手中倒买倒卖黄金饰品１１２５０克，其中案犯方玉林获黄金饰品２５７．１７克，合人民币３万余元。目前，此案正在审理中。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（杨永德　陈保栓）
