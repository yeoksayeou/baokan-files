Title: Recent Announcement of the Science and Life "Zhāng Guāng Cup" Fashion Design Competition
Author: Mèng Rén Quán
Date: 1989-03-06
Edition: 4

Recently, the winners of the Science and Life "Zhāng Guāng Cup" Fashion Design Competition were announced, selecting over ninety sets of fashionable clothing that are both innovative and aesthetically pleasing, suitable for everyday wear. The image shows some of these designs. Photographed by People's Daily reporter Mèng Rén Quán.
