Title: Management Regulations for the Import and Export of Goods in Coastal Open Areas Issued by the General Administration of Customs
Author: Zhang Yi
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 1

To promote the development of coastal open cities and economic open areas, the General Administration of Customs has issued the "Regulations of the Customs of the People's Republic of China on the Management of Goods Entering and Leaving Coastal Open Areas," which will come into effect on April 1, 1989.

The main contents of these regulations include: the management methods for the import and export of goods with reduced or exempted taxes by foreign trade enterprises and production enterprises engaged in import and export business within the open cities and areas; the preferential treatments enjoyed by units or enterprises in the open cities and areas for importing and exporting certain goods; the use of goods imported with reduced or exempted taxes, etc.

### 沿海开放区货物进出境  海关总署颁布管理规定
张毅
1989-03-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　沿海开放区货物进出境
    海关总署颁布管理规定
    新华社北京３月９日电  （记者张毅）为了促进沿海开放城市和沿海经济开放区的发展，海关总署颁布了《中华人民共和国海关对沿海开放地区进出境货物的管理规定》，自１９８９年４月１日起实施。
    这一规定的主要内容包括：开放市、区内从事进出口业务的外贸企业、生产企业减免税进出口货物的管理办法；开放市、区的单位或企业进出口某些货物所享受的优惠；减免税进口物资的使用等等。
