Title: Famous Bridge Tournament Held in Beijing
Author: Xiāo Lín
Date: 1989-03-27
Edition: 3

The Chutouquan Cup Famous Bridge Tournament was held in Beijing on March 26. Lü Zhengcao and Li Xiaolin each won the North-South group championship, while Liu Xue and three other journalists won the East-West group championship.

### 名人桥牌赛在京举行
筱林
1989-03-27
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　名人桥牌赛在京举行
    本报讯  趵突泉杯名人桥牌赛三月二十六日在北京举行，吕正操、李晓林等四人分别获得南北组冠军，刘雪等四位新闻界人士获得东西组冠军。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（筱林）
