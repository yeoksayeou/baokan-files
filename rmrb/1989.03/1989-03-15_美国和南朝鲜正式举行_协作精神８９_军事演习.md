Title: U.S. and South Korea Launch "Team Spirit 89" Military Exercise
Author: XU Baokang
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 14th.

The United States and South Korea have mobilized 200,000 troops to formally begin the "Team Spirit 89" joint military exercise in South Korea early this morning. The exercise involves the U.S. 7th Fleet's "Midway" aircraft carrier, as well as a large number of warships, transport ships, landing craft, and various military aircraft. The participating units include the U.S. 25th Infantry Division and the U.S. Marine Corps 3rd Division.

North Korea has strongly protested to the U.S. and South Korean sides over this. The Korean People's Army has also been put on high alert to deal with any potential emergencies that may arise.

### 美国和南朝鲜正式举行“协作精神８９”军事演习
徐宝康
1989-03-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国和南朝鲜正式举行“协作精神８９”军事演习
    本报平壤３月１４日电  记者徐宝康报道：据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，美国和南朝鲜动员２０万兵力，今天凌晨在南朝鲜正式开始举行“协作精神８９”联合军事演习。
    参加这一演习的有美第７舰队的“中途岛”号航空母舰及大批战舰、运输舰、登陆艇和各种军用飞机。投入演习的部队包括美军第２５步兵师、美海军第三陆战师等。
    朝鲜北方对此已向美国和南朝鲜方面提出强烈抗议。朝鲜人民军也已进入高度戒备状态，以应付可能出现的各种突发事件。
