Title: White Poplar
Author: Zhang Xinquan
Date: 1989-03-06
Edition: 8

    White Poplar
    Zhang Xinquan
    A cluster of small hands, green and swaying
    Under the Flaming Mountains
    In a small town with many donkeys and grape arbors
    As if waiting for many years, many years
    —The procession of small white poplars
    Has grown into a grove
    The qanats no longer steam
    The alluring earthy scent
    After waiting for many years, many years
    The small hands finally raise
    To the most dashing angle
    Here, there, towards our car
    They wave with the pure emotion of green leaves
    This is a dance, a heart song
    On both sides of the dirt road
    Behind the low walls
    Accompanied by the clopping of donkey hooves
    And the beating of hand drums
    The hands are a bit dirty
    But not excessively so
    Together with those dirty little eyes and little noses
    They form the most enchanting scenery on the desert
    The small hands are growing, sprouting
    Painting the boundless wilderness
    Into a screen of color
    On the vast and desolate background
    I also forget myself and raise
    My palms; every tear of mine
    Responds in kind—
    Ah! The future; ah! Kindred spirit

### 白杨
张新泉
1989-03-06
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　白杨
    张新泉
    一片小手，翠绿地摇着
    在火焰山下
    在一个多毛驴多葡萄架的小镇
    像是等待了许多年许多年
    ——小白杨的仪仗
    已长成林带
    坎儿井不再蒸腾
    撩人的土腥
    等待了许多年许多年
    小手们终于举向
    一个最潇洒的角度
    这里，那里，朝我们的汽车
    摇响绿叶般的纯情
    这是一种舞蹈，一种心歌
    在土路两侧
    在矮墙后面
    伴着驴蹄嗒嗒
    手鼓声声
    手有些儿脏
    但并不过份
    和那些脏兮兮的小眼睛小鼻子
    组成戈壁滩上
    最迷人的风景……
    小手在拔节、生长
    把无边的漠野
    彩绘成一方荧屏
    在大荒凉大寂寞的背景上
    我也忘情地升起
    我的手掌；我的每一滴泪
    都在应答——
    啊！未来；啊！知音
