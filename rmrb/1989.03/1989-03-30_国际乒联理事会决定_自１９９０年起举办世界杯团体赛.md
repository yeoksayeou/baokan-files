Title: The International Table Tennis Federation Council Decides to Hold the World Cup Team Competition Starting from 1990
Author: Jiang Jian Guo
Date: 1989-03-30
Edition: 3

The International Table Tennis Federation Council decided today in Dortmund that starting from 1990, a World Cup team competition will be held every two years. The competition will include 16 men's teams and 12 women's teams. The men's teams will include the champions from Asia, Africa, Latin America, North America, Europe, and Oceania, totaling 6 teams. The host country's team, the top 7 teams from the previous World Championships, and two additional teams invited by the International Table Tennis Federation will also participate. The women's teams will be selected in a similar manner. Each team will consist of 3 athletes, and the competition will include 4 singles matches and 2 doubles matches, using a best-of-three format. The competition will be divided into two stages.

The venue for the first competition has not yet been decided, but it is known that the English Table Tennis Association is interested in hosting it.

### 国际乒联理事会决定  自１９９０年起举办世界杯团体赛
江建国
1989-03-30
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　国际乒联理事会决定        
    自１９９０年起举办世界杯团体赛
    本报多特蒙德３月２９日电  记者江建国报道：国际乒联理事会今天上午在多特蒙德决定：自１９９０年起每两年举办一次世界杯团体赛。参加这个团体赛的将有１６个男队和１２个女队。男队中包括亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲、北美洲、欧洲、大洋洲的总共６个冠军队。东道国队、上届世界锦标赛名次靠前的７个队，另外，国际乒联再邀请两个队。女队参加者产生办法类似。每个队由３个运动员组成，共进行４场单打、两场双打，采用三局两胜制，比赛分两阶段进行。
    首届比赛地点尚未决定，据悉英格兰乒协有意承办。
