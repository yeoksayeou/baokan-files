Title: Hong Kong Folk Music Sisters Perform Erhu in Beijing
Author: Xiao Qi
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 4

Sisters from Hong Kong, Xin Xiaohong and Xin Xiaoling, recently performed the erhu at the Beijing Concert Hall. They played pieces such as "Fantasy," "When Will the Moon Be Bright," and "Song of the Ussuri," receiving a warm welcome from the audience.

### 香港民乐姐妹在京奏二胡
晓奇
1989-03-03
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

    香港民乐姐妹在京奏二胡
    来自香港的民乐姊妹辛小红、辛小玲日前在北京音乐厅演奏二胡，表演了《幻》、《明月几时有》、《乌苏里吟》等作品，受到观众热烈欢迎。
                                        （晓奇）
