Title: EC Vice-President Reiterates Support for Restoring China's Contracting Party Status in GATT
Author: Zhuge Canglin
Date: 1989-03-05
Edition: 1

The European Community Vice-President today reiterated support for restoring China's contracting party status in the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (GATT). According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing and the European Community held consultations in Brussels on March 3 on the issue of restoring China's contracting party status in GATT.

A Chinese delegation member said that the consultations were conducted in a "very friendly and constructive atmosphere."

Chinese Vice-Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Shen Jueren and EC Vice-President Frans Andriessen exchanged views after high-level officials from both sides held consultations.

Shen Jueren reviewed the progress made in recent years in China-EC relations, trade, investment, and technology transfer, and emphasized that China attaches great importance to developing friendly cooperative relations with the EC in bilateral and multilateral fields. Andriessen reiterated the EC's "full support" for restoring China's contracting party status in GATT and adopted an open attitude towards resolving related issues.

Shen Jueren reiterated China's principled position on Taiwan's demand to join GATT.

Vice-Minister Shen Jueren also exchanged views with senior officials of the Belgian Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Trade on the development of bilateral economic and trade relations.

### 欧共体副主席重申  支持恢复我关贸总协定缔约国地位
诸葛仓麟
1989-03-05
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　欧共体副主席重申
    支持恢复我关贸总协定缔约国地位
    据新华社布鲁塞尔３月３日电  （记者诸葛仓麟）中国政府和欧洲共同体今天在这里就恢复中国在关税及贸易总协定缔约国地位问题进行了磋商。
    中国代表团人士说，这次磋商是在“十分友好和富有建设性的气氛中”进行的。
    中国经贸部副部长沈觉人和欧共体委员会副主席、对外关系负责人弗朗斯·安德里森在双方高级官员磋商后单独交换了意见。
    沈觉人回顾了近年来中国和欧共体以及贸易、投资和技术转让等领域取得的进展后强调，中国重视发展和欧共体在双边和多边领域的友好合作关系。安德里森重申欧共体“完全支持”恢复中国在关贸总协定的缔约国地位，并对解决与此有关的问题采取开放的态度。
    沈觉人在谈话中重申了对台湾要求参加关贸总协定的原则立场。
    沈觉人副部长今天上午还和比利时对外关系和外贸部高级官员就发展中比双边经贸关系问题交换了意见。
