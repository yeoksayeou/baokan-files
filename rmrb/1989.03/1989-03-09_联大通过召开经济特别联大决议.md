Title: UN General Assembly Passes Resolution to Convene Economic Special Session
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-03-09
Edition: 3

The UN General Assembly today decided to convene a "Special Session to Discuss International Economic Cooperation, Especially the Economic Growth and Development of Developing Countries" in April 1990 at the United Nations headquarters.

The Assembly urged member states to send the highest-level representatives possible to attend the special session.

The Assembly decided to establish a preparatory committee in March to make necessary arrangements for the special session, including drafting the "Final Document."

### 联大通过召开经济特别联大决议
刘开宸
1989-03-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　联大通过召开经济特别联大决议
    本报联合国３月７日电  记者刘开宸报道：第４３届联合国大会今天决定于１９９０年４月在联合国总部召开“专门讨论国际经济合作，特别是发展中国家经济增长和发展的特别会议”。
    大会要求各成员国尽可能派最高级别人员出席特别会议。
    大会决定在３月间建立筹备委员会，为特别会议作必要安排，包括提出“最后文件”草案。
