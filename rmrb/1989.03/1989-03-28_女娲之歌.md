Title: The Song of Nüwa
Author: Wei Minglun
Date: 1989-03-28
Edition: 8

The Song of Nüwa

    A baby cries out—Mama!
    A traveler calls a thousand times—Mama!
    The Western Mama—Eve!
    The Eastern Mama—Nüwa!
    Standing with two peaks of white jade,
    Wearing a double cascade of black cloud hair,
    With a radiant, healthy figure,
    Adorned with a few blooming wildflowers.
    Free and uninhibited,
    Naked and unburdened.
    Gathering the essence of mountains and rivers,
    Absorbing the essence of the sun and moon,
    Scattering seeds of love,
    Planting roots of affection;
    Scattering them across China,
    Planting them to the ends of the earth…
    Saddened by the world's countless disasters,
    Worried by the gods' endless killings,
    Extinguishing the raging fires of war on earth,
    Mending the countless wounds of the sky.
    Creating and developing,
    Nurturing the roses of peace.
    Guiding the Yellow River's nine bends,
    Weaving the five-colored clouds.
    Ah!
    We are the descendants of Nüwa!
    We are the modern-day Nüwa!
    Calling for humanity,
    Revitalizing China.

### 女娲之歌
魏明伦
1989-03-28
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　女娲之歌
    婴儿一声啼——妈妈！
    游子千声唤——妈妈！
    西方的妈妈——夏娃！
    东方的妈妈——女娲！
    挺两座白玉高峰，
    披双肩乌云长发，
    亮一身健美曲线，
    插几朵怒放野花。
    自由、潇洒，
    赤条条来往无牵挂。
    采山川灵秀，
    吸日月精华，
    撒爱的种子，
    播情的根芽；
    撒遍华夏，
    播向天涯……
    悲人间重重灾难，
    忧众神纷纷残杀，
    灭大地熊熊战火，
    补苍天累累伤疤。
    创造、开发，
    浇灌和平玫瑰花。
    导九曲黄河，
    织五彩云霞。
    啊！
    我们是女娲后代！
    我们是当代女娲！
    呼唤人性，
    振兴中华。
