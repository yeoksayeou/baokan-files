Title: Vietnamese Planning Commission Head Replaced
Author:
Date: 1989-03-05
Edition: 3

Vietnamese Planning Commission Head Replaced

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 4 — According to local newspapers today, the Vietnamese Council of State announced the appointment of former Chairman of the Ho Chi Minh City People's Committee, Phan Van Khai, as the head of the State Planning Commission.

    Phan Van Khai is a member of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Communist Party and is considered a rising political star in Vietnam. It is said that he has close ties with Vietnam's top leader, Nguyen Van Linh, and Deputy Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet.

    At the same time, former head of the State Planning Commission, Du Tam Truong, and former head of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations, Vu Dong Giang, were appointed as deputy heads of the newly established State Cooperation and Investment Commission. It is said that this commission will be led by Prime Minister Do Muoi.

### 越南计委主任易人

1989-03-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　越南计委主任易人
    新华社河内３月４日电  据此间报纸今天报道，越南国务委员会宣布，任命前胡志明市人民委员会主席潘文凯为国家计划委员会主任。
    潘文凯是越南共产党中央委员，被认为是越南正在升起的一颗政治明星。据说他同越南最高领导人阮文灵和部长会议副主席武文杰关系密切。
    与此同时，前计划委员会主任窦玉春和前国家对外经济关系委员会主任武东江被任命为新成立的国家合作与投资委员会副主任。据说这个委员会将由部长会议主席杜梅领导。
