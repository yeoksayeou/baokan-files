Title: Newly Elected Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference: Hou Jianru's Biography
Author:
Date: 1989-03-28
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency

	Hou Jianru, born in October 1902 in Yongcheng, Henan; graduated from the first class of the Whampoa Military Academy; member of the China National Democratic Construction Association. He currently serves as the vice chairman of the Central Committee of the China National Democratic Construction Association, president of the Whampoa Alumni Association, and president of the China Council for the Promotion of Peaceful National Reunification. He is also a member of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

	In 1925, he participated in the First and Second Eastern Expeditions. In 1926, he participated in the Northern Expedition, serving as the political representative and director of the political department of the 3rd Division of the 17th Army of the National Revolutionary Army. In 1927, he participated in the Third Armed Uprising in Shanghai and served as the commander of the 20th Army's training regiment under He Long. He also participated in the Nanchang Uprising. After 1932, he successively held the positions of chief of staff of the 30th Division, commander of the 89th Brigade, commander of the 21st Division of the 92nd Army, commander of the 92nd Army, and commander of the 17th Army Group and Tianjin Tanggu Defense Commander. He also served as the deputy director of the Fujian Pacification Headquarters. After 1949, he held the positions of counselor of the State Council, member of the National Defense Council, vice chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, vice chairman of the Standing Committee of the Beijing Municipal People's Congress, and chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the China National Democratic Construction Association. He was also a member of the Second, Third, and Fourth National Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and a member of the Standing Committee of the Fifth and Sixth National Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 新增选的全国政协副主席  侯镜如简历

1989-03-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　新增选的全国政协副主席        
    侯镜如简历
    侯镜如，１９０２年１０月生，河南永城人；毕业于黄埔军校第一期；中国国民党革命委员会成员。他现任民革中央副主席，黄埔同学会会长，中国和平统一促进会会长，第七届全国政协常委。
    他１９２５年参加第一、二次东征；１９２６年参加北伐，任国民革命军第十七军第三师党代表兼政治部主任；１９２７年参加上海第三次武装起义，并任贺龙二十军教导团团长，参加南昌起义；１９３２年后，历任国民党军队第三十师参谋长、八十九旅旅长、九十二军二十一师师长、九十二军军长、十七兵团司令兼天津塘沽防守司令，福州绥靖公署副主任。１９４９年后，他历任国务院参事，国防委员会委员，北京市政协副主席、市人大常委会副主任，民革北京市主委等职。他还是第二、三、四届全国政协委员、第五、六届全国政协常委。　（新华社）（附图片）
