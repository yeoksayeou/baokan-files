Title: Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Calls on Iranian Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-03-06
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, March 4th.

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun today called on Iranian Prime Minister Mir-Hossein Mousavi. The two sides exchanged views on economic cooperation between the two countries and international issues of common concern.

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun expressed appreciation for Iran's active cooperation with the United Nations Secretary-General in resolving the Iran-Iraq conflict and hoped that the two countries would continue their dialogue.

In discussing the Afghan issue, Iranian Prime Minister Mousavi said that Iran would strive to persuade the Afghan Mujahideen to unite and establish a truly non-aligned government, preventing Afghanistan from becoming a second Lebanon.

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun also held talks with Iranian Vice Premier Hamid Mirzadeh on economic and technological cooperation between the two countries and met with Iranian Foreign Minister Ali Akbar Velayati.

	March 4th, Chinese Vice Premier Tian Jiyun meets with Iranian Foreign Minister Ali Akbar Velayati in Tehran.

### 田纪云副总理拜会伊朗总理

1989-03-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　田纪云副总理拜会伊朗总理
    新华社德黑兰３月４日电  国务院副总理田纪云今天在这里拜会了伊朗总理穆萨维，双方就两国经济合作和共同关心的国际问题交换了意见。
    田纪云副总理对伊朗同联合国秘书长积极合作解决两伊争端表示赞赏，并希望两伊继续对话。
    伊朗总理穆萨维在谈到阿富汗问题时说，伊朗将努力劝说阿富汗游击队团结起来，建立一个真正的不结盟的政权，防止阿富汗变成第二个黎巴嫩。
    田纪云副总理今天还同伊朗副总理哈米德·米尔·扎德就两国经济技术合作等问题举行了会谈，并会见了伊朗外交部长韦拉亚提。
    （附图片）
    ３月４日，中国副总理田纪云与伊朗外长韦拉亚提在德黑兰会晤。
