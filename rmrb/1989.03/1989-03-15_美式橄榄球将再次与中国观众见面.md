Title: American Football to Meet Chinese Audience Again
Author: Xiaowu
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 4

Since 1986, when CCTV broadcasted the live coverage of the American Super Bowl football game, Chinese audiences have begun to learn about and understand this sport, often referred to as a "barbaric" athletic event. On March 19th, CCTV will broadcast the live coverage of this year's Super Bowl. This is a collaborative effort organized by CCTV, the American International Language Company, and the American Wilson Company.

### 美式橄榄球将再次与中国观众见面
晓武
1989-03-15
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　美式橄榄球将再次与中国观众见面
    本报讯  自１９８６年中央电视台播放了美国超级杯橄榄球大赛的实况以来，中国观众开始对这项被称为野蛮的体育运动有所了解和认识。３月１９日中央电视台将播出今年超级杯的赛况。这是中央电视台、美国国际传语公司和美国威尔逊公司等三家协作主办的。　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　（晓武）
