Title: Art and Calligraphy Exhibition for the Disabled Opens
Author: Wú Kūnshèng
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 4

The art and calligraphy exhibition "For the Cause of the Disabled" sponsored by the China Welfare Fund for the Disabled opened on February 28 at the National Art Museum of China in Beijing. Comrades Saifuddin Azizi, Fang Yi, Zhao Puxu, Huang Zhen, and Deng Pufang attended the opening ceremony.

### “为残疾人事业捐赠书画联展”开幕
吴坤胜
1989-03-03
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

    “为残疾人事业捐赠书画联展”开幕
    由中国残疾人福利基金会主办的“为残疾人事业捐赠书画联展”2月28日在北京中国美术馆开幕。赛福鼎·艾则孜、方毅、赵朴初、黄镇和邓朴方等同志出席开幕式。（吴坤胜）
