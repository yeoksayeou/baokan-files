Title: Brazilian Ruling Party Holds Eighth Congress
Author: Lan Caiji
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 3

The eighth national congress of the Brazilian ruling party, the Democratic Movement Party, concluded last night after a three-day meeting. The congress elected new leadership members and re-elected Ulisses Guimarães to continue as the party's chairman.

### 巴西执政党举行八大
兰才基
1989-03-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　巴西执政党举行八大
    本报巴西利亚３月１３日电  记者兰才基报道：巴西执政党民主运动党举行的第８次全国代表大会，经过３天会议之后于昨天晚上闭幕，会议选举了该党新的领导成员并推举乌利塞斯·吉马良斯继续担任党的主席。
