Title: Liu Wei Breaks National Speed Skating Record Again
Author: Hu Hao, Wang Xuezhi
Date: 1989-03-19
Edition: 3

The 23-year-old athlete Liu Wei from the People's Liberation Army team broke the national record for the men's 1000 meters speed skating yesterday during the 1989 National Speed Skating Championships held in Changchun. Today, he again broke the national record with a total score of 156.100, surpassing the 157.990 record set by Jilin athlete Pan Dianzhong in 1985.

### 刘伟再破速滑全国纪录
胡浩　王学志
1989-03-19
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　刘伟再破速滑全国纪录
    新华社长春３月１６日电  （记者胡浩、通讯员王学志）解放军队２３岁的选手刘伟在长春举行的１９８９年全国速度滑冰冠军赛上，继昨天打破男子１０００米速滑全国纪录后，今天又以１５６．１００分的总成绩打破了吉林选手潘殿中１９８５年创造的１５７．９９０分的全国纪录。
