Title: Capital University Students Discuss News Reform
Author:
Date: 1989-03-25
Edition: 2

Today, more than 30 student representatives from over 10 institutions, including Peking University, Tsinghua University, and Renmin University of China, were invited to the People's Daily headquarters. They discussed news reform and how to improve the People's Daily with the newspaper's deputy editor-in-chief, Li Renshen, as well as editors and reporters.

During the discussion, the students eagerly shared their views on the current state of China's news industry and the post-reform People's Daily, offering valuable suggestions and opinions. The students noted that the People's Daily has become more readable since its reform and expressed the hope that it will continue to better serve readers and reflect everyday life.

### 首都大学生座谈新闻改革

1989-03-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　首都大学生座谈新闻改革
    本报北京３月２４日讯  今天，北京大学、清华大学、中国人民大学等１０余所院校的３０多名学生代表，应邀来到人民日报社，就新闻改革及如何办好《人民日报》等问题，与本报副总编辑李仁臣以及编辑、记者举行了座谈。
    座谈会上，同学们争相发言，就当前中国新闻界的现状和改版后的《人民日报》，提出了有益的意见和建议。同学们认为《人民日报》改版后可读性增强了，希望今后更加贴近读者，贴近生活。
