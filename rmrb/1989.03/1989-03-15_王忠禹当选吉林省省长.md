Title: Wang Zhongyu Elected Governor of Jilin Province
Author: Li Anda
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 1

Wang Zhongyu was elected governor of Jilin Province at the closing of the second session of the seventh Jilin Provincial People's Congress today. The People's Daily reported from Changchun on March 14: Wang Zhongyu was elected as the governor of Jilin Province.

### 王忠禹当选吉林省省长
李安达
1989-03-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　王忠禹当选吉林省省长
    本报长春３月１４日电  记者李安达报道：在今天闭幕的吉林省第七届人民代表大会第二次会议上，王忠禹当选为吉林省省长。
