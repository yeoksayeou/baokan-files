Title: The China Public Sports Venue Association Established
Author:
Date: 1989-03-28
Edition: 3

The founding conference of the China Public Sports Venue Association was held in Beijing from March 24 to 25, 1989. Representatives from 52 sports venues across the country gathered to discuss the development and construction of sports venues.

Chairman Li Menghua of the All-China Sports Federation and Vice Minister He Zhenliang of the State Sports Commission attended the event to offer their congratulations.

### 中国公共体育场馆协会成立

1989-03-28
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　中国公共体育场馆协会成立
    本报讯  中国公共体育场馆协会成立大会于一九八九年三月二十四日至二十五日在北京举行；全国五十二个体育场馆的代表欢聚一堂，共商体育场馆建设发展大计。
    中华全国体育总会主席李梦华、国家体委副主任何振梁到会祝贺。
