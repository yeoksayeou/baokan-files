Title: Prime Minister Takeshita Reiterates Exclusive Defense and Emphasizes Improving Defense Quality
Author:
Date: 1989-03-21
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 19th.

Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Noboru today stated that while maintaining the Japan-U.S. Security Treaty, Japan should "rely on its own strength to defend its own country."

According to Kyodo News, Takeshita made these remarks at the graduation ceremony of the 33rd class of the National Defense Academy of Japan. Takeshita said that Japan has become an important member of the international community and should actively use its financial resources for world peace and prosperity, contributing to the world.

Takeshita said that by adhering to the basic defense policies of the Constitution of Japan and exclusive defense, Japan is striving to build a high-quality, efficient, and practical defense force. This will contribute to maintaining the military balance between the East and West and to the peace and stability of Asia and the world.

### 竹下重申专守防卫  强调提高防卫质量

1989-03-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　竹下重申专守防卫        
    强调提高防卫质量
    新华社东京３月１９日电  日本首相竹下登今天表示，日本在维持日美安全条约的同时，要“依靠自己的力量保卫自己的国家”。
    据共同社报道，竹下是在日本防卫大学第３３期毕业式上讲这番话的。竹下说，日本已成为国际社会的重要成员，日本应将自己的财力积极用于世界的和平与繁荣，为世界做出贡献。
    竹下说，遵守日本国宪法和专守防卫等基本防卫政策，日本努力建立高质量、高效率和切实的防卫力量，将对保持东西方阵营的军事平衡和亚洲与世界和平与稳定做出贡献。
