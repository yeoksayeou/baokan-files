Title: Spirits Soaring, Artistic Genius Astonishing: The National Disabled Persons' Art Festival Closes
Author: Lai Renqiong
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 2

Spirits Soaring, Artistic Genius Astonishing
The National Disabled Persons' Art Festival Closes

BEIJING, March 11 (Xinhua) — The second National Disabled Persons' Art Festival concluded tonight. Hu Qili, Rui Xiongwen, and Saifuding Aizizi attended the closing ceremony and watched the performance.

This festival is the largest and highest-level disabled persons' art event since the founding of the People's Republic of China. Over 200 performers, filled with passion, showcased the spirited and progressive demeanor of China's disabled persons and their astonishing artistic talents.

The festival awarded first prizes to 10 performances, including the solo song "Ode to the Motherland" and the modern dance "Eagle Disco," and 6 first prizes for creations.

Deng Pufang, chairman of the China Disabled Persons' Federation, spoke at the closing ceremony.

### 精神面貌昂扬　艺术才华惊人  全国残疾人艺术调演闭幕
赖仁琼
1989-03-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　精神面貌昂扬　艺术才华惊人
    全国残疾人艺术调演闭幕
    本报北京３月１１日讯  记者赖仁琼报道：第二届全国残疾人艺术调演今晚闭幕。胡启立、芮杏文、赛福鼎·艾则孜等出席闭幕式并观看了演出。
    本届艺术调演是建国以来规模最大、艺术水平最高的一次残疾人艺术活动。２００多名演员饱含激情的表演，展示了中国残疾人昂扬奋进的精神面貌和令人惊叹的艺术才华。
    本次调演评出女声独唱《祖国赞美诗》、现代舞《雄鹰迪斯科》等表演一等奖１０个，创作一等奖６个。
    中国残联主席邓朴方在闭幕式上讲了话。
