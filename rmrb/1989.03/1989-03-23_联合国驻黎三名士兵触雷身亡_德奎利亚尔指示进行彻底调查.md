Title: Three UN Soldiers Killed in Landmine Explosion in Lebanon; De Cuellar Orders Thorough Investigation
Author:
Date: 1989-03-23
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, March 21st.

According to reports from southern Lebanon, three Irish soldiers serving with the United Nations Interim Force in Lebanon (UNIFIL) were killed on the morning of March 21st when their vehicle struck a landmine in what Israel refers to as the "security zone."

The report stated that Nabih Berri, the leader of the Lebanese "Amal" Movement, had contacted the commander of the UNIFIL and strongly condemned the perpetrators of this incident.

Since the UNIFIL was deployed in Lebanon in 1978, 158 soldiers have lost their lives. The UNIFIL is composed of troops from nine countries, including 600 Irish soldiers. To date, 20 Irish soldiers have been killed in Lebanon.

Xinhua News Agency, United Nations, March 21st.

United Nations Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar today expressed deep regret over the deaths of three Irish soldiers serving with the UNIFIL in southern Lebanon while on duty. He instructed the commander of the UNIFIL to conduct a thorough investigation into this incident.

He said that this tragedy once again underscores the necessity of fully implementing United Nations Security Council Resolution 425.

### 联合国驻黎三名士兵触雷身亡  德奎利亚尔指示进行彻底调查

1989-03-23
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　联合国驻黎三名士兵触雷身亡        
    德奎利亚尔指示进行彻底调查
    新华社贝鲁特３月２１日电  据来自黎巴嫩南部的消息，联合国驻黎巴嫩临时部队的３名爱尔兰籍士兵２１日上午因乘坐的卡车在以色列所谓的“安全地带”内触雷而身亡。
    消息说，黎巴嫩“阿迈勒”运动领导人贝里与联合国临时部队司令官进行了接触，并强烈谴责这一事件的策划者。
    自联合国临时部队１９７８年驻进黎巴嫩以来，已有１５８名士兵丧生。
    联合国临时部队由９个国家组成，其中有爱尔兰籍士兵６００人。迄今已有２０名爱尔兰士兵在黎巴嫩丧生。
    新华社联合国３月２１日电　联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天对联合国驻黎巴嫩临时部队的３名爱尔兰士兵在黎南部执行公务时丧生表示深切遗憾。并指示联合国驻黎临时部队司令对这起事件进行彻底的调查。
    他说，这个悲剧再次说明，全面执行联合国安理会４２５号决议势在必行。
