Title: Austrian Student Wins Men's Ski Jumping Championship at Winter Universiade
Author:
Date: 1989-03-07
Edition: 4

The men's ski jumping competition of the 14th World University Winter Games was held today at the Pine Ridge, 50 kilometers from Sofia. Austrian student Pichler Buchardt scored 202 points to win the men's ski jumping championship at this Winter Universiade.

### 奥选手获大学生冬运会男子高台冠军

1989-03-07
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　奥选手获大学生冬运会男子高台冠军
    新华社索非亚３月５日电  第十四届世界大学生冬季运动会的高台滑雪比赛今天在离索非亚５０公里的松林坡举行。奥地利大学生皮希勒·布克哈特以２０２分获得了本届运动会男子高台滑雪冠军。
