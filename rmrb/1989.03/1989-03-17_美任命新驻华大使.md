Title: The United States Appoints a New Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1989-03-17
Edition: 1

The White House spokesman announced today that U.S. President Bush has appointed James R. Lilley as the new U.S. Ambassador to China, replacing Winston Lord who is about to step down.

The 60-year-old James R. Lilley was born in China. He graduated from Yale University in 1951 and later studied classical Chinese literature at Hong Kong and Columbia University in New York. He is a well-known expert on Asian affairs.

### 美任命新驻华大使

1989-03-17
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　美任命新驻华大使
    新华社华盛顿３月１５日电  白宫发言人今天宣布，美国总统布什已任命李洁明为美国新任驻华大使，接替即将卸任的温斯顿·洛德。
    现年６０岁的李洁明出生于中国，１９５１年毕业于耶鲁大学，后在香港和纽约哥伦比亚大学学习中国古典文学，是著名的亚洲事务专家。
