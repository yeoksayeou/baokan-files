Title: Hu Changlin Appointed Ambassador to the State of Qatar
Author:
Date: 1989-03-02
Edition: 2

    Hu Changlin Appointed Ambassador to the State of Qatar
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 28th

    According to the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, President Yang Shangkun appointed Hu Changlin as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the State of Qatar. Hu Changlin's position as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the United Arab Emirates has been revoked.

### 胡昌林出任驻卡塔尔国大使

1989-03-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    胡昌林出任驻卡塔尔国大使
    新华社北京2月28日电　国家主席杨尚昆根据全国人大常委会的决定，任命胡昌林为中华人民共和国驻卡塔尔国特命全权大使，免去胡昌林的中华人民共和国驻阿拉伯联合酋长国特命全权大使职务。
