Title: Beijing Region Excellent Athletes Indoor Athletics Competition Begins
Author: Xiao Yong
Date: 1989-03-19
Edition: 3

The Beijing Region Excellent Athletes Indoor Athletics Competition began today at the Beijing Gymnasium indoor track and field venue. Jin Ling from the Beijing Institute of Physical Education cleared a height of 1.92 meters in the women's high jump, breaking the 1.91-meter indoor national record set by Zheng Dazhen in 1980. In the men's long jump, Chen Zunrong of the national track and field training team achieved a distance of 7.98 meters, just 2 centimeters short of his own indoor national record.

### 北京地区优秀运动员室内田径赛开始
小勇
1989-03-19
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　北京地区优秀运动员室内田径赛开始
    本报北京３月１８日讯  北京地区优秀运动员室内田径赛今天在北京体育馆室内田径场举行，北京体院的金玲在女子跳高赛中越过１．９２米的高度，刷新了由郑达真１９８０年创造的１．９１米的室内全国纪录。在男子跳远比赛中，国家田径集训队的陈尊荣跳出７．９８米的好成绩，距他本人保持的室内全国纪录仅差２厘米。　　　　　　　　
　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（小勇）
