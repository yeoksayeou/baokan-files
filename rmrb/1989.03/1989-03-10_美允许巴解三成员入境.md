Author: Zhang Qixun
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 3

The U.S. Allows Three PLO Members to Enter the Country

	Washington, March 8 (Reported by Zhang Qixun) The U.S. government announced today that the U.S. has decided to issue entry visas to three officials of the Palestine Liberation Organization to attend a conference that will be held this week at Columbia University in New York.

	The three officials are Nabil Shaath, a senior advisor to Arafat, Afif Safieh, the PLO representative in the Netherlands, and Nicholas Taleb, an external affairs official at the PLO headquarters in Tunisia.

### 美允许巴解三成员入境
张启昕
1989-03-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美允许巴解三成员入境
    本报华盛顿３月８日电  记者张启昕报道：美国政府今天宣布，美国决定发给三名巴勒斯坦解放组织官员的入境签证，出席本周将在纽约哥伦比亚大学举行的一个会议。
    这三名官员是阿拉法特的一位高级顾问纳比勒·沙特，巴解驻荷兰代表阿菲夫·萨菲赫和巴解驻突尼斯总部的一位外事官员尼古拉斯·塔德鲁斯。
