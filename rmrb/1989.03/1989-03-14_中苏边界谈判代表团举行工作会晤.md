Title: Chinese and Soviet Border Negotiation Delegations Hold Working Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 3

The Chinese and Soviet border negotiation delegations held a working meeting. The Western Section working group of the Chinese and Soviet government border negotiation delegations held their first meeting in Moscow from February 20 to March 13. Both sides began discussing the alignment of the western section of the Chinese-Soviet border.

During the meeting, Lu Haozu, head of the Soviet delegation for the Chinese-Soviet border negotiations and deputy foreign minister of the Soviet Union, met with members of the Chinese side of the working group.

### 中苏边界谈判代表团举行工作会晤

1989-03-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　中苏边界谈判代表团举行工作会晤
    新华社莫斯科３月１３日电  中苏两国政府边界谈判代表团西段工作小组２月２０日至３月１３日在莫斯科举行第一次会议，双方开始讨论中苏西段边界线走向问题。
    会议期间，中苏边界谈判苏联政府代表团团长、苏联外交部副部长罗高寿会见了工作小组中方成员。
