Title: Kang Keqing Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-03-06
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 5th.

Kang Keqing, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Honorary Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, met with more than 200 women representatives from Japanese cultural, artistic, educational, and sports organizations this evening at the Great Hall of the People.

The delegation, composed of these women, is the Japan-China Friendship Exchange Delegation visiting China to engage in reciprocal exchanges with their Chinese counterparts. The delegation is led by Yashiro Akari, wife of the late Japanese Foreign Minister Kurusu, and Chairman of the Women's Federation of the Liberal Democratic Party of Japan.

After the meeting, the All-China Women's Federation hosted a banquet to welcome the Japanese guests.

### 康克清会见日本客人

1989-03-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　康克清会见日本客人
    新华社北京３月５日电  全国政协副主席、全国妇联名誉主席康克清，今天晚上在人民大会堂会见了２００多名来自日本文化、艺术、教育、体育等团体的妇女代表。
    由这些妇女组成的日本日中友好交流访华团是前来同中国同行进行对口交流活动的。日本已故外相园田直的夫人、日本自民党各妇女团体联合会会长园田天光光担任代表团总团长。
    会见后，全国妇联设宴欢迎日本客人。
