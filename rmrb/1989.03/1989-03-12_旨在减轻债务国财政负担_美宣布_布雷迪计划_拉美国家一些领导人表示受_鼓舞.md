Title: Aimed at Reducing the Fiscal Burden of Debtor Countries: The U.S. Announces the "Brady Plan"
Author: Cang Lidé
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 1

For the purpose of changing the U.S. policy towards third-world countries' debts, U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Nicholas F. Brady today announced a new plan aimed at reducing the fiscal burden of debtor countries at the State Department.

The plan, known as the "Brady Plan," primarily involves using the International Monetary Fund and the World Bank to provide assistance to creditor banks in reducing the principal and interest of debtor countries. The main method is for these two international banks to raise a special reserve fund. After the creditor banks and debtor countries reach an agreement on debt reduction, the debtor countries can use this fund to buy back the reduced debt at a price lower than the original debt. Additionally, creditor banks can also use bonds of lower value to exchange for the waived debt, with the interest on the bonds guaranteed by the International Monetary Fund or the World Bank.

According to statistics, developing countries, mainly in Latin America, owe the U.S. a total of $400 billion. The debt issue is seen as an important factor in the social and political unrest in some Latin American countries.

Some leaders of Latin American countries have expressed that the intent of the "Brady Plan" is encouraging. However, Venezuelan President Jaime Lusinchi believes that the plan is not bold enough in terms of debt reduction. The Japanese Minister of Finance has expressed "strong support" for this new plan and has pledged financial support.

### 旨在减轻债务国财政负担  美宣布“布雷迪计划”  拉美国家一些领导人表示受“鼓舞”
仓立德
1989-03-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　旨在减轻债务国财政负担
    美宣布“布雷迪计划”       
    拉美国家一些领导人表示受“鼓舞”
    本报华盛顿３月１０日电  记者仓立德报道：为改变美国对第三世界国家的债务政策，美国财政部长布雷迪今天在国务院宣布了一项旨在减轻债务国财政负担的新方案。
    被称作“布雷迪计划”的方案，其主要内容是通过国际货币基金组织和世界银行，为债权银行减免债务国的部分本金和利息提供资助。主要方式是，上述两家国际银行筹集一笔特别储备金。在债权银行与债务国达成减免债务协议后，债务国可用这笔资金以低于原债务的价格买回减免的债务。另外，债权银行也可用较低价值的债券来换取免除的债务，债券的利息可由国际货币基金组织或世界银行予以担保。
    据统计，发展中国家，主要是拉美的重债国，积欠美国的债务高达４０００亿美元。债务问题被视为拉美一些国家社会、政治动荡不安的重要因素。
    拉美国家一些领导人表示，“布雷迪计划”的意图令人鼓舞。但委内瑞拉总统佩雷斯认为该方案在减免债务方面步子还不够大胆。日本财政部长表示“强烈支持”这个新计划，并保证给予财政支援。
