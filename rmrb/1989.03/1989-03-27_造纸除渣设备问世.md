Title: New Paper-Making Sludge Removal Equipment Introduced
Author: Zhúzi
Date: 1989-03-27
Edition: 2

A new type of paper-making sludge removal equipment—the XZF-420Ⅱ type cyclone separator—has recently been introduced in Shijiazhuang.

It is understood that China's paper-making industry annually discharges nearly 4 billion cubic meters of wastewater into rivers, lakes, and seas. Among this, the suspended solids and organic pollutants each exceed 15 million tons, severely polluting the environment. This equipment not only can remove sludge and clean pollutants but also effectively prevent the loss of good pulp.

### 造纸除渣设备问世
竹子
1989-03-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　造纸除渣设备问世
    一新型造纸除渣设备——ＸＺＦ—４２０Ⅱ型旋流式杂质分离器，日前在石家庄问世。
    据了解，我国造纸工业每年向江河湖海排放污水近４０亿立方米，其中悬浮物和有机污染物都在１５０万吨以上，严重污染环境。而该设备不仅可以除渣清污，还能有效防止良浆流失。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（竹子）
