Title: Every Township in Yushu Has Television
Author: Zhè Zhèn Pú
Date: 1989-03-24
Edition: 2

In the southwest of the Qinghai Plateau, covering nearly 200,000 square kilometers, the Yushu Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, with an average altitude of over 4,000 meters, now has television in all 47 townships (towns). The grasslands and mountainous regions are directly connected to the capital, Beijing.

### 玉树乡乡有电视
郅振璞
1989-03-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　玉树乡乡有电视
    本报西宁３月２３日电  记者郅振璞报道：在青海高原西南部近２０万平方公里的玉树藏族自治州，平均海拔４０００米以上的４７个乡（镇），如今乡乡有电视，草原山乡直接和首都北京连在一起。
