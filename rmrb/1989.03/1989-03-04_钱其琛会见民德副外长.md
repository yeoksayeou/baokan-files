Title: Qian Qichen Meets with East German Deputy Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 2

Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met with the visiting East German Deputy Foreign Minister Otto Reinhold today in Beijing.

Otto Reinhold arrived in Beijing on February 24. He held talks with Vice Foreign Minister Deng Zhenpai and met with the Chinese chairman of the Sino-East German Economic and Technological Cooperation Committee, Minister of Chemical Industry Qin Zhongda, among others.

### 钱其琛会见民德副外长

1989-03-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　钱其琛会见民德副外长
    新华社北京３月２日电  外交部长钱其琛今天在北京会见了应邀访华的德意志民主共和国副外长奥特一行。
    奥特副外长于２月２４日抵京后同田曾佩副外长进行了会谈，并会见了中国民德经贸科技合作委员会中方主席、化工部长秦仲达等。
