Title: Haicheng Issues "Green Cards" to Farmers for Priority in Contracting Agricultural and Livestock Projects
Author:
Date: 1989-03-09
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, March 8th.

In the rural areas of Haicheng City, Liaoning Province, farmers holding "green cards" can have priority in contracting various agricultural and livestock production projects.

The "green cards" indicate titles such as assistant technical officer, technical officer, assistant technical expert, and technical expert.

This is a professional technical title certificate issued by the Haicheng City government to farmers who have undergone scientific knowledge and production technology training in agricultural and livestock projects, and have passed the assessment by relevant departments.

### 海城向农民发“绿卡”  承包种养业可优先

1989-03-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　海城向农民发“绿卡”
    承包种养业可优先
    新华社沈阳３月８日电  在辽宁省海城市农村，手持“绿色证书”的农民可以优先承包种养业的各种生产项目。
    “绿色证书”上标明助理技术员、技术员、助理技师、技师等职称。
    这是海城市政府对从事种养业的农民进行科学知识和生产技术培训，经有关部门考核后发给农民的专业技术职称证书。
