Title: South Korean Pastor Mun Ik-hwan Arrives in Pyongyang
Author: XU Baokang
Date: 1989-03-26
Edition: 4

South Korean Pastor Mun Ik-hwan Arrives in Pyongyang

People's Daily, Pyongyang, March 25th. Reporter Xu Baokang reports: South Korean prominent opposition figure and social-political activist Pastor Mun Ik-hwan and his entourage of three arrived in Pyongyang this afternoon by special plane to conduct a friendly visit to North Korea. They will also meet with Chairman Kim Il-sung to discuss the great cause of Korean reunification.

At the press conference held at the airport, Pastor Mun Ik-hwan said that he would, as a member of the nation, exchange candid views with Chairman Kim Il-sung on the future of Korea. Pastor Mun Ik-hwan is known in South Korea for his advocacy of democracy and opposition to dictatorship. Currently, he is an advisor to the "National Alliance for Democracy and Reunification of Korea."

### 南朝鲜牧师文益焕抵平壤
徐宝康
1989-03-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　南朝鲜牧师文益焕抵平壤
    本报平壤３月２５日电  记者徐宝康报道：南朝鲜著名在野人士、社会政治活动家文益焕牧师等一行三人，今天下午乘专机抵达平壤，对朝鲜进行友好访问，并将同金日成主席会晤，共商朝鲜统一大业。
    文益焕牧师在机场举行的记者招待会上说，他将作为民族的一员，在同金日成会晤中，就朝鲜的未来，坦诚的交换意见。文益焕牧师在南朝鲜以争民主、反独裁而著称。目前他是南朝鲜“全国民族民主运动联合会”的顾问。
