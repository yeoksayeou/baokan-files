Title: Vice Chairman Peng Chong Meets British Guests
Author: Pú Yùqīng
Date: 1989-03-26
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 25th.

Today morning, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Chong met with Sir Patrick Mayhew, the British Attorney General, and his entourage at the Great Hall of the People.

Peng Chong expressed the hope that the British judicial community would visit China more often to gain a better understanding of China's political, economic, legal, and cultural situations. Sir Patrick also believed that it was very necessary and important for the judicial communities of the two countries to strengthen exchanges.

### 彭冲副委员长会见英国客人
顾玉清
1989-03-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　彭冲副委员长会见英国客人
    本报北京三月二十五日讯  记者顾玉清报道：今天上午，全国人大常委会副委员长彭冲在人民大会堂会见了英国总检察长帕特里克·梅休爵士一行。
    彭冲希望英国司法界多来中国访问，多了解中国的政治、经济、法律、文化等方面的情况。帕特里克爵士也认为，英中两国司法界加强交流是十分必要和重要的。
