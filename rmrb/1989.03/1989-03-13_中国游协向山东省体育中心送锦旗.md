Title: China Swimming Association Presents Banner to Shandong Provincial Sports Center
Author: Jià Jiànzhōu
Date: 1989-03-13
Edition: 4

The China Swimming Association and the China Diving Team presented a banner inscribed with "The Cradle of the 24th Olympic Games Diving Gold Medals" to the Shandong Provincial Sports Center, which serves as the China Diving Base.

The Shandong Provincial Sports Center's swimming pool is a modern facility that has made significant contributions to the development of diving in China. From June 20 to August 30, 1988, the Chinese Diving Team conducted a two-month closed training camp here in preparation for the Olympic Games. Notable divers such as Gao Min, Xu Yanmei, Xiong Ni, Tan Liangde, Li Kongzheng, and twelve others participated in this intensive training session.

### 中国游协向山东省体育中心送锦旗
贾建舟
1989-03-13
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　中国游协向山东省体育中心送锦旗
    本报济南三月十日电  记者贾建舟报道：今天，中国游泳协会、中国跳水队将一面写有“二十四届奥运会跳水金牌的摇篮”的锦旗，授给了中国跳水基地——山东省体育中心。
    山东省体育中心游泳馆是一座现代化的游泳馆，为我国跳水事业做出了贡献。一九八八年六月二十日至八月三十日，中国跳水队在这里进行为期两个月的奥运会前的封闭训练，著名跳水运动员高敏、许艳梅、熊倪、谭良德、李孔政等十五人参加了集训。
