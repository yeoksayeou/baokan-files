Title: Xiamen's Trade with Taiwan is Active
Author: Wan Hongqiang
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xiamen, March 2nd.

Recently, more than 100 Taiwanese businessmen gathered in Xiamen to participate in a trade negotiation meeting hosted by the Xiamen Youli Company.

According to statistics from relevant departments, since last year, over 1,500 Taiwanese businessmen have come to Xiamen to discuss business.

### 厦门对台贸易活跃
万红强
1989-03-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    厦门对台贸易活跃
    据新华社厦门三月二日电　（记者万红强）近日有一百多位台湾客商云集厦门，参加厦门友利公司举办的两岸贸易洽谈会。
    据有关部门统计，去年以来，来厦门洽谈生意的台湾客商已达一千五百多人次。
