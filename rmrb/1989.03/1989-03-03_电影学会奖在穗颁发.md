Title: The Film Society Awards Presented in Guangzhou
Author: Liang Zhaoming
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 4

The second China Film Performance Art Society "Society Award" presentation ceremony was held in Guangzhou on February 27.

The winners of this year's "Society Award" include Jiang Wen, Xie Yuan, Liu Wei, Pan Hong, and Lü Liping, among 40 individuals.

During the award ceremony, more than 40 famous actors performed a series of wonderful artistic programs.

### 电影学会奖在穗颁发
粱兆明
1989-03-03
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

    电影学会奖在穗颁发
    本报广州电  记者梁兆明报道：中国电影表演艺术学会第2届“学会奖”颁奖仪式2月27日在广州举行。
    获取这届“学会奖”的包括姜文、谢园、刘威、潘虹、吕丽萍等40人。
    在颁奖仪式上，40多位著名演员表演了精彩的文艺节目。
