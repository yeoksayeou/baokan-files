Title: Zhou Nan to Visit Europe
Author:
Date: 1989-03-17
Edition: 2

	Zhou Nan to Visit Europe
	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 16

	The spokesperson for the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced at today's afternoon press conference that Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Nan has been invited and will depart from Beijing on March 26 to visit the United Kingdom, Luxembourg, Italy, and the Federal Republic of Germany.

	The spokesperson also announced that the Chinese government will send a delegation led by Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Li Daoyi to participate in the 45th annual session of the United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific, to be held in Bangkok, Thailand, from March 27 to April 5.

### 周南将访欧

1989-03-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　周南将访欧
    新华社北京３月１６日电  中国外交部发言人在今天下午的新闻发布会上宣布，外交部副部长周南应邀将于３月２６日离京，前往英国、卢森堡、意大利和联邦德国进行访问。
    这位发言人还宣布，中国政府将派以外交部部长助理李道豫为团长的代表团参加将于３月２７日至４月５日在泰国曼谷举行的联合国亚太经社会第４５届年会。
