Title: Promoting Youth Song Creation and Inspiring Patriotism Among Youth
Author: Wáng Cáng
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 4

	National People's Congress Chairman Li Xiannian recently sent a letter to the organizing committee of the China Campus Song Creation Grand Prix, congratulating the Central People's Broadcasting Station and the World Chinese Foundation for organizing this event.

	He said, this grand prix is of great significance in promoting the creation of songs by young people, inspiring their patriotism, and encouraging them to strive upward with a healthy and vigorous spirit. He hopes that all music workers and music enthusiasts will create more and better works to contribute to the cultivation of a new generation of socialists.

### 促进青少年歌曲创作　激发青少年爱国热情  李先念主席鼓励校园歌曲
王藏
1989-03-03
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

    促进青少年歌曲创作　激发青少年爱国热情
    李先念主席鼓励校园歌曲
    全国政协主席李先念最近致函中国校园歌曲创作大奖赛组委会，对中央人民广播电台和华人世界基金会举办的这次活动表示祝贺。
    他说，这次大奖赛对促进青少年歌曲的创作，激发青少年的爱国热情，鼓舞他们奋发向上朝气蓬勃地健康成长具有重要意义。希望广大音乐工作者和音乐爱好者创作出更多更好的作品，为培养社会主义的新一代做出更大的贡献。　（王葳）
