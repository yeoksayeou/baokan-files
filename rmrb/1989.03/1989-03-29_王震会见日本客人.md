Title: Wang Zhen Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-03-29
Edition: 2

Wang Zhen Meets with Japanese Guests

	New China News Agency, Beijing, March 28th - Today in the afternoon, Vice Chairman of the State Wang Zhen met with a delegation from the Japanese House of Representatives, led by Social Democratic Party Central Committee member Kawano Masaru.

	Wang Zhen briefed the Japanese guests on the proceedings of the Second Session of the Seventh National People's Congress and the Second Session of the Seventh Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

	Regarding domestic affairs, Wang Zhen pointed out that China has achieved significant progress in its ten years of reform and opening up, but also encountered some problems and difficulties. He said that reform and opening up must be steadfastly continued.

### 王震会见日本客人

1989-03-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　王震会见日本客人
    新华社北京３月２８日电  国家副主席王震今天下午在人民大会堂会见了日本众议员、社会党中央委员河野正一行。
    王震向日本客人介绍了人大七届二次会议和政协七届二次会议的情况。
    关于国内情况，王震指出，中国在十年的改革、开放中取得了很大的成绩，但也出现了一些问题和困难。他说，改革、开放要坚定不移地坚持下去。
