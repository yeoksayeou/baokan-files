Title: Female PhD Student Achieves High Success Rate in Liver Transplants with New Method
Author: Zhang Zhaoji
Date: 1989-03-16
Edition: 2

A female PhD student at the Second Military Medical University's Changhai Hospital, Sun Junhong, successfully used a method involving donor spleen cells and short-term cyclosporin A. This method has resulted in more than half of the 300 orthotopic liver transplants achieving long-term survival.

Liver transplantation, including surgical techniques, suppressing rejection reactions, and improving survival rates, is a crucial area of research for organ transplant experts. Sun Junhong chose to conduct liver transplant experiments on rats, which have a high degree of surgical difficulty. She achieved a surgical success rate of 92.7% and established a stable and reliable experimental model. This achievement has reached international standards.

### 女博士生用新法  肝移植成功率高
张肇基
1989-03-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　女博士生用新法  肝移植成功率高
    本报上海三月十五日电  第二军医大学长海医院肝胆外科女博士生孙君泓，成功地运用供体脾细胞加短期环孢素Ａ的方法，使三百例原位肝移植中，半数以上能长期存活。
    肝移植的操作技术、抑制排异反应、提高存活期是器官移植专家研究的重要课题。孙君泓选择操作技术难度较大的大鼠进行肝移植实验研究，手术成功率达到百分之九十二点七，并建立了稳定可靠的实验模型。这项成果达到国际水平。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（张肇基）
