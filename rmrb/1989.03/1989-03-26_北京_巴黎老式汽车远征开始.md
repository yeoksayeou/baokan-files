Title: The Beijing–Paris Vintage Car Expedition Begins
Author: Wú Huì
Date: 1989-03-26
Edition: 3

To the accompaniment of enthusiastic applause and cheers, a Fiat-produced Itala vintage car slowly set off, marking the start of the 110-day 1989 Beijing to Paris vintage car expedition.

Despite the chilly evening breeze in Beijing, it did not dampen the enthusiasm for this expedition. Italians residing in Beijing, journalists from the capital's media, and staff from the organizing units all arrived early at Tiananmen Square. The square's surroundings were also filled with many eager onlookers. At 9 PM, Vice Minister of the State Sports Commission He Zhenliang, Deputy Mayor of Beijing Zhang Baifa, and Italian Ambassador to China Alberto Sorella together cut the ribbon symbolizing the starting line. Amid the roar of engines, the Itala vintage car and the nine accompanying vehicles began their 20,000-kilometer journey from Beijing to Paris.

The first stop of this expedition is Shijiazhuang.

### 北京—巴黎老式汽车远征开始
吴骅
1989-03-26
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　北京—巴黎老式汽车远征开始
    本报北京３月２５日讯  记者吴骅报道：在一片热烈的掌声和欢呼声中，意菲亚特公司生产的伊塔拉老式汽车缓缓开动，为时１１０天的１９８９年北京至巴黎老式汽车远征拉开序幕。
    尽管今天晚上北京阵阵凉风，但却没有影响人们对这次远征的兴趣，在京的意大利人士、首都新闻界的记者和主办单位的工作人员都早早地来到了天安门广场，广场四周也站满了许多兴致勃勃的群众。晚９时，国家体委副主任何振梁、北京市副市长张百发和意大利驻华大使阿尔贝托·索里拉一起剪开象征着起跑线的彩带，在一片马达的轰鸣声中，伊塔拉老式汽车和随行的九辆工作车组成的车队开始了北京至巴黎两万余公里的远征。
    此次远征的第一站是石家庄。
