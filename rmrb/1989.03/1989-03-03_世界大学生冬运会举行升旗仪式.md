Title: Opening Ceremony of the World University Winter Games Held
Author: Wang Jinbo
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 4

The opening ceremony of the 1989 World University Winter Games was held this afternoon at the "September" Winter Palace Sports Hall in Sofia.

The national flag of Bulgaria, the flag of the International University Sports Federation, and the flag of the Sofia 1989 World University Winter Games were raised to the top of their poles under the gaze of the crowd. On behalf of the host country, Deputy Chairman of the Bulgarian Sports Federation, D. Butchkov, welcomed the 1,060 athletes from 34 countries and regions and wished them excellent results in the 10-day competition.

Chinese athletes will participate in this winter sports event, competing in short-track speed skating, figure skating, biathlon, and cross-country relay races.

Chinese athletes are determined to perform at their best in this world-class competition and strive to achieve good results.

### 世界大学生冬运会举行升旗仪式
王金柏
1989-03-03
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

    世界大学生冬运会举行升旗仪式
    新华社索非亚3月1日电  （记者王金柏）1989年世界大学生冬季运动会的升旗仪式，今天下午在“九月”冬宫体育馆广场举行。
    保加利亚国旗、世界大学生体联会旗和索非亚1989年世界大学生冬季运动会会旗在众人注目下徐徐升上了杆顶。保加利亚体协副主席迪·布奇科夫代表东道国向来自34个国家和地区的1060名运动员表示欢迎，并祝他们在为期10天的比赛中取得优异成绩。
    中国运动员将参加这次冰雪盛会的短跑道速滑、花样滑冰、现代冬季两项和越野接力赛等项目的比赛。
    中国运动员决心在这次世界性大赛中赛出水平，争取获得好成绩。
