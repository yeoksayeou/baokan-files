Title: Two More Gold Medals Decided at the National Winter Youth Games
Author: Dù Xiǎomíng
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jilin, March 2nd.

The second National Youth Games (Winter) decided two more gold medals tonight here in the speed skating events for men's 1000 meters and women's 1500 meters. The gold medals were won by Wang Lianjun from Harbin and Shen Yan from Changchun, respectively.

### 全国青运会（冬季）又决出两枚金牌
杜晓明
1989-03-04
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　全国青运会（冬季）又决出两枚金牌
    新华社吉林３月２日电  （记者杜晓明）第二届全国青少年运动会（冬季）今晚在这里决出了速度滑冰男子１０００米和女子１５００米两块金牌。它们分别被哈尔滨的王连军和长春的沈艳夺得。
