Title: Partial Personnel Changes in the Kuomintang
Author:
Date: 1989-03-07
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, March 6th.

According to reports from Taiwan newspapers, the Kuomintang Central Standing Committee approved a list of departmental heads on March 4th. Those who have been appointed to new positions include: Central Policy Committee Chairman Lin Dong, Central Secretariat Director Liu Songfan, "Legislative Yuan" Party Secretary-General Huang Zeqing, Central Policy Committee Vice Chairman Rao Yingqi, Li Zongren, Central Cultural Work Committee Vice Chairman Zhao Shaokang, Central Women's Work Committee Vice Chairman Lin Chengzhi, and Central Organization Work Committee Vice Chairman Xie Longsheng.

### 台湾国民党部分人事更动

1989-03-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　台湾国民党部分人事更动
    新华社香港３月６日电  据台湾报纸报道，国民党中常会于３月４日通过了一批部门负责人的任命。
    获任新职的有：中央政策会主任委员林栋，中央秘书处主任刘松藩，“立法委员”党部书记长黄泽青，中央政策会副主任委员饶颖奇、李宗仁，中央文化工作会副主任赵少康，中央妇女工作会副主任林澄枝，中央组织工作会副主任谢隆盛。
