Title: India Denies Sending Advisors to Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1989-03-02
Edition: 3

India yesterday denied reports that it would send advisors to Afghanistan following the Soviet Union's withdrawal from the country.

This was revealed in a news release issued by the Indian Embassy in Pakistan last night.

### 印度否认派遣顾问去阿

1989-03-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    印度否认派遣顾问去阿
    新华社伊斯兰堡3月1日电　印度昨天否认有关在苏联从阿富汗撤军后印度派顾问去阿富汗的报道。
    这是印度驻巴基斯坦使馆昨晚散发的一份新闻公报透露的。
