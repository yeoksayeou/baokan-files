Title: Wind, Clouds, Thunder, and Lightning
Author: Xu Xiong
Date: 1989-03-28
Edition: 8

The Wind
    Soaring from the longing for a gale
    Exulting in the rolling rhythm

The Clouds
    Even if one's life is dull and dim
    One would still wish to shower blessings upon the world

The Spring Thunder
    A roar of anger
    Shattering the cold world

The Lightning
    Life is but a moment
    Yet it adds luster to the heavens and the earth

徐熊
1989-03-28

专栏：

　　风·云·雷·电
    大风
    从狂飙的向往中起飞
    在滚动的旋律里昂扬

    　　　　春雷
    一声怒吼
    震毁寒冷世界
    　　　　闪电
    生命只有一瞬
    却为天地增辉
