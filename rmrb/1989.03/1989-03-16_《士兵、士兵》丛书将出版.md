Title: The "Soldiers, Soldiers" Book Series Will Be Published
Author: Wang Xingzeng
Date: 1989-03-16
Edition: 4

A large series of books titled "Soldiers, Soldiers" will be published in October. This series, edited and published by the Beijing Youth Daily and the Military Translation Press, is directly aimed at soldiers. The series covers five aspects: psychology, behavior, culture, military affairs, and technology. It provides soldiers with systematic textbooks for self-identification, self-selection, self-adjustment, and self-education.

### 《士兵、士兵》丛书将出版
王行增
1989-03-16
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　《士兵、士兵》丛书将出版
    一套直接面向军队士兵而编辑、出版的《士兵、士兵》大型系列丛书，将于１０月出版。这套由北京青年报和军事译文出版社出版的丛书，从心理、行为、文化、军事、科技五个方面，为士兵提供自我分辨、自我选择、自我调节、自我教育的系统教材。　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　（王行增）
