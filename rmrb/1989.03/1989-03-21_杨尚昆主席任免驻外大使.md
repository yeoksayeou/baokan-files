Title: Yang Shangkun Appoints and Dismisses Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1989-03-21
Edition: 2

	Yang Shangkun, President of the People's Republic of China, in accordance with the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, has appointed and dismissed the following ambassadors:

	1. Appoint Li Shichun as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Thailand and concurrently as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Democratic Kampuchea.
	Dismiss Zhang Dewen from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Thailand and concurrently to the Democratic Kampuchea.

	2. Appoint Zhang Dewen as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Socialist Republic of Vietnam.
	Dismiss Li Shichun from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Socialist Republic of Vietnam.

	3. Appoint Zhang Zhen as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Syrian Arab Republic.
	Dismiss Wang Changyi from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Syrian Arab Republic.

	4. Appoint An Fengshi as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Gabonese Republic and concurrently as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Democratic Republic of São Tomé and Príncipe.
	Dismiss Tian Yimin from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Gabonese Republic and concurrently to the Democratic Republic of São Tomé and Príncipe.

	5. Appoint Tian Yimin as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Rwanda.
	Dismiss An Fengshi from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Rwanda.

	6. Appoint Li Fenglin as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Bulgaria.
	Dismiss Teng Shaozhi from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

	7. Appoint Yu Mingsheng as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Jamaica.

	8. Appoint Shi Nailiang to concurrently serve as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Seychelles.

	9. Dismiss Zhang Zhen from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan.

	10. Dismiss Liang Taosheng from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Cape Verde.

### 杨尚昆主席任免驻外大使

1989-03-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　杨尚昆主席任免驻外大使
    新华社北京３月１９日电  中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会的决定任免下列驻外大使：
    一、任命李世淳为中华人民共和国驻泰王国特命全权大使兼驻民主柬埔寨特命全权大使。
    免去张德维的中华人民共和国驻泰王国特命全权大使兼驻民主柬埔寨特命全权大使职务。
    二、任命张德维为中华人民共和国驻越南社会主义共和国特命全权大使。
    免去李世淳的中华人民共和国驻越南社会主义共和国特命全权大使职务。
    三、任命张真为中华人民共和国驻阿拉伯叙利亚共和国特命全权大使。
    免去王昌义的中华人民共和国驻阿拉伯叙利亚共和国特命全权大使职务。
    四、任命安峰石为中华人民共和国驻加蓬共和国特命全权大使兼驻圣多美和普林西比民主共和国特命全权大使。
    免去田逸民的中华人民共和国驻加蓬共和国特命全权大使兼驻圣多美和普林西比民主共和国特命全权大使职务。
    五、任命田逸民为中华人民共和国驻卢旺达共和国特命全权大使。
    免去安峰石的中华人民共和国驻卢旺达共和国特命全权大使职务。
    六、任命李凤林为中华人民共和国驻保加利亚人民共和国特命全权大使。
    免去滕绍志的中华人民共和国驻保加利亚人民共和国特命全权大使职务。
    七、任命喻明生为中华人民共和国驻牙买加特命全权大使。
    八、任命施乃良兼任中华人民共和国驻塞舌尔共和国特命全权大使。
    九、免去张真的中华人民共和国驻约旦哈希姆王国特命全权大使职务。
    十、免去梁涛生的中华人民共和国驻佛得角共和国特命全权大使职务。
