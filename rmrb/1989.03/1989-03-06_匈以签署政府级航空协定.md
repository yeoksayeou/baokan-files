Title: Hungary and Israel Sign Government-Level Aviation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1989-03-06
Edition: 3

Hungary and Israel Sign Government-Level Aviation Agreement

	Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 3: According to a report by the Hungarian News Agency this evening, Hungary's Minister of Transport, Telecommunications, and Construction, Andras Derzsi, visited Israel from February 28 to March 3 and signed a government-level aviation agreement with Israeli Foreign Minister Moshe Arens.

	According to the agreement, air services between Budapest and Tel Aviv will commence at the end of March.

	Hungary and Israel have not yet established formal diplomatic relations.

	This aviation agreement signed by both sides will facilitate the exchange of personnel between the two countries. There are approximately 300,000 Hungarian Jews in Israel.

### 匈以签署政府级航空协定

1989-03-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　匈以签署政府级航空协定
    新华社布达佩斯３月３日电  据匈通社今晚报道，匈牙利交通、电讯和建筑部部长代尔日·安德拉什于２月２８日至３月３日访问了以色列，并同以色列外交部长摩西·阿伦斯签订了政府级航空协定。
    根据协定，从３月底开始，布达佩斯和特拉维夫之间将开始通航。
    匈以至今尚未建立正式外交关系。
    这次双方签署的航空协定将有利于扩大两国人员之间的往来，以色列大约有３０万匈裔公民。
