Title: Rush to Board the Train
Author: Wáng Jiànhuá
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 5

    This photograph captures a moment of intense competition among passengers as they rush to board a train. The scene is chaotic, with individuals pushing and shoving in their eagerness to secure a spot on the train. The image vividly portrays the challenges and struggles faced by people during peak travel times, highlighting the urgency and desperation that often accompany such situations. The photograph serves as a poignant reminder of the everyday struggles and the relentless pursuit of opportunities that define life for many.
