Title: Afghan Mujahideen Alliance Leader Narrowly Escapes Harm
Author:
Date: 1989-03-28
Edition: 4

The leader of the Afghan Islamic Party and the interim government of the Seven-Party Alliance, Gulbuddin Hekmatyar, recently narrowly escaped harm while inspecting Mujahideen positions outside Jalalabad. According to the Afghan News Agency, a bomb dropped by the Kabul regime's air force exploded near a mosque, shattering the mosque's windows and injuring Hekmatyar.

### 阿七党联盟领导人险遭不测

1989-03-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　阿七党联盟领导人险遭不测
    新华社伊斯兰堡３月２６日电  阿富汗伊斯兰党领导人、七党联盟临时政府外长希克马蒂亚尔最近在贾拉拉巴德城外视察游击队阵地时，因遭到喀布尔政权空军袭击而险遭不幸。
    据阿富汗新闻社报道，喀布尔空军投的一颗炸弹在一座寺院附近爆炸，寺院的玻璃窗震碎，把希克马蒂亚尔扎伤。
