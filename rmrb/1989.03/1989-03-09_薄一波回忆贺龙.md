Title: Bo Yibo Remembers He Long
Author:
Date: 1989-03-09
Edition: 1

People's Daily reports: Today, the sixth page of this newspaper features an article written by Bo Yibo about He Long, titled "Recalling the Past: Remembering He Long." The article recounts some of the experiences Bo Yibo had with Comrade He Long, highlighting He Long's noble character and revolutionary achievements.

### 薄一波回忆贺龙

1989-03-09
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　薄一波回忆贺龙
    本报讯  本报今日六版刊登了薄一波撰写的关于贺龙的回忆录，题为《往事追寻忆贺龙》。文章记叙了薄老同贺龙同志相处的一些往事，以说明贺龙同志的高尚品德和革命业绩。
