Title: The Ministry of Chemical Industry Helps Xian County Drill Wells
Author: Zhao Caihe
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 2

    The Ministry of Chemical Industry and the Hebei Provincial Chemical Industry Bureau, along with other units, worked together to drill 19 wells in the deep mountain areas of Xian County in less than two years. This effort has resolved the "difficulty in obtaining water" problem for 20 villages and 7,600 households.

### 化工部帮助涉县打水井
赵彩河
1989-03-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    化工部帮助涉县打水井
    本报讯　化工部和河北省化工厅等单位协同配合，在不到两年的时间里，为涉县深山区农村打井十九眼，使二十个村庄、七千六百多户农民解决了“吃水难”问题。　（赵彩河）
