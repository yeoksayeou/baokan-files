Title: Italian Socialist Party's Congress Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 3

The 22nd Congress of the Italian Socialist Party concluded today in the eastern coastal city of Rimini after four days of sessions. Antonio Cariglia continues to serve as the party's secretary.

### 意社民党党代会结束

1989-03-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　意社民党党代会结束
    据新华社罗马３月１２日电  持续４天的意大利社会民主党第２２届代表大会今天在东部海滨城市里米尼结束，安东尼奥·卡里利亚继续担任党的书记。
