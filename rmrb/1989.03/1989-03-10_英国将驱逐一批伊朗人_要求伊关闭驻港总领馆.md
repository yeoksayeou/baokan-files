Title: Britain to Expel a Group of Iranians and Require Iran to Close Its Consulate-General in Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 3

	British Foreign Secretary Geoffrey Howe announced in the House of Commons on the afternoon of March 8 that, for security reasons, Britain will expel a group of Iranians. He also said that Britain has notified the Iranian government to close its consulate-general in Hong Kong and withdraw its staff there within two weeks.

### 英国将驱逐一批伊朗人  要求伊关闭驻港总领馆

1989-03-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　英国将驱逐一批伊朗人
    要求伊关闭驻港总领馆
    新华社伦敦３月８日电  英国外交大臣杰弗里·豪８日下午在国会宣布，英国出于安全方面的考虑将驱逐一批伊朗人。他还说，英国已通知伊朗政府关闭其在香港的总领事馆，并在两周内撤走那里的工作人员。
