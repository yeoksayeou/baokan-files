Title: "Discovery" Malfunction Resolved
Author: Cang Lide
Date: 1989-03-17
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, March 15th. Reporter Cang Lide reports: The power supply system malfunction on the U.S. space shuttle "Discovery" has been resolved, scientists at the Houston ground control center said. "Discovery" will fly for 5 days as planned and return to Earth on Saturday.

In addition to continuing various scientific experiments, the astronauts also used a 70mm camera to take many photographs of the Earth.

### “发现”号故障排除
仓立德
1989-03-17
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　“发现”号故障排除
    本报华盛顿３月１５日电  记者仓立德报道：美国航天飞机“发现”号上的电力供应系统故障今天已被排除，休斯敦地面控制中心的科学家说，“发现”号将按预定计划飞行５天，星期六返回地面。
    宇航员除继续从事各项科学实验外，还用７０毫米口径摄影机拍摄了许多地球图片。
