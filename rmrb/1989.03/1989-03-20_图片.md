Title: Car Bomb Explodes in Beirut
Author:
Date: 1989-03-20
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency

On March 17th, a car bomb containing 50 kilograms of explosives detonated during the morning rush hour in the eastern part of Beirut, resulting in 10 deaths and 60 injuries. This is a scene of the police investigating the accident site.
