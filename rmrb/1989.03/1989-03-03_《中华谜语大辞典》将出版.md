Title: The Comprehensive Dictionary of Chinese Riddles to Be Published
Author:
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 4

The first authoritative "Comprehensive Dictionary of Chinese Riddles" will be published by Anhui Literature and Arts Publishing House and will be distributed both domestically and internationally. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 《中华谜语大辞典》将出版

1989-03-03
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

    《中华谜语大辞典》将出版
    我国第一部权威性《中华谜语大辞典》，将由安徽文艺出版社出版，并在海内外发行。　（据新华社）
