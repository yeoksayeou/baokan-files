Title: Hong Kong Consultative Committee and Mainland Drafting Committee Discuss the Basic Law
Author: Huang Riye
Date: 1989-03-16
Edition: 2

The delegation of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region Basic Law Consultative Committee, led by Chairman An Ziji, held a meeting with the mainland members of the Basic Law Drafting Committee this morning at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

On February 21, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress decided to publish the draft of the Basic Law of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region for public consultation in both the mainland and Hong Kong. The Basic Law Drafting Committee of Hong Kong entrusted the Consultative Committee with the task of conducting the public consultation in the Hong Kong region. Today, the representatives of the Consultative Committee exchanged views with the mainland drafting committee members on various issues related to the Basic Law.

Chairman Qi Pengfei, Vice Chairman Wang Hanbin, and Secretaries Li Hou and Lu Ping of the Basic Law Drafting Committee of Hong Kong attended the meeting.

### 香港咨询委员　内地起草委员座谈基本法
黄日尧
1989-03-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　香港咨询委员　内地起草委员座谈基本法
    新华社北京３月１５日电  （记者黄日尧）由安子介主任委员率领的香港特别行政区基本法咨询委员会代表团今天上午在钓鱼台国宾馆与基本法起草委员会内地委员举行了会谈。
    今年２月２１日，全国人大常委会决定公布香港特别行政区基本法（草案），在内地和香港广泛征求意见。香港基本法起草委员会委托咨询委员会在香港地区开展对基本法征求意见的工作。今天，咨询委员会代表就基本法有关问题与内地起草委员交流了意见。
    香港基本法起草委员会主任委员姬鹏飞、副主任委员王汉斌以及正副秘书长李后、鲁平出席了座谈会。
