Title: Reply to Readers' Questions
Author:
Date: 1989-03-29
Edition: 2

Yesterday, the second page of this newspaper published an article titled "A New Hospital in Beijing Specializes in Treating Stones." Many readers have called to inquire about the location of the hospital. The hospital is situated within the campus of the First Affiliated Hospital of Beijing Medical College, located at Beijing's Xisi District.

### 答读者问

1989-03-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　答读者问
    昨日本报二版刊登《北京一家新医院专治结石》，读者纷纷来电询问院址。该院位于北京西四北京医科大学第一附属医院院内。
