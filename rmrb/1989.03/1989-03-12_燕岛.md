Title: The Island of Swallows
Author: Liudaqi
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 7

In the Indian Ocean, there is a Seychelles archipelago, which includes a small island named Desnœufs Island, also known as the famous Swallow Island. Originally, this island was home to over 3.5 million swallows. From May to October each year, these birds lay their eggs, and the entire island can yield over 42 million eggs. Therefore, people also call it the "Egg Island."

### 燕岛
刘大逵
1989-03-12
第7版(国际副刊)
专栏：

　　燕岛
    印度洋上有一个塞舌尔群岛，其中有一个小岛叫德斯纳夫岛，是驰名于世的燕岛。原来岛上有３５０多万只海燕生活着，每年５月至１０月是它们生蛋的时候，全岛可以拾到４２０多万只蛋，因此人们又叫它为“蛋岛”。    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
                                        ·刘大逵·
