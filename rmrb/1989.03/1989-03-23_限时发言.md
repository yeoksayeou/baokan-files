Title: Limited Time for Speeches
Author: Meng Xiaoyun
Date: 1989-03-23
Edition: 3

During the discussion of the government work report by the CPPCC Democratic League group, speeches were lively. The meeting chairperson had to announce that each person's speech should not exceed 10 minutes, with a bell ringing at 8 minutes as a "warning." Today, there were as many as 33 speakers, and the warning bell rang continuously in the meeting room.

### 限时发言
孟晓云
1989-03-23
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　限时发言
    政协民盟小组讨论政府工作报告发言踊跃，会议主持人不得不宣布，每人发言不得超过１０分钟，８分钟鸣铃“警告”。今天发言者达３３人之多，会议室里“警”铃不断。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（孟晓云）
