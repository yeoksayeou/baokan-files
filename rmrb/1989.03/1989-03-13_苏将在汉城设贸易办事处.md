Title: The Soviet Union to Establish a Trade Office in Seoul
Author:
Date: 1989-03-13
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 11th.

Seoul - The South Korean news agency Yonhap reported on the 9th that the Soviet Chamber of Commerce will establish a trade office in Seoul on April 3rd.

This was stated by a spokesman for the "Korea Trade Promotion Corporation." He also said that the "Korea Trade Promotion Corporation" will establish its own office in Moscow at the beginning of next month. South Korea will also establish mutual trade offices with Poland and Bulgaria next month.

### 苏将在汉城设贸易办事处

1989-03-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏将在汉城设贸易办事处
    新华社平壤３月１１日电  汉城消息：南朝鲜联合通讯社９日报道，苏联工商会４月３日将在汉城设立贸易办事处。
    这是南朝鲜的“大韩贸易振兴公社”的一位发言人说的。他还说，“大韩贸易振兴公社”下月初在莫斯科也将设立自己的办事处。南朝鲜同波兰和保加利亚互设贸易办事处的事也将在下月进行。
