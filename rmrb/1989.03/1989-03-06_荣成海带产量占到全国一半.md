Title: Rongcheng's Kelp Production Accounts for Half of the Nation's Total
Author: Liu Qiang
Date: 1989-03-06
Edition: 2

Rongcheng's kelp production in 1988 reached 100,000 tons (dried salted product), accounting for half of the nation's total kelp production. Since 1980, Rongcheng City has implemented the practice of inter-cultivating shellfish and kelp, increasing water utilization efficiency by 60% compared to single-cultivating kelp.

### 荣成海带产量占到全国一半
刘强
1989-03-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　荣成海带产量占到全国一半
    本报讯  山东荣成市一九八八年海带（盐干品）总产已达十万吨，占全国海带总产的二分之一。一九八○年以来，荣成市实行贝藻间养，使水体利用率比单养海带提高百分之六十。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（刘强）
