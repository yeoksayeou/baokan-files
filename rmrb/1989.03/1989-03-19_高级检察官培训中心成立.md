Title: Senior Prosecutors Training Center Established
Author: Mao Lei
Date: 1989-03-19
Edition: 2

The Senior Prosecutors Training Center has been established. The People's Daily reported from Beijing on March 17 that Liu Fuzhi, the Prosecutor General of the Supreme People's Prosecutorate, announced today that the China Senior Prosecutors Training Center has been officially established.

This training center aims to provide professional education for senior prosecutors and their potential successors, and to conduct training for job qualifications and positions.

The center will primarily enroll prosecutors at the bureau level or above who are under the age of 50. They must have a university degree in law or equivalent, and generally have 5 to 10 years of experience in legal or prosecutorial work. Liu Fuzhi will serve as the chairman of the center's committee.

### 高级检察官培训中心成立
毛磊
1989-03-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　高级检察官培训中心成立
    本报北京三月十七日讯  记者毛磊报道：最高人民检察院检察长刘复之今天在北京宣布：中国高级检察官培训中心正式成立。
    这一培训中心旨在对高级检察官及其后备人才进行职业教育，开展任职资格和岗位职务培训。
    该培训中心将主要招收五十岁以下的在职处级以上检察官，他们需具有大学法律专业学历或同等学历，还要求一般具有五至十年政法工作或检察工作的经历。刘复之出任该中心委员会主任委员。
