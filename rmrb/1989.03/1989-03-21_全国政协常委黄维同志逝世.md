Title: National Committee Member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Huang Wei Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-03-21
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 20th.

National Committee Member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Huang Wei passed away. Huang Wei, a member of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, died at 3:20 AM on March 20th in Beijing due to a sudden heart attack. He was 85 years old.

Huang Wei was born in January 1904 in Guixi, Jiangxi. He joined the military at an early age and graduated from the first class of the Whampoa Military Academy. He then served in various high-ranking positions in the Nationalist Army, including division commander, corps commander, and army group commander. He participated in the "August 13" Battle of Shanghai. After 1975, he served as a special member of the Committee on Historical and Literary Materials of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. He was a member of the Standing Committee of the Fifth and Sixth National Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 全国政协常委黄维同志逝世

1989-03-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全国政协常委黄维同志逝世
    新华社北京３月２０日电  第七届全国政协常委黄维同志，因心脏病突发，经抢救无效，不幸于３月２０日凌晨３时２０分在北京逝世，终年８５岁。
    黄维同志，江西贵溪人，１９０４年１月出生。早年从军，黄埔军校第一期毕业后，历任国民党部队师长、军长、兵团司令等高级军职，曾参加“八·一三”淞沪抗战。１９７５年后，任全国政协文史资料委员会专员。他是全国政协第五、六届常务委员。
