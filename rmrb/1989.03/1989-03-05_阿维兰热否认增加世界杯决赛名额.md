Title: Avellaneda Denies Increasing World Cup Final Slots
Author: Wú Yìhóng
Date: 1989-03-05
Edition: 4

International Federation of Football Association (FIFA) President João Havelange today denied at a press conference in Riyadh that FIFA is planning to increase the number of slots for Asian and African countries in the World Cup final stage.

He is currently leading a FIFA delegation to Saudi Arabia to watch the fifth FIFA World Youth Championship.

When asked by a reporter whether, given the continuous improvement in the level of football in Asian and African countries, FIFA is considering increasing the number of teams from Asia and Africa in the World Cup final stage, Havelange said that if Asian and African countries want to increase their number of participants, they must first demonstrate the appropriate level of performance in the Olympic football tournament. He said: "So far, no African or Asian team has reached the final or semi-final of the Olympic football tournament, and there are three slots each for Africa and Asia in the Olympics."

### 阿维兰热否认增加世界杯决赛名额
吴毅宏
1989-03-05
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：

　　阿维兰热否认增加世界杯决赛名额
    新华社利雅得３月２日电  （记者吴毅宏）国际足联主席阿维兰热今天在这里举行的记者招待会上，否认国际足联准备增加亚非国家参加世界杯足球决赛阶段比赛的名额。
    他目前正率国际足联代表团在沙特阿拉伯观看第五届世界青年足球锦标赛。
    当有记者问，由于亚非国家足球运动水平不断提高，国际足联是否考虑增加亚、非国家参加世界杯足球决赛阶段比赛的队数？阿维兰热表示，如果亚非国家想增加参赛队数，首先要在奥运会足球赛中表现出应有的水平。他说：“到目前为止，还没有哪一个非洲队或亚洲队在奥运会足球赛中进入决赛和半决赛，而奥运会，非洲和亚洲各有３个名额。”
