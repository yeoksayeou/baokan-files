Date: 1989-03-17
Edition: 2

Outstanding Military Commander and Excellent Political Logistics Leader Tang Tianji's Funeral Held in Beijing

New China News Agency, Beijing, March 16 - The outstanding military commander and excellent political logistics leader of our army, Comrade Tang Tianji, passed away in Beijing on February 20. Party and state leaders and over 500 officers and soldiers from the Beijing garrison paid their respects to Comrade Tang Tianji's body yesterday at the Eight Treasures Mountain Revolutionary Cemetery Hall.

Comrades Zhao Ziyang, Deng Xiaoping, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, Chen Yun, Li Xiannian, Peng Zhen, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Qiao Shi, Hu Qili, Yao Yilin, Song Ping, Hu Yaobang, Bo Yibo, Song Renqiong, Jiang Hua, Li Desheng, Yu Qiuyi, Zhang Aiping, Huang Zhen, Hong Xuezhi, Xiao Jingguang, Li Jukui, and Li Da sent wreaths.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central Advisory Commission, the Central Discipline Inspection Commission, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Central Military Commission, the Military Commission Discipline Inspection Commission, the Central Organization Department, the Ministry of National Defense, and various general departments of the People's Liberation Army sent wreaths.

Leaders who paid their respects to the body of Comrade Tang Tianji, an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, a loyal and tested communist warrior, and a revolutionary of the proletariat, and sent wreaths include: Wang Zhen, Qin Jiwei, Wang Ping, Yang Dezhi, Xiao Ke, Song Shilun, Geng Biao, Habib, Liao Hansheng, Liu Huaqing, Zhao Nanqi, and others.

Comrade Tang Tianji was born in Anren County, Hunan Province, in 1904. He joined the Communist Youth League of China in 1925 and became a member of the Communist Party of China the following year. During the First Revolutionary Civil War, he participated in the Northern Expedition and the August 1 Uprising in Nanchang. During the Second Revolutionary Civil War, he participated in the Jianxi-Sichuan Revolutionary Base and the famous Long March of 25,000 miles. After the Red Army reached the Shaanxi-Gansu Soviet Area, he participated in the famous Eastern and Western Expeditions. During the War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression, Comrade Tang Tianji served as the deputy director of the Eighth Route Army Field Political Department and the Armed Forces Department of the War Mobilization Committee, commander of the Jin-Yu Border Guerrilla Corps, director of the Eighth Route Army Liaison Office in the First War Zone, political commissar of the 129th Division's New First Brigade and the Taihang Fourth District, and commander of the Taihang Fourth District, among other positions. During the War of Liberation, Comrade Tang Tianji served as the secretary of the Jilin Provincial Party Committee and political commissar of the Jilin Military Region, deputy political commissar and director of the First Command Headquarters of the Northeast Front, deputy political commissar and director of the Political Department of the First Army Group of the Northeast, and political commissar of the Changchun Military Control Commission, deputy political commissar and director of the Political Department of the 12th Army Group, and deputy commander and deputy political commissar of the Hunan Military Region, among other positions. After the founding of New China, Comrade Tang Tianji served as the political commissar of the 21st Army Group and director of the Guibei Bandit Suppression Committee, commander of the Hunan Military Region, commander and political commissar of the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project, deputy director of the Financial Commission of the Central-South Military and Political Commission and director of the Construction Engineering Department, political commissar of the Air Defense Forces of the Military Commission, and first deputy director of the Financial Department of the Military Commission, among other positions. Comrade Tang Tianji was elected as a member of the Standing Committee of the Fifth National People's Congress, a member of the Standing Committee of the Fourth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and a member of the Central Discipline Inspection Commission. In 1955, he was awarded the rank of lieutenant general.

Comrade Tang Tianji was loyal to the cause of communism, adhered to party principles, considered the overall situation, understood the big picture, and always put the interests of the party and the people first. He was open-minded, upright, strict with himself, lenient with others, did not calculate personal gains and losses, was honest, clean, modest, cautious, united with comrades, had a democratic style, was fair and upright, connected with the masses, and lived a simple life.

### 优秀军事指挥员、卓越的政治后勤工作领导者  唐天际遗体告别仪式在京举行

1989-03-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　优秀军事指挥员、卓越的政治后勤工作领导者
    唐天际遗体告别仪式在京举行
    新华社北京３月１６日电  我军优秀的军事指挥员、卓越的政治后勤工作领导者唐天际同志于２月２０日在北京逝世。党和国家领导人及驻京部队指战员５００余人，昨天在八宝山革命公墓礼堂向唐天际遗体告别。
    赵紫阳、邓小平、杨尚昆、李鹏、陈云、李先念、彭真、徐向前、聂荣臻、乔石、胡启立、姚依林、宋平、胡耀邦、薄一波、宋任穷、江华、李德生、余秋里、张爱萍、黄镇、洪学智、肖劲光、李聚奎、李达等，送了花圈。
    中共中央、中央顾问委员会、中央纪律检查委员会、全国人大常委会、全国政协、中央军委、军委纪委、中央组织部、国防部，解放军各总部等单位，送了花圈。
    向中国共产党优秀党员、久经考验的忠诚的共产主义战士、无产阶级革命家唐天际同志遗体告别并送花圈的领导同志有：王震、秦基伟、王平、杨得志、肖克、宋时轮、耿飚、习仲勋、廖汉生、刘华清、赵南起等。
    唐天际同志１９０４年出生在湖南省安仁县。他１９２５年加入中国共产主义青年团，翌年转为中国共产党党员。第一次国内革命战争时期，他参加了北阀战争和“八一”南昌起义。第二次国内革命战争时期，他参加了井冈山斗争和举世闻名的二万五千里长征。红一方面军到达陕甘苏区后，他参加了著名的东征和西征。抗日战争时期，唐天际同志任八路军野战政治部民运部和战地总动员委员会武装部副部长，晋豫边游击支队司令，八路军驻第一战区联络处主任，１２９师新一旅、太行四分区政委，太岳四分区司令员等职。解放战争时期，唐天际同志任东北吉东省委书记兼吉东军区政委，吉林军区和东北前线第一指挥所副政委兼主任，东北第一兵团副政委兼政治部主任和长春军管会主任，１２兵团副政委兼政治部主任，湖南军区副司令员兼副政委等职。新中国成立后，唐天际同志曾任２１兵团政委兼桂北剿匪委员会主任，湖南军区司令员，荆江分洪工程总指挥兼工程部队司令员、政委，中南军政委员会财委副主任兼建筑工程部部长，军委防空部队政委，军委财务部第一副部长等职。唐天际同志曾被选为第五届全国人大常委，全国政协第四届常委，中央纪律检查委员会委员。１９５５年被授予中将军衔。
    唐天际同志忠于共产主义事业，坚持党性原则，顾大局，识大体，处处以党和人民的利益为重。他襟怀坦白，光明磊落，严于律己，宽于待人，不计个人得失，为人正直，廉洁奉公，谦虚谨慎，团结同志，作风民主，公道正派，联系群众，生活简朴。
