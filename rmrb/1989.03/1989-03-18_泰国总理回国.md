Title: Thai Prime Minister Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1989-03-18
Edition: 2

Thai Prime Minister Returns Home

	New China News Agency, Beijing, March 17: Thai Prime Minister Chatichai Choonhavan and his wife departed Beijing this afternoon, concluding their official visit to China. They boarded a special plane to return home. Premier Li Peng and his wife, along with others, went to the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse to see them off.

	Li Peng told Chatichai, "You are welcome to visit China anytime, in any capacity." He presented Chatichai with a commemorative album containing photos from his visit to China.

	Chatichai said, "My meeting with Chairman Deng Xiaoping this morning was very beneficial." He expressed the hope that Li Peng could visit Thailand again.

### 泰国总理回国

1989-03-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　泰国总理回国
    新华社北京３月１７日电  泰王国总理差猜·春哈旺和夫人今天下午结束对中国的正式访问，乘专机离开北京回国。国务院总理李鹏和夫人等到钓鱼台国宾馆为贵宾们送行。
    李鹏对差猜总理说，“欢迎您经常来中国，以任何名义都可以。”他向差猜赠送了一本嵌有他访华照片的纪念册。
    差猜说：“我今天上午同邓小平主席的谈话很有益。”他表示希望李鹏能再次访问泰国。
