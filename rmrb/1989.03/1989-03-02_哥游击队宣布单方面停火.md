Title: Colombian Guerrilla Group Announces Unilateral Ceasefire
Author:
Date: 1989-03-02
Edition: 3

The largest guerrilla organization in Colombia, the "Revolutionary Armed Forces," announced that it would begin a unilateral ceasefire starting at 18:00 on February 28th.

In a ceasefire statement issued today, the guerrilla organization said that it decided to implement a unilateral ceasefire "to contribute to the realization of domestic peace."

Among the six major guerrilla organizations in Colombia, the "April 19 Movement" reached a peace agreement with the government in January this year.

### 哥游击队宣布单方面停火

1989-03-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    哥游击队宣布单方面停火
    哥伦比亚最大的游击队组织“革命武装部队”宣布从二月二十八日十八时开始实行单方面停火。
    这支游击队组织今天发表的停火公报说，它决定单方面停火是“要为实现国内和平作出贡献”。
    在哥伦比亚六支主要游击队组织中，“四·一九运动”今年一月已与政府达成和平协议。
