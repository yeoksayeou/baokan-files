Title: The Second National Youth Games Weightlifting Preliminaries Conclude
Author: Hé Huīfēi
Date: 1989-03-16
Edition: 4

The second National Youth Games weightlifting preliminaries have concluded. Two national youth records were broken.

A Xinhua News Agency report from Guangzhou, March 14: (Reporter He Huifly) The five-day second National Youth Games weightlifting preliminaries ended today in Shilong Town, Guangdong Province. Three athletes broke two national youth weightlifting records.

This preliminary competition included 11 weight classes with 110 athletes qualifying for the finals. Due to the adoption of new international rules in this preliminary competition, the time from the athlete's name being called to lifting the barbell was reduced from the original 2 minutes to 1 minute and 30 seconds, speeding up the competition pace and making the atmosphere more intense.

### 第二届全国青运会举重预赛结束  两项全国青年纪录被刷新
何惠飞
1989-03-16
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　第二届全国青运会举重预赛结束
    两项全国青年纪录被刷新
    新华社广州３月１４日电  （记者何惠飞）历时５天的第二届全国青少年运动会举重预赛今天在广东省石龙镇结束，３人３次刷新２项全国青年举重纪录。
    这次预赛共有１１个级别，１１０名选手获决赛资格。由于这次预赛采用了国际新规则，运动员从点名到上场将杠铃提起由原来的２分钟缩短到１分３０秒，使比赛节奏加快，气氛也显得更为紧张。
