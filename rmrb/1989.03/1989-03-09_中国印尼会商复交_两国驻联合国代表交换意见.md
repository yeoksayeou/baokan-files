Title: China and Indonesia Discuss Resumption of Diplomatic Relations
Author: Liú Kāichéng
Date: 1989-03-09
Edition: 1

	Chinese and Indonesian representatives in the United Nations exchanged views on the normalization of diplomatic relations between the two countries on March 6. The exchange was based on an agreement reached in Tokyo in February 1989. The two sides discussed technical issues related to the normalization of diplomatic relations for the first time.

	The Permanent Representative of the People's Republic of China to the United Nations, Ambassador Li Lùyě, and the Permanent Representative of the Republic of Indonesia to the United Nations, Ambassador Satresna, held the discussions.
