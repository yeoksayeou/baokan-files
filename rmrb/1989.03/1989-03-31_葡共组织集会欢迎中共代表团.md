Title: Portuguese Communist Party Holds Rally to Welcome Chinese Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-03-31
Edition: 4

The Portuguese Communist Party held a rally with nearly a thousand people in the central city of Barreiro on the evening of March 29 to warmly welcome the Chinese Communist Party delegation led by Song Ping, a member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee and head of the Organization Department.

Song Ping, at the invitation, spoke at the rally about China's domestic situation and its independent foreign policy.

The General Secretary of the Portuguese Communist Party, Alvaro Cunhal, said at the rally that since the 12th Congress of the Portuguese Communist Party declared the normalization of relations between the two parties last year, the relationship between the two parties has seen new developments.

### 葡共组织集会欢迎中共代表团

1989-03-31
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　葡共组织集会欢迎中共代表团
    新华社里斯本３月２９日电  葡萄牙共产党２９日晚在中部城市巴雷罗举行近千人的群众集会，热烈欢迎以中共中央政治局委员、中组部部长宋平为首的中共代表团访问葡萄牙。
    宋平应邀在群众集会上介绍了中国的国内形势和中国独立自主的对外政策。
    葡共总书记库尼亚尔在集会上说，自从去年葡共十二大宣布葡、中两党关系完全正常化以来，两党关系有了新的发展。
