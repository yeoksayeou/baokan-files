Title: The Philippines No Longer Assists Vietnamese Refugees in Relocating to Third Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-03-22
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency,

The Philippines will no longer assist newly arrived Vietnamese refugees in relocating to third countries. This decision took effect on March 21st, as announced by the Philippine Department of Foreign Affairs on the 21st.

### 菲律宾不再帮助  越难民去第三国

1989-03-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　菲律宾不再帮助
    越难民去第三国
    菲律宾外交部２１日宣布：今后不再帮助新近抵菲的越南难民到第三国去定居。这一决定２１日生效。
                                               （新华社）
