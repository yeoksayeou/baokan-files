Title: Anti-Government Protests in Chile
Author:
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 3

	Some Chilean youths held an anti-government protest in the center of the capital, Santiago, on the 11th, and clashed with the police, resulting in 15 arrests.

	This protest occurred during the government's celebration of the 8th anniversary of the constitution's implementation. This constitution, drafted by the military government, has been condemned by the majority of opposition parties. They have repeatedly called for its amendment.

### 智利发生反政府示威

1989-03-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　智利发生反政府示威
    新华社圣地亚哥３月１２日电  智利一些青年１１日在首都圣地亚哥市中心举行反政府示威，并与警察发生冲突，１５人被捕。
    这次抗议示威是在政府庆祝宪法生效８周年时举行的。这部由军政府制订的宪法受到多数反对党的谴责。他们多次要求修改宪法。
