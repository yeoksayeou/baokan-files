Title: Ding Guanggen Hosts a Banquet for Taiwanese Journalists Covering the Two Sessions
Author:
Date: 1989-03-23
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 21st.

Ding Guanggen, Director of the Taiwan Affairs Office of the State Council, hosted a banquet tonight at the Great Hall of the People to welcome a group of Taiwanese journalists who came to Beijing to cover the sessions of the National People's Congress and the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

The Taiwanese journalists attending the banquet included reporters from the *China Times*, *China Times Express*, *Independent Evening News*, *United Daily News*, *Taiwan Daily News*, *Commercial Times*, and *World Outlook* magazine. Ding Guanggen sincerely hoped that the Taiwanese journalists would convey information from the mainland during their coverage, help eliminate misunderstandings, promote mutual understanding, and build trust.

### 丁关根款待采访两会的台湾记者

1989-03-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　丁关根款待采访两会的台湾记者
    新华社北京３月２１日电  国务院台湾事务办公室主任丁关根今晚在人民大会堂设宴，款待部分前来北京采访全国人大和政协会议的台湾记者。
    出席宴会的台湾记者有：《中国时报》、《中时晚报》、《自立晚报》、《联合报》、《台湾时报》、《工商时报》和《天下》杂志的记者等。丁关根衷心希望台湾记者在采访中，传递大陆信息，帮助消除隔阂，增进了解，增进信任。
