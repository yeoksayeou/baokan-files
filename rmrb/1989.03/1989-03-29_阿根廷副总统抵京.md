Title: Argentine Vice President Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-03-29
Edition: 2

At the invitation of the Chinese government, Argentine Vice President and Senate President Victor Martinez and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this evening to pay an official visit to China.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhu Qizhen, Argentine Ambassador to China Osorio Alarana, and others welcomed the guests at the airport.

### 阿根廷副总统抵京

1989-03-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　阿根廷副总统抵京
    新华社北京３月２８日电  应中国政府的邀请，阿根廷副总统兼参议长维克托·马丁内斯和夫人一行今晚乘飞机抵达北京，对中国进行正式访问。
    外交部副部长朱启祯、阿根廷驻中国大使奥索里奥·阿拉纳等到机场迎接。
