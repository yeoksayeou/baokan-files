Title: Comrade Ni Nanshan Passes Away in Nanchang
Author:
Date: 1989-03-23
Edition: 2

Comrade Ni Nanshan passed away in Nanchang due to illness. The New China News Agency, Nanchang, March 22: Comrade Ni Nanshan, former political commissar of the Fujian Provincial Military Region, passed away due to illness on January 11 in Nanchang, aged 78.

Comrade Ni Nanshan was a native of Dongzhi County, Anhui Province. He joined the Communist Party of China in 1935 and the same year participated in the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army. He held various positions including township and district Soviet chairman, district party secretary, prison warden, section chief, brigade commander, divisional commander, deputy commander of the provincial military region, provincial party secretary, and vice chairman of the provincial political consultative conference. In 1955, he was awarded the rank of Major General.

### 倪南山同志在南昌病逝

1989-03-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　倪南山同志在南昌病逝
    新华社南昌３月２２日电  福建省军区原政委倪南山因病于１月１１日在南昌逝世，终年７８岁。
    倪南山同志是安徽省东至县人，１９３５年加入中国共产党，同年参加中国工农红军；历任乡和区苏维埃主席、区委书记、典狱长、科长、支队长、军分区司令员、省军区副司令员、省委书记、省政协副主席等职。１９５５年被授予少将军衔。
