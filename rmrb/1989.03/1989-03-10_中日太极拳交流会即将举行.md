Title: Sino-Japanese Tai Chi Exchange to Be Held
Author: Yu Lu Ren
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 4

The 1989 Sino-Japanese Tai Chi Exchange Competition will be held on March 16 in Shanghai. This large-scale exchange event between the Chinese and Japanese martial arts communities is jointly organized by the Chinese Wushu Association and the Japanese Tai Chi Federation, and hosted by the Shanghai Municipal Sports Federation.

The Japanese delegation will consist of 252 members, who will perform a collective Tai Chi demonstration involving 200 people at the opening ceremony. They will also participate in a Tai Chi seminar.

### 中日太极拳交流会即将举行
于鲁人
1989-03-10
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　中日太极拳交流会即将举行
    本报北京３月９日讯  由日本３０个太极拳团体参加的１９８９年中日太极拳交流比赛大会将于３月１６日在上海市举行。这届中日武术界的大规模交流活动是由中国武术协会、日本太极拳联盟主办，上海市体育总会承办的。
    日方参加人数多达２５２名，他们将在开幕式上表演由２００人参加的２４式太极拳集体表演，并参加太极拳讲习会。 　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（于鲁人）
