Title: Libyan Government Reorganization
Author:
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 3

The General People's Congress of Libya held a meeting from March 2nd to 9th and passed a resolution to reorganize the government.

According to this resolution, the reorganized General People's Committee (government) consists of 19 members, with all members of the previous government retaining their positions. Additionally, a female Secretary of Education (Minister) and a Secretary of Light Industry were appointed.

### 利比亚政府改组

1989-03-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　利比亚政府改组
    利比亚总人民代表大会３月２日至９日举行会议，通过了一项改组政府的决议。
    根据这项决议，改组后的总人民委员会（政府）有１９名成员，上届政府的全体成员均留任。此外，还任命了一名女教育秘书（部长）和一名轻工业秘书。
