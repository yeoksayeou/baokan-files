Title: Liao Ning Province's Dong Gou County Ensures Water Supply for Grain Production
Author: Ding Peizhong
Date: 1989-03-30
Edition: 5

Liao Ning Province's Dong Gou County has implemented measures such as excavating water sources, constructing dikes and canals, increasing anti-drought equipment, and enforcing water-saving measures to ensure water supply for grain production this year. The image shows water conservancy department workers hurriedly installing 386 newly added water pumps.
