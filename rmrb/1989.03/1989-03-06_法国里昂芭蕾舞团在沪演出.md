Title: The Lyon Ballet Company Performs in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1989-03-06
Edition: 4

The Lyon Ballet Company of the Lyon Opera House recently premiered the modern ballet "Cinderella" in Shanghai, receiving waves of applause from the audience. Shanghai's renowned young ballet dancer Wang Qifeng told reporters after watching the performance: "The French dancers' performance was very successful. The entire production of 'Cinderella' is innovative and unique, filled with modern elements and a sense of humor."

### 法国里昂芭蕾舞团在沪演出

1989-03-06
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　法国里昂芭蕾舞团在沪演出
    法国里昂市歌剧院芭蕾舞团日前在上海首演现代芭蕾舞剧《灰姑娘》，博得观众阵阵掌声。上海著名青年芭蕾舞演员汪齐风观后对记者说：“法国舞蹈家们的演出很成功，整台《灰姑娘》编排得新颖、别致，充满现代气息和幽默感。”
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
