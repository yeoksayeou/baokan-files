Title: Reflections on Life
Author: Hu Zhongkang
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 8

    Exposing ugliness is a form of beauty;
    Because ugliness exists,
    Therefore, beauty needs protection.

    To measure a person's true height,
    Always remove the high heels and tall hats.

    The more lifeless something is,
    The more it likes to put on airs.

    Flowers are fragrant even in the dark;
    Seeds are alive even in the soil;
    Gold is dazzling even in the trash.

    It can wake the dead,
    But it cannot wake those who feign death.

    Appearance, whether beautiful or ugly, has nothing to do with the mirror;
    Character, whether high or low, has nothing to do with position;
    Talent, whether great or small, has nothing to do with age;
    Truth, whether present or not, has nothing to do with power.

    The master is asleep,
    The servant still seeks meaning in the snores.

    The vine clinging to the tree trunk,
    Sometimes climbs higher than the trunk itself.

    Life,
    Cannot always walk silently.

    It should be like a hurricane,
    Roaring;
    It should be like waves,
    Roaring!

### 微思集
胡仲仟
1989-03-10
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　微思集
    胡仲仟
    揭露丑，是一种美；
    因为丑在，
    所以美需要保护。
    量一个人的真正高度，
    总是脱去高跟和高帽子。
    越是缺乏生命力的东西，
    越爱装腔作势。
    花，在黑暗中也是香的；
    种子，在泥土中也是活的；
    黄金，在垃圾中也是夺目的。
    叫得醒死人，
    却叫不醒装死的人。
    容貌美丑，与镜子无关；
    品格高低，与职位无关；
    才能大小，与年龄无关；
    真理有无，与权力无关。
    主子睡着了，
    奴才还要从鼾声中寻找意义。
    攀附在树干上的藤萝，
    有时比树干爬得还高。
    生命，
    不能总是默默地行走。
    它要像飓风，
    生出咆哮；
    它要像波涛，
    发出怒吼！
