Title: Chinese Athletes Win Two Gold Medals at East German Weightlifting Competition
Author: Wáng Défēng
Date: 1989-03-19
Edition: 3

Chinese athletes Liu Shoubin and Qiu Yuanfu won two gold medals today at the "Lance Cup" international weightlifting invitational held in Meissen, East Germany.

In the 52-kilogram category, Qiu Yuanfu achieved a total score of 252.5 kilograms (snatch 112.5 kilograms, clean and jerk 140 kilograms), securing the first place. The Cuban athlete Pedro Negrin finished second with a total score of 250 kilograms.

Liu Shoubin dominated the 60-kilogram category with a total score of 282.5 kilograms (snatch 130 kilograms, clean and jerk 152.5 kilograms). He attempted to break the Asian record of 136 kilograms in the snatch but was unsuccessful. The East German athlete Mark Spawal took the silver medal with a total score of 280 kilograms.

The 56-kilogram category had only one participant, the Cuban athlete Juan Tavella, who won the first place with a total score of 225 kilograms.

In the evening, the 67.5-kilogram and 75-kilogram categories concluded. Soviet athletes Valery and Kasap took the first places with total scores of 310 kilograms and 335 kilograms, respectively. East German athletes Hossler and Hoffman secured the second places in these categories with total scores of 290 kilograms and 317.5 kilograms, respectively.

The East German international weightlifting invitational is in its 18th year. This year's competition features athletes from 15 countries. Chinese athletes are participating for the first time, and all matches will conclude on the 19th.

### 我选手获民主德国举重赛两枚金牌
王德峰
1989-03-19
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　我选手获民主德国举重赛两枚金牌
    新华社柏林３月１７日电  （记者王德峰）我国选手刘寿斌和邱元富今天在民主德国迈森市举行的“兰剑杯”国际举重邀请赛中，获得２枚金牌。
    在５２公斤级的比赛中，邱元富以２５２．５公斤（抓举１１２．５公斤、挺举１４０公斤）的总成绩获第１名，古巴选手佩·内格林以２５０公斤的总成绩名列第２。
    刘寿斌在６０公斤级比赛中独占鳌头，总成绩为２８２．５公斤（抓举１３０公斤、挺举１５２．５公斤）。他在抓举中曾试图冲击１３６公斤的亚洲纪录，但未能如愿。民主德国选手马·施帕瓦尔以总成绩２８０公斤获得银牌。
    参加５６公斤级比赛的只有古巴选手胡安·塔韦拉１人。在无人竞争的情况下，他以总成绩２２５公斤获第１名。
    在晚间结束的６７．５公斤级和７５公斤级的比赛中，苏联选手瓦列里和卡萨普分别以３１０公斤和３３５公斤的总成绩名列榜首，民主德国的两名选手许斯勒和霍夫曼以２９０公斤和３１７．５公斤的总成绩分别获得这两个级别的第２名。
    民主德国国际举重邀请赛今年已经是第１８届，参加本届比赛的有１５个国家的大力士。我国选手是首次参赛，全部比赛将于１９日结束。
