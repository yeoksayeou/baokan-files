Title: Switzerland Provides Documents on Marcos' Assets to the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, March 13th.

Geneva's investigating judge Steiger signed an order over the weekend to hand over documents related to the assets of former Philippine President Marcos in Switzerland to the Philippine judicial authorities.

According to this decision, Switzerland will transfer the bank documents of Marcos' deposits frozen in four banks in Geneva to the Philippine judicial authorities. Marcos' deposits in these four banks amount to 10 million Swiss francs. The total amount of Marcos' deposits frozen by Switzerland reaches 1 billion Swiss francs.

### 瑞士向菲提供马科斯财产文件

1989-03-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　瑞士向菲提供马科斯财产文件
    新华社日内瓦３月１３日电  日内瓦预审法官斯台伯格上周末签署一项法令，同意将与菲律宾前总统马科斯存在瑞士的财产有关的文件移交给菲律宾司法机构。
    根据这一决定，瑞士将把冻结在日内瓦４家银行的马科斯存款的银行材料转交给菲律宾司法机构。马科斯在这四家银行的存款为１０００万瑞士法郎。瑞士冻结的马科斯存款总数达十亿瑞士法郎。
