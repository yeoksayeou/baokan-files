Title: Winner of El Salvador's Election Shares Political Views
Author:
Date: 1989-03-22
Edition: 4

San Salvador's election winner Alfredo Cristiani said on March 20 that he will continue negotiations with the guerrillas to resolve the eight-year-long civil war.

Cristiani emphasized that the democratic process in El Salvador is non-negotiable, and the constitution cannot be subject to negotiation.

He also stated that he will fulfill the Central American Peace Agreement signed by the current President Duarte of El Salvador and other Central American leaders.

He announced that he will privatize the banks that were nationalized by the current government.

### 萨大选获胜者谈政治见解  解决历时八年内战

1989-03-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　萨大选获胜者谈政治见解        
    解决历时八年内战
    新华社墨西哥城３月２０日电  圣萨尔瓦多消息：在３月１９日萨尔瓦多大选中得票最多的阿尔弗雷多·克里斯蒂亚尼２０日指出，他将继续同游击队进行谈判，以解决历时８年之久的内战。
    克里斯蒂亚尼强调说，萨尔瓦多的民主进程是不容谈判的，宪法也不能加以谈判。
    他还指出，他将会履行由萨尔瓦多现总统杜阿尔特和其他中美洲国家领导人签署的中美洲和平协议。
    他宣布将对现政府实施国有化的银行实行私有化。
