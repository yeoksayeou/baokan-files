Title: Huang Zhihong Wins Silver in Women's Shot Put at World Indoor Athletics Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-03-07
Edition: 4

Chinese athlete Huang Zhihong won the silver medal in the women's shot put event at the second World Indoor Athletics Championships held in Budapest on March 5. The gold medal was taken by West German athlete Lorsch with a throw of 20.45 meters. Huang Zhihong secured the silver medal with a throw of 20.25 meters. Another Chinese athlete, Li Meisu, achieved her best throw of 18.08 meters, placing eighth.

### 黄志红获世界室内田径赛女子铅球亚军

1989-03-07
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　黄志红获世界室内田径赛女子铅球亚军
    中国选手黄志红３月５日在布达佩斯举行的第二届世界室内田径锦标赛上夺得女子铅球亚军。冠军被联邦德国选手洛施夺走，成绩是２０米４５。黄志红以２０米２５的成绩夺得亚军。中国另一名选手李梅素最好的成绩是１８米０８，名列第８。
