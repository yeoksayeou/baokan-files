Title: Ugandan President Museveni Arrives in China for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1989-03-21
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency

At the invitation of Chairman Yang Shangkun, Ugandan President Yoweri Museveni and his wife arrived in China today for a state visit from March 21 to 25.

Yoweri Museveni was born in 1944 in Kiyamat in the Ankole district of southern Uganda. In 1972, he established the National Resistance Movement. In March 1979, after the formation of the Uganda National Liberation Front and the Uganda People's Liberation Army, he served as the executive committee member and deputy chairman of the military committee. In June 1980, after the founding of the Uganda Patriotic Movement, he became its chairman and in December of the same year, he ran as the presidential candidate of the party in the national elections. In February 1981, he established the National Resistance Army and served as its commander. In January 1985, he became the interim chairman of the National Resistance Movement. On January 25, 1986, the National Resistance Army seized power, and on January 29, he assumed the presidency.

### 乌干达总统穆塞韦尼  今天到达我国访问

1989-03-21
第1版(要闻)
专栏：人物介绍

　　乌干达总统穆塞韦尼        
    今天到达我国访问
    应杨尚昆主席的邀请，乌干达共和国总统约韦里·穆塞韦尼偕夫人于本月２１日至２５日对我国进行国事访问。
    约韦里·穆塞韦尼总统１９４４年生于乌干达南方省安科莱区基亚马特镇。１９７２年组建民族救国阵线。１９７９年３月乌干达全国解放阵线和乌干达人民解放军成立后，任执行委员兼军事委员会副主席。１９８０年６月乌干达爱国运动成立后任主席，同年１２月作为该党总统候选人参加全国大选。１９８１年２月创建人民抵抗军，任司令。１９８５年１月任全国抵抗运动临时主席。１９８６年１月２５日全国抵抗军夺取政权，２９日出任总统。
    （新华社）
