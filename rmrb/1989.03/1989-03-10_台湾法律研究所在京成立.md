Title: Taiwan Legal Research Institute Established in Beijing
Author: Fang Jin
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 2

	The Taiwan Legal Research Institute was recently established in Beijing by a group of legal experts and scholars from the city who volunteered to form it.

	As economic, cultural, and social exchanges between the mainland and Taiwan have expanded in recent years, along with an increase in the number of people visiting relatives, a series of legal issues have emerged. These issues span almost all legal domains, from civil law to criminal law. The institute aims to study these problems and propose measures to prevent and resolve legal conflicts arising from cross-strait interactions to the relevant authorities.

	Currently, the institute will focus its research on Taiwan's civil law and commercial law, publish periodicals, and organize academic seminars.

### 台湾法律研究所在京成立
方锦
1989-03-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　台湾法律研究所在京成立
    本报北京三月九日讯  由北京部分法学专家和学者自愿组成的台湾法律研究所最近成立。
    随着近年来大陆与台湾经济、文化和社会交往的扩大，以及探亲人数的增加，一系列法律问题应运而生，几乎涉及从民法到刑法等所有法律范畴。这个所将通过研究，向有关当局提出预防和解决两岸间法律问题所带来冲突的措施。
    这个研究所目前将以台湾的民法与商法为研究重点，出版刊物，召集学术研讨会。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（方锦）
