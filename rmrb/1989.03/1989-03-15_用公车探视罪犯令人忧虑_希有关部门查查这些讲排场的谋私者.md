Title: The Use of Official Vehicles for Visiting Prisoners Is a Cause for Concern
Author: Hé Lì
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 4

The use of official vehicles for visiting prisoners has become a trend. Many Beijing residents are concerned and hope that relevant departments will investigate these individuals who seek personal gain and flaunt their status.

According to a report from our newspaper, on March 7th and 8th, which were designated days for family visits to the Beijing Juvenile Detention Center, the center's shuttle bus runs every 50 minutes to and from designated stops to facilitate visits. However, some family members of the detained individuals, in order to show off, use official vehicles for their visits. From 8:30 AM to 11:30 AM on March 8th, over 90 official vehicles from various directions gathered at the center. Among them, there were 34 sedans (excluding taxis), 22 jeeps and small buses, and over 30 trucks, vans, and motorcycles (excluding private motorcycles). These vehicles occupied the small area in front of the detention center. It is understood that such visiting scenes occur at least twice a month, and during holidays, the number of vehicles is even greater. The situation in other prisons in Beijing is similar, and in some cases, the scale is even larger.

### 用公车探视罪犯令人忧虑  希有关部门查查这些讲排场的谋私者
何立
1989-03-15
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：

　　用公车探视罪犯令人忧虑
    希有关部门查查这些讲排场的谋私者
    本报讯  家属动用公车探视罪犯如今成为时髦的事儿。北京不少居民对此十分忧虑，呼吁有关部门查查。他们是些什么人物？
    ３月７日、８日，是北京市少年犯管教所犯人亲属探视的日子。为了方便探视，少管所班车每５０分钟往返指定车站接送一次。但一部分在押犯家长或亲属为讲排场，动用公车探视。从上午８点３０分到１１点３０分就有９０多辆公车从四面八方云集而来，其中小轿车３４辆（不包括出租车），吉普车、小客车２２辆、大小货车、卡车、摩托车（不包括私人摩托）３０多辆，在少管所不大的门前各占一席之地。据了解，像这样的探视场面每月最少两次，如遇节假日车辆更多。北京其他监狱的情况与此类似，甚至规模更大。
