Title: Soviet Armed Hijacker Sentenced to Fifteen Years in Prison
Author:
Date: 1989-03-20
Edition: 4

The Supreme Court of the Russian Federation on March 17 sentenced the ringleader of the armed hijacking of schoolchildren as hostages in December last year in an attempt to flee abroad, Yakshyanyets, to 15 years in prison and confiscated his property.

The other three accomplices in the same case were sentenced to 14 years each, and their properties were also confiscated.

### 苏联武装劫持犯被判十五年徒刑

1989-03-20
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联武装劫持犯被判十五年徒刑
    俄罗斯联邦最高法院１７日判处去年１２月以武装劫持小学生作为人质企图逃往国外的首犯亚克希亚涅茨１５年徒刑，没收其财产。
    同案的另外３名罪犯分别被判刑１４年，没收他们的财产。
    （新华社）
