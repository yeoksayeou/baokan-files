Title: Vice Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei Holds Talks with Albanian Deputy Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-03-30
Edition: 2

China is willing to establish and develop a new type of state-to-state relationship with Albania.

BEIJING, March 29 (Xinhua) -- Chinese Vice Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei said today that China is willing to establish and develop a new type of state-to-state relationship with Albania. Tian made the remarks during talks with a delegation led by Albanian Deputy Foreign Minister Muhamet Kapllani.

During the talks, both sides reviewed the improvement and development of bilateral relations in recent years and exchanged views on future bilateral relations. Tian said that China is willing to establish and develop a new type of state-to-state relationship with Albania on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, in the spirit of seeking common ground while reserving differences, mutual respect, and equality and mutual benefit.

Today, Chinese Vice Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Wang Pinqing held a meeting with the Albanian delegation member, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Konstantin Hoxha.

### 田曾佩副外长与阿尔巴尼亚副外长会谈  我愿同阿发展新型国家关系

1989-03-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　田曾佩副外长与阿尔巴尼亚副外长会谈        
    我愿同阿发展新型国家关系
    据新华社北京３月２９日电  中国副外长田曾佩今天在这里表示，中国愿意同阿尔巴尼亚建立和发展新型的国家关系。田曾佩是在同穆哈梅蒂·卡普拉尼副外长率领的阿尔巴尼亚代表团举行会谈时作此表示的。
    会谈中，双方回顾了近年来两国关系改善和发展的情况，并就今后的双边关系交换了意见。田曾佩表示，中国愿意在和平共处五项原则的基础上，本着求同存异、互相尊重、平等互利的精神，同阿尔巴尼亚建立和发展新型的国家关系。
    今天，中国经贸部副部长王品清同阿尔巴尼亚代表团团员，外贸部第一副部长康斯坦丁·霍查举行了对口会谈。
