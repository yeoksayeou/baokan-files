Title: Anhui Zhenbei Mining Bureau Focuses on Timber Forest Cultivation
Author: Jiang Yan
Date: 1989-03-16
Edition: 5

The Anhui Zhenbei Mining Bureau is paying close attention to the cultivation of timber forests. Currently, there are 5 forest farms, and a timber forest planting base has been established in the Dabie Mountains region. The total forested area has reached 45,247 acres. The image shows the Metasequoia forest in Liuhu Forest Farm.
