Title: Benazir Bhutto Meets Chinese Economic and Trade Delegation
Author: Rényùjùn
Date: 1989-03-01
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Islamabad, February 27th.

Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto of Pakistan today afternoon received the Chinese economic and trade delegation led by Vice Minister Li Lanqing of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade at the Prime Minister's Office in Rawalpindi. Both sides had a cordial and friendly conversation.

During the meeting, Li Lanqing reported to Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto on the work of the Fifth Session of the China-Pakistan Economic, Trade, and Science and Technology Joint Committee, which was held in accordance with the instructions of the two countries' prime ministers. Both sides particularly discussed the issue of Pakistan's trade deficit with China. The meeting decided that China will expand its imports from Pakistan.

### 贝·布托会见我经贸代表团
任毓骏
1989-03-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　贝·布托会见我经贸代表团
    本报伊斯兰堡２月２７日电  记者任毓骏报道：巴基斯坦总理贝·布托今天下午在拉瓦尔品第总理府接见了以中国经贸部副部长李岚清为团长的经贸代表团，双方进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    会见时，李岚清向贝·布托总理汇报了根据两国总理指示而召开的第五次中巴经济、贸易和科技混合委员会的工作情况。双方特别讨论了中巴贸易中的巴方逆差问题，会议决定，中国将扩大对巴方的进口。
