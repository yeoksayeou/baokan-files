Title: Reportage Literature Depicting the Spirit of the Disabled: "1988 Seoul: The Unyielding Flag"
Author:
Date: 1989-03-06
Edition: 4

Recently launched, the monthly literary magazine for the disabled, *China Disabled*, published Pan Yi's long reportage literature, "1988 Seoul: The Unyielding Flag." The work, with its deeply emotional pen, vividly depicts the moving scenes of disabled athletes participating in the Olympics in Seoul.

The Olympics have always been a symbol of strength and beauty. However, on October 15, 1988, the Olympic Stadium in Seoul gathered 4,500 disabled individuals from around the world. Fate had cruelly destroyed their naturally healthy bodies, yet they walked onto the stadium, crooked but magnificent.

Perfection does not exist, but this imperfect beauty, through the perfection of personal strength, becomes heart-stirring and supremely beautiful. Thus, in people's eyes, it becomes harmonious.

China sent 43 of its outstanding citizens to participate in this grand event, winning 44 medals, ranking second in the per capita medal count.

The work records the tears and blood shed by disabled athletes in their pursuit of medals, their tenacious struggle against fate to become strong individuals, and the bittersweet experiences behind the glory.

### 表现残疾人精神风貌的报告文学：《一九八八汉城：不屈的旗帜》
文一
1989-03-06
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　表现残疾人精神风貌的报告文学：
    《一九八八汉城：不屈的旗帜》
    最近创刊的残疾人文学月刊《中国残疾人》，发表了潘屹撰写的长篇报告文学《一九八八汉城：不屈的旗帜》。作品以富于深情的笔触描写了在汉城举行的残疾人参加奥运会的动人情景。
    奥林匹克，历来是力量与美的象征。但在１９８８年１０月１５日，汉城奥林匹克运动场上，聚集着来自世界各地的４５００个伤残人。他们被无情的命运破坏了自然赋予的健全的血肉之躯，他们歪歪斜斜地，但又是雄壮地走向运动场。
    完美是不存在的，而这种残缺的美经过人格力量的完善却美得动人心魄，美得极至。因此，在人的眼中变成和谐。
    中国，派出了她的４３名优秀儿女参加这次盛会，一举夺得４４块奖牌，占人均获牌率第二。
    作品记录了伤残运动员为获得奖牌付出的泪与血的代价，为作生活的强者而与命运顽强抗争，以及荣誉后面的酸甜苦辣。　　　　　　　
　　　（文一）
