Title: At the Traffic Light
Author: Moxinyuan
Date: 1989-03-24
Edition: 3

    At the Traffic Light

    In previous years, during the opening of the two sessions, the representatives' car convoy would encounter green lights all the way, passing smoothly without obstruction. This time, the situation is different; the convoy encounters whatever light is showing. A delegate from Liaoning Province, staying at the Liaoning Hotel outside Deshengmen, said: "This should have been done long ago. Since the venue is far and the roads are congested, we can set out earlier and return later. We shouldn't add too much trouble to the work and life of Beijing's citizens."

    (Moxinyuan)

### 红绿灯前
莫新元
1989-03-24
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　红绿灯前
    往年开两会，代表的车队一路绿灯，畅行无阻。这回开会不一样了，车队赶上什么灯，就是什么灯。住在德胜门外辽宁饭店的一位来自辽宁省的人大代表说：“早就该这样。离会场远，道路拥挤，我们可以早点儿动身，晚点儿回来。不要给北京市民的工作、生活添太多的麻烦。”
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（莫新元）
