Title: Works from Shandong Art Academy on Display in Beijing
Author: Xiaoliang
Date: 1989-03-11
Edition: 4

More than 150 works, including Chinese paintings, calligraphy, New Year prints, oil paintings, woodblock prints, and watercolors, which depict the scenery and customs of Shandong, have been on display at the National Art Museum of China since March 8.

The exhibiting artists include renowned masters such as Wang Qihua, Sui Yifu, and Zhang Yanqing, as well as accomplished middle-aged artists like Zhang Dengtang, Sun Molyong, and Yang Wenren. There are also emerging young talents. Through these artists' works, audiences can glimpse the strength and innovative spirit of Shandong's artists.

### 山东画院作品在京展出
晓梁
1989-03-11
第4版(文化生活)
专栏：

　　山东画院作品在京展出
    １５０余幅表现齐鲁大地风土人情的国画、书法、年画、油画、版画和水彩等作品自３月８日起在中国美术馆展出。
    参展的作者，既有王企华、隋易夫、张彦清等饮誉画坛的丹青老将，也有张登堂、孙墨龙、杨文仁等卓有成就的中年画师，还有崭露头角的后起之秀。透过这些作者的作品，观众能窥见到山东艺术家们的实力和探索精神。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（晓梁）
