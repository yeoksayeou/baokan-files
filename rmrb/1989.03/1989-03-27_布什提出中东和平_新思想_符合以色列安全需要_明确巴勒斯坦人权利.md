Title: Bush Proposes New Ideas for Middle East Peace
Author:
Date: 1989-03-27
Edition: 4

	Bush Proposes New Ideas for Middle East Peace
		Meets Israel's Security Needs
		Clarifies Palestinian Rights

	Xinhua News Agency, Washington, March 25

	U.S. President Bush today called for a "new approach" to address the "serious challenges" facing the Middle East region in order to achieve Middle East peace.

	In a statement marking the 10th anniversary of the Camp David Accords, Bush said that while the accords have achieved historical significance over the past decade, the Middle East today remains a "dangerous region" facing even more serious challenges.

	Ten years ago, the late Egyptian President Sadat and the late Israeli Prime Minister Begin, along with former U.S. President Carter, signed the accords at the presidential retreat in Camp David, Maryland, ending more than 30 years of Egyptian-Israeli war. However, the accords were opposed by many Arab countries.

	Bush said that there are now "hopeful signs" of achieving Middle East peace, and the desire for a peaceful resolution to the region's conflicts is growing. "More and more people realize that a solution that satisfies both Israel's security needs and the legitimate political rights of the Palestinians can be achieved."

	On the same day Bush issued his statement, Egyptian President Mubarak, Jordanian King Hussein, and PLO leader Arafat held a summit in the Egyptian city of Ismailia to discuss the prospects for Middle East peace. Two weeks later, Bush will hold separate talks with Mubarak and Israeli President Shamir.

### 布什提出中东和平“新思想”  符合以色列安全需要  明确巴勒斯坦人权利

1989-03-27
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　布什提出中东和平“新思想”        
    符合以色列安全需要     
    明确巴勒斯坦人权利
    新华社华盛顿３月２５日电  美国总统布什今天要求以一种“新的思想”来面对中东地区面临的“严峻挑战”，以求实现中东和平。
    布什在为纪念戴维营协议十周年发表的声明中说，尽管这个协议在过去的十年中有着历史的成就，但是今天的中东仍是面临更多严峻挑战的“危险地区”。
    １０年前，已故埃及总统萨达特和已故以色列总理贝京在马里兰州的总统休养地戴维营同美国前总统卡特一起签署了这一协议，从而结束了持续３０多年的埃以战争，但是这一协议曾遭到许多阿拉伯国家的反对。
    布什说，目前存在着实现中东和平的“带有希望的迹象”，人们要求和平解决该地区冲突的愿望与日俱增，“越来越多的人明白，一个既满足以色列的安全需要又符合巴勒斯坦人的合法政治权利的解决办法是能够达成的。”
    就在布什发表声明的同一天，埃及总统穆巴拉克、约旦国王侯赛因和巴解组织领导人阿拉法特在埃及的伊斯梅利亚城举行高峰会谈，讨论中东和平的前景问题。两星期后，布什将同穆巴拉克和以色列总统沙米尔分别举行会谈。
