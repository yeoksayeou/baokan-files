Title: Premier Tian Jiyun Leaves Sharjah and Returns to China
Author:
Date: 1989-03-21
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun left Sharjah tonight, concluding his four-day visit to the United Arab Emirates. He will return to China.

Before departing Sharjah, Vice Premier Tian Jiyun expressed his satisfaction with his 17-day visit to five Middle Eastern countries. During this trip, Vice Premier Tian Jiyun visited Iran, Turkey, Iraq, Kuwait, and the UAE.

Today, Vice Premier Tian Jiyun also visited the free trade zone in Dubai and exchanged views with UAE Minister of Defense Muhammad bin Rashid Al Maktoum on the possibility of utilizing natural gas in the UAE.

### 田纪云离沙迦回国

1989-03-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　田纪云离沙迦回国
    新华社科威特３月１９日电  沙迦消息：国务院副总理田纪云今晚结束对阿拉伯联合酋长国为期４天的访问，离开沙迦回国。
    田纪云副总理在离开沙迦前表示，他对此次为期１７天的中东５国之行十分满意。田纪云副总理先后访问了伊朗、土耳其、伊拉克、科威特和阿联酋。
    田纪云副总理今天还访问了迪拜的自由港区并同负责自由港区事务的阿联酋国防部长穆罕默德·本·拉希德·阿勒马克图姆就利用阿联酋天然气的可能性问题交换了意见。
