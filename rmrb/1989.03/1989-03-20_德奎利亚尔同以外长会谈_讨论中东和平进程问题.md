De Cuellar Meets with Israeli Foreign Minister to Discuss Middle East Peace Process

	NEW YORK, March 17 (Xinhua) -- United Nations Secretary-General Javier Perez de Cuellar met here today with visiting Israeli Foreign Minister Shimon Peres to discuss the Middle East peace process.

	A UN spokesperson said the two sides also exchanged views on the situation in the Israeli-occupied territories, the relationship between the UN Interim Force in Lebanon (UNIFIL) and Israeli forces in Lebanon, and the presence of Israeli troops in Lebanon.

	On March 7, Israel closed the Israel-Lebanon border, citing an incident where one of its military vehicles was obstructed while entering a village in the area where UNIFIL is stationed. The UN had protested this action, stating that it violated UN resolutions and the freedom of movement enjoyed by UN military personnel in the region.

### 德奎利亚尔同以外长会谈  讨论中东和平进程问题

1989-03-20
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　德奎利亚尔同以外长会谈
    讨论中东和平进程问题
    新华社联合国３月１７日电  联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天在这里同来访的以色列外长阿伦斯讨论了中东和平进程问题。
    联合国一位发言人说，双方还就以色列占领区形势，联合国驻黎巴嫩临时部队同驻黎巴嫩的以色列部队的关系和以色列部队驻扎在黎巴嫩等问题交换了意见。
    ３月７日，以色列借口它的一辆军车在进入联合国驻黎巴嫩临时部队驻扎地区的一座村庄时遭到阻挡而关闭了以、黎边界。联合国曾对此表示抗议，因为它违反了联合国决议和联合国军事人员在该地区自由行动的特权。
