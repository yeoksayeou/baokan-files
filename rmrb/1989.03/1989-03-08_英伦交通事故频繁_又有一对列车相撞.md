Title: Frequent Traffic Accidents in Britain: Another Train Collision
Author: Chen Te'an
Date: 1989-03-08
Edition: 3

	Frequent Traffic Accidents in Britain: Another Train Collision

	People's Daily, London, March 6th - Reporter Chen Te'an: Just two days after the last incident, Britain has once again experienced a serious train collision, resulting in two deaths and 44 injuries. This is the third major train collision in Britain in the past three months.

	The 1220 train from Milngavie to Springburn and the 1239 train from Springburn to Milngavie collided on the same track outside the Bellgrove station in East Glasgow. The cause of the accident is not yet clear.

	According to another report, the cause of the train collision on the 4th was due to one train driver running a red light.

### 英伦交通事故频繁  又有一对列车相撞
陈特安
1989-03-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　英伦交通事故频繁
    又有一对列车相撞
    本报伦敦３月６日电  记者陈特安报道：时隔２日，今天英国再次发生客运火车相撞交通事故，造成两人死亡、４４人受伤。这是近３个月内英国发生的第３起客车相撞的严重交通事故。
    从米尔恩盖维开往斯普林伯恩的１２２０号列车和从斯普林伯恩开往米尔恩盖维的１２３９号列车在东格拉斯哥的贝尔格罗夫车站外驶入了同一轨道而相撞。事故原因尚不清楚。
    另据报道，４日火车相撞事故原因，是一列火车司机闯红灯。
