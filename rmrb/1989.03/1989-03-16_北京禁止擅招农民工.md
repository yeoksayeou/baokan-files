Title: Beijing Bans Unauthorized Hiring of Migrant Workers
Author: Xia Junsheng
Date: 1989-03-16
Edition: 2

Beijing Bans Unauthorized Hiring of Migrant Workers

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 15th — (Reporter Xia Junsheng) The Beijing Municipal Bureau of Labor has recently issued an emergency notice, prohibiting all departments, units, private enterprises, individual business households, and township enterprises within the Beijing municipal area from hiring migrant workers from other provinces and municipalities without the approval of the Municipal Bureau of Labor. It is also not allowed to hire migrant workers from other provinces who have illegally entered Beijing. Those who have already been hired should be immediately dismissed.

    It is reported that the Beijing Municipal Government has decided to reduce and dismiss 200,000 to 250,000 migrant workers from other provinces this year, and the dismissal work has already begun.

### 北京禁止擅招农民工
夏俊生
1989-03-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　北京禁止擅招农民工
    新华社北京３月１５日电  （记者夏俊生）北京市劳动局近日发出紧急通知，禁止北京市区域内的各部门、各单位以及私营企业、个体工商户和乡镇企业未经市劳动局批准到外省市招用农民工，也不允许擅自招用流入北京的外地农民，已经招用的应立即清退。
    据介绍，北京市政府已经决定在今年压缩和清退外地务工农民２０到２５万，清退工作已开始。
