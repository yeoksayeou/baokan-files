Title: Chinese News Delegation Departs Beijing for Visit to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1989-03-28
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 27th.

At the invitation of the Soviet Journalists' Union, a delegation of seven Chinese journalists, led by the vice-chairman of the Chinese Journalists' Association, Tan Wenrui, departed Beijing by plane this morning for a visit to the Soviet Union.

### 中国新闻代表团昨离京出访苏联

1989-03-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　中国新闻代表团昨离京出访苏联
    本报北京３月２７日讯  应苏联记者协会邀请，由中国记协副主席谭文瑞率领的中国新闻代表团一行七人，今晨乘飞机离开北京去苏联访问。
