Title: Global Oil Prices Rise
Author:
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 3

World oil prices have risen. On March 13, in the New York Mercantile Exchange, the price of April delivery of West Texas Intermediate crude oil futures increased by 53 cents per barrel, reaching $19.03.

In the London market, the price of North Sea Brent crude oil rose from $17 per barrel last weekend to $17.06.

Experts in this field point out that the rise in oil prices is due to a recent fire in a Norwegian North Sea oil field, which has temporarily reduced oil production.

### 世界原油涨价

1989-03-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　世界原油涨价
    新华社纽约３月１３日电  世界石油价格上涨。１３日在纽约商品交易所，美国西得克萨斯中质油４月份的期货价每桶上涨５３美分，达到１９．０３美元。
    在伦敦市场，英国北海布伦特原油价格由上周末的每桶１７美元。上升到１７．０６美元。
    此间石油专家指出，原油价格上涨是因挪威的北海油田最近失火使原油暂时减产所致。
