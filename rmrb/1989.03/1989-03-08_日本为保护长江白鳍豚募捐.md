Title: Japan Raises Funds to Protect the Yangtze Finless Porpoise
Author:
Date: 1989-03-08
Edition: 3

Japanese marine scientists established the "River Dolphin Protection Association" on the 2nd to raise funds for the protection of the Yangtze finless porpoise, a species unique to the Yangtze River.

The president of the River Dolphin Protection Association is Professor Toshiro Kamiya of Tsukuba University. He initiated the establishment of this association after receiving a letter seeking help from Chinese experts.

### 日本为保护长江白鳍豚募捐

1989-03-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　日本为保护长江白鳍豚募捐
    日本海洋学科学家２日成立了“保护河豚协议会”，为保护长江特有的白鳍豚开展募捐活动。
    保护河豚协议会会长是筑波大学教授神谷敏郎。他是在接到中国有关专家的求援信后发起成立这个协议会的。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　（本栏均据新华社）
