Title: Another "Gesar Epic" Singer Discovered in Qinghai

Author: Zhang Xiaoping

Date: 1989-03-28

Edition: 2

    Another "Gesar Epic" Singer Discovered in Qinghai

    XINHUA NEWS AGENCY, XINING, March 27th - Recently, another singer of the epic "Geser" was discovered on the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau. His name is Cai Rangwangdui, a herdsman from Tanggula Township in Golmud City, Haixi Prefecture, Qinghai Province. He is 57 years old.

    In the past two years, the Qinghai Province "Geser" Epic Research Institute has recorded four parts of the "Geser" stories he sings, totaling 254 tapes, equivalent to over 760,000 words. It is said that he can sing 120 parts of the "Geser" stories, and recording all of them would require 6,000 tapes.

### 青海又发现《格萨尔王传》演唱家
张小平
1989-03-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　青海又发现        
    《格萨尔王传》演唱家
    本报西宁３月２７日电  青藏高原上最近又发现一位史诗《格萨尔王传》的藏族演唱家，他叫才让旺堆，是青海海西州格尔木市唐古拉乡的牧民，今年５７岁。
    近两年来，青海省《格萨尔王传》史诗研究所为他演唱的四部《格萨尔王传》故事录了音，一共录２５４盘磁带，相当于７６万多字。据介绍，他可以演唱１２０部《格萨尔王传》故事，全部录下需要使用６０００盘磁带。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（张小平）
