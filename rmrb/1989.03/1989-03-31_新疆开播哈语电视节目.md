Title: Xinjiang Begins Broadcasting in the Kazakh Language
Author: Zeng Kun
Date: 1989-03-31
Edition: 2

Starting from April 1, the 1 million Kazakh people in Xinjiang will be able to watch Kazakh-language programs broadcast by the Xinjiang Television Station. With this, the station has become the one with the most languages broadcast (Mandarin, Uyghur, and Kazakh) in the country.

The Xinjiang Television Station, which transmits programs via communication satellites, has an audience coverage rate of over 80%.

### 新疆开播哈语电视节目
曾坤
1989-03-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　新疆开播哈语电视节目
    本报乌鲁木齐３月３０日电  记者曾坤报道：从４月１日起，新疆１００万哈萨克族群众将收看到新疆电视台播出的哈语节目。至此该台成为全国播出语言最多（汉、维吾尔、哈萨克三种）的电视台。
    通过通讯卫星传送电视节目的新疆电视台，其电视的人口覆盖率已达８０％以上。
