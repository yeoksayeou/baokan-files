Title: The President of Togo Will Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-03-31
Edition: 2

The President of Togo will visit China

	New China News Agency, Beijing, March 30th: The spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced today that at the invitation of Chairman Yang Shangkun, the founder and chairman of the Togo People's Union, General President Gnassingbé Eyadéma and his wife will make a formal visit to China from April 6th to 9th.

	The spokesperson also announced that at the invitation of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, a delegation from the Thai House of Representatives led by the Speaker Banharn Silapa-archa will arrive in Beijing on April 4th to make a friendly visit to our country.

### 多哥总统将访华

1989-03-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　多哥总统将访华
    新华社北京３月３０日电  外交部发言人今天在这里宣布，应杨尚昆主席的邀请，多哥人民联盟创始人、主席，共和国总统纳辛贝·埃亚德马将军和夫人将于４月６日至９日对中国进行正式访问。
    这位发言人同时还宣布，应全国人大常委会的邀请，由泰国下院议长班乍·盖索恩通率领的泰国下院代表团将于４月４日抵京，对我国进行友好访问。
