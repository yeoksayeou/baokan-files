Title: Progress in Research on New Nutrient Solution "Beijing Tianan 203"
Author:
Date: 1989-03-26
Edition: 2

Experts from the Beijing Tianan High-Efficiency Technology Research Institute used biological engineering and genetic breeding methods to obtain a new strain in October last year, which was named "Tianan 203."

Experimental results show that the "Tianan 203" fermentation liquid has the effect of inhibiting or killing cancer cells and can stimulate the body's self-defense mechanisms. It can be used as a nutritional supplement or medication for cancer patients and is an ideal nutritional substance for prolonging life and alleviating symptoms.

### 新营养液“北京天安２０３”研究有进展

1989-03-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　新营养液“北京天安２０３”研究有进展
    据新华社北京３月２４日电  北京天安高效益技术研究所的专家组，去年１０月采用生物工程遗传育种方法，获得一新菌株，被命名为“天安２０３”。
    实验结果表明，“天安２０３”发酵液有抑制或杀死癌细胞的作用，并能激发人体自卫机制，可作为癌症患者的营养品或药物，也是延长生命、减轻症状的理想营养物。
