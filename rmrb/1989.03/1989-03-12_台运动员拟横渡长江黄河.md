Title: Taiwanese Athletes Plan to Swim Across the Yangtze and Yellow Rivers
Author:
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 4

Taiwanese amateur swimmer Wang Han announced at a press conference on March 10 that he plans to lead a team of 40 Taiwanese amateur swimmers to swim across the Yangtze River and the Yellow River this May.

### 台运动员拟横渡长江黄河

1989-03-12
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　台运动员拟横渡长江黄河
    新华社香港三月十一日电  台北消息：台湾业余游泳健将王瀚三月十日举行记者会宣布，他计划带领四十名台湾业余游泳运动员于今年五月横渡长江与黄河。
