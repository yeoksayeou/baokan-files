Title: Schultz Appointed as Boeing Company Director
Author:
Date: 1989-03-08
Edition: 3

	Schultz Appointed as Boeing Company Director

	New York, March 6 (Xinhua) - Frank Shrontz, Chairman of the Board of the Boeing Aircraft Company, announced today in Seattle that former U.S. Secretary of State Schultz has been elected as a director of the company's board.

	The 68-year-old Schultz served as the U.S. Secretary of State from July 1982 to January 1989 during the Reagan administration. Before that, he was the Chairman of the Board of Bechtel Group in San Francisco.

	From 1969 to 1974, Schultz also held several key government positions, including Secretary of Labor, Director of the Office of Management and Budget, Secretary of the Treasury, and Assistant to the President.

	Currently, Schultz is also a professor of international economics at the Stanford University Business School, and a director on the boards of General Motors and Bechtel Group.

### 舒尔茨出任波音公司董事

1989-03-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　舒尔茨出任波音公司董事
    新华社纽约３月６日电  美国波音飞机公司董事长弗兰克·施罗茨今天在西雅图宣布，美前国务卿舒尔茨当选为该公司董事会董事。
    现年６８岁的舒尔茨于１９８２年７月至１９８９年１月在里根政府时期担任国务卿。在这之前他是旧金山比奇特尔集团公司的董事长。
    舒尔茨在１９６９年至１９７４年间，还担任过内阁劳工部长、行政管理和预算局局长、财政部长及总统助理等政府要职。
    舒尔茨目前还是斯坦福大学商业研究院的国际经济学教授，以及通用汽车公司和比奇特尔集团公司董事会董事。
