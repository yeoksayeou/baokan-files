Title: Bookstores Seize Every Opportunity
Author: Zhang Xuping
Date: 1989-03-22
Edition: 3

On the morning of the 21st, staff from the Xinhua Bookstore arrived at the Friendship Hotel, where the CPPCC members were staying, and set up a book stall in the north annex hallway. They brought a wide variety of books, attracting many members. According to the bookstore staff, the most popular books were those on social sciences and novels.

### 书店见缝插针
张旭平
1989-03-22
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　书店见缝插针
    ２１日早晨，新华书店的工作人员就来到政协委员下榻的友谊宾馆，在北配楼门厅摆开了书摊。他们带来的书籍种类繁多，吸引了不少委员。据书店工作人员反映，以社会科学、小说方面的书买者最多。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（张旭平）
