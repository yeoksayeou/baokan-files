Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-03-11
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency

	State Councilor Chen Xitong met with a delegation led by the Minister of the Black Metallurgy Industry of the Soviet Union, Koryakov, on March 9th at the Great Hall of the People. Minister of the Metallurgical Industry Qiyuanjing was also present at the meeting.

	Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Nan and his wife concluded their three-day visit to Macau and departed from Macau in the afternoon of March 9th, returning to the mainland via Zhuhai. Governor of Macau, Vasco Garcia da Silva, and his wife, among others, saw them off at the border.

		(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 外事简讯

1989-03-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外事简讯
    ▲国务委员陈希同９日在人民大会堂会见了苏联黑色冶金工业部部长柯尔帕科夫一行。冶金工业部部长戚元靖会见时在座。
    ▲外交部副部长周南和夫人，结束了对澳门的３天访问，９日下午离开澳门经珠海返回内地。澳门总督文礼治和夫人等到关闸送行。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
