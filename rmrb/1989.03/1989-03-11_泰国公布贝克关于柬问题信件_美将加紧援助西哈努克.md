Title: Thailand Publishes Baker's Letter on the Cambodian Issue; US to Intensify Aid to Sihanouk
Author:
Date: 1989-03-11
Edition: 3

Thailand Publishes Baker's Letter on the Cambodian Issue; US to Intensify Aid to Sihanouk

A spokesperson for the Thai Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated today that U.S. Secretary of State Baker recently wrote a letter to Thai Foreign Minister Siddhi, reaffirming the U.S. position on a comprehensive solution to the Cambodian issue.

The spokesperson said that a comprehensive solution includes the complete withdrawal of Vietnamese troops from Cambodia, the establishment of a temporary coalition government, and the formation of an international monitoring force.

He said that Baker also expressed in the letter that the U.S. intends to increase aid to Prince Sihanouk.

The Thai Ministry of Foreign Affairs released parts of this letter to the media. The spokesperson quoted from the letter, stating that the U.S. will handle its relations with Vietnam with caution.

### 泰国公布贝克关于柬问题信件  美将加紧援助西哈努克

1989-03-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　泰国公布贝克关于柬问题信件
    美将加紧援助西哈努克
    新华社曼谷３月９日电  泰国外交部一位发言人今天说，美国国务卿贝克最近在写给泰国外长西提的一封信中重申了美国关于全面解决柬埔寨问题的立场。
    这位发言人说，全面解决包括越南从柬埔寨全部撤军、建立临时联合政府和成立一支国际监督部队。
    他说，贝克在信中还表示美国打算加紧对西哈努克亲王的援助。
    泰国外交部向新闻界公布了此信的部分内容。这位发言人援引信件说，美国将谨慎地处理同越南的关系。
