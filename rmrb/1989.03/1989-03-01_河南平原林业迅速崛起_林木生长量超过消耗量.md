Title: Rapid Rise of Forestry in Henan Plains: Wood Growth Exceeds Consumption
Author:
Date: 1989-03-01
Edition: 1

Recent results from the Henan Province forestry resource survey indicate that the province's standing timber volume has reached 91.52 million cubic meters, an increase of 34.2% compared to 1980. The forest coverage rate has risen to 14.8%, and the annual wood growth volume is 17.04 million cubic meters, exceeding the annual timber consumption.

One of the reasons for the rapid growth of forest resources in Henan Province is the swift development of forestry in the plains.

According to officials from the provincial forestry department, there are still many unstable factors in Henan's forest resources, and timber consumption is also increasing. If efforts are relaxed, there is still a risk of decline. The future policy is to "improve the plains and focus on the mountains."

### 河南平原林业迅速崛起  林木生长量超过消耗量
李杰　张玉林
1989-03-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　河南平原林业迅速崛起
    林木生长量超过消耗量
    本报郑州２月２８日电  本报记者李杰、新华社记者张玉林报道：近日结束的河南省林业资源普查结果表明：全省活立木蓄积量已达９１５２万立方米，比１９８０年增长３４．２％；森林覆盖率上升到１４．８％，林木年生长量达１７０４万立方米，超过年木材消耗量。
    河南省森林资源增长快的原因之一是平原林业的迅速崛起。
    据省林业部门的同志介绍，河南省林业资源不稳定因素还很多，木材消耗量也在增多，稍有放松，仍有下降可能。今后的方针是“完善平原、主攻山区”。
