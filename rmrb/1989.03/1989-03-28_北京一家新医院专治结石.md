Title: A New Hospital in Beijing Specializes in Treating Stones
Author: Chen Guangman
Date: 1989-03-28
Edition: 2

China's first multi-functional gallbladder and kidney stone treatment center was recently established in Beijing. It is expected to free a large number of patients with urinary system stones and gallstones from the pain of surgery, the inconvenience of water bath lithotripsy, and the harm of radiation.

Over the past two years, the center has treated more than 3,600 cases of urinary system stones, with a complete stone fragmentation rate of over 99.8%.

### 北京一家新医院专治结石
陈光曼
1989-03-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　北京一家新医院专治结石
    本报北京３月２７日讯我国第一家多功能胆、肾碎石治疗中心日前在北京成立，可望使大量泌尿系统结石和胆结石病人从手术的痛苦、水浴碎石的不便、射线的伤害中解脱出来。
    这家中心近２年来已治疗泌尿系统结石３６００多例，结石完全破碎率在９９．８％以上。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（陈光曼）
