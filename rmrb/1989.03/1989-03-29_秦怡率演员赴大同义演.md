Title: Qin Yi Leads Actors to Perform in Datong
Author: Lǐ Píng
Date: 1989-03-29
Edition: 2

Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference delegate and renowned artist Qin Yi led a performance team from the Shanghai Film and Television Company to Datong, Shanxi Province, for a visit and performance on March 22.

In just five days, they performed eight free shows for the miners on the front lines of mining and excavation, attracting an audience of over 25,000 people. On March 26, Qin Yi braved the snow to perform at the mouth of the Yun Gang Mine, receiving a warm welcome.

### 秦怡率演员赴大同义演
李平
1989-03-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　秦怡率演员赴大同义演
    本报北京３月２８日讯  七届全国政协委员、著名艺术家秦怡，３月２２日率上海影视公司演出队赴山西大同矿区慰问演出。
    短短５天内，他们无偿为奋战在采掘第一线的矿工义演８场，观众达２．５万多人。３月２６日，秦怡冒雪前往云岗矿区井口演出，受到热烈欢迎。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（李平）
