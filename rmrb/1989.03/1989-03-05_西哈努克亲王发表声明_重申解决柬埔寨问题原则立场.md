Prince Norodom Sihanouk Issues Statement
Reaffirms Principles for Resolving the Cambodian Issue

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 4: Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, issued a statement on March 4, firmly rejecting any further talks with Hun Sen and reaffirming his principles for resolving the Cambodian issue.

1. First, simultaneously dissolve the Democratic Kampuchea coalition government and the "People's Republic of Kampuchea" (Phnom Penh puppet regime), and then hold elections in Cambodia. The elections should be conducted under the supervision of the United Nations International Supervisory Commission.

2. Replace the Democratic Kampuchea coalition government and the "People's Republic of Kampuchea" with a temporary four-party government. Additionally, establish a four-party national organization, a four-party national army of 10,000 soldiers from each side, and a four-party general staff. The remaining troops will be disarmed under the supervision of the United Nations International Supervisory Commission.

3. Vietnam should unconditionally withdraw all its troops from Cambodia by the end of September 1989, not December 1990. Once Vietnamese troops have completely withdrawn from Cambodia, all foreign aid to the four Cambodian factions should cease.

4. A United Nations peacekeeping force should be sent to Cambodia. Its presence is the best guarantee to "prevent the Khmer Rouge from regaining power."

Prince Sihanouk emphasized in the statement, "If Nguyen Co Thach and Hun Sen refuse my clear demands, I will refuse to see Hun Sen again." He also stated that if the international community holds an informal meeting on the Cambodian issue, he will send a delegation to participate.

### 西哈努克亲王发表声明  重申解决柬埔寨问题原则立场

1989-03-05
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　西哈努克亲王发表声明
    重申解决柬埔寨问题原则立场
    据新华社北京三月四日电  民柬主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王四日发表声明，断然拒绝再次同洪森举行会谈，并重申了解决柬埔寨问题的原则立场。
    一、首先同时解散民柬联合政府和“柬埔寨人民共和国”（金边伪政权），然后在柬埔寨实行大选。选举应当在联合国国际监督委员会的监督下进行。
    二、以四方临时联合政府取代民柬联合政府和“柬埔寨人民共和国”。此外，成立四方全国机构以及每方由一万名军人组成的四方国民军和四方总参谋部。剩余的军队由联合国国际监督委员会负责解除武装。
    三、越南应当无条件地从柬埔寨撤出其全部军队，撤军时间应在一九八九年九月底以前，而不是一九九零年十二月底以前。一旦越南军队全部撤出柬埔寨，一切外国应当停止对柬埔寨四方军队的援助。
    四、应当向柬埔寨派遣联合国维持和平部队。它的存在是“防止红色高棉重新掌权”的最好保证。
    西哈努克亲王在声明中强调，“如果阮基石和洪森拒绝我的上述明确要求，我将拒绝再见洪森。”他表示，如果国际社会举行关于柬埔寨问题的非正式会议，他将派代表团参加。
