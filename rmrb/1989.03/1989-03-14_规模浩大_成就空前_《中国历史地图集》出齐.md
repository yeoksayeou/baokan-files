Title: Magnificent Scale and Unprecedented Achievements: The Complete Publication of "The Historical Atlas of China"
Author: Hou Jian Guo
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 2

The eight-volume set of "The Historical Atlas of China" has been completed. The Chinese Academy of Social Sciences and the China Maps Press held a celebration for it today in Beijing.

This monumental work, proposed by Mao Zedong, was edited by prominent historians of China, including Fan Wenlan, Wu Han, Yin Da, and Tan Qixiang, who successively served as chief editors. It involved over 200 experts in the fields of history, geography, cartography, and ethnology, and took 35 years to complete. The first volume was published in 1982.

The atlas consists of eight volumes, 20 map groups, and includes 304 maps and 549 pages. It covers the political boundaries of every dynasty from the Three Sovereigns and Five Emperors to the end of the Qing Dynasty. Its content is more comprehensive than any existing similar atlas.

Professor Tan Qixiang, 79, from Fudan University, revealed that he is still overseeing the editing of "The National Atlas of China."

### 规模浩大　成就空前  《中国历史地图集》出齐
侯建国
1989-03-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　规模浩大　成就空前
    《中国历史地图集》出齐
    本报北京三月十三日讯  《中国历史地图集》八卷本已经出齐。中国社会科学院和中国地图出版社今天为它举行了庆祝会。
    这部由毛泽东提议编纂，中国历史学权威范文澜、吴晗、尹达、谭其骧先后负责主编的巨著，集中国历史学、地理学、测绘学、民族学的二百多位精英，费时三十五年之久才编绘完。第一册出版于一九八二年。
    这套图集共八册，二十个图组，共有图三百零四幅、五百四十九页，从三皇五帝到清朝末年的历代政治疆域图无不毕载。其内容之丰富超过了已有的任何一部同类图集。
    七十九岁的复旦大学教授谭其骧透露，他还在主编《中国国家大地图集》。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（侯建国）
