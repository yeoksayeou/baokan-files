Title: Qi Pengfei Hosts Banquet for Hong Kong and Macau Representatives and Members
Author:
Date: 1989-03-24
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 23rd.

The Director of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macau Affairs Office, Qi Pengfei, hosted a banquet this evening at the Great Hall of the People to welcome representatives and members from Hong Kong and Macau who came to Beijing to attend the second session of the Seventh National People's Congress and the second session of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

In his toast, he expressed the hope that representatives and members from the Hong Kong and Macau regions would continue to take an interest in the consultation work on the drafting of the Basic Law of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, making it more perfect.

### 姬鹏飞昨欢宴港澳代表委员

1989-03-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　姬鹏飞昨欢宴港澳代表委员
    新华社北京３月２３日电  国务院港澳办公室主任姬鹏飞今晚在人民大会堂设宴，欢迎来京出席七届全国人大二次会议和全国政协七届二次会议的港澳地区的代表和委员。
    他在祝酒词中希望，港澳地区的代表和委员继续关心香港特别行政区基本法起草的咨询工作，使之修改得更加完善。
