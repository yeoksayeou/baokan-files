Title: National Boxing Qualification Tournament Concludes
Author: Sūn Yǒngshèng
Date: 1989-03-27
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, March 25th.

The 1989 National Boxing Championship qualification tournament concluded today in Tianjin after three days and ninety-nine matches. One hundred athletes have secured their spots to compete in the final round of the national boxing tournament, which will be held in Jinan in April.

More than two hundred athletes from twenty-three teams across the country participated in this event. The competition featured eight weight classes.

### 全国拳击资格赛结束
孙永盛
1989-03-27
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　全国拳击资格赛结束
    新华社天津三月二十五日电  （记者孙永盛）一九八九年全国拳击锦标赛资格赛经过三天九十九场比赛，今天在天津结束，一百名运动员取得了参加今年四月在济南举行的全国拳击赛决赛阶段比赛的资格。
    参加这次比赛的有来自全国二十三个代表队的二百多名选手，比赛共设八个级别。
