Title: The United States Conducts Underground Nuclear Test
Author:
Date: 1989-03-11
Edition: 3

The United States today conducted its third underground nuclear test of the year at the Nevada Test Site.

The test began at 6:05 AM local time, with a yield of 2 to 15 kilotons of TNT, within the limits set by the Limited Test Ban Treaty.

This is the 690th nuclear explosion conducted at the site since the United States began testing there in 1951. Last year, the United States conducted a total of 12 nuclear tests.

### 美国进行地下核试验

1989-03-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国进行地下核试验
    新华社华盛顿３月９日电  美国今天在内华达试验场进行了今年以来的第三次地下核试验。
    试验是在当地时间６点零５分开始的，爆炸当量为２至１５万吨ＴＮＴ，在有限禁止核试验条约规定的范围之内。
    这是美国自１９５１年开始在该试验场进行核试验以来的第６９０次核爆炸。去年美国共进行了１２次核试验。
