Title: Riots Erupt Again in Karachi
Author:
Date: 1989-03-23
Edition: 4

Police in Pakistan's largest city, Karachi, clashed with protesters on March 21, resulting in at least 15 injuries.

About 2,000 members of the Pakistan National Alliance held a rally and demonstration in front of the Sindh Provincial Assembly on March 21 to protest the repatriation of Pakistanis stranded in Bangladesh. The demonstrators clashed with the police.

The protesters attempted to storm the assembly building. They damaged over 100 vehicles parked near the assembly building, set fire to more than 10 cars, and attacked several shops. Police officials said that the police fired tear gas at the demonstrators and surrounded more than 30 National Alliance workers.

Currently, Karachi is under curfew. Gatherings and marches are banned for one month. Karachi has seen multiple riots in recent days, with at least 12 fatalities.

### 卡拉奇又发生骚乱

1989-03-23
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　卡拉奇又发生骚乱
    新华社伊斯兰堡３月２２日电  据此间今天报道，巴基斯坦警察３月２１日在全国最大城市卡拉奇与示威群众发生冲突，至少有１５人受伤。
    大约２０００名巴基斯坦全国联盟的成员２１日在信德省议会前举行集会和示威，抗议遣返在孟加拉国陷入困境的巴基斯坦人。示威者与警察发生冲突。
    示威群众试图袭击议会大厦。他们破坏了１００多辆停在议会大厦附近的车辆，放火焚烧了１０多辆汽车，并袭击了一些商店。警方人士说，警察向示威群众施发催泪弹，并包围了３０多名全国联盟的工作人员。
    目前，卡拉奇已实行宵禁。在一月之内，禁止市民集会和游行。卡拉奇近日来多次发生骚乱。至少有１２人丧生。
