Title: Asian Development Bank Loans to India
Author:
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 3

The Asian Development Bank has recently decided to provide India with a loan of $198 million to improve the country's road infrastructure.

India plans to use this loan to improve roads in states such as Andhra Pradesh and Haryana.

India is one of the countries with the largest road transportation networks in the world. Nevertheless, one-third of its villages still lack road access.

### 亚开发银行向印贷款

1989-03-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　亚开发银行向印贷款
    新华社新德里３月３日电  据报道，亚洲开发银行最近决定向印度提供１．９８亿美元的贷款，用以改善印度的公路设施。
    印度计划将这笔贷款用于改善安得拉邦、哈里亚纳邦等的公路。
    印度是世界上拥有最大公路交通网的国家之一，尽管如此，印度还有１／３的村庄未通公路。
