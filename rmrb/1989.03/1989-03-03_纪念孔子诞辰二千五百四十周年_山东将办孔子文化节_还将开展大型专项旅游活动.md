Title: Commemorating the 2540th Anniversary of Confucius's Birth
Author: Jià Jiànzhōu
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 1

		This year on September 28th, it will be the 2540th anniversary of the birth of Confucius, the great thinker and educator of China. (Note: Confucius's birthday is on the 27th day of the eighth lunar month. After joint calculations by the government of Qufu City and the Purple Mountain Observatory in Nanjing, the solar date is determined to be September 28th). On this occasion, the Shandong Provincial Tourism Bureau, the Beijing Municipal Tourism Bureau, and other units will jointly launch a series of large-scale special tourism activities. The Confucius Cultural Festival will also be held from September 26th to October 10th in Qufu, Shandong Province, the birthplace of Confucius.

		The large-scale tourism activities in Confucius's hometown, aimed at promoting Chinese culture and facilitating international cooperation and exchanges, include: Confucius's "Six Arts" (ritual, music, archery, charioteering, calligraphy, and mathematics) activities; Confucius's hometown study tour activities, and eight other tourism activities.

### 纪念孔子诞辰二千五百四十周年  山东将办孔子文化节  还将开展大型专项旅游活动
贾建舟
1989-03-03
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    纪念孔子诞辰二千五百四十周年
    山东将办孔子文化节
    还将开展大型专项旅游活动
    本报济南3月1日电  记者贾建舟报道：今年9月28日，是我国伟大的思想家、教育家孔子诞辰2540周年（注：孔子诞辰农历日期为八月廿七日，经曲阜市政府和南京紫金山天文台共同推算，其公历日期确定为9月28日）。在此之际，山东省旅游局、北京市旅游局等单位，将联合推出一系列大型专项旅游活动。孔子文化节也将于9月26日至10月10日在孔子诞生地山东省曲阜市举行。
    旨在弘扬中华文化、促进中外合作交往的孔子家乡大型旅游活动包括：孔子“六艺”（礼、乐、射、御、书、数）活动；孔子家乡修学活动等10项旅游活动。
