Title: Thai Prime Minister Chatchai Chunhavan Arrives in Beijing for Official Visit
Author:
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 1

At the invitation of Premier Li Peng, General Chatchai Chunhavan, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Thailand, and his wife arrived in Beijing this evening, beginning a four-day official visit to China.

This is Chatchai's first visit to China since taking office as Prime Minister. Accompanying him are Foreign Minister Sitthi Sawetsila and others.

It is understood that during Chatchai's visit, the two premiers will have extensive and in-depth exchanges of views on further strengthening Sino-Thai friendship, developing friendly cooperation in various fields between the two countries, and the Cambodian issue.

Tomorrow, Li Peng will host a grand welcoming ceremony to warmly welcome the distinguished guests.

Upon their arrival in Beijing, the Chinese government's escort team, led by Minister of Health Chen Minzhang and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Liu Shucai, welcomed them at the airport.

### 应邀对我国进行正式访问  差猜总理抵京

1989-03-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　应邀对我国进行正式访问
    差猜总理抵京
    新华社北京３月１４日电  应国务院总理李鹏邀请，泰王国总理差猜·春哈旺上将偕夫人今晚抵京，开始对中国为期４天的正式访问。
    这是差猜任总理以来首次访华。随同差猜来访的还有外交部长西提·沙卫西拉等。
    据悉，在差猜访华期间，两国总理将就进一步加强中泰友谊、发展两国在各个方面的友好合作和柬埔寨问题等广泛、深入地交换意见。
    明天，李鹏将主持隆重仪式，热烈欢迎贵宾。
    差猜一行抵京时，中国政府陪同团团长、卫生部部长陈敏章和外交部副部长刘述卿等到机场迎接。
