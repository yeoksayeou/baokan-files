Title: Agenda for the Second Session of the Seventh National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1989-03-20
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 19th. The agenda for the second session of the Seventh National People's Congress was approved at the preparatory meeting held on March 19, 1989.

1. To hear and deliberate on the report by Premier Li Peng on the work of the government.
2. To hear and deliberate on the report by Vice Premier and Chairman of the State Planning Commission Yao Yilin on the draft of the 1989 national economic and social development plan. To review and approve the execution of the 1988 national economic and social development plan and the 1989 national economic and social development plan.
3. To hear and deliberate on the report by State Councilor and Minister of Finance Wang Boping on the execution of the 1988 state budget and the draft of the 1989 state budget. To review and approve the execution of the 1988 state budget and the 1989 state budget.
4. To deliberate on the draft of the Administrative Litigation Law of the People's Republic of China.
5. To deliberate on the draft of the Procedural Rules of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China.
6. To deliberate on the proposal by the State Council to authorize the People's Congress of Shenzhen and its Standing Committee to formulate local regulations for the Shenzhen Special Economic Zone.
7. To hear and deliberate on the report by Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Chong on the work of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.
8. To hear and deliberate on the report by President of the Supreme People's Court Ren Jianxin on the work of the Supreme People's Court.
9. To hear and deliberate on the report by Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Liu Fuzhi on the work of the Supreme People's Procuratorate.
10. Other matters.
