Title: They Break into the Men's World
Author: Wu Fangwen
Date: 1989-03-08
Edition: 4

	专栏:

	They Break into the Men's World

	Today, in some sports traditionally considered "men's domains," more and more women are entering, demonstrating their equal rights in the sports arena.

		Left top: Jin Zhao from Beijing has the best performance among female players in the National Snooker Individual Open.
		Right top: Assunta Alietta (right) is the first woman in Italy to participate in a boxing match.
		Right: A women's rugby team from Cologne, West Germany, is training. (Photo provided by Xinhua News Agency)
