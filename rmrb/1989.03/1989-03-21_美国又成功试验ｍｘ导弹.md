Title: The United States Successfully Tests the MX Missile Again
Author:
Date: 1989-03-21
Edition: 4

The United States successfully tested the 18th MX missile today at the Vandenberg Base in California, dropping seven non-lethal warheads in two designated areas.

This missile is the United States' primary long-range nuclear missile. U.S. President George H.W. Bush stated that a decision on whether to retain this missile or to modify it into a smaller, single-warhead missile has not yet been made.

### 美国又成功试验ＭＸ导弹

1989-03-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国又成功试验ＭＸ导弹
    新华社华盛顿３月２０日电  美国空军今天在加利福尼亚州范德伯格基地成功地试验发射了第１８颗ＭＸ导弹，并在２个指定领域投下了７颗非杀伤性弹头。
    这种导弹是美国主要的远程核导弹。美国总统布什声称，是否保留这种导弹或者把它改变为小型的单弹头导弹，目前还没有作出决定。
