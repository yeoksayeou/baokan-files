Title: West Germany Begins Research on Space Transportation System
Author: Dài Lǚbīn
Date: 1989-03-02
Edition: 3

West Germany has begun research on the "Sänger" space transportation system, with plans to allocate substantial funds for the project.

According to West German Federal Research and Technology Minister Riesenhuber, the "Sänger" system, named after the West German space scientist Eugen Sänger (1905-1964), is a hypersonic aircraft. It consists of two stages: the lower stage is a horizontally launched aircraft powered by a hydrogen engine, and the upper stage is an orbital aircraft equipped with a rocket engine. The design concept allows both stages to return to Earth and be reused up to 150 times after completing their missions.

Riesenhuber stated that the research on the "Sänger" space transportation system will be conducted in several phases. The first phase, starting in 1988 and ending in 1992, will focus on technical feasibility studies. The second phase (1993-1999) will involve component testing. The final phase will involve collaborative research across Europe, with the primary task of developing experimental devices, aiming to make a decision on the development of the "Sänger" system by 2004.

Riesenhuber announced that to strengthen the research in the first phase, the Federal Research Ministry plans to allocate 2.2 billion Marks in research funding. Additionally, the West German Aerospace Research Institute will provide 860 million Marks, and the aerospace company MBB, along with the West German Research Association, will contribute funds, totaling nearly 4 billion Marks.

### 西德开始研究航天运输系统  研究分三阶段进行　２００４年就发展此系统作出决定
戴履斌
1989-03-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    西德开始研究航天运输系统
    研究分三阶段进行　2004年就发展此系统作出决定
    新华社波恩2月28日电  （记者戴履斌）西德政府拟拨巨款进行“森格尔”号航天运输系统的研究。
    据西德联邦研究技术部长里森胡贝尔27日在波恩宣布，以西德航天科学家森格尔（1905—1964）的名字命名的航天运输系统是一种超高音速飞行器，它将由两组组成，下级是由氢动力装置驱动的水平起飞的飞机，上级是一个装有火箭发动机的轨道飞行器。按照设计思想，两级在完成飞行任务后皆可返回地面，并且可反复使用150次。
    里森胡贝尔说，“森格尔”号航天运输系统的整个研究工作将分几个阶段进行。第一阶段从1988年开始，1992年结束，这是技术方案论证阶段。第二阶段（1993年—1999年）主要进行部件试验。最后阶段将进行全欧合作研究，主要任务是研制实验性装置，于2004年就发展“森格尔”号航天运输系统作出决定。
    里森胡贝尔宣布，为加强第一阶段研究工作，联邦研究部拟拨款2.2亿马克的研究费用，加上西德航空航天研究院提供的8600万马克，航空航天企业MBB公司以及西德研究协会提供的经费，总共将达到近4亿马克。
