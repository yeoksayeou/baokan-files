Title: May Your Child Grow Up Happily
Author: Hong Feng
Date: 1989-03-24
Edition: 8

    May your child grow up happily.
    Hong Feng's poetry and art, one day of sowing love,
    A lifetime of responsibility for this golden apple.
    Generation after generation, hands joined,
    Pray to heaven to entrust the love.
    That forest that shelters from the wind and rain
    Should let go a little,
    Tradition will become lighter.
