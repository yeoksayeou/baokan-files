Title: Italy Urges Release of Mandela
Author:
Date: 1989-03-20
Edition: 4

	Italy Urges Release of Mandela
	Xinhua News Agency, Rome, March 17

	Italian Foreign Minister Andreotti once again urged the South African authorities to release South African black leader Nelson Mandela as soon as possible. Andreotti made this request during a meeting with visiting South African Foreign Minister Botha.

	According to a press release from the Italian Foreign Ministry, Andreotti and Botha exchanged views on the latest developments in Southern Africa, particularly the prospects for Namibian independence.

### 意大利敦促释放曼德拉

1989-03-20
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　意大利敦促释放曼德拉
    新华社罗马３月１７日电  意大利外长安德烈奥蒂１７日再次敦促南非当局尽早释放南非黑人运动领导人纳尔逊·曼德拉。安德烈奥蒂是在会见来访的南非外长博塔时向他提出这一要求的。据意大利外交部发表的一份新闻公报说，安德烈奥蒂同博塔就南部非洲局势的最新发展，尤其是纳米比亚独立的前景等问题交换了意见。
