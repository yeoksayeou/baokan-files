Title: Farmers Focus on Environmental Protection
Author: Wang Jianbao
Date: 1989-03-17
Edition: 1

To raise public awareness about environmental issues, the environmental protection department of Cuishan County, Xuzhou, has been actively promoting environmental knowledge during rural markets and temple fairs. The image shows people at the Sanbao Temple Fair in the second lunar month, attentively viewing the environmental protection poster "Beware of Global Warming."

	Wang Jianbao, photographer
