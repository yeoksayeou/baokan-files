Title: U.S. Senate Approves Health and Human Services and Energy Secretaries
Author:
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 3

The U.S. Senate today approved President Bush's nominees for the Secretaries of Health and Human Services, Louis Sullivan, and Energy, James Watkins.

The Senate today approved Health and Human Services Secretary Sullivan with a vote of 98 to 1. Sullivan is the only African American in Bush's cabinet. Energy Secretary Watkins, who is a retired Navy admiral and nuclear energy expert, was approved unanimously by the Senate. So far, 13 out of the 17 key cabinet members requiring Senate approval have been confirmed.

### 美参院批准卫生和公众服务能源部长

1989-03-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美参院批准卫生和公众服务能源部长
    新华社华盛顿3月1日电　美国参议院今天批准了布什总统任命的卫生和公众服务部部长路易斯·沙利文和能源部长詹姆斯·沃特金斯。
    参院今天以98票对1票批准的卫生和公众服务部部长沙利文是布什内阁中唯一的黑人。获得参院一致通过的能源部长沃特斯金是一位退休的海军上将和原子能专家。到目前为止，需经参议院批准的17位布什内阁主要成员，已有13位获得了批准。
