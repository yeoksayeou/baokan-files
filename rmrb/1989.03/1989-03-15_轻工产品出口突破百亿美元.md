Title: Light Industrial Products Exports Surpass $100 Billion
Author: Li Xiaogang
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 1

Light Industrial Products Exports Surpass $100 Billion

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 14 — (Reporter Li Xiaogang) According to the latest statistics from the customs, in 1988, the national export value of light industrial products exceeded $100 billion, accounting for 1/5 of the total national export value. The goal of achieving $100 billion in light industrial product exports by 1990 was realized two years ahead of schedule.

### 轻工产品出口突破百亿美元
李晓岗
1989-03-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　轻工产品出口突破百亿美元
    据新华社北京３月１４日电  （记者李晓岗）据海关最新统计，１９８８年全国轻工产品出口额突破１００亿美元，占全国出口总额的１／５，提前两年实现１９９０年轻工产品出口的目标。
