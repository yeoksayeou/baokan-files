Title: The 14th National Congress of the Communist Party of India Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 3

The 14th National Congress of the Communist Party of India concluded today in Kolkata, India's largest city.

Indian Communist Party General Secretary R. Rajan in his closing speech outlined the main tasks facing the Indian Communist Party at present and expressed the determination to "strive for a new India."

In discussing the unity between the Indian Communist Party and the Communist Party of India (Marxist), Rajan said that while there may be differences on certain issues, the two parties are united on the main tasks.

### 印共十四大闭幕

1989-03-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　印共十四大闭幕
    据新华社新德里３月１２日电  印度共产党第１４次全国代表大会今天在印度最大城市加尔各答闭幕。
    印度共产党总书记拉·拉奥在闭幕大会上发表讲话时阐明了目前印度共产党面临的主要任务，并表示“为建立一个新印度而努力”。
    在谈到印度共产党和印度共产党（马）的团结问题时，拉奥说，两党在某些问题上可能存在着分歧，但是在主要任务方面是一致的。
