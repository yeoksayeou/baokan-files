Title: Outline of the Development of China's Political System in Contemporary China
Author:
Date: 1989-03-31
Edition: 6

The book "Outline of the Development of China's Political System in Contemporary China," written by Zheng Qian, Pang Song, Han Gang, and Zhang Zhanbin, has been published by the Chinese Communist Party History Press. The book narrates the history of the establishment and development of China's political system from 1949 to 1987, summarizes the experiences and lessons learned, and is the first domestic monograph to study this issue.

### 《当代中国政治体制发展概要》

1989-03-31
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：

　　《当代中国政治体制发展概要》
    由郑谦、庞松、韩钢、张占斌撰写的《当代中国政治体制发展概要》一书，已由中共党史资料出版社出版。本书叙述了１９４９至１９８７年中国政治体制建立和发展的历史，总结了经验教训，是国内第一本研究这个问题的专著。
