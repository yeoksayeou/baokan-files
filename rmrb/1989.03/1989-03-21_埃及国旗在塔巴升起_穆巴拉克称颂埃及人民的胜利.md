Egyptian Flag Rises Over Taba
Mubarak Praises Egyptian People's Victory

BEIJING, March 19 (Xinhua) — Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak today, at noon, held the red, white, and black tricolor flag in both hands and, with the steps of a soldier, walked to the center of the Taba region. In front of numerous high-ranking government officials and foreign envoys, he personally raised the Egyptian flag. At this moment, the joy of celebrating Taba's return to the motherland swept across Egypt.

In his speech at the flag-raising ceremony, he praised the great historical victory of the Egyptian people in achieving peace. He solemnly declared to the world, "Peace in this region is not an impossible dream." The editorial of the newspaper "Al-Ahram" pointed out that Egypt finally regained Taba, "for the first time in the Middle East, a territorial dispute was resolved through negotiations."

### 埃及国旗在塔巴升起  穆巴拉克称颂埃及人民的胜利
林皎明
1989-03-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　埃及国旗在塔巴升起        
    穆巴拉克称颂埃及人民的胜利
    本报开罗３月１９日电  记者林皎明报道：埃及总统穆巴拉克今天中午双手捧着红、白、黑３色国旗，迈着军人矫健的步伐，走向塔巴地区中央，在众多政府高级官员和外国使节面前，亲手升起埃及国旗。此时，埃及全国欢庆塔巴回归祖国怀抱的情绪达到高潮。
    他在升旗仪式上发表讲话，称颂埃及人民实现和平的伟大历史胜利。他严肃地向世界宣告，“本地区和平绝不是不可能实现的”。《金字塔报》社论指出，埃及最终收回塔巴，“在中东地区第一次通过谈判解决领土争端”。
