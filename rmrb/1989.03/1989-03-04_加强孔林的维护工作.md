Title: Strengthen the Maintenance of Kong Lin
Author: Tian Yueyong
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 6

	Call for Action

	Kong Lin in Qufu, Shandong is a key cultural relic protected by the State Council, attracting hundreds of domestic and foreign tourists daily.

	Currently, some nearby farmers are climbing over the walls into Kong Lin to harvest firewood. They then slide the heavy bundles of firewood down the walls. The north wall of Kong Lin has already been damaged in over 30 places. Even more seriously, some farmers are illegally cutting down trees in Kong Lin.

	It is hoped that the local government will take measures to manage Kong Lin according to the law.

		Wuqing Qufu Detachment, Tian Yueyong

### 加强孔林的维护工作
田月永
1989-03-04
第6版(读者来信)
专栏：呼吁

　　加强孔林的维护工作
    山东曲阜孔林是国务院公布的重点文物保护单位，每天都有数以百计的中外游人来此观光游览。
    目前，附近一些农民纷纷攀越围墙进入孔林，收割柴草，又把很沉重的柴草捆从围墙上滑下，仅孔林北墙已损坏３０多处。更为严重的是，有的农民竟大肆偷砍滥伐孔林树木。
    希望当地政府采取措施，依法管理好孔林。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　武警曲阜中队　田月永
