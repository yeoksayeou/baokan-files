Title: Huang Zhen Appointed Ambassador to the United Arab Emirates
Author:
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 2

Huang Zhen Appointed Ambassador to the United Arab Emirates

New China News Agency, Beijing, March 2 — According to the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Yang Shangkun, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Huang Zhen as the Chinese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the United Arab Emirates.

### 黄振出任驻阿拉伯联合酋长国大使

1989-03-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    黄振出任驻阿拉伯联合酋长国大使
    新华社北京3月2日电　杨尚昆主席根据人大常委会的决定，任命黄振为中国驻阿拉伯联合酋长国特命全权大使。
