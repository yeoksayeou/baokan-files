Title: Peking Opera "Cao Cao and Yang Xu" Wins China Drama Society Award
Author:
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 4

The recent meeting of the China Drama Society's Chairman's Work Conference decided to award the Peking Opera "Cao Cao and Yang Xu" performed by the Shanghai Peking Opera Troupe with the "China Drama Society Award." The society's charter stipulates that this award is to recognize outstanding and pioneering creative performances or research achievements. "Cao Cao and Yang Xu" is the first to receive this high-level award.

### 京剧《曹操与杨修》获中国戏曲学会奖

1989-03-03
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

    京剧《曹操与杨修》获中国戏曲学会奖
    本报讯  最近召开的中国戏曲学会会长工作会议作出决定，授予上海京剧院演出的《曹操与杨修》以“中国戏曲学会奖”。该会章程规定，设立此项奖是为奖励优秀的有开拓性的创作演出或研究成果。京剧《曹操与杨修》是获得这一高规格奖励的第一个。
