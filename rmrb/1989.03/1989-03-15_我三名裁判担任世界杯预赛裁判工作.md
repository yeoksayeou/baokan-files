Title: Three Chinese Referees to Officiate in World Cup Qualifiers
Author: Yang Rong
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 4

Three Chinese referees will officiate in the World Cup qualifiers.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 14 - (Correspondent Yang Rong) The Chinese Football Association's Referees Committee announced in Kunming's Haidong Sports Training Base that, according to the nomination by the Asian Football Confederation, Chinese international football referees Chen Shengcai, Zhang Jicheng, and Yu Jingyin will officiate in the second round of the 14th World Cup qualifiers in the Asian zone, which will be held in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, on March 15. Chen Shengcai will serve as the main referee.

Chen Shengcai and the others departed for Riyadh by airplane on March 11. This is the second time that Chinese football referees have participated in the officiating of World Cup qualifiers.

### 我三名裁判担任世界杯预赛裁判工作
杨嵘
1989-03-15
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　我三名裁判担任世界杯预赛裁判工作
    新华社昆明３月１４日电  （通讯员杨嵘）中国足球办公室下属的裁判委员会在昆明海埂体育训练基地宣布：根据亚洲足联提名，我国国际级足球裁判员陈胜才、张继成和于敬崟将担任３月１５日在沙特阿拉伯的利雅得举行的第十四届世界杯赛亚洲区预选赛第二组（沙特阿拉伯——叙利亚）的裁判工作，其中陈胜才任主裁判。
    陈胜才等人已于３月１１日乘飞机前往利雅得。这是中国足球裁判第二次参加世界杯外围赛的裁判工作。
