Title: Northwest Hotel Returns Gift Items
Author:
Date: 1989-03-22
Edition: 6

Reply to Feedback

The People's Daily published a letter from a reader on January 21st on the fifth page titled "The New Shop of the God of Wealth, Gifts from All Directions." The letter criticized our hotel. We sincerely accept the reader's criticism and have decided to immediately return the gifts sent by relevant units.

Ershanxi Hotel, Shaanxi

### 西北饭店退还赠品

1989-03-22
第6版(读者来信)

                                      陕西长安西北饭店
