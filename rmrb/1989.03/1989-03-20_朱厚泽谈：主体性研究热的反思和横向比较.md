Title: Zhu Houze on the Reflection and Horizontal Comparison of the Heat of Subjectivity Research
Author: 朱厚泽 (Zhu Houze)
Date: 1989-03-20
Edition: 6

	Zhu Houze:
		Reflections and Horizontal Comparisons on the Heat of Subjectivity Research

	The journal "Modernization" published an article by Zhu Houze in its second issue this year, stating: Now, people are reflecting on the 20th century from various angles. In the first half of this century, the capitalist world was filled with crises, shocks, wars, and various kinds of unrest. However, after World War II, the situation changed, especially in those newly independent countries. Due to their adoption of different development strategies, choices of different development models, and establishment of different systems, the gaps in social conditions, economic development, and political life among them have become significantly widened. It is probably against this backdrop that the issue of subjectivity has become a major topic of serious study in China's academic circles and the international intellectual community. From a micro perspective, the role of the subject in the process of cognition is one of the hot topics in the philosophical circle's study of epistemology; from a macro perspective, a class, a party, a leadership group, at the historical turning points in social development, what kind of choices they make will have a very great impact on the long-term development of their country and region. I raise this point to illustrate that whether it is for a small enterprise or a larger scope of a country or a society, as we face the end of a century, we should seriously reflect on the paths we have taken, compare them with different countries and regions, draw some beneficial lessons from them; at the same time, we should have a good look ahead to the new century, what it will be like? With what kind of appearance and posture should we welcome its arrival?

### 朱厚泽谈：主体性研究热的反思和横向比较
朱厚泽
1989-03-20
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：新视角

    朱厚泽谈：
    　　　　主体性研究热的反思和横向比较
    《现代化》杂志今年第二期发表朱厚泽的文章说：现在，人们都在对二十世纪这一百年从不同角度进行反思。在这一百年中，前五十年，资本主义世界充满着危机、震荡、战争和各种各样的不安。但是，从第二次世界大战以后，情况有了改变，特别是那些新取得独立的国家，由于各自采取了不同的发展战略，选择了不同的发展模式，建立了不同的体制，彼此间的社会状况、经济发展和政治生活的差距十分显著地拉大了。大概就是在这样一个背景下，主体性问题成了我国学术界和国际思想界认真研究的重大课题。从微观上讲，主体在认识过程中的作用，是哲学界关于认识论研究的热门话题之一；从客观上看，一个阶级，一个政党，一个领导集团，在社会发展的那些历史性转折的关头，究竟做出了什么样的选择，对于他的国家、他们地区的长远发展，会产生很大很大的影响。我提出这一点，是想说明一个问题，即无论就一个小的企业来讲，还是就更大范围的一个国家、一个社会来讲，在我们面临着一个世纪就要过去的时候，是应该认真来思索一下我们所走过的道路，与不同的国家、不同的地区加以比较，从中吸取一些有益的东西；同时，很好地放眼展望一下新的世纪，它将是一个什么样子？我们应该以一个什么样的面貌与姿态迎接它的到来？
