Date: 1989-03-17
Edition: 8

High Mountains and Flowing Waters

The Front

I

March without rain. The wind from afar in March.
As always, with renewed and fresh details, the damp longing hangs on the clothesline. You smiled...
Your charm has soaked my emotions, and I have bathed in your radiant gaze, like the sun and moon.

Acquaintance. Silence.

II

By the willow shore, under the bright moon, in the breeze, in the morning light... Days, a distant and warm song.
You ignite your vivid presence in the dense forest and among the flowers, and I pick your smiles and tenderness while braving the wind and rain.

III

The moon ascends to the zenith, a profound and fresh theme, an ancient and unfallen longing—gazing at the stars and the full moon.
The fish in my dreams swim and dart in your night, drinking drop by drop of longing.

IV

Crossing that threshold, passing through that grove, circling that reef, we reach our own sea.
The tide is coming. Let us stride across the waves.

### 高山流水
前方
1989-03-17
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　高山流水
    前方
    一
    三月无雨。三月风自远方来。
    一如既往，翻新簇新的细节，湿漉漉的相思晾上衣竿。你笑了……
    你的妩媚浸濡了我的情感，我又沐浴了你丽日明月般的目光。
    相识。无言。
    　　　　二
    柳岸边，明月下，清风里，晨光中……时日，一支遥远温馨的歌。
    你在密林花丛点燃起你的鲜明，我在风雨兼程中采摘你的笑靥你的温柔。
    　　　　三
    月亮行至中天，一个隽永而清新的主题，一颗亘古未落的思念——望星空望月圆。
    我梦的游鱼摇头摆尾游弋于你的夜，饮一滴滴相思。
    　　　　四
    越过那道门槛，穿过那片林带，绕过那座礁石，便是我们自己的海了。
    潮汐来了。我们踏浪去吧。
