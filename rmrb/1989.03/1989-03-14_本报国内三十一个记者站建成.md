Title: The People's Daily Completes Construction of 31 Domestic News Bureaus
Author:
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 1

The People's Daily's bureau in Qinghai was officially established today. Principal leaders of the Qinghai Provincial Party Committee and government, Yin Kesheng and Song Ruixiang, attended the reception and delivered speeches. With this, the reconstruction and new construction of the People's Daily's 31 domestic bureaus have been successfully completed.

Previously, the People's Daily's bureau in Shaanxi was officially established on March 7. Provincial and municipal party and government leaders Zhang Boxing, Hou Zongbin, An Qiyuan, and Yuan Zhengzhong attended the reception and delivered speeches.

Deputy Editor-in-Chief Bao Yujin presided over the establishment receptions for the two bureaus.

The reconstruction and new construction of the People's Daily's bureaus across all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions began in the second half of 1986.

### 本报国内三十一个记者站建成

1989-03-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　本报国内三十一个记者站建成
    本报西宁３月１３日电  人民日报驻青海记者站于今天正式成立。青海省委、省政府主要领导同志尹克升、宋瑞祥等出席招待会并讲了话。至此，人民日报驻国内３１个记者站的重建和新建工作已圆满完成。
    此前，人民日报驻陕西记者站于３月７日正式成立，省、市党政负责人张勃兴、侯宗宾、安启元、袁正中等出席招待会并讲了话。
    本报副总编辑保育钧主持了上述两个记者站的建站招待会。
    人民日报驻全国各省、市、自治区记者站的重建和新建工作，是从１９８６年下半年开始的。
