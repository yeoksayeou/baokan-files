Title: Guangzhou Prepares to Build a "Metro"
Author: Liang Zhaoming
Date: 1989-03-11
Edition: 2

Guangzhou Prepares to Build a "Metro"

A reporter from this newspaper, Liang Zhaoming, reports from Guangzhou on March 10th: The International Urban Underground Railway Technology Exchange Conference and Exhibition held in Guangzhou revealed that the feasibility study for the Guangzhou Metro has been completed and is now in the stage of being submitted for approval. The entire project is estimated to cost 4.05 billion yuan, with approximately 300 million US dollars in foreign exchange. The main sources of funding will be government allocations, social fundraising, corporate self-raising, and foreign loans.

### 广州筹建“地铁”
梁兆明
1989-03-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　广州筹建“地铁”
    本报广州３月１０日电  记者梁兆明报道：在广州举行的国际城市地下铁路技术交流会和图片展览会透露，广州地铁建设，已完成可行性研究，进入上报审批阶段。整个工程预计投资４０．５亿元人民币，外汇约占３亿多美元。资金来源主要是政府拨款、社会集资、企业自筹和国外贷款。
