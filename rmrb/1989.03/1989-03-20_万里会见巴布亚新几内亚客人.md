Title: Wan Li Meets with Guests from Papua New Guinea
Author: Jiang Lurong
Date: 1989-03-20
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 19th.

Wan Li Meets with Guests from Papua New Guinea

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 19th (reporter Jiang Lurong), Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Wan Li, said today that China is concerned about the peace and development of the South Pacific region.

He said that China supports and appreciates the efforts made by South Pacific countries, including Papua New Guinea, to strengthen regional unity and cooperation, maintain regional peace and stability, and revitalize the regional economy.

Wan Li hosted and dined with a delegation from the Papua New Guinea Parliament led by Speaker Dennis Yang this evening at the Great Hall of the People. The delegation, invited by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, arrived in Beijing yesterday for a one-week friendly visit to China. This is the first delegation from the Papua New Guinea Parliament to visit China since the two countries established diplomatic relations in 1976. Wan Li stated that the delegation's visit will deepen mutual understanding and friendship between the parliaments of the two countries. Friendly exchanges between the two parliaments will contribute to the development of friendly cooperative relations between the two countries.

Dennis Yang, the Speaker, said that this is his and the delegation members' first visit to a socialist country, and everything they have seen in Beijing has left a deep impression on them. Dennis Yang expressed the hope for the development of joint ventures between the two countries. He said that the Papua New Guinea Parliament encourages local entrepreneurs to develop economic and trade cooperation with China.

This afternoon, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Sun Qimeng, held talks with Speaker Dennis Yang. Both sides exchanged views on further developing friendly relations between the parliaments and peoples of the two countries, as well as cooperation in economic, trade, and other fields.

### 万里会见巴布亚新几内亚客人
姜鲁榕
1989-03-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　万里会见巴布亚新几内亚客人
    据新华社北京３月１９日电  （记者姜鲁榕）全国人大常委会委员长万里今天在这里说，中国关心南太平洋地区的和平与发展事业。
    他说，中国支持并赞赏包括巴布亚新几内亚在内的南太平洋国家为加强区域团结和合作、维护区域和平与稳定、振兴地区经济而作出的努力。
    万里今晚在人民大会堂会见并宴请由丹尼斯·扬议长率领的巴布亚新几内亚议会代表团。代表团应全国人大常委会的邀请于昨天抵京，对中国进行为期一周的友好访问。这是中巴自１９７６年建交后来访的第一个巴布亚新几内亚议会代表团。万里表示，代表团的来访有利于加深中巴两国议会之间的相互了解和友谊。两国议会之间的友好交往将为两国友好合作关系的发展作出贡献。
    丹尼斯·扬议长说，这是他和代表团成员第一次访问社会主义国家，在北京所看到的一切，给他们留下了深刻的印象。丹尼斯·扬表示希望两国发展合资企业。他说，巴布亚新几内亚议会鼓励本国企业家同中国发展经济、贸易合作。
    今天下午，全国人大常委会副委员长孙起孟同丹尼斯·扬议长举行会谈。双方就进一步发展两国议会和人民间的友好关系以及两国在经济、贸易等领域的合作等问题交换了意见。
