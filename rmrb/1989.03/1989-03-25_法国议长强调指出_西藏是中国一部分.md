Title: French Speaker Emphasizes that Tibet is Part of China
Author:
Date: 1989-03-25
Edition: 4

French Senate President Alain Poher on March 23 in Paris emphasized that France has always regarded "Tibet as a part of China and Tibet should not be independent."

Poher expressed this stance at a luncheon hosted by Chinese Ambassador to France Zhou Jue.

Zhou Jue briefed Poher on the true situation of the recent unrest in Tibet and the Chinese government's principled position on the incident. He said that the Tibet issue is China's internal affair, and the central government is prepared to negotiate with the Dalai Lama, which in no way means that we agree to Tibet's independence or semi-independence.

The host and guests also exchanged views on issues of common concern.

### 法国议长强调指出  西藏是中国一部分

1989-03-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　法国议长强调指出        
    西藏是中国一部分
    新华社巴黎３月２３日电 法国参议院议长阿兰·波埃２３日在巴黎强调指出，法国始终认为“西藏是中国的一个组成部分，西藏不应该独立”。
    波埃是在中国驻法大使周觉为他举行的午餐会上表示这一立场的。
    周觉向波埃介绍了最近西藏发生骚乱的真实情况和中国政府对这一事件的原则立场。他说，西藏问题属于中国的内政，中央政府准备同达赖喇嘛谈判，这丝毫不意味着我们同意西藏独立或半独立。
    宾主双方还就共同关心的问题交换了意见。
