Title: The 18th Session of the Islamic Conference of Foreign Ministers Opens
Author: Wú Wénbīn
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, March 13th.

The 18th session of the Islamic Conference of Foreign Ministers opened this afternoon in Riyadh, the capital of Saudi Arabia. Forty-five Islamic countries' foreign ministers and their representatives, as well as representatives from thirty international and Islamic organizations, attended the meeting to discuss issues facing the Islamic world.

Host country King Fahd presided over the opening ceremony. In his opening speech, he called on the attendees to strive for greater unity, stating that unity is the solid foundation for Islamic cooperation.

The chairman of this session, Saudi Foreign Minister Prince Faisal, further emphasized in tonight's first working meeting that Islamic unity must be strengthened so that Islamic countries can face the challenges and problems they encounter. He also stressed the need to convene an international peace conference for the Middle East, stating that such a conference is an effective way to resolve disputes comprehensively and justly.

Regarding the situation in Afghanistan, Prince Faisal praised the Soviet withdrawal as a positive development in the Afghan issue and expressed the hope that it could be the beginning of the Soviet Union establishing mutual respect and friendly neighborly relations.

He also hoped that the work of the Arab Six-Party Committee on the Lebanese issue would receive sincere cooperation from all parties and the international community to formulate a plan to resolve the Lebanese crisis.

Prince Faisal told reporters that the most important issues facing the Islamic world, including Afghanistan, the Iran-Iraq conflict, and the Palestinian issue, have all shown encouraging and positive beginnings.

This four-day conference has established four committees on political, news, legal affairs; economic affairs; cultural affairs; and administrative and financial affairs. They will discuss more than sixty items and are expected to conclude on Thursday afternoon.

### 伊斯兰会议组织外长会议开幕  呼吁加强团结对付挑战  会议将讨论中东和平等问题
吴文斌
1989-03-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　伊斯兰会议组织外长会议开幕
    呼吁加强团结对付挑战   
    会议将讨论中东和平等问题
    本报大马士革三月十三日电  记者吴文斌报道：第十八次伊斯兰会议组织外长会议今天下午在沙特阿拉伯首都利雅得开幕，来自四十五个伊斯兰国家的外长及他们的代表和三十个国际组织和伊斯兰组织的代表出席了今天的会议，讨论伊斯兰世界面临的问题。
    东道国国王法赫德主持了开幕式。他在开幕词中呼吁与会者努力加强团结。他说，团结是伊斯兰进行合作的坚实基础。
    本届会议主席、沙特外交大臣费萨尔亲王在今天晚上举行的第一次工作会议上进一步强调，必须加强伊斯兰的团结，以便使伊斯兰国家能够对付面临的问题和挑战。他还强调必须召开中东和平国际会议，称中东和平国际会议是全面公正解决争端的有效途径。
    关于阿富汗问题，费萨尔亲王赞扬苏联撤军是阿富汗问题的一个积极发展，并表示希望它能成为苏联继续建立相互尊重和友好睦邻关系的开端。
    他还希望黎巴嫩问题阿拉伯六方委员会的工作得到各方和国际社会的诚挚合作，以便为解决黎巴嫩危机制订一个方案。
    费萨尔对记者发表谈话说，伊斯兰世界面临的最重要的问题其中包括阿富汗、两伊争端和巴勒斯坦问题，都已出现了令人鼓舞的良好开端。
    这次历时四天的会议，共组成了政治新闻法律事务、经济事务、文化事务和行政财政事务四个委员会，将对六十多个议题进行讨论，预计在星期四下午闭幕。
