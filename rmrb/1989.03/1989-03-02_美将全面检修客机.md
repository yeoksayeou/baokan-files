Title: The U.S. Plans to Conduct Comprehensive Maintenance on Passenger Aircraft
Author: Zhang Qixun
Date: 1989-03-02
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 28th February

    The U.S. Plans to Conduct Comprehensive Maintenance on Passenger Aircraft

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 28th February, Reported by Zhang Qixun: Due to the frequent accidents involving passenger aircraft in recent years, the U.S. aviation industry today submitted a report to the Federal Aviation Administration, proposing to conduct large-scale maintenance on aging passenger aircraft.

    This plan requires all airlines to maintain over 1,300 Boeing aircraft, with an estimated cost of around $800 million. The Federal Aviation Administration has begun reviewing this plan, and once approved, it will be implemented immediately.

### 美将全面检修客机
张启昕
1989-03-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美将全面检修客机
    本报华盛顿2月28日电　记者张启昕报道：由于近年来客机不断发生事故，美国民航界今天向联邦航空管理局提出一份报告，计划对飞行年久的客机进行大规模检修。
    这项计划要求各航空公司对1300多架波音客机进行检修，总共需要花费8亿美元左右。联邦航空管理局已经开始审核这项计划，一经批准，就将执行。
