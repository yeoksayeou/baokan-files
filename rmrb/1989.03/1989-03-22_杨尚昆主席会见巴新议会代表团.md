Author:
Date: 1989-03-22
Edition: 1

Chairman Yang Shangkun Meets with Papua New Guinea Parliamentary Delegation

	BEIJING, March 21 (Xinhua) -- Chairman Yang Shangkun today morning met with a parliamentary delegation from Papua New Guinea led by Speaker Dennis Young in the Great Hall of the People.

	Yang Shangkun welcomed the first parliamentary delegation from Papua New Guinea. He said that exchanges between the parliaments of China and Papua New Guinea, where they can share experiences and learn from each other, are beneficial.

	Yang Shangkun praised Papua New Guinea's efforts in maintaining peace and stability in the South Pacific region.

	Dennis Young said that his visit and that of the delegation is a good beginning. He believed that China is the best partner for Papua New Guinea.

	Both sides also exchanged views on international situations.

	Vice Chairman Sun Qimeng of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress attended the meeting.

### 杨尚昆主席会见巴新议会代表团

1989-03-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　杨尚昆主席会见巴新议会代表团
    据新华社北京三月二十一日电  国家主席杨尚昆今天上午在人民大会堂会见由丹尼斯·扬议长率领的巴布亚新几内亚议会代表团。
    杨尚昆对巴布亚新几内亚的第一个议会代表团来访表示欢迎。他说，中巴两国议会间的往来，互相介绍经验，互相学习，是一件有益的事情。
    杨尚昆对巴布亚新几内亚为维护南太平洋地区和平与稳定所作出的努力表示赞赏。
    丹尼斯·扬说，他和代表团的访问是一个良好的开端。他认为中国是巴布亚新几内亚的最好合作伙伴。
    双方还就国际形势等问题交换了意见。
    全国人大常委会副委员长孙起孟等参加了会见。
