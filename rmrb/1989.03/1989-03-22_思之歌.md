Title: Ode to Thought
Author: Wang Chengdian
Date: 1989-03-22
Edition: 8

There are those who have trained for half a century
Just to wait for a moment to confess
There are those who spend their lives acting
But never get to fully understand a single line

Even if the tongue is cut out
One would not sing in hell
Even if crippled for life
One must sever the pus-filled bones

Rather than dance with a guilty conscience
It is better to shout curses

Before the black flowers bloom
Red flowers always fetch a good price
What can be adhered to?
What can be doubted?
What can be hated?
What can be forgiven?

In every corner of the world
There lives a group of people
Who hold the truth with ample evidence

When clouds are abundant, the sky seems chaotic
When clouds are scarce, the sky seems bland
A sky adorned with only a few chest flowers
Is blessed

A pinch of salt in the sugar
Reaches the highest level of sweetness
The stem of a melon that bears sweet fruit
Is bitter to the point of discomfort
Because of the birth of the atom bomb that annihilates life
The world appears relatively peaceful

Crickets chirp loudly
The autumn night is all the more tranquil
With the contrast of withered stems and fallen leaves
The summer lotus blooms even more beautifully
The blood of the sentimental seeps into the heartless iron
And possesses a shocking red hue

### 思之歌
王承典
1989-03-22
第8版(要闻)
专栏：

　　思之歌
    王承典
    有的人操练了半个世纪
    只为等待一个表白的瞬间
    有的人一生都在演戏　
    却来不及吃透一句台词
    　　　　　　　　
    即使割掉舌头
    也不做地狱的歌手
    即使终生残废
    也要斩断流脓的骨头
    与其昧着良心跳舞
    何如放开嗓子诅咒
    　　　　　　　
    在黑色的花朵上市以前
    红花总能卖个好价钱
    能恪守什么
    能怀疑什么
    能憎恨什么
    能宽容什么
    每一方天地里
    都生活着一批
    论据充足的真理持有者
    　　　　　　　　　　
    云多了显得凌乱
    云没了显得平淡
    只佩戴几朵胸花的蓝天
    有福了
    　　　　　　　　　　
    糖里加点儿盐
    方能达到甜的最高水准
    分娩出甜瓜的瓜蒂儿
    苦得叫人难受　
    由于灭绝生灵的原子弹的诞生
    这世界才显得相对和平
    蛐蛐们使劲地吵闹
    秋夜愈是宁静
    有了残梗败叶的映衬
    夏日荷花才美得出众
    多情的血渗进无情的铁
    便具有了惊人的腥红
