Title: Mastering Multiplication and Division Quickly
Author: Xǔ Bǎojiàn
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 2

15 mixed addition and subtraction operations involving four-digit numbers were completed in just 13 seconds. Guo Jing, a student from Qiqihar, and her friend demonstrated the "Variational Multiplication and Division Quick Calculation Method" here today to experts and scholars, receiving high praise.

The inventor of this calculation method is Yu Wen Yongquan, deputy director of the Qiqihar Financial Research Institute. (Xǔ Bǎojiàn)

### 乘除速算一口清
许宝健
1989-03-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　乘除速算一口清
    本报北京３月１１日讯  １５个４位数的加减混合运算，仅用１３秒钟就得出结果。来自齐齐哈尔市的女学生郭晶和小伙伴今天在这里向专家、学者表演“变式乘除一口清速算法”，受到高度评价。
    这一算法发明者是齐齐哈尔财政研究所副所长宇文永权。（许宝健）
