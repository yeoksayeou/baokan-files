Title: 5,000 Officials Sent to the Countryside in Guizhou
Author: Pan Ditu
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Guizhou, March 11th.

    Recently, Guizhou has dispatched 5,000 officials to impoverished areas. This initiative has been ongoing in Guizhou for three years. This year's officials sent to the countryside are primarily composed of professionals and technical personnel. They will mainly work through methods such as paired poverty alleviation, project-based poverty alleviation, and industry-specific poverty alleviation.

### 贵州五千干部下乡
潘帝都
1989-03-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　贵州五千干部下乡
    本报贵阳三月十一日电  记者潘帝都报道：近日贵州抽调了五千名干部到贫困地区。这项工作在贵州已坚持了三年。今年的下乡干部以业务和技术人员为主，主要采取挂钩扶贫、项目扶贫和行业对口扶贫的方式开展工作。
