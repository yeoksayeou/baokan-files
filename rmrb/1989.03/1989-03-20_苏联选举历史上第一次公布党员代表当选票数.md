Title: For the First Time in Soviet Election History, the Number of Votes for Party Representatives is Announced
Author:
Date: 1989-03-20
Edition: 4

The Soviet press today published the results of the Soviet Communist Party's election of Soviet People's Representatives.

According to reports, General Secretary Gorbachev of the CPSU Central Committee received 629 votes in favor and 12 votes against. Among the 12 elected representatives, including members and alternate members of the Politburo and Central Secretaries, Ligachev received the most votes against, totaling 78; Yakovlev followed with 59 votes against. Of the remaining 9, the highest number of votes against was 26, and the lowest was 2.

Among the 100 elected CPSU representatives, 52 received 641 votes in favor, all of whom are mostly ordinary workers or grassroots enterprise cadres.

The Central Committee Plenum held on March 15 was conducted through secret ballot voting. This is the first time in Soviet election history that the number of votes in favor and against each candidate has been made public.

### 苏联选举历史上第一次公布党员代表当选票数

1989-03-20
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联选举历史上第一次公布党员代表当选票数
    新华社莫斯科３月１９日电  苏联报刊今天公布了苏联共产党选举苏联人民代表的结果。
    据报道，苏共中央总书记戈尔巴乔夫获６２９张赞成票，１２张反对票。在当选代表的１２名政治局委员、候补委员和中央书记中，利加乔夫得到的反对票最多达７８张；雅科夫列夫其次，得５９张反对票。其余９人中，得反对票最多的为２６票，最少的２票。
    所有当选的１００名苏共代表中有５２人获得６４１张全部赞成票，他们大多为普通的工人或企业基层干部。
    ３月１５日召开的苏共中央全会是以无记名投票方式进行选举的。将每个候选人得到的赞成票和反对票的数目公布于众，这在苏联选举史上还是第一次。
