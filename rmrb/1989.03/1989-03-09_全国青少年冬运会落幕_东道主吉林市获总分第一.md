Title: The Second National Youth Winter Games Concludes
Author: Liang Xuezeng
Date: 1989-03-09
Edition: 4

The Second National Youth Winter Games concluded with the lowering of a light blue flag symbolizing the flight of seagulls, accompanied by the majestic melody of the "Olympic Hymn." The 18-day event came to an end in Jilin City. Jilin City secured the top spot with 19 gold medals, while Harbin City followed with 9.75 gold medals.

In recent years' major national ice and snow sports events, Heilongjiang athletes, for the first time, were surpassed by their old rivals, the athletes from Jilin Province.

This competition marked the first time that various cities and prefectures participated as individual units. The cities and prefectures that ranked from third to eighth in total gold medals were: the People's Liberation Army (7.33 gold medals), Changchun City (7 gold medals), Tonghua City (3.67 gold medals), Qiqihar City (3.25 gold medals), Hunjiang City (3 gold medals), Yichun City (2 gold medals). Altay Region (1 gold medal), Suihua Region (1 gold medal), and Jiamusi City (1 gold medal) tied for ninth place.

Zhang Caizhen, Vice Minister of the State Sports Commission, awarded the only athlete who broke a national youth record in this competition, Tong Haiyan from Harbin, for her achievement. Tong Haiyan set a new national youth record in the women's 3000-meter speed skating event with a time of 4 minutes and 47.97 seconds.

Zhang Caizhen, on behalf of the State Sports Commission, presented a banner to the host city, Jilin City, and the Jilin City Sports Commission.

### 全国青少年冬运会落幕  东道主吉林市获总分第一
梁学增
1989-03-09
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　全国青少年冬运会落幕
    东道主吉林市获总分第一
    本报吉林市３月８日电  伴随着雄壮的《运动员进行曲》乐曲，象征着海燕翱翔的浅蓝色方旗徐徐降下。至此，历时１８天的第二届全国青少年冬运会在此间落幕。吉林市（１９枚）荣登金牌总数第一宝座，哈尔滨市（９．７５枚）列第二位。
    在近些年的全国重大冰雪运动会上，黑龙江选手首次败给了老对手吉林省选手。
    这次大赛首次是以各地市（盟）为单位参赛，获金牌总数３至８名的是：解放军（７又１／３枚）、长春市（７枚）、通化市（３又２／３枚）、齐齐哈尔市（３又１／４枚）、浑江市（３枚）、伊春市（２枚）。阿尔泰地区（１枚）、绥化地区（１枚）、佳木斯市（１枚）并列第９名。
    国家体委副主任张彩珍向此次大赛唯一打破全国少年纪录的哈尔滨队佟海燕颁了奖。佟海燕以４分４７秒９７的成绩打破了女子３０００米速滑全国少年纪录。
    国家体委副主任张彩珍代表国家体委向东道主吉林市政府和市体委赠送了锦旗。　　　　　　　　　　　
　　（梁学增）
