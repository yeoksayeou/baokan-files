Title: Violet Iris Color-Changing Glaze Ceramic Sculpture Wins "Golden Dragon Soaring Award"
Author:
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 4th.

A ceramic sculpture that changes color under different light sources—Violet Iris Color-Changing Glaze Ceramic Sculpture—manufactured by the Jingdezhen Sculpture Porcelain Factory, has won the "Golden Dragon Soaring Award" from the Ministry of Light Industry.

The Violet Iris Color-Changing Glaze Ceramic Sculpture, which uses neodymium oxide as its color base, appears purple-blue or purple-red in sunlight; red under incandescent light; sky blue under fluorescent light; deep blue under high-pressure mercury lamps; and orange-red under high-pressure sodium lamps.

### 紫罗兰变色釉瓷雕获“金龙腾飞奖”

1989-03-04
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　紫罗兰变色釉瓷雕获“金龙腾飞奖”
    一种能在不同光源下变幻色彩的瓷雕——紫罗兰变色釉瓷雕，由景德镇雕塑瓷厂制成，获轻工部“金龙腾飞奖”。
    以稀土氧化钕为色基的紫罗兰变色釉装饰瓷雕后，在阳光下呈紫蓝或紫红色；在白炽灯下呈红色；在日光灯下呈天蓝色；在高压汞灯下呈深蓝色；在高压钠灯下呈橙红色。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　（据新华社）
