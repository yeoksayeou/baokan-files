Title: Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto Meets with Chi Haotian
Author:
Date: 1989-03-05
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Islamabad, March 4th.

Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto met with the delegation of the Chinese People's Liberation Army led by Chief of the General Staff Chi Haotian today.

During the meeting, Benazir Bhutto and Chi Haotian exchanged views on international and regional issues of mutual concern.

Benazir Bhutto said that her visit to China last month was very successful. She said, "We look forward to Premier Li Peng's visit to Pakistan this year."

She hopes that the friendly relations and cooperation between Pakistan and China will be further strengthened in the future.

Chi Haotian conveyed greetings from Chinese leaders Zhao Ziyang, Deng Xiaoping, Li Peng, and Li Xiannian to Benazir Bhutto. Benazir Bhutto also asked Chi Haotian to convey her greetings to the Chinese leaders.

### 贝·布托总理会见迟浩田

1989-03-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　贝·布托总理会见迟浩田
    新华社伊斯兰堡３月４日电  巴基斯坦总理贝·布托今天在这里会见了由迟浩田参谋长率领的中国人民解放军代表团。
    会见中，贝·布托同迟浩田就共同关心的国际和地区问题交换了看法。
    贝·布托说，上月她对中国的访问很成功。她说：“我们期待着中国总理李鹏今年访问巴基斯坦。”
    她希望今后巴中友好关系和合作将得到加强。
    迟浩田向贝·布托转达了中国领导人赵紫阳、邓小平、李鹏和李先念对她的问候。贝·布托也让迟浩田转达她对中国领导人的问候。
