Title: Ten Thousand Egrets Reside in Flower City
Author: Pan Jiyao
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 2

Over ten thousand night herons have made their home on a small island of just 2500 square meters in the heart of Guangzhou's bustling Liuxi Lake Park. They have built their nests and are raising their young there.

	Newspaper reporter Pan Jiyao
