Title: Mexican New Ambassador Presents Credentials to Yang Shangkun
Author:
Date: 1989-03-31
Edition: 2

The new Mexican Ambassador, Jorge Eduardo Navarrete, presented his credentials to State Chairman Yang Shangkun this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People. Navarrete arrived in Beijing on March 23.

### 墨新大使向杨尚昆递交国书

1989-03-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　墨新大使向杨尚昆递交国书
    新华社北京３月３０日电  墨西哥新任驻华大使豪尔赫·爱德华多·纳瓦雷特今天下午在人民大会堂向国家主席杨尚昆递交了国书。纳瓦雷特是３月２３日抵京的。
