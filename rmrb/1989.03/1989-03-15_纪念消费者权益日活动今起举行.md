Title: Activities to Commemorate International Consumers' Rights Day Begin Today
Author: Fú Gāng
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 14th.

Today, March 15th, is "International Consumers' Rights Day." In China, 1,170 cities will see consumer organizations holding various commemorative activities. The main contents include: promoting consumers' legal rights, on-site handling of consumer complaints, and organizing industrial and commercial enterprises to provide consultation services.

### 纪念消费者权益日活动今起举行
傅刚
1989-03-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　纪念消费者权益日活动今起举行
    据新华社北京３月１４日电  （记者傅刚）３月１５日是“国际消费者权益日”，我国将有１１７０个城市的消费者组织举行各种形式的纪念活动。主要内容是：宣传消费者的合法权利，现场受理消费者的投诉，组织工商企业进行咨询服务等。
