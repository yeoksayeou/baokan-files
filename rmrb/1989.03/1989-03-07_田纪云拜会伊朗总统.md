Title: Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meets with Iranian President
Author:
Date: 1989-03-07
Edition: 3

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meets with Iranian President

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, March 5 — Vice Premier Tian Jiyun, who is currently visiting Iran, met with Iranian President Ali Khamenei today in Tehran. The two sides held talks on bilateral relations and other issues.

Khamenei highly praised the relations between Iran and China during the talks.

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun said to President Khamenei, “The Chinese government will do its best to promote economic cooperation with Iran. China’s capabilities are limited, but our intentions are sincere.”

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun handed over a letter from Chairman Yang Shangkun to President Khamenei. In the letter, Chairman Yang Shangkun invited President Khamenei to visit China. President Khamenei gladly accepted the invitation.

### 田纪云拜会伊朗总统

1989-03-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　田纪云拜会伊朗总统
    新华社德黑兰３月５日电  正在伊朗访问的国务院副总理田纪云今天在这里拜会了伊朗总统哈梅内伊，双方就两国关系等问题举行了会谈。
    哈梅内伊在会谈中高度地评价了伊中两国关系。
    田纪云副总理对哈梅内伊总统说，“中国政府将尽力推动同伊朗的经济合作，中国的力量有限，但愿望是真诚的。”
    田纪云副总理向哈梅内伊总统转交了杨尚昆主席的一封信件。杨尚昆主席在信中邀请哈梅内伊访问中国，哈梅内伊总统愉快地接受了这一邀请。
