Title: Salvador Guerrillas Halt Talks with Government
Author:
Date: 1989-03-11
Edition: 3

	Salvador Guerrillas Halt Talks with Government

	Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City: San Salvador reports that on March 8, the Salvadoran guerrillas decided to suspend negotiations seeking direct dialogue with the government.

	This was announced by the Farabundo Marti National Liberation Front of El Salvador on March 8.

### 萨游击队不同政府谈判

1989-03-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　萨游击队不同政府谈判
    新华社墨西哥城电 圣萨尔瓦多消息：萨尔瓦多游击队８日决定中断寻求同政府举行直接对话的谈判。
    这是萨尔瓦多法拉本多·马蒂民族解放阵线８日宣布的。
