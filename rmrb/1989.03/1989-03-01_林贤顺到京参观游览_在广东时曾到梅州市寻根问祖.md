Title: Lin Xianshun Visits Beijing for Sightseeing
Author: Qi Hanshuao
Date: 1989-03-01
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 28th.

Lin Xianshun, a former Nationalist air force major, arrived in Beijing today after completing his visit to Guangdong Province.

During his stay in Guangdong, Lin visited Guangzhou, where he toured the city, paid tribute at the Yellow Flower Hill Martyrs' Cemetery, visited the Whampoa Military Academy, and paid his respects at the former residence of Dr. Sun Yat-sen. He also visited Zhongshan City and the Zhuhai Special Economic Zone. Additionally, he returned to his ancestral hometown in Meizhou City, Guangdong Province, where he was warmly welcomed by his fellow townspeople.

It is understood that Lin will continue his sightseeing in Beijing, where he will meet with leaders from relevant departments, as well as Huang Zhicheng and Li Dawai.

### 林贤顺到京参观游览  在广东时曾到梅州市寻根问祖
齐含笑
1989-03-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　林贤顺到京参观游览
    在广东时曾到梅州市寻根问祖
    新华社北京２月２８日电  （记者齐含笑）从台湾飞回大陆的原国民党空军中校飞行员林贤顺结束在广东省的参观访问，今日到达北京。
    林贤顺在广东省逗留期间，游览了广州市容，参观了黄花岗七十二烈士墓、黄埔军校旧址，拜谒了孙中山先生故居，并到中山市、珠海经济特区参观访问。他还回到祖籍广东省梅州市寻根问祖，受到家乡父老的热情欢迎。
    据了解，林贤顺将在北京继续参观游览，有关部门领导及黄植诚、李大维等将与他见面。
