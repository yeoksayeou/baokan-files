Title: Selection of Excellent Songs for the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation
Author: Lai Renqiong
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 4

To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the nation, the Beijing Youth Cultural Consultation and Exchange Center, the Chinese Musicians Association, and the Yan'an Lu Xun Art Academy Alumni Association are jointly organizing a selection activity for "Songs that Stir Up My Beautiful Memories" from the past 40 years since the founding of the nation.

All types of songs created since the founding of the nation are eligible for selection. Excellent songs created in Hong Kong, Taiwan, and overseas by Chinese people that are popular in mainland China can also be selected. Songs created before liberation but continue to be popular after liberation will receive an honorable mention.

A panel of judges, consisting of famous musicians, singers, and related experts such as Lü Qi, Guang Weiran, Li Huanzhi, Liu Chi, Fu Gengchen, Wang Liping, Guo Lanying, and Hu Songhua, will select 60 songs and 10 outstanding singers based on public voting.

The selection activity will begin in late March and end in late May. Ballots, selection instructions, and candidate songs will be published in the newspaper soon.

### 建国４０周年优秀歌曲评选在即
赖仁琼
1989-03-14
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　建国４０周年优秀歌曲评选在即
    本报讯  记者赖仁琼报道：为迎接建国四十周年，北京青年文化咨询交流中心、中国音乐家协会、延安鲁艺校友会等单位联合举办建国四十年来“唤起我美好回忆的那些歌”评选活动。
    凡建国以来各类创作歌曲均在评选之列。港台及海外华人创作，在大陆流行的优秀歌曲亦可入选。虽在解放前创作，但在解放后继续流行的歌曲精粹将获得荣誉奖。
    由吕骥、光未然、李焕之、刘炽、傅庚辰、王立平、郭兰英、胡松华等著名音乐家、歌唱家及有关专家组成的评委会将根据群众的评选选出６０首歌曲、１０名优秀歌唱演员。
    评选活动将从３月下旬开始至５月底结束。选票、评选说明和候选歌曲将于近日在报上公布。
