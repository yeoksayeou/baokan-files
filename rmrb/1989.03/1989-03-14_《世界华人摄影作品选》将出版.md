Title: Selection of Photographic Works by Overseas Chinese to Be Published
Author:
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 4

The large-scale photography collection "Selection of Photographic Works by Overseas Chinese," compiled by returned overseas Chinese photographers Fang Xuehui and Ge Qian, will be published by the China Friendship Publishing Company.

The collection features 124 outstanding works by 80 overseas Chinese, Chinese-American, and Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan photographers. It is a high-quality collection showcasing the works of renowned photographers.

### 《世界华人摄影作品选》将出版

1989-03-14
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　《世界华人摄影作品选》将出版
    本报讯  由归侨摄影家方学辉和葛骞主编的大型摄影集《世界华人摄影作品选》，将由中国友谊出版公司出版。
    影集收入８０位华侨、华人摄影家和台港澳摄影家的１２４幅佳作，是荟萃名家名作的高档次影集。
