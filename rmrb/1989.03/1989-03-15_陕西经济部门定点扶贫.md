Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 2

Shaanxi Economic Departments Implement Targeted Poverty Alleviation

Xian, March 14 (Xinhua) — From 1986 to 1988, Shaanxi Province saw 815,000 impoverished households and 3.7 million people resolve their food and clothing needs. However, there are still 520,000 impoverished households remaining. Starting this year, the provincial Party Committee and the provincial government have assigned 56 economic departments to 33 key impoverished counties and 14 generally impoverished counties with more difficulties. They will implement targeted poverty alleviation, ensuring that the assignments remain unchanged for three years.

### 陕西经济部门定点扶贫
景险峰
1989-03-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　陕西经济部门定点扶贫
    本报西安３月１４日电  记者景险峰报道：１９８６至１９８８这三年，陕西省又有８１．５万贫困户、３７０万人解决了温饱问题。然而，目前仍有５２万贫困户。从今年起，省委、省政府５６个经济部门对３３个重点贫困县和困难较多的１４个一般贫困县定点定员，实行联县扶贫，一定３年不变。
