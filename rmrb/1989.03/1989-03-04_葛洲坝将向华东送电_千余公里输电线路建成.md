Title: Gezhouba to Supply Power to East China
Author: Wú Róng Jiàn
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 1

Gezhouba to Supply Power to East China
1,000-Kilometer Transmission Line Completed

New China News Agency, Wuhan, March 3 — The last giant converter transformer for the Gezhouba direct current converter station was safely installed on March 1. This marks the imminent start of power transmission from the Gezhouba Hydropower Station to East China.

The Gezhouba direct current converter station has a transmission capacity of 600,000 kilowatts, making it the highest voltage level and largest capacity direct current converter station in China. All the equipment for the station was imported from abroad, totaling 7 transformers. The 1,080-kilometer-long transmission line has also been completed.

### 葛洲坝将向华东送电  千余公里输电线路建成
吴荣建
1989-03-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　葛洲坝将向华东送电
    千余公里输电线路建成
    新华社武汉３月３日电  （通讯员吴荣建）葛洲坝直流换流站最后一台巨型换流变压器，３月１日安全落位。它预示着葛洲坝水电站发出的强大电流即将向华东地区输送。
    葛洲坝直流换流站，输送容量为６０万千瓦，是目前我国电压等级最高、容量最大的直流换流站。全站设备全部从国外引进，共有７台变压器。目前全长１０８０公里的输电线路也已建成。
