Title: The State Council's Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office Hosts a Banquet for Hong Kong and Macao Journalists
Author:
Date: 1989-03-22
Edition: 2

The State Council's Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office hosted a banquet tonight at the Beijing International Hotel for journalists from Hong Kong and Macao who are covering the second session of the Seventh National People's Congress and the second session of the Seventh Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. Vice Directors Li Hou and Lu Ping of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office had a cordial conversation with the journalists. The atmosphere at the event was relaxed and lively.

### 国务院港澳办宴请港澳记者

1989-03-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　国务院港澳办宴请港澳记者
    新华社北京３月２１日电  国务院港澳办公室今晚在北京国际大厦宴请了采访七届全国人大二次会议和全国政协七届二次会议的港澳记者。国务院港澳办公室副主任李后、鲁平同记者们进行了亲切的交谈。会上气氛轻松、活跃。
