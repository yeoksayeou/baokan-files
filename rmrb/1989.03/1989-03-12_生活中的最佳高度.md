Date: 1989-03-12

    The Optimal Heights in Daily Life
    Desks: The height should be such that when standing upright with palms flat on the desk, there is no need to bend or flex the elbows. The optimal height is approximately 75-80 centimeters.
    Lighting: The height of incandescent bulbs above the desk should be 100 centimeters for a 60-watt bulb, 50 centimeters for a 52-watt bulb, and 30 centimeters for a 15-watt bulb. For fluorescent lights, the height above the desk should be 150 centimeters for a 40-watt bulb, 140 centimeters for a 30-watt bulb, 110 centimeters for a 20-watt bulb, and 55 centimeters for an 8-watt bulb.
    Pillows: The height should be equal to the width of one shoulder, allowing the head to tilt slightly forward, the neck muscles to relax fully, and breathing to remain unobstructed, ensuring normal blood supply to the chest. The optimal height is approximately 15 centimeters, and it can be adjusted lower for children. For the elderly, the pillow should not be too high to avoid insufficient blood supply to the head.
    Shoe Heels: For young men, the heel should not exceed 3 centimeters. For women, the optimal heel height is 4-6 centimeters. For the elderly, the optimal heel height is 2 centimeters. For children and teenagers, whose foot arches are not fully developed, flat shoes are recommended.
    Chairs: The height of the seat from the ground should be about 1 centimeter lower than the length of the calf.
    Beds: The bed should be slightly higher than the user's knees to make getting in and out of bed convenient.
    Stoves: The stove is generally 65-70 centimeters high, and the pot rack should be 4 centimeters away from the burner. Whether using a flat-bottomed pot or a pointed-bottomed pot, the pot rack should be elevated to maximize the use of heat.
    Shower Heads: The height from the ground to the shower head should be 200-220 centimeters, allowing people to move freely in the bathroom without hitting the shower head, and the water flow should be just right.

### 生活中的最佳高度

1989-03-12
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

　　生活中的最佳高度
    桌子：应为身体直立、两手掌平放于桌面上不必弯腰或屈肘关节为佳，约７５—８０厘米。
    照明：白炽灯的灯泡距桌面高度，６０瓦为１００厘米，５２瓦为５０厘米，１５瓦为３０厘米。日光灯距桌面高度，４０瓦为１５０厘米，３０瓦为１４０厘米，２０瓦为１１０厘米，８瓦为５５厘米。
    枕头：应与一侧肩宽相等，这样可使头略向前弯曲，颈部肌肉充分放松，呼吸保持通畅，胸部血液供应正常。其高度约为１５厘米，儿童可相应降低。老人用枕头不宜过高以免头部供血不足。
    鞋跟：男青年不应超过３厘米，女鞋后跟高度以４—６厘米为宜，老年人最好为２厘米，少年儿童因足弓尚未发育完全，以穿平跟鞋为好。
    座椅：椅面距地面的高度应低于小腿的长度１厘米左右。
    床铺：以略高于使用者的膝盖为宜，使上下床感到方便。
    灶台：灶台一般在６５—７０厘米，锅架离火口４厘米为宜。无论使用平底锅还是尖底锅，都应把锅架撑起，以保证最大限度地利用火力。
    淋浴喷头：自地面至喷头一般在２００—２２０厘米，人在卫生间内不会碰撞到喷头上，喷下的水流又恰到好处。
    　　　　　　　（２月９日《中国青年报》）
