Title: Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Visits the United Arab Emirates
Author:
Date: 1989-03-18
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kuwait, March 16th.

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun arrived in Abu Dhabi, the capital of the United Arab Emirates, on the afternoon of March 16th from Kuwait, beginning a three-day official visit.

According to news from Abu Dhabi, Tian Jiyun told the press at the airport that since China and the UAE established diplomatic relations in 1984, the relationship between the two countries has developed smoothly. During his visit, he will hold talks with UAE leaders on issues of mutual concern.

The UAE is the last stop of Tian Jiyun's five-nation tour of the Middle East.

Xinhua News Agency, Kuwait, March 16th.

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun, who is visiting Kuwait, suggested to Kuwaiti Minister of Commerce and Industry Khalid during a meeting on March 16th that China and Kuwait should utilize their respective advantages in manpower and resources to undertake one or two major cooperation projects, elevating the relationship between the two countries to a new level.

Khalid expressed that this suggestion is very important to Kuwait and believes it "aligns with Kuwait's investment intentions."

### 田纪云抵阿联酋访问

1989-03-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　田纪云抵阿联酋访问
    新华社科威特３月１６日电  中国副总理田纪云１６日下午从科威特抵达阿拉伯联合酋长国首都阿布扎比，开始为期３天的正式访问。
    据来自阿布扎比的消息说，田纪云在机场对新闻界表示，中阿两国自１９８４年建交以来，相互关系发展顺利。在访问期间，他将同阿联酋领导人就共同关心的问题进行会谈。
    阿联酋是田纪云西亚５国之行的最后一站。
    新华社科威特３月１６日电　正在科威特访问的中国副总理田纪云１６日在会见科威特商业和工业大臣哈立德时建议说，中科两国应利用各自在人力和资源的有利条件搞一个两个大的合作项目，把两国的关系发展到一个新的水平。
    哈立德表示，这个建议对科威特很重要，认为它“符合科威特的投资意向”。
