Title: The Journal "Seeking Truth" Holds a Symposium on Current Economic Issues
Author:
Date: 1989-03-03
Edition: 6

The journal "Seeking Truth" held a symposium on current economic issues on February 27. The journal invited experts and scholars from the capital, as well as officials from relevant departments, to discuss economic development and economic reform. The speakers shared their views on the new situations and their own perspectives since the Third Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee, focusing on rectifying the economic environment, rectifying the economic order, and deepening the reform comprehensively.

### 《求是》杂志召开当前经济问题座谈会

1989-03-03
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：理论简讯

    《求是》杂志召开当前经济问题座谈会
    本报讯　2月27日，《求是》杂志社邀请首都专家学者、有关部门负责人，座谈当前经济发展和经济改革问题。发言者谈了十三届三中全会以来，治理经济环境，整顿经济秩序，全面深化改革中的新情况及自己的看法。
