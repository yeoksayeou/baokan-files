Title: May 31 is "World No-Tobacco Day"
Author: Wén Mǐn
Date: 1989-03-21
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 18th.

A reporter learned today from a telephone conference held by the National Health and Hygiene Committee that the World Health Organization has decided to designate May 31 as this year's "World No-Tobacco Day."

This is another day dedicated to maintaining human health and opposing smoking, following last year's World No-Tobacco Day on April 7.

### ５月３１日为“世界无烟日”
温闽
1989-03-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　５月３１日为“世界无烟日”
    新华社北京３月１８日电  （实习生温闽）记者今天从全国爱卫会电话会议上获悉，世界卫生组织决定将５月３１日作为今年的“世界无烟日”。
    这是继去年４月７日的世界无烟日以来的又一个维护人类健康、反对吸烟的日子。
