Title: 53 New Cities Established Last Year, Total Number of Cities in the Country Reaches 434
Author: Xing Xuan
Date: 1989-03-02
Edition: 1

	1988 saw the establishment of 53 new cities across the country with the approval of the State Council. Among these, 5 were prefecture-level cities and 48 were county-level cities. Additionally, 8 county-level cities were upgraded to prefecture-level cities, and 6 implemented the system of city administration over counties. By the end of 1988, China had a total of 434 cities (including 3 municipalities directly under the Central Government). Among these, there were 183 prefecture-level cities and 248 county-level cities. Of the 183 prefecture-level cities, 164 administered 739 counties.

	The new cities established in 1988 are as follows: Wu'an in Hebei Province; Gujiao and Shuozhou (prefecture-level) in Shanxi Province; Kaiyuan in Liaoning Province; Jilin Province's Ji'an, Huadian, Longjing, Hunchun, Jiutai, and Da'an; Heilongjiang Province's Fujin, Tieli, Shangzhi, Shuangcheng, and Mishan; Yixing in Jiangsu Province; Yiwu, Dongyang, Cixi, and Fenghua in Zhejiang Province; Guichi in Anhui Province; Ningde in Fujian Province; Zhangshu and Fengcheng in Jiangxi Province; Ruzhou, Jiyuan, Yuzhou, Weihui, Huixian, and Dengzhou in Henan Province; Laizhou, Tengzhou, Laoling, Wengcheng, and Lecheng in Shandong Province; Zaoyang, Qianjiang, Guangshui, and Dangyang in Hubei Province; Yuanjiang in Hunan Province; Shanwei, Heyuan, Yangjiang, and Qingyuan (all prefecture-level) in Guangdong Province; Guigang in the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region; Guanghan, Jiangyou, Dujiangyan, and Emeishan in Sichuan Province; Yulin, Ankang, and Shangzhou in Shaanxi Province; and Delingha in Qinghai Province.

### 去年新设城市五十三个  全国城市总数达到四百三十四个
邢萱
1989-03-02
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    去年新设城市五十三个
    全国城市总数达到四百三十四个
    本报讯　1988年，经国务院批准，全国新设城市53个。其中，地级市5个，县级市48个。另外，由原县级市升格为地级市8个，实行市领导县体制的6个。截至1988年底，我国共有设市城市434个（包括3个直辖市）。其中，地级市183个，县级市248个。在183个地级市中，有164个地级市领导739个县。
    1988年新设市如下：河北省的武安市；山西省的古交市、朔州市（地级）；辽宁省的开原市；吉林省的集安市、桦甸市、龙井市、珲春市、九台市、大安市；黑龙江省的富锦市、铁力市、尚志市、双城市、密山市；江苏省的宜兴市；浙江省的义乌市、东阳市、慈溪市、奉化市；安徽省的贵池市；福建省的宁德市；江西省的樟树市、丰城市；河南省的汝州市、济源市、禹州市、卫辉市、辉县市、邓州市；山东省的莱州市、滕州市、乐陵市、文登市、乐城市；湖北省的枣阳市、潜江市、广水市、当阳市；湖南省的沅江市；广东省的汕尾市、河源市、阳江市、清源市（四市都为地级）；广西壮族自治区的贵港市；四川省的广汉市、江油市、都江堰市、峨眉山市；陕西省的榆林市、安康市、商州市；青海省的德令哈市。　　　　　（邢萱）
