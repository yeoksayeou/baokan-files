I. Key Industries and Products to be Supported

(1) Agriculture, Forestry, Animal Husbandry, Fishery, and Agricultural Machinery

	Grain, cotton, oilseeds, sugar crops, meat, eggs, poultry, dairy, silk cocoons, tea, vegetables, natural rubber, wool
	High-quality dried and fresh fruits (utilizing mountainous areas)
	Seedlings, forest cultivation, fast-growing and high-yielding forests, protective forests, high-quality medicinal materials, local medicinal materials
	Aquaculture (utilizing existing water surfaces) and distant-water fisheries
	Fertilizers, pesticides and their intermediates, veterinary drugs, agricultural films, suitable agricultural machinery and spare parts
	Compound and mixed feed, feed additives

(2) Light Industry

	Grain processing industry
	Vegetable oil processing industry
	Sugar industry
	Salt industry
	Pulp, paper, and paperboard
	Synthetic detergents and raw materials
	Special products for ethnic minorities
	Daily necessities, small hardware
	Energy-saving new-type electric light sources and lamps
	Daily-use ceramics, enameled products
	Famous-brand household refrigerators and compressors
	State-famous liquors, famous and high-grade cigarettes
	Infant and toddler food
	Famous-brand bicycles, famous-brand pianos
	Export products

(3) Textile Industry

	Yarn, cloth
	Cotton textiles (excluding small cotton mills)
	Blended wool and pure synthetic fiber wool products
	Ramie, hemp products
	Silk products, knitted products
	Textile printing and finishing products
	Chemical fiber pulp
	Acrylic, nylon, acrylic-chlorine, polyester, vinylon, viscose fibers
	New-type special chemical fibers
	Special industrial-use textiles
	Appropriate and marketable clothing and corresponding accessories

(4) Transportation, Postal, and Telecommunications

	Coal, agricultural, and foreign trade key material transportation, passenger transportation
	Local and long-distance calls, user telegrams

(5) Coal Industry

	Coal mining (excluding unlicensed mining)
	Coal washing
	Coal-water slurry products
	Low-heat-value fuel comprehensive development and utilization

(6) Electric Power Industry

	Hydropower, thermal power (including cogeneration), nuclear power, other power (excluding oil-fired power generation and small steam turbine units within large power grids), power transmission and transformation

(7) Petroleum and Natural Gas Extraction

(8) Ferrous Metallurgy

	Iron ore, chromium ore, manganese ore (excluding unlicensed mining)
	Pig iron, steel, thin plates, silicon steel sheets, strip steel, galvanized plates, tin-plated plates, medium and thick plates, extra-thick plates, seamless pipes, oil pipes, heavy rails, wheels, and wheel rims, ferroalloys

(9) Non-ferrous Metallurgy

	Aluminum, copper, lead, zinc, tin, nickel, gold, silver, rare earth minerals (excluding unlicensed mining)
	Aluminum, copper, lead, zinc, tin, nickel, gold, silver smelting products
	Copper, aluminum plates, strips, foils, pipes, and rods
	Rare earth smelting and application products
	Fluoride salts

(10) Chemical and Petrochemical Industry

	Chemical minerals (excluding unlicensed mining)
	Alkalis, acids, and other basic chemical raw materials, organic chemical raw materials and intermediates
	Primary and secondary petroleum processing products
	Ethylene, plastics, synthetic resins, synthetic rubber, synthetic fiber monomers
	Fine chemical products
	Export rubber products, radial tires

(11) Machinery Industry

	Large-scale power generation equipment and corresponding power transmission and transformation equipment
	Mold manufacturing (precision stamping, precision forging, injection molds)
	Numerically controlled, high-precision, heavy-duty metal cutting machine tools
	High-efficiency, multi-station forging and pressing equipment
	Large-scale, precision bearings
	Hydraulic components and hydraulic parts, pneumatic components, sealing components
	Industrial automation, electrical, optical, navigation, quality inspection and monitoring, measurement instruments and meters
	Complete sets of equipment for mining, petroleum, coal, chemical, petrochemical, metallurgy, ports, docks, electrical, electronics, cement, new-type textiles, and new-type papermaking
	Castings, forgings, heat treatment, electroplating products
	Environmental protection machinery
	State-designated automobiles and assemblies, spare parts
	Railway locomotives, passenger cars, freight cars
	Ships of 0.5 million tons and above
	Containers

(12) Electronics Industry

	Economy-of-scale, specialized production of electronic components, integrated circuits
	State-designated cathode ray tubes and glass bulbs
	Electronic computers and peripheral equipment
	Optoelectronic devices, sensitive elements, and sensors
	Electronic special materials
	State-designated large-scale program-controlled and user-program-controlled switching equipment
	State-designated mobile communication, microwave communication, fiber-optic communication, satellite communication system equipment
	Famous-brand color televisions

(13) Building Materials and Other Non-metallic Mineral Products

	New-type wall materials and roofing materials
	425-grade and above high-grade cement and special cement
	High-quality flat glass and deep-processed glass products
	Carbon products, refractory raw materials, and new-type refractory materials
	Export non-metallic minerals and products
	Import-substituting building sanitary ceramics

(14) Pharmaceutical Industry

	High-efficiency and scarce chemical raw materials and pharmaceutical intermediates
	Biochemical drugs
	Radioactive drugs, anti-tumor drugs, family planning drugs
	Chinese herbal pieces, famous-brand Chinese patent medicines

(15) Forestry Industry

	Comprehensive utilization of wood
	Forest chemical products

(16) Aerospace Industry (omitted)

(17) Emerging Industries

	Microelectronics technology
	New materials
	Biotechnology
	Information and communication technology
	Isotope radiation and laser technology
	Marine development

(18) Export Products with Good Economic Benefits, Especially Processed Goods

	State-approved military products
