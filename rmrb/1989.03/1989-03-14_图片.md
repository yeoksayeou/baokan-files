Title: First Match Between Women's Football Teams from Both Sides of the Taiwan Strait
Author: Lü Binghui
Date: 1989-03-14
Edition: 4

On March 12th, women's football teams from both sides of the Taiwan Strait competed for the first time in Hong Kong. The Guangdong team won 1:0 against the Ming Chuan team from Taiwan. The image shows Hong Hui from the Guangdong team and Yang Yacheng from the Ming Chuan team contesting for the ball.
