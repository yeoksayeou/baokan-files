Title: Red Crane Island
Author: Kui
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 7

In the West Indies, there is a sparsely populated island called Bonaire. The island is home to tens of thousands of long-legged red cranes, hence it is also known as "Flamingo Island" – Red Crane Island. The red crane, also known as the great crane or flamingo, is one of the world's rare birds.

### 红鹤岛

1989-03-12
第7版(国际副刊)
专栏：

　　红鹤岛
    在西印度群岛中，有一个人迹稀少的博内尔岛，岛上有几万只长腿红鹤，所以人们又称它为“佛拉明戈岛”——红鹤岛。红鹤又名大鹤、火烈鸟，是世界珍禽之一。
