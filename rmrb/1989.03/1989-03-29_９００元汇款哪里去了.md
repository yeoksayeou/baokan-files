Title: Where Did the 900 Yuan Remittance Go?
Author: Jiang Xingxuan
Date: 1989-03-29
Edition: 6

	I remitted 900 yuan to Nanjing from the Zhenyuan County Post Office on July 29th of last year. However, the recipient informed me that they did not receive the money. I have repeatedly inquired at the Zhenyuan County Post Office and filled out two inquiry forms, but I have not found any information about it. I do not know where the 900 yuan remittance has gone?

		Zhenyuan County, Guizhou	Jiang Xingxuan

### ９００元汇款哪里去了
江星轩
1989-03-29
第6版(读者来信)
专栏：监督哨

　　９００元汇款哪里去了
    我于去年７月２９日在镇远县邮电局往南京汇款９００元，但收款人来信说没有收到这笔钱。我多次到镇远县邮电局查询，并两次填写了查讯单，都没有查到。不知这９００元汇款哪里去了？
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　贵州镇远县　江星轩
