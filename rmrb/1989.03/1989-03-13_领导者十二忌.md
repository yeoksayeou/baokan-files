Title: Twelve Things Leaders Should Avoid
Author: Jiànguó
Date: 1989-03-13
Edition: 5

The following are twelve things that leaders should avoid:

1. Avoid making promises and then breaking them;
2. Avoid making casual promises that cannot be kept;
3. Avoid speaking recklessly and making baseless comments;
4. Avoid being moody and acting on impulse;
5. Avoid favoring or discriminating based on personal likes and dislikes;
6. Avoid having a narrow mind and being jealous of talented people;
7. Avoid being pleased by flattery and promoting unscrupulous individuals;
8. Avoid being greedy for fame and wealth, and taking advantage of others;
9. Avoid shifting blame to others and being afraid to take responsibility;
10. Avoid being harsh to others and lenient with oneself;
11. Avoid adopting a patriarchal attitude and using authority to intimidate others;
12. Avoid being arrogant and disregarding laws and regulations.

### 领导者十二忌
建国
1989-03-13
第5版(国内专页（政治·法律）)
专栏：

　　领导者十二忌
    一、忌言而无信、出尔反尔；
    二、忌轻易许诺、不予兑现；
    三、忌信口雌黄、妄加评说；
    四、忌喜怒无常、感情用事；
    五、忌凭己好恶、处事不公；
    六、忌心胸狭窄、妒能嫉贤；
    七、忌闻颂则喜、重用小人；
    八、忌贪名好利、巧取豪夺；
    九、忌诿过于人、怕负责任；
    十、忌对人苛严、于己宽松；
    十一、忌家长作风、以势压人；
    十二、忌妄自尊大、目无法纪。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（建国）
