Title: Friendly News Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-03-04
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, March 1st.

	● Indian Prime Minister and Congress Party (India) Chairman Rajiv Gandhi said on February 28 in New Delhi that India and China can conduct extensive cooperation while striving to build their respective countries.
	Gandhi expressed this view during a meeting with the delegation from the Chinese Communist Party led by Zhu Liang, the head of the International Liaison Department, who was visiting India. He emphasized that the two countries should play a greater role in the development of global relations.
	When discussing his recent visit to China, Gandhi praised Chairman Deng Xiaoping's points on establishing a new international political order and a new international economic order based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.
	Zhu Liang conveyed a letter from CCP General Secretary Zhao Ziyang to Gandhi and extended greetings from the Chinese leadership.

	● At the invitation of the Ministry of the Armed Forces of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, a delegation from the People's Liberation Army of China, led by Major General Song Wenzhong, Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, arrived in Pyongyang by plane on March 1 to conduct a friendly visit to North Korea.

	● Jordanian Prime Minister Rifai met with the outgoing Chinese Ambassador to Jordan, Zhang Zhen, on March 2 in Amman.

	● Yemeni Socialist Party General Secretary Badr and Chairman of the Supreme People's Council Ali Attaas separately received the outgoing Chinese Ambassador to Yemen, Huang Zhen, on March 2.

	● Pakistani President Muhammad Zia-ul-Haq awarded the Military Excellence Medal to General Zhang Zhen, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, at the Presidential Palace on March 1, in recognition of his outstanding contributions to the friendly relations between the armed forces of China and Pakistan. Zhang and his delegation arrived in Islamabad on February 27 for a nine-day visit to Pakistan.

	● Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Noboru met with Wang Dexian, President of the Bank of China, at the Prime Minister's official residence on March 2. During the meeting, Takeshita expressed that the Japanese government would actively support private enterprises in expanding investments in China and look forward to the continuous development of economic relations between Japan and China.

### 友好简讯

1989-03-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　友好简讯
    ●印度总理、国大党（英）主席拉·甘地２月２８日在新德里说，印度和中国在共同努力建设各自的国家时可以进行广泛的合作。
    拉·甘地在会见正在印度访问的中联部部长朱良率领的中国共产党代表团时谈了这一看法。他强调，两国应在发展全球关系中发挥更大作用。
    在谈到他最近对中国的访问时，拉·甘地对邓小平主席关于在和平共处五项原则基础上建立国际政治新秩序和国际经济新秩序的论点表示赞赏。
    朱良向拉·甘地转递了中共中央总书记赵紫阳写给他的一封信并转达了中国领导人对他的问候。
    ●应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国武装力量部的邀请，以中国人民解放军国防部外事局局长宋文中少将为团长的中国人民解放军外事代表团，于３月１日乘飞机抵达平壤，对朝鲜进行友好访问。
    ●约旦首相里法伊２日在安曼接见了即将离任的中国驻约旦大使张真。
    ●民主也门社会党总书记比德和最高人民委员会主席团主席阿塔斯２日分别接见即将离任的中国驻民主也门大使黄振。
    ●巴基斯坦总统吴拉姆·伊沙克·汗３月１日在总统府授予中国人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田军事卓越勋章，表彰他为中巴两国军队的友好事业所作出的卓越贡献。迟浩田一行是２月２７日抵达伊斯兰堡对巴基斯坦进行为期９天访问的。（据新华社）
    ●日本首相竹下登３月２日在首相官邸会见中国银行行长王德衍。会见时，竹下表示，日本政府将积极支持民间企业扩大对华投资，期待日中经济关系不断发展。　　　（本报专电）
