Title: Teacher Money Ren Dedicated to Educating Soldiers
Author: Zhang Tongzhi
Date: 1989-03-10
Edition: 5

    Teacher Money Ren, from Nanjing Xiaozhuang Normal School, is dedicated to helping the soldiers of the Jiangsu Provincial Armed Police Corps' Directly Affiliated Detachment improve their cultural knowledge. He cares deeply about their growth and progress, earning him the title of "Campus Gardener" from the soldiers. The photo shows Money Ren teaching the soldiers.
