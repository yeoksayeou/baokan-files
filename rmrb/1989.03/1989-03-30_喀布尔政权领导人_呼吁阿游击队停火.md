Title: Kabul Regime Leader Calls for Afghan Mujahideen to Cease Fire
Author:
Date: 1989-03-30
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 29th.

Kabul regime leader Najibullah on March 28th appealed to the commanders of the Afghan Mujahideen to stop fighting against the government forces and promised them local autonomy.

Najibullah said in a speech broadcast on television that the Mujahideen who accept the ceasefire can retain their weapons.

### 喀布尔政权领导人  呼吁阿游击队停火

1989-03-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　喀布尔政权领导人        
    呼吁阿游击队停火
    据新华社北京３月２９日电  喀布尔消息：喀布尔政权领导人纳吉布拉３月２８日呼吁阿富汗境内游击队指挥官们停止同政府军交战，并答应给他们地方自治。
    纳吉布拉在通过电视台发表的讲话中说，接受停火的游击队可保留自己的武器。
