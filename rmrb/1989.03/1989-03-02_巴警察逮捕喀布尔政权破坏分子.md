Title: Pakistani Police Arrest Kabul Regime Saboteurs
Author:
Date: 1989-03-02
Edition: 3

Pakistani police authorities announced on March 1st that Pakistani police have recently arrested eleven saboteurs of the Kabul regime.

The police stated that these eleven saboteurs belong to five terrorist organizations active in Pakistan's Balochistan Province.

The police said that these saboteurs carried out forty-two acts of sabotage, explosions, and terrorist incidents in several major cities in Pakistan, resulting in the deaths of many people and injuries to hundreds.

### 巴警察逮捕喀布尔政权破坏分子

1989-03-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴警察逮捕喀布尔政权破坏分子
    巴基斯坦警察当局三月一日宣布，巴基斯坦警察最近逮捕了十一名喀布尔政权的破坏分子。
    警方说，这十一名破坏分子来自在巴基斯坦俾路支省活动的五个恐怖组织。
    警方说，这些破坏分子在巴基斯坦一些大城市里制造了四十二起破坏、爆炸及恐怖事件，导致许多人死亡，数百人受伤。
