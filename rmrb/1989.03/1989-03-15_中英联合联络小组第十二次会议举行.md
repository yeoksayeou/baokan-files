Title: The Twelfth Meeting of the Sino-British Joint Liaison Group Held
Author: Huang Riyao
Date: 1989-03-15
Edition: 2

The twelfth meeting of the Sino-British Joint Liaison Group was held this morning in the pleasant spring weather of Beijing. The British chief representative, McPherson, humorously remarked that the Chinese side had arranged this meeting during the fine spring weather, which would be conducive to both sides' discussions on issues.

The Chinese chief representative, Ke Zaixu, and the British chief representative, McPherson, first reviewed the progress made by the Joint Liaison Group over the past three and a half years since its establishment and expressed pride in the achievements made so far. Both sides wished for a satisfactory outcome from this meeting.

The meeting is scheduled to conclude on March 17.

### 中英联合联络小组第十二次会议举行
黄日尧
1989-03-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　中英联合联络小组第十二次会议举行
    新华社北京３月１４日电  （记者黄日尧）中英联合联络小组第十二次会议今天上午在春色宜人的北京举行。英方首席代表麦若彬风趣地说，中方把这次会议安排在春光明媚的天气下举行，将有助于双方对问题的探讨。
    中方首席代表柯在铄和英方首席代表麦若彬首先回顾了联合联络小组成立三年半以来所取得的进展，并对过去所取得的成绩感到自豪。双方预祝这次会议取得满意的结果。
    会议订于３月１７日结束。
