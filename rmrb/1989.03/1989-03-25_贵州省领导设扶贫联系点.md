Title: Leaders of Guizhou Province Establish Poverty Alleviation Contact Points
Author: Pan Di Du
Date: 1989-03-25
Edition: 2

To further explore the path and experience of poverty alleviation work and to better guide the province's poverty alleviation efforts, recently, Guizhou Provincial Party Secretary Liu Zhengwei and Governor Wang Chao Wen have established contact points in the two impoverished counties of Luodian and Zhijin. In the future, they will spend more time in these two counties to understand the poverty alleviation situation and study new problems.

### 贵州省领导设扶贫联系点
潘帝都
1989-03-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　贵州省领导设扶贫联系点
    本报贵阳３月２４日电记者潘帝都报道：为进一步探索扶贫工作的路子和经验，更好地指导全省的扶贫工作，最近，贵州省委书记刘正威和省长王朝文分别在罗甸和织金两个贫困县建立了联系点。今后，他们将较多地到这两个县了解扶贫情况，研究新问题。
