Title: China National Football Team Defeats Bangladesh Again in World Cup Qualifiers
Author:
Date: 1989-03-05
Edition: 4

The Chinese national football team, which defeated Bangladesh 2-0 in Guangzhou last month, won again against Bangladesh on the evening of March 4 in Dhaka with the same score of 2-0.

This was the third match for the Chinese team in the World Cup football qualifiers in the Asian Zone Group 5. Currently, the Chinese team has won all three of their matches.

### 世界杯预选赛亚洲区第五组  中国足球队再胜孟加拉国队

1989-03-05
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：

　　世界杯预选赛亚洲区第五组
    中国足球队再胜孟加拉国队
    本报讯  上月２３日在广州以２∶０战胜孟加拉国队的中国国家足球队，３月４日晚在达卡再胜孟加拉国队，比分是２∶０。
    这是中国足球队在世界杯足球赛预选赛亚洲区第五组的第三场比赛。目前，中国队三战三胜。
