Title: Not Saying That We Can Pay Money Instead of Grain?
Author: Zhao Wenbin
Date: 1989-03-07
Edition: 5

---

    —Isn't it said that if there's no grain, we can pay money?
    —If all the peasant brothers pay this way...

                    Zhao Wenbin
              Originally published in *Hebei Daily*
