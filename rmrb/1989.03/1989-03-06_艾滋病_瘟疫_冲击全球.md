Title: The AIDS "Plague" Strikes the World
Author:
Date: 1989-03-06
Edition: 1

Today, the seventh page of this newspaper features two articles, "Exploring the Mysteries of AIDS" and "The Impact of the AIDS 'Plague,'" along with relevant charts and diagrams. These articles introduce the spread of AIDS around the world, the changes in the lifestyles and moral values of some Westerners, and the efforts of contemporary medicine to conquer this disease. They also report on the progress made by various countries in exploring the mechanisms, treatments, and diagnoses of AIDS.

### 艾滋病“瘟疫”冲击全球

1989-03-06
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　艾滋病“瘟疫”冲击全球
    本报讯  今天本报第七版国际专页刊登了《探索艾滋病的奥秘》、《艾滋病“瘟疫”的冲击》两篇文章，及有关图表，介绍了艾滋病在世界蔓延的情况，以及一些西方人的生活方式和道德观念开始发生变化；报道了艾滋病是当代医学努力攻克的堡垒，以及世界各国探索艾滋病的机制、治疗和诊断已取得的进展。
