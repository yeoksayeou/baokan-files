Title: Fake Trademark Activities Rampant, Nation to Crack Down
Author: Pan Gang
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 1

Fake trademark activities have become a serious problem in economic life, said Gan Guoping, deputy director of the State Administration for Industry and Commerce.

Statistics show that last year alone, five provinces and municipalities, including Henan, Zhejiang, Sichuan, Anhui, and Beijing, handled more than 3,800 cases of trademark infringement and counterfeiting. Among these, about 50% of the cases involved alcoholic beverages. Additionally, several cases of counterfeiting foreign trademarks were also handled.

Gan Guoping stated that counterfeiting trademarks disrupts the legal procedures of trademark management, infringes upon the legitimate rights of trademark owners, deceives consumers, and causes significant losses to industrial and agricultural production. He urged local industry and commerce administration departments to work closely with all sectors of society, especially with judicial authorities, to severely crack down on the manufacture and sale of counterfeit goods, promote fair competition among enterprises, and ensure the healthy development of socialist commodity economy.

In related news, the State Administration for Industry and Commerce held a press conference today, publicly addressing the infringement of IBM Corporation's trademark exclusive rights by five companies in Shenzhen: Shenzhen Baohua Science and Technology Development Joint Venture Company, Shenzhen Chaogong Technology Co., Ltd., Shenzhen Hanhan Electronics Technology Co., Ltd., Shenzhen Zijing Electronics Industry Co., Ltd., and Shenzhen Microcomputer Co., Ltd.

On September 26, 1988, IBM Corporation commissioned a trademark agent to file a complaint with the Shenzhen Municipal Administration for Industry and Commerce, requesting legal action against certain Shenzhen companies for assembling and selling counterfeit IBM-branded computers, thereby infringing upon its registered trademark exclusive rights. The Guangdong Provincial and Shenzhen Municipal Administrations for Industry and Commerce immediately formed a joint investigation team to investigate the accused companies, ordering them to cease all infringing activities and sealing off the sites and items involved in the suspected infringement.

In February of this year, the Shenzhen Municipal Administration for Industry and Commerce decided to fine the aforementioned five law-breaking companies a total of 662,600 yuan and ordered them to remove the counterfeit IBM trademark labels from the computers and destroy all packaging materials bearing the counterfeit IBM trademark.

### 假冒商标活动猖獗  国家将予严厉打击  深圳五企业侵犯ＩＢＭ商标遭重罚
潘岗
1989-03-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　假冒商标活动猖獗  国家将予严厉打击
    深圳五企业侵犯ＩＢＭ商标遭重罚
    本报北京３月１１日讯  记者潘岗报道：国家工商局副局长甘国屏在此间称“当前假冒商标已经成为经济生活中的一个严重问题。”
    据统计，去年仅河南、浙江、四川、安徽、北京５省市就查处了侵权和假冒商标案件３８００多起，其中，酒类商品假冒侵权案件约占全部商标违法案件的５０％以上。同时，还查处了一些假冒外国商标案件。
    甘国屏说，假冒商标破坏商标管理的法律程序，侵犯商标所有人的合法权益，欺诈消费者，给工农业生产带来严重损失。他要求各地工商管理机关与社会各方面，特别是与司法机关密切配合，严厉打击制造销售假冒商品的行为，促进企业之间的正当竞争，保障社会主义商品经济健康地发展。
    又讯　国家工商局今天召开新闻发布会，公开处理了侵犯美国ＩＢＭ公司商标专用权的深圳宝华科学技术发展联合公司、深圳超群科技有限公司、深汉电子技术有限公司、深圳紫金电子工业公司、深圳微型电脑有限公司。　　
    １９８８年９月２６日，美国ＩＢＭ公司委托商标代理人向深圳市工商局投诉，要求依法查处深圳市一些企业擅自组装、销售假冒ＩＢＭ商标计算机，侵犯其注册商标专用权的行为。广东省和深圳市工商局即组成联合调查组对被诉企业进行调查，当即责令侵权人停止一切侵权行为，并查封了涉嫌侵权活动的现场和物品。
    今年２月，深圳市工商局决定对上述５家违法企业处以罚款６６．２６万元，并责令其消除计算机上的假冒商标标识，销毁一切带有假冒ＩＢＭ商标标识的包装物。
