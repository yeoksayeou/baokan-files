Title: Thai Prime Minister Chatchai Chunhawan Ends Visit to China, Returns to Bangkok; Thailand and China Agree on Principles to Resolve the Cambodian Issue
Author: Huang Yong
Date: 1989-03-20
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, March 17th.

Thai Prime Minister Chatchai Chunhawan said tonight in Bangkok that Thailand and China have agreed on three principles to resolve the Cambodian issue: the withdrawal of Vietnamese troops from Cambodia; the establishment of a four-party national coalition government led by Prince Norodom Sihanouk; and the reduction of military aid to all factions in Cambodia.

Chatchai Chunhawan concluded his four-day official visit to China and returned to Bangkok tonight.

At a press conference held at Bangkok International Airport, he briefed the media on his meetings with Chinese leaders. Regarding his meeting with Deng Xiaoping, he said that both sides discussed the Cambodian issue and agreed on the aforementioned three principles to bring peace to Cambodia and the region.

Chatchai mentioned that during his visit to China, he met with Prince Norodom Sihanouk in Beijing and reaffirmed Thailand's support for the prince. He added that Prince Norodom Sihanouk will be invited to visit Thailand and Indonesia at the end of April.

Suwich Yodmanee, the spokesperson for the Thai Prime Minister's Office, who accompanied Chatchai on his visit to China, told our reporter tonight, "This visit to China has yielded significant results. The prime minister is satisfied with the warm and enthusiastic reception from the Chinese side."

Additionally, a large delegation of private enterprise businessmen who accompanied Chatchai on his visit met with high-level Chinese leaders and saw a broad prospect for economic cooperation between the two countries. They also expressed satisfaction with the visit.

### 差猜总理结束访华返抵曼谷  泰中对解决柬问题意见一致
黄勇
1989-03-20
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　差猜总理结束访华返抵曼谷
    泰中对解决柬问题意见一致
    新华社曼谷３月１７日电  （记者黄勇）泰国总理差猜·春哈旺今晚在这里说，泰中两国一致同意解决柬埔寨问题的三项原则：越南从柬埔寨撤军；建立以西哈努克亲王为首的四方民族联合政府；减少对柬埔寨各方的军事援助。
    差猜总理结束了对中国为期４天的正式访问，今晚回到曼谷。
    他在曼谷国际机场举行的记者招待会上介绍了会见中国领导人的情况。他在谈到会见邓小平的情况时说，双方讨论了柬埔寨问题，双方的观点是一致的，即按上述三原则解决柬埔寨问题，以便为柬埔寨和本地区带来和平。
    差猜说，访问中国期间，他在北京会见了西哈努克亲王，重申了泰国对亲王的支持。他说，西哈努克亲王将应邀于４月底先后访问泰国和印度尼西亚。
    随同差猜访问中国的泰国总理府发言人苏维·育玛尼今晚对本社记者说：“差猜总理这次访华取得了很大成果”，“总理对中国方面给予的隆重热情接待表示满意。”
    此外，这次随团访华的大批私营企业商人会见了中国高层领导人，看到了中泰经济合作有广阔的前景，他们对访问也“表示满意”。
