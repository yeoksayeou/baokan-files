Title: Notice of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 11th.

Notice of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress

The seventh session of the National People's Congress should have 2,978 representatives. By the time of the sixth session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, there were 2,965 representatives in attendance, with 13 representatives yet to be elected by the relevant election units. Currently, one representative has been elected by Jilin Province, one by the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, one by Guangdong Province, and one by Hainan Province. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress agrees with the report of the Committee on the Examination of Representative Qualifications regarding the qualifications of the newly elected representatives, confirming that the qualifications of the newly elected seventh session of the National People's Congress representatives Chen Shixing, Hu Dechai (Yao), Li Shaozhen (female), and Bao Keming are valid. This is hereby announced. Since the sixth session of the seventh session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, two representatives have passed away: Qian Dika from the People's Liberation Army and Xun Xiang from Guizhou Province. The seventh session of the National People's Congress currently has 2,967 representatives, with 11 representatives still to be elected by the relevant election units.

National People's Congress Standing Committee
March 11, 1989

### 全国人大常委会公告

1989-03-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全国人大常委会公告
    新华社北京３月１１日电  全国人民代表大会常务委员会公告
    第七届全国人民代表大会代表应为２９７８人，到全国人大常委会第六次会议时实有代表２９６５人，缺１３名代表待有关选举单位补选。现已由吉林省补选出代表１人，广西壮族自治区补选出代表１人，广东省补选出代表１人，海南省补选出代表１人。全国人民代表大会常务委员会同意代表资格审查委员会关于补选代表的代表资格的审查报告，确认补选的第七届全国人民代表大会代表陈世兴、胡德才（瑶族）、李绍珍（女）、鲍克明的代表资格有效。特此公布。自第七届全国人大常委会第六次会议以来，有２名全国人大代表逝世：解放军钱抵千、贵州省宦乡。第七届全国人民代表大会现共有代表２９６７人，还缺１１名代表尚待有关选举单位补选。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　全国人民代表大会常务委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年３月１１日
