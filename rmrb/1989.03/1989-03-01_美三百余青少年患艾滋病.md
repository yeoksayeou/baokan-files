Title: Over 300 American Teenagers Infected with AIDS
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-03-01
Edition: 3

Over 300 American Teenagers Infected with AIDS

    Our reporter Liu Kai Chen reports from New York on February 27th: According to recent materials released by the U.S. "Center for Disease Control," there are already 345 teenagers in the United States who have contracted AIDS.

    The main causes of AIDS among these teenagers are primarily due to promiscuous sexual behavior. Some cases are also due to drug injection, blood transfusion infections, and homosexuality.

### 美三百余青少年患艾滋病
刘开宸
1989-03-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美三百余青少年患艾滋病
    本报纽约２月２７日电  记者刘开宸报道：根据美国“控制疾病中心”最近公布的材料，美国青少年中已有３４５人患艾滋病。
    这些艾滋病患者的病因主要是由于性滥交，也有些人是由于注射毒品、输血传染和同性恋。
