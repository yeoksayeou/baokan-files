Title: The U.S. and the PLO to Hold Second Round of Talks
Author:
Date: 1989-03-12
Edition: 3

The U.S. State Department confirmed on the 9th that the U.S. Ambassador to Tunisia will soon hold a second round of formal talks with a representative of the PLO. However, no specific date for this round of talks was provided.

### 美巴将举行第二轮会谈

1989-03-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美巴将举行第二轮会谈
    美国国务院９日证实，美国驻突尼斯大使同巴解组织代表不久将举行第二轮正式会谈，但没有说明举行这一轮会谈的具体日期。　　　　
  　　　（据新华社）
