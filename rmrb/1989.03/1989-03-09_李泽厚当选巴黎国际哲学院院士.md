Title: Li Zehou Elected as a Member of the Paris International Academy of Philosophy
Author: Hua Xin
Date: 1989-03-09
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 8th.

Renowned scholar and researcher at the Institute of Philosophy of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, Li Zehou, recently replied to the Paris International Academy of Philosophy, agreeing to be elected as a formal member (i.e., academician).

This is the second Chinese scholar to enter the Paris International Academy of Philosophy after Feng Youlan in the 1930s.

The Paris International Academy of Philosophy was established in 1937 with the aim of "bringing together outstanding representatives of the world's philosophical community" to "strengthen the exchange of academic views." It has 115 academician positions. The current 110 academicians are all world-renowned philosophers. Li Zehou was elected as an academician at the Brighton conference in the UK in August 1988.

### 李泽厚当选巴黎国际哲学院院士
华新
1989-03-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　李泽厚当选巴黎国际哲学院院士
    本报北京３月８日讯  著名学者、中国社会科学院哲学所研究员李泽厚日前复信巴黎国际哲学院，同意当选为该院正式成员（即院士）。
    这是继冯友兰在３０年代当选后，进入巴黎国际哲学院的第二名中国学者。
    巴黎国际哲学院成立于１９３７年，宗旨是“使世界哲学界的优秀代表人物”“加强学术观点的交流”，设有院士席位１１５名。现有的１１０名院士均为世界著名的哲学家。李泽厚是在１９８８年８月英国布莱顿会议上当选为院士的。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　（华新）
