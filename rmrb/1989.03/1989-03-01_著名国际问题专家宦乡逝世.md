Title: Renowned International Affairs Expert Hou Xiang Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-03-01
Edition: 2

Renowned international affairs expert, member of the Seventh National People's Congress Foreign Affairs Committee, and Director-General of the China International Studies Center, Hou Xiang, passed away at 11 AM today in Shanghai due to illness, aged 80.

Hou Xiang had previously served as a member of the Sixth National People's Congress Standing Committee and Vice Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee, as well as Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences.

### 著名国际问题专家宦乡逝世

1989-03-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　著名国际问题专家宦乡逝世
    本报上海２月２８日电  著名国际问题专家、第七届全国人民代表大会外事委员会委员、中国国际问题研究中心总干事宦乡因病医治无效，今天１１时在上海逝世，终年８０岁。
    宦乡曾任六届全国人大常委会委员、外事委员会副主任，中国社会科学院副院长。
