Title: Fushun Launches Food Hygiene Insurance
Author:
Date: 1989-03-31
Edition: 2

Fushun Launches Food Hygiene Insurance

According to Xinhua News Agency in Shenyang on March 30, Fushun City Insurance Company has pioneered the launch of food hygiene liability insurance in the country, attracting nearly 10,000 enterprises and individuals in the city to participate in the insurance.

### 抚顺开办食品卫生保险

1989-03-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　抚顺开办食品卫生保险
    据新华社沈阳３月３０日电  抚顺市保险公司在国内率先开办食品卫生责任保险，吸引全市近万个企业和个人参加投保。
