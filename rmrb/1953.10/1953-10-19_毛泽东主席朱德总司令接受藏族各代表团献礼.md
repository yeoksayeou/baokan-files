Title: Chairman Mao Zedong and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Receive Gifts from Tibetan Delegations
Author:
Date: 1953-10-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army received flags and gifts on the morning of the 18th from the Tibetan National Day Observation Group, the Qamdo National Day Observation Group, the Sichuan Province Tibetan Autonomous Region National Day Observation Delegation, the Tibetan Delegation attending the inaugural meeting of the Chinese Buddhist Association, the Tibetan Delegation attending the First National Congress of Members of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce in Beijing, the Tibetan Youth Visiting Group, and the Qamdo Area Youth Visiting Group. After receiving the gifts, they also met with the heads and deputy heads of the above delegations, including Dorje Phagmo Puncog Rabgye and Gungtang Tsemonling Jigme Gyatso, a total of 18 people.
    Also present were: Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Committee, Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Li Weihan, Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Vice-Chairman Liu Geping, and Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 毛泽东主席朱德总司令接受藏族各代表团献礼

1953-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席朱德总司令接受藏族各代表团献礼
    【新华社十八日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席和中国人民解放军朱德总司令，在十八日上午接受了西藏国庆观礼团、昌都国庆观礼团、四川省藏族自治区国庆观礼代表团、出席中国佛教协会成立会议的西藏代表团、来京参加中华全国工商业联合会第一次全国会员代表大会的西藏代表团、西藏青年参观团、昌都地区青年参观团的献旗、献礼。受礼毕并接见了以上代表团的团长和副团长朵噶·彭错饶杰、公德林·晋美吉村等十八人。
    在场的有：中央人民政府委员会秘书长林伯渠，中央人民政府政务院副总理邓小平、政务院秘书长习仲勋，中央人民政府民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉、副主任委员刘格平和中国人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华等。
