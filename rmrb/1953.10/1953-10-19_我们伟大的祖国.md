Title: Our Great Motherland
Author:
Date: 1953-10-19
Page: 1

Our great motherland:
    Workers at the Dalian General Machinery Factory manufactured a large number of 500-meter drilling machines, supporting drilling work in mines across the country. The workers here are packing the drilling machines, ready to be transported to various locations.

### 我们伟大的祖国

1953-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大连通用机器厂职工制造了大批五百公尺钻探机，支援了各地矿山的钻探工作。这里的工人正把钻探机装箱，准备运往各地。
