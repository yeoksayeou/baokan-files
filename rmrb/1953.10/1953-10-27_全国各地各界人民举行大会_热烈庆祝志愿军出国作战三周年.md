Title: People from All Walks of Life Across the Country Hold Meetings to Warmly Celebrate the Third Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers Entering Korea to Fight

Author:

Date: 1953-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: People from all over the country warmly celebrated the third anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea to fight. Major cities held celebration meetings and extended condolences to the wounded and sick Volunteers recuperating locally.

　　More than 700 representatives from all walks of life in the Northeast and Shenyang held a meeting on the 25th to commemorate the third anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the Korean War. At the meeting, Zhu Weiren, chairman of the Shenyang branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association, said: We must continue to deepen the Resist-America Aid-Korea movement with practical actions to extensively and deeply carry out the production increase and economy practice movement, support the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army, ensure that the Korean Armistice Agreement is not violated, and strive for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue. Xu Yingde, director of the Shenyang Liaison Office of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, also spoke at the meeting. He first extended his warmest congratulations to the Chinese people for their glorious victory in the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea over the past three years. He said: Today, the U.S. aggressors are still unwilling to accept defeat and are still obstructing and undermining the Korean armistice. However, we are confident that with the support of the peace-loving people of the world, led by the Soviet Union, the united strength of the Korean and Chinese people will definitely shatter the sinister plans of the warmongers.

　　More than 2,000 representatives from all walks of life in Shanghai held a celebration meeting on the 25th. Xia Yan, vice chairman of the Shanghai branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association, spoke at the meeting. He pointed out that we must be vigilant against the conspiracies of the U.S. aggressors, continue to deepen the Resist-America Aid-Korea movement, learn from the patriotism and internationalism of the Volunteers, vigorously carry out the production increase and economy practice movement, and strive to support the Chinese People's Volunteers and Korea. After speeches by other representatives, the meeting unanimously passed a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army.

　　People from all walks of life in Central-South and Wuhan gathered on the 25th to celebrate the third anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea to fight. After Li Erzhong, chairman of the Wuhan branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association, spoke at the meeting, Chen Mingshu spoke on behalf of the organizations of various democratic parties in Wuhan. They unanimously congratulated the great victory achieved by the Chinese and Korean People's Armies and expressed their heartfelt gratitude to the Chinese and Korean People's Armies. Chen Mingshu said: The great Resist-America Aid-Korea movement has strengthened the spiritual and political unity of the people. The Chinese and Korean People's Armies have stopped aggression and played a significant role in the recovery and development of our economy. In the future, we must continue to support the Chinese and Korean People's Armies, strengthen preferential treatment work, and do our best to help the Korean people carry out recovery and construction work.

　　Chongqing held a meeting of people from all walks of life to commemorate the third anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea to fight on the 25th. There were 800 representatives from all walks of life in the Southwest and Chongqing who attended the meeting. Ren Baige, vice chairman of the Southwest Resist-America Aid-Korea General Branch and chairman of the Chongqing branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association, Pan Dakui, vice chairman of the Chongqing branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association, Li Fuke, representative of the Southwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Xiong Kewu, representative of various democratic parties in the Southwest and Chongqing, spoke at the meeting. They unanimously pointed out that our most concrete action to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea is to deeply carry out the production increase and economy practice movement and complete and overfulfill the country's economic construction plan.

　　People from all walks of life in Xi'an held a meeting on the 25th to celebrate the third anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea to fight. More than 2,000 people's representatives from all walks of life participated in the meeting. Yang Mingxuan, chairman of the Northwest Resist-America Aid-Korea General Branch, spoke at the meeting. He said: Over the past three years, in addition to a large number of outstanding young people directly participating in the struggle on the front lines of Resist-America Aid-Korea, people of all ethnic groups in the Northwest have also donated many aircraft, sent comfort groups and cultural and artistic work groups to Korea to express their condolences, and more than 1,300 people volunteered to go to Korea to participate in transportation and health care work. Women of all ethnic groups also meticulously embroidered more than 400,000 needlework kits to comfort the "most beloved people." Yang Mingxuan emphatically pointed out: People of all ethnic groups in the Northwest must be extra vigilant, continue to work hard, increase production, practice strict economy, and strive to overfulfill the national plan, so as to continue to support the Chinese People's Volunteers with practical actions and strive for the accurate implementation of the armistice agreement and the peaceful settlement of the Korean issue. Bai Wanlin, a representative of the recuperating members of the Chinese People's Volunteers, spoke next. More than 500 representatives of all ethnic groups in Dihua also held a celebration meeting on the 25th, expressing their high respect for the Volunteers.

　　More than 1,000 representatives of all ethnic groups from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Suiyuan Province, and Guisui City held a meeting on the 25th to celebrate the third anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea to fight. The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Resist-America Aid-Korea General Branch, the Suiyuan Province Resist-America Aid-Korea Branch, and other units also held photo exhibitions.

　　Around the 25th, various places also established comfort organizations to express their condolences to the recuperating Volunteers in their respective areas. Beijing began performing Peking Opera for the recuperating Volunteers on the 24th. Guangzhou held a celebration meeting on the 24th to report good news and celebrate the families of the Chinese People's Volunteers' heroes in the city.

### 全国各地各界人民举行大会  热烈庆祝志愿军出国作战三周年

1953-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    全国各地各界人民举行大会
    热烈庆祝志愿军出国作战三周年
    据新华社讯：全国各地人民热烈庆祝中国人民志愿军出国作战三周年。各大城市都举行了庆祝大会并慰问在当地休养的志愿军伤病员。
    东北和沈阳市各界代表七百多人在二十五日举行纪念中国人民志愿军出国作战三周年大会。会上，沈阳市抗美援朝分会主席朱维仁讲话，他说：我们要以广泛地、深入地开展增产节约运动的实际行动，来继续深入抗美援朝运动，支持中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军，保障朝鲜停战协定不受任何破坏，并争取和平解决朝鲜问题。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆沈阳联络所所长徐应德也在会上讲话。他首先向三年来在抗美援朝斗争中获得辉煌胜利的中国人民致最热烈的祝贺。他说：今天，美国侵略者还不甘心失败，还在阻挠和破坏朝鲜停战。但是，我们确信，在以苏联为首的全世界爱好和平人民的支援下，朝中人民的团结力量，一定能彻底粉碎战争贩子的凶恶计划。
    上海市各界代表二千多人在二十五日举行了庆祝大会。上海市抗美援朝分会副主席夏衍在会上讲话。他指出我们必须对美国侵略者的阴谋活动提高警惕，继续深入开展抗美援朝运动，学习志愿军的爱国主义和国际主义精神，大力开展增产节约运动，并努力支援中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民。大会在其他代表讲话后，一致通过了给中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军的致敬电。
    中南和武汉各界人民在二十五日集会庆祝中国人民志愿军出国作战三周年。武汉市抗美援朝分会主席李尔重在会上讲话后，由陈铭枢代表各民主党派在武汉市的组织讲话。他们一致祝贺中朝人民军队所取得的伟大胜利，并向中朝人民军队致以衷心的感谢。陈铭枢说：伟大的抗美援朝运动加强了人民精神上和政治上的团结。中朝人民军队制止了侵略，对我国经济的恢复和发展起了重大的作用。我们今后必须继续支援中朝人民军队，加强优抚工作，并尽力帮助朝鲜人民进行恢复和建设工作。
    重庆市各界人民纪念中国人民志愿军出国作战三周年大会于二十五日举行。到会的有西南和重庆市各界代表共八百人。西南抗美援朝总分会副主席、重庆市抗美援朝分会主席任白戈、重庆市抗美援朝分会副主席潘大逵、中国人民解放军西南军区代表李夫克、各民主党派在西南及重庆市组织的代表熊克武等都先后在会上讲话。他们一致指出我们当前抗美援朝最具体的行动是深入开展增产节约运动，完成和超额完成国家的经济建设计划。
    西安市各界人民于二十五日开会庆祝中国人民志愿军出国作战三周年。参加大会的有各界人民代表二千多人。西北抗美援朝总分会主席杨明轩在会上讲话。他说：三年来全西北除有大批优秀的青年直接参加了抗美援朝前线的斗争外，西北各族人民还捐献了许多飞机，派遣了慰问团和文艺工作团赴朝慰问，还有一千三百多人志愿赴朝参加运输和卫生医疗工作。各族妇女还精心绣制了四十多万件针线包慰问“最可爱的人”。杨明轩着重指出：西北各族人民必须加倍警惕，继续努力，增加生产，厉行节约，争取超额完成国家计划，以实际行动继续支援中国人民志愿军，为争取停战协定的确切实施和朝鲜问题的和平解决而奋斗。接着讲话的有中国人民志愿军休养员代表白万林等。迪化市各族人民代表五百多人也在二十五日举行了庆祝大会，表示了对志愿军的崇高敬意。
    内蒙古自治区、绥远省、归绥市各族人民代表一千多人在二十五日举行庆祝中国人民志愿军出国作战三周年大会。内蒙古自治区抗美援朝总分会、绥远省抗美援朝分会等单位还举办了图片展览会。
    二十五日前后，各地还成立了慰问组织在各该地慰问志愿军休养员。北京市从二十四日开始向志愿军休养员演出了京剧。广州市在二十四日举行了向全市中国人民志愿军功臣的家属报喜庆功大会。
