Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's Telegram to Premier Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1953-10-07
Page: 1

    Premier Zhou Enlai's Telegram to Premier Grotewohl
    To Comrade Otto Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic,
    Respected Premier,
    On the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, I extend warm congratulations to the peace-loving people of all Germany, the government of the German Democratic Republic, and to you, Premier.
    I wish the German Democratic Republic—the staunch bastion of all peace-loving forces in Germany—further consolidation and prosperity. May the unbreakable friendship between China and Germany be increasingly strengthened and developed in the cause of safeguarding world peace.
                                      Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
    October 5, 1953, Beijing

### 周恩来总理致格罗提渥总理电

1953-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理致格罗提渥总理电
    德意志民主共和国总理奥·格罗提渥同志尊敬的总理同志：
    值此德意志民主共和国成立四周年之际，我谨向爱好和平的全德人民，德意志民主共和国政府和您，总理同志，致以热烈的祝贺。
    祝德意志民主共和国——全德爱好和平力量的坚强堡垒获得进一步的巩固和繁荣。祝德中两国之间牢不可破的友谊在维护世界和平事业中日益巩固与发展。
                                      中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来
    一九五三年十月五日于北京
