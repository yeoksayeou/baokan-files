Title: The Council of the Chinese Buddhist Association Passes Resolution: Xirao Gyatso Acts as President

Author:
Date: 1953-10-31
Page: 1

    The Council of the Chinese Buddhist Association Passes Resolution
    Xirao Gyatso Acts as President

    (Xinhua News Agency, 29th) The Chinese Buddhist Association held its first Standing Council meeting in Beijing on the 27th and unanimously resolved that the first Vice President of the Chinese Buddhist Association, Xirao Gyatso, act as president.

### 中国佛教协会理事会通过决议  喜饶嘉措代理会长

1953-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中国佛教协会理事会通过决议
    喜饶嘉措代理会长
    【新华社二十九日讯】中国佛教协会于二十七日在京举行第一次常务理事会议，一致议决由中国佛教协会第一副会长喜饶嘉措代理会长。
