Our Great Motherland
　　The Anshan Seamless Steel Tube Plant began its hot test rolling on the afternoon of the 27th of this month, producing the first seamless steel tube in Chinese history. This is the scene of a red-hot steel billet emerging from a 1200-degree high-temperature heating furnace and quickly passing through a piercing machine to become a steel tube.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍山无缝钢管厂于本月二十七日下午开始热试轧，生产了我国历史上第一根无缝钢管。这是通红的钢坯从一千二百度的高温加热炉吐出来，迅速钻过穿孔机而成为钢管的情形。
