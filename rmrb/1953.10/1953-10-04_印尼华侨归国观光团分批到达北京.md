Title: Indonesian Overseas Chinese Return Visit Group Arrives in Beijing in Batches
Author:
Date: 1953-10-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency:

    The first group of eight representatives of the Indonesian Overseas Chinese Return Visit Group, led by Chairman Hong Zaide, arrived in Beijing by plane on September 30.

    [Xinhua News Agency, 2nd] The second group of 98 members of the Indonesian Overseas Chinese Return Visit Group arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 2nd.

    Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Zhuang Xiquan and Li Tiemin, Vice Chairmen of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission; Fei Zhendong, Wang Jiyuan, Hong Sisi, Zhang Shuming, and Zhao Lingde, members of the Commission; Lu Xinyuan, Peng Guanghan, and Chen Manyun, Deputy Directors of the General Office; Zhuang Mingli, representative of the Overseas Chinese Group of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; and more than 100 returned overseas Chinese and overseas Chinese students in Beijing.

### 印尼华侨归国观光团分批到达北京

1953-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    印尼华侨归国观光团分批到达北京
    据新华社讯：印尼华侨归国观光团第一批代表八人在团长洪载德率领下，于九月三十日乘飞机到达北京。
    【新华社二日讯】印尼华侨归国观光团第二批团员九十八人，于二日晚乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：华侨事务委员会副主任委员庄希泉、李铁民，委员费振东、王纪元、洪丝丝、张殊明、赵令德，办公厅副主任卢心远、彭光涵、陈曼云，人民政协全国委员会华侨组代表庄明理及在京归侨和华侨学生等百余人。
