Title: Molotov's congratulatory telegram to Premier Zhou

Author:

Date: 1953-10-01
Page: 1

    Molotov's Congratulatory Telegram to Premier Zhou
    To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, please accept my heartfelt congratulations, Premier and Minister. I wish the comprehensive cooperation and unbreakable friendship between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China, for the benefit of our two peoples and to guarantee lasting world peace, further consolidation and development.
                                        V. Molotov, Moscow, September 30, 1953

### 莫洛托夫给周总理的贺电

1953-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫给周总理的贺电
    中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部部长
    周恩来同志：
    值此中华人民共和国成立四周年之际，总理兼部长同志，请您接受我衷心的祝贺，并祝苏联和中华人民共和国为了我们两国人民的福利与保证世界持久和平的利益的全面合作与牢不可破的友谊，获得进一步的巩固和发展。
                                        维·莫洛托夫一九五三年九月三十日于莫斯科
