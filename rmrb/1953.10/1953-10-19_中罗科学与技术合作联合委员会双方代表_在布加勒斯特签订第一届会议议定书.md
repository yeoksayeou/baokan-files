Title: Representatives of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation Sign Protocol of the First Meeting in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1953-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The protocol of the first meeting of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation was signed in Bucharest on the 15th. The protocol was signed by Emil Stanciu, Chairman of the Romanian group and First Deputy Chairman of the Romanian State Planning Committee, and Li Renjun, Chairman of the Chinese group and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government. The signing ceremony was attended by Chiroiu Nicolae, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Romanian People's Republic, Wang Youping, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Romanian People's Republic, and members and experts of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation.

### 中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会双方代表  在布加勒斯特签订第一届会议议定书

1953-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会双方代表
    在布加勒斯特签订第一届会议议定书
    【新华社十九日讯】中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会第一届会议议定书已于十五日在布加勒斯特签订。议定书由罗马尼亚组主席、罗马尼亚国家计划委员会第一副主席埃米尔·斯坦裘，及中国组主席、中央人民政府燃料工业部副部长李人俊签字。参加签字仪式的有：罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部副部长却罗尤·尼古拉，中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使王幼平及中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会双方委员和专家。
