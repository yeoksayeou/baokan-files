Title: East German Government Gifts Giant Crane to China
Author:
Date: 1953-10-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The government of the German Democratic Republic has gifted a giant tower crane that was exhibited in China to our country. The gifting ceremony was held on the 20th at the construction site 3538 of the Beijing Municipal Construction Engineering Bureau.
    Representing the German Democratic Republic government in gifting the crane was Mr. Konig, the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China. Representing our country in accepting the gift was Mr. Wan Li, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Construction Engineering of the Central People's Government. Participating in the ceremony from the German side were Mr. Gregor, Minister of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic, and Mr. Duerber, Commercial Counselor of the Embassy in China, and others. From the Chinese side were Mr. Chen Yongqing, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Construction Engineering of the Central People's Government, Mr. Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Mr. Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade. In addition, there were over 300 cadres and workers from the Ministry of Construction Engineering of the Central People's Government, the Beijing Municipal Construction Engineering Bureau, and the 3538 construction site.
    Note: This tower crane is 30 meters high, and its lifting capacity can reach up to 6 tons. It can transport objects of different weights from 7.5 meters to 20 meters away from the base. Its maximum lifting height can reach 40.3 meters, making it very suitable for high-rise buildings. Currently, this crane is already in use at the 3538 construction site of the Beijing Municipal Construction Engineering Bureau.

### 民主德国政府赠给我国巨型起重机

1953-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府赠给我国巨型起重机
    【新华社二十日讯】德意志民主共和国政府已将在中国展览过的一座塔式巨型旋臂起重机赠送给我国。赠送典礼于二十日在北京市建筑工程局三五三八工地现场上举行。
    代表德意志民主共和国政府赠送这座起重机的是德意志民主共和国驻中国大使柯尼希。代表我国接受的是中央人民政府建筑工程部副部长万里。参加典礼的，德方有：德意志民主共和国对外与国内贸易部部长格里哥尔，驻中国大使馆商务参赞杜尔伯等。我方有：中央人民政府建筑工程部办公厅主任陈永清，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎。此外，尚有中央人民政府建筑工程部、北京市建筑工程局及三五三八工地干部和工人三百余人。
    按：这座塔式旋臂起重机高达三十公尺，它的起重能力最高可达六吨，能把不同轻重的物件运离机座七公尺半到二十公尺。它的起重高度最高可达四十点三公尺，因此很适用于高层建筑。目前，这架起重机已在北京市建筑工程局三五三八工地上应用。
