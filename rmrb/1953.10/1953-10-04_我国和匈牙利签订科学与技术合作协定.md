Title: China and Hungary Sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1953-10-04
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd) To enhance scientific and technological cooperation between China and Hungary and strengthen the friendly relations between the two peoples, the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic signed the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement in Beijing on October 3rd.

Representing their respective governments in signing the agreement were Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government for China, and Sándor Haraszti, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China for Hungary.

Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Jia Tuofu, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government, Xu Yixin, Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wen Jize, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. On the Hungarian side were Nagy, Commercial Counselor of the Hungarian People's Republic Embassy in China, and Csatórdai, First Secretary.

### 我国和匈牙利签订科学与技术合作协定

1953-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和匈牙利签订科学与技术合作协定
    【新华社三日讯】为增进中、匈两国间科学与技术的合作，加强两国人民间的友谊关系，中华人民共和国政府与匈牙利人民共和国政府于十月三日在北京签订了中匈科学与技术合作协定。
    代表两国政府在协定上签字者，中国方面为中央人民政府邮电部部长朱学范，匈牙利方面为匈牙利人民共和国驻我国特命全权大使夏法朗柯。
    参加签字仪式者，中国方面有中央人民政府国家计划委员会副主席贾拓夫，外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新，文化教育委员会广播事业局副局长温济泽，外交部交际处处长王倬如等。匈牙利方面有匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使馆商务参赞那吉，一等秘书察托尔道伊等。
