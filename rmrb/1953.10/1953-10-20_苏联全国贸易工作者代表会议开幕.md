Title: Soviet National Conference of Trade Workers Opens

Author:
Date: 1953-10-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th. TASS Moscow, 18th: The Soviet National Conference of Trade Workers opened in Moscow to discuss measures for expanding and improving state-owned, cooperative, and collective farm trade.

Participants in the conference included the heads of the Soviet Ministry of Trade and the Ministries of Trade of the various union republics and autonomous republics, directors of trade bureaus of various states, border regions, and cities, representatives of consumer cooperatives, store managers, store assistants, and representatives of daily-use goods industry.

On the 17th, the Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Minister of Trade of the Soviet Union, A.I. Mikoyan, delivered a comprehensive report on further expanding Soviet trade.

The conference is continuing.

### 苏联全国贸易工作者代表会议开幕

1953-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联全国贸易工作者代表会议开幕
    【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十八日讯：苏联全国贸易工作者代表会议在莫斯科开幕，讨论扩大和改进国营、合作社及集体农庄贸易的措施。
    参加会议的有苏联贸易部及各加盟共和国、自治共和国贸易部的负责人，各州、各边疆区及各市贸易局局长，消费合作社代表，商店经理，商店助理人员及日用品工业代表。
    十七日，苏联部长会议副主席兼苏联贸易部部长阿·伊·米高扬，在会上作了关于进一步扩大苏维埃贸易的全面的报告。
    会议还在继续举行。
