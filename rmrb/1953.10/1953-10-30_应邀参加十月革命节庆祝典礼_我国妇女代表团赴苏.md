Title: Our Women's Delegation Visits the Soviet Union to Attend the October Revolution Celebration Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1953-10-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency:

    Our Women's Delegation Visits the Soviet Union to Attend the October Revolution Celebration Ceremony

    The All-China Democratic Women's Federation, at the invitation of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, has sent a Chinese women's delegation to the Soviet Union to participate in the celebration ceremony for the 36th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. The delegation departed from Beijing for Moscow on the 24th.

    List of the Chinese Women's Delegation:
    Head of Delegation:
  Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
    Deputy Head of Delegation:
  Yang Zhihua, Deputy Director of the Women Workers' Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
  Li Zhen, Director of the Cadre Department of the Air Defense Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
    Secretary-General:
  Zou Dexin, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
    Members (in order of stroke count of surname):

  Li Yeli, Deputy Township Head of Tuoxi Township, Yongxin County, Jiangxi Province, Special-Class Agricultural Labor Model of the County, Head of the Special-Class Model Mutual Aid Group of the Province, and Chairman of the Agricultural Production Cooperative
  Li Jiansheng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee
  Li Baoguang, Deputy Director of the Rural Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
  Shen Cuizhen, Director of the Women and Children's Welfare Department of the Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation
