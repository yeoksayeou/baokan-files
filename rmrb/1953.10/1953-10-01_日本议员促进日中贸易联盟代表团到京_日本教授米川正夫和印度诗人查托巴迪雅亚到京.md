Title: Delegation from the League of Japanese Diet Members Promoting Japan-China Trade Arrives in Beijing. Japanese Professor Masao Yonekawa and Indian Poet Chattopadhyaya Arrive in Beijing.
Author:
Date: 1953-10-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. A delegation of twenty-four members from the "League of Diet Members Promoting Japan-China Trade" arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 30th.

Those who went to welcome them included Ji Chao-ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Xie Xiao-nai, Director of the Liaison Department.

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Japanese professor Masao Yonekawa, who was invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane from Moscow on the 30th.

Zhu Shilun, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, was at the airport to welcome him.

Also at the airport to welcome him were Togo Kameda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and Santaro Tametomo, who recently arrived in China, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Indian poet and member of parliament, Harindranath Chattopadhyaya, who came to our country for a visit at our invitation, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 30th.

Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Zheng Zhenduo, Director of the Institute of Literature of Peking University, poets Ai Qing and Yuan Shuipai, and composer Ding Shande were at the airport to welcome him.

Goberdan, Counselor of the Indian Embassy in China, was also at the airport to welcome him.

### 日本议员促进日中贸易联盟代表团到京  日本教授米川正夫和印度诗人查托巴迪雅亚到京

    日本议员促进日中贸易联盟代表团到京
    日本教授米川正夫和印度诗人查托巴迪雅亚到京
