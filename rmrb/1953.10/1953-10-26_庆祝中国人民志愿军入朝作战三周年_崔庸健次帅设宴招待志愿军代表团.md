Title: Cui Yongjian Held a Banquet to Entertain the Delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers to Celebrate the Third Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers Army's Entry into Korea to Fight
Author: 
Date: 1953-10-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 25th:  To celebrate the third anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' entry into Korea to fight, Cui Yongjian, Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, held a banquet in Pyongyang on the evening of the 24th to entertain the Chinese People's Volunteers delegation headed by Li Da, Chief of Staff of the Chinese People's Volunteers.

Representatives of the General Delegation and the First General Sub-Delegation of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea were also invited to attend the banquet.

Also invited to attend the banquet were Gan Yetao, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, and diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries stationed in Korea.

Korean attendees included Kim Il, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Cui Changyi, Vice-Premier of the Cabinet, Kim Kwang-hyup, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, and other party and government leaders and senior generals of the People's Army.

At the beginning of the banquet, Vice-Marshal Cui Yongjian delivered a speech. He expressed his deep gratitude and congratulations to the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers on behalf of the Korean people and the entire officers and men of the Korean People's Army. He said: On this meaningful day, I feel supremely honored to be with the hero models, senior commanders of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and representatives of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea. He proposed a toast first to the longevity of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people who selflessly assisted the Korean people.  The hall resounded with lasting applause.

Li Da, Chief Representative of the Chinese People's Volunteers Delegation, and Xing Xiping, Deputy Head of the慰问团, spoke successively, expressing their gratitude for the hospitality of Vice-Marshal Cui Yongjian. During the banquet, the guests repeatedly toasted to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, Marshal Kim Il Sung, and Chairman Malenkov.

### 庆祝中国人民志愿军入朝作战三周年  崔庸健次帅设宴招待志愿军代表团

1953-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国人民志愿军入朝作战三周年
    崔庸健次帅设宴招待志愿军代表团
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相兼民族保卫相崔庸健次帅，为庆祝中国人民志愿军入朝作战三周年，二十四日晚在平壤市举行宴会，招待以中国人民志愿军参谋长李达将军为首的中国人民志愿军代表团。
    中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团和第一总分团的代表们也应邀出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的还有我国驻朝鲜大使馆代办甘野陶，苏联和各人民民主国家驻朝鲜外交使节和外交官员。
    朝鲜方面出席作陪的有：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长金一，内阁副首相崔昌益，朝鲜人民军总参谋长金光侠及其他党政首长和人民军高级将领。
    宴会开始时，崔庸健次帅致词。他代表朝鲜人民和朝鲜人民军全体将士向中国人民志愿军指挥员、战斗员表示深深的感谢和祝贺。他说：在这个有意义的日子里，我能和中国人民志愿军的英雄模范、高级指挥员以及中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团的代表同志们在一起，感到无上的光荣。他提议首先为中国人民的伟大领袖、真诚无私地援助朝鲜人民的毛泽东主席的万寿无疆而干杯。全场响起了经久不息的掌声。
    中国人民志愿军代表团首席代表李达将军和慰问团副总团长邢西萍先后讲话，对崔庸健次帅的招待，表示感谢。席间，宾主频频为毛泽东主席、金日成元帅、马林科夫主席的健康干杯。
