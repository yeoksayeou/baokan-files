Title: CPPCC National Committee Hosts Reception for Overseas Chinese Representatives Returning for Sightseeing; Overseas Chinese Representatives from Korea, Vietnam, Burma, India, and Indonesia Visit Guanting Reservoir
Author:
Date: 1953-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a reception on the afternoon of the 13th to entertain all members of the five overseas Chinese sightseeing groups returning from Korea, Vietnam, Burma, India, and Indonesia, as well as the Burmese Overseas Chinese Sports Delegation and overseas Chinese representatives returning from Africa for sightseeing.

Those present at the reception included: Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee; Huang Yanpei and Chen Qiyou, Standing Committee members; Situ Meitang and Fei Zhendong, members; and Mei Gongbin, Deputy Secretary-General, among others.

Also invited to attend were: Lian Guan, member of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and Peng Guanghan, Director of the General Office.

Vice-Chairman Li Jishen, in his speech, first extended a warm welcome to the overseas Chinese compatriots returning for sightseeing. He then described the great victory of the Resist-America, Aid-Korea struggle, the tremendous achievements of the motherland's economic recovery work over the past four years, and the general line of national economic construction during the transition period, and called on all overseas Chinese compatriots to unite under the banner of patriotism and strive to safeguard their legitimate rights and interests.

Ma Yusheng, head of the Korean Overseas Chinese Sightseeing Group; Liao Sheng, head of the Vietnamese Overseas Chinese Sightseeing Group; and Qiu Yijue, head of the Burmese Overseas Chinese Sightseeing Group, spoke successively on behalf of the Indonesian, Indian, and Burmese Overseas Chinese Sightseeing Groups. They all expressed their pride in the growing strength of the motherland and stated that they would convey all the new atmosphere they had seen in the motherland to every overseas Chinese compatriot, respond to Chairman Mao's call, strengthen the unity of overseas Chinese, and love the great motherland.

The reception was filled with a warm atmosphere from beginning to end.

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: A group of 318 people from the five overseas Chinese sightseeing groups returning from Korea, Vietnam, Burma, India, and Indonesia, as well as the Burmese Overseas Chinese Sports Delegation and the Indonesian Overseas Chinese Soccer Team, visited the motherland's peaceful construction project—the famous Yongding River Guanting Reservoir project—on the 10th.

After listening to Wang Sen, Deputy Director of the Guanting Reservoir Engineering Bureau, introduce the reservoir project, the overseas Chinese representatives visited in groups the high dam project, clad in granite, which was completed ahead of schedule before National Day, the spacious and smooth spillway project, and the water intake tower project, which is currently undergoing the final phase of construction. The great reservoir construction project left a deep impression on the overseas Chinese representatives.

### 政协全国委员会招待归国观光的华侨代表  朝鲜、越南、缅甸、印度和印尼华侨归国代表参观官厅水库

1953-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会招待归国观光的华侨代表
    朝鲜、越南、缅甸、印度和印尼华侨归国代表参观官厅水库
    【新华社十三日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在十三日下午举行招待会，招待朝鲜、越南、缅甸、印度、印尼五个华侨归国观光团的全体团员及缅甸华侨体育代表团和非洲归国观光的华侨代表。
    出席招待会的有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李济深、陈叔通，常务委员黄炎培、陈其尤，委员司徒美堂、费振东及副秘书长梅龚彬等。
    应邀作陪的有：中央人民政府华侨事务委员会委员连贯、办公厅主任彭光涵。
    李济深副主席在致词中首先向归国观光团的侨胞表示热烈的欢迎。他接着叙述了抗美援朝斗争的伟大胜利，祖国四年来经济恢复工作的巨大成就和国家在过渡时期经济建设的总路线，并号召全体侨胞在爱国主义的旗帜下团结起来，为保卫自己的正当权益而奋斗。
    朝鲜华侨归国观光团团长马玉声、越南华侨归国观光团团长廖胜、缅甸华侨归国观光团团长邱贻厥代表印尼、印度、缅甸华侨归国观光团相继发言，他们一致为祖国的日益强大而感到自豪，并表示要把在祖国看到的一切新气象传达给海外的每一个侨胞，响应毛主席的号召，加强侨胞的团结，热爱伟大的祖国。
    招待会自始至终洋溢着热烈的气氛。
    【新华社十三日讯】朝鲜、越南、缅甸、印度和印尼五个华侨归国观光团及缅甸华侨体育代表团、印尼华侨回国球队一行三百一十八人，在十日参观了祖国的和平建设——著名的永定河官厅水库工程。
    侨胞代表们听取了官厅水库工程局副局长王森关于水库工程的介绍后，分组参观了在国庆前夕提前完工的披着花岗石外衣的高大的拦河坝工程和宽敞平滑的溢洪道工程、以及正在进行最后一期施工的进水塔工程。伟大的水库建设工程给侨胞代表们留下了深刻的印象。
