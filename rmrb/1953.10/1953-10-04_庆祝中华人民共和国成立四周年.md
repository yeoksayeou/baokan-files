On the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the World Federation of Trade Unions, on behalf of its eighty million members and the millions of other workers around the world who support it, congratulates the Chinese workers and cheers for the great achievements they have made in the country's economic construction and the struggle for peace.

The Korean Professional Federation sends a telegram of congratulations to our All-China Federation of Trade Unions

To the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

On the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the great People's Republic of China, the Central Committee of the Korean Professional Federation extends warm fraternal congratulations to the brotherly Chinese working class and all working people through you.

The founding of the People's Republic of China is a historically significant victory of world significance won by the great Chinese people under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and its wise leader Mao Zedong in the struggle for freedom and independence. At the same time, it is also a strong guarantee for the final victory of the oppressed nations of the East in the struggle for liberation. We are confident that the people of Korea and China, united in the sacred war against the common enemy, American imperialism, will achieve victory in the great cause of building a prosperous future for our two countries and defending the security and peace of the world and the Far East.

We wish you and the brotherly Chinese working class brilliant achievements in the struggle to build a strong People's Democratic China! With brotherly greetings!

Central Committee of the Korean Professional Federation
October 1, 1953

Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, Hoang Quoc Viet, sends a telegram of congratulations to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions

To the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

On the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China on October 1, 1953, we, on behalf of all the working people of Vietnam, cordially congratulate the Chinese working class and people on the victories they have achieved in construction.

We are very excited by the great achievements of the Chinese people in large-scale economic construction, in the movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, and in the preparations for convening the National People's Congress. These achievements have had a great impact on the Vietnamese people's war of resistance.

We express our heartfelt gratitude to Chairman Mao, the Chinese working class, and the Chinese people for their great help to the Vietnamese workers and people, and wish the Chinese working class and people more and more brilliant victories on all fronts.

Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor Hoang Quoc Viet
September 28, 1953, Vietnam

The Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions sends a telegram of congratulations to our All-China Federation of Trade Unions

To the All-China Federation of Trade Unions: Dear Comrades!

On the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we extend warm congratulations to you on behalf of the revolutionary trade union movement and all the working people of Czechoslovakia.

The people of Czechoslovakia follow your selfless creative labor with great concern and are proud with you of the great achievements of the People's Republic of China over the past four years.

The historic victory of the armistice in Korea, won by the peace-loving forces of the world, ended the heroic struggle of the Korean people and the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers against imperialist aggression. This victory once again shows that the great Chinese people, under the leadership of the glorious Communist Party of China, are an invincible defender of peace in Asia and the world.

Dear Comrades, we wish you greater victories in your efforts to strive for the economic and cultural upsurge of your beautiful motherland in the future, and in the struggle to defend freedom, democracy and peace with the working people of the world.

Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions
October 1, 1953

We wish the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and all the working people of China to continue to achieve great achievements on their path in the coming years, just as they have in the four years since the establishment of the first people's regime, because these achievements are a powerful weapon against the world's imperialist aggression plans.

Long live the unbreakable friendship between the people of our two countries!

Long live world peace!

### 庆祝中华人民共和国成立四周年

1953-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国成立四周年
    印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺给毛主席的贺电中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东阁下：
    欣逢贵国国庆，我愉快地向阁下表达印度尼西亚共和国人民和我个人的最诚挚的祝贺和最好的愿望。印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺
              一九五三年九月三十日于雅加达印度尼西亚共和国总理
      沙斯特罗阿米佐约给周外长的
      贺电中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来阁下：
    值此贵国国庆佳节，我代表印度尼西亚共和国政府，并以我个人名义，极其愉快地向阁下致诚挚的祝贺和最好的愿望。
            印度尼西亚总理兼代外交部部长
                        沙斯特罗阿米佐约
              一九五三年九月三十日于雅加达世界工会联合会总书记
    赛扬给刘少奇同志的贺电刘少奇同志：
    值此中华人民共和国成立四周年之际，世界工会联合会谨代表八千万会员和全世界支持它的其他千百万工人，向中国工人祝贺，并为他们在祖国的经济建设和争取和平的斗争中所获得的巨大成就而欢呼。
    中国工人的努力已获得全世界工人的敬仰和支持。
