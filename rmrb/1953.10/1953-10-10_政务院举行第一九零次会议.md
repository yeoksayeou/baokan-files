Title: The Government Administration Council Holds Its 190th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th—The 190th session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 8th. The meeting heard and approved the report on transportation work made by Zhang Bojun, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Transportation, and approved various appointments and removals, as well as the proposed appointments and removals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一九零次会议

1953-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一九零次会议
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百九十次政务会议在八日下午举行。会议听取了和批准了中央人民政府交通部章伯钧部长所作的关于交通工作的报告，通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
