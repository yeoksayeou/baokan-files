Title: Two Representatives of the All-India Trade Union Congress Arrive in Beijing on a Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1953-10-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 4th:

    Desai and Vasanwada, two representatives of the All-India Trade Union Congress who are visiting China at our invitation, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 4th.

    Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Dong Xin, Director of the General Office of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Qin Dayuan, Deputy Director, representatives of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, and personnel from the Indian Embassy, among others.

### 应邀来我国访问印全国职工大会两代表到京

1953-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问印全国职工大会两代表到京
    【新华社四日讯】来我国访问的印度全国职工大会代表德赛、瓦萨瓦达二人于四日晚乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中华全国总工会书记处办公室主任董昕、副主任秦达远，中印友好协会代表和印度大使馆人员等多人。
