Title: Our Side and the US Side Agree to Allow Government Personnel from Both Sides to Enter the Demilitarized Zone to Make Preliminary Arrangements for Equipment for the Political Conference Preparatory Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1953-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, October 22nd: The two sides of the Military Armistice Commission today agreed in principle to allow government representatives and assistants from both sides to enter the Demilitarized Zone and the Military Armistice Commission headquarters area to investigate equipment available for the Political Conference Preparatory meeting and to make preliminary arrangements.

The secretaries' meeting of the Military Armistice Commission also agreed to a specific provision by the commission allowing the US side to send no more than twenty representatives and assistants to the Military Armistice Commission headquarters area to carry out the aforementioned work. The Korean-Chinese side will, upon request, immediately notify the US side of the number of its own government representatives and assistants entering the Military Armistice Commission headquarters area for the same purpose.

### 我方和美方同意双方政府人员进入非军事区对政治会议预备会议的设备作出初步安排

1953-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我方和美方同意双方政府人员进入非军事区对政治会议预备会议的设备作出初步安排
    【新华社开城二十二日电】军事停战委员会双方今天在原则上同意允许双方的政府代表及助理人员进入非军事区及军事停战委员会总部地区，调查可供政治会议预备会议之用的设备，并作出初步安排。
    军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议还同意委员会具体规定美方得派出不超过二十名的代表和助理人员进入军事停战委员会总部地区执行上述工作。朝中方面将在提出要求时立即把为了同样目的而进入军事停战委员会总部地区的自己一方的政府代表及助理人员的数目通知美方。
