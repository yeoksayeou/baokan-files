Title: British "Worker's Daily" Demands Removal of U.S. Special Agents from POW Camps; Indian Officials Point Out that U.S. Special Agents Sabotage Explanation Work
Author:
Date: 1953-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 28th: The London "Worker's Daily" published an editorial on the 27th denouncing the U.S. for instructing Syngman Rhee and Chiang Kai-shek agents to sabotage the explanation work. The editorial said: "People must openly demand that all Syngman Rhee and Chiang Kai-shek agents in the neutral zone prisoner-of-war camps be imprisoned to prevent them from undermining the armistice agreement. Syngman Rhee's current actions to sabotage the explanation work are solely aimed at undermining the entire armistice and resuming the war."

The editorial said: "The reign of terror imposed by Syngman Rhee's agents on Korean prisoners of war in the neutral zone prisoner-of-war camps in Korea has brought the explanation work regarding the repatriation issue to a standstill." The editorial pointed out: "If no immediate action is taken against Syngman Rhee's agents in the prisoner-of-war camps, it will only encourage Syngman Rhee and his men to take even more extreme measures." The editorial concluded: "Syngman Rhee is only powerful because he has the support of the United States. Britain must demand that the U.S. government no longer encourage Syngman Rhee to undermine peace in Korea."

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: According to a France-Presse dispatch from New Delhi: "Indian officials in New Delhi emphasized on the 27th that the main obstacle preventing the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission from functioning properly is the existence of leaders in the prisoner-of-war camps who use various methods – even violence – to prevent prisoners of war from listening to explanations." The Indian officials pointed out that these "leaders" are special agents of the "Taiwan government or the South Korean government."

### 英“工人日报”要求清除战俘营的美方特务  印度官方人士指出美方特务破坏解释工作

1953-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    英“工人日报”要求清除战俘营的美方特务
    印度官方人士指出美方特务破坏解释工作
    【新华社柏林二十八日电】伦敦“工人日报”二十七日发表社论斥责美方指使李、蒋特务破坏解释工作。社论说：人们必须公开要求把在中立区战俘营中的所有李承晚和蒋介石特务监禁起来，不让他们破坏停战协定。李承晚目前破坏解释工作的做法完全是为了破坏整个停战和恢复战争。”
    社论说：“李承晚特务在朝鲜中立区的战俘营中对朝鲜战俘施行恐怖统治，已使对战俘进行的关于遣返问题的解释工作陷于僵局。”社论指出：“如果不对战俘营里的李承晚特务立刻采取行动，那只会鼓励李承晚和他手下的人采取变本加厉的措施。”社论最后说：“李承晚只是由于得到美国的支持才有力量的。英国必须要求美国政府不再鼓励李承晚破坏朝鲜和平”。
    【新华社二十八日讯】据法新社记者自新德里报道：新德里的“印度官方人士二十七日强调说，使中立国遣返委员会无法正常进行工作的主要障碍是战俘营中有领袖存在，这些人使用各种办法——甚至使用暴力来阻止战俘去听解释。”印度官方人士指出，这些“领袖”就是“台湾政府或南朝鲜政府”的特务。
