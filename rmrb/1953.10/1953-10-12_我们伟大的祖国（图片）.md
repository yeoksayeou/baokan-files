Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-10-12
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The navigation markers in the middle reaches of the Yangtze River are being completely converted to Soviet-style inland river navigation marks. This will not only ensure navigation safety but also improve water transport efficiency. The picture shows workers at the Chenglingji Navigation Mark Section in the middle reaches of the Yangtze River installing huge guide markers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    长江中游的导航标志正全部改设成苏联式的内河航标。这样不仅可以保证航运安全，而且提高了水运效率。这是长江中游城陵矶航标段的工人正在装设巨大导标的情形。
