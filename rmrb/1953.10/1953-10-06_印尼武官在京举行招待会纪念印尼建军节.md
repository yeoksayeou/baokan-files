Title: Indonesian Military Attaché Holds Reception in Beijing to Commemorate Indonesian Armed Forces Day
Author:
Date: 1953-10-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 5th. To commemorate the 8th anniversary of the founding of the Indonesian Armed Forces, Lieutenant Colonel Salim, the Indonesian Military Attaché to China, held a reception in Beijing on the 5th.

Among those invited to attend were: Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; and many senior generals from the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Also invited to attend were: Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, as well as military attachés from the Soviet, Polish, and Czechoslovakian embassies in China, were also invited to attend.

The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 印尼武官在京举行招待会纪念印尼建军节

1953-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼武官在京举行招待会纪念印尼建军节
    【新华社五日讯】为纪念印度尼西亚共和国第八届建军节，印度尼西亚驻我国大使馆武官沙林中校五日在北京举行招待会。
    应邀到会的有：中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任甘泗淇，人民革命军事委员会办公厅主任萧向荣以及中国人民解放军陆、海、空军高级将领多人。
    应邀到会的还有：中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫和北京市人民政府副市长张友渔。
    各国驻华使节与外交官员以及苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆武官也应邀参加。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
