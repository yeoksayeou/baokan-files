Title: The Fourth National Day of the People's Republic of China in the Capital (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1953-10-03
Page: 6

October 1st, all circles in the capital solemnly held a celebration ceremony for the fourth National Day of the People's Republic of China in Tiananmen Square. Chairman Mao reviewed the armed forces defending the peaceful construction of the motherland and the parade of over 400,000 people in the capital engaged in peaceful construction. This is the Air Force and armored forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army being reviewed. (Reported by our reporter Zhu Bo)

Chairman Mao frequently raised his hand in salute while reviewing the armed forces and the parading crowds from the Tiananmen reviewing stand. (Special reporters for this newspaper Wu Benli, Hou Bo)

Representatives from the Chinese People's Volunteers' National Day Observation Delegation applauded with joy on the reviewing stand. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Xue Yin)

The capital's workers carried production charts and models through Tiananmen Square. They assured Chairman Mao that they would strive to increase production and practice economy, and to strive to fulfill and overfulfill production plans. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Shi Shaohua)

Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhu De, received a report on the National Day military parade from Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission. (Liberation Army Pictorial, Liu Feng)

As the military parade began, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhu De, rode in a car past the front of the troops standing at attention, reviewing them and extending festive greetings. (Liberation Army Pictorial, Tang Maolin)

Foreign guests on the reviewing stand. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Cao Xinghua)

Farmers from the suburbs of Beijing carried charts of abundant grain harvests through Tiananmen Square. They assured Chairman Mao that they would strive to increase the production of grain and cash crops to support the country's economic construction. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ling)

Infantry troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army pass through Tiananmen Square. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Huiru)

Students held up flowers and cheered for Chairman Mao. They promised to respond to Chairman Mao's call to be "healthy in body, good in study, and good in work." (Xinhua News Agency reporter Dai Gezhi)

Naval forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army being reviewed (This newspaper, Li Yu)

Citizens of the capital cheer for Chairman Mao. (Beijing Daily reporter Jiang Dingbao)

Night scene of Tiananmen in the capital on National Day. (This newspaper, Li Yu, Zhang Li)

### 中华人民共和国第四届国庆节在首都（图片）

1953-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国第四届国庆节在首都
    十月一日，首都各界在天安门广场隆重举行中华人民共和国第四届国庆节庆祝典礼。毛主席检阅了保卫祖国和平建设的武装部队和首都四十余万从事和平建设的游行队伍。这是受检阅的中国人民解放军空军和装甲部队。　本报记者朱波摄
    毛主席在天安门检阅台上检阅武装部队和游行队伍时，频频举手致意。　本报特约记者  吴本立、侯波摄
    中国人民志愿军国庆节归国观礼代表团的代表们在观礼台上欢欣鼓掌。　新华社记者  雪印摄
    首都工人队伍抬着生产图表和模型通过天安门广场。他们向毛主席保证：努力增产节约，为完成和超额完成生产计划而奋斗。
    新华社记者  石少华摄
  中国人民解放军总司令朱德接受中央人民政府革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊关于国庆阅兵的报告。
    解放军画报社  刘峰摄
    阅兵式开始，中国人民解放军总司令朱德乘车驰过整队而立的部队前面，检阅并向他们致节日的祝贺。
    解放军画报社唐茂林摄在观礼台上的外宾们。　新华社记者  曹兴华摄
    北京郊区农民队伍抬着粮食丰产图表通过天安门广场。他们向毛主席保证：努力增产粮食和经济作物来支援国家的经济建设。
  　新华社记者  袁苓摄
    中国人民解放军步兵部队通过天安门广场。
    新华社记者  喻惠如摄
    学生们高举着鲜花向毛主席欢呼。他们保证响应毛主席的号召，做到“身体好，学习好，工作好”。　新华社记者戴戈之摄受检阅的中国人民解放军海军部队　本报李棫摄首都市民队伍向毛主席欢呼。北京日报记者江定保摄国庆节首都天安门的夜景。
