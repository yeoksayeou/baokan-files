Title: Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Hosts Farewell Banquet for France-China Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th: The President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Xiruo, hosted a banquet at the Beijing Peace Hotel on the evening of the 5th to bid farewell to the entire delegation of the France-China Friendship Association, who are about to return to their country.

Attending as guests were: Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhou Gengsheng and Chen Hansheng, Secretary-General Wu Maosun, Council Members Shao Lizi, Mei Ru'ao, Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Zhu Xuefan, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Liu GuanYi, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Sha Kofu, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Zou Dexin, and representatives from the capital's cultural, scientific, and educational circles including Xiao San, Cao Yu, Hua Luogeng, Fei Xiaotong, and Fang Shishan, totaling more than sixty people.

At the banquet, President Zhang Xiruo delivered a speech, saying that the delegation of the France-China Friendship Association, headed by Mr. D'eni, has conducted three weeks of visits in our country and celebrated our National Day with us. The mutual understanding and friendship between the people of China and France has been further advanced due to the delegation's visit. We thank the delegation for bringing the friendship and concern of the French people to the Chinese people, and we are confident that all the representatives will bring the friendship and concern of the Chinese people to the French people after returning to their country.

Subsequently, the head of the delegation, D'eni, delivered a reply, expressing his reluctance to leave China tomorrow. He said that we have gained a profound impression during our visit to China. The labor enthusiasm of Chinese workers and peasants is an example for us. We saw the feelings for us in the eyes of Chinese children. We have seen your strength and the prospects for development, and we are very moved and excited. This emotion and excitement has strengthened our confidence and strength in the struggle. After returning to our country, we will strive to consolidate and expand the friendship and cultural exchanges between the people of France and China.

Finally, President Zhang Xiruo presented gifts to the delegation on behalf of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. The Secretary of the delegation, Storas, said upon receiving the gifts, we will bring these gifts back to France as the highest honor. Please believe that our whole hearts are with you.

The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere from beginning to end.

### 中国人民外交学会欢送法中友协代表团

1953-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民外交学会欢送法中友协代表团
    【新华社五日讯】中国人民外交学会会长张奚若五日晚在北京和平宾馆设宴欢送即将返国的法中友好协会代表团全体代表。
    出席作陪的有：中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、陈翰笙，秘书长吴茂荪，理事邵力子、梅汝璈、冀朝鼎，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，中华全国文学艺术界联合会秘书长沙可夫，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长邹得心，及首都文化、科学、教育界代表萧三、曹禺、华罗庚、费孝通、方石珊等六十余人。
    宴会上张奚若会长致词说，以窦尼先生为首的法中友协代表团，在我国进行了三个星期的参观和访问，并和我们一起欢度了我国国庆节。中法两国人民间的相互了解和友谊，由于代表团的访问，又向前推进了一步。我们感谢代表团把法国人民对中国人民的友谊与关心带给了我国人民，我们并深信各位代表回国后，定会把中国人民对法国人民的友谊与关心带给法国人民。
    接着代表团团长窦尼致答词，他对于明天就要离开中国表示依依不舍。他说，我们在访问中国期间，获得了深刻的印象。中国工人、农民的劳动热情是我们的榜样。我们从中国儿童的眼睛里看到对我们的感情。我们看到了你们的力量和发展的前途，我们十分感动和兴奋。这种感动和兴奋加强了我们斗争的信心和力量。我们回国后，将为巩固和扩大法中两国人民间的友谊和文化交流而奋斗。
    最后张奚若会长代表中国人民外交学会向代表团赠送礼物，代表团秘书斯托拉斯在接受礼物时说，我们把这些礼物作为无上的光荣带回法国去。请你们相信，我们整个的心是和你们在一起的。
    宴会自始至终洋溢着热烈友好的空气。
