Title: Finnish Minister Knoring Recalled Home

Author:
Date: 1953-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th—Finnish Minister to China, Mr. Knoring, was recalled by the Finnish government and left Beijing by car with his wife on the 28th. Those who saw him off at the station included Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and diplomatic officials from various countries. Also seeing him off at the station were Mr. Brondstandt, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Finnish Legation in China, and members of the legation.

### 芬兰公使克诺林奉调返国

1953-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰公使克诺林奉调返国
    【新华社二十八日讯】芬兰共和国驻华公使克诺林奉芬兰政府命令调任离华，在二十八日偕夫人乘车离京。到站送行的有中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如，对外贸易部交际处处长杨勉及各国外交官员等。到站送行的还有芬兰驻华公使馆临时代办勃朗斯坦特及使馆人员。
