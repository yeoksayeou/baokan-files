Title: People of Various Countries Warmly Celebrate the Fourth Anniversary of the Founding of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1953-10-04
Page: 4

    People of Various Countries Warmly Celebrate the Fourth Anniversary of the Founding of Our Country
    Mongolian People's Republic
    Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, warmly celebrated the National Day of the People's Republic of China.
    On October 1st, all newspapers in the Mongolian capital published large portraits of Chairman Mao Zedong on the front page, as well as congratulatory telegrams from the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic to Chairman Mao and from Premier Zeedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic to Premier Zhou Enlai. The newspapers also published editorials and articles introducing the great achievements made by the new China in various fields over the past four years.
    The Mongolian Broadcasting Station broadcast a special program introducing the new China on the same day. Chinese films were shown in Ulaanbaatar cinemas.
      India
    To celebrate the fourth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the India-China Friendship Association held a celebration meeting at the New Delhi Municipal Hall on the evening of October 1st.
    Dr. Rao, Chairman of the Delhi India-China Friendship Association, presided over the meeting, stating that the interests of both India and China lie in peace. He also emphasized the importance of friendship between India and China for maintaining peace in Asia.
    Kang Maozhao, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy, spoke, detailing the planned peaceful construction being carried out by the Chinese people.
    Dr. Gyan Chand, convener of the All-India Conference of the India-China Friendship Association, delivered a speech, talking about his visit to China last year.
      Finland
    The Finland-China Friendship Association held a meeting at the University of Helsinki on September 27th to celebrate the fourth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    Among those attending the celebration were Finnish Prime Minister Kekkonen and his wife, the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Finnish Communist Party Pesi and his wife, and celebrities from all walks of life. Also present were the Czechoslovak and Hungarian ministers to Finland, as well as the Soviet, Polish, and Romanian charges d'affaires and diplomats, totaling 200 people.
    Chairman of the Finland-China Friendship Association, Mr. Yevnpee, delivered an opening speech, describing China's great achievements over the past four years and wishing for further development of economic and cultural exchanges between China and Finland. Subsequently, writer Mrs. Kurula recited a poem praising China. The meeting also featured music and singing performances, and finally passed a congratulatory telegram from the Finland-China Friendship Association to the Government of the People's Republic of China, which was greeted with enthusiastic applause from the audience.
                          (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 各国人民热烈庆祝我国成立四周年

1953-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    各国人民热烈庆祝我国成立四周年
    蒙古人民共和国
    蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托热烈庆祝中华人民共和国国庆。
    十月一日，蒙古首都各报都在第一版上登载了毛泽东主席的大幅像片，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团给毛主席的贺电，蒙古人民共和国泽登巴尔总理给周恩来总理的贺电。各报并发表社论和文章，介绍新中国四年来在各方面获得的巨大成就。
    蒙古广播电台在当天播送了介绍新中国的特别节目。乌兰巴托的各电影院上映了中国影片。
      印度
    为庆祝中华人民共和国成立四周年，印中友好协会于十月一日晚在新德里市政厅举行了庆祝大会。
    德里印中友好协会主席拉奥博士主持这次大会，他说，印度和中国的利益都是和平。同时他强调印中两国的友谊对维持亚洲和平的重要性。
    中国大使馆文化参赞康矛召讲话，他详细介绍了中国人民正在进行有计划的和平建设。
    印中友好协会全印大会的召集人吉安·昌德博士发表了演说，谈到他在去年访问中国的情景。
      芬兰
    芬中友好协会九月二十七日在赫尔辛基大学开会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立四周年。
    参加庆祝会的有芬兰总理吉科宁夫妇，芬兰共产党中央委员会总书记培西夫妇与各界名流。到会的还有捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利驻芬兰公使及苏联、波兰、罗马尼亚驻芬兰代办和外交官等共二百人。
    芬中友好协会主席叶文佩致开幕词，叙述了中国四年来的伟大成就，并祝中芬两国的经济、文化交流获得更进一步的发展。接着，作家柯鲁拉夫人朗诵了一首赞扬中国的诗歌。会上并表演了音乐、歌唱节目，最后通过芬中友好协会致中华人民共和国政府的贺电，全场热烈鼓掌。
                          （据新华社讯）
