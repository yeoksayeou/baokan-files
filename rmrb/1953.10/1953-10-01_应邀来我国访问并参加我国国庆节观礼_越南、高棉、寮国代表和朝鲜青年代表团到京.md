Title: Vietnamese, Cambodian, and Laotian Representatives and Korean Youth Delegation Arrive in Beijing, Invited to Visit China and Attend National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1953-10-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Participants in China's National Day celebrations, Vietnamese representatives of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Shi Shan, Chen Yuexiu, Cambodian representatives of the United Front for Free Khmer, Gao Mani, Lao representatives of the United Front for Free Laos, Wu Da Ma·Wubaxi, and one Vietnamese translator arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of September 29th.  Welcoming them at the station were Liu Guanyi, Tang Mingzhao, Zhu Shilun from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Guan Shixiong from the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Luo Feng from the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, Zhang Xiuzhu, Fang Ming from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Liu Yiyun from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and others.
    Personnel from the Vietnamese Embassy in China also welcomed them at the station.
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th.  Invited by the China New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth to participate in National Day celebrations in China, a five-member Korean youth delegation, led by head of the delegation Xuan De, arrived in Beijing on the evening of September 30th.
    Welcoming them at the station were Qu Tangliang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, Wu Xueqian, Director of the International Liaison Department, Liu Xiyuan, Deputy Director of the Youth Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, and others.
    Also present at the station to welcome them was Xu Zhe, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China.

### 应邀来我国访问并参加我国国庆节观礼  越南、高棉、寮国代表和朝鲜青年代表团到京

1953-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问并参加我国国庆节观礼
    越南、高棉、寮国代表和朝鲜青年代表团到京
    【新华社三十日讯】参加我国国庆观礼的越南越中友好协会代表诗山、陈越秀、高棉自由民族统一战线代表高玛尼，寮国自由民族统一战线代表武大玛·乌巴锡和越南翻译一人等于九月二十九日下午乘车抵京。到车站欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会刘贯一，唐明照，朱世纶，中华全国民主青年联合会关世雄，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会洛风，中华全国总工会张修竹、方明，中华全国民主妇女联合会刘亦云等人。
    越南驻华使馆人员亦到车站欢迎。
    【新华社三十日讯】应中国新民主主义青年团和中华全国民主青年联合会邀请，来我国参加国庆观礼的朝鲜青年代表团一行五人，在团长玄德率领下，在九月三十日晚到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记区棠亮、国际联络部部长吴学谦，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部青年部副部长刘西元等。
    到车站欢迎的还有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办徐哲。
