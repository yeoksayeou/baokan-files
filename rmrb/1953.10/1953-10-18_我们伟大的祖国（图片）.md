    Our Great Motherland
    Pineapples in the suburbs of Guangzhou have had a bumper harvest this year. The largest pineapples weigh six or seven pounds each, and the average ones weigh more than two pounds. This young farmer is harvesting the abundant pineapples.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    广州市郊区的菠萝今年获得丰收。最大的菠萝每个有六、七斤重，一般的也有两斤多重。这位青年农民正在收割丰盛的菠萝。
