Title: German Ambassador König Holds National Day Reception; Vice Chairman Zhu De and Foreign Diplomatic Envoys Invited to Attend

Author:

Date: 1953-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th.
German Democratic Republic Ambassador to China, König, held a reception in Beijing at 7:00 PM on October 7th to celebrate the fourth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

Those invited to attend included: Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council Guo Moruo, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Member of the Central People's Government Zhang Zhizhong, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council Xi Zhongxun, Ministers of the Central People's Government Shen Yanbing, Zhang Xiruo, Huang Jing, Vice Ministers Wu Xiuquan, Zhou Yang, Xu Xuehan, Lai Jifa, Zhang Linzhi, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Huang Kecheng, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central Military Commission of the Central People's Government Gan Siqi, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Wu Youxun, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Zhang Youyu, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Yang Chengwu, heads of people's organizations in the capital, combat heroes, model workers, and representatives from literary and art circles.

Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries stationed in China were also present.

The entire members of the German Democratic Republic Scientific and Cultural Delegation, the German Youth Delegation, who came to attend the National Day celebrations in China, as well as the Vice Chairman of the German Writers Association, Stefan Hermlin, and the Chairman of the Berlin Writers Association, Jan Petersen, also attended the reception.

A total of 400 guests attended the banquet. Throughout the event, a warm and friendly atmosphere prevailed. The hosts and guests frequently toasted to the health of President Pieck, Chairman Mao Zedong, and the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government headed by Comrade Malenkov.

### 德大使柯尼希举行国庆招待会  朱德副主席和各国外交使节应邀赴会

1953-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    德大使柯尼希举行国庆招待会
    朱德副主席和各国外交使节应邀赴会
    【新华社七日讯】德意志民主共和国驻我国大使柯尼希于十月七日下午七时在北京举行招待会，庆祝德意志民主共和国成立四周年。
    应邀赴会的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德，政务院副总理郭沫若，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，中央人民政府委员张治中，政务院文化教育委员会副主任习仲勋，中央人民政府各部部长沈雁冰、张奚若、黄敬，副部长伍修权、周扬、徐雪寒、赖际发、张霖之，中国人民解放军副总参谋长黄克诚，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任甘泗淇，中国科学院副院长吴有训，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔，京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武，首都各人民团体负责人，战斗英雄，劳动模范与文艺界代表等。
    到会的并有各国驻我国外交使节及使馆人员。
    前来参加我国国庆节观礼的德意志民主共和国科学文化考察团、德国青年代表团全体团员及德国作家协会副主席斯蒂芬·赫姆林，柏林市作家协会主席杨·贝特尔逊也参加了招待会。
    宴会共到来宾四百人，席间自始至终洋溢着热烈友好的气氛，宾主频频为皮克总统、毛泽东主席及以马林科夫同志为首的苏联共产党及政府领袖的健康干杯。
