Title: Korean Fatherland United Front Central Committee Holds Meeting, Adopts Resolution Supporting Report on Work of Delegation Visiting Soviet Union, Forms Reception Committee to Welcome Chinese Delegation to Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-10-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 15th: The Korean Central News Agency reported that the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front held a meeting on the 12th to hear the report on the work of the Korean government delegation's visit to the Soviet Union. The meeting also discussed preparations for welcoming and receiving the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea.
    Those attending the meeting included: Premier of the Cabinet Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Presidium of the Fatherland United Democratic Front Kim Dal-hyun, and heads of various party, government, and social organizations.
    Premier Kim Il-sung delivered a report on the work of the government delegation's visit to the Soviet Union. After hearing this report, the meeting passed a corresponding resolution, expressing full support for the report, and calling on all political parties and social organizations to educate all the people with the spirit of eternal friendship between Korea and the Soviet Union and the internationalist friendly solidarity between Korea and China and other people's democratic countries, and to organize and mobilize them to learn from the Soviet Union's advanced culture, technology, and valuable struggle experience.
    The meeting then discussed preparations for welcoming and receiving the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea and formed a reception committee. The committee consists of thirty-two members, with Kim Chun-hae, a member of the Presidium of the Fatherland United Democratic Front, as chairman.

### 朝鲜祖国统一战线中央举行会议通过支持访苏代表团工作报告的决定组成迎接委员会欢迎我国赴朝慰问团

1953-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜祖国统一战线中央举行会议
    通过支持访苏代表团工作报告的决定组成迎接委员会欢迎我国赴朝慰问团
    【新华社平壤十五日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社报道：朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会在十二日举行会议，听取了朝鲜政府代表团访苏工作的报告。会议并讨论了欢迎和接待中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团的准备工作。
    出席会议的有：内阁首相金日成，祖国统一民主主义战线议长团议长金达铉等各党、政和社会团体负责人。
    金日成首相在会上作了政府代表团访苏工作的报告。会议在听取了这一报告之后通过了相应的决定，表示完全支持这个报告，并号召各政党、社会团体要以朝苏两国的永恒友谊及朝鲜与中国、各人民民主国家的国际主义的友好团结精神教育全体人民，组织和动员他们学习苏联先进的文化、技术和宝贵的斗争经验。
    会议接着讨论了欢迎和接待中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团的准备工作，并组成了迎接委员会。委员会由三十二人组成，由祖国统一民主主义战线议长团委员金天海任委员长。
