Title: Celebrating the 36th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution: "Soviet Film Week" to be Held in Twenty Cities Across the Country
Author:
Date: 1953-10-31
Page: 3

    To celebrate the 36th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association have decided to hold a "Soviet Film Week" in twenty major cities across the country from November 7th to November 13th, screening the following Soviet color films:

    "Lights Shine on in Koordi" and "Far from Moscow" (feature films), "In the Arctic Ocean" and "Soviet Circus Performances" (documentaries), "Collection of Soviet Animated Films" (including "The Princess and the Seven Heroes", "Forest Travelers", and "The Story of the Yellow Crane"), and the documentary short film "European Basketball Championship" will also be shown. The cities holding "Soviet Film Week" are: Beijing, Tianjin, Taiyuan, Shenyang, Harbin, Changchun, Lushun-Dalian, Anshan, Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Jinan, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Changsha, Chongqing, Chengdu, Kunming, Xi'an, and Lanzhou.

    Cities not holding "Soviet Film Week" will also screen films related to the October Revolution during the celebration of the October Revolution Day. At that time, film projection teams from various regions will widely screen Soviet films in factories, mines, and rural areas. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 庆祝十月社会主义革命三十六周年  全国二十个城市将举行“苏联电影周”

1953-10-31
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月社会主义革命三十六周年
    全国二十个城市将举行“苏联电影周”
    中央人民政府文化部和中苏友好协会总会为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十六周年，决定自十一月七日到十一月十三日在全国二十个主要城市举行“苏联电影周”，放映苏联彩色影片：
    “光明照耀到克奥尔地村”、“远离莫斯科的地方”（以上故事片）、“在北冰洋上”、“苏联杂技表演”（以上记录片）、“苏联动画片集”（包括“公主和七勇士”、“森林旅行者”、“黄鹤的故事”三个节目，并同时放映记录短片“欧洲篮球锦标赛”）。举行“苏联电影周”的城市是：北京、天津、太原、沈阳、哈尔滨、长春、旅大、鞍山、上海、南京、杭州、济南、武汉、广州、长沙、重庆、成都、昆明、西安、兰州。
    不举行“苏联电影周”的城市，在庆祝十月革命节期间也将放映与十月革命有关的影片。各地电影放映队届时将在工厂、矿山、农村普遍放映苏联影片。    （新华社）
