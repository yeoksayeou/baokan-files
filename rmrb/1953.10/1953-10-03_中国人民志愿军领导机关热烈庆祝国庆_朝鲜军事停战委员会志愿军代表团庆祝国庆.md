Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Leadership Organ Warmly Celebrates National Day; Korean Military Armistice Commission Volunteer Army Delegation Celebrates National Day
Author:
Date: 1953-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 2nd: The leadership organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army (CPVA) held a grand gathering on the afternoon of October 1st to warmly celebrate the National Day of the motherland. Attending the meeting were Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, General Deng Hua, Deputy Director of the Political Department, General Zhang Nansheng, and other leaders, as well as representatives from various departments and directly subordinate units, totaling more than 5,000 people.

The venue for the celebration was decorated solemnly and grandly. On the rostrum hung the national emblem of the motherland and a huge portrait of the great leader Chairman Mao. As the meeting began, the band played the national anthem of the motherland. At this moment, all personnel stood in solemn attention, artillery salutes were fired, and in the solemn sound of the national anthem, the bright five-star red flag was slowly raised into the sky in the center of the square.

General Deng Hua delivered a speech amidst warm applause. He first extended the highest respect to the great leader Chairman Mao and warm congratulations to the people of the motherland on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. After excitedly narrating the brilliant achievements of the motherland in various fields over the past four years, General Deng Hua said: This year is the first year of our motherland's Five-Year Plan for economic construction. Under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao, the motherland will definitely complete the task of industrialization and steadily transition to socialism. General Deng Hua pointed out: In order to defend our great motherland and assist the Korean people, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army has carried out a just war of resistance against aggression on the Korean front for nearly three years, and together with the heroic Korean People's Army, defeated the enemy and achieved a great victory in the Korean armistice. However, the American aggressors are not reconciled to their defeat, and their aggressive ambitions have not been abandoned. We will resolutely implement the instructions of the Central People's Government Committee of our country and Commander Peng Dehuai, heighten our vigilance, and, together with the Korean People's Army, strictly guard our positions, prepare to crush any possible sudden attacks from the other side at any time, and strive unremittingly to defend the construction of the motherland, ensure the thorough implementation of the Korean Armistice Agreement, and strive for a peaceful solution to the Korean issue. Finally, General Deng Hua called on all commanders and fighters to respond to the call of the motherland and the Chinese Communist Party's Chinese People's Volunteer Army Committee, practice strict economy, tighten expenditures, cherish every grain of rice, every bullet, every needle and every thread from the motherland, and contribute their strength to the nationwide movement of increasing production and practicing economy with the concerted efforts of the people of the whole country.

The meeting ended in a warm atmosphere. An evening celebration was held after the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 2nd: On the National Day of the motherland, all staff members of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation to the Korean Military Armistice Commission, wearing neat uniforms and adorned with bright "Resist America, Aid Korea Commemorative Medals" and various medals and decorations, held a celebration meeting for the National Day of the motherland in "Manwoltae" Square, a scenic spot in Kaesong. After the celebration, athletes from the Volunteer Army delegation held various ball games. In the evening, a celebration evening was held. The Kaesong City Committee of the Korean Workers' Party also organized a song and dance performance to express celebration and condolences.

On the eve of National Day, General Du Ping, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, gave a report on the current situation and tasks at a cadre meeting of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation to the Military Armistice Commission.

### 中国人民志愿军领导机关热烈庆祝国庆  朝鲜军事停战委员会志愿军代表团庆祝国庆

1953-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军领导机关热烈庆祝国庆
    朝鲜军事停战委员会志愿军代表团庆祝国庆
    【新华社朝鲜前线二日电】中国人民志愿军领导机关于十月一日下午隆重集会，热烈庆祝祖国国庆节。出席大会的有中国人民志愿军副司令员邓华将军、政治部副主任张南生将军等首长以及各部门和直属部队的代表五千多人。
    庆祝大会会场布置得庄严、隆重。主席台上高挂着祖国国徽和伟大领袖毛主席的巨幅画像。大会开始时，乐队高奏祖国国歌。这时全体人员肃立，礼炮齐鸣，在庄严的国歌声中，广场中央灿烂的五星红旗徐徐升上天空。
    邓华将军在热烈的掌声中致词。他首先代表中国人民志愿军全体指挥员战斗员向伟大领袖毛主席致崇高的敬意，向祖国人民致热烈的祝贺。邓华将军在兴奋地叙述了祖国四年来各方面的光辉成就后说：今年是我们祖国五年经济建设计划开始的第一年。祖国在伟大领袖毛主席的领导下，一定会完成工业化的任务，稳步地过渡到社会主义。邓华将军指出：为了保卫我们伟大的祖国与援助朝鲜人民，中国人民志愿军在朝鲜前线进行了将近三年的正义的反侵略战争，和英雄的朝鲜人民军一道打败了敌人，取得了朝鲜停战的伟大胜利。但是美国侵略者是不甘心于它的失败的，它们的侵略野心并未放弃。我们坚决执行我国中央人民政府委员会和彭德怀司令员的指示，提高警惕，和朝鲜人民军一起严守阵地，准备随时粉碎来自对方的任何可能的突然侵犯，为保卫祖国的建设，为保障朝鲜停战协定的彻底实施和争取和平解决朝鲜问题而奋斗不懈。邓华将军最后号召全体指挥员战斗员响应祖国和中国共产党中国人民志愿军委员会的号召，厉行节约，紧缩开支，爱护祖国的一粮一弹、一针一线，在全国人民一致努力的增产节约运动中贡献自己的力量。
    大会在热烈的气氛中结束。会后举行了庆祝晚会。
    【新华社开城二日电】在祖国国庆日，朝鲜军事停战委员会中国人民志愿军代表团全体工作人员，穿着整齐的服装，佩戴着灿烂的“抗美援朝纪念章”和各种奖章、勋章等，在开城名胜“满月台”广场举行了庆祝祖国国庆节大会。庆祝会结束后，志愿军代表团的运动员们举行了各种球类比赛。晚上，举行庆祝晚会。朝鲜劳动党开城市委员会也组织歌舞演出会，表示庆祝和慰问。
    国庆前夕，中国人民志愿军政治部副主任杜平将军在军事停战委员会中国人民志愿军代表团的干部会议上，作了关于目前形势与任务的报告。
