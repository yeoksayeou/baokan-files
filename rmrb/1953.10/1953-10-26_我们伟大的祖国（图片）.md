Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-10-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The first volume of the Chinese edition of "The Complete Works of Stalin" was simultaneously released in twenty-five major cities across the country on the 25th of this month. The workers of the Xinhua Printing Factory in Beijing have the glorious task of printing "The Complete Works of Stalin." These female workers are carefully inspecting the quality of the cover.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    斯大林全集中文版第一卷已于本月二十五日在全国二十五个大城市同时发行。北京新华印刷厂的工人们光荣地担负了印刷斯大林全集的任务。这几位女工正在谨慎地检查封面的质量。
