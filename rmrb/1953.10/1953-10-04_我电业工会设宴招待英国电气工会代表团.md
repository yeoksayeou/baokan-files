Title: My Electrical Workers' Union Hosts a Banquet for the British Electrical Trades Union Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-10-04
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Cai Shubin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Electrical Workers' Union, hosted a banquet on the afternoon of the 2nd to entertain the entire delegation of the British Electrical Trades Union, who are visiting our country.
    Those present as hosts included Liu Yingyuan and Huang Minwei, Vice Chairmen of the Chinese Electrical Workers' Union, and Hong Haiquan, Secretary-General.
    Xu Daben, Vice Minister of the Central Ministry of Fuel Industry, and Zhang Bin, Deputy Director of the Central Ministry of Fuel Industry's Electricity Management Bureau, and Zhang Tiezheng, Deputy Director of the Hydroelectric Power Engineering Bureau, were also invited to attend.
    At the beginning of the banquet, Chairman Cai Shubin delivered a speech, extending a warm welcome to the British working-class brothers who had come from afar.
    Walter Charles Stevens, the head of the delegation, in his reply speech, not only expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the warm hospitality of the Chinese hosts but also emphasized the significance of the British Electrical Trades Union delegation's visit to China. His speech received enthusiastic applause.
    During the banquet, the guests and hosts frequently toasted world peace and the friendship and unity between the Chinese and British working-class brothers.
    Finally, on behalf of the British electrical workers, head of delegation Stevens presented a beautifully carved bronze statue to the Chinese Electrical Workers' Union as a gift symbolizing the unity of the working class of China and Britain. The banquet then ended with enthusiastic applause.

### 我电业工会设宴招待英国电气工会代表团

1953-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

     我电业工会设宴招待英国电气工会代表团
    【新华社二日讯】中国电业工会全国委员会主席蔡书彬在二日下午设宴招待来我国参观、访问的英国电气工会代表团全体代表。
    出席作陪的有中国电业工会副主席刘英源、黄民伟，秘书长洪海泉等人。
    应邀出席作陪的还有中央燃料工业部副部长徐达本及中央燃料工业部电业管理总局副局长张彬、水力发电工程局副局长张铁铮。
    宴会开始时，首先由蔡书彬主席致词，向远道而来的英国工人兄弟表示热烈欢迎。
    代表团团长华尔特·查尔斯·斯蒂文斯在致答词时，除了对中国主人的热情款待表示衷心感谢外，强调指出了此次英国电气工会代表团来华访问的意义。他的发言获得了热烈的掌声。
    席间，宾主频频为世界和平、为中英两国工人兄弟间的友谊与团结干杯。
    最后，斯蒂文斯团长代表英国电气工人，向中国电业工会赠送了一件雕刻精美的铜像，作为象征中英两国工人阶级团结的礼品。至此，宴会在热烈的掌声中结束。
