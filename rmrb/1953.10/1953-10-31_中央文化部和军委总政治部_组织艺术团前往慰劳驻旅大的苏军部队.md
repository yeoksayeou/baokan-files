Title: Central Ministry of Culture and General Political Department of the Military Commission Organize Art Troupe to Comfort Soviet Troops Stationed in Lüda

Author:

Date: 1953-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. To celebrate the 36th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, the Central People's Government Ministry of Culture and the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission have jointly organized the "Art Troupe to Comfort Soviet Troops Stationed in Lüda." The troupe departed from Beijing on the evening of the 30th to perform for the Soviet troops stationed in Lüda City.

The art troupe consists of more than 600 artists from units such as the Central Experimental Opera House, the China Acrobatic Troupe, the Cultural Work Troupe of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, and the Cultural Work Troupe of the Political Department of the Central-South Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Prominent artists include Li Bo, Li Guoquan, Guo Lanying, Guan Yuhe, Yu Cun, and Ha Shu Wang.

Before departure, the art troupe prepared many wonderful music, dance, acrobatics, and opera programs. The music includes: Chinese and Soviet songs in chorus, solo, and duet; folk instrument performances; and orchestral performances. The dances include: Chinese folk dance, classical dance, and military dances; Soviet folk dance and military dances. The acrobatics include: Jar Balancing, Plate Spinning, martial arts, and magic. The opera is "Wang Gui and Li Xiangxiang."

The head of the art troupe is Li Zhaobing, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission. The deputy heads are: Zhu Ming, Wang Dizi, Jin Ziguang, Wang Yafan, Wu Baihua, and Wang Jingshan.

### 中央文化部和军委总政治部  组织艺术团前往慰劳驻旅大的苏军部队

1953-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中央文化部和军委总政治部
    组织艺术团前往慰劳驻旅大的苏军部队
    【新华社三十日讯】为庆祝十月社会主义革命三十六周年，中央人民政府文化部、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部特联合组织“慰劳驻旅大苏军艺术团”，在三十日晚由北京出发赴旅大市，对驻旅大苏军部队作慰劳演出。
    艺术团由中央实验歌剧院、中国杂技团、中国人民解放军海军政治部文艺工作团、中国人民解放军中南军区政治部文艺工作团等单位的六百多个艺术工作者组成，其中著名的艺术家有李波、黎国荃、郭兰英、关玉和、于村、佫树旺等多人。
    艺术团在出发前，准备了很多精彩的音乐、舞蹈、杂技、歌剧节目。其中音乐有：中国歌曲和苏联歌曲的合唱、独唱、重唱，民间乐器演奏，管弦乐演奏等；舞蹈有：中国民间舞蹈、古典舞蹈、部队舞蹈，苏联民间舞蹈、部队舞蹈等；杂技有：顶坛子、耍碟子、武术、魔术等；歌剧有“王贵与李香香”。
    艺术团由中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳任团长，副团长是：朱明、王地子、金紫光、王亚凡、吴白桦、王荆山。
