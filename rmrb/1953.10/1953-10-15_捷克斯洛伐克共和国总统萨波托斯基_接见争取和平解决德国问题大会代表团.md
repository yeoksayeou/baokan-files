Title: President Zápotocký of the Czechoslovak Republic Receives Delegation from the Conference for a Peaceful Solution to the German Question
Author:
Date: 1953-10-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 14th — According to TASS, the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Antonín Zápotocký, received on the 12th at the Presidential Palace the delegation from the Czechoslovak Conference for a Peaceful Solution to the German Question. The Chairman of the Czechoslovak Peace Defence Committee, Ms. Hodinová-Spurná, reported to President Zápotocký on the work of the conference. Subsequently, President Zápotocký spoke, emphasizing that the Czechoslovak people sincerely strive for a peaceful solution to the German question.

### 捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基  接见争取和平解决德国问题大会代表团

1953-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基
    接见争取和平解决德国问题大会代表团
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安·萨波托斯基，十二日在总统府接见了捷克斯洛伐克争取和平解决德国问题大会的代表团。捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会主席霍迪诺娃—斯普尔纳，向萨波托斯基总统报告了大会的工作。接着，萨波托斯基总统讲话。他着重指出，捷克斯洛伐克人民真诚地为和平解决德国问题而努力。
