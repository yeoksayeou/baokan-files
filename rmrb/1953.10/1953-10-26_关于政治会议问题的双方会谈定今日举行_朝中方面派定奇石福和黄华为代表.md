Title: Bilateral Talks on the Political Conference Issue Scheduled for Today; DPRK and China Designate Ki Sok-bok and Huang Hua as Representatives

Author:
Date: 1953-10-26
Page: 1

    Bilateral Talks on the Political Conference Issue Scheduled for Today
    DPRK and China Designate Ki Sok-bok and Huang Hua as Representatives
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, October 25] The bilateral talks on the political conference issue are scheduled to hold their first meeting at 11:00 AM on October 26th at Panmunjom. The representative of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is Ki Sok-bok, Councilor of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China is Huang Hua, Councilor of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. Assistant personnel from both sides will meet at 9:00 AM on the 26th to make arrangements for the first meeting of the bilateral talks on the political conference issue.
    Both sides have agreed that the bilateral talks on the political conference issue will be held in the building used by the DPRK and Chinese side's secretariat within the headquarters area of the Military Armistice Commission in Panmunjom. This spacious and bright rectangular conference room, constructed of solid wood and similar to the conference room used during the armistice negotiations, is one of the three buildings built and provided by the DPRK and Chinese side for use by the Military Armistice Commission and other headquarters.

### 关于政治会议问题的双方会谈定今日举行  朝中方面派定奇石福和黄华为代表

1953-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    关于政治会议问题的双方会谈定今日举行
    朝中方面派定奇石福和黄华为代表
    【新华社开城二十五日电】关于政治会议问题的双方会谈定于十月二十六日上午十一时在板门店举行首次会议。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表为朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府外务省参事奇石福，中华人民共和国中央人民政府代表为中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部参事黄华。双方助理人员将在二十六日上午九时开会，为关于政治会议问题的双方会谈的首次会议进行安排。
    双方已同意关于政治会议问题的双方会谈，在板门店军事停战委员会总部区内朝中方面秘书处使用的房屋中举行。这所用坚实的木材造成的长方形会议室宽敞明朗，与停战谈判时使用的会议室相似，是朝中方面负责修建、提供军事停战委员会等三个总部使用的三座建筑物之一。
