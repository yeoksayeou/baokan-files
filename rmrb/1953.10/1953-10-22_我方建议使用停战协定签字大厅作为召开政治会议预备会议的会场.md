Title: Our Side Suggests Using the Armistice Agreement Signing Hall as the Venue for the Preliminary Meeting of the Political Conference
Author:
Date: 1953-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, October 21st: At the meeting of the Secretaries of the Military Armistice Commission on the 21st, Colonel Zhu Ran, Secretary of the Korean-Chinese side, made the following notification to the U.S. side: "Regarding the issue of the building to be used for the preliminary meeting for the convening of the political conference, our side believes that: Since both governments have agreed to hold the preliminary meeting in Panmunjom, and the Military Armistice Commission and its subordinate bodies, the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission and its subordinate bodies, and other institutions will soon be moved to the new headquarters area in the next few days, it is appropriate to use the building of the Armistice Agreement Signing Hall to convene the preliminary meeting."

Colonel Coleman, Secretary of the U.S. side, claimed that he would convey this suggestion to the appropriate personnel and respond to our side as soon as possible.

### 我方建议使用停战协定签字大厅作为召开政治会议预备会议的会场

1953-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    我方建议使用停战协定签字大厅作为召开政治会议预备会议的会场
    【新华社开城二十一日电】二十一日军事停战委员会秘书长会议上，朝中方面秘书长朱然上校对美方提出下列通知：“关于为召开政治会议预备会议而使用的房屋问题，我方认为：鉴于双方政府既已同意在板门店召开预备会议，而军事停战委员会及其附属机构、中立国监察委员会及其附属机构以及其他机构在最近几天即将迁移至新总部区，因此使用停战协定签字大厅的房屋召开该预备会议是适宜的。”
    美方秘书长柯尔曼声称，他将把这一建议转达给适当人员，并尽速将其意见回答我方。
