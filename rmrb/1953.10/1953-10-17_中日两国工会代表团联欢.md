Title: Sino-Japanese Trade Union Delegations Hold Joint Celebration
Author:
Date: 1953-10-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 15th. Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation attending the Third World Trade Union Congress, and the delegation members held a joint celebration with the Japanese trade union delegation attending the Congress as observers at the Vienna Cultural Salon on the evening of the 13th. Nakamura Mitsuo, head of the Japanese trade union delegation, deputy head Nakano Hidekei, and Nakabayashi Takeki, as well as the other 17 members of the delegation, were invited to attend.

At the meeting, guests repeatedly toasted to the friendship between the workers of the two countries.

### 中日两国工会代表团联欢

1953-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中日两国工会代表团联欢
    【新华社维也纳十五日电】出席世界工会第三次代表大会的中国代表团团长刘宁一和团员们，十三日晚在维也纳文化沙龙和列席大会的日本工会代表团举行联欢。日本工会代表团团长中村满夫、副团长中野秀庆和中林刚毅以及代表团的其他人员共十七人均应邀到会。
    会上宾主频频为两国工人的友谊而干杯。
