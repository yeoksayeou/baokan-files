Title: International Friends Visit the Yongding River Guanting Reservoir Project
Author:
Date: 1953-10-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, 3rd. The Polish cultural delegation headed by Włodzimierz Sokorski, the Czechoslovak cultural delegation headed by Marie Majerová, the Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation headed by Shi Shan, the France-China Friendship Association delegation headed by Henri Dougnac, the scientific and cultural study delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Dr. Gerhard Harig, and the British Electrical Trades Union delegation headed by Walter Charles Stevens, visited the large-scale construction project of New China, the Yongding River Guanting Reservoir Project, on the 3rd.

Also visiting were Mr. and Mrs. Vercors, the French representatives invited to China to attend the commemoration conference of the world's four cultural celebrities, Jan Petersen, a writer from the German Democratic Republic, Masao Yonekawa, a Japanese writer, and Tametaro Tande, the secretary of Professor Ikuo Oyama, the chairman of the Japanese Peace Supporters Committee, as well as William Morrow, former Labor Party member of the Australian Parliament, Kou Mani, the Cambodian representative, and Vouthama Oupathasic, the Laotian representative, who came to China to participate in the National Day celebrations.

Accompanying the visitors were Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, and representatives from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, and the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

The foreign guests visited the main projects, such as the dam and spillway, which were completed ahead of schedule to welcome National Day not long ago, and the intake tower, which is under intense construction for the final phase, with great interest.

### 国际友人参观永定河官厅水库工程

1953-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    国际友人参观永定河官厅水库工程
    【新华社保定三日电】以沃吉米日·索科尔斯基为首的波兰文化代表团，以玛丽·玛耶洛娃为首的捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团，以诗山为首的越中友好协会代表团，以亨利·窦尼为首的法中友好协会代表团，以格哈德·哈里希博士为首的德意志民主共和国科学文化考察团和以华尔特·查尔斯·斯蒂文斯为首的英国电气工会代表团，三日参观了新中国的大规模的建设工程——永定河官厅水库工程。
    同来参观的尚有应邀来我国参加世界四位文化名人纪念大会的法国代表维尔高尔夫妇，德意志民主共和国作家扬·贝特尔逊，日本作家米川正夫和日本拥护和平委员会主席大山郁夫教授的秘书淡德三郎以及来我国参加国庆观礼的澳大利亚前工党议员威廉·毛罗、高棉代表高玛尼和寮国代表武大玛·乌巴锡。
    陪同参观者有中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国总工会、中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局和中国人民外交学会等单位的代表。
    外宾们以极大的兴趣参观了不久以前为迎接国庆节而提前完工的拦河坝、溢洪道和正在紧张进行最后一期施工的进水塔等主要工程。
