Title: Attending the Executive Committee Meeting of the League of Red Cross Societies: Chinese Red Cross Delegation Travels to Switzerland
Author:
Date: 1953-10-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The second regular meeting of the Executive Committee of the League of Red Cross Societies this year will be held in Geneva, Switzerland from October 28th to 31st. A five-member delegation from the Red Cross Society of China, led by Su Jingguan, Standing Director of the Society and head of the delegation, departed by plane on the morning of the 19th to attend the meeting.

### 出席红十字会协会执委会常会  我国红十字会代表团赴瑞士

1953-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    出席红十字会协会执委会常会
    我国红十字会代表团赴瑞士
    【新华社十九日讯】红十字会协会执行委员会本年第二次常会将于十月二十八日到三十一日在瑞士日内瓦召开。中国红十字会代表团一行五人，由该会常务理事、代表团团长苏井观率领，于十九日晨乘飞机启程前往出席会议。
