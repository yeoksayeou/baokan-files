Title: Polish Military Attaché Holds Reception in Beijing to Celebrate Polish Army Day
Author:
Date: 1953-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 12th: To celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Army, Colonel Monat, the military attaché of the Polish People's Republic Embassy in China, held a reception in Beijing on the evening of the 12th.

Those invited to the reception included: Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Zhu De, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission Huang Kecheng, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Fu Zhong, Director of the General Office Xiao Xiangrong, Commander of the Artillery of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Chen Xilian, Commander of the Air Defense Zhou Shidi, Deputy Commander of the Air Force Wang Bingzhang, Deputy Commander of the Navy Fang Qiang, Deputy Commander of the Armored Forces Nie Heting, Deputy Commander of the Public Security Forces Deng Shaodong, Chief of Staff of the North China Military Region Yang Chengwu, and many senior generals from the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Also invited were heads of departments of the Central People's Government, including Shen Yanbing, Wu Xiuquan, Wang Shoudao, Ding Xilin, and Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Zhang Youyu.

Representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers' delegation returning from Korea to attend the National Day celebrations and combat heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army were also invited.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries' embassies in China, as well as deputy military attachés from the Soviet and Czechoslovak embassies in China, were also invited.

The government delegation of the Polish People's Republic Economic Exhibition attended as guests.

Polish Military Attaché Colonel Monat delivered a speech first, congratulating the growing friendship between the Polish People's Army and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and congratulating the ever-growing strength of the forces led by the Soviet Union in defense of world peace.

Commander-in-Chief Zhu De then delivered a speech. He said that the Polish People's Army is a new type of army, established in the great war against German fascism. The Polish People's Army, together with the Soviet army, liberated its own territory and dealt a devastating blow to the German aggressors, thus writing a glorious page in its history. Today, the Polish People's Army is vigilantly defending the freedom and independence of its motherland and protecting the Polish people in carrying out socialist construction smoothly in their own country. Commander-in-Chief Zhu raised his glass to wish the Polish People's Army greater achievements in the great cause of defending the motherland and peace.

The reception was filled with a friendly atmosphere throughout.

Furthermore, on the eve of Polish Army Day, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent a telegram to Marshal Rokossovsky, Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic, expressing his congratulations.

### 波兰武官在京举行招待会庆祝波兰建军节

1953-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰武官在京举行招待会庆祝波兰建军节
    【新华社十二日讯】为庆祝波兰人民军建军第十周年，波兰人民共和国驻我国大使馆武官莫纳特上校十二日晚在北京举行招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的有：中国人民解放军总司令朱德，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长黄克诚，总政治部副主任傅钟，办公厅主任萧向荣，中国人民解放军炮兵司令员陈锡联，防空司令员周士第，空军副司令员王秉璋，海军副司令员方强，装甲兵副司令员聂鹤亭，公安部队副司令员邓少东，华北军区参谋长杨成武，及中国人民解放军陆、海、空军高级将领多人。
    应邀到会的还有中央人民政府各部首长沈雁冰、伍修权、王首道、丁西林和北京市人民政府副市长张友渔。
    应邀到会的还有中国人民志愿军国庆节归国观礼代表团代表和中国人民解放军战斗英雄。
    各国驻华使节与外交官员，和苏联、捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆正副武官也应邀到会。
    波兰人民共和国经济展览会政府代表团出席作陪。
    波兰大使馆武官莫纳特上校首先致词，祝贺波兰人民军和中国人民解放军之间的友谊日益加强，祝贺以苏联为首的保卫世界和平的力量日益强大。
    朱德总司令接着致词。他说，波兰人民军是一支新型的军队，它是在反对德国法西斯的伟大战争中建立起来的。波兰人民军曾与苏联军队一起解放了自己的国土，给德国侵略者以毁灭性的打击，从而在自己的历史上写下了光辉的一页。今天波兰人民军正警惕地保卫着祖国的自由与独立，保卫着波兰人民在自己的国家内顺利地进行社会主义建设。朱总司令举杯祝波兰人民军在保卫祖国及和平的伟大事业中获得更大的成就。
    招待会始终洋溢着友好的气氛。
    又讯：中国人民解放军朱德总司令在波兰人民军建军节前夕，曾致电波兰人民共和国国防部部长罗科索夫斯基元帅，表示祝贺。
