Title: Indonesian Ambassador to China, Mononutu, calls on Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials
Author:
Date: 1953-10-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: Mononutu, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Indonesia to China, accompanied by Isa Matari, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia in China, called on Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, at 10:00 a.m. on the 23rd to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. Also present were Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 印尼驻我国大使莫诺努图拜会章汉夫副部长  商谈呈递国书事宜

1953-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼驻我国大使莫诺努图拜会章汉夫副部长
    商谈呈递国书事宜
    【新华社二十三日讯】印度尼西亚共和国驻华首任特命全权大使莫诺努图，偕印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂，在二十三日上午十时，前往拜会我中央人民政府外交部章汉夫副部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的还有：外交部亚洲司司长陈家康和交际处处长王倬如。
