Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-10-29
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Our country's newly built, large-scale Taiyuan Thermal Power Plant began construction on the 24th of this month. It is one of the 141 enterprises that the Soviet Union is comprehensively assisting our country in building and renovating. This is the scene of powerful bulldozers beginning to excavate the first foundation earth after the groundbreaking ceremony.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国新建的规模巨大的太原电热厂，已于本月二十四日动工。它是苏联全面援助我国新建和改建的一百四十一个企业中的一个。这是在开工典礼之后，强大的推土机开始挖掘第一块基础土方的情形。
