Title: CPI Acting General Secretary Namboodiripad Issues Statement Calling on Indian People to Stop US Sabotage of Explanation Work
Author:
Date: 1953-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 21st: The Acting General Secretary of the Communist Party of India, Namboodiripad, issued a statement calling on the Indian people to stop the US, Syngman Rhee, and Chiang Kai-shek bandit gangs from obstructing the explanation work for prisoners of war and sabotaging the Korean Armistice Agreement. The statement was published in the October 18th issue of the Communist Party of India Central Committee's official publication, "New Age Weekly."

Namboodiripad said, "The armistice agreement is a major obstacle to the plans to re-launch the war. The US agreed to the armistice out of compulsion, because the whole world demanded an end to the war, and even the ruling groups of countries like Britain and France raised this demand."

Namboodiripad pointed out that Dulles ordered the US's South Korean puppets to oppose the implementation of the armistice agreement by all means, even threatening to "march north"; the US delegation forced the UN General Assembly to pass a resolution regarding the composition of the Korean political conference as stipulated, all in order to oppose the implementation of the armistice agreement.

Namboodiripad said, "It is only because the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China have shown great patience that these provocations by Syngman Rhee and his patrons have not been able to defeat the armistice."

Namboodiripad demanded that the Indian people pay attention to prevent the current armistice from being sabotaged, and that this opportunity be used to find a long-term solution to the Korean issue. He called on the Indian people to launch a powerful movement under the following slogans:

"1. Prisoners of war should have complete freedom to decide their future, without being obstructed by the provocations and atrocities carried out by Kuomintang and Syngman Rhee's secret agents;
    2. Revise the UN resolution on the membership of the political conference, so that this conference becomes a conference that can peacefully resolve the Korean issue;
    3. Let the People's China join the United Nations, and peacefully resolve all Asian tensions with the cooperation of the great People's Republic of China."

### 印共代理总书记南布德里巴德发表声明  号召印度人民制止美国破坏解释工作的行为

1953-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印共代理总书记南布德里巴德发表声明
    号召印度人民制止美国破坏解释工作的行为
    【新华社新德里二十一日电】印度共产党代理总书记南布德里巴德发表声明，号召印度人民制止美国、李承晚和蒋介石匪帮阻挠对战俘进行解释工作和破坏朝鲜停战协定的行为。这篇声明刊载在十月十八日出版的印度共产党中央委员会机关刊物“新世纪周报”上。
    南布德里巴德说：“停战协定对于重新发动战争的计划是个主要的障碍，美国同意停战是被迫的，因为全世界都要求停止战争，甚至英、法等国的统治集团也提出了这个要求。”
    南布德里巴德指出，杜勒斯命令美国的南朝鲜傀儡们用一切办法来反对实施停战协定，甚至扬言要“向北进军”；美国代表团迫使联合国大会通过关于朝鲜政治会议的决议中所规定的会议组成的办法，都是为了反对实施停战协定。
    南布德里巴德说：“由于朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和中华人民共和国表现了极大的忍耐，李承晚及其庇护人所进行的这些挑衅行为才没有能使停战遭受失败”。
    南布德里巴德要求印度人民注意不要使目前的停战遭到破坏，而且要利用这个机会来找出朝鲜问题的长久解决办法。他号召印度人民在以下几个口号下发起一个强有力的运动：
    “一、战俘有完全的自由来决定他们的前途，而不致被国民党和李承晚的特务所进行的挑衅行动和暴行所阻挠；
    二、修改联合国关于政治会议成员问题的决议，使这个会议成为一个可以和平解决朝鲜问题的会议；
    三、让人民中国加入联合国，在伟大的中华人民共和国的合作下和平解决一切亚洲紧张局势的问题。”
