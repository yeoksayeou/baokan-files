Premier Tsedenbal's Telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai

To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the first anniversary of the signing of the Agreement on Economic and Cultural Cooperation between Mongolia and China, I extend my most sincere congratulations to you, Comrade Premier, and to the Government of the People's Republic of China and to all the people of our friendly and great China. The past year has proven the enormous significance of this historic document for the peoples of our two countries. During this year, the fraternal friendly relations between the Mongolian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China have continuously gained further development and consolidation, because this friendly relationship is built on the principles of proletarian internationalism and on the basis of the unity of our interests in the struggle for peace, democracy and socialism. I firmly believe that for the welfare of the people of our two countries, and for the sake of the camp of peace and democracy headed by the great Soviet Union—the faithful and powerful friend of the people of our two countries—the unbreakable friendship and fraternal cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic will be increasingly consolidated and developed.

                                        Yu. Tsedenbal, Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic
                                        Ulaanbaatar, October 3, 1953

### 泽登巴尔总理致周恩来总理电

1953-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔总理致周恩来总理电
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来同志：
    值此蒙中经济及文化合作协定签订一周年之际，谨向您，总理同志，并向中华人民共和国政府及我们友好的伟大的中国全体人民致以最诚挚的祝贺。过去的一年证明了这个历史性的文件对于我们两国人民是有着巨大的意义的。在这一年中，蒙古人民共和国与中华人民共和国之间的兄弟般的友好关系不断地获得了更进一步的发展与巩固，因为这种友好关系是建立在无产阶级国际主义原则的基础上，是建立在我们争取和平民主与社会主义的斗争中利益一致的基础上的。我坚信：为了我们两国人民的福利，为了以伟大的苏联—我们两国人民忠实的强大的朋友—为首的和平民主阵营，中华人民共和国与蒙古人民共和国之间牢不可破的友谊与兄弟般的合作将日益巩固与发展。
                        蒙古人民共和国总理尤·泽登巴尔
                        一九五三年十月三日于乌兰巴托
