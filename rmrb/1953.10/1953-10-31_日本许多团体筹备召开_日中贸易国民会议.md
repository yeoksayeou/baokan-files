Title: Many Japanese Groups Prepare to Convene "Japan-China Trade National Conference"
Author:
Date: 1953-10-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 30th: Many Japanese groups enthusiastic about promoting trade between the people of China and Japan and enhancing friendship between the two peoples are preparing to organize a "Japan-China Trade National Conference," and it is scheduled to be formally convened in early November.

    Representatives of groups participating in the preparatory work include: Tsunejiro Hiratsuka, Director of the "Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade"; Shoichiro Ando, Chairman of the "National Association of Local Assembly Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade"; Jiichiro Matsumoto, Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association; Kumaichi Yamamoto and Gishitaro Hirano, Chairmen of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Conference; Koichi Uda, Chairman of the Kansai Japan-China Trade Promotion Conference; and representatives from many places such as Hokkaido, Kyoto, and Nagasaki. The purpose of convening the "Japan-China Trade National Conference" is to promote the realization of the request to invite a delegation from the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to visit Japan and the mutual dispatch of industrial and commercial delegations between the people of China and Japan, as well as to promote the concrete realization of the Japan-China trade agreement.

### 日本许多团体筹备召开“日中贸易国民会议”

1953-10-31
第4版()
专栏：

    日本许多团体筹备召开“日中贸易国民会议”
    【新华社三十日讯】东京消息：热心于促进中日两国人民间贸易、增进两国人民间友谊的日本许多团体，正在着手筹备组织“日中贸易国民会议”，并预定在十一月上旬正式召开。
    参加筹备工作的团体代表有：日本“国会议员促进日中贸易联盟”理事长平冢常次郎，“全国地方议员促进日中贸易联盟协议会”主席安藤章一郎，日中友好协会会长松本治一郎，日中贸易促进会议主席山本熊一、平野义太郎，关西日中贸易促进会议主席宇田耕一，和北海道、京都、长崎等许多地方的代表。召开“日中贸易国民会议”的宗旨，在于促进邀请中国人民政治协商会议代表团访问日本和中日两国人民间互派工商业界代表团的要求的实现以及促进中日贸易协议的具体实现。
