Title: Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao Zedong from the Second Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers
Author:
Date: 1953-10-08
Page: 1

To our most beloved Chairman Mao:
    How fortunate we are! The day before yesterday, you, along with Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yun, and other leaders of the Party and the government, met with us. The heart of each of us was stirred by supreme joy. We are truly unable to describe the encouragement we received.
    Our congress has ended. Tomorrow, we will return to our respective posts, and we will work even better to repay your, and the Party and government under your leadership’s, deep concern and expectations for us.
    At the congress, we heard Premier Zhou Enlai's report. He explained to us in detail the general line you laid down for our country's gradual realization of socialist industrialization and socialist transformation during the transition period, enabling us to have a clearer understanding of the path of the motherland towards socialist development. We are filled with confidence and have increased courage to fight resolutely, together with the people of the whole country, for the great task of realizing socialist national industrialization.
    At the congress, we also reviewed and discussed the work of the past few years, and we deeply feel that our work is still far from meeting the requirements of the motherland for us. In the future, we will continue to transform ourselves, study Marxism-Leninism and the policies of the Chinese Communist Party in a more planned way, delve into the struggle life of the people, grasp the direction of literature and art serving workers, peasants, and soldiers, truthfully reflect the magnificent face of the great era, and create works with high ideological and artistic qualities.
    Please accept our heartfelt gratitude and most sublime respect for you and your closest comrades-in-arms.
                                    The entire body of representatives of the Second Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers
      October 6, 1953

### 中国文学艺术工作者第二次代表大会  向毛泽东主席致敬电

1953-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中国文学艺术工作者第二次代表大会
    向毛泽东主席致敬电我们最敬爱的毛主席：
    我们是多么幸福啊，前天你偕同刘少奇副主席、朱德副主席、周恩来总理、陈云副总理和其他党和政府的领袖们来和我们见了面。我们每个人的心都被无上的欢乐所激荡。我们实在无法形容我们所受到的鼓励。
    我们的大会结束了。明天，我们将回到各人的岗位，我们将更好地工作，来报答你和你所领导的党和政府对我们的深切的关怀和期待。
    在大会上，我们听到了周恩来总理的报告。他详细地向我们阐述了你所规定的我国逐步实现社会主义工业化和社会主义改造的过渡时期的总路线，使我们对祖国向社会主义发展的道路有了更明确的认识。我们充满了信心，增加了勇气，将和全国人民一道坚决为实现社会主义的国家工业化的伟大任务而奋斗。
    在大会上，我们也检查了和讨论了这几年来的工作，我们深刻地感到我们的工作离开祖国对我们的要求还很远。今后，我们将继续改造自己，更有计划地学习马克思列宁主义和中国共产党的政策，深入人民群众的斗争生活，掌握文艺为工农兵服务的方向，真实地反映伟大时代的雄伟面貌，创造出具有高度思想性和艺术性的作品。
    请接受我们对于你和你最亲密的战友们的衷心的感激和最崇高的敬礼。
                                    中国文学艺术工作者第二次代表大会全体代表
      一九五三年十月六日
