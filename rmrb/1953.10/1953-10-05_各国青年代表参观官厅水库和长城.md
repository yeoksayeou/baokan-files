Title: Youth Representatives from Various Countries Visit the Guanting Reservoir and the Great Wall
Author:
Date: 1953-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, 4th -- A group of 94 youth representatives from North Korea, Romania, and Germany who came to China for a visit and to attend the National Day celebrations, as well as youth representatives from India, Pakistan, Nepal, Indonesia, Ceylon, the Union of South Africa, Thailand, Cuba, Brazil, Argentina, Chile, Guatemala, Bolivia, Australia, New Zealand, Syria, Lebanon, and Iraq who attended the Fourth World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival, visited China's largest reservoir - the Yongding River Guanting Reservoir project on the 4th, and toured the Great Wall, a great building in Chinese history.

    At the Guanting Reservoir construction site, the youth representatives from various countries, with excitement and keen interest, visited the dam and spillway projects that the reservoir builders completed ahead of schedule to welcome National Day with a high degree of patriotic labor enthusiasm, and the water intake tower project, which is in the final stage of construction.

### 各国青年代表参观官厅水库和长城

1953-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    各国青年代表参观官厅水库和长城
    【新华社保定四日电】来我国访问和参加国庆节观礼的朝鲜、罗马尼亚、德国青年代表团和出席第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的印度、巴基斯坦、尼泊尔、印度尼西亚、锡兰、南非联邦、泰国、古巴、巴西、阿根廷、智利、危地马拉、玻利维亚、澳大利亚、新西兰、叙利亚、黎巴嫩和伊拉克等国的青年代表一行九十四人，在四日参观了我国的第一大水库——永定河官厅水库工程，并游览了我国历史上的伟大建筑——长城。
    在官厅水库工地上，各国青年代表怀着兴奋的心情和浓厚的兴趣，参观了水库建设者为了迎接国庆节、以高度的爱国主义劳动热情提前完工的拦河坝、溢洪道工程和正在紧张进行最后一期施工的进水塔工程。
