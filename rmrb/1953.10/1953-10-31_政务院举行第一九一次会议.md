Title: Government Administration Council Holds 191st Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th—The 191st session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 29th. The meeting heard and approved the report by Minister of Railways Teng Daiyuan of the Central People's Government on the situation of railway work and future work arrangements. The meeting approved various lists of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第一九一次会议

1953-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一九一次会议
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百九十一次政务会议在二十九日下午举行。会议听取了和批准了中央人民政府铁道部滕代远部长所作的关于铁道工作情况及今后工作部署的报告。会议通过了各项任免名单。
