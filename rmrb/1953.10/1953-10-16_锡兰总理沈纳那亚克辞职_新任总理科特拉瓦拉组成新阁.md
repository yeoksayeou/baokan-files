Title: Ceylon Prime Minister Senanayake Resigns; New Prime Minister Kotelawala Forms New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1953-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 15th: Ceylon Prime Minister Dudley Senanayake resigned on the 12th. The British Governor-General of Ceylon, Lord Soulbury, appointed John Kotelawala, the former Minister of Transport and Works, as the new Prime Minister.

On the 13th, Ceylon's new Prime Minister, Kotelawala, announced his cabinet list. He removed J.R. Jayewardene from the post of Minister of Finance and appointed him Minister of Agriculture. The former Minister of Agriculture and Food, Gunetilleke, was appointed Minister of Finance. In addition, most of the former cabinet ministers remained in their posts.

### 锡兰总理沈纳那亚克辞职  新任总理科特拉瓦拉组成新阁

1953-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理沈纳那亚克辞职
    新任总理科特拉瓦拉组成新阁
    【新华社十五日讯】科伦坡消息：锡兰总理杜德莱·沈纳那亚克在十二日辞职，英国派驻锡兰的总督索尔伯里勋爵任命原任运输与工程部部长约翰·科特拉瓦拉继任总理。
    锡兰新总理科特拉瓦拉十三日宣布了他的内阁名单。他免掉了杰·鲁·贾雅瓦德尼的财政部长职，任命他为农业部长。前农业与粮食部部长古涅狄莱克被任命为财政部长。此外，前任内阁部长大多数都继续留任。
