Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-10-01
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The appearance of our country’s capital, Beijing, is becoming more and more magnificent with the beginning of the country’s planned economic construction. This is Chang'an Street, running east and west across Tiananmen Square. Today, the 500,000-strong parade celebrating National Day in the capital will pass through this wide street to receive Chairman Mao's review.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国的首都——北京的面貌，随着国家有计划的经济建设的开始而变得一天比一天更加壮丽了。这是横贯天安门广场的东西长安街。今天，首都庆祝国庆节的五十万人游行队伍将从这条宽广的街道上经过，接受毛主席的检阅。
