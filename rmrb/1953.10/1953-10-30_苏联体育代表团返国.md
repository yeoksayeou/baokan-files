Title: Soviet Sports Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1953-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: The Soviet sports delegation, which was invited to visit our country, returned home by plane from Beijing on the 29th. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Huang Zhong, Deputy Secretary-General, responsible cadres from the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the Cultural Department Club of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission, the North China Administrative Council's Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and the Beijing Municipal People's Government's Physical Culture and Sports Commission, as well as many sports workers from our country.

Safonov, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, and embassy staff also went to the airport to see them off.

During their one-and-a-half-month stay in our country, the Soviet sports delegation visited seven cities including Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang, Nanjing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Wuhan, and gave a total of twenty gymnastics performances, with an audience of more than 519,000 people. They also held several report meetings and symposiums with our sports workers, and visited and toured famous historical sites and scenic spots in various places. The delegation was warmly welcomed by the broad masses of the people wherever they went. Their highly skilled gymnastics performances left a deep impression on the people of our country and encouraged the people and sports workers of our country to further develop gymnastics.

The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission held a farewell banquet for all members of the Soviet sports delegation on the eve of their departure from Beijing.

### 苏联体育代表团返国

1953-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联体育代表团返国
    【新华社二十九日讯】应邀来我国访问的苏联体育代表团，已在二十九日乘飞机离北京返国。前往机场欢送的有中央人民政府体育运动委员会秘书长荣高棠、副秘书长黄中，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部俱乐部、华北行政委员会体育运动委员会和北京市人民政府体育运动委员会负责干部以及我国体育工作者多人。
    苏联驻华大使馆一等秘书萨福隆和使馆人员也到机场欢送。
    苏联体育代表团在我国逗留一个半月期间，访问了北京、天津、沈阳、南京、上海、广州、武汉等七个城市，共作了二十场体操表演，观众达五十一万九千多人。他们还和我国体育工作者举行了多次报告会和座谈会，并参观和游览了各地的名胜古迹。代表团每到一地，就受到我国广大人民热烈的欢迎。他们具有高度技能的体操表演给我国人民留下了深刻的印象，并鼓舞了我国人民和体育工作者进一步开展体操运动的信心。
    中央体育运动委员会在苏联体育代表团离北京前夕，曾设宴欢送代表团全体人员。
