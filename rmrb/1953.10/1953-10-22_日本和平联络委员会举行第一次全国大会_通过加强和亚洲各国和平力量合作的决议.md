Title: Japan Peace Liaison Committee Holds First National Congress; Passes Resolution on Strengthening Cooperation with Peace Forces in Asian Countries
Author:
Date: 1953-10-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, October 21st: The Japan Peace Liaison Committee for the Asian and Pacific Region, in commemoration of the first anniversary of the Asian and Pacific Region Peace Conference held in Beijing, the capital of China, last October, convened its first national congress in Tokyo on the 17th and 18th to discuss the issue of strengthening ties between Japan and the peace movements of Asian and Pacific countries.

    More than 200 representatives from labor union organizations such as the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the Japan Teachers' Union, and the National Railway Workers' Union, as well as the Japanese Red Cross Society and academic and cultural groups, participated in the conference. Japanese peace activists such as Goro Hani also attended the meeting.

    The conference read out congratulatory telegrams from foreign peace organizations. Representatives from the Japanese Communist Party, the Labor-Farmer Party, the left-wing Socialist Party, and other parties delivered speeches at the meeting. The conference passed a resolution to strengthen cooperation with peace forces in Asian and Pacific countries. All representatives attending the conference unanimously expressed their gratitude for China's goodwill in assisting Japanese nationals to return home and expressed their hope to further strengthen the friendly relations between the people of Japan and China. The conference also decided to launch a nationwide campaign to invite representatives of the Chinese Red Cross Society to visit Japan.

### 日本和平联络委员会举行第一次全国大会  通过加强和亚洲各国和平力量合作的决议

1953-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    日本和平联络委员会举行第一次全国大会
    通过加强和亚洲各国和平力量合作的决议
    【新华社二十一日讯】东京消息：亚洲及太平洋区域日本和平联络委员会，为了纪念去年十月在中国首都北京举行的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的一周年，于十七、十八两日在东京召开了第一次全国大会，讨论加强日本和亚洲及太平洋各国和平运动的联系问题。
    参加大会的有日本工会总评议会、日本教职员工会、国营铁道工会等工会组织，日本红十字会和学术、文化团体的代表共二百多人。日本和平人士羽仁五郎等也出席了大会。
    大会宣读了国外和平组织的贺电。日本共产党、劳农党、左派社会党及其他党派的代表都在会上发表了演说。大会通过了加强与亚洲及太平洋各国的和平力量合作的决议。参加大会的全体代表一致对中国善意协助日侨回国表示感谢，并希望进一步加强日、中两国人民的友好关系。大会并决定展开关于邀请中国红十字会代表访问日本的全国性运动。
