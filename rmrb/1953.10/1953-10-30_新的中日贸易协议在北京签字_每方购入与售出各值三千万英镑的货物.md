Title: New Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement Signed in Beijing; Each Side to Purchase and Sell Goods Worth 30 Million Pounds
Author:
Date: 1953-10-30
Page: 1

    New Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement Signed in Beijing
    Each Side to Purchase and Sell Goods Worth 30 Million Pounds
    [Xinhua News Agency, 29th] A new Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement, representing a further effort by the people of China and Japan to develop Sino-Japanese trade, was signed in Beijing at 11:00 am on October 29th. The new agreement is based on the Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement signed on June 1, 1952. In accordance with the principles of equality, mutual benefit, and exchange of similar goods, each side will purchase and sell goods worth 30 million pounds.
    Signing on behalf of the Chinese side were: Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and representatives participating in the negotiations, Lu Xuzhang, Ni Weiting, Shu Ziqing, Zhan Wu, Liu Jinsheng, Shang Guangwen, Ma Yimin, Gao Shangneng, Zou Siyi, Zhang Zheng, and Feng Tiecheng. Signing on behalf of the Japanese side were: Masanosuke Ikeda, head of the delegation of the "Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade," Natsuo Eto, Kei Hoashi, Koichi Nakamura, deputy heads of the delegation, and delegation members Toshio Tanaka, Ginzo Nagashima, Takechiyo Matsuda, Sanzunoske Nakamura, Soichiro Kita, Shigeyoshi Matsumae, Kihachiro Kimura, Akira Kazami, Goro Sudo, Yasushi Yuge, Koichi Uda , Satataro Murayama, Kiyoshi Hirano, Zenkichi Oshima, Den Kawakatsu, Toichiro Taniguchi, Hidejiro Sakurazawa, Yoshitaka Kikuchi, and Tetsuo Nakano.
    Attending the signing ceremony of the Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement were: Ji Chaoding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, committee members Lei Renmin, Chen Weiji, Meng Yongqian, Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department, Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Zhao Anbo, advisor to the Red Cross Society of China.
    Guests attending the signing ceremony also included Japanese peace activists Togo Kameda and Uemon Nakamura.
    After the Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement was signed, Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Masanosuke Ikeda, head of the delegation of the "Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade," delivered speeches successively. (Full text on page 4 of today's newspaper)
    In the evening, Nan Hanchen hosted a banquet for all members of the delegation of the Japanese "Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade."

### 新的中日贸易协议在北京签字  每方购入与售出各值三千万英镑的货物

1953-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    新的中日贸易协议在北京签字
    每方购入与售出各值三千万英镑的货物
    【新华社二十九日讯】作为中日两国人民为发展中日贸易而进一步努力的开始的新的中日贸易协议，已于十月二十九日上午十一时在北京签字。新的协议是以一九五二年六月一日所签订的中日贸易协议为基础，根据平等互利和同类物资相易的原则，每方购入与售出各为价值三千万英镑的货物。
    代表中国方面签字的是：中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸和参加这次谈判的代表卢绪章、倪蔚庭、舒自清、詹武、刘今生、商广文、马一民、高尚能、邹斯颐、张政、冯铁城。代表日本方面签字的是：日本“国会议员促进日中贸易联盟”代表团团长池田正之辅，副团长江藤夏雄、帆足计、中村高一和团员田中稔男、长岛银藏、松田竹千代、中村三之丞、喜多壮一郎、松前重义、木村禧八郎、风见章、须藤五郎、弓削靖、宇田耕一、村山佐太郎、平野清、大岛善吉、川胜传、谷口藤一郎、樱泽秀次郎、菊池善隆、中野撤夫。
    参加中日贸易协议签字仪式的有：中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎，委员雷任民、陈维稷、孟用潜，联络部部长谢筱乃，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会联络部副部长唐明照和中国红十字会顾问赵安博。
    参加签字仪式的来宾还有日本和平人士龟田东伍和中村玩右卫门。
    中日贸易协议签字后，中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸和日本“国会议员促进日中贸易联盟”代表团团长池田正之辅相继发表了谈话。（全文见今日本报第四版）
    晚间，南汉宸设宴招待日本“国会议员促进日中贸易联盟”代表团全体团员。
