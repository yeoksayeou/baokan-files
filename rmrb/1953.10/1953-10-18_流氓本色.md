    Scoundrelly Nature
    By Fang Cheng
    Under the strong counterattack of the heroic people of China and Korea, and through the efforts of the governments of China and Korea, the Soviet government, and peace-loving people all over the world, the American aggressors had no choice but to cease fire. However, they continue to expose their conspiracy to undermine the armistice and maintain international tensions at all times.
