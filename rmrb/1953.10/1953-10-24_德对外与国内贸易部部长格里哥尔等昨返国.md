Title: German Minister of Foreign and Domestic Trade Grigore and Others Return Home Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1953-10-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: Kurt Grigore, Minister of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic, and two others, who came to our country to discuss further development of Sino-German trade relations, left Beijing by plane this morning, October 23rd, to return home. Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Zhang Huadong, Director of the Import Bureau, Fu Shenglin, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau, Jia Shi, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau, Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department, and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to the airport to see them off. Also present at the airport to see them off were Durber, Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, and many others.

### 德对外与国内贸易部部长格里哥尔等昨返国

1953-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    德对外与国内贸易部部长格里哥尔等昨返国
    【新华社二十三日讯】前来我国商谈进一步发展中德贸易关系的德意志民主共和国对外与国内贸易部部长库尔特·格里哥尔等三人，已在二十三日晨乘飞机离京返国。前往机场欢送的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒、进口局局长张化东、出口局副局长傅生麟、第二局副局长贾石、交际处处长杨勉及外交部交际处副处长王拓等。前往欢送的还有：德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆商务参赞杜尔伯等多人。
