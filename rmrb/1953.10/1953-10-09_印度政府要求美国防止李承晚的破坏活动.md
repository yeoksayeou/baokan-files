Title: Indian Government Asks United States to Prevent Syngman Rhee's Sabotage

Author:

Date: 1953-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, dispatch from October 8th: TASS dispatch from October 7th: According to an Associated Press correspondent in Delhi, reliable sources have revealed that the Indian government has, through diplomatic channels, requested the United States government to take measures to prevent South Korea from obstructing the work of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission. It is understood that a representative of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs expressed exceptional concern to U.S. Ambassador Allen regarding South Korea's threat to forcibly release Chinese and North Korean prisoners of war currently under the guard of Indian troops in the neutral zone. Indian officials have requested the United States and other member states of the United Nations to work together to prevent any incidents that would hinder the normal activities of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission.

Furthermore, according to Delhi's "Indian Express," the Indian Ministry of External Affairs invited U.S. Ambassador to Delhi, Allen, on the 6th and discussed with him issues related to recent events in Korea.

### 印度政府要求美国防止李承晚的破坏活动

1953-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    印度政府要求美国防止李承晚的破坏活动
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社七日讯：据美联社驻德里记者报道，据可靠方面透露，印度政府通过外交途径要求美国政府采取措施，以防止南朝鲜阻挠中立国遣返委员会的工作。据悉，印度外交部代表对南朝鲜威胁要强迫释放目前在中立区由印度部队看管的中国和北朝鲜战俘一事，向美国大使艾伦表示异常忧虑；印度官方代表已请求美国和联合国其他会员国协同一致来防止任何有碍中立国遣返委员会正常活动的事端。
    又讯：据德里“印度快报”报道，印度外交部于六日邀请美国驻德里大使艾伦，并和他讨论与在朝鲜最近发生的事件有关的问题。
