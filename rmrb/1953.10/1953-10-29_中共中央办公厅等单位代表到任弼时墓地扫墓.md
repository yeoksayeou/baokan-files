Title: Representatives from the General Office of the CPC Central Committee and Other Units Sweep任弼时's Tomb

Author:

Date: 1953-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: Representatives from the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Women's Democratic Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, the Beijing Municipal People's Government and other units, as well as the family of Comrade Ren Bishi, went to the tomb of Comrade Ren Bishi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Honorary Chairman of the Central Committee of China's New Democratic Youth League, at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery in the western suburbs of Beijing on the morning of the 27th to sweep the tomb on the occasion of the third anniversary of his death.

### 中共中央办公厅等单位代表到任弼时墓地扫墓

1953-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央办公厅等单位代表到任弼时墓地扫墓
    【新华社二十七日讯】十月二十七日是中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会名誉主席任弼时同志逝世三周年纪念日。中国共产党中央委员会办公厅、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会、北京市人民政府等单位代表及任弼时同志家属等于二十七日上午前往北京西郊八宝山革命烈士公墓任弼时同志墓地扫墓。
