Title: Congratulating the First Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement - Premier Zhou Enlai's Telegram to Premier Tsedenbal
Author:
Date: 1953-10-05
Page: 1

    To Comrade Tsedenbal, Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to the Mongolian people, the government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and to you, Premier Comrade.
    This agreement marks a new historical stage in the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Mongolia. It is of utmost importance in promoting the economic and cultural construction of both countries and strengthening the friendship between the two peoples.
    For the prosperity of Mongolia and China, for the strength of the world peace and democracy camp headed by the great Soviet Union, and for peace in the Far East and the world, I wish our fraternal and unbreakable friendship will be further consolidated and developed.
                  Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Administrative Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
                   October 4, 1953, Beijing

### 祝贺中蒙经济及文化合作协定签订一周年  周恩来总理致泽登巴尔总理电

1953-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺中蒙经济及文化合作协定签订一周年
    周恩来总理致泽登巴尔总理电
    蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔同志：
    值此中蒙经济及文化合作协定签订一周年之际，谨向蒙古人民、蒙古人民共和国政府及您，总理同志，致以衷心的祝贺。
    这个协定标志着中蒙两国间的友好合作关系进入了新的历史阶段，它对于促进两国间的经济与文化建设，加强两国人民之间的友谊有着极重要的意义。
    为了蒙中两国的繁荣，为了以伟大的苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营的强大，为了远东与世界的和平，祝我们两国之间兄弟般的、牢不可破的友谊更加巩固与发展。
                  中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来
                   一九五三年十月四日北京
