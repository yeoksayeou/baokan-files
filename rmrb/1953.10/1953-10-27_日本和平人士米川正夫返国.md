Title: Japanese Peace Advocate, Masao Yonekawa, Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1953-10-27
Page: 1

    Japanese Peace Advocate, Masao Yonekawa, Returns Home
    (Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 26th) Professor Masao Yonekawa, a Japanese peace advocate who was invited to visit our country, left Guangzhou this morning, the 26th, via Hong Kong to return to his country. Among those who saw him off at the station were Qiu Zhe, Vice-Chairman of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others.

### 日本和平人士米川正夫返国

1953-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    日本和平人士米川正夫返国
    【新华社广州二十六日电】应邀来我国参观访问的日本和平人士米川正夫教授，在二十六日晨离广州取道香港回国。到车站欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广东省分会副主席丘哲等人。
