Title: Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao Zedong from All Comrades of the National Track and Field, Gymnastics, and Cycling Games
Author:
Date: 1953-10-08
Page: 3

To our beloved Chairman Mao:
    We report to our beloved leader with boundless excitement: The first National Track and Field, Gymnastics, and Cycling Games since the founding of the People's Republic of China concluded victoriously today after intense competition.
    The holding of this games demonstrates that mass sports activities and sports competitions in our country have been widely developed in schools and the armed forces, and are gradually being developed in factories, government offices, and rural areas. Moreover, our sports technical level has gradually improved. In this competition, new national records were created in nineteen track and field events for both men and women. The glorious achievements of this games are all due to your constant care and encouragement.
    This games has consistently been conducted in the spirit of humbly learning, unity and friendship, promoting collectivism and revolutionary heroism. All the participants demonstrated a high degree of organization and discipline, showed good sportsmanship by not being arrogant in victory or discouraged in defeat, and actively learning from each other. We assure you that we will continue to promote the excellent spirit and style of this games, carry out mass sports activities across the country, and on this basis, intensify training and improve our skills to strive for new records.
    Respected Chairman Mao, we deeply know that our motherland has entered a new period of planned national economic construction, and the people of the whole country are shouldering the glorious and arduous task of building and defending our motherland. We must follow your instruction to "develop sports and strengthen the people's physique," actively publicize and organize the broad masses of the people to participate in sports activities, so that the people of the whole country can have strong bodies and abundant energy, and strive to gradually realize the socialist industrialization of our motherland and gradually transition to a socialist society.

    All Comrades of the 1953 National Track and Field, Gymnastics, and Cycling Games
    October 7, 1953

### 全国田径、体操、自行车运动大会全体同志  向毛泽东主席致敬电

1953-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    全国田径、体操、自行车运动大会全体同志
    向毛泽东主席致敬电亲爱的毛主席：
    我们怀着无比兴奋的心情向亲爱的领袖报告：新中国成立以来第一次全国田径、体操、自行车运动大会，经过热烈的竞赛，今天胜利地闭幕了。
    这次大会的举行，说明我国人民群众性的体育活动和运动竞赛已经在学校和部队中广泛地开展起来，工厂、机关和农村中也正在逐步开展。而且我们的运动技术水平也逐步地提高了。在这次竞赛中，有男女田径赛十九个项目创造了全国的新纪录。这次大会能够获得这些光辉的成就，都是由于您的不断地关怀和鼓舞。
    这次大会，始终是在虚心学习、团结友爱、发扬集体主义和革命英雄主义的精神下进行的。到会的全体人员都表现了高度的组织性和纪律性、胜不骄、败不馁、积极进取、互相学习的良好体育道德。我们向您保证：一定要继续发扬这次大会的优良的精神和作风，开展全国群众性的体育运动，并在这个基础上，加紧锻炼，提高技术，为创造新的纪录而努力。
    敬爱的毛主席，我们深深知道祖国已进入有计划的国民经济建设的新时期，全国人民担负着建设祖国和保卫祖国光荣而又艰巨的任务，我们一定要遵照您的“发展体育运动，增强人民体质”的指示，积极地宣传和组织广大人民群众参加体育活动，使全国人民都能有强壮的身体和充沛的精力，为逐步实现祖国社会主义工业化和逐步地过渡到社会主义社会而奋斗。
                一九五三年全国田径、体操、自行车运动大会全体同志一九五三年十月七日
