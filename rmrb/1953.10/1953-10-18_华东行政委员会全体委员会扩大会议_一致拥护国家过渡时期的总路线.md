Title: The Enlarged Meeting of the East China Administrative Committee Unanimously Endorses the General Line for the Transition Period

Author: 
Date: 1953-10-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 17th. The East China Administrative Committee held an enlarged plenary meeting from October 12th to 14th. At the meeting, Committee Member Chen Yi first gave a report on the content of the 49th Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the 24th, 25th, and 26th meetings of the Central People's Government Committee. He analyzed the current international and domestic situation, explained the general line for the transition period, and put forward several suggestions on how to implement this general line in East China and Shanghai. He emphasized that East China and Shanghai are a key area in the country for carrying out the socialist transformation of private industry and commerce. If East China and Shanghai do well and smoothly, it will promote the whole country; it will also have a huge impact on tapping industrial potential, coordinating with the country's Five-Year Plan for economic construction, and creating wealth for the country.

The meeting then unfolded into a discussion, with Chen Jiageng, Liu Hongsheng, Shen Zhiyuan, Miao Hainan, Chen Pixian, and 23 others speaking successively at the meeting. The speakers all focused on the issue of socialist transformation of private industry and commerce during the transition period, using various facts and experiences to illustrate the necessity and correctness of this policy. Some private industrial and commercial representatives attending as observers introduced the improving and thriving situation of their production after accepting state processing orders or implementing joint state-private ownership; some expressed their willingness to work hard to create conditions for accepting socialist transformation. All representatives expressed their support for the general line of the country during the transition period and expressed their willingness to strive for its gradual realization.

Finally, Committee Member Chen Yi made a summary of the meeting, and the "Resolution on Endorsing the Various Reports of the 49th Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the 24th, 25th, and 26th Meetings of the Central People's Government Committee" was unanimously adopted.

### 华东行政委员会全体委员会扩大会议  一致拥护国家过渡时期的总路线

1953-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    华东行政委员会全体委员会扩大会议
    一致拥护国家过渡时期的总路线
    【新华社上海十七日电】华东行政委员会在十月十二日到十四日举行全体委员会扩大会议。会议首先由陈毅委员作了关于中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第四十九次扩大会议和中央人民政府委员会第二十四、二十五、二十六次会议内容的传达报告。他分析了当前国际和国内的形势，阐释了过渡时期的总路线，并对华东和上海贯彻执行这一总路线的办法提出了几项意见。他着重指出：华东和上海在实行对私营工商业的社会主义改造中，在全国来说是一个重点地区，华东和上海做得好，做得很顺利，将对全国起推动作用；对于发挥工业潜在力，配合国家五年经济建设计划，为国家创造财富，也将有巨大作用。
    会议接着展开讨论，陈嘉庚、刘鸿生、沈志远、苗海南、陈丕显等二十八人在会上相继发言。发言者都围绕着在过渡时期对私营工商业进行社会主义改造的问题，用各种事实和体验来说明这一政策的必要性和正确性。有些私营工商界列席代表在发言中介绍了他们接受国家加工定货或实行公私合营后生产日趋好转和欣欣向荣的情况；有的表示将努力创造条件，接受社会主义改造。所有代表的发言，都对国家过渡时期的总路线表示拥护，并愿为其逐步实现而努力。
    会议最后由陈毅委员作总结，并一致通过了“关于拥护中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第四十九次扩大会议和中央人民政府委员会第二十四次、二十五次、二十六次会议的各项报告的决议”。
