Title: Korean Embassy Holds Reception to Celebrate the Third Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's Entry into Korea

Author:

Date: 1953-10-27
Page: 1

    To Celebrate the Third Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's Entry into Korea
    Korean Embassy Holds Reception
    [Xinhua News Agency, October 25th] To celebrate the third anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's participation in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, Xu Zhe, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, held a film reception on the evening of October 25th.
    More than 800 people were invited to attend the reception, including responsible persons from the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea and the Beijing Branch of the Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, as well as wounded and sick veterans of the Volunteer Army, family members of Volunteer Army soldiers, and representatives from various people's organizations.
    Diplomatic personnel from foreign embassies in China were also invited to attend.

### 庆祝志愿军入朝作战三周年  朝大使馆举行招待会

1953-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝志愿军入朝作战三周年
    朝大使馆举行招待会
    【新华社二十五日讯】为庆祝中国人民志愿军抗美援朝参战三周年，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办徐哲于二十五日晚举行电影招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的有中国人民抗美援朝总会、北京市抗美援朝分会负责人，志愿军伤病员和志愿军家属及各人民团体代表等共八百余人。
    各国驻华使馆外交人员也应邀出席。
