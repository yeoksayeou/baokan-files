Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Advocates Round Table Format for Political Conference
Author: 
Date: 1953-10-15
Page: 1
　
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th Dispatch: According to the Indian News Service on the 12th, Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunarjo, who arrived in Delhi on the 11th for a visit, emphasized to reporters: If the scheduled Korean political conference is to achieve any results, it should not be limited to the belligerent parties. The political conference should have the nature of a round table meeting, with participants including not only the belligerent parties, but also other countries.

### 印尼外长主张政治会议采取圆桌会议形式

1953-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼外长主张政治会议采取圆桌会议形式
    【新华社十三日讯】据印度新闻处十二日讯：十一日到达德里访问的印度尼西亚外交部长苏纳约，向新闻记者强调说：预定举行的朝鲜政治会议，如果要得到任何结果，不应该仅限于交战方面参加。政治会议应该有圆桌会议的性质，参加者不仅有交战方面，也应该有其他国家。
