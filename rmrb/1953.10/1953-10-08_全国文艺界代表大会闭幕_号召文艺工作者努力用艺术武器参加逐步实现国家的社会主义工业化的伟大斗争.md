Title: National Literary and Art Circles Congress Concludes, Calling on Literary and Art Workers to Strive to Use Artistic Weapons to Participate in the Great Struggle to Gradually Realize the Country's Socialist Industrialization

Author:
Date: 1953-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Second Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers concluded on the afternoon of October 6th.

At the meeting on the morning of the 6th, all the representatives unanimously adopted the following resolution:

"The Second Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers unanimously supports Premier Zhou Enlai's political report and instructions on literary and art work, and unanimously agrees with Comrade Zhou Yang's report on the situation of literary and art work in the past four years and the future tasks. The Congress calls on all literary and art workers across the country, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, to grasp the direction of serving workers, peasants, and soldiers, delve into real life, improve artistic cultivation, strive for artistic practice, and use artistic weapons to participate in the great struggle to gradually realize the country's socialist industrialization."

Subsequently, the Congress revised and adopted the "Constitution of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles" and conducted the election of the members of the Second National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles.

Then, the Congress unanimously passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and a telegram of respect to the Chinese People's Volunteers.

After listening to the speech of Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Congress unanimously resolved: "The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and its member organizations shall join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as group members, and calls on all literary and art workers across the country to strive to learn from the advanced experience of Soviet literature and art, strengthen the exchange of literature and art between China and the Soviet Union, and consolidate and develop the sacred friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union in the common cause of defending world peace."

At the meeting on the afternoon of the 6th, the Cuban poet Guillén, who was invited to our country to participate in this year's commemoration of the four world cultural celebrities, attended the meeting and spoke, expressing the congratulations of Cuban progressive writers to the Congress.

Then, Hu Qiaomu, Deputy Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, made a report to all the representatives attending the Congress on the organizational tasks of literary and art groups in striving for the prosperity of literature and art in our country.

Mao Dun delivered the closing speech on behalf of the presidium of the Congress. After analyzing the gains of the Congress, he called on literary and art workers across the country to take serious study, serious creation, and adherence to criticism and self-criticism as regular tasks.

The Congress declared its conclusion amidst thunderous applause.

### 全国文艺界代表大会闭幕  号召文艺工作者努力用艺术武器参加逐步实现国家的社会主义工业化的伟大斗争

1953-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    全国文艺界代表大会闭幕
    号召文艺工作者努力用艺术武器参加逐步实现国家的社会主义工业化的伟大斗争
    【新华社七日讯】中国文学艺术工作者第二次代表大会，已在十月六日下午闭幕。
    六日上午的大会上，全体代表一致地通过了下列的决议：
    “中国文学艺术工作者第二次代表大会一致拥护周恩来总理的政治报告和对文学艺术工作的指示，并一致同意周扬同志的关于四年来文学艺术工作状况和今后任务的报告。大会号召全国文学艺术工作者，在中国共产党领导下，掌握为工农兵服务的方向，深入实际生活，提高艺术修养，努力艺术实践，用艺术的武器来参加逐步实现国家的社会主义工业化的伟大斗争。”
    随后，大会修正通过了“中国文学艺术界联合会章程”，并且进行了中国文学艺术界联合会第二届全国委员会委员的选举。
    接着，大会一致地通过了向毛主席致敬的电文和向中国人民志愿军致敬的电文。
    大会在听了中苏友好协会总会副总干事李沾吾的讲话后，一致决议：“中国文学艺术界联合会及其所属会员团体加入中苏友好协会为团体会员，并号召全国文学艺术工作者，努力学习苏联文学艺术事业的先进经验，加强中苏两国文学艺术的交流，巩固和发展中苏两国人民在保卫世界和平的共同事业中的神圣的友谊。”
    在六日下午的大会上，应邀来我国参加今年世界四位文化名人纪念会的古巴诗人纪廉到会讲话，表示古巴进步作家对于大会的祝贺。
    接着，中共中央宣传部副部长胡乔木向出席大会的全体代表作了关于文学艺术团体为争取我国文学艺术的繁荣的组织任务的报告。
    茅盾代表大会主席团致闭幕词。他在分析了大会的收获之后，号召全国文学艺术工作者把认真学习、认真创作、坚持批评与自我批评作为经常的任务。
    大会在雷鸣一样的掌声中宣告结束。
