Title: Romanian Cultural Delegation and British Electrical Trades Union Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-10-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. According to the 1953 implementation plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the Romanian People's Republic Cultural Delegation, consisting of six members led by Chairman Eugen Frunza, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 30th, invited to visit our country and attend the National Day celebrations.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them were: Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council; Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and literary and art figures from the capital, Yang Shuo, Miao Tianrui, Chen Youxin, and Li Zhishu.

Guo Zuowen, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, and embassy staff also went to the airport to welcome them.

The delegation list is as follows:

Head of the Delegation: Eugen Frunza, poet.

Members: Gheorghe Calinescu, literary critic, writer, university professor, academician of the Academy of Sciences.
	Benitato Grigore, professor of pharmacology, academician of the Academy of Sciences.
	Staniscu Lucia, opera soloist.
	Cioroveanu Ilicea, pianist.
	Georgiu Stefan, violinist.

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The British Electrical Trades Union Delegation, consisting of eight members, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 30th, invited by the National Committee of the Chinese Electrical Workers' Union to visit our country and attend the National Day celebrations.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them were: Cai Shubin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Electrical Workers' Union, Vice Chairmen Huang Minwei and Liu Yingyuan, Secretary-General Hong Haiquan, and Fang Ming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

The delegation list is as follows:

Head of the Delegation: Walter Charles Stevens (General Secretary of the British Electrical Trades Union)

Members: A.C. Batchelor (Member of the Executive Committee of the British Electrical Trades Union)
	W.D. Benson (National Officer of the British Electrical Trades Union)
	M. Crofts (Electrical Worker)
	H. Howells (Electrical Worker)
	S. Lillicrap (Electrical Worker)
	G. Stuart (Electrical Worker)
	G. Lee (Electrical Worker)

### 罗文化代表团和英电气工会代表团到京

1953-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    罗文化代表团和英电气工会代表团到京
    【新华社三十日讯】根据中罗文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划，应邀前来我国访问并参加我国庆节观礼的罗马尼亚人民共和国文化代表团一行六人，在团长欧伊根·弗朗查率领下，于三十日乘飞机到达北京。
    前赴机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，外交部交际处副处长王拓，以及首都文学艺术界人士杨朔、缪天瑞、陈又新、李志曙等人。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使郭佐文及使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    代表团名单如下：
    团长：欧伊根·弗朗查，诗人。
    团员：格奥尔基·卡利尼斯库，文学批评家、
          作家、大学教授、科学院院士。
          本尼塔托·格里戈里，药物学教授、科
          学院院士。
          斯丹尼斯库·露奇亚，歌剧独唱家。
          契罗维亚努·伊利查，钢琴家。
          乔治乌·斯特凡，提琴家。
   【新华社三十日讯】应中国电业工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问并参加国庆观礼的英国电气工会代表团一行八人，于三十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    赴机场欢迎的有：中国电业工会全国委员会主席蔡书彬，副主席黄民伟、刘英源，秘书长洪海泉及中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长方明等人。
    代表团名单如下：
