Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Statement
Author: 
Date: 1953-10-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th—Moscow newspapers on the 11th published the reply statement of Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, regarding the three notifications from the United States government, transmitted through the Swedish government, concerning the political conference.
