Title: Our Country's慰问团 Arrives in Pyongyang; Korean Representatives from All Circles and Chinese People's Volunteer Army Representatives Give a Warm Welcome at the Station
Author:
Date: 1953-10-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 21st. The entire body of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, the First General Sub-Delegation, and five performing arts troupes from the general delegation, totaling over 1,000 people, arrived in Pyongyang, the democratic capital of Korea, at 10:00 AM (Korean time) on the 21st via a special train, led by General He Long, the head of the delegation. Accompanying the delegation were Kim Cheon-hae, Chairman of the Welcoming Committee specially formed by the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland to welcome and host the delegation, and several committee members. Li Da, Chief of Staff of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, who greeted the delegation in Andong, and Gan Yetao, Chargé d'Affaires of our embassy in Korea, also arrived on the same train.

The delegation members were warmly welcomed by all circles in Pyongyang. Those who welcomed them at Pyongyang Station included: Park Jeong-ae and Kim Il, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Hong Myeong-hui, representative of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland and Vice Premier of the Cabinet; Choi Yong-kun, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense; Park Eui-wan, Vice Premier; Park Young-bin, Director of the Organization and Guidance Department of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party; Kim Chang-man, Director of the Propaganda and Agitation Department; Yoon Gong-heum, Minister of Finance; Ko Bong-ki, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Kim Ung, Vice Minister of National Defense; Jung Ryul, Vice Minister of Culture and Propaganda; Kim Gwang-hyeop, Chief of General Staff of the Korean People's Army; Choi Jong-hak, Director of the General Political Bureau; Jeong Yeon-pyo, Chairman of the Pyongyang People's Committee; heads of Korean political parties and social organizations; senior generals and combat heroes of the Korean People's Army; labor heroes, model workers, and representatives from all walks of life in the Pyongyang area, totaling over 5,000 people. Also present at the station to welcome the delegation were diplomatic officials from our country, the Soviet Union, and other countries stationed in Korea. Chinese People's Volunteer Army units from nearby areas also sent representatives to Pyongyang Station to welcome the delegation.

That morning, representatives from all walks of life and people's soldiers in Pyongyang City, dressed in festive attire, holding the national flags of Korea, China, and the Soviet Union, as well as portraits of Premier Kim Il-sung, Chairman Mao Zedong, and Chairman Malenkov, along with welcome banners and flags, and holding flowers, lined up to gather at the station.

As the delegation's special train entered Pyongyang Station, sustained cheers and applause resounded on the platform. When the entire delegation, headed by General He Long, and the First General Sub-Delegation disembarked, they warmly shook hands with the welcoming Korean party, government, and military leaders, and stepped out of the station gate in their company. The cheering crowd rushed forward to offer flowers to the delegation members, and warmly shook hands and embraced them.

Subsequently, a grand welcoming rally was held in the square. The military band played the national anthems of Korea and China. Hong Myeong-hui, representative of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland, delivered a welcoming speech, expressing heartfelt gratitude on behalf of the Korean people for the great internationalist assistance given by the Chinese people. He said: Now, the American aggressors have not abandoned their ambition to invade Korea, China, and dominate the world, and are creating various conspiracies in an attempt to undermine the Korean political conference. However, the united strength of the Korean and Chinese people, supported by the peace and democracy camp headed by the great Soviet Union, will surely shatter any conspiracies and plans of the aggressors and gloriously defend the freedom and independence of Korea and China and peace in the Far East.

Kim Gwang-hyeop, Chief of General Staff of the Korean People's Army, extended a warm welcome to the delegation on behalf of the Korean People's Army. He said: All officers and soldiers of our Korean People's Army must maintain a high degree of vigilance, continuously strengthen the combat effectiveness of our troops, and further consolidate the steel-like fighting unity of the Korean and Chinese people's armies. We must repay the assistance and expectations of the people of the great Soviet Union, China, and various people's democratic countries for the Korean people with actions to strive for the peaceful reunification of our fatherland.

General He Long, head of the delegation, delivered a reply amidst warm applause. He said: We, the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, feel very honored to come to Pyongyang and meet with the people of Pyongyang. First of all, I would like to pay tribute to the heroic city of Pyongyang and to the victorious people of Pyongyang. The Korean armistice has been achieved, and the Korean people have been victorious under the wise leadership of Premier Kim Il-sung. The Chinese people have sent a delegation to comfort and congratulate the heroic Korean people, to comfort and congratulate the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who have won a great victory, and to express our lofty respect to our dear Premier Kim Il-sung. At the same time, we will also conduct extensive visits in Korea to convey the heroic achievements of the Korean people, the Korean People's Army, and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to the Chinese people, in order to further enhance the fraternal friendship between the people of Korea and China.

The welcoming rally concluded in the national anthems of Korea and China and amidst warm cheers. When the delegation representatives drove through the main streets of Pyongyang, they were warmly welcomed by the citizens of Pyongyang lining the streets.

### 我国赴朝慰问团到达平壤  朝鲜各界和我志愿军代表到车站热烈欢迎

1953-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我国赴朝慰问团到达平壤
    朝鲜各界和我志愿军代表到车站热烈欢迎
    【新华社平壤二十一日电】中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团及第一总分团全体人员及总团的五个文工团共一千多人，在总团长贺龙将军率领下，于二十一日上午十时（朝鲜时间）乘专车到达朝鲜民主首都平壤市。陪同慰问团到达的有朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会为欢迎和招待慰问团而特别组成的迎接委员会委员长金天海和委员多人。到安东迎接慰问团的中国人民志愿军代表、中国人民志愿军参谋长李达将军和我国驻朝鲜大使馆代办甘野陶也同车到达。
    慰问团人员受到平壤市各界的热烈欢迎。到平壤车站欢迎的有：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、金一，朝鲜祖国统一战线中央委员会代表、内阁副首相洪命熹，副首相兼民族保卫相崔庸健，副首相朴义琓，劳动党中央组织指导部部长朴永彬，宣传鼓动部部长金昌满，财政相尹公钦，外务省副相高峰起，民族保卫省副相金雄，文化宣传省副相郑律，朝鲜人民军总参谋长金光侠，总政治局局长崔宗学，平壤市人民委员会委员长郑然彪，朝鲜各政党、社会团体负责人，朝鲜人民军高级将领、战斗英雄以及平壤地区的劳动英雄、模范和各界人民代表五千多人。到车站欢迎的还有我国和苏联及各国驻朝鲜的外交官员。附近地区的中国人民志愿军部队也派代表到平壤车站欢迎。
    这天清晨，穿着节日盛装的平壤市各界人民代表和人民战士们，举着朝、中、苏三国的国旗和金日成首相、毛泽东主席、马林科夫主席的画像、欢迎标语和旗帜，手捧鲜花，唱着歌曲，列队向车站聚集。
    慰问团专车一进入平壤车站，站台上就响起了经久不息的欢呼声和掌声。当以贺龙将军为首的慰问团总团和第一总分团的全体人员下车后，与欢迎的朝鲜党政军首长亲切地握手并在他们的陪同下步出车站大门时，欢呼着的人群纷纷拥上前去向慰问团人员献花，并和他们热烈地握手、拥抱。
    接着，盛大的欢迎会在广场上举行。军乐队奏起了朝中两国国歌。朝鲜祖国统一战线中央委员会代表洪命熹首先致欢迎词，他代表朝鲜人民对中国人民给予的伟大的国际主义援助表示衷心的感谢。他说：现在，美国侵略者还没有放弃侵略朝鲜、中国和称霸世界的野心，正在制造各种阴谋，企图破坏朝鲜政治会议。但是，受到以伟大的苏联为首的和平民主阵营支持的朝中两国人民的团结力量，一定能粉碎侵略者的任何阴谋和策划，光荣地保卫朝中两国的自由独立与远东的和平。
    朝鲜人民军总参谋长金光侠代表朝鲜人民军对慰问团表示热烈欢迎。他说：我们朝鲜人民军全体将士一定要保持高度警惕性，不断加强部队的战斗力，进一步巩固朝中人民军队钢铁般的战斗团结。我们要以争取完成祖国和平统一的行动来报答伟大的苏联、中国和各人民民主国家的人民对于朝鲜人民的援助和期望。
    慰问团总团长贺龙将军在热烈的掌声中致答词。他说：我们中国人民赴朝慰问团来到平壤，同平壤市人民见面，感到非常荣幸。我首先向英雄的平壤市致敬，向胜利了的平壤市人民致敬。朝鲜停战已经实现了，朝鲜人民在金日成首相的英明领导下已经胜利了。中国人民派遣了慰问团，来慰问和祝贺英雄的朝鲜人民，慰问和祝贺获得了伟大胜利的朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军，并向亲爱的金日成首相表达崇高的敬意。同时，我们在朝鲜还要进行广泛的访问，把朝鲜人民、朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的英雄业绩传达给中国人民，以更加增进朝中两国人民兄弟般的友谊。
    欢迎会在朝中两国国歌声和热烈的欢呼声中结束。当慰问团代表们乘车驰过平壤各大街时，受到了平壤市民的夹道欢迎。
