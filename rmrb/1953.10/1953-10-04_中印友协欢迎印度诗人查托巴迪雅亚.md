Title: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Welcomes Indian Poet Chattopadhyaya
Author:
Date: 1953-10-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, and Chen Hansheng, Vice President, held a reception on October 2nd to welcome the Indian poet Harindranath Chattopadhyaya.

Those present as companions included: Lao She, Wu Zuoren, Wu Maosun, Ji Xianlin, Zhang Mingyang, Liu Baiyu, and Deng Tuo, directors of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association; and Mei Lanfang, Xia Yan, Ke Zhongping, Zheng Zhenduo, Tian Han, Ai Qing, Yuan Shuipai, Tian Jian, Xie Bingxin, He Luting, Ding Shande, Zhou Xiaoyan, Ma Shaobo, Zhang Junxiang, Sun Shen, Zhu Ming, Zou Difan, Hua Junwu from the literary and artistic circles.

The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 中印友协欢迎印度诗人查托巴迪雅亚

1953-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    中印友协欢迎印度诗人查托巴迪雅亚
    【新华社二日讯】中印友好协会会长丁西林、副会长陈翰笙十月二日举行酒会欢迎印度诗人哈林德拉纳特·查托巴迪雅亚。
    出席作陪的有：中印友好协会理事老舍、吴作人、吴茂荪、季羡林、张明养、刘白羽、邓拓；文学艺术界梅兰芳、夏衍、柯仲平、郑振铎、田汉、艾青、袁水拍、田间、谢冰心、贺绿汀、丁善德、周小燕、马少波、张骏祥、孙慎、朱明、邹荻帆、华君武等。
    酒会在友好的空气中举行。
