Title: Romanian Writer Valerian Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Galan Valerian, a journalist and writer from Romania, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 24th for a visit to China under the 1953 execution plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Valerian is currently an editor at "Spark," the organ of the Romanian Workers' Party. His work "Dawn of the Slaves" has won the National Prize of the Romanian People's Republic.

### 罗马尼亚作家瓦勒里安到京

1953-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚作家瓦勒里安到京
    【新华社二十五日讯】根据中罗文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划来华访问的记者和作家加兰·瓦勒里安已于二十四日乘飞机抵达北京。瓦勒里安现为罗马尼亚工人党机关报“火花报”编辑，他的著作“奴隶们的黎明”曾获罗马尼亚人民共和国国家奖金。
