Title: Youth League and National Youth Federation Host Banquet for Youth Representatives from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1953-10-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 1st.
    The Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Democratic Youth Federation jointly hosted a banquet on the evening of National Day to welcome youth representatives from various countries who were invited to visit China and participate in the National Day celebrations.

    The following youth representatives were invited to attend the banquet: Chile, Argentina, Cuba, Iraq, Indonesia, Vietnam , Pakistan, Nepal, Union of South Africa, Australia, New Zealand, Syria, Lebanon , Bolivia, Guatemala, India, Brazil, Thailand, German Democratic Republic, Romania, Ceylon, Laos, Democratic People's Republic of Korea and other countries.

    The hosts present at the banquet included: Hu Yaobang, Liu Daosheng, Luo Yi, Qu Tangliang, Zhang Ze, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and Qian Sanqiang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, Secretary-General Yu Beichen, Deputy Secretary-Generals Ding Cong, Sun Fuling, and Guan Shixiong.

    At the banquet, Hu Yaobang, on behalf of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and Qian Sanqiang, on behalf of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, extended a warm welcome to the youth representatives of various countries.

    During the banquet, the guests and hosts frequently toasted to the profound friendship between Chinese youth and youth from various countries in the cause of defending world peace.

### 青年团和全国青联欢宴各国青年代表

1953-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    青年团和全国青联欢宴各国青年代表
    【新华社一日讯】中国新民主主义青年团和中华全国民主青年联合会，在国庆节晚上联合欢宴应邀来我国访问并参加国庆观礼的各国青年代表。
    应邀参加宴会的有：智利、阿根廷、古巴、伊拉克、印度尼西亚、越南、巴基斯坦、尼泊尔、南非联邦、澳大利亚、新西兰、叙利亚、黎巴嫩、玻利维亚、危地马拉、印度、巴西、泰国、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、锡兰、寮国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国等国的青年代表。
    主人方面出席宴会的有：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、刘导生、罗毅、区棠亮、章泽、胡克实，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席钱三强、秘书长于北辰、副秘书长丁聪、孙孚凌、关世雄等。
    宴会上，胡耀邦代表中国新民主主义青年团、钱三强代表中华全国民主青年联合会对各国青年代表表示热忱的欢迎。
    席间，宾主频频为中国青年与各国青年在保卫世界和平事业中的深厚友谊而干杯。
