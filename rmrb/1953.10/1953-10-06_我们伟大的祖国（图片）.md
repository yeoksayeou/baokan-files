Our great motherland
The development of the Li Jinming Mongolian-Han Joint Agricultural Production Cooperative in the Right Banner of Horqin, Inner Mongolia, fully demonstrates the spirit of mutual assistance and unity between the Mongolian and Han peoples. Zhang Xiuzhi, the leader of the women's production team of this cooperative, is shearing wool with Jin Mushe (left), a Mongolian female member.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    内蒙古科尔沁右翼前旗李金铭蒙汉联合农业生产合作社的发展，充分表现了蒙汉人民互助团结的精神。这个合作社的妇女生产队队长张秀芝正在和蒙族女社员金木舍（左）一起剪羊毛。
