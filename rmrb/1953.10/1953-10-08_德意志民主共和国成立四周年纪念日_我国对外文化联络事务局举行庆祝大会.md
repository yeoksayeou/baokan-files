Title: Celebration Rally Held by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations to Commemorate the Fourth Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1953-10-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government held a rally in Beijing on the 7th to celebrate the fourth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.  Those present included: Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education of the Central People's Government; Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of Culture; Zeng Zhaolun, Deputy Minister of Higher Education; Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education; Zhou Jianren, Deputy Director of the Publishing Administration; Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; leading members of various people's organizations; and over a thousand people from cultural, educational, and other circles in the capital.
    Mr. Koenig, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, was also invited to attend.
    Also present were diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries to China.
    The Scientific and Cultural Delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Gerhard Harig, the German Youth Delegation headed by Werner Erb, the Vice Chairman of the German Writers Association Stephan Hermlin, and the Chairman of the Berlin Writers Association Jan Petersen also attended the celebration rally.
    The celebration opened solemnly with the national anthems of Germany, China, and the Soviet Union.  At the rally, Minister Zhang Xiruo spoke first. He extended warm congratulations to the government and people of the German Democratic Republic. He described in detail the brilliant achievements made by the people of the German Democratic Republic in the past four years in political, economic, and cultural construction, as well as in the struggle for peaceful reunification, and the growing friendly and cooperative relations between China and Germany over the past four years.  He said: The Chinese and German people will unite even more closely in the peaceful and democratic family headed by the Soviet Union and march towards new victories.
    Next, Ambassador Koenig spoke. He pointed out that the current task of the democratic and patriotic forces in Germany is to stop the conspiracy of the warmongers, restore national unity, and establish a unified, peaceful, and democratic Germany. The fortress in this struggle is the German Democratic Republic. He then described the close political, economic, and cultural relations between China and Germany over the past four years, pointing out that the friendly cooperation between the two peoples is becoming increasingly consolidated and developed. He said: The source of our strength and confidence in the struggle lies in the awareness of great friendship and international solidarity, and we will do everything possible to carry out the struggle against our common enemy in the spirit of true patriotism and internationalism.
    After the meeting, the Central Experimental Opera House Orchestra and the China Peking Opera Troupe successively performed famous German classical music and Peking Opera and other art programs.

### 德意志民主共和国成立四周年纪念日  我国对外文化联络事务局举行庆祝大会

1953-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国成立四周年纪念日
    我国对外文化联络事务局举行庆祝大会
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局特于七日在北京举行庆祝德意志民主共和国成立四周年大会。到会的有：中央人民政府教育部部长张奚若，外交部副部长伍修权，文化部副部长丁西林，高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，教育部副部长韦悫，出版总署副署长周建人，对外文化联络事务局副局长陈忠经，各人民团体负责人与首都文化教育及其他各界人士千余人。
    德意志民主共和国驻我国大使柯尼希也应邀到会。
    到会的还有苏联和各人民民主国家驻我国外交使节和使馆人员。
    以格哈德·哈里希为首的德意志民主共和国科学文化考察团，以温纳·埃尔本为首的德国青年代表团，德国作家协会副主席斯蒂芬·赫姆林，柏林市作家协会主席杨·贝特尔逊也出席了庆祝大会。
    庆祝会在德、中、苏三国国歌声中隆重开幕。会上，首先由张奚若部长讲话。他向德意志民主共和国政府和德国人民致以热烈的祝贺。他详细叙述了德意志民主共和国人民四年来在政治、经济、文化建设等方面与争取和平统一的斗争中所取得的辉煌成就，及中德两国之间的友好合作关系四年来日益发展的情况。他说：中国人民和德国人民将更加紧密地团结在以苏联为首的和平民主大家庭中向着新的胜利前进。
    接着，柯尼希大使讲话。他指出，德国民主爱国力量当前的任务是制止战争贩子的阴谋，恢复国家统一以及建立一个统一、和平与民主的德国。这个斗争中的堡垒就是德意志民主共和国。他接着叙述了四年来中德两国间在政治、经济及文化上的密切关系，指出，两国人民之间的友好合作日益巩固与发展。他说：我们在斗争中力量与信心的源泉乃是意识到伟大的友谊与国际团结，我们将尽一切力量以真正爱国主义与国际主义的精神来进行反对我们共同敌人的斗争。
    会后，由中央实验歌剧院管弦乐队及中国京剧团先后演出了德国著名的古典乐曲和京剧等文艺节目。
