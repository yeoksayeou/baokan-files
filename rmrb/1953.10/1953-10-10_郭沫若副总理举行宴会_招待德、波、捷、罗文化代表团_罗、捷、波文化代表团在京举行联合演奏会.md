Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Premier, Hosts Banquet to Entertain Cultural Delegations from Germany, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Romania. Romanian, Czechoslovakian, and Polish Cultural Delegations Hold Joint Concert in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-10-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, dated 9th: Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government and Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council, hosted a banquet in Beijing on the evening of the 9th to entertain the Scientific and Cultural Delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Harrich, the Polish Cultural Delegation headed by Sokolsky, the Czechoslovakian Cultural Delegation headed by Majerová, and the Romanian Cultural Delegation headed by Francha.

Attending the banquet were: Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education, Li Dequan, Minister of Health, Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture, Yang Xiufeng, Huang Songling, and Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Ministers of Higher Education, Wei Que, Vice Minister of Education, Ye Shengtao, Chen Kehan, and Sa Kongliao, Deputy Directors of the Publishing Administration, Xu Yixin, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, and Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and representatives from the literary, artistic, and scientific circles of the capital: Xia Yan, Xiao San, Shakoff, Ke Zhongping, Shao Quanlin, Liu Baiyu, Zheng Zhenduo, Chen Baichen, Cao Yu, Hua Junwu, Qian Sanqiang, and more than one hundred others.

Also attending the banquet were Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Kanzala, Romanian Ambassador to China, Guo Zuowen, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, König, and Secretary of the Polish Embassy in China, Kra.

Before the banquet began, Vice Premier Guo delivered a speech, warmly welcoming the cultural delegations from the fraternal countries to visit China. He said that the visit of so many outstanding scientists, educators, writers, and artists to China would further promote the friendship and cultural exchange between our peoples. Vice Premier Guo expressed his gratitude to the guests for their lectures, symposiums, and performances in the capital, congratulated them on their gains during their visits in the capital, and wished all the delegations greater achievements during their visits to various parts of China.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the friendship between the Chinese, Romanian, Czechoslovak, Polish, and German peoples.

The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere throughout.

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The artists from the Romanian, Czechoslovakian, and Polish cultural delegations, who are currently visiting Beijing, held a joint concert in Beijing on the evening of the 8th.

Attending the performance were: Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Yang and Ding Xilin, Vice Ministers of Culture, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, and representatives from cultural and artistic circles of the capital, totaling more than 700 people.

Also attending the performance were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, as well as the Romanian Cultural Delegation, the Czechoslovakian Cultural Delegation, the Polish Cultural Delegation, and the Scientific and Cultural Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, who are currently visiting Beijing.

Before the performance began, Ding Xilin delivered a speech, warmly welcoming the holding of the concert. He pointed out that the visit of the cultural delegations from the fraternal countries to China this time would further strengthen cultural exchanges between each other.

Then the performance began. The performance programs included piano solos, violin solos, female and male solo singing, and poetry recitations. All the programs performed were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 郭沫若副总理举行宴会  招待德、波、捷、罗文化代表团  罗、捷、波文化代表团在京举行联合演奏会

1953-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副总理举行宴会
    招待德、波、捷、罗文化代表团
    罗、捷、波文化代表团在京举行联合演奏会
    【新华社九日讯】中央人民政府政务院副总理兼政务院文化教育委员会主任郭沫若九日晚在北京设宴招待以哈里希为首的德意志民主共和国科学文化考察团，以索科尔斯基为首的波兰文化代表团，以玛耶洛娃为首的捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团，以弗朗查为首的罗马尼亚文化代表团。
    出席宴会作陪的有：中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，教育部部长张奚若，卫生部部长李德全，文化部副部长丁西林，高等教育部副部长杨秀峰、黄松龄、曾昭抡，教育部副部长韦悫，出版总署副署长叶圣陶、陈克寒、萨空了，外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新，中国科学院副院长陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训，以及中央人民政府各部门首长和首都文学、艺术、科学界人士：夏衍、萧三、沙可夫、柯仲平、邵荃麟、刘白羽、郑振铎、陈白尘、曹禺、华君武、钱三强等一百余人。
    出席宴会的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉，罗马尼亚驻华大使郭佐文，德意志民主共和国驻华大使柯尼希和波兰驻华大使馆秘书克拉。
    宴会开始前，郭副总理致词对各兄弟国家文化代表团的前来我国访问，表示热烈欢迎。他说，这样多的优秀的科学家、教育家、文学家和艺术家前来我国访问，更进一步地促进了我们之间的人民友谊和文化交流。郭副总理对来宾们在首都的讲演、座谈和表演表示感谢，祝贺他们在首都参观访问中所得的收获，并预祝各代表团到我国各地参观中取得更多成就。
    席间宾主频频为中罗、中捷、中波、中德人民间的友谊干杯。
    宴会始终洋溢着热烈友好的气氛。
   【新华社讯】正在首都进行参观访问的罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰三个文化代表团的艺术家们，于八日晚在北京举行联合演奏会。
    出席观看表演的有：中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若，外交部副部长伍修权，文化部副部长周扬、丁西林，中央人民政府各部门首长和首都文化艺术界代表等七百余人。
    出席观看表演的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员，以及正在首都参观访问的罗马尼亚文化代表团、捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团、波兰文化代表团和德意志民主共和国科学文化考察团。
    表演开始前，丁西林在致词中对演奏会的举行，表示热烈欢迎。他指出各兄弟国家文化代表团这次到中国访问，将更进一步加强彼此间的文化交流。
    接着表演开始。表演节目包括钢琴独奏、小提琴独奏、女声和男声独唱及诗歌朗诵等。表演的各项节目都受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
