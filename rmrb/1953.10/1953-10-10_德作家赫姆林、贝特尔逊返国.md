Title: German Writers Hermlin and Betelson Return Home
Author:
Date: 1953-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: German Democratic Republic writer and Vice-Chairman of the German Writers' Association, Stephan Hermlin, and the Chairman of the Berlin Writers' Association, Jan Betelson, left Beijing by plane on the 8th to return to their country. They came to China for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Literary Workers, according to the 1953 executive plan of the Sino-German cultural cooperation agreement.

Those who saw them off at the airport included: Ke Zhongping, Feng Zhi, Sha Ting, Chen Baichen, Ma Feng, and others from the Council of the Chinese Writers Association.

Mrs. Grutner, First Secretary of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, also saw them off at the airport.

Hermlin and Betelson visited China for nearly a month and a half. They visited Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, and other places, and visited the Palace Museum and the Revolution Museum in Beijing, Lu Xun's tomb and Lu Xun Memorial Hall in Shanghai, the Peasant Movement Training Institute in Guangzhou, which was once presided over by Chairman Mao, and the Guangzhou Commune Memorial Hall, etc.; they toured famous scenic spots and historical sites in various places.

### 德作家赫姆林、贝特尔逊返国

1953-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    德作家赫姆林、贝特尔逊返国
    据新华社讯：德意志民主共和国作家、德国作家协会副主席斯蒂芬·赫姆林与柏林市作家协会主席杨·贝特尔逊于八日乘飞机离京返国。他们是根据中德文化合作协定一九五三年度执行计划应中华全国文学工作者协会的邀请到我国来参观访问的。
    到机场欢送的有：中国作家协会理事会理事柯仲平、冯至、沙汀、陈白尘、马烽等。
    德意志民主共和国驻我国大使馆一等秘书葛鲁特纳夫人也到机场欢送。
    赫姆林和贝特尔逊在中国作了为期将近一个半月的访问，曾经到上海、杭州、广州等地访问，并参观了北京的故宫博物院、革命博物馆，上海的鲁迅墓、鲁迅纪念馆，广州毛主席曾主持过的农民讲习所及广州公社纪念馆等；游览了各地名胜古迹。
