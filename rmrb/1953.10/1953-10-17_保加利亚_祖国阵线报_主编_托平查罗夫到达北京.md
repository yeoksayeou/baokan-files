Title: Editor-in-Chief of Bulgarian "Fatherland Front" Newspaper, Topincharov, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-10-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Vladimir Topincharov, a Bulgarian journalist, has arrived in Beijing by plane on the 15th, at the invitation of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement's 1953 implementation plan.

Topincharov is the editor-in-chief of the Bulgarian "Fatherland Front" newspaper and a professor in the Department of History at Sofia State University.

### 保加利亚“祖国阵线报”主编  托平查罗夫到达北京

1953-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚“祖国阵线报”主编
    托平查罗夫到达北京
    【新华社十六日讯】根据中保文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划，应邀来我国访问的保加利亚记者弗拉季米尔·托平查罗夫已于十五日乘飞机抵达北京。
    托平查罗夫是保加利亚“祖国阵线报”的主编和国立索非亚大学历史系教授。
