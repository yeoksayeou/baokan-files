Title: Congratulating the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the Third Anniversary of Their Entry into Korea, Marshal Kim Il-sung Sends a Telegram to General Peng Dehuai
Author:
Date: 1953-10-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 25th: Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, sent a telegram to General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, on the 25th, congratulating the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the third anniversary of their entry into Korea. The telegram is as follows:
    To General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army:

    On the occasion of the third anniversary of the heroic Chinese People's Volunteer Army's entry into Korea, I would like to extend my warm congratulations and gratitude to you and, through you, to all the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on behalf of all the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army and in my own name.

    The brave Chinese People's Volunteer Army, holding high the banner of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, fought alongside the Korean People's Army, inflicting a fatal blow on the American armed aggressors and achieving a great victory, making immortal contributions to the cause of security in the Far East and world peace.

    The great contributions and the model of internationalism demonstrated by the heroic Chinese People's Volunteer Army in the sacred struggle of the Korean people to defend their motherland's freedom and independence will forever remain deeply in the hearts of our people.

    As they welcome the third anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's entry into Korea, all the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army are further strengthening their determination to consolidate and develop the results of the armistice jointly achieved with the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. I wish you and all the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army even more glorious achievements in the struggle to defend the security of the Far East and lasting world peace.

    Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army
    Pyongyang, October 25, 1953

### 祝贺中国人民志愿军入朝作战三周年  金日成元帅致电彭德怀将军

1953-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺中国人民志愿军入朝作战三周年
    金日成元帅致电彭德怀将军
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅二十五日致电中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军，祝贺中国人民志愿军入朝作战三周年。电文如下：中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军
    值此英雄的中国人民志愿军入朝作战三周年之际，我谨以全体朝鲜人民军将士和我个人的名义，向您并通过您向中国人民志愿军全体将士致以热烈的祝贺与感谢。
    高举抗美援朝的旗帜参加朝鲜战争的英勇的中国人民志愿军部队和朝鲜人民军一起，给了美国武装侵略者以致命的打击，获得了伟大的胜利，对远东的安全与世界和平的事业建立了不朽的功勋。
    英雄的中国人民志愿军在朝鲜人民捍卫祖国的自由、独立的神圣斗争中所建立的伟大功勋和表现的国际主义模范，将千秋万代地深深留在我国人民的心里。
    全体朝鲜人民军将士们在迎接中国人民志愿军入朝作战三周年之际，正在进一步坚定自己的决心，来巩固并发展和中国人民志愿军所共同取得的停战的成果。谨祝您和中国人民志愿军全体将士在保卫远东的安全与世界的持久和平的斗争中获得更加光辉的成果。
    朝鲜人民军最高司令官元帅金日成
    一九五三年十月二十五日于平壤
