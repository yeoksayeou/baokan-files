Title: Soviet Sports Delegation Holds Gymnastics Performance in Beijing; Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Zhu De and Others Attend and Watch

Author:

Date: 1953-10-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th. The Soviet sports delegation held a gymnastics performance in Beijing on the afternoon of the 30th. This performance was hosted by the Central People's Government Sports Commission.

Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Gao Gang, and Premier of the Government Administration Council Zhou Enlai attended the performance and received the head of the sports delegation, Gramadsky, before the program began.

Also attending the performance were: Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council Guo Moruo and Deng Xiaoping, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Director of the Central Sports Commission He Long, Vice Chairmen of the State Planning Commission Li Fuchun and Jia Tuofu, Deputy Directors of the Government Administration Council Political and Legal Committee Peng Zemin and Luo Ruiqing (concurrently Minister of Public Security), Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang, Deputy Minister of Geology He Changgong, Deputy Chairman of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission Liu Geping, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Gan Siqi, Director of the General Logistics Department Yang Lisan, Chairman of the Ningxia Provincial People's Government Xing Zhaotang, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Jiageng, Shao Lizi, Xu Deheng, Chen Shaoxian, and Lu Han, Director of the General Office of the CPC Central Committee Yang Shangkun, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Cai Chang, Vice Chairman Deng Yingchao, as well as sports workers, cadres from organizations, groups, and the military in the capital.

Also attending the performance was the Chinese People's Volunteer Army National Day observation delegation headed by General Yang Dezhi.

Soviet Ambassador to China Kuznetsov and embassy staff also attended the performance.

The Soviet sports delegation performed fourteen extremely brilliant gymnastics skill programs at the performance. These programs repeatedly won enthusiastic applause from those present.

### 苏联体育代表团在京举行体操表演会  中央人民政府副主席朱德等出席观看

1953-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联体育代表团在京举行体操表演会
    中央人民政府副主席朱德等出席观看
    【新华社三十日讯】苏联体育代表团于三十日下午在北京举行体操表演会。这一表演会由中央人民政府体育运动委员会主持。
    中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、高岗，政务院总理周恩来出席观看，并在节目开始前，接见了体育代表团团长格拉玛德斯基。
    出席表演会的还有：政务院副总理郭沫若、邓小平，秘书长习仲勋，中央体育运动委员会主任贺龙，国家计划委员会副主席李富春、贾拓夫，政务院政治法律委员会副主任彭泽民、副主任兼公安部部长罗瑞卿，外交部副部长王稼祥，地质部副部长何长工，中央民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任甘泗淇，总后勤部部长杨立三，宁夏省人民政府主席邢肇棠，北京市副市长吴晗，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员陈嘉庚、邵力子、许德珩、陈劭先、卢汉，中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅、副主席邓颖超以及首都体育工作者和机关、团体、部队的干部多人。
    出席表演会的还有：以杨得志将军为首的中国人民志愿军国庆节归国观礼代表团。
    苏联驻华大使库兹湼佐夫和使馆人员也出席了表演会。
    苏联体育代表团在表演会上表演了十四个极为精采的体操技巧节目。这些节目，都一再博得了到会者热烈的鼓掌。
