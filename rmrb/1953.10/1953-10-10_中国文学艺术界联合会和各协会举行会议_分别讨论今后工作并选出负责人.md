Title: China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Various Associations Hold Meetings to Discuss Future Work and Elect Leaders
Author:
Date: 1953-10-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 9th October:

The China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the various associations, including the China Writers Association, the China Dramatists Association, the China Musicians Association, and the China Artists Association, separately held national committee and council meetings to discuss future work and elect the chairmen, vice-chairmen, and other leaders of the Federation and the various associations.

The first meeting of the Second National Committee of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles was held on the morning of October 9th. At the meeting, Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, Ding Ling, Zheng Zhenduo, Xia Yan, Ba Jin, Ke Zhongping, Lao She, Tian Han, Ouyang Yuqian, Mei Lanfang, Hong Shen, Yang Hansheng, Cai Chusheng, Yuan Muzhi, Qi Baishi, Jiang Feng, Lü Ji, Ma Sicong, and Chen Yi were unanimously elected as members of the Presidium of the Second National Committee of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, forming the Presidium. Guo Moruo was elected as the Chairman of the Presidium, Mao Dun and Zhou Yang as Vice-Chairmen, and Yang Hansheng as Secretary-General. At the meeting, opinions were exchanged on organizing political and professional study for the literary and art circles, coordinating with government cultural departments to reward outstanding literary and artistic works, and strengthening foreign literary and artistic exchanges.

The Council of the China Writers Association held a meeting on the morning of October 9th. At the meeting, Mao Dun was elected as Chairman, and Zhou Yang, Ding Ling, Ba Jin, Ke Zhongping, Lao She, Feng Xuefeng, and Shao Quanlin were elected as Vice-Chairmen, forming the Presidium of the Council of the China Writers Association. The China Musicians Association, the China Artists Association, and the China Dramatists Association also held council meetings successively. The China Dramatists Association elected Tian Han as Chairman, and Ouyang Yuqian, Mei Lanfang, and Hong Shen as Vice-Chairmen. The China Musicians Association elected Lü Ji as Chairman, and Ma Sicong and He Luting as Vice-Chairmen. The China Artists Association elected Qi Baishi as Chairman, and Jiang Feng, Liu Kaiqu, Ye Qianyu, Wu Zuoren, and Cai Ruohong as Vice-Chairmen.

After discussions by the council or the standing council, each association decided to establish certain organizations to be responsible for specifically promoting various literary and artistic works; and all elected the leaders of these organizations. Each association has a creation committee or studio, as well as working departments for popularization work, research on national literature and art, editing and publishing, and funds and welfare. In addition, the Writers Association also has an affiliated literary seminar, the Dramatists Association also has a performing arts committee and an actors' club, and the Artists Association also has a department specifically in charge of exhibition work.

In addition, the China Quyi Research Association held a council meeting on October 4th, electing Wang Zunsan as Chairman.

The All-China Dance Workers Association decided to reorganize into a research-oriented group for dance workers and appointed Dai Ailian, Wu Xiaobang, Tian Yu, and others to be responsible for the preparations.

### 中国文学艺术界联合会和各协会举行会议  分别讨论今后工作并选出负责人

1953-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中国文学艺术界联合会和各协会举行会议
    分别讨论今后工作并选出负责人
   【新华社九日讯】中国文学艺术界联合会和中国作家、戏剧家、音乐家、美术家等各个协会分别举行全国委员会和理事会，讨论今后工作，并推选出联合会及各个协会的主席、副主席等负责人。
    中国文学艺术界联合会第二届全国委员会第一次会议在十月九日上午举行，会上一致推选出郭沫若、茅盾、周扬、丁玲、郑振铎、夏衍、巴金、柯仲平、老舍、田汉、欧阳予倩、梅兰芳、洪深、阳翰笙、蔡楚生、袁牧之、齐白石、江丰、吕骥、马思聪、陈沂等二十一人为中国文学艺术界联合会第二届全国委员会主席团委员，组成主席团；并推选出郭沫若为主席团主席，茅盾、周扬为副主席，阳翰笙为秘书长。会上并对组织文艺界的政治和业务学习、协同政府文化部门奖励优秀文艺作品和加强对外文艺交流等项工作交换了意见。
    中国作家协会理事会在十月九日上午举行会议，会上选出茅盾为主席，周扬、丁玲、巴金、柯仲平、老舍、冯雪峰、邵荃麟等七人为副主席，组成中国作家协会理事会主席团。中国音乐家、美术家、戏剧家等协会均先后举行了理事会；中国戏剧家协会推选出田汉为主席，欧阳予倩、梅兰芳、洪深为副主席。中国音乐家协会推选出吕骥为主席，马思聪、贺绿汀为副主席。中国美术家协会推选出齐白石为主席，江丰、刘开渠、叶浅予、吴作人、蔡若虹为副主席。
    各个协会经过理事会或常务理事会的讨论，都决定设立一定的机构，以负责具体推动各种文学艺术工作；并都推选出了这些机构的负责人。各个协会都设有创作委员会或创作室，和普及工作，民族文学艺术的研究，编辑和出版，基金和福利等工作部门。另外，作家协会还附设有文学讲习所，戏剧家协会还设有表演艺术委员会和演员俱乐部，美术家协会还设有专管展览会工作的部门。
    此外，中国曲艺研究会在十月四日举行了理事会，推选出王尊三为主席。
    中华全国舞蹈工作者协会决定改组为舞蹈工作者的研究性质的团体，并推定戴爱莲、吴晓邦、田雨等人负责筹备。
