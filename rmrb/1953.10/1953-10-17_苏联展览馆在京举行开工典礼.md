Title: Groundbreaking Ceremony Held for Soviet Exhibition Hall in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-10-17
Page: 1

    Soviet Exhibition Hall Groundbreaking Ceremony Held in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, October 15th] Construction of the grand Soviet Exhibition Hall project commenced in Beijing on the 15th. A solemn groundbreaking ceremony was held at the construction site that morning.
    Those attending the ceremony included: Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Wang Guangwei, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government; Song Yuhe, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Construction Engineering of the Central People's Government; Li Zheren, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government; Ji Chaoding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Wang Jiqi, Deputy Director of the Central Design Institute; Liu Ren, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Liu Laifu, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions; bureau directors and deputy directors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and all construction workers participating in the hall's construction project.
    Foreign guests invited to attend the ceremony included: Vasnov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in the People's Republic of China; Navicki and Danishenko, Deputy Trade Representatives of the Soviet Union in China; Soviet architects Andreyev and Gisnova, and engineer Gochman.
    At the beginning of the ceremony, Mayor Peng Zhen delivered a speech. He said: The Soviet Exhibition Hall project is starting construction today. After this project is completed, our great friend, the Soviet Union, will hold an exhibition here on the great achievements of the Soviet Union in economic and cultural construction. This will greatly help our people to further understand the great achievements of Soviet socialist construction, for us to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, for promoting the friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, and for the large-scale, planned construction undertakings we are currently undertaking. It will be of great help and significance.
    Mayor Peng Zhen called on all workers and cadres participating in the Soviet Exhibition Hall project to give full play to their high enthusiasm and creativity, overcome all difficulties, and complete it on schedule.
    Subsequently, Navicki, the Deputy Trade Representative of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Soviet architect Andreyev spoke successively. They unanimously pointed out: Today we and you are working together on a building of great significance. This building will be of great significance for further consolidating and developing the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples, because during the exhibition, the Chinese people will become more familiar with the achievements of the Soviet people in industry, agriculture, culture and education.
    Finally, Li Yu, Chairman of the Trade Union of the Soviet Exhibition Hall Construction Site of the First Construction Engineering Company of the Beijing Municipal Construction Engineering Bureau, spoke on behalf of all employees. He said: We resolutely respond to the call of Mayor Peng Zhen, under the leadership of the Party and the government, and with the cordial help and guidance of Soviet experts, we will give full play to our high enthusiasm and creativity, work hard to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, overcome all difficulties, and ensure that we complete this glorious task on schedule and strive to complete it ahead of schedule.
    At the end of the ceremony, Mayor Peng Zhen broke ground, and this great construction project officially began.

### 苏联展览馆在京举行开工典礼

1953-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联展览馆在京举行开工典礼
    【新华社十五日讯】规模宏大的苏联展览馆工程已于十五日在北京开工建造。这天上午，工地上隆重地举行了开工典礼。
    参加典礼的有：北京市人民政府市长彭真，中央人民政府国家计划委员会副秘书长王光伟，中央人民政府建筑工程部副部长宋裕和，中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长李哲人，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎，中央设计院副院长汪季琦，中共北京市委员会副书记刘仁，北京市总工会副主席刘莱夫，北京市人民政府各局局长、副局长和参加建馆工程的全体建筑工人。
    应邀参加典礼的外宾有：苏联驻中华人民共和国大使馆参赞华士诺夫，苏联驻华商务副代表那维琪、丹尼辛柯，苏联建筑师安德烈耶夫、吉斯诺娃，工程师郭赫曼。
    典礼开始，彭真市长致词。他说：苏联展览馆的工程今天就要动工了，在这个工程完成后，我们伟大的朋友——苏联，将在这里举办关于苏联在经济和文化建设上的伟大成就的展览会。这对于使我国人民进一步认识与了解苏联社会主义建设的伟大成就，对于我们学习苏联的先进经验，对于增进中苏两国人民之间的友谊，对于我们正在进行着的、大规模的、有计划的各项建设事业，都将会有很大的帮助和重要的意义。
    彭真市长号召参加苏联展览馆工程工作全体工人和干部，发挥高度的积极性和创造性，克服一切困难，来按期完成它。
    接着，苏联驻华大使馆商务副代表那维琪和苏联建筑师安德烈耶夫相继讲话。他们一致指出：今天我们和你们共同来为有着崇高意义的建筑而工作。这个建筑对于进一步巩固和发展中苏两国人民的友谊将有很大的意义，因为在展览期间，中国人民将更进一步熟悉苏联人民在工业、农业、文化和教育方面的成就。
    最后，北京市建筑工程局第一建筑工程公司苏联展览馆工地工会主席李育代表全体职工讲话。他说：我们坚决响应彭真市长的号召，在党和政府的领导下，在苏联专家亲切的帮助和指导下，发挥高度的积极性和创造性，努力学习苏联先进经验，克服一切困难，保证按期并争取提前完成这一光荣的任务。
    典礼结束时，彭真市长破土，这一伟大的建筑工程正式开始动工。
