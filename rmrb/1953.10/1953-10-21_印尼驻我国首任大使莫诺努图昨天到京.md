Title: Indonesian First Ambassador to Our Country, Mononutu, Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1953-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 20th: Arnold Mononutu, the first Ambassador of the Republic of Indonesia to our country, and his party arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 20th. Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Yang Chengwu, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command, were among those who went to the station to welcome them.

Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries to China also went to the station to welcome them. Isha Mahdi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia in China, and all the staff of the embassy also went to welcome them.

### 印尼驻我国首任大使莫诺努图昨天到京

1953-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼驻我国首任大使莫诺努图昨天到京
    【新华社二十日讯】印度尼西亚共和国驻我国首任大使阿诺德·莫诺努图等一行于二十日晚乘车抵京。赴车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府外交部办公厅主任王炳南，亚洲司司长陈家康，交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔，京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武等。
    前往车站欢迎的还有各国驻华使节和使馆人员。印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂和全体馆员也前往欢迎。
