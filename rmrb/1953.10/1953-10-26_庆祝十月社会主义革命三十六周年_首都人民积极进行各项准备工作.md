Title: Celebrating the 36th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution: The People of the Capital Actively Carry Out Various Preparatory Works
Author:
Date: 1953-10-26
Page: 1

    Celebrating the 36th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
    The People of the Capital Actively Carry Out Various Preparatory Works
    [Xinhua News Agency, October 25th] The people of the capital are actively preparing to celebrate the 36th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.
    People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a celebration meeting on November 7th. Around the October Revolution Day, some larger factories, mines, enterprises, government organs, and schools in the capital will hold small celebration meetings or symposiums. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will hold a report meeting. The Beijing Association for the Popularization of Science and Technology will hold a report meeting with the theme of publicizing advanced Soviet science and technology. Some members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's delegation to the Soviet Union who are in Beijing will also be invited to report to the people of the capital on their impressions of their visit to the Soviet Union.
    Starting from the holiday, the six major cinemas in the capital will continuously screen five color Soviet films. The film projection team of the Beijing Municipal People's Government's Cultural Affairs Administration will screen Soviet films for farmers in the suburbs of the capital. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions will jointly hold a photo exhibition at the Laboring People's Cultural Palace. Many pictures introducing the lives and construction of the Soviet people will also be exhibited in many factories, mines, construction sites, and state-owned farms in the suburbs of the capital, as well as in some scenic spots. Around November 7th, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will hold Soviet music and film evenings related to the commemoration of the October Revolution Day.
    Currently, various preparatory works are actively underway.

### 庆祝十月社会主义革命三十六周年  首都人民积极进行各项准备工作

1953-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月社会主义革命三十六周年
    首都人民积极进行各项准备工作
    【新华社二十五日讯】首都人民正在积极筹备庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十六周年。
    首都各界人民将在十一月七日举行庆祝大会。在十月革命节前后，首都一些较大的厂矿、企业、机关和学校，将举行小型的庆祝会或座谈会。北京市中苏友好协会将举行报告会。北京市科学技术普及协会，将举办以宣传苏联先进科学技术为内容的报告会。中苏友好协会总会赴苏参观团一些在京的团员们，也将被邀请向首都人民作访苏观感的报告。
    从节日开始，首都六大电影院将连续放映五个彩色的苏联影片。北京市人民政府文化事业管理处的电影放映队，将对首都郊区农民放映苏联影片。北京市中苏友好协会和北京市总工会，将在劳动人民文化宫联合举行图片展览。许多介绍苏联人民的生活和建设的图片，也将在首都许多工厂、矿山、工地和郊区的国营农场以及一些风景区里展览。十一月七日左右，北京市中苏友好协会将举办有关纪念十月革命节的苏联音乐和电影晚会。
    现在，各项准备工作正在积极进行中。
