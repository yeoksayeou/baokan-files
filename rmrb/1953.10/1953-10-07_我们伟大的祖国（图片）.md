    Our Great Motherland
    Graduate students of the Mining Machinery Department of the Beijing Mining Institute are conducting production internships in the Jixi Coal Mine in Northeast China to master mining production skills. They are studying the structure of the cutting disc and crushing rods of an automatic combined coal mining machine.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京矿业学院机械系采掘机械事业的研究生，为了掌握矿业的生产技能，在东北鸡西煤矿井下进行生产实习。他们正在研究自动联合采煤机截盘和破碎杆的构造。
