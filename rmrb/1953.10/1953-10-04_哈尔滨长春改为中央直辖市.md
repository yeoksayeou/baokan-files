Title: Harbin and Changchun Reclassified as Directly Subordinate Municipalities

Author: 
Date: 1953-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency

Harbin and Changchun Reclassified as Directly Subordinate Municipalities

[Xinhua News Agency] The State Administration Council of the Central People's Government has decided to reclassify Harbin, formerly under Songjiang Province, and Changchun, formerly under Jilin Province, as municipalities directly under the Central Government, to be administered on behalf of the Central Government by the Northeast Administrative Committee, in order to meet the needs of the cities' increasing development.

### 哈尔滨长春改为中央直辖市

1953-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨长春改为中央直辖市
    【新华社讯】中央人民政府政务院决定将松江省属的哈尔滨和吉林省属的长春两市改划为中央直辖市，由东北行政委员会代管，以适应两市工作日益发展的需要。
