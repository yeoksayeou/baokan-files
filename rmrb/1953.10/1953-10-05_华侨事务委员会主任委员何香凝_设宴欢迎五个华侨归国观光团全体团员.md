Title: He Xiangning, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, Hosts a Banquet Welcoming All Members of Five Overseas Chinese Visiting Groups

Author:
Date: 1953-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. He Xiangning, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 4th to welcome all members of the five Overseas Chinese visiting groups from Korea, Vietnam, Burma, India, and Indonesia.

Those present as guests included: Liao Chengzhi, Li Tiemin, and Zhuang Xiquan, Vice Chairmen of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission; Situ Meitang, Fei Zhendong, Zhao Lingde, Lian Guan, Xu Jingcheng, Peng Zemin, Shao Lizi, Wang Jiyuan, and Hong Sisi, members of the Commission; and Lu Xinyuan, Peng Guanghan, and Chen Manyun, Deputy Directors of the General Office.

Also attending the banquet were: Yao Zhongming, our country's ambassador to Burma; Zhao Zhongshi, our country's consul general to Indonesia; and Liu Yufeng, our country's consul general to India.

Before the banquet began, the five Overseas Chinese visiting groups from Korea, Vietnam, Burma, India, and Indonesia jointly presented flags to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, the Chinese People's Volunteers, and Chairman He Xiangning, which were accepted by Chairman He Xiangning.

After the flag presentation, Hong Zaide, leader of the Indonesian Overseas Chinese visiting group, delivered a speech on behalf of the five Overseas Chinese visiting groups.

He expressed immense excitement about the strength of the motherland. He said that every overseas Chinese person feels infinite glory and pride, and with the strength of the motherland, the patriotic unity of overseas Chinese is also expanding and consolidating day by day.

Chairman He Xiangning delivered a reply speech, expressing a warm welcome to the various Overseas Chinese visiting groups for returning to the country for sightseeing. She pointed out that the motherland has begun large-scale economic construction in a planned way this year, and the future of the motherland is infinitely bright.

At the banquet, the guests frequently toasted to the health of Chairman Mao.

### 华侨事务委员会主任委员何香凝  设宴欢迎五个华侨归国观光团全体团员

1953-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    华侨事务委员会主任委员何香凝
    设宴欢迎五个华侨归国观光团全体团员
    【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府华侨事务委员会主任委员何香凝于四日晚设宴欢迎朝鲜、越南、缅甸、印度、印尼五个华侨归国观光团全体团员。
    出席作陪的有：华侨事务委员会副主任委员廖承志、李铁民、庄希泉，委员司徒美堂、费振东、赵令德、连贯、许敬诚、彭泽民、邵力子、王纪元、洪丝丝，办公厅副主任卢心远、彭光涵、陈曼云等人。
    出席宴会的还有：我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明，我国驻印尼总领事赵仲时及我国驻印度总领事柳雨峰等。
    宴会开始前，朝鲜、越南、缅甸、印度、印尼五个华侨归国观光团联合向毛主席、朱总司令、中国人民志愿军及何香凝主任委员献旗，由何香凝主任委员受旗。
    献旗后，印尼华侨归国观光团团长洪载德代表五个华侨归国观光团献词。
    他对祖国的强大，表示万分兴奋。他说，每个华侨，都感到无限的光荣与骄傲，随着祖国的强大，华侨的爱国团结，也日趋扩大与巩固。
    何香凝主任委员致答词，她对各华侨归国观光团的回国观光，表示十分欢迎。她指出祖国今年已开始进行有计划的大规模经济建设，祖国的前途，光芒万丈。
    宴会上，宾主频频为毛主席的健康干杯。
