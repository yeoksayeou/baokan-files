Title: Hoashi Kei Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-10-10
Page: 1

    Hoashi Kei Arrives in Beijing
   [Xinhua News Agency, 9th] Hoashi Kei, a member of the delegation of the "Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade," arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 9th. Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, was present to greet him. Nakamura Koichi, Deputy Head of the delegation of the "Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade," and others also came to greet him.

### 帆足计到达北京

1953-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    帆足计到达北京
   【新华社九日讯】日本“国会议员促进日中贸易联盟”代表团团员帆足计于九日晚到达北京。中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎曾前往迎接。日本“国会议员促进日中贸易联盟”代表团副团长中村高一等也前往迎接。
