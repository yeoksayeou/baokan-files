Title: General Notice of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to All Subordinate Organizations: Celebrating the 36th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1953-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: The General Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association recently issued a notice to all its subordinate organizations regarding the celebration of the 36th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. The notice first pointed out: This year's celebration of the October Revolution should focus on publicizing that the great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union is the most powerful guarantee for defending world peace; publicizing the Soviet Union's great, comprehensive, long-term, and selfless assistance to China's economic construction; mobilizing the broad masses to respond to Chairman Mao's call to learn from the Soviet Union's experience in socialist construction and to learn from the Soviet Union's advanced science and technology to build our country, thereby further strengthening Sino-Soviet friendship and promoting the movement to learn from the Soviet Union, in coordination with the current nationwide campaign to increase production and practice economy in our country, and in coordination with the overall task of gradually realizing socialist industrialization and socialist transformation in our country.

The notice stipulates that the specific content of the publicity for celebrating the October Revolution is: 1. Publicize the Soviet government's and the Soviet people's consistent and enormous assistance to the Chinese people, focusing on the Soviet government's promise to help China build and renovate 141 large-scale enterprises. This great, long-term, and selfless assistance is of extremely important significance for laying the foundation for China's industrialization and modernization of national defense, and for strengthening the power of the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union. 2. Publicize the Soviet Union's experience in socialist labor emulation and increasing production and practicing economy, publicize the history of the Soviet people's arduous struggle in economic construction, and in conjunction with the Chinese people's revolutionary tradition of arduous struggle, mobilize the people to give play to the spirit of perseverance in overcoming difficulties, launch the movement to increase production and practice economy, so as to increase production, practice strict economy, and strive to complete and overfulfill the national plan. 3. Publicize the importance of learning from the Soviet Union for China's construction cause; publicize the implementation of Chairman Mao's instructions on learning the theories of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, learning the Soviet Union's experience in socialist construction, and learning the Soviet Union's advanced science and technology to build our country; publicize the conscientious implementation of the suggestions of Soviet experts, humbly and conscientiously learning from Soviet experts, in order to do a good job in production, do a good job in capital construction, and cultivate construction cadres in a planned way. 4. Ethnic minority areas can, according to specific circumstances, publicize how the various nationalities of the Soviet Union are united in the Soviet family and how they work together to develop the economy and culture of all nationalities.

Regarding the methods of celebrating the October Revolution, the notice also made some specific provisions: Central municipalities, cities where provincial people's governments are located, cities with Soviet friends, and industrial and mining enterprises with Soviet experts should hold representative celebration meetings or celebration evenings on or around November 7th. Other small and medium-sized cities, organs, groups, and schools can hold various report meetings or symposiums according to specific circumstances. Industrial and mining enterprises can hold report meetings or symposiums on learning from Soviet advanced experience in connection with the movement to increase production and practice economy and the implementation of Soviet experts' suggestions. Rural areas can focus on holding appropriate gatherings in state-owned farms and agricultural production cooperatives. The Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture and the General Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will hold "Soviet Film Week" in 20 large and medium-sized cities across the country, and cinemas in other areas that do not hold "Soviet Film Week" should show Soviet films as much as possible. During the celebration of the October Revolution, propaganda vehicles and film teams of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in various places should be dispatched in full force, and film teams and slide projector teams of other systems should also be dispatched to small towns and rural areas as much as possible to show Soviet films and slides. Large and medium-sized cities will hold large and medium-sized photo exhibitions and window displays. Various factories, mines, enterprises, cultural centers, and clubs should hold poster exhibitions as much as possible, and rural areas may hold photo or poster traveling exhibitions as appropriate.

### 中苏友好协会总会通知所属各级组织  庆祝十月社会主义革命卅六周年

1953-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会总会通知所属各级组织
    庆祝十月社会主义革命卅六周年
    【新华社二十三日讯】中苏友好协会总会最近向其所属各级组织发出关于庆祝十月社会主义革命节三十六周年的办法的通知。通知首先指出：今年庆祝十月革命节，应着重宣传中苏两国人民的伟大友谊是保卫世界和平的最有力的保证，宣传苏联对于我国经济建设的伟大的、全面的、长期的、无私的援助，动员广大群众响应毛主席关于学习苏联社会主义建设的经验、学习苏联先进的科学技术来建设我们国家的号召，借以进一步加强中苏友好，推动学习苏联的运动，以配合我国当前在全国范围内开展的增产节约运动，配合我国逐步实现社会主义工业化和社会主义改造的总任务。
    通知规定庆祝十月革命节的具体宣传内容是：一、宣传苏联政府和苏联人民一贯对中国人民的巨大援助，着重宣传苏联政府答应帮助我国新建与改建一百四十一项规模巨大的企业，此种伟大的长期的无私的援助，对于奠定我国工业化和国防现代化的基础，壮大以苏联为首的和平民主阵营的力量，具有极其重大的作用。二、宣传苏联进行社会主义劳动竞赛和增产节约的经验，宣传苏联人民在经济建设中艰苦奋斗的历史，并结合我国人民艰苦奋斗的革命传统，动员人民发挥克服困难的坚毅精神，开展增产节约运动，以增加生产，厉行节约，争取完成和超额完成国家计划。三、宣传学习苏联对于我国建设事业的重要意义；宣传贯彻执行毛主席关于学习马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的学说，学习苏联社会主义建设的经验，学习苏联的先进科学和技术来建设我们国家的指示；宣传认真执行苏联专家的建议，虚心和认真地向苏联专家学习，以搞好生产，搞好基本建设，有计划地培养建设干部。四、各少数民族地区根据具体情况，可宣传苏联各民族如何团结在苏维埃大家庭中，如何为发展各民族的经济文化而共同努力。
    关于庆祝十月革命节的办法，通知也作了若干具体规定：中央直辖市、省人民政府所在城市、有苏联友人的城市以及有苏联专家的工矿企业，均在十一月七日或前后举行有代表性的庆祝会或开庆祝晚会。其他中小城市、机关、团体、学校可根据具体情况举行各种报告会或座谈会。工矿企业结合增产节约运动和贯彻执行苏联专家建议的情况，可举行学习苏联先进经验的报告会或座谈会。农村可有重点地在国营农场、农业生产合作社举行适当的集会。文化部电影局与中苏友好协会总会在全国二十个大、中城市举办“苏联电影周”，其他不举办“苏联电影周”的各地影院尽可能放映苏联影片。在庆祝十月革命节期间，各地中苏友好协会的宣传车、电影队全部出动，其他系统的电影队、幻灯队亦应尽可能出动到小城镇和农村，放映苏联影片和幻灯。各大、中城市举行大、中型的图片展览会及橱窗展览，各工矿、企业、文化馆、俱乐部尽可能举办挂图展览，农村可酌情举办图片或挂图巡回展览。
