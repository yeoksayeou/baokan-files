Author:
Date: 1953-10-11

    Our Great Motherland
    A power plant under construction in Xinjiang is progressing rapidly due to the guidance of Soviet experts and the support of installation workers from Northeast China. This is the scene of the plant's director (first from the right), Soviet experts (second from the right), and Chinese engineers studying the plant's installation project on-site.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在新疆建设的一座发电厂，由于苏联专家的指导和东北安装工人的支援，工程进展迅速。这是该厂厂长（右第一人）、苏联专家（右第二人）和我国工程师在现场研究全厂安装工程的情形。
