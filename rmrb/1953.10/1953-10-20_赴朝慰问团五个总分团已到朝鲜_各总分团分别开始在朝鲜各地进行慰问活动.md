Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th. The Second, Third, Fifth, Sixth, and Eighth General Sub-Delegations of the Third Chinese People's 慰问團 Delegation to Korea have arrived in Korea successively and have begun 慰问 activities in various parts of Korea.
    The Second General Sub-Delegation of the Third Chinese People's 慰问團 Delegation to Korea arrived in Sariwon, Hwanghae Province, Korea, on the afternoon of the 16th to 慰问 the Korean military and civilians and various units of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in Hwanghae Province. People from all walks of life in Hwanghae Province and Sariwon City, as well as representatives from a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, held a grand welcome meeting at the station. Kim Yeol, Chairman of the Hwanghae Provincial Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and a representative of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delivered welcoming speeches at the meeting. Then, Zhao Shoushan, head of the Second General Sub-Delegation, delivered a reply. He said: "We, the Chinese people, are determined to work with the Korean people to redouble our efforts to correctly implement the terms of the Korean Armistice Agreement and peacefully resolve the Korean issue, and will strive to support the Korean people in their reconstruction work." Finally, the Chairman of the Hwanghae Provincial People's Committee, on behalf of the people of Hwanghae Province, presented a banner to the Second General Sub-Delegation. The banner was embroidered with the words: "Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship and unity between the Korean and Chinese people!"
    The Third, Fifth, and Sixth General Sub-Delegations of the Third Chinese People's 慰问團 Delegation to Korea arrived in Wonsan, Gangwon Province, Korea, on the 12th and 16th respectively, to 慰问 the Korean military and civilians and various units of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the east coast of Korea. When these three General Sub-Delegations arrived in Wonsan, they were warmly welcomed by the heads of the Party and government organs, heads of social organizations, and people from all walks of life in Gangwon Province and Wonsan City. On the 14th, the art troupe of the Sixth General Sub-Delegation performed a 慰问 performance at a welcome meeting held by people from all walks of life in Wonsan City. On the 15th, the Sixth General Sub-Delegation began to 慰问 personnel from various factories, enterprises, and organs, as well as farmers in the suburbs, in four groups.
    The Eighth General Sub-Delegation of the Third Chinese People's 慰问團 Delegation to Korea arrived in Anju, South Pyongan Province, Korea, on the 15th to 慰问 Korean railway workers and their families, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army railway troops, and staff. Along the way, they were warmly welcomed by the Korean people and the Korean-Chinese People's troops. Those who welcomed them at the Sinuiju station included: Kim Cheon-hae, one of the chairmen of the Presidium of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front; Kim Chang-jun, Director of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front; responsible persons of the Sinuiju City People's Committee; and local people. Those who welcomed them at Anju station included: Kim Dong-cheol, Vice-Minister of the Korean Ministry of Railways and Director of the Political Bureau; responsible persons of the Anju Railway Administration and the Anju County Party and government organs and social organizations; and representatives from all walks of life in the local area. Also at the station to welcome them were responsible persons of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's Korean front railway troops and commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army railway troops. The welcomers held a grand welcome meeting in the square in front of Anju Station. Kim Dong-cheol, Vice-Minister of the Korean Ministry of Railways and Director of the Political Bureau, delivered a welcoming speech at the meeting. On behalf of all employees of the Korean railway sector, he expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Chinese people and Chairman Mao Zedong, and praised the immortal achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army railway troops on the Korean battlefield. He said that the Korean people must further strengthen friendship and unity with the fraternal Chinese people and strive to defend peace. Ha Feng'a, head of the Eighth General Sub-Delegation, spoke, expressing his warm gratitude to the Korean people for their welcome, and conveying to the Korean people and Korean-Chinese railway workers present the love and concern of all the Chinese people for them. The Eighth General Sub-Delegation has now begun 慰问 activities.

### 赴朝慰问团五个总分团已到朝鲜  各总分团分别开始在朝鲜各地进行慰问活动

1953-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    赴朝慰问团五个总分团已到朝鲜
    各总分团分别开始在朝鲜各地进行慰问活动
    【新华社平壤十九日电】中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团第二、第三、第五、第六、第八共五个总分团已先后到达朝鲜，并分别开始在朝鲜各地进行慰问。
    中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团第二总分团在十六日下午到达朝鲜黄海道沙里院，慰问黄海道朝鲜军民及中国人民志愿军各部队。黄海道和沙里院市各界人民和中国人民志愿军某部代表在车站上举行了隆重的欢迎会。朝鲜劳动党黄海道委员会委员长金烈和中国人民志愿军某部的代表在会上致欢迎词。接着由第二总分团团长赵寿山致答词。他说：“我们中国人民坚决和朝鲜人民一道，为正确实行朝鲜停战协定条款与和平解决朝鲜问题而加倍努力，并将努力支援朝鲜人民进行恢复建设工作。”最后由黄海道人民委员会委员长代表黄海道人民将锦旗一面赠给第二总分团。锦旗上绣着：“朝鲜人民和中国人民永恒不灭的友谊团结万岁！”
    中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团第三总分团、第五总分团和第六总分团分别在十二日和十六日到达朝鲜江原道元山市，慰问朝鲜东海岸的朝鲜军民和中国人民志愿军各部队。这三个总分团在到达元山时，都受到了江原道和元山市党政机关的首长、社会团体负责人和各界人民的热烈欢迎。十四日第六总分团的文艺工作团在元山市各界人民的欢迎会上作了慰问演出，第六总分团在十五日开始分四批慰问元山市各工厂、企业和各机关的人员以及郊区农民。
    中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团第八总分团在十五日到达朝鲜平安南道安州，慰问朝鲜铁路工作人员及其家属、中国人民志愿军铁道部队和工作人员。沿途他们受到了朝鲜人民和朝中人民部队的热烈欢迎。在新义州车站欢迎他们的有：朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线议长团议长之一金天海、朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会书记局局长金昌俊，新义州市人民委员会负责人及当地人民群众等。在安州车站欢迎的有：朝鲜铁道省副相兼政治局局长金东哲、安州铁道管理局和安州郡党政机关、社会团体的负责人以及当地各界人民代表等。到车站欢迎的还有中国人民志愿军朝鲜前线铁道部队负责人和志愿军铁道部队的指挥员、战斗员们。欢迎者在安州车站前的广场上，举行了一个盛大的欢迎会。朝鲜铁道省副相兼政治局局长金东哲在会上致欢迎词。他代表朝鲜铁道部门全体员工，向中国人民和毛泽东主席表示衷心的感激，并赞扬了中国人民志愿军铁道部队在朝鲜战场上所建立的不朽功勋。他说：朝鲜人民一定要和兄弟般的中国人民进一步加强友谊团结，为保卫和平而奋斗。第八总分团团长哈丰阿讲话，他对朝鲜人民的欢迎表示热烈感谢，并向到会的朝鲜人民和朝中铁路工作人员转达了全中国人民对于他们的热爱和关怀之情。第八总分团现已开始进行慰问。
