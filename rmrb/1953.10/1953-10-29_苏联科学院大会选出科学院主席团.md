Title: USSR Academy of Sciences General Assembly Elects Presidium

Author:

Date: 1953-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 27th: According to TASS, the General Assembly of the USSR Academy of Sciences held its closing session in Moscow on the 26th.

	The General Assembly elected the Presidium of the USSR Academy of Sciences. Academician A. Nesmeyanov, President of the Academy of Sciences, and Academician A. Topchiev, Academic Secretary-General of the Presidium of the Academy of Sciences, were not re-elected as their terms had not expired. I. Bardin was re-elected as Vice-President of the Academy of Sciences, and K. Ostrovityanov, an authority on political economics, was elected as Vice-President.

	The Presidium of the Academy of Sciences is composed of the following academicians: I. Kurchatov, V. Nemchinov, T. Lysenko, I. Petrovsky, and the secretaries of the Academy's departments, M. Keldysh (Department of Physical and Mathematical Sciences), M. Dubinin (Department of Chemical Sciences), A. Oparin (Department of Biological Sciences), D. Shcherbakov (Department of Geological and Geographical Sciences), S. Khristianovich (Department of Technical Sciences), G. Alexandrov (Department of Economics, Philosophy, and Law), M. Tikhomirov (Department of History), and V. Vinogradov (Department of Literature and Language).

### 苏联科学院大会选出科学院主席团

1953-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院大会选出科学院主席团
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联科学院大会于二十六日在莫斯科举行了闭幕会议。
    大会选举了苏联科学院主席团。科学院院长亚·涅斯米扬诺夫和科学院主席团学术秘书长亚·托普切夫的任期未满，没有进行改选。伊·巴尔金再度当选为科学院副院长，政治经济学权威康·奥斯特罗维季扬诺夫当选为副院长。
    科学院主席团由下列各院士组成：伊·库尔查托夫，瓦·涅姆钦诺夫，特·李森科，伊·彼得罗夫斯基，以及科学院各部的秘书麦·克尔迪什（物理数学部），米·杜比宁（化学部），亚·奥巴林（生物科学部），德·谢尔巴科夫（地质和地理科学部），谢·赫里斯佳诺维奇（技术科学部），格·亚历山德罗夫（经济学、哲学和法学部），米·季霍米罗夫（历史学部），维·维诺格拉多夫（文学和语言学部）。
