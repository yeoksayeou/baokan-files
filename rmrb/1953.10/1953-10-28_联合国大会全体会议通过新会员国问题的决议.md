Title: UN General Assembly Passes Resolution on the Question of New Member States
Author:
Date: 1953-10-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 27th dispatch from TASS: The United Nations General Assembly held a plenary meeting on the 23rd. The meeting unanimously adopted the draft resolution of the Special Political Committee on the question of new member states. The resolution stipulates the establishment of a conciliation committee to study whether an agreement can be reached on the issue of admitting new member states to the United Nations. The conciliation committee shall submit its report to the eighth session of the General Assembly or, at the latest, to the ninth session.

The plenary meeting also adopted a resolution on expanding the political rights of women in regions where women do not fully enjoy political rights, as well as several draft resolutions of other working committees, and elected fifteen members of the International Law Commission.

### 联合国大会全体会议通过新会员国问题的决议

1953-10-28
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国大会全体会议通过新会员国问题的决议
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社纽约讯：联合国大会在二十三日举行全体会议。会议一致通过了特别政治委员会关于新会员国问题的决议草案。决议规定设立一个斡旋委员会来研究能否在准许新会员国加入联合国问题上达成协议。斡旋委员会须向第八届大会或至迟向第九届大会提出它的工作报告。
    全体会议并通过了在妇女没有充分享受到政治权利地区扩大妇女政治权利的决议，以及其他工作委员会的若干项决议草案，选出了国际法委员会的十五个委员。
