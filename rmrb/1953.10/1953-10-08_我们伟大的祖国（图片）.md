    Our Great Motherland
    Our People's Navy is rapidly growing stronger. The students of naval academies have achieved remarkable results in studying advanced Soviet military science and excellent technology. Students from the school participated in the National Day celebrations in the capital. This is their neat and orderly marching formation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国人民海军正在迅速壮大。海军学校的学员们学习苏联先进军事科学和优良技术有显著的成就。该校学员曾参加首都国庆节庆祝典礼。这是他们的分列式的严整的阵容。
