Title: U.S. Aircraft Intrude Again into Our Territory's Airspace Twice

Author:

Date: 1953-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 9th: The number of U.S. side's aerial violations of the armistice agreement has reached 65, and the total number of recorded aerial and ground violations of the armistice agreement has reached 80. At the meeting of secretaries of the Military Armistice Commission on the 9th, Colonel Zhu Ran, secretary of the Korean-Chinese side, raised two incidents of U.S. aerial violations of the armistice agreement and demanded that the U.S. side pay attention.
    The details of the two incidents are as follows: At approximately 4:50 PM on September 27th, a U.S. military jet aircraft intruded from the southwest into the Ongjin Peninsula on the west coast, and after intruding over Ongjin and Haeju, flew south. At approximately 9:20 AM on October 3rd, a U.S. military aircraft flew across the military demarcation line and intruded over Heights 938.2 and 586.1 southeast of Kimhwa, then flew southeast back to the U.S. side's area.

### 美机又两次侵入我方地区上空

1953-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    美机又两次侵入我方地区上空
   【新华社开城九日电】美方空中违反停战协定事件已达六十五次，全部空中和地面违反停战协定事件的记录共达八十次。在军事停战委员会秘书长九日会议上，朝中方面秘书长朱然上校提出美方的两次空中违反停战协定事件，要求美方注意。
    两次事件经过如下：九月二十七日下午四时五十分左右，美方军用喷气式飞机一架，由西南方侵入西海岸的瓮津半岛，侵入瓮津和海州上空后向南方飞去。十月三日上午九时二十分左右，美方军用飞机一架飞越军事分界线，侵入金城东南的九三八点二高地和五八六点一高地上空，然后向东南飞回美方地区。
