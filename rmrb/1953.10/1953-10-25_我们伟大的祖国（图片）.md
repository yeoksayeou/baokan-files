Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-10-25
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    With a great spirit of internationalism, the Chinese People's Volunteers are helping the Korean people heal the wounds of war and rebuild a peaceful and happy life. This little Korean girl is giving an apple to a Volunteer soldier participating in the reconstruction of Pyongyang, expressing her heartfelt love for her Volunteer uncle.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军正以伟大的国际主义精神，帮助朝鲜人民医治战争的创伤，重建和平幸福的生活。这位朝鲜小姑娘正把一个苹果送给参加重建平壤的一位志愿军战士，表达她对志愿军叔叔的衷心热爱。
