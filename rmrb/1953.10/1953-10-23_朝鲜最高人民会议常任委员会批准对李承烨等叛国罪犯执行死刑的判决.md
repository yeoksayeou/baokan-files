Title: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea Approves Death Sentences for Traitorous Criminals Such as Li Cheng-yeop

Author:
Date: 1953-10-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 22nd: According to the Korean Central News Agency, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea reviewed and discussed the Supreme Court's verdict on the Li Cheng-yeop traitorous gang and passed a resolution on the 14th, approving the death sentences for ten criminals: Li Cheng-yeop, Zhao Yiming, Lin He, Park Seung-won, Lee Kang-guk, Bae Cheol, Baek Hyung-bok, Cho Yong-bok, Meng Jong-ho, and Seol Jeong-sik.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会批准对李承烨等叛国罪犯执行死刑的判决

1953-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会批准对李承烨等叛国罪犯执行死刑的判决
    【新华社平壤二十二日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社报道：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会审议和讨论了最高裁判所对于李承烨叛国匪帮的判决，在十四日通过决议，批准对李承烨、赵一明、林和、朴胜源、李康国、裴哲、白亨福、赵镛福、孟钟镐、薛贞植十名罪犯执行死刑。
