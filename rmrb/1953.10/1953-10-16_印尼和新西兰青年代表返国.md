Title: Indonesian and New Zealand Youth Representatives Return Home
Author:
Date: 1953-10-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 15th. The Indonesian and New Zealand youth representatives who were invited to visit our country and participate in the National Day celebrations, as well as those who were passing through our country on their way back from the 4th World Youth and Students Peace and Friendship Festival, left Guangzhou this morning on the 15th to return to their countries. Representatives from the China New Democratic Youth League South China Working Committee and other units were at the station to see them off. Correction: In the "Decision of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council on Revising the Leadership Relations of Institutions of Higher Education" published on page one of this newspaper on October 11, "Promulgated on October 6, 1953" should be changed to "Promulgated on October 11, 1953."

### 印尼和新西兰青年代表返国

1953-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼和新西兰青年代表返国
    【新华社广州十五日电】应邀来我国访问并参加国庆节观礼的以及路经我国返国的出席第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的印度尼西亚和新西兰的青年代表，在十五日晨离广州回国。到车站欢送的有中国新民主主义青年团华南工作委员会等单位的代表。更正十月十一日本报第一版刊载的“中央人民政府政务院关于修订高等学校领导关系的决定”中，“一九五三年十月六日公布”改为“一九五三年十月十一日公布”。
