Title: Picture
Author: Qian Sijie
Date: 1953-10-31
Page: 1

    The picture shows the representative of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Ki Sok-bok (second from the left), speaking at the first meeting of the bilateral talks on the political conference issue. First from the left is Huang Hua, the representative of the People's Republic of China; third from the right on the opposite side is Arthur Dean, the representative of the United States.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie
