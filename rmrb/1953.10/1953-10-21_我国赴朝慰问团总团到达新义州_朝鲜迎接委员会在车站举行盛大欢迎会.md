Title: Our Country's Delegation to North Korea Arrives in Sinuiju; Korean Reception Committee Holds Grand Welcome Rally at the Station
Author:
Date: 1953-10-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Sinuiju, October 20th: The Chinese People's Third Delegation to North Korea, headed by General He Long, along with all members and staff of the First General Sub-Delegation, totaling over one thousand people, arrived in Sinuiju, Korea, at 5:30 PM and 7:00 PM on October 20th via special train.

Those who went to Sinuiju Station to welcome the delegation included Kim Cheon-hae, Chairman of the Reception Committee specially formed by the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic United Front for the Fatherland to welcome and receive the Chinese People's Third Delegation to North Korea, as well as committee members Kim Min-san, Jeong Seong-eon, Choi Jong-hak, Han Seol-ya, Kim Chang-jun, Kim Yeong-sam, and Park Deok-hwan, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison of the Ministry of Culture and Propaganda of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Also present at the station to welcome the delegation were Heo Bin, Chairman of the Korean Workers' Party's North Pyongan Province Committee, representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations, as well as representatives of workers, farmers, soldiers, women, youth, and students from the Sinuiju area.

The national flags of Korea, China, and the Soviet Union, as well as portraits of Marshal Kim Il-sung, Chairman Mao Zedong, and Chairman Malenkov, were hung at the Sinuiju Station.

When the special trains carrying the General Delegation and the First General Sub-Delegation arrived at Sinuiju Station, they were warmly welcomed by representatives from all walks of life in Korea, led by Kim Cheon-hae, Chairman of the Reception Committee.

General He Long, the head of the General Delegation, and Xing Xiping, the deputy head of the General Delegation and head of the First General Sub-Delegation, as well as Zhang Bojun, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Naiqi, Zhu Xuefan, Chen Yi, Wu Han, Liu Zhiming, Kang Keqing, Mei Lanfang, Lao She, the deputy heads of the General Delegation, and members of the delegation, received flowers from members of the Korean Young Pioneer Corps as they disembarked and shook hands warmly with the welcomers.

A grand welcome rally was held at Sinuiju Station. At the rally, Kim Seon-cheol, Vice Chairman of the Sinuiju People's Committee, delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the Korean people and all the people of Sinuiju, expressing warm welcome and gratitude to the Chinese people and all members of the Chinese People's Third Delegation to North Korea headed by General He Long.

Subsequently, General He Long, the head of the General Delegation, delivered a speech at the welcome rally, warmly congratulating the Korean military and civilians for their great victory in defeating the American imperialist aggression and defending the freedom and independence of their motherland. He also conveyed the determination of the entire Chinese people to actively support the Korean people in their peaceful construction.

After a brief rest in Sinuiju, the General Delegation and the First General Sub-Delegation, accompanied by members of the Reception Committee welcoming the delegation, left Sinuiju by special train for Pyongyang, the democratic capital of Korea.

[Another Dispatch] The Chinese People's Third Delegation to North Korea left Shenyang by special train at 7:00 AM on the 20th and passed through Andong at 4:10 PM on the same day en route to Korea.

When the delegation left Shenyang, those who went to the station to see them off included: Lin Feng, First Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; He Jinnian, Deputy Commander of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Wen Jianping, Secretary-General of the Northeast Branch of the China Anti-American and Aid Korea Association; Jiao Ruoyu, Mayor of the Shenyang People's Government; Zhu Weiren, Chairman of the Shenyang Branch of the China Anti-American and Aid Korea Association, as well as the heads of the Northeast and Shenyang organizations of various democratic parties, the heads of various people's organizations in the Northeast and Shenyang, and more than 800 soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, workers, youth, students, and Young Pioneers. Also present at the station to see them off was Seo Eung-deok, Director of the Shenyang Liaison Office of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in our country.

All members of the First General Sub-Delegation of the Chinese People's Third Delegation to North Korea also left Andong for North Korea at the same time as the General Delegation.

### 我国赴朝慰问团总团到达新义州  朝鲜迎接委员会在车站举行盛大欢迎会

1953-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我国赴朝慰问团总团到达新义州
    朝鲜迎接委员会在车站举行盛大欢迎会
    【新华社新义州二十日电】以贺龙将军为首的中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团及第一总分团全体团员及工作人员一千余人，已于十月二十日下午五时三十分及下午七时先后乘专车到达朝鲜新义州市。
    前往新义州车站欢迎的有朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会为欢迎和招待中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团而特别组成的迎接委员会委员长金天海和委员金民山、郑圣彦、崔宗学、韩雪野、金昌俊、金映三及朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府文化宣传省对外文化联络局局长朴德焕等。前往车站欢迎的还有朝鲜劳动党平安北道委员会委员长许彬和各民主党派、人民团体人士及新义州地区的工人、农民、军人、妇女、青年、学生代表等多人。
    新义州车站上，悬挂着朝、中、苏三国的国旗和金日成元帅、毛泽东主席、马林科夫主席的画像。
    慰问团总团和第一总分团专车到达新义州车站时，受到以迎接委员会委员长金天海为首的朝鲜各界代表的热烈欢迎。
    慰问团总团团长贺龙将军和总团副团长兼第一总分团团长邢西萍，总团副团长章伯钧、蔡廷锴、章乃器、朱学范、陈沂、吴晗、刘芝明、康克清、梅兰芳、老舍及慰问团团员们下车时，接受了朝鲜少年团团员们的献花，和欢迎者亲切地握手。
    在新义州车站，举行了盛大的欢迎大会。在欢迎大会上，新义州市人民委员会副委员长金铣哲致欢迎辞，他代表朝鲜人民和新义州市全体人民向中国人民和以贺龙将军为首的中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团全体团员表示热烈的欢迎和感谢。
    接着，慰问团总团团长贺龙将军在欢迎大会上发表演说，他热烈地祝贺朝鲜军民击败美帝国主义侵略、保卫自己祖国自由独立的伟大胜利，并转达了全中国人民积极支援朝鲜人民进行和平建设的决心。
    慰问团总团及第一总分团全体人员在新义州略事休息后，即在欢迎慰问团的迎接委员会委员们的陪同下，乘专车离开新义州，前往朝鲜民主首都平壤。
    【又讯】中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团，在二十日上午七时，乘专车离开沈阳，并于当天下午四时十分过安东前往朝鲜。
    赴朝慰问团总团离开沈阳时，前往车站欢送的有：中共中央东北局第一副书记林枫，中国人民解放军东北军区副司令员贺晋年，东北抗美援朝总分会秘书长温建平；沈阳市人民政府市长焦若愚，沈阳市抗美援朝分会主席朱维仁，以及各民主党派东北和沈阳市组织的负责人，东北及沈阳市各人民团体负责人和中国人民解放军战士、工人、青年学生、少年先锋队员等八百多人。前往车站欢送的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆沈阳联络所所长徐应惪。
    中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团第一总分团全体人员，亦与总团同时离安东赴朝。
