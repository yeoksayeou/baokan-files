Title: U.S. Aircraft Again Intrudes into Korean-Chinese Side Area
Author:
Date: 1953-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 5th: At the meeting of the Secretaries of both sides of the Military Armistice Commission on the 5th, Zhu Ran, Colonel and Secretary of the Korean-Chinese side, raised the issue of another aerial violation of the armistice agreement by the U.S. side on October 1st and requested the U.S. side to pay attention. Including this incident, the number of aerial violations of the armistice agreement by the U.S. side has reached sixty-one. The total number of aerial and ground violations of the armistice agreement has reached seventy-six.

The details of this aerial violation of the armistice agreement by the U.S. side are as follows: At approximately 2:50 PM on October 1st, four U.S. fighter planes crossed the military demarcation line and intruded into the airspace over Sanjuli, southwest of Kimhwa, circling over the area for two minutes before flying back to the U.S. side.

### 美机又一次侵入朝中方面地区

1953-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    美机又一次侵入朝中方面地区
    【新华社开城五日电】在五日的军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议上，朝中方面秘书长朱然上校提出十月一日美方又发生一起空中违反停战协定的事件，要求美方注意。连同这次事件在内，美方的空中违反停战协定事件已达六十一次，全部空中和地面违反停战协定事件的次数共达七十六次。
    这次美方空中违反停战协定事件的经过如下：十月一日下午二时五十分左右，美方作战飞机四架飞越军事分界线，侵入金城西南的三巨里上空，在该地上空盘旋两分钟后飞回美方地区。
