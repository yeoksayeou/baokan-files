Title: The Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission Sends Another Letter to the U.S. Side, Demanding That the U.S. Side Repair the Interpretation Equipment Before October 14

Author: 
Date: 1953-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, October 10th: The Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission today once again demanded that the U.S. side, if it refuses to complete the construction of the interpretation equipment by October 14th as proposed by the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, should allow the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers to construct such equipment.

    In a letter to Hamblen today, Lieutenant General Thimayya, Chairman of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, said, "The Commission is greatly disappointed by your letter stating that the second interpretation site (Editor's note: i.e., permanent equipment) will be completed 'as soon as possible.' The Commission believes it is entitled to know the exact date on which all equipment will be handed over to it."
    He also requested Hamblen to confirm the verbal notification made by the U.S. side that the road from Dongchang-ri to the northern half of the Demilitarized Zone, which the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission recommended to be built, "can be used by the Commission on October 11th."
    Thimayya said, "The Commission now awaits your final reply at 9:00 a.m. on Sunday, October 11th."

### 中立国遣返委员会再度致函美方  要求美方在十月十四日以前修好解释设备

1953-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中立国遣返委员会再度致函美方
    要求美方在十月十四日以前修好解释设备
    【新华社开城十日电】    中立国遣返委员会今天再度要求美方：如果美方拒绝在中立国遣返委员会提出的在十月十四日以前完成修建解释设备的工程，就让朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军来修建这种设备。
    中立国遣返委员会主席蒂迈雅中将在今天给汉布伦的信中说：“你来信说第二解释场所（编者按：即永久性设备）将‘尽快地’予以完成，使得委员会甚为失望。委员会认为它有权知道全部设备交给它处理的确切日期。”
    他还要求汉布伦证实美方提出的口头通知，即：中立国遣返委员会建议修筑的东场里到非军事区北半部的道路“可在十月十一日供本委员会使用”。
    蒂迈雅说：“本委员会现在等待你在星期日，十月十一日，九时提出最后答复。”
