Title: Tunisian President Leads by Example in Blood Donation
Author:
Date: 1989-05-25
Edition: 3

Tunisian President Leads by Example in Blood Donation

According to the Tunisian African News Agency, on National Blood Donation Day, Tunisian President Ben Ali donated blood on May 25 at the Carthage Palace in Tunis.

The report stated that President Ben Ali's action is aimed at further encouraging citizens to participate in the noble humanitarian act of voluntary blood donation.

Ben Ali is 52 years old and has been serving as the President of Tunisia since November 7, 1987.

### 突尼斯总统带头献血

1989-05-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　突尼斯总统带头献血
    据突尼斯非洲通讯社报道，在全国献血日之际，突尼斯总统本·阿里２５日在突尼斯的迦太基宫献血。
    报道说，本·阿里总统带头献血旨在进一步鼓励公民参加无偿献血这一高尚的人道主义行动。
    本·阿里今年５２岁，１９８７年１１月７日担任突尼斯总统。
      　　　（据新华社）
