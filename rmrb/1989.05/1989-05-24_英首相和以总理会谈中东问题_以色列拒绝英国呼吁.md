Title: British Prime Minister and Israeli Prime Minister Discuss Middle East Issues
Author: Chen Te'an
Date: 1989-05-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London, May 23rd.

British Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher pointed out after her meeting with Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Shamir yesterday evening that there are still obvious differences between Britain and Israel on the issue of how to achieve peace in the Middle East. Britain urged Israel not to reject negotiations with the PLO.

Xinhua News Agency, London, May 23rd.

Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Shamir on May 23rd rejected Britain's call for Israel to hold talks with the Palestine Liberation Organization to peacefully resolve the Middle East issue.

Shamir said in a speech at a banquet hosted by British Jewish leaders on the evening of the 23rd, "It is very strange that civilized and democratic governments welcome these primary terrorists and ask us to consider negotiating with them and allow them to participate in the peace process."

Shamir claimed at a press conference in London on the 23rd, "The PLO has no interest in making peace with us; they just want to establish a Palestinian state in this region immediately."

### 英首相和以总理会谈中东问题  以色列拒绝英国呼吁
陈特安
1989-05-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　英首相和以总理会谈中东问题
    以色列拒绝英国呼吁
    本报伦敦５月２３日电  记者陈特安报道：英国首相撒切尔夫人昨晚在与以色列总理沙米尔会谈后指出，在如何赢得中东和平问题上，英国和以色列仍然存在明显的分歧。英国敦促以色列不要拒绝同巴解谈判。
    据新华社伦敦５月２３日电　以色列总理沙米尔２３日拒绝了英国要以色列同巴勒斯坦解放组织举行会谈，和平解决中东问题的呼吁。
    沙米尔２３日晚上在英国犹太人领袖举行的一个宴会上发表讲话时说，“文明和民主的政府欢迎这些首要的恐怖主义分子，并要求我们考虑同他们谈判，允许他们参与和平进程，我们对此感到很奇怪。”
    沙米尔２３日在伦敦举行的一次记者招待会上宣称，“巴解组织对于同我们搞和平毫无兴趣，他们只是想在这一地区立即建立一个巴勒斯坦国。”
