Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1989-05-14
Edition: 2

	▲ On the afternoon of May 14, Song Ping, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, met with a delegation from the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, led by Central Committee member Prodan Stoyanov, at the Great Hall of the People. The Bulgarian guests arrived in Beijing on May 13 at the invitation of the Chinese side.

	▲ On May 14, General Der Huotian, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army of China, met and hosted a banquet for General Edwin Peredo Camacho, Commander-in-Chief of the Bolivian Armed Forces, and his delegation at the Great Hall of the People. Peredo arrived in Beijing in the afternoon of the same day at the invitation of the Ministry of National Defense, beginning his official friendly visit to China.

	▲ At the invitation of the Moroccan Independence Party, a delegation from the Chinese Communist Party, led by Li Lian, Secretary-General of the Central Advisory Commission, departed Beijing in the evening of May 14 to attend the 12th National Congress of the Independence Party, which will be held from May 19 to 21.

	▲ On the evening of May 13, the China National Technical Import and Export Corporation and the French company Dechaineaux signed a supply contract at the Great Hall of the People. According to the contract, the French company will provide a production line for the Jinxi Fertilizer Project in Liaoning, using natural gas as raw material, with an annual output of 300,000 tons of synthetic ammonia and 520,000 tons of urea. The contract uses a mixed loan from the French government.

	State Councilor Zhu Jiahu, Minister of the Chemical Industry Qin Zhongda, and French Ambassador to China Marc, among others, attended the signing ceremony.

### 简讯

1989-05-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　简讯
    ▲中共中央政治局委员宋平１４日下午在人民大会堂会见了由中央委员普罗丹·斯托扬诺夫率领的保加利亚共产党中央休假团。保加利亚客人应邀于１３日到达北京。
    ▲中国人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田１４日在人民大会堂会见并宴请玻利维亚三军总司令埃德文·佩雷多·卡马乔上将一行。佩雷多应国防部邀请于当日下午抵京，开始对中国进行正式友好访问。
    ▲应摩洛哥独立党邀请，以中央顾问委员会秘书长李力安为团长的中国共产党代表团１４日晚离京赴摩参加于本月１９日至２１日举行的独立党第十二次全国代表大会。
    ▲中国技术进出口总公司与法国德希尼布公司１３日晚在人民大会堂签订了一项供货合同。根据合同，法国公司将向辽宁锦西大化肥项目提供一套以天然气为原料，年产３０万吨合成氨、５２万吨尿素的生产装置。合同使用法国政府混合贷款。
    国务委员邹家华、化工部部长秦仲达和法国驻华大使马乐等出席了签字仪式。
