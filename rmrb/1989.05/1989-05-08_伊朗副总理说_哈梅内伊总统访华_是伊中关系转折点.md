Title: Iranian Vice Premier Says President Khamenei's Visit to China Marks a Turning Point in Sino-Iranian Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-05-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, May 6th.

Iranian Vice Premier Hamid Mirzadeh said on May 6th that Iranian President Ali Khamenei's upcoming visit to China will mark a turning point in Sino-Iranian relations.

Mirzadeh predicted that this visit will promote economic cooperation between the two countries and said that Iran is prepared to use Chinese technology in its post-war reconstruction.

Khamenei will be the first Iranian president to visit China since the Iranian Islamic Revolution.

### 伊朗副总理说  哈梅内伊总统访华  是伊中关系转折点

1989-05-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　伊朗副总理说    
    哈梅内伊总统访华    
    是伊中关系转折点
    新华社德黑兰５月６日电  伊朗副总理哈米德·米尔·扎德６日对新闻界说，伊朗总统阿里·哈梅内伊即将对中国进行的访问将是伊中关系的一个转折点。
    扎德副总理预言，这次访问将促进两国间的经济合作，并说伊朗准备在其战后重建中使用中国的技术。
    哈梅内伊总统将是伊朗伊斯兰革命以来第一位访问中国的伊朗总统。
