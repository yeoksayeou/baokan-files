Title: Li Keran Donates $100,000 to the China Arts Festival Foundation
Author: Qian Zhongming
Date: 1989-05-12
Edition: 4

Chinese master of traditional painting Li Keran recently donated $100,000 to the China Arts Festival Foundation. This morning, the Ministry of Culture held a solemn and simple donation ceremony at the "Jiushen Hall" to commend Li Keran's valuable spirit of supporting the development of China's art industry.

Mr. Li said in his speech of thanks: "Due to historical reasons, Chinese culture and arts have not yet been widely understood by people around the world. It is like a pearl covered in dust, not yet shining with its due brilliance. I hope that through the continuous efforts of the Arts Festival, we can develop various forms of art and gradually introduce them to the world, making this Eastern pearl shine brightly."

(Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 12)

### 李可染向中国艺术节基金会捐款１０万美元
千仲明
1989-05-12
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　李可染向中国艺术节基金会捐款１０万美元
    本报北京５月１２日讯  我国国画艺术大师李可染先生，近日向中国艺术节基金会捐赠１０万美元。今天上午，文化部在“孑民堂”举行了庄重朴素的捐赠仪式，以表彰李可染先生支持我国艺术事业发展的可贵精神。
    李先生在答谢辞中说：“中国的文化艺术由于历史的原因，至今还没有被世界人们广泛的理解。它还似一颗蒙尘的明珠，没有发出应有的光芒。我想通过艺术节连续不断地把我们各种艺术发展起来，逐渐走向世界，使这颗东方明珠发出灿烂的光芒。”
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（千仲明）
