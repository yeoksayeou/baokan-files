Title: New Orthopedic Brace Put into Use in Beijing
Author: Feng Junjun
Date: 1989-05-15
Edition: 2

A 14-year-old boy with a 40-degree spinal curvature has seen his spine corrected to 10 degrees after several months of treatment with a new type of orthopedic brace. The child has regained most of his daily living and learning abilities.

This new orthopedic brace is a technology introduced from North America. It has been jointly put into use in Beijing by Peking Union Medical College Hospital and Beijing Hospital No. 514.

### 新型矫形支具在京投入使用
冯军军
1989-05-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　新型矫形支具在京投入使用
    本报讯  记者冯军军报道：一位１４岁的少年脊柱侧弯４０度，应用一种新型矫形支具治疗数月，脊柱弯度纠正为１０度。孩子重新恢复了大部分生活学习功能。
    这种新型矫形支具是由北美引进的一项新技术。已由北京协和医院与北京五一四医院协作，在北京投入使用。
