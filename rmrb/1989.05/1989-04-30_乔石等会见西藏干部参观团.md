Title: Joa Chishu and Others Meet with Tibet Officials Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-04-30
Edition: 2

Joa Chishu and other leading comrades of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee held a cordial meeting with the entire delegation of Tibet officials in the Huairen Hall of Zhongnanhai this morning. They also took a group photo with the delegation.

Tian Jiyun, Yan Mingfu, Xi Zhongxun, Apu Awang Jinmei, Liao Hansheng, Yang Jingren, and Sima Yimaiti, along with responsible comrades from the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, attended the meeting and the group photo. Joa Chishu, on behalf of the CPC Central Committee, extended a warm welcome to the delegation and asked them to convey the greetings of the Central Committee to all the cadres and people of various ethnic groups in Tibet. He hoped that through the visit, experiences could be exchanged, national unity could be enhanced, and in combination with the actual situation in Tibet, all the work in Tibet could be carried out in a solid and practical manner.

### 乔石等会见西藏干部参观团

1989-04-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　乔石等会见西藏干部参观团
    据新华社北京４月３０日电  中共中央政治局常委乔石等领导同志，今天上午在中南海怀仁堂亲切会见了西藏干部参观团全体同志，并同大家合影留念。
    田纪云、阎明复、习仲勋、阿沛·阿旺晋美、廖汉生、杨静仁、司马义·艾买提以及中共中央统战部、国家民委的负责人，参加了会见和合影。乔石代表党中央向参观团的同志表示欢迎并请他们转达中央对西藏广大各族干部和群众的问候。他希望通过参观，交流经验，增进民族团结，结合西藏的实际情况，扎扎实实地把西藏的各项事业搞上去。
