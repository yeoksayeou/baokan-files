Title: The Village Bookseller
Author:
Date: 1989-05-02
Edition: 1

In Shanxi Province's Yuanping County, a young man named Li Tiancai from Xiazhuang Village became an individual bookseller after failing the college entrance examination. For six years, he has been traveling from village to village, spreading spiritual civilization and agricultural scientific knowledge, and has been warmly welcomed by the masses.
