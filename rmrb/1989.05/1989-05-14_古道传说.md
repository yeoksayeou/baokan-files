Title: Legends of the Ancient Path
Author: Zhang Zuying 画, Hongying 诗
Date: 1989-05-14
Edition: 8

	The sky is right before my eyes
	The door is right before my eyes
	Everything that appeared in my dreams is right before my eyes
	As long as the sky and the sky become one
	As long as my heart is still beating
	What is there to fear in being left alone in the wilderness?
	In the night, I breathe quietly
	Like owning the air, like owning time
	Knocking on the ancient door to open it for the new century
