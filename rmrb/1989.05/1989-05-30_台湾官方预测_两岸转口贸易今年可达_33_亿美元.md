Title: Taiwan Officials Predict Two-Side Transshipment Trade to Reach $3.3 Billion This Year
Author:
Date: 1989-05-30
Edition: 2

Taipei officials from the "Economic Construction Committee" of the Executive Yuan stated yesterday that cross-strait transshipment trade is growing rapidly. It is estimated that this year's trade volume will reach $3.3 billion, an increase of over 20% compared to last year's $2.7 billion.

The Taiwan-based China Economic Research Institute noted that recently, the mainland's exports to Taiwan, particularly in 26 categories of textiles, indicate that the mainland has improved its textile processing capabilities. Meanwhile, the products Taiwan exports to the mainland show the largest growth in the machinery category. This suggests that future cross-strait transshipment trade will shift from primary processed goods to precision products.

### 台湾官方预测  两岸转口贸易今年可达33亿美元

1989-05-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    台湾官方预测
    两岸转口贸易今年可达33亿美元
    新华社香港5月30日电  台北消息：台湾“行政院经济建设委员会”有关官员昨天表示，海峡两岸转口贸易增长越来越快，预计今年贸易总额将可达33亿美元，比去年的27亿美元增长二成以上。
    台湾中华经济研究院表示，近来大陆对台输出的纺织26类商品显示大陆已提高了纺织品精加工能力，同时，台湾输往大陆的商品中也以机械类增长最大，可见，未来两岸转口贸易的商品将由初级加工品向精密产品转变。
