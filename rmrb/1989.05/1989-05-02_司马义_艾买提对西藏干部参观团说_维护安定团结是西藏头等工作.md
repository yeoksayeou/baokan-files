Title: Sima Yi·Aimaiti on the Tibet Delegation: Maintaining Stability and Unity is Tibet's Top Priority
Author:
Date: 1989-05-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 2nd.

Sima Yi·Aimaiti, the head of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, today emphasized that maintaining national unity, strengthening ethnic unity, enhancing the cohesion of all ethnic groups, and consolidating and developing a stable and united political situation are important tasks in ethnic work at present.

The Central United Front Work Department and the State Ethnic Affairs Commission held a meeting this afternoon to discuss Tibet's ethnic work with the entire Tibet delegation. Sima Yi·Aimaiti said at the meeting that continuing to develop the stable and united situation in Tibet, maintaining national unity, and strengthening great ethnic unity are the overriding tasks in Tibet's work at present.

Tibet's cadres must, on the one hand, resolutely fight against any actions that split the country, and on the other hand, focus on improving Tibet's economic development and enhancing ethnic education.

Vice Chairman of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, Wu Jinghua, and Vice Minister of the Central United Front Work Department, Zhang Shengzuo, also spoke at the meeting.

### 司马义·艾买提对西藏干部参观团说  维护安定团结是西藏头等工作
格来
1989-05-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　司马义·艾买提对西藏干部参观团说    
    维护安定团结是西藏头等工作
    新华社北京５月２日电（记者格来）国家民委主任司马义·艾买提今天强调指出，维护祖国统一，加强民族团结，增强各民族的凝聚力，巩固、发展安定团结的政治局面，是当前民族工作的重要任务。
    中共中央统战部、国家民委今天下午在这里邀请西藏干部参观团全体成员就西藏民族工作进行了座谈。司马义·艾买提在座谈会上说，继续发展西藏安定团结的局势，维护祖国统一，加强民族大团结，是当前西藏工作压倒一切的任务。
    西藏的干部一方面要同分裂祖国的行为作坚决的斗争，一方面把西藏的经济工作搞上去，把民族教育搞上去。
    国家民委副主任伍精华、中央统战部副部长张声作等也在会上讲了话。
