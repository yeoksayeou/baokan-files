Title: Farmer Doctor Jiao Ronghua Donates a Giant Ginseng
Author: Zhang Yunde, Li Jiacheng
Date: 1989-05-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, May 27th.

Farmer doctor Jiao Ronghua donates a giant ginseng.

The giant ginseng, measuring 36 centimeters in height and weighing 4.75 kilograms, resembles a statue of a Luohan.

Jiao Ronghua purchased this ginseng for 70 yuan from a farmer in August of last year. According to a team led by Professor Hong Jinqi of the Shaanxi Provincial Academy of Traditional Chinese Medicine, this ginseng has been growing underground for at least 700 years. It is "a rare treasure in medical history" and "the largest ginseng ever discovered in the world."

### 农民医生乔荣华  献出特大何首乌
张运德　李家成
1989-05-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　农民医生乔荣华
    献出特大何首乌
    新华社西安５月２７日电  （通讯员张运德、李家成）陕西省汉阴县农民医生乔荣华将一特大何首乌献给国家。
    这颗何首乌高３６厘米，重４．７５公斤，酷似一尊罗汉。
    乔荣华是去年８月间以７０元价格从一农民手中买回这颗何首乌的。据以洪敬群教授为首的省中医学院考察组鉴定，这颗何首乌在地下至少生长了７００年，“属医药史上罕见的珍品”，“是目前世界上发现的最大的一棵。”
