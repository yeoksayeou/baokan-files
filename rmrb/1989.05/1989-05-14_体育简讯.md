Title: Sports News
Author: Bāo Hán Xiǎo Yǒu
Date: 1989-05-14
Edition: 4

Sports News

	▲ To commemorate Liu Changchun, the first Chinese athlete to participate in the Olympic Games and a renowned sprinter, Dalian University of Technology erected a half-body bronze statue of the late Professor Liu Changchun on the occasion of its 40th anniversary. The unveiling ceremony was held on May 12.

		(Bāo Hán)

	▲ On the afternoon of May 13, editors and reporters from 12 news organizations in the capital participated in the first "Seagull—Gordon Cup" billiards tournament at Beijing's Zhongshan Park. Liu Zhankun from the China Youth Daily emerged victorious with an undefeated record, securing the first place in the 16-ball event.

		(Xiǎo Yǒu)

### 体育简讯
包涵　晓友
1989-05-14
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　体育简讯
    ▲为纪念首次参加奥林匹克运动会的第一个中国运动员、短跑宿将刘长春，大连理工学院在建校４０周年之际，为已去世的刘长春教授建造了一座半身青铜塑像。塑像揭幕仪式５月１２日举行。
　　　　　　　（包涵）
    ▲５月１３日下午，首都１２家新闻单位的编辑记者在北京中山公园参加了首届海燕——高登杯台球赛，中国青年报刘占坤以不败战绩获得１６彩球第一名。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（晓友）
