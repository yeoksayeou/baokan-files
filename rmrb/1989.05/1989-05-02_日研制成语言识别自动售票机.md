Title: Japan Develops Language Recognition Automatic Ticket Vending Machine
Author:
Date: 1989-05-02
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London

According to a report by the *Financial Times*, Toshiba Electronics of Japan is developing a language recognition automatic ticket vending machine.

The prototype of the language recognition automatic ticket vending machine has completed a field test at a train station in Osaka. The machine can recognize 26 station names. Passengers need only insert a magnetic stripe payment card into the vending machine and say the name of the station they wish to go to, and the machine will automatically dispense the ticket, deducting the fare from the payment card.

Toshiba's design team, after studying the vocal characteristics of 1,000 people, has programmed the machine to recognize spoken station names. The main advantage of this automatic ticket vending machine is that it can eliminate the need for a keyboard and screen.

### 日研制成语言识别自动售票机

1989-05-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　日研制成语言识别自动售票机
    新华社伦敦电  据《金融时报》报道，日本东芝电子公司正在研制语言识别自动售票机。
    语言识别自动售票机原型已在大阪的一个火车站完成了实地试验。自动售票机能够识别２６个站名。旅客只要把磁纹付款卡插入售票机，说出自己要去的站名，售票机就把车票自动送出，车费从付款卡上扣除。
    东芝公司的设计人员在研究了１０００人的声音特征后，给自动售票机编制出口语站名的程序。这种自动售票机的主要优点是，能够省掉键盘和屏幕。
