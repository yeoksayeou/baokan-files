Title: The Asian Cup Table Tennis Tournament Group Stage Concludes
Author: Wáng Jùn Pō
Date: 1989-05-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 14th.

The first round of the 7th Asian Cup Table Tennis Tournament concluded here tonight.

The notable feature of the men's singles group round-robin matches was the fierce challenge posed by newcomers to the veterans. Two newcomers from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea faced off against veterans and played at a high level. Kim Kun-chol lost 1-2 to Chen Longcan of China, while Kim Myong-jun defeated Chen Zhibin of China 2-1.

The winners of each men's group are: Chen Longcan (China), Kim Jae-seok (South Korea), Kim Myong-jun (North Korea), Saito Kiyoshi (Japan), An Jae-hyeong (South Korea), Ma Wenge (China), Kim Jin-myong (North Korea), Yu Shentong (China). The runners-up in each group are: Kim Kun-chol (North Korea), Shibata Hiroshi (Japan), Chen Zhibin (China), Xie Wentang (Taiwan), Feng Shengqin (Taiwan), Moon Kye-hyun (South Korea), Matsushita Koiji (Japan), Kang Hee-jung (South Korea).

Qiao Hong (China), Cheung Mei Wa (Hong Kong), Chan Tan Lei (Hong Kong), Yu Sun-bok (South Korea) won the first place in each women's group; Lee Jung-im (South Korea), Ding Yaping (China), Uchiyama Kyoko (Japan), Sato Rika (Japan) finished second in their respective groups.

The top two finishers in each group will advance to the knockout stage tomorrow.

### 亚洲杯乒乓球赛小组赛结束  各路强手今日进入淘汰赛
王俊璞
1989-05-14
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　亚洲杯乒乓球赛小组赛结束    
    各路强手今日进入淘汰赛
    新华社北京５月１４日电（记者王俊璞）第七届亚洲杯乒乓球赛第一阶段比赛今晚在这里结束。
    男子单打小组循环赛的显著特点是新手向老将发起猛烈挑战。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国两名新手遇到老将都打出了高水平，金根哲以１∶２负于中国陈龙灿，金明俊以２∶１战胜了中国的陈志斌。
    获得男子各组第一名的是：陈龙灿（中国）、金泽洙（南朝鲜）、金明俊（朝鲜）、斋藤清（日本）、安宰亨（南朝鲜）、马文革（中国）、金振明（朝鲜）、于沈潼（中国）。列小组第二的是：金根哲（朝鲜）、涩谷浩（日本）、陈志斌（中国）、谢文堂（中国台北）、冯圣钦（中国台北）、文奎玟（南朝鲜）、松下浩二（日本）、姜熙璨（南朝鲜）。
    乔红（中国）、齐宝华（香港）、陈丹蕾（香港）、余顺福（南朝鲜）获女子各小组第一名；李正任（南朝鲜）、丁亚萍（中国）、内山京子（日本）、佐藤利香（日本）列小组第二。
    获小组前两名的选手明天开始进行淘汰赛。
