Title: Comrade Lai Yi's Funeral Held

Author:

Date: 1989-05-08

Edition: 4

The funeral for Comrade Lai Yi, former Deputy Political Commissar of the Nanjing Military Region, was held on May 5 at the Huashan Hotel in Nanjing. Approximately 500 friends and comrades from the Nanjing Military Region and local units paid their last respects.

Comrade Lai Yi passed away on April 26 in Nanjing at the age of 86.

Leaders such as Zhao Ziyang, Deng Xiaoping, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, Li Xiannian, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Qiao Shi, Hu Qili, Yao Yilin, Qin Jiwei, Wang Ping, Li Desheng, Yang Dezhi, Yu Quli, Song Shilun, Zhang Jinfu, Zhang Aiping, Chen Pihua, Peng Chong, Wei Guoqing, Ye Fei, Liao Hansheng, Fang Yi, Kang Keqing, Wang Enmao, Hong Xuezhi, and Liu Huaqing, as well as the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central Military Commission, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Ministry of National Defense, the three main departments of the People's Liberation Army, the Military Commission Discipline Inspection Commission, the Nanjing Military Region, and the provincial and municipal leadership of Hunan, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Anhui, Fujian, Jiangxi, Shanghai, and Lai Yi's hometown, sent wreaths.

In 1926, Lai Yi joined the Communist Youth League of China in his hometown of Pingjiang County, Hunan. The following year, he participated in the famous Autumn Harvest Uprising and, under the leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong, took the oath to join the Communist Party of China. He then followed Mao Zedong to Jinggangshan. He served in various positions, including Deputy Officer of the Red Fourth Army Third Column, Political Director of the Directly Affiliated Unit of the Red Fourth Front Army, Deputy Political Director of the Political Department of the New Fourth Army Sixth Column, Deputy Political Director of the Political Department of the New Fourth Army Fourth Division and Political Commissar of the Twelfth Brigade, Political Commissar of the Health Department of the East China Military Region, Deputy Political Commissar and Political Director of the Political Department of the Su North Military Region, First Deputy Secretary of the Su North Area Party Committee, and Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Nanjing Military Region. During the revolutionary war years, he participated in the battles of Huangyan Jie, the first to fifth counter-encirclement campaigns in the Central Soviet Area, the Long March, the winter counter-sweep in Huabei, the Huabei and Su Nan campaigns, and the Yangtze River crossing. He led from the front, fought bravely, commanded decisively, and effectively led and organized ideological and political work, making an indelible contribution to the liberation of the Chinese people.

After the founding of New China, he contributed to the strengthening of the army. During the ten years of turmoil, he fought resolutely against the Lin Biao and the "Gang of Four" counter-revolutionary clique. He maintained a high degree of unity with the Party Central Committee and firmly supported the line, principles, and policies since the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee. He was a representative of the Fourth National People's Congress, a member of the Third and Fourth National Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and a standing member of the Fifth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. In 1955, he was awarded the rank of Major General.

Comrade Lai Yi was a loyal and tested communist warrior, an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, a revolutionary of the proletariat, and an excellent leader of political work in our army. He had a firm communist ideal and belief, was devoted to the revolutionary cause, had a strong sense of responsibility for the revolutionary cause, completed tasks resolutely, had a strong sense of party discipline and organizational discipline, was far-sighted, humble, approachable, democratic in style, united with comrades, and connected with the masses. He was upright, fair, sought truth from facts, strict with himself, set an example, was consistent in words and deeds, lived a simple life, and was strict with his family and children.

### 赖毅同志遗体告别仪式举行

1989-05-08
第4版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　赖毅同志遗体告别仪式举行
    新华社南京５月８日电  向南京军区原副政治委员赖毅同志遗体告别的仪式５月５日在南京华山饭店礼堂举行。赖毅同志在宁生前友好及驻宁部队干部、战士约５００人向他作最后诀别。
    赖毅同志因病于４月２６日在南京逝世，享年８６岁。
    赵紫阳、邓小平、杨尚昆、李鹏、李先念、徐向前、聂荣臻、乔石、胡启立、姚依林、秦基伟、王平、李德生、杨得志、余秋里、宋时轮、张劲夫、张爱萍、陈丕显、彭冲、韦国清、叶飞、廖汉生、方毅、康克清、王恩茂、洪学智、刘华清等领导同志，中共中央、中央军委、全国政协、国防部、解放军三总部、军委纪委、南京军区、湖南、江苏、浙江、安徽、福建、江西、上海以及赖毅同志原籍县领导机关送了花圈。
    赖毅１９２６年在家乡湖南平江县加入中国共产主义青年团，次年参加了著名的秋收起义并在毛泽东同志主持下宣誓加入中国共产党，随后跟随毛泽东上井冈山。他历任红四军第三纵队司令部副官、红四方面军直属队政治处主任、新四军第六支队政治部副主任、新四军第四师政治部副主任兼第十二旅政治委员、华东军区卫生部政治委员、苏北军区副政治委员兼政治部主任、苏北区党委第一副书记、南京军区政治部副主任等职。革命战争年代他先后参加了黄洋界保卫战，中央苏区第一至五次反“围剿”，二万五千里长征，淮北冬季反“扫荡”；涡北、宿南战役和渡江作战。他身先士卒，作战勇敢，指挥果断，并卓有成效地领导组织开展思想政治工作，为中国人民的解放事业作出了不可磨灭的贡献。
    新中国成立后，他为加强部队建设作出了贡献。１０年动乱中，他同林彪、“四人帮”反革命集团进行了坚决的斗争。他自觉与党中央保持高度一致，坚决拥护十一届三中全会以来的路线、方针、政策。他是第四届全国人大代表，第三、四届全国政协委员，第五届全国政协常委。１９５５年，他被授予中将军衔。
    赖毅同志是久经考验的忠诚的共产主义战士，中国共产党的优秀党员，无产阶级革命家，我军优秀的政治工作领导者。他有坚定的共产主义理想和信念，对革命事业忠心耿耿；他有强烈的革命事业心和高度的责任感，完成任务坚决；他有坚强的党性观念和组织纪律观念，顾全大局，富有远见；他谦虚谨慎，平易近人，作风民主，团结同志，联系群众；他为人正派，办事公道，实事求是；他严于律己，以身作则，言行一致，艰苦朴素，对家属子女要求严格。
