Title: The Domestic Production Rate of "Santana" Exceeds 32%
Author:
Date: 1989-05-30
Edition: 2

Shanghai Volkswagen Automobile Co., Ltd. has overcome difficulties related to funding, raw materials, and energy shortages, achieving new progress in the domestic production of the Shanghai "Santana" sedan. By the end of April, 585 components have been recognized, with a cumulative domestic production rate of 32.49% and an assembly savings rate of 14%. Since April, 35 items have been sent to Volkswagen for sample approval.

Currently, the first batch of 750 sets of molded carpets for the "Santana" sedan's trunk, produced by Shanghai Polyester Factory, has been provided to Shanghai Volkswagen.

### “桑塔纳”国产化率逾32％

1989-05-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    “桑塔纳”国产化率逾32％
    本报上海5月30日电  上海大众汽车有限公司克服资金、原材料、能源短缺等困难，取得上海“桑塔纳”轿车国产化工作的新进展。截至4月底，已有585项零部件被认可，累计国产化率达到32.49％，装车节汇率达到14％。4月以来，各厂送大众公司进行工装样品认可的有35项。
    目前，上海丙纶厂生产的“桑塔纳”轿车行李箱三块成型地毯首批750套，已经提供给上海大众公司。
