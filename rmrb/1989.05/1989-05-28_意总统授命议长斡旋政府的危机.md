Title: Italian President Tasks Speaker to Mediate Government Crisis
Author: Luo Jinbiao
Date: 1989-05-28
Edition: 3

Italian President Cossiga yesterday afternoon tasked Senate Speaker Giovanni Spadolini with exploring ways to resolve the government crisis, paving the way for the formation of a new cabinet.

After accepting the mission, Spadolini issued a statement saying that Italy must face the 1992 European Single Market with a sound economic and financial situation and an effective political system. Therefore, temporary solutions cannot be adopted, and a stable and effective government must be organized. Spadolini will begin consultations with various parliamentary parties next Wednesday to find a way to resolve the government crisis.

Italian Prime Minister De Mita submitted his resignation on May 19, plunging the Italian government into a crisis.

### 意总统授命议长斡旋政府的危机
罗晋标
1989-05-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　意总统授命议长
    斡旋政府的危机
    本报罗马５月２７日电  记者罗晋标报道：意大利总统科西加昨天下午授命参议院议长乔瓦尼·斯帕多利尼试探解决政府危机的办法，以便为组织新内阁铺平道路。
    斯帕多利尼接受试探使命后发表声明表示，必须使意大利以良好的经济财政状况和有效的政治体制来迎接１９９２年欧洲统一大市场，为此，不能采取临时解决办法，而需要组织一个稳定而有效的政府。斯帕多利尼将于下星期三开始与议会各政党磋商，寻找解决政府危机的办法。
    意大利政府总理德米塔于５月１９日提出辞职而使意大利政府陷入危机。
