Title: Iranian President Khamenei to Arrive in Beijing for Visit
Author:
Date: 1989-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency

**Person Introduction**

Iranian President Khamenei

At the invitation of Chairman Yang Shangkun of the People's Republic of China, President Sayyid Ali Khamenei of the Islamic Republic of Iran will pay an official friendly visit to China from May 9 to 14.

Khamenei was born in 1939 in Mashhad, a holy city in eastern Iran, into a religious family. He began receiving specialized training in theology during his youth. He has been awarded the religious title of Hojatoleslam, which is second only to Ayatollah. Subsequently, under the leadership of Khomeini, he participated in political activities against the Shah. After 1964, he was arrested and imprisoned multiple times. After the establishment of the Islamic Republic of Iran in 1979, he successively held positions such as member of the Revolutionary Council, Deputy Minister of Defense, Commander of the Revolutionary Guards, Imam of Tehran, Member of the Islamic Parliament, and Representative of Khomeini on the Supreme Defense Council. In October 1981, he assumed the position of the third President of the Islamic Republic of Iran and was re-elected in 1985. He currently also serves as Chairman of the Supreme Defense Council and Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Cultural Revolution.

President Khamenei attaches great importance to the development of friendly cooperative relations between China and Iran, believing that there are broad areas and great potential for cooperation between the two countries, and expressing the need to make greater efforts for this purpose.

### 伊朗总统哈梅内伊  今日抵京访问我国

1989-05-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：人物介绍

　　伊朗总统哈梅内伊    
    今日抵京访问我国
    应中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆的邀请，伊朗伊斯兰共和国总统赛义德·阿里·哈梅内伊将于５月９日至１４日对中国进行正式友好访问。
    哈梅内伊总统１９３９年生于伊朗东部圣城马什哈德的一个宗教世家，青少年时期开始受神学方面的专门训练。曾获得过仅次于阿亚图拉的宗教称号霍贾特伊斯兰。此后，他在霍梅尼的领导下参与反对巴列维国王的政治活动。１９６４年后多次被捕入狱。１９７９年伊朗伊斯兰共和国成立后，先后任革命委员会成员、国防部副部长、革命卫队司令、德黑兰教长、伊斯兰议会议员和霍梅尼在最高国防委员会代表等职。１９８１年１０月出任伊朗伊斯兰共和国第三任总统，１９８５年连任至今。现兼任最高国防委员会主席和文化革命最高委员会主席。
    哈梅内伊总统重视发展中伊两国友好合作关系，认为两国的友好合作有着广泛的领域和很大的潜力，表示有必要为此作出更大的努力。
