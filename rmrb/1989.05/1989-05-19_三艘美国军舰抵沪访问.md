Title: Three U.S. Navy Ships Arrive in Shanghai for a Visit
Author: Chen Maodi
Date: 1989-05-19
Edition: 2

Three U.S. Navy ships, led by the commander of the U.S. Seventh Fleet, Admiral Mooz, arrived in Shanghai this afternoon for a four-day friendly and ceremonial visit.

Admiral Nie Kuiqu of the East Sea Fleet of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and other officials welcomed Mooz and the U.S. naval officers at the Waigongqiao Pier and held a welcoming ceremony.

In the evening, Nie Kuiqu hosted a banquet to welcome the U.S. naval officers. During the banquet, Nie Kuiqu and Mooz made toasts, expressing their shared wish that the visit by the U.S. Navy ships would further promote friendly relations between China and the United States, as well as between the two militaries.

### 三艘美国军舰抵沪访问
陈毛弟
1989-05-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　三艘美国军舰抵沪访问
    新华社上海５月１９日电（记者陈毛弟）三艘美国军舰在美国海军第七舰队司令莫兹海军中将率领下，今天下午抵达上海，进行为期四天的友好的礼节性访问。
    中国人民解放军东海舰队司令聂奎聚海军中将等前往外虹桥码头迎接莫兹等美国海军官兵，并举行了欢迎仪式。
    晚上，聂奎聚举行宴会，欢迎美国海军官兵访沪。聂奎聚和莫兹在宴会上祝酒，共同祝愿通过美国海军军舰的访问，进一步发展中美两国和两军之间的友好关系。
