Title: Coordinate Inter-provincial Procurement Work, Supervise Origin Areas for Price Based on Quality
Author: Zhang Yi
Date: 1989-05-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 11th.

The spring cocoons are gradually coming to market, and joint work groups appointed by the State Council from relevant departments have begun to go to the main cocoon-producing areas for inspections, supervision, and coordination. This is to strengthen the leadership of cocoon procurement work.

The joint work groups are composed of personnel from the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade, the Ministry of Supervision, the State Price Bureau, the State Administration for Industry and Commerce, and the China Silk Import and Export Corporation. They are divided into seven groups and stationed in Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Anhui, Sichuan, Shandong, and other places.

The main tasks of the work groups are to publicize and implement the two notices from the State Council on strengthening the management of silk and silk products, to coordinate inter-provincial procurement work, to supervise the implementation of quality-based pricing in the main producing areas, and to assist local authorities in handling illegal activities that occur during cocoon procurement.

### 协调省际收购工作　督促产地按质论价  国务院工作组分赴蚕茧主产区
张毅
1989-05-11
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　协调省际收购工作　督促产地按质论价    
    国务院工作组分赴蚕茧主产区
    据新华社北京５月１１日电（记者张毅）春茧陆续上市，由国务院指派有关部门组成的联合工作组开始陆续到蚕茧主产区进行检查、督促、协调，以加强对蚕茧收购工作的领导。
    联合工作组由经贸部、监察部、国家物价局、国家工商局和中国丝绸进出口总公司抽调干部组成，分为７个组，分驻江苏、浙江、安徽、四川、山东等地。
    工作组的主要任务是宣传贯彻国务院关于加强茧丝绸经营管理的两个通知，协调省际之间的收购工作，督促各主产地按质论价，协助地方处理在蚕茧收购中出现的违法事件。
