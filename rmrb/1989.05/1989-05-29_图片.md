Title: Young Farmer's Innovative Farming
Author: 
Date: 1989-05-29
Edition: 5

Young farmer Bai Yongliang from Liu Shu Tun Village in Cha Fang District, Zhangjiakou City, employs an interplanting cultivation method to produce greenhouse vegetables. Within a year, he manages to plant and harvest five different crops, achieving an average income of 15,000 yuan per acre. Look! While he is pulling up radishes, the cucumbers are already laden with fruit.
