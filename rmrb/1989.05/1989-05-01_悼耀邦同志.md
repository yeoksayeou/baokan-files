Title: In Memory of Comrade Yao Bang
Author: Chagan
Date: 1989-05-01
Edition: 8

In memory of Comrade Yao Bang
Mongolian   Chagan

A pine nut falls
Heavily hitting my forehead
I look up at the sky in terror
A star falls

I shudder
A dream wakes me up like this
The announcer's voice is bitter
The atmosphere darkens

That hand
Warmly waving its five fingers
Drops down

Life is lonely from here on
Poetry is lonely from here on

I walk into the quiet forest
Treading on the floating leaves
Looking for something
But return empty-handed

May 15, 1989, evening
Beijing, Anwai Xingye Studio

### 悼耀邦同志
查干
1989-05-01
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　悼耀邦同志
    蒙古族  查干
    一粒松籽掉落下来
    沉沉打在额头
    惊恐地仰望天空时
    一颗星在殒落
    我不寒而栗
    梦　这样醒了
    播音员的声音很苦涩
    氛围此时暗然下来
    那分散开五指
    亲切挥动的手
    垂下去了
    生活至此寂寞
    诗　至此寂寞
    我走入幽静的林子
    踏开浮叶
    想寻找什么
    却　空手而归
    　　　　　　　　　一九八九年四月十五日晚
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　北京安外星野斋
