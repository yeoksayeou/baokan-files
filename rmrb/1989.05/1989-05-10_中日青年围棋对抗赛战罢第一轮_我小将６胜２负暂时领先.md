Title: The First Round of the 1989 China-Japan Youth Go Competition Concludes
Author: Jiang Hong
Date: 1989-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 10th.

The first round of the 1989 China-Japan Youth Go Competition began today at the China-Japan Go Institute in Beijing. In the first eight matches, the Chinese team secured 6 wins and 2 losses, taking a temporary lead.

The competition is part of a series of events commemorating the 10th anniversary of the signing of the China-Japan Treaty of Peace and Friendship. The Chinese team consists of 8 young players, all under the age of 20, including 6 males and 2 females. The Japanese team, also consisting of 8 players, includes 7 males and 1 female.

The competition will continue with the second round tomorrow. The Chinese team will strive to maintain their lead and aim for an overall victory.

专栏：体育

　　中日青年围棋对抗赛战罢第一轮    
    我小将６胜２负暂时领先
    据新华社北京５月１０日电（记者江红）１９８９年中日青年围棋对抗赛今天在北京中日围棋会馆拉开战幕。在首轮８盘比赛中，中国队６胜２负，暂时领先。
