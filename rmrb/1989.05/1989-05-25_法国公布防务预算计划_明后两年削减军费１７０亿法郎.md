Title: France Announces Defense Budget Plan: Military Spending to be Cut by 17 Billion Francs Over Next Two Years
Author: Lü Wǎng Dà
Date: 1989-05-25
Edition: 3

France has announced its defense budget plan, revealing that military spending for the next two years will be 210 billion francs, a reduction of 17 billion francs from the original plan, with an approximate growth rate of 4%.

The Élysée Palace issued a statement today, affirming that while ensuring economic health, France will provide the necessary military expenditures and reaffirm its independent and reliable defense policy.

This plan will be submitted to the cabinet meeting for review and approval on June 7.

### 法国公布防务预算计划  明后两年削减军费１７０亿法郎
吕网大
1989-05-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　法国公布防务预算计划
    明后两年削减军费１７０亿法郎
    本报巴黎５月２４日电  记者吕网大报道：法国明后两年军费开支为２１００亿法郎，比计划减少１７０亿法郎，增长率约４％。
    爱丽舍宫今天为此发表的公告中说，法国在保证经济健康发展的同时，给予必要的军事支出，并重申其独立和可靠的防务政策。
    这项计划将于６月７日提交内阁会议审查通过。
