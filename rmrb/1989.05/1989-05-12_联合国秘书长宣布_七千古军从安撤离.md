Title: UN Secretary-General Announces 7,000 Cuban Troops Withdraw from Angola
Author: Liú Kāi Chén
Date: 1989-05-12
Edition: 3

UN Secretary-General Announces 7,000 Cuban Troops Withdraw from Angola

	People's Daily, United Nations, May 11 - Reporter Liu Kai Chen reports: UN Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar said today that, according to UN verification, from the beginning of this year to now, more than 7,000 Cuban troops have withdrawn from Angola and returned home.

	De Cuéllar reported to the Security Council today on the activities of the UN Angola Verification Mission. He praised the mission for effectively carrying out the tasks assigned by the Security Council and also commended the good cooperation from the governments of Angola and Cuba.

	The mission was established by the Security Council in December last year. It was dispatched to Angola at the beginning of this year and deployed to relevant airports and ports to begin verifying the phased withdrawal of Cuban troops and equipment from Angola.

	The mission consists of 70 military observers from 10 countries and 35 civilian personnel from other countries, led by Brazilian Brigadier General Ferreira Gomes.

	According to the agreement reached by Angola, Cuba, and South Africa in December last year, all Cuban troops will withdraw from Angola starting from April 1 of this year within 27 months.

### 联合国秘书长宣布  七千古军从安撤离
刘开宸
1989-05-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　联合国秘书长宣布    
    七千古军从安撤离
    本报联合国５月１１日电  记者刘开宸报道：联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天说，经联合国核查，从今年初到现在，已有７０００多古巴军队撤离安哥拉回国。
    德奎利亚尔今天向安理会报告了联合国安哥拉核查团的活动情况，他赞扬核查团有效地执行了安理会交给的任务，也赞扬安哥拉和古巴政府的很好的合作。
    核查团是根据安理会决定在去年１２月建立的。今年初派到安哥拉，部署到有关机场和港口，开始核查古巴军队和装备分阶段撤出安哥拉的情况。
    核查团由来自１０国的７０名军事观察员和其它国家的３５名文职人员组成，由巴西的准将费雷拉·戈麦斯率领。
    根据安哥拉、古巴、南非去年１２月达成的协议，全部古巴军队从今年４月１日起在２７个月内撤离安哥拉。
