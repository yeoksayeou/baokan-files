Title: Prince Norodom Sihanouk Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-05-17
Edition: 2

Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, and his wife arrived in Beijing this evening from Bangkok.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Liu Shucai and others welcomed them at the capital airport.

### 西哈努克亲王抵京

1989-05-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　西哈努克亲王抵京
    新华社北京５月１７日电  民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人今晚由曼谷抵达北京。
    中国外交部副部长刘述卿等到首都机场迎接。
