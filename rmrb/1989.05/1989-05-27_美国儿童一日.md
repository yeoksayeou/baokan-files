Title: A Day in the Life of American Children
Author: Feng Yu Zhi
Date: 1989-05-27
Page: 8

According to recent materials published by the American Committee for the Protection of Children, in the United States, every day:

* 72 infants under one month old die;
* 110 infants under one year old die;
* 9 children are shot to death;
* 5 adolescents commit suicide;
* 2,753 teenage girls become pregnant;
* 1,099 teenage girls have abortions;
* 1,287 teenage girls give birth to illegitimate children;
* 988 children are abused;
* 3,288 children run away from home;
* 49,322 children are in reformatories;
* 2,269 babies are born out of wedlock;
* 2,989 children become victims of their parents' divorce.

### 美国儿童一日
冯玉芝
1989-05-27
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

　　美国儿童一日
    据美国保卫儿童基金会最近公布的材料，美国一天中有：
    ７２名未满月的婴儿夭折；
    １１０名未满周岁婴儿死亡；
    ９名儿童被枪杀；
    ５名少年自杀；
    ２７５３名少女怀孕；
    １０９９名少女打胎；
    １２８７名少女产下私生子；
    ９８８名儿童受到虐待；
    ３２８８名儿童离家出走；
    ４９３２２名儿童在感化院；
    ２２６９名婴儿非婚出生；
    ２９８９名儿童成为父母离异的牺牲品。
    　　　　（５月５日《青年参考》冯玉芝译）
