Title: Roller Skate Bicycle Sport
Author:
Date: 1989-05-12
Edition: 4

This is a new sport that has emerged in California, USA. People ride these bicycles and enjoy the thrill of high-speed sliding. Roller skate bicycles are slightly smaller than ordinary bicycles and do not have pedals. Riders can lie prone on the bicycle and freely slide down various slopes, with speeds reaching over 100 kilometers per hour.

		Xinhua News Agency

### 滑行自行车运动（图片）

1989-05-12
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育周末

    滑行自行车运动
    这是在美国加利福尼亚新兴的滑行自行车运动。人们骑在这种车上尽情享受着高速滑行的乐趣。滑行自行车比普通自行车稍小，没有脚踏传动装置。骑车人可俯卧在上，沿各种坡路自由滑下，时速最高可达100公里以上。
                新华社发
