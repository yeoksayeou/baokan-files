Title: World Bank Provides Loan to China
Author:
Date: 1989-05-06
Edition: 3

World Bank Provides Loan to China

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, May 5 - The World Bank announced on May 4 that it has decided to provide China with a credit of 39.4 million Special Drawing Rights (approximately $52 million) through its affiliate, the International Development Association.

This fund will be used to support a five-year plan for constructing a local health service network in China. The implementation areas include the provinces of Zhejiang, Jiangxi, and Shaanxi, with an estimated total cost of $113 million. More than half of this amount will be provided by local governments.

The loan provided by the World Bank for this plan is an interest-free loan with a repayment period of 35 years, including a 10-year grace period.

### 世界银行向我国提供贷款

1989-05-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　世界银行向我国提供贷款
    新华社华盛顿５月５日电  世界银行４日宣布，该行决定通过其所属的国际开发协会向中国提供３９４０万特别提款权（约合５２００万美元）的信贷。
    这笔款项将用于资助中国一项为期５年的地方卫生服务网建设计划。这项计划的实施区包括浙江、江西和陕西三省，预计耗资１．１３亿美元，其中一半以上由地方政府提供。
    世界银行为该计划提供的贷款为无息贷款，偿还期为３５年，其中包括１０年的宽限期。
