Title: India Does Not Invite Israel to Participate in Asian Athletics Championships
Author:
Date: 1989-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency

New Delhi newspapers reported on the 19th that the Indian Amateur Athletics Association will not invite Israel to participate in the 8th Asian Athletics Championships to be held in New Delhi from November 14th to 19th this year.

The Indian Amateur Athletics Association sent invitations on the 12th of this month to 40 of the 41 member associations of the Asian Amateur Athletics Association, inviting them to participate in the Asian Athletics Championships. However, Israel was the only one not invited to participate.

### 印度不邀请以色列参加亚洲田径赛

1989-05-23
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　印度不邀请以色列参加亚洲田径赛
    印度新德里报纸十九日报道，印度业余田径联合会不邀请以色列参加今年十一月十四日至十九日在新德里举行的第八届亚洲田径赛。
    印度业余田联本月十二日向亚洲业余田联所属的四十一个会员协会中的四十个会员协会发出了邀请信，邀请它们参加亚洲田径赛，唯独不邀请以色列参赛。
      　　　　　　　（据新华社）
