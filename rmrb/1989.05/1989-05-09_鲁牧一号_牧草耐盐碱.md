Title: "Lumai No. 1" Grass Resistant to Salt and Alkalinity
Author: Yin Jianhua
Date: 1989-05-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, May 9th.

The Shandong Academy of Agricultural Sciences has developed a type of grass suitable for cultivation in coastal saline-alkaline soils, named "Lumai No. 1."

This type of grass has strong resistance to salt, waterlogging, and low temperatures. Experimental planting at the Laixi County Grassland Experimental Station showed that in heavily alkaline soils where the salt content in the plow layer reaches 0.5% to 0.6%, other crops cannot survive, but "Lumai No. 1" can still grow normally.

"Lumai No. 1" grass resembles wheat in appearance, with a height of 1.5 to 1.6 meters. It can be harvested twice a year, with a yield of about 5,000 kilograms per mu.

### “鲁牧一号”牧草耐盐碱
尹建华
1989-05-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　“鲁牧一号”牧草耐盐碱
    新华社济南五月九日电  （记者尹建华）山东农科院培育出适宜滨海盐碱地种植的牧草——“鲁牧一号”。
    这种牧草耐盐碱、耐渍涝、耐低温的能力强。寿光县牧草实验站种植实验表明，在土壤耕层含盐量高达千分之五至千分之六的重碱地里，其他作物不能生存，它却能照常生长。
    “鲁牧一号”牧草株形很像小麦，株高可达一点五米——一点六米，一年两收，亩产五千公斤左右。
