Title: Li Peng Meets with Secret Protection Workers
Author:
Date: 1989-05-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 13th.

Li Peng, Qiao Shi and other comrades met with all the representatives attending the National Secret Protection Work Conference this morning in Zhongnanhai and took a group photo with them. Song Jian, Wang Fang, Zhou Jiaxuan and others also participated in the meeting.

After the meeting, Secretary of the State Council Luogan attended the conference and delivered a speech.

### 李鹏会见保密工作者

1989-05-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏会见保密工作者
    新华社北京五月十三日电  李鹏、乔石等同志今天上午在中南海会见了出席全国保密工作会议的全体代表，并同大家一起合影留念。宋健、王芳、邹家华等也参加了会见。
    会见后，国务院秘书长罗干出席了会议并在会上讲了话。
