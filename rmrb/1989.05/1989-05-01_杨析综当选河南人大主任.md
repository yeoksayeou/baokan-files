Title: Yang Xixiang Elected Chairman of Henan People's Congress
Author: Zhang Xiaohua
Date: 1989-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, April 29th

Yang Xixiang, Secretary of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, was elected Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Henan Provincial People's Congress today at the second session of the seventh Henan Provincial People's Congress. The meeting also elected Hou Zhiying as Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Henan Provincial People's Congress.

### 杨析综当选河南人大主任
张晓华
1989-05-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　杨析综当选河南人大主任
    新华社郑州４月２９日电  （记者张晓华）中共河南省委书记杨析综，今天在河南省七届人大二次会议上当选为省人大常委会主任。会议还增选侯志英为省人大常委会副主任。
