Title: PLA and Beijing Youths Secure First Two Gold Medals at National Youth Games
Author: Wáng Jiànmín
Date: 1989-05-11
Edition: 4

The People's Liberation Army (PLA) youth men's handball team and the Beijing youth women's handball team won the first two gold medals at the second National Youth Games today.

According to Xinhua News Agency in Shenyang, May 11 (Reporter Wang Jianmin), the People's Liberation Army youth men's handball team and the Beijing youth women's handball team secured the first two gold medals at the second National Youth Games held here today.

### 解放军北京小将获青运会头两块金牌
王健民
1989-05-11
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　解放军北京小将获青运会头两块金牌
    据新华社沈阳５月１１日电（记者王健民）解放军青年男子手球队和北京青年女子手球队今天在这里夺得了第二届全国青少年运动会头两枚金牌。
