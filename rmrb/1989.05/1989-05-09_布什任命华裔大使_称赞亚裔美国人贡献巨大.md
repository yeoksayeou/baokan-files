Title: Bush Appoints Chinese-American Ambassador
Author: Zhang Liang
Date: 1989-05-09
Edition: 3

Bush Appoints Chinese-American Ambassador
Praise for Contributions of Asian Americans

	Washington, May 9 (Reported by Zhang Liang) President Bush yesterday proposed to appoint American citizen of Chinese descent, Zhang Zhi Xiang, as the U.S. Ambassador to Nepal. Ms. Zhang has been serving as the Assistant Director of the Asia and Near East Bureau of the U.S. Agency for International Development since 1987. Zhang Zhi Xiang is the first Chinese-American to be appointed as a U.S. Ambassador.

	This nomination was announced by Bush yesterday during the opening ceremony of the "Asian Pacific American Heritage Week" held in the White House Rose Garden. In his speech, Bush also recalled his experience as the U.S. Liaison to China and said that the Asian community holds a "special place" in his heart. He quoted a Chinese proverb, "前人栽树，后人乘凉" (The predecessors plant the trees, and the successors enjoy the shade). Bush continued, saying that over the decades, Asian Americans have planted the trees of prosperity, opportunity, and dignity, and future generations of Americans will be grateful for the shade they provide.

### 布什任命华裔大使  称赞亚裔美国人贡献巨大
张亮
1989-05-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　布什任命华裔大使    
    称赞亚裔美国人贡献巨大
    本报华盛顿５月９日电  记者张亮报道：布什总统昨天提议任命美籍华人张之香为美国驻尼泊尔大使。张女士自１９８７年以来一直担任美国国际开发署亚洲及近东局助理局长。张之香是美国第一位华人大使。
    此项提名是布什昨天在白宫玫瑰园举行的“亚太裔美国人传统周”开幕式上宣布的。布什在讲话中还回顾了他出任美国驻中国联络处主任时的经历，并说亚裔社团在他的心目中占有“特殊的地位”。他还援引中国的一句俗语“前人栽树，后人乘凉”。布什接着说，几十年来亚裔美国人种下了繁荣、机遇和尊严之树，美国的后代将会感谢他们撒下的阴凉。
