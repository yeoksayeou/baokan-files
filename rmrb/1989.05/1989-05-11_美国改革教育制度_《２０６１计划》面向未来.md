Title: The United States Reforms Its Education System: The 2061 Plan Looks to the Future
Author:
Date: 1989-05-11
Edition: 1

The United States, to ensure its leading position in the field of science and technology in the future, has begun to formulate a bold plan to fundamentally reform its current education system. For details, see today's seventh page for a report by our correspondent: "The 2061 Plan Looking to the Future."
