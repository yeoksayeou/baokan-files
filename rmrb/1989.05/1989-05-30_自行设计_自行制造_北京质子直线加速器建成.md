Title: Beijing Proton Linear Accelerator Completed Through Self-Design and Self-Manufacturing
Author:
Date: 1989-05-30
Edition: 2

The Beijing proton linear accelerator, designed and manufactured independently by China, has been completed and recently passed the appraisal at the Institute of High Energy Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

This accelerator, with an energy of 35 mega-electron volts and a pulse current strength of 60 milliamperes, integrates many cutting-edge technologies from various disciplines, such as ultra-high-frequency radio, high voltage, high vacuum, precision machinery, high-precision optical and electromagnetic measurement, and computer automatic control technology.

It is primarily used for the preparation of medical short-lived isotopes and research on neutron therapy for cancer. Most of the important short-lived neutron-deficient isotopes in nuclear medicine, such as carbon-11, fluorine-18, gallium-67, iodine-123, and thallium-201, can be produced on the isotope preparation device of this accelerator. The fast neutron generation system, treatment positioning system, neutron dose measurement system, and automatic control system of this accelerator form China's first neutron therapy device, which will provide a platform for the medical community to conduct neutron therapy research. Additionally, it can provide proton beams for nuclear physics and nuclear chemistry experimental research for some basic research units and universities across the country.

Scientists believe that the completion of the Beijing proton linear accelerator marks a new level in China's accelerator research and development. It will make important contributions to the development of China's accelerator technology and its applications.

### 自行设计  自行制造  北京质子直线加速器建成

1989-05-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    自行设计  自行制造
    北京质子直线加速器建成
    新华社北京5月30日电  我国自行设计和制造的北京质子直线加速器建成，最近在中国科学院高能物理研究所通过鉴定。
    这台能量为35兆电子伏，脉冲流强为60毫安的质子直线加速器，综合了许多学科的尖端技术，如超高频无线电、高电压、高真空、精密机械、高精度光测及电磁测量、计算机自动控制技术等。
    它主要用于医用短寿命同位素的制备和中子治癌的研究。核医学中大部分重要的短寿命缺中子同位素，如碳11、氟18、镓67、碘123、铊201等，都能在这台加速器的同位素制备装置上生产；这台加速器的快中子产生系统、治疗定位系统、中子剂量测试系统和自控系统又组成了我国第一台中子治癌装置，将为医学界开展中子治癌研究提供场所。此外，还可为全国部分基础研究的单位和高等院校提供核物理和核化学实验研究的质子束。
    科学家们认为，北京质子直线加速器的建成，标志着我国加速器研制达到了一个新的水平，并将为我国加速器技术及其应用事业的发展，作出重要的贡献。
