Title: The State Council Office Announces Unified Management of Nuclear Power Construction
Author:
Date: 1989-05-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 11th.

The State Council Office has recently issued a notice stating that the construction of nuclear power nationwide must be managed in a centralized and unified manner.

The notice states that recently, various localities have proposed the construction of nuclear power plants. Given the complexity of nuclear power technology, stringent safety requirements, and the impact on energy structure and rational layout, nuclear power construction must be planned and coordinated at the national level. The notice stipulates that nuclear power construction must be included in the national plan, with the Ministry of Energy responsible for unified planning and leadership. The China National Nuclear Corporation is authorized and entrusted to implement unified management and is directly responsible for nuclear safety management work.

### 国务院办公厅通知  核电建设实行统一管理

1989-05-11
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　国务院办公厅通知    
    核电建设实行统一管理
    新华社北京５月１１日电  国务院办公厅近日发出通知，要求全国核电建设必须实行集中统一管理。
    《通知》说，最近，一些地方纷纷提出兴建核电站的要求，鉴于核电技术复杂，安全要求严格，涉及能源结构和合理布局等一系列问题，对全国的经济发展影响很大，核电建设必须由国家统筹规划。《通知》中规定，核电建设必须纳入国家计划，由能源部统一规划和领导，授权和委托中国核工业总公司实行统一管理，并对核安全管理工作负直接责任。
