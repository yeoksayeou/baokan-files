Title: International Conference on Artificial Weather Modification Held
Author: Xie Lianhui
Date: 1989-05-13
Edition: 2

Scarcity of water resources and natural disasters such as droughts, hail, and fog have significant impacts on various sectors of the global economy. Recently, 87 foreign scholars from 25 countries and over 100 Chinese experts gathered in Beijing to participate in the World Meteorological Organization's Fifth International Conference on Artificial Weather Modification and the Application of Cloud Physics. The conference aimed to discuss strategies for defending against natural disasters and transforming unfavorable factors into favorable ones. Participants exchanged experiences and insights on artificial weather modification and the application of cloud physics.

### 人工影响天气国际会议举行
谢联辉
1989-05-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    人工影响天气国际会议举行
    水资源紧缺和干旱、冰雹、大雾等天气对世界经济各领域影响重大。来自２５个国家的８７名外国学者和我国百余名专家，日前在北京参加世界气象组织第五次人工影响天气和应用云物理科学会议，探讨防御自然灾害，化不利因素为有利因素的途径，交流人工影响天气和云物理应用经验。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（谢联辉）
