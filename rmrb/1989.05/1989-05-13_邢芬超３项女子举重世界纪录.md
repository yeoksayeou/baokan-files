Title: Xing Fenchao Sets 3 Women's Weightlifting World Records
Author: Xie Huibin
Date: 1989-05-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, May 13th

Today, the national women's weightlifting championship opened in Taiyuan, and good news came from the 44kg class competition. The 16-year-old athlete from Guangdong, Xing Fenchao, set three world records in one competition.

### 邢芬超３项女子举重世界纪录
冀惠斌
1989-05-13
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　邢芬超３项女子举重世界纪录
    据新华社太原５月１３日电（记者冀惠斌）今日在太原揭开战幕的全国女子举重锦标赛传出捷报，年仅１６岁的广东选手邢芬在４４公斤级比赛中一人超了３项世界纪录。
