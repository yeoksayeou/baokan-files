Title: The Jiaozuo Memorial Hall Reopens After Renovation
Author: Wáng Wēi  Jīng Xīn
Date: 1989-05-30
Edition: 2

The Jiaozuo Memorial Hall has reopened after renovation. According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Jinan on May 30th, the Jiaozuo Memorial Hall in Boshan District, Zibo City, Shandong Province, was reopened after renovation during a gathering to commemorate the 25th anniversary of the passing of the exemplary Party cadre Jiaozuo.

Boshan District, Zibo City, is the hometown of Comrade Jiaozuo. During the 1960s, when the nation widely promoted learning from Jiaozuo, the Zibo Municipal Party Committee decided to build the Jiaozuo Memorial Hall in his hometown. Since its opening, over 400,000 people from home and abroad have visited the hall.

### 焦裕禄纪念馆重新修复开放
王威  京新
1989-05-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    焦裕禄纪念馆
    重新修复开放
    据新华社济南五月三十日电  （通讯员王威、京新）山东省淄博市博山区最近在集会纪念党的好干部焦裕禄逝世二十五周年时，宣布博山焦裕禄纪念馆重新修复开放。
    淄博市博山区是焦裕禄同志的故乡。六十年代全国普遍开展向焦裕禄学习的时候，中共淄博市委决定在他的故乡修建焦裕禄纪念馆，建馆以来国内外有四十多万人前来参观。
