Title: Zhao Ziyang's Visit to North Korea Concludes Successfully as He Returns to Beijing
Author: Wen Xian Xu Baokang
Date: 1989-04-30
Edition: 1

	Zhao Ziyang's Visit to North Korea Concludes Successfully as He Returns to Beijing
	Li Peng, Qiao Shi, and Tian Jiyun Welcome Him at Beijing Station
	Zhao Ziyang Calls Gold Day-chung to Thank Him for His Warm Hospitality During His Return Journey

	BEIJING, April 30 (Xinhua) — After successfully concluding his official friendly visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, CPC Central Committee General Secretary Zhao Ziyang returned to Beijing by special train this morning.

	Those who went to Beijing Railway Station to welcome Zhao Ziyang included: CPC Central Committee Politburo Standing Committee member and Premier Li Peng, CPC Central Committee Politburo Standing Committee member Qiao Shi, CPC Central Committee Politburo member and Vice Premier Tian Jiyun, CPC Central Committee Secretariat member and Office Director Wen Jiabao, Central Military Commission member and Director of the General Logistics Department Zhao Nanqi, Minister of Railways Li Senmao, and Deputy Minister of the International Liaison Department Li Shucen. The Chargé d'affaires of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, Pak Yongun, was also at the station to welcome him.

	Accompanying Zhao Ziyang on his visit, Wu Xueqian, Hong Xuezhi, and Zhu Liang returned to Beijing by the same train.

	PYONGYANG, April 29 (Xinhua) — CPC Central Committee General Secretary Zhao Ziyang concluded his visit to the DPRK today and departed by special train for his return journey.

	The General Secretary of the Workers' Party of Korea, Kim Il-sung, came to the state guesthouse to accompany Zhao Ziyang to the station, where they were greeted by 500,000 people from various sectors of Pyongyang along the route.

	At the Pyongyang station, Zhao Ziyang, accompanied by Kim Il-sung, inspected the honor guard. Those who came to see him off at the station also included Workers' Party of Korea Politburo Standing Committee member and Secretary Kim Jong-il, as well as Wu Jin-u, Yon Hyong-muk, Pak Song-chol, and Ri Chong-yu.

	PYONGYANG, April 29 (Xinhua) — During his return journey, CPC Central Committee General Secretary Zhao Ziyang sent a telegram to Workers' Party of Korea General Secretary Kim Il-sung, expressing his gratitude for the grand, enthusiastic, and warm welcome and hospitality he received during his visit.

	The telegram stated that during the visit, we were all warmly welcomed by the Workers' Party of Korea and the Korean people, which made us deeply feel the friendship of the Korean people once again. I had a friendly and cordial exchange of views with Comrade Kim Il-sung and other Korean leaders, which further deepened our mutual understanding and strengthened Sino-DPRK friendship. I am very satisfied with the results of this visit. We sincerely wish the Korean people success in their socialist construction and in their struggle for the independent and peaceful reunification of their country.

### 赵紫阳访朝圆满结束回京  李鹏乔石田纪云等到北京站迎接  回国途中致电金日成感谢盛情接待
温宪　徐宝康
1989-04-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　赵紫阳访朝圆满结束回京    
    李鹏乔石田纪云等到北京站迎接
    回国途中致电金日成感谢盛情接待
    本报北京４月３０日讯  记者温宪报道：在圆满结束对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的正式友好访问后，中共中央总书记赵紫阳今天上午乘专列回到北京。
    前往北京火车站迎接赵紫阳总书记的有：中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理李鹏，中共中央政治局常委乔石，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理田纪云，中共中央书记处候补书记、中央办公厅主任温家宝，中央军委委员、总后勤部部长赵南起，铁道部部长李森茂，中联部副部长李淑铮等。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办裴溶在等也到车站迎接。
    陪同赵紫阳总书记访问的吴学谦、洪学智、朱良等同车回到北京。
    本报平壤４月２９日电　记者徐宝康报道：中共中央总书记赵紫阳今天结束对朝鲜的访问，乘专列离开这里回国。
    朝鲜劳动党总书记金日成来到国宾馆，陪同赵紫阳前往车站，沿途受到５０万平壤各界人民的夹道欢送。
    在平壤车站，赵紫阳在金日成陪同下，检阅了仪仗队。到车站送行的还有朝鲜劳动党中央政治局常委、中央书记金正日以及吴振宇、延亨默、朴成哲、李钟玉等。
    新华社平壤４月２９日电　中国共产党中央委员会总书记赵紫阳在回国途中致电金日成总书记，感谢他在访问期间给予的盛大、热烈、隆重的欢迎和接待。
    电报说，访问期间，我们都受到了朝鲜劳动党和朝鲜人民的盛大、热烈、隆重的欢迎，使我们再一次深深地感受到朝鲜人民的美好情谊。我同金日成同志和其他朝鲜领导同志亲切友好地互通情况，充分交换意见，进一步加深了相互理解，增进了中朝友谊。我对这次访问所取得的成果感到十分满意。我们衷心祝愿朝鲜人民在建设社会主义，争取自主和平统一祖国的事业中不断取得成就。
