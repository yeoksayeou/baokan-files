Title: A New Method for Permanent Tidal Current Prediction Emerges
Author: Tang Guangxin
Date: 1989-05-29
Page: 2

The "permanent tidal current prediction method," developed by Associate Researcher Xiu Rizhen of the First Institute of Oceanography under the State Oceanic Administration, has recently passed a technical appraisal in Qingdao.

The method proposed by this project, which uses the tidal force at the substellar point of the tidal-generating celestial body as an indicator and relies on measured data, is a new approach to tidal current prediction and is the first of its kind in both domestic and international contexts.

The review committee concluded that since the tidal force at the substellar point of the tidal-generating celestial body can accurately characterize the mechanical properties of the tidal force field, the method's use of this point's tidal force as an indicator for permanent tidal current prediction is theoretically sound.

### 潮流场永久预报方法问世
汤光信
1989-05-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　潮流场永久预报方法问世
    本报讯  由国家海洋局第一海洋研究所副研究员修日晨主持研究的“潮流场永久预报方法”，最近在青岛通过技术鉴定。
    该项课题所提出的以引潮天体星下点处引潮力为指标、以实测资料为依据的“潮流场永久预报方法”，是潮流预报的一种新方法，系国内外首创。
    评审委员会认为，由于引潮天体星下点处的引潮力能准确地表征出引潮力场的力学特征，因此，该方法选用该点引潮力作为潮流场永久预报指标，理论上是成立的。
      　　　　　　　（汤光信）
