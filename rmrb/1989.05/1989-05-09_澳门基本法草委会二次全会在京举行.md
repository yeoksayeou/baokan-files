Title: The Second Plenary Session of the Basic Law Drafting Committee for the Macao SAR Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-05-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 9th.

The second plenary session of the Basic Law Drafting Committee for the Macao Special Administrative Region was held this morning at the Great Hall of the People in Beijing. All 48 members from the mainland and Macao attended the meeting.

The main agenda for this session included: (1) preliminary discussions on the structure of the Macao Basic Law and determining the drafting method for the Basic Law's structure; (2) reviewing and approving the working rules of the Basic Law Drafting Committee for the Macao Special Administrative Region.

The meeting was chaired by Ji Pengfei, Director of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office and Chairman of the Basic Law Drafting Committee for the Macao Special Administrative Region. Ji Pengfei stated that since the first plenary session, the Macao members, adhering to the principles of democracy, consultation, and openness, have completed the organizational work for the Macao Basic Law Consultative Committee. Various social organizations have held seminars and discussions on Basic Law issues, established Basic Law concern groups, and proposed many positive and valuable opinions and suggestions. The mainland members, through studying relevant materials on Macao, have gained an understanding and recognition of the situation in Macao.

The session will conclude tomorrow.

### 澳门基本法草委会二次全会在京举行

1989-05-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　澳门基本法草委会二次全会在京举行
    新华社北京５月９日电  中华人民共和国澳门特别行政区基本法起草委员会第二次全体会议今天上午在北京人民大会堂举行。４８名内地及澳门委员全部出席了今天的会议。
    这次会议的主要议程是：（一）初步讨论澳门基本法结构的内容并确定基本法结构的起草办法；（二）审议通过澳门特别行政区基本法起草委员会工作规则。
    会议由国务院港澳办公室主任、澳门基本法起草委员会主任委员姬鹏飞主持。姬鹏飞说，自第一次全体会议以来，澳门委员本着民主、协商、开放的精神，完成了澳门特别行政区基本法咨询委员会的筹组工作，一些社会团体举行了有关基本法问题的讲座和研讨会，成立了关注基本法小组，提出一些积极的具有参考价值的意见和建议。内地委员通过研究澳门问题的有关资料，对澳门有了了解和认识。
    这次会议将于明天闭幕。
