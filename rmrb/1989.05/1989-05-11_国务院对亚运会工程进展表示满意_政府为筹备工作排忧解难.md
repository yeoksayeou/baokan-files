Title: The State Council Expresses Satisfaction with the Progress of the Asiad Projects and Resolves Issues for the Preparatory Work
Author: Lǐ Lì
Date: 1989-05-11
Edition: 4

The State Council has expressed satisfaction with the progress of the Asiad projects and has resolved some practical issues for the projects.

This was revealed by Zhang Baifu, Vice Chairman of the Organizing Committee for the 11th Asian Games and Deputy Mayor of Beijing, this morning at the handover ceremony for donations to the Asiad at the Beijing Zhongshan Calligraphy and Painting Society. He said that on May 9, the State Council held a full meeting to hear a report on the progress of the construction of the 11th Asiad projects and related organizational preparatory work. The State Council expressed satisfaction with the progress of the Asiad projects and resolved some specific issues in the preparatory work for the Asiad. It is understood that the main issue is the funding problem.

Zhang Baifu happily said that with the leadership of the Party Central Committee and the State Council, and the strong support of compatriots at home and abroad, we have the ability, conditions, and confidence to successfully host the Asiad.

### 国务院对亚运会工程进展表示满意　政府为筹备工作排忧解难
李莉
1989-05-11
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　国务院对亚运会工程进展表示满意
  　政府为筹备工作排忧解难
    新华社北京５月１１日电（记者李莉）国务院对亚运会工程进展表示满意，并为工程解决了一些实际问题。
    这是第十一届亚运会组委会副主席、北京市副市长张百发今天上午在北京中山书画社向亚运会捐画交接仪式上告诉大家的。他说，５月９日，国务院全体会议听取了第十一届亚运会工程建设进展情况及有关组织筹备工作的汇报，对亚运会工程的进展等表示满意，并在会上基本解决了亚运会筹备工作中的一些具体问题。据悉，主要是资金问题。
    张百发高兴地说，有党中央、国务院的领导，海内外同胞的大力支持，我们有能力、有条件、有信心把亚运会开好。
