Title: Japan's Foreign Exchange Reserves Exceed $100 Billion
Author:
Date: 1989-05-07
Edition: 3

Japan's foreign exchange reserves have exceeded $100 billion.

According to statistics released by Japan's Ministry of Finance, by the end of April this year, Japan's foreign exchange reserves had surpassed $100 billion, reaching $100.361 billion.

Since January 1988, Japan's foreign exchange reserves have consistently ranked first in the world.

### 日本外汇储备超过千亿美元

1989-05-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　日本外汇储备超过千亿美元
    据新华社东京电  据日本大藏省日前公布的统计资料说，到今年４月底为止，日本的外汇储备额已突破１０００亿美元，达到了１００３．６１亿美元。
    自１９８８年１月以来，日本的外汇储备额就一直居世界第一位。
