Title: Premier Li Peng Signs State Council Order: Martial Law Imposed in Parts of Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-05-20
Edition: 1

The State Council Order of the People's Republic of China on the Implementation of Martial Law in Parts of Beijing

In view of the serious turmoil that has occurred in Beijing, which has disrupted social stability, interfered with the normal lives of the people, and undermined social order, it is necessary to take decisive measures to stop the turmoil, maintain social stability in Beijing, ensure the safety of people's lives and property, protect public property from being infringed upon, and ensure that the central government organs and the Beijing municipal government can carry out their official duties normally. In accordance with the provisions of Article 89, Item 16 of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, the State Council has decided: From 10:00 a.m. on May 20, 1989, martial law will be implemented in parts of Beijing. The Beijing municipal government will organize and implement it, and take specific martial law measures as needed.

	State Council Premier Li Peng

	May 20, 1989

### 李鹏总理签署国务院令  北京部分地区实行戒严

1989-05-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏总理签署国务院令
    北京部分地区实行戒严
    新华社北京５月２０日电  中华人民共和国国务院关于在北京市部分地区实行戒严的命令
    鉴于北京市已经发生了严重的动乱，破坏了社会安定，破坏了人民的正常生活和社会秩序，为了坚决制止动乱，维护北京市的社会安宁，保障公民的生命和财产安全，保障公共财产不受侵犯，保障中央国家机关和北京市政府正常执行公务，根据中华人民共和国宪法第８９条第１６项的规定，国务院决定：自１９８９年５月２０日１０时起在北京市部分地区实行戒严，由北京市人民政府组织实施，并根据实际需要采取具体戒严措施。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　国务院总理　李鹏
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年５月２０日
