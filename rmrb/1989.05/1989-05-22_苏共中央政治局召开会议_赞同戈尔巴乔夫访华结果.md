Title: The Central Committee of the CPSU Politburo Holds a Meeting to Endorse the Results of Gorbachev's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1989-05-22
Edition: 1

The Central Committee of the CPSU Politburo held a meeting on May 20th, endorsing the results of the official visit to China by Mikhail Gorbachev, General Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

According to a report by TASS on May 21st, the Politburo noted with deep satisfaction that the high-level talks between the Soviet Union and China were of epoch-making significance, marking the beginning of a new phase in Sino-Soviet relations.

The Politburo pointed out that the consensus reached on the new foundation for relations between the two countries and their parties was a major achievement of this meeting. This new foundation reflects mutual respect for sovereignty, independence, consideration of each other's interests, and a commitment to fully developing cooperation between two equal partners and friendly neighbors. The Politburo expressed the belief that the normalization of Sino-Soviet relations will not only be beneficial for Sino-Soviet relations but will also have a positive impact on the situation in the Asia-Pacific region and the world.

### 苏共中央政治局召开会议  赞同戈尔巴乔夫访华结果

1989-05-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　苏共中央政治局召开会议
    赞同戈尔巴乔夫访华结果
    新华社莫斯科５月２１日电  苏共中央政治局２０日开会，赞同苏共中央总书记、苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席戈尔巴乔夫对中国进行正式访问所取得的结果。
    据塔斯社２１日报道，政治局深为满意地指出，苏中高级会晤具有划时代意义，开辟了苏中关系的新阶段。
    政治局指出，就苏中两国和两党关系新基础达成一致意见是这次会晤的重要成果，新基础反映了双方相互尊重主权、独立自主、考虑双方利益和全力发展两个平等伙伴和友好邻邦的合作。政治局表示相信，苏中关系正常化不仅对苏中关系，而且对亚太地区和世界形势会产生有利的影响。
