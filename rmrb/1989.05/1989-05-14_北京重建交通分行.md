Title: Beijing Reestablishes Traffic Bank Branch
Author: Zhao Xinglin
Date: 1989-05-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 14th.

Beijing has reestablished the Traffic Bank branch, which had been suspended for 31 years, and it is now operational.

It is reported that the Traffic Bank has a history of over 80 years in China. Since the State Council decided to reestablish the Traffic Bank in 1986, more than 40 domestic branches have been established. It has also established agency relationships with over 190 foreign institutions.

### 北京重建交通分行
赵兴林
1989-05-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　北京重建交通分行
    本报讯中断了３１年的交通银行北京分行筹建就绪并开业。
    据介绍，交通银行在我国已有８０多年历史。自１９８６年国务院决定重新建立交通银行以来，目前国内已建分支机构４０多家，已与国外１９０多家机构建立了代理行关系。　
　　　　　（赵兴林）
