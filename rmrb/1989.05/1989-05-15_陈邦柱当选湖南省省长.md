Title: Chen Bangzhu Elected as Governor of Hunan Province
Author: Wu Xinghua
Date: 1989-05-15
Edition: 2

Chen Bangzhu was elected as the governor of Hunan Province today at the second session of the seventh Hunan Provincial People's Congress. Chen Bangzhu, currently 54 years old, has a college education.

### 陈邦柱当选湖南省省长
吴兴华
1989-05-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　陈邦柱当选湖南省省长
    本报长沙５月１５日电  记者吴兴华报道：今天，在湖南省第七届人民代表大会第二次会议上，代理省长陈邦柱当选为湖南省省长。陈邦柱现年５４岁，大专文化。
