	Brief News

	▲ Vice Premier Tian Jiyun of the State Council met with a delegation led by German Federal Minister for Economic Cooperation, Jürgen W. Möllemann, in the Zhongnanhai Purple Light Pavilion at 4 p.m. on May 4. Möllemann arrived in China on May 1 at the invitation of Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade, Zheng Tuobin, to attend the annual meeting of the Asian Development Bank.

	▲ General Xu Xin, Vice Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army of China, met with a delegation led by General Jörg Kuehn, Inspector General of the German Air Force, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on the morning of May 4. General Wang Hai, Commander of the Air Force, was present at the meeting.

### 简讯

1989-05-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　简讯
    ▲国务院副总理田纪云４日下午在中南海紫光阁会见德意志联邦共和国经济合作部部长瓦恩克一行。瓦恩克是应对外经济贸易部部长郑拓彬邀请于５月１日来华访问，并出席亚洲开发银行年会的。
    ▲中国人民解放军副总参谋长徐信上将４日上午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了联邦德国空军监察长容库特中将一行。空军司令员王海上将会见时在座。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
