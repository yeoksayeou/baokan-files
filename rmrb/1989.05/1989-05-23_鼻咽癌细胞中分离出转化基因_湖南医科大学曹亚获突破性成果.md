Title: Malignant Transformation Gene Isolated from Nasopharyngeal Carcinoma Cells
Author:
Date: 1989-05-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, May 23rd.

A malignant transformation gene has been isolated from nasopharyngeal carcinoma cells by Cao Ya, an associate researcher at the Tumor Research Institute of Hunan Medical University. She recently brought back the gene, which she personally isolated from a Chinese nasopharyngeal carcinoma cell line, from the United States. Experts believe that this pioneering achievement provides a practical approach to exploring the pathogenesis of nasopharyngeal carcinoma at the molecular level. It may also offer new theories and treatment methods for the study of nasopharyngeal carcinoma pathogenesis.

### 鼻咽癌细胞中分离出转化基因  湖南医科大学曹亚获突破性成果

1989-05-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　鼻咽癌细胞中分离出转化基因
    湖南医科大学曹亚获突破性成果
    据新华社长沙５月２３日电  湖南医科大学肿瘤研究所助理研究员曹亚，最近从美国带回了她亲手从中国鼻咽癌细胞株中分离出来的恶性转化基因。专家们认为，这一首创性成果，为从分子水平上探讨鼻咽癌发病机理提供了切实可行的途径，可能为鼻咽癌的发病学提出新的理论和新的治疗办法。
