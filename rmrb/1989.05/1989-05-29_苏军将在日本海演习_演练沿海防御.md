Title: Soviet Military to Conduct Exercises in the Sea of Japan
Author:
Date: 1989-05-29
Edition: 3

The Soviet military will conduct exercises in the Sea of Japan.

The Soviet Pacific Fleet will hold exercises from July 10 to 12 in the northwestern part of the Sea of Japan, involving 10,000 personnel to practice coastal defense.

The exercises will include 20 warships, 37 aircraft, and helicopters. The Soviet government has invited naval observers from 14 countries, including China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the United States, Japan, Canada, Vietnam, and India, to observe the exercises.

The Pacific Fleet is the strongest of the Soviet Union's four fleets. According to Soviet Defense Minister Yazov, the Pacific Fleet has 55 large surface vessels (including 2 aircraft carriers), 48 nuclear attack submarines, and numerous aircraft.

### 苏军将在日本海演习  演练沿海防御

1989-05-29
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏军将在日本海演习
    演练沿海防御
    新华社莫斯科５月２９日电  据塔斯社今天报道，苏联太平洋舰队将于今年７月１０—１２日在日本海西北部举行有１万名官兵参加的演习，以演练沿海防御问题。
    参加演习的将有２０艘作战舰艇、３７架飞机和直升机。苏联政府已邀中国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、美国、日本、加拿大、越南和印度等１４国海军派观察员观看演习。
    太平洋舰队是苏联四大舰队中实力最雄厚的舰队。据苏联国防部长亚佐夫透露，太平洋舰队拥有５５艘大型水面舰艇（其中２艘航空母舰）、４８艘攻击型核潜艇以及许多飞机。
