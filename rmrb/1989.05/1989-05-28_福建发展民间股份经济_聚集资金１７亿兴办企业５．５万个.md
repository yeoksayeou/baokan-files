Title: Fujian Develops Private Sector Shareholding Economy
Author: Zhang Mingqing
Date: 1989-05-28
Edition: 1

A form of private sector shareholding economy with broad functions in aggregating social funds is flourishing in the coastal rural areas of Fujian. Currently, this economic model has aggregated over 1.7 billion yuan in funds, establishing more than 55,000 private sector shareholding enterprises, with their output accounting for one-quarter of the total output of township enterprises in the province.

Based on the formation and structure of shares, the private sector shareholding enterprises in Fujian can be categorized into four types: joint household, transformed, Sino-foreign joint ventures, and mixed.

Local experts believe that shareholding enterprises, in terms of aggregating social production factors, adopt the principle of "voluntary investment, free withdrawal, unite if compatible, disperse if not." This allows various social production factors to flow and combine based on the principle of efficiency.

### 福建发展民间股份经济  聚集资金１７亿兴办企业５．５万个
张铭清
1989-05-28
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　福建发展民间股份经济
    聚集资金１７亿兴办企业５．５万个
    本报福州电 记者张铭清报道：一种具有广泛聚集社会资金功能的民间股份经济，正在福建沿海农村蓬勃发展。目前，这种经济形式已聚集了１７亿多元资金，兴办了５．５万多个民间股份企业，其产值占全省乡镇企业总产值的１／４。
    以股份的形成和结构划分，这里的民间股份企业有联户、转化、中外合营和混合４种类型。
    此间人士认为，股份企业在聚合社会生产要素方面，采取“投股自愿、退股自由，合则聚、不合则散”的原则，使社会上各种生产要素得以依效益原则流动组合。
