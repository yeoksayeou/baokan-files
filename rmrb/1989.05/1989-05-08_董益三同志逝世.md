Title: Comrade Dong Yisi Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-05-08
Edition: 4

Comrade Dong Yisi Passes Away

New China News Agency, Beijing, May 8th: Member of the 7th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Researcher of Historical Materials at the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Comrade Dong Yisi, passed away due to illness on May 1st, 1989, in Beijing, at the age of 85.

Comrade Dong Yisi was from Qianjiang County, Hubei Province. He graduated from the Whampoa Military Academy Wuhan Branch and had served as a Major General in the National Government's Ministry of Defense and as a Major General in the Second Bureau of the 15th Pacification Headquarters. After 1983, he successively served as a member of the 6th and 7th National Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

In accordance with Comrade Dong Yisi's will to "keep the funeral simple, not hold a farewell ceremony, and not hold a memorial service," his body was cremated today, and his ashes are placed in the Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery in Babaoshan.

### 董益三同志逝世

1989-05-08
第4版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　董益三同志逝世
    新华社北京５月８日电  政协第七届全国委员会委员、全国政协文史资料研究专员董益三同志，因病医治无效，于１９８９年５月１日在北京逝世，终年８５岁。
    董益三同志，湖北潜江县人，毕业于黄埔军校武汉分校，曾任国民政府国防部少将部员，第十五绥靖区司令部第二处少将处长。１９８３年后，他先后任政协第六、七届全国委员会委员。
    遵照董益三同志“丧事从简，不搞遗体告别，不开追悼会”的遗嘱，他的遗体今日已火化，骨灰安放在八宝山革命公墓。
