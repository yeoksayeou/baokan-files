Title: Norodom Sihanouk Discusses Conditions for Returning to Phnom Penh
Author: Lǐ Yǒngmíng
Date: 1989-05-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 3rd.

Democratic Kampuchea Chairman Prince Norodom Sihanouk said he has rejected the proposal by Phnom Penh regime Prime Minister Hun Sen to establish a "National Supreme Council" to arrange elections after Vietnamese troops withdraw from Cambodia.

Sihanouk made these remarks at a press conference in Jakarta this morning. He has held the fourth round of talks with Hun Sen on the Cambodian issue in Jakarta.

Sihanouk also said that during the fourth round of talks, he proposed two points: the establishment of a four-party government to arrange elections; and the reorganization of the "People's Republic of Kampuchea" backed by Vietnam.

Sihanouk mentioned that on the issue of establishing a four-party government, Hun Sen should consider including the Khmer Rouge (Kampuchean side).

When discussing Hun Sen's invitation for him to return to Phnom Penh and participate in the Phnom Penh government, Sihanouk said that his return to Phnom Penh must have two prerequisites: the reorganization of the Phnom Penh government and the complete withdrawal of Vietnamese troops from Cambodia.

In discussing the election issue, Sihanouk emphasized that to conduct free and fair elections, the existing Phnom Penh government should be reorganized on the basis of a multi-party system.

At the press conference, Sihanouk also condemned Vietnam's attempt to establish an "Indochina Federation."

The next round of talks between Sihanouk and Hun Sen will be held in Paris on July 24th and 25th.

### 西哈努克谈返金边条件  越南全部撤军　改组金边政权  西哈努克拒绝建立“全国最高委员会”
李永明
1989-05-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　西哈努克谈返金边条件    
    越南全部撤军　改组金边政权   
    西哈努克拒绝建立“全国最高委员会”
    新华社雅加达５月３日电  （记者李永明）民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王说，他已拒绝了金边政权总理洪森提出的建议——在越南军队撤出柬埔寨后，成立一个负责安排大选的“全国最高委员会”。
    西哈努克是今天上午在雅加达举行的一次记者招待会上说这番话的。在雅加达，他已同洪森举行了关于柬埔寨问题的第４轮会谈。
    西哈努克还说，在第４轮会谈中，他提出两点建议——成立由４方组成的政府以安排大选；越南扶植的“柬埔寨人民共和国”应当改组。
    西哈努克谈到，在成立４方政府问题上，洪森应把红色高棉（民柬方面）考虑进去。
    西哈努克在谈到洪森邀请他回到金边和参加金边政权问题时说，他回金边必须有两个先决条件——改组金边政权和越军从柬埔寨全部撤出。
    在谈到大选问题时，西哈努克强调，为进行自由和公正的大选，现存的金边政府应当在多党制的基础上进行改组。
    在招待会上，西哈努克还谴责了越南建立“印支联邦”的企图。
    西哈努克与洪森的下一轮会谈将于７月２４日和２５日在巴黎举行。
