U.S. President's Special Advisor Visits China to Report on U.S.-Soviet Foreign Ministers' Meeting

	New China News Agency, Beijing, May 14 - U.S. President and Secretary of State Special Advisor on Arms Control, Edward Rowny, today in Beijing reported to the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the recent U.S.-Soviet Foreign Ministers' meeting in Moscow.

	Deputy Foreign Minister Liu Huaqiu and Li Daoyu held talks with Rowny. U.S. Ambassador to China, James Lilley, also attended the talks.

	Rowny arrived in Beijing this morning and is scheduled to leave tomorrow.

### 美国总统特别顾问来华  通报美苏外长会晤情况

1989-05-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　美国总统特别顾问来华  通报美苏外长会晤情况
    新华社北京５月１４日电  美国总统和国务卿军备控制问题特别顾问爱德华·罗尼今天在北京向中国外交部通报了近日美苏外长莫斯科会晤的情况。
    外交部部长助理刘华秋和李道豫与罗尼举行了会谈。美国驻华大使李洁明参加了会谈。
    罗尼是今晨抵达北京的，定于明日离京。
