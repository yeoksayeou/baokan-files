Title: Shandong Rural New Regulation: Using Residential Land Requires Payment
Author: Wáng Jìn Yè
Date: 1989-04-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, April 29th.

More than 4,000 administrative villages in Shandong have implemented the system of paid use of residential land.

Last year, this province began to implement the system of paid use of residential land. The regulation stipulates that users must pay a certain amount of usage fee to the collective based on the area and location of the residential land. This approach not only reflects the collective's status as the owner of the residential land but also curbs some farmers' unreasonable demands for residential land, initially achieving the goal of using residential land economically and reasonably.

### 山东农村新规定  使用宅基地需交费
王进业
1989-04-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　山东农村新规定     
    使用宅基地需交费
    据新华社济南４月２９日电  （记者王进业）山东已有４０００多个行政村实行宅基地有偿使用。
    去年这个省推行宅基地有偿使用制度。规定使用者要根据宅基地面积、地理位置等，向集体交纳一定数额的使用费。这样做既体现了集体作为宅基地所有者的地位，也抑制了一些农户对宅基地的不合理要求，初步实现了使用宅基地要节约、合理的方针。
