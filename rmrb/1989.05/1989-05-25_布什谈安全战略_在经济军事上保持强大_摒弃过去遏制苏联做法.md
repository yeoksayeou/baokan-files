Title: Bush Discusses Security Strategy
Author:
Date: 1989-05-25
Edition: 3

    Bush Discusses Security Strategy
    Maintaining Strength in Economy and Military
    Abandoning Past Practices of Containing the Soviet Union

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, May 24 - U.S. President Bush said today that to face a rapidly changing world, America's strategic goals for the next 10 years and the next century must include both security and stability.

    He said that to achieve these two goals, America must formulate a vigorous and adaptable strategy, and must remain strong in economic, diplomatic, and military aspects.

    Bush made these remarks in a speech at the Coast Guard Academy in Connecticut.

    Bush believes that an important opportunity for the United States now is to abandon the past practice of containing the Soviet Union, and to bring the Soviet Union back into the international community. Bush said this would help achieve international stability.

    When discussing America's defense strategy, Bush emphasized the need to maintain an effective deterrent force while continuing the momentum of arms reduction.

    He pointed out that deterrence is the "core" of America's defense strategy, and that nuclear weapons are the key to deterrence in today's world. He reiterated the need to continue research and deployment of the "Star Wars" program.

    When discussing the issue of arms reduction negotiations between the United States and the Soviet Union, Bush said that unilateral arms reductions by the Soviet Union "are not enough to eliminate the quantitative advantage that the Soviet Union currently maintains." He urged the Soviet Union to publish accurate defense budgets to make military actions more transparent.

### 布什谈安全战略  在经济军事上保持强大  摒弃过去遏制苏联做法

1989-05-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　布什谈安全战略
    在经济军事上保持强大
    摒弃过去遏制苏联做法
    新华社华盛顿５月２４日电  美国总统布什今天说，为了面对一个急剧变化的世界，美国在今后１０年和下个世纪的战略目标必须包括安全和稳定两个方面。
    他说，要实现这两个目标，美国必须制订出充满活力的、适应性强的战略，必须在经济上、外交上和军事上保持强大。
    布什是在康涅狄格州海岸警卫队学院发表的一次演讲中说这番话的。
    布什认为，美国现在的一个重要机会就是摒弃过去遏制苏联的做法，使苏联回到国际社会中来。布什说，这将有助于实现国际稳定。
    在谈到美国的防务战略时，布什强调必须保持有效的威慑力量，同时保持削减军备的势头。
    他指出，威慑是美国防务战略的“核心”，而核武器则是当今世界威慑的关键。他重申要继续进行“星球大战”计划的研究和部署工作。
    在谈到美苏两国间削减军备谈判的问题时，布什说，苏联单方面的削减军备“不足以消除苏联目前所保持的数量上的优势”。他敦促苏联公布准确的国防预算，以使军事行动更加公开化。
