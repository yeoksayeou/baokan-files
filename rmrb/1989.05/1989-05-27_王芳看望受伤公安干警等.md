Title: Wang Fang Visits Injured Public Security Officers
Author:
Date: 1989-05-27
Edition: 1

State Councilor and Minister of Public Security Wang Fang, along with other relevant leaders from the Ministry of Public Security, visited a hospital in the suburbs of Beijing on the afternoon of May 26th. They visited public security officers and armed police personnel who were injured by a few lawless elements while stopping the turmoil. They expressed their sincere concern and care.

Wang Fang and other comrades also visited the Dongcheng District Public Security Bureau in the afternoon of the same day to visit public security officers who have been on duty day and night. They encouraged them to make new contributions to maintaining the capital's public security.

### 王芳看望受伤公安干警等

1989-05-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　王芳看望受伤公安干警等
    据新华社北京５月２７日电  国务委员、公安部长王芳以及公安部有关领导同志５月２６日下午前往京郊医院，看望了在制止动乱中被少数不法分子打伤的公安干警和武警指战员，向他们表示亲切的慰问。
    王芳等同志还于当日下午前往北京市东城公安分局看望了日夜坚守岗位的公安干警，勉励他们为维护首都治安作出新贡献。
