Title: Milk Supply Surges in Six Major Cities
Author: Pǔ Lìyè, Zēng Jìshuāng
Date: 1989-05-02
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 2nd.

The milk supply development project in Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Wuhan, Nanjing, and Xi'an, funded by the World Food Programme, has seen significant progress after five years of implementation. The per capita milk consumption has increased from 11.1 kilograms to 21.8 kilograms.

### 六大城市奶类供应激增
蒲立业　曾继双
1989-05-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　六大城市奶类供应激增
    新华社北京五月二日电  （记者蒲立业、实习生曾继双）由世界粮食计划署提供无偿援助的我国北京、上海、天津、武汉、南京、西安六大城市奶类发展项目，经过五年实施，人均消费奶由十一点一公斤提高到二十一点八公斤。
