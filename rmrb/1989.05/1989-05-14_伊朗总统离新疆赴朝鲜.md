Title: Iranian President Departs Xinjiang for North Korea
Author:
Date: 1989-05-14
Edition: 2

Iranian President Seyed Ali Khamenei and his delegation concluded their visit to the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region. Today at dawn, they departed from Urumqi by special plane to visit North Korea.

The Chinese government's escort team leader, Minister of Energy Huang Yiqing, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Huaiyuan, and Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Tiemuer Dawameti, among others, went to the airport to see off President Khamenei and his delegation.

### 伊朗总统离新疆赴朝鲜

1989-05-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　伊朗总统离新疆赴朝鲜
    新华社乌鲁木齐５月１４日电  伊朗总统赛义德·阿里·哈梅内伊一行结束在新疆维吾尔自治区的参观访问，今天清晨乘专机离开乌鲁木齐赴朝鲜访问。
    中国政府陪同团团长、能源部部长黄毅诚，外交部副部长齐怀远，新疆维吾尔自治区主席铁木尔·达瓦买提等到机场为哈梅内伊总统一行送行。
