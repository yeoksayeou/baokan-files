Title: Delegations of ADB Member Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author: Jiang Qianhong, Wu Yingchun
Date: 1989-05-01
Edition: 2

Delegations from member countries of the Asian Development Bank (ADB) have been arriving in Beijing. ADB Director Guo Wanrong led the delegation from Taiwan to Beijing this afternoon. Officials from the ADB Preparatory Committee welcomed them at the airport. The 22nd Annual Meeting of the ADB Board of Governors will be held in Beijing from May 4 to May 6.

Delegations attending the 22nd Annual Meeting of the ADB Board of Governors have been arriving in Beijing. The photo shows Guo Wanrong, a representative from Taiwan, at the capital airport. (Photo by Zhang Jing)

### 亚行成员代表团陆续抵京
蒋千红　吴迎春
1989-05-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　亚行成员代表团陆续抵京
    本报北京５月１日讯  记者蒋千红、吴迎春报道：参加亚洲开发银行第二十二届理事会年会的各成员代表团以及亚行高级官员已陆续抵京。亚洲开发银行理事郭婉容女士率中国台北代表团也于今日下午飞抵北京。亚银筹委会官员到机场迎接。这届年会将于５月４日至５月６日在北京举行。
