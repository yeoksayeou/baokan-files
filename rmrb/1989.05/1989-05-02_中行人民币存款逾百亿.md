Title: China Bank's RMB Deposits Exceed 100 Billion
Author: Zhū Wéihuá
Date: 1989-05-02
Page: 2

    China Bank's RMB savings deposits have for the first time surpassed the 10 billion yuan mark. As of April 20 this year, the balance of RMB savings deposits has reached 10.27 billion yuan.

    In the course of financial system reform, China Bank has shifted from mainly handling international settlements and foreign exchange-related banking business to establishing extensive networks, attracting RMB deposits, and striving for the modernization, networking, and automation of savings business.

### 中行人民币存款逾百亿
朱维华
1989-05-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　中行人民币存款逾百亿
    本报讯  中国银行人民币储蓄存款，首次突破１００亿元大关。截至今年４月２０日，人民币储蓄存款余额已达１０２．７亿元。
    在金融体制改革中，中国银行由原来办理国际结算和与外汇有关的银行业务为主，进而广设网点，吸收人民币存款，并力求储蓄业务电子化、网络化、自动化。（朱维华）
