Title: Absorbing Idle Funds from Farmers
Author: Huaguiren
Date: 1989-05-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, May 11th.

Jiangxi Province is actively utilizing idle funds from farmers to develop household and joint household enterprises that use agricultural and sideline products as raw materials.

Currently, the province has 610,000 such enterprises, employing 1.25 million farmers. The total output value of these enterprises is 4.19 billion yuan, accounting for about half of the total number of people and total output value of township enterprises in the province. This year, these "two-household" enterprises will absorb an additional 200,000 to 300,000 surplus laborers.

### 吸收农民手中闲散资金  江西办起６１万两户企业
华桂勤
1989-05-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　吸收农民手中闲散资金    
    江西办起６１万两户企业
    新华社南昌５月１１日电（记者华桂勤）江西省农村积极利用农民手中的闲散资金，发展以农副产品为原材料的户办、联户办企业。
    目前，这个省“两户”企业发展到６１万多家，从业农民１２５万人，企业总产值４１．９亿元，分别占全省乡镇企业总人数和总产值的一半左右。今年，全省“两户”企业将新吸收剩余劳动力２０至３０万人。
