Title: The Ninth Day of the Ninth Lunar Month Designated as "Elderly Day" in Guangdong
Author: Liang Zhaoming
Date: 1989-04-30
Edition: 2

The recent seventh session of the seventh Standing Committee of the Guangdong Provincial People's Congress approved a proposal from the provincial government to designate the ninth day of the ninth lunar month as "Guangdong Elderly Day." This decision aims to carry forward and promote the traditional Chinese virtue of respecting the elderly.

### 九月初九定为  广东“老人节”
梁兆明
1989-04-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　九月初九定为    
    广东“老人节”
    本报广州四月三十日电  记者梁兆明报道：日前结束的广东省七届人大常委会第七次会议通过了省政府的议案，将农历九月初九定为“广东省老人节”，以继承和发扬中华民族尊老敬老的传统美德。
