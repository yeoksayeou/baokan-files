Title: Ortega Visits Italy
Author: Luo Jinbiao
Date: 1989-05-06
Edition: 3

Ortega Visits Italy
Requires the EEC to Provide Assistance

		BEIJING, May 6 (Xinhua) -- Nicaraguan President Daniel Ortega visited Italy from May 3 to 5, holding talks with Italian Prime Minister Ciriaco De Mita and meeting with Italian President Francesco Cossiga, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Nilde Iotti, and leaders of Italy's major political parties.

		Ortega briefed the Italian leaders on Nicaragua's economic recovery and political reforms, requesting economic assistance and political support from Italy and other EEC member states.

		Since the beginning of last year, Italy and Nicaragua have been implementing a three-year plan with Italy providing $150 million in aid. During the talks, De Mita expressed Italy's continued support for Nicaragua, emphasizing that "economic development and democratization should be closely linked, and Italy's aid is aimed at encouraging the achievement of this goal." Italian leaders expressed support for the peace process in Central America and hoped for positive results from the Central American peace talks.

		Italy is the first stop on Ortega's European tour, which will also take him to the Federal Republic of Germany, France, Spain, Norway, and Sweden.

### 奥尔特加出访意大利  要求欧共体提供援助
罗晋标
1989-05-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　奥尔特加出访意大利    
    要求欧共体提供援助
    本报罗马５月６日电  记者罗晋标报道：尼加拉瓜总统奥尔特加５月３日至５日访问意大利，同意大利总理德米塔进行了会谈，并会见了意大利总统科西加、众议院议长约蒂以及意大利主要政党领导人。
    奥尔特加总统向意大利领导人介绍了尼加拉瓜正在进行的经济恢复和政治改革的情况，要求意大利和欧共体其他成员国给以经济上的援助和政治上的支持。
    意大利和尼加拉瓜从去年初起执行一项意方提供１．５亿美元援助的３年计划。德米塔总理在会谈中表示意大利政府将继续支持尼加拉瓜，但强调“经济发展和民主化进程应当紧密联系，意大利的援助是为了鼓励实现这一目标”。意大利领导人表示支持中美洲和平进程，并希望中美洲和平谈判取得积极成果。
    意大利是奥尔特加总统欧洲之行的第一站，他将继续访问联邦德国、法国、西班牙、挪威和瑞典。
