Title: Funeral of Li Jianquan, a Loyal Communist Warrior, Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-05-08
Edition: 1

Party and state leaders, along with over 1,000 people from various sectors in Beijing, held a farewell ceremony for Li Jianquan, an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, a loyal and steadfast communist warrior, a revolutionary of the proletariat, and an excellent political worker of the People's Liberation Army, at the General Logistics Department of the People's Liberation Army on May 8.

Li Jianquan passed away in Beijing on April 24 at the age of 79. He previously served as a member of the Political Bureau of the 8th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, a standing member of the Central Advisory Commission, and a vice chairman of the Standing Committee of the 5th National People's Congress.

The General Logistics Department of the People's Liberation Army was solemn and respectful. Li Jianquan's body was placed among flowers and cypress trees, covered with the flag of the Communist Party of China. Soldiers stood guard on either side.

Zhao Ziyang, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, Wan Li, Qiao Shi, Hu Qili, Yao Yilin, Wang Zhen, and others attended the ceremony and sent wreaths. They slowly walked to Li Jianquan's body, bowed in respect, and expressed their condolences to Li Jianquan's wife, Yang Yinhu, and his family.

Those who attended the ceremony and sent wreaths also included Li Tieying, Wu Xueqian, Song Ping, Rui Xingwen, Yan Mingfu, Wen Jiabao, Wang Ping, Wang Shoudao, Wu Xiuquan, Liu Lantao, Jiang Hua, Yang Dezhi, Yu Quli, Song Shilun, Zhang Jinfu, Chen Pishan, Chen Xilian, Duan Junyi, Ji Pengfei, Huang Zhen, Kang Shien, Xi Zhongxun, Peng Chong, Zhu Lirun, Apuo Awangjinmei, Zhou Guocheng, Ye Fei, Liao Hansheng, Chen Mu Hua, Wang Hanbin, Zou Jiaxuan, Ren Jianxin, Fang Yi, Yang Jingren, Kang Keqing, Qu Wu, Ma Wenrui, Qian Xuesen, Qian Weichang, Hu Sheng, Cheng Sixian, Qian Zhengying, Hong Xuezhi, Liu Huaqing, and others.

Deng Xiaoping, Chen Yun, Li Xiannian, Peng Zhen, Deng Yingchao, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Tian Jiyun, Jiang Zemin, Li Ruihuan, Li Ximing, Yang Ruidai, Qin Jinguang, Ding Guangen, Bo Yibo, Song Renqiong, Li Yimang, Li Desheng, Xiao Ke, Zhang Aiping, Lu Dingyi, Hu Qiaomu, Geng Biao, Huang Hua, Cheng Zihua, Wei Guoqing, Saifuding Aizizi, Yan Jici, Rong Yiren, Ni Zhifu, Fei Xiaotong, Sun Simian, Lei Jianjun, Wang Bingqian, Song Jian, Wang Fang, Li Guixian, Chen Xitong, Chen Junsheng, Liu Fuzhi, Wang Renzhong, Gu Mu, Babula Gelengjie, Hu Ziyang, Zhou Peiyuan, Wang Guangying, Deng Zhaoxiang, Zhao Puji, Ba Jin, Liu Jingji, Wang Enmao, Sun Xiaocun, Lu Jiaxi, Su Buqing, Simayi Aimaiti, Hou Jianru, Ding Guangxun, and others sent wreaths.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, the Central Advisory Commission, the Central Discipline Inspection Commission, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Supreme People's Court, the Supreme People's Procuratorate, the central committees of various democratic parties, relevant central departments and directly affiliated units, people's organizations, the Beijing Municipal Government, the Jiangxi Provincial Government, the Sichuan Provincial Government, and the leadership of Linchuan County in Jiangxi also sent wreaths.

Li Jianquan's ashes will be placed in the Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery at Babaoshan.

专栏：

　　久经考验的忠诚的共产主义战士   
    李井泉遗体告别仪式在京举行   
    赵紫阳杨尚昆李鹏等参加　邓小平陈云李先念等送花圈
    新华社北京５月８日电  党和国家领导人同首都各界１０００多人今天下午在解放军总后勤部礼堂，向中国共产党的优秀党员、久经考验的忠诚的共产主义战士、无产阶级革命家、我军优秀的政治工作者李井泉同志的遗体告别。
    李井泉同志是４月２４日在京病逝的，终年７９岁。他生前曾任第八届中共中央政治局委员、中共中央顾问委员会常委、第五届全国人大常委会副委员长。
    今天，解放军总后勤部礼堂庄严肃穆，哀乐低回。李井泉同志的遗体安放在鲜花翠柏丛中，身上覆盖着中国共产党党旗，两侧肃立着持枪守灵的礼兵。
    赵紫阳、杨尚昆、李鹏、万里、乔石、胡启立、姚依林、王震等，参加告别仪式并送了花圈。他们缓步走到李井泉同志的遗体前鞠躬致哀，并向李井泉同志的夫人杨荫梧及其亲属表示亲切慰问。
    前去向李井泉同志遗体告别并送花圈的还有：李铁映、吴学谦、宋平、芮杏文、阎明复、温家宝、王平、王首道、伍修权、刘澜涛、江华、杨得志、余秋里、宋时轮、张劲夫、陈丕显、陈锡联、段君毅、姬鹏飞、黄镇、康世恩、习仲勋、彭冲、朱学范、阿沛·阿旺晋美、周谷城、叶飞、廖汉生、陈慕华（女）、王汉斌、邹家华、任建新、方毅、杨静仁、康克清（女）、屈武、马文瑞、钱学森、钱伟长、胡绳、程思远、钱正英（女）、洪学智、刘华清等。
    邓小平、陈云、李先念、彭真、邓颖超（女）、徐向前、聂荣臻、田纪云、江泽民、李瑞环、李锡铭、杨汝岱、秦基伟、丁关根、薄一波、宋任穷、李一氓、李德生、肖克、张爱萍、陆定一、胡乔木、耿飚、黄华、程子华、韦国清、赛福鼎·艾则孜、严济慈、荣毅仁、倪志福、费孝通、孙起孟、雷洁琼（女）、王丙乾、宋健、王芳、李贵鲜、陈希同、陈俊生、刘复之、王任重、谷牧、帕巴拉·格列朗杰、胡子昂、周培源、王光英、邓兆祥、赵朴初、巴金、刘靖基、王恩茂、孙晓村、卢嘉锡、苏步青、司马义·艾买提、侯镜如、丁光训等，送了花圈。
    中共中央、全国人大常委会、国务院、中央军委、中顾委、中纪委、全国政协、最高人民法院、最高人民检察院、各民主党派中央，中央有关部门、直属单位，人民团体，北京市、江西省、四川省的领导机关和江西临川县领导机关等，也送了花圈。
    李井泉同志的骨灰将安放在八宝山革命公墓。
