Title: Soviet Pilot Defects, Lands MiG-29 in Turkey
Author:
Date: 1989-05-21
Edition: 3

A Soviet pilot defected and landed a MiG-29 fighter jet in the Turkish city of Trabzon on the Black Sea coast on May 20, apparently seeking political asylum.

An unnamed Turkish official said the fully armed fighter jet was fired upon during its flight to Turkey, injuring the pilot, who has been sent to a hospital. If the pilot requests asylum, it is likely to be granted.

Turkish Foreign Ministry spokesman Inar Batu said Turkey will return the Soviet MiG-29 aircraft to the Soviet Union. The Turkish government has informed the Soviet Union of its decision to return the aircraft.

### 苏一飞行员逃亡  驾米格—２９战斗机降土耳其

1989-05-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏一飞行员逃亡
    驾米格—２９战斗机降土耳其
    新华社安卡拉５月２０日电  据土耳其官方消息说，一名苏联飞行员２０日驾驶一架米格—２９战斗机降落在土耳其的黑海沿岸城市特拉布宗，其目的显然是要求政治避难。
    不愿透露姓名的土官员说，这架全副武装的战斗机在飞往土耳其途中曾遇到了火力截击，飞行员负伤，现已被送往医院。如果这个飞行员提出避难要求可能将得到满足。
    土耳其外交部发言人伊纳尔·巴图说，土将把这架苏联米格—２９飞机交还苏联，土政府已把交还飞机的决定通知苏联。
