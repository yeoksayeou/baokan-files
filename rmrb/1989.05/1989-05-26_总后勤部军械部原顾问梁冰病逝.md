Title: Former Advisor to the General Logistics Department's Armaments Department, Liang Bing, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-05-26
Edition: 2

	Former Advisor to the General Logistics Department's Armaments Department, Liang Bing, passed away due to illness. The New China News Agency reports from Beijing on May 24: An excellent Party member of the Communist Party of China, a loyal communist warrior, an outstanding leader in the military's armaments work, and former advisor to the General Logistics Department's Armaments Department, Comrade Liang Bing, passed away due to illness in Beijing on May 11. He was 71 years old.

	Comrade Liang Bing was from Baoding, Hebei Province. He joined the revolutionary cause in 1936, enlisted in the army in 1937, and joined the Communist Party of China in 1938. He served in various positions, including soldier, squad leader, platoon leader, political instructor, political commissar of a regiment, acting division commander, director of the Weapons Procurement Bureau of the General Logistics Department's Armaments Department, and director of the General Logistics Department's Armaments Department. During the war years, he participated in major battles such as the Liaoshen and Pingjin campaigns.

	During the Korean War, he participated in the Third, Fourth, and Fifth Campaigns and defensive operations. After the founding of the People's Republic of China, he long served in leadership positions in the military's armaments sector, dedicating all his efforts to ensuring the military's victory in combat and the modernization of armaments work.

### 总后勤部军械部原顾问梁冰病逝

1989-05-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　总后勤部军械部原顾问梁冰病逝
    新华社北京５月２４日电  中国共产党的优秀党员、忠诚的共产主义战士、我军优秀的军械工作领导干部、总后军械部原顾问梁冰同志因病于５月１１日在北京逝世，终年７１岁。
    梁冰同志是河北省保定市人，１９３６年参加革命工作，１９３７年入伍，１９３８年加入中国共产党。历任战士、班长、排长、政治指导员、团政治委员、代理师长、总后军械部武器订购局局长、总后军械部部长等职。战争年代，他参加过辽沈、平津等重大战役。
    在抗美援朝中参加过第三、四、五次战役和防御作战。中华人民共和国成立后，他长期担任我军军械部门的领导职务，为保障我军的作战胜利，为军械工作的现代化建设付出全部心血。
