Title: Gorbachev Says Upon Returning to Moscow: We Have Achieved a Great Thing
Author: Shēn Fǎliáng
Date: 1989-05-19
Edition: 3

Gorbachev said upon returning to Moscow: We have achieved a great thing

According to Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 18 (Reporter Shen Faliang) Soviet leader Gorbachev said late on the 18th at Moscow's Vnukovo-2 Airport to the Chinese Embassy's Chargé d'Affaires Zhang Zhen, who came to welcome him back from his visit to China: "We have achieved a great thing. It can be said that both we and you are satisfied. This visit has been successful in all aspects, and our relations have entered a new stage."

Those who returned to Moscow on the same plane included Soviet Foreign Minister Shevardnadze, Politburo member Yakovlev, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Maslyukov, and Minister of Health Chazov, who accompanied him on his visit to China.

Soviet party and government leaders Ryzhkov, Medvedev, Ligachov, and others came to the airport to welcome them.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 19, Soviet people from all walks of life warmly welcome the normalization of Sino-Soviet relations, believing that this is a major event in today's world and will play an immeasurable role in the development of relations between the two countries and the stability of the Asia-Pacific region and the world situation.

Soviet Minister of Foreign Economic Relations Katyshev told our reporter, "This is indeed a historic visit, something that the vast majority of Soviet people have been looking forward to for a long time, and a major event supported by the Chinese people. I myself have been looking forward to it for 20 or 30 years."

Soviet famous scholar Albatrov academician said, the disappearance of military confrontation on the longest land border in the world is conducive to the relaxation of the Far East and the international situation, and China, the Soviet Union and their neighboring countries will have greater security.

### 戈尔巴乔夫回到莫斯科时说  我们完成了一件大事
沈法良
1989-05-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　戈尔巴乔夫回到莫斯科时说     
    我们完成了一件大事
    据新华社莫斯科５月１８日电（记者沈法良）苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫１８日晚在莫斯科伏努科沃二号机场对迎接他访华归来的中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办张震说：“我们完成了一件大事。应该说，我们和你们都感到满意。这次访问在各个方面都取得了成功，我们的关系进入了一个新的阶段。”
    同机返回莫斯科的有陪同他访华的苏联外交部长谢瓦尔德纳泽、苏共中央政治局委员雅科夫列夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席马斯柳科夫和卫生部长恰佐夫等人。
    苏联党政领导人雷日科夫、梅德韦杰夫、利加乔夫等到机场迎接。
    据新华社莫斯科５月１９日电　苏联各界人士热烈欢迎苏中两国关系正常化，认为这是当今世界的一个重大事件，对今后两国关系的发展和亚太地区及世界局势的稳定起着不可估量的作用。
    苏联对外经济联络部长卡图谢夫对本社记者说，“这确实是一次具有历史意义的访问，是绝大多数苏联人盼望已久的，也是中国人民拥护的重大事件。我本人也为此期待了二三十年。”
    苏联著名学者阿尔巴托夫院士说，世界上最长的陆地边界上军事对抗的消失，对远东和国际缓和都有促进作用，中苏及其邻国都将有更大的安全感。
