Title: A Villager in Shandong Province Establishes a Stone Carving Craft Factory
Author: Zhang Yaxin
Date: 1989-05-06
Edition: 5

    In Shangdianzi Village, Jiaxiang County, Shandong Province, farmer Du Yunbiao has established a stone carving craft factory, leveraging the local abundance of green stone and the historical tradition of stone carving.

    This report was photographed by Zhang Yaxin, a reporter for this newspaper.
