Title: The "Zheng He" Ship Ends Its Visit to the United States
Author:
Date: 1989-05-04
Edition: 2

The Chinese People's Navy training ship "Zheng He" successfully concluded its visit to the United States' Hawaii and returned to Qingdao on May 2.

The "Zheng He" ship set sail from the Qingdao naval base on March 31 and arrived at Pearl Harbor, Hawaii, on April 11. During the week-long visit, the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Navy engaged in extensive friendly exchanges with the officers and soldiers of the U.S. Navy. The commander of the North Sea Fleet, Major General Ma Xinchun, who led the ship on the visit, was invited to Washington, D.C., where he met with the principal leaders of the U.S. Navy.

### “郑和”舰结束对美国访问

1989-05-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　“郑和”舰结束对美国访问
    新华社青岛五月三日电  中国人民海军“郑和号”训练舰圆满结束了对美国夏威夷的访问，于五月二日返回青岛。
    “郑和”舰三月三十一日从青岛军港启航，四月十一日抵达夏威夷珍珠港。在为期一周的访问中，中国人民海军官兵与美国海军官兵进行了广泛友好的交往。率舰出访的北海舰队司令员马辛春中将应邀前往华盛顿，会见了美国海军主要领导。
