Title: Cyclone Strikes Bangladesh
Author:
Date: 1989-05-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Dhaka, May 28th.

A severe cyclone with wind speeds reaching 90 kilometers per hour struck southern, southwestern, and northwestern parts of Bangladesh yesterday afternoon, resulting in at least 12 deaths and thousands of injuries.

According to official sources, many people went missing as the cyclone swept through coastal areas. There are concerns that approximately 150 fishermen may have drowned.

Currently, communication between the capital Dhaka and the affected regions has been disrupted. Thousands of houses have been destroyed, and tens of thousands of hectares of crops have been severely damaged.

### 旋风袭击孟加拉国

1989-05-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　旋风袭击孟加拉国
    新华社达卡５月２８日电  一场时速达９０公里的剧烈旋风昨天下午袭击了孟加拉国南部、西南部和西北部，至少有１２人死亡，数千人受伤。
    据官方人士说，当旋风刮过沿海地区时，有许多人失踪。人们担心约有１５０名渔民可能已被淹死。
    目前，孟加拉国首都达卡和受灾地区之间的通讯已经中断，数千座房屋被夷为平地，成千上万公顷的农作物也受到了严重影响。
