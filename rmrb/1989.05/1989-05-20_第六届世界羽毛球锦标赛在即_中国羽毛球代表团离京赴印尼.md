Title: The Sixth World Badminton Championships Are Imminent
Author: Zhou Zongxin Liang Jinxiong
Date: 1989-05-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 20th.

Some members of the Chinese badminton team departed Beijing this afternoon by airplane, traveling via Singapore to Jakarta, Indonesia, to participate in the first Sudirman Cup World Mixed Team Championships, which will be held from May 24th to 28th.

From May 29th to June 4th, they will also participate, along with other team members, in the Sixth World Badminton Championships, which will be held in Jakarta. The remaining team members will depart for Indonesia next week.

### 第六届世界羽毛球锦标赛在即  中国羽毛球代表团离京赴印尼
周宗欣　梁金雄
1989-05-20
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　第六届世界羽毛球锦标赛在即
    中国羽毛球代表团离京赴印尼
    新华社北京５月２０日电  （记者周宗欣 梁金雄）中国羽毛球代表团部分成员今天下午乘飞机离开北京，经新加坡去印度尼西亚首都雅加达参加５月２４日至２８日举行的首届苏迪曼杯世界混合团体赛。
    ５月２９日至６月４日，他们还将同代表团其他成员一起，参加在雅加达举行的第六届世界羽毛球锦标赛。代表团其他成员将在下周离京赴印尼。
