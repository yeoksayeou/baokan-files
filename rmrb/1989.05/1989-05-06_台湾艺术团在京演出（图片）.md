Title: Taiwan Art Troupe Performs in Beijing
Author: Lǐ Xuérén
Date: 1989-05-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 6th.

On May 6th, the "Huaxia Cultural Art Delegation" from Taiwan held a "Huaxia Cultural Showcase" event at the Beijing International Hotel. Nearly 20 models from Beijing and Taiwan showcased nearly 200 outfits designed by the renowned Taiwanese fashion designer Cai Mengxia.

The article is accompanied by a photograph taken by Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Xueren.

### 台湾艺术团在京演出（图片）
李学仁
1989-05-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

   台湾艺术团在京演出
    5月6日，台湾“华夏文化艺术访问团”在北京国际饭店举行“华夏文化展演”活动。来自北京和台湾的近20名模特展示了台湾著名时装设计师蔡孟夏女士设计的近200套服装。
    新华社记者　李学仁摄
