Title: The First Domestic Trade Enterprise in the Wool Textile Industry
Author: Meng Renquan
Date: 1989-05-22
Page: 5

The "old brand" enterprise Xinxin Woolen Textile Dyeing Factory in Wuxi became the first domestic trade enterprise in the wool textile industry nationwide in the second half of last year. The factory has been meticulously focusing on quality and emphasizing business credibility. This year, its products have been sold to 45 countries and regions, earning over 20 million USD in foreign exchange. The image shows foreign business clients directly visiting the factory to discuss business and inspect the textile products. Photographed by reporter Meng Renquan of this newspaper.
