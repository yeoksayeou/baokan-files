Title: Tornado and Hailstorm Strike Six Villages in Tunchang
Author:
Date: 1989-05-04
Edition: 2

    A rare tornado and hailstorm hit six villages in Nanlu and Wupo Towns of Tunchang County, Hainan Province, from May 1 to May 3. The largest hailstones weighed up to 5 kilograms.

    The disaster resulted in one death, six injuries, and damage to over 2,000 homes and 6,400 acres of crops. The agricultural losses alone amounted to 2.4 million yuan.

### 龙卷风冰雹袭击屯昌六村

1989-05-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　龙卷风冰雹袭击屯昌六村
    新华社海口５月４日电  ５月１日至３日下午，海南省屯昌县南吕镇和乌坡镇的６个村遭到历史上罕见的龙卷风和冰雹袭击，其中最大的冰雹有５公斤重。
    这次灾害，造成１人死亡，６人受伤，２０００余间民房和６４００亩农作物被打坏。仅农作物的损失价值２４０万元。
