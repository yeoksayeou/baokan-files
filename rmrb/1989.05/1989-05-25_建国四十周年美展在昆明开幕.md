Title: National Art Exhibition for the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation Opens in Kunming
Author: Dù Kuíchāng
Date: 1989-05-25
Edition: 4

The national printmaking exhibition was recently held in Kunming. This is the first genre of art to be exhibited as part of the national art exhibition celebrating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the nation. The remaining 12 genres will be exhibited in Beijing, Shenyang, Shanghai, Nanjing, Guangzhou, and other 7 cities, and then the award-winning works from each genre will be brought together for a grand exhibition in Beijing during the National Day celebrations.

The 408 printmaking works currently on display at the Yunnan Art Museum were selected by the judges from over 700 works submitted by the art associations of 30 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions across the country. These printmaking works, with their unique expression techniques, elegant and charming charm, and rich and varied themes, have aroused great interest among the audiences in the Spring City.

### 建国四十周年美展在昆明开幕
杜奎昌
1989-05-25
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　建国四十周年美展在昆明开幕
    新华社昆明电  （记者杜奎昌）全国版画展最近在昆明举行。这是庆祝建国４０周年全国美展开始展出的第一个画种。其余１２个画种，将分别在北京、沈阳、上海、南京、广州等７个城市展出，然后将各画种展出的获奖作品集中起来，国庆节期间在北京举行总展。
    这次在云南美术馆展出的４０８件版画作品，是评委们从全国３０个省、市、自治区美协送来的７００多件作品中精选出来的。这些版画作品，以其独特的表现手法、优美别致的韵味、丰富多采的题材，引起了春城观众的浓厚兴趣。
