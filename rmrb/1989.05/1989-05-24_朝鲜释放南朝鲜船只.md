Title: North Korea Releases South Korean Vessel
Author:
Date: 1989-05-24
Edition: 3

North Korea today released a South Korean vessel that had been detained for intruding into its territorial waters.

The Korean Central News Agency reported today that the vessel was seized on May 7 after it intruded into North Korean waters in the West Sea. The four crew members on board admitted to their mistake of illegally entering North Korean waters. North Korean authorities, considering their attitude, decided to handle the matter leniently.

### 朝鲜释放南朝鲜船只

1989-05-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　朝鲜释放南朝鲜船只
    据新华社平壤５月２３日电  朝鲜今天释放了一艘因入侵北方海域而被扣押的南朝鲜船只。
    朝鲜中央通讯社今天报道说，这艘船是５月７日在朝鲜西海侵入北方海域时被扣押的，船上的４名船员承认了自己非法侵入北方水域的过失。朝鲜有关方面根据他们的态度，决定给予宽大处理。
