Title: UN Secretary-General Urges Acceleration of Resolution to Central American Crisis
Author:
Date: 1989-05-29
Edition: 1

UN Secretary-General Urges Acceleration of Resolution to Central American Crisis

According to Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, May 28th: Guatemala City news: UN Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar on the 28th in Guatemala City called for accelerating the resolution of the Central American crisis.

De Cuéllar said upon arriving in Guatemala City that the current negotiation process in the Central American region has reached a stalemate. However, this meeting, due to its very specific goals, will take a decisive step in solving the problems in the Central American region.

He also revealed that he would meet with the President, Foreign Minister, and other leaders of Guatemala to discuss ways to resolve the conflict in Central America.

### 联合国秘书长呼吁  加速解决中美洲危机

1989-05-29
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　联合国秘书长呼吁
    加速解决中美洲危机
    据新华社墨西哥城五月二十八日电  危地马拉城消息：联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔二十八日在危地马拉城呼吁加速解决中美洲危机。
    德奎利亚尔在抵达危地马拉城时发表讲话说，目前，中美洲地区的谈判进程陷入僵局。但是，这次大会由于有很具体的目标，它将在解决中美洲地区问题方面迈出决定性的一步。
    他还透露，他将同危地马拉总统、外长和其他领导人就解决中美洲冲突的方式进行会晤。
