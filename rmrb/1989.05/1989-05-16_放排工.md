Title: The Log Driver
Author: Jiang Sanli
Date: 1989-05-16
Page: 8

The gentle pats on both banks
Have lulled the world to sleep
Moonlight and dreams drift along with the current
The rough log-driving songs
Have carried away the carefree days under the sun
In the dead of night, the distant flute on the shore
Accompanied by the sound of oars splashing,
Shakes the heart
The sleepless one
Uses longing to discern the direction
In a place where no footprints can be left
Footprints are left… and
The loneliness that is trodden upon
Rumbles like thunder

### 放排工
蒋三立
1989-05-16
第8版(副刊)
专栏：大地

　　放排工
    蒋三立
    拍遍两岸的温柔
    抚睡了这世界
    月光和梦也顺水而漂
    粗犷的排歌
    带走了太阳下无所牵挂的日子
    深夜，岸上远处的洞箫
    伴着掌掉的桨声，震颤着心
    那个睡不上觉的人
    用思念分辨着方向
    在留不了脚印的地方
    　　留下了脚印……并且
    被踏响的寂寞也声震如雷
