Title: Polish Consultative Council Calls for Implementing the Spirit of the Round Table Conference
Author:
Date: 1989-05-20
Edition: 3

The Polish Consultative Council today held a meeting, calling on all Polish political forces to implement the spirit of the "Round Table Conference," making the upcoming June elections in Poland "an important stage in the process of establishing a new democratic order in Poland."

The Polish Consultative Council is a permanent body of the "Round Table Conference." Its tasks include overseeing the implementation of the "Round Table Conference" resolutions, promoting the smooth resolution of major domestic issues, and mediating in cases of social conflict.

### 波兰协商委员会号召  贯彻圆桌会议精神

1989-05-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　波兰协商委员会号召
    贯彻圆桌会议精神
    新华社华沙５月１９日电  波兰协商委员会今天举行会议，号召波兰各政治力量贯彻“圆桌会议”精神，使波兰将于６月举行的竞选活动成为“建立波兰民主新秩序过程中的一个重要阶段”。
    波兰协商委员会是“圆桌会议”的常设机构。
    它的任务是检查“圆桌会议”决议的执行情况，促进国内重大问题的顺利解决，以及在发生社会冲突情况下行使调停职能。
