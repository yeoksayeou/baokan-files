Title: Hands
Author: Dù Mèifēn
Date: 1989-05-28
Edition: 8

Friends, my friends,
My young friends!
Across the vast distance of ten thousand miles,
You wave to me.
Let me hold your arms,
Let me grasp your hands,
Laboring hands, creating hands,
Hands ready to offer to humanity.
Let me see your hands,
With chalk dust, with ink stains,
With formaldehyde traces...
But never stained with fresh blood.
Though they have not been wiped
On perfume and handkerchiefs,
They have solemnly sworn,
These hands are clean hands.
In China, among the eleven billion hands,
How many pairs are there
That scheme and plot, that are enemies of the people,
Evil black hands?
				May 17th evening
				In the aircraft cabin above the Pacific Ocean

### 手
杜妹芬
1989-05-28
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　手
    杜妹芬
    朋友啊朋友，
    我年轻的朋友！
    迢遥万里外，
    你们向我招手。
    让我挽住你们的臂，
    让我握住你们的手，
    劳动的手，创造的手，
    准备向人类奉献的手。
    让我看看你们的手，
    有粉笔末，有墨痕，
    有福尔马林水渍……
    但是从来没有沾染鲜血啊。
    虽然没有在
    香水餐巾上擦过，
    但是曾经庄严地宣誓，
    这样的手是干净的手。
    中国：十一亿双手中
    到底一共有几双
    玩弄阴谋的、与人民为敌的
    罪恶的黑手？
      　　　　　　五月十七日黄昏
      　　　　　太平洋上机舱中
