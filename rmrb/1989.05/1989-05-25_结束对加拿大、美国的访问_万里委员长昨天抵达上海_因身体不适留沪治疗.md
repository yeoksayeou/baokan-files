Title: Ending Visits to Canada and the United States
Author: Chen Mao Di
Date: 1989-05-25
Edition: 1

National People's Congress Standing Committee Chairman Wan Li concluded his visits to Canada and the United States and returned from the United States. He arrived in Shanghai at 3 a.m. today.

Shanghai Municipal Party Secretary Jiang Zemin, Standing Committee of the Shanghai People's Congress Chairman Ye Gongqi, and Mayor Zhu Rongji, among others, went to the airport to welcome him.

Chairman Wan Li is experiencing health issues. After a medical examination, he will remain in Shanghai for treatment.

### 结束对加拿大、美国的访问  万里委员长昨天抵达上海  因身体不适留沪治疗
陈毛弟
1989-05-25
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　结束对加拿大、美国的访问
    万里委员长昨天抵达上海
    因身体不适留沪治疗
    新华社上海５月２５日电  （记者陈毛弟）全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长万里结束了对加拿大、美国的访问，从美国回国，于今天凌晨３时抵达上海。
    中共上海市委书记江泽民、市人大常委会主任叶公琦、市长朱熔基等同志前往机场迎接。
    万里委员长因身体不适，经医生检查，留沪治疗。
