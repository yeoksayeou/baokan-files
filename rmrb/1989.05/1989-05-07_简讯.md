Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1989-05-07
Edition: 2

	▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Chen Muhua held a working meeting with the delegation of the Spanish Senate led by President Jose Federico de Cavahal Perez at the Great Hall of the People on the morning of May 6.

	▲ Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Qichen met with President of the Spanish Senate Jose Federico de Cavahal Perez and his delegation in Beijing on the afternoon of May 6. Both sides exchanged views on bilateral relations and international issues of mutual concern.

	▲ Vice Premier and Minister of Finance Wang Bingqian hosted a dinner for Indian Finance Minister Chavan and his delegation at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on the evening of May 6. Chavan came to Beijing to attend the 22nd Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors of the Asian Development Bank.

	▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liao Hansheng hosted a dinner for the delegation of the Spanish Senate led by President Federico de Cavahal in Beijing on May 7.

	▲ Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Qichen met and hosted a dinner for Japanese Foreign Minister Uno Sōsuke, who was passing through Beijing on his way back to Japan after visiting the Soviet Union and Mongolia, on May 7. Both sides exchanged views on international issues of mutual concern.

	▲ Vice Premier and Minister of Finance Wang Bingqian met and hosted a dinner for Fiji's Finance Minister Kamikamica and his delegation in Beijing on May 7. Kamikamica was on an official friendly visit to China after attending the 22nd Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors of the Asian Development Bank, which had just concluded in Beijing. The Fijian guests will visit Hangzhou, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Shenzhen.

	▲ Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin met and hosted a dinner for the Nepalese government's economic and trade delegation led by Finance Minister Pradhan in Beijing on May 7.

### 简讯

1989-05-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　简讯
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长陈慕华６日上午在人民大会堂同由何塞·费德里科·德卡瓦哈尔·佩雷斯议长率领的西班牙参议院代表团举行工作会谈。
    ▲外交部长钱其琛６日下午在北京会见了西班牙参议院议长何塞·费德里科·德卡瓦哈尔·佩雷斯和他率领的参议院代表团。双方就双边关系和共同关心的国际问题交换了意见。
    ▲国务委员兼财政部部长王丙乾６日晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请印度财政部长恰范一行。恰范是来京参加亚洲开发银行理事会第２２届年会的。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长廖汉生５月７日在北京宴请了由参议长费德里科·德卡瓦哈尔率领的西班牙参议院代表团。
    ▲外交部长钱其琛７日在北京会见并宴请了出访苏联和蒙古后途经北京回国的日本外相宇野宗佑，双方就共同关心的国际问题交换了意见。
    ▲国务委员兼财政部长王丙乾７日在北京会见并宴请了斐济财政部长卡米卡米萨一行。卡米卡米萨是在出席刚刚在北京结束的亚洲开发银行理事会第２２届年会之后对中国进行正式友好访问的。斐济客人将前往杭州、上海、广州和深圳进行参观访问。
    ▲对外经济贸易部部长郑拓彬７日在北京会见并宴请了由财政大臣普拉丹率领的尼泊尔王国政府经济贸易代表团。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　（新华社）
