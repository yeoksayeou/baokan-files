Title: Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Says One Country Has No Right to Interfere in Another's Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1989-05-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 15th.

Foreign Ministry Spokesperson said
One Country Has No Right to Interfere in Another's Internal Affairs

The Foreign Ministry spokesperson said today here that China has always advocated that a country's internal affairs should be managed by its own people, and other countries have no right and should not interfere under any pretext.

The spokesperson made these remarks in response to a reporter's question about China's comments on the recent speech by the U.S. President regarding the situation in Panama.

### 外交部发言人说  一国无权干涉别国内政

1989-05-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外交部发言人说    
    一国无权干涉别国内政
    新华社北京５月１５日电  外交部发言人今天在这里说，中国一贯主张一个国家的内部事务应由本国人民自己来管，别的国家无权也不应以任何借口进行干涉。
    发言人是在回答记者有关中国对最近美国总统关于巴拿马局势的讲话有何评论时说这番话的。
