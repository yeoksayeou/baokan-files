Title: Youth, Sun, Hope
Author: Zeng Jigang
Date: 1989-05-03
Page: 8

Youth, Sun, Hope

In commemoration of the 70th anniversary of the May Fourth Movement, this poster by Zeng Jigang captures the spirit of the era. The vibrant colors and dynamic composition reflect the energy and optimism of the youth, symbolizing the dawn of a new era. The sun, a universal symbol of hope and progress, shines brightly, illuminating the path forward. The youth, with their faces turned towards the future, embody the determination and resilience of a generation ready to take on the challenges of the time. This artwork serves as a powerful reminder of the enduring legacy of the May Fourth Movement and the unyielding spirit of the Chinese people.
