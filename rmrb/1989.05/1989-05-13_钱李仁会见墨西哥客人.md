Title: Qian Liren Meets with Mexican Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-05-13
Page: 2

People's Daily reports: On the morning of May 13th, Qian Liren, the president of the People's Daily, met with Yuli Julius, a columnist and international coordinator for the Mexican newspaper *Excélsior*, who is in China to cover the high-level meeting between China and the Soviet Union.

### 钱李仁会见墨西哥客人

1989-05-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　钱李仁会见墨西哥客人
    本报讯  五月十三日上午，人民日报社社长钱李仁在人民日报社会见了来华采访中苏高级会晤的墨西哥《至上报》专栏作家、国际协调员尤里·朱里奥斯。
