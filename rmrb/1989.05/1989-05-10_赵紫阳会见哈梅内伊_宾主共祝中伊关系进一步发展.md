Zhao Ziyang Meets with Khamenei

Both Sides Wish for Further Development of Sino-Iranian Relations

	BEIJING, May 10 (Xinhua) — General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhao Ziyang, met with the visiting President of the Islamic Republic of Iran, Ayatollah Seyed Ali Khamenei, in Zhongnanhai this afternoon. Zhao welcomed Khamenei's visit, saying it was a significant event in the history of Sino-Iranian relations following the visit of Speaker Rafsanjani in 1985.

	Khamenei expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome he received. He said he believed this visit would further promote the development of relations between Iran and China.

	Zhao analyzed the current international situation to Khamenei. He pointed out that Iran has enormous potential for development. He believed that Iran, with the current favorable international situation and the achievement of a ceasefire, would make further efforts to develop itself.

	When discussing the Iran-Iraq conflict, Zhao expressed his delight that both sides had accepted UN Security Council Resolution 598 and achieved a ceasefire. He emphasized that as a friend of both Iran and Iraq, China hoped the two countries would show mutual understanding and compromise to achieve permanent peace as soon as possible.

	Finally, Zhao asked Khamenei to convey his greetings to Speaker Rafsanjani upon his return to Iran.

### 赵紫阳会见哈梅内伊  宾主共祝中伊关系进一步发展
孙毅
1989-05-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　赵紫阳会见哈梅内伊    
    宾主共祝中伊关系进一步发展
    本报北京５月１０日讯  记者孙毅报道：中共中央总书记赵紫阳今天下午在中南海会见应邀来访的伊朗伊斯兰共和国总统霍贾特伊斯兰·赛义德·阿里·哈梅内伊时说，总统阁下的这次来访是继拉夫桑贾尼议长１９８５年的来访之后，中伊关系史上的又一件大事，对此表示热烈欢迎。
    哈梅内伊总统对这次来访受到热烈欢迎表示感谢。他说，相信这次来访一定会进一步推动伊中两国关系的发展。
    赵紫阳向哈梅内伊介绍了他对国际形势的分析。他指出，伊朗在发展中有着巨大的潜力。相信伊朗在当前有利的国际形势和已经实现停火的基础上，进一步努力发展自己。
    谈到两伊争端时，赵紫阳对双方接受联合国安理会５９８号决议、实现停火表示高兴。他强调，中国作为两伊的朋友，希望两国互谅、互让，尽早实现永久和平。
    最后，赵紫阳请哈梅内伊回国后转达他对拉夫桑贾尼议长的问候。（附图片）
