Title: A Farewell Ceremony for Yang Xiguang, an Excellent Leader in the Field of Propaganda and Education
Author:
Date: 1989-05-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 24th.

The farewell ceremony for the late Yang Xiguang, an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, a loyal communist warrior, and an excellent leader in the field of propaganda and education, was held this afternoon at the Baobing Revolutionary Cemetery Hall.

Yang Xiguang passed away on May 14 due to illness in Beijing at the age of 74.

During his lifetime, Yang Xiguang served as a member of the Standing Committee of the 6th and 7th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and as the editor-in-chief and advisor of the Guangming Daily. In May 1978, when he was the editor-in-chief of the Guangming Daily, he oversaw the revision and boldly decided to publish the special commentary article "Practice is the Sole Criterion for Testing Truth" by a special commentator of the Guangming Daily. This sparked a nationwide discussion on the criterion of truth, preparing public opinion and ideologically for the 11th Plenary Session of the 3rd Central Committee to re-establish the Marxist ideological line, making a significant historical contribution.

Li Xiannian, Deng Yingchao, Hu Qili, Rui Xiongwen, Yan Mingfu, Lu Dingyi, Chen Pishan, Xi Zhongxun, Peng Chong, Zhou Guocheng, Yan Jici, Ye Fei, Wang Renzhong, Fang Yi, Gu Mu, Yang Jingren, Kang Keqing, Pabala Geleinrangjie, Hu Ziyang, Zhou Peiyuan, Wang Guangying, Deng Zhaoqiang, Zhao Puxiu, Qu Wu, Ba Jin, Ma Wenrui, Liu Jingji, Wang Enmao, Qian Xuesen, Qian Weichang, Hu Sheng, Sun Xiaocun, Cheng Siyuan, Lu Jiashi, Qian Zhengying, Su Buqing, Sima Yi Aimaidi, and others sent wreaths.

Yan Mingfu, Ma Wenrui, Qian Zhengying, and others, along with over 300 people from various sectors in the capital, friends of Yang Xiguang, and staff of the Guangming Daily, attended the farewell ceremony.

### 宣传教育战线上的优秀领导工作者  杨西光同志遗体告别仪式在京举行

1989-05-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    宣传教育战线上的优秀领导工作者
    杨西光同志遗体告别仪式在京举行
    新华社北京5月24日电  向中国共产党的优秀党员，忠诚的共产主义战士，宣传、教育战线上的优秀领导工作者杨西光同志的遗体告别仪式，今天下午在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。
    杨西光同志是5月14日因病在北京逝世的，终年74岁。
    杨西光同志生前曾任第六、七届全国政协常务委员，光明日报总编辑、顾问。1978年5月，他任光明日报总编辑时，曾主持修改并果敢地决定发表了《实践是检验真理的唯一标准》的光明日报特约评论员文章，引发了在全国范围内开展的关于真理标准问题的讨论，为十一届三中全会重新确立马克思主义的思想路线做舆论上和思想上的准备做了大量工作，作出了历史性的贡献。
    李先念、邓颖超、胡启立、芮杏文、阎明复、陆定一、陈丕显、习仲勋、彭冲、周谷城、严济慈、叶飞、王任重、方毅、谷牧、杨静仁、康克清、帕巴拉·格列朗杰、胡子昂、周培源、王光英、邓兆祥、赵朴初、屈武、巴金、马文瑞、刘靖基、王恩茂、钱学森、钱伟长、胡绳、孙晓村、程思远、卢嘉锡、钱正英、苏步青、司马义·艾买提等送了花圈。
    阎明复、马文瑞、钱正英等同志以及首都各界和杨西光同志生前友好、光明日报社职工300多人，参加了告别仪式。
