Title: The Los Angeles Amateur Sports Foundation Awards Xu Yanmei
Author:
Date: 1989-05-13
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency,

    The Los Angeles Amateur Sports Foundation has awarded the "1988 World Cup Award" to Xu Yanmei, an outstanding Chinese diver. The trophy was received on behalf of Xu Yanmei by the Chinese Consulate General in Los Angeles on May 9.

    Other recipients of the "1988 World Cup Award" include the famous American sprinter Joyner, the Soviet gymnastics star Artemov, and others.

### 美一体育基金会向许艳梅颁奖

1989-05-13
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　美一体育基金会向许艳梅颁奖
    中国杰出跳水运动员许艳梅荣获了洛杉矶业余体育运动基金会颁发的“１９８８年世界杯奖”。奖杯已由中国洛杉矶总领事馆在５月９日代为领取。
    同时荣获“１９８８年世界杯奖”的还有：美国著名短跑女将乔伊纳、苏联体操名将阿尔捷莫夫等。　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
