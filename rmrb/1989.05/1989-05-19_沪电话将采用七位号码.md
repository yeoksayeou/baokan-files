Title: Shanghai to Adopt Seven-Digit Phone Numbers
Author: Liu Shi'an  Bao Wenjun
Date: 1989-05-19
Edition: 2

Shanghai will become the first city in the country to uniformly implement a seven-digit phone number system starting this November.

An official from the Shanghai Post and Telecommunications Administration introduced that after nearly five years of preparation, the seven-digit dialing project, one of the major technical renovation projects for Shanghai's telephones, has basically completed the complex technical renovation tasks. The total investment for this project exceeds 10 million yuan.

Currently, the capacity of Shanghai's 400,000 telephone exchange devices is insufficient to meet user needs, and the six-digit number system has been in use for over 30 years. After implementing the seven-digit number system, Shanghai's telephone number resources will increase tenfold, accommodating 800 exchanges and 400 to 500 million telephone exchange devices. This will create conditions to alleviate the tense telecommunication situation and resolve the issues of "difficulty in installing, dialing, and connecting" telephones.

### 沪电话将采用七位号码
刘士安　包文俊
1989-05-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　沪电话将采用七位号码
    本报上海五月十九日电  今年十一月，上海将成为全国第一个统一实行电话七位号码制的城市。
    上海市邮电管理局的官员介绍，经过近五年的准备，作为上海电话重大技术改造项目之一的七位拨号工程，已经基本完成复杂的技术改建任务。该项工程的总投资超过一千万元。
    目前，上海拥有的四十余万门电话交换设备容量难以满足用户需要，六位号码制已使用了三十多年。实行七位号码制以后，上海电话号码资源扩大了十倍，即具有八百个电话局所、四百至五百万门交换设备的容量，这为缓解电信紧张状况，解决电话“难装、难打、难通”的矛盾创造了条件。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（刘士安　包文俊）
