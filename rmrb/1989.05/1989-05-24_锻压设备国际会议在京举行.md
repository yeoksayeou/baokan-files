Title: International Conference on Forging and Pressing Equipment Held in Beijing
Author: Jin Rendi
Date: 1989-05-24
Page: 2

The first international conference on the research and design of forging and pressing equipment was held from May 22 to 24 at Tsinghua University. The 69 representatives attending the conference came from 10 different countries. During the three-day conference, the representatives presented a total of 70 papers, of which 47 were from Chinese representatives.

### 锻压设备国际会议在京举行
金仁弟
1989-05-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    锻压设备国际会议在京举行
    本报讯  第一届国际锻压设备研究及设计会议5月22日至24日在清华大学召开。参加会议的69名代表分别来自10个国家。在3天的会议中，代表们共发表论文70篇，其中我国代表的论文47篇。（金仁弟）
