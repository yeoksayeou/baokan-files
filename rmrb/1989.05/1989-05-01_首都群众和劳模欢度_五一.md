Title: Capital Residents and Labor Models Celebrate "May Day"
Author: Xiaojiaobao Yangqing
Date: 1989-05-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 1st - Thousands of residents and labor models in the capital, Beijing, celebrated the annual May Day International Labor Day under clear skies. They visited various entertainment venues to enjoy the festivities.

Today, Tiananmen Square was filled with a festive atmosphere. The tall buildings surrounding the square were adorned with fluttering red flags. The gates of Tiananmen were decorated with hanging lanterns, with a portrait of Chairman Mao Zedong prominently displayed in the center. A large portrait of Dr. Sun Yat-sen was newly placed in front of the Monument to the People's Heroes. The square was bustling with visitors, many of whom took commemorative photos with Tiananmen as the backdrop. The Great Hall of the People was also open to the public as usual, with long lines of visitors.

In the evening, the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions held a vibrant "May Day" garden party for capital labor models at the Workers' Cultural Palace. Over 15,000 labor models, representatives of labor model collectives, and their family members from across the city gathered to celebrate, filling the entire palace with a festive atmosphere.

Leaders from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions visited to extend their greetings to the labor models. They hoped that the labor models would uphold the glorious traditions of the working class, adhere to the four cardinal principles during the rectification and deepening of reforms, maintain stability, and exhibit a sense of ownership and creativity in production and work, making new contributions.

### 首都群众和劳模欢度“五一”
萧家保　杨青
1989-05-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　首都群众和劳模欢度“五一”
    新华社北京５月１日电（记者萧家保、杨青）首都北京今天迎来了一年一度的“五一”国际劳动节。成千上万的群众和劳动模范在风和日丽中来到各个游乐场所，欢庆节日。
    今天的天安门广场一派节日气氛。广场四周的高大建筑物上都高高飘扬着红旗。天安门城楼上宫灯高悬，正中是毛泽东主席的画像。人民英雄纪念碑前新安放了孙中山先生的巨幅画像。广场上游人如织，纷纷以天安门为背景，拍下一张有意义的纪念照片。人民大会堂也像往常一样对外开放，参观者的队伍排成一字长蛇。
    晚上，北京市总工会在劳动人民文化宫举行丰富多彩的首都劳动模范“五一”游园晚会，全市历届劳动模范、劳动模范集体的代表及其家属共１５０００多人欢聚在一起，节日气氛弥漫了整个文化宫。
    中华全国总工会和北京市总工会的领导到这里向劳模们表示节日的慰问。他们希望劳模们发扬工人阶级的光荣传统，在治理整顿和深化改革中，坚持四项基本原则，维护大局，保持安定，在生产和工作中发挥主人翁精神和创造精神，再做新贡献。
