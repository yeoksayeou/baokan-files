Title: Two Types of Reagent Kits Passed Evaluation
Author: Dong Yuwen Zhang Mingwang
Date: 1989-05-05
Page: 2

Bo Dong Yuwen Zhang Mingwang

Two types of reagent kits have passed evaluation. According to a report from our newspaper, the director of the Molecular Biology Research Room at the Navy General Hospital, Associate Professor Wang Huimei, and her assistants have recently developed reagent kits for detecting superoxide dismutase in human serum using enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay and nitrite methods. These kits have been clinically tested in more than 30 hospitals nationwide and have proven to be of significant practical value in detecting kidney diseases, acute myocardial infarction, and gastrointestinal tumors. This achievement has passed the evaluation.

　　两种试剂盒通过鉴定
    本报讯  海军总医院分子生物学研究室主任、副教授王惠媛和她的助手，新近研制出检测人血清超氧化物歧化酶类的酶联免疫吸附和亚硝酸试剂盒，经国内３０多家医院临床使用，证明对检测肾脏疾病、急性心肌梗塞和消化道肿瘤患者有重要实用价值。该成果已通过鉴定。
