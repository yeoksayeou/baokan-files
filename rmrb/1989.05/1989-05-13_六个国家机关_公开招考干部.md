Title: Six State Organs Openly Recruit Officials
Author:
Date: 1989-05-13
Edition: 1

Six state organs, including the Supervision Ministry, the State Audit Administration, the State Statistics Bureau, the State Environmental Protection Administration, the State Bureau of Building Materials, and the State Taxation Bureau, began recruiting staff today.

Hundreds of students from universities such as Peking University, Tsinghua University, the Central University for Nationalities, the Beijing Institute of Economics and Management, and the China University of Political Science and Law, came to the registration site set up at the State Bureau of Building Materials to sign up. Some students even traveled from Henan and Shanxi provinces to register.

### 六个国家机关  公开招考干部

1989-05-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　六个国家机关  公开招考干部
    新华社北京５月１３日电  监察部、国家审计署、国家统计局、国家环境保护局、国家建材局、国家税务局等６个国家机关今天开始招考工作人员。
    数百名来自北大、清华、中央民族学院、北京经济管理学院、中国政法大学等高校的学生今天到设在国家建材局的报名站报了名，还有一些学生是从河南、山西等地赶来报名的。
