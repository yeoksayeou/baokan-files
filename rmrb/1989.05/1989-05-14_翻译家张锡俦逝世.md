Title: Renowned Translator Zhang Xiqu Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-05-14
Edition: 4

Renowned translator and Russian language educator Zhang Xiqu passed away in Beijing on May 5 due to illness. He was 84 years old. Zhang Xiqu had served as the deputy director of the Russian Language Translation Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and as the president and party secretary of the Beijing Foreign Language Institute. His representative translations, including "Day and Night," have had a wide influence among readers.

### 翻译家张锡俦逝世

1989-05-14
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　翻译家张锡俦逝世
    新华社北京电  著名翻译家、俄语教育家张锡俦同志因病于５月５日在北京逝世，终年８４岁。张锡俦曾任中共中央俄文编译局副局长、北京外国语学院院长兼党委书记。他的代表译作《日日夜夜》等在读者中产生了广泛影响。
