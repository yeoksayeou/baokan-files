Title: Thug Zhang Shenghua Executed for Disfiguring Young Woman
Author:
Date: 1989-05-15
Edition: 2

Thug Zhang Shenghua, who intentionally disfigured a young woman, was recently executed in Binzhou City, Shandong Province.

Zhang Shenghua, a resident of Binzhou City with no occupation, was a vagrant. Over the past few years, driven by jealousy and a desire for revenge against young women, he targeted well-dressed and attractive young women. During the day, he would follow and spy on the residences of young women, and at night, he would enter their homes with a knife and a mask to commit his crimes.

### 毁坏女青年面容  流氓张胜华伏法

1989-05-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　毁坏女青年面容  流氓张胜华伏法
    新华社济南５月１５日电  以故意毁坏女青年面容为目的的流氓犯张胜华日前在山东省滨州市伏法。
    流氓犯张胜华，滨州市人，无业游民。近几年，他出于对女青年嫉妒、报复心理，寻找穿着入时、长相漂亮的年轻姑娘为作案对象。白天尾随、窥视女青年住所，夜间持刀蒙面入室作案。
