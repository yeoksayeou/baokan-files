Philippines Land Purchase Scandal
Aquino Orders Investigation

	MANILA, May 26 (Xinhua) -- Philippine President Corazon Aquino has ordered an investigation into the alleged land purchase scandal according to the government's comprehensive land reform program.

	Corazon Aquino made the announcement at a press conference in Manila today. She said she had asked Land Reform Minister Philip Juico to take a leave of absence immediately to allow the National Bureau of Investigation to conduct a thorough investigation into the matter.

	According to reports, 22 officials from the Land Reform Department and the Philippine Land Bank are suspected of being involved in the case. They abused their power and bought 1,889 hectares of land worth 62.6 million pesos (2.9 million US dollars) from a private company for only 3 million pesos (139,000 US dollars).

### 菲传出土地买卖丑闻  阿基诺指示进行调查

1989-05-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　菲传出土地买卖丑闻
    阿基诺指示进行调查
    新华社马尼拉５月２６日电  菲律宾总统科拉松·阿基诺已指示依据政府的全面土地改革纲领，对所传购买土地丑闻进行调查。
    科·阿基诺是今天在马尼拉举行的一次记者招待会上宣布上述指示的。她说，她已要求土地改革部长菲利普·胡伊科立即去度假，以使国家调查局对此事进行彻底调查。
    据报道，２２名土地改革部和菲律宾土地银行的官员涉嫌卷入了此案。他们滥用职权，只花３００万比索（１３．９万美元）从一家私人公司那里买下价值６２６０万比索（２９０万美元）的１８８９公顷的地产。
