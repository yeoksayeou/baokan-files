Title: Chinese Athletes Break Two World Records at the Sixth World Model Boat Championship
Author: 孙永盛　陈炳其
Date: 1989-05-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 24th.

Chinese athletes secured three more gold medals and broke two world records at the ongoing Sixth World Model Boat (Powerboat) Championship held here today.

In the morning, 20-year-old Lu Wei Feng won his second gold medal in this championship in the F3-E class (free design speedboat) competition with a time of 16 seconds.

In the second round of the F1-E class (electric motor speedboat) competition, which featured five countries and 10 athletes, Jiangsu's Jiang Jian Dong broke the world record of 14.8 seconds held by West German athlete Hans Leiner with a time of 13.8 seconds.

In the youth F3-E class competition, Chinese athlete Qiu Wei Qiang broke the world record of 22.7 seconds set by Bulgarian athlete Y. Stof with a time of 21.9 seconds, securing the championship in this event. Another Chinese athlete, Zhang Xue Ru from Shenzhen, won the youth F3-V class event with a time of 25.7 seconds.

In the youth B1 class competition, Bulgarian athlete Petrov broke the world record of 7.786 seconds held by Soviet athlete Shakhaizen with a time of 7.59 seconds.

### 第六届世界航海模型锦标赛  中国选手破两项世界纪录
孙永盛　陈炳其
1989-05-25
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　第六届世界航海模型锦标赛
    中国选手破两项世界纪录
    新华社天津５月２４日电  （记者孙永盛、陈炳其）中国选手今天在这里举行的第六届世界航海模型（动力艇）锦标赛中，又夺得３枚金牌，并打破两项世界纪录。
    ２０岁的陆炜峰在今天上午结束的Ｆ３—Ｅ级（自由设计竞速艇）比赛中，以１６秒的成绩，获得了他在这次锦标赛上的第二个冠军。
    在有５个国家１０名选手参加的Ｆ１—Ｅ级（电动机动力竞速艇）第二轮比赛中，中国选手蒋建栋（江苏）以１３秒８的成绩打破了联邦德国选手汉斯·莱纳保持的１４秒８的世界纪录。
    在青年组Ｆ３—Ｅ级比赛中，中国选手丘伟强以２１秒９的成绩，打破了保加利亚选手约·斯多夫创造的２２秒７的世界纪录，并获得了这个项目的冠军。另一名中国选手张学儒（深圳）以２５秒７的成绩获得青年组Ｆ３—Ｖ级项目的冠军。
    保加利亚选手彼得洛夫在青年组Ｂ１级比赛中，以７秒５９的成绩打破苏联选手沙哈齐恩保持的７秒７８６的世界纪录。
