Title: Prince Sihanouk Proposes Five-Point Plan at Banquet Hosted by Thai Prime Minister Chatchai
Author: Liu Aicheng
Date: 1989-04-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, April 29th.

Prince Sihanouk today at noon, at a banquet hosted by Thai Prime Minister Chatchai, reiterated his five-point proposal. 1. The Vietnamese invasion forces must withdraw completely and unconditionally from Cambodia under the supervision and verification of the International Supervisory Commission (ICC). The member countries of this supervisory commission will be selected by an international conference held in Paris, not proposed unilaterally by Vietnam. 2. Simultaneously, the illegal "People's Republic of Kampuchea" and the legitimate Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea will be dissolved, and a temporary quadripartite government will be established. The four Cambodian factions must agree to have an equal number of troops. The remaining military personnel will be disarmed and disbanded by the International Supervisory Commission. 3. Through the United Nations or the possible Paris international conference, international peacekeeping forces will be sent to Cambodia as much as possible. 4. Under the supervision of the International Supervisory Commission and the assistance of international peacekeeping forces (if sent), elections will be held to produce a National Assembly. 5. Cambodia will once again become a neutral and non-aligned nation.

Prince Sihanouk said that Hun Sen and Vietnam must at least accept the first, second, and fourth of these reasonable proposals.

He said that if Hun Sen and Vietnam do not accept these main proposals, his talks with Hun Sen from May 2 to 3 in Jakarta, the possible third Jakarta meeting, and the possible "round table conference" in Paris with the participation of the four Cambodian factions will all be ineffective.

### 在泰国总理差猜举行的欢迎宴会上  西哈努克再次提出五点建议
刘爱成
1989-04-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　在泰国总理差猜举行的欢迎宴会上    
    西哈努克再次提出五点建议
    本报曼谷４月２９日电  记者刘爱成报道：西哈努克亲王今天中午在泰国总理差猜为他举行的宴会上发表讲话时再次提出五点原则性建议。１、越南侵略军必须在国际监督委员会（ＩＣＣ）的监督和核实下全部地、无条件地从柬撤出。这个监督委员会的成员国将由在巴黎召开的国际会议选择而不是由越南单方面提出。２、同时解散非法的“柬埔寨人民共和国”和合法的民柬政府，建立临时四方政府。柬四方部队必须同意拥有同等数量的军队。其余的军事人员将由国际监督委员会解除武装和解散。３、通过联合国或可能召开的巴黎国际会议尽可能向柬派国际维持和平部队。４、在国际监督委员会监督和国际维持和平部队（如果派的话）协助下举行大选，产生国民代表大会；５、柬埔寨将再次成为一个中立和不结盟国家。
    西哈努克亲王说，洪森和越南至少必须接受这些合情合理建议中的第一、二、四条。
    他说如果洪森和越南不接受其中的主要建议，他与洪森５月２日至３日在雅加达的会谈、有可能召开的雅加达第三次会议以及有可能在巴黎召开的柬四方参加的“圆桌会议”都将只会是消极的。
