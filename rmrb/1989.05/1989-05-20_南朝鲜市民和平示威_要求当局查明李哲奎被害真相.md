Title: South Korean Citizens Hold Peaceful Protests Demanding Investigation into Lee Cheol-Kyu's Murder
Author: XU Baokang
Date: 1989-05-20
Edition: 3

	South Korean Citizens Hold Peaceful Protests
	Demanding Investigation into Lee Cheol-Kyu's Murder

	BEIJING, May 17 (Xinhua) -- SEOUL, May 16 -- More than 20,000 professors, students, and citizens in Gwangju, South Korea, took to the streets today to hold a peaceful protest. They demanded that the authorities investigate the true circumstances surrounding the murder of Lee Cheol-Kyu and decided to hold a "General Citizens' Assembly" tomorrow.

	Lee Cheol-Kyu was a student at Chonnam National University who advocated for democracy and signed petitions against dictatorship. Before his murder, he served as the editor-in-chief of the school newspaper "Democratic Korea."

	On May 10, Lee Cheol-Kyu's body was found in a reservoir in the northern part of Gwangju.

### 南朝鲜市民和平示威  要求当局查明李哲奎被害真相
徐宝康
1989-05-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　南朝鲜市民和平示威
    要求当局查明李哲奎被害真相
    本报平壤５月１７日电  记者徐宝康报道：汉城消息，南朝鲜光州市２万多名大学教授、学生和市民１６日走上街头，举行和平示威，强烈要求当局查明李哲奎被害真相，并决定今天举行“泛市民大会”。
    李哲奎是光州朝鲜大学一名主张民主、反对独裁的签名学生。被害前，任校刊“民主朝鲜”报的编辑委员长。
    ５月１０日，李哲奎的尸体在光州北区的一个水库里被发现。
