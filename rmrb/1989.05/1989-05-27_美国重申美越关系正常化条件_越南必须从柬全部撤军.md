Title: The United States Reiterates Conditions for Normalization of U.S.-Vietnam Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-05-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, May 26th.

The United States reaffirmed today that normalization of U.S.-Vietnam relations can only be achieved under the condition of reaching an "acceptable political solution to the Cambodian issue, which includes the complete withdrawal of Vietnamese troops." He noted that if Vietnam continues to cooperate with the United States in searching for the remains of Americans missing in the Vietnam War and other humanitarian issues, this will affect the process of normalizing bilateral relations. Boucher made these remarks in response to a recent speech by Vietnamese Foreign Minister Nguyen Co Thach on U.S.-Vietnam relations. Nguyen Co Thach said he expected U.S.-Vietnam relations to normalize next year.

### 美国重申美越关系正常化条件  越南必须从柬全部撤军

1989-05-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国重申美越关系正常化条件
    越南必须从柬全部撤军
    新华社华盛顿５月２６日电  美国国务院副发言人鲍彻今天重申，美越关系只有在就柬埔寨问题达成一项“可以接受的政治解决办法，其中包括全部撤出越南军队的前提下”才能实现。他指出，如果越南继续在美国寻找越战中美失踪人员的遗骸和其它人道问题上抱合作态度，这将会对双边关系正常化的进程产生影响。鲍彻是在评述越南外长阮基石最近关于越美关系的一次讲话时说这番话的。阮基石说，他预计越美关系明年可能实现正常化。
