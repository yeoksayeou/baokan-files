Title: Garbage Collection Ship Appears on the Yangtze River
Author: Miǎo Fán Zú
Date: 1989-05-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, May 5th.

Recently, the first garbage collection ship has appeared on the Yangtze River. This ship, named "Mei Qing 1," is a 40-ton motorized iron barge belonging to Hongwei Village in the suburbs of Nanjing. It operates on the 50-plus kilometer stretch of the river from Meixia Mountain to Xinsheng Yu, providing paid services to clean up sewage and garbage from various ships along the route.

### 长江上出现垃圾清运船
苗凡卒
1989-05-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　长江上出现垃圾清运船
    新华社武汉５月５日电  （实习生苗凡卒）最近，长江上出现了第一艘垃圾清运船。这艘名为“梅清１号”的清运船是４０吨级的机动铁驳船，归南京市郊区红卫村所有，航行于长江梅霞山至新生圩５０多公里的江面上，对沿线港区各类船只的污水、垃圾实行有偿清运。
