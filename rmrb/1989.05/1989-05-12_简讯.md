Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1989-05-12
Edition: 2

	Vice Chairman Wang Zhen met with the German Federal Republic's delegation on agriculture on May 11th in Beijing. The delegation, led by Georg Gallus, the State Secretary of the Federal Republic of Germany's Ministry of Food, Agriculture and Forestry, was in China to participate in the 9th meeting of the Sino-German Economic Mixed Commission's Food and Forestry Working Group, held from May 9th to 11th.

	On May 11th, a spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs responded to questions from reporters regarding the visit of U.S. warships to China on May 18th, stating, "This is a friendly, ceremonial visit."

	Kang Keqing, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Honorary President of the All-China Women's Federation, met and hosted a banquet for a delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association's Women's Division on May 11th at the Great Hall of the People. The delegation was led by Ito Toyoko, a member of the National Headquarters.

	On May 11th, Vice Premier and Minister of the Ministry of Machine-Building Industry Zhu Qizhen met with a delegation from the Philippines led by Rebolardo Guzman, the First Deputy Minister of Transportation. The Philippine guests arrived in Beijing on May 10th. Their purpose for this visit is to inspect China's production of large buses to determine whether to import such vehicles from China.

### 简讯

1989-05-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　简讯
    ▲国家副主席王震１１日在北京会见了由联邦德国粮食和农林部国务秘书乔治·加卢斯率领的农业代表团。代表团是来参加于９日至１１日举行的中国和联邦德国经济混合委员会粮食农林工作组第９次会议的。
    ▲外交部发言人１１日在新闻发布会上回答记者关于美国军舰将于５月１８日访问中国的问题时说，“这是一次友好的、礼节性的访问。”
    ▲全国政协副主席、全国妇联名誉主席康克清１１日在人民大会堂会见并宴请由全国本部理事伊藤丰子率领的日本日中友协妇女代表团。
    ▲国务委员兼机电部部长邹家华１１日在北京会见了菲律宾交通部第一副部长里鲍尔多·古兹曼一行。菲律宾客人是１０日抵京的。他们此行目的是考察中国生产大客车的水平，以便决定是否从中国进口这种车辆。
