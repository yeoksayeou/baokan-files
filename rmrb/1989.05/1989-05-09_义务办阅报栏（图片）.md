	Voluntary Newspaper Stand
	In May 1987, retired teacher Kuāng Míng from Yúxī Primary School in Kāi County, Sichuan Province, set up a voluntary newspaper stand on the civilized demonstration street of Dōng Qú River in the county town. She updates the newspapers daily, attracting many readers.
