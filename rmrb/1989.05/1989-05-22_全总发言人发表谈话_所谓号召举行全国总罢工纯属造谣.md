Title: Spokesperson for the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Issues Statement: Alleged Call for Nationwide General Strike is Pure Rumor
Author:
Date: 1989-05-22
Edition: 1

	The All-China Federation of Trade Unions spokesperson issued a statement today. Recently, there have been rumors in some places that the "All-China Federation of Trade Unions is calling for a nationwide general strike." The spokesperson for the All-China Federation of Trade Unions stated on May 22 that this is purely a malicious rumor.

	The spokesperson solemnly pointed out that the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has repeatedly emphasized in recent days that the broad masses of workers should firmly hold their posts, do a good job in production and work, maintain a stable and united situation, and enable reform and opening up and modernization construction to proceed in a stable social environment. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions reiterates and emphasizes this consistent stance.

### 全总发言人发表谈话  所谓号召举行全国总罢工纯属造谣

1989-05-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　全总发言人发表谈话
    所谓号召举行全国总罢工纯属造谣
    本报讯  近日，一些地方谣传“全国总工会号召举行全国总罢工”。中华全国总工会发言人５月２２日说，这纯属别有用心的造谣。
    发言人郑重指出，全国总工会最近一再强调，广大职工应当坚守岗位，搞好生产和工作，维护安定团结的局面，使改革开放和现代化建设能在稳定的社会环境中进行。全总再次重申和强调这个一贯态度。
