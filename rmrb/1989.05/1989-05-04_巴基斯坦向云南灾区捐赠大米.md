Title: Pakistan Donates Rice to Yunnan Disaster Area
Author: Qin Shuwen
Date: 1989-05-04
Edition: 2

Today morning, the handover ceremony for the Pakistani aid rice to China was held here. The Ministry of Civil Affairs of China will use this batch of rice to help alleviate the living difficulties of the people in the Yunnan earthquake-stricken area.

At the end of 1988, Pakistani President Ghulam Ishaq Khan wrote a letter to Chinese President Yang Shangkun, expressing deep sympathy and condolences for the series of natural disasters that occurred in China over the past year. At the same time, Pakistani Ambassador Akram Zaki, on behalf of the Pakistani government and people, donated 2,000 tons of rice to China as humanitarian aid to the disaster-stricken areas.

### 巴基斯坦向云南灾区捐赠大米
秦淑文
1989-05-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦向云南灾区捐赠大米
    本报北京五月四日讯  今日上午，巴基斯坦援华救灾大米交接仪式在这里举行。国家民政部将把这批大米用于解决云南地震灾区人民的生活困难。
    一九八八年底，巴基斯坦总统伊沙克·汗写信给我国家主席杨尚昆，对中国一年来发生的一系列自然灾情深表同情和慰问。同时，由巴驻华大使阿克拉姆·扎基代表巴政府和人民向我捐赠两千吨大米，作为对灾区人民的人道主义援助。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（秦淑文）
