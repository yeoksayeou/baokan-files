Title: Kim Dae-jung Criticizes Roh Tae-woo and Demands Resignation of Current Cabinet
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-05-08
Page: 3

People's Daily, Seoul, May 8th. Reporter Xu Baokang reports: Seoul news, South Korean Peace Democratic Party Chairman Kim Dae-jung on the 6th at a press conference, demanded that Roh Tae-woo's current cabinet must resign en masse.

Kim Dae-jung said that recently, the authorities have in fact suspended the settlement of the Chun Doo-hwan corruption issue and the realization of democratization. This has brought confusion to the people, and the current cabinet must bear full responsibility for this.

Kim Dae-jung pointed out that to stabilize public sentiment, the current cabinet must resign en masse. He emphasized that in this extraordinary session of the National Assembly, the Peace Democratic Party will definitely strive to implement the stance of forcing the cabinet to resign en masse.

### 金大中批评卢泰愚  要求现内阁总辞职
徐宝康
1989-05-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　金大中批评卢泰愚    
    要求现内阁总辞职
    本报平壤５月８日电  记者徐宝康报道：汉城消息，南朝鲜和平民主党总裁金大中６日在记者招待会上，向卢泰愚提出，现内阁必须总辞职。
　
    金大中说，最近当局事实上已中断了对全斗焕舞弊等问题的清算和实现民主化。这给国民带来了混乱，现内阁必须对此负全部责任。
    金大中指出，为稳定人心，现内阁必须总辞职。他强调说，在这次临时国会上，和平民主党一定要努力贯彻迫使内阁总辞职的立场。
