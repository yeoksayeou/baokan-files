Title: British Scientists Refute Cold Fusion
Author:
Date: 1989-05-08
Edition: 3

British scientists have finally dashed the hopes of cold fusion.

According to a report by the *Sunday Times* today, renowned British scientists from the Harwell Nuclear Laboratory have concluded that cold fusion is not viable.

Since British Martin Fleischmann of Southampton University and American Stanley Pons of the University of Utah announced on March 23 that they had achieved nuclear fusion in an electrolytic cell, experts from the British Atomic Energy Authority have been examining this method.

David Williams, the head of the examination team, said yesterday: "We found neither heat nor radiation."

The newspaper reported that Williams' conclusion is considered decisive because the examination was conducted with the assistance of Fleischmann.

Williams said: "We repeated Fleischmann and Pons' experiment using much more sensitive equipment, but the phenomena they described did not occur."

Meanwhile, scientists from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and the California Institute of Technology in the United States recently also announced that Pons and Fleischmann's experiment contains significant errors, and they may have been misled by incorrect calculations.

### 英科学家否定冷核聚变

1989-05-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　英科学家否定冷核聚变
    新华社伦敦５月７日电  据《星期日泰晤士报》今天报道，世界著名的哈韦尔核实验室的英国科学家终于埋葬了冷核聚变的希望。
    自从英国南安普敦大学马丁·弗莱希曼和美国犹他大学斯坦利·庞斯于３月２３日宣布在电解电池中获得核聚变后，英国原子能局的专家一直在检查这一方法。
    检查工作小组组长戴维·威廉斯博士昨天说：“我们既没有发现热量，也没有发现辐射。”
    这家报纸说，威廉斯博士的断语被认为是决定性的，因为检查工作是在弗莱希曼的帮助下进行的。
    威廉斯博士说：“我们用灵敏得多的设备重复了弗莱希曼和庞斯的实验”，但是没有发生他们所说的现象。
    另据华盛顿消息，美国麻省理工学院和加州理工学院的科学家最近也宣布，庞斯和弗莱希曼的实验具有重大谬误，他们可能是被错误的计算结果愚弄了。
