Title: Large Deposit of McFanshan Stone Mine Discovered in Gan Nan
Author: Lu Jing
Date: 1989-05-28
Edition: 2

A large deposit of McFanshan stone has been discovered in the southern part of Jiangxi province, specifically in XinFeng. Preliminary exploration has revealed a potential reserve of 500 million tons.

Over the course of more than two years, geologists and medical professionals from Jiangxi have conducted extensive research on the mineral composition, biological activity, and pathological indicators of the McFanshan stone in Gan Nan. They have found that it contains a variety of minerals and trace elements beneficial to the human body.

### 赣南发现大储量麦饭石矿
卢晶
1989-05-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　赣南发现大储量麦饭石矿
    新华社南昌５月２７日电  （记者卢晶）江西南部信丰境内最近发现一个麦饭石矿，初步探明远景储量５００万吨。
    江西地质界、医学界的科技工作者经过２年多的时间，对赣南麦饭石矿的矿物化学成分、生物活性及病理等指标测定，发现含有人体需要的多种矿物质及有益的微量元素。
