Title: Prince Sihanouk Calls for International Conference in Paris During Credential Presentation
Author: Liú Àichéng
Date: 1989-05-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Odongkili Province, Sre Ambel County (Cambodia), May 8th. Reporter Liu Aicheng reports: Prince Sihanouk, chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, emphasized today that only the genuine withdrawal of Vietnamese troops can ensure Cambodia's true independence.

Prince Sihanouk made these remarks while receiving the letters of credence from the new ambassadors of China, North Korea, and Senegal to the Democratic Kampuchea at a base of the Sihanouk National Army here. Sihanouk said, "Although Vietnam has announced that it will withdraw all its troops from Cambodia by the end of September, I do not know whether it will be a genuine withdrawal or not. Therefore, we demand effective international supervision. Only the genuine withdrawal of Vietnamese troops can guarantee our true independence. Hence, we demand an international conference in Paris to establish and deploy an international supervisory body in Cambodia to supervise the withdrawal of Vietnamese troops, ensure fair elections and peace. This international supervisory body should stay in Cambodia for at least five years."

Prince Sihanouk suggested to Chinese Ambassador Li Shichun that the United Nations Secretary-General, China, the United States, the United Kingdom, France, the Soviet Union, Japan, India, Australia, New Zealand, the six ASEAN countries, Vietnam, Laos, and the four Cambodian factions should participate in the Paris international conference. He hopes that China will send representatives to attend.

Prince Sihanouk also briefly informed the ambassadors about his recent meeting with Hun Sen in Jakarta. He pointed out that there is still a long way to go in the process of seeking a political solution to the Cambodian issue. He stressed that the future Cambodian government must first be a temporary four-party coalition government and a temporary four-party military force.

### 西哈努克在接受外国大使国书时发表谈话  要求在巴黎举行国际会议  建议中美苏英法等国参加
刘爱成
1989-05-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　西哈努克在接受外国大使国书时发表谈话    
    要求在巴黎举行国际会议    
    建议中美苏英法等国参加
    本报奥多棉吉省三隆县（柬埔寨）５月８日电  记者刘爱成报道：民柬主席西哈努克亲王今天在这里强调，只有使越南真正撤军才能使柬埔寨真正独立。
    西哈努克亲王是在这里的一个西哈努克民族军基地接受中国、朝鲜和塞内加尔新任驻民柬大使递交国书时说这番话的。西哈努克说，“越南虽然宣布了９月底从柬全部撤军，但我不知道它是真撤还是假撤。所以我们要求有效的国际监督。只有使越南真正撤军，才能保证我们真正的独立。因此，我们要求在巴黎召开国际会议，产生并在柬部署国际监督机构，以监督越南撤军，保证大选与和平，这个国际监督机构要在柬至少驻５年时间。”
    西哈努克亲王对中国大使李世淳说，他建议联合国秘书长、中国、美国、英国、法国、苏联、日本、印度、澳大利亚、新西兰、东盟六国、越南、老挝及柬四方参加巴黎国际会议。希望中国到时能派代表参加。
    西哈努克亲王还分别向大使们简单通报了他与洪森最近在雅加达的会谈情况。他指出，在寻求政治解决柬问题进程中仍有很长的路要走。他强调未来柬埔寨政府首先必须是临时四方联合政府和临时四方军队。
