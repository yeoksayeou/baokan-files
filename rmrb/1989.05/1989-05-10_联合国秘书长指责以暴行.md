Title: UN Secretary-General Condemns Israeli Atrocities
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-05-10
Page: 3

Reporter Liu Kai Chen, United Nations, May 8th

UN Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar today expressed deep concern over the heavy casualties in the Israeli-occupied territories, particularly in the Gaza Strip, over the weekend.

A UN spokesperson stated today that the Secretary-General regretted that such a bloody incident occurred on the day marking the end of the Muslim fasting month of Ramadan.

According to reports, Israeli occupying forces killed at least three people and injured more than 130 in Gaza on the 6th.

### 联合国秘书长指责以暴行
刘开宸
1989-05-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：联合国动态

　　联合国秘书长指责以暴行
    本报联合国５月８日电  记者刘开宸报道：联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天对上周末以色列占领区，特别是加沙地带的流血事件中伤亡很多“深表不安”。
    联合国发言人今天在一项声明中说，秘书长对此流血事件竟然发生在穆斯林“斋月”结束之日表示遗憾。
    据报道，以色列占领军６日在加沙地带屠杀巴勒斯坦平民，至少打死３人，打伤１３０多人。
