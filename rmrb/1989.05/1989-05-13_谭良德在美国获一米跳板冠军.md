Title: Tan Liangde Wins 1-Meter Springboard Championship in the U.S.
Author:
Date: 1989-05-13
Edition: 4

Chinese athlete Tan Liangde won the 1-meter springboard diving championship at the Alamo International Diving Meet in the United States on May 12. The champion of the 1-meter springboard event at last week's World Cup Diving Competition, Mark Lenzi of the United States, finished second this time.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 谭良德在美国获一米跳板冠军

1989-05-13
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　谭良德在美国获一米跳板冠军
    中国选手谭良德１２日获得美国阿拉莫国际跳水赛１米跳板跳水冠军。上周世界杯跳水赛这个项目的冠军，美国的马克·伦齐这次只获得了第２名。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
