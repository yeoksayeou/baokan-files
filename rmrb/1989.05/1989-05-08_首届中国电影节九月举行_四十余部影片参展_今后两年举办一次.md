Title: The First China Film Festival to be Held in September
Author: Lǐ Tóng
Date: 1989-05-08
Page: 2

The first China Film Festival will open in Beijing on September 21st and close in Shenzhen on September 27th.

The Ministry of Radio, Film, and Television is organizing this major cultural event, which includes four main activities: a screening of excellent Chinese films, selecting excellent films produced between 1988 and 1989 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the nation. Over 40 films will be showcased, including about 20 feature films and 20 short films, which encompass art films, science and education films, and documentaries. The Ministry of Radio, Film, and Television will also award the Excellent Film Prize. From September 17th to 22nd, the second China Film Exhibition will be held, inviting over 140 film industry professionals from various countries to Beijing to view Chinese films produced in the past two years. Additionally, there will be premieres, academic discussions, and public events related to the promotion of Chinese films.

Films already selected for the screening include "The Founding Ceremony" (Changchun Film Studio), "The Baise Uprising" (Guangxi Film Studio), "The Majestic Kunlun Mountains" (August First Film Studio), "Dream of the Red Chamber" (Beijing Film Studio), "The Last Aristocrats" (Shanghai Film Studio), "The Business" (Zhujiang Film Studio), and "Norman Bethune" (co-produced by August First Film Studio and Canada).

At today's press conference, Chen Haosu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Radio, Film, and Television, stated that the China Film Festival will be held every two years and will gradually develop into an international film festival.

Chen Haosu also, on behalf of the China Film Festival Organizing Committee, extended an invitation to film professionals from Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macao.

### 首届中国电影节九月举行  四十余部影片参展　今后两年举办一次
李彤
1989-05-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　首届中国电影节九月举行    
    四十余部影片参展　今后两年举办一次
    本报北京五月八日讯  记者李彤报道：第一届中国电影节将于今年九月二十一日在北京开幕，二十七日在深圳闭幕。
    由广播电影电视部主办的这一大型文化活动包括四项内容：优秀中国影片汇映展，选择一九八八年至一九八九年为庆祝建国四十周年所摄制的优秀影片四十余部参展，其中故事片和包括美术片、科教片、纪录片在内的短片各二十部左右；颁发广播电影电视部优秀影片奖；在九月十七日至二十二日举办电影市场，即第二届中国电影展，邀请一百四十余位各国电影界人士来京选看近两年生产的中国影片；其他与宣传中国电影有关的首映活动、学术研讨活动和群众性活动。
    已经选定参加汇映展的影片有《开国大典》（长影）、《百色起义》（广西）、《巍巍昆仑》（八一）、《红楼梦》（北影）、《最后的贵族》（上影）、《商界》（珠影）和八一厂与加拿大合拍的《白求恩——一个英雄的成长》等。
    在今天举行的新闻发布会上，广播电影电视部副部长陈昊苏说，中国电影节将每两年举行一次，它将逐步发展成为带有国际色彩的电影盛会。
    陈昊苏还代表中国电影节组织委员会向台湾及港澳地区的电影工作者发出邀请。
