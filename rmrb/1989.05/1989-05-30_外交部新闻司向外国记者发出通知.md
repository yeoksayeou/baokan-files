Title: Ministry of Foreign Affairs Information Department Issues Notice to Foreign Journalists
Author:
Date: 1989-05-30
Edition: 2

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs Information Department has issued a notice to foreign news agencies' correspondents in China. The content is as follows:

On May 20, 1989, the Beijing Municipal Government issued the second and third orders, regulating matters that foreign nationals should pay attention to during the martial law period in certain areas of Beijing, and issues related to journalists' reporting. Please comply with these regulations. During this period, for matters related to journalists' reporting activities in Beijing, please contact the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal Government directly.

### 外交部新闻司向外国记者发出通知

1989-05-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外交部新闻司向外国记者发出通知
    新华社北京5月30日电  据悉，外交部新闻司已向各国新闻机构驻华记者发出通知，内容如下：
    1989年5月20日北京市人民政府发布了第二号和第三号令，就在北京市部分地区实行戒严期间外国人应注意的事项和记者采访问题作出规定，请遵照执行。在此期间，记者在北京市进行采访活动有关事宜，请径与北京市人民政府外事办公室联系。
