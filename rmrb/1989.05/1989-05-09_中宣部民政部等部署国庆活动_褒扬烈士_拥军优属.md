Title: Central Propaganda Department and Ministry of Civil Affairs Deploy National Day Activities
Author:
Date: 1989-05-09
Edition: 2

The Central Propaganda Department, the Ministry of Civil Affairs, and other departments recently required all regions to actively organize activities to honor revolutionary martyrs and to do a good job in supporting the military and caring for military families during the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. This is to carry forward and develop the revolutionary tradition, and to promote reform and the construction of a spiritually and materially civilized society.

A notice jointly issued by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's Propaganda Department, the Ministry of Civil Affairs, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Communist Youth League of China, and the All-China Women's Federation, requires all regions to, in conjunction with the publicity work for the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, vigorously publicize the contributions and sacrifices made by the People's Liberation Army in the establishment, defense, and construction of socialist New China. It also requires publicizing the historical merits of the beneficiaries of preferential policies in the creation of the republic and their new contributions to promoting reform and the construction of a spiritually and materially civilized society. Additionally, it requires publicizing that military-civilian unity is an important guarantee for the victory of the revolutionary and construction cause.

The notice states that all regions should, in accordance with their specific circumstances, organize the masses and youth to carry out various forms of activities to commemorate revolutionary martyrs and to express condolences to families of martyrs and military personnel, disabled soldiers, and soldiers and armed police stationed on the border and coastal defense.

### 中宣部民政部等部署国庆活动  褒扬烈士　拥军优属

1989-05-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　中宣部民政部等部署国庆活动    
    褒扬烈士　拥军优属
    新华社北京５月９日电  中宣部、民政部等近日要求各地在庆祝建国四十周年之际，积极开展褒扬烈士的活动，做好拥军优属工作，以继承和发扬革命传统，促进改革和两个文明建设。
    由中共中央宣传部、民政部、全国总工会、共青团中央和全国妇联联合发出的通知，要求各地结合建国四十周年的宣传工作，大力宣传解放军为创立、保卫和建设社会主义新中国所作的贡献和牺牲精神，宣传优抚对象为创建共和国所立下的历史功绩和在促进改革与两个文明建设中所作出的新贡献，宣传军民团结是夺取革命和建设事业的重要保证。
    通知说，各地应结合具体情况，组织群众、青少年开展多种形式的纪念革命先烈和慰问烈军属、伤残军人及驻守在边防、海防的解放军指战员、武警部队活动。
