Title: The Security Council Supports the Plan for Namibian Independence
Author: Liú Kāichén
Date: 1989-05-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New York, May 24th.

The Security Council Supports the Plan for Namibian Independence

The People's Daily, New York, May 24th. Reporter Liú Kāichén reports: The United Nations Security Council today expressed its firm support for the efforts made by UN Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar to resume the implementation of the plan for Namibian independence.

After Pérez de Cuéllar reported to the Security Council on the recent situation in Namibia, the Security Council President Crispin Tickell issued a statement emphasizing the importance of all parties strictly adhering to Security Council Resolution 435 regarding Namibian independence. He also pointed out that the Secretary-General is authorized by the Security Council to be responsible for implementing the UN plan.

### 安理会支持纳独立计划
刘开宸
1989-05-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：联合国简讯

　　安理会支持纳独立计划
    本报联合国５月２４日电  记者刘开宸报道：联合国安理会全体理事国今天表示坚决支持联合国秘书长德奎利亚尔为恢复执行关于纳米比亚独立计划所做的努力。
    在德奎利亚尔今天向安理会全体理事国代表报告最近的纳米比亚形势后，安理会本月主席克里斯平·蒂克尔发表声明强调有关各方严格遵守关于纳米比亚独立的安理会４３５号决议的重要性，并指出秘书长是由安理会授权负责使联合国计划付诸实施的。
