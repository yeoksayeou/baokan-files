Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1989-05-15
Edition: 3

    Queen Beatrix of the Netherlands and her husband, Prince Claus, received some Chinese students and visiting scholars studying in the Netherlands at the Royal Palace in Amsterdam on May 9.

    A delegation led by Chairman Ni Zhi Fu of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions concluded its visit to Finland on May 14 and departed for Stockholm, the capital of Sweden.

    The second session of the China-Greece Economic Joint Committee, which lasted for three days, opened in Athens on May 8.

    (According to Xinhua News Agency)
