Title: U.S. Secretary of State Baker Says Israel Should Engage in Serious Dialogue
Author:
Date: 1989-05-23
Edition: 3

U.S. Secretary of State Baker today addressed a pro-Israel lobbying group, stating, "It is time for Israel and the Palestinians to engage in serious dialogue."

Baker pointed out that Israel should completely abandon the unrealistic notion of a "Greater Israel," stop annexation, halt the establishment of settlements, allow schools to reopen, and extend a hand to the Palestinians who deserve their political rights. He also mentioned that the United States hopes Arab countries will take practical steps towards reconciliation with Israel.

It is believed that this is the strongest statement made by the Bush administration towards Israel since taking office, emphasizing the reaffirmation of U.S. Middle East policy.

### 美国务卿贝克说  以色列应认真对话

1989-05-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国务卿贝克说
    以色列应认真对话
    本报华盛顿５月２２日电  美国国务卿贝克今天向一个亲以色列的游说集团发表讲话时说，“现在是以色列和巴勒斯坦人进行认真对话的时候了”。
    贝克指出，以色列应该彻底放弃“大以色列”这种不现实的幻想，抛弃吞并，停止建立定居点的活动，允许学校复课，并向理应享受其政治权利的巴勒斯坦人“伸出手来”。他还说，美国希望阿拉伯国家对以色列采取实际的和解步骤。
    据认为，这是布什政府执政以来对以色列发表的一次措词强硬的讲话，其基调是重申美国的中东政策。
