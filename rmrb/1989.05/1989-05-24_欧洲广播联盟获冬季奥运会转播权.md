Title: European Broadcasting Union Secures Winter Olympics Broadcast Rights
Author: Shì Guāngyào
Date: 1989-05-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 23rd.

The European Broadcasting Union has secured the exclusive radio and television broadcast rights for the 17th Winter Olympics in Europe.

The European Broadcasting Union, the International Olympic Committee, and the organizing committee of the 17th Winter Olympics held a tripartite meeting in Geneva on the 22nd, approving the European Broadcasting Union's exclusive rights to broadcast the 17th Winter Olympics on radio and television in Europe.

According to the approved agreement, the European Broadcasting Union will pay $24 million, of which $15 million will be for the broadcast rights, and $9 million will be used for service fees and technical assistance.

### 欧洲广播联盟获冬季奥运会转播权
施光耀
1989-05-24
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　欧洲广播联盟获冬季奥运会转播权
    新华社日内瓦５月２３日电  （记者施光耀）欧洲广播联盟获第十七届冬季奥运会在欧洲的独家无线电广播和电视转播权。
    欧洲广播联盟、国际奥委会和第十七届冬季奥运会组委会２２日在日内瓦举行了三方联席会议，批准欧洲广播联盟拥有在欧洲独家无线电广播和电视转播第十七届冬季奥运会的权利。
    根据已批准的协定，欧洲广播联盟将支付２４００万美元，其中１５００万美元支付转播权，９００万美元用作服务费用和技术补助。
