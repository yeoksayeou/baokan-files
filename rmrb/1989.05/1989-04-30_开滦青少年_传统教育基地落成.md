Title: Opening of the Kailuan Youth Traditional Education Base
Author: Lú Xiǎngtāo
Date: 1989-04-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 29th.

The "Kailuan Mining Area Youth Traditional Education Base," which has been under construction for many years, was completed just before May 4th. On the morning of April 28th, the Communist Youth League of Tangshan City and the Mining Bureau Youth League held a tree-planting ceremony.

### 开滦青少年  传统教育基地落成
卢向韬
1989-04-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　开滦青少年    
    传统教育基地落成
    据新华社北京４月２９日电  （通讯员卢向韬）筹建多年的“开滦矿区青少年传统教育基地”，“五四”前夕告落成。４月２８日上午共青团唐山市委与矿务局团委举行了树碑仪式。
