Title: Soviet Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Visits China
Author:
Date: 1989-05-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 13th.

At the invitation of the Foreign Ministry's Information Department, the Deputy Minister of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, Head of the Information Department, and Spokesperson for the Foreign Ministry, G. I. Grashchenkov, arrived in China on May 12, 1989, for a visit.

During his stay in Beijing, Grashchenkov held a working meeting with Li Zhaoxing, Director of the Information Department of the Chinese Foreign Ministry. Grashchenkov will also visit Tianjin for a tour.

### 苏联外交部新闻局长访华

1989-05-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联外交部新闻局长访华
    本报讯  应外交部新闻司邀请，苏联外交部部务委员、新闻局长、外交部发言人格拉西莫夫于一九八九年五月十二日来华访问。
    在京期间，格拉西莫夫同中国外交部新闻司司长李肇星举行了工作会谈。格拉西莫夫还将前往天津参观。
