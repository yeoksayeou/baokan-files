Title: Arab League Foreign Ministers' Meeting Opens; Suggests Re-admitting Egypt
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-05-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Casablanca, May 22

The Arab League Foreign Ministers' Meeting, held in preparation for the upcoming Arab Summit, opened on the evening of May 21st in this city.

The foreign ministers will present specific suggestions to the summit, scheduled for May 23rd to 24th, to re-admit Egypt as a member of the Arab League. Egyptian Foreign Minister Magid attended the foreign ministers' meeting. This is the first time Egypt has attended such a meeting since it was suspended from membership in 1979 for signing the Camp David Accords.

Kalibi warmly welcomed the Egyptian foreign minister's attendance at the meeting, stating, "This great brotherly country will resume its important role in Arab joint action from today." He highly praised Egypt's contributions to the Arab nation.

Kalibi pointed out that the Arab nation is facing enormous challenges and threats, and it must strengthen unity and coordinate steps to jointly face reality.

Regarding the Arab-Israeli conflict, Kalibi said that the Arab League's Fez, Amman, and Algiers summits have already formulated principles for resolving the dispute. "An international conference is the path to achieving the Arab peace plan." He pointed out that Shamir's election plan is intended to suppress the Palestinian uprising.

Kalibi's speech also touched on the two Iran-Iraq relations and the Lebanese crisis, which will be discussed at this meeting.

Egyptian Foreign Minister Magid spoke at the meeting, expressing satisfaction with the progress of the Arab League Foreign Ministers' Meeting.

### 阿盟外长会议开幕  建议重新接纳埃及
林皎明
1989-05-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　阿盟外长会议开幕
    建议重新接纳埃及
    本报卡萨布兰卡５月２２日电  记者林皎明报道：为即将召开的阿拉伯特别首脑会议作准备的阿盟外长会议，２１日晚上在这里开幕。
    外长们将向２３日到２４日的首脑会议提出具体建议，重新接纳埃及为阿盟会员国。埃及外长马吉德出席了外长会议。这是埃及自１９７９年签署戴维营协议后被中止会员资格以来，第一次出席这样的会议。
    卡利比热烈欢迎埃及外长出席这次会议，指出“这个伟大的兄弟国家从今天起恢复在阿拉伯共同行动中发挥重要作用”。他高度评价了埃及对阿拉伯民族所作出的贡献。
    卡利比指出，阿拉伯民族正面临巨大挑战和威胁，它必须加强团结、协调步伐，共同面对现实。
    关于阿以争端，卡利比说，阿拉伯联盟非斯、安曼和阿尔及尔３次首脑会议已经制订出解决争端的原则。“国际会议是导致实现阿拉伯和平计划的途径”。他指出，沙米尔选举计划的真实意图是阻止巴勒斯坦人民起义。
    卡利比讲话还涉及到本次会议将讨论的两伊关系问题和黎巴嫩危机。
    埃及外长马吉德在会上发言，对阿盟外长会议进程表示满意。
