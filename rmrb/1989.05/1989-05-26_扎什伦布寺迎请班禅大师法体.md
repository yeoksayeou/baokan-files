Title: Zhashi Lumbun Monastery Welcomes the Relics of the Panchen Lama
Author: Liu Wei
Date: 1989-05-26
Edition: 2

    Zhashi Lumbun Monastery Welcomes the Relics of the Panchen Lama

    Xinhua News Agency, Shigatse, May 26th — Reporter Liu Wei reports: Today, Shigatse in Tibet held a grand religious ceremony attended by approximately 40,000 people. The ceremony involved welcoming the relics of the Panchen Lama from Deqen Pochang to Zhashi Lumbun Monastery for the public to pay their respects.

### 扎什伦布寺迎请班禅大师法体
刘伟
1989-05-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　扎什伦布寺迎请班禅大师法体
    本报日喀则５月２６日电  记者刘伟报道：西藏日喀则今天举行由约四万人参加的隆重宗教仪式，把班禅大师的法体从德钦颇章迎请到扎什伦布寺，供广大僧俗群众瞻仰。
