Title: China's Television "Guinness Book of World Records" Series "Greatest of China" to Begin Filming
Author: Xiao Chen Gang Ji
Date: 1989-05-12
Edition: 4

To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, to promote the splendid and long history of the Chinese nation, to showcase the ever-changing development of China, to inspire the people's patriotism and national pride, and to promote understanding of China by people around the world, the television stations of Beijing, Anhui, Hubei, Hunan, Fujian, Guangdong, Gansu, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hebei, Henan, Heilongjiang, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Liaoning, Inner Mongolia, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shandong, Shanghai, Shanxi, Shaanxi, Tianjin, Xinjiang, Tibet, Yunnan, and Zhejiang recently held a meeting in Beijing. They decided to jointly produce the large-scale television series "Greatest of China." This is an unprecedented large-scale horizontal cooperation among domestic television stations.

The television series "Greatest of China" is a magazine-style series that combines knowledge, interest, and appreciation. It includes our great motherland's famous mountains and rivers, scenic spots, ancient architecture, historical relics, scientific inventions, construction achievements, outstanding figures, rare birds and animals, and unique flowers and plants, among other things that can be considered the "world's best" or "China's best." The first installment of the series is initially planned to have more than 30 episodes. The aforementioned television stations will also shoot sequels and strive to make this series the television "Guinness Book of World Records" of China. The series will be broadcast simultaneously on the prime-time slots of television stations in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions across the country before the National Day this year.

### 中国的电视“吉尼斯大全”  系列片《中华之最》将开拍
晓晨　刚毅
1989-05-12
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　中国的电视“吉尼斯大全”    
    系列片《中华之最》将开拍
    本报北京五月十二日讯  为庆祝中华人民共和国成立四十周年，弘扬中华民族灿烂悠久的历史文化，展示前进中的中国日新月异的发展变化，激发人民的爱国热情和民族自豪感，促进世界各国人民对中国的了解，北京、安徽、湖北、湖南、福建、广东、甘肃、广西、贵州、河北、河南、黑龙江、江苏、江西、辽宁、内蒙古、宁夏、青海、山东、上海、山西、陕西、天津、新疆、西藏、云南、浙江等二十七个省、市、自治区电视台最近在北京举行了联席会议。决定联合摄制大型电视系列片《中华之最》。这是国内电视台空前规模的横向大联合。
    电视系列片《中华之最》是一部集知识性、趣味性、欣赏性于一身的杂志式的系列片。它包容了我们伟大祖国的名山大川、风光名胜、古代建筑、历史文物、科技发明、建设成就、杰出人物、珍禽异兽、奇花异草等等称得上是“世界之最”或“中国之最”的千姿百态的内容。该系列片第一辑初定为三十集以上。上述各电视台还将拍摄续集，力图将该系列片办成中国的电视“吉尼斯大全”。该系列片将于今年国庆前夕在全国各省市自治区电视台的黄金时间同时播出。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（晓晨　刚毅）
