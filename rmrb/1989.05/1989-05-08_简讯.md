Date: 1989-05-08
Edition: 2

	Brief News

	▲ Vice Premier Yao Yilin of the State Council met with Nepalese Finance Minister Pradhan on May 8 in Zhongnanhai. The two sides had a sincere and friendly discussion on the development of trade between China and Nepal.

	▲ Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin held talks with Nepalese Finance Minister Pradhan in Beijing on May 8.

	▲ Vice Premier Wu Xueqian met with a delegation led by former Irish Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Peter Barry in Beijing on May 8.

	▲ State Councilor and Minister of Finance Wang Bingqian met with and hosted a banquet for a delegation led by Finance Minister of Western Samoa, Tuilaepa Sailele Malielegaoi, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on May 8.

	▲ State Councilor and Mayor of Beijing Chen Xitong met with a delegation led by Senate President Federico de Carvajal of Spain on May 8. Beijing and Madrid established sister-city relations in 1985.

	▲ The Beijing branch of the French European and American Bank officially opened on May 8. This is the 85th representative office of a foreign bank in China.

### 简讯

1989-05-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　简讯
    ▲国务院副总理姚依林５月８日在中南海会见尼泊尔财政大臣普拉丹，宾主就发展中尼两国贸易问题进行了诚挚友好的谈话。
    ▲对外经济贸易部部长郑拓彬８日在北京同尼泊尔财政大臣普拉丹举行了会谈。
    ▲吴学谦副总理８日在北京会见了爱尔兰前副总理兼外长彼得·巴里一行。
    ▲国务委员兼财政部长王丙乾８日在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请了西萨摩亚财政部长图依拉埃帕·萨依莱莱一行。
    ▲国务委员、北京市长陈希同８日会见了由参议长费德里科·德卡瓦哈尔率领的西班牙参议院代表团。北京和马德里１９８５年结为友好城市。
    ▲法国欧洲联合银行北京代表处８日正式开业。这是外国银行在中国开设的第８５家代表处。　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
