Title: India Completes Its First Solar Power Station
Author:
Date: 1989-05-07
Edition: 3

India's first solar power station was completed on May 3. This small-scale solar power station is located in the Solar Energy Center in Gurgaon, Haryana State, in northern India, and has a generating capacity of 50 kilowatts.

Currently, Japan is the only country in Asia that has a solar power station.

### 印度建成首座太阳能电站

1989-05-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　印度建成首座太阳能电站
    新华社新德里五月四日电  印度第一座太阳能发电站于三日建成。这座小型太阳能发电站位于印度北部哈里亚纳邦古尔冈市的太阳能中心，其发电能力为五十千瓦。
    目前，亚洲只有日本一个国家拥有太阳能发电站。
