Title: Pan Beilei Elected as Vice Governor of Shaanxi
Author:
Date: 1989-05-01
Edition: 2

Pan Beilei, a 48-year-old Taiwanese Hmong democrat, was elected as the Vice Governor of Shaanxi Province at the conclusion of the second session of the Seventh Shaanxi Provincial People's Congress today.

The session was held in Xi'an.

### 潘蓓蕾当选陕西副省长

1989-05-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　潘蓓蕾当选陕西副省长
    新华社西安四月三十日电  在今天结束的陕西省第七届人大二次会议上，现年四十八岁的台湾籍高山族民主人士潘蓓蕾当选为陕西省副省长。
