Title: Who Says We're Raising Prices Arbitrarily?
Author: Zhū Gēnhuá
Date: 1989-05-22
Page: 5

New brand, new packaging, new pricing.

Singing for 2,000 yuan, this is called quality for quality pricing.

Foreign goods, of course, should be a bit more expensive!

The raw materials I use have all increased in price, so why can't I raise my prices?

I only have price reductions here, no price increases.

People are selling for 1.5 yuan per pound, I'm selling for 1 yuan.

This is the price when I bought the goods!

### 谁说我们乱涨价？（图片）
朱根华
1989-05-22
第5版(国内专页（经济）)
专栏：

    谁说我们乱涨价？
    朱根华
    新牌子，新包装，新的定价。
    唱一首二千元，这叫优质优价
    外国货嘛，当然得贵一点！
    所用原料全涨了，就不许我涨？
    我这儿可只有落价，没有涨价。
    人卖一块五一斤，我卖一块。
    进货时就这价！
