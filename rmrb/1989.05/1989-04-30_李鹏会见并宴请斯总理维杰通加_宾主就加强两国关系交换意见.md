Title: Premier Li Peng Meets and Dines with Sri Lankan Prime Minister Vijetunga
Author:
Date: 1989-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 30th.

	Premier Li Peng met and dined with Sri Lankan Prime Minister Vijetunga this evening at the Great Hall of the People. The two leaders exchanged views on strengthening bilateral relations in a friendly atmosphere.

	Vijetunga, who is in China for a visit before attending the 22nd Annual Meeting of the Asian Development Bank in Beijing in early May, arrived in Beijing this afternoon.

	Vijetunga said during the meeting, "My choice of China as the first country to visit after becoming Prime Minister shows the friendly relations between Sri Lanka and China."

	The two premiers exchanged views on strengthening Sino-Sri Lankan relations in a friendly atmosphere.

	Premier Li Peng said, "The Chinese people deeply sympathize with the Sri Lankan people for the drought they have suffered. The Chinese government is willing to provide some relief supplies within its capacity to express the goodwill of the Chinese people."

	After the meeting, Premier Li Peng hosted a banquet for the Sri Lankan guests.

	In his toast, Li Peng said, "Sino-Sri Lankan friendship has withstood the test of time and has become a model for countries with different social systems to develop friendly cooperation on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence."

	Vijetunga said in his speech, "The basis of our bilateral relations is the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, which is also the cornerstone of our foreign policies." He praised China's aid to Sri Lanka and introduced Sri Lanka's domestic development plans.

	Also present at the banquet were Vice Premier and Minister of Finance Wang Bingqian and others.

### 李鹏会见并宴请斯总理维杰通加  宾主就加强两国关系交换意见
温宪
1989-04-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏会见并宴请斯总理维杰通加    
    宾主就加强两国关系交换意见
    本报北京４月３０日讯  记者温宪报道：国务院总理李鹏今天晚上在人民大会堂会见了斯里兰卡总理维杰通加。维杰通加一行是在出席５月上旬在北京召开的亚洲开发银行第２２届年会前顺访我国，于今天下午乘飞机抵达北京的。
    维杰通加总理在会见时说：“我担任总理后选择中国为第一个出访国家，这表明了斯中两国间的友好关系。”
    两国总理在友好的气氛中就加强中斯两国关系交换了意见。
    李鹏总理说，中国人民对斯里兰卡人民遭受旱灾表示深切的同情。中国政府在力所能及的范围内愿意向斯里兰卡提供一点救济物资，以表达中国人民的一点心意。
    会见后，李鹏总理设宴款待斯里兰卡贵宾。
    李鹏在祝酒辞中说，中斯友谊经受了时间的考验，已成为不同社会制度国家在和平共处五项原则基础上发展友好合作的典范。
    维杰通加总理在讲话中说，我们两国之间关系的基础是和平共处五项原则，而这又是我们两国外交政策的基石。他赞扬了中国对斯里兰卡的援助，并介绍了斯里兰卡国内发展计划。
    出席宴会的还有国务委员兼财政部长王丙乾等。
