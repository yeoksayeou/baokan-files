Title: "Hemostatic Fiber" New Drug Shows Remarkable Effectiveness
Author: ZHAO Kai, ZHUO Zhenpu
Date: 1989-05-08
Page: 2

**From the Four Corners**

The remarkable "hemostatic fiber" new drug, jointly developed by the Institute of Hematology of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences and Chengdu University of Science and Technology, is now being produced in collaboration by the Sichuan Deyang Pharmaceutical Factory and the Qinghai Bone Gelatin Factory. Its series of products are currently popular in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions across the country, including Taiwan Province. After being used in 192 types of injuries and illnesses and 923 cases of bleeding, its hemostatic effectiveness rate reached 96.2%. In 139 cases of brain tumor surgery (including 81 cases of malignant tumors), the hemostatic effectiveness rate reached 97%.

### “止血纤维”新药显奇效
赵凯　郅振璞
1989-05-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　“止血纤维”新药显奇效
    本报西宁五月八日电  由中国医学科学院血液学研究所和成都科技大学等单位协作研制的神奇“止血纤维”，经四川德阳制药厂和青海骨胶厂联营生产，其系列产品如今在包括台湾省在内的我国各省市区畅销不衰。经用于一百九十二个伤病种、九百二十三个出血病例，它的止血有效率高达百分之九十六点二；脑瘤外科手术一百三十九例（其中恶性肿瘤八十一例），止血有效率达百分之九十七。　　　　　　　　　　　
　
　　　　　　　　　　　　（赵凯　郅振璞）
