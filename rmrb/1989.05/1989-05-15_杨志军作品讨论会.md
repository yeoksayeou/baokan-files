Title: Discussion on Yang Zhijun's Works
Author: Lu Wen
Date: 1989-05-15
Edition: 6

The discussion meeting on the works of Yang Zhijun, the winner of the 1988 Wenhui Literary Newcomer Award, was held on May 10 in Beijing. The meeting was organized by the Lu Xun Literary Institute of the Chinese Writers Association and the Qinghai Provincial Writers Association. Invited to the meeting were critics and editors from Beijing, Shanxi, Gansu, and Qinghai, who conducted an in-depth discussion on Yang Zhijun's overall creation and specific works.

The attendees agreed that Yang Zhijun's works have a unique and pioneering theme, breaking away from the current literary creation dominated by a soft and delicate style, presenting a harsh and tragic artistic imagination to the audience. The series of contradictions in his novels, such as life and death, destruction and creation, meaning and meaninglessness, and man and nature, reflect a new cultural morality and value system. Yang Zhijun's works are both rich in rational realism and contain irrational absurdity. The harsh and cold western environment and western spirit depicted in his works provide convincing evidence for the existence and characteristics of western literature, making the concept of western literature clearer. However, Yang Zhijun's works are not flawless. The weakened plot often affects the reading effect, and the strengthened literary color can make some parts of the work difficult to understand.

### 杨志军作品讨论会
鲁文
1989-05-15
第6版(文艺评论)
专栏：

　　杨志军作品讨论会
    《文汇报》八八文学新人奖获奖作家杨志军的作品讨论会，于五月十日在北京召开。会议由中国作协鲁迅文学院与青海省文联主持，应邀到会的北京、山西、甘肃、青海的评论家、编辑针对杨志军的创作整体及具体作品进行了较为深入的讨论。
    与会同志认为，杨志军的创作具有其独特的、开创性的主题，打破了以阴柔风格为主流的当前的文学创作状况，为人们展示了严酷的悲剧性的艺术想象世界。他的小说中描述的生与死、毁灭与创造、意义与无意义、人与自然等一系列对立面构成的矛盾，反映了一种新的文化道德、价值观念。杨志军的创作既有较为浓重的理性的现实主义色彩，又不乏非理性的荒诞内容。作品中表现的严酷冷峻的西部环境与西部精神，为西部文学的存在与特色提供了具有说服力的证据，使西部文学的概念更加清晰了。但是，杨志军的作品并不是完美无缺的，淡化的故事情节往往影响了阅读效果，强化的文学色彩又会造成作品中某些部分的艰涩。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（鲁文）
