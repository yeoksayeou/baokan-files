Title: North Korea Achieves Success in Room-Temperature Nuclear Fusion
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-05-08
Edition: 3

North Korea Achieves Success in Room-Temperature Nuclear Fusion

	Pyongyang, May 8 (Xinhua) — According to the Korean Central News Agency, scientists at Kim Il-sung University have recently succeeded in conducting room-temperature nuclear fusion experiments.

	The scientists at the university placed palladium and platinum electrodes in heavy water and then performed electrolysis. The result was the release of neutrons.

	Another research group used a similar method and, through experiments, not only verified the above results but also discovered new phenomena.

	The report states that the success of Kim Il-sung University's room-temperature nuclear fusion experiment demonstrates the broad prospects for developing new energy sources in the field of new technologies. Scientists are currently conducting in-depth research on the results achieved.

### 朝试验室温核聚变成功
徐宝康
1989-05-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　朝试验室温核聚变成功
    本报平壤５月８日电  记者徐宝康报道：据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，金日成综合大学科研所的科学家们，最近试验成功室温核聚变。
    这所大学的科学家们，在重水里放入钯电极和铂电极，然后进行电解，结果发现释放出了中子。
    另一科研小组用类似的方法，经过试验不仅验证了上述试验结果，而且还发现了新的现象。
    报道说，金日成综合大学室温核聚变的试验成功，展示了在新的科技领域里开发新能源的广阔前景。目前，科学家们正在深入研究取得的成果。
