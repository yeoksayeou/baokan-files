Title: The First Gold Medal Decided at the World Model Boat Racing Championship
Author: Chen Bingqi, Sun Yongsheng
Date: 1989-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 23rd.

Shanghai athlete Lu Weifeng won the first gold medal at the sixth World Model Boat (Powerboat) Championship today, breaking a world record.

The 20-year-old Lu Weifeng achieved an outstanding result of 15.9 seconds and 146.82 points in the final round of the F3-V class freestyle design slalom race, securing the first championship of this tournament.

This result broke the world record of 16.1 seconds and 146.78 points set by Guangdong athlete Chen Zhaolun yesterday.

Lu Weifeng also achieved a result of 16.1 seconds and 146.78 points in the F3-E class race yesterday.

The second and third places in the F3-V class were won by Chen Zhaolun and Hunan athlete Ou Yang Xiangyang, with results of 16.1 seconds (146.78 points) and 16.5 seconds (146.70 points), respectively.

Athletes from Bulgaria, East Germany, West Germany, Hong Kong, and China participated in this event, totaling 11 athletes.

The competition consisted of three rounds, with the best result from each athlete counted as their final score.

The originally scheduled decision for the top three in the F3-E class was postponed due to strong winds, along with other events.

The judging committee of the championship announced this afternoon that the competition will be postponed by one day.

### 世界航海模型赛决出首枚金牌
陈炳其　孙永盛
1989-05-23
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　世界航海模型赛决出首枚金牌
    新华社天津５月２３日电  （记者陈炳其、孙永盛）上海选手陆炜峰今天在这里夺得第六届世界航海模型（动力艇）锦标赛第一枚金牌，并打破一项世界纪录。
    ２０岁的陆炜峰今天在自由设计的绕标竞速艇Ｆ３—Ｖ级的最后一轮比赛中，以１５秒９和１４６．８２分的优异成绩赢得本届锦标赛的第一个冠军。
    他的这个成绩打破了广东选手陈兆伦昨天创造的１６秒１和１４６．７８分的世界纪录。
    陆炜峰昨天在Ｆ３—Ｅ级比赛中也赛出１６秒１和１４６．７８分的成绩。
    获得Ｆ３—Ｖ级第二和第三名的是陈兆伦和湖南选手欧阳向阳。
    他们的成绩分别是１６秒１（１４６．７８分）和１６秒５（１４６．７０分）。
    来自保加利亚、民主德国、联邦德国、香港和中国的１１名运动员参加了这个项目的角逐。
    比赛一共进行３轮，以其中的最好成绩作为个人的项目成绩。
    原定今天将决出Ｆ３—Ｅ级前三名，由于风大，同其他项目一起暂停。
    锦标赛的裁判委员会今天下午宣布，比赛往后延期１天。
