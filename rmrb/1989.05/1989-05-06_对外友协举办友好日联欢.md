Title: The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Hosts a Friendship Day Gala
Author:
Date: 1989-05-06
Edition: 2

More than 600 Chinese and foreign guests gathered at the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries this evening to celebrate "Friendship Day" and to mark the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Association.

Wu Xueqian, Hab Comin, and Liao Hansheng attended the event.

### 对外友协举办友好日联欢

1989-05-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　对外友协举办友好日联欢
    新华社北京五月五日电  六百多位中外各界人士今晚在中国人民对外友好协会聚集一堂，举办“友好日”联欢活动并庆祝对外友协成立三十五周年。
    吴学谦、习仲勋、廖汉生等出席了晚会。
