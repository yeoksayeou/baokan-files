Title: The ART Cup Chinese Instrument International Competition to be Held
Author: Yu Qingxin
Date: 1989-05-28
Edition: 4

The ART Cup Chinese Instrument International Competition will be held. According to a report, the "ART Cup Chinese Instrument International Competition," organized by the editorial department of *People's Music*, will take place from June 1 to June 6 in Beijing for the semifinals and finals.

This international competition of Chinese instruments is the first of its kind in the 40 years since the founding of the country. It aims to promote Chinese national music culture and, through the competition, make Chinese national music known and loved by people around the world.

The organizing committee of the competition has invited experts and scholars from the music circles of mainland China and Taiwan, as well as renowned scholars from the international music community, to serve as art consultants and judges.

The preliminary round of the "ART Cup Chinese Instrument International Competition" was held on March 27. From over 400 contestants from various regions across the country, as well as Hong Kong, Taiwan, Singapore, Macau, Japan, France, and other countries and regions, more than 150 contestants were selected to advance to the semifinals.

### ＡＲＴ杯中国乐器国际比赛将举行
于庆新
1989-05-28
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　ＡＲＴ杯中国乐器国际比赛将举行
    本报讯  由《人民音乐》编辑部等主办的“ＡＲＴ杯中国乐器国际比赛”，将于６月１日至６日在北京举行复赛、决赛。
    这次国际性的中国乐器比赛是建国４０年来的第一次，以宣传中国民族音乐文化为宗旨，通过比赛，使中国民族音乐得到世界人民的瞩目和喜爱。
    比赛组委会聘请了大陆和台湾音乐界的专家学者，并特邀了国外音乐界知名学者担任艺委、评委。
    “ＡＲＴ杯中国乐器国际比赛”的初赛已于３月２７日举行，从来自全国各地区及香港、台湾、新加坡、澳门、日本、法国等国家和地区的选手４００多人中评出１５０多名选手进入复赛。
      　　　　　　　（于庆新）
