Title: Soviet Foreign Ministry Spokesperson: The Sino-Soviet High-Level Meeting is of Great Significance
Author:
Date: 1989-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 12th.

The Soviet Foreign Ministry spokesperson, Perfiliev, said at a press conference on May 12th that the upcoming Sino-Soviet high-level meeting is not only of great significance for further developing bilateral relations but also for promoting the normalization of the international situation and for establishing a new and better political atmosphere in Asia and the world. Perfiliev also announced that Soviet Foreign Minister Shevardnadze, Politburo member Yakovlev, and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Chairman of the State Planning Committee Maslyukov will accompany Gorbachev on his visit to China.

### 苏联外交部发言人说  苏中高级会晤有重要意义  可推动国际间形成更良好政治气氛

1989-05-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　苏联外交部发言人说    
    苏中高级会晤有重要意义    
    可推动国际间形成更良好政治气氛
    新华社莫斯科５月１２日电  苏联外交部发言人佩尔菲利耶夫１２日在新闻发布会上说，即将举行的苏中高级会晤，不仅对进一步发展双边关系具有重要意义，而且对加深国际局势健康化，对在亚洲与全世界建立新的更良好的政治气氛的趋势也具有重要意义。佩尔菲利耶夫还宣布，苏联外交部长谢瓦尔德纳泽、苏共中央政治局委员雅科夫列夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席兼计委主席马斯柳科夫等将陪同戈尔巴乔夫访华。
