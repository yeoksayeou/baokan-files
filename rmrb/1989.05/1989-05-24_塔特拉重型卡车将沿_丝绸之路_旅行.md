Title: Tatra Heavy Truck to Travel Along the Silk Road
Author:
Date: 1989-05-24
Edition: 4

On May 25, a Tatra GTL 815 heavy truck produced in Czechoslovakia will depart from Beijing, continuing its global tour across five continents.

This global tour is held to celebrate the 100th anniversary of the invention of the automobile and the 90th anniversary of the first car produced in Czechoslovakia. The Tatra Global Travel Team from Czechoslovakia drove this Tatra heavy truck out of Prague on March 18, 1987, planning to spend three years traveling through more than 60 countries and regions.

After departing from Beijing, the Tatra Global Travel Team will drive along the ancient Silk Road, passing through Shijiazhuang, Zhengzhou, Luoyang, Xi'an, Tianshui, Lanzhou, Jiayuguan, Dunhuang, Hami, Turpan, Urumqi, Kuerle, Aksu, Kashgar, and Tashkurgan. On June 24, they will enter Pakistan through the Red Mountain Pass in Xinjiang, covering a distance of approximately 7,000 kilometers over 31 days.

### 塔特拉重型卡车将沿“丝绸之路”旅行

1989-05-24
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　塔特拉重型卡车将沿“丝绸之路”旅行
    新华社北京５月２３日电  ５月２５日，一辆捷克斯洛伐克生产的塔特拉ＧＴＬ８１５型重型卡车将从北京出发，继续其五大洲的环球旅行。
    此次环球旅行是为庆祝世界汽车诞生１００周年以及捷克斯洛伐克诞生第一辆汽车９０周年而举行的。捷克斯洛伐克塔特拉环球旅行团驾驶这辆塔特拉重型卡车于１９８７年３月１８日从布拉格出发，计划用３年时间，旅游６０多个国家和地区。
    捷克斯洛伐克塔特拉环球旅行团从北京出发后，将沿古老的“丝绸之路”行驶，途经石家庄、郑州、洛阳、西安、天水、兰州、嘉峪关、敦煌、哈密、吐鲁番、乌鲁木齐、库尔勒、阿克苏、喀什、塔什库尔干，６月２４日由新疆红其拉甫山口进入巴基斯坦，全程约７０００公里，为期３１天。
