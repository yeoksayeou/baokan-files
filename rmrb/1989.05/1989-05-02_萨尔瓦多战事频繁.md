Title: Frequent Battles in El Salvador
Author:
Date: 1989-05-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, May 1st

Frequent Battles in El Salvador

San Salvador - In April of this year, the guerrilla forces in El Salvador engaged in frequent battles with the government army, resulting in heavy casualties on both sides. According to the announcement by the guerrilla radio station "We Will Win" on May 1st, the guerrilla forces launched offensives against the government army across the country throughout April, killing or wounding 587 government soldiers, destroying a batch of military facilities, and blowing up or damaging a large number of power and communication facilities across the country. The guerrilla forces stated that the purpose of intensifying attacks on the government army is to pressure the government to enter into dialogue with the guerrilla forces to end the civil war.

### 萨尔瓦多战事频繁

1989-05-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　萨尔瓦多战事频繁
    据新华社墨西哥城５月１日电  圣萨尔瓦多消息：今年４月份，萨尔瓦多游击队与政府军战斗频繁，双方人员伤亡惨重。据萨游击队《我们必胜》电台１日宣布，游击队４月份在全国各地向政府军发动攻势，共打死打伤政府军官兵５８７名，破坏了一批军事设施，并在全国各地炸毁或破坏了大量的输电和通讯设备。游击队声称，对政府军加强攻势的目的在于压政府就结束内战问题与游击队对话。
