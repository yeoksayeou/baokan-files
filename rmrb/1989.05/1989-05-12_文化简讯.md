Title: Cultural News
Author:
Date: 1989-05-12
Page: 4

　　Japanese renowned singer Ayako Asaka performed the song "My Life" on the evening of May 10 at the Beijing International Hotel. This song won the first prize in the "Ayako Asaka Song Lyrics Grand Prize Contest" organized by the Guangming Daily. The contest received over 20,000 song lyrics in total.

　　The "1989 Sino-Japanese Calligraphy Art Exhibition," jointly organized by the Japanese Education Calligraphy Institute and the Beijing Branch of the Chinese Calligraphers Association, was held from May 10 to 14 at the National Art Museum of China in Beijing.

　　The Cultural Department Senior Cadres Choir, consisting of 50 members, led by Director and Conductor Qiu Li, recently traveled to Nanchang and Wuhan for a series of performances to boost morale.

　　The poetry collection "Red Handkerchief" by Yan Ziqing has been published by Sichuan Literature and Art Publishing House.

### 文化简讯

1989-05-12
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　文化简讯
    △日本著名歌星芦京子五月十日晚在北京国际饭店演唱了歌曲《我的人生》，这首歌在光明日报社主办的“芦京子演唱歌词大奖征集活动”中获得一等奖，这项活动共收到二万余首歌词。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　（闻）
    △由日本教育书艺院和中国书协北京分会等联合举办的《一九八九年中日书法艺术作品联展》五月十日至十四日在北京中国美术馆举行。
　　（文一）
    △文化部老干部合唱团一行五十人，在团长兼指挥秋里率领下，近期赴南昌、武汉慰问演出。　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（张世英）
　　△言子清诗集《红手帕》已由四川文艺出版社推出。　　　　（立）
