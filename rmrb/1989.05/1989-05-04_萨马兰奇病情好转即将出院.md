Title: Samaranch's Condition Improves, Ready to Be Discharged
Author:
Date: 1989-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd.

A spokesman for a hospital in Barcelona, Spain, announced on May 3rd that Juan Antonio Samaranch, the President of the International Olympic Committee, is set to be discharged. Samaranch was hospitalized on the evening of April 27th due to illness and was diagnosed with colon bleeding, but his condition is not severe. After receiving a blood transfusion and medication, the bleeding in the upper part of his colon has stopped. His physical condition is gradually improving, and he began eating normally starting from May 3rd. Samaranch has been on a liquid diet since his hospitalization, and his body is still somewhat weak.

### 萨马兰奇病情好转即将出院

1989-05-04
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　萨马兰奇病情好转即将出院
    本报讯  据新华社报道：西班牙巴塞罗那市一家医院的发言人３日宣布，在该院接受治疗的国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇即将出院。萨马兰奇因病于４月２７日晚住院后，被确诊患有结肠出血，但是病情不很严重。医生对他输血后辅以药物治疗，目前结肠上部流血已经止住。他的身体状况逐渐好转，从３日起开始正常饮食。萨马兰奇住院后由于一直进流食，身体仍有些虚弱。
