Title: The United Nations Again Urges Vietnam to Withdraw from Cambodia
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-05-26
Edition: 3

The United Nations Economic and Social Council today again called, without naming names, for the withdrawal of Vietnamese troops from Cambodia, so that the Cambodian people can enjoy basic freedoms and human rights, including the right to self-determination.

The resolution on Cambodian human rights was adopted with 43 votes in favor, including China, 7 votes against, including the Soviet Union, and 2 abstentions from Iraq and Libya.

The Economic and Social Council urged the United Nations Secretary-General to make every effort to achieve a comprehensive political settlement of the Cambodian issue and to restore basic human rights within Cambodia.

### 联合国再次呼吁越从柬撤军
刘开宸
1989-05-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：联合国简讯

　　联合国再次呼吁越从柬撤军
    本报联合国５月２４日电  记者刘开宸报道：联合国经济及社会理事会今天再次不指名地呼吁越南军队撤出柬埔寨，以便让柬埔寨人民享有基本自由和人权，包括自决权。
    关于柬埔寨人权的此项决议在表决时，中国等４３票赞成，苏联等７票反对，伊拉克和利比亚两票弃权。
    经社理事会要求联合国秘书长努力促成柬埔寨问题的全面政治解决，并恢复柬埔寨境内的基本人权。
