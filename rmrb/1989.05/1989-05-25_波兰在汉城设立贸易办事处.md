Title: Poland Establishes Trade Office in Seoul
Author:
Date: 1989-05-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 25th.

Poland has formally established a trade office in Seoul, South Korea.

It is the fourth trade office set up by a socialist country in Seoul. Prior to this, Hungary, Yugoslavia, and the Soviet Union had already established their respective trade representative offices in Seoul.

According to a Seoul radio broadcast, Poland's trade office in Seoul was established in accordance with an agreement signed between Poland and South Korea in November of last year in Seoul.

### 波兰在汉城设立贸易办事处

1989-05-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　波兰在汉城设立贸易办事处
    新华社平壤５月２５日电  汉城消息：波兰２４日在南朝鲜汉城正式设立了贸易办事处。
    它是社会主义国家在汉城开设的第４个贸易办事处。在此之前，匈牙利、南斯拉夫和苏联已相继在汉城设立了自己的贸易代表机构。
    据汉城电台广播，波兰驻汉城的这个贸易办事处，是根据波兰和南朝鲜去年１１月在汉城签署的一份协定而设立的。
