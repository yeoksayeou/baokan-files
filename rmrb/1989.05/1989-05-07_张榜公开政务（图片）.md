Date: 1989-05-07

Publicizing Government Affairs on a Bulletin Board

To strengthen public supervision, the county committee and government of Zhangqiu County, Shandong Province, recently published 45 items of government affairs on a bulletin board. These items include the responsibilities and division of labor among cadres, major administrative decisions for the entire county, standards for the supply of economic and production materials, and family planning, among other topics.
