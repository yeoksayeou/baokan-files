Title: UAE and Saudi Arabia Provide Large Sums of Aid to the PLO
Author:
Date: 1989-05-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Casablanca, May 25th.

The President of the United Arab Emirates, Al Nahyan, who is attending the Arab Summit in Casablanca, announced that his country has provided $10 million in aid to support the Palestinian uprising.

Another high-ranking Saudi Arabian official said on May 15th that since 1979, Saudi Arabia has provided $850 million in aid to the Palestine Liberation Organization. It is not yet clear how much aid the UAE has provided in total to the PLO.

Earlier this year, Saudi King Fahd decided to increase monthly aid to the PLO by $6.02 million to support the Palestinian uprising.

### 阿联酋沙特巨款援助巴解

1989-05-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　阿联酋沙特巨款援助巴解
    新华社卡萨布兰卡５月２５日电  正在这里出席阿拉伯特别首脑会议的阿拉伯联合酋长国总统阿勒纳哈扬宣布，他的国家为支持巴勒斯坦起义斗争提供了１０００万美元的援助。
    另据沙特阿拉伯一高级官员本月１５日说，沙特从１９７９年至今已向巴勒斯坦解放组织提供了８亿５千万美元的援助。目前尚不清楚阿联酋总共向巴解组织提供了多少援助。
    今年早些时候，沙特国王法赫德决定给巴解组织每月增加６０２万美元的援助，以支持巴勒斯坦起义斗争。
