Title: Thousands of South Africans March for Slain Anti-Apartheid Activist
Author:
Date: 1989-05-07
Edition: 3

On May 6, thousands of black and white South Africans linked arms and chanted slogans as they marched through the streets of Johannesburg. They were paying their last respects to David Webster, a white anti-apartheid activist who was recently murdered. The procession highlighted the growing unity among South Africans of different races in their struggle against the apartheid regime.
