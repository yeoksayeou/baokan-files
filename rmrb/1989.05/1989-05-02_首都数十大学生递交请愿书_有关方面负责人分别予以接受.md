Title: Capital Students Submit Petition
Author:
Date: 1989-05-02
Edition: 1

Capital students submit petition
Relevant officials accept petitions

New China News Agency, Beijing, May 2nd: Today, dozens of students from universities in the capital submitted a petition to the relevant authorities. The petition is addressed to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the State Council, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

Officials from the Information Office of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the Information Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the Information Office of the State Council respectively accepted the petitions.

### 首都数十大学生递交请愿书  有关方面负责人分别予以接受

1989-05-02
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　首都数十大学生递交请愿书    
    有关方面负责人分别予以接受
    新华社北京五月二日电  首都高校数十名学生今天下午向有关方面递交了他们给全国人大常委会、国务院和中共中央的“请愿书”。
    全国人大常委会办公厅信访局和中共中央办公厅、国务院办公厅信访局负责人分别接受了“请愿书”。
