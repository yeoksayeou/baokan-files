Title: The China-Japan Information Society Discussion Conference Concludes
Author: Pān Gǎng
Date: 1989-05-06
Page: 2

The China-Japan Information Society Discussion Conference, themed "The Construction and Life of the Information Society," concluded today in Beijing. More than ten experts and professors from both China and Japan presented and discussed Japan's experiences in the initial stage of constructing an information society, the relationship between the information society and China's reforms and economic construction, and explored the development trends and strategies for the information society at the end of this century and the beginning of the 21st century.

### 中日信息社会讨论会结束
潘岗
1989-05-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　中日信息社会讨论会结束
    本报北京五月六日讯  以“信息社会的建设和生活”为主题的中日讲演讨论会今天在京结束。中日两国十余位专家、教授，分别介绍、论证了日本在信息社会初级阶段的建设经验、信息社会与中国的改革和经济建设的关系；并就本世纪末、二十一世纪初的未来信息社会发展趋势及对策进行了探讨。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（潘岗）
