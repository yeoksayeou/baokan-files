Title: Three Advanced Enterprises in Shaanxi Province Removed from Their Titles
Author: Jing Xianfeng
Date: 1989-05-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, May 5th.

Three enterprises in Shaanxi Province—Shanxi Jinghua Electrical Appliance Factory, Baoji Organic Chemical Factory, and Xi'an City Post and Telecommunications Bureau—have recently had their "Provincial Advanced Enterprise" titles quietly revoked.

These enterprises have made several management mistakes and have had a series of accidents that have had negative impacts. Several other "Provincial Advanced Enterprises" have received yellow-card warnings.

### 陕西３家先进企业摘牌
景险峰
1989-05-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　陕西３家先进企业摘牌
    本报西安５月５日电  记者景险峰报道：陕西庆华电器制造厂、宝鸡有机化工厂和西安市电信局３个单位的“省级先进企业”牌子，前不久被悄然摘去。
    这几家企业管理失误较多，连续发生事故产生了不良影响。另有几家“省级先进企业”受到黄牌警告。
