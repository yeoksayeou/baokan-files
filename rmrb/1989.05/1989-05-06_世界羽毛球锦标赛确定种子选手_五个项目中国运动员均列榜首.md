Title: Seed Players for the World Badminton Championships Confirmed
Author: Liang Jinxiong, Zhou Zongxin
Date: 1989-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 5th.

The seed players for the various events in the sixth World Badminton Championships, which will begin on May 29 in Jakarta, have been confirmed. Chinese athletes top the list for all five events.

The current year's All England Men's Singles champion, Yang Yang, is ranked first in the men's singles. The Danish veteran Frost, who is over 30, is second. Bear Guo Bao (China) and Sugiyarto (Indonesia) are listed as the third and fourth seeds. Bo-E. Larsen (Denmark), Zhang Qingwu (China), Ro Tianning (Indonesia), and Zhao Jianhua (China) are ranked fifth to eighth. Yang Yang, Zhao Jianhua, Sugiyarto, and Bo-E. Larsen are in the upper half of the draw.

The top two seeds in the women's singles are Li Lingwei and Han Aiping. Lee Young-sook (South Korea) and Huang Hua (China) are ranked third and fourth. The fifth to eighth seeds are Wang Lianxiang (Indonesia), Tang Jiuhong (China), K. Magnusson (Sweden), and K. Larsen (Denmark). Additionally, Chinese athletes Shang Fumei and Shi Xiaohui are among the ninth to sixteenth seeds.

The top three pairs in the men's doubles are Li Yongbo/Tian Bingyi (China), Park Joo-bong/Lee Sang-bok (South Korea), and R. Sidik/J. Sidik (Malaysia).

Lin Ying/Guan Weizhen (China), Jeong Myung-hee/Hwang Hye-young (South Korea), and Sun Xiaqing/Zhou Lei (China) are ranked first to third in the women's doubles.

Chinese athletes Wang Pengren/Shi Fangjing and South Korean pair Park Joo-bong/Jeong Myung-hee are tied for the top seed in the mixed doubles. The third to eighth seeds are Hong Chong-ming/Verawaty (Indonesia), Yang Anson/M. Bengtsson (Sweden), Jiang Guoliang/Nong Qunhua (China), J. Knudsen/N. Nielsen (Denmark), Jan Paulsen/D. Kjaer (Denmark), and A. J. Gilmour/G. Gow (England).

The first Sudirman Cup World Mixed Team Championships also completed the draw. The six A-level teams are divided into two groups for the preliminary rounds. China, Sweden, and Denmark are in Group A, while Indonesia, South Korea, and England are in Group B. The Chinese team will face Sweden on May 24, kicking off the team championships.

### 世界羽毛球锦标赛确定种子选手  五个项目中国运动员均列榜首
梁金雄　周宗欣
1989-05-06
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　世界羽毛球锦标赛确定种子选手   
    五个项目中国运动员均列榜首
    新华社北京５月５日电  记者梁金雄、周宗欣今天从中国羽毛球协会获悉，本月２９日在雅加达开始的第六届世界羽毛球锦标赛各项种子选手座次已经确定，全部５个项目的头号种子选手都是中国运动员。
    今年全英赛男单冠军杨阳排在男单首位。已过而立之年的丹麦老将弗罗斯特居第二。熊国宝（中国）和苏吉亚托（印尼）被列为三至四号种子。波—埃·拉尔森（丹麦）、张青武（中国）、罗天宁（印尼）和赵剑华（中国）列五至八号种子。其中，杨阳、赵剑华、苏吉亚托、波—埃·拉尔森分在上半区。
    女单一、二号种子选手是名将李玲蔚和韩爱平，南朝鲜的李英淑和中国的黄华列为三至四号种子。列五至八号种子选手是王莲香（印尼）、唐九红（中国）、克·马格努松（瑞典）、基·拉尔森（丹麦）。此外，中国选手尚幅梅、史晓惠在９至１６号种子选手之列。
    男双前三对种子选手是李永波／田秉毅（中国）、朴柱奉／李相福（南朝鲜）、拉·西德克／贾·西德克（马来西亚）。
    林瑛／关渭贞（中国）、郑明熙／黄惠英（南朝鲜）、孙小青／周雷（中国）分列女双前三位种子。
    我国选手王朋仁／史方静和南朝鲜的朴柱奉／郑明熙难分伯仲，并列混双一号种子。列三至八号种子的是洪忠中／维拉华蒂（印尼）、扬·安通松／玛·本特松（瑞典）、蒋国良／农群华（中国）、耶·克努森／内·尼尔森（丹麦）、享·斯瓦勒／多·克耶尔（丹麦）和安·吉德／吉·高尔斯（英格兰）。
    首届苏迪曼杯世界混合团体赛也抽签完毕。６个Ａ级队先分两组预赛，中国、瑞典和丹麦队分在Ａ组，Ｂ组队是印度尼西亚、南朝鲜和英格兰。中国队２４日将首战瑞典队，揭开本届团体赛的战幕。
