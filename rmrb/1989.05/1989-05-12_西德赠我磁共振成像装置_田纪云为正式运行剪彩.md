Title: West Germany Donates Magnetic Resonance Imaging Equipment to China
Author: 陈雁 (Chen Yan)
Date: 1989-05-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 12th.

The magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) equipment donated by the Federal Republic of Germany's Chancellor Helmut Kohl to the Chinese government was officially put into operation today at the China Rehabilitation Research Center. Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council Tian Jiyun and German Government State Secretary Rünger cut the ribbon at the inauguration ceremony.

This state-of-the-art medical imaging device, designed and manufactured by Siemens of the Federal Republic of Germany, is the most advanced and fully equipped MRI machine in China. It can diagnose minute physiological structures or pathological changes within the human body, enabling early detection of tumors, strokes, hemorrhages, inflammations, and degenerative diseases.

German Government State Secretary Rünger read out Chancellor Kohl's congratulatory message at the inauguration ceremony.

### 西德赠我磁共振成像装置  田纪云为正式运行剪彩
陈雁
1989-05-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　西德赠我磁共振成像装置    
    田纪云为正式运行剪彩
    据新华社北京五月十二日电（记者陈雁）由联邦德国总理科尔赠送给中国政府的礼物——磁共振成像装置，今天在中国康复研究中心正式运行。中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理田纪云和联邦德国政府国务秘书冷格尔在运行典礼上剪彩。
    这套由联邦德国西门子公司设计、制造的强磁场超导型八十年代最新医学成像设备，是目前在我国磁场最强、配置最齐全的现代化医院诊断设备，可诊断人体内微小的生理结构或病理改变，早期发现肿瘤、梗塞、出血、炎症、变性等病理改变。
    联邦德国政府国务秘书冷格尔在运行典礼上宣读了联邦德国总理科尔的贺辞。
