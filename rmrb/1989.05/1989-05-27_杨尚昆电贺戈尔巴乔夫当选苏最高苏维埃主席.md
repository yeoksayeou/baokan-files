Title: Yang Shangkun Congratulates Gorbachev on Elected as Chairman of the Supreme Soviet
Author:
Date: 1989-05-27
Edition: 1

The President of the People's Republic of China, Yang Shangkun, sent a telegram on May 26 to congratulate Mikhail Gorbachev on his election as Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To Mikhail Sergeyevich Gorbachev, Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics,

On the occasion of your election as Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, please accept my sincere congratulations and best wishes for your success in this important and responsible position.

Not long ago, you made a historic visit to the People's Republic of China. Your meeting with the Chinese leadership marked the end of the past and the beginning of a new future, achieving the normalization of Sino-Soviet relations. I wish the relations between China and the Soviet Union to continue to develop and strengthen.

### 杨尚昆电贺戈尔巴乔夫当选苏最高苏维埃主席

1989-05-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　杨尚昆电贺戈尔巴乔夫
    当选苏最高苏维埃主席
    新华社北京５月２７日电  中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆２６日打电报给苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫，祝贺他当选为苏联最高苏维埃主席。电报全文如下：苏联最高苏维埃主席米·谢·戈尔巴乔夫同志
    在您当选为苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席之际，谨表示衷心的祝贺，祝您在这一崇高、负责的岗位上取得成就。
    不久前您对中华人民共和国进行了有历史意义的访问。您同中国领导人举行的会晤结束了过去、开辟了未来，实现了中苏关系正常化。祝中苏两国关系不断得到发展和加强。
