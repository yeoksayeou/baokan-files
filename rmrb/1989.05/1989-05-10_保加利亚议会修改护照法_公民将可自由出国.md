Title: Bulgarian Parliament Amends Passport Law
Author:
Date: 1989-05-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, May 9th.

Every citizen of Bulgaria will have the right to obtain an exit passport and freely enter and leave Bulgaria, and temporarily or permanently reside abroad.

This is the new regulation after the Bulgarian National Assembly's 9th session of the 10th term amended the exit passport law on May 9th.

Bulgarian Foreign Minister Mladenov said in the meeting that the new passport law is a manifestation of Bulgaria's social democracy and humanism, and it is in line with the spirit of the human rights provisions in the final document of the Vienna Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe.

### 保加利亚议会修改护照法  公民将可自由出国

1989-05-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　保加利亚议会修改护照法    
    公民将可自由出国
    新华社索非亚５月９日电  保加利亚每个公民将有权领取出国护照，自由出入保加利亚，到国外临时或长期居住。
    这是９日举行的保国民议会第９届１０次会议对出国护照法作了修改后的新规定。
    保加利亚外长姆拉德诺夫在会上发言说，新的护照法是保社会民主与人道主义的表现，它同维也纳欧安会最后文件中关于人权的精神是相符的。
