Title: A Major Boat Capsizing Accident Occurs in Hanshou
Author: Wú Xīnghuá
Date: 1989-05-29
Edition: 2

A major boat capsizing accident occurred in Hanshou County.

The People's Daily, Changsha, May 29th: According to a telephone report from the office of the Changde Municipal People's Government in Hunan Province to the Hunan Provincial Government, this morning, nearly one hundred villagers from Zhouwentang Township in Hanshou County, which is under the jurisdiction of Changde City, boarded a borrowed twelve-ton capacity motor-sailboat to carry out flood channel obstruction removal work in the Yuanjiang River. At around 10:15 AM, a capsizing accident occurred. By the time of this report at 8 PM, it has been confirmed that 54 people have died.

### 汉寿发生一起重大翻船事故
吴兴华
1989-05-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　汉寿发生一起重大翻船事故
    本报长沙五月二十九日电  记者吴兴华报道：据湖南省常德市人民政府办公室向湖南省政府电话报告：今天上午，该市所属的汉寿县周文庙乡近百名村民，乘坐借来的一条装载量为十二吨的机帆船，在沅江江中进行洪道扫障工作。上午十点十五分左右，发生翻船事故。到记者下午八点发稿时为止，经了解，已查明死亡五十四人。
