Title: The Top Ten Trading Nations in the World
Author:
Date: 1989-05-12
Edition: 7

Recently, the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (GATT) published materials on the ten largest trading countries or regions in the world for the year 1988. Below are the foreign trade volumes of these countries or regions (in billions of US dollars).

	United States		781.0
	West Germany		545.0
	Japan			452.0
	France			341.0
	United Kingdom		334.0
	Italy			268.0
	Canada			227.0
	Soviet Union		218.0
	Netherlands		202.0
	South Korea		113.0
