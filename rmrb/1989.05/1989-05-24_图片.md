Title: The First Generation of Tibetan Horse Carriage Drivers
Author: Yang Changhai
Date: 1989-05-24
Edition: 5

    In Nayi Township, Xiahe County, Gannan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, Gansu Province, the first generation of Tibetan horse carriage drivers has emerged. Now, they have entered the market to engage in short-distance transportation, marking the end of the traditional method of using yaks for transportation. The image shows a team of Tibetan horse carriages entering the city to transport bricks and tiles for a construction site. Photographed by Yang Changhai.
