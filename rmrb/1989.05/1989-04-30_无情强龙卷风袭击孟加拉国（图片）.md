Title: Ruthless Strong Tornado Strikes Bangladesh
Author:
Date: 1989-04-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

On April 28, Bangladesh President H.M. Ershad (second from right) visited the central region affected by the tornado.

A devastating tornado recently struck the central region of Manikganj in Bangladesh, resulting in over 1,000 deaths, more than 10,000 injuries, and leaving 100,000 people homeless. The image depicts the grim scene of a rural village reduced to ruins by the tornado.

### 无情强龙卷风袭击孟加拉国（图片）

1989-04-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    无情强龙卷风袭击孟加拉国
    4月28日，孟加拉国总统艾尔沙德（右二）到遭到龙卷风袭击的中部地区视察。

    孟加拉国中部马尼拉克甘杰日前遭到特大龙卷风袭击，造成1000多人死亡，1万多人受伤，10万人无家可归。图为一个被龙卷风夷为平地的乡村小镇惨景。
