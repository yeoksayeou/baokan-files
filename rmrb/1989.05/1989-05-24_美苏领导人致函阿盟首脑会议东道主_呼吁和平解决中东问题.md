Title: US and Soviet Leaders Write to Host of Arab Summit
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-05-24
Edition: 3

US President Bush and Soviet leader Gorbachev each wrote letters to the host of this year's Arab Summit, King Hassan II of Morocco, urging a peaceful resolution to the Middle East problem.

Bush, in his letter, stated that the Israeli proposal includes political negotiations over the final status of the occupied territories. The Soviet Union does not reject the Israeli suggestion, acknowledging that it contains positive elements, and requests that Israel further clarify its position. The United States believes that the Israeli proposal "can open a dialogue path for negotiations between Israel and the Palestinians over the occupied territories."

Gorbachev, in his letter to Hassan II, believes that the conditions for progress in the Middle East peace process are already in place. He advocates for the United Nations to play a role and convene an international conference on Middle East peace. Gorbachev stated that the Soviet Union does not reject "other arrangements" for achieving Middle East peace goals.

### 美苏领导人致函阿盟首脑会议东道主  呼吁和平解决中东问题
林皎明
1989-05-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美苏领导人致函阿盟首脑会议东道主
    呼吁和平解决中东问题
    本报卡萨布兰卡５月２４日电  记者林皎明报道：美国总统布什和苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫分别给本届阿拉伯特别首脑会议东道主、摩洛哥国王哈桑二世写信，从不同角度呼吁和平解决中东问题。
    布什总统在信中表示，以色列方案包含对被占领土最终地位的政治谈判，苏联不排斥以色列建议，承认其中包含积极因素，要求以色列进一步阐明。美国认为，以色列建议“可以在以色列与巴勒斯坦人之间就被占领土举行谈判开创对话途径”。
    戈尔巴乔夫在给哈桑二世的信中认为，中东地区和平进程取得进展的条件已经具备。他主张联合国发挥作用，召开中东和平国际会议。戈尔巴乔夫表示，苏联并不拒绝为实现中东和平目标的“其它安排”。
