Title: Newly Formed Chinese Men's Basketball Team Competes in Japan
Author:
Date: 1989-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 4th.

The newly formed Chinese men's basketball team competed in Japan and suffered consecutive losses to the South Korean team and the Japanese team.

The international men's basketball invitational tournament held in Tokyo concluded on May 2. The newly formed Chinese national men's basketball team finished with one win and two losses, securing third place. The South Korean team won all three of their matches, taking first place. The Japanese national team finished with two wins and one loss, securing second place. The Japanese university team lost all three of their matches and finished in last place.

### 新组建的中国男篮出征日本  接连负于南朝鲜队和日本队

1989-05-04
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　新组建的中国男篮出征日本    
    接连负于南朝鲜队和日本队
    本报讯  据新华社报道：在东京举行的国际男子篮球邀请赛５月２日结束。新组建的中国国家男篮以一胜二负的成绩获得第三名。南朝鲜队三战三胜，名列第一；日本国家队二胜一负，获得亚军；日本大学生队三战皆负，敬陪末座。
