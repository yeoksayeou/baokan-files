Title: Wan Li Begins U.S. Visit in Washington
Author: Wei Guoqiang
Date: 1989-05-22
Edition: 1

	Wan Li, chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, arrived in Washington, D.C., today to begin his official friendly visit to the United States.

	Wan Li will stay in Washington for five days, meeting with U.S. President George H.W. Bush, Vice President Dan Quayle, Secretary of State James Baker, Secretary of Defense Dick Cheney, leaders of both houses of Congress, and some members of Congress. He will then visit Florida and New York.

	Wan Li arrived here after successfully concluding his 10-day visit to Canada.

### 万里抵华盛顿开始访美
魏国强
1989-05-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　万里抵华盛顿开始访美
    据新华社华盛顿五月二十二日电  （记者魏国强）中国人大常委会委员长万里今天上午抵达美国首都华盛顿，开始对美国进行正式友好访问。
    万里将在华盛顿逗留五天，会见美国总统布什、副总统奎尔、国务卿贝克、国防部长托尔、参众两院领导人及部分国会议员。此后，他将前往佛罗里达州和纽约等地参观访问。
    万里是在圆满结束对加拿大的十天访问后抵达这里的。
