Title: Soviet Leader Gorbachev Arrives in Beijing Today
Author:
Date: 1989-05-14
Edition: 1

Soviet Leader Gorbachev Arrives in Beijing Today

The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and General Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, Mikhail Sergeyevich Gorbachev, will arrive in our country on the 15th for an official visit. This is the first visit by the highest-level Soviet leader to our country since 1959.

Gorbachev is a Russian, born on March 2, 1931, in the village of Privolnoe in the Stavropol Krai of southern USSR to a peasant family. At the age of 15, he worked as a mechanic at the tractor repair station of a state farm. In 1950, he entered the Law Department of Moscow University and joined the Communist Party of the Soviet Union in 1952. After graduating with honors in 1955, he returned to his hometown to work, serving successively as the Deputy Head of the Propaganda Department of the Komsomol Committee of the Krai, the First Secretary of the Stavropol City Komsomol Committee, and the Second and First Secretary of the Komsomol Committee of the Krai. In March 1962, he became the Party organizer of the Stavropol Krai Party organization of the State Farm-Sovkhoz Production Management Department, and in December of the same year, he became the Head of the Party Organization Department of the Stavropol Agricultural Krai Party Committee. From September 1962 to 1967, he completed correspondence courses at the Stavropol Agricultural Academy and obtained a diploma in agricultural economics.

In September 1966, Gorbachev became the First Secretary of the Stavropol City Party Committee, in August 1968, the Second Secretary of the Stavropol Krai Party Committee, and in April 1970, the First Secretary. Since 1971, he has been a member of the Central Committee of the CPSU and has served as a delegate to the 22nd, 24th, 25th, 26th, and 27th Congresses of the CPSU and a delegate to the 19th National Congress of the CPSU.

In November 1978, Gorbachev became a Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, in November 1979, a Candidate Member of the Politburo of the CPSU Central Committee, and in October of the following year, a full member. On March 11, 1985, he was elected General Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee.

He is a member of the 8th to 11th Supreme Soviets of the USSR and has also served as a member of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation. From 1985 to 1988, he was a member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. On October 1, 1988, he was elected Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

After taking office as General Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, Gorbachev implemented a series of major measures domestically, launching economic and political reforms. He adjusted the Soviet Union's foreign policy step by step according to the "new thinking."

Gorbachev has been awarded three Orders of Lenin, the Order of the October Revolution, and the Order of the Red Banner of Labor.

(Xinhua News Agency) (with photo)

### 苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫  今日抵京访问我国

1989-05-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：人物介绍

　　苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫    
    今日抵京访问我国
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席、苏共中央总书记米哈伊尔·谢尔盖耶维奇·戈尔巴乔夫将于１５日抵达我国进行正式访问，这是自１９５９年以来苏联最高级领导人对我国的第一次访问。
    戈尔巴乔夫是俄罗斯人，１９３１年３月２日生于苏联南部斯塔夫罗波尔边疆区普里沃利诺耶村的农民家庭。从１５岁起，在农庄的拖拉机机械站任机械师。１９５０年他进入莫斯科大学法律系学习，１９５２年加入苏联共产党。１９５５年以优异成绩毕业后回到家乡工作，先后任苏联共产主义青年团边疆区委宣传部副部长、斯塔夫罗波尔团市委第一书记，边疆区团委第二、第一书记。１９６２年３月任苏共斯塔夫罗波尔区域农庄—农场生产管理局边疆区党的组织员，同年１２月任苏共斯塔夫罗波尔农业边疆区党机关局局长。１９６２年９月—１９６７年他完成了斯塔夫罗波尔农业学院的函授学习，获得农业经济学的毕业证书。
    １９６６年９月戈尔巴乔夫任苏共斯塔夫罗波尔市委第一书记，１９６８年８月任苏共斯塔夫罗波尔边疆区委第二书记，１９７０年４月任第一书记。他从１９７１年起为苏共中央委员，历任苏共第２２、２４、２５、２６和２７次代表大会代表和苏共第１９次全国代表会议代表。
    １９７８年１１月戈尔巴乔夫任苏共中央书记，１９７９年１１月任苏共中央政治局候补委员，次年１０月升为政治局委员。１９８５年３月１１日，当选为苏共中央总书记。
    他是苏联第８—１１届最高苏维埃代表，也曾任俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃代表，从１９８５到１９８８年为苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员。１９８８年１０月１日当选为苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席。
    戈尔巴乔夫就任苏共中央总书记后，在国内采取一系列重大措施，展开经济和政治体制改革。他按照“新思维”，逐步调整苏联的对外政策。
    戈尔巴乔夫曾获３枚列宁勋章，及十月革命勋章和劳动红旗勋章。
