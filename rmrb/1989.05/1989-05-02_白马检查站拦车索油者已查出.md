Title: Individuals Demanding Fuel at the Bai Ma Checkpoint Have Been Identified
Author:
Date: 1989-05-02
Page: 6

Your newspaper published a letter from a reader on February 11th on the fifth page titled "Vehicles at the Bai Ma Checkpoint Only Allowed to Pass After Being Refueled." Upon investigation, it was found that the individuals demanding fuel were two temporary workers sent by the county's grain bureau to perform duties. The Ningxia County government immediately instructed relevant departments to investigate the responsibility and handle the involved personnel.

Ningxia Public Security Bureau Traffic Police Corps

### 白马检查站拦车索油者已查出

1989-05-02
第6版(读者来信)
专栏：答复反应

　　白马检查站拦车索油者已查出
    你报２月１１日第五版刊登《白马车辆检查站灌走汽油才放行》的读者来信。经查，拦车索油是县粮食局派来执勤的两名临时工所为。中宁县政府立即责成有关部门查清责任，处理有关人员。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　宁夏公安厅交通警察总队
