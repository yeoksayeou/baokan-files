Title: The Full Meeting of the Drafting Committee for the Basic Law of the Macao SAR Concludes
Author: Huang Riyao
Date: 1989-05-10
Edition: 2

The full meeting of the Drafting Committee for the Basic Law of the Macao Special Administrative Region concluded today in Beijing.

The meeting reviewed and approved the "Working Rules of the Drafting Committee for the Basic Law of the Macao Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China." The meeting also passed the "Decision on Establishing a Special Group to Draft the Structure of the Basic Law of the Macao Special Administrative Region (Draft)." It was decided to establish a drafting group consisting of the secretary-general of the drafting committee, one deputy secretary-general, and three members from the mainland and three from Macao to be responsible for drafting the structure of the Basic Law of the Macao Special Administrative Region (Draft). The members are: Liu Zhaohua, Xu Chongde, Xiao Yuyun, Wu Rongke, Wu Jianhui, Hu Choucheng, Huang Hanqiang, and Lu Ping. The conveners of the drafting group are Lu Ping and Hu Choucheng.

The plenary session this afternoon was presided over by Ji Pengfei, Director of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office and Chairman of the Drafting Committee for the Basic Law of the Macao Special Administrative Region. He said that the special group should carefully study the relevant provisions of the Sino-Portuguese Joint Declaration, conduct in-depth investigations and understand the actual situation in Macao, widely solicit and listen to the opinions of people from all walks of life, and do a good job in drafting the Basic Law of the Macao Special Administrative Region.

The meeting decided that the third plenary session of the Drafting Committee for the Basic Law of the Macao Special Administrative Region will be held from November 18 to 20, 1989, in Guangzhou.

### 澳门基本法草委会全会闭幕  通过工作规则成立基本法结构起草小组
黄日尧
1989-05-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　澳门基本法草委会全会闭幕    
    通过工作规则成立基本法结构起草小组
    新华社北京５月１０日电（记者黄日尧）中华人民共和国澳门特别行政区基本法起草委员会第二次全体会议今天下午在北京闭幕。
    这次会议审议通过了《中华人民共和国澳门特别行政区基本法起草委员会工作规则》。会议同时通过了《关于成立起草澳门特别行政区基本法结构（草案）专门小组的决定》，决定成立一个由起草委员会秘书长，副秘书长１人，内地委员和澳门委员各３人组成的基本法结构（草案）起草小组，负责起草澳门特别行政区基本法结构（草案）。他们是：刘焯华、许崇德、肖蔚云、吴荣恪、吴建璠、胡厚诚、黄汉强、鲁平，起草小组的召集人是鲁平、胡厚诚。
    今天下午的全体会议由国务院港澳办公室主任、澳门基本法起草委员会主任委员姬鹏飞主持。他说，专门小组要认真研究中葡联合声明的有关规定，深入调查了解澳门的实际情况，广泛征询和听取各界人士的意见，做好澳门特别行政区基本法的起草工作。
    会议决定，澳门特别行政区基本法起草委员会第三次全体会议于１９８９年１１月１８日至２０日在广州举行。
