Title: Nine New Products of the Great Wall Microcomputers Finalized
Author: Pī yī
Date: 1989-05-27
Edition: 2

Nine new products of the Great Wall series microcomputers, designed and produced by the China Great Wall Computer Group Corporation, were finalized today at a meeting presided over by the Ministry of Machinery and Electronics. This marks a new stage in the development of domestically produced microcomputers in China, characterized by series development and intensive production.

The nine new products include the Great Wall 386, 286EX, 286B, 0520DH, and 0520EM mainframes, the Great Wall 300 and 100A monitors, and the CMGA and CEGA display cards. Among these, the domestication of 16-bit and 32-bit microcomputers is a key national scientific and technological project for the "Seventh Five-Year Plan."

### 长城微机九种新产品定型
皮一
1989-05-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　长城微机九种新产品定型
    本报北京５月２７日讯  由中国长城计算机集团公司设计、生产的长城系列微机９种新产品，今天通过了机电部主持的设计生产定型。这表明我国国产化微机已进入开发系列化、生产集约化新阶段。
    这９项新产品包括长城３８６、２８６ＥＸ、２８６Ｂ、０５２０ＤＨ和０５２０ＥＭ型主机，长城３００和１００Ａ型监视器，以及ＣＭＧＡ和ＣＥＧＡ显示卡。其中，１６位和３２位微机国产化是国家“七五”重点科技攻关项目。
        （皮一）
