Title: Che Peiqin Elected as ADB Executive Director
Author:
Date: 1989-05-05
Edition: 2

    Che Peiqin, Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau and member of the Board of Directors of the People's Bank of China, was elected today as the Executive Director of the People's Republic of China to the Asian Development Bank at the 22nd Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors of the Asian Development Bank.

    China is the third-largest shareholder of the Asian Development Bank and constitutes a separate constituency, with an Executive Director's office set up at the ADB headquarters.

### 车培钦当选亚行执行董事

1989-05-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　车培钦当选亚行执行董事
    新华社北京５月５日电  中国人民银行理事会理事兼外事局局长车培钦今天在亚洲开发银行理事会第二十二届年会上，当选为中华人民共和国驻亚行执行董事。
    中国为亚洲开发银行的第三大认股国，单独构成选区并在亚行总部设有执行董事办公室。
