Title: The Complete Works of Guan Hanqing Published
Author: Han Ming
Date: 1989-05-21
Edition: 4

The most complete collection to date of the works of the world-renowned cultural figure and Chinese ancient dramatist Guan Hanqing, "The Complete Works of Guan Hanqing" (edited and annotated by Wu Guoqing), has been published by Guangdong Higher Education Press. Renowned drama expert Professor Wang Shisi wrote the preface and provided the "ci" (lyric) for the book.

专栏：

　　《关汉卿全集》问世
