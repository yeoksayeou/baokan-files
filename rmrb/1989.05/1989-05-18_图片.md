Title: Military Academy Assists in Local Military Training to Enhance Public Defense Awareness
Author: Zhang Ya Xin
Date: 1989-05-18
Edition: 5

The People's Liberation Army Xi'an Army Academy is assisting local efforts in conducting military training to strengthen the public's defense consciousness. The image shows military instructors teaching shooting techniques to trainees at the Xi'an People's Grand Hall.

Photo by Zhang Ya Xin, reporter for this newspaper.
