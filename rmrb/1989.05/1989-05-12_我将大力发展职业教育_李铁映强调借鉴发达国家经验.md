Title: I Will Vigorously Develop Vocational Education
Author: Zhu Yunlong
Date: 1989-05-12
Edition: 2

I will vigorously develop vocational education
Li Tieying emphasizes learning from the experience of developed countries

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 11 (reporter Zhu Yunlong): State Councilor and Minister of the State Education Commission Li Tieying said today that China will vigorously develop vocational and technical education and gradually establish a vocational and technical education system.

He said that the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the National People's Congress, and relevant government departments have discussed this matter many times and have made decisions.

Li Tieying made these remarks this afternoon at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse while meeting with a delegation of vocational education experts from the Federal Republic of Germany, led by Professor Klinger, State Secretary of the Federal Ministry of Economic Cooperation. He also said that to develop vocational education, China must introduce equipment and technology, and learn from the experience of developed countries in terms of theory, textbooks, and systems.

The guests were invited by the State Education Commission and arrived in China on April 24 for a visit.

### 我将大力发展职业教育  李铁映强调借鉴发达国家经验
朱云龙
1989-05-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　我将大力发展职业教育    
    李铁映强调借鉴发达国家经验
    据新华社北京５月１１日电（记者朱云龙）国务委员兼国家教委主任李铁映今天在这里说，中国将大力发展职业技术教育，逐步建立起职业技术教育体系。
    他说，中共中央、全国人大和政府有关部门已经就此事进行过多次讨论，并作出了决定。
    李铁映是今天下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见由联邦经济合作部国务秘书冷格尔教授率领的联邦德国职业教育专家组时说这番话的。他还说，要发展职业教育，中国还必须引进设备和技术，在理论、教材和体系方面借鉴发达国家的经验。
    客人们应国家教委邀请于４月２４日来华进行访问。
