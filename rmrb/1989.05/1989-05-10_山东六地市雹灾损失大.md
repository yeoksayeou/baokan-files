Title: Severe Hailstorm Causes Significant Damage in Six Cities of Shandong
Author: Jià Jiànzhōu
Date: 1989-05-10
Page: 2

From 12:00 to 18:30 on May 8th, six cities in Shandong Province—Weifang, Dongying, Huimin, Qingdao, Dezhou, and others—experienced a severe wind and hailstorm. Over 20 counties and districts were affected. Preliminary statistics indicate that approximately 3.7 million mu (246,667 hectares) of crops were damaged, 170,000 homes were destroyed, and 119 people were injured by the hail. The affected areas generally experienced hail for 5 to 15 minutes, with wind speeds of 5 to 7 levels and gusts exceeding 8 levels, accompanied by light rain. The largest hailstones had a diameter of 18 millimeters, and the hail on the ground was about 3 centimeters thick.

### 山东六地市雹灾损失大
贾建舟
1989-05-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　山东六地市雹灾损失大
    本报济南５月１０日电  ５月８日１２时至１８时半，山东省潍坊、东营、惠民、青岛、德州等６个地市的２０多个县、市（区），遭受一场风雹灾害的袭击。据初步统计，受灾农作物约达３７０多万亩，刮坏房屋１．７万余间，有１１９人被砸伤。这些受灾的地方一般降雹５至１５分钟，风力５—７级，阵风８级以上，伴有小雨。最大冰雹的直径为１８毫米，地面冰雹厚度约３厘米。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（贾建舟）
