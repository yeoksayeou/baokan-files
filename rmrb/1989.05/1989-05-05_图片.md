Title: Renowned Spanish Singer Montserrat Caballé Performs in Beijing
Author: Xiao Yinchang
Date: 1989-05-05
Page: 8

The world-renowned Spanish singer Montserrat Caballé held solo concerts on April 27th and 29th at the Beijing Concert Hall. The performances were enthusiastically received by the audience.
