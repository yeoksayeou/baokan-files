Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1989-05-18
Edition: 2

	▲ On May 17th, in the afternoon, Politburo Standing Committee member and Party School President Qiao Shi met and hosted a luncheon for a delegation from the Kim Il-sung Higher Party School of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Vice President Kim Ki-bum.

	▲ On May 17th, in the afternoon, Politburo Standing Committee member and Vice Premier Yao Yilin met with a delegation from the Finnish National Coalition Party led by Chairman and Minister of Trade and Industry Suominen.

		(According to Xinhua News Agency)
