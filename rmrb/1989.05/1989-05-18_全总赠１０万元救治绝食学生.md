Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Donates 100,000 Yuan to Treat Hunger-Striking Students
Author:
Date: 1989-05-18
Edition: 2

Some students from several universities in Beijing have been on a hunger strike in Tiananmen Square, which has drawn widespread attention and sympathy from workers nationwide. Workers are deeply concerned about the health of the hunger-striking students. To help the students regain their strength and improve their health as soon as possible, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has donated 100,000 yuan to the Beijing Red Cross Society to treat students whose health has been affected by the hunger strike.

### 全总赠１０万元救治绝食学生

1989-05-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全总赠１０万元救治绝食学生
    本报讯  北京部分高校学生在天安门广场绝食的请愿行动，引起广大职工的极大关注和广泛同情。广大职工对绝食同学的健康状况十分焦虑。为了使绝食同学早日恢复体力，增进健康，中华全国总工会特向北京市红十字会赠款人民币十万元，用以救治因绝食而身体健康受到损伤的同学们。
