Title: Lamenting Comrade Yao Wenyuan
Author: Lai Dachao
Date: 1989-05-10
Edition: 6

	The lessons from youth education are unforgettable, and the news of his passing is heart-wrenching.
	His virtues, talents, and achievements are all outstanding, and his honesty and integrity are also exemplary.
	He neither boasts of his contributions nor shifts blame, and his consideration for the greater good is praiseworthy.
	His revolutionary life is without regret, and he is deeply respected by the Party and the people.
