Title: Moroccan Prime Minister Reiterates PLO as Sole Representative of Palestinians on Eve of Arab Summit
Author:
Date: 1989-05-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, May 17th.

Moroccan Prime Minister Reiterates PLO as Sole Representative of Palestinians on Eve of Arab Summit

Tunisian newspapers reported today that Moroccan Prime Minister Izzedin Laraki, currently visiting Tunisia, reiterated that the Palestine Liberation Organization is the sole legitimate representative of the Palestinians at the upcoming Arab summit.

He told Tunisian journalists that the most important thing now is to adhere to the principles adopted at the 1974 Arab summit in Rabat, Morocco, because the Palestinian people's cause has made "heartening" progress.

He said the current Arab summit will seek a comprehensive solution to the Palestinian problem and find a solution acceptable to all Arab countries.

### 摩首相在阿盟首脑会议前夕  重申巴解是巴唯一代表

1989-05-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　摩首相在阿盟首脑会议前夕    
    重申巴解是巴唯一代表
    新华社突尼斯５月１７日电  突尼斯报纸今天报道，正在突尼斯访问的摩洛哥首相伊兹丁·拉腊基重申，巴勒斯坦解放组织是出席即将召开的阿拉伯首脑会议唯一合法的巴勒斯坦代表。
    他在接见突尼斯报界人士说，目前最重要的是坚持１９７４年在摩洛哥拉巴特举行的阿拉伯首脑会议上通过的原则，因为巴勒斯坦人民的事业取得了“振奋人心”的发展。
    他说，本届阿拉伯特别首脑会议将寻求全面解决巴勒斯坦问题，找到一种为所有阿拉伯国家所能接受的解决办法。
