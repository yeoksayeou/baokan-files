Title: BBC Employees Strike

Author:

Date: 1989-05-27

Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London, May 26th.

Thousands of journalists, technicians, and other staff members of the British Broadcasting Corporation (BBC) went on a 24-hour strike on May 26th, demanding higher wages.

This is the fourth 24-hour strike by the company's employees in the past month and is part of the recent wave of nationwide strikes in Britain.

A spokesperson for the National Union of Journalists, Don Brind, said that if the management does not respond to the union's demands, they will launch more flash strikes.

### 英广播公司雇员罢工

1989-05-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　英广播公司雇员罢工
    新华社伦敦５月２６日电  英国广播公司的数千名记者、技术人员和其他工作人员２６日举行了２４小时罢工，要求增加工资。
    这是最近一个多月来这家公司的工作人员第４次举行２４小时罢工，它是最近一个时期英国全国性罢工浪潮的一部分。
    公司记者联合会发言人唐·布林德说，如果资方拒绝对工会的要求作出反应，他们就将发动更多的闪电式罢工。
