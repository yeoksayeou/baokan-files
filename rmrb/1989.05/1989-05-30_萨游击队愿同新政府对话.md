Title: Salvador Guerrillas Willing to Dialogue with New Government
Author:
Date: 1989-05-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, May 29th.

San Salvador - The Farabundo Marti National Liberation Front, a guerrilla organization in El Salvador, reiterated on May 29th that it is prepared to engage in peaceful dialogue with the upcoming government led by Cristiani.

The guerrillas, in their peace proposal, pledged to cease destructive activities, continue dialogue with all political parties, and achieve a conditional ceasefire. They demanded that the government halt its repression.

The Nationalist Republican Alliance's presidential candidate, Cristiani, won the election on March 19th and will take office on June 1st.

### 萨游击队愿同新政府对话

1989-05-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    萨游击队愿同新政府对话
    新华社墨西哥城5月29日电  圣萨尔瓦多消息：萨尔瓦多游击队组织法拉本多·马蒂民族解放阵线29日重申，它准备同克里斯蒂亚尼领导的下届政府举行和平对话。
    游击队在和平建议中承诺停止破坏活动、继续同各政党对话和实现有条件的停火。游击队要求政府停止镇压。
    民族主义共和联盟总统候选人克里斯蒂亚尼在3月19日的大选中当选，6月1日就职。
