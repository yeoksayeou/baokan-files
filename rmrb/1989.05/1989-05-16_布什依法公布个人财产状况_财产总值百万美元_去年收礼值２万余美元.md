Title: Bush Publishes Personal Assets According to Law
Author: Cang Lide
Date: 1989-05-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, May 15th.

U.S. President Bush, in accordance with U.S. law, officially released his personal asset information for 1988 yesterday.

Bush's listed assets total over 1 million U.S. dollars.

By the end of last year, he owned a trust fund worth 998,000 U.S. dollars, which yielded 156,000 U.S. dollars in income that year. Additionally, he has personal assets valued at nearly 97,000 U.S. dollars, but this does not include his villa in Kennebunkport, Maine.

Bush reported receiving gifts valued at over 21,000 U.S. dollars in 1988, including: 10 country music tapes (100 U.S. dollars), two sets of horseshoes (218 U.S. dollars), 12 small soldier figurines (125 U.S. dollars), a set of "Parker" brand pens (425 U.S. dollars), a fishing reel (100 U.S. dollars), a cowboy shirt and hat (124 U.S. dollars), a silver plate gifted by the President of Cyprus (125 U.S. dollars), and a porcelain vase gifted by the government of Liaoning Province, China (120 U.S. dollars).

Mrs. Bush, Barbara, also reported receiving two gifts: a scarf valued at 175 U.S. dollars and a "Tiffany" brand gold watch valued at 7,800 U.S. dollars.

### 布什依法公布个人财产状况  财产总值百万美元　去年收礼值２万余美元
仓立德
1989-05-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　布什依法公布个人财产状况    
    财产总值百万美元　去年收礼值２万余美元
    本报华盛顿５月１５日电  记者仓立德报道：美国总统布什按照美国法律规定，昨天正式公布他１９８８年个人财产状况。
    布什开列的财产清单总值超过１００万美元。
    到去年年底以前，他拥有一笔９９．８万美元的信托资产，当年获益１５．６万美元。此外还有价值近９．７万美元的个人财产，但不包括他在缅因州肯尼班克港的一座别墅。
    布什报告了他在１９８８年接受的价值超过２．１万美元的礼品，其中有：１０盘乡村音乐磁带（１００美元）、两套马蹄铁（２１８美元）、１２个小型士兵玩偶（１２５美元）、１套“派克”牌钢笔和圆珠笔（４２５美元）、鱼竿绕线轴（１００美元）、牛仔衫及帽（１２４美元）、以及塞浦路斯总统赠送的１只银盘（１２５美元）和中国辽宁省政府赠送的１只瓷花瓶（１２０美元）等。
    布什夫人巴巴拉也报告了她得到的两件赠品：１条价值１７５美元的围巾和１块价值７８００美元的“蒂法尼”牌金表。
