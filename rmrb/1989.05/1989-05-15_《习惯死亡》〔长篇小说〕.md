Title: Getting Used to Death [Novel]
Author: Zhū Xiángqīng
Date: 1989-05-15
Page: 6

The novel does not have a substantial plot, and most characters do not have names. The timeline is disjointed, and emotions shift rapidly. The author is neither narrating a story nor developing characters but rather expressing the protagonist's sense of life in his sexual experiences—a feeling of irreparable, irrevocable, and unfixable fragmentation. For a "him" who has died three times and ultimately committed suicide calmly, he is reflecting quietly after seeing through fame and fortune, looking down on the masses after turning to God. He gazes back from beyond death, dwelling in absolute zero, standing alone like a fallen mast. Thus, the novel gains a near-religious mood and state of mind. All worldly matters, such as political turmoil, power struggles, and heart-wrenching pain, become insignificant. Even for evil and its perpetrators, he harbors a sense of pity, seeing through their inner loneliness, and then forgives them.

In terms of artistic pursuit, the novel surpasses the author's previous works. Notably, it reflects a shift in his understanding of the world: from "wholeness" to "fragmentation." The world is fragmented, and so is the heart. He seems dissatisfied with the suffering of a Prometheus-like life, as exemplified by Xu Lingjun.

This new perspective on observing the world imbues every life sensation in the novel with poetic wings, prompting readers to make extensive associations. (Zhū Xiángqīng)

### 《习惯死亡》〔长篇小说〕
朱珩青
1989-05-15
第6版(文艺评论)
专栏：

　　《习惯死亡》〔长篇小说〕
    作者张贤亮　载《文学四季》一九八九年第二期
    小说没有什么像样的故事，人物大多没有姓名，时空交错，情绪转换迅速，作家既不在叙述故事，也不在塑造人物，而旨在抒写主人公在性爱生活中的生命感觉，一种无法弥合、无法挽回、无法重新修复的破碎感。对于一个死过三次，最后又从容自杀的“他”来说，他是在看透了功名利禄之后宁静的回顾，是在皈依上帝之后俯视芸芸众生。他在超死亡中回眸，在绝对零度下栖息，像一根落帆之桅孑然挺立。这样，小说就获得了一种近乎宗教的情绪和心境。人世间的一切，诸如政治风云的变幻，勾心斗角的厮杀，撕心裂肺的痛苦都无所谓了。即使对于罪恶和罪恶的制造者，他也还怀有某种悲悯的情绪，看穿了他们内心的寂寞，尔后，也就宽恕他们了。
    在艺术追求上，小说超越了作家已往的作品。突出的是他对世界本体认识上的转变：从“完整”到“破碎”。世界的破碎，心的破碎。他对许灵均式的受难的普罗米修斯的人生似乎也不满起来了。
    这种新的观察世界的观点的变化，使得小说的每一个生命感觉都像插上了诗意的翅膀，它使人们发出广泛的联想。（朱珩青）
