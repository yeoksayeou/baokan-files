Title: Gorbachev Leaves Beijing for Shanghai Visit
Author: Xīn Huái Shí
Date: 1989-05-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 18th.

Gorbachev Leaves Beijing for Shanghai Visit

Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and General Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, Gorbachev, and his wife Raisa left Beijing by special plane this morning to visit and tour Shanghai.

President Yang Shangkun went to the Diplomatic Guesthouse No. 18 to see off the Soviet dignitaries.

Yang Shangkun told Gorbachev, "This is the first time in 30 years that the leaders of the two countries have met, and we have discussed the main issues."

Gorbachev said, "I think so too."

Yang Shangkun said, "Since we have started, it will be easier from now on."

Gorbachev said he was very pleased to visit China at the invitation of Yang Shangkun. The atmosphere of this visit is very friendly.

He said, "Maslyukov told me that yesterday he had a very interesting conversation with Vice Premier Tian Jiyun. Economic cooperation between the two countries will be carried out on a broad scale, and work in this regard has already begun."

Yang Shangkun said, "We have already made progress."

Gorbachev said, "The joint communiqué that we are about to issue is a very good communiqué, a very good document."

Yang Shangkun said, "This is the merit of the two countries' foreign ministers. They have done a lot of work."

Gorbachev said, "We should congratulate them."

### 戈尔巴乔夫离京赴沪访问  杨尚昆到国宾馆亲切话别
辛怀时
1989-05-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　戈尔巴乔夫离京赴沪访问    
    杨尚昆到国宾馆亲切话别
    据新华社北京５月１８日电（记者辛怀时）苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席、苏共中央总书记戈尔巴乔夫和夫人赖莎一行今天上午乘专机离京赴上海参观访问。
    国家主席杨尚昆到戈尔巴乔夫和夫人下榻的钓鱼台国宾馆第１８号楼为苏联贵宾送行。
    杨尚昆对戈尔巴乔夫说，这次两国领导人是３０年来第一次会晤，谈了主要问题。
    戈尔巴乔夫说，他也是这样想的。
    杨尚昆说，既然开了个头，以后就好办了。
    戈尔巴乔夫说，他非常高兴应杨尚昆的邀请来中国访问。这次访问的气氛是非常友好的。
    他说，“马斯柳科夫跟我讲过，昨天他同田纪云副总理进行了很有意思的交谈。两国经济合作将广泛地进行，这方面的工作已经开始了。”
    杨尚昆说，“我们已经向前走了。”
    戈尔巴乔夫说，“我们将要发表的联合公报是个非常好的公报，是个很好的文件。”
    杨尚昆说，“这是两国外交部长的功劳。他们做了不少工作。”
    戈尔巴乔夫说，应该向他们表示祝贺。
