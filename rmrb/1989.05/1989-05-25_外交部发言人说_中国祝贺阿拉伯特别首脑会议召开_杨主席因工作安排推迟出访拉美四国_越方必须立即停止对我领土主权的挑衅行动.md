Title: Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Says China Congratulates the Opening of the Arab Summit
Author:
Date: 1989-05-25
Edition: 1

Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Says
	China Congratulates the Opening of the Arab Summit
	Yang Shangkun Delays Visit to Four Latin American Countries Due to Work Schedule
	Vietnam Must Immediately Stop Provocations Against Our Territorial Sovereignty

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 25 -- Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Li Jinhua said at a press conference today that the special Arab summit held in Casablanca is a major event in the Arab world. China congratulates the successful convening of the summit and appreciates the spirit of unity demonstrated at the summit.

	She said, China believes this summit will make positive contributions to further strengthening Arab unity, promoting the Middle East peace process, easing the situation in Lebanon, and achieving peace and stability in the Middle East.

	The spokesperson also said that following the visit of Vietnamese People's Army Chief of Staff Duan Kui to some islands in the South China Sea illegally occupied by Vietnam, Vietnamese Vice Chairman of the Council of State Nguyen Quyet recently conducted a so-called "inspection" of these islands. This is a serious provocation against China's territorial sovereignty.

	She emphasized that the Vietnamese side must immediately stop such provocative actions.

	When asked about the visit of State Chairman Yang Shangkun to Latin America, the spokesperson said that due to work schedule arrangements, Yang Shangkun's planned visit to Mexico, Brazil, Uruguay, and Argentina in late May this year has been postponed to a later time this year.

### 外交部发言人说  中国祝贺阿拉伯特别首脑会议召开  杨主席因工作安排推迟出访拉美四国  越方必须立即停止对我领土主权的挑衅行动

1989-05-25
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　外交部发言人说
    中国祝贺阿拉伯特别首脑会议召开
    杨主席因工作安排推迟出访拉美四国
    越方必须立即停止对我领土主权的挑衅行动
    新华社北京５月２５日电　外交部发言人李金华今天在新闻发布会上说，在卡萨布兰卡召开的阿拉伯特别首脑会议是阿拉伯世界的一件大事。中国对会议的成功召开表示祝贺，对会议体现的团结精神表示赞赏。
    她说，中国相信这次会议将为进一步加强阿拉伯团结，推动中东和平进程，缓和黎巴嫩形势，实现中东地区的和平与稳定作出积极贡献。
    这位发言人还说，继越南人民军总参谋长段奎之后，越南国务委员会副主席阮决最近又到中国南沙群岛被越南非法侵占的一些岛屿进行所谓的“视察”。这是对中国领土主权的严重挑衅。
    她强调指出，越南方面必须立即停止这种挑衅行动。
    当有的记者问及国家主席杨尚昆出访拉美一事时，发言人说，由于工作安排的原因，杨尚昆主席原订于今年５月底对墨西哥、巴西、乌拉圭和阿根廷４个拉美国家的访问推迟到今年晚些时候进行。
