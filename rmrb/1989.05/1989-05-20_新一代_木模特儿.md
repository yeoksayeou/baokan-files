Title: The New Generation of Mannequins
Author: Tong Chenglin
Date: 1989-05-20
Edition: 7

Recently, the Federation of Small and Medium-Sized Clothing Enterprises in Tokyo, Japan, held an exhibition with a unique theme. The exhibition showcased the "new generation" of clothing mannequins. These "new generation" clothing mannequins are equipped with complex mechanical and electronic devices inside, allowing their waists and limbs to coordinate naturally and realistically mimic many human movements. According to the developers, among the exhibited "new generation" clothing mannequins, some standouts can perform over 200 different poses.

The developers believe that as the times change and science advances, the mannequins in clothing enterprises and stores should not just be stiff and stationary, but should be lively and dynamic. For example, in clothing enterprises, using these dynamic mannequins can help improve the quality of clothing and enhance the design of styles. In clothing stores, using these dynamic mannequins can attract many customers, making the business more prosperous. They predict that in the not-too-distant future, these unique "new generation" mannequins will definitely appear in the display windows of major stores in Tokyo.

### “新一代”木模特儿
仝成林
1989-05-20
第7版(国际副刊)
专栏：

　　“新一代”木模特儿
    不久前，日本东京的中小型服装企业联合会举办了一次别开生面的展览会，会上展出了“新一代”服装木模特儿。这些“新一代”服装木模特儿的体内装有一种复杂的机械和电子设备，使其腰部和肢部能够有机地配合起来，非常自然而逼真地模仿出真人的许多动作。据研制者介绍，展出的“新一代”服装木模特儿中，有一些佼佼者，能做出２００多种姿势。
    研制者认为，时代在不断发展，科学在不断进步，服装企业和服装商店里的木模特儿也不应只是死死板板的站立式的，而应是栩栩如生的活动式的。例如，在服装企业中，使用这种活动式的木模特儿，有利于提高服装的质量和改进服装的式样；在服装商店里使用这种活动式的木模特儿，可以招徕众多的顾客，使店家的生意更加红火。他们预计，在不远的将来，这种别开生面的“新一代”木模特儿定会出现在东京各大商店的橱窗里。　　　　　　　　　　　　　·仝成林·
