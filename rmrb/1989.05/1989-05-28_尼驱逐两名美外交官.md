Title: Nicaragua Expels Two U.S. Diplomats
Author:
Date: 1989-05-28
Edition: 3

Nicaragua expels two U.S. diplomats

Xinhua News Agency, Managua, May 25th: Nicaraguan Foreign Minister D'Escoto announced on the 25th that two U.S. diplomatic personnel have been declared persona non grata for interfering in Nicaragua's internal affairs.

D'Escoto stated that the Nicaraguan government has evidence showing that the Second Secretary of the U.S. Embassy in Nicaragua, Franklin, and the diplomat responsible for Central American labor affairs, Williamson, have frequently contacted Nicaraguan opposition groups and directly participated in the teachers' strike in the northern region.

### 尼驱逐两名美外交官

1989-05-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　尼驱逐两名美外交官
    新华社马那瓜电尼加拉瓜外交部长德斯科托２５日宣布，２名美国外交官员由于干涉尼加拉瓜内政而被宣布为不受欢迎的人。
    德斯科托外长指出，尼加拉瓜政府有证据表明，美国驻尼加拉瓜使馆二等秘书弗兰克林和负责中美洲劳工事务的外交官威廉姆松最近频繁地接触尼加拉瓜反对派，并直接参与了北部教工的罢教事件。
