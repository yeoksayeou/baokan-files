Title: Pakistan Becomes a Weapons Exporter
Author:
Date: 1989-05-16
Edition: 3

Pakistan has become an exporter of small conventional weapons, with exports going to more than 30 countries including the United States and France.

In addition to small conventional weapons, Pakistan is currently developing advanced large-scale weapons and equipment, having already produced ground-to-ground and ground-to-air missiles, modern radars, and anti-tank weapon systems. At the same time, it has begun producing military aircraft.

### 巴基斯坦成为武器出口国　

1989-05-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦成为武器出口国　
    新华社伊斯兰堡５月１５日电  据巴联社今天报道，巴基斯坦已成为小型常规武器出口国，出口对象包括美国、法国等３０多个国家。
    除小型常规武器外，巴基斯坦目前正在发展先进的大型武器装备，已生产出地对地、地对空导弹、现代化雷达、反坦克武器系统等装备。同时，它也开始生产军用飞机。
