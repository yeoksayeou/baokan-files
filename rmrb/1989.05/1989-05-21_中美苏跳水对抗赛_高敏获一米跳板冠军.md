Title: Sino-US-Soviet Diving Competition - Gao Min Wins 1-Meter Springboard Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-05-21
Edition: 4

World-renowned diver Gao Min today won the women's 1-meter springboard event with a score of 449.20 points at the Sino-US-Soviet diving competition held in Lauderdale, USA.

Soviet Union's Elena Rashko scored 444.80 points to take second place, while the United States' Julie Farrell scored 441.70 points to secure third place. Another Chinese athlete, Zhang Yuping, scored 425.40 points to finish fourth.

In the men's 1-meter springboard event, Mark Lenzi of the United States, who recently won the gold medal at the Sixth World Cup, defeated China's Tan Liangde with a score of 610.85 points to claim first place. Tan Liangde and Li Deliang of China scored 562.10 points and 556.05 points respectively, to take second and third places.

Currently, the Chinese team leads the overall standings with a total of 18 points. The United States team is in second place with 15 points, while the Soviet Union team is in third place with 12 points.

### 中美苏跳水对抗赛  高敏获一米跳板冠军

1989-05-21
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　中美苏跳水对抗赛
    高敏获一米跳板冠军
    新华社华盛顿５月１９日电  世界名将高敏今天在美国劳德代尔堡举行的中、美、苏跳水对抗赛上以４４９．２０分夺得女子１米跳板第一名。
    苏联的伊莲娜·拉什科以４４４．８０分获第二名，第三名是美国的朱莉·法雷尔（４４１．７０分）。中国另一名选手张玉萍以４２５．４０分获第４名。
    在男子１米跳板比赛中，不久前在第六届世界杯赛中夺得金牌的美国选手马克·伦齐再次战胜中国的谭良德，以６１０．８５分夺得第一名。谭良德和李德亮分别以５６２．１０分和５５６．０５分获第二、第三名。
    目前，中国队以总分１８分，暂居团体第一。美国队获１５分，居第二，苏联队只获得１２分。
