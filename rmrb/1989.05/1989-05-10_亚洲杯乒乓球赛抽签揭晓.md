Title: The Draw for the Asian Cup Table Tennis Tournament is Announced
Author:
Date: 1989-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency

The draw for the Asian Cup Table Tennis Tournament has been announced.

Men's Singles:
Group A: Chen Longcan (China), Kim Kun-chol (North Korea), Iwasaki Seishin (Japan);
Group B: Kim Jae-su (South Korea), Shibata Hiroshi (Japan), Mu Jassim (Iraq);
Group C: Chen Zhibin (China), Kim Myung-jun (North Korea), Chan Man-sang (Hong Kong);
Group D: Saito Seiichi (Japan), Xie Wen-tang (Chinese Taipei), Lim Jun Liang (Malaysia);
Group E: An Jae-hong (South Korea), Feng Sheng-chin (Chinese Taipei), Ma Gandawan (Thailand);
Group F: Ma Wen-ge (China), Moon Kwai-mun (South Korea), Sayyaf (Pakistan), Sa Haq (Bangladesh);
Group G: Matsushita Koji (Japan), Kim Jin-myung (North Korea), Arif Khan (Pakistan), An Aginayde (Philippines);
Group H: Yu Shentong (China), Kang Hee-jung (South Korea), Lim Mei Liang (Malaysia).

Women's Singles:
Group A: Qiao Hong (China), Huang Mei-zhen (Chinese Taipei), Lee Jung-jim (South Korea), Lee Mai-kun (Malaysia);
Group B: Chee Poo Hwa (Hong Kong), Ding Yaping (China), Lee Mi-suk (North Korea), Juma (Iraq);
Group C: Uchiyama Kyoko (Japan), Chan Tan-lai (Hong Kong), Chen Yue-zhen (Chinese Taipei), Leong Mei-yun (Malaysia);
Group D: Yu Sun-bok (North Korea), Kwon Mi-suk (South Korea), Sato Rika (Japan).
