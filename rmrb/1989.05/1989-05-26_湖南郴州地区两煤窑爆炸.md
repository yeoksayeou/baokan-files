Title: Two Coal Mine Explosions in Chenzhou Region, Hunan
Author:
Date: 1989-05-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, May 26th.

Within fifteen days, two coal mine gas explosions occurred in the Chenzhou region of Hunan, resulting in the deaths of twenty-six people.

At around 12:00 PM on May 13th, a gas explosion occurred at the Fu Jinling Coal Mine in Liangyuan Village, Laowangping Township, Chen County. The mine, which had been sealed off four times previously, saw ten deaths and one severe injury. Prior to this, at 9:20 AM on April 28th, a gas explosion occurred at an individual coal mine in Hejiashan Village, Xiaojia Township, Jiahua County. All sixteen workers operating underground at the time were killed.

### 湖南郴州地区两煤窑爆炸

1989-05-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　湖南郴州地区两煤窑爆炸
    新华社长沙五月二十六日电  湖南郴州地区十五天内接连发生两起煤矿瓦斯爆炸事故，共死亡二十六人。
    五月十三日十二时左右，曾被查封过四次的郴县廖王坪乡凉源村富近岭煤矿发生瓦斯爆炸，被炸死十人，重伤一人。在此之前，四月二十八日上午九时二十分，嘉禾县肖家镇乐塘村河家山一家个体煤矿发生瓦斯爆炸，在井下作业的十六名民工全部遇难。
