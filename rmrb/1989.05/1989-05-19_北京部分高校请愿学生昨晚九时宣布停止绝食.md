Title: Students from Some Beijing Universities Announce End to Hunger Strike at 9 PM Last Night
Author:
Date: 1989-05-19
Edition: 1

Students from some Beijing universities who had been on a hunger strike in Tiananmen Square for seven days announced the end of their hunger strike last night at 9 PM.

This was announced by the hunger strike command center through a loudspeaker set up in the square at around 9 PM.

### 北京部分高校请愿学生昨晚九时宣布停止绝食

1989-05-19
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　北京部分高校请愿学生昨晚九时宣布停止绝食
    新华社北京５月１９日电  在天安门广场绝食请愿七天的北京部分高校学生今天晚上宣布停止绝食。
    这是绝食团指挥部通过设在广场的喇叭于晚上九时许宣布的。
