Title: Mayor Chen Xitong Signs Beijing Municipal People's Government Order
Author:
Date: 1989-05-20
Edition: 1

Mayor Chen Xitong signs
Beijing Municipal People's Government Order
(No. 1)

In accordance with the martial law order issued by Premier Li Peng on behalf of the State Council for certain areas in Beijing, to swiftly stop social unrest and maintain normal work, production, teaching, research, and social life order in the capital, the Beijing Municipal People's Government hereby issues this order:

1. Martial law will be imposed on Dongcheng District, Xicheng District, Chongwen District, Xuanwu District, Shijingshan District, Haidian District, Fengtai District, and Chaoyang District starting from 10:00 AM on May 20, 1989;

2. During the martial law period, it is strictly forbidden to hold parades, petition, strike, or engage in any other activities that disrupt normal order;

3. It is strictly forbidden for anyone to spread rumors, organize, make speeches, or distribute leaflets to incite social unrest;

4. It is strictly forbidden to storm party, government, and military leadership organs, or to storm radio, television, and communication facilities, or to damage important public facilities, or to engage in any acts of beating, smashing, looting, or arson;

5. It is strictly forbidden to harass embassies of foreign countries in China and United Nations offices in Beijing;

6. During the martial law period, if any of the above prohibited activities occur, public security personnel, armed police, and People's Liberation Army on duty have the right to take all necessary measures to stop them.

All citizens are requested to comply with the above provisions.

Mayor Chen Xitong
May 20, 1989

(No. 2)

To implement the martial law order issued by the State Council for certain areas in Beijing and to maintain normal order in the capital, the Beijing Municipal People's Government hereby issues this order:

1. Foreigners must comply with all orders issued by the Beijing Municipal People's Government in accordance with the martial law order signed by Premier Li Peng;

2. During the martial law period, foreigners are not allowed to participate in any activities by Chinese citizens that violate the martial law order;

3. Those who violate the above provisions will be stopped by the on-duty personnel using all necessary measures.

Mayor Chen Xitong
May 20, 1989

(No. 3)

During the martial law period, the following regulations are made for journalists:

1. It is strictly forbidden for domestic and foreign journalists to use interviews for incitement and provocative reporting;

2. Without the approval of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, foreign journalists, Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan journalists are not allowed to enter government agencies, organizations, schools, factories, enterprises, and communities for interviews, photography, and video recording;

3. Those who violate the above provisions will be stopped by the on-duty personnel.

Mayor Chen Xitong
May 20, 1989

(Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 20)

### 陈希同市长签署  北京市人民政府令

1989-05-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　陈希同市长签署
    北京市人民政府令
    （第一号）
    根据李鹏总理签署的国务院关于在北京市部分地区戒严令，为迅速制止社会动乱，维护首都正常的工作、生产、教学、科研、社会生活秩序，北京市人民政府特发布此令：
    一、自１９８９年５月２０日１０时起对东城区、西城区、崇文区、宣武区、石景山区、海淀区、丰台区、朝阳区实行戒严；
    二、在戒严期间，严禁游行、请愿罢课、罢工和其他聚众妨害正常秩序的活动；
    三、严禁任何人以任何方式制造和散布谣言，进行串联、演讲，散发传单；煽动社会动乱；
    四、严禁冲击党政军领导机关，严禁冲击广播、电视、通讯等重要单位，严禁破坏重要公共设施，严禁打、砸、抢、烧等一切破坏活动；
    五、严禁骚扰各国驻华使馆和联合国驻京机构；
    六、在戒严期间，发生上述应予禁止的活动，公安干警、武警部队和人民解放军执勤人员有权采取一切手段，强行处置。
    以上各项，望全体市民遵照执行。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　市长　陈希同
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年５月２０日
    　　　　（第二号）
    为了执行国务院关于在北京市部分地区戒严令，维护首都的正常秩序，北京市人民政府特发布此令：
    一、外国人必须遵守北京市人民政府根据国务院李鹏总理签署的戒严令所发布的各项命令；
    二、在戒严期间，外国人不准介入中国公民违反戒严令的活动；
    三、违反以上规定的，执勤人员有权采取一切手段，予以制止。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　市长　陈希同
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年５月２０日
    　　　　（第三号）
    在戒严期间，对记者采访特作如下规定：
    一、严禁中外记者利用采访，进行挑唆、煽动性宣传报道；
    二、未经北京市人民政府批准，外国记者、港澳台记者不得进入机关、团体、学校、厂矿、企业、街道进行采访、拍照、录相等活动；
    三、违反以上规定的，执勤人员有权予以制止。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　市长　陈希同
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年５月２０日
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社北京５月２０日电）
