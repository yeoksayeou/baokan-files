Title: Funeral for Wang Libin Held
Author:
Date: 1989-05-10
Edition: 2

The funeral for Wang Libin, former State Councilor and retired cadre, and member of the Communist Party of China, was held today in the first farewell hall of the Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery in Beijing.

Wang Libin passed away in Beijing on May 1, 1989, at the age of 79.

Wang Libin was born in Jingjiang County, Jiangsu Province, in 1910 and joined the Communist Party of China in 1930.

During his lifetime, he served as the vice president of the Fujian Provincial People's Court, the deputy director of the Institute of Minority Languages at the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, and was appointed as a State Councilor in July 1979.

### 王利宾遗体告别仪式举行

1989-05-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　王利宾遗体告别仪式举行
    新华社北京５月１０日电  原国务院参事、离休干部，中国共产党党员王利宾同志遗体告别仪式，今天在北京八宝山革命公墓第一告别室举行。
    王利宾同志是１９８９年５月１日在北京逝世的，终年７９岁。
    王利宾１９１０年出生于江苏省靖江县，１９３０年加入中国共产党。
    他生前曾任福建省人民法院副院长，中国科学院哲学社会科学部少数民族语言研究所副所长等职，１９７９年７月任国务院参事。
