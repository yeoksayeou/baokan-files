Title: Beijing Hosts the "First International Youth Calligraphy Exhibition"
Author: Ren Zhi
Date: 1989-05-07
Edition: 4

Beijing is hosting the "First International Youth Calligraphy Exhibition" to commemorate the 70th anniversary of the May Fourth Movement. The exhibition will be held from May 9 to 14 at the National Art Museum of China. This calligraphy exhibition is jointly organized by the All-China Youth Federation and the preparatory committee of the Chinese Youth Calligraphers Association, and is an important part of the "National Youth Culture and Science Expo." The exhibition will feature over 400 calligraphy works from young calligraphers from 13 countries and regions. Senior calligraphers Qi Gong, Chu Tu-Nan, Zhao Pu-Chu, Wu Zuo-Ren, and Sha Meng-Hai will serve as artistic advisors for this exhibition.

### 北京举办“首届国际青年书法展览”
任之
1989-05-07
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　北京举办“首届国际青年书法展览”
    本报讯  为纪念“五四”运动７０周年，“首届国际青年书法展览”将于５月９日至１４日在中国美术馆举行。此次书法展是由全国青联和中华青年书法家协会筹备组联合举办的，属于“全国青年文化科技博览会”的重要展出项目之一。展览会上将展出１３个国家和地区青年书法家的４００余件书法作品。老一辈书法家启功、楚图南、赵朴初、吴作人、沙孟海将担任此次书法展的艺术顾问。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（任之）
