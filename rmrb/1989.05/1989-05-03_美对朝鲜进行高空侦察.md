Title: The U.S. Conducts High-Altitude Reconnaissance Over North Korea
Author:
Date: 1989-05-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 2nd.

The U.S. Conducts High-Altitude Reconnaissance Over North Korea

According to a report by the Korean Central News Agency, a U.S. U-2 strategic reconnaissance aircraft flew over North Korea's west coast along the military demarcation line to the east coast at 7:30 AM today, conducting high-altitude reconnaissance over the North.

The report stated that similar reconnaissance activities occurred over 30 times in April alone. Meanwhile, the U.S. also dispatched E-3A early warning aircraft to conduct reconnaissance over the North six times.

The report also mentioned that the U.S. has moved 10 RF-4C reconnaissance aircraft belonging to the 15th Tactical Reconnaissance Squadron of the U.S. Air Force stationed in Okinawa, Japan, into South Korea.

The report pointed out that the U.S.'s actions are deliberately escalating tensions on the Korean Peninsula.

### 美对朝鲜进行高空侦察

1989-05-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美对朝鲜进行高空侦察
    新华社平壤５月２日电  据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，美国Ｕ—２战略侦察机今天７时３０分从朝鲜西海岸上空沿军事分界线飞行至朝鲜东海岸，对北方进行高空侦察。
    报道说，类似的侦察活动仅在４月份就达３０余次。同时，美国还派遣Ｅ—３Ａ早期预警飞机对北方侦察达６次。
    报道说，美国还把驻在日本冲绳的美国空军第１５战术侦察飞行队所属的１０架ＲＦ—４Ｃ侦察机运进南朝鲜。
    报道指出，美国的所作所为是蓄意激化朝鲜半岛的紧张局势。
