Title: Shen Jianqiang Sets New Asian Record in 100m Butterfly
Author: Tan Jun
Date: 1989-05-02
Edition: 4

Shanghai International Swimming Invitational and National Swimming Championships produced another outstanding result today. Shanghai's Shen Jianqiang in the men's 100-meter butterfly final, achieved a time of 53.87 seconds, surpassing the previous Asian record of 54.48 seconds. The original record was set by China's Zheng Jian.

Hebei's Wang Dali in the men's 400-meter individual medley final, recorded a time of 4 minutes 31.17 seconds, breaking Xie Jun's national record of 4 minutes 32.24 seconds.

Australian female swimmer Julie Pye performed exceptionally well today. She won the 200-meter freestyle final with a time of 2 minutes 3.85 seconds.

Heilongjiang's Huang Xiaomin won the women's 200-meter breaststroke gold medal with a time of 2 minutes 32.71 seconds. Australia's Samantha Riley took the second place.

### 沈坚强创百米蝶泳亚洲最好成绩
谭军
1989-05-02
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　沈坚强创百米蝶泳亚洲最好成绩
    新华社上海５月２日电  （记者谭军）上海国际游泳邀请赛暨全国游泳锦标赛今天又出好成绩。上海选手沈坚强在男子１００米蝶泳决赛中，以５３秒８７的成绩超过了５４秒４８的亚洲最好成绩。原成绩是中国的郑健创造的。
    河北队王大力在男子４００米混合泳决赛中以４分３１秒１７的成绩，打破了谢军的４分３２秒２４的全国纪录。
    澳大利亚女选手朱莉·皮尤今天表现出色，她在２００米自由泳决赛中，以２分０３秒８５的成绩夺得第１名。
    黑龙江的黄晓敏以２分３２秒７１的成绩夺得女子２００米蛙泳金牌。澳大利亚的萨曼莎·赖利获得第２名。
