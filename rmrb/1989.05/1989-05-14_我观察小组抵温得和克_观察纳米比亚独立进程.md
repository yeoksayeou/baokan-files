Title: Chinese Observation Group Arrives in Windhoek to Monitor Namibia's Independence Process
Author:
Date: 1989-05-14
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Windhoek, May 14th.

A Chinese observation group arrived in Windhoek on May 14th to monitor Namibia's independence process.

The four-member Chinese observation group will observe the entire process of implementing United Nations Security Council Resolution 435 on Namibia's independence.

Several countries have already sent their own observation groups, including frontline states in Africa, some North European and Western European countries, the Soviet Union, and the United States.

### 我观察小组抵温得和克  观察纳米比亚独立进程

1989-05-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我观察小组抵温得和克  观察纳米比亚独立进程
    新华社温得和克５月１４日电  观察纳米比亚独立进程的中国小组１４日抵达温得和克。
    这个由４人组成的中国观察小组将观察实施联合国安理会关于纳米比亚独立的４３５号决议的全部过程。
    一些国家已派遣了自己的观察小组。其中有非洲前线国家、北欧和西欧一些国家、苏联以及美国。
