Title: I Participate in the United Nations Trusteeship Council
Author:
Date: 1989-05-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, United Nations, May 15th.

Chinese Ambassador to the United Nations, Yu Mengjia, attended the United Nations Trusteeship Council meeting today. This is the first time China has participated in the work of the Council since it resumed its seat in the United Nations in 1971.

Ambassador Yu Mengjia stated that China's decision to participate in the Trusteeship Council's work is to contribute to enhancing the constructive role of the United Nations on the international stage.

This meeting will consider the economic and social development as well as the autonomy of the Micronesian Islands in the Pacific. These islands are the last remaining trust territories under the administration of the United Nations and are currently under U.S. administration.

### 我参加联合国托管理事会

1989-05-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我参加联合国托管理事会
    新华社联合国５月１５日电  中国常驻联合国大使俞孟嘉今天出席了联合国托管理事会会议，这是中国自１９７１年恢复在联合国席位以来第一次参加该理事会的工作。
    俞孟嘉大使说，中国决定参加托管理事会工作是为了对加强联合国在国际舞台上富于建设性的作用作出贡献。
    这次会议将考虑太平洋中密克罗尼西亚群岛的经济和社会发展以及自治问题。这些岛屿是联合国的最后托管地区，目前在美国的行政管理之下。
