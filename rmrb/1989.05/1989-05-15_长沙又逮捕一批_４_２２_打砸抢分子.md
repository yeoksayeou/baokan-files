Title: Changsha Arrests Another Batch of "4.22" Vandals
Author: Wú Xīnghuá
Date: 1989-05-15
Edition: 2

	Changsha Arrests Another Batch of "4.22" Vandals

	XINHUA, Changsha, May 15 - Changsha Public Security Bureau, with the approval of the procuratorial organs, arrested 17 more individuals involved in the "4.22" incident in Changsha. As of now, a total of 26 individuals involved in the Changsha "4.22" incident have been arrested.

	Among the 17 individuals arrested today, there are 6 workers, 4 unemployed youth from both local and outside areas, 4 individuals residing in Changsha who work as temporary or part-time workers in local units or individual businesses, and 3 migrant workers from rural areas.

### 长沙又逮捕一批“４·２２”打砸抢分子
吴兴华
1989-05-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　长沙又逮捕一批“４·２２”打砸抢分子
    本报长沙５月１５日电  记者吴兴华报道：今天，在此间召开的公捕大会上，长沙市公安机关经检察机关批准，又依法逮捕了１７名参与长沙“４·２２事件”的打砸抢分子，到目前为止，已有２６名参与长沙“４·２２”事件的不法分子被捕。
    今天逮捕的这１７名不法分子，其中工人６人，本市和外地来此的待业青年４人，家住长沙市、在长沙市的单位或个体户当临时工或帮工的人员４人，进城务工的农民３人。
