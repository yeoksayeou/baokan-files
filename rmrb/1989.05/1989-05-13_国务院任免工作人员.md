Title: State Council Appoints and Dismisses Officials
Author:
Date: 1989-05-13
Edition: 2

	The State Council recently appointed Zhang Weichao as the Consul-General of the People's Republic of China in Leningrad, and Li Shichun to concurrently serve as the Representative of the People's Republic of China to the United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific. Zhang Zhen was relieved of his position as the Consul-General of the People's Republic of China in Leningrad, and Zhang Dewen was relieved of his concurrent position as the Representative of the People's Republic of China to the United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific.

### 国务院任免工作人员

1989-05-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　国务院任免工作人员
    新华社北京５月１１日电  国务院最近任命张伟超为中华人民共和国驻列宁格勒总领事，李世淳兼任中华人民共和国驻联合国亚洲及太平洋经济社会委员会代表；免去张震的中华人民共和国驻列宁格勒总领事职务，张德维兼任的中华人民共和国驻联合国亚洲及太平洋经济社会委员会代表职务。
