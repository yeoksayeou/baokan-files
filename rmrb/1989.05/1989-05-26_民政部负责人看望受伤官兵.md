Title: Official from the Ministry of Civil Affairs Visits Injured Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1989-05-26
Edition: 1

The deputy minister of the Ministry of Civil Affairs, Fan Baojun, today visited the 304th Hospital of the People's Liberation Army in the afternoon, representing the Ministry of Civil Affairs to visit the soldiers of the People's Liberation Army who were injured while carrying out martial law duties in parts of the capital.

Fan Baojun said, "Supporting the military and caring for families of soldiers, and supporting the government and loving the people, is our glorious tradition. At present, we must especially cherish and maintain military-political and military-civilian unity. We must educate the masses to understand and support the People's Liberation Army in carrying out martial law duties. Military and civilians should work together to quickly restore normal order in the capital."

### 民政部负责人看望受伤官兵

1989-05-26
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　民政部负责人看望受伤官兵
    新华社北京５月２６日电  民政部副部长范宝俊今天下午代表民政部前往解放军３０４医院，看望在执行首都部分地区戒严任务中被打伤的解放军官兵。
    范宝俊说，拥军优属、拥政爱民是我们的光荣传统。在当前，我们要特别珍视和维护军政、军民团结。我们要教育群众理解和支持解放军执行戒严任务，军民同心协力，尽快恢复首都的正常秩序。
