Title: Artistic Elites Gather, Spectacular Performances Abound: The Shenzhen Zhuhai International Arts Festival Opens
Author: Wáng Cǔ Lú Zǔ Pǐn
Date: 1989-05-06
Edition: 1

Artistic elites gather, spectacular performances abound: The Shenzhen Zhuhai International Arts Festival opens

	People's Daily, Shenzhen, May 6th — Reporters Wang Cu and Lu Zupin report: The China Shenzhen Zhuhai International Arts Festival opened at 7 PM today.

	Participating in this arts festival are: the American Music and Dance Troupe, the Japanese Ballet Artists Group, Zairean singer Abeti Masikini, the Austrian "Vienna Music Association" Orchestra, the Soviet Artists Troupe led by Ulanova, the Italian Naples Folk Song Singing Troupe, and the Turkish Folk Arts Troupe, among others.

	Gu Mu, on behalf of the Arts Festival Organizing Committee, presented certificates to hundreds of artists as a memento. Chinese and foreign artists performed brilliant acts at the opening ceremony, and the audience erupted in thunderous applause.

### 艺术精英云集　精采表演纷呈  深圳珠海国际艺术节开幕
王楚　卢祖品
1989-05-06
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　艺术精英云集　精采表演纷呈    
    深圳珠海国际艺术节开幕
    本报深圳５月６日电  记者王楚、卢祖品报道：中国深圳珠海国际艺术节今天下午７时开幕。
    参加这次艺术节的有：美国音乐舞蹈团、日本芭蕾舞艺术家小组、扎伊尔女歌唱家阿贝蒂·玛西基妮、奥地利“维也纳音乐会一协会”乐团、乌兰诺娃率领的苏联艺术家演出团、意大利拿波里民歌演唱团、土耳其民间艺术团等。
    谷牧代表艺术节筹委会给数百位艺术家颁发证书以作纪念。中外艺术家在开幕式上表演了精彩节目，全场掌声雷动。
