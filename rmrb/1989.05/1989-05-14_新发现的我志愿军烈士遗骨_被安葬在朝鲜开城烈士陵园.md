Title: Recently Discovered Remains of Chinese People's Volunteers Buried in Kaesong Martyrs' Cemetery
Author: LÍ NÉNGQĪNG
Date: 1989-05-14
Edition: 3

The burial ceremony for 19 sets of remains of Chinese People's Volunteers recently discovered in South Korea was held this afternoon at the Chinese People's Volunteers Martyrs' Cemetery in Kaesong, a border city on the Military Demarcation Line in Korea.

The 19 sets of remains of the Chinese People's Volunteers were handed over to the Chinese and Korean sides by the "United Nations Command" side at the 495th meeting of the Military Armistice Commission held this morning at Panmunjom.

This is the largest number of remains of Chinese People's Volunteers discovered in South Korea since the Korean War armistice. In addition, several hundred items used by the volunteers were also handed over to the Chinese and Korean sides.

At 3 PM today, the Korean side held a burial ceremony for the 19 sets of remains of the Chinese People's Volunteers, played the national anthems of China and Korea, and fired 30 gunshots by the Korean People's Army soldiers. General Choi Ui-hyeong, the chief representative of the Chinese and Korean side of the Military Armistice Commission, and Colonel Wang Dahui, the military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, delivered eulogies, highly praising the great achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteers in the Korean War.

Lieutenant General Ri Hong-sun, Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, Major General Tian Sheng, the Chinese People's Volunteers representative of the Military Armistice Commission of the Chinese and Korean side, the commanders of the Kaesong region, the officials of the Kaesong city, the officials of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, and all members of the Chinese People's Volunteers Liaison Office in Kaesong attended the burial ceremony and laid wreaths.

### 新发现的我志愿军烈士遗骨  被安葬在朝鲜开城烈士陵园
黎能清
1989-05-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　新发现的我志愿军烈士遗骨   
    被安葬在朝鲜开城烈士陵园
    新华社开城５月１２日电（记者黎能清）新近在南朝鲜境内发现的１９具中国人民志愿军烈士遗骨安葬仪式，今天下午在朝鲜军事分界线边境城市开城的中国人民志愿军烈士陵园举行。
    这１９具志愿军烈士遗骨，是今天上午在板门店召开的朝鲜军事停战委员会第４９５次秘书长会议上由军事停战委员会“联合国军”方面移交给朝中方面的。
    这是自朝鲜停战后，在南朝鲜境内发现志愿军烈士遗骨最多的一次。同时发现的还有数百件志愿军烈士使用过的各种遗物，也已交给朝中方面。
    今天下午３点，朝鲜方面为这１９具志愿军烈士遗骨举行了安葬仪式，并演奏中朝两国国歌，朝鲜人民军战士鸣枪３０响。朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员崔义雄少将，中国驻朝鲜大使馆武官王大惠分别致悼词，高度赞扬中国人民志愿军在朝鲜祖国解放战争中立下的丰功伟绩。
    朝鲜人民军副总参谋长李洪顺少将，朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面中国人民志愿军委员田胜少将，开城地区驻军、开城市负责人以及中国驻朝鲜大使馆的官员、中国人民志愿军驻开城联络处全体成员参加了安葬仪式，并献了花圈。
