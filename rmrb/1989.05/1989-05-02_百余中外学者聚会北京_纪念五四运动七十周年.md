Title: Over a Hundred Scholars from Home and Abroad Gather in Beijing to Commemorate the 70th Anniversary of the May Fourth Movement
Author:
Date: 1989-05-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 2nd.

Over a hundred scholars from home and abroad gathered in Beijing to commemorate the 70th anniversary of the May Fourth Movement. The "International Academic Symposium on the 70th Anniversary of the May Fourth Movement" was held in Beijing on May 1st after a year of preparation.

Over 120 experts and scholars from China, Singapore, the United States, the Soviet Union, Japan, Australia, South Korea, Brazil, as well as Taiwan and Hong Kong regions, will discuss the theme "The May Fourth Movement and the Problem of Chinese Intellectuals." They will delve into topics such as the May Fourth Movement and traditional culture, the May Fourth Movement and Western thought, and the historical position and role of Chinese intellectuals in modern times.

This conference is jointly organized by the Academic Committee of the Chinese Culture Academy, the Beijing 21st Century Research Institute, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, and the University of Hong Kong. The aim is to explore the impact of the May Fourth Movement on China and the role of intellectuals in modern society, economy, culture, and political life. The conference also seeks to summarize the historical experiences and lessons learned from the conflicts and exchanges between Chinese and Western cultures since the May Fourth Movement.

### 百余中外学者聚会北京  纪念五四运动七十周年

1989-05-02
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　百余中外学者聚会北京   
    纪念五四运动七十周年
    本报北京讯  “纪念五四运动７０周年国际学术研讨会”经过一年时间的筹备，５月１日在京召开。
    来自中国、新加坡、美国、苏联、日本、澳大利亚、南朝鲜、巴西等国和台湾、香港地区的１２０余名专家、学者将以“五四与中国知识分子问题”为主题，就五四与传统文化、五四与西方思潮、中国知识分子在近现代的历史地位与作用等专题进行研讨。
    这次会议是由中国文化书院学术委员会、北京２１世纪研究院、香港中文大学、香港大学合办的，旨在探讨五四对中国的影响及知识分子在现代社会、经济、文化、政治生活中的地位和作用，总结自五四以来中国文化与西方文化的矛盾撞击和相互交流融合的历史经验和教训。
