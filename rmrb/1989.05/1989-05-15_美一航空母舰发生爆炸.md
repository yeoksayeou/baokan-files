Title: An American Aircraft Carrier Explodes
Author:
Date: 1989-05-15
Edition: 3

An American aircraft carrier, the "America," exploded off the coast of North Carolina. Two people were killed in the accident.

The explosion occurred in the second fuel pump room of the ship at around 7 PM local time on May 13, causing a large fire. The fire was eventually controlled by the ship's firefighting team.

The cause of the accident is still under investigation. The ship will continue its mission to the Mediterranean and the Indian Ocean as planned.

This is the third maritime accident for the U.S. Navy in a month.

### 美一航空母舰发生爆炸

1989-05-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美一航空母舰发生爆炸
    新华社华盛顿５月１４日电  美国大西洋舰队司令部发言人今天说，美国“阿美利坚”号航空母舰在美国北卡罗来纳州沿海发生爆炸事故，造成２人死亡。
    该舰第２号燃油泵房在当地时间１３日下午７点发生爆炸，并引起大火，经舰上消防队员扑救，大火被控制。
    该发言人说，事故原因正在调查之中，该舰仍将按原计划驶往地中海和印度洋执勤。
    这是美国海军一个月之内发生的第３次海上事故。
