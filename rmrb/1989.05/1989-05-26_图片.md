Title: Lome, the Capital of Togo
Author: Lǐ Běn Zhōng
Date: 1989-05-26
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency

Lome, the capital of Togo, is a coastal city known for its beautiful scenery and is often referred to as the "Geneva of Africa." The city's tourism industry is well-developed, generating an annual income of approximately $20 million.
