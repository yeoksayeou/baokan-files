Title: Saving Costs by Making Car Nets
Author: Luo Mingjun
Date: 1989-05-20
Page: 5

Every year, a large number of car nets are required to transport pigs externally. The Food Station in Tongxixi District, Qujiang County, Chongqing, organized its employees to weave car nets during their spare time to save costs. Over seven years, they have saved the country 160,000 yuan. The photo shows employees weaving car nets.
