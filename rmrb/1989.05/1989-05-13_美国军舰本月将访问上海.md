Title: U.S. Warship to Visit Shanghai This Month
Author:
Date: 1989-05-13
Edition: 2

	U.S. warship to visit Shanghai this month

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 13: It has been agreed upon by both the Chinese and American sides that a U.S. warship will visit Shanghai on May 19.
