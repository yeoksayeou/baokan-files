Title: The Presidential Gold Cup Football Tournament Begins
Author:
Date: 1989-05-23
Edition: 4

The sixth Presidential Gold Cup International Football Tournament began on the evening of May 21 in Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh. The South Korean team, the Indian team, the Chinese Liaoning team, and the Bangladesh Green team are in the first group. The Iranian team, the Thai team, the American Seahorse team, and the Bangladesh Red team are in the second group. The top two teams from each group will advance to the semi-finals, where they will play in a crossover format. The winning teams will then compete for the championship.

### 总统金杯足球赛开始

1989-05-23
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　总统金杯足球赛开始
    第六届总统金杯国际足球赛二十一日晚在孟加拉国首都达卡开幕。南朝鲜队、印度队、中国辽宁队和孟加拉国绿队分在第一组，伊朗队、泰国队、美国海马队和孟加拉国红队分在第二组，获两个组前两名的队将进行交叉半决赛，胜队争夺冠军。
