Title: Walking Out of the Gorge
Author: Jiang Sanli
Date: 1989-05-17
Page: 8

	Walking Out of the Gorge
	Jiang Sanli

	Just like this, we walk out of the gorge,
	Let the vast expanses of withered grass
	Become hazy folk paintings.
	The heart, because it is open, becomes open.
	Our eyes begin to brighten.
	The most touching songs
	Come from people's bright eyes,
	From the passionate collision of heart with heart.
	Let everything bathe in the gaze.
	In the bathing, achieve success and joy.

### 走出峡谷
蒋三立
1989-05-17

专栏：

　　走出峡谷
    蒋三立
    就这样一直走出峡谷

    来自心和心的热烈撞击
    让一切都在目光中沐浴
    在沐浴中获得成功与欢悦
