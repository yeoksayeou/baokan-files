Title: Xingtai Seals 78 Vacant Houses
Author:
Date: 1989-05-26
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shijiazhuang, May 25th.

Recently, Xingtai City in Hebei Province sealed off 78 vacant houses that have been unoccupied for more than half a year. Among these, 37 houses have already been reallocated to households without housing or those with severe housing shortages.

It is understood that 80 percent of the sealed vacant houses are those where the occupants have acquired or retained additional housing for their children. Among these, there are 15 houses that were acquired in advance for underage children, known as "children's rooms."

### 邢台查封七十八套空闲房

1989-05-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　邢台查封七十八套空闲房
    新华社石家庄五月二十五日电  河北省邢台市最近查封了七十八套无人居住达半年以上的空闲住房，其中三十七套已重新分配给无房户或住房拥挤的群众。
    据了解，这次被查封的空闲房百分之八十是住户为其子女多要或留用的，其中给未成年的子女提前要占的“童子房”就有十五套。
