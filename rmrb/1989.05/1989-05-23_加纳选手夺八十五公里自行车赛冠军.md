Title: Ghanaian Athlete Wins 85km Cycling Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-05-23
Edition: 4

Ghanaian athlete Joseph Sakyi won the 85km cycling championship on May 20 in the capital city of Accra, completing the race in 2 hours, 10 minutes, and 54 seconds. He will represent Ghana in the cycling event at the World Youth and University Student Festival to be held in Pyongyang in July. The 28-year-old Sakyi had previously won the 80km cycling championship in Accra on April 22 with a time of 2 hours, 6 minutes, and 3 seconds.

### 加纳选手夺八十五公里自行车赛冠军

1989-05-23
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　加纳选手夺八十五公里自行车赛冠军
    加纳全国冠军约瑟夫·萨基二十日在首都阿克拉以二小时十分五十四秒夺得八十五公里自行车赛冠军，并将代表加纳参加今年七月在平壤举行的世界青年和大学生联欢节的自行车比赛。二十八岁的萨基四月二十二日曾在阿克拉以二小时六分三秒的成绩，赢得八十公里自行车赛冠军称号。
