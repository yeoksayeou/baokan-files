Title: Beijing Hosts the Third Star Sea Cup Children's Piano Competition
Author: Tong Guli Ke
Date: 1989-05-19
Edition: 4

The third Star Sea Cup Children's Piano Competition in Beijing will open on May 19 at the Central Conservatory of Music. A total of 585 young participants from Beijing will compete in two professional groups and four amateur groups. The oldest contestant is 12 years old, and the youngest is just 5 years old. The competition will conclude on May 28.

### 北京市举办第三届星海杯儿童钢琴赛
童古丽珂
1989-05-19
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　北京市举办第三届星海杯儿童钢琴赛
    本报讯  北京市第三届星海杯儿童钢琴比赛，５月１９日将在中央音乐学院拉开帷幕。届时北京市的５８５名小朋友分别参加专业两个组和业余四个组的角逐。他们中最大的１２岁，最小的只有５岁。比赛将于本月２８日闭幕。  　（童古丽珂）
