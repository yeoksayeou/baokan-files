Title: World Bank's China Office Continues Operations
Author: Chen Ming
Date: 1989-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 30th.

The World Bank's office in China has stated that recent reports in some Hong Kong publications claiming that the World Bank has closed its office in China and suspended all loan negotiations are false.

An official from the office said, "The World Bank has been conducting various operations in China. We believe that the Chinese government's policy of opening up and reform will not change."

### 世界银行驻中国代表处一直在华进行各项业务
陈铭
1989-05-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    世界银行驻中国代表处
    一直在华进行各项业务
    新华社北京5月30日电  （记者陈铭）世界银行驻中国代表处今天声明：香港一些报刊最近两天关于世界银行关闭驻中国代表处和中止所有贷款谈判的报道是不真实的。
    这个代表处的一位官员说：“世界银行一直在中国进行各项业务。我们相信中国政府开放改革政策是不会改变的。”
