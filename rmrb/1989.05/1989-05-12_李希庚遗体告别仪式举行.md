Title: Funeral of Li Xigang Held
Author:
Date: 1989-05-12
Edition: 2

The funeral of Li Xigang, former deputy director of the General Office of the Central Military Commission, was held today at the Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery in Beijing.

Li Xigang passed away on May 2 in Beijing due to illness, at the age of 71.

Yang Shangkun, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Qin Jiwei, Wu Xiuquan, Liu Lantao, Cheng Zihua, Wei Guoqing, Liao Hansheng, Deng Zhaoxiang, Lü Zhengcao, Li Da, Yang Baibing, and others sent wreaths. The Central Military Commission, the Ministry of National Defense, the Ministry of Communications, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the China-Russia Friendship Association, the General Staff, the General Political Department, the General Logistics Department, the Military Commission's Discipline Inspection Commission, the Military Commission's General Office, the Shanxi Provincial and Wenshui County leadership, and other units sent wreaths.

Yan Mingfu, Wen Jiabao, Wang Ping, Yang Dezhi, Yu Qiuyi, Zhang Aiping, Chen Xilian, Geng Biao, Ye Fei, Hong Xuezhi, Liu Huaqing, Yang Chengwu, Chen Zaidao, Li Jukui, Chi Haotian, Zhao Nanqi, and other leaders attended the funeral and sent wreaths.

Li Xigang was a native of Wenshui County, Shanxi Province. He joined the revolution in 1936 and the Communist Party of China in 1937. He held various positions, including Director of the Propaganda Department of the Anti-Japanese Allied Army in Meng County, Deputy Director of the Editorial Department of the Jin-Cha-Ji Daily, Deputy Director of the New China News Agency's Jin-Cha-Ji Frontline Mobile Division, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the 19th Army Group of the North China Field Army, Director of the New China News Agency's 19th Army Group Division, and Director of the Cultural Department. He also served as Director of the Propaganda Department of the 19th Army Group of the Chinese People's Volunteers, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the PLA Daily, Deputy Secretary-General of the China-Russia Friendship Association, Secretary of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and responsible person for the Political Department of the Ministry of Communications. He was elected as a representative of the Sixth National People's Congress and a representative of the 13th National Congress of the Communist Party of China. During the war years, he endured hardships and bravely took on heavy responsibilities, completing various tasks assigned by the organization with excellence. He made significant contributions to the victory of the Anti-Japanese War, the War of Liberation, and the Korean War. After the founding of the People's Republic, he conscientiously implemented and propagated the Party's line, principles, and policies in his work in news and propaganda organizations. During the ten years of turmoil, he adhered to principles and fought resolutely, passing severe tests. During his time at the General Office of the Military Commission, he conscientiously implemented the instructions of the Party Central Committee and the Central Military Commission, paid attention to understanding and studying the characteristics of military work in the new era, and actively proposed constructive suggestions. He made contributions to the revolutionary, modern, and regularized construction of the military and the construction of the Military Commission's organs.

Li Xigang was a loyal Communist warrior, a long-tested member of the Communist Party of China, and an outstanding political and news worker. He was loyal to the Party and the people, devoting his life to the revolutionary and construction causes of the Chinese people.

### 李希庚遗体告别仪式举行

1989-05-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　李希庚遗体告别仪式举行
    新华社北京５月１１日电  中央军委办公厅原副主任李希庚遗体告别仪式，今天在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。
    李希庚同志因病于５月２日在北京逝世，终年７１岁。
    杨尚昆、徐向前、聂荣臻、秦基伟、伍修权、刘澜涛、程子华、韦国清、廖汉生、邓兆祥、吕正操、李达、杨白冰等送了花圈。中央军委、国防部、交通部、对外友协、中苏友协、解放军三总部、军委纪委、军委办公厅、山西省和文水县领导机关等单位送了花圈。
    阎明复、温家宝、王平、杨得志、余秋里、张爱萍、陈锡联、耿飚、叶飞、洪学智、刘华清、杨成武、陈再道、李聚奎、迟浩田、赵南起等领导同志参加向遗体告别，并送了花圈。
    李希庚同志是山西省文水县人，１９３６年参加革命，１９３７年加入中国共产党，历任晋察冀东北区盂县牺盟会宣传部长，晋察冀日报社编辑部副部长，新华社晋察冀前线野战分社副社长，华北野战军１９兵团宣传部副部长兼兵团新华分社社长、文化部部长，志愿军１９兵团宣传部部长，解放军报社副总编辑，中苏友协总会副秘书长，对外友协书记处书记，交通部政治部负责人等职，曾当选为六届全国人大代表、党的十三大代表。在战争年代，他历经艰难困苦，勇于承担重任，出色地完成了组织交予的各项任务，为抗日战争、解放战争和抗美援朝战争的胜利作出了积极的贡献。建国以后，他在从事新闻、宣传的组织领导工作中，认真贯彻和宣传党的路线方针政策。在十年动乱中，他坚持原则，坚持斗争，经受了严峻考验。在军委办公厅工作期间，他认真贯彻党中央和中央军委的指示，注重了解和研究新时期军队工作的特点，积极提出建设性建议，为我军的革命化、现代化、正规化建设和军委机关建设作出了贡献。
    李希庚同志是忠诚的共产主义战士，久经考验的中国共产党党员，优秀的政治工作者、新闻工作者。他忠于党、忠于人民，把毕生精力献给了中国人民的革命和建设事业。　（附图片）
