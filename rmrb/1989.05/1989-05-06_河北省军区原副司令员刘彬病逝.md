Title: Former Deputy Commander of Hebei Military Region Liu Bin Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-05-06
Edition: 2

Former Deputy Commander of Hebei Military Region Liu Bin passed away due to illness on April 21 in Beijing, aged 77.

A farewell ceremony for Liu Bin was held on April 30 at the Hall of the Revolutionary Martyrs in Babaoshan, Beijing. Nie Rongzhen, Qin Jiwei, Yang Dezhi, and other leaders, as well as the Beijing Military Region, Hebei Military Region, and the Party, government, and military organs of Shanghang County, Fujian Province, sent wreaths. Wang Peng and others attended the farewell ceremony and sent wreaths.

Liu Bin was a native of Shanghang County, Fujian Province. In 1928, he joined the revolution during the famous Minxi Uprising. The following year, he was enlisted in the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army and joined the Communist Youth League of China. In 1931, he became a member of the Communist Party of China. He held various positions, including soldier, operations staff officer, chief of the cipher bureau, battalion commander, regiment commander, chief of staff of the provincial military region, and deputy commander of the Inner Mongolia Military Region. During the Land Revolution War, he participated in the first to fifth counter-encirclement campaigns in the Central Soviet Area and the Long March. During the War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression, he participated in the Battle of Pingxing Pass, the defense of the Shaan-Gan-Ning Border Region, and the major campaign to disrupt transportation on the Pinghan Railway. Under extremely harsh, cruel, and dangerous conditions during various anti-sweeping operations, he demonstrated extraordinary willpower and courage, successfully organizing and completing communication and command liaison tasks for the Jin-Cha-Ji Military Region. During the War of Liberation, he participated in the Battle of Datong, the defense of Zhangjiakou, the Battle of Shijiazhuang, the two campaigns in Suiyuan, and the Pingjin Campaign. After the founding of the People's Republic, he led his troops to complete the task of suppressing bandits and traitors, consolidating the newly established people's regime. In 1955, he was awarded the rank of Major General.

Liu Bin was an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, a loyal communist warrior tested by time, and an excellent military commander of our army. During the arduous years of war, he fought bravely, participated in countless battles, commanded decisively, and completed various tasks excellently, receiving multiple commendations and rewards from the Military Commission and the General Headquarters. After the founding of the nation, he maintained and carried forward the spirit of hard work, dedicating a great deal of effort and energy to the construction of border defense and border economic development. During the ten years of turmoil, he resolutely resisted and fought against the counter-revolutionary clique of Lin Biao and the "Gang of Four." In extremely difficult circumstances, he did a great deal of work to maintain the stability of the troops, uphold the reputation of our Party and our army, and implement the Party's policy of national unity. He consistently and resolutely implemented the Party's lines, principles, and policies. He was strict with himself, kind to others, diligent and frugal, honest and upright, and selfless, embodying the noble revolutionary qualities and strong party principles of a communist.

### 河北省军区原副司令员刘彬病逝

1989-05-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　河北省军区原副司令员刘彬病逝
    新华社北京５月５日电  河北省军区原副司令员刘彬同志因病于４月２１日在北京逝世，享年７７岁。
    向刘彬同志遗体告别仪式４月３０日在北京八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。聂荣臻、秦基伟、杨得志等，北京军区、河北省军区和福建上杭县党、政、军机关送了花圈。王平等参加了向遗体告别仪式并送了花圈。
    刘彬同志是福建省上杭县人，１９２８年在著名的闽西暴动中投身革命，翌年编入中国工农红军，并加入中国共产主义青年团。１９３１年转为中国共产党党员。历任战士、作战参谋、机要科长、通信营长、通讯大队长、省军区参谋长兼军区后勤司令员、内蒙古军区副司令员等职。土地革命战争时期，他参加了中央苏区第一至第五次反“围剿”斗争和二万五千里长征。抗日战争时期，他参加了平型关战斗、陕甘宁边区卫戍战、平汉大破交通战。在历次反“扫荡”极其艰苦、残酷、险恶的条件下，以非凡的毅力和勇气，出色地组织和完成了晋察冀军区通信指挥联络保障任务。解放战争时期，他参加了解放大同战役、张家口保卫战、石家庄战役、两次绥远战役和平津战役。全国解放后，他率部队完成了剿匪肃特任务，巩固了新生的人民政权。１９５５年，他被授予少将军衔。
    刘彬同志是中国共产党优秀党员，久经考验的共产主义忠诚战士，我军优秀的军事指挥员。他在艰苦的战争年代，出生入死，身经百战，指挥果断，出色地完成各项任务，多次受到军委和总部的表彰奖励。建国后，他保持和发扬艰苦奋斗的精神，为边防建设和边疆经济建设奉献了大量的心血和精力。在十年动乱期间，他对林彪、“四人帮”反革命集团的倒行逆施进行了坚决的抵制和斗争，在极其困难的情况下，为保持部队稳定、维护我党我军的声誉及党的民族团结政策做了大量的工作。他一贯坚决贯彻和执行党的路线、方针和政策。他严于律己，宽厚待人，克勤克俭、一身清廉、光明磊落、正直无私，表现了一个共产党人高尚的革命品质和坚强的党性原则。
