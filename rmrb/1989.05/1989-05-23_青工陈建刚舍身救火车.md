Title: Young Worker Chen Jiangang Sacrifices Himself to Save a Train
Author: Cao Hongwei
Date: 1989-05-23
Edition: 2

Young worker Chen Jiangang sacrificed himself to save a train.

Xinhua News Agency, Shijiazhuang, May 22nd - (Correspondent Cao Hongwei) In Jin County, Hebei Province, young worker Chen Jiangang bravely gave his life to protect the safety of a train.

On May 13th at 4:35 PM, a freight train heading from Shijiazhuang to Dezhou was about to enter Jin County Railway Station. The barrier at the western crossing had already lowered. Suddenly, a startled donkey cart rushed through the barrier and into the center of the tracks, heading towards the train which was 50 meters away and traveling at 80 kilometers per hour. A train derailment accident seemed imminent. At this critical moment, Chen Jiangang, who was only 22 years old and on duty at the crossing, rushed forward without hesitation. He struggled to pull the donkey cart off the tracks. The train derailment was averted, but Chen Jiangang heroically sacrificed his life.

### 青工陈建刚舍身救火车
曹宏伟
1989-05-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　青工陈建刚舍身救火车
    新华社石家庄５月２２日电  （通讯员曹宏伟）河北晋县火车站青年工人陈建刚为了保护列车安全英勇地献出了自己的生命。
    ５月１３日下午４点３５分，一列从石家庄开往德州方向的货车即将进入晋县火车站，车站西道口的栏杆已经落下。突然，一辆受惊的驴车钻过道口栏杆进入铁道中心，与距道口５０米时速８０公里的火车相遇，眼看一场列车颠覆事故就要发生。在这千钧一发之际，正在道口防护值班的年仅２２岁的陈建刚奋不顾身地冲了上去，拚命把驴车从铁道中心往下拉。火车颠覆事故避免了，陈建刚却光荣地牺牲了。
