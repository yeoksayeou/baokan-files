Title: New U.S. Ambassador to China Presents Credentials to Chairman Yang
Author:
Date: 1989-05-08
Edition: 2

New U.S. Ambassador to China Presents Credentials to Chairman Yang

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 8 — State Chairman Yang Shangkun this morning accepted the credentials of the new U.S. Ambassador to China, James Lilley, at the Great Hall of the People.

Lilley arrived in Beijing on May 2.

It is reported that Lilley, aged 60, was born in China. He graduated from Yale University in 1951 and later studied classical Chinese literature at the University of Hong Kong and Columbia University in New York. He is a renowned expert on Asian affairs. From 1986 to 1989, Lilley served as the U.S. Ambassador to South Korea.

### 美驻华新大使向杨主席递交国书

1989-05-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　美驻华新大使向杨主席递交国书
    新华社北京５月８日电  国家主席杨尚昆今天上午在人民大会堂接受美国新任驻华大使李洁明递交的国书。
    李洁明于本月２日抵京。
    据悉，现年６０岁的李洁明出生于中国，１９５１年毕业于耶鲁大学，后来在香港和纽约哥伦比亚大学学习中国古典文学，是著名的亚洲事务专家。１９８６—１９８９年间，李洁明曾任美国驻南朝鲜大使。
