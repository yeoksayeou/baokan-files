Title: Sino-Australian Cooperation: The "Light Music" Radio Program Begins Broadcasting
Author:
Date: 1989-05-19
Edition: 4

	China International Radio and the Australian high-tech company AWA's cooperative project, the "Light Music" program, officially began broadcasting in Beijing on May 16. Australian Prime Minister Hawke specially wrote a congratulatory message for this program. This program provides listeners with 4 hours of content daily, including international news, weather forecasts, and famous songs composed and performed by renowned composers and singers around the world. The "Light Music" program will also gradually form a broadcasting network in cities such as Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Guangzhou, Xi'an, and Nanning in China. Every day from 7 AM to 9 AM, the Light Music FM program broadcasts English news on China International Radio. From 9 AM to 1 PM, it broadcasts music.

### 中澳合作  广播节目“轻松音乐”开播
文一
1989-05-19
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　中澳合作    
    广播节目“轻松音乐”开播
    本报北京讯  中国国际广播电台和澳大利亚高技术公司ＡＷＡ的合作项目“轻松音乐”节目５月１６日在京正式开播，澳大利亚总理霍克为这个节目专门写了贺词。这个节目每天为听众提供４个小时节目，内容包括国际新闻、天气预报以及由世界知名的作曲家和歌星创作演出的名曲。“轻松音乐”节目还将在我国的上海、天津、沈阳、广州、西安和南宁等城市逐步联成广播网络。每天上午７时至９时，轻松调频节目在中国国际电台播放英语新闻。９时至下午１时播放音乐。　　　　　
　　（文一）
