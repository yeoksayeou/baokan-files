Title: 40 Megabyte Magnetic Disk Servo Writing Device Developed
Author: Wáng Xīyuán
Date: 1989-05-02
Page: 2

Four Corners

A 40 Megabyte Magnetic Disk Servo Writing Device has been developed. The People's Daily reports: Correspondent Wáng Xīyuán reports: Located in Huaihua City, Hunan Province, the original "Third Front" enterprise, Jiannan Machinery Factory, in collaboration with Huazhong University of Science and Technology, has successfully developed a writing device capable of writing 1000 tracks on a 26.5 mm circular magnetic disk—the 40 Megabyte Magnetic Disk Servo Code Writing Device. This fills a gap in China's small magnetic disk servo writing technology.

Small magnetic disk drives are currently one of the most advanced types of external storage devices for computers. Similar to how photographic film stores images or magnetic tape stores sound, they can store a large amount of textual information. A 40 Megabyte magnetic disk can store information equivalent to 400 books, each containing 100,000 characters, and this information can be written on just three disk plates the size of an adult's palm. Currently, only two or three countries in the world can produce such devices.

### ４０兆磁盘伺服刻写装置问世
王溪元
1989-05-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　４０兆磁盘伺服刻写装置问世
    本报讯  记者王溪元报道：位于湘西怀化市的原“三线企业”建南机器厂，与华中理工大学联合研制成功能够在２６．５毫米环形磁盘上刻写出１０００个磁道的刻写装置——４０兆磁盘伺服码刻写装置，为我国小型磁盘伺服刻写技术填补了一项空白。
    小型磁盘驱动器是当前世界上最先进的一种计算机的外部存贮设备，像照相底片存入影象、录音磁带存入声音一样，可以存入大量文字信息。４０兆磁盘，即可存入相当于每本１０万字的４００本书的信息，而这种信息只需写在相当于成人巴掌大小的三个盘片上。现在世界上只有两三个国家能够生产。
