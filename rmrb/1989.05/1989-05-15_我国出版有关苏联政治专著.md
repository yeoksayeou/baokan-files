Title: China Publishes Political Monographs on the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1989-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency reports: The publishing departments in China have recently released a batch of political monographs on the Soviet Union.

The Liaoning University Press has published the Soviet political monograph "No Other Choice—The Experience, Lessons, and Future of Socialism." This book is authored by 34 renowned Soviet scholars. The authors present a new perspective, discussing the merits and demerits of the Soviet Union's 70-year revolution from multiple disciplines, fields, and broad perspectives. They provide sharp and historical evaluations of the economic models and political structures that hindered the development of Soviet society, as well as several major political cases.

The Chinese Academy of Social Sciences Press has successively published a series of books titled "The Reforms of the Gorbachev Era." These books systematically introduce the main contents, ideas, development trends, problems encountered, and strategies adopted by Gorbachev in various fields of reform. The series includes "Starting from Enterprise Reform—Gorbachev's Economic System Reform," "Democratization and Openness," "A Unified World of Interdependence," and "The Wind of Reform from Moscow." These works are respectively written by Chinese researchers.

### 我国出版有关苏联政治专著

1989-05-15
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　我国出版有关苏联政治专著
    本报综合新华社消息：我国出版部门最近出版了一批有关苏联问题的政治专著。
    辽宁大学出版社出版的苏联政治专著《别无选择——社会主义的经验、教训和未来》，由苏联３４位著名学者撰写而成。作者以一种全新的思维观念，从多学科、多领域、大跨度地论述了苏联７０年革命的功过是非，对阻碍苏联社会发展的经济模式和政治结构，以及一些重大政治公案，都给予尖锐的、历史性评价。
    中国社会科学出版社陆续出版的系列书《戈尔巴乔夫年代的改革》，系统地介绍了戈尔巴乔夫在各个领域进行改革的主要内容、思路、发展趋向、遇到的问题及采取的对策，其中有《从企业改革入手——戈尔巴乔夫的经济体制改革》、《民主化·公开性》、《一个相互依赖的统一世界》及《来自莫斯科的改革之风》等。这些著作分别由我国研究人员撰写。
