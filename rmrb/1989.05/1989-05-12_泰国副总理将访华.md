Title: Thailand's Deputy Prime Minister to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-05-12
Edition: 2

	Thailand's Deputy Prime Minister to Visit China
	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 11th: The spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced today at a press conference that, at the invitation of the Chinese government, Deputy Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Thailand, Pichai Radakorn, along with his wife, will visit China from May 26th to June 3rd for a friendly visit.

### 泰国副总理将访华

1989-05-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　泰国副总理将访华
    新华社北京五月十一日电  外交部发言人今天在新闻发布会上宣布，应中国政府的邀请，泰王国副总理披猜·拉达军偕夫人将于五月二十六日至六月三日对我国进行友好访问。
