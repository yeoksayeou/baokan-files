Title: Hu Qili and Others Meet with Cultural Relics Workers
Author:
Date: 1989-05-07
Edition: 2

Hu Qili, Li Tieming, Rui Xingwen, Huang Zhen, Xi Zhongxun, Zhou Gucheng, and Fang Yi and other leading comrades met with representatives attending the National Cultural Relics Work Conference this afternoon in the Great Hall of the People and had a group photo taken.

After the meeting, Comrade Li Tieming and some of the representatives held a discussion on the preservation and utilization of cultural relics.

### 胡启立等会见文物工作者

1989-05-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　胡启立等会见文物工作者
    新华社北京５月６日电  胡启立、李铁映、芮杏文、黄镇、习仲勋、周谷城、方毅等领导同志今天下午在人民大会堂会见了出席全国文物工作会议的代表，并合影留念。
    会见结束后，李铁映同志和部分代表举行座谈，就文物的保存、利用等问题交换了意见。
