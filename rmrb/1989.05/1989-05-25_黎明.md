Title: Dawn
Author: Liú Péi
Date: 1989-05-25
Edition: 8

Dawn
Liu Pei

The sky
A dark blue scarf that has ceased to flutter
Covered with strange, unique patterns
Vaguely, seagulls spread their wings and soar
Leaving eternal silhouettes on the sky's canvas
Looking down upon the night's river
The water is still, like a solid black mass
The dream boat has set sail from the endless source
Yet it has not broken through this deathly calm
In the distance, a lonely lighthouse
Flickers with white, ethereal light
But ultimately, it is swallowed by the thick night
Only that red dagger
Embedded in the distant horizon
An irresistible desire
When this dagger is suddenly withdrawn
Blood will gush out in unrestrained verses…

### 黎明
刘培
1989-05-25
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　黎明
    刘培
    天空
    一块停止飘动的暗蓝色纱巾
    布满了意象奇特的斑点
    隐约中有江鸥展翅飞翔
    在天幕上留下了永恒的倩影
    俯瞰夜色中的大江
    水宁静成黑色的固体
    梦中之舟已从不息的源头启航
    却没有冲出这片死亡的静谧
    远方有孤独的航标灯
    不断闪烁着白色的灵光
    但最终仍被浓重的黑夜吞噬
    只有那一把红色的匕首
    镶嵌在遥远的地平线上
    一种不可抗拒的欲望
    当这把匕首猛然抽回时
    血必将喷泻出不羁的诗行……
