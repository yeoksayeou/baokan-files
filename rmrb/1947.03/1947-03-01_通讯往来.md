Date: 1947-03-01
Page: 1

　　Correspondence
    1. Comrade Wang Zhijian of Daming: Please report on the situation and experience of carrying out anti-traitor revenge and realizing land reform in the newly recovered areas of Daming. Submissions can be broadcast by the Jinnan Reporter Group radio station.
    2. Comrade Xiao Fang of Handan City: Please report on the situation of prices, markets, commerce, and the lives of citizens in Handan City since November with specific facts.
    3. Correspondents from Zuoquan, Lucheng, Pingshun, Yushe, and other counties: Your reports on the February filling movement were very comprehensive. What is the current focus of the movement? What specific experiences have you gained in deepening the movement? And what specific problems currently exist? Please seize the time to report. And please select one or two typical villages to report on in March.
    4. Joint Office and Correspondents of Licheng: The news of the integration of winter production and land reform in Beiliu Village has been broadcast to various places by the Yan'an headquarters, which believes it has great propaganda value. We hope to continue to organize the specific process of integrating production and mass movement in the village, the masses' thoughts and demands, various specific methods, steps, and experiences and lessons. This will be of great help to the current integration of land reform and production movement.
      The People's Daily Correspondence Liaison Section
        March 1

### 通讯往来
本报通讯联络科
1947-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来
    一、大名王志坚同志：请报导大名新收复区进行反奸复仇实现土地改革的情形及经验，来稿可找冀南记者团电台播发。
    二、邯郸市萧方同志：请用具体事实报导邯市自十一月以来物价、市场、商业以及市民生活的情形。
    三、左权、潞城、平顺、榆社等县通讯员：二月份你处填补运动报导很全面。目前运动中心如何？深入运动有何具体的经验？以及目前存在着一些什么具体问题？请抓紧报导。并望选择一二典型村在三月份加以报导。
    四、黎城联合办公室暨通讯员：北流村冬季生产与土地改革结合消息，延安总社已播发各地，并认为宣传价值极大，望继续整理该村生产与群运结合的具体过程，群众思想及要求，各种具体方法、步骤以及经验教训。这对于目前土地改革与生产运动结合，将有很大帮助。
      本报通讯联络科
        三月·一日
