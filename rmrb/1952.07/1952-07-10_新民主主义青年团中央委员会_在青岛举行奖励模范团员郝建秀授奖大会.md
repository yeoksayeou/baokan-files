Title: Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China Held Award Ceremony in Qingdao to Honor Model League Member Hao Jianxiu
Author:
Date: 1952-07-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, July 8th: The award ceremony of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China to honor model Youth League member Hao Jianxiu was grandly held in Qingdao on the 6th. Participants in the meeting included Xue Shangshi, Deputy Secretary of the Qingdao Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhang Gongzhi, Deputy Mayor of Qingdao Municipal People's Government, Lu Zhao, Secretary of the Qingdao Municipal Committee of the Youth League, all the representatives attending the Qingdao Municipal Youth League Members' Representative Conference, all the workers of the "Hao Jianxiu Group," and representatives from various factories, institutions, organizations, and schools, totaling more than 1,200 people.

    At the award ceremony, Lin Ping, Director of the Propaganda Department of the Shandong Provincial Working Committee of the Youth League, read out the "Decision of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China on Rewarding Comrade Hao Jianxiu" on behalf of the Central Committee of the League. Then, amidst warm applause from the audience, he presented the award certificate to Hao Jianxiu. In his speech, Lin Ping called on Youth League members to actively participate in the increase production and practice economy movement, and to learn from Hao Jianxiu's spirit of loving the motherland, loving Chairman Mao, and her conscious attitude towards labor. Hao Jianxiu delivered a reply to the conference. She expressed that under the leadership of the Party and the League, she would actively study political theory, cultural and scientific knowledge, and advanced production experience, guarantee the overfulfillment of the increase production and practice economy plan, and create more wealth for the country.

### 新民主主义青年团中央委员会  在青岛举行奖励模范团员郝建秀授奖大会

1952-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    新民主主义青年团中央委员会
    在青岛举行奖励模范团员郝建秀授奖大会
    【新华社青岛八日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会奖励模范青年团员郝建秀授奖大会六日在青岛隆重举行。参加大会的有中国共产党青岛市委员会副书记薛尚实、青岛市人民政府副市长张公制、青年团青岛市委员会书记鲁钊以及出席青岛市青年团员代表会议的全体代表、“郝建秀小组”全体工人和各工厂、机关、团体、学校的代表共一千二百余人。
    在授奖大会上，青年团山东省工作委员会宣传部长林萍代表团中央宣读了“中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会关于奖励郝建秀同志的决定”，接着在全场热烈的掌声中把奖状授给郝建秀。林萍在讲话中号召青年团员们积极地参加增产节约运动，学习郝建秀热爱祖国、热爱毛主席的精神及自觉的劳动态度。郝建秀向大会致答词。她表示要在党和团的领导下，积极学习政治理论、文化科学知识及先进生产经验，保证超额完成增产节约计划，为国家创造更多的财富。
