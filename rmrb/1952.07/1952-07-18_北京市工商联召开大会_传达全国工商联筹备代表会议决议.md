Title: Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce Convenes a Conference to Convey Resolutions of the Preparatory Representative Meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce
Author:
Date: 1952-07-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: The Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce convened a conference on the 17th to convey the proceedings and resolutions of the Preparatory Representative Meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. Attending the conference were members of the Industry and Commerce Federation offices from various districts, representatives from various industries, and representatives of stallholders, totaling over 5,000 people. In addition, representatives from Beijing’s state-owned specialized companies, public enterprise companies, and cooperative headquarters were also invited to participate. After delivering a report, Fu Huating, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, put forward six suggestions to the city’s industrial and commercial circles: First, conscientiously implement the various resolutions of the Preparatory Representative Meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. Second, launch a study movement to conscientiously study and discuss the Common Program and meeting documents, especially the report by Chen Yun, Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council, and earnestly comply with them in light of actual conditions. Third, enhance business confidence, welcome the autumn peak season for production and sales, launch a production movement, and actively improve production and business, and develop the exchange of goods between urban and rural areas. Fourth, under the leadership of the working class and the state-owned economy, improve labor-capital relations and public-private relations. Fifth, resolutely avoid the “five poisons,” strive to improve operations, reduce costs, and improve product quality. Sixth, require all industries, offices, and branches to widely and deeply disseminate the information, making it known to every household, and promptly reflect their opinions.

### 北京市工商联召开大会  传达全国工商联筹备代表会议决议

1952-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市工商联召开大会
    传达全国工商联筹备代表会议决议
    【新华社十七日讯】北京市工商业联合会于十七日召开大会传达中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议经过及决议。参加大会的有各区工商联办事处委员，各行业代表，摊商代表等五千余人。此外还邀请了北京市国营专业公司、公营企业公司、合作总社等单位代表参加。北京市工商业联合会主任委员傅华亭在传达报告后，对全市工商界提出了六点意见：第一，认真地贯彻执行中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议的各项决议。第二，展开学习运动，认真学习讨论共同纲领及会议文件，特别是政务院财经委员会陈云主任的报告，结合实际切实遵行。第三，提高经营信心，迎接秋季产销旺季，开展生产运动，积极搞好生产和业务，展开城乡物资交流。第四，在工人阶级与国营经济领导下，搞好劳资关系和公私关系。第五，坚决不犯“五毒”，努力改善经营，降低成本，提高产品质量。第六，要求各行业、各办事处、各分会、广泛深入地进行传达，做到家喻户晓，并及时反映他们的意见。
