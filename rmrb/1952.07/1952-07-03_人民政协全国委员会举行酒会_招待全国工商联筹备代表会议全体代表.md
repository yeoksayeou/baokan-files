Title: National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Holds Reception to Greet All Delegates to the Preparatory Conference of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce
Author:
Date: 1952-07-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a reception at the Beijing Hotel at 7:30 p.m. on June 30 to greet all delegates attending and observing the preparatory meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. More than 30 people from the National Committee of the CPPCC attended the reception, including Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Shen Junru, Li Jishen, Li Weihan, Ma Xulun, Zhang Naiqi, Xu Deheng, Zhang Xiruo, He Xiangning, Feng Wenbin, Liang Xi, and Shao Lizi.

Also invited were Chen Yun, Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, Deputy Director Bo Yibo, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, and heads of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

At the reception, everyone frequently raised their glasses to congratulate the success of the preparatory meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce.

### 人民政协全国委员会举行酒会  招待全国工商联筹备代表会议全体代表

1952-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会举行酒会
    招待全国工商联筹备代表会议全体代表
    【新华社二日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会于六月三十日晚七时半在北京饭店举办酒会，招待出席和列席中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议的全体代表。中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会出席酒会的有周恩来、朱德、沈钧儒、李济深、李维汉、马叙伦、章乃器、许德珩、张奚若、何香凝、冯文彬、梁希、邵力子等三十余人。
    应邀到会的还有中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会主任陈云，副主任薄一波，及中央人民政府各部门首长，和北京市人民政府首长等。
    酒会上，大家频频举杯祝贺中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议的成功。
