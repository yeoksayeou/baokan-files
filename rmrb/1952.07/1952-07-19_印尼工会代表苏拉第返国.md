Title: Indonesian Trade Union Representative Surat Returned Home
Author: 
Date: 1952-07-19
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 17th: Surat, a member of the Indonesian trade union delegation invited to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations, left Guangzhou on the 16th to return home. The first secretary of the Indonesian trade union delegation, Subijanto, and eight others had already left Guangzhou on June 29th to return home.

### 印尼工会代表苏拉第返国

1952-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼工会代表苏拉第返国
    【新华社广州十七日电】应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼的印度尼西亚工会代表团团员苏拉第十六日离广州回国，印尼工会代表团第一秘书苏比延多等八人，已先在六月二十九日离广州回国。
