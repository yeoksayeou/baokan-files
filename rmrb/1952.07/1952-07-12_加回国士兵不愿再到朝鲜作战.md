Title: Returned Canadian Soldiers Refuse to Fight in Korea Again
Author:
Date: 1952-07-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, July 9th (TASS): According to Canada's "The Tribune," Canadian soldiers who returned to Winnipeg after spending a year on the Korean front told newspaper reporters that no force could make them fight in Korea again, and they were absolutely unwilling to return to Korea.

### 加回国士兵不愿再到朝鲜作战

1952-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    加回国士兵不愿再到朝鲜作战
    【新华社讯】塔斯社渥太华九日电：据加拿大的“先驱报”报道：在朝鲜前线逗留一年后回到温尼伯的加拿大士兵们对报纸记者说：没有力量可以使他们再到朝鲜去作战，他们决不愿意回到朝鲜去。
