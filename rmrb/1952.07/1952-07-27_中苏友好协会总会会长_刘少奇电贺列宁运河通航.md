Title: Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Sends Congratulations on the Inauguration of the Lenin Canal
Author:
Date: 1952-07-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, sent a telegram to Mr. Ginisov, Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, on the 21st to congratulate the Soviet Union on the inauguration ceremony of the Lenin Volga-Don Navigation Canal, scheduled for the 27th. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To Mr. Ginisov, Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Moscow:
    The completion of the Lenin Volga-Don Navigation Canal, a great communist construction project of the Soviet Union, is another great victory for the brilliant thought of Lenin and Stalin. It demonstrates the increasing realization of the noble human ideal – the communist system – on Soviet soil, and shows the ever-increasing strength of the Soviet Union, the bulwark of world peace. The achievements of the great Soviet people in peaceful construction have inspired the people of all countries in the world who are opposing wars of aggression and defending lasting peace, and especially the Chinese people who are building their own country and creating conditions for entering socialism. The Chinese people rejoice in this victory. On the occasion of the inauguration of the Lenin Canal, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to the great Soviet people and pay my sincere friendly respects to the builders who participated in this project.
                             Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
                                      July 21, 1952

### 中苏友好协会总会会长  刘少奇电贺列宁运河通航

1952-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会总会会长
    刘少奇电贺列宁运河通航
    【新华社二十六日讯】苏联列宁伏尔加河—顿河运河定于二十七日举行通航典礼，中苏友好协会总会会长刘少奇特于二十一日致电苏联对外文化协会主席吉尼索夫表示祝贺。电文如下：莫斯科苏联对外文化协会吉尼索夫主席：
    苏联伟大的共产主义建设工程列宁伏尔加河—顿河通航运河的建成，是列宁和斯大林光辉思想的又一次伟大胜利。它表明了人类崇高思想——共产主义制度在苏维埃国土上的日益接近实现，表明了世界和平堡垒——苏联国力的愈益强盛。伟大的苏联人民在和平建设中所获得的这种成就鼓舞了正在反对侵略战争、保卫持久和平的世界各国人民，特别是鼓舞了正在建设自己祖国、创立进入社会主义条件的中国人民。中国人民欢庆这一胜利。际兹列宁运河举行通航之日，特向伟大的苏联人民表示热烈的祝贺，并向参加这一工程的建设者们致亲切的友谊的敬礼。
                             中苏友好协会总会会长刘少奇
                                      一九五二年七月二十一日
