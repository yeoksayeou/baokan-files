Title: Calcutta India-China Friendship Association Holds Photo Exhibition to Commemorate the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1952-07-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 27th: To commemorate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Calcutta India-China Friendship Association is holding a photo exhibition from July 26th to August 1st at the hall of the local Islamia College. The exhibition mainly focuses on the history of the Chinese People's Liberation Army over the past 25 years, and also includes photos about China's land reform and the liberation of Chinese women. In addition, there are exhibits of Chinese art. On August 1st, a mass rally will be held at the exhibition venue to commemorate the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 加尔各答印中友协举办图片展览  纪念中国人民解放军建军节

1952-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    加尔各答印中友协举办图片展览
    纪念中国人民解放军建军节
    【新华社新德里二十七日电】为纪念中国人民解放军建军二十五周年，加尔各答印中友好协会从七月二十六日到八月一日在当地回教学院大厅举行图片展览。展览内容主要是关于中国人民解放军二十五年来的历史，并有关于中国土地改革和中国妇女解放的图片。此外，还有关于中国艺术的展览品。八月一日，在展览会会址将举行群众大会以纪念中国人民解放军建军节。
