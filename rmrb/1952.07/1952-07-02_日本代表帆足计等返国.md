Title: Japanese Representatives Hoashi Kei and Others Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-07-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 30th - Japanese representatives Hoashi Kei, Miyakoshi Kisuke, and their secretary Nakao Kazuo, who attended the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, arrived in Guangzhou by plane from Beijing on the 29th and left Guangzhou for home on the 30th. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Zhang Luchun, Vice-Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others.

### 日本代表帆足计等返国

1952-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

    日本代表帆足计等返国
    【新华社广州三十日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的日本代表帆足计、宫腰喜助及其秘书中尾和夫一行三人，二十九日从北京乘飞机抵广州，已在三十日离广州回国。前往车站欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州市分会副主席张醁村等多人。
