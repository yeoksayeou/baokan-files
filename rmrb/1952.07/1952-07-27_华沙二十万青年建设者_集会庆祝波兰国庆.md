Title: 200,000 Young Builders in Warsaw Rally to Celebrate Polish National Day
Author:
Date: 1952-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 24th: According to the Polish News Agency, 200,000 young people gathered in Warsaw for the Polish Youth Builders' Congress held a rally on the 22nd to celebrate Polish National Day and to celebrate the Polish Parliament's adoption of the Constitution of the People's Republic of Poland. At the meeting, they represented all the young people of Poland, swearing allegiance to their people's motherland.

Early in the morning on the 22nd, the magnificent songs of the young builders spread from the congress venue - Victory Square - to the crowded streets nearby. On the venue, steelworkers, miners, sailors, engineers, farmers, and builders of new cities and factories - builders of a prosperous and strong new Poland - formed close ranks. At 10 a.m., the cheers of "Long live Bierut" interrupted the music and songs. The band played the Polish national anthem, and President Bierut appeared on the podium. Members of the Political Bureau of the Polish United Workers' Party, Polish government personnel, and people from all walks of life attended the meeting. Foreign guests attending the meeting included representatives of the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League, the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and the International Union of Students.

President Bierut delivered a speech to the young builders, congratulating the youth on National Day and the brilliant achievements of the young builders, and calling on them to continue to devote themselves to the great ideal of building socialism. During Bierut's speech, the shouts of "Stalin, Bierut, Peace!" "Long live our dear motherland!" "Long live the builders of the People's Poland!" shook the huge square.

Then, the Chairman of the Central Committee of the Polish Youth League, Matewin, stepped onto the podium and led all the youth to raise their hands in a solemn oath. These outstanding Polish young men and women all raised their hands and solemnly swore allegiance to their motherland. After the oath, the whole audience sang the magnificent youth song: "Song of Freedom." 25,000 pigeons were released into the sky above the venue.

### 华沙二十万青年建设者  集会庆祝波兰国庆

1952-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    华沙二十万青年建设者
    集会庆祝波兰国庆
    【新华社二十四日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：聚集在华沙参加波兰青年建设者大会的二十万青年，二十二日举行大会庆祝波兰国庆节，并庆祝波兰议会通过波兰人民共和国宪法。他们在会上代表全波兰青年，宣誓效忠于他们的人民祖国。
    二十二日清早，青年建设者的雄壮的歌声，就从大会会场——胜利广场传遍了附近的人山人海的街道。会场上，钢铁工人、矿工、水手、工程师、农民、学生、新城市和工厂的建设者——繁荣富强的新波兰的建设者排成了紧密的队伍。上午十时，
    “贝鲁特万岁”的欢呼声，打断了音乐和歌声，乐队演奏着波兰国歌，这时，贝鲁特总统出现在主席台上。参加大会的有波兰统一工人党政治局委员，波兰政府人员，各界人士。参加大会的外宾有苏联列宁共产主义青年团的代表，世界民主青年联盟和国际学生联合会的代表。
    贝鲁特总统向青年建设者发表了演说，他向青年们祝贺国庆，祝贺青年建设者的光辉成就，并号召他们继续献身于建设社会主义的伟大理想。贝鲁特演说的时候，“斯大林，贝鲁特，和平！”“我们亲爱的祖国万岁！”“人民波兰建设者万岁！”的欢呼声震撼了巨大的广场。
    接着，波兰青年联盟中央委员会主席马特温走上主席台，领导全体青年举手宣誓，这些优秀的波兰男女青年都跟着举起手来，庄严地宣誓效忠祖国。宣誓以后，全场唱起雄壮的青年歌曲：“自由之歌”。会场上放出二万五千只鸽子，在上空飞翔。
