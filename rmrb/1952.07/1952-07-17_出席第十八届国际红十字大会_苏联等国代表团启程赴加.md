Title: Soviet Union and Other Countries' Delegations Depart for Canada to Attend the 18th International Red Cross Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-07-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th dispatch from TASS: The Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union has dispatched a delegation on the 15th to participate in the 18th International Red Cross Conference. This conference is scheduled to open on July 26th in Toronto, Canada.
    The head of the delegation is Slavin, and the members include Bashkov, Chigarenko, Sokirkin, Sperenskaya, and others.
    Soviet government representative Zonov, Ukrainian Republic government representative Hogor, and Belarusian Republic government representative Belyatsky will also participate in the work of the conference.

### 出席第十八届国际红十字大会  苏联等国代表团启程赴加

1952-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    出席第十八届国际红十字大会
    苏联等国代表团启程赴加
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十六日讯：苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会已派遣代表团于十五日启程前往参加第十八届国际红十字大会。这次大会订于七月二十六日在加拿大的多伦多开幕。
    代表团团长是司拉文，团员有巴什科夫、齐加连科、索基尔金、斯彼伦斯卡雅等人。
    苏联政府代表左诺夫、乌克兰共和国政府代表霍贺尔、白俄罗斯共和国政府代表贝利雅茨基等人，也将参加大会的工作。
