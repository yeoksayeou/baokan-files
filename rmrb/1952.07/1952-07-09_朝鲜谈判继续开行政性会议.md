Title: Korean Negotiations Continue with Administrative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-07-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 8th. The plenary session of both delegations to the Korean armistice negotiations continued its administrative meeting on the 8th to discuss the issue of repatriation of prisoners of war.

### 朝鲜谈判继续开行政性会议

1952-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判继续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城八日电】八日朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团大会继续进行行政性会议，讨论战俘遣返问题。
