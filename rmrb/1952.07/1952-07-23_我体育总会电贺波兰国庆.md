Title: All-China Sports Federation Sends Congratulations on Polish National Day
Author:
Date: 1952-07-23
Page: 1

    [From Our Own Correspondent]

    All-China Sports Federation Sends Congratulations on Polish National Day
    [By Our Correspondent] The All-China Sports Federation sent a telegram to the Polish Supreme Committee for Physical Culture and Sports on July 22, congratulating them on Polish National Day. The telegram stated: "On the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of Poland, we extend our warm congratulations to all the Polish people, sports workers, and athletes on behalf of Chinese sports workers and athletes. Polish people's sports have developed significantly under the care and leadership of the United Workers' Party and the government. We wish you even greater achievements in the struggle to complete the Six-Year Plan and in developing the Polish people's sports."

### 我体育总会电贺波兰国庆

1952-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我体育总会电贺波兰国庆
    【本报讯】中华全国体育总会于七月二十二日致电波兰最高体育事务委员会，祝贺波兰国庆。电文称：值此波兰共和国国庆日之际，谨代表中国体育工作者和运动员向波兰全体人民、体育工作者和运动员致以热烈的祝贺。波兰人民体育运动，在统一工人党和政府的关怀、领导下，已有显著发展。谨祝你们在完成六年计划的斗争及发展波兰人民的体育事业中获得更大的成就。
