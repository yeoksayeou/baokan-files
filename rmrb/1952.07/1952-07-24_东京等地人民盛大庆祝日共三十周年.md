Title: People in Tokyo and other places grandly celebrate the 30th anniversary of the Japanese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1952-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 22nd TASS: According to the "Akahata" newspaper: On the day of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Japanese Communist Party (July 15), a 10,000-person rally was held in Nagoya. Those who spoke at the meeting included Horie Muraichi, chairman of the Japan-Soviet Friendship Association, and Communist Party Congressman Tajima. The rally held in Osaka was attended by 45,000 people.

    A celebration was held in Tokyo to commemorate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party. Representatives from 250 labor unions and other democratic organizations participated in the celebration.

### 东京等地人民盛大庆祝日共三十周年

1952-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    东京等地人民盛大庆祝日共三十周年
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社东京二十二日讯：“赤旗报”消息：在日本共产党成立三十周年的那一天（七月十五日），名古屋举行了一个万人大会。在会上讲话的有日苏亲善协会主席堀江邑一及共产党国会议员田岛。在大阪举行的大会有四万五千人参加。
    在东京举行了纪念共产党成立三十周年的联欢会。参加联欢会的有二百五十个工会及其他民主团体的代表。
