Title: Korean Government Vigorously Adopts Suffering Orphans
Author:
Date: 1952-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 24th (TASS): The barbaric bombing of Korean cities and villages by the American air force has orphaned thousands of children. The government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has built a vast network of children's homes, schools and boarding schools for these children of patriotic martyrs, so that these children who have lost their parents can be well cared for. Many members of the Korean Democratic Women's League, in order to assist the government in raising war orphans, have adopted these children who have lost their parents.

### 朝鲜政府大力收养受难孤儿

1952-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府大力收养受难孤儿
   【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社平壤二十四日讯：美国空军野蛮地轰炸朝鲜的城市和乡村，已使数千儿童成为孤儿。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府为这些爱国烈士的子女修建了广大的儿童之家、学校和寄宿学校网，使这些丧失了父母的孩子们能够得到很好的照顾。朝鲜民主妇女同盟的许多盟员，为了协助政府抚育战争孤儿，纷纷收养这些丧失怙恃的孩子们。
