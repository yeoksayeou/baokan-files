Our Great Motherland
Sun Zhenhai, a nursery worker at the Beijing Railway Bureau, created the method of propagating poplar by burying branches, opening up a broad path for large-scale propagation of poplar and increased forestry production in our country in the future. This is a scene of Sun Zhenhai pruning in a three-year-old poplar nursery.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京铁路分局苗圃工人孙振海创造的毛白杨埋条繁殖法，开辟了今后我国大量繁殖毛白杨和林业增产的宽广道路。这是孙振海正在三年生的毛白杨苗圃中进行整枝的情形。
