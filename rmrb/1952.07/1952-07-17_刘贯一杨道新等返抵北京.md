Title: Liu Guanyi, Yang Daoxin, and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese delegation to the World Peace Council's Special Session in Berlin, and specially invited representatives Yang Daoxin, Guo Fang, Fang Shishan, Liu Chongle, and their staff, a total of seven people, returned to Beijing by plane on the 16th. Wu Maosun and Cui Yueli, Deputy Secretaries-General of the China Peace Committee, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 刘贯一杨道新等返抵北京

1952-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    刘贯一杨道新等返抵北京
    【新华社十六日讯】我国出席世界和平理事会柏林特别会议代表团秘书长刘贯一，特邀代表杨道新、郭芳、方石珊、刘崇乐和工作人员一行七人，十六日乘飞机返抵北京，到机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长吴茂荪、崔月犁等人。
