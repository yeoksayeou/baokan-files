Title: Yoshida Government Announces So-Called "Subversive Activities Prevention Act" To Take Effect Regularly, Japanese Democratic Organizations Continue to Firmly Protest Fascist Law, Japanese Cultural Workers' Conference Decides to Launch Campaign to Ban Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-07-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 18th: TASS Tokyo, July 17th: According to the "Asahi Shimbun," Justice Minister Atsutaro Kimura declared that the so-called "Subversive Activities Prevention Act" will take effect on July 21st.
	Japanese democratic organizations continue to resolutely protest this fascist law. According to the "World Peace News," the Japanese Peace Protection Committee issued a statement calling on the Japanese people to continue to fight for the repeal of the law. According to "Kowa Shimbun," the General Peace Loving Patriotic Movement Council also issued a statement condemning this repressive law as violating the Constitution and suppressing the freedom of the people.
	Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 18th: TASS Tokyo, July 18th: According to the "Social Times," Japanese cultural workers at their conference decided to launch a campaign to demand the prohibition of the use of bacteriological weapons, and to request the Japanese and American governments to approve as soon as possible the "Protocol for the Prohibition of the Use of Asphyxiating, Poisonous or Other Gases, and of Bacteriological Methods of Warfare" concluded in Geneva.

### 吉田政府宣布所谓“防止破坏活动法案”定期生效  日本民主组织继续坚决抗议法西斯法律  日本文化工作者会议决定发起禁用细菌武器运动

1952-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    吉田政府宣布所谓“防止破坏活动法案”定期生效
    日本民主组织继续坚决抗议法西斯法律
    日本文化工作者会议决定发起禁用细菌武器运动
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社东京十七日讯：据“朝日新闻”消息：日本法务府总裁木村笃太郎宣称：所谓“防止破坏活动法”将于七月二十一日生效。
    日本各民主组织继续坚决抗议该项法西斯法律。据“世界和平报”报道：日本拥护和平委员会发表声明，号召日本人民继续为争取取消该项法律而斗争。“讲和新闻”消息，全面媾和爱国运动协议会也发表声明，指责这项镇压法令违反宪法，压制人民的自由。
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社东京十八日讯：据“社会时报”称，日本文化工作者在他们的会议上决定发动一次运动，要求禁止使用细菌武器，并要求日本与美国政府早日批准在日内瓦订立的“关于禁用毒气或类似毒品及细菌方法作战议定书”。
