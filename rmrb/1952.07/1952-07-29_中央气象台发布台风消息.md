Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Releases Typhoon News
Author:
Date: 1952-07-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued the following typhoon message at 7 p.m. on the 28th:
　　At 2 p.m. on July 28th, the center of the typhoon was located 240 li east of Luzon Island in the Philippines, at 18 degrees north latitude and 123.5 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind force near the center could reach level 12 (i.e., 234 li per hour). At 160 li from the center, the wind force could reach level 10 (i.e., 180 li per hour). At 400 li from the center, the wind force could reach level 7 (i.e., 110 li per hour). It is currently moving in a west-northwesterly direction at a speed of 40 li per hour. It is predicted that by 2 p.m. on the 29th, the center of the typhoon may move to 300 li northwest of Luzon Island (i.e., 20.4 degrees north latitude, 119.1 degrees east longitude). The sea area from south of Wenzhou to Yulin Port on Hainan Island will have large waves, and the coastal areas of Fujian and Guangdong may experience winds of around level 6, which will continue to increase.

### 中央气象台发布台风消息

1952-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台发布台风消息
    【新华社二十八日讯】中央气象台二十八日下午七时发布台风消息如下：
    七月二十八日下午二时台风中心位于菲律宾的吕宋岛东面二百四十华里的洋面上，即北纬十八度东经一二三点五度，中心附近最大风力可达十二级
    （即每小时二三四华里）离中心一六○华里处，风力可达十级（即每小时一八○华里）离中心四○○华里处风力可达七级（即每小时一一○华里）。目前正向西北偏西方向进袭，每小时移动四○华里，预测二十九日下午二时台风中心可能移至吕宋岛西北三○○华里处。（即北纬二○点四度，东经一一九点一度）从温州以南到海南岛榆林港之间的海面将有较大的风浪，福建、广东沿海风力可能达到六级左右，并将继续加大。
