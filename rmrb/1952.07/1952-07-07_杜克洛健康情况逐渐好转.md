Title: Duclos's Health Gradually Improving
Author:
Date: 1952-07-07
Page: 4

    Duclos's Health Gradually Improving
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 6th, dispatch from Tass, Paris] The "Humanité" has published communiqués on the health of Maurice Thorez, Secretary of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, for several days. Duclos's doctors pointed out: "Duclos's health is slowly improving. Due to overstrain, it is expected that he will need a long time to recover."

### 杜克洛健康情况逐渐好转

1952-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    杜克洛健康情况逐渐好转
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社巴黎讯：“人道报”连日发表关于法国共产党中央委员会书记杜克洛的健康情况的公报。杜克洛的医师们指出：“杜克洛的健康情况正在慢慢好转。由于紧张过度，预料他恢复健康所需的时间很长。”
