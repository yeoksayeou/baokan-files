Title: National Higher Education Enrollment Committee Decides to Establish Seventy-Eight Examination Districts Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1952-07-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 15th:

    To facilitate senior high school graduates applying for higher education institutions this summer, the National Higher Education Enrollment Committee has decided to set up seventy-eight examination districts across the country, allowing young people in each district to apply for national higher education institutions nearby. The examination districts are as follows:

    North China Region: (Ten)
    Beijing, Tianjin, Baoding, Tangshan, Changli, Zhuoxian, Taiyuan, Yuncheng, Zhangjiakou, Gui Sui.

    Northeast Region: (Ten)
    Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin, Dalian, Qiqihar, Jinzhou, Mudanjiang, Tonghua, Yanji, Jiamusi.

    East China Region: (Twenty-Four)
    Shanghai, Nanjing, Qingdao, Jinan, Xuzhou, Tai'an, Fuzhou, Zhangzhou, Nanping, Wuhu, Bengbu, Hefei, Anqing, Yangzhou, Nantong, Huaiyin, Yancheng, Taizhou, Jinhua, Wenzhou, Hangzhou, Ningbo, Wuxi, Suzhou.

    Central South Region: (Twenty-Two)
    Wuhan, Xiangyang, Shashi, Changsha, Hengyang, Changde, Lingling, Nanchang, Shangrao, Ganzhou, Ji'an, Kaifeng, Nanyang, Luoyang, Guangzhou, Haikou, Shantou, Meixian, Guilin, Liuzhou, Nanning, Wuzhou.

    Southwest Region: (Nine) (Senior high school graduates will concentrate on studying before the exam, only nine exam districts will be set up)
    Chongqing, Wanxian, Nanchong, Chengdu, Luzhou, Qingmuguan, Ya'an, Guiyang, Kunming.

    Northwest Region: (Three) (Senior high school graduates will concentrate on studying before the exam, only three exam districts will be set up)
    Xi'an, Lanzhou, Dihua.

### 全国高等学校招生委员会决定全国设七十八个考区

1952-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    全国高等学校招生委员会决定全国设七十八个考区
    【新华社十五日讯】为了便利今年暑假高中毕业学生投考高等学校，全国高等学校招生委员会决定在全国设置七十八个考区，各区青年可就近报考全国高等学校。考区如下：
    华北区：（十个）
    北京、天津、保定、唐山、昌黎、涿县、太原、运城、张家口、归绥。
    东北区：（十个）
    沈阳、长春、哈尔滨、大连、齐齐哈尔、锦州、牡丹江、通化、延吉、佳木斯。
    华东区：（二十四个）
    上海、南京、青岛、济南、徐州、泰安、福州、漳州、南平、芜湖、蚌埠、合肥、安庆、扬州、南通、淮阴、盐城、泰州、金华、温州、杭州、宁波、无锡、苏州。
    中南区：（二十二个）
    武汉、襄阳、沙市、长沙、衡阳、常德、零陵、南昌、上饶、赣州、吉安、开封、南阳、洛阳、广州、海口、汕头、梅县、桂林、柳州、南宁、梧州。
    西南区：（九个）（在考前高中毕业生集中学习，只设九个考区）
    重庆、万县、南充、成都、泸州、青木关、雅安、贵阳、昆明。
    西北区：（三个）（在考前高中毕业生集中学习，只设三个考区）西安、兰州、迪化。
