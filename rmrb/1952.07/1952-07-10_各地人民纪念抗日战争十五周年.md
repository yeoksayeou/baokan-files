Title: People Across the Country Commemorate the 15th Anniversary of the War of Resistance Against Japan
Author:
Date: 1952-07-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th. On the 15th anniversary of the "July 7th" Anti-Japanese War, people all over the country held commemorative activities with heavy and indignant feelings. They expressed their firm opposition to the shameless conspiracy of the United States to revive Japanese militarism.

    Representatives from all walks of life in Shanghai held a forum on the eve of "July 7th". At the meeting, Li Chunqing, Jin Zhonghua, and Liu Simu first gave reports on opposing the revival of Japanese militarism by the United States and defending peace in the Far East and the world. In his report, Jin Zhonghua pointed out that American imperialism is attempting to revive Japanese militarism to serve as its accomplice in the aggression against Asia. American imperialism has embarked on the old path of failure of Japanese imperialism. More than 20 representatives of workers, peasants, local organizations of democratic parties, and medical circles spoke or submitted written opinions at the meeting. They unanimously expressed that they must do a good job in production and take practical actions to strengthen the resistance against the United States and aid Korea, and support the Japanese people's national liberation struggle to shatter the aggressive conspiracy of American imperialism!

    People from all walks of life in Wuhan, Changsha, Kaifeng, Nanchang, Nanning and other cities held small-scale forums. People in various places generally recalled the brutal crimes of the Japanese invaders in killing our people during the War of Resistance Against Japan, and the heroic struggle of our people against the enemy. At the forums held by the Wuhan Municipal People's Congress Consultative Committee and the Federation of Industry and Commerce, attendees also demanded public trial and severe punishment of Japanese war criminals.

    Responsible persons of various people's organizations and local organizations of democratic parties in Guangzhou delivered speeches on the 7th to commemorate the 15th anniversary of the "July 7th" Anti-Japanese War. Lin Qiangyun, director of the preparatory committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, said: "The working class in Guangdong Province made great contributions in the War of Resistance Against Japan. Today, as we commemorate the 15th anniversary of the 'July 7th' War of Resistance, we must carry forward the glorious tradition of the past, strive to carry out the movement to increase production and practice economy, continue to vigorously support the Chinese and Korean troops, and smash the aggressive conspiracy of American imperialism!" Li Zhangda, Director of the Southern General Branch of the China Democratic League, Chen Rutang, Director of the South China Provisional Working Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, as well as well-known figures from industrial and commercial circles and religious circles, unanimously stated that they would never tolerate the revival of Japanese militarism and were determined to vigorously launch the movement to increase production and practice economy, strengthen the strength of resistance against the United States and aid Korea, and defend peace in Asia and the world.

    At the radio forum held by representatives of all walks of life in Chongqing to commemorate the 15th anniversary of the "July 7th" War of Resistance, Ren Baige, chairman of the Chongqing branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Federation, said: American imperialism is now replacing Japanese imperialism and becoming the irreconcilable enemy of the Chinese people and the Asian people. It is attempting to revive Japanese militarism; but its conspiracy will be shattered by the iron fists of the Chinese people. Chu Tunan, chairman of the Southwest General Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Federation, said: The Chinese people are determined to unite with the people of the Far East and Asia, take on the cause of maintaining peace, and stop the American conspiracy of aggression in the Far East and Asia. People from all walks of life in Kunming, Guiyang, Nanchong and other places in the Southwest also held commemorative meetings.

    More than 1,000 representatives from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the Northwest held a grand meeting in Xi'an on the evening of the 7th to commemorate the 15th anniversary of the "July 7th" Anti-Japanese War. Yang Mingxuan, chairman of the Northwest General Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Federation, said in his speech: When commemorating this festival, the people of all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the Northwest must expose the conspiracy of American imperialism to continue armed aggression against China and Asia. He called on people to take practical actions to widely launch the movement to increase production and practice economy to combat American imperialism. Mu Huaxi, a member of the Northwest Ethnic Affairs Commission, and Luosang, director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Xi'an, unanimously stated in their speeches: The people of all ethnic groups in the Northwest cannot tolerate the behavior of American imperialism in reviving Japanese militarism and expanding the war. We must unite more closely around the Chinese Communist Party and contribute all our strength to strengthen the resistance against the United States and aid Korea. Xiong Kejian, a labor model from a clothing factory, reported that in the past month, the tailoring group alone saved the country more than 500 million yuan in the task of sewing winter clothes. He said that he would continue to increase production and practice economy in the future to create more wealth for the motherland. More than 1,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Gansu Province and Lanzhou City also gathered on the 7th to commemorate the event.

### 各地人民纪念抗日战争十五周年

1952-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    各地人民纪念抗日战争十五周年
    【新华社八日讯】在“七七”抗日战争十五周年纪念日，各地人民都怀着沉痛和愤慨的心情举行了纪念活动。他们表示坚决反对美国重新复活日本军国主义的无耻阴谋。
    上海各界代表在“七七”前夕举行了座谈会。会上，首由李纯青、金仲华、刘思慕分别就反对美国复活日本军国主义、保卫远东及世界和平问题作了报告。金仲华在报告中指出，美帝国主义又在企图复活日本军国主义，作为它侵略亚洲的帮凶。美帝国主义已经走上了日本帝国主义失败的老路了。工人、农民、各民主党派地方组织和医务界代表二十多人相继在会上发言或提出书面意见。他们一致表示要搞好生产，以实际行动来加强抗美援朝，并声援日本人民的民族解放斗争，粉碎美帝国主义的侵略阴谋！
    武汉、长沙、开封、南昌、南宁等市各界人民都分别举行了小型座谈会。各地人民在会上普遍回忆了在抗日战争中日本侵略者杀害我国人民的惨暴的罪行，和我国人民对敌人的英勇斗争。在武汉市各界人民代表会议协商委员会和工商业联合会分别召开的座谈会上，到会者并要求公审和严惩日本战犯。
    广州市各人民团体及各民主党派地方组织的负责人在七日分别发表谈话，纪念“七七”抗日战争十五周年。广东省总工会筹备会主任林锵云说：“广东全省的工人阶级，过去在抗日战争中，曾经有过很大的贡献。今天，我们纪念‘七七’抗战十五周年，一定要发扬过去的光荣传统，努力开展增产节约运动，继续大力支援中朝部队，粉碎美帝国主义的侵略阴谋！”中国民主同盟南方总支部主任委员李章达、中国国民党革命委员会华南临时工作委员会主任委员陈汝棠以及工商界、宗教界著名人士都一致表示绝对不能容忍日本军国主义复活，决心大力展开增产节约运动，增强抗美援朝力量，保卫亚洲和世界和平。
    在重庆市各界人民代表举行的纪念“七七”抗战十五周年广播座谈会上，重庆市抗美援朝分会主席任白戈说：美帝国主义现在正代替了日本帝国主义，成为中国人民和亚洲人民不共戴天的仇敌，它企图复活日本军国主义；但是它的阴谋会在中国人民的铁拳下碰得粉碎的。西南抗美援朝总分会主席楚图南说：中国人民坚决与远东及亚洲人民团结一致，把维护和平的事业担当起来，制止美国在远东和亚洲的侵略阴谋。西南区昆明、贵阳、南充等地各界人民也都举行了纪念会。
    西北各族各界代表一千多人七日晚在西安市举行“七七”抗日战争十五周年纪念大会。西北抗美援朝总分会主席杨明轩在发言中说：西北各族各界人民在纪念这一节日时，必须揭发美帝国主义妄想继续武装侵略中国、侵略亚洲的阴谋。他号召用广泛开展增产节约运动的实际行动来打击美帝国主义。西北民族事务委员会委员穆华希、班禅驻西安办事处处长罗桑在发言中一致表示：西北各族人民不能容忍美帝国主义复活日本军国主义扩大战争的行为，我们要更紧密地团结在中国共产党的周围，贡献一切力量加强抗美援朝。被服厂劳动模范熊克剑报告该厂最近一个月来在缝制冬装任务中，光剪裁组就给祖国节省了五亿多元，他表示今后要继续增产节约，为祖国创造更多财富。甘肃省、兰州市各族各界一千多人也在七日集会纪念。
