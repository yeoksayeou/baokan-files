Title: Members of the Korean People's Delegation to China Express Their Impressions of China
Author:
Date: 1952-07-10
Page: 4
　　
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang] After returning home, members of the Korean People's Delegation to China successively gave talks, speeches, or articles on their impressions of China.

    Delegate Jiang Zhongxi (transliteration), a worker, gave a talk to the press. He vividly described the scene of the Chinese people warmly welcoming their Korean comrades. He said: We saw solemn construction and a thriving material and cultural life in New China. The rapid development and increasing strength of New China have strengthened our victorious belief and our determination to create glorious labor achievements.

    Delegate Li Jiyin (transliteration), a Republic Hero, published an article entitled "Five Hundred Million People Are on Our Side," saying: The 500 million Chinese people under the banner of Mao Zedong have been mobilized as one. They have launched a vigorous movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea and a struggle to increase production, sincerely supporting the Korean people. Although we do not speak the same language, our hatred of the U.S. invading army and our determination to defend lasting world peace are exactly the same.

    Delegate Zhu Changjun (transliteration) gave a report to entrepreneurs, merchants, and religious figures in the Pyongyang area on the 4th. He praised the great struggle of the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao to build a new China and the great achievements of the movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea. He said: The Chinese people, with the brilliant achievements of the production increase and austerity struggle and the "Three-Anti" and "Five-Anti" movements, have further strengthened the internationalist movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea. The great victory of the Chinese people has infinitely inspired the fighting will of the Korean people. The assistance of the Chinese people is a guarantee for the Korean people to achieve greater victories.

    Delegate Jin Changjun said in a talk: After visiting China, I am more full of confidence in victory in the war against U.S. aggression. Because I deeply realize that the 500 million Chinese people are standing closely with us. They are continuing to strongly support our war of resistance against aggression with various practical actions.

    Delegate Kim Doo-chil (transliteration), a Republic Hero, said: The Chinese people, who love labor, democracy, and peace, all love the heroic Korean people. The kind concern of the Chinese people for us makes us deeply grateful and strengthens our will to annihilate the enemy.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团团员发表访华观感

1952-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团团员发表访华观感
    【新华社平壤讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团团员返国后相继就自己的访华观感发表谈话、演说或文章。
    代表团团员、工人姜仲锡（译音）向新闻界发表谈话。他生动地介绍了中国人民热烈欢迎朝鲜战友的情形。他说：我们在新中国看到了庄严的建设和欣欣向荣的物质文化生活。新中国的飞跃发展和日益强大，使我们的胜利信念和创造光辉的劳动功勋的决心更加坚强了。
    代表团团员、共和国英雄李基殷（译音）发表题为“五万万人民在我们这一边”的文章说：中国五万万人民在毛泽东旗帜下已经像一个人似地动员起来了。他们展开了轰轰烈烈的抗美援朝运动与增产斗争，诚心诚意地支援着朝鲜人民。虽然言语不通，但是，我们憎恨美国侵略军和坚决保卫世界持久和平的心情是完全一样的。
    代表团团员朱昌俊（译音）四日向平壤地区的企业家、商人与宗教界人士作报告。他盛赞中国人民在毛主席领导下为建设新中国而进行着的伟大斗争和抗美援朝运动的巨大成就。他说：中国人民以增产节约斗争与“三反”“五反”运动的光辉成绩，进一步加强了国际主义的抗美援朝运动。中国人民的伟大胜利无限地鼓舞了朝鲜人民的斗争意志。中国人民的援助是朝鲜人民取得更大胜利的保证。
    代表团团员金昌俊发表谈话说：访问中国后，我对于抗美战争更加充满了胜利信心。因为，我深深体会到中国五万万人民是和我们亲密地站在一起的。他们正以各种实际行动，继续大力支援着我们的反侵略战争。
    代表团团员、共和国英雄金斗七（译音）说：爱劳动、爱民主、爱和平的中国人民都热爱英雄的朝鲜人民。中国人民对我们的亲切关怀，使我们深为感激，使我们歼敌的意志更为坚强。
