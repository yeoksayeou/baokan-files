Title: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Learning Committee Holds its First Report Meeting; Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai Delivers a Report on Studying the Common Program

Author:
Date: 1952-07-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st - The Learning Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held its first report meeting on studying the Common Program on June 30th. At the meeting, Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai delivered a report. In his report, Vice Chairman Zhou elaborated in detail on the preamble and general program of the Common Program and its relevant basic principles, in two parts: the great victory of the Chinese people and the struggle for China's independence, democracy, peace, unity, and prosperity. More than 1,700 people attended the report meeting, including members of the CPPCC National Committee in Beijing, heads of central committees of various democratic parties and people's organizations, heads of commissions, ministries, committees, academies, administrations, and banks of the Central People's Government, as well as representatives attending and observing the preparatory representative meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce and the enlarged meeting of the Second General Meeting of the China Democratic National Construction Association in Beijing.

### 人民政协全国委员会学习委员会举行首次报告会  周恩来副主席作关于学习共同纲领的报告

1952-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会学习委员会举行首次报告会
    周恩来副主席作关于学习共同纲领的报告
    【新华社一日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会学习委员会，在六月三十日举行学习人民政协共同纲领的首次报告会。会上，周恩来副主席作了报告。周副主席在报告中，就中国人民的伟大胜利和为中国的独立、民主、和平、统一和富强而奋斗两个部分，详尽地阐释了共同纲领的绪言和总纲及其有关的各项基本原则。参加这一报告会的有：人民政协全国委员会在京委员，各民主党派中央和人民团体的负责人，中央人民政府各委、部、会、院、署、行首长，以及正在北京参加全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议和民主建国会第二次总会扩大会议的出席、列席代表等共一千七百多人。
