Title: China and Hungary Complete This Year's Trade Negotiations and Sign Protocol
Author:
Date: 1952-07-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 25th:

    The trade delegations of the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Hungary to the trade negotiations with China completed the 1952 trade negotiations in an atmosphere of mutual understanding, sincerity, and friendship, and on July 21st in Budapest, they signed the Protocol between China and Hungary on the Extension and Amendment of the 1951 Agreement on the Exchange of Goods and Payments between the two countries. The signatory on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China was Fang Fusheng, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Hungary; the signatory on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of Hungary was János Csaár, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of Hungary. Participating in the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were all members of the trade delegation of the People's Republic of China and diplomatic officials of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Hungary; on the Hungarian side were all members of the trade delegation of the People's Republic of Hungary to China and representatives of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Hungary. The signing of the trade protocol between China and Hungary will further strengthen the trade relations between the two countries.

### 中匈两国完成今年贸易谈判并签订议定书

1952-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈两国完成今年贸易谈判并签订议定书
    【新华社二十五日讯】中华人民共和国商务代表团与匈牙利人民共和国对华贸易谈判代表团在互相谅解和诚恳友好的气氛中完成了一九五二年的贸易谈判，并已于七月二十一日在布达佩斯签订了中匈关于延长并修正一九五一年两国间货物交换及付款协定之议定书。代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府签字者为中华人民共和国驻匈牙利大使馆临时代办方复生；代表匈牙利人民共和国政府签字者为匈牙利人民共和国对外贸易部副部长亚诺什·恰沙尔。参加签字仪式者：中国方面有中华人民共和国商务代表团全体团员及中华人民共和国驻匈牙利大使馆外交官员；匈牙利方面有匈牙利人民共和国对华贸易谈判代表团全体团员及匈牙利人民共和国外交部代表。中匈两国贸易议定书的签订，将进一步加强中匈两国间的贸易关系。
