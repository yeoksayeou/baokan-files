Title: Italian Agricultural Workers Hold General Strike
Author:
Date: 1952-07-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rome: According to TASS, more than two million agricultural workers across Italy held a 24-hour general strike on the 29th, demanding increased family allowances.

    After Italian agricultural workers demanded increased family allowances, the Italian Landowners' League refused to meet this legitimate demand. Representatives of the Italian General Confederation of Labor, the Italian Confederation of Labor Unions of the Working People, and the Italian Labor Alliance, disregarding the interference of Labor Minister Rubinacci, called on agricultural workers to hold a general strike on July 28.

    Recently, the struggle of workers in Italian industrial and commercial enterprises to defend their right to live has achieved some victories because of their unity in the struggle.

### 意大利农业工人举行总罢工

1952-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利农业工人举行总罢工
    【新华社三十日讯】据塔斯社罗马讯：意大利全国各地二百多万农业工人在二十九日举行了一次二十四小时的总罢工，要求增加家庭津贴。
    在意大利农业工人提出增加家庭津贴的要求后，意大利地主联盟拒绝满足这个正当要求。意大利总工会、意大利劳动人民工会联合会和意大利劳工联盟的代表们就不顾劳工部长鲁宾纳齐的干涉，在七月二十八日号召农业工人举行总罢工。
    最近，意大利工、商企业中的工人们保卫生活权利的斗争已经获得了若干胜利，因为他们在斗争中团结一致。
