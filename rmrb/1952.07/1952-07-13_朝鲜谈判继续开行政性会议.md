Title: Korean Negotiations Continue with Administrative Meetings
Author:
Date: 1952-07-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 12th. The plenary session of both delegations to the Korean armistice negotiations continued its administrative meetings on the 12th to discuss the repatriation of prisoners of war.

### 朝鲜谈判继续开行政性会议

1952-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判继续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城十二日电】十二日朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团大会继续进行行政性会议，讨论战俘遣返问题。
