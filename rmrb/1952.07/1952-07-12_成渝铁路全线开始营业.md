Title: Entire Chengdu-Chongqing Railway Begins Operation
Author:
Date: 1952-07-12
Page: 2

    Entire Chengdu-Chongqing Railway Begins Operation

    The entire Chengdu-Chongqing Railway has officially commenced operations on July 6th, issuing all kinds of passenger tickets and handling all types of freight transport. Currently, Chengdu Station and the major stations along the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway are piled high with goods prepared for shipment. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 成渝铁路全线开始营业

1952-07-12
第2版()
专栏：

    成渝铁路全线开始营业
    成渝铁路全线已自七月六日起正式开始营业，发售各种客运票和办理各种货物运输。目前，成都车站和成渝铁路沿线各大车站，都堆满了整装待运的货物。（新华社）
