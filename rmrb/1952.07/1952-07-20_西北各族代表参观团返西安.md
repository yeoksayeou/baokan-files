Title: Northwest Ethnic Groups Delegation Returns to Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1952-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, July 18th: The Northwest Ethnic Groups Delegation, consisting of 86 members and led by the head of the delegation, Zhang Leshan (Hui nationality), returned to Xi'an by train on the evening of the 13th of this month. Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the Northwest Military and Administrative Committee, invited the representatives to a forum on the afternoon of the 16th. During the meeting, the representatives spoke enthusiastically, excitedly describing the great achievements of the motherland in politics, economy, and culture that they had witnessed, as well as the correct implementation of the Common Program's ethnic policies and the policy of freedom of religious belief by local people's governments. They expressed their intention to widely publicize these situations to the people of all ethnic groups.

The Northwest Ethnic Groups Delegation included representatives from ten ethnic groups. They departed from Xi'an on May 23rd and, over the course of more than 50 days, visited factories, schools, and scenic spots in Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places. Representatives of the Hui, Uyghur, Tibetan, Mongolian, and other ethnic groups also visited the Yonghe Temple, the Panchen Lama's Pagoda Courtyard in Beijing, and mosques and Buddhist temples in various places. They will return to their respective ethnic regions in two or three days to deliver reports to the people of all ethnic groups.

### 西北各族代表参观团返西安

1952-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    西北各族代表参观团返西安
    【新华社西安十八日电】西北各民族代表参观团一行八十六人，在团长张乐山（回族）率领下，已在本月十三日晚乘火车返抵西安。西北军政委员会副主席张治中在十六日下午邀请代表们举行座谈会。会上，代表们踊跃发言，兴奋地叙述他们亲眼看到的祖国在政治、经济、文化各个方面的伟大成就，各地人民政府正确贯彻共同纲领民族政策和宗教信仰自由政策的情形，他们表示要把这些情形广泛地向各族人民宣传。
    西北各民族代表参观团，共包括十个民族的代表，五月二十三日从西安出发，在五十多天的时间内，曾参观了北京、天津、上海、杭州等地的工厂、学校和名胜古迹；回、维吾尔、藏、蒙等族的代表还分别参观了北京的雍和宫、班禅塔院和各地的清真寺、佛教寺院。他们在二、三日内即将分别返回本民族地区，向各族人民作传达报告。
