Title: Tangshan Huaxin Textile Factory: Self-Criticism Regarding Neglecting to Summarize Advanced Experiences
Author:
Date: 1952-07-21
Page: 2

Response to readers' criticisms and suggestions in the People's Daily

Tangshan Huaxin Textile Factory

Self-Criticism Regarding Neglecting to Summarize Advanced Experiences

To the Editor: Zhang Zhenhe's criticism published in the People's Daily on May 19th regarding the leadership of the Tangshan Huaxin Textile Factory (a joint state-private enterprise) not valuing the summarization of advanced experiences is correct. We sincerely accept it and express our gratitude to Comrade Zhang Zhenhe for his enthusiastic assistance.

We did not have a sufficient understanding of the role that advanced work methods play in the country's production and construction work, and therefore we did not devote enough effort to summarizing and promoting advanced experiences in our leadership work. As a result of this criticism, we have instructed the planning department, the engineer's office, and workshop cadres to begin studying and organizing existing advanced work methods, to summarize those that have not been summarized, and to supplement those that have not been summarized thoroughly. In addition, we plan to establish a specialized organization during the increase production and practice economy movement to be responsible for summarizing and promoting advanced experiences, mobilizing the masses to come up with ideas and find shortcuts, create new working methods, and victoriously carry out the increase production and practice economy movement.

Tangshan Huaxin Textile Co., Ltd. Factory

### 唐山华新纺织厂  关于不重视总结先进经验的检讨

1952-07-21
第2版()
专栏：对人民日报读者批评建议的反应

    唐山华新纺织厂
    关于不重视总结先进经验的检讨
    编辑同志：五月十九日人民日报刊载的张振和同志对唐山华新纺织厂（公私合营）领导方面不重视总结先进经验的批评是正确的，我们诚恳地接受，并对张振和同志的热情帮助表示感谢。
    对于先进工作法在祖国生产建设工作中所起的作用，我们是认识不够的，因而在我们领导的工作中也就没有拿出足够的力量来总结和推广先进经验。由于这次的批评，我们已责成计划科、工程师室及车间干部着手对已有的先进工作法进行研究整理，把没有总结的，总结出来；总结得不充实的，加以补充。此外，我们还准备在增产节约运动中，组织专门机构，负责总结及推广先进经验，发动群众想办法，找窍门，创造新的工作方法，胜利地开展增产节约运动。
    唐山华新纺织股份有限公司工厂
