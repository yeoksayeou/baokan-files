Title: Australian Trade Union Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1952-07-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 10th: The head of the Australian Trade Union Delegation, Mr. Wright, and delegate Mr. Collens, who were invited to China to attend the May Day celebrations, left Guangzhou this morning to return home. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Lin Qiangyun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, and dozens of other trade union representatives.

### 澳大利亚工会代表团返国

1952-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚工会代表团返国
    【新华社广州十日电】应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼的澳大利亚工会代表团团长莱特、团员柯兰西，在十日晨离广州回国。前往车站欢送的有广东省总工会筹备会主任林锵云等工会代表数十人。
