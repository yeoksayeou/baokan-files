Title: Korean Central News Agency Reports on the US Aircraft Indiscriminate Bombing of Pyongyang Suburbs

Author: 
Date: 1952-07-19
Page: 1

    Korean Central News Agency
    Reports on the US Aircraft Indiscriminate Bombing of Pyongyang Suburbs
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 18th] TASS, Pyongyang, July 18th: The Korean Central News Agency reported on the situation of US aircraft bombing the suburbs of Pyongyang on July 11th, saying: On the 11th, day and night, American planes not only barbarously bombed and strafed the center of Pyongyang, but also bombed and strafed the suburbs of Pyongyang. American pilots not only cruelly murdered farmers in the fields, but also dropped napalm bombs on their houses. US aircraft dropped about 300 bombs on the village of Songhyon-ri (transliteration); 235 houses were destroyed, and 142 residents were killed, including many pregnant women and elderly people. Jiseong-in Village (transliteration) was also brutally bombed. Bombs flattened 50 houses in the village, killing many civilians and destroying a lot of grain. US aircraft dropped a large number of napalm bombs on many villages near Pyongyang and indiscriminately strafed them.
    The People's Government provided a great deal of assistance to the refugees who were barbarously bombed by US aircraft. The People's Committee distributed food and clothing to them.

### 朝鲜中央通讯社  报道美机滥炸平壤市郊情况

1952-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜中央通讯社
    报道美机滥炸平壤市郊情况
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社平壤十八日讯：朝鲜中央通讯社报道美机于七月十一日轰炸平壤市郊的情形说：十一日白天和晚上，美国飞机不但野蛮地轰炸和扫射平壤市中心，而且还轰炸及扫射平壤市郊。美国驾驶员不但残忍地杀害田野间的农民，而且还把凝固汽油弹投向他们的屋舍。美国飞机对圣贤里（译音）村约投掷了三百枚炸弹；有二百三十五所房子被炸毁，一百四十二个居民被炸死，其中有许多怀孕的妇女和老年人。积善人村（译音）也遭到残酷的轰炸。炸弹把这所村庄内的五十所房子炸平了，炸死了许多平民，还炸毁了不少粮食。美国飞机对平壤附近的许多村庄投掷了大量凝固汽油弹，并滥肆扫射。
    人民政府对遭到美国飞机野蛮轰炸的难民给予大量援助。人民委员会将粮食和衣服分发给他们。
