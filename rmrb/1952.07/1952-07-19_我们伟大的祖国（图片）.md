    Our Great Motherland
    Dalian Chemical Plant has formulated a plan to increase production and save 181,000 tons of grain throughout the year. A production competition is vigorously underway in all units of the plant. This is a scene of the plant's fire-extinguishing train transporting freshly produced coke for cooling.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大连化学工厂制订了全年增产节约十八万一千余吨粮食的计划，全厂各单位的生产竞赛正在热烈进行。这是该厂的消火车正把刚出炉的焦炭送去冷却的情形。
