Title: CPPCC National Committee Member Rong Desheng Passes Away in Wuxi
Author:
Date: 1952-07-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 30th: Rong Desheng, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, member of the East China Military and Political Committee, member of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Deputy Director of the Southern Jiangsu People's Administrative Office, and General Manager of Wuxi Shenxin Textile Factory, passed away in Wuxi City on the morning of July 29th at 7:00 AM due to purpura. The State Council of the Central People's Government has sent a telegram of condolence. The East China Military and Political Committee has decided to form a funeral committee composed of Zeng Shan, Leng Yuqiu, Guan Wenwei, Liu Xiansheng, and Qian Sunqing to hold a public memorial service at a fixed time.

### 人民政协全国委员会委员  荣德生在无锡逝世

1952-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会委员
    荣德生在无锡逝世
    【新华社三十日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员、华东军政委员会委员、中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会委员、苏南人民行政公署副主任、无锡申新纱厂总经理荣德生患紫斑症，于七月二十九日上午七时在无锡市逝世。中央人民政府政务院已致电吊唁。华东军政委员会已决定由曾山、冷御秋、管文蔚、刘先胜、钱孙卿等组织治丧委员会，定期举行公祭。
