Title: Zhang Xiruo Sends Condolences on the Death of Pekkala
Author:
Date: 1952-07-05
Page: 4

    Zhang Xiruo Sends Condolences on the Death of Pekkala

    [Xinhua News Agency, 4th] Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, sent a telegram to the Finnish-Chinese Association, expressing condolences on the death of the association's chairman, Pekkala. The telegram reads: "To Mrs. Lauri Ketonen, Secretary of the Finnish-Chinese Association: I am shocked to hear of the passing of Mr. Pekkala, Chairman of your association. Please accept my deepest condolences to your association and to Mr. Pekkala's family. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs."

    [Xinhua News Agency, 3rd] Helsinki News: Pekkala, a prominent Finnish public figure and chairman of the Finnish-Chinese Association, passed away in Helsinki on the evening of June 30th due to a stroke. Pekkala was one of the leaders of the Finnish People's Democratic League and is currently a Member of Parliament. He served as Prime Minister of the Finnish Cabinet led by the People's Democratic League from 1945 to 1948.

### 张奚若电唁皮可拉逝世

1952-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

    张奚若电唁皮可拉逝世
    【新华社四日讯】中国人民外交学会会长张奚若致电芬中协会，吊唁该会主席皮可拉之丧。电文如下：“芬中协会秘书劳利开能夫人：惊悉贵会主席皮可拉先生逝世，谨向贵会及皮可拉先生之家属致深切哀悼。中国人民外交学会会长张奚若”
    【新华社三日讯】赫尔辛基消息：芬兰著名公众领袖、芬中协会主席皮可拉于六月三十日晚在赫尔辛基因中风逝世。皮可拉是芬兰人民民主联盟领袖之一，现为议会议员，曾于一九四五年至一九四八年担任由人民民主联盟领导的芬兰内阁总理。
