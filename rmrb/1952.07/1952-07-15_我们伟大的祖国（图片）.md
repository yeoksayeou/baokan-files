    Our Great Motherland
    Various canned foods manufactured by China's food industry sector are being supplied to people all over the country in large quantities. This is one of China's larger-scale modern food factories – Shanghai Public Yimin Food Factory No. 1. This machine is an automatic transportation soybean washing machine for manufacturing milk substitute powder.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国食品工业部门所制造的各种罐头，正大量地供应全国人民。这是我国规模较大的现代化食品工厂之一——上海公营益民食品第一厂。这一部机器是制造代乳粉的自动运输洗豆机。
