Title: Japanese People Grandly Celebrate the 30th Anniversary of the Japanese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1952-07-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th: According to TASS, July 15th marked the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Japanese Communist Party. On this day, the "Akahata" (Red Flag) newspaper published a commemorative issue. "Akahata" reported that the Japanese Communist Party had received congratulatory telegrams from fraternal Communist and Workers' parties in China, the United Kingdom, New Zealand, India, Canada, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, Belgium, and Cyprus.

"Akahata" reported that on July 14th, grand rallies were held in twenty locations across Japan to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Japanese Communist Party. Police attempted to disperse the rallies and make arrests.

On the 15th, approximately 160 more celebratory rallies were held across Japan. Police everywhere arrested participants in the rallies. According to an announcement on the 16th by the Japanese National Police Headquarters, a total of 116 people were arrested nationwide.

The newspaper "Laborer," which resumed publication on July 15th, published a congratulatory telegram from the All-Japan Federation of Industrial Trade Unions to the Japanese Communist Party. The telegram emphasized that the Japanese Communist Party has pointed out a path for the Japanese nation.

"Akahata" described the preparations for a large peace festival being held for this anniversary. The Preparatory Committee is composed of trade union leaders, outstanding scientists, literary and artistic workers, and representatives from nearly thirty trade unions and democratic organizations.

Many mass organizations will hold exhibitions for the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Japanese Communist Party.

### 日本人民盛大庆祝日共三十周年

1952-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    日本人民盛大庆祝日共三十周年
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社东京讯：七月十五日是日本共产党成立三十周年纪念日，在这一天“赤旗报”出版了纪念刊。“赤旗报”报道，日本共产党接到了中国、英国、新西兰、印度、加拿大、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、比利时和塞浦路斯各国的兄弟共产党和工人党的贺电。
    “赤旗报”报道，七月十四日，日本全国二十个地方举行了盛大的集会，庆祝日本共产党成立三十周年，警察曾企图驱散集会并进行逮捕。
    十五日，在日本全国各地又约有一百六十多处举行庆祝集会。警察到处逮捕参加集会的群众。据日本国家警察本部十六日宣布，全国各地共逮捕了一百一十六人。
    在七月十五日复刊的报纸“劳动者”发表了全日本产业别工会会议致日本共产党的贺电。贺电中强调指出，日本共产党向日本民族指出了一条道路。
        “赤旗报”叙述了为这个纪念日举行的大和平节的筹备情况。筹备委员会是由工会领袖、杰出的科学家、文学艺术工作者和差不多三十个工会和民主团体的代表组成的。
    许多群众团体将为日本共产党成立三十周年举行展览会。
