Title: International Olympic Games Opening: Soviet Union, Hungary, Poland, and Sixteen Other Countries Obtain Football Tournament Qualification
Author:
Date: 1952-07-21
Page: 4

    International Olympic Games Opening
    Soviet Union, Hungary, Poland, and Sixteen Other Countries Obtain Football Tournament Qualification
    (Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 20th) The 15th International Olympic Games opened on the afternoon of the 19th at the Helsinki Central Stadium in the Finnish capital. Although it rained that day, the stands were packed with more than 70,000 spectators. The President of the Republic of Finland, Paasikivi, also attended the opening ceremony.
    Before the opening of the Games, athletes from the 66 participating countries paraded around the stadium. Traditionally, the Greek athletes led the procession, followed by athletes from other countries in alphabetical order. The Soviet team was led by the famous weightlifter Kuzenko, carrying the Soviet flag, followed by female athletes in blue dresses and white skirts, then male athletes in white shirts and white trousers, and speedboat drivers in sailor suits. The audience applauded and cheered enthusiastically for these young, strong, and excellent athletes from socialist countries.
    The Chairman of the Finnish Olympic Games Organizing Committee, Forenke, delivered a welcoming speech at the opening ceremony. The President of the Republic of Finland, Paasikivi, then declared the Games open. At this moment, the Olympic Games horns sounded in the stadium, the Olympic Games flag was slowly raised, and salutes were fired. The famous Finnish track and field athlete Nurmi appeared in the stadium with the Olympic torch. Soon, the Olympic flame flickered in the stadium and on the stadium tower. After the choir and band played the Olympic Games anthem, representatives from various countries took an oath, guaranteeing their faithful adherence to the spirit of the Games.
    The opening ceremony concluded with the Finnish national anthem.
    (Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 20th) According to TASS, the football elimination matches held before the opening of the Olympic Games ended on the 16th. Sixteen national teams have qualified for the tournament: the Soviet Union, Hungary, Poland, Finland, Denmark, Brazil, Egypt, Italy, Luxembourg, Turkey, Sweden, the Antilles, West Germany, Yugoslavia, Norway, and Austria. The American football team suffered a crushing defeat in the elimination match, losing 0-8 to the Italian football team. Britain (the birthplace of football) was defeated 3-5 by Luxembourg. The American and British football teams have lost their qualification.

### 国际奥林匹克运动会开幕  苏匈波等十六国获得足球比赛权

1952-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

    国际奥林匹克运动会开幕
    苏匈波等十六国获得足球比赛权
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：第十五届国际奥林匹克运动会于十九日下午在芬兰首都赫尔辛基中央体育场开幕。这天虽然下雨，但是看台上还是挤满了七万多观众。芬兰共和国总统巴锡基维也出席了开幕仪式。
    运动会开幕前，由参加运动会的六十六个国家的选手举行绕场游行。队伍按照传统由希腊选手带头，其他国家的选手按字母次序排列。苏联选手队伍由著名举重家库兹森科打着苏联国旗领头，接着是穿着蓝衣白裙的女运动员，再后是穿着白衣白裤的男运动员和穿上水手服装的快艇驾驶员。全场观众都向这些年青健壮的社会主义国家的优秀运动员热烈鼓掌欢呼。
    芬兰奥林匹克运动会组织委员会主席佛伦凯在开幕式上致欢迎词。芬兰共和国总统巴锡基维接着宣布运动会开幕。这时，体育场上响起了奥林匹克运动会的号角声，奥林匹克运动会的会旗冉冉上升，礼炮齐鸣。芬兰著名径赛运动家诺米拿着奥林匹克火炬在体育场出现。不久，体育场和体育场高塔上也闪动着奥林匹克的熊熊火光。在合唱队和乐队演奏奥林匹克运动会会歌之后，各国代表就举行宣誓，保证忠实遵守比赛的精神。
    开幕式最后在芬兰国歌声中宣告结束。
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：奥林匹克运动会在开幕前举行的足球淘汰比赛已在十六日结束。十六国的球队获得了比赛权，它们是：苏联、匈牙利、波兰、芬兰、丹麦、巴西、埃及、意大利、卢森堡、土耳其、瑞典、安的列斯群岛、西德、南斯拉夫、挪威和奥地利。美国足球队在淘汰比赛中遭到惨败，以零对八输给意大利足球队，英国（足球发源地）以三比五被卢森堡击败，美英两国足球队已失去比赛权。
