Title: Burmese Overseas Chinese Sightseeing Group Returns to Burma
Author:
Date: 1952-07-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 18th. The Burmese Overseas Chinese Sightseeing Group, a party of thirty-three people, left Guangzhou in two groups on the 17th and 18th, passing through Hong Kong to return to Burma. Wu Zhizhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Committee of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Cai Chuyin, Deputy Director, Yi Meihou, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Overseas Chinese Association, and more than 100 representatives from Guangzhou's people's organizations, democratic parties, and returned Overseas Chinese went to the station to see them off.
　　Since arriving in Guangzhou from Fuzhou on the 12th, the sightseeing group attended a joint banquet held by the Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City Consultative Committees and local Guangzhou organizations of various democratic parties. They also visited the Overseas Chinese College of South China University, the Guangdong Overseas Chinese Middle School, and the reception center for returned distressed Overseas Chinese, where they were warmly welcomed by Overseas Chinese students and returned distressed Overseas Chinese.

### 缅甸华侨回国观光团返缅

1952-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸华侨回国观光团返缅
    【新华社广州十八日电】缅甸华侨回国观光团一行三十三人，在十七和十八日分两批离广州取道香港返缅甸。广东省人民政府华侨事务委员会主任委员伍治之、副主任委员蔡楚吟、广州市归国华侨联谊会筹备会主任委员蚁美厚及广州市各人民团体、民主党派和归国华侨代表共一百多人前往车站送行。
    观光团自十二日由福州抵达广州后，曾出席广东省和广州市协商委员会、各民主党派广州地方组织等机关和团体的联合欢宴，并参观了南方大学华侨学院、广东省华侨中学和归国难侨招待所，受到侨生和归国难侨的热烈欢迎。
