Title: Relief for People in East China Affected by Typhoon Disaster
Author:
Date: 1952-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 26th. The East China Military and Administrative Committee has allocated 6 billion yuan to provide relief to the people of Zhejiang and Fujian provinces who were affected by the recent tropical storm (i.e., typhoon). Zhejiang Province will receive 4 billion yuan, and Fujian Province will receive 2 billion yuan.

    The East China Military and Administrative Committee's Zhejiang Disaster Area慰问 Inspection Group, composed of the East China Production and Disaster Relief Committee and the East China Military and Administrative Committee's Ministry of Civil Affairs, Ministry of Water Resources, Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry, and Ministry of Health, departed on the 25th to慰问 the disaster-stricken people in the Ningbo, Taizhou, Wenzhou, and Jinhua special districts of Zhejiang Province, which were severely affected by the disaster, and to inspect the damage caused by the tropical storm.

    According to the instructions of the East China Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters, the disaster areas in Zhejiang are vigorously engaged in post-disaster recovery. The Zhejiang Provincial People's Government has allocated 7.5 million catties of grain to provide relief to the disaster-stricken people.

### 华东救济受台风灾害的人民

1952-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    华东救济受台风灾害的人民
    【新华社上海二十六日电】华东军政委员会已拨款六十亿元，救济浙江、福建两省在这次热带风暴（即台风）侵袭中受灾的人民。浙江省计四十亿元，福建省二十亿元。
    由华东生产救灾委员会和华东军政委员会民政部、水利部、农林部、卫生部等单位组成的华东军政委员会浙江灾区慰问视察组，已于二十五日出发，前往浙江省受灾较重的宁波、台州、温州、金华等专区慰问受灾人民，并视察热带风暴所造成的灾害的情况。
    根据华东防汛抗旱总指挥部的指示，浙江受灾地区已在大力从事善后，浙江省人民政府已拨出七百五十万斤粮食，救济受灾的人民。
