Title: Southwest Military and Political Committee Chairman Liu Bocheng Issues Order Commending Southwest Railway Bureau Employees for Completing Chengdu-Chongqing Railway Opening
Author:
Date: 1952-07-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 2nd. Chairman Liu Bocheng of the Southwest Military and Political Committee issued an order commending the employees of the Southwest Railway Engineering Bureau for completing the task of opening the entire Chengdu-Chongqing Railway to traffic ahead of schedule. At the same time, the Chongqing dockworkers who completed the transportation of Chengdu-Chongqing Railway materials ahead of schedule were also commended.

The employees of State-owned Factories 101 and 104 were rewarded by the Central Heavy Industry Department for completing the tasks of steel rail and accessories for the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway ahead of schedule. The two factories held grand award ceremonies before "July 1st", awarding the prize flags, certificates and bonuses awarded by the Central People's Government Heavy Industry Department, and reading out Minister He Changgong's congratulatory letter.

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 2nd. The opening of the entire Chengdu-Chongqing Railway has received congratulations from people all over the country. The Southwest Railway Engineering Bureau has received congratulatory telegrams and a large number of congratulatory banners from all over the country in recent days. On "July 1st", Chongqing newspapers published, with great fanfare, congratulatory inscriptions from Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government on the successful completion of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway. Chairman Mao's inscription was: "Congratulate the opening of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway, continue to work hard to build the Tiantian Railway." Vice Chairman Zhu De's congratulatory message was: "Celebrate the completion of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway, we must build the Tiantian Railway well, and connect the Sichuan-Guizhou-Yunnan-Guangxi-Hunan railways." The newspapers also published inscriptions by Zhang Lan, Guo Moruo, Liu Bocheng, Chairman of the Southwest Military and Political Committee, Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairman, and He Long, Commander of the Southwest Military Region. The newspapers also published special articles by Wu Yuzhang, Teng Daiyuan and others celebrating the opening of the entire Chengdu-Chongqing Railway. Heads of democratic parties in the Southwest Region and Chongqing also wrote articles to congratulate. Xiong Kewu, convener of the Sichuan-Kang Temporary Working Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, wrote that the fact that the entire Chengdu-Chongqing Railway is open to traffic indicates a truth to us: Only under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, the Chinese people can develop from the victory of liberation to the victory of construction. Chongqing Xinhua Daily, Dagong Daily, and Workers' Daily all published editorials commemorating "July 1st" and celebrating the opening of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway.

### 西南军政委员会刘伯承主席发布命令  嘉奖西南铁路局职工完成成渝路通车任务  中央重工业部奖励国营一零一、一零四两厂职工

1952-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    西南军政委员会刘伯承主席发布命令
    嘉奖西南铁路局职工完成成渝路通车任务
    中央重工业部奖励国营一零一、一零四两厂职工
    【新华社重庆二日电】西南军政委员会刘伯承主席发布命令，嘉奖西南铁路工程局职工提前完成成渝铁路全线通车任务。同时，并嘉奖了提前完成成渝铁路器材运输任务的重庆市海员工人。
    国营一零一、一零四两厂职工由于提前完成成渝铁路的钢轨和配件任务，受到中央人民政府重工业部的奖励。两厂在“七一”前夕，举行了隆重的授奖典礼，颁发了中央人民政府重工业部授予的奖旗、奖状和奖金，并宣读了何长工部长的贺信。
    【新华社重庆二日电】成渝铁路全线通车受到全国人民的祝贺。西南铁路工程局连日都收到来自全国各地的贺电和大批祝贺的锦旗。“七一”重庆各报都以巨大篇幅刊载了中央人民政府毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、周恩来总理对成渝铁路胜利完工的祝词题字。毛主席的题字是：“庆贺成渝铁路通车，继续努力修筑天成路”。朱德副主席的祝词是：“庆祝成渝铁路完工，一定要把天成铁路修好，并把川黔滇桂湘等铁路联系起来。”各报还刊载了张澜、郭沫若和西南军政委员会刘伯承主席、邓小平副主席、西南军区贺龙司令员等人的题词。各报并刊出吴玉章、滕代远等人庆祝成渝铁路全线通车的专文。西南区和重庆市各民主党派负责人也都撰文祝贺。中国国民党革命委员会川康临时工作委员会召集人熊克武著文说，成渝铁路全线通车这一事实指示了我们一条真理：只有在中国共产党和毛主席领导下，中国人民才可能从解放的胜利发展到建设的胜利。重庆新华日报、大公报、工人日报都发表了纪念“七一”并庆祝成渝铁路通车的社论。
