Title: Korean Armistice Talks Continue Recess for Two Days
Author:
Date: 1952-07-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 16th—The administrative meeting of the delegations of both sides of the Korean armistice talks continued its recess for two days on July 16th and 17th, and will resume at 11:00 a.m. on the 18th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判继续休会两日

1952-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判继续休会两日
    【新华社开城十六日电】朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团大会行政性会议于七月十六、十七继续休会两日，至十八日上午十一时复会。
