Title: Austrian Peace Council Issues Message to the People, Welcomes the World People's Peace Congress to be Held in Austria
Author:
Date: 1952-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 15th: According to TASS, the Executive Committee of the Austrian Peace Council met in Linz, with Father Kock presiding. World Peace Council member and Austrian writer Fischer reported on the resolutions of the World Peace Council's special session. He emphasized that one of the most important resolutions of this session was the decision to convene the World People's Peace Congress in Vienna on December 5th of this year. After discussing this resolution, the Executive Committee unanimously adopted a message to the Austrian people, stating: "The fact that the World People's Peace Congress will be held in Vienna encourages all peace-loving people in Austria. The task of Austrian peace fighters is to make the broad masses of the people aware of the purpose of this congress. We must intensify our efforts to enlist more people to join the ranks of the struggle for peace."

### 奥和平理事会发表告人民书  欢迎世界人民和平大会在奥举行

1952-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    奥和平理事会发表告人民书
    欢迎世界人民和平大会在奥举行
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社维也纳十五日讯：奥地利和平理事会执行委员会在林嗣开会，由考克神父担任主席。世界和平理事会理事、奥地利作家费希尔在会上就世界和平理事会特别会议的决议作了报告。他着重指出，这次会议最重要的决议之一，就是决定在今年十二月五日在维也纳召开世界人民和平大会。执行委员会在讨论了这个决议之后，一致通过告奥地利全国人民书说：世界人民和平大会在维也纳召开一事，鼓舞着奥地利全国爱好和平的人民。奥地利和平战士的任务就是使广大人民知道这次大会的目的。我们必须加紧努力争取更多的人民参加到争取和平的队伍里来。
