Title: Korean Negotiations Continue with Administrative Meetings
Author:
Date: 1952-07-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 5th. The plenary session of the delegation of both sides of the Korean armistice negotiations continued its administrative meeting on the 5th to discuss the issue of prisoner of war repatriation.

### 朝鲜谈判继续开行政性会议

1952-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判继续开行政性会议
   【新华社开城五日电】五日朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团大会继续进行行政性会议，讨论战俘遣返问题。
