Title: Korean Negotiations Continue with Administrative Meetings
Author:
Date: 1952-07-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 7th. The plenary session of both delegations to the Korean armistice negotiations continued its administrative meetings on the 7th to discuss the issue of prisoner of war repatriation.

### 朝鲜谈判继续开行政性会议

1952-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判继续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城七日电】七日朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团大会继续进行行政性会议，讨论战俘遣返问题。
