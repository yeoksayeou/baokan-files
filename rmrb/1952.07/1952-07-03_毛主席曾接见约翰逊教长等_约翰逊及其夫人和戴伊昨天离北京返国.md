Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Meets with Dean Johnson et al.; Johnson and his wife, and Day, left Beijing yesterday to return home.
Author:
Date: 1952-07-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, on the eve of Dean Johnson of Canterbury, England, returning to his country, received Dean Johnson and his wife, and Mr. Day, a lecturer in international affairs at Oxford University, England, on the afternoon of June 29th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd: Dean Johnson of Canterbury, England, his wife, and Mr. Day, a lecturer in international affairs at Oxford University, left Beijing by plane on the 2nd to return to their country. Chen Shutong, Zhang Xiruo, Xu Baoju, Tan Tiwu, Hu Zhongchi, Wu Maosun, and religious figures He Chengxiang, Wang Zizhong, Chen Wenrun, and many others went to the airport to see them off.

### 毛主席曾接见约翰逊教长等  约翰逊及其夫人和戴伊昨天离北京返国

1952-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席曾接见约翰逊教长等
    约翰逊及其夫人和戴伊昨天离北京返国
    【新华社二日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席在英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊先生离华返国前夕——六月二十九日下午，接见了约翰逊教长及其夫人，英国牛津大学国际问题讲师戴伊。在座的有中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理。
    【新华社二日讯】英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊及其夫人和牛津大学国际问题讲师戴伊，二日乘飞机离北京返国，到机场送行的有陈叔通、张奚若、许宝驹、谭惕吾、胡仲持、吴茂荪和宗教界人士何成湘、王梓仲、陈文润等多人。
