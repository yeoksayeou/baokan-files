Title: Eight American Planes Intruded into Andong, Indiscriminately Straffing
Author:
Date: 1952-07-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 12th - Eight American invading Korean Air Force F-86 jet fighters intruded into our Northeast airspace at 13:57 on July 11th and indiscriminately strafed Andong, wounding 47 of our residents and killing two. Our air force, in order to defend our country's inviolable airspace, took off to meet them and drove them back. The American planes fled towards Yongsan, Korea, via Dahushan and Changdianhekou at 14:05.

### 美机八架竟侵入安东滥施扫射

1952-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    美机八架竟侵入安东滥施扫射
    【新华社沈阳十二日电】美国侵朝空军F——86喷气式战斗机八架，于七月十一日下午一时五十七分，侵入我东北领空，在安东滥施扫射，我居民四十七人受伤，二人死亡。我空军为保卫我祖国不可侵犯的领空，曾起飞迎击，将其击退。该批美国飞机于下午二时零五分经大虎山、长甸河口，向朝鲜境内永山方向逃逸。
