Title: French Communist Party Central Committee Member Colombani Regains Freedom
Author:
Date: 1952-07-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 21st: According to TASS, Paris, July 21st: Colombani, member of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, member of the Administrative Committee of the French General Confederation of Labor, and secretary of the Rhône-Alpes Regional Union, has been temporarily released from prison. He was imprisoned in early July after being falsely accused of participating in the so-called "Toulon Communist conspiracy." "L'Humanité Dimanche" said: "Following the news of Sti's release, the patriots of Toulon and Marseille are delighted. They are determined to continue the struggle to expose the conspiracy instigated by Brunaud and Debrou."

### 法国共产党中央委员  哥伦比尼恢复自由

1952-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    法国共产党中央委员
    哥伦比尼恢复自由
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社巴黎二十一日讯：法国共产党中央委员会委员、法国总工会行政委员会委员和罗尼河口省工会联合会书记哥伦比尼已经暂时恢复自由，他因为被诬参加所谓“土伦共产党阴谋”而于七月上旬被捕下狱。“人道报星期日刊”说：“紧接着斯梯出狱的消息之后传来这个消息，土伦和马赛的爱国志士都感到高兴。他们决心继续进行斗争，揭发布吕纳和德勃拉所鼓动的阴谋。”
