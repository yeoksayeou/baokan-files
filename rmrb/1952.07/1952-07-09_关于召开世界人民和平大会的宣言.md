Declaration on Convening the World People's Peace Congress

The ongoing war in Korea, the use of large-scale weapons of mass destruction, the revival of German and Japanese militarism, and the violent means of destroying national independence have caused consternation among all people, even those who have not yet felt the danger of war. People in many countries are gradually realizing that, whether they like it or not, they are in danger of being dragged step by step into a full-scale war.

Millions of people are demanding the prohibition of weapons of mass destruction, the reduction of all armaments under strict supervision, and the conclusion of a peace pact. Within parliaments, trade unions, and political, social, and religious organizations, a new mood is developing that is conducive to maintaining peace. In order to reverse the direction of events and ensure peace, it is possible and necessary for all these forces to cooperate.

The World People's Peace Congress will open in Vienna on December 5, 1952. Holding a wide-ranging people's vote will guarantee the work of preparing for this congress in all countries. All people, men and women, regardless of opinion or belief, come together to discuss and make your decisions! Elect your representatives to attend this grand event. Your desire for peace must be expressed.

In order to achieve the goals that will be determined by everyone together, the World People's Peace Congress will unite all people, regardless of their views, affiliations, or groups, as long as they hope to reduce armaments, obtain security and national independence, hope to freely choose their way of life, and hope to ease international tensions.

The World People's Peace Congress will unite all those who hope that the spirit of consultation will triumph over the decision of force.

Peace can be saved!
Peace must be saved!

### 关于召开世界人民和平大会的宣言

1952-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    关于召开世界人民和平大会的宣言
    在朝鲜继续进行着的战争、大规模杀人的武器的使用、德日军国主义的复活，以及摧残民族独立的暴力手段，引起了所有人们的惊骇，甚至连那些还没有感觉到战争危险的人也不例外。许多国家的人民逐渐认识到：不管他们愿不愿意，他们有被一步步拖入全面战争的危险。
    千百万人民要求禁用大规模毁灭的武器，在严格监督下裁减一切军备，缔结和平公约。在议会、工会以及政治、社会和宗教团体里面，一种有利于维护和平的新情绪正在发展起来。为了扭转形势发展的方向和保障和平，这一切力量有可能而且也有必要通力合作。
    一九五二年十二月五日，世界人民和平大会将在维也纳开幕。举行范围极广的人民投票，将使一切国家中筹备这个大会的工作得到保证。所有的人们，不论男女，不分意见和信仰，集合在一起讨论并作出你们的决定吧！选出你们出席这次盛会的代表吧。你们的要求和平的意愿务必要表达出来。
    为了达到将由大家共同来确定的目标，世界人民和平大会将把所有的人团结起来，不管他们抱什么主张、属于什么派别或团体，只要他们希望裁减军备，获得安全和民族独立，希望自由选择他们的生活方式，并希望缓和国际紧张局面。
    凡是希望协商精神战胜武力决定的人，世界人民和平大会都要把他们团结起来。
    和平可以拯救！
    和平必须拯救！
