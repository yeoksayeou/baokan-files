Title: Our Two Delegations Arrive in Prague to Attend the 18th International Red Cross Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-07-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 20th: The Chinese government delegation and the Chinese Red Cross delegation attending the 18th International Red Cross Conference, led by delegation heads Su Jingguan and Li Dequan respectively, arrived in Prague on the afternoon of the 19th and will proceed to Toronto, Canada, to participate in the conference.

### 出席第十八届国际红十字大会  我国两代表团到达布拉格

1952-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

    出席第十八届国际红十字大会
    我国两代表团到达布拉格
    【新华社布拉格二十日电】出席第十八届国际红十字大会的我国政府代表团和中国红十字会代表团，分别在团长苏井观和李德全率领下，于十九日下午抵达布拉格，即转赴加拿大多伦多参加会议。
