Title: Government Administration Council Holds 143rd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th—The 143rd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 4th. At the meeting, Liu Jingfan, Deputy Director of the Central Committee for Practicing Economy and Combating Waste, delivered a report on the concluding work of the Three-Anti Movement.

### 政务院举行一四三次会议

1952-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一四三次会议
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百四十三次政务会议在四日下午举行。会上，中央节约检查委员会刘景范副主任作了关于进行三反运动的结束工作的报告。
