Title: Chinese Delegation Arrives in Berlin to Attend World Peace Council Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1952-07-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 30th: The Chinese delegation to the special session of the World Peace Council, led by head of delegation Guo Moruo, arrived in Berlin on the 29th by special plane from Moscow. Those who welcomed them at the airport included: German Peace Committee Secretary-General Wellmann, and Ji Pengfei, head of the Chinese Diplomatic Mission to Germany. World Peace Council members Liu Ningyi and Li Yimang had already arrived here from Prague on the evening of the 28th.

The special session of the World Peace Council is scheduled to be held in the Social Insurance Building here from July 1st.

### 出席世界和平理事会会议  我国代表团抵达柏林

1952-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会会议
    我国代表团抵达柏林
    【新华社柏林三十日电】中国出席世界和平理事会特别会议的代表团在团长郭沫若率领下，于二十九日从莫斯科乘专机飞抵柏林。到机场欢迎的有：德国和平委员会秘书长卫尔曼、中国驻德外交使团团长姬鹏飞等。世界和平理事会理事刘宁一和李一氓已在二十八日晚由布拉格抵达此间。
    世界和平理事会特别会议定于七月一日起在此间社会保险大楼举行。
