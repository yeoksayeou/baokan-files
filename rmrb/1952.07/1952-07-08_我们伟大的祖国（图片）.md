    Our Great Motherland
    Gao YuBao is a literary soldier of the People's Liberation Army. His spirit of studying culture and the great perseverance and achievements he has shown in literary creation have greatly inspired the soldiers and the broad masses of workers and peasants who are currently enthusiastically studying culture. This is the scene of Gao YuBao (right) discussing and revising the draft of his novel with the literary worker Huang Cao (left) from his unit.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    高玉宝是人民解放军的一位文艺战士，他学习文化的精神和在文艺创作上所表现的巨大毅力和成就，对目前正在热烈学习文化的战士和广大工农群众，起了极大的鼓舞作用。这是高玉宝（右）正在和部队文艺工作者荒草（左）研究修改他的小说初稿的情形。
