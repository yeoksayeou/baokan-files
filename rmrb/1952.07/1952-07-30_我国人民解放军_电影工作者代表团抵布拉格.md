Title: Chinese People's Liberation Army Film Workers Delegation Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1952-07-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 29th. At the invitation of the Czechoslovak Ministry of National Defense to participate in the first Czechoslovak Army Film Festival, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Film Workers Delegation, led by head of delegation Yu Ji, arrived in Prague on the 26th. The Czech Army Film Festival will begin on August 4th and will be held at the Czech Army's summer training camp. In addition to Czechoslovak films, outstanding films from the Soviet Union, China, and the People's Democratic countries will be screened.

### 我国人民解放军  电影工作者代表团抵布拉格

1952-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我国人民解放军
    电影工作者代表团抵布拉格
    【新华社布拉格二十九日电】应捷克斯洛伐克国防部邀请参加捷克斯洛伐克第一届军队电影节的我国人民解放军电影工作者代表团，由团长虞棘率领，在二十六日抵达布拉格。捷军队电影节将在八月四日开始，在捷军队的夏季训练营举行。除了捷克斯洛伐克电影外，将献映苏联、中国和人民民主国家的杰出的影片。
