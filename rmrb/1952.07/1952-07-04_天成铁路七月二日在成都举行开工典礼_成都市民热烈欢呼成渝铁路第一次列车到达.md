Title: Tiancheng Railway Groundbreaking Ceremony Held in Chengdu on July 2nd; Chengdu Citizens Cheer the Arrival of the First Train on the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway
Author:
Date: 1952-07-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, July 3rd. Following the full opening of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway, the Tiancheng Railway held its groundbreaking ceremony in Chengdu on the 2nd. Chairman Mao's inscription, "Congratulations on the opening of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway, continue to work hard to build the Tiancheng Railway," gave great encouragement to the people and railway workers of Western Sichuan. More than 50,000 people from all walks of life in Western Sichuan and Chengdu attended the ceremony. The ceremony was presided over by Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, and was held at the western end of Chengdu Railway Station, the connecting point of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway and the Tiancheng Railway. Minister Teng Daiyuan, Deputy Commander of the Southwest Military Region Li Da, Member of the Southwest Military and Political Committee Cheng Zijian, and Director of the Western Sichuan People's Administrative Office Li Jingquan broke ground with shovels, accompanied by military music and continuous applause and cheers from the masses, celebrating the start of this new construction project.

    Prior to this, the Tiancheng Railway had partially commenced construction on June 1st. Five work zones have been established in the area of ​​more than 100 kilometers from Chengdu to Mianyang. A part of the Southwest Railway Engineering Headquarters transferred from the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway and a certain railway engineer of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have arrived at the construction site one after another and started work.
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, July 3rd. The first train on the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway from Chongqing arrived at Chengdu Station at 11:05 am on the 2nd, and was warmly welcomed by more than 50,000 people from all walks of life in Western Sichuan and Chengdu. Along with the train to Chengdu were: Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, Li Da, Deputy Commander of the Southwest Military Region, Li Yong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, representatives from railway bureaus across the country, heads of party, government and military organs of the Southwest Region, Chongqing City, and the three administrative regions of Eastern Sichuan, Southern Sichuan, and Northern Sichuan, representatives of democratic parties, representatives of people's organizations, and labor models from industry and agriculture, totaling more than 600 people.

    As the train slowly entered the station, thunderous applause erupted from the welcomers waiting on both sides of the railway. They threw colorful paper flowers at the train, and songs, cheers, and waving flowers and red flags turned the station into a sea of ​​color and joy.

    At the welcoming meeting, Li Zonglin, Mayor of Chengdu, delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the people of Western Sichuan and Chengdu. Minister Teng Daiyuan then spoke at the meeting. Minister Teng Daiyuan congratulated the people of Western Sichuan on behalf of Chairman Mao. He said: Western Sichuan is rich in resources, and the successful construction of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway will bring prosperity and happiness to the people of Western Sichuan and Chengdu. He said that the Southwest will continue to build a series of railways such as Tiancheng, Sichuan-Yunnan, and Sichuan-Guizhou in the future. He called on people from all walks of life and all railway workers to continue to give full play to their enthusiasm for production and make greater achievements in future national construction. Then, Li Yong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, Li Xiaoting, Director of the Southwest People's Supervisory Committee, and Peng Dixian, Chairman of the Chengdu Provisional Working Committee of the China Democratic League, spoke successively, unanimously praising the rapid success of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway under the leadership of the Central People's Government, the Chinese Communist Party, and Chairman Mao, and expressed their warm congratulations.

    At the meeting, representatives from all walks of life in Western Sichuan and Chengdu presented banners and flowers to the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, all the workers who built the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway, the Southwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the Southwest Military and Political Committee, and the Southwest Military Region. Students of all ethnic groups from the Southwest Nationalities Institute and Young Pioneers from Chengdu City performed waist drums and dances.

### 天成铁路七月二日在成都举行开工典礼  成都市民热烈欢呼成渝铁路第一次列车到达

1952-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    天成铁路七月二日在成都举行开工典礼
    成都市民热烈欢呼成渝铁路第一次列车到达
    【新华社成都三日电】继成渝铁路全线通车之后，天成铁路于二日在成都举行开工典礼。毛主席亲笔题赠的“庆贺成渝铁路通车，继续努力修筑天成路”的题字给了川西人民和川西铁路职工巨大鼓舞。参加典礼的有川西成都各界五万多人。典礼由中央人民政府铁道部滕代远部长主持，在成都车站西端即成渝铁路和天成铁路的联结点举行，滕代远部长和西南军区李达副司令员、西南军政委员会程子健委员、川西人民行政公署李井泉主任举锄挖土动工，两旁军乐齐奏，群众不断鼓掌欢呼，庆祝这一个新的建设工程的开始。
    在此以前，天成铁路已在六月一日起部分开工，从成都到绵阳的百余公里地区内，已建立了五个工区，从成渝铁路转移来的西南铁路工程总队一部和中国人民解放军某铁路工程师都已先后到达工地，开始工作。
    【新华社成都三日电】由重庆开到成都的成渝铁路第一列火车在二日上午十一时五分到达成都车站，受到川西区和成都市各界人民五万余人的盛大欢迎。随着这列火车来到成都的有：中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远、西南军区副司令员李达、中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席李永和全国各铁路局代表，西南区、重庆市和川东、川南、川北三个行署区的党政军机关首长、各民主党派代表、人民团体代表以及工、农业劳动模范等共六百多人。
    当列车徐徐驶入车站时，迎候在铁道两旁的欢迎者掌声雷鸣，纷纷向列车投掷彩色缤纷的纸花，歌声、欢乐声以及挥舞着的鲜花与红旗，把车站变成了彩色与欢乐的海洋。
    在欢迎会上，成都市人民政府李宗林市长代表川西区和成都市各界人民致欢迎词后，滕代远部长在大会上讲话。滕代远部长代表毛主席向川西人民祝贺。他说：川西物产丰富，成渝铁路修筑成功，将给川西和成都人民带来繁荣和幸福。他说西南今后还要继续修筑天成、川滇、川黔等等一系列的铁路，他号召各界人民和全体铁路职工，继续发挥生产积极性，在今后的国家建设中，做出更多更大的成绩。接着，中国铁路工会全国委员会李永副主席，西南人民监察委员会李筱亭主任、中国民主同盟成都临时工作委员会彭迪先主任委员相继讲话，他们一致赞扬在中央人民政府、中国共产党、毛主席的领导下，成渝铁路迅速修筑成功，表示热烈祝贺。
    在会上，川西区和成都市各界人民代表，向中央人民政府铁道部、修筑成渝铁路的全体职工、中共中央西南局、西南军政委员会和西南军区献旗、献花。西南民族学院的各族学员和成都市的少年儿童队员并表演腰鼓和歌舞。
