Title: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government Moves to Gui Sui
Author:
Date: 1952-07-05
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, Gui Sui, 4th) The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, following the instructions of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, moved to Gui Sui on July 1st. Ulanhu, Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, arrived in Gui Sui on June 27th. More than 1,000 representatives from various agencies, organizations, schools, and citizens of Suiyuan Province and the city welcomed him at the train station. A grand welcoming party was held that evening.

### 内蒙古自治区人民政府移驻归绥

1952-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区人民政府移驻归绥
    【新华社归绥四日电】内蒙古自治区人民政府遵照中央人民政府政务院指示，已于七月一日移驻归绥。内蒙古自治区人民政府主席乌兰夫先于六月二十七日抵达归绥。绥远省、市各机关、团体、学校、市民代表共一千余人到车站欢迎。当晚并举行了盛大的欢迎晚会。
