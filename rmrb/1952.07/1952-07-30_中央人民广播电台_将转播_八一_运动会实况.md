Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Coverage of "August 1st" Sports Meet
Author:
Date: 1952-07-30
Page: 1
    Central People's Broadcasting Station
    To Broadcast Live Coverage of "August 1st" Sports Meet
    General Political Department of the Military Commission and Others Issue Notice to Organize Listening
    [This newspaper reports] The Central People's Broadcasting Station has decided to broadcast live coverage of various exciting performances and final events of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's "August 1st" Army Day 25th Anniversary All-Army Sports Meet, starting from "August 1st." The General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, the Broadcasting Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, and the All-China Sports Federation jointly issued a notice on the 28th, calling on all units of the country's armed forces and all sports workers to pay attention to listening at the time, and to organize commanders, fighters, or the masses of the people in their respective units to listen as much as possible. The notice hopes that after listening, all units will report the listening situation and opinions to the Central People's Broadcasting Station (or the General Political Department, the All-China Sports Federation) in order to gain experience, improve sports propaganda work, and further develop sports activities among the troops and the masses of the people.
    The Central People's Broadcasting Station has decided the following dates, times, and content for the live broadcast of the "August 1st" Sports Meet:
    August 1st: 14:45 to 17:00, Opening Ceremony of the Games.
    August 3rd: 18:30 to 20:00, Men's basketball game between the Polish Basketball Team and the "August 1st" Basketball Team.
    August 6th: 7:30 to 11:15, Military sports events (horse race, obstacle course race, dressing race, grenade throwing race, motorcycle race, and equestrian performance).
    August 7th: 7:30 to 9:00, Military sports events (horse race, equestrian performance).
    August 8th: 9:00 to 10:30, Men's basketball final; 15:00 to 17:00, Soccer final.
    At the same time, during the games, the station will report the competitions and performances of the previous day, that morning, and the entire day in the news programs from 6:00 to 6:15, the news programs from 12:30 to 12:45, the troop programs from 12:45 to 13:15, the troop programs from 19:00 to 19:30, and the news programs from 22:30 to 22:45, in that order; and will preview the competitions and performances for the entire day, that afternoon, and the next day. In addition, the station will report in detail the progress of the games, introduce outstanding units and individuals participating in the competitions and performances in the troop programs, the children's programs from 18:30 to 19:00, the cultural life programs from 20:30 to 21:00, and the joint broadcast programs of people's radio stations across the country from 21:00 to 21:30. The specific dates, times, and content will be announced separately.

### 中央人民广播电台  将转播“八一”运动会实况

1952-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台
    将转播“八一”运动会实况
    军委总政治部等通知组织收听
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台决定于“八一”起转播中国人民解放军“八一”建军节二十五周年全军体育运动大会各种精彩表演及决赛项目的实况。中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部、政务院文化教育委员会广播事业局和中华全国体育总会二十八日联名发出通知，号召全国各部队及所有体育工作者届时注意收听，并尽可能组织本单位指战员或人民群众收听。通知希望各单位在收听以后，向中央人民广播电台（或总政治部、体育总会）汇报收听情况和意见，借以取得经验，改进体育宣传工作，进一步开展部队和人民群众的体育运动。
    中央人民广播电台决定转播“八一”体育运动大会实况的日期、时间及转播内容如下：
    八月一日：十四点四十五分至十七点，大会开幕式。
    八月三日：十八点三十分至二十点，波兰篮球代表队与“八一”篮球队男子篮球比赛。
    八月六日：七点三十分至十一点十五分，军事体育项目（马赛、超越障碍比赛、着装比赛、投弹比赛、摩托车比赛及马术表演）。
    八月七日：七点三十分至九点，军事体育项目（马赛、马术表演）。
    八月八日：九点至十点三十分，男子篮球决赛；十五点至十七点，足球决赛。
    同时，在大会期间，该台将在每天六点至六点十五分的新闻节目、十二点三十分至十二点四十五分的新闻节目、十二点四十五分至十三点十五分的部队节目、十九点至十九点三十分的部队节目及二十二点三十分至二十二点四十五分的新闻节目中，依次报告前一天、当天上午及当天全天的比赛、表演情形；预告当天全天、当天下午及第二天的比赛、表演项目。此外，并将在部队节目、十八点三十分至十九点的少年儿童节目、二十点三十分至二十一点的文化生活节目及二十一点至二十一点三十分的全国各地人民广播电台联播节目中，详细报道大会进行情形，介绍参加比赛和表演的优秀单位和人物，具体日期、时间和内容将另行预告。
