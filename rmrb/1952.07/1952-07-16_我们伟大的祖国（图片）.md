Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-07-16
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    A large number of new-style school supplies produced by the farm tool factory in Jilin Province, Northeast China, will be provided by the People's Government for the use of the vast number of farmers in the province, creating better conditions for this year's agricultural bumper harvest competition movement throughout the province. This is a scene of workers at the factory installing and inspecting new farm tools.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    东北吉林省农具工厂出品的大批新式学具，将由人民政府供给全省广大的农民使用，为今年全省的农业丰产竞赛运动，创造更好的条件。这是该厂工人正在安装和检查新式农具的情形。
