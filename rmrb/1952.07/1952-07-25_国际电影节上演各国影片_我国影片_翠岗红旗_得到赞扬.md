Title: International Film Festival Features Films from Various Countries; Chinese Film "Red Flag on Cui Gang" Praised
Author:
Date: 1952-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 23rd: According to TASS, the seventh International Film Festival held in Karlovy Vary, Czechoslovakia, has so far screened ten feature films and more than forty-five documentaries. Audiences were deeply moved by the Soviet film "The Unforgettable Year 1919." The Chinese film "Red Flag on Cui Gang" also received praise from the audience. In recent days, the film festival has screened films from progressive production companies in many capitalist countries. These include the Italian film "Beware, Bandits!", the Indonesian film "A Cripple," the Indian film "Babul" and the Uruguayan film "The Thief of Dreams."

### 国际电影节上演各国影片  我国影片“翠岗红旗”得到赞扬

1952-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

   国际电影节上演各国影片
   我国影片“翠岗红旗”得到赞扬
   【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社布拉格二十二日讯：在捷克斯洛伐克卡罗维·发利举行的第七届国际电影节，到现在为止已放映了十部故事片和四十五部以上的纪录片。观众都深深地被苏联影片“难忘的一九一九年”所感动。中国影片“翠岗红旗”也得到观众的赞扬。在最近几天中，电影节上演了许多资本主义国家进步制片公司的影片。其中包括意大利影片“当心，强盗来了”、印度尼西亚影片“一个跛子”、印度影片“巴不拉”和乌拉圭影片“盗梦者”。
