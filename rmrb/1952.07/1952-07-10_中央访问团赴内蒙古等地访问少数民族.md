Title: Central Visiting Group Visits Ethnic Minorities in Inner Mongolia and Other Regions
Author:
Date: 1952-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th:

    The Central Visiting Group, heading to the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Suiyuan, and the Northeast region to visit ethnic minorities, departed from Beijing on the 9th, led by the Head of the Group, Peng Zemin, and Deputy Heads of the Group, Sa Kongliao (Mongolian), Pengsike (Mongolian), and A'aisha (Uyghur). Those who came to the station to see them off included Yu Xinqing, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government Committee; Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Tao Xijin, Secretary-General of the Political and Legal Committee of the Central People's Government; Ye Duyi, Deputy Secretary-General; Liu Chun, Vice President of the Central Institute for Nationalities; members of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission in Beijing, and representatives from relevant committees, ministries, and commissions of the Central Government, totaling several dozen people.
    The Central Visiting Group, on behalf of the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao, will conduct visits and express regards to the various ethnic minorities in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Suiyuan, and the Northeast region. The visiting group is carrying a large number of gifts from Chairman Mao to the various ethnic minorities, including photos and inscriptions of Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou, as well as banners, commemorative badges, projectors, slides, and various books and pictorials. In addition, there are a large number of medical equipment, medicines, and books related to hygiene and medicine. The visiting group also has a photography team, a film projection team, and a slide projection team accompanying them.

### 中央访问团赴内蒙古等地访问少数民族

1952-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中央访问团赴内蒙古等地访问少数民族
    【新华社九日讯】前往内蒙古自治区、绥远和东北地区访问少数民族的中央访问团，在团长彭泽民、副团长萨空了（蒙族）、朋斯克（蒙族）、阿艾沙（维吾尔族）的率领下，于九日由北京出发。前往车站欢送的有中央人民政府委员会办公厅副主任余心清，人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观，中央人民政府政务院政治法律委员会秘书长陶希晋、副秘书长叶笃义，中央民族学院副院长刘春，中央民族事务委员会在京委员及中央有关各委、部、会的代表共数十人。
    中央访问团代表中央人民政府和毛主席，将在内蒙古自治区、绥远和东北地区向各少数民族进行访问和慰问。访问团携带有毛主席送给各少数民族的大批礼物，其中包括毛主席、朱德副主席、周总理的像片和题词，以及锦旗、纪念章、幻灯机、幻灯片和各种图书画报等。此外还有大批的医疗器材、药品和有关卫生医药的书籍。访问团并有摄影队、电影放映队、幻灯放映队随行。
