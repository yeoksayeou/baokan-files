Our Great Motherland
Factories and enterprises in various places are constantly building dormitories for workers. This is the newly built worker dormitory on Yanan West Road in Qingdao. This worker's family has just moved into the new house, and they are looking out from the balcony.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地的工厂、企业部门都在不断地为工人修建宿舍。这是青岛延安西路新建的工人宿舍。这一个工人家庭刚搬到新屋子里来，他们正在阳台上眺望。
