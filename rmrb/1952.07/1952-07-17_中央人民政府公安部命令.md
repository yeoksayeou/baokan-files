Title: Order of the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government
Author: Luo Ruiqing
Date: 1952-07-17
Page: 1

    Order of the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government
    (52) Public Governance Character
    No. 119
    The "Interim Measures for the Control of Counter-Revolutionary Elements" has been reviewed and approved by the State Council of the Central People's Government and will be promulgated and implemented from now on. People's public security organs at all levels must educate their subordinates and mobilize the masses to implement it.
    Minister Luo Ruiqing
    July 17, 1952

### 中央人民政府公安部命令
罗瑞卿
1952-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府公安部命令
    （52）公治字
    第一一九号
    “管制反革命分子暂行办法”，业经中央人民政府政务院审核批准，自即日起公布施行。各级人民公安机关务须教育所属，动员群众，贯彻执行。
    部长  罗瑞卿
    一九五二年七月十七日
