Title: Burmese Trade Union Representative, Thakkin Khin Su, Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1952-07-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 22nd: Thakkin Khin Su, head of the Burmese Trade Union Delegation invited to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations, left Guangzhou on the 22nd to return home. Among those who went to the station to see him off were Lin Qiangyun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, and Liao Limin, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions.

### 缅甸工会代表德钦钦素返国

1952-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸工会代表德钦钦素返国
    【新华社广州二十二日电】应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼的缅甸工会代表团团长德钦钦素二十二日离广州回国。前往车站欢送的有广东省总工会筹备会主任林锵云、广州市总工会筹备会秘书长廖立民等人。
