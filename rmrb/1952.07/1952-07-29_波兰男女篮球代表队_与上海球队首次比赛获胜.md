Title: Polish Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Win First Matches Against Shanghai Teams
Author:
Date: 1952-07-29
Page: 1
    
    Polish Men's and Women's Basketball Teams
    Win First Matches Against Shanghai Teams
    (Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 28th) The Polish basketball team, currently visiting Shanghai, held its first friendly matches with Shanghai teams on the afternoon of the 27th. The Polish team won the matches. In the first women's basketball match, the Polish team played against the Shanghai Student Federation team, with the Polish team winning 69-40. In the men's basketball match, the Polish team played against the Shanghai Workers' United team, with the Polish team winning 78-60.

### 波兰男女篮球代表队  与上海球队首次比赛获胜

1952-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰男女篮球代表队
    与上海球队首次比赛获胜
    【新华社上海二十八日电】在上海访问的波兰篮球代表队二十七日下午与上海的球队举行首次友谊比赛。比赛结果，波兰队获胜。第一场女子篮球赛，波兰队对上海学联队，波兰队以六十九比四十获胜，男子篮球赛，波兰队对上海工人联合队，波兰队以七十八比六十获胜。
