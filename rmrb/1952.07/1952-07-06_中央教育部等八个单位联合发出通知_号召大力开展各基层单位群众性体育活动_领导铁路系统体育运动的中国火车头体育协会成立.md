Title: The Central Ministry of Education and Eight Other Units Jointly Issued a Notice Calling for Vigorous Development of Mass Sports Activities in Grassroots Units; The China Railway Sports Association, which Leads Sports in the Railway System, was Established
Author:
Date: 1952-07-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 5th. The Central People's Government Ministry of Education, Ministry of Health, General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, All-China Federation of Trade Unions, All-China Democratic Women's Federation, All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and All-China Students' Federation issued a "Joint Notice on Implementing the Guiding Principles and Tasks of Sports Movement from the Inaugural Meeting of the All-China Sports Federation." The text is as follows:
    The inaugural meeting of the All-China Sports Federation was held in Beijing from June 20th to 24th. In the future, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the Central People's Government, the All-China Sports Federation will surely promote the further organized and led development of sports activities throughout the country. We fully agree with the guiding principles and tasks of sports movement determined by the All-China Sports Federation and are willing to actively assist in their realization. Sports is a widespread and regular mass activity, as well as a long-term and important construction undertaking of the country. It has significant positive effects on enhancing people's physique, strengthening national defense, and promoting economic construction. It must be developed soundly through the joint efforts of relevant organs and organizations at all levels throughout the country. Organizations at all levels should follow Chairman Mao's instruction to "develop sports and enhance people's physique," conscientiously study the spirit of the various documents discussed at the inaugural meeting of the All-China Sports Federation, actively assist local sports organizations in vigorously developing mass sports activities in grassroots units, and strive for the popularization and regularization of national sports.

    Xinhua News Agency, July 5th. The "China Railway Sports Association," which leads sports activities in the national railway system, was established in Beijing on June 30th.
    At the inaugural meeting of the association, Teng Daiyuan was elected as chairman, Lü Zhengcao, Lu Ping, and Li Jiebo were elected as vice-chairmen, and thirty-six members were elected as committee members. After the establishment of the association, the first plenary meeting of committee members was held, and eighteen standing committee members were elected. The standing committee meeting decided that the National Railway Swimming Selection Competition will be held in Tianjin this year, and the National Railway Track and Field Tournament and National Forms Sports Performance Selection will be held in Shanghai.

    Over the past three years, sports activities in the national railway system have developed vigorously. The number of railway employees participating in sports activities has reached more than 200,000, there are more than 3,200 various teams, and more than 1,200 units have sports fields and simple sports equipment.

### 中央教育部等八个单位联合发出通知  号召大力开展各基层单位群众性体育活动  领导铁路系统体育运动的中国火车头体育协会成立

1952-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中央教育部等八个单位联合发出通知
    号召大力开展各基层单位群众性体育活动
    领导铁路系统体育运动的中国火车头体育协会成立
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府教育部、卫生部、人民革命军事委员会总政治部、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会发出“关于贯彻中华全国体育总会成立大会的今后体育运动方针任务的联合通知”。文如下：
    六月二十日至二十四日在北京举行了中华全国体育总会成立大会。今后在中国共产党和中央人民政府的领导下，中华全国体育总会必将推动体育运动在全国进一步有组织有领导地发展。我们完全同意中华全国体育总会所确定的体育运动的方针任务，并愿积极协助其实现。体育运动是一种广泛性经常性的群众活动，也是国家的一项长期的重要的建设事业，对于增强人民体质、加强国防和经济建设有重大的积极作用，必须在全国各有关机关、团体的共同努力下才能得到健全的发展。各级组织应遵照毛主席“发展体育运动，增强人民体质”的指示，切实研究中华全国体育总会成立大会所讨论的各种文件的精神，积极地协助各地的体育组织大力开展各基层单位群众性的体育活动，为国民体育运动的普及和经常化而奋斗。
    【新华社五日讯】领导全国铁路系统中体育运动的“中国火车头体育协会”已于六月三十日在北京成立。
    协会的成立大会上选出滕代远为会长，吕正操、陆平、李颉伯为副会长，并选出委员三十六人。协会成立后，召开了第一次全体委员会议，选出常务委员十八人。常务委员会议决定：今年将在天津举行全国铁路游泳选拔比赛，在上海举行全国铁路田径赛大会和民族形式体育表演选拔。
    　三年来，全国铁路系统中的体育运动已经有了蓬勃的发展，铁路员工参加体育运动的人数已达二十多万，各种球队已达三千二百多个，并有一千二百多个单位有了运动场和简单的体育设备。
