Title: Japanese Publication of Volume VIII of Stalin's Collected Works

Author:
Date: 1952-07-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 2nd. TASS dispatch: Volume VIII of J.V. Stalin's Collected Works—which includes "Concerning Questions of Leninism"—has been published and is on sale in Japan.

### 日本出版斯大林全集第八卷

1952-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    日本出版斯大林全集第八卷
    【新华社讯】塔斯社东京二日电：约·维·斯大林全集第八卷——其中包括“论列宁主义的几个问题”——已在日本出版并发售。
