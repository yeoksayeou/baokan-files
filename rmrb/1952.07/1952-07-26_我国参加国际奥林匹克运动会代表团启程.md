Title: Our Country's Delegation to the International Olympic Games Sets Off
Author:
Date: 1952-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: The All-China Sports Federation received a telegram on the 18th from Erik von Frenckell, Chairman of the Organizing Committee of the 15th International Olympic Games, inviting athletes from the All-China Sports Federation to participate in the 15th International Olympic Games in Helsinki. On the 23rd, the All-China Sports Federation replied to Erik von Frenckell, saying: "We have decided to send a sports delegation from the People's Republic of China to Helsinki to participate in the 15th Olympic Games. The delegation will be led by Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, with Dong Shouyi, member of the International Olympic Committee and Chief Advisor of the delegation, accompanying them. The delegation includes a basketball team, a football team, swimmers, and staff, totaling forty people. Xinhua News Agency reporter Hu Qi will also accompany the delegation."

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: The delegation of the People's Republic of China, participating in the 15th International Olympic Games held in Helsinki, the capital of Finland, departed from Beijing by plane on the 25th to attend the games. Those who came to the airport to see them off included Zhang Yu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government and Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, Ma Yi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Hao Renchu, Standing Committee Member of the All-China Sports Federation, and representatives from the North China Sports Federation and the Beijing Sports Federation.

### 我国参加国际奥林匹克运动会代表团启程

1952-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我国参加国际奥林匹克运动会代表团启程
    【新华社二十五日讯】中华全国体育总会十八日接到第十五届国际奥林匹克运动会筹备委员会主席伊利克·冯·佛伦凯的来电，邀请中华全国体育总会运动员参加赫尔辛基第十五届国际奥林匹克运动会。二十三日，中华全国体育总会给伊利克·冯·佛伦凯复电说：“我们决定派中华人民共和国体育代表团前往赫尔辛基参加第十五届奥林匹克运动会。代表团由中华全国体育总会副主席兼秘书长荣高棠率领，国际奥林匹克委员会委员、本代表团总指导董守义同行，代表团包括篮球队、足球队、游泳选手和工作人员等四十人。另新华社记者胡骑亦随代表团同来。”
    【新华社二十五日讯】参加在芬兰首都赫尔辛基举行的第十五届国际奥林匹克运动会的中华人民共和国代表团，于二十五日由北京乘飞机启程赴会。到机场欢送的有中央人民政府教育部副部长、中华全国体育总会副主席章悫，青年团中央委员会副秘书长马仪，中华全国体育总会常务委员郝人初及华北体育总分会、北京市体育分会等代表多人。
