Title: Chairman Zhang Lan of the China Democratic League Sends a Telegram to the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee Congratulating the Completion of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway
Author: Zhang Lan
Date: 1952-07-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Chairman Zhang Lan of the China Democratic League sent a telegram to the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, congratulating the successful opening of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway on July 1st. The text of the telegram is as follows: Chairman Liu, Vice Chairman He, Vice Chairman Deng, and Vice Chairman Wang of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee:
    The Chengdu-Chongqing Railway, longed for for decades, can be successfully opened on the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China. This fully proves that the people of the Southwest, under the leadership of Chairman Mao, can unleash great power and achieve today's victory. Sending a special telegram to congratulate.
    Zhang Lan, June 30th

### 中国民主同盟张澜主席  致电西南军政委员会祝贺成渝铁路通车
张澜
1952-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    中国民主同盟张澜主席
    致电西南军政委员会祝贺成渝铁路通车
    【新华社三十日讯】中国民主同盟张澜主席电西南军政委员会，祝贺七月一日成渝铁路胜利通车，电文如下：西南军政委员会刘主席、贺副主席、邓副主席、王副主席：
    渴望数十年的成渝铁路，能在中国共产党三十一周年纪念日胜利通车，这充分证明了西南人民在毛主席领导下，才可能发挥伟大力量，获得今天的胜利成功。特电祝贺。
    张澜　六月三十日
