Title: Chairman Mao Zedong's Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Pieck
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1952-07-11
Page: 1

    To Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and to all the Delegates of the Second Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany:
    On behalf of the Communist Party of China, the Chinese people, and myself, I extend warm congratulations to the Second Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany! I wish the Congress complete success and wish the German people new victories in the cause of striving for the unification and democracy of Germany and defending world peace!
                    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                July 8, 1952
