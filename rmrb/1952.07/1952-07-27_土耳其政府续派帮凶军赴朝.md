Title: Turkish Government Continues to Send Accomplice Troops to Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-07-27
Page: 4

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, July 25th] TASS Sofia, July 25th: The Bulgarian Telegraph Agency reports that the Turkish government is continuing to send mercenary troops to Korea to be used by the American interventionist forces. Ankara Radio broadcast that another Turkish army will sail from Izmir to Korea on July 29th.

### 土耳其政府续派帮凶军赴朝

1952-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    土耳其政府续派帮凶军赴朝
    【新华社讯】塔斯社索非亚二十五日电：据保加利亚通讯社报道，土耳其政府继续派遣雇佣兵部队赴朝鲜，供美国干涉军驱使。安卡拉电台广播说：又有一支土耳其军队将于七月二十九日从伊斯密尔乘船赴朝鲜。
