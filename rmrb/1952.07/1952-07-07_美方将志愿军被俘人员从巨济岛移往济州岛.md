Title: US Side Moves Chinese People's Volunteer Army Prisoners of War from Geoje Island to Jeju Island
Author:
Date: 1952-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 4th (TASS dispatch): According to news agency reports: The "United Nations" Command has begun moving 5,600 Chinese prisoners of war from Geoje Island to Jeju Island. Official communiqués state that this is the first batch of 35,000 prisoners of war to be moved from Geoje Island within ten days. After these 35,000 people are moved, there will be no more Chinese prisoners of war on Geoje Island.

### 美方将志愿军被俘人员从巨济岛移往济州岛

1952-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    美方将志愿军被俘人员从巨济岛移往济州岛
    【新华社讯】塔斯社纽约四日电：据通讯社消息：“联合国”军司令部已开始把五千六百名中国战俘从巨济岛移往济州岛。官方公报说，这是十天内要从巨济岛移走的三万五千名战俘的第一批。这三万五千人移完后，巨济岛上就没有中国战俘了。
