Title: Korean Negotiations Staff Meeting Continues
Author:
Date: 1952-07-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 28th. The staff officers of both sides at the Korean armistice negotiations continued to discuss the wording details of the draft armistice agreement at a meeting on the 28th.

### 朝鲜谈判参谋会议继续举行
