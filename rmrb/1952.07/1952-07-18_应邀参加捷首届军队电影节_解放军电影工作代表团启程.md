Title: PLA Film Delegation Departs to Attend Czechoslovakia's First Army Film Festival
Author:
Date: 1952-07-18
Page: 1

    Invited to Participate in Czechoslovakia's First Army Film Festival
    PLA Film Delegation Departs
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] At the invitation of the Czechoslovak Ministry of National Defense, the Chinese People's Liberation Army has dispatched a film delegation to Prague to participate in the first Army Film Festival to be held by the Czechoslovak People's Armed Forces in early August. The delegation departed on the 12th of this month. The military attaché of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China hosted a banquet to see the delegation off.
    The delegation consists of nine members, including head Yu Ji, Secretary-General Hu Jiemin, members Shen Ximeng, Hao Yusheng, Zhang Dou, Tian Hua, Li Changhua, and staff members Yang Chengfu and Tu Yong. At the first Czechoslovak Army Film Festival, the delegation will present nine films from our country, including the newsreels "National Day 1951", "Resist America, Aid Korea", "The Army Liberating Tibet Advances", "Red Flag Waves to the West", "Production Front", and the feature films "Steel Fighter", "People's Warrior", "Red Flag on Cui Gang", and "Victory Reunion".

### 应邀参加捷首届军队电影节  解放军电影工作代表团启程

1952-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加捷首届军队电影节
    解放军电影工作代表团启程
    【新华社十七日讯】中国人民解放军应捷克斯洛伐克国防部邀请，派遣电影工作代表团赴布拉格参加捷克斯洛伐克人民武装部队将于八月初举行的首届军队电影节。代表团已于本月十二日启程。捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆武官曾设宴为代表团饯行。
    代表团由团长虞棘、秘书长胡介民，团员沈西蒙、郝玉生、张斗、田华、李长华，工作人员杨成夫、涂庸等九人组成。代表团将在捷克斯洛伐克首届军队电影节大会上献映我国新闻纪录片“一九五一年国庆节”、“抗美援朝”、“解放西藏大军行”、“红旗漫卷西风”、“生产战线”和艺术片“钢铁战士”、“人民的战士”、“翠岗红旗”、“胜利重逢”等九部影片。
