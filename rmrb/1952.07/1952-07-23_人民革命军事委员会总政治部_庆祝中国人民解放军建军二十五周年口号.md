Title: General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Slogans Celebrating the 25th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1952-07-23
Page: 1

The General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission
Slogans Celebrating the 25th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army

	I. Celebrate the great "August 1st" Army Day of the Chinese People!
	II. Pay tribute to the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, the creators and leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army!
	III. Pay tribute to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army!
	IV. Pay tribute to the Chinese People's Liberation Army for defending peace and national security! Long live the powerful Chinese People's Liberation Army! Strive to build the Chinese People's Liberation Army into a modernized national defense force!
	V. Pay tribute to the Chinese People's Volunteers for resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, defending our homes and country! Long live the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers! Strive to continue strengthening the resistance against U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, and defending peace!
	VI. Eternal glory to the martyrs who gloriously sacrificed themselves in the Chinese People's Revolutionary War!
	VII. Pay tribute to the comrades who were gloriously wounded in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea!
	VIII. Pay tribute to the revolutionary veterans!
	IX. Pay tribute to the families of revolutionary martyrs and the families of revolutionary soldiers!
	X. Oppose U.S. imperialism's occupation of Taiwan!
	XI. Oppose U.S. imperialism's delaying and sabotaging the Korean armistice negotiations!
	XII. Oppose U.S. imperialism's implementation of inhumane bacteriological warfare!
	XIII. Oppose arming Japan! Oppose arming West Germany! Oppose the U.S. imperialism's conspiracy to expand military bases, expand armaments, and prepare for new wars of aggression!
	XIV. Always be vigilant against imperialist aggressors! Strive to defend national security, defend the sacred territory, territorial waters, and airspace of the motherland! Strive to defend peace in the East and the world!
	XV. Comrades of the Chinese People's Liberation Army! Launch large-scale cultural studies, and universally improve cultural standards! Strengthen military sports, and cultivate strong physical fitness! Learn modern military technology and command art! Study Marxism-Leninism!
	XVI. Build a strong people's air force and people's navy! Strengthen the construction of various technical arms!
	XVII. Build a modernized logistics system!
	XVIII. Build various regularized military schools!
	XIX. Militiamen and militia workers! Universally strengthen training, maintain social order, and build a strong reserve force for the national defense army!
	XX. Combat heroes and model workers! Lead the entire army, continue to improve the level of patriotic and internationalist consciousness, and promote revolutionary heroism!
	XXI. Welcome the new victories in the great cause of national defense construction and economic construction!
	XXII. Long live the People's Republic of China!
	XXIII. Long live the Chinese Communist Party!
	XXIV. Long live the great unity of the people of the world!

1952-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    人民革命军事委员会总政治部
    庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十五周年口号
    一、庆祝中国人民伟大的“八一”建军节！
    二、向中国人民解放军的创造者与领导者中国共产党与毛泽东主席致敬！
    三、向中国人民解放军朱德总司令致敬！
    四、向保卫和平与祖国安全的中国人民解放军致敬！强大的中国人民解放军万岁！为建设中国
     人民解放军成为现代化的国防军而奋斗！
    五、向抗美援朝保家卫国的中国人民志愿军致敬！英雄的中国人民志愿军万岁！为继续加强抗
     美援朝、保卫和平而奋斗！
    六、在中国人民革命战争中光荣牺牲的烈士们永垂不朽！
    七、向为抗美援朝而光荣负伤的同志们致敬！
    八、向革命荣誉军人致敬！
    九、向革命烈士家属和革命军人家属致敬！
    十、反对美帝国主义侵占台湾！
    十一、反对美帝国主义拖延和破坏朝鲜停战谈判！
    十二、反对美帝国主义实行灭绝人性的细菌战！
    十三、反对武装日本！反对武装西德！反对美帝国主义扩张军事基地、扩张军备、准备新的侵略
     战争的阴谋！
    十四、时刻警惕帝国主义侵略者！为保卫祖国安全，保卫祖国神圣的领土、领海、领空而奋斗！
     为保卫东方与世界和平而奋斗！
    十五、中国人民解放军的同志们！展开大规模的文化学习，普遍提高文化水平！加强军事体育运
     动，养成坚强的体力！学习现代军事技术和指挥艺术！学习马克思列宁主义！
    十六、建设强大的人民空军、人民海军！加强各技术兵种建设！
    十七、建设现代化的后勤工作！
    十八、建设各种正规化的军事学校！
    十九、民兵和民兵工作同志们！普遍加强训练，维护治安，建设强大的国防军的后备力量！
    二十、战斗英雄和模范工作者同志们！带动全军，继续提高爱国主义和国际主义的觉悟水平，发
     扬革命英雄主义！二十一、迎接伟大的国防建设和经济建设事业的新胜利！二十二、中华人民共和国万岁！二十三、中国共产党万岁！二十四、全世界人民大团结万岁！
