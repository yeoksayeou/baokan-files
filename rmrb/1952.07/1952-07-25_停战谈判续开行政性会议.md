Title: Armistice Negotiations Resume with Administrative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-07-25
Page: 1

    Armistice Negotiations Resume with Administrative Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 24th] The plenary session of the delegation from both sides of the Korean armistice negotiations held an administrative meeting on the 24th to continue discussions on the issue of repatriation of prisoners of war.

### 停战谈判续开行政性会议

1952-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    停战谈判续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城二十四日电】二十四日朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团大会继续举行行政性会议，讨论战俘遣返问题。
