Title: Beijing Post Office Issues Commemorative Stamps for the War of Resistance Against Japan

Author: 
Date: 1952-07-07
Page: 1

    Beijing Post Office Issues Commemorative Stamps for the War of Resistance Against Japan
    [This newspaper's dispatch] To commemorate the fifteenth anniversary of the War of Resistance Against Japan, post offices in Beijing will begin issuing "Fifteenth Anniversary of the War of Resistance Against Japan" commemorative stamps starting on the 7th.

### 北京邮局发售抗日战争纪念邮票

1952-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    北京邮局发售抗日战争纪念邮票
    【本报讯】为了纪念抗日战争十五周年，北京市各邮局定七日起开始发售“抗日战争十五周年纪念”邮票。
