Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-07-05
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    China's first large-scale water gate - the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Inlet Gate - has been fully completed. This modern water gate, weighing more than 400,000 tons, is 1,054 meters long and has 54 openings, with a maximum flow rate of 12,800 cubic meters per second, capable of containing one-fifth of the Jingjiang River's floodwaters. It will bring continuous harvests and long-term happiness to the people in this area.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国第一个大水闸——荆江分洪进洪闸已全部完工。这个四十多万吨的现代化水闸，全长一千零五十四公尺，共有五十四孔，最大流量为一万二千八百秒立方公尺，可以吞蓄荆江洪水的五分之一。它将给这一带人民带来不断的丰收和长远的幸福。
