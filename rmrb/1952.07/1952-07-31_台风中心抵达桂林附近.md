Title: Typhoon Center Reaches Near Guilin
Author:
Date: 1952-07-31
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 30th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued the following typhoon warning at 7:00 PM on July 30th:
    The typhoon center landed near Shanwei, Guangdong Province, on the morning of July 30th. At 8:00 AM, it reached 100 li west of Guangzhou. At 2:00 PM, it reached near Guilin, Guangxi Province, with greatly weakened intensity. In the next 24 hours, the maximum wind speed near the center will be approximately 130 li per hour. The threat to Zhejiang, Fujian, and the coastal areas of southern Jiangsu has now been lifted, but there will still be significant wind and rain in Hunan, northern Guangxi, and eastern Guizhou. Wind and waves in the coastal areas of Guangdong are also gradually weakening, with a maximum wind speed of approximately 120 li per hour.

### 台风中心抵达桂林附近

1952-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    台风中心抵达桂林附近
    【新华社三十日讯】中央气象台三十日下午七时发布台风警报如下：
    七月三十日晨台风中心由广东的汕尾附近登陆，八时抵广州以西一百华里处，十四时抵广西的桂林附近，强度大为减弱。未来二十四小时内，中心附近最大风速约每小时一百三十华里，浙江、福建及苏南沿海的威胁现已解除，但湖南、广西北部及贵州东部一带仍有较大的风雨。广东沿海地区风浪亦渐弱，最大风速，约每小时一百二十华里。
