Title: Weather Bureau Issues Tropical Storm Warning
Author:
Date: 1952-07-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 18th—The Central Weather Bureau issued the following tropical storm warning at 7:00 PM on July 18th:
    The center of the tropical storm discovered in the East China Sea reached the sea area 400 li southeast of Wenzhou (27 degrees north latitude, 122.5 degrees east longitude) at 2:00 PM today. The maximum wind speed near the center is about 140 li per hour. It is currently moving in a northwest direction at a speed of 30 li per hour. In the next 12 hours, the storm will land on the coast near Wenzhou. There will be wind and rain in the coastal areas of Jiangsu, Zhejiang, and Fujian.

### 气象台发布热带风暴警报

1952-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台发布热带风暴警报
    【新华社十八日讯】七月十八日下午七时中央气象台发布热带风暴警报如下：
    在我国东海发现的热带风暴中心已于今日下午二时到达温州东南四百华里的海面上（北纬二七度，东经一二二点五度），中心附近最大风速约每小时一百四十华里。目前正以每小时三十华里的速度，向西北方向进行，未来十二小时内，风暴即将在温州附近海岸登陆，江苏、浙江、福建沿海一带都有风雨。
