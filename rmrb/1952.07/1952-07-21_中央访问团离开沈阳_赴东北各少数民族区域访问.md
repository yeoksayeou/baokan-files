Title: Central Visiting Group Leaves Shenyang to Visit Ethnic Minority Areas in Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1952-07-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 20th – The Central Visiting Group left Shenyang on the 19th to visit various ethnic minority areas in Northeast China. During its stay in Shenyang, the Central Visiting Group listened to reports on the work regarding ethnic minorities from the Northeast People's Government and the Shenyang Municipal People's Government. They also held discussions with representatives of the Hui, Korean, and Mongolian ethnic groups in Shenyang, visited schools, cultural centers, and cooperatives of various ethnic groups. The group conveyed Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government's deep concern for the ethnic minorities and also visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall.

### 中央访问团离开沈阳  赴东北各少数民族区域访问

1952-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中央访问团离开沈阳
    赴东北各少数民族区域访问
    【新华社沈阳二十日电】中央访问团十九日离开沈阳，分途去东北各少数民族区域进行访问。中央访问团在沈阳期间，曾先后听取了东北人民政府和沈阳市人民政府关于少数民族工作情况的报告，并与沈阳市回族、朝鲜族、蒙族代表举行座谈，访问了各族的学校、文化馆和合作社等，传达了毛主席和中央人民政府对少数民族的深厚关怀，还参观了东北工业陈列馆。
