    Our Great Motherland
    The award ceremony of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League to model Youth League member Hao Jianxiu was grandly held in Qingdao on the 6th of this month. This is a photo of model Youth League member and national textile industry labor model Hao Jianxiu after receiving the certificate of merit.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会奖励模范青年团员郝建秀授奖典礼大会，于本月六日在青岛隆重举行。这是模范青年团员、全国纺织业劳动模范郝建秀接受奖状后的留影。
