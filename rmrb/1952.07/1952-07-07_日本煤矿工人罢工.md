Title: Japanese Coal Miners Strike
Author:
Date: 1952-07-07
Page: 4

    Japanese Coal Miners Strike
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 6th] News from Tokyo: Approximately 170,000 workers from eight major coal mining companies in Japan, including Mitsui and Mitsubishi, began a 24-hour strike on the 5th. The reason for the strike is the management's refusal to meet the workers' demands for summer allowances. According to Kyodo News, the strike is estimated to have reduced coal production by 78,000 tons per day.

### 日本煤矿工人罢工

1952-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    日本煤矿工人罢工
    【新华社六日讯】东京消息：日本三井、三菱等八大煤矿公司的工人约十七万人，从五日起开始举行二十四小时罢工。罢工的原因是由于资方拒绝工人关于发给夏季津贴的要求。据共同社讯，据估计，罢工使煤产量一天减少七万八千吨。
