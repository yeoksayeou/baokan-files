Title: Malayan National Liberation Army Attacks
Author:
Date: 1952-07-12
Page: 4

　　Singapore News Agency, 11th: The Malayan National Liberation Army continued its attacks in Perak, Pahang, Johor and other states in June, inflicting heavy blows on the British colonial army.
　　According to Anglo-American news agencies: On June 2nd, the Malayan National Liberation Army ambushed a team of "Special Police" in southeastern Perak, killing eight colonial police officers and wounding nine. On the 19th, the National Liberation Army ambushed a British colonial army unit belonging to the "Manchester Regiment" in the Cameron Highlands of Pahang, killing five British soldiers and one colonial police officer, and wounding four. On June 29th, the National Liberation Army ambushed a convoy of grain trucks escorted by armored vehicles and jeeps in the Segamat area of Johor, killing one European British colonial "Security Force" officer and one colonial policeman.

### 马来亚民族解放军出击

1952-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    马来亚民族解放军出击
    【新华社十一日讯】新加坡消息：马来亚民族解放军在六月份继续在霹雳、彭亨、柔佛等州出击，予英国殖民军以沉重打击。
    据英美通讯社透露：六月二日，马来亚民族解放军在霹雳东南部伏击一队“特别警察”，共打死殖民警察八名，打伤九名。十九日，民族解放军在彭亨州的金马仑高原伏击一队属于“曼彻斯特团”的英国殖民军，共打死英军五名、殖民警官一名，打伤四名。二十九日，民族解放军在柔佛州昔加挽区，伏击一队由装甲车和吉普车护送的运粮车，打死了一名欧洲籍的英国殖民“保安队”军官及一名殖民警察。
