Our Great Motherland
Taihu Lake is one of China's famous lakes. The scenery along the lake is beautiful, the land is fertile, and the fish production is extremely abundant. This is the Taihu Lake water near Wuxi. Fishing boats are taking advantage of the evening to sail back to the fishing village on the opposite bank.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    太湖是我国著名的湖泊之一。沿湖风景秀丽，土地肥沃，鱼产极为丰富。这是无锡附近的太湖水面，渔船正趁着暮色驶回对岸的渔乡去。
