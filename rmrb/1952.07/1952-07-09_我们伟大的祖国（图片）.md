Author:
Date: 1952-07-09

    Our Great Motherland
    Many new record creators have emerged in factories and mines in the Northeast. Sun Xixu, a paper machine worker in the Third Workshop of the Northeast State-owned Third Paper Mill, created a method of using flat water pipes to flush paper ears, saving four people per shift per paper machine. This is a picture of Sun Xixu using a flat water pipe to flush paper ears.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    东北各厂矿中涌现了许多新纪录创造者。东北国营第三造纸厂第三车间抄纸工人孙希旭，创造了利用扁水管冲纸耳子的办法，使每台抄纸机每班节省四个人力。这是孙希旭正用扁水管冲纸耳子的情形。
