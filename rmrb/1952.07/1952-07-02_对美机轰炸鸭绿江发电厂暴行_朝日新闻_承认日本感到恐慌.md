Title: On the US Aircraft Bombing of the Yalu River Power Plant Atrocity, "Asahi Shimbun" Admits Japan Felt Panic
Author:
Date: 1952-07-02
Page: 4

    On the US Aircraft Bombing of the Yalu River Power Plant Atrocity
    "Asahi Shimbun" Admits Japan Felt Panic
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 1st] TASS, Tokyo, 1st: The "Asahi Shimbun" published an editorial saying: The barbaric bombing of the Yalu River power plant by American aircraft shocked Britain and India, and Japan was also panicked by it.
    The newspaper concluded: We earnestly hope that a truce agreement can be reached in Korea.

### 对美机轰炸鸭绿江发电厂暴行  “朝日新闻”承认日本感到恐慌

1952-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

    对美机轰炸鸭绿江发电厂暴行
    “朝日新闻”承认日本感到恐慌
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社东京一日讯：“朝日新闻”发表社论说：美国飞机对鸭绿江发电厂所进行的野蛮轰炸使英国和印度为之震惊，而日本同样也因此感到恐慌。
    该报结语说：我们恳切地希望在朝鲜能达成停战协定。
