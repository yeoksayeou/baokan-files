Title: Resolution of the Preparatory Representative Conference of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce on the Opening Speech by Chairman Chen Shutong and the Speech by Director Chen Yun of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council
Author:
Date: 1952-07-02
Page: 2

The meeting listened to the opening speech by Chairman Chen Shutong and the speech by Director Chen Yun of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council. After careful discussion, everyone felt very satisfied and unanimously accepted and supported them. They pledged to do a good job of conveying them after returning to their localities, launch discussions on Chairman Chen Shutong's opening speech and Director Chen Yun's speech, and fully mobilize local industrialists and businessmen to carry out the following tasks:

I. Under the leadership of the working class and the state-owned economy, and under the overall national economic plan, give play to the enthusiasm for production and management.

II. In accordance with the Notice on Study Issues from the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, study the Common Program and the policies and laws of the People's Government in a planned and organized manner, and regularly conduct criticism and self-criticism to achieve a combination of study and business and consistency between words and deeds, and resolutely oppose and eliminate the "five poisons."

III. Promote and assist the reorganization of local federations of industry and commerce and trade associations, and unite and assist large, medium and small enterprises so that each can be in its proper place. In some areas, the interests of ethnic minorities and overseas Chinese business circles must also be taken into consideration.

IV. Practice increasing production and practicing economy, continue to strengthen the work of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, and actively participate in various patriotic movements.

Everyone unanimously recognized that under the wise leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao, the work of restoring our people's economy has been successfully completed. In the future, we will carry out large-scale, planned economic construction to industrialize our country. Industrialists and businessmen across the country will still shoulder important responsibilities in the great economic construction work in the future. Therefore, industrialists and businessmen across the country must resolutely and conscientiously implement the above-mentioned tasks and strive to complete the glorious mission in history.

### 中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议  关于陈叔通主席开幕词和政务院财政经济委员会陈云主任讲话的决议

1952-07-02
第2版()
专栏：

    中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议
    关于陈叔通主席开幕词和政务院财政经济委员会陈云主任讲话的决议
    会议听取了陈叔通主席的开幕词，和中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会陈云主任的讲话，经过认真的讨论，大家感到十分满意，一致接受和拥护；保证回到本地后做好传达工作，展开关于陈叔通主席开幕词和陈云主任讲话的讨论；并充分发动当地工商业者进行下列各项工作：
    一、在工人阶级和国营经济的领导下，在国家总的经济计划下，发挥生产与经营的积极性。
    二、根据中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会关于学习问题的通知，有计划、有组织地学习共同纲领和人民政府的政策法令，经常展开批评和自我批评，以求学习与业务相结合和言行的一致，坚决反对和清除“五毒”。
    三、推动和协助各地工商业联合会及同业公会的改组，并使大中小企业团结互助，各得其所；在某些地区并须照顾少数民族和华侨工商界的利益。
    四、厉行增产节约，继续加强抗美援朝工作；并积极参加各种爱国运动。
    大家一致认识在伟大领袖毛主席的英明领导下，我国人民经济的恢复工作已经胜利地完成了；今后将要进行大规模的、有计划的经济建设，使我们的国家工业化。全国工商业者在今后伟大的经济建设工作中，仍将担负重要的责任。因此全国工商业者必须坚决地、认真地贯彻执行以上各项工作，为完成历史上的光荣任务而奋斗。
