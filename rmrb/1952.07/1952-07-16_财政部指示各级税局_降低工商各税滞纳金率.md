Title: Ministry of Finance Instructs Tax Bureaus at All Levels to Lower Late Payment Surcharge Rates for Industrial and Commercial Taxes
Author:
Date: 1952-07-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 15th: The Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government has instructed tax bureaus at all levels to reduce the late payment surcharge rates for industrial and commercial taxes. Following the "Five-Anti" campaign, prices have become increasingly stable, and the People's Bank of China has comprehensively lowered interest rates on deposits and loans since June. Therefore, the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government specifically instructs all levels of tax bureaus nationwide to uniformly lower the current late payment surcharge for all industrial and commercial taxes from one percent to zero point five percent, effective July 16th.

### 财政部指示各级税局  降低工商各税滞纳金率

1952-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    财政部指示各级税局
    降低工商各税滞纳金率
    【新华社十五日讯】中央人民政府财政部指示各级税局降低工商各税滞纳金率。“五反”以后，物价益趋平稳，国家银行自六月份起已全面降低了存放款的利息，因此，中央人民政府财政部特指示全国各级税务局自七月十六日起将现行工商各税的滞纳金，一律从百分之一降低为百分之零点五。
