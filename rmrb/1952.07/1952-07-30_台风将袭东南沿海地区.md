Title: Typhoon to Strike Southeast Coastal Areas
Author:
Date: 1952-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 29th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued the following typhoon emergency warning at 7:00 PM on the 29th: At 2:00 PM on the 29th, the center of the typhoon was located 500 li east of Dongsha Island (118.8 degrees East longitude, 20.8 degrees North latitude). It is currently moving northwest at a speed of 50 li per hour. The maximum wind speed near the center is 350 li per hour. At a distance of 1,000 li from the center, the wind speed can still reach 150 li per hour. The wind speed in the coastal area near Fuzhou has reached 150 li per hour. In the next 12 hours, the center of the typhoon will make landfall on the coast between Guangzhou and Xiamen. Coastal areas of Fujian and Guangdong will experience gales, torrential rain, and giant waves, with a maximum wind speed of 300 li per hour or more. Coastal areas of Zhejiang, Fujian, and Guangdong will experience strong winds and rain, with a maximum wind speed of 250 li per hour. Wind force will also increase in the southern coastal areas of Jiangsu.

### 台风将袭东南沿海地区

1952-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    台风将袭东南沿海地区
    【新华社二十九日讯】二十九日下午七时中央气象台发布台风紧急警报如下：二十九日下午二时，台风中心位于东沙岛以东五百华里（东经一一八·八度、北纬二○·八度）的地方，目前正以每小时五十华里的速度向西北方向进行，中心附近最大风速达每小时三百五十华里，距中心一千华里的地方，风速也可达到每小时一百五十华里，福州附近沿海一带的风速已达到每小时一百五十华里，未来十二小时内，台风中心将在广州与厦门之间的海岸登陆，福建、广东沿海一带有狂风暴雨和巨浪，最大风速可达每小时三百华里以上，浙江沿海及福建、广东一带有大风雨，最大风速可达每小时二百五十华里，江苏南部沿海地区风力也将增强。
