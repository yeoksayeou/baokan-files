Title: The 18th International Red Cross Conference to be Held in Canada, Our Government Delegation and Red Cross Delegation Leave Beijing for the Conference, Korean Red Cross Representative Kim Min-san and Two Others Depart from Beijing on the Same Day
Author:
Date: 1952-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 14th: The 18th International Red Cross Conference is scheduled to be held in Toronto, Canada, from July 26th to August 7th. Our government delegation and the Chinese Red Cross delegation attending the conference departed from Beijing by plane for Toronto on the 14th. Our government delegation is composed of Chief Representative Su Jingguan, Representatives Huan Xiang, Huang Hua, Advisor Zhou Gengsheng and other eight people; the Chinese Red Cross delegation is composed of Chief Representative Li Dequan, Representatives Wu Yunfu, Liao Gailong and other ten people. Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Wu Xiuquan, Vice President of the Chinese Red Cross Society Hu Lansheng and other twenty people.

Additionally, the Chinese Red Cross delegation will also attend the 22nd Council Meeting of the International Red Cross Association to be held in Toronto, Canada on July 23rd.

Xinhua News Agency, July 14th: Korean Red Cross representatives Kim Min-san, Ki Seok-bok, Kim Bae-jun and other three people, who are participating in the 18th International Red Cross Conference in Toronto, Canada, departed from Beijing by plane on the 14th. The Korean Ambassador to China Kwon O-jik and others went to the airport to see them off.

### 第十八届国际红十字大会将在加拿大举行  我政府代表团与红十字会代表团离京赴会  朝鲜红十字会代表金民山等三人同日由北京出发

1952-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    第十八届国际红十字大会将在加拿大举行
    我政府代表团与红十字会代表团离京赴会
    朝鲜红十字会代表金民山等三人同日由北京出发
    【新华社十四日讯】第十八届国际红十字大会定于七月二十六日到八月七日在加拿大多伦多召开。出席这一大会的我政府代表团和中国红十字会代表团十四日乘飞机离京赴多伦多。我政府代表团由首席代表苏井观，代表宦乡、黄华，顾问周鲠生等八人组成；中国红十字会代表团由首席代表李德全，代表伍云甫、廖盖隆等十人组成。前往机场欢送的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权、中国红十字会副会长胡兰生等二十余人。
    又中国红十字会代表团也将出席七月二十三日在加拿大多伦多召开的国际红十字协会第二十二届理事会。
    【新华社十四日讯】参加在加拿大多伦多召开的第十八届国际红十字大会的朝鲜红十字会代表金民山、奇石福、金培浚等三人，已在十四日由北京乘飞机出发。朝鲜驻华大使权五稷等多人至机场送行。
