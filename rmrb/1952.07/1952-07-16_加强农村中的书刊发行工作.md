Title: Strengthening the Distribution of Books and Periodicals in Rural Areas
Author: Wu Kaiping
Date: 1952-07-16
Page: 6

Letter to the Editor Summary:

    Strengthening the Distribution of Books and Periodicals in Rural Areas
    I am a revolutionary soldier. Our unit is stationed in a village fifteen miles away from the county seat. Comrades want to buy books, but they can't. Many farmers feel the same way. I suggest that distribution agencies in various places set up mobile book stalls to travel to various villages, and set up bookstores in township fairs or cooperatives. This is good for the development of cultural undertakings.
    Wu Kaiping

### 加强农村中的书刊发行工作
吴开平
1952-07-16
第6版()
专栏：来信摘要

    加强农村中的书刊发行工作
    我是一个革命军人。我们部队驻在离县城十五里的乡村里。同志们想购买书籍，但买不到。很多农民也有这样的感觉。我建议各地发行机构举办流动售书站，巡回到各村工作，并在乡镇集市上或合作社中设立售书处。这对发展文化事业是有好处的。
    吴开平
