Title: Mrs. Felton Arrives in Beijing on Invitation
Author: 
Date: 1952-07-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd—Mrs. Felton of Britain, winner of the Stalin International Peace Prize for "Strengthening International Peace," arrived in Beijing by plane on July 2nd for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. Zhang Xiruo, Kang Keqing, Liu Qingyang and others were at the airport to welcome her.

### 费尔顿夫人应邀抵京

1952-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    费尔顿夫人应邀抵京
    【新华社二日讯】“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人英国费尔顿夫人，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请，在二日乘飞机抵达北京访问。前往机场欢迎的有张奚若、康克清、刘清扬等多人。
