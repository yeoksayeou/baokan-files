Title: U.S.-Japanese Reactionaries Sign Agreement Allowing U.S. Military to Use Japanese Military Equipment
Author:
Date: 1952-07-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 28th (TASS dispatch): News reports state that the U.S.-Japan Joint Committee signed an agreement on July 26th, allowing the U.S. armed forces to permanently utilize 300 facilities and temporarily utilize 303 facilities within Japan.

    The "Asahi Shimbun" stated: The U.S. armed forces will utilize 52 factories, of which 15 will be permanently utilized, including two aircraft factories of Tachikawa Company, four factories of Fuji Industries, and two factories of Japan Steel Pipe Company, etc.

### 美日反动派签订一项协定  允许美军利用日本军事设备

1952-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    美日反动派签订一项协定
    允许美军利用日本军事设备
    【新华社讯】塔斯社东京二十八日电：报纸上的消息报道：美日联合委员会在七月二十六日签订了一项协定，允许美国武装部队永久利用三百处和暂时利用三百零三处日本境内的设备。
    “朝日新闻”说：美国武装部队将利用五十二家工厂，其中有十五家是永久利用的，包括立川公司的两个飞机厂、富士产业公司的四个工厂、日本钢管公司的两个工厂等。
