Title: Government Administration Council Holds 144th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-07-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 14th—The 144th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 11th. After hearing the explanation given by Chen Yu, Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the Ministry's work summary for 1951 and the main points of work for 1952. The meeting also passed several motions regarding appointments and removals, as well as motions to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第一四四次会议

1952-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一四四次会议
    【新华社十四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百四十四次政务会议在十一日下午举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府燃料工业部陈郁部长所作的说明之后，批准了该部一九五一年工作总结与一九五二年工作要点。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
