Title: Indian Red Cross Distributes Grant from China

Author: 
Date: 1952-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 23rd: The Central Committee of the Indian Red Cross Society, at an emergency meeting recently held, decided to expend 360,000 rupees from the 421,941 rupees donated by the Red Cross Society of China not long ago to the people in the famine areas of India. The meeting approved the allocation of cash in varying amounts to several provincial branches of the Indian Red Cross Society to provide relief to those larger famine areas. The West Bengal Provincial Branch received 100,000 rupees; the Bombay Province, Madras Province, Punjab Province, and Rajasthan State Branches each received 50,000 rupees; and the Saurashtra State and Uttar Pradesh Province Branches each received 30,000 rupees. The remaining 61,941 rupees will be stored as a reserve to cope with any emergencies that may occur in the near future.
    Secretary-General Zoli of the Indian Red Cross Society, on behalf of the Indian Red Cross Society, expressed gratitude "for the great and generous gesture of the Red Cross Society of China in supporting our charitable work." The Hindi weekly "New Dawn" in Lucknow commented: "The Chinese people have once again demonstrated their sincere sympathy for the Indian people." "This fraternal sympathy of the Chinese people towards us will become a new link to further strengthen Sino-Indian friendship."

### 印度红十字会发放我国赠款

1952-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    印度红十字会发放我国赠款
    【新华社新德里二十三日电】印度红十字会的中央委员会在最近举行的紧急会议上，决定从中国红十字总会不久以前捐赠给印度粮荒区域人民的四十二万一千九百四十一卢比款项中，支用三十六万卢比。会议批准了拨给印度红十字会若干省分会数额不等的现款，以赈济那些较大的粮荒地区。西孟加拉省分会得到了十万卢比；孟买省、马德拉斯省、旁遮普省和刺查斯坦邦的分会各得五万卢比；苏拉史特拉邦和北方省分会各得三万卢比。剩下的六万一千九百四十一卢比储存下来，作为应付最近将来可能发生的任何紧急事件之用。
    印度红十字会秘书长左利代表印度红十字会“对于中国红十字总会在支持我们的慈善工作方面的伟大、慷慨的举动”，表示感激。勒克瑙地方的印地文周刊“新黎明”评论说：“中国人民又一次证明了他们对印度人民的由衷的同情。”“中国人民对我们的这种兄弟般的同情，将成为进一步加强印中友谊的新的环节。”
