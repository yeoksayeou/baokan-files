Title: Soviet Athletes Arrive in Finnish Capital to Participate in International Olympic Games
Author:
Date: 1952-07-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 18th: According to TASS, outstanding Soviet athletes participating in the 15th International Olympic Games, led by Romanov, Acting Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers' Committee on Physical Culture and Sports, have successively arrived at the venue of the Games: Helsinki, the capital of Finland. The Games will be held from July 19th to August 3rd. The Soviet Union will participate in athletics, gymnastics, wrestling, boxing, weightlifting, basketball, football, shooting, swimming, diving, water polo, rowing, cycling, fencing, equestrian, speedboat racing, and modern pentathlon.

### 参加国际奥林匹克运动会  苏联选手抵芬兰首都

1952-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    参加国际奥林匹克运动会
    苏联选手抵芬兰首都
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社消息：苏联参加第十五届国际奥林匹克运动会的优秀选手，由苏联部长会议体育与运动委员会代理主席罗曼诺夫率领已陆续抵达运动会举行地点：芬兰首都赫尔辛基。运动会将从七月十九日到八月三日举行。苏联参加的项目有田径赛、体操、角力、拳击、举重、篮球、足球、射击、游泳、跳水、水球、划船、自行车比赛、击剑、赛马、快艇竞赛和新五项运动。
