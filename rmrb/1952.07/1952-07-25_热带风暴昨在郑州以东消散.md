Title: Tropical Storm Dissipated East of Zhengzhou Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th—The Central Meteorological Observatory released a message at 7:00 PM on July 24th, announcing the dissipation of the tropical storm:

    The tropical storm that landed in China near Sanmen Bay, north of Wenzhou, on the morning of July 19th, moved to the vicinity of Zhengzhou on the evening of July 23rd and dissipated east of Zhengzhou on the morning of July 24th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 24th—The tropical storm warning for the entire East China region has been lifted. According to the tropical storm report released by the Shanghai Meteorological Observatory at 8:00 PM on July 23rd: The tropical storm has gradually dissipated on the mainland, and the wind force in the East China region has generally weakened to below level 6. The tropical storm warning for the entire East China region has been lifted since 8:00 PM on July 23rd. However, the coastal areas of East China are currently experiencing high tides at the end of the month, and all relevant parties should still pay attention to prevention.

### 热带风暴昨在郑州以东消散

1952-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    热带风暴昨在郑州以东消散
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央气象台二十四日下午七时发布热带风暴消灭消息：
    七月十九日晨，在温州以北三门湾附近登陆我国的热带风暴，二十三日晚中心位置移至郑州附近后，已于二十四日上午在郑州以东消灭。
    【新华社上海二十四日电】华东全区热带风暴警报已告解除。据上海气象台七月二十三日晚八时发布的热带风暴报告称：热带风暴在大陆已经逐渐消散，华东地区风力普遍减弱到六级以下；自二十三日二十点钟起，华东全部地区热带风暴警报解除。但是华东沿海一带，正逢月底高潮，各有关方面仍应注意防范。
