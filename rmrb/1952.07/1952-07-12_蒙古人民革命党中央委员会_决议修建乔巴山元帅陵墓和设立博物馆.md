Title: Resolution of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party on the Construction of Marshal Choibalsan's Mausoleum and the Establishment of a Museum
Author:
Date: 1952-07-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, 11th — The Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party has resolved to build a mausoleum for Marshal Choibalsan and establish a museum commemorating Marshal Choibalsan.

    The blueprint for the construction of Marshal Choibalsan's mausoleum, designed by architect Chimed, has been approved by the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party. A committee composed of Lamosuren (Chairman), Maidar, Zhanchiv, Bada, Sabuniyam, and others has been specially established to lead the work of building the mausoleum.

    Marshal Choibalsan's mausoleum and sarcophagus will be constructed of high-quality marble and granite. The construction of Marshal Choibalsan's mausoleum will be the responsibility of the Ministry of National Defense of the Mongolian People's Republic.

    At the same time, a committee headed by Shirendev has also been formed to prepare for the establishment of the Marshal Choibalsan Museum. The committee recently discussed and approved a general plan for establishing the Marshal Choibalsan Museum, collecting commemorative materials, and publishing illustrated books about the life of Marshal Choibalsan.

### 蒙古人民革命党中央委员会  决议修建乔巴山元帅陵墓和设立博物馆

1952-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党中央委员会
    决议修建乔巴山元帅陵墓和设立博物馆
    【新华社十一日讯】乌兰巴托消息：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会决议修建乔巴山元帅的陵墓与成立纪念乔巴山元帅的博物馆。
    由建筑师契米德所设计的修建乔巴山元帅陵墓的蓝图已经由蒙古人民革命党中央委员会政治局批准。为了领导建筑陵墓的工作，特别设立了一个由拉莫苏伦（主席）、迈达尔、詹契夫、巴达、萨布尼亚姆等人组成的委员会。
    乔巴山元帅的陵墓和石椁将用上等大理石和花岗石修建。乔巴山元帅陵墓的修建工程将由蒙古人民共和国国防部负责。
    同时，以希伦迪布为首的筹备设立乔巴山元帅博物馆的委员会也已经组成。该委员会最近讨论并批准了设立乔巴山元帅博物馆、搜集纪念材料和出版附有插图的有关乔巴山元帅生平事迹的书籍的总计划。
