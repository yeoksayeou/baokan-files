Title: Invited to Our Country to Participate in the "August 1st" Festival, the Czechoslovak Army Art Troupe Departed in Batches
Author:
Date: 1952-07-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 22nd: The first group of members of the Czechoslovak "Vít Nejedlý Army Art Troupe," led by the head of the troupe, Lieutenant Colonel Jaroslav, left Prague for China on the 19th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the art troupe will participate in the 25th anniversary celebration of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on August 1st and will perform in various parts of China. The other members of the art troupe will depart for China in the coming days.

### 应邀来我国参加“八一”节  捷军队文艺工作团分批启程

1952-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加“八一”节
    捷军队文艺工作团分批启程
    【新华社布拉格二十二日电】捷克斯洛伐克“维特、聂德列欧军队艺术文艺工作团”第一批团员于十九日由该团团长雅罗斯拉夫中校率领，离布拉格前往中国。文艺工作团应中国人民解放军的邀请，将在八月一日参加中国人民解放军建军二十五周年庆祝大会，并将在中国各地演出。文艺工作团的其他团员，将于今后数日内起程赴中国。
