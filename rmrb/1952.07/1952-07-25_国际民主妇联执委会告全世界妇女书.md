Title: Letter from the Executive Committee of the Women's International Democratic Federation to Women of the World
Author:
Date: 1952-07-25
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, via TASS)

Women of the world! The World People's Peace Congress will be held on December 5th in Vienna. This will be a gathering of all people who hope that international negotiations and cooperation will triumph over violence and war. Women, the World People's Peace Congress is also your congress, because war will destroy your hopes and destroy those you love. You cannot be indifferent to this danger. Do not forget that war can be prevented, and the joint efforts of people all over the world can maintain peace. The World People's Peace Congress is a unique world forum where people from all over the world will declare their will to strive for peace and point out ways to maintain peace. Women! You must start working immediately. You must remember that every peace fighter is someone who defends your children and the happiness of your family. You must visit people door-to-door, and tell them that the World People's Peace Congress is about to be held. You should visit women of all faiths and opinions, discuss together, and decide to elect representatives to attend the World People's Peace Congress. You must tirelessly explain to your relatives, friends, and neighbors that war can and must be avoided, the Korean War must be stopped! Atomic and bacteriological weapons must be banned! Women! Let the will of each of you and the will of millions of other families unite into an invincible force for peace. Peace will surely prevail.

### 国际民主妇联执委会告全世界妇女书

1952-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联执委会告全世界妇女书
    全世界的妇女们！世界人民和平大会将在十二月五日在维也纳举行，这将是所有希望国际谈判和合作战胜暴力和战争的人们的一次集会。妇女们，世界人民和平大会也是你们的大会，因为战争会毁灭你们的希望，毁灭你们所爱的人，在这种危险之前，你们不能漠不关心。不要忘记，战争是可以防止的，全世界人民的共同努力是可以维护和平的。世界人民和平大会是一个世界无双的讲坛，全世界的人民将在这个讲坛上宣布他们争取和平的意志并指出维护和平的方法。妇女们！你们必须立即开始工作，你们必须记住：每一个和平战士都是保卫你们的孩子和你们的家庭幸福的人。你们要挨家挨户去访问，去告诉人们说，世界人民和平大会就要召开了。你们要去访问具有各种信仰、各种主张的妇女，共同讨论并决定选举代表出席世界人民和平大会。你们要不倦地向你们的亲友和邻居解释，战争是可以而且必须避免的，朝鲜战争必须制止！原子武器和细菌武器必须禁止！妇女们！让你们每个人的意志和其他家庭的千百万人的意志联合起来，变成无敌的和平力量，和平一定会胜利的。            （新华社据塔斯社讯）
