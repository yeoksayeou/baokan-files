Title: Ji Chao Ding Leaves Beijing for Vienna to Attend Standing Committee Meeting of the International Trade Promotion Committee
Author:
Date: 1952-07-20
Page: 1

    To attend the Standing Committee Meeting of the International Trade Promotion Committee
    Ji Chao Ding Leaves Beijing for Vienna
    [Xinhua News Agency, 19th] Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, left Beijing on the 18th by plane to Moscow, and then transferred to Vienna, to attend the Standing Committee meeting of the International Trade Promotion Committee held in Vienna on the 25th of this month.

### 出席国际贸易促进委员会常委会议  冀朝鼎离北京赴维也纳

1952-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    出席国际贸易促进委员会常委会议
    冀朝鼎离北京赴维也纳
    【新华社十九日讯】中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎十八日离京飞莫斯科转维也纳，出席本月二十五日在维也纳举行的国际贸易促进委员会常务委员会会议。
