Title: Hoashi and Others Return Home to Popular Welcome
Author:
Date: 1952-07-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 6th: Japanese representatives Hoashi Kaoru and Miyakoshi Kisuke, who signed the Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement, returned to Tokyo on the 1st. They were warmly welcomed by people from all over Japan, reflecting the enthusiasm of the Japanese people for the Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement and their urgent desire to restore Sino-Japanese trade.
On the 1st, hundreds of people went to Haneda Airport in Tokyo to welcome Hoashi Kaoru and Miyakoshi Kisuke, including: Yamamoto Kumaichi, Chairman of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Conference, members of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, members of the Diet, and representatives of labor unions and people's organizations. Hoashi and Miyakoshi gave speeches at the airport, both expressing their determination to continue working to restore trade between Japan and China.
On the 3rd, a grand rally was held in Osaka to welcome Hoashi and Miyakoshi, attended by 5,000 people. After the meeting, the masses held a demonstration.

### 帆足计等返国受到人民欢迎

1952-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    帆足计等返国受到人民欢迎
    【新华社六日讯】东京消息：签订中日贸易协议的日方代表帆足计和宫腰喜助，已在一日返抵东京。他们受到各地日本人民的热烈欢迎，这反映出日本人民欢迎中日贸易协议的热情和要求恢复日中贸易的迫切愿望。
    一日到东京羽田机场欢迎帆足计和宫腰喜助的有：日中贸易促进会议主席山本熊一、中日贸易促进会会员、国会议员、工会和人民团体的代表等数百人。帆足和宫腰在机场发表谈话，一致表示将继续努力，使日本和中国恢复贸易。
    三日，大阪举行盛大的集会欢迎帆足和宫腰，到会者达五千人。会后群众并举行示威游行。
