Title: Iranian Prime Minister Mossadegh Resigns
Author:
Date: 1952-07-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran dispatch: Iranian Prime Minister Mossadegh has resigned. The King of Iran appointed Ghavam as the Iranian Prime Minister on July 18.

### 伊朗首相摩萨台辞职

1952-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

    伊朗首相摩萨台辞职
    【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社德黑兰讯：伊朗首相摩萨台业已辞职。伊朗国王已在七月十八日任命盖凡姆为伊朗首相。
