Title: Our Young Pioneers Delegation to Hungary
Author:
Date: 1952-07-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 10th: At the invitation of the Hungarian Working Youth League, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League has sent ten outstanding Young Pioneers to Hungary. They departed on the 8th to participate in the Hungarian Young Pioneers summer camp activities. The delegation was selected from ten cities: Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Chongqing, Changsha, Xi'an, Dihua (Uyghur), Tangshan, Jilin, and Wuxi. They will spend this summer happily with the outstanding Hungarian Young Pioneers.

### 我少年儿童队代表队赴匈

1952-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我少年儿童队代表队赴匈
    【新华社十日讯】应匈牙利劳动青年联盟的邀请，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会派遣的十名优秀的少年儿童队员于八日启程前赴匈牙利，参加匈牙利少年先锋队夏令营的活动。代表队是从北京、上海、天津、重庆、长沙、西安、迪化（维吾尔族）、唐山、吉林、无锡等十个城市选出的。他们将与匈牙利优秀的少年先锋队员一起愉快地度过今年的暑期。
