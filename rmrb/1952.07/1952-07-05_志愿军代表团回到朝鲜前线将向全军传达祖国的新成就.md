Title: Volunteer Army Delegation Returns to Korean Front, Will Convey New Achievements of the Motherland to the Entire Army
Author: 
Date: 1952-07-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 4th: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation that returned to China has returned to the Korean front the previous week. Li Xuesan, head of the delegation, gave a detailed report to the leaders of the Volunteer Army on the people of the motherland's love for the Volunteer Army, the work of giving preferential treatment to the families of martyrs and soldiers, and the glorious achievements of the motherland in all aspects. The leaders of the Volunteer Army listened to this report with excitement. After listening to the report, Deputy Commander Deng Hua said: The love, care, and all-out support of the people of the motherland for the Volunteer Army have deeply moved us. The delegation should convey these new atmospheres and new achievements they have seen in the motherland to the troops. The entire army will gain unparalleled strength from this communication report, and all commanders, combatants, and staff will continue to display the spirit of heroism and hard work, fight more battles, and do a good job to repay Chairman Mao, the Chinese Communist Party, and the people of the motherland for their love and care for us. The returning delegation will conduct communication reports to various units separately according to the instructions of Deputy Commander Deng Hua.

### 志愿军代表团回到朝鲜前线将向全军传达祖国的新成就

1952-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军代表团回到朝鲜前线将向全军传达祖国的新成就
    【新华社朝鲜前线四日电】中国人民志愿军归国代表团已于周前回到朝鲜前线。代表团团长李雪三就祖国人民热爱志愿军、优待烈属军属工作、祖国在各方面的辉煌成就向志愿军首长作了详尽的汇报，志愿军首长兴奋地听取了这个汇报。邓华副司令员在听完汇报以后说：祖国人民对志愿军的热爱、关怀和尽力的支援，使我们很受感动。代表团应将自己在祖国所见到的这些新气象、新成就传达到部队中去。全军将因这个传达报告而增加无比的力量，全体指挥员、战斗员和工作员将继续发挥英勇艰苦的精神，多打胜仗，做好工作来报答毛主席、中国共产党和祖国人民对我们的热爱和关怀。归国代表团将根据邓华副司令员的指示到各部队去分别进行传达报告。
