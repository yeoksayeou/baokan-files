Title: American Aggressors Use Japanese Police to Invade Korea
Author: 
Date: 1952-07-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 24th (TASS dispatch):
    American imperialists are utilizing a Japanese police force to fight against the Korean people. "Peace News" reports: 700 members of the Japanese Police Reserve Force, after receiving three months of training at the Sagamihara Camp, were sent to Korea, and 20 of them have already been killed in action. "Yamato Shimbun" reports: The Japanese Police Reserve Force will send another 1,000 personnel to Korea on August 3rd. "The Worker" newspaper writes: At the Sagamihara Camp in Gunma Prefecture, members of the Police Reserve Force and 150 soldiers of Syngman Rhee are receiving training under the guidance of American officers.

### 美国侵略者利用日本警察侵朝

1952-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    美国侵略者利用日本警察侵朝
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社东京二十四日讯：美帝国主义者正在利用一支日本警察部队对朝鲜人民作战。“和平新闻”报道：日本警察后备队曾有七百人在相模原营场受过三个月训练后被派到朝鲜去，其中已有二十人战死。“大和新闻”报道：日本警察后备队将在八月三日再派一千人到朝鲜去。“劳动者”报写道：在群马县的相模原营场，警察后备队的队员与一百五十名李承晚的士兵正在美国军官指导下受训练。
