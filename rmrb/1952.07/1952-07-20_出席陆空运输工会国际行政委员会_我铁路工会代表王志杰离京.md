Title: Railway Union Representative Wang Zhijie Leaves Beijing to Attend the International Administrative Committee of the Land and Air Transport Workers' Union
Author:
Date: 1952-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 18th: Wang Zhijie, the representative of the Chinese Railway Union, has left Beijing by plane on the 18th for Bucharest, the capital of Romania, to attend the fourth session of the International Administrative Committee of the Land and Air Transport Workers' Union, which will be held in Bucharest on July 23rd. Li Jiebo, Chairman of the National Committee of the Railway Union, and Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, were among those who went to the airport to see him off.

### 出席陆空运输工会国际行政委员会  我铁路工会代表王志杰离京

1952-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    出席陆空运输工会国际行政委员会
    我铁路工会代表王志杰离京
    【新华社十八日讯】陆空运输工人工会国际第四届行政委员会会议将于七月二十三日在罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特召开。出席这一会议的中国铁路工会代表王志杰已于十八日由北京乘机前往布加勒斯特。赴机场欢送的有铁路工会全国委员会主席李颉伯、中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明等。
