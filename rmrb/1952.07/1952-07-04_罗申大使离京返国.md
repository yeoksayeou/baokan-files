Title: Ambassador Roshchin Departs Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1952-07-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd: Soviet Union's first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Roshchin, left Beijing for home on the 1st, upon being recalled. Those who went to the station to see him off included: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Wu Xiuquan; Director of the General Office, Wang Bingnan; Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department, Xu Yixin; Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Wu Yuzhang and Shen Junru; Secretary-General, Qian Junrui; President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Xiruo; and Vice President, Hu Yuzhi, among others. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China and the Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, Gudev, along with all embassy staff, also went to bid farewell.

### 罗申大使离京返国

1952-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    罗申大使离京返国
   【新华社三日讯】苏联首任驻华特命全权大使罗申在一日奉调离京返国。前往车站送行的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，办公厅主任王炳南，苏联东欧司副司长徐以新，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、沈钧儒，总干事钱俊瑞，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长胡愈之等人。各国驻华使节和苏联驻华大使馆临时代办顾德夫暨全体馆员也前往欢送。
