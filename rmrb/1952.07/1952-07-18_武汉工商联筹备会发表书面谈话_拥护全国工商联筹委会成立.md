Title: Wuhan Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee Issues Written Statement, Endorsing the Establishment of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee
Author:
Date: 1952-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 5th. The Wuhan Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee issued a written statement on the 3rd, expressing its support, through practical actions, for the establishment of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee and the resolutions passed at the preparatory representative meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. The statement said, "From now on, industrialists and businessmen in the new China have a national leadership organization. This is an epoch-making event in the history of China's business community, made possible only under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party." The statement continued, "The preparatory representative meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce passed resolutions regarding Chairman Chen Shutong's opening address and Director Chen Yun's speech. These resolutions clearly and specifically pointed out the general guidelines for the future work of the Federation of Industry and Commerce. We guarantee to actively and diligently mobilize industrialists and businessmen in Wuhan to do a good job in the four major tasks listed in the resolutions, resolutely and conscientiously implement these guiding resolutions, and strive to complete the glorious tasks that the business community should undertake in the great economic construction work." Currently, under the leadership of the working class and the state-owned economy, various industries throughout the city are working to improve new labor-capital relations and public-private relations, and are separately establishing new collective labor-capital contracts or agreements. The processing and ordering aspect has also shown some new atmosphere. In addition, in accordance with the instructions of the Academic Committee of the Wuhan Municipal People's Representative Conference Municipal Consultative Committee, we have formulated a study plan to study the Common Program, using these practical actions to welcome the establishment of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee.

### 武汉工商联筹备会发表书面谈话  拥护全国工商联筹委会成立

1952-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    武汉工商联筹备会发表书面谈话
    拥护全国工商联筹委会成立
    【新华社汉口五日电】武汉市工商业联合会筹备会三日发表书面谈话，表示以实际行动拥护全国工商业联合会筹备委员会的成立和全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议上的各项决议。谈话称：“从此新中国工商业者有了全国性的领导机构，这是中国工商界历史上一件划时代的大事，只有在毛主席和中国共产党英明领导下才有了这种可能。”谈话称：“全国工商联筹备代表会议通过了关于陈叔通主席开幕词和陈云主任讲话的决议，这一决议明确地、具体地指出了工商业联合会今后工作的总方针，我们保证积极努力充分发动武汉市工商业者做好决议内所列四项重大工作，坚决地认真地贯彻执行这一指导性决议，为完成在伟大经济建设工作中工商界应担负的光荣任务而奋斗。”现在，全市各行业正在工人阶级和国营经济领导下，搞好新的劳资关系和公私关系，分别订立新的劳资集体合同或协议。加工订货方面也呈现了一些新气象。此外，我们并遵照武汉市各界人民代表会议市协商委员会学委总会的指示，制订了学习计划，学习共同纲领，以这些实际行动迎接全国工商业联合会筹备委员会的成立。
