Title: People in Kingston, England, Demonstrate Against US Military Presence

Author:

Date: 1952-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 8th -- TASS reports: July 4th is American Independence Day. One hundred and seventy-six years ago, America gained independence from British colonial rule, but this year, this day became a day for the British people to fight against the American occupiers and demand independence.

On July 5th, residents of Kingston, a central city in the US military zone in Surrey, held a demonstration to protest the presence of American troops in Britain. The demonstrators marched through the city, holding huge banners with slogans such as: "Keep Britain Clean!" "Yanks Go Home!" "The Yanks say no East-West trade, we say yes!" "Don't let the Yanks drag us into war!" "Stop the slaughter in Korea!" "No War Against China!" "We Need Peace!"

The London "Daily Worker" specifically published an editorial on the intolerable sight of American troops celebrating Independence Day on British soil, calling on the British people to "Liberate Britain from the Americans!" The editorial stated: "The unbridled warmongering displayed by American politicians and generals shocks the British people, and the servile performance of British politicians at every turn enrages them. They know that there are currently no fewer than thirty American air bases and more than fifty thousand troops in Britain, and that more new bases are being built, with troop numbers set to increase to at least seventy-five thousand." Referring to Ridgway's scheduled "visit" to Britain on July 9th, the editorial said: "Ridgway's speeches and actions have convinced the British people that he is a true MacArthur-style figure. He initiated germ warfare in Korea." The "Daily Worker" called on the people to fight to expel the American occupiers. It angrily asked: "When will Britain declare its national independence and adopt a policy that puts world peace and the welfare of the British people first? When will it send these American troops, who do not protect the British people but threaten them, back home?"

### 英国金斯敦城人民示威  抗议美军驻扎英国

1952-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    英国金斯敦城人民示威
    抗议美军驻扎英国
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社伦敦讯：七月四日是美国国庆节。一百七十六年以前，美国从英国殖民者统治下获得了独立，但是今年此时却成了英国人民向美国占领者进行斗争要求独立的日子。
    七月五日，美军驻区的一个中心城市、萨利郡的金斯敦城居民举行了示威游行，抗议美国军队驻扎英国。示威群众在全城游行。他们举着巨大的横幅，上面写着：“保持英国的洁净！”“把美国佬送回国去！”“美国佬说不要进行东西方贸易，我们说要！”“不要被美国佬拖进战争！”“停止在朝鲜的屠杀！”“不要对中国作战！”“我们需要和平！”
    伦敦“工人日报”特地就美国军队在英国国土上庆祝独立日这种不可容忍的景象发表社论，号召英国人民“把英国从美国手里解放出来！”社论说：“美国政客和将领们所表现的毫无自制的战争狂使英国人民震惊，而英国政客们每一次奴颜婢膝的表现又使他们愤恨。他们知道，目前美国占领军在英国有不下于三十处的空军基地，五万多兵员，而且还有更多的新基地正在建筑中，兵员至少要增加到七万五千人。”社论在提到李奇微定于七月九日来英“访问”以后说：“李奇微的演说和行动使得英国人民相信：他是一个道地的麦克阿瑟式的人。细菌战就是他在朝鲜的时候开始的。”“工人日报”号召人民为逐出美国占领者而斗争。它愤怒地问道：“英国要到什么时候才宣布自己的民族独立并采取一个把世界和平和英国人民的福利放在第一位的政策呢？它要到什么时候才把这些并不保护英国人民，倒反而威胁英国人民的美国军队送回家去呢？”
