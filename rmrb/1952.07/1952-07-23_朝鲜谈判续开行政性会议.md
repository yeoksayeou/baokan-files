Title: Korean Negotiations Continue with Administrative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-07-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 22nd. The two delegations to the Korean armistice negotiations continued their administrative meeting on the 22nd to discuss the issue of prisoner of war repatriation.

### 朝鲜谈判续开行政性会议

1952-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城二十二日电】二十二日朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团大会继续举行行政性会议，讨论战俘遣返问题。
