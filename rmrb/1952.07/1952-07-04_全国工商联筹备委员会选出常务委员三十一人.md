Title: All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee Elects Thirty-One Standing Committee Members
Author:
Date: 1952-07-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd—The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce held its first meeting on the 3rd. After the meeting adopted the working regulations of the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee, it unanimously elected thirty-one standing committee members (the director and deputy directors of the preparatory committee are ex officio standing committee members). The list of standing committee members is as follows:

　　Chen Shutong, Li Zhuchen, Nan Hanchen, Zhang Naiqi, Meng Yongqian, Sheng Pihua, Xu Dixin, Rong Yiren, Fu Huating, Chen Jingshe, Huang Changshui
　　Hu Zi'ang, Gong Tianmin, Li Xiangjiu, Li Zheren, Le Songsheng, Zhou Shutao, Liu Peizhi, Ye Yutian, Xiang Shuxiang, Miao Hainan, Hua Yuqing
　　Xiang De, Li Zhuo'an, Yi Meihou, Qian Jiaju, Sha Qianli, Shi Fuliang, Liu Hongsheng, Xue Muqiao, Ye Jizhuang

### 全国工商联筹备委员会选出常务委员三十一人

1952-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商联筹备委员会选出常务委员三十一人
    【新华社三日讯】中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会三日召开首次会议。会议通过了筹备委员会常务委员会工作规程后，一致选出常务委员三十一人（筹备委员会主任委员和副主任委员为当然常务委员）。常务委员名单如下：
    陈叔通　李烛尘　南汉宸　章乃器　孟用潜　盛丕华　许涤新　荣毅仁　傅华亭　陈经畬　黄长水
    胡子昂　巩天民　李象九　李哲人　乐松生　周叔弢　刘珮芝　叶雨田　项叔翔　苗海南　华煜卿
    向　德　李琢庵　蚁美厚　千家驹　沙千里　施复亮　刘鸿生　薛暮桥　叶季壮
