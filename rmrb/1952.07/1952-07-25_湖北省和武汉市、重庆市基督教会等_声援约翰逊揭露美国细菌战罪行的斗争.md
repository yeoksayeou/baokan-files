Title: Hubei Province and Wuhan, Chongqing Christian Churches, etc. Voice Support for Johnson's Struggle to Expose American Bacteriological Warfare Crimes
Author:
Date: 1952-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, July 24th: Christian churches in Hubei Province and Wuhan City sent a telegram to the Archbishop of Canterbury, Johnson, in England, supporting his just action in exposing the crimes of American aggressors in conducting bacteriological warfare. The telegram is as follows: Chairman Guo Moruo of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, please forward to Archbishop Johnson:

	We, all Christians of various churches in Wuhan, Hubei Province, firmly support your righteous action in exposing and accusing American aggressors of conducting bacteriological warfare in Korea and China, and support your efforts to safeguard world peace. All the facts you recently provided to England about what you saw and heard in China fully demonstrate the spirit of Christ to "bear witness to the truth." We unanimously protest against the slander and persecution of you by the British ruling clique. Their claws can never cover the eyes and ears of the world, nor can they erase the truth. We firmly believe that "peace will surely triumph over war."

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, July 24th: The Chongqing Christian Study Group, the Chongqing YMCA, the Chongqing Theological Seminary, the Anglican Church, the Methodist Church, the Lutheran Church, and 24 other religious organizations held a meeting on the 23rd to protest against the British government's conspiracy to persecute Archbishop Johnson.

	Pastor Chen Chonggui, vice chairman of the preparatory committee of the Chinese Christian Anti-American and Aid-Korea Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee, made a report at the meeting. He represented the religious circles of the Southwest in resolutely supporting Archbishop Johnson's just action in exposing the crimes of American aggressors in using bacteriological weapons.

	Many pastors and believers condemned the British ruling clique's despicable behavior in plotting to persecute Archbishop Johnson at the meeting. The meeting unanimously adopted a joint protest letter from Chongqing Christian organizations. The protest letter stated: We Christians in Chongqing express our infinite respect and enthusiastic support for Archbishop Johnson's just action in upholding the truth. We Christians in Chongqing want to solemnly tell the reactionary clique in England: No matter what despicable and vicious means are used to harm Archbishop Johnson, their reactionary conspiracy will be thoroughly shattered by the opposition of peace-loving people. Christians all over the world will take practical actions to support the peace warrior, Archbishop Johnson.

	Catholics in Chongqing also held a forum on the same day to protest against the British government's persecution of Archbishop Johnson.

### 湖北省和武汉市、重庆市基督教会等  声援约翰逊揭露美国细菌战罪行的斗争

1952-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    湖北省和武汉市、重庆市基督教会等
    声援约翰逊揭露美国细菌战罪行的斗争
    【新华社汉口二十四日电】湖北省、武汉市各基督教会致电英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊，支持他揭露美国侵略者进行细菌战罪行的正义行动。电文如下：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会郭沫若主席请转约翰逊教长：
    我们湖北省武汉市各教会全体基督徒，坚决支持你揭露并控诉美国侵略者在朝鲜和中国进行细菌战的罪行，拥护你为保卫世界和平而努力的正义行动。你最近向英国所提供你在中国所见所闻的一切事实，充分地表现了基督“为真理作见证”的精神。我们一致抗议英国统治集团对你的诽谤和迫害。他们的魔掌决不能遮尽天下人的耳目，更不能抹煞真理。我们坚决相信“和平定能战胜战争”。
    中华基督教圣公会湘鄂教区    中华基督教湖北大会

    【新华社重庆二十四日电】重庆市基督教学习会、重庆市基督教青年会、重庆市神学院、圣公会、卫理公会、路德会等三十个宗教团体，在二十三日举行集会，抗议英国政府阴谋迫害约翰逊教长。
    中国基督教抗美援朝三自革新运动委员会筹委会副主席陈崇桂牧师在会上作了报告。他代表西南的宗教界坚决拥护约翰逊教长揭露美国侵略者使用细菌武器罪行的正义行动。
    很多牧师和教徒们在会上谴责英国统治集团阴谋迫害约翰逊教长的卑鄙行为。会上一致通过了重庆市基督教团体的联合抗议书。抗议书中说：我们重庆市的基督教徒对于约翰逊教长坚持真理的正义行动，表示无限的敬意和热诚的支持。我们重庆市的基督教徒要正告英国的反动集团：不管以任何卑鄙毒辣手段所欲加害约翰逊教长的反动阴谋，终必在爱好和平人民的反对下彻底粉碎。全世界的基督教徒，将以实际行动来支持和平战士约翰逊教长。
    重庆市的天主教徒也在同日举行了座谈会，抗议英国政府迫害约翰逊教长。
