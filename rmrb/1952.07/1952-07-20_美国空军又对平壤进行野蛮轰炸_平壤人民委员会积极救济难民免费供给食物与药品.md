Title: U.S. Air Force Again Savagely Bombs Pyongyang; Pyongyang People's Committee Actively Provides Relief to Refugees, Offering Free Food and Medicine
Author:
Date: 1952-07-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: According to TASS, Pyongyang: On the 15th of July, in broad daylight, the U.S. Air Force again carried out a robber-like air raid on Pyongyang, conducting savage bombing in the eastern part of the city and strafing with machine guns. Many people were killed and wounded, and dozens of families were destroyed.
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: TASS Pyongyang, July 19th: As a result of the fierce and barbaric air raids carried out by American aircraft on Pyongyang on July 11th and 12th, the Pyongyang People's Committee has taken many measures to help the affected people.
The Food Department of the People's Committee provides free food to all disaster victims every day and issues additional rations. All those injured by the bombing are sent to hospitals, medical clinics, and medical stations for treatment. Disaster victims receive free medicine, clothing, shoes, socks, and other daily necessities. On the 14th alone, the People's Committee distributed more than a thousand pairs of shoes, many clothes, and socks to the victims.

### 美国空军又对平壤进行野蛮轰炸  平壤人民委员会积极救济难民免费供给食物与药品

1952-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    美国空军又对平壤进行野蛮轰炸
    平壤人民委员会积极救济难民免费供给食物与药品
    【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社平壤讯：七月十五日白昼，美国空军又对平壤施行强盗般的空袭，在市区的东部进行野蛮的轰炸并用机关枪扫射。有许多人死伤和几十个家庭被摧毁。
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社平壤十九日讯：由于美国飞机在七月十一日和十二日对平壤进行的凶狠野蛮的空袭，平壤人民委员会已采取了许多措施来帮助受灾的人民。
    人民委员会粮食部每天免费供给所有灾民食物，并发给额外的配给。所有被炸伤的人都送到医院、医疗所和医疗站医治。灾民免费得到药品、衣服、鞋袜和其他日用必需品。在十四日一天，人民委员会就发给灾民一千多双鞋、许多衣服和袜子。
