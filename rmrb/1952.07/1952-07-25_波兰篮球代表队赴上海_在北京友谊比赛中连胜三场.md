Title: Polish Basketball Team Visits Shanghai; Wins Three Consecutive Friendly Matches in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-07-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 24th -- After arriving in Beijing, the Polish basketball team played friendly matches with the men's and women's basketball teams of the All-China Sports Federation's Sports Training Class on the afternoon of the 22nd. The women's team match resulted in a 51-31 victory for the Polish team. The men's team match was canceled due to rain. On the afternoon of the 24th, the Polish team played friendly matches against the Beijing Joint Team. As a result, the Polish women's team defeated the Beijing Joint Team 69-29, and the men's team won 72-47. In the matches, the Polish team demonstrated high skill and excellent teamwork.

The Polish basketball team departed for Shanghai by train on the evening of the 24th for a visit, where they will also hold friendly matches with the Shanghai basketball team.

### 波兰篮球代表队赴上海  在北京友谊比赛中连胜三场

1952-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰篮球代表队赴上海
    在北京友谊比赛中连胜三场
    【新华社二十四日讯】波兰篮球代表队抵京后，在二十二日下午与中华全国体育总会体育训练班的男、女篮球队进行友谊比赛。女队比赛结果五十一比三十一，波兰队获胜。男子队比赛，因雨未能举行。二十四日下午，波兰队与京联队作友谊比赛。结果波兰女队以六十九比二十九，男队以七十二比四十七战胜京联队。在比赛中，波兰队表现了高度的技术和良好的合作。
    波兰篮球代表队已于二十四日晚乘火车赴上海访问，并将与上海的篮球队举行友谊比赛。
