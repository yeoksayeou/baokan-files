 　 Our Great Motherland
    The Communist Party of China has been established for thirty-one years. All the contributions our party has made to the cause of the Chinese people's revolution and construction over the past thirty-one years are inseparable from the wise leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong. This is Comrade Mao Zedong reviewing a report on the construction of the new China.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-01
第1版()
专栏：
 　 我们伟大的祖国
    中国共产党成立三十一周年了。我们的党三十一年来对于中国人民革命和建设事业所作的一切贡献，都是和毛泽东同志的英明领导分不开的。这是毛泽东同志正在审阅有关新中国建设工作的一项报告时的情形。
