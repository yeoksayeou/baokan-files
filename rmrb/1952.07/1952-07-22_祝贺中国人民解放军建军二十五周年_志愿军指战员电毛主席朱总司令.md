Title: Congratulating the 25th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Commanders and Fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers Send Telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu
Author:
Date: 1952-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, July 21st: The entire command staff, combatants, and workers of the Chinese People's Volunteers sent a telegram to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all comrades of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, warmly congratulating the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

The congratulatory telegram stated: Over the 25 years since the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, under the education and leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, Chairman Mao, and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, it has heroically crushed the brutal attacks of domestic counter-revolutionary armed forces; defeated the invading armies of Japanese imperialism; eliminated the eight million Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops armed by American imperialism; liberated the entire Chinese mainland; and established New China. Now, it is accelerating its modernization and regularization, it is the loyal defender of New China, and it is also a powerful armed force for safeguarding world peace.

The congratulatory telegram stated: The Chinese People's Volunteers, composed of outstanding sons and daughters of the Chinese people, have been assisting the Korean People's Army in resisting the American aggressors on the Korean battlefield, fighting heroically for nearly two years. Inspired by the glorious example of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, we have overcome numerous difficulties, grown stronger with each battle, and defeated the most vicious and barbaric American invading army in the world with inferior equipment. We are continuing to fight for the cause of defending peace, until the final victory!

### 祝贺中国人民解放军建军二十五周年  志愿军指战员电毛主席朱总司令

1952-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺中国人民解放军建军二十五周年
    志愿军指战员电毛主席朱总司令
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十一日电】中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员和工作员致电毛主席、朱总司令并中国人民解放军全体同志，热烈祝贺中国人民解放军建军二十五周年。
    贺电说：中国人民解放军建军二十五周年来，在中国共产党、毛主席、朱总司令的教养和领导下，英勇地粉碎了国内反革命武装的残酷进攻；打败了日本帝国主义的侵略军队，消灭了美帝国主义武装的八百万蒋介石匪军；解放了全中国大陆；建立了新中国。现在，它正加紧现代化、正规化的建设，它是新中国忠诚的捍卫者，又是保卫世界和平的一支强大的武装力量。
    贺电说：由中国优秀儿女组成的抗美援朝志愿军，在朝鲜战场上协助朝鲜人民军抗击美国侵略者，英勇奋战已近两年。在中国人民解放军光辉榜样的鼓舞下，我们克服了重重困难，在战斗中愈战愈强，以劣势装备打败了目前世界上最凶恶、最野蛮的美国侵略军。我们正为保卫和平事业继续战斗，一直到最后胜利！
