Title: The Soviet Union Replaces Ambassadors to Czechoslovakia and Romania

Author: 
Date: 1952-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 7th (TASS): The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has appointed Lavrentiev as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the Romanian People's Republic and relieved him of his duties as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the Czechoslovak Republic.

The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has relieved Kavtaradze of his duties as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the Romanian People's Republic.

The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has appointed Bogomolov as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the Czechoslovak Republic.

The Council of Ministers of the USSR has relieved Bogomolov of his duties as Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联更换驻捷罗两国大使

1952-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联更换驻捷罗两国大使
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团已任命拉夫伦捷夫为苏联驻罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使，并解除了他所担任的苏联驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国特命全权大使的职务。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团解除了卡夫塔拉兹所担任的苏联驻罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使的职务。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团已任命鲍格莫洛夫为苏联驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国特命全权大使。
    苏联部长会议解除了鲍格莫洛夫所担任的苏联外交部副部长的职务。
