Title: Korean Negotiations Continue with Administrative Session

Author:
Date: 1952-07-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 23rd: The plenary session of the delegation of both sides in the Korean armistice negotiations continued to hold an administrative meeting on the 23rd to discuss the issue of prisoner of war repatriation.

### 朝鲜谈判续开行政性会议

1952-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城二十三日电】二十三日朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团大会继续举行行政性会议，讨论战俘遣返问题。
