Title: Representatives from Guatemala and Other Countries Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-07-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th: The Guatemalan representatives Silva, Gonzalez, Sanchez, and Valenzuela, the Mexican representatives Gomez, Alvarez, and Maldonado, and the Nicaraguan representative Torres, a total of eight people who were late for the preparatory meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference, left Beijing by plane on the 9th via Moscow to return to their respective countries. Zhang Xiruo, Wu Maosun, Hu Zhongchi, Yuan Shuipai and many others were at the airport to see them off.

### 危地马拉等国代表返国

1952-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    危地马拉等国代表返国
    【新华社九日讯】参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议迟到的危地马拉代表西尔瓦、冈萨莱斯、桑切斯和瓦伦瑞拉，墨西哥代表哥麦斯、阿尔瓦雷斯和马尔多纳多，尼加拉瓜代表托莱斯一行八人，九日乘飞机离北京取道莫斯科分别回国。到机场送行的有张奚若、吴茂荪、胡仲持、袁水拍等多人。
