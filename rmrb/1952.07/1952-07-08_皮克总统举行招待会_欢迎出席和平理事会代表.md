Title: President Pieck Holds Reception to Welcome Representatives Attending the Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-07-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 6th (TASS dispatch): President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic held a reception to welcome representatives attending the special session of the World Peace Council. Many representatives from the political and cultural circles and people's organizations of the German Democratic Republic and Greater Berlin attended the reception. In his welcoming speech, Pieck thanked the representatives attending the meeting for their assistance to the German people's struggle for peace and national unity.

### 皮克总统举行招待会  欢迎出席和平理事会代表

1952-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    皮克总统举行招待会
    欢迎出席和平理事会代表
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社柏林六日讯：德意志民主共和国总统皮克举行招待会，招待参加世界和平理事会特别会议的代表。参加招待会的有德意志民主共和国和大柏林市的政治界、文化界以及人民团体的许多代表。皮克在招待会上致欢迎辞，感谢参加会议的代表们对德国人民争取和平与国家统一的斗争所给予的援助。
