Title: Celebrating the National Day of the Polish Republic: Polish Ambassador Kiryluk Holds Reception; Chairman Mao and Foreign Minister Zhou Send Separate Telegrams to the Polish President and Foreign Minister to Offer Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1952-07-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: Polish Ambassador to China, Kiryluk, held a reception on the 22nd to celebrate the National Day of the Polish Republic.
    Those invited to attend included: Zhu De and Li Jishen, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government; Lin Boqu, Secretary-General; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government Administration Council; Guo Moruo, Vice Premier; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; and over two hundred heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and Wu Han, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, etc.
    Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries in China. The Polish basketball team, currently visiting China and led by Bogusław, also attended the reception.
    The reception was conducted in a friendly and warm atmosphere.
    Furthermore: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent telegrams to Polish President Bolesław Bierut and Polish Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski respectively on the 20th to celebrate the Polish National Day.

### 庆祝波兰共和国国庆  波兰大使基里洛克举行招待会  毛主席周外长分电波总统和外交部长祝贺

1952-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝波兰共和国国庆
    波兰大使基里洛克举行招待会
    毛主席周外长分电波总统和外交部长祝贺
    【新华社二十二日讯】波兰驻华大使基里洛克于二十二日举行招待会，庆祝波兰共和国国庆日。
    应邀出席的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理周恩来、副总理郭沫若，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，以及中央人民政府各部门首长和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等二百多人。
    应邀出席的还有各国驻华使节和使馆馆员。现正访问我国的波兰篮球代表队由波古斯拉夫率领，也出席了招待会。
    招待会在友好热烈气氛中进行。
    又讯：我国中央人民政府毛泽东主席和周恩来外交部长，在二十日分别致电波兰总统贝鲁特和波兰外交部长斯克列杰夫斯基，庆祝波兰国庆节。
