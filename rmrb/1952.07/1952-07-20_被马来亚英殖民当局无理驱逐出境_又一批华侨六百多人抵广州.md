Title: Another Group of More Than 600 Overseas Chinese Arrives in Guangzhou After Being Unreasonably Deported by the British Colonial Authorities in Malaya
Author:
Date: 1952-07-20
Page: 1

    Unreasonably Deported by the British Colonial Authorities in Malaya
    Another Group of More Than 600 Overseas Chinese Arrives in Guangzhou
    [Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 19th] The eighteenth group of 650 Overseas Chinese, unreasonably deported by the British colonial authorities in Malaya, arrived in Guangzhou on the 18th and received a warm welcome and regards from the Guangzhou Municipal Overseas Chinese Affairs Office.

### 被马来亚英殖民当局无理驱逐出境  又一批华侨六百多人抵广州

1952-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    被马来亚英殖民当局无理驱逐出境
    又一批华侨六百多人抵广州
    【新华社广州十九日电】被马来亚英国殖民当局无理驱逐出境的第十八批华侨六百五十人在十八日抵达广州，受到广州市侨务机关的热烈欢迎和慰问。
