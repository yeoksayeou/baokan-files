Title: Commemorating the 25th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Liberation Army: National Post Offices to Issue Commemorative Stamps
Author:
Date: 1952-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: "Commemorating the 25th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army" commemorative stamps will be available for sale in post offices across the country starting August 1st. This batch of commemorative stamps has four designs: 1. The Chinese People's Liberation Army and Weapons (red), 2. Army, artillery, and tanks (green), 3. Navy and fleet (purple), 4. Air Force and aircraft formation (brownish-red). The face value of each is 800 yuan.

### 庆祝解放军建军二十五周年  全国邮局将发售纪念邮票

1952-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝解放军建军二十五周年
    全国邮局将发售纪念邮票
    【新华社二十五日讯】“庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十五周年”纪念邮票，将于八月一日起在全国各地邮局发售。这批纪念邮票有四种图案：一、中国人民解放军和武器（红色），二、陆军和大炮、坦克（绿色），三、海军和舰队（紫色），四、空军和机群（棕红色）。面值都是八百元。
