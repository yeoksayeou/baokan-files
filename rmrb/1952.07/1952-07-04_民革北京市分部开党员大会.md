Title: The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Beijing Municipal Branch Holds a Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1952-07-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The Beijing Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held its Third Party Congress on June 29th, with over 400 party members attending. Li Jishen, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and Central Committee members in Beijing attended the congress. Guests included Li Leguang, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, representatives of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and representatives of local organizations of various democratic parties in Beijing.

    In his speech to the congress, Li Jishen encouraged party members to strengthen their studies, transform their thinking, and unite around the Chinese Communist Party to advance together. When invited to speak, Li Leguang expressed the hope that the members of the Beijing Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang would further enrich their organizational life, vigorously develop the organization, and closely connect with the masses on the basis of the victory of the "Three-Anti" and "Five-Anti" campaigns, and work together for the capital's production and construction.

    Ning Wu, the person in charge of the Beijing Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, delivered a work summary report on behalf of the Second Committee of the Beijing Municipal Branch. Subsequently, representatives of various groups spoke one after another, unanimously agreeing that the main direction of future efforts should be to carry out ideological remolding movements, consolidate and develop the organization, and pay particular attention to combining leadership with the masses, so as to better complete political tasks.

    The congress elected Jiang Guangnai, Su Younong, Tan Tiwu, and other fifteen people as members of the Third Committee of the Beijing Municipal Branch, and Zhang Yinghou, Wang Rongzhang, and other five people as alternate members. The congress also passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao, a congratulatory telegram celebrating the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party, a telegram of respect to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and a telegram of respect to Chairman Li Jishen.

### 民革北京市分部开党员大会

1952-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    民革北京市分部开党员大会
    【新华社讯】中国国民党革命委员会北京市分部于六月二十九日举行第三次党员大会，到会党员四百余人。中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会主席李济深和在京中央委员都参加了这次大会。来宾有中国共产党北京市委员会统一战线工作部部长李乐光，北京市人民政府代表和各民主党派北京市地方组织的代表。
    李济深在向大会的讲话中鼓励党员加强学习，改造思想，团结在中国共产党的周围，共同前进。李乐光在应邀讲话时，希望中国国民党革命委员会北京市分部的党员在“三反”“五反”的胜利基础上，进一步充实组织生活，大力发展组织，密切联系群众，共同为首都生产建设而努力。
    中国国民党革命委员会北京市分部负责人宁武代表北京市分部二届委员会作了工作总结报告。接着，各小组代表相继发言，一致认为今后努力方向，主要为开展思想改造运动，巩固发展组织，尤其要注意领导与群众相结合，以便能更好地完成政治任务。
    大会选出了蒋光鼐、苏幼农、谭惕吾等十五人为北京市分部第三届委员会委员，张应侯、王荣章等五人为候补委员。大会并通过了向毛主席致敬电，庆祝中国共产党建党三十一周年贺电，向中国人民志愿军致敬和向李济深主席致敬电。
