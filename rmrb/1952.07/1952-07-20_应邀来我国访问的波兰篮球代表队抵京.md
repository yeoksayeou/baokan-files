Title: Polish Basketball Delegation Arrives in Beijing for Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1952-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Polish basketball delegation, invited to visit our country by the All-China Sports Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 19th.

The Polish basketball delegation was established in 1948 and is the most outstanding basketball team of the Polish people. Many of its members are outstanding People's Athletes. The team has participated in many international competitions. This visit to China includes a total of thirty-five people, divided into men's and women's teams, led by Kozusznik Boguslaw, Deputy Minister of Health of the Republic of Poland. The Polish basketball delegation will visit Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Shenyang and other cities in our country, and will have friendly matches with men's and women's basketball teams in various places.

When the Polish basketball delegation arrived in Beijing, those who went to welcome them included Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education of the Central People's Government and Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, Huang Zhong, Acting Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and more than one hundred people from the sports community. Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilok, and embassy staff also went to the airport to welcome them.

On the evening of the 19th, Ma Xulun, Minister of Education of the Central People's Government and Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, hosted a special banquet to welcome all members of the Polish basketball delegation. Also attending the banquet were Wei Que, Kang Keqing, Rong Gaotang, Wu Han, Ma John and dozens of others. Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilok, was also invited to attend the banquet.

[Our Newspaper] The opening ceremony of the Polish basketball delegation's friendly basketball matches in our country will be held at 7:15 pm on July 21 at the Xian Nong Tan Public Stadium in Beijing. After the opening ceremony, the delegation will have friendly matches with the men's and women's basketball teams of the All-China Sports Federation's Sports Training Class. The delegation will also have friendly matches with the "Beijing United," "August 1st," and Northeast Military Region men's and women's basketball teams at the Xian Nong Tan Public Stadium at 7:15 pm on July 23, 4:00 pm on August 3, and the evening of August 4, respectively. On July 26, August 5, and August 11, the delegation will visit Shanghai, Tianjin, and Shenyang successively and conduct friendly matches with basketball teams in those locations.

### 应邀来我国访问的波兰篮球代表队抵京

1952-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问的波兰篮球代表队抵京
    【新华社十九日讯】应中华全国体育总会邀请来我国访问的波兰篮球代表队在十九日乘飞机抵北京。
    波兰篮球代表队成立于一九四八年，是波兰人民最优秀的篮球队，其成员有许多是成绩卓越的人民体育家。该队曾参加过多次的国际比赛，这次来中国的共有三十五人，分为男女两队，由波兰共和国卫生部副部长科佐施尼克·波古斯拉夫率领。波兰篮球代表队在我国将访问北京、天津、上海、沈阳等城市，和各地的男女篮球队分别作友谊比赛。
    波兰篮球代表队抵达北京时，前往欢迎的有中央人民政府教育部副部长、中华全国体育总会副主席韦悫、全国体育总会代秘书长黄中、外交部交际处副处长沈平和体育界人士共一百多人。波兰驻我国大使基里洛克和大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    十九日晚，中央人民政府教育部部长、中华全国体育总会主席马叙伦特设宴欢迎波兰篮球代表队全体人员。参加欢宴的还有韦悫、康克清、荣高棠、吴晗、马约翰等数十人。波兰驻我国大使基里洛克也应邀出席了宴会。
    【本报讯】波兰篮球代表队在我国作篮球友谊比赛的开幕式，将于七月二十一日下午七时十五分在北京先农坛公共体育场举行。举行开幕式后，该代表队即将与中华全国体育总会体育训练班男女篮球队分别作友谊比赛。该代表队并将于七月二十三日下午七时十五分、八月三日下午四时、八月四日晚，在先农坛公共体育场分别与“京联”、“八一”、东北军区等男女篮球队作友谊比赛。七月二十六日、八月五日、八月十一日，该代表队将先后访问上海、天津、沈阳等地与各该地篮球队进行友谊比赛。
