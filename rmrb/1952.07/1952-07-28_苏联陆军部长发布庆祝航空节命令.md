Title: Soviet Army Minister Issues Order Celebrating Aviation Day
Author:
Date: 1952-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 27th. TASS, Moscow, July 27th: Soviet Army Minister Vasilevsky issued the following order on the 27th to celebrate Soviet Aviation Day:

    Comrades pilots and navigators, engineers and mechanics, gunners and radio operators, ground crew, and workers in the aviation industry!

    Comrades soldiers and non-commissioned officers! Comrades officers and generals!

    Today, the Soviet Army and all the people of our country celebrate Soviet Aviation Day together.

    On this Aviation Day, I would like to extend my greetings and congratulations to you.

    I wish all personnel of our glorious Stalinist aviation and all workers in the aviation industry new achievements in further strengthening the aviation power of our Soviet motherland.

    In commemoration of Aviation Day, I order: Today, July 27, 1952, a twenty-gun salute shall be fired in our country's capital, Moscow, and in the capitals of each of the Union Republics.

    Long live the Soviet Air Force!

    Long live the heroic Soviet people and their armed forces!

    Long live the Soviet government!

    Long live the great party of Lenin and Stalin, the inspirer and organizer of all our victories!

    Glory to our wise leader, great teacher, and talented helmsman, Comrade Stalin!
                         Marshal Vasilevsky, Soviet Army Minister

### 苏联陆军部长发布庆祝航空节命令

1952-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联陆军部长发布庆祝航空节命令
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十七日讯：苏联陆军部长华西列夫斯基在二十七日发布庆祝苏联航空节的命令如下：
    驾驶员和领航员、工程师和机械士、射击手和无线电报务员、地勤人员、航空工业的工人同志们！
    战士和士官同志们！校尉和将军同志们！
    今天，苏联军队和我国全体人民一道庆祝苏联航空节。
    值此航空节之际，我谨向你们致意和祝贺。
    我祝我们光荣的斯大林航空事业全体人员和航空工业全体工人在更进一步加强我们苏维埃祖国的航空力量方面取得新的成就。
    为了纪念航空节，我命令：今天，一九五二年七月二十七日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科及各加盟共和国首都各鸣放礼炮二十响。
    苏联空军万岁！
    英雄的苏联人民及其武装部队万岁！
    苏联政府万岁！
    我们一切胜利的鼓舞者和组织者——伟大的列宁—斯大林的党万岁！
    光荣归于我们英明的领袖、伟大的导师和天才的舵手斯大林同志！
                         苏联陆军部部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基
