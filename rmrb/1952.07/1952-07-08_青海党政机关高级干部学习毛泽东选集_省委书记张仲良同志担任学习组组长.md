Title: Qinghai Party and Government Organs Senior Cadres Study "Selected Works of Mao Zedong"; Comrade Zhang Zhongliang, Secretary of the Provincial Party Committee, Serves as Head of the Study Group
Author:
Date: 1952-07-08
Page: 3

    Qinghai Party and Government Organs Senior Cadres Study "Selected Works of Mao Zedong"
    Comrade Zhang Zhongliang, Secretary of the Provincial Party Committee, Serves as Head of the Study Group
    The Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China has decided to organize senior cadres of provincial-level Party and government organs to study "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" from July 1. Comrade Zhang Zhongliang, Secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, will serve as the head of the study group. The study schedule is as follows: from July 1 to the end of December, study Volume 1 of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" and "On Contradiction" in Volume 2 of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong." The study will be divided into four units: the theory of establishing base areas, military strategic thinking, the anti-Japanese national united front policy, and ideological methodology. The study method will focus on self-study. The weekly study time should be at least six hours. After completing each unit, participants should write study心得 (xinde - insights/reflections) or papers and organize discussions. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 青海党政机关高级干部学习毛泽东选集  省委书记张仲良同志担任学习组组长

1952-07-08
第3版()
专栏：

    青海党政机关高级干部学习毛泽东选集
    省委书记张仲良同志担任学习组组长
    中国共产党青海省委员会决定从七月一日起，组织省级党政机关高级干部学习“毛泽东选集”，由中共青海省委员会书记张仲良担任学习组组长，学习进度：从七月一日起到十二月底，学习“毛泽东选集”第一卷，及“毛泽东选集”第二卷中的“矛盾论”。学习分为建立根据地学说、军事战略思想、抗日民族统一战线政策及思想方法论等四个单元进行。学习方法以自学为主。每周学习时间至少六小时。参加学习的人员在学完每一单元后，要写学习心得或论文，并组织讨论。（新华社）
