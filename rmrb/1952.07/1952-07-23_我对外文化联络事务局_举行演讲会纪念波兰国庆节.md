Title: Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau Holds Lecture to Commemorate Polish National Day
Author:
Date: 1952-07-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: The Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government held a lecture at the Beijing Theater on the 22nd to celebrate the 8th anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Poland. Yang Qiliang, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Poland, was invited to speak on "Poland Marching Towards Socialism." More than 800 cadres and students from various Beijing organizations attended the lecture. Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau, Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, and Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also attended the lecture. Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilo K, and embassy staff members also attended the lecture.

The "Poland Today" photo exhibition, sponsored by the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau, also opened at the Beijing Library on the 22nd.

### 我对外文化联络事务局  举行演讲会纪念波兰国庆节

1952-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我对外文化联络事务局
    举行演讲会纪念波兰国庆节
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局，为庆祝波兰共和国成立八周年纪念日，二十二日在北京剧场举行演讲会，请我国驻波兰大使馆参赞杨琪良讲“向社会主义迈进的波兰”。听讲的有北京各机关干部和学生等共八百多人。对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、文化部副部长丁西林、外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航等人也出席了演讲会。出席演讲会的还有波兰驻华大使基里洛克及大使馆馆员们。
    对外文化联络事务局举办的“今日的波兰”图片展览会，也从二十二日起在北京图书馆展出。
