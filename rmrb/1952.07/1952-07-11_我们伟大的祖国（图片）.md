Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-07-11
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Chengdu-Chongqing Railway was fully opened to traffic on July 1st. This is a group of representatives of various ethnic minorities from western Sichuan taking the first train from Chengdu Station to Chongqing Station. They were warmly welcomed by the masses when they stepped out of the station.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    成渝铁路已于七月一日全线通车。这是由成都车站乘坐第一列火车到达重庆车站的川西各少数民族代表。他们在走出车站时，受到群众的热烈欢迎。
