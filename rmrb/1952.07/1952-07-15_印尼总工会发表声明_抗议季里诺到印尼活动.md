Title: Indonesian General Federation of Trade Unions Issues Statement Protesting Quirino's Activities in Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1952-07-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 12th: The "Abadi" newspaper of July 10th published a statement by the Indonesian General Federation of Trade Unions protesting the visit of Philippine President Quirino to Indonesia because he wanted to drag Indonesia into the aggressive Pacific Pact. Joining the pact is contrary to the Indonesian people's struggle for world peace. The Indonesian General Federation of Trade Unions called on the people, all honest patriots and peace fighters, to support its demands for the establishment of a national united front, for improved living standards, for genuine democracy, for genuine national independence and for lasting world peace.

### 印尼总工会发表声明  抗议季里诺到印尼活动

1952-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼总工会发表声明
    抗议季里诺到印尼活动
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社雅加达十二日讯：七月十日的“阿巴第报”刊载印尼总工会的声明，抗议菲律宾总统季里诺到印尼来，因为他想把印尼拖进侵略性的太平洋集团里去，而参加该集团是和印尼人民争取世界和平的斗争目的相违背的。印尼总工会号召人民、一切正直的爱国人士与和平战士，支持其争取建立民族统一战线、争取提高生活水平、争取真正民主，争取真正民族独立以及争取世界持久和平的要求。
