China Democratic National Construction Association General Meeting Enlarged Session "July 1st" Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao

Respected Chairman Mao:

After the great victory of the "Three-Anti" and "Five-Anti" Movements, the China Democratic National Construction Association decided to convene the Second General Meeting Enlarged Session. On the opening day, coinciding with the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, we feel extremely excited. We respectfully salute you on behalf of all members of this association!

The main task of our meeting is: to conduct discussions based on your instructions on the principles and tasks of this association, to unify and clarify our understanding of these basic issues, so as to better unite, educate, and transform the national bourgeoisie of China, to prevent and eliminate the "five evils," earnestly implement the Common Program, and strengthen the great construction of the motherland. Under the leadership of the great working class and its representative, the Communist Party of China, and under your leadership, we are confident that we can undertake such a task. We send this telegram of respect and express our heartfelt gratitude for your instructions.

China Democratic National Construction Association Second General Meeting Enlarged Session
July 1, 1952

### 民建总会扩大会议“七一”向毛主席致敬电

1952-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    民建总会扩大会议“七一”向毛主席致敬电敬爱的毛主席：
    在“三反”“五反”运动已经取得伟大的胜利之后，民主建国会决定召开第二次总会扩大会议。开幕之日，适逢中国共产党建党三十一周年纪念，我们感到无比的兴奋。谨代表本会全体会员，向您致崇高的敬礼！
    我们这次会议的主要任务是：以您对本会方针任务的指示为依据，进行研讨，把我们对于这些基本问题的认识一致起来，明确起来，以便更好地团结、教育、改造中国的民族资产阶级，为防止、消除“五毒”，认真执行共同纲领，加强祖国的伟大建设而斗争。在伟大的工人阶级及其代表者中国共产党的领导之下，在您的领导之下，我们确信能够把这样的任务担当起来。谨电致敬，并对您的指示，特别表示由衷的感谢。
                            民主建国会第二次总会扩大会议
                                    一九五二年七月一日
