Title: Northwest Military and Political Committee Sends Two Visiting Groups to Tibetan Areas in Gansu and Qinghai

Author:
Date: 1952-07-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 4th. In order to further strengthen national unity and understand the living conditions of the Tibetan people, the Northwest Military and Political Committee organized a visiting group to the southern Tibetan area of Gansu and a visiting group to the pastoral areas of southern Qinghai. They departed from Xi'an on June 27th to visit Xiahe, Zhuoni, Lintan in Gansu Province and Tongren, Tongde, Angla, and Henan four banners (Mongolian banners) in Qinghai Province. The head of the Qinghai Tibetan area visiting group is Xirao Gyatso (member of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, Vice Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, Tibetan), and the deputy heads are Chen Bikui (Deputy Director of the General Office of the Northwest Military and Political Committee) and Huang Wenyuan (Director of the Animal Husbandry Department of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, Mongolian). The head of the Gannan Tibetan area visiting group is Huang Zhengqing (member of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, Deputy Minister of the Animal Husbandry Department, Tibetan), and the deputy heads are Ma Fuchen (member of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, Deputy Minister of the Industry Department, Hui), Ren Qian (member of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Civil Affairs), and Zhu Xiafu (Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Gansu Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China). The visiting groups all have medical, trade, animal husbandry, and film projection teams, and carry with them a large number of gifts such as portraits of Chairman Mao, commemorative badges, and Tibetan pictorials.

### 西北军政委员会两访问团分赴甘肃、青海藏区访问

1952-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    西北军政委员会两访问团分赴甘肃、青海藏区访问
    【新华社西安四日电】西北军政委员会为进一步加强民族团结和了解藏族人民的生活情况，组织甘肃南部藏区访问团和青海南部牧区访问团，在六月二十七日从西安出发，分赴甘肃省夏河、卓尼、临潭和青海省同仁、同德、昂拉、河南四旗（蒙旗）等地访问。青海藏区访问团团长为喜饶嘉措（西北军政委员会委员、青海省人民政府副主席，藏族），副团长为陈必贶（西北军政委员会办公厅副主任）、黄文源（青海省人民政府畜牧厅厅长，蒙族）。甘南藏区访问团团长为黄正清（西北军政委员会委员、畜牧部副部长，藏族），副团长为马辅臣（西北军政委员会委员、工业部副部长，回族）、任谦（西北军政委员会委员，民政部副部长）、朱侠夫（中共甘肃省委员会统一战线工作部部长）。访问团内均设有医疗、贸易、畜牧、电影放映等组，并携带毛主席画像、纪念章、藏文画报等大批礼物。
