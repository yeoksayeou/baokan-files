Title: Marshal Stalin Receives Renowned Peace Activist Nenni
Author:
Date: 1952-07-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th: TASS, Moscow: Stalin received Nenni, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, on the 17th and held a conversation.

### 斯大林大元帅接见著名和平战士南尼

1952-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林大元帅接见著名和平战士南尼
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：斯大林在十七日接见世界和平理事会副主席南尼，并进行了谈话。
