Title: China and Bulgaria Sign Agreement on Exchange of Goods and Payments for This Year
Author:
Date: 1952-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th -- The trade negotiations between the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China and the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of Bulgaria were completed in an atmosphere of mutual understanding and sincere friendship. The "Agreement on the Exchange of Goods and Payments between China and Bulgaria for 1952" was signed in Sofia on the 21st. Cao Xiangren, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Bulgaria, signed on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Ganev, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, signed on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria. The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included all personnel of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Bulgaria and members of the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China. The Bulgarian side included Liu. Gerasimov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and members of the trade negotiation delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria. Both sides unanimously agreed that this agreement is the first formal economic cooperation between the two countries in history and will contribute to the development of economic construction in both countries and the cause of defending world peace.

### 中保两国签订今年交换货物及付款协定

1952-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    中保两国签订今年交换货物及付款协定
    【新华社二十七日讯】中华人民共和国商务代表团和保加利亚人民共和国国外贸易部之间，在互相谅解和诚恳友好的气氛中完成了一九五二年的贸易谈判，于二十一日在索非亚签订了中保一九五二年交换货物及付款协定。代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府签字的是中华人民共和国驻保加利亚大使曹祥仁，代表保加利亚人民共和国政府签字的是保加利亚人民共和国国外贸易部部长加涅夫。中国方面参加签字仪式的有中华人民共和国驻保加利亚大使馆全体人员及中华人民共和国商务代表团团员，保加利亚方面有保加利亚人民共和国外交部副部长刘·格拉西摩夫及保加利亚人民共和国的贸易谈判代表团团员。双方代表一致认为：此项协定是两国在历史上第一次的正式经济合作，将有助于两国经济建设的发展和保卫世界和平的事业。
