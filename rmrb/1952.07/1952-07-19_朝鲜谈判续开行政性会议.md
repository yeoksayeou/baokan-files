Title: Korean Negotiations Resume in Administrative Session
Author: 
Date: 1952-07-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 18th—Following a four-day recess at our suggestion, the Korean armistice negotiations resumed administrative sessions on the 18th.

### 朝鲜谈判续开行政性会议

1952-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城十八日电】朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团大会在我方建议下休会四天后，十八日继续进行行政性会议。
