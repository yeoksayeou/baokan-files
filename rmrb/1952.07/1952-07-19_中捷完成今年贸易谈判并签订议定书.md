Title: China and Czechoslovakia Complete Trade Negotiations and Sign Protocol This Year
Author: 
Date: 1952-07-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 18th: The trade negotiations between the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China and the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Czechoslovak Republic were completed in an atmosphere of mutual understanding and sincere friendship. The two parties signed a protocol in Prague on July 15th on extending and amending the 1951 agreement on the exchange of goods and payments between the two countries. Ambassador Tan Xilin signed on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China; Minister of Foreign Trade Dr. Gregor signed on behalf of the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic. The barter measures stipulated in the protocol will further strengthen the trade relations between China and Czechoslovakia.

### 中捷完成今年贸易谈判并签订议定书

1952-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷完成今年贸易谈判并签订议定书
    【新华社布拉格十八日电】中华人民共和国商务代表团和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府国外贸易部之间在互相谅解和诚恳友好的气氛中完成了一九五二年的贸易谈判，并已于七月十五日在布拉格签订了中捷关于延长并修正一九五一年两国间货物交换与付款协定之议定书。代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府签字者为谭希林大使；代表捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府签字者为国外贸易部部长格里哥尔博士。议定书中所规定的易货措施将进一步加强中捷两国的贸易关系。
