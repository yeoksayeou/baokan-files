Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Congratulations to Polish Trade Unions on Polish National Day
Author:
Date: 1952-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st — The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions, warmly congratulating them on the National Day of the Polish Republic on the 22nd. The telegram reads as follows: Comrade Korosiewicz:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Polish Republic, I would like to extend brotherly congratulations to you and all the working people of Poland on behalf of the Chinese working class.

    We deeply admire the increasingly great achievements made by the Polish people in all aspects of politics, economy, and culture under the leadership of the United Workers' Party headed by Comrade Beirut since liberation. We wish you even greater success in completing the Six-Year Plan and defending world peace. All-China Federation of Trade Unions, July 21st

### 我全国总工会电波兰工会祝贺波兰国庆

1952-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我全国总工会电波兰工会祝贺波兰国庆
    【新华社二十一日讯】二十二日为波兰共和国国庆日，中华全国总工会特致电波兰工会中央理事会，热烈祝贺。电文如下：柯罗西维兹同志：
    值此波兰共和国国庆日之际，谨代表中国工人阶级向您和波兰全体劳动人民致兄弟般的祝贺。
    波兰人民自解放以来在以贝鲁特同志为首的统一工人党领导下，在政治、经济、文化各方面所获得的日益伟大的成就，使我们深为钦佩。谨祝你们在完成六年计划及保卫世界和平的事业上获得更大的成就。中华全国总工会七月二十一日
