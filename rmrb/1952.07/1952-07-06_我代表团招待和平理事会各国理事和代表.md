Title: Our Delegation Hosts Councilors and Representatives of the World Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1952-07-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 5th -- The Chinese delegation attending the World Peace Council Special Meeting held a reception here on the evening of the 3rd to host councilors and representatives of various countries of the Council. More than one hundred guests from the Soviet Union, North Korea, India, Japan, Indonesia, Britain, the United States, France, and other countries attended the reception. Among them were famous peace activists Ehrenburg, Fadeyev, Neruda, Vaisanen, Kingsley, Wantueleli and others. Ji Pengfei, head of our country's diplomatic mission in Germany, also attended the reception. The reception proceeded in a friendly and united atmosphere. The Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference to be held in Beijing this autumn was the main topic of discussion among the representatives of Asian countries. They all expressed that the people of Asian countries must strengthen their unity and struggle to shatter the schemes of the American imperialists to expand the war and to defend peace in Asia and the world. At the reception, Guo Moruo frequently toasted the councilors of the World Peace Council and representatives of various countries for lasting world peace. At the reception, the delegation also presented various Chinese publications to the guests.

### 我代表团招待和平理事会各国理事和代表

1952-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我代表团招待和平理事会各国理事和代表
    【新华社柏林五日电】出席世界和平理事会特别会议的中国代表团三日晚在此间举行招待会，招待理事会的各国理事和代表。出席招待会的有苏联、朝鲜、印度、日本、印尼、英国、美国、法国以及其他各国的来宾一百余人。其中有著名和平人士爱伦堡、法捷耶夫、聂鲁达、法奇、金斯伯利、万徒勒里等人。我国驻德外交使团团长姬鹏飞大使也参加了招待会。招待会在友好和团结的气氛中进行。今秋将在北京举行的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议，是亚洲各国代表的主要话题。他们都表示亚洲各国人民必须加强团结，为粉碎美帝国主义者扩大战争的阴谋和保卫亚洲和世界和平而斗争。在招待会上，郭沫若频频和世界和平理事会理事与各国代表为世界持久和平而干杯。在招待会上代表团并且把中国各种出版物赠送来宾。
