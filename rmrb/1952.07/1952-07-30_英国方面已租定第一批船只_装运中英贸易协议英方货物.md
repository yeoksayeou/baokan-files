Title: British Side Has Chartered First Batch of Ships to Load British Goods Under Sino-British Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1952-07-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, London, July 26th (TASS): According to the "Daily Worker", the British side has chartered the first batch of ships to load British goods to be shipped to China under the agreement of the Moscow International Economic Conference in April this year. This news was announced to the newspaper by a member of the International Trade Association, which was established in London to implement the agreement signed after the International Economic Conference.

### 英国方面已租定第一批船只  装运中英贸易协议英方货物

1952-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    英国方面已租定第一批船只
    装运中英贸易协议英方货物
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十六日讯：据“工人日报”消息，英国方面已租定第一批船只，装载按照本年四月莫斯科国际经济会议的协议运往中国的英国货物。这项消息是为实行国际经济会议之后所签订的协议而在伦敦设立的国际贸易协会的会员向该报发表的。
