Title: Panchen Lama Solemnly Ascends the Throne and Meditates
Author:
Date: 1952-07-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 4th: On the morning of June 23rd, Panchen Erdeni solemnly ascended the throne and meditated in the main hall of Tashilhunpo Monastery in Xigaze, where successive Panchen Lamas held their enthronement ceremonies. The chief Kalon of the Tibetan local government, Ngapa Tupten Kunga, presented a Hada to Panchen Erdeni on behalf of the Tibetan local government. Before the ascension, General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, specially sent precious gifts such as gold sand, jade bowls, and various ivory carvings to Panchen Erdeni and extended congratulations. The Panchen Erdeni Master Jampel Gyatso Rinpoche, Zasa Lamin Yishe Tsultrim, the Great Lama Ngawang Jinpa, the Living Buddhas of various monasteries, and the main officials of the Tibetan local government at all levels attended the ascension ceremony. Responsible cadres of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Xigaze also went to offer congratulations.

### 班禅隆重升座参禅

1952-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅隆重升座参禅
    【新华社拉萨四日电】六月二十三日上午班禅额尔德尼在日喀则札什伦布寺历代班禅举行坐床典礼的大殿内，隆重举行了升座参禅。西藏地方政府首席噶伦然巴·图丹滚清代表西藏地方政府向班禅额尔德尼献哈达。升座前，中央人民政府代表张经武将军特派员向班禅额尔德尼赠送金沙、玉碗和各种象牙雕刻等珍贵礼品，并致祝贺。参加升座仪式的有班禅额尔德尼大师甲央活佛、扎萨拉敏益喜楚臣、大喇嘛纳旺金巴、各寺院活佛及西藏各级地方政府的主要官员。人民解放军驻日喀则部队的负责干部，也前往祝贺。
