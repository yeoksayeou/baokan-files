Title: Chinese Students' Association in the UK Sends Respects to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1952-07-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The Chinese Students' Association in the UK, on the occasion of holding its 1952 Congress, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao to express their respects. The text of the telegram is as follows: Chairman Mao: The Chinese Students' Association in the UK, on the occasion of this 1952 Congress, respectfully extend our highest respect to you and the people of the motherland. And we respectfully wish you even greater victories in the work of socialist construction, in defending the motherland, defending peace, and in the struggle against aggression and germ warfare. All students are unanimously determined to strengthen internal unity, enhance patriotic awareness, and strive to return to the motherland as soon as possible to participate in construction. We respectfully wish you good health and long live the People's Republic of China!
The Chinese Students' Association in the UK. June 30th

### 留英中国学生总会电毛泽东主席致敬

1952-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    留英中国学生总会电毛泽东主席致敬
    【新华社九日讯】留英中国学生总会在举行一九五二年大会之际，特电毛主席致敬。电文如下：毛主席：留英中国学生总会，届此一九五二年大会，谨向您及祖国人民致无上敬意。并恭祝在新民主主义建设工作中及保卫祖国，保卫和平，反侵略，反细菌战之斗争中，获得更大的胜利。全体同学一致决意加强内部团结，提高爱国认识，争取早日回国参加建设。敬祝您身体健康并中华人民共和国万岁！
    留英中国学生总会  六月三十日
