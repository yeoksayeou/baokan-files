Title: Ethnic Minority Representatives Attending the National Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Meeting Visit Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1952-07-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 10th:

    A group of over ten ethnic minority representatives who attended the preparatory meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce left Beijing on the morning of the 10th to visit Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places. Chen Shutong, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and many cadres from the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission went to the station to see them off.

    These ethnic minority representatives came from Tibet, Xinjiang, Gansu, Yunnan, Xikang, Guizhou, Guangdong, and other regions, including Tibetan, Uyghur, Hui, Miao, Yao, and other ethnic groups. During their stay in Beijing, they visited the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, the Qinghe Woolen Mill, the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute, and the Agricultural Machinery School, and also toured the capital's famous scenic spots and historical sites.

### 出席全国工商联筹备代表会议的  少数民族代表赴各地参观

1952-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    出席全国工商联筹备代表会议的
    少数民族代表赴各地参观
    【新华社十日讯】出席中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议的少数民族代表一行十余人，在十日上午离京赴天津、上海、杭州等地参观。前往车站欢送的有中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会主任委员陈叔通及中央民族事务委员会干部多人。
    这些少数民族代表来自西藏、新疆、甘肃、云南、西康、贵州、广东等地区，包括藏、维吾尔、回、苗、瑶等民族。他们在京期间，曾参观了石景山钢铁厂、清河制呢厂、华北农业科学研究所、机耕学校，并游览了首都的名胜古迹。
