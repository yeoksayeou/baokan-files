Title: Newspapers from Beijing to Southwest and Northwest Regions to Implement Airmail Delivery Starting "August 1st"
Author:
Date: 1952-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 28th: In order to enable the vast number of readers in remote areas to read newspapers published in Beijing more quickly, the Postal Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government has decided to launch airmail delivery of newspapers from Beijing to the Southwest and Northwest regions starting August 1st of this year. The airmail delivery of newspapers will initially cover four titles: People's Daily, Worker's Daily, Guangming Daily, and China Youth Daily. In the past, it took about a month for remote areas to receive newspapers from Beijing. After implementing airmail delivery, it will only take eight or nine days, or even two or three days. The airmail postage for newspapers will be uniformly charged an additional 700 yuan per copy per issue.

### 北京寄往西南西北地区报纸  “八一”开始实行航空邮递

1952-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    北京寄往西南西北地区报纸
    “八一”开始实行航空邮递
    【新华社二十八日讯】中央人民政府邮电部邮政总局为了使边远地区的广大读者能更快地看到北京出版的报纸，决定从今年八月一日起，举办由北京到西南、西北地区的报纸航空邮递业务。航空邮递报纸先开办人民日报、工人日报、光明日报和中国青年报四种。过去边远地区要一个月左右才能看到北京的报纸，实行航空邮递后，只要八、九天甚至两三天。报纸航空邮费每份每期一律加收七百元。
