    Our Great Motherland
    The sanitation work in many cities this summer has created miracles, with flies and mosquitoes almost completely eliminated. This is a picture of sanitation workers in Beijing, who are spraying insecticide into weeds and reed marshes to completely eliminate mosquito larvae.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今年夏天许多城市的卫生工作都造成了奇迹，苍蝇蚊子等几乎绝迹。这是北京市的卫生工作人员，他们正向杂草苇塘中喷洒杀虫药水，彻底消灭孑孓。
