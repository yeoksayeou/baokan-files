Title: Korean Armistice Staff Meeting Continues
Author: 
Date: 1952-07-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 30th. The staff officers of both sides at the Korean armistice negotiations continued to discuss the detailed wording of the draft armistice agreement at a meeting on the 30th.
