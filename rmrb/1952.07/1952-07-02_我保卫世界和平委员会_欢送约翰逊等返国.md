Title: China Peace Committee Welcomes Johnson and Others' Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-07-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The China Peace Committee held a reception on the 1st to bid farewell to the Dean of Canterbury Hewlett Johnson of Britain, his wife, and Oxford University International Affairs Lecturer, G.M. Gathorne-Hardy, who were invited to visit our country and are about to return home. The reception was presided over by Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee. Among those present were Zhang Xiruo, Situ Meitang, Xu Baoju, Wang Zizhong, Ma Jian, Juezan, and more than 100 people from cultural and educational circles in the capital. Vice Chairman Chen Shutong delivered a speech at the meeting, expressing his reluctance to part with Johnson and others as they returned home. He pointed out that their visit to China would help strengthen the friendship between the Chinese and British people. Dean Johnson then gave a reply, talking about his impressions of visiting New China. The reception was held in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 我保卫世界和平委员会  欢送约翰逊等返国

1952-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我保卫世界和平委员会
    欢送约翰逊等返国
    【新华社一日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会在一日举行酒会，欢送应邀前来我国参观后即将返国的英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊及其夫人和牛津大学国际问题讲师戴伊。酒会由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通主持。出席作陪的有：张奚若、司徒美堂、许宝驹、王梓仲、马坚、巨赞，以及首都文化和教育界人士一百多人。陈叔通副主席在会上致词，对约翰逊等回国表示惜别之意。他指出他们这次访问中国有助于加强中英两国人民的友谊。约翰逊教长接着致答辞，畅谈了访问新中国的印象。酒会在友好热烈气氛中进行。
