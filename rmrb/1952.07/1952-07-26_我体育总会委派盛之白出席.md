Title: All-China Sports Federation Appoints Sheng Zhizhi to Attend

Author:
Date: 1952-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: The International Amateur Basketball Federation, the International Amateur Swimming Federation, and the Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA) will each hold their representative conferences in Helsinki, Finland in July. The All-China Sports Federation has telegraphed the aforementioned federations, appointing Sheng Zhizhi as the representative to attend this year's representative conferences.

### 我体育总会委派盛之白出席

1952-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我体育总会委派盛之白出席
    【新华社二十五日讯】国际业余篮球联合会、国际业余游泳联合会、足球协会国际联合会将分别在七月间在芬兰赫尔辛基举行代表大会，中华全国体育总会已致电上述各联合会，委派盛之白为出席本届代表大会的代表。
