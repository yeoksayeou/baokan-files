Title: Guo Moruo and Others Return to Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-07-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 15th. Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation to the Special Session of the World Peace Council in Berlin, along with delegates Cai Tingkai, Zhang Bojun, Wu Yaozong, Mei Ru'ao, Rong Yiren, Deng Wenzhao, and ten staff members, returned to Beijing by plane on the 15th. They were greeted at the airport by Chen Shutong, Shen Junru, Li Siguang, Zhang Hanfu, Luo Longji, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Fan Changjiang, Wu Maosun, Cui Yueli, and many others.

### 郭沫若等昨天返抵北京

1952-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若等昨天返抵北京
    【新华社十五日讯】我国出席世界和平理事会柏林特别会议代表团团长郭沫若，代表蔡廷锴、章伯钧、吴耀宗、梅汝璈、荣毅仁、邓文钊和工作人员一行十人，十五日乘飞机返抵北京，到机场欢迎的有陈叔通、沈钧儒、李四光、章汉夫、罗隆基、许德珩、许宝驹、范长江、吴茂荪、崔月犁等多人。
