Title: Ma Yinchu and Chen Hansheng Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-07-25
Page: 1

    Ma Yinchu and Chen Hansheng Return to Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: Ma Yinchu and Chen Hansheng, Chinese council members who attended the World Peace Council's special meeting in Berlin, returned to Beijing by plane on the 24th. Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 马寅初和陈翰笙返抵北京

1952-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    马寅初和陈翰笙返抵北京
    【新华社二十四日讯】出席世界和平理事会柏林特别会议的我国理事马寅初和陈翰笙，二十四日乘飞机返抵北京，到机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一等人。
