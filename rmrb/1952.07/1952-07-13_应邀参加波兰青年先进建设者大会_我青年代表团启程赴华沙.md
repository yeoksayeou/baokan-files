Title: Our Youth Delegation Sets Off for Warsaw to Attend the Polish Youth Advanced Builders' Congress
Author:
Date: 1952-07-13
Page: 1

    Invited to Attend the Polish Youth Advanced Builders' Congress
    Our Youth Delegation Sets Off for Warsaw
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] At the invitation of the Polish Youth League, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League has dispatched a Chinese youth delegation to Poland to participate in the Polish Youth Advanced Builders' Congress to be held in Warsaw from the 20th to the 22nd of this month. The delegation departed on the 11th. The delegation is led by Yu Beichen, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Youth League Central Committee. The members include: Chen Lian, Deputy Minister of the Children's Department of the Youth League Central Committee, Chen Nianzu, Deputy Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Guangzhou Municipal Work Committee of the Youth League, Gu Faming, advanced tunneling worker and youth labor model from Northeast Xi'an Coal Mine, and Wang Linghao, steel worker and youth labor model from Anshan Iron and Steel Plant, and a translator.

### 应邀参加波兰青年先进建设者大会  我青年代表团启程赴华沙

1952-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加波兰青年先进建设者大会
    我青年代表团启程赴华沙
    【新华社十二日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会应波兰青年联盟的邀请，派遣中国青年代表团赴波兰参加本月二十日到二十二日在华沙举行的波兰青年先进建设者大会。代表团已于十一日启程。代表团由青年团中央统一战线工作部部长于北辰率领，团员有：青年团中央少年儿童部副部长陈琏，青年团广州市工作委员会宣传部副部长陈念祖，东北西安煤矿先进掘进工人、青年劳动模范谷发明和鞍山钢铁厂钢铁工人、青年劳动模范王凌好等四人及翻译一人。
