Title: Central Nationalities Visiting Delegation Arrives in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1952-07-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 16th. The Central Visiting Delegation, representing the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao to visit various minority nationalities in Northeast China, arrived in Shenyang at around 3:00 PM on the 15th, led by Peng Zemin, the head of the delegation, and Sa Kongliao, Peng Sike, and A'aisha, the deputy heads of the delegation. Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, Wang Guangwei, Secretary-General of the United Front Work Department of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Jiao Ruoyu, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, and more than 3,000 people from various minority nationality organizations and their representatives in Shenyang, including Ma Shifen (Hui nationality), Piao Baixi (Korean nationality), and Saiyin (Mongolian nationality), went to the station to welcome them and held a grand welcome meeting in front of the station. At the meeting, Vice Chairman Gao Chongmin delivered a welcome speech. He expressed his warm welcome to the Central Visiting Delegation for representing the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao in visiting the minority nationalities in Northeast China, and expressed his deep gratitude to the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao for their concern and solicitude for the nationalities in Northeast China. Peng Zemin, head of the Central Visiting Delegation, said in his reply: The fraternal nationalities in Northeast China have made great contributions to the great movement to resist US aggression and aid Korea, protect our homes and defend the country, and to the country's national defense construction and economic construction. This fully embodies the patriotism and internationalism of the people of Northeast China, which is a great victory for Chairman Mao's leadership and Chairman Mao's national policy.
    The Central Visiting Delegation will stay in Shenyang for several days and then go to various parts of Northeast China to visit minority nationalities such as the Korean, Mongolian, and Oroqen nationalities.

### 中央少数民族访问团抵沈阳

1952-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中央少数民族访问团抵沈阳
    【新华社沈阳十六日电】代表中央人民政府和毛主席来东北访问各少数民族的中央访问团，在团长彭泽民、副团长萨空了、朋斯克、阿艾沙率领下，于十五日下午三时许抵达沈阳。东北人民政府副主席高崇民、中共中央东北局统一战线工作部秘书长王光伟、沈阳市副市长焦若愚，以及沈阳市各少数民族团体及其代表马世芬（回族）、朴百锡（朝鲜族）、赛音（蒙族）等三千多人前往车站欢迎，并在车站前举行了盛大的欢迎会。会上，高崇民副主席致欢迎词。他对中央访问团代表中央人民政府和毛主席前来访问东北各少数民族，表示热烈欢迎，并对中央人民政府和毛主席对东北各民族的关怀和慰问，表示深深感谢。中央访问团团长彭泽民致答词说：东北各兄弟民族对于伟大的抗美援朝、保家卫国运动，对于祖国的国防建设、经济建设都有很大贡献。这充分体现了东北人民的爱国主义与国际主义精神，这是毛主席的领导和毛主席的民族政策的伟大胜利。
    中央访问团将在沈阳逗留数日，然后前往东北各地向朝鲜族、蒙族、鄂伦春族等少数民族进行访问。
