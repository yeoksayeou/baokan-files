    Our Great Motherland
    Mountain climbing competitions are very beneficial to the health of young children. Many young children's teams in various places often organize mountain climbing competitions. This is a scene of the mountain climbing competition held by the young children of Zhubaoxiang Primary School in Hangzhou at Geling, West Lake.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    爬山比赛对少年儿童的健康十分有益。各地许多少年儿童队都常常发起爬山比赛。这是杭州市珠宝巷小学的少年儿童在西湖葛岭举行爬山比赛的情形。
