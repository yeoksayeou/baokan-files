Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-07-25
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Along the coast of Bohai Bay in our country, there are nearly ten million mu of uncultivated virgin land, suitable for large-scale mechanized farming. The Central Ministry of Agriculture is preparing to establish state-owned mechanized farms here in a planned way. The picture shows the situation of the wasteland survey team sent by the Ministry of Agriculture surveying the soil on the coastal wasteland.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国渤海湾沿岸有近千万亩未开垦的处女地，这些土地适宜于大规模的机械耕作。中央农业部正准备在这里有计划地建立国营机耕农场。这是农业部派出的荒地勘查团正在沿海荒地上勘查土壤的情形。
