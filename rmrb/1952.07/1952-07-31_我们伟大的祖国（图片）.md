    Our Great Motherland
    The cultural studies of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have achieved significant results. Li Guangzheng, a famous combat hero, led a class in a cultural crash course school in a certain unit of the Central South Military Region, and won the glorious title of "Learning Model Class." This is a scene of Li Guangzheng and his classmates studying geography.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民解放军的文化学习有了重大的成绩。著名战斗英雄李广正，在中南军区某文化速成学校领导一个班，取得了“学习模范班”的光荣称号。这是李广正和同学们研究地理的情形。
