Title: Chinese Delegation Arrives in Moscow En Route to Helsinki Olympic Games
Author:
Date: 1952-07-29
Page: 1

    To participate in the Olympic Games in Finland
    Chinese Delegation Arrives in Moscow
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th] The Chinese delegation, consisting of forty members led by Rong Gaotang, arrived in Moscow on the 27th by plane to participate in the 15th International Olympic Games held in Helsinki, the capital of Finland. Ivanov, Vice Chairman of the Sports and Physical Culture Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers, and others welcomed the delegation at the airport and delivered welcome speeches. Many Soviet male and female athletes presented flowers to our delegation members, creating a very warm atmosphere.

### 赴芬兰参加奥林匹克运动会  我国代表团抵达莫斯科

1952-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    赴芬兰参加奥林匹克运动会
    我国代表团抵达莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科二十八日电】参加在芬兰首都赫尔辛基举行的第十五届国际奥林匹克运动会的我国代表团一行四十人，在团长荣高棠率领下于二十七日飞抵莫斯科。苏联部长会议体育与运动委员会副主席伊凡诺夫等到机场欢迎并致欢迎词。苏联男女运动员多人并向我团员献花，情况甚为热烈。
