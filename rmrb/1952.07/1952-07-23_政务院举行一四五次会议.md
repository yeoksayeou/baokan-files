Title: Government Administration Council Holds 145th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-07-23
Page: 1
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: The 145th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 18th. The meeting, after listening to the explanation given by Qian Zhiguang, Deputy Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Textile Industry, approved the ministry's work summary for 1951 and its guidelines and tasks for 1952. The meeting also approved several appointment and removal cases.

### 政务院举行一四五次会议

1952-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一四五次会议
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百四十五次政务会议在十八日下午举行。会议在听取中央人民政府纺织工业部钱之光副部长所作的说明后，批准了该部一九五一年工作总结及一九五二年方针任务。会议并通过任免案多起。
