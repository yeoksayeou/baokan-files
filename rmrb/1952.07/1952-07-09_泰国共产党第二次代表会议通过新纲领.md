Title: The Communist Party of Thailand's Second National Congress Adopts New Program
Author:
Date: 1952-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th, Bangkok: According to newspaper reports, the Communist Party of Thailand recently held its Second National Congress. The General Secretary of the Communist Party of Thailand, Nai Prasong Wongwiwet, delivered a report at the meeting entitled "Mobilize Millions of People to Participate in the Struggle for Independence and Democracy." During the discussions, the delegates reviewed the shortcomings in the Party's past work and focused on overcoming the left and right-leaning deviations in establishing a united front. Based on the current international situation and the specific domestic conditions, the meeting adopted a resolution on "The Current Demands of the People and the Current Policies of the Communist Party of Thailand" as the Party's new program. The meeting also discussed the principles of Party building and put forward suggestions for revising the Party Constitution.

The meeting elected a new Central Committee with Nai Prasong Wongwiwet as General Secretary. The meeting also issued a declaration.

### 泰国共产党第二次代表会议通过新纲领

1952-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    泰国共产党第二次代表会议通过新纲领
    【新华社七日讯】曼谷消息：据报纸刊载，泰国共产党最近举行了第二次全国代表会议。泰国共产党总书记乃巴颂·旺威越在会上作了“动员千百万人民参加争取独立和民主的斗争”的报告。代表们在讨论中检讨了过去党的工作中的缺点，并着重地讨论了克服在建立统一战线上的左右倾偏向问题。会议根据当前的国际形势和国内的具体情况，通过了“目前人民的要求和泰国共产党当前的政策”的决议，作为党的新纲领。会议并讨论了建党原则和提出了修改党章的建议。
    会议选出了以乃巴颂·旺威越为总书记的新的中央委员会。会议并发表了宣言。
