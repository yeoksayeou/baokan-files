Title: National Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Representative Meeting Presents Flag to Chairman Mao as a Tribute

Author:
Date: 1952-07-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. At the Central People's Government's State Council Auditorium on the afternoon of the 30th, Chen Shutong, Nan Hanchen, Song Zichun, Liu Peizhi, Yang Haicheng, Abulizi (Uyghur), Qian Sunqing, Chen Jingshe (Hui), Tang Zhijing (Yao), Peng Shaonong, Dun Kang·Gesang Wangjun (Tibetan), Yi Meihou and others, representing the Preparatory Representative Meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, presented a flag to Chairman Mao as a tribute. At the same time, Gui Xiangba·Gongga Najia and Sanduo Cang·Losang Genden, representatives of the Tibet region attending this meeting, represented the Dalai Lama, and Dun Kang·Gesang Wangjun and Luo Youren (Tibetan) represented the Panchen Erdeni, to present gifts to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou respectively. Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou personally met with the representatives and accepted the flag and gifts. Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Lin Boqu, Secretary-General, Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the State Council, Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court, Li Weihan, Secretary-General of the State Council and concurrently Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government, also attended the flag presentation and gift-giving ceremony. Persons in charge of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, various democratic parties, people's organizations, and responsible cadres of central government agencies, as well as all representatives attending the Preparatory Representative Meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, totaling several hundred people, participated in the ceremony.

### 全国工商联筹备代表会议  向毛主席献旗致敬

1952-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商联筹备代表会议
    向毛主席献旗致敬
    【新华社三十日讯】中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议三十日下午由陈叔通、南汉宸、宋子纯、刘珮芝、杨海成、阿不力支（维族）、钱荪卿、陈经畲（回族）、唐志精（瑶族）、彭劭农、顿康·格桑旺君（藏族）、蚁美厚等为代表，在中央人民政府政务院礼堂向毛主席献旗。同时，出席这次会议的西藏地区代表桂香巴·贡尕那嘉、三多仓·罗桑格登代表达赖喇嘛，顿康·格桑旺君、罗友仁（藏族）代表班禅额尔德尼，分别向毛主席和周总理献礼。毛主席和周总理亲自接见各代表并接受献旗、献礼。出席这次献旗、献礼仪式的还有中央人民政府副主席李济深、秘书长林伯渠、政务院副总理董必武、最高人民法院院长沈钧儒、中央人民政府政务院秘书长兼民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉、中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清等。参加典礼仪式的有中国人民政协全国委员会、各民主党派、人民团体负责人和中央各机关的负责干部以及出席中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议的全体代表共数百人。
