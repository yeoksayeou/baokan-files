Title: Japan's "Police Reserve Force" of 1,000 Men, Trained by the U.S. Military, to be Secretly Dispatched to Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 17th. TASS Tokyo, July 17th dispatch: According to the Japanese Democratic Youth League's newspaper "Young Warriors," one thousand personnel from the "Police Reserve Force" will be secretly dispatched to Korea on July 20th. They have already undergone two months of intense military training at the U.S. military camp in Sagamihara, Gunma Prefecture.

The Central Committee of the Democratic Youth League has lodged a protest, pointing out that this measure is part of the U.S. imperialists' military plan to drag Japan into the war.

### 日本“警察后备队”一千人  受美军训练将秘密派往朝鲜

1952-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    日本“警察后备队”一千人
    受美军训练将秘密派往朝鲜
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社东京十七日讯：据日本民主青年团机关报“年轻的战士”称：一千名“警察后备队”人员将于七月二十日秘密派往朝鲜。他们已在群马县相模原的美国军营受了两个月紧张的军事训练。
    民主青年团中央委员会提出了抗议，指出这种措施是企图把日本拖入战争的美帝国主义者的军事计划的一部分。
