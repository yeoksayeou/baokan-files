Title: Two Chinese Delegations Attend the 18th International Red Cross Conference, En Route to Canada via Prague
Author:
Date: 1952-07-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 23rd: The Chinese government delegation and the Chinese Red Cross delegation, participating in the 18th International Red Cross Conference, departed Prague by plane on the 22nd, heading to Toronto, Canada, via Brussels, Belgium, to attend the conference.

### 出席第十八届国际红十字大会  我国两代表团经布拉格赴加

1952-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    出席第十八届国际红十字大会
    我国两代表团经布拉格赴加
    【新华社布拉格二十三日电】参加第十八届国际红十字大会的我国政府代表团和中国红十字会代表团，于二十二日乘飞机离布拉格，取道比利时的布鲁塞尔前往加拿大多伦多赴会。
