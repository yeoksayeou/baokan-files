Title: US's New Conspiracy to Screen Prisoners of War: Attempting to Transport Our Prisoners of War to the Marshall Islands
Author:
Date: 1952-07-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, July 29th (TASS): "Today's News" published a dispatch from its Tokyo correspondent, reporting a new criminal conspiracy by the American aggressors against Korean and Chinese prisoners of war. The dispatch stated: According to American reporters recently arriving in Tokyo from Busan, the US military authorities on Geoje Island, under orders from the US Department of Defense, are conducting screenings of Chinese and Korean prisoners of war in order to transport them to the Marshall Islands in the Pacific Ocean. The dispatch stated: The Americans will use these prisoners of war to test various weapons to determine their effectiveness in future wars.

### 美方甄别战俘的新阴谋  企图将我方战俘运往马绍尔群岛

1952-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    美方甄别战俘的新阴谋
    企图将我方战俘运往马绍尔群岛
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩二十九日讯：“今天报”刊载该报驻东京记者的通讯，报道美国侵略者对朝鲜与中国战俘的新的罪恶阴谋。通讯说：据最近从釜山到东京的美国记者透露，巨济岛上的美军当局奉美国国防部的命令，已在对中国和朝鲜的战俘进行甄别，以便把他们运往太平洋上的马绍尔群岛。通讯说：美国人将用这些战俘试验各种武器，以判断这些武器在将来战争时的效力究竟如何。
