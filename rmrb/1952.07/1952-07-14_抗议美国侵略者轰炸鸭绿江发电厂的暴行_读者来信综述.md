Title: Protesting the Atrocity of American Aggressors Bombing the Yalu River Power Plant – A Summary of Readers' Letters
Author:
Date: 1952-07-14
Page: 2

Readers' Letters

    Protesting the Atrocity of American Aggressors Bombing the Yalu River Power Plant
    -- A Summary of Readers' Letters
    Readers from all over the country have written letters protesting the atrocity of American imperialism in bombing our peaceful Yalu River Power Plant. They are willing to continue to support the Korean front with practical actions and strive to restore the hydroelectric station's project.
    All the technical personnel of the Comprehensive Industrial Testing Institute of the Central Ministry of Heavy Industry wrote in a letter: We must, together with the people of the whole country, carry out the campaign to increase production and practice economy, strengthen planning, improve work efficiency, and strive to do a good job in resisting America and aiding Korea. We are willing to use all our strength to cooperate with the Korean people to rebuild the damaged parts of the hydroelectric station to show our determination to support the cause of peace and our serious protest against American imperialism.
    The first session of the second district people's representatives conference in Beijing, representing 160,000 people, guaranteed to actively participate in the patriotic production increase and economy campaign and the patriotic health campaign, to continue to support the Korean war of resistance against aggression with practical actions, to respond to the atrocities of American imperialism.
    All the students of the Electrical Engineering Class of the Factory Management Department of the China People's University wrote in a letter expressing their determination to turn their indignation into strength together with the people of the whole country, to study harder, and to strive for the construction of the country's electrical industry.
    The professors of the Hydraulics Group of the Civil Engineering Department of Tsinghua University and all the graduating students were extremely angry when they heard about the shameless atrocities of American imperialism. They unanimously requested to be assigned to the Yalu River to take on the glorious task of restoring the hydroelectric station.
    Zhou Zhenqing, a clerk at the Xiechang Cloth Shop in Yuanling, Hunan, wrote in a letter: This extremely shameless crime of the American aggressors will surely be severely punished by the Chinese and Korean people and the people of the world.

### 抗议美国侵略者轰炸鸭绿江发电厂的暴行——读者来信综述

1952-07-14
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    抗议美国侵略者轰炸鸭绿江发电厂的暴行
    ——读者来信综述
    全国各地读者纷纷来信，对美帝国主义轰炸我和平设备鸭绿江发电厂的暴行提出抗议，并愿以实际行动继续支援朝鲜前线，并为恢复水电站的工程而努力。
    中央重工业部综合工业试验所全体技术人员来信说：我们要和全国人民一致开展增产节约运动，加强计划性，提高工作效率，努力做好抗美援朝工作，并愿以一切力量协同朝鲜人民努力重建水电站被破坏部分，来表示我们拥护和平事业的决心，和对美帝国主义的严重抗议。
    北京一区第二届第一次各界人民代表会议，代表十六万人民保证积极地参加爱国增产节约运动和爱国卫生运动，以实际行动继续支援朝鲜反侵略战争，来回答美帝国主义的暴行。
    中国人民大学本科工厂管理系电业班全体同学来信表示，决与全国人民一道将愤恨变为力量，加紧学习，为建设祖国的电气事业而努力。
    清华大学土木系水力组教授和即将毕业的全体同学听到美帝国主义的无耻暴行，万分愤怒，他们一致要求分配到鸭绿江边去，担负恢复水电站的光荣工作。
    湖南沅陵协昌布店店员周振清来信说：美国侵略者这一极端无耻的罪行，一定会受到中朝人民及全世界人民的严厉的惩罚。
