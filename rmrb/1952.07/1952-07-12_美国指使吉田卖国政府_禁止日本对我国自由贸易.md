Title: The United States Directs the Traitorous Yoshida Government to Ban Japan's Free Trade with Our Country
Author: 
Date: 1952-07-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 11th: TASS Tokyo, July 11th: According to the "Mainichi Shimbun", the U.S. government recently notified the Japanese government through the U.S. Embassy in Tokyo that the U.S. government rejected Japan's request to lift the ban on exporting certain items to China.

### 美国指使吉田卖国政府  禁止日本对我国自由贸易

1952-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美国指使吉田卖国政府
    禁止日本对我国自由贸易
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社东京十一日讯：据“每日新闻”报道，美国政府最近曾通过东京美国大使馆通知日本政府说，美国政府拒绝日本所提的解除禁止若干种物品对中国输出的禁令的要求。
