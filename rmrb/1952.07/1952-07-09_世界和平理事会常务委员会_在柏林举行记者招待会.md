Title: World Peace Council Standing Committee Holds Press Conference in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1952-07-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin: The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council held a press conference in Berlin on the 7th, attended by many German and foreign journalists. At the meeting, Farge first delivered a speech, and Ehrenburg (Soviet Union), Farge (France), Donini (Italy), Darr (United States), D'Arboussier (Africa), Fiallo (Brazil), Montagu (Britain) and others answered questions raised by the reporters. They explained the great significance of the resolutions passed by the special meeting of the World Peace Council, and answered questions related to the German problem, the use of bacteriological weapons by the United States in Korea, disarmament, and the World People's Peace Congress scheduled to be held in December this year.

### 世界和平理事会常务委员会  在柏林举行记者招待会

1952-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常务委员会
    在柏林举行记者招待会
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：世界和平理事会常务委员会于七日在柏林举行记者招待会，到会的有德国和外国记者多人。会上首先由法奇致词，爱伦堡（苏联）、法奇（法国）、多尼尼（意大利）、达尔（美国）、达波赛（非洲）、费雅洛（巴西）、蒙塔古（英国）等人分别答复了记者们所提出的问题。他们说明了世界和平理事会特别会议所通过的各项决议的重大意义，并答复了有关德国问题、美国在朝鲜使用细菌武器、裁减军备及定在今年十二月举行的世界人民和平大会等方面的问题。
