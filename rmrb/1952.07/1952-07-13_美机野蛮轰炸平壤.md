Title: U.S. Planes Barbarically Bomb Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1952-07-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 11th: On July 11th, American planes carried out a barbaric and concentrated bombing and strafing of Pyongyang. During the afternoon air raid, more than eighty American planes participated in the two-and-a-half-hour bombing and strafing, dropping a total of more than six hundred bombs on the city.

The purpose of this bombing of Pyongyang by American planes was to destroy residential and public buildings and to massacre the residents of Pyongyang. American planes strafed residential areas in the city and dropped a large number of delayed-action bombs. American aerial bandits used machine guns to strafe the market in western Pyongyang. In the Kyongsang-ri area, the homes of peaceful residents and a hospital were bombed.

### 美机野蛮轰炸平壤

1952-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    美机野蛮轰炸平壤
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社平壤十一日讯：七月十一日，美国飞机对平壤进行了野蛮的集中轰炸和扫射。在下午的空袭中，有八十多架美国飞机参加了这次延续两个半小时的轰炸和扫射，共在这个城市投掷了六百多个炸弹。
    美国飞机对平壤进行的这次轰炸，其目的在于毁坏住宅和公共建筑物，并屠杀平壤居民。美国飞机扫射市内居民区，并投下大量延期爆炸弹。美国空中强盗用机枪扫射位于平壤西部的市场。在庆尚里一带，和平居民的住宅和一个医院遭到轰炸。
