The Fly's Lament
(A quick-rhythm poem from a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army)

　　I, a fly, am born filthy,
　　Where there is filth, there I hide.
　　Don't look down on my appearance,
　　Truman has a good impression of me.
　　He suffered defeat in Korea,
　　So he kowtowed and begged me to help.
　　The Chemical Corps manufactures germs,
　　These germs are loaded onto my body,
　　Airplanes drop me on the battlefield,
　　And I spread the germs everywhere.
　　Whoever's sanitation is not good,
　t let them off when they run into me:
　　I will immediately spread germs on them,
　　Making them sick, if not to death!
　　Since I arrived on the Korean battlefield,
　　Things are very different from what the war criminals thought!
　　The Chinese People's Volunteer Army does excellent sanitation work,
　　It's like casting a net of heaven and earth.
　　Filthy things cannot be found,
　　Bowls, chopsticks, food, and meals are all hidden in cupboards;
　　DDT is sprayed all over me,
　　Everywhere there's the sound of fly swatters going "ping pong."
　　My brothers are dying all over the place,
　　Truman is very sad!
　　The Korean War relies on me,
　　Now his heart is cold!
　　If sanitation work continues regularly,
　　My life will also see King Yama;
　　I only hope that someone's sanitation work is not done well,
　　And sloppy people are my good targets;
　　I only hope that the sanitation work there slacks off a bit,
　　To give me a chance to act.
　　I keep my eyes wide open and watch carefully,
　　This is my only small hope.

### 苍蝇自叹

1952-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苍蝇自叹
    （志愿军某部快板诗）
    我苍蝇生来就肮脏，
    那里肮脏我那里藏。
    别看我长得不像样，
    杜鲁门对我有好印象。
    他在朝鲜遭受失败，
    就磕头作揖请我帮忙。
    化学兵团制造毒菌，
    毒菌就往我身上装，
    飞机投我到战场上，
    我再把毒菌到处扬。
    谁的卫生做得不好，
    碰着我苍蝇我不能让：
    我马上给他撒毒菌，
    叫他不死也病一场！
    自从我到了朝鲜战场上，
    和战犯们想的大两样！
    志愿军卫生工作做得好，
    好像撒下了天罗地网。
    肮脏的东西找不到，
    碗筷菜饭都在橱里藏；
    滴滴涕往我身上撒，
    到处是苍蝇拍子乒乓响。
    众弟兄死的遍地是，
    杜鲁门心里好悲伤！
    朝鲜战争依靠着俺，
    这下他心里凉又凉。
    卫生工作保持经常，
    我的命也要见阎王；
    我只盼谁的卫生工作做不好，
    拉塌人是我的好对象；
    我只盼那儿卫生工作松点劲，
    让我有机会活动一场。
    瞪大眼睛我留神看，
    只有这一点点小希望。
