Title: China Youth Art Troupe Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-07-31
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency report: The China Youth Art Troupe, which went abroad in July 1951 to participate in the Third World Youth and Student Peace Festival in Berlin, returned to Beijing on the 25th, led by the head of the troupe, Zhou Weizhi. The performances of the China Youth Art Troupe in various artistic competitions at the Peace Festival received high praise. The dance won the collective first prize, the composition and male bass solo won the second prize, the flute and piano solos won the third prize, and the female soprano solo won the "Excellent Performance" certificate. The acrobatics program was praised by the conference. After the festival, the troupe visited nine countries, including the Soviet Union, and performed 418 shows in these countries, with an audience of 2.3 million. Some members of the troupe who went to Albania to perform will return to China in late August.

### 我国青年文工团返抵北京

1952-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我国青年文工团返抵北京
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：在一九五一年七月出国参加柏林第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节的中国青年文工团在团长周巍峙率领下，已于二十五日回到北京。中国青年文工团参加和平联欢节各种艺术竞赛的表演节目曾获得很高的评价，舞蹈得集体一等奖，作曲及男低音独唱得二等奖，长笛及钢琴独奏得三等奖，女高音独唱得“优秀表演”奖状。杂技节目受到了大会的表扬。在联欢节后，该团曾访问了苏联等九个国家，并在这些国家演出四百一十八场，观众达二百三十万人。前往阿尔巴尼亚表演的一部分团员将在八月下旬返国。
