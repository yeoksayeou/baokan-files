Title: China Red Cross Society Donates 2 Billion Yuan to Relieve Famine-Stricken People in India
Author:
Date: 1952-07-01
Page: 4

    China Red Cross Society
    Donates 2 Billion Yuan to Relieve Famine-Stricken People in India
    【Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 30th】 Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, on behalf of the Red Cross Society of China, donated RMB 2 billion, equivalent to 421,941 Indian rupees, to relieve the famine-stricken people in India. The donation was delivered to Mrs. Kaur, President of the Indian Red Cross Society, on June 21st by Shen Jian, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in India.
    In her reply to President Li Dequan's letter, Mrs. Kaur said that she herself and the Indian Red Cross Society are very grateful for the generous and noble assistance from the Chinese Red Cross Society.
    Note: This donation was made by the Red Cross Society of China in implementing the resolution of the Executive Committee of the League of Red Cross Societies at the Geneva Conference to relieve the famine in India, and was raised through donations from all parts of China.

### 我国红十字总会  赠款二十亿元救济印度粮荒区域人民

1952-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我国红十字总会
    赠款二十亿元救济印度粮荒区域人民
    【新华社新德里三十日电】中国红十字总会会长李德全代表中国红十字总会赠送救济印度粮荒区域人民的赠款人民币二十亿元，折合印度卢比四十二万一千九百四十一盾，已在六月二十一日由中国驻印度大使馆申健代办送交印度红十字会会长考尔夫人。
    考尔夫人在答复李德全会长的信中说，她本人和印度红十字会十分感谢中国红十字总会的慷慨和高贵的帮助。
    按：这笔赠款是中国红十字总会执行了国际红十字会协会执行委员会在日内瓦会议上所提出的救济印度灾荒的决议，在中国各地募捐得来的。
