Title: Nationwide Preparations in Indonesia for Indonesian Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-03-01
Page: 4

    TASS Jakarta, 26th Dispatch: “People's Daily” reports that preparations are underway throughout Indonesia for the Indonesian Communist Party Congress to be held in the latter half of March. Workers, peasants, and youths are all very concerned about this upcoming Party Congress and are studying the draft Party Program and the report by Party General Secretary Aidit. In the Yogyakarta area alone, more than 120,000 copies of the draft Party Program and Aidit's report have been distributed.

### 印尼全国人民筹备印尼共产党代表大会

1954-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼全国人民筹备印尼共产党代表大会
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社雅加达二十六日讯：“人民日报”报道，印尼全国各地正在筹备将于三月下半月召开的印尼共产党代表大会。工人、农民和青年们都非常关怀这个即将到来的党代表大会，并正在研究党纲草案和党总书记艾地的报告。仅在日惹一地，党纲草案和艾地的报告就发出了十二万多份。
