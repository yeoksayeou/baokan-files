Title: Comrade Molotov Meets with Voters and Delivers Speech
Author:
Date: 1954-03-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to TASS, V.M. Molotov, candidate for deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, met with voters in the Molotov electoral district of Moscow on the 11th in the Hall of Columns of the Trade Union Building. Workers from industry and transportation, office workers, scientists, and cultural and artistic workers attended the meeting. When Molotov took his seat on the rostrum, he was warmly welcomed by all those present.

Voters spoke at the meeting, including: Kazakova, a female worker from the Molotov Machine Building Factory; Ostrovsky, a staff member of the USSR Ministry of Coal Industry; Dr. of Biology, Filatova; and locomotive driver Rostovtsev. They spoke of Molotov's long-term outstanding contributions to the country and his efforts to defend world peace, and pointed out the heartfelt love and infinite admiration of the entire Soviet people for Molotov. They also called on all voters to vote for the candidates nominated by the League of Communists and Non-Party Members.

Subsequently, Molotov delivered a speech at the meeting, which was warmly welcomed by those present.

### 莫洛托夫同志会见选民发表演说

1954-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫同志会见选民发表演说
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人、苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫，十一日在工会大厦圆柱大厅和莫斯科莫洛托夫选区的选民会面。参加大会的有工业和运输业的工人、机关工作者、科学家和文化艺术工作者。当莫洛托夫在主席台上就座时，受到了全体到会者的热烈欢迎。
    选民们纷纷在会上讲了话。其中包括：莫洛托夫机器制造工厂女工卡扎科娃、苏联煤矿工业部的工作人员奥斯特洛夫斯基、生物学博士费拉托娃、机车司机罗斯托夫采夫等。他们谈到了莫洛托夫长时期以来对国家的卓越贡献和为保卫世界和平而作的努力，并指出了全苏联人民对莫洛托夫的衷心爱戴和无限敬仰。他们并号召全体选民投票选举党和非党联盟所提出的候选人。
    接着，莫洛托夫在会上发表了演说，受到到会者的热烈欢迎。
