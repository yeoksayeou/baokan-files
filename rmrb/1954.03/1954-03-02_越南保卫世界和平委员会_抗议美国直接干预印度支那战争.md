Title: Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace Protests Direct U.S. Intervention in the Indochina War
Author:
Date: 1954-03-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 28th: The Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace vehemently protests the direct U.S. intervention in the war of aggression in Vietnam.

	The protest stated that in the past few years, U.S. war-mongers have provided increasing military "aid" to the French colonialists, and they have been intervening ever more brazenly in the wars of aggression in Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos. Their aim in doing so is to obtain greater profits and to turn this peninsula into a U.S. strategic base and colony.

	Recently, the United States has further intervened in the Indochina War, dispatching U.S. aircraft to slaughter the people of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos. The hundreds of so-called technicians the United States has sent to Indochina are in reality U.S. Air Force officers and personnel.

	At a time when people all over the world are actively struggling to defend peace and ease international tensions, U.S. war-mongers are colluding with French colonialists in an attempt to prolong and expand the war of aggression in Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos. This not only violates the sovereignty of these countries, but also seriously threatens the security of the people of Southeast Asian countries. Like the French colonialists, the U.S. imperialists have once again exposed themselves as destroyers of world peace and enemies of countries struggling for independence and freedom.

	However, no matter what vicious conspiracies the American and French imperialists may resort to, the Vietnamese people will fight more resolutely to win national independence and contribute to the defense of peace in Asia and the world. Under the leadership of President Ho Chi Minh, in close unity with the peoples of Cambodia and Laos, and with the sympathy and support of the people of the world, the Vietnamese people will surely win final victory.

### 越南保卫世界和平委员会  抗议美国直接干预印度支那战争

1954-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    越南保卫世界和平委员会
    抗议美国直接干预印度支那战争
   【新华社一日讯】据越南通讯社二月二十八日讯：越南保卫世界和平委员会激烈抗议美国直接干预越南的侵略战争。
    抗议书说，在过去几年中，美国战争贩子给法国殖民者以越来越多的军事“援助”，他们越来越猖狂地干预越南、高棉和寮国的侵略战争。他们这样做的目的是要获得更大的利润，并把这个半岛变为美国的战略基地和殖民地。
    最近，美国又进一步干预印度支那战争，派遣了美国飞机屠杀越南、高棉和寮国人民。美国派往印度支那的数百名所谓技术人员，实际上是美国的空军军官与人员。
    正当全世界人民都在为保卫和平与缓和国际紧张局势而积极斗争的时候，美国战争贩子和法国殖民者勾结，企图拖延和扩大越南、高棉和寮国的侵略战争，这不仅侵犯了这些国家的主权，还严重地威胁了东南亚国家人民的安全。美帝国主义者就像法国殖民者一样，再次暴露了他们自己是世界和平的破坏者，是正在为独立与自由而斗争的国家的敌人。
    但是，不管美国和法国帝国主义者会使出怎样恶毒的阴谋，越南人民将更坚决地斗争，以争取国家的独立，为保卫亚洲与全世界和平作出贡献。在胡志明主席领导下，在与高棉、寮国人民紧密团结之下，在全世界人民的同情与支持之下，越南人民一定可以获得最后的胜利。
