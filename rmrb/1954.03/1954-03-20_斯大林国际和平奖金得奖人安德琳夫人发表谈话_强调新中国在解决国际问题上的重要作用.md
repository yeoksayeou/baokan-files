Title: Madame Andrée, Stalin International Peace Prize Laureate, Issues Statement Emphasizing the Important Role of New China in Solving International Problems
Author:
Date: 1954-03-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, March 17th: The newspaper "Life and Peace" published a statement by André Andreen, winner of the Stalin International Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations", regarding the role played by the People's Republic of China in resolving international issues.

      Madame André Andreen said: The whole world knows that the People's Republic of China must be allowed to represent China in the United Nations Organization as soon as possible. This is not only for fairness to China, but mainly for the United Nations Organization, that is, for peace.

      Madame André Andreen emphasized the important role played by New China in solving international problems. She said: Without considering the voice of a quarter of the world's population, one cannot speak of a full expression of world public opinion.

### 斯大林国际和平奖金得奖人安德琳夫人发表谈话  强调新中国在解决国际问题上的重要作用

1954-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林国际和平奖金得奖人安德琳夫人发表谈话
    强调新中国在解决国际问题上的重要作用
      【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社斯德哥尔摩十七日讯：“生活与和平报”刊载了“加强国际和平”
  斯大林国际奖金得奖人安德烈·安德琳夫人的一篇谈话，谈到中华人民共和国在解决国际问题上所
  起的作用。
      安德烈·安德琳夫人说：全世界都知道必须尽快地让中华人民共和国在联合国组织中代表中
  国。这不仅是为了对中国的公平，而主要是为了联合国组织，也就是为了和平。
      安德烈·安德琳夫人强调指出新中国在解决国际问题上起的重要作用。她说：不考虑世界四分
  之一人口的呼声，世界舆论的意见便谈不上充分表达出来。
