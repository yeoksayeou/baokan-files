Title: Good Rain in Major Grain-Producing Areas of Yunnan Province
Author:
Date: 1954-03-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Yunnan Province experienced widespread spring rainfall between March 15th and 21st. The major grain-producing areas of Yuxi, Yiliang, Kunming, Dali, Chuxiong, Mengzi, Yuanjiang, Wenshan, Lancang, and Cheli, the capital of the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Region, all received rain. This spring rain is very helpful for the spring harvest crops, and in some southern areas, it is already possible to sow seeds and transplant rice seedlings in a timely manner.

### 云南省主要产粮地区喜雨

1954-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    云南省主要产粮地区喜雨
    据新华社讯：云南省在三月十五日到二十一日间普降春雨。主要产粮地区玉溪、宜良、昆明、大理、楚雄、蒙自、元江、文山、澜沧及西双版纳傣族自治区首府车里等地都下了雨。这场春雨对小春作物很有帮助，南部有些地区已可及时撒种栽秧。
