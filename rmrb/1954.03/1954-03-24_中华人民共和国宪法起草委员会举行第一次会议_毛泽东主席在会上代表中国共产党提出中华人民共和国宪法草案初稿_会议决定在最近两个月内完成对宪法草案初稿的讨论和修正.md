Title: The Preparatory Committee for the Constitution of the People's Republic of China Holds its First Meeting; Chairman Mao Zedong Presents the Initial Draft of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China on behalf of the Chinese Communist Party; The Meeting Decides to Complete the Discussion and Revision of the Initial Draft of the Constitution within the Next Two Months
Author:
Date: 1954-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: The first meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Constitution of the People's Republic of China was held on the afternoon of the 23rd. The meeting was presided over by Chairman Mao Zedong. The attendees included Chairman Mao Zedong of the Constitution Drafting Committee, and members Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Li Weihan, He Xiangning, Shen Junru, Shen Yanbing, Zhou Enlai, Hu Qiaomu, Ulanhu, Ma Yinchu, Ma Xulun, Chen Yun, Chen Shutong, Chen Boda, Zhang Lan, Guo Moruo, Xi Zhongxun, Huang Yanpei, Peng Dehuai, Cheng Qian, Dong Biwu, Liu Shaoqi, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, Bo Yibo, a total of twenty-six people.
    At the meeting, Chairman Mao Zedong presented the initial draft of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, drafted by the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, on behalf of the Chinese Communist Party. After hearing the explanation of the initial draft of the constitution by Committee Member Chen Boda, the meeting decided to complete the discussion and revision of the initial draft of the constitution within the next two months, so as to submit it to the Central People's Government Committee for approval and promulgation as a draft. In addition to the plenary meeting of the Constitution Drafting Committee, group discussions will also be held in conjunction with the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and will be distributed to the leading organs of major administrative regions and provinces and cities, as well as the local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations for discussion. ### 中华人民共和国宪法起草委员会举行第一次会议  毛泽东主席在会上代表中国共产党提出中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿  会议决定在最近两个月内完成对宪法草案初稿的讨论和修正

1954-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国宪法起草委员会举行第一次会议
    毛泽东主席在会上代表中国共产党提出中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿
    会议决定在最近两个月内完成对宪法草案初稿的讨论和修正
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华人民共和国宪法起草委员会第一次会议在二十三日下午举行。会议由毛泽东主席主持。出席会议的有宪法起草委员会主席毛泽东，委员宋庆龄、李济深、李维汉、何香凝、沈钧儒、沈雁冰、周恩来、胡乔木、乌兰夫、马寅初、马叙伦、陈云、陈叔通、陈伯达、张澜、郭沫若、习仲勋、黄炎培、彭德怀、程潜、董必武、刘少奇、邓小平、邓子恢、薄一波等共二十六人。
    会上，毛泽东主席代表中国共产党提出了中共中央所起草的中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿。会议在听了陈伯达委员关于宪法草案初稿的说明后，决定在最近两个月内完成对宪法草案初稿的讨论和修正，以便提请中央人民政府委员会作为草案批准公布。讨论除由宪法起草委员会全体会议进行外，并会同中国人民政协全国委员会进行分组讨论，同时分发各大行政区、各省市的领导机关和各民主党派、各人民团体的地方组织讨论。（附图片）
