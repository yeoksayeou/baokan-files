Title: Lao People's Liberation Army Annihilates Over Two Hundred Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1954-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Laos, 27th: The Vietnam News Agency reported from Laos on the 27th that on March 22nd, the Lao People's Liberation Army ambushed the first battalion of the enemy's Foreign Legion Fifth Regiment, stationed in the Kong Keng area, sixty kilometers southeast of Thakhek, in central Laos, annihilating 298 enemy troops, including Battalion Commander Borden de Shack and other officers. They also captured a large quantity of weapons and ammunition.

### 寮国人民解放军歼敌两百多名

1954-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    寮国人民解放军歼敌两百多名
   【新华社二十八日讯】据越南通讯社寮国二十七日讯：三月二十二日，寮国人民解放军在中寮伏击属于驻扎在他曲东南六十公里的孔肯地区的外籍军团第五团的敌军第一营，歼灭敌军二百九十八名，其中包括营长伯登·德夏克和另一些军官，还缴获了大量的武器和弹药。
