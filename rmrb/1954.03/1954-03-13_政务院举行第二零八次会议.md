Title: The State Council Holds its 208th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 11th: The 208th meeting of the Central People's Government Council of State was held on the afternoon of the 11th. The meeting heard and approved the "Report on Publishing Work in 1953 and the Guiding Principles and Tasks for 1954" and the "Publishing Construction Plan for 1954" presented by Hu Yuzhi, Director of the Central People's Government Publishing Administration. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal lists.

### 政务院举行第二零八次会议

1954-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二零八次会议
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百零八次政务会议在十一日下午举行。会议听了和批准了中央人民政府出版总署署长胡愈之所作关于“一九五三年出版工作和一九五四年方针任务的报告”和“一九五四年出版建设计划”的报告。会议并通过了各项任免名单。
