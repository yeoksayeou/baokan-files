Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-03-03
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Gaokan and Xinglong agricultural producers' cooperatives in the suburbs of Shenyang have jointly formulated a plan to increase production per mu of land by 27.6 percent (excluding paddy fields) on top of last year's increase, and to expand the vegetable planting area to 83 percent to meet the needs of national construction. This shows members of the Xinglong Agricultural Production Cooperative actively improving the soil, putting sandy soil into clay soil to loosen the soil, and preparing for spring plowing.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    沈阳市郊高坎、兴隆两个农业生产合作社已联合订出每亩土地在去年增产的基础上再增产百分之二十七点六（不包括水田在内）的增产计划，并把蔬菜的种植面积扩大到百分之八十三，以供应国家建设的需要。这是兴隆农业生产合作社的社员们，正积极改良土壤，把砂土放在黏土里，使土质松散，作好春耕前的准备工作。
