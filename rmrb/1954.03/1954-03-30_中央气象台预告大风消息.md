Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Forecasts Gale Message
Author:
Date: 1954-03-30
Page: 1

    Central Meteorological Observatory Forecasts Gale Message
    [Xinhua News Agency, 29th] The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a gale message at 8:00 PM on the 29th: It is expected that in the next 24 hours, there will be gales of about force 6 in central and northern Inner Mongolia and throughout Northeast China.

### 中央气象台预告大风消息

1954-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台预告大风消息
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央气象台二十九日下午八时发布大风消息：预计未来二十四小时内，内蒙古中部、北部和东北全区有六级左右的大风。
