Title: Widespread Activities Across North China to Comfort the People's Liberation Army

Author: 
Date: 1954-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: The Sixth General Sub-Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army and its affiliated sub-delegations have launched extensive and in-depth comforting activities in various parts of North China.

The Sixth General Sub-Delegation and the sub-delegations of various provinces and cities have successively held comfort meetings from February 21st to 23rd, respectively, to comfort all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army North China Military Region, Hebei Provincial Military Region, Shanxi Provincial Military Region, Mengsui Military Region, and Tianjin Garrison. Representatives of the comfort delegations also went deep to comfort the soldiers guarding the North China coastal defense line. At each comfort meeting, the representatives of the comfort delegations conveyed the care and expectations of the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, Chairman Mao, and the people of the whole country to the People's Liberation Army, and reported to the PLA the general line of the country during the transition period and the great achievements in various fields.

Representatives of the comfort delegations also went deep into the organs, companies, schools, hospitals, etc. of various troops to conduct comfort activities. Representatives of the comfort delegations and labor models among the representatives held symposiums with heroes and meritorious individuals of the troops, and reported their model deeds to each other. The soldiers of a certain anti-aircraft unit guarding the airspace of the capital day and night gave a combat exercise for the comfort delegation. The representatives also visited the exhibition hall set up by the soldiers of the anti-aircraft unit for the comfort delegation. The representatives of the comfort delegation also warmly comforted a certain infantry unit that had been reviewed by Chairman Mao three times on National Day, and visited the August 1st School of the North China Military Region.

When the representatives of the Third Sub-Delegation of the Sixth General Sub-Delegation comforted the troops stationed in Tianjin, they also accompanied the soldiers to visit ten major factories in Tianjin and swept the tombs at the Tianjin Martyrs Cemetery. Yang Yupuo, the father of Yang Lian’di, a first-class hero of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and many labor models went to various troops to report on their model deeds and the care received by the families of martyrs and soldiers from the government and the respect from people of all walks of life. Wu De, Deputy Head of the Sixth General Sub-Delegation and Head of the Third Sub-Delegation (Tianjin Sub-Delegation), went deep into the cooking squad of a certain PLA unit to conduct comfort activities and presented flowers to Wu Chunjiang, a model cook. The representatives of the Third Sub-Delegation also comforted the wounded and sick soldiers of a certain PLA hospital. Representatives of the Sixth General Sub-Delegation and its affiliated Fourth Sub-Delegation (Inner Mongolia Sub-Delegation), Fifth Sub-Delegation (Hebei Sub-Delegation), and Sixth Sub-Delegation (Shanxi Sub-Delegation) also launched in-depth comfort activities in Tangshan, Qinhuangdao, Shijiazhuang, Zhangjiakou, Handan, Datong, Changzhi, Linfen and other places, respectively.

### 华北区各地慰问解放军的活动广泛展开

1954-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    华北区各地慰问解放军的活动广泛展开
    据新华社讯：全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团第六总分团和所属各分团，在华北各地展开了广泛深入的慰问活动。
    第六总分团和所属各省、市分团已先后在二月二十一日到二十三日分别举行了慰问大会，慰问中国人民解放军华北军区、河北省军区、山西省军区、蒙绥军区和天津警备区的全体指挥员和战斗员。慰问团代表还深入慰问了守卫在华北海防线上的战士们。在各次慰问大会上，慰问团代表都传达了中国共产党、中央人民政府、毛主席和全国人民对人民解放军的关怀和期望，并向解放军报告了国家在过渡时期的总路线和各方面的伟大成就。
    慰问团代表还深入各部队的机关、连队、学校、医院等进行慰问。慰问团代表和代表中的劳动模范与部队的英雄、功臣举行了座谈会，互相报告自己的模范事迹。日夜保卫着首都领空的防空部队某阵地的战士，给慰问团作了战斗演习。代表们还参观了防空部队的战士们为慰问团布置的展览馆。慰问团代表还热情地慰问了曾三次在国庆节受毛主席检阅的某部步兵，访问了华北军区八一学校。
    第六总分团第三分团代表在天津慰问驻军时，还陪同战士们参观了天津十个大工厂，并到天津市烈士陵园扫墓。中国人民志愿军一级英雄杨连第烈士的父亲杨玉璞和很多劳动模范到各部队报告了他们的模范事迹与烈属、军属受政府关怀和各界人民尊敬的情形。第六总分团副团长、第三分团（天津分团）团长吴德深入解放军某部炊事班进行慰问，并向模范炊事员吴春江献花。第三分团的代表还慰问了某部医院的解放军伤病员。第六总分团和所属第四分团（内蒙古分团）、第五分团（河北分团）、第六分团（山西分团）的代表并分别在唐山、秦皇岛、石家庄、张家口、邯郸、大同、长治、临汾等地展开了深入的慰问活动。
