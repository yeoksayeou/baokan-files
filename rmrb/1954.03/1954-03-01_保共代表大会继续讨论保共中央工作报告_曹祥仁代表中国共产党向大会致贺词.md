Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Congress Continues Discussion of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Work Report, Cao Xiangren Delivers Congratulatory Speech on Behalf of the Chinese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1954-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 28th: According to TASS, the Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party continued its discussion on the summary report of the work of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the summary report of the work of the Central Inspection Committee on the afternoon of the 26th and the 27th.

At the afternoon session on the 26th, Yugov, member of the Political Bureau of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, and Panchevski, Minister of National Defense, spoke. At the meeting, Cao Xiangren delivered a congratulatory speech on behalf of the Chinese Communist Party. The delegates greeted him with stormy applause.

The morning and afternoon sessions on the 27th continued the discussion of the reports. Speakers included Ge. Zhankov, member of the Political Bureau of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, To. Prahov, Chairman of the Central Council of Bulgarian Trade Unions, He. Radevski, Secretary-General of the Bulgarian Writers Association, and Ra. Damyanov, member of the Political Bureau of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee. The speakers all enthusiastically supported the foreign and domestic policies of the Communist Party and the People's Government, pointing out the victory of the Party's general line (the line of building a socialist foundation in the country with the help of the Soviet Union and in cooperation with the People's Democratic countries). The delegates used many examples to illustrate the tremendous changes that have taken place in Bulgaria from the Fifth Party Congress to the Sixth Party Congress, and also pointed out some shortcomings in the work of the Party and government organs and in the work of individual sectors of the national economy. The speakers paid great attention to the issue of further improving agriculture.

At the morning session, the representatives of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Romanian Workers' Party, and the Albanian Labor Party delivered congratulatory speeches to the congress, which were warmly welcomed by the attendees.

### 保共代表大会继续讨论保共中央工作报告  曹祥仁代表中国共产党向大会致贺词

1954-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    保共代表大会继续讨论保共中央工作报告
    曹祥仁代表中国共产党向大会致贺词
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚共产党第六次代表大会，二十六日下午和二十七日继续讨论保加利亚共产党中央委员会工作总结报告和中央检查委员会工作总结报告。
    在二十六日下午会议上发言的有保共中央政治局委员于哥夫和国防部部长潘切夫斯基等。会上，曹祥仁代表中国共产党致贺词。大会代表们报以暴风雨般的掌声。
    二十七日上午会议和下午会议继续讨论报告。在会上发言的有：保共中央政治局委员格·詹科夫、保加利亚总工会中央理事会主席托·普拉霍夫、保加利亚作家协会总书记赫·拉杰夫斯基和保共中央政治局委员拉·达米扬诺夫等。发言的人都热烈地支持共产党和人民政府的外交政策和国内政策，指出党的总路线（在苏联帮助和与各人民民主国家合作下，在国内建设社会主义基础的路线）的胜利。代表们用许多事例来说明保加利亚从第五次党代表大会到第六次党代表大会这段期间所发生的巨大变化，同时还指出了党和政府机关工作中、国民经济个别部门工作中所存在的一些缺点。发言者对进一步提高农业的问题极为注意。
    在上午会议上向大会致贺词的有捷克斯洛伐克共产党、匈牙利劳动人民党、罗马尼亚工人党和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的代表，受到到会者的热烈欢迎。
