Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Gale Warning
Author:
Date: 1954-03-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 25th—The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a gale warning at 8:00 PM on the 25th: It is predicted that in the next 24 hours, western and northern North China, southern Shaanxi, northern Henan, and northwestern Hubei will experience northerly winds of force 5 to 6; the Shandong Peninsula will have southerly winds of around force 6; and southern Hebei will have southwesterly winds of force 5 to 6 turning to northwesterly winds. Starting tomorrow at noon, coastal areas of Jiangsu and northern coastal areas of Zhejiang will have southerly winds of force 5 to 6; the lake area near Dongting Lake and Poyang Lake will have southerly winds of force 5 to 6; and the Bohai Sea area will also have northerly winds of around force 6.

### 中央气象台发布大风消息

1954-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台发布大风消息
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央气象台二十五日下午八时发布大风消息：预计未来二十四小时内，华北西部和北部、陕西南部、河南北部、湖北西北部，有五、六级偏北风；山东半岛有六级左右的偏南风；河北南部有五、六级的西南风转西北风。明日中午起，江苏沿海和浙江北部沿海地区，将有五、六级偏南风；洞庭湖与鄱阳湖附近的湖口地区将有五、六级的偏南风；渤海地区也将有六级左右的偏北风。
