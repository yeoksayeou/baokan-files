Title: Formation of New Syrian Government
Author:
Date: 1954-03-04
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, March 2nd: According to TASS, following the army uprising in northern Syria, the resignation of Shishakli, and the dissolution of parliament, the Syrian government and the Army General Command recognized Hashim al-Atassi as President of the Republic. Atassi was ousted by Shishakli's coup in 1951.

　　Atassi signed an order on the 1st to form a new government with Sabri al-Asali as Prime Minister.

### 叙利亚新政府组成

1954-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚新政府组成
   【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社贝鲁特二日讯：在叙利亚北部地区军队起义、施舍克里辞职和议会解散以后，叙利亚政府和陆军总司令部承认了哈希姆·阿塔西为共和国总统，阿塔西是因施舍克里在 
    一九五一年发动政变而下台的。
    阿塔西于一日签署了成立以萨勃里·阿萨里为总理的新政府的命令。
