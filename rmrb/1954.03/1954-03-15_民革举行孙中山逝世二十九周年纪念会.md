Title: RCCK Holds Memorial Meeting on the 29th Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Death
Author:
Date: 1954-03-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: The Central Committee and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (RCCK) held a memorial meeting on the 29th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's death on the 12th, attended by more than 300 people. The memorial meeting was presided over by He Xiangning. She said: As we commemorate Mr. Sun Yat-sen during the in-depth study phase of the General Line for the Transition Period, we must carry forward his spirit of "taking Russia as our teacher" and continuously striving for progress. Under the unified leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, Chairman Mao Zedong, and Party and government organizations at all levels, we must transform our thinking, do our jobs well, and strive to implement the General Line and build socialism. She called on all members to deeply study the General Line, work hard to transform their thinking, and fight against all non-socialist ideas.
    At the memorial meeting, Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General of the RCCK Central Standing Committee, also gave a report on "The General Line Study and Ideological Remolding Issues."
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, March 14th: To commemorate the 29th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's death, on the morning of the 12th, Leng Yu, Vice Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial People's Government, Hui Yuyu, Mayor of the Nanjing Municipal People's Government, Shao Hengqiu, Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Ye Yucang, Deputy Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Branch of the China Democratic League, Kang Yongren, Secretary-General of the Nanjing Municipal Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, and more than 30 representatives from all walks of life in Nanjing paid their respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and presented wreaths.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 14th: On March 12th, the anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's death, Chen Yi, Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, specially sent people to present wreaths at Sun Yat-sen's former residence. Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, Jin Zhonghua, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal People's Congress Consultative Committee, and representatives from the East China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, and the Shanghai local organizations of various democratic parties also went to Sun Yat-sen's former residence to present flowers and pay their respects.

### 民革举行孙中山逝世二十九周年纪念会

1954-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    民革举行孙中山逝世二十九周年纪念会
    【新华社十四日讯】中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会和北京市分部在十二日举行孙中山逝世二十九周年纪念会，出席纪念会的有三百多人。纪念会由何香凝主持。她说：正当学习过渡时期总路线深入阶段的时候纪念孙中山先生，我们要能发扬他“以俄为师”和不断求进步的精神，在中国共产党、中央人民政府和毛泽东主席以及各级党政的统一领导下，改造思想，做好岗位工作，为贯彻实施总路线、努力建设社会主义而奋斗。她号召全体成员深入学习总路线，努力改造思想，和一切非社会主义的思想作斗争。
    在纪念会上并由民革中央常务委员会副秘书长屈武作“关于总路线学习与思想改造问题”的报告。
    【新华社南京十四日电】为纪念孙中山逝世二十九周年，十二日上午，江苏省人民政府副主席冷遹、南京市人民政府市长惠浴宇、中国国民党革命委员会南京市分部主任委员邵恒秋、中国民主同盟南京市支部副主任委员叶雨苍、中国民主建国会南京市分会秘书长康永仁以及南京市各界代表三十多人拜谒中山陵，并献花圈。
    【新华社上海十四日电】三月十二日——孙中山逝世纪念日，上海市人民政府市长陈毅特派人到孙中山故居献花圈。上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年、上海市各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席金仲华和中共中央华东局、中共上海市委员会、各民主党派上海地方组织的代表等也都到孙中山故居献花瞻仰。
