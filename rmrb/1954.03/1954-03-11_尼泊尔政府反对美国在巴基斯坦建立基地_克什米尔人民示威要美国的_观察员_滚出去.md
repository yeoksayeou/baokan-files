Title: Nepalese Government Opposes US Establishing Base in Pakistan; Kashmir People Demonstrate Demanding US “Observers” Get Out
Author:
Date: 1954-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 9th: Nepalese Foreign Minister D.R. Regmi delivered a speech in Kathmandu on the 6th, opposing US military "aid" to Pakistan. He said: If the United States establishes military bases in Pakistan, Nepal cannot but be affected, because Nepal's eastern border is only fifteen miles away from East Pakistan. He called on the Nepalese people to strengthen their vigilance and oppose this military alliance. He said that this military alliance is a "conspiracy to establish military bases in this part of the world and drag these areas into war."

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 9th: Kashmiri people held a demonstration in front of the United Nations office in Jammu on the 7th. The marching masses held placards with the words "American Observers Get Out of Kashmir" and chanted slogans against the US-Pakistan military treaty.

Jagdish Chandra Gupta, a prominent figure in Kashmir, told reporters: If the American "observers" do not withdraw from Kashmir immediately, more mass demonstrations will need to be held.

### 尼泊尔政府反对美国在巴基斯坦建立基地  克什米尔人民示威要美国的“观察员”滚出去

1954-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔政府反对美国在巴基斯坦建立基地
    克什米尔人民示威要美国的“观察员”滚出去
    【新华社新德里九日电】尼泊尔外交大臣德·尔·雷格米六日在加德满都发表演说，反对美国对巴基斯坦的军事“援助”。他说：假如美国在巴基斯坦建立了军事基地，尼泊尔不可能不受到影响，因为尼泊尔的东部边界离东巴基斯坦只有十五英里。他要求尼泊尔人加强警惕并反对这种军事同盟。他说，这种军事同盟是“在世界的这一部分建立军事基地并把这些地区拖入战争的阴谋”。
    【新华社新德里九日电】克什米尔民众七日在查谟的联合国办事处门前举行示威游行。游行的群众手拿着“美国观察员滚出克什米尔”的标语牌，并高呼反对美巴军事条约的口号。
    克什米尔著名人士贾格迪什·钱德拉·古普塔对记者们说：假如美国“观察员”不立即撤离克什米尔，那就需要举行更多的群众性的示威游行。
