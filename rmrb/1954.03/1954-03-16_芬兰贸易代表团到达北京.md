Title: Finnish Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-03-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 15th:

    Finnish Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, March 15th] The Finnish trade delegation, led by head of delegation Mr. Vahlala, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 15th.
    Yang Lin, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Zhou Liecheng, Section Chief of the Protocol Department, and Zhang Yunxiao, Commercial Counselor of our country's Embassy in Finland, who is currently in China, went to the airport to greet them.
    Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Sundström, and embassy staff also went to greet them.

### 芬兰贸易代表团到达北京

1954-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰贸易代表团到达北京
    【新华社十五日讯】芬兰贸易代表团一行四人，在团长瓦里拉率领下，于十五日下午抵京。
    前往机场迎接的有我中央人民政府对外贸易部第三局副局长杨琳、交际处科长周烈承和现在国内的我国驻芬兰公使馆商务专员张云啸。
    芬兰驻华公使孙士敦和使馆人员也前往迎接。
