Title: We Stand Under the Same Cloud – Dedicated to the Delegation of the Korean People's Visit to China
Author: Yan Chen (严辰) Pinyin: Yán Chén
Date: 1954-03-15
Page: 6

We Stand Under the Same Cloud
　　—Dedicated to the Delegation of the Korean People's Visit to China
　　Yan Chen

　　I.

　　We stand under the same cloud,
　　The same star shines above us,
　　The China South Liberation Medal hangs on your chest,
　　I also cherish my National Flag Medal.

　　In your coarse, stiff hair,
　　There still lingers the wind and frost of the North Manchurian grasslands,
　　Your strong arms,
　　Once braved the perilous waves of the Yangtze River.

　　On my worn sleeves,
　　There clings the dust of Shangganling,
　　The soles of my shoes, which have trekked across mountains and rivers,
　　Are plastered with the mud of the Kum River.

　　We stand under the same cloud,
　　The same star shines above us,
　　We stand guard side by side by the sea,
　　Our hearts surge like the ocean.

　　II.

　　The stream gently flows past the village,
　　We lie under the same roof,
　　We have defended the lands of two countries,
　　Bearing the scars of the same kind of bullets.

　　My mother once used her warm embrace,
　　To warm your swollen, frostbitten feet,
　　She cared for you as she cared for me,
　　Washing your clothes, mending your socks.

　　When I was delirious with illness,
　　Amani watched over me day and night,
　　Her rough hands were full of love,
　　Comforting me like my own dear mother.

　　The stream gently flows past the village,
　　We lie under the same roof,
　　Your home in Hwanghae Province
　　Is like my old home at the foot of Changbai Mountain.

　　III.

　　The spring breeze has opened the apricot blossoms in Jiangnan,
　　And also the winter jasmine by the Chongchon River,
　　The earth awaits planting,
　　We need labor, prosperity, and peace.

　　The water of the Jaeryong Reservoir,
　　It also flows through Guanting, through the Buddhist Tzu Range,
　　In Pyongyang, I pile up bricks one by one,
　　Just as I would pile them in Beijing with my own hands.

　　Your grand chorus of rebuilding your homeland,
　　Echoes the steps of our geological exploration team,
　　The roads that pass through your mountains and rivers,
　　Also lead to our cities and villages.

　　The spring breeze has opened the apricot blossoms in Jiangnan,
　　And also the winter jasmine by the Chongchon River,
　　We hold hands tightly,
　　Advancing towards a glorious and victorious tomorrow!

### 我们站在同一片云彩下——献给朝鲜人民访华代表团
严辰
1954-03-15
第6版()
专栏：

    我们站在同一片云彩下
    ——献给朝鲜人民访华代表团
    严辰
    一
    我们站在同一片云彩下，
    头上同一颗星星在闪亮，
    中南解放纪念章挂在你胸前，
    我也珍爱着我的国旗勋章。
    你的粗硬的头发里，
    还留着北满草原的风霜，
    你的强有力的手臂，
    曾抢渡过长江的惊涛骇浪。
    我的磨破了的袖管上，
    沾着上甘岭的尘土，
    我跋涉过千山万水的鞋底，
    糊着金城川的泥浆。
    我们站在同一片云彩下，
    头上同一颗星星在闪亮，
    我们并肩守望在海边，
    心头仿佛海水一样激荡。
    二
　  小溪轻轻地流过村前，
    我们躺在同一个屋檐下，
    我们保卫过两个国家的土地，
    身上留着同一种子弹的伤疤。
    我的妈妈曾用她温暖的胸怀，
    暖过你浮肿的冻僵的双脚，
    她像体贴我一样体贴你，
    为你浆洗衣裳、缝补鞋袜。
    我病得神志昏迷的时候，
    阿妈尼日夜看护过我，
    她的粗糙的手充满慈爱，
    抚慰着我就像我亲爱的妈妈。
    小溪轻轻地流过村前，
    我们躺在同一个屋檐下，
    你的黄海道的故居
    仿佛我长白山下的老家。
    三
  　春风吹开了江南的杏花，
    也吹开了清川江边的迎春，
    大地等待着播种，
    我们需要劳动、繁荣与和平。
    见龙蓄水池的流水，
    它也流过官厅、流过佛子岭，
    我在平壤堆砌着一块块砖头，
    就像我亲手把它堆砌在北京。
    你们重建家园的大合唱，
    和我们地质勘探队的步伐相呼应，
    经过你们山岭河流的道路，
    也都通到我们的城市和乡村。
    春风吹开了江南的杏花，
    也吹开了清川江边的迎春，
    我们紧紧地手挽着手，
    向光辉的胜利的明天迈进！
