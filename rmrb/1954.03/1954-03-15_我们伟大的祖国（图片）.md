Author:

Our Great Motherland
All provinces and cities across the country are enthusiastically carrying out activities to express appreciation and support to the People's Liberation Army, who are defending the security of the motherland and the happy lives of the people. This is the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Political Department's Art Troupe performing the Korean folk dance "Flower of Friendship" for the soldiers of the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各省、市热烈展开慰问保卫祖国安全和人民幸福生活的人民解放军。这是中国人民志愿军政治部文艺工作团，为中南军区、第四野战军战士演出朝鲜民间舞蹈——友谊之花。
