Title: Tibetan Monastic and Secular People's Representatives Hold a Meeting to Express Regards to All Commanders and Fighters of the PLA Entering Tibet

Author:
Date: 1954-03-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, March 27th: Tibetan monastic and secular people's representatives gathered on the afternoon of March 22nd to express their regards to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army entering Tibet. Attending the meeting were responsible cadres, model heroes, and representatives from various organs and troops of the PLA Tibet Military Region, as well as responsible officials from the Tibetan local government, totaling over 200 people. The Panchen Erdeni also sent a representative to attend the meeting.

    After the meeting began, General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, General Tan Guansan, political commissar of the PLA Tibet Military Region, Apei·Awang Jinmei, first deputy commander, and Li Jue, chief of staff, received flowers presented by Tibetan local monastic and secular people amidst military music. Subsequently, Galun So Kang Wangqing Gelai, representative of the Tibetan local government, and Grand Lama Wang Lejie, representative of the Panchen Erdeni, delivered congratulatory speeches respectively. They unanimously praised the great spirit of the PLA entering Tibet for faithfully implementing the agreement and actively participating in various constructions in Tibet over the past few years, and the tremendous achievements made in building Tibet and consolidating the southwest national defense of the motherland. They expressed their commitment to support the PLA entering Tibet with the greatest strength and jointly build a new Tibet. Representatives of the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni also presented flags to the PLA entering Tibet respectively, expressing their regards. The brocade flag of the Dalai Lama was inscribed with "Respect to the PLA entering Tibet, who defends the southwest national defense of the motherland, faithfully implements national policies, and actively participates in the construction of Tibet!" The brocade flag of the Panchen Erdeni was inscribed with "Build Tibet, Consolidate National Defense." The meeting also read out the congratulatory telegrams sent by the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni to all commanders and fighters of the PLA entering Tibet. General Tan Guansan, political commissar of the Tibet Military Region, expressed his gratitude for the regards of the Tibetan local monastic and secular people in his reply speech, and pointed out that the PLA entering Tibet will continue to strengthen unity with people from all walks of life in Tibet, resolutely implement the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet, and jointly build a new Tibet with the Tibetan people. Finally, the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association and the Lhasa Primary School performed for the PLA representatives attending the meeting as a gesture of goodwill.

### 西藏僧俗人民代表举行大会慰问解放军进藏部队全体指战员

1954-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏僧俗人民代表举行大会慰问解放军进藏部队全体指战员
   【新华社拉萨二十七日电】西藏僧俗人民代表在三月二十二日下午集会，慰问中国人民解放军进藏部队全体指挥员、战斗员。参加大会的有人民解放军西藏军区的负责干部、功臣模范、各机关、部队代表及西藏地方政府负责官员共二百多人。班禅额尔德尼也派代表参加了大会。
    大会开始后，中央人民政府代表张经武将军、人民解放军西藏军区政治委员谭冠三将军、第一副司令员阿沛·阿旺晋美和参谋长李觉，在军乐声中接受了西藏地方僧俗人民的献花。接着，由西藏地方政府代表噶伦索康·旺清格来和班禅额尔德尼的代表大喇嘛王乐阶分别致慰问词。他们一致赞扬人民解放军进藏部队几年来忠实执行协议、积极参加西藏各项建设的伟大精神和建设西藏、巩固祖国西南国防各方面作出的巨大成绩，表示要以最大的力量支援进藏的人民解放军，共同建设新西藏。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的代表并分别向人民解放军进藏部队献旗，表示慰问。达赖喇嘛的锦旗上写着“向保卫祖国西南国防，忠实执行民族政策，积极参加西藏建设的人民解放军进藏部队致敬！”班禅额尔德尼的锦旗上面写着“建设西藏，巩固国防”。大会还宣读了达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼向进藏部队全体指挥员、战斗员发出的慰问电。西藏军区政治委员谭冠三将军在致答谢词中，对西藏地方僧俗人民的慰问表示谢意，并指出进藏部队今后将继续加强和西藏各阶层人民的团结，坚决执行和平解放西藏办法的协议，和西藏人民共同建设新西藏。最后，拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会和拉萨市小学校向到会的人民解放军代表作慰问演出。
