Title: China's All-China Federation of Cooperatives Delegation Departs to Attend the Hungarian Rural Consumer Cooperative Representatives Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-03-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 27th.

    The delegation of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, invited by the Hungarian Federation of Cooperatives to attend the 3rd Congress of the Hungarian Rural Consumer Cooperatives, headed by Meng Yongqian, Vice Chairman of the Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, comprising four people, departed by plane this morning, the 27th, for Hungary.  Present at the airport to see them off were the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Szobek, and embassy officials, as well as Deng Jie, Vice Chairman of the Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, and others.

### 参加匈牙利农村消费合作社代表会  我国合作总社代表团启程

1954-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    参加匈牙利农村消费合作社代表会
    我国合作总社代表团启程
    【新华社二十七日讯】中华全国合作社联合总社应匈牙利合作社联合会邀请参加匈牙利农村消费合作社第三届代表大会的代表团，以中华全国合作社联合总社理事会副主任孟用潜为首等四人，于二十七日晨乘飞机启程赴匈。到机场欢送的有匈牙利人民共和国驻华特命全权大使苏贝克及使馆官员，中华全国合作社联合总社理事会副主任邓洁等。
