Title: Military Commission Departments and PLA Beijing Troops Hold Gatherings to Commemorate the Great Revolutionary Teacher Stalin; Volunteer Army Leading Organs Hold Commemorative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-03-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 5th: The People's Liberation Army (PLA) departments stationed in Beijing and troops, with infinitely deep and heartfelt feelings, held separate meetings on the 5th to commemorate the first anniversary of the death of Stalin, the leader and teacher of the world's working people.

　　The General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission held a cadre meeting on the afternoon of the 5th to commemorate the first anniversary of Stalin's death. At the commemorative meeting, Huang Yuan, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department, reported on the great contribution of the great Stalin to the proletarian revolutionary cause and world peace. Deputy Director Huang Yuan said in his report that in commemorating the first anniversary of Comrade Stalin's death, we must study Lenin and Stalin's theory on the transitional period and strive to implement the Party's general line during the transitional period; we must study Stalin's military science and strive to build our army into a modern and regularized revolutionary army; we must study Stalin's theory on party building, strengthen the unity of the Party, and strive to victoriously complete the current great revolutionary tasks. After the commemorative meeting, the film "Stalin Lives Forever in Our Hearts" was screened.

　　The General Staff Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, the General Cadre Department, the General Logistics Department, and other organs, as well as the leading organs and troops of the PLA Navy, Artillery, Armored Forces, Air Defense Forces, Engineering Corps, Public Security Forces, Railway Corps stationed in Beijing, and the leading organs and troops of the PLA North China Military Region and stationed in Beijing, also held meetings on the 5th to commemorate the first anniversary of Stalin's death. Heads of various departments delivered reports on Stalin's life and Stalin's doctrines at the meetings, and films about Stalin, including "Stalin Lives Forever in Our Hearts," were screened. Many organs, cadres and soldiers also held symposiums. Many cadres and soldiers expressed that they would commemorate the first anniversary of the death of the great Stalin with practical actions of strengthening study and working hard.

　　Many troop newspapers and periodicals published editorials, commemorative articles, and pictures about Stalin to commemorate the first anniversary of Stalin's death. The wall newspapers in the troops also issued special issues commemorating the anniversary of Stalin's death. Some units also held photo exhibitions introducing Stalin's life.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 5th: The leading organ of the Chinese People's Volunteers (CPV) held a grand gathering on the evening of March 4th to commemorate the first anniversary of the death of the great Stalin. Yang Dezhi, Deputy Commander of the CPV, Du Ping and Zhang Nansheng, Deputy Directors of the Political Department, Wang Yunrui, Deputy Chief of Staff, and more than 600 leading cadres from various departments attended the meeting.

　　Deputy Commander Yang Dezhi made a commemorative report at the meeting. He said: Comrade Stalin, the great leader of the world communist movement and the most beloved friend and teacher of the Chinese people, has been dead for a full year. Comrade Stalin's death caused unparalleled deep sorrow to all peace-loving people in the world. Not only at that time, but today, and for billions of years to come, mankind will always miss him.

　　Deputy Commander Yang Dezhi said: Over the past year, the Soviet Union, China, and various people's democratic countries, as well as the working people all over the world, have firmly inherited Stalin's legacy, turned grief into strength, and achieved new victories in all aspects, greatly advancing the great cause of communism.

　　Deputy Commander Yang Dezhi elaborated in detail on the achievements of the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union over the past year and the great contributions of Comrade Stalin's life, especially pointing out the great sympathy and support that Comrade Stalin gave to the just War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea of our people.

　　Deputy Commander Yang Dezhi said: Today, we are commemorating the first anniversary of Comrade Stalin's death at the outpost of defending peace in the Far East. To commemorate Comrade Stalin, we must strive to defend the victories already won in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea; we must study Stalin's theory on the socialist construction of the Soviet Union in order to more deeply understand and implement the Party's general line during the transition period; and we must study the advanced Soviet military science created by Stalin to further advance the modernization of our army. Today, it is especially important and urgent to better study Lenin and Stalin's theory on party building, study the documents of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Sixth Central Committee of the CPC, enhance our sense of responsibility for the revolutionary cause and vigilance against enemy conspiracies, and strive to implement the "Resolution on Strengthening Party Unity" of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Sixth Central Committee of the CPC. Deputy Commander Yang Dezhi called on all attendees to resolutely overcome the sentiment of blind pride in accordance with the resolution of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Sixth Central Committee of the CPC, firmly grasp the weapon of criticism and self-criticism, continue to maintain the fine style of humility and prudence, unite more closely, and work diligently to defend the socialist construction of our great motherland and strive for a peaceful settlement of the Korean issue.

　　After the meeting, the famous Soviet historical film "The Unforgettable Year 1919" was shown.

### 军委各部和解放军驻京各部队  集会纪念伟大的革命导师斯大林  志愿军领导机关举行纪念大会

1954-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    军委各部和解放军驻京各部队
    集会纪念伟大的革命导师斯大林
    志愿军领导机关举行纪念大会
    【新华社五日讯】人民解放军驻京各机关、部队以无限深沉怀念的心情，在五日分别举行大会纪念全世界劳动人民的领袖和导师斯大林逝世一周年。
    中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部在五日下午举行干部大会，纪念斯大林逝世一周年。在纪念大会上，总政治部宣传部副部长黄远报告了伟大斯大林对无产阶级革命事业和世界和平的伟大贡献。黄远副部长报告中说，纪念斯大林同志逝世一周年，我们要学习列宁斯大林关于过渡时期的学说，为贯彻党在过渡时期的总路线而奋斗；要学习斯大林军事科学，为建设我军为现代化正规化的革命军队而奋斗；要学习斯大林关于建党的学说，增强党的团结，为胜利地完成当前伟大的革命任务而奋斗。纪念大会后并放映“斯大林永远活在我们的心里”影片。
    中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总参谋部各部、总干部部、总后勤部等机关和人民解放军驻京海军、炮兵、装甲兵、防空部队、工兵、公安部队、铁道兵等领导机关和部队，人民解放军华北军区领导机关和驻京部队，也都在五日举行大会纪念斯大林逝世一周年。各部首长都在大会上作了关于斯大林生平事迹和斯大林学说的报告，并放映了“斯大林永远活在我们的心里”和有关斯大林事迹的影片。许多机关、部队的干部和战士并举行座谈会。许多干部、战士都表示将以加强学习、努力工作的实际行动，来纪念伟大斯大林逝世一周年。
    许多部队中的报纸、刊物并刊登了纪念斯大林逝世一周年的社论，纪念文章和有关斯大林的图片。部队中的墙报也出了纪念斯大林逝世周年的专刊。有些单位并举办了介绍斯大林生平事迹的图片展览。
    【新华社平壤五日电】中国人民志愿军领导机关三月四日晚隆重集会纪念伟大的斯大林逝世一周年。中国人民志愿军杨得志副司令员，政治部杜平、张南生副主任，王蕴瑞副参谋长及各部门负责干部等六百多人参加了大会。
    杨得志副司令员在会上作了纪念报告。他说：全世界共产主义运动的伟大领袖、中国人民最亲爱的朋友和导师斯大林同志逝世整整一年了。斯大林同志的逝世，使全世界一切爱好和平的人民都感到了无可比拟的深沉的悲痛。不但在当时，在今天，就是在今后亿万年，人类也将永远怀念他。
    杨得志副司令员说：一年来，苏联、中国和各人民民主国家以及全世界劳动人民，坚定地继承了斯大林的遗志，把悲痛化为力量，在各方面都取得了新的胜利，把伟大的共产主义事业大大推向前进了。
    杨得志副司令员详细阐述了一年来以苏联为首的和平民主社会主义阵营的成就和斯大林同志一生的伟大贡献，特别指出了斯大林同志对我国人民正义的抗美援朝运动所给予的极大的同情和支持。
    杨得志副司令员说：我们今天是在保卫远东和平的前哨岗位上来纪念斯大林同志逝世一周年。我们纪念斯大林同志，要努力保卫抗美援朝战争已获得的胜利，要学习斯大林关于苏联社会主义建设的理论，以求更深刻地领会和贯彻党在过渡时期的总路线；要学习斯大林所创造的先进的苏联军事科学，把我军现代化的建设事业推进一步。在今天，尤其重要而迫切的是要更好地学习列宁、斯大林关于党的建设的理论，学习中共四中全会的各项文件，提高我们对于革命事业的责任心和对于敌人阴谋的警惕性，为贯彻执行中共四中全会“关于增强党的团结的决议”而斗争。杨得志副司令员号召全体到会人员按照党的四中全会的决议，坚决克服盲目骄傲情绪，紧紧掌握批评和自我批评的武器，继续保持谦虚、谨慎的优良作风，更加紧密地团结起来，兢兢业业地为保卫我们伟大祖国的社会主义建设，为争取朝鲜问题的和平解决而进行持久不懈的斗争。
    会后，放映了苏联历史名片“难忘的一九一九年”。
