    Our Great Motherland
    The state-run Qinghai Sanjiaocheng Sheep Breeding Farm is located in the Haiyan Tibetan Autonomous Region of Qinghai. The task of this sheep breeding farm is to improve the existing Tibetan sheep breeds in Qinghai in order to increase the income of herdsmen. This is a Langa sheep on the breeding farm. Managers check the weight of the sheep monthly and understand their development.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营青海三角城种羊场设在青海海宴藏族自治区境内。这个种羊场的任务是改良青海的现有藏系羊的羊种，以增加牧民的收入。这是种羊场上的兰哈羊，管理人员按月检查羊只的体重，并了解其发育情况。
