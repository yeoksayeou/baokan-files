Title: Korean Art Troupe's Inaugural Performance Held Last Night
Author:
Date: 1954-03-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: The Korean People's Delegation Art Troupe to China gave a grand inaugural performance at Huairentang in the capital last night. This performance was held after the welcome rally for the Korean People's Delegation to China by people from all walks of life in the capital.

Attending the performance were: Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Dehuai, Peng Zhen, Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, and more than 1,400 people from all walks of life in the capital.

The art troupe performed a total of eighteen song and dance numbers, which fully demonstrated the heroic spirit of the Korean military and civilians in the war and reconstruction. "Eungbong Mountain Ballad" is a song praising the heroic soldiers of the Korean People's Army guarding the "1211" height, famous as a heroic high ground. "Our Pride" depicts a victorious battle in which the heroic People's Army annihilated the American invading army. The "Farmers' Song" dance showed the collective labor and tradition of singing and dancing of Koreans when transplanting rice seedlings and weeding more than 1,300 years ago. "Song of Restoration" and "Song of Construction" showed the optimism and strong confidence in victory of the Korean people in reconstruction. Finally, "Peace and Friendship," symbolizing the friendship and unity of peace-loving people all over the world headed by the Soviet Union, was performed.

Most of the performers are recipients of national medals and awards, including the famous Republic's Meritorious Actors Lim Su-hyang, Ahn Ki-ok, and Ting Nam-hee. Many members of the troupe were recipients of awards at this year's Korean People's Army Founding Day. Most of these people are recipients of awards from the World Youth and Student Festival.

The audience responded with long and enthusiastic applause to these wonderful performances. At the end of the performance, the youth of the capital presented flowers to all the comrades of the art troupe.

### 朝鲜艺术团揭幕演出昨晚举行

1954-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜艺术团揭幕演出昨晚举行
   【新华社十六日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团艺术团十六日晚在首都怀仁堂隆重举行揭幕演出。这次演出是在首都各界欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团大会之后举行的。
    出席观看演出的有：刘少奇，周恩来，董必武，陈云，郭沫若，邓小平，彭德怀，彭真，沈钧儒，陈叔通，和首都各界人士共一千四百多人。
    艺术团共演出十八个歌舞节目，这些节目充分表现出了朝鲜军民在战争和重建中的英雄气概。“应峰山谣”是歌颂守卫在以英雄高地闻名的“一二一一”高地的朝鲜人民军英雄们的。“我们的骄傲”描写了英雄的人民军歼灭美国侵略军的一次胜利的战斗。“农民歌”歌舞表现了朝鲜人在一千三百多年前从事插秧锄草时的集体劳动和喜好歌舞的传统。“恢复之歌”和“建设之歌”表现出了朝鲜人民在重建中的乐观情绪和坚强的胜利信心。最后演出象征以苏联为首的全世界爱好和平人民的友谊团结的“和平与友谊”。
    参加演出的人员绝大部分是国家勋章和奖章的获得者，其中有著名的共和国功勋演员林素香、安基玉、丁南希。有不少团员是今年朝鲜人民军建军节的受奖人。这些人大多是世界青年与学生联欢节的受奖者。
    这些精彩节目演出时，观众都报以经久不息的掌声。演出结束时，首都青年向艺术团全体同志献鲜花。
