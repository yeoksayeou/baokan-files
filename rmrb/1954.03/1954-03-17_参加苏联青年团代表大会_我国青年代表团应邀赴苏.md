Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Invited to Soviet Union to Attend Soviet Komsomol Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-03-17
Page: 1

    Attending the Soviet Komsomol Congress
    Chinese Youth Delegation Invited to Soviet Union
   [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] At the invitation to attend the 12th Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union, the Chinese Youth Delegation departed by plane for the Soviet Union on the morning of the 13th. The 12th Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union will be held on March 19th. The head of the delegation is Comrade Liu Daosheng, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League. Members of the delegation include Xiang Nan, Secretary of the East China Working Committee of the Youth League; Ge Buhai, Secretary of the North China Working Committee of the Youth League; Lu Jindong, Deputy Director of the Organization Department of the Central Committee of the Youth League; Zeng Delin, Secretary of the Chongqing Municipal Committee of the Youth League; and Wang Jialiu, Secretary of the University Working Committee of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League.

### 参加苏联青年团代表大会  我国青年代表团应邀赴苏

1954-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    参加苏联青年团代表大会
    我国青年代表团应邀赴苏
   【新华社十六日讯】应邀参加苏联列宁共产主义青年团第十二次代表大会的我国青年代表团已在十三日晨乘机赴苏。苏联列宁共产主义青年团第十二次代表大会将在三月十九日召开。代表团由中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记刘导生同志任团长，代表团团员为青年团华东工作委员会书记项南，青年团华北工作委员会书记葛步海，青年团中央委员会组织部副部长路金栋，青年团重庆市委员会书记曾德林和青年团北京市委员会大学工作委员会书记汪家镠。
