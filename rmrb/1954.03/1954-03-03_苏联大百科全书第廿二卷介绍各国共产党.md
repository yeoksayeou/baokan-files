Title: The Soviet Encyclopedia, Volume 22, Introduces Communist Parties of Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 2nd: The Soviet Encyclopedia, Volume 22, has recently been published. This volume is entirely devoted to explaining words beginning with the Russian letter "K". This volume devotes considerable space to a detailed introduction of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and then the Communist Party of China with the second most space. It also introduces the communist parties of various countries in the world. When introducing the history and activities of the Communist Party of China, this volume is divided into the following sections: 1. The establishment of the Communist Party of China and the period of the First Revolutionary Civil War; 2. The period of the Second Revolutionary Civil War; 3. The period of the War of Resistance Against Japan; 4. The period of the Third Revolutionary Civil War; 5. The Communist Party of China is the leading force of the People's Republic of China.

### 苏联大百科全书第廿二卷介绍各国共产党

1954-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联大百科全书第廿二卷介绍各国共产党
    【新华社莫斯科二日电】苏联大百科全书第二十二卷已在最近出版。本卷全部是阐释以俄文字母“K”为首的单词。本卷以大量篇幅详细介绍了苏联共产党，并以次多的篇幅介绍了中国共产党。此外还介绍了世界各国共产党。本卷在介绍中国共产党的历史和活动时分成以下各部分：一、中国共产党的成立、第一次国内革命战争时期；二、第二次国内革命战争时期；三、抗日战争时期；四、第三次国内革命战争时期；五、中国共产党是中华人民共和国的领导力量。
