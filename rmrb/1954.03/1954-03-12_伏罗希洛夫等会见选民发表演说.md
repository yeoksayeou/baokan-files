Title: Voroshilov and Others Meet with Voters and Deliver Speeches
Author: 
Date: 1954-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Leningrad, March 11th: According to TASS, the candidate for the Soviet Supreme Soviet deputy, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, K. Ye. Voroshilov, met with voters at a voter's meeting in the Kirov constituency of Leningrad on the 10th. More than 2,000 people, including advanced producers, scientists, doctors, students, and Baltic sailors from the Kirov constituency, participated in the meeting. When Voroshilov took his seat on the podium, the hall resounded with stormy applause.

Voters spoke at the meeting one after another. Among them were Chernyavsky, Secretary of the Kirov District Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Kartashev, an old worker at the Kirov factory who has won four medals, Vinogradov, a meritorious doctor of Russia, and Zaitchenko, an advanced fitter. In their speeches, they talked about the labor achievements of the people of Leningrad in welcoming the election, the changes that have taken place in Leningrad during the era of Soviet power, and the determination of the Soviet people to implement the Communist Party's magnificent plan to further improve the people's welfare. They also expressed their belief that on March 14, the voters will unanimously vote for Voroshilov, an outstanding activist of the Communist Party, to be a representative to the Supreme Soviet.

Then, Voroshilov delivered a speech at the meeting, which was constantly interrupted by applause.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 11th: According to TASS, N. A. Bulganin, candidate for the Soviet Supreme Soviet Nationalities Council deputy, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, and Minister of National Defense of the USSR, attended the voter's meeting in the Moscow city constituency at the Column Hall of the Trade Union Building on the 10th and met with voters. When Marshal Bulganin took his seat on the podium, the attendees applauded warmly.

The meeting was presided over by Kapitonov, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. Many voters spoke at the meeting. Among them were Malinkin, a fitter at the Moscow Electric Bulb Factory, Academician Shevelin, Corresponding Member of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and Levchenko, an advanced female worker at the Petrel Shoe Factory. In their speeches, they talked about the political and labor upsurge launched by the Soviet people on the eve of the election, and pointed out the Communist Party's tireless concern for the happiness of the people. They praised Marshal Bulganin's outstanding role in the Great Patriotic War and in consolidating the Soviet Union's defense forces, and called on all voters in the capital to unanimously vote for him as a representative to the Soviet Supreme Soviet.

Marshal Bulganin delivered a speech at the meeting, which was warmly welcomed by the attendees.

Xinhua News Agency, Kursk, March 11th: Voters in the Kursk constituency held a meeting on the 10th to meet Posperov, a candidate for the Soviet Supreme Soviet deputy in the district and secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

After the start of the meeting, Kotlyarov, a driver from the Kursk station locomotive depot, Ruchenko, a professor at the Kursk Medical Research Institute, and Tikina, a female member of the collective farm and a hero of socialist labor, spoke successively. They called on voters to unanimously vote for Posperov, the candidate of the Communist Party and the non-party alliance.

Posperov delivered a speech at the meeting. He described the achievements made by the Soviet people since the last election of the Soviet Supreme Soviet. He pointed out that the Soviet Union's heavy industry has developed to a high level, creating conditions for rapidly increasing the production of daily necessities. He also talked about the measures of the Communist Party and the government to rapidly develop agriculture, light industry, and the food industry, as well as the tasks in developing Soviet science, literature, and art. Posperov concluded by talking about the principles of the Soviet state's peaceful foreign policy. His speech was warmly welcomed by the attendees.

Xinhua News Agency, Alma-Ata, March 11th: Ponomarenko, candidate for the Soviet Supreme Soviet deputy and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Kazakhstan, participated in the voter's meeting in the Alma-Ata city constituency on the 10th and delivered a speech to the voters.

At the voter's meeting, Tashenov, an engineer from the Turkestan-Siberian Railway Administration, Zhanbekova, a female worker from the Altai Cotton Textile Factory, and Saulanbayev, Vice President of the Kazakh Academy of Sciences, spoke successively. They all talked about the growing political enthusiasm and production enthusiasm of the working people of Kazakhstan, and the determination of the people of Kazakhstan to contribute all their strength to realizing the program of communist construction formulated by the Party. Voters unanimously expressed their belief that Ponomarenko would never betray the people's trust in him.

Ponomarenko delivered a speech at the meeting, expressing his deep gratitude for the voters' trust. He cited many facts to illustrate the continuous surge of the Soviet national economy, the continuous increase in the income of working people, and the year-by-year increase in people's purchasing power. Ponomarenko also talked about the development of Soviet science, culture, and healthcare. He also emphasized the tasks of the working people of Kazakhstan to further develop the republic's economy, especially to reclaim land to expand agricultural production. When Ponomarenko cheered for the unbreakable friendship of all nationalities in the Soviet Union, for the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and for the great Soviet people—builders of communism—the hall resounded with stormy applause.

### 伏罗希洛夫等会见选民发表演说

1954-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫等会见选民发表演说
    【新华社十一日讯】　据塔斯社列宁格勒讯：苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人、苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·叶·伏罗希洛夫，十日在列宁格勒基洛夫选区的选民大会上会见了选民。参加大会的有基洛夫选区的先进生产者、科学家、医生、学生和波罗的海海员等共两千多人。当伏罗希洛夫在主席台上就座时，会场上响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
    选民们纷纷在会上讲了话。其中有苏联共产党基洛夫区委员会书记切尔尼亚斯基、曾四度获得奖章的基洛夫工厂老工人卡尔塔谢夫、俄罗斯功勋医生维诺格拉多夫、先进钳工扎伊钦科等。他们在讲话中谈到了列宁格勒人在迎接选举中所获得的劳动成就，谈到了列宁格勒在苏维埃政权时代中所发生的变化，以及苏联人民执行共产党关于进一步增进人民福利的宏伟的计划的决心。他们并表示相信，到三月十四日，选民们将一致投票选举共产党的杰出活动家伏罗希洛夫为最高苏维埃代表。
    接着，伏罗希洛夫在会上发表了演说，他的演说不断被掌声所打断。
    【新华社十一日讯】　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃民族院代表候选人、苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联国防部部长尼·亚·布尔加宁，十日在工会大厦圆柱大厅参加了莫斯科市选区的选民大会，和选民会面。当布尔加宁元帅在主席台上就座时，到会者热烈鼓掌。
    大会由苏联共产党莫斯科市委员会书记卡皮托诺夫主持。许多选民在会上都讲了话。其中有莫斯科电灯泡制造厂的钳工马林金、苏联科学院通讯院士谢维林、海燕制鞋工厂先进女工列夫钦科等。他们在讲话中谈到了苏联人民在选举前夕所掀起的政治和劳动热潮，指出了共产党对人民幸福的不倦的关怀。他们盛赞布尔加宁元帅在伟大的卫国战争中以及在巩固苏联国防力量方面所起的杰出的作用，并号召首都的全体选民一致投票选举他为苏联最高苏维埃代表。
    布尔加宁元帅在会上发表了演说，受到与会者的热烈欢迎。
      【新华社十一日讯】　据塔斯社库尔斯克讯：库尔斯克选区的选民在十日举行大会，会见该区苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人、苏联共产党中央委员会书记波斯伯洛夫。
    大会开始后，库尔斯克车站机务段的司机科特利亚罗夫、库尔斯克医药研究所教授鲁钦科、集体农庄女庄员、社会主义劳动英雄季克金娜等相继发言。他们号召选民一致投票选举共产党和非党联盟的候选人波斯伯洛夫。
    波斯伯洛夫在会上发表了演说。他叙述了苏联人民在上届苏联最高苏维埃选举以来所取得的成就。他指出，苏联的重工业已经发展到了高度的水平，为迅速地提高日用品生产创造了条件。他还谈到共产党和政府关于急速发展农业、轻工业和食品工业的措施，以及在发展苏联科学、文学和艺术方面的任务。波斯伯洛夫最后谈到了苏维埃国家的和平外交政策的原则。他的演说受到了与会者的热烈欢迎。
    【新华社十一日讯】　据塔斯社阿拉木图讯：苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人、哈萨克共和国共产党中央委员会第一书记波诺马连科，十日参加了阿拉木图市选区的选民大会，并向选民发表了演说。
    在选民大会上，土耳其斯坦—西伯利亚铁路管理处工程师塔谢诺夫、阿尔泰棉纺工厂女工詹别科娃、哈萨克科学院副院长萨乌兰巴耶夫等相继发言。他们都谈到了哈萨克劳动人民的日益增长的政治热情和生产积极性，以及哈萨克人民为实现党所制订的共产主义建设纲领而贡献全部力量的决心。选民一致表示相信波诺马连科绝不会辜负人民对他的信任。
    波诺马连科在会上发表了演说，对选民的信任表示深切的感谢。他列举出许多事实，说明苏联国民经济不断高涨、劳动人民收入不断增加、人民购买力逐年提高的情形。波诺马连科并谈到了苏联科学、文化和保健事业的发展。他还着重指出了哈萨克劳动人民进一步发展共和国经济，特别是开垦土地以扩大农业生产的任务。当波诺马连科为苏联各族人民的牢不可破的友谊，为苏联共产党和伟大的苏联人民——共产主义建设者而欢呼时，会场上响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
