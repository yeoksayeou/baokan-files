Title: Mexican Newspapers Denounce the U.S. for Infringing Upon the Interests of Latin American Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-03-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

Mexican Newspapers Denounce the U.S. for Infringing Upon the Interests of Latin American Countries

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Many Latin American countries' newspapers continue to condemn the U.S. for pushing enslavement policies at the Caracas Conference.

    Mexico's "La Voz de Mexico" published an article denouncing the conspiracies of U.S. imperialism in Latin America. The article stated: "The Latin American people will oppose the anti-national agreements concluded at this conference and will never recognize them. The people of Latin American countries believe that the Caracas Conference only intends to drag them into war and enslavement, attempting to force them to believe in fabricated communist threats. However, based on their own experience and deep convictions, they all believe that the real threat to our countries comes from North American imperialism – the main enemy of our national interests comes from that side."

### 墨西哥报纸斥美国侵犯拉丁美洲各国利益

1954-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    墨西哥报纸斥美国侵犯拉丁美洲各国利益
    据新华社讯：许多拉丁美洲国家的报纸继续谴责美国在加拉加斯会议上推行奴役政策。
    墨西哥的“墨西哥呼声报”刊载了一篇谴责美帝国主义在拉丁美洲的阴谋活动的文章，文章中写道：“拉丁美洲的人民群众将反对在这次会议上缔结的反民族的协定，并永远也不承认这种协定。拉丁美洲各国人民认为，加拉加斯会议只是想把他们拖进战争和奴役的境地，企图硬叫他们相信捏造的共产主义威胁，但是根据他们亲身的经验和深刻的信念，他们都认为，对我们这些国家的真正的威胁是来自北美帝国主义——我们民族利益的主要敌人是来自那一方面。”
