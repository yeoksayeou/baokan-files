    Our Great Motherland
    The Pearl River, the largest river in South China, ranks second only to the Yangtze River in terms of transportation volume among all rivers in the country. Guangzhou is the transportation hub of the Pearl River. Ships and rafts frequently and busily sail on the river.

    我们伟大的祖国
