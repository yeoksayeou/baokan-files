Title: Kim Ung-gi and Other Delegation Leaders Visit Yin Weichen Vegetable Production Cooperative; Deputy Delegation Leader Li Yong-ho Reports to Military Commission Directly Subordinate Organs and PLA Troops Stationed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 20th:

    Kim Ung-gi, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, along with deputy heads Kim Dal-hyun, Li Dong-ying, and representatives Gye Eung-sang and four others, accompanied by Chai Zemin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, visited the Yin Weichen Vegetable Production Cooperative in the suburbs of the city on the afternoon of the 20th and held a discussion with the cooperative members.
    The cooperative members warmly received the envoys of the Korean people. During the discussion, Yin Weichen said that he had participated in the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea for慰问in October last year and saw the Korean people's love and support for the Chinese People's Volunteers. Yin Weichen introduced the development of the vegetable production cooperative. He said that when the cooperative was established in 1952, there were only eight households, and now it has grown to one hundred and forty-eight households. Yin Weichen said that the expanded vegetable production cooperative must do a good job in this year's production to support the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean people in their post-war construction.
    Kim Ung-gi, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, and the representatives asked Yin Weichen about his experience in running the cooperative and said that they would introduce these experiences to Korean farmers.
    After the discussion, the representatives of the delegation visited the vegetable greenhouses and storage cellars of the Yin Weichen Vegetable Production Cooperative.

     Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: The directly subordinate organs of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission and the Chinese People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Beijing held a report meeting on the afternoon of the 20th, inviting Li Yong-ho, Deputy Head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, to give a report.
    The attendees included Li Yong-ho, Deputy Head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, Deputy Minister of the National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Commander of the Navy of the Korean People's Army, and representatives Li Jun-geun, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, "Republic Hero" Ryu Man-soo, Jang Hyun-hwa, Park Chan-soo, Choi Bun-ok, female tank driver of the Korean People's Army, and model combatant of the Korean People's Army.
      Also attending the meeting were Liu Zhijian, Director of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, Fang Qiang, Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, Duan Dezhang, Deputy Director of the Political Department, Wu Tao, Director of the Political Department directly under the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and guards hero He Fuxiang, combat hero Liang Rongbao, model worker Wang Zhansheng, Shen Yunfen, and representatives from various organs and units, totaling more than 800 people.
    Li Yong-ho reported on the great victories achieved by the Korean people and the Korean People's Army in the anti-aggression war of more than three years and the many heroic deeds they displayed in the struggle; reported on the high degree of internationalist unity and friendship shown by the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers in coordinated operations; reported on the many deeds of the volunteer soldiers in helping the Korean people in post-war reconstruction. Li Yong-ho also congratulated the Chinese People's Liberation Army on its greater achievements and victories in the cause of defending national construction and world peace. Li Yong-ho's report was warmly welcomed by the attendees.

### 金应基团长等访问殷维臣蔬菜生产合作社  李永镐副团长向军委直属机关和解放军驻京部队作报告

1954-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    金应基团长等访问殷维臣蔬菜生产合作社
    李永镐副团长向军委直属机关和解放军驻京部队作报告
   【新华社二十日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基、副团长金达铉、李东英和代表桂应祥等七人，由北京市人民政府副秘书长柴泽民陪同，在二十日下午访问了市郊殷维臣蔬菜生产合作社，并和社员们举行了座谈。
    社员们热情地接待了朝鲜人民的使者。座谈中，殷维臣说，他去年十月曾参加中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团去过英雄的朝鲜，看到朝鲜人民对中国人民志愿军的热爱和支援。殷维臣向客人们介绍了蔬菜生产合作社的发展经过。他说，该社在一九五二年成立时只有八户，现在已发展到一百四十八户。殷维臣说，扩大了的蔬菜生产合作社一定要做好今年的生产工作，以支援中国人民志愿军和支援朝鲜人民进行战后的建设。
    朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基和代表们向殷维臣询问了办社经验，并表示要把这些经验介绍给朝鲜农民。
    座谈结束后，代表团的代表们参观了殷维臣蔬菜生产合作社培植蔬菜的温房和贮藏产品的菜窖。
     【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会直属机关和中国人民解放军驻京部队，二十日下午举行报告会，请朝鲜人民访华代表团副团长李永镐作报告。
    参加大会的有朝鲜人民访华代表团副团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫省副相、朝鲜人民军海军司令官李永镐、代表朝鲜人民军总政治局宣传部副部长李浚根、“共和国英雄”柳万洙、张贤化、朴赞洙，朝鲜人民军坦克女司机、朝鲜人民军模范战斗员崔粉玉。
      参加大会的有中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部宣传部部长刘志坚，中国人民解放军海军副司令员方强、政治部副主任段德彰，人民革命军事委员会直属政治部主任吴涛和警卫英雄贺福祥、战斗英雄梁荣宝、模范工作者王占胜、沈蕴芬和各机关、部队代表等共八百多人。
    李永镐在会上报告了朝鲜人民和朝鲜人民军在三年多的反侵略战争中所取得的伟大胜利及他们在斗争中所表现的许多英勇事迹；报告了朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军在协同作战中所表现的高度的国际主义的团结和友谊；报告了志愿军战士在战后帮助朝鲜人民进行重建工作的许多事迹。李永镐并在报告中祝贺中国人民解放军在保卫祖国建设和世界和平的事业中，取得更大的成就和胜利。李永镐的报告，受到到会人员的热烈欢迎。
