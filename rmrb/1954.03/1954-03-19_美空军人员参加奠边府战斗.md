Title: US Air Force Personnel Participate in Dien Bien Phu Battle
Author:
Date: 1954-03-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. US Air Force combat personnel are participating in the current battle of Dien Bien Phu along with flight personnel from the notorious Claire Chennault's "Civil Air Transport Company." These Air Force combat personnel were denied by the US government as being "non-combatants." Chennault company sources admit that one transport plane has been destroyed by the Vietnamese People's Army's artillery fire.

According to Associated Press reporter Hampson on the 16th, twenty-four pilots from Chennault's company, "with the help of six other US Air Force combat personnel," are flying "at least ten US Air Force C-119 Flying Boxcar transport planes" over Indochina. The acting manager of this company, Bridge, admitted: These American flight personnel are "airdropping American supplies and French paratroopers" onto the Dien Bien Phu battlefield. Bridge also said that he was prepared to increase the twelve C-119 aircraft "loaned to the French" to sixteen in order to "keep at least twelve aircraft constantly in operation."

### 美空军人员参加奠边府战斗

1954-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    美空军人员参加奠边府战斗
      【新华社十八日讯】美国空军战斗人员和劣迹昭彰的陈纳德“民用航空运输公司”的飞行人员一起参加目前正在奠边府进行的战斗。这些空军战斗人员美国政府曾抵赖为“非战斗人员”。陈纳德公司人士承认，已有运输机一架被越南人民军的炮火击毁。
    据美联社记者汉普逊十六日报道，陈纳德公司的二十四名驾驶员，“在其他六名美国空军战斗人员的帮助下”，正在驾驶“至少有十架美国空军C一一九型飞行车厢运输机”在印度支那上空活动。这个公司的代理经理布立奇承认：这些美国飞行人员正在把美国的供应品和法国的降落伞部队，“空投到奠边府战场上”。布立奇还说，他准备把“借给法国人”的十二架C一一九型飞机增加到十六架，以便“至少保持有十二架飞机不断地进行活动”。
