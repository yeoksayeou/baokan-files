Title: Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Mao from the Capital's Women's Gathering Commemorating International Women's Day "March 8th"
Author: 
Date: 1954-03-08
Page: 1

    The Capital's Women's Gathering Commemorating International Women's Day "March 8th"
    Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Mao
Respected Chairman Mao:
    Under the leadership and with the kind concern of the Chinese Communist Party and yourself, the women of the capital have continuously improved their political awareness and contributed their strength to the construction of the motherland. As we joyously celebrate our own festival, International Women's Day "March 8th," in the second year of the country's planned economic construction, we would like to extend to you our cordial and lofty respects!
    The general line for the nation during the transition period, as instructed by you, illuminates the road for the people of the whole country to gradually transition to a happy socialist society, and illuminates the road for the complete emancipation of women. We firmly assure you that under your leadership, together with the people of the whole country, we will actively produce, work hard, and study hard, to build our country into a great socialist country and realize the complete emancipation of women! We respectfully wish you good health.
    The Capital's Women's Gathering Commemorating International Women's Day "March 8th"
    March 7, 1954

### 首都各界妇女纪念“三八”国际妇女节大会  给毛泽东主席的致敬电

1954-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界妇女纪念“三八”国际妇女节大会
    给毛泽东主席的致敬电敬爱的毛主席：
    首都妇女在中国共产党和您的领导与亲切关怀下，不断地提高政治觉悟，为祖国的建设事业贡献了自己的力量。当我们在祖国开始了有计划经济建设的第二年，欢欣鼓舞地纪念自己的节日——“三八”国际妇女节的时候，谨向您致以亲切的、崇高的敬礼！
    您指示的国家在过渡时期的总路线，照耀着全国人民逐步过渡到幸福的社会主义社会的大道，照耀着妇女彻底解放的大道。我们坚决地向您保证：一定要在您的领导下，和全国人民一道，积极生产、努力工作、努力学习，为把我国建设成为一个伟大的社会主义国家，实现妇女彻底解放事业而奋斗！　敬祝身体健康
    首都各界妇女纪念“三八”国际妇女节大会
    一九五四年三月七日
