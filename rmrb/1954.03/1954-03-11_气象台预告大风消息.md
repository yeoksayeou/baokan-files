Title: Weather Forecast Announces Gale Warning
Author:
Date: 1954-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 10th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a gale warning at 9:00 PM on the 10th: Starting on the morning of the 11th, southern Hebei, northern Henan, Shaanxi, Shanxi, and the Bohai Sea area will experience northerly winds of around force six, with the windy area moving from north to south.

### 气象台预告大风消息

1954-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台预告大风消息
   【新华社十日讯】中央气象台十日下午九时发布大风消息：十一日早晨起，河北南部、河南北部、陕西、山西和渤海一带将有六级左右的偏北风，风区从北向南移动。
