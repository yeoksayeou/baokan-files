Title: Comrade Khrushchev Meets with Voters and Delivers a Speech; Shvernik Meets with Voters in Sverdlovsk
Author:
Date: 1954-03-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th dispatch from Moscow: According to TASS, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and candidate for Deputy of the Supreme Soviet, N.S. Khrushchev, met with voters from the Kalinin constituency in Moscow on the 7th and delivered a speech at the voters' meeting. Workers, engineers, technicians, scientists, doctors, teachers, students, housewives, and representatives of the Soviet Army from various factories in the Kalinin constituency attended the meeting. When Khrushchev took his seat on the podium, all the voters present stood up and applauded warmly.

The meeting was presided over by Sokolov, the secretary of the Moscow Kalinin District Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. Many voters spoke enthusiastically at the meeting. They talked about the achievements of the Soviet state in various fields, as well as their labor achievements in welcoming the election of the Supreme Soviet. They unanimously stated that they would enthusiastically vote for Khrushchev as a deputy of the Supreme Soviet on election day (March 14th). Zabdálov, a lathe operator from the "Milling Cutter" factory, said: Our factory unanimously nominated Comrade Khrushchev, an outstanding activist of the Communist Party and the Soviet state, as a candidate. Many brilliant achievements of the Muscovites are inseparable from his name. By voting for Khrushchev, we are expressing our unanimous trust and support for the policies of our beloved Communist Party. Grigoriev, an engineer from the "Hammer and Sickle" Steel Factory, spoke about the production competition launched by the factory workers to welcome the election: The staff of our factory has guaranteed to complete the first quarter plan ahead of schedule, increase the production of hundreds of tons of steel and steel products, and consumer goods worth tens of thousands of rubles. Today, I am very happy to tell everyone that all the employees of our factory are victoriously fulfilling their guarantees. Corresponding academician of the Academy of Sciences Kirilin, doctor Makarova and others also spoke at the meeting.

Then, Khrushchev delivered a long speech to the voters. He pointed out that the people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union are closely united around their Communist Party Central Committee and the Soviet government. They wholeheartedly agree with and unanimously support the policies formulated by the Party. Khrushchev spoke in detail about the achievements and future tasks of the Soviet people under the leadership of the Communist Party in the economic, political, and cultural fields. He especially emphasized the recent series of resolutions of the Communist Party and the government on rapidly developing agriculture, light industry, and the food industry while developing heavy industry to further improve the people's living standards, and pointed out the importance of expanding grain production and reclaiming land in agriculture. Khrushchev also talked about strengthening the Soviet state and the Soviet Union's policy of maintaining peace. Finally, he called on the Soviet people to strive for the victory of the Communist Party and the non-Party people's alliance in the current election of the Soviet Supreme Soviet, and to strive for the continuous progress of the strong Soviet motherland.

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th dispatch from Sverdlovsk: Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, N.M. Shvernik, met with voters from the Sverdlovsk constituency at the Sverdlovsk Opera and Ballet Theatre on the 7th. About two thousand people attended the meeting.

Those who spoke at the meeting included Peter, the representative of the Sverdlovsk Party organization of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Masly, a worker from the Ural Heavy Machinery Manufacturing Factory, Smirnova, a female member of a collective farm, and Pushkareva, a professor from the Ural Polytechnic Institute. They pointed out that the working people of Sverdlovsk City and Sverdlovsk Oblast unanimously nominated Shvernik as a candidate and expressed their satisfaction with his acceptance of the nomination. They called on all voters to unanimously vote for the candidates of the Communist Party and the non-Party people's alliance.

Shvernik spoke at the meeting. He talked about the achievements made by the Soviet people under the leadership of the Communist Party in the economic and cultural fields in 1953, and pointed out the new tasks of further improving the material and cultural living standards of the people. Finally, he cheered for the Soviet people - the builders of a communist society, for the Communist Party and the government of the Soviet Union, and the audience burst into prolonged applause.

### 赫鲁晓夫同志会见选民发表演说  什维尔尼克在斯维德洛夫斯克和选民会见

1954-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫同志会见选民发表演说
    什维尔尼克在斯维德洛夫斯克和选民会见
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫，七日在莫斯科加里宁选区的选民大会上和选民会晤并发表了演说。参加大会的有加里宁选区各工厂工人、工程师、技术人员、科学工作者、医师、教师、学生、家庭主妇和苏军代表。当赫鲁晓夫在主席台上就座时，到会的选民全体起立，热烈鼓掌。
    大会由苏联共产党莫斯科加里宁区委员会书记索科洛夫主持。许多选民在会上都热情地讲了话。他们谈到了苏维埃国家各方面的成就，以及他们在迎接最高苏维埃选举中所取得的劳动成绩。他们一致表示要在选举日（三月十四日）那一天热烈投票选举赫鲁晓夫为最高苏维埃代表。“铣刀”工厂的车工扎布达洛夫说：我们工厂曾一致提出共产党和苏维埃国家的杰出活动家赫鲁晓夫同志为候选人。莫斯科人的许多光辉的事迹是和他的名字分不开的。我们投票选举赫鲁晓夫，就是表示我们一致信任和支持我们亲爱的共产党的政策。“镰刀与锤子”钢铁工厂的工程师格里戈耶夫在谈到该厂工人展开生产竞赛迎接选举的情形时说：我们工厂的工作人员曾保证提前完成第一季度计划，增产数百吨钢和钢材，以及价值数万卢布的消费品。今天我很高兴地向大家说，我们工厂的全体职工正在胜利地完成自己的保证。在会上发言的还有科学院通讯院士基里林、医生马卡罗娃等。
    接着，赫鲁晓夫向选民们发表了长篇演说。他指出，苏联各族人民紧密地团结在自己的共产党中央委员会和苏维埃政府的周围。他们衷心赞同并一致拥护党所制定的政策。赫鲁晓夫详细谈到了苏联人民在共产党的领导下在经济、政治、文化各方面所获得的成就和今后的任务。他特别强调最近共产党和政府关于在发展重工业的同时急速发展农业、轻工业和食品工业以进一步提高人民生活的一连串决议，并指出了在农业方面扩展谷物生产及开垦土地的重要性。赫鲁晓夫还谈到了加强苏维埃国家和苏联维护和平的政策。他最后号召苏联人民争取共产党和非党人民联盟在本届苏联最高苏维埃选举中的胜利，争取强大的苏维埃祖国的不断进步。
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社斯维德洛夫斯克讯：全苏工会中央理事会主席尼·米·什维尔尼克，七日在斯维德洛夫斯克的歌剧和巴蕾舞剧院会见了斯维德洛夫斯克选区的选民。到会的约有两千人。
    在会上讲话的有苏联共产党斯维德洛夫斯克党组织的代表皮特尔、乌拉尔重型机器制造厂工人马斯利、集体农庄女庄员斯米尔诺娃、乌拉尔综合技术学院教授普什卡列娃等。他们指出：斯维德洛夫斯克市和斯维德洛夫斯克州的劳动人民一致提名什维尔尼克为候选人，并对他接受提名表示满意。他们号召所有的选民一致投票选举共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人。
    什维尔尼克在会上讲话。他谈到一九五三年苏联人民在共产党的领导下在经济和文化各方面所取得的成就，并指出了进一步提高人民物质和文化生活水平的新任务。他最后为苏联人民——共产主义社会的建设者，为苏联共产党和政府欢呼，全场响起了经久不息的掌声。
