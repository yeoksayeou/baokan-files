Title: Polish United Workers' Party Second Congress Continues; CPSU Central Committee First Secretary Khrushchev Delivers Congratulatory Speech; Representatives of the Chinese Communist Party and the French Communist Party Deliver Congratulatory Speeches Respectively
Author:
Date: 1954-03-14
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 13th) According to the Polish Press Agency in Warsaw: The Second Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party continued on the 11th.

    In the morning session, Wa. Rozga (formerly translated as S. Rozna), Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of the Party, made a report of the Central Auditing Commission on the second item on the agenda.

    After Rozga's report, the representatives launched a heated discussion on the first and second items on the agenda. Those who spoke at the morning and afternoon sessions included: Polish United Workers' Party Central Political Bureau members A. Zawadzki and R. Zambrowski, Minister of Culture and Art W. Sokolski, Polish United Workers' Party Warsaw Committee First Secretary Pawlak, Krakow Provincial Committee First Secretary Titkov, Stalinogrod Provincial Committee First Secretary Olszewski, Opole Provincial Committee First Secretary Nowak, and Marceli Nowotko Agricultural Production Cooperative Director Francis, of Poznan Province. In their speeches, they pointed out the great achievements made by the Polish people in all aspects of socialist construction under the leadership of the United Workers' Party. They also put forward criticisms and opinions on the shortcomings existing in the work of the Party and the government.

    Amidst prolonged applause, the head of the Soviet Communist Party delegation, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, Khrushchev, addressed the meeting. He also read out the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party to the Congress. After the congratulatory message was read, the entire audience stood up and cheered for the Soviet Communist Party.

    The Chinese Communist Party representative, Zeng Yongquan, also read out the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party at the meeting. Then, the representatives stood up to pay tribute to the People's Republic of China, which is advancing victoriously along the road of peaceful construction.

    French Communist Party representative Guyot also delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting and was warmly welcomed by the participants.

### 波兰统一工人党第二次代表大会继续举行  苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫在会上致贺词  中国共产党和法国共产党代表分别致贺词

1954-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党第二次代表大会继续举行
    苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫在会上致贺词
    中国共产党和法国共产党代表分别致贺词
   【新华社十三日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰统一工人党第二次代表大会在十一日继续举行。
    在上午会议上，党中央检查委员会主席瓦·罗兹加（前译斯·罗兹纳）就第二项议程作了中央检查委员会的报告。
    罗兹加报告后，代表们就对第一、二项议程展开了热烈的讨论。在上午和下午会议上发言的有：波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员亚·萨瓦茨基和罗·萨姆布罗夫斯基，文化艺术部部长沃·索科尔斯基，波兰统一工人党华沙委员会第一书记帕夫拉克、克拉科夫省委员会第一书记蒂特科夫、斯大林诺格罗德省委员会第一书记奥尔谢夫斯基、奥波尔省委员会第一书记诺瓦克，波兹南省玛塞丽·诺沃蒂科农业生产合作社主任弗朗西等。他们在发言中指出了波兰人民在统一工人党的领导下在社会主义建设各方面所取得的巨大成就。他们还对党和政府工作中存在的缺点提出了批评和意见。
    在全场经久不息的掌声中，苏联共产党代表团团长、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫在会上致词。他还宣读了苏联共产党中央委员会致大会的贺词。贺词宣读毕，全场起立为苏联共产党欢呼。
    中国共产党代表曾涌泉也在大会上宣读了中国共产党中央委员会的贺词。接着，代表们起立向沿着和平建设的道路胜利前进的中华人民共和国致敬。
    法国共产党代表葛约也在会上致贺词，受到与会者的热烈欢迎。
