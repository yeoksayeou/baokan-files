Title: Grand Gathering in Jakarta Celebrates the Closing of the Indonesian Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-03-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta: TASS, Jakarta—Over 100,000 people participated in a grand gathering in Jakarta on the 21st to celebrate the closing of the Fifth Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party. The working people of Jakarta and farmers from the suburbs attended the rally, carrying banners that read: "Long Live the Fifth Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party—Congress of National Independence, Freedom, Democracy, and International Peace."

    The General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, D.N. Aidit, delivered a speech at the meeting, summarizing the work of the Party Congress and elaborating on the tasks of the democratic forces in preparing for the upcoming elections.

### 雅加达盛会庆祝印尼共产党代表大会闭幕

1954-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    雅加达盛会庆祝印尼共产党代表大会闭幕
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社雅加达讯：十万以上的人参加二十一日在雅加达举行的庆祝印尼共产党第五次代表大会闭幕的大会。雅加达的劳动人民和四郊的农民拿着横旗参加大会。横旗上写着：“印尼共产党第五次代表大会——民族独立、自由、民主与国际和平的代表大会万岁”。
    印尼共产党总书记艾地在会上发表演说，他总结了党代表大会的工作，并详细论述了民主力量为将要到来的选举而作准备的任务。
