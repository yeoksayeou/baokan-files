Title: Our Journalists' Delegation Returns Home from Soviet Union

Author: 
Date: 1954-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd. The Chinese journalists' delegation visiting the "Pravda" newspaper left Moscow by plane on the evening of the 1st to return home. Seeing them off at the airport were "Pravda" Editor-in-Chief Shepilov, members of the "Pravda" Editorial Board, and editorial staff from the People's Democratic Countries Department. "Pravda's" former Beijing correspondent, Dzhilyusin, will accompany the delegation to the Sino-Soviet border. Also seeing them off at the airport was Ge Baoquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our Embassy in the Soviet Union. Accompanying the delegation to Beijing was Domagotskikh, "Pravda's" new correspondent in Beijing.
The delegation spent fifty days studying at "Pravda" and visited Moscow and Leningrad. Before leaving, the delegation held a reception to bid farewell to "Pravda" and the Soviet press.

### 我新闻工作者代表团离苏返国

1954-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我新闻工作者代表团离苏返国
    【新华社莫斯科二日电】中国新闻工作者访问“真理报”代表团于一日晚乘飞机离开莫斯科回国。到机场送行的有“真理报”总编辑谢皮洛夫、“真理报”编委会委员和人民民主国家部编辑人员。“真理报”前驻北京记者杰柳辛将送代表团到苏中边境。到机场送行的还有我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办戈宝权。随同代表团去北京的还有“真理报”新任驻北京记者多玛郭茨基赫。
    代表团在“真理报”进行了五十天的学习，并在莫斯科和列宁格勒作了参观。代表团行前曾举行招待会，向“真理报”和苏联新闻界告别。
