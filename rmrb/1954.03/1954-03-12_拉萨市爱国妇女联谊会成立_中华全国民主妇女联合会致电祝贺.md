Title: Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation Established; All-China Democratic Women's Federation Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author:

Date: 1954-03-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation Established
All-China Democratic Women's Federation Sends Congratulatory Telegram

    More than 600 women from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Lhasa gathered to commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day. At the commemoration meeting, the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation was formally established. The meeting was presided over by Yaxi Tsering Dolma, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation. Zhang Jingwu, Representative of the Central People's Government, General Tan Guansan, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Kalon Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Representative of the Tibetan Local Government, and Wangdu, Representative of the Panchen Kanpo Lija Office in Lhasa, also attended the meeting and spoke, congratulating the formal establishment of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation. In his speech, General Zhang Jingwu called on women from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Lhasa to unite more closely through their own organizations and contribute more to the construction of a new Tibet and the defense of world peace. After Tsetan Dolkar made a work summary of the Preparatory Committee of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation and a report on the work tasks of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation at the meeting, the meeting approved the charter of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation with warm applause and unanimously approved Yaxi Tsering Dolma, Tsetan Dolkar, and Renzeng Buti as directors, Yanggang, Tsering Lhamo Rinchen Dolma, Tsering Zhuma, Yuezhong, and Li Jing as deputy directors, and Yu Shiyu and 24 others as committee members.
    The conference also received pennants and congratulatory messages from General Zhang Jingwu, Representative of the Central People's Government, the Dalai Lama, the Tibetan Local Government, the Panchen Lama's Office in Lhasa, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the Southwest Working Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and various organs and troops of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Lhasa. The pennant presented by the Dalai Lama was inscribed in Tibetan with the words "Study hard, love the motherland, and build a new Tibet."
    In the evening, women of all ethnic groups held a joint celebration, singing and cheering to their hearts' content, celebrating the establishment of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation.

To: Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation, Tibet, Lhasa

    We are delighted to learn that your association will be formally established on March 8th, and we extend our heartfelt and warm congratulations. With your efforts, the close friendship of the women of Lhasa is developing day by day. We sincerely hope that you will further unite women from all walks of life in Lhasa on the basis of your past work and actively participate in and support various construction tasks, and strive to develop and build a new Tibet and defend world peace.

                  All-China Democratic Women's Federation

### 拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会成立  中华全国民主妇女联合会致电祝贺

1954-03-12
第3版()
专栏：

    拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会成立
    中华全国民主妇女联合会致电祝贺
    拉萨市各族各界妇女六百多人集会，纪念“三八”国际妇女节。在纪念会上，并正式成立了拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会。大会由拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会筹备委员会主任委员尧西·泽仁卓玛主持。中央人民政府代表张经武将军、中国人民解放军西藏军区政治委员谭冠三将军、西藏地方政府代表噶伦阿沛·阿旺晋美和班禅堪布会议厅驻拉萨代表旺德也都参加了这次大会，并在会上讲话，祝贺拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会的正式成立。张经武将军在讲话中号召拉萨市各族各界妇女通过自己的组织，更紧密地团结起来，为建设新西藏和保卫世界和平贡献出更大的力量。泽丹卓噶在大会上，作了关于拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会筹备委员会的工作总结和拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会工作任务的报告后，大会在热烈的掌声中通过了拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会会章，并一致通过尧西·泽仁卓玛、泽丹卓噶、仁增布智为主任，杨岗、泽仁拉姆·仁钦卓玛、才仁珠玛、月仲、李静为副主任，于式玉等二十四人为委员。
    大会并收到中央人民政府代表张经武将军、达赖喇嘛、西藏地方政府、班禅驻拉萨办事处、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主妇女联合会西南区工作委员会和人民解放军驻拉萨各机关、部队赠送的锦旗、贺电。达赖喇嘛赠送的锦旗上用藏文写着“努力学习，热爱祖国，建设新西藏”的字样。
    晚上，各族妇女并举行了联欢晚会，她们尽情地欢呼歌唱，欢庆拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会的成立。
                                  （新华社）西藏拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会：
    欣闻你会将于三月八日正式成立，谨致衷心的热烈的祝贺。在你们的努力下，拉萨市妇女的亲密友谊，正日益发展，我们热诚希望你们在过去工作的基础上，进一步团结拉萨市各界妇女积极参加和支援各项建设工作，为发展和建设新西藏、为保卫世界和平而努力奋斗。
                  中华全国民主妇女联合会
