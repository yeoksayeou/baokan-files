Title: Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee Holds Meeting; Decides to Organize Group Discussions on the Initial Draft of the Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-03-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 24th—The 53rd meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held on March 16th. The meeting discussed the issue of organizing responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations to hold group discussions on the initial draft of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China. The meeting decided to invite the responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations and people from all walks of life to form seventeen groups to discuss the initial draft of the Constitution. The committee members present exchanged views on some specific issues of organizing this discussion.

### 政协全国委员会常务委员会举行会议  决定分组讨论宪法草案初稿

1954-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常务委员会举行会议
    决定分组讨论宪法草案初稿【新华社二十四日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第五十三次会议在三月十六日举行。会议讨论了组织各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人员分组讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿的问题。会议决定邀请各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人和各界人士共组成十七个小组讨论宪法草案初稿。到会各委员对组织这一讨论的若干具体问题交换了意见。
