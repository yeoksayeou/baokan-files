Title: British Business Representatives Arrive in Moscow for Business Negotiations Again
Author:
Date: 1954-03-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 17th: According to TASS, Mr. S. Mackinnon, Chairman of the British-Soviet Chamber of Commerce and Chairman of the British United Engineering Federation, as well as representatives from machine manufacturing companies such as "Kandall and Gent," "John Lange," and "Ward-Walter," have recently arrived in Moscow to conduct business negotiations with Soviet foreign trade organizations.

### 英商人代表又一批到莫斯科进行商务谈判

1954-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    英商人代表又一批到莫斯科进行商务谈判
   【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：英苏商会主席、英国机器制造联合会主席斯·马吉纳斯先生，以及“坎达尔和詹特”、“约翰·兰格”和“瓦尔德—沃尔特尔”等机器制造公司的代表们已在最近抵达莫斯科，和苏联对外贸易机构进行商务谈判。
