Title: Government Administration Council Holds 210th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-03-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 26th — The 210th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 25th. The meeting heard and approved the report made by Fu Zuoyi, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Water Resources, on the summary of four years of water conservancy work and future work tasks. The meeting also passed various appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行第二一零次会议

1954-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二一零次会议
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百一十次政务会议在二十五日下午举行。会议听了和批准了中央人民政府水利部傅作义部长所作的关于四年水利工作总结和今后工作任务的报告。会议并通过了各项任免案。
