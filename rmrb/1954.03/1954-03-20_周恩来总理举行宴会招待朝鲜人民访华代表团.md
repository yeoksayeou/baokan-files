Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Banquet to Host the Korean People's Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1954-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government held a banquet on the evening of the 19th to host the Korean People's Delegation to China and various artistic troupes.

The entire Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Chief of Delegation Kim Ung-gi, and the artistic troupes accompanying the delegation to our country attended the banquet.

Also present at the banquet were Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and personnel from the embassy.

Academician Yudin, Ambassador of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to China, and envoys from various People's Democratic countries to China also attended the banquet.

The Chinese side attending the banquet included: Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Deng Xiaoping, Chen Shutong, Peng Dehuai, Cheng Qian, Peng Zhen, Deng Zihui, Xi Zhongxun, Su Yu, Huang Kecheng, Zhang Zongxun, Xiao Hua, Zhang Zhizhang, Gao Kelin, Jia Tuofu, Xie Juezai, Luo Ruiqing, Bo Yibo, Zeng Shan, Teng Daiyuan, Zhang Bojun, Chen Shaoxian, Wang Kunlun, Luo Longji, Zhang Naiqi, Shao Lizi, Huang Shaohong, Peng Zemin, Ma Yinchu, Ye Jizhuang, Li Weihan, Lu Dingyi, Shen Yanbing, Liu Jingfan, Wang Heshou, Zhao Erlu, Chen Yu, Zhu Xuefan, Li Shucheng, Liang Xi, Fu Zuoyi, Li Dequan, Chu Tunan, Hu Yuzhi, Li Kenong, Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, Qian Zhiguang, Ding Xilin, Liu Zhiming, Wei Que, Cai Tingkai, Cao Juru, Xu Liqing, Xiao Jingguang, Liu Yalou, Chen Xilian, Wang Zhen, Chen Shiju, Zhou Shidi, Wu Han, Liu Yazi, Wang Shao'ao, Yan Xichun, Xu Deheng, Lin Yun, Lai Ruoyu, Zhang Yun, Liao Chengzhi, Qu Tangliang, Tian Demin, Li Zhuchen, Hou Debang, Zhu Kezhen, Cheng Zihua, Qian Junrui, Rong Gaotang, Peng Sike, Wang Zizhong, Zhao Fusan, Da Punsheng, Xia Yan, Yang Hansheng, Feng Xuefeng, Xiao San, Shi Dongshan, Zhou Weizhi, Wei Wei, Shi Lianxing, Ye Shengilan, as well as prominent figures from all walks of life and representatives of various fraternal ethnic groups in our country, totaling more than 300 people.

Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech at the banquet, welcoming the Korean People's Delegation to China. He said: "I believe that the Korean People's Delegation's enthusiastic visit and the outstanding performances of the various artistic troupes will greatly encourage the Chinese people and will further develop and consolidate the fighting friendship and close unity between the Chinese and Korean peoples. The strength of this friendship and unity is a powerful guarantee for peace in the Far East."

Chief of Delegation Kim Ung-gi delivered a speech expressing his gratitude. He said: "We, the Korean people, deeply recognize how precious the unbreakable friendship and unity forged between the Korean people under the leadership of the Korean Workers' Party and Marshal Kim Il-sung and the Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and its great leader Chairman Mao Zedong is, and regard consolidating and developing this friendship and unity as our sacred task."

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. During the meal, the hosts and guests toasted to the friendship between the peoples of Korea and China, and to the health of Marshal Kim Il-sung and Chairman Mao Zedong.

After the banquet, the distinguished guests attended an evening performance by Chinese artists. (Attached picture) Premier Zhou Enlai (right) and Chief of Delegation Kim Ung-gi (left) at the welcoming banquet.

### 周恩来总理举行宴会招待朝鲜人民访华代表团

1954-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理举行宴会招待朝鲜人民访华代表团
      【新华社十九日讯】中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来在十九日晚设宴招待朝鲜人民访华代表团和各艺术团体。
    以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团和随代表团前来我国的艺术团全体人员都出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一和使馆人员。
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻我国大使尤金院士和各人民民主国家驻我国的使节也出席了宴会。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：董必武、陈云、郭沫若、邓小平、陈叔通、彭德怀、程潜、彭真、邓子恢、习仲勋、粟裕、黄克诚、张宗逊、萧华、张志让、高克林、贾拓夫、谢觉哉、罗瑞卿、薄一波、曾山、滕代远、章伯钧、陈劭先、王昆仑、罗隆基、章乃器、邵力子、黄绍竑、彭泽民、马寅初、叶季壮、李维汉、陆定一、沈雁冰、刘景范、王鹤寿、赵尔陆、陈郁、朱学范、李书城、梁希、傅作义、李德全、楚图南、胡愈之、李克农、章汉夫、伍修权、钱之光、丁西林、刘芝明、韦悫、蔡廷锴、曹菊如、徐立清、萧劲光、刘亚楼、陈锡联、王震、陈士榘、周士第、吴晗、柳亚子、王绍鏊、严希纯、许德珩、林云、赖若愚、章蕴、廖承志、区棠亮、田德民、李烛尘、侯德榜、竺可桢、程子华、钱俊瑞、荣高棠、朋斯克、王梓仲、赵复三、达浦生、夏衍、阳翰笙、冯雪峰、萧三、史东山、周巍峙、魏巍、石联星、叶盛兰以及各界著名人士和我国各兄弟民族的代表等共三百多人。
    周恩来总理在宴会上致词，欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团来访。他说：我相信，朝鲜人民访华代表团这次的热情访问和各艺术团的卓越演出，将使中国人民获得极大的鼓舞，并将使中朝两国人民的战斗友谊和亲密无间的团结更加发展和巩固。这种友谊和团结的力量就是远东和平的有力保证。
    金应基团长致词表示谢意。他说：我们朝鲜人民都深刻地认识到，在朝鲜劳动党和金日成元帅领导下的朝鲜人民和在中国共产党及其伟大的领袖毛泽东主席领导下的中国人民所结成的牢不可破的友谊团结，是多么高贵的事情，并把巩固和发展这个友谊团结，看成是自己神圣的任务。
    宴会在热烈和友好的空气中进行。席间，宾主都为朝中两国人民的友谊，为金日成元帅和毛泽东主席的健康干杯。
    宴会后，贵宾们出席了由中国艺术家表演的晚会。（附图片）周恩来总理（右）和金应基团长（左）在欢迎宴会上。
