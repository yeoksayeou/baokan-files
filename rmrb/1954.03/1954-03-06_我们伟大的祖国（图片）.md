Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1954-03-06
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Xuzhangcheng Agricultural Production Cooperative in Fenghu Township, Fuzhou Suburban District, established before the spring plowing last year, has fully utilized the advantages of collective management, overcame last year's severe drought, and achieved a bumper harvest of sugarcane. These female members are busily harvesting sugarcane.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    去年春耕前成立的福州市郊区凤湖乡许章成农业生产合作社，充分发挥了集体经营的优越性，战胜了去年的严重旱灾，使甘蔗获得了丰收。这些女社员正在紧张地收割甘蔗。
