Title: Delhi Trade Union Organizations Jointly Host Delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1954-03-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 3rd: More than seventy trade union organizations in Delhi jointly held a reception on February 25th to welcome the delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, which is currently visiting India. Yuan Zhongxian, the Chinese Ambassador to India, was invited to attend the reception.

K.P. Shankara, Chairman of the Delhi Branch of the Indian National Trade Union Congress and a member of the Delhi State Legislative Assembly, welcomed the delegation's visit in his speech and expressed his hope that "the fraternal friendship between Indian and Chinese workers will continue to develop." He said that because this is the first Chinese workers' delegation to visit India, he felt particularly pleased to welcome them.

Li Jiebo, head of the delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, said in his reply that the delegation's visit to India has proven to be very beneficial. He said that sending more trade union delegations between India and China would help Indian and Chinese workers to better understand each other.

The reception was held in a pleasant atmosphere.

The delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions visited various trade union organizations on the morning of the 25th. On the 26th, the delegation left Delhi for Mumbai to visit.

### 德里工会组织联合招待我国总工会代表团

1954-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    德里工会组织联合招待我国总工会代表团
    【新华社新德里三日电】德里七十多个工会组织在二月二十五日联合举行招待会，欢迎现在印度访问的中华全国总工会代表团。中国驻印度大使袁仲贤应邀出席了招待会。
    印度全国职工大会德里分会主席、德里邦立法会议议员克·普·山卡拉在致词中欢迎代表团的访问，并表示希望“印度与中国工人之间的兄弟友谊继续不断地发展”。他说：因为这是第一个访问印度的中国工人代表团，因此在欢迎时感到特别的愉快。
    中华全国总工会代表团团长、中华全国总工会书记处书记李颉伯在答词中说：代表团在印度的访问证明是很有益的。他说：在印中两国之间互派更多的工会代表团，将有助于印度和中国工人相互间更好的了解。
    招待会在愉快的气氛中进行。
    中华全国总工会代表团二十五日晨访问了各工会组织。代表团在二十六日离开德里前往孟买访问。
