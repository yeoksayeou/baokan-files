Title: Japan "Sohyo" Holds Mass Meeting to Overthrow the Yoshida Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1954-03-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 2nd—A mass meeting to overthrow the Yoshida corrupt cabinet, sponsored by the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo) and other organizations, was held in Tokyo on the 1st. Approximately 15,000 people participated in the meeting.

The meeting passed a resolution opposing the American "Mutual Security Assistance," opposing the rearmament budget, opposing the deprivation of teachers' political rights, opposing the revision of the Police Law, and demanding a thorough investigation of corruption cases.

After the meeting, the participants held a demonstration. The marching crowd shouted slogans such as "Overthrow the Yoshida Cabinet" in front of the headquarters of Yoshida's Liberal Party and the Diet. The Yoshida government dispatched police to violently suppress the demonstration and arrested one demonstrator.

### 日本“总评”举行打倒吉田内阁群众大会

1954-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    日本“总评”举行打倒吉田内阁群众大会
    【新华社二日讯】东京消息：日本工会总评议会等团体主持的打倒吉田贪污内阁群众大会于一日在东京举行。参加大会的约有一万五千人。
    大会通过决议，反对美国“共同安全援助”，反对重整军备的预算，反对剥夺教职员政治权利，反对修改警察法，并要求彻查贪污案等。
    大会结束后，与会群众举行了示威游行。游行队伍在吉田自由党总部和国会门前高呼“打倒吉田内阁”的口号。吉田政府出动了警察横暴地进行镇压，并逮捕了示威者一人。
