Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Holds Meeting, Approves Transfer of Crimea to Ukraine
Author:
Date: 1954-03-01
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th: TASS, Moscow, 27th – The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has issued the following decree regarding the transfer of the Crimean Oblast of the Russian Federation to the Ukrainian Republic:
　　In view of the commonality of the Crimean Oblast with the economy of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic, the proximity of its territory, and close economic and cultural ties, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics hereby announces:
　　The joint application of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic to transfer the Crimean Oblast from the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic to the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic is approved.
　　K. Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics
　　N. Pegov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics
　　February 19, 1954, at the Moscow Kremlin
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th: According to TASS, Moscow, 27th – The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR held a meeting on the 19th to discuss and approve the joint application submitted by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Ukrainian Republic to transfer the Crimean Oblast from the Russian Federation to the Ukrainian Republic.
　　Representatives from the Russian Federation, the Ukrainian Republic, the Crimean Oblast, as well as the cities of Simferopol and Sevastopol, also attended the meeting.
　　Tarasov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, and Korotchenko, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Ukrainian Republic, spoke at the meeting, respectively, and read out the resolutions of their respective presidiums on the issue. Tarasov pointed out that due to the Crimean land's proximity to Ukraine, the commonality of their economies, and their close economic and cultural ties, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation resolved to request the transfer of the Crimean Oblast to the Ukrainian Republic, believing that this would help strengthen the fraternal ties between the Ukrainian and Russian peoples and would be in line with the overall interests of the Soviet state. Korotchenko said that the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Ukrainian Republic and the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian Republic discussed the proposal of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, considering the proposal as a friendly act, demonstrating the Russian people's infinite trust and love for the Ukrainian people. This decision would be met with gratitude and support from the entire Ukrainian people.
　　Shvernik, a member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Rashidov, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Kuusinen also spoke during the discussion. They explained the great significance of this action.
　　Subsequently, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR unanimously adopted the decree on transferring the Crimean Oblast from the Russian Federation to the Ukrainian Republic.
　　Finally, Voroshilov delivered a speech. He pointed out that the transfer of the Crimean Oblast to the Ukrainian Republic is an important action with national significance, which proves that in the great and strong fraternal family of all nationalities in the Soviet Union, the unity and unbreakable friendship between the Russian and Ukrainian peoples has been further strengthened. This important action once again proves that the relations between the various independent and autonomous socialist republics of the Soviet Union are based on true equality, genuine understanding, and respect for each other's interests in order to achieve common prosperity. Only in a country like the Soviet Union can some territorial issues between the constituent republics be resolved so justly. The historical development of the Crimean Oblast and its territorial and economic status are of great significance to the entire Soviet state. Both long ago and not long ago, enemies repeatedly attempted to seize the Crimean Peninsula from Russia, attempting to establish a military base here to attack Russia and Ukraine. But the Russian and Ukrainian peoples have always jointly dealt a head-on blow to the shameless aggressors and driven them out of Crimea. Crimea was, is, and always will be a Soviet fortress on the Black Sea. Voroshilov pointed out that this friendly action is taking place at a time when the Soviet people are solemnly celebrating a day of significant historical significance—the 300th anniversary of Ukraine's reunification with Russia. The friendship of all nationalities in the Soviet Union is one of the most important foundations of our multinational socialist country, and it is the source of its invincible strength, prosperity, and power. Under the leadership of our glorious Communist Party, the people of all nationalities in our country will continue to develop and strengthen their unbreakable brotherly friendship in the future.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团举行会议  批准将克里米亚划归乌克兰

1954-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团举行会议
    批准将克里米亚划归乌克兰
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十七日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布关于将俄罗斯联邦的克里米亚州划归乌克兰共和国的命令如下：
    鉴于克里米亚州与乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国经济的共同性、领土的接近和密切的经济与文化联系，苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团特宣布：
    批准俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团与乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团的联合申请，将克里米亚州自俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国划归乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国。
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团主席克·伏罗希洛夫
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团秘书纳·别哥夫
    一九五四年二月十九日　　于莫斯科克里姆林宫
     【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十七日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团十九日举行会议，讨论并批准了俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团和乌克兰共和国最高苏维埃主席团联合提出的将克里米亚州自俄罗斯联邦划归乌克兰共和国的申请。
    俄罗斯联邦、乌克兰共和国、克里米亚州以及森佛罗波尔市和塞瓦斯托波尔市的代表也参加了会议。
    俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫和乌克兰共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席科罗特钦科先后在会上发言，并宣读了各该主席团关于这一问题的决议。塔拉索夫指出，由于克里米亚土地毗邻乌克兰、它们经济的共同性以及它们的密切的经济与文化联系，俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团决议请求将克里米亚州划归乌克兰共和国，认为这将有助于加强乌克兰和俄罗斯人民之间的兄弟般的联系，而且符合苏维埃国家的总的利益。科罗特钦科说，乌克兰共和国最高苏维埃主席团和乌克兰共和国部长会议讨论了俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团的建议，认为这个建议是一个友好的行动，证明了俄罗斯人民对乌克兰人民的无限信任与热爱。这个决定将受到全体乌克兰人民的感激与拥护。
    在讨论中发言的还有苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员什维尔尼克，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席拉希多夫和库西宁。他们说明了这一行动的重大意义。
    接着，苏联最高苏维埃主席团一致通过了关于将克里米亚州自俄罗斯联邦划归乌克兰共和国的命令。
    最后，伏罗希洛夫发表了演说。他指出，将克里米亚州划归乌克兰共和国是一个具有全国意义的重要行动，这证明在苏联各民族的伟大、坚强的兄弟大家庭中，俄罗斯人民与乌克兰人民的团结和牢不可破的友谊有了进一步的加强。这个重要行动再一次证明：苏联的各个独立自主的社会主义加盟共和国之间的关系，是建立在真正的平等、确实的谅解和尊重彼此的利益以求达到共同繁荣的目的这样一个基础上的。只有在像苏联这样的国家里才能这样公正地解决各加盟共和国之间的一些领土问题。克里米亚州的历史发展及其领土地位和经济地位，对整个苏维埃国家都具有重大的意义。敌人无论是在很久以前以及不久以前，都曾一再地企图从俄国手中夺取克里米亚半岛，企图在这里建立进攻俄罗斯和乌克兰的军事基地。但是俄罗斯人民和乌克兰人民每一次都共同给了无耻的侵略者以迎头痛击，并把他们逐出了克里米亚，克里米亚曾经是、今后始终还是苏联在黑海上的要塞。伏罗希洛夫指出：这一友好的行动正值苏联人民隆重庆祝具有显著历史意义的日子——乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年的时候。苏联各民族的友谊就是我们多民族的社会主义国家的最重要的基础之一，就是它不可战胜的力量、繁荣和强大的泉源。在我们光荣的共产党的领导下，我国各族人民今后还将继续发展和加强自己的牢不可破的兄弟的友谊。
