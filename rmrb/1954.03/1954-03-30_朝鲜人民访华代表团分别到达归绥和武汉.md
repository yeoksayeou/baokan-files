Title: Korean People's Delegation to China Arrives in Gui Sui and Wuhan Respectively
Author:
Date: 1954-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Gui Sui, March 29th:

    Part of the Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by delegation leader Kim Ung-gi, and accompanying members of the art troupe, accompanied by Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, and Xu Ying, Deputy Director of the General Office of the North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, arrived in Guisui City, the capital of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, on the morning of the 28th.

    Those who welcomed them at the station included Wang Zaitian and Sun Lanfeng, Vice Chairmen of the Preparatory Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Consultative Committee; Hu Zhaoheng, Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region General Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association; Su Qianyi, Deputy Secretary of the Inner Mongolia Branch Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Yang Zhilin, Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government; Ruan Muhhan, Mayor of Guisui City; and responsible persons from various people's organizations and representatives from all ethnic groups, totaling more than 1,500 people.

    As the delegation members alighted from the train, shouts of "Kim Il-sung, Mao Zedong" and slogans of "Learn from the heroic Korean people" resounded. People wearing various ethnic costumes shook hands and embraced the delegation members.

    A grand welcoming ceremony was held at the station. Wang Zaitian delivered a speech on behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, warmly welcoming the arrival of the Korean People's Delegation to China. Delegation leader Kim Ung-gi delivered a reply speech, expressing his gratitude and admiration for the actions of the people of all ethnic groups in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region in actively participating in the Resist America, Aid Korea movement. In the evening, the Preparatory Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Consultative Committee and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region General Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association jointly hosted a banquet to welcome the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, March 29th:

    Part of the Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Deputy Leaders Li Yong-ho and Li Dong-young, and the Korean People's Army Ensemble, accompanied by Chu Tunan, Director of the Literacy Campaign Committee of the Central People's Government, arrived in Wuhan City at 8:20 pm on the 28th. At 11:15 pm, they boarded a special ship to visit and perform in various parts of the Southwest.

    Those who welcomed them at the station and saw them off at the dock included Chen Mingshu, Vice Chairman of the Central-South General Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association; Zhang Pinghua, Second Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Wuhan Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee; Li Erzhong, Chairman of the Wuhan Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association; Sun Yaohua, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central-South Administrative Committee; Chen Jingyu and Song Kanfu, Deputy Mayors of the Wuhan Municipal People's Government, as well as representatives of the stationed troops, responsible persons from local organizations of various democratic parties, people's organizations, and people from all walks of life.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团分别到达归绥和武汉

1954-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团分别到达归绥和武汉
    【新华社归绥二十九日电】以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员和随行艺术团人员，由中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林、中共中央华北局办公厅副主任徐英陪同，在二十八日上午到达内蒙古自治区首府——归绥市。
    到车站欢迎的，有内蒙古自治区协商委员会筹备委员会副主席王再天、孙兰峰，内蒙古自治区抗美援朝总分会副主席胡昭衡，中共中央内蒙古分局副书记苏谦益，内蒙古自治区人民政府副主席杨植霖，归绥市人民政府市长阮慕韩，以及各人民团体负责人，各族各界代表等共一千五百余人。
    当代表团人员下车时，“金日成、毛泽东”的欢呼声和“向英勇的朝鲜人民学习”的口号声响成一片。穿着各种民族服装的人们纷纷和代表团人员握手拥抱。
    在车站上举行了盛大的欢迎会。王再天代表内蒙古自治区各族人民致词，热烈欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团的来临。金应基团长致答词，对内蒙古自治区各族人民积极参加抗美援朝的行动表示感谢和敬佩。晚间，内蒙古自治区协商委员会筹备委员会和内蒙古自治区抗美援朝总分会联合设宴欢迎代表团。
    【新华社武汉二十九日电】以副团长李永镐和李东英为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员和朝鲜人民军协奏团，在中央人民政府扫除文盲工作委员会主任委员楚图南陪同下，于二十八日下午八时二十分到达武汉市；十一时十五分，乘专轮前往西南各地访问、演出。
    到车站欢迎和到码头欢送的，有中南抗美援朝总分会副主席陈铭枢，中共武汉市委员会第二书记、武汉市各界人民代表会议协商委员会主席张平化，武汉市抗美援朝分会主席李尔重，中南行政委员会副秘书长孙耀华，武汉市人民政府副市长陈经畬、宋侃夫以及驻军代表、各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体负责人和各界群众。
