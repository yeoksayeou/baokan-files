Title:慰问团慰问驻海南岛的解放军 (Comfort Delegation Visits PLA Troops Stationed on Hainan Island)
Author:
Date: 1954-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 10th: Representatives from the General Delegation and the Fifth Sub-delegation of the National People's Comfort Delegation to the People's Liberation Army have begun visiting the People's Liberation Army troops guarding the southern coastal frontier of the motherland – Hainan Island.

    The representatives of the Comfort Delegation arrived in Hainan Island on the 7th. On the 8th, a comfort meeting was held in Haikou City to extend greetings to all commanders and fighters of the Hainan Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the troops stationed on Hainan Island. Hu Keshi, Deputy Head of the General Delegation, delivered a message of comfort at the meeting, expressing respect and greetings to all commanders and fighters of the various service branches stationed on Hainan Island, as well as to all commanders and fighters who had persisted in the revolutionary guerrilla warfare on Hainan Island for more than 20 years. At the meeting, representatives of the Comfort Delegation also presented flags and flowers to the leaders of the troops and heroic models, and pinned commemorative badges on them. After the meeting, the art troupe and drama troupe of the Comfort Delegation held comfort performances.
    After visiting the troops stationed near Haikou City, the representatives of the Comfort Delegation will go to various troops stationed on Hainan Island to conduct comfort activities.

### 慰问团慰问驻海南岛的解放军

1954-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    慰问团慰问驻海南岛的解放军
    【新华社广州十日电】全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团总团和第五总分团的代表，已开始慰问守卫在祖国南方海防前哨——海南岛的人民解放军。
    慰问团代表于七日到达海南岛，八日在海口市举行慰问大会，慰问中国人民解放军海南军区和驻守在海南岛的各部队的全体指挥员、战斗员。慰问团副总团长胡克实在会上致慰问词，向驻海南岛的各兵种部队及曾经坚持海南岛二十多年革命游击战争的全体指挥员、战斗员表示敬意和慰问。会上，慰问团代表还向部队首长和英雄模范们献旗、献花和佩带纪念章。会后，由慰问团的文艺工作团、剧团举行慰问演出。
    慰问团的代表慰问驻海口市附近地区的部队后，将分头深入驻海南岛各地的部队进行慰问。
