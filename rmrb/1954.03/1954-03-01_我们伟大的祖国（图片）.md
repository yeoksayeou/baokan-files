    Our Great Motherland
    The Chinese People's Liberation Army guarding the border of the motherland has established a close friendship with the people of all ethnic groups. This Tajik herdsman from the Pamir Plateau, during the winter slack season, used camels to transport grain to the troops.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-03-01

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    守卫祖国边疆的中国人民解放军和各族人民建立了亲密的友谊。这位帕米尔高原的塔吉克族牧民，在冬闲时用骆驼给部队运来了粮食。
