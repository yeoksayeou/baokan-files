Title: The CPSU Central Committee Held a Plenary Session and Passed a Resolution on Khrushchev's Report on Agriculture
Author:
Date: 1954-03-07
Page: 1

    The CPSU Central Committee Held a Plenary Session
    Passed a Resolution on Khrushchev's Report on Agriculture
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th] TASS, Moscow, 6th: Announcement of the Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union:
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a plenary session a few days ago.
    The meeting heard and discussed the report by N.S. Khrushchev on further expanding grain production in the Soviet Union and reclaiming virgin and abandoned land, and passed corresponding resolutions.
    Workers from local party organizations, Soviets, agricultural and procurement agencies, collective farm chairmen, and state farm workers participated in the discussion of this issue.

### 苏共中央委员会举行全体会议  就赫鲁晓夫所作关于农业的报告通过决议

1954-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央委员会举行全体会议
    就赫鲁晓夫所作关于农业的报告通过决议
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科六日电：苏联共产党中央委员会全体会议公告：
    苏联共产党中央委员会几天前举行了全体会议。
    会议听取并讨论了尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫所作关于进一步扩展苏联谷物生产及开垦处女地和荒废的土地的报告，并通过了相应的决议。
    地方党组织、苏维埃、农业和采购机关的工作人员，集体农庄主席和国营农场的工作人员参加了这个问题的讨论。
