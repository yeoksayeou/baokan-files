Title: Delegation Arrives at Changshan Islands to Console Coastal Defense Troops; Activities to Console the PLA Unfold at the Foziling Reservoir Construction Site
Author: 
Date: 1954-03-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency: Representatives from the General Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, the First General Sub-Delegation (Volunteer Army), and the Fourth General Sub-Delegation arrived at the Changshan Islands on the 5th to comfort the People's Liberation Army coastal defense troops who vigilantly guard the front lines of coastal defense. Representatives from the delegation and members of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Political Department Art Troupe, who accompanied the delegation, have been carrying out extensive and in-depth consolation activities on the islands for several days. On the 6th, the delegation held a consolation meeting to comfort the People's Liberation Army stationed on the Changshan Islands. At the consolation meeting, Li Gang, Deputy Head of the First General Sub-Delegation, He Lin, Representative of the Fourth General Sub-Delegation, Wang Zibin, Deputy Head of the Second Sub-Delegation, and others spoke. On behalf of the Communist Party of China, the Central People's Government, Chairman Mao, and the people of the whole country, they extended their highest respect and cordial greetings to the heroes who guard the forefront of the motherland's coastal defense day and night. Representatives of the delegation also presented flags and gifts to the troops at the meeting, and awarded commemorative medals to the leaders of the troops. After the meeting, the Volunteer Army Art Troupe gave a brilliant consolation performance for the soldiers. On the 7th, representatives of the delegation visited the leadership organs of the troops and listened to reports from the leaders of the troops. On the 8th and 9th, representatives of the delegation, the art troupe, and the film team set off in groups to conduct consolation activities in depth on various islands, ships, and companies. On the 9th, Li Gang, Deputy Head of the First General Sub-Delegation, and some representatives went to the ships to comfort the sailors and had close conversations with all the sailors. Representatives of the delegation also went to the cemetery of the martyrs who heroically sacrificed themselves for the liberation of the Changshan Islands to sweep the tombs and presented wreaths to pay their respects.

Xinhua News Agency: From the 5th to the 12th, some representatives of the Sixth Sub-Delegation of the Fourth General Sub-Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army comforted the People's Liberation Army participating in the construction of the Foziling Reservoir project. The delegation warmly carried out consolation activities at the construction site. Yu Likui and Yu Guang, Deputy Heads of the Sixth Sub-Delegation of the Fourth General Sub-Delegation, made consolation reports to all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army participating in the construction of the reservoir project. The delegation's art troupe gave a brilliant consolation performance at the construction site, and the film team screened the latest films for the soldiers. The delegation also held symposiums for heroes and model workers, symposiums for office cadres, and symposiums for women representatives at the construction site. The delegation also went deep into each company and construction site hospital to conduct visits.

The commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army participating in the construction of the Foziling Reservoir project were very grateful for the care and consolation from the people of the whole country, and presented letters of determination to the Sixth Sub-Delegation of the Fourth General Sub-Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army. They pledged to further strengthen their studies, strive to master technology, strengthen unity with technical workers, and better serve the construction of the motherland.

### 慰问团代表到达长山列岛慰问海防部队  佛子岭水库工地上展开慰问解放军的活动

1954-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    慰问团代表到达长山列岛慰问海防部队
    佛子岭水库工地上展开慰问解放军的活动
    据新华社讯：全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团总团、第一总分团（志愿军）和第四总分团的代表，在五日到达长山列岛，慰问警惕地守卫在海防前哨的人民解放军海防部队。慰问团的代表和随慰问团同来的中国人民志愿军政治部文艺工作团团员们，连日在岛上展开了广泛深入的慰问活动。六日，慰问团举行慰问大会，慰问驻守在长山列岛的人民解放军。在慰问大会上，第一总分团副团长李钢、第四总分团代表何霖、第二分团副团长王子彬等都讲了话。他们代表中国共产党、中央人民政府、毛主席和全国人民，向日夜守卫在祖国海防最前哨的英雄们致崇高的敬意和亲切的慰问。慰问团代表并在会上向驻军献旗、献礼，给驻军首长挂纪念章。会后，志愿军文工团为战士们作了精采的慰问演出。七日，慰问团代表访问了驻军领导机关，并听取了驻军首长的报告。八日、九日，慰问团代表、文工团和电影队，分组出发深入各岛屿、舰艇和连队进行慰问。九日，第一总分团副团长李钢和部分代表到舰艇上去慰问了水兵，并和全体水兵作了亲密的交谈。慰问团代表还到为解放长山列岛而英勇牺牲的烈士墓地扫墓，并献花圈致敬。
    据新华社讯：五日到十二日，全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团第四总分团第六分团部分代表，慰问了参加修筑佛子岭水库工程的人民解放军。慰问团在工地上热烈进行了慰问活动。第四总分团第六分团副团长余立奎和余光，向参加修筑水库工程的人民解放军全体指挥员、战斗员作了慰问报告。慰问团文工团在工地上作了精采的慰问演出，电影队为战士们放映了最新的影片。慰问团还在工地上召开了英雄、模范座谈会，机关干部座谈会，妇女代表座谈会。慰问团并深入到每个连队和工地医院进行访问。
    参加修筑佛子岭水库工程的人民解放军指挥员、战斗员十分感谢全国人民对他们的关怀和慰问，纷纷向全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团第四总分团第六分团献决心书。他们保证进一步加强学习，努力钻研技术，加强和技术工人的团结，更好地为祖国建设服务。
