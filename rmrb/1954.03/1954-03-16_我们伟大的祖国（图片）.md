    Our Great Motherland
    This is Gao Jinsong, the young female section chief of the Second Workshop of the No. 18 Factory of the Northeast Bureau of Mechanical Industry. She humbly studies production technology and helps workers solve problems in production, and is already a skilled section chief.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是东北机械工业管理局第十八厂第二车间年青的女工段长——高锦松。她虚心地学习生产技术，帮助工人解决生产中的问题，已是一位熟练的工段长了。
