Title: Chairman Mao Sends Congratulations on Kim Tu-bong’s Sixty-Fifth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1954-03-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government sent a telegram on March 14th to congratulate Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on his sixty-fifth birthday, which falls on March 17th. The telegram reads: "On the occasion of your sixty-fifth birthday, I extend to you my warm congratulations. I wish you good health and greater achievements in your work of restoring the national economy and striving for the peaceful reunification of Korea."

### 毛主席电贺金枓奉六十五岁寿辰

1954-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺金枓奉六十五岁寿辰
    【新华社十六日讯】三月十七日为朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉六十五岁寿辰，我中央人民政府毛泽东主席在三月十四日致电祝贺。电文称：“在您六十五岁寿辰之际，谨向您致以热烈的祝贺。祝您身体健康，并祝您在恢复国民经济、争取和平统一朝鲜的工作中获得更多的成就。”
