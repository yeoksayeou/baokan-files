Title: Resolution of the USSR Council of Ministers and the CPSU Central Committee on Specific Measures for Further Expanding Grain Production and Reclaiming Virgin and Fallow Land
Author:
Date: 1954-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th (TASS dispatch): The USSR Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union have adopted a resolution stipulating specific measures for implementing the resolution of the CPSU Central Committee's plenary session "On Further Expanding Grain Production in the USSR and on Reclaiming Virgin and Fallow Lands."

The resolution points out that in 1954-1955, 13 million hectares of virgin and fallow land (note: one hectare equals fifteen Chinese mu) will be reclaimed in parts of Kazakhstan, Siberia, the Urals, the Volga River region, and the North Caucasus to expand the grain sown area. This is an important and feasible way to develop grain production and is a major task for the country. Good harvests can be obtained on these fertile lands without large investments. In 1955, these lands will be able to harvest 1.1 to 1.2 billion poods of grain (note: one pood equals 32.76 Chinese jin). The USSR Council of Ministers and the CPSU Central Committee approved the initiatives of some union republics and states to expand the grain sown area of collective farms and state farms in these regions by more than 1.1 million hectares beyond the original plan.

In 1954-1955, new state farms specializing in the production of wheat, millet, oats, corn, and other grains, as well as some sunflower and flax seeds, will be established in the reclaimed areas. This year, the investment in the construction of new grain state farms and existing state farms in the reclaimed areas has been increased by 400 million rubles. In these new state farms, all work will be fully mechanized. The main production unit of the state farm is a tractor grain production team, which should be allocated tractors, combine harvesters, and other agricultural machinery and equipment.

This year, many experts and skilled workers have been dispatched from existing machine tractor stations, state farms, industrial enterprises, and other sectors of the national economy to state farms and machine tractor stations in the reclaimed areas. The resolution approved the proposal of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League to send 100,000 young volunteers skilled in operating agricultural machinery to work in machine tractor stations and state farms in the reclaimed areas and stipulated various incentives and material benefits for experts and skilled workers who are preparing to go to reclaim new land.

By July 1, 1954, 14,000 tractor drivers will be trained in various training classes in machine tractor stations and state farms.

The resolution also gave detailed instructions to various ministries and departments on the production of agricultural machinery and the timely supply of tractors, fuel, equipment, and tools to the reclaimed areas, as well as surveying work and the organization of public canteens and recreational facilities for agricultural workers in the newly reclaimed areas.

The USSR Council of Ministers and the CPSU Central Committee called on the country to prepare the field work carefully this spring and to maximize the expansion of the grain sown area.

### 苏联部长会议和苏共中央决议  规定进一步扩大谷物生产和开垦生荒地和熟荒地的具体措施

1954-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议和苏共中央决议
    规定进一步扩大谷物生产和开垦生荒地和熟荒地的具体措施
    【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十八日讯：苏联部长会议和苏联共产党中央委员会通过了一项决议，规定了执行苏共中央全体会议“关于进一步扩大苏联的谷物生产和关于开垦生荒地和熟荒地”的决议的具体措施。
    决议指出，一九五四—一九五五年内，在哈萨克、西伯利亚、乌拉尔、伏尔加河流域和北高加索的部分地区，将要开垦一千三百万公顷生荒地和熟荒地（按：一公顷合十五市亩），以扩大谷物播种面积，这是发展谷物生产的一个重要的而且可行的办法，是国家的一项主要任务。在这些肥沃的土地上，不需要大量的投资就可以获得很好的收成。在一九五五年，这些土地将可以收获十一亿到十二亿普特谷物（按：一普特合三十二点七六市斤）。苏联部长会议和苏共中央委员会批准了一些加盟共和国和州的倡议，把这些地区的集体农庄和国营农场的谷物播种面积比原计划扩大一百一十万公顷以上。
    一九五四—一九五五年，在已开垦的地区将成立新的国营农场，专门生产小麦、黍、燕麦、玉蜀黍和其他谷物，并生产一部分向日葵和亚麻种籽。今年用于开垦地区新的谷物国营农场和现有的国营农场的建设投资，增加了四亿卢布。在这些新的国营农场里，一切工作将完全机械化。国营农场的主要的生产单位是一个拖拉机谷物生产队，应以拖拉机、收割联合机以及其他的农业机器和设备分配给这个生产队。
    今年，已从现有的机器拖拉机站、国营农场以及工业企业和国民经济其他部门派遣了许多专家和熟练工人到开垦地区的国营农场和机器拖拉机站去。决议批准苏联列宁共产主义青年团所提出的派遣十万名操使农业机器的青年志愿工作者到开垦地区的机器拖拉机站和国营农场去工作的建议，并规定给那些准备去开垦新地的专家和熟练工人以各种鼓励和物质福利。
    在一九五四年七月一日以前，将有一万四千名拖拉机手在机器拖拉机站以及国营农场的各种训练班中受训。
    决议还就农业机器的生产和及时给开垦地区供应拖拉机、燃料、设备和工具的问题，以及勘测工作和关于新开垦地区内农业工作者的公共食堂与文娱设备的组织问题，向各部、署作了详尽的指示。
    苏联部长会议和苏共中央号召在今年春季周密地准备田间工作，并使谷物的播种面积得到最大限度的扩充。
