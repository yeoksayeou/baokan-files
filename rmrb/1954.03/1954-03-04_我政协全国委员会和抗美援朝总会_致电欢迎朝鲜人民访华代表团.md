Title: The National Committee of the CPPCC and the China Peace Committee Cables to Welcome the Visit of the Korean People's Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1954-03-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the China Peace Committee cabled the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Fatherland Front to welcome the visit of the Korean People's Delegation to China. The cable reads as follows: Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Fatherland Front:

    The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the China Peace Committee, with great joy, learned that the Korean Democratic Fatherland Front has decided to send a delegation of the Korean people to visit our country. On behalf of the Chinese people, we extend our fraternal and warm welcome to the Korean People's Delegation, which is about to depart for our country. The people of China and Korea have forged an unbreakable, steel-like unity with blood in the struggle against American aggression and in defense of world peace. This unity is a powerful guarantee for the victory of our two peoples. We firmly believe that the visit of the Korean People's Delegation to our country will further consolidate and develop the lofty internationalist unity and friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples, enhance mutual assistance and cooperation between China and Korea, and contribute to our common cause of safeguarding peace in the Far East and the world. We hereby send this cable of welcome.
    With respectful greetings!

    The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
    The China Peace Committee
    March 2, 1954

### 我政协全国委员会和抗美援朝总会  致电欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团

1954-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我政协全国委员会和抗美援朝总会
    致电欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团
   【新华社三日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中国人民抗美援朝总会致电朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会，欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团来我国访问。电文如下：朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会：
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中国人民抗美援朝总会以十分欢愉的心情，获悉朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线决定派遣朝鲜人民访华代表团莅临我国访问。我们代表中国人民谨向即将启程前来我国的朝鲜人民访华代表团表示兄弟般的热烈的欢迎。中朝两国人民在反对美国侵略、保卫世界和平斗争中已以鲜血结成了牢不可破的钢铁般的团结，这种团结是我们两国人民取得胜利的有力保证。我们深信，这次朝鲜人民访华代表团的光临我国，必将进一步巩固和发展中朝人民崇高的国际主义的团结友谊、增强中朝两国之间的互助合作，有助于我们保卫远东与世界和平的共同事业。谨此驰电欢迎。
    并致敬礼！
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
    中国人民抗美援朝总会
    一九五四年三月二日
