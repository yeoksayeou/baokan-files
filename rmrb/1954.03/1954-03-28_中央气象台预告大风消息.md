Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Forecasts Gale News
Author:
Date: 1954-03-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a gale warning at 10:00 PM on March 27th: It is expected that within the next 24 hours, North China, the Bohai Sea, the Liaodong Peninsula, the Shandong Peninsula, and the northern Yellow Sea will experience northerly winds of force six to seven, with the wind direction shifting from northwest to southeast.

### 中央气象台预告大风消息

1954-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台预告大风消息
    【新华社二十七日讯】三月二十七日下午十时中央气象台发布大风消息：预计未来二十四小时内，华北、渤海、辽东半岛、山东半岛和黄海北部将有六级到七级的偏北风，风向是西北向东南方向移动。
