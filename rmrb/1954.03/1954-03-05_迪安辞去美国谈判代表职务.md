Title: Dean Resigns as U.S. Negotiation Representative
Author: 
Date: 1954-03-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 4th. TASS, Washington, March 3rd: U.S. State Department Spokesman, Mr. Sultam, announced at a press conference on the 2nd that Dean had resigned from his position as U.S. Special Representative to the Panmunjom negotiations.

### 迪安辞去美国谈判代表职务

1954-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    迪安辞去美国谈判代表职务
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社华盛顿三日讯：美国国务院发言人苏丹姆二日在记者招待会上宣布：迪安已辞去美国出席板门店谈判特派代表的职务。
