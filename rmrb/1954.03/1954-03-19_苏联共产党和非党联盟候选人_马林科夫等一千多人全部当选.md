Title: Candidates of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Non-Party League, Including Malenkov and More Than 1,000 Others, All Elected
Author:
Date: 1954-03-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 18th: The Central Election Commission of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union announced on the 18th the list of deputies elected to the Union Council and the National Council of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union in the elections on March 14th, according to TASS.

    A total of 1,347 representatives were elected to the Union Council and the National Council of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, all of whom are representatives of the Communist Party and the Non-Party League. Among them are the most outstanding activists of the Communist Party and the Soviet state, including Malenkov, Molotov, Khrushchev, Voroshilov, Bulganin, Kaganovich, Mikoyan, Saburov, Pervukhin, Shvernik, Suslov, Pospelov, and Shatalin.

    Among the elected representatives are also: Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Kosygin, Malyshev, and Tevosyan, and Ministers of various ministries of the Soviet Union Benediktov, Zverev, Alexandrov, Likhachev, Beshchev and others.

    Many workers in industrial enterprises and transportation, as well as outstanding agricultural workers, were also elected as deputies to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, including: lathe operator Bykov, locomotive driver Blazhenov, construction worker Shirkov, textile worker Chutkikh, steelworker Debankov, rolling worker Chursinov, weaver Matyerikova, miner Valigura, collective farm chairmen Generalov, Puzanchikov, Burkatskaya, tractor drivers Angelina, Gitálov, and collective farm member Lyuskova, and others.

    Soviet voters also elected many scientists, writers, artists, and educators as deputies to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. Among them are: scientists Nesmeyanov, Lysenko, Barkin, Tsizin, Bakulev, Paradin, Pankratova, Lebedeva, Ambartsumian, Skobeltsyn; writers Fadeyev, Sholokhov, Leonov, Tikhonov, Korneichuk, Ehrenburg, Panferov, Wasilewska, Surkov, Tychyna; actors Tarasova, Cherkasov, Aleksandrovskaya; composer Solovyov-Sedoy; female teacher Martyanova, etc. Among the newly elected deputies to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union are also representatives of various nationalities of the Soviet Union. Soviet youth also account for a large proportion of the deputies to the Supreme Soviet.

### 苏联共产党和非党联盟候选人  马林科夫等一千多人全部当选

1954-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党和非党联盟候选人  马林科夫等一千多人全部当选
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃中央选举委员会，十八日公布了在三月十四日的选举中当选为苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院代表的名单。
    苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院的当选代表共有一千三百四十七人，他们都是共产党和非党联盟的代表。其中有共产党和苏维埃国家的最出色的活动家马林科夫、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫、伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、米高扬、萨布罗夫、别尔乌辛、什维尔尼克、苏斯洛夫、波斯伯洛夫、夏塔林。
    在当选的代表中还有：苏联部长会议副主席柯西金、马利歇夫、捷沃西安，苏联各部部长别涅吉克托夫、兹维列夫、亚历山德罗夫、利哈契夫、别什切夫等人。
    许多工业企业和运输业的工人及优秀的农业劳动者，也当选为苏联最高苏维埃代表，其中有：车工比科夫、机车司机布拉仁诺夫、建筑工人希尔科夫、纺织工人楚特基赫、炼钢工人捷班科夫、压延工人楚尔西诺夫、织布工玛捷里科娃、矿工瓦利古拉，集体农庄主席盖涅拉洛夫、普赞奇科夫、布尔卡茨卡娅，拖拉机手安格琳娜、吉塔洛夫和集体农庄女庄员柳斯科娃等人。
    苏联选民还选举许多科学家、文学家、艺术家和教育工作者为苏联最高苏维埃代表。其中有：科学家涅斯米扬诺夫、李森科、巴尔金、齐津、巴库列夫、帕拉金、潘克拉托娃、列别杰娃、阿姆巴楚米安、斯科贝尔琴；作家法捷耶夫、萧洛霍夫、列昂诺夫、吉洪诺夫、考涅楚克、爱伦堡、潘费罗夫、华西列夫斯卡雅、苏尔科夫、蒂奇娜；演员塔拉索娃、契尔卡索夫、亚历山德罗夫斯卡娅；作曲家索洛维耶夫—谢多伊；女教师马尔佳扬诺娃等。在新当选的苏联最高苏维埃代表中，还有苏联各民族的代表。苏联青年在最高苏维埃代表中也占很大的比例。
