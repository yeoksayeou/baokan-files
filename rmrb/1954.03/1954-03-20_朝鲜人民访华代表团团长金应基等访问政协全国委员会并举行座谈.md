Title: Korean People's Delegation Head Kim Eung-gi and Others Visit the CPPCC National Committee and Hold Symposium

Author:
Date: 1954-03-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 19th:

    Korean People's Delegation Head Kim Eung-gi and Others
    Visit the CPPCC National Committee and Hold Symposium

    A portion of the Korean People's Delegation visited the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) on the afternoon of the 19th and held a symposium.

    The participants in the symposium included: Kim Eung-gi, head of the Korean People's Delegation; Kim Dal-hyun, Baek Nam-un, Lee Dong-young, and Lee Young-ho, deputy heads; and delegation members Gye Eung-sang, Yoon Bong-jin, Lee Gwang-guk, Lee Yong-gyu, Hong Myeon-hoo, Yoon Cheong-woo, Lee Jong-soo, Kim Seok-ryong, Gu Bu-ryeok, Gil Jin-seop, Baek Man-ho, and Lee Won-mo, totaling seventeen people.

    The Chinese side present at the symposium included: Guo Moruo and Chen Shutong, vice-chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee; Li Weihan, secretary-general; and standing committee members Wang Kunlun, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Naiqi, Liang Xi, Shao Lizi, Luo Longji, Xu Deheng, Qian Sanqiang, Li Dequan, and Liao Chengzhi, among others.

    The symposium concluded after three hours in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基等访问政协全国委员会并举行座谈

1954-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基等
    访问政协全国委员会并举行座谈
    【新华社十九日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团的一部分代表在十九日下午访问了中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会，并举行了座谈。
    参加座谈会的有朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基，副团长金达铉、白南云、李东英、李永镐，代表团团员桂应祥、尹凤镇、李光国、李龙奎、洪冕厚、尹澄宇、李钟秀、金硕龙、具富力、吉镇燮、白万镐、李元懋等十七人。
    中国方面出席座谈会的有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席郭沫若、陈叔通，秘书长李维汉，常务委员王昆仑、章伯钧、章乃器、梁希、邵力子、罗隆基、许德珩、钱三强、李德全、廖承志等。
    座谈会在诚挚友好的空气中进行了三小时后结束。
