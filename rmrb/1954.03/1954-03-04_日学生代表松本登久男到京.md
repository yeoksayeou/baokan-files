Title: Japanese Student Representative Matsumoto Tokuo Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:

    Japanese Student Representative Matsumoto Tokuo Arrives in Beijing
   [Xinhua News Agency] Matsumoto Tokuo, Vice Chairman of the All-Japan Federation of Student Self-Government Associations, who attended the Vienna meeting of the Executive Committee of the International Union of Students, arrived in China for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Student Federation. Matsumoto Tokuo arrived in Beijing by plane on February 27th. Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Student Federation, Wang Enhai, Secretary-General of the Beijing Student Federation, and others went to the airport to welcome him.
    Kameta Togo, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and Japanese peace activists Nakamura Kanjūrō and others also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 日学生代表松本登久男到京

1954-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    日学生代表松本登久男到京
   【新华社讯】出席国际学生联合会执行委员会维也纳会议的全日本学生自治会总联合会副主席松本登久男，应中华全国学生联合会的邀请，前来我国参观访问。松本登久男于二月二十七日乘机抵达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中华全国学生联合会主席田德民，北京市学生联合会秘书长王恩海等人。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍，日本和平人士中村翫右卫门等人也前往机场欢迎。
