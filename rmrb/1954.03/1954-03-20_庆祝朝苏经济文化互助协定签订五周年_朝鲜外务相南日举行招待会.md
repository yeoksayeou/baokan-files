Title: Korean Foreign Minister Nam Il Holds Reception to Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of the Signing of the Korean-Soviet Economic and Cultural Mutual Assistance Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-03-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th, Pyongyang: TASS reports that on the 17th, Korean Democratic People's Republic Foreign Minister Nam Il held a reception to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Korean-Soviet Economic and Cultural Mutual Assistance Agreement.

    Among those attending the reception were: Deputy Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Park Chang-ok and Kim Il; Vice Premiers of the Cabinet of the Korean Democratic People's Republic, Park Eui-wan, Choi Chang-ik, and Hong Myung-hui; and Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Korean Democratic People's Republic, Lee Young.

    Also present at the reception were Soviet Ambassador to the Korean Democratic People's Republic, S.P. Suzdalev, and personnel from the Soviet Embassy, as well as Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China to the Korean Democratic People's Republic, Gan Yetao, and other envoys from People's Democratic countries stationed in Korea.

    Nam Il and Suzdalev delivered speeches at the reception.

    The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝朝苏经济文化互助协定签订五周年  朝鲜外务相南日举行招待会

1954-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝苏经济文化互助协定签订五周年
    朝鲜外务相南日举行招待会
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：十七日，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日举行招待会，庆祝朝苏经济文化互助协定签订五周年。
    出席招待会的有：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴昌玉和金一，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相朴义琓、崔昌益和洪命熹，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议议长李英。
    出席招待会的还有苏联驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使斯·普·苏兹达列夫和苏联大使馆人员，中华人民共和国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代办甘野陶和其他人民民主国家驻朝鲜使节。
    南日和苏兹达列夫在会上相互致词。
    招待会在热烈和友好的空气中进行。
