Title: Indonesian Politicians Welcome Our Country's Participation in the Geneva Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-03-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 9th (TASS): Indonesian political figures are widely discussing the upcoming Geneva Conference and welcome the participation of the People's Republic of China in the Geneva Conference. According to a report by Antara News Agency, Indonesian Deputy Speaker of Parliament, Mr. Tambunan, made a statement welcoming the invitation of the People's Republic of China to participate in the Geneva Conference and emphasized that the People's Republic of China must be admitted to the United Nations. He said that hundreds of millions of Chinese people have every right to participate in international organizations.

The Indonesian press attaches great importance to Tambunan's remarks.

### 印尼政界人士欢迎我国参加日内瓦会议

1954-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼政界人士欢迎我国参加日内瓦会议
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社雅加达八日讯：印尼政界人士广泛讨论即将举行的日内瓦会议，对中华人民共和国参加日内瓦会议表示欢迎。据安塔拉通讯社报道：印尼国会副议长谭布兰曾发表谈话，欢迎邀请中华人民共和国参加日内瓦会议，并着重指出：必须让中华人民共和国加入联合国。他说：数亿中国人民完全有权参加国际组织。
    印尼报界对谭布兰的谈话都表示重视。
