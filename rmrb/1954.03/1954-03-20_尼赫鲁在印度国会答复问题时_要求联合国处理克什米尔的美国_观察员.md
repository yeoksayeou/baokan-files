Title: Nehru, in Response to Questions in the Indian Parliament, Requests the United Nations to Deal with the American "Observer" in Kashmir
Author:
Date: 1954-03-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: According to the Indian News Service, Indian Prime Minister Nehru reiterated on the 16th in the Indian Parliament, in response to questions, that the United Nations stationed American "Observer" in Kashmir cannot be considered neutral. He said, "A situation has now arisen where no officer of the United States Army can be considered neutral in the Kashmir dispute." Nehru said, "We have informally conveyed our opinion to the United Nations Secretariat and hope that they will deal with this matter as soon as possible."

### 尼赫鲁在印度国会答复问题时  要求联合国处理克什米尔的美国“观察员”

1954-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁在印度国会答复问题时
    要求联合国处理克什米尔的美国“观察员”
    【新华社十八日讯】据印度新闻处报道，印度总理尼赫鲁十六日在印度国会答复问题时重申：不能认为联合国驻克什米尔的美国“观察员”是中立的。他说：“现在已发生了一种情况，这种情况使得美国陆军的任何军官不能被认为是在克什米尔争端中的中立者了。”尼赫鲁说：“我们已经非正式地把我们的意见传达给联合国秘书处了，希望他们早日处理这件事。”
