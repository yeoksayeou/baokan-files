Title: Soviet Writers' Union Committee Holds Plenary Session, Decides to Convene Second All-Union Congress of Writers in November This Year
Author:
Date: 1954-03-09
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 6th: According to TASS, the plenary session of the Soviet Writers' Union Committee, recently held in Moscow, concluded on the 4th. The meeting discussed the issue of convening the Second All-Union Congress of Writers this year. The First Secretary of the Soviet Writers' Union Committee, Surkov, delivered a report at the meeting on the tasks of various writers' organizations in preparing for the congress. He pointed out that Soviet literature has made significant progress in the twenty years since the First Congress. The upcoming Second Congress will summarize past work and formulate plans for the further development of multi-ethnic Soviet literature. The meeting then discussed this report. Writers such as Simonov, Zlobin, and Tur-Sonzadze spoke during the discussion.
    The meeting finally decided to convene the Second All-Union Congress of Writers in Moscow on November 15, 1954. The Chairman of the Soviet Writers' Union Committee, Fadeyev, will deliver a general report on the state and tasks of Soviet literature at the congress. Prominent writers will deliver reports on various aspects of the development of Soviet literature. In addition, the distinguished writer and public figure Ehrenburg will deliver a speech on modern progressive foreign literature. The congress will also discuss the revision of the Soviet Writers' Union's charter. In the period before the congress, newspapers will conduct extensive discussions on the main issues of Soviet literature and criticism.
    In addition: The Soviet Writers' Union Committee held a plenary meeting on the 3rd to commemorate the 300th anniversary of Ukraine's reunification with Russia.

### 苏联作家协会委员会举行全体会议  决定今年十一月召开第二次全苏作家代表大会

1954-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联作家协会委员会举行全体会议
    决定今年十一月召开第二次全苏作家代表大会
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：最近在莫斯科举行的苏联作家协会委员会全体会议已于四日闭幕。会议讨论了今年召开第二次全苏作家代表大会的问题。苏联作家协会委员会第一书记苏尔科夫在会上作了关于各个作家组织筹备代表大会的任务的报告。他指出，苏维埃文学在第一次代表大会以后的二十年中有了巨大的进步。即将召开的第二次代表大会将总结过去的工作，并拟订进一步发展多民族的苏维埃文学的计划。会议接着对这个报告进行了讨论。在讨论中发言的有西蒙诺夫、兹洛宾和图尔—森札杰等作家。
    会议最后决定一九五四年十一月十五日在莫斯科召开第二次全苏作家代表大会。苏联作家协会委员会主席法捷耶夫将在大会上作关于苏维埃文学状况和任务的总报告。著名的作家将作关于苏维埃文学各方面发展的报告。此外杰出的作家和公众领袖爱伦堡将在会上作关于现代外国进步文学的演说。代表大会还要讨论修改苏联作家协会会章的问题。在代表大会召开以前的这一段时期，报纸将就苏维埃文学和批评的主要问题进行广泛的讨论。
    又讯：苏联作家协会委员会三日曾举行全体会议，纪念乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年。
