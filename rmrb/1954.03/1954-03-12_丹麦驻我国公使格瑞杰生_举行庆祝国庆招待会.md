Title: Danish Minister to China, Gregerse, Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1954-03-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 11th—Danish Minister to China, Mr. Gregerse, held a reception in Beijing on the 11th to celebrate Denmark's National Day.

More than 100 people were invited to attend the reception. Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, were all invited to attend.

Also present were diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries stationed in China.

The reception was conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

### 丹麦驻我国公使格瑞杰生  举行庆祝国庆招待会

1954-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    丹麦驻我国公使格瑞杰生
    举行庆祝国庆招待会
      【新华社十一日讯】　丹麦驻我国公使格瑞杰生于十一日在北京举行招待会，庆祝丹麦国庆日。
    应邀参加招待会的共一百余人。中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若、外交部副部长章汉夫、对外贸易部副部长雷任民、文化部副部长丁西林及北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等均应邀到会。
    到会的还有各国驻我国外交使节及使馆人员。
    招待会在友好空气中进行。
