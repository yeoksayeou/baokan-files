Title: Khmer Southwest Military and Civilians Annihilated Over Two Thousand Enemies Last Year
Author:
Date: 1954-03-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Vietnam News Agency, 18th: According to late news from Cambodia, the Cambodian People's Liberation Army and the people in southwestern Cambodia intensified guerrilla activities in 1953, causing heavy losses to the enemy. In this year, the military and civilians in southwestern Cambodia conducted a total of 506 battles of various sizes. According to incomplete statistics, 2,108 enemy troops were killed, wounded, or captured, including one colonel, two majors, seven captains, thirteen lieutenants, and thirty-two sergeants. The Cambodian People's Liberation Army also destroyed 21 French military vehicles, shot down one plane, and seized 112 various weapons, over 20,000 rounds of ammunition, and a large amount of military equipment. In addition, the military and civilians in southwestern Cambodia also carried out many activities to sabotage enemy transportation.

### 高棉西南军民去年歼敌两千余

1954-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    高棉西南军民去年歼敌两千余
   【新华社十九日讯】越南通讯社十八日讯：据高棉迟到消息，高棉西南部的高棉人民解放军和人民在一九五三年加紧了游击活动，使敌人遭受很多损失。在这一年中，高棉西南部军民总共进行了五百零六次大小战斗，据不完全的统计：毙、伤、俘敌军达二千一百零八名，其中有上校一名、少校两名、上尉七名、中尉十三名和军士三十二名。高棉人民解放军还击毁了法军汽车二十一辆，烧毁飞机一架，缴获各种武器一百一十二件、子弹二万多发和大量的军事装备。此外，高棉西南部的军民还进行了许多破坏敌人交通运输等活动。
