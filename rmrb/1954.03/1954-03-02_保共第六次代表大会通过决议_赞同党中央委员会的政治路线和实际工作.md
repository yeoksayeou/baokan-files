Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Sixth Congress Passes Resolution Endorsing the Political Line and Practical Work of the Party Central Committee
Author: 
Date: 1954-03-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 1st. According to TASS: The Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party continued on the evening of February 27th. The meeting concluded the discussion on the summary report of the work of the Party Central Committee and the summary report of the work of the Central Inspection Committee. Thirty-eight delegates spoke during the two-day discussion. They fully agreed with the two reports. The congress passed a resolution unanimously endorsing the political line and practical work of the Party Central Committee and instructed Party organizations at all levels to take the spirit of the Party Central Committee's work summary report as a guide in their practical work. The congress also adopted the summary report of the work of the Central Inspection Committee.
    The congress heard the report of the Chairman of the Credentials Committee, B. Tainov. He announced that a total of 908 delegates attended the congress.
    The congress will continue.

### 保共第六次代表大会通过决议  赞同党中央委员会的政治路线和实际工作

1954-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    保共第六次代表大会通过决议
    赞同党中央委员会的政治路线和实际工作
    【新华社一日讯】据塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚共产党第六次代表大会，在二月二十七日晚间继续进行。会议结束了关于党中央委员会工作总结报告和中央检查委员会工作总结报告的讨论。在两天讨论中发言的有三十八个代表。他们完全同意这两个报告。大会通过决议，一致赞同党中央委员会的政治路线和实际工作，并责成各级党组织在实际工作中以党中央工作总结报告的精神为指针。大会也通过了中央检查委员会的工作总结报告。
    大会听取了资格审查委员会主席鲍·塔伊诺夫的报告。他宣布：出席这次大会的代表共有九百零八人。
    大会将继续进行。
