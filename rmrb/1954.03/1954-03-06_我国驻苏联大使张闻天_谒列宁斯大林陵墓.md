Title: Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, Visits the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1954-03-06
Page: 1

    Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian
    Visits the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th] On the first anniversary of the death of the great revolutionary teacher Stalin, Zhang Wentian, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, led personnel from the embassy and members of the trade delegation in a visit to the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum, and presented wreaths on behalf of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Central People's Government of The People's Republic of China to pay their respects.

### 我国驻苏联大使张闻天  谒列宁斯大林陵墓

1954-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻苏联大使张闻天
    谒列宁斯大林陵墓
    【新华社莫斯科五日电】在伟大的革命导师斯大林逝世一周年纪念日，我驻苏联大使张闻天率领大使馆人员和商务代表团团员，拜谒了列宁斯大林陵墓，并代表中国共产党中央委员会和中华人民共和国中央人民政府献花圈致敬。
