Title: Korean People's Delegation to China Tours Capital's Famous Sites
Author:
Date: 1954-03-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: The Korean People's Delegation to China toured famous sites and historical monuments in the capital on the 16th. In the morning, the entire delegation, led by head of the delegation Kim Ung-gi, visited Beihai Park and the Temple of Heaven. The delegates all expressed their admiration for the ancient Chinese architecture and the beautiful scenery of Beihai Park. Many delegates said, "This is the great creation of the Chinese working people." In the afternoon, most of the delegates, accompanied by Wu Zhongchao, Director of the Palace Museum, and Chen Qiao, Deputy Director, visited the Palace Museum. They were greatly interested in visiting the Ancient Art Exhibition Hall, the Painting Gallery, the Ceramics Gallery, and the Exhibition of Calligraphy by Famous Figures of Past Dynasties, and praised the Chinese government's care for ancient cultural relics. Yoon Cheong-woo, a delegate and deputy editor of "New Korea News," wrote in the Palace Museum's memorial book: "Under the care of the people's government, ancient Chinese cultural relics can belong to the people."

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团游览首都名胜古迹

1954-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团游览首都名胜古迹
    【新华社十六日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团十六日游览参观了首都的名胜古迹。上午，代表团全体代表在团长金应基率领下，游览了北海公园和天坛。代表们对于中国古代建筑和北海公园的美丽景色，都赞叹不已。很多代表说：“这是中国劳动人民的伟大创造。”下午，大部分代表在故宫博物院院长吴仲超、副院长陈乔陪同下，参观了故宫博物院。他们在参观古代艺术品陈列馆和绘画馆、陶瓷馆、历代名人墨迹展览时，都感到极大的兴趣，并赞扬我国政府对于古代文物的爱护。代表团代表、“新朝鲜社”副主笔尹澄宇在故宫博物院的纪念册上写道：“在人民政权的关怀下，中国古代文化遗物才能成为人民所有。”
