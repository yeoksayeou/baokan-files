Title: Communist Party of India Politburo Issues Statement Supporting Nehru's Speech Rejecting US Military "Aid"
Author:
Date: 1954-03-04
Page: 4
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 3rd: The Politburo of the Communist Party of India issued a statement on the 2nd, supporting Indian Prime Minister Nehru’s speech in the People's Assembly rejecting the acceptance of US military "aid" and condemning US military "aid" to Pakistan.
    The statement of the Politburo of the Communist Party of India is as follows:
    "Mr. Nehru's statement in Parliament on March 1st, clarifying the Indian government's attitude towards the US military aid to Pakistan, will receive the heartfelt support of every patriotic Indian. His categorical rejection of Eisenhower's insulting suggestion to give India similar military 'aid', his exposure of the hypocritical claim that the so-called US aid to Pakistan is to strengthen Pakistan's anti-aggression forces, and his blunt condemnation of the US's open declaration to control Asian countries through such interventionist acts in Asia, all of these are in line with Indian public opinion and will be welcomed by the democratic and peace-loving forces all over the world.
    "Especially his statement that 'American military observers in Kashmir can no longer be considered by us as neutrals in this dispute, therefore their presence is inappropriate.' This statement is a step of great significance in terms of taking active measures to crush the US conspiracy against India and Asia.
    "The Politburo of the Communist Party of India, now meeting in Delhi, will discuss the situation arising from the confirmation of the US-Pakistan military treaty and decide on steps to further strengthen the future struggle of the Indian people to defend peace and freedom."

### 印度共产党政治局发表声明  支持尼赫鲁拒绝美国军事“援助”的演说

1954-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    印度共产党政治局发表声明
    支持尼赫鲁拒绝美国军事“援助”的演说
   【新华社新德里三日电】印度共产党政治局二日发表声明，支持印度政府总理尼赫鲁在人民院发表的拒绝接受美国军事“援助”并谴责美国给巴基斯坦以军事“援助”的演说。
    印度共产党政治局的声明如下：
    “尼赫鲁先生三月一日在议会发表的阐明印度政府对于美国给予巴基斯坦以军事援助的态度的声明，将得到每一个爱国的印度人的衷心支持。他断然拒绝艾森豪威尔给予印度以类似的军事‘援助’的侮辱性建议，他揭发所谓美国给予巴基斯坦的援助是为了加强巴基斯坦的反侵略力量的伪善说法，他直言不讳地指责美国公然宣称要控制亚洲国家的这种干涉亚洲的行径，所有这些都是符合印度的公众舆论的，并将受到全世界的民主与爱好和平力量的欢迎。
    “尤其是他说‘在克什米尔的美国军事观察员现在不再能被我们认为是在这个争端中的中立者，因此，他们的出现是不适当的。’他的这种说法，就采取积极措施来粉碎美国对印度与亚洲的阴谋的意义来说，是一个有重大意义的步骤。
    “现在在德里举行会议的印度共产党政治局将讨论因美巴军事条约的确定而引起的局势，并决定步骤来进一步加强今后印度人民保卫和平与自由的斗争。”
