Title: Soviet Communist Party Delegation Returns Home from Poland
Author:
Date: 1954-03-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: According to TASS, the Soviet Communist Party delegation, led by Comrade Khrushchev, which participated in the Second Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party, left Warsaw on the 18th to return to the Soviet Union and arrived in Moscow on the 19th.

Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, and Ochab, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, accompanied the Soviet Communist Party delegation to the Polish border.

### 苏联共产党代表团离波返国

1954-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党代表团离波返国
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社消息：参加波兰统一工人党第二次代表大会的苏联共产党代表团，在团长赫鲁晓夫率领下，于十八日离华沙返苏联，于十九日抵达莫斯科。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特和中央委员会书记处书记奥哈布，曾陪送苏联共产党代表团到波兰边境。
