Title: The State Council Holds Its 209th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The 209th session of the State Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 18th. The meeting heard a report from Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, on spring plowing and production, and approved the "Directive of the State Council of the Central People's Government on Spring Plowing and Production." The meeting heard and approved the summary report on the disaster situation and relief work in 1953 by Xie Juezai, Deputy Director of the Central Production and Disaster Relief Committee. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行第二零九次会议

1954-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二零九次会议
    【新华社十九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百零九次政务会议在十八日下午举行。会议听了中央人民政府农业部李书城部长所作的关于春耕生产的说明，通过了“中央人民政府政务院关于春耕生产的指示”。会议听了和批准了中央生产救灾委员会谢觉哉副主任所作的关于一九五三年灾情和救灾工作总结报告。会议并通过了各项任免案。
