Title: Capital Women Prepare to Celebrate "March 8th" International Women's Day

Author:

Date: 1954-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: Women from all walks of life in the capital are actively preparing to commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day. The Beijing Democratic Women's Federation recently held an enlarged meeting of its executive committee, deciding to widely publicize the general line of the state during the transition period to women through various activities commemorating "March 8th" International Women's Day. On March 7th, women from all walks of life in the capital will hold a grand meeting to commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day. The Beijing Democratic Women's Federation will hold a reception, separately entertaining the female people's representatives elected in the district's general election, as well as the family members of revolutionary martyrs and military personnel. Relevant units will hold symposiums for female labor models and advanced producers in industry and agriculture, get-togethers and symposiums for female workers and peasants, and various small symposiums and report meetings. The urban areas will also organize meetings between outstanding women from all fields and the general female population. The Labor People's Cultural Palace and the cultural centers in each district will hold photo exhibitions and commemorative evening parties.

### 首都妇女筹备纪念“三八”节

1954-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    首都妇女筹备纪念“三八”节
    据新华社讯：首都各界妇女积极筹备纪念“三八”国际妇女节。日前北京市民主妇女联合会召开执委扩大会议，决定通过纪念“三八”节的各项活动，广泛地向妇女群众宣传国家在过渡时期的总路线，三月七日首都各界妇女将举行纪念“三八”国际妇女节的大会。北京市民主妇女联合会将举行招待会，分别招待城区普选中当选的女人民代表和烈属、军属。各有关单位将分别举行工农业女劳动模范和先进生产者座谈会，女工、农妇的联欢座谈会，各种小型座谈会、报告会等。城区还将组织各方面的妇女优秀人物和妇女群众见面。劳动人民文化宫和各区文化馆将举办图片展览和纪念晚会。
