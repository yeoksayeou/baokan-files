Title: Canadian Prime Minister Hopes for Positive Results from Geneva Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-03-11
Page: 4
    
    (Xinhua News Agency, March 10th) TASS, New York, March 8th: According to an Associated Press correspondent in Seoul, South Korea, Canadian Prime Minister Louis St. Laurent, who is currently in South Korea, told reporters that he hopes the Geneva Conference "will yield positive results."
    According to a United Press reporter, when asked whether inviting representatives of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference would mean recognition of the People's Republic of China government, St. Laurent said: "I don't know if it means recognition, but I think we will have to realistically look at the facts one day and recognize that the current Chinese government is a government that accords with the wishes of the people."

### 加拿大总理希望日内瓦会议获得积极结果

1954-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    加拿大总理希望日内瓦会议获得积极结果
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社纽约八日讯：据美联社驻汉城记者报道，现在在南朝鲜的加拿大总理圣劳伦向记者说，他希望日内瓦会议“将得到积极的结果”。
    据合众社记者报道，圣劳伦在回答邀请中华人民共和国代表参加日内瓦会议是否就意味着承认中华人民共和国政府这一问题时说：“我不知道这是否意味着承认，但我认为，我们总有一天不得不现实地来看待事实，并承认现在的中国政府是一个符合人民愿望的政府。”
