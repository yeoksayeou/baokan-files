Title: All Circles in the Capital Hold Grand Meeting in Huai Ren Hall to Welcome the Korean People's Delegation to China; Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi Received the Delegation Last Night
Author: 
Date: 1954-03-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: Beijing:
    All circles in the capital held a grand meeting at Huai Ren Hall on the evening of the 16th to warmly welcome the Korean People's Delegation to China.

    The entire members of the Korean People's Delegation to China, led by head of the delegation Kim Ung-gi, and the three art troupes accompanying the delegation attended the meeting.

    Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Liu Shaoqi, Zhang Lan, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Peng Dehuai, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC and Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC and Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea Peng Zhen, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Su Yu, Huang Kecheng, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua and others attended the meeting.

    The meeting was attended by: members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, representatives of all democratic parties and people's organizations, non-party democratic personages, representatives of various fraternal ethnic groups in our country, heads of various committees, ministries, administrations, institutes and banks under the State Council of the Central People's Government, heads of various departments of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of the North China Administrative Committee, the North China Branch of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, the Beijing Municipal Branch of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, as well as well-known figures in science, education, literature, journalism, combat heroes, and others.

    Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and embassy staff also attended the welcoming meeting.

    Foreign guests attending the welcoming meeting included Yudin, Ambassador of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to China, and heads of diplomatic missions of the People's Democratic Countries to China.

    More than 1,400 people attended the welcoming meeting.

    In the center of the rostrum of the meeting hall hung huge portraits of Chairman Mao Zedong, Marshal Kim Il-sung, and Chairman Malenkov, flanked by the national flags of China, Korea, and the Soviet Union, surrounded by flowers and plants. When the Korean People's Delegation to China, led by head of the delegation Kim Ung-gi, entered the venue, the audience burst into prolonged applause.

    At 7:30 PM, the welcoming meeting began with the solemn national anthems of Korea and China. Guo Moruo delivered a welcoming speech at the welcoming meeting (full text to be published separately).

    After Guo Moruo's welcoming speech, the head and deputy head of the Korean People's Delegation to China received flowers presented by the youth of the capital amidst thunderous applause.

    Then, the head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, Kim Ung-gi, delivered a reply speech (full text to be published separately). Kim Ung-gi's speech repeatedly received thunderous applause from the welcomers.

    At the welcoming meeting, the guests and hosts presented each other with banners. The Korean People's Delegation to China presented two banners each to the National Committee of the CPPCC and the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea; the National Committee of the CPPCC and the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea presented two banners each to the Korean People's Delegation to China. During the banner presentation, the band played majestic music, and the hall erupted with prolonged applause.

    After the welcoming meeting, the art troupe of the Korean People's Delegation to China held an inaugural performance.

    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Liu Shaoqi received all members of the Korean People's Delegation to China, led by head of the delegation Kim Ung-gi, and the heads of the three art troupes at 7:20 PM on the 16th.

    Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, was present at the reception.

    Academician Yudin, Ambassador of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to China, was also present.

    From the Chinese side, Zhang Lan, Zhou Enlai, Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Dehuai, Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, Peng Zhen, Xi Zhongxun, Su Yu, Huang Kecheng, Xiao Hua, Liao Chengzhi, and Chen Yi were present. (Picture attached)

### 首都各界在怀仁堂举行盛会欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团  刘少奇副主席昨晚接见代表团

1954-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界在怀仁堂举行盛会欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团
    刘少奇副主席昨晚接见代表团
    据新华社讯：首都各界人民十六日晚七时半在怀仁堂举行盛大欢迎会，热烈欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团。
    以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团和随代表团前来的三个艺术团体全体人员出席了大会。
    中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、张澜，政务院总理周恩来，副总理董必武、陈云、邓小平，中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席沈钧儒，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通，中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席彭真，政务院秘书长习仲勋，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕、黄克诚，总政治部副主任萧华等出席了大会。
    参加欢迎大会的有：中国共产党中央委员会委员，全国各民主党派、各人民团体的代表和无党派民主人士，我国各兄弟民族的代表，中央人民政府政务院及所属各委、部、署、院、行首长，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会各部首长，中国人民解放军高级将领，华北行政委员会、华北抗美援朝总分会、北京市人民政府、北京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会、北京市抗美援朝分会的负责人，以及著名的科学、教育、文艺、新闻界人士和战斗英雄等。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一和使馆人员也参加了欢迎会。
    参加欢迎大会的外宾有苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻我国大使尤金和人民民主国家驻我国使节。
    参加欢迎大会的共有一千四百多人。
    大会主席台的中央悬挂着毛泽东主席、金日成元帅和马林科夫主席的巨幅画像，两旁悬挂着中、朝、苏三国国旗，周围布满花草。当以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团步入会场时，全场响起经久不息的掌声。
    七时三十分，欢迎大会在庄严的朝中两国国歌的乐声中开始。郭沫若在欢迎大会上致欢迎词（全文另发）。
    郭沫若致欢迎词后，在雷动的掌声中，朝鲜人民访华代表团团长、副团长接受了首都青年的献花。
    接着，金应基团长致答词（全文另发）。金应基团长的讲话，一再得到欢迎者的雷鸣般掌声。
    欢迎会上宾主互献锦旗，朝鲜人民访华代表团把两面锦旗献给中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会和中国人民抗美援朝总会；中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会和中国人民抗美援朝总会把两面锦旗献给朝鲜人民访华代表团。献旗时，乐队奏起雄壮的乐曲，会场上响起经久不息的掌声。
    欢迎会结束后，接着由朝鲜人民访华代表团艺术团举行揭幕演出。
   【新华社十六日讯】中央人民政府刘少奇副主席在十六日晚七时二十分接见以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团全体团员和三个艺术团体的负责人。
    接见时在座的有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一。
    在座的还有苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻我国大使尤金院士。
    中国方面在座的有张澜，周恩来，董必武，陈云，郭沫若，邓小平，彭德怀，沈钧儒，陈叔通，彭真，习仲勋，粟裕，黄克诚，萧华，廖承志，陈沂。（附图片）
