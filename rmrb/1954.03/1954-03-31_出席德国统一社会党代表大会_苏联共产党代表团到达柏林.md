Title: Soviet Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Berlin to Attend the German Socialist Unity Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-03-31
Page: 4

    To Attend the German Socialist Unity Party Congress
    Soviet Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Berlin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 30th] TASS, Berlin, March 30th: The Soviet Communist Party delegation attending the Fourth Congress of the German Socialist Unity Party arrived in Berlin on March 29th. The delegation included: A.I. Mikoyan, Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party and Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers; M.A. Suslov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party; D.T. Shepilov, Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda"; and A.A. Smirnov.
    Welcoming the delegation at Schönefeld Airport were Walter Ulbricht, First Secreatry of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party and Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Germany, and Hermann Matern and Friedrich Oelsner, members of the Politburo of the German Socialist Unity Party.
    Representatives of the Soviet Embassy, led by V.S. Semyonov, Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, also welcomed the delegation.

### 出席德国统一社会党代表大会  苏联共产党代表团到达柏林

1954-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    出席德国统一社会党代表大会
    苏联共产党代表团到达柏林
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社柏林三十日讯：出席德国统一社会党第四次代表大会的苏联共产党代表团于三月二十九日到达柏林。代表团包括：苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏联部长会议副主席阿·伊·米高扬；苏联共产党中央委员会书记米·安·苏斯洛夫；“真理报”总编辑德·特·谢皮洛夫；阿·阿斯米尔诺夫。
    在什涅菲尔德机场欢迎代表团的有德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记、德意志民主共和国副总理瓦尔特·乌布利希、德国统一社会党政治局委员赫·马特恩和弗·厄斯纳。
    欢迎代表团的还有以苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使弗·斯·谢麦诺夫为首的苏联大使馆代表。
