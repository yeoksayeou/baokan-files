Title: Bulgarian Publication of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," Bulgarian Edition, Volume 1
Author:
Date: 1954-03-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, March 22nd: Bulgaria has recently published the first volume of the Bulgarian edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong." The journal "Philosophical Thought," published by the Institute of Philosophy of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, published an article commenting on it. The article analyzed in detail the works of Comrade Mao Zedong from March 1926 to July 1937, and introduced "On Practice" at great length. The article stated that the characteristics of the articles in this volume of Comrade Mao Zedong's works are: a profound understanding of Marxism, the creative application of Marxism to Chinese reality, and the combination of theory and practice. The article emphasized that the publication of the Bulgarian edition of the Selected Works of Mao Zedong will help the Bulgarian people understand the great victories achieved by the Chinese people, and will help consolidate and strengthen the friendship between the people of Bulgaria and China.

### 保加利亚出版“毛泽东选集”保文版第一卷

1954-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚出版“毛泽东选集”保文版第一卷
    【新华社二十二日讯】据保加利亚通讯社索非亚十九日讯：保加利亚最近出版了“毛泽东选集”保加利亚文版第一卷。保加利亚科学院哲学研究所机关刊物“哲学思想”发表了一篇文章加以评介。文章详尽地分析了毛泽东同志从一九二六年三月到一九三七年七月这段时期中的著作，并以巨大篇幅介绍了“实践论”。文章说：毛泽东同志的这一卷著作中的文章的特点是：对马克思主义的深刻的认识，把马克思主义创造性地运用于中国实际，以及理论和实践的结合。文章着重说：毛泽东选集保文版的出版，将有助于保加利亚人民了解中国人民所获得的伟大胜利，并有助于巩固和加强保中两国人民的友谊。
