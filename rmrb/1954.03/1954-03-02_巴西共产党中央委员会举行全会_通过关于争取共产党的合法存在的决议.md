Title: Brazilian Communist Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session, Passes Resolution on Striving for the Legal Existence of the Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1954-03-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, February 28th: The Brazilian newspaper "People's Daily" published the resolution of the Central Committee of the Brazilian Communist Party adopted at its plenary session in December 1953 regarding the struggle for the legal existence of the Communist Party.
    The Central Committee pointed out the role played by the Communist Party among the broad working people and the masses in the struggle for peace, freedom, national independence, and people's democracy. The resolution stated that in order to strengthen this struggle, it is necessary to strengthen the ties between the Party and the masses, strive for the Party's legal existence, and participate in the Brazilian elections to be held in 1954. The Central Committee decided to submit a new application to the Supreme Electoral Court regarding the inclusion of the Communist Party in the electoral lists and entrusted the Presidium of the Central Committee to strive for formal permission for the Communist Party to participate in the 1954 elections.

### 巴西共产党中央委员会举行全会  通过关于争取共产党的合法存在的决议

1954-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西共产党中央委员会举行全会
    通过关于争取共产党的合法存在的决议
    【新华社一日讯】据塔斯社蒙得维的亚二月二十八日讯：巴西“人民报”刊载了巴西共产党中央委员会一九五三年十二月全体会议关于争取共产党合法存在的决议。
    中央委员会指出共产党在广大的劳动人民和人民群众中间，为和平、自由、民族独立和人民民主的斗争中所起的作用。决议说，为了加强这个斗争，必须加强党和群众的联系，争取党的合法存在和参加巴西一九五四年举行的选举。中央委员会决定提交最高选举院一件关于把共产党列入选举名单的新的申请书，并委托中央委员会主席团争取正式允许共产党参加一九五四年的选举。
