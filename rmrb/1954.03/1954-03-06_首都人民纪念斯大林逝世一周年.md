Title: People in the Capital Commemorate the First Anniversary of Stalin's Death
Author:
Date: 1954-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 5th: Today and yesterday, the people of the capital commemorated the first anniversary of the death of Stalin, the great teacher of the working people of the world and the dear friend of the Chinese people, with deep feelings of remembrance.

Commemorative activities began on the 4th. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries jointly sponsored a report meeting on the evening of the 4th to commemorate the first anniversary of Comrade Stalin's death. More than 500 people, including workers, students, government officials, and staff members of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing, attended the meeting. Soviet expert Pavlov gave a report on "Stalin is the Great Successor to Lenin's Cause." After the meeting, the film "The Defence of Tsaritsyn" was shown. The Beijing Municipal People's Government also held a memorial meeting on the 4th.

Factories, schools, and government organizations have successively launched various mass commemorative activities. The chairman of the trade union of the Beijing No. 1 Machine Tool Factory, which has received strong assistance from Soviet experts, broadcast to the entire factory on the afternoon of the 4th, calling on all employees to overfulfill the production plan and use practical actions to accelerate the industrialization of the motherland to mourn Comrade Stalin. The employees of Xinhua Printing Factory, which is printing "The Complete Works of Stalin," held memorial meetings and symposiums on the evening of the 5th. Many workers said at the symposiums that they would print "The Complete Works of Stalin" more beautifully and deliver them to readers across the country in a timely manner. The Chinese employees of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital and the Soviet Exhibition Center construction site, which symbolize the great friendship between China and the Soviet Union, more deeply felt Stalin's care and love for the Chinese people, and held commemorative activities on the 4th and 5th, respectively. Wu Yuzhang, president of the Renmin University of China, elaborated on Stalin's great contributions to the revolutionary cause and revolutionary theory at the memorial meeting of teachers and students of the university. The university library and the Marxism-Leninism teaching and research department also held photo exhibitions about Comrade Stalin's works and life. Students of Tsinghua University held a poetry recitation meeting, reciting poems commemorating Stalin. To commemorate the first anniversary of Comrade Stalin's death, the Beijing Library arranged photos of Stalin's life in the display windows, and set up a special shelf in the reading room to introduce Stalin's works. On the evening of the 5th, the library invited translator Liu Liaoyi to report to readers on "Stalin and Literature," and recited four poems praising Stalin, including Soviet writer Pavlenko's "The Power of Promise," Turkish revolutionary poet Hikmet's "I Remember," and Chinese cultural worker Li Ruifu's "He Lives Forever." The Xidan branch of the library invited scientist Zuo Kai to tell readers about "Stalin's Plan for Transforming Nature." In addition to recommending books about Stalin's concern for children to young readers, the children's branch of the library also showed slides of "Stalin's Life."

In recent days, a continuous stream of people has flocked to public cultural and recreational venues such as Beihai Park, the Working People's Cultural Palace, and the Dongsi District Cultural Center to view photo exhibitions on the life and work of the great revolutionary teacher Comrade Stalin. The cinemas showing the film "Stalin Lives Forever in Our Hearts" were packed for every show.

### 首都人民纪念斯大林逝世一周年

1954-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    首都人民纪念斯大林逝世一周年
    【新华社五日讯】今天和昨天，首都人民以深切怀念的心情，纪念全世界劳动人民的伟大导师、中国人民亲爱的朋友斯大林逝世一周年。
    各项纪念活动从四日起就展开了。北京市中苏友好协会和北京苏联对外文化协会四日晚联合主办了纪念斯大林同志逝世一周年的报告会。参加这个会的有工人、学生、机关干部和中国人民解放军驻京部队工作人员等五百多人。会上由苏联专家巴甫洛夫做了“斯大林是列宁事业的伟大的继承者”的报告，会后并放映了“保卫察里津”影片。北京市人民政府也在四日举行了纪念大会。
    工厂、学校和机关相继展开了各种群众性的纪念活动。得到苏联专家大力帮助的北京第一机床厂的工会主席四日下午向全厂广播，号召全体职工超额完成生产计划，用加速祖国工业化的实际行动来悼念斯大林同志。正印制“斯大林全集”的新华印刷厂职工于五日晚分别举行了纪念会、座谈会。许多工人在座谈会上表示：要把“斯大林全集”印制得更精美，及时送到全国读者的手里。标志着中苏伟大友谊的北京苏联红十字医院和苏联展览馆工地的中国职工们，更深切地体会到斯大林对我国人民的关怀和热爱，于四、五日分别举行了纪念活动。中国人民大学校长吴玉章在该校师生的纪念大会上阐述了斯大林对革命事业和革命理论的伟大贡献。该校图书馆和马克思列宁主义教研室还举办了有关斯大林同志著作、生平的图片展览。清华大学学生举行诗歌朗诵会，朗诵纪念斯大林的诗。北京市图书馆为纪念斯大林同志逝世一周年，在橱窗布置了有关斯大林生平的照片，阅览室设了介绍斯大林著作的专架。该馆于五日晚邀请翻译工作者刘辽逸给读者报告“斯大林与文学”，会上朗诵了苏联作家巴甫连科的“诺言的力量”、土耳其革命诗人希克梅特的“我想起”和我国文化工作者李蕤夫的“他永远活着”等四首歌颂斯大林的诗篇。该馆的西单分馆请科学工作者左垲给读者讲述“斯大林改造自然计划”。该馆儿童分馆除向少年儿童读者推荐有关斯大林关怀小朋友的图书外，并给小朋友放映了“斯大林的生平”幻灯。
    连日来，川流不息的人群涌向北海公园、劳动人民文化宫和东四区文化馆等公共文娱场所，观看伟大革命导师斯大林同志生平事业的图片展览。放映“斯大林永远活在我们的心里”影片的影院，每场都告满座。
