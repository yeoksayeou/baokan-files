Title: The Soviet Government Handed a Memorandum to the U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union Explaining the Soviet Union's Opinions on the Preparatory Work for the Geneva Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-03-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 18th: TASS, Moscow, March 18th: Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs V.V. Kuznetsov received U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union Charles Bohlen on March 17th and informed him of the Soviet government's opinion on the U.S. government's memorandum of March 5th. This memorandum concerned some issues regarding the preparatory work for the Geneva Conference to be held on April 26, 1954. The memorandum pointed out that the U.S. government had discussed these issues with the governments of the United Kingdom and France and reached a consensus.
    V.V. Kuznetsov handed a memorandum to the U.S. Ambassador. In this memorandum, the Soviet government explained its views on the preparation of the Geneva Conference and specifically pointed out that the Soviet government accepted the proposal to hold the conference at the League of Nations Building in Geneva. The memorandum also pointed out that the Soviet government had discussed its views with the government of the People's Republic of China, and the government of the People's Republic of China had expressed its agreement with these views.

### 苏联政府交给美国驻苏大使一件备忘录说明苏联对日内瓦会议准备工作的意见

1954-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联政府交给美国驻苏大使一件备忘录
    说明苏联对日内瓦会议准备工作的意见
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十八日讯：苏联外交部副部长瓦·瓦·库兹涅佐夫三月十七日接见美国驻苏联大使查尔斯·波伦，把苏联政府对美国政府三月五日的备忘录的意见通知他。这个备忘录所涉及的是关于在一九五四年四月二十六日召开日内瓦会议的准备工作的一些问题。备忘录指出，美国政府已经和联合王国政府和法国政府讨论过这些问题，并取得一致意见。
    瓦·瓦·库兹湼佐夫把一份备忘录交给美国大使。苏联政府在这份备忘录中，说明了它对准备日内瓦会议的意见，并且特别指出：苏联政府接受在日内瓦国联大厦举行会议的建议。备忘录也指出，苏联政府已经把它的意见和中华人民共和国政府讨论过，中华人民共和国政府表示同意这些意见。
