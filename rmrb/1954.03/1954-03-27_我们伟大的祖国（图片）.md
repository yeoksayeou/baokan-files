    Our Great Motherland
    In the south of our motherland, peach blossoms are already in full bloom. Farmers are busy with spring plowing. This is a scene of farmers in the Fanshen Agricultural Production Cooperative of Jiuhu Township, Longxi, Fujian, plowing the fields.
