Title: Visiting Delegation慰问 Visits Peng Pai Martyr's Mother
Author: 
Date: 1954-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:

    The Fifth Sub-Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, while慰问ing the local troops stationed in Haifeng area, Guangdong Province, also comforted the eighty-year-old Red Army mother and revolutionary martyr Peng Pai's mother, Old Mrs. Peng. On March 11th and 12th, Tan Yubao, Deputy Head of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army and Head of the Fifth Sub-Delegation, and Deputy Heads Li Minghao and Li Bogang led some representatives to慰问 Old Mrs. Peng. Deputy Head Tan Yubao greeted Old Mrs. Peng warmly upon meeting her. They also presented a flag and gifts on behalf of the Communist Party of China, the Central People's Government, Chairman Mao, and the people of the whole country to Old Mrs. Peng. Volunteer Army Representative Su Min also presented Old Mrs. Peng with valuable ginseng brought back from Korea. Old Mrs. Peng was very grateful for the care of Chairman Mao and the people of the whole country, and she repeatedly wished Chairman Mao good health and all comrades of the Volunteer Army success in their work. Old Mrs. Peng also led the representatives to visit Peng Pai's former residence.

### 慰问团慰问彭湃烈士的母亲

1954-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    慰问团慰问彭湃烈士的母亲
    据新华社讯：全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团第五总分团在广东省海丰地区向当地驻军进行慰问中，同时慰问了八十高龄的红军妈妈、革命烈士彭湃的母亲彭老太太。三月十一、十二两天，全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团副总团长、第五总分团团长谭余保，副团长李明灏、李伯刚先后率领部分代表慰问了彭老太太。谭余保副总团长一见了彭老太太就亲切地向她问好。他们并代表中国共产党、中央人民政府、毛主席和全国人民向彭老太太献旗献礼。志愿军代表苏民也把从朝鲜带回来的名贵人参献给彭老太太。彭老太太十分感谢毛主席和全国人民的关怀，她一再祝福毛主席身体健康，祝志愿军全体同志工作胜利。彭老太太并带领着代表们参观了彭湃的故居。
