Title: Chinese Journalists Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd—The Chinese journalists delegation that visited the "Pravda" delegation, after more than fifty days of study and visits in the Soviet Union, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 3rd. "Pravda's" newly appointed Beijing correspondent, Domagotskikh, also came to Beijing on the same flight with the delegation. Representatives from the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, as well as负责人 (responsible persons) and staff from the People's Daily, Xinhua News Agency, Central People's Broadcasting Station, Worker's Daily, China Youth Daily, and Beijing Daily, were at the airport to welcome them.

Also at the airport to welcome them were S.F. Antonov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, Koren, "Pravda's" Beijing correspondent, Bianmianov, head of the TASS Beijing branch, and Kozhevnikov, "Literary Gazette's" Beijing correspondent, and others.

### 我新闻工作者代表团回京

1954-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我新闻工作者代表团回京
    【新华社三日讯】中国新闻工作者访问“真理报”代表团，在苏联进行了五十多天的学习和参观后，于三日下午乘飞机回到北京。“真理报”新任驻北京记者多玛郭茨基赫也和代表团同机来京。到机场欢迎的有中共中央宣传部和中央人民政府外交部的代表，人民日报、新华通讯社、中央人民广播电台、工人日报、中国青年报、北京日报的负责人和工作人员多人。
    到机场欢迎的还有苏联驻华大使馆参赞司高磋，“真理报”驻北京记者科仁，塔斯社北京分社社长边缅诺夫和“文学报”驻北京记者科热夫尼科夫等。
