Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1954-03-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 25th: The Korean Central News Agency reported on the 24th that the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party issued the following communique regarding the plenary session of the Central Committee in March 1954:

    The Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party held a plenary session from March 21st to 23rd, 1954. The meeting discussed the following issues: 1. Regarding the various shortcomings in the industrial and transportation sectors, and the current tasks of the party, state, and economic organs and their staff to correct these shortcomings; 2. Regarding organizational matters.

    Regarding the first issue, the meeting heard a report by Comrade Kim Il-sung. Subsequently, the participants in the meeting engaged in a heated discussion of the report and adopted a corresponding resolution. After Comrade Kim Il-sung delivered a report on the second issue, the plenary session adopted a corresponding resolution on organizational matters.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央委员会举行全体会议

1954-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会举行全体会议
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】朝鲜中央通讯社二十四日讯：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会发表关于举行中央委员会一九五四年三月全体会议的公报如下：
    一九五四年三月二十一日到二十三日，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会举行了全体会议。会议上讨论了以下问题：一、关于在产业、运输部门中的各种缺点，和为了纠正这些缺点，党、国家和经济机关及其工作人员的当前任务；二、关于组织问题。
    关于第一个问题，会议听取了金日成同志的报告。接着，会议的参加者就这个报告进行了热烈的讨论，并通过了相应的决定。在金日成同志就第二个问题作了报告以后，全体会议对于组织问题通过了相应的决定。
