Title: Signing of This Year's China-Germany Trade and Payments Agreement; Head of Our Business Delegation Xu Xuehan Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1954-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin: Xu Xuehan, head of the Chinese business delegation to the German Democratic Republic and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Berlin by train from Warsaw on March 6th to sign the 1954 China-Germany Trade and Payments Agreement. Welcoming him at the station were Willi Huettenrauch, State Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic, and other representatives. Wang Yutian, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, Zhang Guangdou, Commercial Counselor, and Zhang Huadong, Deputy Head of the Chinese Business Delegation (who had arrived in Berlin earlier), were also at the station to welcome him.

### 签订今年中德换货和付款协定  我商务代表团长徐雪寒到柏林

1954-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    签订今年中德换货和付款协定
    我商务代表团长徐雪寒到柏林
    【新华社柏林讯】前往德意志民主共和国的中国商务代表团团长、中华人民共和国中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒于三月六日由华沙乘火车到达柏林，前来签订一九五四年中德换货和付款协定。在车站欢迎的有德意志民主共和国对外与国内贸易部国务秘书韦利·胡坦劳赫和其他代表。我国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆参赞王雨田和商务参赞张光斗及中国商务代表团副团长张化东（已先到柏林）等也到车站欢迎。
