Title: People of Poland, Romania, Democratic Germany, India, and other countries commemorate the first anniversary of the death of the great Stalin
Author:
Date: 1954-03-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch: The people of Poland and Romania commemorate the first anniversary of the death of Stalin, the great successor to Lenin's cause.

	On March 5th, working people across Poland participated in memorial meetings held in various locations. At the memorial meeting in Warsaw, attendees listened attentively to reports on Stalin's life and career, as well as his friendly assistance to the Polish people. Memorial meetings were held in various mines, metallurgical factories, and other industrial enterprises in the Stalinogród Province. More than two thousand workers from the Stalin Factory in Poznań Province gathered to commemorate the first anniversary of Stalin's death. In addition, many towns in Poland also held exhibitions introducing Stalin's life and struggles. The central organ publication of the Polish-Soviet Friendship Association, the "Friendship" weekly, published a special commemorative issue and published an editorial entitled "First Anniversary of Stalin's Death."

	Romanian newspapers devoted considerable space on the 5th to commemorate the first anniversary of Stalin's death. The newspapers all published commemorative editorials and carried many commemorative articles by famous politicians, cultural figures, and artists. C.I. Parhon, chairman of the Romanian-Soviet Friendship Association, published an article in "Spark" newspaper entitled "A Friend Loved and Unforgettable by the Romanian People," saying: The Romanian working people closely link Comrade Stalin's brilliant image with their infinite love and gratitude for the great liberator, the Soviet Union, the Soviet people, and the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The Romanian people have always regarded Stalin as their great friend, in times of difficulty they received his support, and his wisdom will always help them build their new life.

	Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th dispatch: The working people of the German Democratic Republic commemorate the first anniversary of the death of Stalin, the great teacher of the working people of the world, with deep remembrance. Factories, institutions, and schools in the Berlin Democratic District and throughout the Republic held memorial meetings on the 5th to commemorate the first anniversary of Stalin's death. The memorial meeting in the Berlin Democratic District was held in front of the Stalin statue on Stalin Avenue, and the statue was filled with wreaths presented by the working people. Newspapers in the Berlin Democratic District all published commemorative editorials or special issues on the 5th. In Riesa, a steel industry center in Saxony, 25,000 working people held an unveiling ceremony on the 4th for the newly completed Stalin statue. The statue is 4.2 meters high and is the largest Stalin statue in the Republic. When building this statue, steel workers in Riesa actively participated in special work shifts and sent their excess wages to build the statue.

	In the year since Stalin's death, the working people of the German Democratic Republic have actively striven to join the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and commemorated their great teacher and friend by participating in activities of the German-Soviet Friendship Association and by increasing production and practicing economy. In the past year, the number of members of the German-Soviet Friendship Association has increased to more than 3.1 million, with nearly 140,000 new members added in November alone. Workers at the "Marx-Engels" potash plant in Unterbreizbach produced 530 tons of potash above plan on the eve of the first anniversary of Stalin's death.

	Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 7th dispatch: The name of the great Stalin lives forever in the hearts of millions of Indian people. On March 5th, on the occasion of the first anniversary of Stalin's death, people across India commemorated him.

	In Delhi, the specially established Stalin Memorial Day Committee convened a memorial meeting on the evening of the 5th at Urdu Park. A total of 1,500 people attended the meeting. Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, served as Chairman.

	The meeting began with the recitation of poems praising and mourning Stalin. Chairman Kitchlew deeply remembered the deceased great man, and he especially spoke of his meeting with Stalin last year. He praised the Soviet Union's unwavering peace policy. He condemned the United States' military "aid" to Pakistan and exposed the United States' conspiracy to "use Asians to fight Asians." In his speech, Kitchlew called on the Indian people to strengthen the peace movement to defend peace in Asia and the world.

	Z.A. Ahmad, member of the Politburo of the Communist Party of India, paid tribute to Stalin on behalf of the Communist Party of India. He also said: "Stalin was the greatest leader of the world peace movement." He called on the people to study, understand, and learn from Stalin's life and writings.

	At the end of the meeting, all participants stood in silence for one minute for Stalin.

	Memorial gatherings were held in major cities in India—Calcutta, Bombay, Madras, Lucknow, and many other towns. The Benares Committee of the Communist Party of India also held a "Stalin Week" from February 27th to March 5th.

	On this memorial day, many newspapers and magazines published special commemorative pages for Stalin. The "Freedom Daily" editorial on the 5th, entitled "Commemorating Stalin," wrote: "Today, the Soviet Union and the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union are striving to defend peace in accordance with the principle of meeting people's needs to the greatest extent put forward by Stalin; countless working people and progressive people in the capitalist and enslaved world are holding high the torch of the peace policy indicated by Stalin and moving forward."

### 波兰、罗马尼亚、民主德国、印度等国人民  纪念伟大的斯大林逝世一周年

1954-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰、罗马尼亚、民主德国、印度等国人民
    纪念伟大的斯大林逝世一周年
    【新华社七日讯】波兰、罗马尼亚人民纪念列宁事业的伟大继承者斯大林逝世一周年。
    三月五日，波兰全国的劳动人民参加了各地举行的纪念会。在华沙的纪念会上，与会者静听关于斯大林的生平及事业和他对波兰人民的友好援助的报告。斯大林诺格罗德省的各个矿场、冶金工厂和其他工业企业都举行了纪念会。波兹南省斯大林工厂的两千多个工人集会纪念斯大林逝世一周年。此外，波兰许多城镇还举办了介绍斯大林生平和斗争的展览会。波苏友好协会中央机关刊物——“友谊”周刊，出版了纪念专号，并以“斯大林逝世一周年”为题发表了社论。
    罗马尼亚各报在五日都以大量篇幅纪念斯大林逝世一周年。各报都发表了纪念的社论刊载了著名的政治家、文化界和艺术界人士的许多纪念文章。罗苏友好协会主席巴洪在“火花报”上发表题为“罗马尼亚人民所热爱和永志不忘的朋友”的文章说：罗马尼亚劳动人民都把斯大林同志的光辉形象，和他们对于伟大的解放者苏联、苏联人民及苏联共产党的无限热爱与感激密切地联结在一起。罗马尼亚人民过去、现在和将来都把斯大林当作自己的伟大的朋友，在危难的时刻他们得到了他的支援，他的智慧永远帮助他们建设自己的新生活。
    【新华社柏林七日电】德意志民主共和国各地劳动人民以深切怀念的心情纪念全世界劳动人民的伟大导师斯大林逝世一周年。柏林民主区和共和国各地的工厂、机关和学校，五日举行了斯大林逝世一周年纪念会。柏林民主区的纪念会在斯大林大街的斯大林铜像前举行，铜像前面放满了劳动人民敬献的花圈。柏林民主区各报都在五日发表了纪念的社论或专刊。在萨克森州的炼钢业中心里萨市，二万五千劳动人民在四日举行了新落成的斯大林铜像揭幕典礼。这座铜像高四点二公尺，是共和国最大的一座斯大林铜像。在建造这座铜像时，里萨市的钢铁工人曾踊跃地参加了特别工作班，把他们得到的超额工资送去建造铜像。
    斯大林逝世的一年来，德意志民主共和国的劳动人民积极争取参加德国统一社会党，并以参加德苏友好协会的行动和增产节约的成绩来纪念自己的伟大导师和朋友。一年来德苏友好协会会员的数目已增加到三百一十万人以上，单在去年十一月就将近增加了十四万新会员。安特布莱吞巴赫的“马克思—恩格斯”制钾厂的工人们，在斯大林逝世一周年前夕超计划生产了五百三十吨钾。
    【新华社新德里七日电】伟大的斯大林的名字永远活在千百万印度人民的心里。三月五日，在斯大林逝世一周年的时候，印度各地人民都纪念他。
    在德里，特别成立的斯大林纪念日委员会五日晚在乌尔都公园召开纪念大会。参加大会的共有一千五百人。全印和平理事会主席赛福丁·克其鲁博士担任主席。
    大会在朗诵着歌颂和悼念斯大林的诗篇的声中开始。主席克其鲁深切地怀念已故的伟人，他特别谈到去年他会见斯大林时的情景。他盛赞苏联的坚持不渝的和平政策。他谴责美国给予巴基斯坦的军事“援助”，并揭露了美国“用亚洲人打亚洲人”的阴谋。克其鲁在致词中号召印度人民加强和平运动，来保卫亚洲与世界和平。
    印度共产党政治局委员兹·阿·艾哈迈德代表印度共产党向斯大林致敬。他并且说：“斯大林是世界和平运动的最伟大的领袖。”他号召人民研究、了解并学习斯大林的生平和著作。
    在会议结束时，全体参加者都起立为斯大林默哀一分钟。
    在印度的各大城市——加尔各答、孟买、马德拉斯、勒克瑙以及其他许多城镇都举行了纪念集会。印度共产党贝拿勒斯委员会并从二月二十七日到三月五日举行了“斯大林周”。
    在这一个纪念日，许多报纸和杂志都出了斯大林纪念专页。“自由报”五日的题为“纪念斯大林”的社论写道：“今天，苏联和以苏联为首的社会主义阵营遵照着斯大林所提出的最大限度地满足人们的需要的原则，尽力来保卫和平；资本主义和被奴役世界的无数劳动人民和进步人民都高举着斯大林所指示的和平政策的火炬前进。”
