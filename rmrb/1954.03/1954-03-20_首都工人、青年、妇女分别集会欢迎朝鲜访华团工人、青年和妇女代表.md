Title: Capital Workers, Youth, and Women Hold Rallies to Welcome Korean Visiting Delegation of Workers, Youth, and Women Representatives
Author:
Date: 1954-03-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 19th:

    Capital Workers, Youth, and Women Hold Rallies
    To Welcome Korean Visiting Delegation of Workers, Youth, and Women Representatives
    The worker, youth, and women representatives of the Korean People's Visiting Delegation to China attended welcome rallies held by workers, youth, and women in the capital on the evening of the 18th and gave reports. At the meetings, Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Ze, Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and Zhang Yun and Li Dequan, Vice Chairwomen of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, exchanged banners with the representatives, who also presented Kim Il-sung commemorative badges.
    Worker representatives of the delegation, including Li Zhongxiu, Jin Zhixian, Chen Shengyuan, and Li Zhongxie, attended the welcome rally jointly held by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions. Zhu Xuefan delivered a welcome speech at the meeting. He said: The unity and friendship between the working classes and peoples of China and Korea is an extremely powerful force that no imperialism can defeat. The working classes of China and Korea will unite even more closely in the future and work together to defend the cause of world peace. Representative Li Zhongxiu gave a report to the more than 600 worker representatives from the capital who attended the meeting. He said: In the war to defend the independence and freedom of their country, the Korean working class actively responded to the call of Marshal Kim Il-sung, displayed the spirit of unyielding hard work, and ensured the needs of the front lines. More than 90% of workers in various industrial sectors participated in mass movements to increase production and practice economy. The heroic Korean workers have demonstrated invincible strength to the people of the world. They are now throwing themselves into the work of economic recovery and construction with unparalleled enthusiasm. Li Zhongxiu's report repeatedly aroused prolonged applause from the audience.
    At a welcome rally held by more than 800 young people in the capital, representatives Kim Jinze, Zhang Xianhua, Huang Jian, and Cui? were warmly welcomed into the venue. Kim Jinze reported to the assembly on the countless heroic deeds of Korean youth in various positions in the struggle against the invaders. He said: During the war, all the youth of the Republic enthusiastically responded to Marshal Kim Il-sung's call of "Everything for the front line" and "Everything for victory." Within a few days of the start of the war, 900,000 young people put down their hammers, hoes, and pens and asked to go to the front lines. In the ranks of the People's Army, 477 "Heroes of the Republic" emerged, and hundreds of thousands of young men and women were rewarded by the state. His report was interrupted by enthusiastic applause from time to time. "Hero of the Republic" Zhang Xianhua and others also spoke at the meeting. Zhang Xianhua reported on his deeds of holding his position and defeating the American and Rhee armies. At the meeting, Cui? warmly shook hands with Chinese young friends. He said, "Brother Luo Shengjiao is like my own brother. Seeing you all makes me as excited as seeing Brother Luo Shengjiao."
    The women of the capital also warmly welcomed the women representatives of the delegation, Xu Yingji, Ju Fuli, and Cui Fenyu. After Li Dequan delivered a welcome speech, Xu Yingji gave a report to more than 600 people at the meeting. She described the great contributions of the heroic Korean women in the war of national defense and reconstruction. She said: During the war, many women resolutely took up arms and went to the front lines, creating many touching deeds that will be passed down through the ages to defend their motherland. The broad masses of women displayed a high degree of patriotic labor enthusiasm in the rural areas, factories, and mines in the rear, overcoming all difficulties and supporting the war. Now, Korean women are working hard to rebuild their beautiful homeland. In her report, she also warmly talked about the deep friendship between Korean mothers and the Chinese People's Volunteers.

### 首都工人、青年、妇女分别集会欢迎朝鲜访华团工人、青年和妇女代表

1954-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    首都工人、青年、妇女分别集会
    欢迎朝鲜访华团工人、青年和妇女代表
    【新华社十九日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团的工人、青年、妇女代表，十八日晚分别出席了首都工人、青年、妇女举行的欢迎会，并作了报告。会上，由中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记章泽，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴、李德全分别和代表们互赠锦旗，代表们并赠送了金日成纪念章。
    代表团工人代表李钟秀、金直铉、陈生渊、李钟燮等出席了中华全国总工会和北京市总工会联合举行的欢迎会。会上朱学范致欢迎词。他说：朝中两国工人阶级和人民的团结友谊，是一支无比强大的力量，是任何帝国主义所不可战胜的。朝中两国工人阶级今后将更紧密地团结，共同为保卫世界的和平事业而奋斗。代表李钟秀向到会的首都六百多工人代表作了报告。他说：在保卫祖国独立、自由的战争中，朝鲜工人阶级积极响应了金日成元帅的号召，发挥了不屈不挠艰苦劳动的精神，保证了前线的需要。各产业部门的工人百分之九十以上参加了群众性的增产节约竞赛运动。英勇的朝鲜工人在全世界人民面前显示了不可战胜的力量。他们现在又正以无比的热情投入了经济的恢复和建设工作。李钟秀的报告不断激起了全场经久不息的掌声。
    在首都八百多青年举行的欢迎会上，代表金辰泽、张贤化、黄健、崔?等四人被人们热情地迎入会场。金辰泽向大会报告了朝鲜青年在反抗侵略者的斗争中在各种岗位上的无数可歌可泣的英雄事迹。他说：在战争中，共和国全体青年热烈响应金日成元帅“一切为了前线”，“一切为了胜利”的号召。在战争开始的几天内有九十万青年放下铁锤、锄头、笔杆，纷纷要求奔赴前线。在人民军的队伍中涌现了四百七十七名“共和国英雄”，并有数十万男女青年受到国家的奖励。他的报告不时被热烈的掌声打断。“共和国英雄”张贤化等也在会上讲了话。张贤化报告了自己坚守阵地击溃美李军的事迹。崔?在会上和中国青年朋友们亲切地握手，他说：“罗盛教哥哥如同我的亲哥哥，我见了大家就像看见了罗盛教哥哥一样的兴奋。”
    首都妇女也满怀热情地欢迎代表团的妇女代表徐英姬、具富力、崔粉玉等。李德全致欢迎词后，徐英姬向大会六百多人作了报告。她叙述了英雄的朝鲜妇女在卫国战争和恢复建设中的巨大贡献。她说：在战争中，许多妇女毅然地拿起枪杆上前线，为保卫祖国创造了许多万古流传的动人事迹。广大妇女在后方的农村、工厂和矿山中发挥高度的爱国主义劳动热情，战胜一切困难，支援了战争。现在，朝鲜妇女正在为重建美丽的家园而努力。在报告中，她并热情地谈到朝鲜的母亲们和中国人民志愿军之间的深厚友谊。
