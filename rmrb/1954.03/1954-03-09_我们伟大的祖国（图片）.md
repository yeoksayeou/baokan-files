Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-03-09

Our great motherland:
    New China's women and men are working together to actively strive for the realization of socialist industrialization. This is a female welder from the structural workshop of the Anshan Iron and Steel Company's Metal Structure Engineering Company, welding gas pipelines for the newly built factory building.

    我们伟大的祖国
