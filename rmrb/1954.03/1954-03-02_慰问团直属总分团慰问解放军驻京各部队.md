Title: The慰问团直属General Sub-Group Visits PLA Troops Stationed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-03-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency: The Deputy Leaders of the General Sub-Group Directly Under the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, Zhang Youyu, Nie Zhen, Chen Qiyuan, Zeng Zhaolun, Qian Duansheng, Hou Debang, Hong Shen, Liang Sicheng, Shi Dongshan, and Liu Laifu, led representatives of the General Sub-Group to visit various organs, institutes, and schools of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, as well as various services and arms and troops of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing, from February 24th to March 1st.

    The慰问团representatives paid tribute to the People's Navy troops stationed in Beijing on behalf of the people of the whole country, thanking the People's Navy for its outstanding contributions in the struggle to defend the motherland, and for its achievements in protecting the coastline, islands, and fishermen's production. Representatives of the People's Navy troops stationed in Beijing stated that they would accelerate the work of building a modern People's Navy and more vigilantly defend the motherland's coastal defenses.

    The慰问团paid tribute to the People's Air Force troops stationed in Beijing who vigilantly defend the capital and guard the capital's skies. The representatives also visited the airport and had cordial conversations with many heroic pilots, mechanics, ground crew, and commanders. The increasing strength of the People's Air Force left a strong impression on the representatives.

    The慰问团visited the armored forces stationed in Beijing and visited the commanders and fighters of the armored forces, and visited the troops' classrooms, dormitories, and training grounds. The armored forces stationed in Beijing held shooting performances and tank performances to welcome the envoys of the motherland. The modern and magnificent lineup of the People's Armored Forces and the excellent technology and learning achievements of the commanders and fighters deeply moved the representatives.

    On behalf of the Central People's Government and the people of the capital, the慰问团expressed sincere gratitude and condolences to the commanders, fighters, and staff of the Public Security Forces stationed in Beijing and the Beijing Municipal Public Security Corps. These troops guard their posts day and night, vigilantly protecting the safety of the central organs and the people of all walks of life in the capital, enabling them to live in peace and carry out production work smoothly. They have made many achievements over the years and produced many heroes and model figures.

    The慰问团also visited the engineering troops of the People's Liberation Army, expressing sincere respect and gratitude to the cadres and soldiers of the engineering troops who are participating in the construction of the motherland.

    The慰问团representatives also visited the Communications Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and the communications troops stationed in Beijing, other departments, bureaus, offices, factories, and other organs and institutes; they also visited the Literary and Art Work Troupe of the General Political Department, the "August First" Film Studio, the Military Band of the General Staff Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and other units. The慰问团also visited the medical staff of the hospitals of various troops and the wounded and sick soldiers of the People's Liberation Army who were recuperating.

    The various drama troupes, ballad troupes, and acrobatic troupes of the General Sub-Group directly under the慰问团also actively carried out慰问performances.

### 慰问团直属总分团慰问解放军驻京各部队

1954-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    慰问团直属总分团慰问解放军驻京各部队
    据新华社讯：全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团直属总分团副团长张友渔、聂真、陈其瑗、曾昭抡、钱端升、侯德榜、洪深、梁思成、史东山、刘莱夫率领总分团代表，在二月二十四日到三月一日，分别到人民革命军事委员会各机关、院、校和人民解放军驻京各兵种、部队进行慰问。
    慰问团代表全国人民向人民海军驻京部队致敬，感谢人民海军在保卫祖国的斗争中的卓越的贡献，和保护海岸线和岛屿以及渔民生产的成绩。人民海军驻京部队代表表示，他们将更加速建设现代化人民海军的工作，更加警惕地保卫祖国海防。
    慰问团向警惕地保卫着首都，警卫着首都天空的人民空军驻京部队致敬。代表们并参观了机场，还和许多英勇的飞行员、机械员、地勤人员和指挥员进行了亲切的晤谈。人民空军的日益强大，给代表们留下了强烈的印象。
    慰问团慰问了装甲兵驻京部队，并访问了装甲兵的指挥员、战斗员，参观了部队的课堂、宿舍和演习场。装甲兵驻京部队为欢迎祖国人民的使者并举行了射击表演和坦克表演。人民装甲兵现代化的雄伟阵容和指挥员、战斗员们卓越的技术和学习成绩，深深感动了代表们。
    慰问团代表中央人民政府和首都人民向驻京公安部队和北京市公安总队的指挥员、战斗员和工作人员表示深切的感谢和慰问。这些部队日日夜夜守卫在自己的岗位上，警惕地保卫着中央机关的安全，保卫着首都各界人民，使他们安静地生活和顺利进行生产工作，几年来建树了许多功绩，出现了许多英雄、模范人物。
    慰问团还慰问了人民解放军的工程部队，向正在参加祖国建设的工程部队的干部和战士们表示深切的敬意和感谢。
    慰问团代表同时慰问了人民革命军事委员会通讯部和驻京通讯部队，其他部、局、处、厂等机关和院、校；还慰问了总政治部文艺工作团、“八一”制片厂、人民革命军事委员会总参谋部军乐团等单位。慰问团还慰问了各部队医院的医务工作人员和在休养中的人民解放军伤员和病员。
    慰问团直属总分团各剧团曲艺团、杂技团，也积极进行了慰问演出。
