Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-03-25
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The "May First" Collective Farm in Chengde County, Rehe Province, reaped a bountiful harvest last year, the second year after its establishment. It has decided to produce more grain this year to support national economic construction. This is the livestock farm of the "May First" Collective Farm. A delegation of workers from Anshan is visiting the farm and touring here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    热河省承德县“五一”集体农庄，去年获得了建庄后第二年的丰收，决定在今年生产更多的粮食来支援国家经济建设。这是“五一”集体农庄的牧畜场。鞍山工人访问农民代表团在这里参观。
