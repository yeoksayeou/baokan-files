    Our Great Motherland
    The section of the Chengdu-Aba Highway from Xuecheng Town, Li County to Shanjiao Dam, connecting the Chengdu Plain and the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Sichuan Province, was opened to traffic on February 11th. This is the scene of a float passing over the Ridi Zhai Bridge in Li County.
