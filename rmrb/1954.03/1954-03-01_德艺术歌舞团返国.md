Title: German Art Troupe Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The National People's Art Troupe of the German Democratic Republic has successfully concluded its visit and performances in our country. With the exception of a few members who traveled to Beijing to return home by plane, the majority of the troupe, led by Professor Hans Pischner, departed from Harbin on February 27th via special train to return home.

During its two-month visit to China, the National People's Art Troupe of the German Democratic Republic held 34 song and dance performances and 9 concerts in our capital and major cities, with a total audience of more than 140,000 people. The wonderful songs, dances, and musical programs performed by the art troupe were warmly welcomed and praised by the Chinese people.

The art troupe arrived in Harbin on February 23rd. During its four-day stay in Harbin, it gave two song and dance performances, and after the final performance on the 25th, held a closing ceremony to mark the end of its visit and performances in China. At the closing ceremony, Lu Qien, the Mayor of Harbin, delivered a speech of thanks on behalf of the Central Ministry of Culture and the people of Harbin. He thanked the song and dance troupe for its positive contributions to strengthening the friendship between the people of China and Germany, and promoting cultural exchange between the two countries. Hans Pischner, the head of the art troupe, delivered a farewell speech, expressing his intention to convey the Chinese people's sympathy for the German people's struggle for peace and unity to the German people.

Lu Qien, the Mayor of Harbin, held a reception to bid farewell to the song and dance troupe.

### 德艺术歌舞团返国

1954-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    德艺术歌舞团返国
    据新华社讯：德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团胜利结束了在我国的访问演出，除少数团员到北京乘飞机返国外，大部分团员在团长汉斯·皮什纳教授率领下，已于二月二十七日乘专车离哈尔滨返国。
    德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团来我国的两个多月中，先后在我国首都和各大城市举行了三十四场歌舞表演和九次音乐会，观众共达十四万多人。艺术歌舞团演出的精彩歌舞和音乐节目受到我国人民热烈欢迎和赞扬。
    艺术歌舞团二月二十三日到哈尔滨，在哈尔滨停留的四天中，曾作了两场歌舞表演，并在二十五日最后一场演出后，举行了结束在我国访问演出的闭幕式。闭幕式上哈尔滨市市长吕其恩代表我中央文化部和哈尔滨市人民致谢词。感谢歌舞团在加强中德两国人民友谊，和促进两国文化交流上所作的良好的贡献。艺术歌舞团团长汉斯·皮什纳致告别词，表示要把中国人民对德国人民争取和平、统一的同情很好地传达给德国人民。
    哈尔滨市市长吕其恩曾举行酒会欢送歌舞团。
