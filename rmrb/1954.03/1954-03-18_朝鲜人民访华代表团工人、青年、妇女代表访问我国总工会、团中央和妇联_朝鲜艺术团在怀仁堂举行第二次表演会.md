Title: Korean People's Delegation Workers, Youth, and Women Representatives Visit the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the All-China Women's Federation; Korean Art Troupe Holds Second Performance at Huairentang

Author:
Date: 1954-03-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 17th. The workers, youth, and women representatives of the Korean People's Delegation visited the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation on the 17th, respectively, and held discussions.

    The head of the delegation, Kim Ung-gi, led representatives Ri Jong-su, Jin Saeng-yeon, Kim Jik-hyeon, and Ri Jong-seop to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and were warmly welcomed by Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Vice Chairman Zhu Xuefan, and heads of various departments and industrial trade unions. In his welcoming speech, Lai Ruoyu emphasized that the long-standing friendship between the Chinese and Korean people has been solidified with noble blood through the war against the American aggressors. In the future, our Chinese and Korean people will not only jointly strive for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue, but also jointly achieve great victories in economic construction. Subsequently, representative Ri Jong-su introduced the heroic struggle of the Korean working class in the Patriotic War and the situation of carrying out reconstruction work after the armistice. At the meeting, Kim Ung-gi, head of the delegation, expressed his deep gratitude to the Chinese working class for their brotherly assistance to the Korean people.

    At the Central Committee of the Youth League, the youth representatives, led by Deputy Head of the delegation Baek Nam-un and Ri Yeong-ho, were warmly welcomed. Among these representatives were the young "Republic Heroes" Ryu Man-su, Jang Hyeon-hwa, and Park Chan-su. Hu Yaobang, Luo Yi, Rong Gaotang, and Zhang Ze, secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Youth League, heads of various departments, Qu Tangliang, vice chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Tian Demin, chairman of the All-China Student Federation, and more than 50 labor models from industry and agriculture in Beijing held a forum with the distinguished guests. In his speech, Hu Yaobang said: We are honored to have such heroic brothers and friends as you. He said: In the War of Resistance Against Japan and the Chinese Liberation War, Korean youth shed blood on Chinese soil; in the Korean people's struggle against aggression, Chinese youth shed blood on Korean soil. This unbreakable friendship is profound and great. At the meeting, representative Kim Jin-taek paid tribute to the Chinese youth on behalf of the Korean youth, pointing out that the achievements of the Chinese youth and the Chinese people in building a socialist society greatly encouraged the Korean people's confidence in victory.

    Zhang Yun, vice chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and heads of various departments warmly received the delegation's representatives Seo Young-hee, Gu Bu-ryeok, and Choi Bun-ok. Deputy Head of the delegation Lee Dong-yeong also participated in the gathering of women. In her welcoming speech, Zhang Yun pointed out that the great victories achieved by Korean women and the Korean people in the struggle against imperialist aggression and in reconstruction are a great contribution to the cause of defending peace in the East and the world, and also a great assistance to our peaceful construction. The heroic sisters of Korea are the pride of women all over the world. At the meeting, representative Seo Young-hee first expressed her heartfelt respect to the Chinese women who supported the front lines in Korea. She said: War brings particularly great sacrifices to women and children, so Korean women will continue to fight for the unification, independence, and peace of the motherland and the world.

    During these visits and discussions, everyone cordially exchanged information and experiences about their work. The atmosphere was full of warm friendship.

Xinhua News Agency, March 17th. The performance of the art troupe of the Korean People's Delegation, sponsored by the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the China People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, was held for the second time at Huairentang on the evening of the 17th.

    More than 1,000 people watched the performance, including cadres from various departments in the capital, representatives attending various professional conferences held in Beijing, and Soviet experts in Beijing.

    The art troupe performed eighteen wonderful programs. These programs vividly displayed the bold character of the heroic, hardworking, and talented Korean people. Every program was warmly welcomed by the audience, and the performers bowed several times to the audience's long and enthusiastic applause.

    At the end of the performance, the youth of the capital presented flowers to the art troupe. The entire audience stood up, and the applause lasted for a long time.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团工人、青年、妇女代表访问我国总工会、团中央和妇联  朝鲜艺术团在怀仁堂举行第二次表演会

1954-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团工人、青年、妇女代表
    访问我国总工会、团中央和妇联
    朝鲜艺术团在怀仁堂举行第二次表演会
   【新华社十七日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团的工人、青年、妇女代表，十七日分别访问中华全国总工会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国民主妇女联合会，并举行了座谈。
    代表团团长金应基率领李钟秀、陈生渊、金直铉、李钟燮等代表到总工会，受到了总工会主席赖若愚、副主席朱学范和各部、各产业工会负责人的热情欢迎。赖若愚在欢迎词中强调地指出：中朝人民历史悠久的友谊，经过反对美国侵略者的战争，已经以高贵的鲜血凝结在一起了。今后我们中朝人民将一面共同争取朝鲜问题的和平解决，一面在经济建设中共同取得伟大的胜利。接着，代表李钟秀介绍了朝鲜工人阶级在卫国战争中的英勇斗争，和停战后进行恢复建设工作的情况。会上，代表团团长金应基对中国工人阶级给予朝鲜人民兄弟般的援助，表示了深切的谢意。
    在青年团中央，由代表团副团长白南云、李永镐带领的青年代表们，受到了亲热的欢迎。这些代表中有年青的“共和国英雄”柳万洙、张贤化、朴赞洙等。青年团中央书记处书记胡耀邦、罗毅、荣高棠、章泽和各部负责人，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席区棠亮，中华全国学生联合会主席田德民，以及北京市工业、农业的劳动模范等五十多人，和贵宾们举行了座谈会。胡耀邦在讲话中说：我们以有你们这样英雄的兄弟和朋友而感到光荣。他说：在抗日战争和我国解放战争中，朝鲜青年在中国土地上洒过鲜血；在朝鲜人民反侵略斗争中，中国青年又在朝鲜土地上流了血，这种牢不可破的友谊是深厚而伟大的。会上，代表金辰泽代表朝鲜青年向中国青年致敬，他指出：中国青年和中国人民在建设社会主义社会中的各项成就，大大鼓舞了朝鲜人民的胜利信心。
    全国妇联副主席章蕴和各部负责人，热情地接待了代表团徐英姬、具富力、崔粉玉等代表，代表团副团长李东英也参加了妇女们的聚会。章蕴在欢迎词中指出：朝鲜妇女和朝鲜人民在反对帝国主义侵略斗争和恢复建设中，所取得的伟大胜利，是对保卫东方和世界和平事业的巨大贡献，也是对我国和平建设的极大援助。朝鲜英雄的姊妹们是全世界妇女的骄傲。会上，代表徐英姬首先向支援朝鲜前线的中国妇女表示衷心的敬意。她说：战争给妇女和儿童带来特别大的牺牲，所以朝鲜妇女将继续为争取祖国的统一、独立和全世界的和平而奋斗。
    在这些访问和座谈会上，大家亲切地交谈了工作情况和经验。充满着热烈友爱的空气。
      【新华社十七日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会和中国人民抗美援朝总会主办的朝鲜人民访华代表团艺术团表演会，十七日晚在怀仁堂进行第二次演出。
    观看演出的有首都各机关干部、在京召开的各专业会议的代表，以及在京的苏联专家等共一千多人。
    艺术团共演出十八个精彩的节目。这些节目生动地显示了英勇勤劳和高歌善舞的朝鲜人民的豪放性格。每一个节目都受到了观众热烈欢迎，表演者都在观众长久的热烈的掌声中谢幕数次。
    表演会结束时，首都青年向艺术团献花。会场全体起立，掌声经久不息。
