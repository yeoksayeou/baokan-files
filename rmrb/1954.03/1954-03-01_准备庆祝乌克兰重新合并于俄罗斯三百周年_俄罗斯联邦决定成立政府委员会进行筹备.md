Title: Preparing to Celebrate the 300th Anniversary of the Reunification of Ukraine with Russia; Russian Federation Decides to Establish Government Committee for Preparations
Author:
Date: 1954-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 27th: TASS Moscow, 27th -- The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation have passed a resolution to establish a government committee to prepare and hold celebrations for the 300th anniversary of the reunification of Ukraine with Russia. The committee includes sixty people: social activists, advanced industrial and agricultural workers, and science, culture, and art workers. A.M. Puzanov has been appointed as the chairman of the committee.

### 准备庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年  俄罗斯联邦决定成立政府委员会进行筹备

1954-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    准备庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年
    俄罗斯联邦决定成立政府委员会进行筹备
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十七日讯：苏联俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团和俄罗斯联邦部长会议通过了一项决议，决定成立一个政府委员会来筹备并举行乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年的庆祝活动。参加该委员会的有社会活动家，先进的工业和农业工作者，科学、文化和艺术工作者共六十人。亚·麦·普扎诺夫被任命为该委员会的主席。
