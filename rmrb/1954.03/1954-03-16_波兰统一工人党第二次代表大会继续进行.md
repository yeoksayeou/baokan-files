Title: Second Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party Continues
Author:
Date: 1954-03-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 15th: The Second Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party continued on the 13th, according to a report from the Polish News Agency.

    During the morning session, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, H. Minc, delivered a report on the third item on the agenda: "The Main Economic Tasks of the Last Two Years (1954 and 1955) of the Six-Year Plan (1950-1955)".

    In the afternoon session, the delegates began to discuss Minc's report. The speakers in the discussion included: Minister of Foreign Trade Dąbrowski, Minister of Agriculture and Food Industry Hoffman, Secretary of the Stalinogród Provincial Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Gierek, and Chairman of the Presidium of the Warsaw City People's Council Albrecht, among others. In their speeches, they pointed out that the people's democratic system has enabled Poland's economy to achieve unprecedented development. On this basis, the Party has put forward a plan to rapidly improve the welfare of the working people. In the future, the production potential of various sectors of the national economy must be utilized to realize this plan as quickly as possible.

    At the meeting on the 13th, representatives of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the British Communist Party delivered congratulatory messages to the Congress. The chairman of the Congress also read out a congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party. The congratulations from these fraternal parties were warmly welcomed by the delegates to the Congress.

### 波兰统一工人党第二次代表大会继续进行

1954-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党第二次代表大会继续进行
    【新华社十五日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰统一工人党第二次代表大会十三日继续进行。
    在上午会议上，波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员希·明兹就第三项议程“六年计划（一九五零—一九五五年）最后两年（一九五四年和一九五五年）的主要经济任务”作了报告。
    在下午会议上，代表们开始讨论明兹的报告。在讨论中发言的有：对外贸易部部长董布罗夫斯基，农业与食品工业部部长霍夫曼，波兰统一工人党斯大林诺格罗德省委员会书记格雷克，华沙市人民会议主席团主席阿尔布列赫特等。他们在发言中指出人民民主制度使波兰经济得到空前未有的发展。在这个基础上，党提出了迅速提高劳动人民福利的计划。今后必须利用国民经济各部门的生产潜力来尽速实现这个计划。
    在十三日会议上向大会致贺词的有保加利亚共产党和英国共产党的代表。大会主席还宣读了朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的贺电。这些兄弟党的祝贺都受到大会代表们的热烈欢迎。
