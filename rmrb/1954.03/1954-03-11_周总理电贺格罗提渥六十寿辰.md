Title: Zhou Enlai Cables Congratulations to Grotewohl on his Sixtieth Birthday
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1954-03-11
Page: 1

    Zhou Enlai Cables Congratulations to Grotewohl on his Sixtieth Birthday

    Xinhua News Agency, March 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government of China sent a telegram to congratulate Premier Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram reads: Respected Comrade Premier:
    On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, please accept my heartfelt congratulations. I wish you good health and new achievements in the cause of striving to build a unified, independent, democratic, and peace-loving Germany.
                                              Zhou Enlai
                                    March 9, 1954, in Beijing

### 周总理电贺格罗提渥六十寿辰
周恩来
1954-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺格罗提渥六十寿辰
    【新华社十日讯】我国中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理，致电祝贺德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥的六十寿辰。电文称：敬爱的总理同志：
    在您六十寿辰之际，请接受我衷心的祝贺。祝您身体健康，并祝您在争取建立一个统一、独立、民主和爱好和平的德国的事业中获得新的成就。
                                              周恩来
                                    一九五四年三月九日于北京
