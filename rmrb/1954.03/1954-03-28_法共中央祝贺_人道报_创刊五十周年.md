Title: French Communist Party Central Committee Congratulates "L'Humanité" on its Fiftieth Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1954-03-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, March 26th: According to TASS, April 18th is the 50th anniversary of the founding of "L'Humanité," the organ of the French Communist Party. The Central Committee of the French Communist Party issued a statement on this occasion, pointing out that "L'Humanité" is now one of the largest newspapers of the French working class, a newspaper of the people, and the mouthpiece of the French nation.

The statement said: "'L'Humanité' is a constant defender of the demands of the working people in urban and rural areas, an active fighter for democratic freedoms, a fighter for maintaining the independence of our motherland, maintaining international workers' solidarity, and opposing colonial oppression, and also a maintainer of fraternal solidarity among the people of all countries in the struggle to build lasting peace."

The statement emphasized: "L'Humanité" is striving to unite all patriotic forces in France to prevent the ratification of the "European Army" treaty, to thwart the plan for West German rearmament, and to fight for the implementation of an independent French policy of peace.

The Central Committee of the French Communist Party called on party organizations at all levels to strengthen the promotion of "L'Humanité."

### 法共中央祝贺“人道报”创刊五十周年

1954-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    法共中央祝贺“人道报”创刊五十周年
   【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社巴黎二十五日讯：四月十八日是法国共产党机关报“人道报”创刊五十周年纪念日。法国共产党中央委员会为此发表声明，指出“人道报”现在是法国工人阶级最大的报纸之一，是人民的报纸，是法兰西民族的喉舌。
    声明说：“‘人道报’是城乡劳动人民的要求的经常保卫者，是争取民主自由的积极战士，是维护我们祖国的独立、维护国际工人团结和反对殖民压迫的战士，也是各国人民在建立持久和平的斗争中的兄弟团结的维护者。”
    声明着重指出：“人道报”正在争取团结法国一切爱国力量来阻止“欧洲军”条约的批准，来挫败西德重新武装的计划，并且为执行独立的法国和平政策而斗争。
    法国共产党中央委员会号召各级党组织加强“人道报”的推销工作。
