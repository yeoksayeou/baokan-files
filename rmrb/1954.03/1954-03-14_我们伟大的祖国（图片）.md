Author:
Date: 1954-03-14
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In Shijiazhuang, Hebei Province, more than 2,600 handicraft households have gradually organized themselves into handicraft production cooperatives. Due to the establishment of supply and marketing relationships with state-owned enterprises, the production of these cooperatives has greatly improved, and production equipment has also been significantly improved. This is a scene of members of the Shijiazhuang Jicheng Cotton Weaving Production Cooperative working in front of a warping machine.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    河北省石家庄市两千六百多户手工业者，逐步组织了手工业生产合作社。由于和国营企业建立了供销关系，这些合作社的生产有了很大的提高，生产设备也有显著的改善。这是石家庄集成棉织生产合作社的社员在整经机前工作的情形。
