Author:
Date: 1954-03-02

    Our Great Motherland
    The Changshan Islands, located between the Shandong and Liaodong Peninsulas in our country, consist of many islands of varying sizes and serve as the gateway to the Bohai Bay. Now, production cooperatives have been organized on the islands, and the lives of the fishermen are getting better day by day. This is a scene of members of the Dakou Village Fishery Production Cooperative on Tuoji Island of the Changshan Islands,晒新制成的渔网.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    长山列岛在我国山东半岛和辽东半岛之间，有许多大大小小的岛屿，是渤海湾的门户。现在岛上组织了生产合作社，渔民的生活一天天地好起来了。这是长山列岛砣矶岛大口村渔业生产合作社的社员，正在晒新制成的渔网。
