Title: Polish United Workers' Party Congress Opens; Bierut Delivers Report of the Central Committee of the United Workers' Party; Delegations from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Fraternal Parties Attend the Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-03-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 11th—According to the Polish Press Agency, the Second Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party opened in Warsaw on the 10th.

    The delegation from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, headed by Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, as well as delegations and representatives from China, the German Democratic Republic, other People's Democracies of Eastern Europe, and Communist and Workers' Parties of France, Italy, the United Kingdom, Germany, and other countries of Western Europe, attended the Congress.

    At the opening of the Congress, Cyrankiewicz, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, delivered the opening address. All participants stood in silence to mourn the great successor to Lenin's cause, J.V. Stalin. Subsequently, the Congress elected the presidium of the Congress, composed of members and alternate members of the Party Central Political Bureau, including Bierut, secretaries of the Central Secretariat, and others. The heads of the delegations from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and other Communist and Workers' Parties were also invited to join the presidium.

    The Congress adopted the following agenda:
    I. Report of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party – Reporter: B. Bierut.
    II. Report of the Central Auditing Commission – Reporter: S. Rozna.
    III. The main economic tasks for the last two years (1954 and 1955) of the Six-Year Plan (1950-1955) – Reporter: H. Minc.
    IV. The tasks for the development of agriculture in 1954 and 1955 and the necessary measures to increase agricultural production – Reporter: Z. Nowak.
    V. Organizational work and revision of the Party Constitution – Reporter: E. Ochab.
    VI. Election of the Party's highest leading bodies.

    After the adoption of the Congress agenda, Bierut, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, delivered a report on the first item on the agenda.

### 波兰统一工人党代表大会开幕  贝鲁特作统一工人党中央委员会的报告  苏联共产党和各兄弟党代表团参加大会

1954-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党代表大会开幕
    贝鲁特作统一工人党中央委员会的报告
    苏联共产党和各兄弟党代表团参加大会
    【新华社十一日讯】　据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰统一工人党第二次代表大会于十日在华沙开幕。
    以苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联共产党代表团，中国、德意志民主共和国、东欧其他人民民主国家、以及法国、意大利、英国、德国和西欧其他国家的共产党和工人党的代表团和代表，参加了大会。
    大会开始时，由波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员西伦凯维兹致开幕词。与会者全体起立为列宁事业的伟大继承者约·维·斯大林默哀。接着，代表大会选出了以贝鲁特为首的党中央政治局委员和候补委员、中央书记处书记等组成大会主席团。苏联共产党代表团以及其他各国共产党和工人党代表团的团长也被邀参加主席团。
    代表大会通过议程如下：
    一、波兰统一工人党中央委员会报告——报告人：波·贝鲁特。
    二、中央检查委员会报告——报告人：斯·罗兹纳。
    三、六年计划（一九五零—一九五五年）最后两年（一九五四年和一九五五年）的主要经济任务——报告人：希·明兹。
    四、一九五四年和一九五五年发展农业的任务和增加农业生产的必要措施——报告人：泽·诺瓦克。
    五、组织工作和修改党章——报告人：爱·奥哈布。
    六、选举党的最高领导机构。
    大会议程通过后，波兰统一工人党中央委员会主席贝鲁特在会上就第一项议程作了报告。
