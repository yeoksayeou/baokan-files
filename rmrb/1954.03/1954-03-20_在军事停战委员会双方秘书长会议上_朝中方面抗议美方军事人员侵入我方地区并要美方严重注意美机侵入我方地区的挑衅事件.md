Title: At the Meeting of Secretaries of Both Sides of the Military Armistice Commission, Korean-Chinese Side Protests U.S. Military Personnel Infiltrating Our Area and Demands the U.S. Side Pay Serious Attention to Provocative Incidents of U.S. Aircraft Intrusion into Our Area
Author:
Date: 1954-03-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, March 19th: At the meeting of secretaries of both sides of the Military Armistice Commission on March 19th, the Korean-Chinese side protested to the U.S. side regarding the violation of the armistice agreement by two U.S. military personnel who crossed the military demarcation line and entered our area within the demilitarized zone on March 17th. At the same meeting, the Korean-Chinese side also brought ten provocative incidents of U.S. military aircraft recently intruding into our area to the serious attention of the U.S. side. The Korean-Chinese side demanded that the U.S. side take concrete measures to ensure that similar violations of the agreement do not occur again.

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, March 18th: At 10:00 AM on the 17th, two civil police officers of the U.S. side, originally belonging to the Black Watch Regiment of the 1st Commonwealth Division, violated the armistice agreement by crossing the military demarcation line and entering our non-military zone, located 700 meters southeast of Qingtingli, northeast of Kaesong. These two individuals were immediately detained by our side. At 12:00 PM today, personnel from both sides of the Joint Observer Team No. 2 conducted an on-site investigation of this violation of the armistice agreement. During the investigation, the two U.S. civil police officers, Phillips (service number SA5485) and Piles (service number SB19443), both admitted to the fact that they violated the agreement. The opposing team members of the Joint Observer Team No. 2 also acknowledged the aforementioned facts. After the investigation, our side immediately handed these two individuals back to the other side.

### 在军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议上  朝中方面抗议美方军事人员侵入我方地区并要美方严重注意美机侵入我方地区的挑衅事件

1954-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    在军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议上
    朝中方面抗议美方军事人员侵入我方地区并要美方严重注意美机侵入我方地区的挑衅事件
    【新华社开城十九日电】在三月十九日的军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议上，朝中方面就美方两名军事人员于三月十七日违反停战协定、越过军事分界线侵入非军事区内我方地区一事，向美方提出抗议。同一会上，朝中方面并就美方军用飞机最近侵入我方地区进行挑衅事件十起，提起美方的严重注意。朝中方面要求美方采取具体措施，以保证类似的违反协定事件不再发生。
    【新华社开城十八日电】十七日上午十时，美方的二名原属英联邦第一师黑卫团的民政警察在开城东北的青廷里东南七百多公尺处，违反停战协定，越过军事分界线，进入我方非军事区。此二人当即被我方拘留。今天十二时，联合观察小组第二组的双方人员共同对这次违反停战协定的事件进行了现场调查。在调查中，这二名美方民政警察菲利浦斯（军号SA五四八五）和派尔斯（军号SB一九四四三）都承认了他们违反协议的事实。联合观察小组第二组的对方组员也承认了上述事实。调查后，我方即将这二人交还对方。
