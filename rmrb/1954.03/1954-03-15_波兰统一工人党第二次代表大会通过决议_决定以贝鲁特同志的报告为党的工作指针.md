Title: Polish United Workers' Party Second Congress Passes Resolution Deciding to Take Comrade Beirut's Report as a Guide for Party Work
Author:
Date: 1954-03-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 14th: According to the Polish Press Agency, the Second Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party continued its discussion of the Central Committee report on the 12th.

    The Chairman of the Credentials Committee, A. Dolinski, delivered a report at the morning session. He announced that a total of one thousand two hundred and sixty-eight delegates were attending this Congress, and only nine delegates were unable to attend due to unforeseen circumstances.

    Those who spoke at the morning and afternoon sessions in the discussion of the Central Committee report included: Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Konstanty Rokossowski and Władysław Jóźwiak-Witold, Chairman of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions, W. Kłosewicz, Chairman of the Central Council of the Polish Women's League, A. Musiałowa, Chairman of the Central Council of the Polish Writers' Union, L. Kruczkowski, Chairman of the Olsztyn Provincial Council of the Polish Youth Union, G. Skok, and outstanding shock worker and bricklayer, J. Beck, among others. In their speeches, they pointed out the great achievements made by the Polish people in laying the foundation of socialism since the First Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party. They also pointed out the shortcomings existing in the work of the Party and the government, as well as in certain sectors of the national economy, and put forward suggestions for improving the work.

    The Congress unanimously passed a resolution, approving the political line of the Party Central Committee and deciding to take the report made by Comrade Beirut as a guide for the Party's work.

    At the meeting on the 12th, representatives of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Romanian Workers' Party, the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Italian Communist Party, and the Belgian Communist Party delivered congratulatory messages to the Congress, which were warmly welcomed by the attendees.

    The Congress will continue.

### 波兰统一工人党第二次代表大会通过决议  决定以贝鲁特同志的报告为党的工作指针

1954-03-15
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党第二次代表大会通过决议
    决定以贝鲁特同志的报告为党的工作指针
    【新华社十四日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰统一工人党第二次代表大会十二日继续讨论中央委员会报告。
    资格审查委员会主席阿·多林斯基在上午会议上作了报告。他宣布：出席这次代表大会的代表共有一千二百六十八人，只有九位代表因故不能出席。
    在上午和下午会议上讨论中央委员会报告中发言的有：波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员康·罗科索夫斯基和弗·约兹维亚克—维托尔德、波兰工会中央理事会主席维·克沃谢维奇、波兰妇女联盟中央理事会主席阿·穆霞沃娃、波兰作家协会中央理事会主席利·克鲁奇科夫斯基、波兰青年联盟奥尔兹丁省理事会主席根·斯科克和杰出的突击工作者、砌砖工人约·贝克等。他们在发言中指出了自波兰统一工人党第一次代表大会以来波兰人民在奠定社会主义基础的工作中所取得的巨大成就。他们也指出了党和政府以及国民经济某些部门工作中所存在的缺点，并提出了改进工作的意见。
    大会一致通过决议，赞同党中央委员会的政治路线并决定以贝鲁特同志所作的报告作为党的工作指针。
    在十二日会议上向大会致贺词的有捷克斯洛伐克共产党、匈牙利劳动人民党、罗马尼亚工人党、德国统一社会党、意大利共产党和比利时共产党的代表，受到到会者的热烈欢迎。
    大会将继续进行。
