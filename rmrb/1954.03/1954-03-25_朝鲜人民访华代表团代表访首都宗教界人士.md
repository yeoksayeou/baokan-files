Title: Representatives of the Korean People's Delegation to China Visit Religious Circles in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1954-03-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: Representatives of the religious circles within the Korean People's Delegation to China, led by Chairman Kim Eung-gi, visited religious figures in the capital on the 24th and held a symposium. In addition to Chairman Kim Eung-gi, delegation representatives Yoon Bong-jin, Lee Kwang-guk, Lee Yong-gyu, Baek Man-ho, and Lee Won-mo also participated.

Chen Qiyuan, leader of the Religious Affairs Group of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Deputy Leader He Chengxiang, as well as prominent figures from the Buddhist community in the capital such as Ju Zan, Zhou Shujia, Guo Peng, and Christian figures such as Wang Zizhong, Jiang Yizhen, and Zhao Fusan, also attended the symposium.

Chen Qiyuan presided over the symposium. At the meeting, Chen Qiyuan delivered a welcome speech, and He Chengxiang introduced the religious policy of the People's Republic of China and reported on the participation of Chinese religious circles in the patriotic movement and the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea.

Chairman Kim Eung-gi spoke at the meeting. He briefly reported on the active participation of religious followers in various patriotic activities by the Korean people in the war to defend their homeland and resist the American aggressors.

Finally, religious figures from both Korea and China fully exchanged information on the situations in their respective religious circles.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团代表访首都宗教界人士

1954-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团代表访首都宗教界人士
    据新华社讯：朝鲜人民访华代表团中的宗教界人士，二十四日在金应基团长率领下，访问了首都宗教界人士，并举行了座谈会。参加座谈会的除金应基团长外，还有代表团代表尹凤镇、李光国、李龙奎、白万镐、李元懋等。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会宗教事务组组长陈其瑗、副组长何成湘，以及首都佛教界人士巨赞、周叔迦、郭朋和基督教人士王梓仲、蒋翼振、赵复三等，都参加了座谈会。
    座谈会由陈其瑗主持。会上，陈其瑗致欢迎词后，何成湘介绍了中华人民共和国的宗教政策，并报告了中国宗教界参加爱国运动和抗美援朝的情况。
    金应基团长在会上讲话。他扼要地报告了朝鲜人民在保卫祖国、反抗美国侵略者的战争中，教徒们积极参加各种爱国活动的情况。
    最后，朝中两国宗教界人士充分交流了两国宗教界方面的情况。
