Title: Comrade Kasuga Shōichi Assumes Chairmanship of the JCP Central Guidance Department
Author:
Date: 1954-03-28
Page: 4

    Comrade Kasuga Shōichi Assumes Chairmanship of the JCP Central Guidance Department
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 26th] Tokyo Dispatch: The Central Guidance Department of the Japanese Communist Party held a press conference on the 24th to announce that, according to the decision made at the Central Guidance Department meeting on the 22nd, Comrade Kasuga Shōichi has assumed the chairmanship of the Party Central Guidance Department. The JCP Central Guidance Department also announced that the JCP will further dedicate itself to uniting the people of the entire nation to fight against the US rearmament of Japan and the US-Japan "Mutual Defense Assistance Agreement" which enslaves Japan, and to overthrow the Yoshida corrupt and traitorous cabinet.

### 春日正一同志就任日共中央指导部主席

1954-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    春日正一同志就任日共中央指导部主席
    【新华社二十六日讯】东京消息：日本共产党中央指导部于二十四日举行记者招待会宣布：根据二十二日举行的党中央指导部会议的决定，由春日正一同志担任党中央指导部的主席。日共中央指导部并宣布：日共将进一步致力于团结全国人民为反对美国重新武装日本和奴役日本的美日“共同防御援助协定”、为打倒吉田贪污卖国内阁而斗争。
