Author:
Date: 1954-03-11

    Our Great Motherland
    Han Guihua, a female worker at the Tangshan Joint State-Private Huaxin Textile Company, used the Hao Jianxiu work method to create a new national record of watching 1,400 spindles, creating 770 million yuan of wealth for the motherland and completing one and a half years' worth of tasks in one year. This is Han Guihua (right) exchanging experiences with female workers in the workshop.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    唐山公私合营华新纺织公司女工韩桂花，运用了郝建秀工作法，创造了看一千四百纱锭的全国新纪录，给祖国创造了七亿七千万元的财富，一年完成一年半的任务。这是韩桂花（右）和车间女工交流经验的情形。
