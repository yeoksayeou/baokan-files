Title: Government Administration Council Holds Two Hundred and Fourth Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-03-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 27th: The two hundred and fourth meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of January 28th.
    The meeting listened to and approved the "Report on the Basic Situation and Future Work Tasks of the Chinese Academy of Sciences" made by Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the "Report on the Work of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Delegation to the Soviet Union" made by Qian Sanqiang, Head of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Delegation to the Soviet Union.
    The meeting unanimously agreed to the "Report of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the People's Government of Suiyuan Province on Incorporating Suiyuan Province into the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Abolishing the Establishment of Suiyuan Province." This report will be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.
    The meeting adopted the "Instruction of the Central People's Government Administration Council on Strengthening the Forecasting, Warning, and Prevention of Disastrous Weather."
    In addition, the meeting also approved various lists of appointments and removals and lists of appointments and removals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第二零四次会议

1954-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二零四次会议
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百零四次政务会议在一月二十八日下午举行。
    会议听了和批准了中国科学院院长郭沫若所作的“关于中国科学院的基本情况和今后工作任务的报告”和中国科学院访苏代表团团长钱三强所作的“关于中国科学院访苏代表团工作的报告”。
    会议一致同意“内蒙古自治区人民政府和绥远省人民政府关于将绥远省划归内蒙古自治区并撤消绥远省建制的报告”。这个报告将提请中央人民政府委员会批准。
    会议通过了“中央人民政府政务院关于加强灾害性天气的预报、警报和预防工作的指示”。
    此外，会议并通过了各项任免名单和提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免的名单。
