Title: Indonesian Communist Party Congress Adopts New Party Program and Constitution, Elects Central Committee, and Establishes Politburo
Author:
Date: 1954-03-23
Page: 4

    Indonesian Communist Party Congress Adopts New Party Program and Constitution
    Elects Central Committee, and Establishes Politburo
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 22nd] TASS Jakarta dispatch: The Fifth Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party concluded on the 21st. The main slogan of this congress was: Consolidate the national united front on the basis of an unbreakable worker-peasant alliance, and fight for the country's complete independence, for genuine democracy, and for international peace. The congress stipulated the Party's tasks in eliminating the remnants of the colonial system, eliminating poverty, and eliminating foreign capital control.
    After extensive discussions, the congress adopted the new Party Program and Party Constitution. Party representatives from many places in Java, Sumatra, Kalimantan, Sulawesi, and other islands participated in the discussions. The congress elected a Central Committee composed of thirteen members and three alternate Central Committee members. The Central Committee established a Politburo, composed of five members: Aidit, Lukman, Njoto, Sudisman, and Sakirman.
    Aidit was re-elected as General Secretary of the Central Committee, and Lukman and Njoto were elected as Deputy General Secretaries.
    After the closing of the Party's Fifth Congress, a mass rally was held in Jakarta on the 21st.

### 印尼共产党代表大会通过新党纲和新党章  选出党中央委员会，设立了政治局

1954-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼共产党代表大会通过新党纲和新党章
    选出党中央委员会，设立了政治局
   【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社雅加达讯：印度尼西亚共产党第五次代表大会在二十一日闭幕。这次大会的主要口号是：在牢不可破的工农联盟的基础上巩固民族阵线，为国家的完全独立、为真正的民主与国际和平而斗争。大会规定了党在消除殖民制度残余、消除贫困和外国资本控制方面的任务。
    大会经过广泛的讨论，通过了新党纲和新党章。爪哇、苏门答腊、加里曼丹、苏拉威西及其他岛屿上的许多地方的党代表都参加了讨论。大会选出了一个由十三人组成的党中央委员会和三个候补中央委员。党中央委员会设立了政治局，由艾地、鲁克曼、约多、苏蒂斯曼和萨基尔曼五个委员组成。
    艾地再度当选为党中央委员会总书记，鲁克曼和约多二人当选为副总书记。
    在党第五次代表大会闭幕后，二十一日在雅加达举行了一个群众大会。
