Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Third Plenary Session Passes Resolution on Organizational Matters
Author:
Date: 1954-03-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 26th: The Korean Central News Agency reported on the 25th: The Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party issued the following communiqué:

The Third Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party in March 1954 passed the following resolution on organizational matters:

(1) In view of the appointment of Comrades Park Chang-ok and Kim Il, Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, as Deputy Premiers of the Cabinet, it is decided to relieve Comrades Park Chang-ok and Kim Il of their posts as Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party.

(2) It is decided to co-opt Comrade Park Young-bin, Director of the Organization and Guidance Department of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and Comrade Park Kim-chul, Director of the Cadre Department of the Korean Workers' Party, as members of the Political Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party.

According to Xinhua News Agency: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decree on the 23rd: (1) Decided to abolish the Ministry of Urban Management and transfer all its work to the State Construction Committee; (2) Decided to establish the Ministry of Electricity; Appointed Kim Doo-sam as Minister of Electricity, relieving him of his original position as Minister of Heavy Industry; (3) Decided to establish the Ministry of Fisheries; Appointed Zhu Hwang-sup as Minister of Fisheries, relieving him of his original position as Minister of Urban Management.

According to Xinhua News Agency: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decree on March 23rd: (1) Appointed Park Chang-ok as Deputy Premier of the Cabinet and Chairman of the State Planning Committee; (2) Appointed Kim Il as Deputy Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of Agriculture; (3) Appointed Choi Chang-ik, Deputy Premier of the Cabinet, concurrently as Minister of Finance; (4) Appointed Jung Il-ryong, Deputy Premier of the Cabinet, concurrently as Minister of Heavy Industry; (5) Appointed Park Ui-wan, Deputy Premier of the Cabinet, concurrently as Minister of Light Industry; (6) Relieved Jung Joon-taek of his original position as Chairman of the State Planning Committee and appointed him as Minister of Chemical Building Materials Industry; (7) Relieved Yoon Gong-heum of his original position as Minister of Finance and appointed him as Minister of Commerce; (8) Park Moon-gyu will be assigned elsewhere, relieving him of his position as Minister of Agriculture of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; (9) Relieved Lee Joo-yeon of his position as Minister of Commerce of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; (10) Baek Hong-kwon will be assigned elsewhere, relieving him of his position as Minister of Chemical Building Materials Industry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; (11) Lee Jong-ok will be assigned elsewhere, relieving him of his position as Minister of Light Industry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央三月全会通过关于组织问题的决定

1954-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央三月全会通过关于组织问题的决定
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】朝鲜中央通讯社二十五日讯：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会发表公报如下：
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会一九五四年三月全体会议通过了关于组织问题的决定如下：
    （一）鉴于朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴昌玉同志和金一同志已被任命为内阁副首相，决定解除朴昌玉同志和金一同志的朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长的职务。
  （二）决定补选朝鲜劳动党中央委员会组织指导部部长朴永彬同志和朝鲜劳动党干部部部长朴金喆同志为朝鲜劳动党中央政治委员会委员。
    据新华社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会在二十三日发布政令：（一）决定废除都市经营省，原该省的一切工作移交给国家建设委员会；（二）决定成立电气省；任命金斗三为电气相，解除他的重工业相的原职；（三）决定成立水产省；任命朱晃燮为水产相，解除他的都市经营相的原职。
    据新华社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会在三月二十三日发布政令：（一）任命朴昌玉为内阁副首相兼国家计划委员会委员长；（二）任命金一为内阁副首相兼农业相；（三）任命内阁副首相崔昌益兼任财政相；（四）任命内阁副首相郑一龙兼任重工业相；（五）任命内阁副首相朴义琓兼任轻工业相；（六）解除国家计划委员会委员长郑准泽的原职，任命他为化学建筑材料工业相；（七）解除财政相尹公钦的原职，任命他为商业相；（八）朴文圭另有任用，解除其朝鲜民主主义人民共和国农业相的职务；（九）解除李周渊所任朝鲜民主主义人民共和国商业相的职务；（十）白鸿权另有任用，解除其朝鲜民主主义人民共和国化学建筑材料工业相的职务；（十一）李钟玉另有任用，解除其朝鲜民主主义人民共和国轻工业相的职务。
