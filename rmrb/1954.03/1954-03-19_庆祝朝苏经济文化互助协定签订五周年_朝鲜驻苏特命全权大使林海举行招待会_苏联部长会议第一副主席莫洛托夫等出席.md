Title: Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Signing of the Korean-Soviet Economic and Cultural Mutual Assistance Agreement, Korean Ambassador to the Soviet Union Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Lim Hae Holds Reception, Soviet First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Molotov and Others Attend
Author: 
Date: 1954-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 18th: According to TASS, Korean Ambassador to the Soviet Union Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary, Lim Hae, held a reception on March 17th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Soviet-Korean Economic and Cultural Mutual Assistance Agreement.

The Soviet side attending the reception included: First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, V.M. Molotov; Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Minister of Trade of the Soviet Union, A.I. Mikoyan; Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, M.P. Tarasov; Deputy Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Ya.F. Gorkin; Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet, M.A. Yasnov; Director of TASS, N.G. Palgunov; Marshal of the Soviet Union, S.M. Budyonny; Admiral of the Fleet, N.G. Kuznetsov; General of the Army, M.S. Malinin; Marshal of the Signal Corps, I.T. Peresypkin; Admiral, G.I. Levchenko; Commander of the Moscow City Defense, Major General I.S. Kolesnikov; senior officials from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of Foreign Trade, Ministry of Culture, and Ministry of Higher Education; representatives of the masses; workers in science, literature, and the arts; and representatives of the Soviet and foreign press.

Also attending the reception were Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from other countries to the Soviet Union.

At the reception, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Lim Hae, and V.M. Molotov exchanged speeches.

### 庆祝朝苏经济文化互助协定签订五周年  朝鲜驻苏特命全权大使林海举行招待会  苏联部长会议第一副主席莫洛托夫等出席

1954-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝苏经济文化互助协定签订五周年
    朝鲜驻苏特命全权大使林海举行招待会
    苏联部长会议第一副主席莫洛托夫等出席
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：三月十七日，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使林海举行招待会，庆祝苏朝经济文化互助协定签订五周年。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫，苏联部长会议副主席兼苏联贸易部部长阿·伊·米高扬，俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席麦·普·塔拉索夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副秘书亚·费·戈尔金，莫斯科市苏维埃主席米·阿·雅斯诺夫，塔斯社社长尼·格·帕尔古诺夫，苏联元帅谢·米·布琼尼，海军上将纳·格·库兹涅佐夫，陆军大将麦·斯·马利宁，通讯兵元帅伊·特·佩雷塞普金，海军上将格·伊·列夫钦科，莫斯科市城防司令伊·斯·科列斯尼科夫少将，苏联外交部、对外贸易部、文化部和高等教育部的高级官员，群众代表，科学、文学和艺术工作者以及苏联和外国的报界代表。
    出席招待会的还有中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天及其他国家驻苏使节和使馆人员。
    招待会上，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国特命全权大使林海和维·米·莫洛托夫互相致词。
