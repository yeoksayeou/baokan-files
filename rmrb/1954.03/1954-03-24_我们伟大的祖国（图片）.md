Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-03-24
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland: Our country's first domestically produced large passenger ship, the "Minzhong," held its launching ceremony on the 20th of this month. This passenger ship was designed by the joint state-private venture Minsheng Shipping Company and built by the Jiangnan Shipyard. The passenger ship has four decks of cabins, providing nine hundred and seventy-four berths, and can simultaneously carry three hundred and fifty metric tons of cargo.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国我国自制的第一艘巨大客轮“民众号”，已于本月二十日下午举行下水典礼。这艘客轮是由公私合营民生轮船公司设计、江南造船厂建造的。客轮有四层舱房，设有九百七十四个舱位（睡铺），可以同时载运三百五十公吨货物。
