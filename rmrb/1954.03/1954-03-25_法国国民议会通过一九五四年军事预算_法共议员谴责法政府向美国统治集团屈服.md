Title: French National Assembly Passes 1954 Military Budget; French Communist Party Deputies Condemn French Government for Yielding to American Ruling Clique
Author:
Date: 1954-03-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, March 21st: According to TASS, the French National Assembly, after a night of heated debate, passed the 1954 military budget, amounting to 1,110.5 billion francs. 403 deputies voted in favor of the budget, and 203 deputies (including Communist Party, Progressive Republican Union, and Socialist Party deputies) voted against it.

    During the debate, Communist Party deputy Virion criticized the 1954 military budget, pointing out that this budget "will allow the United States to permanently control the French armed forces." Another Communist Party deputy, Shiavonni, said: The government's continued pursuit of the hopeless colonial war in Indochina shows that it has succumbed to the demands of the American ruling clique.

    The budget was also criticized by Socialist Party deputies. The Socialist Party deputies' representative, Kapdevie, said: The budget was drafted based on "a completely unrealistic and dangerous premise."

### 法国国民议会通过一九五四年军事预算  法共议员谴责法政府向美国统治集团屈服

1954-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    法国国民议会通过一九五四年军事预算
    法共议员谴责法政府向美国统治集团屈服
    【新华社二十四日讯】据塔斯社巴黎二十一日讯：法国国民议会经彻夜激辩后，通过数达一万一千一百零五亿法郎的一九五四年军事预算。四百零三个议员投票赞成这个预算，二百零三个议员（包括共产党、进步共和联盟和社会党的议员）投票反对。
    共产党议员维戎在辩论中批评一九五四年的军事预算，并指出这个预算“要使美国永远控制法国武装部队”。另一共产党议员希奥沃尼说：政府继续在印度支那进行没有希望的殖民战争，这就表明了它已经向美国统治集团的要求屈服。
    预算也受到社会党议员的批评。社会党议员的代表加普德维说：预算是根据“一个完全不符合实际的、危险的前提”拟订的。
