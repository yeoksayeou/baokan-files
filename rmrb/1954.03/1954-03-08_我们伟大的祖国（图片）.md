    Our Great Motherland
    Shanghai Textile Industry School is training many technical personnel from worker backgrounds. This is Huang Zhiping, an excellent student at the school (formerly a female worker at the state-owned Shanghai No. 19 Cotton Textile Factory), practicing geometric drawing.
