Title: Mass Meeting in Bombay Opposes US-Pakistan Military Treaty
Author:
Date: 1954-03-05
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 4th. A large-scale mass meeting and demonstration against the US-Pakistan military treaty was held in Bombay on February 28th. The meeting was presided over by S.S. Mirajkar, member of the Central Committee of the Indian Communist Party and Vice-President of the All-India Trade Union Congress. In his speech, he pointed out the threat of the US-Pakistan military treaty to India and called on the Indian people to jointly oppose this war treaty.

### 孟买举行反对美巴军事条约的群众大会

1954-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    孟买举行反对美巴军事条约的群众大会
    【新华社新德里四日电】二月二十八日，孟买举行了一个规模盛大的反对美巴军事条约的群众大会和示威游行。大会由印度共产党中央委员会委员、全印总工会副主席斯·山·密拉志卡尔担任主席。他在致词时指出了美巴军事条约对于印度的威胁，并号召印度人民共同反对这个战争条约。
