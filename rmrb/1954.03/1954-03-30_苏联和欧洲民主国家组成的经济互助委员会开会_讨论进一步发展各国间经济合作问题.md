Title: Meeting of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance of the Soviet Union and European Democratic Countries to Discuss Further Development of Economic Cooperation Between Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-03-30
Page: 4

    Council for Mutual Economic Assistance of the Soviet Union and European Democratic Countries Meets
    Discussing Further Development of Economic Cooperation Between Countries
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th] According to TASS, the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance, composed of the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Hungary, Bulgaria, Albania, and the German Democratic Republic, held a meeting in Moscow on the 26th and 27th.
    The meeting discussed the issue of further developing economic cooperation between the participating countries, in order to develop the peaceful economy, industry, and agriculture of each country, increase the production of daily necessities, and thereby improve the people's living standards. The meeting also discussed the exchange of experience in national economic planning and the further expansion of trade between participating countries and between participating countries and other countries.
    Note: The Council for Mutual Economic Assistance was established in Moscow in January 1949. The initial participants were the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, and Hungary. Later, Albania and the German Democratic Republic also joined. The task of the council is to exchange economic experience, provide technical assistance to each other, and provide mutual assistance in raw materials, food, machinery, and equipment.

### 苏联和欧洲民主国家组成的经济互助委员会开会  讨论进一步发展各国间经济合作问题

1954-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和欧洲民主国家组成的经济互助委员会开会
    讨论进一步发展各国间经济合作问题
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：由苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、德意志民主共和国组成的经济互助委员会二十六日和二十七日在莫斯科举行了会议。
    会议讨论了进一步发展参加委员会各国之间的经济合作的问题，以便发展各国的和平经济、工业和农业，增加日用品的生产，从而提高人民的生活水平。会议还讨论了交换国民经济计划方面的经验、进一步扩大与会各国之间及与会各国和其他国家之间的贸易等问题。
    按：经济互助委员会是一九四九年一月间在莫斯科成立的，开始参加的有苏、波、捷、罗、保、匈六国。后来，阿尔巴尼亚和德意志民主共和国也先后加入。委员会的任务是交换经济方面的经验，彼此进行技术援助，并在原料、粮食、机器设备等方面进行互助。
