Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Uproot Many Enemy Strongholds in Provinces Including Guang'an
Author:
Date: 1954-03-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th: According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 9th, the Vietnamese military and civilians have uprooted many enemy strongholds in Guang'an and Hedong provinces in North Vietnam.
    On the evening of February 26, the Vietnamese People's Armed Forces attacked the enemy in the Dongchao area of Guang'an Province, killing or wounding 65 enemy soldiers in the Dongchao stronghold. They also completely eliminated the enemy strongholds near Dongchao, killing or wounding 60 enemy soldiers, and seizing all of the enemy's weapons and ammunition. On the evening of February 27, the Vietnamese People's Army uprooted the French army stronghold of Guangbei in Zhangmei County, Hedong Province, killing or wounding 37 enemy soldiers and seizing 58 enemy weapons of various types, as well as a large amount of ammunition and military equipment.
    In addition, the People's Armed Forces of Chaofeng County, Guangzhi Province, Central Vietnam, intercepted the enemy forces "sweeping" Chaofeng County on February 22. According to preliminary statistics, 100 enemy soldiers were killed or wounded.

### 越南军民在广安等省拔除敌军据点多处

1954-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    越南军民在广安等省拔除敌军据点多处
    【新华社十日讯】据越南通讯社九日讯：越南军民在北越广安、河东两省拔除敌军据点多处。
    二月二十六日晚上，越南人民武装进攻广安省东潮地区的敌军，杀伤东潮据点敌军六十五名，并全部拔除东潮附近地区的敌军据点，杀伤敌军六十名，缴获敌军的全部武器和弹药。二月二十七日晚上，越南人民军拔除了河东省章美县的法军据点广悲，杀伤敌军三十七名，缴获敌军各种类型的武器五十八件以及大量的弹药和军事装备。
    另外，中越广治省潮丰县的人民武装在二月二十二日截击对潮丰县“扫荡”的敌军，据初步统计，杀伤敌军一百名。
