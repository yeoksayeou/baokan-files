Title: West Berlin Citizens Hold Demonstrations Against "European Defence Community"
Author:
Date: 1954-03-02
Page: 4

    West Berlin Citizens Hold Demonstrations
    Against "European Defence Community"
    Xinhua News Agency: According to the "Neues Deutschland" newspaper, on February 26th, residents of the British sector of Berlin held signs reading: "No European Defence Community!" "Adenauer Get Out!" The demonstrators also distributed leaflets stating: "No European Defence Community, Yes to a Peace Treaty!" Workers and unemployed workers in the Wedding district (French sector of Berlin) also participated in demonstrations against Adenauer's policies and against rising prices.
    In addition, demonstrations were held in the Tempelhof district (American sector of Berlin) against the "European Defence Community," against conscription, and against rising prices.

### 西柏林市民举行示威游行  反对“欧洲防务集团”

1954-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    西柏林市民举行示威游行
    反对“欧洲防务集团”
    据新华社讯：据“新德意志报”报道，二月二十六日，柏林英管区的居民们手中拿着标语牌，上面写着：“不要欧洲防务集团！”“阿登纳滚蛋！”游行者并散发传单，上面写着：“不要欧洲防务集团，要和约！”韦定区（柏林法管区）的工人和失业工人也参加了反对阿登纳政策和反对提高物价的示威游行。
    此外，在泰姆派霍夫区（柏林美管区）也举行了示威游行，反对“欧洲防务集团”，反对征兵和提高物价。
