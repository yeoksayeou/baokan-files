Title: China's Youth Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-03-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 17th: The Chinese youth delegation of six members, led by Liu Daosheng, arrived in Moscow on the afternoon of the 14th, having been invited to participate in the 12th Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union. Saruzhrei, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, and Rommloevsky and Ivanova, Vice Chairmen of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, and others welcomed them at the airport.

When the Chinese youth delegation passed through Irkutsk, Novosibirsk, Sverdlovsk, and other places, they were welcomed by the local Communist Youth League organizations.

### 我国青年代表团到莫斯科

1954-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我国青年代表团到莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科十七日电】应邀参加苏联列宁共产主义青年团第十二次代表大会的我国青年代表团一行六人，在团长刘导生率领下已在十四日下午到达莫斯科。苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记萨鲁日雷，苏联青年反法西斯委员会副主席罗姆洛耶斯基、伊凡诺娃等人到机场欢迎。
    我国青年代表团经过伊尔库次克、新西伯利亚、斯维德洛夫斯克等地时，都受到当地的青年团组织的欢迎。
