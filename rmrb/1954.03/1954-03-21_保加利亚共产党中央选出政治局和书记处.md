Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Elects Politburo and Secretariat
Author:
Date: 1954-03-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, March 16th. According to the "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" Issue No. 10, 1954: The Central Committee elected at the Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party held its first meeting on March 4th. The meeting elected the following comrades to form the Politburo of the Central Committee: Members: V. Chervenkov, G. Damyanov, A. Yugov, G. Zhankov, R. Damyanov, T. Zhivkov, G. Tsankov, E. Staikov, I. Mikhailov; Alternate members: T. Poptokhov, P. Panchevski.
    The meeting also elected the following comrades to form the Secretariat of the Central Committee: T. Zhivkov (First Secretary), G. Ganev and B. Taskov.

### 保加利亚共产党中央选出政治局和书记处

1954-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚共产党中央选出政治局和书记处
    【新华社十六日讯】据“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”一九五四年第十期报道：保加利亚共产党第六次代表大会上选出的中央委员会，在三月四日举行了第一次会议。会议选出了下列同志组成中央委员会政治局：委员：伏·契尔文科夫，格·达米扬诺夫，安·于哥夫，格·詹科夫，拉·达米扬诺夫，托·日夫科夫，格·赞科夫，恩·斯塔伊科夫，伊·米哈伊洛夫；候补委员：托·普托霍夫，彼·潘切夫斯基。
    会议还选出了下列同志组成中央委员会书记处：托·日夫科夫（第一书记），吉·加涅夫和鲍·塔斯科夫。
