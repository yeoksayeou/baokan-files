Title: Syrian President Shishakli Resigns
Author:
Date: 1954-03-01
Page: 4

(TASS Beirut, February 27th) Damascus Radio broadcast the following statement on the 26th at 3 PM local time:

    This morning, at a meeting of the Syrian Parliament, the Speaker of the House of Representatives, Maamun al-Kuzbari, serving as chairman, read a letter of resignation from the former President of Syria, Shishakli.
    Subsequently, Parliamentarian Ishaq took over from Kuzbari in presiding over the meeting and announced that, according to the Syrian Constitution, Speaker Maamun al-Kuzbari is the interim President of the Syrian Republic.
    Kuzbari then spoke, announcing that Shishakli had resigned. According to the Constitution, the Speaker of Parliament will temporarily perform the duties of President of the Republic until a new president is elected by the people within two months after the resignation of the former president.

### 叙利亚总统施舍克里辞职

1954-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚总统施舍克里辞职
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社贝鲁特二十七日讯：大马士革电台在二十六日当地时间下午三时广播了一篇声明如下：
    今天上午，在叙利亚议会的会议上，担任主席的众议院议长马蒙·卡兹贝里宣读了叙利亚前总统施舍克里辞职的信。
    接着，议员伊夏克接替卡兹贝里主持会议，并宣布根据叙利亚宪法，议长马蒙·卡兹贝里为叙利亚共和国临时总统。
    卡兹贝里继起发言。他宣布，施舍克里已经辞职。根据宪法，在前总统辞职后两个月内人民选出新总统之前，由议会议长暂时执行共和国总统的职务。
