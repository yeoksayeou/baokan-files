Title: New Product Trial-Manufactured by State-Owned Shanghai Mining Machinery Factory – Forging Drill Machine Successfully

Author: Zhang Shenming

Date: 1954-03-31
Page: 2

    The state-owned Shanghai Mining Machinery Factory has successfully trial-manufactured a new product: a forging drill machine. The picture shows workers conducting a final inspection of the completed forging drill machine. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Shenming photographed.
