Title: Korean People's Delegation to China Visits North, Northwest, and Southwest China; Warmly Welcomed in Zhangjiakou and Shijiazhuang

Author:
Date: 1954-03-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 25th—The Korean People's Delegation to China and various art troupes, after completing their visit and performances in Beijing, departed from Beijing on the morning of the 25th, splitting into two groups to visit and perform in various parts of the country. The northern route, led by head of the delegation Kim Ung-gi and deputy heads Kim Dal-hyun and Baik Nam-un, accompanied by Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, will visit and perform in North and Northwest China. The southern route, led by deputy heads Lee Dong-yeong and Lee Young-ho, accompanied by Chu Tu-nan, Director of the Literacy Campaign Committee of the Central People's Government, will visit and perform in Southwest China.

More than one hundred people went to the station to see them off, including Liao Cheng-zhi, Chen Yi, Liu Guan-yi, Yu Xin-qing, Yi Li-rong, Nie Zhen, He Ying, and Chai Zemin.

Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and embassy staff also went to the station to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, Zhangjiakou, March 25th—Some members of the Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Kim Ung-gi, and accompanying art troupe members, who are visiting Northwest China, arrived in Zhangjiakou City, Hebei Province, at 6:00 PM on the 25th aboard a special train, accompanied by Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, and Xu Ying, Deputy Director of the General Office of the North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

Zhangjiakou Railway Station was newly decorated. As the delegation's special train slowly entered the station, newly installed wired broadcasting devices broadcast the news of the arrival of the heroic people's envoys to the city's more than 200,000 citizens.

More than 2,000 people went to the station to welcome them, including Lou Ping, Vice Chairman of the Zhangjiakou People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee and Chairman of the Zhangjiakou Branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association; Ge Qi, Second Secretary of the Zhangjiakou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Wang Shaowen, Mayor of the Zhangjiakou Municipal People's Government; representatives of the garrison troops; heads of people's organizations; and representatives from all walks of life.

A grand welcome meeting was held in the station square. Lou Ping delivered a speech on behalf of the people of the city, warmly welcoming the delegation's arrival. Head of the Delegation Kim Ung-gi also spoke at the meeting.

In the evening, the Zhangjiakou People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee and the Zhangjiakou Branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association jointly hosted a banquet to welcome the delegation. The delegation will tour Zhangjiakou City and will be invited to participate in report meetings, forums, and performances.

Xinhua News Agency, Shijiazhuang, March 25th—Some members of the Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Deputy Heads Lee Dong-yeong and Lee Young-ho, and the Korean People's Army Ensemble, arrived in Shijiazhuang City at 6:00 PM on the 25th, accompanied by Chu Tu-nan, Director of the Literacy Campaign Committee of the Central People's Government, and Bai Jian, Secretary-General of the North China Administrative Committee. Lee Bong-gu, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, also accompanied them.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Qi Yiding, Chairman of the Shijiazhuang People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee and Chairman of the Shijiazhuang Branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association; Zhang Pingdong, Vice Chairman of the Shijiazhuang Prefecture Branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association; Dong Dong, Deputy Secretary of the Shijiazhuang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Huang Qingqi, Secretary of the Shijiazhuang Prefectural Committee of the Communist Party of China; Kang Xiumin, Mayor of the Shijiazhuang Municipal People's Government; Cui Minsheng, Deputy Commissioner of Shijiazhuang Prefecture; and local garrison commanders Fu Chongbi, Xie Liang, and Jia Jianlin.

A grand welcome meeting was held in the station square. After Qi Yiding delivered a welcome speech, Deputy Head Lee Young-ho delivered a reply, expressing his heartfelt gratitude and admiration to the people of Shijiazhuang for their warm welcome and contributions to the Resist-America Aid-Korea struggle.

After the meeting, the road from the station to the guesthouse was filled with crowds cheering along the way. People scrambled to shake hands with the distinguished guests. Thousands of people gathered on the road in front of the guesthouse building, continuously cheering the distinguished guests upstairs.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团去华北西北西南访问分别到达张家口和石家庄受到热烈欢迎

1954-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团去华北西北西南访问
    分别到达张家口和石家庄受到热烈欢迎
    【新华社二十五日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团及各艺术团体在北京访问、演出结束后，已在二十五日上午离京，分两路到全国各地进行访问、演出。北路由金应基团长和金达铉、白南云副团长率领，在中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林陪同下，赴华北、西北进行访问、演出；南路由李东英、李永镐副团长率领，在中央人民政府扫除文盲工作委员会主任委员楚图南陪同下，赴西南各地进行访问、演出。
    前往车站欢送的有：廖承志、陈沂、刘贯一、余心清、易礼容、聂真、何英、柴泽民等一百多人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一和使馆人员也到车站欢送。
    【新华社张家口二十五日电】到我国西北各地访问的、以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员和随行艺术团人员，在中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林、中共中央华北局办公厅副主任徐英等陪同下，二十五日下午六时乘专车首先到达河北省张家口市。
    张家口车站装饰一新，当代表团专车徐徐进站的时候，新安设的有线广播器向全市二十多万市民播送了英雄人民的使者光临本市的消息。
    前往车站欢迎的有张家口各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席、张家口市抗美援朝分会主席娄平，中共张家口市委第二书记葛启，张家口市人民政府市长王绍文和驻军代表、人民团体负责人以及各界群众代表共二千多人。
    在车站广场上，举行了隆重的欢迎大会。娄平在会上代表全市人民，热烈欢迎代表团的光临。金应基团长也在会上讲了话。
    晚间，张家口市各界人民代表会议协商委员会和张家口市抗美援朝分会联合设宴欢迎代表团。代表团在张家口市将参观游览并被邀请参加报告会、座谈会，演出等。
    【新华社石家庄二十五日电】以副团长李东英和李永镐为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员和朝鲜人民军协奏团，在中央人民政府扫除文盲工作委员会主任委员楚图南和华北行政委员会秘书长白坚陪同下，于二十五日下午六时到达石家庄市。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆一等秘书李凤九也陪同前来。
    到车站欢迎的有：石家庄市各界人民代表会议协商委员会主席、石家庄市抗美援朝分会主席齐一丁，石家庄专区抗美援朝分会副主席张屏东，中共石家庄市委副书记董东，中共石家庄地委书记黄庆祺，石家庄市人民政府市长康修民，石家庄专区副专员崔民生和当地驻军首长傅崇碧、谢良、贾剑林等多人。
    在车站广场上举行了盛大欢迎会，齐一丁致欢迎词之后，由李永镐副团长致答词，他对石家庄市各界人民对他们的热情的欢迎和在抗美援朝斗争中的贡献表示衷心的感谢和敬佩。
    会后，在由车站到招待所的路上，挤满了夹道欢呼的人群，人们争着和贵宾们握手，在招待所大楼前的马路上聚集着几千群众，向楼上的贵宾们作经久不息的欢呼。
