Title: Hungary Reduces Prices of Some Daily Necessities
Author:
Date: 1954-03-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic has decided to reduce the retail prices of edible oils and meats starting from the 15th. The price reductions cover animal fats, vegetable oils, and margarine in the edible oil category, and all fresh meat, cooked meat, and canned meats in the meat category. This price reduction will benefit the Hungarian people by 500 million forints (Hungarian currency) in one year.

### 匈牙利减低部分日用品价格

1954-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利减低部分日用品价格
    据新华社讯：匈牙利人民共和国部长会议决定自十五日起开始减低食用油类和肉类的零售价格。减价的范围，在食用油类中包括动物油脂、植物油和人造奶油；在肉类中包括一切生肉、熟肉和罐头肉类。这次减价将使匈牙利人民在一年内得到五亿福林（匈币名）的好处。
