Title: Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee Elects Politburo and Secretariat
Author:
Date: 1954-03-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw: According to the Polish Press Agency in Warsaw, the newly elected Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party held its first plenary session on the 17th.

    The plenary session elected the Politburo of the Central Committee. The following were elected as members of the Politburo: Bolesław Bierut, Aleksander Zawadzki, Józef Cyrankiewicz, Hilary Minc, Zenon Nowak, Konstantin Rokossovsky, Edward Ochab, Jakub Berman, Franciszek Mazur, Władysław Jóźwiak, Stanisław Radkiewicz, Władysław Dworakowski, Roman Zambrowski, Adam Rapacki, and Hilary Chełchowski were elected as alternate members.

    The plenary session elected Bolesław Bierut as the First Secretary of the Central Committee. Edward Ochab, Franciszek Mazur, and Władysław Dworakowski were elected as Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee.

    The plenary session also elected the Central Auditing Commission of the Party. Władysław Jóźwiak was elected as Chairman of the Commission.

    In view of Bolesław Bierut being elected as the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and considering that he must concentrate his efforts on Party work, the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, upon Bierut's application, relieved him of his position as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic.

    The State Council appointed Józef Cyrankiewicz as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, Hilary Minc and Zenon Nowak as First Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, and Jakub Berman as Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

    In view of Hilary Minc being appointed as First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Władysław Dworakowski being elected as Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, the State Council relieved them of their positions as Chairman of the State Economic Planning Commission and Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, respectively.

    The Central Auditing Commission of the Polish United Workers' Party also held its first meeting. Stanisław Matuszewski was elected as Chairman of the Commission.

### 波兰统一工人党中央选出政治局和书记处

1954-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党中央选出政治局和书记处
   【新华社二十日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：新选出的波兰统一工人党中央委员会十七日举行了第一次全体会议。
    全体会议选出了中央委员会政治局。当选为政治局委员的有：波·贝鲁特，亚·萨瓦茨基，约·西伦凯维兹，希·明兹，泽·诺瓦克，康·罗科索夫斯基，爱·奥哈布，雅·贝尔曼，弗·马茹尔，弗·约兹维亚克，斯·拉德凯维奇，瓦·德沃拉科夫斯基，罗·萨姆布罗夫斯基，阿·拉帕茨基和希·海乌霍夫斯基当选为候补委员。
    全体会议选出波·贝鲁特为中央委员会第一书记。爱·奥哈布，弗·马茹尔和瓦·德沃拉科夫斯基当选为中央委员会书记处书记。
    全体会议还选出了党中央监察委员会。弗·约兹维亚克当选为该委员会主席。
    鉴于波·贝鲁特被选为波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记并考虑到他必须集中力量从事党的工作，波兰人民共和国国务委员会根据贝鲁特的申请，解除了他的波兰人民共和国部长会议主席的职务。
    国务委员会任命约·西伦凯维兹为波兰人民共和国部长会议主席，希·明兹和泽·诺瓦克为部长会议第一副主席，雅·贝尔曼为部长会议副主席。
    鉴于希·明兹已被任命为部长会议第一副主席和瓦·德沃拉科夫斯基当选为波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记处书记，国务委员会分别解除了他们的国家经济计划委员会主席和部长会议副主席的职务。
    波兰统一工人党中央检查委员会也举行了第一次会议。斯·马杜舍夫斯基被选为委员会主席。
