Title: Capital Women Celebrate "March 8th" International Women's Day
Author:
Date: 1954-03-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 8th—The women of the capital celebrated "March 8th" International Women's Day.

　　On this day, the Working People's Cultural Palace and other public recreational places were open to women for free, and hundreds of thousands of women enjoyed singing, dancing, and sightseeing. In the cultural garden of the Working People's Cultural Palace, female soldiers and female students of the People's Liberation Army were lightly dancing folk dances. More than four thousand women watched the Pingju opera "Women Representatives" at the Labor Theater. The health exhibition in Zhongshan Park attracted many sightseeing women, who carefully listened to the explanations of the commentators. Cultural centers in Xisi, Dongdan, Xidan and other districts held photo exhibitions and art evenings for women. The Cultural Center in Dongsi district also held a model exhibition on the implementation of the Marriage Law and an art evening.

　　Districts, agencies and organizations held commemorative meetings, report meetings or seminars on the 8th. At the commemorative meeting of more than 1,200 female cadres of the North China first-level directly affiliated agencies and troops, Tian Xiujuan, director of the North China Working Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, called on everyone to stand at the forefront of North China's women and strive to realize the general line. Li Baoguang, Deputy Director of the Rural Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, who visited the Soviet Union last year, gave a report on her impressions of her visit to the Soviet Union at the meeting. A forum for female labor models, advanced producers and outstanding family members of workers was held in the auditorium of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions.

　　The Beijing Democratic Women's Federation and the Beijing Daily jointly convened a forum for female labor models in industry and agriculture on the 6th. Participants included labor models from factories, excellent female ticket sellers from tram companies, directors of agricultural production cooperatives in the suburbs, and female members of the Red Star Collective Farm. At the symposium, they unanimously stated that they would strengthen unity, support each other, and work together to realize socialism. Family members of workers at the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant held a forum with local rural women on the 7th, with more than 100 people participating. Members of the Chinese Women's Delegation to the Soviet Union in Beijing also gave reports on their impressions of their visit to the Soviet Union to women from all walks of life before and after the festival.

　　The Beijing Democratic Women's Federation will also hold receptions on the 9th and 10th to entertain female representatives elected in the urban area general election and family members of martyrs and soldiers.

### 首都妇女欢度“三八”国际妇女节

1954-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    首都妇女欢度“三八”国际妇女节
    【新华社八日讯】首都妇女们欢度“三八”国际妇女节。
    这一天，劳动人民文化宫等公共游览场所，都为妇女们免费开放，成千上万的妇女在尽情地歌舞、游览。在劳动人民文化宫的文化园里，人民解放军的女战士和女学生轻快地跳着集体舞。劳动剧场有四千多名妇女在观看“妇女代表”评剧。中山公园的卫生展览会吸引了很多游览的妇女，她们细心地听着讲解员的讲解。西四、东单、西单等区的文化馆为妇女们举办了图片展览会和文艺晚会。东四区的文化馆也举办了贯彻婚姻法模型展览和文艺晚会。
    各区、各机关团体八日分别举行了纪念会、报告会或座谈会。在华北一级直属机关、部队的一千二百多名女干部的纪念会上，中华全国民主妇女联合会华北区工作委员会主任田秀涓号召大家要站在华北妇女的前列，为实现总路线而奋斗。去年曾赴苏联参观的中华全国民主妇女联合会农村工作部副部长李宝光在会上作了关于访苏观感的报告。在北京市总工会的礼堂里，举行了女工劳动模范、先进生产者和优秀的职工家属的座谈会。
    北京市民主妇女联合会和北京日报六日联合召开了工农业女劳动模范座谈会，参加座谈的有工厂里的劳动模范、电车公司优秀的女售票员、郊区农业生产合作社的社长、红星集体农庄的女庄员。在座谈会上，她们一致表示要加强团结，互相支援，为实现社会主义共同努力。石景山钢铁厂的职工家属与当地农村妇女在七日举行了座谈会，参加座谈的有一百多人。在京的中国妇女访苏代表团团员，也在节日前后向各界妇女作了关于访苏观感的报告。
    北京市民主妇女联合会在九、十两日还将举行招待会，分别招待城区普选中当选的女代表和烈属、军属。
