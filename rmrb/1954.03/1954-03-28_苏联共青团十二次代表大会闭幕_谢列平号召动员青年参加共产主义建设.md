Title: Closing of the 12th Congress of the Soviet Komsomol: Shelepin Calls for Mobilization of Youth to Participate in Communist Construction
Author:
Date: 1954-03-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The 12th Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League (Komsomol) of the Soviet Union concluded on the 26th.

    The congress elected, through secret ballot, a Central Committee composed of 150 members and a Central Auditing Commission composed of 31 members. These include advanced workers, agricultural innovators, young cultural workers, and leading cadres of the Komsomol organizations.

    The Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, Shelepin, delivered a closing speech at the meeting. He pointed out: This congress was conducted in an atmosphere of criticism and self-criticism on matters of principle. Its basic spirit is to strive to further improve the Komsomol's communist education work among young people and to mobilize all the strength of young patriots to actively participate in the country's communist construction. He also expressed heartfelt gratitude to the foreign guests for their congratulations to the congress.

    The congress finally adopted a telegram of salute to the Central Committee of the CPSU.

    On the 26th, the foreign guests who delivered congratulatory speeches to the congress included: Djerjinson, representative of the Norwegian Communist Youth League; Manuel Gomez, representative of the Progressive Youth of Chile; Johan Wolf, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Netherlands Youth Union; Schwaber, Chairman of the Free Austrian Youth League, as well as youth representatives from Iran, Venezuela, Israel, Uruguay, Syria, Cuba, and Spain. A congratulatory telegram from the American Labor Youth League was also read at the meeting. These congratulatory speeches and telegrams all spoke of the struggles of the youth of their respective countries for a better future and for world peace, and the fervent desire of millions of young people to strengthen friendship with Soviet youth.
    【Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 26th (TASS)】: The 12th Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union continued its sessions on the 25th. The meeting concluded the discussion on Dumanova's summary report on the work of the Lenin Young Pioneers, and heard and discussed the report on the revision of the League's Constitution.

    A total of twenty-three people spoke during the discussion of Dumanova's report over three days. Those who spoke at the morning session on the 25th talked about various aspects of the Young Pioneers organization work, and they attached great importance to the issue of aesthetic education for children. Speakers pointed out that millions of students in the Soviet Union participate in drama, dance, and chorus groups, as well as other amateur arts activities. Half a million children alone participate in the arts groups organized by workers' clubs and trade union cultural palaces. After the discussion, Dumanova made a concluding speech. She pointed out that the discussion fully demonstrated that the cadres of the Komsomol and the Young Pioneers are filled with enthusiasm for further improving the work of the Young Pioneers.

    Next, V. Kochemasov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, gave a report on the third item on the agenda, concerning the revision of the League's Constitution. Kochemasov said: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has put forward some huge and important tasks to the Komsomol. In order to complete these tasks, the Komsomol should constantly improve its work. Kochemasov then reported to the delegates on certain revisions and supplements made to some articles of the League's Constitution. He pointed out that these revisions and supplements to the League's Constitution are to make it fully suitable for the new tasks facing the Komsomol, but the basic principles of the Constitution have not changed.

    The meeting then began a discussion on Kochemasov's report. Representatives from the youth league organizations of Moscow, Ukraine, Belarus, Uzbekistan, and Turkmenistan spoke during the afternoon session. They unanimously supported the draft amendment to the League's Constitution and put forward some supplementary opinions.

    The congress then passed a resolution approving the League's Constitution, which had been revised and supplemented during the discussion.

    A delegation from the Soviet Armed Forces extended congratulations to the congress at the evening session, and was warmly welcomed by the attendees.

    On the 25th, foreign guests who extended congratulations to the congress included: Yadamzhavyn Süren, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League; Kainulainen, Chairman of the Finnish Democratic Youth League; Blasland, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Australian Youth League; Karlsson, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Swedish Communist Party in charge of youth work; Vildot, General Secretary of the Belgian People's Youth Organization; Vagner, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Danish Communist Youth League, as well as youth representatives from Canada, Brazil, and Greece. Their congratulatory speeches were repeatedly warmly welcomed by the delegates. As of this day, youth representatives from twenty-four countries had extended congratulations to the congress.

    The congress continued on the 26th.

### 苏联共青团十二次代表大会闭幕  谢列平号召动员青年参加共产主义建设

1954-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联共青团十二次代表大会闭幕
    谢列平号召动员青年参加共产主义建设
    据新华社讯：苏联列宁共产主义青年团第十二次代表大会在二十六日闭幕。
    大会以不记名投票方式选出了由一百五十人组成的中央委员会，和由三十一人组成的中央监察委员会。其中有先进的工人、农业革新者、青年文化工作者和共青团组织的领导干部。
    列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记谢列平在会上致闭幕词。他指出：这次代表大会是在对原则性的问题进行批评和自我批评的气氛中进行的。它的基本精神是为进一步改进共青团在青年中所进行的共产主义教育工作，为动员青年爱国者的一切力量来积极参加祖国的共产主义建设而奋斗。他并对外国来宾对大会的祝贺表示衷心的感谢。
    代表大会最后通过了向苏共中央致敬的电文。
    二十六日向大会致贺词的外国来宾有：挪威共产主义青年团代表杰尔琴逊、智利进步青年代表曼纽尔·戈麦斯、荷兰青年联盟中央委员会主席约翰·沃尔夫、自由奥地利青年联盟主席施瓦伯，以及伊朗、委内瑞拉、以色列、乌拉圭、叙利亚、古巴和西班牙等国青年代表。会上还宣读了美国劳动青年联盟的贺电。这些贺词和贺电都谈到了各该国青年为美好的未来、为世界和平而进行的斗争，和千百万青年对加强和苏联青年的友谊的热烈愿望。
   【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联列宁共产主义青年团第十二次代表大会在二十五日继续开会。会议结束了对杜马诺娃关于列宁少年先锋队工作总结报告的讨论，并听取和讨论了关于修改团章的报告。
    三天来在讨论杜马诺娃的报告中发言的共有二十三人。在二十五日上午会议上发言的人谈到了少年先锋队组织工作的各个方面，他们非常重视对儿童进行审美教育的问题。发言者指出，苏联有数百万学生参加了戏剧、舞蹈和合唱小组以及其他业余文艺活动。仅参加工人俱乐部和工会文化宫所组织的文艺小组的就有五十万名儿童。讨论结束后，由杜马诺娃作了总结发言。她指出，讨论情况充分证明，共青团和少年先锋队的干部都充满着进一步改进少年先锋队的工作的热情。
    接着，苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记维·科切马索夫就第三项议程作了关于修改团章的报告。科切马索夫说：苏联共产党中央委员会向共青团提出了一些巨大而重要的任务，为了完成这些任务，共青团应当经常改进自己的工作。科切马索夫接着向代表们报告了对团章的某些条款所作的修改和补充。他指出对团章作这些修改和补充，是为了使它能完全适合共青团所面临的新任务，但团章的基本原则并没有改变。
    会议接着就科切马索夫的报告展开了讨论。在下午会议讨论时发言的有莫斯科、乌克兰、白俄罗斯、乌兹别克和土库曼青年团组织的代表。他们一致拥护修改团章草案，并提出了一些补充意见。
    代表大会接着通过决议，批准了在讨论过程中经过修改和补充的团章。
    苏联武装部队代表团在晚间会议上向代表大会致贺，受到到会者的热烈欢迎。
    二十五日向代表大会致贺的外国来宾有：蒙古革命青年团中央委员会书记雅达姆苏伦、芬兰民主青年联合会主席凯努莱宁、澳大利亚青年联盟中央委员会书记布拉斯兰德、瑞典共产党中央委员会负责青年工作的书记卡尔逊、比利时人民青年组织总书记维尔多特、丹麦共产主义青年团中央委员会书记瓦格纳，以及加拿大、巴西和希腊的青年代表。他们的贺词一再受到与会代表们的热烈欢迎。截至这一天为止，向代表大会致贺的已经有二十四个国家的青年代表。
    代表大会在二十六日继续进行。
