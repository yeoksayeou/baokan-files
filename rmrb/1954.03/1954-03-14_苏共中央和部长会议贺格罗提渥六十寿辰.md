Title: CPSU Central Committee and Council of Ministers Congratulate Grotewohl on His Sixtieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1954-03-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 11th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union sent a telegram to Otto Grotewohl, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Premier of the German Democratic Republic, congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday. The text of the telegram is as follows:

    On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Council of Ministers extend fraternal congratulations to you, one of the outstanding organizers and leaders of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and the working class, and wish you good health.

    We wish you new achievements in the development of the German Democratic Republic—the strong fortress of the peace-loving forces of the German people—in the struggle for the vital interests of the working people of Germany, for the reunification of Germany on the basis of peace and democracy, and for the consolidation of peace and international security.

### 苏共中央和部长会议贺格罗提渥六十寿辰

1954-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央和部长会议贺格罗提渥六十寿辰
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十一日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议致电德国统一社会党中央委员会主席、德意志民主共和国总理奥托·格罗提渥，祝贺他的六十寿辰。电文如下：
    在您六十寿辰的时候，苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议谨向您，德国统一社会党和工人阶级杰出的组织者和领导者之一，致以兄弟般的祝贺，并祝您身体健康。
    我们祝您在德意志民主共和国——德国人民爱好和平力量的坚强堡垒——的发展中，在为德国劳动人民的切身利益而进行的斗争中，为在和平与民主的基础上重新统一德国和为巩固和平与国际安全而进行的斗争中，获得新的成就。
