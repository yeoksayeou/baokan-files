Title: CPC Central Committee Congratulates Indonesian Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-03-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 18th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a congratulatory telegram to the Fifth Congress of the Communist Party of Indonesia, which began on the 15th in Jakarta. The telegram reads as follows:

    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia and to all the delegates of the Fifth National Congress of the Communist Party of Indonesia, comrades:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, on behalf of all members of the Communist Party of China, extends its heartfelt congratulations to the Fifth National Congress of the Communist Party of Indonesia.

    We believe that the convening of the Congress will enable the Communist Party of Indonesia to unite all the people of Indonesia to achieve further success in the great cause of striving for national independence, democratic freedom, and defending world peace.

    We wish the Congress a complete success!

              The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                 March 3, 1954

### 中共中央贺印尼共产党代表大会

1954-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央贺印尼共产党代表大会
    【新华社十八日讯】中国共产党中央委员会电贺于十五日开始在雅加达举行的印度尼西亚共产党第五次代表大会。电文如下：印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会并转印度尼西亚共产党第五次代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会代表中国共产党全体党员向印度尼西亚共产党第五次全国代表大会致以衷心的祝贺。
    我们相信，大会的召开将使印度尼西亚共产党团结全印度尼西亚人民为争取民族独立、民主自由以及保卫世界和平的伟大事业，获得更进一步的成就。
    谨祝大会胜利成功！
              中国共产党中央委员会 
                 一九五四年三月三日
