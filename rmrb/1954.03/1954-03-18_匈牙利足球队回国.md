Title: Hungarian Football Team Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-03-18
Page: 1

    Hungarian Football Team Returns Home

    Xinhua News Agency: The Hungarian National Mixed Football Team left Beijing by plane on the 17th, heading to Hungary via Moscow.

    Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and members of the Central Institute of Physical Education football team and other teams, were at the airport to see them off.

    Hungarian Ambassador Szobek and embassy staff also went to the airport to see them off.

    The Hungarian National Mixed Football Team stayed in our country for more than a month, visiting cities such as Beijing, Shanghai, and Wuhan. They played a total of eleven friendly matches with teams from our Central Institute of Physical Education, "August 1st," China Railway Sports Association, and football teams from Northeast, East China, Central South, and Southwest China. The Hungarian football team also held three exhibition matches. A total of 250,000 spectators attended. Wherever they went, they were warmly welcomed by the broad masses of our people. The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission organized sports workers and football team members to study with the Hungarian team. In the midst of their busy schedule, the team leader, general instructor, coach, and players of the Hungarian football team also held seminars, reports, and training demonstrations for our sports workers and football players.

    The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government held a banquet on the evening of the 16th to bid farewell to all members of the Hungarian National Mixed Football Team.

### 匈牙利足球队回国

1954-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利足球队回国
    据新华社讯：匈牙利国家足球混合队，十七日乘飞机离开北京取道莫斯科返匈牙利。
    到机场欢送的有中央体育运动委员会秘书长荣高棠等和中央体育学院足球队等队的队员。
    匈牙利大使苏贝克和使馆人员也到机场欢送。
    匈牙利国家足球混合队在我国共逗留了一个多月，访问了北京、上海、武汉等城市，和我国中央体育学院、“八一”、中国火车头体育协会以及东北、华东、中南、西南等足球队共进行了十一场友谊比赛。匈牙利足球队还举行了三场表演赛。各地观众共达二十五万人。他们每到一地，都受到我国广大人民的热烈欢迎。中央体育运动委员会组织了体育工作者和足球队队员，随同匈牙利队学习。匈牙利足球队的领队、总指导、教练以及队员们在紧张的比赛中，还为我国体育工作者、足球运动员举行了座谈会、报告会以及训练示范表演。
    中央人民政府体育运动委员会曾在十六日晚举行宴会欢送匈牙利国家足球混合队全体人员。
