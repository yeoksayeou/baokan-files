Title: Fifth Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party Adopts New Party Program and Unanimously Approves the Political Line and Practical Activities of the Party Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-03-20
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 18th, TASS dispatch: The delegates attending the Fifth Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party unanimously adopted the political report of the Central Committee delivered by Aidit, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, and approved the political line and practical activities of the Central Committee.

    The congress passed a resolution calling for the continuation and expansion of the struggle for the complete independence of the country, the establishment of a truly democratic system in Indonesia, and for peace and friendship between nations.

    The congress adopted the new party program of the Communist Party, which put forward the specific task of establishing a genuine national front.

    The party program pointed out that the current task of the Indonesian Communist Party is to liberate the Indonesian people from enslavement and poverty, and to ensure that the people live a free and happy life.

### 印尼共产党第五次代表大会通过新党纲  并一致同意党中央的政治路线和实际活动

1954-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼共产党第五次代表大会通过新党纲
    并一致同意党中央的政治路线和实际活动
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社雅加达十八日讯：参加印度尼西亚共产党第五次代表大会的代表，一致通过了印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会总书记艾地所作的中央委员会的政治报告，并同意中央委员会的政治路线和实际活动。
    大会通过决议，号召继续和扩大争取国家的完全独立、争取在印度尼西亚建立真正民主的制度以及争取国家之间的和平与友谊的斗争。
    大会通过共产党的新党纲，这个党纲提出了建立真正的民族阵线的具体任务。
    党纲指出，印度尼西亚共产党的当前任务是使印度尼西亚人民摆脱奴役和贫困，并保证人民过着自由和幸福的生活。
