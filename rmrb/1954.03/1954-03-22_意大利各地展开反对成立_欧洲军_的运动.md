Title: Movement Against the Establishment of the "European Army" Unfolds Across Italy
Author:
Date: 1954-03-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rome, March 20th (TASS): The Italian Committee for the Defense of Peace issued a statement saying that a movement against the establishment of the "European Army" has unfolded across Italy. The Committee for the Defense of Peace of the Reggio Emilia province called on intellectuals nationwide to participate in a broad movement to expose the hidden threats of the "European Defense Community." Various protest rallies have been held in Genoa, Palermo, Barmia, Macerata, and other cities in Italy. In Montecucco, Pesaro province, ten thousand people participated in a rally against the arming of West Germany.
    On March 19, an inter-provincial conference on the economic consequences of the "European Defense Community," hosted by peace advocates, opened in Milan. Participating in the conference were numerous parliamentarians, economists, trade union leaders, business owners, and merchants from northern Italy. The task of the conference is to discuss all the clauses in the "European Defense Community" treaty that, once adopted, would have adverse effects on the economic life of Italy.

### 意大利各地展开反对成立“欧洲军”的运动

1954-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利各地展开反对成立“欧洲军”的运动
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社罗马二十日讯：意大利拥护和平运动委员会发表声明说，全意大利展开了反对成立“欧洲军”的运动。勒佐—艾米利亚省的拥护和平运动委员会号召全国知识分子参加广泛的运动，揭穿“欧洲防务集团”隐藏的威胁。在热那亚、巴勒摩、巴米亚、马彻拉塔和意大利的其他城市举行了各种抗议集会。在贝沙罗省的蒙特果城，有一万人参加反对武装西德的集会。
    三月十九日，和平拥护者主持的讨论“欧洲防务集团”经济后果的省际代表会议在米兰开幕了。参加代表会议的有许多议员、经济学家、工会领导人、企业主和意大利北部的商人。代表会议的任务是：讨论“欧洲防务集团”条约中一旦被采用就将对意大利经济生活发生不良影响的全部条文。
