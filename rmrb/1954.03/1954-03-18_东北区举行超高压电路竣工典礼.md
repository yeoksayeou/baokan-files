Title: Northeast Region Holds Completion Ceremony for Ultra-High Voltage Circuit
Author:
Date: 1954-03-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fushun, March 16th: The completion ceremony for the Northeast Region's 220,000-volt ultra-high voltage transmission line was held in Fushun City on March 15th. The assembly passed a letter of triumph to Chairman Mao.

Attending leaders such as Xu Daben, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government, Han Guang, Deputy Director of the Northeast Financial and Economic Committee, Soviet experts, worker representatives who participated in the construction, farmer representatives who supported the construction, and guests, all congratulated this great victory in China's electric power industry construction. Xu Daben, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government, said at the meeting: With the selfless assistance of Soviet experts and the enthusiastic support of the people of the whole country, all the staff and workers overcame numerous difficulties and successfully completed this line 65 days ahead of schedule and officially transmitted electricity. This is of great significance for ensuring the development of Northeast China's industry and the country's economic construction. Labor model Qiao Xueliang, representing all the staff and workers participating in the project construction, expressed his determination: Under the brilliant illumination of the Party's general line, we are determined and confident to continue learning from the Soviet Union, build more ultra-high voltage transmission lines, and make greater contributions to the realization of the country's socialist industrialization.

The assembly presented flags, flowers, and engineering commemorative medals to fourteen Soviet experts who helped with the construction; and sent forty-eight letters of thanks to fraternal factories and mines, farmers along the line, and relevant units that supported the project construction. The assembly also rewarded the design, construction, material supply, and iron tower processing units that contributed to the construction, as well as nineteen labor models, sixty-three advanced producers, advanced workers, model and advanced teams that emerged during the construction. Among these award-winning models are Zhu Huanxue, the iron tower squad leader who continuously improved technology and shortened the tower assembly time by three-quarters, Qiao Xueliang, the construction site director who implemented new construction methods and completed tasks ahead of schedule, and Yang Guangqin, the assistant engineer who learned five kinds of advanced Soviet design experience and improved design quality.

This ultra-high voltage transmission line was officially put into operation on January 27th, and its quality has been tested by more than forty days of safe operation. Now, it is continuously transmitting tens of millions of kilowatt-hours of powerful current to industrial cities such as Anshan, Shenyang, Fushun, and Fuxin, to meet the needs of industrial production and construction.

### 东北区举行超高压电路竣工典礼

1954-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    东北区举行超高压电路竣工典礼
      【新华社抚顺十六日电】东北区二十二万伏超高压送电线路的竣工典礼大会，三月十五日在抚顺市举行。大会通过了向毛主席的报捷信。
    到会的中央人民政府燃料工业部副部长徐达本、东北财政经济委员会副主任韩光等首长，苏联专家，参加建设的职工代表，支援建设的农民代表和来宾等，都祝贺我国电力工业建设的这一巨大胜利。中央人民政府燃料工业部副部长徐达本在大会上说：这条线路在苏联专家的无私帮助和全国人民的热忱支援下，全体职工克服了重重困难，已胜利地提前六十五天建成，并正式送电。这对保证东北工业发展和祖国经济建设有着重大的意义。劳动模范乔学亮代表参加工程建设的全体职工表示决心说：在党的总路线的光辉照耀下，我们有决心和信心继续学习苏联，建设更多的超高压送电线路，为实现国家社会主义工业化作出更大的贡献。
    大会向十四位帮助建设的苏联专家献旗、献花、献工程纪念章；对支援工程建设的兄弟厂矿、沿线农民和有关单位发出四十八封感谢信。大会并奖励了建设有功的设计、施工、材料供应、铁塔加工等单位，以及建设中涌现出来的十九个劳动模范、六十三个先进生产者、先进工作者、模范和先进班组。在这些受奖的模范中，有不断改进技术、缩短组塔工时四分之三的铁塔班长朱焕学，实行新施工法、提前完成任务的工地主任乔学亮，学习五种苏联先进设计经验、提高设计质量的助理工程师杨光琴等。
    这条超高压送电线路在一月二十七日已正式送电，它的质量已经过了四十多天安全运行的考验。现在它正把上千万度的强大电流，源源送向鞍山、沈阳、抚顺和阜新等工业城市，满足工业生产和建设的需要。
