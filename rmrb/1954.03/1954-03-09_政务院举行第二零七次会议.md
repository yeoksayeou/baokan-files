Title: The Government Administration Council Holds Its 207th Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1954-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th—The 207th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of March 6th. The meeting heard and approved the "Report on the Current Basic Situation and Future Work Plan of the Textile Industry" made by Chen Weiji, Deputy Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Textile Industry. The meeting approved various lists of appointments and removals and various lists of appointments and removals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第二零七次会议

1954-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二零七次会议
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百零七次政务会议在三月六日下午举行。会议听了和批准了中央人民政府纺织工业部副部长陈维稷所作的“关于纺织工业目前基本情况与今后工作部署的报告”。会议通过了各项任免名单和提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免的各项名单。
