Title: The Supreme Soviet of the USSR to Convene its First Meeting Regularly
Author:
Date: 1954-03-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 26th: TASS reports: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued an order on the 24th regarding the convocation of a meeting of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. The order states: In accordance with Article 55 of the Constitution of the USSR, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has decided to convene the first session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the USSR in Moscow on April 20th.

### 苏联最高苏维埃定期开第一次会议

1954-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃定期开第一次会议
   【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团在二十四日颁布了关于召开苏联最高苏维埃会议的命令。命令说：根据苏联宪法第五十五条，苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定于四月二十日在莫斯科召开第四届苏联最高苏维埃第一次会议。
