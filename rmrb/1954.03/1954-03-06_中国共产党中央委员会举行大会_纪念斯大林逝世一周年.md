Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Holds a Grand Meeting to Commemorate the First Anniversary of Stalin's Death
Author:
Date: 1954-03-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 5th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China held a grand meeting at Huairentang Hall in the capital on the evening of the 5th to commemorate the first anniversary of the death of the great revolutionary teacher Stalin.

Attending the meeting were members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Dong Biwu, Peng Dehuai, and Peng Zhen, members of the CPC Central Committee, senior cadres of the CPC Central Committee organs and the Central People's Government organs, senior commanders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, leading members of the central organs of various democratic parties and people's organizations, prominent figures from the academic, educational, artistic, journalistic, publishing, and religious circles in the capital, industrial and agricultural model workers of Beijing, members of the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and responsible cadres of various departments, members of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC and responsible cadres of various departments, and responsible cadres of the North China Administrative Council and the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

The Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to China, Yudin, personnel from the embassy, and Soviet experts in Beijing also attended the memorial meeting.

Also attending the memorial meeting were envoys and embassy personnel from various people's democratic countries to China.

Those attending the meeting, with deep remembrance of Stalin, entered the venue successively from the entrance where a statue of Stalin surrounded by flowers was placed.

On the rostrum of the memorial meeting hung a giant portrait of Stalin, decorated with flowers and palm trees below the portrait. On the blue curtains on both sides of Stalin's portrait, the silver words "1953" and "1954" were adorned from left to right.

At 7 p.m., the presidium of the meeting, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Dong Biwu, Peng Dehuai, Peng Zhen, and Yudin, stepped onto the rostrum, and all those present applauded warmly.

The memorial meeting was presided over by Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee. In his opening remarks, he spoke of the Chinese people's deep remembrance of Comrade Stalin, and that the Chinese people, like the Soviet people, the people of various people's democratic countries, and the peace-loving people of all countries in the world, had turned grief into strength after Stalin's death, and were striving for the cause of peace and democracy, and for the cause of communism, under the banner of Lenin and Stalin. He proposed that everyone stand up to pay tribute to Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the world communist movement. After all those present stood in silence to pay their respects, Chen Yun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, delivered a speech. At the end of his speech, he shouted: Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people under the name of Stalin! Long live the teachings of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin! Eternal glory to Stalin, the great successor to Lenin's cause! The audience erupted in lasting applause.

At 8:10 p.m., the memorial meeting concluded with the band playing "The Internationale." (Image attached)

### 中国共产党中央委员会举行大会  纪念斯大林逝世一周年

1954-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会举行大会
    纪念斯大林逝世一周年
    【新华社五日讯】中国共产党中央委员会于五日晚在首都怀仁堂举行伟大的革命导师斯大林逝世一周年纪念大会。
    参加大会的有中共中央委员会政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、董必武、彭德怀、彭真，中共中央委员，中共中央机关和中央人民政府机关的高级干部，中国人民解放军的高级指挥员，各民主党派、人民团体中央机关的负责人，首都学术、教育、艺术、新闻、出版、宗教界著名人士，北京市工业和农业劳动模范，中共中央华北局委员和各部门负责干部，中共北京市委员会委员和各部门负责干部，华北行政委员会和北京市人民政府的负责干部。
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻我国特命全权大使尤金和大使馆人员以及在京的苏联专家也参加了纪念大会。
    参加纪念大会的还有各人民民主国家驻我国使节和使馆人员。
    参加大会的人们，怀着对于斯大林的深切的怀念从安放着用鲜花围绕的斯大林塑像的入口处，陆续走进会场。
    纪念会的主席台上，挂着斯大林的巨像，像的下面装饰着鲜花和棕树。在斯大林像两侧的蓝色幕布上，自左至右分别缀有“一九五三”“一九五四”的银色字样。
    七时，大会主席团刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、董必武、彭德怀、彭真、尤金走上主席台，全体到会者热烈鼓掌。
    纪念大会由中共中央书记处书记刘少奇主持。他在开会词中谈到中国人民非常深切地怀念斯大林同志，中国人民和苏联人民，各人民民主国家人民以及全世界爱好和平的各国人民一样，在斯大林逝世后，化悲痛为力量，在列宁斯大林旗帜下，为和平与民主的事业，为共产主义事业而奋斗。他提议全体起立，向世界共产主义运动的伟大领袖和导师斯大林致敬。全体到会者肃立默哀后，中共中央书记处书记陈云讲话。他在讲话结束时高呼：在斯大林名义下的中苏两国人民的牢不可破的友谊万岁！马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的学说万岁！列宁事业的伟大继承者斯大林的英名永垂不朽！全场响起经久不息的掌声。
    八时十分，纪念大会在乐队高奏“国际歌”声中结束。（附图片）
