Title: Hungary's Giant Automated Iron Smelting Furnace Put Into Production
Author:
Date: 1954-03-06
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 3rd: Budapest News: The first blast furnace of the Stalin Metallurgical United Enterprise (in Stalinvaros City), the largest construction project in Hungary's Five-Year Plan (1950-1954), was put into production on February 28th. This blast furnace, designed and built by Soviet experts, has a fully mechanized and automated production process. After the blast furnace is put into production, it will increase the country's pig iron production by 30 percent. The pig iron it produces daily can be made into 140 tractors, 300 combine harvesters, or 400 seeders after being refined into steel.
    A grand celebration was held at the Stalin Metallurgical United Enterprise on the day the blast furnace began production. Rakosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, Nagy, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and other party and government leaders attended the celebration. Soviet Ambassador to Hungary, Kiselyov, was also invited to participate. M. Zofenyicz, Minister of the Hungarian Steel and Machine Manufacturing Industry, spoke at the meeting. He pointed out the important significance of putting this blast furnace into production for strengthening industry and developing agriculture, and thanked the Soviet Union for its friendly assistance in the construction of the blast furnace. He also thanked the blast furnace construction workers.
    Fifty-five construction workers who had made outstanding achievements in the construction of the blast furnace were rewarded by the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic.

### 匈牙利一巨型自动化炼铁炉投入生产

1954-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利一巨型自动化炼铁炉投入生产
    【新华社布拉格三日电】布达佩斯消息：匈牙利五年计划（一九五零—一九五四年）中最大的建设工程斯大林冶金联合企业（在斯大林瓦罗斯城）的第一座高炉，已在二月二十八日投入生产。这座由苏联专家设计建造的高炉，全部生产过程都是机械化和自动化的。高炉投入生产后将使全国铣铁产量增加百分之三十。它每日生产的铣铁在炼成钢后，可以制造一百四十台拖拉机、三百台收割联合机或四百台播种机。
    在高炉开始生产的这一天，斯大林冶金联合企业里举行了盛大的庆祝会。匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记拉科西、部长会议主席纳吉和其他党政领袖，都参加了庆祝会。苏联驻匈牙利大使基谢列夫也应邀参加。匈牙利钢铁和机器制造工业部部长米·若费尼奇在会上讲了话。他指出这座高炉投入生产后对于加强工业和发展农业的重要意义，并感谢苏联在建造高炉工作中的友好帮助。他并向高炉建筑工人致谢。
    五十五位在建造高炉工作中成绩卓著的建筑工作者，得到了匈牙利人民共和国主席团的奖励。
