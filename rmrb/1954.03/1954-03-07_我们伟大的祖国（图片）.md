    Our Great Motherland
    The state-owned Jieyang Sugar Factory is one of the five major state-owned machine-made sugar factories in Guangdong Province. It began mass production at the end of 1951. It can now squeeze more than 800 tons of sugarcane per day, producing 800 bags of sugar per day (100 kg per bag). This is the "Pomegranate Flower Brand" granulated sugar waiting to be shipped.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营揭阳糖厂是广东省五大国营机器制糖工厂之一，于一九五一年年底开始大量生产。现在每天可榨甘蔗八百多吨，日产糖八百包（每包一百斤）。这是待运的“榴花牌”砂糖。
