Title: Capital's Women's Circles Commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day Conference - Saluting the Women's International Democratic Federation
Author:
Date: 1954-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:
    The capital's women's circles commemorating the "March 8th" International Women's Day Conference sent a salute to Madame Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, and Madame Marie-Claude Vaillant-Couturier, Secretary-General. The full text is as follows:

    Dear Madame Eugénie Cotton and Madame Marie-Claude Vaillant-Couturier:

    On the occasion of commemorating "March 8th" International Women's Day, the women of Beijing, the capital of the People's Republic of China, extend to you our cordial respect! We are determined to unite and respond to your call, and under the leadership of the Women's International Democratic Federation, work together with peace-loving people all over the world to build a peaceful world, and to seek a rich, free and happy life for every family in every country.

    The Commemorative Meeting for "March 8th" International Women's Day by Women from All Circles in Beijing, Capital of the People's Republic of China
    March 7, 1954

### 首都各界妇女纪念“三八”国际妇女节大会  向国际民主妇女联合会致敬

1954-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界妇女纪念“三八”国际妇女节大会
    向国际民主妇女联合会致敬
    【新华社讯】首都各界妇女纪念“三八”国际妇女节大会向国际民主妇女联合会主席欧仁妮·戈登夫人、总书记玛丽·克劳德·瓦扬—古久里夫人的致敬电。全文如下：
    敬爱的欧仁妮·戈登夫人、玛丽·克劳德·瓦扬—古久里夫人：
    值此纪念“三八”国际妇女节之际，中华人民共和国首都——北京的妇女们谨向你们致以亲切的敬意！我们决心团结一致响应你们的号召，在国际民主妇女联合会的领导下，与全世界爱好和平的人们一道，为建立一个和平的世界，为谋求每一个国家的每一个家庭的丰富、自由与幸福的生活而努力。
    中华人民共和国首都北京市各界妇女纪念“三八”国际妇女节大会
    一九五四年三月七日
