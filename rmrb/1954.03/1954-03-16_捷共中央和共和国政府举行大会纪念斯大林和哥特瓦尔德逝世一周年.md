Title: Meeting Held by the CPCz Central Committee and the Government of the Republic to Commemorate the First Anniversary of the Death of Stalin and Gottwald
Author:
Date: 1954-03-16
Page: 4

    The CPC Central Committee and the Government of the Republic held a meeting in Prague on the morning of the 14th to commemorate the first anniversary of the death of Stalin and Gottwald. More than a thousand people attended the meeting, including: President of the Republic Zapotocky, Prime Minister Siroky, First Secretary of the CPC Central Committee Novotny, other party and government leaders, heads of various people's organizations, and representatives from all walks of life. Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Filyubin, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia Li Qingquan, and diplomatic envoys of various people's democratic countries to Czechoslovakia also attended the meeting.
    The memorial meeting was held under the slogan "Continue to complete the cause of Comrade Gottwald under the banner of Lenin-Stalin, and strive to build socialism in our country!" Novotny delivered the opening speech. Then, Zapotocky made a memorial report. Combining the current situation in Czechoslovakia, he discussed Stalin's theory and Gottwald's legacy. He also discussed the importance of party unity, strengthening the combination of the party's policies and the masses, enhancing revolutionary vigilance, and learning from the experience of the Soviet Communist Party.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 15th - On the afternoon of the 14th, the first anniversary of the death of the late President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Gottwald, representatives from all walks of life in Czechoslovakia's party, government, and military, led by President Zapotocky, went to Gottwald's tomb to lay wreaths. Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Filyubin led the embassy staff, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia Li Qingquan led the embassy staff, and Deputy Head of the Chinese Commercial Delegation Lin Haiyun led the delegation members to lay wreaths at the tomb. Members of the diplomatic missions of various countries to Czechoslovakia also went to the tomb to lay wreaths and pay their respects.

### 捷共中央和共和国政府举行大会纪念斯大林和哥特瓦尔德逝世一周年

1954-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共中央和共和国政府举行大会纪念斯大林和哥特瓦尔德逝世一周年
    【新华社布拉格十五日电】捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会和共和国政府，十四日上午在布拉格举行斯大林和哥特瓦尔德逝世一周年纪念大会。参加大会的有：共和国总统萨波托斯基、总理西罗基，捷共中央第一书记诺沃提尼，其他党、政首长，以及各人民团体负责人和各界代表等一千多人。苏联驻捷大使费留宾，我国驻捷大使馆临时代办李清泉和各人民民主国家驻捷外交使节也参加了大会。
    纪念大会在“在列宁—斯大林的旗帜下继续完成哥特瓦尔德同志的事业，为在我国建成社会主义而奋斗！”的口号下进行。大会由诺沃提尼致开幕词。接着，萨波托斯基作了纪念报告。他结合捷克斯洛伐克目前的情况，论述了斯大林的学说和哥特瓦尔德的遗训。他还论述了党的团结、加强党的政策与群众的结合、提高革命警惕性和学习苏联共产党的经验的重要性。
    【新华社布拉格十五日电】在捷克斯洛伐克共和国故总统哥特瓦尔德逝世一周年纪念日——十四日下午，以总统萨波托斯基为首的捷克斯洛伐克党、政、军各界代表前往哥特瓦尔德陵墓敬献花圈。苏联驻捷大使费留宾率领大使馆人员，我国驻捷大使馆临时代办李清泉率领大使馆人员，我国商务代表团副团长林海云率领团员前往陵墓献花圈。各国驻捷外交使团人员也前往陵墓献花圈致敬。
