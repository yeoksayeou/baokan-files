Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Deputy Candidates Meet with Voters; Suslov and Shatalin Meet with Voters and Deliver Speeches Respectively
Author:
Date: 1954-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 10th: According to TASS, as the election day for the Soviet Supreme Soviet (March 14th) approaches, Supreme Soviet deputy candidates are meeting with voters in various places.

On the 8th and 9th, deputy candidates Suslov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and Shatalin, met with voters and delivered speeches at voter conferences in Saratov and Alexandrovsk respectively. They explained the rapid growth of the Soviet national economy since the last Supreme Soviet election, pointed out the tasks of the Soviet people in further developing various sectors of the national economy, especially agriculture, light industry, and the food industry, and also talked about the peaceful foreign policy of the Soviet government. They both expressed that they would never betray the trust of the voters in them.

Deputy candidate Kirichenko, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine, also met with voters at a voter conference in Kiev.

First Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union Vasilevsky, and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Kuznetsov, also met with voters in Voronezh and Saratov respectively.

Voters across the Soviet Union have also recently met with many advanced workers, collective farmers, scientists, teachers, writers, and engineers who have been nominated as candidates. In Gorky, the lathe operator A. Melnikov of the Red Sormovo Factory was warmly welcomed at the voter conference. Voters know him as a famous proposer of production rationalization and an active social worker. In Rostov, the voter conference attended by the deputy candidate, the famous Soviet writer Sholokhov, was filled with a warm and harmonious atmosphere. Voters talked to him about their views and opinions on literature, and put forward the urgent demands of the broad readership for new artistic works. In the Ukrainian city of Romny, Ma. Chirchinko, chairman of the "Telman" collective farm, recently met with her voters. Many voters pointed out that she is an experienced organizer of livestock breeding work and has great prestige among collective farmers. They regard her as a loyal daughter of the people and believe that she will do her best to achieve new achievements in agriculture.

According to Xinhua News Agency: The election campaign for the Soviet Supreme Soviet once again shows the great role of Soviet women in national life. Many advanced female workers, female collective farm members, female scientists, female teachers, female doctors, and female artists have been registered as deputy candidates in various electoral districts.

In the Russian Federation, A. Tarasova, an outstanding Soviet theater worker, has been registered as a candidate in Moscow. It is not the first time that voters have given her such an honor. In 1950, Tarasova was elected as a deputy to the Soviet Supreme Soviet. For four years, she has not failed the people's trust. She is very concerned about all the needs of the voters, and personally solves many problems raised by the residents in her constituency. The textile worker and active social activist Liu. Ananyeva has been registered as a candidate in Noginsk City near Moscow. Ananyeva is an advanced worker, and her method of effectively using spinning machines has been promoted among textile workers in China, Poland, Hungary, and Albania. In the last election of the Soviet Supreme Soviet, she was enthusiastically supported by voters and elected as a deputy. Ta. Dmitrieva is a pediatrician. She has worked in Yakusha, Siberia for more than 20 years and enjoys a high reputation. She has become a friend of thousands of Yakusha women because she has helped them raise their children well and healthy. One female deputy candidate is a mountain-dwelling woman, Na. Sebrukhova. She teaches at a middle school in the village of Kamlyuko and is considered one of the best female teachers in the Kabardino-Balkarian Autonomous Republic of the Caucasus. She works hard and is enthusiastic about social activities, and is respected by voters.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人纷纷会见选民  苏斯洛夫和夏塔林分别会见选民发表演说

1954-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人纷纷会见选民
    苏斯洛夫和夏塔林分别会见选民发表演说
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社消息：苏联最高苏维埃选举日（三月十四日）就要到了，这几天来，最高苏维埃代表候选人正在纷纷会见各地的选民。
    在八日和九日两天，代表候选人、苏联共产党中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫和夏塔林，分别在萨拉托夫和亚历山大罗夫斯克的选民大会上和选民会晤并发表了演说。他们说明上届最高苏维埃选举以来苏联国民经济迅速高涨的情况，指出了苏联人民在进一步发展国民经济各部门，特别是农业、轻工业和食品工业方面的任务，还谈到了苏联政府的和平外交政策。他们都表示绝不辜负选民对他们的信任。
    代表候选人、乌克兰共产党中央委员会第一书记基里钦科也在基辅的选民大会上和选民会晤。
    苏联国防部第一副部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基，苏联外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫也分别在沃罗涅兹和萨拉托夫会见了选民。
    苏联各地的选民最近也和许多被提名为候选人的先进工人、集体农民、科学家、教师、作家、工程师见面。在高尔基，红色索尔莫沃工厂的旋工阿·麦尔尼科夫，在选民大会上受到了热烈的欢迎。选民们都知道他是一个著名的生产合理化建议者和积极的社会工作者。在罗斯托夫，代表候选人、苏联著名作家萧洛霍夫出席的选民大会，充满着热烈融洽的空气。选民们向他谈到了他们对文学的看法和意见，并提出了广大读者对于新的艺术作品的迫切要求。在乌克兰的罗姆尼城，“台尔曼”集体农庄主席玛·契尔秦科最近和她的选民们会面时，许多选民都指出她是一个富有经验的饲畜工作的组织者，在集体农民中有很大威信。他们把她看作是人民的忠实女儿，相信她将尽力在农业方面取得新的成就。
    据新华社讯：苏联最高苏维埃的选举运动再次显示了苏联妇女在国家生活中的巨大作用。许多先进女工、集体农庄女庄员、女科学家、女教师、女医生和女艺术家，已被各地选区登记为代表候选人。
    在俄罗斯联邦，苏联杰出的戏剧工作者阿·塔拉索娃，已在莫斯科被登记为候选人。选民已经不是第一次给予她这样的荣誉了。在一九五○年，塔拉索娃就被选为苏联最高苏维埃的代表。四年来她没有辜负人民的信任，她很关心选民们的一切需要，而且亲自解决她的选区内的居民们所提出的许多问题。纺织女工、积极的社会活动家柳·安娜涅娃，已被登记为莫斯科附近的诺金斯克城的候选人。安娜涅娃是先进工人，她所发明的有效使用纺纱机的方法，已在中国、波兰、匈牙利和阿尔巴尼亚的纺织工人中推广。在上届苏联最高苏维埃选举时，她曾获得选民们的热烈支持而当选为代表。塔·德米特里耶娃是一位儿科医生。她在西伯利亚的雅库沙工作了二十多年，享有很高的声誉。她成了上千个雅库沙地方的妇女的朋友，因为她帮助她们把孩子养育得很好而且很健康。有一位妇女代表候选人是生长在山地的妇女纳·塞布茹霍娃。她在卡姆柳科村的一个中学里教书，被认为是高加索的卡巴尔达自治共和国的优秀的女教师之一。她努力工作并热心社会活动，得到了选民们的尊敬。
