Title: Speranskaya Departs Beijing for Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-03-31
Page: 3

　　Speranskaya Departs Beijing for Moscow

　　Speranskaya, a representative of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union, who was invited to Beijing to attend the opening ceremony of the new site of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital, left Beijing for Moscow on March 25. Those who went to the station to see her off included Hu Lansheng, Vice President and Secretary-General of the Red Cross Society of China, Lin Shixiao, Deputy Secretary-General, Ni Feijun, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Zhang Zhen, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Bazhenov, President of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital, Zhu Zhongli, Vice President, and many Soviet experts from the hospital. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 斯拜朗斯卡雅离京返莫斯科

1954-03-31
第3版()
专栏：

    斯拜朗斯卡雅离京返莫斯科
    应邀前来北京参加北京苏联红十字医院新院址开幕典礼的苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会代表斯拜朗斯卡雅于三月二十五日离京返莫斯科。前往车站欢送的有中国红十字会总会副会长兼秘书长胡兰生，副秘书长林士笑、倪斐君，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中苏友好协会总会联络部副主任张震，北京苏联红十字医院院长巴任诺夫、副院长朱仲丽和该院苏联专家多人。（新华社）
