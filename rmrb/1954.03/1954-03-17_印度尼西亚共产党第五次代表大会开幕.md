Title: Fifth Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party Opens
Author:
Date: 1954-03-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta: The Fifth Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party opened in Jakarta on the 15th, according to a TASS dispatch. The General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, D.N. Aidit, delivered a political report on behalf of the Central Committee at the Congress.

The Congress will adopt the Party Program and the Party Constitution.

The Congress received many congratulatory telegrams and letters from domestic organizations and fraternal parties abroad.

### 印度尼西亚共产党第五次代表大会开幕

1954-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党第五次代表大会开幕
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社雅加达讯：印度尼西亚共产党第五次代表大会十五日在雅加达开幕。印尼共产党中央委员会总书记艾地在代表大会上代表中央委员会作了政治报告。
    代表大会将通过党纲和党章。
    大会收到了许多国内团体和国外兄弟党的贺电和贺信。
