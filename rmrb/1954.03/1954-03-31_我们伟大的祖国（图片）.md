Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-03-31
Page: 1

Our great motherland, New China, is a place where people of all ethnic groups live happy lives, and joyful songs can be heard everywhere on the frontier. This is a scene of Tajik people on the Pamir Plateau performing a horse-riding dance.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国新中国各族人民都过着愉快的生活，边疆上到处可以听到欢乐的歌声。这是帕米尔高原上的塔吉克族人民跳骑马舞的场面。
