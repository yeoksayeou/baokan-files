Title: Polish President Appoints a Batch of Military Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-09-05
Edition: 3

Polish President and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces Jaruzelski appointed, on September 4, a number of military leaders based on the recommendation of the Minister of Defense. Shumski was appointed as the First Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Army, Pukhava as the Deputy Chief of the General Staff in charge of operational matters, Kulyata as the Chief of the General Logistics Department of the People's Army, Bovochuk as the Director of the Military Training Department of the People's Army, Zalevski as the Commander of the Coastal Military District, Viletski as the Commander of the Silesian Military District, Stelmashuk as the Commander of the Warsaw Military District, and Gotovava as the Commander of the Air Force.

### 波总统任命一批军队领导人

1989-09-05
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    波总统任命一批军队领导人
    据新华社华沙9月4日电　波兰总统兼武装部队统帅雅鲁泽尔斯基4日根据国防部长建议，任命舒姆斯基中将为人民军第一副总参谋长，普哈瓦少将为负责作战事务的副总参谋长，库利亚塔中将为人民军总后勤部主任，博沃丘赫少将为人民军军事训练部部长，扎莱夫斯基少将为人民军沿海军区司令，维莱茨基少将为西里西亚军区司令，斯泰尔马舒克中将为华沙军区司令，戈托瓦瓦少将为空军司令。
