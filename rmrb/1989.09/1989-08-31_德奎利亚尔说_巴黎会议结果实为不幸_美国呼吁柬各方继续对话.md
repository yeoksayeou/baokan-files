Title: De Cuellar Says Paris Conference Results Are Unfortunate, U.S. Urges Cambodian Parties to Continue Dialogue
Author:
Date: 1989-08-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, August 30th.

United Nations Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar said in a telegram today that the results of the Paris International Conference on Cambodia are "unfortunate" for Cambodia, especially for the Cambodian people.

In a telegram sent to the Paris International Conference, which concluded this afternoon, de Cuéllar said that after a month of intense work, it is now time to evaluate the results of the conference. He said, "One fact is clear: although this ship had hopes of reaching the shores of peace, it ultimately did not arrive. We did not achieve the comprehensive agreement to resolve the issue that we had originally hoped to sign."

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, August 30th.

U.S. State Department spokesman Richard Boucher said today that the Paris International Conference on Cambodia did not produce a comprehensive solution, and the United States hopes that the various political forces in Cambodia will continue to engage in dialogue rather than military confrontation.

Boucher made these remarks after the Paris conference adjourned.

Boucher also said that the United States will continue to support Prince Sihanouk.

### 德奎利亚尔说  巴黎会议结果实为不幸  美国呼吁柬各方继续对话

1989-08-31
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    德奎利亚尔说
    巴黎会议结果实为不幸
    美国呼吁柬各方继续对话
    新华社巴黎8月30日电  联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天在一份电报中说，柬埔寨问题巴黎国际会议的结果对柬埔寨，特别是对柬埔寨人民来说，“实为不幸”。
    在发给今天下午结束的巴黎国际会议的电报中，德奎利亚尔说，经过一个月的紧张工作，现在该评价会议的结果了。他说：“有一事实很明显，即尽管这艘航船时有希望到达和平的彼岸，但它最终并没有到达。我们没有达成原先希望能签署的一项全面解决问题的协议。”
    新华社华盛顿8月30日电美国国务院发言人塔特怀勒今天说，关于柬埔寨问题的巴黎国际会议没有产生全面的解决方案，美国希望柬埔寨各派政治力量继续以对话取代军事对抗。
    塔特怀勒是在巴黎会议休会之后说这番话的。
    塔特怀勒还说，美国将继续支持西哈努克亲王。
