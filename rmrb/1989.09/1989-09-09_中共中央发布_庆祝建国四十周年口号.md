Title: Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Issues Slogans to Celebrate the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation
Author:
Date: 1989-09-09
Edition: 1

	The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has issued slogans to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	1.	Celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China with great enthusiasm!

	2.	Celebrate the great achievements of China's socialist modernization and reform!

	3.	Celebrate the victory in stopping the turmoil and suppressing the counter-revolutionary riot!

	4.	Salute to all workers, peasants, intellectuals, and patriotic figures from all walks of life across the country!

	5.	Salute to the People's Liberation Army, public security officers, and armed police forces who have made outstanding contributions in defending the People's Republic of China!

	6.	Unite the entire Party and the people of the nation to strive for the construction of socialism with Chinese characteristics!

	7.	Firmly implement the line, principles, and policies of the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee! Firmly implement the basic line of the Party in the primary stage of socialism!

	8.	Closely unite around the Party Central Committee and strive for the tasks proposed by the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee!

	9.	Uphold the Four Cardinal Principles and resolutely oppose bourgeois liberalization!

	10.	Persist in reform and opening up, and strive to complete the tasks of rectifying the economic environment, improving economic order, and deepening comprehensive reform!

	11.	Concentrate on developing productive forces to strive for the strategic goals of the national economy!

	12.	Be self-reliant, work hard, increase production, practice economy, and build the country with thrift!

	13.	Strengthen the construction of socialist spiritual civilization and become a socialist citizen with ideals, morality, culture, and discipline!

	14.	Develop education, revitalize science and technology, and flourish culture!

	15.	Implement the policy of letting a hundred flowers bloom and a hundred schools of thought contend!

	16.	Strengthen the construction of socialist democracy and legal system, and consolidate and develop a stable and united political situation!

	17.	Uphold integrity and punish corruption by law to establish a good social atmosphere!

	18.	Strengthen the construction of the Party, carry forward the Party's fine traditions and style, and serve the people wholeheartedly!

	19.	Strengthen and improve ideological and political work, and strive to carry out patriotic and socialist education!

	20.	Rely on the working class wholeheartedly, consolidate and develop the alliance between workers and peasants!

	21.	Respect knowledge and respect talents!

	22.	Youth should study hard and be the backbone of socialist modernization!

	23.	Protect the legitimate rights and interests of women and children, and give full play to the role of women!

	24.	Implement family planning, control population growth, and improve the quality of the population!

	25.	Protect and improve the ecological environment, benefit the people, and benefit future generations!

	26.	Strengthen the construction of the people's army to be revolutionary, modern, and regular!

	27.	Uphold ethnic equality, strengthen ethnic unity, and promote the common prosperity of all ethnic groups!

	28.	Strive to develop the broadest patriotic united front!

	29.	Promote the peaceful reunification of the motherland according to the concept of "one country, two systems"!

	30.	Welcome compatriots from Taiwan, Hong Kong, Macao, and overseas Chinese to strive together for the reunification of the motherland and the rejuvenation of China!

	31.	Expand opening up to the outside world, and develop economic, technological, scientific, and cultural exchanges and cooperation with other countries!

	32.	Persist in an independent foreign policy of peace, and actively develop friendly and cooperative relations with all countries in the world!

	33.	Uphold national sovereignty and national dignity, and resolutely oppose any country or organization interfering in China's internal affairs!

	34.	Promote world peace and development, and strive to establish a new international political and economic order!

	35.	Strive to build China into a prosperous, democratic, and civilized socialist modern country!

	36.	Long live the great People's Republic of China!

	37.	Long live the great Communist Party of China!

	38.	Long live the great Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought!

	39.	Long live the great unity of all ethnic groups in China!

	40.	Long live the great unity of all peoples of the world!

### 中共中央发布  庆祝建国四十周年口号

1989-09-09
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    中共中央发布
    庆祝建国四十周年口号
    新华社北京9月9日电　中共中央发布庆祝中华人民共和国成立四十周年口号
    一、热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立四十周年！
    二、热烈欢呼我国社会主义现代化建设和改革的伟大成就！
    三、热烈欢呼制止动乱、平息反革命暴乱的胜利！
    四、向全国工人、农民、知识分子和各界爱国人士致敬！
    五、向在保卫人民共和国的斗争中立下卓越功勋的中国人民解放军、公安干警和武警部队致敬！
    六、全党全国人民团结起来，为建设具有中国特色的社会主义而奋斗！
    七、坚定不移地贯彻执行党的十一届三中全会以来的路线、方针、政策！坚定不移地贯彻执行党在社会主义初级阶段的基本路线！
    八、紧密地团结在党中央周围，为实现党的十三届四中全会提出的各项任务而奋斗！
    九、坚持四项基本原则，旗帜鲜明地反对资产阶级自由化！
    十、坚持改革开放，努力完成治理经济环境、整顿经济秩序和全面深化改革的任务！
    十一、集中力量发展社会生产力，为实现国民经济的战略目标而奋斗！
    十二、自力更生，艰苦奋斗，增产节约，勤俭建国！
    十三、加强社会主义精神文明建设，做有理想、有道德、有文化、有纪律的社会主义公民！
    十四、发展教育，振兴科技，繁荣文化！
    十五、贯彻百花齐放、百家争鸣的方针！
    十六、加强社会主义民主和法制建设，巩固发展安定团结的政治局面！
    十七、坚持廉洁奉公，依法惩治腐败，树立良好的社会风气！
    十八、加强党的建设，发扬党的优良传统和作风，全心全意为人民服务！
    十九、加强和改进思想政治工作，努力开展爱国主义和社会主义教育！
    二十、全心全意依靠工人阶级，巩固和发展工农联盟！
    二十一、尊重知识，尊重人才！
    二十二、青年要努力学习，做社会主义现代化建设的生力军！
    二十三、维护妇女儿童的合法权益，发挥妇女的积极作用！
    二十四、实行计划生育，控制人口增长，提高人口素质！
    二十五、保护和改善生态环境，造福人民，造福后代！
    二十六、加强人民军队的革命化、现代化、正规化建设！
    二十七、坚持民族平等，加强民族团结，促进各民族共同繁荣！
    二十八、努力发展最广泛的爱国统一战线！
    二十九、按照“一国两制”构想，促进祖国和平统一！
    三十、欢迎台湾同胞、港澳同胞和海外侨胞为统一祖国、振兴中华而共同奋斗！
    三十一、扩大对外开放，发展对外经济技术、科学文化的交流和合作！
    三十二、坚持独立自主的和平外交政策，积极发展同世界各国的友好合作关系！
    三十三、维护国家主权和民族尊严，坚决反对任何国家和组织对我国内政的干涉！
    三十四、促进世界的和平与发展，为建立国际政治经济新秩序而努力！
    三十五、为把我国建设成为富强、民主、文明的社会主义现代化国家而奋斗！
    三十六、伟大的中华人民共和国万岁！
    三十七、伟大的中国共产党万岁！
    三十八、伟大的马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想万岁！
    三十九、全国各族人民大团结万岁！
    四十、全世界人民大团结万岁！
