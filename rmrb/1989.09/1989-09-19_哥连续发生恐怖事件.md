Title: Colombia Sees Series of Terrorist Incidents
Author:
Date: 1989-09-19
Page: 3

Colombia's drug cartels carried out a series of bombings and assassinations on September 17th, in retaliation for the government's month-long large-scale anti-drug campaign.

In the capital, Bogota, three bomb explosions occurred on the same day, causing severe damage to three banks and injuring one security guard. Additionally, the drug cartels shot and killed a journalist who advocated for anti-drug efforts and launched a homemade rocket at the U.S. Embassy in Colombia.

### 哥连续发生恐怖事件

1989-09-19
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　哥连续发生恐怖事件
    哥伦比亚贩毒集团十七日接二连三制造炸弹爆炸和暗杀等恐怖事件，以报复政府一个月来开展的大规模扫毒斗争。
    在首都波哥大十七日连续发生三起炸弹爆炸事件，有三家银行受到严重损害，一名保安人员受伤。此外，贩毒集团还枪杀了一名主张扫毒的记者并向美国驻哥伦比亚使馆发射了一枚土制火箭。
