Title: De Soto to Oversee Central American Peace Process on Behalf of De Cuellar
Author:
Date: 1989-09-04
Edition: 3

United Nations advance team to arrive in Nicaragua this weekend

NEW YORK, Sept. 1 (Xinhua) -- United Nations Secretary-General Javier Perez de Cuellar today appointed Alvaro de Soto as his representative to oversee the Central American peace process.

De Soto, an Assistant Secretary-General, serves as Perez de Cuellar's senior advisor on Central American affairs. At a news conference, he said that coordinating the efforts of various international organizations to monitor the Central American peace process is a daunting and complex task.

De Soto said that the demobilization of Nicaraguan anti-government forces should be carried out on a "voluntary" basis.

He said that the United Nations cannot disarm the Nicaraguan anti-government forces.

He also said that at the request of the five Central American heads of state, an international verification group will be established to contact the Nicaraguan anti-government forces to determine their intentions. A military body under it will fully supervise the progress of the demobilization work.

An "advance team" appointed by the UN Secretary-General will depart for Nicaragua this weekend to prepare for the establishment of a UN observer group to verify compliance with security principles.

### 德索托代表德奎利亚尔监督中美洲的和平进程  联合国先遣队将于周末抵尼

1989-09-04
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    德索托代表德奎利亚尔监督中美洲的和平进程
    联合国先遣队将于周末抵尼
    新华社联合国9月1日电  联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天任命阿尔瓦罗·德索托作为他的代表监督中美洲和平进程。
    德索托是联合国助理秘书长，担任佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔的中美洲问题高级顾问。他在新闻发布会上说，他面临的协调各国国际组织监督中美洲和平进程的努力是一项艰巨而复杂的任务。
    德索托说，遣散尼加拉瓜反政府武装应贯彻“自愿”的原则。
    他说，联合国无法对尼反政府武装缴械。
    他还说，应中美洲五国首脑的要求，将成立一个国际核查小组同尼加拉瓜反政府武装接触，以确定他们的意愿。它下属的一个军事机构将全面监督遣散工作的进程。
    由联合国秘书长指派的一个“先遣队”将于本周末前往尼加拉瓜，筹备建立一个联合国观察组，以核查遵守安全原则的情况。
