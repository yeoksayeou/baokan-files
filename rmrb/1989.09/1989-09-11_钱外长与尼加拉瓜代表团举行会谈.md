Title: Chinese Foreign Minister Meets with Nicaraguan Delegation
Author: He Chongyuan
Date: 1989-09-11
Edition: 2

Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen held talks with a delegation from Nicaragua led by Ruiz, a member of the National Directorate of the Sandinista National Liberation Front and Minister of External Cooperation, in a sincere and friendly atmosphere at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse this morning.

Foreign Minister Qian pointed out that the Chinese people's resolve to follow the socialist path, safeguard national sovereignty and dignity is unshakable and will not yield to external pressure.

In discussing the current international situation, Foreign Minister Qian introduced China's principles of foreign policy, stating that China will continue to develop friendly and cooperative relations with all countries on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

Minister Ruiz gave a detailed account of Nicaragua's domestic situation and foreign policy to Foreign Minister Qian. He emphasized that Nicaragua's development direction is socialism, but currently, it is implementing the principles of non-alignment, pluralistic politics, and a mixed economy. Nicaragua is willing to develop relations with all countries, especially neighboring countries. When discussing the Taiwan issue, Minister Ruiz stated that Nicaragua acknowledges that Taiwan is an inseparable part of Chinese territory, and this principle will not change.

Foreign Minister Qian expressed that China supports Nicaragua's struggle to maintain national independence and sovereignty and to develop its national economy. He also expressed admiration for the achievements of the Nicaraguan people. After the talks, Foreign Minister Qian hosted a banquet for Minister Ruiz and his entourage.

This afternoon, Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin held talks with the Nicaraguan delegation on bilateral economic and trade relations.

### 钱外长与尼加拉瓜代表团举行会谈
何崇元
1989-09-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　钱外长与尼加拉瓜代表团举行会谈
    本报北京9月11日电　记者何崇元报道：钱其琛外长今天上午在钓鱼台国宾馆与尼加拉瓜桑地诺民族解放阵线全国领导委员会成员、对外合作部长鲁伊斯在诚挚友好的气氛中举行了会谈。
    钱外长指出，中国人民坚决走社会主义道路、维护国家主权和尊严的决心是坚定不移的，决不会屈服于外来压力。
    在谈到当前对国际形势的看法时，钱外长介绍了我国对外政策的原则，指出中国将在和平共处五项原则的基础上，继续发展同世界各国的友好合作关系。
    鲁伊斯部长向钱外长详细介绍了尼加拉瓜的国内形势和对外政策。他强调说，尼加拉瓜的发展方向是社会主义，但目前实行的是不结盟、多元政治和混合经济三项原则。尼加拉瓜愿意发展同世界各国，特别是周围邻国的关系。在谈到台湾问题时，鲁伊斯部长表示，尼加拉瓜承认台湾是中国领土不可分割的一部分，这个原则立场决不会改变。
    钱外长表示，中国支持尼加拉瓜为维护国家独立和主权、发展民族经济进行的斗争，对尼加拉瓜人民取得的成就表示钦佩。会谈结束后，钱外长宴请了鲁伊斯部长及其随行人员。
    今天下午，对外经济贸易部部长郑拓彬同尼加拉瓜代表团就双边经贸关系举行了会谈。
