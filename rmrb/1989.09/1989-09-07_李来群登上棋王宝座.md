Date: 1989-09-07
Edition: 3

Li Laiqun Ascends to the Throne of the Chess King

BEIJING, Sept. 7 (Xinhua) — Li Laiqun today secured victory in the fifth round of the second Chinese Chess King Tournament, defeating the previous champion Hu Ronghua with a record of four wins and one loss, thus becoming the new Chess King.

According to experts who watched this year's Chess King Tournament, the current Chinese chess scene is led by the new generation of young players represented by Li Laiqun, Lü Qin, and Zhao Guorong. The veteran champion Hu Ronghua has also expressed his hope that more young players will surpass him.

### 李来群登上棋王宝座
陈昭
1989-09-07
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    李来群登上棋王宝座
    本报北京9月7日讯　记者陈昭报道：李来群今日在第二届中国象棋棋王战的第五轮角逐中，再度告捷，从而以四胜一负的成绩击败上届棋王胡荣华，成为新一届棋王。
    据观看了本届棋王赛的有关权威人士称，目前以李来群、吕钦、赵国荣三位年轻选手为代表，领中国象棋棋坛新一代之风骚。老冠军胡荣华也多次表示，希望更多的年轻棋手超过他。
