United Nations Military Observer Group Arrives in Honduras to Supervise the Disbandment of Nicaraguan Anti-Government Forces

	Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, September 10 – Tegucigalpa: A United Nations military observer group arrived in Honduras on September 10 to discuss the establishment of a peacekeeping force to advance the peace process in Central America.

	The military observer group, led by Brazilian General Ferreira and UN Secretary-General Advisor Wandeler, arrived in Honduras at the request of the five Central American countries. Their primary mission is to verify and supervise the demobilization of Nicaraguan anti-government forces within Honduras, in accordance with the agreements reached at the 5th Summit of Central American Heads of State. The military observer group will also engage with the Nicaraguan anti-government forces to determine their intentions.

	The demobilization of Nicaraguan anti-government forces was stipulated in the document signed by the five Central American heads of state in Tegucigalpa on August 7.

### 联合国军事观察组抵洪　监督遣散尼反政府武装

1989-09-11
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    联合国军事观察组抵洪
　　监督遣散尼反政府武装
    新华社墨西哥城9月10日电　特古西加尔巴消息：联合国军事观察员小组10日抵达洪都拉斯，以探讨成立一支和平部队来推进中美洲和平进程。
    由巴西将军费雷拉和联合国秘书长顾问旺德雷尔率领的军事观察员小组是应中美洲5国政府要求前来洪都拉斯的。他们的主要任务是根据第5次中美洲首脑会议达成的协议，对遣散洪都拉斯境内的尼加拉瓜反政府武装进行核查和监督。军事观察员小组还将同尼加拉瓜反政府武装进行接触，以确定他们的意愿。
    遣散尼加拉瓜反政府武装是中美洲5国首脑8月7日在洪都拉斯特拉签署的文件中规定的。
