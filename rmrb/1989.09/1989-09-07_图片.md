Title: Public Security Forces in Nanjing Border Inspection Station Uphold Strict Discipline and Courteous Service
Author: Lǐ Déhé
Date: 1989-09-07
Page: 5

    The Nanjing Border Inspection Station of the Jiangsu Provincial Public Security Forces adheres to strict discipline and courteous service. To date, they have supervised 23,000 flights, inspected 1.4 million passengers, and checked 2.7 million pieces of luggage. They have discovered over 5,000 prohibited items, including firearms, ammunition, and knives. The image shows a female inspector carefully verifying documents. Photograph by Lǐ Déhé.
