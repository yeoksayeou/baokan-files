Title: Reviewing the Achievements of Foreign Investment
Author: Pān Gǎng, Zhào Xīnglín
Date: 1989-09-25
Page: 2

To showcase the achievements of foreign-invested enterprises in China over the past decade of reform and opening up, the State Council has approved the organization of an exhibition by the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade, the State Council's Special Zone Office, and the State Planning Commission. The exhibition, titled "Exhibition of Achievements by Foreign-Invested Enterprises," will be held from November 9 to 15 at the China International Exhibition Center in Beijing.

It is reported that nearly 700 enterprises from 37 provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government, special economic zones, and municipalities with separate plans will participate in this exhibition. The participating enterprises cover more than ten industries, including energy, transportation, machinery, chemicals, electronics, textiles, light industry, agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry, fisheries, and tourism services. Foreign partners include investors from 44 countries and regions, such as Hong Kong, Macao, the United States, Japan, the United Kingdom, France, West Germany, and Australia. The exhibition will also invite foreign businesspeople who have invested in China and representatives from some world-renowned multinational corporations to visit.

### 检阅吸收外资工作成果  外资企业展将在京举行
潘岗　赵兴林
1989-09-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    检阅吸收外资工作成果
    外资企业展将在京举行
    本报讯　为了展示我国改革开放10年来外商投资企业所取得的丰硕成果，经国务院批准，对外经济贸易部、国务院特区办和国家计委，将于11月9日至15日，在北京的中国国际贸易展览中心举办“外商投资企业成果展览”。
    据悉，这次展览会将有37个省、自治区、直辖市、经济特区、计划单列市的近700个企业参加，参展企业分布于能源、交通、机械、化工、电子、纺织、轻工、农林牧渔、旅游服务等十多个行业，国外合作者包括港澳、美、日、英、法、联邦德国、澳大利亚等44个国家和地区的投资者。展览会还将邀请已在我国投资举办企业的外商及一些世界著名的跨国公司的外宾参观。（潘岗　赵兴林）
