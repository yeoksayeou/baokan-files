Title: Message to My Hometown
Author:
Date: 1989-09-08
Page: 4

Message to My Hometown

A thousand scenes delight the heart and mind.

	Yan Jici
	(Yan Jici, from Dongyang, Zhejiang Province, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, renowned scientist)

	In dreams, thirty-seven autumns have passed,
	My heart forever longs for Hangzhou.
	Zhejiang has always been home to many talents,
	In the call for modernization, all strive to excel.

	Su Buqing
	(Su Buqing, from Pingyang County, Zhejiang Province, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, renowned mathematician)

	Zhejiang is the hometown of Lu Xun, Mao Dun, Yu Dafu, and Xia Yan. Learn from their sincere dedication to the people and the country.

	Xie Jin
	(Xie Jin, from Shangyu County, Zhejiang Province, renowned film director)

	“Message to My Hometown” is a calligraphy by Mao Dun from his lifetime.

### 寄语故乡

1989-09-08
第4版(国庆专版)
专栏：

    寄语故乡
    万千景色畅天怀。
                    严济慈
    （严济慈，浙江东阳市人，全国人大常委会副委员长，著名科学家）
    别梦迢迢卅七秋，归心永忆是杭州。
    由来吾浙多英俊，四化声中争上游。
                    苏步青
    （苏步青，浙江平阳县人，全国政协副主席，著名数学家）
    浙江是鲁迅、茅盾、郁达夫、夏衍的故乡。学习他们对人民、对祖国赤诚的献身精神。
                    谢　晋
    （谢晋，浙江上虞县人，著名电影导演）《寄语故乡》是茅盾生前的题字。
