Title: Some Telephones in Beijing Implement Automatic Billing
Author:
Date: 1989-09-06
Edition: 2

	Some hotels, restaurants, and post and telecommunications service halls in Beijing have recently been equipped with a small, automatic billing device for their telephones. Whether for international, domestic, or local calls, the device displays the call duration during the conversation and prints out a bill with details such as call duration, call charges, service fees, and a transaction number upon call completion.

	This fully automatic telephone billing system was developed by HuaYu Communications Technology Company and the Beijing Long-Distance Telephone Bureau.

### 北京部分电话实行自动计费

1989-09-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    北京部分电话实行自动计费
    本报讯　北京一些宾馆、饭店、邮电营业厅的电话上，新近配装了一个小巧的自动计费装置。无论是国际、国内、市内的电话，通话时显示时间，通话结束即能打印出记有通话时长、话费、服务费、流水号等内容的话单。
    这种全自动电话计费系统由华宇通信技术开发公司和北京长途电话局研制。
