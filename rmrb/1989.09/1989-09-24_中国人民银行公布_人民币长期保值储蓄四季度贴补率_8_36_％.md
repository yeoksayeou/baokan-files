Title: People's Bank of China Announces the Fourth Quarter Compensation Rate for Long-Term Currency Savings
Author:
Date: 1989-09-24
Edition: 2

The People's Bank of China today announced the compensation rate for long-term currency savings deposits for the fourth quarter of 1989.

According to statistics from the National Bureau of Statistics on the price indices for social commodity retail and service items in the second quarter of 1989 and the first quarter of 1988, the compensation rate for fixed-term savings deposits of three years or more for the fourth quarter of 1989 is 8.36% annually.

All fixed-term savings deposits of three, five, and eight years that mature in the fourth quarter of this year will be compensated at the above rate.

### 中国人民银行公布  人民币长期保值储蓄四季度贴补率8.36％

1989-09-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中国人民银行公布
    人民币长期保值储蓄四季度贴补率8.36％
    新华社北京9月23日电　中国人民银行今天公布了1989年第四季度人民币长期保值储蓄存款贴补率。
    根据国家统计局统计的1989年第二季度和1988年第一季度的社会商品零售和服务项目价格总指数计算，1989年第四季度3年期以上定期储蓄存款的保值贴补率为年率8.36％。
  凡在今年第四季度内到期的三年、五年、八年的定期储蓄存款，均按上述贴补率给予贴补。
