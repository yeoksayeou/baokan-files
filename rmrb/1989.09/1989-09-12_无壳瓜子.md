Title: Shell-less Pumpkin Seeds
Author: Shun Long
Date: 1989-09-12
Edition: 4

Shell-less pumpkin seeds produced in Wuwei, Gansu, as a new type of food, won the gold medal at the 1988 National First Pumpkin Seeds and Roasted Snacks Exhibition and Evaluation. They are the result of successful hybridization and selection by the Wuwei Regional Agricultural Science Institute using American feed-grade shell-less gourds and local early gourds. They are shell-less, and the seeds contain various trace elements, vitamins, and amino acids, making them nutritionally rich.

### 无壳瓜子
顺龙
1989-09-12
第4版(国庆专版)
专栏：社会主义事业在前进

　　无壳瓜子
    甘肃武威出产的无壳瓜子，作为一种新型食品，在1988年全国首届瓜子、炒货展评会上获得了金奖。它是武威地区农科所采用美国饲用无种壳西葫芦与当地早西葫芦杂交选育成功的。它无壳、瓜子含有多种微量元素、维生素和氨基酸，营养丰富。
          （顺龙）
