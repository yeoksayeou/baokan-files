Author:
Date: 1989-09-20
Edition: 3

Friendship News

	● The first deputy chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Lukyanov, expressed great satisfaction with the visit of the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation to China.
	He said upon his return to Moscow by special plane on the 18th: This meeting was conducted at a high level, and the negotiations went very smoothly.

	● On the 18th, Deputy Prime Minister and Director of the Office of Scientific and Technological Progress and Application of Poland, Janowski, received the Chinese government delegation led by Vice Chairman of the State Science and Technology Commission, Li Xue'e, who was visiting Poland.

	● The first plenary meeting of the Sino-Indian Economic, Trade, and Scientific and Technological Joint Working Group was held on the 18th in New Delhi.
	Chinese delegation leader Zheng Tuobin and Indian delegation leader Dinesh Singh delivered speeches at the opening ceremony.
	It is understood that a Sino-Indian trade agreement for 1989-1990 will be signed at the conclusion of this meeting on the 20th.

	● The 10th meeting of the Sino-Swiss Economic Mixed Committee was held on the 18th in Bern, the capital of Switzerland. The meeting discussed the prospects for economic cooperation between the two countries and bilateral trade issues.

	● On the 18th, General Secretary of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Jakes, showed great interest in visiting the Chinese exhibit of machine tools at the 31st International Machinery Exhibition in Brno and expressed the hope for further development of cooperation with China in various fields.

	● The first large-scale exhibition of Cizhou kiln ceramics from China opened on the 18th at the National Art Museum in Ankara, the capital of Turkey. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好简讯

1989-09-20
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：友好简讯

　　友好简讯
    ●苏联最高苏维埃第一副主席卢基扬诺夫对苏联最高苏维埃代表团在中国进行的访问表示非常满意。
    他18日乘专机回到莫斯科时在机场说：这次会晤是在高层次进行的，谈判进行得很顺利。
    ●波兰政府副总理兼科技进步和应用署署长雅诺夫斯基18日接见了正在波兰访问的由国家科委副主任李绪鄂率领的中国政府代表团。
    ●中印经济贸易科学技术联合工作小组第一次全体会议18日在新德里开幕。
    参加会议的中国代表团团长郑拓彬和印度代表团团长迪内西·辛格分别在开幕式上发表讲话。
    据悉，本次会议在20日结束时将签署一项中印1989—1990年贸易议定书。
    ●中瑞经济混合委员会18日在瑞士首都伯尔尼举行第10次会议，会议就两国间的经济合作前景以及双边贸易等问题进行了讨论。
    ●捷共中央总书记雅克什18日在布尔诺第31届国际机械博览会上，以很大的兴趣参观了中国展出的机床，并表示捷希望进一步发展同中国在各方面的合作。
    ●中国首次大型磁州窑陶瓷展览18日在土耳其首都安卡拉国家美术馆开幕。　（据新华社）
