Edition: 2

    Announcement by the People's Bank of China on the Issuance of the 40th Anniversary Commemorative Coin of the People's Republic of China

    To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Bank has decided to issue a commemorative coin made of copper-nickel alloy. The announcement is as follows:

    1. The commemorative coin will be issued nationwide in limited quantities starting from September 28, 1989.

    2. The commemorative coin is made of copper-nickel alloy, has a silver-gray color, a diameter of 30 millimeters, and a face value of one yuan, which is equivalent to the current circulation of the Renminbi.

    3. The obverse of the commemorative coin features the national emblem, the eastern gate of the Great Hall of the People, fireworks, the year "1989," and the denomination "One Yuan." The reverse side features Arabic artistic script "40," a five-pointed star, a dove of peace, the first two bars of the national anthem's prelude, and the inscriptions "40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China" and "1949-1989."

    4. Counterfeiting and melting of commemorative coins are strictly prohibited. Violators will be dealt with according to law.

    Governor: Li Guixian
    September 25, 1989

### 就发行国庆铜镍质纪念币  中国人民银行通告

1989-09-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    就发行国庆铜镍质纪念币
    中国人民银行通告
    新华社北京9月26日电　中国人民银行关于发行中华人民共和国成立四十周年纪念币的通告
    为庆祝中华人民共和国成立四十周年，我行决定发行铜镍质纪念币一枚。特通告如下：
    一、纪念币自1989年9月28日起向全国限量发行。
    二、纪念币为铜镍合金制成，呈银灰色，直径三十毫米，面值一元，与现行人民币等值流通。
    三、纪念币正面图案为国徽、人民大会堂东门外景、节日礼花和年号“1989”、面值“壹圆”字样；背面图案为阿拉伯艺术字——“40”、五角星、和平鸽、国歌前奏中前两小节的乐谱及“中华人民共和国成立四十周年”、“1949—1989”字样。
    四、严禁伪造、熔化纪念币，违者依法论处。
                        行长：李贵鲜
                            1989年9月25日
