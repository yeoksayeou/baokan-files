Title: Gorbachev Meets with Suharto
Author:
Date: 1989-09-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 11th.

	Gorbachev met with the visiting Indonesian President Suharto on September 11th.

	According to TASS, the two leaders discussed the fundamental issues of bilateral relations during their lengthy private talks.

	After the talks, Gorbachev and Suharto signed a joint statement on the basis of friendly relations and cooperation between the Soviet Union and Indonesia. The statement affirmed that the two countries will develop friendly relations and constructive cooperation on the basis of complete respect for independence, the right of each country to freely choose its own development path, adherence to the principles of sovereign equality, non-interference in internal affairs, and mutual benefit. Both sides plan to deepen political dialogue, steadily develop economic and trade cooperation, and inject new impetus into the development of bilateral relations.

	The statement also mentioned the international situation, emphasizing that the use of force and the threat of force as means of foreign policy are no longer acceptable. Both sides believe that the normalization of Sino-Soviet relations is of great significance, and the steps taken to normalize relations between Indonesia and China are also very important. These efforts will be conducive to the cause of peace and stability in the Asia-Pacific region and the world.

	Suharto arrived in the Soviet Union on September 7th for a visit. After visiting Uzbekistan and Leningrad, he arrived in Moscow. He will conclude his visit on September 12th.

### 戈尔巴乔夫同苏哈托会谈  认为印尼和中国关系正常化有利亚太和平稳定

1989-09-12
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　戈尔巴乔夫同苏哈托会谈
　  认为印尼和中国关系正常化有利亚太和平稳定
    新华社莫斯科9月11日电 苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫11日同前来访问的印度尼西亚总统苏哈托举行了会谈。
    据塔斯社报道，两位领导人在长时间的单独会谈中，讨论了两国关系的原则问题。
    会谈后，戈尔巴乔夫和苏哈托签署了关于苏联印尼友好关系和合作基础的声明。声明确定，两国今后将在完全尊重独立、每个国家有权自由选择发展道路、恪守主权平等原则、不干涉内政和互利的基础上发展友好关系和建设性合作。双方打算加深政治对话，稳定地发展经贸合作，给两国关系以新的推动。
    声明谈到国际形势时说，双方强调武力和以武力相威胁的政策已不能作为对外政策的手段。双方认为，苏中关系正常化具有重要意义，并认为，为使印度尼西亚和中国关系正常化所采取的步骤很重要，这将有利于亚太地区和世界和平与稳定的事业。
    苏哈托是9月7日抵苏访问的，他是在乌兹别克和列宁格勒参观访问后抵达莫斯科的。他将于12日结束访问。
