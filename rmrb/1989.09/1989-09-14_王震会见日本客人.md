Title: Wang Zhen Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-09-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 14th.

Vice Chairman of the State, Honorary President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, Wang Zhen, met with the President of the Japanese Study and Research Society, Mr. Koga Hiroshi, and his entourage today at the Great Hall of the People. Wang Zhen praised Koga Hiroshi for his contributions to the development of Sino-Japanese friendly relations and the strengthening of cultural exchanges between the two countries. He hoped that Koga would continue to work towards this goal. The Study and Research Society is one of the largest publishing houses in Japan and has had extensive exchanges with China, having published the complete works of Lu Xun and Lao She, and co-produced the film "The Story of the Panda" with China.

Koga Hiroshi expressed his willingness to continue working to promote friendship and cultural exchanges between Japan and China.

Wang Zhen introduced that China's political situation is stable, and China continues to implement the policy of reform and opening up, welcoming more foreign investors to come to China for investment and cooperation. Koga Hiroshi was invited to visit by the China International Friendship Association.

### 王震会见日本客人

1989-09-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　王震会见日本客人
    新华社北京9月14日电国家副主席、中日友协名誉会长王震今天在人民大会堂会见了日本学习研究社社长古冈滉一行。王震称赞古冈滉为发展中日友好关系、加强中日文化交流作出了贡献，希望他继续为此努力。学习研究社是日本最大的出版社之一，与中国交往多年，曾出版鲁迅和老舍全集并与中国合拍了影片《熊猫的故事》。
    古冈滉表示愿意继续为增进日中友好和文化交流而努力。
    王震介绍说，中国的政局是稳定的，中国继续实行改革开放政策，欢迎更多的外商来华投资、合作。古冈滉是应对外友协邀请来访的。
