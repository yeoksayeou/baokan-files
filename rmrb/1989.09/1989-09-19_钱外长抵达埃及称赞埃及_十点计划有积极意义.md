Title: Chinese Foreign Minister Arrives in Egypt Praises Egypt's 10-Point Plan
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-09-19
Edition: 3

Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen arrived in Cairo, Egypt, today, starting a four-day visit to the country. This is his second stop on his current Middle East tour.

Egyptian Deputy Foreign Minister Boutros Boutros-Ghali and other government officials welcomed the Chinese delegation at the airport.

At the airport, Foreign Minister Qian said he would exchange views with Egyptian officials on the current situation in the Middle East and the friendly relations between the two countries. When asked about Egypt's recent 10-point plan regarding elections in the occupied Palestinian territories, Foreign Minister Qian said the plan has positive significance.

### 钱外长抵达埃及称赞埃及　十点计划有积极意义
林皎明
1989-09-19
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　钱外长抵达埃及称赞埃及
　　十点计划有积极意义
    本报开罗9月18日电　记者林皎明报道：中国外交部长钱其琛今天下午由约旦飞抵埃及首都开罗，开始对埃及为期4天的访问。
    这是他此次中东之行的第二站。
    埃及外交国务部长加利等政府官员到机场迎接中国客人。
    钱外长在机场表示，他将与埃及政府官员就中东目前局势及两国友好关系交换意见。在记者问及埃及最近提出的有关在巴勒斯坦被占领土进行选举的10点计划时，钱外长说，这份计划是有积极意义的。
