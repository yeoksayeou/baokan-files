Title: UN Observers to Monitor Nicaraguan Elections; Lopez Agrees to UN Peacekeeping Force in Honduras
Author:
Date: 1989-09-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, United Nations, September 11th.

A United Nations spokesperson said today that the UN's election monitoring team in Managua has fully commenced its work in Nicaragua.

The monitoring team officially began its operations on August 25. Currently, the team is strengthening its organizational structure and establishing contacts with Nicaragua's Supreme Electoral Council and all political organizations.

The spokesperson said the monitoring team has started verifying election activities and is inspecting various polling districts across the country.

Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, September 11th.

Tegucigalpa, Honduras: Honduran President Ascona met on September 11th with a delegation led by Brazilian General Ferreira, who presented proposals for establishing a UN peacekeeping force in Central America.

This news was announced by Honduran Foreign Minister Lopez in the capital, Tegucigalpa. He stated that due to Honduras' advantageous geographical position, bordering Nicaragua, El Salvador, and Guatemala, his country agrees to host the UN peacekeeping force in Central America.

He added that the UN military observer team will visit the camps of Nicaraguan anti-government forces in Honduras on September 12th and inspect the border between Honduras and El Salvador to assess the situation there.

Additionally, a delegation of U.S. congressmen visiting Nicaragua expressed in Managua on September 11th that if Nicaragua does not hold democratic elections, the U.S. government will continue to provide aid to the anti-government forces in Nicaragua.

### 联合国观察组将监督尼选举　洛佩斯同意联合国中美洲和平部队驻洪

1989-09-12
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　联合国观察组将监督尼选举
　　洛佩斯同意联合国中美洲和平部队驻洪
    新华社联合国9月11日电　联合国发言人今天说，联合国驻马那瓜监督选举观察小组的工作已经在尼加拉瓜全面展开。
    该观察小组是于8月25日正式开始工作的。目前该小组正在加强自身的组织工作，并同尼加拉瓜的最高选举委员会以及所有政治组织发展联系。
    这位发言人说，观察小组已开始了选举活动的核查工作，并且对这个国家的各个选区进行视察。
    新华社墨西哥城9月11日电　特古西加尔巴消息：洪都拉斯总统阿斯科纳11日会见了由巴西将军费雷拉率领的联合国军事观察员小组，听取了他们关于成立联合国驻中美洲和平部队的意见。
    这一消息是洪都拉斯外长洛佩斯在首都特古西加尔巴宣布的。他说，他的国家因具备同尼加拉瓜、萨尔瓦多和危地马拉接壤的优越条件，同意把联合国驻中美洲和平部队设在洪都拉斯。
    他还说，联合国军事观察员小组12日还将访问在洪都拉斯的尼加拉瓜反政府武装的营地，并视察洪都拉斯和萨尔瓦多边境以了解那里的局势。
    另据来自马那瓜的消息，正在尼加拉瓜访问的一个美国议员代表团11日在马那瓜表示，尼加拉瓜如不实行民主选举，美国政府将准备继续向尼反政府武装力量提供援助。
