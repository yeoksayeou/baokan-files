Title: "Only Socialism Can Save and Develop China" Symposium to be Held in Beijing on October 10th

Author:

Date: 1989-09-29

Page: 6

Theory Research

Column: Publishing News

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 29th.

To promote the in-depth study and implementation of the spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee, and to clarify some ideological and theoretical issues that have been confused by bourgeois liberalization, the Chinese Historical Materialism Research Association and the Philosophy Research Institute of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, along with the editorial department of "Philosophical Dynamics," will hold a symposium titled "Only Socialism Can Save and Develop China" from October 10th to 19th, 1989, at the Aizhi Mountain Resort in Beijing's Changping District. The symposium will feature lectures by renowned scholars and experts on topics such as the materialist view of history and the choice of the Chinese socialist path, the inevitability of China's socialist path as seen from the development of modern Chinese history, reform and opening up and Western culture, and nine other subjects. The symposium welcomes a wide range of theoretical workers, propaganda workers, ideological and political workers, philosophy teachers, and philosophy enthusiasts to actively sign up and participate.

### “只有社会主义才能救中国发展中国”研讨会十月十日在京举行

1989-09-29
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：出版消息

    “只有社会主义才能救中国发展中国”研讨会十月十日在京举行
    为了推动深入学习贯彻十三届四中全会精神，澄清被资产阶级自由化搞乱了的一些思想理论问题，中国历史唯物主义研究会和中国社会科学院哲学研究所《哲学动态》编辑部于一九八九年十月十日至十九日在北京昌平爱智山庄举行“只有社会主义才能救中国发展中国”为主题的研讨会。会上将请我国著名学者、专家主讲：唯物史观和中国社会主义道路的选择、从中国近代史的发展看中国走社会主义道路的必然性、改革开放与西方文化等十二个题目。欢迎广大理论工作者、宣传工作者、思想政治工作者、哲学教师及哲学爱好者踊跃报名参加。
