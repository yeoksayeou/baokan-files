Title: Japanese Officials Inspect the Northern Territories
Author:
Date: 1989-09-07
Page: 3

Japanese Cabinet Management Agency Director-General Mizuno Kiyoshi on September 7th boarded a ship in Nemuro City, Hokkaido, and inspected from the sea the Northern Territories occupied by the Soviet Union after World War II. Mizuno stated after the inspection that the Northern Four Islands are Japan's inherent territory, and in the future, the movement to demand the return of the Northern Territories from the Soviet Union will continue.

### 日本官员巡视北方领土

1989-09-07
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    日本官员巡视北方领土
    日本内阁总务厅长官水野清七日在北海道根室市乘船从海上巡视了二次大战后被苏联占据的北方领土。水野在巡视后发表谈话重申，北方四岛是日本的固有领土，今后仍要开展要求苏联归还北方领土的国民运动。
