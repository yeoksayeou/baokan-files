Title: Wan Li Meets with Ms. Yang Xiaoyan
Author:
Date: 1989-09-28
Edition: 1

Wan Li, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Honorary Chairman of the Chinese Bridge Association, met with Yang Xiaoyan, an American-born Chinese and world champion of women's bridge, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse today.

Wan Li thanked Yang Xiaoyan, who serves as an advisor to the Chinese Bridge Association, for her efforts in helping China improve its bridge skills. He also expressed his hope that Chinese players will strive for excellence and win the world championship. Yang Xiaoyan, on behalf of the North American Bridge Association, presented Wan Li with a commemorative plaque, recognizing his contributions to the development of bridge in China and the promotion of exchanges between the bridge communities of China and the United States. Wu Shaozu, Director of the State Sports Commission, and Rong Gaotang, Chairman of the Chinese Bridge Association, attended the meeting.

### 万里会见杨小燕女士

1989-09-28
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    万里会见杨小燕女士
    新华社北京九月二十七日电  人大常委会委员长、中国桥牌协会名誉主席万里今天在钓鱼台国宾馆亲切会见了美籍华人、世界女子桥牌赛冠军杨小燕女士。
    万里感谢担任中国桥牌协会顾问的杨小燕为帮助中国提高桥牌水平所作的努力，同时希望中国选手能够奋发图强，夺取世界冠军。杨小燕代表北美桥牌协会向万里赠送了一块纪念牌，表彰他为促进桥牌在中国的发展、促进中美桥牌界的交流做出的贡献。国家体委主任伍绍祖、中国桥协主席荣高棠等参加了会见。
