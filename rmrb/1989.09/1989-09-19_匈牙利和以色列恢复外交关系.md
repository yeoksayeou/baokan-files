Title: Hungary and Israel Resume Diplomatic Relations
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-09-19
Edition: 3

Hungary and Israel today resumed ambassador-level diplomatic relations that had been severed for 22 years. This is the first socialist country in Eastern Europe to restore formal diplomatic relations with Israel since the 1967 Middle East War.

### 匈牙利和以色列恢复外交关系
林皎明
1989-09-19
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　匈牙利和以色列恢复外交关系
    本报开罗9月18日电　记者林皎明报道：匈牙利与以色列今天恢复中断22年的大使级外交关系。这是1967年中东战争后东欧第一个社会主义国家恢复与以色列的正式外交关系。
