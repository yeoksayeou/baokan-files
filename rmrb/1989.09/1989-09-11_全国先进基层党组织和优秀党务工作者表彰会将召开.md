Title: National Advanced Grassroots Party Organizations and Outstanding Party Affairs Workers to be Honored
Author: Yin Pin Duan
Date: 1989-09-11
Page: 2

    The People's Daily has learned from the Central Organization Department that, with the approval of the Central Committee, the National Conference to Honor Advanced Grassroots Party Organizations and Outstanding Party Affairs Workers will be held in Beijing from September 20 to 22. This is a major event in our Party's political life and a grand gathering of great concern to both Party members and the public.

    Held after the decisive victory in suppressing the turmoil and counter-revolutionary riot in the capital and the successful conclusion of the Fourth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee, and on the eve of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic, this conference is of great significance in implementing the spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session, strengthening Party building, enhancing the Party's cohesion, attractiveness, and combat effectiveness, raising the awareness of the entire Party to implement the Party's basic line, improving the construction of grassroots Party organizations, enhancing the quality of Party members, and inspiring a strong sense of historical responsibility among a wide range of Party affairs workers.

### 全国先进基层党组织和优秀党务工作者表彰会将召开
尹品端
1989-09-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全国先进基层党组织和优秀党务工作者表彰会将召开
    本报讯　记者尹品端从中组部获悉：经中央批准，全国先进基层党组织和优秀党务工作者表彰会，将于9月20日至22日在北京召开。这是我们党政治生活中的一件大事，也是为党内外所关心的一次盛会。
    在制止动乱、平息首都反革命暴乱取得决定性胜利，党的十三届四中全会胜利闭幕之后和建国40周年前夕召开的这次会议，对于贯彻落实四中全会精神，大力加强党的建设，增强党的凝聚力、吸引力和战斗力，提高全党贯彻党的基本路线的自觉性，搞好党的基层组织建设，提高党员素质，激励广大党务工作者的历史责任感，都具有十分重要的意义。
