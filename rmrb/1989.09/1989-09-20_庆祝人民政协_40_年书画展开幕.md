Title: Exhibition Celebrating 40 Years of the People's Political Consultative Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-09-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 18th.

An exhibition celebrating the 40th anniversary of the People's Political Consultative Conference opened this morning at the National People's Congress Hall.

This exhibition features over 200 pieces of calligraphy and painting, all created by members of the various levels of the People's Political Consultative Conference. The calligraphy "Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference" by Li Xiannian, Chairman of the National People's Congress, is prominently displayed at the entrance. Among the calligraphy works are pieces by Wang Renzhong, such as "Together Through Wind and Rain for 40 Years"; Fang Yi's "Strong Grass in the Wind, True Gold in the Fire"; and Zhao Puchu's "Thousand Autumns" and others.

Wang Renzhong and Fang Yi, Vice Chairmen of the National People's Congress, cut the ribbon for the exhibition.

### 庆祝人民政协40年书画展开幕

1989-09-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　庆祝人民政协40年书画展开幕
    据新华社北京9月18日电　庆祝人民政协40年书画展今天上午在全国政协礼堂开幕。
    这次展览共展出书画作品200余幅，都是政协各级委员创作的。全国政协主席李先念书写的“庆祝中国人民政治协商会议成立40周年”题词，悬挂在入口处。在书法作品中，有王任重的“风雨同舟四十年”；方毅的“疾风知劲草，烈火见真金”；赵朴初的“千秋岁”等。
    全国政协副主席王任重、方毅为书画展剪彩。
