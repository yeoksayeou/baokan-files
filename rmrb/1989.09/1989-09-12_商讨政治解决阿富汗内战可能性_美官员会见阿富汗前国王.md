Title: Discussing the Possibility of a Political Solution to the Afghan Civil War - U.S. Officials Meet Former Afghan King
Author:
Date: 1989-09-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 11 September

The U.S. State Department today confirmed that a senior diplomat from the U.S. Embassy in Italy met with former Afghan King Zahir Shah in Rome on September 8.

Reports indicate that the U.S. government, dissatisfied with the military stalemate in Afghanistan and the inaction of the Afghan resistance's interim government, dispatched an envoy to discuss with Zahir Shah the possibility of a political solution to the Afghan civil war.

The Washington Post noted that this meeting signifies a clear shift in U.S. policy on the Afghan issue. The paper stated, "The U.S. now aims to expand its contacts with various Afghan factions."

However, State Department spokesman Richard Boucher said that U.S. policy on the Afghan issue has not changed. He said that U.S. representatives have met with Zahir Shah in the past and hope to continue such meetings in the future.

Boucher said that the U.S. has always viewed the Afghan interim government as a key factor in the process of resolving the Afghan issue. At the same time, the U.S. does not rule out holding talks with other resistance leaders.

### 商讨政治解决阿富汗内战可能性　美官员会见阿富汗前国王

1989-09-12
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　商讨政治解决阿富汗内战可能性
　　美官员会见阿富汗前国王
    新华社华盛顿9月11日电　美国务院今天证实，美驻意大利大使馆的一名高级外交官9月8日在罗马会见了阿富汗前国王查希尔。
    有报道称，美国政府因不满阿富汗战场军事对峙的僵局和阿富汗抵抗组织临时政府无所作为的状况，于是派遣一名特使同查希尔商讨政治解决阿富汗内战的可能性。
    《华盛顿邮报》指出，这次会见标志着美国在阿富汗问题上一个明显的政策变化。该报说，“美国现在是想扩大同阿富汗各派的联系。”
    然而，美国务院发言人鲍彻说，美国有关阿富汗问题的政策没有变化。他说，美国代表过去会见过查希尔，而且希望今后能继续同他会面。
    鲍彻说，美国一直把阿富汗临时政府看作是解决阿富汗问题过程中的关键因素，同时，美国也不排除同其他抵抗力量领导人进行会谈。
