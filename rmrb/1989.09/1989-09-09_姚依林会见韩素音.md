Title: Vice Premier Yao Yilin Meets with Han Suyin
Author:
Date: 1989-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 9th.

Vice Premier Yao Yilin met with English writer Han Suyin this afternoon at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse. They discussed China's economic policies.

### 姚依林会见韩素音

1989-09-09
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    姚依林会见韩素音
    新华社北京9月9日电　国务院副总理姚依林今天下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了英籍女作家韩素音，同她就中国的经济政策进行了交谈。
