Title: Ligachev Visits East Germany
Author: Xǔ Hóngzhì
Date: 1989-09-13
Edition: 3

Ligachev Visits East Germany

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 13th - Correspondent Xu Hongzhi reports: On September 12th, Soviet Communist Party Central Committee Politburo member and Central Committee Secretary Ligachev led a delegation from the Soviet Communist Party to Berlin for a visit. Yesterday, Ligachev held talks with Werner Krolikowski, a member of the Central Committee Politburo and Central Committee Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. During the talks, Ligachev and Krolikowski jointly condemned West Germany's illegal actions of enticing East German citizens to leave their country through illegal means, which violate international law.

### 利加乔夫访问民德
许宏治
1989-09-13
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　利加乔夫访问民德
    本报柏林9月13日电　记者许宏治报道：苏共中央政治局委员、中央书记利加乔夫9月12日率苏共中央代表团飞抵柏林进行访问。昨天，利加乔夫同德国统一社会党中央政治局委员、中央书记维尔纳·克罗利科夫斯基进行了会谈。在会谈中，利加乔夫和克罗利科夫斯基一致谴责西德诱惑民德公民以非法手段离开自己国家的违反国际法的行为。
