Title: The Unrivaled Turkish Weightlifter Suleymanoglu Wins Three Gold Medals
Author: Lǐ Chéngguì
Date: 1989-09-19
Edition: 3

The Unrivaled Turkish Weightlifter Suleymanoglu Wins Three Gold Medals

	Xinhua News Agency, Athens, September 17 (Reporter Li Chenggui) - Turkish champion Suleymanoglu today won the 60-kilogram weight class at the 59th World Weightlifting Championships held here. He lifted 145 kilograms in the snatch and 172.5 kilograms in the clean and jerk, ultimately securing a total of 317.5 kilograms. This performance earned him three gold medals in the snatch, clean and jerk, and overall total.

	In the snatch competition, China's Wang Caixi lifted 122.5 kilograms, placing sixth. Wang Caixi's clean and jerk result was 157.5 kilograms, ranking seventh. Wang Caixi's total score of 280 kilograms placed him fifth in the overall standings.

### 土耳其大力士无敌手　苏莱马诺尔古连夺三枚金牌
李成贵
1989-09-19
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　土耳其大力士无敌手
　　苏莱马诺尔古连夺三枚金牌
    新华社雅典9月17日电　（记者李成贵）土耳其名将苏莱马诺尔古今天在这里举行的第59届世界举重锦标赛60公斤级比赛中，抓举起145公斤、挺举起172.5公斤，最后以317.5公斤的总成绩获得了这个级别抓举、挺举和总成绩3枚金牌。
    在抓举比赛中，中国的王才喜举起了122.5公斤，列第6名。王才喜挺举成绩157.5公斤，名列第7。王才喜最后以280公斤获总成绩第5名。
