Title: China's National Football Team Defeats Kuwait's Champion Team 3-0
Author: Lǐ Shìxīng
Date: 1989-09-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kuwait, September 23rd

China's national football team defeated Kuwait's Al-Arabi team 3-0 this evening at the Al-Arabi Club Stadium in Kuwait.

Al-Arabi is the champion team of Kuwait's football league. In the 44th minute of the first half, Wu Qunli of the Chinese team dribbled through the defense and passed the ball in, and Tang Yaodong inserted himself in front of the goal and scored. In the 22nd minute of the second half, Zhu Bo of the Chinese team passed the ball in from the right side, and Mai Chao inserted himself and headed the ball into the net to score. In the final two minutes of the game, Liu Haiguang of the Chinese team made a perfectly timed pass, and Zhang Xiaowen quickly caught up and added another goal with a header. The Chinese football team also observed matches featuring teams from Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and others.

### 中国足球队3∶0战胜科威特冠军队
李士兴
1989-09-25
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    中国足球队3∶0战胜科威特冠军队
    据新华社科威特9月23日电　（记者李士兴）今晚在此间阿拉伯人俱乐部体育场，中国队3∶0胜科威特阿拉伯人队。
    阿拉伯人队是科威特足球冠军队。上半场第44分钟时，中国队吴群立带球突破传中，唐尧东门前插上射门，进球得分。下半场第22分钟时，中国队朱波右路传中，麦超插上头球破门得分。中国队柳海光在终场前两分钟一脚传球恰到好处，张小文迅速赶上，头球再添一分。中国足球队还观摩了沙特阿拉伯、卡塔尔等队比赛。
