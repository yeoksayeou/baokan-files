Title: Li Peng Meets with the Wife of the President of Bangladesh
Author:
Date: 1989-09-21
Edition: 2

Li Peng, Premier of the State Council, and his wife, Zhu Lin, met with the visiting wife of the President of Bangladesh, Roshan Ershad, and some of her entourage at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse this evening. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Li Peng first, on behalf of the Chinese government and people, warmly welcomed Roshan Ershad's visit to China and her attendance at the inauguration ceremony of the new building of the China-Bangladesh Friendship School (Beijing First Experimental Primary School). Li Peng expressed his gratitude for the assistance provided by Bangladesh to the China-Bangladesh Friendship School. He said that China and Bangladesh are friendly neighbors, and there exists a traditional friendly cooperative relationship between the two countries. The fact that President Ershad's wife personally attended the inauguration ceremony of the China-Bangladesh Friendship School fully demonstrates the Bangladesh government's high regard for the development of China-Bangladesh friendship and the deep friendship between the Bangladeshi people and the Chinese people. Li Peng requested Roshan Ershad to convey his greetings to President Ershad. Li Peng said that he looks forward to meeting President Ershad in Dhaka later this year.

Roshan Ershad thanked Li Peng for taking the time to meet with her and expressed her gratitude to the Chinese government and people for the warm and friendly reception during her visit to China. She also conveyed President Ershad's greetings to the Chinese leadership.

She said that President Ershad and she herself look forward to Premier Li Peng and his wife visiting Bangladesh this year.

After the meeting, Li Peng hosted a banquet in honor of the Bangladeshi guests.

### 李鹏会见孟加拉国总统夫人

1989-09-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见孟加拉国总统夫人
    新华社北京9月20日电  李鹏总理和夫人朱琳今天晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了来访的孟加拉国总统夫人罗珊·艾尔沙德和部分随行人员，并进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    李鹏首先代表中国政府和人民热烈欢迎罗珊·艾尔沙德女士访华并出席中孟友谊小学（北京第一实验小学）新校舍落成典礼。李鹏对孟加拉国给予中孟友谊小学的帮助表示感谢。他说，中孟是友好近邻，两国存在着传统的友好合作关系。艾尔沙德总统夫人亲自出席中孟友谊小学落成典礼，这充分体现了孟加拉国政府对发展中孟友谊的高度重视以及孟加拉国人民对中国人民的深厚友谊。李鹏请罗珊·艾尔沙德转达中国领导人对艾尔沙德总统的亲切问候。李鹏说，他期待着今年晚些时候在达卡与艾尔沙德总统会晤。
    罗珊·艾尔沙德感谢李鹏在百忙中会见她，感谢中国政府和人民在她访华期间所给予的热情友好的款待，并转达了艾尔沙德总统对中国领导人的亲切问候。
    她表示，艾尔沙德总统和她本人盼望着李鹏总理和夫人能于今年访问孟加拉国。
    会见后，李鹏设宴款待孟加拉国贵宾。
