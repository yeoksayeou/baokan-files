Author:
Date: 1989-09-13
Edition: 4

	Domestic test-tube livestock pioneer – Dr. Xurizhan

	Mongolian biologist Dr. Xurizhan successfully cultivated the world's first "test-tube goat" in Japan five years ago, earning him the title "Father of the Test-Tube Goat." After returning to China in 1984, with the support of relevant departments, he established an experimental animal research center with 1980s technology. After more than two years of effort, he achieved more than ten major breakthroughs. On March 10 of this year, China's first "test-tube sheep" was born in the research center. 159 days later, on August 15, China's first "test-tube cow" was also born here. In less than half a year, the research center achieved two national firsts and, with its own strength, caught up with advanced international counterparts in similar research institutions. The birth of test-tube livestock will greatly promote the process of improving livestock breeds in China.

### 国内试管家畜的首创者——旭日干博士

1989-09-13
第4版(国庆专版)
专栏：社会主义事业在前进

　　国内试管家畜的首创者——旭日干博士
    蒙古族生物学家旭日干博士五年前在日本成功地培育出了世界上第一胎“试管山羊”，被誉为“试管山羊之父”。1984年回国后，在有关部门的支持下，建起了具有80年代水平的实验动物研究中心。经过两年多的努力，取得了十多项重大成果。今年3月10日，我国第一胎“试管绵羊”在研究中心降生，159天后的8月15日，我国第一胎“试管牛”也在这里诞生。不到半年时间，这个研究中心夺得了两项全国第一，并以自己的实力赶上了国际上先进的同类研究机构。试管家畜的诞生将极大地促进我国家畜的良种化进程。
