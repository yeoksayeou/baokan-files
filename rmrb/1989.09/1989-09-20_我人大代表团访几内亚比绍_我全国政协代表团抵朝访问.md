Title: Chinese Delegations Visit Guinea-Bissau and North Korea
Author:
Date: 1989-09-20
Edition: 3

	Chinese Delegation Visits Guinea-Bissau

	New delegates from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, led by Vice Chairman Wang Hanbin, concluded a three-day friendly visit to Guinea-Bissau on September 18.

	During the visit, the Chinese delegation held working talks with a delegation from the National People's Assembly of Guinea-Bissau, led by Speaker Lopes. President Vieira of Guinea-Bissau received the entire Chinese delegation.

	The Chinese delegation also visited several socio-economic facilities in Guinea-Bissau, including the Cassungue Hospital, which was built with Chinese aid.

	Chinese Delegation Arrives in North Korea

	New delegates from the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, led by Vice Chairman Wang Enmao, arrived in Pyongyang by train today to conduct a friendly visit to North Korea.

	Kim Hyok-chun, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland of Korea, and Chinese Ambassador to North Korea Wen Yizhan welcomed the delegation at the station.

### 我人大代表团访几内亚比绍　我全国政协代表团抵朝访问

1989-09-20
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　我人大代表团访几内亚比绍
　　我全国政协代表团抵朝访问
    新华社达喀尔9月18日电　以全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长王汉斌为首的中国人大代表团18日结束了对几内亚比绍的3天友好访问。
    访问期间，中国代表团同以几内亚比绍全国人民议会议长洛佩斯为首的几内亚比绍议会代表团进行了工作会谈。几内亚比绍总统维埃拉接见了中国代表团全体成员。
    中国代表团还参观了几内亚比绍的一些社会经济设施，其中包括中国援建的卡松果医院。
    新华社平壤9月17日电　以副主席王恩茂为团长的中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会代表团今天乘火车到达平壤，对朝鲜进行友好访问。
    朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央书记局长柳浩俊和中国驻朝大使温业湛到车站迎接。
