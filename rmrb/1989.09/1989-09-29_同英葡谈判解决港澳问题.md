Title: Negotiations with Britain and Portugal to Resolve the Hong Kong and Macau Issues
Author:
Date: 1989-09-29
Page: 7

In July 1983, representatives from China and Britain began talks on the Hong Kong issue. On September 18 of the following year, the two sides reached a satisfactory agreement on the Hong Kong issue and initialed the agreement in Beijing on the 26th. On December 19 of the same year, the premiers of China and Britain formally signed the Joint Declaration in Beijing, and exchanged ratification documents on May 27, 1985. The Joint Declaration stipulates that the Chinese government will resume sovereignty over Hong Kong on July 1, 1997, and implement a series of special policies for Hong Kong, which will remain unchanged for 50 years.

The peaceful resolution of the Hong Kong issue through the "one country, two systems" policy has set a precedent for resolving the Macau issue. Representatives from China and Portugal began talks in July 1986, and on March 26, 1987, they initialed the Sino-Portuguese Joint Declaration on the Macau issue in Beijing. The declaration was formally signed by the two countries' premiers on April 13 and exchanged ratification documents on January 15, 1988. The Sino-Portuguese Joint Declaration stipulates that the Chinese government will resume sovereignty over Macau on December 20, 1999, and implement a series of special policies for Macau under the "one country, two systems" principle, which will remain unchanged for 50 years.

The Chinese government's policy on resolving the Hong Kong and Macau issues takes into account the history and current situation of Hong Kong and Macau, and considers the legitimate interests of all parties involved. It is practical and reasonable. This policy not only benefits the long-term stability and prosperity of Hong Kong and Macau, contributes to the cause of national reunification and the Four Modernizations, but also provides new experience for the peaceful resolution of international disputes.

### 同英葡谈判解决港澳问题

1989-09-29
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    同英葡谈判解决港澳问题
    1983年7月，中英两国代表团就香港问题开始会谈，翌年9月18日就香港问题圆满地达成了协议，于26日在北京草签。同年12月19日，中英两国总理在北京正式签署《联合声明》，并于1985年5月27日互换了批准书。《联合声明》规定，中国政府于1997年7月1日收回香港，行使主权，对香港将采取一系列特殊政策，并在50年内不予改变。
    按照“一国两制”政策和平解决香港问题，为解决澳门问题提供了范例。中葡两国代表团自1986年7月开始会谈，1987年3月26日在北京草签了《中葡关于澳门问题的联合声明》，并由两国总理于4月13日正式签署，1988年1月15日互换批准书。中葡《联合声明》规定，中国政府于1999年12月20日对澳门恢复行使主权，根据“一国两制”的方针，对澳门执行一系列特殊政策，并在50年内不变。
    中国政府解决港澳问题的政策，考虑了港澳的历史和现状，照顾到有关各方的正当利益，是实事求是和合情合理的。它不仅有利于港澳的长期稳定和繁荣，有利于祖国的统一大业和四化建设，而且为和平解决国际争端提供了新的经验。
