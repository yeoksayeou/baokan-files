Title: Colombian Foreign Minister Advocates Strengthening Colombia-China Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-09-02
Edition: 3

Colombian Foreign Minister Julio Londono on August 31 in the capital Bogota emphasized that Colombia will continue to develop friendly cooperative relations with China.

Foreign Minister Londono made these remarks during a meeting with the Chinese Ambassador to Colombia, Wang Weisheng, at the Colombian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Londono said that Colombia has been paying close attention to the recent events in China. He revealed that although the Colombian government has been under pressure from certain domestic and foreign groups, President Barco's government still insists on maintaining the existing friendly relations between Colombia and China and opposes foreign interference in China's internal affairs.

### 哥外长主张加强哥中关系

1989-09-02
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    哥外长主张加强哥中关系
    新华社波哥大电　哥伦比亚外长胡利奥·隆多尼奥31日在首都波哥大强调说，哥伦比亚将继续发展同中国的友好合作关系。
    隆多尼奥外长是在哥伦比亚外交部会见中国驻哥伦比亚大使王嵎生时发表这一看法的。隆多尼奥外长说，哥伦比亚一直在十分关注着不久前中国发生的事件。他透露，尽管哥伦比亚政府受到来自国内外某些集团的压力，但巴尔科总统的政府仍坚持哥、中两国业已存在的友好关系并反对外国干涉中国内政。
