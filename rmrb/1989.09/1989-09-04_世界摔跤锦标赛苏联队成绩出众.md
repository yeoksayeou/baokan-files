Title: The Soviet Team Achieves Outstanding Results at the World Wrestling Championships
Author: Shi Guangyao
Date: 1989-09-04
Edition: 3

The World Men's Freestyle Wrestling Championships concluded on September 3 in Martigny, Switzerland. Soviet athletes performed exceptionally well, securing 4 gold medals, 2 silver medals, and 2 bronze medals. The United States won 2 gold and 4 silver medals. The remaining 4 gold medals were taken by athletes from Iran, North Korea, Bulgaria, and South Korea.

The gold medalists for the last five freestyle wrestling events yesterday were: 48 kg: Kim Jong-shin (South Korea); 57 kg: Kim Young-sik (North Korea); 62 kg: John Smith (USA); 82 kg: Y. Dzhafarov (Soviet Union); 90 kg: Khadartsev (Soviet Union).

The 11-day World Championships, held after the Seoul Olympics, was the first major wrestling event since the Olympics. More than 250 male and female athletes from over 70 countries and regions participated in the competition.

### 世界摔跤锦标赛苏联队成绩出众
施光耀
1989-09-04
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    世界摔跤锦标赛苏联队成绩出众
    新华社日内瓦9月4日电　（记者施光耀）世界男子自由式摔跤锦标赛3日在瑞士马尔提尼落幕。苏联运动员成绩突出，共赢得4块金牌、两块银牌和两块铜牌。美国夺得2金、4银。其余4块金牌分别被伊朗、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、保加利亚和南朝鲜运动员捧走。
    昨天自由式摔跤最后5个项目的金牌得主是：48公斤级：金锺信（南朝鲜）；57公斤级：金英植（朝鲜）；62公斤级：约翰·史密斯（美国）；82公斤级：叶·贾夫雷洛夫（苏联）；90公斤级：哈达尔采夫（苏联）。
    历时11天的世界锦标赛是汉城奥运会后的第一次世界摔跤大赛。70多个国家和地区的250多名男、女运动员参加了比赛。
