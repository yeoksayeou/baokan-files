Title: Yang Shangkun Appoints and Dismisses Ambassador to the U.S.
Author:
Date: 1989-09-08
Edition: 2

The President of the People's Republic of China, Yang Shangkun, has appointed and dismissed ambassadors to foreign countries in accordance with the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress:

Jia Qizhong is appointed as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the United States of America.

Han Xu is relieved of his post as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the United States of America.

### 杨尚昆任免驻美大使

1989-09-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    杨尚昆任免驻美大使
    新华社北京9月8日电　中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会的决定任免驻外大使：
    任命朱启祯为中华人民共和国驻美利坚合众国特命全权大使。
    免去韩叙的中华人民共和国驻美利坚合众国特命全权大使职务。
