Title: Arab League Condemns Israel's Missile Test Launch
Author:
Date: 1989-09-28
Edition: 3

The Arab League Council held a special meeting in Tunis on the 25th, condemning Israel's test launch of a medium-range missile into the Mediterranean Sea on the 14th.

The meeting was convened at the request of Libya.

	(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 阿盟谴责以发射导弹试验

1989-09-28
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    阿盟谴责以发射导弹试验
    阿拉伯联盟理事会二十五日在突尼斯召开特别会议，谴责以色列于十四日向地中海发射一枚中程导弹的试验。
    这次会议是应利比亚的请求召开的。
            （据新华社）
