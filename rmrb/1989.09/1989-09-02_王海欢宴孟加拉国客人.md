Title: Wang Hai Hosts a Banquet for Bangladeshi Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-09-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 2nd.

General Wang Hai hosted a banquet for Bangladeshi guests at the Great Hall of the People this evening to welcome General Montaz Ahmed, Chief of Staff of the Bangladesh Air Force, and his delegation.

Speaking at the banquet, Wang Hai said that General Montaz and his delegation are the first foreign military delegation to visit China after the suppression of the counter-revolutionary rebellion in Beijing. This fully demonstrates that the friendship between China and Bangladesh, and between the two militaries, is sincere and can withstand the test of storms.

Montaz said in his speech that there is an everlasting brotherly relationship between Bangladesh and China, and between the two militaries. The two countries share the same views on the problems facing the world today. This morning, Wang Hai hosted a ceremony at the Air Force Headquarters to welcome Montaz and his delegation.

### 王海欢宴孟加拉国客人

1989-09-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    王海欢宴孟加拉国客人
    据新华社北京9月2日电　空军司令员王海今天晚上在人民大会堂举行宴会，欢迎孟加拉国空军参谋长蒙塔兹·艾哈迈德少将一行。
    王海在宴会上讲话说，蒙塔兹将军一行是中国在平息了发生在北京的反革命暴乱之后，迎来的第一个外国空军高级代表团。这充分说明了中孟两国、两军之间的友谊是真诚的，是经得起风浪考验的。
    蒙塔兹在讲话时说，孟中两国、两军之间存在着永恒的兄弟关系。两国对当今世界面临的问题上有着一致的看法。今天上午，王海在空军司令部主持仪式，欢迎蒙塔兹一行。
