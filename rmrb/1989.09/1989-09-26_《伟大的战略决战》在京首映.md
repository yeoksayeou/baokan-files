Title: The Great Strategic Battle Premieres in Beijing
Author: Gai Yumin
Date: 1989-09-26
Edition: 4

The large-scale military educational documentary film "The Great Strategic Battle" premiered in Beijing today.

The film employs a wealth of precious historical documents and film materials to comprehensively, systematically, and truthfully reflect the decisive battles of the War of Liberation. It reveals the truth that the People's Republic of China was born through arduous struggles under the leadership of the Party. It vividly showcases the outstanding strategic guidance and battlefield command of military leaders such as Mao Zedong, the fine traditions of the People's Liberation Army, and the significant contributions made by millions of people in this great battle.

The film is divided into three parts: the first part includes "Before the Decisive Battle" and "The Liaoshen Campaign"; the second part is "The Huaihai Campaign"; the third part is "The Pingjin Campaign." Deng Xiaoping, Chen Yun, and Nie Rongzhen, who personally commanded the Huaihai, Liaoshen, and Pingjin campaigns, each wrote the title for this film.

### 《伟大的战略决战》在京首映
盖玉珉
1989-09-26
第4版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    《伟大的战略决战》在京首映
    新华社北京9月25日电  （记者盖玉珉）大型军事教育文献影片《伟大的战略决战》今天在北京首次放映。
    这部影片运用大量珍贵的文献资料和电影资料，全面、系统、真实地反映了解放战争的决战场面。揭示了中华人民共和国在党的领导下经过艰苦奋战才得以诞生的真理，生动地显示了毛泽东等老一辈无产阶级军事家卓越的战略指导和战役指挥艺术，人民解放军的优良传统，以及千百万人民为这场伟大决战所作出的重要贡献。
    影片共分三集：第一集包括“决战之前”和“辽沈战役”两部分；第二集是“淮海战役”；第三集是“平津战役”。亲自指挥淮海、辽沈、平津三大战役的邓小平、陈云、聂荣臻同志分别为这部影片题写了片名。
