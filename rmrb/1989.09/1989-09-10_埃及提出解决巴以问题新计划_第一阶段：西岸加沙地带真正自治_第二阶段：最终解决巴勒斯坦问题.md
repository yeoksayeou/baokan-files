Title: Egypt Proposes New Plan to Resolve the Palestinian-Israeli Issue
Author:
Date: 1989-09-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kuwait, September 9th.

Egypt has recently proposed a 10-point plan regarding elections in the Palestinian occupied territories, aiming to provide a solution acceptable to both the PLO and the Israeli government, helping them break out of the current stalemate.

Kuwait's *Al-Torath* newspaper today revealed Egypt's plan. Egypt's proposal suggests resolving the Palestinian issue in two stages. The first stage is transitional. During this stage, Palestinians in the occupied territories of the West Bank and Gaza Strip will achieve genuine autonomy. However, the specifics of this autonomy still require further negotiation. The second stage will ultimately resolve the Palestinian issue, including the status of the occupied territories.

The plan supports holding free elections in the occupied territories, based on United Nations Security Council Resolutions 242 and 338, and the principle of "land for peace." It demands that elections in the occupied territories be held under international supervision, though it does not specify that this supervision must be under the United Nations.

The plan also requires that Arab residents of East Jerusalem participate in the elections. During the election period, Israeli troops will temporarily withdraw from the election areas and cease further Jewish settlement activities in the occupied territories.

The plan does not emphasize the direct participation of the PLO in the election preparation phase and negotiations, but it leaves room for the PLO to participate in the process.

Additionally, Egypt's plan demands that a comprehensive resolution to the Palestinian issue guarantee the legitimate political rights of the Palestinian people, but it does not mention the establishment of a Palestinian state.

### 埃及提出解决巴以问题新计划　第一阶段：西岸加沙地带真正自治　第二阶段：最终解决巴勒斯坦问题

1989-09-10
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　埃及提出解决巴以问题新计划
　　第一阶段：西岸加沙地带真正自治
　　第二阶段：最终解决巴勒斯坦问题
    新华社科威特9月9日电　埃及最近提出了一个关于在巴勒斯坦被占领土上进行选举的10点计划，旨在提供一个为巴解组织和以色列政府都能接受的方案，使他们摆脱目前的僵局。
    科威特《火炬报》今天披露了埃及的这一计划。埃及的计划建议分两个阶段解决巴勒斯坦问题。第一阶段是过渡性的。在这一阶段中，约旦河西岸和加沙地带被占领土上的巴勒斯坦人将实行真正的自治。但实行真正的自治这一点还有待进一步商谈。第二阶段将最终解决巴勒斯坦问题，被占领土的地位问题也将得到解决。
    计划同意通过在被占领土上进行自由选举的方案，并要求选举要以联合国安理会的242号和338号决议以及“以土地换和平”的原则为基础。要求被占领土上的选举在国际监督下进行，但没有要求必须要在联合国的监督下进行。
    计划还要求，东耶路撒冷的阿拉伯居民也参加选举。在选举期间，以色列军队从选举地区暂时撤出，并停止在被占领土上继续搞犹太人定居计划。
    计划没有强调巴解组织直接参加选举准备阶段的活动和谈判，但为巴解组织参加这一进程留有余地。
    此外，埃及的计划还要求巴勒斯坦问题全面和平的解决要保证巴勒斯坦人民合法的政治权利，但没有提及建立巴勒斯坦国的问题。
