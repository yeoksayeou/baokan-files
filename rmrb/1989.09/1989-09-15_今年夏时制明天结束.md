Title: Summer Time Ends Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1989-09-15
Edition: 1

According to the relevant regulations of the State Council Office on the implementation of daylight saving time, this year's daylight saving time will end at 2:00 AM (Beijing Summer Time) on September 17th. Please adjust your clocks back by one hour at that time.

### 今年夏时制明天结束

1989-09-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　今年夏时制明天结束
    新华社北京电　根据国务院办公厅关于实行夏时制的有关规定，今年的夏时制于9月17日凌晨2时（北京夏令时）结束。到时请注意把钟表拨慢一小时。
