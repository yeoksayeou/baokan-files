Title: Reception for Foreign Experts Held in Beijing for National Day
Author: Kong Xiaoning, Zhou Qing
Date: 1989-09-29
Edition: 2

This evening, the State Bureau of Foreign Experts held a reception for foreign experts in the Great Hall of the People to celebrate the National Day. Premier Li Peng, Vice Premiers Yao Yilin, and Tian Jiyun, along with over 1,350 foreign experts and their family members, gathered to celebrate the great festival of the Chinese people of all ethnic groups.

Yao Yilin, on behalf of the Chinese government and Premier Li Peng, extended a warm welcome to the experts and conveyed sincere greetings for the holiday, expressing heartfelt thanks for their hard work.

In his speech, Yao Yilin pointed out that the Central Committee of the Party reiterated at the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee that China will adhere to the independent and peaceful foreign policy and the policy of reform and opening up. China will continue to develop friendly relations with all countries on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and expand economic and cultural exchanges. As an important part of the reform and opening-up policy, we will continue to strengthen the introduction of foreign intelligence, expand talent exchanges and cooperation with governments and non-governmental organizations of various countries. In the future, while fully utilizing the talents at home, we will continue to invite foreign experts to help with the construction of China, and according to actual needs, send people abroad to learn advanced technologies and beneficial management experiences.

### 外国专家国庆招待会在京举行  李鹏姚依林田纪云到会祝贺节日
孔晓宁  周庆
1989-09-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外国专家国庆招待会在京举行
    李鹏姚依林田纪云到会祝贺节日
    本报北京9月28日讯　记者孔晓宁、周庆报道：今晚，国家外国专家局在人民大会堂宴会厅举行外国专家国庆招待会，国务院总理李鹏、副总理姚依林、田纪云等与外国专家及其家属1350多人欢聚一堂，共同庆祝中国各族人民的盛大节日。
    姚依林代表中国政府和李鹏总理向专家们表示热烈欢迎，并致以诚挚的节日问候，对他们的辛勤劳动，表示衷心的感谢。
    姚依林在讲话中指出，党中央在十三届四中全会上重申，我国坚持独立自主的和平外交政策不变，坚持改革开放的方针不变。我国将在和平共处五项原则基础上继续同世界各国发展友好关系，扩大经济文化交流。作为改革开放政策的一个重要组成部分，我们将继续加强引进国外智力工作，扩大与各国政府以及民间组织之间的人才交流与合作。今后我们要在充分发挥国内现有人才作用的同时，继续聘请外国专家来华帮助建设，并根据实际需要，派人到国外学习先进技术和有益的经营管理经验。
    李铁映、宋健、邹家华、陈俊生以及罗干、张劲夫和中央有关部门的领导同志也出席了招待会。
