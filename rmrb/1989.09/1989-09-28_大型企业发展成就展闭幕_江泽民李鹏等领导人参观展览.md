Title: Large-Scale Enterprise Development Achievements Exhibition Closes
Author:
Date: 1989-09-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 27th.

The "China's Industry Over Forty Years—Large-Scale Enterprise Development Achievements Exhibition" concluded today in Beijing.

Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, Qiao Shi, Yao Yilin, Song Ping, Li Tieying, Wang Ping, Li Desheng, Xiao Ke, Yu Quli, Chen Xilian, Duan Junyi, Hab Comber, Zhu Lianfen, Ni Zhifu, Chen Muxia, Lei Jianjun, Wang Bingqian, Zhou Peiyuan, Qian Weichang, and Hong Xuezhi, along with relevant officials from the Central Committee and the State Council, visited the exhibition with great interest. Jiang Zemin and Li Peng, among others, fully affirmed the important position and role of large-scale enterprises in the national economy during their visit. They pointed out that large-scale enterprises have a tremendous driving effect on the development of China's productive forces and, to a large extent, determine the scale, level, and benefits of industrial production.

The exhibition, organized by the State Council Development Research Center and specifically managed by the "Management World" magazine, attracted people from all walks of life in the capital. During the one-week exhibition, 200,000 people visited.

### 大型企业发展成就展闭幕  江泽民李鹏等领导人参观展览

1989-09-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    大型企业发展成就展闭幕
    江泽民李鹏等领导人参观展览
    新华社北京9月27日电　中国工业四十年——大型企业发展成就展今天在这里闭幕。
    江泽民、李鹏、乔石、姚依林、宋平、李铁映、王平、李德生、肖克、余秋里、陈锡联、段君毅、习仲勋、朱学范、倪志福、陈慕华、雷洁琼、王丙乾、邹家华、周培源、钱伟长、洪学智，以及中央和国务院有关部委的负责人高兴地参观了展览会。江泽民、李鹏等同志参观时充分肯定了大型企业在国民经济中的重要地位和作用。他们指出，大型企业对我国生产力的发展具有巨大的推动作用，在很大程度上决定着工业生产发展的规模、水平和效益。
    由国务院发展研究中心举办、管理世界杂志社具体承办的这次展览会吸引了首都各界人士。在为期一周的展出中有20万人前来参观。
