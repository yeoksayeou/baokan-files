Title: The Chinese Football Team Arrives in Kuwait
Author:
Date: 1989-09-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kuwait, September 19th.

The Chinese football team, consisting of 25 members, arrived here last night. Today, the Chairman of the Asian Olympic Council and the President of the Kuwait Football Association, Sheikh Fahad Al-Ahmed Al-Sabah, met with the delegation led by the President of the Chinese Football Association, Nian Weisuo.

The Chinese team will play two friendly matches against the Kuwait national team and the Kuwait Football Club League team on the 20th and 23rd, respectively.

### 中国足球队抵达科威特

1989-09-21
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    中国足球队抵达科威特
    新华社科威特9月19日电　中国足球队一行25人昨晚抵达这里。亚奥理事会主席兼科威特足协主席谢赫·法赫德·艾哈迈德·萨巴赫亲王今天会见了率队前来的中国足协主席年维泗。
    中国队将于20日和23日分别同科威特国家队和科威特足球俱乐部联队进行两场友谊赛。
