Title: Schools in the West Bank Reopen
Author:
Date: 1989-08-31
Edition: 3

	According to reports from the occupied territories, 50,000 Palestinian middle school students in the West Bank under Israeli occupation returned to school on the 30th.

	On the first day of school, the campuses of various middle schools were calm, with no reports of conflicts. On July 22nd, approximately 20,000 elementary school students in the West Bank had already returned to 1,000 primary schools.

### 约旦河西岸中学重新开课

1989-08-31
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    约旦河西岸中学重新开课
    据来自被占领土的消息，在以色列占领下的约旦河西岸五万名巴勒斯坦中学生三十日返回学校上课。
    在开学的第一天，各中学校园平静，没有关于冲突的报道。七月二十二日，西岸的约二万名小学生已回到一千所小学上课。　（据新华社）
