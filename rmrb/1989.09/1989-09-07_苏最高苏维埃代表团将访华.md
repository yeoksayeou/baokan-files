Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-09-07
Edition: 1

The spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced today at a press conference:

At the invitation of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, a delegation from the Soviet Supreme Soviet, led by the First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Supreme Soviet, Lukyanov, will arrive in Beijing on September 12 to make a friendly visit to China.

At the invitation of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of China, Henry Ruiz, a member of the National Directorate of the Sandinista National Liberation Front of Nicaragua and the Minister of External Cooperation, will visit China from September 9 to 15.

At the invitation of Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Nan, the Governor of Macao, Vasco Garcia, will visit Beijing and Xi'an from October 4 to 8.

### 苏最高苏维埃代表团将访华

1989-09-07
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    苏最高苏维埃代表团将访华
    新华社北京9月7日电　外交部发言人今天在新闻发布会上宣布：
    应全国人大常委会的邀请，由苏联最高苏维埃第一副主席卢基扬诺夫率领的苏联最高苏维埃代表团将于9月12日抵京，对中国进行友好访问。
    应中国外交部的邀请，尼加拉瓜桑地诺民族解放阵线全国领导委员会成员、对外合作部长亨利·鲁伊斯将于9月9日至15日访华。
    应中华人民共和国外交部副部长周南的邀请，澳门总督文礼治将于10月4日至8日访问北京和西安。
