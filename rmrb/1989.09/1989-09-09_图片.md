Title: Leaders of the China Bank Branch in Gu Cheng County, Hubei Province, Actively Assist Enterprises
Author: Shuai Yu
Date: 1989-09-09
Edition: 5

	Leaders of the China Bank branch in Gu Cheng County, Hubei Province, frequently visit factories to convey domestic and international information. They help enterprises to activate and effectively utilize funds, playing an active role in helping enterprises to earn more foreign exchange.
