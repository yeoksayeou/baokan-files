Title: Retired Naval Officer Jin Hu Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-09-23
Edition: 2

Retired naval officer Jin Hu passed away due to illness on August 9 in Beijing, at the age of 78.

Comrade Jin Hu joined the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in 1931 and became a member of the Communist Party of China the same year. He held various positions, including platoon leader, company leader, deputy battalion commander, regiment commander, division commander, and deputy director of the Naval Headquarters Armament Department. In 1961, he was awarded the rank of Rear Admiral.

### 海军离休干部靳虎病逝

1989-09-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    海军离休干部靳虎病逝
    新华社北京电海  军离休干部靳虎同志因病于八月九日在北京逝世，终年七十八岁。
    靳虎同志一九三一年参加中国工农红军，同年加入中国共产党。历任排长、连长、副营长、团长、师长和海军司令部军械部副部长等职。一九六一年被授予海军少将军衔。
