Title: The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Holds a Reception
Author:
Date: 1989-09-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 29th.

The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries held a reception here tonight to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Attending the reception were the Soviet Union's delegation of veterans of the Soviet Union's Society for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, led by Arkhipov, former First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers; the delegation of the Korea-China Friendship Association, led by Yun Ki-jong, Minister of Finance of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; the delegation of the Czechoslovak-China Friendship Association, led by Stefan Mulin, Chairman of the Czechoslovak-China Friendship Association; the delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association, led by Mukou Rokurou, Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association; delegations from the friendship associations of the United States, Colombia, Canada, Malta, the Netherlands, Belgium, and other countries; some foreign friends residing in Beijing; and some leaders of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.

Chapter Wenjin, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, made a speech at the reception.

The Yunnan National Folk Dance Troupe performed artistic programs for the guests.

### 对外友协举行招待会  一些国家友好访问团出席

1989-09-30
第4版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    对外友协举行招待会
    一些国家友好访问团出席
    据新华社北京9月29日电  中国人民对外友好协会今晚在这里举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    出席今晚招待会的有以苏联前部长会议第一副主席阿尔希波夫为团长的苏联对外友协老战士团，以朝鲜财政部长尹基贞为团长的朝中友协代表团，以捷中友协主席什提凡·穆林为团长的捷中友协代表团，以日中友协会长向坊隆为团长的日中友协访华团，来自美国、哥伦比亚、加拿大、马耳他、荷兰、比利时等国的友协代表团，在京的部分外国友好人士，以及中国一些对外友好协会的负责人等。
    对外友协会长章文晋在招待会上致辞。
    云南民族民间歌舞演出团为客人们演出了文艺节目。
