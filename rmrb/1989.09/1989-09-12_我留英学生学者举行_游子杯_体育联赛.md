Title: Chinese Students and Scholars in the UK Hold the "Roaming Son Cup" Sports Tournament
Author:
Date: 1989-09-12
Edition: 3

To enrich the lives of 2,800 Chinese students and scholars studying in the UK and to improve their physical fitness, the second "Roaming Son Cup" sports tournament, organized by the Second All-England Chinese Students and Scholars Association and assisted by the Education Section of the Chinese Embassy in the UK, has commenced across various regions in the UK.

The competition includes five sports: basketball, volleyball, soccer, table tennis, and badminton. Preliminary rounds are being held in five different regions: the London area, the southern UK, the central UK, the Manchester area, and the northern UK. Each region will determine one representative team for each sport to compete in the finals.

The finals will be held on September 16 and 17 at the University of Leeds.

### 我留英学生学者举行“游子杯”体育联赛

1989-09-12
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　我留英学生学者举行“游子杯”体育联赛
    本报伦敦9月11日电　为丰富2800名中国留英学子的留学生活，增强体质，由第二届全英中国学生学者联谊会主办，我驻英使馆教育处协助的第二届“游子杯”体育联赛已在英国各地全面展开。
    比赛项目为三大球两小球，即篮球、排球、足球、羽毛球和乒乓球。预赛分别在英国的五个管区内进行。五个管区为伦敦地区，英国南部地区，英国中部地区，英国曼彻斯特地区和英国北部地区，每管区每个比赛项目决出一个代表队参加决赛。
    决赛将于9月16、17两日在利兹大学举行。
