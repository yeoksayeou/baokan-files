Title: Yang Shangkun Meets with Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation, Says Sino-Pakistani Friendship Has Deep Foundations
Author:
Date: 1989-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 26th.

State Chairman Yang Shangkun today met with a Pakistani parliamentary delegation in the Great Hall of the People and said that Sino-Pakistani relations are a "model of friendly relations between countries with different social systems, and the friendship between the two countries is all-weather."

He said during the meeting with the delegation, led by Pakistan's National Assembly Speaker Mairaj Khalid, that China and Pakistan have a traditional friendship, and the relationship between the two countries has deep foundations. The two countries have had similar historical experiences and now share the common goals of maintaining world peace and developing their national economies.

Khalid agreed with Yang Shangkun's remarks. He said, "Whether it is sunny or rainy, the peoples of Pakistan and China will stand together as true friends. The friendship between Pakistan and China will be continuously consolidated and developed."

During the meeting, Yang Shangkun introduced the current situation in China. He said that in the 40 years since the founding of the nation, China has undergone tremendous changes, although there have been mistakes, the achievements are enormous, and we have always actively corrected mistakes and deviations.

He reiterated that China's established policy of reform and opening up will not change; "not only will it not change, but we will also further deepen the reforms and expand the opening up."

Khalid said that the reform and opening up have greatly enhanced China's status and image abroad, and it is inspiring that China continues to adhere to reform and opening up. He said that it is an honor to visit China on the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Yang Shangkun expressed the belief that the delegation's visit will contribute to enhancing exchanges and cooperation between the two countries' parliaments and the friendly cooperative relationship between the two countries and their peoples.

National People's Congress Standing Committee Vice Chairman Rong Yiren was present at the meeting.

### 杨尚昆会见巴基斯坦议会代表团时说  中巴两国友谊基础深厚

1989-09-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨尚昆会见巴基斯坦议会代表团时说
    中巴两国友谊基础深厚
    新华社北京九月二十六日电　国家主席杨尚昆今天在人民大会堂会见巴基斯坦议会代表团时说，中巴关系是“不同社会制度国家发展友好关系的典范，两国间的友谊是全天候的。”
    他在会见由巴基斯坦国民议会议长迈拉吉·哈立德率领的代表团时说，中国和巴基斯坦有传统的友谊，两国关系有着深厚的基础；两国在历史上有共同的遭遇，现在都面临着维护世界和平和发展国民经济的共同目标。
    哈立德表示同意杨尚昆的说法。他说：“不论晴天还是雨天，巴中两国人民都会作为真正的朋友站在一起。巴中友谊将得到不断巩固和发展。”
    会见时，杨尚昆介绍了当前中国国内的情况。他说，建国四十年来，中国发生了翻天覆地的变化，尽管也有失误，但建设成就是巨大的，并且我们总是积极地纠正失误和偏差。
    他重申，中国改革开放的既定方针、政策不变；“不仅不变，而且我们还将进一步深化改革，扩大开放”。
    哈立德说，改革开放极大地提高了中国在海外的地位和形象，中国继续坚持改革开放很鼓舞人心。他说，能在中国国庆四十周年之际来访“感到高兴和荣幸”。
    杨尚昆表示相信，代表团的来访将为增进两国议会的交流与合作以及两国和两国人民之间的友好合作关系作出贡献。
    会见时全国人大常委会副委员长荣毅仁在座。
