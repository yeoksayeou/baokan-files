Title: Wan Li Meets with Pakistani Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 14th.

Wan Li, chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met with Masood Hussain, director of the Pakistan Strategic Studies Institute, this afternoon in the Great Hall of the People.

Wan Li said during the meeting that China and Pakistan have very friendly cooperative relations in the fields of politics, economy, culture, and science and technology. He hoped that both sides would make joint efforts to strengthen exchanges and further promote the development of this friendly cooperative relationship.

Wan Li said that it is necessary and beneficial for scholars from China and Pakistan to exchange views on current international situations and international strategic issues.

Hussain said that Pakistan and China have a special friendly cooperative relationship based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. No matter what happens in China, Pakistan expresses sympathy and support.

Hussain is visiting China at the invitation of the Beijing International Strategic Studies Society.

### 万里会见巴基斯坦客人

1989-09-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　万里会见巴基斯坦客人
    新华社北京9月14日电 全国人大常委会委员长万里今天下午在人民大会堂会见了巴基斯坦战略研究所所长马苏德·胡赛因。
    万里在会见时说，中国同巴基斯坦在政治、经济、文化和科技等方面有着非常友好的合作关系。他希望今后双方共同努力，加强交往，进一步促进这种友好合作关系的发展。
    万里说，中巴两国学者就当前国际形势和国际战略问题交换意见是必要的、有益的。
    胡赛因说，巴中两国有着建立在和平共处五项原则基础上的特殊友好合作关系，无论中国发生什么事情，巴基斯坦都表示同情和支持。
    胡赛因是应北京国际战略问题学会邀请来华访问的。
