Title: Some Countries and Political Party Leaders Send Congratulatory Messages to Chinese Leaders on the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1989-09-30
Edition: 3

Some countries and political party leaders have sent congratulatory messages to Chinese leaders to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	New China News Agency, Beijing, September 30 - The General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Chairman of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Il-sung, sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Yang Shangkun, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Wan Li, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, to warmly congratulate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	The telegram said: Over the past 40 years, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, the hardworking and brave Chinese people have highly carried forward the revolutionary spirit of self-reliance and hard struggle, achieving the great feat of building a prosperous and powerful socialist new China on the vast land of China. Especially since the Third Plenary Session of the 11th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Chinese people have adhered to the four cardinal principles, carried out reform and opening up, and achieved tremendous development in the struggle to build socialism with Chinese characteristics.

	The telegram pointed out: The Korean people are genuinely happy about the great achievements made by the Chinese people in the socialist revolution and socialist construction, and about the recent timely suppression of the counter-revolutionary riot and the development of a stable and united political situation.

	The telegram said: The Korean people have always cherished the everlasting friendship between Korea and China and are convinced that the friendship, unity, and cooperation between the two parties, the two countries, and the two peoples will be further consolidated and developed in accordance with the needs of the revolutionary development of the two countries and the cause of peace in Asia and the world.

	New China News Agency, Beijing, September 29 - The leaders of the Cambodian National Resistance Forces, including Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, Hun Sen, Prime Minister of the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea, and Khieu Samphan, Vice Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea in charge of foreign affairs, sent a letter to Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Deng Xiaoping, Chairman of the Central Military Commission, Yang Shangkun, President of the People's Republic of China, Li Peng, Premier of the State Council, and Li Xiannian, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, to warmly congratulate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	The congratulatory letter said: On the occasion of the Chinese people's enthusiastic celebration of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we, on behalf of the Cambodian people, the Cambodian National Resistance Movement, the three armed forces of Cambodia, and the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea, and in our personal capacities, extend our warmest congratulations to you and, through you, to the great Chinese people, the Chinese government, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	The letter pointed out: Over the past 40 years, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese government, the Chinese people have made tremendous efforts and sacrifices to achieve remarkable achievements in all fields, transforming the backward old China into a rapidly modernizing strong country. These achievements not only serve the interests of the Chinese people but also contribute to world peace and security, especially the peace and security of Southeast Asia, which continues to be threatened by Vietnam's new form of aggression against Cambodia.

	The letter said: The People's Republic of China pursues a foreign policy based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, firmly supports the national liberation struggles of all peoples, and plays an increasingly important role in international affairs, enjoying ever-increasing prestige. We are genuinely pleased with all the achievements made by your country in domestic politics and international affairs. We wish the Chinese people, under the guidance of the grand blueprint of the Four Modernizations and the policy of reform and opening up, new and brilliant victories in achieving the goals of the Seventh Five-Year Plan.

	The letter said: On this occasion, we deeply appreciate your generous support for our struggle for national liberation and resistance against Vietnamese aggression. Our struggle has made gratifying progress, which is inseparable from your support. This support has also made a significant contribution to the comprehensive resolution of the Cambodian problem in accordance with the five-point proposal of Prince Sihanouk.

	New China News Agency, Beijing, September 29 - Recently, leaders of some countries and political parties have sent telegrams to Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Yang Shangkun, President of the People's Republic of China, Li Peng, Premier of the State Council, and Wan Li, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, to warmly congratulate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	Nicolae Ceaușescu, General Secretary of the Romanian Communist Party and President of the Socialist Republic of Romania, and Constantin Dăscălescu, Prime Minister of the Government, jointly sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Yang Shangkun, President of the People's Republic of China, Li Peng, Premier of the State Council, and Wan Li, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

### 一些国家和政党领导人电函我国领导人  祝贺中华人民共和国成立四十周年

1989-09-30
第3版(国际新闻·国内新闻)
专栏：

    一些国家和政党领导人电函我国领导人
    祝贺中华人民共和国成立四十周年
    新华社北京9月30日电　朝鲜劳动党中央委员会总书记、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国主席金日成，今天致电江泽民总书记、杨尚昆主席和万里委员长，热烈祝贺中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    贺电说，中华人民共和国成立40年来，勤劳勇敢的中国人民在中国共产党的领导下，高度发扬自力更生、艰苦奋斗的革命精神，取得了在广阔的中国大地上建成一个繁荣昌盛的社会主义新中国的豪迈成就。特别是中共中央十一届三中全会以来，中国人民坚持四项基本原则，实行改革开放，在建设具有中国特色的社会主义的斗争中取得了巨大的发展。
    贺电指出，朝鲜人民对兄弟的中国人民在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中取得的巨大成就和不久前及时平息反革命暴乱，并正在发展安定团结的政治局面感到由衷的高兴。
    贺电说，朝鲜人民一向珍视永恒的朝中友谊，并确信两党、两国、两国人民之间的友谊、团结和合作关系必将根据两国革命发展和亚洲与世界和平事业的需要，日益巩固和发展。
    新华社北京9月29日电　柬埔寨民族抵抗力量领导人、民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克、民主柬埔寨联合政府总理宋双、民主柬埔寨负责外交事务的副主席乔森潘今天致函中共中央总书记江泽民、中央军委主席邓小平、国家主席杨尚昆、国务院总理李鹏和全国政协主席李先念，热烈祝贺中华人民共和国成立40周年。
  贺信说，值此中国人民热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年之际，我们谨代表柬埔寨人民、柬埔寨民族抵抗运动、柬埔寨三方武装力量和民柬联合政府并以我们个人名义向你们并通过你们向伟大的中国人民、中国政府和中国人民解放军表示最热烈祝贺。
    贺信指出，40年间，中国人民在中国共产党和中国政府的领导下，经过艰苦努力和作出巨大牺牲，在各方面取得了惊人的成就，把落后的旧中国建设成为一个正在迅速实现现代化的强国。这些成就不仅符合中国人民的利益，而且有利于世界和平与安全，特别是东南亚的和平与安全，而那里继续受到越南新形式的侵柬战争的威胁。
    贺信说，中华人民共和国奉行以和平共处五项原则为基础的外交政策，坚决支持各国人民的民族解放斗争，在国际上发挥着日益重大的影响，并享有越来越高的威望。我们对贵国在国内政治方面和国际事务中所取得的所有成就感到由衷的高兴。我们祝愿中国人民在四个现代化的宏图和改革开放政策的指引下，为实现第七个五年计划的目标取得新的，辉煌的胜利。
    贺信说，借此机会，我们深切感谢你们对我们正在进行的争取民族解放、抵抗越南侵略的斗争给予了慷慨的支援。我们的斗争取得可喜的进展，这和你们的支援是分不开的。这种支援也为柬埔寨问题根据西哈努克亲王的五点建议获得全面解决作出了重大贡献。
