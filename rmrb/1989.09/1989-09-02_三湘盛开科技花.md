Title: The Blossoming of Science and Technology in the Three Xiang Region
Author: Jin Ke
Date: 1989-09-02
Page: 4

Three Xiang Region: A Blossoming of Science and Technology

· Hybrid Rice ·

The three-line hybrid rice system was first successfully researched in Hunan Province. Professor Yuan Longping, who led this research, received the National Special Invention Award, the United Nations Intellectual Property Invention Award, the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization Science Award, and the British Rank Prize.

· “Galaxy” Computer ·

China's first billion-operations-per-second supercomputer, the "Galaxy" machine, was born in Changsha at the National University of Defense Technology in 1983. In 1985, the university developed China's first "Galaxy" fully digital simulation computer, a type of super-high-speed computer with high computational accuracy, fast speed, high stability, and ease of use.

· Magnetic Levitation Train ·

On March 8, 1989, China's first magnetic levitation experimental train prototype was born in Hunan. The magnetic levitation train has no engine and no wheels; it relies on a massive magnetic force to lift the body off the track and is propelled forward by a linear induction motor, capable of speeds exceeding 500 kilometers per hour.

· Heterologous Test-Tube Baby ·

By June 7 of this year, China's first heterologous test-tube baby turned one year old. This male infant, weighing 3 kilograms, was born at the Second Affiliated Hospital of Hunan Medical University, marking that China's reproductive engineering research has reached an advanced world level.

· Biped Walking Robot ·

China's first biped walking robot, which reached the international advanced level of the 1980s, began walking in Hunan during the Spring Festival of 1988. It weighs 16 kilograms, stands 75 centimeters tall, and has a human-like leg structure with hip, knee, and ankle joints.

### 三湘盛开科技花
金柯
1989-09-02
第4版(国庆专版)
专栏：社会主义事业在前进

    三湘盛开科技花
    ·杂交水稻·
    杂交水稻三系配套，首先在湖南省研究成功。主持这项研究的袁隆平教授荣获国家特等发明奖和联合国知识产权发明奖、联合国科教文科学奖、英国郎克奖。
    ·“银河”计算机·
    我国第一台亿次巨型电子计算机——“银河”机，1983年诞生在长沙国防科技大学。1985年，该大学又研制出我国第一台“银河”全数字仿真计算机，这是一种超高速计算机，具有运算精度高、速度快、高稳定、使用方便等特点。
    ·磁悬浮列车·
    1989年3月8日，我国第一台磁悬浮实验列车样车诞生在湖南。磁悬浮列车没有引擎，没有轮子，靠巨大磁力使车身悬离轨道，由直线感应电机推动前进，时速可达500公里以上。
    ·异体试管婴儿·
    到今年6月7日，我国第一个异体试管婴儿满周岁了。这个体重3公斤重的男婴降生在湖南医科大学附二医院，标志着我国生殖工程的研究已达到了世界先进水平。
    ·步行机器人·
    我国第一台具有80年代国际先进水平的两足步行机器人，1988年春节期间开始在湖南行走，它重16公斤，高75厘米，外形像人的两腿，有胯、膝、脚腕等关节。（金　柯）
