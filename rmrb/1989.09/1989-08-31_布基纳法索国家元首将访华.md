Title: The Head of State of Burkina Faso to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-08-31
Edition: 1

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs spokesperson announced today that at the invitation of Chairman Yang Shangkun, the Chairman of the Popular Front for Burkina Faso, Head of State, and Prime Minister Captain Blaise Compaoré, along with his wife, will pay an official visit to China from September 7 to 12.

The spokesperson also announced at today's press conference that at the invitation of the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Arab Republic of Egypt, and the Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Syrian Arab Republic and the Republic of Tunisia, Foreign Minister Qian Qichen will pay friendly visits to the aforementioned four countries from September 16 to 26.

The spokesperson stated that at the invitation of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, a delegation from the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Vice Chairman Sun Shengbin, will pay a friendly visit to China from September 6 to 20.

### 布基纳法索国家元首将访华

1989-08-31
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    布基纳法索国家元首将访华
    新华社北京8月31日电　外交部发言人今天在此间宣布，应杨尚昆主席的邀请，布基纳法索人民阵线主席、国家元首、政府首脑布莱斯·孔波雷上尉偕夫人将于9月7日至12日对中国进行正式访问。
    这位发言人在今天下午举行的新闻发布会上还宣布，应约旦哈希姆王国副首相兼外交大臣、阿拉伯埃及共和国副总理兼外交部长、阿拉伯叙利亚共和国和突尼斯共和国外交部长的邀请，外交部长钱其琛将于9月16日至26日对上述四国进行友好访问。
    发言人说，应全国人大常委会的邀请，以孙圣弼副议长为团长的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议代表团将于9月6日至20日对中国进行友好访问。
