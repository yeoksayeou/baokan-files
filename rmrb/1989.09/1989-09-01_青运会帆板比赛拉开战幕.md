Title: The Youth Games Sailing Competition Kicks Off
Author: Mǎ Wéiqiū
Date: 1989-09-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Yingkou, September 1st

At 1:30 PM today, a starting gunshot echoed, and colorful sails competed on the sea surface of Gaizhou Bay in Yingkou. The second National Youth Games sailing competition officially began.

Sailing is a new water sports event added to the National Youth Games and is the 11th final event before the opening of this Youth Games. Teams from Anhui, Shanghai, Hebei, Guangdong, Shandong, Guangxi, Zhejiang, Hubei, Fujian, Sichuan, Hainan, and Liaoning will compete in the Men's and Women's Tempest and Men's and Women's DⅡ Type categories. The competition will consist of 7 rounds and will conclude on the 8th.

According to local experts, the gold medals for the four categories are likely to be won by the stronger teams from Shandong, Zhejiang, and Liaoning.

### 青运会帆板比赛拉开战幕
马维秋
1989-09-01
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    青运会帆板比赛拉开战幕
    新华社营口9月1日电　（通讯员马维秋）今天下午1时30分，一声发令枪响，营口盖县鲅鱼圈海面彩帆竞发，第二届全国青运会帆板比赛揭开战幕。
    帆板比赛是我国青运会新增的一个水上项目，也是本届青运会开幕前进行的第十一个决赛项目。来自安徽、上海、河北、广东、山东、广西、浙江、湖北、福建、四川、海南、辽宁的12支运动队，将进行温得色弗尔男子组、女子组和DⅡ型男子组、女子组4个项目的比赛。比赛将分7轮进行，8日结束。
    据此间行家分析，4个项目的金牌得主，可能在实力较强的山东、浙江、辽宁队之间产生。
