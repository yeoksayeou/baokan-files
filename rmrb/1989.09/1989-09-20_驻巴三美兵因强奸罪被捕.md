Title: Three U.S. Soldiers Arrested for Rape in Panama
Author:
Date: 1989-09-20
Edition: 3

Three U.S. soldiers have been arrested by Panamanian police for the rape of a Panamanian woman. The soldiers are Christian Johnson, James Strassinger, and Juan Ocacio, all of whom are stationed in the Panama Canal Zone.

The incident has sparked strong reactions across Panamanian society, as American soldiers in Panama have been involved in various misdeeds.

### 驻巴三美兵因强奸罪被捕

1989-09-20
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　驻巴三美兵因强奸罪被捕
    据巴拿马《晨报》18日报道，3个美国士兵因犯有强奸巴拿马妇女罪而被巴拿马警方逮捕。他们是美国驻巴拿马运河区部队士兵克里斯蒂安·约翰逊、詹姆斯·斯特拉辛格和胡安·奥卡西奥。
    美国兵在巴拿马首都为非作歹已在巴拿马社会各界引起强烈反响。
