Title: The Global Environmental Protection Conference Opens in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1989-09-11
Edition: 3

The "Global Environmental Protection Tokyo Conference," co-hosted by the Japanese government and the United Nations Environment Programme, opened on the morning of September 11 in Tokyo.

The main topic of this conference is how to address issues such as the destruction of the ozone layer, the reduction of tropical rainforests, global warming, and environmental pollution. Scientists and government officials from 23 countries, including China, Japan, the United States, and the United Kingdom, attended the conference.

### 全球环保会议在东京开幕

1989-09-11
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　全球环保会议在东京开幕
    由日本政府和联合国环境规划署共同主持的“全球环境保护东京会议”十一日上午在东京举行。
    这次会议的中心议题是如何治理臭氧层被破坏、热带雨林减少、地球气温趋暖、公害等全球性环境污染问题。来自中国、日本、美国、英国等二十三个国家的科学工作者和政府人士出席了会议。
