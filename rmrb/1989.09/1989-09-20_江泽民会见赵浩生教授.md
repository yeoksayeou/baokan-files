Jiang Zemin Meets with Professor Zhao Haosheng

	BEIJING, Sept. 19 (Xinhua) -- CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin met with retired professor Zhao Haosheng of Yale University in the afternoon of Sept. 19 at Zhongnanhai.
