Benxi Steel Utilizes "Ginseng Steel" to Develop a Batch of New Steel Varieties

Benxi, Liaoning - Benxi Iron and Steel Company has utilized its own high-quality "Ginseng Steel" to develop 36 unique new steel varieties. Among these, 5 items fill gaps in the national market, and 4 items have replaced imports, saving the country a significant amount of foreign exchange. In the first eight months of this year, the development of new products alone has generated over 40 million yuan in additional profits.

To replace imported steel with domestically produced steel, the company established a special leadership group for the development of high-quality special steel new products in 1985.

### 本钢利用人参铁开发一批新钢种
高显洲　张岩松
1989-09-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    本钢利用人参铁开发一批新钢种
    本报讯　辽宁本溪钢铁公司利用本厂生产的优质“人参铁”，研制开发出36个独具特色的新钢种，其中有5项填补了国家空白，有4项顶替了进口，为国家节约了大量外汇。今年头8个月，仅开发新产品一项就多创利4000多万元。
    为了用国产钢材顶替进口钢材，从1985年开始，这个公司专门成立了优质专用钢新产品开发领导小组。
      （高显洲　张岩松）
