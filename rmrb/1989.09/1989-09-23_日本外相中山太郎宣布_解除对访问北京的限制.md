Title: Japanese Foreign Minister Nakasone Tarō Announces Lifting of Restrictions on Visits to Beijing
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-09-23
Edition: 3

Japanese Foreign Minister Nakasone Tarō announced today morning at a press conference after the cabinet meeting that the Japanese government has decided to lift restrictions on Japanese citizens visiting Beijing starting from September 25th.

Regarding the third yen loan and other economic cooperation projects, the foreign minister stated that after further observing the trends of international financial institutions and the Chinese economy, a cautious study will be conducted.

### 日本外相中山太郎宣布  解除对访问北京的限制
于青
1989-09-23
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    日本外相中山太郎宣布
    解除对访问北京的限制
    本报东京9月22日电　记者于青报道：日本外相中山太郎今天上午在内阁会议后举行记者会见时宣布，日本政府决定从本月25日起，解除对日本人访问北京的限制。
    关于第三次日元贷款等经济合作项目，外相表示，待继续观察世界金融机构和中国经济的动向后，做慎重研究。
