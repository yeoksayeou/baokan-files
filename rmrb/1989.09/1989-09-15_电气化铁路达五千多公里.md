Title: Electric Railway Reaches Over 5,000 Kilometers
Author: Hou Jia Yin
Date: 1989-09-15
Edition: 1

The construction of electrified railways in China has made remarkable progress. By the end of August this year, the length of electrified railways had reached 5,835 kilometers, accounting for 11 percent of the total operational length of railways nationwide. Of this, 5,000 kilometers were built in the past ten years, which is five times the length of electrified railways built in the previous twenty years. Currently, there are seventeen electrified railways in operation, including the Baocheng Railway, Yang'an Railway, Shitai Railway, Chengyu Railway, and Beitongpu Railway.

### 电气化铁路达五千多公里
侯嘉荫
1989-09-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　电气化铁路达五千多公里
    据新华社北京电　（记者侯嘉荫）我国电气化铁路建设有了突飞猛进的发展。截至今年八月底，电气化里程已达五千八百三十五公里，占全国铁路总营运里程的百分之十一，其中近十年修建了五千公里，相当于前二十年电气化里程的五倍。目前建成通车的电气化铁路有宝成铁路、阳安铁路、石太铁路、成渝铁路、北同蒲铁路等十七条。
