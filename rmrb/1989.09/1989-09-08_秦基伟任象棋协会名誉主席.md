Title: Qin Jiwei Appointed Honorary Chairman of the Chinese Chess Association
Author:
Date: 1989-09-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 8th.

State Councilor and Minister of Defense Qin Jiwei today, at the closing ceremony of the 1989 Chinese Chess King Tournament held here, accepted the certificate of Honorary Chairman of the Chinese Chess Association from the Chairman of the Chinese Olympic Committee, He Zhenliang.

Qin Jiwei presented the winning trophy to the new chess king, Li Laiqun, at the closing ceremony and extended his congratulations.

### 秦基伟任象棋协会名誉主席

1989-09-08
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    秦基伟任象棋协会名誉主席
    本报北京9月8日讯　国务委员、国防部长秦基伟今天在这里结束的1989年中国象棋棋王赛的闭幕式上，接受了中国奥委会主席何振梁给予他的中国象棋协会名誉主席的证书。
    秦基伟在闭幕式上向新棋王李来群颁发了优胜奖杯并表示祝贺。
