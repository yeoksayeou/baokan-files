Title: China Wishes Burkina Faso Stability and Development
Author:
Date: 1989-09-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 21st.

China wishes Burkina Faso social stability and economic development.

This was stated by Foreign Ministry spokesman Li Zhaoxing at a press conference today when responding to a question about the recent unrest in Burkina Faso. Li Zhaoxing said that Burkina Faso's head of state, Blaise Compaoré, had a "successful" visit to China this month. He added that relations between China and Burkina Faso have always been good.

### 中国祝愿布基纳法索稳定和发展

1989-09-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中国祝愿布基纳法索稳定和发展
    新华社北京9月21日电　外交部发言人李肇星今天在新闻发布会上说，中国祝愿布基纳法索社会稳定、经济发展。
    他是在回答记者提出有关布基纳法索不久前发生动乱的问题时作此表示的。李肇星说，布基纳法索国家元首布莱斯·孔波雷这个月对中国的访问是“成功的”。他说，中布两国的关系一直发展得很好。
