Title: Rodriguez, Interim President, Says U.S. Provoked Panama's Political Crisis
Author: Hu Tai Ran
Date: 1989-09-04
Edition: 3

Panama's interim President Francisco Rodriguez emphasized in a nationwide broadcast tonight that the U.S. government provoked the current political crisis in Panama, and therefore, it must be resolved through extraordinary means.

When discussing President Bush's announcement that the U.S. does not recognize Panama's interim government and will not engage in diplomatic contact with it, Rodriguez stated, "As a people who love peace, Panamanians 'have never, and will never, want to be enemies of the American people.' He said, 'If the current U.S. government's foreign policy can break free from the fictitious myths created by the appendages of previous administrations, I believe that many important U.S. interests are actually in harmony with the national interests of Panama.'

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Panama City on September 3, Panama's newly appointed interim President Francisco Rodriguez announced the formation of a new cabinet, which will take office on the morning of September 4.

The new cabinet consists of 12 ministers. Apart from the foreign minister, whose appointment has not yet been announced, five others are former ministers from the previous government.

### 罗德里格斯临时总统指出  美挑起巴拿马政治危机
胡泰然
1989-09-04
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    罗德里格斯临时总统指出
    美挑起巴拿马政治危机
    据新华社巴拿马城9月1日电　巴拿马临时总统弗朗西斯科·罗德里格斯今晚在向全国发表广播电视讲话中强调指出，美国政府挑起巴目前这场政治危机，因此必须通过一种不寻常的方法来解决它。
    在谈到布什总统宣布美国不承认巴拿马临时政府并不同它进行外交接触时，罗德里格斯表示，作为热爱和平的人民，巴拿马人“过去、现在和将来都不愿意成为美国人民的敌人”。他说，“如果美国现政府的外交政策要摆脱前几任政府的附属机构制造的虚构神话，我相信，美国许多重要利益恰恰是同巴拿马国家利益相协调的。”
    据新华社巴拿马城9月3日电　（记者胡泰然）巴拿马新任临时总统弗朗西斯科·罗德里格斯今天宣布组成新内阁，9月4日上午就职。
    新内阁仍由12名部长组成。除外长人选暂未公布外，其他有5人是前政府部长。
