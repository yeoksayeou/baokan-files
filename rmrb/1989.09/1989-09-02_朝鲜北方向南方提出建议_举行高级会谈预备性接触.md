Title: North Korea Proposes High-Level Talks with the South
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-09-02
Edition: 3

North Korea has proposed to the South to hold preparatory contacts for high-level talks.

	Pyeongyang, September 2 (Xinhua) — Reporter Xu Baokang: The North Korean delegation for the preparatory contacts for high-level political and military talks between North and South Korea, led by Baek Nam-jun, called South Korean chief representative Song Han-ho on September 1. Baek proposed that the third round of vice-ministerial-level preparatory contacts be held on September 18 at the "Peace House" on the South Korean side of Panmunjom to discuss the preparations for high-level political and military talks between North and South Korea.

	Baek said in the phone call that to achieve reconciliation and unification between North and South, it is necessary to conduct dialogues and contacts between representatives of various levels and individuals, in addition to government-level talks.

	The vice-ministerial-level talks between North and South Korea, which were suspended in March this year, have now lasted for six months.

### 朝鲜北方向南方提出建议  举行高级会谈预备性接触
徐宝康
1989-09-02
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    朝鲜北方向南方提出建议
    举行高级会谈预备性接触
    本报平壤9月2日电　记者徐宝康报道：朝鲜北南高级政治军事会谈预备性接触北方代表团团长白南俊1日打电话给南方代表团首席代表宋汉虎，建议9月18日在板门店南方一侧的“和平之家”举行第3次副部长级预备性接触，共商筹备举行北南高级政治军事会谈。
    白南俊在电话中说，为实现北南之间的和解和统一，既要进行当局之间的会谈，又要开展各阶层代表以及个别人士之间的对话和接触。
    朝鲜北南副部长级会谈今年3月中断以来，至今已达6个月之久。
