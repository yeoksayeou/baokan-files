Title: The World Tourism Organization Conference Closes
Author:
Date: 1989-09-03
Page: 3

The eighth session of the World Tourism Organization Conference concluded on September 2 in Paris. More than 500 representatives from 106 member countries or associated countries, including China, attended the meeting.

The conference opened on August 28. The main topics discussed were how to improve tourism services, expand the scope of the tourism industry, and adapt to the development of the global tourism industry in the new situation.

### 世界旅游组织大会闭幕

1989-09-03
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    世界旅游组织大会闭幕
    第八届世界旅游组织大会九月二日在巴黎闭幕，来自中国等一百零六个成员国或联系国的五百多位代表出席了会议。
    这次大会于八月二十八日开幕，主要议题是在新的形势下，如何改进旅游业的服务、扩大旅游业的范围以及如何适应世界旅游业的发展。
