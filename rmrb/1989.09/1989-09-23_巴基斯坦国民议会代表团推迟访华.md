Title: Pakistani National Assembly Delegation Delays Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1989-09-23
Edition: 2

A reporter learned today from the office of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress that the delegation of the Pakistani National Assembly, led by Speaker Mairaj Khalid, which was originally scheduled to visit from September 24 to October 2, has postponed its visit to China due to domestic reasons.

### 巴基斯坦国民议会代表团推迟访华

1989-09-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    巴基斯坦国民议会代表团推迟访华
    新华社北京9月22日电　记者今天从全国人大常委会办公厅获悉，原定9月24日至10月2日来访的由议长迈拉吉·哈立德率领的巴基斯坦国民议会代表团因其国内原因推迟访华。
