Title: The Establishment of the Chinese Society of Marine Law
Author:
Date: 1989-09-17
Edition: 2

The Chinese Society of Marine Law held its inaugural conference in Beijing from September 13 to 15.

The conference elected Judge Nie Zhengxi of the International Court of Justice and former Director of the State Oceanic Administration Luo Yiru as honorary presidents, and Vice Director of the State Oceanic Administration Chen Bingxin as president.

The primary tasks of the society will be to vigorously promote the study of marine law, to engage in academic exchanges on marine law issues, and to provide consultation and advice on marine legislation to relevant authorities.

### 海洋法学会成立

1989-09-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　海洋法学会成立
    本报讯　中国海洋法学会9月13日至15日在北京举行首届大会。
    这次会议推举国际法院法官倪征?、前国家海洋局局长罗钰如为名誉会长，国家海洋局副局长陈炳鑫为会长。
    大力促进对海洋法的研究，开展海洋法问题的学术交流活动，并向有关主管部门提供海洋立法的咨询意见和建议，将成为这个学会的主要任务。
