Title: Coal Miner Dormitories
Author: Duzonghe
Date: 1989-09-24
Edition: 1

The Yanzhou Mining Bureau's Xinglongzhuang Coal Mine has constructed a 22-story dormitory building for coal miners, with a total floor area of 18,000 square meters. The dormitory is equipped with complete facilities, is clean and hygienic, and provides a quiet and comfortable environment. It has created a good setting for miners to study, rest, and entertain themselves.

### 矿工公寓（图片）
杜宗河
1989-09-24
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    矿工公寓
    山东兖州矿务局兴隆庄煤矿盖了一座22层的矿工公寓，建筑面积1.8万平方米。公寓内部设施齐全，清洁卫生，安静舒适，为矿工创造了一个良好的学习、休息和娱乐的环境。
            杜宗河摄
