Date: 1989-09-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 24th.

    Foreign Affairs Brief
    ▲Vice Premier and Governor of the People's Bank of China, Li Guixian, led a delegation to leave Beijing for Washington, D.C., on September 23 to attend the 44th Annual Meeting of the International Monetary Fund and the World Bank.

    The meeting is scheduled to open on September 26 and conclude on September 28.

    ▲Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Honorary President of the All-China Women's Federation, Kang Keqing, met and hosted a banquet for the Thai Women's Delegation led by Chen Shuqing, Advisor to the Thai Women's Institute and former President, on September 23 in Beijing.

    ▲Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the All-China Women's Federation, Chen Muhua, met with the Hong Kong Women's Delegation led by Kong Aijiu, Vice Chairman of the Women's Committee of the Chinese General Chamber of Commerce in Hong Kong, and Zhu Lianfen, Director of the Chinese General Chamber of Commerce in Hong Kong, on September 22 in the Great Hall of the People.

    ▲Vice Premier and Minister of Public Security, Wang Fang, met with the delegation from the Ministry of the Interior's Traffic Management Department of the German Democratic Republic, led by Major General Herbert Marli, Director of the Traffic Management Bureau, on September 22 in the Great Hall of the People.

    ▲Director of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office, Ji Pengfei, met with 21 cultural and sports figures from Hong Kong who came to Beijing to observe the Chinese Film Festival and participate in the Second China Arts Festival and the Asian Men's Basketball Tournament on September 22 at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

    ▲President of the Supreme People's Court, Ren Jianxin, hosted a dinner for Philippine Supreme Court Justice Navas and his wife on the evening of September 22 in the Great Hall of the People. The hosts and guests had a friendly discussion on further strengthening cooperation between the two countries' legal communities.

    ▲The Ministry of Culture held a banquet on September 22 at the Kunlun Hotel in Beijing to welcome the renowned Bolshoi Ballet of the Soviet Union, which consists of 130 members. The ballet troupe arrived in China on September 8 according to the Sino-Soviet cultural exchange plan. The dancers have already performed eight shows in Nanjing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou, all of which were warmly received.

### 外事简讯

1989-09-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员兼中国人民银行行长李贵鲜9月23日率团离开北京前往美国华盛顿出席国际货币基金组织和世界银行第44届联合年会。
    本届年会定于9月26日开幕，9月28日结束。
    ▲全国政协副主席、全国妇联名誉主席康克清23日在北京会见并宴请了以泰国妇女院顾问、前主席占沙蒙·瓦他纳威肯
  （陈淑卿）为团长的泰国妇女代表团。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、全国妇联主席陈慕华9月22日在人民大会堂会见由团长、香港中华总商会妇女委员会副主任孔爱菊和副团长、香港中华总商会会董朱莲芬率领的香港妇女考察团。
    ▲国务委员兼公安部部长王芳9月22日在人民大会堂会见了由交通管理局局长赫里伯尔特·马利少将率领的德意志民主共和国内务部交通管理代表团。
    ▲国务院港澳办公室主任姬鹏飞9月22日在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了前来北京观摩中国电影节的香港电影界人士以及参加第二届中国艺术节和亚洲男子篮球赛的文化体育人士共21人。
    ▲最高人民法院院长任建新9月22日晚上在人民大会堂会见并设宴招待菲律宾最高法院大法官纳瓦萨和夫人。宾主就进一步加强两国法律界之间的合作进行了友好的谈话。
    ▲文化部9月22日晚在北京昆仑饭店举行宴会，款待名闻遐迩的苏联国家模范大剧院芭蕾舞团一行130余人。大剧院芭蕾舞团是根据中苏文化交流计划于8日来华的。舞蹈家们已先后在南京、上海、杭州演出8场，均受到热烈欢迎。
                                      （据新华社）
