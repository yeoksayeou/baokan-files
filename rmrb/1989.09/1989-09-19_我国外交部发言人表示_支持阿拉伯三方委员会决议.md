Title: China's Ministry of Foreign Affairs Spokesperson Expresses Support for the Resolution of the Arab Tripartite Committee
Author:
Date: 1989-09-19
Edition: 2

The spokesperson for China's Ministry of Foreign Affairs said today that the Chinese government actively supports the unremitting efforts of the Arab Tripartite Committee to resolve the Lebanese crisis. The spokesperson also highly evaluates and supports the resolution issued by the Tripartite Committee on September 16, which provides a new opportunity for achieving a comprehensive ceasefire and national reconciliation in Lebanon.

The spokesperson said that the Chinese government is deeply concerned about the severe situation in Lebanon and expresses deep sympathy for the Lebanese people who have long suffered from the ravages of war.

The spokesperson said: "We sincerely hope that all parties involved in the Lebanese conflict will prioritize the greater good and actively cooperate with the Tripartite Committee's goodwill efforts, making their own beneficial contributions to the political resolution of the Lebanese issue."

### 我国外交部发言人表示　支持阿拉伯三方委员会决议

1989-09-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　我国外交部发言人表示
　　支持阿拉伯三方委员会决议
    新华社北京9月18日电　外交部发言人今天说，中国政府积极支持阿拉伯三方委员会为解决黎巴嫩危机所做的不懈努力，并高度评价和支持三方委员会本月16日发表的决议，它为在黎巴嫩实现全面停火和民族和解提供了新的契机。
    他说，中国政府对黎巴嫩的严峻局势十分关切，并对黎巴嫩人民长期蒙受战乱之灾深表同情。
    发言人说：“我们衷心希望黎巴嫩冲突各方以大局为重，积极配合三方委员会的善意努力，为政治解决黎巴嫩问题作出自己有益的贡献”。
