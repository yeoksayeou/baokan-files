Title: Treasures Abound Across the Vast Grasslands
Author: Zhang Yuling
Date: 1989-09-13
Edition: 4

The phrase "East forests, West iron, South grain, North livestock, and coal everywhere" vividly describes the natural resources of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. Consider the following statistics:

    △ The grassland area of Inner Mongolia is approximately 880,000 square kilometers, with 690,000 square kilometers of effective natural pastures, ranking first in the nation. This year, the total number of livestock in the region reached 47.5 million head (units), setting a historical record.

    △ The total forest area in Inner Mongolia is 265,000 acres, with a per capita forest area of 14.3 acres and a per capita timber volume of 151 cubic meters, both ranking first in the nation.

    △ The coal-bearing area in the region is 92,000 square kilometers, with a potential reserve of over 1 trillion tons. Currently, four of the nation's five largest open-pit coal mines are located in Inner Mongolia. There are 15 mines with reserves exceeding 1.5 billion tons.

    △ The Baiyun Obo iron mine is one of the few rare earth iron ore deposits in China. This mine contains 12 types of iron ores, 12 types of rare earth ores, and 26 other useful minerals, with immense economic value and a promising future.

    △ The reserves of rare earth oxides in Inner Mongolia amount to 100 million tons, with industrial reserves of 38 million tons, accounting for 97% of the nation's total. This is five times the combined reserves of all other countries in the world.

    △ Inner Mongolia has abundant natural soda reserves, with 55 million tons already proven, making it the leader in the nation. This ensures the development of China's chemical industry.

    △ Inner Mongolia's production of potatoes, sugar beets, cashmere, castor seeds, licorice, astragalus, ephedra, wolfberry, zeolite, salt, dairy products, and shellac ranks among the top in the country.

### 千里草原遍地宝
张玉岭
1989-09-13
第4版(国庆专版)
专栏：社会主义事业在前进

　　千里草原遍地宝
    “东林西铁、南粮北牧遍地煤”，是人们对内蒙古自治区自然资源的形象描述。请看几组数字：
    △内蒙古的草原面积约88万平方公里，有效天然牧场69万平方公里，居全国第一。今年，全区大小牲畜达到4750万头（只），创历史最高水平。
    △内蒙古的林地总面积26.5万亩，人均有林面积14.3亩，人均占有木材量151立方米，均居全国之首。
    △全区含煤面积达9.2万平方公里，远景储量达1万亿吨以上。目前全国五大露天煤矿有4个在内蒙古。储量在15亿吨以上的矿有15个。
    △白云鄂博铁矿为我国目前少有的含铌稀土铁矿床。这种矿含各种铁矿12种、稀土矿12种、其它有用矿26种，经济价值前途无量。
    △内蒙古的稀土氧化物储量达1亿吨，工业储量达3800万吨，占全国97％，为世界其他各国储量总和的5倍。
    △内蒙古的天然碱储量极丰，现已探明的就达5500万吨，为全国之冠。它为发展我国的化工工业提供了保证。
    △内蒙古的土豆、甜菜、山羊绒、蓖麻籽、甘草、黄芪、麻黄、肉苁蓉、麦饭石、食盐、乳制品、栲胶等，其产量在国内名列前茅。
            （张玉岭辑）
