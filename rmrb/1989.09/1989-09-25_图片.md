On May 1, 1963, Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife Wang Guangmei visited Cambodia on a friendly visit. Queen Kossamak of Cambodia and Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk welcomed them at the airport. Two hundred thousand people lined the streets of Phnom Penh to welcome them.
