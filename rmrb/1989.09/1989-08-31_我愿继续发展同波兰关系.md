Title: I Am Willing to Continue Developing Relations with Poland
Author:
Date: 1989-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 31st.

Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Li Zhaoxing today said that the composition of the Polish government is Poland's own affair. China is willing to continue maintaining and developing friendly cooperative relations with Poland on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

Li Zhaoxing made these remarks at a press conference in response to a question from a foreign journalist.

He said that China will continue to proceed from its own national conditions, adhere to the Four Cardinal Principles, persist in the policy of reform and opening up, and build socialism with Chinese characteristics.

### 我愿继续发展同波兰关系

1989-08-31
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    我愿继续发展同波兰关系
    新华社北京8月31日电　外交部发言人李肇星今天说，波兰政府的组成是波兰自己的事情。中国愿意在和平共处五项原则的基础上继续保持和发展同波兰的友好合作关系。
    李肇星是在新闻发布会上回答外国记者的问题时说这番话的。
    他说，中国将继续从自己的国情出发，坚持四项基本原则，坚持改革开放的方针，建设有中国特色的社会主义。
