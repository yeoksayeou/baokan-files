Title: The 5th Far East and South Pacific Region Disabled Games Opens
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-09-15
Edition: 3

The 5th Far East and South Pacific Region Disabled Games opened this afternoon at the Kobe University track and field stadium. A total of 1,650 disabled athletes and coaches from 41 countries and regions attended the opening ceremony, including 56 Chinese disabled athletes. This is the largest disabled games held to date, and it is also the first time China has sent the most athletes. The Crown Prince of Japan and the Minister of Health gave speeches at the opening ceremony.

This games features 13 events: archery, athletics, badminton, fencing, judo, lawn bowling, swimming, shooting, table tennis, weightlifting, wheelchair basketball, wheelchair tennis, and football. The wheelchair basketball competition began today. Chinese athletes will compete in athletics, swimming, table tennis, and shooting.

The Chinese delegation, led by Vice Chairman Zhu Deru of the Chinese Disabled Persons' Federation, arrived in Kobe in two groups on September 10 and 12, totaling 80 people. The Japanese delegation has 545 athletes, making it the largest delegation at the games. Taiwan, China, sent 36 athletes to participate for the first time.

### 远东及南太平洋地区伤残人运动会开幕　我五十六名选手角逐四个项目
于青
1989-09-15
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　远东及南太平洋地区伤残人运动会开幕
　　我五十六名选手角逐四个项目
    本报东京9月15日电　记者于青报道：今天下午，第五届远东及南太平洋地区伤残人运动会在神户大学生田径运动场开幕。来自41个国家和地区的1650名伤残人运动员和教练员出席了开幕式，其中有56名中国的伤残人运动员。这是迄今规模最大的一次伤残人运动会，也是中国派出选手最多的一次。日本皇太子和厚生大臣在开幕式上致辞。
    本届运动会设有13个比赛项目：射箭、田径、羽毛球、击剑、柔道、草地滚球、游泳、射击、乒乓球、举重、轮椅篮球、轮椅网球、足球。今天轮椅篮球的比赛已经开始。中国选手只参加田径、游泳、乒乓球、射击四个项目。
    以中国伤残人体协副主席朱德录为团长的中国伤残人体育代表团，一行80人于本月10日和12日分两批飞赴神户。日本代表团有545名运动员参赛，是出席大会的最大代表团。中国台北派出36名运动员第一次参赛。
