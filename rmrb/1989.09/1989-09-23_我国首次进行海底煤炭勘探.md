Title: China Conducts First Seabed Coal Exploration
Author: Lü Zongkuan
Date: 1989-09-23
Edition: 2

China's first seabed coal resource exploration work, organized jointly by the China Coal Research Institute, Shandong Mining Institute, Shanghai Marine Geology Bureau, and Shandong Longkou Mining Bureau, officially began in early September. In the shallow sea about three kilometers north of Longkou City, the drilling platform drilled the first borehole.

According to relevant experts, conducting engineering geological exploration of seabed coal resources is a first in China. This exploration will provide a scientific basis for China to carry out seabed coal mining and is of great significance for the development and utilization of marine resources.

### 我国首次进行海底煤炭勘探
吕宗宽
1989-09-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    我国首次进行海底煤炭勘探
    本报讯　由中国煤炭科学研究院、山东矿院、上海海洋地质局和山东龙口矿务局联合组织的渤海湾海底煤炭资源勘探工作，九月初正式开始。在龙口市市区北约三公里的浅海中，钻井平台打下了第一个钻孔。
    据有关专家介绍，进行海底煤炭资源的工程地质勘探，在我国还是首次。这次勘探，将为我国进行海底原煤开采提供科学依据，对于海洋资源的开发和利用等也具有重大意义。　（吕宗宽）
