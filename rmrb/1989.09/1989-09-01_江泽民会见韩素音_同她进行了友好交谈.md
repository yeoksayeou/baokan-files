Title: Jiang Zemin Meets with Han Suyin
Author:
Date: 1989-09-01
Edition: 1

Jiang Zemin met with British writer Han Suyin this afternoon in Zhongnanhai and had a friendly conversation with her.

New China News Agency, Beijing, September 1—The General Secretary of the Communist Party of China, Jiang Zemin, met with British writer Han Suyin this afternoon in Zhongnanhai and had a friendly conversation with her.

### 江泽民会见韩素音  同她进行了友好交谈

1989-09-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民会见韩素音
    同她进行了友好交谈
    新华社北京九月一日电　中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在中南海会见英籍女作家韩素音，同她进行了友好的交谈。
