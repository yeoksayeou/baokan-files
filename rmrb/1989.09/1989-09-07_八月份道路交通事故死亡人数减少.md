Title: Road Traffic Accident Deaths Decrease in August
Author:
Date: 1989-09-07
Edition: 2

In August, there were a total of 22,938 road traffic accidents nationwide, resulting in 4,396 deaths and 14,701 injuries. The direct economic loss amounted to 31.44 million yuan. Compared to the same period last year, the number of accidents, deaths, and injuries decreased by 6.76%, 10.7%, and 6.88% respectively, while economic losses increased by 1.73%. Since the beginning of this year, the overall trend of traffic accidents has been stable with a downward trend. However, since June, there have been frequent major and serious accidents, especially those involving more than 10 deaths, which have increased significantly compared to last year.

### 八月份道路交通事故死亡人数减少

1989-09-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    八月份道路交通事故死亡人数减少
    新华社北京9月7日电　8月份，全国共发生道路交通事故22938起，死亡4396人，受伤14701人，直接经济损失3144万元。与去年同期比较，事故次数和死、伤人数分别减少6.76％、10.7％和6.88％，经济损失增加1.73％。今年以来，交通事故总的趋势是稳中有降，但自6月份以来重大恶性事故屡屡发生，特别是一次死亡10人以上的恶性事故比去年明显增多。
