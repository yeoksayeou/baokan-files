Title: A Foreign AIDS Virus Carrier Leaves Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1989-09-13
Edition: 2

A foreign AIDS virus carrier was today expelled from Shanghai by the Shanghai Health and Quarantine Bureau of the People's Republic of China at 10:25 AM and left the country by air.

The Shanghai Health and Quarantine Bureau has been conducting AIDS monitoring on people entering and leaving the country since April 1987. On September 9 of this year, during a monitoring of foreign workers in Shanghai, the bureau discovered this foreign AIDS virus carrier.

### 一外籍艾滋病毒感染者离沪

1989-09-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　一外籍艾滋病毒感染者离沪
    本报上海9月13日电　今天上午10时25分，一名外籍艾滋病病毒感染者被中华人民共和国上海卫生检疫所依法“请出”上海，乘飞机离境。
    上海卫生检疫所从1987年4月开始对出入境的人员实施艾滋病监测。今年9月9日，该所对来沪工作的外籍人员实施监测时，发现了这例外籍艾滋病病毒感染者。
