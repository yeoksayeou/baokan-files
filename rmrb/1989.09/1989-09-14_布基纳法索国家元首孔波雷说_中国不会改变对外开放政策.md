Title: Burkina Faso Head of State Compaore Says China Will Not Change Its Open Policy
Author:
Date: 1989-09-14
Edition: 3

Burkina Faso Head of State Compaore Says
China Will Not Change Its Open Policy

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 13th - According to Jiji Press, Burkina Faso People's Front Chairman, Head of State, and Prime Minister Blaise Compaore said tonight during his meeting with Japanese Prime Minister Kiichi Miyazawa, "China's open policy will not retreat."

Compaore had just concluded his visit to China before arriving in Japan. Miyazawa, during his meeting with Compaore, said that he felt reassured after hearing Compaore's assessment of the situation in China.

Compaore also discussed bilateral relations between Burkina Faso and Japan with Miyazawa. He hoped that the friendly relations between the two countries would continue and expressed his gratitude for Japan's aid to Burkina Faso.

### 布基纳法索国家元首孔波雷说　中国不会改变对外开放政策

1989-09-14
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　布基纳法索国家元首孔波雷说
　　中国不会改变对外开放政策
    新华社东京9月13日电　据时事社报道，正在此间访问的布基纳法索人民阵线主席、国家元首、政府首脑布莱斯·孔波雷今晚在同日本首相海部会谈时说，“中国的开放政策不会倒退”。
    孔波雷是刚刚结束对中国的访问后来到日本的。海部同孔波雷会谈时说，听了孔波雷对中国形势的判断，他感到放心。
    孔波雷还同海部就布日两国关系问题进行了交谈，他希望两国间的友好关系能继续下去，并对日本给予布基纳法索的援助表示感谢。
