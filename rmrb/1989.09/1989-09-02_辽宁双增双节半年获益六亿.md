Title: Liaoning Achieves Six Hundred Million Yuan in Benefits from Double Increase and Double Saving Campaign in the First Half of the Year
Author: Ma Yi
Date: 1989-09-02
Edition: 2

The "Double Increase and Double Saving" campaign organized by the Liaoning Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, aiming for "every worker donates 100 yuan, ten million workers contribute ten billion yuan," has shown significant results in the first half of the year. The campaign has achieved benefits totaling 630 million yuan, completing 63% of the annual target.

Workers across Liaoning Province have taken on the role of masters, utilizing their wisdom and creativity to offer suggestions and advice to their enterprises. In the first half of the year alone, workers in Anshan City proposed over 72,000 rationalization suggestions, of which 26,000 were adopted. These suggestions have resulted in increased revenue and savings of 112 million yuan, achieving 74% of the annual target.

### 辽宁双增双节半年获益六亿
马义
1989-09-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    辽宁双增双节半年获益六亿
    新华社沈阳九月二日电　（记者马义）辽宁省总工会组织开展的“全省职工人均献百元，千万职工争创十亿元”的双增双节立功竞赛活动，上半年已见成效，共获益六点三亿元，已完成全年目标的百分之六十三。
    辽宁省广大职工以主人翁姿态，发挥自己的聪明才智，纷纷向企业出谋献策提建议。上半年，仅鞍山市职工提出的合理化建议就有七万二千多条，已采纳二万六千多条，增收节支达一点一二亿元，完成了全年目标的百分之七十四。
