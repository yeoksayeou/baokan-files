Title: Kaysone Phomvihane to Visit China at the Invitation of Premier Li Peng
Author:
Date: 1989-09-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 28th.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs spokesperson announced today that at the invitation of Premier Li Peng of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Kaysone Phomvihane, Chairman of the Council of Ministers and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party of the Lao People's Democratic Republic, will pay an official friendly visit to China from October 5 to 12.

### 应李鹏总理邀请  凯山·丰威汉将访华

1989-09-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    应李鹏总理邀请  凯山·丰威汉将访华
    本报北京9月28日讯　外交部发言人今天宣布，应中华人民共和国国务院总理李鹏的邀请，老挝人民民主共和国部长会议主席、老挝人民革命党中央委员会总书记凯山·丰威汉，将于10月5日至12日对中国进行正式友好访问。
