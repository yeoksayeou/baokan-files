Title: China Team Breaks 3 World Records, Wins 24 Gold Medals on First Day of Fifth Far East and South Pacific Disabled Games

Author: Yu Qing

Date: 1989-09-17

Page: 3

The fifth Far East and South Pacific Disabled Games began today. The Chinese team broke three world records in track and field and swimming events, winning 24 gold medals, 7 silver medals, and 3 bronze medals.

The three world records broken by the Chinese team are as follows: Liu Wenhua, a male athlete from Guizhou Province with high-level amputations of both legs, broke the world record for the 100-meter butterfly in the A1 category with a time of 1 minute 21.83 seconds; Ke Yuxian, a 25-year-old seamstress from Huangshi City, Hubei Province, with a high-level amputation of the right leg, broke the world record for the A2 category long jump with a leap of 3 meters, surpassing the previous record of 2.66 meters. This is the first world record broken at this games; the Chinese team, consisting of Wang Jianqing, Qiu Xuewen, Sun Changting, and Wang Huiwen, broke the world record for the 4x400-meter relay, achieving a time of 3 minutes 54.1 seconds and defeating the Australian team, which won the gold medal in the same event at the Seoul Paralympics. Sun Changting is a first-class meritorious soldier from the Battle of Laoshan, and Qiu Xuewen is a farmer from Chaoyang, Liaoning Province.

Today, the Chinese team won 12 of their 24 gold medals in track and field events.

### 第五届远东及南太平洋地区伤残人运动会首日比赛　中国队破3项世界纪录获24枚金牌
于青
1989-09-17
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　第五届远东及南太平洋地区伤残人运动会首日比赛
　　中国队破3项世界纪录获24枚金牌
    本报东京9月16日电　记者于青报道：第五届远东及南太平洋地区伤残人运动会今天开始比赛。中国队在田径和游泳比赛中打破3项伤残人世界纪录、获得24枚金牌、7枚银牌、3枚铜牌。
    中国队打破的3项世界纪录是：来自贵州省双腿高位截肢的A1级男子选手刘文华，以1分21秒83的成绩打破了这个级别100米蝶泳的世界纪录；来自湖北省黄石市25岁的缝纫女工、右下肢高位截肢的A2级选手柯玉仙，以3米的成绩打破了2米66的这个级别的跳远世界纪录，这是本届运动会打破的第一项世界纪录；由王建清、仇学文、孙长亭、王辉赟四名选手组成的中国代表队，打破了4×400米接力比赛的世界纪录，成绩是3分54秒1，并击败了汉城伤残人奥运会这个项目的冠军澳大利亚代表队。中国选手孙长亭是老山作战的一等功臣，仇学文是辽宁省朝阳地区的农民。
    今天中国队获得的24枚金牌中12枚是在田径比赛中获得的。
