Title: The 13th Meeting of the Sino-British Joint Liaison Group to be Held in London
Author:
Date: 1989-09-23
Edition: 2

The 13th meeting of the Sino-British Joint Liaison Group will be held in London from September 27 to 29, 1989. The Chinese delegation will be led by Chief Representative Ambassador Ke Zaixiu, who will head the Chinese delegation to London for the meeting.

Other members of the delegation include Chinese representatives: Zheng Weirong (Resident Representative), Qiao Zonghui (Deputy Director of Xinhua News Agency's Hong Kong Branch), Wang Jiaji (Resident Representative), Zhang Youyun (Counsellor of the Chinese Embassy in the United Kingdom), as well as relevant experts and staff.

### 中英联合联络小组会议将在伦敦举行

1989-09-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中英联合联络小组会议将在伦敦举行
    新华社香港9月22日电　据此间中英联合联络小组中方代表处消息，中英联合联络小组第13次会议将于1989年9月27日至29日在伦敦举行。中方首席代表柯在铄大使将率领中方代表团赴伦敦出席会议。
    代表团其他成员包括中方代表：郑伟荣（常驻代表）、乔宗淮（新华社香港分社副社长）、王家骥（常驻代表）、张幼云（中国驻英国大使馆参赞）以及有关专家和工作人员。
