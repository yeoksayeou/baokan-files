Title: Ministry of Public Security Issues Notice: National Implementation of Resident ID Card Usage and Verification System Starting September 15th
Author:
Date: 1989-09-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 8th.

Notice of the Ministry of Public Security of the People's Republic of China on the Implementation of the Resident ID Card Usage and Verification System Nationwide

Since the implementation of the "Regulations on the Resident ID Card of the People's Republic of China" and its detailed rules, the issuance of resident ID cards has been basically completed in most regions across the country. The conditions for implementing the resident ID card usage and verification system nationwide are now in place. To prove the identity of citizens, facilitate citizens in engaging in political, economic, and other social activities, safeguard the legal rights of citizens, strengthen social management, and maintain social order, with the approval of the State Council, it is decided that starting from September 15, 1989, the resident ID card usage and verification system will be implemented nationwide. The relevant matters are hereby notified as follows:

1. Citizens should carry their resident ID cards with them when going out to facilitate usage or verification.

2. The resident ID card is applicable to the following matters that require proof of identity:

   (1) Voter registration;
   (2) Household registration;
   (3) Military registration;
   (4) Marriage registration;
   (5) Enrollment and employment;
   (6) Notarization services;
   (7) Travel to border management areas;
   (8) Applying for exit procedures;
   (9) Participation in litigation activities;
   (10) Handling of motor vehicle, boat driver's licenses and vehicle registration certificates, non-motor vehicle licenses;
   (11) Issuance of individual business licenses;
   (12) Handling of personal credit matters;
   (13) Participation in social insurance, receiving social relief;
   (14) Handling of civil aviation flight procedures;
   (15) Checking in at hotels for registration;
   (16) Withdrawal of remittances and mail;
   (17) Mailing items for sale;
   (18) Handling of other matters.

3. All levels of administrative management departments, enterprises, institutions, people's organizations, and relevant departments in society, when handling matters involving citizens' rights and requiring proof of identity, should verify the photograph and registered information of the ID card holder and record the certificate number.

   No handling unit or staff member shall, for any reason, detain or hold the resident ID card of a citizen as collateral.

4. Public security organs have the right to verify the resident ID cards of citizens when performing duties. Citizens being verified shall not refuse.

   Public security officers performing duties shall first present their identification when verifying the resident ID cards of citizens.

5. For violations of the resident ID card management, minor cases should be subject to criticism and education; serious cases constituting violations of public order management shall be punished according to the "Regulations on Penalties for Public Order Disruption." Those who forge, alter resident ID cards, or steal resident ID cards with serious circumstances constituting a crime shall be held criminally responsible according to Article 167 of the "Criminal Law of the People's Republic of China."

This is hereby notified.

### 公安部发出通告  居民身份证使用查验制度  九月十五日起在全国实施

1989-09-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    公安部发出通告
    居民身份证使用查验制度
    九月十五日起在全国实施
    新华社北京9月8日电　中华人民共和国公安部关于在全国实施居民身份证使用和查验制度的通告
    《中华人民共和国居民身份证条例》及其实施细则实施以来，全国大多数地区颁发居民身份证工作已基本完成，在全国实施居民身份证的使用和查验制度的条件目前已经具备。为证明公民身份，便利公民进行政治、经济和其他社会活动，保障公民的合法权益，加强社会管理，维护社会秩序，经国务院批准，决定自1989年9月15日起，在全国范围内实施居民身份证的使用和查验制度。特将有关事项通告如下：
    一、公民外出活动应当随身携带居民身份证，以备使用或者接受查验。
    二、居民身份证适用于公民办理下列需要证明身份的权益事项：
    （一）选民登记；
      （二）户口登记；
    （三）兵役登记；
    （四）婚姻登记；
      （五）入学、就业；
    （六）办理公证事务；
    （七）前往边境管理区；
    （八）办理申请出境手续；
    （九）参与诉讼活动；
    （十）办理机动车、船驾驶证和行驶证，非机动车执照；
      （十一）办理个体营业执照；
    （十二）办理个人信贷事务；
    （十三）参加社会保险，领取社会救济；
    （十四）办理搭乘民航飞机手续；
      （十五）投宿旅店办理登记手续；
      （十六）提取汇款、邮件；
      （十七）寄卖物品；
    （十八）办理其他事务。
    三、国家各级行政管理部门、企业事业单位、人民团体和社会各有关部门，在办理涉及公民权益事务需要证明居民身份时，应当核查居民身份证的持证人的相片和登记内容，并登记证件的编号。
    任何承办单位和工作人员不得以任何理由扣留或者抵押公民的居民身份证。
    四、公安机关在执行任务时，有权查验公民的居民身份证，被查验的公民不得拒绝。
    执行任务的公安干警在查验公民的居民身份证时，应当首先出示本人的工作证件。
    五、对于违反居民身份证管理的行为，情节较轻的，应当给予批评、教育；情节较重，构成违反治安管理行为的，依据《治安管理处罚条例》予以处罚。伪造、变造居民身份证的或者窃取居民身份证情节严重构成犯罪的，依照《中华人民共和国刑法》第一百六十七条的规定追究刑事责任。
    特此通告。
            一九八九年九月八日
