Title: The Second Flower Expo Opens
Author: Cheng Huifen
Date: 1989-09-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 28th.

The second China Flower Expo opened its doors on September 26th with an opening ceremony. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Honorary President of the China Flower Association, Chen Muhua, cut the ribbon to officially open the event. The main venue is located at the Beijing Agricultural Exhibition Hall, with additional venues set up in Fengtai Flower Village and Shijingshan.

The expo showcases a wide array of flowers and plants, providing a platform for exhibitors to display their latest products and technologies. Visitors can enjoy the beautiful floral displays and learn about the latest trends in horticulture. The event aims to promote the development of the flower industry and enhance cultural exchanges.

### 第二届花卉博览会开幕
程蕙芬
1989-09-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    第二届花卉博览会开幕
    本报讯　记者程蕙芬报道：第二届中国花卉博览会于9月26日举行了开幕仪式，人大常委会副委员长、中国花协名誉会长陈慕华为开幕式剪彩。博览会主会场设在北京农业展览馆，丰台花乡和石景山分别设立了分会场。
