Title: Guan Huairen's Effective Treatment of Male Infertility
Author: Zheji
Date: 1989-09-28
Edition: 2

Guan Huairen, a physician at the Male Infertility Research Institute of the Political Department of the Zhejiang Provincial Military Region, has, after 20 years of dedicated research and practice, unveiled the mysteries of male functional anejaculation. Over the past two years, he has treated 1094 patients, curing 1007 of them, with a cure rate of over 92%.

Guan Huairen's unique combination of traditional Chinese and Western medicines in acupuncture therapy can stimulate the cerebral cortex and spinal cord to achieve highly selective excitement, thus achieving the therapeutic goal. Recently, Guan Huairen was awarded the title of Excellent Civilian Cadre by the Nanjing Military Region.

### 管怀仁善治男性不育症
浙记
1989-09-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    管怀仁善治男性不育症
    本报讯　浙江省军区政治部男性不育研究所管怀仁医师，经过20年的精心研究和实践，揭开了男子功能性不射精这个神秘领域的奥秘。两年多来，已收治患者1094人，治愈1007人，治愈率达92％以上。
    管怀仁独创的中西药复方针剂，能使大脑皮层和脊髓产生高度选择性兴奋，达到治疗的目的。最近，管怀仁被南京军区评为优秀文职干部。（浙记）
