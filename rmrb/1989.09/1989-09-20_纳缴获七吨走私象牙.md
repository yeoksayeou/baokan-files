Title: Namibia Seizes Seven Tons of Smuggled Ivory
Author:
Date: 1989-09-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

Namibian police have seized approximately seven tons of smuggled ivory in the country's largest-ever ivory smuggling case, according to a report by the Windhoek newspaper on the 18th. Six individuals were arrested in connection with the case.

It is understood that most of the ivory originated from Angola, with a smaller portion coming from Zambia.

### 纳缴获七吨走私象牙

1989-09-20
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　纳缴获七吨走私象牙
    据温得和克报纸18日报道，纳米比亚警察组织周末破获该国最大一起走私象牙案，缴获约7吨象牙，逮捕6人。
    据悉，大部分象牙来自安哥拉，少数来自赞比亚。
