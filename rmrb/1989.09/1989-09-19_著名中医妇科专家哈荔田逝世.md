Title: Renowned Gynecologist Ha Lidian Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-09-19
Edition: 2

Renowned gynecologist Ha Lidian passes away

A New China News Agency report from Tianjin: Ha Lidian, a renowned gynecologist who had practiced medicine for 60 years and was a member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, passed away on September 9 due to illness. He was 78 years old.

Ha Lidian was an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China. Before his passing, he served as the deputy director of the Tianjin Municipal Health Bureau and the president of Tianjin University of Traditional Chinese Medicine.

Ha Lidian was born into a family of traditional Chinese medicine practitioners in Baoding, Hebei Province. He graduated from the Huabei National Medical College in Beijing. Over the course of 60 years, he made significant contributions to the development of traditional Chinese medicine in China. He also founded Tianjin University of Traditional Chinese Medicine, training a large number of traditional Chinese medicine practitioners for the country. After Ha Lidian's passing, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Yang Jingren and others sent condolence messages.

### 著名中医妇科专家哈荔田逝世

1989-09-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　著名中医妇科专家哈荔田逝世
    新华社天津电　从医60个春秋的全国政协委员、著名中医妇科专家哈荔田教授因病于9月9日逝世，终年78岁。
    哈荔田是中国共产党的优秀党员，生前曾任天津市卫生局副局长、天津中医学院院长。
    哈荔田出生于河北省保定市一个回族世医之家，毕业于北平华北国医学院。60年来他为我国中医事业的发展作出了突出贡献。他还创建了天津中医学院，为我国培养了大批中医药人才。哈荔田同志逝世以后，全国政协副主席杨静仁等发来唁电。
