Title: Poland and the EEC Sign a Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1989-09-21
Page: 3

The trade cooperation agreement between the European Economic Community (EEC) and Poland was signed on September 19 in Warsaw. The current chairman of the EEC Council of Ministers, French Foreign Minister Roland Dumas, and Polish Foreign Minister Tadeusz Skubiszewski signed the agreement. (Reported by our correspondent)

### 波兰欧共体签订贸易协定

1989-09-21
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    波兰欧共体签订贸易协定
    欧洲共同体与波兰之间的贸易合作协定9月19日在华沙签字。欧共体部长理事会现任主席、法国外长迪马和波兰外长斯库比谢夫斯基在协定上签了字。　（本报专电）
