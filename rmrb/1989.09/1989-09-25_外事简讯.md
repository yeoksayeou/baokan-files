Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-09-25
Edition: 2

Vice Premier Wu Xueqian met with Malaysian Minister of Housing and Local Government Lee Kim Lion and his delegation at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on September 24. Wu expressed satisfaction with the development of Sino-Malaysian relations in recent years.

Wu said, "China and Malaysia are both developing countries. There are no fundamental conflicts of interest between us, only a shared desire for development. Furthering our relations on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence will benefit the economic development of both nations."

He told the guests, "China will continue to pursue an independent and peaceful foreign policy, strive for a long-term peaceful international environment, and focus on developing the national economy and modernizing the country."

Lee said that during his visit, he had seen the tremendous achievements China had made in the 10 years since it began its reform and opening-up policy.

Minister of Construction Lin Hanxiong was present at the meeting.

	▲	Minister of International Liaison Department Zhu Liang met and hosted a dinner for a delegation of Romanian party workers on holiday, led by the Secretary of the Orheiul Vechi County Committee of the Romanian Communist Party, Stefanescu, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on September 24.

		(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 外事简讯

1989-09-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务院副总理吴学谦9月24日在钓鱼台国宾馆会见马来西亚住房和地方政府部部长李金狮一行时，对近年来中马关系的发展表示满意。
    吴学谦说，中国和马来西亚都是发展中国家，没有根本的利害冲突，只有共同发展的愿望。两国在和平共处五项原则的基础上进一步发展关系，有助于各自民族经济的发展。
    他对客人说，中国将继续奉行独立自主的和平外交政策，争取长期的国际和平环境，集中精力发展国民经济，进行现代化建设。
    李金狮说，在访问中他看到中国改革开放10年来取得了巨大的成就。
    建设部部长林汉雄会见时在座。
    ▲中联部部长朱良24日在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请了由罗共奥尔特县委书记斯特凡内斯库率领的罗马尼亚党的工作者休假团。
            （据新华社）
