Title: Jiang Zemin Attends Performance by North Korean Art Troupe
Author: Yang Guoqian Li Huilin
Date: 1989-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 28th. (Reporters Yang Guoqian, Li Huilin) CCP Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin and Central Political Bureau Standing Committee member Li Ruihuan watched a performance by the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Art Troupe at the Tianqiao Theater this evening. The performance featured songs and dances rich in national flavor.

When the performance ended, Jiang Zemin, Li Ruihuan, and other leaders went on stage to congratulate the North Korean artists on their successful performance. Jiang Zemin said, "Your performance was excellent, with good political content and high artistic standards, providing a beautiful experience."

Before the performance, Jiang Zemin and Li Ruihuan also met with the troupe leader Kim Jong-ho and the main performers at the Tianqiao Theater. Jiang Zemin told the North Korean artists, "The peoples of China and North Korea share a deep friendship, and we highly value the friendly relations between our two countries."

Kim Jong-un, the North Korean ambassador to China, was also present at the meeting. The ambassador conveyed greetings from Chairman Kim Il-sung and Kim Jong-il to Jiang Zemin. Jiang Zemin also asked the ambassador to convey his greetings to Kim Il-sung and Kim Jong-il, saying, "Please tell Chairman Kim Il-sung and Comrade Kim Jong-il that we specially came to watch the performance."

Others who attended the performance included CCP Central Political Bureau member Li Tianying, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Xi Zhongxun, and Acting Minister of Culture He Jingzhi.

### 江泽民观看朝艺术团演出
杨国强  李怀林
1989-09-29
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民观看朝艺术团演出
    据新华社北京9月28日电　（记者杨国强、李怀林）中共中央总书记江泽民，中央政治局常委李瑞环，今天晚上在天桥剧场观看了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国艺术团演出的具有浓郁民族风格的歌舞节目。
    演出结束时，江泽民、李瑞环等领导同志走上舞台，祝贺朝鲜艺术家们演出成功。江泽民说，你们的演出很精彩，既有很好的政治内容，又有很高的艺术水平，给人以美的享受。
    演出前，江泽民、李瑞环等还在天桥剧场亲切会见了艺术团团长金正浩和主要演员。江泽民对朝鲜艺术家们说：“中朝两国人民有着战斗友情，我们非常珍视两国间的友好关系。”
    会见时在场的有朝鲜驻华大使朱昌骏。大使转达了金日成主席和金正日对江泽民的问候。江泽民也请大使转达他对金日成和金正日的问候，并说：“请您转告金日成主席和金正日同志，我们专门来看了演出。”
  观看演出的还有中共中央政治局委员李铁映、全国人大常委会副委员长习仲勋、文化部代部长贺敬之等。
