Title: Shaanxi Commemorates the 40th Anniversary of Yang Huicheng's Martyrdom
Author: Jing Xianfeng
Date: 1989-09-20
Edition: 2

Recently, more than 200 people from various sectors in Shaanxi Province gathered at the Yang Huicheng Martyrs' Cemetery to hold a memorial and grave-sweeping activity, commemorating the 40th anniversary of the martyrdom of the great patriotic democratic general Yang Huicheng.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's United Front Work Department presented a wreath to the tomb of General Yang Huicheng. The Shaanxi Provincial Committee, Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee, Provincial Government, Provincial Political Consultative Conference, Provincial Military Region, and the leading organs of Xi'an City also presented wreaths at the tomb of General Yang Huicheng. The children, old subordinates, and relatives and friends of General Yang Huicheng participated in this commemorative activity.

### 陕西祭奠杨虎城殉难40周年
景险峰
1989-09-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　陕西祭奠杨虎城殉难40周年
    本报西安9月19日电　记者景险峰报道：日前，陕西省各界人士200余人在杨虎城烈士陵园举行祭奠扫墓活动，纪念伟大的爱国民主将领杨虎城将军殉难40周年。
    中共中央统战部向杨虎城将军的陵墓敬献了花篮。陕西省委、省顾委、省人大常委会、省政府、省政协、省军区，以及西安市的领导机关，在杨虎城将军的陵前敬献了花圈。杨虎城将军的子女和老部下以及亲朋故旧参加了这一纪念活动。
