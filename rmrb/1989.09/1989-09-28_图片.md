Title: Chairman Li Xiannian Welcomes Bangladeshi President Hussain Muhammad Ershad's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1989-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 1985

In July 1985, Chairman Li Xiannian warmly welcomed the visit of Bangladeshi President Hussain Muhammad Ershad to China. This visit marked a significant milestone in the friendly relations between the two countries. During his stay, President Ershad held extensive talks with Chinese leaders, discussing various aspects of bilateral cooperation and regional issues. The visit underscored the deepening ties and mutual respect between China and Bangladesh, paving the way for future collaborations in economic, cultural, and political domains.
