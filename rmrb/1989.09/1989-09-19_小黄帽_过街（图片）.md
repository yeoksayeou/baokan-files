Title: Little Yellow Hats Crossing the Street
Author: Liú Yīngyì
Date: 1989-09-19
Page: 2

To ensure the traffic safety of primary school students, Beijing Municipality began implementing the "patrol squad system" among the first batch of 100,000 students yesterday. The photo shows a group of students wearing little yellow hats, holding patrol flags and safety signs, crossing the street.
