Title: The United Nations and Australia Donate Funds to Our Disaster-Affected Areas
Author: Yang Guilan
Date: 1989-09-15
Edition: 2

The United Nations Development Programme, the United Nations Disaster Relief Coordination Office, and the Australian Government donated funds to the Chinese Government in a ceremony held this morning in Beijing. The total amount of $150,000 will be used to purchase food, blankets, and other supplies to aid the victims of the severe floods that struck Sichuan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Zhejiang, and Anhui provinces in July and August of this year.

Australian Ambassador to China, Mr. Sadler, Deputy Representative of the UNDP in China, Mr. Bostock, and Director of the International Department of the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade of China, Mr. Wang Tiance, signed the relevant documents on behalf of their respective governments or organizations.

Mr. Wang Tiance, on behalf of the Chinese Government, expressed heartfelt gratitude to the international organizations and friendly countries for their disaster relief assistance.

### 联合国与澳大利亚向我灾区赠款
杨贵兰
1989-09-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　联合国与澳大利亚向我灾区赠款
    本报北京9月15日讯　记者杨贵兰报道：联合国开发计划署、联合国救灾协调员办事处和澳大利亚政府向中国政府赠款的仪式，今天上午在这里举行。总额为15万多美元的赠款，将用于购买食品、被子等物，救济我国四川、江苏、江西、浙江、安徽等省在今年7、8月份遭受特大洪灾的灾民。
    澳大利亚驻华大使沙德伟、开发署驻华副代表柏思涛以及我国经贸部国际司司长王天策，分别代表各自的政府或组织在有关赠款文件上签了字。
    王天策代表中国政府对国际组织和友好国家向我国提供救灾援助表示衷心的感谢。
