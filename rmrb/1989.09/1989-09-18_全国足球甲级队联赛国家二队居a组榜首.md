Title: National Second Team Tops Group A in National Football First Division League
Author:
Date: 1989-09-18
Edition: 3

The first round of matches in Group A of the National Football First Division League concluded on September 17. In the final match of this round, the National Second Team defeated last year's national champion, Liaoning Team, with a score of 1:0. This victory secured the National Second Team the top spot in Group A with 16 points. Liaoning Team followed with 13 points.

The final two rounds of Group A matches were held on September 15 and 17 in Tianjin and Jinan. The other results were as follows: The PLA Team lost to the National Second Team 0:3 and defeated the Guangzhou Team 1:0; Liaoning Team defeated Guangzhou Team 3:1; Shanghai Team won against Guangdong Team and Shandong Team with a score of 1:0 each; Tianjin Zhonghuan Team drew with Shandong Team 0:0 and lost to Guangdong Team 1:2.

The second round of Group A matches will commence on September 21 in Tianjin and Jinan.

### 全国足球甲级队联赛国家二队居A组榜首

1989-09-18
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　全国足球甲级队联赛国家二队居A组榜首
    本报讯　全国足球甲级队联赛A组第一循环的比赛，到9月17日已告一段落。国家二队在本循环最后一战中，以1∶0力克一直保持不败的去年全国冠军辽宁队，从而以积16分的成绩暂列A组榜首。辽宁队以13分次之。
    A组第一循环的最后两轮比赛9月15日、17日分别在天津、济南两市举行，其他几场结果是：八一队负于国家二队0∶3、胜广州队1∶0；辽宁队胜广州队3∶1；上海队以1∶0先后胜广东队、山东队；天津中环队0∶0平山东队、1∶2负于广东队。
    A组第二循环的比赛将于9月21日起在天津、济南揭开战幕。
