Title: The Second National Youth Games Closes
Author: Wāng Dàzhāo
Date: 1989-09-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 16th.

The closing ceremony of the Second National Youth Games was held this afternoon at the Liaoning Gymnasium.

Song Renqiong, Lü Dong, Rong Gaotang, and other leaders, along with officials from the State Sports Commission and Liaoning Province, attended the ceremony and awarded medals to some athletes who won gold medals, set records, and received the sportsmanship award.

In his closing remarks, Wu Shaozu, the head of the State Sports Commission, said the Youth Games were grand, enthusiastic, exciting, and successful. The games showcased excellent sportsmanship and standards, trained new talents, set numerous new records, and produced many outstanding young athletes. He hoped that young athletes would train hard and scientifically, and that sports departments at all levels would cultivate more excellent reserve talents to promote the development of sports.

Over the past 10 days, 36 sports delegations from various provinces, municipalities, autonomous regions, the People's Liberation Army, and industry sports associations, totaling more than 4,000 young athletes, came to the Liaoning-Shenyang region to compete in the final stage of the Youth Games. During the competitions, 1 athlete broke 1 world record, 2 athletes broke 2 world youth records, 3 athletes broke 3 Asian records, and many national and national youth records were also set. The host Liaoning, along with Beijing, Jiangsu, Shandong, Guangdong, Sichuan, Hubei, Henan, Guangxi, and Hunan, 10 delegations were awarded the sportsmanship award.

The State Sports Commission decided that in the future, the Youth Games would be merged into the National Games. The Second National Youth Games, which have been held twice, thus completed their historical mission.

### 第二届全国青少年运动会闭幕　宋任穷等为部分获奖运动员颁奖
汪大昭
1989-09-17
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　第二届全国青少年运动会闭幕
　　宋任穷等为部分获奖运动员颁奖
    本报沈阳9月16日电　记者汪大昭报道：第二届全国青少年运动会闭幕式今天下午在辽宁体育馆举行。
    宋任穷、吕东、荣高棠等和国家体委及辽宁省负责同志出席闭幕式，并向部分获金牌、创纪录和获得体育道德风尚奖的运动员、运动队或代表团授奖。
    国家体委主任伍绍祖在闭幕词中讲到青运会开得隆重、热烈、精彩、圆满，赛出了风格和水平，锻炼了新手，创造了一大批新纪录，涌现出一大批新人才。他希望广大青少年运动员要刻苦而科学地训练，各级体育部门培养更多的优秀后备人才，推动体育事业的发展。
    10余天来，各省、市、自治区以及解放军、行业体协的36个体育代表团，共4000多名小将来到辽沈大地，参加了本届青运会决赛阶段的争夺。比赛中有1人超1项世界纪录，2人超2项世界青年纪录，3人超3项亚洲纪录，还创造了多项全国纪录和全国青少年纪录。东道主辽宁以及北京、江苏、山东、广东、四川、湖北、河南、广西、湖南10个代表团荣获体育道德风尚奖。
    国家体委决定今后将青运会合并入城运会，举办了两届的全国青少年运动会至此完成了自己的历史使命。
