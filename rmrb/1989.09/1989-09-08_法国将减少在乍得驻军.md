Title: France to Reduce Troops in Chad
Author:
Date: 1989-09-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency,

French Defense Minister Chevènement announced in Paris on the 7th that France will reduce its troops stationed in Chad from 1,500 to 1,300. He made the announcement after a meeting of the National Assembly's Defense Committee.

### 法国将减少在乍得驻军

1989-09-08
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    法国将减少在乍得驻军
    法国国防部长舍韦内芒七日在巴黎宣布，法国将把它驻扎在乍得的军队从一千五百人减少到一千三百人。他是在国民议会国防委员会会议后宣布的。　（据新华社）
