Title: Cultural Gala Held at Zhongnanhai's Huairentang
Author:
Date: 1989-09-23
Edition: 2

To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Central Office, the Office of the Central Advisory Commission, and the Central Organization Department held a cultural gala at Huairentang in Zhongnanhai, Beijing, tonight. The gala featured performances by some of China's renowned artists.

Attending the gala were leaders Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, Li Xiannian, Peng Zhen, Qiao Shi, Song Ping, Wang Zhen, and other senior comrades from the Central Advisory Commission. After the performance, the central leaders went on stage to shake hands with the performers and took a group photo.

### 中南海怀仁堂举行文艺晚会  江泽民等观看演出

1989-09-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中南海怀仁堂举行文艺晚会
    江泽民等观看演出
    新华社北京9月22日电  为庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年，中央办公厅、中顾委办公厅和中央组织部，于今晚在北京中南海怀仁堂举行文艺晚会。晚会上，我国一些著名的艺术家们表演了精采的文艺节目。
    出席晚会的有江泽民、李鹏、李先念、彭真、乔石、宋平、王震等领导同志及中顾委的老同志们。演出结束后，中央领导同志上台同演员们亲切握手并合影留念。
