Title: Comprehensive Treatise on Oracle Bone Script
Author: Wáng Yǔxìn
Date: 1989-09-25
Edition: 6

	"Comprehensive Treatise on Oracle Bone Script" by Wang Yuxin is a systematic monograph on the study of oracle bone script, possessing high academic value. The entire book summarizes the achievements of oracle bone script research over the past ninety years, systematically and scientifically discusses the basic principles, research methods, and topics of oracle bone script studies, introduces important works and the research characteristics of famous oracle bone script scholars, and discusses the new branch of the study of Western Zhou oracle bones. The book is appended with a chronicle of major events in oracle bone script studies, a bibliography of works, and a list of oracle bone script studies and Western Zhou oracle bone script works from New China, providing useful reference materials.

### 《甲骨学通论》王宇信著  中国社会科学出版社出版

1989-09-25
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：

    《甲骨学通论》王宇信著
     中国社会科学出版社出版
    《甲骨学通论》是一部系统论述甲骨学研究的专著，具有很高的学术价值。全书总结了九十年来甲骨学研究的成果，系统而科学地论述了甲骨学的基本规律、研究方法与课题，介绍了重要甲骨学论著及著名甲骨学家的研究特点，并论述了西周甲骨这一新分支学科。书后附有甲骨学大事记、著录书目及新中国甲骨学论著目、西周甲骨论著目等工具性资料。
