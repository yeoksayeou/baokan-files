Title: NATO Conducts Naval and Air Military Exercises
Author:
Date: 1989-09-10
Page: 3

According to a well-informed source from NATO, it was revealed in Brussels on September 8th that NATO will conduct a new round of naval and air military exercises from September 8th to 21st.

(Xinhua News Agency)

### 北约举行海空军事演习

1989-09-10
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　  北约举行海空军事演习
    据北约消息灵通人士8日在布鲁塞尔透露，北约将于9月8日—21日举行一次新的海空军事演习。
            （据新华社）
