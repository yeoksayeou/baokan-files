Title: China Signs Oceanic Petroleum Contract with the U.S. and Australia
Author:
Date: 1989-09-08
Edition: 2

China National Offshore Oil Corporation signed a petroleum contract for a contract area in the Zhujiangkou Basin of the South China Sea with the Far East Division of Occidental Petroleum Corporation of the United States, Ampolex Exploration Pty. Ltd. of Australia, and AGL Petroleum Ltd. of Australia in Beijing today.

### 我与美澳签署海洋石油合同

1989-09-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    我与美澳签署海洋石油合同
    据新华社北京9月7日电  中国海洋石油总公司与美国西方石油远东公司、澳大利亚安波尔勘探有限公司、澳大利亚AGL石油公司，今天在北京签署了中国南海珠江口盆地一个合同区的石油合同。
