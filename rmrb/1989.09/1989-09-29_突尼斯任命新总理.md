Title: Tunisia Appoints New Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency,

Tunisia has appointed a new prime minister. Tunisian President Ben Ali named former Justice Minister Hamed Karoui as the new prime minister on September 27.
