Title: Benxi County Shuts Down Unlicensed Small Coal Mines
Author:
Date: 1989-09-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang

Benxi County in Liaoning Province recently shut down more than 200 illegal small coal mines and seized over 1,000 tons of illegally mined coal. Those with serious offenses will face criminal charges. The rampant and illegal mining practices have now been effectively curbed.

### 本溪县关闭无证小煤窑

1989-09-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    本溪县关闭无证小煤窑
    新华社沈阳电　辽宁省本溪县最近关闭了200多个非法开采的小煤窑，没收非法开采的煤炭1000多吨。对情节严重的还将追究刑事责任。现在已基本上煞住了滥采滥挖歪风。
