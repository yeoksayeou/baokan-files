Title: Stability in the Ceasefire Between Iran and Iraq for a Year
Author:
Date: 1989-09-01
Edition: 3

According to the Iraqi News Agency, quoting a member of the United Nations military observer group overseeing the ceasefire between Iran and Iraq, the UN's Resolution 598, which called for a ceasefire between the two countries, has been in effect since August 20 last year. The ceasefire has been stable. This individual stated that since the ceasefire began, there have been no major conflicts between the two sides.

### 一年来两伊停火稳定

1989-09-01
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    一年来两伊停火稳定
    据伊拉克通讯社援引联合国监督伊朗、伊拉克停火的军事观察团人士的话说，联合国通过的呼吁两伊停火的598号决议去年8月20日生效以来，两伊停火稳定。这位人士说，自停火以来，双方没有发生过大的冲突。
