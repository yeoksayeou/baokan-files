Title: Stars
Author: Qi Xiangchun
Date: 1989-09-10
Page: 8

Stars
High in the sky, the stars hang above the drifting clouds,
They cannot carry away my soul.
I cannot distinguish who is the brightest in the Milky Way,
Nor do I constantly ponder over this.
Though I am lonely, I do not envy the light,
For without light, there would be no shining life for me.
I do not despise the darkness,
For only in the darkness can my light be more vivid.
I only hope that the wind will drive away the clouds,
So that my faint light can shine upon the earth,
Shine upon the world.

　　星星
