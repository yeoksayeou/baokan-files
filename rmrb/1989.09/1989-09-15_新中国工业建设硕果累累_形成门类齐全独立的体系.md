Title: China's Industrial Construction Achieves Remarkable Results and Forms a Comprehensive and Independent System
Author: Zhang Guo Rong
Date: 1989-09-15
Edition: 1

	China's industrial economy has achieved tremendous success over the past 40 years, establishing an independent and relatively comprehensive socialist industrial system.

	From 1950 to 1988, the state's investment in fixed assets for state-owned enterprises reached over 1,000 billion yuan, completing more than 3,000 large and medium-sized industrial projects. According to international industry classifications, except for the nuclear power industry, which is still under construction, China has established all 500-plus industries. China can now rely on its own technological capabilities to equip large-scale mines, power plants, metallurgy, petrochemical, automotive, and shipbuilding industries.

	In 1988, China's total industrial output value was 1,822.5 billion yuan. Compared to 1949, this represents a 134-fold increase, with an average annual growth rate of 13.4%. Such a high growth rate is rare among countries worldwide.

	The rapid development of industrial production has significantly enhanced China's position in the global economy. Steel production has risen from the 26th position in 1949 to the 4th, coal from the 9th to the 1st, crude oil from the 27th to the 5th, electricity generation from the 25th to the 4th, cement and cloth to the 1st, sulfuric acid and fertilizers to the 3rd, and television sets to the 2nd.

	According to the National Bureau of Statistics, China's industrial fixed assets reached 1,064.1 billion yuan in 1988, an 85-fold increase from 12.4 billion yuan in 1949. Currently, China has 11 large coal mines with an annual output of over 100 million tons, 3 large oil fields with an annual output of over 200 million tons, and 4 power grids with an installed capacity of over 10 million kilowatts.

### 新中国工业建设硕果累累　形成门类齐全独立的体系
张国荣
1989-09-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　新中国工业建设硕果累累
　　形成门类齐全独立的体系
    本报北京9月15日讯　记者张国荣报道：经过40年的建设和发展，我国工业经济取得了举世公认的巨大成就，建立了独立的、门类比较齐全的社会主义工业体系。
    从1950年到1988年，国家全民所有制工业固定资产投资达10000多亿元，相继建成3000多个大中型工业项目。从国际通行行业分类来看，除正在建设的核电业外，其余500多个工业行业，我国均已建立。基本上能够依靠自己的技术力量装备大型矿山、电站、冶金、石油化工、汽车、造船等重要工业。
    1988年我国工业总产值为18225亿元，按可比价格计算，比1949年增长134倍，平均每年增长13.4％，这样高的增长速度在世界各国是不多见的。
    工业生产的飞速发展，大大提高了我国工业在世界经济中的地位，钢由1949年的第26位上升到第4位，原煤由第9位上升到第1位，原油由第27位上升到第5位，发电量由第25位上升到第4位，水泥、布上升到第1位，硫酸、化肥上升到第3位，电视机上升到第2位。
    据国家统计局统计，去年我国工业固定资产达10641亿元，比1949年的124亿元增长85倍。现在我国已拥有年产原煤1000万吨以上的大煤矿11个，年产原油2000万吨以上的大油田3个，装机容量在1000万千瓦以上的电网4个。
