Title: Qinghai's Silicomanganese Resources Create $9 Million in Foreign Exchange
Author: Zhèng Zhēn Pú
Date: 1989-09-17
Edition: 2

    Qinghai Province has developed several silicon manganese enterprises in the Yellow River basin areas of Haidong, Hainan, Huangnan, Hainan, Xining, and other cities and prefectures over the past year, ensuring a sufficient supply of silicon manganese resources that meet export standards. This year, Qinghai Province has exported 11,000 tons of silicon manganese, earning the country over $9 million in foreign exchange.

### 青海硅铁资源多创汇美元九百万
郅振璞
1989-09-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　青海硅铁资源多创汇美元九百万
    本报西宁9月16日电　记者郅振璞报道：青海省去年以来在沿黄流域的海东、海南、黄南、海北、西宁等地、州、市发展一批硅铁企业，符合出口的硅铁资源货源充足。今年以来，青海省硅铁出口已达11000吨，为国家创汇900多万美元。
