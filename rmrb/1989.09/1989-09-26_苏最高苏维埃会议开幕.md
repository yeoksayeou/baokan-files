Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Congress Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-09-26
Page: 3

The second session of the Soviet Supreme Soviet opened in Moscow on the 25th. This session is scheduled to last for two months. According to a report by TASS, the session will first review economic and political reform documents such as the 1990 national plan and budget. Representatives hope that the new national plan and budget will help to halt the deterioration of the national economy.

### 苏最高苏维埃会议开幕

1989-09-26
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    苏最高苏维埃会议开幕
    苏联最高苏维埃第二次会议二十五日在莫斯科开幕。这次会议预定进行两个月。据塔斯社报道，会议将首先审议一九九○年国家计划和预算等经济、政治改革文件。代表们希望，新的国家计划和预算将有助于制止国家经济状况恶化。
