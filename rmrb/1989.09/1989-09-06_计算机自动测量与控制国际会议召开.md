Title: International Conference on Computer-Aided Measurement and Control Held
Author: He Huangbiao
Date: 1989-09-06
Page: 2

People's Daily, Beijing, September 6th.

The International Symposium on Computer-Aided Measurement and Control, jointly initiated by China's Ministry of Aerospace Industry and the Polish Atomic Energy Commission, opened today in Beijing. Over a hundred experts from Poland, the United States, the Soviet Union, West Germany, Hungary, Italy, and China attended the conference, submitting nearly 70 academic papers.

Since the 1980s, China's aerospace industry has developed over 120 types of functional modules and 32 systems, bringing the country's ground-based measurement, control, and launch systems to an international standard.

### 计算机自动测量与控制国际会议召开
何黄彪
1989-09-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    计算机自动测量与控制国际会议召开
    本报北京9月6日讯　记者何黄彪报道：由中国航空航天工业部与波兰原子能委员会联合发起的计算机自动测量与控制国际研讨会，今天在这里召开。来自波兰、美国、苏联、西德、匈牙利、意大利等国和中国的百余名专家参加了会议，并提交近70篇学术论文。
    我国航空航天部从80年代开始，已研制120余种功能模件及32套系统，使我航天地面测控发射系统技术步入国际水平。
