Title: Cuba to Withdraw Troops from Ethiopia
Author:
Date: 1989-09-08
Page: 3

The Cuban government announced on the 7th that starting from the 9th of this month, Cuban troops stationed in Ethiopia will begin to withdraw back to their home country.

A memorandum from the Cuban Revolutionary Armed Forces stated, "Considering that the situation has undergone favorable changes, the governments of Cuba and Ethiopia have agreed that Cuban soldiers currently in Ethiopia will begin to withdraw back to Cuba starting from September 9th."

### 古将从埃塞俄比亚撤军

1989-09-08
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    古将从埃塞俄比亚撤军
    古巴政府七日宣布，从本月九日开始，在埃塞俄比亚的古巴军人将陆续撤回国。
    古巴革命武装力量部的一份备忘录说，“考虑到情况已发生了有利的变化，古巴和埃塞俄比亚两国政府同意目前在埃塞俄比亚的古巴士兵从九月九日开始撤回古巴”。
