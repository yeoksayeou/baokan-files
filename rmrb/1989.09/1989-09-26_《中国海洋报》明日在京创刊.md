Title: China's First National Marine Newspaper to Launch in Beijing Tomorrow
Author: Guo Haixuan
Date: 1989-09-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 26th.

China's first national marine newspaper, "China Marine News," will launch in Beijing tomorrow, September 27th. The newspaper will adhere to the Party's journalistic principles, uphold the Four Cardinal Principles, and propagate the Party and the state's major policies and strategies regarding marine work, as well as introduce China's coastal regions' economic takeoff trends and experiences. It will report on the achievements of various sectors within the national marine system amidst the reform tide, convey domestic and international marine management, development, and technological information, and engage in academic discussions and consulting services.

### 《中国海洋报》明日在京创刊
郭海轩
1989-09-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    《中国海洋报》明日在京创刊
    本报讯　我国海洋战线第一张全国性报纸——《中国海洋报》，将于9月27日在北京创刊。该报将在党的新闻方针指引下，坚持四项基本原则，宣传党和国家关于海洋工作的大政方针以及海洋发展战略，介绍我国沿海地区经济起飞的态势与经验，报道全国海洋系统各行各业在改革大潮中取得的成就，传递国内外海洋管理、开发与科技信息，开展学术讨论与咨询服务。
            （郭海轩）
