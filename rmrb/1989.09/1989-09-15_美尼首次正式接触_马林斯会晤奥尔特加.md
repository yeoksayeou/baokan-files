Title: U.S.-Nicaragua Hold First Official Contact
Author: Zhang Qixun
Date: 1989-09-15
Page: 3

Recorded by our reporter Zhang Qixun in Washington on September 13th.

A senior U.S. government official recently met with Nicaraguan President Daniel Ortega to discuss U.S.-Nicaragua relations, marking the first official contact between the two countries.

According to a report by The New York Times today, U.S. Assistant Secretary of State for Congressional Relations, Alan D. Fiers, accompanied a delegation of four U.S. congressmen to Nicaragua a few days ago and met with President Ortega on September 10. During the meeting, Fiers conveyed to Ortega that if Nicaragua holds free and fair elections next February, U.S.-Nicaragua relations could improve. He also proposed that Nicaragua allow a U.S. government delegation to observe the elections. Ortega expressed that the U.S. must stop supporting the Nicaraguan contra rebels as a fighting force and that the supplies of clothing, food, and housing provided to them should be used to help them return to their home country.

The current U.S. policy is to avoid high-level substantive contact with Nicaraguan officials. Fiers' participation in the meeting with Ortega is an exception. A Washington lawyer representing the Nicaraguan government, Reinaldo B. Figueredo, said the atmosphere of the meeting was harmonious. He hopes this will lead to more direct contact between the U.S. and Nicaraguan governments and ultimately to the normalization of relations between the two countries.

### 美尼首次正式接触　马林斯会晤奥尔特加
张启昕
1989-09-15
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　美尼首次正式接触
　　马林斯会晤奥尔特加
    本报华盛顿9月13日电　记者张启昕报道：美国政府高级官员最近会见了尼加拉瓜总统奥尔特加，讨论美尼关系，进行了美尼之间的首次正式接触。
    据《纽约时报》今天消息，美国国务院负责国会事务的助理国务卿马林斯几天前随同一个由4名众议员组成的美国代表团前往尼加拉瓜访问，并于9月10日参加了与奥尔特加总统的会见。在会见中，马林斯向奥尔特加说明，如果尼加拉瓜明年2月举行自由、公正的选举，美尼关系将能改善。他还提出，希望在选举时尼加拉瓜能允许美国派出一个政府代表团前往观察。奥尔特加表示，美国必须停止继续把尼加拉瓜反政府武装作为一支作战力量加以保持。美国向他们提供的衣、食、住经费应该用于协助他们返回本国。
    美国政府的现行政策是避免与尼加拉瓜官员进行高级的实质性接触。马林斯参加会见奥尔特加是美尼之间的首次破例。一位代表尼加拉瓜官方的华盛顿律师赖克勒称，这次会见的谈话气氛和谐，他希望这将导致美尼政府间的更多直接接触，并最后导致两国关系正常化。
