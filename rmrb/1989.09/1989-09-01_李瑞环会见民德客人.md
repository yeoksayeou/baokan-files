Title: Li Ruihuan Meets with East German Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-09-01
Edition: 1

Li Ruihuan, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, met with a delegation from the Broadcasting Commission of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Chairman Becker, this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People.

Li Ruihuan expressed gratitude for the support provided by the party and government of the German Democratic Republic in China's efforts to quell the counter-revolutionary riots.

He briefed Becker on the causes of the recent counter-revolutionary riots in Beijing and the lessons learned from them.

Both sides exchanged views on strengthening ideological education and propaganda work.

Minister of Radio, Film, and Television Ai Zhensheng was present at the meeting.

### 李瑞环会见民德客人

1989-09-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李瑞环会见民德客人
    新华社北京9月1日电　中共中央政治局常委、书记处书记李瑞环今天下午在人民大会堂会见了民主德国广播委员会主席贝克尔一行。
    李瑞环对民主德国党和政府在中国平息反革命暴乱这件事情上所给予的支持表示感谢。
    他向贝克尔介绍了不久前北京发生反革命暴乱的原因以及从中吸取的教训。
    宾主双方交换了对加强思想教育和宣传工作的看法。
    广播电影电视部部长艾知生会见时在座。
