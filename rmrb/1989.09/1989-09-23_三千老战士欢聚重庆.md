Title: 3,000 Veterans Gather in Chongqing
Author: Lǐ Wéipíng
Date: 1989-09-23
Edition: 2

	Three thousand veterans of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's Southwest Service Team gathered in Chongqing on September 20 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the team's founding.

	Forty years ago, 16,000 cadres and revolutionary youth from the Southwest Service Team left the comforts of big cities to advance into the Southwest. They dedicated their wisdom and youth to the grand cause of revitalizing the Southwest. Former leaders of the Southwest Service Team, including Song Renqiong, Chen Xilian, and Duan Junyi, sent congratulatory messages and telegrams to the 40th anniversary celebration.

### 三千老战士欢聚重庆
李维平
1989-09-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    三千老战士欢聚重庆
    据新华社重庆9月21日电  （记者李维平）带着满头银发，载着西南风尘，中国人民解放军西南服务团的3000多老战士，9月20日汇聚重庆，共庆建团40周年。
    40年前，西南服务团1.6万名干部、革命青年毅然放弃大城市安逸舒适的生活，挺进大西南。他们把自己的聪明才智、青春年华奉献给了振兴西南的壮丽事业。原西南服务团老领导宋任穷、陈锡联、段君毅等向建团40周年大会发来了贺信和贺电。
