Title: Where Did the Seat Cushions Go?
Author: Wang Xujing
Date: 1989-09-20
Edition: 6

On August 13, at the starting point of Route 1 of the electric tram in Baojia Garden, Nanchang City, I observed for half an hour and found that the cushions on the soft seats in six trams, numbered 05971, 05973, 05975, 05978, 05990, and 05995, were mostly missing, leaving only the frames. Passengers who boarded the trams shook their heads and sighed, saying, "Ah! Even the Nanchang City Public Transportation Company..."

Jiangxi Xincheng County Armed Police Detachment
Wang Xujing
