Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Says China Will Strengthen Cooperation with the Non-Aligned Movement
Author:
Date: 1989-09-12
Edition: 2

The spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs said today that China will, as always, support the principles and purposes of the Non-Aligned Movement and strengthen friendly cooperative relations with the Non-Aligned Movement and its member states.

The spokesperson said in response to a reporter's question that the ninth summit of the heads of state of non-aligned countries, which has just concluded, achieved positive results and will have an important impact on further playing the role of the Non-Aligned Movement in the new situation.

He said, "We believe that under the leadership of the new chairman, the Non-Aligned Movement will continue to strive for its basic purposes and goals, and make greater contributions to maintaining world peace, promoting economic development, and the progress of human society."

### 外交部发言人说　我将加强同不结盟运动合作

1989-09-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外交部发言人说
　　我将加强同不结盟运动合作
    新华社北京9月12日电　外交部发言人今天在这里说，中国将一如既往地支持不结盟运动的原则和宗旨，加强同不结盟运动及其成员国的友好合作关系。
    这位发言人在回答记者的提问时说，刚刚结束的第九次不结盟国家首脑会议取得了积极的成果，对在新形势下进一步发挥不结盟运动的作用将产生重要影响。
    他说，“我们相信，在新一届主席的领导下，不结盟运动将继续为实现其基本宗旨和目标而努力，并为维护世界和平、促进经济发展和人类的进步事业作出更大的贡献。”
