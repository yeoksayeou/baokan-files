Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th September.

	Foreign Affairs Brief

	▲ Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and Chairman of the Soong Ching Ling Foundation, Kang Keqing, met with a delegation from the German Democratic Republic (GDR) led by Egon Krenz, a member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Central Secretary, and Vice Chairman of the Council of State of the GDR, at the Soong Ching Ling's former residence on the 26th.

	▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (NPC), Rong Zhiqiong, met with a delegation from the Kenya African National Union (KANU) led by J. R. Lijodi, a member of the National Executive Committee, on the afternoon of the 26th at the Great Hall of the People. KANU is the ruling party of Kenya. Since 1986, when KANU established relations with the Communist Party of China, cooperation between the two parties has developed rapidly. Rong Zhiqiong expressed the hope during the meeting that the relations between China and Kenya, as well as between the two parties, would continue to develop in all areas.

	The Kenyan guests were invited by the International Liaison Department of the Communist Party of China to visit China. They will also visit Tianjin and Guiyang.

	▲ Abdulah Amad Nasuf, the first deputy speaker, and Said Bakar, the third deputy speaker of the Federal Assembly of the Comoros, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 27th at the invitation of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. They will participate in the National Day celebrations in China. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Rong Jieqiong, and others welcomed the guests at the airport.

	▲ General Yang Baibing, Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, met with Horst Brünner, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Director of the General Political Department of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic, in Beijing on the 26th.

	▲ At the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, foreign military attachés and their spouses visited Tianjin from September 23rd to 25th.

### 外事简讯

1989-09-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲中顾委副主任宋任穷26日下午在人民大会堂会见了由全国执委贾·里·利焦迪率领的肯尼亚非洲民族联盟代表团。肯尼亚非洲民族联盟是肯尼亚的执政党。1986年，肯盟同中国共产党建立关系后，两党之间合作发展较快。宋任穷在会见时希望中肯两国和两党在各个领域里的关系不断发展。
    肯尼亚客人是应中联部的邀请来华访问的。他们还将去天津、贵阳等地访问。
    ▲全国政协副主席、宋庆龄基金会主席康克清26日在宋庆龄故居会见了由德国统一社会党中央政治局委员、中央书记、民主德国国务委员会副主席埃贡·克伦茨率领的德意志民主共和国党和国家代表团。
    ▲应全国人大常委会的邀请，科摩罗联邦议会第一副议长阿卜杜拉·阿迈德·纳索夫和第三副议长赛义德·巴卡尔一行27日晚上抵京，对中国进行友好访问并参加中国国庆活动。全国人大常委会副委员长雷洁琼等到机场迎接客人。
    ▲中国人民解放军总政治部主任杨白冰上将26日在北京会见了德意志民主共和国国防部副部长、人民军总政治部主任霍斯特·布吕纳尔上将。
    ▲应中国国防部邀请，外国驻华武官和夫人于9月23日至25日赴天津进行了参观访问。（据新华社）
