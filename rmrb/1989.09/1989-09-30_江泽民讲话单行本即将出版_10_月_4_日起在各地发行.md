Title: Jiang Zemin's Speech to be Published as a Single Volume
Author:
Date: 1989-09-30
Edition: 4

Jiang Zemin's speech at the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China will be published as a single volume by the People's Publishing House. Starting from October 4th, it will be distributed in bookstores across the country.

This book will also be published by the Nationalities Publishing House in Mongolian, Tibetan, Uyghur, Kazakh, and Korean, totaling five languages.

### 江泽民讲话单行本即将出版  10月4日起在各地发行

1989-09-30
第4版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    江泽民讲话单行本即将出版
    10月4日起在各地发行
    新华社北京9月29日电　江泽民同志在庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年大会上的讲话将由人民出版社出版单行本，10月4日起在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
    这本书还将由民族出版社用蒙古、藏、维吾尔、哈萨克、朝鲜五种文字出版。
