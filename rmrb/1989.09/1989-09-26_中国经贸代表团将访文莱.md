Title: Chinese Economic and Trade Delegation to Visit Brunei
Author:
Date: 1989-09-26
Edition: 2

A Chinese economic and trade delegation will visit Brunei.

	BEIJING, Sept. 25 (Xinhua) -- The Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade informed this reporter that, at the invitation of the Ministry of Industry and Primary Resources of Brunei Darussalam, a Chinese government economic and trade delegation led by Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Gu Yunjian will pay an official visit to Brunei from Sept. 27 to 30.

	The delegation's visit aims to exchange views with the Brunei side on establishing bilateral direct trade relations and carrying out various forms of mutually beneficial cooperation.

### 中国经贸代表团将访文莱

1989-09-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中国经贸代表团将访文莱
    新华社北京9月25日电　本社记者从经贸部获悉，应文莱达鲁萨兰国政府工业及初级产品资源部的邀请，由对外经济贸易部部长助理谷永江率领的中国官方经济贸易代表团将于9月27日至30日对文莱进行首次正式访问。
    代表团这次访问旨在与文方就建立双边直接贸易关系、开展多种形式的互利合作问题交换意见。
