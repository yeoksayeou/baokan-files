Title: Panama Establishes Provisional Government
Author: Guo Weicheng
Date: 1989-09-01
Edition: 3

Panama Establishes Provisional Government
Rodriguez Assumes Role of Provisional President
Panama's State Council Condemns U.S. Aggression

MEXICO CITY, Aug. 31 (Xinhua) — The State Council of Panama today decided to establish a new provisional government and appointed Rodriguez as the provisional president to succeed Palma, whose term as acting president ends on September 1.

Various television stations in Mexico City reported that the State Council, composed of Panama's military commander-in-chief General Noriega, acting president Palma, and government ministers, held a special meeting this morning to discuss the situation in Panama.

PANAMA CITY, Aug. 31 (Xinhua) — The State Council of Panama issued a statement today condemning the "brutal aggression" by the United States against Panama over the past two years.

The highest advisory body, composed of the military, parliament, and government, issued a statement after appointing the new provisional government. The statement said that in recent years, the United States' aggressive actions against Panama have included economic sanctions, interference in Panama's elections, and hostile actions in violation of the Panama Canal Treaty.

### 巴拿马成立临时政府  罗德里格斯任临时总统  巴拿马国务委员会谴责美国侵略
郭伟成
1989-09-01
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    巴拿马成立临时政府
    罗德里格斯任临时总统
    巴拿马国务委员会谴责美国侵略
    本报墨西哥城8月31日电  记者郭伟成报道：巴拿马国务委员会今天决定成立新临时政府，并任命罗德里格斯为临时政府总统，接替9月1日任期届满的现政府代总统帕尔马。
    墨西哥城各电视台报道，由巴拿马国防军总司令诺列加将军、代总统帕尔马及政府部长组成的国务委员会今天上午举行特别会议，讨论了巴拿马面临的局势。
    新华社巴拿马城8月31日电　巴拿马国务委员会31日发表声明，谴责美国近2年来对巴拿马进行“粗暴侵略”。
    由军队、议会和政府组成的最高顾问机构国务委员会在任命新的临时政府后发表声明说，近年来，美国对巴拿马的侵略行径包括经济制裁、干涉巴拿马大选和违背巴、美新运河条约而采取的敌对行动。
