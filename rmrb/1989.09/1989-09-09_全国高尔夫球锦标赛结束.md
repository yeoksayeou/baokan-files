Title: The National Golf Championship Concludes
Author: Wú Huì
Date: 1989-09-09
Page: 3

Reporter Wu Hui reports from Beijing: The 1989 National Golf Championship concluded yesterday at the Beijing Golf Club. The Zhongshan Golf Club team from Guangdong province swept all the titles in the men's and women's team and individual competitions. Apart from the Beijing women's team securing the team runner-up position, the second and third places in all other events were also taken by teams from Guangdong, specifically from Zhongshan, Zhuhai, and Shenzhen, indicating a trend of stronger golf performance in southern China compared to the north.

According to officials from the Chinese Golf Association, this competition serves as the first step in selecting athletes for the Asian Games. The final selection will require further rounds of competition and evaluation.

### 全国高尔夫球锦标赛结束
吴骅
1989-09-09
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    全国高尔夫球锦标赛结束
    本报北京9月9日讯　记者吴骅报道：1989年全国高尔夫球锦标赛昨天在北京高尔夫球俱乐部结束。广东中山高尔夫球俱乐部队囊括了男女团体赛、男女个人赛的全部冠军。除北京女队获团体亚军外，其余各项的第2、3名均为广东的中山、珠海和深圳队包揽，显示出我国高尔夫球运动南强北弱的趋势。
    据中国高尔夫球协会人士称，这次比赛将作为挑选我国参加亚运会队员的第一步，最终确定人选还要再经过几番较量和选择。
