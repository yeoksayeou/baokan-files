Title: Delegations from the National Assembly of Pakistan and the Federal Assembly of the Comoros to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-09-22
Edition: 2

A delegation from the National Assembly of Pakistan and the Vice Chairman of the Federal Assembly of the Comoros will visit China.

	BEIJING, Sept. 21 (Xinhua) -- The spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs today announced that, at the invitation of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, a delegation from the National Assembly of Pakistan, headed by Speaker Mairaj Khalid, will pay a friendly visit to China from Sept. 24 to Oct. 2.

	The spokesman also announced at the news briefing this afternoon that, at the invitation of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the First Vice Chairman of the Federal Assembly of the Comoros, Abdulla Ahmed Nasuf, and the Third Vice Chairman, Said Bakar, will visit China from Sept. 27 to Oct. 2.

### 巴基斯坦国民议会代表团科摩罗联邦议会副议长将访华

1989-09-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    巴基斯坦国民议会代表团科摩罗联邦议会副议长将访华
    新华社北京9月21日电  外交部发言人今天宣布，应全国人大常委会的邀请，由议长迈拉吉·哈立德率领的巴基斯坦国民议会代表团一行十二人将于9月24日至10月2日对中国进行友好访问。
    发言人在下午的新闻发布会上还宣布，应全国人大常委会的邀请，科摩罗联邦议会第一副议长阿卜杜拉·阿迈德·纳索夫和第三副议长赛义德·巴卡尔将于9月27日至10月2日访华。
