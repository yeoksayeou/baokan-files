Title: Chinese and Tunisian Foreign Ministers Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1989-09-26
Edition: 3

Chinese and Tunisian Foreign Ministers Hold Talks
Both Sides Reaffirm Support for the Palestinian Cause

	NEW DELHI, Sept. 24 (Xinhua) -- Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen and Tunisian Foreign Minister Hedi Es-Sebei held a private meeting here this morning.

	During the meeting, both sides introduced their respective domestic situations.

	The Chinese side expressed understanding of the changes that have taken place in Tunisia since November 7, 1987, which have brought Tunisia into a new stage of development.

	The Tunisian side expressed understanding of the events that occurred in Beijing, the capital of China, in June this year, considering it purely an internal affair of China and that foreign countries should not interfere.

	The two foreign ministers also discussed the international situation.

	Both sides expressed satisfaction with the cooperative relationship between the two countries and discussed ways to further strengthen bilateral cooperation.

	NEW DELHI, Sept. 24 (Xinhua) -- Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen and Tunisian Foreign Minister Hedi Es-Sebei reaffirmed here today that China and Tunisia support the just cause of the Palestinian people.

	Speaking at a welcoming luncheon hosted by Es-Sebei, Qian said, "China highly appreciates Tunisia's valuable contributions to strengthening unity and cooperation among Arab and Maghreb countries, to supporting the just cause of the Palestinian people, and to seeking a fair and comprehensive solution to the Middle East problem."

	He reaffirmed China's consistent position of advocating a political solution to the Middle East problem and supporting the convening of an international peace conference on the Middle East under the auspices of the United Nations. He emphasized China's support for the flexible and pragmatic policies adopted by the PLO and condemned Israel's policy of aggression and expansion.

	Es-Sebei said at the banquet, "Due to Israel's stubborn and arrogant attitude, all efforts to achieve a comprehensive and just solution have been continuously sabotaged, and the Palestinian people continue to experience increasingly cruel tragedies."

	Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen arrived in Tunis from Damascus on September 24, beginning a three-day official visit to Tunisia.

	Tunisia is the last stop of the Chinese Foreign Minister's Middle East tour.

### 中突两国外长举行会谈  双方重申支持巴勒斯坦事业

1989-09-26
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    中突两国外长举行会谈
    双方重申支持巴勒斯坦事业
    新华社突尼斯9月24日电  中国外长钱其琛和突尼斯外长埃谢赫今天上午在这里举行单独会谈。
    会谈时，双方介绍了各自国内的情况。
    中国方面对突尼斯1987年11月7日以后所发生的变革表示理解。这一变革使突尼斯进入了一个新的发展阶段。
    突尼斯方面对今年6月中国首都北京发生的事件表示理解，认为这完全是中国的内政，外国不应进行干涉。
    两国外长在会谈时还讨论了国际局势。
    双方对两国间的合作关系表示满意，并讨论了进一步加强双边合作的途径。
    新华社突尼斯9月24日电中国外长钱其琛和突尼斯外长埃谢赫今天在这里重申，中、突两国支持巴勒斯坦正义事业。
    钱其琛在埃谢赫为他举行的欢迎午宴上发表讲话说，中国高度赞扬突尼斯为加强阿拉伯国家和马格里布国家的团结与合作，为支持巴勒斯坦正义事业和谋求公正和全面地解决中东问题作出的宝贵贡献。
    他重申中国要求政治解决中东问题和支持在联合国主持下召开中东和平国际会议的一贯立场。他强调中国支持巴解组织采取的灵活和注重实际的政策，并谴责以色列的侵略扩张政策。
    埃谢赫在宴会上发表讲话说，由于以色列顽固坚持傲慢态度，不断破坏为达成一项全面和公正的解决办法所作出的一切努力，巴勒斯坦人民继续经历着日益残酷的悲剧。
    中国外长钱其琛是9月24日从大马士革飞抵突尼斯，开始对突尼斯进行为期3天的正式访问的。
    突尼斯是中国外长中东之行的最后一站。
