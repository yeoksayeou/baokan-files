Title: Urumqi – “The City of Roses”
Author:
Date: 1989-09-01
Edition: 2

	Urumqi – “The City of Roses”

	Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, September 1 – In the summer of this year, 220,000 rose bushes bloomed in vibrant shades of purple, red, yellow, and white across the streets of Urumqi.

	The rose is the city flower of Urumqi. It is planted along several major roads, in street gardens, and in the courtyards of many institutions and schools. Currently, there is an average of one rose bush for every five residents. The Urumqi Municipal Institute of Gardening and Plant Research is also cultivating new varieties to extend the blooming period.

	Today, Urumqi's greenery coverage has more than doubled since the early days of liberation, with 4.69 square meters of public green space available per person.

### 乌鲁木齐——“玫瑰城”

1989-09-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    乌鲁木齐——“玫瑰城”
    新华社乌鲁木齐9月1日电　姹紫、嫣红、鹅黄、雪白，22万墩玫瑰花于今年盛夏时节，开放在乌鲁木齐街头。
    玫瑰，是乌鲁木齐的市花，全市十几条主要干道和街心花园以及不少机关学校的庭院内都种上了它。现在，平均每5个市民就有1墩玫瑰花。乌鲁木齐市园林植物研究所还在培养新品种，以延长它的花期。
    如今，乌鲁木齐的绿化覆盖率比解放初增加了1倍多，人均占有公共绿地4.69平方米。
