Title: Revolutionary of the Proletariat and Excellent Communist Party Member, Beijing Military Region Advisor Zhang Nansheng Passes Away

Author:

Date: 1989-09-15

Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 15th.

A veteran revolutionary of the proletariat, an excellent Communist Party member, outstanding political work leader of the People's Liberation Army, and advisor to the Beijing Military Region, Zhang Nansheng passed away due to illness in Beijing on August 25th. He was 84 years old.

A farewell ceremony for Zhang Nansheng was held today at the Baobao Mountain Revolutionary Cemetery Hall. Jiang Zemin, Deng Xiaoping, Li Peng, Li Xiannian, Peng Zhen, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Qiao Shi, Yao Yilin, Song Ping, Li Ruihuan, Li Tieying, Li Ximing, Bo Yibo, Liu Lan Tao, Li Desheng, Lu Dingyi, Chen Pishan, Duan Junyi, Geng Biao, Cheng Zihua, Peng Chong, Ni Zhifu, Wang Hanbin, Chen Xitong, Liu Fuzhi, and others, as well as leaders from the Central Committee of the Communist Party, the Central Military Commission, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Ministry of National Defense, the General Staff, General Political Department, General Logistics Department of the People's Liberation Army, Military Commission Discipline Inspection Commission, Beijing Military Region, and the city and county of Liancheng, Fujian Province, sent wreaths.

Yang Shangkun, Wan Li, Qin Jiwei, Song Renqiong, Wang Ping, Yang Dezhi, Xiao Ke, Yu Quli, Chen Xilian, Ji Pengfei, Huang Zhen, Liao Hansheng, Zhao Puchu, Hong Xuezhi, Liu Huaqing, and others attended the farewell ceremony and sent wreaths.

Zhang Nansheng was born in Liancheng County, Fujian Province. He joined the revolution in 1929, joined the Communist Party of China in 1930, and participated in the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in May of the same year. He served as the political commissar of a Red Army regiment, political commissar of a brigade, director of the organization department of the political department of the Red 31st Army, director of the organization department of the political department of the Eighth Route Army, deputy director of the organization department of the political department of the field headquarters, director of the organization department of the political department of the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Military Region, director of the organization department of the political department of the North China Military Region, deputy director of the political department of the North China Military Region, political commissar and director of the political department of the corps, political commissar of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, deputy political commissar of the Beijing Military Region, and a member of the Standing Committee of the Fifth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. He was awarded the rank of lieutenant general in 1955. He participated in the first to fifth counter-encirclement campaigns in the Central Soviet Area and the Long March of 25,000 li. During the War of Resistance Against Japan, he participated in the Hundred Regiments Offensive and anti-sweeping operations against the enemy. During the War of Liberation, he participated in the battles of Shangdang and Jinzhong. During the Korean War, he participated in the command of the battles to repel the enemy's autumn offensive and the summer counteroffensive of 1953. He fought in numerous battles, was brave in combat, and made contributions to the establishment and defense of New China. He long engaged in political work, consciously adhered to the principles of the army's political work, and worked hard to strengthen the revolutionary, modern, and regularized construction of the troops. During the ten years of turmoil, he fought resolutely against the Lin Biao and the "Gang of Four" counter-revolutionary clique. He conscientiously implemented the line, principles, and policies of the Party's Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee and maintained a high degree of political unity with the Party Central Committee.

Zhang Nansheng always maintained and carried forward the fine traditions and style of the Party and the army. He had lofty revolutionary ideals and a strong sense of party spirit, good democratic style and mass perspective, vigorous work enthusiasm, and a spirit of hard struggle. He was loyal to the Party, worked hard, was upright and honest, easy-going and sincere, modest and cautious, united with comrades, was clean and upright, and had a strong sense of justice.

### 无产阶级革命家　中共优秀党员　北京军区顾问张南生逝世

1989-09-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　无产阶级革命家　中共优秀党员
　　北京军区顾问张南生逝世
    新华社北京9月15日电久经考验的忠诚的共产主义战士、无产阶级革命家、中国共产党的优秀党员、我军优秀的政治工作领导者、北京军区顾问张南生同志因病于8月25日在京逝世，终年84岁。
    向张南生同志遗体告别的仪式今天在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。江泽民、邓小平、李鹏、李先念、彭真、徐向前、聂荣臻、乔石、姚依林、宋平、李瑞环、李铁映、李锡铭、薄一波、刘澜涛、李德生、陆定一、陈丕显、段君毅、耿飚、程子华、彭冲、倪志福、王汉斌、陈希同、刘复之等，中共中央、中央军委、全国政协、国防部、解放军三总部、军委纪委、北京军区以及福建省连城县等党政军领导机关送了花圈。
    杨尚昆、万里、秦基伟、宋任穷、王平、杨得志、肖克、余秋里、陈锡联、姬鹏飞、黄镇、廖汉生、赵朴初、洪学智、刘华清等参加了遗体告别仪式并送了花圈。
    张南生出生于福建省连城县。1929年参加革命，1930年加入中国共产党，同年5月参加中国工农红军。历任红军大队政委、团政委、红三十一军政治部组织部部长，八路军师政治部组织部部长、野战总部政治部组织部副部长；晋冀鲁豫军区政治部组织部部长、华北军区政治部组织部部长、华北军区政治部副主任，兵团政委兼政治部主任、中国人民志愿军政治部主任、北京军区副政委，五届全国政协常委等职。他1955年被授予中将军衔。他参加了中央苏区的第一至第五次反“围剿”斗争和二万五千里长征。抗日战争中，他参加了百团大战及对敌反扫荡。解放战争中，参加了上党、晋中等战役。在抗美援朝中，参与指挥了粉碎敌人秋季攻势和1953年夏季反击战等战役。他身经百战，作战勇敢，为建立和保卫新中国作出了贡献。他长期从事政治工作，自觉坚持我军政治工作的原则，为加强部队的革命化、现代化、正规化建设辛勤工作。在十年动乱中，他同林彪、“四人帮”反革命集团进行了坚决的斗争。他认真贯彻执行党的十一届三中全会以来的路线方针政策，在政治上与党中央保持高度一致。
    张南生同志一直保持和发扬了我党我军的优良传统和作风。他具有崇高的革命理想和坚强的党性、良好的民主作风和群众观念、旺盛的工作热情、艰苦奋斗的革命精神。他对党忠贞不渝，艰苦奋斗，光明磊落，平易坦诚，谦虚谨慎，团结同志，廉洁奉公，一身正气。（附图片）
　　新华社
