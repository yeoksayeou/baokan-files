Title: The Establishment of the Chinese Saying and Singing Art Research Center
Author:
Date: 1989-09-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 12th.

The Chinese Saying and Singing Art Research Center, which aims to promote Chinese culture and advance the theoretical research of saying and singing arts in China, was established in Beijing today. The center is composed of a group of middle-aged and young saying and singing artists and theorists. Ma Kun serves as the director, and Li Xifan serves as the advisor.

### 中华说唱艺术研究中心成立

1989-09-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　中华说唱艺术研究中心成立
    本报北京9月12日讯　以弘扬中华文化、活跃和推动我国说唱艺术理论研究为宗旨，以一批中青年说唱艺术家理论家为骨干队伍的中国艺术研究院中华说唱艺术研究中心，今天在京成立。姜昆任中心主任，李希凡任顾问。
