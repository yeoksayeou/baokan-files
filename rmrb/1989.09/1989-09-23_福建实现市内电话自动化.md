Title: Fujian Achieves Urban Telephone Automation
Author: Zhang Mingqing
Date: 1989-09-23
Edition: 2

Fujian Achieves Urban Telephone Automation

FUZHOU, Sept. 21 (Xinhua) -- The era of using "crank-style" telephones in cities and above in Fujian Province has come to an end. Fujian's postal and telecommunications industry has thus entered the ranks of the nation's advanced level.

Currently, the province's urban telephone exchange capacity has increased from 100,000 lines in 1978 to over 180,000 lines today. The number of long-distance circuits has increased from 696 to 3,487, with 58 percent of these being automatic telephones. Communication links have been established with 156 countries and regions around the world.

### 福建实现市内电话自动化
张铭清
1989-09-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    福建实现市内电话自动化
    本报福州9月21日电　记者张铭清报道：福建省县以上城市使用“摇把子”式电话的历史今天结束。福建邮电通讯事业从此进入全国先进行列。
    目前，全省市话交换机自动容量从1978年的万门增加到现在的18万多门，长话电路从696条增加到现在的3487条，其中自动电话占58％。开通了与世界上156个国家和地区的通信联系。
