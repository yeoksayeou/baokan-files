Title: Prince Sihanouk Writes to Chairman Yang Shangkun, Hoping for Continued Chinese Support for the Cambodian People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1989-09-29
Edition: 4

The leader of the Cambodian National Resistance Forces and Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, Norodom Sihanouk, recently wrote a letter to Chairman Yang Shangkun, hoping that the Chinese government will continue to support the just cause of the Cambodian people.

In his letter, Sihanouk said: "The Paris International Conference on the Cambodian Problem concluded its work on August 30, 1989. During the conference, the Socialist Republic of Vietnam demonstrated that it has no political sincerity and is untrustworthy in seeking a comprehensive, fair, and lasting solution to the so-called Cambodian problem."

Sihanouk said: "Unless all types of Vietnamese troops and all Vietnamese immigrants withdraw from Cambodia under effective United Nations supervision, and unless genuine national reconciliation among all Cambodians is achieved, creating the necessary conditions for peace, security, and stability so that the Cambodian people can freely decide their future under United Nations supervision, the so-called 'Cambodian problem' cannot be considered solved. The sole objective of the Cambodian National Resistance Forces is to realize the heartfelt wishes of the Cambodian people, that is, to restore peace, independence, and freedom to Cambodia. We do not want anything that belongs to Vietnam. What we demand is that Vietnam return to us what belongs to Cambodia and the Cambodian people."

In his concluding remarks, Prince Sihanouk said: "On behalf of the Cambodian National Resistance Forces and the United Government of Democratic Kampuchea, I request that you and your government continue to support the just cause of the Cambodian people and support the resolution on 'The Situation in Cambodia' at the upcoming 44th session of the United Nations General Assembly, which calls for the unconditional and complete withdrawal of all foreign troops from Cambodia, thereby allowing the Cambodian people to exercise their inalienable right to self-determination through free elections under United Nations supervision."

### 西哈努克亲王致函杨尚昆主席  希望中国继续支持柬人民斗争

1989-09-29
第4版(国际新闻·国内新闻)
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王致函杨尚昆主席
    希望中国继续支持柬人民斗争
    据新华社北京9月28日电　柬埔寨民族抵抗力量领导人、民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王最近写信给杨尚昆主席，希望中国政府继续支持柬埔寨人民的正义事业。
    西哈努克在信里说：“巴黎柬埔寨问题国际会议已于1989年8月30日结束工作。在会议期间，越南社会主义共和国表明，在寻求所谓的柬埔寨问题的全面、公正、持久的解决方案方面，它毫无政治诚意，不讲信义。”
    西哈努克说：“除非各类越军和所有的越南移民在联合国有效监督下从柬埔寨撤走，除非所有柬埔寨人之间的真正的民族和解得以实现，从而为柬埔寨人民在联合国的监督下自由地作出决定，创造必要的和平、安全与稳定的条件，否则，所谓的‘柬埔寨问题’就不能被认为已经得到解决。柬埔寨民族抵抗力量的唯一目标是实现柬埔寨人民的衷心愿望，即恢复柬埔寨的和平、独立和自由。属于越南的东西我们什么都不要。我们所要求的是越南把属于柬埔寨和柬埔寨人民的东西归还给我们。”
    西哈努克亲王最后在信中说：“我谨代表柬埔寨民族抵抗力量和民主柬埔寨联合政府，请阁下和贵国政府继续支持柬埔寨人民的正义事业，并在即将召开的第四十四届联合国大会上对‘关于柬埔寨局势’的决议给予支持，该决议要求所有外国军队无条件地全部撤离柬埔寨，从而使柬埔寨人民在联合国监督下，通过自由的大选行使他们不可剥夺的自决权。”
