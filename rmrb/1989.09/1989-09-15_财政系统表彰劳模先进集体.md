Title: Financial System Honors Labor Models and Advanced Collectives
Author: Wang Jinhong
Date: 1989-09-15
Edition: 2

The Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Personnel, and the China Finance and Trade Union jointly held a telephone conference in Beijing yesterday to honor Li Shisong and 52 other national labor models in the financial system, as well as 60 advanced collectives, including the financial bureau of the Xinjiang and Boke County Mongol Autonomous County. State Councilor and Minister of Finance Wang Bingqian attended the event to congratulate and deliver a speech.

### 财政系统表彰劳模先进集体
王锦鹄
1989-09-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　财政系统表彰劳模先进集体
    本报北京9月15日讯　记者王锦鹄报道：经国务院批准，财政部、人事部和中国财贸工会昨天联合在北京召开电话会，表彰李士松等53名全国财政系统劳动模范、新疆和布克赛尔蒙古自治县财政局等60个先进集体。国务委员兼财政部长王丙乾到会祝贺并讲了话。
