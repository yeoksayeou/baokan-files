Title: Li Xiannian Meets and Hosts a Banquet for Bishta
Author:
Date: 1989-09-07
Edition: 1

Li Xiannian, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, met and hosted a banquet for former Nepalese Prime Minister Kirti Nidhi Bista and his wife this evening at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

Li Xiannian had a friendly and cordial conversation with Bista on bilateral relations, the situation in South Asia, and the international situation.

Li Xiannian's wife Lin Jialin, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, and Nepalese Ambassador to China Khatri attended the meeting and dinner. Bista is visiting China at the invitation of the Foreign Affairs Institute.

### 李先念会见宴请比斯塔

1989-09-07
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李先念会见宴请比斯塔
    新华社北京9月7日电　政协全国委员会主席李先念今晚在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请尼泊尔王国前首相基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔和他的夫人。
    李先念同比斯塔就两国关系、南亚地区局势及国际形势进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    李先念主席的夫人林佳楣、中国人民外交学会会长韩念龙和尼泊尔驻华大使卡特里参加了会见和晚宴。比斯塔是应外交学会邀请访华的。
