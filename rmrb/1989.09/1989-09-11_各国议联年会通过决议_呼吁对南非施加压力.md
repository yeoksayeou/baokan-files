Title: Inter-Parliamentary Union Annual Meeting Passes Resolution Calling for Pressure on South Africa
Author:
Date: 1989-09-11
Edition: 3

The 82nd Inter-Parliamentary Union conference today unanimously passed a resolution calling for increased pressure on the South African government to ensure fair and independent elections in Namibia.

The one-week conference concluded today here.

The conference also decided to organize an observation team to go to Namibia to verify the legitimacy of the elections and then report to the Inter-Parliamentary Union Council.

Namibia is scheduled to hold its first free elections in November this year to end 74 years of South African colonial rule and achieve independence in April 1990.

The resolution expressed deep concern over the South African government's actions to undermine Namibia's independence and demanded that the South African government strictly adhere to the United Nations Charter and relevant UN resolutions on the issue of Namibia.

### 各国议联年会通过决议　呼吁对南非施加压力

1989-09-11
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　各国议联年会通过决议
　　呼吁对南非施加压力
    新华社伦敦9月9日电　第82届议会联盟会议今天一致通过决议，呼吁对南非当局施加更大压力，以确保在纳米比亚举行公正的独立选举。
    这一为期一周的会议今天在这里闭幕。
    会议还决定组织观察团前往纳米比亚，核查选举的合法性，然后向议会联盟理事会汇报。
    纳米比亚将于今年11月举行第一次自由选举，以便结束长达74年的南非殖民统治，在1990年4月实现独立。
    决议对南非当局破坏纳米比亚独立的行为深表关切，并且要求南非当局严格遵守联合国宪章及联合国有关纳米比亚问题的决议。
