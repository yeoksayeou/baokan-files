Title: China to Host Multiple International Events in October
Author: Wāng Dàzhāo
Date: 1989-09-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 27th.

The State Sports Commission held a press conference this morning to brief the media on the preparations for several major upcoming events in China.

From October 4th to 7th, the Sixth Asian Archery Cup will be held at the Beijing Western Suburbs Shooting Range. Teams from Chinese Taipei, Hong Kong, South Korea, Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, India, China, and Australia have registered to participate. The tournament will use an elimination format to determine the champions in four categories: men's and women's individual all-around and team events.

From October 18th to 22nd, the Third Asian Roller Skating Championships will take place in Hangzhou. This emerging sport, commonly known as roller skating, has a 10-year history in China. The Chinese team, consisting of 22 athletes, will compete against roller skaters from 10 countries and regions. South Korea, India, and Japan are the main contenders for the eight speed skating gold medals and five figure skating gold medals, while the Macau team won the roller hockey championship in the previous edition two years ago.

From October 13th to 19th, China will host the Fifth World Cup Parachuting Championship for the first time. "Sky heroes" from the Soviet Union, East Germany, West Germany, Switzerland, Italy, Denmark, Turkey, Japan, Thailand, Australia, Iraq, and Belgium will join Chinese athletes in Chengdu for precision and freestyle parachuting competitions. Following the retirement of world champions Li Rongrong and Yu Mei, the Chinese team will field a roster of young talents.

### 10月份我承办多项国际赛事  北京、杭州、成都恭候选手们登场亮相
汪大昭
1989-09-28
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    10月份我承办多项国际赛事
    北京、杭州、成都恭候选手们登场亮相
    本报北京9月27日讯　记者汪大昭报道：国家体委今天上午在这里向新闻界通报了近期我国承办几项重要赛事的筹备情况。
    10月4日至7日，第六届亚洲杯射箭赛将在北京西郊射击场举行。报名参赛的有中国台北、香港、南朝鲜、新加坡、泰国、印度尼西亚、印度、中国以及澳大利亚队，届时将试用淘汰制的比赛方法，决出男女个人全能及团体赛的四项冠军。
    10月18日至22日，第三届亚洲轮滑锦标赛将在杭州举行。这项俗称旱冰的新兴项目在我国只有10年的历史。由22名选手组成的中国队将同来自10个国家和地区的轮滑好手一比高低。其中，南朝鲜、印度和日本选手是8枚速度轮滑金牌和5枚花样轮滑金牌的主要争夺者，而澳门队则在两年前的上届比赛中夺得轮滑球冠军。
    10月13日至19日，我国还将首次承办世界性高水平大型跳伞比赛——第五届世界杯跳伞冠军赛。来自苏联、民主德国、联邦德国、瑞士、意大利、丹麦、土耳其、日本、泰国、澳大利亚、伊拉克、比利时的“空中英雄”，将在成都与中国选手一道，进行定点和特技跳伞比赛。在李荣荣、于梅等世界冠军退役后，中国队将以年轻小将出战。
