Title: Chinese Men's Volleyball Team Wins Two Matches in Asian Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-09-19
Edition: 3

The fifth Asian Men's Volleyball Championship entered its third day today in Seoul. In Group C, the Chinese team defeated Australia 3-1. In Group B, the Japanese team easily defeated the United Arab Emirates 3-0. The Chinese team won their first match on September 15th against the Chinese Taipei team, winning 3-1.

### 亚洲男排赛中国队两战告捷

1989-09-19
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　亚洲男排赛中国队两战告捷
    新华社北京9月17日电　据外电报道，在汉城举行的第5届亚洲男子排球锦标赛今天进入第三天。中国队在C组比赛中，以3∶1战胜澳大利亚队。B组的日本队以3∶0轻取阿拉伯联合酋长国队。中国队15日首战中国台北队，以3∶1取胜。
