Title: 78 Foreign Experts Receive Honor Certificates for Contributions to China's Construction
Author:
Date: 1989-09-29
Page: 2

Today, the State Bureau of Expertise for Foreigners awarded honor certificates to 78 foreign experts working in China, recognizing their contributions to China's socialist construction.

According to preliminary statistics, in the 10 years since the reform and opening-up, a total of 120,000 foreign experts from over 60 countries and regions have been invited to work in China, which is four times the number in the previous 30 years. They have worked in 58 departments, 30 provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government, as well as at the grassroots level and construction sites, engaging in teaching, scientific research, journalism, publishing, technical consulting, and improving management. The vast majority of them have worked with a friendly attitude towards the Chinese people, making invaluable contributions to China's socialist construction. They have also enhanced mutual understanding and friendship between the Chinese and foreign peoples.

### 为我国建设事业作出贡献  78名外国专家获荣誉证书

1989-09-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    为我国建设事业作出贡献
    78名外国专家获荣誉证书
    本报北京9月28日讯　今天，国家外国专家局向在中国工作的78名外国专家颁发荣誉证书，表彰他们在我国社会主义建设事业中所作出的贡献。
    据初步统计，改革开放10年来，应聘、应邀来华工作的各类外国专家累计12万人，是前30年的4倍。他们来自世界60多个国家和地区，分别在我国58个部门、30个省、自治区、直辖市的基层单位和建设现场从事教学、科研、新闻、出版、技术咨询、改善经营管理等方面的工作。他们中的绝大多数人怀着对中国人民的友好感情，为我国的社会主义建设做出了宝贵的贡献，同时也增进了中外人民之间的了解和友谊。
