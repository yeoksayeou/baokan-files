Title: Overseas Chinese in France Celebrate National Day and Send Greetings to CCTV's National Day Gala
Author:
Date: 1989-09-30
Edition: 3

On the afternoon of September 24, overseas Chinese and Chinese residents in France held a grand celebration to mark the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China in Paris. During the event, a telegram addressed to China Central Television's National Day Gala was read aloud. The telegram is as follows:

Dear sisters and brothers, friends:

We are celebrating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China with you on the distant land of France. Our hearts are pounding, and our blood is surging. We have so much to say, but in this brief message, we can only express our longing and love for our motherland as overseas Chinese. We firmly believe that our beloved China will make even greater strides on the path of reform and opening up and will proudly stand among the ranks of the world's advanced nations. Let us raise a cup of the sweetest and most fragrant wine to toast the 40th anniversary of the People's Republic of China. May our motherland become ever stronger, more prosperous, and flourishing!

	Traveling Overseas Chinese Club in France, European Times, French Chinese Mutual Aid Association,
	French Chaozhou Association, French Shanghai Friendship Association, Traveling Overseas Zhongzheng Association,
	Traveling Overseas Guangzhao Association, Traveling Overseas Haiqing Sports Association, Franco-Chinese Sports Association,
	Paris Office of China International Airlines, Paris Branch of the Bank of China.

### 旅法华侨华人国庆联欢会  致电我中央电视台国庆晚会

1989-09-30
第3版(国际新闻·国内新闻)
专栏：

    旅法华侨华人国庆联欢会
    致电我中央电视台国庆晚会
    本报讯　9月24日下午，法国各界华侨华人在巴黎隆重举行庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年文艺联欢会。会上宣读了旅法华侨俱乐部等十一个与会单位致中国中央电视台国庆联欢晚会的电报。电报全文如下：亲爱的姐妹兄弟们，朋友们：
    我们在遥远的法兰西土地上和你们一起庆贺中华人民共和国成立四十周年。我们的心在跳跃，我们的热血在奔腾，我们有千言万语要对你们说。但在这短短的电文中，只能表达出我们海外游子对祖国的无限向往与热爱。我们坚信，可爱的中华一定会在改革开放的道路上更快更好地前进，一定会抬头挺胸地走进世界的先进行列。让我们为中华人民共和国四十周年大庆共进一杯最浓最香的蜜酒。祝祖国日益强大、繁荣昌盛！
              旅法华侨俱乐部、欧洲时报、法国华裔互助会、
        法国潮州同乡会、法国上海联谊会、旅法崇正总会、
        旅法广肇同乡会、旅法海青体育会、法华体育会、中
        国国际航空公司驻巴黎办事处、中国银行巴黎分行。
