Title: UN Two Funds Provide Aid for Women and Children's Health Project in Tibet
Author: Liú Wěi
Date: 1989-09-26
Page: 2

Tibet will receive $800,000 in aid from the United Nations Population Fund and the United Nations Children's Fund to support women and children's health initiatives in 16 counties.

According to the World Health Organization's coordination, the two funds have approved a $20.5 million project to strengthen women and children's health cooperation in China. This project will be implemented in 300 counties across 27 provinces and municipalities, including 16 counties in Tibet. The project will commence in 1990 and run for five years.

Coqga, the director of the Tibet Health Department, recently informed a reporter that the aid will be used for women and children's health work.

### 联合国两基金会提供援助  西藏将实施妇幼保健项目
刘伟
1989-09-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    联合国两基金会提供援助
    西藏将实施妇幼保健项目
    本报拉萨电  记者刘伟报道：西藏卫生厅厅长卓嘎日前告诉记者，西藏16个县将得到联合国人口基金会和儿童基金会援助的80万美元，用于妇幼保健工作。
    据介绍，在世界卫生组织的协调下，上述两基金会支援中国2050万美元的“加强中国妇幼卫生合作项目”已经立项。该项目在中国27个省市300个县开展妇幼保健工作，西藏16个县亦包括其中，该项目1990年始执行，为期5年。
