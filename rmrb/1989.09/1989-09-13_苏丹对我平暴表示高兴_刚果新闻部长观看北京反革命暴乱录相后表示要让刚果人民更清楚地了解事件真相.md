Title: Sudan Expresses Satisfaction with China's Suppression of Counter-Revolutionary Riot
Author:
Date: 1989-09-13
Edition: 3

Sudan's Revolutionary Command Council Chairman and Prime Minister, General Omar al-Bashir, today expressed satisfaction with the Chinese government's suppression of the counter-revolutionary riot during a meeting with Chinese Ambassador to Sudan Hui Zhen.

Ambassador Hui Zhen briefed him on the recent situation in China regarding the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riot and China's independent foreign policy.

General Omar al-Bashir expressed satisfaction with China's ability to quickly suppress the riot and stabilize the situation. He also stated that Sudan will continue to strive to develop friendly and cooperative relations with China.

The Secretary of the Central Committee of the Congolese Labor Party and Minister of Information, Paul Ngatsé, highly praised the Chinese government's suppression of the counter-revolutionary riot in Beijing this evening.

Ngatsé made this statement after watching a video about the truth of the counter-revolutionary riot in Beijing at the Chinese Embassy in Congo.

He said, "We fully understand the events that took place in Beijing. This is a lesson for countries implementing an open-door policy." He also suggested broadcasting the video of the truth about the riot on Congolese television to help the Congolese people better understand the events that occurred in Beijing.

### 苏丹对我平暴表示高兴  刚果新闻部长观看北京反革命暴乱录相后表示要让刚果人民更清楚地了解事件真相

1989-09-13
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    苏丹对我平暴表示高兴
    刚果新闻部长观看北京反革命暴乱录相后表示要让刚果人民更清楚地了解事件真相
    新华社喀土穆9月12日电　苏丹救国革命指挥委员会主席、政府总理巴希尔中将今天在会见中国驻苏丹大使惠震时表示对中国政府平息反革命暴乱感到满意。
    惠震大使向他介绍了中国最近平息反革命暴乱的情况和我国独立自主的外交政策。
    巴希尔中将对中国能在短期内平息暴乱稳定局势感到满意，并表示，苏丹将继续努力发展同中国的友好、合作关系。
    新华社布拉柴维尔9月12日电　刚果劳动党中央委员会书记处书记、新闻部长保罗·恩加特塞今晚高度赞扬中国政府平息在北京发生的反革命暴乱。
    恩加特塞是在中国驻刚果大使馆观看了关于北京反革命暴乱真相的录相后表明这一立场的。
    他说：“我们十分理解北京发生的事件，这对实施对外开放政策的国家是一个教训。”他还建议在刚果电视台播放这场暴乱真相的录相，以便使刚果人民更清楚地了解北京发生的这一事件。
