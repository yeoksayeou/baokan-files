Title: Yao Yilin to Visit East Germany, Qian Qichen to Visit Iran
Author:
Date: 1989-09-22
Edition: 2

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs spokesperson announced today that at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and the government of the German Democratic Republic, CPC Central Committee Politburo Standing Committee member and Vice Premier Yao Yilin will lead a Chinese party and government delegation to the German Democratic Republic from October 2 to 9 to attend the 40th anniversary celebrations of the founding of the German Democratic Republic and conduct a friendly visit.

The spokesperson also announced at the press conference that at the invitation of Iranian Foreign Minister Ali Akbar Velayati, Foreign Minister Qian Qichen will pay a friendly visit to the Islamic Republic of Iran from October 6 to 8.

### 姚依林将出访民德  钱其琛将访问伊朗

1989-09-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    姚依林将出访民德  钱其琛将访问伊朗
    新华社北京9月21日电  外交部发言人今天宣布，应德国统一社会党中央委员会和德意志民主共和国政府邀请，中共中央政治局常委、国务院副总理姚依林将率中国党政代表团于10月2日至9日赴德意志民主共和国参加德意志民主共和国建国40周年庆典并进行友好访问。
    这位发言人在新闻发布会上还宣布，应伊朗伊斯兰共和国外交部长韦拉亚提邀请，外交部长钱其琛将于10月6日至8日对伊朗伊斯兰共和国进行友好访问。
