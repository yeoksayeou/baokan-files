Title: Habits of the Mind and Others Visit Injured and Disabled Athletes
Author: Jiang Zhengmao
Date: 1989-09-08
Edition: 3

The Vice Chairman of the National People's Congress, Habits of the Mind, the Vice Chairman of the State Sports Commission, He Zhenliang, the member of the Central Advisory Commission, Qian Xinzhi, and the Chairman of the China Disabled Persons' Federation, Deng Puqiang, along with other relevant leaders, braved the rain this morning to visit the Beijing Institute of Physical Education. They visited the entire delegation of the Chinese team that is about to depart for Japan to participate in the Fifth Far East and South Pacific Region Disabled Sports Games. They encouraged the athletes to uphold the spirit of tenacious struggle and strive for good results.

### 习仲勋等看望伤残人运动员
江正茂
1989-09-08
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    习仲勋等看望伤残人运动员
    本报北京9月8日讯　记者江正茂报道：全国人大副委员长习仲勋、国家体委副主任何振梁、中顾委委员钱信忠以及中国残疾人联合会主席邓朴方等有关方面的负责人，今天上午冒雨前往北京体育学院，看望即将出征日本参加第五届远东及南太平洋地区伤残人运动会的中国代表团全体成员，鼓励他们发扬顽强拚搏精神，争取好成绩。
