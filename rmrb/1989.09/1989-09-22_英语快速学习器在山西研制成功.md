Title: English Rapid Learning Device Successfully Developed in Shanxi
Author: Yao Zheng
Date: 1989-09-22
Edition: 2

A new type of English learning aid—a box-style "English Rapid Learning Device"—has recently been successfully developed by the Shanxi Yizhi Educational Supplies Research Institute. Its exterior is a plastic box equipped with a magnifying glass display, slightly larger in volume than a cigarette box. However, its internal storage capacity is equivalent to several textbooks combined. The content includes English grammar rules, common auxiliary verbs, and the habitual usage of verbs, among other things. It also provides users with over 500,000 sentences of daily English expressions through a matching method.

### 英语快速学习器在山西研制成功
药征
1989-09-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    英语快速学习器在山西研制成功
    本报讯　一种新型英语学习教具——盒式“英语快速学习器”，近期已由山西益智科教用品研究所研制成功。它的外型是一个装有放大镜显示器的塑料盒，体积较香烟盒略大，然而其内储量却相当于几本教科书的总合。其中内容包括英语语法规则，常用助动词和动词的习惯用法等等，并用搭配的方法为使用者提供了多达五十余万句的英语日常用语。　（药征）
