Title: China's Industrial Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-09-04
Edition: 5

		China's Industrial Leaders
		(Part Four)

		Building Materials Industry

		The most advanced cement enterprise in terms of technology: Hebei Jidong Cement Plant. Built and put into operation in December 1984. It adopts the new dry process pre-decomposition technology, with an annual cement production capacity of 1.55 million tons.

		The most advanced float glass production line: Shanghai Yaohua Glass Company. Built and put into operation in December 1988. Production capacity is 4 million weight boxes per year.

		Light Textile Industry

		The largest paper mill: Jiamusi Paper Mill. Built and put into operation in October 1957. Paper production capacity is 150,000 tons per year.

		The fastest paper production enterprise: Fujian Qingzhou Paper Mill. Built and put into operation in 1959. Annual paper production is 85,000 tons, with the highest machine speed of 550 meters per minute.

		The largest salt field: Hebei Tanggu Salt Field. Gradually expanded. Salt production capacity has reached 1.19 million tons per year.

		The largest beet sugar factory: Heilongjiang Hongguang Sugar Factory and Jilin Xinzhongguo Sugar Factory. Built and put into operation in 1954 and 1955 respectively. Sugar production capacity is 60,000 tons per year each.

		The largest sugarcane sugar factory: Guangdong Shunde Sugar Factory and Guangdong Zhongshan Sugar Factory. Built and put into operation in 1965 and 1955 respectively. Sugar production capacity is 82,500 tons per year each.

		The largest brewery: Qingdao Brewery. Gradually expanded. Annual beer production is 130,000 tons.

		The largest synthetic leather factory: Yantai Synthetic Leather Factory. Built and put into operation in December 1984. Annual synthetic leather production is 3 million square meters.

		The largest modern fiber enterprise: Jiangsu Yizheng Chemical Fiber Company. Built and put into operation in December 1988. Production capacity is 240,000 tons of polyester staple fiber and 252,000 tons of polyester chips per year.

		The most advanced modern enterprise for nylon 66 curtain fabric: Henan Pingdingshan Nylon Curtain Fabric Factory. Built and put into operation in October 1983, expanded in September 1987. Annual production capacity is 13,000 tons of coated curtain fabric, with an additional 13,000 tons capacity added in 1987.

		Transportation

		The longest newly built railway main line: Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway. Built and put into operation in December 1965. From Lanzhou to Urumqi, the total length is 1,894.6 kilometers.

		The longest railway double track: Beijing-Guangzhou Railway from Fengtai to Hengyang. Built and put into operation in December 1971. The total length is 1,751.6 kilometers. The Hengyang-Guangzhou section is under construction, with a total length of 528.1 kilometers.

### 中国工业之最

1989-09-04
第5版(国内专页（经济）)
专栏：

    中国工业之最
    （四）
                    建材工业
    工艺技术最新的水泥企业：河北冀东水泥厂。1984年12月建成投产。采用干法窑外分解新技术，年产水泥155万吨。
    最新工艺浮法玻璃生产线：上海耀华玻璃公司。1988年12月建成投产。生产能力400万重量箱／年。
                  轻纺工业
    最大的造纸厂：佳木斯造纸厂。1957年10月建成投产。产纸能力15万吨／年。
    车速最高的造纸企业：福建青州造纸厂。1959年建成投产。年产纸8.5万吨，车速最高550米／分钟。
    最大的盐场：河北塘沽盐场。逐步扩建。产盐能力已达119万吨／年。
    最大的甜菜糖厂：黑龙江红光糖厂、吉林新中国糖厂。分别于1954年和1955年建成投产。产糖能力各6万吨／年。
    最大的甘蔗糖厂：广东顺德糖厂、广东中山糖厂。分别于1965年和1955年建成投产。产糖能力各8.25万吨／年。
    最大的啤酒厂：青岛啤酒厂。逐步扩建。年产啤酒13万吨。
    最大的合成革厂：烟台合成革厂。1984年12月建成投产。年产合成革300万平方米。
    最大的现代化化纤企业：江苏仪征化纤联合公司。1988年12月建成投产。生产能力涤纶短纤维24万吨、聚脂切片25.2万吨／年。
    最新的锦纶66帘子布现代化企业：河南平顶山锦纶帘子布厂。1983年10月建成投产，1987年9月扩建。年生产能力浸胶帘子布1.3万吨，1987年又扩建1.3万吨。
                  交通运输
    最长的新建铁路正线：兰新线。1965年12月建成营运。从兰州至乌鲁木齐全长1894.6公里。
    最长的铁路复线：京广线丰台衡阳段。1971年12月建成营运。全长1751.6公里。衡阳广州段在建设中，全长528.1公里。
                      （原载《奋进的四十年》）
