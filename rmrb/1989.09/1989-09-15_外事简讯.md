Foreign Affairs Brief

	▲ Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen departed Beijing on September 15, transiting through Pakistan, at the invitation to visit Jordan, Egypt, Syria, and Tunisia. He will then proceed to New York to attend the 44th session of the United Nations General Assembly.

	▲ Chen Mu Hua, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the All-China Women's Federation, met on September 14 with a delegation of Brazilian female legislators led by Congressman Eurico Micheles at the Great Hall of the People.

	▲ Ye Fei, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met on September 14 with a delegation of Thai businesspeople led by Dr. Zhang Ke Xu, a member of the Thai Senate, economic advisor to Thai Prime Minister Chatchai, and Chairman of the Sunhua Cheng Group, at the Great Hall of the People.

	▲ A Chinese government delegation led by Vice Premier Zhu Rongji departed Beijing on September 14 for Romania to attend the 10th session of the Sino-Romanian Economic and Technological Cooperation Commission.

### 外事简讯

1989-09-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：外事简讯

　　外事简讯
    ▲中国外交部长钱其琛9月15日离开北京，取道巴基斯坦，应邀前往约旦、埃及、叙利亚和突尼斯4国进行友好访问，然后去纽约出席第44届联合国大会。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、全国妇联主席陈慕华9月14日在人民大会堂会见了以巴西众议员欧尼塞·米契莱斯为团长的巴西女议员代表团。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长叶飞9月14日在人民大会堂会见了由泰国国会上议院议员、泰国总理差猜的经济顾问、顺和成集团有限公司总裁张珂旭博士率领的泰国工商访华考察团。
    ▲由国务委员邹家华率领的中国政府代表团14日离京前往罗马尼亚，参加中罗政府经济技术合作委员会第十次会议。
              （新华社）
