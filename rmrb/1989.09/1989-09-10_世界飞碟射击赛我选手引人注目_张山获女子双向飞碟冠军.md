Title: Chinese Athletes Make a Splash at the World Skeet Shooting Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-09-10
Edition: 3

Chinese athletes have made a notable impression at the World Skeet Shooting Championship. Zhang Shan won the women's double trap title.

NEW DELHI, Sept. 9 (Xinhua) — The World Skeet Shooting Championship continued today in Montecatini. Chinese athlete Zhang Shan won the individual title in the women's double trap event with a score of 214 hits.

This is the second gold medal won by a Chinese woman at this world championship. On Sept. 8, the Chinese team consisting of Zhang Shan, Wu Lanying, and Feng Meimei scored 415 hits to win the women's double trap team title. In today's final, Italy's Ann Parini and Hungary's Eva Vasvari finished second and third respectively, both with 210 hits. In the shoot-off, Parini hit 7 targets, while Vasvari hit 6. Italy's Giovanni Giannelli won the men's double trap title with 223 hits, and Czechoslovakia's Jan Hanecek won the junior men's double trap title with 216 hits.

### 世界飞碟射击赛我选手引人注目　张山获女子双向飞碟冠军

1989-09-10
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　世界飞碟射击赛我选手引人注目
　　张山获女子双向飞碟冠军
    新华社罗马9月9日电　世界飞碟射击锦标赛今天继续在蒙特卡蒂尼进行。中国选手张山在女子双向飞碟比赛中，以214中获得个人冠军。
    这是中国女选手在这次世界大赛上夺得的第二枚金牌。8日，由张山、巫兰英和冯梅梅组成的中国队以415中的成绩夺得了女子双向飞碟团体冠军。在今天的决赛中，意大利的安·帕里尼和匈牙利的埃·瓦斯瓦丽分别名列这个项目的第二、三名，成绩均为210中。她俩在加赛中，帕里尼7中，瓦斯瓦丽6中。意大利的焦万南杰洛以223中获男子个人双向赛冠军、捷克斯洛伐克选手扬·哈嫩以216中夺得青年个人双向赛金牌。
