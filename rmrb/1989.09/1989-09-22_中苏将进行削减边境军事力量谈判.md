Title: China and the Soviet Union to Negotiate Reduction of Border Military Forces
Author:
Date: 1989-09-22
Edition: 2

    China and the Soviet Union have expressed a desire to reduce military forces along their shared border, and both sides will form expert groups to conduct negotiations on this matter.

    The spokesperson made these remarks in response to a journalist's question at a press conference held this afternoon. He said that the timing of the negotiations is still under discussion.

### 中苏将进行削减边境军事力量谈判

1989-09-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中苏将进行削减边境军事力量谈判
    新华社北京9月21日电　外交部发言人李肇星在今天下午的新闻发布会上说，中国和苏联都表示了要裁减两国边境军事力量的愿望，双方将组成专家小组就此进行谈判。
    发言人是在回答记者提问时说这番话的。他说，谈判的时间还在商谈。
