Title: Zero Magnetic Field Laboratory Officially Put into Use
Author:
Date: 1989-09-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 27th.

The first zero magnetic field laboratory in China, built by the Institute of Geophysics under the China Earthquake Administration, was completed and passed national acceptance yesterday, officially going into use and opening to the nation.

The zero magnetic field laboratory creates a space with zero magnetic field within the Earth's magnetic field. If a person enters this space, it can be considered that they have escaped the Earth's magnetic field and various electromagnetic interferences. Even a compass will lose its ability to point north. This laboratory fills a gap in China's research and measurement work in weak magnetic fields, and its performance indicators are at an advanced level compared to similar laboratories abroad.

### 零磁空间实验室正式投入使用

1989-09-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    零磁空间实验室正式投入使用
    本报北京9月27日讯　我国第一座零磁空间实验室由国家地震局地球物理研究所建成，昨天通过国家验收正式投入使用，并向全国开放。
    零磁空间实验室是在地球磁场中建立一个零磁场空间，人若进入这个空间内可以认为是脱离了地球磁场及各种电磁干扰。指北针也失去了指北的作用。该实验室填补了我国在弱磁实验研究及弱磁测量工作方面的一项空白，其性能指标与国外同类实验室相比具有先进水平。
