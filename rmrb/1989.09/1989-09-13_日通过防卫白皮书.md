Title: Japan Approves Defense White Paper
Author:
Date: 1989-09-13
Page: 3

The Japanese Cabinet approved the 1989 version of the Defense White Paper on September 12, which points out that military threats in the Far East still exist. To ensure the country's security and development, Japan should strengthen its unity with Western countries and continue its efforts in defense.

According to the white paper, Japan's defense expenditure for the 1989 fiscal year is 391.98 billion yen, which is 1.006% of the government's projected gross national product for the year.

### 日通过防卫白皮书

1989-09-13
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    日通过防卫白皮书
    日本内阁12日通过的1989年版防卫白皮书指出，远东的军事威胁依然存在，日本为确保国家的安全与发展，应加强同西方国家的团结，继续在防卫方面作出努力。
    据白皮书披露，1989年度日本的防卫费为39198亿日元，为政府所预测的该年度国民生产总值的1.006％。
