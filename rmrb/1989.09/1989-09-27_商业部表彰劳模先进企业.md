Title: Ministry of Commerce Honors Model Workers and Advanced Enterprises
Author: Pan Gang
Date: 1989-09-27
Edition: 2

The Ministry of Commerce and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions held a telephone conference today to announce the conferment of the title of National Commercial Special Labor Model to 88 commercial workers, the title of National Commercial Labor Model to 893 commercial workers, and the title of National Advanced Commercial Enterprise (Unit) to 201 units.

It is reported that among the national commercial labor models being honored, there are managers, factory directors, and heads of departments, as well as workers, engineering and technical personnel, warehouse keepers, agricultural and sideline product procurement staff, shop clerks, barbers, foot masseurs, chefs, and individual commercial operators.

### 商业部表彰劳模先进企业
潘岗
1989-09-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    商业部表彰劳模先进企业
    本报北京九月二十六日讯　记者潘岗报道：商业部、中国财贸工会全国委员会今天召开电话会议，决定授予八十八位商业职工全国商业特级劳动模范称号，授予八百九十三位商业职工全国商业劳动模范称号，授予二百零一个单位为全国商业先进企业（单位）。
    据悉，在表彰的全国商业劳模中，有企业的经理、厂长、主任，有工人、工程技术人员、仓储保管人员、农副产品收购员、商店营业员、理发员、修脚工、烹饪师以及个体商业者。
