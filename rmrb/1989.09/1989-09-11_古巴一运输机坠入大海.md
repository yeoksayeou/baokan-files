Title: A Cuban Transport Plane Crashes into the Sea
Author:
Date: 1989-09-11
Page: 3

A Cuban Air Force transport plane crashed into the sea on September 8th during a training flight. Of the eight crew members, only one was rescued, while the remaining seven are still missing.

### 古巴一运输机坠入大海

1989-09-11
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　古巴一运输机坠入大海
    古巴空军一架运输机八日在一次训练飞行中坠入大海，八名机组人员除一人获救外，其余七人仍下落不明。
