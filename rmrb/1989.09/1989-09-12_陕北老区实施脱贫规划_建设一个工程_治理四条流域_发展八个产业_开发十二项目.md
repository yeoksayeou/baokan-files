    Shaanbei Old Revolutionary Base Implements Poverty Alleviation Plan
    Construct One Project, Manage Four River Basins
    Develop Eight Industries, Launch Twelve Projects

    XINHUA NEWS AGENCY, YAN'AN, Sept. 12 (Reporter Meng Xi'an) — What path should be taken to enable the Shaanbei Old Revolutionary Base to achieve prosperity and poverty alleviation? The Shaanbei Old Revolutionary Base Economic Development Symposium, which concluded today in Yan'an, adopted the 1988-2000 poverty alleviation and prosperity plan for key resource development and utilization. The plan involves implementing one project, managing four river basins, developing eight industries, and launching twelve projects.

    The Shaanbei Old Revolutionary Base has undergone significant changes over the past decade of reform. However, compared to other regions in the country, Shaanbei remains a poverty-stricken area. Out of 33 counties and cities, 21 are still listed as key national poverty-stricken counties, and 21% of the population still faces food security issues.

    The specific content of the plan is as follows: One project — effectively manage 4.1 million mu of barren sands in Yulin, cultivate 200,000 mu of grain fields, 200,000 mu of fish ponds, 300,000 mu of fruit trees, and 270,000 mu of licorice; Four river basins — effectively manage the Wuding River, Xingzi River, Huangpu River, and Tuhui River basins; Eight industries — in industry, develop petroleum, cigarettes, wool and leather, and coal; in agriculture, develop tobacco cultivation, fruit tree planting, livestock, and tuber crops. The eight leading industries should form a scale, potentially increasing output value by 5.1 billion yuan; Twelve projects — develop cattle breeding, cow bezoar, licorice, black currant, asparagus, young bamboo shoots, rose, eucommia, cornelian cherry, acanthopanax, ephedra, pepper, and yam as auxiliary development projects for the eight leading industries. Upon completion, these projects can increase output value by 3.4 to 5.4 billion yuan.

### 陕北老区实施脱贫规划　建设一个工程　治理四条流域　发展八个产业　开发十二项目
孟西安
1989-09-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　陕北老区实施脱贫规划
　　建设一个工程　治理四条流域
　　发展八个产业　开发十二项目
    本报延安9月12日电　记者孟西安报道：走什么样的路子，才能使陕北老区早日脱贫致富？今天在延安结束的陕北老区经济开发座谈会通过了陕北老区1988年至2000年脱贫致富重点资源开发利用规划方案，即实施1个工程，治理4条流域，发展8个产业，开发12个项目。
    陕北革命老区经过10年改革，面貌发生了巨大变化，但与全国其它地区相比，陕北仍然是个贫困地区，33个县市中，被列为全国重点贫困县的仍有21个，还有21％的群众温饱问题尚未解决。
    这个规划的具体内容是：1个工程——治理好榆林410万亩荒沙，开造粮田20万亩，鱼塘2万亩，营造水果30万亩，甘草27万亩；4条流域——治理好无定河、杏子河、黄甫川、秃尾河流域；8个产业——工业上发展石油、卷烟、毛纺皮革、煤炭，农业上发展烟草种植、果树种植、畜牧和薯类，8个主导产业要形成规模，可增加产值51亿元；12个项目——开发牛体培植牛黄、甘草、黑醋栗、芦笋、小蘖、玫瑰、杜仲、山茱萸、刺五加、麻黄、花椒、薯蓣，做为8项主导产业的辅助开发项目，完成后可增加产值34至54亿元。
