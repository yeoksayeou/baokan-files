Title: North Korea Calls on Countries to Pressure South Korea to Release Lim Su-kyung
Author:
Date: 1989-09-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 11th.

North Korea Calls on Countries to Pressure South Korea to Release Lim Su-kyung

According to the Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 11th, the North Korean Committee for the Peaceful Reunification of the Fatherland issued a statement on the 10th, calling on governments, political parties, organizations, and people from all walks of life in various countries to pressure the South Korean authorities to release South Korean student Lim Su-kyung.

The statement said that Lim Su-kyung's visit to Pyongyang was a patriotic act aimed at achieving national reunification. Under no circumstances should this act be regarded as a crime, regardless of the reasons given.

### 朝鲜呼吁各国　迫使南朝鲜当局释放林秀卿

1989-09-11
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　朝鲜呼吁各国
　　迫使南朝鲜当局释放林秀卿
    据新华社平壤9月11日电　据朝鲜《劳动新闻》今天报道，朝鲜祖国和平统一委员会10日发表声明，呼吁各国政府、政党、团体和各界人士迫使南朝鲜当局释放南朝鲜大学生林秀卿。
    声明说，林秀卿访问平壤是争取国家统一的爱国行动，无论以什么理由都不能将这一行动看作犯罪。
