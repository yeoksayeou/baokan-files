Title: Veteran of the Xinhai Revolution Wang Zhaopei Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-09-23
Edition: 2

Veteran of the Xinhai Revolution, member of the Central Supervisory Committee of the China National Democratic Construction Association, member of the Standing Committee of the Fujian Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and advisor to the Fujian Provincial Committee of the China National Democratic Construction Association, Mr. Wang Zhaopei, passed away in Fuzhou on September 17 at the age of 100.

Mr. Wang Zhaopei was a native of Zhangzhou, Fujian. Born in 1890, he joined the Revolutionary Alliance in 1907. In 1908, he secretly went to Taiwan to recruit members for the Revolutionary Alliance. In 1910, he returned to Zhangzhou to participate in the activities to liberate Zhangzhou. Later, he went to Japan to study medicine, joined the Chinese Revolutionary Party, and returned to China to establish the Zhaopei Hospital in Fuzhou.

After the liberation, he served as a member of the Standing Committee of the Fujian Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, a representative of the Fujian Provincial People's Congress, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of the Fuzhou Municipal People's Congress, vice-chairman of the Fuzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and vice-chairman of the Fujian Provincial Committee of the China National Democratic Construction Association.

During Mr. Wang Zhaopei's hospitalization, Chen Guangyi, secretary of the Fujian Provincial Committee, and Wang Zhaoguo, governor of the province, visited the hospital several times to check on his condition.

### 辛亥革命老人王兆培病逝

1989-09-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    辛亥革命老人王兆培病逝
    新华社福州九月二十日电　辛亥革命老人，民革中央监察委员、福建省政协常委、福建省民革顾问王兆培先生九月十七日在福州病逝，享年一百岁。
    王兆培先生是福建漳州人，生于一八九○年。他一九○七年参加同盟会，一九○八年潜往台湾发展同盟会员，一九一○年回漳州参加光复漳州活动，后到日本学医，参加中华革命党，回国后在福州开设兆培医院。
    解放后他历任福建省政协常委、省人大代表，福州市人大常委会副主任、市政协副主席，省民革副主委等职。
    福建省委书记陈光毅、省长王兆国等在王兆培先生住院期间，多次到医院探望。
