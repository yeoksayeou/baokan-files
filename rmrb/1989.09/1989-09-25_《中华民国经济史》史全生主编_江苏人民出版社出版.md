Title: Economic History of the Republic of China
Author: Lan Dalì
Date: 1989-09-25
Page: 6

The book "Economic History of the Republic of China" edited by Shi Quansheng is the first monograph published after liberation that studies the social economy of the Republic of China period. The book provides a comprehensive explanation of the development and changes in the social economy throughout the entire Republic of China period, with a focus on the period from 1927 to 1949 during the rule of the Nanjing National Government. It particularly emphasizes the economic conditions in the areas under the Kuomintang's control from 1927 to 1937, the economy of the rear areas during the War of Resistance against Japanese Aggression, and the economy of the areas occupied by Japanese imperialism.

    这是解放以后出版的第一部研究民国时期社会经济的专著。该书在全面阐述整个民国时期社会经济发展变化的基础上，重点放在一九二七—一九四九年的南京国民政府统治时期。其中对一九二七—一九三七年的国民党统治区经济、抗日战争时期大后方的经济和日本帝国主义占领的沦陷区经济的阐述，尤为重点突出。
