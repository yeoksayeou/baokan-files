Title: Outstanding Grassroots Soldiers of the PLA Share Their Advanced Experiences in Beijing
Author: Luoyuwen
Date: 1989-09-30
Edition: 4

The PLA General Staff, General Political Department, and General Logistics Department held a grand meeting today to report on the advanced experiences of outstanding grassroots soldiers in the PLA who have dedicated themselves to national defense.

Five comrades from the grassroots level gave typical speeches at the meeting. They are: Chen Huaping, the political instructor of a communication company in a certain unit of the Jinan Military Region, who loves his position and does a good job in the ideological and political work of the soldiers; Wang Xinmin, a military doctor who has dedicated himself for nineteen years to a border outpost in the deep grasslands of Xilingol, Inner Mongolia; Guo Hekui, the camp commander of the oil pipeline management team of the Qinghai-Tibet logistics department of the General Logistics Department, who has rooted himself in the Tanggula Mountains; Ye Ling, a female assistant engineer of the Nanjing Military Region Survey and Mapping Brigade, who has dedicated her knowledge and youth to the military surveying and mapping cause; and Teng Wanming, the company commander of the 5th Company of a certain unit of the Shenyang Military Region, who has diligently performed his duties and striven to build a strong company. Their reports were met with rounds of applause from 1,000 officers and soldiers from the PLA's ground, naval, and air forces stationed in Beijing.

Leaders from the Central Military Commission, including Hong Xuezhi, Liu Huaqing, Qin Jiwei, Chen Haodian, Yang Baibing, and Zhao Nanqi, attended the meeting. Yang Baibing, the director of the General Political Department, spoke at the meeting, reviewing the achievements made in grassroots construction in the military over the past few years. He praised the broad grassroots cadres in the military for maintaining a firm and correct political direction, rooting themselves in the grassroots, working hard, and making silent contributions. They have made outstanding contributions in strengthening grassroots construction in their respective positions.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 29th (reporter Luoyuwen), the PLA General Staff, General Political Department, and General Logistics Department held a reception tonight to welcome military heroes and models attending the National Labor Model Conference in Beijing and grassroots advanced representatives participating in the National Day activities.

In his speech, General Staff Director Chen Haodian first, on behalf of the Central Military Commission and the General Staff, General Political Department, and General Logistics Department, extended warm welcome and congratulations to the heroes and models and grassroots advanced representatives. He also extended holiday greetings to all officers and soldiers, civilian cadres, workers, and families in the military. Leaders from the Central Military Commission, including Hong Xuezhi, Liu Huaqing, Yang Baibing, and Zhao Nanqi, as well as leaders from the PLA General Staff, General Political Department, General Logistics Department, Military Commission Discipline Inspection Commission, and Military Commission Office, attended the reception.

### 全军优秀基层官兵  在京介绍先进事迹  三总部举行欢迎英模招待会
罗玉文
1989-09-30
第4版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    全军优秀基层官兵  在京介绍先进事迹
    三总部举行欢迎英模招待会
    据新华社北京九月二十九日电  （记者罗玉文）解放军三总部今天隆重举行全军优秀基层官兵献身国防先进事迹报告会。
    来自基层的五位同志在报告会上作了典型发言。他们是：热爱指导员岗位做好战士思想政治工作的济南军区某部通信连指导员陈华平；十九年如一日献身内蒙古锡林郭勒草原深处边防哨所的军医王新民；扎根唐古拉山的总后青藏部输油管线管理团工营营长郭和奎；把知识和青春献给军事测绘事业的南京军区测绘大队女助理工程师叶玲；克尽职守，努力建设过硬连队的沈阳军区某部五连连长滕万明。他们的报告赢得驻京陆海空军一千名官兵的阵阵掌声。
    中央军委领导同志洪学智、刘华清、秦基伟、迟浩田、杨白冰、赵南起出席了报告会。总政治部主任杨白冰在讲话中回顾了军队几年来基层建设上所取得的成就，称赞全军广大基层干部保持了坚定正确的政治方向，多年来扎根基层，艰苦奋斗，默默奉献，在各自的岗位上为加强基层建设作出了突出贡献。
    据新华社北京九月二十九日电（记者罗玉文）解放军三总部今晚举行招待会，欢迎来京出席全国劳模大会的军队英雄模范和进京参加国庆活动的基层先进代表。
    总参谋长迟浩田在致辞中，首先代表中央军委和总参谋部、总政治部、总后勤部向英雄模范和基层先进代表表示欢迎和祝贺，并向全军广大官兵、文职干部、职工和家属表示节日祝贺。中央军委负责人洪学智、刘华清、杨白冰、赵南起及解放军三总部、军委纪委、军委办公厅的领导同志出席了招待会。
