Title: The Fourth News Documentary Film Exhibition Held in Zibo
Author: Liu Guanquan
Date: 1989-09-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, September 9th.

The Fourth China News Documentary Film Exhibition Week began in Zibo, Shandong Province, on September 6th. A total of 89 news documentaries are participating in this exhibition week.

According to Zhao Hua, the chief editor of the Central News Documentary Film Studio, the studio has produced over 10,000 films in the 40 years since the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 第四届新闻纪录电影展在淄博举行
刘关权
1989-09-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    第四届新闻纪录电影展在淄博举行
    据新华社济南9月9日电 （记者刘关权）第四届中国新闻纪录电影展览周，从9月6日起在山东省淄博市举行。参加这次展览周的新闻纪录片有89部。
    据中央新闻纪录电影制片厂总编辑赵化介绍，建国40年来，中央新闻纪录电影制片厂共摄制了一万多本影片。
