Title: Construction Bank's Savings Deposits Double Since the Beginning of the Year
Author: Su Ke
Date: 1989-09-02
Edition: 2

	The People's Bank of China has, under conditions of severe financial strain, actively taken measures, strengthened internal management, and vigorously promoted savings. Significant achievements have been made. As of August 20, the balance of residents' savings deposits has reached 23.4 billion yuan, doubling the 11.6 billion yuan at the beginning of the year.

### 建设银行储蓄存款比年初翻一番
苏克
1989-09-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    建设银行储蓄存款比年初翻一番
    本报讯　中国人民建设银行在资金极为紧张的情况下，积极采取措施，强化内部管理，大抓储蓄，取得很大成效。截至8月20日，居民储蓄存款余额已达234亿元，比年初的116亿元翻一番。　（苏克）
