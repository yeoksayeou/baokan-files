Title: National Diving Competition Opens
Author: Zheng Wenjie
Date: 1989-09-21
Edition: 3

The Healthy Power Cup National Diving Championship opened on September 20 in Sanshui, Guangdong. World-class athletes such as Xu Yanmei and Tan Liangde, along with other top divers from 15 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions, will compete over the next six days. In the first day's competition, Guangdong's Tan Liangde and Guangxi's Yu Xiaoling won the men's and women's 1-meter springboard titles with scores of 416.01 and 575.10, respectively.

### 全国跳水赛揭幕
郑文杰
1989-09-21
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    全国跳水赛揭幕
    本报讯　健力宝杯全国跳水锦标赛9月20日在广东三水揭幕。包括世界名将许艳梅、谭良德等来自15个省、市、自治区的跳水好手，将参加为期6天的角逐。在第一天的比赛中，广东谭良德和广西余晓玲分别以416.01分和575.10分夺取男女1米跳板冠军。
              （郑文杰）
