Title: China Consistently Supports the Non-Aligned Movement
Author:
Date: 1989-08-31
Edition: 1

China has consistently supported the Non-Aligned Movement's principles of independence and non-alignment, as well as its stance against imperialism, colonialism, racism, and all forms of external domination and hegemony, and its struggle to establish a new international economic order.

In response to a journalist's question about China's comments on the upcoming summit of non-aligned leaders, the spokesperson stated, “We wish this conference a complete success and hope that the Non-Aligned Movement will play an even more important role on the contemporary international stage.”

### 中国一贯支持不结盟运动

1989-08-31
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    中国一贯支持不结盟运动
    新华社北京8月31日电　外交部发言人李肇星今天下午在新闻发布会上说，中国一贯支持不结盟运动独立自主和非集团的宗旨及其反帝、反殖、反对种族主义和反对一切形式的外来统治和霸权、争取建立国际经济新秩序的立场。
    在有记者问及中国政府对即将召开的不结盟首脑会议有何评论时，发言人说，“我们预祝这次会议取得圆满成功，并希望不结盟运动在当代国际舞台上发挥更重要的作用。”
