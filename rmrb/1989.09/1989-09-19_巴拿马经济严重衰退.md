Title: Panama's Economy in Severe Recession
Author:
Date: 1989-09-19
Edition: 3

Panama's economy is in severe recession. The prolonged political crisis and U.S. economic sanctions have caused unprecedented economic decline. According to official materials from Panama, the country's GDP in 1988 decreased by 17 percent, regressing to the level of a decade ago. The number of unemployed people in the country has doubled in the past two years, exceeding 23 percent. Meanwhile, the Panamanian government's fiscal revenue has significantly decreased.

### 巴拿马经济严重衰退

1989-09-19
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　巴拿马经济严重衰退
    连续二年多的政治危机和美国的经济制裁给巴拿马造成了前所未有的经济衰退。根据巴拿马官方的材料说，巴拿马的一九八八年国民生产总值下降了百分之十七，倒退到十年前的水平。该国失业人数在最近二年增加了一倍，超过百分之二十三。与此同时，巴拿马政府的财政收入大幅度减少。
          （据新华社）
