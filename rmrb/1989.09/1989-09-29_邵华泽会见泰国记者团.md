Title: Shao Huaze Meets with Thai Journalist Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-09-29
Edition: 2

The General Editor of the People's Daily, Shao Huaze, held a banquet this evening to welcome the Thai journalist delegation led by the General Editor of the *Xin Zhongyuan Daily*, He Yun.

The Thai journalist delegation, consisting of 6 people, arrived in Beijing on September 27 at the invitation of the People's Daily. They will participate in the activities marking the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and will also visit Xi'an.

### 邵华泽会见泰国记者团

1989-09-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    邵华泽会见泰国记者团
    本报北京9月28日讯　《人民日报》总编辑邵华泽今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎以《新中原报》总编辑何韵为团长的泰国记者团。
    泰国记者团一行6人，是应人民日报社邀请于9月27日到京的。他们将参加我国国庆40周年活动，并将去西安访问。
