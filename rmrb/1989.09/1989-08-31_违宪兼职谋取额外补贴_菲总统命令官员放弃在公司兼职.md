Title: Unconstitutional Part-time Jobs for Extra Allowances
Author:
Date: 1989-08-31
Edition: 3

The Philippine President Corazon Aquino ordered cabinet ministers and other officials to give up their part-time jobs in state-owned or government-controlled companies on August 29.

A report released by Senator Teofisto Guingona on August 28 revealed that 57 government officials had violated the constitution by holding multiple positions and receiving extra allowances. The top 10 names on the list are cabinet ministers.

The Philippine Constitution prohibits the President, Vice President, cabinet ministers, deputy ministers, or their assistants from holding part-time jobs or positions in state-owned enterprises or government-controlled companies (including subsidiaries) during their tenure.

It was disclosed that the ministers who received the most extra allowances from part-time jobs were: Budget Minister Guillermo Carague, Trade Minister Jose Concepcion Jr., and Finance Minister Vicente Jayme.

### 违宪兼职谋取额外补贴  菲总统命令官员放弃在公司兼职

1989-08-31
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    违宪兼职谋取额外补贴
    菲总统命令官员放弃在公司兼职
    新华社马尼拉8月29日电  菲律宾总统科拉松·阿基诺29日命令在国营或政府控制的公司里兼职的内阁部长和其他官员放弃那些兼职。
    参议员特奥菲斯托·金戈纳28日发表的一份报告指出，有57名政府官员违反宪法规定，他们因担任多项职务而收到额外津贴，名单上的前10名是内阁部长。
    菲律宾宪法规定，禁止总统、副总统、内阁部长和副部长或者他们的助手在任职期间兼职或者在国营企业或政府控制的公司（包括子公司）里任职。
    据透露，因兼职而得到额外补贴最多的部长有：预算部长圭莱尔莫·卡拉格、贸易部长小何塞·康塞普西翁和财政部长维森特·海梅。
