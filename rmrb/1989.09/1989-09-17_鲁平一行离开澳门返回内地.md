Title: Lu Ping and His Delegation Leave Macau and Return to Mainland China
Author:
Date: 1989-09-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Macau, September 16th.

Lu Ping, Deputy Director of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macau Affairs Office, Secretary-General of the Basic Law Drafting Committee for Macau, and Convenor of the Basic Law (Draft) Structure Drafting Group, along with six other mainland members of the drafting group, concluded their 13-day consultation visit to Macau and departed today to return to the mainland.

On the 14th, the Basic Law (Draft) Structure Drafting Group held its second working meeting here to discuss the drafting of the Basic Law (Draft) structure. The meeting decided to complete the drafting of the structure (draft) by mid-October, and after it is approved by the next meeting of the drafting group, it will be published to solicit opinions from various sectors of society once again.

### 鲁平一行离开澳门返回内地

1989-09-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　鲁平一行离开澳门返回内地
    据新华社澳门9月16日电　国务院港澳办公室副主任、澳门基本法起草委员会秘书长、基本法结构（草案）起草小组召集人鲁平和起草小组内地委员一行6人，结束在澳门13天的咨询访问活动，于今天上午离开澳门返回内地。
    14日，基本法结构（草案）起草小组在此间举行第二次工作会议，研究基本法结构（草案）起草事宜。会议决定，在10月中旬之前完成结构（草案）的起草工作，经起草小组下次会议讨论通过后予以公布，再次征询各界人士的意见。
