Title: My Beloved Country
Author: Juzi
Date: 1989-09-28
Edition: 8

In a few more days,
It will be your 40th birthday.
I went to the flower shop, but no bouquet of flowers
Could express my heartfelt wishes.
I want to pick all the red leaves from Xishan,
I want to pluck the azaleas from every corner of Jinggangshan,
I want to weave a silver chain with the stream's flowing water,
I want to weave brocade with the colorful clouds,
I want to send you a boat of love with my sparkling eyes,
I want to warm your heart with my hot blood...

Country,
My beloved country,
We do not need to sigh over the hardships we have already endured,
We will not be afraid of the obstacles we may encounter on the road ahead,
We do not mind the injustices we have suffered,
We will not yield to criticism from outside.
What we value is the happiness of 1.1 billion people,
What we value is correcting our own shortcomings,
What we value is the dignity of the nation,
What we value is the security of the republic.
Open the doors and windows to welcome the east wind,
The Chinese nation will never fall,
Let us welcome the day of soaring!

1989 September, Paris

### 我可爱的祖国
桔子
1989-09-28
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    我可爱的祖国
    桔　子
    再过几天
    就是你四十岁的生日。
    我去过花店没有哪一束鲜花
    能表达我深切的心愿。
    我要采来西山所有的红叶，
    我要摘来井冈遍野的杜鹃，
    我要用小溪的流水编成银链，
    我要用彩云织成锦缎，
    我要用盈盈眼波送你爱的小舟，
    我要用热血温暖你的心田……
    祖国
    我可爱的祖国
    我们不必叹息已经受过太多的苦难，
    我们不会惧怕前路还要碰到的阻拦，
    我们并不介意曾经蒙受的委屈，
    我们不会屈服来自外面的责难。
    我们重视的是十一亿人的幸福，
    我们重视的是纠正自己的缺点，
    我们重视的是民族的自尊，
    我们重视的是共和国的安全。
    敞开的门窗迎接东风吧，
    中华民族再不会倒下，
    让我们迎接腾飞的一天！
            一九八九年九月于巴黎
