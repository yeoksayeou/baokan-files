Title: The World Energy Conference Opens in Montreal
Author:
Date: 1989-09-19
Page: 3

The 14th General Assembly of the World Energy Conference opened in Montreal, Canada, on the afternoon of September 17. Attending the conference were 3,700 energy decision-makers and experts from various countries and regions, including more than 40 ministers. China's Minister of Energy, Huang Yizheng, also led a delegation to attend the conference.

### 世界能源会议在加开幕

1989-09-19
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　世界能源会议在加开幕
    世界能源会议第十四次代表大会十七日下午在加拿大的蒙特利尔市开幕。出席会议的有来自世界各国和地区的能源决策人士和专家共三千七百人，其中包括四十多位部长。中国能源部长黄毅诚也率团出席大会。
