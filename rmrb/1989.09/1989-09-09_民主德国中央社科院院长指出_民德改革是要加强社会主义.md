Title: Director of the Central Institute of Social Sciences of the GDR Emphasizes that GDR Reforms Aim to Strengthen Socialism
Author:
Date: 1989-09-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 8th.

The director of the Central Institute of Social Sciences of the German Democratic Republic (GDR), Reinhold, emphasized in his speech at the opening ceremony of the 1989-1990 academic year that the purpose of the GDR's reforms is to strengthen socialism and to develop socialism to a higher stage.

He pointed out that the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED) has always paid attention to the continuity of policies and reforms when formulating social development plans. The SED will fully value the successful experiences and traditions proven by practice and pay attention to their application under new conditions.

He said that the GDR pays attention to combining the basic principles of Marxism-Leninism with the national and historical conditions of the country. The SED regards the revolutionary process of the GDR as a history of social reform.

Reinhold said that this is the reform of the GDR, which is fundamentally different from the reforms suggested by bourgeois and social democratic politicians and ideological experts. According to their "suggestions," the "reform" would ultimately lead the GDR to a form of bourgeois democracy and capitalist society.

### 民主德国中央社科院院长指出  民德改革是要加强社会主义

1989-09-09
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    民主德国中央社科院院长指出
    民德改革是要加强社会主义
    新华社柏林9月8日电  德国统一社会党中央委员、中央社会科学院院长赖因霍尔德8日在科学院1989—1990年度学习年开幕式上讲话时强调，民主德国改革的目的是为了加强社会主义，把社会主义发展到一个更高的阶段。
    他指出，德国统一社会党在制定社会发展方案时，一贯注意把政策的连续性同改革辩证地统一起来。德国统一社会党将充分重视被实践证明是成功的、好的经验和传统，并注意在新的条件下加以运用。
    他说，民主德国注意把马列主义的基本原理同本国的民族和历史条件结合起来。德国统一社会党把民主德国革命的进程看作是一部社会改革史。
    赖因霍尔德说，这就是民主德国的改革，它同资产阶级和社会改良主义政治家、意识形态专家建议民主德国实行的改革是截然不同的。按照他们的“建议”进行“改革”，最终只能使民主德国向某种形式的资产阶级民主和资本主义社会转变。
