Title: American Scholars Discover a Giant Hydrogen Cloud
Author:
Date: 1989-08-31
Edition: 3

Astronomers from the Arecibo Observatory and Cornell University in the United States recently discovered a rotating hydrogen cloud that is ten times larger than the Milky Way. This discovery has provided new insights into the formation of galaxies in the universe.

A news release from Cornell University today stated that this massive nebula could potentially become a galaxy in the future. Its existence indicates that new star systems are still forming in the universe. Previously, many scientists believed that all galaxies were formed billions of years ago.

### 美学者发现一巨大氢云团

1989-08-31
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    美学者发现一巨大氢云团
    新华社纽约8月29日电　美国阿雷西沃天文台和康奈尔大学的天文学家们，最近发现了一个比银河系还要大10倍的自转氢云团。这一发现使人类对宇宙中星系形成产生新的认识。
    康奈尔大学今天发表的一份新闻公报说，这个巨大的星云团可能是未来的一个星系。它的存在表明，一些新的恒星系仍在宇宙中形成着。在此之前，许多科学家认为，所有星系都是在数十亿年前形成的。
