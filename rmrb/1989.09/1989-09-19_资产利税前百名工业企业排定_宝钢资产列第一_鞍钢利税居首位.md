Title: Top 100 Industrial Enterprises by Assets and Taxes Before Profits Ranked: Baosteel Leads in Assets, Anshan Steel Tops in Taxes and Profits
Author:
Date: 1989-09-19
Edition: 1

Among China's more than 10,000 large and medium-sized industrial enterprises, which have the most assets and which generate the most taxes and profits? The latest data from the National Bureau of Statistics shows that the modernized steel complex Shanghai Baosteel, built in the 1980s, has a net fixed asset value of 10.87 billion yuan, ranking first nationwide. Anshan Iron and Steel Company, with over 70 years of history and China's first steel base, has a total tax and profit of 2.35 billion yuan, ranking first nationwide.

The National Bureau of Statistics ranked the top 100 enterprises by their net fixed asset value and total tax and profit for 1988. These enterprises have a combined net fixed asset value of 151.8 billion yuan and a total tax and profit (excluding resource taxes) of 46.2 billion yuan, accounting for nearly one-third of all large and medium-sized enterprises.

The top 10 enterprises by net fixed asset value are: Shanghai Baosteel, Victory Oilfield Command, Daqing Petroleum Administration Bureau, Anshan Iron and Steel Company, Central Plains Petroleum Exploration Bureau, Liahe Petroleum Exploration Bureau, Wuhan Iron and Steel Company, Shandong Petrochemical Company, Gezhouba Hydropower Plant, and Daqing Petrochemical Company. These enterprises each have a net fixed asset value of over 3.1 billion yuan.

The top 10 enterprises by total tax and profit are: Anshan Iron and Steel Company, Capital Iron and Steel Company, Beijing Yanshan Petrochemical Company, Wuhan Iron and Steel Company, Yuxi Cigarette Factory in Yunnan, Shanghai Petrochemical Company, Daqing Petroleum Administration Bureau, Shanghai Cigarette Factory, Shandong Petrochemical Company, and Daqing Petrochemical Company. These enterprises each have a total tax and profit of over 1.1 billion yuan.

Today's issue of the China Statistical Information Gazette publishes the list of the top 100 enterprises by net fixed asset value and total tax and profit.

### 资产利税前百名工业企业排定　宝钢资产列第一　鞍钢利税居首位

1989-09-19
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　资产利税前百名工业企业排定
　　宝钢资产列第一
　　鞍钢利税居首位
    新华社北京9月18日电　（记者吴士深）在我国万余家大中型工业企业中，谁的资产最丰、谁的利税最多？国家统计局提供的最新资料显示，80年代新建的现代化钢铁联合体上海宝山钢铁总厂固定资产净值达108.7亿元，列全国第一；拥有70多年历史的我国第一个钢铁基地鞍山钢铁公司利税总额达23.5亿元，居全国首位。
    国家统计局按1988年大中型企业固定资产净值和利税总额多寡，各排定出前100名企业。它们的固定资产净产值共计1518亿元，利税总额（不包括资源税）达462亿元，均接近全部大中型企业的1／3。
    名列固定资产净值前10名的企业是：上海宝山钢铁总厂、胜利油田会战指挥部、大庆石油管理局、鞍山钢铁公司、中原石油勘探局、辽河石油勘探局、武汉钢铁公司、齐鲁石油化工公司、葛洲坝水力发电厂、大庆石油化工总厂。这些企业固定资产净值均在31亿元以上。
    名列前10名的利税大户是：鞍山钢铁公司、首都钢铁公司、北京燕山石化公司、武汉钢铁公司、云南玉溪卷烟厂、上海石油化工总厂、大庆石油管理局、上海卷烟厂、齐鲁石油化工公司、大庆石油化工总厂。这些企业利税总额均超过11亿元。
    今天出版的中国统计信息报，刊登了固定资产净值和利税总额前100名企业的名单。
