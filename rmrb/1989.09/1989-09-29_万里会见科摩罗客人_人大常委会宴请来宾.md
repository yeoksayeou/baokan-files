Wan Li Meets with Comoros Guests

People's Congress Standing Committee Hosts Reception for Visitors

New China News Agency, Beijing, September 28 (Reporter: Zhu Yunlong) — Today in the afternoon, Wan Li, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met with Abdullah Ahmed Nassuf, First Deputy Speaker of the Federal Assembly of the Comoros, and Said Bakar, Third Deputy Speaker, and their entourage at the Great Hall of the People.

Wan Li expressed his welcome and thanks to the Comoros guests for their visit to China and for participating in the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Wan Li said that the exchange between the parliaments of China and the Comoros and the development of friendly cooperation between the two countries are in the interests of the peoples of both countries.

Wan Li said that the content of China's independent and peaceful foreign policy includes adhering to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, developing ties and friendly cooperation with Third World countries, and supporting African countries in maintaining national independence and defending national sovereignty. It also includes supporting the struggle of the peoples of Southern Africa against South Africa's apartheid policy and supporting the Namibian people in their struggle for national independence.

Nassuf conveyed greetings and good wishes from Comoros President Ahmed Abdallah Mohamed to Wan Li.

Nassuf said that the people of the Comoros have deep feelings for the people of China. China was one of the first countries to recognize the independence of the Comoros. The Comoros love peace. The two countries have many common points in their foreign policies. The Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence advocated by China are also the basic principles of the Comoros' foreign policy.

According to the New China News Agency, Beijing, September 28 — Tonight, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held a banquet at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse to welcome Abdullah Ahmed Nassuf, First Deputy Speaker of the Federal Assembly of the Comoros, and Said Bakar, Third Deputy Speaker, and their entourage.

At the request of Wan Li, Vice Chairman Lei Jieqiong presided over the banquet this evening.

### 万里会见科摩罗客人  人大常委会宴请来宾
朱云龙
1989-09-29
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    万里会见科摩罗客人
    人大常委会宴请来宾
    新华社北京九月二十八日电  （记者朱云龙）全国人大常委会委员长万里今天下午在人民大会堂会见了科摩罗联邦议会第一副议长阿卜杜拉·阿迈德·纳索夫和第三副议长赛义德·巴卡尔一行。
    万里对科摩罗贵宾来华访问并参加中华人民共和国成立四十周年的庆祝活动表示欢迎和感谢。
    万里说，中国与科摩罗两国议会间开展交流、两国发展友好合作关系符合两国人民的利益。
    万里说，中国独立自主的和平外交政策的内容包括奉行和平共处五项原则，发展与第三世界国家的联系和友好合作，也包括支持非洲国家维护民族独立、捍卫国家主权，支持南部非洲人民反对南非种族隔离政策的斗争，支持纳米比亚人民争取民族独立的斗争。
    纳索夫向万里转达了科摩罗总统艾哈迈德·阿卡杜拉·阿卜杜拉曼的问候和良好祝愿。
    纳索夫说，科摩罗人民对中国人民怀有深厚感情。中国是最早承认科摩罗独立的国家之一。科摩罗酷爱和平。科中两国在对外政策方面有许多一致的地方，中国倡导的和平共处五项原则也是科摩罗对外政策的基本点。
    据新华社北京九月二十八日电全国人大常委会今晚在钓鱼台国宾馆举行宴会，欢迎科摩罗联邦议会第一副议长阿卜杜拉·阿迈德·纳索夫和第三副议长赛义德·巴卡尔一行。
    受万里的委托，雷洁琼副委员长主持了今晚的宴会。
