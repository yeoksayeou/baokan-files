Title: Libyan Leader Jalud Expresses Joy Over China's Suppression of Riots
Author:
Date: 1989-09-05
Edition: 3

Libyan Deputy Leader Jalud today said in Tripoli that China's current policies, especially its open-door policy, will promote the development of international socialism.

Jalud told the visiting Chinese Minister of Health, Chen Minzhang, that the Libyan people are pleased with China's suppression of the counter-revolutionary riots in June. He said that the Third World countries are gratified that China has not yielded to imperialists and continues to follow the path it has chosen. Jalud said that the Chinese revolution has set an example for the people of the world. He hopes that cooperation between Libya and China will develop faster and better.

Chen Minzhang told Jalud that China will adhere to the socialist path and will not allow any interference in its internal affairs.

### 利比亚领导人贾卢德  对我平息暴乱感到高兴

1989-09-05
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    利比亚领导人贾卢德
    对我平息暴乱感到高兴
    新华社的黎波里9月4日电　利比亚二号领导人贾卢德今天在这里说，中国当前的政策，特别是开放政策，将促进国际社会主义的发展。
    贾卢德对前来出席利比亚革命20周年庆典的中国卫生部长陈敏章说，利比亚人对中国6月份平息反革命暴乱感到高兴。他说，第三世界国家对中国拒不向帝国主义者屈服、继续走自己所选择的道路而感到欣慰。贾卢德说，中国革命为世界人民树立了榜样。他希望利中两国的合作得到更快更好的发展。
    陈敏章对贾卢德说，中国将坚持社会主义道路，不允许任何人干涉中国内政。
