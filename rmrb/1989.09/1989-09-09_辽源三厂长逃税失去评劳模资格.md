Title: Three Factory Directors in Liaoyuan Lose Labor Model Status for Tax Evasion
Author:
Date: 1989-09-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, September 8th.

Three directors of contracted enterprises in Liaoyuan City, Jilin Province, have recently been disqualified from being named provincial labor models for failing to pay their personal income adjustment taxes.

Since taking over the factories, these three directors have earned substantial bonus incomes and should have paid personal income adjustment taxes of 5,000 yuan, 6,000 yuan, and 61,000 yuan respectively, but they have not paid any of it.

### 辽源三厂长逃税失去评劳模资格

1989-09-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    辽源三厂长逃税失去评劳模资格
    新华社长春9月8日电  吉林省辽源市有3名承包企业的厂长，因不缴纳个人收入调节税，最近被取消省劳动模范的评选资格。
    辽源市这3名厂长承包工厂以来获得了可观的奖金收入，应分别缴纳个人收入调节税5000元、6000元和61000元，但他们一直未缴。
