Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Emphasizes Unity with the Party and Contributing to the Nation
Author: Guō Jiā
Date: 1989-09-27
Edition: 4

The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League held a youth symposium to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China at the Great Hall of the People today. Over 40 outstanding young representatives gathered to exchange ideas and discuss the glorious achievements of the past 40 years.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Hab Zhenxun attended the symposium and delivered a speech. He said that youth have always been the vanguard and main force in the revolutionary and construction causes. He encouraged the broad masses of young people to learn from the self-reliance and hard work of the older generation of revolutionaries, to inherit and carry forward the fine traditions of our party, to have a strong patriotic enthusiasm, and to have a firm and correct political direction, and to achieve greater successes in the four modernizations and reform and opening up.

Tú Xuěxìn, the foreman of the steelmaking workshop of the First Steel Plant of Shougang, a national labor model, Wáng Xīntíng, the director of the village committee and secretary of the branch of the Communist Youth League of Buloutun Village, Miyun County, Beijing, Wáng Qiáng, a security officer of a certain unit of the martial law troops, the "Guardian of the Republic," Wáng Hùníng, a professor of the Department of International Politics of Fudan University, a national advanced worker, and Fù Xiàngdōng, a student of the Class of 1987 of Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, spoke at the meeting respectively.

In his speech, Song Defu, the first secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, required the broad masses of young people to learn from the older generation of revolutionaries, to unite with the party in heart and mind, to contribute to the country with wisdom and strength, and to dedicate their lifelong efforts to the cause of the people.

The symposium was presided over by Liu Yandong, a member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League. Li Yuanchao and Li Keqiang, members of the Secretariat, attended the symposium.

### 团中央迎国庆青年座谈会强调  与党同心同德　为国献力献策
郭嘉
1989-09-27
第4版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    团中央迎国庆青年座谈会强调
    与党同心同德　为国献力献策
    本报北京9月26日讯　记者郭嘉报道：今天，团中央在人民大会堂召开各界青年迎国庆40周年座谈会。40多位优秀青年代表欢聚一堂，交流思想，畅谈40年来的辉煌成就。
    全国人大常委会副委员长习仲勋出席了今天的座谈会并讲了话。他说，青年历来是革命和建设事业中的突击队和生力军。他勉励广大青年学习老一辈革命家自力更生、艰苦奋斗精神，继承和发扬我们党的优良传统，既要有强烈的爱国热情，还要有坚定正确的政治方向，在四化建设和改革开放中取得更大成绩。
    首钢第一炼钢厂炼钢车间班长、全国劳模屠学信，北京密云县布老屯乡学艺厂村委会主任、团支部书记王新亭，戒严部队某部保卫干事、“共和国卫士”王强，复旦大学国际政治系教授、全国先进工作者王沪宁，北京航空航天大学87级学生傅向东等分别在会上发言。
    团中央第一书记宋德福在讲话时要求广大青年向老一辈革命家学习，与党同心同德，为国献力献策，把毕生的精力献给人民的事业。
    座谈会由团中央书记处书记刘延东主持，书记处书记李源潮、李克强参加了座谈会。
