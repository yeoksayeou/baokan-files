Title: Publication of the Biographical Albums "In Memory of Mao Zedong" and Others
Author: Lǐ Guāngrú
Date: 1989-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 9th.

The publication of the biographical albums "In Memory of Mao Zedong" and others was celebrated today at a symposium held in the Great Hall of the People. Attendees commemorated the great achievements of the older generation of revolutionaries and expressed their deep affection for them.

The People's Publishing House has revised and republished four large historical albums: "In Memory of Mao Zedong," "In Memory of Liu Shaoqi," "In Memory of Zhou Enlai," and "In Memory of Zhu De." These albums vividly depict the extraordinary lives of these four great leaders.

At the symposium, Hu Sheng, Ding Guangen, Deng Ligong, Wang Renzhi, and Wang Guangmei, among others, delivered speeches.

Nie Rongzhen sent a congratulatory message for the publication of the albums.

Xi Zhongxun, Kang Keqing, He Jingzhi, and others attended the meeting.

### 《纪念毛泽东》等传记图册发行
李光茹
1989-09-09
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    《纪念毛泽东》等传记图册发行
    据新华社北京9月9日电  （记者李光茹）毛主席辞世已有13年，但他仍活在人民心中。今天在人民大会堂举行的《纪念毛泽东》等传记图册发行座谈会上，人们缅怀老一辈革命家的丰功伟绩，抒发怀念之情。
    文物出版社重新修订出版的《纪念毛泽东》、《纪念刘少奇》、《纪念周恩来》、《纪念朱德》四部大型历史图册，再现了这四位老人家波澜壮阔的一生。
    座谈会上，胡绳、丁关根、邓力群、王忍之、王光美等同志相继发言。
    聂荣臻同志为图册的出版写来贺信。
    习仲勋、康克清、贺敬之等出席了会议。
