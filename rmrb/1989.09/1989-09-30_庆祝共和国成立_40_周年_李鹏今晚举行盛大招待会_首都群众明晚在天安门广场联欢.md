Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Republic
Author:
Date: 1989-09-30
Edition: 1

To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, Premier Li Peng will host a grand reception at the Great Hall of the People this evening, September 30. The capital's residents will hold a grand celebration at Tiananmen Square on the evening of October 1. Central People's Broadcasting Station and China Central Television will broadcast live the proceedings of these two events. Additionally, a gala evening of performing arts will be held at the Great Hall of the People on the evening of September 30.

### 庆祝共和国成立40周年  李鹏今晚举行盛大招待会  首都群众明晚在天安门广场联欢

1989-09-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    庆祝共和国成立40周年
    李鹏今晚举行盛大招待会
    首都群众明晚在天安门广场联欢
    新华社北京9月29日电　为庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年，国务院总理李鹏9月30日晚上将在人民大会堂举行盛大招待会，首都群众将于10月1日晚上在天安门广场举行盛大联欢晚会。中央人民广播电台、中央电视台将现场直播这两项活动的实况。另外，人民大会堂将于9月30日晚上举行文艺晚会。
