Title: Does He Look Like Zhu Jianhua?
Author: Wang Dazhao
Date: 1989-09-12
Edition: 3

Does he look like Zhu Jianhua?

The 17-year-old Shanghai youngster Ni Jian has been practicing the high jump for only one year. This time, in the high jump competition at the Youth Games, he cleared 2.14 meters, winning the bronze medal. His 1.99-meter frame and excellent physical condition have already attracted the attention of many experts.
