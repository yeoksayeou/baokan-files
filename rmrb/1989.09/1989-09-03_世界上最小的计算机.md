Title: The World's Smallest Computer
Author:
Date: 1989-09-03
Edition: 7

Fujitsu, a Japanese company, has developed the world's smallest personal computer aimed at consumers in the United States and Western Europe. This pocket-sized electronic calculator is 22 centimeters long, 10 centimeters wide, and 2 centimeters thick, resembling a notebook and is very convenient to use. According to the designers, its main advantage is that it can connect to the network of International Business Machines (IBM). This miniature computer is equipped with a liquid crystal display and a small keyboard, weighing a total of 450 grams. It is reported that the battery can ensure the device operates continuously for 100 hours. This pocket-sized computer will soon enter the market.

### 世界上最小的计算机
新译
1989-09-03
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    世界上最小的计算机
    日本富士通公司研制成以美国和西欧消费者为对象的世界上最小的个人计算机。袖珍电子计算机长22厘米、宽10厘米、厚2厘米，样子像一本笔记本，使用很方便。据设计师们说，它的主要优点是能够连接到国际商用机器公司的计算机网上。这种微型计算机装有液晶屏和小型键盘，总共重450克。据介绍，蓄电池电源可保证仪器连续工作100小时。这种袖珍计算机，将于最近进入市场。　·新译·
