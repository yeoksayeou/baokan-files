Title: Praise Should Focus on Spiritual Encouragement
Author: Yuan Jianda
Date: 1989-09-12
Edition: 2

Praise Should Focus on Spiritual Encouragement

Song Renqiong Affirms the Methods of the National "Two Awards" Commendation

Our reporter Yuan Jianda reports from Beijing: Today, Song Renqiong, Vice Chairman of the Central Advisory Commission of the Communist Party of China, said at the reporting meeting of the National "Old Age Contribution Elite Award" and "Filigree of Filial Piety Gold List Award" selection guidance committee that carrying out the "two awards" selection activities can promote the construction of socialist spiritual civilization and improve the moral level of respecting the elderly in society. Commendation should focus on spiritual encouragement and political honor. He pointed out that in terms of hard work and thrift, senior comrades should set an example.

The "two awards" selection activities were initiated by the National Committee on Aging at the beginning of last year, and jointly organized by the China Senior Citizens' Magazine and the China Senior Citizens' News. So far, a total of 1,905 advanced individuals have been recommended and selected nationwide.

### 表彰应着眼精神鼓励　宋任穷肯定全国“两奖”表彰方法
袁建达
1989-09-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　表彰应着眼精神鼓励
　　宋任穷肯定全国“两奖”表彰方法
    本报北京9月11日讯　记者袁建达报道：中共中央顾问委员会副主任宋任穷今天在全国“老有所为精英奖”、“敬老好儿女金榜奖”评选指导委员会汇报会上说，开展“两奖”评选活动可以促进社会主义精神文明建设，提高社会的尊老敬老的道德水准。表彰应着眼于精神鼓励、政治荣誉。他指出，在艰苦奋斗、勤俭节约上，老同志要做出表率。
    “两奖”评选活动是去年年初由全国老龄委员会倡导、中国老年杂志社和中华老年报社发起，联合全国26家老年报刊共同举办的。到目前为止，已在全国推荐、评选出先进人物1905名。
