Title: Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Accepting He Jingzhi's Resignation from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1989-09-04
Edition: 2

	Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Accepting He Jingzhi's Resignation from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Education, Science, Culture, and Health Committee of the National People's Congress

	(Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 4) The Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress decided at its ninth session to accept He Jingzhi's resignation from the position of member of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress, to be reported to the next session of the National People's Congress for confirmation; and to accept his resignation from the position of member of the Education, Science, Culture, and Health Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress.

	In accordance with the provisions of the "Organic Law of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China" that "members of the Standing Committee shall not hold positions in state administrative organs, judicial organs, and procuratorial organs; if they hold such positions, they must resign from the Standing Committee," the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress decided to accept He Jingzhi's resignation from the position of member of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress, to be reported to the next session of the National People's Congress for confirmation; and to accept his resignation from the position of member of the Education, Science, Culture, and Health Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress.

### 全国人大常委会关于接受贺敬之辞去人大常委会委员的请求的决定

1989-09-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    全国人大常委会关于接受贺敬之辞去人大常委会委员的请求的决定
    新华社北京9月4日电  全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于接受贺敬之辞去全国人大常委会委员职务和全国人大教科文卫委员会委员职务的请求的决定
      （1989年9月4日通过）
    根据《中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会组织法》关于“常务委员会的组成人员不得担任国家行政机关、审判机关和检察机关的职务；如果担任上述职务，必须向常务委员会辞去常务委员会的职务”的规定，第七届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九次会议决定：接受贺敬之辞去第七届全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员职务的请求，报请全国人民代表大会下次会议确认；并接受他辞去第七届全国人民代表大会教育科学文化卫生委员会委员职务的请求。
