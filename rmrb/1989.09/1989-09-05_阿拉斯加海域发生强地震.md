Title: Strong Earthquake Hits Alaskan Waters
Author:
Date: 1989-09-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

A strong earthquake measuring 7.1 on the Richter scale struck the waters off the southern coast of Alaska on the morning of September 4. According to the Alaska Tsunami Warning Center, the earthquake occurred about 140 miles west of Kodiak Island in the ocean at 5:15 AM.

### 阿拉斯加海域发生强地震

1989-09-05
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    阿拉斯加海域发生强地震
    美国阿拉斯加南部海域9月4日早晨发生了7.1级的强烈地震。据阿拉斯加海啸预警中心报告，这次地震发生在科迪亚克岛西南140英里的洋面上，时间为清晨5点15分。
            （据新华社）
