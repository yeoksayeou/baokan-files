Title: Analysis of How Some College Students Can Get Out of the Wrong Path
Author:
Date: 1989-09-05
Edition: 1

	The fifth page of today's newspaper features an article that discusses the changes in the ideological and emotional states of college students over the past decade. It analyzes their own shortcomings, reflects on mistakes in societal guidance, and explores the correct paths to guide young students out of their misconceptions.
