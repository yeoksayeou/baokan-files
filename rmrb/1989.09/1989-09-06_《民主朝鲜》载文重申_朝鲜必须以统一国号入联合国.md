Title: North Korea Must Join the United Nations Under a Unified Name
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-09-06
Edition: 3

	The People's Daily, Pyongyang, September 6th, Reporter Xu Baokang reports: The organ of the North Korean government, the Democratic Korea, today published an article strongly condemning South Korea's continued push to "join the United Nations," which is an attempt to create "two Koreas" internationally and make it a fait accompli.

	The article states that recently, the South Korean foreign minister declared that regardless of whether it is "joint entry" or "separate entry," South Korea will create an "atmosphere" to achieve this goal in any form.

	The article points out that the actions of the South Korean authorities are an attempt to legalize the division of Korea internationally, which is a challenge to the Korean people who yearn for unity and the world's fair public opinion.

	The article states that South Korea's attempt to "join the United Nations," whether from the perspective of the will of the Korean people for unification, the principle of self-determination of nations, or even from the perspective of the United Nations Charter and United Nations General Assembly resolutions, is illegitimate.

	The article points out that the solution to the problem of Korea joining the United Nations must be after the realization of North-South unification under a federal system, with one name and one seat, to join the United Nations.

### 《民主朝鲜》载文重申  朝鲜必须以统一国号入联合国
徐宝康
1989-09-06
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    《民主朝鲜》载文重申
    朝鲜必须以统一国号入联合国
    本报平壤9月6日电　记者徐宝康报道：朝鲜政府机关报《民主朝鲜》今天发表文章，强烈谴责南朝鲜继续推进“加入联合国”的企图，是在国际上制造“两个朝鲜”，并使之成为既成事实。
    文章说，不久前，南朝鲜外长宣称，不论是“共同加入”，还是“单独加入”联合国，南朝鲜将不拘形式地为实现这一目标而制造“气氛”。
    文章指出，南朝鲜当局的这一举动，是企图使朝鲜的分裂在国际上合法化，这是对渴望统一的朝鲜人民以及世界公正舆论的挑战。
    文章说，南朝鲜“加入联合国”的企图，不论是从朝鲜民族统一的意志上看，还是从民族自决的原则上说，甚至从联合国宪章和联合国大会决议上说，都是非正当的。
    文章指出，解决朝鲜加入联合国问题，必须在以联邦制实现北南统一之后，以一个国号，一个席位，加入联合国。
