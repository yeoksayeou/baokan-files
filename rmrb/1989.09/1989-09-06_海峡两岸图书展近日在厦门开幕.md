Title: The Cross-Strait Book Exhibition Opens in Xiamen
Author: Zhang Mingqing
Date: 1989-09-06
Edition: 2

The first window for book exchanges between the two sides of the Taiwan Strait opened here on September 4. The "Cross-Strait Book Exhibition" held by the Xiamen International Book Exchange Center, which just opened, showcases over 10,000 books.

Among the exhibited books, there are over 7,000 titles from nearly 400 publishers and institutions from the mainland, and over 5,000 titles from Hong Kong and Taiwan. Additionally, there are exhibits of art and crafts, tourist souvenirs, stationery, woodblock prints, video tapes, laser discs, metal paintings, cultural office supplies, and New Year's greetings cards.

### 海峡两岸图书展近日在厦门开幕
张铭清
1989-09-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    海峡两岸图书展近日在厦门开幕
    本报厦门电　记者张铭清报道：海峡两岸进行图书交流的第一个窗口，九月四日在这里启开。刚开业的厦门对外图书交流中心开办的“海峡两岸图书展”，展示了万余种图书。
    展出的图书中，有七千多种来自内地的近四百家出版社和机构，有五千多种来自港台。此外，有工艺美术、旅游纪念品、文房四宝、木板水印画、录像带、激光唱片、金属画、文化办公用品及贺年片等。
