Title: The State Council Appoints and Dismisses National Officials
Author:
Date: 1989-09-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 4th.

The State Council on August 31, 1989, appointed He Jingzhi as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, acting as the Minister, and relieved Yuan Chongwu of his position as Vice Chairman of the State Science and Technology Commission; on August 4, appointed Wang Shouqiang as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Water Resources, and relieved Liu Hongru of his position as Vice Governor of the People's Bank of China.

### 国务院任免国家工作人员

1989-09-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国务院任免国家工作人员
    新华社北京9月4日电　国务院1989年8月31日任命贺敬之为文化部副部长，代理部长职务，免去阮崇武的国家科学技术委员会副主任职务；8月4日任命王守强为水利部副部长，免去刘鸿儒的中国人民银行副行长职务。
