Title: Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao Compatriots Eagerly Contribute to Their Hometowns: 10 Years of Donations Totaling 3.6 Billion Yuan
Author:
Date: 1989-09-10
Page: 4

From 1978 to 1988, the entire province approved the acceptance of donations from overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots for public welfare projects, totaling 3.6 billion yuan.

The main projects include:

	•	Construction, expansion, and renovation of schools: 7,716 (including Shantou University, Jiaoying University, Wuyi University, Shaoguan University, and Xijiang University)
	•	Construction and expansion of hospitals: 874
	•	Construction of senior care facilities: 619
	•	Construction of kindergartens and nurseries: 685
	•	Construction of libraries and theaters: 285
	•	Construction of Overseas Chinese Federation buildings: 528
	•	Construction of bridges: 2,663
	•	Construction of highways: 7,793 kilometers
	•	Construction of water conservancy and hydropower projects: 896

### 华侨港澳同胞热心造福桑梓　十年捐资36亿元

1989-09-10
第4版(国庆专版)
专栏：社会主义事业在前进

　  华侨港澳同胞热心造福桑梓
　　十年捐资36亿元
    1978至1988年，全省经批准接受华侨、港澳同胞捐赠办公益事业的款物折合人民币36亿元。
    主要项目有：
    新建扩建修建学校　7716所（包括汕头、嘉应、五邑、韶关、西江大学）
    新建扩建医院　874所
    兴建敬老院　619个
    建托儿所、幼儿园　685个
    建图书馆、影剧院　285个
    建侨联大厦　528栋修
    建桥梁　2663座
    修筑公路　7793公里
    建水利水电工程　896宗
