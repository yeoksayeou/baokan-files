Title: China's First Translation Introducing Marxist Theory – *A Record of Western Civil Law*
Author:
Date: 1989-09-09
Page: 8

The first Chinese publication to introduce Marx's life and theories was likely the book *A Record of Western Civil Law*, published in 1898 by the Shanghai Guangxuehui. The original title of this book was *The History of Socialism*, authored by K. Kapp. It was first published by the British Blackie Publishing House in 1892 and introduced to China in 1898. At that time, China was facing an unprecedented national crisis, and progressive Chinese intellectuals were advocating for learning from the West and reforming to strengthen the nation. The British missionary Timothy Richard was then heading the largest Christian publishing house in China, the Shanghai Guangxuehui. To align with the reform and modernization trends, the publishing house released numerous books promoting Western Christianity and introducing the history, geography, politics, economy, and culture of various European and American countries. Alongside these, it also introduced various socialist ideologies prevalent in Europe and America at the time, including Marxist theory. K. Kapp's *The History of Socialism* was commissioned by Timothy Richard and translated into Chinese by Hu Yugu for the first time. It was published by the Shanghai Guangxuehui in the summer of 1898 under the title *A Record of Western Civil Law*. Due to limited circulation, the book's influence was quite narrow.

After K. Kapp's death in 1912, the British scholar P. S. re-edited *The History of Socialism*, adding content on the history of socialist movements in various countries from 1892 to 1913. This revised edition was translated by Li Ji and published by the Shanghai Xin Qingnian She in 1920, with a foreword by Cai Yuanpei.

Regarding Marxist theory, the book provides a detailed introduction and commentary on Marx's dialectical materialism, historical materialism, political economy, and scientific socialism. It also gives a relatively objective account of Marx's two major discoveries and the theory that capitalism will inevitably be replaced by socialism.

### 中国第一部介绍马克思学说的译著——《泰西民法志》

1989-09-09
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

    中国第一部介绍马克思学说的译著
    ——《泰西民法志》
    关于马克思的学说何时传入中国，学术界历来众说纷纭。
      据我们接触到的材料来看，在中国最先介绍马克思生平和马克思学说的中文读物，应该是1898年上海广学会出版的《泰西民法志》。
      《泰西民法志》原书名为
  《社会主义史》，著者是柯卡普，1892年该书由英国布莱克书局第一次出版发行，1898年传入中国。当时正值中国面临空前严重的民族危机，先进的中国人要求学习西方、变法图强的维新思潮激荡于中国大地。英国传教士李提摩太正主持基督教设在中国的最大的出版机构“上海广学会”。为了迎合变法维新的思潮，出版了不少宣传西方基督教和介绍欧美各国史地、政治、经济、文化的书刊，并且附带着介绍了当时广泛流行于欧美的各种社会主义思潮，其中包括马克思的学说。柯卡普的《社会主义史》便是受李提摩太委托，由胡贻谷首次译成中文，在1898年夏由上海广学会出版，书名译为《泰西民法志》。该书出版后，由于发行数量有限，传播范围也极其狭隘。
    1912年柯卡普去世后，英国人皮司对《社会主义史》作了增删重订，新增了1892至1913年间世界各国社会主义运动的历史。皮司的增订本由李季翻译。1920年由上海新青年社出版，蔡元培先生为之作序。
    关于马克思学说，该书详尽地介绍和评述了马克思的辩证唯物主义、历史唯物主义、政治经济学和科学社会主义，对马克思的两大发现以及资本主义必将被社会主义所取代的理论，也作了较为客观的介绍。
  （1989年第4期《历史知识》）
