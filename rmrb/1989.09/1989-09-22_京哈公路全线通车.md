Title: The Beijing-Harbin Expressway Opens to Traffic
Author: Lǐ Āndá
Date: 1989-09-22
Edition: 2

The Beijing-Harbin Expressway, a crucial transportation artery in Northeast China, has fully opened to traffic. On September 20, a grand opening ceremony was held in Changchun City. This marks the complete connection of the expressway from Harbin to Beijing.

The Changchun to Harbin section of the Beijing-Harbin Expressway is a key project during China's Seventh Five-Year Plan. The entire section spans 234 kilometers, with 160 kilometers in Jilin Province and 74.4 kilometers in Heilongjiang Province. Construction began in 1987 in both provinces. Over the course of two years, 94.2 kilometers of the original third-level gravel road were reconstructed, and 138.8 kilometers of new road were built.

### 京哈公路全线通车
李安达
1989-09-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    京哈公路全线通车
    本报长春9月20日电　记者李安达报道：在国庆40周年即将到来之际，我国东北地区重要交通干线——京哈公路长春至哈尔滨段全面竣工，9月20日在长春市举行通车仪式。至此，哈尔滨至北京公路交通大动脉全线贯通。
    京哈公路长春至哈尔滨段，是国家“七五”期间的一项重点工程。全段共234公里，其中在吉林省境内160公里，黑龙江省境内74.4公里。这一工程从1987年起分别在两省动工。在两年多时间内，改建原三级渣油路94.2公里，新修138.8公里。
