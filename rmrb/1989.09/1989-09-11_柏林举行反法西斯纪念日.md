Title: Berlin Holds Anti-Fascist Memorial Day
Author:
Date: 1989-09-11
Edition: 3

East Berlin held a rally on the 10th to commemorate the day of the victims of fascism and the anti-fascist struggle. Approximately 200,000 people, along with Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the GDR, Stoph, and Chairman of the People's Chamber, Sindermann, attended the rally.

### 柏林举行反法西斯纪念日

1989-09-11
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　柏林举行反法西斯纪念日
    民主德国首都柏林十日举行集会，纪念法西斯受害者和反法西斯斗争日。约二十万群众和民德部长会议主席斯多夫、人民议院主席辛德曼等领导人出席了集会。
