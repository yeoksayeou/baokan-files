Title: IOC Agrees with FIFA Decision: Next Olympic Football Tournament Athletes Limited to 23 Years Old
Author:
Date: 1989-09-01
Edition: 3

The International Olympic Committee (IOC) agreed on August 31 to FIFA's decision to limit the age of athletes participating in the Olympic football tournament. FIFA announced on August 29 that athletes participating in the 1992 Barcelona Olympic football tournament must be under 23 years old.

The IOC and FIFA have had differing opinions on the issue of limiting the age of football athletes for over a year. The IOC believes that all football athletes, including professional stars like Maradona, should be allowed to participate in the Olympics. However, FIFA believes that without age restrictions on Olympic athletes, the influence of the World Cup football tournament would be diminished.

### 国际奥委会同意国际足联决定  下届奥运会足球赛选手年龄限制在23岁以内

1989-09-01
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    国际奥委会同意国际足联决定
    下届奥运会足球赛选手年龄限制在23岁以内
    新华社华盛顿8月31日电　国际奥委会31日表示同意国际足联关于限制参加奥运会足球赛运动员年龄的决定。国际足联8月29日宣布，参加1992年西班牙巴塞罗那奥运会足球赛的运动员年龄必须得在23岁以下。
    国际奥委会与国际足联在是否限制足球运动员年龄一事上一年多来一直存在分歧。奥委会认为应该让所有足球运动员包括像马拉多纳那样的职业球星都参加奥运会比赛。而国际足联则认为，如果不对奥运会选手的年龄加以限制，则会削弱世界杯足球赛的影响。
