Title: Japanese Prime Minister Visits Mexico
Author: Guo Weicheng
Date: 1989-09-05
Page: 3

People's Daily, Mexico City, September 4th

Japanese Prime Minister Kaifu Toshiki arrived in Mexico City this afternoon for a three-day official visit. This is the first visit by a Japanese prime minister to Mexico in the past eight years.

Kaifu said upon his arrival in Mexico that this visit provides an excellent opportunity for the leaders of Japan and Mexico to exchange views on the current international situation. Tonight, at the state banquet hosted by President Salinas in his honor, Kaifu expressed Japan's willingness to expand cooperation with Mexico in the fields of science and technology. President Salinas thanked Japan for its support in helping Mexico resolve its debt issues. It is understood that Kaifu will discuss bilateral relations with President Salinas, focusing on financial and economic cooperation between the two countries. Kaifu recently stated in the United States that Japan will provide loans to Mexico in advance to help resolve its debt issues. On international issues, in addition to peace in Central America and international peace and security, the main topic of discussion will be strengthening multilateral cooperation; Mexico will request Japan's assistance in joining the Pacific Rim cooperation.

The visit to Mexico is part of Kaifu's North American tour.

### 日本首相访问墨西哥  表示将提前向墨提供解决债务贷款
郭伟成
1989-09-05
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    日本首相访问墨西哥
    表示将提前向墨提供解决债务贷款
    本报墨西哥城9月4日电　记者郭伟成报道：日本首相海部俊树今天下午到达墨西哥城进行为期3天的正式访问。这是最近8年以来，日本首相对墨西哥的首次访问。
    海部首相在抵达墨西哥时向新闻界说，他这次访问为日墨两国领导人就当前国际局势交换意见提供了极好的机会。今天晚上，海部首相在萨利纳斯总统为他举行的国宴上表示，日本愿意扩大与墨西哥在科学、技术方面的合作。萨利纳斯总统对日本在墨西哥解决债务方面给予的支持表示感谢。据悉，海部首相将同萨利纳斯总统讨论日墨双边关系，主要是两国在财政金融方面的合作。海部首相日前在美国已经表示，日本将提前向墨西哥提供用于解决债务的贷款。在国际问题方面，除了中美洲和平及国际和平与安全外，将主要讨论加强多边合作；墨西哥将要求日本帮助下加入环太平洋合作。
    对墨西哥的访问是海部首相北美之行的一部分。
