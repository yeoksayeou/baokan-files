Title: Stop the Theft of Maritime Cargo
Author: Gong Weixin
Date: 1989-09-12
Edition: 6

During the first two months of this year, more than 10 cargo ships from Lianjiang, Fuan, Jinjiang, and Nan'an counties in Fujian province stole 210 tons of white sugar en route to Shanghai and Zhejiang, causing losses of 700,000 yuan for the relevant units. From January 2 to February 22, eight ships carrying white sugar from Xiamen and Putian in Fujian to Zhejiang and Shanghai stole over 150 tons of sugar, averaging more than 18 tons per ship. At the end of January, the Huangqi Border Police Station in Lianjiang County caught the Lianjiang Huanghai 119 vessel stealing over 5 tons of white sugar; the Ankai Border Police Station also caught the Lianmen 1 vessel stealing over 5 tons of white sugar.

We appeal to the relevant departments to take effective measures to promptly stop the theft of maritime cargo.

	Shanghai City, Huangpu Road 106, Red Building, Room 201, Gong Weixin

### 制止偷盗海运物资
宫维新
1989-09-12
第6版(读者来信)
专栏：呼吁

　　制止偷盗海运物资
    今年第一季度的两个多月里，在我国东海海运线上，福建的连江、福安、晋江、南安4县的10多条运输船，自盗承运到上海、浙江等地的白砂糖210吨，致使有关单位损失70万元。1月2日至2月22日，由福建厦门和莆田两地发往浙江、上海等地的8船白糖，承运人在途中盗窃白糖150多吨，平均每条船自盗18吨多。1月底，连江县黄岐边防派出所抓获连江黄海119号轮偷盗白砂糖5吨多；安凯边防派出所也抓获连门1号轮偷盗白砂糖5吨多。
    呼吁有关部门采取有效措施，及时制止偷盗海运物资。
                  上海市黄浦路106号红楼201室　宫维新
