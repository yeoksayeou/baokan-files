Title: Qingdao Wushu Sanshou Tournament Concludes
Author: Huang Lidian
Date: 1989-09-19
Edition: 3

The three-day national sanshou tournament concluded on September 17 in Qingdao. The host team, Shandong, demonstrated strong prowess, securing 5 gold medals out of the 8 weight categories for both men and women. Wuhan Institute of Physical Education team won 2 gold medals, and Liaoning team won 1 gold medal. Athletes from Wuhan Institute of Physical Education, Shanghai Institute of Physical Education, Liaoning, Hubei, Shanghai, and Shandong (Qingdao Refrigerator Factory) participated in the competition, totaling over 50 male and female athletes.

### 青岛武术散打赛结束
黄丽东
1989-09-19
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　青岛武术散打赛结束
    本报讯　历时3天的全国散打擂台赛9月17日在青岛结束。东道主山东队实力雄厚，摘取了男女8个级别的5枚金牌，武汉体院队获得2枚金牌，辽宁队获得1枚金牌。来自武汉体院、上海体院、辽宁、湖北、上海、山东（青岛冰柜厂）等6支队伍50多名男女选手参加了角逐。
        （黄丽东）
