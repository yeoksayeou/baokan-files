Title: Family Planning Brings Tremendous Benefits
Author: Yǔ Chánghóng
Date: 1989-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 13th. (Reporter Yu Changhong) Since the 1970s, China has fully implemented family planning, effectively controlling the rapid growth of the population and producing tremendous economic benefits. According to calculations based on the birth rate in 1970, by the end of 1988, the country had reduced the number of births by more than 200 million, saving society 300 billion yuan in child-rearing costs alone.

According to statistics, based on the 1986 consumption level, raising a newborn to the age of 16 costs 18,000 yuan in urban areas and 7,000 yuan in rural areas. Demographers point out that reducing the number of births, to some extent, means increasing social income. Family planning is far from being a "money-losing" investment; it is a venture with relatively low investment and high returns.

Economists vividly describe the "output" benefits of family planning: saving 300 billion yuan is equivalent to 2.15 times the national GDP in 1988; it is equivalent to 460 times the current annual national investment in health care; with this amount of money, more than 100 new steel plants like Baogang could be built. If invested in education, it could ensure the normal operation of 1,000 universities for 50 years. Therefore, all sectors of society should recognize the significant role of family planning and work together to promote this work.

### 计划生育带来巨大效益　十八年少生两亿多人节省抚养费三万亿
于长洪
1989-09-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    计划生育带来巨大效益
　　十八年少生两亿多人节省抚养费三万亿
    据新华社北京9月13日电　（记者于长洪）我国自70年代全面实行计划生育以来，不仅有效地控制住了人口过快增长的势头，而且产生了巨大的经济效益。按1970年生育水平推算，到1988年底，全国少出生了2亿多人，仅抚养费一项就为社会节约3万亿元。
    据统计，按1986年消费水平计算，将一个新生儿抚养到16岁，在城市需要花费18000元，在农村需要花费7000元。人口学家据此指出，减少人口出生，在某种意义上来说，就等于增加社会收入，计划生育远非只投入不产出的“赔钱”买卖，而是一项投入较少、收益很大的事业。
    经济界人士对计划生育“产出”效益的描述是生动的：节约3万亿元，这相当于1988年我国国民生产总值的2.15倍；相当于现在国家每年投入卫生事业费的460倍；用这笔钱可以新建100多个宝山钢铁总厂；如投入教育事业，可保证1000所高校正常开支50年。因此，社会各方面应当看到计划生育工作的巨大作用，要齐心协力抓好这项工作。
