Title: Rafsanjani Resigns from Military Post
Author:
Date: 1989-09-03
Edition: 3

Iranian President Rafsanjani resigned from his position as acting commander-in-chief of the armed forces on September 2.

According to Tehran Radio, Rafsanjani stated in a letter to Iran's Supreme Leader Khamenei that he wished to concentrate on handling national affairs. Khamenei accepted Rafsanjani's resignation and will assume the role of commander-in-chief of the armed forces in accordance with the provisions of the Iranian Constitution.

### 拉夫桑贾尼辞去军职

1989-09-03
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    拉夫桑贾尼辞去军职
    伊朗总统拉夫桑贾尼九月二日辞去了武装部队代总司令的职务。
    据德黑兰电台报道，拉夫桑贾尼在给伊朗最高领袖哈梅内伊的一封信中表示，希望集中精力处理国家事务。哈梅内伊接受了拉夫桑贾尼的辞呈，并且将根据伊朗宪法的规定担任武装部队总司令。
