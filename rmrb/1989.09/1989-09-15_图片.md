Title: Bangkok's "Holiday Market"
Author:
Date: 1989-09-15
Edition: 7

Bangkok's "Holiday Market." This market operates on Saturdays and Sundays. Vendors from various regions sell a wide array of goods including clothing, food, handicrafts, and other items. The market is bustling with activity.
