Title: The National Chess Championship Ranks in Beijing
Author: Miú Lǔ
Date: 1989-09-19
Edition: 3

The national chess championship ranks were determined in Beijing on September 15. Reporter Miú Lǔ reports: The 1989 national chess championship, which lasted 15 days, saw Shanxi player Yè Jiāngchuān "walking on a tightrope" to reach the top of the men's A group podium, thus winning the championship for the fifth time.

Beijing's Xiè Jūn and Méng Yuè won the women's A and B group championships, respectively. The men's B group champion is Wú Xībīn from Heilongjiang. During the award ceremony after the competition, Lǐ Zhōngjiàn, Yáng Xiàn, Yè Róngguāng, Lín Tǎ, Zhāng Wěidá, and the 10-year-old Zhū Xiàn were officially awarded the title of International Chess Federation Master.

### 全国国际象棋个人赛在京排定名次
缪鲁
1989-09-19
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　全国国际象棋个人赛在京排定名次
    本报讯　记者缪鲁报道：9月15日，1989年全国国际象棋个人赛在北京排定名次。山西棋手叶江川在为期15天的弈战中，“踏着钢丝绳”走上男子甲组领奖台的最高层，从而第五次摘取这一比赛的桂冠。
    北京姑娘谢军和孟悦分别获得个人赛女子甲、乙组冠军。男子乙组冠军是来自黑龙江的吴玺斌。在赛后的颁奖仪式上，李中健、杨献、叶荣光、林塔、张伟达以及年仅10岁的诸宸等人，被正式授予国际棋联大师称号。
