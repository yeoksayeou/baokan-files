Title: Khmer People's National Liberation Armed Forces Capture Vietnamese Military Outpost
Author:
Date: 1989-09-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 16th.

Khmer People's National Liberation Armed Forces Capture Vietnamese Military Outpost

According to a broadcast by the Democratic Kampuchea Radio today, the Khmer People's National Liberation Armed Forces captured a Vietnamese military camp-level outpost on September 3, killing and wounding 16 Vietnamese soldiers.

The broadcast said that the National Liberation Armed Forces launched a surprise attack on the Vietnamese camp-level outpost in Anbi Village, Tembor District, Battambang Province, killing 5 Vietnamese soldiers on the spot and wounding 11 others. They seized 33 various types of guns and 4 B-40 rocket launchers, and destroyed tons of ammunition.

According to another report, the Khmer People's National Liberation Armed Forces captured two Vietnamese outposts in the northwest of Phnom Penh on September 7, in the line of defense of the Vietnamese in Tevong District, Pursat Province, killing 13 Vietnamese soldiers and wounding 18, including one battalion and one company-level officer each. The National Liberation Armed Forces destroyed a weapons depot in the battle and seized a batch of weapons and ammunition. At the same time, 11 villages near the Vietnamese outpost were temporarily liberated by the National Liberation Armed Forces.

### 民柬国民军攻克越军据点

1989-09-17
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　民柬国民军攻克越军据点
    新华社北京9月16日电　据民主柬埔寨电台今天广播，民柬国民军9月3日又拔掉越军1个营级据点，打死打伤越军16人。
    广播说，国民军9月3日突袭了马德望省特莫博县安比村的越军营级据点，当场打死越军5人，打伤11人，缴获各式枪支33支和4支B—40火箭筒，并销毁成吨弹药。
    另据报道，民柬国民军9月7日在金边西北战区攻克越军在磅士卑省特崩县防线的两个据点，打死越军13人，打伤18人，其中包括打死营、连级军官各1名。国民军在战斗中击毁武器仓库1座，缴获一批武器弹药。同时，越军据点附近的11个村庄一度被国民军解放。
