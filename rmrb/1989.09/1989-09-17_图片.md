Title: At the Post House, Dismount First
Author: Fang Cheng
Date: 1989-09-17
Page: 8

First, dismount at the post house,
Follow the walls and pillars to find your poetry.
—Poem by Bai Juyi

(June 17th, Zhuhai Special Zone Daily, Fang Cheng's drawing)
