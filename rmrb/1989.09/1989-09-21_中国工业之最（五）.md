Date: 1989-09-21
Edition: 5

    China's Industrial Leaders (Five)
        Transportation

    Longest Electrified Railway: The Lanzhou-Baoji section of the Longhai Line. Completed and operational in May 1984. The total length is 511 kilometers. The Zhengzhou-Baoji section is under construction, with a total length of 684 kilometers.

    Longest Newly Built Railway Bridge: The Huanghe Bridge on the Xinhe Line between Changyuan and Dongming. Completed in 1985. The total length is 10,282 meters, making it also the longest railway bridge in Asia.

    Longest Highway: The Qinghai-Tibet Highway. Completed and operational in 1985. The total length from Xining to Lhasa is 1,937 kilometers. This is the largest and longest second-class highway built on the "Roof of the World" at the highest altitude.

    Longest Combined Railway and Highway Bridge: The Nanjing Yangtze River Bridge. Completed and operational in 1968. The total length is 6,772 meters (4,588 meters for the highway bridge), with the main bridge being a double-track railway bridge.

    Port with the Largest Cargo Throughput: Shanghai Port. Began expansion in 1957. After more than 30 years of reconstruction and expansion, the cargo throughput has reached 113 million tons. Since 1984, it has entered the ranks of the world's top 10 ports with over 100 million tons of cargo throughput.

    Largest Container Terminal: Tianjin Port Container Terminal. Completed in 1985. With a throughput capacity of 30 million tons per year.

    Largest and Newest 100,000-ton Berth: The ore terminal at Beilun Port in Zhejiang. Completed in 1982. It has three 100,000-ton berths. The coal terminal at Shitou Port in Shandong and the oil terminal at Lianfengwan in Dalian Port were completed in December 1985 and 1986, respectively, each with one 100,000-ton berth.

### 中国工业之最（五）

1989-09-21
第5版(国内专页（经济）)
专栏：

    中国工业之最（五）
        交通运输
    最长的电气化铁路：陇海线宝鸡兰州段。1984年5月建成营运。全长511公里。郑州宝鸡段在建设中，全长684公里。
    最长的新建铁路大桥：新荷线长垣东明黄河大桥。1985年铺通。全长10282米，也是亚洲最长的铁道桥。
    最长的公路：青藏公路。1985年建成营运。西宁至拉萨全长1937公里。这是在海拔最高的“世界屋脊”上建成的我国工程规模最大、里程最长的二级公路。
    最长的铁路公路两用大桥：南京长江大桥。1968年建成营运。全长6772米（公路桥4588米），主桥为双线铁路桥。
    吞吐量最大的港口：上海港。1957年开始扩建。经30多年改建、扩建，货物吞吐量已达1.13亿吨。自1984年起跨入世界10个超亿吨港口行列。
    最大的集装箱码头：天津港集装箱码头。1985年建成。吞吐能力300万吨／年。
    最新最大的10万吨级码头泊位：浙江北仑港矿石码头。1982年建成。有3个10万吨级泊位。山东石臼港煤码头、大连港鲇鱼湾油码头分别于1985年12月和1986年建成，各有一个10万吨级泊位。
      （摘自《奋进的四十年》）
