Title: A Natural Gas Well is Drilled in the Southern Suburbs of Urumqi
Author: Ni Weigong, Wu Jiezhi
Date: 1989-09-25
Edition: 2

On September 18, the 6008 Drilling Team of the Northwest Petroleum Geological Bureau under the Ministry of Geology and Mineral Resources drilled a natural gas well with a daily output of 13,000 cubic meters in a location about 50 kilometers south of Urumqi City. This achievement verifies that this region has geological conditions suitable for the generation and accumulation of oil and gas.

### 乌市南郊打出天然气井
钮惟恭  吴杰志
1989-09-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    乌市南郊打出天然气井
    新华社乌鲁木齐9月24日电　（通讯员钮惟恭、吴杰志）9月18日，地矿部西北石油地质局6008钻井队在乌鲁木齐市南50多公里处打出一口日产13000立方米的天然气井。这项成果，验证了这个地区具有油气生成和聚集的地质条件。
