Title: New York Stock Market Sets New Record
Author:
Date: 1989-09-02
Edition: 3

New York Stock Market Sets New Record
Dow Jones Industrial Average Reaches 2752.09 Points

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 1 - The New York stock market closed at a new high on September 1, with the Dow Jones Industrial Average reaching 2752.09 points.

This is the second time this week that the index has set a new historical high. On August 28, the index had risen to 2743.36 points, its previous highest record. This week, the index has increased by 19.73 points.

The New York stock market remains strong due to the belief that the U.S. economy will not experience a recession this year, inflationary pressures are easing, and interest rates are relatively low, which has increased investors' interest in purchasing stocks.

### 纽约股市又创最高纪录  道·琼斯平均指数达二千七百五十二点零九点

1989-09-02
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    纽约股市又创最高纪录
    道·琼斯平均指数达二千七百五十二点零九点
    新华社纽约9月1日电　纽约股票市场1日收盘时，道·琼斯30种工业股票平均指数又创新的最高水平，达到2752.09点。
    这是这项指数在本周内第二次创其历史最高纪录。8月28日，这项指数曾升到2743.36点的历史最高纪录。本周这项指数计上升19.73点。
    纽约股市坚挺，其因是美国经济今年不可能出现衰退，通货膨胀压力趋缓。利率偏低，促使投资者购买股票的兴趣增加。
