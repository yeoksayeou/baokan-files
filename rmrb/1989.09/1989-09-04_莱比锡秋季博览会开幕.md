Title: The Leipzig Autumn Fair Opens
Author: Xǔ Hóngzhì
Date: 1989-09-04
Edition: 3

The 1989 Leipzig Autumn Fair opened on September 3rd. Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic, Stoph, and Chairman of the People's Chamber, Sindermann, attended the opening ceremony.

The General Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Chairman of the Council of State, Honecker, did not attend the opening ceremony due to illness.

Stoph and Sindermann, along with other leaders, visited the Chinese pavilion. The People's Republic of China pavilion at this year's autumn fair is composed of 21 industrial and foreign trade enterprises from Shanghai, showcasing high-quality household appliances, watches, bicycles, and textiles produced in Shanghai. Stoph wrote a message in the guest book.

### 莱比锡秋季博览会开幕
许宏治
1989-09-04
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    莱比锡秋季博览会开幕
    本报柏林9月4日电　记者许宏治报道：1989年莱比锡秋季博览会9月3日隆重开幕。民主德国部长会议主席斯多夫、人民议院主席辛德曼等出席了开幕式。
    德国统一社会党中央总书记、国务委员会主席昂纳克因病没有出席开幕式。
    斯多夫、辛德曼等领导人参观了中国馆。参加本届秋季博览会的中华人民共和国馆由上海市21家工业和外贸企业组成，展出上海市生产的优质家用电器、钟表、自行车等轻工产品和纺织品等。斯多夫在贵宾留言簿上题了词。
