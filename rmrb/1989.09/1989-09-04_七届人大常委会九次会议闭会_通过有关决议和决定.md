Title: Seventh NPC Standing Committee Concludes Ninth Session
Author:
Date: 1989-09-04
Edition: 1

The ninth session of the Seventh National People's Congress Standing Committee concluded this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People, where votes were cast on the agenda items discussed during the session.

Wan Li, the Chairman, presided over the meeting.

The meeting adopted the following decisions and resolutions:

1. A resolution by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress to extend the period for soliciting opinions on the draft Basic Law of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region until the end of October.
2. A decision on the timing of the elections for representatives to the people's congresses at the county and township levels.
3. A decision to approve the treaty between the governments of China and Mongolia on the regime of the China-Mongolia border and the handling of border issues.
4. A decision to approve the United Nations Convention Against Illicit Traffic in Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances.

Upon the request of Premier Li Peng, the meeting decided to relieve Wang Meng of his post as Minister of Culture.

Li Peng stated in the proposal that Wang Meng is a renowned writer in China who has repeatedly expressed his desire to step down from his leadership position to focus on literary creation and art criticism. After careful consideration, it is proposed to respect Wang Meng's request and approve his resignation from the position of Minister of Culture.

The meeting, in accordance with the relevant provisions of the Organic Law of the National People's Congress and based on the situation of He Jingzhi taking up his post at the Ministry of Culture and his personal request, decided to accept his resignation from the position of member of the Seventh National People's Congress Standing Committee, to be confirmed by the next session of the National People's Congress; and to accept his resignation from the position of member of the Seventh National People's Congress Committee on Education, Science, Culture, and Health.

Vice Chairpersons Hab Zongxun, Peng Chong, Zhu Lifan, Yan Jizi, Rong Yiren, Ye Fei, Liao Hansheng, Ni Zhifu, Chen Mu Hua, Fei Xiaotong, Sun Qimeng, Lei Jieqiong, and Wang Hanbin attended the meeting.

State Councilor Wang Fang and President of the Supreme People's Court Ren Jianxin attended the meeting as non-voting participants.

### 七届人大常委会九次会议闭会  通过有关决议和决定

1989-09-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    七届人大常委会九次会议闭会
    通过有关决议和决定
    新华社北京9月4日电  七届全国人大常委会第九次会议今天下午在人民大会堂举行闭幕会，就这次会议审议的有关议案进行表决。
    万里委员长主持会议。
    会议通过了全国人大常委会关于将香港特别行政区基本法草案的征求意见时间延长至10月底止的决议，关于县、乡两级人民代表大会代表选举时间的决定，关于批准中蒙两国政府关于中蒙边界制度和处理边境问题的条约的决定，关于批准联合国禁止非法贩运麻醉药品和精神药物公约的决定。
    会议根据国务院总理李鹏的提请，决定免去王蒙文化部部长的职务。
    李鹏在议案中说，王蒙同志是我国著名作家，他曾多次提出，希望能尽快从领导岗位上退下来，专心从事文学创作和文艺评论工作。经研究，拟尊重王蒙同志的请求，同意他辞去文化部部长职务。
    会议根据贺敬之到文化部任职的情况和他本人的请求，根据全国人大组织法的有关规定，决定接受他辞去七届全国人大常委会委员职务的请求，报请全国人民代表大会下次会议确认；并接受他辞去七届全国人大教科文卫委员会委员职务的请求。
    副委员长习仲勋、彭冲、朱学范、严济慈、荣毅仁、叶飞、廖汉生、倪志福、陈慕华、费孝通、孙起孟、雷洁琼、王汉斌，出席会议。
    国务委员王芳、最高人民法院院长任建新列席了会议。
