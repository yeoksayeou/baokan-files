Title: U.S. Crime Rate Soars
Author:
Date: 1989-09-12
Page: 3

The number of inmates in U.S. prisons surged by 46,004 in the first half of 1989, the highest increase in 64 years, with many of the new inmates involved in drug-related cases. According to a report released by the U.S. Department of Justice on September 10, the current prison population stands at 673,565, double the number in 1980. Prisons are overcrowded, and the government has requested $1.2 billion from Congress for the 1990 fiscal year to expand federal prisons.

### 美国犯罪人数剧增

1989-09-12
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　美国犯罪人数剧增
    1989年上半年美国监狱关押的犯人人数急剧增加46004人，半年内所增人数比往年一年还多，是64年来最高纪录，其中许多人与毒品案件有关。据9月10日美国司法部门发表的报告，目前美国监狱关押犯人673565人，为1980年的两倍。监狱人满为患，政府已要求国会1990年度拨款12亿美元，扩建联邦监狱。
            （本报专电）
