Publishing News

The book "Postwar American History: 1945–1986," edited by Liu Xuyi and Yang Shengmao, has been published by the People's Publishing House.

The book "Lancang River – Little Sun," edited by Tian Fang, Lin Fatang, and Bi Daolin, has been published by Yunnan People's Publishing House.

The book "Land and Peasants – The Law and Politics of China's Land Revolution," authored by Chen Hefu, has recently been published by Liaoning People's Publishing House.
