Title: Ben Johnson's 100m World Record Cancelled
Author:
Date: 1989-09-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency reported: The deputy chairman of the International Amateur Athletics Federation, Arne Ljungqvist, announced on September 4 in Barcelona, Spain, that the world record of 9.83 seconds set by Canadian athlete Ben Johnson in the men's 100 meters at the 1987 World Athletics Championships in Rome would be annulled. Ljungqvist made this statement after a meeting of the International Amateur Athletics Federation's council. However, the council did not discuss whether to revoke Johnson's gold medal from that competition.

The International Amateur Athletics Federation council has provided a new list of world records, which will be discussed at the federation's general assembly on September 5 and will be published soon.

Currently, the best recorded time for the men's 100 meters, excluding Johnson's 9.83 seconds, is 9.92 seconds, achieved by American athlete Carl Lewis at the 1988 Seoul Olympics.

Ljungqvist stated that the International Amateur Athletics Federation is determined to severely punish athletes who use banned substances. Johnson is just the beginning; any world record holder found to have used banned substances in the past six years will have their record annulled.

### 约翰逊的百米世界纪录被取消

1989-09-05
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    约翰逊的百米世界纪录被取消
    本报讯　据新华社报道：国际业余田联副主席阿尔内·荣德奎斯特九月四日在西班牙的巴塞罗那宣布，加拿大运动员本·约翰逊一九八七年九月在罗马世界田径锦标赛上创造的九秒八三的男子一百米跑的世界纪录将被取消。荣德奎斯特是在国际业余田联召开的理事会之后说这番话的。但这次理事会没有讨论是否要收回约翰逊在那次比赛上所获得的金牌。
    国际业余田联理事会已提供了一份新的世界纪录名单，国际业余田联全体大会将于五日讨论这份名单，并于近日公布。
    目前，男子一百米跑除约翰逊九秒八三外的世界最好成绩是，美国选手刘易斯去年在汉城奥运会上跑出的九秒九二。
    荣德奎斯特说，国际业余田联决心从重处罚服用违禁药物的田径运动员，约翰逊只是个开始，凡在过去六年内用过违禁药物的世界纪录保持者，一经查出，便取消其世界纪录。
