Title: Farewell Ceremony for Poet Li Xue'ao Held
Author:
Date: 1989-09-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 16th.

The farewell ceremony for the renowned proletarian literary warrior, Communist Party of China member, and famous poet Li Xue'ao was held this afternoon at the Baobao Mountain Revolutionary Cemetery Hall.

Li Xue'ao was a well-known worker poet in China. He published fifteen poetry collections, including "The Song of the Printers," "Spring in Beijing," and "Ode to Heroes," as well as numerous other poems. His poetry is known for its pure and genuine emotions, rich life experiences, and a distinct sense of the times and unique artistic style. As a result, he has won the love of many readers. Li Xue'ao was one of the first generation of successful poets nurtured by the Party after the founding of New China. He consistently adhered to the Party's literary policies and guidelines, advocating for literature to serve the people and socialism. Throughout his life, he worked diligently and made unremitting contributions. On September 6, 1989, illness took his life at the age of 56.

Members of the Political Bureau Standing Committee of the CPC Central Committee, Li Ruihuan, attended the farewell ceremony today. Xu Weicheng, He Jingzhi, Gao Zhanxiang, and other relevant department heads, as well as Wei Wei, Wang Meng, Haoran, and more than 200 others, attended the ceremony. Li Ximing, Chen Xitong, and other Beijing municipal leaders, along with prominent figures in the literary world, sent wreaths.

### 诗人李学鳌遗体告别仪式举行

1989-09-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    诗人李学鳌遗体告别仪式举行
    新华社北京9月16日电  优秀的无产阶级文艺战士、中国共产党党员、著名诗人李学鳌同志遗体告别仪式，今天下午在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。
    李学鳌是我国著名工人诗人。出版过《印刷工人之歌》、《北京的春天》、《英雄颂》等十五部诗集和大批诗作。他的诗感情纯真朴实，生活气息浓郁，有鲜明的时代感和独特的艺术风格。因而，赢得了广大读者的喜爱。李学鳌是新中国成立后，党培养的第一代颇有成就的诗人。他一贯坚持党的文艺方针和政策；坚持文艺为人民服务、为社会主义服务的方向。一生勤勤恳恳、不息奉献。1989年9月6日，病魔夺去了他的生命，终年56岁。
    中共中央政治局常委李瑞环参加了今天的遗体告别仪式。徐惟诚、贺敬之、高占祥等有关部门负责人，以及魏巍、王蒙、浩然等200多人参加了告别仪式。李锡铭、陈希同等北京市负责人和文艺界知名人士送了花圈。
