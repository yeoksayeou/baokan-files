Title: Belgium Qualifies for the World Cup
Author: Wang Shujuan
Date: 1989-09-07
Edition: 3

Belgium Qualifies for the World Cup

BEIJING, Sept. 7 (Xinhua) — Last night, the Belgian team defeated the Portuguese football team with a score of 3-0, effectively securing their spot in next year's World Cup. In the match held at the Heysel Stadium in Brussels, the Belgian team displayed agile attacking and solid defending. In the first half, Ceulemans scored the first goal. Shortly after the start of the second half, Vandereycken scored two goals within ten minutes.

### 世界杯外围赛  比利时队出线
王淑娟
1989-09-07
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    世界杯外围赛
    比利时队出线
    本报布鲁塞尔九月七日电　昨晚比利时队以三比○的成绩轻取葡萄牙足球队，实际获得参加明年世界杯足球赛的资格。在布鲁塞尔海塞尔体育场举行的这场比赛中，比利时队进攻灵活，防守坚固。上半场瑟勒芒斯首开纪录。下半场开始后不久，旺代伦登在十分钟内先后踢进两个球。　（王淑娟）
