Title: Peng Chong Meets with North Korean Guests
Author: Wēn Xiàn
Date: 1989-09-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 23rd.

Peng Chong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met today in the morning with a delegation from the Central Prosecutorate of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Deputy Director Kim Chang-Uk, at the Great Hall of the People. Peng Chong briefed the North Korean guests on China's legal system construction.

At noon, Liu Fuzhi, Prosecutor-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, met and hosted a banquet for Kim Chang-Uk and his delegation at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

The purpose of the North Korean guests' visit to China this time is to further exchange information and experiences on the procuratorial work of the two countries.

### 彭冲会见朝鲜客人
温宪
1989-09-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    彭冲会见朝鲜客人
    本报北京9月23日讯　记者温宪报道：全国人大常委会副委员长彭冲今天上午在人民大会堂会见了由副所长金昌旭率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国中央检察所代表团。彭冲向朝鲜客人介绍了中国法制建设情况。
    中午，最高人民检察院检察长刘复之在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请了金昌旭一行。
    朝鲜客人此次访华旨在就两国检察工作进一步交流情况和经验。
