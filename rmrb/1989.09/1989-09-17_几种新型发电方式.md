Title: Several New Types of Power Generation
Author: Wen Yi
Date: 1989-09-17
Page: 8

Researchers in Leningrad, Soviet Union, have begun developing an "underground compressed air power station." The principle behind this is that when user electricity demand decreases, excess electricity is used to compress air, which is then stored underground. During peak electricity demand, the stored air is pressurized and sent to a gas turbine to generate electricity.

A power plant utilizing the deep cooling properties of liquefied natural gas has been constructed by Tokyo Electric Power Company on the eastern side of Tokyo. Unlike thermal power generation, which converts heat energy from burning liquefied gas into electrical energy, "cold-heat power generation" utilizes the expansion force generated when liquefied natural gas at -162°C vaporizes and increases in volume by 600 times. This force drives a turbine to generate electricity. Since the natural gas is not burned, this is an energy-saving method of power generation.

A 580-megawatt high-efficiency magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) power generation system is under construction at the Ryazan Natural Gas Thermal Power Station in the Soviet Union. This system consists of a 270-megawatt MHD generator and a 310-megawatt steam turbine generator. It first uses natural gas to burn air to 1700°C. Then, potassium carbonate is added to this high-temperature gas to create plasma, which is sent into a magnetic field to convert thermal energy into electrical energy. The resulting direct current is then converted into alternating current and sent to a 500-kilovolt transmission network. The thermal efficiency of this power generation system is approximately 50% higher than that of conventional thermal power generators and can operate continuously for 10,000 hours.

### 几种新型发电方式
文异
1989-09-17
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

　　几种新型发电方式
    贮藏空气发电　苏联列宁格勒的科研人员开始研制“地下贮藏空气发电站”，其原理是，当用户的用电量减少时，利用多余的电力来压缩空气，并将其贮藏于地下，而用电高峰到来时再将贮藏的空气加高压，送至气体透平机进行发电。
    冷热发电　一种利用液化天然气深冷性质发电的电站，已由日本东京电力公司在东扇岛建成。与燃烧液化气的火力发电将热能转换成电能不同，“冷热发电”是当—162℃的液化天然气气化时，其体积将膨胀增大600倍，利用这一膨胀产生的力来驱动透平从而达到发电的目的。由于天然气未经燃烧，因此是一种省能源的发电方式。
    磁流体发电　苏联梁赞天然气火力发电站正在建设580兆瓦的高效磁流体发电系统，这一系统由270兆瓦的磁流体发电机和310兆瓦的蒸汽透平发电机组成。它首先利用天然气把空气燃烧到1700℃。然后在这种高温气体中加入碳酸钾制造等离子送入磁场，将热能转换成电能，再将发生的直流电变成交流电送至500千伏输电网。这种发电系统的热效率约超过一般火力发电机组热效率的50％，可连续运转1万小时。
  （9月5日《科技日报》广异文）
