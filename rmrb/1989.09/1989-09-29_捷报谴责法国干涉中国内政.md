Title: Czech Newspaper Condemns French Interference in China's Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1989-09-29
Edition: 4

The Czech newspaper *Rudé právo* today published a commentary condemning the French government's interference in China's internal affairs.

The commentary stated that the French government's support for fugitives determined to overthrow the current Chinese government and its permission for the so-called "Chinese Democracy Front" to hold a founding conference in Paris is "interference in the internal affairs of a sovereign state" and "a serious violation of the spirit of the United Nations Charter."

The commentary also pointed out that recent incidents in Paris, including attacks on Chinese trade mission vehicles, harassment of Chinese passersby, and other anti-China provocations, indicate that the French government is determined to sabotage all positive aspects of relations with the People's Republic of China. China has the right to condemn Paris' actions.

### 捷报谴责法国干涉中国内政

1989-09-29
第4版(国际新闻·国内新闻)
专栏：

    捷报谴责法国干涉中国内政
    新华社布拉格9月28日电　捷克斯洛伐克《红色权利报》今天发表短评，谴责法国政府干涉中国内政。
    评论指出，法国政府支持决心推翻中国现政府的逃亡分子和允许所谓的民主中国阵线在巴黎举行成立大会，是“对一个主权国家内政的干涉”，“严重违背了联合国宪章的精神”。
    评论还指出，近日在巴黎发生的对中国商务代表处车辆的袭击，对中国过往人员的滋扰和其他反华挑衅行动表明，法国政府决心破坏同人民中国关系中的一切积极的东西，中国理当谴责巴黎的做法。
