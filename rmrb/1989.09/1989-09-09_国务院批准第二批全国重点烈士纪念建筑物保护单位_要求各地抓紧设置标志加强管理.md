Title: State Council Approves Second Batch of National Key Martyrs' Memorial Buildings for Protection
Author:
Date: 1989-09-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 9th

The Ministry of Civil Affairs issued a notice on the eve of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the nation, announcing the 36 sites approved by the State Council on August 20 as the second batch of national key martyrs' memorial buildings for protection. The notice requires the relevant civil affairs departments of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government to promptly set up protection signs for the approved sites in their regions and strengthen protection and management work.

The second batch of national key martyrs' memorial buildings for protection includes: Tianjin Jixian County Pan Mountain Martyrs' Cemetery, Hebei Nangong Suinan Martyrs' Cemetery, Hebei Tangshan Jidong Martyrs' Cemetery, Shanxi Xingxian Jin-Sui Martyrs' Cemetery, Inner Mongolia Wuchuan County Daqingshan Revolutionary Heroes Memorial Monument, Jilin Siping City Martyrs' Cemetery, Jilin "Four Defend Lianjiang" Martyrs' Cemetery, Heilongjiang Mudanjiang "Eight Heroines Jump into the River" Martyrs' Group Sculpture, Shanghai Martyrs' Cemetery, Jiangsu Ganpu Anti-Japanese Mountain Martyrs' Cemetery, Anhui Liu'an Huabei Martyrs' Cemetery, Hefei City Anhui Revolutionary Martyrs' Exhibition Hall, Fujian Longyan Minxi Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery, Fujian Minhou Lin Xiangqian Martyrs' Cemetery, Jiangxi Shangrao Maojialing Martyrs' Cemetery, Shandong Qixia Jiaodong Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery, Jinan Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery, Shandong Mengyin Menglianggu Battle Martyrs' Cemetery, Henan Xin County E'Yu-Huai Su Area Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery, Hubei Hong'an Huangma Uprising and E'Yu-Huai Su Area Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery, Hubei Yangxin Xiang-E'Gan Border Area E'Dongnan Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery, Wuhan City Xiang Jingyue Martyrs' Cemetery, Wuhan City Shi Yang Martyrs' Cemetery, Changsha City Hunan Martyrs' Park, Guangdong Huifen County Martyrs' Cemetery, Guangzhou 19th Route Army Shanghai Anti-Japanese War Fallen Soldiers Cemetery, Hainan Wanning Liu Lianling Martyrs' Monument, Haikou Li Shuo Xun Martyrs' Memorial Pavilion, Sichuan Tongjiang Wangping Martyrs' Cemetery, Tibet Nangqian Mountain South Martyrs' Cemetery, Shaanxi Zichang Revolutionary Martyrs' Memorial Hall, Shaanxi Chang'an Yang Huicheng Martyrs' Cemetery, Lanzhou City Martyrs' Cemetery, Gansu Gaotai Martyrs' Cemetery, Xining City Martyrs' Cemetery, Xinjiang Yining Martyrs' Cemetery.

### 国务院批准第二批全国重点烈士纪念建筑物保护单位   要求各地抓紧设置标志加强管理

1989-09-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国务院批准第二批全国重点烈士纪念建筑物保护单位
    要求各地抓紧设置标志加强管理
    本报北京9月9日讯　民政部于建国40周年前夕发出通知，公布了国务院8月20日批准的第二批全国重点烈士纪念建筑物保护单位36处，要求有关省、自治区、直辖市民政部门对本地区被批准的保护单位抓紧设置保护标志，并加强保护管理工作。
    第二批全国重点烈士纪念建筑物保护单位是：天津蓟县盘山烈士陵园、河北南宫冀南烈士陵园、河北唐山冀东烈士陵园、山西兴县晋绥烈士陵园、内蒙古武川县大青山革命英雄纪念碑、吉林四平市烈士陵园、吉林“四保临江”烈士陵园、黑龙江牡丹江“八女投江”烈士群雕、上海市烈士陵园、江苏赣榆抗日山烈士陵园、安徽六安皖西烈士陵园、合肥市安徽革命烈士事迹陈列馆、福建龙岩闽西革命烈士陵园、福建闽侯林祥谦烈士陵园、江西上饶茅家岭烈士陵园、山东栖霞胶东革命烈士陵园、济南革命烈士陵园、山东蒙阴孟良崮战役烈士陵园、河南新县鄂豫皖苏区革命烈士陵园、湖北红安黄麻起义和鄂豫皖苏区革命烈士陵园、湖北阳新湘鄂赣边区鄂东南革命烈士陵园、武汉市向警予烈士陵园、武汉市施洋烈士陵园、长沙市湖南烈士公园、广东海丰县烈士陵园、广州十九路军淞沪抗日阵亡将士陵园、海南万宁六连岭烈士纪念碑、海口李硕勋烈士纪念亭、四川通江王坪烈士陵园、西藏乃东山南烈士陵园、陕西子长革命烈士纪念馆、陕西长安杨虎城烈士陵园、兰州市烈士陵园、甘肃高台烈士陵园、西宁市烈士陵园、新疆伊宁烈士陵园。
