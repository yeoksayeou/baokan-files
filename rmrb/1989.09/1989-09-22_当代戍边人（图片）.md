Title: Contemporary Border Guards
Author: Qiu Mingquan
Date: 1989-09-22
Page: 4

The broad masses of soldiers and officers guarding the long border lines of our motherland are striving to complete various tasks centered around border patrols, ensuring the peace and tranquility of the nation. The photograph depicts a patrol squad from a certain border defense unit of the Xinjiang Military Region, who, filled with the spirit of national defenders, recently trekked through the snow along the border line.
