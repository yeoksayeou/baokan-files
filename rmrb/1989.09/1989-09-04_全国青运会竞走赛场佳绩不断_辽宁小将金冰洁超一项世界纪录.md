Title: Excellent Results Continue at the National Youth Games Walking Events
Author: Chen Guangming, Wang Zhongqi
Date: 1989-09-04
Edition: 3

Excellent results continue at the National Youth Games walking events. A young athlete from Liaoning, Jin Bingjie, breaks a world record.

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, September 4th – (Reporter Chen Guangming, Correspondent Wang Zhongqi) Today's morning session of the second National Youth Games saw the women's A group 10,000-meter race walk final. Jin Bingjie, an 18-year-old from Liaoning, achieved a time of 43 minutes 15.6 seconds, surpassing the world record of 43 minutes 36.41 seconds set by Soviet athlete Nikolayev in Kiev last July 30th.

In this event, she also broke the Asian record of 43 minutes 52.1 seconds with another Liaoning athlete, Li Jingxue. Li Jingxue's time was 43 minutes 48 seconds. The previous Asian record was set by Liaoning athlete Chen Yueping in the 1987 National Games.

Li Mingcai, an 18-year-old athlete from Shandong, broke the national youth record in the men's A group 20,000-meter race walk final held this morning, with a time of 1 hour 25 minutes 15.8 seconds.

In the men's B group 10,000-meter race walk final, which concluded today, 17-year-old athletes Liang Songli from Liaoning, Tian Niantang from Shandong, and Chen Shaoguo from Yunnan, with times of 41 minutes 38.6 seconds, 41 minutes 59.5 seconds, and 42 minutes 13.3 seconds respectively, all broke the national youth record of 42 minutes 22.7 seconds.

### 全国青运会竞走赛场佳绩不断  辽宁小将金冰洁超一项世界纪录
陈光明  王忠奇
1989-09-04
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    全国青运会竞走赛场佳绩不断
    辽宁小将金冰洁超一项世界纪录
    新华社鞍山9月4日电　（记者陈光明、通讯员王忠奇）今天上午在这里举行的第二届全国青运会女子甲组10000米竞走决赛中，辽宁18岁的金冰洁以43分15秒6的成绩，超过由苏联竞走运动员尼科拉杰娃去年7月30日在苏联基辅创造的43分36秒41的该项世界纪录。
    在这项比赛中，她还和另一位辽宁选手李景雪打破43分52秒1的亚洲纪录。李景雪的成绩是43分48秒。原来的这项亚洲纪录是由辽宁选手陈跃玲于1987年在全运会上创造的。
    18岁的山东运动员李明才在今天上午举行的全国二青会男子甲组20000米竞走决赛中，以1小时25分15秒8的成绩，打破该项全国青年纪录。
    在今天结束的第二届全国青运会男子乙组10000米竞走决赛中，同为17岁的辽宁运动员梁松利、山东运动员田年堂、云南运动员陈绍果，分别以41分38秒6、41分59秒5、42分13秒3的成绩，一同打破了该项目42分22秒7的全国少年纪录。（附图片）
    9月4日，在全国青运会女子甲组1万米竞走决赛中，金冰洁（前）以43分15秒6超世界纪录。新华社记者　郑永吉摄
