Title: Bogota Bans Protests and Gatherings
Author:
Date: 1989-09-07
Edition: 3

Bogota bans protests and gatherings. Starting from the 6th, Colombia's capital, Bogota, has prohibited all forms of protests and gatherings to maintain public order and ensure the safety of its citizens.

### 波哥大禁止游行集会

1989-09-07
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    波哥大禁止游行集会
    哥伦比亚首都波哥大从六日起禁止一切形式的游行和集会活动，以维护公共秩序和保障市民的安全。
