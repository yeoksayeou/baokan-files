Title: "The Handbook of the History of the Founding of the Nation" Will Be Published
Author:
Date: 1989-09-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 16th.

On the occasion of celebrating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the nation, a historical reference book with considerable value, "The Handbook of the History of the Founding of the People's Republic of China," is being published and distributed by the Xinhua Publishing House.

This handbook systematically introduces the materials on the socialist revolution and construction since the birth of New China, reflecting the general situation and development changes of New China, showcasing the brilliant achievements in politics, military, economy, culture, education, science and technology, and diplomacy under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, especially the practical results of reform and opening up since the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee of the Party.

### “建国史手册”将出版

1989-09-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　“建国史手册”将出版
    据新华社北京9月16日电　在庆祝建国40周年之际，一部颇有参考价值的历史资料工具书——《中华人民共和国建国史手册》由新华出版社出版发行。
    这部手册系统地介绍了新中国诞生以来社会主义革命和建设的资料，反映了新中国的概貌和发展变化，显示了在中国共产党领导下，我国政治、军事、经济、文化、教育、科技和外交等方面取得的光辉成就，特别是党的十一届三中全会以来改革开放的实践成果。
