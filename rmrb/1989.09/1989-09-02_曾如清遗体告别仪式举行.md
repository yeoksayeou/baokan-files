Title: The Funeral of Comrade Zeng Ruqing is Held
Author:
Date: 1989-09-02
Edition: 2

The farewell ceremony for the late Comrade Zeng Ruqing, former Deputy Political Commissar of the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps, was held on May 8 in Nanjing.

Comrade Zeng Ruqing passed away from illness on April 27 in Nanjing at the age of 74. Some party and state leaders, as well as leaders from the Nanjing Military Region, Lanzhou Military Region, Xinjiang Military Region, Jiangsu Province, and the hometown of Comrade Zeng Ruqing in Jiangxi Province and Jian City, sent wreaths.

Comrade Zeng Ruqing joined the revolutionary activities in 1929, joined the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in 1931, and joined the Communist Youth League of China in the same year. He transferred to the Communist Party of China in 1932. He served as the Inspector of the Education Department of the Provisional Central Government of the Chinese Soviet Republic, Political Commissar of the New Fourth Army Corps, Political Commissar and Commander of the Field Army, Political Commissar of the Army, Second Political Commissar of the Jiangsu Military Region, and Standing Committee Member of the Jiangsu Provincial Party Committee. He was awarded the rank of Major General in 1955.

Comrade Zeng Ruqing was a loyal communist warrior who had undergone long-term tests, an excellent party member, an outstanding political worker and military commander of our army. He had firm communist ideals and beliefs and was devoted to the revolutionary cause. He had a strong sense of revolutionary responsibility and a high sense of duty, worked seriously and meticulously, and had a strong sense of party discipline and organizational discipline, considering the overall situation and being open-minded. He was modest and cautious, united with comrades, connected with the masses, and cared for subordinates. He was upright, fair, and pragmatic. He was honest and upright, and lived a simple and frugal life. (Accompanying photo)

### 曾如清遗体告别仪式举行

1989-09-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    曾如清遗体告别仪式举行
    新华社南京8月31日电　向原新疆生产建设兵团副政治委员曾如清同志遗体告别的仪式5月8日在南京举行。
    曾如清同志因病于4月27日在南京逝世，终年74岁。部分党和国家领导人，南京军区、兰州军区、新疆军区、江苏省以及曾如清同志原籍江西省、吉安市领导机关送了花圈。
    曾如清同志1929年参加革命活动，1931年参加中国工农红军，同年加入中国共产主义青年团，1932年转入中国共产党。他历任中华苏维埃临时中央政府教育部巡视员、新四军团政治委员、野战军师长兼政治委员、军政治委员、江苏省军区第二政治委员、江苏省委常委等职。他1955年被授予少将军衔。
    曾如清同志是久经考验的忠诚的共产主义战士，中国共产党的优秀党员，我军优秀的政治工作者和军事指挥员。他有坚定的共产主义理想和信念，对革命事业忠心耿耿；他有强烈的革命事业心和高度的责任感，工作认真负责，一丝不苟；他有坚强的党性观念和组织纪律观念，顾全大局，襟怀坦白；他谦虚谨慎，团结同志，联系群众，关心部属；他为人正派，办事公道，实事求是；他廉洁奉公，艰苦朴素。（附图片）
