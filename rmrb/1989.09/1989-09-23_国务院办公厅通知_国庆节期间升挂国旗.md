Title: Notice from the General Office of the State Council on Raising the National Flag During the National Day Period
Author:
Date: 1989-09-23
Edition: 1

	The General Office of the State Council issued a notice on September 20 regarding the raising of the national flag during the National Day period. The full text of the notice is as follows:

	This year marks the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. To enthusiastically celebrate this grand occasion and to strengthen patriotic education among all ethnic groups in the country, the following instructions are given regarding the raising of the national flag during the National Day period:

	1. All government agencies, organizations, enterprises, and public institutions should raise the national flag during the National Day period this year; where conditions permit, residents in urban and rural areas can also raise the national flag.

	2. The national flag is a symbol of the state. Loving and respecting the national flag is a concrete expression of patriotism. The raising and lowering of the national flag should be done seriously and solemnly, and any disrespectful behavior should be prevented. The methods for raising and lowering the national flag will continue to be implemented in accordance with the "Temporary Measures for the Raising of the National Flag of the People's Republic of China" issued by the Central Propaganda Department.

### 国务院办公厅通知  国庆节期间升挂国旗

1989-09-23
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    国务院办公厅通知
    国庆节期间升挂国旗
    新华社北京9月22日电  国务院办公厅9月20日发出关于国庆节期间升挂国旗的通知，通知全文如下：
    今年是中华人民共和国成立四十周年。为了热烈庆祝这一盛大节日，加强对全国各族人民进行爱国主义教育，现就国庆节期间升挂国旗的有关问题通知如下：
    一、今年国庆节期间，各机关、团体、企业、事业单位均应升挂国旗；城乡群众居住地有条件的，可以升挂国旗。
    二、国旗是国家的象征，热爱、尊重国旗是爱国主义的具体表现。升挂国旗要做到严肃认真，防止任何不庄重的现象发生。国旗的升降办法仍按中央宣传部《关于中华人民共和国国旗升挂的暂行办法》执行。
