Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-09-10
Edition: 2

	Vice Premier Zou Jiahua met with Mr. Cao Guangbiao, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Hong Kong Yung Nee Enterprise Co. Ltd. and Vice Chairman of the Board of Directors of Hong Kong Dragon Airlines, and his delegation of three on September 9 in Beijing.

	Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhao Puchi met with the Burundian Government Cultural Delegation on September 9 at the Great Hall of the People.

	Minister of Energy Huang Yizheng met with and hosted a banquet for the delegation led by Vice Chairman of the Electric Power Industry Committee Ri Chong-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on September 9 at the Great Hall of the People.

### 外事简讯

1989-09-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外事简讯
    ▲国务委员邹家华9日在北京会见了香港永新企业有限公司董事长、香港港龙航空公司副董事长曹光彪一行三人。
    ▲全国政协副主席赵朴初9日在人民大会堂会见了布隆迪政府文化代表团。
    ▲能源部部长黄毅诚9日在人民大会堂会见并宴请了由电力工业委员会副委员长李忠成率领的朝鲜电力代表团。
