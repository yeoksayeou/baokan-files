Title: State Council Office for Financial, Tax, and Price Inspection Notifies: All Units Should Immediately Conduct Self-Inspections, with a Deadline of October 15

Author:

Date: 1989-09-12

Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 12th.

The State Council Office for Financial, Tax, and Price Inspection today issued a notice, requiring all departments, companies, enterprises, and units to strictly follow the spirit and requirements of the State Council's "Notice on Conducting the 1989 Financial, Tax, and Price Inspection," to immediately deploy and conduct self-inspections. The deadline for self-inspections must not exceed October 15.

The notice pointed out that conducting the 1989 financial, tax, and price inspection is a major measure to implement the spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee, to combat corruption, punish "official profiteering," uphold discipline, strengthen the legal system, and carry out rectification and order. All departments, companies, enterprises, and units must, during the self-inspection, raise their awareness, correct their attitudes, overcome the mentality of taking chances, actively check various illegal and undisciplined behaviors, and voluntarily report truthfully without concealing or avoiding the serious issues.

The notice pointed out that for any illegal and undisciplined issues that are voluntarily self-inspected and reported within the inspection period, they can be handled leniently according to the law. Otherwise, once discovered, they will be strictly punished according to the relevant national financial, tax, and price regulations, and no leniency will be shown.

### 国务院财税物价大检查办公室通知　各单位应立即开展自查工作　自查期限不得超过10月15日

1989-09-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    国务院财税物价大检查办公室通知
　　各单位应立即开展自查工作
　　自查期限不得超过10月15日
    新华社北京9月12日电　国务院税收、财务、物价大检查办公室今天发出通知，要求所有部门、公司、企业和单位都要严格按照国务院《关于开展1989年税收、财务、物价大检查的通知》精神和要求，立即部署和开展自查工作，自查期限最迟不得超过10月15日。
    通知指出，开展1989年税收、财务、物价大检查是贯彻落实党的十三届四中全会精神，反对腐败，惩治“官倒”，严肃纪律，加强法制，搞好治理整顿的一项重大措施。所有部门、公司、企业和单位在自查中，都要提高认识，端正态度，克服侥幸心理，主动检查各种违法乱纪行为，自觉如实上报，不得隐匿不报或避重就轻。
    通知指出，凡属在自查期限内，主动自查自报的违法乱纪问题，均可依法从宽处理。否则，一经查出，一律按国家有关财政、税收、物价的处罚规定，依法从严惩处，不能变通。
