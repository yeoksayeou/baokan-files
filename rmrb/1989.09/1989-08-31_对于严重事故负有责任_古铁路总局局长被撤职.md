Title: Cuban Railway Chief Dismissed for Serious Accidents
Author:
Date: 1989-08-31
Edition: 3

Cuban Railway Chief Dismissed for Serious Accidents

	According to Xinhua News Agency in Havana on August 30, the Cuban Ministry of Transportation decided on August 30 to remove Cuban Railway General Bureau Director Gerardo Simon and several other officials from their posts.

	A statement from the Ministry of Transportation said that the Ministry also removed the heads of two regional railway bureaus.

	The statement also said that the above decisions were made because Simon and other railway bureau officials were responsible for two serious railway accidents that occurred in August.

### 对于严重事故负有责任  古铁路总局局长被撤职

1989-08-31
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    对于严重事故负有责任
    古铁路总局局长被撤职
    据新华社哈瓦那8月30日电　古巴运输部30日决定，解除古巴铁路总局局长赫拉尔德·西蒙和其他一些官员的职务。
    运输部的一份公告说，运输部还解除了两个地区铁路分局领导人的职务。
    公告还说，作出上述决定是因为西蒙等铁路局官员应对8月份接连发生2次严重铁路事故负责。
