Title: The State Sports Commission, the China Disabled Persons' Federation, and Others Congratulate the Chinese Disabled Sports Delegation Again
Author:
Date: 1989-09-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

The State Sports Commission, the China Disabled Persons' Federation, and the China Disabled Sports Association jointly sent a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese Disabled Sports Delegation in Kobe on September 20, celebrating their outstanding achievements. The telegram stated that the Fifth Far East and South Pacific Games for the Disabled had concluded. It was learned that in this Games, you won a total of 99 gold medals, 23 silver medals, and 11 bronze medals, ranking first in the total number of gold medals and breaking 15 world records. This is the best result achieved by the Chinese Disabled Sports Delegation in participating in international and continental competitions. This fully demonstrates the concern of the Party and the state for the disabled, and showcases the spirit of striving for national glory and fighting hard of the Chinese disabled athletes. We once again extend our warm congratulations to you. We look forward to your safe return.

### 国家体委中国残疾人联合会等  再次电贺我伤残人体育代表团

1989-09-21
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    国家体委中国残疾人联合会等
    再次电贺我伤残人体育代表团
    本报讯　9月20日，国家体委、中国残疾人联合会、中国伤残人体育协会联名向在神户的中国伤残人体育代表团再次发去贺电，祝贺他们取得的优异成绩。电文说，第五届远东及南太平洋地区伤残人运动会已落下帷幕。获悉你们在这届运动会中共取得99枚金牌、23枚银牌、11枚铜牌、金牌总数居首位并打破15项世界纪录的辉煌战绩。这是我伤残体育代表团历年参加国际、洲际大赛取得的最好成绩。这充分体现了党和国家对伤残人的关怀，展示了我伤残人运动员为国争光、奋力拚搏的精神风貌。再次对你们表示热烈祝贺。我们期待着你们的顺利归来。
