Title: Czechoslovakia Destroys Twenty Tanks
Author:
Date: 1989-09-13
Page: 3

According to a report by the Czech News Agency, Czechoslovakia on September 12th sent 20 T54M tanks to a repair facility for destruction. In accordance with the arms reduction measures announced by Czechoslovakia at the end of January this year, Czechoslovakia will destroy 850 tanks, and these 20 tanks are the first batch to be destroyed.

### 捷销毁二十辆坦克

1989-09-13
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    捷销毁二十辆坦克
    据捷通社报道，捷克斯洛伐克12日将20辆T54M型坦克送往修配厂销毁。根据捷今年1月底所宣布的裁军措施，捷将销毁850辆坦克，这20辆坦克是销毁的第一批。
