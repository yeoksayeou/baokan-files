Title: Eight Hundred Li of Dongting Lake and Eight Hundred Li of Trees
Author: Liu Yangchu Tang Daibai
Date: 1989-09-02
Page: 4

The sprawling Dongting Lake, covering an area of eight hundred li, now boasts 256 million mu of artificial forests. Over 3,200 kilometers of roads and 14,100 kilometers of waterways have been greened, with more than 13 billion trees planted along fields, dikes, canals, and roadsides. This amounts to over 200 trees per person. The timber stock has reached over 51.7 million cubic meters, demonstrating increasingly favorable ecological and economic benefits.
