Title: U.S. Military Exercises in the Panama Canal Zone
Author:
Date: 1989-09-10
Edition: 3

U.S. military forces in the Panama Canal Zone conducted military exercises on September 8, causing tensions to rise again in the already tense situation in Panama.

In the afternoon of September 8, 150 U.S. Marine Corps personnel stationed at the Rodman military base, aboard 13 light tanks and armored personnel carriers, headed to the "Panameña" region along the Pacific coast to participate in the exercises. The exercises included seizing and controlling three small hills at the mouth of the Panama Canal. Panama's new interim government believes this is a "prelude to a U.S. military invasion of Panama."

### 美军在巴拿马运河区演习

1989-09-10
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　美军在巴拿马运河区演习
    美国驻巴拿马运河区部队8日举行军事演习，使得一度缓和的巴拿马局势再度紧张起来。
    8日下午，驻扎在罗德曼军事基地的150名美国海军陆战队员，分乘13辆轻型坦克和运兵车，前往太平洋沿岸“巴拿梅娜”地区参加演习。演习内容包括占领控制巴拿马运河河口的3座小山。巴拿马新的临时政府认为，这是美国“军事入侵巴拿马的前兆”。
