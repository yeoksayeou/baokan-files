Title: Britain Strives to Improve Waste Recycling
Author:
Date: 1989-09-04
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency

Britain generates 20 million tons of garbage annually. Environmental workers say that at least 80% of this waste can be recycled. However, Britain's recycling rate is low: only 16% of glass is recycled (compared to 50% in West Germany, Denmark, the Netherlands, and Switzerland), less than one-third of waste paper is recycled, and only 8% of metal cans are recycled (less than half the European average). Britain produces 6 billion glass containers each year, but there are only 3,000 glass recycling points, averaging one for every 16,000 people. In the Netherlands, there is one for every 1,500 people. Britain produces 8.7 million tons of paper annually, requiring 90 million trees. There are over 40 grades of paper in Britain, and recycled paper that is not sorted by grade can only be used to make cardboard.

Effective waste recycling can reduce the need for landfills, decrease environmental pollution, and save energy. Recycling one ton of aluminum cans saves 95% of the energy compared to producing one ton from bauxite. Recycling glass saves 25% of energy, paper saves 50%, and plastic saves 80%.

To improve Britain's lagging waste recycling, *The Daily Telegraph* and "Friends of the Earth" have launched a nationwide recycling campaign. They urge the British government to include regulations in the upcoming "Green Bill" this autumn, requiring local authorities to develop recycling plans.

### 大力回收废品  英国不甘落伍

1989-09-04
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    大力回收废品  英国不甘落伍
    英国每年产生2000万吨垃圾。环境工作者说，其中至少有80％的垃圾可以回收。但是，英国的废品回收率很低：回收玻璃16％（联邦德国、丹麦、荷兰和瑞士都达50％），回收废纸不到1／3，回收金属罐头8％（不到欧洲平均数的一半）。英国每年生产60亿个玻璃容器，但是只有3000个废玻璃收集处，平均1.6万人1处。荷兰平均1500人1处。英国每年生产870万吨纸，要用9000万棵树。英国纸的等级有40多种，回收后不分等级的纸只能做硬纸板。
    废品回收工作做得好，可以减少倾倒垃圾的场地，减少环境污染，节约能源。
    用回收的铝罐头盒炼一吨铝，比用铝矾土炼一吨铝可节约能源95％。玻璃回收节能25％，纸回收节能50％，塑料回收节能80％。
    为了改变英国在废品回收方面的落后状况，《每日电讯报》和“地球之友”已发起在全英国开展废品回收运动，他们要求英国政府在今秋的“绿色法案”中作出规定，要求地方当局制订回收计划。
                      （新华社）
