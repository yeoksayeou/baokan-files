Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation: A Major Calligraphy and Painting Exhibition Opens
Author: Yì Jiǎnrú
Date: 1989-09-01
Edition: 2

	Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation: A Major Calligraphy and Painting Exhibition Opens
	BEIJING, Sept. 1 (Xinhua) -- A major calligraphy and painting exhibition to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the nation opened today at the Chinese People's Revolution Military Museum.

	The 400-plus works on display are diverse and vibrant, each showcasing unique styles. The artists have expressed their enthusiasm in praising the prosperity and flourishing of our great motherland, adding new treasures to the long-standing tradition of calligraphy and painting. The selected masterpieces were chosen from over 11,000 entries, with 204 works receiving awards. Additionally, 193 works from renowned artists both at home and abroad are on special display. Yang Dezhi and Yang Chengwu cut the ribbon for the exhibition.

### 庆祝建国四十年书画大联展开幕
易俭如
1989-09-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    庆祝建国四十年书画大联展开幕
    据新华社北京9月1日电  （记者易俭如）为庆祝建国40周年而举办的全国书画大联展，今天在中国人民革命军事博物馆开幕。
    参展的400余幅翰墨之作多姿多彩，各领风骚。作者们以洋溢的激情歌颂伟大祖国的繁荣昌盛，为源远流长的书画艺术增添瑰宝。展出的佳作是从11000多件参赛作品中遴选的，其中204件作品获奖。特约海内外名家作品193幅。杨得志、杨成武为展览剪彩。
