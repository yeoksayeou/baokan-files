Title: The Soviet Union Launches 6 Satellites with One Rocket
Author:
Date: 1989-09-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 15th.

According to TASS, the Soviet Union launched six satellites into near-Earth orbit using a single "Cyclone" rocket on September 14.

The initial orbital parameters for the six satellites are as follows: one revolution around the Earth—114 minutes, apogee—1435 kilometers, perigee—1394 kilometers, orbital inclination—82.6 degrees.

In addition to scientific instruments, the satellites are equipped with radio systems for precise orbital data measurement and wireless telemetry systems for transmitting the operational status of scientific instruments to the ground.

Currently, the equipment installed on the satellites is functioning normally. The coordinate calculation center is processing the continuously transmitted information.

### 苏用一枚火箭发射6颗卫星

1989-09-17
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　苏用一枚火箭发射6颗卫星
    新华社莫斯科9月15日电　据塔斯社报道，苏联14日用一枚“旋风”号火箭将6颗卫星送上近地轨道。
    6颗卫星进入预定轨道的初始参数为：绕地一周——114分钟、远地点——1435公里、近地点——1394公里、轨道夹角——82.6度。
    除科学仪器外，卫星上还装有准确测定轨道数据的无线电系统、向地面传送科学仪器工作情况的无线遥测系统。
    目前，安放在卫星上的装置工作正常。坐标计算中心正在处理不断传回的信息。
