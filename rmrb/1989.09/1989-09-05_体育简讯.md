Date: 1989-09-05
Edition: 3

	Sports News

	▲ The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications will issue a commemorative postcard with postage on September 6, 1989, titled "_Second National Youth Games 1989·9·Shenyang_."

	The second National Youth Games of the People's Republic of China will open on September 6, 1989, in Shenyang, Liaoning. The postcard features the emblem of this games and the newly constructed stadium in Shenyang for this event, with a blue sky and green field as the background. The postcard design includes the mascot of the games, a small tiger holding a torch, leading energetic young athletes running on a red track.

	▲ The 1989 National Golf Tournament began on September 5 at the Beijing Shunyi Golf Club. More than 30 athletes from seven clubs in Beijing, Tianjin, Zhongshan, Zhuhai, and Shenzhen are competing fiercely. The tournament will conclude on September 8.

### 体育简讯

1989-09-05
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    体育简讯
    ▲邮电部定于一九八九年九月六日发行《第二届全国青少年运动会一九八九·九·沈阳》纪念邮资明信片一枚。
    中华人民共和国第二届全国青少年运动会将于一九八九年九月六日在辽宁沈阳开幕。明信片的邮票图案是本届运动会会徽和沈阳市为本届运动会新建的体育场，并以蓝天草地做底色；明信片图案是本届运动会的吉祥物小老虎举着火炬，带领朝气蓬勃的青少年运动员奔跑在红色的跑道上。
    ▲一九八九年全国高尔夫球比赛九月五日在北京顺义高尔夫球俱乐部开杆。来自北京、天津、中山、珠海和深圳等地七个俱乐部队的三十余名选手展开了激烈角逐。比赛将于八日结束。
