Title: The Y-7 Aircraft Achieves "Double Ten Thousand" Safe Flights
Author: Li Bing Sheng
Date: 1989-09-07
Edition: 2

The Y-7 aircraft has safely flown over "double ten thousand": as of September 4, the cumulative safe flight hours reached 100,900, and the number of safe takeoffs and landings reached 100,100. This is a milestone in the development of China's aviation industry.

The Y-7 aircraft was developed by the Xi'an Aircraft Industrial Corporation under the personal care of Premier Zhou Enlai. It is currently the only domestically produced passenger aircraft in China. It has been in service since 1984, with a total of 51 aircraft delivered so far. Among these, 42 are operating on 76 domestic routes.

### “运七”机安全飞行“双十万”
李秉胜
1989-09-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    “运七”机安全飞行“双十万”
    本报西安9月7日电　截至9月4日，我国生产的“运七”飞机安全飞行突破“双十万”：累计安全飞行10.09万个小时，安全起落10.01万次。这是我国航空工业发展的一个里程碑。
    “运七”飞机是在周恩来总理亲切关怀下，由西安飞机工业公司研制成功的我国目前唯一的国产客机。1984年开始投入使用，至今已交付51架，其中有42架在国内76条航线上飞行。　（李秉胜）
