Title: Ong Teng Cheong Re-elected as President of Singapore
Author:
Date: 1989-09-01
Edition: 3

Ong Teng Cheong was re-elected as the President of Singapore on August 31 in the parliamentary election. He will continue to serve as President for the next four years.

### 黄金辉连任新加坡总统

1989-09-01
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    黄金辉连任新加坡总统
    新加坡总统黄金辉8月31日在国会选举中再次获胜，在今后4年里，他将继续担任总统职务。
