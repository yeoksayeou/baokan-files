In mid-August, the largest-scale artillery battle since the outbreak of the Lebanese Civil War in 1975 took place between Lebanese Christian militias and Syrian forces. Large numbers of residents in Beirut fled their homes, leaving only those who had to live underground.

The top picture shows a grandmother teaching her grandson in a trench.

The left picture shows a family living in an underground basement.
