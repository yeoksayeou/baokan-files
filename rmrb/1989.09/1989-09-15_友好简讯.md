Title: Friendly News Briefs
Author:
Date: 1989-09-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th.

Friendly News Briefs

● Jorge Risquet, a member of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of Cuba, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Minister of Foreign Relations, met with Zhu Liang, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Minister of the International Liaison Department, and his entourage in Havana on the afternoon of the 13th. Both sides had a friendly conversation.

● Mei Ping, the new Chinese Ambassador to the Republic of Malta, presented his credentials to Maltese President Vincent Tabone at the Presidential Palace on the morning of the 14th.

● Pakistani President Ghulam Ishaq Khan met with a delegation from the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region of China, led by Mao Dexue, Vice Chairman of the People's Government of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, in Islamabad on the 14th. During the meeting, Ghulam Ishaq Khan stated that the close friendship between Pakistan and China has withstood the test of time and political changes in both countries. As time goes by, the friendship between the two countries will be further strengthened and developed.

● On the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the collaborative achievement between the publishing industries of China and the Soviet Union, the "Encyclopedia of New China" (Russian edition), was officially presented to readers at the Moscow International Book Fair on the 14th. This encyclopedia was jointly published by the Soviet "Progress" Publishing House and the Chinese Foreign Languages Press. It includes many new materials introducing contemporary Chinese life.

● The Chinese silk fashion show and silk product exhibition opened on the 14th in Sofia, receiving a warm welcome from the Bulgarian audience. This fashion show and product exhibition were organized by the China National Silk Import and Export Corporation at the invitation of the Bulgarian "Industrial Association for Artificial Fibers." The purpose is to introduce to the Bulgarian people the level of production and supply capabilities of Chinese silk fashion. Both sides will further explore the possibilities of cooperation in this area.

● China has reached preliminary agreements on information cooperation with Japan and the United States respectively.

From September 9th to 13th, the 21st annual meeting of the American Information Industry Association was held. The Chinese delegation attending the meeting reached a preliminary agreement with the Japan Information Industry Association on cooperation in large-scale information software projects between the two countries.

At the same time, the China Information Industry Association also reached a preliminary agreement with the American Information Industry Association on training Chinese information industry entrepreneurs.

### 友好简讯

1989-09-15
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：友好简讯

　　友好简讯
    ●古巴共产党政治局委员、书记处书记、对外关系部部长豪尔赫·里斯克特13日下午在哈瓦那会见了正在古巴访问的中共中央委员、中联部部长朱良及其随行人员，双方进行了友好的谈话。
    ●中国新任驻马耳他共和国大使梅平14日上午在总统府向马耳他共和国总统文森特·塔博恩递交了国书。
    ●巴基斯坦总统伊沙克·汗14日在伊斯兰堡会见了以新疆维吾尔自治区人民政府副主席毛德华为团长的中国新疆维吾尔自治区代表团。会见时，伊沙克·汗说，巴中之间的亲密友谊经受住了时间和两国政治变迁的考验。随着时间的流逝，两国间的友谊将不断得到巩固和发展。
    ●在中华人民共和国成立40周年即将到来之际，中苏两国出版界合作的成果《新中国百科全书》（俄文版）14日在莫斯科国际书展上正式与读者见面。这部百科全书是由苏联“进步”出版社同中国外交出版社合作出版的。它收集了介绍当代中国生活情况的许多新的材料。
    ●中国丝绸时装表演和丝绸商品展览14日在索非亚开幕，受到保加利亚观众的热烈欢迎。
    这次时装表演和商品展览是中国丝绸进出口总公司应保加利亚“为人工业协会”邀请举办的。目的是为了向保加利亚人民介绍中国丝绸时装的制作水平和产品供货能力的情况。双方将进一步探讨两国在这方面进行合作的可能性。
    ●中国与日本和美国分别达成关于信息合作的初步协议。
    9月9日至13日，美国信息工业协会举行第21届年会。出席年会的中国代表团与日本信息产业协会就两国之间进行信息软件大型项目的合作达成初步协议。
    同时，中国信息产业商会还与美国信息工业协会就培训中国信息产业的企业家达成一项初步协议。
                          （据新华社）
