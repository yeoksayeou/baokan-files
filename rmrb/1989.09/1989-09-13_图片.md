Title: Children Welcoming the Liberation Army in Beijing
Author: Chen Zhi
Date: 1989-09-13
Page: 6

In February 1949, after the peaceful liberation of Beijing, the People's Liberation Army held an entry ceremony, which was warmly welcomed by the people. The picture shows children from Beijing enthusiastically sitting on the cannons of the soldiers in front of the East Side of the Arrow Tower and the front of the railway station. Photographed by Chen Zhi.
