Title: Qin Jiyue Meets with Pakistani Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-09-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 5th.

Qin Jiyue Meets with Pakistani Military Delegation

Today evening, State Councilor and Minister of National Defense Qin Jiyue met and hosted a banquet for the Pakistani military delegation led by Defense Secretary Zedi at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

In the afternoon, General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Der Hao-tian met with Zedi and his delegation.

### 秦基伟会见巴基斯坦军事代表团

1989-09-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    秦基伟会见巴基斯坦军事代表团
    据新华社北京9月5日电　国务委员兼国防部长秦基伟今天晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请由国防秘书泽迪率领的巴基斯坦军事代表团。
    下午，中国人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田会见了泽迪一行。
