Title: China's Import and Export Volume Increased 90-Fold Since Founding
Author: Zhang Guorong
Date: 1989-09-01
Page: 2

Since the founding of New China, our country has adhered to the principles of equality and mutual benefit, practicality, diversity, and common development in international exchanges, and has vigorously developed foreign trade, achieving remarkable results.

According to statistics from the National Bureau of Statistics, China has now established trade relations with 184 countries and regions. The country's ranking in global trade volume has risen from 32nd in 1978 to 16th in 1988. Last year, the total import and export volume was 90 times that of 1950. Among them, the total export volume increased by 85.4 times, and the total import volume increased by 94.3 times.

### 我国进出口总额建国后增九十倍
张国荣
1989-09-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    我国进出口总额建国后增九十倍
    本报北京9月1日讯  记者张国荣报道：新中国成立以来，我国在国际交往中坚持平等互利、讲求实效、形式多样、共同发展的原则，大力开展对外贸易，成绩斐然。
    据国家统计局统计，现在我国已经同世界上184个国家和地区开展贸易，进出口总额在世界的位次由1978年的第32位上升到1988年的第16位。去年进出口总额比1950年增长90倍，其中出口总额增长85.4倍，进口总额增长94.3倍。
