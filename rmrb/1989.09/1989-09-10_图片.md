Title: Worker's Fall and Recovery
Author: Cháng Mínglù
Date: 1989-09-10
Page: 5

On March 11, Liu Huimin, a female worker from the Hemp Textile Factory in Zhengzhou, Henan Province, fell from the fifth floor. After receiving meticulous treatment at the local military hospital, her life was saved. Upon learning of her family's financial difficulties, workers from the Hemp Textile Factory and local residents donated money generously. The image shows Liu Huimin (center) bidding farewell to medical staff on August 26 as she is discharged from the hospital. Photograph by Cháng Mínglù.
