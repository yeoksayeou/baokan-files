Title: Two Books by Comrade Deng Xiaoping Published
Author:
Date: 1989-08-31
Edition: 1

The two books "Comrade Deng Xiaoping on Upholding the Four Cardinal Principles and Opposing Bourgeois Liberalization" and "Comrade Deng Xiaoping on Reform and Opening Up," edited by the Party Literature Research Office of the CPC Central Committee and published by the People's Publishing House, have been released in bookstores in Beijing and other regions. The two books include more than 50 articles, most of which are full texts, totaling nearly 200,000 words. These are important speeches and talks given by Comrade Deng Xiaoping since the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee, reflecting his consistent views on these two fundamental issues.

The Central Propaganda Department of the CPC recently issued a notice stating that organizing cadres and the masses at all levels to study these two books is of great significance in raising awareness of the importance of the current serious political struggle, improving ideological and theoretical levels, and enhancing the conscious implementation of the spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee.

These two books are also published by the Nationalities Publishing House in Mongolian, Tibetan, Uyghur, Kazakh, and Korean, totaling five languages.

### 邓小平同志两本书出版  中宣部通知组织干部群众认真学习

1989-08-31
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    邓小平同志两本书出版
    中宣部通知组织干部群众认真学习
    新华社北京8月31日电　由中共中央文献研究室编辑、人民出版社出版的《邓小平同志论坚持四项基本原则反对资产阶级自由化》、《邓小平同志论改革开放》两本书，已在北京和各地新华书店陆续发行。收入这两本书的50多篇文稿，除少数是节选外，多数是全文，近20万字，都是邓小平同志自党的十一届三中全会以来的重要讲话和谈话，反映了他在这两个基本问题上的一贯思想。
    中共中央宣传部最近发出通知，指出组织各级干部和广大群众学好这两本书，对于提高对当前这场严重的政治斗争意义的认识，提高思想理论水平，提高贯彻执行十三届四中全会精神的自觉性，具有重要意义。
    这两本书同时由民族出版社用蒙古、藏、维吾尔、哈萨克、朝鲜五种文字出版。
