Title: The Party Branch of Xiaomaying Village in Wuzhi County, Henan Province
Author: wáng tiān dìng
Date: 1989-09-03
Edition: 5

The Party branch of Xiaomaying Village in Wuzhi County, Henan Province, has been consecutively recognized as an "Advanced Party Branch" by the Jiaozuo Municipal Committee for three years. The photo shows the branch secretary Zhang Xitong (second from the left) having a heart-to-heart talk with a fish farming specialist. Photographed by Wang Tian ding.
