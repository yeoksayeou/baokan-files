Title: Hong Kong's Top Rankings
Author: Sha Xiumin
Date: 1989-09-21
Page: 4

Hong Kong's top rankings:

	•	Hong Kong leads the world in watch exports.
	•	Hong Kong leads the world in toy exports.
	•	Hong Kong leads the world in high-end garment exports.
	•	Hong Kong leads the world in electric fan exports.
	•	Hong Kong leads the world in flashlight exports.
	•	Hong Kong leads the world in electric hair curler exports.
	•	Hong Kong leads the world in private container terminals.
	•	Hong Kong, along with London and New York, is one of the world's three major financial centers.
	•	Hong Kong, along with London and Zurich, is one of the world's three major gold trading centers.
	•	Hong Kong, along with New York and Rotterdam, is one of the world's three major container ports.
