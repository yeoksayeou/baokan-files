Title: Beijing International Television Week Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 1st.

The first large-scale international cultural exchange event on the capital's screens—“Beijing International Television Week”—opened this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People.

Friends from 15 delegations representing 14 countries and regions, including Czechoslovakia, North Korea, Egypt, France, East Germany, Hungary, Japan, Mexico, Pakistan, Poland, the United States, the Soviet Union, Yugoslavia, and Hong Kong, gathered with colleagues from China's film and television industry. In a warm and joyful atmosphere, they enjoyed performances by Chinese and foreign actors, including magic shows, solo singing, and orchestral performances of famous tunes from the participating countries.

Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Li Ruihuang, a member of the Secretariat, State Councilor, and Mayor of Beijing, Chen Xitong, and Minister of Radio, Film, and Television, Ai Zhisheng, attended the opening ceremony. Diplomatic envoys from the participating countries and representatives from television stations in various provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions were also present.

Mayor Chen Xitong, on behalf of the Beijing Municipal Government and the city's 10 million residents, extended warm congratulations on the opening of the Television Week. He said that the event would not only enliven Beijing's television screens and enrich the cultural lives of its residents but also promote cultural exchanges and friendly relations between Beijing and other countries, fostering mutual understanding and friendship between the people of Beijing and the people of other nations.

Organized by the Beijing Television Station, the “Beijing International Television Week” will broadcast from August 31 to September 6, featuring 15 television dramas provided by 18 television stations, along with 58 documentaries, animated films, and art films.

According to Long Xinmin, Director of the Beijing Television Station and Chairman of the Organizing Committee of the Television Week, the participating television dramas and documentaries cover a wide range of topics and styles, reflecting rich national characteristics. In particular, the documentary and landscape films are meticulously crafted, poetically depicting the customs and people of the participating countries. He hopes that the Television Week will contribute to the exchange of television arts and the development of friendly cooperation between Chinese and foreign television stations.

During the Television Week, a Beijing International Knowledge Competition for middle school students and a long-distance mountain climbing race up the Mutianyu section of the Great Wall, with over 2,000 participants, will also be held. The leading actors from the Mexican television series “Thorns” and the female lead from “Banka” will join Chinese dubbing actors, film directors, and television actors for a get-together. Performers from the Ershan Folk Drum Troupe of Ansai County, Shaanxi Province, will also come to Beijing to participate in the large-scale ethnic arts evening—“Mother River”—which will feature over 800 performers.

### 北京国际电视周开幕  李瑞环等同14个国家和地区的朋友出席开幕式

1989-09-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    北京国际电视周开幕
    李瑞环等同14个国家和地区的朋友出席开幕式
    据新华社北京9月1日电  （记者苗素群）首都荧屏第一次大规模的国际文化交流盛会——“北京国际电视周”今天下午在人民大会堂隆重开幕。
    来自捷克斯洛伐克、朝鲜、埃及、法国、民主德国、匈牙利、日本、墨西哥、巴基斯坦、波兰、美国、苏联、南斯拉夫和香港等14个国家和地区的15个代表团的朋友们同中国影视界的同行们相聚一堂，在热烈、欢快的气氛中，欣赏由中外演员表演的魔术、独唱和军乐团演奏的各参展国家名曲。
    中共中央政治局常委、书记处书记李瑞环、国务委员、北京市长陈希同、广播电影电视部部长艾知生，参展国驻华使节，以及各省、市、自治区电视台代表出席了开幕式。
    陈希同市长代表北京市政府和全市1000万市民向电视周开幕表示热烈祝贺。他说，电视周不但会活跃北京的电视荧屏，丰富市民的文化生活，还将促进北京同各国的文化交流和友好往来，增进北京人民和各国人民的相互了解和友谊。
    由北京电视台主办的“北京国际电视周”于8月31日至9月6日展播18家电视台提供的15部电视剧和58部纪录片、动画片和艺术片。
    据电视周组委会主任、北京电视台台长龙新民介绍，参展的电视剧和纪录片题材多样，风格各异，有较浓郁的民族风格。尤其是纪录片和风光片的拍摄手法考究，诗情画意般地展现了各参展国的风土人情。他希望电视周将为电视艺术交流和发展中外电视台的友好合作关系作出贡献。
    据悉，电视周期间还将举办由北京中学生参加的北京国际知识竞赛和有2000多人参加的攀登慕田峪长城越野赛。墨西哥电视连续剧《坎坷》中男女主角的扮演者和《卞卡》中的女主角将与中国的配音演员、译制导演、影视演员联欢。陕西安塞农民腰鼓队的演员也将赴京参加有800多人参加的大型民族文艺晚会——《母亲河》的演出。
