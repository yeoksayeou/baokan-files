Title: The State Decides to Raise Cotton Procurement Prices
Author: Wáng Chángliàng Zhào Zéjūn
Date: 1989-09-04
Edition: 1

	The People's Daily reports that the State has decided to raise the procurement price for cotton. Starting from the 1989 new cotton season, the procurement price for each 50 kilograms of standard-grade lint cotton will be increased by 60 yuan on the basis of the 1988 "three-seven" ratio price increase. The procurement prices for other grades of cotton will be calculated according to the original grade difference rate. The procurement prices for substandard cotton will be determined by each province, autonomous region, and municipality.

	The Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Commerce, and the State Price Bureau jointly issued a notice stating that this measure is aimed at further mobilizing the enthusiasm of farmers to plant and sell cotton, promoting the stable development of cotton production, maintaining normal procurement order, and ensuring the smooth completion of the procurement plan. The notice states that for each 50 kilograms of sawtooth cotton within the same grade, the procurement price will be adjusted to 8.2 yuan based on the same grade lint cotton price, with a subsidy of 5.8 yuan.

### 国家决定提高棉花收购价  每50公斤标准级皮辊棉提价60元
王长亮　赵泽琨
1989-09-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    国家决定提高棉花收购价
    每50公斤标准级皮辊棉提价60元
    本报讯　记者从国家物价局获悉，自1989年新棉上市起，国家决定，每50公斤标准级皮辊棉收购价格，在1988年度“倒三七”比例加价基础上提高60元，其余各等级棉花收购价按原定的等级差率计算。等外棉花收购价格由各省、区、市自定。
    财政部、商业部和国家物价局为此联合发布的通知说，这一措施是为了进一步调动农民种植和交售棉花的积极性，促进棉花生产稳定发展，维护正常的收购秩序，保证收购计划的顺利完成。通知说，锯齿棉级内各等级的收购价在同级皮棉价格的基础上，每50公斤仅耗补贴由5.8元调整到8.2元。
                      （王长亮　赵泽琨）
