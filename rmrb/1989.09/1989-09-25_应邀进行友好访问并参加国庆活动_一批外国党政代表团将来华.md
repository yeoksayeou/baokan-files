Title: Invited to Conduct Friendly Visits and Participate in National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1989-09-25
Edition: 1

A batch of foreign party and government delegations will come to China

New China News Agency, Beijing, September 24 - The spokesperson for the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China announced today: At the invitation of the Communist Party of China, a batch of foreign party and government delegations will come to China during the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China to conduct friendly visits and participate in China's National Day activities.

The delegation from the German Democratic Republic, led by Egon Krenz, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Secretary of the Central Committee, and the Vice Chairman of the State Council of the German Democratic Republic, will visit China from September 25 to October 2. The delegation includes 7 members, including Central Committee member and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers Wolfgang Rauhfus.

The delegation from the National Unity and Progress Party of Burundi, led by Alfons Kadge, Deputy Coordinator of the National Standing Committee, will visit from September 25 to October 7.

Jairu Lijodi, a member of the National Executive Committee of the Kenya African National Union and the Deputy Minister of Information and Broadcasting of the Government, will lead a delegation to arrive in Beijing on September 25 for a two-week visit to China.

A delegation led by Leonel Soto, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Cuba, will visit China from September 29 to October 9.

Raúl Baca, the leader of the Democratic Left Party of Ecuador and the Minister of Social Welfare, and his wife will visit China from September 25 to October 6.

A delegation led by Minoru Kawakami, Chairman of the Japan-China Special Committee of the Japan Socialist Party, will visit China from September 29 to October 1.

### 应邀进行友好访问并参加国庆活动  一批外国党政代表团将来华

1989-09-25
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    应邀进行友好访问并参加国庆活动
    一批外国党政代表团将来华
    新华社北京9月24日电　中共中央对外联络部发言人今天宣布：应中国共产党邀请，建国40周年期间将有一批外国党政代表团来华进行友好访问并参加中国国庆活动。
    德国统一社会党中央政治局委员、中央书记、民主德国国务委员会副主席埃贡·克伦茨率领的德意志民主共和国党和国家代表团将于9月25日至10月2日访华。代表团成员有中央委员、部长会议副主席沃尔夫冈·劳赫富斯等7人。
    以全国常务书记处副协调员阿尔方斯·卡德盖为团长的布隆迪民族统一进步党代表团将于9月25日至10月7日来访。
    肯尼亚非洲民族联盟全国执委、全国司库兼政府新闻广播部副部长贾·里·利焦迪将率领代表团于9月25日抵京，访华两周。
    古巴共产党中央书记莱昂内尔·索托一行定于9月29日至10月9日访华。
    厄瓜多尔民主左派党领导人、社会福利部长劳尔·巴卡夫妇定于9月25日至10月6日来华访问。
    日本社会党日中特别委员会委员长河上民雄一行将于9月29日至10月1日访华。
