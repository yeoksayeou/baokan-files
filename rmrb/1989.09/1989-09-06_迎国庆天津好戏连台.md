Title: Tianjin Presents a Grand Stage of Performances to Welcome National Day
Author:
Date: 1989-09-06
Edition: 2

While preparing for the "Autumn by the Haihe River" to welcome National Day, several dozen famous performers from Tianjin, including Ma Sanli, Wang Yuting, and Xiao Hua Yulan, recently formed a "Voluntary Performing Arts Troupe" to visit and perform for free for frontline workers, farmers, and soldiers. In addition to hosting the second Tianjin Drama Festival and the Tianjin Music Gathering, Tianjin will also showcase 13 outstanding programs.

### 迎国庆天津好戏连台

1989-09-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    迎国庆天津好戏连台
    本报天津9月6日电　在筹办“海河之秋”迎接国庆的同时，天津马三立、王玉磬、小花玉兰等数十位名演员日前自发组成“文艺义务演出团”，为一线工、农、兵作纯义务访问演出。天津除举办第二届戏剧节、津门曲荟等专业演出，还有13项优秀节目展演。
