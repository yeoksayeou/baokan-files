Title: Yang Shangkun Bids Farewell to Compaoré
Author:
Date: 1989-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 9th.

President Yang Shangkun this afternoon went to the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse to bid farewell to Burkinabé President and Head of Government Blaise Compaoré.

Yang Shangkun said to Compaoré: "Your visit this time has been very successful. You have held talks with China's major party and government leaders."

Compaoré and his delegation are departing this afternoon for Chengdu and will then go to Shanghai for a visit. Yang Shangkun briefly introduced the situation in Chengdu and Shanghai to Compaoré and wished him a pleasant journey.

### 杨尚昆同孔波雷话别

1989-09-09
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨尚昆同孔波雷话别
    据新华社北京9月9日电　国家主席杨尚昆今天下午前往钓鱼台国宾馆同布基纳法索人民阵线主席、国家元首、政府首脑布莱斯·孔波雷话别。
    杨尚昆对孔波雷说：“你这次访问很成功，同中国的主要党、政领导人都进行了会谈。”
    孔波雷一行今天下午启程前往成都访问，然后去上海参观。杨尚昆简要地向孔波雷介绍了成都和上海的情况，并祝他旅途愉快。
