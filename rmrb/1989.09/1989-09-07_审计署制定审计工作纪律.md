Title: The Audit Office Formulates Audit Work Discipline
Author:
Date: 1989-09-07
Edition: 2

The Audit Office has recently formulated the "Six Disciplines for Audit Personnel in Audit Work" in accordance with the actual situation of audit work, and has notified the audited units to supervise the implementation.

The six disciplines emphasize that when audit personnel go to the audited units to perform audit tasks, they must adhere to the following:

1. Do not accept invitations to dine;
2. Do not accept gifts or purchase low-priced, scarce commodities;
3. Do not solicit or accept bribes;
4. Do not use their authority to seek personal gain;
5. Do not conceal any illegal or disciplinary issues found;
6. Do not disclose secrets involved in the audit.

The Audit Office stresses that those who violate audit disciplines will be seriously dealt with.

### 审计署制定审计工作纪律

1989-09-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    审计署制定审计工作纪律
    新华社北京9月7日电　最近，审计署结合审计工作的实际情况，制定了《审计人员在审计工作中六项纪律》，并通知被审计单位监督执行。
    这六项纪律强调审计机关工作人员到被审计单位执行审计任务时，必须做到：
    一、不接受宴请；
    二、不接受礼品或购买低价、紧俏商品；
    三、不索贿、受贿；
    四、不利用职权为个人谋私利；
    五、不隐瞒查出的违法违纪问题；
    六、不泄露审计中涉及到的机密。
    审计署强调，将对违反审计纪律的人员进行严肃处理。
