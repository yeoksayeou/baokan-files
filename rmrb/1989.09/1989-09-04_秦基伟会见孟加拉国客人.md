Title: Qin Jianwei Meets with Bangladeshi Guests

Author:

Date: 1989-09-04

Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 4th.

Qin Jianwei, State Councilor and Minister of National Defense, met with a delegation led by Lieutenant General Montaz Ahmed, Chief of Staff of the Bangladesh Air Force, this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People.

Qin Jianwei said that the peoples of China and Bangladesh have a traditional friendship. Since the establishment of diplomatic relations, friendly cooperation between the two countries and their militaries has developed smoothly. He said that both China and Bangladesh are part of the Third World, and their friendship is sincere.

China's suppression of the counter-revolutionary rebellion in June this year received sympathy and support from the government and people of Bangladesh.

Montaz said that Bangladesh and China have the best friendly cooperative relations. Both countries are part of the Third World and love peace.

### 秦基伟会见孟加拉国客人

1989-09-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    秦基伟会见孟加拉国客人
    据新华社北京9月4日电　国务委员兼国防部长秦基伟今天下午在人民大会堂会见了孟加拉国空军参谋长蒙塔兹·艾哈迈德少将一行。
    秦基伟说，中孟两国人民有着传统的友谊。中孟建交后，两国、两军的友好合作关系得到了顺利发展。他说，中孟同属第三世界，它们的友谊是真诚的。
    中国今年6月平息反革命暴乱，得到了孟加拉国政府和人民的同情和支持。
    蒙塔兹说，孟中两国有着最好的友好合作关系。两国同属第三世界，都热爱和平。
