Title: Bangladesh Welcomes Pakistan's Return to the Commonwealth
Author:
Date: 1989-09-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Dhaka, 17th September.

Bangladesh welcomes Pakistan's return to the Commonwealth.

A spokesman for the Bangladesh Ministry of Foreign Affairs said today in Dhaka that Bangladesh welcomes Pakistan's return to the Commonwealth.

The Secretary-General of the Commonwealth, Shridath Ramphal, announced in London on the 15th that Pakistan will rejoin the Commonwealth on October 1st of this year.

Pakistan withdrew from the Commonwealth in 1972 to protest the Commonwealth's recognition of Bangladesh.

He said that Bangladesh has been urging the member countries of the Commonwealth to agree to readmit Pakistan. The Pakistani government has expressed its thanks and appreciation to Bangladesh for this.

### 孟欢迎巴基斯坦重返英联邦

1989-09-18
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　孟欢迎巴基斯坦重返英联邦
    新华社达卡9月17日电孟加拉国外交部一位发言人今天在这里说，孟加拉国欢迎巴基斯坦重返英联邦。
    英联邦秘书长什里达恩·兰法尔15日在伦敦宣布说，巴基斯坦将于今年10月1日重新加入英联邦。
    巴基斯坦于1972年退出英联邦，以抗议英联邦承认孟加拉国。
    他说，孟加拉国一直在敦促英联邦成员国同意再次接受巴基斯坦。对此，巴基斯坦政府已向孟加拉国表示了它的感谢与赞赏。
