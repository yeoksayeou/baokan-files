Title: Li Peng Meets and Dines with Pao Yue-kong
Author:
Date: 1989-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 29th.

    Premier Li Peng of the State Council met and dined with Hong Kong's prominent figure Pao Yue-kong this noon at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

    Li Peng and Pao Yue-kong exchanged views on the economic situations of Hong Kong and the mainland.

    Ma Jianfeng, Director of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office, attended the meeting and the banquet.

    This morning, Ma Jianfeng and Pao Yue-kong discussed the work of the Basic Law Drafting Committee for Hong Kong.

### 李鹏会见并宴请包玉刚

1989-09-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见并宴请包玉刚
    新华社北京九月二十九日电　国务院总理李鹏今天中午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请了香港知名人士包玉刚。
    李鹏同包玉刚就香港和内地的经济情况交换了意见。
    国务院港澳办公室主任姬鹏飞参加了会见和宴会。
    今天上午，姬鹏飞主任和包玉刚商讨了香港基本法起草委员会的有关工作。
