Title: Foreign Affairs Briefing
Author:
Date: 1989-09-09
Edition: 2

	National People's Congress Standing Committee Vice Chairman Ye Fei met with the delegation led by Vice Chairman of the Guyana-China Friendship Association and member of the People's National Congress Party of Guyana, Bipat, on September 9 at the Great Hall of the People.

	National People's Congress Standing Committee Vice Chairman and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Ni Zhifu, met with the delegation led by Chairman of the Seychelles National Workers' Union, Charles, on September 9.

	State Councilor and Minister of Finance, Wang Bingqian, met with the delegation led by Governor of the State Bank of Czechoslovakia, Potacek, on September 9 at the Great Hall of the People.

	National People's Congress Standing Committee Vice Chairman and President of the All-China Women's Federation, Chen Muhua, met and hosted a banquet for the delegation led by the Secretary of State for Social Affairs of the Central African Republic and President of the Central African Democratic Women's Federation, Madame Genevieve Lombilo, on September 9 at the Great Hall of the People. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 外事简讯

1989-09-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长叶飞9日在人民大会堂会见了圭亚那中国友好协会副主席、圭亚那人民全国大会党议员比帕特一行。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、中华全国总工会主席倪志福9日会见了塞舌耳全国工人工会主席查尔斯一行。
    ▲国务委员兼财政部部长王丙乾9日在人民大会堂会见了由行长波塔奇率领的捷克斯洛伐克国家银行代表团。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、全国妇联主席陈慕华9日在人民大会堂会见并宴请了由中非共和国社会事务国务秘书、中非民主妇女联盟主席热纳维埃芙·隆比洛夫人率领的中非妇女代表团。　（新华社）
