Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 31st.

**Foreign Affairs Brief**

▲ Vice Premier Wu Xueqian met with the Secretary-General of the Asian-African Legal Consultative Committee, Enzanga, on August 31st at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse. Enzanga arrived in Beijing on August 27th at the invitation of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to understand the situation in Beijing after the suppression of the protests and to discuss with the Chinese side the specifics of the 29th annual meeting of the Asian-African Legal Consultative Committee, scheduled to be held in Beijing from March 12th to 17th, 1990.

▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liao Hansheng, met and hosted a banquet for a delegation from the Brazilian Democratic Movement Party, led by Congressman Luis Soayer, on August 31st at the Great Hall of the People. The delegation arrived in Beijing on August 29th at the invitation of the Communist Party of China.

▲ State Councilor Chen Xitong granted an interview to the Chairman of the Editorial Board of *Le Figaro*, Perefitt, on August 31st in Beijing, answering the questions he raised.

▲ The second session of the China-Democratic Yemen Joint Economic, Technical, and Trade Committee concluded in Beijing on August 30th. The session began on August 23rd in Beijing. At the conclusion of the meeting, Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin met with the delegation from the Democratic Yemen government, led by Minister of Housing Construction Muhammad Awad Dubba. Vice Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Lü Xuejin and Dubba signed four documents, including an economic and technical cooperation agreement, on behalf of their respective governments.

### 外事简讯

1989-08-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务院副总理吴学谦31日在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了亚非法律协商委员会秘书长恩赞加。恩赞加应外交部邀请，于27日抵京，前来了解中国平暴以后北京的局势，并同中方商谈将于1990年3月12日至17日在北京举行亚非法协第29届年会的具体事宜。
    ▲人大常委会副委员长廖汉生31日在人民大会堂会见并宴请了由众议员路易斯·索耶尔率领的巴西民主运动党代表团。代表团应中国共产党邀请于29日抵京。
    ▲国务委员陈希同31日在北京接受了《费加罗报》编辑委员会主席佩雷菲特的采访，回答了他提出的问题。
    ▲中国和民主也门政府经济技术和贸易合作混合委员会第二届会议30日在北京结束。本届会议于8月23日在北京开始。会议结束时，对外经济贸易部部长郑拓彬会见了前来参加本届会议的以建设住房部长穆罕默德·阿瓦德·杜巴为团长的民主也门政府经济贸易代表团。对外经济贸易部副部长吕学俭和杜巴分别代表本国政府签署了经济技术合作协定等4个文件。　（据新华社）
