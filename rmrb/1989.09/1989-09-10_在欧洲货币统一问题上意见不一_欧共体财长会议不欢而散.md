Title: Divergent Views on European Monetary Unification Lead to Unsuccessful Meeting of EC Finance Ministers
Author:
Date: 1989-09-10
Edition: 3

The European Community's finance ministers and central bank governors held a joint meeting in Antibes, France, today to discuss European monetary unification. The meeting ended without significant progress and in an unharmonious manner.

British Chancellor of the Exchequer Nigel Lawson expressed opposition to the long-term plan proposed by the EC Commission President Jacques Delors, which involves first establishing a European Central Bank and ultimately issuing a unified European currency.

Lawson presented an alternative proposal during the meeting, advocating for enhanced consultation among the economic and financial institutions of the 12 EC member states, with the primary goal of stabilizing prices. However, this proposal received a lukewarm response from the attendees.

The ministerial-level meeting began on September 8. The ministers engaged in heated debates on the issue of monetary unification.

### 在欧洲货币统一问题上意见不一　欧共体财长会议不欢而散

1989-09-10
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　  在欧洲货币统一问题上意见不一
　　欧共体财长会议不欢而散
    新华社巴黎9月9日电　讨论欧洲货币统一问题的欧洲共同体财政部长和中央银行总裁联席会议今天晚上在法国昂蒂布不欢而散，没有取得重大进展。
    英国财政大臣劳森在今天的会议上表示，英国反对共同体委员会主席德洛尔提出的长远计划，即首先建立欧洲中央银行，最终实现发行欧洲统一货币的计划。
    劳森在今天提出的一项反建议中，主张应该首先加强共同体12国经济和财政机构之间的协商，把稳定物价作为主要目标。英国的反建议在与会者中反应冷淡。
    这次部长级会议是9月8日开幕的。部长们对货币统一问题进行了激烈辩论。
