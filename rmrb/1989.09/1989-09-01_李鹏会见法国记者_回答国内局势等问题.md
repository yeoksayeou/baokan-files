Title: Li Peng Meets with French Journalists and Answers Questions on Domestic Situation
Author:
Date: 1989-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 1st.

Premier Li Peng met with the Chairman of the Editorial Board of the French newspaper "Le Figaro," Mr. Pereffit, this afternoon in the Zhongnanhai's Ziguangge. Li Peng provided detailed responses to questions regarding China's current domestic situation, internal and external policies, and Sino-French relations.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhu Qizhen, was present during the meeting.

### 李鹏会见法国记者  回答国内局势等问题

1989-09-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见法国记者
    回答国内局势等问题
    新华社北京九月一日电　国务院总理李鹏今天下午在中南海紫光阁会见法国《费加罗报》编辑委员会主席佩雷菲特，详细地回答了他提出的关于当前中国国内局势、中国内外政策和中法关系等方面的问题。
    会见时外交部副部长朱启祯在座。
