Title: "Bai Gong" Calligraphy and Painting Exhibition in Beijing
Author: Lai Renqiong
Date: 1989-09-24
Edition: 2

The largest-scale exhibition since the founding of the nation, the "Welcome National Day National Hundred Palaces Calligraphy and Painting Teaching Achievements Joint Exhibition," opened this morning at the China Children and Youth Activity Center in Beijing. A total of 171 youth palaces and children's activity centers from 28 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions across the country selected more than 700 children's calligraphy and painting works for display.

A selection committee composed of famous calligraphers and painters conducted a comprehensive evaluation of the more than 700 works. A total of 40 first prizes, 60 second prizes, and 90 third prizes were awarded.

At the opening ceremony, Qi Gong, Chairman of the Chinese Calligraphers Association, and Liu Kaituo, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, cut the ribbon.

### “百宫”书画在京联展
赖仁琼
1989-09-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    “百宫”书画在京联展
    本报北京9月23日讯　记者赖仁琼报道：建国以来规模最大、首次以少年宫为参展单位的“迎国庆全国百宫书画教学成果联展”今天上午在北京中国儿童少年活动中心开幕。全国有28个省、市、自治区的171个少年宫、儿童活动中心选送的700余件儿童书画参展。
    由著名书画家组成的评选委员会对700余幅作品进行了全面评比。共评出一等奖40名，二等奖60名，三等奖90名。
    中国书法家协会主席启功、中国美术家协会副主席刘开渠在开幕式上剪彩。
