Title: Ministry of Water Resources Honors Advanced Water Management Counties
Author: Zhang Minhua
Date: 1989-09-12
Edition: 2

The Ministry of Water Resources has awarded the title of "National Advanced Water Management County" to 101 counties, including Pan Mountain in Liaoning, Huan Tai in Shandong, and Yuan Yang in Henan. This announcement was made by Yang Zhenhuai, the Minister of Water Resources, at a conference on "Northern Rural Water Management Construction" held recently in Liaoning.

These 101 counties have made outstanding achievements in water management, improving production conditions, and promoting agricultural increases, especially over the past three years and particularly since last winter.

### 水利部表彰水利先进县
张民华
1989-09-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　水利部表彰水利先进县
    新华社沈阳9月12日电　（记者张民华）水利部部长杨振怀近日在辽宁举行的“北方农村水利建设现场会”上宣布：授予辽宁盘山、山东桓台、河南原阳等101个县“全国水利建设先进县”称号。
    这101个县近3年来特别是去冬以来，在兴修水利、改善生产条件、促进农业增产等方面做出了突出成绩。
