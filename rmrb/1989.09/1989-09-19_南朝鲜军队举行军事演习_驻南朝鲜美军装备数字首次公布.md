Title: South Korean Military Conducts Military Exercise; Equipment Numbers of U.S. Forces in South Korea Released for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1989-09-19
Edition: 3

South Korean Military Conducts Military Exercise

According to a report from the New China News Agency in Pyongyang on the 18th, the South Korean military conducted an offensive military exercise this afternoon near the demilitarized zone in the central part of South Korea.

In this military exercise, codenamed "89 Pioneer Annihilation Operation," the South Korean military deployed F-16 and F-4 fighter jets, as well as tanks, armored vehicles, and 155mm artillery.

South Korean President Roh Tae-woo and over 3,000 South Korean military and political figures attended the military exercise.

Equipment Numbers of U.S. Forces in South Korea Released for the First Time

According to a report from the New China News Agency in Pyongyang on the 18th, Seoul: On the 17th, the South Korean Ministry of Defense, in a report submitted to the National Assembly, for the first time disclosed the number of U.S. troops stationed in South Korea and their equipment.

According to the figures provided in this report, the total number of U.S. troops currently stationed in South Korea is over 43,000, including 26,000 in the Army, 10,000 in the Air Force, 470 in the Navy, and 5,400 Department of Defense officials.

Equipment situation: over 100 fighter jets, including three squadrons of F-4 Phantom II fighter jets, two squadrons of F-16 fighter jets, and one squadron of F-10 fighter jets. Over 100 armored vehicles. Additionally, there is one squadron of Lance missiles.

### 南朝鲜军队举行军事演习　驻南朝鲜美军装备数字首次公布

1989-09-19
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　南朝鲜军队举行军事演习
　　驻南朝鲜美军装备数字首次公布
    据新华社平壤9月18日电　据汉城广播电台今晚报道，南朝鲜军队今天下午在朝鲜军事分界线中部南朝鲜一侧的附近地区进行攻击性的军事演习。
    在这次代号为“89先锋歼灭作战”的军事演习中，南朝鲜军队出动了“F—16”、“F—4”等战斗机和坦克、装甲车、155毫米火炮等武器装备。
    南朝鲜总统卢泰愚和南朝鲜军政要员3000多人出席观看了这次军事演习。
    据新华社平壤9月18日电　汉城消息：南朝鲜国防部17日在向国会提供的一份材料中，首次公布了驻扎在南朝鲜的美军及其武器装备数字。
    据这份材料提供的数字表明，现驻南朝鲜美军总数为43000多名，其中陆军26000名，空军10000名，海军470名，美国防部官员5400名。
    武器装备情况：战斗机100多架，其中包括3个大队的F—4鬼怪式战斗机，2个大队的F—16战斗机，1个大队的F—10战斗机。战车100多辆。另外还有一个大队的长矛式导弹。
