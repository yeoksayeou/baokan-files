Title: New Type of Contraceptive Ring Effective
Author: Jin Ke
Date: 1989-09-03
Edition: 7

A new type of contraceptive device is being tested in 10 European countries, and it may eventually replace birth control pills. This device is a flexible plastic ring that releases small amounts of the two hormones typically found in oral contraceptives.

Women insert this ring into the vagina and remove it for one week during their menstrual period.

The current trials aim to assess whether this vaginal ring will be accepted. The Dutch manufacturer, Organon, reports that over 90% of women who have used the ring prefer it over other contraceptive methods.

One obvious advantage of the vaginal ring is that women do not need to remember to take a pill every day. The contraceptive ring is also safer than pills. Secondly, oral contraceptives absorb hormones through the intestines, resulting in significant fluctuations in hormone levels in the blood. In contrast, the body absorbs hormones from the contraceptive ring through the vaginal wall at a steady rate. A stable supply means a smaller dose of hormones is needed. Thirdly, and perhaps most importantly, the ring has limited impact on the liver. Hormones absorbed through the intestines go directly to the liver, while the hormones released by the contraceptive ring bypass the liver and enter the bloodstream directly.

Researchers believe that high levels of sex hormones entering the liver are the primary cause of the main side effects of birth control pills. Estrogens can negatively affect blood clotting, and sex hormones can cause unfavorable changes in lipid levels in the blood, increasing the risk of heart disease.

So far, approximately 400 women from 10 European countries have participated in this comprehensive trial. None of the participants have become pregnant.

### 新型避孕环效果好
金克
1989-09-03
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    新型避孕环效果好
    一种新型避孕用具正在欧洲10国进行试验，它可能最终取代避孕丸。这种装置是一个柔韧的塑料小环，这种环施放少量口服避孕丸里通常含有的两种激素。
    妇女把这种环放入阴道，当月经期时，把环取下一周。
    目前的试验旨在估计这种阴道环是否能被接受。荷兰制造厂商奥根农公司说，用过这种环的90％以上的妇女说，比起其他避孕方法，她们更喜欢这种环。
    阴道环的一个明显优点是，妇女不需要记住每天服药片。这种避孕环比药丸更安全。其二，服用药丸是通过肠道吸收激素，所以血液中的激素含量有很大差别。而机体通过阴道壁从避孕环吸收激素的速度却是稳定的。供给量稳定意味着激素的剂量可以小一些。第三，也许是最重要的优点是这种环对肝脏的影响有限。通过肠道吸收的激素直接进入肝脏，而避孕环施放的激素绕过肝脏直接进入血流。
    研究人员认为，进入肝脏的高含量性激素，是引起避孕丸的主要副作用的原因。雌激素会对血液凝结的方式产生不利的影响；而性激素类可能使血液中类脂的含量发生不利的变化，从而增加得心脏病的危险。
    迄今为止，有10个欧洲国家的约400名妇女参加了这个综合中心的试验。参加试验的妇女迄今没有一个怀孕。
                ·金克·
