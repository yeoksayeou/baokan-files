Title: Algerian President Dismisses Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-09-10
Edition: 3

The Algerian presidential palace issued a statement on September 9 announcing that Algerian President Chadli Bendjedid had dismissed Prime Minister Kasdi Merbah. President Chadli appointed Mouloud Hamrouche, the secretary of the presidential palace, as the new prime minister.

### 阿尔及利亚总统罢免总理

1989-09-10
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　阿尔及利亚总统罢免总理
    阿尔及利亚总统府9日发表一项公报宣布，阿尔及利亚总统沙德利解除了卡斯迪·梅尔巴的总理职务，并任命总统府秘书长穆卢德·阿姆鲁什为新总理。
