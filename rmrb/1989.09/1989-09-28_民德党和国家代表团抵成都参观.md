Title: Delegation from the Socialist Unity Party and the State of the GDR Arrives in Chengdu for a Visit
Author: He Can Yong
Date: 1989-09-28
Edition: 2

A delegation from the Socialist Unity Party and the State of the German Democratic Republic, led by Egon Krenz, a member of the Politburo and the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party, and Vice Chairman of the State Council of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Chengdu, Sichuan today for a visit. They were warmly welcomed by party and government leaders from Sichuan Province and Chengdu City.

In the evening, Yang Ruidai, a member of the Politburo of the Communist Party of China and Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee, had a cordial meeting with Egon Krenz and hosted a banquet in honor of the German Democratic Republic guests.

### 民德党和国家代表团抵成都参观
贺灿永
1989-09-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    民德党和国家代表团抵成都参观
    据新华社成都9月27日电　（记者贺灿永）由德国统一社会党中央政治局委员、中央书记、民主德国国务委员会副主席埃贡·克伦茨率领的德意志民主共和国党和国家代表团，今日抵达四川成都参观访问，受到四川省、成都市党政负责人的热忱欢迎。晚上，中共中央政治局委员、四川省委书记杨汝岱同埃贡·克伦茨进行了亲切会面，并设宴款待民主德国客人。
