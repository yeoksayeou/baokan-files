Title: The "Gold Medal Drilling Team"
Author: Xú Yīngjié
Date: 1989-09-26
Edition: 2

Shěn Guàn (Korean ethnicity, second from the right) is the twelfth captain of the 2012 drilling team of the Daqing Oilfield, which was previously led by the "Iron Man" Wang Jinxi. He led the entire team of workers to achieve the record of completing 6 wells in one month using imported drilling rigs, and has consecutively won the "Gold Medal Drilling Team" honor from the Petroleum and Natural Gas General Company for three years.

	Newspaper reporter Xú Yīngjié

### “金牌钻井队”（图片）
徐英杰
1989-09-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    “金牌钻井队”
    申冠（朝鲜族，右一）是“铁人”王进喜生前领导过的大庆油田2012钻井队的第十二任队长。他带领全队工人创出了用进口钻机1个月钻完6口井的最高纪录，连续三年获石油天然气总公司“金牌钻井队”光荣称号。
                            新华社记者　徐英杰摄
