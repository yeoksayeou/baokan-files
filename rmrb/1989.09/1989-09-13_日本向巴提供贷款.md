Title: Japan Provides Loan to Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1989-09-13
Edition: 3

Japan and Pakistan signed a loan agreement in Islamabad on September 12. According to the agreement, Japan will provide a loan of $134 million to Pakistan to help the country reform its financial sector. The reform plan includes allocating loans based on market supply and demand information, improving the efficiency of the banking system, and establishing a more effective public debt issuance mechanism. The annual interest rate for this loan is 2.5%, with a repayment period of 25 years. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 日本向巴提供贷款

1989-09-13
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    日本向巴提供贷款
    日本和巴基斯坦12日在伊斯兰堡签订了一项贷款协议。根据这项协议，日本将向巴基斯坦提供1.34亿美元的贷款，帮助这个国家对金融部门进行改革。这项改革计划包括：根据市场供求信息分配贷款；提高银行系统的工作效率和建立更加有效的发行公债体制等。这笔贷款的年利率为2.5％，偿还期为25年。　（据新华社）
