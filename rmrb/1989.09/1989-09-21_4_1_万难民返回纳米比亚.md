Title: 41,000 Refugees Return to Namibia
Author:
Date: 1989-09-21
Edition: 3

The United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees announced on the 19th in Windhoek that the organized repatriation of Namibian refugees living abroad has been completed, with 41,090 refugees safely returning home. This repatriation effort began on June 12 of this year.

Among the 41,090 refugees who returned home, the majority came from Angola and Zambia. The United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees also assisted Namibian refugees living in 42 other countries to return to Namibia. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 4.1万难民返回纳米比亚

1989-09-21
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    4.1万难民返回纳米比亚
    联合国难民事务高级专员办事处19日在温得和克发表声明宣布，有组织地遣返流亡国外的纳米比亚人的工作已经结束，41090名难民安全返回家园。这一遣返工作是今年6月12日开始的。
    在41090名返回家园的难民中，大多数是来自安哥拉和赞比亚。联合国难民事务高级专员办事处还帮助生活在其他42个国家的纳米比亚难民返回纳米比亚。　（据新华社）
