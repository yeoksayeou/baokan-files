Title: One Minute to Launch
Author: Wang Jinjun
Date: 1989-09-28
Edition: 8

In the vast river of time,
There are countless
Minutes of leisure, tranquility, and relaxation.
Only the minutes on this magical land
Hide an eternal excitement.

One minute,
Condenses countless vibrant seasons of spring, summer, autumn, and winter.
Condenses five thousand years of humiliation and glory.
Condenses a rocket scientist's lifelong ambition buried in the desert.
Condenses the voices of justice amidst the tumultuous clouds.
Condenses the heat of the sun and the coldness of the moon.
Condenses all the wheels of reform and opening-up, golden and silver, racing forward.

One minute,
The blood surges a million times,
The hardworking bees produce honey a million times,
The buds of osmanthus blossom a million times,
The radiant light of life shines a million times,
The melodious cooing of doves echoes a million times,
The boundless waves of information surge a million times…

The missile god,
In 60 seconds,
Receives the motherland's final check-up:
      “Heart” normal, “nerves” normal,
      “Temperature” normal, “circulation” normal.

The invincible rocket soldiers
Hand over a completely qualified health report to the motherland.

The great news,
In an instant,
Will descend upon
The solemn and sacred land shaped like a rooster crowing loudly.

One minute to launch.
For this one minute,
History has traversed a journey so arduous and long.

### 发射进入一分钟准备
王晋军
1989-09-28
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    发射进入一分钟准备
    王晋军
    在时间的浩浩长河里
    荡漾着多少
    悠闲　平静　松弛的一分钟
    唯有这片神奇土地上的一分钟
    隐藏了永恒不泯的激动
    一分钟
    浓缩了数十个峥嵘如织的春夏秋冬
    浓缩了上下五千年的屈辱与光荣
    浓缩了一位火箭专家掩埋在戈壁下的毕生宏愿
    浓缩了属于正义的坼裂风云的呼声
    浓缩了太阳的炽热，月亮的冷峻
    浓缩了所有奔驰的改革之金轮和开放之银轮
    一分钟啊
    向上的血液千万次翻腾
    辛劳的蜜蜂千万次酿造
    孕蕾后的栀子花千万次开放
    斑斓绚丽的生活之光千万次闪耀
    委婉悠扬的鸽哨千万次鸣奏
    无边无涯的信息束卷起千万次浪潮……
    导弹神在60秒内
    接受母亲最后的体检
      “心脏”正常，“神经”正常
      “体温”正常，“血液循环”正常
    万无一失的火箭兵
    向祖国递上一张完全合格的体检表
    巨大的喜讯　刹那间
    就会降临在
    放声啼喔的雄鸡形的庄严神圣的国土之上
    发射进入一分钟准备
    为了这一分钟
    历史走过了多么艰难漫长的征程
