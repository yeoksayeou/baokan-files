Title: Today's Chengdu
Author: Zhang Xincheng
Date: 1989-09-12
Page: 1

News Photography Contest

Chengdu, the capital of Sichuan Province, has accelerated its urban construction through multiple channels and levels of fundraising, significantly improving its cityscape. Over the past decade, the city has built 13 million square meters of new housing, increasing the per capita living space to 7.3 square meters. The newly constructed Shu Du Avenue and the First Ring Road have greatly improved urban traffic. The image shows a glimpse of the newly built Qingyang Residential Area.
