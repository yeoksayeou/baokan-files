Title: Leaders of Bahrain and Tunisia Meet with Chinese Foreign Minister Qian
Author: Yan Shiyuan Liu Jian
Date: 1989-09-27
Edition: 3

Leaders of Bahrain and Tunisia Meet with Chinese Foreign Minister Qian

According to Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, September 25 (Reporters Yan Shiyuan, Liu Jian) Palestinian President Yasser Arafat met with visiting Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen this evening and had a friendly and cordial conversation.

Foreign Minister Qian conveyed greetings from Chinese leaders to Arafat and handed over a personal letter from Chairman Yang Shangkun. In the letter, Yang expressed his welcome and anticipation for Arafat's visit to China. Arafat thanked the Chinese leaders for their greetings and asked Foreign Minister Qian to convey his greetings to the Chinese leaders.

Arafat briefed Qian on the latest developments in the Middle East situation. Foreign Minister Qian reaffirmed China's consistent stance on advocating for an international peace conference on the Middle East, attended by the five permanent members of the UN Security Council and relevant parties.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, September 25 (Reporters Yan Shiyuan, Liu Jian) Tunisian Prime Minister Hedi Baccouche met with Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen on September 25 in Tunis.

During the meeting, Baccouche expressed satisfaction with the friendly and cooperative relations between Tunisia and China, believing that such relations are very solid. Qian praised Tunisia's important role in the Arab world and the Arab Maghreb region.

### 巴突两国领导人分别会见钱外长
阎世缘  刘建
1989-09-27
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    巴突两国领导人分别会见钱外长
    据新华社突尼斯9月25日电  （记者阎世缘、刘建）巴勒斯坦国总统阿拉法特今晚在这里接见了来访的中国外长钱其琛，并同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    钱外长向阿拉法特总统转达了中国领导人对他的问候，并转交了杨尚昆主席写给他的亲笔信。杨主席在信中表示欢迎和期待阿拉法特访华。阿拉法特感谢中国领导人的问候，并请钱外长转达他对中国领导人的问候。
    阿拉法特向钱其琛介绍了中东局势的最新发展。钱外长重申了中国政府一贯主张召开有联合国安理会五个常任理事国和有关各方参加的中东和平国际会议的立场。
    据新华社突尼斯9月25日电　（记者阎世缘、刘建）突尼斯总理巴库什25日在突尼斯接见了中国外长钱其琛。
    接见时，巴库什对突中两国间的友好合作关系表示满意，认为这种关系是十分牢固的。钱其琛赞扬突尼斯在阿拉伯世界和阿拉伯马格里布地区发挥的重要作用。
