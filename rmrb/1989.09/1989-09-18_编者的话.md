Title: Editor's Note
Author:
Date: 1989-09-18
Page: 7

On October 1, 1949, the People's Republic of China was established. This marked the beginning of a new era in Chinese history and opened a new chapter in Chinese diplomacy.

Over the past 40 years, under the personal leadership of comrades Mao Zedong, Zhou Enlai, and Deng Xiaoping, our country has achieved great successes in diplomacy and continues to play a significant role and exert a considerable influence on the world stage.

In these 40 years, our country has withstood pressure from superpowers, safeguarded national independence, sovereignty, and dignity; advocated and upheld the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, established increasingly close and broad friendly cooperative relations with the vast majority of countries in the world, engaged in economic, trade, and cultural exchanges, and set a brilliant example for building new types of international relations; adhered to the principles of internationalism, contributed to the cause of the world's people in opposing colonialism, imperialism, and hegemonism, striving for and safeguarding national independence, defending world peace, accelerating economic development, and promoting human progress, and created a favorable international environment for the modernization of China's socialist construction.

### 编者的话

1989-09-18
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

　　编者的话
    1949年10月1日，中华人民共和国宣告成立。中国历史进入了新纪元，中国外交也揭开了新的篇章。
    40年中，在毛泽东、周恩来和邓小平等同志的亲自领导下，我国外交取得了伟大成就，在世界舞台上发挥了和继续发挥着重要作用和影响。
    40年来，我国顶住了超级大国的压力，维护了国家的独立、主权和尊严；我国倡导和坚持和平共处五项原则，同世界上绝大多数国家建立了日益密切、日益广泛的友好合作关系，开展了经济、贸易和文化往来，为建立新型的国际关系树立了光辉的范例；我国遵循国际主义原则，对世界人民反对殖民主义、帝国主义和霸权主义，争取和维护民族独立，捍卫世界和平，加速经济发展，促进人类进步的事业，作出了自己的贡献，并为我国的社会主义现代化建设赢得了有利的国际环境。
    值庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年之际，本报从今天起，按时间顺序，以类似大事记的形式，回顾我国40年来的重大外交活动和取得的伟大成就。
