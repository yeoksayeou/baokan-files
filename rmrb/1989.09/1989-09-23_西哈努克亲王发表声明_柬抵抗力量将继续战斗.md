Title: Prince Norodom Sihanouk Issues Statement: Cambodian Resistance Forces Will Continue to Fight
Author:
Date: 1989-09-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 21st.

	Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of Democratic Kampuchea, issued a statement in Beijing today saying that as long as there are Vietnamese troops on Cambodian soil, the Cambodian resistance forces will continue to fight.

	Prince Sihanouk said that Vietnam claims it will withdraw all its troops from Cambodia between September 21 and September 26. "For this reason, I declare that in reality, after September 27, tens of thousands of armed Vietnamese, disguised as 'Cambodian' officers, soldiers, and militia, will still remain in the military and militia of the Hun Sen-Heng Samrin regime (Phnom Penh regime). In addition, 1 million Vietnamese colonists will still be on our land, continuing to plunder our natural resources."

	He said, "The existence of this fact will force the Cambodian National Resistance Movement, which I lead, to continue the sacred struggle for the liberation of the Cambodian nation until the Cambodian National Resistance Movement believes that there are no more Vietnamese colonialists on Cambodian soil."

	He said, "The Cambodian National Resistance Movement, which I lead, and Democratic Kampuchea continue to demand the dispatch of a United Nations international monitoring organization and United Nations peacekeeping forces to Cambodia to supervise and confirm the true withdrawal of Vietnamese troops from Cambodia, whether they are genuine or disguised as Phnom Penh soldiers. All illegal immigrants should also leave Cambodia and be repatriated under United Nations supervision."

	Sihanouk said, "The Cambodian National Resistance Movement, which I lead, and Democratic Kampuchea declare that the treaties signed by the so-called 'State of Cambodia' with foreign countries, all laws and regulations enacted domestically, and all government, administrative, and legislative documents of the Hun Sen regime are illegal 'works' of the Hun Sen regime."

	Prince Sihanouk pointed out, "Only Sihanouk's five-point plan can reasonably, justly, and durably solve the 'Cambodian problem.' The Cambodian problem is not a problem 'among Cambodians.' It is a problem between the aggressor Vietnam and the invaded Cambodia."

### 西哈努克亲王发表声明  柬抵抗力量将继续战斗

1989-09-23
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王发表声明
    柬抵抗力量将继续战斗
    据新华社北京9月21日电  民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王今天在北京发表声明说，只要柬埔寨领土上还有越南军队，柬埔寨抵抗力量就将继续战斗下去。
    西哈努克亲王说，越南宣称9月21日到9月26日将从柬埔寨撤出全部越南军队。“为此，我声明，实际上，在9月27日以后，仍将有数万名武装的、装扮成‘柬埔寨’军官、士兵和民兵的越南人留在洪森—韩桑林政权（金边政权）的军队和民兵中。此外，还有100万越南殖民者仍然留在我国的土地上，继续掠夺我国的自然资源。”
    他说：“这一事实的存在将迫使我所领导的柬埔寨民族抵抗运动继续进行神圣的柬埔寨民族解放斗争，直到柬埔寨民族抵抗运动认为在柬埔寨领土上再也没有越南殖民主义者的足迹为止。”
    他说：“我所领导的柬埔寨民族抵抗运动继续要求向柬埔寨派遣联合国国际监察机构和联合国维持和平部队以监督和确认越南军队全部从柬埔寨撤走其真实的，或是伪装成金边官兵的军队。所有非法移民也应离开柬埔寨并在联合国监督下被遣送回国。”
    西哈努克说：““我所领导的柬埔寨民族抵抗运动和民主柬埔寨声明，所谓的‘柬埔寨国’的金边政权与外国签定的条约，国内制定的一切法令、法律以及其政府的、行政的、立法的文件都是洪森政权的非法‘作品’。”
    西哈努克亲王指出：“只有西哈努克的5点计划可以使‘柬埔寨问题’合理地、公正地和持久地解决。柬埔寨问题决不是一个‘柬埔寨人之间’的问题。而是一个侵略者的越南和被侵略的柬埔寨之间的问题。”
