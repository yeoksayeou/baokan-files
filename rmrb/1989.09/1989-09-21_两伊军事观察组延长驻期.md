Title: UN Military Observer Group in Iran and Iraq Extends Stay
Author:
Date: 1989-09-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, September 21st.

The United Nations Under-Secretary-General, Martti Ahtisaari, announced on September 19th in Tehran that the United Nations Iran-Iraq Military Observer Group (UNIIMOG) will extend its stay for another six months. Ahtisaari made the announcement after concluding his visit to Baghdad and arriving in Tehran. He met with Iran's Minister of Defense and Logistics of the Armed Forces, Akbar Dourkan, in Tehran. According to reports, Ahtisaari attempted to convince Dourkan of Iraq's sincerity in pursuing peace.

### 两伊军事观察组延长驻期

1989-09-21
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    两伊军事观察组延长驻期
    联合国副秘书长古尔丁19日在德黑兰宣布，联合国驻两伊军事观察小组将延长驻期6个月。古尔丁是在结束对巴格达访问后来到德黑兰的。他在德黑兰会见了伊朗国防和武装部队后勤部长阿克巴尔·杜尔坎。据报道，他试图说服杜尔坎相信伊拉克的和平诚意。
