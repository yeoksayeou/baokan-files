Title: Myanmar Announces Implementation of Independent and Active Foreign Policy
Author:
Date: 1989-09-13
Edition: 3

    Myanmar announced
    the implementation of an independent and active foreign policy.

    Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, September 11 - The State Law and Order Restoration Council of Myanmar announced that since it took power on September 18 last year, it has been implementing an independent and active foreign policy.

    On September 9, Secretary of the State Law and Order Restoration Council, Khin Nyunt, held a special press conference. He explained that the essence of Myanmar's current foreign policy is: to maintain world peace and security, prevent world war, oppose imperialism and colonialism, and on the basis of equality, cooperation, mutual respect, non-interference in internal affairs, and the five principles of peaceful coexistence, to maintain friendly relations with all countries.

    Khin Nyunt also said that Myanmar opposes foreign countries establishing military bases on its territory.

### 缅甸宣布　执行独立积极外交政策

1989-09-13
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　缅甸宣布
　　执行独立积极外交政策
    新华社仰光9月11日电　缅甸国家治安建设委员会宣布，自从它去年9月18日执政开始，即执行独立和积极的外交政策。
    9月9日，缅甸国家治安建设委员会秘书钦纽举行了特别记者招待会，他解释说，缅甸今天的对外政策的实质是：维护世界和平与安全，防止世界战争，反帝、反殖，在平等、合作、互相尊重、不干涉别国内政、坚持和平共处五项原则的基础上和所有国家保持友好关系。
    钦纽还说，缅甸反对外国在其领土建立军事基地。
