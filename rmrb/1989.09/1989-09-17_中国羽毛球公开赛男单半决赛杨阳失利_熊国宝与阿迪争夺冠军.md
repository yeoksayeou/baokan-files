Title: Chinese Open Badminton Men's Singles Semifinal: Yang Yang Loses; Xiong Guobao to Face Adi for the Championship
Author: He Huifei Zhang Jiachang
Date: 1989-09-17
Edition: 3

Chinese star Yang Yang lost tonight in the men's singles semifinal of the 1989 China Open Badminton Championships held here, falling to Indonesia's Adi with scores of 15-17, 11-15, and thus missing out on the final. Another Chinese player, Xiong Guobao, defeated Indonesia's Supriyanto with scores of 6-15, 15-12, 15-6 to secure a spot in the final.

The final between Xiong Guobao and Adi will take place tomorrow evening.

On the 15th, the quarterfinals were held, determining the top four players in each event. China has four players in the women's singles semifinals, three pairs in the men's and women's doubles semifinals, two in the men's singles semifinals, and one pair in the mixed doubles semifinals. The gold, silver, and bronze medals in women's singles are already secured by Chinese players.

Chinese players have achieved good results in women's singles, women's doubles, and men's doubles events.

### 中国羽毛球公开赛男单半决赛杨阳失利　熊国宝与阿迪争夺冠军
何惠飞　张家昌
1989-09-17
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　中国羽毛球公开赛男单半决赛杨阳失利
　　熊国宝与阿迪争夺冠军
    新华社佛山9月16日电　（记者何惠飞、张家昌）中国名将杨阳今晚在这里进行的1989年中国羽毛球公开赛男子单打半决赛中，以15∶17、11∶15输给了印度尼西亚的阿迪，失去了参加决赛的资格。另一名中国选手熊国宝以6∶15、15∶12、15∶6战胜了印尼的苏普里扬托，获得决赛权。
    熊国宝和阿迪的决赛将于明晚进行。
    15日晚进行了四分之一决赛，决出了各项比赛的前四名。中国有4名选手进入了女单半决赛，进入男、女双打半决赛的各有3对，两人进入了男单半决赛，而进入混双半决赛的中国选手只有1对。女子单打的金、银、铜牌已属中国选手所有。
    中国选手在女单、女双、男双等项比赛中取得好成绩。
