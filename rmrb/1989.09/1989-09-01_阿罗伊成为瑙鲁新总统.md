Title: Aroi Becomes New President of Nauru
Author:
Date: 1989-09-01
Edition: 3

Nauru, a Pacific island nation, saw its President, Hammer DeRoburt, step down following a vote of no confidence in the parliament.

Australian and New Zealand newspapers reported that Kenneth Aroi, who was previously an advisor to DeRoburt, criticized the government's poor financial management and moved a vote of no confidence in the parliament. On August 17, Aroi defeated DeRoburt by a vote of 9 to 6, becoming the new president of Nauru. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 阿罗伊成为瑙鲁新总统

1989-09-01
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    阿罗伊成为瑙鲁新总统
    太平洋岛国瑙鲁总统哈默·德罗伯特因在议会的一次不信任案投票中失败而下台。
    澳新报纸报道，原为德罗伯特顾问的肯纳斯·阿罗伊指责政府财政状况不佳，在议会中对政府提出不信任案。8月17日投票表决，阿罗伊以9比6战胜德罗伯特，成为瑙鲁新总统。　（据新华社）
