Title: Philippine Foreign Minister Reiterates Recognition of One China
Author:
Date: 1989-09-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, September 16th.

Philippine Foreign Minister Raul Manglapus stated at a press conference held in Manila today that the Philippine government acknowledges only one China, which is the People's Republic of China.

Manglapus made these remarks in response to a reporter's question about a recent visit to Manila by a Taiwanese economic delegation, which proposed expanding trade and investment in the Philippines and developing political relations.

Manglapus said, "We only have economic ties with Taiwan, and this relationship will not change our political relations with the People's Republic of China."

### 菲律宾外长重申只承认一个中国

1989-09-18
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　菲律宾外长重申只承认一个中国
    新华社马尼拉9月16日电　菲律宾外交部长劳尔·曼格拉普斯今天在此间举行的记者招待会上说，菲律宾政府承认只有一个中国，那就是中华人民共和国。
    曼格拉普斯外长是针对有记者问到最近访问马尼拉的台湾经济代表团提出扩大对菲律宾的贸易和投资，发展政治关系时说这番话的。
    曼格拉普斯说，我们同台湾只有经济联系，这种关系不会导致同中华人民共和国政治关系的改变。
