Title: The Television Series "In the Annals of the Republic" Premieres
Author: He Huangbiao
Date: 1989-09-24
Edition: 2

The 12-episode television series "In the Annals of the Republic," jointly produced by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the China Film Press, and the International Department of China Central Television, held its premiere today.

General Secretary of the Communist Party of China Jiang Zemin gave instructions on July 26, saying, "I hope you will work hard to make the film a success." Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Xiannian inscribed the title.

This television series, with its abundant materials, sincere emotions, and documentary style, propagandizes the great achievements made since the Party's Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee in reform and opening up and in wholeheartedly relying on the working class. It reviews and re-enacts the advanced deeds of labor models from all trades nationwide, recipients of the "May 1st Labor Medal," and individuals and enterprises that have won other honorary titles.

### 电视片《在共和国的史册上》面世
何黄彪
1989-09-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    电视片《在共和国的史册上》面世
    本报北京9月23日讯　记者何黄彪报道：由中华全国总工会、中国电影出版社、中央电视台对外部联合摄制的12集电视系列专题片《在共和国的史册上》，今日在这里举行了首映式。
    中共中央总书记江泽民7月26日曾为此片作了“希望你们努力把片子拍好”的批示，全国政协主席李先念为片名题词。
    这部电视系列片以翔实的资料、真挚的感情、纪实的形式宣传了党的十一届三中全会以来改革开放和全心全意依靠工人阶级所取得的巨大成就；回顾与再现了全国各行业劳动模范、
  “五一”劳动奖章获得者及赢得其它荣誉称号的个人和企事业单位的先进事迹。
