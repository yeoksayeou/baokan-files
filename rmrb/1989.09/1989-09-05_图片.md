Title: The Wind Comes from Taihang
Author: Lü Zuitao
Date: 1989-09-05
Page: 8

Recently, the Beijing Military Region's War Friendship Song and Dance Troupe created and performed the song and dance drama "The Wind Comes from Taihang," which praises the military and civilians of Taihang Mountain during the Anti-Japanese War.
