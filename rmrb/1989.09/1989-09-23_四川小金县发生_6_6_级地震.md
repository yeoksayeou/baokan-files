Title: A 6.6 Magnitude Earthquake Hits Sichuan's Xiajin County
Author:
Date: 1989-09-23
Edition: 2

According to measurements by China's seismic network, at 10:25 AM Beijing time on September 22, a 6.6 magnitude earthquake struck the northern part of Xiajin County, Sichuan Province (31.5 degrees north latitude, 102.6 degrees east longitude).

Preliminary reports indicate that the town of Lianghekou in Xiajin County suffered the most severe damage, with houses collapsing, rocks falling, and roads cracking. The Xiajin Phosphorus Mine also sustained damage. It is known that nearly 20 people were injured. The earthquake was strongly felt in Xiajin, Malkang, and Jinchuan.

### 四川小金县发生6.6级地震

1989-09-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    四川小金县发生6.6级地震
    新华社北京9月22日电　据我国地震台网测定，9月22日北京时间10时25分，在四川省小金县北部（北纬31.5度，东经102.6度）发生6.6级地震。
    据初步了解，小金县两河口乡破坏最重，房屋倒塌，山石滚落，公路出现裂缝，小金磷矿也有破坏。目前已了解到近20人受伤。小金、马尔康、金川等地强烈有感。
