Title: Feisal Discusses Draft of Lebanese National Reconciliation Document
Author:
Date: 1989-09-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 19th.

Riyadh: Saudi Arabian Foreign Minister Prince Faisal said yesterday that the seven-point peace proposal for Lebanon is only the "first step" towards achieving comprehensive reconciliation in the war-torn country.

Prince Faisal said that the next phase should involve political reforms. He also introduced the content of the "Draft of the National Reconciliation Document" submitted to Lebanese parliamentarians.

According to the Saudi Press Agency, the document includes four main points: conducting political reforms; restoring full sovereignty of the Lebanese people over their territory; liberating southern Lebanon from Israeli occupation; and the relationship between Lebanon and Syria.

Regarding the second point, the document stipulates that Syrian troops will assist Lebanon's legal military in establishing national authority within a maximum of two years, electing a president, forming a national unity government, and confirming political reforms through constitutional means.

The document states that upon the expiration of the deadline, the Syrian government will redeploy its troops in the Bekaa Valley and other areas according to the decision of the Lebanese national unity government. The agreement will also specify the number of Syrian troops stationed in the area and the duration of their stay.

This document follows the seven-point peace proposal presented by the foreign ministers of Saudi Arabia, Morocco, and Algeria on September 16 in Jeddah.

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, September 17th.

The French Foreign Ministry issued a statement on September 17 expressing support for the seven-point peace plan proposed by the Arab League's three-party committee on Lebanon on September 16 to resolve the Lebanese crisis.

Xinhua News Agency, London, September 18th.

A spokesman for the British Foreign Office said today that Britain welcomes the statement issued by the Arab League's three-party committee on September 16 in Jeddah regarding the Lebanese issue. He said that the seven-point peace plan proposed in the statement provides an opportunity to end the bloodshed in Lebanon.

### 费萨尔谈黎民族谅解文件草案　法英支持阿盟7点和平计划

1989-09-20
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　费萨尔谈黎民族谅解文件草案
　　法英支持阿盟7点和平计划
    据新华社北京9月19日电　利雅得消息：沙特阿拉伯外交大臣费萨尔亲王昨天说，关于黎巴嫩问题的7点和平建议仅仅是使这个战乱国家实现全面和解的“第一步”。
    费萨尔亲王说，下一阶段应该进行政治改革。他还介绍了向黎巴嫩议员们提交的“民族谅解文件草案”的内容。
    据沙特通讯社报道，这个文件包括4方面内容：进行政治改革；恢复黎巴嫩人对自己领土的全部主权；把南黎巴嫩从以色列占领下解放出来；黎巴嫩和叙利亚的关系问题。
    在谈到第二方面时，文件规定，叙利亚军队将帮助黎巴嫩的合法军队在最多2年内建立起国家权威，选举总统、组成民族团结政府并确认以宪法手段进行政治改革。
    文件说，在限期满后，叙利亚政府将根据黎民族联合政府的决定“重新把军队部署在贝卡谷地”等地。协议还将规定驻在该地叙军的人数和驻扎时间。
    这一文件是继16日沙特阿拉伯、摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚3国外长16日在吉达提出的7点和平建议之后又一重要建议。
    据新华社巴黎9月17日电　法国外交部17日发表一份公报，表示支持阿拉伯联盟黎巴嫩问题三方委员会16日提出的解决黎巴嫩危机的七点和平计划。
    据新华社伦敦9月18日电　英国外交部发言人今天指出，英国欢迎阿盟三方委员会9月16日在吉达发表的关于黎巴嫩问题的声明，他说，这项声明中所提出的7点和平计划提供了结束黎巴嫩流血的机会。
