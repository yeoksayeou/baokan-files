Title: The Commune Erects a Ladder to Heaven
Author: Lìn Chángbīn
Date: 1958-11-15
Page: 8

　　Great rivers and small streams flow into the sea,
　　People's communes bloom everywhere:
　　High-yield satellites fill the sky,
　　Grain piles up into ten thousand mountains,
　　Peasants see themselves as workers,
　　Blast furnaces smelt iron and steel;
　　Women untie their foot-binding ropes,
　　Leaving the kitchen to work,
　　Women are strong in everything they do,
　　Embroidering flowers and sewing clothes,
　　Tender buds, blooming flowers,
　　Laughing happily in the kindergarten;
　　Cockscomb flowers, old vegetable reds,
　　Endless joy in the houses of happiness;
　　Sweeter than saccharin,
　　A sweet life enters the heart!
　　The commune erects a ladder to heaven,
　　Heaven is communism!

### 公社搭起上天梯
赁常彬
1958-11-15
第8版()
专栏：

    公社搭起上天梯
    赁常彬
    大川小川归大海，
    人民公社百花开：
    高产卫星飞满天，
    粮食堆成万座山，
    庄稼汉把工人当，
    高炉炼铁又炼钢；
    妇女解开绊脚索，
    走出厨房去干活，
    妇女干活样样强，
    绣花绣朵缝衣裳，
    嫩芽芽，朵朵花，
    幼儿园里笑哈哈；
    鸡冠花，老菜红，
    幸福院中乐无穷；
    要甜甜不过糖精，
    幸福生活甜进心！
    公社搭起上天梯，
    天堂就是共产主义！
