Title: Tianjin People Welcome Korean Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-11-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 22nd: The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, passed through Tianjin at 8:45 this morning en route to Beijing by special train.

Also on the train passing through Tianjin were the Korean military delegation headed by General Kim Kwang-hyup and Lieutenant General Heo Bong-hak.

The distinguished Korean guests were warmly welcomed by the people of Tianjin. As the special train arrived at the station, the loudspeakers on the platform played "The East is Red" and "Song of General Kim Il-sung." Premier Kim Il-sung and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Yan Dakai, Secretary of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Ma Li, Vice Governor of Hebei Province, Wan Xiaotang, First Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Li Gengtao, Mayor of Tianjin, Wang Daobang, Commander of the Hebei Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and other leading figures from the Party, government, and military, met with responsible persons from all walks of life and the masses who had come to welcome them, and accepted flowers from a group of young women. One hundred spirited male and female militiamen from the Red Flag Regiment of the Tianjin Wool Processing Factory and the Dongfeng Division of Tianjin University, held rifles in formation and were inspected by the distinguished guests. At this time, the sound of gongs and drums reverberated on the platform, and shouts of "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people" and "Long live Premier Kim Il-sung" rose and fell, creating a joyful scene. As the special train started at nine o'clock, Premier Kim Il-sung stood at the door of the carriage for a long time, warmly waving to the cheering masses.

### 天津人民欢迎朝鲜贵宾

1958-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    天津人民欢迎朝鲜贵宾
    新华社天津22日电　以金日成首相为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团乘专车去北京途中，今天上午八点四十五分路过天津。
    同车路过天津的还有以金光侠大将、许凤学中将为首的朝鲜军事代表团。
    朝鲜贵宾们受到了天津人民的热烈欢迎。当专车到达车站时，站台上的扩音器里响起了“东方红”和“金日成将军之歌”。金日成首相等贵宾们由前来车站欢迎的中共河北省委书记阎达开、河北省副省长马力、中共天津市委第一书记万晓塘、天津市市长李耕涛、中国人民解放军河北军区司令员王道邦中将等陪同，同前来欢迎的党、政、军各界负责人和人民群众见了面，接受了一群女青年的献花。天津市绒毛加工厂红旗团和天津大学东风师一百名精神抖擞的男女民兵，持枪列队，接受了贵宾们的检阅。这时，站台上的群众敲锣打鼓，“中朝两国人民友谊万岁”、“金日成首相万岁”的欢呼声和口号声此起彼伏，一片欢乐的景象。当专车在九时开动的时候，金日成首相还久久地站在车厢门口，向欢呼的群众热情招手致意。
