Title: North Korean and Vietnamese Party and Government Leaders Hold Talks, Pham Van Dong Hosts Banquet for North Korean Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-11-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 29th: The Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, headed by Chairman Kim Il-sung, held talks here this morning with the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Workers' Party, headed by Chairman Ho Chi Minh; in the afternoon, the North Korean government delegation, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, held talks with the Vietnamese government delegation, headed by Prime Minister Pham Van Dong.

The Vietnamese side present at the talks between the leaders of the North Korean and Vietnamese parties included Truờng Chinh, Pham Van Dong, Vo Nguyen Giap, Hoang Van Hoan, Le Duc Tho, Pham Hung, Nguyen Duy Trinh, Nguyen ChiThanh, and Le Thanh Nghi, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Workers' Party, and Ung Van Khiem and Nguyen Thi Thap, members of the Party Central Committee.

The North Korean side present at the talks included Pak Jong-ae, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Nam Il, Kim Kwang-hyop, members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee, and Ri Il-gyong, Kim Chang-chol, members of the Central Committee.

Tran Xuan Do, the Vietnamese Ambassador to North Korea, was also present at the talks.

The talks proceeded in an atmosphere of cordial friendship.

The Vietnamese side present at the talks between the two governments included: Deputy Prime Ministers Phan Ke Toai and Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of Culture Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Foreign Trade Phan Anh, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ung Van Khiem, and Vietnamese Ambassador to North Korea Tran Xuan Do.

The North Korean side included Pak Jong-ae, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Vice-Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il, Minister of National Defense Kim Kwang-hyop, Minister of Education and Culture Ri Il-gyong, and North Korean Ambassador to Vietnam Kim Chang-chol.

The two sides discussed some important issues in the current international situation and issues related to the two countries. The two sides reached a consensus in the discussions.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 28th: Prime Minister Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a banquet tonight to welcome the North Korean government delegation headed by Premier Kim Il-sung. President Ho Chi Minh attended the banquet.

Prime Minister Pham Van Dong said in his speech that in order to welcome the Korean comrades, the Vietnamese people are determined to further strengthen the brotherly friendship and mutual assistance and cooperation between the two countries, further strengthen the unity between the socialist countries headed by the great Soviet Union, and at the same time firmly express our determination to fight to the finish and win decisive victories in the socialist construction of North Vietnam and in the struggle for the peaceful reunification of our country, just like the heroic Korean people.

Premier Kim Il-sung then delivered a speech amidst warm applause, saying, "The strength of our two peoples is growing stronger day by day, and American imperialism and its lackeys are becoming increasingly isolated. Socialist construction is proceeding victoriously in North Korea and North Vietnam, which is a decisive guarantee for the peaceful reunification of our two countries."

After the banquet, Prime Minister Pham Van Dong accompanied Premier Kim Il-sung to the Hanoi Opera House to watch a performance by the Song and Dance Troupe.

### 朝越两国党政领导人会谈  范文同欢宴朝鲜政府代表团

1958-11-30
第3版()
专栏：

    朝越两国党政领导人会谈
    范文同欢宴朝鲜政府代表团
    新华社河内29日电　以金日成委员长为首的朝鲜劳动党中央今天上午在这里同以胡志明主席为首的越南劳动党中央举行了会谈；下午，以金日成首相为首的朝鲜政府代表团同以范文同总理为首的越南政府代表团举行了会谈。
    朝越两党领导人在会谈时，越南方面出席的有越南劳动党中央政治局委员长征、范文同、武元甲、黄文欢、黎德寿、范雄、阮维祯、阮志清、黎清毅，党中央委员雍文谦和阮氏十。
    朝鲜方面出席会谈的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，中央常务委员会委员南日、金光侠，中央委员李一卿、金昌哲。
    出席这次会谈的还有越南驻朝鲜大使陈春度。
    会谈在亲密友好的气氛中进行。
    两国政府领导人会谈时，越南方面出席会谈的有：副总理潘继遂、武元甲，文化部部长黄明鉴，对外贸易部部长潘英，外交部副部长雍文谦，越南驻朝鲜大使陈春度。
    朝鲜方面有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，副首相兼外务相南日，民族保卫相金光侠，教育文化相李一卿，朝鲜驻越南大使金昌哲。
    双方讨论了目前国际局势中的一些重要问题和有关两国之间的问题。双方在讨论中取得了一致看法。
    据新华社河内28日电　越南民主共和国总理范文同今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎以金日成首相为首的朝鲜政府代表团。胡志明主席出席了这次宴会。
    范文同总理首先致词说，为了欢迎朝鲜的同志们，越南人民决心要进一步密切两国之间兄弟般的友谊和互助合作，进一步加强以伟大的苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国之间的团结，同时坚决表示要像英勇的朝鲜人民一样，在越南北方的社会主义建设和争取和平统一祖国的斗争中，决战决胜。
    金日成首相接着在热烈的掌声中发表讲话说，“我们两国人民的力量正在日益增强，美帝国主义和他的走狗日益孤立，在朝鲜和越南北半部社会主义建设正在胜利地进行，这是两国人民和平统一自己祖国的决定性的保证。
    宴会结束后，范文同总理陪同金日成首相等到河内大戏院观看文工团的表演。
