Title: State Council Appointment and Removal List
Author:
Date: 1958-11-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency

    State Council Appointment and Removal List
    Xinhua News Agency reported that the State Council appointed and removed a batch of personnel from state organs, embassies abroad, and universities on September 24 and October 20. The list is as follows:
    Appointed Song Shaowen, Liu Daifeng, and Chai Shufan as Deputy Directors of the State Capital Construction Commission;
    Appointed Jia Tuofu, Han Zheyi, An Zhiwen, and Zhu Lizhi as Deputy Directors of the State Planning Commission;
    Appointed Guo Hongtao and Xue Muqiao as Deputy Directors of the State Economic Commission;
    Appointed Zhao Fasheng as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Food;
    Appointed He Bingzhang as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry;
    Appointed Xie Bangzhi and Wang Weigang as Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Justice;
    Appointed Luo Jun as Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries;
    Appointed Ai Wen as Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam;
    Appointed Guo Moruo as President of the University of Science and Technology of China;
    Removed You Fengqi from the post of Deputy Director of the Bureau of Government Offices Administration of the State Council, and Chen Xu from the post of Deputy Director of the General Office of the Secretariat of the State Council;
    Removed Zhu Lizhi from the post of Vice Minister of the Ministry of Communications;
    Removed Xie Bangzhi from the post of Vice Minister of the Ministry of Supervision.

### 国务院任免名单

1958-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社讯　国务院在9月24日和10月20日任免了一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校的工作人员，名单如下：
    任命宋劭文、刘岱峰、柴树藩为国家基本建设委员会副主任；
    任命贾拓夫、韩哲一、安志文、朱理治为国家计划委员会副主任；
    任命郭洪涛、薛暮桥为国家经济委员会副主任；
    任命赵发生为粮食部副部长；
    任命贺秉章为煤炭工业部副部长；
    任命谢邦治、王维纲为司法部副部长；
    任命罗俊为对外文化联络委员会副主任；
    任命艾汶为中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使馆文化参赞；
    任命郭沫若为中国科学技术大学校长；
    免去犹凤岐的国务院机关事务管理局副局长职务，陈煦的国务院秘书厅副主任职务；
    免去朱理治的交通部副部长职务；
    免去谢邦治的监察部副部长职务。
