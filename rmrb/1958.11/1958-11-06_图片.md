Title: Picture: Left Sea, Leaf Lightly Granted
Author: Ye Qianyu
Date: 1958-11-06
Page: 8

Poetry and Painting

　　Planted on the hillside is a field of jade corn, its towering green leaves already forming a shade.
　　Learning both literature and martial arts to chase the advanced, the commune's eternal labor sound.
　　Left Sea Poem, Leaf Lightly Granted Painting
