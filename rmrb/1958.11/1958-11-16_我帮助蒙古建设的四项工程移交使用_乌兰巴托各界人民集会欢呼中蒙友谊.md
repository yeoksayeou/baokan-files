Title: Four Projects Aided by China for Construction in Mongolia Handed Over for Use: People from All Circles in Ulaanbaatar Gather to Cheer Sino-Mongolian Friendship
Author:
Date: 1958-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 14th: People from all circles in Ulaanbaatar held a rally on the afternoon of the 14th to celebrate the handover for use of four construction projects in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia, built with China's grant aid: the vegetable farm, expert guest house, gymnasium, and sports ground. Mongolian party and government leaders Damba, Tsedenbal, Sambuu, and others, as well as foreign diplomatic envoys to Mongolia, attended the rally.

Bat-Ochiryn Bazargur, head of the Mongolian government delegation participating in the handover of these four construction projects and Mayor of Ulaanbaatar, and Xie Fusheng, head of the Chinese government delegation and Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, spoke at the meeting successively. Bat-Ochiryn Bazargur said in his speech that the people of Ulaanbaatar had recently accepted the "Peace Bridge" and other construction projects aided by China, and today they were accepting four new construction projects built with China's assistance. He said that these construction projects are the crystallization of the friendship between the Mongolian and Chinese people, and the Mongolian people highly appreciate the assistance of their Chinese friends and express their boundless joy.

Before the start of the meeting, Mongolian party and government leaders and foreign diplomatic envoys visited the above-mentioned four construction projects. The Mongolian government delegation also held a reception to celebrate the handover.

### 我帮助蒙古建设的四项工程移交使用  乌兰巴托各界人民集会欢呼中蒙友谊

1958-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我帮助蒙古建设的四项工程移交使用
    乌兰巴托各界人民集会欢呼中蒙友谊
    新华社乌兰巴托14日电　乌兰巴托各界人民14日下午举行集会，庆祝中国无偿援助蒙古在乌兰巴托建设的蔬菜农场、专家招待所、体育馆、运动场等四项建设项目移交使用。蒙古党政领导人达姆巴、泽登巴尔、桑布等和各国驻蒙古的外交使节出席了集会。
    参加这四项建设项目的移交工作的蒙古政府代表团团长、乌兰巴托市市长巴特扎尔格勒和中国政府代表团团长、中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生先后在会上讲了话。巴特扎尔格勒在讲话中说，乌兰巴托人民不久以前接受了中国援助的“和平桥”等建设项目，今天又接受中国帮助建成的四个新建设项目。他说，这些建设项目是蒙中两国人民友谊的结晶，蒙古人民对中国朋友们的援助给以很高的评价并且表示无限的高兴。
    在大会开始以前，蒙古党政领导人和各国外交使节，参观了上述四项建设项目，蒙古政府代表团还举行了庆祝移交的招待会。
