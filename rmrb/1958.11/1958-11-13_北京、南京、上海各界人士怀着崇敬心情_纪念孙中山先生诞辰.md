Title: Personages from All Circles in Beijing, Nanjing, and Shanghai Commemorate Sun Yat-sen's Birthday with Deep Respect

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 12th: More than 400 people from all walks of life in the capital held a memorial ceremony at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in Biyun Temple, Xiangshan, at noon today to commemorate the 92nd anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.

Today's participants from the Chinese Communist Party included Lin Boqu, Chen Yi, Wu Yuzhang, Liao Chengzhi, Xu Bing, and Ping Jiesan.

From the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang were Li Jishen, He Xiangning, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Zhizhong, Xiong Kewu, Deng Baoshan, Chen Shaokuan, Shao Lizi, Chen Shaoxian, Zhu Xuefan, Liu Wenhui, Tang Shengzhi, Weng Wenhao, Mei Gongbin, Zhu Yunshan, Wang Kunlun, Dan Maoxin, Ning Wu, and all the delegates attending the Fourth National Congress of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang.

Also participating in the commemoration were Chen Qiyou, Ji Fang, Guo Zecheng, and representatives of other democratic parties.

In front of the statue of Mr. Sun Yat-sen in the memorial hall, were placed flower baskets from Mao Zedong, Zhou Enlai, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Chen Yi, Wu Yuzhang, Xu Xiangqian, Chen Geng, Li Weihan, Liao Chengzhi, Xu Bing, and Fang Fang; and flower baskets from Li Jishen, He Xiangning, Cheng Qian, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Zhizhong, Xiong Kewu, Deng Baoshan, Chen Shaokuan, Shao Lizi, Chen Jiageng, Li Renren, Chen Qiyuanyuan, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Fourth National Representative Conference of the Kuomintang Revolutionary Committee.

The memorial ceremony began at eleven o'clock. Lin Boqu, Wu Yuzhang, Li Jishen, and He Xiangning presented flowers to the statue of Sun Yat-sen. Afterwards, all stood in silence to pay tribute to the portrait of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.

Today, people from all walks of life and comrades-in-arms of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, with a feeling of reverence, visited the exhibition rooms on both sides of the memorial hall, viewing some photographs, manuscripts and calligraphy of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's struggle against imperialism and for the realization of the Three People's Principles during his lifetime.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, November 12th: On today's 92nd anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, Guan Wenwei and Wu Yifang, vice governors of Jiangsu Province, Wang Zhaoquan and Liu Shuxun, vice mayors of Nanjing, Li Leping and Chen Heqin, vice chairmen of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and responsible persons and representatives of local organizations of democratic parties and provincial and municipal people's organizations, totaling more than 30 people, went to the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum to pay their respects.

At about 9 a.m., the participants held a memorial ceremony in the memorial hall of the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum. After presenting wreaths, all the participants slowly entered the mausoleum to pay their respects to the reclining statue of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. The memorial ceremony ended at 10 o'clock.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 12th: To commemorate the 92nd anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, Chen Pixian and Wei Wenbo, secretaries of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Shuzhou, Jin Zhonghua, Xu Jianguo, and Sheng Pihua, vice mayors of Shanghai, and Li Zhaohuan, vice chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and others, visited Sun Yat-sen's former residence this morning. They also presented flower baskets to the portrait of Mr. Sun Yat-sen on behalf of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Kuomintang Revolutionary Committee, respectively. ### 北京、南京、上海各界人士怀着崇敬心情  纪念孙中山先生诞辰

1958-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    北京、南京、上海各界人士怀着崇敬心情
    纪念孙中山先生诞辰
    新华社12日讯　首都各界人士四百多人，今天中午在香山碧云寺孙中山纪念堂举行纪念仪式，纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十二年。
    今天参加纪念仪式的，中国共产党方面有林伯渠、陈毅、吴玉章、廖承志、徐冰和平杰三等。
    中国国民党革命委员会方面有李济深、何香凝、蔡廷锴、张治中、熊克武、邓宝珊、陈绍宽、邵力子、陈劭先、朱学范、刘文辉、唐生智、翁文灏、梅龚彬、朱蕴山、王昆仑、但懋辛、宁武等以及出席中国国民党革命委员会第四届全国代表大会的全体代表。
    参加纪念仪式的还有陈其尤、季方、郭则沉等其他民主党派的代表。
    在纪念堂孙中山先生的塑像前，放着毛泽东、周恩来、林伯渠、董必武、陈毅、吴玉章、徐向前、陈赓、李维汉、廖承志、徐冰、方方的花篮；放着李济深、何香凝、程潜、蔡廷锴、张治中、熊克武、邓宝珊、陈绍宽、邵力子、陈嘉庚、李任仁、陈其瑗等以及政协全国委员会和民革第四届全国代表大会的花篮。
    纪念仪式在十一时开始。林伯渠、吴玉章、李济深、何香凝向孙中山像献了鲜花，随后，全体肃立向孙中山先生遗像致敬。
    今天，各界人士和孙中山先生生前战友，怀着崇敬的心情，在纪念堂两旁的展览室里，参观了孙中山先生生前为反对帝国主义、为实现三民主义而进行斗争的一些照片，遗著和遗墨等。
    新华社南京12日电　在今天孙中山先生诞辰九十二周年纪念日，江苏省副省长管文蔚、吴贻芳，南京市副市长王昭铨、刘树勋，政协江苏省委员会副主席李乐平、陈鹤琴，各民主党派地方组织及省、市人民团体的负责人和代表三十多人，前往中山陵谒陵。
    上午九时许，参加谒陵的人在中山陵墓室祭堂内举行了谒陵仪式。敬献花圈以后，全体谒陵人员徐步进入陵寝，瞻仰孙中山先生的卧像。谒陵仪式在十时结束。
    新华社上海12日电　为纪念孙中山先生诞生九十二周年，中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显、魏文伯，上海市副市长刘述周、金仲华、许建国、盛丕华，政协上海市委员会副主席黎照寰等，今天上午瞻仰了中山故居。他们并分别代表中共上海市委员会、上海市人民委员会、政协上海市委员会、民革上海市委员会等单位，向孙中山先生遗像敬献了花篮。（附图片）
