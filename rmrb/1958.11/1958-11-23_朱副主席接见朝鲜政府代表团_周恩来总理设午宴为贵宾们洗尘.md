Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Receives Korean Government Delegation Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Luncheon to Welcome the Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-11-23
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd: Vice Chairman Zhu De received Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and all members of the Korean government delegation, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Park Jung-ae, Deputy Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nam Il, Minister of National Defense, Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of Education and Culture, Ri Il-gyeong, and Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho, at 5:30 p.m. today.
    Present at the reception were Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, and Cheng Qian, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Zhang Su, Vice Minister of Education Liu Aifeng, and Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Xiaoguang.
    Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd: Premier Zhou Enlai received Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and all members of the Korean government delegation: Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Park Jung-ae, Deputy Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nam Il, Minister of National Defense, Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of Education and Culture, Ri Il-gyeong, and Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho, at 1:00 p.m. today. Present at the reception were Vice Premiers of the State Council Peng Dehuai, He Long, and Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, and Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Xiaoguang.
    Following the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a luncheon to welcome the distinguished guests. (Attached photo)

### 朱副主席接见朝鲜政府代表团  周恩来总理设午宴为贵宾们洗尘

1958-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席接见朝鲜政府代表团
    周恩来总理设午宴为贵宾们洗尘
    新华社22日讯　朱德副主席今天下午五时半接见了金日成首相和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团的全体团员、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁副首相兼外务相南日，民族保卫相金光侠，教育文化相李一卿，朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐。
    接见时在座的有，周恩来总理，陈毅副总理，李济深、郭沫若、程潜副委员长，国防委员会副主席张治中，外交部副部长曾涌泉，文化部部长沈雁冰，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，教育部副部长刘皑风，我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光等。
    新华社22日讯　周恩来总理今天下午一时接见了金日成首相和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团全体团员：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁副首相兼外务相南日，民族保卫相金光侠，教育文化相李一卿，朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐。接见时在座的有，国务院副总理彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅，外交部副部长张闻天，我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光等。
    接见以后，周恩来总理设午宴为贵宾们洗尘。（附图片）
