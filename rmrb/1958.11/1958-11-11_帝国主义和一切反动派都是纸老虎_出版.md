Title: Publication of "Imperialism and All Reactionaries Are Paper Tigers"
Author:
Date: 1958-11-11
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The People's Publishing House has edited "Imperialism and All Reactionaries Are Paper Tigers" for cadres and people throughout the country to study, and it was published today. The main contents of this book, in addition to the important political document "Comrade Mao Zedong on Imperialism and All Reactionaries Are Paper Tigers" published in the People's Daily, also collect some important documents of Premier Zhou Enlai, Minister Chen Yi, and Minister Peng Dehuai on the current situation in Taiwan, as well as important editorials and commentaries in Red Flag and People's Daily. In addition, the Class A version of this book also includes some remarks from the United States, Japan, Britain, as well as the Chiang Kai-shek Group and Hong Kong. These negative teaching materials can be used as reference for study.
The first batch of 400,000 copies of this book have been printed in Beijing, and will be distributed starting tomorrow; at the same time, they will also be printed in Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Shenyang, Xi'an, Chongqing, Jiujiang and other places.

### “帝国主义和一切反动派都是纸老虎”出版

1958-11-11
第6版()
专栏：

    “帝国主义和一切反动派都是纸老虎”出版
    新华社10日讯　人民出版社编辑的供全国干部和各界人民学习的“帝国主义和一切反动派都是纸老虎”已在今天出版。这本书的主要内容除人民日报发表的“毛泽东同志论帝国主义和一切反动派都是纸老虎”这一重要的政治文献外，还收集了周恩来总理、陈毅部长、彭德怀部长有关目前台湾局势问题的一些重要文件，以及红旗，人民日报上有关的重要社论和评论。此外，这本书的甲种版本还附录了美、日、英以及蒋介石集团和香港等方面的一些言论，这些反面教材，可供学习时参考。
    这本书在北京第一批印了四十万册，从明天起开始发行；同时，还分别在上海、武汉、广州、沈阳、西安、重庆、九江等地印刷。
