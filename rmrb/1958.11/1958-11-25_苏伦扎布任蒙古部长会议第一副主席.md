Title: Tsedenbal Appointed First Deputy Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1958-11-25
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 23rd: According to today's "Unen" newspaper in Mongolia: The Presidium of the Mongolian Great People's Khural appointed Tsedenbal as First Deputy Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers.

### 苏伦扎布任蒙古部长会议第一副主席

1958-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    苏伦扎布任蒙古部长会议第一副主席
    新华社乌兰巴托23日电　据蒙古“真理报”今天报道：蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团任命苏伦扎布为蒙古部长会议第一副主席。
