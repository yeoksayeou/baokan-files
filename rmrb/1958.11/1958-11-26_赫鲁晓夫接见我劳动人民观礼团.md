Author: 
Date: 1958-11-26
Page: 4

    Khrushchev Receives Chinese Laboring People's Observation Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 24th: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU, Khrushchev, received the Chinese Laboring People's Delegation to the 41st Anniversary Celebrations of the October Revolution, headed by Liu Lantao, member of the Central Committee of the CPC and alternate secretary of the Secretariat, at the Kremlin at 5 p.m. today, and held a long and cordial conversation. Members of the delegation spoke of their impressions of visiting Moscow, Leningrad, Belarus, and Ukraine.
    Present at the meeting were Andreev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Popova, Chairman of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Andropov, Minister of the Central Committee of the CPSU, among others.
    Also present at the reception was Liu Xiao, China's ambassador to the Soviet Union. (Attached picture)

### 赫鲁晓夫接见我劳动人民观礼团

1958-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见我劳动人民观礼团
    新华社莫斯科24日电　苏联部长会议主席、苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫，今天下午五时在克里姆林宫接见了以中共中央委员、中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛为首的中国劳动人民参加十月革命四十一周年观礼团，并且进行了长时间亲切友好的谈话。观礼团人员谈到了他们访问莫斯科、列宁格勒、白俄罗斯和乌克兰的印象。
    接见时在座的有苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫，苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席波波娃和苏共中央部长安德罗波夫等人。
    接见时在座的还有我国驻苏联大使刘晓。（附图片）
