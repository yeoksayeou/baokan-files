Title: US Protestant Church Leaders' Conference Criticizes US Government's Hostile Policy Toward China; Dulles' Warmongering Provocations Meet With Cold Reception
Author:
Date: 1958-11-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd: According to TASS, New York, November 22nd: A conference of 500 leaders of the American Protestant churches in Cleveland, Ohio, has now ended. U.S. Secretary of State Dulles delivered a venomous speech on the 18th, viciously attacking the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and "International Communism." However, the attendees did not heed Dulles' warmongering provocations. They unanimously criticized the U.S.'s hostile policy towards China and demanded that the U.S. government strive for peaceful coexistence with socialist countries.

In a letter to the churches, the conference pointed out that American Christians should demand that the U.S. government consider changing its policy toward China, because this policy is "detrimental to the international whole…and keeps our people ignorant of the situation in China."

Attendees criticized the argument for "limited" war. In the letter, they emphasized that it is absolutely necessary to prevent war, not to localize it. They also called for "more decisive measures to seek ways of coexisting with communist countries."

The letter also criticized the U.S. government's policy of forming blocs and called for arms reduction.

### 美国新教教会领袖会议  抨击美政府敌视中国政策  杜勒斯的好战叫嚣受到冷遇

1958-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    美国新教教会领袖会议
    抨击美政府敌视中国政策
    杜勒斯的好战叫嚣受到冷遇
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社纽约22日讯：美国新教教会五百名领导人在俄亥俄州克利夫兰举行的会议现在已经结束。美国国务卿杜勒斯18日曾经在会上发表了一篇恶毒地攻击苏联、中华人民共和国和“国际共产主义”的外交政策演说，但是到会者并没有听杜勒斯的这种好战叫嚣，他们一致抨击美国敌视中国的政策，并且要求美国政府努力谋求同社会主义国家和平共处。
    会议在给教会的信中指出，美国基督教徒应该要求美国政府考虑改变它对中国的政策，因为这种政策“有害于国际整体……并且使我国人民对中国情况一无所知”。
    到会者指责了“有限”战争的论调。他们在信中强调指出，绝对必要的是防止战争，而不是使战争局部化。他们还要求“采取更坚决的措施来寻求同共产党国家共处的途径”。
    这封信还指责美国政府实行的拼凑集团的政策，要求裁减军备。
