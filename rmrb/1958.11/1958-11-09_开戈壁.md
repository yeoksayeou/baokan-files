Title: Opening Up the Gobi

Author:

Date: 1958-11-09
Page: 5

Opening up the Gobi, opening up the Gobi, reclaiming alkaline land,
Cutting grass and removing roots with deep plowing.
Transforming the landscape, clothing it in green,
Millions of mu of fertile land,
Golden rice and wheat gleaming.

\t\t——A Soldier’s Song
