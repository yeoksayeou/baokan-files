Title: Opening the Flaming Mountains
Author: Wang Jiaduo
Date: 1958-11-30
Page: 6

    Opening the Flaming Mountains
    The Flaming Mountains are treasure mountains,
    Countless treasures locked inside,
    Heroic sons and daughters unlock them,
    Ten thousand tons of black gold flow out.
              (Wang Jiaduo)

### 打开火焰山
王家铎
1958-11-30
第6版()
专栏：

    打开火焰山
    火焰山，是宝山，
    无数宝藏锁里边，
    英雄儿女把锁开，
    万吨黑金流出来。
              （王家铎）
