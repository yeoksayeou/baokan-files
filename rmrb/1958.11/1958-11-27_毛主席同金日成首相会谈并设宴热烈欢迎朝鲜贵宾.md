Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Meets with Premier Kim Il-sung and Hosts Banquet to Warmly Welcome Distinguished Korean Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, November 26th: Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Kim Il-sung held talks in Wuhan City this afternoon.

    The Korean side participating in the talks included Korean government delegation members Park Jung-ae, Nam Il, Kim Kwang-hyup, Ri Il-gyong, and Ri Yong-ho.

    The Chinese side participating in the talks included Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, and Peng Zhen.
   Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, November 26th: Chairman Mao Zedong hosted a grand banquet in Wuhan City tonight to welcome Premier Kim Il-sung and all the distinguished guests of the Korean government delegation. The Korean military delegation was also invited to attend the banquet.

    Chairman Mao Zedong raised his glass at the banquet to warmly welcome Premier Kim Il-sung and all the distinguished Korean guests to visit China, and wished them good health. Premier Kim Il-sung raised his glass to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, who constantly cares for the Korean people and is respected by the Korean people, and other Chinese leaders.

    Tonight, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee Liu Shaoqi, Premier of the State Council and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee Zhou Enlai, and General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee Deng Xiaoping attended the banquet.

    Also attending the banquet were Peng Zhen, Li Fuchun, He Long, Li Xiannian, Ke Qingshi, Li Jingquan, Tan Zhenlin, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee who were present, Ulanhu, Chen Boda, Bo Yibo, alternate members of the Political Bureau, Tan Zheng, Li Xuefeng, secretaries of the Central Secretariat, Yang Shangkun, alternate secretary of the Secretariat, members and alternate members of the CPC Central Committee, responsible persons of various government departments, and secretaries of various provincial and municipal party committees Luo Ruiqing, Wang Shoudao, Shu Tong, Li Baohua, Lin Tie, Zhao Erlu, Ouyang Qin, Xie Fuzhi, Wu Zhipu, Lü Zhengcao, Tao Zhu, Zeng Xisheng, Wang Enmao, Ye Fei, Liu Ren, Huang Huoqing, Liu Lanbo, Wu De, Zhang Desheng, Zhang Linzhi, Jiang Weiqing, Liao Luyan, Jiang Hua, Wang Heshou, Wang Renzhong, Zhang Zhongliang, Tao Lujia, Peng Tao, Liu Jianxun, Wang Feng, Zhou Xiaozhou, Zhang Pinghua, Yu Qiuli, Luo Yuchuan, Yang Shangkui, Zhou Lin, and Gao Feng.

    Also present at the banquet were Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Zhao Xinchu and Xu Daoqi, Secretaries of the CPC Hubei Provincial Committee, General Chen Zaidao and Lieutenant General Li Chengfang, leading officers of the Wuhan Garrison, General Li Zhimin, who accompanied the Korean military delegation to Wuhan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea Qiao Xiaoguang, Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the CPC Wuhan Municipal Committee, and Li Erzhong, Secretary of the Municipal Committee.

    After the banquet, Chairman Mao Zedong and others, together with Premier Kim Il-sung and others, watched wonderful programs performed by the Wuhan Chu Opera Troupe, the Sichuan Sichuan Opera Theatre, and the Wuhan Han Opera Troupe. (Attached photo)

### 毛主席同金日成首相会谈并设宴热烈欢迎朝鲜贵宾

1958-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席同金日成首相会谈并设宴热烈欢迎朝鲜贵宾
    新华社武汉26日电　毛泽东主席和金日成首相今天下午在武汉市举行了会谈。
    朝鲜方面参加会谈的，有朝鲜政府代表团团员朴正爱、南日、金光侠、李一卿、李永镐同志。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、邓小平、彭真同志。
   新华社武汉26日电　毛泽东主席今晚在武汉市举行盛大宴会，欢迎金日成首相和朝鲜政府代表团的全体贵宾。朝鲜军事代表团也应邀出席了宴会。
    毛泽东主席在宴会上举杯致词，热烈欢迎金日成首相和朝鲜全体贵宾访问中国，并且祝他们身体健康。金日成首相举杯为经常关怀朝鲜人民并且为朝鲜人民所敬爱的毛泽东主席和中国的其他领导人的身体健康干杯。
    今晚，在这里的中华人民共和国副主席、中共中央副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长、中共中央副主席刘少奇，国务院总理、中共中央副主席周恩来，中共中央总书记邓小平都出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，还有在这里的中共中央政治局委员彭真、李富春、贺龙、李先念、柯庆施、李井泉、谭震林，政治局候补委员乌兰夫、陈伯达、薄一波，中央书记处书记谭政、李雪峰，书记处候补书记杨尚昆，中共中央委员、候补委员，政府各部门负责人，各省委、市委书记罗瑞卿、王首道、舒同、李葆华、林铁、赵尔陆、欧阳钦、谢富治、吴芝圃、吕正操、陶铸、曾希圣、王恩茂、叶飞、刘仁、黄火青、刘澜波、吴德、张德生、张霖之、江渭清、廖鲁言、江华、王鹤寿、王任重、张仲良、陶鲁笳、彭涛、刘建勋、汪锋、周小舟、张平化、余秋里、罗玉川、杨尚奎、周林、高峰。
    湖北省省长张体学，中共湖北省委书记处书记赵辛初、许道琦，武汉驻军首长陈再道上将、李成芳中将，陪同朝鲜军事代表团来武汉访问的李志民上将，外交部副部长曾涌泉，中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光，武汉市市长刘惠农，中共武汉市委第一书记宋侃夫、市委书记处书记李尔重等人也出席了宴会。
    宴会结束以后，毛泽东主席等和金日成首相等一起观看了武汉市楚剧团、四川省川剧院、武汉市汉剧团演出的精彩节目。（附图片）
