Workers' Poetry and Paintings

　　Steel already comes out while the stars twinkle,
　　Breaking ground at midnight to build factories,
　　See the factory buildings when the sun comes out;
　　Watch iron smelting in the setting sun,
　　Steel already comes out while the stars twinkle.
　　(Tianjin Construction Workers)

　　Could it be that workers know immortal magic?
　　Thoroughbred horse, Red Hare horse,
　　What are they compared to us?
　　Factory buildings are completed in a day,
　　Skyscrapers pierce the clouds in half a day.
　　Sun Wukong blinks his eyes on his cloud,
　　Could it be that workers know immortal magic?
　　If you ask what this is all about,
　　This is rapid construction.
    Li Bingchang, Beijing Second Construction Company

　　Three stories built overnight
　　The sun rises in the east, the land is red,
　　Construction workers show their heroism,
　　Yesterday it was still a barren mountain ridge,
　　Three stories built overnight.
    Xie Manquan, Chongqing Construction Worker (with picture)

　　He Didn't Go To The Wrong Place
    Painted by Li Shengya, Processing Plant, Jilin Province Third Construction Company

　　In the Same Day
    Painted by Zhao Qichang and Zhang Jinlan, Beijing Municipal Real Estate Administration Construction Company

### 工人的诗画

1958-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    工人的诗画
    星星眨眼已出钢
    三更破土盖工厂，
    太阳出来见厂房；
    夕阳西照看炼铁，
    星星眨眼已出钢。
        （天津市建筑工人）
    莫非工人会仙法
    千里驹，赤兔马，
    同咱一比它算啥；
    一日厂房就落成，
    半天高楼云里插。
    悟空云头把眼眨，
    莫非工人会仙法？
    要问这是咋会事，
    这是快速施工法。
    北京市第二建筑公司  李炳昌
    一夜盖好楼三层
    日出东方遍地红，
    建筑工人逞英雄，
    昨天还是荒山岭，
    一夜盖好楼三层。
    重庆市建筑工人  谢满全（附图片）
    他并没有走错地方
    吉林省第三建筑公司加工厂　李生亚画
    在同一天里　北京市房产管理局修建公司　赵其昌　张金兰画
