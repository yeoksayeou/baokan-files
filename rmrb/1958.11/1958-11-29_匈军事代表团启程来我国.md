Title: Hungarian Military Delegation Departs for China
Author:
Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 28th: A Hungarian military delegation headed by Minister of Defense, General Révész Géza, left Budapest this morning for a month-long friendly visit to China.

The delegation is accompanied by Colonel Hu Binpu, Chinese military attache to Hungary.

### 匈军事代表团启程来我国

1958-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    匈军事代表团启程来我国
    新华社布达佩斯28日电　以国防部长雷维斯·格佐上将为首的匈牙利军事代表团，今晨离开布达佩斯前往中国作为期一月的友好访问。
    代表团由中国驻匈牙利武官胡彬甫上校陪行。
