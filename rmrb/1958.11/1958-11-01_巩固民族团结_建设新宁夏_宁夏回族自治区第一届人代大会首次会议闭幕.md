Title: Consolidate National Unity and Build a New Ningxia: The First Session of the First People's Congress of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region Concludes
Author:
Date: 1958-11-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Yinchuan, October 31st. The first session of the First People's Congress of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, lasting seven days, victoriously concluded on October 30th.

During the session, all the delegates listened to the instructions given to the congress by Lin Boqu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, representative of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao; the "Work Report" made by Liu Geping, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Autonomous Region; as well as the "Explanation on the Draft Organizational Regulations of the People's Congress and the People's Committee of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region," the "Report on the Implementation of the 1958 National Economic Plan and the Draft 1959 National Economic Plan of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region," and the "Report on the Adjustment of the 1958 Fiscal Budget and the Draft 1959 Fiscal Budget of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region." The delegates held enthusiastic discussions on these reports. The delegates unanimously approved the above reports and expressed their full commitment to completing the glorious task pointed out by Vice Chairman Lin Boqu in his instructions: to accelerate the comprehensive Great Leap Forward of socialist construction and strive to completely change the face of the Autonomous Region in the shortest possible time. The sixty-six delegates who spoke at the meeting all expressed their determination, under the leadership of the Party, to raise their socialist and communist ideological awareness, prevent and overcome localism, local nationalism, and Han chauvinism that hinder national unity, strengthen national unity, and, in accordance with the spirit of the Party's general line for socialist construction, under the battle cry of "Ride the Wind and Waves, Work Hard for Three Years, and Change the Face of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region" put forward by the Ningxia Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, to launch a high tide of the Great Leap Forward in industrial and agricultural production.

After full deliberation and discussion, all the delegates put forward sixty-eight proposals concerning industrial and agricultural production, political and legal affairs, finance and economics, culture, education, and health.

The meeting unanimously adopted the Resolution on the Work Report of the Ningxia Region, the Resolution on the Report on the Adjustment of the 1958 Fiscal Budget and the Draft 1959 Fiscal Budget, the Resolution on the Report on the Implementation of the 1958 National Economic Plan and the Draft 1959 National Economic Plan, and the Resolution on the Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congress and the People's Committee of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region.

Liu Geping, Chairman of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, said in his closing speech: This meeting was held under the cordial concern of the Central Committee. Vice Chairman Lin Boqu, on behalf of the Party Central Committee, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao, gave important instructions on our work. This instruction is of extreme importance to our region's future work and must be resolutely implemented in future work. He asked the delegates to implement the various resolutions and spirit of the congress into the actions of the people throughout the region, continue to strengthen and consolidate national unity under the leadership of the Party, rely on and mobilize the masses, carry forward the spirit of hard struggle, and strive to complete and overfulfill various construction tasks, and struggle to build a socialist new Ningxia.

During the meeting, representatives from the Central Committee and various provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities all spoke at the meeting, warmly congratulating the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region. The congress also received many congratulatory telegrams, letters, and banners from all over the country.

The congress passed a congratulatory telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, and also passed a message of condolences to the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front.

### 巩固民族团结　建设新宁夏  宁夏回族自治区第一届人代大会首次会议闭幕

1958-11-01
第2版()
专栏：

    巩固民族团结　建设新宁夏
    宁夏回族自治区第一届人代大会首次会议闭幕
    新华社银川31日电　宁夏回族自治区第一届人民代表大会第一次会议，历时七日，在10月30日胜利闭幕。
    会议期间，全体代表听了中共中央、中央人民政府和毛主席的代表林伯渠副委员长对大会作的指示，自治区筹备委员会主任刘格平作的“工作报告”，以及“关于宁夏回族自治区人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例草案的说明”、“关于宁夏回族自治区1958年国民经济计划执行情况和1959年国民经济计划草案的报告”、“关于宁夏回族自治区1958年财政预算调整情况和1959年财政预算草案的报告”，对这些报告进行了热烈的讨论。代表们一致同意以上报告，并表示全力完成林伯渠副委员长在指示中指出的加速实现社会主义建设全面大跃进，争取在最短期间彻底改变自治区面貌的光荣任务。在会上发言的六十六位代表，都表示要坚决在党的领导下，提高社会主义和共产主义思想觉悟，防止和克服妨碍民族团结的地方主义、地方民族主义和大民族主义，加强民族团结，根据党的社会主义建设总路线的精神，在中共宁夏工委提出的“乘风破浪，苦战三年，改变宁夏回族自治区面貌”的战斗口号下，掀起工农业生产大跃进的高潮。
    全体代表经过充分的酝酿讨论，提出了有关工农业生产、政法、财经、文教卫生等方面的议案六十八件。
    会议一致通过了关于宁夏地区工作报告的决议，关于1958年财政预算调整情况和1959年财政预算草案报告的决议，关于1958年国民经济计划执行情况和1959年国民经济计划草案的报告的决议，关于宁夏回族自治区人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）的决议。
    宁夏回族自治区主席刘格平在致闭幕词中说：这次会议是在中央亲切的关怀下召开的。林伯渠副委员长代表党中央、中央人民政府和毛主席对我们的工作作了重要指示，这一指示对我区今后工作具有极其重要的意义，在今后的工作中必须坚决贯彻执行。他要求代表们把大会的各项决议和精神贯彻到全区人民行动中去，在党的领导下，继续加强和巩固民族团结，依靠群众和发动群众，发扬艰苦奋斗的精神，努力完成和超额完成各项建设任务，为建设社会主义的新宁夏而奋斗。
    会议期间，中央及各省、自治区、市的代表，都在会上讲了话，热烈祝贺宁夏回族自治区的成立。大会同时还收到全国各地的许多贺电、贺信和贺幛。
    大会通过了向中共中央和毛主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会刘少奇委员长、国务院周恩来总理的致敬电，还通过了致福建前线中国人民解放军将士的慰问电。
