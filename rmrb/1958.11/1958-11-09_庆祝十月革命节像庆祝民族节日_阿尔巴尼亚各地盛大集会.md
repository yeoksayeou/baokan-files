Title: Celebrating the October Revolution as if Celebrating a National Holiday: Grand Gatherings Throughout Albania
Author:
Date: 1958-11-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, November 7th. People across Albania held grand gatherings on the 6th to celebrate the 41st anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

    The celebration in the capital, Tirana, was presided over by Kambo, Secretary of the Albanian Labor Party. Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, and Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and other party and state leaders attended the celebration.

    At the meeting, Koleka, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, delivered a report on the October Revolution. He emphasized that the Albanian people will always safeguard the friendship with the Soviet Union and strengthen the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

    On the evening of the 6th, tens of thousands of Tirana citizens, holding high the national flags of Albania and the Soviet Union, flocked to the city center from all directions as if celebrating their own national holiday.

    Skanderbeg Square was brightly lit, and colorful fireworks rose into the night sky after a long rain. Joyful people sang songs of the October Revolution in front of the Soviet Embassy.

### 庆祝十月革命节像庆祝民族节日  阿尔巴尼亚各地盛大集会

1958-11-09
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月革命节像庆祝民族节日
    阿尔巴尼亚各地盛大集会
    据新华社地拉那7日电　阿尔巴尼亚全国各地人民6日分别集会隆重庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十一周年。
    首都地拉那的庆祝大会由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党书记卡博主持。劳动党中央第一书记霍查和部长会议主席谢胡等党和国家的领导人都出席了庆祝会。
    会上由劳动党中央政治局委员科列加作了有关十月革命的报告。他强调指出，阿尔巴尼亚人民将永远维护和苏联的友谊，增强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结。
    6日傍晚成千上万的地拉那市民高举着阿苏两国国旗，像庆祝自己的民族节日似的从城市的四面八方拥向了市中心。
    斯堪德培广场上灯火辉煌，五色缤纷的礼花升上了久雨初晴的夜空，欢乐的人们在苏联大使馆前高唱十月革命的歌曲。
