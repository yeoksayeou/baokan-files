Title: Western Newspapers Continuously Reveal American Conspiracy to Intensify "Two Chinas" Plot; Dulles Attempts to Entice Western European Countries to Support Him
Author:
Date: 1958-11-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd Dispatch: New York - The "Wall Street Journal" revealed the next step in the American conspiracy to create "Two Chinas."

	The newspaper reported in its "Washington Wire" column that after the Dulles-Chiang talks communique was issued, "Dulles will try to entice Europe (referring to Western European countries) to support Chiang Kai-shek." "American] diplomatic officials hope that Formosa (Taiwan) will be recognized as a long-term free fortress." The newspaper pointed out that this is the "next step [America] hopes to take."

	The newspaper also revealed that the United States will use arguments emphasizing that the Chiang Kai-shek group "guarantees not to attack the mainland" and promoting that "not supporting Chiang will harm all Asian anti-communists" to entice its Western European allies.

Xinhua News Agency, 30th Dispatch: Paris - The French newspaper "Le Monde" published an article on the 29th by its Washington correspondent, Henri Pierre, pointing out that the United States is playing with a "Two Chinas" conspiracy. Pierre said, "Simply put, the United States puts China in the ranks of divided countries, such as Vietnam, Korea, and Germany. Before this, the United States only knew one China, Formosa China. Now, after Chiang Kai-shek formally gave up (attacking the mainland) under pressure, the (American) official policy is the policy of 'Two Chinas'."

	Pierre said, "However, observers also pointed out that the Chinese Communists just do not want the existence of 'Two Chinas'. Resuming the shelling is not to prepare for an attack, but more to show to world opinion and the Kuomintang army that Beijing maintains the initiative and can resume and stop the shelling at will. In general, Beijing will not help Dulles out of the difficult situation he has fallen into, but will continue to use the divisions between Washington and Formosa (Taiwan) to divide them."

	The article concluded, "Chiang Kai-shek's renunciation of the use of force has been greatly exaggerated in order to create a more favorable atmosphere for (cooperation) with European allies, but the situation remains dangerous."

### 西方报纸不断透露  美国加紧搞“两个中国”阴谋  杜勒斯企图诱骗西欧国家给他撑腰

1958-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    西方报纸不断透露
    美国加紧搞“两个中国”阴谋
    杜勒斯企图诱骗西欧国家给他撑腰
    新华社2日讯　纽约消息：“华尔街日报”透露了美国制造“两个中国”的阴谋的下一个步骤。
    这家报纸在它的“华盛顿电讯”一栏中报道说，在杜蒋会谈公报发表以后，“杜勒斯将钓诱欧洲（指西欧国家）支持蒋介石”。“（美国）外交官员希望福摩萨（台湾）被承认为长期的自由堡垒。”报纸指出，这是美国“希望采取的下一个步骤”。
    这家报纸还透露，美国将用强调蒋介石集团“保证不进攻大陆，”以及鼓吹“不支持蒋就会损害所有亚洲反共人士”的论据来诱骗它的西欧盟国。
    新华社30日讯　巴黎消息：法国“世界报”29日刊载该报驻华盛顿特派记者亨利·皮埃尔的一篇文章中指出，美国正在玩弄“两个中国”的阴谋。皮埃尔说，“简单说来，美国把中国放在分裂的国家之列，如像越南、朝鲜和德国。到此以前，美国只知道一个中国，福摩萨中国。现在，在蒋介石在压力下正式放弃（进攻大陆）后，（美国）官方的政策就是‘两个中国’的政策。”
    皮埃尔说，“然而，观察家们又指出：中国共产党人恰好就是不要‘两个中国’的存在。恢复炮击不是为了准备进攻，而更多地是为了向世界舆论和国民党军队表明北京保持着主动，可以随便恢复和停止炮击。总的说来，北京不会去帮助杜勒斯走出他自己陷进去的困难境地，而倒会继续利用华盛顿和福摩萨（台湾）之间的分歧以分化它们。”
    文章最后说，“蒋介石的放弃使用武力被大大地夸大了，以便制造更有利于跟欧洲同盟者（合作）的气氛，但是局势仍然是危险的。”
