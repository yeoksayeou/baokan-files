Title: Beijing Holds Meeting of Women Activists: Cai Chang Says Women Can Do Anything and Do Everything Well
Author:
Date: 1958-11-22
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th — Nearly 200,000 street women in Beijing's urban areas have participated in various kinds of labor, and all 400,000 able-bodied women in the suburbs have participated in agricultural labor. These women activists reported on their work achievements and exchanged experiences at the Beijing Women Activists Representative Meeting held from the 18th to the 20th.
    The capital's women have participated in labor in all walks of life and have made outstanding achievements. Among them, 130,000 street women participated in steelmaking, and more than 20,000 women in the suburbs invested in steelmaking and mining. Among the thousands of activists are the nationally renowned outstanding female salesperson from Tianqiao Department Store and the girls of the "Jiulan Group" who made contributions to the Ming Tombs labor.
    Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Wan Li, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Party Committee Secretariat, and Vice Mayor of Beijing, attended the meeting to congratulate the women. Cai Chang said that the capital's women activists have contributed greatly to socialist construction and have become role models for women to learn from. Facts have proved that women can do anything and do everything well. She particularly explained the importance of running public canteens, nurseries and other welfare undertakings, and asked women not to underestimate these tasks. Cai Chang called on everyone to continue to work hard to improve their ideological awareness and cultural and technical level, become all-rounders who are both red and expert, and strive to make greater achievements in the Great Leap Forward next year.
    More than 900 women activists participated in this meeting. The meeting also approved the list of 90 representatives from Beijing to attend the National Women's Socialist Construction Activists Representative Meeting.

### 北京市举行妇女积极分子代表会议  蔡畅说，妇女一切都能干，一切都能干好

1958-11-22
第6版()
专栏：

    北京市举行妇女积极分子代表会议
    蔡畅说，妇女一切都能干，一切都能干好
    新华社20日讯　北京市城区的街道妇女已经有近二十万人参加了各种劳动，郊区四十万有劳动力的妇女全部参加了农业劳动。这些妇女中的积极分子们在18日到20日举行的北京市妇女积极分子代表会议上汇报了工作成绩，交流了经验。
    首都妇女们参加了各行各业的劳动，并且做出了出色的成绩。其中参加炼钢的街道妇女有十三万人，郊区投入炼钢采矿的妇女达两万人以上。在成千上万名积极分子中，有闻名全国的天桥商场的优秀女售货员，有在十三陵劳动中立了功的“九兰组”的姑娘。
    全国妇联主席蔡畅、中共北京市委书记处书记、北京市副市长万里等到会向妇女们祝贺。蔡畅说，首都妇女积极分子为社会主义建设贡献了很大力量，成了妇女们学习的榜样。事实证明，妇女一切都能干，一切都能干好。她特别说明了开办公共食堂、托儿所等福利事业的重要意义，要求妇女们不要轻视这些工作。蔡畅号召大家继续努力提高思想觉悟和文化技术水平，成为又红又专的多面手，争取在明年大跃进中作出更大成绩。
    参加这次会议的妇女积极分子有九百多人。会议还通过了北京市出席全国妇女建设社会主义积极分子代表会议的九十名代表的名单。
