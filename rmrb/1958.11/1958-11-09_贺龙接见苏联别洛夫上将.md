Title: He Long Receives Soviet General Belov
Author:
Date: 1958-11-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, November 8th: Vice Premier of the State Council He Long received General Belov, Chairman of the Soviet Union's Voluntary Association for Assistance to the Army, Navy, and Air Force, who is visiting China, on the afternoon of the 7th. After a cordial and friendly conversation, General Belov presented Vice Premier He Long with the highest honorary medal of the Soviet Union's Voluntary Association for Assistance to the Army, Navy, and Air Force.

General Belov will go to Shanghai today.

### 贺龙接见苏联别洛夫上将

1958-11-09
第3版()
专栏：

    贺龙接见苏联别洛夫上将
    新华社8日讯　国务院贺龙副总理7日下午接见了来我国访问的苏联支援陆海空军志愿协会主席别洛夫上将。在亲切而友好的谈话后，别洛夫上将将苏联支援陆海空军志愿协会最高荣誉奖章赠给贺龙副总理。
    别洛夫上将于今日去上海。
