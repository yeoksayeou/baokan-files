Title: 100-Kilometer Cycling Race Sets New World Record Again
Author:
Date: 1958-11-15
Page: 6

    100-Kilometer Cycling Race Sets New World Record Again
    (Our report) Tianjin Institute of Physical Education student Li Zengfu created an excellent time of two hours, ten minutes, and thirteen and seven-tenths seconds in the 100-kilometer cycling race held on the Beijing-Tianjin Highway on the morning of the 14th. Li Zengfu's time not only broke the world record of two hours, twenty minutes, and forty-four and eight-tenths seconds set by Italian Bannedetti, but was also more than a minute faster than the recent time of two hours, twelve minutes, and six and five-tenths seconds set by Tianjin athlete Gao Jukun.

### 百公里自行车赛  又创世界新成绩

1958-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    百公里自行车赛  又创世界新成绩
    本报讯　天津体育学院学员李增福14日上午在京津公路上举行的一百公里自行车比赛中，创造了二小时十分十三秒七的优异成绩。李增福这次的成绩不仅打破了意大利人班尼德蒂所创的二小时二十分四十四秒八的世界纪录，而且比最近天津运动员高聚坤所创的二小时十二分六秒五的成绩也快一分多。
