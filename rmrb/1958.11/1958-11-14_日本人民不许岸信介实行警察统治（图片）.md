Title: Japanese People Forbid Nobusuke Kishi from Implementing Police Rule (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-14
Page: 5

    The mass movement against revising the Police Law is vigorously unfolding in Japan. People from all walks of life in Japan are joining the struggle to oppose the Kishi Nobusuke government turning Japan into a police state.
  (1) Two thousand women in Tokyo held a demonstration on November 5.
  (2) Japanese cultural figures held a demonstration on November 4.
  (3) The demonstration procession of people from the Japanese drama and film circles.
  (4) People from the Japanese art world painted portraits for pedestrians on the streets to raise funds for the anti-Police Law struggle.

### 日本人民不许岸信介实行警察统治（图片）

1958-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    日本人民不许岸信介实行警察统治
    反对修改警察法的群众运动，在日本风起云涌地展开着。日本各阶层人民纷纷投入斗争，反对岸信介政府把日本变成警察国家。
  （1）东京两千妇女在11月5日举行示威。
  （2）日本文化界人士在11月4日举行示威。
  （3）日本戏剧电影界人士的示威队伍。
  （4）日本美术界人士在街头为行人画像，为反警察法斗争筹集基金。
