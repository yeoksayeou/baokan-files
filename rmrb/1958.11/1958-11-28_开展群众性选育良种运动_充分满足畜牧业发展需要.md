Title: Launch a Mass Movement to Select and Breed Fine Breeds to Fully Meet the Needs of Livestock Development
Author:
Date: 1958-11-28
Page: 3

(Reported by this newspaper) The National Animal Husbandry Work On-Site Conference, jointly convened by the Ministry of Agriculture, the Ministry of State Farms and Land Reclamation, and the Ministry of Commerce in Tieling County, Liaoning Province, closed on the 24th. The conference called on all localities to earnestly implement the principle of "combining indigenous and foreign methods, emphasizing selection and breeding, self-reliance, focusing on local solutions, and supplementing with external support and adjustment." It called for a mass movement to select and breed fine breeds in every people's commune, with everyone caring about fine breeds. The aim is to fundamentally meet the needs of the great development of animal husbandry for breeding livestock within two to three years. It also aims to continuously improve the quality of existing fine breeds of livestock so that our country has a large number of excellent breeding livestock, in order to universally improve the physique of livestock. The conference also decided to launch a magnificent mass livestock bumper harvest movement in 1959 to fight for a greater leap forward in livestock production.

The conference believes that great achievements have been made in animal breeding and improvement work across the country in recent years, but they still fall far short of meeting the current needs of the great development of animal husbandry for breeding livestock. This is mainly because many comrades have right-leaning conservative ideas about animal breeding work, especially in the breeding work line, there is a serious dogmatism of worshiping foreign breeds and belittling domestic breeds; relying only on a few scientific research institutions and farms to do breeding work behind closed doors, without organizing the broad masses of farmers and herdsmen to do it. This "few, slow, poor, and expensive" working method, which is divorced from reality and the masses, has greatly affected the speed of animal breeding development.

The conference pointed out that the supply of breeding livestock is a central issue in the great development of animal husbandry. To solve the problem of insufficient breeding livestock, the following principles must be followed: First, we must earnestly implement the mass line and launch a people's commune movement for selecting and breeding fine breeds. Second, the direction of breeding livestock must be determined based on the needs of socialist construction, agricultural production, and people's lives. Third, we must start from our own foundation and rapidly cultivate our own excellent new varieties. Fourth, we must actively carry out livestock breeding and improvement campaigns, combining popularization and improvement. Fifth, we must vigorously improve and improve feeding management. Sixth, we must comprehensively and actively carry out various livestock and poultry breeding work. However, at present, we should especially focus on the breeding of horses and sheep.

This conference also studied and arranged the overall livestock production work for 1959. The guiding principle for developing animal husbandry next year is: to highly develop both the quantity of livestock and universally improve the quality of livestock; to attach importance to the overall increase in livestock products and to accelerate the solution of the urgent needs of agriculture and transportation for livestock power. To this end, the conference called on all localities to launch a magnificent mass livestock bumper harvest movement in 1959 and comprehensively implement the eight fundamental measures to ensure the high-speed development of livestock: "water, grass, feed, breed, reproduction, management (feeding management), prevention (epidemic prevention), and technology (artificial insemination)."

The conference also studied and determined the allocation plan for breeding livestock and draft animals in 1959, and required all localities to strengthen the management of livestock markets. (Attached picture)

### 开展群众性选育良种运动  充分满足畜牧业发展需要

1958-11-28
第3版()
专栏：

    开展群众性选育良种运动  充分满足畜牧业发展需要
    本报讯　24日闭幕的由中央农业部、农垦部、商业部在辽宁省铁岭县联合召开的全国种畜工作现场会议，要求各地认真贯彻“土洋并举，选育并重，自力更生，就地解决为主，外地支援调剂为辅”的方针，开展一个社社培育良种、人人关心良种的群众性选育良种运动，争取在两三年内从根本上满足畜牧业大发展对种畜的需要，继续大力提高现有良种牲畜的品质，使我国拥有大量优良种畜，以便普遍提高牲畜体质。会议并且决定在1959年开展一个波澜壮阔的牲畜大群丰产运动，为促进畜牧业生产的更大跃进而斗争。
    会议认为：几年来全国各地种畜繁殖和改良工作取得了很大成绩，但是还远远满足不了当前畜牧业大发展对于种畜的需要。这主要是由于许多同志对种畜工作存在着右倾保守思想，特别是在培育种畜的工作路线上，严重存在着迷信外国良种轻视本国良种的教条主义；只是依靠少数科学研究机关和农牧场关起门来做育种工作，没有组织广大农牧民去干。这种脱离实际脱离群众的“少、慢、差、费”工作方法，大大地影响了种畜发展工作的速度。
    会议指出：种畜供应是畜牧业大发展中的一个中心问题。解决种畜不足的问题，必须遵循这样几个原则：首先，要认真贯彻群众路线，开展一个人民公社选育良种运动。其次，要从社会主义建设、农业生产及人民生活需要出发，来确定种畜培育方向。第三，必须要从本国的基础出发，迅速培育出自己的优良新品种。第四，要积极开展牲畜繁殖改良运动，把普及和提高结合起来。第五，要大力提高和改善饲养管理。第六，要全面地积极地进行各种畜禽育种工作。但是目前尤其应该以马和羊的育种工作作为重点。
    这次会议还研究安排了1959年全面的畜牧业生产工作。明年发展畜牧业的方针是：既要高度发展牲畜数量，又要普遍提高牲畜质量；既重视全面增产畜产品，又要加速解决农业、运输方面所迫切需要的牲畜动力问题。为此，会议要求各地要在1959年开展一个规模壮阔的牲畜大群丰产运动，全面贯彻执行保证高速度发展牲畜的“水、草、料、种、繁（殖）、管（饲养管理）、防（疫）、工（人工授精）”八项根本措施。
    会议还研究和确定了1959年种畜和耕畜的调剂方案，要求各地加强对牲畜市场的管理。（附图片）
