Title: Ding Xilin Hosts Banquet for Afghan Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-11-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 20th -- Ding Xilin, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a banquet this evening to welcome the Afghan Cultural Delegation headed by Dr. Ahmed Ali Achad. Ding Xilin delivered a speech at the banquet, warmly welcoming the delegation for bringing the precious friendship of the Afghan people and the sincere desire to further strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries. The head of the delegation, Ahmed Ali Achad, spoke at the banquet.

### 丁西林设宴招待阿文化代表团

1958-11-21
第5版()
专栏：

    丁西林设宴招待阿文化代表团
    据新华社20日讯　对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林今天晚间举行宴会，欢迎以阿哈默德·阿里·阿查德博士为首的阿富汗文化代表团。丁西林在宴会上致词，他热情地欢迎代表团带来了阿富汗人民的可贵友情和进一步加强两国友好关系的真诚愿望。代表团团长阿哈默德·阿里·阿查德在宴会上讲了话。
