Inexhaustible Solar Energy
Gao Shiqi

The sun is the commander of light and heat and the primary source of energy, driving away darkness and cold, and promoting the change and development of all things.

The sun is a very large celestial body, a ball of incredibly hot gas, with a diameter of 1.4 million kilometers, 110 times longer than the diameter of our Earth. Its surface temperature is 6,000 degrees Celsius, and its internal temperature reaches 20 million degrees Celsius.

At such high temperatures, the gases that make up the sun cannot exist in molecular form, but appear in atomic form.

At such high temperatures, violent atomic reactions take place inside the sun. The result of this reaction is that hydrogen nuclei are transformed into helium nuclei, releasing a large amount of energy.

According to scientists' calculations, 500 million tons of hydrogen nuclei are converted into helium nuclei inside the sun every second, and each gram of hydrogen nuclei converted into helium nuclei emits energy equivalent to the energy released by burning 15 tons of gasoline. Therefore, the total energy emitted by the sun is staggering.

This huge solar energy is radiated into space, passing through 150 million kilometers of vacuum and a thick atmosphere, and the energy that reaches the Earth's surface accounts for only one two-billionth of the total energy. Even so, the solar energy that reaches the land is as high as 1.7 billion kilowatts. This energy is equivalent to the total energy of 17 million power plants with a generating capacity of 100,000 kilowatts each.

They heat the air on high mountains, plains, cities, and villages… The heated air expands in volume and decreases in density, so it flies upward, and the cold and heavy air above runs to replenish it, so wind is generated on the ground.

They evaporate water from rivers, oceans, fields, and forests... and collect it in the clouds, turning it into sparse and dense raindrops that fall to the ground. Rainwater flows on the ground and converges into rivers of all sizes.

They have an inextricable relationship with green plants. Under the sunlight, the chlorophyll in green leaf cells turns carbon dioxide and water into starch to promote plant growth.

Due to the close relationship between solar energy and plants, we know that agricultural output is related to the utilization of solar energy per unit area each year. If solar energy is fully utilized, our current high-yield records will be greatly improved.

How to directly utilize solar energy?

In ancient times, our ancestors knew how to use the sun's heat to dry clothes and food.

From the time of the father of Russian science, Lomonosov (1711-1765), scientists have studied the problem of directly using solar energy. It was not until after the October Revolution that this research work obtained the necessary conditions for development.

Since 1936, Soviet experts have conducted a series of theoretical and experimental studies, and first created various solar-powered machines for heating, boiling, distilling, and drying fruits and vegetables; they invented solar-powered kitchens, underground heat-storing greenhouses, cooling devices, reflectors for treating diseases, steam boilers... and so on.

Now the utilization of solar energy has become a new and very important department of science and technology.

My country's solar energy industry is also developing on the basis of learning from the Soviet Union, and the first batch of solar energy utilizers has begun production.

These solar energy utilizers include solar water heaters, solar water boilers, solar cookers, solar bathrooms, solar dryers, and solar drying sterilizers, some of which are still in design.

The direct utilization of solar energy can save a lot of fuel. This is an important measure in developing the socialist economy.

### 用之不尽取之不竭的太阳能
高士其
1958-11-04
第8版()
专栏：知识小品

    用之不尽取之不竭的太阳能
    高士其
    太阳是光和热的统帅，是能的最主要的源泉，它驱走黑暗与寒冷，推动着万物的变化和发展。
    太阳是一个非常巨大的天体，是一团炽热无比的气体球，它的直径是一百四十万公里，要比我们地球的直径长一百一十倍，它的表面温度是摄氏六千度，它的内部温度达到二千万度。
    在这样高的温度下，组成太阳的气体不能以分子状态存在，而是以原子状态出现。
    在这样高的温度下，太阳内部进行着剧烈的原子反应，这种反应的结果：氢原子核转变成为氦原子核，而放出大量的能。
    根据科学家的核算，太阳内部每秒钟都有五亿吨氢原子核转变成为氦原子核，而每一克氢原子核转变成氦原子核的时候，所发出的能量就相等于燃烧十五吨汽油所发出的能量。这样，太阳所发出的总能量就很惊人了。
    这样巨大的太阳能，向宇宙空间射出，穿过一万五千万公里的真空区域，穿过深厚的大气层，射到地球表面的能量，只占总能量的二十亿分之一。而射到陆地上来的太阳能还有十七亿瓩之多。这些能量，相当于一千七百万座发电量为十万瓩发电厂的总能量。
    它们在高山、平地、城市和乡村……把空气蒸热。蒸热的空气体积胀大、密度缩小，就会向上飞腾，而上面的冷而重的空气就跑来补充，于是地面上就产生了风。
    它们从河流、海洋、田野和森林……把水分蒸发收留在云层里，变成疏疏密密的雨点，降落到地面上。雨水在地面上流动，又汇合成大大小小的河流。
    它们和绿色植物结了不解缘，绿叶细胞的叶绿素，在阳光的照耀下，就会把二氧化碳和水作成淀粉，以促进植物的生长。
    由于太阳能和植物的密切关系，我们就知道农业的产量，是同每年单位面积上的太阳能的利用有关的。如果把太阳能加以充分利用，我们现在的丰产纪录还会提高很多。
    怎样直接利用太阳能呢？
    在古老的年代里，我们的祖先，早就知道利用太阳的热来晒干衣服和食品了。
    从俄国科学之父——罗蒙诺索夫时代起（1711年—1765年），科学家们就曾对直接利用太阳能的问题加以研究，直到了十月革命以后，这种研究工作才获得必要的条件而发展起来了。
    从1936年起，苏联专家们进行了一系列的理论和实验的研究，并且首先创造了各种取暖、煮沸和蒸馏以及干燥水果、蔬菜的太阳能机械；发明了利用太阳能的厨房、地下蓄热的暖室、冷却装置、医疗疾病用的反射器、蒸汽锅炉……等。
    现在太阳能的利用，已经成为科学技术的一个新的非常重要的部门了。
    我国太阳能的工业，也在学习苏联的基础上发展起来了，第一批太阳能利用器已经开始生产了。
    这些太阳能利用器，包括太阳能热水器、太阳能沸水器、太阳能炊事器、太阳能浴室、太阳能干燥器和太阳能干燥消毒器，有的还在设计中。
    太阳能的直接利用，可以大量节约燃料，在发展社会主义经济上，这是一项重要的措施。
