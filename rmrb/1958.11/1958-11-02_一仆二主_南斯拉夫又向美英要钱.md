Title: Serving Two Masters: Yugoslavia Again Asks the US and Britain for Money
Author:
Date: 1958-11-02
Page: 4

    Serving Two Masters
    Yugoslavia Again Asks the US and Britain for Money
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 30th - The US State Department announced that Yugoslavia recently formally begged the United States for a new loan of $100 million.
    State Department Press Officer White revealed at a press conference that in addition to this loan, the Yugoslav government will also negotiate with US diplomatic representatives in Belgrade about begging for surplus US agricultural products, including wheat. Yugoslavia received $62.2 million in surplus agricultural products from the United States last year.
    White said that the US government is studying Yugoslavia's request.
    Xinhua News Agency, London, 1st - Yugoslav State Secretary for Foreign Affairs Popovic, who is visiting Britain, has concluded his talks with British Foreign Secretary Lloyd. Popovic has already left London on the 1st to return to Yugoslavia. It is reported that the main purpose of Popovic's visit to Britain was to beg Britain for a loan of 15 million pounds. On the same day that the communique was issued, Popovic said at a press conference in London that he had raised the issue of borrowing money from Britain during his talks with Lloyd. Popovic also said: "We hope to get a positive response as soon as possible so that we can know what we can count on when making plans. And I must say that Mr. Lloyd has expressed his understanding of this wish."

### 一仆二主  南斯拉夫又向美英要钱

1958-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    一仆二主
    南斯拉夫又向美英要钱
    新华社30日讯  华盛顿消息：美国国务院宣布，南斯拉夫最近正式向美国乞求一亿美元的新贷款。
    美国国务院新闻发布官怀特在记者招待会上透露，除了这笔贷款以外，南斯拉夫政府还将在贝尔格莱德同美国外交代表谈判乞讨美国剩余农产品问题，其中包括小麦。南斯拉夫去年从美国得到了六千二百万美元的剩余农产品。
    怀特说，美国政府正在研究南斯拉夫的这个要求。
    据新华社1日讯　伦敦消息：在英国访问的南斯拉夫外交国务秘书长波波维奇已经结束了同英国外交大臣劳埃德的会谈。波波维奇已经在1日离伦敦回南斯拉夫。据报道，波波维奇这次访问英国的主要目的，是向英国乞求一千五百万英镑贷款。公报发表的同一天，波波维奇在伦敦的记者招待会上说，他在同劳埃德会谈时候提出了向英国借款问题，波波维奇并且说：“我们盼望尽快得到积极的答复，以便在作计划时可以知道我们能够指望些什么。而且我必须说，劳埃德先生对这一愿望已经表示谅解”。
