Title: Men's 100km Cycling Time Shortened to Under Two Hours: Fujian Athletes Set New World Record
Author:
Date: 1958-11-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 26th November: On the morning of the 25th, at the Fujian Province Fuzhou, Xiamen, Quanzhou, Jinjiang Special District and Fujian Frontline Forces athletic and cycling competition held in Fuzhou, eight male athletes broke the national record for the men's 100km cycling event, with six of them surpassing the world's best time for the men's 100km cycling event set by Italian athlete Benedetti in 1942. Additionally, four female athletes broke the national record for the women's 25km cycling event.

In the men's 100km cycling event, Fuzhou tricycle worker Yan Chunhua (with a time of one hour, fifty-six minutes, thirty-eight and eight tenths seconds), Xiamen tricycle worker Xu Huigen (two hours, two minutes, thirty-two and six tenths seconds), Fujian Frontline Forces cycling coach Shan Changchun (two hours, six minutes, eight seconds), athlete Zhang Minghui (two hours, eight minutes, fifty-six seconds), Putian cycling worker Dai Meijiu (two hours, seventeen minutes, twenty-seven and four tenths seconds) and Zhangzhou athlete Xu Nanxing (two hours, nineteen minutes, forty-three and eight tenths seconds), who participated in the test, all surpassed the world record of two hours, twenty minutes, forty-four and eight tenths seconds. Furthermore, Xiamen tricycle worker Zhuang Yuguang and Nanping athlete Zhang Nengcai, who participated in the test, also broke the national record of two hours, fifty-one minutes, fifteen seconds for the men's 100km cycling event set by Qian Huaiyu in 1957, with times of two hours, twenty-three minutes, forty-two and eight tenths seconds and two hours, thirty-two minutes, fifty-one seconds respectively.

In the women's 25km cycling event, seventeen-year-old Li Yufeng, a student at Putian No. 2 Middle School (thirty-five minutes, twenty-one and two tenths seconds), Li Jingying (thirty-seven minutes, twenty-seven and two tenths seconds) and Hong Shubin (thirty-eight minutes, forty-one seconds), students at Xiamen Jimei Overseas Chinese补习 School, and Chen Guoshan (forty-three minutes, twenty and eight tenths seconds), a cadre of the Fujian Provincial Department of Commerce, broke the national record of forty-four minutes, two seconds for the women's 25km cycling event set by Cui Shufang in 1957.

This competition was organized by the Fujian Provincial Sports Commission and took place on a 10.5-kilometer road near Fuzhou, with the route being covered back and forth. The referees for this competition were national referees Weng Zulie, Chen Jinming, Cheng Tiansi and Chen Yongzuo.

### 男子百公里自行车赛成绩缩短到两小时以内  福建运动员创世界新成绩

1958-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    男子百公里自行车赛成绩缩短到两小时以内
    福建运动员创世界新成绩
    新华社福州26日电　25日上午，在福州市举行的福建省福州、厦门、泉州、晋江专区和福建前线部队等五个单位田径、自行车对抗赛中，有八位男运动员打破了男子百公里自行车比赛的全国纪录，其中有六人打破了意大利运动员班尼德蒂1942年创造的男子百公里自行车的世界最高成绩；另有四位女运动员打破了女子二十五公里自行车比赛的全国纪录。
    在男子百公里自行车比赛中，福州市三轮车工人严春华（成绩一小时五十六分三十八秒八），厦门市三轮车工人徐惠根（二小时二分三十二秒六），福建前线部队自行车教练员单长春（二小时六分八秒）、运动员张明会（二小时八分五十六秒），莆田自行车工人戴妹九（二小时十七分二十七秒四）和参加测验的漳州市运动员徐南星（二小时十九分四十三秒八）等六人的成绩都打破了二小时二十分四十四秒八的世界纪录。此外厦门市三轮车工人庄玉光和参加测验的南平市运动员张能才也分别以二小时二十三分四十二秒八和二小时三十二分五十一秒的成绩打破了1957年钱怀玉创造的男子百公里自行车二小时五十一分十五秒的全国纪录。
    在女子二十五公里的自行车比赛中，十七岁的莆田第二中学学生李玉凤（三十五分二十一秒二），厦门市集美华侨补习学校学生李景莹（三十七分二十七秒二）、洪淑彬（三十八分四十一秒）和福建省商业厅干部陈国珊（四十三分二十秒八）打破了1957年崔淑芳创造的女子二十五公里自行车比赛的四十四分二秒的全国纪录。
    这次比赛是由福建省体育运动委员会举办，在福州近郊十点五公里的公路上往返进行的，执行这次比赛裁判的是国家裁判员翁祖烈、陈金铭、程天泗和陈永祚。
