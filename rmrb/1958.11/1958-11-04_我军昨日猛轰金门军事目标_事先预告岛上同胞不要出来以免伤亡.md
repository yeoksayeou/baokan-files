Title: Our Army Fiercely Bombards Military Targets on Kinmen Yesterday, Pre-Warning Island Compatriots Not to Come Out to Avoid Casualties

Author:

Date: 1958-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 3rd. As of 4:00 PM today, a single day, our Fujian Front troops fired over ten thousand shells at the Kinmen Islands, fiercely bombarding military targets there. To ensure that compatriots, both military and civilian, on the Kinmen Islands avoid preventable casualties, our Fujian Front Headquarters broadcast the following timely warning to Kinmen yesterday (2nd): "Attention, military and civilian compatriots on the Kinmen Islands: Today, November 2nd, is an even day. We fired not a single shot, and you received supplies. Tomorrow, November 3rd, is an odd day, and you must not come out. Attention! Attention!"

### 我军昨日猛轰金门军事目标  事先预告岛上同胞不要出来以免伤亡

1958-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我军昨日猛轰金门军事目标
    事先预告岛上同胞不要出来以免伤亡
    新华社福建前线3日电　今日为单日，截至下午四时止，我福建前线部队对金门群岛发射了一万数千发炮弹，猛烈地轰击了该处的军事目标。为了使金门群岛军民同胞避免可以避免的伤亡，我福建前线司令部昨（2）日已经向金门作了如下的广播，提出了及时的预告：“金门群岛军民同胞们注意：今日，11月2号，是个双日，我们一炮未打，你们得到补给。明日，11月3号，是个单日，你们千万不要出来。注意！注意！”
