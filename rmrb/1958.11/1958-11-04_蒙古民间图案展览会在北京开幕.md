Title: Mongolian Folk Pattern Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-11-04
Page: 5

    Mongolian Folk Pattern Exhibition Opens in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: According to the 1958 implementation plan of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the "Mongolian Folk Pattern Exhibition" opened today at Zhongshan Park in Beijing.
    The exhibition's 183 exhibits are mostly Mongolian folk patterns, with a small number of paper-cuts and engravings. These works are simple in style and have strong national characteristics. They are all used to decorate daily necessities, horse gear, and buildings, beautifying people's lives and fully reflecting the Mongolian people's love for a happy life. These exhibits have been unanimously praised by visitors.

### 蒙古民间图案展览会在北京开幕

1958-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古民间图案展览会在北京开幕
    据新华社1日讯　根据中蒙文化合作协定1958年执行计划，“蒙古民间图案展览会”今天在北京中山公园开幕。
    这个展览会的一百八十三件展品，大部分是蒙古民间图案，也有少部分剪纸和刻纸。这些作品，风格淳朴，有浓厚的民族特色，并都是用以装饰日用品、马具和建筑物，美化人民生活的，充分反映了蒙古人民对幸福生活的热爱。这些展品受到参观者的一致赞赏。
