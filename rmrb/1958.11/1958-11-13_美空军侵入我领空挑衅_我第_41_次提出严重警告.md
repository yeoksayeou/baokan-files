Title: U.S. Air Force Intrudes into Our Airspace to Provoke; We Issue 41st Serious Warning

Author:
Date: 1958-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. On November 11th, a U.S. military transport aircraft, C-54 type, intruded into the airspace over the Lajiwei and other islands of the Wanshan Archipelago in Guangdong Province, circling for more than forty minutes. On the same day, a U.S. fighter jet, F-86D type, intruded into the territorial airspace over the areas of Matsu and Dongyin in Fujian Province. Regarding this continued military provocation by the United States, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 41st serious warning.

### 美空军侵入我领空挑衅  我第41次提出严重警告

1958-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    美空军侵入我领空挑衅
    我第41次提出严重警告
    新华社12日讯　11月11日美国军用运输机C—54型一架，侵入我国广东省万山群岛的垃圾尾等地上空，盘旋达四十余分钟之久。同日，美国战斗机F—86D型机一架，侵入我国福建省马祖、东引地区的领海上空。对于美国继续进行的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命第四十一次提出严重警告。
