Title: People Yearn for the Kremlin: Fourteen Million Visited in Over Three Years
Author:
Date: 1958-11-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 5th: According to TASS, since the Moscow Kremlin was opened to the public on July 20, 1955, it has received fourteen million visitors. Visitors admired the scenery of the Kremlin and visited rare historical buildings: the State Armory, the Cathedral of the Dormition, the Cathedral of the Annunciation, the Cathedral of the Archangel, and casting artworks from the 16th to the 18th centuries.

	Lenin's office and residence in the Kremlin are the most cherished places for the Soviet people. In recent years, tens of thousands of people have visited here, including a large number of foreign visitors.

	The Kremlin is a witness to many major historical events in the life of the Soviet people. The Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union are located here. The congresses of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the plenary sessions of the Central Committee of the CPSU, the meetings of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, and other important meetings are also held here.

	Currently, large-scale restoration work is underway in the Kremlin. Recently, another ancient Russian art building—a bishop's palace—was restored.

### 人心向往克里姆林宫  三年多来有一千四百万人前往参观

1958-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    人心向往克里姆林宫
    三年多来有一千四百万人前往参观
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科5日讯：莫斯科克里姆林宫自1955年7月20日开放以来，已经接纳了一千四百万观众。参观的人观赏了克里姆林宫的风光，参观了罕见的历史建筑物：国家兵器库、圣母升天大教堂、神告堂、天使堂以及十六世纪至十八世纪的铸造艺术品。
    列宁在克里姆林宫的办公室和住房是苏联人民最珍贵的地方。最近几年来，有数以万计的人参观了这里，其中有大批外国观众。
    克里姆林宫是苏联人民生活中发生的许多重大历史事件的见证者。苏联最高苏维埃和苏联部长会议就设在这里面。苏联共产党的代表大会、苏共中央全体会议、苏联最高苏维埃和俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃的会议以及其他一些重要的会议也是在这里举行的。
    目前，在克里姆林宫正在进行着大规模的修复工程。最近又修复了一个古俄罗斯艺术建筑物——一座主教宫。
