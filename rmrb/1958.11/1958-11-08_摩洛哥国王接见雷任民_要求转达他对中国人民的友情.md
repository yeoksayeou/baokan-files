Title: Moroccan King Receives Lei Renmin, Asks Him to Convey His Friendship to the Chinese People
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, November 4th: Moroccan King Mohammed V received Lei Renmin, head of the Chinese government trade delegation, and Ke Hua, deputy head, for the fourth time.

Lei Renmin congratulated the King on the recent establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Morocco and conveyed Chairman Mao Zedong's congratulations and gratitude to the King for his keen interest in developing friendly relations between the two countries. Lei Renmin also expressed his respect to the King for his contribution to Morocco's independence.

Mohammed V asked Lei Renmin to convey his friendly feelings to Mao Zedong and the Chinese people. He said that the friendly relations between the two countries had been recorded in history. Chinese tea will forever symbolize the bond of friendship between the peoples of the two countries.

The Moroccan King expressed his satisfaction with the trade agreement signed between the two governments. He said that this agreement embodies the policy of mutual respect and mutual benefit. He said that he was very pleased with the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries, as this would strengthen cooperation and friendly relations between China and Morocco.

The King expressed gratitude for China's participation in the Casablanca International Exposition. He hoped that China would be more involved in Morocco's economic activities.

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, November 5th: Lei Renmin, head of the Chinese government trade delegation, held a reception in Rabat on the evening of the 4th to celebrate the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Morocco.

Those present at the reception included Moroccan Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Economy Abd al-Rahim Bu Abid, Chairman of the National Consultative Assembly Ben Barka, Deputy Foreign Minister Busetta, and many foreign envoys stationed in Rabat.

The Chinese delegation left Rabat on the 5th and will make short visits to several cities in Morocco before returning home.

### 摩洛哥国王接见雷任民  要求转达他对中国人民的友情

1958-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥国王接见雷任民
    要求转达他对中国人民的友情
    新华社拉巴特4日电　摩洛哥国王穆罕默德五世第四次接见了中国政府贸易代表团正团长雷任民和副团长柯华。
    雷任民就最近中摩建立外交关系的事向国王表示祝贺，并且向国王转达了毛泽东主席的祝贺和对国王热衷于发展两国间友好关系一事的感谢。雷任民还向国王对摩洛哥独立所作的贡献表示敬意。
    穆罕默德五世要求雷任民转告他对毛泽东和中国人民的友好感情。他说，两国间的友好关系在历史上已有记载。中国的茶叶永远象征着两国人民间的友好纽带。
    摩洛哥国王对于两国政府签订的贸易协定表示满意。他说，这一协定体现了互相尊重和互利的政策。他说，他对于两国之间建立外交关系感到非常高兴，因为这将加强中摩两国的合作和友好关系。
    国王对于中国参加卡萨布兰卡国际博览会表示感谢。他希望中国将更多地参与摩洛哥的经济活动。
    新华社拉巴特5日电　中国政府贸易代表团团长雷任民4日晚上在拉巴特举行招待会，庆祝中摩两国建立外交关系。
    出席招待会的有摩洛哥副首相兼国民经济大臣阿卜杜勒·拉希姆·布阿比德，国民咨询议会主席本·巴尔卡，副外交大臣布塞塔和许多国家驻拉巴特的使节。
    中国代表团在5日离开拉巴特，代表团将在摩洛哥一些城市作短时间的访问后回国。
