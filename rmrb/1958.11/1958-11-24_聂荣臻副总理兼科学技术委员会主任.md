Title: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Concurrently Appointed as Director of the Science and Technology Commission

Author:
Date: 1958-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd. Chairman Mao Zedong, according to the decision of the 102nd meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, appointed Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen concurrently as Director of the Science and Technology Commission of the People's Republic of China.

### 聂荣臻副总理兼科学技术委员会主任

1958-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻副总理兼科学技术委员会主任
    新华社23日讯　毛泽东主席根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第一百零二次会议的决定，任命聂荣臻副总理兼任中华人民共和国科学技术委员会主任。
