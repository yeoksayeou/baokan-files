Title: Building Giant Ships, Singing Boat Songs
Author: Xing Lianming
Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 8

　　Building Giant Ships, Singing Boat Songs
　　Worker of Dalian Shipyard　Xing Lianming
　　Sesame flowers bloom higher and higher,
　　Red flags flutter in the east wind.
　　Folk songs are nice to hear, so I sing,
　　I build giant ships and sing boat songs.
　　Morning Song
　　The sun rises in the east, a sheet of red,
　　Thousands of troops march into action.
　　Pioneering the ten-thousand-ton ship,
　　Overwhelming England, America, and Japan.
　　Only beginning to climb to the summit,
　　Good news spreads, we take the lead.
　　Noon Song
　　Thousands of horses gallop with heroic spirit,
　　Forgetting sleep and food, working non-stop!
　　Seizing every second, racing against time,
　　To make a great contribution to the ten-thousand-ton!
　　Every inch of time is worth an inch of gold,
　　How can I feel at ease if I put down my work?
　　Evening Song
　　Evening glow flies, filling the sky with red,
　　Every drop of sweat is golden and glittering,
　　Ten thousand welding lights are like sky lanterns,
　　Old and young show their might,
　　Who says I'm old?
　　Living to see Communism, with plenty to spare.
　　Night Song
　　The Big Dipper has seven stars in the sky,
　　The shipyard is densely packed with stars,
　　The early winter sea breeze stings with cold,
　　Swinging the sledgehammer, sweeping away the north wind,
　　Turning night into day,
　　One day is equal to twenty years.
　　Ending Song
　　Ten-thousand-ton giant ship flies into the sky,
　　The Five-Star Red Flag rides the east wind,
　　Traveling ten thousand miles a day, spreading its wings,
　　Looking back and laughing at stupid America and England.
　　The Party's leadership produces heroes,
　　Heroic miracles are endless,
　　No matter how clever my tongue is like a lark,
　　It's hard to sing the praises of heroes completely.

### 造巨轮唱船歌
邢连明
1958-11-29
第8版()
专栏：

    造巨轮唱船歌
    大连造船厂工人　邢连明
    芝麻花开节节高，
    迎着东风红旗飘。
    山歌好听俺来唱，
    我造巨轮唱船歌。
    晨歌
    日出东方一片红，
    千军万马齐出动。
    首创纪录万吨船，
    压倒英美和日本。
    仅仅开始登尖端，
    捷报频传我占先。
    午歌
    万马奔腾气势雄，
    废寝忘食干不停！
    分秒不让抢时间，
    为了万吨立大功！
    寸寸光阴寸寸金，
    放下活儿怎安心？
    夕歌
    晚霞飞来满天红，
    滴滴汗珠金莹莹，
    万道焊光是天灯，
    老头小伙逞威风，
    谁说我老？
    活到共产主义有余零。
    夜歌
    天上北斗有七星，
    船台星星密层层，
    初冬海风刺人寒，
    横挥大锤扫北风，
    黑天当白天，
    一天等于二十年。
    尾歌
    万吨巨轮飞天空，
    五星红旗乘东风，
    日行万里展鹏程，
    回首笑望笨美英。
    党的领导出英雄，
    英雄奇迹多无穷，
    任我嘴巧如百灵，
    也难唱尽英雄颂。
