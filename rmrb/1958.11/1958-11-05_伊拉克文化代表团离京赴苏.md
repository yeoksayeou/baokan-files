Title: Iraqi Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-05
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, November 4th. The Iraqi cultural delegation, led by Abud Zarzara, left Beijing by plane this morning for a visit to the Soviet Union, after visiting various places in China for more than a month.
    Among those who saw them off at the airport were Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and many others.

### 伊拉克文化代表团离京赴苏

1958-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克文化代表团离京赴苏
    新华社4日讯　以阿布德·扎尔扎拉团长为首的伊拉克文化代表团在我国各地参观访问了一个多月以后，已在今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往苏联访问。
    到机场送行的，有对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊等多人。
