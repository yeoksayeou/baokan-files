Title: Latin American Countries, Eager to Shake off the Impact of Capitalist Market Depression, Take Advantage of the East Wind
Author:
Date: 1958-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th, New York Dispatch: Trade between Latin American countries and socialist countries this year will increase significantly compared to 1957. According to the recently published "Latin American Business News," the total value of Latin American countries' exports to the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, Hungary, Romania, and Albania will increase by 30% compared to last year, reaching 160 million US dollars. The total value of Latin American countries' imports from socialist countries may reach 100 million US dollars, an increase of 20% compared to last year.

The publication said that due to the sluggishness of the capitalist countries' markets, "Latin American countries are increasingly eager to find new markets for their products." The publication pointed out, "This appears to be one of the main factors in the increase in trade between Latin American countries and socialist countries."

### 渴望摆脱资本主义市场不景气的影响  拉丁美洲国家借东风

1958-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    渴望摆脱资本主义市场不景气的影响
    拉丁美洲国家借东风
    新华社9日讯　纽约消息：今年拉丁美洲国家和社会主义国家之间的贸易将比1957年显著增加。据最近出版的“拉丁美洲商业要闻”报道说，拉丁美洲国家向苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、匈牙利、罗马尼亚和阿尔巴尼亚的出口总值将比去年增加30％，达到一亿六千万美元。拉丁美洲国家从社会主义国家的进口总值可达一亿美元，比去年增加20％。
    这个刊物说，由于资本主义国家市场的不景气，“拉丁美洲国家愈来愈渴望为他们的产品寻找新的市场”。刊物指出，“看来这是拉丁美洲国家和社会主义国家贸易增加的主要因素之一”。
