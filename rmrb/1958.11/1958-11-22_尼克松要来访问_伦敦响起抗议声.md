    Nixon to Visit, Protests Ring Out in London

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 18th. Under the auspices of the London District Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain, a demonstration was held here to oppose the upcoming visit of US Vice President Nixon to London.

    Demonstrators carried signs reading "Nixon Go Home!" and "Nixon Means War!" in the busiest commercial district of London's West End. They called on all patriots to participate in a protest rally to be held on the 25th (the day Nixon is scheduled to arrive) to send Nixon packing.

### 尼克松要来访问  伦敦响起抗议声

1958-11-22
第5版()
专栏：瘟神临门人人厌

    尼克松要来访问  伦敦响起抗议声
    据新华社伦敦电　在英国共产党伦敦区委员会的发起下18日这里举行了示威游行，反对美国副总统尼克松即将来伦敦访问。
    示威者掮着“尼克松滚回去！”“尼克松就是战争！”的标语牌在伦敦西城最繁华的商业区举行了游行。他们号召一切爱国人士参加将于25日（即尼克松预定到达的那一天）举行的抗议大会，把尼克松轰回去。
