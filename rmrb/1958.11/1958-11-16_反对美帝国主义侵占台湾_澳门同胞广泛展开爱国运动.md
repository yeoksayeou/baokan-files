Title: Macao Compatriots Widely Launch Patriotic Movement Against US Imperialism's Occupation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1958-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 15th: For more than two months, Macao compatriots from all walks of life have widely launched a patriotic movement against US imperialism's occupation of our territory, Taiwan, and have used various means to expose the aggressive crimes of US imperialism.

After Premier Zhou Enlai's statement on the situation in the Taiwan Strait area was published, Macao's labor unions, organizations, schools, neighborhood organizations, etc. held hundreds of gatherings to protest against US imperialism's aggressive activities in the Taiwan Strait area. By the end of October, more than 80,000 Macao compatriots had participated in various protest rallies. Well-known figures and representatives from all walks of life in Macao have also published talks in newspapers, strongly condemning the aggressive crimes of US imperialism. More than 30,000 people also signed their names to express their determination to oppose US imperialism's aggression.

On November 2nd, the Macao Chinese Women's Federation held a garden party to oppose US imperialism's aggression and expose its aggressive crimes, with more than 4,000 women from all walks of life participating in the gathering. Macao's seafarers, tourism, bus, Western food and bread, theater, water and electricity, and shoe industry trade unions, as well as other social organizations, have also recently held various gatherings to oppose US imperialism's aggression against Taiwan.

### 反对美帝国主义侵占台湾  澳门同胞广泛展开爱国运动

1958-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    反对美帝国主义侵占台湾
    澳门同胞广泛展开爱国运动
    新华社广州15日电　澳门各界同胞两个多月来广泛展开了反对美帝国主义侵占我国领土台湾的爱国运动，并运用各种方式揭露美帝国主义的侵略罪行。
    周恩来总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明发表以后，澳门各工会、社团、学校、街坊组织等就举行了数以百计的集会，抗议美帝国主义在台湾海峡地区的侵略活动。到10月底止，澳门同胞已经有八万多人参加了各种抗议集会。澳门的社会著名人士和各阶层代表人物，也纷纷在报纸上发表谈话，强烈谴责美帝国主义的侵略罪行。有三万多人还签名表示他们反对美帝国主义侵略的决心。
    澳门中华妇女联合会11月2日举行了一个反对美帝国主义侵略和揭露美帝国主义侵略罪行的游园会，有四千多名各界妇女参加了这个集会。澳门的海员、旅业、公共汽车、西菜面包、戏院、水电和鞋业等各业工会及其他社会团体最近也分别举行了反对美帝国主义侵略台湾的各种集会。
