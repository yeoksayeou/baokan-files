Title: Bulgarian and Czechoslovakian Party and Government Delegations Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1958-11-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: According to Bulgarian newspapers, the Bulgarian and Czechoslovakian party and government delegations began talks in Sofia on the 21st.
    The Bulgarian side participating in the talks included: Zhivkov, Yugov, R. Damyanov, Staiko, and four others. The Czechoslovakian side participating in the talks included all members of the party and government delegation headed by Novotny.
    The talks proceeded in a very cordial atmosphere with complete consensus.
    Before the talks began, Novotny led all members of the delegation in paying respects to Bulgarian party and government leaders Zhivkov and Yugov. The delegation also laid a wreath at the Dimitrov Mausoleum.

### 保捷两国党政代表团会谈

1958-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    保捷两国党政代表团会谈
    据新华社讯　据保加利亚报纸报道，保加利亚和捷克斯洛伐克两国党政代表团21日在索非亚开始举行会谈。
    保加利亚方面参加会谈的是：日夫科夫、于哥夫、拉·达米扬诺夫、斯塔伊科夫等七人。捷克斯洛伐克方面参加会谈的是以诺沃提尼为首的党政代表团全体人员。
    会谈是在非常亲切和意见完全一致的气氛中进行的。
    会谈开始前，诺沃提尼曾率领全体代表团人员拜会了保加利亚党政领导人日夫科夫和于哥夫。代表团并曾在季米特洛夫陵墓前献了花圈。
