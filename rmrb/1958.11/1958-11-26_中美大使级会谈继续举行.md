Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 25th. The 84th meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw at 2:00 pm today.

    The meeting lasted one hour and fifty-three minutes.

    After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would continue on December 12th (Friday) at 2:00 pm.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1958-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社华沙25日电　中美两国大使级会谈第八十四次会议今天下午二时在华沙举行。
    本次会议历时一小时五十三分。
    会后双方宣布，下次会议将在12月12日（星期五）下午二时继续举行。
