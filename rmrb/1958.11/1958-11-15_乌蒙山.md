Title: Wumeng Mountain
Author: Chen Yongjin
Date: 1958-11-15
Page: 8

　　Wumeng Mountain
　　Chen Yongjin
　　
　　Wumeng Mountain is shrouded in heavy fog,
　　In the heavy fog comes the mighty army.
　　Each soldier has a strong will,
　　Urging the Iron Marshal to swiftly raise his banner.

　　Wumeng Mountain is misty with rain,
　　In the misty rain rise the furnaces.
　　Ten thousand furnaces rise from the ground,
　　At the sound of the order, satellites are launched.

　　Wumeng Mountain is pouring with rain,
　　What can the torrential rain do to us?
　　The army waves its arms, and Wumeng moves,
　　The grand plan will surely be realized soon.

　　Wumeng Mountain is covered with green pines,
　　Pine waves rise, and the west wind is cool.
　　Let the west wind increase a thousandfold,
　　An iron will is hard to break.

　　Wumeng's cliffs are steep and dangerous,
　　The Red Army's heroic name is passed down on the mountain.
　　Remembering the Red Army's battle on Wumeng,
　　Spirits are high, and strength is endless.

　　Wumeng Mountain is ablaze with red light,
　　The red light shines upon the long sky.
　　Even more delighted is the night on Wumeng today,
　　Sparks fly to welcome the festival.

　　Wumeng Mountain is fluttering with red flags,
　　The red flags flutter as iron stars fly.
　　The army sings with abandon,
　　The molten iron surpasses the Pan River in length.

### 乌蒙山
陈勇进
1958-11-15
第8版()
专栏：

    乌蒙山
    陈勇进
    乌蒙山上雾沉沉，
    沉沉雾里来三军。
    三军个个意志强，
    力促铁帅忙升帐。
    乌蒙山上雨蒙蒙，
    蒙蒙雨中起熔炉。
    万座熔炉平地起，
    一声号令放卫星。
    乌蒙山上雨滂沱，
    滂沱大雨奈我何。
    三军挥臂乌蒙动，
    雄伟谋划定早成。
    乌蒙山上松苍苍，
    松涛起处西风凉。
    任你西风加万倍，
    意志如铁也难摧。
    乌蒙绝壁悬崖险，
    红军山上英名传。
    忆起红军战乌蒙，
    精神抖擞力无穷。
    乌蒙山上火光红，
    红红火光映长空。
    更喜今日乌蒙夜，
    铁花飞溅迎佳节。
    乌蒙山上飘红旗，
    红旗飘飘铁星飞。
    三军纵情齐欢唱，
    铁水胜过盘水长。
