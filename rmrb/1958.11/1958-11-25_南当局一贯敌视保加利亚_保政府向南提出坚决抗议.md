Title: South Authorities Consistently Hostile to Bulgaria, Bulgarian Government Lodges Firm Protest with South

Author:
Date: 1958-11-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Bulgarian government delivered a verbal note to the Yugoslav government on the 19th, firmly protesting the Yugoslav authorities' consistent policy of hostility towards the Bulgarian people. The note reminded the Yugoslav government that the policy it was pursuing "is not conducive to improving relations between the two countries, but only benefits the enemies of peace and mutual understanding among the Balkan peoples."

This note is related to the recent anti-Bulgarian people and the People's Republic of Bulgaria movement vigorously carried out by Yugoslavia surrounding the 40th anniversary of the Dobro Pole Battle and the 42nd anniversary of the Kajmakcalan Battle.

The note stated, "Many facts show that Yugoslavia has recently been carrying out a widespread and irresponsible hostile movement against the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the leading organs of the Bulgarian Communist Party, against the Bulgarian people and all Bulgarians. Participating in this smear campaign are not only Yugoslav journalists and 'historians,' but also the most prominent leaders of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia—Tito, Kolisevski, Vukmanovic, and other leading figures."

The note refutes the Yugoslav government's claim that Bulgaria interfered in Yugoslavia's internal affairs and points out that, on the contrary, facts show that Yugoslavia did indeed attempt to interfere in Bulgaria's internal affairs.

The note finally reminds the Yugoslav government that it will bear full responsibility for the adverse consequences that may arise from its consistent policy of hostility towards the Bulgarian people.

### 南当局一贯敌视保加利亚  保政府向南提出坚决抗议

1958-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    南当局一贯敌视保加利亚
    保政府向南提出坚决抗议
    据新华社讯　保加利亚政府19日向南斯拉夫政府提出口头照会，坚决抗议南斯拉夫当局一贯执行敌视保加利亚人民的政策。照会提醒南斯拉夫政府注意，它奉行的“这种政策不利于改善两国之间的关系，而只对和平和巴尔干各国人民相互谅解的敌人有利”。
    这项照会同南斯拉夫最近围绕多勃罗—波列战役四十周年和喀马查兰战役四十二周年而大力进行的反对保加利亚人民和保加利亚人民共和国的运动有关。
    照会说，“有许多事实表明，最近南斯拉夫正在进行广泛而不负责任的敌对运动，反对保加利亚人民共和国和保加利亚共产党的领导机关，反对保加利亚人民和全体保加利亚人。参加这一诽谤运动的不仅有南斯拉夫的新闻记者和“历史学家”，而且有南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟的最显赫的领导人——铁托、科利谢夫斯基、伏克曼诺维奇和其他领导人士。”
    照会驳斥南斯拉夫政府所谓保加利亚干涉南斯拉夫内政的一些说法，并指出，相反地，事实表明，南斯拉夫的确企图干涉保加利亚内政。
    照会最后提醒南斯拉夫政府注意，它要对由于它一贯执行敌视保加利亚人民的政策而可能产生的不良后果负全部责任。
