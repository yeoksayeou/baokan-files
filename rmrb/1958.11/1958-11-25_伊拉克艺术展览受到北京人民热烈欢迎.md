Title: Iraqi Art Exhibition Warmly Welcomed by Beijing People
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-25
Page: 5

    Iraqi Art Exhibition Warmly Welcomed by Beijing People
    Xinhua News Agency, 24th - The "Art Exhibition of the Republic of Iraq" concluded in Beijing today. The exhibition began on November 3rd.
    The exhibition was warmly welcomed by the people of Beijing. More than 100,000 people visited the exhibition. After visiting, many viewers left comments in the opinion book, congratulating the Iraqi people on the great victory they achieved in the struggle against imperialism.
    The Iraqi government has donated some of the exhibits to our people as souvenirs.

### 伊拉克艺术展览受到北京人民热烈欢迎

1958-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克艺术展览受到北京人民热烈欢迎
    新华社24日讯　“伊拉克共和国艺术展览会”今天在北京结束。这个展览会是11月3日起举行的。
    这个展览会受到了北京市民的热烈欢迎。有十万多人参观了展览会。许多观众参观后还在意见本上留下意见，祝贺伊拉克人民在反对帝国主义斗争中获得的伟大胜利。
    伊拉克政府已经将展览会的部分展品赠给我国人民作为纪念品。
