Title: Finnish Ambassador to China, Sundström, is ill; Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen visit the hospital to inquire.
Author:
Date: 1958-11-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Finnish Ambassador to China, Sundström, is currently hospitalized due to illness. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi visited the hospital on the afternoon of the 5th to express their condolences and concern to Ambassador Sundström.

### 芬兰驻华大使孙士敦患病  周总理陈外长去医院探问

1958-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰驻华大使孙士敦患病
    周总理陈外长去医院探问
    新华社5日讯　芬兰驻华大使孙士敦因病正在医院治疗，周恩来总理和陈毅副总理兼外交部长于5日下午前往医院看望，向孙士敦大使表示慰问和关切。
