Title: Darkness Will Eventually End, Light Will Surely Arrive: The CPC Central Committee Sends a Congratulatory Message to the Communist Party of Greece on its 40th Anniversary, Wishing the KKE Greater Achievements in the Struggle for Peace, Democracy, and Social Progress
Author:
Date: 1958-11-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 16th: November 17th marks the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Greece. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China today sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Greece, expressing congratulations. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Greece,
Dear Comrades:

On the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Greece, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, on behalf of all members of the Communist Party of China, extends fraternal congratulations to all members of the Communist Party of Greece.

Since its establishment, the Communist Party of Greece has waged a resolute struggle for the freedom, democracy, and social progress of the Greek people. During the Second World War, the Communist Party of Greece, together with the people, fought bloody battles to resist the fascist bandits. Immediately after the war, the Communist Party of Greece, in extremely difficult circumstances, unyieldingly carried out a tenacious armed struggle of self-defense to defend Greece's independence, freedom, and social progress, and later carried out arduous underground work. The Communist Party of China expresses its infinite admiration for the heroic struggle and indomitable spirit of the Greek Communists.

This year, the Greek people's patriotic democratic movement has made significant progress. This progress is inseparable from the efforts made by the Communist Party of Greece in striving for and consolidating the unity of all patriotic and democratic forces. It vividly demonstrates the irrepressible rise of the Greek people's desire for peace, democracy, and social progress, and the prestige and influence of the Communist Party of Greece is growing day by day. Darkness will eventually end, and light will surely arrive.

We wish the Communist Party of Greece greater achievements in uniting all forces that can be united, in the struggle to free Greece from imperialist control, to strive for domestic political democracy and social progress, and to strive for peace in the Balkans and the world.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 16, 1958

### 黑暗终有尽头　光明必会到来  中共中央电贺希共成立四十周年  祝希共在争取和平、民主和社会进步中获得更大成就

1958-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    黑暗终有尽头　光明必会到来
    中共中央电贺希共成立四十周年
    祝希共在争取和平、民主和社会进步中获得更大成就
    新华社16日讯　11月17日是希腊共产党成立四十周年纪念。中国共产党中央委员会今天打电报给希腊共产党中央委员会，表示祝贺。电报全文如下：希腊共产党中央委员会亲爱的同志们：
    欣逢希腊共产党成立四十周年纪念，中国共产党中央委员会谨代表中国共产党全体党员向希腊共产党全体党员致以兄弟般的祝贺。
    希腊共产党自成立以来，就为争取希腊人民的自由、民主和社会进步进行了坚决的斗争。在第二次世界大战期间，希腊共产党同人民一起，浴血奋战，抵抗了法西斯强盗。在战争刚结束以后，希腊共产党又在极其困难的处境下，不屈不挠地为保卫希腊的独立、自由和社会进步而进行了顽强的自卫的武装斗争，以后又进行了艰苦的地下工作。中国共产党对于希腊共产党人的英勇奋斗和坚毅不屈精神，表示无限的钦佩。
    今年希腊人民的爱国民主运动，有了显著的进展，这个进展同希腊共产党对于争取和巩固一切爱国民主力量的团结所作的努力分不开的。它生动地表明了希腊人民对于和平、民主和社会进步的愿望不可抑止的高涨，而希腊共产党的威望和影响也正在日益增长。黑暗终有尽头，光明必会到来。
    祝希腊共产党在团结一切可能团结的力量，为争取希腊摆脱帝国主义的控制，争取实现国内政治民主和社会进步，争取巴尔干和世界和平的斗争中获得更大的成就。
                     中国共产党中央委员会　1958年11月16日
