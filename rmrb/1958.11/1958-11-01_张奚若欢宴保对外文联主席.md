Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts a Banquet for the Bulgarian Chairman of the Foreign Cultural Relations Committee

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency: Ms. E. Avramova, Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Friendly and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing on the 29th at the invitation of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Zhang Xiruo, Chen Zhongjing and others were at the airport to welcome her.

On the 31st, Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet to welcome her. Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Chen Zhongjing, Secretary-General, and others attended the banquet. Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Mr. Panchevski and his wife, attended the banquet.

### 张奚若欢宴保对外文联主席

1958-11-01
第5版()
专栏：

    张奚若欢宴保对外文联主席
    据新华社讯　应对外文化联络委员会邀请来华访问的保加利亚对外友好与文化联络委员会主席埃·阿芙拉莫娃于29日抵京。前往机场欢迎的有张奚若、陈忠经等人。
    31日对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若设宴欢迎。出席宴会的有对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、秘书长陈忠经等。保加利亚驻我国大使潘切夫斯基夫妇出席了宴会。
