Title: Iraqi Government Arrests Arif; Iraqi Public Supports Government's Strong Action Against Conspirators
Author:
Date: 1958-11-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, November 4th: Iraqi Republic Radio broadcast tonight a statement by Iraqi Republic Armed Forces Commander-in-Chief Qassem announcing that Arif, the Iraqi Republic's Ambassador to West Germany, was arrested in Baghdad on the 4th.

The announcement stated that Arif was arrested "in view of the needs of public interest and repeated attempts to disrupt public order." The announcement said: "Arif will be brought to trial on charges of conspiracy against the security of the motherland, so that everyone knows that the interests of the people and the security of the Iraqi Republic are above personal interests."

The announcement also stated that Arif was arrested after returning to Baghdad without authorization and consent.

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, November 4th: Iraqi Republic Radio broadcast many telegrams tonight from all over the country expressing support for the government's arrest of Iraqi Ambassador to West Germany, Arif.

These telegrams congratulated the government for taking strong action against conspirators who endanger the security of the young Iraqi Republic.

Some telegrams demanded severe punishment for the conspirators.

### 伊拉克政府逮捕阿里夫  伊公众支持政府对阴谋分子采取有力行动

1958-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克政府逮捕阿里夫
    伊公众支持政府对阴谋分子采取有力行动
    新华社巴格达4日电　伊拉克共和国电台今天晚间广播的伊拉克共和国武装部队总司令卡塞姆发表的一项公告宣布，伊拉克共和国驻西德大使阿里夫4日在巴格达被逮捕。
    公告宣布，逮捕阿里夫，是“鉴于公共利益的需要和屡次企图破坏公共治安”。公告说：“将以对祖国安全进行阴谋活动的罪名将阿里夫交付审判，以使大家知道：人民的利益和伊拉克共和国的安全高于个人利益。”
    公告还说，阿里夫是在未经调动和同意的情况下擅自回到巴格达以后被逮捕的。
    新华社巴格达4日电　伊拉克共和国电台今天晚间广播了很多国内各地发来的表示支持政府逮捕伊拉克驻西德大使阿里夫的电报。
    这些电报表示祝贺政府对危害年青的伊拉克共和国的安全的阴谋分子采取强有力的行动。
    有一些电报要求严惩阴谋者。
