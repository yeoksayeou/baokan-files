Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Encourages Democratic Parties to Persist in Continuously Carrying Out Fundamental Self-Reform
Author:
Date: 1958-11-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Vice Premier Chen Yi delivered an important report this morning to the representatives attending the National Congresses of various democratic parties. He encouraged the democratic parties and their members to closely follow the new situation of the great leap forward in the socialist construction of the motherland, and to persist in continuously carrying out thorough and fundamental self-reform.

The report meeting today was organized by the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. In his nearly four-hour report, Vice Premier Chen Yi spoke on several issues, including studying Comrade Mao Zedong's document "On Imperialism and All Reactionaries Are Paper Tigers," the issue of democratic parties carrying out fundamental self-reform, and the issue of people's communes.

More than 1,700 people listened to Vice Premier Chen Yi's report. Among them were the leaders of various democratic parties, as well as non-party democratic personages.

### 陈毅副总理勉励民主党派  坚持不断进行自我根本改造

1958-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理勉励民主党派
    坚持不断进行自我根本改造
    新华社26日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午向出席各个民主党派全国代表大会的代表等，作了重要的报告。他勉励民主党派和他们的成员紧紧跟上祖国社会主义建设大跃进的新形势，坚持不断地进行彻底的自我根本改造。
    今天的报告会是由中共中央统一战线工作部组织的。陈毅副总理在近四个小时的报告中，讲了关于学习毛泽东同志“论帝国主义和一切反动派都是纸老虎”文件的若干问题，关于民主党派进行自我根本改造的问题以及人民公社问题。
    有一千七百多人听了陈毅副总理的报告。其中有各民主党派的负责人、以及无党派民主人士等。
