Title: Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai Send Telegram to Hoxha, Leshi, and Shehu Congratulating Albania on the 14th Anniversary of Liberation. Foreign Minister Chen Yi Sends Telegram to Foreign Minister Shtulla to Express Congratulations

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a cable to Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Comrade Hadji Leshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, and Comrade Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, congratulating Albania on the 14th anniversary of its liberation. The telegram reads as follows:
To Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Comrade Hadji Leshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, Comrade Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers:

	On the occasion of the 14th anniversary of the liberation of Albania, we, on behalf of the Chinese people, the Chinese Communist Party, and the Chinese government, extend our warm and fraternal congratulations to the Albanian people, the Albanian Labor Party, and the Albanian government.

	The hardworking and brave Albanian people, with their glorious revolutionary traditions, have made tremendous achievements in the noble cause of building socialism and defending European and world peace under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party and the government of the People's Republic of Albania for the past fourteen years. In the struggle against modern revisionism, the Albanian Labor Party and the people, loyal to the principles of proletarian internationalism, have demonstrated an unwavering stance and have contributed to safeguarding the purity of Marxism-Leninism and consolidating the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union. The Chinese people regard all the achievements of the fraternal Albanian people as their own and are proud of them.

	The fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Albanian peoples is developing and consolidating further. The Albanian government and people have given strong support to the Chinese people's struggle against the intensified military provocations and war threats of U.S. imperialism. The Chinese people are very grateful for this fraternal support. We are deeply convinced that the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Albanian peoples, built on the foundation of proletarian internationalism, will last forever.

	We wish the Albanian people new and greater achievements in the struggle to build socialism and defend world peace.

	Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong
	Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi
	Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
					November 28, 1958, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, today sent a telegram to Comrade Behar Shtylla, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Albania, congratulating Albania on the 14th anniversary of its liberation. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Behar Shtylla, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Albania:

	On the occasion of the 14th anniversary of the liberation of Albania, please accept my warmest congratulations, Comrade Minister.

	I wish you greater success in the noble cause of strengthening the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, in implementing the peaceful foreign policy of the Albanian government, and in the struggle to safeguard European and world peace.

	Long live the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Albanian peoples!

	Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi
	November 28, 1958, in Beijing

### 毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来电霍查、列希、谢胡  祝贺阿尔巴尼亚解放十四周年  陈毅外长致电什图拉外长表示祝贺

1958-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来电霍查、列希、谢胡
    祝贺阿尔巴尼亚解放十四周年
    陈毅外长致电什图拉外长表示祝贺
    新华社28日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理今天打电报给阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩·霍查同志、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈·列希同志、部长会议主席穆·谢胡同志，祝贺阿尔巴尼亚解放十四周年。电文如下：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记
  恩·霍查同志，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团
  主席哈·列希同志，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆·谢胡同志：
    当阿尔巴尼亚解放十四周年纪念日的时候，我们谨代表中国人民、中国共产党和中国政府向阿尔巴尼亚人民、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和阿尔巴尼亚政府致热烈的、兄弟的祝贺。
    具有光荣革命传统的、勤劳勇敢的阿尔巴尼亚人民，十四年来，在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府的领导下，在建设社会主义、保卫欧洲和世界和平的崇高事业中取得了巨大的成就。在反对现代修正主义的斗争中，忠于无产阶级国际主义原则的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和人民表现了坚定不移的立场，对捍卫马克思、列宁主义的纯洁和巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结作出了贡献，中国人民把兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民的一切成就都看成是自己的成就并且以此而感到自豪。
    中、阿两国人民的兄弟友谊正在进一步的发展和巩固。阿尔巴尼亚政府和人民对中国人民反对美国帝国主义对我国加紧军事挑衅和战争威胁的斗争给予了有力的支持，对这种兄弟支持，中国人民十分感谢。我们深信，建筑在无产阶级国际主义基础上的中、阿两国人民之间的兄弟友谊将万古常青。
    祝阿尔巴尼亚人民在建设社会主义和保卫世界和平的斗争中取得新的、更大的成就。
    中国共产党中央委员会主席　中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长　刘少奇
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                      1958年11月28日于北京
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天致电阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部长贝·什图拉同志，祝贺阿尔巴尼亚解放十四周年。电文如下：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部长贝·什图拉同志：
    值此阿尔巴尼亚解放十四周年之际，部长同志，请接受我最热烈的祝贺。
    祝你在致力于加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营团结的崇高事业中，在执行阿尔巴尼亚政府的和平外交政策和维护欧洲和世界和平的斗争中取得更大的成就。
    中、阿两国人民之间的牢不可破的兄弟友谊万岁！
                            中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                            1958年11月28日于北京
