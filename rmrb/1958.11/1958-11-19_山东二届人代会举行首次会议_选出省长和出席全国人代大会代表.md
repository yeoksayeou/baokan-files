Title: Shandong Second People's Congress Holds First Meeting, Elects Governor and Representatives to National People's Congress

Author:
Date: 1958-11-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 18th. The first session of the Second People's Congress of Shandong Province was held from October 27 to November 2.

The meeting listened to and discussed the work report made by Tan Qilong on behalf of the Provincial People's Committee. It believed that the people of Shandong had made great achievements in socialist construction in the past year. An unprecedented bumper harvest was achieved in agricultural production. It is estimated that the total grain output will reach 80 billion jin, more than quadrupling last year's output, with an average of about 1,500 jin of grain per person; the total cotton output will reach 12 million dan, an increase of nearly three times over last year; peanut, flue-cured tobacco, hemp and other cash crops and animal husbandry have all achieved unprecedented leaps forward. The leap forward situation in industrial production is also very encouraging. Township and commune industries in Shandong have blossomed everywhere. From January to September, the total industrial output value of the province reached 5.45 billion yuan, accounting for 101.9% of the guaranteed target for the year, and one and a half times the total industrial output value for the whole of 1957. Under the guidance of the policy of taking steel as the key link, Shandong Province can overfulfill the annual steel production plan of one million tons. Finance and trade, transportation, culture and education, and scientific research have also achieved rapid development.

The meeting held that the above achievements were the result of correctly implementing the general line of socialist construction, the victory of the all-people rectification and the anti-rightist struggle, and the victory of opposing decentralism, localism, and right-leaning opportunism.

The meeting pointed out that the current general task of Shandong Province is to continue to implement the Party's general line of socialist construction, deepen the technical revolution and cultural revolution, launch a new and greater upsurge in production and construction on the basis of consolidating and improving the people's communes, ensure the completion and overfulfillment of various tasks this year, and make all preparations for a greater leap forward next year, and strive to fundamentally change the face of Shandong a year ahead of schedule.

The meeting elected Tan Qilong as Governor of Shandong Province by secret ballot, and Bai Rubing, Yang Xuanwu, Deng Chenxi, Chao Zhefu, Liu Minsheng, Li Chengzhi, Li Yu'ang, Yu Xiu, Zhang Zhusheng, Wang Zhe, Miao Hainan, and Li Yuchao as Vice Governors. Wang Yunsheng was elected as the President of the Shandong Provincial Higher People's Court, and Ding Zixun and 42 others were elected as members of the Provincial People's Committee. Seventy-seven people were elected as representatives to the Second National People's Congress. They are: Ding Lide, Wang Shoudao, Wang Xuncai, Wang Shenlin, Wang Meigong, Deng Tuo, Wei Que, Qiu Jin, Liu Changsheng, Liu Ningyi, Liu Minsheng, Liu Huimin, Liu Xianzhi, Liu Tonggao, Sun Xiaocun, Zhu Xi, Zhu Xuefan, Chen Shaomin, Chen Lei, Chen Mengyuan, Li Kezuo, Li Chengzhi, Li Shunzhang, Wu Defeng, Gu Mu, Shen Yanbing, Yu Xinqing, Bi Dexian, Lü Hongbin, Jin Ming, Jin Baozhen, Zhou Ren, Lin Zun, Hu Yaobang, Hu Sheng, Fan Wenlan, Fan Shiren, Zhao Dan, Lang Xianfen, Hao Jianxiu, Xu Bing, Xu Yunbei, Xu Shigao, Xu Zuoxia, Xu Meisheng, Xu Jianchun, Xia Yan, Xia Nai, Guo Yonghuai, Guo Yicheng, Chao Zhefu, Kang Sheng, Xu Zhizhen, Zhang Tianmin, Zhang Zhaomei, Zhang Hanying, Zhang Gongzhi, Zhang Xi, Cui Dexi, Zeng Shan, Shu Tong, Tong Dizhou, Feng Yuanjun, Yang Dezhi, Yang Yunyu, Dong Bian, Cai Banghua, Zang Kejia, Teng Jinglu, Teng Huchen, Qian Ying, Qian Zhiguang, Qian Changzhao, Xie Juezai, Wei Xiuying, Tan Qilong, Feng Yunhe.

Prior to the election, the meeting revoked the qualifications of rightists Ding Ling and Zhang Dongmu as representatives to the First National People's Congress, removed rightists Wang Zhuoru and Yuan Ziyang from their positions as Vice Governors, and removed Fan Yusui, Zhang Dongmu, Wu Guangrong, and Wang Yuecun from their positions as members of the Provincial People's Committee.

### 山东二届人代会举行首次会议  选出省长和出席全国人代大会代表

1958-11-19
第6版()
专栏：

    山东二届人代会举行首次会议
    选出省长和出席全国人代大会代表
    新华社济南18日电　 省第二届人民代表大会第一次会议于10月27日到11月2日举行。
    会议听取并讨论了谭启龙代表省人民委员会所作的工作报告，认为：山东人民一年来在社会主义建设事业上获得了巨大的成就。农业生产空前大丰收，预计粮食总产量可达八百亿斤，比去年翻了两番还多，平均每人有粮食一千五百斤左右；棉花总产量可达一千二百万担，比去年增长近三倍；花生、烤烟、大麻等经济作物和畜牧业都获得了空前的跃进。工业生产的跃进形势也很令人鼓舞。山东的乡社工业遍地开花，1至9月份全省工业总产值完成了五十四亿五千万元，为年度计划必成数的101.9％，为1957年全年工业产值的一倍半。在以钢为纲的方针指导下，山东省全年一百万吨钢铁生产计划可以超额完成。财贸、交通运输、文化教育和科学研究工作，也都得到了飞跃的发展。
    会议认为，上述成就的获得，是正确贯彻执行社会主义建设总路线的结果，是全民整风、反右派斗争的胜利，是反对分散主义、地方主义、右倾机会主义的胜利的结果。
    会议指出山东省当前的总任务是：继续贯彻执行党的社会主义建设总路线，深入开展技术革命和文化革命，在巩固提高人民公社的基础上，发动新的更大的生产建设高潮，保证完成和超额完成今年的各项任务，并为明年更大跃进做好一切准备，为提前一年从根本上改变山东面貌而奋斗。
    会议以无记名投票方式，选出谭启龙为山东省省长，白如冰、杨宣武、邓辰西、晁哲甫、刘民生、李澄之、李予昂、余修、张竹生、王哲、苗海南、李宇超等十二人为副省长，选出王云生为山东省高级人民法院院长，丁子勋等四十二人为省人民委员会委员。选出七十七人为第二届全国人民代表大会代表，他们是：丁履德、王首道、王洵才、王深林、王美恭、邓拓、韦悫、丘金、刘长胜、刘宁一、刘民生、刘惠民、刘先志、刘同诰、孙晓村、朱洗、朱学范、陈少敏、陈雷、陈孟元、李克佐、李澄之、李顺章、吴德峰、谷牧、沈雁冰、余心清、毕德显、吕鸿宾、金明、金宝珍、周仁、林遵、胡耀邦、胡绳、范文澜、范式人、赵丹、郎咸芬、郝建秀、徐冰、徐运北、徐士高、徐佐夏、徐眉生、徐建春、夏衍、夏鼐、郭永怀、郭贻诚、晁哲甫、康生、许之桢、张天民、张兆美、张含英、张公制、张玺、崔德锡、曾山、舒同、童第周、冯沅君、杨得志、杨蕴玉、董边、蔡邦华、臧克家、滕景禄、滕虎忱、钱瑛、钱之光、钱昌照、谢觉哉、魏秀英、谭启龙、鄷云鹤。
    会议在选举以前撤销了右派分子丁玲、张东木第一届全国人民代表大会代表资格，罢免了右派分子王卓如、袁子扬的副省长职务和范予遂、张东木、吴光荣、王月村四人的省人民委员会委员职务。
