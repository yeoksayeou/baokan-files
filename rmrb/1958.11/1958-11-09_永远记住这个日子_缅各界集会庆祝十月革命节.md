Title: Always Remember This Day: Burmese Circles Gather to Celebrate the October Revolution

Author:

Date: 1958-11-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 7th. Burmese parties and the Burma-Soviet Cultural Association held a gathering in Rangoon on the 7th to celebrate the 41st anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

　　U Aung Than, Chairman of the Burmese National United Front, who presided over the meeting, said in his speech that the reason we celebrate this great festival is because the October Revolution brought immeasurable benefits to those people who, like us, once lived as slaves under the shackles of colonialism. He said that the October Revolution made a great contribution to our cause of anti-aggression struggle, and it shines like a beacon guiding us forward. We should not and cannot forget this day, and at the same time, we should remember this day forever with great respect. In his speech, U Aung Than also talked about the impressions he gained during his visit to the Soviet Union.

### 永远记住这个日子  缅各界集会庆祝十月革命节

1958-11-09
第3版()
专栏：

    永远记住这个日子
    缅各界集会庆祝十月革命节
    新华社仰光7日电　缅甸各党派和缅苏文化协会7日在仰光举行集会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十一周年。
    主持集会的缅甸民族团结阵线主席吴昂丹在会上发表演说说，我们所以要庆祝这个伟大的节日，是因为十月革命给那些同我们一样一度在殖民主义束缚下过着奴隶生活的人民带来了不可估量的好处。他说，十月革命对我们反侵略斗争的事业做出了巨大的贡献，它像指路明灯一样照耀着我们前进。我们不应该而且也不能忘记这个日子，同时要怀着极大的敬意永远记住这个日子。吴昂丹在演说中还谈到他在访问苏联期间所获得的印象。
