Title: Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement Next Year Implementation Plan Signed, Zhang Xiruo Holds Banquet to See Off Avramova
Author:
Date: 1958-11-28
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The 1959 implementation plan of the China-Bulgaria Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed this morning in Beijing. Representing the two sides were Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of China, and Ye. Avramova, Chairman of the Committee for Friendly and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of Bulgaria.
    The cultural cooperation between China and Bulgaria in 1959 will see new developments compared to 1958. The 1959 implementation plan stipulates that China and Bulgaria will send medical workers, educators, writers, artists, playwrights, journalists and filmmakers to visit each other's countries next year, send art performers to perform, mutually translate and publish each other's literary works and other books, exchange information on cultural education, and continue to promote direct contacts and cooperation between universities and educational research institutions of both sides. In addition to the current students and graduate students in each other's countries who will continue to study in the academic year 1959-1960, the two countries will also send some more students or graduate students to each other next year.
    Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Panchevsky, and Zhang Zhixiang and Qu Wu, Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of China, attended today's signing ceremony.
    Zhang Xiruo and Ye. Avramova both spoke after the signing, warmly congratulating the signing of the 1959 implementation plan.
    Xinhua News Agency, 27th: Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a banquet at noon today to see off Ye. Avramova, Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Friendly and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, who is about to end her visit and leave China.
    Zhang Xiruo and Ye. Avramova delivered speeches at the banquet, warmly congratulating the further consolidation and development of cultural exchanges and friendly cooperation between China and Bulgaria, and congratulating the friendship and unity of the two peoples.
    Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Panchevsky, was invited to attend the banquet.
    Zhang Zhixiang, Qu Wu, and Luo Jun, Deputy Directors of the Foreign Cultural Commission, Liu Ruilong, President of the China-Bulgaria Friendship Association, Zhou Zhuan, former Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, and heads of relevant departments attended the banquet.

### 中保文化合作协定明年执行计划签字  张奚若设宴欢送阿弗拉莫娃

1958-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中保文化合作协定明年执行计划签字
    张奚若设宴欢送阿弗拉莫娃
    新华社27日讯　中国保加利亚文化合作协定1959年执行计划今天上午在北京签订。代表双方签字的是我国对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若和保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会主席叶·阿弗拉莫娃。
    中保两国1959年的文化合作将比1958年有新的发展。 1959年执行计划中规定，中保双方明年将互派医学工作者、教育工作者、作家、美术家、戏剧家、新闻工作者和电影工作者等到对方的国家访问，互派艺术表演家进行演出，相互翻译和出版对方国家的文学作品和其他书籍，交换有关文化教育的资料，继续促进双方高等学校与教育研究机构建立直接的联系与合作。两国除目前在对方国家的留学生和研究生将在1959到1960这个学年中继续学习外，明年还将互相增派一些留学生或研究生。
    保加利亚驻中国大使潘切夫斯基和我国对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、屈武等参加了今天的签字仪式。
    张奚若和叶·阿弗拉莫娃在签字后都讲了话，热烈祝贺1959年执行计划的签字。
    新华社27日讯　对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若今天中午举行宴会，欢送即将结束访问离开我国的保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会主席叶·阿弗拉莫娃。
    张奚若和叶·阿弗拉莫娃在宴会上先后致词，热烈祝贺中保两国文化交流和友好合作关系的进一步巩固与发展，祝贺两国人民的友谊与团结。
    保加利亚驻中国大使潘切夫斯基应邀出席宴会。
    对外文委副主任张致祥、屈武、罗俊，中保友好协会会长刘瑞龙，我国前任驻保加利亚大使周竹安和有关部门负责人出席宴会作陪。
