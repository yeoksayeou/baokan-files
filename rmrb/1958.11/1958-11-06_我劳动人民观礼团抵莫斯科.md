Title: Our Laboring People's Observation Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1958-11-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 4th: The Chinese Laboring People's Observation Delegation, consisting of eighteen members, arrived in Moscow by plane on the afternoon of November 3rd, invited to celebrate the 41st anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Gorshkov, Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Rogov, Editor-in-Chief of the "Soviet-Chinese Friendship" magazine.

Li Zewang, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, and others also welcomed them at the airport.

Gorshkov delivered a welcome speech at the airport.

Qian Ying, the deputy head of the delegation, also spoke.

Liu Lantao, the head of the delegation, will arrive in Moscow from Denmark in the next few days. Delegation members Xie Bingxin and Liu Xuexin have already arrived earlier.

### 我劳动人民观礼团抵莫斯科

1958-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    我劳动人民观礼团抵莫斯科
    据新华社莫斯科4日电　应邀前来庆祝十月社会主义革命四十一周年的中国劳动人民观礼团一行十八人，3日下午乘飞机抵莫斯科。
    到机场迎接代表团的有苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫、苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席团副主席戈尔什科夫、“苏中友好”杂志总编辑罗果夫等人。
    中国驻苏联大使馆参赞李则望等也到机场迎接。
    戈尔什科夫在机场致欢迎词。
    代表团副团长钱瑛也讲了话。
    代表团团长刘澜涛日内即将由丹麦到达莫斯科，团员谢冰心和刘学新已先期到达。
