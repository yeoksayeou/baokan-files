Title: The Government of the Republic of Iraq Announces that the Width of Iraqi Territorial Waters is Twelve Nautical Miles
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 18th: The government of the Republic of Iraq promulgated a decree today stipulating that the width of Iraqi territorial waters is twelve nautical miles. The decree stipulates that if the territorial waters of Iraq overlap with the territorial waters of neighboring countries, the boundary of the territorial waters will be determined through agreement.

### 伊拉克共和国政府宣布  伊拉克领海宽度为十二浬 

1958-11-19
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克共和国政府宣布
    伊拉克领海宽度为十二浬
    新华社巴格达18日电　伊拉克共和国政府今天颁布一项法令，规定伊拉克领海的宽度为十二海里。法令规定，如果伊拉克的领海区域同邻近国家的领海区域有重叠的时候，将通过协议来决定领海的界线。
