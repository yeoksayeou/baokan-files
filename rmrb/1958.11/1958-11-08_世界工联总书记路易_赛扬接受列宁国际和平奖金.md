Title: World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Louis Saillant Receives Lenin International Peace Prize
Author:
Date: 1958-11-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 5th: World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Louis Saillant received the "Strengthening International Peace" Lenin International Prize in Moscow on the 4th. The award ceremony was held in the Kremlin.

    Representatives from all walks of life in Moscow attended the award ceremony.

    Academician Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the Lenin International Peace Prize Committee, presented the prize to Saillant and congratulated him. Skobeltsyn said that Louis Saillant is a representative of the working class and the most organized labor force in the world's majority of countries, standing at the forefront of the struggle for peace and social progress. He said: "Under the leadership of Louis Saillant, the World Federation of Trade Unions has consistently linked its activities to safeguard the interests of workers with the struggle for peace, easing international tensions, and striving for mutual approach and cooperation among countries."

    WFTU Secretary Peña spoke at the ceremony. He talked about Saillant's contribution to the cause of peace.

    Saillant delivered a reply. He emphasized that the lofty honor of the Lenin International Peace Prize should first belong to the World Federation of Trade Unions and the French working class.

    Grishin, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Brazhenov, member of the Presidium of the Soviet Peace Committee, and others congratulated Saillant. People presented him with souvenirs. (Attached picture)

### 世界工联总书记路易·赛扬接受列宁国际和平奖金

1958-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工联总书记路易·赛扬接受列宁国际和平奖金
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬4日在莫斯科接受了“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金。授奖典礼在克里姆林宫举行。
    出席授奖典礼的有莫斯科各界代表。
    列宁国际和平奖金委员会主席斯科贝尔琴院士把奖金授予赛扬，并且向他表示祝贺。斯科贝尔琴说，路易·赛扬是工人阶级、世界大多数国家站在争取和平和社会进步最前线的、最有组织的劳动大军的代表。他说：“由于路易·赛扬的领导，世界工会联合会把它维护职工利益的活动经常同争取和平、缓和国际紧张局势、争取各国互相接近与合作的斗争联系在一起。”
    参加典礼的世界工联书记培尼亚在会上讲了话。他谈到了赛扬对和平事业所作的贡献。
    赛扬致了答词。他强调说，列宁国际和平奖金这种崇高的荣誉，首先应该属于世界工会联合会，属于法国工人阶级。
    全苏工会中央理事会主席格里申、苏联保卫和平委员会主席团委员布拉任诺夫等人纷纷向赛扬表示祝贺。人们向他赠送了纪念品。（附图片）
