Title: Polish Weightlifting Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-11-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th—The Polish weightlifting team, led by Haler Mieczysław, Director of the International Department of the Polish Supreme Sports Committee, arrived in Beijing at 3:20 PM on November 27th. They will participate in the weightlifting competition among China, the Soviet Union, and Poland held in Beijing.

The twelve athletes on this weightlifting team are all of Poland's best weightlifters.

### 波兰举重队到京

1958-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰举重队到京
    据新华社27日讯　波兰最高体育委员会国际司司长哈勒·密支斯拉夫率领波兰举重队，于27日下午三时二十分到达北京。他们将参加在北京举行的中、苏、波三国举重比赛。
    这个举重队的十二个运动员，都是波兰最优秀的举重选手。
