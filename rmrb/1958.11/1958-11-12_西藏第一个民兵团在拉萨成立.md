Title: Tibet's First Militia Regiment Established in Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1958-11-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, November 11th: Tibet's first militia regiment was inaugurated in Lhasa City on the 8th. This militia regiment is composed of Tibetan and Han staff members from various institutions in Lhasa, many of whom are veterans who marched into Tibet eight years ago.

### 西藏第一个民兵团在拉萨成立

1958-11-12
第6版()
专栏：

    西藏第一个民兵团在拉萨成立
    据新华社拉萨11日电　西藏高原上的第一个民兵团8日在拉萨市宣布成立。这个民兵团由拉萨市各机关藏、汉族工作人员组成，其中很多人是八年前进军西藏时的老战士。
