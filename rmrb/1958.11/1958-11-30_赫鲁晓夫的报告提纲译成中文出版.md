Title: Khrushchev's Report Outline Translated into Chinese and Published
Author:
Date: 1958-11-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The full text of Comrade Khrushchev's report outline on the "Control Figures for the Development of the Soviet National Economy in 1959-1965," which he is preparing to deliver at the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, has been translated into Chinese, and a separate edition has been published by the People's Publishing House. To meet the needs of the broad masses of readers, this book will be printed simultaneously in Beijing, Shanghai, Chongqing, Wuhan, Shenyang, and Xi'an.

### 赫鲁晓夫的报告提纲译成中文出版

1958-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫的报告提纲译成中文出版
    新华社29日讯　赫鲁晓夫同志准备在苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会上作的关于“1959——1965年发展苏联国民经济的控制数字”的报告提纲全文已译成中文，单行本已由人民出版社出版。为了满足广大读者需要，本书将同时在北京、上海、重庆、武汉、沈阳、西安等地印造。
