Title: Khrushchev Once Stated that the Soviet Union Would Aid Iraq's Peaceful Construction
Author:
Date: 1958-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 29th: According to TASS, Youssef, head of the Iraqi peace delegation, said at a press conference in Moscow on the 28th that Soviet Premier Khrushchev recently stated during a meeting with the Iraqi peace delegation that the Soviet Union would provide Iraq with assistance in all peaceful construction endeavors.

The press conference was arranged by the Soviet Peace Defense Committee. At the conference, Youssef spoke about the delegation's meeting with Khrushchev.

Youssef said that it is very clear to us that the Soviet government and its leader Khrushchev support the struggle of people of all countries for peace and resolutely condemn the imperialist conspiracies in the Arab East.

Youssef also said that Khrushchev emphasized that the struggle of the people of all countries for peace is also a struggle for freedom and progress.

At the conference, Youssef emphasized the goodwill of the Soviet people and government towards the national liberation struggles of the Arab peoples. All Soviet people warmly sympathize with the Iraqi people who are striving for freedom and independence.

At the press conference, the Iraqi peace delegation also jointly issued a statement with the Soviet Peace Defense Committee and the Soviet Association for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Arab Eastern Countries.

The statement pointed out that the struggle of the Arab peoples for national liberation, against imperialist aggression, and to safeguard national independence and sovereignty is also a struggle to safeguard universal peace.

### 赫鲁晓夫曾经表示  苏联援助伊拉克和平建设

1958-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫曾经表示
    苏联援助伊拉克和平建设
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社报道：伊拉克和平人士代表团团长优素福28日在莫斯科举行的一次记者招待会上说，苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫最近在同伊拉克和平人士代表团会晤时曾经表示，苏联将在一切和平建设方面给伊拉克以援助。
    这次招待会是苏联保卫和平委员会安排的。优素福在会上谈到了代表团同赫鲁晓夫会见的情况。
    优素福说，苏联政府和它的首脑赫鲁晓夫支持各国人民争取和平的斗争，并且坚决谴责帝国主义在阿拉伯东方的阴谋活动，这对我们来说是很显然的。
    优素福还说，赫鲁晓夫曾经强调指出，各国人民争取和平的斗争也是争取自由和进步的斗争。
    优素福在会上强调指出了苏联人民和政府对阿拉伯各国人民的民族解放斗争的善意态度。所有的苏联人都热烈地同情争取自由和独立的伊拉克人民。
    在这次招待会上，伊拉克和平人士代表团还同苏联保卫和平委员会和苏联同阿拉伯东方国家友好和文化联系协会共同发表了一项声明。
    声明指出，阿拉伯各国人民争取民族解放、反对帝国主义侵略行动、维护民族独立和主权的斗争，同时也是维护普遍和平的斗争。
