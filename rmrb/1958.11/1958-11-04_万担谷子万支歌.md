Title: Ten Thousand Loads of Millet, Ten Thousand Songs

Author:

Date: 1958-11-04
Page: 8

    Ten Thousand Loads of Millet, Ten Thousand Songs
  Millet is high,
  Songs are plentiful,
  Ten thousand loads of millet, ten thousand songs,
  Singing a song on the upper ridge,
  Loading several baskets of grain on the lower ridge,
  Carts and boats transport grain like shuttles.
    (Baisha Zhou, Yuanjiang, Hunan)

### 万担谷子万支歌

1958-11-04
第8版()
专栏：

    万担谷子万支歌
  谷子高，
  歌也多，
  万担谷子万支歌，
  上垅刚唱歌一首，
  下垅又装谷几箩，
  车船运谷像穿梭。
    （湖南沅江白沙州）
