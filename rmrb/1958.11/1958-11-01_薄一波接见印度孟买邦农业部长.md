Title: Bo Yibo Receives Indian Bombay State Agriculture Minister
Author:
Date: 1958-11-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: Vice Premier Bo Yibo received Naik, the Agriculture Minister of the Bombay State Government of India, who was invited to visit China by the Chinese Society of Agronomy, along with his wife and secretary, Moreet, this morning.

Yang Xiandong, Chairman of the Chinese Society of Agronomy, was present at the meeting.

Konnampilly, Chargé d'Affaires of the Indian Embassy in China, was also present at the meeting.

### 薄一波接见印度孟买邦农业部长

1958-11-01
第5版()
专栏：

    薄一波接见印度孟买邦农业部长
    新华社31日讯　薄一波副总理今日上午接见了由中国农学会邀请来我国访问的印度孟买邦政府农业部长纳伊克和夫人及秘书莫里特。
    陪同接见的有中国农学会理事长杨显东。
    接见时印度驻华大使馆代办康南皮利也在座。
