Title: U.S. Aircraft Intrudes into China's Territorial Airspace; 43rd Serious Warning Issued
Author:
Date: 1958-11-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 29th: On November 29th, a batch of two U.S. F-104 fighter planes intruded into the territorial airspace over the areas of Mawei and Pingtan in Fujian Province on three separate occasions. Regarding this continued military provocation by the United States, a spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 43rd serious warning.

### 美国飞机侵入我领海上空  我提第四十三次严重警告

1958-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    美国飞机侵入我领海上空
    我提第四十三次严重警告
    新华社29日讯　11月29日美国战斗机F—104机型一批两架，先后三次侵入我福建省马祖、平潭地区的领海上空，对于美国继续进行的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命第四十三次提出严重警告。
