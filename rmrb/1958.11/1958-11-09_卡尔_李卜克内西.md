Title: Karl Liebknecht
Author:
Date: 1958-11-09
Page: 3

    Karl Liebknecht
    Berlin has not and will never forget him,
    He led the people and fought the enemy.
    Today in every place,
    The masses are growing, storming against the chains.
    The world of slaves—the world of the masters is terminally ill,
    It is crumbling and heading for destruction. (Attached image)

### 卡尔·李卜克内西

1958-11-09
第3版()
专栏：

    卡尔·李卜克内西
    柏林没有并且永远不会将他遗忘，
    他领导着人民，同敌人较量。
    今天在任何一个地方，
    群众都在成长，暴风雨般向锁链冲撞。
    奴隶世界——老爷的世界已病入膏育，
    它摇摇欲坠，趋向灭亡。（附图片）
