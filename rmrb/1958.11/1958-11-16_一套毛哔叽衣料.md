    A Wool Serge Suit's Worth of Fabric
    1.6 to 1.7 kilograms of raw wool (unwashed) can be spun into one meter of woolen fabric. Approximately four kilograms of raw wool can be woven into enough fabric for a suit (calculated at 2.5 meters). A head of improved sheep (descendants of fine wool and semi-fine wool sheep crosses) can generally produce around four kilograms of wool per year. According to this calculation, one head of improved sheep can supply the raw material to weave a wool serge suit for people each year.

### 一套毛哔叽衣料

1958-11-16
第7版()
专栏：

    一套毛哔叽衣料
    一点六至一点七公斤原毛（没有洗净羊毛），可以纺织一公尺毛料，四公斤左右的原毛，可以织成够做一套衣服的料子（按二公尺五计算）。一头改良羊（用细毛、半细毛羊杂交的后代），一般每年可以生产四公斤左右羊毛。根据这样计算，一头改良羊，每年可以为人们供应纺织一套毛哔叽的原料。
