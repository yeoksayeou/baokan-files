Title: German Writer Arnold Zweig Accepts Lenin International Peace Prize

Author:

Date: 1958-11-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 19th: According to TASS, the distinguished German writer and peace fighter Arnold Zweig accepted the “Strengthening International Peace” Lenin International Prize in Moscow on the 18th. The award ceremony was held in the Kremlin.

Academician Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the Lenin International Prize Committee, presented the prize to Zweig and extended warm congratulations to him.

Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, Popova, Chairperson of the Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the poet Surkov then delivered congratulatory speeches.

Zweig delivered a reply.

### 德国作家茨威格  接受列宁国际和平奖金

1958-11-20
第5版()
专栏：

    德国作家茨威格
    接受列宁国际和平奖金
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：杰出的德国作家、和平战士阿诺德·茨威格18日在莫斯科接受了“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金。授奖典礼在克里姆林宫举行。
    列宁国际奖金委员会主席斯科贝尔琴院士把奖金授给茨威格后，向他表示热烈的祝贺。
    苏联保卫和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫、苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席团主席波波娃、诗人苏尔科夫等人相继致贺词。
    茨威格致了答辞。
