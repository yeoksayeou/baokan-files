    Butadiene-Sodium Rubber
    Rubber is divided into two major categories: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber is extracted from rubber plants. Synthetic rubber has been developed in the last twenty years. So far, there are many varieties of synthetic rubber, the most important and commonly used ones being butadiene-sodium, butadiene-styrene, butyl, nitrile, chloroprene, and silicone rubber. The raw materials for these synthetic rubbers are relatively cheap and readily available. For example, petroleum cracking gas, natural gas, coal, agricultural and sideline products, and wild plants can all be made into synthetic rubber after chemical treatment.

    Butadiene-sodium rubber is one of the earliest and simplest types of synthetic rubber. Its main raw material is alcohol.

    Butadiene-sodium rubber has the characteristics of general synthetic rubber and can be used alone or mixed with natural rubber to make a variety of rubber products, such as tires, bicycle tires, rubber tubes, flat belts, rain cloths, and raincoats. Rubber shoes made of butadiene-sodium rubber are shiny and durable. (Attached picture)

### 丁钠橡胶

1958-11-23
第6版()
专栏：工业知识

    丁钠橡胶
    橡胶分为天然橡胶和合成橡胶两大类。天然橡胶是从橡胶植物中提炼出来的。合成橡胶是近二十几年发展起来的。到目前为止，合成橡胶已有很多品种，最主要和最常用的有丁钠、丁苯、丁基、丁腈、氯丁和硅橡胶等。这些合成橡胶的原料都比较便宜易得。例如石油裂化气、天然气、煤、农副产品和野生植物等，只要经过化学处理，都能做成合成橡胶。
    丁钠橡胶是合成橡胶中最早和最简单的一种。其主要原料是酒精。
    丁钠橡胶具有一般合成橡胶的特性，可以单独或与天然橡胶混合后制成多种橡胶制品，例如轮胎、自行车胎、胶管、平带以及雨布、雨衣等。用丁钠橡胶制造的胶鞋，光亮而耐穿。（附图片）
