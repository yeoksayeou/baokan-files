Title: Salutations! Comrade Xie Erjiuqinke
Author: Wu Lue
Date: 1958-11-16
Page: 8

Salutations! Comrade Xie Erjiuqinke,
    I seem to see your broad brow,
    Late at night, tirelessly thinking for a Miao language plan;
    I seem to see your blue eyes,
    Staring at one letter for a long time before moving to another.

    Salutations! Comrade Xie Erjiuqinke,
    Your child needs your caress;
    Your students are waiting for you to solve their difficulties,
    For the sake of our Miao children for generations to come,
    You do not hesitate to travel across mountains and rivers,
    Traveling thousands of miles to the Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau.

    Salutations! Comrade Xie Erjiuqinke,
    What shall we use to express our respect?
    The bull horn cup is only two inches deep,
    It cannot hold our overflowing feelings;
    The lusheng only has six notes,
    It cannot express our grateful hearts.

    Salutations! Comrade Xie Erjiuqinke,
    When you come to the Miao family for the second time,
    We will no longer present wordless silk banners to friends,
    Nor will we use our mouths to pass on ancient lyrics,
    The children will write your name in Miao,
    And the young people at the dating grounds will also express their love in letters.

    Salutations! Comrade Xie Erjiuqinke,
    The Miao family follows the Russian nation forward,
    The Qingshui River and the Volga sing the same song,
    We don't need to erect a monument for you,
    The Miao language is eternal friendship.

### 敬礼！谢尔玖琴柯同志
伍略
1958-11-16
第8版()
专栏：

    敬礼！谢尔玖琴柯同志
    苗族　伍略
    敬礼！谢尔玖琴柯同志，
    我仿佛看见你宽大的前额，
    深夜，在为一篇苗文方案不倦地思考；
    我仿佛看见你兰色的眼睛，
    久久地盯着一个字母再移向另一个字母。
    敬礼！谢尔玖琴柯同志，
    你的孩子正需要你的抚爱；
    你的学生等待你去解答疑难，
    为了我们苗家子孙万代，
    你不辞劳苦涉水跋山，
    迢迢千里来到了云贵高原。
    敬礼！谢尔玖琴柯同志，
    用什么来表示我们的敬意？
    牛角杯只有二寸深，
    它盛不了我们洋溢的感情；
    芦笙也才有六个音，
    它诉不完我们感激的心情。
    敬礼！谢尔玖琴柯同志，
    当你第二次来到苗家的时候，
    我们不再用无字锦旗赠送友人，
    也不再用口来传授古老的歌词，
    孩子们就用苗文写下你的名字，
    游方场上的青年还用书信来表达爱情。
    敬礼！谢尔玖琴柯同志，
    苗家跟着俄罗斯民族前进，
    清水江和伏尔加同唱一曲，
    我们不必为你树立纪功碑，
    苗文，就是永恒的友谊。
