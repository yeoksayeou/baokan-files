Title: Soviet Singer Shaposhnikov Holds First Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-11-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th—Soviet male mezzo-soprano singer, People's Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic Sergei Shaposhnikov, and pianist accompanist, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Levgeni Lebedev, who were invited to visit and perform in our country, gave their first performance at the People's Theater tonight. At the solo concert, the Soviet singer sang famous works by composers such as Shaporin, Voroshilov, Kotiulov, Grieg, Mozart, and Verdi.
    Shaposhnikov's singing was warmly welcomed by the audience in the capital. After each song was sung, he was greeted with thunderous applause from the entire audience.

### 苏联歌唱家沙波什尼柯夫  在京举行首次演出

1958-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联歌唱家沙波什尼柯夫
    在京举行首次演出
    新华社15日讯　应邀来我国访问演出的苏联男中音歌唱家、俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国人民演员谢尔盖·沙波什尼柯夫和钢琴伴奏、俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋演员莱甫根尼·列别节夫，今晚在人民剧场首次演出。苏联歌唱家在独唱音乐会上演唱了沙波林、沃罗申、柯秋罗夫、格里格、莫扎特、威尔第等作曲家的著名作品。
    沙波什尼柯夫的演唱受到首都听众的热烈欢迎。每个歌曲唱完后，都受到全场经久不息的掌声。
