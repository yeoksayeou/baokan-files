Title: The Stream is White and Clear
Author: Wei Chuantong
Date: 1958-11-24
Page: 8

    The stream is clear,
    The stream is white,
    Figures flash beneath the iron chain bridge,
    Viewing red leaves on Yangzhi Terrace.
    The stream is white,
    The stream is clear,
    Joyfully watching Mt. Kumgang guard the East Sea,
    My heart deeply loves the People's Army.
    The stream is clear,
    The stream is white,
    Now coming to Mt. Kumgang,
    I love Korea even more.
    The People's Army,
    The Volunteer Army,
    Beat the American bandits into a daze,
    Their heroic deeds are known throughout the world.
    Triangle Hill,
    Heartbreak Ridge,
    Imperialism's stench is spread far and wide,
    Socialism is the vanguard.
    The stream is white,
    The stream is clear,
    The water is as clear as a mirror, reflecting fine steeds,
    All the world eagerly reads "Farewell to the God of Plague."
    [Recorded below Nine Dragon Abyss in Korea, October 1958.]

### 溪水白又清
魏传统
1958-11-24
第8版()
专栏：

    溪水白又清
    魏传统
    溪水清，
    溪水白，
    铁索桥下闪人影，
    仰止台上看红叶。
    溪水白，
    溪水清，
    喜看金刚镇东海，
    余心深爱人民军。
    溪水清，
    溪水白，
    今来金刚山，
    更爱高丽国。
    人民军，
    志愿军，
    打得美寇伤脑筋，
    英雄业绩天下闻。
    上甘岭，
    伤心岭，
    帝国主义臭名扬，
    社会主义是标兵。
    溪水白，
    溪水清，
    水明如镜照骏马，
    普天竞读“送瘟神”。
    〔1958年10月记于朝鲜九龙渊下。〕
