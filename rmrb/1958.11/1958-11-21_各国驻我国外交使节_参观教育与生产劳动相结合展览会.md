Title: Diplomatic Envoys from Various Countries to China Visit Exhibition on Combining Education and Productive Labor
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 20th—Today, diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in the People’s Republic of China visited the Exhibition on Combining Education and Productive Labor. The guests viewed with great interest in the comprehensive hall and some local halls the brilliant achievements made in a short period by schools at all levels in our country in implementing the policy of combining education with productive labor in teaching, production, and scientific research. They also watched the on-site operation demonstrations of relevant exhibits.

### 各国驻我国外交使节  参观教育与生产劳动相结合展览会

1958-11-21
第5版()
专栏：

    各国驻我国外交使节
    参观教育与生产劳动相结合展览会
    新华社20日讯　今天上午，各国驻中华人民共和国的外交使节和外交官员，到教育与生产劳动相结合展览会参观。来宾们在综合馆以及部分地方馆，很有兴趣地观看了我国各级学校贯彻教育与生产劳动相结合的方针，很短的时期内在教学、生产和科学研究等方面所取得的辉煌成绩，还看了有关展品的实地操作表演。
