Title: Yemen's Crown Prince Badr Receives Chinese Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-11-28
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hodeidah, 25th -- Yemen's Crown Prince Badr received all members of the Chinese economic delegation headed by Shi Sheng this afternoon.
    Present at the meeting were Zhang Yue, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Yemen, and Hassan bin Ali, Deputy Minister of Education of Yemen.

### 也门王太子巴德尔接见我经济代表团

1958-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    也门王太子巴德尔接见我经济代表团
    新华社荷台达25日电　也门王太子巴德尔今天下午接见了由石生率领的中国经济代表团的全体团员。
    接见时在座的，有中国驻也门公使馆临时代办张越和也门副教育大臣哈桑·本·阿里。
