Title: Korean Art Troupe Arrives in Beijing for Performance Tour Across China
Author:
Date: 1958-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The art troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, consisting of 150 people, led by Zhao Lingchu, head of the art troupe and director of the Art Bureau of the Ministry of Education and Culture of Korea, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon.

The art troupe was warmly welcomed at the Beijing Railway Station by Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and responsible persons from relevant departments such as the Sino-Korean Friendship Association. Hundreds of actors and actresses from the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Cultural Troupe, which is currently performing in Beijing, and several famous art groups in the capital, enthusiastically lined the station to welcome the Korean friends and presented flowers to all members of the art troupe.

Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yonghao, and many staff members of the embassy also came to the station to welcome the troupe.

An Seong-hee, a Merited Artist of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and famous female dancer, Kim Wan-woo, a Merited Artist of the Republic and bass soloist, and other members of the art troupe were already familiar old friends with many actors and actresses of the Volunteer Army Cultural Troupe when they were in Korea. When they met again at the Beijing Railway Station today, they immediately shook hands warmly, embraced each other, and exchanged words of farewell in Chinese and Korean. The station was filled with a joyful atmosphere of friendship.

This large comprehensive art troupe has come to China for a friendly visit according to the 1958 implementation plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement. They will visit Beijing, Wuhan, Guangzhou, and Shenyang for a month, performing many programs including famous Korean and Chinese songs, folk and classical music and dances, etc. (Attached picture)

### 朝鲜艺术团到京将在我国各地访问演出

1958-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜艺术团到京将在我国各地访问演出
    新华社16日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国艺术团一行一百五十人，由艺术团团长、朝鲜教育文化省艺术局局长赵灵出率领，今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    艺术团在北京车站受到了我国对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥和中朝友好协会等有关部门负责人的热烈欢迎。正在北京演出的中国人民志愿军文工团和首都几个著名艺术团体的几百位男女演员，在车站上热情地夹道欢迎朝鲜朋友，向艺术团全体人员献了鲜花。
    朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐和大使馆许多工作人员也到车站欢迎。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国功勋演员，著名女舞蹈家安圣姬，共和国功勋演员、男低音独唱家金完羽和艺术团的其他演员，同志愿军文工团的许多男女演员在朝鲜时就是很熟识的老朋友，当他们今天在北京车站重新见面的时候，双方立刻热烈握手，相互拥抱，用中国话和朝鲜话互道阔别之情，车站上充满了一片欢乐的友好气氛。
    这个大型的综合性艺术团是根据中朝文化合作协定1958年执行计划前来我国作友好访问的。他们将在北京、武汉、广州和沈阳等地访问一个月，演出包括朝鲜和中国的著名歌曲、民间和古典的音乐与舞蹈等许多节目。（附图片）

                    新华社记者　郑震孙摄
