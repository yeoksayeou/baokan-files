Title: Two Stud Bulls in Xichong County Break National Mating Record
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-28
Page: 3

    Xichong County's Two Stud Bulls Break National Mating Record

    (Our dispatch) Sichuan Province's Xichong County used two domestic, superior stud bulls (QinChuan and NanYang breeds) this year for mating, setting the highest national record for stud bull mating. These two stud bulls mated a total of 8,199 cows, accounting for 70% of the county's cows that should be mated. Including the cows mated through natural means, the county has basically achieved full mating and breed improvement of its draft cattle.

### 西充县两头种公牛破全国配种纪录

1958-11-28
第3版()
专栏：

    西充县两头种公牛破全国配种纪录
    本报讯　四川省西充县今年利用两头国产的优良种公牛（秦川牛和南阳牛）配种，创造了全国种公牛配种最高的纪录。这两头种公牛一共配母牛八千一百九十九头，占全县应配母牛头数的70％；加上自然交配的母牛在内，全县基本上实现了耕牛全配化和良种化。
