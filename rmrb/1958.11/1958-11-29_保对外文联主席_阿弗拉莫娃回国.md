Title: Bulgarian Chairman of the Committee for Friendly and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Avramova, Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 4
    
    Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: The Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Friendly and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Y. Avramova, concluded her visit to our country and left Beijing by plane this afternoon to return home.

### 保对外文联主席  阿弗拉莫娃回国

1958-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    保对外文联主席
    阿弗拉莫娃回国
    据新华社28日讯　保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会主席叶·阿弗拉莫娃结束在我国的访问后，已于今天下午乘飞机离开北京回国。
