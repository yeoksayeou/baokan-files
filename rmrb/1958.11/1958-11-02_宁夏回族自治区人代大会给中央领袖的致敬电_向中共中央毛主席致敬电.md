Title: Telegram of Respect from the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region People's Congress to Central Leaders; Telegram of Respect to the CCP Central Committee and Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1958-11-02
Page: 2

Telegram of Respect from the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region People's Congress to Central Leaders
Telegram of Respect to the CCP Central Committee and Chairman Mao

Respected Party Central Committee, Respected Chairman Mao:

Under the cordial care of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region has been established. As the people of all ethnic groups in the region joyfully celebrate the establishment of the Autonomous Region, we would like to express our utmost gratitude and highest respect to the beloved Party Central Committee and beloved Chairman Mao! We solemnly guarantee that the people of all ethnic groups in the region will unite even more closely under the leadership of the Party, sweep away all obstacles on the road ahead, and advance towards the great goals of socialism and communism together with the people of the whole country! Under the glorious illumination of the Party's general line for socialist construction, we will redouble our efforts to usher in a new high tide of greater leaps forward in various construction undertakings. On the basis of the victories already achieved, we will ride the wind and waves, work hard for three years, change the face of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, and make our due contribution to building a socialist motherland with modern industry, modern agriculture, and modern science and culture ahead of schedule!

Respectfully,
With the highest respect!
The First Session of the First People's Congress of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region
October 30, 1958

Telegram of Respect to Chairman Liu Shaoqi

Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress:

We would like to report to you with unparalleled excitement on behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in Ningxia: According to the "Resolution on the Establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region" passed by the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress, the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region is now formally established. The establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region will further strengthen the unity of the people of all ethnic groups in the Ningxia region, enhance the socialist and communist awareness of the people, and greatly encourage the revolutionary drive of the people of all ethnic groups, ushering in a greater leap forward in industrial and agricultural production and various construction undertakings. In the future, under the leadership of the Communist Party, under the glorious illumination of the Party's general line for socialist construction, we will mobilize all forces for the further prosperity of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region and for the rapid development of the socialist construction of the motherland, and work hard and forge ahead with the people of all ethnic groups in the country!

We send our respectful greetings.
The First Session of the First People's Congress of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region
October 30, 1958

Telegram of Respect to Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council

Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council:

The Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region has been formally established after a year of deliberation and preparation under the direct leadership and close care of the State Council and yourself. The establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region marks another glorious victory for the Party and the state's ethnic policies. We firmly believe that under the illumination of the Party's general line for socialist construction and encouraged by the situation of the Great Leap Forward across the country, the establishment of the Autonomous Region will further stimulate the revolutionary drive of the people of all ethnic groups in the region. We have firm confidence in uniting the people of all ethnic groups in the region more closely, and under the leadership of the Party Central Committee and the Central People's Government, while continuing to complete the socialist revolution in politics and ideology, we will push the mass technical revolution and cultural revolution to a new high tide, so that industrial and agricultural production and various construction undertakings will leap forward at a higher speed, complete and overfulfill the region's Second Five-Year Plan, and strive to build a prosperous and happy new Ningxia and accelerate the socialist construction of the great motherland!

We send our respectful greetings.
The First Session of the First People's Congress of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region
October 30, 1958

### 宁夏回族自治区人代大会给中央领袖的致敬电  向中共中央毛主席致敬电

1958-11-02
第2版()
专栏：

    宁夏回族自治区人代大会给中央领袖的致敬电
    向中共中央毛主席致敬电敬爱的党中央、敬爱的毛主席：
    宁夏回族自治区在党中央和毛主席的亲切关怀下，已经建立起来了。当此全区各族人民欢欣鼓舞地庆祝自治区成立的时候，我们谨向亲爱的党中央、亲爱的毛主席表示万分的感激和崇高的敬意！我们庄严地保证，全区各族人民一定要更加紧密地团结在党的领导下，扫清前进道路上的一切障碍，和全国人民一起，向着社会主义和共产主义的伟大目标前进！我们要在党的社会主义建设总路线的光辉照耀下，干劲百倍地掀起各项建设事业更大跃进的高潮，在已经取得的胜利的基础上，乘风破浪，苦战三年，改变宁夏回族自治区的面貌，为提前建成具有现代工业、现代农业和现代科学文化的社会主义祖国作出我们应有的贡献！
    敬致
    崇高的敬礼！
              宁夏回族自治区第一届人民代表大会第一次会议
                                        1958年10月30日
    向刘少奇委员长致敬电全国人民代表大会常务委员会刘委员长：
    我们谨以无比兴奋的心情，代表宁夏各族人民向您报告：根据第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议通过的“关于成立宁夏回族自治区的决议”，宁夏回族自治区现在正式成立了。宁夏回族自治区的成立，必将进一步加强宁夏地区各族人民的团结，提高人民群众的社会主义和共产主义觉悟，大大鼓舞各族人民的革命干劲，掀起工农业生产和各项建设事业的更大跃进。今后，我们在共产党的领导下，在党的社会主义建设总路线的光辉照耀下，调动一切力量，为宁夏回族自治区的进一步繁荣，为祖国社会主义建设事业的迅速发展，和全国各族人民一道，艰苦奋斗，勇往直前！
    谨电致敬
                宁夏回族自治区第一届人民代表大会第一次会议
                                         1958年10月30日
     向周总理致敬电国务院周总理：
    宁夏回族自治区在国务院和您的直接领导和密切关怀下，经过一年的酝酿准备，现在正式成立了。宁夏回族自治区的成立，标志着党和国家民族政策的又一光辉胜利。我们坚信，在党的社会主义建设总路线的照耀下，在全国大跃进的形势鼓舞下，自治区的成立，必将进一步激发全区各族人民的革命干劲。我们有坚定的信心，把全区各族人民更加紧密的团结起来，在党中央和中央人民政府的领导下，在继续完成政治上和思想上社会主义革命的同时，把群众性的技术革命和文化革命推向新的高潮，使工农业生产和各项建设事业以更高的速度向前跃进，完成和超额完成全区的第二个五年计划，为建成繁荣幸福的新宁夏，为加速伟大祖国的社会主义建设事业而奋斗！
    谨电致敬
              宁夏回族自治区第一届人民代表大会第一次会议
                                        1958年10月30日
