Title: Our Military Delegation Returning from Poland Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-11-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 7th. The Chinese military delegation headed by Marshal Ye Jianying returned to Beijing by train today after visiting Poland.

	Those who went to the station to greet them included Senior General Su Yu, Senior General Zhang Zongxun, Senior General Peng Shaohui, Senior General Song Shilun, Senior General Yang Zhicheng, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong and other senior generals of the People's Liberation Army.

	Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilok, and military attachés also came to the station to greet them.

### 我访波军事代表团返京

1958-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我访波军事代表团返京
    新华社7日讯　以叶剑英元帅为首的我国军事代表团，在访问波兰后，今天乘火车返抵北京。
    前往车站迎接的有粟裕大将、张宗逊上将、彭绍辉上将、宋时轮上将、杨至成上将、刘志坚中将、萧向荣中将等人民解放军高级将领。
    波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克和武官等也到车站迎接。
