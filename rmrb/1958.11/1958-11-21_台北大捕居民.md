Title: Large-Scale Arrests of Residents in Taipei
Author:
Date: 1958-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 20th -- According to a Taipei dispatch broadcast by Manila Radio last night, the Chiang Kai-shek clique's Taipei City Police Department recently conducted a "security check" and arrested 336 people. Many were arrested for allegedly being "deserters," "forging resident certificates," and "violating household registration regulations."

### 台北大捕居民

1958-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    台北大捕居民
    新华社20日讯　据马尼拉电台昨天晚上广播的台北消息，蒋介石集团台北市警察局最近举行了一次“安全检查”，逮捕了三百三十六人。许多人被指为“逃兵”、“伪造居民证”、“违反户口条例”等而遭逮捕。
