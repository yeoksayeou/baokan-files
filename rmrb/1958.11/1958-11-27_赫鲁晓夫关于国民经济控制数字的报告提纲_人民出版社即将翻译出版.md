Title: Khrushchev's Outline Report on National Economic Control Figures to be Translated and Published by People's Publishing House
Author:
Date: 1958-11-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. Recently, the Soviet "Pravda" published Comrade Khrushchev's outline report on "Control Figures for the Development of the Soviet National Economy in 1959-1965," which he will present at the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and the "Outline on Strengthening the Connection between Schools and Life and Further Developing the National Education System" of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union. Both have been translated by the People's Publishing House and will be published soon.

### 赫鲁晓夫关于国民经济控制数字的报告提纲  人民出版社即将翻译出版

1958-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫关于国民经济控制数字的报告提纲
    人民出版社即将翻译出版
    新华社26日讯　最近苏联“真理报”发表了赫鲁晓夫同志准备在苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会上提出的关于“1959—1965年发展苏联国民经济的控制数字”的报告提纲，以及苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议“关于加强学校同生活的联系和进一步发展全国国民教育制度”的提纲，都已由人民出版社翻译，日内即将出版。
