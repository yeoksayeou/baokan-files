Title: German People's Army Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing for Performance Tour in China

Author:

Date: 1958-11-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 20th – The Erich Weinert Song and Dance Troupe of the German Democratic Republic's People's Army, invited by the Ministry of National Defense of our country to visit and perform, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon under the leadership of Colonel Josef Schutz and Colonel Brandel.

At the airport, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, on behalf of all officers and soldiers, extended a warm welcome to the comrades-in-arms from the Western forefront of the socialist camp. Amidst military music, the distinguished guests received flowers from members of the Cultural Work Troupe of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Air Force Cultural Work Troupe.

Among those welcoming the distinguished guests at the airport were Major General Wei Chuantong, Naval Major General Wei Tianlu, Air Force Major General Wang Pingshui, Senior Colonel Bai Wenhua, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, Senior Colonel Li Wei, and officers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing.

Also welcoming at the airport were Vice Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Qu Wu, Secretary-General of the Sino-German Friendship Association Wang Yuanfang, and teachers and students from the Beijing Dance School.

Also at the airport to welcome were Pirbach, Counselor of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, Lieutenant Colonel Van Inhode, Military Attaché, and other embassy staff. (Attached photo)

The Erich Weinert Song and Dance Troupe of the German Democratic Republic's People's Army arrived in Beijing on November 20th. The photo shows all the troupe members walking out of the airport accompanied by welcomers. Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Xuezeng

### 应邀来我国访问演出  德人民军歌舞团抵京

1958-11-21
第5版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问演出
    德人民军歌舞团抵京
    新华社20日讯　应我国防部邀请来我国进行访问演出的德意志民主共和国人民军埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团，在领队约瑟夫·舒茨上校和团长布兰德尔上校率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    在机场上，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任刘志坚中将代表全军官兵，向远道而来的守卫在社会主义阵营西方前哨的战友表示热情的欢迎。在军乐声中，贵宾们接受了中国人民解放军总政治部文艺工作团和空军文艺工作团团员们的献花。
    在机场欢迎贵宾的有魏传统少将，魏天禄海军少将，王平水空军少将，白文华大校，朱开印大校，李伟大校和中国人民解放军陆、海、空三军驻北京部队的军官。
    北京市副市长冯基平，对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武，中德友好协会秘书长王元方和北京舞蹈学校的师生们，也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆参赞皮尔巴赫，武官范因赫德中校和使馆其他人员。（附图片）
    德意志民主共和国人民军埃利希·魏纳特歌舞团于11月20日到达北京。图为全体团员在欢迎者陪同下步出机场。新华社记者　李学增摄
