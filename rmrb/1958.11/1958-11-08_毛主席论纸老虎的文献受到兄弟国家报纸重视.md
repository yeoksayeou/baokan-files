Title: Mao Zedong's Writings on Paper Tigers Receives Attention from Newspapers of Brotherly Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-11-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 7th: Major newspapers and news agencies in brotherly countries such as Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Hungary, and East Germany have recently published news reports about the People’s Daily publishing the important document "Comrade Mao Zedong on Imperialism and All Reactionaries Are Paper Tigers."
    Some newspapers also reprinted excerpts of the document and the People’s Daily editorial note.

### 毛主席论纸老虎的文献受到兄弟国家报纸重视

1958-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席论纸老虎的文献受到兄弟国家报纸重视
    新华社7日讯　保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、民主德国等兄弟国家的主要报纸或通讯社近日来都先后发表消息，报道了人民日报刊登“毛泽东同志论帝国主义和一切反动派都是纸老虎”这一重要文献的情况。
    有些报纸还摘要转载了文献和“人民日报”编辑部的按语。
