Title: The U.S. Continues to Give Weapons to the Chiang Kai-shek Troops
Author:
Date: 1958-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Taipei, 31st. U.S. interventionists are stepping up the firepower of the Chiang Kai-shek troops and continuing to hand over new weapons to Chiang Kai-shek's army. On the 30th, the head of the U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group in Taiwan, Dunn, announced the transfer of a batch of M-41 Walker Bulldog tanks to the Chiang Kai-shek army armored forces. At the same time, the U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group is training a newly established transportation battalion of the Chiang Kai-shek army in Tainan Zuoying, to train them in the use of U.S. military amphibious landing craft for three months to half a year. Four of these military transport ships are already being shipped from Manila to Taiwan. Another four will arrive in Taiwan in November. These ships will be transferred to the Chiang Kai-shek army to participate in military transport from Taiwan to Kinmen.

### 美国继续给蒋军武器

1958-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    美国继续给蒋军武器
    新华社31日讯　台北消息：美国干涉者正在为蒋军增强火力配备，继续把新武器交给蒋介石军队。30日，美国驻台湾军事援助顾问团团长窦恩宣布把一批M—四一瓦克恶犬式型坦克移交给蒋军装甲部队。同时，美国军事援助顾问团在台南左营训练蒋军新成立的一个运输大队，以三个月到半年的时间训练他们使用美军的水陆两用平底补给船。已经有四艘这种军运船正由马尼拉运到台湾。另外还有四只将在11月间抵台。这些船只将移交给蒋军，参加从台湾到金门的军事运输。
