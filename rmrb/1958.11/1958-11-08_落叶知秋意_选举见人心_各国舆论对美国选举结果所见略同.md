Title: Fallen Leaves Herald Autumn, Elections Reveal the People's Hearts: International Opinion Agrees on the Outcome of the US Elections
Author:
Date: 1958-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The results of the US Congressional and local elections have provoked reactions in international public opinion. Newspapers and news agencies in the United States and other countries have generally agreed that the Republican Party's crushing defeat in the elections reflects the American people's dissatisfaction with the current domestic and foreign policies of the US government. At the same time, many newspapers pointed out that the Democratic Party's victory does not mean that US policies will change much.

### 落叶知秋意　选举见人心  各国舆论对美国选举结果所见略同

1958-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    落叶知秋意　选举见人心
    各国舆论对美国选举结果所见略同
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：美国国会和地方选举的结果，引起各国舆论的反应。美国和其他国家的报刊和通讯社都纷纷认为共和党在选举中惨败反映了美国人民对美国政府现行内外政策的不满。同时许多报纸指出民主党的获胜并不意味着美国的政策会有多大的改变。
