Title: Soviet Delegation Arrives in Geneva to Attend Expert Meeting on Preventing Surprise Attacks
Author:
Date: 1958-11-11
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, November 10th. TASS, Geneva, November 9th: A Soviet delegation, headed by Soviet First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, V.V. Kuznetsov, arrived in Geneva today to attend the expert meeting on measures to prevent surprise attacks, between member states of the Warsaw Treaty and the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, which is scheduled to be held in Geneva on the 10th.

    At the Geneva airport, Kuznetsov made the following statement to the press: "The Soviet delegation has arrived in Geneva to participate in a meeting that should provide recommendations to relevant governments, in conjunction with certain measures on disarmament, regarding the possibility of preventing surprise attacks. As everyone knows, the initiative to convene this meeting was put forward by the Soviet government. Of course, we will make every effort to make the work here a success, to contribute to reducing the danger of a new war, and to developing peaceful cooperation between countries.”

### 参加关于防止突然袭击问题的专家会议  苏联代表团到达日内瓦

1958-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    参加关于防止突然袭击问题的专家会议
    苏联代表团到达日内瓦
    新华社10日讯　塔斯社日内瓦9日讯：以苏联外交部第一副部长库兹?佐夫为首的苏联代表团今天抵达日内瓦，参加将于10日在日内瓦举行的华沙条约和北大西洋公约的参加国关于防止突然袭击的措施问题的专家会议。
    库兹?佐夫在日内瓦飞机场向新闻界发表了如下的声明：“苏联代表团抵达日内瓦参加一个会议，这个会议应当结合裁军方面的某些措施向有关的政府提出关于防止突然袭击的可能性的建议。大家知道，召开这个会议的倡议是苏联政府提出的。当然，我们将尽一切努力来使这里的工作成功，对减少新战争的危险和发展各国间的和平合作作出贡献。”
