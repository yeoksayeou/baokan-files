Title: Indian "Blitz" Weekly Reveals: U.S. Conspiracy to Use Iran as Fifth Fleet Base
Author:
Date: 1958-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 29th. According to a recent issue of the "Blitz" weekly, in talks on concluding a U.S.-Iran military treaty, the United States will select Iran's southern coast as the base for the planned U.S. Fifth Fleet. The weekly said that during U.S. Secretary of Defense McElroy's recent visit to Iran, U.S. naval experts accompanying him inspected the Iranian ports of Bushehr-Shapur and Chahbahar for this purpose.

It also wrote that, according to U.S. naval officials, the southern coast of Iran is "strategically and politically the most suitable area to become the new fleet base," because "no matter what happens in any coastal area of Asia and Africa, it is possible to take relatively action from this area."

### 印度“闪电”周报揭露  美国阴谋利用伊朗变为第五舰队基地

1958-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    印度“闪电”周报揭露
    美国阴谋利用伊朗变为第五舰队基地
    新华社新德里29日电　据最近一期的“闪电”周报报道说，在举行关于缔结美国—伊朗军事条约的会谈中，美国将选择伊朗的南部海岸作为计划中的美国第五舰队驻扎的基地。这个周报说，在美国国防部长麦克耳罗伊最近访问伊朗期间，陪他前去的美国海军专家们曾经为此目的观察了伊朗的布什尔·沙普尔和沙赫巴哈尔等港口。
    它又写道，据美国海军负责人员说，伊朗南部海岸“无论从战略上和从政治上来说，都是成为新舰队基地的最合适的地区”，因为“亚非任何沿海地区不管发生什么情况，都有可能从这个地区作出相对的行动”。
