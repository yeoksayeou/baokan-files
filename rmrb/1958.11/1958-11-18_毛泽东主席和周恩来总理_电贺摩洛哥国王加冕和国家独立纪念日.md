Title: Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai Send Telegrams to Congratulate the King of Morocco on His Coronation and National Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1958-11-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to His Majesty King Mohammed V of the Kingdom of Morocco, congratulating him on the 29th anniversary of his coronation and the National Independence Day of Morocco. The telegram reads as follows:

    To His Majesty King Mohammed V of the Kingdom of Morocco:

    On the occasion of the 29th anniversary of Your Majesty's coronation and the National Independence Day of your country, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of Morocco on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish Morocco prosperity and its people happiness, and I wish Your Majesty good health.

    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    November 17, 1958, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to His Excellency Ahmed Balafrej, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Morocco, congratulating him on the King of Morocco's coronation day and the National Independence Day of Morocco. The telegram reads as follows:

    To His Excellency Ahmed Balafrej, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Morocco:

    On the occasion of His Majesty the King's coronation day and Your country's National Independence Day, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to the Government of the Kingdom of Morocco and to Your Excellency on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

    We firmly believe that the friendly cooperative relations and traditional friendship between China and Morocco will develop further with the establishment of diplomatic relations between our two countries. We wish the Moroccan people further achievements in safeguarding national sovereignty and building their own country.

                         Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                         November 17, 1958, in Beijing

### 毛泽东主席和周恩来总理  电贺摩洛哥国王加冕和国家独立纪念日

1958-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席和周恩来总理
    电贺摩洛哥国王加冕和国家独立纪念日
    新华社17日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电摩洛哥王国国王穆罕默德五世，祝贺国王加冕二十九周年和摩洛哥国家独立纪念日。电文如下：摩洛哥王国国王穆罕默德五世陛下：
    欣逢陛下加冕二十九周年和贵国国家独立纪念日，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向陛下和摩洛哥人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝摩洛哥国家繁荣，人民幸福，并祝陛下身体健康。
    中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
          1958年11月17日于北京
    新华社17日讯　周恩来总理今天致电摩洛哥王国首相艾哈迈德·贝拉弗里杰，祝贺摩洛哥国王加冕日和摩洛哥国家独立纪念日。电文如下：摩洛哥王国首相艾哈迈德·贝拉弗里杰阁下：
    值此贵国国王陛下加冕日和贵国国家独立纪念日的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义向摩洛哥王国政府和阁下致以热诚的祝贺。
    我们深信中摩两国之间的友好合作关系和传统友谊将随着我们两国外交关系的建立而得到新的发展。祝摩洛哥人民在维护国家主权和建设自己国家的事业中获得进一步的成就。
                         中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来		          1958年11月17日于北京
