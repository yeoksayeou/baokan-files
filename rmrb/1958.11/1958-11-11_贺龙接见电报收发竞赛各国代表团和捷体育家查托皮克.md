Title: He Long Receives Delegations from Various Countries Participating in the Telegraph Transmission and Reception Competition and Czech Athlete Zatopek
Author:
Date: 1958-11-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 10th: Vice Premier He Long of the State Council today received the delegation leaders and all athletes from Bulgaria, Democratic Germany, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mongolia, Poland, and the Soviet Union, who came to Beijing to participate in the 1958 International Radio Rapid Telegraph Transmission and Reception Friendship Competition.

    Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and Li Da, Director of the All-China Athletic Federation, were present at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, November 10th: He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission, today received the famous Czechoslovak athlete Zatopek and his wife, who are currently visiting our country.

    Rong Gaotang and Huang Zhong, Vice Chairmen of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, were also present at the reception.

### 贺龙接见电报收发竞赛各国代表团和捷体育家查托皮克

1958-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

    贺龙接见电报收发竞赛各国代表团和捷体育家查托皮克
    新华社10日讯　国务院副总理贺龙今天接见了来北京参加1958年国际无线电快速收发报友谊竞赛的保加利亚、民主德国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、蒙古、波兰、苏联等国家的代表团团长及全体运动员。
    接见时在座的有国家体委副主任荣高棠，中国人民国防体育协会主任李达等。
    新华社10日讯　国务院副总理兼体育运动委员会主任贺龙，在今天接见了正在我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克著名体育家查托皮克夫妇。
    接见时在座的还有国家体委副主任荣高棠、黄中。
