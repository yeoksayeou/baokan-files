Title: UN Observers in Lebanon to Withdraw Before End of Month
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-10
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 8th. "The Masses" newspaper today quoted Lebanese official sources as saying that the Lebanese government verbally requested UN observer group leader, Prasa, to withdraw the observers on the 7th. These people said that an agreement has been reached on the issue of withdrawing the UN observers without formally making any request. The withdrawal of the observers will be completed before the end of November.

### 联合国驻黎观察员将在月底前撤走

1958-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    联合国驻黎观察员将在月底前撤走
    新华社大马士革8日电　“群众报”今天援引黎巴嫩官方人士的话说，黎巴嫩政府7日已经口头要求联合国观察团团长普拉萨撤出观察员。这些人士说，已经对不用正式提出任何要求而撤出联合国观察员的问题上达成协议。撤出观察员的工作将在11月底以前完成。
