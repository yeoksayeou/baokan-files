Title: Teams of People March up the Mountain Slope
Author: Tang Zhixue
Date: 1958-11-09
Page: 2

　　Teams of people march up the mountain slope
　　Lofty mountains and flowing rivers,
　　Teams of people march up the mountain slope;
　　Loud blasts shatter the cliff rocks,
　　Truckloads of ore are mined in abundance;
　　Rows of blast furnaces billow with blue smoke,
　　Furnaces of molten iron glow red in the sky;
　　Batches of steel are transported to factories,
　　Iron oxen arrive in the fields.
　　　　(Nanchong, Tang Zhixue)

### 一队队人马上山坡
南充　唐治学
1958-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    一队队人马上山坡
  一座座高山一条条河，
  一队队人马上山坡；
  一声声巨响崖石破，
  一车车矿石采得多；
  一排排高炉冒青烟，
  一炉炉铁水红满天；
  一批批钢材运进厂，
  一架架铁牛到田间。
        （南充　唐治学）
