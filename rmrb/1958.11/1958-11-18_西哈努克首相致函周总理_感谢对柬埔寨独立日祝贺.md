Title: Sihanouk Sends Letter to Premier Zhou Thanking Him for Congratulating Cambodia's Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1958-11-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Premier Zhou Enlai recently received a letter from Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia. Prince Sihanouk thanked Premier Zhou Enlai in the letter for his congratulations on the anniversary of the independence of the Kingdom of Cambodia.

Prince Sihanouk said in the letter: "On the occasion of our country's Independence Day, I express my warm gratitude to you for your telegram of congratulations to the Royal Government, the Cambodian people and myself. The example of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the admirable achievements of the Chinese people are a valuable encouragement for us to continue to strive to build our motherland. I once again express to you our confidence in the future of the friendly relations between our two countries based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence."

### 西哈努克首相致函周总理  感谢对柬埔寨独立日祝贺

1958-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克首相致函周总理
    感谢对柬埔寨独立日祝贺
    新华社17日讯　周恩来总理最近收到柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王的一封信。西哈努克亲王在信中感谢周恩来总理对柬埔寨王国独立纪念日的祝贺。
    西哈努克亲王在信中说：“在我国独立纪念日的时候，承蒙来电向王国政府、柬埔寨人民和我本人致贺，我向你表示热烈的感谢。中华人民共和国政府的范例和中国人民的令人赞佩的成就是对我们继续努力建设我们祖国的可贵的鼓舞。我再一次地向你表示我们对我们两国在和平共处五项原则基础上的友好关系的前途的信心。”
