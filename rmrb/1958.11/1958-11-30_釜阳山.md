Title: Mount Fuyang
Author: Yang Tongjiang
Date: 1958-11-30
Page: 6

    Mount Fuyang
    Mount Fuyang in Xushui, treasures hidden for tens of thousands of years,
    Unnoticed in the past, now smiles bloom,
    Opening the treasure trove, transformed into a mountain of treasures.
                (Yang Tongjiang)
