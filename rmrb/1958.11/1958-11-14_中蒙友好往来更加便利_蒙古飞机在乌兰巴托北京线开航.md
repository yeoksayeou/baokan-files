Title: More Convenient Friendly Exchanges Between China and Mongolia: Mongolian Planes Inaugurate Ulaanbaatar-Beijing Route

Author:

Date: 1958-11-14
Page: 6

    To further facilitate friendly exchanges between China and Mongolia
    Mongolian Planes Inaugurate Ulaanbaatar-Beijing Route
    Xinhua News Agency: In accordance with the air transportation agreement signed on January 17, 1958, between the governments of China and the Mongolian People's Republic, the planes of the Civil Aviation Administration of the Mongolian People's Republic officially inaugurated the Ulaanbaatar-Beijing route on the 11th.
    There are three flights per month on this route, flying from Ulaanbaatar to Beijing on the 1st, 11th, and 21st, and from Beijing to Ulaanbaatar on the 2nd, 12th, and 22nd.
    Tserendondov, Deputy Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing today on the first flight and was welcomed by responsible persons from the Civil Aviation Administration of China.
    The Civil Aviation Administration of China has already opened a route between Beijing and Ulaanbaatar. Now that the Mongolian People's Republic has opened its route, it will further facilitate friendly exchanges between the two peoples.

### 中蒙友好往来更加便利  蒙古飞机在乌兰巴托北京线开航

1958-11-14
第6版()
专栏：

    中蒙友好往来更加便利
    蒙古飞机在乌兰巴托北京线开航
    新华社讯　根据我国政府和蒙古人民共和国政府1958年1月17日签订的航空交通协定，蒙古人民共和国航空交通管理局的飞机，11日正式开航乌兰巴托—北京航线。
    这条航线每月有三趟班机，一日、十一日、二十一日由乌兰巴托飞北京，二日、十二日、二十二日由北京飞乌兰巴托。
    蒙古人民共和国航空交通管理局副局长策林道尔吉今天乘第一班飞机来到北京，受到中国民用航空局负责人的欢迎。
    中国民用航空局在北京—乌兰巴托间的航线早已开航，现在蒙古人民共和国开航后，将进一步便利两国人民的友好往来。
