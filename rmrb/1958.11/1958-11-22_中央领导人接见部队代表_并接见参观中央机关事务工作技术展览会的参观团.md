Title: Central Leaders Receive Military Representatives and Visiting Group Attending the Central Government Offices Affairs Technology Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, November 21st: CCP Central Committee Vice-Chairmen Zhou Enlai and Zhu De, Politburo members Lin Boqu, Peng Dehuai, and He Long, Secretariat Secretary Wang Jiaxiang, as well as Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Marshal Ye Jianying, Admiral Xiao Jingguang, General Xiao Hua, General Zhang Zongxun, and General Chen Xilian, this morning received all representatives attending the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy Fourth-Level Cadre Conference, the Artillery Party Committee Enlarged Meeting, and the Beijing Air Force Units Party Committee Enlarged Meeting.
    Comrade Zhou Enlai and Comrade Zhu De and others also had cordial conversations with some of the representatives.

    Xinhua News Agency, November 21st: CCP Central Committee Vice-Chairmen Zhou Enlai and Zhu De, Politburo members Lin Boqu, Peng Dehuai, and He Long, Secretariat Secretary Wang Jiaxiang, and Central Committee member Nie Rongzhen, this morning received all personnel from the visiting groups from nineteen provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities, and five special districts who came to Beijing to visit the Central Government Offices Affairs Technology Exhibition.

### 中央领导人接见部队代表  并接见参观中央机关事务工作技术展览会的参观团

1958-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中央领导人接见部队代表
    并接见参观中央机关事务工作技术展览会的参观团
    新华社21日讯　中共中央副主席周恩来、朱德，政治局委员林伯渠、彭德怀、贺龙，书记处书记王稼祥，以及聂荣臻元帅、叶剑英元帅、萧劲光海军大将、萧华上将、张宗逊上将、陈锡联上将等，今天上午接见了出席中国人民解放军海军四级干部会议、出席炮兵党委扩大会议和出席北京空军部队党委扩大会议的全体代表。
    周恩来同志和朱德同志等还同一部分代表作了亲切谈话。
    新华社21日讯　中共中央副主席周恩来、朱德，政治局委员林伯渠、彭德怀、贺龙，书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员聂荣臻，今天上午接见了前来北京参观中央机关事务工作技术展览会的来自十九个省、自治区、市和五个专区的参观团的全体人员。
