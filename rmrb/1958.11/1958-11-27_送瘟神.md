Title: Sending Away the God of Plague
Author: He Jun
Date: 1958-11-27
Page: 5
　
　　Sending Away the God of Plague
　　He Jun
　　Nixon,
　　Beaten everywhere,
　　Imperialists have bad memories,
　　The scar heals, but they forget the pain,
　　And go looking for trouble again.
　　Sending away the God of Plague,
　　A surge of anger in London,
　　Like rats running across the street, everyone beats them,
　　Darting into the Prime Minister's residence,
　　To temporarily avoid the storm.
　　Covered in sweat,
　　Trembling with fear,
　　"War monger, go home!"
　　The shouts outside the window deafening,
　　Frightening the Vice President.
　　In Brazil,
　　The situation is the same,
　　Rockefeller goes to visit,
　　Workers offer condolences as a welcome,
　　A half-mast flag hangs in the air.
　　The God of Plague arrives,
　　Everyone who sees him gets a headache,
　　People everywhere burn paper boats,
　　The firelight shines red across the five continents,
　　The paper tiger is so embarrassed!

### 送瘟神
禾君
1958-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    送瘟神
    禾君
    尼克松，
    到处被人轰，
    帝国主义记性坏，
    好了伤疤忘了疼，
    又去找壁碰。
    送瘟神，
    伦敦怒潮涌，
    老鼠过街人人打，
    钻入首相官邸中，
    暂时避避风。
    满头汗，
    胆战心惊恐，
    “战争贩子滚回去”，
    窗外喊声震耳聋，
    吓坏副总统。
    在巴西，
    情形一般同，
    洛克菲勒去访问，
    工人致哀当接风，
    半旗悬空中。
    瘟神到，
    谁见谁头疼，
    人民到处烧纸船，
    火光照得五洲红，
    纸老虎好窘！
