Title: Capital Holds Memorial Service for Comrades Zheng Zhenduo, Cai Shufan, etc.; United Arab Republic, Afghanistan, and other countries send telegrams and letters of condolence

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 31st. A memorial service for Zheng Zhenduo, Cai Shufan, and 14 other comrades who died in a plane crash was held this morning at the Capital Theater.

    The hall was surrounded by wreaths from all sectors of the capital, relevant departments, and friends of the deceased, creating a solemn atmosphere.

    At 9:00 AM, the memorial service began. As mournful music played, Chen Yi, Guo Moruo, Shen Yanbing, and Zhang Xiruo slowly walked to the portraits of the deceased comrades, presented wreaths, and observed a moment of silence.

    Zhang Xiruo delivered a eulogy at the meeting. He said that Zheng Zhenduo, Cai Shufan, and the other 14 beloved comrades have parted ways with us forever. Their common and noble quality was their boundless loyalty to the socialist construction of the motherland and to the cause of world peace, and they tirelessly engaged in selfless labor. This noble quality is a model for us to learn from.

    Shen Yanbing reported on the life of Comrade Zheng Zhenduo, Zeng Yongquan reported on the life of Comrade Cai Shufan, and Chu Tunan reported on the life of Comrade Ma Shi'an and the other 13 comrades.

    The conference announced that after the passing of these 16 comrades, the funeral committee had received 224 condolence telegrams and letters from various parties at home and abroad.

    Peng Zhen, Bo Yibo, Bao Erhan, Ye Jizhuang, Zhou Yang, Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Hanfu, and responsible persons from relevant departments, as well as representatives from all sectors of the capital and friends of the deceased, totaling more than 1,400 people, attended the memorial service.

    General Wang Ping and some members of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation attended the memorial service.

    Many foreign envoys and diplomatic officials stationed in China also attended the memorial service today.

    The memorial service ended at 10:00 AM. At noon, the ashes of the 16 deceased comrades, including Zheng Zhenduo and Cai Shufan, were buried in Babaoshan.

    Xinhua News Agency, October 31st. After the deaths of Zheng Zhenduo, Cai Shufan, and the other 14 comrades in a plane crash, the Chinese government and the work units of the deceased comrades received many condolence telegrams and letters from abroad.

    On October 23, Ragab, the ambassador of the United Arab Republic to China, sent a memorandum to the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, conveying President Nasser's condolences and forwarding the condolence telegrams from Kamal el-Din Hussein, Minister of Education of the United Arab Republic, and Ahmed Najib Hashim, Minister of Education of the Egyptian Region.

    On October 20, Sardar Mohammed Daoud, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Mohammed Naim, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Del Ali Amukhat Popal, Minister of Education, sent telegrams to Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, etc., expressing their deep condolences for the 16 deceased comrades.

    Many foreign envoys stationed in China and departments of culture, education, sports, and foreign cultural liaison, as well as friendly organizations to China, also sent telegrams and letters to express their condolences for the deceased comrades.

    Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, and relevant departments have all sent replies to express their gratitude. (Attached picture)

### 首都追悼郑振铎蔡树藩等同志  阿联阿富汗等国来电来函表示吊唁

1958-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    首都追悼郑振铎蔡树藩等同志
    阿联阿富汗等国来电来函表示吊唁
    新华社31日讯　因飞机失事遇难的郑振铎、蔡树藩等十六位同志追悼大会今天上午在首都剧场举行。
    会场四周摆满了首都各界、各有关部门和遇难同志生前友好赠送的花圈，充满了肃穆气氛。
    九时，追悼大会开始。在奏哀乐声中，陈毅、郭沫若、沈雁冰、张奚若缓步走到遇难同志的遗像面前，向遇难同志敬献花圈，默哀悼念。
    张奚若在会上致悼词。他说，郑振铎、蔡树藩等十六位亲爱的同志和我们永别了。他们共同的高贵的品质，是对祖国的社会主义建设和对世界和平事业无限忠诚，并不懈地进行着忘我的劳动。这种高贵的品质是我们学习的榜样。
    沈雁冰报告了郑振铎同志的生平事迹，曾涌泉报告了蔡树藩同志的生平事迹，楚图南报告了马适安等十四位同志的生平事迹。
    大会宣布在这十六位同志遇难以后，治丧委员会曾收到了国内外各方面的唁电、唁函二百二十四件。
    参加追悼大会的还有彭真、薄一波、包尔汉、叶季壮、周扬、廖承志、章汉夫和有关方面的负责人，以及首都各界代表和遇难同志生前友好等共一千四百多人。
    中国人民志愿军代表团王平将军和部分团员参加了追悼大会。  
    驻我国的许多外国使节和外交官员今天也参加了追悼大会。
    追悼大会在十时结束。中午，郑振铎、蔡树藩等十六位遇难同志的骨灰在八宝山安葬。
    新华社31日讯　郑振铎、蔡树藩等十六位同志因飞机失事遇难后，我国政府和遇难同志的工作单位，收到了来自国外的许多唁电和唁函。
    阿拉伯联合共和国驻华大使拉加卜于10月23日向我国外交部送致备忘录，转述纳赛尔总统的哀悼之意，并转致阿拉伯联合共和国教育部长卡迈勒丁·侯赛因，埃及地区教育部长艾哈迈德·纳吉布·哈希姆的唁电。
    阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德，副相兼外交大臣穆罕默德·纳依姆，教育大臣德尔·阿里·阿木哈特·波帕尔等于10月20日分别致电我国周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长等，对十六位遇难同志表示深切的哀悼。
    许多国家的驻华使节和文化、教育、体育、对外文化联络等部门以及对我友好团体也致电和来函，对遇难同志表示哀悼。
    周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长和有关部门都已分别复电答谢。（附图片）
