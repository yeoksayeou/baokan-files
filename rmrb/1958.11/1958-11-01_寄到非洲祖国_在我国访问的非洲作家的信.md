Title: Sent to African Homeland: Letter from African Writers Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1958-11-01
Page: 8
To our African homeland,
A letter from African writers visiting China

We, the writers from Angola, Ghana, Uganda, Nigeria, Senegal, and Somaliland currently traveling in the People's Republic of China, extend our greetings from Beijing to the people of our distant homelands. We have witnessed with excitement and joy in China the astonishing progress, particularly in economic construction and industrial development.

We have a duty to tell the African patriots—the workers and intellectuals who are fighting for African independence: only with complete economic and political independence can a formerly semi-colonial and backward country achieve such great accomplishments.

In order to gain independence, to eliminate the divisions created by the imperialists in Africa, and to make Africa a modern, multi-ethnic great power like China, we urge all our African compatriots to unite and strive for African unity.

We vow to do everything in our power to fulfill our aspirations. We fully believe that the people of our countries will join us in striving for the victory of the cause of independence and unity of our African homeland.

October 30, 1958, Beijing

[Editor's Note] Recently, eight writers from the distant African continent were invited to visit China. They are: Diop and Osman from Senegal, Hafeez Abu from Nigeria, Andrade and Cruz from Angola, Alachari from Somaliland, Senoga from Uganda, and Doudou from Ghana. The above short article was written by them in Beijing. Below, we have selected two poems by African poets to help readers understand the situation on the African continent today and the African people's desire for independence and freedom.

### 寄到非洲祖国  在我国访问的非洲作家的信

1958-11-01
第8版()
专栏：

    寄到非洲祖国
    在我国访问的非洲作家的信
    我们，现在正在中华人民共和国旅行的安哥拉、加纳、乌干达、尼日利亚、塞内加尔和索马里兰的作家们，在北京向遥远的祖国人民致意。我们在中国兴奋和愉快地看到了中国惊人的进步，特别是在经济建设和工业发展方面。
    我们有责任告诉非洲的爱国者——正在为非洲的独立而斗争的劳动者和知识分子：只有经济上和政治上的完全独立，一个过去为半殖民地和落后的国家才能获得这样伟大的成就。
    为了获得独立，为了消除帝国主义者在非洲所制造的分裂现象，为了使非洲成为一个像中国一样现代化的，多民族的大国，我们吁请非洲的全体同胞，一致行动起来，争取非洲的统一。
    我们宣誓，尽一切力量完成我们的志愿。我们充分相信，我国人民一定会和我们一起，为争取非洲祖国的独立和统一事业的胜利而努力。
                              1958年10月30日　北京
    〔编者附记〕最近有八位来自遥远的非洲大陆的作家应邀访问中国。他们是：塞内加尔的迪奥普、奥斯曼，尼日利亚的哈费兹·阿布，安哥拉的安德拉代、克路兹，索马里兰的阿拉查里，乌干达的森诺迦和加纳的都杜。上面这篇短文，是他们在北京写的。下面，我们选载了两首非洲诗人的诗，帮助读者了解今天非洲大陆的情况和非洲人民的要求独立自由的心情。
