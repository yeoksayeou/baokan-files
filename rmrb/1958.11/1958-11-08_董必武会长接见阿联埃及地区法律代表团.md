Title: Chairman Dong Biwu Receives Legal Delegation from the Egyptian Region of the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, November 7th: Dong Biwu, Chairman of the China Political Science and Law Association, met with the delegation of legal workers from the Egyptian region of the United Arab Republic in Chengdu yesterday and held friendly talks. After the meeting, he hosted a luncheon.
    The delegation arrived in Chengdu yesterday from Chongqing.

### 董必武会长接见阿联埃及地区法律代表团

1958-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    董必武会长接见阿联埃及地区法律代表团
    新华社成都7日电　中国政治法律学会会长董必武，昨天在成都接见了阿拉伯联合共和国埃及地区法律工作者代表团，进行了友好的交谈。接见以后，并设便宴招待。
    代表团是在昨天从重庆到达成都的。
