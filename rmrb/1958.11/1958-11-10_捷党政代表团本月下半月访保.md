Title: Czechoslovak Party and Government Delegation to Visit Bulgaria in the Second Half of This Month
Author:
Date: 1958-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 8th: It was announced here that at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Bulgarian Government, a Czechoslovak Party and Government delegation will pay a friendly visit to Bulgaria in the second half of November.

### 捷党政代表团本月下半月访保

1958-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    捷党政代表团本月下半月访保
    新华社布拉格8日电　此间宣布：捷克斯洛伐克党和政府代表团应保加利亚共产党中央委员会和保加利亚政府的邀请，将在11月下半月到保加利亚作友好访问。
