Title: Erect a Benchmark on Mount Emei
Author:
Date: 1958-11-09
Page: 6

Erect a Benchmark on Mount Emei
          (I)
    Men, women, old, and young are all mobilized,
    Striving with full vigor for five years;
    Deep and shallow, large and small, all work together,
    Oil and gas bloom all over Sichuan.
          (II)
    Traversing the myriad mountains of Sichuan,
    Drilling through the earth thousands of feet;
    Strive for the upstream and work harder,
    Erect a benchmark on Mount Emei!

### 峨嵋山上立标杆

1958-11-09
第6版()
专栏：

    峨嵋山上立标杆
          （一）
    男女老少总动员，
    鼓足干劲战五年；
    深浅大小齐动手，
    油气花开遍四川。
          （二）
    踏遍四川万重山，
    凿穿地球千万尺；
    力争上游再努力，
    峨嵋山上立标杆！
