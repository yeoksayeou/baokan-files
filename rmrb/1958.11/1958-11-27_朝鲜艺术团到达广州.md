Title: Korean Art Troupe Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 26th: The Korean Democratic People's Republic Art Troupe, comprising 148 members and led by head of the troupe Zhao Lingchu, arrived in Guangzhou by train this afternoon. They were warmly welcomed by Guangzhou Vice Mayor Sun Leyi and representatives from the literary and art circles of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City.

The Korean artists will hold two grand performances at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall on the evenings of the 28th and 29th.

### 朝鲜艺术团到达广州

1958-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜艺术团到达广州
    新华社广州26日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国艺术团一行一百四十八人，在团长赵灵出率领下今天下午乘火车到达广州，受到了广州市副市长孙乐宜和广东省、广州市文艺界人士的热烈欢迎。
    朝鲜艺术家们将于28和29日的晚上在中山纪念堂举行两场盛大的演出。
