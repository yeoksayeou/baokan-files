Title: Welcoming Premier Kim Il-sung
Author: Lao She
Date: 1958-11-23
Page: 8

    Welcoming Premier Kim Il-sung
    Lao She
    Welcome, welcome,
    The most respected distinguished guest of the Chinese people,
    The most loyal friend of the Chinese people!
    Please accept our salute and flowers,
    Please allow us to warmly shake your hand!
    Welcome, welcome,
    Today the children are all wearing new clothes,
    Men and women, young and old, are vying to hand you flowers,
    Six hundred million people cheer for you, thank you:
    What you bring is the lofty friendship of the Korean people!
    Welcome, welcome,
    The precious gift you bring – the friendship of the people,
    Gives each of us tremendous encouragement,
    We must learn from the heroic Korean people,
    And make the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people
    Even more beautiful than all the flowers!
    Welcome, welcome,
    To welcome you, today is our festival,
    Our people's communes are all singing your praises,
    Every worker, peasant, merchant, student, and soldier in the commune is cheering:
    We must build socialism faster and better!
    Welcome, welcome,
    The gentle breeze you bring from the Yalu River
    Fills us with indescribable warmth,
    Please convey to our friends – the Korean people:
    The friendship between China and Korea will last forever, glorious and brilliant!

### 欢迎金日成首相
老舍
1958-11-23
第8版()
专栏：

    欢迎金日成首相
    老舍
    欢迎您，欢迎您，
    中国人民最敬爱的贵宾，
    中国人民最忠实的朋友！
    请您接受我们的敬礼与鲜花，
    请您允许热情地握握您的手！
    欢迎您，欢迎您，
    今天孩子们都穿上新衣，
    老少男女都争着把鲜花递在您手里，
    六亿人民向您欢呼，向您致谢：
    您带来的是朝鲜人民崇高的友谊！
    欢迎您，欢迎您，
    您带来的珍贵礼品——人民的友谊，
    给我们每个人莫大的鼓励，
    我们一定向英雄的朝鲜人民学习，
    叫中朝人民的友好比一切鲜花
    都更美丽！
    欢迎您，欢迎您，
    为欢迎您，今天就是我们的节日，
    我们的人民公社都在歌唱您的功绩，
    公社的每个工农商学兵都在欢呼：
    我们一定要更快更好地建设社会主义！
    欢迎您，欢迎您，
    您带来鸭绿江上的和风
    使我们感到无可形容的温暖，
    请您转达给我们的朋友——朝鲜人民：
    中朝两国的友谊，地久天长，永远光辉灿烂！
