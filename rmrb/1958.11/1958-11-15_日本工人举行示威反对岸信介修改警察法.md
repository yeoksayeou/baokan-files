Title: Japanese Workers Hold Demonstrations Against Kishi Nobusuke's Revision of the Police Law
Author:
Date: 1958-11-15
Page: 5

    In the struggle of the Japanese people against the "Police Officer Duties Execution Law Amendment Bill," 5.5 million organized workers stood at the forefront and took forceful actions. Due to the strike of railway workers, 191 trains were stopped, and 103 trains were delayed. Workers in every workshop across the country held workshop meetings for one hour or more to express their protests. (Attached pictures)
    The picture shows the situation when Nosaka Sanzo delivered a speech at Shinagawa Station to more than 800 workers and students who gathered to support the railway workers.
    The picture shows the situation of a short-term strike held by railway workers at the Tamachi depot.

### 日本工人举行示威反对岸信介修改警察法

1958-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    日本工人举行示威反对岸信介修改警察法
    在日本人民反对“警察官职务执行法修正案”的斗争中，五百五十万有组织的工人站在前列，采取了有力的行动。由于铁路工人的罢工，一百九十一列火车停驶，一百零三列火车误时。全国每个车间的工人都举行了一小时或一小时以上的车间大会，表示抗议。（附图片）
