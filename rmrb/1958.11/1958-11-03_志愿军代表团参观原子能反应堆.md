Title: Volunteer Army Delegation Visits Atomic Energy Reactor
Author:
Date: 1958-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd—The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation, headed by General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping, accompanied by Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Zheng Lin, Deputy Director of the Institute of Atomic Energy of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, visited China's first heavy water experimental atomic energy reactor and cyclotron this morning.

After the visit, the delegation wrote inscriptions for the staff here, warmly congratulating the motherland on this scientifically significant achievement.

### 志愿军代表团参观原子能反应堆

1958-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军代表团参观原子能反应堆
    新华社2日讯　以杨勇上将和王平上将为首的中国人民志愿军代表团以及志愿军文艺工作团，在中国科学院副院长竺可桢和中国科学院原子能研究所副所长郑林等陪同下，今天上午参观了我国第一座重水型实验性原子能反应堆和回旋加速器。
    代表团参观后为这里的工作人员题词，热烈庆贺祖国这一科学上具有重大意义的成就。
