Title: Arise, Act!
Author: Lùyì Dé Nuòluóníyǎ
Date: 1958-11-01
Page: 8

    Arise, Act!
    Mozambique, Luís de Noronha
    You are asleep! But life is moving forward, ah, enigmatic motherland! You are asleep! And life is leaping forward with time, progress surrounds the whole world, but your slumbering dreams seem so heavy and bitter, it has lasted for hundreds of years. Howling beasts stagger, greedily gulping hot blood, the forest where you live, where not a ray of light can penetrate... Ah, Africa! When foreign rule was established on the land, how could you sleep, tell me! Wake up, answer! Wake up, greedy crows are already circling around you, it spreads its wings above your head, above the sleeping person, wanting to do bloody and heinous deeds... Wake up, you should listen in your dreams, ah, slumbering female slave! The sound of progress now echoes throughout the world: "Ah, Africa, it is time! Arise, act!"
               (Translated by Shi Min)

### 起来，行动吧！
路易·德·诺罗尼亚
1958-11-01
第8版()
专栏：

    起来，行动吧！
    莫三鼻给　路易·德·诺罗尼亚你睡了！而生活在前进，啊，谜一般的祖国呀！你睡了！而生活在跟随着时间向前跃进，进步环绕着整个世界，而你沉睡的迷梦却显得那么沉重而苦辛，它已经延续了好几百年。嗥叫的野兽曳步而行，贪婪地大口吞进热腾腾的鲜血，你所居住的森林，那儿透不进一线光明……啊，阿非利加！当国土上建立了异国的统治时，你怎能安睡，你说呀！清醒一下，回答吧！醒醒吧，贪婪的乌鸦早就在你四周打转，它在你头顶，沉睡的人的上空伸展翅膀，想要来干血腥的万恶勾当……醒醒吧，你且在梦里听一听，啊，沉睡了的女奴隶呀！进步的声音，如今响彻了整个世界：“啊，阿非利加，是时候了！起来，行动吧！”
               （史敏译）
