Title: Taiwan is Chinese Territory, Not a Colony; Chiang Kai-shek Opposes Holding a "Referendum;" Shouts About Organizing an Anti-Communist Alliance, But Says Success Depends on the United States
Author:
Date: 1958-11-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 16th: New York dispatch: Columbia Broadcasting System broadcast on the evening of the 15th a statement by Chiang Kai-shek to the company's reporters saying that it "absolutely should not" raise the issue of holding a "referendum" in Taiwan. He said, "We are not a colony."

Chiang Kai-shek said this while answering reporters' questions. The reporter asked him: Would he answer "the demands of foreign critics" and hold a referendum in Taiwan to decide whether to continue to maintain the Kuomintang "government," unite with the Communist Party, or place Taiwan "under United Nations trusteeship."

It is reported that Chiang Kai-shek was "agitated" when answering this question, saying: "This question should not be raised at all," "We are not a colony." Chiang Kai-shek emphasized that Taiwan is a part of Chinese territory.

Chiang Kai-shek also stated that he has the right to blockade the mainland's ports, regardless of the "ceasefire" situation in the Taiwan Strait. He emphasized that he "has the right" to blockade because it is a matter of China's internal affairs.

Chiang Kai-shek arrogantly clamored that he supports the establishment of an anti-communist alliance including Taiwan, South Korea, South Vietnam, the Philippines, and Japan. He said that whether such an alliance can be formed depends on the United States.

### 台湾是中国领土不是殖民地  蒋介石反对举行“公民投票”  叫嚣组织反共联盟又说能否成功要看美国

1958-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    台湾是中国领土不是殖民地
    蒋介石反对举行“公民投票”
    叫嚣组织反共联盟又说能否成功要看美国
    新华社16日讯　纽约消息：哥伦比亚广播公司15日晚上广播蒋介石对这家公司记者的谈话说，“根本不应该”提出在台湾举行一次“公民投票”的问题，他说，“我们不是一个殖民地。”
    蒋介石是在回答记者的问题时这样说的。这个记者问他：他是否会回答“国外的批评家的要求”，在台湾举行一次公民投票，决定是继续维持国民党“政府”呢，还是同共产党联合，或是把台湾“置于联合国托管之下”。
    据悉，蒋介石在回答这个问题时情绪是“激动的”，他说：“根本不应当提出这个问题”，“我们不是一个殖民地”。蒋介石强调指出，台湾是中国领土的一部分。
    蒋介石还表示他有权封锁大陆的各港口，不管台湾海峡的“停火”局势如何。他强调他之所以“有权”封锁，是因为这是中国的内政问题。
    蒋介石狂妄地叫嚣他赞成成立一个包括台湾、南朝鲜、南越、菲律宾、日本在内的反共联盟，他说，是否能组成这样一个联盟，要看美国。
