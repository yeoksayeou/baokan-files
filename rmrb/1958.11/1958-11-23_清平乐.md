Title: Qing Ping Yue
Author: Li Jishen
Date: 1958-11-23
Page: 2

    Qing Ping Yue
    Li Jishen
    Welcoming Premier Kim Il-sung's Third Visit to China
    Red flags are waving.
    The Premier is seen again.
    We once fought shoulder to shoulder,
    Victory songs resounded in the heavens.
    You have visited China many times,
    Deep friendship is frequently added.
    Today the east wind is mighty,
    Brightly blooming flowers of friendship.

### 清平乐
李济深
1958-11-23
第2版()
专栏：

    清平乐
    李济深
    欢迎金日成首相三度访华
    红旗招展。
    首相重相见。
    曾是并肩同作战，
    凯奏声闻霄汉。
    数承来访中华，
    情深谊厚频加。
    今日东风浩荡，
    灿开友好之花。
