Title: Bulgarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-11-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 1st. The Bulgarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation, consisting of three members led by Stoyan Dzhulemezov, Vice Chairman of the Bulgarian State Planning Committee and Chairman of the Bulgarian side, arrived in Beijing by plane today to attend the third meeting of the Sino-Bulgarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee.
    Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Agriculture and Chairman of the Chinese side, Wang Xinyuan, Vice Minister of Light Industry and member of the committee, and Wang Zhuozhi, Commercial Counselor of our Embassy in Bulgaria, were at the airport to welcome them.
    Ivan Balov, Commercial Counselor of the Bulgarian Embassy in China and member of the Bulgarian side, also greeted them at the airport.

### 保加利亚科技合作代表团抵京

1958-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚科技合作代表团抵京
    新华社1日讯　前来我国出席中保科技合作委员会第三届会议的保加利亚科技合作代表团一行三人，在保方组主席、保加利亚国家计划委员会副主席斯托扬·久列梅佐夫率领下今日乘机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有中方组主席、农业部副部长杨显东，委员、轻工业部副部长王新元和我驻保商务参赞王琢之等。
    保加利亚驻华使馆商务参赞、保方组委员伊万·巴洛夫也到机场迎接。
