Title: Nosaka Sanzo, Miyamoto Kenji Hold Press Conference, Announcing the Circumstances of the JCP Central Committee Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1958-11-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, November 25th: Nosaka Sanzo, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, and Miyamoto Kenji, Secretary-General, held a press conference this afternoon, releasing the report and resolutions passed by the Third Plenary Session of the JCP Central Committee.

It was announced that from November 20th to 23rd, the Japanese Communist Party held its Third Plenary Session of the Central Committee. At the meeting, Miyamoto Kenji delivered a report on behalf of the Central Secretariat. In his report, Miyamoto discussed the development of the Party's activities since the Second Plenary Session, the current international and domestic situation, the upsurge of various people's movements, and the Party's current and future tasks. The meeting unanimously adopted Miyamoto's report.

The meeting also discussed the current workers' movement, student movement, current demands and struggles regarding the social security system, local election struggles, and financial issues.

The meeting unanimously passed the following three resolutions: First, a resolution supporting the joint statements issued by the four Japanese delegations to China with relevant Chinese parties and supporting the joint statements issued by the two delegations to North Korea with relevant Korean organizations; Second, a resolution supporting the movement of Korean residents in Japan to return to their homeland; Third, a resolution on the struggle to win the Matsukawa Incident trial.

At the press conference, Miyamoto also announced the establishment of national joint struggle organizations in the people's demands to open up Japan-China relations and the struggle against the revision of the "Police Duties Execution Law". It was announced that there are a total of nineteen joint struggle organizations in various prefectures and counties demanding the opening of Japan-China relations; forty-six joint struggle organizations opposing the revision of the "Police Duties Execution Law"; and nine joint struggle organizations for comprehensive mass struggles. The Japanese Communist Party participates formally or as an observer in eighteen, twenty-eight, and seven of these joint struggle organizations, respectively.

### 野坂参三、宫本显治举行记者招待会  宣布日共中央全会情况

1958-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    野坂参三、宫本显治举行记者招待会
    宣布日共中央全会情况
    新华社25日讯　东京消息：日本共产党中央委员会主席野坂参三、总书记宫本显治今天下午举行记者招待会，发表了日共中央第三次全体会议所通过的报告和决议。
    据宣布，从11月20日起到23日，日本共产党举行了中央委员会第三次全体会议，在会上宫本显治代表中央干部会做了报告。宫本在报告中谈到自从二中全会以来党的活动的发展、目前国际国内形势和人民各种运动的高涨、党当前和今后的任务等各项问题。会议一致通过了宫本的报告。
    会议还就当前的工人运动、学生运动、关于社会保障制度的目前的要求和斗争、关于地方选举斗争、财政等问题进行了讨论。
    会议一致通过了如下三项决议：一、关于支持日本四个访华代表团分别同中国有关方面发表的共同声明和支持两个访朝代表团同朝鲜有关团体发表的共同声明的决议；二、关于支持旅日朝鲜侨民回国运动的决议；三、关于为争取松川事件裁判胜利而斗争的决议。
    宫本在招待会上还发表了在人民要求打开日中关系和反对修改“警察官职务执行法”的斗争中全国建立联合斗争组织的情况。据宣布，各府县单位要求打开日中关系运动的共同斗争组织共有十九个；反对修改“警职法”的共同斗争组织有四十六个；综合性群众斗争的共同斗争组织有九个。在这些共同斗争组织中日本共产党正式地或以观察员身份参加的分别有十八个、二十八个、七个。
