Title: Foreign Cultural Commission Holds Banquet to Welcome Korean Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1958-11-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: The Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee held a banquet this evening to warmly welcome all members of the Korean Democratic People's Republic Art Troupe, headed by Director Zhao Lingchu.

At this evening's banquet, outstanding Korean artists from the friendly neighboring country gathered with Chinese literary and art figures and members of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army song and dance troupe to enjoy friendship toasts. The entire banquet was filled with an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere from beginning to end.

Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Commission, delivered a speech at the beginning of the banquet, warmly welcoming and thanking the Korean Art Troupe for bringing the profound friendship and rich and colorful musical and dance art of the Korean people. He said that the heroic Korean people's great contributions to defending world peace, helping the Chinese people's revolutionary struggle, and carrying out peaceful construction are something the Chinese people will never forget. He also said that the Chinese people have always loved Korean song and dance art very much, and the leaps forward and new achievements made by the Korean people in cultural and artistic fields in recent years have given the Chinese literary and art circles many new inspirations. Zhang Zhixiang warmly wished the Korean Art Troupe a great success in this visit and performance, and that it will make an important contribution to enhancing the friendship between the two peoples.

Director Zhao Lingchu delivered an extremely enthusiastic speech at the banquet. He said that they had just recently bid farewell to the last group of Volunteer Army warriors in Korea, and today they were able to gather with Chinese friends, making it impossible to describe the excitement in his heart. He also said that the Korean Art Troupe brought songs from the depths of the Korean people's hearts, and these songs will express the Korean people's incomparable and deep friendship with the brotherly Chinese people. Director Zhao Lingchu warmly hoped that this performance will further consolidate the militant friendship forged with blood between the people of Korea and China.

Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Korean Ambassador to China Li Yonghao, Secretary-General of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association Li Deqi, and Head of the Volunteer Army Song and Dance Troupe Lu Yi also delivered speeches and toasts at the banquet, warmly congratulating the further development of the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people and the friendly cooperation between the art circles of the two countries.

At the banquet, Kim Wan-woo, a meritorious actor and bass soloist of the Korean Democratic People's Republic, Kim Jung-soon, a soprano soloist, Choi Chang-eun, a tenor soloist, and many male and female actors of the art troupe, enthusiastically sang many folk songs from Korea and China. Actors from the Volunteer Army Song and Dance Troupe and singers from the capital also sang impromptu performances. The friendly songs of the artists of the two countries and the enthusiastic applause that followed the songs made the banquet full of friendship even more joyful.

### 对外文委举行宴会  欢迎朝鲜艺术团

1958-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    对外文委举行宴会
    欢迎朝鲜艺术团
    新华社17日讯　对外文化联络委员会今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎以赵灵出团长为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国艺术团全体人员。
    在今晚的宴会上，来自友好邻邦的朝鲜优秀艺术家们同中国文艺界人士和志愿军文工团人员欢聚一堂，畅饮友谊酒，整个宴会自始至终充满了极其热烈，的友好气氛。
    对外文委副主任张致祥在宴会开始时首先致词，热烈欢迎和感谢朝鲜艺术团带来了朝鲜人民的深厚友谊和丰富多采的音乐舞蹈艺术。他说，英雄的朝鲜人民对于保卫世界和平，对于帮助中国人民革命斗争和进行和平建设等方面所作的巨大贡献，是中国人民永远不能忘记的。他还说，中国人民一向十分热爱朝鲜的歌舞艺术，朝鲜人民这几年来在文化艺术方面的跃进和所取得的新成就，给了中国文艺界许多新的启示。张致祥热情地预祝朝鲜艺术团这次访问演出获得巨大成功，并且对增进两国人民的友谊作出重要贡献。
    赵灵出团长在宴会上发表了极其热情的讲话。他说，他们不久前刚刚在朝鲜送别了最后一批志愿军勇士，今天又能同中国朋友欢聚一堂，真是无法形容自己内心的激动。他还说，朝鲜艺术团带来了发自朝鲜人民内心深处的歌声，这歌声将表达出朝鲜人民对兄弟的中国人民无比深厚的友情。赵灵出团长热烈地希望这次演出将进一步巩固朝中两国人民用鲜血凝成的战斗友谊。
    文化部部长沈雁冰，朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐，中朝友好协会秘书长李得奇和志愿军文工团团长鲁艺等人，也先后在宴会上致词祝酒，热烈祝贺中朝人民友谊的进一步发展和两国艺术界的友好合作。
    宴会上，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国功勋演员、男低音独唱家金完羽，女高音独唱家金贞顺，男高音独唱家崔昌殷和艺术团的许多男女演员，热情地演唱了朝中两国的许多民歌，志愿军文工团演员和首都歌唱家也即席演唱。两国艺术家的友谊歌声和随着歌声响起的热烈掌声，使得充满友谊的宴会更加欢乐起来。
