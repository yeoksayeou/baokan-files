Title: East German Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-11-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd. The government trade delegation from the German Democratic Republic, led by Willi Huettenrauch, State Secretary and First Deputy Minister of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic, and one member, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to discuss trade issues between China and Germany for 1959 and long-term trade. The deputy head of the delegation, Imgard Altenson, Director of the China Department of the Ministry of Foreign and Domestic Trade, and fourteen members had arrived in Beijing earlier.
　　Lin Haiyun, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of China, Liu Ruoming, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau, and Zhao Jichang, Commercial Counselor of our country in the German Democratic Republic, welcomed them at the airport. Norbert, Commercial Counselor of the German Democratic Republic in China, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 民主德国贸易代表团抵京

1958-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国贸易代表团抵京
    新华社23日讯　前来我国商谈中德1959年度及长期贸易问题的德意志民主共和国政府贸易代表团团长、德意志民主共和国对外与对内贸易部国务秘书兼第一副部长维利·许滕劳赫和团员一人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团副团长、对外与对内贸易部中国处处长伊姆嘉特·阿尔脱松和团员十四人，已经先期到达北京。
    我对外贸易部副部长林海云、第二局副局长刘若明和我国驻民主德国商务参赞赵继昌等到机场欢迎。民主德国驻华商务参赞诺伯特也到机场欢迎。
