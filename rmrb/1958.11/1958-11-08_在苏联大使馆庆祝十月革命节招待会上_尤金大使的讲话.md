Title: Ambassador Yudin's Speech at the Reception Celebrating the October Revolution Day at the Soviet Embassy
Author:
Date: 1958-11-08
Page: 2

Dear comrades and friends! Respected guests!

Today we gather here to celebrate the national holiday of the Soviet people—the 41st anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

In the decades since the revolution, the Soviet people have undergone very arduous trials in all aspects, both internal and external, and have built a new social system—socialism—and are gradually transitioning to a higher stage of social development—communism.

The forty-first year of Soviet socialist construction marks a new, significant, and unprecedented development in all aspects of Soviet politics, economy, and culture.

The new achievements of Soviet science are world-renowned. The new proof of these achievements is the successful launch of our country's third artificial Earth satellite. It weighs nearly one and a half tons. The third artificial satellite has been traveling in space for almost half a year, orbiting the Earth two thousand five hundred times. This provides a scientific basis for developing the science of cosmic space.

Now the entire Soviet people are welcoming the 21st Congress of the CPSU. This congress will study and adopt the control figures for the 1959-1965 national economic development plan of our country. The completion of the new seven-year plan is a huge step forward towards communism. This will enable our country to catch up with the United States in the production of major products, thus marking a decisive boundary in the economic competition between the two world systems of socialism and capitalism.

The forty-first year of our country's development has been a year of unremitting struggle for peace, for friendship and cooperation among all peoples, and against all kinds of conspiracies by the arsonists of a new war. This year marks a new victory for the policy of peaceful coexistence between countries with different social systems, for maintaining and consolidating peace, and for developing economic and cultural relations with all countries. The firm actions of the Soviet government and all peace-loving people are conducive to maintaining peace and forcing the reckless imperialist adventurers to retreat.

The struggle for peace is the sacred cause of all peoples. Expressing the feelings and wishes of the Soviet people, our country, together with other peace-loving countries, is advancing and fighting thoroughly for the unconditional cessation of atomic and hydrogen weapon tests, and for the permanent ban on such weapons of mass destruction.

The remarkable achievements in the development of the People's Republic of China and all the countries participating in the world socialist camp are new and outstanding proof of the victory of the Great October Socialist Revolution and the invincible ideas of Marxism-Leninism. Every new achievement of socialist countries is a new step for mankind along the road of progress.

Today, on this day of our national holiday, I would like to mention once again the Soviet people's brotherly love and solidarity towards our Chinese friends. We, together with the Chinese people, are very pleased to see the great achievements made by the Chinese working people on all fronts of economy and culture under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and its leader, Comrade Mao Zedong. The victory of the Chinese people's revolution has made it possible for the Chinese people to develop the economy, science, and culture at an unprecedented speed, and to consolidate the power and national defense of their people's democratic state.

The brotherly friendship and unbreakable alliance between the Soviet and Chinese peoples are a reliable guarantee for further achievements of the countries of the peace and socialism camp. This year marks a new achievement in the consolidation of Soviet-Chinese friendship. The recent talks between Comrade Khrushchev and Comrade Mao Zedong once again proved that our two peoples and communist parties are in complete agreement on current important issues. This meeting once again demonstrated the indestructible strength of Soviet-Chinese friendship and our determination to defend peace and international security, as well as our strong determination to repel aggressors.

The Soviet people unanimously support the just struggle of the Chinese people to liberate Taiwan and angrily condemn the provocative actions of the US imperialists in this region.

The Soviet government, embodying the will of the Soviet people, solemnly declares that the US imperialists' invasion of the People's Republic of China will also mean an invasion of the Soviet Union. The unwavering and eternal friendship between the Soviet and Chinese peoples has once again been proven here!

The Soviet people extend brotherly greetings to the great Chinese people who are building socialism and resolutely opposing the imperialist aggressors' interference in China's internal affairs!

On the 41st anniversary of the October Revolution, our people extend warm greetings to the people of all countries who are striving for peace and international cooperation and opposing the threat of a new war!

Long live the 41st anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution!

I propose a toast:

To the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the Soviet and Chinese peoples and the peoples of all socialist countries,

To the friendship and cooperation of the people of all countries in the world,

To world peace,

Cheers!

### 在苏联大使馆庆祝十月革命节招待会上  尤金大使的讲话

1958-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

    在苏联大使馆庆祝十月革命节招待会上
    尤金大使的讲话亲爱的同志们和朋友们！敬爱的贵宾们！
    今天我们在这里聚会，来庆祝苏联人民的国庆节——伟大的十月社会主义革命四十一周年。
    革命以后这几十年来，苏联人民经历了非常艰苦的内外的各方面的考验，建成了一种新的社会制度——社会主义，并且正在逐步过渡到社会发展的更高的阶段——共产主义。
    苏联建设社会主义的第四十一年，标志着苏联政治、经济和文化等各方面新的重大的空前未有的发展。
    苏联科学的新成就在世界上是尽人皆知的。这些成就的新证明，就是我国成功地发射了第三颗人造地球卫星。它的重量将近一吨半。第三颗人造卫星在宇宙间旅行差不多有半年了，它绕着地球运行了两千五百周。这就给发展关于宇宙空间科学提供了科学根据。
    现在全苏联人民正在迎接苏共第二十一次代表大会。这次大会将要研究和通过1959到1965年我国国民经济发展计划的控制数字。新的七年计划的完成是向共产主义前进的巨大的一步。这就使得我国可以在主要产品的生产方面赶上美国，因此这是社会主义和资本主义两个世界体系在经济比赛中的一个决定性的界线。
    我们国家发展的第四十一年，是苏联为和平、为各国人民之间的友谊和合作、为反对新战争玩火者的各种各样的阴谋而进行坚持不懈的斗争的一年。这一年标志着不同社会制度国家和平共处的政策、维护和巩固和平，发展和一切国家的经济和文化关系的政策的新胜利。苏联政府和一切爱好和平人民的坚决行动，有利于维护和平，并且迫使轻举妄动的帝国主义冒险分子后退。
    争取和平的斗争是各国人民的神圣事业。我们的国家表达出苏联人民的心情和愿望，同其他爱好和平的各国人民共同前进，彻底为毫无条件的停止原子武器和氢武器的试验，为永远禁用这种大规模毁灭性武器而斗争。
    中华人民共和国和所有参加世界社会主义阵营的国家发展中的卓越成就，是伟大十月社会主义革命和所向无敌的马列主义思想胜利的新的卓越的证明。社会主义国家的每一个新成就都是人类沿着进步道路发展的新的一步。
    今天，在我们国庆节的这一天，我想再一次提到苏联人民对我们中国朋友们的兄弟般的热爱和团结的感情。我们和中国人民一起，很高兴地看到在中国共产党和他的领袖毛泽东同志的英明领导之下，中国劳动人民在经济和文化一切战线上所取得的伟大的成就。中国人民革命的胜利使得中国人民有可能以空前的高速度来发展经济、科学和文化，巩固自己的人民民主国家的威力和国防力量。
    苏中两国人民之间的兄弟般的友谊和牢不可破的联盟是和平和社会主义阵营各国获得进一步成就的可靠保证。今年这一年标志着苏中友谊巩固的新成就。赫鲁晓夫同志和毛泽东同志不久以前的会谈，再一次证明了我们两国人民和共产党关于目前重要问题的观点是完全一致的。这次会谈再一次表明了苏中友谊的不可摧毁的力量和我们保卫和平和国际安全的决心，以及回击侵略者的坚强决心。
    苏联人民一致支持中国人民解放台湾的正义斗争，并且愤怒谴责美帝国主义者在这个地区的挑衅行动。
    苏联政府体现着苏联人民的意志，它郑重声明，美帝国主义者对中华人民共和国的进犯也将意味着对苏联的进犯。苏中两国人民之间不可动摇的永恒的友谊在这里再一次得到了证明！
    苏联人民向正在建设社会主义，坚决反对帝国主义侵略者干涉中国内政的伟大中国人民，致以兄弟的敬礼！
    在十月革命四十一周年这一天，我国人民向争取和平和国际合作，反对新战争威胁的各国人民致以热烈的敬礼！
    伟大的十月社会主义革命四十一周年万岁！
    我提议：
    为苏中两国人民之间和社会主义各国人民之间的永恒和牢不可破的友谊，
    为世界各国人民的友谊和合作，
    为世界和平，
    干杯！
