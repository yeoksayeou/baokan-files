Title: Beijing Circles Gather to Celebrate Albanian Liberation Day
Author:
Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th - 1,500 people from all walks of life in Beijing held a joint celebration at the Beijing Workers' Club this evening to warmly celebrate the 14th anniversary of Albania's liberation.
	This celebration was held by the Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association and the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions. Albanian Ambassador to China, Balili, embassy staff, and Albanian students in China were invited to attend the celebration this evening.
	After Lu Heng, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting, Ge Baoquan, Director of the China-Albanian Friendship Association, spoke. Ge Baoquan introduced the glorious history of the Albanian people's struggle and the great achievements made in socialist construction. He warmly congratulated Albania on achieving newer successes in the cause of building socialism, and wished the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Albanian people would develop and consolidate day by day.
	The audience welcomed Albanian Ambassador to China, Balili, with prolonged and warm applause to speak. When Ambassador Balili introduced the construction achievements of the Albanian people, he said that Albania has now established a new industry, with industrial output increasing seventeen times compared to before World War II; agriculture has been collectivized and mechanized; illiteracy has been eliminated, and the living standards of the people have greatly improved. He said: With the leadership of our Labor Party and the help of our friends, we will surely achieve greater victories in the future.
	Ambassador Balili said that in the past fourteen years, the Albanian people have overcome many difficulties on the road to construction. The US and British imperialists and the Yugoslav Titoists have been deliberately undermining our country. But victory belongs to us because we have the leadership of the Labor Party and the support and assistance of loyal friends—the people of the great Soviet Union, China, and other socialist countries.
	Ambassador Balili expressed firm support for our people's struggle against US imperialism's occupation of Taiwan and interference in our internal affairs.
	The evening featured screenings of films such as "Guests from Albania" and a dance party.
	The photo exhibition introducing Albania, organized by the China-Albanian Friendship Association in Zhongshan Park, also opened today.

### 北京各界集会庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放日

1958-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    北京各界集会庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放日
    据新华社28日讯　北京市各界人民一千五百人今天晚间在北京市工人俱乐部举行联欢晚会，隆重庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十四周年。
    这个晚会是由中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会和北京市工会联合会举行的。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使巴利里和大使馆工作人员以及阿尔巴尼亚在中国的留学生，应邀参加了今天的晚会。
    北京市工会联合会副主席鲁恒在会上致祝词后，中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会理事戈宝权讲了话。戈宝权介绍了阿尔巴尼亚人民的光荣斗争历史和在社会主义建设中取得的巨大成就。他热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚在建设社会主义的事业中获得更新的成就，并祝中国和阿尔巴尼亚人民的牢不可破的兄弟友谊日益发展和巩固。
    全场以长时间的热烈掌声欢迎阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使巴利里讲话。巴利里大使介绍阿尔巴尼亚人民的建设成就时说，现在阿尔巴尼亚已经建立了新的工业，工业产量比第二次世界大战前增加了十七倍；农业已实现了合作化和机械化；文盲现象已经扫除，人民的生活水平大大提高了。他说：有我们的劳动党的领导，有我们朋友的帮助，今后我们一定会获得更大的胜利。
    巴利里大使说，在过去的十四年里，阿尔巴尼亚人民在建设的道路上克服了不少的困难。美、英帝国主义者和南斯拉夫铁托主义者一直蓄意破坏我国。但是胜利是属于我们的，因为我们有劳动党的领导，有忠诚的朋友——伟大的苏联、中国以及其它社会主义国家的人民的支持和援助。
    巴利里大使对我国人民反对美帝国主义侵占台湾、干涉我国内政的斗争，表示坚决的支持。
    晚会放映了“来自阿尔巴尼亚的客人”等影片并举行了舞会。
    中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会在中山公园举办的介绍阿尔巴尼亚的图片展览会，也在今天开幕。
