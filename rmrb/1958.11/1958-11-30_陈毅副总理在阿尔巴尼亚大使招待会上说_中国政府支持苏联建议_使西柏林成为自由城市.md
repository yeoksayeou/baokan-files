Title: Chen Yi Says at Albanian Ambassador's Reception: The Chinese Government Supports the Soviet Proposal to Make West Berlin a Free City
Author:
Date: 1958-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Deputy Prime Minister Chen Yi said tonight at the reception given by Albanian Ambassador to China, Piro Balle, in celebration of the 14th anniversary of the liberation of Albania, that the Chinese government fully supports the Soviet government's reasonable proposal to end the occupation status of Berlin and to make West Berlin a demilitarized free city.

In his speech at the reception, Deputy Prime Minister Chen Yi said: "The socialist camp has always adhered to a peaceful foreign policy. Recently, the Soviet Union proposed to end the occupied status of Berlin and to make West Berlin a demilitarized free city. This proposal greatly helps to ease the tense situation in Europe and the world. The Chinese government fully supports this reasonable proposal of the Soviet Union and believes that there is no reason for the troops of the United States, Britain, and France to remain in West Berlin any longer."

### 陈毅副总理在阿尔巴尼亚大使招待会上说  中国政府支持苏联建议  使西柏林成为自由城市

1958-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理在阿尔巴尼亚大使招待会上说
    中国政府支持苏联建议  使西柏林成为自由城市
    新华社29日讯　陈毅副总理今晚在阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使巴利里庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十四周年的招待会上说，中国政府完全支持苏联政府关于结束柏林的被占领状态，并且使西柏林成为非军事的自由城市的合理建议。
  陈毅副总理在招待会上的讲话中说：“社会主义阵营各国一向坚持和平的对外政策，最近苏联提出了结束柏林的被占领状态，并且使西柏林成为非军事的自由城市。这个建议大大有助于和缓欧洲和世界的紧张局势。中国政府完全支持苏联的这一合理的建议，并且认为美国、英国、法国的军队没有任何理由再停留在西柏林了。
