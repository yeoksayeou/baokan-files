Title: Chiang Kai-shek Group "Foreign Minister" Huang Shao-gu Said: Suggestion of Trusteeship for Taiwan is Ridiculous
Author:
Date: 1958-11-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 16th. Chiang Kai-shek group "Foreign Minister" Huang Shao-gu, who is visiting France, said in a conversation with reporters on the 15th regarding the situation in the Taiwan Strait that the suggestion made by British Labour Party member Attlee to place Taiwan under the trusteeship of the United Nations is "ridiculous."

Huang Shao-gu also said that "There is absolutely no question of withdrawing our (referring to the Taiwan authorities) troops from the coastal islands."

### 蒋介石集团“外交部长”黄少谷说  托管台湾的建议是可笑的

1958-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    蒋介石集团“外交部长”黄少谷说
    托管台湾的建议是可笑的
    新华社16日讯　巴黎消息：正在访问法国的蒋介石集团“外交部长”黄少谷15日在对记者谈到台湾海峡局势时说，英国工党艾德礼提出的把台湾置于联合国的托管之下的建议是“可笑的”。
    黄少谷还说，“绝对谈不上把我们（指台湾当局）的军队撤出沿海岛屿的问题”。
