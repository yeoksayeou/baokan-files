Title: Ten Million Illiterates in the United States
Author:
Date: 1958-11-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 17th Dispatch from TASS: According to statistics from the US "Bureau of the Census," there are currently 60 million adults in the United States with less than a high school education, of which approximately 10 million are illiterate.

### 美国有一千万文盲

1958-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    美国有一千万文盲
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：据美国“国情普查局”统计，目前美国共有六千万中学文化程度以下的成年人，其中约有一千万人是文盲。
