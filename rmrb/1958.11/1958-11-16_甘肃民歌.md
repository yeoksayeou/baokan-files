Gansu Folk Songs
　　Two giant hands pull the Tao River,
　　One foot kicks over Huajia Ridge,
　　One foot flattens Stone Mountain,
　　One drill pierces through Jiudian Gorge,
　　One hammer breaks through the Gates of Hell.
　　One cannon blasts through the ten-thousand-zhang cliff,
　　One shovel digs the canal wide,
　　Two giant hands pull the Tao River,
　　The Tao River obediently circles the mountain peak.

　　Girls Dig Canals at the Edge of the Sky
　　The Min Mountains are ten thousand ren high, reaching the sky,
　　Girls dig canals at the edge of the sky,
　　White clouds are worn as flowers,
　　Picking stars to make earrings.
　　Beneath the hoe, the mountain bows its head,
　　Clear water follows the iron shovel's flow,
　　Girls come to the canal to see their reflection,
　　Celebrating the harvest all over the mountains and plains.

### 甘肃民歌

1958-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    甘肃民歌
    两只巨手牵洮河
    一脚踢翻华家岭，
    一脚踏平石头山，
    一钻凿穿九甸峡，
    一锤打通鬼门关。
    一炮轰破万丈岩，
    一锨挖得渠道宽，
    两只巨手牵洮河，
    洮河乖乖绕山尖。
    姑娘开渠在天边
    岷山万仞高连天，
    姑娘开渠在天边，
    白云朵朵当花戴，
    摘颗星星做耳环。
    镢头底下山低头，
    清水跟着铁锨流，
    姑娘渠边来照影，
    漫山遍野庆丰收。
