Title: North Korea Discovers Many New Mineral Deposits
Author:
Date: 1958-11-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th - According to the Korean Central News Agency on the 20th: More than a dozen promising deposits of nickel, copper, iron, coal, quartz, and placer gold have been discovered in Jagang Province, a mountainous region in northern Korea.

　　A nickel mine discovered by a geological exploration team affiliated with the Korean Ministry of Metal Industry has reserves of 50,000 tons. Agricultural cooperative members and Young Pioneers in Junggang County discovered seams of bituminous coal.

　　Under the call of the Korean Workers' Party, a mass movement to develop underground resources has been underway since October of this year. Since Korean geologists discovered a large iron ore deposit estimated to contain several hundred million tons in Yuson County, North Hamgyong Province, last month, the broad masses of the people have been discovering new metal mines and coal mines almost every day.

### 朝鲜发现许多新矿藏

1958-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜发现许多新矿藏
    据新华社平壤20日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社20日讯：在朝鲜北部山区慈江道已经发现十多个有希望的镍、铜、铁、煤、石英和沙金等矿藏。
    朝鲜金属工业省所属的一个地质勘探队发现的一个镍矿有五万吨蕴藏量。中江郡的农业合作社社员和少先队员们则发现了烟煤的煤层。
    在朝鲜劳动党的号召下，从今年10月份起已经开展了一个开发地下资源的群众性运动。从朝鲜地质学家们上个月在咸镜北道游仙郡发现一个蕴藏量估计有好几亿吨的大铁矿以来，广大人民几乎每天都在发现新的金属矿和煤矿。
