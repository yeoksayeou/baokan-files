Title: Report New Achievements, Accept New Tasks: National Youth Activist Congress Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1958-11-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The Second National Youth Socialist Construction Activist Congress is scheduled to open in Beijing on the afternoon of the 21st.

This congress is being held exactly three years and two months after the first National Youth Socialist Construction Activist Congress. Over the past three years, and especially in the past year, under the leadership of the Party, the youth of the country have, together with the people of the country, implemented the Party’s general line for socialist construction on all fronts. In the great leap forward, they have created many astonishing miracles with their selfless labor. Under the education and inspiration of the Party, the communist ideology, character, and style of the youth have also been greatly promoted. At this congress, the youth representatives from various regions, entrusted by the youth of the country, will report to the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao on the achievements they have made on all fronts, accept the new tasks proposed by the Party Central Committee to the youth of the country, and, through the congress, learn from each other, exchange experiences, and launch a movement to compare vigor, learn skills, catch up with the advanced, and create miracles, mobilizing the youth of the country to strive for the accelerated building of socialism.

The number of youth activists from various regions participating in this congress reaches five thousand, which is more than three times the number of participants in the first Youth Activist Congress. Among them are more than thirty ethnic groups, including Han, Mongolian, Hui, Zhuang, Uyghur, Tibetan, Manchu, Miao, Korean, Yi, Buyi, Kazakh, Dongxiang, Dong, Yao, Li, Dai, Bai, Hani, and Lisu. There are representatives of advanced individuals and advanced collectives from steel bases, machinery industry, construction industry, chemical industry, basic construction, light industry, postal and telecommunications, transportation, forestry, handicrafts, agriculture, animal husbandry, fishery, commerce, service industries, finance, culture and education, health, sports, science, public security, and militia, as well as representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and combat heroes.

Nearly half of the youth activists are representatives of advanced collectives. The vast majority of the representatives are Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members, and there are also more than forty "Red Scarves." The youngest representative is eleven years old, and the oldest is around thirty, with the vast majority being young people between the ages of nineteen and twenty-five. According to incomplete statistics, more than eight hundred are female youth activists and female representatives of youth advanced collectives.

The congress will be held for more than ten days. At the meeting, important instructions from the CPC Central Committee will be heard. A report will be heard from the responsible comrades of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League. More than one hundred youth activists will also speak at the conference, introducing their exemplary deeds and advanced experiences. The youth of Beijing will present gifts to the conference during the meeting.

### 汇报新成就  接受新任务  青年积极分子大会今日开幕

1958-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    汇报新成就  接受新任务
    青年积极分子大会今日开幕
    据新华社20日讯　第二次全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会定于21日下午在北京开幕。
    这次大会距离第一次全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会召开的时间整整是三年零两个月。三年来，特别是近一年来，全国青年在党的领导下，和全国人民一道在各个战线上贯彻执行党的社会主义建设总路线，在全民大跃进中以自己的忘我劳动创造出许多惊人的奇迹，青年们在党的教育和感召下，共产主义的思想、品质和风格也大大发扬。在这次大会上，各地的青年代表受全国青年的委托，将要向党中央和毛主席汇报他们在各个战线上所取得的成就，接受党中央向全国青年提出的新任务，并通过大会互相学习，交流经验，掀起一个比干劲，学本领，赶先进，创奇迹的运动，动员全国青年为加速建成社会主义而奋斗。
    参加这次大会的各地青年积极分子为数共达五千人，这个数字比第一次青年积极分子大会的参加人数增加了两倍以上。他们之中有汉、蒙、回、僮、维吾尔、藏、满、苗、朝鲜、彝、布依、哈萨克、东乡、侗、瑶、黎、傣、白、哈尼、傈僳等三十多个民族，有钢铁基地、机械工业、建筑工业、化学工业、基本建设、轻工业、邮电、交通、林业、手工业、农业、畜牧业、渔业、商业、服务性行业、金融、文教、卫生、体育、科学、公安、民兵等方面的先进人物和先进集体的代表，有中国人民解放军的代表和战斗英雄。
    青年积极分子中将近二分之一是先进集体的代表。绝大多数代表都是共产党员和青年团员，也有四十多名“红领巾”。代表们年龄最小的是十一岁，最大的是三十岁左右，绝大多数是十九岁到二十五岁的青年。根据不完全统计，有八百多人是女青年积极分子和青年先进集体的女代表。
    大会将要举行十几天。会上将听取中共中央的重要指示。听取团中央负责同志的报告，还将有一百多位青年积极分子在大会上发言，介绍他们的模范事迹和先进经验。北京市的青年将要在会议期间向大会献礼。
