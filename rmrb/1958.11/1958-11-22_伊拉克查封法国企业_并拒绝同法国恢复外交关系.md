Title: Iraq Seizes French Businesses and Refuses to Restore Diplomatic Relations with France
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 19th -- The government of the Republic of Iraq announced that, in view of the disgraceful atrocities committed by France in Algeria, it has refused to restore diplomatic relations with France and has seized all French companies and businesses operating in Iraq. The government of the Republic of Iraq has also prohibited all Iraqi companies and merchants from engaging in any economic relations with French companies and businesses, and has prohibited the import of all French goods, even through third parties.

### 伊拉克查封法国企业  并拒绝同法国恢复外交关系

1958-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克查封法国企业
    并拒绝同法国恢复外交关系
    新华社巴格达19日电　伊拉克共和国政府宣布，鉴于法国在阿尔及利亚犯下的可耻的暴行，它已经拒绝同法国恢复外交关系，并且查封了法国在伊拉克经营的所有公司和商号。伊拉克共和国政府还禁止伊拉克的一切公司和商人同法国公司和商号发生任何经济关系，同时禁止进口一切法国货物，即使是通过第三者。
