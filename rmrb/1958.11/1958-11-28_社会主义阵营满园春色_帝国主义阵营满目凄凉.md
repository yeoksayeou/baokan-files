Title: The Socialist Camp is Full of Spring Scenery, The Imperialist Camp is Full of Desolation

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, November 27th. The Czechoslovakian Party and Government Delegation, headed by Novotny, concluded its week-long visit to Bulgaria and left Sofia by special train this morning.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Bulgarian Party and Government leaders Zhivkov and Yugov, as well as diplomatic envoys from some countries stationed in Sofia.

### 社会主义阵营满园春色  帝国主义阵营满目凄凉

1958-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    社会主义阵营满园春色  帝国主义阵营满目凄凉
    新华社索非亚27日电　以诺沃提尼为首的捷克斯洛伐克党政代表团结束了在保加利亚为期一周的访问，已在今天上午乘专车离开索非亚。
    前往车站送行的有保加利亚党政领导人日夫科夫、于哥夫等人，以及一些国家驻索非亚的外交使节。
