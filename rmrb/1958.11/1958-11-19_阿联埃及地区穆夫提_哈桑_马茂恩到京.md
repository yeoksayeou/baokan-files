Title: Mufti of the Egyptian Region of the United Arab Republic, Hassan Ma'moun, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-11-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Hassan Ma'moun, Mufti (i.e., religious legal officer) of the Egyptian Region of the United Arab Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 17th for a friendly visit to China at the invitation of Burhan, Chairman of the China Islamic Association, and Da Pongsheng, Vice Chairman.

### 阿联埃及地区穆夫提  哈桑·马茂恩到京

1958-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联埃及地区穆夫提
    哈桑·马茂恩到京
    据新华社讯　应中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉、副主任达浦生邀请来我国进行友好访问的阿拉伯联合共和国埃及地区穆夫提（即宗教教法说明官）哈桑·马茂恩，已经在17日乘飞机到达北京。
