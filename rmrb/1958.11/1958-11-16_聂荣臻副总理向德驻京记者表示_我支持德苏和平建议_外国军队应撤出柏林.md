Title: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Tells German Correspondents in Beijing: I Support the Soviet Proposal for Peace; Foreign Troops Should Withdraw from Berlin
Author:
Date: 1958-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen met with Hanschke, a correspondent from the German News Agency stationed in Beijing, on November 15th. Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the meeting.

In the conversation, Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen stated that the Chinese government and the Chinese people fully agree with the proposals put forward by the government of the German Democratic Republic for a peaceful resolution of the German question and the easing of tensions in Europe. At the same time, we actively support the proposal of Comrade Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, on ending the occupation status of Berlin and withdrawing foreign troops from Berlin.

### 聂荣臻副总理向德驻京记者表示  我支持德苏和平建议  外国军队应撤出柏林

1958-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻副总理向德驻京记者表示
    我支持德苏和平建议  外国军队应撤出柏林
    新华社15日讯　国务院副总理聂荣臻于11月15日接见了德意志通讯社驻北京记者汉希克。接见时在座的有外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
    聂荣臻副总理在谈话中表示，中国政府和中国人民对德意志民主共和国政府为了和平解决德国问题和缓和欧洲紧张局势而提出的主张，表示完全同意，同时，积极支持苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫同志关于结束柏林的占领状态并从柏林撤退外国军队的建议。
