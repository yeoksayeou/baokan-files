Title: Chen Yi Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Gromyko
Author:
Date: 1958-11-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 6th: Foreign Minister Chen Yi today sent a telegram to Soviet Foreign Minister A.A. Gromyko, congratulating him on the 41st anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. The full text is as follows:
    To Comrade A.A. Gromyko, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:
    On the occasion of the 41st anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, please allow me to extend my warm and heartfelt congratulations to you.
    I wish the Soviet Union's peaceful foreign policy even more brilliant achievements!
    Long live the victory of the new era opened up by the October Revolution in international relations!
    Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people!
           Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
           November 6, 1958, in Beijing

    祝苏联的和平对外政策获得更加光辉的成就！

    中苏两国人民永恒的、牢不可破的友谊万岁！
           中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
           一九五八年十一月六日于北京
