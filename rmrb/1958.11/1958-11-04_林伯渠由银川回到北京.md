Title: Lin Boqu Returns to Beijing from Yinchuan
Author:
Date: 1958-11-04
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd: Lin Boqu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, who represented the CPC Central Committee, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao to Yinchuan to congratulate the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, returned to Beijing by train this afternoon.

Xie Fumin, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, who went to Yinchuan to offer congratulations, also returned to Beijing on the same train.

Those who greeted him at the station included Xi Zhongxun, Zhang Su, Gong Zirong, Sa Kongliao, and Xie Hechou.

When Lin Boqu left Yinchuan by train on the evening of the 1st, leading figures from the Ningxia Party, government, and military, as well as over a thousand people from various ethnic groups, went to the station to see him off. On the way from Yinchuan city to the station, more than five thousand people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life lined the streets to bid farewell. The people who saw him off were filled with excitement, applauding enthusiastically and shouting slogans, expressing their gratitude to the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao for their concern for the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region.

### 林伯渠由银川回到北京

1958-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

    林伯渠由银川回到北京
    新华社3日讯　代表中共中央、中央人民政府和毛主席到银川祝贺宁夏回族自治区成立的，中共中央政治局委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠，今天下午乘火车回到北京。
    到银川祝贺的全国人民代表大会民族委员会副主任委员谢扶民也同车回到北京。
    到车站迎接的有，习仲勋、张苏、龚子荣、萨空了、谢鹤筹等。
    林伯渠在1日晚间乘车离开银川时，宁夏党、政、军和各人民团体负责人以及一千多名各族群众到车站送行。在林伯渠由银川城到车站的途中，五千多各族各界群众，夹道欢送，欢送的人们满怀激动的心情，热烈鼓掌，高呼口号，感谢党中央和毛主席对宁夏回族自治区的关怀。
