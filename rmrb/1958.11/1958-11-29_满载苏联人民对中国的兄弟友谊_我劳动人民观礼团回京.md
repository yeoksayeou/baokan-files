Title: Filled with the Soviet People's Brotherly Friendship towards China, Our Laboring People's Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency report: Our laboring people's delegation, which participated in the celebrations for the 41st anniversary of the October Revolution, concluded its visit to the Soviet Union on the 27th and returned to China by "Tu-104" passenger plane from Moscow. Those who saw them off at the airport included Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Deputy Chairman Dubrovinina, Vizhilin, Deputy Chairman of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Sherbakov, a responsible staff member of the CPSU Central Committee. Liu Xiao, China's ambassador to the Soviet Union, and embassy staff also went to the airport to see them off.
    The delegation visited Moscow, Leningrad, Minsk, Kiev and other cities in the Soviet Union, and was warmly received by the local Soviet people like brothers.
    The delegation returned to Beijing on the afternoon of the 27th.

### 满载苏联人民对中国的兄弟友谊  我劳动人民观礼团回京

1958-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    满载苏联人民对中国的兄弟友谊
    我劳动人民观礼团回京
    据新华社讯　我国劳动人民参加十月革命四十一周年观礼团27日结束在苏联的访问，乘“图——一○四”客机离莫斯科回国。到机场欢送的有苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫、副主席杜伯洛维娜、苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会副主席维兹日林、苏共中央负责工作人员谢尔巴科夫等人。我国驻苏联大使刘晓及使馆人员也到机场送行。
    代表团在苏联访问了莫斯科、列宁格勒、明斯克、基辅等城市，受到当地苏联人民兄弟般的热情接待。
    观礼团在27日下午回到北京。
