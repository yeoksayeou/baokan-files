Title: American People Oppose Creating "Two Chinas" - U.S. "Workers Weekly" Denounces Dulles's Aggressive Conspiracy
Author:
Date: 1958-11-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd, New York dispatch: The American "Workers Weekly" published an article by Joseph North on November 2nd, which accused the U.S. government of plotting to create "two Chinas."

The article pointed out that the ultimate goal of the policy pursued by the U.S. government is to overthrow the government of the People's China. "Dulles is trying by all means to continue his nonsense, saying that the Chinese civil war is an international dispute. His 'two Chinas' policy is to create a 'legitimate' excuse to overthrow the People's (China) government in some way at some time."

The article said, "No matter what the circumstances, (the U.S. government's conspiracy to create "two Chinas") is against the interests of the American people and the world—because this policy contains the bad seeds of a third world war." Referring to Dulles's trip to Taiwan, the article pointed out the contradictions and differences between the United States and the Chiang Kai-shek authorities. The article specifically pointed out that the U.S. and Chiang Kai-shek have different interpretations of Chiang Kai-shek's statement that he "will not use force to return to the mainland."

### 美国人民反对制造“两个中国”  美国“工人周报”斥杜勒斯的侵略阴谋

1958-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    美国人民反对制造“两个中国”
    美国“工人周报”斥杜勒斯的侵略阴谋
    新华社3日讯　纽约消息：美国“工人周报”2日刊载了约瑟夫·诺恩的一篇文章，文章指责美国政府阴谋制造“两个中国”。
    文章指出，美国政府推行的政策的最终目的是推翻人民中国政府，“杜勒斯千方百计地继续他的无稽之谈，说什么中国内战是一个国际性的争端。他的那种关于‘两个中国’的政策就是想制造‘合法的’借口以便根据某些方式在某一个时候推翻人民（中国）政府。”
    文章说“不管情况如何，（美国政府制造“两个中国”的阴谋）是违背美国全国人民和全世界的利益的——因为这种政策蕴藏着爆发第三次世界大战的坏种子。”文章在谈到杜勒斯的台湾之行时，指出了美国和蒋介石当局之间的矛盾和分歧。文章特别指出，关于蒋介石“不使用武力重返大陆”的说法，美蒋之间的解释是不相同的。
