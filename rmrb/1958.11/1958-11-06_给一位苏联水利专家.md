Title: To a Soviet Water Conservancy Expert
Author: Sun Xiaoping
Date: 1958-11-06
Page: 8

    To a Soviet Water Conservancy Expert
    Sun Xiaoping, Sanmenxia Engineering Bureau
  You inspect the Yellow River under the scorching sun,
  Without a straw hat or umbrella,
  Endlessly wiping the sweat from your face,
  Your clothes soaked with sweat.
  You seem to see the clear river water,
  See the golden wheat waves and the towering chimneys,
  Perhaps because you are too excited,
  You can't help but feel a surge of emotion.

### 给一位苏联水利专家
孙肖平
1958-11-06
第8版()
专栏：

    给一位苏联水利专家
    三门峡工程局　孙肖平
  你头顶烈日在黄河边视察，
  不戴草帽也不撑伞，
  脸上的汗水揩不断，
  身上的汗湿透了衣衫。
  你仿佛看到清清的河水，
  看到金黄的麦浪和林立的烟囱，
  大约是因为太兴奋了，
  你禁不住一阵阵鼻子发酸。
