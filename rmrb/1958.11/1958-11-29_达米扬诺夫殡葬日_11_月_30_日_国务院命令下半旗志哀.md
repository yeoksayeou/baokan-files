Title: Damyanov's Funeral Day - November 30th - State Council Orders Half-Mast Flag in Mourning

Author:

Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: The State Council of the People's Republic of China issued an order on November 28th that on November 30th – the day of the funeral of Comrade Georgi Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria – all organs, troops, factories, mines, enterprises, schools, and people's organizations in the locations of the People's Committees of all provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government, and the People's Committees of all cities, shall fly flags at half-mast for one day of mourning. (Attached picture)

Party and state leaders visited the Bulgarian Embassy in China to offer condolences on Damyanov's death. Front row from right to left: Chen Shutong, Guo Moruo, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yi, Li Jishen, Shen Junru.
　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong

### 达米扬诺夫殡葬日—11月30日  国务院命令下半旗志哀

1958-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    达米扬诺夫殡葬日—11月30日
    国务院命令下半旗志哀
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国国务院11月28日发出命令，在11月30日——保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席格·达米扬诺夫同志殡葬的日子，全国各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会和各市人民委员会所在地的机关、部队、厂矿、企业、学校和人民团体一律下半旗一日志哀。（附图片）
    党和国家领导人到保加利亚驻中国大使馆吊唁达米扬诺夫逝世。前排自右至左：陈叔通、郭沫若、周恩来、陈　毅、李济深、沈钧儒。
　　　　　　　新华社记者　邹健东摄
