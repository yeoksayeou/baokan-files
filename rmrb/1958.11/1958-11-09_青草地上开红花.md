Title: Red Flowers Blooming on the Green Grassland
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-09
Page: 5

Red flowers blooming on the green grassland,
Joyful news arriving at my home,
Longing day and night,
The news of the establishment of the People's Commune has come.
　—Kazakh folk song

### 青草地上开红花

1958-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    青草地上开红花青草地上开红花，有件喜事到我家，白天想啊夜间盼，建立人民公社的消息传来啦。
　——哈萨克民歌
