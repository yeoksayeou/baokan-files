Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Argentinian Medical Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-11-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Argentinian medical delegation headed by Professor Camis at noon today. During the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi spoke at length about the friendship between the Chinese people and the people of Latin America, and wished the friendship between the Chinese people and the peoples of Latin America and Argentina would grow day by day. Vice Premier Chen Yi, through the guests, expressed his gratitude for the contributions made by Argentinian leprosy expert Professor Schuhmann, who came to China last year at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association to give lectures.

Professor Camis, head of the Argentinian medical delegation, spoke enthusiastically about his impressions of visiting various parts of China, praised the great achievements of China's industry and agriculture, and said that when he visits again in five years, he would like to work to strengthen Sino-Argentinian friendship. The guests and hosts chatted happily for an hour before saying goodbye. Present at the reception were Fu Lianzhang, President of the Chinese Medical Association, and Yang Keqin, Professor of Orthopedics at Beijing Medical College, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见阿根廷医学代表团

1958-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见阿根廷医学代表团
    新华社27日讯　陈毅副总理今天中午接见了以团长卡密斯教授为首的阿根廷医学代表团。接见时，陈毅副总理畅谈了中国人民和拉丁美洲人民的友谊，并祝中国人民同拉丁美洲人民和阿根廷人民的友谊日益发展。陈毅副总理通过来宾们，对去年应中华医学会邀请前来我国讲学的阿根廷麻疯病学专家斯胡曼教授所作的贡献，表示感谢。
    阿根廷医学代表团团长卡密斯教授畅谈参观中国各地观感，盛赞中国工农业的伟大成就，表示五年之后当再来观光，愿意为加强中阿友谊而努力。宾主们欢谈一小时才告别。接见时在座的有，中华医学会会长傅连暲，北京医学院骨科教授杨克勤等。
