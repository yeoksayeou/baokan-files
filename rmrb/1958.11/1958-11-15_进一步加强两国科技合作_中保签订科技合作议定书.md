Title: Further Strengthen Scientific and Technological Cooperation Between the Two Countries: China and Bulgaria Sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol
Author:
Date: 1958-11-15
Page: 4

    Further Strengthen Scientific and Technological Cooperation Between the Two Countries
    China and Bulgaria Sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol
    Xinhua News Agency, November 14th: The third session of the Sino-Bulgarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee was held in Beijing from November 2nd to 13th. According to the provisions of the meeting protocol, the People's Republic of China will provide the People's Republic of Bulgaria with technical data on food, hygiene, medicine, water conservancy and irrigation, metallurgy, coal mining equipment, etc., and will provide crop and tree seeds; the People's Republic of Bulgaria will provide the People's Republic of China with technical data on pectin production, hydrometeorological instruments, etc., as well as crop and tree seeds, and will send food canning experts to China for technical assistance.
    The protocol was signed by Yang Xiandong, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of Agriculture, and Stoyan Dzhulemezov, Chairman of the Bulgarian Group of the Committee and Vice Chairman of the Planning Committee.

### 进一步加强两国科技合作  中保签订科技合作议定书

1958-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    进一步加强两国科技合作
    中保签订科技合作议定书
    新华社14日讯　中国保加利亚科学技术合作委员会第三届会议，自11月2日到13日在北京举行。根据会议议定书的规定，中华人民共和国将供给保加利亚人民共和国食品、卫生、医药、水利灌溉、冶金、采煤设备等技术资料，并且供给农作物和树木种子；保加利亚人民共和国将供给中华人民共和国果胶生产、水文气象仪器等技术资料、以及农作物和树木种子，并且将派遣食品罐头专家到中国进行技术援助。
    在议定书上签字的是委员会中国组主席、农业部副部长杨显东和委员会保加利亚组主席、计划委员会副主席斯托扬·久列梅佐夫。
