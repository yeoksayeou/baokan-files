Title: Chairman Mao is the Sun
Author:
Date: 1958-11-09
Page: 5

Chairman Mao is the sun
Chairman Mao is the sun, shining brightly on Xinjiang,
People of all ethnic groups work hard,
Songs of harvest spread everywhere.
          -- Uyghur folk song

1958-11-09

          ——维吾尔民歌
