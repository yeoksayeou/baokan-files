Title: Premier Zhou Sends Telegram to Prime Minister Sihanouk, Congratulating Cambodia's Independence Day

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to Cambodian Prime Minister Sihanouk, warmly congratulating the Kingdom of Cambodia's Independence Day. The telegram is as follows:

His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia:

On the occasion of the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia, I would like to extend warm congratulations to Your Highness and, through Your Highness, to the Government and people of the Kingdom of Cambodia on behalf of the Government and people of the People's Republic of China.

I wish Your Highness and the Cambodian people further achievements in the cause of safeguarding national independence and seeking national prosperity. May the friendly and cooperative relations established between China and Cambodia on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence be continuously consolidated and developed.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 8, 1958, Beijing

### 周总理电西哈努克首相  祝贺柬埔寨独立纪念日

1958-11-09
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理电西哈努克首相
    祝贺柬埔寨独立纪念日
    新华社8日讯　周恩来总理今天致电柬埔寨王国首相西哈努克，热烈祝贺柬埔寨王国独立纪念日。电文如下：
    柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下：
    值此柬埔寨王国独立纪念日之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向殿下并且通过殿下向柬埔寨王国政府和柬埔寨人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    祝殿下和柬埔寨人民在维护民族独立和谋求国家繁荣的事业中获得进一步的成就。祝中柬两国在和平共处五项原则的基础上所建立起来的友好合作关系不断巩固和发展。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
                      1958年11月8日于北京
