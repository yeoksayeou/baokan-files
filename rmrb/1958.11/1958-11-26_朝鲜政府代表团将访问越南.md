Title: Korean Government Delegation to Visit Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1958-11-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 25th. The Vietnam Democratic Republic's Ministry of Foreign Affairs' announcement, published in today's newspapers, states that at the invitation of the government of the Vietnam Democratic Republic, a government delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, will visit Vietnam on November 28th.

### 朝鲜政府代表团将访问越南

1958-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团将访问越南
    新华社河内25日电　今天各报刊载的越南民主共和国外交部的公告说，以金日成首相为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团应越南民主共和国政府的邀请，将在11月28日到越南访问。
