Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De's Gift to Prime Minister Sihanouk: Ten "Jiefang" Brand Trucks Arrive in Phnom Penh
Author:
Date: 1958-11-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 18th. The ten "Jiefang" brand trucks that Vice Chairman Zhu De gave to Prince Sihanouk as a gift during his visit to China in August this year arrived in Phnom Penh on the evening of the 17th.

These Chinese-made trucks are the first to be transported to Cambodia. They are parked next to the Phnom Penh Cambodian Royal Socialist Youth League Building. Since early this morning, many people have been visiting these trucks, carefully observing this Chinese product.

### 朱德副主席给西哈努克首相的礼品  十辆“解放牌”卡车运抵金边

1958-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席给西哈努克首相的礼品
    十辆“解放牌”卡车运抵金边
    新华社金边18日电　西哈努克亲王在今年8月间访问中国时，朱德副主席送给他的礼物十辆“解放牌”卡车在17日晚间运到金边。
    这种中国制造的卡车是第一次运到柬埔寨。他们停在金边柬埔寨皇家社会主义青年联盟大厦旁边。从今天清早开始，就有许多人去参观这些卡车，他们仔细观察了这种中国的产品。
