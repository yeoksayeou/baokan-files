Title: Czechoslovakian Sports and Hunting Weapons Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th. The Czechoslovakian Sports, Training and Hunting Weapons Exhibition opened in Beijing this afternoon.

    The exhibition is held in the sample exhibition hall of the Commercial Office of the Czechoslovakian Embassy in China. On display are approximately one hundred pieces of various high-quality international competition target shooting weapons, pre-military training weapons, pistols, rifles, and air guns. Czechoslovakia has produced firearms for more than 120 years. Their sports and other weapons exhibited at the 1958 Brussels International Exposition won all the awards.

### 捷运动射猎武器展览会在京开幕

1958-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    捷运动射猎武器展览会在京开幕
    新华社25日讯　捷克斯洛伐克运动、教练及射猎用武器展览会，今天下午在北京开幕。
    展览会设在捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使馆商务处的样品展览所内。展出各种精良的国际竞赛打靶射击用武器、兵役前实习用武器，手枪、步枪和气枪，共约一百件。捷克斯洛伐克生产枪支有一百二十多年的历史。他们在1958年布鲁塞尔国际博览会上展出的运动等方面的武器，曾全部获奖。
