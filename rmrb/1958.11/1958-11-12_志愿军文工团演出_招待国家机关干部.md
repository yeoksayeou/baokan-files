Title: PLA Volunteer Army Performing Arts Troupe Performs for State Organ Cadres
Author:
Date: 1958-11-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Performing Arts Troupe performed tonight at the Tianqiao Theater, entertaining staff members from central state organs.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and responsible persons from relevant government departments watched the performance.

The performing arts troupe presented a song and dance living newspaper "Volunteer Army Battle Song" as well as chorus, crosstalk, and clapper talk programs. The program also included Korean folk musical instrument "Gayageum" solos and the dance "Sword Dance". Each program was warmly applauded by the audience.

After the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Minister Luo Ruiqing, and others went on stage to shake hands with the performers, expressing their congratulations. Representatives of the state organ staff presented flower baskets to the performing arts troupe.

### 志愿军文工团演出  招待国家机关干部

1958-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军文工团演出  招待国家机关干部
    新华社11日讯　中国人民志愿军文工团今晚在天桥剧场演出，招待中央国家机关工作人员。
    陈毅副总理和政府有关部门负责人观看了演出。
    文工团演出了歌舞活报剧“志愿军战歌”及合唱、相声、快书等节目。节目中还有朝鲜民族乐器“枷椰琴”弹唱和舞蹈“刀舞”。每个节目都受到观众的热烈鼓掌。
    演出结束后，陈毅副总理、罗瑞卿部长等登台同演员握手，表示祝贺。国家机关工作人员代表向文工团献了花篮。
