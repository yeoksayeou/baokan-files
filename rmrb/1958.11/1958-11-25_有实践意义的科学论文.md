    Scientific Papers with Practical Significance

    The article "The Science of High Wheat Yields" written by the Crop Breeding and Cultivation Research Institute of the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences, published on page 7 of the November 12th People's Daily, is a scientific paper with both theoretical value and practical significance.

    This year, China's wheat achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest, and the miracles in production practice shattered the outdated concepts of agricultural science. The paper incisively analyzes the high-yield experiences of wheat in various parts of China and summarizes new scientific theories. From this article, we can also see that China's scientific research work is closely integrated with production practice and the working masses.
      Wuhan Suburbs, Red Star Commune, Feng Yi

### 有实践意义的科学论文
冯一
1958-11-25
第8版()
专栏：读者·作者·编者

    有实践意义的科学论文
    11月12日人民日报第七版上中国农业科学院作物育种栽培研究所写的“小麦高产的科学”，是一篇既有理论价值、又有实践意义的科学论文。
    今年我国小麦得到了史无前例的大丰收，生产实践中的奇迹粉碎了农业科学的陈腐观念。论文中精辟地分析了我国各地小麦高产经验，总结出新的科学理论。从这篇文章也看到我国的科学研究工作正在同生产实践、同劳动群众密切结合。
      武汉郊区红星公社　冯一
