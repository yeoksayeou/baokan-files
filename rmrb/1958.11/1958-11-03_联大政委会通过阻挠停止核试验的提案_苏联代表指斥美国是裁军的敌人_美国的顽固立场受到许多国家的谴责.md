Title: UN Political Committee Passes Proposal Obstructing the Cessation of Nuclear Tests; Soviet Representative Accuses the US of Being an Enemy of Disarmament; US Stubborn Stance Condemned by Many Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-11-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd November dispatch from New York: The United Nations General Assembly's Political Committee concluded its discussion on disarmament on October 31. Regarding the issue of halting nuclear weapon tests, the United States, colluding with a group of countries aligned with its position, forcibly passed the US proposal presented under the name of the "Seventeen-Nation Proposal."

The US proposal avoids explicitly stipulating that the United Nations General Assembly demands the unconditional and permanent cessation of nuclear weapon tests. This proposal superficially and hypocritically calls for efforts to reach an agreement on the cessation of nuclear weapon tests as soon as possible and requires all parties participating in the Geneva negotiations not to conduct further tests during the negotiations. In reality, it merely serves the US and British stance of fundamentally unwilling to unconditionally and permanently cease testing.

The Soviet proposal, which explicitly stipulated that the United Nations General Assembly demands the unconditional and permanent cessation of nuclear tests, as well as the compromise proposal put forward by India and thirteen other countries, which stipulated that the Assembly demands an immediate cessation of tests until the countries concerned reach an agreement on technical measures for monitoring the cessation of tests, were both rejected in the Political Committee by the United States and the majority who followed it.

When the US proposal was put to a vote, there were forty-nine votes in favor, nine against, and twenty-three abstentions. It is noteworthy that the majority of Asian and African countries, and half of the European countries (including some neutral countries such as Austria, Finland, and Sweden) did not vote in favor of the US proposal, and many of them severely condemned it.

The United States also used its mechanical majority to veto the Soviet proposal to reduce the military budgets of the four countries of the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France by 10% to 15% and to use the saved funds to assist underdeveloped countries.

In this way, the United States and the countries that echo it have obstructed the UN General Assembly's Political Committee from passing resolutions on disarmament that are in line with the interests of the people of all countries.

Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd dispatch according to TASS, New York: The Soviet delegation to the United Nations General Assembly issued a statement on November 1 regarding the passage of the "Seventeen-Nation Proposal" by the General Assembly's Political Committee.

The statement pointed out that this result occurred under abnormal circumstances, with the US delegation exerting pressure and engaging in unscrupulous tactics. The United States has once again obstructed the United Nations from passing resolutions that are in line with the interests of peace and the security of all countries. The statement said that the United States started the voting machine and used the votes of its military bloc allies to pass a resolution that is beneficial to it, a resolution which not only cannot promote the complete and unconditional prohibition of nuclear weapon tests, but also hinders the satisfactory resolution of this issue.

The statement said that if anyone still had illusions about the true position of the US and British governments on the issue of stopping nuclear weapon tests, then now this illusion is completely shattered.

### 联大政委会通过阻挠停止核试验的提案  苏联代表指斥美国是裁军的敌人  美国的顽固立场受到许多国家的谴责

1958-11-03
第5版()
专栏：

    联大政委会通过阻挠停止核试验的提案
    苏联代表指斥美国是裁军的敌人
    美国的顽固立场受到许多国家的谴责
    据新华社2日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会政治委员会10月31日结束了关于裁军问题的讨论。在关于停止核武器试验问题方面，美国纠合一批附合它的立场的国家强行通过了以“十七国提案”名义提出的美国提案。
    美国提案避免明确规定联合国大会要求无条件地永远停止核武器试验。这个提案表面上虚伪地要求努力早日就停止核武器试验问题达成协议以及要求参加日内瓦谈判的各方在谈判期间不进一步进行试验，实际上它只是为美英根本不愿意无条件地永远停止试验的立场服务。
    明确规定联合国大会要求无条件地永远停止核试验的苏联提案，以及规定大会要求立即停止试验直到有关国家就关于监督停止试验的技术措施达成协议时为止的印度和其他十三国提出的折衷性提案，在政治委员会上都被美国以及随从它的多数拒绝了。
    在表决美国提案时，赞成的有四十九票，九票反对，二十三票弃权。值得注意的是：大多数亚非国家，半数欧洲国家（其中包括奥地利、芬兰、瑞典等一些中立国家）都没有对美国提案投赞成票，而且其中许多国家严厉地谴责了它。
    美国并且利用机械多数否决了苏联关于削减苏美英法四国军事预算10％到15％和把节省下来的部分资金援助不发达国家的提案。
    这样，美国和同美国一鼻孔出气的国家就阻挠了联大政治委员会在裁军问题通过符合各国人民利益的决议。
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：苏联出席联合国大会代表团在1日就大会政治委员会通过“十七国提案”发表声明。
    声明指出，这一结果是在美国代表团施加压力和玩弄不体面的手腕的不正常情况下发生的。美国这一次又阻挠联合国通过符合和平和各国人民安全利益的决议。声明说，美国开动了表决机器，用它的军事集团盟国的票通过了对它有利的决议，这个决议不仅不能促进完全地、无条件地禁止核武器试验，而且妨碍圆满解决这一问题。
    声明说，如果有人对美英两国政府在停止核武器试验问题上的真正立场还有幻想的话，那么现在这种幻想彻底破灭了。
    声明接着强调说，但是值得注意的还有这样一个重要的事实：大多数亚非国家（其中包括印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸、锡兰、阿拉伯联合共和国、伊拉克、沙特阿拉伯、摩洛哥、加纳、埃塞俄比亚、日本），半数欧洲国家（其中包括芬兰、瑞典、奥地利这样一些中立国家）都没有给美国的决议投赞成票。而且其中许多国家严厉地谴责了它。
    声明说，美国公然反对停止核武器试验，拒绝苏联关于削减四大国军备预算的建议，从而表明它打算继续进行军备竞赛和使国际局势尖锐化。由此可见，美国代表的爱好和平的言论只不过是一种迷惑公众的掩护而已。这就是这次辩论的重要总结。
    声明接着指出，美国和盲目支持它的军事政策的英国在绝大多数的人类的眼睛中再一次暴露了它们是和平和裁军的敌人。它们必须对它们这种行动的全部后果负责。欧洲、亚洲、非洲和美洲一切真诚的和平拥护者都不能不谴责它们这种行动。
    声明最后说，政治委员会通过的这项决议是联合国通过的被生活所抛弃的许多决议中的一个。这个决议不能够阻止各国人民和各个爱好和平国家的强大运动，他们都要求缓和国际紧张局势、停止军备竞赛和无条件地永远停止核武器试验。
