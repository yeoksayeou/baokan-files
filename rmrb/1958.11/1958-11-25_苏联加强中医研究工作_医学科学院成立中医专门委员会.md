Title: The Soviet Union Strengthens Research on Traditional Chinese Medicine; Academy of Medical Sciences Establishes Specialized Committee on Traditional Chinese Medicine
Author:
Date: 1958-11-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 24th: TASS reports: The Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences has established a specialized committee to lead scientific research into acupuncture and other therapies widely used in Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM).

    The Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences recently held a meeting to discuss the establishment of the TCM Committee. Soviet scientists who had studied the practical experience of TCM in China spoke at the meeting. They cited a large number of facts to prove that traditional Chinese acupuncture has great significance in medical treatment. Professor Graschenkov, the head of the laboratory specializing in the study of the theoretical foundations of Chinese medicine, said that acupuncture is effective in treating high blood pressure, migraines, neuritis, and bronchial asthma.

    Currently, the Soviet Union has laboratories in Moscow and Leningrad specializing in the study of traditional Chinese medical methods. A group of specially trained doctors work in these laboratories, and they are preparing to publish a large number of books on acupuncture.

### 苏联加强中医研究工作  医学科学院成立中医专门委员会

1958-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联加强中医研究工作
    医学科学院成立中医专门委员会
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联医学科学院成立了一个专门委员会，来领导对中医所广泛采用的针灸疗法和其他疗法的科学研究工作。
    苏联医学科学院主席团日前举行会议，讨论了成立中医委员会的问题。曾在中国研究中医实际经验的苏联科学家们纷纷在会上发言。他们列举了大量事实，证明中国传统的针灸疗法在医疗上有很大的意义。专门从事中国医学理论基础的研究工作的实验室领导人格拉申科夫教授说，用针灸疗法来治疗血压高、偏头痛、神经炎、支气管喘息症效果良好。
    现在，苏联在莫斯科和列宁格勒各设有一个实验室，专门研究中国传统的医疗方法。有一批经过专门训练的医生在这些实验室工作，他们准备大量出版有关针灸疗法的书籍。
