Title: Nie Rongzhen Receives Bulgarian Science and Technology Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th - Vice Premier of the State Council, Nie Rongzhen, today received Stoyan Djulemezov, head of the Bulgarian Science and Technology Delegation, and all members of the delegation, who are in China to attend the third meeting of the China-Bulgaria Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee.

    Present at the reception were Yang Xiandong, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the China-Bulgaria Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee and Vice Minister of Agriculture, Wang Xinyuan, member and Vice Minister of Light Industry, and others.

### 聂荣臻接见保科技代表团

1958-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻接见保科技代表团
    新华社13日讯　国务院副总理聂荣臻今天接见了前来我国出席中国保加利亚科学技术合作委员会第三届会议的保加利亚科学技术代表团团长斯托扬·久列梅佐夫和代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有，中国保加利亚科学技术合作委员会中国组主席、农业部副部长杨显东、委员、轻工业部副部长王新元等。
