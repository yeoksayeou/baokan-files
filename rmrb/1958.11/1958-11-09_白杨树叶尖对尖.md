Title: Poplar Leaves Tip to Tip
Author:
Date: 1958-11-09
Page: 5

    Poplar leaves tip to tip
    Poplar leaves tip to tip,
    My heart is linked with Little Sister's.
    Let's speak our minds when we meet:
    Let's see who leaps forward the fastest.
          ——New Hui Folk Song

### 白杨树叶尖对尖

1958-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    白杨树叶尖对尖白杨树叶尖对尖，我和尕妹心相连。见面说出知心话：看谁跃进在前面。
          ——回族新民歌
