Title: First Creation of Rapid Chemical Analyzer
Author:
Date: 1958-11-28
Page: 7

Scientific Trends

    The workers Zhou Zongxiang, Wang Zongxiang, and others from the Materials Research Institute of the Mechanical Manufacturing and Technology Science Research Institute, based on their practical experience, have pioneered a set of automatic chemical analyzers that can solve the chemical processes for manganese, silicon, and phosphorus in ordinary steel.

    The main parts of the instrument are: automatic equipment that starts from dissolving the sample to preparing solutions of a certain concentration, automatic equipment that changes the compounds required for a certain element, automatic quantitative dispensing equipment, automatic color development equipment, and automatic glass washing system equipment. These individual automated devices are combined into a whole automated device controlled by an electric motor mechanical system. Using this instrument to analyze manganese, silicon, and phosphorus in ordinary steel only requires pressing a button, and the entire analysis process can be completed in 3.5-5 minutes; using it in conjunction with a Pulfrich photometer can obtain accurate results, avoiding human error. The successful manufacture of the Type I automatic analyzer has freed chemical analysts from tedious labor. This is a major achievement in changing manual labor in metallurgical laboratories.

### 首创特快化学分析仪

1958-11-28
第7版()
专栏：科学动态

    首创特快化学分析仪
    机械制造与工艺科学研究院材料研究所的工作者周宗祥、王宗祥等，根据自己的实践经验，首创了一套能够解决普通钢中锰硅磷三元素的化学工序自动分析仪。
    仪器中的主要部分是：自动的从溶解试样开始起配制一定浓度溶液的设备、自动的改变某元素所需要的化合物的设备、自动定量分液设备、自动发色设备以及自动洗涤玻璃系统的设备等。这些个别自动化设备，组合成为以电机机械系统来控制的整个自动化设备。使用这台仪器来分析普通钢中锰硅磷只需揿揿按钮，全部分析工序就能于3.5—5分钟完毕；用它配合以普氏光度计能得到准确的结果。避免人为的误差。Ⅰ型自动分析仪的制造成功，解除了化学分析人员的烦倦的劳动。这是改变金属化验室手工劳动的重大成就。
