Title: Birds of a Feather Flock Together; US Democratic Leader Promises Support for Republican Government's Foreign Policy
Author:
Date: 1958-11-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 8th. News: U.S. Senate Democratic leader Johnson guaranteed on the 7th that the Democrats, who won the election, will continue to support the Eisenhower government's foreign policy in Congress for the next two years.

He said that a country "cannot exist if it expresses two opinions on foreign affairs and national defense."

At the same time, Johnson also advocated for a review of current U.S. foreign policy "so that responsible officials may be able to propose some bold and imaginative new plans."

### 两貉原属一丘  美民主党领袖作出了保证  支持共和党政府外交政策

1958-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    两貉原属一丘
    美民主党领袖作出了保证
    支持共和党政府外交政策
    新华社8日讯　华盛顿消息：美国参议院民主党领袖约翰逊7日保证，获得了选举胜利的民主党人今后两年内将在国会中继续支持艾森豪威尔政府的外交政策。
    他说，一个国家“要是在外交事务和国防方面发表两种意见，它就不能够存在”。
    同时，约翰逊又主张对美国当前的外交政策进行一番检查，“以便使负责官员有可能提出某些大胆而富有想像力的新计划来”。
