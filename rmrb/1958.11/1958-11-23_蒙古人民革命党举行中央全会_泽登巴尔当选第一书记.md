Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Holds Central Plenary Session; Tsedenbal Elected First Secretary
Author:
Date: 1958-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 22nd: According to a report by the Mongolian News Agency: The Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party held a plenary session from November 20th to 22nd. The meeting elected Comrade Tsedenbal as the First Secretary of the Party Central Committee and Comrade Damba as the Second Secretary.

    The plenary session, in accordance with Comrade Damba's request, relieved him of his original position as First Secretary of the Party Central Committee. Damba made this request in order to strengthen the centralized leadership of the Party.

    The Central Plenary Session also relieved Comrade Sürenjav and Comrade Damdin of their positions as Secretaries of the Central Committee. These two comrades have been assigned to other posts.

    The plenary session also discussed the current situation of state-owned and cooperative trade and measures to further improve the work, as well as the tasks of improving the work of the Mongolian Trade Unions.

    The Central Plenary Session made corresponding resolutions on the above issues.

### 蒙古人民革命党举行中央全会  泽登巴尔当选第一书记

1958-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党举行中央全会
    泽登巴尔当选第一书记
    新华社乌兰巴托22日电　据蒙古通讯社报道：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会11月20日到22日举行了全体会议。会议选举了泽登巴尔同志为党中央第一书记，达姆巴同志为第二书记。
    全体会议根据达姆巴同志的请求，解除了他原来担任的党中央第一书记的职务，达姆巴为了加强党的集中领导而提出了这项请求。
    中央全会还解除了苏伦扎布同志和达姆丁同志中央书记的职务，这两位同志另有任命。
    这次全体会议还讨论了关于当前国营贸易和合作贸易的状况和进一步改进工作的措施，以及关于改善蒙古工会工作的任务。
    中央全会就上述问题作出了相应的决议。
