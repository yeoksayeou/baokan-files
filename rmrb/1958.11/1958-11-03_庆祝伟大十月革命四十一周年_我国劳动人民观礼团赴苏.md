Title: To Celebrate the 41st Anniversary of the Great October Revolution, Our Country's Laboring People's Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-11-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: To celebrate the 41st anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, the Chinese Laboring People's Delegation, consisting of twenty members, departed for Moscow by plane on the morning of November 2nd. The head of the delegation is Liu Lantao, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and alternate secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The deputy heads are Qian Ying, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, deputy secretary of the Central Supervisory Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Minister of Supervision of the People's Republic of China; Bao Erhan, vice chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and deputy director of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China; Zhao Shoushan, Standing Committee Member of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Governor of Shaanxi Province. The members of the delegation (in order of stroke count of surnames) are Wang Wenjuan, Wang Tiemin, Liu Xuexin, Li Chengzhi, Song Fugen, Lu Huixian, Ma Feng, Ji Jie, Zhou Guanwu, Zheng Yiping, Hou Yong, Yang Kanghua, Liao Mosha, Liao Bokang, Jiang Yan, Dai Fanglan, and Xie Bingxin. Head of delegation Liu Lantao will go to the Soviet Union directly from Denmark, and members Liu Xuexin and Xie Bingxin have already arrived in Moscow.

At the airport to see them off were Gong Zirong, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Qi Yanming, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council; Shen Bochun, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the CPPCC; Wang Yutian, Director of the Department of Socialist Countries of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense; Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director; Liao Chengzhi and Shao Lizi, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General; Qu Tangliang and Wang Zhixiang, Directors; Xi Zhanyuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Liu Xiyuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League; Wu Qian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation; Zhang Xiaomei, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Zeng Ping, Secretary-General, and others.

V. Lixiakin, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, and other responsible staff members were also at the airport to see them off.

### 庆祝伟大十月革命四十一周年  我国劳动人民观礼团赴苏

1958-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝伟大十月革命四十一周年
    我国劳动人民观礼团赴苏
    新华社2日讯　应邀前往苏联庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十一周年的中国劳动人民观礼团一行二十人，于2日上午起程飞往莫斯科。代表团团长是中共中央委员、中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛，副团长是中共中央委员、中共中央监察委员会副书记、中华人民共和国监察部部长钱瑛，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会民族委员会副主任委员包尔汉，中共陕西省委常委、陕西省省长赵寿山。团员（以姓氏笔划为序）是王文娟、王铁民、刘学新、李澄之、宋富根、陆惠先、马烽、季洁、周贯五、郑依平、侯永、杨康华、廖沫沙、廖伯康、蒋燕、戴芳澜、谢冰心。团长刘澜涛将由丹麦直接去苏联，团员刘学新、谢冰心已先期到莫斯科。
    到机场欢送的有中共中央办公厅副主任龚子荣，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭，政协全国委员会副秘书长申伯纯，外交部社会主义国家司司长王雨田，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若、副主任张致祥，中苏友好协会总会副会长廖承志、邵力子、副秘书长戈宝权、理事区棠亮、王之相，中华全国总工会书记处书记郗占元，共青团中央书记处书记刘西元，全国妇联书记处书记吴綪，北京市中苏友好协会副会长张晓梅、秘书长曾平等人。
    苏联大使馆参赞魏立夏金和其他负责工作人员也到机场欢送。
