Title: Indonesian Ambassador to China Calls on Premier Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1958-11-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. Wiyogo Atmodarminto, the Indonesian Ambassador to China, this afternoon paid a visit to Premier Kim Il-sung, who is leading the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on a visit to our country.

Present at the meeting were Nam Il, Vice-Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea, and Li Yong-ho, the Korean Ambassador to China.

### 印度尼西亚驻华大使拜会金日成首相

1958-11-24
第2版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚驻华大使拜会金日成首相
    新华社23日讯　印度尼西亚驻我国大使维约普拉诺托，今天下午拜会了率领朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团正在我国访问的金日成首相。
    拜会时，在座的有朝鲜内阁副首相兼外务相南日和朝鲜驻我国大使李永镐。
