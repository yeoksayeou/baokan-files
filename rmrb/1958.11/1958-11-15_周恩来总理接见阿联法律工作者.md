Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Legal Workers from the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 14th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the delegation of legal workers from the Egyptian region of the United Arab Republic, headed by Hafez Sabeq, Attorney General of the United Arab Republic, this evening. Present at the reception were Wu Defeng, Zhang Zhizhang, and Zhang Youyu, vice presidents of the Chinese Political Science and Law Society. Ragab, the Ambassador of the United Arab Republic to China, was also present at the reception.

### 周恩来总理接见阿联法律工作者

1958-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见阿联法律工作者
    新华社14日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间接见了以阿拉伯联合共和国总检察长哈菲兹·萨贝克为首的阿联埃及地区法律工作者访华代表团。接见时在座的有中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰、张志让、张友渔等。接见时，阿拉伯联合共和国驻中国大使拉加卜也在座。
