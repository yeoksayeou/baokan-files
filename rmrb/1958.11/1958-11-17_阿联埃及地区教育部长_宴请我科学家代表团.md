Title: 阿联 Egyptian Regional Minister of Education Hosts Banquet for Chinese Scientist Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-11-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 15th -- The Minister of Education of the Egyptian Region of the United Arab Republic, Hashim, held a reception here today to welcome the Chinese scientist delegation currently visiting the UAR.

　　Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to the UAR, Counselor Lin Zhaonan, and other officials from the Chinese Embassy attended the reception.

　　Also present at the reception were Egyptian Regional Deputy Minister of Education Nahas, Cairo University President Mustafa Said, and the heads of various departments of the university.

### 阿联埃及地区教育部长  宴请我科学家代表团

1958-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联埃及地区教育部长
    宴请我科学家代表团
    据新华社开罗15日电　阿拉伯联合共和国埃及地区教育部长哈希姆今天在这里举行招待会，欢迎目前正在阿联访问的中国科学家代表团。
    出席招待会的有中国驻阿联大使陈家康、参赞林兆南和中国大使馆的其他官员。
    出席招待会的还有埃及地区教育部副部长纳哈斯、开罗大学校长穆斯塔法·赛义德和这所大学各院系的负责人。
