Title: US Aircraft Intrudes into Our Territorial Airspace for Provocation; Our 42nd Serious Warning

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th - On November 16th, a US Navy P2V patrol plane intruded into our territorial airspace over the Yushan Islands in Zhejiang Province and the Pingtan area in Fujian Province. Regarding this continued military provocation by the United States, a spokesman of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 42nd serious warning.

### 美机先后侵入我领海上空挑衅  我第42次提出严重警告

1958-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    美机先后侵入我领海上空挑衅
    我第42次提出严重警告
    新华社16日讯　11月16日美国海军P2V巡逻机一架先后侵入我浙江省渔山列岛和福建省平潭地区的领海上空。对于美国继续进行的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命第四十二次提出严重警告。
