Title: Vietnam Smashes American Spy Organization
Author:
Date: 1958-11-25
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 23rd: According to a report in today's "People's Daily" in Hanoi, Vietnamese public security organs, with the assistance of the people, smashed an American spy organization in early November. This organization was distributed in Nam Dinh, Hanoi, and Haiphong.

According to reports, the public security organs also uncovered many weapons and radios used by the organization for espionage activities. These weapons and radios all had American trademarks.

The investigation is continuing.

### 越南破获美国间谍组织

1958-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    越南破获美国间谍组织
    新华社河内23日电　据河内“人民报”今天报道，越南公安机关在人民群众的协助下，在11月初破获了美国的一个间谍组织。这个组织分布在南定、河内和海防。
    据报道，公安机关还查出了这个组织用以进行间谍活动的许多武器和电台。这些武器和电台上面都有美国的商标。
    侦查工作正在继续进行中。
