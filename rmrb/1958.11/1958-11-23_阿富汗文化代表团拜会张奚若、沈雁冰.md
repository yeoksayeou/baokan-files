Title: Afghan Cultural Delegation Pays Visits to Zhang Xiruo and Shen Yanbing
Author:
Date: 1958-11-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd: The Afghan Cultural Delegation, headed by Dr. Ahmed Ali Achad, paid visits to Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, on the morning of November 21st.

On the afternoon of November 21st, all members of the delegation went to the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to express their deep condolences to the wife of Zheng Zhenduo, the head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation to Afghanistan who died in a plane crash, and the wife of the deputy head, Cai Shufan, and presented them with gifts.

### 阿富汗文化代表团拜会张奚若、沈雁冰

1958-11-23
第3版()
专栏：

    阿富汗文化代表团拜会张奚若、沈雁冰
    新华社22日讯　以阿哈默德·阿里·阿查德博士为首的阿富汗文化代表团21日上午拜会了对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若和文化部部长沈雁冰。
    代表团的全体团员21日下午到对外文化联络委员会，向因飞机失事遇难的我国赴阿文化代表团团长郑振铎的夫人和副团长蔡树藩的夫人表示深切的慰问，并向她们赠送了礼物。
