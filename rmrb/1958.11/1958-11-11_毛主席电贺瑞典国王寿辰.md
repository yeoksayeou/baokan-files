Title: Chairman Mao Cables Congratulations to the King of Sweden on His Birthday
Author:
Date: 1958-11-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to King Gustaf VI of Sweden, congratulating him on his birthday. The text is as follows:

His Majesty King Gustaf VI of Sweden:

On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday, I extend warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the Swedish people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, and wish the Kingdom of Sweden prosperity and its people happiness.

Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
November 10, 1958, in Beijing

### 毛主席电贺瑞典国王寿辰

1958-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺瑞典国王寿辰
    新华社10日讯　毛泽东主席致电瑞典王国国王古斯塔夫六世，祝贺他的寿辰。文如下：瑞典王国国王古斯塔夫六世陛下：
    当陛下寿辰的时候，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义，向陛下和瑞典人民致以热烈的祝贺，并祝瑞典国家繁荣，人民幸福。
  中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
            1958年11月10日于北京
