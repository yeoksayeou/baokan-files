    Rockefeller Arrives in Brazil; Brazilian Workers Fly Flags at Half-Mast
    Xinhua News Agency, Sao Paulo, 20th - The trade union federation here today flew flags at half-mast to express their condolences for the visit of the newly elected Governor of New York State, Nelson Rockefeller, to Brazil. The purpose of his visit is to inspect his organizations and enterprises in Brazil, which occupy a very important position in this country.

### 洛克菲勒到巴西  巴西工人挂半旗

1958-11-22
第5版()
专栏：瘟神临门人人厌

    洛克菲勒到巴西  巴西工人挂半旗
    据新华社圣保罗20日电　这里的工会联合会今天为新当选的美国纽约州州长纳尔逊·洛克菲勒到巴西的访问升半旗表示致哀。他这次访问的目的，是要视察他在巴西的组织和企业，这些组织和企业在这个国家内占有很重要的地位。
