Title: Chairman of Moroccan National Consultative Assembly Says Moroccan People Welcome Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Morocco
Author:
Date: 1958-11-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, November 1st: Mehdi Ben Barka, Chairman of the Moroccan National Consultative Assembly, said that the Moroccan people are very happy about the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Morocco, because "this strengthens Morocco's international position and expands the scope of its friendship and support. Friendship and support are exactly what we need in our current delicate historical stage."

He spoke enthusiastically about the Consultative Assembly delegation's visit to China in August 1957. He said, "The delegation gathered a lot of knowledge for Morocco's future construction."

### 摩国民咨询议会主席说  摩人民欢迎中摩建交

1958-11-03
第5版()
专栏：

    摩国民咨询议会主席说
    摩人民欢迎中摩建交
    新华社拉巴特1日电　国民咨询议会主席迈赫迪·本·巴尔卡说，摩洛哥人民对于中国和摩洛哥两国建立外交关系的事感到非常高兴，因为“这加强了摩洛哥的国际地位和扩大了它的友谊和支持的范围。而在我们目前的这个微妙的历史阶段，友谊和支持正是我们所需要的。”
    他热情地谈到咨询议会代表团1957年8月到中国访问的事。他说：“代表团为摩洛哥未来的建设搜集了许多知识。”
