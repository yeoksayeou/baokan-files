Title: Party Secretary Runs Canteen "Experimental Plot" (Picture)
Author: Liu Xinning
Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 3

Party Secretary Runs Canteen "Experimental Plot"
    Ding Baofang, the Party Secretary of the Yuejin People's Commune in Yingcheng County, Hubei Province, is running an "experimental plot" in a canteen. He (wearing a white apron) is soliciting opinions on the canteen's food from the commune members who are eating.

    党委书记搞食堂“试验田”
    湖北省应城县跃进人民公社党委书记丁葆芳在一个食堂里搞“试验田”。他（身围白围裙者）正向吃饭的社员们征求对食堂饭菜的意见。                   新华社记者　刘心宁摄
