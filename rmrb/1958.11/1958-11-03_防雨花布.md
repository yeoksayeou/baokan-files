Title: Rainproof Calico
Author:
Date: 1958-11-03
Page: 2

	Leap Forward Showcase

	Rainproof Calico
	Recently, a type of rainproof calico has been successfully trial-produced in Shanghai. Its colors are as bright and beautiful as ordinary calico. Wearing clothes made of this calico eliminates the need for a raincoat on rainy days.
	·Universal Sewing Machine·
	Recently, the Shanghai Joint State-Private Xiechang Sewing Machine Factory has produced a new type of Invincible brand universal sewing machine.
	This sewing machine has a beautiful and elegant appearance. Equipped with a specially made "cam," it can automatically embroider sixty kinds of beautiful patterns, as well as overcast edges, sew buttons, make buttonholes, and sew pleats.
	This sewing machine can also be equipped with a small electric motor and lighting, making it suitable for home use.

### 防雨花布

1958-11-03
第2版()
专栏：跃进橱窗

    防雨花布
    最近在上海试制成功一种防雨花布，颜色同普通花布一样鲜艳美丽。穿上这种花布衣服，下雨天就用不着穿雨衣。·万能缝纫机·
    最近，上海公私合营协昌缝纫机厂制成一种新型的无敌牌万能缝纫机。
    这种缝纫机，外形美观大方，机上配有特制的“凸轮”，能自动绣出六十种美丽的花纹，还能锁边、钉纽、锁眼、缝衣褶等。
    这种缝纫机上还可以安装小型的电动机和照明电灯，适合于家庭使用。
