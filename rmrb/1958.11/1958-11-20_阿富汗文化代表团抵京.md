Title: Afghan Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-11-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The Afghan cultural delegation, consisting of five members and led by Dr. Ahmad Ali Achad, arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday and today. This delegation is in China for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 阿富汗文化代表团抵京

1958-11-20
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗文化代表团抵京
    新华社19日讯　阿富汗文化代表团一行五人，在团长阿哈默德·阿里·阿查德博士率领下，已经在今天和昨天分别乘飞机到达北京。这个代表团是应我对外文化联络委员会邀请来我国访问的。
