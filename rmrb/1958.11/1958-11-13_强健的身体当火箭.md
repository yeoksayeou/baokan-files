Title: A Strong Body as a Rocket

Author: 

Date: 1958-11-13
Page: 6

　　A strong body as a rocket,
　　Be a model in production,
　　Be a player on the sports field,
　　Launch satellites for steel and grain,
　　A strong body as a rocket.
　　　　—Qingzhen, Guizhou—

### 强健的身体当火箭

1958-11-13
第6版()
专栏：体育运动

    强健的身体当火箭
    生产上当模范，
    运动场上当选手，
    钢铁粮食放卫星，
    强健的身体当火箭。
          —贵州清镇—
