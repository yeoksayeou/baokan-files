Title: Chinese Affairs Should Be Solved by the Chinese Themselves: Ceylon's "People's Daily" Condemns U.S. Interference in China's Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1958-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 31st. The Ceylon evening paper "People's Daily" published an editorial today on the situation in the Taiwan Strait area, stating, "The United States stationing armed forces thousands of miles away from America is attempting to interfere in the internal affairs of another country."

The editorial said, "This action by the United States brings a dangerous threat to the entire world."

The editorial concluded, "The only thing the United States should do is leave this place and allow the Chinese on the mainland and Chiang Kai-shek (who is, after all, also Chinese) to resolve these issues through peaceful negotiations."

### 中国人的事该中国人解决  锡兰“人民报”谴责美国干涉中国内政

1958-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人的事该中国人解决
    锡兰“人民报”谴责美国干涉中国内政
    新华社科伦坡31日电　锡兰晚报“人民报”今天就台湾海峡地区局势发表社论说，“美国把武装部队驻扎在离开美国几千英里以外的地方是想干涉另一个国家的内政。”
    社论说，“美国的这种作法也就是把危险的威胁带给整个世界。”
    社论最后说，“美国应当做的唯一事情是离开这个地方，让大陆上的中国人和蒋介石（他毕竟也是中国人）通过和平谈判来解决这些问题。
