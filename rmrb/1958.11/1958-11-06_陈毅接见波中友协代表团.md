Title: Chen Yi Receives Polish-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-11-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the delegation of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association, led by head of the delegation Bulkin, and all its members this morning. Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Foreign Cultural Relations, was present at the meeting.

Also present was Polish Ambassador to China, Kiryok.

### 陈毅接见波中友协代表团

1958-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见波中友协代表团
    新华社5日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了波中友协代表团团长布尔金和全体团员。接见时在座的有对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥。
    在座的还有波兰驻中国大使基里洛克。
