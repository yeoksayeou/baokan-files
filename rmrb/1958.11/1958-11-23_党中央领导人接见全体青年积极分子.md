Title: Central Party Leaders Receive All Young Activists

Author:
Date: 1958-11-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: Zhou Enlai and Zhu De, Vice Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee, Lin Boqu and He Long, members of the Central Political Bureau, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Central Secretariat, and Nie Rongzhen, member of the Central Committee, received all the activists attending the Second National Congress of Young Socialist Construction Activists this morning.

Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the secretaries of the Secretariat accompanied the reception today.

### 党中央领导人接见全体青年积极分子

1958-11-23
第3版()
专栏：

    党中央领导人接见全体青年积极分子
    新华社22日讯　中共中央副主席周恩来、朱德，中央政治局委员林伯渠、贺龙，中央书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员聂荣臻，今天上午接见了出席第二次全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的全体积极分子。
    今天陪同接见的有共青团中央书记处第一书记胡耀邦和书记处的书记们。
