Title: Soviet Azerbaijan Song and Dance Troupe Performs for the Volunteer Army Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Soviet Azerbaijan National Song and Dance Troupe performed a wonderful song and dance program for the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation at the Tianqiao Theater tonight. The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation and Performing Arts Troupe, headed by General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping, attended tonight's performance.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, also watched tonight's performance.

Tonight, Soviet artists performed more than 20 Soviet and Chinese song and dance programs for the Volunteer Army heroes, which were warmly welcomed by the audience. Some of the programs were replayed two or three times at the audience's repeated requests.

After the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Chairman Guo Moruo, General Yang Yong, and General Wang Ping went on stage to shake hands with the Soviet artists one by one, congratulating them on the success of the performance. The Volunteer Army delegation also presented flower baskets to the song and dance troupe.

Finally, all the members of the song and dance troupe and the Volunteer Army heroes went to the Beijing Hotel lobby for a warm and exuberant get-together.

### 苏联阿塞拜疆歌舞团为志愿军代表团表演歌舞

1958-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联阿塞拜疆歌舞团为志愿军代表团表演歌舞
    新华社9日讯　苏联阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团今晚在天桥剧场为中国人民志愿军代表团演出了精彩的歌舞节目。以杨勇上将、王平上将为首的中国人民志愿军代表团和文工团出席了今天的晚会。
    陈毅副总理和中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若等也观看了今晚的演出。
    今晚，苏联艺术家为志愿军英雄们演出的二十多个苏联和中国的歌舞节目，受到观众们的热烈欢迎。其中有些节目在观众的一再要求下，重演了两、三次。
    演出结束后，陈毅副总理、郭沫若主席和杨勇上将、王平上将登台和苏联艺术家们一一握手，祝贺他们演出的成功。志愿军代表团并向歌舞团献了花篮。
    最后，歌舞团全体人员还和志愿军英雄们到北京饭店大厅，进行了热情洋溢的联欢。
