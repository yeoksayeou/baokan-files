Title: Azerbaijan State Song and Dance Ensemble Leaves Harbin for Home

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

    The Soviet Azerbaijan State Song and Dance Ensemble concluded its visit and performances in China and left Harbin for home on the morning of the 27th.

    The Azerbaijan State Song and Dance Ensemble arrived in Harbin on the 19th.

### 阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团离哈尔滨回国

1958-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团离哈尔滨回国
    据新华社讯　苏联阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团结束了在我国的访问演出，27日上午离开哈尔滨回国。
    阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团是19日到达哈尔滨的。
