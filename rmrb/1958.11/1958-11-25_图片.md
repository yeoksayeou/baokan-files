Deutsche Press Agency manuscript (Xinhua News Agency dispatch):

    November 9th marked the 40th anniversary of the German November Revolution. On the eve of the anniversary, young people in Munich, West Germany, held a silent march (without speaking) to commemorate the anti-fascist fighters who were killed in concentration camps and to protest against the new militarism in West Germany.
