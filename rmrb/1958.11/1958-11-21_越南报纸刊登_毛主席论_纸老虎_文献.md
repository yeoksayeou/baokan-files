Title: Vietnamese Newspapers Publish Chairman Mao's Writings on "Paper Tigers"
Author:
Date: 1958-11-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 13th. The Vietnamese "People's Army Newspaper" today began to publish the full text of "Comrade Mao Zedong on Imperialism and All Reactionaries Are Paper Tigers." The newspaper today used a portion of the first page and the entire third page to publish the first part and a section of the second part of this document.

Hanoi's "New Era Newspaper" began serializing the summary of this document from November 10th.

### 越南报纸刊登  毛主席论“纸老虎”文献

1958-11-21
第5版()
专栏：

    越南报纸刊登
    毛主席论“纸老虎”文献
    新华社河内13日电　越南“人民军队报”今天开始全文刊载“毛泽东同志论帝国主义和一切反动派都是纸老虎”的文献。这家报纸今天以第一版的一部分版面和第三版全版刊载了这个文献的第一部分和第二部分的一节。
    河内“新时代报”从11月10日起开始连载这个文献的摘要。
