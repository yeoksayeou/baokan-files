Title: Chilean Communist Party Eleventh Congress Opens, To Revise Party Constitution and Elect Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1958-11-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, 18th: The Eleventh National Congress of the Chilean Communist Party opened today in Santiago. This is the first congress held by the Chilean Communist Party after ending ten years of underground struggle and gaining legal status. The congress is being held in the Chilean National Assembly Building.

According to Chilean newspapers, more than 300 delegates are participating in the congress, which will discuss the following agenda: the Chilean people's struggle for national liberation and peace; striving for the organizational, political, and ideological consolidation of the Communist Party; the Party's work among the broad masses; revision of the Party Constitution; and election of the Central Committee.

In the run-up to the congress, party organizations across the country held delegate meetings, which comprehensively discussed the Chilean Communist Party's line on a broad and democratic basis.

The Chilean Communist Party was founded in 1922. Its predecessor was the left wing of the Chilean Socialist Party, which formed the Socialist Workers' Party in 1912. In the thirty-six years since its founding, the Party has carried out a series of struggles to defend the interests of the Chilean people.

### 智共十一次代表大会开幕  将修改党章并选出中央委员会

1958-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    智共十一次代表大会开幕
    将修改党章并选出中央委员会
    新华社18日讯　圣地亚哥消息：智利共产党第十一次全国代表大会今天在圣地亚哥开幕，这是智利共产党结束了十年地下斗争生生、取得合法地位之后举行的第一次代表大会。代表大会在智利国民大会大厦举行。
    据智利报纸报道，参加这次大会的有三百多名代表，大会将讨论下列议程：智利人民争取民族解放与和平的斗争；争取共产党在组织上、政治上和思想上的巩固；党在广大群众中的工作；修改党章；选举中央委员会。
    在代表大会前夕，全国各地党组织都举行了代表会议，会议在广泛民主的基础上全面地讨论了智利共产党的路线。
    智利共产党成立于1922年。它的前身是智利社会党左翼于1912年组成的社会劳工党。党在成立以来的三十六年中进行过一系列保卫智利人民利益的斗争。
