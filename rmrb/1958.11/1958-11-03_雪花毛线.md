    Leap Forward Showcase

    Snowflake Yarn
    The Qinghe Woolen Mill in Beijing has recently produced a type of snowflake yarn. This yarn is made by twisting together strands of thread in two different shades of the same color. When knitted into sweaters, it looks like a layer of snowflakes has fallen on it, making it very elegant.

### 雪花毛线

1958-11-03
第2版()
专栏：跃进橱窗

    雪花毛线
    北京清河毛纺厂最近生产了一种雪花毛线。这种毛线是以两种深浅不同颜色的批线拧合而成的。用它织成毛衣，像落上一层雪花似的，十分雅致。
