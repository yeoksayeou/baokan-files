Title: Presenting Cherished Souvenirs to Beloved Leaders: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation Presents Gifts from the Korean People to Chairman Mao and Other Leaders
Author:
Date: 1958-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 13th: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation, led by General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping, presented a portion of the cherished souvenirs given to the Volunteer Army by the Korean people upon their return home to Chairman Mao Zedong and other leading comrades of the Party Central Committee on the 12th. The gifts for Chairman Mao included a "Korea-China Friendship" medal awarded to the Volunteer Army by the Korean government, fifteen Korean records given to the Volunteer Army by the Korean people, a set of silver-plated smoking utensils, a silver kettle, three silver dishes and three silver cups, two bronze bowls, a pine and white crane embroidered mirror frame, and other precious gifts.

At the same time, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation presented a portion of the souvenirs given to the Volunteer Army by the Korean people to Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, and Lin Biao, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, and other responsible comrades of the CPC Central Committee.

The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation also distributed a portion of the souvenirs to Vice Chairmen and Vice Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Chairmen and Vice Chairpersons of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), Vice Chairmen of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, key leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and key responsible persons of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC, the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, and the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea.

### 把心爱的纪念品送给心爱的领袖  志愿军代表团将朝鲜人民赠品送给毛主席和其他领导人

1958-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    把心爱的纪念品送给心爱的领袖
    志愿军代表团将朝鲜人民赠品送给毛主席和其他领导人
    新华社13日讯　以杨勇上将、王平上将为首的中国人民志愿军代表团，12日将归国时朝鲜人民赠送给志愿军的大批纪念品中一部分心爱的送给毛泽东主席和党中央其他领导同志。送给毛主席的纪念品中有朝鲜政府颁发给志愿军的“朝中友谊”纪念章一枚，朝鲜人民赠给志愿军的朝鲜唱片十五张，镀银烟具一套，银壶一把，银碟、银杯各三个，铜碗两个，青松白鹤刺绣镜框一个，和其他珍贵礼品。
    志愿军代表团同时把朝鲜人民赠给志愿军的一部分纪念品，分别送给了中国共产党中央委员会副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、林彪，总书记邓小平和中共中央其他负责同志。
    志愿军代表团还把一部分纪念品分送给全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长，政协全国委员会副主席，中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席，中国人民解放军主要领导人，以及中共北京市委员会、北京市人民委员会、政协北京市委员会、北京市抗美援朝分会的主要负责人。
