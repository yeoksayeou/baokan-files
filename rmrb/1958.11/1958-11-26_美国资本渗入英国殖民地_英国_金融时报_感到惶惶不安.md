Title: US Capital Infiltration of British Colonies Causes Anxiety for British "Financial Times"
Author:
Date: 1958-11-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, London, November 24th: The deepening infiltration of British colonies by American monopoly organizations is causing increasing unease among British capitalists.

    The "Financial Times," a publication of British industry, commerce, and finance, expressed this unease. It is particularly concerned about the recent significant increase in the role of American companies in the economy of Jamaica. The newspaper, referring to the recent discovery of abundant bauxite deposits on the island, emphasized that none of the three companies exploiting these mines are British.

    The newspaper stated that, as a result, "Britain is increasingly relegated to a secondary position in terms of investment compared to the United States." The "intensified infiltration of American forces into this region" is a cause for concern.

    The "Financial Times" concluded: "What is attracting American capital? This question is not difficult to answer. Similarly, the reasons for the expansion of American investment in Australia and other British dominions are not difficult to explain. This is because participating in the internal production processes of the British Commonwealth allows American industrialists to obtain the benefits of imperial preference."

### 美国资本渗入英国殖民地  英国“金融时报”感到惶惶不安

1958-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    美国资本渗入英国殖民地
    英国“金融时报”感到惶惶不安
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社伦敦24日讯：美国垄断组织的加深渗入英国殖民地使英国资本家越来越感到不安。
    英国工商界和金融界的“金融时报”流露了这种不安的情绪。它特别担心的是，最近在牙买加岛的经济中，美国公司的作用大大地增长。报纸在提到不久以前在岛上发现了许多丰富的铁矾土矿这件事时强调指出，开采这些矿的三家公司中，竟没有一家是英国的。
    报纸说，这样一来，“同美国相比，英国在投资方面就日益退居于次要地位了”。“美国势力的加紧渗入这一地区”使人感到担忧。
    “金融时报”最后写道：“什么东西在吸引美国资本？这个问题是不难解答的。同样，造成美国在澳大利亚和在英国的其他自治领扩大投资额的原因，也是不难解释的，这就是因为参与英联邦内部的生产过程会使美国工业家有可能获得帝国优惠制的好处。”
