Title: With Soviet Assistance, Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy: Polish Cyclotron Begins Operation
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th – According to TASS: On November 22nd, a grand ceremony was held at the Institute of Nuclear Physics in Krakow, Poland, to mark the start of operation of Poland's first cyclotron. This accelerator, with an energy of 13 million electron volts, was built with Soviet assistance.

    Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, attended the ceremony. He said at the meeting that Poland's first atomic reactor had already started working in June with Soviet assistance, and today, thanks to the great help of Soviet friends, the first cyclotron has also been completed. These two important nuclear physics research equipment will play a huge role in the work of Polish scientists in studying the peaceful uses of atomic energy. Thanks to Soviet assistance, Poland has become a country with considerable capacity to develop atomic energy for the service of the national economy.

    Cyrankiewicz expressed sincere gratitude for the selfless and fraternal assistance given by the Soviet Union.

### 在苏联帮助下和平利用原子能  波兰回旋加速器开始运转

1958-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    在苏联帮助下和平利用原子能
    波兰回旋加速器开始运转
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社消息：11月22日在波兰克拉科夫城原子核研究所里隆重地举行了波兰第一座回旋加速器的开始运转典礼。这座加速器的能量是一千三百万电子伏特，是在苏联援助下建造的。
    波兰人民共和国部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹出席了这次仪式。他在会上说，今年6月间在苏联的帮助下波兰第一座原子反应堆已经开始工作，而今天同样由于苏联朋友的大力帮助，第一座回旋加速器又落成了。这两个重要的原子核物理研究装备将在波兰科学家研究和平利用原子能的工作上发挥巨大的作用。由于苏联的帮助，波兰已成为一个拥有相当能力来发展原子能为国民经济服务的国家了。
    西伦凯维兹对苏联所给予的无私的、兄弟般的帮助表示真诚的感谢。
