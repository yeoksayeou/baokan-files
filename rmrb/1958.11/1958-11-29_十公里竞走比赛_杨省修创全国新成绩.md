Title: 10km Race Walking Competition: Yang Shengxiu Sets New National Record
Author:
Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 6
　
　　10km Race Walking Competition
　　Yang Shengxiu Sets New National Record
　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 27th – In the 10km race walking competition held at the Xi'an Institute of Physical Education track and field meet on the 24th, Yang Shengxiu, a freshman in the sports department, broke the national record of 52 minutes and 12.6 seconds with a time of 48 minutes and 39.3 seconds, and Li Yongquan broke the same record with a time of 50 minutes and 4.4 seconds. Yang Shengxiu's time is the new national record.

### 十公里竞走比赛  杨省修创全国新成绩

1958-11-29
第6版()
专栏：

    十公里竞走比赛
    杨省修创全国新成绩
    新华社西安27日电　24日在西安体育学院田径运动会的十公里竞走比赛中，运动系一年级学生杨省修以四十八分三十九秒三的成绩、李永泉以五十分四秒四的成绩打破了五十二分十二秒六的全国纪录，杨省修的成绩是全国最新的成绩。
