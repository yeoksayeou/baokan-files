Title: A Noble Gift: The CPSU Central Committee Presents a Film Documenting the Life of the Great Lenin to the CPC Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1958-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 14th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (CPSU) today presented a film documenting the life of the great revolutionary teacher Lenin, titled "Vladimir Ilyich Lenin," to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC).

    The film presentation ceremony took place in Beijing this evening. Yudin, Member of the CPSU Central Committee and Soviet Ambassador to China, entrusted by the CPSU Central Committee, presented the film to the CPC Central Committee. Chen Yi, Member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau, Vice Premier of the State Council, and Minister of Foreign Affairs, accepted this noble gift on behalf of the CPC Central Committee.

    Participating in the film presentation ceremony were Zhang Wentian, Zhang Hanfu, Luo Guibo, and Zeng Yongquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director; Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and many others. Many comrades from the Soviet Embassy in China also attended the film presentation ceremony.

    Comrades from both countries watched the film documenting the great life of the revolutionary teacher Lenin with reverence. Many of the shots in the film were taken on the spot during Lenin's leadership of the revolutionary struggle. This is a precious historical record.

    After the film screening, Comrade Yudin spoke first. He said, "I believe that after watching this film, Chinese workers, farmers, intellectuals, Communist Party members, and the broad masses of people will love Lenin, our talented revolutionary teacher and the founder of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, even more. Let this film further consolidate the unity of our two parties and further develop the friendship between the people of our two countries. Long live Marxism-Leninism!"

    Comrade Chen Yi then spoke. He said, "This film vividly presents Lenin's great image, revolutionary spirit, and great style before us, giving us an unparalleled and profound education and tremendous encouragement." Comrade Chen Yi added, "The path of the October Revolution pioneered by the great Lenin is also the path of the Chinese people; the Chinese people are advancing along the path of the October Revolution, and the people of the whole world will also advance along this path. Long live Marxism-Leninism!"

    At the film presentation ceremony, comrades from both countries also enjoyed the Chinese colorful documentary film "Coral Island."

### 崇高的赠礼　苏共中央把纪录伟大列宁生平的影片送给中共中央

1958-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    崇高的赠礼
  　苏共中央把纪录伟大列宁生平的影片送给中共中央
    新华社14日讯　苏联共产党中央委员会今天把一部纪录伟大革命导师列宁生平的影片“弗拉基米尔·伊里奇·列宁”，赠送给中国共产党中央委员会。
    赠送影片的仪式今天晚间在北京举行。苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联驻中国大使尤金受苏共中央的委托，把这部影片赠送给中国共产党中央委员会，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅代表中共中央接受了这崇高的赠礼。
    参加赠送影片仪式的，有外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫、罗贵波、曾涌泉，文化部部长沈雁冰，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若、副主任张致祥，对外贸易部副部长李强等多人。苏联驻中国大使馆的许多同志也参加了赠送影片的仪式。
    两国的同志怀着崇敬的心情观看了这部纪录革命导师列宁伟大一生的影片。影片中的许多镜头都是在列宁生前领导革命斗争时实地拍摄下来的。这是具有历史意义的珍贵纪录。
    在影片放映以后，尤金同志首先讲话。他说，我相信中国的工人、农民、知识分子、共产党员和广大人民看了这部影片以后，一定会更加热爱我们天才的革命导师、苏联共产党的创始人列宁。让这部影片使我们两党的团结更加巩固，使两国人民的友谊更加发展。马克思列宁主义万岁！
    陈毅同志接着讲话。他说，这部影片把列宁的伟大形象，列宁的革命精神和伟大风格，活生生地呈现在我们面前，使我们受到了无比深刻的教育和巨大的鼓舞。陈毅同志又说，伟大的列宁开创的十月革命的道路，也是中国人民的道路；中国人民正是沿着十月革命的道路前进的，全世界人民也将沿着这条道路前进。马克思列宁主义万岁！
    在赠送影片仪式上，两国的同志还欣赏了中国五彩纪录片“珊瑚岛”。
