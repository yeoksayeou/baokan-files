Title: Korean Art Troupe Visits and Performs in Guangzhou and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1958-11-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 24th: The art troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by the head of the troupe, Zhao Lingchu, left Beijing by train this afternoon to visit and perform in Guangzhou and other places.

During their stay in Beijing, the Korean artists gave five wonderful performances for the people of the capital, which won extremely warm welcome and praise from the audience.

### 朝鲜艺术团赴广州等地访问演出

1958-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜艺术团赴广州等地访问演出
    新华社24日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国艺术团今天下午由团长赵灵出率领乘火车离开北京，前往广州等地进行访问演出。
    朝鲜艺术家们在北京期间，为首都人民作了五场精彩的演出，博得了观众极为热烈的欢迎和好评。
