Title: Further Implement the Party's Policy on Traditional Chinese Medicine: National Conference on Traditional Chinese Medicine Opens
Author:
Date: 1958-11-18
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, November 17th. The National Conference on Traditional Chinese Medicine opened in Baoding on November 17th. Xu Yunbei, Vice Minister of Health, delivered a work report at the meeting.
    This conference will use the spirit of "using the virtual to bring along the real" to summarize the work of traditional Chinese medicine in the past few years after criticizing the erroneous ideas of belittling and rejecting traditional Chinese medicine. It will also use the spirit of rectification to liberate thought, adopt the form of great airing of views, great contending, and great debates to thoroughly eliminate bourgeois ideology in the work of traditional Chinese medicine, achieve unity in ideological understanding, further implement the Party's policy on traditional Chinese medicine, and promote a faster and more comprehensive leap forward in the work of traditional Chinese medicine. On the basis of consistent ideological understanding, the conference will also continue to arrange the work of traditional Chinese medicine for 1959 and beyond.
    This conference is also a field conference. The Hebei Provincial Department of Health will introduce at the meeting their experience in recent years of launching mass movements under the correct leadership of the Hebei Provincial Party Committee of the CPC and Party committees at all levels, and implementing the Party's policy on traditional Chinese medicine. During the conference, in addition to visiting the Hebei Provincial Exhibition of Traditional Chinese Medicine, the delegates will also visit An'guo County, Hebei Province, which is famous for the production of traditional Chinese medicine. In addition, the conference will widely exchange experiences in the work of traditional Chinese medicine from various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions. Responsible cadres from health departments (bureaus), medical colleges (schools), and pharmaceutical departments of various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions across the country are participating in the conference.
    Zhang Jiafu, Deputy Director of the Second Office of the State Council, and Zhang Chengxian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Hebei Provincial Party Committee of the CPC, attended the conference.

### 进一步贯彻党的中医政策  全国中医中药工作会议开幕

1958-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

    进一步贯彻党的中医政策
    全国中医中药工作会议开幕
    新华社保定17日电　全国中医中药工作会议17日在保定开幕。卫生部副部长徐运北在会上作了工作报告。
    这次会议要用以虚带实的精神，总结在批判轻视、排斥中医的错误思想以后几年来的中医工作；并将以整风的精神，解放思想，采取大鸣大放大辩论的形式，彻底消除中医工作方面的资产阶级思想，达到思想认识上的统一，进一步贯彻党的中医政策，使中医中药工作更快地全面跃进。在思想认识一致的基础上，会议还将继续安排1959年和今后的中医中药的工作。
    这次会议也是现场会议。河北省卫生厅将在会上介绍他们近年来在中共河北省委和各级党委正确领导下，大搞群众运动，贯彻党的中医政策的经验。会议期间，代表们除了参观河北省中医中药展览会外，还将到中药生产闻名的河北省安国县参观。此外，会议还将广泛交流各省、市、自治区在中医中药工作中的经验。参加会议的有全国各省、市、自治区卫生厅（局）、医学院（校）和医药部门的负责干部。
    国务院第二办公室副主任张稼夫和中共河北省委书记处书记张承先出席了这次会议。
