Title: Ode to the Four Transformations of Soil
Author:
Date: 1958-11-05
Page: 2

    Ode to the Four Transformations of Soil
    The four transformations of soil, oh so mighty,
    Self-reliance creates the world,
    Transforming labor into great liberation,
    Transforming the countryside into modernity.
