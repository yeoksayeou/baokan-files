Title: Song of Xiamen
Author: Li Ju
Date: 1958-11-17
Page: 8

Song of Xiamen

    One
    Xiamen Island, Xiamen Island,
    The sea waves are ten thousand feet high,
    White herons bring flower seeds,
    And plant flowers and grass here.
    Xiamen Island, Xiamen Island,
    Giant rocks are used as drums,
    Stone pillars are like forests,
    They will block the American devils.
    Xiamen Island, Xiamen Island,
    Red lychees, green bananas,
    The paper tiger glares blankly,
    It wants to eat but can't reach.
    Xiamen Island, Xiamen Island,
    Building a long dike in the sea,
    If Lu Ban were still alive,
    He would surely come to seek advice.

    Two
    Xiamen Island, I wish you well,
    You are the glory of the motherland,
    Facing the滔滔sea,
    Blow the horn of heroes.
    The Eastern giant commands me,
    To be friends with Xiamen,
    Accompanied by the roaring of ten thousand cannons,
    Accompanied by ten thousand acres of waves.
    The paper tiger stretches out its feet,
    The black crow is also cawing strangely,
    This is so hateful,
    We must shout "Fire!"
    Cannon, I beg you to roar,
    Cannon, I beg you to call out,
    Ten thousand rockets fire together,
    Stride across the sea and run.

    Three
    Xiamen Island, I wish you well,
    The stars shine high in the sky,
    Thousands of sailboats leave the port,
    The sails are like flying birds.
    Birds, fly!
    The red flag is flying on the sea,
    Torpedoes escort you,
    Cannons pave the way for you.
    Heroic city, heroic island,
    Living with countless heroes,
    This peninsula on the sea,
    Is originally a castle of heroes.
    The furnace fire on the island is burning,
    Steel flowers and sea waves shine on each other,
    Play the horn of heroes,
    Fire the giant cannons of the motherland.

    Four
    Xiamen Island, heroic island,
    Standing on the forefront of the motherland,
    Warriors light the lamp of peace,
    The lamp of peace shines high.
    We come to the heroic island,
    We are proud of you,
    Sing for you, sing for you,
    Dance for you, laugh for you.
    We go to the front line,
    Take the soldiers as teachers,
    Take the cannon as a pen,
    Take the sea as ink.
    Here, let's sing heartily,
    Who cares if the words are good or bad;
    I wish I could be a white heron,
    Planting some flowers and grass;
    I wish I could be a cannonball,
    Scare that American devil to death!

### 厦门歌
里局
1958-11-17
第8版()
专栏：

    厦门歌
    里局
    一
    厦门岛，厦门岛，
    海上浪万丈高，
    白鹭衔来花籽，
    在这里种下花草。
    厦门岛，厦门岛，
    巨石当作鼓敲，
    石柱好像是森林，
    它要挡住美国佬。
    厦门岛，厦门岛，
    红荔枝，绿香蕉，
    纸老虎干瞪眼，
    它想吃它吃不到。
    厦门岛，厦门岛，
    大海里筑长堤，
    倘使鲁班还活着，
    他一定要来请教。
    二
    厦门岛，祝你好，
    你是祖国的光荣，
    面向滔滔大海，
    吹起英雄号角。
    东方巨人命令我，
    来和厦门结知交，
    伴着万炮齐鸣，
    伴着万顷浪涛。
    纸老虎伸起脚，
    黑乌鸦也在怪叫，
    这是多么可恶，
    我们要高呼开炮！
    大炮我请你怒吼，
    大炮我请你呼号，
    万支火箭齐发，
    跨上大海奔跑。
    三
    厦门岛，祝你好，
    群星在空中高照，
    千只帆船出港，
    船帆有如飞鸟。
    群鸟呵，飞行吧，
    红旗在海上飘，
    鱼雷为你送行，
    大炮为你开道。
    英雄城，英雄岛，
    住着无数英豪，
    这一座海上半岛，
    本是英雄的城堡。
    岛上炉火在燃烧，
    钢花海涛相照耀，
    吹奏吧英雄号角，
    点放吧祖国巨炮。
    四
    厦门岛，英雄岛，
    站在祖国最前哨，
    勇士点起和平灯，
    和平的灯高高照。
    我们来到英雄岛，
    为你感到骄傲，
    为你歌，为你唱，
    为你舞，为你欢笑。
    我们走上阵地，
    拜战士做老师，
    把大炮当做笔，
    把海水当做墨。
    在这里纵情高歌，
    哪管词句好不好；
    但愿做一头白鹭，
    种下几棵花草；
    但愿做一颗炮弹，
    吓死那美国佬！
