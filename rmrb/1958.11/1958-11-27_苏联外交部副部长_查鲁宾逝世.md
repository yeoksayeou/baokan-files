Title: Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Zarubin Dies
Author:
Date: 1958-11-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 26th: According to TASS, Georgy Nikolaevich Zarubin, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, died of a serious illness on November 24th. Zarubin was born in 1900 and was 58 years old.

### 苏联外交部副部长  查鲁宾逝世

1958-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联外交部副部长
    查鲁宾逝世
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会候补委员、苏联外交部副部长格·尼·查鲁宾患重病不幸于11月24日逝世。查鲁宾生于1900年，享年五十八岁。
