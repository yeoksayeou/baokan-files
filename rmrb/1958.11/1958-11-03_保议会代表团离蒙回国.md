Title: Bulgarian Parliamentary Delegation Departs Mongolia for Home

Author:
Date: 1958-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 2nd: The Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation, led by Chervenkov, concluded its visit to Mongolia and departed from Ulaanbaatar by plane this afternoon to return home.

　　At the airport, Chervenkov delivered a farewell speech, once again expressing gratitude for the warm hospitality of the Mongolian people. He also wished the Mongolian people greater achievements in socialist construction.

　　Sürenjav, Chairman of the Presidium of the Mongolian Great People's Hural, delivered a farewell speech, asking the Bulgarian delegation to take back to the Bulgarian people the best wishes of the Mongolian people.

　　Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included: Baljinyam, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Mongolian Great People's Hural, ShagdarSurun, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of Mongolia, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia.

### 保议会代表团离蒙回国

1958-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    保议会代表团离蒙回国
    新华社乌兰巴托2日电　由契尔文科夫率领的保加利亚国民议会代表团结束在蒙古的访问后，在今天下午从乌兰巴托乘飞机回国。
    契尔文科夫在飞机场上致告别词，再次表示感谢蒙古人民的热情招待。他并且祝蒙古人民在社会主义建设中取得更大的成就。
    蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席苏伦扎布致欢送词，他请保加利亚的代表团把蒙古人民对保加利亚人民的最好祝愿带回去。
    到机场送行的还有：蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席巴尔吉尼雅姆、蒙古代理外交部长沙格达尔苏伦，以及各国驻蒙古的外交使节。
