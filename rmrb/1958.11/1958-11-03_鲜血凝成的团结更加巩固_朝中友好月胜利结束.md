Title: Unity Cemented by Blood Further Consolidated; Sino-Korean Friendship Month Concludes Victoriously
Author:
Date: 1958-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 31st. The Korean Central News Agency reported today on the conclusion of "Sino-Korean Friendship Month", stating that through the activities of "Sino-Korean Friendship Month," the Korean people have expressed their sincere respect and love for the Chinese People's Volunteers, and have shown the people of the world that the friendship and unity between the Korean and Chinese people, cemented by blood, will last forever.

The report said that the visit of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea during the "Sino-Korean Friendship Month" increased the significance of the month, and the establishment of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association will play a significant role in further consolidating and developing the friendship, unity, and cooperation between the people of the two countries.

The report stated that during the "Sino-Korean Friendship Month," which took place from October 1st to 31st, the people of Korea held rich and colorful celebrations, and the working people in various regions organized Sino-Korean friendship production teams and shock brigades, expanding the production increase competition.

The report said that during this period, Korean social activists, scientists, and artists who had visited China widely held lectures and symposiums introducing China's construction achievements and Sino-Korean friendship, with more than 2.83 million people participating. The Korean people and the People's Army also invited Chinese People's Volunteer officers and soldiers to hold 235 friendship gatherings, expressing their gratitude and feelings of farewell to the volunteers.

The report said that during the "Sino-Korean Friendship Month," cinemas across the country held "Chinese Film Week," and various regions also held photo exhibitions introducing China's Great Leap Forward and Sino-Korean friendship, published a collection of short stories praising the great achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteers called "Benevolent and Righteous Friends" and a collection of poems called "Glory Belongs to You," as well as a "Collection of Sino-Korean Friendship Songs" including twenty-six songs. In addition, artistic performances, literary evenings, poetry evenings, and Chinese music evenings with the theme of Sino-Korean friendship were widely held.

### 鲜血凝成的团结更加巩固  朝中友好月胜利结束

1958-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    鲜血凝成的团结更加巩固
    朝中友好月胜利结束
    据新华社平壤31日讯　朝鲜中央通讯社今天在关于“朝中友好月”结束的报道中说，朝鲜人民通过“朝中友好月”的活动，向中国人民志愿军表示了衷心的尊敬和热爱，并且向全世界人民显示了以鲜血凝成的朝中两国人民间的友谊团结将万古长青。
    报道说，在“朝中友好月”中，中国人民代表团对朝鲜的访问，增加了“朝中友好月”的意义，而朝中友好协会的成立对进一步巩固和发展朝中两国人民间的友谊团结和合作，将起重大作用。
    报道说，从10月1日到31日举行的“朝中友好月”期间，朝鲜各地人民举行了丰富多采的庆祝活动，各地劳动人民组织了朝中友好生产小组和突击队，扩大了增产竞赛。
    报道说，在这一时期，访问过中国的朝鲜社会活动家、科学家、文艺界人士广泛举行了介绍中国建设成就和朝中友谊的讲演会、座谈会，参加的群众有二百八十三万多人。朝鲜人民和人民军还邀请了中国人民志愿军官兵举行了二百三十五次友谊联欢晚会，表示他们对志愿军的感谢和惜别的情意。
    报道说，在“朝中友好月”中，全国的电影院都举行了“中国电影周”，各地还举办了介绍中国大跃进和中朝友谊的图片展览会，出版了歌颂中国人民志愿军伟大功勋的短篇小说集“仁义的朋友”和诗集“光荣属于你们”，以及包括二十六首歌曲的“朝中友好歌曲集”。另外，还广泛举行了以朝中友好为内容的艺术演出、文学晚会、诗人晚会和中国音乐晚会等。
