A Little Song for New Water Conservancy Tools

　　(I)
　　The capstan hauls earth with great efficiency,
　　Pulling both large and small carts with ease.
　　It can haul five or six carts at a time,
　　As if moving Mount Tai itself.

　　(II)
　　A small railway track, dozens of yards long,
　　Homemade trains carry the mud along.
　　A machine and rope provide the pull,
　　Able to handle eight hundred cubic meters a day in full.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（Jiangsu）
