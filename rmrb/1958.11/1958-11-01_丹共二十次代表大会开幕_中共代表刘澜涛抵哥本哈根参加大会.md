Title: Danish Communist Party Twentieth Congress Opens; Chinese Communist Party Representative Liu Lantao Arrives in Copenhagen to Attend the Congress
Author:
Date: 1958-11-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 31st: Copenhagen dispatch: The Twentieth Congress of the Danish Communist Party opened at nine o'clock this morning.

The main agenda items for this congress are: (1) Various work reports of the Danish Communist Party Central Committee: Work report of the Central Committee (reporter: Thomsen, Secretary of the Danish Communist Party Central Committee), work report of the Central Supervisory Committee, and work report of the Central Financial Audit Committee; (2) Report on amendments to the Party Constitution (reporter: Jens Risgaard Knudsen, Vice Chairman of the Danish Communist Party Central Committee); (3) Election of the Central Committee, Central Supervisory Committee, and Central Financial Audit Committee.

In addition to the members and alternate members of the Danish Communist Party Central Committee, 180 delegates are attending this congress.

Twenty-five representatives from fraternal Communist and Workers' parties from sixteen countries, including the Soviet Union and China, are also participating in the congress.

The congress is scheduled to last three days. The congress today listened to and discussed the reports on the work of the Central Committee.

Before the congress, a plenary meeting of the Central Committee was held to arrange the final preparations for the congress.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 30th: Copenhagen dispatch: Liu Lantao, representative of the Chinese Communist Party to the Twentieth Congress of the Danish Communist Party, member of the CPC Central Committee, and alternate secretary of the Secretariat, arrived in Copenhagen, the capital of Denmark, by plane on the 29th.

The Soviet Communist Party delegation to the Danish Communist Party Congress, headed by Pospelov, also arrived on the same plane.

Among those who welcomed them at the airport were A. Jensgaard, Vice Chairman of the Danish Communist Party Central Committee and member of the Central Executive Committee, and I. Norlund, member of the Danish Communist Party Central Executive Committee. That afternoon, the Danish Communist Party Central Committee held a reception to welcome the delegations of various fraternal parties that had arrived in Copenhagen.

### 丹共二十次代表大会开幕  中共代表刘澜涛抵哥本哈根参加大会

1958-11-01
第5版()
专栏：

    丹共二十次代表大会开幕
    中共代表刘澜涛抵哥本哈根参加大会
    新华社柏林31日电　哥本哈根消息：丹麦共产党第二十次代表大会今天上午九时开幕。
    这次代表大会的主要议程有：（1）丹共中央各项工作报告：中央委员会工作报告（报告人：丹共中央书记托姆森），中央监察委员会工作报告，中央财务审计委员会工作报告；（2）修改党章报告（报告人：丹共中央委员会副主席任生）；（3）选举中央委员会、中央监察委员会和中央财务审计委员会。
    出席这次代表大会的代表除丹共中央委员、候补委员外有一百八十位代表。
    参加大会的还有苏联、中国等十六个国家的兄弟共产党和工人党的二十五位代表。
    大会准备开三天。大会今天听取和讨论有关中央工作的报告。
    在代表大会开会前曾举行了中央委员全体会议，安排大会的最后筹备工作。
    新华社柏林30日电　哥本哈根消息：参加丹麦共产党第二十次代表大会的中国共产党代表、中共中央委员、书记处候补书记刘澜涛已于29日乘飞机到达丹麦首都哥本哈根。
    以波斯别洛夫为首的参加丹共代表大会的苏联共产党代表团也同机抵达。
    到机场欢迎的有丹共中央委员会副主席、中央执行委员会委员阿·任生和丹共中央执行委员会委员伊·内隆德。当天下午，丹共中央委员会举行酒会，欢迎已经到达哥本哈根的各兄弟党的代表团。
