Title: Discussing Prevention of Surprise Attacks; Second Secret Meeting Held
Author:
Date: 1958-11-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, November 12th: The East-West conference discussing the prevention of surprise attacks held its second secret meeting today under the chairmanship of Soviet representative Kuznetsov. A communique issued after the meeting stated that the representatives participating in the meeting continued to discuss the conference's work plan. Representatives from Czechoslovakia, Albania, and the Soviet Union spoke at the meeting. The U.S. representative spoke on behalf of the Western delegation.

The next meeting is scheduled for the 13th.

### 讨论防止突袭会议  举行第二次秘密会议

1958-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    讨论防止突袭会议
    举行第二次秘密会议
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦12日讯：讨论防止突然袭击问题的东西方会议今天在苏联代表库兹涅佐夫的主持下举行了第二次秘密会议。会后发表公报说，参加会议的代表继续讨论了会议的工作计划。会上发言的有捷克斯洛伐克、阿尔巴尼亚和苏联的代表。代表西方代表团发言的是美国代表。
    下次会议定于13日举行。
