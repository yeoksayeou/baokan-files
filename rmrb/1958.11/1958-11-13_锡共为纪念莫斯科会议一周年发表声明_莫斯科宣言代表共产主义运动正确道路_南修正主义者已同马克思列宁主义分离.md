Title: Ceylon Communist Party Issues Statement Commemorating Anniversary of Moscow Meeting: Moscow Declaration Represents the Correct Path for the Communist Movement; Yugoslav Revisionists Have Separated Themselves from Marxism-Leninism
Author:
Date: 1958-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, November 11th: The Ceylon Communist Party has issued a statement reiterating its support for the two declarations adopted by the Communist and Workers' Parties at the Moscow meeting in 1957, and condemning Yugoslav revisionism.

The statement was issued by the General Secretary of the Ceylon Communist Party, Pieter Keuneman, to commemorate the anniversary of the Moscow meeting.

The statement said, "This meeting clearly demonstrated the unity of Communist and Workers' Parties throughout the world and the tremendous development of Communist and Workers' Parties in various parts of the world."

The statement said, "This meeting demonstrated the maturity of the international communist movement, its loyalty to Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, and its determination to fight against various manifestations of revisionism and dogmatism."

The statement pointed out that the declaration adopted at the meeting "summarized the experience of the world communist movement, made a profound Marxist-Leninist analysis of the current situation, and formulated correct guidelines for socialist and progressive movements in various places."

The statement said, "The Ceylon Communist Party fully supports these documents, which enrich our understanding of the situation and provide us with invaluable ideological weapons to carry out our work."

The statement said, "The Moscow Declaration of Communist and Workers' Parties very correctly focused attention on the fact that revisionism is today the main danger to the Marxist-Leninist communist movement."

The statement said, "The program adopted by the League of Communists of Yugoslavia at its recent congress, and the subsequent actions of the Yugoslav revisionists, fully demonstrate the correctness of this warning and show the profound truth of Comrade Khrushchev's calling revisionism imperialism's 'Trojan horse.'"

The statement accused the Yugoslav revisionists of "slandering the Soviet Union and the socialist camp, whitewashing imperialism and its aggressive plans, denying the fundamental contradiction between socialism and capitalism in the modern world, covering up the reality of modern capitalist countries, betraying proletarian internationalism, and negating the Leninist theory of the role of the Communist Party." The statement said, "All of this shows that the Yugoslav revisionists have separated themselves from Marxism-Leninism and are playing the role of defenders and supporters of capitalist and bourgeois ideology, and they are doing so at a time when world capitalism has entered a period of acute danger, and it needs the help of revisionism in order to spiritually frustrate and weaken the struggle of the working masses."

The statement said, "The Yugoslav revisionists apparently hoped to put forward their so-called 'program' to replace the Moscow Declaration of Communist and Workers' Parties. However, these hopes have been fundamentally shattered, and all Communist and Workers' Parties have rejected and condemned this revisionist program, and have united even more firmly behind the Moscow Declaration and Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism."

The statement said, "The Ceylon Communist Party also severely condemns and rejects the revisionist views of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, considering them to be contrary to Marxism-Leninism and only beneficial to the bourgeoisie and imperialist warmongers. We uphold the Moscow Declaration, believing that it represents the correct path forward for the communist movement and the cause of Marxism-Leninism."

### 锡共为纪念莫斯科会议一周年发表声明  莫斯科宣言代表共产主义运动正确道路  南修正主义者已同马克思列宁主义分离

1958-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    锡共为纪念莫斯科会议一周年发表声明
    莫斯科宣言代表共产主义运动正确道路  南修正主义者已同马克思列宁主义分离
    新华社科伦坡11日电　锡兰共产党发表声明，重申支持各国共产党和工人党在1957年莫斯科会议上所通过的两个宣言，并且谴责南斯拉夫的修正主义。
    这个声明是锡兰共产党总书记克尼曼为纪念莫斯科会议一周年而发表的。
    声明说，“这次会议明显地表现了全世界各国共产党和工人党的团结一致和世界各地的共产党和工人党的巨大发展。”
    声明说，“这次会议表明了国际共产主义运动的成熟，表明了它对马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义的忠诚，表明了它为反对各种修正主义和教条主义的表现而进行斗争的决心。”
    声明指出，这次会议所通过的宣言“总结了世界共产主义运动的经验，对现代的形势作了深刻的马克思列宁主义的分析，为各地的社会主义和进步运动制订了正确的行动方针。”
    声明说，“锡兰共产党完全拥护这些文件，它们丰富了我们对情况的了解，使我们得到了无价的思想武器来进行我们的工作。”
    声明说，“各国共产党和工人党的莫斯科宣言十分正确地把注意力集中在这一事实上：今天修正主义是对马克思列宁主义的共产主义运动的主要危险。”
    声明说，“南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟在最近的代表大会上所通过的纲领以及随后南斯拉夫修正主义者的行动充分证明了这种警告的正确性，表明了赫鲁晓夫同志把修正主义称为帝国主义的
  ‘特洛伊木马’的深刻真理。”
    声明指责南斯拉夫修正主义者“诽谤苏联和社会主义阵营，粉饰帝国主义和它的侵略计划，否认现代世界的社会主义和资本主义之间的根本矛盾，掩盖现代资本主义国家的现实，背弃无产阶级国际主义的和否定列宁主义关于共产党的作用的理论。”声明说，“所有这些都表明，南斯拉夫修正主义者已经与马克思列宁主义分离，正在起着资本主义和资产阶级思想的辩护人和支持者的作用，而他们是在这样一个时候做的：世界资本主义进入了尖锐危险的时期，它需要修正主义的帮助，以便在精神上挫折和削弱劳动群众的斗争。”
    声明说，“南斯拉夫修正主义者显然希望提出他们的所谓‘纲领’来代替各国共产党和工人党的莫斯科宣言。但是，这些希望已经被根本粉碎了，所有的共产党和工人党都拒绝和谴责这个修正主义纲领，甚至更加坚定地团结在莫斯科宣言及马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义的后面。”
    声明说，“锡兰共产党也严厉谴责和拒绝南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟的修正主义观点，认为这种观点是违反马克思列宁主义的，它们只有利于资产阶级和帝国主义战争贩子。我们拥护莫斯科宣言，认为它代表了共产主义运动和马克思列宁主义事业的前进的正确道路。”
