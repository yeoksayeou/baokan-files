Title: Polish and Soviet Leaders Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1958-11-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 10th: TASS reported from Moscow on November 10th that a delegation from the Polish People's Republic held talks with the leaders of the Soviet government and the Communist Party of the Soviet Union in the Kremlin this morning. The Polish side participating in the talks included: Gomulka, Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, and other members of the Polish People's Republic delegation.

The Soviet side participating in the talks included: Voroshilov, Kirichenko, Kozlov, Mikoyan, Suslov, Khrushchev, Gromyko, and others.

### 波苏两国领导人举行会谈

1958-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

    波苏两国领导人举行会谈
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科10日讯：波兰人民共和国代表团今天上午同苏联政府和苏联共产党的领导人在克里姆林宫举行了会谈。波兰方面参加会谈的有：哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹和波兰人民共和国代表团的其他成员。
    苏联方面参加会谈的有：伏罗希洛夫、基里钦科、科兹洛夫、米高扬、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、葛罗米柯等人。
