Title: Praising the Communal Canteen

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-13
Page: 3

A Column: Everyone Praises the Communal Canteen

　　Praising the Canteen

　　Apricot Blossom Village, Peach Blossom Hamlet,
　　Eight wives praise the communal canteen;
　　Osmanthus tea, bumper harvest soup,
　　Eight-treasure rice, fragrant and steaming.

　　The elderly eat canteen meals,
　　Their lives as long as South Mountain, lasting ten thousand years;
　　The youth eat canteen meals,
　　Shouldering Mount Tai, chasing the sun;
　　The women eat canteen meals,
　　Each one becomes a clever girl;
　　The children eat canteen meals,
　　Growing into sky-propping kings overnight;
　　The blacksmiths eat canteen meals,
　　A three-room thatched hut can refine steel;
　　The carpenters eat canteen meals,
　　Able to make stone mills grow wings;
　　The cadres eat canteen meals,
　　A red sun rises in their hearts;
　　The commune members all eat canteen meals,
　　Even stony slopes can produce rice.

### 夸食堂

1958-11-13
第3版()
专栏：公共食堂人人夸

    夸食堂
    杏花村，桃花庄，
    八个老婆夸食堂；
    桂花茶，丰收汤，
    八宝米饭喷喷香。
    老人吃了食堂饭，
    寿比南山万年长；
    青年吃了食堂饭，
    挑起泰山赶太阳；
    妇女吃了食堂饭，
    个个变成巧姑娘；
    小孩吃了食堂饭，
    一夜长成托天王；
    铁匠吃了食堂饭，
    三间草棚能炼钢；
    木匠吃了食堂饭，
    能叫石磨长翅膀；
    干部吃了食堂饭，
    心中升起红太阳；
    社员都吃食堂饭，
    石坡也能产米粮。
