Title: Ambassador Liu Xiao Holds Reception for Chinese Observation Delegation

Author:
Date: 1958-11-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 26th - Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao held a reception tonight for the Chinese laboring people's observation delegation participating in the 41st anniversary of the October Revolution on the occasion of their departure from the Soviet Union. Invited to the reception were Nina Popova, Chairman of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, A.A. Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Vice Chairman Sachukov, Minister of Higher Education Yelyutin, Soviet Hero A. Fedorov, Director of the Far East Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Tsimianin, and Xie Erbakov, a responsible staff member of the CPSU Central Committee.

The entire Chinese laboring people's delegation headed by Liu Lantao, as well as embassy staff, attended the reception. Ambassador Liu Xiao, delegation leader Liu Lantao, Andreyev, Popova, and Sachukov all spoke at the reception. The reception took place in a very friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 刘晓大使为我国观礼团举行招待会

1958-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    刘晓大使为我国观礼团举行招待会
    新华社莫斯科26日电　我国驻苏联大使刘晓今晚为我国劳动人民参加十月革命四十一周年观礼团离开苏联举行招待会。应邀出席招待会的有苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席波波娃，苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫，副主席萨丘科夫，高等教育部部长叶留金，苏联英雄阿·费多罗夫，外交部远东司司长齐米亚宁，苏共中央负责工作人员谢尔巴科夫等。
    以刘澜涛为首的我国劳动人民代表团全体人员和使馆人员都出席了招待会。刘晓大使、刘澜涛团长和安德烈耶夫、波波娃、萨丘科夫都在会上讲了话。招待会在十分友好热烈的气氛中进行。
