Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 6

Students of Beijing Normal College Compile History of Chinese Poetry

(Our dispatch) Beijing – One hundred students (first-year freshmen) of Class 14, Department of Chinese Language and Literature, Beijing Normal College, are currently compiling a one-million-word History of Chinese Poetry, spanning from the pre-Qin period to the present, with the support of the Party committee and teachers. In order to collect materials, the students have almost visited all relevant cultural and historical research departments in Beijing, collecting over a thousand volumes of folk song and folk rhyme literature. They have now written an outline of the History of Chinese Poetry, consisting of approximately 50,000 words.

### 北京师范学院学生编写中国诗歌史

1958-11-29
第6版()
专栏：

    北京师范学院学生编写中国诗歌史
    本报讯　北京师范学院中文系第十四班一百个学生（一年级新生）最近在党委和老师的支持下，正在编写一部由先秦到现在共一百万字的中国诗歌史。学生们为了搜集材料几乎跑遍了北京各有关文史研究部门，搜集了一千余册民歌民谣的文献资料。现在，他们已写出了一份约五万字的中国诗歌史大纲。
