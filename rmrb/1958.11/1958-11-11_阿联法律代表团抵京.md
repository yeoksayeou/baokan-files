Title: 阿联 Legal Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-11
Page: 4

    The U.A.R. Legal Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, November 10th: The United Arab Republic (Egyptian Region) legal workers delegation, headed by Hafez Sabek, Attorney General of the U.A.R., arrived in Beijing on the 9th at the invitation of Dong Biwu, President of the China Political Science and Law Society. Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Wu Defeng, Vice President and First Secretary of the Secretariat of the China Political Science and Law Society, and Deputy Director of the First Office of the State Council; Da Puhan, Vice President of the China-United Arab Republic Friendship Association and Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, and many others.
    This delegation arrived in Guangzhou, China on November 1st and has visited Guangzhou, Wuhan, Chongqing, Chengdu and other places, and was received by President Dong Biwu in Chengdu.

### 阿联法律代表团抵京

1958-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联法律代表团抵京
    新华社10日讯　以阿拉伯联合共和国总检察长哈菲兹·萨贝克为首的阿联埃及地区法律工作者访华代表团一行六人，应中国政治法律学会会长董必武的邀请在9日到北京。到机场欢迎的有：中国政治法律学会副会长兼书记处第一书记、国务院第一办公室副主任吴德峰，中国—阿拉伯联合共和国友好协会副会长、中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生等多人。
    这个代表团是11月1日到我国广州的，曾先后在广州、武汉、重庆、成都等地参观，并在成都受到了董必武会长的接见。
