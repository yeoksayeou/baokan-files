Title: Bicycle 100km (Road) Race: Gao Jukun Sets New World Record
Author:
Date: 1958-11-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 9th: In a test race held on the Beijing-Tianjin highway on the morning of the 8th, Tianjin cyclist and Fifth Glass Factory worker Gao Jukun set a new world record in the 100km bicycle race with a time of 2 hours, 12 minutes, and 6.5 seconds, breaking the previous world record of 2 hours, 20 minutes, and 44.8 seconds set by Italian Bennedetti in 1942.

Gao Jukun is the third athlete in China to break the world record in the 100km bicycle race, following Li Yudi from Beijing and Wang Chenglin from Shanghai. His time is 12.4 seconds faster than Li Yudi's and 6 minutes and 52.9 seconds faster than Wang Chenglin's.

The referees for this competition were national referee Liu Zhanfa and first-class referee Tian Ruilin.

### 自行车百公里（公路）比赛  高聚坤创造世界新成绩

1958-11-10
第6版()
专栏：

    自行车百公里（公路）比赛
    高聚坤创造世界新成绩
    据新华社天津9日电　天津市自行车运动员8日上午在京津公路上举行的测验比赛中，第五玻璃厂工人高聚坤以二小时十二分六秒五创造自行车百公里的世界最高成绩，打破了意大利人班尼德蒂在1942年所创造的二小时二十分四十四秒八的世界最高成绩。
    高聚坤是继北京李郁棣和上海王成林之后、我国第三个打破百公里自行车世界最高成绩的运动员。他的成绩比李郁棣和王成林的成绩快十二秒四和六分五十二秒九。
    执行这次比赛裁判的是国家裁判柳占发和一级裁判田瑞林。
