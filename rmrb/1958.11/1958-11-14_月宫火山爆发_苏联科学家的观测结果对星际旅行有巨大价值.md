Title: Lunar Volcano Eruption: Soviet Scientists' Observations of Great Value for Interstellar Travel
Author:
Date: 1958-11-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th: TASS reports: Volcanic eruptions also occur on the Moon. Soviet astronomer Dr. Kozyrev recorded a lunar volcanic eruption on November 3rd.

    Around 4:00 AM Moscow time on November 3rd, Kozyrev, using a 50-inch reflector at the Crimean Astrophysical Observatory, obtained an unusual view of the central peak of the Alphonsus crater. The crater appeared red. After 6:00 AM, the brightness of this peak suddenly increased by almost double. This increase in brightness lasted for thirty minutes.

    Mikhailov, Chairman of the Astronomical Council of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, believes that Kozyrev's observations are of great scientific value, especially for celestial evolution and future interstellar travel. He said that in the past it was thought that the formation of the basic features of the lunar topography (craters) was the result of meteorite impacts on the Moon, and we can now believe that this view is completely incorrect. Volcanic eruptions show that the Moon, like the Earth, also has mountain-building processes that create undulations in the ground.

### 月宫火山爆发  苏联科学家的观测结果对星际旅行有巨大价值

1958-11-14
第6版()
专栏：

    月宫火山爆发
    苏联科学家的观测结果对星际旅行有巨大价值
    新华社13日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：月球上也有火山爆发现象，苏联天文学家科齐列夫博士11月3日纪录到了月球上的一次火山爆发。
    11月3日莫斯科时间上午四点钟左右，科齐列夫在克里米亚天体物理天文台用五十吋反射镜观测时，获得了阿尔芬斯寰形山中央山峰一种不寻常的景色。寰形山显出了红色。在六点钟以后，这个山峰的亮度突然增加了几乎一倍。亮度增加现象延续了三十分钟。
    苏联科学院天文学委员会主席米哈伊洛夫认为，科齐列夫的观测具有巨大的科学价值，特别是对天体演化学和未来的星际旅行。他说，过去曾经认为月球地形基本特点（寰形山）的形成是陨石撞击月球的结果，我们现在可以认为，这种看法是完全不正确的。火山爆发表明，月球像地球一样，也存在着造成地面起伏的造山过程。
