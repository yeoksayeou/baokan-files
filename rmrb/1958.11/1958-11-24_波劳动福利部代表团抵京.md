Title: Polish Labor and Welfare Ministry Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-11-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd – The delegation from the Polish Ministry of Labor and Social Welfare, invited by the Ministry of Internal Affairs to visit China, arrived in Beijing by train this morning, led by Mr. A. Sawaicki, Minister of Labor and Social Welfare of the Polish People's Republic and member of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party. Xie Juezai, Minister of Internal Affairs, and Vice Ministers Wang Ziyi, Wang Yifu, and Chen Qiyuan welcomed them at the station.

### 波劳动福利部代表团抵京

1958-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    波劳动福利部代表团抵京
    新华社23日讯　应内务部邀请来我国访问的波兰劳动与社会福利部访华代表团，在波兰人民共和国劳动与社会福利部部长、波兰统一工人党中央委员会委员亚·萨瓦茨基率领下，今天上午乘火车到达北京。内务部部长谢觉哉，副部长王子宜、王一夫、陈其瑗等到车站欢迎。
