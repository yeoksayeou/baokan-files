Title: Chen Yi Receives the Chairman of the Bulgarian Foreign Cultural Association

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received Y. Aframova, Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Foreign Friendship and Cultural Relations, this morning. Aframova is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Present at the reception was Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 陈毅接见保对外文联主席

1958-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见保对外文联主席
    新华社7日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会主席叶·阿弗拉莫娃。阿弗拉莫娃是应我对外文化联络委员会的邀请来中国访问的。
    接见时在座的有对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥。
