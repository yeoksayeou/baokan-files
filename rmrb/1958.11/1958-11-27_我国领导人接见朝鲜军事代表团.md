Title: Our Country's Leaders Receive the Korean Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, November 26th: Party and state leaders Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, and Peng Zhen received all the distinguished guests of the Korean Military Delegation in Wuhan this afternoon. (Picture attached)

　　Chairman Mao shaking hands with members of the Korean Military Delegation during the reception.
　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo (facsimile photo)

### 我国领导人接见朝鲜军事代表团

1958-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我国领导人接见朝鲜军事代表团
    新华社武汉26日电　党和国家的领导人毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、邓小平、彭真今天下午在武汉市接见了朝鲜军事代表团的全体贵宾。（附图片）
