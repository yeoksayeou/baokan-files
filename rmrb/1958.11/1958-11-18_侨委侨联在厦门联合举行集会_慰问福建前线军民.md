Title: Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission and Overseas Chinese Federation Jointly Hold Meeting to Console the Military and Civilians on the Fujian Front

Author:

Date: 1958-11-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Xiamen, November 17th: The Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission and the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese jointly held a consolation meeting in Xiamen City on the 16th to express their regards to the People's Liberation Army soldiers and the people on the Fujian front.

    Fang Fang, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, delivered a speech at the meeting praising the achievements of the People's Liberation Army in safeguarding and building the motherland. He said that the vast number of returned overseas Chinese and their families should take the heroic actions of the People's Liberation Army as an example, work hard, study hard, and fully support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan, Penghu, Jinmen, and Matsu, driving the American aggressors out of our territory, building the motherland, and striving for lasting peace in Asia and the world.

    Fang Fang also presented banners on behalf of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission and the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese to the frontline PLA troops, the Xiamen Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the Municipal People's Committee.

    The leaders of the frontline troops and Xiao Feng, Secretary of the Xiamen Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered speeches at the meeting, expressing their heartfelt gratitude for the warm condolences from the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission and the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, as well as the vast number of overseas Chinese.

### 侨委侨联在厦门联合举行集会  慰问福建前线军民

1958-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    侨委侨联在厦门联合举行集会
    慰问福建前线军民
    新华社厦门17日电　华侨事务委员会和中华全国归国华侨联合会，16日在厦门市联合举行慰问大会，慰问福建前线人民解放军将士和人民。
    华侨事务委员会副主任方方在会上致词赞扬人民解放军在保卫祖国和建设祖国的事业中树立的功绩。他说，广大的归侨、侨眷要以解放军英勇的行动作为榜样，努力劳动，努力学习，全力支援解放军为解放台湾、澎湖、金门、马祖，为把美国侵略者赶出我国领土，为建设祖国和争取亚洲与世界持久和平而奋斗。
    方方还代表华侨事务委员会和中华全国归国华侨联合会向前线解放军部队、中共厦门市委员会和市人民委员会赠送了锦旗。
    前线部队首长和中共厦门市委书记萧枫在会上致词，对华侨事务委员会和中华全国归国华侨联合会以及广大侨胞的热情慰问，表示衷心的感谢。
