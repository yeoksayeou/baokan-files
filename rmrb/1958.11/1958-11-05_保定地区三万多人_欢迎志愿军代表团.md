Title: Over 30,000 People in Baoding Region Welcome the Volunteer Army Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-11-05
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, November 4th: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation, headed by General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping, arrived in Baoding by special train today and received a warm welcome from over 30,000 people from all walks of life in the Baoding region.
    Accompanying the delegation were Liu Guanyi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Qu Tangliang, Secretary-General of the General Association Against US Aggression and Aid Korea, and Xie Xuegong, Secretary of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China. Li Yuenong, First Secretary of the CPC Baoding Prefectural Committee, Chen Zirui, Second Secretary, Yang Peisheng, Commissioner of the Baoding Commissioner's Office, and the heads of the Baoding municipal party, government and military were at the station to welcome them.
    The Volunteer Army delegation stepped out of the station amidst cheers and the sound of gongs, drums, and firecrackers. At the station, Yang Peisheng, Commissioner of the Baoding Commissioner's Office, delivered a speech on behalf of the six million people of the region, expressing a warm welcome to the most beloved people. General Wang Ping delivered a speech thanking the people of Baoding for their kindness.
    At the banquet held for the Volunteer Army delegation, leading cadres of the party and government organs in the Baoding region and representatives from all walks of life greeted the Volunteer Army representatives, feeling as close as family. Sun Guochen, the father of the Volunteer Air Force hero Sun Shengluo, who lived in Baoding, and his mother Wang Huizhen also attended the banquet. During the banquet, General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping personally presented commemorative medals to the father and mother of Sun Shengluo.
    At 9:30 a.m., a radio rally was held at the Baoding City Stadium to welcome the Volunteer Army delegation. At the meeting, Hao Tiemin, the mayor of Baoding, and General Zhang Mingyuan, the representative of the Volunteer Army delegation, delivered speeches. The party and government organs and people from all walks of life in the Baoding region presented banners to the Volunteer Army delegation.

### 保定地区三万多人  欢迎志愿军代表团

1958-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    保定地区三万多人
    欢迎志愿军代表团
    据新华社保定4日电　以杨勇上将、王平上将为首的中国人民志愿军代表团今天乘专车到保定，受到保定地区各界人民三万多人热烈欢迎。
    陪同代表团前来的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长刘贯一、抗美援朝总会秘书长区棠亮、中共河北省委书记处书记解学恭等。前往车站欢迎的有中共保定地委第一书记李悦农、第二书记陈子瑞，保定专员公署专员杨培生及保定市党政军负责人等。
    志愿军代表团在万众欢呼声和锣鼓、鞭炮声中步出车站。在车站上，保定专员公署专员杨培生代表全区六百万人民向最可爱的人致词表示热烈的欢迎，王平上将致词感谢保定人民的盛情。
    在为志愿军代表团举行的宴会上，保定地区党政领导机关的负责干部和各界代表与志愿军代表们相互问候，亲如一家。家住保定的志愿军空军英雄孙生禄烈士的父亲孙国臣和母亲王惠珍也参加了宴会。席间，杨勇上将和王平上将亲手给孙生禄烈士的父亲和母亲挂上了纪念章。
    上午九点半，在保定市体育场召开了欢迎志愿军代表团的广播大会。会上，保定市市长郝铁民和志愿军代表团代表张明远将军讲了话。保定地区党政机关和各界人民向志愿军代表团献了旗。
