Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Delivers Report to the Volunteer Army Delegation on the International Situation and the Situation in the Taiwan Region
Author:
Date: 1958-11-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th. Today, Vice Premier Chen Yi delivered a report to the entire Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation, headed by General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping, on the current international situation and the situation in the Taiwan Strait region. In his report, Vice Premier Chen Yi also discussed the great achievements and bright future of our people in various industrial and agricultural constructions under the wise leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao. He encouraged the victorious returning Volunteer Army officers and soldiers to guard against arrogance and impetuosity, be modest and simple, carry forward the glorious tradition of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, actively participate in the great leap forward of socialist construction, and together with the people of the whole country, continue to wage a resolute struggle against U.S. imperialism, and continue to strive for the liberation of our motherland's territory of Taiwan, Penghu, Jinmen, and Mazu.

Vice Premier Chen Yi said that the Chinese People's Volunteer Army is the vanguard of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, and the hero who punctured the paper tiger of U.S. imperialism. Today, after returning to the motherland, they must strive to be advanced and always maintain their glory in the posts of building and defending the motherland.

### 陈毅副总理向志愿军代表团作报告  论述国际形势和台湾地区局势

1958-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理向志愿军代表团作报告
    论述国际形势和台湾地区局势
    新华社9日讯　今天，陈毅副总理向以杨勇上将和王平上将为首的中国人民志愿军代表团全体人员，作了关于目前国际形势和台湾海峡地区局势问题的报告。陈毅副总理在报告中还论述了我国人民在党和毛主席的英明领导下，工农业各项建设所取得的伟大成就和灿烂美好的远景。他勉励胜利归来的志愿军官兵们，要戒骄戒躁，谦逊朴实，发扬抗美援朝斗争的光荣传统，积极地参加到社会主义建设大跃进中去，并和全国人民一道，继续进行同美帝国主义的坚决斗争，继续为解放祖国的领土台湾、澎湖、金门、马祖而努力。
    陈毅副总理说，中国人民志愿军是抗美援朝的先锋，是戳穿美帝国主义纸老虎的英雄，今天，在回到祖国以后，在建设和保卫祖国的岗位上，也要力争先进，永远保持光荣。
