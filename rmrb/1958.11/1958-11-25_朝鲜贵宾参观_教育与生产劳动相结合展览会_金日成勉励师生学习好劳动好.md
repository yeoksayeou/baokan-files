Title: Korean Guests Visit "Exhibition on the Combination of Education and Productive Labor" Kim Il-sung Encourages Teachers and Students to Study Well and Labor Well
Author:
Date: 1958-11-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 24th: Premier Kim Il-sung, along with the Korean Government Delegation and the Korean Military Delegation, accompanied by Vice Premier He Long, visited the "Exhibition on the Combination of Education and Productive Labor" today.

The distinguished guests spent over three hours touring the comprehensive hall and the Jilin hall of the exhibition, gaining an understanding of the achievements made in various parts of China and Jilin Province in implementing the policy of combining education with productive labor proposed by the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. At the end of the visit, Premier Kim Il-sung inscribed for the exhibition: "The combination of education and production is the best policy for cultivating builders of socialism and communism." During the visit, Premier Kim Il-sung also constantly encouraged the young teachers, students, and staff working at the exhibition to study and work harder and achieve even greater results.

In the past, Premier Kim Il-sung led the Korean people in Jilin Province and other parts of Northeast China, providing long-term assistance to the Chinese people's revolutionary struggle. Today, after visiting the comprehensive hall for more than two hours, he still went to visit the Jilin hall, regardless of fatigue, to learn about the province's new achievements in developing education. Premier Kim Il-sung and Comrade Pak Jeong-ae took photos with the young pioneers in the "Young Pioneer" small factory in this hall and encouraged them to study hard. Comrade Pak Jeong-ae, along with other distinguished guests from the Korean Government Delegation and the Korean Military Delegation, also presented them with commemorative badges engraved with the image of Kim Il-sung.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed during their visit, with crowds cheering everywhere. Many young teachers and students presented the distinguished guests with new products they had made themselves.

Accompanying the distinguished guests on the visit were Vice Minister of Education Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and others.

### 朝鲜贵宾参观“教育与生产劳动相结合展览会”  金日成勉励师生学习好劳动好

1958-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜贵宾参观“教育与生产劳动相结合展览会”
    金日成勉励师生学习好劳动好
    新华社24日讯　金日成首相同朝鲜政府代表团和朝鲜军事代表团的全体贵宾，今天由贺龙副总理陪同，参观了“教育与生产劳动相结合展览会”。
    贵宾们以三个多小时的时间，参观了展览会的综合馆和吉林馆，了解了我国各地和吉林省在执行中共中央提出的教育与生产劳动相结合的方针以来所取得的成就。金日成首相在结束参观时为展览会亲笔题词：“教育与生产相结合是培养社会主义和共产主义建设者的最好方针”。金日成首相在参观中还不时地勉励在展览会上工作的青年教师、学生和工作人员，要他们更好地学习和劳动，做出更大的成绩。
    金日成首相过去领导朝鲜人民在吉林省和东北其他地区长时期地帮助过中国人民的革命斗争，今天他在综合馆参观了两个多小时以后，仍然不顾疲劳，专门去参观了吉林馆，了解了这个省在发展教育事业方面的新成就。金日成首相和朴正爱同志在这个馆的“红领巾”小工厂中，同男女少先队员们一起照了像，勉励他们努力学习。朴正爱同志同朝鲜政府代表团和朝鲜军事代表团的其他贵宾，还把雕有金日成像的纪念章送给他们。
    贵宾们参观时受到极其热烈的欢迎，到处都有夹道欢呼的人群。许多青年教师和学生把他们亲手做成的新产品献给了贵宾。
    陪同贵宾们参观的，还有教育部副部长刘皑风，外交部副部长曾涌泉等人。
