Title: Kim Il-sung Leaves Guangzhou to Visit Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 28th. After concluding their visit to Guangzhou, the North Korean government delegation led by Premier Kim Il-sung left by special plane at 9:14 this morning to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Phan My, Deputy Minister of the Prime Minister's Office of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Tran Xuan Do, Vietnamese Ambassador to North Korea, and Deng Zhengliao, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied them.

This morning, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea Qiao Xiaoguang, and General Li Zhimin, who accompanied the Korean military delegation to Guangzhou, went to the airport to bid farewell to the distinguished guests. Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province and Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, Wang De, First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, as well as responsible persons from the provincial and municipal CPPCC, various democratic parties, and people's organizations, all went to the airport to bid farewell to the distinguished guests. Also at the airport to see them off were North Korean Ambassador to China Li Yong-ho and members of the North Korean military delegation and the North Korean art troupe, as well as heads of consulates in Guangzhou from Czechoslovakia, Vietnam, Poland, and other countries.

Young Pioneers presented flowers to Premier Kim Il-sung and other distinguished North Korean guests. Before boarding the plane, Premier Kim Il-sung repeatedly shook hands with those who came to see them off.

The delegation will return to our country for further visits after its visit to Vietnam.

### 金日成首相离广州去越南访问

1958-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成首相离广州去越南访问
    新华社广州28日电　金日成首相率领的朝鲜政府代表团结束在广州的访问后，今天上午九点十四分乘专机前往越南民主共和国访问。越南民主共和国总理府副部长潘美、越南驻朝鲜大使陈春度、外交部礼宾司副司长邓振辽陪同前往。
    今天早晨，周恩来总理、贺龙副总理、外交部副部长曾涌泉、中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光和陪同朝鲜军事代表团来广州的李志民上将到机场欢送贵宾。广东省省长、中共广东省委书记陈郁、广州市市长朱光、中共广州市委第一书记王德、中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长文年生中将，以及省、市政协和各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人都到机场欢送贵宾。到机场欢送贵宾的还有朝鲜驻我国大使李永镐和朝鲜军事代表团、朝鲜艺术团的团员们，捷克斯洛伐克、越南、波兰等国驻广州领事馆负责人。
    少先队员把鲜花献给金日成首相和其他朝鲜贵宾。金日成首相在上机前同欢送者一再握手告别。
    代表团在越南访问后将回我国继续进行访问。
