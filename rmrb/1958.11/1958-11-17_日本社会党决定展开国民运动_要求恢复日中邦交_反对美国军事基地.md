Title: Japanese Socialist Party Decides to Launch National Movement Demanding Restoration of Sino-Japanese Diplomatic Relations and Opposition to American Military Bases
Author:
Date: 1958-11-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th: According to a broadcast by Japan Broadcasting Corporation today, the Japanese Socialist Party has decided to launch a national movement to fight for the restoration of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations, the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen bombs, opposition to military bases, the return of Okinawa Island by the United States, and the defense of the Constitution. The Socialist Party has also decided to link this movement with the movement against the Kishida Nobusuke government's revision of the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty."

The Socialist Party has decided to dispatch organization staff to various regions from the end of November to December to carry out preparatory work.

### 日本社会党决定展开国民运动  要求恢复日中邦交  反对美国军事基地

1958-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    日本社会党决定展开国民运动
    要求恢复日中邦交  反对美国军事基地
    新华社16日讯　东京消息：据日本广播协会电台今天广播，日本社会党决定展开一个国民运动，要求为恢复日中邦交、禁止原子弹和氢弹、反对军事基地、要美国归还冲绳岛和保卫宪法而斗争。社会党还决定要使这个运动同反对岸信介政府修改日美“安全条约”的运动联系起来。
    社会党已经决定从11月底到12月派遣组织工作人员到各地去做准备工作。
