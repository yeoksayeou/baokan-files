Title: Marshal Malinovsky Awarded Second "Gold Star" Medal
Author:
Date: 1958-11-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 22nd: According to TASS, prominent Soviet military leader and Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Rodion Malinovsky has been awarded a second "Gold Star" medal and the title of "Hero of the Soviet Union" for the second time.

The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, in the order awarding the medal, stated: "This high honor is awarded in recognition of Marshal Malinovsky's great contributions to the Soviet state and the Soviet Armed Forces on the occasion of his sixtieth birthday. A bust of Malinovsky will be erected in his hometown of Odessa."

Malinovsky actively participated in the Civil War from 1918 to 1920. In 1930, he received specialized education at the Soviet Frunze Military Academy.

During World War II, Malinovsky actively participated in the Battle of Stalingrad to crush the fascist forces. In 1943, he commanded the Southwestern Front in a series of victories, liberating the Donbass and southern Ukraine. In 1944, he served as commander of the Second Ukrainian Front, leading Soviet troops to drive the German fascist forces out of Romania and Hungary.

Under the leadership of Marshal Malinovsky, the troops of the Soviet Transbaikal Front defeated the Japanese Kwantung Army in August 1945.

Marshal Malinovsky has been awarded Soviet orders and medals several times for his repeated military achievements.

### 马利诺夫斯基元帅  荣获第二枚“金星”勋章

1958-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    马利诺夫斯基元帅
    荣获第二枚“金星”勋章
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科22日讯：苏联著名军事家、苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅荣获第二枚“金星”勋章，再度获得“苏联英雄”称号。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团在颁发勋章的命令中说：“在马利诺夫斯基六十寿辰的时候为了表彰他对苏维埃国家和苏联武装部队的巨大功勋，特授予他这种崇高的奖赏。在元帅的故乡敖德萨城将建立马利诺夫斯基的半身铜像。”
    马利诺夫斯基积极地参加了1918年到1920年的国内战争，1930年，在苏联伏龙芝军事学院受过专门教育。
    在第二次世界大战期间，马利诺夫斯基积极地参加了粉碎法西斯军队的斯大林格勒战役。他在1943年指挥西南方面军打了一连串的胜仗，解放了顿巴斯和乌克兰南部，1944年任乌克兰第二方面军司令，领导苏军把德国法西斯军队赶出了罗马尼亚和匈牙利。
    在马利诺夫斯基元帅的领导下，苏联外贝加尔战线的军队在1945年8月击溃了日本关东军。
    马利诺夫斯基元帅由于屡建战功，曾经数次荣获苏联的勋章和奖章。
