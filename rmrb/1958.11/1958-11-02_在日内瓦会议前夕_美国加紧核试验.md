Title: U.S. Intensifies Nuclear Testing on the Eve of Geneva Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-11-02
Page: 4

    On the Eve of the Geneva Conference
    U.S. Intensifies Nuclear Testing
    Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: According to American news agencies, the United States is hastily conducting a series of nuclear explosion tests in the run-up to the Geneva conference on the cessation of nuclear weapons testing.
    On the 29th, the United States conducted three atomic explosion tests at the Nevada Test Site.
    Another explosion test was conducted on the 30th. It is reported that the United States has conducted nineteen explosion tests this autumn so far. Since the Soviet Union announced its unilateral cessation of nuclear testing on March 31st of this year, the United States has conducted a total of thirty-two experimental explosions.

### 在日内瓦会议前夕  美国加紧核试验

1958-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    在日内瓦会议前夕
    美国加紧核试验
    据新华社31日讯　据美国通讯社报道，美国在讨论关于停止核武器试验的日内瓦会议前夕，急急忙忙地进行了一系列的核爆炸试验。
    29日美国在内华达州原子试验场进行了三次原子爆炸试验。
    30日继续进行了一次爆炸试验。据报道，到现在为止，今年秋天美国已进行了十九次爆炸试验。自从今年3月31日苏联宣布单方面停止核试验以来，美国一共进行了三十二次试验性爆炸。
