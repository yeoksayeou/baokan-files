Title: Chen Yi Receives Austrian Journalist Barkada
Author:
Date: 1958-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Barkada, a journalist from the Austrian newspaper "Die Presse," on the morning of the 14th. Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was also present.

### 陈毅接见奥记者巴尔卡达

1958-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见奥记者巴尔卡达
    据新华社讯　陈毅副总理14日上午接见了奥地利“新闻报”记者巴尔卡达。陪同接见的有外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
