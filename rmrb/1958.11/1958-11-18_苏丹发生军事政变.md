Title: Military Coup in Sudan
Author:
Date: 1958-11-18
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 17th: According to a broadcast by Sudan Radio this morning, a military coup occurred in Sudan this morning. The coup was led by Ibrahim Abboud, Commander-in-Chief of the Sudanese Army.
    According to a Middle East News Agency dispatch from Khartoum, the radio station in Omdurman, across the river from Khartoum, broadcast this morning that Abboud announced at 3:00 AM today the dissolution of all Sudanese political parties, the prohibition of all gatherings and demonstrations in the provinces, and the prohibition of the publication of all newspapers until the Ministry of Interior issues new orders.
    He also announced that the coup authorities will do their best to resolve all outstanding issues between Sudan and the United Arab Republic, and to mend the artificial cracks between the two countries, thereby improving relations between them.

### 苏丹发生军事政变

1958-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹发生军事政变
    新华社开罗17日电　据苏丹电台今天早上广播，苏丹在今天清晨发生了军事政变。领导政变的是苏丹军队总司令易卜拉欣·阿布德。
    据中东通讯社喀土穆消息，喀土穆对岸恩图曼地方的电台今天早上广播说，阿布德在今天清晨三点钟宣布解散一切苏丹政党，禁止在各省举行各种集会和示威游行，禁止出版一切报纸，直到内政部发布新的命令时为止。
    他还宣布，政变当局将尽力解决苏丹和阿拉伯联合共和国之间一切悬而未决的问题，并且弥补两国之间的人为的裂缝，从而改善两国之间的关系。
