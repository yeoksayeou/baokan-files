Title: Anti-American and Aid-Korea Association Hosts Banquet to See Off Volunteer Army Delegation; Send Them to New Fronts, Win New Honors; Peng Dehuai Receives Delegation and Encourages Them to Always Be the Most Beloved People
Author:
Date: 1958-11-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th. This noon, the Chinese People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association hosted a banquet to see off the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation headed by General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping.

Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association, Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman, Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Anti-American and Aid-Korea Branch, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and others attended today's banquet.

During the banquet, Guo Moruo and Liao Chengzhi raised their glasses to wish all comrades of the Volunteer Army new honors in defending the motherland and participating in the socialist construction of the motherland in the future.

When the Volunteer Army representatives toasted their loved ones, they said that they would never let down the expectations of the people of the motherland, and they must use the same vigor they used to defeat the American imperialists on the Korean battlefield to defend and build the motherland.

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th. Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, received all members of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation headed by General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping this morning and had a cordial conversation.

Minister Peng Dehuai had just returned to Beijing after inspecting work in other places. In his speech, he encouraged everyone to firmly remember Chairman Mao's famous saying, "Modesty helps one to progress, while conceit makes one lag behind," to always pay attention to guarding against arrogance and rashness, and to always be the most beloved people.

### 抗美援朝总会设宴欢送志愿军代表团  送上新阵地　夺取新荣誉  彭德怀接见代表团勉励永做最可爱的人

1958-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    抗美援朝总会设宴欢送志愿军代表团
    送上新阵地　夺取新荣誉
    彭德怀接见代表团勉励永做最可爱的人
    新华社11日讯　今天中午，中国人民抗美援朝总会设宴欢送以杨勇上将、王平上将为首的中国人民志愿军代表团。
    抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若、副主席廖承志、北京市抗美援朝分会主席张奚若、北京市副市长吴晗等出席了今天的宴会。
    席间，郭沫若和廖承志举杯祝志愿军全体同志今后在保卫祖国和参加祖国社会主义建设中取得新的荣誉。
    志愿军代表们在向亲人们敬酒时表示，决不辜负祖国人民的期望，一定要拿出在朝鲜战场打败美帝的干劲来保卫祖国和建设祖国。
    新华社11日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，今天上午接见以杨勇上将、王平上将为首的中国人民志愿军代表团全体人员，并作了亲切的谈话。
  彭德怀部长刚从外地视察工作后回到北京。他在谈话中勉励大家要牢牢记住毛主席说的“虚心使人进步，骄傲使人落后”的名言，时刻注意戒骄戒躁，永远做最可爱的人。
