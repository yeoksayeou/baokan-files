Title: Sanmenxia Sings of Aswan
Author: Liu Lanshan
Date: 1958-11-16
Page: 8

    Sanmenxia Sings of Aswan
    Liu Lanshan
    On the banks of the roaring, cheering Yellow River,
    We see you standing tall beside the Nile;
    Please accept the congratulations of the Sanmenxia Dam workers,
    We are singing for your commencement.

    We sing of the children of the Nile,
    They are so brave and strong:
    The Anglo-French pirates were driven away by them,
    American conspiracies cannot strangle Aswan.

    We sing of our dear Volga,
    It carries such selfless warmth,
    Coming from the north to the banks of the Nile,
    Extending to you the most powerful arm.

    Great friend, sister Aswan,
    We stand shoulder to shoulder, arm in arm,
    For the happiness of our respective homelands to blossom,
    Let us spur our horses and capture the old Dragon King!

### 三门峡歌唱阿斯旺
刘岚山
1958-11-16
第8版()
专栏：

    三门峡歌唱阿斯旺
    刘岚山
    在奔腾欢呼的黄河之滨，
    望见你高高站在尼罗河旁；
    请接受三门峡水坝工人的祝贺，
    我们正在为你开工而歌唱。
    我们歌唱尼罗河的儿女，
    他们那样勇敢而又坚强：
    英法海盗曾被他们赶走，
    美国阴谋扼杀不了阿斯旺。
    我们歌唱亲爱的伏尔加，
    它带着那无私的热心肠，
    从北方来到了尼罗河畔，
    伸给你以最有力的臂膀。
    伟大的朋友、姊妹阿斯旺，
    我们肩并肩、臂膀挽着臂膀，
    为了各自祖国的幸福花开，
    让我们快马加鞭擒住老龙王！
