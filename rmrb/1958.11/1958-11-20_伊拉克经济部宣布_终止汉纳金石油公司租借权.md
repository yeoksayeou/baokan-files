Title: Iraqi Ministry of Economy Announces Termination of Hannekin Oil Company Lease

Author: 
Date: 1958-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, November 18th: The Iraqi Ministry of Economy issued a statement this afternoon announcing the termination of the lease granted to the Hannekin Oil Company, one of the four oil companies operating in Iraq and a subsidiary of the British Iraqi Petroleum Company.

The Hannekin Oil Company's lease was originally scheduled to expire in 1996. The company manages the production and refining of oil from the Hannekin oilfield north of Baghdad. It produces 500,000 tons of oil annually.

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, November 18th: "National News" published on the 17th the Iraqi Ministry of Economy's decision to establish a Foreign Trade Commission.

The policies to be adopted by this commission include: prohibiting the import of luxury goods, restricting the import of unnecessary goods, prohibiting the import of goods from countries hostile and unfriendly to Iraq and Arab countries, increasing the import of necessary consumer goods that are conducive to Iraq's industrialization, protecting local industries, and no longer importing goods from countries with which Iraq has a trade surplus.

### 伊拉克经济部宣布  终止汉纳金石油公司租借权

1958-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克经济部宣布
    终止汉纳金石油公司租借权
    新华社巴格达18日电　伊拉克经济部今天下午发表声明，宣布终止给汉纳金石油公司的租借权，这个公司是在伊拉克营业的四个石油公司之一，它是英伊石油公司的一个子公司。
    汉纳金石油公司的租借权原来要到1996年满期。这个公司经营巴格达以北汉纳金油田的石油生产和提炼。它每年生产五十万吨石油。
    新华社巴格达18日电　“国家报”17日发表了伊拉克经济部关于成立对外贸易委员会的决定。
    这个委员会将采取的政策包括：禁止输入奢侈品、限制输入不必要的商品、禁止从对伊拉克和阿拉伯国家敌视和不友好的国家中输入货物、增加输入有助于伊拉克工业化的必要的消费物资、保护地方工业、以及不再从对伊拉克贸易有顺差的国家输入货物。
