Title: Chinese Delegation Departs for Cairo to Attend Asian-African Economic Conference
Author: 
Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: The Chinese delegation to the Asian-African Economic Conference, scheduled to be held in Cairo on December 8th, departed from Beijing by plane today, led by the head of the delegation Nan Hanchen and the deputy head Ji Chao Ding.

    The delegation includes experts from China in the fields of trade, banking, insurance, and economic research.

### 参加亚非经济会议  我国代表团启程赴开罗

1958-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    参加亚非经济会议
    我国代表团启程赴开罗
    据新华社28日讯　出席即将于12月8日在开罗召开的亚非经济会议的中国代表团，在团长南汉宸、副团长冀朝鼎率领下，今天乘飞机从北京启程。
    代表团包括我国贸易、银行、保险、经济研究等方面的专家。
