Title: Beijing Holds Grand Meeting Today to Welcome Korean Guests; Radio and Television Stations to Broadcast the Meeting Live

Author:

Date: 1958-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: A grand meeting will be held by people from all walks of life in Beijing on the afternoon of the 24th to welcome the Government Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the meeting live on its first program starting at 3:50 p.m. on that day. Beijing Television will also broadcast it simultaneously.

### 北京今日盛会欢迎朝鲜贵宾  大会实况将由广播电台和电视台转播

1958-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    北京今日盛会欢迎朝鲜贵宾
    大会实况将由广播电台和电视台转播
    新华社23日讯　北京市各界人民将在24日下午举行大会，欢迎以金日成首相为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团。中央人民广播电台从当天下午三时五十分开始将在第一种节目中转播大会实况。北京电视台也将同时转播。
