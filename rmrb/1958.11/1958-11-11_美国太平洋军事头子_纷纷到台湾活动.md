Title: American Pacific Military Chiefs Flurry to Taiwan for Activities
Author:
Date: 1958-11-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch from Taipei: American military personnel are frequently going to Taiwan to conduct aggressive activities. After completing the Pacific Air Force Conference in Manila yesterday, the Commander of the U.S. Pacific Air Force, Carter, flew to Taipei, where he held a series of talks with Smott, the commander of the U.S. forces in Taiwan, and others. Carter clamored in Taipei that "the United States does not intend to withdraw its air force from Taiwan."

The Commander of the U.S. Pacific Fleet, Hopwood, is scheduled to go to Taiwan for activities tomorrow. He plans to stay for six days, inspect U.S. military bases and the U.S. Seventh Fleet in Taiwan, and hold talks with Chiang Kai-shek and the U.S. military commanders stationed in Taiwan respectively.

### 美国太平洋军事头子  纷纷到台湾活动

1958-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

    美国太平洋军事头子
    纷纷到台湾活动
    新华社10日讯　台北消息：美国军事人员频繁到台湾进行侵略活动。美国太平洋空军总司令卡特昨天在马尼拉开完太平洋美空军会议后，乘飞机到了台北，同侵台美军司令斯摩特等人举行了一系列的会谈。卡特在台北叫嚷说，“美国不打算把它的空军部队撤出台湾”。
    美国太平洋舰队总司令霍普伍德定于明天到台湾活动，他准备逗留六天，视察台湾美军基地和美国第七舰队，并且分别同蒋介石和侵台美军将领会谈。
