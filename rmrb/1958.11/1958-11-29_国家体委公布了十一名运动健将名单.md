Title: State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Announces List of Eleven Master Athletes
Author:
Date: 1958-11-29
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China announced on the 26th the list of eleven master athletes. Ten of them are Guangdong Province water polo athletes: Xu Yuehan, Chen Guoheng, Li Zhuohua, Lin Zhiquan, Huang Fuxun, Li Chiyi, Che Ruyi, Liu Weinan, Ye Hongrong, and Ye Ziyou. The other one is Hebei Province swimming athlete Mu Ruilong.

### 国家体委公布了十一名运动健将名单

1958-11-29
第6版()
专栏：

    国家体委公布了十一名运动健将名单
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会在26日公布了十一名运动健将名单。其中十名是广东省水球运动员：徐约翰、陈国衡、黎卓华、林芝权、黄阜峋、李炽颐、车汝棣、刘伟南、叶洪荣、叶子由。另一名是河北省的游泳运动员穆瑞龙。
