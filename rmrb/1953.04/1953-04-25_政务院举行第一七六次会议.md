Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 176th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-04-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th—The 176th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 24th. The meeting heard a report by Wei Que, head of the Chinese Cultural and Educational Delegation, on the cultural and educational inspection of the People's Democratic Countries of Eastern Europe. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals, as well as appointment proposals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一七六次会议

1953-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一七六次会议
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百七十六次政务会议在二十四日下午举行。会议听取了中国文化教育考察团韦悫团长所作的关于东欧各人民民主国家文化教育考察的报告。会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案。
