Title: Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts a Banquet for the Mongolian Art Troupe
Author: 
Date: 1953-04-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th:
Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen hosted a banquet on the afternoon of the 29th to entertain all members of the Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe, who were invited to visit China. The Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Jalkhasaikhan, was also invited to attend.
Among those present were Zhou Yang, Li Dequan, Zhang Xiruo, Fan Changjiang, Ding Xilin, Liu Ren, Zhang Youyu, Wu Han, Cai Tingkai, and other well-known figures from Beijing's literary and art circles, including Shakov, Tian Han, Cao Yu, Zhao Shuli, Ouyang Shanzun, Jiao Juyin, and others, totaling more than 70 people.
At the banquet, Mayor Peng Zhen and the head of the Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe, Oyun, delivered speeches successively. The banquet proceeded in an exceptionally warm and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to the health of Comrade Tsedenbal, leader of the Mongolian People's Republic, to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, leader of the Chinese people, to the eternal friendship and cooperation between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples, and to the united world peace, democracy, and socialist camp led by the Soviet Union.

### 北京市彭真市长  设宴招待蒙古艺术团

1953-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市彭真市长
    设宴招待蒙古艺术团
    【新华社二十九日讯】北京市人民政府彭真市长在二十九日下午设宴招待应邀前来我国访问的蒙古人民共和国艺术团全体人员。蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使贾尔卡赛汗也应邀参加。
    出席作陪的有周扬、李德全、张奚若、范长江、丁西林、刘仁、张友渔、吴晗、蔡廷锴等及北京市文学、艺术界著名人士沙可夫、田汉、曹禺、赵树理、欧阳山尊、焦菊隐等共七十多人。
    在宴会上，彭真市长、蒙古人民共和国艺术团团长奥云曾相继致词。宴会在异常热烈友好的气氛中进行。宾主频频举杯为蒙古人民共和国人民领袖泽登巴尔总理的健康，为中国人民领袖毛泽东主席的健康，为蒙、中两国人民永远友好合作，为以苏联为首的团结一致的世界和平民主社会主义阵营而干杯。
