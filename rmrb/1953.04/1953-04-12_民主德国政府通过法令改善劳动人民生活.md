Title: East German Government Passes Decrees to Improve the Lives of Working People
Author:
Date: 1953-04-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 11th: According to a report by the German News Agency Berlin: The government of the German Democratic Republic passed decrees on April 9th, deciding to adopt measures to further improve the lives of the working people. The content of the decrees includes: improving the food in the canteens of various factories, abolishing the rationing system for textiles and socks, implementing a new food rationing system in the German Democratic Republic and the Democratic Sector of Greater Berlin, and increasing the variety of bread sold to residents.
    According to the government's decree, measures to improve food in factory canteens will be implemented starting May 1st, which will result in a doubling of the supply of meat, oil, and sugar in the canteens. The rationing of textiles will be abolished starting April 13th, and at the same time, the pricing of textiles and shoes in state-owned companies, cooperatives, and private stores will be lowered.
    The new food rationing system will narrow the scope of ration card distribution, with the ultimate goal of completely abolishing ration cards.
    The new decree is in line with the consistent policy of the government of the German Democratic Republic to systematically improve the lives of the working people and is the result of the efforts of the working people of Democratic Germany to increase production and reduce production costs.

### 民主德国政府通过法令改善劳动人民生活

1953-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府通过法令改善劳动人民生活
    【新华社十一日讯】据德意志通讯社柏林讯：德意志民主共和国政府在四月九日通过法令，决定采取措施，进一步改善劳动人民生活。法令内容包括：改善各工厂食堂的伙食、废除纺织品和鞋袜的配给制度、在德意志民主共和国和大柏林民主区实行新的食物配给证制度、增加卖给居民的面包的种类。
    根据政府的法令，改善工厂食堂伙食的措施将于五月一日起付诸实行，结果将使食堂的肉类、油脂和糖的供应增加一倍。纺织品的配给制将于四月十三日起废除，同时将降低国营公司以及合作社和私营商店纺织品和鞋的定价。
    新的食物配给证制度将缩小配给证发放范围，以最后达到完全取销配给证。
    新的法令符合于德意志民主共和国政府系统地改善劳动人民生活的一贯的政策，并且是民主德国劳动人民增进生产和减少生产成本的努力的成果。
