Our Great Motherland
The Fushun Coal Mine Industrial School adopts a teaching method that combines theory and practice, enabling students to quickly master technology in a short period. These coal mining students are interning at the Fushun Open-Pit Coal Mine. Here, they are visiting a drilling machine drilling into a coal seam.

    我们伟大的祖国
    抚顺煤矿工业学校采用理论和实际相结合的教学方法，使学生在短期内就能迅速掌握技术。这几位采煤科的学生正在抚顺露天煤矿实习。他们在这里参观钻探机钻探矿层。
