Title: Representative of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace travels to Paris to attend the memorial service for Yves Farge
Author:
Date: 1953-04-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 5th: Li Yimang, representative of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, left Prague by plane on the afternoon of the 4th to attend the memorial service for Yves Farge, former chairman of the French National Peace Council, in Paris.

### 我保卫和平委员会代表赴巴黎参加法奇的追悼会

1953-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    我保卫和平委员会代表赴巴黎参加法奇的追悼会
    【新华社布拉格五日电】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会代表李一氓在四日下午乘飞机离布拉格，前往巴黎参加前法国全国和平理事会主席伊夫·法奇的追悼会。
