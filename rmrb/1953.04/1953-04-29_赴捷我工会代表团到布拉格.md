Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1953-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 27th: At the invitation of the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia, the three-member delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, led by head of delegation Zhao Min, arrived here on the 26th to participate in the "May Day" celebration and the celebration of the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia on May 9th.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Jaroslav Kolar, Secretary of the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia, Josef Kunohsky, Director of the International Department of the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia, and representatives of the Chinese Embassy in Prague.

Song Enxi, another member of the delegation, had previously attended the International Administrative Committee meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions of Leather, Fur and Hide Workers and had arrived here earlier.

### 赴捷我工会代表团到布拉格

1953-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    赴捷我工会代表团到布拉格
    【新华社布拉格二十七日电】应捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会邀请，前来参加“五一”节庆祝大会和五月九日的捷克斯洛伐克解放八周年纪念大会的中华全国总工会代表团一行三人，由团长赵敏率领于二十六日到达此地。
    到机场欢迎的有：捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会书记雅罗斯拉夫·科拉尔、捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会国际部主任约瑟夫·库诺赫斯基和中国驻布拉格大使馆的代表。
    代表团的另一位团员宋恩锡曾出席世界工联皮革皮毛皮货工人工会国际行政委员会的会议，他已先行到达此地。
