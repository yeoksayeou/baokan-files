Title: The Communist Party of Sweden Holds Its Sixteenth Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-04-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, April 6th: According to TASS, the 16th Congress of the Communist Party of Sweden opened in Stockholm on April 4th. More than 330 representatives attended. Representatives of fraternal Communist and Workers' parties from Poland, Bulgaria, the German Democratic Republic, France, Britain, Finland, Denmark, and Norway also attended the meeting as guests. At the beginning of the congress, everyone stood in silence for one minute in mourning for J. V. Stalin and Klement Gottwald.

The congress received congratulatory telegrams from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Communist Party of China, the Korean Workers' Party, and other fraternal parties of people's democratic countries.

The congress heard a report by Hilding Hagberg, Chairman of the Communist Party of Sweden, on the domestic and international situation and the tasks of the party. Hagberg spoke about Sweden's economic situation, pointing out that the current policies of the Swedish government have led to a decline in Swedish exports, a decrease in orders for enterprises, and a continuous increase in unemployment. He said that there is a way out of Sweden's current economic situation, which is to expand trade with the Soviet Union and the people's democratic countries. Hagberg pointed out at the end of his report that the struggle for peace must be taken as a central task. He said that the struggle to defend democratic rights is an inseparable part of the struggle for peace and national independence.

In the speeches during the discussion, the representatives pointed out that the struggle for national independence, peace, and improving living standards is an urgent task for the working people. The congress approved the political line and practical work of the Party Central Committee over the past period.

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, April 7th: According to TASS, the 16th Congress of the Communist Party of Sweden closed on April 6th.

The congress unanimously adopted a new party program - "The Swedish Path to Socialism."

In his concluding remarks, Party Chairman Hilding Hagberg emphasized that the congress played an important role in strengthening party unity and ensuring that the entire party unanimously supported the party's political line formulated according to the theories of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin.

The congress passed a letter to the Swedish working people, calling on them to strengthen their struggle and unite in action against the declining living standards and increasing unemployment.

A new Central Committee and Supervisory Committee were elected at the meeting.

Hilding Hagberg was unanimously re-elected as the Chairman of the Party.

### 瑞典共产党举行第十六届代表大会

1953-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典共产党举行第十六届代表大会
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩讯：瑞典共产党第十六届代表大会四月四日在斯德哥尔摩开幕。出席代表共有三百三十余人。波兰、保加利亚、德意志民主共和国、法国、英国、芬兰、丹麦和挪威的兄弟的共产党与工人党的代表，也作为来宾出席了会议。大会开始时，全体曾肃立为约·维·斯大林和克利门特·哥特瓦尔德默哀一分钟。
    大会收到了苏联共产党、中国共产党、朝鲜劳动党以及其他人民民主国家兄弟党的贺电。
    大会听取了瑞典共产党主席希尔丁·哈格堡关于国内外形势和党的任务的报告。哈格堡谈到瑞典的经济情况，指出瑞典政府的现行政策已经使得瑞典出口下降，企业的定货减少，失业人数不断增加。他说，有一条脱出瑞典目前经济状况的道路，那就是扩大与苏联及人民民主国家的贸易。哈格堡在他报告的结尾指出，必须把为和平而斗争作为一项中心任务，他说，为保卫民主权利而进行的斗争是为争取和平与民族独立而进行的斗争的不可分的一部分。
    在讨论的发言中，代表们都指出，为民族独立、和平与提高生活水平而斗争是劳动人民迫切的任务。大会批准了党中央委员会过去一个时期的政治路线和实际工作。
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩七日讯：瑞典共产党第十六届代表大会已于四月六日闭幕。
   代表大会一致通过了新的党纲——“瑞典走向社会主义的道路”。
    党主席希尔丁·哈格堡在总结发言中强调指出，代表大会在加强党的团结以及使全党一致拥护根据马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的学说拟订的党的政治方针方面，起了重要的作用。
    大会通过了告瑞典劳动人民书，要求他们加强斗争，加强一致行动，反对生活水平日益降低和失业日益增加。
    会上选出了新的中央委员会和监察委员会。
    希尔丁·哈格堡被一致连选为党的主席。
