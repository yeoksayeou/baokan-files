Title: World Peace Council Secretariat Issues Communiqué, States Worldwide Campaign to Demand Five Powers Hold Consultations Unfolds
Author:
Date: 1953-04-17
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 15th: TASS reports: The Secretariat of the World Peace Council has issued a communiqué, signed by Joliot-Curie, President of the World Peace Council. The communiqué reads as follows:

　　The Committee for the Letter to the Governments of the Five Great Powers, appointed by the World People’s Peace Congress, wrote separately to the governments of the five great powers on March 29th.

　　In accordance with the resolution of the World People’s Peace Congress, the Committee requested the governments of the five great powers to hold consultations to conclude a peace pact. The Committee also wrote to the governments of other countries, requesting these countries' public support for the Committee's actions.

　　A broad movement advocating consultations among the five great powers is vigorously unfolding worldwide.

　　Local peace groups, especially national peace committees and all other peace organizations, have launched large-scale movements in various countries to support the above-mentioned actions of the World People's Peace Congress Committee.

　　In order to unite all those who sponsor new actions aimed at genuinely easing international tensions, the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council is scheduled to hold a meeting from May 5th to 6th, 1953. This meeting will also decide the date of the next session of the World Peace Council.

### 世界和平理事会书记处发表公报指出全世界展开要求五大国举行协商的运动

1953-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会书记处发表公报指出全世界展开要求五大国举行协商的运动
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社布拉格十五日讯：世界和平理事会书记处发表了由世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里签发的公报，公报如下：
    世界人民和平大会任命的致五大国政府书委员会于三月二十九日已分别写信给五大国政府。
    根据世界人民和平大会的决议，委员会要求五大国政府举行协商，以缔结和平公约。委员会并写信给其他国家的政府，要求这些国家的公众支持委员会的行动。
    全世界正在蓬勃展开主张五大国举行协商的广泛运动。
    各地和平团体，特别是各国和平委员会和一切其他和平组织，在各国发动了大规模运动来支持世界人民和平大会委员会的上述行动。
    为了团结所有赞助目的在于真正缓和国际紧张局势的新行动的人，世界和平理事会常务委员会定于一九五三年五月五日到六日举行会议。这次会议还要决定世界和平理事会下届会议的日期。
