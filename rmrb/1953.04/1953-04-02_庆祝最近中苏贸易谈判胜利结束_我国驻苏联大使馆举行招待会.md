Title: Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union Holds Reception Celebrating the Successful Conclusion of Recent Sino-Soviet Trade Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1953-04-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 1st. To celebrate the successful conclusion of the recent Sino-Soviet trade negotiations and the signing of the protocol and agreements between China and the Soviet Union on the turnover of goods in 1953, Ge Baoquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, held a reception on the evening of March 30th.

Among those invited from the Soviet side were: Mikoyan, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Internal and Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union; Kabanov, First Deputy Minister of Internal and Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, and Kumykin and Borisov, Deputy Ministers; Pushkin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; responsible personnel from the Ministry of Internal and Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; and staff members of various Soviet trade companies.

Also invited were: Koleka, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania; Damiánov, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria; Gregor, Minister of Foreign Trade of the German Democratic Republic; Dąbrowski, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Polish People's Republic; Dvořák, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Czechoslovak Republic; Blagoeva, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria; Idenjab, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic; Siklós, Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic; Rim Hai, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Vosahlik, Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic; Appelt, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic; Natanaeli, Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of Albania; Duku, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian People's Republic; and Maleczk, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish People's Republic.

From the Chinese side, those present included: Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government; Song Shaowen, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council; Xu Tiemin and Li Tao, Counselors of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union; Li Qiang, Commercial Counselor; Major General Ji He, Military Attaché; and all members of the Chinese Commercial Delegation headed by Ye Jizhuang, Vice Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Trade, as well as staff members of the Embassy.

At the reception, Ge Baoquan and Ye Jizhuang frequently raised their glasses, toasted to the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, to the expansion and development of economic and friendly relations and trade between China and the Soviet Union, and to the health of Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Molotov, First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and Mikoyan, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Internal and Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union. Mikoyan and Kumykin also raised their glasses successively to the friendly relations between China and the Soviet Union, to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people, and to Premier Zhou Enlai.

The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere and did not end until midnight.

### 庆祝最近中苏贸易谈判胜利结束  我国驻苏联大使馆举行招待会

1953-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝最近中苏贸易谈判胜利结束
    我国驻苏联大使馆举行招待会
     【新华社莫斯科一日电】为庆祝最近中苏贸易谈判的胜利结束、中苏间关于一九五三年度货物周转的议定书等议定书和协定的签订，我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办戈宝权在三月三十日晚举行招待会。
    应邀到会的，苏联方面有：苏联部长会议副主席兼苏联国内和对外贸易部部长米高扬，苏联国内和对外贸易部第一副部长卡巴诺夫和副部长库米金、波里索夫，苏联外交部副部长普希金，苏联国内和对外贸易部及苏联外交部负责人员，及苏联各贸易公司工作人员等。
    应邀到会的，还有：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席科列加，保加利亚人民共和国部长会议副主席达米扬诺夫，德意志民主共和国对外贸易部部长格里哥尔，波兰人民共和国对外贸易部部长董布罗夫斯基，捷克斯洛伐克共和国对外贸易部部长德沃夏克，保加利亚人民共和国大使布拉戈耶娃，蒙古人民共和国大使伊丹甲布，匈牙利人民共和国大使希克拉丹，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使林海，越南民主共和国大使阮良朋，捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使沃萨格里克，德意志民主共和国外交使团团长阿佩尔特，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国公使纳塔纳伊利，罗马尼亚人民共和国临时代办杜库，波兰人民共和国临时代办马列茨基。
    我国出席作陪的有：中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春，政务院财政经济委员会秘书长宋劭文，我国驻苏联大使馆参赞许铁民和李滔、商务参赞李强，武官吉合少将以及以中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任兼对外贸易部部长叶季壮为首的我国商务代表团全体团员及大使馆工作人员等。
    招待会上，戈宝权和叶季壮频频举杯，为中苏两国人民永久的牢不可破的友谊，为中苏两国的经济友好关系和贸易的扩大与发展，为苏联部长会议主席马林科夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫和苏联部长会议副主席兼苏联国内和对外贸易部部长米高扬等人的健康干杯。米高扬和库米金也先后举杯为中苏两国的友好关系，为中国人民的领袖毛泽东主席、为周恩来总理的健康干杯。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行，直至午夜才尽欢而散。
