Title: Beijing People Actively Prepare to Celebrate "May Day"

Author:

Date: 1953-04-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: People from all walks of life in Beijing are actively carrying out preparatory work to celebrate the "May Day" International Labor Day. The Beijing Municipal Celebration of "May Day" International Labor Day Preparatory Committee has been established and held its first meeting on the 11th. The Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, in order to welcome "May Day" and the Seventh National Labor Congress, has decided to: hold a propaganda activity week and garden party at the Beijing Labor People's Cultural Palace from May 2nd to 10th; hold a historical photo exhibition of the Chinese workers' movement; organize workers' cultural performances to entertain the representatives of the Seventh National Labor Congress; at the end of April, the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions will jointly hold a radio conference with the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station to publicize the great achievements of the Chinese workers' movement and the significance of convening the Seventh National Labor Congress; before "May Day" Labor Day, among the cadres and workers in factories, mines and enterprises, conduct a general publicity and study of the resolutions of the Second Plenary Session of the Sixth Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the "Outline for the Propaganda of the Seventh National Labor Congress" in order to further mobilize the broad masses of workers and staff to launch patriotic labor emulation drives to welcome "May Day" Labor Day and the Seventh National Labor Congress.

### 北京市人民积极筹备庆祝“五一”节

1953-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市人民积极筹备庆祝“五一”节
    【新华社十五日讯】北京市各界人民积极进行庆祝“五一”国际劳动节的筹备工作。北京市庆祝“五一”国际劳动节筹备委员会已成立，并在十一日举行了第一次会议。北京市总工会为了迎接“五一”节和第七次全国劳动大会，决定：五月二日到十日，在北京市劳动人民文化宫举办宣传活动周和游园大会；举行中国工人运动历史图片展览会；组织工人文艺演出，招待第七次全国劳动大会代表；四月底，北京市总工会将和北京市人民广播电台联合举办广播大会，宣传中国工人运动的伟大成就和召开第七次全国劳动大会的意义；“五一”劳动节前，在各工厂、矿山和企业单位的干部和职工中，普遍地进行一次关于中华全国总工会第六届执行委员会第二次全体会议决议和“第七次全国劳动大会宣传大纲”的宣传和学习，以便更进一步地动员广大职工群众，开展爱国主义劳动竞赛，来迎接“五一”劳动节和第七次全国劳动大会。
