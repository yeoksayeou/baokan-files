Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-04-14
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Workers in the metal structure workshop of the No. 17 Factory of the Northeast Machinery Bureau, when manufacturing boilers, used the advanced Soviet welding method to replace the riveting method, saving 35% of materials, reducing working hours by 30%, and completing the first electric welded high-pressure boiler five days ahead of schedule. This is the crane lifting the boiler lid, preparing to weld it.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    东北机械局十七厂金属结构车间的工人，在制造锅炉的时候，采用苏联先进的焊接法来代替铆接法，节省材料百分之三十五，减少工时百分之三十，并提前五天完成第一台电焊高压锅炉。这是吊车正将锅炉盖吊起，准备去焊接的情形。
