Title: Tens of Thousands of Working People in Paris Attend Yves Farge's Funeral
Author:
Date: 1953-04-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, June 6th: According to TASS, tens of thousands of working people in Paris gathered at Père Lachaise Cemetery on the 4th to attend the funeral of Yves Farge, Chairman of the French National Peace Council. A memorial service was held at the entrance of the cemetery. Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation and President of the French Women's Union, Jules Duchaussol, Secretary of the French General Confederation of Labour, General Lovi da, a member of the Italian Chamber of Deputies, and a representative of the Italian National Peace Council delivered eulogies at the meeting.

All speakers pointed out the outstanding role played by Farge in the struggle against the Hitlerian occupiers and for national independence and world peace.

In addition to his family and friends, those attending Farge's funeral included French Communist Party leaders headed by Duclos, Fernand Vigne, Secretary-General of the French National Peace Council, representatives of other progressive organizations and members of parliament, as well as well-known figures in the fields of science, literature, and art.

### 巴黎数万劳动人民参加伊夫·法奇的葬礼

1953-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴黎数万劳动人民参加伊夫·法奇的葬礼
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社巴黎讯：四日，好几万巴黎劳动人民集合在拉歇神甫公墓（前译彼·拉舍斯公墓），参加法国全国和平理事会主席伊夫·法奇的葬礼。在公墓入口地方举行了追悼会。世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里、国际民主妇女联合会主席和法兰西妇女联盟主席欧仁妮·戈登、法国总工会书记儒勒·杜沙、意大利议会下院议员罗维达将军和意大利全国和平理事会代表等等在会上致悼辞。
    所有致词的人都指出法奇在反抗希特勒占领者及争取民族独立和世界和平的斗争中所起的卓越作用。
    参加法奇葬礼的，除了他的家属和朋友以外，还有以杜克洛为首的法国共产党领袖，法国全国和平理事会总书记费南·维涅及其他进步组织的代表和国会议员以及著名的科学、文学、艺术界人士。
