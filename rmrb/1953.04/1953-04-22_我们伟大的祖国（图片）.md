Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-04-22
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Farmers in various parts of North China are busily preparing for spring plowing. This is a scene from the Xusizeng Agricultural Production Cooperative in Fancun, a suburb of Shijiazhuang. They are very fond of using new farm tools. The new-style ten-inch walking plow manufactured by the North China Agricultural Machinery General Factory has just been delivered here, and they have gathered around to study its structure and usage.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    华北各地农民正在紧张地准备春耕。这是石家庄郊区范村许思曾农业生产合作社的社员们。他们很喜欢使用新式农具。华北农业机械总厂制造的新式十吋步犁刚运到这里，他们就围拢来研究它的构造和使用方法。
