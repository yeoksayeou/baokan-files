Title: Thorez Returns Home to Enthusiastic Welcome from French Working People
Author:
Date: 1953-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th dispatch from Paris: French Communist Party General Secretary Maurice Thorez returned to Paris on the 10th from the Soviet Union. Thorez went to Moscow for medical treatment in 1950 on the advice of doctors. The French working people warmly welcomed their leader's return. "L'Humanité" reported: The Central Committee of the French Communist Party has received many congratulatory telegrams and letters from all over the country.

### 多列士返国受到法国劳动人民热烈欢迎

1953-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    多列士返国受到法国劳动人民热烈欢迎
    【新华社十三日讯】巴黎消息：法国共产党总书记莫里斯·多列士在十日从苏联回到巴黎。多列士是遵照医生的劝告，在一九五○年去莫斯科就医的。法国劳动人民热烈欢迎他们的领袖归来。“人道报”说：法国共产党中央委员会接到了全国各地发来的许多贺电和贺信。
