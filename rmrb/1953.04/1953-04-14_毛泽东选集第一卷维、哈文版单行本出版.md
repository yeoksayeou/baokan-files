Title: Publication of Single Volumes of Selected Works of Mao Zedong, Uyghur and Kazakh Editions
Author:
Date: 1953-04-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, 13th. The Uyghur and Kazakh versions of the first volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" have recently been published in Xinjiang Province. The single volumes consist of fifteen fascicles, which were translated and published successively by the Xinjiang People's Publishing House from November 1951 to the spring of this year under the leadership of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong Publishing Committee" of the Xinjiang Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee, with more than 375,000 copies distributed. Now, many "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" study groups have begun to appear in the cities and pastoral areas of Xinjiang. The Xinjiang People's Publishing House is currently seeking opinions from readers of all ethnic groups, preparing to re-correct the translation, and will publish volumes in the future.

### 毛泽东选集第一卷维、哈文版单行本出版

1953-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东选集第一卷维、哈文版单行本出版
    【新华社迪化十三日电】毛泽东选集第一卷维吾尔文版和哈萨克文版单行本，最近在新疆省出版。单行本共十五分册，是新疆人民出版社从一九五一年十一月到今年春天，在中共中央新疆分局“毛泽东选集出版委员会”的领导下陆续翻译出版的，已先后发行三十七万五千多册。现在，在新疆的城市及牧区中，开始出现了不少毛泽东选集学习小组。新疆人民出版社目前正在征求各族读者的意见，准备重新校正译文，将来出版卷集。
