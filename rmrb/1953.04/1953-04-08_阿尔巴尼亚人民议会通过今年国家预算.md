Title: Albanian People's Assembly Approves This Year's National Budget
Author:
Date: 1953-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 6th Dispatch: According to TASS, the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania held its fourth regular meeting at the end of March and approved the 1953 national budget案.

After the meeting opened on March 28, presided over by the Chairman of the People's Assembly, M. Prifti, all the deputies stood up and observed a moment of silence for the great Stalin and the late President Gottwald of the Czechoslovak Republic.

The Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, G. Nushi, delivered a report on the 1953 national budget案 at the meeting. He pointed out that this year's budget has an income of 11.35 billion leks and an expenditure of 11.25 billion leks. The funds used for national economic development this year have increased by 43 percent compared to last year. This fund, together with the funds allocated from local budgets for this purpose, accounts for 49 percent of the total budget expenditure. The funds used for cultural and social undertakings account for 21 percent of the budget, an increase of 17 percent compared to last year. Public health expenditure increased by 29 percent compared to last year. Defense expenditure accounts for 10 percent of the budget expenditure.

The deputies of the People's Assembly spoke one after another during the discussion, expressing their agreement with this year's budget案. They unanimously stated that all working people of Albania will work hard to realize the new budget.

After three days of discussion, the parliament unanimously approved the 1953 budget on the 30th.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会通过今年国家预算

1953-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会通过今年国家预算
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会在三月底举行了第四次例会，通过了一九五三年国家预算案。
    会议在三月二十八日由人民议会主席米·普里夫蒂主持开幕后，全体议员起立，为伟大的斯大林和捷克斯洛伐克共和国已故总统哥特瓦尔德默哀。
    部长会议副主席果·努什在会议上作了关于一九五三年国家预算案的报告。他指出：今年预算中的收入为一百一十三亿五千万里克，支出为一百一十二亿五千万里克。今年用在国民经济发展方面的经费比去年增加百分之四十三。这笔经费和地方预算拨款中用在这方面的经费加在一起共占整个预算支出的百分之四十九。用在文化和社会事业方面的经费占预算的百分之二十一，比去年增加百分之十七。公共卫生经费比去年增加百分之二十九。国防经费占预算支出的百分之十。
    人民议会的议员们在讨论中纷纷发言，表示同意今年的预算案。他们并一致表示阿尔巴尼亚全体劳动人民将为实现新预算而努力。
    议会经过三天的讨论后，在三十日一致通过了一九五三年的预算。
