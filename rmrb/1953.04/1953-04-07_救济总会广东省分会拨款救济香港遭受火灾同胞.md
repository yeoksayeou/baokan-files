Title: Relief Association Guangdong Provincial Branch Allocates Funds to Aid Compatriots Affected by Fire in Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1953-04-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 6th – To aid our compatriots in Kowloon Walled City and Tai Kok Tsui, Hong Kong, who have suffered from fires, the Guangdong Provincial Branch of the China People's Relief Association, representing the people of Guangdong Province, has allocated a relief fund of 167 million yuan, which was completely distributed in Hong Kong on the 2nd. Note: A fire broke out in Kowloon Walled City, Hong Kong, on the night of February 24th, destroying more than 400 houses. In the Tai Kok Tsui timber yard area of Kowloon, a fire broke out on the morning of March 13th, destroying more than 200 shops and factories. A total of more than 4,300 disaster victims in both places received relief. The affected compatriots are very grateful for the concern shown by the people of the motherland.

### 救济总会广东省分会拨款救济香港遭受火灾同胞

1953-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    救济总会广东省分会拨款救济香港遭受火灾同胞
    【新华社广州六日电】为救济香港九龙城寨、大角嘴两地遭受火灾的我国同胞，由中国人民救济总会广东省分会代表广东全省人民拨发的救济款一亿六千七百多万元，已于二日在香港分发完毕。按：香港九龙城寨在二月二十四日夜发生火灾，烧毁房屋四百多间。九龙大角嘴木厂区，在三月十三日晨发生火灾，焚毁商店、工厂二百多间。两地受到救济的灾民共有四千三百多人。受灾同胞对祖国人民的关怀都很感激。
