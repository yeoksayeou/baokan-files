Title: Burmese Government Labor Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-04-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th:

    The Burmese Government Labor Delegation, consisting of six members, arrived in Beijing on the evening of April 29th for a visit to our country.  Those who went to the station to greet them included: Li Wenyi, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government; Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Wang Zhijie, Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union; Zhang Beihua, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Machine Manufacturing Trade Union; and Lang Xinkang, Deputy Section Chief of the Reception Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and others.  The list of the Burmese Government Labor Delegation is as follows:
    Head of the Delegation: Bo Muan
    Members: Wu Ge Ge Ji
      Wu Wang Zui
      Wu Mao Ge
      Wu Pei Zhen
      Wu Deng Zhen

### 缅甸政府劳动考察团抵京

1953-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府劳动考察团抵京
    【新华社二十九日讯】前来我国访问的缅甸政府劳动考察团一行六人于四月二十九日晚到达北京。前往车站迎接的有：中央人民政府劳动部办公厅副主任李文宜、中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明、中国铁路工会全国委员会秘书长王志杰、中国机器制造业工会筹备委员会主任张北华、中央人民政府外交部交际处副科长郎新康等人。缅甸政府劳动考察团名单如下：团长：波木昂团员：吴哥哥季
      吴旺最
      吴貌哥
      吴佩阵
      吴登阵
