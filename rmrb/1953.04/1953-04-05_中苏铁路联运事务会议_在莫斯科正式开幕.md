Title: Sino-Soviet Railway Joint Transport Conference Officially Opens in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-04-05
Page: 1
    Sino-Soviet Railway Joint Transport Conference
    Officially Opens in Moscow

    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 4th] The Sino-Soviet Railway Joint Transport Conference officially opened in Moscow on the 1st. Both sides are discussing various matters related to the joint railway transport between China and the Soviet Union in a friendly atmosphere.

    The Chinese delegation attending the conference, led by Mi Yong, Director of the International Joint Transport Bureau of the Ministry of Railways, arrived in Moscow on March 30th. Su Yechai, Director of the International Transportation Bureau of the Soviet Ministry of Transportation, and others welcomed them at the train station.

### 中苏铁路联运事务会议  在莫斯科正式开幕

1953-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏铁路联运事务会议
    在莫斯科正式开幕
    【新华社莫斯科四日电】中苏铁路联运事务会议一日在莫斯科正式开幕。双方正在友好气氛中商谈有关中苏两国间铁路联运的各项事宜。
    出席会议的我国代表团，在铁道部国际联运局局长糜镛率领下于三月三十日抵莫斯科时，苏联交通部国际交通局局长苏耶柴夫等人在火车站欢迎。
