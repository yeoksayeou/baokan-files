Author:
Date: 1953-04-02

    Our Great Motherland
    The renowned pottery industry of Yixing, Jiangsu Province, in our country, has fully restored production and prosperity under the guidance and assistance of the People's Government. This is a kiln in the outskirts of Yixing. In its vicinity, sheds are densely packed, and boats shuttle back and forth, transporting pottery to various places.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国著名的江苏宜兴的制陶业，在人民政府的指导和帮助下，已经全部恢复生产和繁荣起来了。这是宜兴近郊的一个窑场。在它的附近，栈棚密布，往来如梭的船只，正把陶器运往各地。
