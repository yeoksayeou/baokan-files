Title: Labour MP Bevan Accuses British Economy of Relying on the United States
Author:
Date: 1953-04-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 22nd (TASS dispatch): During a general debate in the British House of Commons on the 1953-1954 budget, Labour MP Bevan criticized the British economy's dependence on the United States.

Bevan said: "I think it is a national disgrace for Britain to continue to be so economically dependent on the United States. The British people demand economic independence for Britain..."

Bevan emphasized that Britain had allowed the United States to occupy its permanent peacetime markets. He stressed that this situation must be re-examined as soon as possible, because the idea of expecting Britain to find markets in the United States is unwise.

Bevan concluded: "I think we must look for markets elsewhere, because the markets in Britain and the United States cannot offset each other, whereas markets elsewhere can."

### 英国工党议员比万  指责英国经济依赖美国

1953-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    英国工党议员比万
    指责英国经济依赖美国
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十二日讯：英国下院在就一九五三—一九五四年度预算进行一般辩论时，工党议员比万批评英国的经济依赖美国。
    比万说：“我认为英国继续这样在经济上依赖美国是英国国家的耻辱。英国人民要求英国在经济上独立……”
    比万强调说，英国已经让美国占有它的和平时期的永久市场。他强调，这种情况一定要尽快地重新研究一下，因为期望英国能在美国找到市场这一念头是太不聪明了。
    比万最后说：“我认为我们必须在其他地方另找市场，因为在英国和美国的市场不能互相抵补，而在别处的市场则可以做到这一点。”
