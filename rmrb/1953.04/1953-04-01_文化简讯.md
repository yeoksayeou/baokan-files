Cultural Briefs

    Beijing Film Studio is producing a documentary about the Chinese people mourning Comrade Stalin

    In order to reflect the situation of the Chinese people mourning the great revolutionary teacher Comrade Stalin, the Beijing Film Studio has produced two news specials, reporting in a timely manner on the condolences and memorial services held by people in various places at that time. It will also rush to produce a documentary by the end of April, tentatively titled "He Lives Forever in Our Hearts."

    The main content of the film includes the situation of the Chinese people in various places deeply mourning after hearing the news of Comrade Stalin's death, and the situation of the Chinese people, under the call of Chairman Mao to turn grief into strength, strengthening the country's economic construction and cultural construction with practical actions, and redoubling their efforts to study Stalin's theories. The film will also briefly introduce Comrade Stalin's life and deeds, his guidance to the Chinese revolution, and his selfless assistance to the Chinese people's construction undertakings.

### 文化简讯

1953-04-01
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    文化简讯
    北京电影制片厂摄制我国人民悼念斯大林同志的记录片
    北京电影制片厂为了反映我国人民悼念伟大革命导师斯大林同志的情况，除摄制了两部新闻特辑，及时报道了当时各地人民吊唁和追悼等情形外，并将在四月底赶制出一部记录片，片名暂定“他永远活在我们心里”。
    该片主要内容有我国各地人民在听到斯大林同志逝世的消息后沉痛哀悼的情况，和我国人民在毛主席化悲痛为力量的号召下，用实际行动加强祖国经济建设和文化建设，加倍努力学习斯大林学说的情形。影片中还将概括地介绍斯大林同志的生平事迹和对中国革命的指导，对中国人民建设事业的无私援助等。
