Title: People in Various Places Actively Prepare to Celebrate "May Day"
Author:
Date: 1953-04-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The "May 1st" International Labor Day is approaching, and people in various places have begun to actively prepare for the celebration of this great festival. Cities such as Shenyang, Wuhan, Xi'an, Nanjing, Dihua, and Harbin have established preparatory committees for the celebration of "May 1st" Labor Day. Preparatory committees in various places are carrying out various preparations for the "May 1st" demonstration parade.

Various places are preparing to launch extensive publicity activities on "May 1st". Shenyang City's reporters, propagandists, and all propaganda forces have been organized and are about to go out for publicity. Harbin City has decided to hold a garden party on May 1st, and the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions is also preparing to hold an exhibition at the Workers' Club. Dihua City is preparing for the Secretary of the Xinjiang Branch of the CPC Central Committee to make a report to the cadres of various agencies and organizations in Xinjiang Province and Dihua City on the current situation and tasks around "May 1st". The Xinjiang Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will hold a photo exhibition of Soviet workers.

### 各地人民积极筹备庆祝“五一”节

1953-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    各地人民积极筹备庆祝“五一”节
    【新华社二十二日讯】“五一”国际劳动节就要来到了，各地人民已开始为筹备庆祝这一伟大的节日而积极地活动起来。沈阳、武汉、西安、南京、迪化、哈尔滨等城市已经成立了庆祝“五一”劳动节筹备委员会。各地筹备委员会正进行“五一”示威游行大会的各项准备工作。
    各地准备在“五一”节展开广泛的宣传活动。沈阳市的报告员、宣传员和所有的宣传力量已组织起来，即将出动进行宣传。哈尔滨市决定在五月一日举办游园晚会，市总工会还准备在工人俱乐部举办展览会。迪化市准备在“五一”节前后由中共中央新疆分局书记向新疆省和迪化市各机关、团体干部作关于目前形势与任务的报告。新疆省中苏友好协会将举行苏联工人的图片展览。
