Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Premier Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1953-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 31st. All Moscow newspapers on the 31st published in prominent positions the statement made by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, on the Korean armistice negotiations issue on the 30th.

### 莫斯科各报刊载周总理声明

1953-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报刊载周总理声明
    【新华社莫斯科三十一日电】三十一日莫斯科各报，都以显著地位刊载中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在三十日发表的关于朝鲜停战谈判问题的声明。
