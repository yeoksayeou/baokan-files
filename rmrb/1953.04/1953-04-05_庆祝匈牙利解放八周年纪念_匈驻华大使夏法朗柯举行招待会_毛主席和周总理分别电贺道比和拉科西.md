Title: Celebrating the 8th Anniversary of the Liberation of Hungary: Hungarian Ambassador to China, Szikra Ferenc, Holds Reception; Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams to Dobi and Rákosi
Author:
Date: 1953-04-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: To commemorate the 8th anniversary of the glorious Soviet army's liberation of Hungary, Hungarian Ambassador to China, Szikra Ferenc, held a reception at the Hungarian Embassy at 7:00 PM on April 4th.

    Among those invited to attend were: Gao Gang, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council; Zhang Zhiran, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, figures from the cultural and artistic circles, labor models, combat heroes, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, totaling over 500 people.

    Also invited to the reception were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China.

    The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    In other news: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council sent telegrams to Dobi, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic, and Rákosi, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, respectively, on the 1st, congratulating them on the 8th anniversary of the liberation of the Hungarian People's Republic.

### 庆祝匈牙利解放八周年纪念  匈驻华大使夏法朗柯举行招待会  毛主席和周总理分别电贺道比和拉科西

1953-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利解放八周年纪念
    匈驻华大使夏法朗柯举行招待会
    毛主席和周总理分别电贺道比和拉科西
   【新华社四日讯】四月四日为光荣的苏联军队解放匈牙利八周年纪念日，匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使夏法朗柯特于四日下午七时在匈牙利大使馆举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有：中央人民政府副主席高岗，政务院副总理郭沫若，最高人民法院副院长张志让，人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，外交部副部长伍修权以及中央人民政府各部门首长、各民主党派和人民团体负责人、文化艺术界人士、劳动模范和战斗英雄以及北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等五百余人。
    应邀到会的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    招待会在充满热烈、友好的气氛中进行。
    又讯：我国中央人民政府毛泽东主席和政务院周恩来总理，在一日分别致电匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比和部长会议主席拉科西，祝贺匈牙利人民共和国解放八周年纪念日。
