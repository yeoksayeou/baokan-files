Title: China Peace Committee Congratulates the Opening of the Second National Women's Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-04-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 16th—The China People's Committee for World Peace sent a letter to congratulate the opening of the Second National Women's Congress of China. The text is as follows: To the Second National Women's Congress of China: On the occasion of the opening of the Second National Women's Congress of China, the China People's Committee for World Peace extends warm congratulations to the Congress and all participating representatives, and wishes the Congress a complete success. Chinese women have demonstrated a firm and unremitting fighting spirit and made outstanding contributions in supporting the resistance against U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, safeguarding international peace, and participating in national construction. We firmly believe that this Congress, on the basis of uniting women across the country more closely and summarizing the rich experience of the women's movement, will be able to further mobilize women across the country and the people of the whole country to work together to strengthen the defense of peace in Asia and the world and ensure the completion of the motherland's planned construction.
        China People's Committee for World Peace
            April 16, 1953

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会祝贺第二次全国妇女代表大会开幕

1953-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会祝贺第二次全国妇女代表大会开幕
    【新华社十六日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会致函祝贺中国第二次全国妇女代表大会开幕。文如下：中国第二次全国妇女代表大会：值此中国第二次全国妇女代表大会开幕之际，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会谨向大会及与会全体代表致以热烈的庆贺，并预祝大会获得圆满的成功。中国妇女在支持抗美援朝、维护国际和平和参加国家建设事业中，表现了坚强不懈的斗争精神，有了卓越的贡献。我们深信：这次大会在愈益紧密团结全国妇女、总结妇女运动丰富经验的基础之上，必能更进一步地动员全国妇女和全国人民在一起，为加强保卫亚洲和世界和平、保证祖国计划建设的完成而奋斗。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
            一九五三年四月十六日
