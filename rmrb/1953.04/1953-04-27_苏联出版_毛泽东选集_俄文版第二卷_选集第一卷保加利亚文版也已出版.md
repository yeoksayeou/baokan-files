Title: Soviet Union Publishes Russian Edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Volume II; Bulgarian Edition of Volume I of "Selected Works" Also Published
Author:
Date: 1953-04-27
Page: 1
　
    Xinhua News Agency: Following the publication of Volume I of the Russian edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" in May last year, the Moscow Foreign Languages Publishing House recently published Volume II of the Russian edition, which has been warmly welcomed by Soviet readers. The publishing house divided the Chinese edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Volume II into two volumes—Volumes II and III—for the Russian edition. Volume III of the Russian edition is being prepared for printing and is expected to be released in June or July of this year.
    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 26th: According to TASS, Sofia, 25th: The Bulgarian edition of Volume I of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" has been published. This is a translation by the Bulgarian Communist Party Publishing House based on the Russian edition.

### 苏联出版“毛泽东选集”俄文版第二卷  选集第一卷保加利亚文版也已出版

1953-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联出版“毛泽东选集”俄文版第二卷
    选集第一卷保加利亚文版也已出版
    【新华社讯】莫斯科外国文书籍出版局继去年五月间发行“毛泽东选集”俄文版第一卷后，最近又出版了俄文版第二卷，受到苏联读者的热烈欢迎。出版局是把“毛泽东选集”中文版第二卷分成两卷——第二卷和第三卷用俄文出版的。俄文版第三卷正在准备付印，预计可在今年六、七月间发行。
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社索非亚二十五日讯：“毛泽东选集”第一卷保加利亚文版已经出版。这是保加利亚共产党出版局根据俄文版转译成的。
