    Our Great Motherland
    The No. 53 Factory adopted the method of "awarding prizes for problem solving," giving clear targets for rationalization proposals. This is the problem issued by the Second Workshop for creating the "Fifth Fast Airplane." Group leader Wang Chunyang (left) is handing over the answered questions to a trade union cadre.

1953-04-04

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    五三工厂采用“课题悬赏”的办法，使合理化建议有了明确的目标。这是该厂第二车间创办的“第五架快速飞机”发出的课题。小组长汪纯洋（左）正把解答了的问题交给工会干部。
