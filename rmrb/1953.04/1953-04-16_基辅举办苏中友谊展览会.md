Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Exhibition Held in Kyiv
Author:
Date: 1953-04-16
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Kyiv, April 10th) An exhibition on the friendship between the Soviet and Chinese peoples was held at the Kyiv House of Scientific Workers. The exhibits reflected the history of the Chinese people's struggle for liberation, their efforts to develop the national economy, culture and art, and the Soviet Union's selfless, fraternal assistance to the great Chinese people. The exhibition was very popular. Portraits of Comrade Stalin and Comrade Mao Zedong created by Chinese artists attracted the most attention.

### 基辅举办苏中友谊展览会

1953-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

    基辅举办苏中友谊展览会
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社基辅十日讯    ：基辅科学工作者之家举办了关于苏中两国人民友谊的展览会。展览品反映了中国人民争取解放的历史，为国民经济、文化艺术的发展而奋斗的情况，以及苏联对伟大中国人民的无私的兄弟般的援助。展览会极受欢迎。中国艺术家创作的斯大林同志和毛泽东同志的肖像最受注意。
