Title: Polish and German Newspapers Publish Premier Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1953-04-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 1st: Warsaw newspapers in Poland have published the statement of Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, on the Korean armistice negotiations. "People's Tribune" published this statement under the title: "For the End of the Korean War, For Peace."

Major newspapers in the German Democratic Republic have published the full text or excerpts of Premier Zhou's statement in prominent positions.

The Czechoslovak "Red Right Newspaper" published the full text of the letter from Marshal Kim Il-sung and General Peng Dehuai to Clark on March 30th. Other Czechoslovak newspapers also published excerpts of this letter on the 31st.

The "Austrian People's Voice" published on its front page the letter from Marshal Kim Il-sung and General Peng Dehuai to Clark, broadcast by Radio Beijing on March 29th.

### 波德报纸刊载周总理声明

1953-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    波德报纸刊载周总理声明
    【新华社布拉格一日电】波兰华沙各报刊载了我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来关于朝鲜停战谈判问题的声明。“人民论坛报”刊载这个声明时的标题是：“为了结束朝鲜战争，为了和平”。
    德意志民主共和国各主要报纸都在显著地位刊载了周总理声明的全文或摘要。
    捷克斯洛伐克“红色权利报”在三月三十日刊载了金日成元帅及彭德怀将军答复克拉克的信的全文，捷克斯洛伐克其他报纸三十一日也刊载了这封信的摘要。
    “奥地利人民之声报”在首页刊载了北京电台在三月二十九日广播的金日成元帅及彭德怀将军给克拉克的信。
