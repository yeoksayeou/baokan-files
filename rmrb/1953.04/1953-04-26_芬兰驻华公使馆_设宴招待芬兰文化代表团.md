Title: Finnish Legation in China Hosts Banquet for Finnish Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-04-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 25th:

    The Finnish Minister to China, Mr. and Mrs. Knorr, hosted a luncheon in Beijing on the 25th to entertain the Finnish cultural delegation headed by Mrs. Kekkonen.

    Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, were also invited to the event.

    Also invited were: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Qian Duansheng, Vice Presidents, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, Mei Ru'ao, Luo Longji, Council Members, Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Zhang Menggeng, Deputy Head of the China Acrobatic Troupe, and others.

    Since arriving in Beijing, the Finnish cultural delegation has visited Beihai Park and the Summer Palace, and visited Peking University and the Second Primary School Attached to Beijing Normal University on the 22nd, 23rd, 24th and 25th respectively. Xie Juezai, Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, also held a symposium for the delegation on the construction of China's New Democratic regime.

### 芬兰驻华公使馆  设宴招待芬兰文化代表团

1953-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰驻华公使馆
    设宴招待芬兰文化代表团
    【新华社二十五日讯】芬兰驻华公使克诺林夫妇于二十五日中午在北京设宴招待以吉科宁夫人为首的芬兰文化代表团到京团员。
    中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、对外贸易部副部长雷任民、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗、外交部西欧非洲司司长宦乡、交际处副处长沈平也应邀赴会。
    应邀赴会的还有：中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长周鲠生、陈翰笙、钱端升、秘书长吴茂荪、理事会理事梅汝璈、罗隆基，中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎，中国人民杂技团副团长张梦庚等。
    芬兰文化代表团到北京以后，已于二十二日、二十三日、二十四日和二十五日分别游览了北京名胜古迹北海、颐和园，参观了北京大学、北京师范大学附属第二小学。中央人民政府内务部谢觉哉部长并为代表团举行了关于中国新民主主义政权建设问题的座谈会。
