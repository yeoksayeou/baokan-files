Title: Yelogov and Former Chinese Changchun Railway Soviet Experts Depart for Home; Vice-Minister Lü Zhengcao Presents Premier Zhou's Letter of Appreciation to Yelogov et al.
Author:
Date: 1953-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, April 8th: Yelogov, the principal Soviet representative of the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee responsible for handling the transfer of the Chinese Changchun Railway (CCR), and former Vice-Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Chinese Changchun Railway Company, departed for the Soviet Union on the morning of the 8th from Harbin, after completing the handover of the CCR. Accompanying him were Vasilyev, former Chairman of the Supervisory Board of the CCR Company, and Grunichev, former Director of the CCR Administration. Also returning to the Soviet Union were 24 former Soviet experts from the CCR and their families.

　　At 7:00 PM on the 7th, the Harbin Railway Administration hosted a farewell banquet for Yelogov and others. At the banquet, Lü Zhengcao, Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, read and presented Premier Zhou Enlai's letter of appreciation from the State Council of the Central People's Government to Yelogov, Vasilyev, and Grunichev.

　　At 10:00 AM on the 8th, over 1,000 railway workers and railway school students from Harbin held a warm farewell meeting at the Harbin Railway Station. Lü Zhengcao, Vice-Minister; Yu Guangsheng, the Chinese representative of the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee; Lu Ping, Director of the Harbin Railway Administration; Qiang Xiaochu, Second Secretary of the Songjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government; Yu Lin, President of the Songjiang Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and Lü Qien, Mayor of the Harbin Municipal People's Government, attended the farewell meeting. The Soviet Consul General in Harbin, Dobashin, also went to the station to see them off.

　　Lu Ping, Qiang Xiaochu, and Yang Maolin, an advanced worker from the Harbin Railway, delivered farewell speeches at the meeting. They all expressed their gratitude to Yelogov, Vasilyev, Grunichev, and the former Soviet experts of the CCR for their tremendous assistance to China's railway construction, and expressed their determination to continue strengthening Sino-Soviet friendship and further learn from advanced Soviet experiences under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong.

　　Yelogov delivered a reply speech on behalf of the experts returning to the Soviet Union.

　　After the special train departed from Harbin Station, the well-wishers still waved goodbye to the departing train on the platform with reluctance. Yu Guangsheng, Li Mingzhe, former Vice-Chairman of the Supervisory Board of the CCR Company, Guo Lu, Deputy Director of the Harbin Railway Administration, and Song Chengde, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Harbin Railway Administration, accompanied the train to Manchuria.

### 叶洛果夫和前中长路苏联专家等启程返国  吕正操副部长转交周总理致叶洛果夫等感谢状

1953-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    叶洛果夫和前中长路苏联专家等启程返国
    吕正操副部长转交周总理致叶洛果夫等感谢状
    【新华社哈尔滨八日电】负责办理中长铁路移交事宜的中苏联合委员会苏方主要负责人、前中国长春铁路公司理事会副主席叶洛果夫，在完成中长铁路移交事宜的清理工作后，在八日上午由哈尔滨启程返苏。同行返苏的有前中长铁路公司监事会主席华西里耶夫和前中长铁路管理局局长格鲁尼切夫。同行返苏的还有前中长铁路苏联专家及其家属二十四人。
    七日下午七时，哈尔滨铁路管理局曾设宴欢送叶洛果夫等。在宴会上，中央人民政府铁道部副部长吕正操宣读并转交了中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理致叶洛果夫、华西里耶夫和格鲁尼切夫的感谢状。
    八日上午十时，哈尔滨铁路职工和铁路学校学生一千余人，在哈尔滨车站举行了热烈的欢送会。吕正操副部长，中苏联合委员会我方负责人余光生，哈尔滨铁路管理局局长陆平，中国共产党松江省委员会第二书记、松江省人民政府主席强晓初，松江省中苏友好协会会长于林，哈尔滨市人民政府市长吕其恩等参加了欢送会。苏联驻哈尔滨总领事多巴申也到车站送行。
    陆平、强晓初和哈尔滨铁路先进工作者杨茂林在欢送会上先后致欢送词，他们一致感谢叶洛果夫、华西里耶夫、格鲁尼切夫和前中长铁路的苏联专家给予我国铁路建设事业的巨大帮助，并表示在毛泽东主席领导下继续加强中苏友谊，进一步学习苏联先进经验。
    叶洛果夫代表此次返苏专家向欢送者致答词。
    专车由哈尔滨站开出后，欢送者仍依依不舍地在站台上向开去的列车招手。余光生和前中长铁路公司监事会副主席李明哲、哈尔滨铁路管理局副局长郭鲁以及哈尔滨铁路管理局政治部副主任宋诚德等随车送行到满洲里。
