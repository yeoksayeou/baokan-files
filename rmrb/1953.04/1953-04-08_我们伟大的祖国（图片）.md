    Our Great Motherland
    Farmers across the country are actively engaged in spring plowing, launching a patriotic production increase campaign, and striving for a bumper harvest this year. This is a scene of the Lu Kaisheng mutual aid team in Shangshan Township, Zhongshan County, Guangdong Province, using combined paddy fields for seedling cultivation.

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各地的农民正在积极进行春耕，展开爱国增产运动，争取今年丰收。这是广东省中山县上栅乡陆开胜互助组采用合式秧田育苗的情形。
