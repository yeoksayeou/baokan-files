Title: The US side again murders and wounds five prisoners of war

Author: 
Date: 1953-04-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The U.S. side has murdered and wounded five more of our captured personnel. According to an announcement by the U.S. side, on April 16th, in the Piggery POW camp and the Dongnae POW camp, one prisoner of war was murdered in each camp. The U.S. side lied, saying that one of them was killed by other prisoners of war, and the other "committed suicide." On April 13th, at the Geoje Island POW camp, the U.S. side opened fire on fifty prisoners of war, wounding one of them. On April 14th, at the Yongcho Island POW camp, the U.S. side again wounded one prisoner of war. On April 20th, at the Yongcho Island POW camp, the U.S. side again shot and killed one prisoner of war under the pretext of "escape."

### 美方又杀害和打伤战俘五名

1953-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　美方又杀害和打伤战俘五名
    据新华社讯：美方又杀害和打伤我方被俘人员五名。据美方宣布，四月十六日，在猪仇里战俘营和东莱战俘营各有一名战俘被谋害，美方撒谎说其中一个是被其他战俘杀害的，一个是“自杀”的。四月十三日，在巨济岛战俘营中，美方向五十名战俘开枪射击，打伤战俘一名。四月十四日，美方在龙草岛战俘营中又打伤战俘一名。四月二十日，美方在龙草岛战俘营中，又在“逃跑”的借口下打死战俘一名。
