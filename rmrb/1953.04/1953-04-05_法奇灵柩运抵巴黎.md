Title: Faji's Coffin Arrives in Paris
Author:
Date: 1953-04-05
Page: 4

　　(Xinhua News Agency, April 4th) TASS, Paris, March 3rd: Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, Etienne Fajon, François Billoux, and Raymond Guyot, representatives of the leadership of the French Communist Party, Madame Gordon, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Jules Duchaussat, Secretary of the General Confederation of Labor, representatives from various progressive social organizations, members of the National Assembly, prominent figures in science and the arts, and the working people of Paris, all gathered at the Bourget Airport in Paris on the 2nd to receive the coffin of Yves Farge, Chairman of the French National Peace Council.
　　Farge's coffin was placed in the hall of the Paris Scientific Association. Many prominent figures attended the vigil, including National Assembly member and Committee Member of the Progressive Republican Union, Sibert de Chambrain, and members of the Standing Committee of the National Peace Council, Haïdé de Lavergne and Armand Mitrani, among others.
　　The coffin was covered with wreaths.
　　"L'Humanité" published a notice from the French National Peace Council, announcing that Yves Farge would be buried in the Père Lachaise Cemetery in Paris on April 4th. The notice also stated that the Paris Police Chief, Baylot, had prohibited any processions from being held to commemorate Farge's death.

### 法奇灵柩运抵巴黎

1953-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    法奇灵柩运抵巴黎
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社巴黎三日讯：世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里，法国共产党领导机关代表艾蒂安·法戎、佛朗索瓦·皮佑和雷蒙·葛约，国际民主妇女联合会主席戈登夫人，总工会书记儒勒·杜沙，各进步社会团体代表，国民议会议员，科学界、文艺界著名人士，巴黎的劳动人民等，二日都在巴黎的布尔吉机场迎接法国全国和平理事会主席伊夫·法奇的灵柩。
    法奇的灵柩停放在巴黎科学协会的大厅里。参加守灵的有许多著名人士，其中有国民议会议员、进步共和联盟委员希伯尔·德香布兰，全国和平理事会常务委员会委员爱·达·德拉维热里和阿蒙·密特朗等。
    在灵柩上放满了花圈。
    “人道报”刊载法国全国和平理事会的公告，宣布定于四月四日在巴黎的彼·拉舍斯公墓安葬伊夫·法奇。公告并说，巴黎警察局局长贝洛已禁止为追悼法奇逝世而举行游行。
