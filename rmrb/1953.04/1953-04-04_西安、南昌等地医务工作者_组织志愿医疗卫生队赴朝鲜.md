Title: Medical Workers from Xi'an, Nanchang and Other Places Organize Volunteer Medical Teams to Go to Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-04-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd.

    Volunteer medical teams organized by medical workers from Xi'an and Nanchang, and a volunteer hygiene work team organized by health workers from Shanxi Province, went to the Korean front at the end of last month to carry out medical and health work. Before their departure, people from all walks of life in Xi'an, Nanchang, and Taiyuan held farewell meetings for them respectively.

### 西安、南昌等地医务工作者  组织志愿医疗卫生队赴朝鲜

1953-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    西安、南昌等地医务工作者
    组织志愿医疗卫生队赴朝鲜
    【新华社三日讯】西安、南昌两市医务工作者分别组织的志愿赴朝医疗队，和山西省卫生工作者组织的志愿赴朝卫生工作队，已在上月底相继前往朝鲜前线进行医疗和卫生工作。他们在临行前，西安、南昌和太原市各界人民，分别举行了欢送会。
