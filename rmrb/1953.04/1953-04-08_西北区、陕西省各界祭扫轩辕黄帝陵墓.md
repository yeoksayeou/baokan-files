Title: Northwest Region, Shaanxi Province, All Circles Pay Respects at the Mausoleum of the Yellow Emperor

Author:

Date: 1953-04-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency:
    More than 800 representatives from the Northwest Region and Shaanxi Province's Party and government leading organs and people from all walks of life went to Huangling County, Shaanxi Province, on the 5th (Qingming Festival) to pay respects at the Mausoleum of the Yellow Emperor. The memorial ceremony began solemnly at 9:00 am. Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the Northwest Administrative Committee, and Zhao Shoushan, Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government, as chief celebrants, presented wreaths and respectfully read the eulogy. After the memorial service, the chief celebrants and accompanying celebrants circled the mausoleum and paid homage to the Yellow Emperor Temple.

### 西北区、陕西省各界祭扫轩辕黄帝陵墓

1953-04-08
第3版()
专栏：

    西北区、陕西省各界祭扫轩辕黄帝陵墓
    西北区和陕西省党、政领导机关和各界人民代表八百余人，在五日（清明节）前往陕西省黄陵县祭扫轩辕黄帝陵墓。祭奠仪式在上午九时隆重开始，由主祭人西北行政委员会副主席张治中、陕西省人民政府主席赵寿山敬献花圈，恭读祭文。祭奠毕，主祭人和陪祭人绕陵一周，并晋谒轩辕黄帝庙。（新华社）
