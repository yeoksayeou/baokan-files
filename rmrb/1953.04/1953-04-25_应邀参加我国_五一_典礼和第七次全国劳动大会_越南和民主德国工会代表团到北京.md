Title: Vietnamese and East German Trade Union Delegations Arrive in Beijing to Attend China's "May Day" Celebrations and the Seventh National Labor Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-04-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 24th:

At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the trade union delegations from Vietnam and the German Democratic Republic have arrived in Beijing to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and the Seventh National Labor Congress. They were welcomed by Zhang Jing, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Textile Workers' Union, Wu Ping, Deputy Director of the Women Workers' Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department, Zhuang Yan, and others.

List of the Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation:

Head of Delegation: Trần Bảo, Chairman of the Inspection Committee of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor

Members: Trần Đại Nghĩa, Executive Member of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, National Labor Hero of Vietnam

Secretary: Nguyễn Đăng, Staff Member of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor

List of the German Democratic Republic Trade Union Delegation:

Head of Delegation: Margarete Kołmer, Secretary of the Presidium of the Free German Trade Union Federation, Member of the Presidium of the People's Chamber

Members: Martin Raab, Labor Hero, High-Speed Lathe Worker, Deputy Director of the Patents and Inventions Bureau, Member of the Senate

Translator: Ursula Rhode
