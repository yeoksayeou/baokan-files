Title: Our People Love Czechoslovak Films
Author:
Date: 1953-04-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 17th: Following the opening of the "Czechoslovak Republic Film Week" in twenty cities across our nation, eight Czechoslovak films began screening in these cities on the 15th. These films have been warmly welcomed by our people.

Ten first-run cinemas in Shanghai are simultaneously screening Czechoslovak films. As of the 14th, over 100,000 tickets had been pre-booked. During the screenings, each cinema used slides, broadcasts, and other methods to introduce the content, themes, and main characters of the films, helping audiences understand the great struggles of the Czechoslovak people through the movies. In Hangzhou, four cinemas had pre-sold over 62,000 tickets as of the 14th, and many audience members were already lining up to purchase tickets in front of the cinemas on the morning of the 15th. In Chongqing, all ten cinemas in the city had pre-sold over 227,000 tickets as of the 14th. Many factory workers have requested that film projection teams bring the exhibited films to the factories for screening. In Chengdu, in the three days before the film screenings, cinemas throughout the city had pre-sold 180,000 tickets, including 130,000 tickets booked by workers and cadres from suburban areas and government organizations. In the five cities of Shenyang, Harbin, Dalian, Changchun, and Anshan, the number of viewers on the first day of the Czechoslovak film screenings reached over 200,000. In Shenyang, nearly 600,000 tickets had been pre-booked by the day before the opening.

### 我国人民热爱捷克斯洛伐克影片

1953-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我国人民热爱捷克斯洛伐克影片
    【新华社十七日讯】“捷克斯洛伐克共和国电影周”在我国二十个城市开幕后，八部捷克斯洛伐克影片十五日开始在这些城市上映。这些影片受到我国人民热烈的欢迎。
    上海市十家首轮电影院同时放映了捷克斯洛伐克影片，到十四日为止，预订电影票的已有十万多人。各电影院在放映时，都用幻灯片、广播等方式，介绍影片的内容、主题思想和主要人物，帮助观众从电影中了解捷克斯洛伐克人民的伟大斗争。杭州市的四家电影院到十四日为止已经预售了六万二千多张电影票，在十五日的早晨，各电影院门前就有很多观众在排队购票。重庆市全市十家电影院，截至十四日止已预售出各片座券二十二万七千多张。许多工厂工人都要求电影放映队将展览的影片带到工厂去放映。成都市在电影映出的前三日，全市电影院预售出的座券已达十八万张，其中市郊工人、机关干部订座的有十三万张。沈阳、哈尔滨、旅大、长春、鞍山五个城市在捷克斯洛伐克影片映出的第一日，观众即达二十多万人。沈阳市截至开幕前一天止，预订电影票的就将近有六十万人。
