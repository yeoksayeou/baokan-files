Title: Delegation of the German Industrial Exhibition, Head of Delegation Lange, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1953-04-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 24th:

    Delegation of the German Industrial Exhibition, Head of Delegation Lange, Arrives in Beijing
    Head of the Delegation of the German Democratic Republic Industrial Exhibition, Lange, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 24th. Five members of the delegation came with him, bringing the total number of delegation members arriving in Beijing in batches to eighty.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them were: Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Chen Ming, Director of the Exhibition Department; Xie Xiao Nai, Director of the Liaison Department; and Jia Shi, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government.
    Also at the airport to welcome them were Johannes König, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the rank of Ambassador, Counselor Grutner, Commercial Counselor Dürrbeck (also Deputy Head of the Delegation of the German Democratic Republic Industrial Exhibition), and members of the Exhibition Delegation who had arrived earlier.
    Norbert, Deputy Head of the Government Commercial Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, also came to welcome them.
    The German Democratic Republic Industrial Exhibition is scheduled to open on April 27th at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace. This exhibition is the largest exhibition held abroad by the German Democratic Republic since its establishment. All the exhibits total more than 1,300 boxes, including large-scale long-arm cranes, trucks, textile machines, printing machines, sawmills, precision instruments, physics experiment equipment, electrical tools, etc. The vast majority of the exhibits are orders from our country. During the installation of the exhibits, outstanding workers from the German Democratic Republic will perform various machine operations.

### 德工业展览会代表团  朗格团长等到北京

1953-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    德工业展览会代表团
    朗格团长等到北京
    【新华社二十四日讯】德意志民主共和国工业展览会代表团团长朗格于二十四日乘飞机抵达北京，同来的有代表团团员五人，代表团前后分批到京的团员共计八十人。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎，展览部部长陈明，联络部部长谢筱乃，此外尚有中央人民政府对外贸易部第二局副局长贾石等。
    到机场欢迎的还有德意志民主共和国驻我国外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希，参赞葛鲁特纳、商务参赞杜尔伯（兼此次德意志民主共和国工业展览会代表团副团长）和先期抵京的展览会代表团团员等。
    德意志民主共和国政府商务代表团副团长诺贝尔特亦前往欢迎。
    德意志民主共和国工业展览会定于四月二十七日在北京市劳动人民文化宫开幕。这次展览会是德意志民主共和国成立以来在国外举办的规模最大的展览会。全部展览品共有一千三百多箱，其中包括大型的长臂起重机、卡车、纺织机、印刷机、锯木机、精密器械、物理实验用具、电气工具等。展览品的绝大部分都是我国的订货。在安装展览品期间，民主德国的优秀工人将进行各种机器操作表演。
