Title: Korean Railway Art Troupe Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The entire 131-member Korean Democratic People's Republic Railway Art Troupe, led by its head, Li Qingqiu, returned to Beijing by special train on the morning of the 29th from Harbin. Representatives from the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government and other units welcomed them at the station.

Xu Xinxi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in China, also went to the station to welcome them.

### 朝鲜访华铁道艺术团返京

1953-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华铁道艺术团返京
    据新华社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团全体团员一百三十一人，由团长李青求率领在二十九日上午由哈尔滨乘专车返抵北京。到车站欢迎的有中央人民政府铁道部等单位的代表。
    朝鲜驻华大使馆临时代办徐新锡也到车站欢迎。
