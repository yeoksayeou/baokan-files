Title: Tens of Thousands of Berlin Residents Sign Petitions Demanding Release of the Rosenbergs
Author:
Date: 1953-04-08
Page: 4

    Berlin, April 7 (Xinhua News Agency) - According to the German News Agency in Berlin: By March 20th, the "Committee to Save the Rosenbergs" had collected over 30,000 signatures demanding the release of the Rosenbergs at just one platform in the Friedrichstrasse train station, 20% of which were signatures from West Berlin residents. The "Committee to Save the Rosenbergs" will also hold several campaigns in the next few days dedicated to saving this innocent American patriot couple sentenced to death by the American judicial authorities.
    Over 10,000 signatures were collected in four days on Stalin Avenue and the Stalin Avenue elevated train station. Many West Berlin residents who came to Stalin Avenue to see the socialist construction also participated in the signing.

### 柏林数万人民签名  要求释放罗森堡夫妇

1953-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    柏林数万人民签名
    要求释放罗森堡夫妇
    【新华社讯】据德意志通讯社柏林讯：到三月二十日止，“拯救罗森堡夫妇委员会”单是在腓特烈大街火车站一个站台，就已征集了三万多个要求释放罗森堡夫妇的签名，其中百分之二十是西柏林居民的签名。“拯救罗森堡夫妇委员会”并将在几天内举行若干专为拯救这一对被美国司法当局判处死刑的无辜的美国爱国者的运动。
    在斯大林大街和斯大林大街高架火车站，在四天之内就征集了一万多个签名。很多到斯大林大街来参观社会主义建设的西柏林居民也参加了签名。
