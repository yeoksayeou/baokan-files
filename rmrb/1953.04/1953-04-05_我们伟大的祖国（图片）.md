Our Great Motherland
The cold casting workshop of the Central China Iron and Steel Company has strengthened democratic management, and the quality of its products has improved significantly. This workshop has therefore received bonuses and award banners. This is a train wheel cast by the workshop. The steam crane is loading these wheels onto the train, ready for transport.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    华中钢铁公司冷铸车间加强了民主管理，产品的质量有了显著提高。这个车间已因此得到了奖金和奖旗。这是该车间铸造的火车轮子。蒸气吊车正在把这些车轮装上火车，准备运走。
