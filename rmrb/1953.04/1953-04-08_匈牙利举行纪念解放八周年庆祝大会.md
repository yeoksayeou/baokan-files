Title: Hungary Holds Grand Celebration to Commemorate the Eighth Anniversary of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1953-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 7th, Budapest: The Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic, together with the Budapest City Committee of the Working People's Party and the Budapest Municipal Government, held a grand celebration in the Budapest Opera House on the evening of the 3rd to commemorate the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Hungary by the Soviet Army. The presidium of the conference consisted of Mátyás Rákosi, General Secretary of the Working People's Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic, Ernő Gerő, Deputy General Secretary of the Working People's Party and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, as well as Soviet Ambassador to Hungary, Yevgeny Kiselev, and the heads of government delegations from fraternal countries who came to participate in the eighth anniversary celebration. Dobi opened the conference. Next, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, A. Házi, spoke, first expressing, on behalf of the Hungarian people, his gratitude to the great Soviet Union and Stalin. He said: On the occasion of the eighth anniversary of our liberation, the people of our country guarantee to defend and further strengthen the friendship with the great Soviet Union. The Hungarian people will never forget that the creator and greatest promoter of this friendship is Comrade Stalin. Házi then described the great achievements of the Hungarian people in various aspects of national economic construction over the past eight years and expressed the Hungarian people's determination to build socialism and defend world peace.
    
    The conference then read out the congratulatory telegram from the Soviet Council of Ministers. The heads of the delegations from the People's Republic of China and other countries successively delivered congratulatory speeches at the meeting.
    
    A grand artistic performance was held at the end of the conference.

### 匈牙利举行纪念解放八周年庆祝大会

1953-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利举行纪念解放八周年庆祝大会
    【新华社七日讯】布达佩斯消息：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会、匈牙利人民共和国部长会议，会同劳动人民党布达佩斯市委员会和布达佩斯市政府三日晚在布达佩斯歌剧院举行苏联军队解放匈牙利八周年庆祝大会。大会主席团由劳动人民党总书记、部长会议主席马·拉科西，匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席伊·道比，劳动人民党副总书记、部长会议副主席埃·格罗，以及苏联驻匈牙利大使叶·基谢列夫和前来参加八周年庆祝典礼的各兄弟国家政府代表团团长等组成。大会首由道比致开会词。接着，部长会议副主席阿·哈济讲话，他首先代表匈牙利人民向伟大的苏联和斯大林表示感谢。他说：在我们获得解放八周年纪念日的时候，我国人民保证要保卫和进一步加强对伟大苏联的友谊。匈牙利人民永远不会忘记这种友谊的缔造者和最伟大的促进者就是斯大林同志。哈济接着叙述了匈牙利人民八年来在国民经济建设各方面的巨大成就，并表示了匈牙利人民建设社会主义和保卫世界和平的决心。
    大会随后宣读了苏联部长会议的贺电。中华人民共和国以及各国政府代表团团长相继在会上致祝词。
    大会最后举行了盛大的艺术表演会。
