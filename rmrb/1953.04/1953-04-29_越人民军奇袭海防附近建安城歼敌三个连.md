Title: Vietnamese People's Army Surprise Attack on Jian'an City Near Haiphong Annihilates Three Enemy Companies
Author:
Date: 1953-04-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: According to the Vietnam News Agency on April 25th: The Vietnamese People's Army launched a surprise attack on Jian'an city near Haiphong at 11:00 PM on April 20th. After a 45-minute battle, the People's Army completely annihilated the enemy's 43rd Assault Company in Dengsong, and also removed another stronghold, killing and capturing approximately two companies of enemy troops, including one captain. The People's Army also stormed into the puppet provincial government of Jian'an, killing the puppet provincial governor and puppet deputy provincial governor.
    The People's Army destroyed a power plant, destroyed four artillery pieces and twenty 105mm cannons, ten tanks, and approximately 300 motorcycles, and burned down a gasoline depot and an ammunition depot with eighty houses. The flames from the ammunition depot and gasoline depot did not extinguish until the afternoon of April 21st.
    General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, has sent a telegram to the troops participating in this raid to express his congratulations.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The French invading authorities in Vietnam and the Bao Dai puppet regime's intensified conscription measures to expand the puppet army have been met with fierce opposition from the people in the enemy-occupied areas.
    According to the Vietnam News Agency: On April 3rd, 6,000 people in Nha Trang City in central Vietnam held a demonstration in front of the puppet city government to protest against forced conscription. The entire city held sympathy strikes, school boycotts, and market strikes, paralyzing all activities in the city.
    On April 10th, 1,600 young people who were forcibly arrested and held in the Khanh Hoa concentration camp resolutely refused to accept enemy uniforms.

### 越人民军奇袭海防附近建安城歼敌三个连

1953-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    越人民军奇袭海防附近建安城歼敌三个连
    【新华社二十七日讯】据越南通讯社二十五日讯：越南人民军在四月二十日晚间十一时奇袭海防附近的建安城。经过四十五分钟的战斗，人民军全歼在登松的敌军第四十三突击连，并拔除另一个据点，又打死和俘掳敌军约两连人，其中有一个上尉。人民军还冲入建安的伪省府，打死伪省长和伪副省长。
    人民军毁坏了一座发电厂，击毁四门大炮和二十门一百零五公厘的大炮、十辆坦克、约三百辆摩托车，烧毁一座汽油库和一座有着八十间房屋的弹药库。弹药库和汽油库的火苗一直到四月二十一日的下午才熄灭。
    越南人民军总司令武元甲将军已打电报给参加这次突击的部队表示祝贺。
    【新华社二十七日讯】法国侵越当局和保大傀儡政权加紧征兵以便扩充伪军的措施，遭到敌占区人民的激烈反对。
    越南通讯社的消息说：四月三日，中越南部芽庄市有六千人在伪市府前面举行示威，反对强迫征兵。全市举行同情性罢工、罢课、罢市，使城市的全部活动陷于瘫痪。
    四月十日，被强迫抓去和关在庆和集中营中的一千六百个青年，坚决拒绝接受敌人的军服。
