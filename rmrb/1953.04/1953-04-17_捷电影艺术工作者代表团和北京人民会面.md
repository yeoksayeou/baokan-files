Title: Czechoslovak Film Artists Delegation Meets with People of Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The Czechoslovak Republic Film Artists Delegation met with students, military personnel, and suburban workers of Beijing on the 15th.

From 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM, the head of the delegation, Mr. Fabela (writer, vice-chairman of the committee of the "Barrandov" National Film Studio), members Mr. Vlček (director, Stalin Prize winner), Mr. Benjík (actor, National Prize winner), Ms. Hegreková (actress, National Prize winner, played the female lead in "The New Soldier Rises"), and Mr. Šafář (Lieutenant Colonel, Director of the Army Film Studio) successively met with students at the Capital Cinema and the Dahua Cinema. When the distinguished guests arrived at the Capital Cinema, a group of young students holding flowers rushed forward to present them with flowers and warmly shook hands and embraced them. The distinguished guests also received an equally warm welcome at the Dahua Cinema.

The distinguished guests were also warmly welcomed at the club of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission. At the welcoming ceremony, Chen Yi, Minister of the Culture Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission, delivered a welcome speech. Fabela and Šafář both spoke. The Culture Department of the General Political Department also presented the distinguished guests with a colorful Hunan embroidery, brocade, "Liberation Army Literature," and other gifts.

In the evening, Vlček, Hegreková, and Šafář gathered with teachers and students of Tsinghua University at Tsinghua University. Fabela, Benjík, and Kanera gathered with workers at the Shijingshan Power Plant.

More than 1,600 workers at the Shijingshan Power Plant held a grand welcome meeting. The workers all took out autograph books to ask the representatives to sign their names. A group of children of the employees put their red scarves around the necks of the three representatives. The workers also presented the distinguished guests with many Beijing handicrafts and plaster casts of Beijing landscape scenes.

According to Xinhua News Agency: The Czechoslovak Republic Film Artists Delegation left Beijing at 11:00 AM on the 16th to visit Tianjin and other places.

### 捷电影艺术工作者代表团和北京人民会面

1953-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    捷电影艺术工作者代表团和北京人民会面
    【新华社十六日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国电影艺术工作者代表团十五日和北京市学生、部队及市郊工人会面。
    下午四时至六时，团长法贝拉（作家，“巴兰道夫”国家电影制片厂委员会副主席）、团员伏尔契克（导演、斯大林奖金获得者）、班吉克（演员、国家奖金获得者）、赫格里柯娃（演员、国家奖金获得者、“新战士站起来”女主角的扮演者）和沙夫谢尔（中校、陆军电影制片厂厂长）先后到首都电影院及大华电影院和广大学生会面。贵宾们到首都电影院的时候，一下汽车，一群手持鲜花的少年学生簇拥上前向他们献花并热烈握手拥抱。贵宾们在大华电影院也受到了同样热烈的欢迎。
    贵宾们又在中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会俱乐部，受到部队热烈的欢迎。在欢迎会上，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂致欢迎词，法贝拉和沙夫谢尔都讲了话。总政治部文化部并赠给贵宾们一幅五彩湘绣和锦缎、“解放军文艺”等礼物。
    晚上，伏尔契克、赫格里柯娃、沙夫谢尔到清华大学和清华师生欢聚。法贝拉、班吉克、卡湼拉到石景山发电厂和工人欢聚。
    石景山发电厂一千六百多工人举行了盛大的欢迎会。工人们都拿出纪念册来请代表们签名。一群职工子弟将自己的红领巾套在三位代表的颈子上。工人们并赠送了许多北京手工艺品及石膏制的北京风景片给贵宾们。
    据新华社讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国电影艺术工作者代表团十六日上午十一时离京到天津等地访问。
