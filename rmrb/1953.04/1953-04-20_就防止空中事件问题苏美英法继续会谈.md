Title: USSR, US, UK, and France Continue Talks on Preventing Aerial Incidents
Author:
Date: 1953-04-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 18th: The second meeting of representatives of the military authorities of the United States, Britain, France, and the Soviet Union was held on April 17th at the U.S. military headquarters in Berlin to discuss measures to ensure the safety of flights in the air corridors between Berlin and West Germany and to prevent aerial incidents.

    The U.S. representative at the meeting was Air Force Major General V.S. Stone, the British representative was Air Force Major General G. Edwards Jones, the French representative was Air Force Major General Jouarre, and the Soviet representative was Air Force Lieutenant General I.D. Podgorny.

    The meeting continued to discuss the proposals put forward by the Soviet Union on April 7, 1953.

    The next meeting is scheduled to be held at 3:00 p.m. on April 22nd at the British military government in the British occupation zone of Berlin.

### 就防止空中事件问题苏美英法继续会谈

1953-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    就防止空中事件问题苏美英法继续会谈
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社柏林十八日讯：美国、英国、法国和苏联军事当局代表第二次会议于四月十七日在柏林美军总部举行，讨论保证柏林与西德之间空中走廊飞行安全及防止空中事件的措施问题。
    参加会议的美国代表是维·斯·斯通空军少将、英国代表是格·爱德华兹·琼斯空军少将、法国代表是茹阿尔空军少将、苏联代表是伊·德·波德戈尔尼空军中将。
    会议继续讨论苏联在一九五三年四月七日所提出的建议。
    下次会议定于四月二十二日下午三时在柏林英占区的英国军政府举行。
