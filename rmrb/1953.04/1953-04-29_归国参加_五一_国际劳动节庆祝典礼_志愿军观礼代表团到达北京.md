Title: Volunteer Army Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Return Home and Participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day Celebration

Author:

Date: 1953-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th—The Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation of 159 people, led by head Yuan Shengping, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 28th to return home and attend the "May Day" celebration.

Those who greeted them at the station included: Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea; Standing Committee members Situ Meitang, Li Siguang, Shao Lizi, Ma Xulun, Ma Yinchu, Xu Baoju, Liang Xi, Peng Zemin, Luo Longji; Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea; Wu Han, Vice Chairman; and representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling more than 1,200 people.

Also present at the station to welcome them were Xu Xingxi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and other embassy staff.

Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, delivered a brief welcoming speech at the station. He praised the returning Volunteer Army representatives, saying, "You represent victory, you represent glory, you represent the great patriotism and internationalism of the Chinese people." After briefly reporting on the great victories achieved by the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army fighting side by side and their significance, he expressed the hope that the Volunteer Army representatives would take advantage of their return to the motherland to report on the glorious achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to the people of the motherland, inspire the enthusiasm of the people of the motherland for production and construction and support for the front lines, and let the people of the motherland implement the great and heroic spirit of the Volunteer Army in various tasks.

Next, Yuan Shengping, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army "May Day" Homebound Observation Delegation and Deputy Minister of the Cadre Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, delivered a reply. He first expressed his heartfelt gratitude and high respect to the people of all walks of life in the capital and the people of the whole country on behalf of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. After describing the brilliant achievements made by the people of the motherland in construction and in all aspects over the past three years, which gave the Volunteer Army great encouragement, he said: "This time, we will not only report to the people of the motherland on the victory in the Korean War, but also bring back to the Korean front the brilliant achievements of the motherland's economic construction front, the spirit of the working brothers loving the motherland, and the situation of the people of the motherland supporting the struggle to resist US aggression and aid Korea."

### 归国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝典礼  志愿军观礼代表团到达北京

1953-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    归国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝典礼
    志愿军观礼代表团到达北京
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团一行一百五十九人，在团长袁升平率领下，于二十八日下午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站迎接的有：中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通，常务委员司徒美堂、李四光、邵力子、马叙伦、马寅初、许宝驹、梁希、彭泽民、罗隆基，北京市抗美援朝分会主席张奚若、副主席吴晗，及各民主党派、各人民团体代表等共一千二百多人。
    到车站欢迎的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办徐兴锡和大使馆馆员等多人。
    中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通在车站上致了简短的欢迎词。他以“你们代表着胜利，代表着光荣，代表着中国人民的伟大的爱国主义与国际主义精神”来赞扬全体志愿军归国代表们。他在扼要报告了中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军并肩作战以来所取得的伟大胜利和它的意义后，希望志愿军代表们趁着回国的机会，多多地向祖国人民报道中国人民志愿军的光辉业迹，鼓舞祖国人民生产建设和支援前线的热情，让祖国人民把志愿军伟大而英勇的精神贯彻到各种工作中去。
    接着，中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团团长、中国人民志愿军干部部副部长袁升平致答词。他首先代表中国人民志愿军向首都各界人民及全国人民致以衷心的感谢和崇高的敬意。他在叙述祖国人民在三年来建设上及各方面所取得的光辉成就给予志愿军以莫大的鼓舞后说：“我们这次不仅向祖国人民报告朝鲜战争的胜利情况，而且要把祖国经济建设战线上的光辉成就，工人兄弟热爱祖国的精神，和祖国人民支援抗美援朝斗争的情况带回朝鲜前线去。”
