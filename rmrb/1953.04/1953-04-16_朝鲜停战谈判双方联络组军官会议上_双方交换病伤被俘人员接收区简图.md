Title: At the Liaison Group Officers Meeting of the Korean Armistice Negotiations, Both Sides Exchange Maps of the Sick and Wounded Prisoner of War Reception Areas
Author: 
Date: 1953-04-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, April 15th. The Korean Armistice Negotiations' Korean-Chinese delegation issued the following communiqué on April 15th:
    At the meeting of the Liaison Group officers of both sides held on April 15, both sides exchanged maps of the sick and wounded prisoner of war reception areas established by each side within the Panmunjom meeting area, and provided explanations of the maps to each other. Following the departure of the first group of sick and wounded prisoners of war held by our side from our Tianmo prisoner of war camp on April 14, the second group of sick and wounded prisoners of war held by our side also departed from our Manpo prisoner of war camp and Biktong prisoner of war camp on April 15.

### 朝鲜停战谈判双方联络组军官会议上  双方交换病伤被俘人员接收区简图

1953-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判双方联络组军官会议上
    双方交换病伤被俘人员接收区简图
    【新华社开城十五日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团四月十五日发表公报如下：
    四月十五日举行的双方联络组军官会议上，双方交换了各方在板门店会场区内建立的病伤被俘人员接收区的简图，并互相就简图作了若干说明。继我方收容下的病伤被俘人员的第一批于四月十四日自我方天摩战俘营启程之后，我方收容下的病伤被俘人员的第二批也在四月十五日分别自我方满浦战俘营及碧潼战俘营启程。
