Title: London Peace Committee Meets to Discuss Strengthening the Peace Movement
Author:
Date: 1953-04-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, 12th: The London Peace Committee convened a meeting on April 11 to discuss the work and scale of strengthening and expanding the peace movement. Nearly 200 people attended the meeting, representing trade union district committees and branches, trade union councils, cooperative societies, and local peace committees.

    Gordon Schaffer, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the British Peace Committee, said that the British peace movement has been greatly strengthened. The struggle against arming West Germany and demanding the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact is now receiving extremely broad support. In the discussion, representatives emphasized that it is necessary to strengthen activities for the struggle for peace. Many Labour Party members spoke out to condemn the Labour Party leadership's attempts to restrict ordinary members from participating in the work of the peace struggle. A representative of the Federation of Malayan Democratic Youth and Students in Britain called for an end to the wars in Korea, Vietnam, and Malaya, the repeal of the illegal economic blockade against the People's Republic of China, and the establishment of the broadest trade relations between all countries.

    Professor Bernal, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, pointed out, when speaking about the strength and scale of the world peace movement, that this movement is "a political force for peace that has never existed before." He emphasized the great significance of the peace proposals from the Korean and Chinese side.

    Professor Bernal pointed out that it is necessary to develop broad trade between Western countries and the Soviet Union.

    The meeting adopted a letter to the citizens of London, calling for strengthening the struggle for peace and supporting the Peace Action Congress organized by the British Peace Committee scheduled to be held in Manchester on May 17.
    [Our Newspaper] Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, sent a telegram to the London Peace Committee of Britain on April 10, congratulating the London Peace Committee on the convening of the meeting. The telegram said: "We Chinese people love peace. At the Vienna World People's Peace Congress, we joined the people of the world in calling for 'Stop Existing Wars.' At present, the peace movement calling for an end to the Korean War and easing world tensions has developed increasingly widely throughout the world. We believe that the London Peace Conference will promote the peace movement of London and the British people and further expand the victorious results of the World People's Peace Congress."

### 伦敦和平委员会开会讨论加强和平运动

1953-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    伦敦和平委员会开会讨论加强和平运动
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社伦敦十二日讯：伦敦和平委员会于四月十一日召开会议，讨论加强和扩大和平运动的工作和规模的问题。出席会议的将近二百人，他们代表着工会区委员会及支会，工会理事会，合作社公会和地方和平委员会。
    英国和平委员会执行委员会主席戈登·夏菲尔说，英国和平运动已经大为加强了。反对武装西德和要求缔结五大国和平公约的斗争，现在受到了极广泛的支持。在讨论中代表们强调，必须加强为和平而斗争的活动。许多工党党员发言谴责工党领导方面企图限制普通党员参加和平斗争的工作。留英马来亚民主青年与学生联合会的一个代表呼吁停止朝鲜、越南和马来亚的战争，撤销对中华人民共和国的非法的经济封锁和建立一切国家间最广泛的贸易关系。
    世界人民和平大会常设委员会副主席贝尔纳教授讲到世界和平运动的力量和规模时指出：这个运动是“一个从来没有过的争取和平的政治力量”。他着重指出朝中方面的和平建议的重大意义。
    贝尔纳教授指出，必须发展西方国家与苏联之间的广泛贸易。
    会议通过告伦敦市民书，号召加强和平斗争和支持英国和平委员会组织的定五月十七日在曼彻斯特召开的争取和平行动大会。
    【本报讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会主席张奚若，于四月十日致电英国伦敦和平委员会，祝贺伦敦和平委员会召开的会议，电文说：“我们中国人民是热爱和平的，在维也纳世界人民和平大会上，早就和世界人民一道呼吁着‘停止现有战争’。目前要求结束朝鲜战争，缓和世界紧张局势的和平运动已在全世界范围内日益广泛地发展起来了，我们相信：伦敦和平大会一定会推进伦敦与英国人民的和平运动并进一步扩大世界人民和平大会的胜利成果。”
