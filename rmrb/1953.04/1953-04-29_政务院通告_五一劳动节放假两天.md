Title: Government Administration Council Notice: Two Days Off for May Day

Author:
Date: 1953-04-29
Page: 1

    Government Administration Council Notice
    Two Days Off for May Day
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Central People's Government Administration Council issued a notice on April 28th, the original text of which is as follows:
    It is hereby stipulated that all agencies, organizations, schools, and factories in the capital shall have two days off for the "May 1st" Labor Day this year, on May 1st and May 2nd; May 3rd (Sunday) will be business as usual, without any days off. It is hoped that this will be strictly followed and implemented.

### 政务院通告  五一劳动节放假两天

1953-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院通告
    五一劳动节放假两天
    【本报讯】中央人民政府政务院于四月二十八日发出通告，原文如下：
    兹规定首都各机关、团体、学校、工厂在本年“五一”劳动节，五月一日和五月二日，放假两天；五月三日（星期日）一律照常工作，不放假。望即遵照实行。
