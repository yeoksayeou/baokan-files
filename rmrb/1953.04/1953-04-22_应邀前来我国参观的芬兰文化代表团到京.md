Title: Finnish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing for Visit
Author:
Date: 1953-04-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 21st—The Finnish cultural delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of April 21st.

The list of the delegation is as follows:
Head of the delegation: Mrs. Sylvi Kekkonen (writer, wife of the Finnish Prime Minister)
Deputy Head: Mauri Luma (Member of Parliament, journalist, Vice Chairman of the Finland-Soviet Union Friendship Association)
Member: Kusta Vilkuna (Professor, yet to arrive)
Heikki Savolainen (Doctor)
Allan Ehvenpää (Engineer, Chief Factory Inspector of the Finnish Ministry of Social Affairs)
Ritva Arvelo (Actress)
Aira Koulula (Writer, Principal of Workers' College, Chairman of the Turku Branch of the Finland-China Association)
Anneli Laurikkala (Secretary General of the Finland-China Association)
Taimi Jokinen (Shoe worker)
Pentti Haanpää (Writer)
Matti Kuusensaari (Writer, journalist)
Matti Rädio (Composer)
Aimo Kanerva (Artist)
Kalevi Kilpi (Head of the Finnish Broadcasting Company, yet to arrive)
Tauno Kohonen (Representative for consultation at a mechanical factory)
Matti Ehvenpää (Agricultural instructor)
Georg Backlund (Journalist, translator for the delegation, yet to arrive)

Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Vice Presidents Chen Hansheng and Qian Duansheng, Secretary-General Wu Maosun, Zhou Yang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Secretary Sha Ke夫, Xu Guangping, Vice President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Fang Ming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, Ji Chaoding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, writer Cao Yu, film worker Yu Lan, and Zhang Menggeng, Deputy Head of the Chinese People's Acrobatic Troupe who had previously visited Finland, were at the airport to welcome them.

Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations Affairs of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, and Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, were also at the airport to welcome them.

Finnish Minister to China, Mr. and Mrs. Knorring, and embassy staff were also present at the airport to welcome them. (Attached picture)

Mrs. Kekkonen, head of the Finnish cultural delegation, poses for a photo after receiving flowers from Beijing children after disembarking from the plane. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Qingrui.

### 应邀前来我国参观的芬兰文化代表团到京

1953-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀前来我国参观的芬兰文化代表团到京
    【新华社二十一日讯】应中国人民外交学会邀请前来我国参观的芬兰文化代表团于四月二十一日下午乘飞机抵达北京。
    代表团名单如下：
    团长茜尔薇·吉科宁夫人（作家，芬兰总理夫人）
    副团长毛里·卢玛（国会议员、新闻工作者、芬苏友好协会副主席）
    团员库斯塔·维库那（教授，尚未到京）
    海基·沙伏拉宁（医生）
    艾伦·叶文佩（工程师、芬兰社会事务部工厂督察主任）
    里特娃·亚维罗夫人（电影演员）
    爱拉·柯鲁拉夫人（作家、工人大学校长、芬中协会土尔库分会主席）
    安·劳里凯南夫人（芬中协会秘书长）
    台米·约基宁女士（制鞋工人）
    潘蒂·韩培（作家）
    马蒂·库仁沙里（作家、新闻工作者）
    马蒂·拉狄奥（作曲家）
    爱摩·卡涅瓦（艺术家）
    卡列维·基尔皮（芬兰广播公司处长，尚未到京）
    陶诺·柯霍宁（机械工厂协商代表）
    马蒂·叶文佩（农业指导员）
    格奥尔基·巴克隆（新闻工作者、代表团翻译，尚未到京）
    中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长陈翰笙、钱端升，秘书长吴茂荪，中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席周扬，秘书长沙可夫，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长方明，中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎，作家曹禺，电影工作者于蓝，前赴芬兰中国人民杂技团副团长张梦庚等到机场欢迎。
    中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局副局长陈忠经和外交部交际处副处长沈平也到机场欢迎。
    前往机场欢迎的还有芬兰驻华公使克诺林夫妇及使馆人员。（附图片）
