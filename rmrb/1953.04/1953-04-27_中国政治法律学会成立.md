Title: Establishment of the China Political Science and Law Society
Author: 
Date: 1953-04-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency
    The China Political Science and Law Society held its inaugural meeting in Beijing on the 22nd. The meeting elected Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Xie Juezai, Shi Liang, Xu Deheng, Zhang Zhirang, Qian Duansheng, Zhang Su, Wu Defeng, Ma Xiwu and other twenty people to the presidium. Dong Biwu, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the China Political Science and Law Society, delivered the opening speech. Then Lin Boqu, Ke Bonian, Ma Xiwu and Qian Duansheng spoke successively. Soviet legal expert Sudarikov was also invited to speak. After the meeting adopted the charter and declaration of establishment of the China Political Science and Law Society, Dong Biwu was elected as the Chairman of the China Political Science and Law Society, Shen Junru, Xie Juezai, Wang Kunlun, Ke Bonian, Zhang Zhirang, Qian Duansheng as Vice Chairmen, and Shi Liang, Gao Chongmin, Xu Deheng, Wang Zhixiang, Fei Xiaotong, Zhou Gengsheng, Wu Defeng, Ma Xiwu, Zhang Shushi, Dong Luan, Wei Ming and other thirty-eight people as directors.

### 中国政治法律学会成立

1953-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中国政治法律学会成立
    【新华社讯】中国政治法律学会二十二日在北京举行成立大会。大会选出林伯渠、董必武、谢觉哉、史良、许德珩、张志让、钱端升、张苏、吴德峰、马锡五等二十人为主席团。中国政治法律学会筹备会主席董必武致开幕词。接着林伯渠、柯柏年、马锡五和钱端升相继讲话。苏联法律专家苏达里可夫也应邀讲话。会议在通过中国政治法律学会章程和成立宣言后，选出董必武为中国政治法律学会主席，沈钧儒、谢觉哉、王昆仑、柯柏年、张志让、钱端升为副主席，史良、高崇民、许德珩、王之相、费孝通、周鲠生、吴德峰、马锡五、张曙时、董鲁安、魏明等三十八人为理事。
