Title: Soong Ching Ling Sends Condolences to the French Peace Council on the Death of Yves Farge; Farge's Coffin Transported from Tbilisi to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-04-03
Page: 4

    Soong Ching Ling Sends Condolences to the French Peace Council on the Death of Yves Farge
    Farge's Coffin Transported from Tbilisi to Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 2nd: Soong Ching Ling sent a telegram to the French National Peace Council to express condolences on the death of Yves Farge. The telegram reads as follows: To the French National Peace Council:
    The unfortunate death of Yves Farge, winner of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For the Promotion of Peace Among Nations," member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, and Chairman of the French National Peace Council, is a major loss to the cause of defending world peace. Together with the peace-loving people of Asia and the Pacific region, I extend my deepest sorrow and condolences to the French National Peace Council and the French people. Yves Farge's tireless spirit in striving for lasting world peace will inspire the advancement of world peace defenders. The noble cause of opposing war and defending peace must triumph throughout the world.
                                     Soong Ching Ling
                                     April 2nd
    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: According to TASS, Moscow, 1st: The coffin of Yves Farge, Chairman of the French Peace Council and winner of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For the Promotion of Peace Among Nations," was transported by plane from Tbilisi to Moscow on April 1st. Representatives of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For the Promotion of Peace Among Nations" Committee, the Soviet Peace Committee, the Soviet Trade Unions, the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, and the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, as well as renowned scientists, artists, other public representatives of the Soviet Union, and members of the French postal and telecommunications workers delegation currently visiting Moscow, were all at the airport to pay their final respects to this person who dedicated all his strength to defending and strengthening the cause of world peace.

### 宋庆龄致电法国和平理事会吊唁法奇逝世  法奇灵柩从第比利斯运到莫斯科

1953-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄致电法国和平理事会吊唁法奇逝世
    法奇灵柩从第比利斯运到莫斯科
    【新华社上海二日电】宋庆龄致电法国全国和平理事会吊唁伊夫·法奇逝世。电文如下：法国全国和平理事会：
    “加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金获得者、世界和平理事会常务委员会委员及法国全国和平理事会主席伊夫·法奇的不幸逝世，是保卫世界和平事业的重大损失，我和亚洲及太平洋区域各国爱好和平人民一道，谨向法国全国和平理事会及法国人民表示深切的哀痛与悼唁。伊夫·法奇为争取实现世界持久和平而斗争的不懈精神，将鼓舞世界和平保卫者的前进，反对战争保卫和平的崇高事业一定要在全世界胜利。
                                     宋庆龄                 
                                     四月二日
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科一日讯：法国和平理事会主席、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人伊夫·法奇的灵柩已经在四月一日由飞机从第比利斯运到莫斯科。“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会、苏联拥护和平委员会、苏联工会、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会及苏联青年反法西斯委员会的代表，著名的科学家、艺术家、苏联其他公众代表以及目前正在访问莫斯科的法国邮电工作者代表团人员，都到机场向这个把自己一切力量都贡献给保卫和加强世界和平事业的人致最后的敬意。
