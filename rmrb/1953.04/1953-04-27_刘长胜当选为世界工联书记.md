Title: Liu Changsheng Elected Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-04-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, April 25th: According to TASS Vienna on April 24th: The Executive Bureau meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions, recently held in Vienna, unanimously elected Liu Changsheng as Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, based on the recommendation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 刘长胜当选为世界工联书记

1953-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    刘长胜当选为世界工联书记
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社维也纳二十四日讯：最近在维也纳举行的世界工会联合会执行局会议，根据中华全国总工会的建议，一致选举刘长胜为世界工会联合会书记。
