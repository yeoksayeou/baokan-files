Cultural Briefs

    The General Political Department Cultural Troupe of the Military Commission to Perform in Changchun and Other Places from Shenyang
    The Cultural Troupe of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission left Shenyang on the 20th to perform in Changchun and other places. Since arriving in Shenyang on the 3rd, the cultural troupe has performed 29 wonderful shows including chorus, solo, dance, folk art, acrobatics, and Peking opera. In order to meet the needs of the people of all circles in Shenyang, it held two open-air performances on the 18th and 19th. During its performance in Shenyang, the cultural troupe received and comforted more than 147,000 people, including wounded and sick soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Soviet experts, cadres of government agencies, workers, and students. The performance of the cultural troupe was warmly welcomed by the audience. On the evening of the 19th, the Political Department of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Shenyang Municipal People's Government held a joint farewell party for the cultural troupe.
    The second branch of the cultural troupe left Shenyang on the 16th in advance to perform in Harbin.

    Tianjin Petroleum Industry School to Send Students to Yumen Oil Mine for Internship
    In order to implement the teaching policy of combining theory with practice, the Tianjin Petroleum Industry School will send 100 students to the Yumen Oil Mine for internship this summer vacation. The students of the school have already established close ties with the members of a petroleum exploration team at a construction site in the Northwest. There are currently more than 500 students in Tianjin Petroleum Industry School. The school has three laboratories including chemistry and electrical engineering, and a practice factory. The school's teachers have formed eight teaching and research groups, and are systematically studying advanced Soviet experiences to improve teaching.

### 文化简讯

1953-04-23
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    文化简讯
    军委总政文工团由沈阳去长春等地演出
    中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文艺工作团二十日离开沈阳去长春等地演出。文艺工作团自三日到达沈阳后，共演出了合唱、独唱、舞蹈、曲艺、杂技、京剧等精彩节目二十九场，为了满足沈阳各界人民的要求，又在十八、十九两日举行了两次露天演出。文艺工作团在沈阳演出期间共招待和慰问了中国人民志愿军伤病员、中国人民解放军部队指挥员战斗员、苏联专家、机关干部、工人、学生等十四万七千多人。文艺工作团的演出受到观众的热烈欢迎。十九日晚，中国人民解放军东北军区政治部和沈阳市人民政府举行联欢晚会，欢送文艺工作团。
    文艺工作团第二分团已提前在十六日离开沈阳去哈尔滨演出。
    天津石油工业学校将派学生去玉门油矿实习
    天津石油工业学校为了贯彻理论联系实际的教学方针，今年暑假将派学生一百人到玉门油矿实习。该校学生已经和西北某工地一个石油勘探队的队员们建立了密切的联系。天津石油工业学校现有学生五百多人。学校设有化学、电机等三个实验馆和一个实习工厂。该校教师共成立了八个教研组，正有系统地学习苏联先进经验，改进教学。
