Title: Central-South Military Region and Fourth Field Army Hold Meeting of Representatives of Coastal and Border Defense Troops
Author:
Date: 1953-04-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 18th: The Central-South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held the first session of the Representatives of Coastal and Border Defense Troops in early April. More than 600 people attended the meeting, most of whom were representatives of meritorious personnel and model units.

During the meeting, Tan Zheng, the third political commissar of the Central-South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army, made a report to all representatives, calling on the representatives to further strengthen coastal and border defense work and safeguard the country's construction and the people's happy life. Many representatives of meritorious personnel and model units also made typical speeches, exchanging experiences in safeguarding coastal and border defense work.

During the meeting, the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission sent a telegram encouraging the representatives, hoping that they would continue to work with the commanders and fighters of the entire army to make the country's coastal and border defenses impregnable and establish greater feats for the people of the country. Commanders and fighters of various units of the Central-South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army wrote letters to pay tribute to the representatives of the coastal and border defense troops. The South China Sub-Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, and other party and government organs, schools, and mass organizations presented a large number of consolation gifts, congratulatory letters, and banners; female students, holding flowers, went in groups to the representatives' residences to present flowers and pay tribute.

During the meeting, the Central-South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army awarded dozens of banners and many prizes to model units and individuals; and entrusted the representatives to bring a large number of cultural and recreational equipment to the islands far from the mainland and to the mountains on the border.

During the meeting, the representatives solemnly expressed their determination to turn the country's coastal and border defenses into an iron wall. Letters of determination written by individual and unit representatives, as well as records of the representatives' determination at the symposium, were sent to the conference secretariat, requesting that they be forwarded to Chairman Mao. Maritime hero Lu Xiangyun said: "We will definitely build the island into a fortress on the country's ocean, always vigilant against the enemy's aggressive conspiracies. If the enemy dares to invade, we will resolutely eliminate it in the ocean!" Combat hero Bao Wenting said: "We are the defenders of the country, and we must be ready at all times to fight to the end to defend the country, defend the Far East, and defend world peace!"

### 中南军区、第四野战军举行海防、边防部队代表会议

1953-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中南军区、第四野战军举行海防、边防部队代表会议
    【新华社广州十八日电】中国人民解放军中南军区、第四野战军于四月上旬举行第一届海防、边防部队代表会议。出席这次会议的有六百多人，绝大部分是功臣和模范单位的代表。
    在会议期间，中南军区、第四野战军第三政治委员谭政向全体代表作报告，号召到会代表进一步加强海防、边防守备工作，保卫祖国建设和人民的幸福生活。许多功臣和模范单位代表也作了典型发言，交流了保卫海防、边防工作的经验。
    在会议期间，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部曾来电勉励到会代表，希望他们继续和全军指挥员、战斗员一道，共同努力，使祖国的海防、边防固若金汤，为祖国人民树立更大的功勋。中南军区、第四野战军各部队指挥员战斗员，纷纷写信向海防、边防部队代表致敬。中共中央华南分局、广东省人民政府、广州市人民政府等党、政机关和学校、群众团体赠送了大批慰问品、贺信和锦旗；女学生们拿着鲜花，成群结队地到代表驻地向代表们献花致敬。
    在会议期间，中南军区、第四野战军把数十面锦旗和许多奖品，奖给了模范单位和个人；并把大批文娱器材，委托各代表带到远离祖国大陆的海岛上去，带到边境线上的丛山中去。
    在会议期间，代表们严肃地表示了把祖国的海防、边防变成铜墙铁壁的决心。个人和单位代表写的决心书，以及座谈会上代表们表示决心的记录，纷纷送到会议秘书处，请求转给毛主席。海上英雄鲁湘云说：“我们一定把海岛建成祖国海洋上的堡垒，时刻警惕着敌人的侵略阴谋，如果敌人敢来侵犯，就坚决把它消灭在海洋里！”战斗英雄包文廷说：“我们是祖国的保卫者，一定随时准备着，为保卫祖国、保卫远东与世界和平而奋斗到底！”
