Title: Japanese Communist Party Central Steering Department Issues May Day Slogans: Oppose the Revival of Japanese Militarism, Demand an Immediate End to the Korean War
Author:
Date: 1953-04-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: According to Japan's "Akahata" newspaper, the Central Steering Department of the Japanese Communist Party has issued slogans celebrating the 1953 May Day Labor Day. Excerpts are as follows:
    Smash the slave wage system of hierarchical ranking. Eight-hour workday with wages sufficient for living. Establish a minimum wage system.
    Oppose "industrial rationalization" serving the war, oppose intensified labor, wage cuts, and layoffs.
    Abolish the "Subversive Activities Prevention Law." No prohibition of general strikes. Oppose the malicious revision of the Labor Law and the Police Law.
    Oppose heavy taxes levied for war. Reduce transportation costs, electricity fees, and gas fees. Oppose inflation and demand lower prices.
    Oppose the education bill for the revival of militarism, implement free compulsory education.
    Do not manufacture or transport military supplies, expand peaceful industries without restriction. Save small and medium-sized enterprises.
    Engage in free exchanges with all countries, promote trade with China and the Soviet Union.
    Oppose the revival of militarism, oppose the malicious revision of the constitution, oppose rearmament and conscription.
    All foreign troops must withdraw immediately.
    Abolish the treaties of national betrayal and the "Administrative Agreement," immediately conclude a comprehensive peace treaty.
    Oppose sending troops to Korea. Dissolve the "Security Force" and the "Maritime Safety Force."
    Immediately stop the Korean War. All foreign troops must immediately withdraw from Korea, Taiwan, and all of Asia. Let Asians handle Asian affairs themselves.
    Oppose the use of bacteriological weapons, atomic bombs, and all other weapons of mass slaughter of people.
    Oppose the Eisenhower and Yoshida government's policy of expanding the war. Oppose the abolition of the Yalta Agreement.
    Oppose the Pacific military alliance. Immediately conclude a Five-Power Peace Pact.
    Down with the traitorous Yoshida government! Long live the National Liberation Democratic United Government!
    Long live the united May Day for opposing war and striving for independence and democracy!

### 日共中央指导部发表庆祝“五一”节口号  反对恢复日本军国主义主张立刻停止朝鲜战争

1953-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    日共中央指导部发表庆祝“五一”节口号
    反对恢复日本军国主义主张立刻停止朝鲜战争
    【新华社十一日讯】据日本“赤旗报”消息：日本共产党中央指导部发表庆祝一九五三年“五一”劳动节的口号。摘要如下：
    粉碎职位等级制度的奴隶工资制度。劳动八小时，给以足够生活的工资。确立最低限度的工资制度。
    反对为战争服务的“产业合理化”，反对加强劳动、降低工资和解雇。
    废除“防止破坏活动法”。不许制订禁止总罢工法。反对恶劣地修改劳动法和警察法。
    反对为战争而征收重税。降低运输费、电费和煤气费。反对通货膨胀，要求降低物价。
    反对军国主义复活的教育法案，实行免费的义务教育。
    不制造和运输军需品，无限制地扩大和平产业。拯救中小企业。
    和一切国家自由地交往，促进和中国、苏联的贸易。
    反对恢复军国主义，反对恶劣地修改宪法，反对重整军备和征兵。
    一切外国军队立即撤回去。
    废除亡国条约和“行政协定”，立刻缔结全面和约。
    反对出兵朝鲜。解散“保安队”和“海上警备队”。
    立刻停止朝鲜战争。一切外国军队立刻撤出朝鲜、台湾和全亚洲。由亚洲人自己处理亚洲的事务。
    反对使用细菌武器、原子弹和其他一切大批屠杀人民的武器。
    反对艾森豪威尔和吉田政府的扩大战争政策。反对废除雅尔塔协定。
    反对太平洋军事同盟。立刻缔结五大国和平公约。
    打倒卖国吉田政府。建立民族解放民主联合政府万岁！
    反对战争、争取独立和民主的统一的“五一”节万岁！
