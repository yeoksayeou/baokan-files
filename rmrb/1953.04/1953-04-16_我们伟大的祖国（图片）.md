    Our Great Motherland
    Farmers in various parts of Northeast China are actively preparing for spring plowing. This is a group of female members of the Qianjin Collective Farm in Kaiyuan County, Liaoxi Province. They are inspecting disc plows and stepping up preparations for spring plowing.
