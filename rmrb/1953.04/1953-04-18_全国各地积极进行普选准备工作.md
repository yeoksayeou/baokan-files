Title: Nationwide Active Preparations for General Elections
Author: 
Date: 1953-04-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 17th: Preparations for grassroots elections are actively underway across the country. As of April 17th, the election committees have been established in 32 provinces and cities including Hebei, Shanxi, Liaodong, Liaoxi, Rehe, Heilongjiang, Songjiang, Jilin, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Shandong, Fujian, Anhui, Hubei, Henan, Jiangxi, Guangdong, Sichuan, Shaanxi, Xinjiang, Gansu, Qinghai, Ningxia and Beijing, Shanghai, Shenyang, Chongqing, Xi'an, Lushun-Dalian, Fushun, Anshan, Benxi, and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, in order to guide and promote grassroots election work. The aforementioned provincial and municipal election committees have all been approved by the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council.

Various localities are successively holding meetings on grassroots election work, studying and formulating plans for grassroots election work. The Northeast Administrative Committee convened a meeting of directors of civil affairs departments and bureaus of various provinces and cities on the 12th of this month, and initially studied the steps, timing, and cadre allocation and training for grassroots election work. The Shandong Provincial People's Government recently convened a provincial grassroots election work conference and adopted the provincial pilot township work plan for the general election. The Shenyang Municipal People's Government has initially drafted a citywide general election work plan.

Various provinces (cities) are uniformly planning to transfer a large number of cadres, training them in batches, to participate in grassroots election work. Shanghai will begin training the first batch of cadres participating in election work before the 25th of this month. Wuhan will transfer more than 3,000 cadres for short-term training to undertake grassroots election work. Henan Province will transfer 1,500 people, training them in two groups, to serve as the leading backbone of grassroots election work throughout the province. Qinghai Province will soon transfer county and district civil affairs cadres for intensive training.

Various localities are compiling and writing documents and publicity materials related to the general election to prepare participating election workers for study and promotion. The Shanxi Provincial People's Government has compiled and printed a booklet of "Study Documents for Election Work and Population Survey and Registration Work" and distributed it to relevant cadres for study. The Civil Affairs Bureau of the Northeast Administrative Committee is compiling publicity materials related to the general election. All staff members of the Civil Affairs Bureau of the Central and Southern Administrative Committee officially began studying documents on the election law and election work from the 13th of this month.

### 全国各地积极进行普选准备工作

1953-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    全国各地积极进行普选准备工作
    【新华社十七日讯】全国各地正积极进行基层选举的准备工作。截至十七日止，已有河北、山西、辽东、辽西、热河、黑龙江、松江、吉林、江苏、浙江、山东、福建、安徽、湖北、河南、江西、广东、四川、陕西、新疆、甘肃、青海、宁夏和北京、上海、沈阳、重庆、西安、旅大、抚顺、鞍山、本溪等三十二个省、市以及内蒙古自治区成立了选举委员会，以便指导和推进基层选举工作。上述省、市选举委员会都已经中央人民政府政务院批准。
    各地现正相继举行有关基层选举工作的会议，研究和订定基层选举工作的计划。东北行政委员会在本月十二日召集各省、市民政厅、局长举行会议，初步研究了基层选举工作的步骤、时间和干部的调配、训练等问题。山东省人民政府最近召开了全省基层选举工作会议，通过了全省普选试点乡工作计划。沈阳市人民政府已初步拟订了全市普选工作计划。
    各省（市）正在统一计划抽调大批干部，分批训练，参加基层选举工作。上海市在本月二十五日前将开始第一批参加选举工作干部的训练。武汉市将抽调三千多干部，进行短期训练，担任基层选举工作。河南省将抽调一千五百人，分两批集中训练，作为全省基层选举工作的领导骨干。青海省即将抽调县、区民政干部进行集训。
    各地正在编印和编写有关普选的文件和宣传材料，以备参加选举的工作干部进行学习和展开宣传。山西省人民政府已编印了“选举工作及人口调查登记工作学习文件”的小册子，分发有关干部学习。东北行政委员会民政局正在编写有关普选的宣传材料。中南行政委员会民政局全体工作人员从本月十三日起已正式开始学习关于选举法和选举工作的文件。
