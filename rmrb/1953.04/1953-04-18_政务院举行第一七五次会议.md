Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 175th Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1953-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 17th: The 175th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 17th. After listening to the explanation by Li Lisan, Minister of the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the instruction on dissuading farmers from blindly flowing into cities. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal cases and cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第一七五次会议

1953-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一七五次会议
    【新华社十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百七十五次政务会议在十七日下午举行。会议在听取中央人民政府劳动部李立三部长的说明后，通过了关于劝止农民盲目流入城市的指示。会议并通过各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
