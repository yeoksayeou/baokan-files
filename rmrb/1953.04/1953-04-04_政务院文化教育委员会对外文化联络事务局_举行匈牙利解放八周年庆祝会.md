Title: The Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee of Culture and Education Holds a Celebration of the Eighth Anniversary of the Liberation of Hungary
Author:
Date: 1953-04-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd: The Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee of Culture and Education held a celebration at the Beijing Youth Palace on the afternoon of the 3rd to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the liberation of the People's Republic of Hungary. Attendees included: Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wen Ning, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Committee of Culture and Education, Mei Yi, Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, and more than 600 cadres from various agencies and organizations in Beijing. Also attending the evening were Hungarian Ambassador to China, Száffránkó, and personnel from the Hungarian Embassy. After the national anthems of Hungary, China, and the Soviet Union were played, Minister Shen Yanbing delivered a speech at the celebration. He spoke of the brilliant achievements of the Hungarian people in various fields over the past eight years since liberation, and the new atmosphere of vigorous cultural exchange between the people of China and Hungary under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Rákosi. Ambassador Száffránkó then delivered a speech. He said that since the glorious Soviet army annihilated the German fascists and liberated the entire territory of Hungary, the Hungarian working people have achieved great successes in political, economic, and cultural construction over the past eight years. He described in detail these achievements and the increasingly close cooperative and friendly relations between the people of China and Hungary, and believed that to maintain peace, it is necessary to resolutely unite with the powerful peace camp of 800 million people headed by the Soviet Union.
    The celebration also included a report by Director Mei Yi on "My Impressions of Hungary." He vividly described the happy and beautiful life of the Hungarian people he saw during his visit to Hungary last year, as well as the warm affection of the Hungarian people for the people of the Soviet Union and China.
    Finally, Hungarian dances and Chinese folk art performances were staged at the meeting.
    Another dispatch: The Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee of Culture and Education also held a film evening at the Capital Cinema in Beijing on the evening of the 3rd to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the liberation of the People's Republic of Hungary, where the Hungarian musical film "Erkel" was screened.

### 政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局  举行匈牙利解放八周年庆祝会

1953-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局
    举行匈牙利解放八周年庆祝会
    【新华社三日讯】为庆祝匈牙利人民共和国解放八周年纪念，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局三日下午在北京青年宫举行庆祝会。参加庆祝会的有：中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰、外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、苏联东欧司副司长温宁、文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深和广播事业局局长梅益以及北京各机关团体干部等六百余人。出席晚会的还有匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯和匈牙利大使馆人员。在奏了匈、中、苏三国国歌后，庆祝会首由沈雁冰部长致词，他谈到匈牙利人民解放八年来各方面的光辉成就以及中匈两国人民在毛泽东主席和拉科西主席领导下，文化交流蓬勃向上的新气象。接着夏法朗柯大使致词。他说：自从光辉的苏联军队消灭了德国法西斯和解放了全部匈牙利的领土以后，八年来匈牙利劳动人民在政治、经济、文化建设事业上已获得了巨大的成就。他详尽地叙述了这些成就以及中匈两国人民日益紧密合作的友谊关系，并认为要维护和平，只有坚决地和以苏联为首的八亿人口的强大和平阵营团结一致。
    庆祝会还请梅益局长作了“我在匈牙利的观感”的报告。他生动地叙述了去年访问匈牙利时所看到的匈牙利人民幸福美好的生活以及匈牙利人民对苏中两国人民的热烈的友爱。
    会上，最后还演出了匈牙利舞蹈和中国民间艺术等文艺节目。
    又讯：中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局为庆祝匈牙利人民共和国解放八周年纪念日，并于三日晚在北京首都电影院举行了电影晚会，会上放映了匈牙利音乐影片“埃尔柯尔”。
