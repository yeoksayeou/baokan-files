Title: Second Group of Japanese Nationals Concentrated in Shanghai Repatriated

Author:

Date: 1953-04-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 15th - The second group of 1,018 Japanese nationals concentrated in Shanghai who wished to return to their country left Shanghai on the morning of the 14th aboard the Japanese ships "Hakuryu Maru" and "Hakusan Maru" to return to Japan. More than 100 people from the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese Red Cross Society and the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union went to the dock to see them off. The well-wishers shook hands with the Japanese nationals in the drizzle and wished the friendship between the Chinese and Japanese people would continue to grow. The Japanese nationals also expressed their gratitude for the many considerations provided by the Chinese side.

### 集中在上海的第二批日侨回国

1953-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    集中在上海的第二批日侨回国
    【新华社上海十五日电】集中在上海的第二批愿意回国的日本侨民一千零十八名，在十四日上午分乘日本轮船“白龙丸”、“白山丸”离开上海回日本。到码头欢送的有：中国红十字会上海市分会和中国海员工会上海市委员会工作人员共一百多人。欢送者在细雨中和日本侨民握手道别，互祝中日两国人民的友谊不断增长。日本侨民并对中国方面的多方照顾表示感谢。
