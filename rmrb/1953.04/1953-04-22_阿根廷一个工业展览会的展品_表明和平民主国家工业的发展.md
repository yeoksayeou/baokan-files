Title: Exhibits at an Argentine Industrial Exhibition Demonstrate the Development of Industry in Peaceful and Democratic Countries
Author:
Date: 1953-04-22
Page: 4
　
    Exhibits at an Argentine Industrial Exhibition
    Demonstrate the Development of Industry in Peaceful and Democratic Countries
    [Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, 20th] TASS, Buenos Aires, 18th: The Argentine World Trade Promotion Committee held an industrial exhibition in Buenos Aires. The charts, catalogs, photo albums, and commodity samples on display all illustrate the industrial development achievements of the Soviet Union, the People's Democratic countries, and the People's Republic of China. Films introducing industrial technology were shown in the exhibition hall. The exhibits will later be transported to Rosario, Domensa, and other Argentine cities for exhibition.

### 阿根廷一个工业展览会的展品  表明和平民主国家工业的发展

1953-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷一个工业展览会的展品
    表明和平民主国家工业的发展
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯十八日讯：阿根廷世界贸易促进委员会在布宜诺斯艾利斯举办了一个工业展览会。展出的图表、目录、照片本及货物样本都说明了苏联、人民民主国家与中华人民共和国工业发展的成就。在展览厅中放映了介绍工业技术的影片。展览品以后还将运到罗萨利俄、多门萨及其他阿根廷城市去展览。
