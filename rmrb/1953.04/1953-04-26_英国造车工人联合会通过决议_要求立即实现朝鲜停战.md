Title: British Vehicle Builders Union Passes Resolution Demanding Immediate Armistice in Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th, TASS, London, April 23rd: "News United" reports: The Executive Committee of the National Vehicle Builders Union has passed a resolution demanding an immediate ceasefire in Korea. Another resolution from the Executive Committee calls for the withdrawal of all British troops in Korea.

The "Daily Worker" reports: The Loughton District Peace Council of Essex County demands that the British government "use all its power and influence in the United Nations to achieve a complete armistice in Korea and end the Korean War."

The statement says: "We firmly believe that if lasting world peace is to be achieved, the first step must be the unconditional holding of the fullest negotiations by all the world's major powers."

### 英国造车工人联合会通过决议  要求立即实现朝鲜停战

1953-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    英国造车工人联合会通过决议
    要求立即实现朝鲜停战
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十三日讯：“新闻联合社”报道：全国造车工人联合会执行委员会通过一项决议，要求朝鲜立刻停火。执行委员会的另外一项决议要求撤退在朝鲜的所有英国军队。
    “工人日报”报道：埃赛克斯郡劳夫顿区和平理事会要求英国政府“在联合国使用它一切的力量和影响，使朝鲜达成完全的停战，并结束朝鲜战争”。
    声明说：“我们坚决相信，假使要取得世界的持久和平的话，首先的步骤必须是世界所有强国无条件地举行最充分的谈判。”
