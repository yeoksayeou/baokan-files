    Our Great Motherland
    Peasants in various regions are actively engaging in this year's spring plowing and production campaign. This is the Xiang Jinquan mutual aid team in Duzhou Township, Changshou County, Sichuan. They are taking the lead in applying green manure to strive to increase yields per unit area.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地农民正积极投入今年的春耕生产运动。这是四川长寿县渡舟乡向金全互助组的组员们。他们带头施用青肥，为提高单位面积产量而努力。
