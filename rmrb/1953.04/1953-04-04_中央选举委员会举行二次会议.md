Title: The Central Election Committee Holds Second Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-04-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd. The Central Election Committee held its second meeting on the afternoon of the 3rd, and passed two documents: "Instructions on Grassroots Election Work" and "Answers to Several Questions Regarding Voter Qualifications."

### 中央选举委员会举行二次会议

1953-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中央选举委员会举行二次会议
    【新华社三日讯】中央选举委员会在三日下午举行第二次会议，通过了“关于基层选举工作的指示”及“关于选民资格若干问题的解答”两个文件。
