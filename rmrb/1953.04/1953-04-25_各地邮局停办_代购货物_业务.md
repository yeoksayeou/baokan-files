Title: Post Offices Nationwide to Discontinue "Goods Purchasing on Behalf" Service
Author: 
Date: 1953-04-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th—The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government issued a circular to post offices and telecommunications bureaus nationwide, announcing the discontinuation of the "Goods Purchasing on Behalf" service starting May 1st of this year. This measure aims to concentrate resources and better fulfill postal and telecommunications tasks. Except for goods purchased on behalf of customers by post offices or telecommunications bureaus before April 30th, which will still be processed and settled within a limited time, those who wish to purchase goods from other cities should directly send purchase letters to department stores or place orders with manufacturers in those locations.

### 各地邮局停办“代购货物”业务

1953-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    各地邮局停办“代购货物”业务
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府邮电部通令全国各地邮局和邮电局，从今年五月一日起，停办“代购货物”业务，以便集中力量，更好地完成邮电通信任务。除了在今年四月三十日以前由邮局或邮电局代购的货物，仍旧负责办完并限期清结以外，此后，凡是要向外埠购买货物的，可以直接向各地百货公司函购，或者向各地厂商订购。
