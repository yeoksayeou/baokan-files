Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Invites Representatives from Trade Union Organizations of Various Countries to Attend the "May Day" Celebration and the Seventh National Labor Congress

Author: 
Date: 1953-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 16th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has issued invitations to the World Federation of Trade Unions and trade unions in many countries, inviting them to send delegations to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and the Seventh National Labor Congress to be held in Beijing this year. The invited trade union organizations from various countries include: national trade union organizations from the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, the German Democratic Republic, North Korea, Mongolia, and Vietnam; trade union organizations from India, Pakistan, Indonesia, Burma, Ceylon, Japan, Australia, New Zealand, etc.; trade union organizations from the Middle East, Near East, and North Africa; and trade union organizations from the United Kingdom. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has successively received telegrams from these trade union organizations, expressing their gratitude and acceptance of the invitation.

### 全国总工会邀请各国工会组织派代表来我国参加“五一”节典礼和七次劳大

1953-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    全国总工会邀请各国工会组织派代表来我国参加“五一”节典礼和七次劳大
    【新华社十六日讯】中华全国总工会已发出请帖，邀请世界工会联合会和许多国家的工会派遣代表团前来中国，参加今年在北京举行的“五一”国际劳动节庆祝典礼和第七次全国劳动大会。邀请的各国工会团体有：苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、德意志民主共和国、朝鲜、蒙古、越南各国的全国性工会组织，印度、巴基斯坦、印尼、缅甸、锡兰、日本、澳大利亚、新西兰等国的工会组织，中东、近东、北非各国的工会组织，以及英国的工会组织。中华全国总工会已陆续收到这些工会组织的复电，表示感谢和接受邀请。
