Title: Democratic Youth League Holds Second National Committee Meeting; Approves Decision to Convene Second National Youth Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-04-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 20th—The All-China Democratic Youth Federation held its Second National Committee Meeting in Beijing on March 10th. The meeting discussed and approved the decision to convene the Second National Youth Congress of China, and elected Liao Chengzhi, Qian Sanqiang, Wu Han, Liu Daosheng, Yu Beichen, Qian Weichang, Ding Cong, Li Geng, Ou Tangliang, Mei Gongbin, Guan Shixiong, Keli Geng, Liu Liangmo, Tian Demin, Ge Buhai, Sun Fuling, and Huang Zhensheng as members of the Preparatory Committee for the Second National Youth Congress of China.

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th—The decision of the Second National Committee Meeting of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation regarding the convening of the Second National Youth Congress of China. The full text is as follows:

(1) The Second National Youth Congress of China is scheduled to be held in Beijing on June 10th. The agenda of the congress is as follows: 1. Summarize the work of the Youth Federation over the past four years; 2. Determine the future direction and tasks of the Youth Federation; 3. Revise the Constitution of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation; 4. Re-elect the National Committee.

(2) The number of delegates to the Second National Youth Congress of China is set at 560. The specific distribution is as follows:

	1. Regional Representatives (including youth from workers, farmers, industry and commerce, neighborhoods, ethnic minorities, religious circles, and technical fields) 349.
		North China Region 57; Northeast Region 44;
		Northwest Region 40; East China Region 76;
		Central South Region 56; Southwest Region 63;
		Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region 5; Tibet 5;
		Taiwan 3.

	2. Military Representatives 25.
		Youth of the Chinese People's Liberation Army 15;
		Youth of the Chinese People's Volunteers 10.

	3. Party and Group Representatives 176.
		Youth of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, China Democratic League, China National Democratic Construction Association, China Association for Promoting Democracy, Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, China Zhi Gong Party, Jiusan Society, Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League 20;
		Communist Youth League of China 10;
		All-China Students' Federation 10;
		Young Men's Christian Association of China 5;
		Young Women's Christian Association of China 5;
		Youth Peace Movement Workers 2;
		Youth Trade Union Workers 5;
		Youth Women Workers 5;
		Youth News and Publication Workers 10;
		Youth Sports Workers 10;
		Youth Education Workers 14;
		Youth Natural Science Workers 18;
		Youth Social Science Workers 10;
		Youth Literary and Art Workers 40;
		Youth Medical and Health Care Workers 12.

	4. Overseas Chinese Youth 10.

(3) Method of selection of delegates to the Second National Youth Congress of China:

	1. Elected by youth representative conferences or youth representative meetings convened by the Youth Federations in various cities;
	2. Nominated through consultation with relevant parties.

(4) Delegates from various regions should be organized into regional delegations to go to Beijing according to major administrative regions.

(5) Delegates from various regions should register with the Federation in Beijing on June 6th, 7th, and 8th.
March 10, 1953

### 民主青联举行全国委员会第二次会议  通过召开全国青年第二次代表大会的决定

1953-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    民主青联举行全国委员会第二次会议
    通过召开全国青年第二次代表大会的决定
    【新华社二十日讯】中华全国民主青年联合总会三月十日在北京举行全国委员会第二次会议。会上讨论与通过了关于召开中华全国青年第二次代表大会的决定，并推举廖承志、钱三强、吴晗、刘导生、于北辰、钱伟长、丁聪、李庚、区棠亮、梅龚彬、关世雄、克力更、刘良模、田德民、葛步海、孙孚凌、黄振声等十七人为中华全国青年第二次代表大会筹备委员会委员。
    【新华社二十日讯】中华全国民主青年联合总会全国委员会第二次会议关于召开中华全国青年第二次代表大会的决定。全文如下：
    （一）中华全国青年第二次代表大会订于六月十日在北京召开。大会议程为：一、总结四年来青联的工作；二、确定今后青联工作的方针、任务；三、修改中华全国民主青年联合总会章程；四、改选全国委员会。
    （二）中华全国青年第二次代表大会代表名额定为五百六十名。具体分配如下：
    一、地区代表（包括工人、农民、工商界、街道、少数民族、宗
        教界、技术界等方面青年）三百四十九名。
        华北区五十七名；东北区四十四名；
        西北区四十名；华东区七十六名；
        中南区五十六名；西南区六十三名；
        内蒙古自治区五名；西藏五名；
        台湾三名。
    二、军队代表二十五名。
        中国人民解放军青年十五名；
        中国人民志愿军青年十名。
    三、党派团体代表一百七十六名。
        民革、民盟、民建、民促、农工民主党、致公党、九三学社
        、台盟青年共二十名；
        中国新民主主义青年团十名；
        中华全国学生联合会十名；
        中华基督教青年会五名；
        中华基督教女青年会五名；
        青年和平运动工作者二名；
        青年工会工作者五名；
        青年妇女工作者五名；
        青年新闻出版工作者十名；
        青年体育工作者十名；
        青年教育工作者十四名；
        青年自然科学工作者十八名；
        青年社会科学工作者十名；
        青年文学艺术工作者四十名；
        青年医药卫生保育工作者十二名。
    四、华侨青年十名。
    （三）中华全国青年第二次代表大会代表产生办法：
    一、由各城市青联召开青年代表大会或青年代表会议选举之；
    二、与各有关方面协商推定之。
    （四）各地代表需按各大行政区组织成区域代表团赴京。
    （五）各地代表需在六月六、七、八三日到北京本会报到。一九五三年三月十日
