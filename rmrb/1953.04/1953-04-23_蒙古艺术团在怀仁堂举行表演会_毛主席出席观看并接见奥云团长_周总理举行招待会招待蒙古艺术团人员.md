Title: Mongolian Art Troupe Performs at Huairentang, Chairman Mao Attends and Meets with Head of Delegation Oyun, Premier Zhou Hosts Reception for Members of Mongolian Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1953-04-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe held a music, dance, and acrobatics performance at Huairentang in Beijing on the evening of the 22nd. The performance was hosted by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government attended the performance and met with Oyun, the head of the Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe, and Wang Gang, the political instructor. Accompanying the meeting was Jalkhasaikhan, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China. Also attending the performance were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Li Jishen; Premier of the Government Administration Council Zhou Enlai; Vice Premiers Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping; as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and famous writers, musicians, dancers, artists, and actors from Beijing.

Foreign guests attending the performance included Jalkhasaikhan, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Kuznetsov, the Soviet Ambassador to China, and diplomatic personnel from the Mongolian and Soviet embassies.

The many wonderful programs performed by the Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe were highly praised by those present. These programs fully demonstrated the robust spirit of the hardworking and brave Mongolian people, as well as the brilliant achievements of Mongolian artists in learning from the Soviet Union and developing national art.

The Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe performed at the Beijing Theater on the evenings of the 20th and 21st, respectively, hosting members of the cultural and artistic circles of the capital and foreign diplomats in Beijing.

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held a reception on the evening of the 22nd to entertain all members of the Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe, who were invited to perform in China. Jalkhasaikhan, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, was also invited to attend the reception.

Accompanying the reception were Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Xi Zhongxun, Ma Yinchu, Wu Xiuquan, Zhang Hanfu, Zhou Yang, Ding Xilin, as well as famous figures from the fields of literature, film, fine arts, music, drama, dance, opera, and acrobatics.

At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai and Oyun, the head of the Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe, delivered speeches successively. Guests frequently raised their glasses for the friendship and cooperation between China and Mongolia, for the health of Tedenbal, the people's leader of the Mongolian People's Republic, for the health of Chairman Mao, the leader of the Chinese people, and for the health of the Soviet people's leaders headed by Chairman Malenkov.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 蒙古艺术团在怀仁堂举行表演会  毛主席出席观看并接见奥云团长  周总理举行招待会招待蒙古艺术团人员

1953-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古艺术团在怀仁堂举行表演会
    毛主席出席观看并接见奥云团长
    周总理举行招待会招待蒙古艺术团人员
    【新华社二十二日讯】蒙古人民共和国艺术团于二十二日晚在北京怀仁堂举行音乐、舞蹈、杂技表演会，主持这次演出的是中央人民政府文化部。中央人民政府毛泽东主席出席观看了表演，并接见了蒙古人民共和国艺术团团长奥云、政治指导员王岗。陪同进见的有蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使贾尔卡赛汗。出席观看表演的，还有中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深，政务院总理周恩来，副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，以及中央人民政府各部门首长和北京的著名作家、音乐家、舞蹈家、美术家、演员等。
    出席观看表演的外宾，有蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使贾尔卡赛汗、苏联驻我国大使库兹涅佐夫及蒙、苏两国大使馆的外交人员。
    蒙古人民共和国艺术团演出的许多精采节目，深为到会者所称赞。这些节目，充分表现了勤劳、勇敢的蒙古人民的雄健气魄，以及蒙古文艺工作者在学习苏联和发扬民族艺术上的辉煌成就。
    蒙古人民共和国艺术团曾于二十日和二十一日晚在北京剧场演出，分别招待首都文化艺术界人士和在北京的各国外交人员。
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理在二十二日夜举行招待会，招待应邀前来我国表演的蒙古人民共和国艺术团全体人员。蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使贾尔卡赛汗也应邀参加了招待会。
    出席招待会作陪的，有郭沫若、陈叔通、习仲勋、马寅初、伍修权、章汉夫、周扬、丁西林，以及文学、电影、美术、音乐、戏剧、舞蹈、戏曲、杂技界的著名人士。
    在招待会上，周恩来总理、蒙古人民共和国艺术团团长奥云曾相继致词。会上宾主频频举杯为中蒙两国的友好和合作，为蒙古人民共和国的人民领袖泽登巴尔总理的健康，为中国人民领袖毛主席的健康，为以马林科夫主席为首的苏联人民领袖们的健康干杯。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。（附图片）
    中央人民政府毛泽东主席接见蒙古人民共和国艺术团。右起：毛泽东主席，周恩来总理，蒙古人民共和国驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗，蒙古人民共和国人民演员、乔巴山奖金获得者、蒙古人民共和国艺术团团长奥云，政治指导员王岗。新华社稿    吕厚民摄
