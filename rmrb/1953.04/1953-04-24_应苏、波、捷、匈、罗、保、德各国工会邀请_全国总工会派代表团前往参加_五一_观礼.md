Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Delegations to Attend May Day Celebrations at the Invitation of Trade Unions from the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, and Germany
Author:
Date: 1953-04-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, at the invitation of national trade union organizations from the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, and the German Democratic Republic, has dispatched delegations to attend the May Day celebrations. The delegations departed in batches over three days, from the 21st to the 23rd. Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office, Zhang Xiuzhu, Deputy Director of the Culture and Education Department, and Zhuang Yan and Fang Ming, Deputy Directors of the International Liaison Department, saw them off at the airport. The lists of each delegation are as follows:

    Delegation to the Soviet Union for May Day Celebrations:

    Li Quanchao - Vice-Chairman of the Tianjin Municipal Federation of Trade Unions.
    Zhu Weiren - Chairman of the Shenyang Municipal Federation of Trade Unions.

### 应苏、波、捷、匈、罗、保、德各国工会邀请  全国总工会派代表团前往参加“五一”观礼
