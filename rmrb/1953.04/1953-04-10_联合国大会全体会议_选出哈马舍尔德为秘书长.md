Title: UN General Assembly Elects Hammarskjöld as Secretary-General
Author:
Date: 1953-04-10
Page: 4

    UN General Assembly

    Elects Hammarskjöld as Secretary-General
(Xinhua News Agency, April 9th) TASS, New York: After examining the Security Council's recommendation for the candidate for the new Secretary-General, the UN General Assembly, through a secret ballot, elected Dag Hammarskjöld, Swedish Minister without Portfolio, as the Secretary-General of the United Nations. Fifty-seven votes were in favor of Hammarskjöld. One representative voted against, and one representative abstained.

### 联合国大会全体会议  选出哈马舍尔德为秘书长

1953-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国大会全体会议
    选出哈马舍尔德为秘书长 【新华社九日讯】塔斯社纽约讯：联合国大会全体会议在审查了安全理事会对新任秘书长的人选的推荐以后，用秘密投票方式选出瑞典不管部大臣达格·哈马舍尔德为联合国秘书长。赞成哈马舍尔德的有五十七票。有一个代表投了反对票，一个代表弃权。
