Title: UN Political Committee Concludes Discussion on Proposal to Investigate US Military's Germ Warfare; Polish Representative Points Out US Military's Germ Warfare Is Indisputable
Author:
Date: 1953-04-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York: The United Nations General Assembly's Political Committee held a meeting on the afternoon of April 8th, concluding the discussion on the case entitled "Impartial Investigation of Accusations Regarding the Use of Bacteriological Weapons by American Troops." Representatives from the United States, the United Kingdom, the Philippines, Israel, and other countries spoke at the meeting, supporting the proposal put forward by the United States and other delegations, which stipulates the establishment of a committee composed of representatives from five countries to investigate the accusations regarding the use of bacteriological weapons by American troops in Korea and China.

    Polish representative Katz-Suchy requested the committee to pay attention to the many irrefutable facts that prove the US military command conducted germ warfare in Korea, the statements of scientists, and the confessions of American officers. The Polish representative said that because the majority of the committee disagreed with the Soviet proposal to invite representatives from the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Political Committee could not impartially discuss this issue. Katz-Suchy also emphasized that the proposal from the United States and other countries prejudged the task of the proposed five-nation representative committee to exonerate the United States. This alone shows that the five-nation representative committee will not be impartial.

    The Indonesian representative, in explaining his vote, pointed out that if the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea do not recognize the legitimacy of the committee established in the proposal, the committee will not be able to work effectively. He expressed doubt as to whether it was appropriate to establish such a committee, and said that the Indonesian delegation would abstain from voting. He also pointed out that it was inappropriate for the Political Committee not to agree to listen to and judge the opinions of the representatives of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on the issues under discussion.

    The proposal put forward by the United States and some other delegations to establish a five-nation representative committee was passed by a majority vote. The Soviet Union, Ukraine, Belarus, Poland, and Czechoslovakia voted against the proposal. Representatives from India, Indonesia, and Burma abstained.

### 联大政委会结束关于调查美军进行细菌战的提案的讨论  波兰代表指出美军进行细菌战已无可辩驳

1953-04-11
第4版()
专栏：

    联大政委会结束关于调查美军进行细菌战的提案的讨论
    波兰代表指出美军进行细菌战已无可辩驳
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社纽约讯：联合国大会政治委员会于四月八日下午举行会议，结束了关于名为“公正调查关于美国军队使用细菌武器的控诉”案的讨论。在会上发言的有美国、英国、菲律宾、以色列以及其他一些国家的代表，他们支持美国和其他国家代表团所提出的提案，这个提案规定成立一个五国代表所组成的委员会，调查关于美国军队在朝鲜和中国使用细菌武器的控诉。
    波兰代表卡茨—苏希要求委员会注意证实美军司令部在朝鲜进行了细菌战的许多无可辩驳的事实、科学家的谈话和美国军官的供词。波兰代表说：由于委员会中的大多数不同意苏联提出的邀请中华人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的代表的建议，所以政治委员会就不能公正地讨论这个问题。卡茨—苏希还着重指出：美国和其它一些国家的提案事先就规定建议成立的五国代表委员会的任务是替美国摆脱罪责。只从这一点就可以看出这个五国代表委员会不会是公正的。
    印度尼西亚代表在解释他的投票时指出：如果中华人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国不承认这个提案中规定成立的委员会的合法性，这个委员会就不能有效地进行工作。他对于成立这样一个委员会是否妥当表示怀疑，并且说，印度尼西亚代表团将在投票时弃权，他并且指出：政治委员会没有同意听取和判断中华人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的代表对讨论中的问题的意见，是不妥当的。
    美国和一些其他国家的代表团提出的规定成立一个五国代表委员会的提案以多数票获得通过。苏联、乌克兰、白俄罗斯、波兰和捷克斯洛伐克投票反对这个提案。印度、印度尼西亚和缅甸代表弃权。
