Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Issues Slogans to Celebrate May Day

Author: 
Date: 1953-04-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 22nd: The Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party has issued slogans to celebrate International Labor Day, May 1st. The Central Committee's slogans state: Long live May Day, a review and demonstration of the international unity and fighting strength of workers worldwide! Long live the unbreakable internationalist friendship and unity of communist and workers' parties around the world, headed by the Communist Party of the Soviet Union! Long live the strengthened combat unity of workers worldwide in the struggle against aggression and war, for peace, democracy, socialism, and national independence! Warm congratulations to the great Soviet people who are victoriously building communism under the banner of Lenin and Stalin and inspiring our Fatherland Liberation War! Warm congratulations to the fraternal Chinese people who have achieved brilliant victories in building a new people's democratic China and are providing bloody assistance to our Fatherland Liberation War under the sacred banner of resisting America and aiding Korea! Warm congratulations to the people's democratic countries that are victoriously building socialism and providing fraternal support to the Korean people! Warm congratulations to the peace-loving people all over the world who support the Korean people, and to the peace defenders of the world! Fraternal congratulations to the people of colonies and dependent countries who are bravely fighting against imperialist occupiers for freedom and national independence!

The slogans of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party call upon all workers and all people of the Republic to hold high the ever-victorious banner of Lenin and Stalin, to unite more strongly around Marshal Kim Il-sung, to display a high degree of creativity and dedication, to overfulfill the wartime production plan, to supply the front and rear with more necessities, to further strengthen the struggle against special agents and bacteriological warfare, to completely crush the enemy's various atrocities, and to advance towards final victory. The Central Committee also calls on the people of the southern half of Korea to actively launch struggles and deal heavy blows to the American-Syngman Rhee gang.

The Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party calls on commanders and fighters of the Korean People's Army to further strengthen their joint combat with the fraternal Chinese People's Volunteers and annihilate the enemy. The Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party said: Victory and glory belong to the heroic soldiers of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers!

The Central Committee of the Labor Party finally shouted: Long live the eternal friendship and unity between the great Soviet people and the Korean people! Long live the consolidated fighting friendship and unity between the people of Korea and China! Long live Marshal Kim Il-sung, the beloved leader of the Korean people! Long live Chairman Mao Zedong, the beloved leader of the Chinese people, who sincerely assists the Korean people in their Fatherland Liberation War! Long live Comrade Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, the talented student of Lenin, the loyal comrade-in-arms of Stalin, who actively supports our Fatherland Liberation War! The great cause and thoughts of the great Stalin, for the happiness of mankind, will forever live in the hearts of the Soviet people and all progressive mankind! Long live the invincible banner of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin!

### 朝鲜劳动党中央发布庆祝五一劳动节口号

1953-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央发布庆祝五一劳动节口号
    【新华社平壤二十二日电】朝鲜劳动党中央委员会发布了庆祝五一国际劳动节的口号。劳动党中央的口号说：检阅全世界劳动者的国际团结和战斗力量并进行示威的五一节万岁！以苏联共产党为首的全世界共产党及劳动党的钢铁般的国际主义友谊团结万岁！全世界劳动者在反对侵略与战争，争取和平、民主、社会主义与民族独立的斗争中的战斗团结的加强万岁！对在列宁、斯大林的旗帜下胜利地建设共产主义，并鼓舞我们进行祖国解放战争的伟大的苏联人民，致热烈的祝贺！对在建设人民民主主义新中国的事业中取得辉煌胜利，并在抗美援朝的神圣旗帜下以鲜血援助我们祖国解放战争的兄弟般的中国人民，致热烈的祝贺！对胜利地建设社会主义，并给予朝鲜人民兄弟般的声援的各人民民主国家，致热烈的祝贺！对声援朝鲜人民的全世界爱好和平的人民，对全世界的和平保卫者，致热烈的祝贺！对为自由和民族独立而与帝国主义占领者英勇斗争的殖民地和附属国人民，致兄弟般的祝贺！
    朝鲜劳动党中央的口号号召共和国全体劳动者和全体人民，高举百战百胜的列宁、斯大林的旗帜，更加坚强地团结在金日成元帅的周围，发挥高度的创造性和献身精神，超额完成战时生产计划，供给前后方以更多的必需品，进一步加强反特务和反细菌战的斗争，彻底粉碎敌人的各种暴行，向最后胜利前进。劳动党中央并号召朝鲜南半部的人民积极展开斗争，给美李匪帮以沉重的打击。
    朝鲜劳动党中央号召朝鲜人民军指挥员、战斗员进一步加强和兄弟般的中国人民志愿军的并肩作战，歼灭敌人。朝鲜劳动党中央说：胜利和光荣归于英雄的朝鲜人民军将士们和中国人民志愿军将士们！
    劳动党中央委员会最后高呼：伟大的苏联人民和朝鲜人民之间永恒的友谊团结万岁！朝、中两国人民之间巩固的战斗的友谊团结万岁！朝鲜人民敬爱的领袖金日成元帅万岁！中国人民敬爱的领袖、真诚地援助朝鲜人民进行祖国解放战争的毛泽东主席万岁！列宁的天才学生、斯大林的忠实战友、积极声援我们祖国解放战争的苏联部长会议主席马林科夫同志万岁！为人类幸福的伟大的斯大林的永垂不朽的伟大事业和思想，将永远活在苏联人民和全世界进步人类的心中！必胜不败的马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的旗帜万岁！
