Title: All-China Women's Federation Holds First Meeting of the Second Executive Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-04-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held its first meeting of the Second Executive Committee on April 25th. A total of ninety-three executive committee members and alternate executive committee members attended. The meeting unanimously approved Soong Ching Ling and He Xiangning as honorary chairpersons, and elected twenty-five standing committee members. Furthermore, one chairperson and five vice-chairpersons were elected from among the standing committee members. The full list is as follows:
    Chairperson: Cai Chang
    Vice-Chairpersons: Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, Xu Guangping, Shi Liang, Zhang Yun
    Standing Committee Members: Yang Zhihua, Kang Keqing, Luo Qiong, Cao Mengjun, Zeng Xianzhi, Yang Chun, Xu Zhiyin, Wang Jingcheng, Shen Zijiu, Wang Ziru, Zhang Zifang, Lu Cui, Li Baoguang, Dong Bian, Tian Xiujuan, Lei Jieqiong, Qu Tangliang, Deng Yuzhi, Liu Wang Liming.
    After the meeting, thirty representatives were sent to visit Honorary Chairperson He Xiangning, who is currently ill.

### 全国妇联召开第二届执委会第一次会议

1953-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联召开第二届执委会第一次会议
    【新华社二十五日讯】四月二十五日，中华全国民主妇女联合会召开第二届执行委员会第一次会议。出席执委、候补执委共九十三人。会上一致通过宋庆龄、何香凝为名誉主席，并选出常务委员二十五人；又在常务委员中推选主席一人，副主席五人。全部名单如下：
    主席：蔡畅
    副主席：邓颖超、李德全、许广平、史良、章蕴
    常务委员：杨之华、康克清、罗琼、曹孟君、曾宪植、杨纯、徐芝寅、王竟成、沈兹九、王子如、张子芳、陆璀、李宝光、董边、田秀涓、雷洁琼、区棠亮、邓裕志、刘王立明。
    会后，派出代表三十人，慰问正在病中的名誉主席何香凝。
