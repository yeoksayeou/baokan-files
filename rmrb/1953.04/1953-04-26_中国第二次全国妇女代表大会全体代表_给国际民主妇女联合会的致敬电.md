Title: Telegram of Greetings from All Representatives of the Second National Women's Congress of China to the Women's International Democratic Federation

Author: 
Date: 1953-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: Telegram of Greetings from the Second National Women's Congress of China to the Women's International Democratic Federation. The full text is as follows: To Madame Gordon, Chairwoman of the Women's International Democratic Federation:

    We, the representatives attending the Second National Women's Congress of China, extend our warmest greetings to you and the Women's International Democratic Federation!

    At this congress, we summarized the achievements of the Chinese women's movement over the past four years and formulated the tasks of the women's movement during China's national construction period. We warmly support the convocation of the World Women's Congress and have elected a delegation representing the broad masses of women in our country to attend this congress. We, all the women of China, are determined to unite more closely under the banner of the Women's International Democratic Federation with hundreds of millions of women around the world and strive tirelessly to defend world peace and the rights of women and children.
        All Representatives of the Second National Women's Congress of China
                                April 23, 1953

### 中国第二次全国妇女代表大会全体代表  给国际民主妇女联合会的致敬电

1953-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中国第二次全国妇女代表大会全体代表
    给国际民主妇女联合会的致敬电
    【新华社二十四日讯】中国第二次全国妇女代表大会给国际民主妇女联合会的致敬电。全文如下：国际民主妇女联合会主席戈登夫人：
    我们——出席中国第二次全国妇女代表大会的代表们，向您和国际民主妇女联合会致以最亲切的敬礼！
    在这次大会上，我们总结了四年来中国妇女运动的成就，并订出了我国国家建设时期的妇女运动的任务。我们热烈地拥护世界妇女大会的召开，并已选出了能代表我国广大妇女的代表团出席这个大会。我们全中国妇女决心在国际民主妇女联合会的旗帜下，和全世界亿万妇女更加紧密地团结在一起，为保卫世界和平，保卫妇女儿童的权利而不倦地奋斗。
        中国第二次全国妇女代表大会全体代表
                                一九五三年四月二十三日
