Title: Czechoslovak Film Workers Delegation Heads to Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1953-04-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, April 18th: The Czechoslovak Republic film artists delegation of six people, left Tianjin for Nanjing at noon on the 18th. More than one hundred representatives from all circles in Tianjin went to the station to see them off.

The distinguished guests arrived in Tianjin on the 16th. They visited the state-owned Tianjin No. 1 Cotton Mill, Tianjin University, the Workers' Theater, and cinemas showing Czechoslovak Republic films to meet with the people of Tianjin. More than 4,000 textile workers and steelworkers held a grand welcoming ceremony in the auditorium of the state-owned Tianjin No. 1 Cotton Mill, gathering with them in brotherly friendship.

### 捷电影工作者代表团去南京

1953-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    捷电影工作者代表团去南京
    【新华社天津十八日电】捷克斯洛伐克共和国电影艺术工作者代表团一行六人，十八日中午由天津去南京。津市各界代表一百多人前往车站欢送。
    贵宾们在十六日来到天津。他们曾先后到国营天津第一棉纺织厂、天津大学、工人剧院及正在上演捷克斯洛伐克共和国影片的电影院和天津人民会面。四千多名纺织工人和钢铁工人在国营天津第一棉纺织厂礼堂举行盛大的欢迎会，以兄弟般的友情和他们欢聚。
