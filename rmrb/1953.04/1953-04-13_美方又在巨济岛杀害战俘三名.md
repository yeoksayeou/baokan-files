Title: The US Side Murdered Three More Prisoners of War on Koje Island
Author:
Date: 1953-04-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 12th. Following the murder of one of our captured personnel by the US side at the Nonsan POW camp on the 7th, according to the US side, on the 9th, the US side created two more incidents in the Koje Island POW camp, killing three of our captured personnel.

### 美方又在巨济岛杀害战俘三名

1953-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    美方又在巨济岛杀害战俘三名
    【新华社开城十二日电】继七日美方在论山战俘营谋害我方被俘人员一人后，据美方透露，九日在巨济岛战俘营内，美方又制造两次事件，杀害我方被俘人员三人。
