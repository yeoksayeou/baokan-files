Title: Philippine People's Liberation Army Continues Attacks
Author:
Date: 1953-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Manila: The Associated Press in Manila revealed: The Philippine People's Liberation Army launched an attack in southern Luzon on the evening of March 29th, defeating the Quirino puppet army. The People's Liberation Army attacked the barracks of the puppet army in Maragondon City, thirty miles southwest of Manila, and ambushed a puppet army patrol team in southern Luzon. According to figures released by the Philippine puppet government's Ministry of National Defense, which were obviously reduced, the puppet army suffered a total of nine casualties in these two battles.

### 菲律宾解放军不断出袭

1953-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾解放军不断出袭
    【新华社讯】美联社马尼拉消息透露：菲律宾人民解放军在三月二十九日晚在吕宋南部出击，打败了季里诺傀儡军。人民解放军在马尼拉西南方三十英里的玛拉银崙城进攻傀儡军的兵营，并在吕宋南部伏击一队傀儡军巡逻队。根据菲律宾傀儡政府国防部公布的显然缩小了的数字，傀儡军在这两场战斗中共死伤九人。
