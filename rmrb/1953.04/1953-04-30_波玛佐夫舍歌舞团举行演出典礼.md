Title: The Mazowsze Polish Song and Dance Ensemble Holds Performance Premiere

Author:
Date: 1953-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The musical dance performance of the Mazowsze Polish Song and Dance Ensemble, sponsored by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, grandly opened at the Beijing Theater on the afternoon of the 29th.

Present at the opening ceremony were Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Vice Ministers Ding Xilin, Ma Xulun, Minister of Higher Education, Wei Que, Vice Minister of Education, as well as well-known Chinese artists, heads of various literary and artistic organizations, and representatives from various government agencies and organizations, totaling more than 1,000 people.

Also present at the opening ceremony were Kiriloek, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Poland to China, and embassy staff.

The ceremony began with the Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble singing the national anthems of China, Poland, and the Soviet Union.

Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, delivered the opening speech, saying: We are excited to welcome the Mazowsze Polish Song and Dance Ensemble to perform in our country, and we wish the song and dance ensemble success in its visit and performance in our country. The Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble has performed in the great Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries and has been warmly welcomed, establishing a lofty reputation. This song and dance ensemble not only successfully promotes the excellent traditions of Polish national art, but also the programs it performs have the most precious artistic characteristics of youth, joy, and beauty. These programs are a portrayal of the new life of the Polish people who are moving towards socialism, and they are also the most welcomed and appreciated by the Chinese people today. Our Chinese literary and art workers should learn from every fraternal country, learn their art with national characteristics and folk flavors, and also learn their advanced experience in organizing and creating these arts. There is no doubt that the performance of the Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble will be of particular value to our assistance.

Jan Niwinsiki, Head of the Mazowsze Polish Song and Dance Ensemble, said at the opening ceremony: It is a great happiness for us to come to the People's Republic of China this time. We want to know your great country, your prosperous life and splendid culture. You are our model in the heroic construction struggle for peace and happiness. We are honored to be able to show the Chinese people some parts of Polish folk art. We also very much hope to come into contact with China's excellent, long-standing folk art and the achievements of China's new modern art creation. The Polish party, government, and people believe that the visit and performance of the Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble in China will further strengthen the friendship between the Polish and Chinese people who are united under the thoughts of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin.

The performance of the Mazowsze Polish Song and Dance Ensemble at the opening ceremony was a great success. In the performance, the song and dance ensemble sang "Ode to Stalin," "The East is Red," and "Song of Beirut" with majestic singing and reverent feelings; and performed many beautiful folk art programs in the form of collective singing and collective dance. Twelve folk songs were sung today, all of which have been artistically processed by Polish musicians, which shows the new direction of Polish folk art, including "In the Kadydlo Forest", "Rural Girl", "How Difficult", "Bachelor", "Guests are Coming", "Marusha's Wedding", "Little Bird", "Under the Pine Tree", etc., some of the songs are cheerful and lively, vividly expressing the healthy love of young men and women in rural areas, and some are vast and strong, fully reflecting the life and beautiful fantasies of the working people. Segtinsky's "Youth March" praises the Polish youth's struggle to build socialism with unparalleled power. The song and dance troupe performed seven folk dances that have been adapted and arranged, including "Olenjer," "Zuraw," "Povurnek," and "Mazurka Dance." These programs have lively and vigorous dance postures, many changes in formation, and very beautiful composition, fully expressing the happy and joyful life of the Polish working people. These programs performed by the song and dance ensemble were warmly welcomed by the audience. During and after the performance of the song and dance ensemble, more than 100 young literary and art workers in the capital presented flowers to all the members of the song and dance ensemble to thank them for their outstanding performance.

### 波玛佐夫舍歌舞团举行演出典礼

1953-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    波玛佐夫舍歌舞团举行演出典礼
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央人民政府文化部主办的波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团音乐舞蹈演出，二十九日下午在北京剧场隆重举行开幕典礼。
    出席开幕典礼的有中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，副部长丁西林，高等教育部部长马叙伦，教育部副部长韦悫和我国著名艺术家，各文艺团体负责人，各机关、团体代表等共一千多人。
    出席开幕典礼的还有波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克和大使馆人员。
    典礼在玛佐夫舍歌舞团高唱中国、波兰、苏联三国国歌声中开始。
    中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林致开幕词，他说：我们怀着兴奋的心情来欢迎波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团在我国的演出，并预祝歌舞团在我国访问和演出得到成功。玛佐夫舍歌舞团曾经在伟大的苏联和各人民民主国家演出而受到热烈的欢迎，树立了崇高的声誉。这个歌舞团不但成功地发扬了波兰民族艺术的优秀传统，而且它所演出的节目都具有年青、愉快、美丽等艺术上最宝贵的特征。这些节目都是正在走向社会主义的波兰人民新生活的写照，也是今天中国人民所最欢迎和能够欣赏的。我们中国文艺工作者要向每一个兄弟国家学习，学习他们具有民族特色和民间风味的艺术，同时也学习他们整理和创造这些艺术的先进经验。毫无疑问，玛佐夫舍歌舞团的演出，对于我们的帮助将是特别宝贵的。
    波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团团长潘努夫尼克在开幕典礼上讲话说：这次能来到中华人民共和国，对我们来说是一种巨大的幸福。我们想认识你们的伟大的国家，认识你们的繁荣的生活和灿烂的文化。在争取和平幸福的英勇建设斗争中，你们是我们的榜样。我们感到光荣的，是我们能使中国人民看到波兰民间艺术的某些部分。我们也非常希望能接触到中国的优秀的历史悠久的民间艺术和中国新的现代艺术创作上的成就。波兰的党、政府和人民都相信玛佐夫舍歌舞团在中国的访问和演出，将进一步加强在马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林思想下团结一致的波中两国人民的友谊。
    波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团在开幕典礼上的演出获得极大的成功。在表演中，歌舞团以雄壮的歌声和崇敬的感情歌唱了“斯大林颂”、“东方红”、“贝鲁特之歌”；又用集体歌唱和集体舞蹈的方式演出了许多优美的民间艺术节目。今天演唱的民歌有十二个，都是经波兰人民音乐家的艺术加工的，这显示出波兰民间艺术的新方向，其中“在卡吉德洛森林里”、“农村姑娘”、“好难哪”、“单身汉”、“客人来了”、“玛露霞的婚礼”、“小鸟”、“在松树下”等，有的歌声明朗、愉快，生动地表现着农村青年男女的健康的爱情，有的情调辽阔、健壮，充分反映了劳动人民的生活气息和美丽的幻想。塞格廷斯基作曲的“青春进行曲”，歌颂了波兰青年们以无比强大的力量为建设社会主义的斗争。歌舞团表演了经过改编整理的“奥连捷尔”、“茹拉夫”、“波伏尔涅克”、“玛茹卡舞”等七个民间舞蹈。这些节目舞姿活泼、矫健，队形变化很多，构图非常美丽，充分表现波兰劳动人民生活的幸福愉快。歌舞团表演的这些节目都为观众所热烈欢迎。在歌舞团的表演和表演结束后，曾有首都青年文艺工作者一百多人向歌舞团全体人员献花，以感谢他们的卓越的表演。
