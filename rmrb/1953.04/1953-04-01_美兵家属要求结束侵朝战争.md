Title: American Soldiers' Families Demand End to War of Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 23rd (TASS): According to the "Daily Worker," Florence Kogel, chairman of the Illinois "Save Our Sons Committee," published some excerpts from the nine hundred letters she received. These letters were sent by American soldiers' relatives and parents from all over the United States, all of whom support the committee's efforts to end the Korean War.

    De. E. from Sutherland, Iowa, wrote: "We mothers must do everything in our power to end this terrible war."

    The parents of a soldier from a small town in Colorado wrote in a letter: "You have done a brilliant job for the peace movement. I hope your work continues and your strength continues to grow. The greatest movement in the world is the peace movement."

    A letter from Delavan, Wisconsin, said: "I just saw news of your committee in the 'Chicago Daily News.' I would be very willing if I could do something to stop this massacre. I know there are more mothers waiting for someone to tell them how to help this movement." A group called "World Brotherhood" wrote in a letter from a small town in New Hampshire: "We wholeheartedly support your efforts." An elderly printer, 75 years old, said in a letter: "What our troops did in Korea in the past and are still doing there now is a criminal act. In my opinion, the Korean War is the most shameful stain in American history."

### 美兵家属要求结束侵朝战争

1953-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    美兵家属要求结束侵朝战争
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社纽约二十三日讯：据“工人日报”报道，美国伊利诺斯州“拯救我们的儿子委员会”主席弗洛伦斯·柯基尔公布了她所收到的九百封信件的一些摘录。这些信件是美国各处的美军亲属及父母寄来的，他们都支持这个委员会为结束朝鲜战争而作的努力。
    衣阿华州苏塞兰的德·埃写道：“我们做母亲的一定尽我们所有的力量来结束这个可怕的战争。”
    一个士兵的父母从科罗拉多州一个小城镇发的信中说：“你们为和平运动已做了辉煌的工作。我盼望你们的工作坚持下去，你们的力量不断增强。全世界最伟大的运动就是和平运动。”
    从威斯康星州迪拉梵寄出的一封信说：“我刚从‘芝加哥每日新闻’报上看到你们委员会的消息。如果我能做点事情来阻止这场屠杀，我将是非常愿意的。我知道还有更多的母亲，她们正在等待着有人能告诉她们如何来帮助这个运动。”一个名为“世界兄弟会”的团体从新罕布什尔州的一个小城镇发的信说：“我们全心全意地支持你们的努力。”一位七十五岁的老年印刷工人在信中说：“我们军队过去在朝鲜干的和现在仍在那里干的都是犯罪行为。照我的看法，朝鲜战争是美国历史上最可耻的污点。”
