Title: Agreement Reached on the Transportation and Handover of Sick and Wounded Prisoners of War in Korean Armistice Negotiations Between Liaison Group Officers of Both Sides
Author:
Date: 1953-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, April 13th: The Korean Armistice Negotiation Delegation of the Korean and Chinese side issued the following communique on April 13th:

    At the liaison group officers meeting between both sides on April 13th, our side informed the other side that among the non-Korean sick and wounded prisoners of war that our side is about to repatriate, in addition to sick and wounded prisoners of war of American, British, Turkish, Canadian, Greek, and Dutch nationalities, there are also sick and wounded prisoners of war of Filipino, South African, Australian and Colombian nationalities.

    Both sides discussed certain specific administrative details in the process of transporting and handing over the sick and wounded prisoners of war at the meeting, and reached an agreement.

### 朝鲜停战谈判双方联络组军官会议关于病伤战俘的运送与交接问题取得协议

1953-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判双方联络组军官会议关于病伤战俘的运送与交接问题取得协议
    【新华社开城十三日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团四月十三日发表公报如下：
    四月十三日的双方联络组军官会议上，我方通知对方：在我方即将遣返的非朝鲜籍病伤被俘人员中，除包括美国、英国、土耳其、加拿大、希腊、荷兰籍的病伤被俘人员外，并包括菲律宾、南非洲、澳大利亚与哥伦比亚籍的病伤被俘人员。
    双方在会上就病伤被俘人员的运送与交接过程中的某些具体行政细节问题进行了讨论，并取得了协议。
