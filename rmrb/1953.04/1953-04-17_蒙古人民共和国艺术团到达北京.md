Title: Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-04-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 16th: The Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic, led by its head Zhanbale Daoerji, arrived in Beijing on the 16th for a visit to our country.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government; Zhao Feng, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture; Lai Yali, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; He Ying, Deputy Director; Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Government Administration Council's Culture and Education Committee; Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and figures from the cultural and artistic circles such as Cao Yu, Chen Qitong, Wang Chaowen, Jin Ziguang, and representatives of literary and art workers, workers, and students in the capital, totaling more than 1,500 people.

Jalkhasaikhan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the station.

As the special train carrying the Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic arrived, cheers erupted at the station. More than a hundred Young Pioneers enthusiastically presented flowers to Zhanbale Daoerji, the head of the art troupe, and all its members, to express their respect.

A welcome rally was then held at the station.

Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, delivered a welcome speech. Ding Xilin first extended a warm welcome to the Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic, led by Zhanbale Daoerji, which included national people's actors, national meritorious actors, famous writers, composers, painters, and outstanding art workers, to the capital of our country. He said that the Chinese people have always been concerned about the remarkable achievements of our close friendly country, the Mongolian People's Republic, in building its motherland and advancing towards the socialist road, and love the vigorously developing national art of the Mongolian people. Ding Xilin pointed out that through the visit and artistic performances of the Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic in our capital and major cities across the country, we will not only learn more intimately about the great achievements of the Mongolian people's national art under the leadership of their great leader, Prime Minister Tsedenbal, but also, due to this visit and performance, the cultural and artistic exchanges between China and Mongolia will be further developed, and the friendly cooperation between the Chinese and Mongolian people in the cause of defending world peace will be further strengthened and consolidated.

Zhanbale Daoerji, head of the Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic, delivered a reply, first representing the Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic in extending sincere and warm respect to the people of the new China's capital, Chinese art workers, and the great Chinese people. He said that the art workers and people of the Mongolian People's Republic sincerely rejoice in the great achievements of our friends, the great Chinese people, under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong. We, the art workers and people of the Mongolian People's Republic, are watching with admiration the unprecedented prosperity of the great Chinese people's culture and art. Zhanbale Daoerji said that the friendship between the people of Mongolia and China is unbreakable, and this friendship is developed and consolidated on the basis of the welfare of the two peoples and the interests of the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union. He said that we, the representatives of the Mongolian People's Republic's art workers who have come to China, are sincerely delighted to be able to introduce the young art of our motherland to the Chinese people and to directly see the great achievements of the People's Democratic China. ### 蒙古人民共和国艺术团到达北京

1953-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国艺术团到达北京
    【新华社十六日讯】应邀前来我国访问的蒙古人民共和国艺术团在团长占巴乐道尔基率领下，在十六日到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林，文化部办公厅副主任赵沨，外交部办公厅副主任赖亚力，外交部亚洲司司长陈家康，副司长何英，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，外交部交际处副处长沈平，和文化艺术界人士曹禺、陈其通、王朝闻、金紫光等，以及首都文艺工作者、工人、学生的代表共一千五百余人。
    蒙古人民共和国驻我国特命全权大使贾尔卡赛汗和使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
    当蒙古人民共和国艺术团所乘的专车到达时，车站上响起一片欢声，百余名带红领巾的少年儿童队员热情地向艺术团团长占巴乐道尔基和全体团员献花致敬。
    接着车站上举行了欢迎大会。
    中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林致欢迎词。丁西林首先对以占巴乐道尔基为首的包括国家人民演员、国家功勋演员、名作家、作曲家、画家和优秀的艺术工作者的蒙古人民共和国艺术团的来到我国首都，表示热烈的欢迎。他说：中国人民一直关心着我们亲密的友邦蒙古人民共和国在建设自己祖国向社会主义道路上迈进的各方面卓越的成就，并且热爱蒙古人民蓬勃发展的民族艺术。丁西林指出：通过这次蒙古人民共和国艺术团在我国首都和全国各大城市的访问和艺术表演，我们将不但更亲切地了解到蒙古人民在其伟大领袖泽登巴尔总理领导下的民族艺术的伟大成就，并且由于这次访问和演出，中蒙两国文化艺术上的交流将更进一步发展，中蒙两国人民在保卫世界和平事业中的友好合作也将更进一步的加强和巩固。
    蒙古人民共和国艺术团团长占巴乐道尔基致答词，他首先代表蒙古人民共和国艺术团向新中国首都的人民、中国艺术工作者和伟大的中国人民致以衷心的热烈的敬意。他说：蒙古人民共和国艺术工作者和蒙古人民真诚地为我们的朋友伟大的中国人民在毛泽东主席英明的领导下获得巨大的成就感到欣喜。我们蒙古人民共和国艺术工作者和蒙古人民怀着赞扬的心情注视着伟大中国人民文化艺术空前未有的繁荣。占巴乐道尔基说：蒙中两国人民的友谊是牢不可破的，这种友谊是以两国人民的福利和以苏联为首的和平民主阵营的利益为基础而发展巩固起来的。他说：我们来到中国的蒙古人民共和国艺术工作者代表们能够把自己祖国年青的艺术介绍给中国人民，能够直接看到人民民主中国伟大的成就，感到衷心的喜悦。（附图片）
    应邀前来我国作访问演出的蒙古人民共和国艺术团在四月十六日到达北京。北京各界人民在车站举行盛大欢迎会，前排左起第八人为蒙古人民共和国艺术团团长占巴乐道尔基。新华社记者齐观山摄
