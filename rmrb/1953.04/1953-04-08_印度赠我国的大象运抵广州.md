Title: Indian Elephant Gift to China Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1953-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 7th -- The elephant gifted to China by the Prime Minister of India Nehru on behalf of the Indian government, escorted by its keeper Ali, arrived in Guangzhou on the 6th, and will be transported to Beijing when the weather is slightly warmer. This fourteen-year-old elephant is named "Asa" and weighs approximately one and a half tons.

### 印度赠我国的大象运抵广州

1953-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    印度赠我国的大象运抵广州
    【新华社广州七日电】印度总理尼赫鲁代表印度政府赠送给我国的一只大象，由它的饲养者阿里护送于六日运抵广州，待天气稍暖时再运往北京。这只十四龄的象名叫“阿萨”，体重约一吨半。
