Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-04-29
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The broad masses of workers and staff in major and medium-sized cities across the country are enthusiastically launching labor competitions. The Li Jinquan team of the repair department of the Beijing Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant completed the state production plan for April ten days ahead of schedule. The workers are here vying to see the good news report.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各大、中城市的广大职工正在热烈展开劳动竞赛。北京石景山钢铁厂修理部李金泉小组提前十天完成了四月份国家生产计划。工人们正在这里争看喜报。
