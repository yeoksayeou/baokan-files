Title: Delegation from the Chinese Academy of Sciences Continues Visit in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-04-24
Page: 4

    Delegation from the Chinese Academy of Sciences Continues Visit in Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 22nd: According to TASS Moscow, April 22nd, the delegation from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, after returning to Moscow from Kyiv, continues to visit Moscow and the activities of scientific institutions. The delegates visited the Kremlin and the Museum of the Revolution. On the 21st, they visited the Soviet Academy of Sciences again, where the Vice-President of the Academy, V. Volgin, introduced them to the publication of Soviet scientific books. Volgin pointed out that the total number of scientific books published in the Soviet Union has increased twenty-five times compared to before the October Revolution. The number of scientific books published in the post-war years alone has increased threefold. There are more than forty kinds of scientific journals published in the Soviet Union.
    The Chinese guests were very interested in a recent book exhibition held by the Publishing Bureau of the Soviet Academy of Sciences.

### 我科学院访苏代表团  继续在莫斯科参观

1953-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我科学院访苏代表团
    继续在莫斯科参观
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：中国科学院访苏代表团由基辅回到莫斯科后，继续参观莫斯科的生活和科学机关的活动。代表们参观了克里姆林宫和革命博物馆。他们在二十一日再度访问了苏联科学院，由科学院副院长维·沃尔京向他们介绍苏联科学书籍出版的情况。沃尔京指出：现在苏联出版的科学书籍的总数量已增加为十月革命前的二十五倍。单是在战后这几年内出版的科学书籍，其数量就增加到三倍。在苏联发行的各种科学杂志计有四十多种。
    中国客人对苏联科学院出版局最近举行的一次书籍展览会很感兴趣。
