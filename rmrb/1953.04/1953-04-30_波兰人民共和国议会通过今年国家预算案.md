Title: Polish People's Republic Parliament Passes This Year's National Budget

Author:

Date: 1953-04-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 28th: According to the Polish Press Agency, the Parliament of the Polish People's Republic held its second session from the 25th to the 27th. The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Bolesław Bierut, participated in the meeting.

After the opening of the meeting, all members of parliament stood in silence to mourn J.V. Stalin. Then they mourned K. Gottwald.

The Minister of Finance, T. Dietrich, delivered a report on the 1953 national budget at the meeting. The report pointed out that the budget stipulates further industrialization of Poland on a higher technical basis and a significant increase in the production of consumer goods. Nearly 50 percent of the budget expenditure is for national economic construction, and about 25 percent is for social and cultural measures.

After three days of heated discussion, the parliament unanimously adopted the 1953 national budget on the 27th.

Before the closing of the parliament, and amid prolonged and enthusiastic applause, the decree issued by the Council of Ministers on March 7, renaming the city of Katowice to Stalinogród and renaming the province of Katowice to Stalinogród province to commemorate Comrade Stalin forever, was unanimously approved.

### 波兰人民共和国议会通过今年国家预算案

1953-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰人民共和国议会
    通过今年国家预算案
    【新华社二十八日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰人民共和国议会在二十五日至二十七日举行了第二次会议。波兰部长会议主席波·贝鲁特参加了会议。
    会议开幕后，全体议员肃立，为约·维·斯大林默哀。接着他们又为克·哥特瓦尔德默哀。
    财政部长塔·迪特里赫在会议上作了关于一九五三年国家预算案的报告。报告指出，预算案规定在更高的技术基础上使波兰进一步工业化，并大大增加消费品的产量。预算支出中差不多有百分之五十是国民经济建设方面的经费，约有百分之二十五是社会文化措施的经费。
    议会在经过三天的热烈讨论后，二十七日一致通过了一九五三年的国家预算案。
    议会闭幕前，并在长时间的热烈掌声中，一致批准了部长会议在三月七日颁发的将卡托维兹市改名为斯大林诺格罗德市和将卡托维兹省改名为斯大林诺格罗德省以示永远纪念斯大林同志的法令。
