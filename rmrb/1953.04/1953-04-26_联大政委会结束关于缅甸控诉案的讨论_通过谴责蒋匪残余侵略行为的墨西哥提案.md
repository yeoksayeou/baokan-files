Title: UN General Assembly Political Committee Concludes Discussion on Burma's Complaint; Passes Mexican Proposal Condemning Chiang Kai-shek Remnants' Acts of Aggression
Author:
Date: 1953-04-26
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, New York, April 23rd) According to TASS, New York, April 23rd: The Political Committee of the United Nations General Assembly concluded its discussion on April 22nd regarding the Burmese government's complaint against the aggression of the Kuomintang troops in Burma.

	Indian representative Menon expressed strong support for Burma's position. Menon cited newspaper reports pointing out that just two days earlier, a new force had invaded Burma, aiming to establish a second front to aid the troops under pressure from Burmese forces. Menon denounced the Kuomintang's "blatant and unscrupulous acts of aggression." Menon sharply criticized the speech of the Kuomintang member Chiang Ting-fu. He finally requested the General Assembly to adopt the Burmese proposal.

	Burmese representative U Myint Thein then spoke, saying: It is clear that all representatives participating in the discussion know that the Kuomintang is indeed guilty of aggression. He continued: Nevertheless, it is apparent that the governments of certain countries still want to "turn a blind eye to the plunder of the aggressors" because they are unwilling to harm their relations with Chiang Kai-shek. U Myint Thein, referring to Chiang Ting-fu's complaint that Burma adopted a "non-neighborly" attitude towards the Chiang Kai-shek group, said that as for Burma's attitude, we must emphasize that since Chiang Kai-shek became unpopular in China and was driven out of mainland China by the Chinese people, the Burmese government believes that if Burma is to adopt a "neighborly attitude" towards the Chinese people, Burma must recognize the legitimate government of the Chinese people. U Myint Thein concluded by saying that Burma would be very disappointed if it did not receive the support it needed to stop the aggressors and their supporters.

	Chairman Muniz then announced that the general discussion was over and the committee began to study the proposals of Burma, Argentina and Mexico separately.

	The Mexican representative explained the provisions of his slightly modified proposal. The Mexican proposal originally "demanded" that the foreign troops in Burma be disarmed, and then detained or withdrawn, but now it was changed to "declare that these foreign troops must be disarmed," and then detained or withdrawn.

	At the same time, Argentina and Chile jointly put forward an amendment to the Mexican proposal, advocating adding another paragraph to it.

	Peruvian representative Belaunde requested the adoption of the Mexican proposal as amended by Argentina and Chile, and believed that the General Assembly should first call on both parties to resolve the issue before issuing a condemnation.

	The Egyptian representative supported the Mexican proposal.

	The Argentine representative then announced the withdrawal of the Argentine proposal.

	The representatives of South Africa, New Zealand and Turkey also supported the Mexican proposal and the amendments to it.

	Soviet delegation leader Vyshinsky spoke. He first pointed out that even Chiang Kai-shek's supporters cited facts to prove Chiang Kai-shek's acts of aggression. Then he said that the Soviet delegation firmly supported the Burmese proposal. Vyshinsky said that the Mexican proposal had some shortcomings, and the amendments of Argentina and Chile had even greater shortcomings. Because the amendments of Argentina and Chile even referred to the Kuomintang group as the "Chinese government." Moreover, this amendment did not stipulate that the Kuomintang bandits who invaded Burmese territory should be disarmed before they were withdrawn.

	The delegations of Poland, Sweden, Lebanon and other countries supported Vyshinsky's speech

### 联大政委会结束关于缅甸控诉案的讨论  通过谴责蒋匪残余侵略行为的墨西哥提案

1953-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    联大政委会结束关于缅甸控诉案的讨论
    通过谴责蒋匪残余侵略行为的墨西哥提案
    【新华社二十四日讯】据塔斯社纽约二十三日讯：联合国大会政治委员会四月二十二日结束了关于缅甸政府控诉国民党军队侵略缅甸案的讨论。
    印度代表梅农表示极力支持缅甸的态度。梅农援引报纸的消息指出：仅在两天以前，一支新部队侵入缅甸，目的是要建立第二条战线以援救受到缅甸部队压力的军队。梅农斥责国民党的这种“公然的、肆无忌惮的侵略行为”。梅农尖锐地抨击国民党分子蒋廷黻的演说。他最后要求大会通过缅甸提案。
    缅甸代表吴敏登接着发言，他说：很明显，所有参加讨论的代表都知道国民党是的确犯有侵略罪行的。他继续说：尽管如此，显然某些国家的政府还是想“对侵略者的这种掠夺行为采取不闻不问的态度”，因为它们不愿意损害它们与蒋介石之间的关系。吴敏登谈到蒋廷黻埋怨缅甸对于蒋介石集团采取“非睦邻”的态度时说，至于缅甸的态度，我们必须强调指出，自从蒋介石在中国不得人心、并且被中国人民赶出中国大陆以后，缅甸政府就认为假使缅甸要对中国人民采取“睦邻态度”，缅甸就必需承认中国人民的合法政府。吴敏登最后说，假使缅甸没有得到它所需要的支持来制止侵略者及其支持者的话，缅甸将会感到很失望。
    主席慕尼兹然后宣布一般讨论至此结束，委员会开始分别研究缅甸、阿根廷和墨西哥的提案。
    墨西哥代表说明他的稍经修改的提案的各项规定。墨西哥提案原来“要求”在缅甸的外国军队解除武装，随后予以拘禁或撤退，现在则改为“宣布这些外国军队必须解除武装”，然后予以拘禁或撤退。
    同时，阿根廷和智利联合提出了对墨西哥提案的修正案，主张在其中再补充一段。
    秘鲁代表贝朗德要求通过经过阿根廷和智利修正的墨西哥提案，并认为大会在发出谴责之前，首先应该呼吁双方解决这个问题。
    埃及代表支持墨西哥的提案。
    阿根廷代表接着宣布撤回阿根廷的提案。
    南非、新西兰和土耳其代表也支持墨西哥提案及对这个提案的修正案。
    苏联代表团团长维辛斯基发言。他首先指出，甚至连蒋介石的支持者也举出事实证明蒋介石的侵略行为。接着他说，苏联代表团坚决支持缅甸的提案。维辛斯基说，墨西哥的提案本来就有一些缺点，阿根廷和智利的修正案的缺点更大。因为阿根廷和智利的修正案竟把国民党集团称之为“中国政府”。而且，这个修正案没有规定在撤退侵占缅甸领土的国民党匪帮之前，先把他们解除武装。
    波兰、瑞典、黎巴嫩和其他一些国家的代表团支持维辛斯基的发言。
    瑞典代表对阿根廷和智利修正案提出责问，并指出瑞典政府承认中华人民共和国中央人民政府是中国唯一合法的政府。他还指出，这个修正案并未提到在撤退侵略缅甸的国民党匪帮之前，必须先把他们解除武装。
    阿根廷和智利代表又修改了他们的修正案，删去了受到批评的几点。
    黎巴嫩代表提出了好几个修正案请政治委员会考虑，这些修正案显著地改善了墨西哥提案，使这个提案更加明确了。这些修正案特别规定要谴责国民党匪帮对缅甸的敌对的侵略行为。尽管美国和英国以及一些拉丁美洲国家的代表提出反对，但是委员会还是通过了黎巴嫩代表团的修正案。苏联、白俄罗斯、乌克兰、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克代表团以及亚洲阿拉伯国家和一些拉丁美洲国家的代表团赞成这些修正案。
    接着便对墨西哥提案进行表决。赞成这个提案的有五十八票，其中包括苏联、乌克兰、白俄罗斯、波兰和捷克斯洛伐克。缅甸代表和国民党分子弃权。
