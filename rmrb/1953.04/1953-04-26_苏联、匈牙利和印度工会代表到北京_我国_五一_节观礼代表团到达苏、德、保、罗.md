Title: Soviet, Hungarian, and Indian Trade Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing / Chinese "May Day" Delegation Arrives in the Soviet Union, Germany, Bulgaria, and Romania
Author:
Date: 1953-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and the Seventh National Labor Congress, the Soviet and Hungarian trade union delegations arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 25th. Zhu Xuefan and Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Jiebo, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, Wang Xuezeng, Vice-Chairman, Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Textile Trade Union, Xu Xin, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union, Zhou Xifeng, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Machinery Manufacturing Trade Union, Jiang Tao, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Chemical Industry Trade Union, and Xu Zhizhen, Yang Zhihua, Dong Xin, Li Xian, Fang Ming, and Zhuang Yan, heads of various departments of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, as well as Thornton and Kutzmink of the Asia-Australia Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, were present at the airport to welcome them.

Also present to welcome them were Soviet Ambassador Kuznetsov, Hungarian Embassy Counselor Shibka, and embassy staff.

List of the Soviet Trade Union Delegation:
	Head: I. V. Goroshkin, Member of the Secretariat of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions
	P. Ye. Barmasov, Chairman of the Sverdlovsk City Trade Union
	T. A. Tashirova, Chairman of the State Trade Union Committee of the Uzbek Republic
	I. T. Soloviev, Train Driver, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
	L. I. Ananyeva, Textile Worker, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
	D. M. Orudzheva, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Azerbaijan Republic Primary and Secondary School Trade Union
	A. G. Jiuzin, Staff Member of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions
	A. Masko, Staff Member of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions

List of the Hungarian Trade Union Delegation:
	Head: Zento Erno, Member of the Secretariat of the Hungarian Trade Union Central Council
	Borozki Ferenc, International Secretary-General of the Chemical Workers' Union

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and the Seventh National Labor Congress, the first group of five Indian trade union representatives arrived in Beijing by train at 11:00 p.m. on the 25th. Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Jiebo, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, Qiu Jin, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Trade Union, Song Chuan, Deputy Director of the Working Committee of the Agricultural, Forestry, and Water Conservancy Trade Union of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others were present at the station to welcome them.

List of the First Group of Indian Trade Union Representatives:
	Sibnath Banerjee, President of the Indian Labor Association
	Sudin Kumar Pramanik, Member of the Presidium of the Indian United Trade Union Congress
	Trivedi Parmanand, Member of the Presidium of the Indian United Trade Union Congress
	Beswarat Dube, Member of the Presidium of the Indian United Trade Union Congress
	Shyam Keshan Parmanand, Member of the Working Committee of the Indian United Trade Union Congress

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 25th: The delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, led by Cai Shufan, which was invited by the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" celebrations in the Soviet Union, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 24th. An. Shevchenko, Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, responsible cadres of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, and representatives of the central committees of various trade unions were present at the airport to welcome them. Ge Baoquan, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, also welcomed them at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 25th: The Chinese trade union delegation to the German Democratic Republic "May Day" celebrations, headed by Xi Zhanyuan, Vice-Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, arrived here yesterday. The delegation was invited by the Free German Trade Union Federation. Adolf Det, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Free German Trade Union Federation, other personnel of the German Democratic Republic, and personnel from the diplomatic mission of China to the German Democratic Republic welcomed them at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th, Sofia Dispatch: The delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent to Bulgaria to participate in the "May Day" celebrations, led by Shao Ziyan, arrived in Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, by plane on the 24th. Ma. Kirilova, Secretary of the Central Council of the Bulgarian General Trade Union, and other representatives welcomed them at the airport. Personnel from the Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria also welcomed them at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th, Bucharest Dispatch: The delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent to Romania to participate in the "May Day" celebrations, led by Chen Yutian, arrived in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, by plane on the 24th. Representatives of the Central Council of the Romanian General Trade Union welcomed them at the airport.

### 苏联、匈牙利和印度工会代表到北京  我国“五一”节观礼代表团到达苏、德、保、罗

1953-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联、匈牙利和印度工会代表到北京
    我国“五一”节观礼代表团到达苏、德、保、罗
    【新华社二十五日讯】应邀前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝典礼和第七次全国劳动大会的苏联和匈牙利工会代表团，在二十五日下午乘飞机抵达北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、刘宁一，中国铁路工会全国委员会主席李颉伯、副主席王学增，中国纺织工会全国委员会主席陈少敏，中国邮电工会全国委员会副主席徐欣，中国机器制造业工会筹备委员会副主任周西风，中国化学工会筹备委员会主任江涛，以及中华全国总工会各部门负责人许之桢、杨之华、董昕、李仙、方明、庄焰等人，以及世界工会联合会亚澳联络局的桑顿和库茨明克。
