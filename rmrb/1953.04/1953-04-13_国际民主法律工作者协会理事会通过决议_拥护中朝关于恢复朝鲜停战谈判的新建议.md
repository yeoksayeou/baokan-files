Title: Council of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Adopts Resolution Supporting Sino-North Korean New Proposal on Restoring Korean Armistice Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1953-04-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 12th. The Council of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers held a meeting in Prague from the 8th to the 10th. The meeting adopted resolutions supporting the new proposals of the Chinese and Korean governments on restoring the Korean armistice negotiations, on convening an international conference to defend democratic rights and supporting the initiating committee organized by Giuseppe Nitti to convene this international conference, and on safeguarding the rights of mothers and women.

The meeting was opened by the President of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, Pritt. The Secretary-General of the Association, Nordmann, reported on the activities of the Council since the last meeting, discussing how some members of the Association are fulfilling their tasks in the struggle for world peace and people's rights. He also discussed the persecution of democratic legal workers in capitalist countries. The meeting also held extensive discussions on the Association's participation in the preparations for the international conference to defend democratic rights.

The resolution adopted by the meeting supporting the new proposal of the Chinese and Korean governments on restoring the Korean armistice negotiations stated: "The Council of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers warmly welcomes the proposals made by the governments of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on the issue of prisoners of war, which aim to immediately achieve a Korean armistice, which is conducive to the reconstruction of world peace, especially the reconstruction of peace in the Far East.

The Council welcomes the resumption of armistice negotiations; and calls upon the United Nations and all governments to accept and support this proposal in order to ensure the success of the current negotiations. The Council calls on legal workers in all countries around the world to use their talents and abilities to restore the principles of international law and ensure that these principles are respected, regarding the treatment and repatriation of prisoners of war; regarding aggression, war crimes and inhuman crimes, especially crimes caused by the use of bacteriological weapons and other weapons of mass destruction."

### 国际民主法律工作者协会理事会通过决议  拥护中朝关于恢复朝鲜停战谈判的新建议

1953-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

    国际民主法律工作者协会理事会通过决议
    拥护中朝关于恢复朝鲜停战谈判的新建议
    【新华社布拉格十二日电】国际民主法律工作者协会理事会八日到十日在布拉格举行会议。会议通过了拥护中国和朝鲜政府关于恢复朝鲜停战谈判的新建议、关于召开国际保卫民主权利会议和拥护由朱塞佩·尼蒂组织的召开这个国际会议的发起委员会、关于维护母亲和妇女的权利等决议。
    会议由国际民主法律工作者协会主席普里特致开会词。协会总书记诺尔曼报告理事会从上次会议以后的活动情况，他谈到协会一部分会员怎样在争取世界和平和人民权利的斗争中执行他们的任务。他又谈到资本主义国家迫害民主法律工作者的情形。会议并广泛讨论了关于协会参加筹备国际保卫民主权利会议的问题。
    会议通过的拥护中国和朝鲜政府关于恢复朝鲜停战谈判的新建议的决议说：“国际民主法律工作者协会理事会热烈欢迎中华人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府所提出的关于战俘问题的建议，这个建议的目的在于立即实现朝鲜停战，而朝鲜停战是有利于重建世界和平，特别是重建远东和平的。
    理事会欢迎恢复停战谈判；并要求联合国及各国政府接受和支持这个建议，以便保证目前的谈判得到成功。理事会向全世界各国的法律工作者呼吁，请他们运用他们的才干和能力来恢复国际法原则，并使这些原则受到尊重，这些原则是关于战俘的待遇和遣返；关于侵略、战争罪行和不人道的罪行，特别是使用细菌武器和其他大规模杀人武器所造成的罪行。”
