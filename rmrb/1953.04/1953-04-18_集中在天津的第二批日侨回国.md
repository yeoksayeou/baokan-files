Title: The Second Group of Japanese Nationals Concentrated in Tianjin Returns Home

Author: 
Date: 1953-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 17th - The second group of 1,915 Japanese nationals concentrated in Tianjin Municipality who wished to return to their country left Tanggu New Port at 4 PM on the 17th aboard the Japanese ship "Takasago Maru" to return to Japan. Staff members from the Tianjin Branch of the Chinese Red Cross Society and the North China Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union went to the pier to see them off. The Japanese nationals expressed their deep gratitude to the Chinese people for their multifaceted care in assisting their return home.

### 集中在天津的第二批日侨回国

1953-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    集中在天津的第二批日侨回国
    【新华社天津十七日电】集中在天津市的第二批愿意回国的日本侨民一千九百一十五人，在十七日下午四时乘日本轮船“高砂丸”离开塘沽新港回日本。中国红十字会天津市分会和中国海员工会华北区委员会的工作人员，前往码头欢送。日本侨民对中国人民多方面照顾他们回国表示十分感激。
