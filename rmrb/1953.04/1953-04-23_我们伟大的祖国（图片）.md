Properly advancing the sowing time can prevent wheat diseases and pests. Tractor drivers at the Chahayang State-owned Mechanical Farm in Heilongjiang Province are driving "Stalin-80" tractors, pulling three seeders and three fertilizer spreaders, to sow wheat seventeen days earlier than last year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    适当地提前播种，可以防止小麦发生病虫害。黑龙江国营查哈阳机械农场的拖拉机手们，正驾驶着“斯大林八十号”拖拉机，牵引着三台播种机和三台播肥机，比去年提前十七天进行小麦播种。
