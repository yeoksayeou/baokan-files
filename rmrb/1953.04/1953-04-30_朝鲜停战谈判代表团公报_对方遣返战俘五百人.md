Title: Korean Armistice Negotiation Delegation Communiqué: Opposing Side Repatriates 500 Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1953-04-30
Page: 1

    Korean Armistice Negotiation Delegation Communiqué
    Opposing Side Repatriates 500 Prisoners of War
    【Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, April 29th】The Korean Armistice Negotiation Korean and Chinese delegation issued a communiqué on April 29th as follows:
    On April 29th, the opposing side repatriated 500 sick and wounded captured personnel of the Korean People's Army to our side at Panmunjom.

### 朝鲜停战谈判代表团公报  对方遣返战俘五百人

1953-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判代表团公报
    对方遣返战俘五百人
    【新华社开城二十九日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团四月二十九日发表公报如下：
    四月二十九日，对方在板门店遣返给我方朝鲜人民军病伤被俘人员五百人。
