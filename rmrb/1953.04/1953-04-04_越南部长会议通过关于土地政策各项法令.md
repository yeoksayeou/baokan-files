Title: Vietnamese Ministerial Council Passes Various Decrees on Land Policy
Author:
Date: 1953-04-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam Dispatch from Vietnam News Agency, March 3rd: The Vietnamese Ministerial Council held a meeting in mid-March, presided over by President Ho Chi Minh.  Participating in the meeting were Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly, Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, General Vo Nguyen Giap, and ministers and vice-ministers of various government ministries.

    The meeting heard reports from delegations sent by the government to the Northwest region, reports from delegations sent by the National Assembly and the Vietnamese National United Front to the Fourth Interzone, and a report from the Minister of Justice on the trial of reactionaries in Hung An Township, Nghe An Province, Fourth Interzone, for sabotaging the war of resistance. The meeting discussed a report on mobilizing the masses made by Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong.

    The meeting then discussed and passed various decrees on land policy and the work of mobilizing the masses.

### 越南部长会议通过关于土地政策各项法令

1953-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    越南部长会议通过关于土地政策各项法令
    【新华社三日讯】据越南通讯社北越讯：越南部长会议三月中旬在胡志明主席主持下举行会议。参加会议的有越南国民大会常务委员会主席孙德胜、副总理范文同、武元甲将军和政府各部部长和副部长。
    会议听取了政府派到西北地区去的代表团的报告、国民大会和越南国民联合战线派到第四联区去的代表团的报告、司法部长关于审判第四联区义安省兴安乡的反动分子破坏抗战的案件的报告。会议讨论了副总理范文同所作的关于发动群众的报告。
    会议接着讨论并通过了关于土地政策和发动群众工作的各项法令。
