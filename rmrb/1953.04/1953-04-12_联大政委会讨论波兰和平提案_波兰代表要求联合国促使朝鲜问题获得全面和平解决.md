Title: UN Political Committee Discusses Polish Peace Proposal: Polish Representative Calls on UN to Promote Comprehensive Peaceful Resolution of Korean Issue
Author:
Date: 1953-04-12
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, New York, April 10th: The United Nations General Assembly's Political Committee began discussing the Polish proposal on "Measures to Eliminate the Threat of Another World War and Strengthen International Peace and Friendly Cooperation" on the morning of April 9th. The Polish representative, Skrzeszewski, spoke to explain the Polish proposal. He emphasized that, in the current international situation, it is of particular importance for the United Nations to strive for a comprehensive peaceful resolution of the Korean issue, as suggested in the first part of the proposal. He said: The attitude of governments towards the Korean issue is the touchstone for people all over the world to judge the attitude of governments towards the issue of world peace. Skrzeszewski briefly explained the second part of the Polish proposal: urging the permanent members of the Security Council to reduce armaments and troops by one-third within one year, and to immediately and unconditionally prohibit atomic weapons. After elaborating on the third part of the proposal regarding the request to conclude a Five-Power Peace Pact, Skrzeszewski finally emphasized that all problems can be resolved through negotiation. The Soviet representative, Vyshinsky, then spoke, expressing his support for the Polish delegation's proposal.

### 联大政委会讨论波兰和平提案  波兰代表要求联合国促使朝鲜问题获得全面和平解决

1953-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    联大政委会讨论波兰和平提案
    波兰代表要求联合国促使朝鲜问题获得全面和平解决
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社纽约十日讯：联合国大会政治委员会在四月九日上午开始讨论波兰提出的“关于消除另一次世界大战威胁、加强国际和平与友好合作的措施”的提案。波兰代表斯克热歇夫斯基发言解释波兰提案，他着重指出，在目前国际情况下，联合国如按照提案第一部分所建议的那样，努力促使朝鲜问题获得全面的和平解决，是具有特殊重要意义的。他说：各国政府对朝鲜问题的态度，是世界各国人民判断各国政府对世界和平问题的态度的试金石。斯克热歇夫斯基扼要说明了波兰提案的第二部分：敦促安全理事会常任理事国在一年内裁减军备和军队三分之一，以及立即无条件地禁止原子武器。斯克热歇夫斯基在阐明了提案中关于要求缔结五大国和平公约的第三部分以后，最后强调指出一切问题都是能够通过谈判解决的。苏联代表维辛斯基接着发言，他表示支持波兰代表团的提案。
