Title: New Navigation Season Begins on Lenin Volga-Don Canal
Author:
Date: 1953-04-28
Page: 4

    New Navigation Season Begins on Lenin Volga-Don Canal
    [Xinhua News Agency, 25th] According to TASS, the new navigation season on the Lenin Volga-Don Canal has begun. The first ship to pass through the canal was the "Composer Varlamov." It departed from Krasnoarmeysk, passed through the Lenin Canal and the Tsimlyansk Reservoir, and successfully arrived in Rostov at noon on the 19th. In recent days, an increasing number of ships have been navigating the canal. The canal's navigation operations have been further automated, and new passenger routes have been opened. This year, passenger transport on this canal will increase threefold compared to last year, and freight transport will increase by nearly double.

### 列宁伏尔加河—顿河运河新航运季开始

1953-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    列宁伏尔加河—顿河运河新航运季开始
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社消息：列宁伏尔加河—顿河运河的新航运季已经开始。第一艘通过这条运河的轮船是“作曲家瓦拉莫夫号”。它从红军城出发，经过列宁运河、齐姆良海，于十九日正午顺利地抵达了罗斯多夫。这几天来，有愈来愈多的船只在运河上航行。运河的航运工作已经进一步自动化，新的客运线已经开辟了。今年这条运河的客运将比去年增加两倍，货运将比去年增加将近一倍。
