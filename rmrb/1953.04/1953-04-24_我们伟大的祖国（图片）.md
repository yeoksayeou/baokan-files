Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-04-24
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The construction of a new-style control gate at Zouma River Mouth in Sichuan is proceeding smoothly. After the completion of this gate, it will be able to more effectively control the flow of the Zouma River and the Puyang River, so as to strengthen the long-standing Dujiangyan irrigation project in Sichuan. Workers are binding steel bars on this newly built bridge gate.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    四川走马河口修建新式节制闸的工程正在顺利进行。这个闸修成以后，可以更有效地控制走马河和蒲阳河的流量，以加强历史悠久的四川都江堰的水利工程。工人们正在这个新建的桥闸上绑扎钢筋。
