Title: General Administration of Press and Publication Instructs National Bookstores to Do a Good Job in Supplying Books and Periodicals for Festivals

Author:

Date: 1953-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th—The Central People's Government's General Administration of Press and Publication instructed the national Xinhua Bookstore, International Bookstore, and China Book Distribution Company to uniformly implement the Central People's Government's Ministry of Commerce decision to cease the practice of state-owned commercial enterprises offering festival discounts. At the same time, it instructed each bookstore that on important festivals or anniversaries, they should still fully prepare the supply and promotion of books and periodicals, and should not weaken supply and promotion work due to the cancellation of festival discounts.

### 出版总署指示全国书店  做好节日书刊供应工作

1953-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    出版总署指示全国书店
    做好节日书刊供应工作
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府出版总署指示全国新华书店、国际书店和中国图书发行公司应一律执行中央人民政府商业部关于停止国营商业节日减价办法的决定。同时指示各该书店，每逢重要节日或纪念日，仍应充分做好节日书刊的供应工作和宣传工作，不应因取消节日减价，而削弱了供应和宣传工作。
