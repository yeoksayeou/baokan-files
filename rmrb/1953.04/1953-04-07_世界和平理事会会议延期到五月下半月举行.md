Title: World Peace Council Meeting Postponed to Second Half of May
Author:
Date: 1953-04-07
Page: 4

　　World Peace Council Meeting Postponed to Second Half of May
    [Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 6th] TASS, Budapest, 4th: Due to suggestions from peace supporters' organizations in some countries to postpone the World Peace Council meeting originally scheduled to open in Budapest on April 10th, the leadership of the World Peace Council has decided to postpone the meeting to the second half of May. The meeting location remains in Budapest.

### 世界和平理事会会议延期到五月下半月举行

1953-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会会议延期到五月下半月举行
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯四日讯：由于有些国家的和平拥护者的组织建议把原定在四月十日在布达佩斯开幕的世界和平理事会会议延期，世界和平理事会的领导机构已决定把这次会议延期到五月下半月举行，会议地址仍在布达佩斯。
