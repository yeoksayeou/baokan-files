Title: Korean Armistice Negotiation Delegates' Meeting Rescheduled for Tomorrow; Both Sides Continue to Repatriate Sick and Wounded POWs at Panmunjom
Author:
Date: 1953-04-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, April 24th: The Korean Armistice Negotiation Delegation of the Korean and Chinese side issued the following communique on April 24th:

　　The ninth meeting of the Liaison Group of both sides was held on April 24th. At the meeting, both sides agreed that, for administrative reasons, the meeting of the delegation of both sides would be held at 2:00 PM on April 26th.

　　On April 23rd, our side notified the other side that this time we would not only repatriate sick and wounded POWs held in our POW camps, but also repatriate sick and wounded POWs held in our front-line hospitals. Therefore, the number of people to be repatriated by our side will be higher than the estimated figure originally notified to the other side. On April 24th, the other side also notified our side that the number of sick and wounded POWs to be repatriated to our side this time will also be higher than the estimated figure originally notified to our side by the other side.

　　On April 24th, both sides continued to repatriate sick and wounded POWs at Panmunjom. Our side repatriated to the other side fifty Korean sick and wounded POWs, forty American sick and wounded POWs, four British sick and wounded POWs, four Turkish sick and wounded POWs, one Canadian sick and wounded POW, and one Dutch sick and wounded POW, totaling one hundred people. The other side repatriated to our side four hundred and ninety-eight sick and wounded captured personnel of the Korean People's Army.

### 朝鲜停战谈判代表团大会改在明日举行  双方继续在板门店遣返病伤战俘

1953-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判代表团大会改在明日举行
    双方继续在板门店遣返病伤战俘
    【新华社开城二十四日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团四月二十四日发表公报如下：
    四月二十四日双方联络组举行第九次会议，会上双方同意：基于行政性的理由，双方谈判代表团大会改在四月二十六日下午二时举行。
    四月二十三日我方曾通知对方，我方此次不仅将遣返我方战俘营中所收容的病伤战俘，而且将遣返我方在前线病院所收容的病伤战俘，因此我方将予遣返的人数较原通知对方的估计数字有所增加。四月二十四日对方也通知我方，对方此次遣返给我方的病伤战俘的人数也将较对方原通知我方的估计数字有所增加。
    四月二十四日双方继续在板门店遣返病伤战俘，我方遣返给对方朝鲜籍病伤战俘五十人，美籍病伤战俘四十人，英籍病伤战俘四人，土耳其籍病伤战俘四人，加拿大籍病伤战俘一人，荷兰籍病伤战俘一人，总共一百人。对方则遣返给我方朝鲜人民军病伤被俘人员四百九十八人。
