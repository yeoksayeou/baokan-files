Title: Korean Armistice Negotiations: Korean-Chinese Delegation Communiqué - The Other Side Repatriates 491 Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1953-04-29
Page: 4

    Korean Armistice Negotiations: Korean-Chinese Delegation Communiqué
    The Other Side Repatriates 491 Prisoners of War
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, April 28th] The Korean Armistice Negotiation's Korean-Chinese delegation released the following communiqué on April 28th: On April 28th, the other side repatriated to us 491 sick and wounded captured personnel of the Korean People's Army at Panmunjom.

### 朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团公报  对方遣返战俘四百九十一人

1953-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团公报
    对方遣返战俘四百九十一人
    【新华社开城二十八日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团四月二十八日发表公报如下：四月二十八日对方在板门店遣返给我方朝鲜人民军病伤被俘人员四百九十一人。
