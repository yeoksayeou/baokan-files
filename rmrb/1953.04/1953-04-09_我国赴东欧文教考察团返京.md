Title: China's Cultural and Educational Delegation to Eastern Europe Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-04-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: The People's Republic of China's Cultural and Educational Delegation to Eastern European People's Democratic Countries, led by head of the delegation Wei Que and deputy head Peng Kang, returned to Beijing on the 4th.  Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, Liu Shi and Lin Liru, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Education, Huang Xiaozeng, Director of the General Office, Ge Zhicheng, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Higher Education, and Wen Ning, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, were at the airport to greet them.
    The delegation departed in September 1952, and over the course of seven months visited six countries: Poland, the German Democratic Republic, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, and Bulgaria.  In accordance with the cultural cooperation agreements between China and these countries, the delegation conducted investigations into the cultural and educational undertakings of these countries.  Wherever the delegation went, it received warm welcomes and assistance from the governments and peoples of those countries.

### 我国赴东欧文教考察团返京

1953-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我国赴东欧文教考察团返京
    【新华社八日讯】中华人民共和国赴东欧人民民主国家文化教育考察团已于四日在团长韦悫，副团长彭康率领下返抵北京。到机场迎接的有中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江，教育部副部长柳湜、林砺儒，办公厅主任黄啸曾，高等教育部办公厅副主任葛志成与外交部苏联东欧司副司长温宁等。
    考察团自从一九五二年九月出国，在七个月内先后访问了波兰、德意志民主共和国、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚等六个国家。考察团根据中国与这些国家间的文化合作协定，对这些国家的文化教育事业进行了考察。考察团所到之处，受到各该国政府和人民热烈的欢迎与帮助。
