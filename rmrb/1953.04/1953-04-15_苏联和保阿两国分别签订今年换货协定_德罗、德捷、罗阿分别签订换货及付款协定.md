Title: USSR and Bulgaria and Albania Sign This Year's Trade Agreements Respectively. East Germany, Romania, Czechoslovakia and Albania Sign Trade and Payment Agreements Respectively
Author:
Date: 1953-04-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to TASS, the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of Bulgaria signed the Soviet-Bulgarian trade agreement for 1953 in Moscow on March 31. The trade volume between the two countries stipulated therein greatly exceeds that of 1952. According to the agreement, the Soviet Union will export industrial equipment, agricultural machinery, rolled ferrous metals, cotton, petroleum products, chemicals, etc. to Bulgaria. Bulgaria will export minerals, tobacco, meat, wine, fresh fruit, dried fruit, tomato products and other consumer goods to the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to TASS, the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of Albania signed the trade agreement for 1953 in Moscow on the 8th. The trade volume stipulated in this agreement is significantly higher than that of 1952. According to the agreement, the Soviet Union will export grains, petroleum industry equipment, equipment for mechanizing construction work, agricultural machinery, fertilizers, rolled ferrous and non-ferrous metals, chemicals, etc. to Albania. Albania will export oil, asphalt, copper, leather, wool, tobacco, citrus fruits and other items to the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin: According to TASS, the German Democratic Republic and the Romanian People's Republic signed the trade and payment agreement for 1953 in Berlin on March 28. According to the agreement, trade between the two countries will be greatly expanded. This year, Romania will export agricultural products and products of the coal mining industry, chemical industry, heavy machinery manufacturing industry and other industries to the German Democratic Republic; the German Democratic Republic will export machinery manufacturing industry products, precision instruments, optical instruments and electrical equipment, etc. to Romania. Also, the German Democratic Republic and the Czechoslovak Republic signed a long-term trade and payment agreement in Prague on April 1.

Xinhua News Agency: According to TASS, the Romanian People's Republic and the Albanian People's Republic signed the trade and payment agreement for 1953 in Bucharest on March 31. According to this agreement, Albania will export copper, chromium, oranges, olives, etc. to Romania, and Romania will export petroleum products, chemicals, cement, paper, grains, etc. to Albania.

### 苏联和保阿两国分别签订今年换货协定  德罗、德捷、罗阿分别签订换货及付款协定

1953-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和保阿两国分别签订今年换货协定
    德罗、德捷、罗阿分别签订换货及付款协定
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联和保加利亚人民共和国三月三十一日在莫斯科签订了苏保一九五三年换货协定。其中规定的两国间的贸易额大大地超过了一九五二年。根据协定，苏联将向保加利亚输出工业设备、农业机器、黑色压延金属、棉花、石油产品、化学品等。保加利亚将向苏联输出矿产、烟草、肉类、葡萄酒、鲜果、干果、番茄制成品和其他消费品。
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国八日在莫斯科签订了一九五三年换货协定。这个协定规定的贸易额比一九五二年有显著的增加。根据协定，苏联将向阿尔巴尼亚输出谷物、石油工业设备、使建筑工作机械化的设备、农业机器、肥料、黑色和有色压延金属、化学品等。阿尔巴尼亚将向苏联输出石油、沥青、铜、皮革、羊毛、烟草、柑橘类水果等物品。
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：德意志民主共和国和罗马尼亚人民共和国三月二十八日在柏林签订了一九五三年换货和付款协定。根据协定，两国间的贸易将大为扩大。罗马尼亚今年将向德意志民主共和国输出农产品和采煤工业、化学工业、重型机器制造工业及其他工业的产品；德意志民主共和国将向罗马尼亚输出机器制造工业产品、精确仪器、光学仪器和电工器材等。又讯：德意志民主共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国四月一日在布拉格签订了长期的换货和付款协定。
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国三月三十一日在布加勒斯特签订了一九五三年换货及付款协定。根据这个协定，阿尔巴尼亚将向罗马尼亚输出铜、铬、橘子、橄榄等，罗马尼亚将向阿尔巴尼亚输出石油产品、化学品、水泥、纸张、谷类等。
