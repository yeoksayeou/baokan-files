Title: Gromyko Appointed First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Malik Appointed Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom
Author:
Date: 1953-04-20
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th. TASS Moscow, 18th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has relieved Andrei Gromyko of his duties as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the United Kingdom.

    The Council of Ministers of the USSR has appointed Andrei Gromyko as First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th. TASS Moscow, 18th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has appointed Yakov Malik as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the United Kingdom.

    The Council of Ministers of the USSR has relieved Yakov Malik of his duties as Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

### 葛罗米柯任外交部第一副部长马立克任苏联驻英大使

1953-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯任外交部第一副部长马立克任苏联驻英大使
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十八日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团解除安德烈·葛罗米柯的苏联驻英国特命全权大使的职务。
    苏联部长会议任命安德烈·葛罗米柯为苏联外交部第一副部长。
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十八日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命雅科夫·马立克为苏联驻英国特命全权大使。
    苏联部长会议解除雅科夫·马立克的苏联外交部副部长的职务。
