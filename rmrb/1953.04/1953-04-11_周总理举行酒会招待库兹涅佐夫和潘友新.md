Title: Premier Zhou Holds Reception for Kuznetsov and Panyushkin
Author:
Date: 1953-04-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 10th—Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, held a reception in Beijing this afternoon to welcome Kuznetsov, the newly appointed Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, and to bid farewell to Panyushkin, the former Soviet Ambassador to China.

    Ambassador Kuznetsov, former Ambassador Panyushkin, and all diplomatic personnel from the Soviet Embassy attended the reception upon invitation.

    Those present to accompany them included: Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government; Vice Premiers of the State Administration Council Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping; Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen; as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, leaders of various people's organizations, and figures from the cultural, educational, scientific, and artistic circles, totaling more than 200 people.

    At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech expressing his regret at Ambassador Panyushkin's departure and extending a warm welcome to Ambassador Kuznetsov. He also wished Ambassador Kuznetsov new achievements in promoting the relations between China and the Soviet Union. Subsequently, former Ambassador Panyushkin and Ambassador Kuznetsov delivered speeches in response. Former Ambassador Panyushkin spoke about his positive impressions of the New China. Ambassador Kuznetsov expressed his willingness to work towards further developing and consolidating the friendship, unity, and cooperation between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union.

    The reception proceeded in an atmosphere filled with cordiality and friendship. The guests frequently toasted to the great and unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, to the health of the Soviet government leaders headed by Comrade Malenkov, and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people.

    A musical and dance evening was held after the reception.

### 周总理举行酒会招待库兹涅佐夫和潘友新

1953-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理举行酒会招待库兹涅佐夫和潘友新
    【新华社十日讯】我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，十日下午在北京举行酒会，欢迎苏联新任驻我国特命全权大使库兹涅佐夫并欢送苏联驻我国前任大使潘友新。
    库兹涅佐夫大使、潘友新前大使及苏联大使馆全体外交人员都应邀出席。
    出席作陪的有：中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，政务院副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，北京市人民政府市长彭真，以及中央人民政府各部门首长，各人民团体负责人，文化、教育、科学、艺术界人士等共二百余人。
    会上，周恩来总理致辞，对潘友新大使的离任表示惜别，对库兹涅佐夫大使表示热烈欢迎，并祝库兹涅佐夫大使在促进中苏两国邦交上获得新的成就。接着，潘友新前大使和库兹涅佐夫大使相继致答辞。潘友新前大使谈到了他对于新中国所获得的良好印象。库兹涅佐夫大使表示愿为进一步发展和巩固中苏两国人民的友谊、团结与合作而努力。
    酒会在充满着亲切友好的空气中进行。宾主频频为中苏两国人民伟大的、牢不可破的友谊，为以马林科夫同志为首的苏联政府领袖们的健康，为中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东主席的健康干杯。
    会后并举行音乐舞蹈晚会。
