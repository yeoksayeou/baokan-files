Title: Central and Beijing People's Broadcasting Stations to Broadcast Live May Day Parade and Opening Ceremony of the Seventh Labor Congress Respectively
Author:
Date: 1953-04-30
Page: 3

    Central People's Broadcasting Station is scheduled to broadcast live the "Capital People's Celebration of May 1st International Labor Day Parade" starting at 9:30 AM on May 1st.
    Beijing People's Broadcasting Station is scheduled to rebroadcast the live opening ceremony of the Seventh National Labor Congress from 2:15 PM on May 2nd. The station, in conjunction with the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, has also decided to jointly host a "Broadcast Program on the Seventh National Labor Congress" starting on May 3rd, broadcasting twice a day. The first broadcast will be at noon, from 12:15 PM to 12:45 PM. The second broadcast will be in the afternoon, from 6:15 PM to 6:45 PM.

### 中央和北京人民广播电台  将分别广播五一节游行大会和第七次劳动大会开幕典礼实况

1953-04-30
第3版()
专栏：

    中央和北京人民广播电台
    将分别广播五一节游行大会和第七次劳动大会开幕典礼实况
    中央人民广播电台定于五月一日从上午九点三十分开始，广播“首都各界人民庆祝五一国际劳动节游行大会”的实况。
    北京人民广播电台定于五月二日下午二时十五分起，转播第七次全国劳动大会开幕典礼的实况，该台并与北京市总工会决定从五月三日起，联合举办“关于第七次全国劳动大会的广播节目”，每天分别播音两次。第一次播音在中午，从十二时十五分至十二时四十五分。第二次播音在下午，从六时十五分至六时四十五分。
