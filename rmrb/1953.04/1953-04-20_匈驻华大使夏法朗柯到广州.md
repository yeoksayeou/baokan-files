Title: Hungarian Ambassador to China, Szarkó Ferenc, Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1953-04-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 19th: The Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Szarkó Ferenc, and his wife, along with two others, arrived in Guangzhou from Beijing on the evening of the 18th. Ambassador Szarkó Ferenc will personally preside over the opening of the Hungarian People's Republic Exhibition in Guangzhou. Among those welcoming him at the station were Chen Zhifang, Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, and leading members of the Municipal People's Government's Culture and Education Committee, Cultural Bureau, Foreign Affairs Office, and other units.

### 匈驻华大使夏法朗柯到广州

1953-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    匈驻华大使夏法朗柯到广州
    【新华社广州十九日电】匈牙利人民共和国驻我国特命全权大使夏法朗柯和大使夫人等一行四人在十八日晚间自北京到达广州。夏法朗柯大使将亲自主持即将在广州开幕的匈牙利人民共和国展览会。到车站欢迎的有广州市人民政府秘书长陈志方和市人民政府文化教育委员会、文化局、外事处等单位负责人等。
