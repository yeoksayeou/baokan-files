Title: Czech Trade Delegation Head I. Sochorek Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th—I. Sochorek, head of the Trade Delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic and Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, arrived in Beijing on the 4th. Those who went to the airport to greet him were Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Sun Chun, Deputy Director of the Import Bureau, Fu Shenglin, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau, Yi Qintang, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau, Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department, and Wen Ning, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

Also at the airport to welcome him was Li Zhuozhi, Commercial Counselor of our Embassy in Czechoslovakia.

K. Sára, the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, and J. Kitlička, Deputy Head of the Czechoslovak Trade Delegation, who was already in Beijing, also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 捷商务代表团团长伊·索契克到北京

1953-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    捷商务代表团团长伊·索契克到北京
    【新华社四日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国商务代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长伊·索契克于四日抵达北京。前往机场迎接的有我中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒、进口局副局长孙纯、出口局副局长傅生麟、第二局副局长衣钦堂、交际处处长杨勉以及外交部苏联东欧司副司长温宁、交际处处长王倬如等。
    到机场欢迎的还有我驻捷大使馆商务参赞李琢之。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉及先已在京的捷克斯洛伐克商务代表团副团长基特利黑等亦前往机场迎接。
