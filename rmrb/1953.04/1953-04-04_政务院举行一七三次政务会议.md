Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 173rd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-04-04
Page: 1

    The Government Administration Council Holds its 173rd Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, 3rd] The 173rd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 3rd. The meeting approved the "Instructions of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on Conducting a National Population Census and Registration in Preparation for the General Election." The meeting also approved various appointment and removal cases and appointment cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行一七三次政务会议

1953-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一七三次政务会议
    【新华社三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百七十三次政务会议在三日下午举行。会议通过了“中央人民政府政务院为准备普选进行全国人口调查登记的指示”。会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案。
