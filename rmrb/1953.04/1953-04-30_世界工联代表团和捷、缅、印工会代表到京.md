Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Delegation and Trade Union Representatives from Czechoslovakia, Burma, and India Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-04-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th—At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) delegation and the Czechoslovak Trade Union delegation, who came to China to participate in the "May 1st" International Labor Day and the Seventh National Labor Congress, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 29th. Liu Changsheng, member of the Standing Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Secretary of the WFTU Secretariat, who had attended the WFTU Executive Bureau meeting in Vienna, accompanied them. Zhu Xuefan, Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Lai Ruoyu, Secretary-General, Li Jiebo, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, Wang Weigang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Second Machine Industry Trade Union, Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Textile Trade Union, Xu Xin, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union, Song Chuan, Deputy Director of the Agricultural, Forestry, and Water Conservancy Trade Union Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Chen Xiweng, Deputy Director of the Workers' Publishing House, and Xu Zhizhen, Zhang Xiuzhu, Jin Ziming, and other heads of various departments of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, went to the airport to welcome them.
    Sangdun, member of the WFTU Asia-Australia Liaison Bureau, and staff member Kutzmink also went to welcome them.
    Czechoslovak Ambassador Kang Shala also went to the airport to welcome them.
    The list of the World Federation of Trade Unions delegation:
    Head: Louis Saillant, General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions
    Members: Sangdun, Member of the WFTU Executive Committee, Member of the WFTU Asia-Australia Liaison Bureau (already in Beijing)
      Vydimali, Secretary of the International Trade Union of Agricultural and Forestry Workers of the WFTU
      Löhrich, Editor-in-Chief of "World Trade Union Movement"
    Secretary: Lewie
    The list of the Czechoslovak Trade Union delegation:
    Head: Maria Trojanova, Secretary of the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions
    Members: Jana Mizera, Labor Hero of the Molotov Steel Plant
    Translator: Horub, Staff member of the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th—At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Burmese trade union representatives and the second group of Indian trade union representatives, a total of thirteen people, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 29th to participate in the "May 1st" International Labor Day and the Seventh National Labor Congress. Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Wang Xuezeng, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, Qiu Jin, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Trade Union, Song Chuan, Deputy Director of the Agricultural, Forestry, and Water Conservancy Trade Union Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Xu Zhizhen, Fang Ming, and other heads of various departments of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, went to the station to welcome them.
    Deqin La Zui, head of the Burmese trade union delegation, who had arrived in Beijing earlier after attending the Vienna meeting of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Trade Unions, also went to the station to welcome them.
    List of the Burmese trade union delegation:

    Kang En, Secretary of the Bombay Dock Workers' Union of India
    Daru, Chairman of the Ahmedabad Municipal Employees Union of India

    Sinha, General Secretary of the Chapra Union of Bihar Province, India

### 世界工联代表团和捷、缅、印工会代表到京

1953-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    世界工联代表团和捷、缅、印工会代表到京
    【新华社二十九日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请，前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节和第七次全国劳动大会的世界工会联合会代表团和捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团，在二十九日下午乘飞机抵达北京。陪同前来的有前往维也纳参加世界工联执行局会议的中华全国总工会常务委员会委员、世界工联书记处书记刘长胜。前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、刘宁一、秘书长赖若愚、中国铁路工会全国委员会主席李颉伯，中国第二机械工业工会全国委员会主席王维纲，中国纺织工会全国委员会主席陈少敏，中国邮电工会全国委员会副主席徐欣，中华全国总工会农林水利工会工作委员会副主任宋川，工人出版社副社长陈希文以及中华全国总工会各工作部门负责人许之桢、张修竹、金子明等人。
    前往欢迎的还有世界工联亚澳联络局委员桑顿和工作人员库茨明克。
    到机场欢迎的还有捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉。世界工会联合会代表团名单：团长：路易·赛扬  世界工会联合会总书记团员：桑顿  世界工联执行委员会委员、世界工联亚澳联络局委员（
      已在北京）
      维第玛利  世界工联农业造林工人工会国际书记
      勒里希  “世界工会运动”总编辑秘书：勒维捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团名单：团长：玛丽亚·特罗雅诺娃  捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会书记团员：雅纳·米泽拉  莫洛托夫钢铁厂劳动英雄译员：霍鲁布  捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会工作人员
    【新华社二十九日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请，前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节和第七次全国劳动大会的缅甸工会代表和印度第二批工会代表
    一行十三人，在二十九日晚间乘火车到达北京。到车站欢迎的有：中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席王学增，中国海员工会筹备委员会副主任丘金，中华全国总工会农林水利工会工作委员会副主任宋川，以及中华全国总工会各工作部门负责人许之桢、方明等人。
