Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Congratulates Workers of All Countries on "May Day"
Author:
Date: 1953-04-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions cabled the World Federation of Trade Unions on April 28th to congratulate them on "May Day" International Labor Day. The text of the cable is as follows: World Federation of Trade Unions, Vienna:

    On the occasion of the arrival of the 1953 "May Day" International Labor Day, on behalf of the Chinese workers, I would like to extend warm congratulations to you—the loyal defender of the interests of workers all over the world.

    In order to welcome the great festival of workers' solidarity around the world and the 7th National Labor Congress of China, which will open in Beijing on May 2nd, all Chinese workers have launched a warm labor emulation drive. Under the leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese working class is striving with unparalleled confidence to realize our country's first five-year national construction plan.

    Under the banner of the World Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese working class is working to strengthen the international solidarity of the working class and defend peace in Asia and the world.

    Long live the solidarity of the working class all over the world!
                                   All-China Federation of Trade Unions

According to Xinhua News Agency: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has sent cables or letters to the Preparatory Committee of the Egyptian Trade Union Federation, the Tunisian Workers' Union, the Algerian Liaison Committee of Trade Unions, the Moroccan General Confederation of Labor, the South African Trades and Labor Council, the South African Congress of Trade Unions, the Costa Rican Labor Confederation, the Rio Grande do Sul Workers' Union of Brazil, the Cuban Workers' Confederation, the Bolivian Labor Central, the Panama Trade Union Federation, the Colombian Workers' Confederation, the Ecuadorian Workers' Confederation, the Chilean Labor Central, and the Unified Trade Union Committee of Venezuelan Workers, congratulating them on the great festival of the working people of the world—May Day International Labor Day, and congratulating the worker brothers and sisters of all countries on achieving greater victories in the struggle to defend their vital rights and interests, oppose imperialist enslavement, and defend peace.

    [Our Newspaper's Dispatch] On the eve of May Day International Labor Day, the All-China Federation of Cooperatives cabled the Central Union of Consumer Cooperatives and the Central Industrial Production Cooperative Conference of the Soviet Union, the Central Committee of the Consumers' Union of Korea and the Central Committee of the Production Cooperative of Korea, the Central Cooperative Alliance of Poland, the Central Federation of Cooperatives of Czechoslovakia, the Federation of Consumer Cooperatives of the German Democratic Republic, the General Union of Consumer Cooperatives of Romania, the Hungarian Cooperative Alliance, the Central Cooperative Alliance of Bulgaria, the Central Federation of Consumer Cooperatives of Albania, and the Central Union of Consumer Cooperatives of Mongolia, congratulating them on the great festival of the working people of the world, and warmly congratulating them on achieving more consolidated victories in the struggle for the happy future of the working people.

### 我国总工会向各国工人祝贺“五一”

1953-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我国总工会向各国工人祝贺“五一”
    【新华社二十九日讯】中华全国总工会四月二十八日致电世界工会联合会，祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。电文如下：维也纳世界工会联合会
    值此一九五三年“五一”国际劳动节来临之际，谨代表中国工人向你——全世界工人利益的忠实捍卫者，致热烈的祝贺。
    为迎接世界工人团结的伟大节日和将于五月二日在北京开幕的中国第七次全国劳动大会，全中国工人已展开了热烈的劳动竞赛。中国工人阶级在毛泽东同志和中国共产党领导下，正以无比的信心为实现我国第一个五年国家建设计划而奋斗。
    中国工人阶级在世界工联的旗帜下为加强工人阶级的国际团结与保卫亚洲及世界和平而努力。
    全世界工人阶级团结万岁！
                                   中华全国总工会
    据新华社讯：中华全国总工会分别致电或写信给埃及工会联合会筹备委员会、突尼斯工人工会、阿尔尼利亚工会联络委员会、摩洛哥总工会、南非各业劳工理事会、南非非洲人工会、哥斯达黎加劳工联合会、巴西南利俄格兰得州工人工会、古巴工人联合会、玻利维亚总工会、巴拿马工会联合会、哥伦比亚工人联合会、厄瓜多尔工人联合会、智利总工会、委内瑞拉工人统一工会委员会，祝贺全世界劳动人民的伟大节日——五一国际劳动节，并祝贺各国工人兄弟姊妹们在保卫自己的切身权利和利益、反对帝国主义的奴役与保卫和平的斗争中，取得更大的胜利。
    【本报讯】中华全国合作社联合总社在五一国际劳动节前夕，分别致电苏联中央消费合作联盟和中央工业生产合作会议、朝鲜消费组合中央委员会和朝鲜生产合作中央委员会、波兰中央合作联盟、捷克斯洛伐克合作社中央总社、德意志民主共和国消费合作社联合会、罗马尼亚消费合作社联合总社、匈牙利合作联盟、保加利亚中央合作联盟、阿尔巴尼亚消费合作社中央联合社、蒙古消费合作社中央联合社，祝贺全世界劳动人民的伟大节日，并热情祝贺他们在为劳动人民幸福前途的斗争中获得更巩固的胜利。
