Title: The Government Administration Council Holds Its 174th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-04-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency
    The 174th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 10th. The meeting listened to and approved the report by Liu Jingfan, Deputy Director of the People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council, on the "Implementation of the Key Points of Supervision Work in the Second Half of 1952 and the Key Points of Supervision Work from January to June 1953." The meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals and proposals for appointments to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一七四次会议

1953-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一七四次会议
    【新华社讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百七十四次政务会议在十日下午举行。会议听取和批准了政务院人民监察委员会刘景范副主任所作的“一九五二年下半年监察工作要点执行情况及一九五三年一月至六月监察工作要点”的报告。会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案。
