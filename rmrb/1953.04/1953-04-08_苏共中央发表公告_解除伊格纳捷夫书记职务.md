Title: Announcement by the CPSU Central Committee: Ignatiev Relieved of Post as Secretary

Author:

Date: 1953-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th: TASS Moscow, 7th dispatch: Announcement of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

    According to the decision of the plenary session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, S.D. Ignatiev has been relieved of his post as Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

### 苏共中央发表公告  解除伊格纳捷夫书记职务

1953-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央发表公告
    解除伊格纳捷夫书记职务
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联共产党中央委员会公告
    根据苏联共产党中央委员会全体会议的决定，斯·德·伊格纳捷夫已被解除苏联共产党中央委员会书记的职务。
