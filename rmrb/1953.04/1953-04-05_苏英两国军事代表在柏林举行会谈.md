Title: Soviet and British Military Representatives Hold Talks in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1953-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 3rd: According to TASS, Berlin, April 2nd, representatives of the Soviet and British military authorities continued negotiations in Berlin on April 1st regarding the issue of ensuring the safety of aircraft flying in the air corridor over the German Democratic Republic in order to avoid air incidents. These negotiations began on March 31st, based on the March 18th letter from Soviet Army General Chuikov to Sir Kirkpatrick, the British High Commissioner in West Germany.

The Soviet delegation has suggested inviting the American and French military authorities in Germany to participate in these negotiations.

### 苏英两国军事代表在柏林举行会谈

1953-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏英两国军事代表在柏林举行会谈
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社柏林二日讯：苏联和英国两国军事当局代表四月一日继续在柏林进行谈判，谈判是关于保证在德意志民主共和国上空的飞行走廊飞行的飞机的安全以避免发生空中事件的问题。这次谈判在三月三十一日开始，以三月十八日苏联陆军上将崔可夫将军给英国驻西德高级专员柯克帕特里克的信为基础。
    苏联代表团已建议邀请美国与法国驻德国军事当局参加这次谈判。
