Title: New Soviet Ambassador to China, Kuznetsov, Presents Credentials to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1953-04-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd:

    The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to China, Kuznetsov, accompanied by Commercial Representative Migunov, Counselor Gudev, Military Attaché Lieutenant General Kotov, and Counselors Vashnov, Skotsenko, Li Haiqiu, and other diplomatic officials, a total of twenty-eight people, presented his credentials to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government at 5:00 PM on the 3rd. Present were Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Huang Kecheng, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Protocol of the Central People's Government; Xu Yixin, Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Department of Intercourse. Ambassador Kuznetsov delivered a eulogy to Chairman Mao Zedong when presenting his credentials, and Chairman Mao Zedong delivered a reply.
    Ambassador Kuznetsov’s Eulogy:
    Chairman Comrade:
    I am extremely honored to present to you the credentials from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, appointing me as the Ambassador of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to the People's Republic of China.
    Chairman Comrade, I assure you that I will dedicate all my strength to the cause of further developing and consolidating the sincere, fraternal, friendship and close cooperation between the people of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the people of the People's Republic of China. The great and unbreakable friendship and alliance between our two countries not only conforms to the interests of the Soviet and Chinese people, but is also a solid pillar of peace and security in the Far East and the world.
    Chairman Comrade, I am deeply convinced that in carrying out this important mission, I will receive the necessary support and assistance from you and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China.
    Please allow me to wish the great Chinese people and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China further achievements in the cause of building a strong people's democratic state.
    Chairman Comrade, I wholeheartedly wish you success in your activities dedicated to the happiness and prosperity of the People's Republic of China.
    Chairman Mao Zedong’s Reply:
    Ambassador Comrade:
    I am very pleased to accept the credentials presented by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, and I sincerely thank you for your congratulations.
    The Chinese people and the great Soviet people have established an unbreakable friendship through close mutual assistance and cooperation. All the achievements that New China has made in the economic and cultural fields, as well as the implementation of the large-scale national construction plan that is now beginning, are inseparable from the sincere and fraternal assistance of the Soviet people and the Soviet government.
    I am deeply convinced that the great friendship and unity between the Chinese and Soviet peoples will continue to develop and consolidate infinitely under the joint efforts of the two peoples. The endless power generated by our friendship will undoubtedly play an unparalleled role in the cause of peace and security in the Far East and the world.
    Ambassador, Comrade, I warmly welcome you to serve as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to the People's Republic of China. In your efforts to consolidate the friendly cooperation between China and the Soviet Union, you will receive my full support and the full support of the government of the People's Republic of China.
    I wish you complete success in your work.
    Kuznetsov's Biography
    Vasily Vasilyevich Kuznetsov was born in 1901, received higher education, and graduated from the Leningrad Industrial Institute, specializing in metallurgical engineering. From 1926 to 1937, he worked as an engineer in various Soviet enterprises. From 1937 to 1940, he was in charge of leadership work in the Ministry of Ferrous Metallurgy. From 1940 to 1944, he served as Deputy Chairman of the Soviet State Planning Committee. Since 1944, he has served as Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions.
    Kuznetsov is a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, a representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and a representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federated Socialist Republic. He was appointed Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union on March 6, 1953. (Attached photo)

### 苏联新任驻我国大使库兹涅佐夫  向毛泽东主席呈递国书

1953-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联新任驻我国大使库兹涅佐夫
    向毛泽东主席呈递国书
    【新华社三日讯】苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟新任驻我国特命全权大使库兹涅佐夫在三日下午五时偕商务代表米古诺夫、参赞顾德夫、武官柯托夫中将及参赞华士诺夫、司高磋、李海秋等外交官员共二十八人，向我中央人民政府毛泽东主席呈递国书。在场者有中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠、中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来、人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长黄克诚、外交部办公厅主任王炳南、中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清、外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新、交际处处长王倬如。库兹涅佐夫大使在呈递国书时，曾向毛泽东主席致颂词，毛泽东主席致答词。
    库兹涅佐夫大使颂词主席同志：
    我极其荣幸地向您呈递苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团任命我为苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻中华人民共和国大使的国书。
    主席同志，我向您保证：我将把一切力量，贡献给进一步发展和巩固苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和中华人民共和国两国人民之间真诚的、兄弟般的友谊和亲密合作的事业。我们两国之间伟大的、牢不可破的友谊和同盟不但符合于苏联和中国人民的利益，并且是远东和全世界和平及安全的坚固支柱。
    主席同志，我深信，在执行这项责任重大的使命时，我将得到您及中华人民共和国中央人民政府方面必要的支持和协助。
    请允许我预祝伟大的中国人民和中华人民共和国中央人民政府在建设强大的人民民主国家的事业中获得进一步的成就。
    主席同志，我衷心祝您在致力于中华人民共和国的幸福和繁荣的活动中获得成功。
    毛泽东主席答词大使同志：
    我非常高兴地接受您所呈递的苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团的国书，并衷心地感谢您的祝贺。
    中国人民与伟大的苏联人民在亲密的互助合作中建立了牢不可破的友谊。新中国在经济、文化各方面所获得的一切成就，以及目前正在开始的大规模国家建设计划的实施，都与苏联人民、苏联政府的真诚的、兄弟般的援助分不开的。
    我深信：中苏两国人民伟大的友谊与团结定将在两国人民共同的努力下，继续无限地发展与巩固起来。我们的友谊所产生的无穷无尽的力量，必将对于远东及全世界和平与安全的事业起着无比巨大的作用。
    大使同志，我极为热烈地欢迎您出任苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻中华人民共和国的特命全权大使。在您为巩固中苏两国友好合作的工作中，您将得到我和中华人民共和国政府的全力支持。
    我谨祝您在工作上获得完全的成功。
    库兹涅佐夫大使简历
    瓦西里·瓦西里耶维奇·库兹涅佐夫生于一九○一年，受过高等教育，曾毕业于列宁格勒工业学院，专长冶金工程。一九二六年至一九三七年在苏联各企业任工程师。一九三七年至一九四○年在黑色冶金部负责领导工作。一九四○年至一九四四年任苏联国家计划委员会副主席。自一九四四年起任全苏工会中央理事会主席。
    库兹涅佐夫为苏联共产党中央委员会委员，苏联最高苏维埃代表及俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国最高苏维埃代表。自一九五三年三月六日起被任命为苏联外交部副部长。（附图片）
