Title: *Pravda* Publishes Editorial Commenting on Eisenhower's Speech
Author:
Date: 1953-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 25th: *Pravda* today published an editorial entitled "On President Eisenhower's Speech" prominently on the entire front page, and published Eisenhower's speech of April 16th on the third page.

### 真理报发表社论  评论艾森豪威尔演说

1953-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    真理报发表社论
    评论艾森豪威尔演说
    【新华社莫斯科二十五日电】真理报今日在第一版整版地位发表题为“评艾森豪威尔总统的演说”的社论，并在第三版刊载了艾森豪威尔四月十六日发表的演说。
