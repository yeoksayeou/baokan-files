Title: At the Invitation of the Soviet Union, the US and France Will Participate in the Soviet-British Berlin Talks
Author:
Date: 1953-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 5th: TASS reported from London on the 4th that, according to newspaper reports, the US and French High Commissioners to West Germany have accepted the Soviet invitation to send representatives to participate in the British and Soviet talks in Berlin on the issue of air flight safety.

### 在苏联邀请下  美法将参加苏英柏林会谈

1953-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    在苏联邀请下
    美法将参加苏英柏林会谈
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社伦敦四日讯：根据报纸报道，美国与法国驻西德高级专员已经接受了苏联的邀请，派遣代表参加英国与苏联在柏林举行的关于空中飞行安全问题的会谈。
