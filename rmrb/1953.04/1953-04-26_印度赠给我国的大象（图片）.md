Title: The Elephant India Gifted to Our Country (Picture)
Author: Zhang Ruihua
Date: 1953-04-26
Page: 4

    The Elephant India Gifted to Our Country
    The elephant that Indian Prime Minister Nehru, on behalf of the Indian government, gifted to our country, was escorted by its handler, Ali, and transported to Guangzhou on April 6th. It will soon be sent to Beijing for public exhibition. This fourteen-year-old elephant is named "Asa" and weighs one and a half tons. This is a photo of Ali riding on the back of the elephant "Asa," directing it to perform salutes and other actions.

                                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Ruihua

### 印度赠给我国的大象（图片）
张瑞华
1953-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    印度赠给我国的大象
    印度总理尼赫鲁代表印度政府赠送我国的一只大象，由它的饲养者阿里护送于四月六日运抵广州。即将送至北京公开展览。这只十四龄的象名叫“阿萨”，体重一吨半。这是阿里骑在大象“阿萨”背上，指挥它做敬礼等动作。
                                    新华社记者  张瑞华摄
