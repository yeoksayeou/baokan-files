Title: Beijing Holds Meeting to Commemorate Lenin's Birthday

Author: 
Date: 1953-04-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries jointly sponsored a report meeting on the 83rd anniversary of the birth of the great Lenin on the 22nd. More than 800 representatives from factories, schools, and organizations in Beijing participated in the meeting. Zhukov, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, also attended the meeting. Bian Menov, Chief of the TASS General Branch in China, gave a report entitled "Commemorating the 83rd Anniversary of Lenin's Birth." After the report, the Soviet film "Life of Lenin" was shown.

### 北京市举行纪念列宁诞辰报告会

1953-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    北京市举行纪念列宁诞辰报告会
    【新华社二十二日讯】二十二日是伟大的列宁诞生八十三周年纪念日，北京市中苏友好协会和苏联对外文化协会联合主办了报告会。参加这一报告会的有北京市各工厂、学校和机关、团体的代表八百多人，苏联对外文化协会驻中国代表茹克福也参加了报告会。塔斯社驻华总分社社长边缅诺夫以“纪念列宁诞生八十三周年”为题作了报告。报告后并放映苏联影片“列宁生平”。
