Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-04-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Adopting the Soviet Union's comprehensively developed teaching method to educate children is the correct path for children's education in our country. The No. 1 Kindergarten in Caoyang New Village, Shanghai, currently has 349 children of workers. They are receiving education in the all-round development of moral, intellectual, physical, and aesthetic education, and each and every one of them appears particularly lively and lovely. These two children have just finished eating biscuits and are having an interesting conversation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    采用苏联全面发展的教学法来教育儿童，是我国儿童教育的正确途径。上海市曹杨新村第一幼儿园现有工人子女三百四十九人。他们受着德育、智育、体育、美育全面发展的教育，一个个都显得特别活泼可爱。这两个小朋友才吃完了饼干，正在进行有趣的谈话。
