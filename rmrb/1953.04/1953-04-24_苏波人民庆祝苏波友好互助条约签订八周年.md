Title: Soviet and Polish People Celebrate the Eighth Anniversary of the Signing of the Soviet-Polish Treaty of Friendship, Mutual Assistance, and Post-War Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1953-04-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: The people of the Soviet Union and Poland warmly celebrated the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Soviet-Polish Treaty of Friendship, Mutual Assistance, and Post-War Cooperation on the 21st. The Soviet Union's Pravda, Izvestia, and other newspapers in Moscow all published editorials and articles on this day to review the historical significance of the treaty and to discuss the achievements of the Polish people over the past eight years with the assistance of the Soviet Union. In the regions of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic bordering Poland, in cities such as Kiev, Kharkov, and Odessa, Soviet people held celebrations. Many cultural institutions also held reports and exhibitions. W. Lewikowski, the Polish Ambassador to the Soviet Union, held a reception in Moscow on the 21st. Those who attended the reception included V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, other ministers of the Soviet government, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union. Newspapers in Warsaw, the capital of Poland, also published editorials and articles celebrating the festival on the 21st. People from all walks of life in Warsaw held a report meeting on the eve of the anniversary—the 20th. M. Naszkowski, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Poland, gave a report entitled "Joseph Stalin—The Great Friend of the Polish People" at the meeting, elaborating on the great significance of this treaty for the political, economic, and cultural development of People's Poland. Many factories, villages, and agricultural production cooperatives throughout Poland also held celebrations. Participants decided to send telegrams or letters to Soviet friends, expressing their determination to spare no effort to promote and strengthen Soviet-Polish friendship and to strive to safeguard Poland's independence and comprehensive development.

### 苏波人民庆祝苏波友好互助条约签订八周年

1953-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏波人民庆祝苏波友好互助条约签订八周年
    【新华社二十三日讯】苏联和波兰人民在二十一日热烈庆祝苏波友好互助与战后合作条约签订八周年。苏联的真理报、消息报和莫斯科的其他报纸都在这一天发表了庆祝节日的社论和文章，评述这个条约的历史意义，并论述波兰人民八年来在苏联的援助下所获得的成就。在紧邻波兰的苏联乌克兰共和国地区，在基辅、哈尔科夫和敖德萨等城市，苏联人民纷纷举行了庆祝会；许多文化机关还举办了报告会和展览会。波兰驻苏联大使吴·列维科夫斯基二十一日在莫斯科举行了招待会。参加招待会的有苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫，苏联政府的其他部长们和各国驻苏联使节等。波兰首都华沙各报也在二十一日发表了庆祝节日的社论和文章。华沙各界人民在纪念日的前夕——二十日，举行了报告会。波兰外交部副部长马·纳斯科夫斯基在会上作了题为“约瑟夫·斯大林——波兰人民的伟大朋友”的报告，阐述了这个条约对于人民波兰的政治、经济和文化发展的重大意义。波兰全国各地的许多工厂、乡村和农业生产合作社也举行了庆祝会。参加会议的人决定致电或写信给苏联友人，表示他们要不遗余力地为促进和加强波苏友谊、为保障波兰的独立和全面发展而奋斗。
