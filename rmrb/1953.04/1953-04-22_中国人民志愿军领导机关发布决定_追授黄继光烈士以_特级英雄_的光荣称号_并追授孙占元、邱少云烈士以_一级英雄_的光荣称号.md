Title: The Leadership Organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Issues a Decision: Conferred the Honorary Title of "Special Class Hero" on Martyr Huang Jiguang, and Conferred the Honorary Title of "First Class Hero" on Martyrs Sun Zhanyuan and Qiu Shaoyun
Author:
Date: 1953-04-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 21st: The leadership organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army recently issued a decision, posthumously conferring the honorary title of "Special Class Hero" on Martyr Huang Jiguang, who established outstanding meritorious service in the Battle of Shangganling; posthumously conferring the honorary title of "First Class Hero" on the immortal Communist Party member Sun Zhanyuan and the great soldier Qiu Shaoyun; and simultaneously announcing the rescission of the previous decision to posthumously confer the honorary title of "Second Class Hero" on Martyr Huang Jiguang. The Chinese Communist Party Committee of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army also recently issued a decision posthumously recognizing Martyr Huang Jiguang as a member of the Chinese Communist Party and a model member of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. The decision stated: In the glorious Battle of Shangganling, Huang Jiguang heroically and generously sacrificed his life for victory at the most critical moment of the battle. His heroic act inspired the morale of the troops, educated the people of the whole country, and demonstrated his high degree of class consciousness and his infinite loyalty to the cause of defending world peace. He has become a shining banner for Youth League members. Based on his performance during his lifetime and his aspirations, it is specifically decided to posthumously recognize Huang Jiguang as a member of the Chinese Communist Party and confer on him the honorary title of "Model League Member."

### 中国人民志愿军领导机关发布决定  追授黄继光烈士以“特级英雄”的光荣称号  并追授孙占元、邱少云烈士以“一级英雄”的光荣称号

1953-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军领导机关发布决定
    追授黄继光烈士以“特级英雄”的光荣称号
    并追授孙占元、邱少云烈士以“一级英雄”的光荣称号
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十一日电】中国人民志愿军领导机关最近发布决定，追授在上甘岭战役中建立卓越功勋的黄继光烈士以“特级英雄”的光荣称号；追授不朽的共产党员孙占元和伟大的战士邱少云以“一级英雄”的光荣称号；同时并宣布撤消以前追授黄继光烈士以“二级英雄”光荣称号的决定。中国共产党中国人民志愿军某部委员会也在最近发布决定，追认黄继光烈士为中国共产党党员和中国新民主主义青年团模范团员。决定中说：在光辉的上甘岭战役中。黄继光在战斗最紧要的关头，为了胜利而英勇慷慨地献出自己的生命。他的壮烈行为鼓舞了部队的斗志，教育了全国人民，表现了他高度的阶级觉悟和对保卫世界和平事业的无限忠诚。他已经成为青年团员的一面光辉的旗帜。根据他生前的表现和他生前的志愿，特决定追认黄继光为中国共产党党员，并授以“模范团员”的光荣称号。
