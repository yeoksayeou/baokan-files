Title: Swedish Cultural Delegation Invited to Visit China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th—The Swedish Cultural Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation list is as follows:

    Head of Delegation: Karl-Erik MeckQuist, Agronomist
    Secretary: Mrs. Karin Öding, Secretary of the Swedish-Chinese Friendship Association
    Members: Theodor Arvidsson, Bishop
              Harold Bayer, Writer
              Mats Linman, Architect
              Harold Rubinstein, Lawyer, Director of the Swedish-Chinese Friendship Association
              Hjalmar Holmberg, Member of Parliament
              Mrs. Hofsten, Magazine Editor
              Miss Margit Cranz, Doctor

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Vice Presidents Chen Hansheng and Qian Duansheng, Secretary-General Wu Maosun, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Sha Kefu, Deputy Director of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Liao Mengxing, Secretary-General of the Chinese Medical Association Fang Shishan, agronomist Shen Qiyi, architect Zhu Zhaoxue, Wang Zizhong from religious circles, Liao Jingtian from the press, and Zhang Menggeng, deputy head of the Chinese People's Acrobatic Troupe, who had performed in Sweden.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Mr. Schuld, Second Secretary of the Swedish Embassy in China, and other embassy staff.

### 应邀来我国参观的瑞典文化代表团抵北京

1953-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参观的瑞典文化代表团抵北京
    【新华社二十六日讯】应中国人民外交学会的邀请来我国参观的瑞典文化代表团今日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团名单如下：团长：卡尔—艾力克·梅克奎斯特  农学家秘书：卡林·欧丁夫人瑞（典）中友好协会秘书长团员：特奥多尔·阿维德逊  主教
      哈罗德·拜耶尔  作家
      马茨·林曼  建筑家
      哈罗德·鲁宾斯坦律师，瑞（典）中友好协会理事
      赫尔麦·霍姆伯格  国会议员
      霍夫斯顿夫人  杂志编辑
      玛吉特·克兰兹女士  医生
    前往机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长陈翰笙、钱端升，秘书长吴茂荪，中华全国文学艺术界联合会秘书长沙可夫，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中华医学会秘书长方石珊，农学家沈其益，建筑家朱兆雪，宗教界王梓仲，新闻界廖经天及曾赴瑞典演出的中国人民杂技团副团长张梦庚等人。
    前往机场欢迎的还有瑞典驻华大使馆二等秘书修尔德等使馆人员。
