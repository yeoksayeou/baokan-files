Title: Second National Women's Congress of China Elects Delegation to World Women's Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency
    China's Second National Women's Congress
    Elects Delegation to World Women's Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency] The list of the Chinese delegation elected by the Second National Women's Congress of China to attend the World Women's Congress is as follows:
    Cai Chang Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Vice Chairman of the Women's International Democratic Federation
    Li Dequan Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Member of the Council of the Women's International Democratic Federation
    Zhang Yun Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation

    (The following representatives are listed in order of the number of strokes in their surnames)
    Chou Zigang Director of a Nursery
    Fang Lingru Professor, Fudan University, Shanghai
    Ge Yang Editor-in-Chief of "New Observer" magazine
    Bai Lang Writer
    Shen Jilan Agricultural Labor Model, Vice Director of the Li Shunda Agricultural, Forestry and Animal Husbandry Production Cooperative in Shanxi
    Tian Hua Movie Actress
    Li Bo Director of the Women Workers Department of the Central and Southern China Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    Li Yun Secretary-General of the China Welfare Institute
    Shen Yuanhui Chairman of the Board of Directors, Chinese Nursing Association
    Shen Ziji “New China Women” magazine director
    He Zehui Researcher, Institute of Modern Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences
    Laxida Tatar, Deputy Director of the Northwest China Women's Work Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
    Yu Zhiying Trade Union Worker
    Yu Aifeng Director of Obstetrics and Gynecology, Tianjin General Hospital
    Sun Xiaoju National Railway Labor Model, Deputy Dispatcher of the Qiqihar Railway Bureau Dispatch Office
    Wulan Mongolian, Director of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Democratic Women's Federation
    Cao Mengjun Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
    Zhang Xiaomei Director of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation
    Lu Cui Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
    Zhang Yangfen Director of the Gynecology and Obstetrics Hospital of the China Medical University in Shenyang
    Ceren Namu Tibetan, Deputy Director of the Northwest China Women's Work Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
    Feng Bingshan Chief of the Teaching Affairs Section of the Cadre School of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives
    Lei Jieqiong Vice Dean of Academic Affairs, Beijing College of Political Science and Law
    Liao Suhua Director of the Southwest China Women's Work Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
    Deng Yuzhi General Secretary of the National Association of the Young Women's Christian Association of China
    Qu Xixian Young Composer, winner of the composition competition at the 3rd World Youth and Students Peace Festival
    Yan Renying Director of Obstetrics and Gynecology, Affiliated Hospital of Beijing Medical College
