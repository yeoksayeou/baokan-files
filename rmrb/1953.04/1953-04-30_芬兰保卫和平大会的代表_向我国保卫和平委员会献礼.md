Title: Representatives of the Finnish Peace Congress Present Gifts to the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace

Author:
Date: 1953-04-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: On the morning of April 29th at 10:00 AM, Mr. Mauri Luma, Central Committee member of the Finnish Peace Congress and deputy head of the Finnish cultural delegation currently in Beijing, on behalf of the Finnish Peace Congress, presented gifts to the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. The gifts included a copper relief sculpture with the theme of the struggle for peace, a letter from the Finnish Peace Congress, and a small Finnish flag. Accompanied Mr. Mauri Luma to the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to participate in the presentation was Mrs. Eila Kurkiala, a member of the Finnish cultural delegation and a writer.
    Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, accepted the gifts on behalf of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. Also present at the gift presentation were Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department, Zhu Shilun, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department, Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Director of the Organization Department, and Zeng Zhaosheng, Director of the General Office.

### 芬兰保卫和平大会的代表  向我国保卫和平委员会献礼

1953-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰保卫和平大会的代表
    向我国保卫和平委员会献礼
    【新华社二十九日讯】四月二十九日上午十时，芬兰保卫和平大会中央委员，现在北京的芬兰文化代表团副团长毛里·卢玛代表芬兰保卫和平大会向中国人民保卫世界和平委员会献礼，礼品是一块以和平斗争为主题的铜制浮雕，一封芬兰保卫和平大会的来信和一面小型芬兰国旗。偕同毛里·卢玛一起到中国人民保卫世界和平委员会参加献礼的还有芬兰文化代表团团员、作家爱拉·柯鲁拉夫人。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会接受礼品。参加受礼的还有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，联络部副部长唐明照、宣传部副部长朱世纶，组织部副部长王明远，办公厅主任曾昭生。
