Title: President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo Hosts a Banquet to Welcome Members of the Finnish Cultural Delegation to Beijing

Author:
Date: 1953-04-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet in Beijing at 7:00 PM on April 22nd to welcome the members of the Finnish Cultural Delegation to Beijing, headed by Mrs. Kekkonen.

Those attending the banquet as hosts included: Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Hu Yuzhi, and Qian Duansheng, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Yuzhang, President of the People's University of China; Mao Dun and Zhou Yang, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation; Fang Shishan, Secretary-General of the Chinese Medical Association; Ji Chaoding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and many others from the capital's literary and artistic circles, including Cao Yu, Yu Lan, Cao Ming, Zhao Feng, Zhang Menggeng, and model worker Liu Yingyuan.

Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Lu Xuzhang, Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also attended the banquet.

Also invited to attend the banquet were Finnish Envoy to China, Mr. Knorring, and members of the embassy, as well as representatives of the Finnish trade delegation who came to China to negotiate trade.

At the meeting, President Zhang Xiruo first gave a speech, saying: "Although China and Finland are far apart, the friendship between the people of the two countries has not been restricted by geographical conditions. Since the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Finland, this friendship has further developed." He pointed out that the development of cultural and trade relations between China and Finland will undoubtedly play a significant role in enhancing mutual understanding and friendship between the two peoples, as well as in promoting world peace.

Mrs. Kekkonen gave a reply, saying: "This is our first visit to China, and we bring with us the friendship of the Finnish people for the peace-loving Chinese people and the leader of the Chinese people, Mao Zedong. Let me congratulate the growing cooperation between these two countries and their peoples. Our delegation will do everything possible to promote friendship between the two peoples."

Subsequently, Deputy Head of the Delegation, Mauri Luma, and Secretary-General of the Finland-China Association, Mrs. Lauri Kekkonen, presented gifts to the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs on behalf of the Finnish Cultural Delegation.

During the banquet, the guests frequently toasted to Sino-Finnish friendship and to the consolidation and development of Sino-Finnish cultural and trade relations.

### 中国人民外交学会会长张奚若  设宴欢迎芬兰文化代表团到京团员

1953-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民外交学会会长张奚若
    设宴欢迎芬兰文化代表团到京团员
    【新华社二十二日讯】中国人民外交学会会长张奚若于二十二日下午七时在北京设宴欢迎以吉科宁夫人为首的芬兰文化代表团到京团员。
    出席宴会作陪的有：中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、陈翰笙、胡愈之、钱端升，中国人民大学校长吴玉章，中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席茅盾、周扬，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠，中华医学会秘书长方石珊，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎及首都文学艺术界曹禺、于蓝、草明、赵沨、张梦庚、劳动模范刘英源等多人。
    中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗、中国进出口公司经理卢绪章、外交部交际处副处长沈平也出席了宴会。
    应邀参加宴会作陪的还有芬兰驻华公使克诺林与使馆人员及前来我国谈判贸易的芬兰贸易代表团代表。
    会上张奚若会长首先致词说：“中芬两国虽然相隔遥远，但是，两国人民间的友谊并没有受到地理条件的限制。自中芬建交以来，这种友谊更加发展了。”他指出，中芬两国间文化和贸易关系的发展对于两国人民相互间的了解和相互友谊的增进，以及对促进世界和平，无疑地将起很大的作用。”
    吉科宁夫人致答词说：“我们是第一次访问中国，我们带来了芬兰人民对爱好和平的中国人民和中国人民的领袖毛泽东的友谊。让我来祝贺这两个国家及其人民之间的合作逐年增长，我们代表团将尽一切的努力来促进两国人民之间的友谊。”
    接着由代表团副团长毛里·卢玛和芬中协会秘书长劳里凯南夫人代表芬兰文化代表团向中国人民外交学会赠礼。
    席间宾主频频为芬中友好、为芬中文化与贸易关系的巩固与发展而干杯。
