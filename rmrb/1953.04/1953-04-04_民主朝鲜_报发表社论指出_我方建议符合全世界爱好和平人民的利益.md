Title: "Democratic Korea" Publishes Editorial Pointing Out That Our Proposal Conforms to the Interests of Peace-Loving People Worldwide
Author:
Date: 1953-04-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 2nd. "Democratic Korea," the official newspaper of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, published an editorial on the 2nd, warmly endorsing the statement by Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, regarding the Korean armistice negotiations. The editorial stated: Premier Zhou Enlai issued a statement on March 30 concerning the resolution of the issue of exchanging sick and wounded prisoners of war, the resolution of the entire prisoner of war issue, and the achievement of a Korean armistice agreement. On the 31st, Premier Kim Il-sung issued a statement completely agreeing with the analysis of the political situation in the statement by the Government of the People's Republic of China, as well as the various specific conclusions and proposals put forward in that statement.

The editorial pointed out: Premier Kim Il-sung's statement once again demonstrated to the people of the world that the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is making every effort to rapidly realize the Korean armistice desired by all the good people of the world. Therefore, there is no doubt that Premier Kim Il-sung's statement will receive the support and approval of good people all over the world, thereby opening up broad avenues for the rapid achievement of a Korean armistice agreement.

The editorial emphasized: The proposals of the governments of Korea and China once again clearly demonstrate the consistent peaceful policy of the governments of Korea and China in their efforts to quickly achieve an armistice in Korea, peacefully resolve the Korean issue, and safeguard and consolidate world peace. This proposal fully conforms to the interests of peace-loving people all over the world. If the US side is truly willing to achieve an armistice in Korea and no longer trample on world peace, then it should immediately accept our proposal to resume the Korean armistice negotiations and our new proposal to smoothly resolve the entire prisoner of war issue, to show its sincerity for a Korean armistice.

### “民主朝鲜”报发表社论指出  我方建议符合全世界爱好和平人民的利益

1953-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    “民主朝鲜”报发表社论指出
    我方建议符合全世界爱好和平人民的利益
    【新华社平壤二日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府机关报“民主朝鲜”报在二日发表社论，热烈拥护朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成和中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来的关于朝鲜停战谈判问题的声明。社论说：周恩来总理在三月三十日发表了关于解决交换病伤战俘问题和解决全部战俘问题、达成朝鲜停战协定的声明。三十一日，金日成首相发表了关于完全同意中华人民共和国政府声明中的对于政治形势的分析和这个声明所提出的各项具体结论及建议的声明。
    社论指出：金日成首相的声明，在全世界人民面前又一次说明，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府为迅速实现全世界善良人民所渴望的朝鲜停战而尽一切努力。因此，毫无疑问，金日成首相的声明将受到全世界善良人民的支持和赞同，从而为迅速达成朝鲜停战协议打开了广阔的道路。
    社论着重指出：朝、中两国政府的建议，再一次明白地显示了朝、中两国政府为迅速在朝鲜实现停战、和平解决朝鲜问题、维护和巩固世界和平而努力的始终一贯的和平政策。这个建议完全符合全世界爱好和平人民的利益。如果美方真正愿意在朝鲜停战，不再蹂躏世界和平的话，那么就应立即接受我方关于恢复朝鲜停战谈判的建议和我方为了顺利地解决全部战俘问题的新建议，来表示自己对朝鲜停战的诚意。
