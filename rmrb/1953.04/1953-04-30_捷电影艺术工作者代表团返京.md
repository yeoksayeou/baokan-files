Title: Czechoslovakian Film Artists Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-04-30
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Czechoslovak Republic Film Artists Delegation returned to Beijing on the evening of the 29th after visiting the four cities of Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou.  Wang Lanxi, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, and film artists Cai Chusheng, Yu Ji and others were at the station to welcome them.

    Mr. Hedlicka, Cultural Attaché of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and other embassy staff members were also at the station to welcome them.

### 捷电影艺术工作者代表团返京

1953-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    捷电影艺术工作者代表团返京
    据新华社讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国电影艺术工作者代表团在访问天津、南京、上海、杭州四个城市后于二十九日晚返京。到车站欢迎的有中央人民政府文化部电影局副局长王阑西、电影艺术界人士蔡楚生、虞棘等人。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆文化专员赫德利奇卡及其他使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
