Title: Soviet Union Publishes Selected Works of Guo Moruo
Author:
Date: 1953-04-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency

    Soviet Union Publishes Selected Works of Guo Moruo
    [Xinhua News Agency] The Soviet Union's Foreign Literature Publishing Bureau has published a Russian-language edition of Selected Works of Guo Moruo, including the historical drama "Qu Yuan." The prominent Soviet writer Konstantin Simonov recently published an article in Literary Gazette to introduce and comment on it.

### 苏联出版郭沫若选集

1953-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联出版郭沫若选集
    【新华社讯】苏联外国文学作品出版局已出版了俄文的郭沫若选集，其中包括历史剧“屈原”等。苏联著名作家康斯坦丁·西蒙诺夫最近在文学报上发表一篇文章加以评介。
