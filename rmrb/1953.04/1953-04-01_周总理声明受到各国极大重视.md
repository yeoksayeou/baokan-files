Title: Premier Zhou's Statement Receives Great Attention from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1953-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 31st: Premier of the State Administrative Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai's statement on the Korean armistice negotiations has proposed new steps taken by the Chinese and Korean sides to promote the Korean armistice. This statement has aroused great attention among government officials in capitalist countries. According to a March 31st report from Agence France-Presse: Newspapers in capitalist countries are almost entirely commenting on this new proposal.

Premier Zhou's statement immediately became the center of discussion at the United Nations headquarters in New York. According to a March 30th dispatch from Agence France-Presse in New York: Delegations from various countries attending the United Nations believe that this proposal is the long-awaited beginning of achieving a Korean armistice. The prevailing discussion in the United Nations General Assembly lounge is that the proposal is considered "an encouraging sign."

The Indian representative to the United Nations, Menon, said that Zhou Enlai's statement was "great good news," and that the proposal opened a path leading to a more hopeful future for the world. He added that China's proposal brought hope to millions of people. Representatives from New Zealand and South Africa said that Zhou Enlai's statement was an exciting development. A spokesman for the British delegation said that the proposal was "very encouraging." The President of the United Nations General Assembly and Canadian representative, Pearson, said that he considered the proposal a very important statement that should be given close attention.

According to a March 30th report from United Press International in Washington, D.C., representatives of the Arab-Asian group of countries said that they believed that Zhou Enlai's proposal provided the brightest prospect to date, and that China sincerely hoped to end the Korean War.

In Oslo, the capital of Norway, Norwegian Foreign Minister Lange said: "The proposal made by China on the Korean issue gives us reason to hope that there is now a real basis for ending the Korean War."

In London, the capital of the United Kingdom, a British Foreign Office spokesman said that Zhou Enlai's proposal was "very encouraging."

In Australia, Foreign Minister Casey said on March 31st that Zhou Enlai's proposal "will be taken seriously."

Premier Zhou's statement also aroused widespread repercussions in the United States. The New York Herald Tribune commented on March 31st: "The public will regard this statement as a significant step towards peace in Korea." According to a March 30th dispatch from Agence France-Presse in Washington, D.C., Democratic Senator Clements said that Zhou Enlai's proposal was "encouraging."

### 周总理声明受到各国极大重视

1953-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理声明受到各国极大重视
    【新华社三十一日讯】我国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来关于朝鲜停战谈判问题的声明，提出了中朝方面为了促成朝鲜停战而采取的新的步骤。这个声明在资本主义各国政府人士中引起了极大重视。据法新社三十一日消息说：资本主义各国的报纸差不多完全在评论这个新建议。
    周总理声明发表后，立即成为纽约联合国总部谈论的中心。据法新社纽约联合国三十日电说：出席联合国的各国代表团认为这个建议是等待已久的实现朝鲜停战的开始。在联合国大会休息室内流行的议论是，认为这个建议是“一个令人鼓舞的迹象”。
    出席联合国的印度代表梅农说，周恩来的声明是“一个巨大的好消息”，声明中所提出的建议打开了一条导向世界更有希望的前途的道路。他又说，中国的建议为千百万人民带来了希望。新西兰及南非代表说，周恩来的声明是一个令人兴奋的发展。英国代表团发言人说，这个建议“是非常令人鼓舞的事”。联合国大会主席、加拿大代表皮尔逊说，他认为这个建议是一个很重要的声明，应予以密切注意。
    据合众社华盛顿三十日电报道，阿拉伯亚洲集团国家代表说，他们认为，周恩来的建议提供了迄今没有过的最光明的前景，中国诚意希望结束朝鲜战争。
    在挪威首都奥斯陆，挪威外交大臣兰基说：“中国提出的关于朝鲜问题的建议，使我们有理由希望：现在已有了结束朝鲜战争的真正基础了。”
    在英国首都伦敦，英国外交部发言人说：周恩来的提议是“很令人鼓舞的”。
    在澳大利亚，外交部长凯西三十一日说，周恩来的建议“将被认真地对待”。
    周总理的声明在美国国内同样也引起广泛的反响。“纽约先驱论坛报”三十一日发表评论说：“公众将认为这个声明是走向朝鲜和平的显著的一步。”据法新社华盛顿三十日电报道，民主党参议员克莱门兹说，周恩来的建议是“令人鼓舞的”。
