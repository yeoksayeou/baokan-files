Author:
Date: 1953-04-07
Page: 3

Nationwide Qingming Festival Sweeps of Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemeteries
    Vice Chairman Zhu De and Others Pay Respects and Lay Wreaths at the Beijing Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery

On the 5th, it was Qingming Festival, and Party and government organs and representatives of people from all walks of life across the country went to sweep the tombs of revolutionary martyrs.

In Beijing, Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government, along with more than 300 representatives from the General Office of the CPC Central Committee, the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the North China Administrative Committee, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and central, North China, and Beijing municipal organizations and groups, paid respects and laid wreaths at the Beijing Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery. Many friends of the martyrs during their lifetimes also came to mourn. Ren Bishi's tomb was covered with wreaths.

Nearly 10,000 representatives from various organs, people's organizations, democratic parties, the Songhu Garrison District troops, and all sectors of society in East China and Shanghai went to Hongqiao, Dachang, Jiangwan, Gaoqiao, and other places to sweep the tombs of revolutionary martyrs.

More than 1,500 representatives from Party and government organs and people from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City went to the Yuhuatai Martyrs' Cemetery on the Qingming Festival on the 5th to sweep the tombs and lay wreaths to pay their respects.

Representatives from various organs in Guangzhou City went to the Tomb of the Seventy-Two Martyrs at Huanghuagang and other places to sweep the tombs. Representatives of the people from all walks of life in Wuhan City went to the Soviet Volunteer Air Force Cemetery and the tomb of martyr Shi Yang in Hongshan, Wuchang, to sweep the tombs.

More than 2,000 representatives from all walks of life in Xi'an held a public memorial meeting for revolutionary martyrs at the Xi'an Municipal People's Government Square on Qingming Festival. After the meeting, the chief celebrant, accompanying celebrants, and family members of the martyrs also went to the Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery in Balicun, a suburb of the city, to sweep the tombs and offer sacrifices.

On Qingming Festival, more than 700 representatives from all ethnic groups and all walks of life, as well as staff members of organs and organizations, went to the tombs of martyrs such as the late CPC Central Committee member Chen Tanqiu, outstanding CPC members Mao Zemin, Qiao Guozhen, Lin Jilu, and democratic progressive figure Du Chongyuan in the suburbs of Dihua to offer sacrifices and mourn.

In Ulanhot, eastern Inner Mongolia, more than 1,200 representatives from various organs, organizations, and people from all ethnic groups gathered at the People's Liberation War Martyrs Memorial Tower in Martyrs' Park and the Soviet Red Army Martyrs' Tower in People's Square to lay wreaths and solemnly hold a memorial ceremony.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国各地清明节祭扫革命烈士陵墓  朱德副主席等前往北京革命烈士公墓致敬并献花圈

1953-04-07
第3版()
专栏：

    全国各地清明节祭扫革命烈士陵墓
    朱德副主席等前往北京革命烈士公墓致敬并献花圈
    五日是清明节，全国各地党政机关和各界人民代表纷纷前往祭扫革命烈士陵墓。
    在北京，中央人民政府朱德副主席和中共中央办公厅、中共中央华北局、华北行政委员会、北京市人民政府及中央、华北、北京市机关团体代表共三百多人，前往北京革命烈士公墓致敬并献花圈。许多烈士生前好友，也前往悼念。任弼时同志的墓前放满了花圈。
    华东和上海市各机关、各人民团体、各民主党派，淞沪警备区部队和各界代表近万人分赴虹桥、大场、江湾、高桥等地祭扫革命烈士墓。
    江苏省、南京市党政机关和各界人民代表一千五百多人，在五日清明节到雨花台烈士陵扫墓，并献花圈致敬。
    广州市各机关代表到黄花岗七十二烈士墓等处祭扫。武汉市各界人民代表曾分赴苏联志愿空军墓，武昌洪山施洋烈士墓等处祭扫。
    西安各界代表二千多人在西安市人民政府广场举行清明节公祭革命先烈大会。会后，大会的主祭人、陪祭人和烈士家属还到市郊八里村革命烈士陵园扫墓祭奠。
    迪化市郊的故中共中央委员陈潭秋、中共优秀党员毛泽民、乔国桢、林基路和民主进步人士杜重远等烈士的墓前，在清明节有各族各界人民代表和机关团体工作人员七百多人前往奠祭和悼念。
    内蒙古东部区乌兰浩特的各机关团体和各族各界人民代表一千二百多人，集合在烈士公园人民解放战争烈士纪念塔和人民广场苏联红军烈士塔前敬献花圈，隆重举行祭扫仪式。
                                              （据新华社讯）
