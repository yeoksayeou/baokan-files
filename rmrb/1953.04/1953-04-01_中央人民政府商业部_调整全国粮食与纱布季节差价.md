Title: The Ministry of Commerce of the Central People's Government Adjusts Seasonal Price Differences for Grain and Cloth Nationwide
Author: 
Date: 1953-04-01
Page: 1

    Ministry of Commerce of the Central People's Government
    Adjusts Seasonal Price Differences for Grain and Cloth Nationwide
    [Xinhua News Agency, 1st] The seasonal price differences for grain and cloth nationwide were adjusted simultaneously on April 1st. According to regulations from the Ministry of Commerce of the Central People's Government, the national average seasonal price difference for grain will be raised by 3.99 percent, while the price of cloth will decrease by 2 percent in production cities and by an average of 3 to 5 percent in sales cities and rural areas. The price of yarn will decrease by 2.5 percent in production cities. The price of knitted goods will also be reduced according to this standard. Regional price differences for grain and cloth will also be reasonably adjusted at the same time.

### 中央人民政府商业部  调整全国粮食与纱布季节差价

1953-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府商业部
    调整全国粮食与纱布季节差价
    【新华社一日讯】全国粮食与纱布季节差价于四月一日同时调整。据中央人民政府商业部规定，全国粮食季节差价全国平均上调百分之三点九九，布价在产地城市下落百分之二，在销地城市及乡村平均下落百分之三至五。纱价在产地城市下落百分之二点五。针织品价格亦按此标准降低。粮食与纱布的地区差价同时作合理的调整。
