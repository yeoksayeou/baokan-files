Title: Vietnam Holds Second National Peace Conference
Author: 
Date: 1953-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, April 3rd: The Second Vietnam National Peace Conference was held from March 19th to 22nd. The conference discussed the tasks of the Vietnamese people in defending peace and elected a new leadership structure for the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

    After Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, reviewed the development of the world peace movement in recent years, the conference summarized the following achievements of the Vietnamese people in defending world peace since 1950: the work of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace in explaining the close relationship between the Vietnamese War of Resistance and the world peace movement, and in strengthening the friendship between the Vietnamese people and the people of the Soviet Union, China, the People's Democratic countries, and all peace-loving people in the world; the signature campaigns to ban the use of atomic weapons and to demand that the five major powers conclude a peace pact, which collected five million and ten million signatures respectively; the initiation of short-term competition campaigns to celebrate various international peace conferences, and the dispatch of delegations to attend these conferences.
    The conference heard a report by Nguyen Xuan Thuy, deputy head of the Vietnamese delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and the World People's Peace Congress.
    The conference discussed the tasks of the Vietnamese people in the struggle for peace and unanimously agreed that: In 1953, the central work of the Vietnamese people in defending world peace is to: mobilize the masses to implement the policy of rent reduction and interest reduction, enrich the strength of the War of Resistance, and promote the War of Resistance; strengthen friendship with the people of the Soviet Union, China and the People's Democratic countries, and the peace-loving people of the world; and study the resolutions of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and the World People's Peace Congress.
    On the last day, the conference unanimously passed a letter of appeal, calling on the Vietnamese people to work hard to implement the resolutions of the conference. The conference also elected a new leadership structure for the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, consisting of forty-two members. All representatives unanimously elected President Ho Chi Minh as the honorary chairman of the committee.

### 越南举行第二届全国和平大会

1953-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    越南举行第二届全国和平大会
    【新华社四日讯】据越南通讯社北越三日讯：第二届越南全国和平大会在三月十九日到二十二日召开。大会讨论了越南人民保卫和平的任务，并选出了越南保卫世界和平委员会的新的领导机构。
    在越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎庭探回顾了几年来世界和平运动的发展以后，大会总结了一九五○年以来越南人民在保卫世界和平方面的下列成就：越南保卫世界和平委员会在解释越南抗战和世界和平运动的密切关系方面，在加强越南人民和苏联、中国、人民民主国家的人民以及全世界爱好和平的人民的友谊方面所做的工作；在禁止使用原子武器和要求五大国缔结和平公约的签名运动中，分别征集了五百万和一千万个签名；发起庆祝各个国际性和平会议的短期竞赛运动，并派代表团出席这些会议。
    大会听取了出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议和世界人民和平大会的越南代表团副团长阮春水的报告。
    大会讨论了越南人民为和平而斗争的任务，一致认为：在一九五三年，越南人民保卫世界和平的中心工作是：发动群众以贯彻减租减息政策，充实抗战力量，推进抗战；加强和苏联、中国和人民民主国家的人民以及世界爱好和平人民的友谊；学习亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议和世界人民和平大会的决议。
    大会在最后一天一致通过呼吁书，号召越南人民努力执行大会的决议。大会并选出包括四十二名委员的越南保卫世界和平委员会的新的领导机构。全体代表一致推选胡志明主席为委员会的名誉主席。
