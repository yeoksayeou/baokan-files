Title: Mongolian Committee for the Defense of Peace Warmly Supports Premier Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1953-04-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st:

    The Mongolian Committee for the Defense of Peace sent a telegram on the 18th to Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, expressing warm support for the statement by Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, on the Korean armistice negotiations. The telegram stated:

    The statement by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, regarding the Korean peace negotiations, and the statement by V.M. Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, regarding the Korean issue, have been met with broad response and warm support from all the Mongolian people. Our people believe that the peace proposals of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea are the only way to resolve international tensions. These concrete proposals are in line with the fundamental interests of the Mongolian people and the people of all countries around the world who yearn to end the threat of new wars. The Mongolian Committee for the Defense of Peace, representing the views and sentiments of the public in the Mongolian People's Republic, endorses the unanimous demand of people around the world for an armistice in Korea.

### 蒙古保卫和平委员会热烈支持周总理声明

1953-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古保卫和平委员会热烈支持周总理声明
    【新华社二十一日讯】蒙古保卫和平委员会在十八日致电中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘宁一，对于我国周恩来总理兼外交部部长关于朝鲜停战谈判问题的声明表示热烈的支持。电文说：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来关于朝鲜和平谈判的声明和苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫关于朝鲜问题的声明，得到全体蒙古人民广泛的响应和热烈的支持。我国人民认为中华人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的和平建议乃是解决国际紧张局势的唯一途径。这些具体建议，符合热望结束新战争威胁的蒙古人民和全世界各国人民的根本利益。蒙古保卫和平委员会代表着蒙古人民共和国公众的见解和情感，对全世界各国人民关于朝鲜停战的一致要求表示附议。
