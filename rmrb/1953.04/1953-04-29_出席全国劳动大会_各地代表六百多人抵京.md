Title: Over Six Hundred Delegates from Across the Country Arrive in Beijing to Attend the National Labor Conference
Author:
Date: 1953-04-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: As of the 28th, 671 delegates attending the Seventh National Labor Conference and 21 observers have successively reported to the conference. The four volunteer railway worker representatives who resisted America and aided Korea, the Northeast delegation headed by Zhang Weizhen, chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, the Southwest delegation headed by Yang Chao, deputy director of the Southwest Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Inner Mongolia delegation headed by Yang Zhenben, vice chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Federation of Trade Unions, the North China Regional Delegation, and the National Railway Workers' Delegation have all arrived. Delegations from East China, Central South, and Northwest regions, as well as delegations from the ordnance industry, civil aviation, and seafarers, are also arriving in Beijing to register. The remaining delegates are on their way.

### 出席全国劳动大会  各地代表六百多人抵京

1953-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    出席全国劳动大会
    各地代表六百多人抵京
    【新华社二十八日讯】到二十八日为止，已有六百七十一位出席第七次全国劳动大会的代表和二十一位列席代表，陆续向大会报到。出席大会的四位志愿抗美援朝铁路员工代表和以东北总工会主席张维桢为首的东北代表团、以中华全国总工会西南办事处副主任杨超为首的西南代表团、以内蒙古自治区总工会副主席杨珍本为首的内蒙代表团、华北区代表团、全国铁路员工代表团的代表已全部到齐。华东、中南、西北等地区的代表团和兵工、民用航空、海员等代表团也正陆续抵京报到。其余代表正在途中。
