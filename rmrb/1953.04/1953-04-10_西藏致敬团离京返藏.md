Title: Tibetan Delegation Departs Beijing for Tibet

Author:

Date: 1953-04-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 9th: The head of the Tibetan Delegation, Liu Xia·Tudeng Taba, deputy heads Danba Rijie and Dingjia·Duojijicun, led the members of the delegation to bid farewell to the motherland's capital on the 9th and set off for Tibet. Those who came to the railway station to see them off included: Liu Geping, Wang Feng, Liu Chun, Zhang Zhiyi, deputy directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and Yang Jingren and other members in Beijing, Luo Qiong, director of the Propaganda Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the director and deputy directors of the Dalai's Office in Beijing, the Panchen's Office in Beijing, cadres of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission, and representatives of the Central Institute for Nationalities.

The Tibetan Delegation arrived in Beijing on September 30th last year to pay tribute to Chairman Mao and participate in the National Day ceremony. On November 9th, they departed to visit various parts of the motherland. They returned to Beijing on January 31st this year, having visited industrial and mining enterprises, cultural and educational construction, temples, and historical sites in fifteen cities in the Northeast, Inner Mongolia, North China, East China, and Northwest regions. Before leaving Beijing, Liu Xia·Tudeng Taba, Danba Rijie, and delegation member Xuekangjiao broadcast their impressions of visiting various parts of the motherland to Tibet in Tibetan on the Central People's Broadcasting Station; delegation member Zeren Zhuoma broadcast her impressions of attending the World People's Peace Congress.

The first group of Tibetan Delegation members returning to Tibet had already passed through Kangding, the capital of the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province, on April 1st on their way back to Tibet.

### 西藏致敬团离京返藏

1953-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏致敬团离京返藏
    【新华社九日讯】西藏致敬团团长柳霞·土登塔巴、副团长丹巴日杰、定甲·多吉吉村率领该团团员在九日和祖国首都告别，首途返西藏。到火车站欢送的有：中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、汪锋、刘春、张执一和在京委员杨静仁等，中华全国民主妇女联合会宣传部部长罗琼，达赖驻京办事处、班禅驻京办事处正副处长及中央民族事务委员会干部、中央民族学院的代表等。
    西藏致敬团在去年九月三十日到达北京，向毛主席致敬，并参加国庆节典礼。十一月九日出发到祖国各地参观，今年一月三十一日返京，先后参观了东北、内蒙古、华北、华东和西北地区十五个城市的工矿、文教建设和寺院、名胜古迹等。在离京前夕，柳霞·土登塔巴、丹巴日杰和团员雪康角曾在中央人民广播电台用藏语向西藏广播参观祖国各地的观感；团员泽仁卓玛广播了出席世界人民和平大会的观感。
    西藏致敬团第一批回西藏团员，已在四月一日路经西康省藏族自治区首府康定返西藏。
