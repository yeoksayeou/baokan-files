Title: Pakistan Forms New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1953-04-26
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, April 25th: Pakistan's new cabinet was formed on the 18th, with Muhammad Ali as Prime Minister. The Governor-General of Pakistan, Ghulam Muhammad, dismissed Khawaja Nazimuddin from the post of Prime Minister on the 17th and requested Muhammad Ali to form a cabinet. According to a communiqué issued by the Governor-General's Office of Pakistan, Nazimuddin's dismissal was due to the cabinet's inability to cope with the food shortage and economic disorder.

### 巴基斯坦组成新内阁

1953-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦组成新内阁
    【新华社二十五日讯】喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦新内阁在十八日组成，由穆罕默德·阿里任总理。巴基斯坦总督吴拉姆·穆罕默德十七日解除库瓦吉·纳齐穆丁的总理职务，要求穆罕默德·阿里组阁。据巴基斯坦总督府发表的公报说，纳齐穆丁的被解职是因为内阁无力对付粮荒和经济紊乱。
