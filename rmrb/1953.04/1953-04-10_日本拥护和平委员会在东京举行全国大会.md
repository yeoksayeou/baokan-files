Title: Japan Peace Protection Committee Holds National Congress in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1953-04-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 9th. News: Following the People's Congress for Peace in Japan, the national congress of the Japan Peace Protection Committee was convened in Tokyo on March 23rd. Approximately one hundred people, including Ikuo Oyama, participated in the congress. The congress discussed the 1953 policy of the Japan Peace Protection Committee.

### 日本拥护和平委员会在东京举行全国大会

1953-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    日本拥护和平委员会在东京举行全国大会
    【新华社九日讯】东京消息：在日本人民争取和平大会举行之后，日本拥护和平委员会全国大会于三月二十三日在东京召开。参加大会的有大山郁夫等一百人左右。大会讨论了日本拥护和平委员会一九五三年度的方针。
