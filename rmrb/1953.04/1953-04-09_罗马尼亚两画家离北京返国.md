Title: Two Romanian Painters Depart Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1953-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: Noted painters from the Romanian People's Republic, Marcovici Lisia (female) and Taru Eugene, departed Beijing for home on the morning of the 8th after visiting and touring the cities of Beijing and Shanghai, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Datong, and Bengbu in our country. Those who came to the airport to see them off included Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government; Ye Qianyu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Association of Fine Arts Workers; and representatives from the Art Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government. An Ding, Economic Counselor of the Embassy of the Romanian People's Republic in China, and First Secretary Meng Tiannu also came to see them off.

### 罗马尼亚两画家离北京返国

1953-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚两画家离北京返国
    【新华社八日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国名画家马科威·里霞（女）和塔鲁·尤金在我国首都北京和上海、广州、杭州、大同、蚌埠等城市参观访问后已在八日晨离京返国。到机场欢送者有中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，中华全国美术工作者协会副主席叶浅予及中央人民政府文化部艺术事业管理局代表等。罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使馆经济参赞安定及一等秘书孟天努亦前往送行。
