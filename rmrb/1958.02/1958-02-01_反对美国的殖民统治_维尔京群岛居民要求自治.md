Title: Against U.S. Colonial Rule: Virgin Islands Residents Demand Autonomy
Author:
Date: 1958-02-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 31st dispatch from Saint Thomas: Residents of the U.S. territory of the Virgin Islands in the Caribbean held demonstrations in Saint Thomas on the 30th, demanding autonomy.

They petitioned the local government, demanding that the governor of the Virgin Islands be elected, that the local legislature have full power, and that the Virgin Islands send a permanent representative to the U.S. Congress.

The petition emphasized that the people of the Virgin Islands can no longer tolerate the "colonial elements" in the Virgin Islands "Constitution." According to the current "Constitution," the U.S. Department of the Interior has complete jurisdiction over the Virgin Islands.

Petitioners also demanded the replacement of the current Governor Walter Gordon.

The Virgin Islands, with a population of nearly 30,000, was purchased by the United States from Denmark in 1917.

### 反对美国的殖民统治  维尔京群岛居民要求自治

1958-02-01
第5版()
专栏：

　　反对美国的殖民统治
    维尔京群岛居民要求自治
    新华社31日讯　维尔京群岛圣托马斯城消息：位于加勒比海区的美国属地维尔京群岛的居民30日在圣托马斯举行示威，要求实行自治。
    他们向当地政府请愿，要求通过选举来产生维尔京群岛的总督，要求让当地的立法机构有充分权力，并且要求让维尔京群岛派一个常驻代表到美国国会里去。
    请愿书强调说，维尔京人民不能再容忍维尔京“构成法”中的“殖民主义的东西”。根据目前的“构成法”，美国内政部对维尔京享有完全的管辖权。
    请愿者还要求撤换现任总督华尔特·戈登。
    拥有近三万居民的维尔京群岛是美国在1917年从丹麦手中购得的。
