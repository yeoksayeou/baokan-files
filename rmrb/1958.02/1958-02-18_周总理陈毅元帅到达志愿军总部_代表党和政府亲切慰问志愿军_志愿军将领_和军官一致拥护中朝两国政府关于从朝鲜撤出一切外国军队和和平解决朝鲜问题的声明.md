Title: Premier Zhou and Marshal Chen Yi Arrive at Volunteer Army Headquarters, Conveying the Party and Government's Cordial Greetings to the Volunteer Army. Volunteer Army Generals and Officers Unanimously Support the Sino-Korean Governments' Declaration on the Withdrawal of All Foreign Troops from Korea and the Peaceful Settlement of the Korean Question.
Author:
Date: 1958-02-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 16th. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and General Su Yu arrived at the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the evening of the 16th. They were warmly welcomed by the Volunteer Army's generals and officers.

Premier Kim Il-sung and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Nam Il accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai and his party to the train station near the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. Premier Zhou Enlai and Premier Kim Il-sung were warmly welcomed at the station by generals and officers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army headquarters, headed by General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping.

After the band played the national anthems of China and Korea, Premier Kim Il-sung and Premier Zhou Enlai jointly reviewed the guard of honor composed of Chinese People's Volunteer Army soldiers.

Subsequently, Premier Kim Il-sung and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Nam Il returned to Pyongyang by train.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 17th. Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and General Su Yu received the generals and officers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, headed by General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping, at noon today.

During the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai listened to the opinions of the Volunteer Army's generals and officers on the issue of the withdrawal of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army from Korea. The Volunteer Army's generals and officers unanimously expressed their support for the declaration of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on February 5, 1958, and the Chinese Government on February 7, 1958, regarding the withdrawal of all foreign troops from Korea and the peaceful settlement of the Korean question.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 17th. Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and General Su Yu attended a welcome rally held for them by the Chinese People's Volunteer Army this afternoon and met with the generals and officers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

The welcome rally lasted for more than three hours. Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and General Su Yu spoke at the meeting successively. They represented the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese Government in extending their cordial greetings to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and encouraged all officers to guard against arrogance and rashness, study hard, and cultivate themselves into military experts who are both civil and martial, both red and expert. They called on the vast numbers of officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to display a high degree of internationalist spirit, and humbly learn from the merits of the hardworking Korean people and the heroic Korean People's Army. In their speeches, they also introduced the situation of the 600 million people of the motherland building socialism with revolutionary vigor.

The generals and officers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army felt extremely excited after listening to Premier Zhou Enlai's speech, and these speeches were warmly welcomed.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 17th. Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and General Su Yu had a reunion dinner with the generals and officers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on Lunar New Year's Eve. Kim Kwi-nam, Director of Protocol of the Korean Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai to the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, also attended the dinner.

At the dinner, Premier Zhou Enlai proposed a toast to the friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples. He said that this friendship is an eternal friendship, an immortal friendship. Premier Zhou also proposed a toast to the Korean Workers' Party, the Korean government, and the Korean people, and to the health of Premier Kim Il-sung.

The atmosphere at the dinner was lively, and people drank all the famous Chinese Maotai liquor in their glasses. Director Kim Kwi-nam also toasted Premier Zhou Enlai.

The dinner was very lively. General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, said that he felt unspeakably happy to be able to reunite with Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and General Su Yu on Lunar New Year's Eve. He proposed a toast to their health. During the dinner, people continuously walked up to Premier Zhou and toasted him, and the banquet hall was filled with an extremely cordial and harmonious atmosphere. After the dinner, Premier Zhou and others attended the evening party.

### 周总理陈毅元帅到达志愿军总部  代表党和政府亲切慰问志愿军  志愿军将领　和军官一致拥护中朝两国政府关于从朝鲜撤出一切外国军队和和平解决朝鲜问题的声明

1958-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈毅元帅到达志愿军总部
    代表党和政府亲切慰问志愿军
    志愿军将领　和军官一致拥护中朝两国政府关于从朝鲜撤出一切外国军队和和平解决朝鲜问题的声明
    新华社平壤16日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长、张闻天副部长和粟裕大将在16日晚上到达了中国人民志愿军总部。他们受到了志愿军将领和军官的热烈欢迎。
    金日成首相、南日副首相兼外务相一直陪送周恩来总理一行到达中国人民志愿军总部驻地附近的火车站。周恩来总理和金日成首相在车站上受到了以杨勇上将和王平上将为首的中国人民志愿军总部将领和军官的热烈欢迎。
    在乐队奏中朝两国国歌以后，金日成首相和周恩来总理一起检阅了中国人民志愿军战士组成的仪仗队。
    随后，金日成首相和南日副首相兼外务相乘火车返回平壤。
    新华社平壤17日电　周恩来总理、陈毅元帅、张闻天副部长、粟裕大将今天中午接见了以杨勇上将和王平上将为首的中国人民志愿军的将领和军官。
    在接见的时候，周恩来总理曾经就有关中国人民志愿军从朝鲜撤出的问题听取了志愿军将领和军官们的意见。志愿军将领和军官们一致表示拥护朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府1958年2月5日和中国政府1958年2月7日关于从朝鲜撤出一切外国军队和和平解决朝鲜问题的声明。
    新华社平壤17日电　周恩来总理、陈毅元帅、张闻天副部长、粟裕大将在今天下午出席了中国人民志愿军为他们举行的欢迎大会，会见了中国人民志愿军的将军和军官们。
    欢迎大会进行了三个多小时。周恩来总理、陈毅元帅、张闻天副部长、粟裕大将先后在会上讲了话。他们代表中国共产党中央委员会和中国政府，向中国人民志愿军表示亲切的慰问，并且勉励全军干部戒骄戒躁，努力学习，把自己培养成为亦文亦武、又红又专的军事专家。他们号召中国人民志愿军的广大干部和战士发挥高度的国际主义精神，虚心学习勤劳的朝鲜人民和英勇的朝鲜人民军的优点。他们在讲话中还介绍了祖国六亿人民鼓起革命干劲建设社会主义的情况。
    中国人民志愿军的将军和军官们听了周恩来总理的讲话以后，感到异常兴奋，这些讲话受到了热烈的欢迎。
    新华社平壤17日电　周恩来总理、陈毅元帅、张闻天副部长、粟裕大将在春节除夕同中国人民志愿军的将领和军官一起聚餐联欢。陪同周恩来总理到达中国人民志愿军总部的朝鲜外务省礼仪司司长金贵南等也参加了聚餐会。
    周恩来总理在聚餐会上提议为中朝两国人民的友谊干杯。他说，这种友谊是永恒的友谊、不朽的友谊。周总理还提议为朝鲜劳动党、朝鲜政府和朝鲜人民干杯，为金日成首相的健康干杯。
    聚餐会上气氛活跃，人们饮尽了杯中的中国茅台名酒，金贵南司长也同周恩来总理干了杯。
    聚餐会进行得非常热闹。中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将说，除夕晚上能够同周恩来总理、陈毅元帅、张闻天副部长、粟裕大将团聚，感到有说不出的高兴。他提议为他们的健康干杯。席间，人们川流不息地走到周总理的面前，向他敬酒，宴会大厅里洋溢着极为亲切和睦的气氛。聚餐会结束以后，周总理等出席了晚会。
