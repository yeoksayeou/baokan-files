Title: Welcoming Spring Song – A Collection of Street Poems (Part 1)
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1958-02-27
Page: 8

Welcoming Spring Song
    ——A Collection of Street Poems (Part 1)
    Tian Jian
    Dedicated to Nanshuiquan Township
    The mountains are mountains of treasure,
    The trees are money-shaking trees,
    With diligent hands and minds,
    Treasures will be unearthed.
    Dragon King
    Why worry about no rain,
    The Dragon King is among the people—
    Digging large canals by the mountains,
    Building water stations by the villages. (Note)
    Want to know who the Dragon King is?
    The agricultural cooperative's members!
    (Note) An electric pumping station is about to be built in Nanshuiquan Township and near the Guanting Reservoir in Huailai County.
    Rebuilding the Great Wall
    Red flowers build into watchtowers,
    Green leaves weave into battlements,
    Rebuilding a Great Wall,
    To stop flying sand and rolling stones.
    Let reeds and fruit trees,
    Grow on the banks of Guanting Lake.
    Treasure Vase and Treasure Sword (Note)
    Spring water is like a treasure vase,
    An arm is like a treasure sword.
    Carry the treasure vase on your shoulder,
    To fight a decisive battle against conservative thinking;
    Raise the treasure sword high again,
    Turn the waterlogged land into rice paddies.
    (Note) Hero Dong Cunrui, in his childhood, hoped to find a treasure vase to bring spring water to his village: Nanshanbao, Huailai.
    Trees and Seeds
    A seed sown into the soil,
    A sapling can grow out.
    Losing a seed,
    Is losing a tree.
    Hoe and Rice Fork
    Look at the harvest on the hoe
    —Peasant saying
    Water will drip on the hoe,
    Fire will hang on the rice fork;
    Hoe and rice fork,
    Never leave your hands,
    Living in deep mountains and valleys,
    Sow your seeds,
    You can also harvest,
    Several burdens of grain and fruit.
    Fighting a Battle with Wasteland
    The mountain valley rumbles and shakes,
    The rocks tremble,
    This is the hero's hoe,
    Fighting a decisive battle with the wasteland.
    The barren mountains finally roll up their flags,
    Bending over and lowering their heads.
    To the Mountainous Areas
    The mountainous area is like a treasure house,
    With golden thresholds and silver windows;
    Twenty-four solar terms in a year,
    Every solar term has an "autumn."
    There is something to be harvested everywhere in the mountainous area,
    It just depends on whether we reach out our hands.
    Draft Mobilization Order (1)
    High mountains are bronze drums,
    Beat them, the motherland is cheering.
    The hoe resounds in the air,
    Life takes a step forward.
    Draft Mobilization Order (2)
    Trees are flags, the earth is a drum,
    Brothers, quickly wield your hoes,
    Flying sand, rolling stones; ice and snow torrents,
    Have already cleared a path for spring.
    About an Inch of Land
    Even if it is just an inch of land,
    If diligently cultivated,
    There will be several burdens of fruit,
    And rice ears yielding a thousand catties per mu.
    Calculate this account,
    How many times can production potential be increased?
    A Call
    The Great Wall is old,
    But we are very young;
    Boost revolutionary enthusiasm,
    Cross the Great Wall—
    "Plant money-shaking trees all over the mountains,
    Turn reservoirs into cornucopias!" (Note)
    (Note) This is a call from the Huailai County Party Committee at a thousand-person meeting.
    Letter of Determination
    Arms are like long swords,
    Determination is like flames.
    Standing in front of the Party,
    Writing a letter of determination:
    We will use bright long swords,
    To make the earth shake,
    In one spring,
    Plant thousands of mu of orchards!

### 迎春曲——街头诗一束（上）

1958-02-27
第8版()
专栏：

    迎春曲
    ——街头诗一束（上）
    田间
    献给南水泉乡
    山是万宝山，
    树是摇钱树，
    手脑两勤快，
    宝贝就出土。
    龙王
    何愁不下雨，
    龙王在人间——
    山边挖大渠，
    村边造水站。　（注）
    要问龙王他是谁？
    农业社的社员！
    （注）在怀来县南水泉乡和官厅水库的附近地区即将造一座电力扬水站。
    再造一座长城
    红花搭成城楼，
    绿叶编成墙垛，
    再造一座万里长城，
    拦住飞沙走石。
    让苇子和果树，
    长在官厅湖畔。
    宝瓶和宝剑（注）
    泉水好像一只宝瓶，
    手臂好像一把宝剑。
    要把宝瓶背在肩上，
    向保守思想决一战；
    再高高地举起宝剑，
    把涝洼地变成稻田。
    （注）英雄董存瑞，幼年时候曾希望找到一只宝瓶，把泉水带到他的村子：怀来南山堡。
    树和种子
    一粒种子撒下土，
    一棵树苗就能长出。
    丢掉了一粒种子，
    就是丢掉一棵树。
    锄头和稻叉
    锄头上看年景
    ——庄稼话
    锄头上会滴水，
    稻叉上会挂火；
    锄头和稻叉，
    时刻不离手头，
    住在深山野谷，
    播下你的种子，
    你也能够收获，
    几担谷子和水果。
    和荒地打一仗
    山谷隆隆地震动，
    岩石摇摇地发抖，
    这是英雄的锄头，
    正在和荒地决斗。
    荒山终于卷起旗帜，
    弯下腰来低下头。
    给山区
    山区如同一座宝库，
    金的门槛银的窗户；
    一年二十四个节季，
    每一个节季都有“秋”。
    山区到处都有得收，
    就看我们伸不伸手。
    拟动员令（一）
    高山是一面铜鼓，
    敲吧，祖国在欢呼。
    锄头在空中一响，
    生活就前进一步。
    拟动员令（二）
    树木是旗，大地是鼓，
    兄弟们，快挥起锄头，
    飞砂走石；冰雪激流，
    已经为春天让开一条路。
    关于一寸土
    哪怕是一寸土地，
    得到辛勤的栽培，
    就会有几担果子，
    和亩产千斤的谷穗。
    算一算这一笔账吧，
    生产潜力能有几倍？
    一个号召
    长城虽说很古老，
    我们倒是很年轻；
    鼓起革命干劲，
    跨过万里长城——
    “遍山栽起摇钱树，
    水库变成聚宝盆！”　（注）
    （注）这是怀来县委在千人大会上的一个号召
    决心书
    手臂好比是长剑，
    决心好比是火焰。
    站在党的前面，
    写下一封决心书：
    要用雪亮的长剑，
    使地球摇摇发颤，
    在一个春天里，
    栽起千亩果园！
