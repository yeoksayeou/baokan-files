Title: Subandrio Refutes Dulles, Warns Against Interference in Indonesia

Author:

Date: 1958-02-13
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 12th: U.S. Secretary of State Dulles's public remarks at a press conference openly interfering in Indonesia's internal affairs have provoked a strong reaction from the Indonesian government. Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio warned on the 12th against external interference in Indonesia's internal affairs.
    Subandrio said that if the reports about Dulles's remarks are true, the Indonesian government finds this somewhat strange, because Dulles is making evaluations on political issues that are only related to Indonesia's internal affairs. He said that the Indonesian people hope that various different opinions on "guided democracy" will not be used by outside parties to disrupt Indonesia's affairs.
    Subandrio said that he has summoned the U.S. Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to Indonesia, Sterling Cottrell, to request clarification of Dulles's remarks.
    Jodi Gondokusumo of the Foreign Affairs Subcommittee of the Indonesian Parliament commented on the 12th that Dulles's remarks are in effect an interference in Indonesia's internal affairs. Jodi said that Dulles's remarks are parallel to the activities of separatist elements within Indonesia, and they will provide spiritual support to Indonesia's opposition parties.

### 苏班德里约驳斥杜勒斯  警告不要干涉印度尼西亚

1958-02-13
第6版()
专栏：

    苏班德里约驳斥杜勒斯
    警告不要干涉印度尼西亚
    据新华社雅加达12日电　美国国务卿杜勒斯在记者招待会上发表的公开干预印度尼西亚内部事务的谈话，已经引起了印度尼西亚政府方面的强烈反应。印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约12日警告外界不要干预印度尼西亚的内部事务。
    苏班德里约说，如果关于杜勒斯的谈话的消息是真实的话，印度尼西亚政府就觉得这有些奇怪，因为杜勒斯对只是同印度尼西亚内部事务有关的政治问题作出评价。他说，印度尼西亚人民希望那些对“有指导的民主”的各种不同的意见不致于被外界利用来扰乱印度尼西亚的事务。
    苏班德里约说，他已经召见美国驻印度尼西亚临时代办斯特林·科特勒耳，要求澄清杜勒斯的谈话。
    印度尼西亚国会外交小组委员会的佐迪·龚多古苏莫12日评论说，杜勒斯的谈话实际上已经是对印度尼西亚内政的干涉。佐迪说，杜勒斯的谈话同印度尼西亚国内的分裂分子的活动是平行的，它会给予印度尼西亚的反对党派以精神上的支持。
