Title: Further Strengthen the Friendship Between the People of the Soviet Union and India - Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Visits India
Author:
Date: 1958-02-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 15th. A delegation from the Soviet Supreme Soviet, led by Lobanov, Chairman of the Soviet Supreme Soviet of the Union, arrived in New Delhi this morning.

The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Ayengar, Speaker of the Indian House of the People, and Krishna Moorthy Rao, Deputy Chairman of the Council of States. Many Indian members of parliament cheered and greeted the Soviet delegation, many of whom had previously visited the Soviet Union.

While welcoming the delegation members, Ayengar spoke of the pleasant memories of the Indian parliamentary delegation's previous visit to the Soviet Union. He expressed the hope that the Soviet delegation's visit to India would further strengthen the friendship between the two peoples. Lobanov emphasized with friendly sentiments the common desire of the Soviet and Indian people for world peace.

During their three-week stay in India, the Soviet delegation will visit Bombay, Madras, and Calcutta, and will tour some projects, including the Bhilai Steel Plant, which is being built with Soviet assistance.

### 进一步加强苏印两国人民的友谊  苏联最高苏维埃代表团访印

1958-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    进一步加强苏印两国人民的友谊
    苏联最高苏维埃代表团访印
    新华社新德里15日电　以苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团今天上午到达新德里。
    代表团在机场上受到印度人民院议长阿延加尔和联邦院副议长克里希纳·穆蒂·拉奥的热烈欢迎。许多印度国会议员向苏联代表团欢呼致意，其中很多人都曾经访问过苏联。
    阿延加尔在欢迎代表团团员时谈到了以前访问过苏联的印度国会代表团的愉快记忆，他表示希望，苏联代表团的访问印度将进一步加强两国人民之间的友谊。洛巴诺夫以友好的感情强调苏联和印度人民对世界和平的共同愿望。
    苏联代表团在印度的三星期逗留期间将到孟买、马德拉斯和加尔各答访问，并且将参观一些工程，其中包括正在苏联帮助下建设的比莱钢铁厂。
