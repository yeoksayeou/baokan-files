Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Cables Thanks to the Indian People
Author:
Date: 1958-02-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 15th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent a telegram to the people of India, thanking them for their hospitality and friendship. All India Radio broadcast the telegram this evening.

In the telegram, President Ho Chi Minh said that he saw the Indian people's deep sympathy for Vietnam's early reunification, and heard voices of friendship and peace everywhere in India. President Ho Chi Minh congratulated the Indian people on their nation-building activities and said: "We are very happy for your achievements, and we regard them as our own."

Regarding his talks with Nehru, President Ho Chi Minh said in the telegram: We have reached a consensus on some important international issues. We hope that the friendship between the people of Vietnam and India will last forever.

### 胡志明主席电谢印度人民

1958-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席电谢印度人民
    新华社新德里15日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明打电报给印度人民，感谢印度人民的款待和友谊。全印广播电台今天晚间广播了这封电报。
    胡志明主席在电报中说，他看到了印度人民深切同情越南能早日统一，并且在印度到处听到友谊和和平的声音。胡志明主席对印度人民建设国家的活动表示祝贺，并且说：“我们为你们的成就感到非常高兴，我们把它看作自己的成就”。
    关于他同尼赫鲁的会谈，胡志明主席在电报中说：我们在一些重要国际问题上取得了一致的看法。我们希望越南和印度人民之间的友谊万古长存。
