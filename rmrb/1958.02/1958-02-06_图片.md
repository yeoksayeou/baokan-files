    Female students of Longquan Middle School in Longquan County, Zhejiang Province, organized a fertilizer accumulation team, striving to accumulate 1,000 jin of fertilizer per person during the winter vacation. This is them delivering the fertilizer to the agricultural cooperative. Photo by Xu Yonghui (Xinhua News Agency draft)
