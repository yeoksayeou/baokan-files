Title: Visiting the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers: Chinese People's Delegation to Korea Sets Off

Author:

Date: 1958-02-12
Page: 2

Visiting the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers
Chinese People's Delegation to Korea Sets Off
Xinhua News Agency, 11th – The Chinese People's Delegation to Korea left Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 11th to visit the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers.

The delegation is headed by Wang Yifu as the General Head, with Rong Yiren, Wu Tao, Peng Siming, Tong Ying, and Zhang Lifu as Deputy General Heads. It consists of five sub-delegations. The members include over fifty representatives from various people's organizations, labor models from industry and agriculture, families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers, and demobilized soldiers.

The Beijing Pingju Opera Troupe, Beijing Youth Peking Opera Troupe, Tianjin Peking Opera Troupe, Shijiazhuang Silk String Opera Troupe, the Drama Troupe and Skills Team of the General Political Department Cultural Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Naval Political Department Cultural Troupe, and the Air Force Political Department Cultural Troupe, which are accompanying the delegation to Korea, will conduct extensive performance tours to visit both the Korean and Chinese people's armies.

Today, representatives from many organizations came to the station to see them off. Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yonghao, also went to the station to see them off.

### 慰问朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军  中国人民赴朝慰问团启程

1958-02-12
第2版()
专栏：

    慰问朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军
    中国人民赴朝慰问团启程
    新华社11日讯　中国人民赴朝慰问团11日下午乘火车离开北京，前往朝鲜慰问朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军官兵。
    慰问团由王一夫任总团长，荣毅仁、吴涛、彭思明、佟英、张里夫分任副总团长。下属五个分团。团员包括各人民团体、工农业劳动模范、烈军属和复员军人的代表五十多人。
    随同慰问团赴朝的北京市评剧团、北京市青年京剧团、天津市京剧团、石家庄市丝弦剧团、中国人民解放军总政治部文工团话剧团和技艺队、海军政治部文工团、空军政治部文工团等文艺团体，将分别为朝、中两国人民军队广泛地进行慰问演出。
    今天许多单位的代表都到车站送行。朝鲜驻华大使李永镐也到车站欢送。
