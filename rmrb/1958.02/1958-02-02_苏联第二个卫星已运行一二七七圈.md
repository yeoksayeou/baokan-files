Title: Soviet Second Satellite Has Completed 1277 Orbits

Author: 
Date: 1958-02-02
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 1st: According to TASS, as of 6:00 AM Moscow time on February 1st, the second artificial satellite had orbited the Earth one thousand two hundred and seventy-seven times.

### 苏联第二个卫星已运行一二七七圈

1958-02-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联第二个卫星已运行一二七七圈
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：到2月1日莫斯科时间六点正为止，第二个人造卫星已经绕地球转了一千二百七十七圈。
