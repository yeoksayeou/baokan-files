Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Congratulates American Scientists
Author:
Date: 1958-02-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 1st (TASS dispatch): The Soviet Academy of Sciences sent a telegram to American scientists congratulating them on the successful launch of an artificial Earth satellite.

The telegram, addressed to Dr. Detlev W. Bronk, President of the U.S. National Academy of Sciences, and Dr. Joseph Kaplan, Chairman of the U.S. National Committee for the International Geophysical Year, stated: “The Soviet Academy of Sciences congratulates American scientists and engineers on the successful launch of an artificial Earth satellite. We have always firmly believed that the scientific and technical difficulties encountered in experimental satellite launches would certainly be overcome by the American scientists participating in the satellite project according to the International Geophysical Year program.”

The telegram continued: “The Soviet Academy of Sciences expresses its belief that the Soviet and American satellites currently orbiting the Earth will enrich science with new discoveries and will strengthen the cooperation between our two countries' scientists for peaceful purposes.”

The telegram was signed by A.N. Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and I.P. Bardin, Chairman of the Coordination Committee for Scientific Research in Various Sectors of the International Geophysical Year.

### 苏联科学院电贺美科学家

1958-02-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联科学院电贺美科学家
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科1日电：苏联科学院打电报给美国科学家，祝贺发射人造地球卫星成功。
    在发给美国国立科学院院长布朗克和美国国际地球物理年全国委员会主席卡普兰的电报中说：苏联科学院祝贺美国科学家和工程师成功地发射了人造地球卫星。“我们曾经坚信，在试验发射人造卫星过程中所遇到的科学和技术上的困难，定将为根据国际地球物理年计划参加制造卫星的美国科学家所克服。”
    电报说：“苏联科学院表示相信，正在绕地球旋转的苏联卫星和美国卫星将会以新的发现把科学丰富起来，并将加强我们两国科学家为和平目的而进行的合作。”
    在电报上签字的是苏联科学院院长?斯米扬诺夫和国际地球物理年科学研究各部门统筹委员会主席巴尔金。
