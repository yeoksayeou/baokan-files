Title: Celebrating the Signing of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement: Mongolian Ambassador Holds Banquet in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-02-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd—The Mongolian People's Republic Ambassador to China, Lubusan, held a banquet in Beijing on February 22nd to celebrate the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

The entire delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic cultural delegation, headed by the Minister of Culture of the Mongolian People's Republic, Sosorbaram, attended the banquet.

Chinese guests invited to the banquet included Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Xia Yan and Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Ministers of Culture; as well as well-known figures from Beijing's cultural, educational, artistic, and scientific circles.

Ambassador Lubusan said in his speech that the cultural exchanges between Mongolia and China have achieved great success in recent years, and have played an important role in the prosperity of the cultural and artistic undertakings and socialist construction of the two countries. He said that the signing of the Cultural Cooperation Agreement will further promote cultural cooperation and traditional friendship between the two countries.

Head of Delegation Sosorbaram also spoke at the banquet.

Xi Zhongxun, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Hanfu, and Xia Yan all raised their glasses at the banquet to congratulate the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement and the growing development of cultural and artistic undertakings and friendly relations between the two countries.

The Mongolian cultural delegation has concluded its visit to China and will return home on the 23rd.

### 庆祝中蒙文化合作协定签订  蒙古大使在京举行宴会

1958-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中蒙文化合作协定签订
    蒙古大使在京举行宴会
    据新华社22日讯　蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使鲁布桑22日在北京举行宴会，庆祝中蒙两国文化合作协定的签订。
    以蒙古人民共和国文化部部长索苏尔巴拉木为首的蒙古人民共和国文化代表团全体人员出席了宴会。
    中国方面应邀出席宴会的有国务院秘书长习仲勋，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长夏衍、张致祥以及北京文教、艺术、科学界的著名人士。
    鲁布桑大使在讲话中说，几年以来蒙中两国的文化交流已经取得了很大的成绩，对两国文化艺术事业的繁荣和社会主义建设起了重要的作用。他说，这次签订文化合作协定，将进一步促进两国的文化合作和传统的友谊。
    索苏尔巴拉木团长也在宴会上讲了话。
    习仲勋，张奚若，章汉夫，夏衍在宴会上都举杯祝贺中蒙两国文化合作协定的签字以及两国文化艺术事业和友好关系的日益发展。
    蒙古文化代表团已经结束了在中国的访问，将在23日回国。
