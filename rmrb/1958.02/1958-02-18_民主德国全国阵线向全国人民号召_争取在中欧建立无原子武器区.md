Title: National Front of Democratic Germany Calls on the Entire Nation to Strive for the Establishment of a Nuclear-Weapon-Free Zone in Central Europe
Author:
Date: 1958-02-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 16th, TASS dispatch: The National Committee of the National Front of Democratic Germany unanimously adopted a declaration, calling on all cities and towns in the Republic to turn 1958 into a great year of struggle for peace and socialism by the people.

The National Committee suggested that local committees of the National Front actively carry out explanatory work among the residents to further strengthen the worker-peasant regime, and suggested that enterprises in the Republic launch socialist competitions to welcome the upcoming Fifth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany.

The National Committee put forward a task to all German patriots: to actively launch a large-scale struggle to strive for the establishment of a nuclear-weapon-free zone in Central Europe.

### 民主德国全国阵线向全国人民号召  争取在中欧建立无原子武器区

1958-02-18
第6版()
专栏：

    民主德国全国阵线向全国人民号召
    争取在中欧建立无原子武器区
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社柏林16日讯：民主德国全国阵线全国委员会一致通过一项声明，号召共和国各城市和各村镇把1958年变为人民争取和平和社会主义的伟大斗争年。
    全国委员会建议全国阵线各地方委员会在居民中间积极进行解释工作来进一步加强工农政权，并且建议共和国各企业展开社会主义竞赛来迎接即将举行的德国统一社会党第五次代表大会。
    全国委员会对德国全体爱国者提出一个任务：为争取在中欧建立无原子武器区而积极展开大规模斗争。
