Title: Thousands of Cultural and Scientific Figures in West Germany Issue Joint Statement Opposing Bonn Authorities' Atomic Armament Policy
Author:
Date: 1958-02-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, February 2nd: Thousands of cultural and scientific figures in West Germany recently jointly issued a statement condemning the West German government and Christian Democratic Union leaders for pursuing a "extremely harmful" policy of establishing atomic armament. Among those who signed the statement are well-known West German scientists, professors and teachers from universities and other schools, doctors, legal professionals, writers, theater workers, painters, sculptors, musicians, journalists, engineers, architects, students, and religious figures.

The statement calls on the West German Federal Parliament to demand that the Bonn government not equip the West German Bundeswehr with atomic weapons, not manufacture atomic weapons, not store atomic weapons on West German territory, and demands that the Adenauer government revoke the equipment of this kind already installed in West Germany.

### 西德几千名文化科学界人士联名声明  反对波恩当局原子武装政策

1958-02-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　西德几千名文化科学界人士联名声明
    反对波恩当局原子武装政策
    据新华社2日讯　波恩消息：西德几千名文化科学界人士最近联名发表了声明，谴责西德政府和基督教民主联盟领袖们奉行建立原子武装这种“极为有害”的政策。在这个声明上签名的有西德著名科学家、高等学校和其他学校的教授和教师、医生、法律工作者、作家、戏剧工作者、画家、雕刻家、音乐家、新闻工作者、工程师、建筑师、学生以及宗教界人士等。
    声明呼吁西德联邦议院要求波恩政府不用原子武器装备西德国防军，不制造原子武器，不在西德领土上存放原子武器，并且要求阿登纳政府撤消已经在西德设置的这种设备。
