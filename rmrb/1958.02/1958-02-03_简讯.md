　　Brief News
　　Hungary and Mongolia Sign Three-Year Trade and Payment Agreement
　　Budapest Dispatch: A trade and payment agreement between Hungary and Mongolia for 1958-1960, as well as a trade plan for 1958, were signed here on January 31.
　　According to this year's plan, the trade volume between the two countries will greatly exceed that of previous years.
　　Albania and Greece Cooperate in Clearing Mines
　　Tirana Dispatch: The Albanian and Greek governments recently reached an agreement on jointly clearing the Corfu Channel.
　　The Corfu Channel is a narrow waterway located between Saranda in southern Albania and the Greek island of Corfu. Mines were laid here during the Second World War.
　　Hungarian Refugees in Brazil Demand to Return Home
　　Montevideo Dispatch: According to Brazilian newspapers, Hungarian refugees who fled to Brazil in October 1956 have launched a movement to return home. About two hundred Hungarian refugees held a meeting in São Paulo. The meeting unanimously demanded that they be given the opportunity to return to their homeland immediately.

专栏：简讯

　　简讯
　　匈蒙签订三年换货和支付协定
    布达佩斯讯：1月31日在这里签订了匈牙利和蒙古1958—1960年换货和支付协定以及1958年的换货计划。
    按照今年的计划，两国贸易额将大大超过已往各年。
    阿希合作清扫水雷
    地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚和希腊政府最近就共同清扫科孚海峡达成协议。
    科孚海峡是位于阿尔巴尼亚南部萨兰达城和希腊科孚岛之间的一条狭长的水道。在第二次世界大战期间，这里曾经敷设过水雷。
    在巴西的匈难民要求回国
    蒙得维的亚讯：据巴西报纸报道，1956年10月逃亡到巴西去的匈牙利难民掀起了一个回国运动。约有二百个匈牙利难民在圣保罗举行了一个大会。会上一致要求让他们有机会立刻返回祖国。
