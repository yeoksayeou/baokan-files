Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1958-02-01
Page: 1

　　Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    According to the decision of the 93rd session of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, the position of Zhang Naiqi as Minister of the Ministry of Food of the People's Republic of China is removed, the position of Zhang Bojun as Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the People's Republic of China is removed, and the position of Luo Longji as Minister of the Ministry of Forestry Industry of the People's Republic of China is removed.
            Chairman of the People's Republic of China: Mao Zedong
　　　　　　　　　January 31, 1958

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1958-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华人民共和国主席令
    根据中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九十三次会议的决定，撤销章乃器中华人民共和国粮食部部长职务，撤销章伯钧中华人民共和国交通部部长职务，撤销罗隆基中华人民共和国森林工业部部长职务。
            中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
　　　　　　　　　一九五八年一月三十一日
