Title: U.S. Selects Rocket Base in Turkey
Author:
Date: 1958-02-13
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, February 11th: According to today's "Al-Shaab" newspaper, after the Turkish government agreed to establish rocket and missile bases, American experts, accompanied by senior Turkish military officers, recently arrived in Alexandretta, southern Turkey. They will select sites for rocket and missile bases there.

### 美在土耳其选择火箭基地

1958-02-13
第6版()
专栏：

    美在土耳其选择火箭基地
    新华社大马士革11日电　据“群众报”今天报道，在土耳其政府同意建立火箭和导弹基地之后，美国专家们最近已经在土耳其高级军官陪同下，到达土耳其南部的亚历山大勒达。他们将在那里选择火箭和导弹基地的地点。
