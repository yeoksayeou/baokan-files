Title: President Sukarno Returns to Jakarta Amidst Enthusiastic Welcome from Indonesian People; Reaffirms Confidence in Overcoming Current Difficulties

Author:

Date: 1958-02-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 16th: In a welcome rally held by the Indonesian people in front of the Independence Palace on the 16th, Indonesian President Sukarno delivered a brief speech to tens of thousands of enthusiastic citizens, saying: "The Republic of Indonesia is a complete and indivisible nation, and the people are united. Therefore, we are confident in overcoming all current difficulties."

Sukarno, who had just returned from a foreign visit, looked healthy and energetic. He said that all the countries and peoples he had visited expressed their hope that Indonesia would become a strong nation, and they all sympathized with Indonesia.

Acting President Sartono and Deputy Speaker of the Parliament Arifin expressed in their welcoming remarks that they were convinced that under the leadership of President Sukarno, all difficulties could be overcome.

Prime Minister Djuanda said in his welcoming speech that President Sukarno's foreign visit made a great contribution to Indonesia's national struggle, especially to the struggle for the liberation of West Irian.

Indonesian government leaders, prominent figures from all walks of life, and foreign diplomatic envoys to Indonesia all went to the airport to welcome President Sukarno. Along the road from the airport to the Independence Palace, crowds of people stood to welcome him. Radio Republik Indonesia said that this was the most enthusiastic welcome President Sukarno had received on his return from numerous trips abroad.

### 在印度尼西亚人民热烈欢迎下  苏加诺总统回到雅加达  重申有信心克服目前一切困难

1958-02-18
第5版()
专栏：

    在印度尼西亚人民热烈欢迎下
    苏加诺总统回到雅加达
    重申有信心克服目前一切困难
    新华社雅加达16日电　在印度尼西亚人民16日在独立宫前举行的欢迎会上，印度尼西亚总统苏加诺向数以万计的热烈的市民发表简短的演讲说：“印度尼西亚共和国是一个完整的不可分割的国家，人民是团结的，因此，我们有信心克服目前的一切困难。”
    刚从国外访问归来的苏加诺看上去很健康和精神饱满，他说，他所访问过的一切国家和人民，都没有不表示希望印度尼西亚成为一个强国的，他们都同情印度尼西亚。
    代理总统沙多诺和国会副议长阿里芬在他们的欢迎词中表示确信，在苏加诺总统的领导之下，一切困难都能够克服。
    朱安达总理在他的欢迎词中说，苏加诺总统的国外访问对印度尼西亚的民族斗争，特别是对解放西伊里安的斗争，有很大的贡献。
    印度尼西亚政府领导人和各界著名人士以及各国驻印度尼西亚的外交使节都到机场欢迎苏加诺总统。在从机场到独立宫的路上，沿途站着欢迎的人群。印度尼西亚共和国电台说，这是苏加诺总统多次从国外旅行回国所受到的欢迎中最热情的一次。
