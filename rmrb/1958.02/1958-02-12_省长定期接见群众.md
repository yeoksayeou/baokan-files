Title: Provincial Governor Regularly Receives the Masses

Author: 
Date: 1958-02-12
Page: 4

In the Local Newspapers

    Provincial Governor Regularly Receives the Masses
    On the 6th, the front page of the Guangxi Daily published a short but important piece of news: In order to further strengthen the ties between the government and the people, the Governor of Guangxi Province decided to establish a system of regular reception of visiting masses by the Provincial Governor (Deputy Governor) starting from February. The reception time is on the mornings of the 10th and 25th of each month.

### 省长定期接见群众

1958-02-12
第4版()
专栏：在各地报纸上

    省长定期接见群众
    6日，广西日报一版登了一条短小的但却是重要的消息，内容是：广西省省长为了进一步加强政府与人民群众的联系，决定从2月份起建立省长（副省长）定期接见来访群众的制度。接见的时间是每月10日和25日的上午。
