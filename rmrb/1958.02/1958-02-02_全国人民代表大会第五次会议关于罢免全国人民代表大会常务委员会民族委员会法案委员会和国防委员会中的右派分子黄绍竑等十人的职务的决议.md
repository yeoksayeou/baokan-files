Title: Resolution of the Fifth Session of the National People's Congress on the Removal of Rightists Huang Shaohong and Ten Others from Their Positions on the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the Ethnic Affairs Committee, the Legislative Affairs Committee, and the National Defense Committee
Author:
Date: 1958-02-02
Page: 1

    The Fifth Session of the First National People's Congress on February 1, 1958, approved the proposal submitted by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and decided:

    1.  To remove the rightists Huang Shaohong, Long Yun, and Chen Mingshu from their positions as members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress;

    2.  To remove the rightists Fei Xiaotong, Huang Xianfan, and Ou Baichuan from their positions as members of the Ethnic Affairs Committee of the National People's Congress;

    3.  To remove the rightists Zhang Yunchuan, Chen Mingshu, Huang Shaohong, Huang Qixiang, Xie Xuehong, and Luo Longji from their positions as members of the Legislative Affairs Committee of the National People's Congress;

    4.  To remove the rightist Long Yun from his position as Vice-Chairman of the National Defense Committee, and to remove the rightist Huang Qixiang from his position as a member of the National Defense Committee.

### 全国人民代表大会第五次会议关于罢免全国人民代表大会常务委员会民族委员会法案委员会和国防委员会中的右派分子黄绍竑等十人的职务的决议

1958-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会第五次会议关于罢免全国人民代表大会常务委员会民族委员会法案委员会和国防委员会中的右派分子黄绍竑等十人的职务的决议
    一九五八年二月一日第一届全国人民代表大会第五次会议同意全国人民代表大会常务委员会提出的建议，决定：
    一、罢免右派分子黄绍竑、龙云、陈铭枢全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员的职务；
    二、罢免右派分子费孝通、黄现璠、欧百川全国人民代表大会民族委员会委员的职务；
    三、罢免右派分子张云川、陈铭枢、黄绍竑、黄琪翔、谢雪红、罗隆基全国人民代表大会法案委员会委员的职务；
    四、罢免右派分子龙云国防委员会副主席的职务，罢免右派分子黄琪翔国防委员会委员的职务。
