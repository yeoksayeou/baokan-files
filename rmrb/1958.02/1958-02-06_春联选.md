Title: Spring Couplets Selection
Author: Wan Changnan
Date: 1958-02-06
Page: 8

　　Spring Couplets Selection
　　Celebrating a bumper harvest, grain like golden mountains, cotton like the sea
　　Rejoicing in construction, cars like flowing water, horses like dragons
　　Overturning the sea and moving mountains, everyone shows their skills
　　Riding the wind and breaking the waves, everyone displays their heroism
            (Wan Changnan)

　　春联选
