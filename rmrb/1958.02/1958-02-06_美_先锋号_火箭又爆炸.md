Title: US "Vanguard" Rocket Explodes Again
Author:
Date: 1958-02-06
Page: 6

　　US "Vanguard" Rocket Explodes Again
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, February 5th: The US Navy suffered another failure this morning when launching the "Vanguard" rocket at Cape Canaveral, Florida.
    The rocket only ascended a few thousand feet before exploding, resulting in a large ball of fire and a smaller one in the air. Some debris landed near Cape Canaveral.
    The US "Vanguard" rocket exploded once during a test launch on December 6th of last year; it also failed during testing in late January of this year.

### 美“先锋号”火箭又爆炸

1958-02-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　美“先锋号”火箭又爆炸
    新华社5日讯　华盛顿消息：美国海军今天清晨在佛罗里达州卡纳维拉尔角发射“先锋号”火箭时又一次遭到失败。
    火箭只上升到几千英尺就发生爆炸，空中出现了一大团火焰和一小团火焰。有些碎片就落在卡纳维拉尔角附近。
    美国“先锋号”火箭在去年12月6日试验发射时曾经爆炸一次；在今年1月下旬的试验中又发射失败。
