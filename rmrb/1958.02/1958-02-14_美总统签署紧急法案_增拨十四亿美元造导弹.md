Title: U.S. President Signs Emergency Bill, Allocates Additional $1.4 Billion for Missile Production
Author:
Date: 1958-02-14
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, February 13th – U.S. President Eisenhower signed an emergency bill on February 12th, increasing funding by $1.4 billion to accelerate the production of missile weapons.

    Members of the U.S. Congress are actively discussing the issue of increasing the military budget. According to a survey conducted by the United Press, Congress appears certain to increase military spending for the 1959 fiscal year to a level significantly exceeding Eisenhower's original request of $39.8 billion.

### 美总统签署紧急法案  增拨十四亿美元造导弹

1958-02-14
第7版()
专栏：

    美总统签署紧急法案
    增拨十四亿美元造导弹
    据新华社13日讯　美国总统艾森豪威尔12日签署了一个增加十四亿美元来加紧制造导弹武器的紧急法案。
    美国国会人士都在纷纷谈论增加军事预算的问题。据合众社在进行了调查后说，国会看来一定会把1959年财政年度的军事开支增加到大大超过艾森豪威尔原先所要求三百九十八亿美元的水平。
