Title: Soviet Ambassador to China, Academician Yudin, Holds Reception to Welcome Home Four Scientific Delegations Returning from Visits to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-02-11
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Soviet Ambassador to China, Academician Yudin, held a reception on the evening of the 10th to entertain the Chinese Science and Technology Delegation and the science delegations of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Ministry of Higher Education, and the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences who recently returned to Beijing from the Soviet Union. Also invited to the reception were responsible persons from relevant departments of our country, well-known scientists in Beijing, and Soviet scientists and experts who are assisting our country in its work.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and Vice Premier and Director of the Science Planning Commission Nie Rongzhen were also invited to attend the reception.
    Ambassador Yudin was unable to attend the reception due to illness. The reception was hosted by Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China. At the reception, Sudarikov said: The signing of the agreement between the governments of the Soviet Union and China on jointly conducting major scientific and technological research and the Soviet Union's assistance to China in conducting such research, as well as the scientific cooperation agreements between the other three delegations and relevant Soviet departments, once again demonstrated the unbreakable friendship and the development of scientific cooperation between the two countries; fulfilling the tasks in the agreement and cooperative agreements is the glorious internationalist task of the Soviet scientific and technological circles. Sudarikov, on behalf of the Soviet scientific and technological circles, gave assurance that the Soviet scientific and technological circles will spare no effort to fulfill the tasks put forward in the agreement and cooperative agreements.
    Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union, then spoke. He read a letter of thanks from Premier Zhou Enlai to Chairman Bulganin of the Soviet Council of Ministers. The letter said: The Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union, with the help of the CPSU Central Committee, the Soviet government, and Soviet scientists and experts, successfully completed its mission; these Soviet aids will play a significant role in the development of Chinese science and technology. Premier Zhou, on behalf of the Chinese government, expressed his deep gratitude to the Soviet government in the letter; and asked Chairman Bulganin to convey the Chinese government's gratitude to the Soviet scientists and experts who participated in these works. Guo Moruo said that Premier Zhou’s letter represented the opinion of the Chinese scientific and technological circles and also represented the opinion of the 600 million Chinese people.
    After the reception, a science film was shown.

### 苏联驻我国大使尤金院士举行酒会  欢宴访苏回国的四个科学代表团

1958-02-11
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联驻我国大使尤金院士举行酒会
    欢宴访苏回国的四个科学代表团
    新华社10日讯　苏联驻我国大使尤金院士10日晚举行酒会，招待最近从苏联回北京的中国访苏科学技术代表团和中国科学院、高等教育部、中国农业科学院的访苏科学代表团。应邀出席酒会的还有我国有关部门负责人、在北京的著名科学家以及帮助我国工作的苏联科学家、专家等。
    国务院副总理陈毅、副总理兼科学规划委员会主任聂荣臻也应邀出席了酒会。
    尤金大使因病未能出席酒会，酒会由苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫主持。苏达利柯夫在酒会上说：苏中两国政府关于共同进行以及苏联帮助中国进行重大科学技术研究议定书和其他三个代表团同苏联有关部门的科学合作协议的签订，又一次表示了两国牢不可破的友谊和科学合作的发展；完成议定书和合作协议中的任务，是苏联科学技术界光荣的国际主义任务。苏达利柯夫代表苏联科学技术界提出保证：苏联科学技术界一定不遗余力地实现议定书和合作协议提出的任务。
    中国访苏科学技术代表团团长郭沫若接着讲话。他宣读了周恩来总理写给苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁的感谢信。信中说：中国访苏科学技术代表团在苏共中央、苏联政府和苏联科学家、专家们的帮助下，胜利地完成了任务；苏联的这些援助对于中国科学技术的发展将起重大作用。周总理代表中国政府在信中向苏联政府表示深切谢意；并请布尔加宁主席向参加这些工作的苏联科学家和专家们转达中国政府的谢意。郭沫若说，周总理的这封信，代表了中国科学技术界的意见，也代表了中国六亿人民的意见。
    酒会后放映了科学电影。
