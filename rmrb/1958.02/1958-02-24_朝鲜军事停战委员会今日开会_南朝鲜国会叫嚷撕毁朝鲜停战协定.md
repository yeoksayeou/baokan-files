Title: Korean Military Armistice Commission to Meet Today; South Korean National Assembly Calls for Tearing Up Korean Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-02-24
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 23rd: According to the Korean-Chinese side's request, the Military Armistice Commission will hold its 80th meeting in Panmunjom on February 24th. The meeting will discuss issues related to the Korean Armistice Agreement.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 23rd: Seoul dispatch: Just as world public opinion is warmly welcoming the statement issued by the Chinese and Korean governments on the peaceful resolution of the Korean question, the South Korean National Assembly passed a resolution on the 22nd, appealing to the sixteen countries that participated in the war of aggression against Korea to abolish the Korean Armistice Agreement, and to implement "free elections" under so-called "United Nations supervision" according to the illegal resolution of the United Nations on November 14, 1947, in order to "unify" the entire Korea.
    The Associated Press admitted that "this resolution is actually calling on the countries participating in the war under the United Nations flag to resume the war."
    The Syngman Rhee clique and the United States are singing in harmony in obstructing the peaceful resolution of the Korean issue. The South Korean National Assembly's resolution on the 22nd was also addressed to the so-called "United Nations Command."
    While passing this resolution, the Syngman Rhee clique also organized some so-called "demonstrations" calling for a "northward advance" in order to create a war atmosphere.

### 朝鲜军事停战委员会今日开会  南朝鲜国会叫嚷撕毁朝鲜停战协定

1958-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军事停战委员会今日开会
    南朝鲜国会叫嚷撕毁朝鲜停战协定
    新华社平壤23日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯：根据朝中方面的要求，军事停战委员会将在2月24日在板门店举行第八十次会议。会议将讨论有关朝鲜停战协定的问题。
    新华社平壤23日电　汉城消息：正当全世界各国舆论热烈欢迎中朝两国政府发表的关于和平解决朝鲜问题的声明的时候，南朝鲜国会22日通过了一项决议，向参加侵朝战争的十六个国家呼吁要废除朝鲜停战协定，并且要根据联合国1947年11月14日的非法决议在所谓“联合国监督下”实行“自由选举”，来“统一”全朝鲜。
    美联社承认，“这个决议实际上是呼吁在联合国旗帜下参加战争的国家恢复战争。”
    李承晚集团和美国在阻挠和平解决朝鲜问题方面是相互唱和的。南朝鲜国会22日的决议同时也是向所谓“联合国军”司令部提出的。
    在通过这项决议的同时，李承晚集团还组织了一些所谓“示威游行”，叫嚷要“北进”，以制造战争空气。
