Title: Comrade Huang Jing Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1958-02-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Comrade Huang Jing, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Director of the State Technology Commission, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Vice-Chairman of the Science Planning Commission of the State Council, and member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, unfortunately passed away in Guangzhou on the 10th of this month due to pulmonary embolism. According to his will, Comrade Huang Jing's body was cremated in Guangzhou on the 11th. His ashes were flown to Beijing from Guangzhou by special plane on the afternoon of the 12th.

Those who went to the airport to receive Comrade Huang Jing's ashes this afternoon included Vice Premiers Nie Rongzhen and Bo Yibo, and many responsible staff from various Party, government, and military departments. After silently mourning Comrade Huang Jing's ashes at the airport, they took the ashes directly to the mourning hall set up in the Zhongshan Hall of Zhongshan Park and kept vigil in the mourning hall.

Many friends of Comrade Huang Jing also went to Zhongshan Hall today to keep vigil.

### 黄敬同志逝世

1958-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    黄敬同志逝世
    新华社12日讯　中国共产党中央委员会委员、国家技术委员会主任、第一机械工业部部长、国务院科学规划委员会副主任、政协全国委员会委员黄敬同志因患肺动脉栓塞症不治，本月10日不幸在广州逝世。根据遗嘱，黄敬同志的尸体于11日在广州火化。他的骨灰在12日下午由广州专机运抵北京。
    今天下午到机场迎接黄敬同志的骨灰的，有聂荣臻、薄一波副总理和党政军各方面的负责工作人员多人。他们在机场向黄敬同志的骨灰静默致哀后，一直把骨灰送到设在中山公园中山堂内的灵堂，并曾在灵堂内守灵。
    今天前往中山堂守灵的还有黄敬同志的许多生前友好。
