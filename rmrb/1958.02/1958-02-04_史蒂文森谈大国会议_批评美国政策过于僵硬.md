Title: Stevenson Discusses Great Power Conference, Criticizes US Policy as Too Rigid
Author:
Date: 1958-02-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 1st (TASS dispatch): The "American People's Democratic Action Association" held a banquet on January 31 to commemorate Roosevelt. Stevenson, who had been nominated twice as the Democratic presidential candidate, spoke at the meeting. When talking about the issue of great power talks, he said: "We should break the deadlock. If attempts to conclude agreements often carry risks, then not making such attempts carries even greater risks. I think our policy is too rigid..."

### 史蒂文森谈大国会议  批评美国政策过于僵硬

1958-02-04
第5版()
专栏：

　　史蒂文森谈大国会议
    批评美国政策过于僵硬
    据新华社3日讯　据塔斯社纽约1日讯：“美国人民主行动协会”1月31日为纪念罗斯福举行了一次宴会，曾经两次被提名为民主党总统候选人的史蒂文森在会上讲了话。他在谈到大国会谈问题时说：“我们应该打开僵局。如果说缔结协定的尝试常常带有危险性，那末不作这种尝试就有更大的危险性。我想，我们的政策过分僵硬了……”
