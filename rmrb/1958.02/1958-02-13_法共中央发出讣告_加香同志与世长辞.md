Title: Obituary Issued by the French Communist Party Central Committee: Comrade Cachin Passes Away

Author: 
Date: 1958-02-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, February 12th, TASS, Paris, February 12th: The Central Committee of the French Communist Party published the following obituary in the newspapers: “The Central Committee of the French Communist Party sadly announces to all Communist Party members, the French working class and people, and the international workers' movement: Comrade Marcel Cachin, member of the Political Bureau, director of ‘L'Humanité,’ and member of the National Assembly for Paris, has passed away. Our party's oldest and most respected activist passed away on the morning of February 12th, at the age of eighty-nine.”

Marcel Cachin's funeral will be held on February 15th at 2:30 PM local time. (Attached picture)

### 法共中央发出讣告  加香同志与世长辞

1958-02-13
第6版()
专栏：

    法共中央发出讣告
    加香同志与世长辞
    新华社12日讯　塔斯社巴黎12日讯：法国共产党中央委员会对报纸发表讣告如下：“法国共产党中央委员会沉痛地哀告全体共产党员、法国的工人阶级和人民以及国际工人运动：政治局委员、‘人道报’社社长、巴黎的国民议会议员马赛尔·加香同志已经逝世。我们党最老和受人尊敬的活动家已在2月12日早晨与世长辞，享年八十九岁。”
    马赛尔·加香的葬礼将于当地时间2月15日十四点三十分举行。（附图片）
