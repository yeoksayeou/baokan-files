Title: Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1958-02-17
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
    Xinhua News Agency, 15th dispatch: In the news item "Yugov Receives Our Ambassador to Bulgaria," "First Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, Yugov" should read "First Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, Zhivkov."
