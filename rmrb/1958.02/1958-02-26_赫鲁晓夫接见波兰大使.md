Title: Khrushchev Receives Polish Ambassador
Author: 
Date: 1958-02-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 24th (TASS): The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the Polish Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Tadeusz Gede, on the 22nd and held talks with him.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见波兰大使

1958-02-26
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见波兰大使
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫22日接见了波兰驻苏联大使塔·盖德，并且同他进行了谈话。
