Author: Zhao Peng
Date: 1958-02-01

　　Marine Farmland
    On the sea near Qingdao, workers from the Shandong Aquatic Products Aquaculture Farm are laying down rows of bamboo rafts to carry out artificial kelp cultivation. After 1951, the farm used advanced experiences such as "wide-span dense planting," and the cultivation area and output increased rapidly. This year, the planned output will reach more than 7,900 tons. Photo by Zhao Peng (Qingdao Daily Manuscript)

### 海上良田（图片）
赵朋
1958-02-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　海上良田
    在青岛市近海的海面上，山东水产养殖场的工人们放下了一排排的竹筏，实行海带的人工养殖。1951年后，该场运用了“宽幅密植”等先进经验，养殖面积和产量迅速上升，今年计划产量将达到七千九百多吨。　　　　  赵朋摄（青岛日报稿）
