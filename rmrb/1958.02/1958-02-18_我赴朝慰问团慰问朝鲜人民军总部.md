Title: Our Delegation to North Korea Visits the Headquarters of the Korean People's Army
Author:
Date: 1958-02-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 16th: The general delegation of the Chinese People's Goodwill Mission to Korea today visited the headquarters of the Korean People's Army (KPA) in Pyongyang. At a meeting attended by more than 500 KPA generals and officers, Wang Yifu, head of the general delegation, on behalf of the Chinese people, extended warm congratulations to all officers and soldiers of the KPA who have made outstanding contributions to defending their motherland and the cause of peace. He also presented a flag and gifts to the KPA. General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defense of Korea, received the flag and gifts.

In his congratulatory speech, Wang Yifu said that under the leadership of the Korean Workers' Party and the Korean government headed by Comrade Kim Il-sung, the KPA has undergone severe trials and has been tempered into an invincible force. He pointed out that the great achievements of the KPA in the Korean War will always be praised and learned from.

Wang Yifu emphasized that in the struggle against common enemies, the Chinese and Korean people, as well as the KPA and the Chinese People's Volunteers, fought side by side, closely cooperating and defeating the enemy. The people of China and Korea have forged an unbreakable friendship linked by flesh and blood. The Chinese people express their deep gratitude and high respect to the Korean people for the great assistance they have given to the Chinese People's Volunteers both during and after the war.

Lieutenant General Kim Tae-geun, Director of the Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, spoke at the meeting, warmly welcoming the delegation and expressing gratitude to the Chinese people.

At the meeting, Lieutenant General Kim Bong-ryul, Vice Minister of National Defense of Korea, presented a silk banner to the delegation on behalf of the Korean People's Army, which was accepted by Wang Yifu, head of the delegation.

After the meeting, the Song and Dance Troupe of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy gave a performance. After the performance, General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defense of Korea, went on stage to present flower baskets to the performers and shook hands with them one by one.

### 我赴朝慰问团慰问朝鲜人民军总部

1958-02-18
第6版()
专栏：

    我赴朝慰问团慰问朝鲜人民军总部
    新华社平壤16日电　中国人民赴朝慰问团总团今天在平壤慰问了朝鲜人民军总部。王一夫总团长在有五百多名人民军将领和军官参加的大会上，代表中国人民向为保卫自己的祖国和和平事业建立了卓越功勋的朝鲜人民军全体官兵致以热烈祝贺。他并向人民军献旗、献礼，朝鲜民族保卫相金光侠大将受旗、受礼。
    王一夫在会上致慰问词说，朝鲜人民军在以金日成同志为首的朝鲜劳动党和朝鲜政府领导下，经过严重的斗争考验，已锻炼成为一支不可战胜的力量。他指出朝鲜人民军在朝鲜战争中创造的丰功伟绩将永远为人们所传颂和学习。
    王一夫强调说，在反对共同敌人的斗争中，中朝人民及朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军并肩作战，相互密切配合，战胜了敌人。中朝两国人民结成了血肉相连的牢不可破的友谊。中国人民对于无论在战争中和战后都给予中国人民志愿军以极大帮助的朝鲜人民表示深切感谢和崇高的敬意。
    朝鲜人民军政治局局长金泰根中将在会上讲了话，他对代表团表示热烈欢迎，并向中国人民表示感谢。
    会上，朝鲜民族保卫省副相金奉律中将代表朝鲜人民军向代表团献了一面锦旗，王一夫总团长受旗。
    会后，中国人民解放军海军政治部文工团举行慰问演出。朝鲜民族保卫相金光侠大将在演出结束后登台向演员们献花篮并同演员们一一握手。
