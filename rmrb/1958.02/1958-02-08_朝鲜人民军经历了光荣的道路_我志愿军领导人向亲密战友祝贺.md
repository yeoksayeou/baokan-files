Title: The Korean People's Army Has Traversed a Glorious Path; Our Chinese People's Volunteer Army Leaders Congratulate Close Comrades-in-Arms
Author:
Date: 1958-02-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 7th: General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and General Wang Ping, Political Commissar, today sent a telegram to Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, congratulating the Korean People's Army on its 10th anniversary. The telegram reads as follows:

To Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea:

On the glorious occasion of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army, we, on behalf of all commanders and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, extend our highest respect to you and our warm congratulations to the fraternal Korean People's Army.

The heroic Korean People's Army has traversed a glorious and victorious path over the past ten years. We firmly believe that under the leadership of the Korean Workers' Party and yourself, the Korean People's Army will make even greater contributions to safeguarding the Korean Armistice Agreement and striving for the peaceful reunification of its fatherland in this arduous struggle.

　　　　　　　　Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, General Yang Yong
　　　　　　　　Political Commissar, General Wang Ping
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　February 7, 1958

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 7th: General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and General Wang Ping, Political Commissar, today sent a telegram to General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, congratulating the Korean People's Army on its 10th anniversary. The telegram stated that the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, as the closest comrades-in-arms of the Korean People's Army, sincerely wish you greater victories in the great cause of striving for the peaceful reunification of your fatherland and safeguarding world peace.

Major General Wang Yunrui, Chief of Staff of the Volunteer Army, and Lieutenant General Liang Biye, Deputy Political Commissar and Director of the Political Department, also sent telegrams today to General Li Kwon-mu, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, and Lieutenant General Kim Tae-keun, Director of the Political Bureau, respectively, extending their warm congratulations.

### 朝鲜人民军经历了光荣的道路  我志愿军领导人向亲密战友祝贺

1958-02-08
第6版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军经历了光荣的道路
    我志愿军领导人向亲密战友祝贺
    新华社平壤7日电　中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员王平上将今天致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅，祝贺朝鲜人民军建军十周年。电文如下：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅：
    在朝鲜人民军建军十周年的光荣日子里，我们谨代表中国人民志愿军全体指战员向您致以崇高的敬意，向兄弟般的朝鲜人民军致以热烈的祝贺。
    英雄的朝鲜人民军十年来，经历了光荣的胜利的道路。我们深信：在朝鲜劳动党和您的领导下，朝鲜人民军在维护朝鲜停战协定及争取和平统一自己祖国的艰巨斗争中，必将做出更大的贡献。
              中国人民志愿军司令员上将　杨勇
                          政治委员上将　王平
                                      1958年2月7日
    新华社平壤7日电　中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将和政治委员王平上将今天致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫相金光侠大将，祝贺朝鲜人民军建军十周年。电报说，作为朝鲜人民军最亲密的战友的中国人民志愿军，衷心地预祝你们在争取自己祖国的和平统一和维护世界和平的伟大事业中取得更大的胜利。
    志愿军参谋长王蕴瑞少将和副政治委员兼政治部主任梁必业中将也在今天分别打电报给朝鲜人民军总参谋长李权武上将和政治局局长金泰根中将，致热烈的祝贺。
