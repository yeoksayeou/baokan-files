Title: "People's Daily" Editorial Board Sends Condolences to "l'Humanité" Editorial Board on the Passing of Comrade Marcel Cachin
Author:
Date: 1958-02-16
Page: 5

    "People's Daily" Editorial Board Sends Condolences to "l'Humanité" Editorial Board on the Passing of Comrade Marcel Cachin
    To the Editorial Board of "l'Humanité," Dear Comrades:
    We were deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Marcel Cachin, member of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party and Director of "l'Humanité." On behalf of all the staff of the Chinese "People's Daily," we extend our deepest condolences to you.
    Comrade Marcel Cachin was a loyal son of the French working people. He dedicated his life to the French working class and the international communist movement. He also made outstanding contributions to the proletarian news undertaking. We firmly believe that the glorious achievements of Comrade Marcel Cachin's life will forever inspire the French working people and the working people of the world to fight for peace, democracy, and social progress.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 "People's Daily" Editorial Board
                             February 14, 1958, in Beijing

### “人民日报”编委会致电“人道报”编委会  悼唁马赛尔·加香同志逝世

1958-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    “人民日报”编委会致电“人道报”编委会
    悼唁马赛尔·加香同志逝世“人道报”编辑委员会亲爱的同志们：
    惊悉法国共产党政治局委员、“人道报”社长马赛尔·加香同志逝世，我们怀着十分悲痛的心情代表中国“人民日报”全体工作人员向你们致以最深切的哀悼。
    马赛尔·加香同志是法国劳动人民忠实的儿子。他把自己的一生都献给了法国工人阶级和国际共产主义运动。对于无产阶级的新闻事业，他也作出了卓越的贡献。我们深信，马赛尔·加香同志一生的光辉业绩，将永远鼓舞着法国劳动人民和全世界劳动人民，为争取和平、民主和社会进步而斗争。　“人民日报”编辑委员会
                             1958年2月14日于北京
