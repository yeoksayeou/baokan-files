Title: Song of Stopping War with Arms – Commemorating the Fortieth Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1958-02-23
Page: 8

    Song of Stopping War with Arms
    — Commemorating the Fortieth Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army
    Guo Moruo
    In our China, as far back as the Yin and Shang dynasties three thousand years ago,
    we advocated the ideal of "stopping war with arms."
    Our military force is for eliminating war,
    not for initiating war;
    it is for creating peace,
    not for destroying peace.
    This lofty ideal
    was only truly realized forty years ago:
    that is, the first socialist country, the Soviet Union,
    established the people's armed forces,
    the root of eliminating war and creating peace.
    The armed forces of the Soviet people
    first eliminated the counter-revolutionary White Russian bandits,
    eliminated Kolchak and Denikin,
    eliminated foreign interventionist forces.
    They thereby consolidated the Soviet regime,
    making the Red October increasingly glorious;
    until the eve of the fortieth anniversary of the October Revolution in 1957,
    we saw two man-made earth satellites ascend to the sky.
    The armed forces of the Soviet people
    crushed the armed forces of Nazi Germany,
    burying Hitler in the fire pit of Berlin,
    stopping the spread of the fascist plague.
    They thereby saved the people of all Europe from the fate of being enslaved,
    allowing at least half of the German nation to celebrate a new life.
    Who stopped World War II?
    Anyone can answer with certainty:
    It was the Soviet Army, Navy, and Air Force,
    they are the benefactors of all mankind!
    The armed forces of the Soviet people
    eliminated the armed forces of Japanese imperialism.
    The so-called Kwantung Army, which occupied Manchuria,
    was known as the strongest in the Japanese military,
    but was crushed by the Soviet army in a short period,
    forcing Japanese imperialism to surrender unconditionally.
    This was conducive to the victory of the War of Resistance against Japan,
    conducive to the comprehensive liberation of China,
    bringing the dawn of peace back to the East.
    Peaceful force, you are truly heroic!
    You eliminate war and create peace everywhere,
    you are the foundation of the fortress of peace.
    The armed forces of our Chinese people
    take you as a glorious example,
    we drove back the Japanese invaders,
    we eliminated the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang,
    and in the movement to Resist America and Aid Korea,
    we further curbed the arrogance of U.S. imperialism.
    We are comrades, we are brothers,
    we are aimed in the same direction.
    We are comrades, we are brothers,
    "Stopping war with arms" is our ideal.
    Let us open our throats and sing—
    sing of Moscow—Beijing,
    sing of defending world peace,
    sing of Marxism-Leninism,
    sing of the glory of our Party.
    We want to completely eliminate war,
    we want to strive for lasting peace,
    to make the world a democratic family.
        January 29, 1958

### 止戈为武之歌——纪念苏军建军四十周年
郭沫若
1958-02-23
第8版()
专栏：

    止戈为武之歌
    ——纪念苏军建军四十周年
    郭沫若
    在我们中国，远在三千年前的殷商，
    就标榜着“止戈为武”的理想。
    我们的武力是为了消灭战争，
    而不是发动战争；
    是为了产生和平，
    而不是破坏和平。
    这个崇高的理想
    在四十年前才认真实现：
    那就是第一个社会主义国家苏联
    建立了人民的武装力量，
    是消灭战争、产生和平的根源。
    苏联人民的武装力量
    首先消灭了反革命的白俄匪帮，
    消灭了高尔察克和邓尼金，
    消灭了外来的干涉武装。
    他因而巩固了苏维埃政权，
    使红色十月日益光辉灿烂；
    直到一九五七年十月革命四十周年的前夕，
    我们看到两个人造地球卫星上天。
    苏联人民的武装力量
    粉碎了纳粹德国的武装，
    使希特勒葬身在柏林的火窟里，
    使法西斯瘟疫停止了流行。
    他因而挽救了全欧洲人被奴役的命运，
    使日尔曼民族至少一半庆贺新生。
    第二次世界大战是谁制止了的？
    谁也能回答得十分肯定：
    是苏联的陆军、海军、空军，
    他们是全人类的恩人！
    苏联人民的武装力量
    消灭了日本帝国主义的武装。
    侵占着满洲的所谓关东军
    在日寇的武力中号称最为坚强，
    短时期的接触便被苏军粉碎，
    使得日本帝国主义只好无条件投降。
    这就有利于抗日战争的胜利，
    有利于中国的全面解放，
    使和平的曙光又回到东方。
    和平的武力啊，你真正英武！
    你到处消灭战争、产生和平，
    你是和平堡垒的基础。
    我们中国人民的武装力量
    就是以你为光辉的榜样，
    我们打退了日本强盗，
    我们消灭了蒋介石匪帮，
    我们在抗美援朝运动中
    更制止了美帝国主义的猖狂。
    我们是同志，我们是弟兄，
    我们是对准着一个方向。
    我们是同志，我们是弟兄，
    “止戈为武”就是我们的理想。
    让我们放开喉嗓歌唱——
    歌唱莫斯科——北京，
    歌唱保卫世界和平，
    歌唱马克思列宁主义，
    歌唱咱们党的光荣。
    我们要彻底消灭战争，
    我们要争取持久和平，
    使世界成为民主的大家庭。
        1958年1月29日
