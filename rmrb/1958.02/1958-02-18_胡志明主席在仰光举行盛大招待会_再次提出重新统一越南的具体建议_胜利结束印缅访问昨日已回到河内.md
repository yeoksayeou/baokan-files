Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Holds Grand Reception in Rangoon, Reiterates Concrete Proposals for Reunification of Vietnam, Returns to Hanoi Yesterday After Successful Conclusion of Visits to India and Burma

Author:

Date: 1958-02-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 17th. President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a grand reception in the garden of the Presidential Palace in Burma on the evening of February 16th.

U Win Maung, President of Burma, U Nu, Prime Minister, Thakin Tin, Deputy Prime Minister, U Myint Thein, Chief Justice, and the Speakers of the Chamber of Nationalities and the Chamber of Deputies of the Burmese Parliament all attended the reception. Ministers of the Burmese government, prominent figures from Rangoon, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma, totaling over six hundred people, were also present.

After visiting the Shan State, Chairman Ho Chi Minh returned to Rangoon on the morning of the 16th. At 5:00 PM that day, Chairman Ho Chi Minh held a press conference at the Presidential Palace in Burma.

At the press conference, Chairman Ho Chi Minh stated that the Vietnamese people advocate the peaceful reunification of Vietnam on an independent and democratic basis, in accordance with the provisions of the Geneva Accords. The Vietnamese people demand the establishment of a "peaceful, independent, unified, democratic, and prosperous Vietnam."

Chairman Ho Chi Minh reiterated his proposal: "A nationwide general election should be held according to the principles of universal, equal, direct, and secret ballot, and supervised by the International Commission in accordance with the provisions of the Geneva Accords."

Chairman Ho Chi Minh further suggested that after the general election, "the newly elected National Assembly should formulate a constitution for the entire country and form a national coalition government elected by the National Assembly, in order to strengthen the unity between all parties, classes, ethnic groups, and regions."

Chairman Ho said: "The different situations in the northern and southern regions of Vietnam should be taken into account; both regions should have the right to formulate regulations that conform to their own characteristics and do not violate national laws."

Chairman Ho Chi Minh said: "Before the national reunification, the authorities in both the North and the South should be responsible for bringing the two regions closer together, strengthening mutual understanding, and ensuring that organizations or individuals who support peace, reunification, and democracy enjoy democratic freedom."

Referring to the current international situation, Chairman Ho Chi Minh said that peace-loving people all over the world are demanding a high-level conference with the participation of leaders of various countries. He pointed out that actively applying the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence as elucidated in the Sino-Burmese and Sino-Indian joint statements will inevitably consolidate and enhance friendly relations between countries.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh reiterated his strong opposition to aggressive military blocs such as the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, and the Baghdad Pact. He also expressed strong opposition to the conspiracy to drag South Vietnam and other countries of Indochina into the aggressive Southeast Asia Treaty Organization.

Referring to his visit to the Union of Burma, Chairman Ho Chi Minh said that he was warmly welcomed by the Burmese government and people wherever he went. His visit achieved great success. This visit will help further develop the friendly relations between the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Burma.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 17th. President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam returned to Hanoi by plane from Rangoon today, concluding his visit to India and Burma.

After stepping off the plane, Chairman Ho Chi Minh told the 1,700 Hanoi citizens who welcomed him that he brought two precious Tet gifts for the Vietnamese people. One is the friendship and congratulations of the peoples of India and Burma to the Vietnamese people; the other is that the Presidents of India and Burma have accepted his invitation to visit Vietnam.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh said that his visit abroad was to further strengthen the friendship with India and Burma, consolidate and develop the solidarity between the peoples of Asian and African countries, and contribute to Asian and world peace. He said, we have completed this task.

Phan Ke Toai, Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, who accompanied Chairman Ho Chi Minh on his visit to India and Burma, then gave a report on Chairman Ho Chi Minh's visit to India and Burma.

Vietnamese state leaders such as Pham Van Dong, Vo Nguyen Giap, Le Duan, Truong Chinh, and Ton Duc Thang, representatives from all walks of life in Vietnam, diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam, and Indian expatriates were at the airport today to welcome Chairman Ho Chi Minh.

On his way to the Presidential Palace by car, Chairman Ho Chi Minh was warmly cheered by tens of thousands of people.

### 胡志明主席在仰光举行盛大招待会  再次提出重新统一越南的具体建议  胜利结束印缅访问昨日已回到河内

1958-02-18
第6版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席在仰光举行盛大招待会
    再次提出重新统一越南的具体建议
    胜利结束印缅访问昨日已回到河内
    新华社仰光17日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明2月16日晚上在缅甸总统府的花园里举行了盛大的招待会。
    缅甸总统吴温貌、总理吴努、副总理德钦丁、首席法官吴敏登和缅甸国会民族院和代表院的议长都出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有缅甸政府的部长们、仰光的著名人士以及各国驻缅甸的外交使节等共约六百多人。
    胡志明主席在访问了掸邦以后，在16日上午回到仰光。当天下午五时，胡志明主席在缅甸总统府举行了记者招待会。
    胡志明主席在记者招待会上说，越南人民主张按照日内瓦协议的规定，在独立和民主的基础上通过和平的方法来重新统一越南。越南人民要求建立一个“和平、独立、统一、民主和繁荣的越南。”
    胡志明主席再次建议：“根据普遍、平等、直接和不记名投票的原则，进行全国范围的普选，并且由国际委员会按照日内瓦协议的规定进行监督”。
    胡志明主席并且建议，在进行普选以后，“由新选出的国会为全国制定一部宪法，并且组成一个由国会选出的全国联合政府，以便加强各党派、各阶级、各民族和各地区之间的团结。”
    胡主席指出：“应该考虑到越南北方和南方两个地区的不同情况；两地区都应该有权制定符合自己的特点并且不违反国家法律的条例。”
    胡志明主席说：“在全国重新统一以前，南北双方的当局应该负责使两个地区互相接近，以加强彼此间的了解，并且保证那些拥护和平、拥护重新统一和民主的组织或个人得到民主自由”。
    胡志明主席在谈到目前的国际局势时说，全世界爱好和平的人民都在要求召开一个有各国领导人参加的高级会议。他指出，积极运用中缅和中印联合声明中所阐明的和平共处五项原则，就必然会巩固和增进国与国之间的友好关系。
    胡志明主席重申他强烈反对东南亚条约组织、北大西洋公约组织和巴格达条约等侵略性军事集团。他还表示强烈反对把南越和印度支那其他国家拉进侵略性的东南亚条约组织的阴谋。
    胡志明主席在谈到他对缅甸联邦的访问时说，他所到之处都受到缅甸政府和人民的热烈欢迎。他的访问获得了巨大的成就。这次访问有助于越南民主共和国和缅甸之间的友好关系的进一步发展。
    新华社河内17日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明今天乘飞机从仰光回到河内，结束了他对印度和缅甸的访问。
    胡志明主席走出飞机以后，对一千七百名欢迎他的河内市民说，他为越南人民带来了两件宝贵的春节礼物。一件是印缅两国人民对越南人民的友谊和祝贺；另一件是：印度总统和缅甸总统已经接受他的邀请，要来越南访问。
    胡志明主席说，他这次出国访问，是要进一步加强同印缅两国之间的友谊，巩固和发展亚非各国人民之间的团结，为亚洲和世界和平贡献一份力量。他说，我们已经完成了这个任务。
    随同胡志明主席访问印度和缅甸的越南民主共和国副总理潘继遂接着作了关于胡志明主席访问印度和缅甸的报告。
    今天到机场欢迎胡志明主席的有范文同、武元甲、黎笋、长征、孙德胜等越南国家领导人，越南各界的代表，各国驻越南的外交使节和印度侨民等。
    胡志明主席在乘汽车前往主席府的途中，受到成千上万的群众的热情欢呼。
