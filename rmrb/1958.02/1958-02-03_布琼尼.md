　　Budyonny

　　Born in 1883, Budyonny was a prominent Soviet military figure, a people's hero during the Civil War, an outstanding organizer and strategist of the Soviet Army, a Marshal of the Soviet Union, and an alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

　　Budyonny was drafted into the army in 1903 and participated in the Russo-Japanese War of 1904-1905. During World War I, he served in the Tsarist Russian army and began his revolutionary activities. After the February Revolution, he joined the revolutionary army and served as a regimental representative. In February 1918, he organized the Soviet Red Army's cavalry unit and fought against the White Army in the Don region.

　　During the Great Patriotic War, the cavalry units led by Budyonny relentlessly struck the fascist bandits on the Eastern Front, achieving great results. Later, Budyonny, Voroshilov, and Marshal Timoshenko served as commanders-in-chief of the three fronts respectively.

　　Budyonny was awarded many orders and medals.

### 布琼尼

1958-02-03
第6版()
专栏：人物介绍

　　布琼尼
    生于1883年，是苏联卓越的军事活动家，国内战争时期的人民英雄，苏军杰出的组织者和战略家，苏联元帅，苏共中央候补委员。
    布琼尼于1903年被征入伍，参加过1904—1905年日俄战争。第一次世界大战中，他在沙俄军队中服役，并开始了革命活动。二月革命后，他参加革命军队，担任团代表。1918年2月，他组织了苏联红军的骑兵部队，与白匪军在顿河一带作战。
    在伟大的卫国战争时期，布琼尼所领导的骑兵部队，在东线给法西斯匪徒以无情的打击，获得很大战果。后来，布琼尼和伏罗希洛夫、提摩盛科元帅分任三路战线总司令。
    布琼尼荣获了很多勋章和奖章。
