Title: Japanese Steel Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-02-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 16th. The Japanese Steel Delegation, led by Inayama Yoshihiro, the director of the Japan Iron and Steel Exporters' Association and the managing director of Yawata Iron & Steel Co., arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    The delegation consists of twelve people, including representatives from Yawata, Fuji Iron & Steel, Nippon Kokan, and Kawasaki Steel.
    At the airport, Inayama Yoshihiro stated that the delegation would hold trade negotiations with the China National Minerals Corporation and the China National Metals and Minerals Import Corporation regarding coal, iron sand, and other mineral products, as well as steel products.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Shang Guangwen, manager of the China National Minerals Corporation, Li Zhuozhi, manager of the China National Metals and Minerals Import Corporation, and Xiao Fangzhou, deputy secretary-general of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, among others.
    The two deputy heads of the delegation are Nakajima Masaho, managing director of Fuji Iron & Steel Co., and Shimizu Yoshio, managing director of Nippon Kokan Co.

### 日本钢铁代表团到达北京

1958-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    日本钢铁代表团到达北京
    新华社16日讯　由日本钢铁输出协会理事长、八幡制铁公司常务董事稻山嘉宽率领的日本钢铁代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    这个代表团由十二人组成，他们包括八幡、富士制铁、日本钢管、川崎制铁等公司的代表。
    稻山嘉宽在机场上谈到，代表团将同中国矿业公司和中国五金进口公司进行有关煤、铁砂等矿产品和钢铁产品贸易的谈判。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国矿业公司经理商广文，中国五金进口公司经理李琢之和中国国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长萧方洲等人。
    代表团的两位副团长是富士制铁公司常务董事中岛正保和日本钢管公司常务董事清水芳夫。
