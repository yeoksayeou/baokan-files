    A More Theoretical International Article
    The article "Talking about the American Great Drought" written by Comrade Fan Ruoyu in the People's Daily on January 18th, page 6, is not only an international affairs article but also a very good Marxist-Leninist propaganda essay. Explaining international issues with a high degree of theoretical power and logic, and demonstrating the inevitable demise of the capitalist system, has more persuasiveness than general articles that simply discuss the matter at hand. I hope that in the future, we can increase the number of articles with stronger ideological and theoretical content in international propaganda.
                  Dagong Bao, Hu Guopu

### 一篇理论性较强的国际专文
胡果朴
1958-02-05
第8版()
专栏：读者、作者、编者

    一篇理论性较强的国际专文
    1月18日人民日报第六版范若愚同志写的“谈谈美国大水荒”这篇文章，不仅是国际问题的专文，而且是很好的马克思列宁主义宣传论文。以高度的理论力量和逻辑性说明国际问题，论证资本主义制度的必然灭亡，这比一般的就事论事的文章，具有更高的说服力。希望今后在国际宣传方面，增加一些思想性和理论性较强的文章。
                  大公报　胡果朴
