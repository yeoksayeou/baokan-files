Title: Glasgow City Council Passes Resolution Opposing the Establishment of American Rocket Bases in Britain
Author:
Date: 1958-02-24
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, London, February 21st. The Glasgow City Council, the largest city in the Scottish region of Britain (population over one million), today passed a resolution opposing the establishment of American missile bases in Britain. The resolution stated: "The establishment of missile launch bases will not contribute to peace, but will clearly increase the risk of inviting attack."

### 格拉斯哥市议会通过决议  反对在英建立美国火箭基地

1958-02-24
第6版()
专栏：

    格拉斯哥市议会通过决议
    反对在英建立美国火箭基地
    新华社伦敦21日电　英国苏格兰地区最大的城市格拉斯哥（人口超过一百万）市议会今天通过了一项决议，反对在英国建立美国导弹基地，决议说：“建立导弹发射基地不会对和平有任何贡献，而显然地将增加招致攻击的危险。”
