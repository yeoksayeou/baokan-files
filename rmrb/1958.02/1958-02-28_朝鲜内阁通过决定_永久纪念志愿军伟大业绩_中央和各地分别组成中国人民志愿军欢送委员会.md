Title: Korean Cabinet Passes Resolution to Permanently Commemorate the Great Achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteers; Central and Local Authorities Form Chinese People's Volunteers Farewell Committees Respectively
Author:
Date: 1958-02-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 27th: The Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today passed a resolution on permanently commemorating the great achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteers and bidding farewell to the Chinese People's Volunteers. The resolution stated that the important measure taken by the Government of the People's Republic of China regarding the withdrawal of the Chinese People's Volunteers from Korea not only opened up new prospects for the peaceful resolution of the Korean issue, but also made a huge contribution to easing international tensions and consolidating world peace.

The resolution pointed out that the Chinese People's Volunteers entered Korea to participate in the war at the most difficult time of the Korean people's Fatherland Liberation War, further developing the traditional friendship between the people of Korea and China in the common struggle against American imperialism, and setting a new example of proletarian internationalist friendship and solidarity. The Chinese People's Volunteers, together with the Korean people, displayed unparalleled heroism in the struggle against the common enemy, defended the freedom and independence of Korea, and made brilliant contributions to world peace. After the war, the Volunteers actively assisted the Korean people in carrying out reconstruction. The resolution emphasized that the immortal feats and glorious achievements left by the Chinese People's Volunteers in Korea during the war and post-war periods will forever remain in the memory of the Korean people and will shine forever along with the prosperity and development of the Korean nation.

The resolution stipulates that, in order to permanently commemorate the great achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteers who have victoriously completed the glorious task of resisting America and aiding Korea, safeguarding their homes and defending their country, and to bid them farewell, the following tasks shall be carried out:

(1) To send a letter of gratitude signed by the entire Korean people to the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Volunteers;

(2) In order to permanently commemorate the martyrs of the Chinese People's Volunteers who sacrificed themselves on the Korean battlefield after establishing heroic achievements, the "Monument to the Martyrs of the Chinese People's Volunteers" in Pyongyang will be fully completed before October 10, 1958;

(3) Before March 15, 1958, all Chinese People's Volunteers' martyrs' cemeteries in various places will be renovated, and permanent protection measures will be taken;

(4) The central street of Sariwon City, North Hwanghae Province, which has a deep relationship with the Chinese People's Volunteers during the war, will be named Chinese People's Volunteers Street;

(5) All officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers who participated in the Korean War will be awarded the "Fatherland Liberation War Commemorative Medal";

(6) Produce art films and documentary films with the noble achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteers in helping the Korean people carry out the Liberation War and the reconstruction of the People's Economy as the content;

(7) Set the eighth anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea to fight, October 1958, as the Korea-China Friendship Month, and organize and carry out various grand activities to praise the great achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteers.

The Cabinet decision also instructed that all necessary facilities should be provided for the returning Chinese People's Volunteers along the railway lines and in other areas. In addition, a Central Farewell Committee for the Chinese People's Volunteers, headed by Jeong Il-ryong, has been established. Farewell committees have also been established in each province, city, and county.

### 朝鲜内阁通过决定  永久纪念志愿军伟大业绩  中央和各地分别组成中国人民志愿军欢送委员会

1958-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜内阁通过决定
    永久纪念志愿军伟大业绩
    中央和各地分别组成中国人民志愿军欢送委员会
    新华社平壤27日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁今天通过了关于永久纪念中国人民志愿军的伟大业绩和欢送中国人民志愿军的决定。决定说，中华人民共和国政府采取的关于中国人民志愿军从朝鲜撤出的重要措施，不仅对和平解决朝鲜问题打开新的局面，而且对缓和国际紧张局势、巩固世界和平作出了巨大的贡献。
    决定指出，中国人民志愿军在朝鲜人民的祖国解放战争最困难的时候入朝参战，在反对美帝国主义的共同斗争中进一步发展了朝中两国人民间的传统友谊，树立了无产阶级国际主义友谊团结的新的榜样。中国人民志愿军同朝鲜人民一起，在反对共同的敌人的斗争中发挥了无比的英勇精神，保卫朝鲜的自由和独立，并且对世界和平作出了光辉贡献。在战后，志愿军又积极地援助朝鲜人民进行恢复建设。决定强调说，中国人民志愿军在战争中和战后时期在朝鲜留下的不朽功勋和光辉的业绩，将永远留在朝鲜人民的记忆中，将同朝鲜国家的繁荣发展一同永远发射光芒。
    决定规定，为永远纪念胜利完成了抗美援朝、保家卫国的光荣任务而归国的中国人民志愿军的伟大业绩和欢送他们，要进行下列各项工作：
    （一）向中国人民和中国人民志愿军致由全体朝鲜人民签名的感谢信；
    （二）为了永远纪念在朝鲜战场上建立了英雄功绩而牺牲的中国人民志愿军烈士，在1958年10月10日以前全部建成在平壤市的“中国人民志愿军烈士纪念塔”；
    （三）在1958年3月15日以前整修各地的中国人民志愿军烈士墓，并且采取永久的保护办法；
    （四）把在战争中同中国人民志愿军有深厚姻缘的黄海北道沙里院市的中央大街命名为中国人民志愿军街；
    （五）授予参加朝鲜战争的中国人民志愿军全体官兵以“祖国解放战争纪念章”；
    （六）以中国人民志愿军在帮助朝鲜人民进行解放战争和人民经济恢复建设中所建树的高尚功绩为内容，制作艺术影片和纪录影片；
    （七）定中国人民志愿军入朝作战八周年的1958年10月为朝中友好月，组织和进行各种盛大活动，赞扬中国人民志愿军的丰功伟绩。
    内阁决定还指示在铁路沿线和其他地区，要为回国的中国人民志愿军提供一切便利条件。另外，还组成了以郑一龙为首的中国人民志愿军中央欢送委员会。各道、市、郡也分别组成欢送委员会。
