Title: North Korean Ambassador Holds Film Reception to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Army
Author:
Date: 1958-02-08
Page: 1

　　Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Army
    North Korean Ambassador Holds Film Reception
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th – The Ambassador of North Korea to China, Li Yong-ho, and his wife held a film reception in Beijing on the evening of the 7th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army.
    Among those attending the reception were Senior General Li Da, Senior General Gan Siqi, Senior General Zhang Zongxun, Senior General Deng Hua and other high-ranking generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as more than 700 officers from the army, navy and air force stationed in Beijing.
    Heads of diplomatic missions and military attachés from various countries in China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 庆祝人民军建军十周年  朝鲜大使举行电影招待会

1958-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝人民军建军十周年
    朝鲜大使举行电影招待会
    新华社7日讯　朝鲜驻我国大使李永镐暨夫人7日晚在北京举行电影招待会，庆祝朝鲜人民军建军十周年。
    出席招待会的有李达上将、甘泗淇上将、张宗逊上将、邓华上将等中国人民解放军高级将领和驻京陆、海、空军军官七百多人。
    各国驻华使节和武官，也应邀出席了招待会。
