Title: Yemen Demands Departure of British Chargé d'Affaires

Author:

Date: 1958-02-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo: The Yemeni Embassy in Egypt issued a statement on the 30th stating that the Yemeni government has demanded that the British Chargé d'Affaires in Yemen leave Yemen immediately because investigations have proven that he was involved in the recent assassination of the King of Yemen.

### 也门要英代办离境

1958-02-01
第5版()
专栏：

　　也门要英代办离境
    据新华社讯　开罗消息：也门驻埃及公使馆30日发表声明说，也门政府已经要求英国驻也门代办立即离开也门，因为调查结果证明，他同最近也门国王遇刺的事件有关。
