Title: Agreement Reached on the Issue of Yemen Forming a Federation with the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-02-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 6th. After meeting with Egyptian President Nasser today, Yemeni Crown Prince Badr said that he and President Nasser had discussed the issue of Yemen forming a federation with the United Arab Republic. He also said that the two sides had reached an agreement on this issue.

Also participating in the meeting were Badr's ten advisors, as well as Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi, Minister of State for Presidential Affairs Sabri, Speaker of the National Assembly Boghdadi, and Commander-in-Chief of the Egyptian Armed Forces Amer.

The two sides agreed to form a joint committee to draft detailed procedures for talks on forming a federation. This committee is scheduled to begin work on the 7th.

### 也门同阿拉伯联合共和国  结成联邦问题已达成协议

1958-02-08
第6版()
专栏：

    也门同阿拉伯联合共和国  结成联邦问题已达成协议
    新华社开罗6日电　也门王太子巴德尔今天在同埃及总统纳赛尔进行会谈以后说，他同纳赛尔总统讨论了也门同阿拉伯联合共和国结成联邦的问题。他并且说，双方已经就这个问题达成了协议。
    参加这一次会谈的，还有巴德尔的十位顾问以及埃及外交部长法齐、负责总统事务的国务部长萨布里、国民议会议长波格达迪和埃及武装部队总司令阿密尔。
    双方同意由双方共同组成一个委员会拟订有关结成联邦会谈的详细办法。这个委员会预定在7日开始工作。
