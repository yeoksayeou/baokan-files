Title: Celebrating the Eighth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance - Preparatory Activities Actively Underway Among People in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1958-02-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 9th: People from all walks of life in the capital are actively carrying out various preparatory activities to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

　　To celebrate this festival, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will jointly hold a grand celebration. Soviet students and interns in the capital will hold a friendship get-together with Chinese university students on the evening of February 15. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative is also preparing to hold a celebration meeting.

　　During the festival, members of the Chinese Labor People's Delegation who participated in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution in the Soviet Union will be invited to give reports to the people of the capital on their visit to the Soviet Union. The Cultural Center of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will also hold a report meeting on "New Achievements in Soviet Science and Technology," introducing the "Artificial Earth Satellite," the "Lenin Nuclear Icebreaker," and the "Kuibyshev Hydropower Station" to the masses, and will screen Soviet scientific films such as "The Road to the Stars" in conjunction with the reports.

　　The Cultural Center of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will hold Soviet music appreciation concerts and other cultural activities for literary and art enthusiasts in the capital.

### 庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订八周年  首都人民积极进行筹备活动

1958-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订八周年
    首都人民积极进行筹备活动
    据新华社9日讯　首都各界人民正在积极进行各项筹备活动，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订八周年。
    为了庆祝这个节日，中苏友好协会总会与北京市中苏友好协会将联合举行盛大的庆祝会，在首都的苏联留学生和实习生将同中国大学生们在2月15日晚举行友谊联欢会。中苏友好农业生产合作社也正在筹备举行庆祝大会。
    在节日期间，到苏联参加庆祝十月革命四十周年的中国劳动人民代表团的团员们将应邀为首都人民作访问苏联的报告。北京市中苏友好协会文化馆还将举办“苏联科学技术新成就”报告会，向群众介绍“人造地球卫星”、“列宁号原子破冰船”、“古比雪夫水电站”，并配合报告放映苏联“通向星球的道路”等科学影片。
    北京市中苏友好协会文化馆将为首都的文艺爱好者举办苏联音乐欣赏会等文艺活动。
