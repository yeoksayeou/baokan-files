Title: Spring Welcoming Song
Author: Ke Zhongping
Date: 1958-02-08
Page: 8

　　Spring Welcoming Song
　　Ke Zhongping
　　Who are we?
　　Look! We take the east wind,
　　And make it into a horn to blow,
　　We take the spring thunder,
　　And make it into a drum to beat;
　　We wear spring flowers and spring's radiance.
　　We dance with a thousand mountains, ten thousand rivers,
　　We dance until the mountains nod when they see us,
    　　The rivers wag their tails when they see us;
　　    The fields smile and open their mouths when they see us,
　　    We feed the fields, making them fatter day by day.
　　We dance until the sandy beaches also become golden and silver beaches—
　　Haha! Like a large sheepskin,
　　Wrapped in treasures filling the sky!
　　Who are we?
　　We are certain that the east wind will completely overwhelm the west wind, and the spring thunder will utterly shatter the dark clouds!
　　In the socialist world,
　　We are a grand labor song and dance troupe!

### 迎春曲
柯仲平
1958-02-08
第8版()
专栏：

　　迎春曲
　　柯仲平
　　我们是谁？
　　看！我们把东风，
　　作成号来吹，
　　把春雷，
　　作成鼓来擂；
　　我们戴春的花披春的光辉。
　　我们舞起千条山，万条水，
　　舞得山见我们就点头，
    　　水见我们就摆尾；
　　    田地看见我们就笑就张嘴，
　　    我们把田地喂的一天比一天肥。
　　我们舞得沙滩也变成金银滩，——
　　哈哈！那像一张大羊皮，
　　裹着满天的宝贝！
　　我们是谁？
　　我们一定，东风把西风完全压倒，春雷把乌云彻底粉碎！
　　我们在社会主义世界里，
　　我们是一支劳动的歌舞大队！
