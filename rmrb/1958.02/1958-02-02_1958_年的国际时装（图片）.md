　　International Fashion of 1958
    ——America attempts to unify the three aggressive blocs. The upper right pocket of the figure depicts the Russian abbreviation for the "Southeast Asia Treaty Organization." The lower left pocket represents "The North Atlantic Treaty Organization," and the lower right pocket represents the "Baghdad Pact."
