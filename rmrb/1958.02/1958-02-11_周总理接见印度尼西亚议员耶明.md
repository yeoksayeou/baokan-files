Title: Premier Zhou Receives Indonesian Parliamentarian Yamin
Author:
Date: 1958-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Premier Zhou Enlai received Indonesian parliamentarian, former Minister of Education and Culture, Muhammad Yamin, his wife, and accompanying Indonesian friends this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, Secretary-General Wu Maosun, and Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Shuliang.

Indonesian Ambassador to China Wijopranoto and his wife were also present at the meeting.

### 周总理接见印度尼西亚议员耶明

1958-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印度尼西亚议员耶明
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理在今天下午接见了印度尼西亚国会议员、前文教部长穆罕默德·耶明和他的夫人以及同行的印度尼西亚友人。
    接见时在座的有：国务院副总理陈毅、中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪、外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮。
    接见时，印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托和夫人也在座。
