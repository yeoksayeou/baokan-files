　　Rebirth of Waste Cotton
　　The waste cotton processing workshop of the Jianyang County Supply and Marketing Cooperative in Sichuan Province uses waste cotton to make cotton wadding. Last year alone, they made more than 11,000 quilts, saving more than 75,000 jin of raw cotton.　Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Zhongjing

　　废棉的新生
    四川省简阳县供销社的废棉加工房，利用废棉加工制成棉絮。去年一年，就制成一万一千多床棉絮，节约了七万五千多斤原棉。　新华社记者　孙忠靖摄
