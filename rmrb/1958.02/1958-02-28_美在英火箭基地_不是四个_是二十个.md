Title: US Rocket Bases in Britain: Not Four, But Twenty

Author:

Date: 1958-02-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, February 26th: The "Daily Express" commentator, Max, spoke about the establishment of American rocket bases on British territory, saying that instead of the initially announced four, there would be twenty, and these warheads would be supervised by Americans.

### 美在英火箭基地  不是四个　是二十个

1958-02-28
第6版()
专栏：

    美在英火箭基地
    不是四个　是二十个
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社伦敦26日讯：“每日快报”评论员马克斯谈到在英国领土上建立美国火箭基地时说，这种基地不是如最初所宣布的那样建立四个，而是二十个，同时将由美国人来监督这些原子弹头。
