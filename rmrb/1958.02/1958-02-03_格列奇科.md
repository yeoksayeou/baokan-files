    Grechko
    A distinguished Soviet military figure, Marshal of the Soviet Union, and an alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. During the Great Patriotic War, he held leadership positions in the army. After World War II, he served as the Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Forces stationed in Germany.

### 格列奇科

1958-02-03
第6版()
专栏：人物介绍

    格列奇科
    苏联卓越的军事活动家，苏联元帅，苏共中央候补委员。他在伟大卫国战争时期，担任军队的领导工作。二次世界大战后，他曾任驻德苏军部队总司令。
