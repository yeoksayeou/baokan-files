Title: Khrushchev Receives Ambassador Liu Xiao

Author:

Date: 1958-02-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 22nd - Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, on the 21st and had a cordial conversation with him.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见刘晓大使

1958-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见刘晓大使
    新华社莫斯科22日电　苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·赫鲁晓夫21日接见了中国驻苏联大使刘晓，并且同他进行了亲切的谈话。
