Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Indonesian Minister of Public Works and Energy, Nur
Author:
Date: 1958-02-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 4th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Indonesian Minister of Public Works and Energy, Panngelarang Muhammad Nur, and his entourage on the afternoon of February 3rd. Present at the meeting were Minister of Construction Engineering Liu Xiufeng and Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Shuliang, among others.

The Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wijopranoto, was also present.

Panngelarang Muhammad Nur and his entourage left Beijing by plane on the morning of February 4th to visit Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou and other places. After visiting Guangzhou, they will return to their country via Hong Kong.

### 周总理接见印度尼西亚公共工程及动力部部长努尔

1958-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见印度尼西亚公共工程及动力部部长努尔
    新华社4日讯　周恩来总理3日下午接见了印度尼西亚公共工程及动力部部长班额尔兰·穆罕默德·努尔及随员乌玛·玛斯杜基。接见时在座的有建筑工程部部长刘秀峰和外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮等人。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托也在座。
    班额尔兰·穆罕默德·努尔和他的随员已经在4日早晨乘飞机离开北京，前往上海、武汉、广州等地参观。他们将在广州参观后取道香港回国。
