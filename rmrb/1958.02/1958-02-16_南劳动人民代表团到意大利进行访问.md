Title: South Labor People's Delegation Visits Italy
Author:
Date: 1958-02-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, Feb. 15th: According to the Yugoslav News Agency, a delegation from the Socialist Alliance of Working People of Yugoslavia, headed by Vesselinov, member of the Federal Committee of the Socialist Alliance of Working People of Yugoslavia, arrived in Rome on the 14th. The delegation will visit Italy for a week as a return visit to the Socialist Party delegation that visited Yugoslavia in March last year.

### 南劳动人民代表团到意大利进行访问

1958-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    南劳动人民代表团到意大利进行访问
    据新华社15日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社罗马消息：以南斯拉夫劳动人民社会主义联盟联邦委员会主席团委员维塞林诺夫为首的南斯拉夫劳动人民社会主义联盟代表团14日到达罗马。这个代表团将在意大利作为时一周的访问，作为对在去年3月访问过南斯拉夫的社会党代表团的回访。
