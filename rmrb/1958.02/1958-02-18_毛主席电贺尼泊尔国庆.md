Title: Chairman Mao Congratulates Nepal on its National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-02-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram on February 17th to His Majesty Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Deva, King of the Kingdom of Nepal, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Nepal. The telegram reads as follows:

His Majesty Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Deva, King of the Kingdom of Nepal:

On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Nepal, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of Nepal on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the traditional friendship between China and Nepal will continue to develop and strengthen on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. I wish Your Majesty and the people of Nepal greater achievements in the cause of seeking national prosperity.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　February 17, 1958

### 毛主席电贺尼泊尔国庆

1958-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺尼泊尔国庆
    新华社17日讯　毛泽东主席十七日致电尼泊尔王国国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦，祝贺尼泊尔王国国庆。贺电如下：尼泊尔王国国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦陛下：
    值此尼泊尔王国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民，并以我个人的名义向陛下和尼泊尔人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝中尼两国间的传统友谊在和平共处五项原则的基础上继续发展和加强。祝陛下和尼泊尔人民在谋求国家繁荣昌盛的事业中取得更大的成就。
                              中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                        一九五八年二月十七日
