Title: Romanian-Hungarian Party and Government Delegations Sign Joint Statement
Author:
Date: 1958-02-28
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, February 27th: According to local newspaper reports, the Romanian government and Workers' Party delegation and the Hungarian government and Socialist Workers' Party delegation signed a statement on their talks in Bucharest on the evening of the 26th. Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej and János Kádár signed the statement.
　　The full text of the statement will be published on March 2nd.

### 罗匈党政代表团签署联合声明

1958-02-28
第7版()
专栏：

    罗匈党政代表团签署联合声明
    新华社布加勒斯特27日电据这里报纸报道，罗马尼亚政府和工人党代表团和匈牙利政府和社会主义工人党代表团26日晚在布加勒斯特签署了会谈声明。在声明上签字的是乔治乌—德治和卡达尔。
    声明全文将在3月2日公布。
