Title: Left-Wing Members of British Labour Party Announce Formation of New Group
Author: 
Date: 1958-02-14
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, London, February 13th: A number of left-wing members of the British Labour Party, dissatisfied with the right-wing leadership of the Labour Party, announced the formation of a new group on the evening of the 12th. The chairman of this group is Stephen Swingler.
    It is reported that supporters of this group include Labour MPs such as Ian Mikardo, Konni Zilliacus, and Sydney Silverman. They were previously known as "Bevanites," but they are now disappointed with Bevan's compromising attitude towards the right-wing members of the Labour Party.

### 英工党部分左翼党员宣布组成新集团

1958-02-14
第7版()
专栏：

    英工党部分左翼党员宣布组成新集团
    据新华社伦敦13日电　不满英国工党右翼领导机构的一些工党左翼党员12日晚间宣布组成了一个新的集团。这个集团的主席是斯蒂芬·斯文格勒。
    据报道，支持这个集团的包括伊恩·米卡多、康尼·齐里亚库斯和悉尼·锡尔弗曼等工党议员，他们过去被人叫做“比万分子”，但是，现在他们对于比万对工党右翼分子所采取的妥协态度感到失望。
