Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Inflicts Two Heavy Blows on French Troops in One Day
Author: 
Date: 1958-02-16
Page: 6
    
    Algerian National Liberation Army
    Inflicts Two Heavy Blows on French Troops in One Day
    Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, 15th
  Algiers News: The Algerian National Liberation Army dealt two heavy blows to the French colonial army on the 14th. Twenty-two French soldiers were killed in one day.
    According to reports, in the battle that took place in Berrouaghia (seventy kilometers southwest of Algiers), a Liberation Army unit of three to four hundred men, supported by mortars and heavy machine guns, fiercely attacked the French army. Twelve French soldiers were killed and seventeen wounded.
    In the battle of Roumel (eighty kilometers southeast of Algiers), a Liberation Army unit composed of two hundred men engaged in fierce fighting with the French army. Ten French soldiers were killed.
    Two days ago, in the battle that took place in the Constantine region, seven French soldiers were killed. On the 10th, the National Liberation Army shot down a French military aircraft with machine guns.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放军  一天内两次重创法军

1958-02-16
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚民族解放军
    一天内两次重创法军
    据新华社15日讯
  阿尔及尔消息：阿尔及利亚民族解放军14日两次重创法国殖民军。法军在一天之内被击毙二十二人。
    据报道，在贝鲁阿吉亚（阿尔及尔市西南七十公里）发生的战斗中，有三四百人的一支解放军部队在迫击炮和重机枪支援下痛击了法军。法军被击毙十二人，受伤十七人。
    在俄马勒（阿尔及尔市东南八十公里）的战斗中，一支由二百人组成的解放军部队同法军展开激战。法军十人被打死。
    两天前，在君士坦丁地区发生的战斗中，法军有七人毙命。民族解放军10日用机枪击落一架法国军用飞机。
