Spring Couplets Selection

　　Spring arrives in the world, enjoy the fun and add vigor
　　The sun returns to the earth, arrange production and seize the farming season
　　Five Goods and Four Nos to strive to be a model
　　Thousands of troops and horses build the countryside
　　　　(Cai Shengshuo)
