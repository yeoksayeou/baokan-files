Title: Nanping-Eyang Railway Section Opens for Temporary Operation
Author:
Date: 1958-02-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 11th. The Nanping-Fuzhou Railway, the second railway trunk line in Fujian Province, began temporary freight operations on February 10th on the Nanping-Eyang section. The Nanfu Railway branches off from the Nanping Station of the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway, and the Nanping to Eyang section is seventy kilometers long.

### 南平莪洋间铁路临时营业

1958-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    南平莪洋间铁路临时营业
    据新华社福州11日电　福建省第二条铁路干线南平—福州铁路南平莪洋段10日开始举办临时货运业务。南福铁路是从鹰厦铁路的南平车站岔出的，从南平到莪洋段共七十公里。
