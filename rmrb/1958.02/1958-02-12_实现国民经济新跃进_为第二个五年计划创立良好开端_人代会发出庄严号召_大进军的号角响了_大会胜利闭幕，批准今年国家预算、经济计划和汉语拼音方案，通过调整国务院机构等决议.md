Title: Achieve a New Leap Forward in the National Economy and Create a Good Start for the Second Five-Year Plan; The National People's Congress Issues a Solemn Call; The Bugle of the Great March Has Sounded; The Congress Successfully Concludes, Approving This Year's National Budget, Economic Plan, and the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme, and Adopting Resolutions on Adjusting the Structure of the State Council, etc.
Author:
Date: 1958-02-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th -- The Fifth Session of the First National People's Congress closed on the afternoon of the 11th. At today's meeting, all the representatives unanimously passed a resolution calling on the people of all ethnic groups across the country to summon their energy, strive for the top, and fight for the successful realization of the 1958 national budget and national economic plan, and strive for a new leap forward in the national economy in 1958 and to create a good start for the Second Five-Year Plan.

At 4:40 PM, after the discussions concluded, the meeting began to pass various resolutions. After hearing the report of Liu Lantao, Chairman of the Budget Committee, all representatives unanimously passed the resolution on the implementation of the 1957 national budget and the 1958 national budget and the 1958 national economic plan, approving in principle the implementation of the 1957 national budget; approving with amendments the 1958 national budget and the "Report on the Implementation of the 1957 National Budget and the Draft 1958 National Budget" made by Vice Premier and Minister of Finance Li Xiannian on behalf of the State Council; approving the 1958 national economic plan and the "Report on the Draft 1958 National Economic Plan" made by Vice Premier and Director of the State Economic Commission Bo Yibo on behalf of the State Council.

The resolution pointed out: The Congress believes that the implementation of the 1957 national budget was good and expresses satisfaction with the implementation of the 1957 national budget. The Congress agrees to the 1958 national budget. After pointing out the favorable domestic and international situation for completing this year's national budget and national economic plan, the resolution requires all departments, all localities, all enterprises, and all institutions across the country to adapt to the current new situation and, in accordance with the 1958 national budget and national economic plan approved by this meeting, extensively mobilize and organize the masses to conduct discussions, formulate production increase and conservation plans for their respective departments, localities, enterprises, and institutions, so that the national budget and national plan are combined with the enthusiasm of the masses and become the conscious action of the masses.

Subsequently, the meeting passed resolutions on other items on the agenda.

The meeting passed a resolution on the work report of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, approving the work report made by Vice Chairman and Secretary-General Peng Zhen.

The meeting passed a resolution on the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme, approving the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme and agreeing in principle to the report by Director Wu Yuzhang on the current reform of the written language and the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme.

The meeting passed a decision on the adjustment of the organizational structure of the State Council; based on the nomination of Premier Zhou Enlai, the meeting decided on the appointments and removals of certain members of the State Council. The meeting passed a resolution on changing the municipality of Tianjin directly under the central government to a municipality under the jurisdiction of Hebei Province. The meeting passed a resolution on the appointment and removal of the President and Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; based on the nomination of Premier Zhou Enlai, the meeting decided on the candidates for President and Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

After hearing the report on the examination of proposals by Li Xuefeng, Chairman of the Proposal Examination Committee, the meeting passed an opinion on the examination of proposals. This meeting received a total of eighty-one proposals related to various aspects of work. After the Proposal Examination Committee examined each proposal, the meeting decided to submit the proposals of this meeting to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the State Council for handling in accordance with the examination opinions and to submit a report on the handling situation.

Based on the nomination of the Presidium of this meeting, the meeting elected one additional Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress, four members of the Standing Committee, four members of the Nationalities Committee, seven members of the Bills Committee, and two members of the Budget Committee.

Each time a resolution or decision was submitted for voting and passed, prolonged applause resounded throughout the venue.

At 5:35 PM, Executive Chairman Peng Zhen announced that all items on the agenda of this meeting had been completed and that the work of this congress had been successfully completed. At this moment, all representatives stood up, and the orchestra played the national anthem. The representatives applauded enthusiastically for a long time, celebrating the successful conclusion of the congress.

The executive chairmen of today's afternoon session were: Liu Shaoqi, Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Luo Ronghuan, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong. A total of 956 representatives attended today's afternoon session. Heads of various government departments attended the meeting as observers. Foreign envoys and diplomatic officials in China, as well as foreign guests in Beijing, were invited to attend the meeting as observers. (Picture attached)

### 实现国民经济新跃进　为第二个五年计划创立良好开端  人代会发出庄严号召　大进军的号角响了  大会胜利闭幕，批准今年国家预算、经济计划和汉语拼音方案，通过调整国务院机构等决议

1958-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    实现国民经济新跃进　为第二个五年计划创立良好开端
    人代会发出庄严号召　大进军的号角响了
    大会胜利闭幕，批准今年国家预算、经济计划和汉语拼音方案，通过调整国务院机构等决议
    新华社11日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第五次会议11日下午闭幕。全体代表在今天的会议上一致通过决议，号召全国各族人民应当鼓起干劲，力争上游，为争取1958年国家预算和国民经济计划的顺利实现而奋斗，为争取1958年国民经济新的跃进和为第二个五年计划创立一个良好的开端而奋斗。
    下午四时四十分，在大会讨论结束后，会议开始通过各项决议。会议首先听取预算委员会主任委员刘澜涛的报告以后，全体代表一致通过关于1957年国家预算执行情况和1958年国家预算及1958年度国民经济计划的决议，原则批准1957年国家预算的执行情况；修正批准1958年国家预算和李先念副总理兼财政部长代表国务院所作的“关于1957年国家预算执行情况和1958年国家预算草案的报告”；批准1958年度国民经济计划和薄一波副总理兼国家经济委员会主任代表国务院所作的“关于1958年国民经济计划草案的报告”。
    决议指出：大会认为1957年国家预算的执行情况是良好的，对1957年国家预算的执行情况表示满意。大会同意1958年的国家预算。决议在指出对完成今年国家预算和国民经济计划的有利的国内外形势以后，要求全国各部门、各地方、各企业和各事业单位，都应当适应目前的新形势，根据本次会议批准的1958年国家预算和国民经济计划，广泛地发动和组织群众进行讨论，制定本部门、本地方、本企业和本事业单位的增产节约计划，使国家预算和国家计划同群众的积极性结合起来，成为群众的自觉行动。
    接着，会议就大会的其他各项议程通过了各项决议。
    会议通过关于全国人民代表大会常务委员会工作报告的决议，批准彭真副委员长兼秘书长所作的工作报告。
    会议通过关于汉语拼音方案的决议，批准汉语拼音方案，原则同意吴玉章主任关于当前文字改革和汉语拼音方案的报告。
    会议通过关于调整国务院所属组织机构的决定；根据周恩来总理的提名，决定了国务院若干组成人员的任免。会议通过关于将直辖市天津市改为河北省省辖市的决议。会议通过关于中国科学院院长副院长任免问题的决议；根据周恩来总理的提名，决定了中国科学院院长副院长的人选。
    会议在听了提案审查委员会主任委员李雪峰关于提案的审查报告后，通过了关于提案审查的意见。这次会议收到有关各方面工作的提案共八十一件，经过提案审查委员会的逐案审查，会议决定：把这次会议的提案交由全国人民代表大会常务委员会和国务院依照审查意见进行处理，并将处理情况提出报告。
    会议根据本次会议主席团的提名，补选第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长一人、常务委员会委员四人、民族委员会委员四人、法案委员会委员七人、预算委员会委员二人。
    在每项决议和决定提付表决和通过的时候，全场响起经久不息的掌声。
    五时三十五分，执行主席彭真宣布这次会议的各项议程进行完毕，这次大会的工作已胜利完成。这时，全体代表起立，乐队奏国歌。代表们长时间地热烈鼓掌，庆贺大会胜利闭幕。
    今天下午大会的执行主席是：刘少奇、林伯渠、李济深、罗荣桓、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通。出席今天下午大会的代表共有九百五十六人。政府各部门负责人列席了会议。各国驻华使节和外交官员，以及在京的外宾应邀列席了会议。（附图片）
