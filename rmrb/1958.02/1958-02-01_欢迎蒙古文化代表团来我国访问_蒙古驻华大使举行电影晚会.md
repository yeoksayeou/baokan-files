Title: Welcoming the Mongolian Cultural Delegation's Visit to China, Mongolian Ambassador to China Hosts Film Evening

Author: 
Date: 1958-02-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. To welcome the Mongolian cultural delegation's visit to China, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Rubsand and his wife, held a film evening in Beijing on January 31st. The Mongolian film "Mars" was screened at the event.

The entire Mongolian cultural delegation, headed by the Mongolian Minister of Culture, Sosorbaram, attended the evening.

Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Ministers of Culture Zhang Zhixiang and Zheng Zhenduo, along with over 200 people from the capital's literary and art circles, were also invited to attend the evening.

A reception was held before the film evening.

### 欢迎蒙古文化代表团来我国访问  蒙古驻华大使举行电影晚会

1958-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

    欢迎蒙古文化代表团来我国访问
    蒙古驻华大使举行电影晚会
    新华社31日讯　为欢迎蒙古文化代表团来中国访问，蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使鲁布桑和夫人1月31日晚在北京举行电影晚会。晚会上放映了蒙古影片“火星”。
    以蒙古文化部部长索苏尔巴拉木为首的蒙古文化代表团全体人员出席了晚会。
    国务院副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长张致祥、郑振铎和首都文艺界人士共二百多人，也应邀出席了晚会。
    在晚会以前，还举行了酒会。
