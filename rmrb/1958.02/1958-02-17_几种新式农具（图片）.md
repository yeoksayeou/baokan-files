Title: Several New Types of Agricultural Implements (Pictures)
Author: Ding Fengling
Date: 1958-02-17
Page: 2

Several New Types of Agricultural Implements

    The local state-owned Heilongjiang Machinery Factory is currently trial-manufacturing six new types of agricultural implements.

    The upper left picture shows the successfully trial-manufactured universal tillage machine, used for rice paddy production, developing drainage ditches, and building earth embankments.

    The lower left picture shows a washing machine, which is very convenient for washing mud off of radishes and potatoes. It can be hand-cranked or powered by electricity.

    The picture below shows a tractor-drawn double-wheeled five-share plow used for reclaiming wasteland and plowing cultivated land. It can cultivate 0.63 hectares of land per hour.
                        Photographed by Ding Fengling (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 几种新式农具（图片）
丁凤岭
1958-02-17
第2版()
专栏：

    几种新式农具
    地方国营黑龙江机械厂正在试制六种新式农具。
    左图是试制成功的万能耕作机，它是稻田生产、开发顺水沟、筑土梗用的。
    左下图是洗涤机，用它来洗除萝卜、土豆上的泥土非常方便，可以手摇，也可以用电力带动。
    下图是用于开荒地、翻熟地的机引双轮五铧犁，它一小时可以开垦0.63公顷土地。
                        丁凤岭摄（新华社稿）
