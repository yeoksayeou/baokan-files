Title: Spring Songs (Three Songs)
Author: Zang Kejia
Date: 1958-02-18
Page: 8

    Spring Songs (Three Songs)
    Zang Kejia
    I. Listen
    Listen,
    What is this sound
    That makes the mountains shake and the earth move?
    It's like waves surging,
    Thunder roaring,
    A great army marching.
    Before my eyes, a dragon horse
    Is galloping towards a goal,
    Farther, and farther, flashing with fiery red mane.
    II. New "National Airs" of the Socialist Era
    --After Reading the "Best Poems" Published in the "People's Daily"
    Such poems,
    Reading them brightens the eyes;
    Such songs,
    Listening to them stirs the heart.
    The resounding slogan of conquering nature,
    It is full of confidence and heroic spirit!
    Every poem follows a fierce struggle,
    It is the new "National Airs" of the socialist era.
    III. Our Hearts are Equally Excited
    --Thinking of Poet Friends During the Spring Festival
    The Spring Festival has arrived,
    My heart is thinking,
    Thinking of the rural areas of Hebei, the Yumen Oilfield,
    It passed over Wuhan and fell on the Songhua River.
    Although we are not in the same place,
    What we think is the same;
    Although we are not in the same place,
    We are on the same battle line.
    You watch the black oil gushing like a spring;
    You watch the machines turning nimbly;
    On the boundless fields, the production army is leaping forward,
    You, you are standing in the midst of this team.
    The Spring Festival has arrived,
    My heart is thinking;
    Although we are in the north, south, west, and east,
    Our hearts are equally excited.

### 春天的歌（三首）
臧克家
1958-02-18
第8版()
专栏：

    春天的歌（三首）
    臧克家
    一、你听
    你听，
    这是什么响声
    使得山摇地也动？
    它像浪潮在翻滚，
    雷霆在震响，
    大军在进行。
    我眼前，一匹龙马
    向着一个目标在奔腾，
    远了，更远了，闪动着火样的红鬃。
    二、社会主义时代的 新“国风”
    ——读了“人民日报”上所
    发表的“最好的诗”以后
    这样的诗篇，
    读了叫人眼明；
    这样的歌声，
    听了叫人心动。
    征服自然的响亮口号，
    它充满了信心和豪情！
    每一首诗跟着一场猛烈的斗争，
    它是社会主义时代的新“国风”。
    三、我们的心一样的激动
    ——春节念诗友
    春节来到了眼前，
    我的心呀在思念，
    思念河北农村，玉门油矿，
    它驰过武汉，落到了松花江上。
    我们虽然不在一个地方，
    我们所想的却是一样；
    我们虽然不在一个地方，
    我们却在一条战线上。
    你看着黑油像泉水喷涌；
    你看着机器在灵活的转动；
    无边的田野上生产大军在跃进，
    你呀，你就站在这队伍的当中。
    春节来到了眼前，
    我的心呀在思念；
    我们虽然是南北西东，
    我们的心一样的激动。
