Title: Statistical Table of the Implementation of the 1958 Farmland Irrigation and Water Conservation Plan
Author: 
Date: 1958-02-11
Page: 6

    Statistical Table of the Implementation of the 1958 Farmland Irrigation and Water Conservation Plan
    (As of February 10th) (Attached Charts)
    (Note) Recently, in accordance with the new developments in the water conservancy movement, most provinces and regions across the country have revised the original targets for increasing irrigated area for farmland irrigation as follows: Henan 43 million mu, Hebei 40 million mu, Shandong 30 million mu, Jiangsu 14.25 million mu, Shanxi 12 million mu, Shaanxi, Gansu, and Guangxi 10 million mu each, Hubei 8.11 million mu, Guangdong 8 million mu, Anhui 7.19 million mu, Guizhou and Yunnan 6 million mu each, and other provinces such as Jiangxi, Fujian, Zhejiang, Hunan, Inner Mongolia, Qinghai, Liaoning, and Jilin have also increased their targets to varying degrees. Sichuan and Heilongjiang are also studying revisions.
    The completion of soil and water conservation is relatively poor. Except for Gansu, Shanxi, Henan, Anhui, Qinghai and other provinces that are progressing relatively quickly, the overall progress is slow. The total amount completed only accounts for 37.2% of the revised plan targets, which is significantly behind the completion of various farmland irrigation indicators. This situation urgently needs to be improved.

### 1958年度农田水利及水土保持计划实现情况统计表

1958-02-11
第6版()
专栏：

    1958年度农田水利及水土保持计划实现情况统计表
    （到2月10日为止）（附图表）
    （附注）最近全国绝大部分省区，根据水利运动发展新的情况，对原订的农田水利增加灌溉面积指标修订如下：河南四千三百万亩，河北四千万亩，山东三千万亩，江苏一千四百二十五万亩，山西一千二百万亩，陕西、甘肃、广西各为一千万亩，湖北八百一十一万亩，广东八百万亩，安徽七百一十九万亩，贵州、云南各为六百万亩，其它江西、福建、浙江、湖南、内蒙古、青海、辽宁、吉林等省也均有所增加，四川、黑龙江也正在研究修订中。
    水土保持完成情况较差，除甘肃、山西、河南、安徽、青海等省进度较快外，一般进展都比较缓慢。总的完成数量，仅占修订后的计划指标的37.2％，与农田水利各项指标的完成情况相比较，就大大落后了一步。这种情况，急待改善。
