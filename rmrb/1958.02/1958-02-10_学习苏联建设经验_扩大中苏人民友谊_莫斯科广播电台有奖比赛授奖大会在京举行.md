Title: Learning from Soviet Construction Experience, Expanding Sino-Soviet People's Friendship: Moscow Radio Award Ceremony Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-02-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 9th: A Moscow Radio award ceremony celebrating the October Revolution was held in Beijing today.

This award competition was organized by Moscow Radio specifically for Chinese listeners as a "Question and Answer Contest" program from April to August last year to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, said in his opening remarks at the award ceremony that since the "Question and Answer Contest" began, Moscow Radio had received 150,000 letters from Chinese listeners, of which 5,799 people answered all the questions correctly; fourteen people received prizes such as radios, cameras, and watches.

Wen Jize, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, pointed out in his speech that Moscow Radio, through this competition, helped Chinese listeners learn or review the Soviet Union's construction experience, expanded its connection with Chinese listeners, and further strengthened the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people.

Soviet Cultural Counselor to China, Sudakov, also spoke at the evening and, on behalf of Moscow Radio, presented radios to the winners in Beijing - Li Chaoyang, Sun Changming, and Zhao Guorui. Li Chaoyang spoke on behalf of all the winners, expressing deep gratitude to Moscow Radio.

### 学习苏联建设经验　扩大中苏人民友谊  莫斯科广播电台有奖比赛授奖大会在京举行

1958-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　学习苏联建设经验　扩大中苏人民友谊
    莫斯科广播电台有奖比赛授奖大会在京举行
    据新华社9日讯　苏联莫斯科广播电台庆祝十月革命有奖比赛授奖晚会今天在北京举行。
    这次有奖比赛是莫斯科广播电台为了庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年，在去年4月到8月间专门对中国听众组织的一个“回答问题的有奖比赛”节目。
    中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权在授奖晚会的开幕词中说，自“回答问题有奖比赛”开始举行以后，莫斯科广播电台共收到了十五万封中国听众的信，其中有五千七百九十九人答对了全部问题；有十四人得到了收音机、照像机和手表等奖品。
    广播事业局副局长温济泽在讲话中指出，莫斯科电台通过这次比赛，帮助中国听众学习了或者重新温习了苏联的建设经验，扩大了和中国听众的联系，并且更进一步增强了中苏两国人民的友谊。
    苏联驻中国大使馆文化参赞苏达柯夫在晚会上也讲了话，并代表莫斯科广播电台把收音机授给了在北京的得奖人——李朝阳、孙长鸣和赵国瑞等三人，李朝阳代表所有得奖者讲了话，对莫斯科广播电台表示深切的感谢。
