Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army and Korean People's Army Sign Joint Order on Handover of Defense

Author:

Date: 1958-02-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 25th: General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and General Li Kwon Mu, Chief of Staff of the Korean People's Army, signed a joint order at the Volunteer Army Headquarters today regarding the handover of defense responsibilities between the Volunteer Army and the People's Army.

According to the joint statement of the Chinese and Korean governments, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army has decided to withdraw from Korea in batches throughout 1958. The defense responsibilities borne by the Volunteer Army will be taken over by the Korean People's Army. The headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the headquarters of the Korean People's Army have consulted on the issue of handing over defense responsibilities. During the consultation process, both parties jointly formulated a defense handover plan and drafted a joint order on the handover of defense responsibilities, which will be simultaneously issued to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army units and the Korean People's Army units and jointly implemented.

The signing ceremony was held at 8:00 a.m. today. The Chinese People's Volunteer Army was represented by General Yang Yong, General Wang Ping, Major General Wang Yunrui, Major General Ding Laifu, and Major General Zhang Mingyuan. The Korean People's Army was represented by General Li Kwon Mu, Major General Li Chengshi, Major General Kim Il, Major General Park Gilnam, and Major General Li Deyuan.

Currently, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army has formed a Defense Transfer Committee headed by General Yang Yong, and the Korean People's Army is also preparing to establish a Defense Reception Committee.

### 我国志愿军和朝鲜人民军  签定防务交接联合命令

1958-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我国志愿军和朝鲜人民军
    签定防务交接联合命令
    新华社平壤25日电　中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将和朝鲜人民军总参谋长李权武上将，今天在志愿军总部就有关志愿军和人民军双方交接防务的联合命令上签字。
    根据中朝两国政府联合声明，中国人民志愿军决定1958年全部分批撤出朝鲜。志愿军所担负的防务，将由朝鲜人民军接替。中国人民志愿军总部和朝鲜人民军总部就交接防务问题进行了协商，在协商过程中，双方共同作出了防务交接计划，拟定了有关防务交接的联合命令，将向中国人民志愿军部队和朝鲜人民军部队同时发布，并共同执行。
    签字仪式于今天上午八时举行。参加签字仪式的，中国人民志愿军方面有：杨勇上将、王平上将、王蕴瑞少将、丁莱夫少将、张明远少将；朝鲜人民军方面有：李权武上将、李成实少将、金一少将、朴吉南少将、李德元少将等。
    目前中国人民志愿军已组成了以杨勇上将为首的防务移交委员会，朝鲜人民军也正在筹组防务接收委员会。
