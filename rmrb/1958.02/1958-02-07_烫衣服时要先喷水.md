Title: Spray Water Before Ironing Clothes
Author: Hong Zhi
Date: 1958-02-07
Page: 8

Why?

　　Spray Water Before Ironing Clothes
    Clothes are made of long fibers. When dry, these fibers are packed tightly and are not easily reshaped, so it is difficult to iron clothes flat. However, if you spray some water on the clothes, the water molecules will move between the fibers, separating these long fibers. The fiber molecules are no longer so tightly packed. With this extra space, the fiber molecules can stretch and bend. When we iron them with an iron, the fiber molecules will straighten out, and the clothes will be ironed flat.

    When ironing clothes, some people first lay a damp towel on the clothes before ironing. This not only prevents the iron from directly scorching the clothes, but also allows the moisture from the towel to penetrate the clothes evenly, which better allows the fiber molecules to straighten, thus ironing the clothes flat.

　　　　　　　　(Hong Zhi)

### 烫衣服时要先喷水
鸿志
1958-02-07
第8版()
专栏：为什么？

　　烫衣服时要先喷水
    衣服是由一些很长的纤维组成的，在干的时候这些纤维排的很紧，不容易改变形状，因此就不容易把衣服烫平。但是若在衣服上喷上了一些水，那么这些水的分子就会跑到纤维与纤维之间，把这些细长的纤维彼此隔开。纤维分子排得不再是那样紧密了。有了空余的地方，这些纤维分子就可以伸直，也可以弯曲。这时我们再用熨斗去烫，那么就会使纤维的分子平直的伸开，也就把衣服烫平了。
    在烫衣服时，有人用湿手巾先铺在衣服上再去烫，这样不仅可以避免直接用熨斗烫会把衣服烫焦，而且还可以使手巾里的水分均匀的渗到衣服里去，也就能更好地使纤维分子伸直，把衣服烫平。　　　
　　　　　　　　（鸿志）
