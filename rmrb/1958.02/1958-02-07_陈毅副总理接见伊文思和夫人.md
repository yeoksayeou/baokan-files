Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Ivens and His Wife
Author:
Date: 1958-02-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 6th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received Joris Ivens, the internationally renowned film director and International Peace Prize winner, who recently became the artistic advisor to the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, and his wife, in Beijing on the morning of the 3rd.

Also present at the reception were Wang Lanxi, Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, and Ding Qiao, Editor-in-Chief of the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio.

### 陈毅副总理接见伊文思和夫人

1958-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见伊文思和夫人
    新华社6日讯　陈毅副总理3日上午在北京接见了最近就任中央新闻纪录电影制片厂艺术顾问的国际著名电影导演、国际和平奖金获得者尤列斯·伊文思和他的夫人。
    接见时在座的还有文化部电影事业管理局局长王阑西和中央新闻纪录电影制片厂总编辑丁峤。
