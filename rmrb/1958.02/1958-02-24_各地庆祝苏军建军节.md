Title: Various Regions Celebrate Soviet Army Day
Author:
Date: 1958-02-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency: Shanghai, Shenyang, Nanjing, Wuhan, Guangdong, Jilin, Heilongjiang, Inner Mongolia, Xinjiang, Dalian, and other provinces (autonomous regions), cities, and various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held gatherings on February 22nd and 23rd to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

The celebration meeting jointly held by the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Shanghai branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association was presided over by Ke Qingshi, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China. More than 1,000 people from all walks of life attended the celebration meeting. At the meeting, Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison, and Yelisavetkin, Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, delivered speeches. The Soviet Consul General in Shanghai held a banquet on the evening of the 21st to entertain people from all walks of life in Shanghai and celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

More than 1,000 people from all walks of life in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City, as well as representatives of officers and soldiers stationed in Shenyang, attended the Shenyang celebration meeting. Soviet experts in Shenyang and Kim Yong-bin, North Korean Consul General in Shenyang, were also invited to attend the celebration meeting.

More than 3,000 people from all walks of life in Nanjing held a grand gathering on the 23rd. Soviet experts working in Nanjing were invited to attend the celebration meeting. Xu Shiyou, Commander of the Nanjing Military Region, delivered a speech at the meeting on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Nanjing troops and various military academies stationed in Nanjing, extending warm congratulations to the great Soviet people, the heroic Soviet army, and the Soviet experts in Nanjing.

More than 1,300 people from all walks of life and officers and soldiers stationed in Wuhan participated in the celebration meeting held in Wuhan. Soviet experts in Wuhan also attended the meeting. In the evening, the people of Wuhan had a get-together with the Soviet experts in Wuhan.

More than 1,500 representatives from the army, navy, and air force stationed in Guangzhou, as well as people from all walks of life in Guangzhou, held a grand gathering. Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and concurrently Political Commissar of the Guangzhou Military Region, Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Qu Mengjue, Secretary of the Provincial Committee, leaders of the Guangzhou Garrison, and consuls or representatives from the Soviet, Czechoslovak, Polish, and Vietnamese consulates in Guangzhou attended the meeting.

More than 1,000 representatives from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the autonomous region, as well as officers and soldiers of the Inner Mongolia Military Region, participated in the celebration meeting held by the direct organs of the Party, government, and military in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Hohhot City. Ting Mao (Mongolian nationality), Deputy Political Commissar of the Inner Mongolia Military Region, delivered a speech at the meeting. Tse. Dash, Consul General of the Mongolian People's Republic in Hohhot, and experts from the Mongolian People's Republic also attended the meeting.

More than 1,000 people from all walks of life in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region and Urumqi City held a meeting on the 23rd. Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Party of China, Saifuddin, Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, Lieutenant General Tao Zhiyue, Deputy Commander of the Xinjiang Military Region and concurrently Commander of the Production and Construction Corps, and Lieutenant General Guo Peng, Deputy Commander of the Xinjiang Military Region, attended the meeting. Dabashen, Soviet Consul General in Urumqi, and Soviet experts in Urumqi also attended the meeting.

Representatives from all walks of life and local garrisons in Wuhan, Shenyang, Dalian, Changchun, Harbin, and other places paid their respects at the tombs of Soviet martyrs on the 23rd.

### 各地庆祝苏军建军节

1958-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    各地庆祝苏军建军节
    据新华社讯　上海、沈阳、南京、武汉、广东、吉林、黑龙江、内蒙古、新疆、旅大等省（区）、市和中国人民解放军各部队22日和23日分别集会，庆祝苏军建军四十周年。
    中国人民解放军驻上海部队和上海市中苏友好协会联合举行的庆祝大会，由中共上海市委员会第一书记柯庆施主持。参加庆祝大会的有各界人士共一千多人。会上，上海警备区司令员王必成中将和苏联驻上海总领事叶里沙维金在大会上讲了话。苏联驻沪总领事21日晚举行宴会招待上海各界人士，同庆苏军建军四十周年。
    参加沈阳市庆祝大会的有辽宁省、沈阳市各界人民和沈阳驻军官兵代表一千多人，在沈阳的苏联专家和朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬也应邀参加了庆祝大会。
    南京市各界人民三千多人，23日隆重集会。在南京工作的苏联专家都应邀参加了庆祝大会。驻南京部队首长许世友上将在大会上代表南京部队和驻南京各军事院校的全体官兵，向伟大的苏联人民、英雄的苏联军队以及在南京的苏联专家热烈祝贺。
    在武汉举行的庆祝大会，有各界人民和武汉驻军官兵一千三百多人参加，在武汉的苏联专家也参加了大会。晚间，武汉人民同在武汉的苏联专家进行了联欢。
    驻广州陆海空军及广州市各界人民代表一千五百多人举行了盛大集会，中共广东省委第一书记兼驻军政委陶铸、广东省长陈郁、省委书记区梦觉、广州驻军首长及苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、越南等国驻广州领事馆领事或代表，都参加了这个大会。
    内蒙古自治区和呼和浩特市党、政、军直属机关举行的庆祝大会，有自治区各族各界代表和内蒙古军区官兵共一千多人参加。内蒙古军区副政委廷楙（蒙古族）少将在会上讲了话。蒙古人民共和国驻呼和浩特市总领事策·达什和蒙古人民共和国专家也出席了大会。
    新疆维吾尔自治区和乌鲁木齐市各界人民一千多人，23日举行了大会。参加大会的有中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会第一书记王恩茂、新疆维吾尔自治区主席赛福鼎、新疆军区副司令员兼生产建设兵团司令员陶峙岳上将、新疆军区副司令员郭鹏中将。苏联驻乌鲁木齐总领事达巴申和在乌鲁木齐的苏联专家也出席了大会。
    武汉、沈阳、旅大、长春、哈尔滨等地各界代表和当地驻军，于23日分别祭扫当地的苏军烈士墓。
