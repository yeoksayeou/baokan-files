Character Introduction

　　Biryuzov
    Born in 1904, he was an outstanding Soviet military activist, Marshal of the Soviet Union, and an alternate member of the Central Committee of the CPSU. He joined the Red Army in 1922. During the Great Patriotic War, he participated in the command of the Battle of Stalingrad and later served as the commander of the Southern Front. In 1945, the troops he led annihilated the German fascist aggressors on Yugoslav territory. Biryuzov was awarded many orders and medals. After World War II, he was appointed as the commander of the military district.

### 比留佐夫

1958-02-03
第6版()
专栏：人物介绍

　　比留佐夫
    生于1904年，是苏联卓越的军事活动家，苏联元帅，苏共中央候补委员。他在1922年加入红军。在伟大卫国战争时期，他参加了斯大林格勒战役的指挥工作，后任南方战线司令员。1945年，他率领的部队歼灭了在南斯拉夫土地上的德国法西斯侵略军。比留佐夫荣获了许多勋章和奖章。在第二次世界大战后，他被任命为军区司令员。
