Title: Indonesia Willing to Rent Soviet Ships
Author:
Date: 1958-02-05
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 4th. According to Antara News Agency, sources from the Indonesian Maritime Department say that the Indonesian government has accepted in principle the Soviet proposal to lease 35,000-ton ships to Indonesia. The Soviet Union has expressed its willingness to lease ten ships to Indonesia, including some freighters and passenger ships suitable for inter-island shipping in Indonesia. According to sources at the Soviet Embassy in Indonesia, the aforementioned ships can be leased or sold to Indonesia.

　　According to sources from the Maritime Department, technical issues related to the implementation of the above proposal will be discussed in the upcoming talks between the Soviet Union and Indonesia.

### 印度尼西亚愿租苏联船

1958-02-05
第5版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚愿租苏联船
    新华社雅加达4日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，据印度尼西亚海运部人士说，印度尼西亚政府已经在原则上接受苏联提出的把三万五千吨船只出租给印度尼西亚的建议。苏联表示愿意把十艘船只出租给印度尼西亚，其中包括一些适用于印度尼西亚岛际航运的货轮和客轮。据苏联驻印度尼西亚大使馆人士说，上述船只可以出租或者出售给印度尼西亚。
    据海运部人士说，在即将举行的苏联和印度尼西亚的会谈中将要讨论有关实现上述建议的技术问题。
