Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Kinkazu Saionji and Wife
Author:
Date: 1958-02-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 31st: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet on the evening of January 31st for Mr. Kinkazu Saionji, a renowned Japanese peace figure, and his wife. Mr. Saionji recently arrived from Japan to take over the position of Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, previously held by Togo Kameda.

Present as guests were Chen Shutong, Wang Zhen, Li Dequan, Chu Tunan, Liu Ningyi, Li Yimang, Liao Chengzhi, Xu Guangping, Dong Xin, Xia Yan, Wu Han, Mei Lanfang, Ouyang Yuqian, Zhao Puchu, Xie Nanguang, Fang Shishan, and leaders of various people's organizations.

### 郭沫若宴请西园寺公一夫妇

1958-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若宴请西园寺公一夫妇
    新华社31日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若于1月31日晚，宴请日前从日本来华接替龟田东伍担任的亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长职务的日本著名和平人士西园寺公一先生及夫人。
    出席作陪的有陈叔通、王震、李德全、楚图南、刘宁一、李一氓、廖承志、许广平、董昕、夏衍、吴晗、梅兰芳、欧阳予倩、赵朴初、谢南光、方石珊等及各人民团体负责人。
