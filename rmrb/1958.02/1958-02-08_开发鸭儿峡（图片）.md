Author: Hao Changgeng

Developing Ya'erxia
　　After more than a year of drilling, Ya'erxia has been proven to be a new oilfield with industrial value. Workers at Ya'erxia Well No. 1 and the oil selection station of the Yumen Mining Bureau are preparing for a trial extraction of crude oil. Xinhua News Agency reporter Hao Changgeng photo

### 开发鸭儿峡（图片）
郝常耕

专栏：

　　开发鸭儿峡
    鸭儿峡经过一年多的钻探，已经证明是一个具有工业价值的新油田。玉门矿务局在鸭儿峡一号井和选油站工作的工人，正在准备试采原油。　新华社记者　郝常耕摄
