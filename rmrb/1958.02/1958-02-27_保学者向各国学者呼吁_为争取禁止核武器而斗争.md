Title: Bulgarian Scholars Appeal to Scholars of All Countries to Fight for the Prohibition of Nuclear Weapons
Author:
Date: 1958-02-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 26th: The academic committee members of Bulgarian universities held a meeting at the National Sofia University on the 25th and unanimously adopted an appeal to the academic committees of all universities in the world. The appeal called on scholars from universities around the world to fight for the prohibition of testing, use, and production of atomic and hydrogen weapons, for the use of atomic energy for the benefit of mankind, and for the establishment of peaceful relations and the consolidation of cooperation among the people of all countries.

The meeting also adopted a letter to the Secretary-General of the United Nations, calling on the United Nations to promote this struggle.

### 保学者向各国学者呼吁  为争取禁止核武器而斗争

1958-02-27
第6版()
专栏：

    保学者向各国学者呼吁
    为争取禁止核武器而斗争
    新华社索非亚26日电　保加利亚各高等学校学术委员会委员25日在国立索非亚大学举行会议，一致通过了向全世界所有高等学校学术委员会发出的呼吁书。呼吁书号召世界各国高等学校的学者为禁止试验、使用、生产原子武器和氢武器、为把原子能用来给人类谋福利、为建立各国人民间的和平关系和巩固合作而进行斗争。
    会议还通过了致联合国秘书长的信，呼吁联合国组织促进这一斗争。
