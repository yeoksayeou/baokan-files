Title: U.S. Troops Pour Into West Irian
Author:
Date: 1958-02-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 25th. TASS, Delhi, 24th: According to the "Delhi Times" correspondent in Bangkok, citing British military sources, U.S. troops have been arriving in West Irian since last December. In December alone, about sixty U.S. fighter jets arrived from South Korea. The fighter pilots are South Koreans and Black Americans. In addition, U.S. and Dutch engineering troops are frequently deployed to Biak Island Airport.

### 美军涌入西伊里安

1958-02-26
第5版()
专栏：

    美军涌入西伊里安
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社德里24日讯：据印度“德里时报”驻曼谷特派记者引用英国军界人士的消息说，从去年12月起，就开始有美国军队到达西伊里安。单在去年12月间，就有六十架左右的美国歼灭机从南朝鲜到达这里。战斗机的飞行员都是南朝鲜人和美国的黑人。此外，还经常有美国和荷兰的工兵部队开到别克岛机场。
