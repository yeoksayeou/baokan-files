Title: First East German Envoy Arrives in Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1958-02-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade – Erich Mueckenberger, the first ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to Yugoslavia, arrived in Belgrade by plane on the afternoon of January 31st.
