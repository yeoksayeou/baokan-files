Title: Three More National Records Broken at the National Track and Field Championships
Author:
Date: 1958-02-25
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 24th. On the third day of the 1958 National Spring Track and Field Championships, three more national records were broken. The famous Jinan athlete Zheng Fengrong, in the women's triathlon (100 meters, high jump, shot put) competition held today, set a new national record of 2339 points, not only breaking Fu Xueyan's national record of 2169 points, but also exceeding the master athlete standard of 2300 points.
	Chengdu athlete Liu Xingyu, participating in the long jump event of the women's pentathlon today, broke Yang Yumin's national record of 5.54 meters set last year with a score of 5.55 meters. Liu Xingyu will also participate in the women's individual long jump competition on the 25th.
	In the men's discus final, the tall Jinan athlete Sun Jiuyuan threw the discus beyond the national record standard line on his first attempt. His best result today was 45.28 meters, 8 centimeters further than the national record of 45.20 meters set by Li Bingcheng last year.

### 在全国田径运动会上  又有三项全国纪录被打破

1958-02-25
第7版()
专栏：

    在全国田径运动会上
    又有三项全国纪录被打破
    新华社广州24日电　1958年全国春季田径运动会第三天又有三项全国纪录被打破。著名的济南市选手郑凤荣在今天举行的女子三项运动（一百公尺、跳高、铅球）比赛中，创造了二千三百三十九分的全国新成绩，不但打破了傅雪雁保持的二千一百六十九分的全国纪录，而且还超过了运动健将二千三百分的标准。
    成都选手刘兴玉在今天参加女子五项运动中的跳远比赛时，以五公尺五十五公分的成绩打破了杨玉敏去年创造的五公尺五十四公分的全国纪录。刘兴玉还将在25日参加女子单项跳远比赛。
    在男子铁饼决赛中，身体高大的济南选手孙久远第一次就把铁饼掷出了全国纪录的标准线以外，他今天的最好成绩是四十五公尺二十八公分，比李秉诚去年创造的全国纪录（四十五公尺二十公分）远了八公分。
