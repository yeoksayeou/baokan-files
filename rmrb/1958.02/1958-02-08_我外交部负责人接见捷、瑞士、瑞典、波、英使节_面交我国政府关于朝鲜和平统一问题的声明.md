Title: Responsible Person of Our Foreign Ministry Meets with Envoys from Czechoslovakia, Switzerland, Sweden, Poland, and the UK to Present Our Government's Statement on the Peaceful Reunification of Korea

Author: 
Date: 1958-02-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Luo Guibo and Zeng Yongquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs, met with Czechoslovak Ambassador Bušíňák, Swiss Ambassador Béguin, Swedish Ambassador Book, Polish Embassy Counselor Furato, and British Chargé d'Affaires Wen Yusheng on the afternoon of the 7th, respectively, to present the Statement of the Government of the People's Republic of China on the Peaceful Reunification of Korea. They were requested to forward it to their respective governments, and the British government was requested to forward the Chinese government's statement to other countries participating in the United Nations forces in Korea.

### 我外交部负责人接见捷、瑞士、瑞典、波、英使节  面交我国政府关于朝鲜和平统一问题的声明

1958-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我外交部负责人接见捷、瑞士、瑞典、波、英使节
    面交我国政府关于朝鲜和平统一问题的声明
    新华社7日讯　7日下午，外交部副部长罗贵波和曾涌泉，分别接见了捷克斯洛伐克大使布希尼亚克、瑞士大使贝努义、瑞典大使布克、波兰大使馆参赞傅拉托和英国代办文郁生，面交中华人民共和国政府关于朝鲜和平统一问题的声明，请他们转达本国政府，并请英国政府将中国政府的这项声明转交在朝鲜参加联合国军的其他各国政府。
