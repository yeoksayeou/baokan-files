Title: Victorious Completion of Antarctic Continental Exploration Work: Soviet Second Expedition Returns Fully Loaded
Author:
Date: 1958-02-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 27th: After fourteen months of exploration in Antarctica, the Soviet Antarctic Second Expedition victoriously returned to Moscow on February 24th. During this expedition, scientists established three scientific stations deep in the polar region and completed a significant amount of scientific work.

    The expedition, led by Soviet Hero of Socialist Labor, Treshnikov, immediately began meticulous preparations for the advance into the South Geomagnetic Pole region after taking over from the first expedition led by Somov.

    On October 8th of last year, Treshnikov personally led a team of twenty-seven people on an expedition into the Antarctic interior that lasted over two months. In early November, they established the "Komsomolskaya" station at a location 858 kilometers from Mirny, at 74 degrees 5 minutes South latitude and 3,540 meters above sea level. Later, some of the team went to a location 1,000 kilometers from Mirny to establish an intermediate fuel base. Some conducted winter explorations at the temporary "Vostok 1" station. The sled-tractor train reached the South Geomagnetic Pole region in December and established the "Vostok" scientific station at 78 degrees 27 minutes South latitude and 106 degrees 52 minutes East longitude, 1,410 kilometers from Mirny and 3,500 meters above sea level.

    In mid-February, the expedition members also joined the Third Expedition to reach the relatively inaccessible polar region and established the "Sovetskaya" station 1,420 kilometers from Mirny at an altitude of 3,700 meters.

    During this period, the diesel-electric ship "Ob" also carried out extensive exploration work in the area. Scientific staff on the ship explored a section of the Antarctic coastline that had been almost entirely unstudied. In Wilkes Land, the "Ob" explored an area of 20,000 square kilometers.

    The Soviet expedition collected a great deal of valuable data during this exploration. Scientists believe that, based on the data collected, there is reason to suspect that Antarctica is not a single continent, but a large archipelago. Their exploration showed that the actual location of the Wilkes Land coastline is dozens of miles south of its position on current maps. The area named Discovery Land by the British expedition in 1936 is merely a submerged shoal. Geologists collected rock samples from previously unexamined coastal areas protruding from the Antarctic landmass. Traces of carbonized wood from plants that grew in Antarctica 100 million years ago were discovered in sandstone at Cape Horn Bluff. Geological surveys also indicate that Antarctica may have many kinds of useful minerals. Currently, the Soviet Antarctic Third Expedition, led by Tolstikov, has arrived in Antarctica to continue the exploration work.

### 胜利完成对南极大陆的考察工作  苏联第二考察队满载而归

1958-02-28
第7版()
专栏：

    胜利完成对南极大陆的考察工作
    苏联第二考察队满载而归
    据新华社莫斯科27日电　苏联南极大陆第二考察队在南极进行了十四个月的考察后，已经在2月24日胜利回到莫斯科。在这次考察中，科学家们深入极地建立了三个科学站，完成了大量的科学工作。
    这次由苏联社会主义劳动英雄特列什尼科夫率领的考察队，在接替了索莫夫领导的第一考察队工作后，立即开始周密地准备向南地磁极区的进军。
    去年10月8日，特列什尼科夫亲自率领一支由二十七人组成的队伍进行了一次历时两个多月的南极腹地进军。11月上旬，他们在距和平村八百五十八公里的地方，南纬七十四度五分拔海三千五百四十公尺的地方，建立了“共青团”站。以后，一部分人去到距离和平村一千公里的地方建立了一个中途燃料基地。一部分人在临时性的“东方一号”站进行了冬季考察。而雪橇曳引机队则在12月间直抵南地磁极区，在南纬七十八度二十七分和东经一百零六度五十二分的地方建立了“东方”科学站，这里距和平村一千四百一十公里，海拔三千五百公尺。
    2月中旬，考察队人员还会同第三考察队，到达了相当难以到达的极地，在离和平村一千四百二十公里、海拔三千七百公尺的地方建立了“苏维埃”站。
    “鄂毕号”柴油电动船这一时期也在这里展开了广泛的考察工作。这艘船上的科学工作人员考察了南极洲上一段几乎未被考察过的海岸线。在威尔克斯地，“鄂毕号”考察了面积达两万平方公里的一片地方。
    苏联考察队在这次考察中收集了许多珍贵资料。科学家们认为，根据已收集到的资料，有理由设想，南极不是一整片大陆，而是一大片群岛。他们的考察表明，威尔克斯地海岸线的实际位置在目前地图所标出位置以南数十英里的地方。1936年被英国考察队命名为迪斯卡维利地的地区只不过是一片水下浅滩。地质学家们收集了从未考察过的海岸地区突出南极地面的岩石标本。在霍尔恩·勃拉弗角发现了在砂岩中保存的、一亿年前在南极生长的植物的炭化木质的痕迹。地理考察还表明，南极可能有许多种有用矿藏。目前，以托尔斯季科夫为首的苏联南极大陆第三考察队已经到达南极大陆，继续进行考察工作。
