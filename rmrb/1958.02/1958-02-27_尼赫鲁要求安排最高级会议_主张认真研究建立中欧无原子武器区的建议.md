Title: Nehru Calls for Top-Level Meeting, Advocates Serious Study of Proposal to Establish Nuclear-Weapon-Free Zone in Central Europe
Author:
Date: 1958-02-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th, Vienna Dispatch: Indian Prime Minister Nehru, in a conversation with the publisher of the Austrian "Die Presse," Molden, on the 25th, advocated holding a top-level meeting between East and West. He said that peaceful coexistence must be realized among the people of all countries. He requested that arrangements for such a meeting be made seriously now.

Nehru also advocated seriously studying the proposal by Polish Foreign Minister Rapacki to establish a nuclear-weapon-free zone in Central Europe. He added that this proposal "is only one of the possible ways to solve the problem of European security."

He said that India is willing to help promote a top-level meeting between East and West. He also said that Austria, as a neutral country, "can make a significant contribution to the cause of maintaining peace."

### 尼赫鲁要求安排最高级会议  主张认真研究建立中欧无原子武器区的建议

1958-02-27
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁要求安排最高级会议
    主张认真研究建立中欧无原子武器区的建议
    新华社26日讯　维也纳消息：印度总理尼赫鲁25日在对奥地利“新闻报”发行人莫耳登发表谈话时主张举行东西方最高级会议。他说，各国人民之间必须实现和平共处。他要求现在就认真安排这样的会议。
    尼赫鲁还主张认真研究波兰外交部长腊帕茨基关于建立中欧无原子武器区的建议。他又说，这个建议“只是解决欧洲安全问题的可能的办法之一”。
    他说，印度愿意帮助促使东西方举行最高级会议。他还说，奥地利作为一个中立国家“可以对维护和平的事业作出重大的贡献”。
