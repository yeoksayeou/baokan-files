Title: Burhan Hosts Tea Party for Yamin
Author:
Date: 1958-02-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th dispatch: Burhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, and his wife hosted a tea party in Beijing on the evening of the 8th to entertain Indonesian Member of Parliament and former Minister of Education and Culture, Muhammad Yamin, his wife, and accompanying Indonesian friends.

Among those present at the tea party were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Hu Yuzhi and Chen Hansheng, Vice Presidents; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Da Pongsheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association; and Li Chen, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Suwardi, Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy in China, and embassy diplomats were also invited to attend tonight's tea party.

After the tea party, recreational programs were held and a film was shown.

### 包尔汉举行茶会招待耶明

1958-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　包尔汉举行茶会招待耶明
    据新华社8日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉和夫人8日晚在北京举行茶会，招待印度尼西亚国会议员，前文教部长穆罕默德·耶明和他的夫人及同行的印度尼西亚友人。
    出席茶会的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长胡愈之、陈翰笙，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，外交部亚洲司副司长李晨等。
    印度尼西亚驻中国大使馆参赞苏瓦多和使馆外交官员也应邀参加了今晚的茶会。
    茶会以后、举行了余兴节目和放映了电影。
