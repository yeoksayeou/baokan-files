Title: President Ho Chi Minh Arrives in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1958-02-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 14th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, having concluded his visit to India, arrived in Rangoon, the capital of Burma, by plane at 12:30 pm on February 14th for an official visit to Burma at the invitation of the Government of the Union of Burma.

President Ho Chi Minh received a warm welcome upon disembarking from the plane. Over a thousand students, youths, and women shouted "Long Live President Ho Chi Minh!" "Long Live Burmese-Vietnamese Friendship!"

President Ho Chi Minh warmly embraced Burmese President U Win Maung and Prime Minister U Nu, who were at the airport to welcome him. A grand welcoming ceremony was held at the airport. President U Win Maung and President Ho Chi Minh exchanged speeches.

### 胡志明主席到达仰光

1958-02-15
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席到达仰光
    新华社仰光14日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明结束了在印度的访问，于14日十二时三十分从印度乘飞机到达缅甸首都仰光，应缅甸联邦政府的邀请对缅甸进行正式的访问。
    胡志明主席在下飞机时受到了热烈的欢迎，一千多名学生、青年和妇女高呼“胡志明主席万岁！”“缅甸—越南友谊万岁！”
    胡志明主席下飞机后同在机场上欢迎的缅甸总统吴温貌和总理吴努热烈拥抱。机场上举行了隆重的欢迎仪式。吴温貌总统和胡志明主席相互致了词。
