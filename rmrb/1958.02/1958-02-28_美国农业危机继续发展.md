Title: U.S. Agricultural Crisis Continues to Develop
Author:
Date: 1958-02-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, February 27th: A report released by the U.S. Department of Agriculture on the 26th indicates that the chronic agricultural crisis in the United States continues to develop.

The Department of Agriculture's report stated that in 1957, the net income of American farmers (cash income after deducting production costs) decreased by another 4% compared to 1956, "far exceeding the previously estimated decline." The report said that the number of farms in that year was estimated to be 4.85 million, a decrease of 2.3% compared to 1956. The Department of Agriculture also said that it is estimated that the number of farms will further decrease to 4.75 million this year.

The report said that the agricultural population is also decreasing. The agricultural population this year was approximately 20.39 million, a decrease of 2 million compared to the previous year.

### 美国农业危机继续发展

1958-02-28
第6版()
专栏：

    美国农业危机继续发展
    新华社27日讯　华盛顿消息：美国农业部26日发表的一项报告表明，美国的慢性农业危机在继续发展。
    农业部的报告说，1957年美国农民的净收入（除去生产费用以后的现金收入）又比1956年降低了4％，“远远超过预先估计的下降程度”。报告说，这一年的农场数目估计为四百八十五万个，也比1956年减少了2.3％。农业部还说，估计在今年，农场数目将要进一步减到四百七十五万个。
    报告说，农业人口也在减少。这一年的农业人口大约是二千零三十九万人，比一年前减少了二百万人。
