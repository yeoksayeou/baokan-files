Title: Sukarno Warns Rebel Groups; Jakarta Newspapers Express Support
Author:
Date: 1958-02-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 22nd. Several Jakarta newspapers today expressed support for President Sukarno's firm stance against the Hussein rebel group in his speech on the 21st.

    "Eastern Star" stated that President Sukarno strengthened the position of the current cabinet. The unified attitude adopted by the President, Parliament, and the government confronts the rebel group with a solid national unity.

    "Indonesian Torch" said that President Sukarno's speech on the 21st was clearly an order to the current cabinet, requiring it to take swift action.

    Jakarta's "People's Daily" said that February 21st was the first anniversary of the announcement of President Sukarno's plan. At such a time, the President's call to fight the rebel group with all available strength is a powerful warning. The newspaper pointed out that the rebel group is destined to fail.

### 苏加诺对叛乱集团的警告  雅加达报纸表示支持

1958-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺对叛乱集团的警告
    雅加达报纸表示支持
    新华社雅加达22日电　今天雅加达的一些报纸表示支持苏加诺总统在21日发表的演说中对侯赛尼叛乱集团所采取的坚决态度。
    “东星报”说，苏加诺总统加强了现内阁的地位。总统、国会和政府所采取的一致态度，使叛乱集团面对着牢固的民族团结。
    “印度尼西亚火炬报”说，苏加诺总统在21日发表的演说显然是给现内阁的一项命令，要求它迅速采取行动。
    雅加达“人民日报”说，2月21日是苏加诺总统方案公布的一周年。在这样的时候，总统号召用目前所有的全部力量来同叛乱集团进行斗争，这是一个有力的警告。报纸指出，叛乱集团是注定要失败的。
