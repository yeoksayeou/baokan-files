Title: Demanding the Withdrawal of All Foreign Troops from Korea; World Federation of Trade Unions Supports the Korean People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1958-02-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 27th – According to a report today from the correspondent of the Korean Central News Agency in Prague, the World Federation of Trade Unions has expressed its full support for the struggle of the Korean working people against the United States' introduction of atomic weapons into South Korea, demanding the withdrawal of all foreign troops from Korea, and striving for the peaceful reunification of the fatherland.

In a letter to the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, the World Federation of Trade Unions stated that it is closely watching the fact that the Korean armistice is being violated, atomic weapons are being transported into South Korea, and South Korea is being transformed into a base for atomic warfare in the Far East. The World Federation of Trade Unions believes that the aforementioned provocative actions will seriously threaten peace in the Far East and the world.

In its letter, the World Federation of Trade Unions expressed the deep sympathy of this world organization of the working class for the South Korean working people who are suffering under foreign military occupation and dictatorial rule.

The letter stated that the World Federation of Trade Unions is deeply convinced that the solidarity of the Korean working class will be a guarantee for the Korean people to achieve victory in their struggle for the peaceful reunification of the fatherland. The Korean working class will surely achieve a brilliant victory in this struggle, which will contribute to the consolidation of world peace.

### 要求从朝鲜撤退一切外国军队  世界工联支持朝鲜人民的斗争

1958-02-28
第7版()
专栏：

    要求从朝鲜撤退一切外国军队
    世界工联支持朝鲜人民的斗争
    新华社平壤27日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社驻布拉格记者今天报道，世界工会联合会表示完全支持朝鲜劳动人民为反对美国把原子武器运进南朝鲜、要求从朝鲜撤出一切外国军队和争取和平统一祖国的斗争。
    世界工会联合会在它写给朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会的信里说，世界工会联合会密切注视着朝鲜停战正在遭到破坏、原子武器正在被运进南朝鲜，以及南朝鲜正在变成远东原子战争基地的事实。世界工会联合会认为，上述挑衅性行动将严重地威胁远东和世界的和平。
    世界工会联合会在它的信里，表示了这个世界工人阶级的组织对处于外国军队占领和独裁统治下而深受苦难的南朝鲜劳动人民的深切同情。
    信里说，世界工会联合会深信，朝鲜工人阶级的团结一致将是朝鲜人民在为争取和平统一祖国的斗争中取得胜利的保证。朝鲜工人阶级在这一有助于巩固世界和平的斗争中必将取得辉煌的胜利。
