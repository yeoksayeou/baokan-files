Title: Chen Jingkai, Huang Qianghui, and Others Go to the Soviet Union to Participate in International Weightlifting Competition
Author:
Date: 1958-02-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, February 24th: The Chinese weightlifting team, consisting of four members, flew to Moscow on the morning of the 24th to participate in the 1958 Moscow Prize International Weightlifting Individual Competition to be held in Moscow on March 8th.

The countries participating in this international weightlifting individual competition include China, the Soviet Union, Poland, and the United States, among others.

The Chinese athletes participating in the competition are Chen Jingkai, the world record creator in the flyweight class, the famous lightweight athlete Huang Qianghui, and the light heavyweight athlete Zhao Qingkui. The leader of the Chinese weightlifting team is Qu Zhiquan.

### 陈镜开　黄强辉等  赴苏参加国际举重比赛

1958-02-25
第7版()
专栏：

    陈镜开　黄强辉等
    赴苏参加国际举重比赛
    据新华社24日讯　中国举重队一行四人24日上午乘飞机去莫斯科，参加3月8日在莫斯科举行的1958年莫斯科奖国际举重个人比赛。
    参加这次国际举重个人比赛的国家有中国、苏联、波兰、美国等。
    中国参加比赛的运动员有最轻量级世界纪录创造者陈镜开，著名轻量级选手黄强辉和轻重量级选手赵庆奎。中国举重队的领队是曲治全。
