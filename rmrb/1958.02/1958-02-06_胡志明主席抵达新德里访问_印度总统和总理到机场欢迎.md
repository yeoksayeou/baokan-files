Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Arrives in New Delhi for Visit; Indian President and Prime Minister Welcome Him at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1958-02-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 5th:  President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and his entourage arrived in New Delhi, the capital of India, by special plane from Calcutta at noon today, at the invitation of the President and Government of India. He will pay a ten-day visit to India.

Four jet fighters of the Indian Air Force escorted President Ho Chi Minh's special plane from sixty miles outside New Delhi.

When President Ho Chi Minh, dressed in a beige suit, stepped out of the plane with a smile on his face, a 21-gun salute was fired in his honor. As soon as President Ho Chi Minh got off the plane, he warmly embraced Indian President Prasad and Prime Minister Nehru. Both President Prasad and Prime Minister Nehru expressed their warm welcome to him.

Also present at the airport to welcome him were Indian cabinet ministers and other senior officials, Nehru's daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, members of parliament, prominent figures from all walks of life, and foreign diplomatic envoys to India, including Chinese Ambassador to India, Pan Zili.

Personnel from the Consulate General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in New Delhi were at the airport to welcome him.

The band played the national anthems of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and India. Subsequently, President Ho Chi Minh reviewed the guard of honor composed of the Indian Army, Navy, and Air Force.

At the airport, many citizens of Delhi who came to welcome him cheered loudly and held up slogans such as "Long Live Panchsheel," "Long Live the Friendship between India and Vietnam," and the like. A group of children surrounded him and put many garlands on him.

Prime Minister Nehru delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of President Prasad, who was unwell.

Prime Minister Nehru said that President Ho Chi Minh "is one of the greatest revolutionaries in the world." He praised President Ho Chi Minh's character of remaining calm in the face of serious difficulties. Nehru hoped that the Indian people would learn from his glorious life.

Nehru then read out President Prasad's welcoming address, which said: "We all welcome you, the great warrior for freedom, the leader of the people, and the head of state of our friendly country, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. India has always been concerned about the liberation of all countries that are ruled by foreign powers against the will of the people."

In his reply, President Ho Chi Minh said, "We come to your great country with this deep sympathy that links our two peoples together." He said, "India is an independent country today, and a great power, which has made many valuable contributions to the cause of peace in Asia and the world, and is playing an important role on the world stage."

President Ho Chi Minh said that the Geneva Accords made it possible to stop the war of aggression and recognized the sovereignty and independence of Vietnam. The Vietnamese people are currently working hard to consolidate peace, build their country, and reunify their motherland.

President Ho Chi Minh praised India for its contribution to restoring peace in Vietnam, especially as Chairman of the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam. He said that India had made many efforts in supervising and monitoring the implementation of the Geneva Accords in Vietnam. The Vietnamese people thank the Indian government and people.

President Ho Chi Minh said that the friendly relations between India and Vietnam are constantly developing, and that his visit will further strengthen the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries and will help to strengthen the solidarity among Asian and African countries and consolidate world peace.

Finally, President Ho Chi Minh shouted in Hindi: "Long live the lasting friendship between the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Republic of India!" "Long live peace in Asia and the world!" "Long live Panchsheel!" He ended his speech to applause.

Prime Minister Nehru and President Ho Chi Minh rode together in an open-top car to the Presidential Palace. The nine-mile road they passed was beautifully decorated with flags, and many people and colorfully dressed Indian cavalry welcomed the distinguished guests near the Presidential Palace.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 5th:  President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, accompanied by some of his entourage, paid a visit to Indian President Prasad at 5 p.m. today and had a cordial conversation with him.

Prior to this, President Ho Chi Minh and some of his entourage went to Rajghat Gandhi Cremation Ground to lay a wreath brought from Hanoi. President Ho Chi Minh also planted a sapling brought from Hanoi there.

### 胡志明主席抵达新德里访问  印度总统和总理到机场欢迎

1958-02-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席抵达新德里访问
    印度总统和总理到机场欢迎
    据新华社新德里5日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他的随行人员应印度总统和政府的邀请，今天中午从加尔各答乘专机到达印度首都新德里。他将在印度作为期十天的访问。
    印度空军的四架喷气式飞机从离新德里六十英里的地方护送胡志明主席的专机到达。
    当身穿奶黄色服装的胡志明主席满面笑容地步出飞机时，礼炮连鸣二十一响向他致敬。胡志明主席一下飞机，就同印度总统普拉沙德和总理尼赫鲁热情地拥抱。普拉沙德总统和尼赫鲁总理都向他表示热烈欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有印度内阁部长们和其他高级官员，尼赫鲁的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人，议员们，各界著名人士，以及包括中国驻印度大使潘自力在内的各国驻印度外交使节。
    越南民主共和国驻新德里总领事馆人员到机场欢迎。
    乐队奏起越南民主共和国和印度的国歌。随后，胡志明主席检阅了由印度陆、海、空军组成的仪仗队。
    在飞机场上，来欢迎他的众多德里市民向他高声欢呼，并且举着“潘查希拉万岁”，“印度和越南的友谊万岁”等标语。一群儿童围着他，给他戴上许多花环。
    尼赫鲁总理代表身体不适的普拉沙德总统致词表示欢迎。
    尼赫鲁总理说，胡志明主席“是世界上伟大的革命家之一”。他赞扬胡志明主席在严重的困难面前仍然保持冷静的品格。尼赫鲁希望印度人民学习他的光辉的一生。
    尼赫鲁接着宣读普拉沙德总统的欢迎词说：“我们都欢迎你这位争取自由的伟大战士、人民领袖和我们的友好国家——越南民主共和国的国家元首。印度对一切被外国违反人民的意志而加以统治的国家的解放一直是关心的。”
    胡志明主席在致答词时说，“我们是怀着使我们两个民族联系起来的这种深切同情来到你们伟大的国家的。”他说，“印度在今天是独立的国家，并且是一个大国，它有着对亚洲和世界的和平事业作出了很多有价值的贡献的功绩，它在世界舞台上正在起着重要的作用。”
    胡志明主席说，日内瓦协议使侵略战争得以停止，并且承认了越南的主权和独立。越南人民目前正在进行努力来巩固和平，建设他们的国家，并且重新统一他们的祖国。
    胡志明主席赞扬印度对于恢复越南和平、特别作为越南国际监督和监察委员会主席所作出的贡献。他说，印度在监督和监察日内瓦协议在越南的执行状况这件工作上作了许多努力。越南人民感谢印度政府和人民。
    胡志明主席说，印度和越南之间的友好关系正在不断发展，他这次访问将进一步加强两国间的友好和合作关系，并且将有助于加强亚非国家间的团结和巩固世界和平。
    胡志明最后用印地语高呼：“越南民主共和国和印度共和国之间的持久友谊万岁！”“亚洲和全世界和平万岁！”“潘查希拉万岁！”他在掌声中结束了讲话。
    尼赫鲁总理同胡志明主席一起乘敞篷汽车到总统府。他们经过的九英里长的道路上都悬挂着旗帜，布置得很美丽，许许多多人以及服装多彩的印度骑兵在总统府附近，夹道欢迎贵宾。
    新华社新德里5日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明偕同部分随行人员今天下午五时拜访了印度总统普拉沙德，并且同他进行了亲切的交谈。
    在这以前，胡志明主席和部分随行人员前往拉志加特甘地火葬场献了从河内带来的花圈。胡志明主席还在那里种了从河内带来的一棵树苗。
