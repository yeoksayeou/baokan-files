Title: Renowned Japanese Writer Tokunaga Sunao Passes Away; Chinese Writers Association Chairman Mao Dun Sends Condolence Telegram
Author:
Date: 1958-02-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th: Renowned Japanese writer Tokunaga Sunao passed away on the 15th at his residence in Setagaya, Tokyo, due to stomach cancer. He was 58 years old.

Tokunaga Sunao was a member of the Japanese Communist Party, born in 1899 in a poor peasant family in Kumamoto Prefecture, Kyushu, Japan. He worked as a printing factory apprentice, a grocery store clerk, and a cart driver in his childhood. Later, he participated in the labor movement in Tokyo. His famous work, "Street Without Sun," was adapted into a film and translated into Chinese, Russian, and several European languages. His other famous work, "Quiet Mountains," also has a Chinese translation. He visited China in 1955.

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Mao Dun, Chairman of the Council of the Chinese Writers Association, sent a telegram to the New Japan Literature Association on the 16th, expressing condolences on the passing of the renowned Japanese proletarian writer, Tokunaga Sunao.

The telegram said: "We are deeply saddened to hear of the passing of Mr. Tokunaga Sunao. Tokunaga Sunao was an outstanding writer of the Japanese proletariat who made significant contributions to your country's literary cause. He was also a close friend of the Chinese people. Even before his death, he was still concerned about the progress of Sino-Japanese friendship and cultural exchange. On behalf of Chinese writers and readers, I extend my deepest condolences and sympathies to you and the family of Tokunaga Sunao."

### 日本著名作家德永直逝世  中国作协主席茅盾致电吊唁

1958-02-17
第5版()
专栏：

    日本著名作家德永直逝世
    中国作协主席茅盾致电吊唁
    新华社16日讯　东京消息：日本著名作家德永直因患胃癌不治，15日在东京都世田谷的寓所逝世，享年五十八岁。
    德永直是日本共产党党员，1899年生于日本九州熊本县的一个贫农家庭里，小时候曾经当过印刷厂的学徒、粮食店的伙计和马车夫，后来在东京参加了工人运动。他的名著“没有太阳的街”曾经被改编为电影并且被翻译成中文、俄文和好几种欧洲文字。他的另一本名著“静静的群山”也有中文译本，他在1955年曾经来中国访问。
    新华社16日讯　中国作家协会理事会主席茅盾16日致电新日本文学会，吊唁日本著名无产阶级作家德永直的逝世。
    电文说：“惊悉德永直先生逝世，我们感到十分悲痛。德永直是日本无产阶级杰出的作家，曾为贵国文学事业作出重大的贡献；也是中国人民亲密的朋友，逝世前他仍在关心中日友好和文化交流的进展。我谨代表中国作家及读者们向你们及德永直家属表示深切的哀悼及慰问。”
