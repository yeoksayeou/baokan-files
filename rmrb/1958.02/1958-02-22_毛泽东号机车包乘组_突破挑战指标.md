Title: Mao Zedong Locomotive Crew Exceeds Challenge Targets
Author:
Date: 1958-02-22
Page: 3

Mao Zedong Locomotive Crew
    Exceeds Challenge Targets
    The "Mao Zedong" locomotive crew exceeded their goal in the challenge to locomotive crews nationwide during the Spring Festival—towing 1.8 million ton-kilometers per day.

    On the 19th, in a 24-hour period, their three shifts took turns running five one-way trips between Fengtai and Tianjin, towing a total cargo volume of 1,823,481 ton-kilometers. On this day, they also exceeded the axle load by 2,264 tons and saved approximately three tons of coal.
              (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 毛泽东号机车包乘组  突破挑战指标

1958-02-22
第3版()
专栏：

    毛泽东号机车包乘组
    突破挑战指标
    “毛泽东号”机车包乘组，在春节期间超过了向全国机车包乘组挑战的奋斗目标——日牵引一百八十万吨公里。
    在19日一昼夜内，他们三班轮流共在丰台、天津之间往返跑了五个单程，牵引货物总量达到一百八十二万三千四百八十一吨公里。这一天，他们还超轴二千二百六十四吨，省煤约三吨。
              （据新华社讯）
