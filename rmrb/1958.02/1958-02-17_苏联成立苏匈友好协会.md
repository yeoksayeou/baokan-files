Title: Soviet Union Establishes Soviet-Hungarian Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1958-02-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th: The Soviet-Hungarian Friendship Association held its inaugural meeting in Moscow on the evening of the 14th. Representatives from all walks of life from the Russian Federation, Belarus, Estonia, and other republics attended the inaugural meeting.

The meeting elected a council of the Soviet-Hungarian Friendship Association consisting of sixty-five people. At the first meeting of the council, Fedoseyev, Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and Director of the Institute of Philosophy, was elected Chairman of the Council.

### 苏联成立苏匈友好协会

1958-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联成立苏匈友好协会
    新华社16日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏（苏联）匈（匈牙利）友好协会14日晚间在莫斯科举行了成立大会。俄罗斯联邦、白俄罗斯、爱沙尼亚和其他共和国的各界代表参加了成立大会。
    会上选出了由六十五人组成的苏匈友好协会理事会。在理事会的第一次会议上，选举了苏联科学院通讯院士、哲学研究所所长费多谢耶夫为理事会主席。
