Title: Anecdotes from the Great Leap Forward
Author: Zhang Yongmei
Date: 1958-02-18
Page: 8

    Anecdotes from the Great Leap Forward
    40 Million Tons
    There is a glorious number
    That has taken root in our hearts
    We will within fifteen years
    Reach an annual steel production of 40 million tons
    Great! 40 million tons
    Even if I forget
    The birthdate of my loved one
    —I can ask again
    We cannot forget
    40 million tons
    We steelworkers
    Are the mothers of steel
    We nurture with our labor
    A strong child
    —40 million tons
    Woman
    Before her is a lamp
    In her hand is a needle
    Scissors snip, snip
    The night is late
    An old coat is being cut up
    A refurbished jacket is being resewn
    Her scissors are truly sharp
    Cutting away wasteful bad thoughts
    Her needle and thread are truly clever
    Sewing together
    The good family tradition of diligence and thrift
    Zhang Yongmei
