Title: Heroic Drivers Across Tianshan

Author: 
Date: 1958-02-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency report:

	By the Spring Festival, Su Changfu, an advanced automobile driver of the Xinjiang Military Region Production and Construction Corps transportation unit, had driven for six years and eight months without a single accident on the vast Gobi Desert and snow-covered routes north and south of the Tianshan Mountains. This created a national record of 380,000 kilometers of safe driving and no major repairs.
	Currently, Su Changfu is working hard to achieve a record of 500,000 kilometers of safe driving and no major repairs.

### 天山南北的英雄司机

1958-02-25
第3版()
专栏：

    天山南北的英雄司机
    到春节前夕为止，新疆军区生产建设兵团运输部队先进汽车驾驶员苏长福，在天山南北的大戈壁和冰雪线上，已经有六年零八个月没发生过一次事故，创造了三十八万公里安全行驶和无大修的全国最高纪录。
    目前，苏长福正为争取达到五十万公里安全行驶和无大修的纪录而努力。　（据新华社讯）
