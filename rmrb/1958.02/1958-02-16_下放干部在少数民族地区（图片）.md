Title: Cadres Transferred to Ethnic Minority Areas (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-02-16
Page: 4

    Cadres Transferred to Ethnic Minority Areas
    The first group of cadres transferred by the Communist Youth League of Guizhou Province to Taijiang County, Southeast Guizhou Miao and Dong Autonomous Prefecture, humbly learned from the veteran commune members from the very beginning, respected the customs of the ethnic minorities, and got along well with the commune members. Photo by Pang Yongfen (Xinhua News Agency)
    Below: Miao women have excellent embroidery skills. Transferred cadre Yang Zhao learns embroidery from Miao female commune member A Song.
    Right: Transferred cadres and Miao female commune members washing vegetables together by the river.

### 下放干部在少数民族地区（图片）

1958-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    下放干部在少数民族地区
    共青团贵州省委会第一批下放的干部来到黔东南苗族、侗族自治州台江县以后，他们一开始就虚心向老社员们学习，尊重少数民族的风俗习惯，和社员们相处得很好。庞永芬摄（新华社稿）
    下图：苗族妇女挑花技术很好，下放干部杨肇向苗族女社员阿松学习挑花。
    右图：下放干部和苗族女社员们一起在河边洗菜。
