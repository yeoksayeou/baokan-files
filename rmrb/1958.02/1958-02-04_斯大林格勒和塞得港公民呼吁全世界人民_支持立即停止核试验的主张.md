Title: Citizens of Stalingrad and Port Said Call on the People of the World to Support the Proposition to Immediately Halt Nuclear Testing
Author:
Date: 1958-02-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 3rd: According to TASS, Cairo, February 2nd: Citizens of the Soviet city of Stalingrad and the Egyptian city of Port Said jointly issued a letter to the people of all countries on February 2nd, calling on the people of the world to support "the proposition to immediately and unconditionally ban weapons of mass destruction and stop atomic bomb and hydrogen bomb testing."

The letter to the people of all countries was read out when General Safwat, the governor of Port Said and the Suez Canal region, received the delegation from the city of Stalingrad on February 2nd. The delegation arrived in Port Said on January 23rd at the invitation of the Port Said Municipal Council.

The letter to the people of all countries stated that we firmly support the efforts of the Egyptian government and the Soviet government to establish world peace.

### 斯大林格勒和塞得港公民呼吁全世界人民  支持立即停止核试验的主张

1958-02-04
第5版()
专栏：

　　斯大林格勒和塞得港公民呼吁全世界人民
    支持立即停止核试验的主张
    据新华社3日讯　据塔斯社开罗2日讯：苏联斯大林格勒市公民和埃及塞得港市公民2日联合发表告各国人民书，要求全世界人民支持“立即无条件禁止大规模毁灭性武器和停止原子弹、氢弹试验的主张”。
    这项告各国人民书是在塞得港和苏伊士运河地区行政长官萨弗阿特将军在2日接见斯大林格勒市代表团时宣读的。这个代表团应塞得港市政委员会的邀请，在1月23日到达塞得港访问。
    告各国人民书说，我们坚决支持埃及政府和苏联政府建立世界和平的努力。
