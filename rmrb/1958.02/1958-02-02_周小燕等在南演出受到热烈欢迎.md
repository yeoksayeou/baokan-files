Title: Zhou Xiaoyan and Others Warmly Welcomed in Southern Performances

Author: 
Date: 1958-02-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade -- After giving three performances in Skopje and other cities in the People's Republic of Macedonia, Yugoslavia, Chinese soprano Zhou Xiaoyan, pianist Wu Leyi, and others performed in Belgrade on the evening of January 26th. The performances by the Chinese artists were warmly welcomed by the audience.

The Chinese artists will continue to perform in major cities in Yugoslavia.

### 周小燕等在南演出受到热烈欢迎

1958-02-02
第6版()
专栏：

　　周小燕等在南演出受到热烈欢迎
    据新华社贝尔格莱德讯　中国著名歌唱家周小燕和钢琴家吴乐懿等在南斯拉夫马其顿人民共和国首府斯科普里等城市演出三场之后，1月26日晚上在贝尔格莱德作了演出。中国艺术家的表演受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    中国艺术家还将在南斯拉夫各大城市继续演出。
