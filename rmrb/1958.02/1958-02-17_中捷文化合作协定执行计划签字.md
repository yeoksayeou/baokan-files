Title: Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement Execution Plan Signed
Author:
Date: 1958-02-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 16th: The 1958 execution plan for the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Beijing on February 16th.
    Representing the Chinese side was Xia Yan, head of the People's Republic of China Cultural Delegation and Vice Minister of Culture.
    Representing the Czechoslovak side was Dr. Jaroslav Hávelka, head of the Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation and Deputy Minister of Education and Culture of Czechoslovakia.
    Also present at the signing ceremony from the Chinese side were Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Culture, and others.
    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák, also attended the signing ceremony.
    A reception was held after the signing ceremony.
    On the evening of the 15th, Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák, held a reception for the Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation's visit to China.

### 中捷文化合作协定执行计划签字

1958-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中捷文化合作协定执行计划签字
    新华社16日讯　中捷文化合作协定1958年执行计划2月16日在北京签字。
    代表中国方面签字的是中华人民共和国文化代表团团长、文化部副部长夏衍。
    代表捷克斯洛伐克方面签字的是捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克教育文化部副部长雅罗斯拉夫·哈维尔卡博士。
    中国方面出席签字仪式的还有对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，文化部副部长张致祥等。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克也出席了签字仪式。
    签字仪式以后举行了酒会。
    15日晚上，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克为捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团访问中国举行了酒会。
