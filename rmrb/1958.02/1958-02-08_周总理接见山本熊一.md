Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Kumao Yamamoto
Author:
Date: 1958-02-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Premier Zhou Enlai received Kumao Yamamoto, Chairman of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association and Chairman of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition in Guangzhou and Wuhan, on the afternoon of the 7th.

Present at the reception were Chen Yi, Ye Jizhuang, Nan Hanchen, Liao Chengzhi, and Lei Renmin.

### 周总理接见山本熊一

1958-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见山本熊一
    新华社7日讯　周恩来总理在7日下午接见了日本国际贸易促进协会会长、广州、武汉日本商品展览会会长山本熊一。
    接见时在座的，有陈毅、叶季壮、南汉宸、廖承志、雷任民。
