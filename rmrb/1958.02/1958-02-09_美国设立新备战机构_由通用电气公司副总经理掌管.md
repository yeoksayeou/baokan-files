Title: U.S. Establishes New War Preparation Agency Headed by General Electric Vice President

Author: 
Date: 1958-02-09
Page: 7

    U.S. Establishes New War Preparation Agency
    Headed by General Electric Vice President
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 8th – The U.S. Department of Defense announced on the 7th the establishment of a new agency for expanding armaments and preparing for war – the "Advanced Research Projects Agency." An Associated Press dispatch stated that this agency "will be responsible for the overall command of the development of space weapons and military satellites."
    The Department of Defense also announced that Roy Johnson, Vice President of General Electric, has been appointed as the director of the agency.

### 美国设立新备战机构  由通用电气公司副总经理掌管

1958-02-09
第7版()
专栏：

    美国设立新备战机构
    由通用电气公司副总经理掌管
    新华社8日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国防部7日宣布成立一个新的扩军备战机构——“高等研究计划局”。美联社消息说，这个局“将负责全面指挥空间武器和军用卫星的发展”。
    国防部还宣布，通用电气公司副总经理罗伊·约翰逊已经被任命为局长。
