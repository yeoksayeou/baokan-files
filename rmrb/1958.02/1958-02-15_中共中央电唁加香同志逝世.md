Title: CCP Central Committee Sends Condolences on the Death of Comrade Cachin
Author:
Date: 1958-02-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the French Communist Party on the 13th, expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Marcel Cachin, member of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party Central Committee. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
To the Central Committee of the French Communist Party:
We were shocked to learn of the unfortunate death of Comrade Marcel Cachin, which caused great grief to us Chinese Communists. Comrade Cachin was a pioneer and outstanding fighter in the international communist movement. He made tremendous contributions to the revolutionary cause of the French proletariat, and his death is a major loss to the French proletariat and the international proletariat. We would like to express our deepest condolences to the Central Committee of the French Communist Party.

                            The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                 February 13, 1958

### 中共中央电唁加香同志逝世

1958-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电唁加香同志逝世
    新华社14日讯　中国共产党中央委员会13日致电法国共产党中央委员会，吊唁法共中央政治局委员马赛尔·加香同志的逝世。唁电全文如下：法国共产党中央委员会：
    惊悉马赛尔·加香同志不幸逝世，这使我们中国共产党人感到极大的悲痛。加香同志是国际共产主义运动的先辈和卓越战士。他为法国无产阶级的革命事业作出了巨大的贡献，他的逝世是法国无产阶级和国际无产阶级的一个重大损失。我们谨向法国共产党中央委员会表示最深切的吊慰。
                            中国共产党中央委员会
                                 一九五八年二月十三日
