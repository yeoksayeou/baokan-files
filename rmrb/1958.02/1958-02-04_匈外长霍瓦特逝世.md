Title: Hungarian Foreign Minister Horváth Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1958-02-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, February 3rd: Comrade Imre Horváth, member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, passed away on the night of the 2nd after a long illness, at the age of fifty-seven.

### 匈外长霍瓦特逝世

1958-02-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　匈外长霍瓦特逝世
    新华社布达佩斯3日电　匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员、工农革命政府外交部长伊姆雷·霍瓦特同志，在长期患病以后，于2日夜逝世，享年五十七岁。
