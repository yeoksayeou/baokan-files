Title: Stop War Propaganda, Strengthen International Friendship: Czechoslovak News Agency Issues Appeal for Peace
Author:
Date: 1958-02-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 8th: The Czechoslovak News Agency appealed to all news agencies in the world on the 7th to cooperate in safeguarding peace.

The appeal from the Czechoslovak News Agency stated that all of humanity has deeply felt the horrors of the last great war. The latest military weapons, atomic bombs and hydrogen bombs mean preparing for the destruction of humanity and the destruction of civilization. As staff members of news agencies, we have no right to not let millions of readers know the threats contained in dangerous arms race activities. We have a responsibility to truthfully tell the real situation to all of humanity.

The appeal calls on the staff of all news agencies around the world to use common efforts to explain to the people of the world that people can live on this planet without a frantic arms race. The appeal said, let us support the forces of peace every day with our work and with the news we publish.

### 停止战争宣传　加强国际友谊  捷新闻机构发出和平呼吁书

1958-02-09
第6版()
专栏：

　　停止战争宣传　加强国际友谊
    捷新闻机构发出和平呼吁书
    据新华社布拉格8日电  捷克斯洛伐克通讯社7日向全世界所有的通讯社呼吁在保卫和平方面进行合作。
    捷克斯洛伐克通讯社的呼吁书说，全人类曾经严重地感受到上一次大战的恐怖。最新式的军事武器、原子弹和氢弹意味着为毁灭人类、毁灭文明作准备。作为通讯社的工作人员，我们没有权利不让千百万读者知道在危险的备战活动中所包含的威胁。我们有责任把真实情况忠实地告诉全人类。
    呼吁书要求全世界所有通讯社的工作人员用共同的努力向全世界人民解释：人们可以在这个星球上生活而不要疯狂的军备竞赛。呼吁书说，让我们每天用自己的工作、用我们发布的新闻来支持和平力量。
