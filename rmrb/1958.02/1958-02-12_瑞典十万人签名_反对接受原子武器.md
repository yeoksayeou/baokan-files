Title: 100,000 Swedes Sign Petition Against Accepting Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1958-02-12
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, 9th: According to TASS, a delegation composed of representatives from all walks of life in Sweden met with Swedish Prime Minister Erlander on the 6th, expressing their opposition to Sweden acquiring atomic weapons. The delegation handed Erlander a petition against accepting atomic weapons, which was signed by nearly 100,000 people, including 169 writers and 123 pastors.

### 瑞典十万人签名  反对接受原子武器

1958-02-12
第7版()
专栏：

    瑞典十万人签名
    反对接受原子武器
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩讯：据瑞典通讯社报道，一个由瑞典各界代表组成的代表团在6日拜会了瑞典首相埃兰德，表示反对瑞典取得原子武器。代表团交给埃兰德一份反对接受原子武器的请愿书，在这份请愿书上签名的有将近十万人，其中有一百六十九位作家和一百二十三位牧师。
