Title: Bamboo Strips Replace Steel Cables for Transporting Timber
Author: He Bingjun
Date: 1958-02-12
Page: 6

    Bamboo Strips Replace Steel Cables for Transporting Timber
    (Our report) The Hongjiang Branch of the Hunan Forestry Industry Bureau has designed a new tool using local materials: using bamboo strips instead of steel cables to transport timber in the air. The line erected in the Xiaoshi Logging Area of Huitong County is 550 meters long, replacing the actual walking distance of 1,050 meters. Its efficiency is 25 times higher than manual handling.
    The cost of erecting each kilometer of bamboo strip line is only 244 yuan, which is a great reduction compared to the cost of steel cables.
                    (He Bingjun) (with picture)

### 竹条代替钢索运木材
何秉钧
1958-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    竹条代替钢索运木材
    本报讯　湖南森林工业局洪江分局就地取材设计了一种新的工具：用竹条代替钢索在空中运木材。在会同县小市采伐林场架设的线路，全长五百五十公尺，代替了一千零五十公尺的实际人行路。它的效率比人力搬运提高二十五倍。
    架设每公里竹条线路只需要二百四十四元，比钢索成本大大降低。
                    （何秉钧）（附图片）
