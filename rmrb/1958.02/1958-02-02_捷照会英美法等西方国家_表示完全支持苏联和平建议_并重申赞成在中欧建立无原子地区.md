Title: Czechoslovakia Issues Note to Western Countries Including Britain, the U.S., and France, Expressing Full Support for Soviet Peace Proposals and Reaffirming Support for Establishing a Nuclear-Free Zone in Central Europe
Author:
Date: 1958-02-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 1st. According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency on January 31st: The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Czechoslovakia, Mr. Gregor, received the diplomatic envoys of Britain, the United States, France, Norway, Denmark, the Netherlands, Belgium, Turkey, Italy, Greece, and Canada stationed in Prague, and handed them a note. The note stated the attitude of the Czechoslovak government towards the current international situation and expressed full support for the Soviet peace proposals.

The note particularly pointed out the importance of signing a non-aggression treaty between member states of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization and member states of the Warsaw Pact, and reiterated its support for Poland's proposal to establish a nuclear-free zone in Central Europe.

The note also stated that the Czechoslovak government is prepared to participate in the suggested meeting of heads of government of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization member states, the Warsaw Treaty member states, and some other countries, and expressed its belief that the governments of the countries receiving this note will participate in this meeting and contribute to its successful convening.

Gregor subsequently also received the diplomatic envoys of Yugoslavia, Egypt, India, Sweden, Austria, and Afghanistan stationed in Prague (the Soviet government had suggested that these countries participate in the East-West summit meeting), and informed them of the contents of the Czechoslovak government's note to the North Atlantic Treaty Organization member countries.

### 捷照会英美法等西方国家  表示完全支持苏联和平建议  并重申赞成在中欧建立无原子地区

1958-02-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　捷照会英美法等西方国家
    表示完全支持苏联和平建议
    并重申赞成在中欧建立无原子地区
    新华社布拉格1日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社1月31日报道：捷克斯洛伐克外交部第一副部长格里哥尔接见了英国、美国、法国、挪威、丹麦、荷兰、比利时、土耳其、意大利、希腊和加拿大驻布拉格的外交使节，并且交给他们一份照会。照会表明了捷克斯洛伐克政府对目前国际局势的态度，并且表示完全支持苏联的和平建议。
    照会特别指出了在北大西洋联盟成员国和华沙条约缔约国之间签订一项互不侵犯条约的重要性，并且再次表示支持波兰提出的在中欧建立无原子地区的建议。
    照会还说，捷克斯洛伐克政府准备参加建议举行的北大西洋公约组织成员国、华沙条约缔约国和一些其他国家的政府首脑会议，并且表示相信接到它的这个照会的这些国家的政府将会参加这一会议，为它的顺利召开作出贡献。
    格里哥尔随后还接见了南斯拉夫、埃及、印度、瑞典、奥地利和阿富汗（苏联政府曾经建议这些国家参加东西方国家首脑会议）驻布拉格的外交使节，把捷克斯洛伐克政府给北大西洋公约组织成员国家的照会内容告诉他们。
