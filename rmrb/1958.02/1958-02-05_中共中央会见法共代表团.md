Title: CPC Central Committee Meets with French Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-02-05
Page: 1

　　The CPC Central Committee Meets with French Communist Party Delegation

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 4th The CPC Central Committee met with the French Communist Party delegation on the evening of the 4th and had dinner together. The CPC Central Committee attendees included: Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee; Peng Zhen and Chen Yi, members of the Political Bureau; Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau; Li Xuefeng, Secretary of the Central Secretariat; Liu Lantao, alternate secretary of the Central Secretariat; and Zeng Shan and Liu Ningyi, members of the Central Committee.

　　The French Communist Party delegation attendees included: Jean Vermersch, member of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party Central Committee; Jean Berville, member of the Central Committee; and C. Valant, member of the Central Committee Finance Supervisory Commission.

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 4th The French Communist Party delegation headed by Vermersch has concluded its visit to Korea and returned to Beijing on the 4th. Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, Li Jiebo, Deputy Director of the General Office of the CPC Central Committee, and Xiong Fu and Li Qixin, responsible personnel from the organs of the CPC Central Committee, went to the station to welcome them.
    Li Yong-ho, member of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, also came to the station to welcome them.

### 中共中央会见法共代表团

1958-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央会见法共代表团
　　新华社4日讯　中共中央于4日晚同法共代表团会见并共进晚餐。中共中央出席的有：中共中央副主席周恩来，政治局委员彭真、陈毅，候补委员康生，中央书记处书记李雪峰，候补书记刘澜涛，中央委员曾山、刘宁一。
　　法共代表团出席的有：法共中央政治局委员让·维美徐，中央委员让·贝利沃兹，中央财政监察委员卡·瓦兰。
　　新华社4日讯　以维美徐为首的法国共产党代表团已结束了在朝鲜的访问，在4日回到北京。前往车站欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，中央委员刘宁一，中共中央办公厅副主任李颉伯，中共中央机关负责工作人员熊复、李启新。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员李永镐也到车站欢迎。
