Title: French Communist Party Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1958-02-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The French Communist Party delegation, headed by Jean Vermeersch, concluded its visit to China and left by plane on the 11th via the Soviet Union.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zeng Shan and Liu Ningyi, members of the Central Committee, and Zhao Yimin, Xiong Fu, and Li Qixin, responsible personnel of the central organs of the Communist Party of China.

During its visit to China, the French Communist Party delegation held several talks with leading comrades of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. Accompanied by Zeng Shan, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, they also visited shipyards and cotton mills in Shanghai, as well as the Anshan Iron and Steel Complex and the Ming Tombs Reservoir project in Beijing, and met with a broad mass of workers and peasants, who gave them a warm welcome.

### 法共代表团离北京回国

1958-02-12
第2版()
专栏：

    法共代表团离北京回国
    新华社11日讯　以让·维美徐为首的法国共产党代表团，结束了在中国的访问，已经在11日乘飞机取道苏联回国。
    前往机场送行的有中共中央政治局委员陈毅，中央委员曾山、刘宁一和中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏、熊复、李启新等。
    法国共产党代表团在中国访问期间，曾同中共中央领导同志进行了几次会谈；并在中共中央委员曾山陪同下，参观了上海的造船厂、纱厂，还参观了鞍钢、北京十三陵水库工程等，同广大职工、农民见面，受到群众的热烈欢迎。
