Title: World Cup Soccer Groups Determined
Author:
Date: 1958-02-10
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, 9th: The draw for the sixteen national football teams that qualified for the finals of the 1958 World Cup Championship was held on the evening of the 8th to determine the groups.

    These sixteen teams will be divided into four groups for competition. Group 1: West Germany, Argentina, Czechoslovakia, Northern Ireland. Group 2: France, Paraguay, Yugoslavia, Scotland. Group 3: Sweden, Mexico, Hungary, Wales. Group 4: Austria, Brazil, Soviet Union, England.

    The first and second place teams in these four groups will then be divided into two larger groups for competition.

    The matches for these four groups will officially begin on June 8th in four major cities in Sweden, including Stockholm. The final match to compete for the 1958 world championship will be held on June 29th.

### 世界足球赛分定小组

1958-02-10
第6版()
专栏：

    世界足球赛分定小组
    据新华社9日讯　斯德哥尔摩消息：获得参加1958年世界足球锦标赛决赛权利的十六个国家的足球队已经于8日晚上抽签分定小组。
    这十六个队将分成四个组进行比赛。第一组：西德、阿根廷、捷克斯洛伐克、北爱尔兰。第二组：法国、巴拉圭、南斯拉夫、苏格兰。第三组：瑞典、墨西哥、匈牙利、威尔士。第四组：奥地利、巴西、苏联、英格兰。
    这四个小组的第一、二名将再分二个大组进行比赛。
    这四个组的比赛将于6月8日分别在瑞典的斯德哥尔摩等四个大城市正式开始。争夺1958年世界冠军的最后一场比赛在6月29日举行。
