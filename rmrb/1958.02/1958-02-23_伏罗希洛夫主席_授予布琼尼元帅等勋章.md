Title: Chairman Voroshilov Awards Orders to Marshal Budyonny and Others
Author:
Date: 1958-02-23
Page: 6

    Chairman Voroshilov
    Awards Orders to Marshal Budyonny and Others
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 22nd—According to TASS, on the eve of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy, Chairman Voroshilov of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union awarded orders and certificates in the Kremlin on February 21st to the famous Soviet military leaders and Soviet Marshals Budyonny, Grechko, and Biriuzov, who were awarded the title of Hero of the Soviet Union.

### 伏罗希洛夫主席  授予布琼尼元帅等勋章

1958-02-23
第6版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫主席
    授予布琼尼元帅等勋章
    据新华社22日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：在苏联陆海军建军四十周年的节日前夕，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫21日在克里姆林宫授予荣获苏联英雄称号的著名苏联军队领导人、苏联元帅布琼尼、格列奇科和比留佐夫以勋章和证书。
