Title: Mountain Village Scene
Author: Zheng Chengyi Xiao Tian
Date: 1958-02-06
Page: 8
From the Countryside

　　Mountain Village Scene
　　Zheng Chengyi
　　Mountain after mountain, bend after bend,
　　Verdant cypresses line the village entrance,
　　The mountain village is hidden in the clouds.
　　No need to ask for the village road,
　　Bees lead the way.
　　——The bee farm is bustling.
　　Just when you think there's no road left at the bend,
　　The sound of threshing rice echoes from the clouds.
　　——Newly built terraces, layers of gold.
　　Hearing that the film projection team is coming to the mountains,
　　Men, women, old and young, come out of the village to greet them,
　　Running along the utility poles.
  　On Dragon Mountain, songs resound
　　On Dragon Mountain, songs resound,
　　Men, women, old and young, are busy digging ponds,
　　You dig the soil, I fill the baskets,
　　Split bamboo poles, soft and yielding.
　　Dig one load, then another,
　　Dig the pond two fathoms deep,
　　Next year, when the spring thunder roars, the pond will be full of water,
　　As bright as a treasure mirror.
　　……On Dragon Mountain, songs resound,
　　Carp and lotus fill the pond,
　　The culvert opens, the dragon wags its tail,
　　Golden grains pile up like mountains.
　　Who says the dragon's wrath is inviolable?
　　We dig the dragon's claws and the dragon's gall.
　　Songs fill the mountains and valleys,
　　Dig out a treasure mirror to reflect our good times.
  　Busy Night
　　Xiao Tian
　　The cold winter night is bustling,
　　Lanterns dot the fields,
　　Not a lantern festival for the New Year,
　　But commune members busy building terraces.
　　The deep winter night is dark and cold,
　　Young people sweat and soak their clothes,
　　Not from a roaring fire,
　　But from swinging picks and shovels.
　　The cold stars twinkle faintly in the sky,
　　Sparks fly everywhere on the ground,
　　Not children setting off fireworks,
　　But picks turning over stones.
　　Yellow earth rolls, black earth turns,
　　Terraces connect on the ridges,
　　Turn over a few more acres of land this winter,
　　The scene of a bumper harvest is before our eyes.
　　　　　　　　(Shandong  Junan)

### 山村即景
郑成义  晓田  莒南
1958-02-06
第8版()
专栏：来自农村的诗

　　山村即景
　　郑成义
    山里山，弯里弯，
    翠柏连荫村口上，
    山村云里藏。
　  村路不用问，
    蜜蜂把路引。
    ——养蜂场，闹盈盈。
    弯尽疑无路，
    云里打稻声。
    ——新砌梯田层层金。
    闻说放映队进山来，
    男女老少出村迎，
    顺着电杆奔。
  　龙山上，歌声响
    龙山上、歌声响，
    男女老少挖塘忙，
    你打土呀，我装筐，
    对剖竹担软洋洋。
    挖一担，又一担，
    挖得池塘深两丈，
    明年春雷一声水满塘，
    好比宝镜银亮亮。
    ……龙山上，歌声响，
    鲤鱼莲花满池塘，
    涵门一开龙摆尾，
    金黄谷子堆成山。
    谁说龙怒不可犯？
    挖了龙爪挖龙胆。
    山山谷谷歌声满，
    挖出宝镜照咱好时光。
  　夜忙
　　晓田
    寒冬夜晚闹喧天，
    田野灯笼一盏盏，
    不是过年提灯会，
    是社员忙着修梯田。
    深冬夜晚暗又寒，
    年青人流汗湿衣衫，
    不是烤火的火炉旺，
    是他们挥镢头舞铁铣。
    天空寒星微光闪闪，
    地面上火花四处溅，
    不是孩子们放花火，
    是镢头下去把石翻。
    黄土滚滚黑土翻，
    层层梯田岭上连，
    今冬多翻几亩地，
    丰收图景在眼前。
　　　　　　　　（山东  莒南）
