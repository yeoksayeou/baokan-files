Title: Romania and Egypt Reach Trade Volume Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-02-15
Page: 5

Brief News

    Romania and Egypt Reach Trade Volume Agreement
    Cairo Dispatch: The Romanian economic delegation led by Foreign Trade Minister Popescu left here by plane on the morning of the 13th to return home after a week-long visit to Egypt.
    Egyptian President Nasser received the delegation on the 12th.
    It is reported that the negotiations between the Romanian delegation and the Egyptian authorities have reached an agreement on increasing the trade volume between the two countries. At the same time, both parties also agreed to open a direct air route between Cairo and Bucharest this year.

### 罗埃两国达成贸易量协议

1958-02-15
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    罗埃两国达成贸易量协议
    开罗讯：由对外贸易部长波佩斯库率领的罗马尼亚经济代表团在埃及访问一星期后，13日上午乘飞机离开这里回国。
    埃及总统纳赛尔在12日接见了这个代表团。
    据报道，罗马尼亚代表团和埃及当局举行的谈判已经就增加两国之间的贸易量达成了协议。同时，双方也同意今年在开罗和布加勒斯特之间开辟直达航空线。
