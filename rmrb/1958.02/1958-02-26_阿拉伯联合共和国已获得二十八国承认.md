Title: The United Arab Republic Has Been Recognized by Twenty-Eight Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-02-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 25th. It has been announced that twenty-eight countries have recognized the newly formed United Arab Republic.

Following the fifteen countries that recognized the United Arab Republic on February 22nd, thirteen more countries have announced their recognition. These are: China, Yugoslavia, Afghanistan, Italy, the Vatican, Spain, Greece, the Netherlands, India, Indonesia, Ceylon, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Saudi Arabia.

### 阿拉伯联合共和国已获得二十八国承认

1958-02-26
第5版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联合共和国已获得二十八国承认
    新华社开罗25日电　据宣布，已有二十八国承认新成立的阿拉伯联合共和国。
    继十五国在22日承认阿拉伯联合共和国之后，又有十三国宣布承认，这就是：中国、南斯拉夫、阿富汗、意大利、梵蒂冈、西班牙、希腊、荷兰、印度、印度尼西亚、锡兰、越南民主共和国和沙特阿拉伯。
