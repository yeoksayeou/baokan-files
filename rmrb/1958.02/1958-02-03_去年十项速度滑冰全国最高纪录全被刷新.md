Title: Last Year, All Ten National Speed Skating Records Were Broken
Author:
Date: 1958-02-03
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: All ten national records for men's and women's speed skating, announced by the Sports Commission on January 5th this year, have been broken by January 29th. The new national records were all set during the China, North Korea, Romania, and Kazakh SSR speed skating friendship competition held in Alma-Ata. The new records, along with the names of the creators and the dates they were set, are as follows:

Men's Group:
    500 meters: 43.8 seconds Yang Jucheng January 28th
    1500 meters: 2 minutes 17.9 seconds Yang Jucheng January 29th
    5000 meters: 8 minutes 36.2 seconds Li Taiquan January 28th
    10000 meters: 17 minutes 58.4 seconds Li Taiquan January 29th
    Speed Skating All-Around: 196.346 points
    Li Taiquan January 28th, 29th
Women's Group:
    500 meters: 49.6 seconds Liu Fengrong January 26th
    1000 meters: 1 minute 47.1 seconds Yang Yunxiang January 27th
    1500 meters: 2 minutes 47.5 seconds Liu Fengrong January 26th
    3000 meters: 5 minutes 58.2 seconds Yang Yunxiang January 27th
    Speed Skating All-Around: 222.616 points Li Caiying January 26th, 27th

　　去年十项速度滑冰全国最高纪录全被刷新

    一万公尺：十七分五十八秒四　李太权　1月29日
    速度滑冰全能：一九六·三四六分
