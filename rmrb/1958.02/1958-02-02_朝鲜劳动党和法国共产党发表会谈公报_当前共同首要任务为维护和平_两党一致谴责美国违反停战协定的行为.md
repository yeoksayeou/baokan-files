Title: The Korean Workers' Party and the French Communist Party Issue Communiqué on Talks; Current Primary Task is to Safeguard Peace; Both Parties Unanimously Condemn the United States' Actions Violating the Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-02-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 1st: The Korean Workers' Party delegation, headed by Kim Il-sung, and the French Communist Party delegation, headed by Vermersch, signed and issued a joint communiqué between the two party delegations in Pyongyang on January 31.

The communiqué stated that the Korean Workers' Party delegation held talks with the French Communist Party delegation, which was invited to visit Korea. The two party delegations expressed completely一致的意见 on some of the issues discussed.

The communiqué, after analyzing the current international situation, stated that the two party delegations believe that the struggle to safeguard peace and peaceful coexistence is the common primary task of the two parties at present. The communiqué stated that the French Communist Party delegation highly appreciates the Korean Workers' Party's struggle to carry out socialist construction in North Korea, safeguard peace in Korea, and peacefully reunify Korea on a democratic basis, and notes with satisfaction the amazing achievements made by the Korean people in socialist construction.

The two party delegations believe that the divided Korea should be peacefully reunified. The French Communist Party delegation fully supports the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's policy of peacefully reunifying Korea. The two party delegations resolutely protest and condemn the criminal acts of the U.S. imperialists and the Syngman Rhee clique in continuing to blatantly violate the armistice agreement, seriously threatening peace. The communiqué stated that the Korean Workers' Party delegation highly appreciates the French Communist Party's struggle against the arms race policy and to improve the living conditions of the working people. The communiqué stated that the two party delegations believe that the Soviet Union plays a decisive role in the struggle to safeguard peace, and fully support and agree with the Soviet government's initiatives to ease international tensions, ban the production and testing of nuclear weapons, disarmament, and a summit meeting of great powers. The two party delegations denounced the policy of military blocs and demanded the removal of military bases established in foreign countries.

The communiqué emphasized the need to strengthen the unity and fraternal cooperation of the communist and workers' parties of all countries in accordance with the spirit of the Moscow meeting of communist and workers' parties. The communiqué stated that the two party delegations emphasized that in order to strengthen relations and mutual cooperation between the two parties, it is necessary to exchange experiences frequently and develop relations between the two parties.

Participating in the talks on the Korean Workers' Party side were Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee, and Vice-Chairmen Park Jung-ae and Kim Chang-man; on the French Communist Party side were Vermersch, member of the Central Political Bureau, Belliot, member of the Central Committee, and Varin, member of the Central Financial Supervisory Committee.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 1st: The French Communist Party delegation, which was invited by the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party to visit Korea, left Pyongyang by plane today. The delegation visited Korea for five days.

### 朝鲜劳动党和法国共产党发表会谈公报  当前共同首要任务为维护和平  两党一致谴责美国违反停战协定的行为

1958-02-02
第6版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜劳动党和法国共产党发表会谈公报
    当前共同首要任务为维护和平
    两党一致谴责美国违反停战协定的行为
    据新华社平壤1日电　以金日成为首的朝鲜劳动党代表团和以维美徐为首的法国共产党代表团1月31日在平壤签署并发表了两党代表团之间的联合公报。
    公报说，朝鲜劳动党代表团同应邀来朝鲜访问的法国共产党代表团举行了会谈。两党代表团对所讨论的一些问题表示了完全一致的意见。
    公报在分析了当前国际局势以后说，两党代表团认为，为维护和平和和平共处而斗争是两党当前共同的首要的任务。公报说，法国共产党代表团对朝鲜劳动党在朝鲜北部进行社会主义建设、维护朝鲜的和平和在民主的基础上和平统一朝鲜的斗争给予很高的评价，并且很满意地指出朝鲜人民在社会主义建设中所获得的惊人的成就。
    两党代表团认为，分裂的朝鲜应该和平统一。法国共产党代表团完全支持朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和平统一朝鲜的政策。两党代表团坚决抗议并谴责美帝国主义者和李承晚集团继续粗暴地违反停战协定，严重地威胁和平的犯罪行为。公报说，朝鲜劳动党代表团对于法国共产党为反对军备竞赛政策、改善劳动人民的生活条件等而进行的斗争给予很高的评价。公报说，两党代表团认为，苏联在维护和平的斗争中起了决定性的作用，并且完全支持和赞同苏联政府为缓和国际紧张局势、禁止核武器的生产和试验、裁军和大国首脑会议的倡议，两党代表团斥责了军事集团政策，并且要求撤除建立在外国的军事基地。
    公报强调要根据共产党和工人党莫斯科会议的精神加强各国共产党和工人党的统一和兄弟的合作。公报说，两党代表团强调，为了加强两党间的关系和相互合作，要互相经常交换经验，发展两党间的关系。
    参加会谈的，朝鲜劳动党方面有中央委员会委员长金日成，副委员长朴正爱、金昌满；法国共产党方面有中央政治局委员维美徐、中央委员贝利奥兹、中央财政监察委员会委员瓦林。
    据新华社平壤1日电　应朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的邀请来朝鲜访问的法国共产党代表团今天乘飞机离开平壤。代表团在朝鲜共进行了五天访问。
