Title: Former Indonesian Minister of Education and Culture Yamin Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-02-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing—Former Indonesian Minister of Education and Culture, Member of Parliament, Muhammad Yamin, and his wife, along with a party of seven, arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of the 5th.

Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and his wife hosted a banquet on the evening of the 6th to welcome them.

### 印度尼西亚前文教部长耶明到北京

1958-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚前文教部长耶明到北京
    据新华社讯　印度尼西亚国会议员、前文教部长穆罕默德·耶明和他的夫人等一行七人，5日晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    中国人民外交学会会长张奚若和夫人6日晚举行宴会欢迎。
