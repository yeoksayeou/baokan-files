Title: Cabinet Meeting of the United Arab Republic

Author:

Date: 1958-02-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 2nd. The newly declared cabinet of the United Arab Republic held a meeting at noon on the 2nd, presided over by Egyptian President Nasser and Syrian President Kuwatly.

The entire Egyptian and Syrian cabinet attended the meeting. The meeting ended at 3:00 PM. It was revealed that the meeting discussed the statements that the two presidents will deliver to the parliaments of both countries on the 5th.

Nasser awarded the Order of the Nile to those Syrian ministers who participated in discussions regarding the establishment of the United Arab Republic.

### 阿拉伯统一共和国内阁开会

1958-02-04
第5版()
专栏：

　　阿拉伯统一共和国内阁开会
    新华社开罗2日电　新宣告成立的阿拉伯统一共和国的内阁2日中午在埃及总统纳赛尔和叙利亚总统库阿特利主持下举行了会议。
    埃及和叙利亚的全体部长都出席了这次会议。会议在下午三时结束。据透露，会议讨论了两位总统将于5日向两国议会发表的声明。
    纳赛尔把尼罗河勋章授予那些曾经参加讨论成立阿拉伯统一共和国的叙利亚部长们。
