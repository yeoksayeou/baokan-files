　　Lichun (Start of Spring) Farming Proverbs
　　Before Lichun (Start of Spring) and Yushui (Rain Water) in February,
　　Haul soil and deliver manure to prepare the fields;
　　Dig wells, open canals, and repair water conservancy,
　　Check if the farm tools are complete.

### 立春农谚

1958-02-04
第8版()
专栏：

　　立春农谚
　　二月立春雨水前，
　　拉土送粪整田园；
　　打井开渠修水利，
　　检查农具全不全。
