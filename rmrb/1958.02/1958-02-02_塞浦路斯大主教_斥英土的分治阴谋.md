Title: Cypriot Archbishop Condemns British-Turkish Partition Plot
Author:
Date: 1958-02-02
Page: 5

　　Cypriot Archbishop
　　Condemns British-Turkish Partition Plot
    Xinhua News Agency, Athens, February 1st: Cypriot Archbishop Makarios stated on January 30th that he firmly opposes the "partition" of Cyprus plan that Britain and Turkey are plotting. Sources say that during the Baghdad Pact Organization's Ankara meeting, representatives from Britain and Turkey discussed this plan and reached an agreement.
    Makarios told a United Press reporter that "the Greeks of Cyprus will never accept a solution that in any way leads to partition." He said that Cyprus is a "complete geographical unit" and any partition solution would only cause "unpleasant consequences" and "chaos."
    Makarios said that the British "partition" plan is an "attempt to complicate the problem and thus perpetuate the colonial status of Cyprus."

### 塞浦路斯大主教  斥英土的分治阴谋

1958-02-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　塞浦路斯大主教
    斥英土的分治阴谋
    据新华社1日讯　雅典消息：塞浦路斯大主教马卡里奥斯1月30日表示坚决反对英国和土耳其正在阴谋进行的“分治”塞浦路斯的计划。有消息说，在巴格达条约组织安卡拉会议期间，英国和土耳其的代表曾经讨论过这个计划，并且取得了协议。
    马卡里奥斯在接见合众社记者时说，“塞浦路斯的希腊人决不会接受一种以任何方式导向分治的解决办法”。他说，塞浦路斯是一个“完整的地理单位”，任何分治的办法只会引起“不愉快的后果”和“混乱”。
    马卡里奥斯说，英国的“分治”计划是“企图使问题复杂起来从而使塞浦路斯的殖民地地位永久化”。
