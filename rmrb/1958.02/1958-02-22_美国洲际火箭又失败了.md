Title: US Intercontinental Rocket Fails Again
Author:
Date: 1958-02-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 21st Washington Dispatch: The United States Air Force launched an "Atlas" intercontinental missile at Cape Canaveral, Florida on the 20th, but the result, as with previous attempts, ended in failure.

According to the United States Air Force Department, this ninety-foot-long intercontinental missile exploded at high altitude less than two minutes after launch. Because the entire missile was shattered, the Air Force Department could not explain the cause of this test failure.

### 美国洲际火箭又失败了

1958-02-22
第5版()
专栏：

    美国洲际火箭又失败了
    新华社21日讯　华盛顿消息：美国空军20日在佛罗里达州卡纳维拉尔角试验场发射一枚“阿特拉斯”式洲际导弹，但是结果和以前几次一样归于失败。
    据美国空军部说，这枚长九十英尺的洲际导弹在发射不到两分钟后在高空爆炸。由于整个导弹炸得粉碎，以至空军部无法说明这次试验失败的原因何在。
