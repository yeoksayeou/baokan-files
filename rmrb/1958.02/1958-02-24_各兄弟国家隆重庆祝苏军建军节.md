Title: Fraternal Countries Grandly Celebrate Soviet Army Day
Author:
Date: 1958-02-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency report: On the 21st and 22nd, grand gatherings were held in the capitals of various fraternal countries to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

    In Budapest, a celebration meeting was held on the 22nd. Attending the meeting were Hungarian Party and state leaders Dobi, Münnich, Marosán, and representatives of veteran revolutionaries and social organizations. The celebration meeting was opened by Prime Minister Münnich of the Workers and Peasants Revolutionary Government.

    Defense Minister Lieutenant General Révész delivered a report on the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. He said that all officers and soldiers of the Hungarian People's Army are proud that, on the basis of the Warsaw Pact, they, together with the armies of the Soviet Union and other socialist countries, are defending the cause of peace and socialism.

    In Warsaw, a celebration meeting was held in the Palace of Culture and Science. Attending the meeting were Polish United Workers' Party and state leaders Gomułka, Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, representatives of workers from Warsaw enterprises, figures from the scientific and cultural circles, and generals of the Polish army. Diplomatic officials from the Soviet Union and other countries were also invited to attend the meeting.

    Chairman Zawadzki of the Polish Council of State first delivered the opening remarks. He wished the Soviet armed forces new victories in the struggle to consolidate world peace.

    Subsequently, Polish Defense Minister Spychalski delivered a report on the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. He spoke of the fighting friendship established between the armies of the Soviet Union and Poland in the fight against the common enemy, and the great help the Soviet army provided to the Polish army.

    Soviet Ambassador to Poland Abrosimov spoke at the meeting.

    In Sofia, the Bulgarian Ministry of National Defense, the National Committee of the Bulgarian-Soviet Friendship Association, the Sofia City Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Sofia City People's Council, and the Sofia City Committee of the Fatherland Front jointly held a grand gathering on the evening of the 21st. Bulgarian Party and government leaders Yugov, R. Damyanov, Mikhailov, Chervenkov, Staikov, and others attended the meeting.

    Bulgarian Deputy Minister of National Defense Lieutenant General Zhikov delivered a report at the meeting. He said that the Bulgarian People's Army will do its utmost to consolidate the eternal and fraternal friendship with the Soviet armed forces.

    In Bucharest, representatives from all walks of life held a celebration meeting on the 22nd. Attending the meeting were Romanian Party and government leaders Gheorghiu-Dej, Stoica, Maurer, the Hungarian government and Socialist Workers' Party delegation headed by Kádár, who are currently visiting here, and military attachés of fraternal countries stationed in Romania.

    Romanian Minister of the Armed Forces, General Sălăjan, delivered a report on the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army at the meeting. In his report, he welcomed and supported the joint Chinese-North Korean statement announcing the withdrawal of the Chinese People's Volunteers from Korea.

    In Berlin, three thousand working people held a mass celebration meeting on the 21st. When speaking at the meeting, German Democratic Republic Minister of National Defense Stoph pointed out: The National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic, which maintains a fighting friendship with the world's most modern and powerful army—the Soviet Army—is defending the democratic system and socialist construction achievements of democratic Germany. All attempts by the Adenauer group to use military aggression to restore capitalism in the German Democratic Republic are doomed to failure.

    Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Forces in Germany, General of the Army Zakharov, also spoke at the meeting.

    In Ulaanbaatar, the Mongolian People's Army and representatives from the Party and government organs and working people of Ulaanbaatar jointly held a celebration meeting on the evening of the 22nd to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. Mongolian state leaders Sambu, Tsedenbal, Surenjab, Damdin, and Tsende, as well as a Soviet army delegation invited by the Mongolian Ministry of Military and Public Security to participate in the celebrations, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Mongolia attended the celebration meeting.

    Mongolian Minister of Military and Public Security Lieutenant General Dorj spoke at the meeting. He congratulated the Soviet army on its glorious festival on behalf of the Mongolian people and army.

    A member of the Soviet army delegation, Soviet Hero Major General Abramov, who once fought alongside the Mongolian army to defeat the Japanese invaders, also spoke at the meeting.

    In Tirana, the celebration meeting on the 22nd was jointly organized by the Ministry of National Defense, the Albanian-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Presidium of the Tirana Democratic Front. Participating in the meeting were: Albanian Party of Labor and state leaders Hoxha, Shehu, and Lechi, as well as diplomatic officials from the Soviet Union and other countries stationed in Albania.

    Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Director of the Political Department of the People's Army, Lieutenant General Bekteshi, delivered a report on the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

    Other cities in Albania also held celebration meetings for Soviet Army Day.

### 各兄弟国家隆重庆祝苏军建军节

1958-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    各兄弟国家隆重庆祝苏军建军节
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：21日、22日在各兄弟国家的首都分别举行了盛大集会，隆重庆祝苏军建军四十周年。
    在布达佩斯，22日举行了庆祝大会。出席大会的有匈牙利党和国家领导人道比、明尼赫、马罗山等，以及革命运动的老战士和社会团体的代表。庆祝大会由工农革命政府总理明尼赫主持开幕。
    国防部长雷维斯中将在会上作了关于苏军建军四十周年的报告。他说，匈牙利人民军的全体官兵和将领引为自豪的是，在华沙条约的基础上，他们同苏联和其他社会主义国家的军队一起捍卫着和平和社会主义事业。
    在华沙，庆祝大会在文化科学宫内举行。出席大会的有波兰统一工人党和国家领导人哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹，华沙各企业工人的代表，科学文化界人士，波兰军队的将领等。苏联和其他国家的外交官员也应邀出席了大会。
    波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基首先致开会词。他祝苏联武装力量在巩固世界和平的斗争中获得新的胜利。
    接着，波兰国防部长斯彼哈尔斯基作了关于苏军建军四十周年的报告。他谈到苏波两国军队在反对共同敌人中建立起来的战斗友谊，以及苏联军队对波兰军队的巨大帮助。
    苏联驻波兰大使阿布罗西莫夫在会上讲了话。
    在索非亚，保加利亚国防部、保苏友好协会全国委员会、保加利亚共产党索非亚市委员会、索非亚市人民会议和祖国阵线索非亚市委员会，21日晚举行了盛大集会。保加利亚党政领导人于哥夫、拉·达米扬诺夫、米哈伊洛夫、契尔文科夫、斯塔伊科夫等出席了会议。
    保加利亚国防部副部长吉科夫中将在会上作了报告。他说，保加利亚人民军将用全力巩固同苏联武装力量的永恒的、兄弟般的友谊。
    在布加勒斯特，各界代表22日举行了庆祝大会。出席大会的有罗马尼亚的党政领导人乔治乌—德治、斯托伊卡、毛雷尔，正在这里访问的以卡达尔为首的匈牙利政府和社会主义工人党代表团，以及兄弟国家驻罗马尼亚的武官。
    罗马尼亚武装部队部部长萨拉扬上将在大会上作了关于苏军建军四十周年的报告。他在报告中表示欢迎和支持宣布中国人民志愿军撤出朝鲜的中朝联合声明。
    在柏林，劳动人民三千人21日举行群众庆祝大会。德意志民主共和国国防部长斯多夫在大会上讲话时指出：同世界上最现代化和最强大的军队——苏联军队保持着战斗友谊的德意志民主共和国国家人民军，捍卫着民主德国的民主制度和社会主义建设成就。阿登纳集团要用军事侵略来在德意志民主共和国恢复资本主义的一切企图都是注定要失败的。
    驻德苏军总司令萨哈罗夫大将也在大会上讲了话。
    在乌兰巴托，蒙古人民军和乌兰巴托市的党、政机关以及劳动人民代表22日晚上联合举行庆祝苏联军队建军四十周年的大会。蒙古国家负责人桑布、泽登巴尔、苏伦扎布、达姆丁、曾德和应蒙古军事和公安部邀请来这里参加庆祝会的苏联军队代表团以及各国驻蒙古的外交使节出席了庆祝大会。
    蒙古军事和公安部部长道尔吉中将在会上讲话。他代表蒙古人民和军队祝贺苏联军队的光荣节日。
    苏联军队代表团团员、曾经同蒙古军队一齐击败日本侵略军的苏联英雄阿伯拉莫夫少将也在会上讲了话。
    在地拉那，22日的庆祝大会由国防部、阿苏友好协会和地拉那民主阵线主席团联合组织。参加大会的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和国家的领导人霍查、谢胡、列希，以及苏联和其他国家驻阿尔巴尼亚的外交官员。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、人民军政治部主任别克特希中将作了关于苏联军队建军四十周年的报告。
    阿尔巴尼亚的其他城市也举行了苏军建军节的庆祝大会。
