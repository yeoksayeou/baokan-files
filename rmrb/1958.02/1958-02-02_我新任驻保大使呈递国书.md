Title: New Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria Presents Credentials

Author:

Date: 1958-02-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 1st: Zhu Qiwu, the newly appointed Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Bulgaria, accompanied by all the diplomatic officials of the Embassy, presented his credentials to Ni. Georgiev, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Bulgaria, on the morning of January 31.

Present at the presentation of credentials were: Yo. Milev, member of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Bulgaria, Foreign Minister Ka. Lukanov, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yo. Stepanov, and others.

When Ambassador Zhu Qiwu presented his credentials, he delivered a eulogy to Vice-Chairman Georgiev, who delivered a reply.

In his eulogy, Ambassador Zhu Qiwu said that Bulgaria has made great achievements in socialist construction, made significant contributions to consolidating the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and made unremitting efforts to ease international tensions and safeguard peace in the Balkan region and the world. The increasingly developing friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries not only fully conform to the interests of our two peoples in building socialism, but also contribute to consolidating the unbreakable unity among socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union and safeguarding the noble cause of world peace.

In his reply, Vice-Chairman Georgiev praised China's great achievements in socialist industrialization and its contributions to safeguarding peace. He said that the strength of the socialist camp has now overwhelmed the forces of the imperialist camp. The increasingly developing friendly, cooperative, and mutually beneficial relations between our two countries will not only contribute to the socialist construction of our two countries, but also contribute to strengthening the unbreakable unity of the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union and safeguarding world peace.

On the afternoon of the day he presented his credentials, Ambassador Zhu Qiwu and all the members of the Embassy laid a wreath at the Dimitrov Mausoleum.

### 我新任驻保大使呈递国书

1958-02-02
第6版()
专栏：

　　我新任驻保大使呈递国书
    据新华社索非亚1日电　中华人民共和国新任驻保加利亚大使朱其文1月31日上午偕大使馆全体外交官员，向保加利亚国民议会主席团副主席尼·格奥尔吉耶夫递交了国书。
    递交国书时在场的有：保加利亚国民议会主席团委员约·米列夫、外交部长卡·卢卡诺夫、外交部交际司司长约·斯捷潘诺夫等。
    朱其文大使递交国书时，向格奥尔吉耶夫副主席致颂词，格奥尔吉耶夫副主席致答词。
    朱其文大使致颂词说，保加利亚在社会主义建设的方面取得了巨大的成就，在巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结方面，作出了重大的贡献，在和缓国际紧张局势、维护巴尔干地区和世界和平等方面，也作出了不懈的努力。我们两国之间的日益发展的友好互助合作关系，不仅完全符合我们两国人民建设社会主义的利益，而且也有助于巩固以苏联为首的社会主义各国之间的牢不可破的团结和保卫世界和平的崇高事业。
    格奥尔吉耶夫副主席在答词中赞扬中国在社会主义工业化方面的巨大成就和在维护和平方面的贡献。他说，现在，社会主义阵营力量已经压倒帝国主义阵营的势力。我们两国间的日益发展的友好、合作和互助关系，不仅有助于我们两国的社会主义建设，而且有助于加强以苏联为首的社会主义国家牢不可破的团结和保卫世界和平。
    在递交国书的当天下午，朱其文大使偕大使馆全体人员向季米特洛夫墓献了花圈。
