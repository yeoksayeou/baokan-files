Title: The Situation in Algeria Becomes Increasingly Tense; France Deploys 480,000 Troops but Still Struggles to Cope
Author:
Date: 1958-02-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd dispatch from Paris: According to Paris newspapers, the situation in Algeria is becoming increasingly tense.

On February 22nd, the Algerian National Liberation Army clashed with French colonial troops in Moussaoui, south of Algiers. The Algerian National Liberation Army killed eleven French soldiers and wounded fifteen. At the same time, the Algerian National Liberation Army destroyed a French helicopter. In the fighting on the 20th, which lasted all day, the Algerian National Liberation Army sacrificed ninety soldiers. The fighting was most intense in the area south of Guelma.

According to press reports, 480,000 French troops are now participating in the suppression of the Algerian national liberation movement. According to a French Ministry of Defense spokesman, another 50,000 officers and soldiers will soon be transferred from France and French West and Equatorial Africa to Algeria.

The French police authorities continue to persecute Algerians residing in France. So-called "identity checks" are being carried out in the suburbs and within the city of Paris. In the past ten days, one hundred and two Algerians have been arrested.

### 阿尔及利亚局势越来越紧张  法国用兵四十八万仍然穷于应付

1958-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚局势越来越紧张
    法国用兵四十八万仍然穷于应付
    新华社22日讯　巴黎消息：据巴黎报纸报道，阿尔及利亚的局势越来越紧张。
    阿尔及利亚民族解放军22日在阿尔及尔南部的莫萨维利同法国殖民军发生冲突。阿尔及利亚民族解放军打死了十一名法军，打伤十五名法军。同时，阿尔及利亚民族解放军击毁了一架法国直升飞机。在20日整整一天的战斗中，阿尔及利亚民族解放军牺牲了九十名士兵。这次战斗在格尔马以南一带的战斗最为激烈。
    据报刊统计，现在有四十八万法军参加镇压阿尔及利亚的民族解放运动。据法国国防部发言人说，最近还要从法国和法属西非和赤道非洲调五万名官兵到阿尔及利亚。
    法国警察当局继续在迫害侨居在法国的阿尔及利亚人。巴黎市郊和市内都在进行所谓“身份检查”。最近十天内已经有一百零二名阿尔及利亚人被捕。
