Title: Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-02-07
Page: 4

    Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, February 6th. The Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation, headed by Dr. Jaroslav Havelka, Deputy Minister of Education and Culture of Czechoslovakia, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 6th.
    During their stay in China, the delegation will discuss with the Chinese side the 1958 implementation plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture of China, and others went to the airport to welcome them. Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiňák, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团到京

1958-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团到京
    新华社6日讯　以捷克斯洛伐克教育文化部副部长雅罗斯拉夫·哈维尔卡博士为首的捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团在6日下午乘飞机到北京。
    代表团在我国期间将和我国方面商订中捷文化合作协定1958年执行计划。
    我国文化部副部长夏衍等人曾前往机场欢迎；前往机场欢迎的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克。
