Title: GDR Ambassador to China, Gipner, Recalled Home

Author:

Date: 1958-02-20
Page: 3

    GDR Ambassador to China, Gipner, Recalled Home
    (Xinhua News Agency) - German Democratic Republic's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Gipner, has been recalled home and left Beijing by plane on the 19th. Before leaving Beijing, Ambassador Gipner made farewell calls to Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Mayor Peng Zhen, and Vice Foreign Minister Zeng Yongquan.

### 民主德国驻华大使纪普纳奉调回国

1958-02-20
第3版()
专栏：

    民主德国驻华大使纪普纳奉调回国
    据新华社讯　德意志民主共和国驻华特命全权大使纪普纳奉调回国，已经在19日乘飞机离开北京。纪普纳大使在离开北京前，曾分别向周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长、彭真市长及外交部副部长曾涌泉等作了辞行拜会。
