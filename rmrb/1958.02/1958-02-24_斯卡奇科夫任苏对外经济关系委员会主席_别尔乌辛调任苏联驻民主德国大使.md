Title: Skachkov Appointed Chairman of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the USSR; Pervukhin Transferred to Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-02-24
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, February 21st -- TASS, Moscow, February 20th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR appointed Semyon Andreyevich Skachkov as Chairman of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the Council of Ministers of the USSR.
    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR relieved M.G. Pervukhin of his post as Chairman of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and appointed him as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the USSR to the German Democratic Republic. The former ambassador, Pushkin, has been transferred to other work.
    In addition, the Council of Ministers of the USSR appointed G.N. Zarubin, the former Soviet Ambassador to the United States, as Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR.

### 斯卡奇科夫任苏对外经济关系委员会主席  别尔乌辛调任苏联驻民主德国大使

1958-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    斯卡奇科夫任苏对外经济关系委员会主席
    别尔乌辛调任苏联驻民主德国大使
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科20日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命谢苗·安德烈耶维奇·斯卡奇科夫为苏联部长会议国家对外经济关系委员会主席。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团解除米·格·别尔乌辛苏联部长会议国家对外经济关系委员会主席的职务，任命他为苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使；原任大使普希金另调其他工作。
    又讯：苏联部长会议任命前苏联驻美大使格·尼·查鲁宾为苏联外交部副部长。
