Title: Little Iron Ox (Picture)
Author: Lǐ Jiǔlíng
Date: 1958-02-25
Page: 3

Little Iron Ox

    The small, versatile tractor trial-produced by the Wuhan General Machinery Factory successfully completed a cultivation test on the fields of the Hanqiao No. 1 Cooperative in Hanyang on February 10th. In order to support agricultural production promptly, the factory plans to produce fifty of these small "Iron Oxen" before the spring plowing season. This is truly good news for realizing agricultural mechanization.
                          Xinhua News Agency Reporter Li Jiuling photographed
    ①The small, versatile tractor can cultivate dry land when fitted with a dry-land plow.
    ②The small, versatile tractor can cultivate paddy fields.
    ③The small, versatile tractor can pump water when fitted with a water pump.
    ④The small, versatile tractor can also haul things, with a load capacity of about 2,000 catties (approximately 1000kg).
    ⑤The small, versatile tractor can be fitted with a circular saw for logging.

### 小铁牛（图片）
李九龄
1958-02-25
第3版()
专栏：

    小铁牛
    武汉市通用机器厂试制的小型万能拖拉机，2月10日在汉阳汉桥一社的田地上进行了耕作试验，获得成功。为了及时地支援农业生产，这个厂计划在春耕前生产五十台这种小“铁牛”。这真是实现农业机械化的一个喜讯。
                          新华社记者　李九龄摄
    ①小型万能拖拉机装上旱地犁就可以耕旱地。
    ②小型万能拖拉机可以耕水田。
    ③小型万能拖拉机装上水泵就能抽水。
    ④小型万能拖拉机还可以拖运东西，能载重二千斤左右。
    ⑤小型万能拖拉机安装上圆锯即可伐木。
