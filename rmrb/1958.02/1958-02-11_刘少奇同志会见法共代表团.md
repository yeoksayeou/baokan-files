Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi Meets with French Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, met today with the French Communist Party delegation currently visiting China and held friendly talks with the delegation. On the Chinese side, Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, An Ziwen, member of the Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi participated in the talks. On the French Communist Party delegation side were the head of the delegation, Jean Vermeesch, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, Jean Berlivaux, member of the Central Committee, and C. Varlin, member of the Central Finance Supervisory Committee.

### 刘少奇同志会见法共代表团

1958-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇同志会见法共代表团
    新华社10日讯　中国共产党中央委员会副主席刘少奇今天会见了正在中国访问的法国共产党代表团，并且同代表团进行了友好的会谈。参加谈话的，中国方面还有中央政治局委员彭真、中央委员安子文、刘宁一，法国共产党代表团方面有代表团团长、中央政治局委员让·维美徐，中央委员让·贝利沃兹，中央财政监察委员卡·瓦兰。
