Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Wilson
Author:
Date: 1958-02-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 25th—Premier Zhou Enlai received Harold Wilson, a British Labour Party Member of Parliament and former President of the Board of Trade, on the evening of February 25th.

Present at the reception were Huang Hua, Director of the Department of Western European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见威尔逊

1958-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见威尔逊
    新华社25日讯　周恩来总理25日晚上接见了英国工党议员、前贸易大臣哈罗德·威尔逊。
    接见时在座的有外交部西欧司司长黄华，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪。
