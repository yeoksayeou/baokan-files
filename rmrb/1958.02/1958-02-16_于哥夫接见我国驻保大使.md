Title: Yugov Receives Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1958-02-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 15th. Yugov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, received Zhu Qiwen, the new Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, in Sofia this morning.

### 于哥夫接见我国驻保大使

1958-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    于哥夫接见我国驻保大使
    新华社索非亚15日电　保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记于哥夫，今天上午在索非亚接见了中国新任驻保加利亚大使朱其文。
