Page: 4

    Obituary
    Comrade Huang Jing, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Director of the National Technology Commission of the People's Republic of China, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Industry, Deputy Director of the Science Planning Commission of the State Council, and member of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, passed away due to pulmonary embolism at 4:30 PM on February 10, 1958. A public memorial service will be held at 4:00 PM on February 13th at Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park. Respects from all circles will be received on the morning of February 14th, and the remains will be moved to Babaoshan at 12:00 noon. This obituary is hereby announced.
    The Funeral Committee for Comrade Huang Jing

### 讣告

1958-02-13

专栏：

    讣告
    中国共产党中央委员会委员、中华人民共和国国家技术委员会主任、第一机械工业部部长、国务院科学规划委员会副主任、中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会委员黄敬同志患肺动脉栓塞症于1958年2月10日下午四时三十分逝世。现在定于2月13日下午四时在中山公园中山堂举行公祭，2月14日上午各界吊唁，午十二时移灵八宝山。特此讣告。
    黄敬同志治丧委员会
