Title: Japan Journalists Conference Decides to Invite Chinese News Delegation to Visit Japan
Author:
Date: 1958-02-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 19th: The Japan Journalists Conference Council decided at a meeting held on the 18th to invite a Chinese news delegation to visit Japan in early October of this year.

    The Japan Journalists Conference's work plan for this year, as passed at the meeting, stipulates that the Japan Journalists Conference will make greater efforts to promote the early resolution of the issue of mutually dispatching resident journalists between Japan and China.

    The meeting unanimously supported holding an international journalists meeting.

### 日本新闻工作者会议决定  邀请我国新闻代表团访日

1958-02-22
第5版()
专栏：

    日本新闻工作者会议决定
    邀请我国新闻代表团访日
    据新华社19日讯　东京消息：日本新闻工作者会议理事会在18日举行的会议上，决定在今年10月初邀请中国新闻代表团访问日本。
    会议通过的日本新闻工作者会议今年度的运动方针规定，日本新闻工作者会议将更加努力促使日中双方互派常驻记者问题的早日解决。
    会议一致支持举行国际新闻工作者会见。
