Title: Soviet Union Advances into the Interior of Antarctica, Establishing Sixth Scientific Station Near the Pole
Author:
Date: 1958-02-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, February 16th: According to TASS news: Soviet scientists advancing into the Antarctic interior have reached the inaccessible polar region at 82 degrees south latitude and are beginning to establish the sixth scientific station, "Soviet."

    The new exploration base is 1,420 kilometers from the Mirny Observatory. The ice sheet in this area is 3,700 meters above sea level, and no one has ever been there before. On February 12, the station recorded a temperature of minus 52 degrees Celsius.

    Soviet scientists, driving eight tractor-sleds carrying more than one hundred tons of equipment, braved the severe cold of minus 40 to 45 degrees Celsius, and after a difficult journey, finally reached the end point of the Antarctic summer's planned route. On February 5, when they reached an altitude of 3,600 meters and an extremely deep snow area, the ruts left by the tractors in the loose snow were two Chinese feet deep. Later, they plan to continue deeper into the Antarctic interior.

    The newly built scientific station is led by Babarykin, an experienced polar worker. Five people will spend the Antarctic winter there this year. In the coming Antarctic autumn and winter, they will regularly conduct meteorological, upper-air meteorological, solar radiation, and glacier observations in accordance with the International Geophysical Year program. These observations have already begun.

    The Soviet expedition now has six scientific stations in Antarctica: Mirny, Pionerskaya, Oasis, Komsomolskaya, Vostok, and Soviet.

### 苏联向南极腹地进军  在极地附近建立第六个科学站

1958-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联向南极腹地进军
    在极地附近建立第六个科学站
    新华社16日讯  据塔斯社消息：向南极腹地进军的苏联科学家们已经到达南纬八十二度难以到达的极地附近，着手建立第六个科学站“苏维埃”。
    新的考察基地距和平村观测台有一千四百二十公里，这个地区冰原高度拔海三千七百公尺，以前从来没有一个人到过这里。2月12日这个站纪录的气温是摄氏零下五十二度。
    苏联科学家驾着八辆雪橇牵引机带着一百多吨装备，冒着零下四十度到四十五度的严寒，经过艰辛的路程，终于到达今年南极夏季所规定的路程的终点。2月5日，当他们到达海拔三千六百公尺和雪极深的地方时，牵引机在松雪中留下的辙痕有两市尺深。以后，他们还打算向南极腹地继续深入。
    新建的科学站是由经验丰富的极地工作者巴巴雷金领导的，今年将有五个人在那里度过南极的冬天。在即将来临的南极秋季和冬季他们将按照国际地球物理年的计划定期进行气象、高空气象、日射和冰川观测。现在这些观测已经开始。
    现在苏联考察队在南极洲共有六个科学站：和平村、少先队、绿洲、共青团、东方和苏维埃。
