Author:
Date: 1958-02-01
Page: 5

　　Rocket Scandal Exposed; "Talkative" Person Punished
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 31st - According to an Agence France-Presse report, authorities at the Cape Canaveral rocket testing ground in Florida, USA, have punished a "talkative" staff member with a two-week suspension.
    The testing ground authorities are now stepping up "security" measures to prevent the truth about future test failures from leaking out.

### 火箭丑事外扬  “多嘴”的人挨罚

1958-02-01
第5版()
专栏：

　　火箭丑事外扬  “多嘴”的人挨罚
    新华社31日讯　华盛顿消息：据法新社报道，美国佛罗里达州卡纳维拉尔角火箭试验场当局已经处罚一名“多嘴”的工作人员停职两周。
    现在试验场当局正在加紧“保安”措施，以免走漏未来的试验失败的真相。
