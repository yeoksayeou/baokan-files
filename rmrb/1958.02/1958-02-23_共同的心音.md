Title: A Shared Heartbeat
Author: Wei Chuantong
Date: 1958-02-23
Page: 8

    On this joyous New Year's Day,
    We welcome the 40th anniversary of your army's founding;
    Our shared celebration,
    Our shared spring!
    The lighthouse of the October Revolution,
    Illuminates the source of happiness;
    Crushing the intervention of fourteen nations,
    Sweeping away the White Army's turmoil.
    Remember the past,
    Iron shoes treaded through Sichuan and Shaanxi;
    With a sweeping stroke of the pen,
    "Arming to support the Soviet Union..."
    On the Long March road,
    Commemorating the great Lenin;
    We sing aloud:
    "The Five-Year Plan is completed in four years..."
    Regardless of snowy mountains or grasslands,
    We are full of confidence;
    The Soviet Union has the Red Army!
    China has the Red Army!
    Hitler set fire to you,
    Hideki Tojo set fire to us;
    Burning, burning, burning...
    They were destroyed, we were victorious!
    Two red moons circle in the sky,
    Fire dragons streak across the Yangtze River;
    The east wind prevails,
    The west wind fades.
    Our shared heartbeat,
    Press forward with hard work, press forward with leaps and bounds!
    For global peace,
    For future generations!
    [Note] The slogan "Arming to support the Soviet Union" can still be seen today in the Sichuan-Shaanxi border region.

### 共同的心音
魏传统
1958-02-23
第8版()
专栏：

    共同的心音
    魏传统
    在我们新春的佳日，
    迎接你们建军四十周年；
    我们共同的欢庆，
    我们共同的春天！
    十月革命的灯塔，
    照彻幸福的源泉；
    粉碎十四国干涉，
    扫除白党的骚乱。
    忆昔日，
    铁鞋踏川陕；
    漫笔直挥，
    “武装拥护苏联……”
    长征道上，
    纪念伟大列宁；
    我们高歌：
    “五年计划四年成……”
    无论雪山草地，
    我们满怀信心；
    苏联有红军！
    中国有红军！
    希特勒在你们身上纵火，
    东条英机在我们身上纵火；
    燃烧，燃烧，燃烧……
    毁灭的是他们，胜利的是你我！
    两颗红色月亮临空绕，
    扬子江上火龙飚；
    东风浩荡，
    西风寥寥。
    我们共同的心音，
    乘胜苦干，乘胜跃进！
    为了全球和平，
    为了后世子孙！
    〔注〕“武装拥护苏联”的标语，在川陕边至今犹能见到。
