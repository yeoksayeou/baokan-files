Title: Sino-Foreign Cultural Association Holds Film Evening to Celebrate Ceylon National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-02-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries held a film evening in Beijing tonight to celebrate the tenth anniversary of Ceylon's independence.

    Ceylon's Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Mr. Moorthy, and members of the embassy were invited to attend the evening.

    At the beginning of the evening, Chu Tunan, President of the Sino-Foreign Cultural Association, said that he felt very honored to be able to celebrate Ceylon's National Day for the first time in China with Ceylon friends after the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Ceylon. In the past two years, the friendly exchanges between the peoples of the two countries have increased day by day, and the deep friendship between the peoples of the two countries cannot be destroyed by any force. He also extended warm congratulations to the government and people of Ceylon on behalf of the Sino-Foreign Cultural Association.

    Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Moorthy also spoke at the evening. He said that there had been exchanges between Ceylon and China a long time ago, but today's exchanges are between the peoples of the two countries. The Chinese art troupe was warmly welcomed in Ceylon not long ago. He hoped that Ceylon would also send an art troupe to return this visit this year.

    After the speeches, documentary films such as "Premier Zhou Enlai's Visit to Ceylon" and "Ceylon's Architecture and Art" filmed by Ceylon and the Chinese film "Luohan Money" were shown.

    Zhao Puchu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Buddhist Association, and Vice Secretaries-General Ju Zan and Zhou Shujia, as well as about 300 people from the Buddhist community in Beijing, attended the film evening tonight.

### 对外文协举行电影晚会庆祝锡兰国庆

1958-02-04
第2版()
专栏：

    对外文协举行电影晚会庆祝锡兰国庆
    新华社3日讯　为庆祝锡兰独立十周年，中国人民对外文化协会今晚在北京举行电影晚会。
    锡兰驻中国大使馆临时代办摩尔西和使馆人员应邀出席了晚会。
    对外文化协会会长楚图南在晚会开始时讲话说，能同锡兰朋友一道在中锡两国建交以后第一次在中国庆祝锡兰的国庆，感到十分荣幸。近两年来，两国人民的友好往来日益增多，两国人民之间的深厚友情，是任何力量都不能摧毁的。他还代表对外文化协会向锡兰政府和人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    摩尔西代办也在晚会上讲了话。他说，锡中两国很久以前就有了往来，但是今天的往来才是两国人民之间的往来。中国艺术团不久以前在锡兰受到了热烈欢迎。他希望锡兰今年也派艺术团来回答这个访问。
    讲话以后，放映了锡兰拍摄的“周恩来总理访问锡兰”和“锡兰的建筑与艺术”等纪录影片和中国影片“罗汉钱”。
    中国佛教协会副主席赵朴初和副秘书长巨赞、周叔迦，以及北京佛教界等人士约三百人出席了今晚的电影晚会。
