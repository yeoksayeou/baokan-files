Title: Premier Zhou Leads Delegation Back to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd: The Chinese government delegation led by Premier Zhou Enlai, which visited Korea, returned to Beijing by special plane from Shenyang this morning. The Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho, who accompanied the delegation on its visit to Korea, also arrived in Beijing on the same plane.

At 11:50 AM, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu, and others stepped off the plane with smiles on their faces. They shook hands with Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, He Long, and Li Xiannian, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Commission Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Bao Erhan, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China, who came to the airport to greet them, and accepted flowers presented to them by the staff of the Korean Embassy in China.

Also present at the airport today to welcome them were responsible persons and representatives from relevant departments of the Chinese government and people's organizations, as well as representatives of Korean students in China.

### 周总理率领代表团回到北京

1958-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理率领代表团回到北京
    新华社22日讯　由周恩来总理率领到朝鲜访问的我国政府代表团，今天上午从沈阳乘专机回到北京。陪同代表团去朝鲜访问的朝鲜驻华大使李永镐也同机到京。
    上午十一时五十分，周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长、外交部副部长张闻天、中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕等人笑容满面地步下飞机，他们同前往机场迎接的陈云、彭德怀、贺龙、李先念副总理，彭真副委员长，国防委员会副主席张治中，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长章汉夫和各国驻华使节等一一握手，并接受了朝鲜驻华大使馆人员献给他们的鲜花。
    今天到机场欢迎的还有我国政府有关部门、人民团体的负责人和代表，朝鲜在我国的留学生代表等。
