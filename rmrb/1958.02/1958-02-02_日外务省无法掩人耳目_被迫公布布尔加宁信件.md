Title: Japanese Foreign Ministry Unable to Conceal the Truth, Forced to Publicize Bulganin Letter
Author:
Date: 1958-02-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 1st: TASS Tokyo dispatch: The Japanese Foreign Ministry finally released the letter from Bulganin to the Japanese government dated December 10, 1957, on the afternoon of January 30th. According to reports in various newspapers on January 31st, this letter was released after Socialist Party member Habu Sanjiro questioned it at a Senate meeting on January 30th.
　　The newspapers published a summary of the contents of the letter, and pointed out that the letter called for the establishment of cooperation and disarmament conducive to strengthening peace.

### 日外务省无法掩人耳目  被迫公布布尔加宁信件

1958-02-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　日外务省无法掩人耳目  被迫公布布尔加宁信件
    新华社1日讯　塔斯社东京讯：日本外务省1月30日下午终于公布了1957年12月10日布尔加宁给日本政府的信件。据1月31日各报报道，这封信是社会党议员羽生三七在1月30日参议院会议上提出质询后公布的。
    各报发表了这封信的内容摘要，并且指出，这封信呼吁建立有利于加强和平的合作和裁军。
