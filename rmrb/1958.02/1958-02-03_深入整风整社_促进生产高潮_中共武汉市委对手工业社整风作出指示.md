Title: Deepen Rectification and Commune Consolidation to Promote a Production Upsurge: Wuhan Municipal Party Committee Issues Instructions on Rectification for Handicraft Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1958-02-03
Page: 3

　　Deepen Rectification and Commune Consolidation to Promote a Production Upsurge
    中共武汉市委对手工业社整风作出指示
    本报讯　中共武汉市委根据武汉市手工业合作社整风运动进展情况，强调下一步的中心任务，仍是全面深入的整风整社，争取整风整社的完全胜利，促进新的生产高潮。

    武汉市已有四百七十七个手工业合作社，三万九千多名社员，投入了整风整社运动；其中有一百三十个合作社，在最近可告一段落。

    在已开展整风整社的手工业合作社，已开始开花结果。在鸣放期间，社员们对于社里脱产人员过多、不深入领导生产，提了很多意见。现在，这些问题已基本上获得解决。江汉区第一批开始整风整社的六十三个社，过去脱产人员曾高达总人数的10％以上，在整风整社中，接受群众意见，采取“来自生产，回到生产”，“下放劳动锻炼”，“半脱产”等等办法，现在已经减少到5％左右。许多社的党支部书记、主任和副主任等，纷纷带头下放回生产，留下来的社干现在都是一身数职。许多社干根据“有事当社干，无事当社员”的原则，每周抽出半天、一天的时间参加劳动。这样，大大地密切了社干与社员的关系，群众生产积极性发挥了。

    许多社生产上的关键问题纷纷得到解决，指标纷纷突破，合理化建议大量涌现，主动克服困难，注意产品质量、降低成本、更好的为人民群众服务开始蔚为一种新风气。一钢锹铁锅社过去铁锹上木柄是用人工打眼，现在社员把它改进为用冲床冲，这样过去要二天才能完成的任务，现在半小时就解决了，质量还更加规格合一。

    市委在前后召开的两次会议上，强调地指出：在整风整社已经取得很大成绩的情况下，必须切实保证质量，按照市委要求，全面深入地解决问题。只有如此，才有促进新的生产高潮的稳固基础。整风整社已大体上告一段落的社，要发动群众进行复查，发现不足之处，进行必要的补课。
The Wuhan Municipal Party Committee of the CPC Issues Instructions on Rectification for Handicraft Cooperatives
    This newspaper reports that according to the progress of the rectification movement in the handicraft cooperatives of Wuhan, the Wuhan Municipal Party Committee of the CPC emphasized that the central task for the next step is still to thoroughly and deeply rectify and consolidate the cooperatives, strive for a complete victory in the rectification and consolidation, and promote a new upsurge in production.

    Currently, 477 handicraft cooperatives in Wuhan, with more than 39,000 members, have been involved in the rectification and consolidation movement; among them, 130 cooperatives will come to a conclusion soon.

    In the handicraft cooperatives that have launched the rectification and consolidation movement, results have begun to emerge. During the "blooming and contending" period, members raised many opinions about the excessive number of cadres detached from production and their failure to deeply lead production. Now, these problems have been basically resolved. In the sixty-three cooperatives in the Jianghan District that were the first to start rectification and consolidation, the number of cadres detached from production used to be as high as 10% or more of the total number of people. During the rectification and consolidation, they accepted the opinions of the masses and adopted measures such as "from production, back to production," "transfer to lower levels for labor training," and "semi-detachment," etc., and now it has been reduced to about 5%. Many branch secretaries, directors, and deputy directors of the cooperatives have taken the lead in returning to production. The cadres left behind now hold multiple positions. Many cadres, according to the principle of "cadres when needed, members when not," take half a day or a day each week to participate in labor. In this way, the relationship between cadres and members has been greatly strengthened, and the enthusiasm of the masses for production has been brought into play.

    Many key production problems in many cooperatives have been solved one after another, targets have been broken through one after another, a large number of rationalization proposals have emerged, and a new atmosphere of actively overcoming difficulties, paying attention to product quality, reducing costs, and better serving the people has begun to prevail. In the Yigangqiao Iron Pot Cooperative, the wooden handles of the shovels used to be drilled manually. Now, the members have improved it to use a punching machine, so that the task that used to take two days to complete can now be solved in half an hour, and the quality is more uniform.

    The Municipal Party Committee, at two meetings held before and after, emphasized that under the circumstances that great achievements have been made in the rectification and consolidation, it is necessary to earnestly ensure quality, and in accordance with the requirements of the Municipal Party Committee, to solve problems thoroughly and deeply. Only in this way can there be a solid foundation for promoting a new upsurge in production. Cooperatives whose rectification and consolidation have largely come to an end should mobilize the masses to conduct a review, discover shortcomings, and make necessary supplementary lessons.
