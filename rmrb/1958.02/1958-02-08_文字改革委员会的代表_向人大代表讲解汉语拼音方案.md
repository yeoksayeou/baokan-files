Title: Representatives of the Chinese Writing Reform Committee Explain the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme to NPC Deputies
Author:
Date: 1958-02-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Representatives of the Chinese Writing Reform Committee explained the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme and answered questions to some of the deputies attending the Fifth Session of the First National People's Congress in three separate locations this morning.

During the group discussions in the past few days, some deputies raised some opinions and questions regarding the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme, such as the order and names of the phonetic letters, whether learning the Chinese phonetic alphabet is difficult, the phonetic scheme and dialect issues, etc. Wang Li, a member of the Chinese Writing Reform Committee, gave detailed answers to these questions, which were generally satisfactory to the deputies.

In the other two locations, Lü Shuxiang, a member of the Chinese Writing Reform Committee, explained the pronunciation and spelling of the letters in the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme to more than two hundred deputies; Fu Maoji, a member of the Chinese Writing Reform Committee, explained the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme to some representatives of ethnic minorities.

### 文字改革委员会的代表  向人大代表讲解汉语拼音方案

1958-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　文字改革委员会的代表
    向人大代表讲解汉语拼音方案
    新华社7日讯　出席第一届全国人民代表大会第五次会议的一部分代表，7日上午分三处听了文字改革委员会的代表讲解汉语拼音方案和解答问题。
    在前几天的小组讨论中，部分代表对于汉语拼音方案提出了一些意见和问题，如拼音字母的顺序和名称问题、汉语拼音的学习是否困难、拼音方案和方言问题等等，中国文字改革委员会委员王力对这些问题作了详细的解答，代表们一般都认为满意。
    在另外两处，中国文字改革委员会委员吕叔湘对二百多位代表讲解了汉语拼音方案字母的读法和拼法；文字改革委员会委员傅懋绩对部分兄弟民族代表讲解了汉语拼音方案。
