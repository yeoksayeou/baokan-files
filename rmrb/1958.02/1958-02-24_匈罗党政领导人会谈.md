Title: Hungarian-Romanian Party and Government Leaders Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1958-02-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, February 22nd: The Hungarian government and Socialist Workers' Party delegation headed by Kádár, visited Romanian Council of Ministers Chairman Stoica in Bucharest on the 21st.

On the same day, the delegation was received by Stoica, Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee First Secretary Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Great National Assembly Presidium Chairman Maurer, and other Romanian party and government leaders. During the meeting, the two sides held lengthy talks.

On the afternoon of the 21st, Gheorghiu-Dej hosted a luncheon in honor of the delegation.

### 匈罗党政领导人会谈

1958-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    匈罗党政领导人会谈
    新华社布加勒斯特22日电　以卡达尔为首的匈牙利政府和社会主义工人党代表团，21日在布加勒斯特拜会了罗马尼亚部长会议主席斯托伊卡。
    同一天，代表团受到了斯托伊卡、罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治乌—德治、大国民议会主席团主席毛雷尔以及罗马尼亚党和政府的其他领导人的接见。接见时，双方进行了长时间的交谈。
    21日下午，乔治乌—德治举行午宴，招待代表团。
