Title: Soviet-Vietnamese Sign Book and Newspaper Trade Contract
Author:
Date: 1958-02-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 27th: According to sources from the Vietnam Book and Newspaper Import-Export Bureau, based on the 1958 Soviet-Vietnamese book and newspaper trade contract signed in Hanoi on the 26th between the Vietnam Book and Newspaper Import-Export Bureau and the Soviet International Bookstore, this year Vietnam will import more Soviet science and technology books than last year in order to meet the growing demand of Vietnamese readers to learn from the advanced Soviet science and technology experience.

### 苏越签订书报贸易合同

1958-02-28
第7版()
专栏：

    苏越签订书报贸易合同
    据新华社河内27日电　据越南书报进出口局人士说，根据越南书报进出口局和苏联国际书店26日在河内签订的1958年越苏两国书报贸易合同，今年越南将比去年输入更多的苏联科学技术书籍，以满足越南读者日益增长的学习苏联先进科学技术经验的要求。
