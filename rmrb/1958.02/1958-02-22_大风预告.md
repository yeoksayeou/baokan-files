Title: Gale Forecast
Author:
Date: 1958-02-22
Page: 7
Meteorological Observatory

    Gale Forecast
    This newspaper reports, at 8:00 PM on the 21st, the Central Meteorological Science Research Institute released a gale message: It is expected that in the next twenty-four hours, northern Xinjiang, Gansu, northern Shaanxi, Shanxi, and the western and central parts of Inner Mongolia will experience northwesterly winds of level 6-7. Hebei, northern Henan, Shandong, the southern Northeast Plain, and the southern Yellow Sea will change from southwesterly winds of level 5-6 to northwesterly winds of around level 6. The Bohai Sea and northern Yellow Sea will change from easterly winds of around level 6 to northerly winds of around level 7. Southern Henan, northern Anhui, and northern Jiangsu will change from southwesterly winds of level 5-6 to northwesterly winds of level 5-6.

### 大风预告

1958-02-22
第7版()
专栏：气象台

    大风预告
    本报讯　21日晚八时中央气象科学研究所发布大风消息：预计未来二十四小时内，新疆北部、甘肃、陕西北部、山西、内蒙古西部、中部、将有六、七级西北风。河北、河南北部、山东、东北平原南部、黄海南部、将由五、六级西南风转为六级左右的西北风。渤海、黄海北部将由六级左右的偏东风转为七级左右的偏北风。河南南部、安徽北部、江苏北部将由五、六级西南风转为五、六级西北风。
