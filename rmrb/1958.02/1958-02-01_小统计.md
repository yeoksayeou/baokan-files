　　Small Statistics
    * The total length of irrigation canals in the Soviet Union now reaches 400,000 kilometers. The largest canal has a water discharge capacity of 220 cubic meters per second, greatly exceeding the flow of the River Thames in England.
    * During the Soviet era, the Soviet Union built 106 large reservoirs with a total storage capacity of 68.4 billion cubic meters.
    * In terms of the total area of irrigated land, the Soviet Union ranks third in the world—behind India and China.

### 小统计

1958-02-01
第6版()
专栏：小统计

　　小统计
    *现在苏联全国灌溉沟渠的总长度达四十万公里，其中最大沟渠的放水能力达到每秒二百二十立方公尺，大大超过英国泰晤士河的流量。
    *在苏维埃政权年代，苏联修建了一百零六个大水库，总储水量达六百八十四亿立方公尺。
    *按灌溉土地的总面积，苏联居世界第三位——次于印度和中国。
