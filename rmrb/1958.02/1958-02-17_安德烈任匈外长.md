Title: András Sik Appointed Hungarian Foreign Minister
Author: 
Date: 1958-02-17
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, February 16th: According to a report by the Hungarian News Agency on the 15th, the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic has appointed András Sik as Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 安德烈任匈外长

1958-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    安德烈任匈外长
    新华社布达佩斯16日电　据匈牙利通讯社15日报道，匈牙利人民共和国主席团已经任命西克·安德烈为外交部长。
