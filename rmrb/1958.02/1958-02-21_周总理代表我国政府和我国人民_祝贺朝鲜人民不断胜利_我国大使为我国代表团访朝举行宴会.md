Title: Premier Zhou, on behalf of the Chinese government and people, congratulates the Korean people on their continuous victories. Chinese Ambassador hosts a banquet for the Chinese delegation's visit to Korea.
Author: 
Date: 1958-02-21
Page: 1
	
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th – Premier Kim Il-sung and Premier Zhou Enlai attended a banquet held by Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, Qiao Xiaoguang, this evening and delivered speeches. They congratulated the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples for its eternal duration, increasing consolidation, and continuous development.

	The banquet was held to celebrate the visit of the Chinese government delegation to Korea.

	Premier Zhou said in his speech: The Chinese government delegation will conclude its visit to Korea tomorrow. "We will never forget every moment we spent together with the Korean people in the past week."

	Premier Zhou said: We will certainly bring the great fraternal friendship of the Korean people back to the Chinese people. We will also bring back to the Chinese people the various advanced experiences of the Korean people in socialist construction. On behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people, Premier Zhou wished the Korean people new and continuous victories in the cause of building socialism and in the struggle for the peaceful reunification of the motherland.

	Premier Kim Il-sung said in his speech that the Korean people, in the socialist family headed by the Soviet Union, will forever stand together with the fraternal Chinese people.

	Kim Il-sung said with full confidence: "No matter how great the difficulties, the Korean people will surely realize the peaceful reunification of the motherland. The Korean people will always stand firmly on the eastern frontier, defending peace and socialism.

	Premier Kim Il-sung pointed out that the decision of the People's Republic of China that the Chinese People's Volunteers will completely withdraw from Korea before the end of 1958 "is a manifestation of the Chinese government's consistent and sincere efforts to peacefully resolve the Korean issue."

	He emphasized: "The occupation of South Korea by American troops is the fundamental reason for threatening our country's peace and hindering our country's peaceful reunification."

	"The American aggressors cannot legitimize the presence of their troops in South Korea with any excuse."

	Premier Kim Il-sung added: "We strongly demand that the American troops withdraw from South Korea. The governments of all other countries participating in the 'United Nations Forces' should also take measures as soon as possible to withdraw their troops from South Korea." "If they have the slightest sincerity in the peaceful settlement of the Korean issue, they should withdraw their troops from South Korea."

	Premier Kim Il-sung's speech was received with extremely warm applause.

	The grand banquet began at 7:40 p.m. at the Chinese Embassy in North Korea.

	Among the guests were Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Kim Il, Hong Myung-hee, Nam Il, Jung Joon-taek, Park Ui-wan, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party Park Jung-ae, Kim Chang-man, Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Party Hong Ki-hwang, Minister of National Defense General Kim Kwang-hyup, as well as ministers of the Cabinet, ministers of various departments of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, heads of various democratic parties and social organizations, prominent figures in society, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea.

	At the beginning of the banquet, Ambassador Qiao Xiaoguang delivered a welcome speech. The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere full of friendship. Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi recited two poems he wrote during his visit to Korea at the banquet. The poems praised the achievements of the Korean people in restoration and construction. The recitation was warmly welcomed.

	The Naval Political Department Song and Dance Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, which came to Korea with the Chinese People's Goodwill Mission, performed many wonderful programs during the banquet.

	General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General Wang Ping, Political Commissar, and the head of the General Delegation of the Chinese People's Goodwill Mission to Korea also attended the banquet.

### 周总理代表我国政府和我国人民  祝贺朝鲜人民不断胜利  我国大使为我国代表团访朝举行宴会

1958-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理代表我国政府和我国人民
    祝贺朝鲜人民不断胜利
    我国大使为我国代表团访朝举行宴会
    新华社平壤20日电　金日成首相和周恩来总理今天晚上出席了中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光举行的宴会，并且讲了话。他们祝贺中朝两国人民兄弟般的友谊万古长青，日益巩固，不断发展。
    宴会是为庆祝中国政府代表团访问朝鲜而举行的。
    周总理在讲话中说：中国政府代表团明天就要结束在朝鲜的访问。“我们将永远不会忘记在过去的一星期里我们同朝鲜人民欢聚在一起的每一时刻”。
    周总理说：我们一定会把朝鲜人民兄弟般的伟大友谊带回给中国人民。我们也要把朝鲜人民在社会主义建设中的各种先进经验带回给中国人民。周总理代表中国政府和中国人民，祝朝鲜人民在建设社会主义的事业中和和平统一祖国的斗争中取得新的、不断的胜利。
    金日成首相在讲话中说，朝鲜人民在以苏联为首的社会主义大家庭中，将永远和兄弟般的中国人民站在一起。
    金日成满怀信心地说：“朝鲜人民不管有多大困难，一定会实现祖国的和平统一。朝鲜人民将永远坚定地站在东方前哨上，捍卫和平和社会主义。
    金日成首相指出，中华人民共和国关于中国人民志愿军在1958年年底以前全部撤出朝鲜的决定，“是中国政府始终一贯为朝鲜问题的和平解决而作出的真挚努力的表现。”
    他强调指出：“美国军队的强占南朝鲜，是威胁我国的和平和阻挠我国和平统一的基本原因。”
    “美国侵略者用任何借口也不可能使他们的军队在南朝鲜的驻扎合法化。”
    金日成首相又说：“我们强烈地要求美国军队撤出南朝鲜。参加‘联合国军’的其他所有国家的政府，也应该尽速采取措施，从南朝鲜撤出自己的军队。”“如果它们对于朝鲜问题的和平解决有丝毫的诚意，它们就应当从南朝鲜撤出它们的军队。”
    金日成首相的讲话受到了极其热烈的鼓掌欢迎。
    盛大的宴会是在晚上七时四十分在中国驻朝鲜大使馆开始的。
    来宾中有朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健，内阁副首相金一、洪命熹、南日、郑准泽、朴义琓，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、金昌满，北朝鲜民主党中央委员会委员长洪箕璜，民族保卫相金光侠大将以及内阁各相、劳动党中央各部部长，各民主政党和社会团体的负责人，社会著名人士和各国驻朝鲜使节等。
    宴会开始的时候，乔晓光大使致了欢迎词。宴会一直在充满了友谊的气氛中进行。陈毅副总理兼外交部长在宴会上朗诵了他访问朝鲜时写的两首诗，诗词赞扬了朝鲜人民恢复建设的成就。朗诵受到热烈的欢迎。
    随中国人民赴朝慰问团来朝鲜慰问的中国人民解放军海军政治部文工团等在宴会进行中表演了许多精彩节目。
    中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员王平上将和中国人民赴朝慰问团总团的负责人也出席了宴会。
