Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1958-02-11
Page: 3

    Order of the State Council of the People's Republic of China

    The "Provisional Regulations of the State Council on the Retirement of Workers and Staff," "Provisional Regulations of the State Council on the Wage Treatment of Ordinary Workers and Handymen in Enterprises, Institutions, and State Organs," "Provisional Regulations of the State Council on the Length of Apprenticeship and Living Allowances for Apprentices in State-owned, Joint State-Private, Cooperative, and Individually-operated Enterprises and Institutions," and "Provisional Regulations of the State Council on the Holidays and Wage Treatment for Workers and Staff Returning Home to Visit Relatives" were, after being approved in principle as drafts by the 85th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on November 16, 1957, organized by the provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government, and various departments for discussion by employees, with more than 3.1 million people participating in the discussions. Based on the opinions of employees in various regions, research and revisions were made, and then passed by the 70th plenary meeting of the State Council on February 6, 1958, and are now promulgated for implementation.
      Premier of the State Council　Zhou Enlai　February 9, 1958

### 中华人民共和国国务院命令
周恩来
1958-02-11
第3版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国国务院命令
    “国务院关于工人、职员退休处理的暂行规定”、“国务院关于企业、事业单位和国家机关中普通工和勤杂工的工资待遇的暂行规定”、“国务院关于国营、公私合营、合作社营、个体经营的企业和事业单位的学徒的学习期限和生活补贴的暂行规定”和“国务院关于工人、职员回家探亲的假期和工资待遇的暂行规定”在一九五七年十一月十六日经过全国人民代表大会常务委员会第八十五次会议作为草案原则批准后，由各省、自治区、直辖市和各部门组织职工进行了讨论，参加讨论的有三百一十余万人。根据各地职工讨论的意见作了研究修改，又经过一九五八年二月六日国务院全体会议第七十次会议通过，现在公布施行。
      国务院总理　周恩来　一九五八年二月九日
