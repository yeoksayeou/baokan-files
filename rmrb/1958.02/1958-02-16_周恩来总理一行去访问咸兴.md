Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Entourage Visit Hamhung
Author:
Date: 1958-02-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 15th: Premier Zhou Enlai and the Chinese Government Delegation he leads, accompanied by Premier Kim Il Sung, left Pyongyang by train tonight to visit Hamhung.

Tonight, the Pyongyang station was brilliantly lit, and the newly built station building was hung with portraits of Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Kim Il Sung, as well as huge Chinese and Korean national flags. Those who saw them off at the station included Korean Vice Premiers Kim Il, Hong Myung-hui, and Jung Joon-taek, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Park Jung-ae and Kim Chang-man, and other Korean party and government leaders, as well as General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General Wang Ping, Political Commissar, and personnel from the Chinese Embassy.

After Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and General Su Yu shook hands with those seeing them off, they boarded the train and headed for Hamhung.

Also accompanying the delegation were Korean Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il, Korean People's Army Chief of Staff General Lee Kwon-mu, and Kim Kwi-nam, Director of Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, among others.

### 周恩来总理一行去访问咸兴

1958-02-16
第2版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理一行去访问咸兴
    新华社平壤15日电　周恩来总理和他率领的中国政府代表团今天晚上由金日成首相陪同，乘火车离开平壤，前往咸兴参观访问。
    今天晚上平壤车站上灯火辉煌，新建成的车站大楼上高悬着毛泽东主席和金日成首相的画像和巨幅的中朝两国国旗。到车站送行的有朝鲜内阁副首相金一、洪命熹、郑准泽，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、金昌满等朝鲜党政领导人和中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员王平上将以及中国大使馆人员。
    周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长、张闻天副部长、粟裕大将同送行者握手告别后，登车向咸兴进发。
    陪同代表团前往的还有朝鲜内阁副首相兼外务相南日、朝鲜人民军总参谋长李权武上将，外务省仪礼部长金贵南等。
