Title: Chinese People's Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang to Express Sympathy

Author: 
Date: 1958-02-14
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 13th: The general delegation of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea arrived in Pyongyang by train this morning and was warmly welcomed by more than 2,000 people in Pyongyang.

Among those who greeted the delegation at Pyongyang Station were An Mo, Vice Minister of Education and Culture of Korea, Kim Tae-geun, Lieutenant General and Director of the Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, and generals of the Korean People's Army, and Yoon Sung-bok, Vice Chairman of the Korean Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Wang Yongnian, Colonel and representative of the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers, also came to the station to welcome them. Cao Keqiang, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, and embassy staff also came to the station to welcome the delegation.

An Mo, Vice Minister of Education and Culture of Korea, delivered a welcome speech at the station, expressing a warm welcome to the delegation.

He said that the delegation's activities in Korea would make a great contribution to the further development and consolidation of the friendship between the people of Korea and China.

Wang Yifu, the head of the delegation, then spoke, praising the brilliant achievements of the Korean people in the struggle for the peaceful reunification of their country and the construction of socialism.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 13th: More than 800 representatives from all walks of life in Pyongyang held a welcome rally at the Moranbong Theatre this evening to welcome the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea.

Kim Yong-jin, Chairman of the Pyongyang Municipal Committee, expressed a warm welcome to the delegation in his speech.

Wang Yifu, the head of the delegation, then spoke at the meeting, expressing his gratitude for the warm welcome and reception from the people of Pyongyang. He said that the Korean people's struggle against aggression is a great support to the Chinese people's peaceful construction, especially the care and love shown by the Korean people to the Chinese People's Volunteers over the years, which deserves the deep gratitude of the Chinese people.

### 中国人民赴朝慰问团到平壤

1958-02-14
第7版()
专栏：

    中国人民赴朝慰问团到平壤
    据新华社平壤13日电　中国人民赴朝慰问团总团今天上午乘火车到达平壤，受到平壤市二千多群众的热烈欢迎。
    到平壤车站欢迎慰问团的有朝鲜教育文化省副相安漠，朝鲜人民军政治局局长金泰根中将和朝鲜人民军将领，朝鲜对外文化联络协会副委员长尹成福等。到车站欢迎的还有中国人民志愿军总部代表王永年上校。中国驻朝鲜大使馆参赞曹克强和大使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
    朝鲜教育文化省副相安漠在车站上致欢迎词，他对慰问团表示热烈欢迎。
    他说，慰问团在朝鲜的活动将对进一步发展和巩固朝中两国人民之间的友谊作出巨大贡献。
    王一夫总团长接着讲话，他赞扬了朝鲜人民在争取祖国和平统一和建设社会主义的斗争中取得的光辉成就。
    据新华社平壤13日电　平壤市各界代表八百多人，今天晚上在牡丹峰剧场举行欢迎大会，欢迎中国人民赴朝慰问团。
    平壤市委员会委员长金容镇在讲话中，对慰问团表示热烈欢迎。
    慰问团总团长王一夫接着在会上讲话，他对平壤市人民的热情欢迎和接待表示感谢。他说，朝鲜人民所进行的反抗侵略的斗争，对中国人民的和平建设事业是一个莫大的支持，特别是朝鲜人民几年来对中国人民志愿军的关怀爱护，这些都是值得中国人民深切感谢的。
