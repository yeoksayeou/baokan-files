Title: Japanese Foreign Minister Proposes Convening Asian Foreign Ministers Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-02-09
Page: 7

    Japanese Foreign Minister Proposes
    Convening Asian Foreign Ministers Conference
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 7th: Japanese Foreign Minister Aiichiro Fujiyama said at a press conference on February 4th that he had suggested convening a meeting of Asian foreign ministers when he met with Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio in Jakarta on January 21st. In addition, he also conveyed this suggestion to the Indian government through the Indian Ambassador to Japan.
    Subandrio issued a statement to the press on February 1st, expressing his agreement with Fujiyama Aiichiro's above-mentioned proposal.

### 日本外务相建议  召开亚洲外长会议

1958-02-09
第7版()
专栏：

    日本外务相建议
    召开亚洲外长会议
    据新华社7日讯　东京消息：日本外务相藤山爱一郎4日在记者招待会上说，他在1月21日在雅加达同印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约会谈时，曾经建议召开一次亚洲国家的外交部长会议。此外，他还曾经把这项建议通过印度驻日本的大使转达给印度政府。
    苏班德里约在1日曾经向报界发表声明，表示同意藤山爱一郎的上述建议。
