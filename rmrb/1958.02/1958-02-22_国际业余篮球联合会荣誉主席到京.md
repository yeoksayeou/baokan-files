Title: Honorary President of the International Amateur Basketball Federation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-02-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 21st -- At the invitation of the All-China Sports Federation, Mr. Léon Bouffard, Honorary Life President of the International Amateur Basketball Federation, and his wife arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 20th.

During their visit to China, Mr. and Mrs. Léon Bouffard will learn about the development of sports in New China and visit the achievements of New China's construction.

### 国际业余篮球联合会荣誉主席到京

1958-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    国际业余篮球联合会荣誉主席到京
    新华社21日讯　应中华全国体育总会邀请来我国访问的国际业余篮球联合会终身荣誉主席列昂·布法尔和他的夫人，于20日下午乘火车到达北京。
    列昂·布法尔夫妇在我国访问期间，将了解新中国体育运动的发展情况和参观新中国建设成就。
