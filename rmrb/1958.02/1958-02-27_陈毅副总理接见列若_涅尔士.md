Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Rezső Nyers

Author:
Date: 1958-02-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received all members of the Hungarian cooperative delegation led by Rezső Nyers, Chairman of the Hungarian Cooperative Federation, this morning.

Present at the reception were Zhang Qilong and Guo Xianrui, Deputy Directors of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives.

Also present at the reception was Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nográdi.

### 陈毅副总理接见列若·涅尔士

1958-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见列若·涅尔士
    新华社26日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天上午接见了以匈牙利合作社联合社主席列若·涅尔士为首的匈牙利合作社代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有中华全国供销合作总社副主任张启龙、郭献瑞等。
    接见时，匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第也在座。
