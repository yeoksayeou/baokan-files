Title: French Troops Again Invade Tunisian Territory, Provoke
Author:
Date: 1958-02-14
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, February 13th: A Tunisian official communiqué released late on February 12th stated that French troops stationed in Algeria once again violated the Tunisian border on February 9th, intruding into a small Tunisian village fifteen miles from the border. During this border crossing incident, French troops killed two Tunisian civilians and illegally searched shops and residences in the village.

### 法军又侵入突境挑衅

1958-02-14
第7版()
专栏：

    法军又侵入突境挑衅
    新华社13日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯官方12日深夜发表公报说，驻阿尔及利亚法军部队9日又一次侵犯突尼斯国境，侵入一个离开边境十五英里的突尼斯小村庄。在这次越境事件中，法军打死了两名突尼斯平民，并且非法地搜查了这个村庄里的店铺和住宅。
