Title: Chen Yi Receives Mongolian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-02-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 31st. Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received the Mongolian cultural delegation headed by Mongolian Minister of Culture Sosorbaram in Beijing on the afternoon of January 31st.

Present at the meeting were delegation member and Mongolian Ambassador to China, Lubusan; delegation member and Vice Chairman of the Mongolian Committee of Science and Higher Education, Yalinmubier; and delegation members Muzhamuyangdaoerji, Heshkete, and Luoqin.

Also present at the meeting on the Chinese side were Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan and Deputy Director of the Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Liu Daosheng, and others.

### 陈毅接见蒙古文化代表团

1958-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　陈毅接见蒙古文化代表团
    新华社31日讯　国务院副总理陈毅1月31日下午在北京接见了以蒙古人民共和国文化部部长索苏尔巴拉木为首的蒙古文化代表团。
    接见时，代表团团员、蒙古驻中国大使鲁布桑，团员、蒙古科学和高等教育委员会副主席雅林木毕尔和团员木扎姆杨道尔吉、和什克特、罗钦等人都在座。
    接见时，中国方面在座的有文化部副部长夏衍和中国科学院哲学社会科学部副主任刘导生等人。
