Title: German-Chinese Association Chairman Shen Ke Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-02-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 3rd. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Shen Ke, Chairman of the German (West German) - Chinese Association, arrived in Beijing this afternoon for a visit to China.

### 德中协会主席沈克到北京

1958-02-04
第2版()
专栏：

    德中协会主席沈克到北京
    新华社3日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的德（西德）中协会主席沈克，今天下午到达北京。
