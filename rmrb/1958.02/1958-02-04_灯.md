Title: Lamp
Author: Li Ying
Date: 1958-02-04
Page: 8

Column: Poems from the Countryside

　　Lamp
　　Li Ying
　　A cluster of lamps east of the village,
　　Illuminating a group of figures;
　　A cluster of lamps west of the village,
　　Illuminating a group of figures.
　　East of the village, digging river mud,
　　Aimed at accumulating four million catties of fertilizer;
　　West of the village, building scaffolding,
　　To expand the irrigated land, striking ten wells.
　　Shovels and pulleys are in competition,
　　Wagons full of mud mixed with wagons full of laughter,
　　This is us studying the "Forty Articles,"
　　The whip cracks, whipping away the stars in the sky.
　　Oh, night shaking off the frost,
　　Oh, night soaked with sweat,
　　Zhangjia Village, I see you,
　　You are so ancient and yet so young.
　　You are a happy bird,
　　These two patches of light are wings, carrying you soaring.
　　Ah! Motherland—my proud mother,
　　Waiting for the rising sun, how many more miles will you leap forward!

### 灯
李瑛
1958-02-04
第8版()
专栏：来自农村的诗

    灯
　　李瑛
　  村东一片灯，
    照着一群人影；
    村西一片灯，
    照着一群人影。
    村东挖河泥，
    要积肥四百万斤；
    村西在搭架子，
    要扩大水浇地，打出十眼井。
    铁锹和滑车在竞赛，
    满车泥拌满车笑声，
    这就是我们在读“四十条”，
    鞭梢响，抽掉了满天的星星。
    抖落了霜的夜哟，
    淋湿了汗的夜哟，
    张家村，我看见了你，
    你那样古老又那样年轻。
    你是一只快乐的鸟，
    这两片灯光是翅膀，载你飞腾。
    呵！祖国——我骄傲的母亲，
    等旭日升起，你又跃进了多少路程！
