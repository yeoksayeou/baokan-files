　　Gentlemanly Dealings

　　America's "peacemaker" stance regarding the French Air Force's bombing of a Tunisian village cannot hide the two knives it has drawn.

　　Associated Press reporter Grover said in a dispatch that the United States is using this incident to "inject some Western influence," "take over French bases," replace France in providing "aid" to Tunisia, and expand its influence in North Africa. This is how the United States entered Indochina when France was wrestling there.

　　Grover sees this shameful American tactic of "filling the vacuum" as very gentlemanly, saying it's like "carrying water on both shoulders at the same time." Naturally, there is a basis for speaking so gentlemanly, because the United States always pretends to be very gentlemanly when engaging in ugly dealings.

### 斯文的勾当

1958-02-20
第3版()
专栏：札记

    斯文的勾当
    美国对法国空军轰炸突尼斯村庄事件装出的“和事佬”姿态，并不能掩住它亮出的两把刀子。
    美联社记者格罗弗的一条消息说，美国正在利用这一事件“补充进去一些西方的影响”，“接管法国基地”，代替法国对突尼斯的“援助”，扩大它在北非的势力。当法国在印度支那摔跤的时候，美国就是这样进入那里的。
    格罗弗把美国这个“填补真空”的可耻手法，看得很斯文，说这是“用两个肩膀同时挑水”。自然，说得斯文一些，也是有根据的，因为，美国干丑恶勾当时总是装得很斯文的。
