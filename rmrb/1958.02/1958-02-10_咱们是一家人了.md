Title: We are all one family
Author: Huang Yanpei
Date: 1958-02-10
Page: 8

　　We are all one family
　　Huang Yanpei
    (The following poem is based on phrases from a letter dated December 28, 1957, from Meng Zhengxiang, a cadre sent to work at the Flame Agricultural Cooperative in Xingtai County.)
　　One
　　I have started working.
　　I live in Qianluzi Village.
　　The cooperative arranged everything well for me,
　　I embraced the members,
　　Chatting and laughing,
　　We are all one family now.
　　Closely united, becoming one with each other.
　　Accepting the leadership of the Communist Party,
　　Everyone is embarking on the bright road of socialism.
　　Two
　　Farmers plant whatever they eat,
　　Usually millet is ground into flour, eating their fill every day.
　　Supporting the country's industrial construction,
　　They also sell quite a bit of surplus grain.
　　Now we are eating rice.
　　Everyone cheers:
　　Thanks to Chairman Mao's blessings,
　　Thanks to the leadership of the Communist Party.
　　Three
　　Carrying soil all day long, not feeling distressed,
　　The right shoulder is pressed red and swollen,
　　But now the difficulty has been overcome.
　　The township head said to me:
　　It's still early,
　　To lay a foundation for future busy farming seasons,
　　We must also train our hands to be rough.
　　Four
　　I am a new farmer now.
　　Farmers are hardworking and simple,
　　Treating people sincerely and thoughtfully,
　　Their class stance is clear.
　　Greatly helping me transform.
　　I humbly learn from the farmers:
　　Learning the farmers' class essence;
　　Learning agricultural production techniques.
　　Through labor and training,
　　Radically transforming myself.
　　Everyone brings out revolutionary vigor,
　　Setting off a high tide of agricultural production.

### 咱们是一家人了
黄炎培
1958-02-10
第8版()
专栏：

　　咱们是一家人了
　　黄炎培
    （邢台县火焰农业合作社下放干部孟征祥1957年12月28日来信，节取它的词句写成一首诗。）
　　一
　　我开始干活了。
　　我在前炉子村住。
　　社里为我布置得很好，
　　我和社员们拥抱，
　　有说有笑，
　　咱们是一家人了。
　　紧紧结合，打成一片。
　　接受共产党的领导，
　　大家走上社会主义光明大道。
　　二
　　农民是种什么、吃什么的，
　　平时小米磨面，天天吃饱。
　　支援国家工业建设，
　　还卖出余粮不少。
　　现在吃到大米饭了。
　　大家欢呼著：
　　靠毛主席的福，
　　靠共产党领导。
　　三
　　整天抬土，不觉得苦恼，
　　右肩压得又红又肿，
　　现在困难已经克服了。
　　乡长对我说：
　　时候还早，
　　为今后农忙打下基础，
　　还要把双手锻炼得粗糙。
　　四
　　我做新农民了。
　　农民艰苦朴素，
　　待人诚恳周到，
　　阶级立场是鲜明的。
　　大大地帮助我改造。
　　我虚心向农民学习：
　　学习农民的阶级本质；
　　学习农业生产技术。
　　经过劳动锻炼，
　　脱胎换骨地改造。
　　大家拿出革命干劲，
　　掀起农业生产高潮。
