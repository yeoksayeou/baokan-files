Title: Central Committee Leaders of the CPC Receive Representatives of Two Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1958-02-07
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 5th. Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Chen Yi, He Long, and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, received the representatives attending the Second National Congress of the China Highway Transportation Trade Union and the representatives attending the First National Congress of the China Agricultural Water Conservancy Trade Union on the afternoon of February 5th.

### 中共中央领导人接见两个工会的代表

1958-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　中共中央领导人接见两个工会的代表
    新华社5日讯　中共中央副主席朱德，中央政治局委员陈毅、贺龙、李先念，在5日下午接见了出席中国公路运输工会第二次全国代表大会的代表和出席中国农业水利工会第一次全国代表大会的代表。
