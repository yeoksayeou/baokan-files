Title: International Mothers' Standing Committee to Hold Meeting in Sofia
Author:
Date: 1958-02-15
Page: 5

Brief News

    International Mothers' Standing Committee to Hold Meeting in Sofia
    Sofia News: The Chairman of the Bulgarian Democratic Women's Committee, Rada Todorova, told the press that the Third Meeting of the International Mothers' Standing Committee will be held in Sofia from February 20th to 22nd. Representatives from more than thirty countries will attend the meeting to jointly discuss issues related to the prohibition of the testing, production, and use of atomic and hydrogen weapons, strengthening the ties between mothers of all countries, and educating children in the spirit of justice, friendship, and peace.

### 国际母亲常设委员会将在索非亚举行会议

1958-02-15
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    国际母亲常设委员会将在索非亚举行会议
    索非亚讯：保加利亚民主妇女委员会主席拉达·托多罗娃对报界发表谈话说，国际母亲常设委员会第三次会议将在2月20日到22日在索非亚举行。将有三十多个国家的代表出席这次会议，共同商讨有关禁止试验、生产和使用原子和氢武器，加强各国母亲间的联系，用正义、友谊和和平精神教育儿童等问题。
