Title: Guangdong Governor Chen Yu and CPPCC Chairman Tao Zhu Receive Heads of Japanese Commodity Exhibition

Author:

Date: 1958-02-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 9th. Guangdong Governor Chen Yu and Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), Tao Zhu, received Yamamoto Kumaichi, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association and President of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition, and Mori Shonai, Deputy Head of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Group, at 6:00 p.m. on February 8th, and hosted a banquet for them. At the banquet, Governor Chen Yu expressed his pleasure at the exhibition being held in Guangzhou, and raised his glass in a toast to Sino-Japanese friendship.

After the banquet, Chen Yu and Tao Zhu, accompanied by Yamamoto Kumaichi and others, visited the Japanese Commodity Exhibition.

### 广东省长陈郁和政协主席陶铸接见日本商品展览会负责人

1958-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    广东省长陈郁和政协主席陶铸
    接见日本商品展览会负责人
    据新华社广州9日电　广东省省长陈郁和中国人民政治协商会议广东省委员会主席陶铸，8日下午六时接见了日本商品展览会在广州的负责人日本国际贸易促进协会会长、日本商品展览会会长山本熊一、日本商品展览团副团长森井庄内等，并设宴招待了他们。在宴会上，陈郁省长表示对这次展览会在广州展出感到很高兴，并举杯为中日友好祝贺。
    宴会后，陈郁、陶铸在山本熊一等陪同下，参观了日本商品展览会。
