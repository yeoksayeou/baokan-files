Title: Three Marshals, Biryuzov and Others, Awarded the Title of Hero of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-02-03
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 1st: TASS reported from Moscow on the 1st that the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union awarded the title of Hero of the Soviet Union to Soviet Marshals Biryuzov and Grechko, in recognition of their wise command of the troops and the fearlessness and heroism they displayed in the struggle against the German fascist invaders during the Great Patriotic War.
    At the same time, Marshal Budyonny was also awarded the title of Hero of the Soviet Union, in recognition of his outstanding contributions to the creation of the Soviet armed forces and in the struggle to defend the Soviet state, as well as the heroism he displayed.

### 比留佐夫等三位元帅  荣获苏联英雄称号

1958-02-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　比留佐夫等三位元帅
    荣获苏联英雄称号
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社莫斯科1日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团授予苏联元帅比留佐夫和格列奇科以苏联英雄称号，以表彰他们英明地统率军队和在伟大卫国战争时期同德国法西斯侵略者斗争中所表现的大无畏精神和英勇行为。
    同时，还授予布琼尼元帅以苏联英雄称号，以表彰他在创立苏联武装力量的事业中和在保卫苏维埃国家的斗争中的卓越功绩以及他所表现的英勇精神。
