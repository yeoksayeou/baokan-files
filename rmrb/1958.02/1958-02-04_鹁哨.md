Title: Whistle
Author: Bamu
Date: 1958-02-04
Page: 8

　　Whistle
　　Bamu
　　I love the northern sky,
　　Blue and captivating like the sea,
　　Walking on the flat road,
　　Feels like sailing on the sea.
　　The guides are not signposts or lighthouses,
　　But the white pigeon flocks that fill the sky;
　　The crisp whistle sound comes from far to near,
　　A moment later, it's gone without a trace.
　　This sound is a string of youthful odes,
　　It pours out: "Peace! Peace!"
　　It sounds not only in the East, but also in the West,
　　Not only in China, but also belongs to the people of the whole world.

### 鹁哨
巴牧
1958-02-04
第8版()
专栏：

　　鹁哨
　　巴牧
　　我爱北方的天空，
　　蓝得像海水一样迷人，
　　行走在平坦的路上，
　　像置身在海上航行。
　　领航的不是标杆、灯塔，
　　是那满天的白色鹁群；
　　清脆的哨音由远而近，
　　一会儿却又无声无影。
　　这声音是一串青春的颂歌，
　　它倾吐出：“和平！和平！”
　　它不仅响在东方，也响在西方，
　　不仅在中国，也属于全世界人民。
