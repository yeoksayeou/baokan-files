Title: Academy of Sciences' Chief Advisor Razarenko Returns to the Soviet Union; Premier Zhou Confers Letter of Appreciation, President Guo Hosts Farewell Banquet
Author:
Date: 1958-02-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Professor Razarenko, the Soviet Chief Advisor to the Chinese Academy of Sciences, after working diligently in our country for two years, will depart for the Soviet Union on the 12th. On the evening of the 11th, Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, held a farewell banquet. Before the banquet, Guo Moruo, on behalf of Premier Zhou, presented Razarenko with a letter of appreciation and a Sino-Soviet Friendship Medal. Guo Moruo also presented Razarenko with a letter of appreciation and a thank-you letter on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Attending the banquet were Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, Soviet experts working at the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and heads of various departments of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 科学院总顾问拉扎连柯回苏联  周总理授予感谢状　郭院长设宴欢送

1958-02-12
第2版()
专栏：

    科学院总顾问拉扎连柯回苏联
    周总理授予感谢状　郭院长设宴欢送
    新华社11日讯　中国科学院苏联总顾问拉扎连柯教授在我国辛勤工作了两年以后，将在12日动身返苏。11日晚上，中国科学院院长郭沫若举行了欢送宴会。宴会前郭沫若代表周总理授予拉扎连柯感谢状和中苏友谊纪念章。郭沫若又代表中国科学院授予拉扎连柯感谢状和感谢信。
    出席宴会作陪的有：苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫和在中国科学院帮助工作的苏联专家和中国科学院各部门负责人等。
