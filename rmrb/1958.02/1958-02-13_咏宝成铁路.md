Title: Ode to the Baoji-Chengdu Railway
Author: Lü Zhengcao
Date: 1958-02-13
Page: 8

    Ode to the Baoji-Chengdu Railway
    Lü Zhengcao
    I.
    Moving mountains and altering rivers at will, cutting and shaping,
    Iron ladders and stone embankments open a straight line.
    The Qinling Mountains bow, clouds lead the way,
    Eagerly watching the fire dragon fly down from the heavens.

    II.
    Millions in Chengdu smile with joy,
    Celebrating that the Shu Road is no longer difficult.
    Ten thousand miles traversed in an instant,
    Overloaded, heading down to the Central Plains.

### 咏宝成铁路
吕正操
1958-02-13
第8版()
专栏：

    咏宝成铁路
    吕正操
    一
    移山改河任剪裁，铁梯石堑一线开，
    秦岭折腰云带路，争看火龙天上来。
    二
    锦城百万齐开颜，欢庆蜀道不再难，
    万里周流一息至，超轴满载下中原。
