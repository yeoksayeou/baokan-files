Title: Our Acrobatic Troupe's Performances in Morocco Receive Enthusiastic Acclaim
Author:
Date: 1958-02-18
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 17th - The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe's performances in Morocco have been warmly received by the audience. Due to popular demand, the troupe has added nine performances from February 11th to 16th.
    The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe arrived in Morocco on January 17th. From January 18th to February 2nd, the troupe performed twenty shows in Casablanca, with nearly 50,000 audience members.
    On February 4th, the acrobatic troupe performed for the King and royal family at the Moroccan Royal Palace. The King praised the performance highly and exchanged gifts with Zhang Tiesheng, the head of the acrobatic troupe.
    After February 5th, the acrobatic troupe gave public performances in Rabat, with every show sold out and many people unable to buy tickets. The Speaker of the Moroccan Parliament watched the performance twice, and many cabinet ministers and senior government officials also watched the acrobatic troupe's performance.

### 我杂技团在摩洛哥演出受到观众热烈欢迎

1958-02-18
第6版()
专栏：

    我杂技团在摩洛哥演出受到观众热烈欢迎
    新华社17日讯　拉巴特消息：中国杂技团在摩洛哥的演出受到观众的热烈欢迎。由于观众热烈要求，杂技团从2月11日到16日已经加演了九场。
    中国杂技团是在1月17日到达摩洛哥的。从1月18日到2月2日，杂技团在卡萨布兰卡演出了二十场，观众近五万人。
    2月4日杂技团在摩洛哥王宫为国王和王族演出；国王对演出备加赞扬，并且和杂技团团长张铁生互赠礼物。
    2月5日以后，杂技团在拉巴特公演，场场满座，还有许多人买不到票。摩洛哥议会议长看了两遍，许多内阁大臣和政府高级官员也都看了杂技团的表演。
