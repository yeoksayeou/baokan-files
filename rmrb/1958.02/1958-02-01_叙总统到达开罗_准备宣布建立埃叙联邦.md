Title: Syrian President Arrives in Cairo, Preparing to Announce Establishment of Egypt-Syria Federation
Author:
Date: 1958-02-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 31st: Syrian President Kuwatli arrived in Cairo this morning to prepare for the announcement of the establishment of the Egypt-Syria Federation.

　　The Syrian delegation of fifteen includes Prime Minister al-Assali, Foreign Minister Bitar, and other ministers of the Syrian cabinet.

　　Egyptian President Nasser and the entire cabinet welcomed them at the airport. The two presidents traveled by car to the Qubbah Palace. People lined the streets to welcome them.

　　Egyptian Minister of State for Presidential Affairs, Sabri, told reporters at the airport that formal negotiations on the establishment of the Egypt-Syria Federation would begin on February 1st.

### 叙总统到达开罗  准备宣布建立埃叙联邦

1958-02-01
第5版()
专栏：

　　叙总统到达开罗
    准备宣布建立埃叙联邦
    据新华社开罗31日电　叙利亚总统库阿特利在今天上午到达开罗，准备宣布建立埃叙联邦。
    叙利亚的十五人代表团包括总理阿萨利、外交部长比塔尔和叙利亚内阁其他部长。
    埃及总统纳赛尔和政府全体部长在机场欢迎他们。两位总统乘汽车前往库巴宫。人民在街道的两旁列队欢迎。
    埃及负责总统事务的国务部长萨布里在机场告诉记者们，关于建立埃叙联邦的正式谈判将在1日开始。
