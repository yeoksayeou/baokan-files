Title: Soviet Union Establishes Association for Foreign Cultural Relations; "House of Friendship of Peoples" to be Built in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1958-02-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency Report: According to reports from the New China News Agency, the National Representative Conference of Soviet Associations for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries concluded its discussion on the report made by Comrade Popova, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, on the afternoon of the 18th. The conference unanimously adopted the resolution on the establishment of a federation of Soviet Associations for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

The conference also adopted the federation's charter and elected the federation's leadership. Comrade Popova was elected Chairman of the Presidium of the Federation Council.

The most important task of this federation is to help the Soviet people understand the lives, labor, customs, economy, culture, history, and languages of foreign peoples, and to introduce to foreign countries the lives of the Soviet people, the economic, cultural, and scientific development of the various nationalities of the Soviet Union, and the Soviet Union's peaceful foreign policy.

The conference entrusted the federation with the task of preparing for the establishment of the Soviet Union's "House of Friendship of Peoples" in Moscow. This House of Friendship will become the base for the activities of various Soviet foreign friendship associations.

This House of Friendship will open in May as an important venue for promoting friendship between peoples of all countries.

The "House of Friendship of Peoples" will have departments for mass activities, literature and art, science and technology, and will establish a foreign book library, a concert hall, and exhibition halls. The House of Friendship will also hold various commemorative evenings and report meetings, and assist factories, schools, and organizations in carrying out various friendly activities.

### 苏联成立对外文化联系协会  在莫斯科筹建“各国人民友谊之家”

1958-02-22
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联成立对外文化联系协会
    在莫斯科筹建“各国人民友谊之家”
    本报综合报道据新华社讯：苏联各对外友好与文化联系协会全国代表会议，18日下午结束了对苏联对外文化协会理事会主席波波娃所作报告的讨论。会议一致通过了关于成立苏联对外友好与文化联系协会联合会的决议。
    会议还通过了联合会的会章，并且选出了联合会的领导机构。波波娃当选为联合会理事会主席团主席。
    这个联合会的最重要的任务是，帮助苏联人民了解外国人民的生活、劳动、风俗习惯、经济、文化、历史和语言，并且向国外介绍苏维埃人的生活、苏联各族人民的经济、文化和科学的发展情况、苏联的和平外交政策。
    会议委托联合会在莫斯科筹办苏联与外国人民友谊之家。这个友谊之家将成为苏联各对外友好协会活动的基地。
    这座友谊之家将在5月份开幕，作为开展各国人民友谊的重要场所。
    在“各国人民友谊之家”里将设有群众活动、文学艺术、科学技术等部门，并且将建立外国书籍图书馆、音乐厅和展览厅等。友谊之家还将举办各种纪念晚会和报告会，协助工厂、学校和机关进行各种友好活动。
