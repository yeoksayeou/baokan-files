Title: Khrushchev Receives Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-02-19
Page: 3

    Khrushchev Receives Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 18th: TASS, Moscow reports: Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Mićunović, in Moscow on the 17th and spoke with him. The conversation took place at the request of the Yugoslav Ambassador.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见南驻苏大使

1958-02-19
第3版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见南驻苏大使
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫17日在莫斯科接见了南斯拉夫驻苏联大使米丘诺维奇，并且同他谈了话。这次谈话是应南斯拉夫大使的请求而进行的。
