Title: French Laboring People Deeply Mourn the Death of Cachin
Author:
Date: 1958-02-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, February 14th: The working class and laboring people throughout France are deeply mourning the death of Marcel Cachin, an outstanding activist of the French Communist Party and the international workers' movement. Many enterprises across the country have held memorial services for this great friend of the laboring people.
    The Central Committee of the French Communist Party has received tens of thousands of telegrams and letters from various social organizations, trade unions, and individuals, expressing the deep sorrow of the French people.
    Marcel Cachin's coffin has been lying in state in the hall of the "l'Humanité" editorial office since the morning of the 14th. Parisians and residents from other provinces have been streaming here to bid farewell to their beloved comrade and friend Marcel Cachin.
    Cachin's funeral will be held at 2:30 PM on the 15th at the Père Lachaise Cemetery in Paris. Members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Suslov, and member of the Central Committee, Kapitonov, arrived in Paris on the 14th to attend Cachin's funeral.

### 法国劳动人民  沉痛哀悼加香逝世

1958-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    法国劳动人民
    沉痛哀悼加香逝世
    据新华社15日讯　据塔斯社巴黎14日讯：全法国的工人阶级和劳动人民都沉痛地哀悼法国共产党和国际工人运动的杰出活动家马赛尔·加香的逝世。全国很多企业都纷纷为这位劳动人民的伟大朋友举行追悼会。
    法国共产党中央委员会收到各社会团体、工会和个人的成千上万封的电报和信件，这些函电表达了法国人民的深切悲痛。
    马赛尔·加香的灵柩从14日早晨起停放在“人道报”编辑部大厅内。巴黎市民和外省居民川流不息地到这里来同他们敬爱的同志和朋友马赛尔·加香告别。
    加香的葬礼将于15日十四点三十分在巴黎拉谢兹神甫公墓举行。苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员苏斯洛夫和中央委员会委员卡皮托诺夫已经在14日到达巴黎，准备参加加香的葬礼。
