Title: Zhou Enlai Sends Condolences on the Death of Hungarian Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1958-02-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 4th. Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram on February 4th to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Hungarian People's Republic Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Imre Horváth. The telegram stated, "Upon learning of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Imre Horváth, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, I extend my deepest condolences and request that you convey my sympathies to his family."

### 周恩来外长电唁匈外长逝世

1958-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长电唁匈外长逝世
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来4日致电匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府外交部，悼唁伊姆雷·霍瓦特同志逝世。电报说，“惊悉匈牙利工农革命政府外交部长伊姆雷·霍瓦特同志不幸逝世，谨致深切的哀悼，并请向其家属转致慰问。”
