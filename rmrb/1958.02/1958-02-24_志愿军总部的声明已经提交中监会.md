Title: Statement of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Headquarters Submitted to the NNSC
Author:
Date: 1958-02-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 23rd: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Major General Jiang Shanghao, Chief Commissioner of the Korean-Chinese side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, submitted copies of the statement issued by the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on February 20, 1958, to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission and the representatives of Poland, Czechoslovakia, Sweden, and Switzerland on the morning of February 22nd.

### 志愿军总部的声明已经提交中监会

1958-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    志愿军总部的声明已经提交中监会
