More Savings, More Construction

　　Czechoslovak Savings Bank has Nine Million Depositors
　　By the end of 1957, the number of deposit accounts in the Czechoslovak Savings Bank had reached nine million. This means that for every thousand residents in the country, six hundred and seventy-seven have a savings account.

　　Romanian Rural Deposits Doubled Last Year
　　In 1957, the number of deposit accounts in Romania increased by 480,000, and the amount of deposits increased by 663 million lei (Romanian currency) compared to 1956. Rural deposits doubled.

　　Albanian Working People Increase Savings
　　By the end of 1957, the savings of the Albanian working people in savings banks exceeded 481 million leks, while at the end of 1956 it was only about 284 million leks. In recent years, the savings of Albanian agricultural workers, especially members of agricultural producers' cooperatives, have greatly increased. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 多储蓄　搞建设

1958-02-04
第6版()
专栏：多储蓄　搞建设

　　多储蓄　搞建设
    捷银行居民存户九百万个
    到1957年年底为止，捷克斯洛伐克储蓄银行中的存款的户头已经达到了九百万个。这就是说，在全国每一千个居民中间就有六百七十七个人有存折。
    罗农村存款去年增加一倍
    1957年，罗马尼亚全国存款户增加了四十八万户，存款数目比1956年增加了六亿六千三百万列伊（罗币），农村的存款增加了一倍。
    阿劳动人民储蓄增加
    到1957年年底，阿尔巴尼亚劳动人民在储蓄银行的存款超过了四亿八千一百万里克，而在1956年年底时只不过二亿八千四百万里克左右。在最近几年中，阿尔巴尼亚农业劳动者，特别是农业生产合作社社员的存款大大增加了。（新华社）
