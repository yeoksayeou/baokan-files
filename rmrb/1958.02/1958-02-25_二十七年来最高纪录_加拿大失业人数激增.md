Title: Canadian Unemployment Soars to Highest Level in Twenty-Seven Years
Author:
Date: 1958-02-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, February 24th: According to statistics released by the Canadian government, in the week ending January 18th this year, the number of unemployed people in Canada reached 520,000, accounting for 8.9% of Canada's total workforce.

The report said that this figure is the highest number of unemployed people in Canada since the Great Depression of the 1930s. The report also pointed out that the large increase in the number of unemployed is mainly due to the impact of the economic recession in the United States.

### 二十七年来最高纪录  加拿大失业人数激增

1958-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    二十七年来最高纪录
    加拿大失业人数激增
    新华社24日讯　渥太华消息：据加拿大政府宣布的统计，到今年1月18日为止的一周，加拿大的失业人数达到五十二万人，占加拿大全部劳动力的8.9％。
    消息说，这个数字是自从1930年代经济大危机以来加拿大失业人数的最高数字。消息并且指出，失业人数的大量增加主要是由于美国经济衰退的影响。
