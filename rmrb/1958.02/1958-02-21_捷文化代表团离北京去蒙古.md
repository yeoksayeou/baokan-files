Title: Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1958-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 19th: The Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation, headed by Dr. Jaroslav Hávelka, Deputy Minister of Education and Culture of Czechoslovakia, left Beijing for Mongolia by plane on the morning of the 19th, after signing the 1958 executive plan for the Sino-Czechoslovak Cultural Cooperation Agreement with our country. Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, and many others went to the airport to see them off. Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák, also went to the airport to see them off.

On the afternoon of the 17th, Guo Morou, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, met with the Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation.

### 捷文化代表团离北京去蒙古

1958-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    捷文化代表团离北京去蒙古
    新华社19日讯　以捷克斯洛伐克教育文化部副部长雅罗斯拉夫·哈维尔卡博士为首的捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团，在与我国签订了中捷文化合作协定1958年执行计划以后，已在19日晨乘飞机离北京去蒙古。文化部副部长夏衍等多人曾前往机场欢送。前往机场欢送的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克。
    17日下午，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若接见了捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团。
