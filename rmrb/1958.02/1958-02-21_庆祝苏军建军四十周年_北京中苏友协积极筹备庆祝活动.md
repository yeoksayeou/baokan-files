Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army: Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association Actively Prepares for Celebratory Activities
Author:
Date: 1958-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 20th: The Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association is actively preparing to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

    The Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association and the People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing will jointly hold a celebration party on the 21st. The Central People's Broadcasting Station's Radio Drama Troupe will hold a recitation session at the Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center on the evening of the 23rd, reciting poems, novels, and plays about the Soviet Army. The Military Band of the General Staff Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army will also hold Soviet military music concerts on February 27th and 28th.

    Lieutenant General Ouyang Wen, editor-in-chief of the Liberation Army Daily, who recently returned from a visit to the Soviet Union, has been invited by the Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association to give a report to propaganda cadres of various units on the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. The Chinese Musicians Association and the Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association will also jointly invite musician Shi Lemeng to introduce Soviet Army songs to music lovers.

    The photo exhibition celebrating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army, organized by the Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center, has been on display in Beihai Park since February 18th. This exhibition systematically introduces the birth, growth, and strengthening of the Soviet Army and its glorious combat path.

### 庆祝苏军建军四十周年  北京中苏友协积极筹备庆祝活动

1958-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝苏军建军四十周年
    北京中苏友协积极筹备庆祝活动
    新华社20日讯　北京市中苏友好协会正在积极筹备庆祝苏联军队建军四十周年。
    北京市中苏友好协会同中国人民解放军驻京部队将在21日联合举行庆祝晚会。中央人民广播电台广播剧团将于23日晚在北京市中苏友好协会文化馆举行朗诵会，朗诵有关苏军的诗歌、小说和剧本。中国人民解放军总参谋部军乐团也将在2月27日、28日举行苏军军乐演奏会。
    不久前访苏归来的解放军报总编辑欧阳文中将应北京市中苏友好协会邀请，将为各单位宣传干部作关于苏军建军四十周年的报告。中国音乐家协会和北京市中苏友好协会也将联合邀请音乐家时乐蒙为音乐爱好者介绍苏军的歌曲。
    北京市中苏友好协会文化馆举办的庆祝苏军建军四十周年的图片展览已从2月18日起在北海公园展出。这个展览会系统地介绍了苏军的诞生、成长和壮大及其光辉的战斗道路。
