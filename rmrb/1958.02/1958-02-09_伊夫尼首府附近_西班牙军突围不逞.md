Title: Near Ifni Capital: Spanish Troops' Breakout Fails

Author: 
Date: 1958-02-09
Page: 7

　　Near Ifni Capital
    Spanish Troops' Breakout Fails
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch from Larache: According to a communiqué issued here on the 5th by the high command of the "Moroccan Sahara Liberation Army," the Spanish colonial troops compressed in the Sidi-Ifni area near the Ifni capital attempted to break through the encirclement of the Ifni insurgents several times over the weekend, but failed. After several hours of fierce fighting, 246 Spanish soldiers were killed, including five officers. Only five Ifni insurgents were injured.

### 伊夫尼首府附近  西班牙军突围不逞

1958-02-09
第7版()
专栏：

　　伊夫尼首府附近
    西班牙军突围不逞
    新华社7日讯　拉西特消息：据“摩洛哥撒哈拉解放军”高级指挥部5日在此间发表公报说，被压缩在伊夫尼首府西迪—伊夫尼一带的西班牙殖民军部队在上周末曾经多次企图突破伊夫尼起义人民的包围圈而未能得逞。经过连续数小时的激战后，西班牙军队被打死二百四十六人，其中包括军官五人。伊夫尼起义部队只有五人受伤。
