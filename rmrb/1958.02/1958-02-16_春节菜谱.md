Title: Spring Festival Recipes
Author: Liu Haibo
Date: 1958-02-16
Page: 8

    Spring Festival Recipes
    Liu Haibo, Chef, South Vegetable Department, Dongsi Cafeteria, Beijing
    Lotus Seed Pod Tofu (approximate cost: 18 cents)
    Ingredients: 2 blocks of soft tofu, 4 liang of spinach, 4 qian of starch, 2 qian of salt, 3 qian of sesame oil or lard.
    Instructions: 1. Mash the tofu in a bowl into a tofu paste. 2. Wash and finely chop the spinach, marinate with 1 qian of salt for two to three minutes, then squeeze out the spinach juice using a clean cloth. 3. Pour the spinach juice into the tofu paste, add 3 qian of starch (if available, add one egg white), mix well with chopsticks, then divide evenly into ten wine glasses. 4. Shape the spinach residue into small balls like green peas and arrange them on top of the tofu paste to resemble a lotus seed pod (if available, use real green peas). 5. Place the wine glasses containing the tofu paste flat in a steamer and steam for five minutes. Remove and pour onto a plate. 6. Pour the sesame oil or lard into a pot, add 1 qian of salt, 1 qian of starch, and 2 liang of water or broth (add a little monosodium glutamate if available). Bring to a boil over high heat and pour over the steamed tofu. Ready to serve.
    All Red (approximate cost: 27 cents)
    Ingredients: 2 blocks of soft tofu, 2 liang of pork, 0.5 jin of carrots, 0.5 liang of starch, 3 qian of salt.
    Instructions: First, wash the carrots thoroughly, place them in a bowl, and steam over high heat for half an hour. Remove and mash into a very fine carrot paste. Add the soft tofu, minced pork, starch, and salt. Mix well, then steam over high heat for ten minutes. Ready to serve.
    Steamed Fish with Rice Flour (approximate cost: 42 cents)
    Ingredients: 0.5 jin of grass carp, 1 liang of rice flour, 3 qian of soy sauce, 3 qian of lard, 5 qian of red fermented bean curd juice, 4 qian of rice wine, 1 qian of salt, 3 peppercorns.
    Instructions: 1. Remove the scales and innards from the fish and wash thoroughly (if using live fish, do not wash). Chop into strips 1 inch long and 0.7 inch wide. Marinate with rice wine and soy sauce for five minutes. 2. Mix the rice flour, salt, red fermented bean curd juice, and crushed peppercorns thoroughly. Coat the fish strips, then arrange them in a bowl or plate using chopsticks. Place in a steamer and steam over high heat for ten minutes. 3. Melt the lard, add 2.4 liang of water or broth, and pour over the fish. Continue steaming for five minutes. Ready to serve.
    This dish has a unique flavor. Carp, eel, and mandarin fish can all be steamed using this method.
    Lychee Squid (approximate cost: 38 cents)
    Ingredients: 0.5 jin of squid, 2 liang of carrots, 4 liang of celery, 2 liang of potatoes, 1 liang of minced meat, 1 liang of soy sauce, 0.5 liang of peanut oil or lard.
    Instructions: 1. Wash the carrots, potatoes, and celery thoroughly and chop them into fine pieces. 2. Wash the squid (the vegetable market sells pre-cut squid rolls) twice with boiling water, remove, and drain. 3. Heat the oil over high heat, add the minced meat, carrot pieces, and potato pieces and stir-fry for one minute. Add soy sauce and 2 liang of water or broth and stir-fry for two minutes. Add the squid and celery and stir-fry for two minutes, until the squid rolls into lychee shapes. Ready to serve.
    Assorted Hot Pot (approximate cost: 45 cents)
    This hot pot is made with a clay pot, a stove, and various vegetables.
    Instructions: Fill the clay pot with two-thirds water. Add ginger slices, minced green garlic shoots, salt, and 1 liang of fermented black soybeans. Bring to a boil. Add shredded carrots, white radish, potatoes, Chinese cabbage, spinach, wood ear fungus, daylily, glass noodles, dried shrimp, etc. and cook for five minutes. Then, add two deep-fried dough sticks cut into 1-inch lengths and cook for one minute. The hot pot is ready. If there are more people, you can add vegetables at any time.

### 春节菜谱
刘海波
1958-02-16
第8版()
专栏：

    春节菜谱
    北京东四食堂南菜部厨司　刘海波
    莲蓬豆腐（价约一角八分）
    原料：南豆腐两块，菠菜四两，淀粉四钱，食盐二钱，香油或猪油三钱。
    作法：1．把豆腐放在碗内搅成豆腐泥。2．把菠菜洗净切成细末，用一钱盐腌两三分钟，之后再用一块干净布包了挤出菠菜汁。3．把菠菜汁倒入豆腐泥内，加入三钱淀粉（如有鸡蛋，可放一个鸡蛋白），用筷子调匀，再分匀放入十个酒杯内。4．把菠菜渣做成青豆一样，安在豆腐泥上面如莲蓬形（有条件，可放真青豆）。5．把豆腐泥连着酒杯平摆在笼屉内，蒸五分钟取出，倒在盘子中。6．把香油或猪油倒入锅中，加入一钱食盐、一钱淀粉和二两生水或汤（有味精可放一点），在旺火上烧开淋在蒸好的豆腐上面，即成。
    满堂红（价约二角七分）
    原料：南豆腐两块，猪肉二两，胡萝卜半斤，淀粉半两，食盐三钱。
    作法：先将胡萝卜洗干净，放在碗内，在旺火上蒸半小时，取出揽成极细的胡萝卜泥，再加入南豆腐和猪肉末、淀粉、食盐，拌匀，然后再在旺火上蒸十分钟，即可。
    米粉蒸鱼（价约四角二分）
    原料：青鱼半斤，大米粉一两，酱油三钱，猪油三钱，红腐乳汁五钱，黄酒四钱，食盐一钱，胡椒三粒。
    作法：1．将鱼去鳞和五脏洗干净（如是活鱼，不要洗），剁成一寸长、七分宽的条形，加入黄酒和酱油腌五分钟。2．把米粉、食盐、红腐乳汁和压成细末的胡椒拌匀鱼条后，用筷子夹了排列在碗内或盘内，置于笼屉内，在旺火上蒸十分钟。3．再把猪油溶化加生水或汤二两四钱，淋在鱼内，继续蒸五分钟，即成。
    此菜别有风味，鲤鱼、鳝鱼、鳜鱼都可按此法蒸。
    荔枝尤鱼（价约三角八分）
    原料：尤鱼半斤，胡萝卜二两，芹菜四两，土豆二两，肉末一两，酱油一两，花生油或大油半两。
    作法：1．把胡萝卜、土豆和芹菜洗干净后都切成细末。2．把尤鱼（菜市场有发好切成尤鱼卷的盘菜卖）用开水洗二次捞出滤干水。3．把油在旺火上烧热，投入肉末、胡萝卜末、土豆末炒一分钟，再加入酱油和生水或汤二两炒二分钟，再倒入尤鱼和芹菜炒二分钟，等到尤鱼卷成荔枝形即成。
    十锦火锅　（价约四角五分）
    这个火锅是用陶土制的砂锅和一个火炉子以及各种蔬菜煮起来的。
    作法：在砂锅里放三分之二的生水。加入生姜片、青蒜末、食盐和南豆豉一两煮开，再投入切成丝的胡萝卜、白萝卜、土豆、大白菜以及菠菜、木耳、黄花、粉条、虾米皮等，煮五分钟，然后将两根油条切成一寸长加入，再煮一分钟，火锅就成了。如果人多，可以随时加入蔬菜。
