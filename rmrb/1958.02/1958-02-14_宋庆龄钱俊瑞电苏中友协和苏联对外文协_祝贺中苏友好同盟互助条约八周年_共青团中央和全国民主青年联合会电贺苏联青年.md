Title: Song Qingling and Qian Junrui Send Telegrams to the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to Congratulate the Eighth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance; The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and the All-China Youth Federation Send Congratulatory Telegrams to Soviet Youth
Author:
Date: 1958-02-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 13th - Song Qingling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, sent a telegram on the 12th to Andreev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, congratulating the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The telegram is as follows: Dear Comrade Andreev:
	On the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, on behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, I extend our sincerest and warmest congratulations to you, and through you, to the fraternal great Soviet people.
	The countless historical facts of the past eight years have brilliantly confirmed the great significance of this treaty; especially today, when our era is at a new historical turning point, and the strength of socialism has surpassed the strength of imperialism, the far-reaching impact of this treaty is becoming even more apparent.
	We wholeheartedly wish the great Soviet people even more brilliant achievements in the cause of building communism and safeguarding world peace.
	May the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people be forever consolidated and continuously developed.
			President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association　Song Qingling
			Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association　Qian Junrui
						February 12, 1958
Xinhua News Agency, February 13th - Song Qingling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, sent a telegram on the 12th to Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, congratulating the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The telegram reads as follows: Dear Comrade Popova:
	On the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, on behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, I extend our sincerest and warmest congratulations to you, and through you, to the fraternal great Soviet people.
	We wholeheartedly wish the great Soviet people even more brilliant achievements in the cause of building communism and safeguarding world peace.
	Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people!
			President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association　Song Qingling
			Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association　Qian Junrui
						February 12, 1958
Xinhua News Agency, February 13th - The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China and the All-China Youth Federation today sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union and the Committee of Youth Organizations of the Soviet Union, congratulating the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.
	The telegram said: "On the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, we, on behalf of all Chinese youth, extend our warm greetings and heartfelt congratulations to our Soviet youth brothers and sisters.
	Chinese youth deeply admire the great contributions made by the Soviet people and youth under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to the cause of human progress. We are proud that the great alliance between China and the Soviet Union is playing an increasingly important role in the cause of world peace, democracy, and socialism.
	For the bright future of mankind, we sincerely congratulate the Soviet youth on their new achievements in the cause of building communism, and are willing to continue to contribute all our strength to the development and strengthening of the great alliance between China and the Soviet Union.
	Long live the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples and youth!"

### 宋庆龄钱俊瑞电苏中友协和苏联对外文协  祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约八周年  共青团中央和全国民主青年联合会电贺苏联青年

1958-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄钱俊瑞电苏中友协和苏联对外文协
    祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约八周年
    共青团中央和全国民主青年联合会电贺苏联青年
    新华社13日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄、秘书长钱俊瑞，12日致电苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫，祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订八周年。贺电如下：亲爱的安德烈耶夫同志：
    值此中苏友好同盟互助条约签订八周年之际，谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员，向你们，并通过你们，向兄弟般的伟大苏联人民，表示最诚挚最热烈的祝贺。
    八年来的无数历史事实，光辉地证实着这个条约的伟大意义；特别是在今天，当我们的时代已处于新的历史转折点，社会主义力量超过了帝国主义力量的时期，这个条约所起的深远影响，也就愈加明显。
    衷心地祝愿伟大的苏联人民，在建设共产主义和维护世界和平的事业中，取得更加辉煌的成就。
    祝中苏两国人民的伟大友谊永远巩固和不断地发展。
                    中苏友好协会总会会长　宋庆龄
                    中苏友好协会总会秘书长　钱俊瑞
                                          1958年2月12日
    新华社13日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄、秘书长钱俊瑞，12日致电苏联对外文化协会理事会主席波波娃，祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订八周年。电文如下：亲爱的波波娃同志：
    值此中苏友好同盟互助条约签订八周年之际，仅代表中苏友好协会全体会员，向你们，并通过你们，向兄弟般的伟大苏联人民，表示最诚挚最热烈的祝贺。
    衷心地祝愿伟大的苏联人民，在建设共产主义和维护世界和平的事业中，取得更加辉煌的成就。
    中苏两国人民永恒的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
                    中苏友好协会总会会长　宋庆龄
                      中苏友好协会总会秘书长　钱俊瑞
                                        1958年2月12日
    新华社13日讯　中国共产主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合会今天致电苏联共产主义青年团中央委员会、苏联青年组织委员会，祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订八周年。
    电文说：“值此中苏友好同盟互助条约签订八周年之际，我们谨代表全中国青年向苏联青年兄弟姊妹们致热烈的敬意和衷心的祝贺。
    中国青年深深地钦佩苏联人民和青年在苏联共产党领导下对人类进步事业所作的巨大的贡献。我们为中苏两国的伟大同盟在世界和平、民主和社会主义事业中所起的越来越大的作用而骄傲。
    为了人类的美好将来，我们衷心祝贺苏联青年在建设共产主义的事业中取得新的成就，并愿继续为发展和加强中苏两国的伟大同盟而贡献一切力量。
    中苏两国人民和青年之间的兄弟的牢不可破的友谊万岁！”
