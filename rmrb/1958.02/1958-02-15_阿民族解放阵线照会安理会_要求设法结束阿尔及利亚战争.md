Title: Algerian National Liberation Front Appeals to Security Council, Demanding Measures to End Algerian War
Author:
Date: 1958-02-15
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 14th. The Algerian National Liberation Front requested the United Nations Security Council on the 13th to compel France to accept a political solution to end the forty-month-long Algerian War.
    The Front's representative in the United States, Yazid, said in a note to the President of the Security Council, Sobolev (Soviet Union), that "the belligerent France in the Algerian war, emboldened by overseas material and financial aid, is trying to extend the armed conflict to the whole of North Africa." The incident of the French Air Force bombing the civilians of Sakiet Sidi Youssef village in Tunisia "demonstrates the international nature of the conflict between the Algerian people and the French colonialist policy." Therefore, the Security Council cannot ignore the war in Algeria.
    The organization, which leads the Algerian people in the armed struggle for national liberation, said that it is ready to participate in any negotiations chaired by the United Nations aimed at restoring peace in North Africa. It pointed out that the United Nations resolutions on the Algerian issue provided "a reasonable basis for negotiations, which the Algerian National Liberation Front has expressed its acceptance of, but has been rejected by France."

### 阿民族解放阵线照会安理会  要求设法结束阿尔及利亚战争

1958-02-15
第6版()
专栏：

    阿民族解放阵线照会安理会
    要求设法结束阿尔及利亚战争
    新华社14日讯　纽约联合国消息：阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线13日要求联合国安全理事会使法国接受一项政治解决办法，以结束历时四十个月的阿尔及利亚战争。
    这个阵线在美国的代表雅齐德在致安全理事会主席索波列夫（苏联）的照会里说，阿尔及利亚战争的“交战国法国由于有海外物资和财政援助，胆子已经越来越大，它正在设法把武装冲突扩大到整个北非”。法国空军轰炸突尼斯的萨基埃特—西迪—优素福村平民的事件，就“表明了阿尔及利亚人民和法国殖民主义政策的冲突具有国际性质”。因此，安全理事会不能忽视在阿尔及利亚的战争。
    领导阿尔及利亚人民进行民族解放武装斗争的这个组织说，它准备参加由联合国主持的、目的在于恢复北非和平的任何谈判。它指出，联合国关于阿尔及利亚问题的决议提供了“举行谈判的合理基础，这种谈判阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线已经表示接受，却被法国拒绝。”
