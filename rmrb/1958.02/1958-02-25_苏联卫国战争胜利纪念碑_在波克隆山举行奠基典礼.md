Title: Groundbreaking Ceremony for Soviet Patriotic War Victory Monument Held on Poklonnaya Hill
Author:
Date: 1958-02-25
Page: 6

    Soviet Patriotic War Victory Monument
    Groundbreaking Ceremony Held on Poklonnaya Hill

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 24th: According to TASS, the groundbreaking ceremony for the Victory Monument of the Great Patriotic War of the Soviet People was held on February 23rd on the historically significant Poklonnaya Hill. Tens of thousands of working people and the Moscow garrison troops participated in the groundbreaking ceremony.

    Marshal of the Soviet Union and Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Malinovsky, and other Soviet Marshals attended the ceremony. Bobrovnikov, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet Executive Committee, delivered a speech first. He said that the Victory Monument, whose foundation is being laid today, is a symbol of the dauntless heroism of our people and their armed forces, and their loyalty to the great cause of communism.

    After the meeting, the armed forces held a grand military parade.

### 苏联卫国战争胜利纪念碑  在波克隆山举行奠基典礼

1958-02-25
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联卫国战争胜利纪念碑
    在波克隆山举行奠基典礼
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联人民伟大卫国战争胜利纪念碑奠基典礼23日在有历史意义的波克隆山上举行。参加奠基典礼的有成千上万的劳动人民和莫斯科卫戍部队。
    苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅和苏联元帅等参加了典礼。莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席波布罗夫尼科夫首先致词。他说，今天奠基的胜利纪念碑是我国人民和他们的武装部队的大无畏英雄主义、忠于伟大共产主义事业的象征。
    大会结束后，武装部队举行了隆重的阅兵。
