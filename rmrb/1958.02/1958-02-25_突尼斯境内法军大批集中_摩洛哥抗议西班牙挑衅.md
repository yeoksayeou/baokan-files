Title: Large Concentration of French Troops in Tunisia; Morocco Protests Spanish Provocation
Author:
Date: 1958-02-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 22nd: The Tunisian Embassy in Cairo announced this afternoon that a large number of French troops have concentrated in the Remada region after leaving their barracks, despite the Tunisian government's announcement of a blockade on all roads leading to Remada.
    The Tunisian Embassy also announced that Tunisian authorities in Tastour and Kaafer had discovered that some Frenchmen were in possession of large quantities of weapons, including submachine guns, grenades, and rifles.
    Xinhua News Agency, February 24th: Rabat Dispatch: Spain has again amassed troops in Ceuta and Nador in Spanish Morocco, near the northern border posts of the Kingdom of Morocco. The Moroccan government has lodged a strong protest with Spain on the 23rd regarding this provocative action and demanded that Spain withdraw these troops.
    Moroccan sources point out that Spain has recently constructed positions in the aforementioned border area that is recognized as a neutral zone.

### 突尼斯境内法军大批集中  摩洛哥抗议西班牙挑衅

1958-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    突尼斯境内法军大批集中
    摩洛哥抗议西班牙挑衅
    新华社开罗22日电　突尼斯驻开罗大使馆今天下午宣布，大批法军在离开军营之后已经集中到腊马达地区，尽管突尼斯政府宣布封锁通往腊马达的所有道路。
    突尼斯大使馆还宣布，塔斯托和卡阿福的突尼斯当局发现一些法国人持有大批武器，包括手提机关枪、手榴弹和来福枪。
    新华社24日讯　拉巴特消息：西班牙又在西属摩洛哥休达和纳尔多地方邻近摩洛哥王国北部边防哨的地方调集军队。摩洛哥政府已经就这种挑衅行动在23日向西班牙提出强硬抗议，并且要求西班牙把这些军队撤走。
    摩洛哥人士指出，西班牙最近已经在被公认是中立区的上述边境地带构筑阵地。
