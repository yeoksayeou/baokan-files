Title: Hunan's Spring Harvest Crops are Growing Luxuriantly
Author:
Date: 1958-02-12
Page: 4
In local newspapers:

    Hunan's Spring Harvest Crops are Growing Luxuriantly
    *New Hunan Daily*, February 9th, second page, headline: The province's more than 30 million mu of spring harvest crops are growing well. Rape and green manure seedlings are eight or nine inches high; wheat, broad beans, and other crops are lush and verdant, promising a bumper harvest.

### 湖南春收作物生长茂盛

1958-02-12
第4版()
专栏：在各地报纸上

    湖南春收作物生长茂盛
    新湖南报9日二版头条报道：全省三千多万亩春收作物生长良好，油菜、绿肥苗高八、九寸；麦子、蚕豆等葱绿繁茂，可望丰收。
