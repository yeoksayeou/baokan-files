Title: Ifni Uprising People Annihilate Another Batch of Spanish Troops
Author:
Date: 1958-02-16
Page: 6

    Ifni Uprising People
    Annihilate Another Batch of Spanish Troops
    Xinhua News Agency, February 14th: Rabat dispatch: The Moroccan Liberation Army issued a communique on the 13th stating that the armed uprising people of Sidi Ifni killed another 49 Spanish colonial troops in the past three days of fighting, and many Spanish colonial troops were wounded.

### 伊夫尼起义人民  又歼灭一批西班牙军

1958-02-16
第6版()
专栏：

    伊夫尼起义人民
    又歼灭一批西班牙军
    新华社14日讯　拉巴特消息：摩洛哥解放军13日发表公报说：西迪·伊夫尼武装起义人民在过去三天的战斗中又打死了四十九名西班牙殖民军，并且有许多西班牙殖民军被打伤。
