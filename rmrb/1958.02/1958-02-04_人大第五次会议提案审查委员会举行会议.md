Title: Proposals Examination Committee of the Fifth Session of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1958-02-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd — The Proposals Examination Committee of the Fifth Session of the First National People's Congress held its first meeting on the afternoon of the 3rd.
    The meeting discussed and approved the work plan of the Proposals Examination Committee and approved the list of groups of the Proposals Examination Committee.
    The meeting was presided over by Li Xuefeng, the chairman of the committee. At the meeting, Li Zhuchen and Guan Wenwei were elected as vice chairmen of the Proposals Examination Committee.

### 人大第五次会议提案审查委员会举行会议

1958-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　人大第五次会议提案审查委员会举行会议
    新华社3日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第五次会议提案审查委员会3日下午举行第一次会议。
    会议讨论并通过了提案审查委员会工作方案，通过了提案审查委员会分组名单。
    会议由李雪峰主任委员主持。会上推选了李烛尘、管文蔚二位委员为提案审查委员会副主任委员。
