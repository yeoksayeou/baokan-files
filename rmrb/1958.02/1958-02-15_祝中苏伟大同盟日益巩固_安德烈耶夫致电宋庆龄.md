Title: Consolidate the Great Sino-Soviet Alliance
Author:
Date: 1958-02-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th – The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headquarters received a congratulatory telegram today from A. Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, to President Soong Ching Ling on the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance. The full text is as follows: Respected Comrade Soong Ching Ling, Dear Comrades!

    On the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, on behalf of all members of our association, extends fraternal and heartfelt congratulations to you and to all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

    The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is conducting tremendous work in strengthening the friendly ties between the people of our two countries and realizing the great principles of cooperation stipulated in the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance. We deeply appreciate the beneficial work you are undertaking, which is in the fundamental and vital interests of the people of our two countries. In the future, we will continue to work alongside you, together with all our Chinese friends, to develop and consolidate the friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and China.

    Dear Comrades, please accept our sincere wishes for even greater achievements in your activities that benefit the great friendship between the people of our two countries.

    Long live the eternal, unbreakable, fraternal friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and China!
            Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association
                            A. Andreyev
                              February 13, 1958
                                          In Moscow

### 祝中苏伟大同盟日益巩固  安德烈耶夫致电宋庆龄

1958-02-15
第3版()
专栏：

    祝中苏伟大同盟日益巩固
    安德烈耶夫致电宋庆龄
    新华社14日讯　中苏友好协会总会今天收到了苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫致宋庆龄会长祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订八周年的贺电。全文如下：敬爱的宋庆龄同志，亲爱的同志们！
    值此苏中友好同盟互助条约签订八周年之际，苏中友好协会理事会谨代表我会全体会员，向你们并向中苏友好协会的全体会员致以兄弟般的衷心的祝贺。
    中苏友好协会在加强我们两国人民之间的友好联系、实现苏中友好同盟互助条约所规定的伟大的合作原则方面，进行着巨大的工作。我们深深地感谢你们所进行的这项符合于我们两国人民根本切身利益的有益的工作。我们今后将继续和你们并肩携手，与我们全体中国的朋友们在一起，为发展和巩固苏中两国人民的友好而努力。
    亲爱的同志们，请接受我们的真诚的祝愿，愿贵会在进行有益于我们两国人民伟大友谊的活动中，获得更进一步的成就。
    苏中两国人民永恒的牢不可破的兄弟般的友谊万岁！
            苏中友好协会理事会主席
                            安·安德烈耶夫
                              1958年2月13日
                                          于莫斯科
