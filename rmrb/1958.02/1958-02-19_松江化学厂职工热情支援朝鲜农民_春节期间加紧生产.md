Title: Songjiang Chemical Plant Workers Enthusiastically Support Korean Farmers, Intensifying Production During the Spring Festival

Author:

Date: 1958-02-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 17th—More than 800 workers at the Songjiang Chemical Plant broke with convention and continued production as usual during the Spring Festival, stepping up the manufacture of chemical fertilizers to support the spring plowing production of farmers in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

This urgent task was recently assigned to the Songjiang Chemical Plant by the state. According to the state's requirements, the factory is to produce 2,000 tons of phosphate fertilizer for the Korean people before the end of February. After receiving this task, workers in various workshops, departments, and the clinic posted applications one after another, requesting to continue production during the Spring Festival holiday.

### 松江化学厂职工热情支援朝鲜农民  春节期间加紧生产

1958-02-19
第2版()
专栏：

    松江化学厂职工热情支援朝鲜农民
    春节期间加紧生产
    据新华社哈尔滨17日电　松江化学厂八百多名职工打破常规，春节照常生产，加紧制造化学肥料，支援朝鲜民主主义人民共和国农民春耕生产。
    这项紧急任务是国家最近向松江化学厂提出的。根据国家的要求，这个工厂要在2月底以前为朝鲜人民生产出两千吨磷肥。各车间、各科室和医务所的职工们接到这个任务后，纷纷贴出申请书，要求在春节假期中继续坚持生产。
