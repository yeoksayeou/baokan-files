Title: Korean Military Armistice Commission to Meet to Discuss US Violations of Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-02-01
Page: 5

　　Korean Military Armistice Commission to Meet
    To Discuss US Violations of Armistice Agreement
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 31st – A dispatch from the Korean Central News Agency in Kaesong: According to information obtained here, at the request of the Korean-Chinese side, the Military Armistice Commission will hold its 80th meeting in Panmunjom on February 1st.
    The meeting will discuss the issue of US violations of the Armistice Agreement.

### 朝鲜军事停战委会将开会  讨论美方破坏停战协定问题

1958-02-01
第5版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜军事停战委会将开会
    讨论美方破坏停战协定问题
    新华社平壤31日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯：据此间获得的消息，应朝中方面的要求，军事停战委员会将在2月1日在板门店举行第八十次会议。
    会议将讨论美方破坏停战协定的问题。
