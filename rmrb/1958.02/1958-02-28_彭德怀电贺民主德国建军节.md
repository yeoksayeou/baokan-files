Title: Peng Dehuai Sends Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the East German Army
Author:
Date: 1958-02-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 27th: Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, sent a telegram on the 26th to General Stoph, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic, to congratulate him on the anniversary of the founding of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade General Willi Stoph, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic:

On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic, I, on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extend warm congratulations to you and all comrades of the fraternal National People's Army.

The German Democratic Republic is a strong fortress for all German people in their struggle to restore the unity of the German nation. I wish all officers and soldiers of the National People's Army further achievements in the cause of striving for the peaceful reunification of the motherland and safeguarding peace in Europe and the world.

                Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
                                       February 26, 1958

### 彭德怀电贺民主德国建军节

1958-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    彭德怀电贺民主德国建军节
    新华社27日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅26日打电报给德意志民主共和国副总理兼国防部长斯多夫上将，祝贺德意志民主共和国国家人民军建军节。电文如下：德意志民主共和国副总理兼国防部长威利·斯多夫上将同志：
    值此德意志民主共和国国家人民军建军节之际，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体官兵，向您及兄弟般的国家人民军全体同志致以热烈的祝贺。
    德意志民主共和国是全体德国人民为争取恢复德国民族的统一而进行斗争的坚强堡垒。祝国家人民军全体官兵在争取祖国的和平统一和维护欧洲及世界和平的事业中取得进一步的成就。
                中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长　彭德怀
                                       1958年2月26日
