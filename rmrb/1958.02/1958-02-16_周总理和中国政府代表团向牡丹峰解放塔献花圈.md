Title: Premier Zhou and the Chinese Government Delegation Lay a Wreath at the Liberation Tower on Moranbong Hill
Author:
Date: 1958-02-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 15th: Premier Zhou Enlai and the Chinese government delegation he led paid their respects this morning at the Liberation Tower on Moranbong Hill in Pyongyang, which commemorates the Soviet army's liberation of Korea, by laying a wreath.

The wreath-laying ceremony took place at 9:00 AM. After the band played the national anthems of China, Korea, and the Soviet Union, the delegation placed a wreath in front of the tower. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian, General Su Yu, members of the delegation, and Cao Keqiang, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy, stood in solemn respect before the tower.

The ceremony was attended by Korean party and government leaders, including Premier Kim Il-sung of the Cabinet and Chairman Choi Yong-kun of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly. Soviet Ambassador to Korea Puzanov and embassy staff also attended the ceremony.

After laying the wreath, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi, accompanied by Premier Kim Il-sung, ascended Moranbong Hill to view the scenery and overlook the panorama of Pyongyang. This morning, in the gauze-like mist, the beautiful Moranbong Hill and the winding Taedong River below were especially beautiful. The newly built high-rise buildings and the flat, wide streets of Pyongyang were faintly visible in the mist. Premier Zhou Enlai and Premier Kim Il-sung walked along the pedestrian path on Moranbong Hill to the Eulmildae Pavilion at the top of Moranbong Hill. They talked and laughed all the way, and Premier Zhou Enlai continuously praised the scenery of Pyongyang.

### 周总理和中国政府代表团向牡丹峰解放塔献花圈

1958-02-16
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理和中国政府代表团向牡丹峰解放塔献花圈
    新华社平壤15日电　周恩来总理和他率领的中国政府代表团今天上午到平壤市牡丹峰向纪念苏军解放朝鲜的解放塔敬献了花圈。
    献花圈仪式在九时举行。乐队奏中国、朝鲜、苏联三国国歌后，代表团将一个花圈献到塔前。周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长、张闻天外交部副部长、粟裕大将、代表团随行人员和中国大使馆参赞曹克强等在塔前肃立致敬。
    参加献花圈仪式的有朝鲜内阁首相金日成、最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健等党政领导人，苏联驻朝鲜大使普扎诺夫和使馆人员也参加了仪式。
    献花圈后，周恩来总理和陈毅副总理等在金日成首相的陪同下登牡丹峰观览了牡丹峰的风景并俯瞰平壤市全景。今天上午在白纱般的薄雾中，秀拔的牡丹峰和山下蜿蜒的大同江非常美丽，平壤市战后新建的高楼大厦和平坦宽阔的一条条街道在雾色中隐约可见。周恩来总理和金日成首相沿着牡丹峰上的人行道走到牡丹峰的高处乙密台。他们一路谈笑风生，周恩来总理不停地赞美平壤的景色。
