Title: Syrian President Returns to Damascus
Author:
Date: 1958-02-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, February 3rd. Syrian President Kuwatly returned to Damascus by plane from Cairo on February 3rd, after announcing the establishment of the United Arab Republic (formerly translated as the Arab Unified Republic) in Cairo.

Syrian Prime Minister Asali, cabinet ministers, and Syrian army chief of staff Bizri returned to Damascus with President Kuwatly.

When they left Cairo, Egyptian President Nasser and government ministers went to the airport to see them off.

### 叙总统回大马士革

1958-02-05
第5版()
专栏：

　　叙总统回大马士革
    新华社大马士革3日电　叙利亚总统库阿特利到开罗宣告阿拉伯联合共和国（前译阿拉伯统一共和国）成立以后，3日乘飞机从开罗回到大马士革。
    同库阿特利总统一起回到大马士革的有叙利亚总理阿萨利、内阁部长们以及叙利亚军队参谋长比兹里。
    当他们离开开罗的时候，埃及总统纳赛尔和政府部长们曾到机场送行。
