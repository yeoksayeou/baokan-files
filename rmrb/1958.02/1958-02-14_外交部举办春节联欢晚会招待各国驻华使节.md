Title: Ministry of Foreign Affairs Hosts Spring Festival Gala to Entertain Foreign Envoys to China
Author:
Date: 1958-02-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, February 13th: The Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosted a grand Spring Festival gala in Beijing tonight to entertain foreign envoys and all diplomatic officials in China. More than 400 Chinese and foreign guests attended the gala.
    Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, attended the gala. Also present were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Hanfu, Ji Pengfei, and Luo Guibo, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Culture, among others.
    At the gala, the Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe, performing in Beijing, presented brilliant performances.

### 外交部举办春节联欢晚会招待各国驻华使节

1958-02-14
第5版()
专栏：

    外交部举办春节联欢晚会招待各国驻华使节
    新华社13日讯　外交部今晚在北京举办盛大的春节联欢晚会，招待各国驻华使节和所有的外交官员。出席晚会的中外来宾共有四百多人。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长出席了晚会。出席晚会的还有对外贸易部部长叶季壮，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫、姬鹏飞、罗贵波和文化部副部长张致祥等人。
    晚会上，来北京演出的四川歌舞团表演了精采节目。
