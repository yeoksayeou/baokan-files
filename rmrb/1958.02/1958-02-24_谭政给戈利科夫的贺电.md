Title: Tan Zheng's Congratulatory Telegram to Golikov
Author: 
Date: 1958-02-24
Page: 4

To Comrade General F.I. Golikov, Director of the Main Political Department of the Soviet Army and Navy:

    On the occasion of the glorious 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army, I, on behalf of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extend warm and fraternal congratulations to you, and through you, to all political workers of the Soviet Army.
    I wish the Soviet Army new and greater achievements under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, in further strengthening the Party's political work in the Soviet Army and Navy, and in educating all members of the Soviet Army with communist ideology.
    I wish the unbreakable unity and friendship between the armies of China and the Soviet Union will be continuously consolidated and developed.
                      General Tan Zheng
                      Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
                                      February 21, 1958

### 谭政给戈利科夫的贺电

1958-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    谭政给戈利科夫的贺电苏联陆海军总政治部主任弗·依·戈利科夫上将同志：
    值此光荣的苏联军队建军四十周年之际，我谨代表中国人民解放军总政治部向您，并通过您向苏军全体政治工作人员致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    祝苏联军队在苏联共产党领导下，在进一步加强苏联陆海军中的党政工作和以共产主义思想教育苏军全体人员的工作中获得新的、更大的成就。
    祝中苏两国军队间的牢不可破的团结和友谊日益巩固与发展。
                      中国人民解放军总政治部主任　谭　政大将
                                      一九五八年二月二十一日
