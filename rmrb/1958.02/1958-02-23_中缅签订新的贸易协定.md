Title: China and Myanmar Sign New Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-02-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd—To promote the economic and trade relations between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Burma as declared in the joint communiqué of the Premiers of China and Burma on December 12, 1954, and to further implement trade between the two countries, the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Union of Burma signed a new trade agreement in Rangoon on February 21, 1958. Yao Zhongming, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Union of Burma, signed on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China; U Ba San, Secretary of the Ministry of Trade Development of the Union of Burma, signed on behalf of the Government of the Union of Burma.
    Both governments expressed satisfaction with the signing of this new trade agreement, which further contributes to the development of the most sincere relations already existing between the two friendly countries.

### 中缅签订新的贸易协定

1958-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

    中缅签订新的贸易协定
    新华社22日讯　为促进中缅两国总理在1954年12月12日的会谈公报中所宣称中华人民共和国和缅甸联邦之间的经济贸易关系，以及进一步实施两国间的贸易，中华人民共和国政府和缅甸联邦政府1958年2月21日在仰光签订了一个新的贸易协定。中华人民共和国驻缅甸联邦大使姚仲明代表中华人民共和国政府签字；缅甸联邦贸易发展部秘书吴巴山代表缅甸联邦政府签字。
    双方政府对这个进一步贡献于发展两个友好国家间已经存在的最真挚关系的新的贸易协定的签订，互相表示满意。
