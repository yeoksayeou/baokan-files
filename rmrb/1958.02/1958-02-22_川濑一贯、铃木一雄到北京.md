Title: Kawase Ichiguru and Suzuki Kazuo Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-02-22
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, February 21st: Kawase Ichiguru, Vice President of the Japan Export and Import Association and Representative Director of Toho Bussan Company, and Suzuki Kazuo, Managing Director of the Japan-China Trade Association, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou this afternoon. They are visiting China at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

### 川濑一贯、铃木一雄到北京

1958-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    川濑一贯、铃木一雄到北京
    新华社21日讯　日本输出入协会副理事长，东工物产公司代表董事川濑一贯和日本中日贸易会专务理事铃木一雄等今天下午乘飞机自广州到达北京。他们是应中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请来我国访问的。
