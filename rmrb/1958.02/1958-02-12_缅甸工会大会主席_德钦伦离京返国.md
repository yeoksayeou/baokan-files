Title: Chairman of the Trade Union Congress of Burma, Thakkin Lwin, Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1958-02-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - Thakkin Lwin, Chairman of the Trade Union Congress of Burma, who was invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, left Beijing by plane today to return to his country.

Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others went to the airport to see him off.

### 缅甸工会大会主席  德钦伦离京返国

1958-02-12
第2版()
专栏：

    缅甸工会大会主席
    德钦伦离京返国
    新华社10日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会的缅甸工会大会主席德钦伦，已经在今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕等曾到机场欢送。
