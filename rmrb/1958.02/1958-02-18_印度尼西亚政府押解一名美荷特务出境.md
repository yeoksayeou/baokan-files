Title: Indonesian Government Deports American-Dutch Spy
Author:
Date: 1958-02-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 16th: The Indonesian government, based on the recommendation of the Jakarta military authorities, deported a "foreign national," Fan Suoai, on the evening of the 14th. He is accused of engaging in subversive activities.

According to local newspapers, Fan Suoai is a Dutchman with American nationality.

### 印度尼西亚政府押解一名美荷特务出境

1958-02-18
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府押解一名美荷特务出境
    据新华社雅加达16日电　印度尼西亚政府根据雅加达军事当局的建议，在14日晚上把一名“外国国民”范索阿伊押解出境。他被控进行颠覆活动。
    据这里的报纸透露，范索阿伊是有美国国籍的荷兰人。
