Title: Premier Zhou Pays Courtesy Call on North Korean Party and Government Leaders
Author:
Date: 1958-02-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 14th: Premier Zhou Enlai today paid a courtesy call on North Korean Labor Party and government leaders Kim Il-sung, Choi Yong-kun, Park Jung-ai, and others.

Accompanying him were Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, General Su Yu, accompanying members of the Chinese government delegation, and Cao Keqiang, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea.

Also present were North Korean Vice Premiers Kim Il, Hong Myung-hee, Jung Il-yong, Nam Il, Jung Joon-taek, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Labor Party Kim Chang-man, Standing Committee members of the Labor Party Central Committee Kim Kwang-hyup, Lim Hae, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea Hyun Chil-jong, Lee Kuk-ro, Kim Won-bong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Sung-chul, and North Korean Ambassador to China Li Yong-ho.

### 周总理拜会朝鲜党政领导人

1958-02-15
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理拜会朝鲜党政领导人
    新华社平壤14日电　周恩来总理今天下午拜会了朝鲜劳动党和朝鲜政府的领导人金日成、崔庸健、朴正爱等。
    随同前往拜会的有陈毅副总理兼外交部长、张闻天副部长、粟裕大将、中国政府代表团的随行人员和中国驻朝鲜大使馆参赞曹克强。
    拜会时在座的有朝鲜内阁副首相金一、洪命熹、郑一龙、南日、郑准泽，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长金昌满，劳动党中央委员会常务委员会委员金光侠、林海，朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长玄七锺、李克鲁、金元凤，外务省副相朴成哲，朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐。
