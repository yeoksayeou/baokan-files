Title: Guo Moruo, Fan Wenlan, Chu Tunan, Mao Dun Send Telegrams Congratulating Du Bois on His Ninetieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1958-02-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd: The 23rd is the ninetieth birthday of W.E.B. Du Bois, the American black leader, leader of the American peace movement, black historian and writer. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Fan Wenlan, Director of the Institute of History III of the Academy of Sciences, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Mao Dun, Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, sent telegrams to Du Bois to express their congratulations.
    Guo Moruo and Fan Wenlan's congratulatory telegrams stated: "On behalf of the Chinese scientific community, and especially on behalf of the Chinese historical community, we extend our warmest congratulations to you. Your contributions to scientific research and the cause of defending world peace have long been sincerely admired by the academic community and the broad masses concerned with world peace activities in our country. We wish you good health and longevity."
    Chu Tunan's congratulatory telegram stated: "On behalf of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and people from all walks of life in Chinese culture, art, science, and education, I express my warm congratulations. We express our heartfelt respect for your lifelong dedication to cultural, scientific, and artistic endeavors, and for your outstanding efforts for the equal rights of people of color, human happiness, and world peace. We sincerely wish you even greater achievements in these noble causes!"
    Mao Dun's congratulatory telegram stated: "Your diligent work has made beneficial contributions to the cause of human progress, and it encourages people to fight for the defense of peace and against racial discrimination. We hope that you will continue to achieve even more brilliant achievements in your writings and social activities in the future."

### 郭沫若、范文澜、楚图南、茅盾  电贺杜波依斯九十寿辰

1958-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若、范文澜、楚图南、茅盾
    电贺杜波依斯九十寿辰
    新华社22日讯　23日是美国黑人领袖、美国和平运动领导人、黑人历史学家和作家杜波依斯九十寿辰。中国科学院院长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，科学院历史三所所长范文澜，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国作家协会主席茅盾等特分别致电杜波依斯表示祝贺。
    郭沫若和范文澜的贺电说：“我们谨代表中国科学界人士，特别是代表中国的历史学界向您致以最热忱的祝贺。您在科学研究和保卫世界和平事业中作出的贡献，久为我国学术界人士和关心世界和平活动的广大群众所衷心钦佩，愿您健康长寿。”
    楚图南的贺电说：“我谨代表中国人民对外文化协会及中国文化、艺术、科学、教育各界人士，表示热烈的祝贺。对于您以一生精力贡献于文化、科学、艺术事业，并为有色人种的平等权利、人类幸福和世界和平所作卓越的努力，表示衷心的敬意。敬祝您在这些崇高的事业上获得更巨大的成就！”
    茅盾的贺电说：“您的殷勤的劳动给人类进步事业作了有益的贡献，鼓舞着人们为保卫和平和反对种族歧视而斗争。我们希望您今后继续在著作上及社会活动上得到更辉煌的成就。”
