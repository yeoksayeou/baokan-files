Title: Sukarno and Nai Thanom Joint Statement Emphasizes Compliance with Bandung Conference Resolutions
Author:
Date: 1958-02-01
Page: 5
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, 31st. According to Thai newspapers, Indonesian President Sukarno and Thai Prime Minister Nai Thanom emphasized in a joint statement issued on January 29 that Indonesia and Thailand firmly adhere to the principles of the United Nations Charter and the resolutions of the Bandung Conference. The joint statement said that during President Sukarno's stay in Bangkok, he exchanged views with Prime Minister Nai Thanom on the international situation and various issues of common interest to the two countries. Both sides believe that for lasting peace, any international disputes must be resolved peacefully. Therefore, they hope that the West Irian issue will also be resolved peacefully.
    The joint statement also stated that both sides agreed that the existing friendly relations between the two countries must be promoted. Sukarno also invited the King of Thailand to visit Indonesia. Sukarno left Thailand on the 29th and is currently visiting Japan.

### 苏加诺和乃他侬联合声明  强调遵守万隆会议的决议

1958-02-01
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏加诺和乃他侬联合声明  强调遵守万隆会议的决议
    新华社31日讯　曼谷消息：据泰国报纸报道，印度尼西亚总统苏加诺和泰国总理乃他侬在1月29日发表的联合声明里强调，印度尼西亚和泰国坚决遵守联合国宪章的原则和万隆会议的决议。联合声明说，苏加诺总统停留在曼谷的期间，曾经就国际局势和各种同两国共同的利益有关的问题和乃他侬总理交换了意见。双方认为，为了持久和平，任何国际上的纷争都必须通过和平方式来解决。因此，他们希望西伊里安问题也将会获得和平解决。
    联合声明还说，双方一致认为，必须促进两国之间原有的友好关系。苏加诺并且邀请泰国国王访问印度尼西亚。苏加诺已经在29日离开了泰国，目前正在日本访问。
