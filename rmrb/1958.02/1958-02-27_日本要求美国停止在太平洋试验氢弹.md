Title: Japan Demands the United States Stop Testing Hydrogen Bombs in the Pacific
Author:
Date: 1958-02-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 26th: The Japanese Foreign Ministry announced on the 25th that it had delivered a note to the United States government on the 22nd, demanding that the United States stop its planned hydrogen bomb tests in the Pacific this summer.

The note "expressed regret" that the United States insisted on conducting hydrogen bomb tests in the Pacific, disregarding Japan's protests to the United States on this matter. The note stated that Japan would reserve its right to demand compensation for any harm suffered by the Japanese people as a result of the United States' hydrogen bomb tests.

### 日本要求美国停止在太平洋试验氢弹

1958-02-27
第5版()
专栏：

    日本要求美国停止在太平洋试验氢弹
    新华社26日讯　东京消息：日本外务省在25日宣布，外务省已经在22日把一项照会递交给美国政府，要求美国停止它预定今年夏天在太平洋上进行的氢弹试验。
    照会对美国不顾日本曾经就这件事向美国提出的抗议而坚持要在太平洋上进行氢弹试验这件事“表示遗憾”。照会说，日本将保留它对美国进行氢弹试验而使日本人民遭到伤害时要求赔偿的权利。
