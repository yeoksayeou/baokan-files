Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Lays Wreath at Cemetery of Volunteer Army Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1958-02-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 17th. Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and General Su Yu laid wreaths at the Cemetery of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Martyrs near the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army this morning.

　　Today, snow was falling heavily. Premier Zhou Enlai and Marshal Chen Yi, covered in snowflakes, placed the wreaths in front of the Martyrs' Cemetery to the sound of mournful music, then stood in silent tribute for a long time.

　　Generals and officers of the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, headed by General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping, were present at the wreath-laying ceremony. Kim Kye-nam, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Korean Cabinet, who accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai to the Volunteer Army headquarters, also participated in the wreath-laying ceremony.

　　After laying the wreaths, Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Chen Yi and others walked around the cemetery to pay their respects to the Martyrs' Cemetery.

### 周总理到志愿军烈士陵园献花圈

1958-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理到志愿军烈士陵园献花圈
    新华社平壤17日电　周恩来总理、陈毅元帅、张闻天副部长、粟裕大将17日上午到中国人民志愿军总部驻地附近的中国人民志愿军烈士陵园献了花圈。
    今天，这里飘着茫茫白雪。周恩来总理和陈毅元帅等身上落满雪花，他们在哀乐声中把花圈安放在烈士陵园前，然后久久地静默致敬。
    在献花圈的时候，以杨勇上将和王平上将为首的中国人民志愿军总部的将领和军官都在场。陪同周恩来总理到志愿军总部的朝鲜内阁外务省礼仪司司长金贵南也参加了献花圈仪式。
    在献花圈以后，周恩来总理、陈毅元帅等绕行陵园一周，瞻仰烈士陵园。
