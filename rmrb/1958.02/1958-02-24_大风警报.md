Title: Gale Warning
Author:
Date: 1958-02-24
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a gale warning at 7:00 AM on the 23rd:

    Currently, cold air is moving from northwest to southeast in China. It is expected that in the next 24 hours, southern Shaanxi and Shanxi will have northerly winds of force 6-7. Northern Sichuan, Hebei, Henan, Shandong, and areas in the Huai River basin will have northerly winds of about force 6. Provinces in the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River will also have northerly winds of force 6-7, with the maximum wind force on rivers and lakes reaching force 8-9. The wind force in the Bohai Sea, Yellow Sea, and northern East China Sea will also gradually increase to force 6-7, with a northerly direction.

### 大风警报

1958-02-24
第7版()
专栏：气象台

    大风警报
    新华社23日讯中央气象科学研究所23日上午七时发布大风警报：
    目前有冷空气自我国西北向东南方向移动，预计未来二十四小时内，陕西南部和山西将有六、七级的偏北风。四川北部、河北、河南、山东和淮河流域各地将有六级左右的偏北风。长江中下游流域各省也将有六、七级的偏北风，江面湖面最大风力将达八、九级。渤海、黄海和东海北部的风力也将逐渐加强到六、七级，风向偏北。
