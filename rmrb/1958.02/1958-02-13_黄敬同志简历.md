Title: Comrade Huang Jing's Biography
Author:
Date: 1958-02-13
Page: 4

Comrade Huang Jing was born in Shaoxing, Zhejiang Province, in 1911. He is currently a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Director of the State Technological Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building.

In his youth, Comrade Huang Jing began his patriotic activities. Later, he was influenced by communist ideology and joined the Communist Party of China in 1932. In 1933, he served as the Minister of Propaganda of the Qingdao Municipal Committee of the Party. Afterwards, Comrade Huang Jing went to Beijing and engaged in revolutionary activities among students. During the "December 9th" Movement in 1935, he became one of the prominent leaders of this movement. From 1936 to 1937, Comrade Huang Jing served as the Minister of Propaganda and Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Party.

After the outbreak of the Anti-Japanese War, from 1937 to 1944, Comrade Huang Jing successively served as the Secretary of the CPC Jin-Cha-Ji District Committee, the Secretary of the Ji-Zhong District Committee, the Secretary of the Ji-Lu-Yu District Committee, and the Secretary of the Pingyuan Branch Bureau. During this period, Comrade Huang Jing resolutely implemented the instructions of the Party Central Committee and made significant contributions to extensively mobilizing the masses, persisting in the anti-Japanese guerrilla warfare behind enemy lines, and opening up and consolidating the anti-Japanese base areas behind enemy lines.

During the Liberation War, Comrade Huang Jing served as the Director of the Financial and Economic Office of the Jin-Cha-Ji Border Region, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Jin-Cha-Ji Central Bureau, and Minister of the Enterprise Department of the North China People's Government.

In 1949, Comrade Huang Jing served as the Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Mayor of Tianjin, and also served as a member of the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee. In 1952, he was appointed as the Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the Central People's Government. In 1956, he was appointed as the Director of the State Technological Commission of the People's Republic of China, while still serving as the Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building. In the years since the founding of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Huang Jing has made important contributions to urban work and the country's industrial construction. At the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Huang Jing was elected as a member of the Central Committee. (Attached picture)

### 黄敬同志简历

1958-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    黄敬同志简历
    黄敬同志，浙江绍兴人，1911年生。现为中国共产党中央委员会委员，中华人民共和国国家技术委员会主任，兼第一机械工业部部长。
    黄敬同志在青年时代，即开始了爱国活动。以后，接受了共产主义思想的影响，于1932年加入中国共产党。1933年，担任了党的青岛市委宣传部部长。以后，黄敬同志到了北京，在学生中从事革命活动。在1935年的“一二·九”运动中，成为这一运动的著名领袖之一。1936年至1937年，黄敬同志担任党的北平市委宣传部长和市委书记。
    抗日战争爆发以后，从1937年至1944年，黄敬同志先后担任中国共产党晋察冀区党委书记、冀中区党委书记、冀鲁豫区党委书记和平原分局书记等职务。在这个期间，黄敬同志坚决地执行了党中央的指示，在广泛发动群众，坚持敌后抗日游击战争，开辟和巩固敌后抗日根据地方面作了显著的贡献。
    在解放战争时期，黄敬同志曾经担任晋察冀边区财经办事处主任、中国共产党晋察冀中央局副书记和华北人民政府企业部部长等职务。
    1949年，黄敬同志担任中国共产党天津市委员会书记和天津市市长，并任中共中央华北局委员。1952年，被任命为中央人民政府第一机械工业部部长。1956年，被任命为中华人民共和国国家技术委员会主任，仍兼任第一机械工业部部长。在中华人民共和国成立以后的这几年中，黄敬同志对于城市工作和国家的工业建设，有重要的功绩。在中国共产党第八次全国代表大会上，黄敬同志被选为中央委员会委员。（附图片）
