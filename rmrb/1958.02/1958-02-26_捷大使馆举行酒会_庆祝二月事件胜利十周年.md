Title: Czechoslovakian Embassy Holds Reception Celebrating Tenth Anniversary of February Victory

Author:

Date: 1958-02-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 25th. The Czechoslovakian Embassy in China held a reception this evening to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the February Victory in Czechoslovakia. Czechoslovakian Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiak, delivered a speech at the reception. After introducing the February events, he said that it is significant to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the February events at the present time—when the socialist forces around the world are cheering their new achievements, and when the East Wind prevails over the West Wind.

Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi delivered a speech at the reception, congratulating the Czechoslovakian working class on the historical victory achieved in the February events, congratulating the Czechoslovakian people on the brilliant achievements in socialist construction, and wishing good health to the leaders of the Czechoslovakian party and government.

Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Secretary General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and others attended the reception.

After the reception, the Chinese guests watched a Czechoslovakian film at the embassy.

### 捷大使馆举行酒会  庆祝二月事件胜利十周年

1958-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    捷大使馆举行酒会
    庆祝二月事件胜利十周年
    新华社25日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使馆今天晚间举行酒会，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克二月事件胜利十周年。捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使布希尼亚克在酒会上致词。他在介绍了二月事件以后说，在目前——在全世界社会主义力量欢呼自己新的成就的时候，在东风压倒西风的时候来庆祝二月事件十周年纪念日是一件富有意义的事情。
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅在酒会上致词，祝贺捷克斯洛伐克工人阶级在二月事件中取得的历史性的胜利，祝贺捷克斯洛伐克人民在社会主义建设中取得的光辉成就和捷克斯洛伐克党和政府的领导人身体健康。
    国务院副总理聂荣臻，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、陈叔通，国务院秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长曾涌泉等参加了酒会。
    酒会后，中国客人在大使馆观看了捷克斯洛伐克电影。
