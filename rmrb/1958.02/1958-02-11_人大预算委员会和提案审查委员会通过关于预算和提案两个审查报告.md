Title: NPC Budget Committee and Proposal Examination Committee Pass Two Examination Reports on Budget and Proposals
Author:
Date: 1958-02-11
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch: The Budget Committee of the First National People's Congress and the Proposal Examination Committee of the Fifth Session of the First National People's Congress held meetings on the afternoon of the 10th.
    The Budget Committee passed the examination report on the implementation of the 1957 national budget and the draft 1958 national budget; the Proposal Examination Committee passed the examination report on the proposals. The two committees will submit these two reports to the Fifth Session of the First National People's Congress respectively.
    The Budget Committee held a meeting on the 6th to review the implementation of the 1957 national budget and the draft 1958 national budget.
    The Fifth Session of the First National People's Congress received a total of eighty-one proposals. The relevant specialized review groups of the Proposal Examination Committee analyzed and studied these proposals one by one, and put forward preliminary review opinions. The Proposal Examination Committee held its second and third plenary meetings on the 8th and 10th to conduct detailed deliberations on the preliminary review opinions of the various specialized review groups and passed them case by case.

### 人大预算委员会和提案审查委员会通过关于预算和提案两个审查报告

1958-02-11
第2版()
专栏：

    人大预算委员会和提案审查委员会通过关于预算和提案两个审查报告
    新华社10日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会预算委员会和第一届全国人民代表大会第五次会议提案审查委员会，10日下午分别举行了会议。
    预算委员会通过了关于1957年国家预算的执行情况和1958年国家预算草案的审查报告；提案审查委员会通过了关于提案的审查报告。这两个委员会将分别向第一届全国人民代表大会第五次会议提出这两个报告。
    预算委员会曾在6日举行会议，对1957年国家预算的执行情况和1958年国家预算草案进行了审查。
    第一届全国人民代表大会第五次会议共收到提案八十一件，提案审查委员会有关的专业审查小组对这些提案逐条进行了分析研究，提出了初步审查意见。提案审查委员会曾于8日、10日举行了第二次、第三次全体会议，对各专业审查小组的初步审查意见，进行了详细审议，并逐案通过。
