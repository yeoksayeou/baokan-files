Title: Indonesian Prime Minister Orders Government Departments to Stop Ordering Foreign Goods
Author:
Date: 1958-02-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 13th: The Prime Minister and Minister of Finance of Indonesia have ordered all government departments to no longer order goods from foreign countries. The order has been in effect since February 1st. This order was issued because Indonesia's foreign exchange reserves are decreasing.

### 印度尼西亚总理下令  政府部门不再订购外国货

1958-02-15
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总理下令
    政府部门不再订购外国货
    据新华社雅加达13日电　印度尼西亚总理和财政部长已经命令所有政府部门不要再向外国订购货物。命令已经从2月1日起生效。发布这项命令是因为印度尼西亚的外汇储备日益减少。
