Title: American Soldiers Mistake People for Pheasants: South Korean Child Shot

Author:
Date: 1958-02-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 31st - According to the Korean Central News Agency: The South Korean "Seoul Shinmun" reported that recently five more American soldiers in Gyeongsangnam-do shot and wounded a Korean child named Lee Hak-se.

Lee Hak-se is a third-grade elementary school student. He was shot by American soldiers while gathering firewood on a mountain near Banggidong, Samnam-myeon, Ulsan County. His injuries are very dangerous.

This time, these American soldiers shamelessly claimed that they mistook the child for a pheasant and shot at him.

### 美国兵把人当野鸡  南朝鲜小孩遭枪击

1958-02-01
第5版()
专栏：

　　美国兵把人当野鸡  南朝鲜小孩遭枪击
    新华社平壤31日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：南朝鲜“汉城新闻”报道，最近又有五个美国兵在庆尚南道开枪射击一个名叫李学世的朝鲜儿童。
    李学世是小学三年级学生。他在蔚山郡三南面芳基里下方洞附近的一座山上打柴的时候遭到美国兵的枪击，伤势十分危险。
    这一次，这些美国兵又厚颜无耻地说是错把这个小孩当作野鸡来射击了。
