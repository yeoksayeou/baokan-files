Title: Premier Zhou Returned to Pyongyang from the Volunteer Army Headquarters; Before Departure, He Celebrated the Lunar New Year's Eve with the Volunteer Army Officers and Soldiers

Author: 
Date: 1958-02-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 18th. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and General Su Yu left the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army for Pyongyang on the afternoon of the 18th. Officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and local Korean people lined the streets to see them off.

　　The open car carrying Premier Zhou Enlai and his party drove through the crowd of well-wishers for more than twenty minutes. They frequently waved to the people seeing them off and accepted flowers presented by representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and Korean children, as well as gifts from the local people.

　　General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and General Wang Ping, Political Commissar, accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai and his party to the railway station near the residence. Korean Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il and Korean Ambassador to China Li Yong-ho came from Pyongyang specially to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai and his party back to Pyongyang.

　　Rows of Chinese and North Korean national flags fluttered in the wind at the station. After Premier Zhou Enlai inspected the honor guard of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, the Chinese government delegation boarded the train directly to Pyongyang. Before leaving the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, Premier Zhou Enlai and his party had a group photo with the generals of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 18th. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and General Su Yu, accompanied by Korean Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il and others, returned to Pyongyang from the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army tonight.

　　Those who came to the station to welcome them included: Korean Vice Premiers Kim Il and Jung Joon-taek, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party Park Jung-ae and Kim Chang-man, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party Lim Hae, Minister of National Defense General Kim Kwang-hyup, Chairman of the State Planning Commission Li Jong-ok, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Vocational Trade Union Han Sang-doo, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Sung-chul.

　　Qiao Xiaoguang, member of the delegation and Chinese Ambassador to Korea, and personnel from the Embassy also came to the station to welcome them.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 18th. Officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army are celebrating the eighth Spring Festival since going abroad in Korea.

　　On the evening of the 17th, the generals and officers of the Volunteer Army Headquarters, together with Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, General Su Yu, and Kim Gwi-nam, Director of the Protocol Department of Korea, who accompanied Premier Zhou to the Volunteer Army Headquarters, celebrated the Lunar New Year's Eve. The Cultural Troupe of the Political Department of the Volunteer Army performed songs, dances, and Peking Opera at the evening party. At the end of the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai and Director Kim Gwi-nam walked onto the stage together and raised the national flags of China and Korea respectively. At this moment, there was a burst of cheers from both the stage and the audience.

　　A certain unit guarding the west coast of Korea organized a New Year's greeting team of more than 300 people to pay New Year's greetings to the former landlords and families of revolutionary martyrs. Many companies held get-togethers with the local Korean people. Officers and soldiers of a certain unit and the local people lit bonfires at night and jointly performed many song and dance programs.

　　Under the slogan of breaking with convention to celebrate the Spring Festival, a certain unit helping the Korean people build the Bongmyeong Reservoir in North Pyongan Province had two battalions of soldiers heading to the construction site to work early in the morning on the first day of the Spring Festival. Some units also helped the Korean people transport materials during the Spring Festival.

### 周总理从志愿军总部回到平壤  行前曾和志愿军官兵一同欢度除夕

1958-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理从志愿军总部回到平壤
    行前曾和志愿军官兵一同欢度除夕
    新华社平壤18日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长、张闻天副部长和粟裕大将18日下午离开中国人民志愿军总部前往平壤。中国人民志愿军的军官、战士和当地的朝鲜人民夹道欢送。
    周恩来总理一行乘坐的敞篷汽车在欢送群众的行列间行驶了二十多分钟，他们频频向欢送的人招手致意，并且接受了中国人民志愿军代表和朝鲜儿童献的鲜花以及当地人民赠送的礼品。
    中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将和政治委员王平上将一直陪送周恩来总理一行到驻地附近的火车站。朝鲜内阁副首相兼外务相南日、朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐从平壤专程赶来迎接周恩来总理一行回平壤。
    车站上成排的中朝两国国旗随风飘扬。在周恩来总理检阅了中国人民志愿军的仪仗队以后，中国政府代表团就登上火车直驶平壤。周恩来总理一行在离开中国人民志愿军总部以前，曾经同中国人民志愿军将官们一起合影留念。
    新华社平壤18日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长、张闻天副部长和粟裕大将由朝鲜内阁副首相兼外务相南日等陪同，今天晚上由中国人民志愿军总部回到平壤。
    到车站欢迎的有：朝鲜内阁副首相金一、郑准泽，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、金昌满，劳动党中央常务委员林海，民族保卫相金光侠大将，国家计划委员会委员长李钟玉，朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会委员长韩相斗，外务省副相朴成哲等。
    代表团团员、中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光和大使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
    据新华社平壤18日电　中国人民志愿军官兵在朝鲜欢度出国以来的第八个春节。
    17日晚上，志愿军总部的将领和军官们，跟周恩来总理、陈毅元帅、张闻天副部长、粟裕大将以及陪同周总理到志愿军总部来的朝鲜礼仪司司长金贵南等一起，欢度了春节除夕之夜。志愿军政治部文工团在晚会上演出了歌舞和京戏。表演结束时，周恩来总理和金贵南司长一同走上舞台，分别举起中、朝两国国旗。这时，台上台下一片欢呼。
    守卫在朝鲜西海岸的某部队，组织了一支三百多人的拜年队，给从前的老房东以及烈军属拜年。许多连队和驻地朝鲜人民进行了联欢。某部队官兵和驻地群众，在夜里燃起篝火，共同表演了许多歌舞节目。
    在打破常规过春节的口号下，帮助朝鲜人民修建平安北道凤鸣水库的某部队，春节早晨天刚亮就有两个营的士兵开赴工地劳动。还有一些部队在春节中帮助朝鲜人民运送物资。
