Title: Memorial Service for Comrade Huang Jing Held by People from All Walks of Life in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1958-02-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 13th—A grand public memorial service was held this afternoon at Zhongshan Hall by over a thousand people from all walks of life in the capital to mourn the passing of Comrade Huang Jing, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Director of the State Technological Commission, and Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building. Wreaths sent by Party and state leaders Mao Zedong, Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai were placed before Comrade Huang Jing's portrait. Prior to the memorial service, Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, personally visited Comrade Huang Jing's mourning hall to pay his respects.

The public memorial service was presided over by Zhu De, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, He Long, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Nie Rongzhen, Bo Yibo, Lin Boqu, Luo Ronghuan, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Cheng Qian, Zhang Dingcheng, and Zhang Zhizhong. The public memorial service began at 4:00 PM. After the band solemnly played funeral music, Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, presented a wreath to Comrade Huang Jing's portrait, and the entire assembly observed a moment of silence.

Subsequently, Bo Yibo, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, delivered a eulogy. He said, "Comrade Huang Jing was an outstanding member of our Party and a loyal revolutionary fighter of the Chinese working class and the Chinese people. Under the leadership of the Party, Comrade Huang Jing made important contributions to the revolutionary cause of the working class in complex and difficult environments." After recounting Comrade Huang Jing's glorious achievements in dedicating himself to the revolutionary cause, Bo Yibo emphasized, "Comrade Huang Jing possessed a high degree of enthusiasm, creativity, and loyalty to the revolutionary cause of the Party and the working class. He actively strived to integrate the Party's policies with concrete revolutionary practice in his work, making every effort to complete the tasks assigned to him by the Party. His style of being good at uniting comrades and maintaining close ties with the masses, his selfless and fervent enthusiasm for work and study, and his spirit of daring to break with conventions and being good at accepting new things are all examples for us Communists and revolutionary cadres to learn from."

Participating in today's public memorial service were also responsible persons from various departments of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, various departments of state organs, various democratic parties, various people's organizations, and representatives from all walks of life in the capital.

Before the start of the public memorial service, Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, extended his cordial condolences to Comrade Huang Jing's relatives who attended the memorial service.

Today, responsible persons from various central government organs and Beijing municipal organs, working cadres from the State Technological Commission and the First Ministry of Machine Building, as well as factory workers from Beijing, continuously went to Comrade Huang Jing's mourning hall to pay their respects. The people who went to pay their respects were in an endless stream.

Some Soviet experts from the State Technological Commission and the First Ministry of Machine Building also went to the mourning hall today to pay their respects.

### 首都各界人士公祭黄敬同志

1958-02-14
第5版()
专栏：

    首都各界人士公祭黄敬同志
    新华社13日讯　首都各界人士一千多人今天下午在中山堂隆重举行公祭，追悼中国共产党中央委员会委员、国家技术委员会主任、第一机械工业部部长黄敬同志的逝世。黄敬同志的遗像前陈放着党和国家的领导人毛泽东、朱德、刘少奇、周恩来等人送的花圈。公祭以前，中国共产党中央委员会副主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇曾亲往黄敬同志灵堂吊唁。
    公祭仪式由朱德、周恩来、陈云、彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅、李先念、聂荣臻、薄一波、林伯渠、罗荣桓、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通、程潜、张鼎丞、张治中等人担任主祭。公祭仪式在下午四时开始，乐队高奏庄严的哀乐以后，中国共产党中央委员会副主席、中华人民共和国副主席朱德向黄敬同志的遗像敬献花圈，全场默哀。
    接着，中国共产党中央委员会政治局候补委员、国务院副总理薄一波致悼词。他说：“黄敬同志是我们党的优秀的党员，是中国工人阶级和中国人民的忠实的革命战士。在党的领导下，黄敬同志曾经在复杂和艰难的环境里，对工人阶级的革命事业作出了重要的贡献。”薄一波在追述了黄敬同志献身革命事业的光荣业迹后着重指出：“黄敬同志对党和工人阶级的革命事业具有高度的积极性、创造性和忠心，他积极努力把党的方针政策同具体的革命实践在工作中结合起来，千方百计地去完成党所交给他的工作任务。他的善于团结同志、密切联系群众的作风，他的发奋忘我的工作热情和学习热情，他的敢于打破常规、善于接受新鲜事物的精神，都是我们共产党人和革命干部学习的榜样。”
    参加今天公祭仪式的，还有中国共产党中央各部门、国家机关各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人和首都各界代表人士。
    公祭开始前，中国共产党中央委员会副主席、国务院总理周恩来，曾向参加公祭的黄敬同志亲属致以亲切的慰问。
    今天，中央各机关和北京市各机关的负责人、国家技术委员会和第一机械工业部的工作干部、以及北京市的工厂职工等陆续前往黄敬同志的灵堂吊唁。前往吊唁的人们络绎不绝。
    国家技术委员会和第一机械工业部的部分苏联专家，今天也曾前往灵堂吊唁。
