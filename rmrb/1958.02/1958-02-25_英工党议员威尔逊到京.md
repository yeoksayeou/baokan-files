Title: British Labour MP Wilson Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-02-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 24th—Harold Wilson, British Labour MP and former President of the Board of Trade, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. In the evening, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a welcome banquet for Wilson.

### 英工党议员威尔逊到京

1958-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

    英工党议员威尔逊到京
    据新华社24日讯　英国工党议员、前贸易大臣哈罗德·威尔逊应中国人民外交学会邀请今天乘飞机到达北京。晚上，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若为威尔逊举行了欢迎宴会。
