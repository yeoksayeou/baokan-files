Title: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Hosts Reception Welcoming the Return of Four Science Delegations from the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1958-02-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd. Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen held a reception in Beijing tonight to welcome all members of the Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union and the three delegations from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Ministry of Higher Education, and the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences, who recently returned from the Soviet Union.

Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the reception.

Soviet science and technology advisors and experts in Beijing, as well as the counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, were also invited to attend the reception.

Nie Rongzhen said at the reception: During the three months that the Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union, headed by Guo Moruo, was in the Soviet Union, thanks to the active efforts of all comrades in the delegation, and especially thanks to the warm assistance of the Soviet Party, government, and science and technology experts, they have successfully completed their great task: exchanging views with the Soviet side on the long-term plan for the development of science and technology in our country; and signing the protocol between the Chinese and Soviet governments on jointly conducting and Soviet Union helping our country conduct major scientific and technological research. The other three delegations also signed cooperation agreements on scientific research work with the Soviet Academy of Sciences, the Soviet Ministry of Higher Education, and the All-Union Lenin Academy of Agricultural Sciences respectively. This work will play an important role in the development of our country's scientific undertakings.

Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union, said: The protocol and the cooperation agreements of the relevant departments summarize the experience of eight years of scientific cooperation between China and the Soviet Union, and have opened up a brilliant and broad future for the development of science in the two countries; after the protocol is signed, a large number of authoritative Soviet scientists and experts will come to our country to help scientific work, which further reflects the communist spirit of the Soviet Union's selfless assistance to China.

Soviet Embassy Counselor Sudarikov spoke on behalf of the embassy and Ambassador Yudin, who was unable to attend the reception due to illness. He pointed out that the implementation of the protocol and cooperation agreements will undoubtedly further prosper the scientific undertakings of the Soviet Union and China.

Vice Premier Chen Yi spoke at the meeting, encouraging Chinese scientists to take the heroic actions of the hundreds of millions of working people across the country who are vigorously building socialism as an example, contribute their own strength, and follow the road of Soviet development of science.

### 聂荣臻副总理举行酒会  欢迎四个访苏科学代表团返国

1958-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　聂荣臻副总理举行酒会
    欢迎四个访苏科学代表团返国
    新华社2日讯　聂荣臻副总理今晚在北京举行酒会，欢迎最近从苏联回国的中国访苏科学技术代表团以及中国科学院、高等教育部和中国农业科学院三个代表团全体人员。
    陈毅副总理出席了酒会。
    在北京的苏联科学技术顾问和专家、苏联驻华大使馆参赞也应邀出席了酒会。
    聂荣臻在欢迎会上说：以郭沫若为首的中国访苏科学技术代表团在苏联的三个月期间，由于全团同志的积极努力，特别是由于苏联党、政府和科学技术专家们的热情援助，已经胜利地完成了巨大任务：同苏联方面交换了对我国科学技术发展远景规划的意见；签订了中苏两国政府关于共同进行以及苏联帮助我国进行的重大科学技术研究的议定书。其他三个代表团也分别同苏联科学院、苏联高等教育部和全苏列宁农业科学院签订了科学研究工作合作协议。这些工作对于发展我国科学事业将起重要作用。
    中国访苏科学技术代表团团长郭沫若说：议定书和有关部门的合作协议，总结了八年来中苏两国科学合作的经验，并且为今后两国科学发展开辟了光辉的、广阔的前途；议定书签订后，苏联大批权威科学家和专家将要到我国来帮助科学工作，这一点更加体现了苏联对中国的无私援助的共产主义精神。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫代表大使馆和因病未能出席酒会的尤金大使讲话。他指出，议定书和合作协议的实现，毫无疑问地将进一步繁荣苏中两国的科学事业。
    陈毅副总理在会上讲话，勉励中国科学家要以全国几万万劳动人民轰轰烈烈建设社会主义的英雄行为作为榜样，贡献出自己的力量，跟着苏联发展科学的道路前进。
