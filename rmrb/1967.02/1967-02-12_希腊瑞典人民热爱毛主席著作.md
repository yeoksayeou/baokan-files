Title: Greek and Swedish People Love Chairman Mao's Works
Author:
Date: 1967-02-12
Edition: 2

Greek and Swedish people are enthusiastically embracing the works of Chairman Mao. Last year, Greece published sixteen standalone editions of Chairman Mao's works, and the Greek edition of the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" has recently been published in Athens.

The Swedish edition of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong on Politics" has been published in Stockholm.

	New China News Agency, February 11th: According to reports from Athens, the Greek edition of the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" has recently been published in Athens. This book was published by the Athens Historical Publishing House.

	In recent years, many of Chairman Mao's works have been translated into Greek and published in Greece, receiving a warm welcome from the Greek people. In 1966 alone, Greece published sixteen standalone editions of Chairman Mao's works, including: "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art," "On Protracted War," "Problems of Strategy in China's Revolutionary War," "Study and the Current Situation," "Combat Liberalism," "The Manifesto of the Communist Party," and "On Rectifying the Party's Erroneous Ideas," among others. Additionally, the Athens Historical Publishing House published two volumes of "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China" in 1966, introducing China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the Greek people.

	New China News Agency, Stockholm, February 10th: A Swedish edition of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong on Politics" was published in Stockholm at the beginning of this month.

	This collection includes twelve important works by Chairman Mao, such as: "On Practice," "On Contradiction," "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People," "China's Revolution and the Chinese Communist Party," "The Present Situation and Our Tasks," "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," "Talks with the American Correspondent Anna Louise Strong," and "Problems of Strategy in Guerrilla Warfare Against Japan," among others.

	This collection was edited by Boo Gustavsson, the editor of the Swedish "Marxist Forum" magazine, translated by Nils Holmberg, and published by the "Larben and Sjoberg" publishing house in Stockholm.

### 希腊瑞典人民热爱毛主席著作

1967-02-12
第2版()
专栏：

    希腊瑞典人民热爱毛主席著作
    希腊去年一年出版毛主席著作单行本十六种，《毛主席语录》希腊文版最近在雅典出版
    《毛泽东政治著作选集》瑞典文版在斯德哥尔摩出版
    新华社十一日讯　雅典消息：《毛主席语录》希腊文版，最近在雅典出版。这本书是由雅典历史出版社出版的。
    近年来，毛主席的许多著作已经陆续译成希腊文，在希腊出版，深受希腊人民的热烈欢迎。仅在一九六六年，希腊就出版了毛主席著作单行本十六种，其中包括：《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》、《论持久战》、《中国革命战争的战略问题》、《学习和时局》、《反对自由主义》、《〈共产党人〉发刊词》以及《关于纠正党内的错误思想》等。此外，雅典历史出版社还在一九六六年出版了两集《中国的社会主义文化大革命》的小册子，向希腊人民介绍中国的无产阶级文化大革命。
    新华社斯德哥尔摩十日电　斯德哥尔摩本月初出版了一本瑞典文版的《毛泽东政治著作选集》。
    这本选集包括毛主席的十二篇重要著作，其中有：《实践论》、《矛盾论》、《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》、《中国革命和中国共产党》、《目前形势和我们的任务》、《论人民民主专政》、《和美国记者安娜·路易斯·斯特朗的谈话》和《抗日游击战争的战略问题》等。
    这本选集是由瑞典《马克思主义论坛》杂志的编辑布奥·古斯塔夫逊编的，由尼尔斯·霍姆伯格翻译，斯德哥尔摩的“拉本和肖格伦”出版公司出版。
