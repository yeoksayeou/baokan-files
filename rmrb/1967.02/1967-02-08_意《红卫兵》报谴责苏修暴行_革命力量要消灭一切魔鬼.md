Title: Italian "Red Guards" Newspaper Condemns Soviet Revisionist Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1967-02-08
Edition: 5

	Rome, February 7 (New China News Agency) - The organ of the Italian Communist Youth League (Marxist-Leninist), the "Red Guards" newspaper, issued a statement on February 4, expressing full support for the Chinese youth who were brutally attacked by the Soviet revisionist leadership's police while paying tribute to Lenin's Mausoleum and Stalin's Tomb in Moscow. The statement strongly condemned the fascist atrocities committed by the Soviet revisionist leadership against Chinese students, stating: "This atrocity once again exposes the reactionary nature of the Soviet revisionist leadership—they fear revolutionary forces."

	The statement pointed out: "The day is not far when revolutionary forces will annihilate all the demons and monsters in history, and the imperialists and modern revisionists."

	The statement said: "Italian communist youth stand with the revolutionary youth of China and pledge to fight shoulder to shoulder with all those who oppose imperialism and struggle for socialism."

### 意《红卫兵》报谴责苏修暴行  革命力量要消灭一切魔鬼

1967-02-08
第5版()
专栏：

    意《红卫兵》报谴责苏修暴行
    革命力量要消灭一切魔鬼
    新华社七日讯　罗马消息：意大利共产主义青年联盟（马克思列宁主义）机关报《红卫兵》报，二月四日发表声明，“对向列宁陵墓和斯大林墓致敬时在莫斯科遭到（苏联）修正主义领导集团的警察残暴袭击的中国青年表示全力声援。”声明强烈谴责苏联修正主义领导集团镇压中国留学生的法西斯暴行说：“这一暴行再一次暴露了苏联修正主义领导集团的反动本性——害怕革命力量”。
    声明指出：“革命力量消灭历史上的一切妖魔鬼怪，消灭帝国主义者和现代修正主义者的日子已经不远了。”
    声明说：“意大利共产主义青年站在中国革命青年一边，保证同所有反对帝国主义和争取社会主义的人并肩奋斗。”
