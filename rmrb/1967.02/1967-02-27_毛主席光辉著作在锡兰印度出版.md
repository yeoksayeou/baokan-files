Title: Chairman Mao's Brilliant Works Published in Ceylon and India
Author:
Date: 1967-02-27
Edition: 4

Chairman Mao's brilliant works "Serve the People," "In Memory of Norman Bethune," and "The Foolish Old Man Who Removed the Mountains" have been published in Tamil by the Colombo People's Publishing House.

The translator of these three works is Kumalingam.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report from New Delhi: Chairman Mao's brilliant work "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" has been published in a single-volume edition in Malayalam (one of the main languages of South India) recently in Calicut City, Kerala State, India, by the "Marxist Publishers."

Chairman Mao's other two brilliant works have also been published in single-volume editions in Malayalam recently in Calicut City, Kerala State, India, by the "Marxist Publishers." These two works are: "Where Do Correct Ideas Come From?" and "To Be Attacked by the Enemy Is Not a Bad Thing but a Good Thing."

### 毛主席光辉著作在锡兰印度出版

1967-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    毛主席光辉著作在锡兰印度出版
    “老三篇”在科伦坡译成泰米尔文；《论人民民主专政》、《被敌人反对是好事而不是坏事》和《人的正确思想是从那里来的？》在喀拉拉邦译成马拉雅兰文。
    新华社科伦坡电　毛泽东主席的光辉著作《为人民服务》、《纪念白求恩》和《愚公移山》的泰米尔文版，已经由科伦坡人民出版社出版。
    这三篇著作的译者是库马拉林加姆。
    据新华社讯　新德里消息：毛主席的光辉著作《论人民民主专政》的马拉雅兰文（南印度主要语文之一）的单行本，最近在印度喀拉拉邦卡利卡特市，由“马克思主义者出版社”出版。
    毛主席的另两篇光辉著作的马拉雅兰文单行本，最近也在印度喀拉拉邦卡利卡特市，由“马克思主义者出版社”出版。这两篇著作是：《被敌人反对是好事而不是坏事》和《人的正确思想是从那里来的？》。
