Title: Celebrating the Day of Unified Vietnamese South Liberation Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1967-02-16
Edition: 5

	Vietnamese South National Liberation Front's Deputy Head of the Delegation to China, Nguyen Minh Phong, hosted a movie screening reception tonight to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the Day of Unified Vietnamese South Liberation Armed Forces.

	Vice Minister of National Defense Wang Shusheng, leaders from various departments and branches of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and relevant officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and other departments were invited to attend the reception.

	The Charge d'Affaires of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's Embassy in China, Le Chung Chi, Military Attache Colonel Chen Wenpeng, and diplomats and military attaches from various countries in China also attended the reception.

### 庆祝越南南方解放武装力量统一日  阮明芳代团长举行电影招待会

1967-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南南方解放武装力量统一日
    阮明芳代团长举行电影招待会
    据新华社十五日讯越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳今晚举行电影招待会，庆祝越南南方解放武装力量统一日六周年。
    国防部副部长王树声，中国人民解放军各总部、各军种、兵种领导机关负责人，外交部和有关方面负责人，应邀出席了招待会。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始，大使馆武官陈文彭上校，各国驻华使节和武官，也出席了招待会。
