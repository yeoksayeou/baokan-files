Title: Workers at the Sana'a Textile Factory in Yemen Cheer for Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-02-17
Edition: 5

Workers at the Sana'a Textile Factory in Yemen cheer enthusiastically while viewing a photograph of Chairman Mao receiving the Red Guards. They shout, "Long live Chairman Mao! Chairman Mao is the red sun in the hearts of the people of the world."

		Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
