Title: The Roar of Protest Shakes the Universe
Author: 
Date: 1967-02-07
Edition: 6

The roar of protest from the people of China shakes the universe. The "Pursue the Enemy to the End" cannon of the Red Rebels of the Railway Engineering Corps Propaganda Team echoes across the five lakes and four seas, and the winds of revolution sweep through the three mountains and five ranges. The Chinese people's angry roar shakes the universe, expressing the strongest protest against the Soviet revisionists' atrocities! The Soviet revisionist clique, a bunch of jumping jacks, willingly kowtows to U.S. imperialism as a running dog, creating one anti-China incident after another, with wolfish ambitions long harbored. Only fascist thugs would commit such heinous acts: spilling the blood of Chinese students on the streets of Moscow! Only fascist thugs would commit such heinous acts: injuring our diplomatic personnel, a rare and infamous crime! We warn the Soviet revisionist clique: if you do not confess and bow your heads, you will bear all the consequences. The revolutionary people of China will never back down! The Chinese people, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, are fearless and dauntless, daring to climb mountains of blades and traverse seas of fire. What do we fear a few spies, thugs, and hooligans for? Fish eyes cannot pass for pearls; the Chinese people can distinguish between enemies and friends. The Soviet people are our dear brothers, while the Soviet revisionist clique are traitors to Leninism and running dogs of imperialism! The great Lenin's teachings will guide the Soviet people, and together we will fight shoulder to shoulder! The banner of Leninism will once again fly high over the land of the Soviet Union, and the radiance of Mao Zedong Thought will illuminate the entire globe!

专栏：

    抗议的吼声震宇宙
    铁道兵文工团红色造反者“追穷寇”炮台五湖四海怒潮涌，三山五岳风雷起，中国人民愤怒的吼声震宇宙，最最强烈抗议苏修暴行！苏修集团，跳梁小丑，甘心投靠美帝做走狗，制造一桩桩反华事件，狼子野心，蓄谋已久。只有法西斯暴徒，才会下这样的毒手：让中国留学生的鲜血，洒在莫斯科的街头！只有法西斯暴徒，才会下这样的毒手：打伤我国的外交人员，罕见的暴行，万年遗臭！我们正告苏修集团：如果你们不认罪低头，一切后果要你们承担，中国革命人民决不罢休！毛泽东思想武装的中国人民，都是天不怕地不怕的硬骨头，刀山敢上，火海敢闯，还怕几个什么特务、暴徒，打手！鱼目岂能混珠，中国人民能分清敌和友，苏联人民是我们的亲兄弟，苏修集团是列宁主义的叛
  徒，帝国主义的走狗！伟大列宁教导的苏联人民，让我们并肩战斗！列宁主义的旗帜将重新飘扬在苏联土地上，毛泽东思想的光辉一定照遍全球！
