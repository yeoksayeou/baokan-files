Title: The China-Sri Lanka Friendship Association Hosts a Reception for Sri Lanka's National Day
Author:
Date: 1967-02-05
Edition: 6

The China-Sri Lanka Friendship Association held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the 19th anniversary of Sri Lanka's independence.

The president of the China-Sri Lanka Friendship Association, Zhang Zhiren, and the Sri Lankan ambassador to China, Gunawardena, toasted at the reception. The reception was filled with a friendly atmosphere between the peoples of China and Sri Lanka.

Relevant officials Han Nianlong, Zhou Yiping, and representatives of the revolutionary rebels attended the reception. The secretary-general of the Afro-Asian Writers' Permanent Bureau, Senanayake, and Sri Lankan friends in Beijing were also invited to attend the reception.

### 中锡友协为锡兰国庆举行招待会

1967-02-05
第6版()
专栏：

    中锡友协为锡兰国庆举行招待会
    新华社三日讯　中国锡兰友好协会今天下午举行招待会，庆祝锡兰独立十九周年。
    中锡友好协会会长张志让和锡兰驻中国大使古纳瓦德纳在招待会上祝了酒。招待会上洋溢着中锡两国人民友好的气氛。
    有关方面负责人韩念龙、周一萍和革命造反派代表出席了招待会。亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克和在京的锡兰朋友也应邀出席了招待会。
