Title: Let People Understand the Chinese Cultural Revolution - The France-China Friendship Association Holds a Photo Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1967-02-26
Edition: 5

Recently, a photo exhibition about the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China held in Paris has been warmly welcomed by the audience. A prominent position in the exhibition features a quotation from Chairman Mao: "The people, and the people alone, are the motive force in the making of world history." The exhibition displays 100 photos introducing the Chinese Cultural Revolution.

Many audience members also eagerly bought Chairman Mao's works at the exhibition.

The exhibition, which ran from February 13 to 22, was part of the France-China Friendship Association's activities for the week of Franco-Chinese friendship. During the friendship week, Chinese films were also screened.

### 让人们了解中国文化大革命  法中友协举办图片展览会

1967-02-26
第5版()
专栏：

    让人们了解中国文化大革命 
    法中友协举办图片展览会
    新华社巴黎二十三日电　最近在巴黎举办的关于中国无产阶级文化大革命的图片展览会，受到观众的热烈欢迎。在图片展览会的显著地位写着一条毛主席语录：“人民，只有人民，才是创造世界历史的动力。”展览会展出了一百张介绍中国文化大革命的图片。
    不少观众还在展览会上争着购买毛主席著作。
    从二月十三日到二十二日举行的这个展览会，是法中友好协会组织的法中友好周活动的一部分。在友好周内还放映了中国电影。
