Title: Comrade Qabo Visits the Second Machine Tool Factory, Praises China's Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-02-03
Edition: 5

	Comrade Hysni Kapo, a member of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, and Comrade Behar Shytula, a member of the Central Committee, were accompanied by Comrade Chen Yi, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, to visit the Beijing Second Machine Tool Factory, where the revolutionary rebels had won the struggle for power and achieved a double victory in revolutionary production. They were warmly welcomed by more than 2,000 revolutionary workers.

	During the visit, Comrade Kapo held a talk with the revolutionary workers, warmly congratulating them on their victories under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought.

	Comrade Kapo once again highly praised the invincible Mao Zedong Thought and highly commended China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. He pointed out that the great Chinese people, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and guided by Mao Zedong Thought, have launched and led a great proletarian cultural revolution that not only has great significance for China's future but also has great significance for the progressive people of the world and the entire international communist movement. Now, people all over the world see their future and hope in China.

	He said that the Albanian Party of Labour and people have welcomed the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China since its inception and regard the victories of the Chinese people in the cultural revolution as their own victories. He condemned the Soviet revisionist leadership for betraying Marxism-Leninism and pointed out that Comrade Mao Zedong leads China in hoisting the banner of Marxism-Leninism high; the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, which is in a new stage and proceeding successfully, will ensure that the banner of Marxism-Leninism will forever fly high in great socialist China.

	Comrade Kapo encouraged the revolutionary workers to carry out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end as Chairman Mao has taught!

	Comrade Chen Yi thanked Comrade Kapo for his enthusiastic praise of our beloved great leader Chairman Mao, his high evaluation of the great Mao Zedong Thought and China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. He hoped that the revolutionary workers would remember Comrade Kapo's encouragement and set an example in this great proletarian cultural revolution. At this time, a revolutionary rebel worker spoke, expressing their determination to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and contribute their strength to the Chinese revolution and the world revolution.

	The Beijing Second Machine Tool Factory, full of revolutionary spirit, was in high spirits today. At 10 a.m., Comrade Chen Yi accompanied Comrade Kapo and Comrade Shytula to the factory. The welcoming crowd waved red "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and shouted slogans such as "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Comrade Enver Hoxha!" "Long live the eternal friendship between the peoples of China and Albania!" The representatives of the revolutionary rebels of the Beijing Second Machine Tool Factory reported on the process of the struggle for power and the new situation of revolution and production after the power seizure. They also presented their cherished "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" and revolutionary rebel armbands to the Albanian comrades.

	Comrade Vasili Natanaili, the Albanian Ambassador to China, also accompanied the visit.

### 卡博同志参观第二机床厂赞扬我文化大革命  全世界人民从中国看到了未来和希望  革命造反派职工表示要为世界革命贡献力量

1967-02-03
第5版()
专栏：

    卡博同志参观第二机床厂赞扬我文化大革命
    全世界人民从中国看到了未来和希望
    革命造反派职工表示要为世界革命贡献力量
    据新华社二日讯　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、书记处书记希斯尼·卡博同志，中央委员贝哈尔·什图拉同志，今天由中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅同志陪同，参观革命造反派夺权斗争胜利后取得革命生产双丰收的北京第二机床厂，受到二千多名革命职工十分亲切热烈的欢迎。
    卡博同志在参观时同革命职工举行座谈，热烈祝贺革命职工在毛泽东思想指导下取得的胜利。
    卡博同志再一次高度评价战无不胜的毛泽东思想，高度赞扬我国的无产阶级文化大革命。他指出，伟大的中国人民在根据毛泽东思想并由毛主席亲自发动和领导的伟大的无产阶级文化大革命，不但对中国的未来具有重大意义，而且对世界进步人类、对整个国际共产主义运动都具有重大意义。现在，全世界人民都从中国看到了自己的未来和希望。
    他说，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和人民从中国无产阶级文化大革命开展的第一天起，就表示欢迎，并把中国人民在文化大革命中取得的胜利当作自己的胜利。他谴责苏联修正主义领导集团背叛了马克思列宁主义，并指出，毛泽东同志领导的中国举起了马克思列宁主义大旗；已经进入一个新的阶段和正在完全成功地进行的无产阶级文化大革命，将保证马克思列宁主义旗帜永远胜利地在伟大的社会主义中国高高飘扬。
    卡博同志勉励革命职工要照毛主席教导的那样，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！
    陈毅同志感谢卡博同志热情赞颂我们敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席，高度评价伟大的毛泽东思想和我国无产阶级文化大革命。他希望革命职工记住卡博同志的勉励，在这场伟大的无产阶级文化大革命运动中作出模范。这时，一位革命造反派职工发言，表示他们决心更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，为中国革命和世界革命贡献出自己的力量。
    充满着一派革命气象的第二机床厂，今天一片欢腾。上午十时，陈毅同志陪同卡博同志、什图拉同志来到工厂，欢迎群众挥动红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，一遍又一遍地欢呼：“毛主席万岁！”“恩维尔·霍查同志万岁！”“中阿两国人民的战斗友谊万古长青！”北京第二机床厂的革命造反派职工代表介绍了夺权斗争的经过，和夺权以后革命和生产出现的崭新局面。他们还把心爱的《毛泽东选集》、革命造反者袖章赠送给阿尔巴尼亚同志。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利同志也陪同参观。
