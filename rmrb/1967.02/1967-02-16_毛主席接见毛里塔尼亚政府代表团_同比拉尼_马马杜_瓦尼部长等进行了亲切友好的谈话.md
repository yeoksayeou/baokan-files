Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Mauritanian Government Delegation and Holds a Cordial and Friendly Talk with Minister Bilani Mamadou Wani and Others
Author:
Date: 1967-02-16
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao received the Mauritanian Islamic Republic's Minister of Foreign Affairs and Planning, Bilani Mamadou Wani, and the entire delegation of the Mauritanian Islamic Republic government led by him this afternoon. Chairman Mao held a cordial and friendly conversation with the Mauritanian guests. Those who participated in the reception included Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, and others. (Photo attached)

Chairman Mao received the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Planning of the Mauritanian Islamic Republic, Bilani Mamadou Wani (front, fourth from the left), and the entire delegation of the Mauritanian Islamic Republic government led by him. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 毛主席接见毛里塔尼亚政府代表团  同比拉尼·马马杜·瓦尼部长等进行了亲切友好的谈话

1967-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见毛里塔尼亚政府代表团
    同比拉尼·马马杜·瓦尼部长等进行了亲切友好的谈话
    新华社十五日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午接见毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国外交、计划部长比拉尼·马马杜·瓦尼，和由他率领的毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国政府代表团全体成员。毛主席同毛里塔尼亚客人进行了亲切友好的谈话。参加接见的，有国务院总理周恩来，副总理兼外交部长陈毅等。（附图片）
    毛主席接见毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国外交、计划部长比拉尼·马马杜·瓦尼（前左四），以及由他率领的毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国政府代表团全体成员。　新华社记者摄
