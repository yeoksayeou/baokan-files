Author:
Date: 1967-02-07
Edition: 5

Yemeni Ambassador Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Song

New China News Agency, February 6th

The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Yemeni Arab Republic to the People's Republic of China, Muhammad Abdul Wahid Hamid, accompanied by diplomatic staff from the embassy, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Song Qingling of the People's Republic of China this afternoon. Present were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Pengfei.

After the presentation of credentials, Vice Chairman Song had a friendly conversation with Ambassador Hamid.

Vice Chairman Song briefed Ambassador Hamid on the situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in our country, emphasizing the great significance of this revolution. When discussing the friendly relations between the governments and peoples of the two countries, Vice Chairman Song stated that the two countries have always been friendly, and in the struggle against imperialism and for the achievement and consolidation of national independence, the peoples of the two countries have always sympathized with and supported each other. She wished for the continuous development of friendly relations between the two countries.

Ambassador Hamid conveyed the respect and greetings of President Salah and the Yemeni people to the Chinese people and leaders, and expressed gratitude for the support and assistance provided by the Chinese government and people to the Yemeni people.

The Ambassador said that the propaganda and slander of imperialism regarding the Chinese Cultural Revolution are like summer clouds obscuring the sun, which will quickly dissipate, and the sun will eventually shine over the entire globe. Finally, he wished the great leader Chairman Mao and the Chinese people greater achievements in the Cultural Revolution.

### 也门驻华大使向宋副主席递交国书

1967-02-07
第5版()
专栏：

    也门驻华大使向宋副主席递交国书
    新华社六日讯　阿拉伯也门共和国首任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使穆罕默德·阿卜杜勒·瓦西·哈米德偕同大使馆外交人员，今天下午向中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄递交了国书。在场的有外交部副部长姬鹏飞。
    递交国书后，宋副主席同哈米德大使进行了友好的谈话。
    宋副主席向哈米德大使介绍了我国无产阶级文化大革命的情况，并强调这场文化大革命的伟大意义。谈到中也两国政府和人民之间的友好关系时，宋副主席表示，我们两国一向友好，在反对帝国主义、争取和巩固民族独立的斗争中，两国人民一贯相互同情，相互支持。她祝愿两国友好关系不断发展。
    哈米德大使转达了萨拉勒总统和也门人民对中国人民和领导人的尊敬和问候，并感谢中国政府和人民给予也门人民的支持和帮助。
    大使说，帝国主义关于中国文化大革命的宣传和造谣，好象夏天遮盖太阳的乌云，很快就会消失，太阳终将普照全球。最后，他祝愿伟大的领袖毛主席和中国人民在文化大革命中取得更大成就。
