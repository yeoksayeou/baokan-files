Title: The Chinese Embassy in Albania Holds a Reception
Author:
Date: 1967-02-17
Edition: 6

Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Albania Holds Reception
Warmly Welcomes Comrades Kabo, Balluku and Others Returning from China

According to Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, February 16 — Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Albania, Wang Jinchuan, held a reception on the 15th here to warmly welcome the return of Albanian Party and government leaders from their visit to China. He sincerely congratulated them on the complete success of their visit.

Albanian Labor Party Politburo member and Secretariat member Hysni Kapo, Politburo member and Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense Beqir Balluku, Central Committee member Beqir Shytula, Central Committee member and Chief of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army Hysni Çako, and the entire Albanian military delegation attended the reception.

### 我驻阿使馆临时代办举行招待会  热烈欢迎卡博、巴卢库等同志访华归来

1967-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    我驻阿使馆临时代办举行招待会
    热烈欢迎卡博、巴卢库等同志访华归来
    据新华社地拉那十六日电　中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川十五日在这里举行招待会，热情招待访华归来的阿尔巴尼亚党和政府的领导同志，并衷心祝贺他们的访问获得圆满成功。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、书记处书记希·卡博，劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝·巴卢库，劳动党中央委员贝·什图拉，中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部主任希·恰科以及阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团全体成员出席了招待会。
