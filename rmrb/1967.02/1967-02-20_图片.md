Title: Revolutionary Workers and Farmers in Beijing Strive for Production and Quality
Author:
Date: 1967-02-20
Edition: 3

	Revolutionary rebel workers at the Beijing First Machine Tool Factory have clear minds and sharp eyes. They listen to Chairman Mao's words the most and are determined to be models of "grasp revolution, promote production." They unite all revolutionary workers in the factory, insist on doing a good job in production, and strive for high-quality products. Xinhua News Agency photographer.

	Revolutionary masses of the Red Agriculture Production Team in the Yan'an Commune on the outskirts of Beijing are vigorously sending fertilizer to the wheat fields, striving for a bumper harvest in the summer. Xinhua News Agency photographer.
