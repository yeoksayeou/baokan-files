Title: Progressive Indian Publication Praises the Deep Impact of Our Country's Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-02-13
Edition: 4

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi: The January issue of the Urdu-language progressive monthly magazine *Humayun* published in New Delhi features an article that enthusiastically praises Chairman Mao Zedong as a great leader of the proletariat in our time, and praises China's proletarian cultural revolution as a great revolutionary movement that will undoubtedly have a profound impact.

The article states: "Chairman Mao is a great leader of the proletariat in our time. His thoughts represent the living truth of his era. He is the beloved leader of the people in China and beyond."

The article continues: "The goal of the cultural revolution is also to eliminate another powerful enemy, namely old ideas, old culture, old customs, and old habits—the remnants of the ideology of the now-deceased old feudal class and bourgeoisie that lurk within Chinese society like poisonous snakes."

The article states: "In the current proletarian cultural revolution in China, the Red Guards are taking action with great enthusiasm to advance this cultural revolution against all anti-people elements. The cultural revolution will not spare the revisionists, so the Soviet revisionist leadership naturally worries about similar turmoil in their own country. However, their worries are not without basis. The day will come when they pay the price for their betrayal."

The article condemns the anti-China slander by the Soviet revisionist leadership. The article points out, "From the propaganda attacks launched by the Soviet party leadership and revisionists worldwide, one can only draw one conclusion: the Chinese cultural revolution is a great movement that will undoubtedly have a profound impact."

### 印度进步刊物赞扬我国文化大革命深远影响  苏修进行反华诽谤是害怕人民起来造反

1967-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    印度进步刊物赞扬我国文化大革命深远影响
    苏修进行反华诽谤是害怕人民起来造反
    据新华社讯　新德里消息：新德里出版的乌尔都文进步月刊《胡马云》一月号发表一篇文章，热情地赞扬毛泽东主席是当代伟大的无产阶级领袖，赞扬中国的无产阶级文化大革命是一个必将发生深远影响的伟大革命运动。
    文章说：“毛主席是当代的一位伟大的无产阶级领袖。他的思想代表着他的时代的活的真理。他是中国和中国以外的人民的敬爱的领袖。”文章说，人民通过读毛主席的书、运用他的思想和学习他的伟大品质来表达他们对毛泽东主席的热爱。
    文章接着说：“文化革命的目标还在于扫除另一个强大的死敌，即旧思想，旧文化，旧风俗，旧习惯——现在已经死亡的旧封建阶级和资产阶级及其意识形态的遗毒。……中国的文化革命所想消灭的正是这种象毒蛇一样隐藏在中国社会组织内的敌人。”
    文章说，在中国当前的无产阶级文化大革命中，“红卫兵正怀着巨大的热情采取行动，把目前这一场反对一切反人民的东西的文化革命推向前进。文化革命不可能饶过修正主义者，因此苏修领导集团自然担心他们的国家内也会发生这一动荡。然而，他们的担心并不是没有根据的。他们为自己的背信弃义行为付出足够的代价的日子定将到来。”
    文章谴责苏联修正主义领导集团的反华诽谤。文章指出，“人们从苏联党的领导和全世界修正主义者发动的这种宣传攻击中只能得出一个结论：中国文化革命是一个必将发生深远影响的伟大运动。”
