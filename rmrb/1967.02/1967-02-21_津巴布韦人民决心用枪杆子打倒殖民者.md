Title: The People of Zimbabwe Resolve to Overthrow Colonialists with Guns
Author:
Date: 1967-02-21
Edition: 5

Chairman Mao said, “Political power grows out of the barrel of a gun.”

The people of Zimbabwe are determined to overthrow the colonialists with guns.

New China News Agency, Cairo — The official publication of the Zimbabwe African National Union, “Zimbabwe Today,” recently stated that the people of Zimbabwe will definitely overthrow the Smith regime through the current armed struggle. The publication pointed out that to overthrow the fascist regime in Zimbabwe, force is indispensable. The four million people of Zimbabwe have already started to use force and will continue to use force until the fascist rule by a minority in our country is completely eliminated.

### 津巴布韦人民决心用枪杆子打倒殖民者

1967-02-21
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席说：“枪杆子里面出政权”。
    津巴布韦人民决心用枪杆子打倒殖民者
    新华社开罗电　津巴布韦非洲民族联盟机关刊物《今日的津巴布韦》最近一期说，津巴布韦人民通过目前进行的武装斗争，定将推翻史密斯政权。这家刊物指出，要推翻津巴布韦法西斯政权，武力是必不可少的。四百万津巴布韦人民，已经开始使用武力，并将继续使用武力，直到少数人在我国实行法西斯统治被彻底消灭为止。
