Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation to Romania
Author:
Date: 1967-02-05
Edition: 6

Chinese Government Trade Delegation to Romania

New China News Agency, February 4th: The head of the Chinese Government Trade Delegation, Jia Shi, departed today by plane to Romania to sign the 1967 trade agreement.

Those who went to the airport to see him off included officials from the Ministry of Foreign Trade and the Counselor of the Romanian Embassy in China, Istrai Dai.

### 我政府贸易代表团赴罗马尼亚

1967-02-05
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团赴罗马尼亚
    新华社四日讯　中国政府贸易代表团团长贾石，今天上午乘飞机前往罗马尼亚商签一九六七年度贸易议定书。
    前往机场送行的，有对外贸易部负责人和罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆参赞伊斯特拉代等。
