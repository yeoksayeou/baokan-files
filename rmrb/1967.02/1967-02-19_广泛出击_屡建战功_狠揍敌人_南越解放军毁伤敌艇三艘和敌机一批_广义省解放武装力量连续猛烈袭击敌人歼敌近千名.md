Title: Widespread Attacks, Repeated Victories, and Heavy Blows to the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1967-02-19
Edition: 5

The Liberation Army of South Vietnam has destroyed and damaged three enemy boats and a batch of enemy aircraft. The armed forces of Quang Ngai Province have continuously launched fierce attacks on the enemy, killing nearly a thousand enemy soldiers.

Xinhua News Agency, February 15th: Saigon News: On the 15th, the armed forces of South Vietnam attacked the American military airfield in Qui Nhon, Binh Dinh Province, and ambushed American minesweepers on a river near Saigon, causing casualties and losses to the enemy.

According to reports from Western news agencies, on the morning of the 15th, brave and intelligent soldiers of the Liberation Army launched an attack on the American military airfield in Qui Nhon. They infiltrated the airfield and blew up and damaged a batch of helicopters on the tarmac with explosives.

On the morning of the 15th, two American minesweepers were ambushed by the Liberation Army on a river 24 kilometers southeast of Saigon. One minesweeper was seriously damaged by heavy artillery fire from both banks and immediately ran aground, while another was also hit. Three hours later, another American minesweeper was blown up and sunk on the same river after hitting a mine laid by the Liberation Army. It was also reported that on the night of the 14th, the Liberation Army had shelled a unit of the U.S. 4th Infantry Division camping near Pleiku, causing casualties to the enemy.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on the 17th, the armed forces of Quang Ngai Province in South Vietnam have continuously launched fierce attacks on the enemy along the first highway from January 15th to February 2nd, killing nearly a thousand enemy soldiers, including more than 500 American invaders and 200 South Korean mercenaries.

The report said that at the end of January, a camp of American invaders, divided into two groups, launched a "sweep" operation in Phu Nhuan and Phu Long communes in De Phu District. Local forces and guerrillas immediately joined the battle, launching attacks, ambushes, and encirclements, thwarting the enemy's multiple ground and air assaults. They killed and wounded 275 American invaders, shot down four enemy aircraft, and damaged three. On the night of January 31st, local forces in De Phu District launched a fierce attack on another camp of American invaders in the Go Vap area, killing 200 American invaders.

The local forces in Son Tinh District thwarted the "sweep" operation by two battalions of the U.S. Marine Corps in Thanh Ky Commune on January 28th, killing more than 50 enemy soldiers. On February 1st, they also cooperated closely with the military and people of Binh Son District to launch a fierce counterattack against the "sweep" operation by two battalions of South Korean mercenaries in Binh Chu Commune, Binh Son District, and Thanh Ha Commune, Son Tinh District, completely annihilating a company of South Korean mercenaries, killing and wounding 200 enemy soldiers.

### 广泛出击　屡建战功　狠揍敌人  南越解放军毁伤敌艇三艘和敌机一批  广义省解放武装力量连续猛烈袭击敌人歼敌近千名

1967-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    广泛出击　屡建战功　狠揍敌人
    南越解放军毁伤敌艇三艘和敌机一批
    广义省解放武装力量连续猛烈袭击敌人歼敌近千名
    新华社十五日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量十五日袭击了庆和省的芽庄美军机场，伏击了在西贡附近一条河上行驶的美国扫雷艇，使敌人遭到了伤亡和损失。
    据西方通讯社报道，十五日晨，英勇机智的解放军战士向芽庄美军机场发动进攻。他们潜入机场，用炸药炸毁和炸伤一批在停机坪上的直升飞机。
    十五日上午，两艘美国扫雷艇在距离西贡东南二十四公里的一条河上遭到解放武装力量的伏击。一艘扫雷艇被来自两岸的猛烈炮火严重击伤，当即搁浅，另一艘也被击伤。三个小时以后，又有一艘美国扫雷艇在同一条河上因触发解放武装力量布设的水雷而被炸沉。另据报道，解放武装力量在十四日深夜曾炮轰了在波来古附近露宿的美国第四步兵师的一支部队，使敌人遭到了伤亡。
    据新华社河内十七日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广义省解放武装力量在一月十五日到二月二日期间，连续在第一号公路沿线猛烈袭击敌人，消灭敌人近千名，其中包括美国侵略军五百多名和南朝鲜帮凶军二百名。
    报道说，一月底，美国侵略军一个营，分两路向德普县普宁和普光等乡发动“扫荡”。当地地方部队和游击队立即投入战斗，时而袭击，时而伏击，时而把敌人包围起来，粉碎了敌人从地面和空中发动的多次进攻，共打死、打伤美国侵略军二百七十五名，击落敌机四架，击伤三架。一月三十一日夜间，德普县地方部队在鹅怀地区猛烈袭击前来救援的另一营美军的驻地，消灭美国侵略军二百名。
    山晶县地方部队在一月二十八日粉碎了美国海军陆战队两个营对靖溪乡的“扫荡”，消灭敌人五十多名。二月一日又同平山县军民密切配合作战，猛烈反击南朝鲜帮凶军两个营对平山县平珠乡和山晶县靖和乡发动的“扫荡”，全部歼灭南朝鲜帮凶军一个连，共打死、打伤敌人二百名。（附图片）
