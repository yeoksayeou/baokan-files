Title: Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in the Hearts of the World's Revolutionary People
Author: Běijīng Diànyǐng Xuéyuàn Dōngfāng Hóng Gōngshè Hóng Huà Bīng
Date: 1967-02-14
Edition: 5

Chairman Mao is the great savior of the oppressed people and oppressed nations of the world. He is the great leader of the world's revolutionary people. Chairman Mao is the red sun in the hearts of the world's revolutionary people. Under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, the world's revolutionary people have found the correct road to liberation. Mao Zedong Thought is the guiding light of the world's revolutionary people. It is the great banner of the world's revolutionary people. It is the great weapon of the world's revolutionary people. It is the great truth of the world's revolutionary people. It is the great power of the world's revolutionary people. It is the great hope of the world's revolutionary people. It is the great future of the world's revolutionary people.

Long live Chairman Mao!
Long live Mao Zedong Thought!
Long live the great victory of the world's revolutionary people!
