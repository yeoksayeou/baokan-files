	Brief News

	Indian Mizos Arm Forces Attack Indian Reactionary Army

	On February 10, the armed forces of the Mizo people in Assam, northeastern India, ambushed a reactionary army unit, killing ten and wounding many. On February 12, the Mizo armed forces ambushed the reactionary army twice. On February 15, they again opened fire with automatic weapons on some reactionary army outposts. On February 15, the Naga people also attacked and captured some armed reactionary officials. West German Youth Protest U.S. Aggression in Vietnam

	Several hundred West German youths held a demonstration and march in front of the U.S. Consulate in Frankfurt on the afternoon of the 18th, protesting against U.S. aggression in Vietnam. They angrily shouted, "We don't believe Johnson's words! Killing people is still killing people!" South Vietnamese Puppet Army and South Korean Mercenary Troops Clash

	Part of the 5th Battalion of the 2nd Division of the South Vietnamese puppet army stationed in Thuan Phuoc County, Quang Nam Province, recently clashed with a South Korean mercenary unit. Just as both sides were fighting fiercely, U.S. aggressor forces dispatched aircraft to strafe and bomb them. As a result, more than 70 South Korean mercenaries and 40 puppet soldiers were killed.

### 简明新闻

1967-02-21
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    印度米佐族武装袭击印反动军队
    二月十日，印度东部阿萨姆邦米佐部族人民的武装力量伏击了一支反动军队，打死十人，打伤多人。十二日，米佐部族武装两次伏击反动军队。十五日，又用自动武器向反动军队的一些哨所开火。那加族人民二月十五日也袭击和俘虏了一些有武装护卫的反动官员。西德青年示威抗议美国侵略越南
    几百名西德青年十八日下午在法兰克福的美国驻西德总领事馆前举行示威游行，反对美国侵略越南。他们愤怒高呼“我们不相信约翰逊的话！屠杀人民终究是屠杀人民！”。南越伪军和南朝鲜帮凶军队火并
    盘踞在广南省升平县的南越伪军第二师第五团的一部，最近和一股南朝鲜帮凶军发生火并。正当双方打得不可开交的时候，美国侵略军派遣飞机，又给了他们一顿扫射轰炸。结果，有七十多名南朝鲜帮凶军和四十名伪军丧命。
                            （据新华社讯）
