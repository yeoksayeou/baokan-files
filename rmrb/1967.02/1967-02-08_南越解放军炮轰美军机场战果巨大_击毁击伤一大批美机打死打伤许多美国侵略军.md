Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Bombards U.S. Military Airfields with Significant Results
Author:
Date: 1967-02-08
Edition: 4

South Vietnamese Liberation Army Bombards U.S. Military Airfields with Significant Results

	Destruction and Damage to a Large Number of U.S. Aircraft; Many American Invaders Killed and Wounded

	According to a Xinhua News Agency report dated February 7, Saigon news: On the morning of February 7, the armed forces of the South Vietnamese liberation forces bombarded two U.S. military airfields, destroying and damaging a number of enemy aircraft.

	According to reports from Western news agencies, the two airfields that were bombed are the Binh Thuy airfield near Saigon and an airstrip belonging to the U.S. First Cavalry Division northwest of Qui Nhon. Preliminary information indicates that the liberation forces destroyed and damaged nine enemy aircraft at Binh Thuy airfield; they also damaged several aircraft at the airstrip of the First Cavalry Division and killed and wounded a number of U.S. troops at the airstrip.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 7: According to a report from the South Vietnamese Liberation News Agency, on the night of January 21 to the early morning of January 22, the liberation forces in the province of Phuoc Long again attacked the U.S. military airfield in Phuoc Long. Preliminary information indicates that three U.S. helicopters were destroyed, many were damaged, and more than 100 enemy personnel were killed and wounded, the majority of whom were U.S. invaders.

	This is the fourth attack by the liberation forces in Phuoc Long province on this airfield since May 28 of last year. In the previous three attacks, a total of 54 enemy aircraft were destroyed and damaged, and 246 enemy personnel were killed and wounded, the majority of whom were U.S. invaders.

### 南越解放军炮轰美军机场战果巨大  击毁击伤一大批美机打死打伤许多美国侵略军

1967-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    南越解放军炮轰美军机场战果巨大
    击毁击伤一大批美机打死打伤许多美国侵略军
    据新华社七日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量七日清晨炮轰美国侵略军的两个机场，击毁击伤敌机一批。
    据西方通讯社报道，被轰击的两个机场是西贡西南芹苴附近的平绥机场和归仁西北美国第一骑兵师的一个简易机场。据初步透露的消息，解放武装力量击毁击伤平绥机场的敌机九架；打坏了第一骑兵师的简易机场的一些飞机，打死打伤这个机场上的一批美军。
    新华社河内七日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方永隆省解放武装力量，一月二十一日夜间到二十二日凌晨，再次袭击永隆美军机场。据初步消息，炸毁美国直升飞机三架，炸伤多架，打死打伤敌人一百多名，其中大部分是美国侵略军。
    这是永隆省解放武装力量从去年五月二十八日以来，对这个机场进行的第四次袭击。在前三次袭击中，共炸毁炸伤敌机五十四架，打死打伤敌人二百四十六名，其中大部分是美国侵略军。
