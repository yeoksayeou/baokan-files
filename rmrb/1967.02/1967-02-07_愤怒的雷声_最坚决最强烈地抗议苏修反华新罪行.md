Title: The Thunderous Voice of Anger
Author: Ji Peng
Date: 1967-02-07
Edition: 6

Under the dim night of Moscow,
A group of clowns stretched out their evil hands,
Like bats fearing the sun,
They fear our embassy's news window.

Only because the window displays the brightest sun—
Our great leader Chairman Mao inspecting the Little Red Guards.
Only because the window exposes a farce—
The traitors of Leninism trampling on the homeland of the October Revolution.

You fear the facts, you fear the truth,
You fear the invincible Mao Zedong Thought.
You also fear your shameful footprints,
You fear the ironclad evidence of your crimes—the half-broken wooden post of the window…

Robbery and assault show your weakness,
Fascist actions foretell your short lifespan,
Destroying the window cannot erase your crimes,
The evidence of your anti-China actions will forever be recorded in history.

The blood of our students on the Red Square on January 25 still shines with unyielding red light,
The surging waves of protests from 700 million people,
The thunderous voice of protests from 700 million people shakes the heavens.

Warning, new tsars of the Kremlin,
The revolutionary torrent will bury you.
The great and brilliant red flag of Mao Zedong Thought,
Will surely fly high all over the world!

### 愤怒的雷声——最坚决最强烈地抗议苏修反华新罪行
纪鹏
1967-02-07
第6版()
专栏：

    愤怒的雷声
    ——最坚决最强烈地抗议苏修反华新罪行
    纪鹏
    趁着莫斯科的昏暗的夜色，
    一群小丑伸出罪恶的魔掌，
    象害怕太阳的蝙蝠，
    害怕我国使馆的新闻橱窗。
    只因橱窗里高悬着最红最红的太
      阳——
    伟大的导师毛主席在检阅红小
      将。
    只因橱窗里揭穿了一幕丑剧——
      列宁主义的叛徒在践踏十月革
      命的故乡。
    你们害怕事实，害怕真理，
      害怕战无不胜的毛泽东思想。
    你们也害怕自己可耻的脚印，
      害怕自己罪恶的铁证——那橱
      窗的半截木桩……
    抢劫、殴打表现你们的虚弱，
    法西斯的行径预示你们的寿命不
      长，
    捣毁橱窗抹不掉你们的罪行，
    反华的罪证将永远记在历史上。
    红场“一·二五”我留学生的鲜
      血，
    还闪射着不屈的红光，
    七亿人民示威的洪流掀巨浪，
    七亿人民抗议的雷声震天响。
    警告你，克里姆林宫的新沙皇，
      革命的洪流将把你们埋葬。
    伟大的光辉的毛泽东思想红旗，
      必将在全世界高高飘扬！
