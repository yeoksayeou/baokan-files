Title: China and Romania Sign 1967 Trade and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1967-02-17
Edition: 6

China and Romania Sign 1967 Trade and Payment Agreement

NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY BUCHAREST, February 14 - After friendly consultations between the two countries' government trade delegations, the 1967 trade and payment agreement between China and Romania was signed in Bucharest on February 14.

The Chinese government trade delegation leader Jia Shi and the Romanian government trade delegation leader, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade V. Striobor, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective countries.

According to the agreement, the trade volume between China and Romania in 1967 will see a new increase compared to 1966. In 1967, China will supply Romania with minerals, steel, hard alloys, machine tools, chemical products, rice, aquatic products, textiles, and light industrial products, among others. Romania will supply China with drilling equipment, cargo ships, automobiles, chemical products, petroleum products, and steel pipes, among others.

Attending the signing ceremony were Romanian Minister of Foreign Trade Gheorghe Chiu and the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, Li Bin, among others.

After the signing, Striobor hosted a banquet to celebrate the signing of the agreement.

### 中罗签订今年换货和付款协定

1967-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    中罗签订今年换货和付款协定
    新华社布加勒斯特十四日电　经过两国政府贸易代表团在友好的气氛中进行会谈以后，中国和罗马尼亚一九六七年换货和付款协定于十四日在布加勒斯特签字。
    中国政府贸易代表团团长贾石和罗马尼亚政府贸易代表团团长、外贸部副部长瓦·斯特里奥博尔分别在协定上签字。
    按照协定规定，中罗一九六七年度的贸易额将比一九六六年度有新的增长。在一九六七年度，中国将供应罗马尼亚的货物有：矿产品，钢材，硬质合金，机床，化工产品，大米，水产品，纺织品和轻工业品等；罗马尼亚供应中国的货物有：钻探设备，货船，汽车，化工产品，石油产品和钢管等。
    出席签字仪式的有：罗马尼亚外贸部长格·乔拉和中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办李斌等。
    签字后，斯特里奥博尔举行了宴会，祝贺协定的签订。
