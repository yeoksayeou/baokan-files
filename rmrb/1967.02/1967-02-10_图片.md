Title: Mauritania's People Hold Unlimited Admiration for Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-02-10
Edition: 4

A Mauritania youth, with boundless reverence, stands before a portrait of Chairman Mao and tells his family, "This is the great Chairman Mao!" The photo was taken by a Xinhua News Agency reporter.
