Title: The Central Committee Calls for Action, Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants Show Heroism
Author:
Date: 1967-02-24
Edition: 6

The Central Committee has issued a call to action, and the poor and lower-middle peasants are displaying great heroism. They are taking on two heavy burdens at once, firing the first shot for the spring plowing season.
