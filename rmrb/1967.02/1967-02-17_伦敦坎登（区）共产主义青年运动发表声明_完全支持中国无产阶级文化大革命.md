Title: Camden (District) Communist Youth Movement in London Issues Statement Fully Supporting the Chinese Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-02-17
Edition: 5

London, February 14 -- The newly established Camden (District) Communist Youth Movement in London recently issued a statement saying that it fully supports the Chinese proletarian cultural revolution and the decision of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party on the proletarian cultural revolution (the Sixteen Points).

This organization is composed of a group of British youths who oppose revisionism and were expelled from the Communist Youth League of Britain last year for adhering to the Marxist-Leninist position.

The Camden Communist Youth Movement's statement said: "This proletarian cultural revolution, led by Chairman Mao Zedong, is a resolute effort by the Chinese people to eliminate the reactionary ideas and actions that still exist in Chinese society and within the Chinese Communist Party, and to further strengthen China's socialist and communist construction.

"We, as young people, in the spirit of proletarian internationalism, extend our best wishes to the revolutionary Red Guards, young workers, and students participating in this unprecedented activity in China."

The statement pointed out that the Chinese people are "marching forward in big strides to build a socialist society and correctly applying Chairman Mao Zedong's development of Marxism-Leninism in the contemporary era at every stage of their advance."

### 伦敦坎登（区）共产主义青年运动发表声明  完全支持中国无产阶级文化大革命

1967-02-17
第5版()
专栏：

    伦敦坎登（区）共产主义青年运动发表声明
    完全支持中国无产阶级文化大革命
    据新华社伦敦十四日电　新成立的伦敦坎登（区）共产主义青年运动最近发表声明说，它完全支持中国无产阶级文化大革命和中国共产党中央委员会关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定（十六条）。
    这个组织，是由反对修正主义的一批英国青年组成的，他们由于坚持马克思列宁主义的立场，在去年被英国共产主义青年团“开除”。
    坎登共产主义青年运动的声明说：“这场无产阶级文化革命，是由毛泽东主席领导的中国人民的一次毅然决然的努力，去铲除在中国社会和中国共产党内仍然存在的反动思想和行为，进一步加强中国的社会主义和共产主义建设。
    “我们青年本着无产阶级国际主义精神，向参加这次史无前例活动的中国革命的红卫兵、青年工人和学生，表示最好的祝愿。”
    声明指出，中国人民在“大踏步前进建设社会主义社会，并且在他们前进的每一阶段都正确运用毛泽东主席在当代所发展的马克思列宁主义”。
