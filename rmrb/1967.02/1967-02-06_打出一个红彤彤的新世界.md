Title: Strike Out a Red, Radiant New World
Author: Jiěfàngjūn Mǒu Bù Zhànshì Yèyù Chànggēduì
Date: 1967-02-06
Edition: 6

	Strike out a red, radiant new world
	Soldiers' Amateur Singing Troupe of a Certain Unit of the PLA

	甲: Mountains connect with mountains,
	乙: Seas connect with seas,
	甲: The revolutionary torrent,
	乙: Surges and roars.
	甲: North and south of the Yangtze,
	乙: Inside and outside the Great Wall,
	合: The proletarian revolutionary faction unites!
	甲: Crush the enemy's counterattack,
	乙: Smash through all obstacles!
	甲: The capitalist roaders' reactionary line,
	乙: Is beginning to crumble.
	甲: But a handful of bad elements,
	乙: Are still scheming and playing tricks,
	甲: Trying to lead the revolution astray,
	乙: Attempting to sabotage the Cultural Revolution.
	甲: What material incentives,
	乙: What money as the commander,
	甲: What living benefits,
	乙: What monetary bribes,
	合: Pfft! Get lost!
	甲: We are revolutionary soldiers,
	乙: We are not slaves to money!
	合: Chairman Mao cares for us revolutionaries,
	甲: Most affectionately,
	乙: Most concerned,
	甲: Most supportive,
	乙: Most trusting!
	甲: Be red rebels,
	乙: Be steadfast revolutionaries!
	甲: See the farthest,
	乙: Think the broadest;
	甲: Most courageous,
	乙: Most ambitious;
	甲: View money as dung,
	乙: See benefits as dust!
	甲: We revolutionary soldiers,
	乙: Are infinitely loyal,
	甲: Infinitely loving,
	乙: Infinitely believing,
	甲: Infinitely worshipping
	乙: The great thought of Mao Zedong!
	甲: Open your eyes,
	乙: Look to the future;
	甲: Hold your head high,
	乙: March forward!
	甲: Rifle in hand,
	乙: Book in heart;
	甲: Even if waves are high,
	乙: Even if demons and monsters!
	甲: Expose their plots,
	乙: Smash their sugar-coated bullets;
	甲: Kick the anti-party elements,
	乙: Off the stage;
	甲: Trample the ghosts and snakes,
	乙: Underfoot;
	甲: Beat the old world,
	乙: Into a mess;
	甲: Kill the harmful insects,
	乙: Wail in despair!
	合: Let Chairman Mao's revolutionary line,
	Shine brightly and radiate!
	甲: Power,
	乙: We take;
	甲: The country,
	乙: We arrange.
	甲: "Grasp revolution,
	乙: Promote production",
	甲: Two heavy burdens,
	乙: We carry!
	合: Following Chairman Mao,
	Strike out a red, radiant new world!

### 打出一个红彤彤的新世界
解放军某部战士业余演唱队
1967-02-06
第6版()
专栏：

    打出一个红彤彤的新世界
    解放军某部战士业余演唱队甲：山连着山，乙：海连着海，甲：革命洪流，乙：汹涌澎湃。甲：大江南北，乙：长城内外，合：无产阶级革命派联合起来！甲：打垮敌人反扑，乙：摧毁重重障碍！甲：资产阶级反动路线，乙：开始土崩瓦解。甲：可是一小撮坏蛋，乙：还在阴谋耍赖，甲：妄想将革命引入歧途，乙：企图把文化大革命破坏。甲：什么物质刺激，乙：什么钞票挂帅，甲：什么生活福利，乙：什么金钱收买，合：呸！滚开！甲：我们是革命战士，乙：我们不是金钱的奴才！合：毛主席对我们革命派，甲：最体贴，乙：最关怀，甲：最支持，乙：最信赖！甲：做红色造反者，乙：做坚定的革命派！甲：看得最远，乙：想得最开；甲：最有胆识，乙：最有气魄；甲：视金钱如粪土，乙：看福利如尘埃！甲：我们革命战士，乙：对伟大的毛泽东思想，甲：无限忠诚，乙：无限热爱，甲：无限信仰，乙：无限崇拜！甲：放开眼界，乙：看未来；甲：昂首阔步，乙：朝前迈！甲：钢枪在手，乙：宝书在怀；甲：哪怕恶浪重重，乙：哪怕妖魔鬼怪！甲：是阴谋诡计，乙：把它揭开；甲：是糖衣炮弹，乙：把它打碎；甲：把反党分子，乙：踢下台；甲：把牛鬼蛇神，乙：脚下踩；甲：打得旧世界，乙：落花流水；甲：杀得害人虫，乙：呜呼哀哉！合：让毛主席的革命路线，光芒四
    射大放异彩！甲：政权，乙：我们来掌；甲：江山，乙：我们安排。甲：“抓革命，乙：促生产”，甲：两副重担，乙：挑起来！合：跟着毛主席，
    打出一个红彤彤的新世界！
