Title: Mauritania's Minister of Foreign Affairs and Planning Thanks Chinese Leaders
Author:
Date: 1967-02-24
Edition: 5

The Chinese Embassy in Mauritania received a friendly telegram on February 21 from Bilani Mamadou Wane, Mauritania's Minister of Foreign Affairs and Planning, addressed to Chen Yi, China's Minister of Foreign Affairs. In the telegram, Bilani expressed heartfelt thanks to the great revolutionary leader Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Minister Chen Yi for the warm reception given to the Mauritania delegation led by him during their recent visit to China.

In his telegram to Chen Yi, Bilani said: "During my visit to the People's Republic of China, I and our delegation received a very warm and cordial reception. I would like to express my heartfelt thanks to you. I also extend my thanks to the great revolutionary leader, the initiator of the Cultural Revolution, Chairman Mao Zedong, and Premier Zhou Enlai."

Bilani stated: "The results of our talks are very positive, which have established a solid foundation for cooperation between our two countries in all fields. I am deeply convinced that, for the benefit of our two countries and the friendship and understanding between our peoples, this cooperation will continue to strengthen."

### 毛里塔尼亚外交、计划部长电谢我领导人  深信两国友好合作必将日益巩固

1967-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    毛里塔尼亚外交、计划部长电谢我领导人
    深信两国友好合作必将日益巩固
    新华社二十三日讯　中国驻毛里塔尼亚大使馆二十一日收到毛里塔尼亚外交、计划部长比拉尼·马马杜·瓦尼给陈毅外交部长的一份友好电。比拉尼部长在电报中对由他率领的毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国政府代表团最近在中国访问期间受到的热情接待，向伟大的革命领袖毛泽东主席以及周恩来总理和陈毅外交部长表示衷心的感谢。
    比拉尼部长在给陈毅外交部长的电报中说：“在我访问中华人民共和国期间，我和我们代表团受到十分热情和亲切的接待，我谨向你表示衷心的感谢。我也向伟大的革命领袖、文化革命的创始人毛泽东主席和周恩来总理表示感谢。”
    比拉尼部长说：“我们会谈的成果是非常积极的，这使我们两国建立了在各方面进行合作的牢固的联系。我深信，为了我们两国的利益、为了我们两国人民的友谊和谅解，这种联系必将日益巩固。”
