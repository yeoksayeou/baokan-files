Title: Japanese People Highly Praise China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-02-26
Edition: 5

The Japanese people have highly praised China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The picture shows revolutionary masses from Yamaguchi Prefecture, Japan, with banners reading "Long Live the Victory of Mao Zedong Thought" and holding up banners reading "Long Live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution" as they march through Iwakuni City.
