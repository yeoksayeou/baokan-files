Title: "Red Flag" Magazine Editorial "We Must Correctly Handle Cadres" Published as a Separate Volume
Author:
Date: 1967-02-26
Edition: 2

The single-volume edition of the editorial "We Must Correctly Handle Cadres" from the fourth issue of 1967 of "Red Flag" magazine has been published by the People's Publishing House. Starting from the 26th, it will be distributed in Beijing and will be gradually distributed nationwide.

### 《红旗》杂志社论《必须正确地对待干部》单行本出版

1967-02-26
第2版()
专栏：

    《红旗》杂志社论《必须正确地对待干部》单行本出版
    新华社二十五日讯　《红旗》杂志一九六七年第四期社论《必须正确地对待干部》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，从二十六日起在北京发行，并将在全国各地陆续发行。
