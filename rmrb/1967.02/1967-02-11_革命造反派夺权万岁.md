Title: Long Live the Revolutionary Rebels Seizing Power
Author: Lǐ Jiāguāng, Chén Liángyǒu, Hàn Shàoqīng
Date: 1967-02-11
Edition: 6

Revolutionary Rebels Seizing Power, Hurrah!
(Duet)

A: Revolutionary rebels,
B: Each one is bold and spirited.
A: Standing tall and firm,
B: Steadfast as iron and steel.
A: Thunder cannot shake them,
B: Nor can storms blow them down.
A: With Mao Zedong Thought as the banner,
B: They hold the revolutionary flag high.
A: To consolidate the dictatorship of the proletariat,
B: To uproot revisionism,
A: To defend Mao Zedong Thought,
B: To hasten the arrival of communism,
A: Fearless of heaven and earth,
B: Fearless of ghosts and gods.
A: Smashing through all obstacles,
B: Breaking through all resistance.
A: They have forged a revolutionary path,
B: And unleashed a storm of seizure of power.
A: Look! The dawn is ablaze,
    Red flags fluttering everywhere;
B: Listen! The war drums thunder,
    Songs soaring to the heavens.
A: All revolutionary rebels,
B: Under the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought,
A: Unite,
B: And wage the struggle to seize power.
    Resolve firmly,
    Unyielding!

Both: Seize power! Seize power! Seize power!

A: Those few who walk the capitalist road in power,
B: Those stubborn few who implement the reactionary bourgeois line,
A: They face imminent doom,
B: And resort to various tricks:
A: Pretending to confess and reflect,
B: Secretly sabotaging and spreading rumors;
A: Cutting off electricity, water, and transportation,
B: Threatening, bribing, offering money.
A: They play one trick in the open,
B: And another in secret,
A: Trying to deceive and escape.
B: Bah! In your dreams!
A: Bah! Impossible!
A: Revolutionary rebels,
B: Unshaken by wind and waves.
A: Money cannot sway their revolutionary spirit,
B: With iron shoulders, they bear the heavy burden.
Both: "The world is ours.
    The country is ours.
    Society is ours.
    If we do not speak, who will?
    If we do not act, who will?"

A: With Chairman Mao at the helm,
B: With Chairman Mao supporting us,
Both: Seize power! Seize power! Seize power!

A: Launch the general assault,
B: Sound the charge!
A: Seize the party power!
B: Seize the state power!
A: Seize the economic power!
B: Seize the cultural power!
Both: Seize it! Seize it!

A: Above all, most importantly,
B: Holding the reins of power is most important.
A: Our people's army,
B: Is personally created by Chairman Mao.
A: Most obedient to the Party,
B: Holding the red flag high.
Both: The People's Liberation Army should resolutely support the broad masses of the left.
A: This is Chairman Mao's order,
B: This is Chairman Mao's call.
A: We will carry it out resolutely,
B: We will remember it forever.
A: Comrades of the revolutionary rebels,
    We stand firmly with you,
    Pledging to be your strong support,
B: Fighting side by side to drive away tigers and leopards.
A: Sweeping away all filth and dirt,
B: Sweeping away all monsters and demons.
Both: If the enemy does not surrender,
    We will resolutely and thoroughly annihilate them.

### 革命造反派夺权万岁
李加光　陈良友　韩少卿
1967-02-11
第6版()
专栏：

    革命造反派夺权万岁
    （对口词）
    解放军某部　李加光　陈良友　韩少卿甲：革命造反派，乙：个个志气豪。甲：顶天立地，乙：钢铸铁造。甲：霹雳轰不动，乙：狂飙刮不倒。甲：毛泽东思想挂帅，乙：革命大旗举得高。甲：为了巩固无产阶级专
      政，乙：为了把修正主义连根
      挖掉，甲：为了捍卫毛泽东思
      想，乙：为了共产主义早日来
      到，甲：天不怕，地不怕，乙：鬼不怕，神不怕。甲：砸碎层层障碍，乙：冲破重重阻挠。甲：闯出了一条革命路，乙：掀起了夺权的大风
      暴。甲：看！朝霞如火，
    红旗处处飘；乙：听！战鼓隆隆，
    歌声入云霄。甲：一切革命造反派，乙：在毛泽东思想的伟大
      红旗下，甲：联合起来，乙：进行夺权斗争。
    下定决心，
    不屈不挠！合：夺权！夺权！夺权！甲：那一小撮走资本主义
      道路的当权派，乙：那极少数执行资产阶
      级反动路线的顽固
      分子，甲：他们面临灭顶之灾，乙：又耍出种种花招：甲：表面认罪、检讨，乙：暗地破坏、造谣；甲：停电，停水，停交通，乙：威胁，利诱，给钞票。甲：他们阴一套，阳一套，乙：企图蒙混脱逃。甲：呸！做梦！乙：呸！办不到！甲：革命造反派，乙：风吹浪打不动摇。甲：金钱难移造反志，乙：铁肩敢把重担挑。合：“天下者我们的天下。
    国家者我们的国家。
    社会者我们的社会。
    我们不说，谁说？
    我们不干，谁干？”甲：有毛主席为我们掌
      舵，乙：有毛主席给我们撑
      腰。合：夺权！夺权！夺权！甲：发起总攻击，乙：吹响冲锋号。甲：把党权夺过来！乙：把政权夺过来！甲：把财权夺过来！乙：把文权夺过来，合：夺过来！夺过来！甲：千重要，万重要，乙：掌握大权最重要。甲：我们人民军队，乙：是毛主席亲手缔造。甲：最听党的话，乙：红旗举的高。合：人民解放军应该积
      极支持左派广大群
      众。甲：这是毛主席的命令，乙：这是毛主席的号召。甲：我们坚决照办，乙：我们永远记牢。甲：革命造反派的战友
      们，我们坚决和你
      们站在一起，誓做
      你们坚强的后盾，乙：并肩战斗驱虎豹。甲：荡涤一切污泥浊水，乙：把一切牛鬼蛇神横扫。合：敌人不投降，
    就坚决、彻底地把他
      消灭掉。
