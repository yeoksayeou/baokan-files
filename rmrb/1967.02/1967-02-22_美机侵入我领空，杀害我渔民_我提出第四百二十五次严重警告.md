Title: U.S. Aircraft Invade Our Airspace, Kill Our Fishermen
Author:
Date: 1967-02-22
Edition: 3

On February 20th, from 9:07 AM to 1:00 PM, 20 U.S. military aircraft invaded our airspace over the western part of Hainan Island in Guangdong Province. On the same day at 1:30 PM, a batch of two U.S. military aircraft fired on two fishing boats from Wushi Commune, Haikang County, Guangdong Province, which were operating in the central part of the Gulf of Tonkin. One of our fishermen was killed and three were injured, and our fishing boats were damaged. On February 21st, from 5:00 AM to 11:21 AM, 31 U.S. military aircraft again invaded our airspace over the western part of Hainan Island in Guangdong Province. In response to this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, has issued the 425th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我领空，杀害我渔民  我提出第四百二十五次严重警告

1967-02-22
第3版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我领空，杀害我渔民
    我提出第四百二十五次严重警告
    新华社二十一日讯　二月二十日九时零七分至十三时零分，美国军用飞机二十架次，侵入我广东省海南岛西部领空。同日十三时三十分，美国军用飞机一批两架，向正在北部湾中部公海上作业的我广东省海康县乌石公社的两艘渔船，进行扫射，打死我渔民一人，打伤我渔民三人，并将我渔船打坏。二月二十一日五时零分至十一时二十一分，美国军用飞机三十一架次，再次侵入我广东省海南岛西部领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百二十五次严重警告。
