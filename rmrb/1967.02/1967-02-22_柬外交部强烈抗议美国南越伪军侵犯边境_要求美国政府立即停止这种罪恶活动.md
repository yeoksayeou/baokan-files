Title: Cambodian Foreign Ministry Strongly Protests U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Troops' Border Invasions
Author:
Date: 1967-02-22
Edition: 6

The Cambodian Foreign Ministry has strongly protested against the recent incursions by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops into Cambodian territory and demanded that the U.S. government immediately halt these criminal activities.

According to a report by the Xinhua News Agency in Phnom Penh on February 20, the Cambodian News Agency reported on February 20 that the Cambodian Foreign Ministry submitted two notes to the U.S. government on February 18 through the French Embassy in Cambodia. The notes strongly protested the recent aggressive actions by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops against Cambodia and demanded that the U.S. government immediately cease these criminal activities.

The notes pointed out that on February 15, U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops invaded 800 meters into Cambodian territory in Kampong Cham Province and opened fire on Cambodian farmers working in the fields, killing two on the spot.

On February 6, U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops fired on a village in Takeo Province, seriously injuring one villager.

On January 9, three residents of Kampong Cham Province stepped on a landmine planted by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops in Cambodian territory, resulting in one death and two injuries.

The Cambodian Foreign Ministry also submitted two identical protest notes to the Saigon puppet regime through the Australian Embassy in Cambodia.

According to the report, the Royal Government of Cambodia's Foreign Ministry submitted a note to the Thanom government of Thailand on February 18 through the Indonesian Embassy in Phnom Penh, strongly protesting the invasion by Thai armed personnel into Battambang Province on February 2, during which 41 Cambodian workers and eight trucks were abducted.

According to a report by the Xinhua News Agency in Phnom Penh on February 16, the Cambodian News Agency reported on February 16 that the Cambodian Foreign Ministry submitted two notes to the U.S. government on February 13 through the French Embassy in Cambodia, protesting the incursions by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops into Cambodian territory, airspace, and waters from December 20, 1966, to January 22, 1967.

The Cambodian Foreign Ministry submitted a note to the Thai government on February 11 through the Indonesian Embassy in Cambodia, protesting the planting of landmines by Thai personnel on Cambodian territory from January 15 to 30, resulting in injuries and deaths among the Cambodian people.

### 柬外交部强烈抗议美国南越伪军侵犯边境  要求美国政府立即停止这种罪恶活动

1967-02-22
第6版()
专栏：

    柬外交部强烈抗议美国南越伪军侵犯边境
    要求美国政府立即停止这种罪恶活动
    新华社金边二十日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二十日报道，柬埔寨外交部十八日通过法国驻柬埔寨大使馆，向美国政府提交了两项照会，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军最近对柬埔寨进行侵略活动，并要求美国政府立即停止这种罪恶活动。
    照会指出，二月十五日，美国—南越伪军侵入柬埔寨柴桢省境内八百米，并向正在从事田间劳动的柬埔寨农民进行射击，当场打死其中两人。
    二月六日，美国—南越伪军对柬埔寨唝吥省的一个村子进行射击，一个村民受了重伤。
    一月九日，柬埔寨柴桢省三个居民踏响了美国—南越伪军在柬埔寨领土上埋设的一颗地雷，一人被炸死，另外两人被炸伤。
    柬埔寨外交部还将两份内容相同的抗议照会通过澳大利亚驻柬埔寨大使馆提交给西贡傀儡政权。
    据报道，柬埔寨王国政府外交部十八日通过印度尼西亚驻金边大使馆向泰国他侬集团提出照会，强烈抗议泰国武装人员二日侵入柬埔寨马德望省，并且劫走四十一名柬埔寨工人和八辆卡车的侵略事件。
    据新华社金边电　据柬埔寨新闻社十六日报道，柬埔寨外交部十三日通过法国驻柬埔寨大使馆交给美国政府两件照会，抗议美国—南越伪军从一九六六年十二月二十日到一九六七年一月二十二日期间侵犯柬埔寨领土、领空和领海的行为。
    柬埔寨外交部十一日通过印度尼西亚驻柬埔寨大使馆交给泰国政府一件照会，抗议泰国人员从一月十五日到三十日期间，多次在柬埔寨领土上埋设地雷，从而造成柬埔寨人民伤亡的罪行。
