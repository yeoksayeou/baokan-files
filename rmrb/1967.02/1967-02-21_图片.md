Title: Iraqi Laborers and Revolutionary Youths Deeply Love Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1967-02-21
Edition: 4

Iraqi laborers and revolutionary youths deeply love Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought. They say: “Mao Zedong Thought is illuminating the entire world and guiding the revolutionary cause of the people of the world towards victory.” Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
