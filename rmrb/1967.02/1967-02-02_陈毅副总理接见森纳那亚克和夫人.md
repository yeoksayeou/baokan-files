Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Senanayake and His Wife
Author:
Date: 1967-02-02
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi received Senanayake and his wife today morning. The Xinhua News Agency reported that Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Writers' Permanent Bureau, Senanayake, and his wife, who had just returned to Beijing after leading a friendly delegation of the Afro-Asian Writers' Permanent Bureau to visit eight Afro-Asian countries. The delegation led by Senanayake successfully visited Pakistan, Syria, Iraq, Lebanon, Algeria, Guinea, Sierra Leone, and Mali. During the reception, Vice Premier Chen Yi had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi.

### 陈毅副总理接见森纳那亚克和夫人

1967-02-02
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见森纳那亚克和夫人
    新华社一日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了最近率领亚非作家常设局友好代表团访问八个亚非国家之后回到北京的亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克和他的夫人。森纳那亚克率领的代表团胜利地访问了巴基斯坦、叙利亚、伊拉克、黎巴嫩、阿尔及利亚、几内亚、塞拉勒窝内、马里等八个国家。陈毅副总理接见时同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有廖承志。
