Title: Beware of U.S. Imperialist Plots and Eliminate Internal Enemies
Author:
Date: 1967-02-25
Edition: 5

	Beware of U.S. Imperialist Plots and Eliminate Internal Enemies
	Pakistan and Cambodia Newspapers Denounce U.S. Imperialism's Subversive Crimes

	Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, February 21st - Recently, Pakistani newspapers have published editorials and articles emphasizing the need to be vigilant against the espionage and subversive activities of the U.S. Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) in Pakistan.

	The Lahore-based *Pakistan Times* published an editorial on the 20th, exposing the CIA's recent attempts to send representatives abroad through various U.S. foundations and organizations to engage in espionage plots. The editorial stated that the CIA is actively plotting various political conspiracies, and the people of Pakistan and other nations that value their independence must be alert to the CIA's infiltration and activities.

	The Karachi-based *Sunday Mail* recently published an article pointing out that the CIA is trying to infiltrate Pakistani labor, student, and media organizations to create divisionist plots. The article stated, "The Pakistani government must take stronger action to eliminate and investigate those who are willing to act as tools for the U.S. to undermine Pakistan's security and stability for dollars."

	The Rawalpindi-based *Construction Daily* published an article on the 20th stating that the U.S. Embassy and the U.S. Information Agency are centers of espionage, which is a confirmed fact. The article said that every sensible person must be vigilant and guard against U.S. imperialism's interference in their internal affairs through the CIA, which poses a threat to their national sovereignty.

	Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 23rd - The Cambodian *New Telegraph* published an editorial on the 20th exposing the CIA's subversive activities in Cambodia. The editorial said that the CIA's subversive activities in Cambodia are centered in Bangkok. In addition to assigning tasks to traitorous "Free Khmer" elements, U.S. intelligence agencies in Bangkok have specially deployed a group of "experts" to closely monitor the situation in Cambodia. Some of these individuals have maintained close contact with certain Cambodian individuals.

	The editorial stated that the CIA has carried out various provocations against Cambodia, from the crimes committed by traitorous "Free Khmer" elements on the border to writing anonymous letters, distributing leaflets, and spreading rumors and slander.

	The editorial pointed out that the CIA's subversive activities in Cambodia are the same tricks they have played elsewhere. The editorial said, "The CIA's misdeeds around the world are well-known."

	The editorial concluded by calling on the people of Cambodia to unite around Prince Norodom Sihanouk to smash the CIA's subversive plots.

	The aggressive activities of U.S. imperialism have been met with angry condemnation from people around the world. The image shows a massive anti-U.S. demonstration in Multan, West Pakistan. (Xinhua photo)

	The U.S. aggression against Palestine has sparked strong opposition from the Arab people. The image shows Syrian people demonstrating in Damascus in support of the Palestinian struggle. (Photo by Xinhua reporter)

### 警惕美帝阴谋　肃清内部敌人  巴基斯坦柬埔寨报纸谴责美帝国主义的颠覆罪行

1967-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    警惕美帝阴谋　肃清内部敌人
    巴基斯坦柬埔寨报纸谴责美帝国主义的颠覆罪行
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十一日电　巴基斯坦报纸最近连续发表社论或文章，强调指出要对美国中央情报局在巴基斯坦进行的间谍和颠覆活动提高警惕。
    拉合尔的《巴基斯坦时报》二十日发表社论，揭露美国中央情报局最近通过美国一些基金会和其他组织派遣出国代表团进行间谍阴谋活动。社论说，美国中央情报局正在大肆玩弄各种政治阴谋，巴基斯坦人民和其他珍视自己独立的各国人民必须擦亮眼睛，提防美国中央情报局的渗透和活动。
    卡拉奇的《星期日邮报》最近一期发表文章指出，美国中央情报局为了在巴基斯坦制造分裂阴谋，正在竭力打入巴基斯坦的劳工、学生和报纸等组织。
    文章说，“巴基斯坦政府必须采取更强有力的行动来清除和审问那些为了美元而准备充当美国破坏巴基斯坦的安全和稳定的工具的分子。”
    拉瓦尔品第的《建设报》二十日发表文章说，美国大使馆和美国新闻处是间谍中心，这是一个确证了的事实。文章说，每一个有头脑的人必须保持警惕，提防美帝国主义通过中央情报局干涉他们的内部事务，中央情报局是对他们国家主权的威胁。
    新华社金边二十三日电　柬埔寨《新电讯报》二十日发表社论，揭露美国中央情报局对柬埔寨进行颠覆活动。社论说，中央情报局对柬埔寨的颠覆活动中心设在曼谷。除了给卖国贼“自由高棉”分子分配活动任务以外，美国谍报机构在曼谷特别部署了一批“专家”，密切注视着柬埔寨的局势。其中一些人始终同某些柬埔寨人士保持着密切的联系。
    社论说，美国中央情报局对柬埔寨进行了各种各样的挑衅，从卖国贼“自由高棉”分子在边境上不断犯下的罪行，直到写匿名信，散发传单和造谣诬蔑。
    社论指出，中央情报局对柬埔寨搞的一套颠覆活动，也就是在别的地方搞过的那一套把戏。社论说：“中央情报局在全世界干的坏事，是尽人皆知的。”
    社论最后号召柬埔寨人民团结在西哈努克亲王的周围，粉碎中央情报局的颠覆阴谋。
