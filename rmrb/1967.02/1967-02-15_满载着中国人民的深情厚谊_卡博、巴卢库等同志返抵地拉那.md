Title: Brimming with the Deep Affection of the Chinese People, Comrades Kaba and Balluku Return to Tirana
Author:
Date: 1967-02-15
Edition: 6

Albanian Party Central Committee Politburo member and Secretariat member H. Kaba and Party Central Committee member B. Shutla, after concluding their friendly visit to our country, brimming with the deep affection of the Chinese people, returned to Tirana by plane on the 13th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Albanian Party Central Committee Politburo members M. Shehu, G. Nushi, H. Toska, M. Mufitiu, and L. Alija, and Party Central Committee Secretariat member Ch. Spasiu. Also present at the airport to welcome them were the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Wang Jinchuan, and other embassy officials.

Albanian Party Central Committee Politburo member and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defense B. Balluku led a military delegation to our country, concluding their friendly visit. Brimming with the fighting friendship of the Chinese people and army, they returned to Tirana by plane on the 13th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Albanian Party Central Committee Politburo member and Chairman of the Council of Ministers M. Shehu, Politburo member L. Alija, and Politburo candidate members K. Haxhiu and P. Dum. The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Wang Jinchuan, and military attache Li Yifan also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 满载着中国人民的深情厚谊  卡博、巴卢库等同志返抵地拉那

1967-02-15
第6版()
专栏：

    满载着中国人民的深情厚谊
    卡博、巴卢库等同志返抵地拉那
    新华社地拉那十四日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、书记处书记希·卡博和劳动党中央委员贝·什图拉，结束了对我国的友好访问后，满载着中国人民的深情厚谊，十三日乘飞机回到地拉那。
    到机场欢迎的有，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员穆·谢胡，果·努什，哈·托斯卡，马·穆夫蒂乌，拉·阿利雅和劳动党中央书记处书记查·斯巴修等。到机场欢迎的还有中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川和使馆其他外交官员。
    新华社地拉那十四日电　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝·巴卢库率领的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团，结束了对我国的友好访问后，满载着中国人民和军队的战斗友谊，十三日乘飞机回到地拉那。
    到机场欢迎的有，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议主席穆·谢胡，劳动党中央政治局委员拉·阿利雅，政治局候补委员卡·哈兹比乌、彼·杜米等。中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川和武官李一凡等也到机场迎接代表团。
