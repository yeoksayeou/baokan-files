Title: Central Academy of Arts and Crafts "East is Red" Commune Creation
Author:
Date: 1967-02-27
Edition: 6

The Central Academy of Arts and Crafts "East is Red" Commune has been established. The commune is named after the famous song "East is Red," which praises the great leader Chairman Mao. The establishment of this commune signifies a new stage in the development of the arts and crafts movement. It is a great leap forward in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

The "East is Red" Commune is composed of teachers, students, and workers from the Central Academy of Arts and Crafts. They have united under the banner of Mao Zedong Thought to carry out the revolution in arts and crafts. The commune members have pledged to follow Chairman Mao's teachings, to serve the people, and to create works that reflect the spirit of the times.

The commune has set up various workshops and studios to engage in artistic creation and production. They are determined to produce high-quality works that meet the needs of the people and contribute to the socialist revolution and construction. The commune members are also actively participating in the criticism and struggle against the bourgeois reactionary line in arts and crafts.

The establishment of the "East is Red" Commune is a significant event in the history of the Central Academy of Arts and Crafts. It marks the beginning of a new era in the development of arts and crafts in China. The commune will continue to advance under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, striving to create more outstanding works that serve the people and the revolution.
