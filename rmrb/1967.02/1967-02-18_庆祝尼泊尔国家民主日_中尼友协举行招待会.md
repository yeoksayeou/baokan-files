Title: Celebrating Nepal's National Democracy Day
Author:
Date: 1967-02-18
Edition: 5

China-Nepal Friendship Association holds a reception

According to Xinhua News Agency, on the 17th, the China-Nepal Friendship Association held a reception in the afternoon to celebrate Nepal's National Democracy Day.

The president of the China-Nepal Friendship Association, Zhou Jianren, and the Nepalese Ambassador to China, Rendi'er Suba, both spoke at the reception.

Zhou Jianren, in his speech, sincerely wished for the friendship between the peoples of China and Nepal to grow stronger in the struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism.

Ambassador Rendi'er Suba said that the peoples of Nepal and China are good friends. He expressed his belief that the friendship between the two peoples would continue to develop.

The ambassador praised the great achievements of the Chinese people under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao. He said that the achievements of the Chinese people make us happy. He wished Chairman Mao Zedong good health and longevity.

Attending the reception were responsible persons from various relevant sectors in our country and representatives of the revolutionary rebels in the capital. Officials from the Nepalese Embassy in China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 庆祝尼泊尔国家民主日  中尼友协举行招待会

1967-02-18
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝尼泊尔国家民主日
    中尼友协举行招待会
    据新华社十七日讯　中国尼泊尔友好协会今天下午举行招待会，庆祝尼泊尔王国国家民主日。
    中尼友好协会会长周建人和尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴在招待会上讲了话。
    周建人在讲话中衷心祝愿中尼两国人民的友谊，在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的斗争中日益增进。
    伦迪尔·苏巴大使说，尼泊尔和中国两国人民是好朋友。他表示相信两国人民的友谊将不断得到发展。
    大使赞扬了中国人民在伟大领袖毛主席的领导下所取得的巨大成就。他说，中国人民所取得的成就使我们感到高兴。他祝毛泽东主席健康长寿。
    出席招待会的有我国各有关方面负责人和首都革命造反派代表。尼泊尔驻中国大使馆官员也应邀出席了招待会。
