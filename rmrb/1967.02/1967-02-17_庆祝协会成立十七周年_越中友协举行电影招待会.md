Title: Celebrating the 17th Anniversary of the Association's Establishment: The Vietnam-China Friendship Association Holds a Film Screening
Author:
Date: 1967-02-17
Edition: 6

The Vietnam-China Friendship Association held a film screening in Hanoi on the evening of the 15th to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the association's establishment.

Those present included Chen Huiliang, Vice Chairman of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Phan Hong, Vice Chairman of the Vietnam Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and Vu Quoc Vy, among others. The Chinese Embassy in Vietnam's Chargé d'Affaires, Lu Weiqiao, and other diplomatic staff were also invited to attend the screening. The film "The Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought" and other Chinese color films were shown at the event.

### 庆祝协会成立十七周年  越中友协举行电影招待会

1967-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝协会成立十七周年
    越中友协举行电影招待会
    据新华社河内十六日电　越中友好协会十五日晚上在河内市举行电影招待会，庆祝协会成立十七周年。
    出席招待会的有，越中友协副会长陈辉燎、越南对外文化联络委员会副主任范鸿、武国威等。中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陆维钊和大使馆外交官员应邀出席了招待会。招待会上放映了《毛泽东思想的伟大胜利》等中国彩色影片。
