International News Briefs

Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down 10 U.S. Aircraft

Military and civilian forces in North Vietnam's North Thai and Ha Tinh provinces shot down four invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on February 4 and captured a group of U.S. air pirates.

Additionally, it was reported that military and civilian forces in South Ha Tinh province shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on February 3. Military and civilian forces in Binh Dinh province shot down one invading U.S. jet aircraft on February 5.

It was also reported that military and civilian forces in South Ha Tinh, Ninh Binh, Thanh Hoa, and Nghe An provinces shot down four U.S. aircraft on February 4.

U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Forces Invade Cambodia Again

On January 30 at noon, a South Vietnamese puppet military unit crossed the border and invaded the Cambodian province of Kampong Cham, near the Cambodian military outpost of Chhieng Anh Kong. They fired at Cambodian farmers, killing two and injuring six, three of whom were seriously wounded.

Intensifying Infighting in the Saigon Puppet Regime

On January 27, the puppet prime minister of Saigon, Nguyen Cao Ky, ousted the puppet vice prime minister and minister of defense, Nguyen Van Thieu, and replaced him with his confidant, the puppet chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff, Cao Van Vien.

U.S. Troops in Vietnam Exceed 400,000

According to Western news agencies, in the last three weeks of January, the United States sent an additional 10,000 troops to South Vietnam, bringing the total number of U.S. invading troops in South Vietnam to 405,000.

The report stated that by January 30, all 15,000 troops of the U.S. 9th Infantry Division had arrived in South Vietnam.

### 国际简讯

1967-02-06
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    越南军民击落美机十架
    越南北方北太、河西两省军民，四日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机四架，并活捉美国飞贼一批。
    另据公布，南河省军民，三日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。和平省军民五日击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架。
    另据公布，南河、宁平、清化、义安省军民，四日还击落美国飞机四架。
    美国—南越伪军又侵柬
    一月三十日中午，一支南越伪军部队越过边境，侵入柬埔寨波萝勉省省防军柴安贡哨所附近，向柬埔寨农民射击，打死两人、打伤六人，其中三人受重伤。
    西贡傀儡集团内讧加剧
    西贡傀儡集团伪总理阮高其在一月二十七日把伪副总理兼国防部长阮友固赶下台，而由他的亲信伪参谋长联席会议主席高文园接替伪国防部长的职位。
    侵越美军超过四十万人
    据西方通讯社报道，在一月份的后三周里，美国又向南越增运了一万名炮灰，从而使在南越的美国侵略军的总数达到四十万五千人。
    报道说，截至三十日为止，美国第九步兵师所属一万五千人已全部到达南越。
                      （据新华社讯）
