Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Australian and New Zealand University Student Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-02-07
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi received the entire delegation of the Australian and New Zealand University Student Travel Group, led by Malcom, John Pearson, and Tannant, this afternoon and had a cordial conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见澳、新大学生旅行团

1967-02-07
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见澳、新大学生旅行团
    新华社六日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天下午接见了由马尔昆、约翰·皮尔逊、坦南特率领的澳大利亚和新西兰大学生旅行团全体成员，和他们进行了亲切的谈话。
