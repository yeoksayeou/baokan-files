Comrade Wang Jiadao's Speech

New China News Agency, Harbin, February 1—Comrade Wang Jiadao, Commander of the Heilongjiang Provincial Military Region, delivered a speech at the Heilongjiang Province Red Rebel Great Alliance Great Seizure of Power Rally. The full text is as follows:

Red Rebel comrades, revolutionary comrades:

On behalf of all the officers and soldiers of the Heilongjiang Provincial Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I extend my warmest congratulations to this rally and to all the red rebels in the province for their great victories in the struggle to seize power!

The great alliance and seizure of power by the red rebels is excellent!

We will resolutely carry out Chairman Mao's orders, firmly stand on the side of the revolutionary left, and resolutely support the revolutionary left in their great alliance and seizure of power!

We have made all preparations for battle. Whenever the genuine proletarian revolutionary forces need our assistance, we will immediately mobilize and provide full support! Anyone who dares to resist the seizure of power by the revolutionary left, anyone who dares to sabotage the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, will be firmly suppressed without mercy!

Comrades of the proletarian revolutionary rebels! Let us unite under the great banner of Mao Zedong Thought, fight together, and achieve victory together!

Defend to the death the proletarian revolutionary line represented by Chairman Mao!

Long live the dictatorship of the proletariat!

Long live, ten thousand times long live, the great teacher, the great leader, the great supreme commander, and the great helmsman Chairman Mao!

### 汪家道同志的讲话

1967-02-02
第2版()
专栏：

    汪家道同志的讲话
    新华社哈尔滨一日电　黑龙江省军区司令员汪家道同志在黑龙江省红色造反者大联合大夺权誓师大会上的讲话，全文如下：红色造反者战友们、革命的同志们：
    我代表中国人民解放军黑龙江省军区部队全体指战员，对这次大会、对全省红色造反者在夺权斗争中取得的重大胜利，表示最热烈的祝贺！
    红色造反者大联合、大夺权，好得很！
    我们坚决执行毛主席的命令，坚定地站在革命左派一边，坚决支持革命左派大联合、大夺权！
    我们已经做好了一切战斗准备，凡是真正的无产阶级革命派需要我们援助，我们就立即出动，全力支援！谁敢反抗革命左派夺权，谁敢破坏无产阶级文化大革命，就坚决镇压，决不留情！
    无产阶级革命造反派的战友们！让我们在毛泽东思想的伟大旗帜下，团结在一起，战斗在一起，胜利在一起！
    誓死保卫以毛主席为代表的无产阶级革命路线！
    无产阶级专政万岁！
    伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！
