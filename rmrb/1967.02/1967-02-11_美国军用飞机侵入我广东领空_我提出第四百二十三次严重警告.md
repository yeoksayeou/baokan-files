Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrude into Our Guangdong Airspace
Author:
Date: 1967-02-11
Edition: 5

U.S. military aircraft intrude into our Guangdong airspace
We issue the 423rd serious warning

New China News Agency, February 10th

On February 10th, from 10:35 to 10:45, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into our Guangdong Province's airspace over the region east of Yulin, Hainan Island. On the same day, from 6:59 to 7:19, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into our Guangdong Province's airspace over Dongdao and Yongxing Island. On the same day, from 7:30 to 8:07, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into our Guangdong Province's airspace over Dongdao and Yongxing Island. On the same day, from 13:00 to 13:19, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into our Guangdong Province's airspace over Dongdao and Yongxing Island. Regarding the U.S. military aircraft's military provocation, our Ministry of Foreign Affairs spokesperson, on instructions, issues the 423rd serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我广东领空  我提出第四百二十三次严重警告

1967-02-11
第5版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我广东领空
    我提出第四百二十三次严重警告
    新华社十日讯　二月十日十时三十五分至十时四十五分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛榆林以东地区领空。同日六时五十九分至七时十九分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省东岛、永兴岛地区领空。同日七时三十分至八时零七分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省东岛、永兴岛地区领空。同日十三时至十三时十九分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省东岛、永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百二十三次严重警告。
