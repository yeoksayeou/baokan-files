Title: Celebrating the 19th Anniversary of the Founding of the Korean People's Army
Author:
Date: 1967-02-09
Edition: 4

The military attaché of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea’s embassy in China, Colonel Kim Hak, held a reception tonight to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the Korean People’s Army.

Vice Premier of the State Council Xie Fuzhi, and relevant officials Xiao Jinguang, Peng Shaohui, and Fu Zhong, were invited to attend the reception.

The acting chargé d'affaires of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea’s embassy in China, Kim Jae-suk, as well as ambassadors and military attachés from various countries in China, also attended the reception.

### 庆祝朝鲜人民军建军十九周年  朝鲜驻华大使馆武官举行招待会

1967-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝鲜人民军建军十九周年
    朝鲜驻华大使馆武官举行招待会
    新华社八日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆武官金鹤大校今晚举行招待会，庆祝朝鲜人民军建军十九周年。
    国务院副总理谢富治，有关方面负责人萧劲光、彭绍辉、傅钟等应邀出席了招待会。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑，各国驻华使节和武官，也出席了招待会。
