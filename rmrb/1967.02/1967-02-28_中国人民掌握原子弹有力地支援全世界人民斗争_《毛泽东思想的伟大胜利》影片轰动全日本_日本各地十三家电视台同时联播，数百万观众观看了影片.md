Title: The Chinese People's Mastery of the Atomic Bomb Powerfully Supports the Struggle of All Peoples of the World
Author:
Date: 1967-02-28
Edition: 5

The Chinese people's mastery of the atomic bomb powerfully supports the struggle of all peoples of the world. The film "The Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought" has caused a sensation in all of Japan. Thirteen television stations across Japan broadcast the film simultaneously, and millions of viewers watched it.

	New China News Agency, Tokyo, February 26 – The Chinese color documentary film "The Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought," which introduces China's three successful nuclear tests, was first publicly screened on February 26 in a special program on Tokyo Television. At the same time, twelve other television stations across Japan broadcast the film. Approximately millions of viewers watched this Chinese film, and it caused a sensation throughout Japan.

	"The Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought" is a gift from the Chinese side to the headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine). Soon, the headquarters and branches of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine) will organize screenings across the country.

	A young worker in Tokyo, after watching the film, excitedly told a Chinese reporter, "I deeply feel that China's successful nuclear tests are a great victory of Mao Zedong Thought." He said, "Under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao, the Chinese people, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, have overcome countless difficulties, mastered the atomic bomb, and are supporting the revolutionary struggles of all peoples of the world." The young worker said that because China has mastered the atomic bomb, it has dealt a heavy blow to imperialism and modern revisionism, making a significant contribution to world peace.

	A housewife from Katsushika Ward in Tokyo, who is involved in Japan-China friendship activities, said, "Seeing from the film that the Chinese people, under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, have achieved one victory after another in developing nuclear weapons, we feel as excited as the Chinese people." She added, "China's successive successful nuclear tests are a great encouragement to the international proletariat."

	The February 26 issue of "Chōshū Shinbun" recommended the film to its readers, saying, "China's development of nuclear weapons is for the liberation of all peoples of the world and for world peace."

### 中国人民掌握原子弹有力地支援全世界人民斗争  《毛泽东思想的伟大胜利》影片轰动全日本  日本各地十三家电视台同时联播，数百万观众观看了影片

1967-02-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中国人民掌握原子弹有力地支援全世界人民斗争
    《毛泽东思想的伟大胜利》影片轰动全日本
    日本各地十三家电视台同时联播，数百万观众观看了影片
    新华社东京二十六日电　介绍中国三次核试验成功的中国彩色纪录影片《毛泽东思想的伟大胜利》，二十六日在日本东京电视台特别节目中首次公开放映。同时，有日本各地十二家电视台联播。约有数百万观众观看了这部中国影片，影片轰动了全日本。
    《毛泽东思想的伟大胜利》这部影片是中国方面赠送给日中友好协会（正统）总部的。不久，将由日中友好协会（正统）总部和支部在全国各地主持放映。
    东京一位青年工人看了这部影片以后，激动地对中国记者说，我深刻地感到中国几次核试验的成功是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。他说：“在伟大的领袖毛主席的领导下，用毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民排除万难，掌握了原子弹，正在支援全世界人民的革命斗争。”这位青年工人说，由于中国掌握了原子弹，狠狠地打击了帝国主义和现代修正主义，为世界和平做出了重大贡献。
    东京葛饰区的一位从事日中友好活动的家庭妇女说，从影片中看到中国人民在毛泽东思想指导下，为发展核武器取得一次又一次胜利，我们同中国人民一样感到万分兴奋。中国核试验的一次又一次成功，是对国际无产阶级的极大鼓舞。
    二十六日出版的《长周新闻》向读者推荐这部影片说，中国发展核武器是为了全世界人民的解放和世界的和平。
