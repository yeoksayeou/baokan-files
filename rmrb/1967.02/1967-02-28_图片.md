Title: Shanghai Agricultural Machinery Factory Revolutionary Rebels Seize the Revolution and Promote Production to Support Spring Ploughing
Author:
Date: 1967-02-28
Edition: 3

	The revolutionary rebels of the Shanghai Agricultural Machinery Factory are seizing the revolution and promoting production to support spring ploughing. In the first half of February, they completed their production plan on time. This is a scene of revolutionary rebels manufacturing in large quantities the compressed sprayers urgently needed in rural areas.

	Xinhua News Agency Photographer
