	Brief News

	The military and civilians of North Vietnam shot down five invading U.S. planes.

	The military and civilians of Hai Phong City and Thanh Hoa Province in North Vietnam shot down three U.S. aggressor planes on the 18th and 20th.

	Additionally, it was announced that the militia of Bu Gia Map Province and the military of Vinh Linh District each shot down one U.S. aggressor plane on the 16th.

	Guerrillas in the Tad Tien region of Laos sank an enemy boat.

	On February 5, guerrillas in the Tad Tien region of Xieng Khouang Province in Laos sank an enemy motorboat on the Nam San River, killing and wounding many enemy soldiers.

	In the anti-"sweeping" battles on January 12 and 15, local forces and guerrillas in Kang Khe District, Sam Neua Province, annihilated 16 enemy soldiers and seized five light machine guns and other weapons.

	Somalian people support the struggle of the people of French Somaliland.

	More than a thousand Somalian students and citizens held a demonstration march in Mogadishu on the morning of the 21st, supporting the struggle of the people of French Somaliland for independence.

	The demonstrators carried many placards, on which were written: "French colonialists, get out of French Somaliland," "Down with imperialism." At 9:30, the demonstrators gathered at the intersection in front of the French Embassy and held a sit-in protest for about ten minutes. They shouted slogans condemning De Gaulle. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 简明新闻

1967-02-23
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南北方军民又击落入侵美机五架
    越南北方海防市和清化省军民，在十八日和二十日击落美国强盗飞机三架。
    另据补充公布，广平省布泽县民兵和永灵区军民在十六日各击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    老挝塔通地区游击队击沉敌艇一艘
    老挝川圹省塔通地区游击队二月五日在南桑河上击沉敌人机动艇一艘，打死打伤敌人多名。
    甘蒙省坎格县地方部队和游击队，一月十二日和十五日两天在反“扫荡”战斗中，共歼灭敌人十六名，缴获轻机枪五挺和其它武器多件。
    索人民支持法属索马里人民的斗争
    一千多名索马里学生和市民二十一日上午在摩加迪沙举行示威游行，支持法属索马里人民争取独立的斗争。
    游行的人们拿着许多标语牌，上面写着：“法国殖民主义者滚出法属索马里”、“帝国主义该死”。九点半，示威者来到法国大使馆前面的十字街头，举行了大约十分钟静坐示威。他们高呼谴责戴高乐的口号。（据新华社讯）
