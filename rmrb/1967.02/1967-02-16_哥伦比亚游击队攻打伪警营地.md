Title: Colombian Guerrillas Attack Fake Police Camp
Author:
Date: 1967-02-16
Edition: 6

Colombian guerrillas have recently been continuously attacking government troops and seizing enemy weapons to arm themselves.

Early this year, guerrillas in the city of Saldinata in the province of Norte de Santander attacked a police camp, disarmed the police, and injured the local police chief. This guerrilla unit then killed one enemy and wounded several more in a battle with pursuing police.

In the city of Sucre in the province of Santander, a small guerrilla unit of the National Liberation Army launched a surprise attack on a government military patrol, wounding several enemies and seizing a large number of weapons and ammunition before retreating.

Additionally, in the city of Alboredas in the province of Norte de Santander, guerrillas also ambushed a police squad.

### 哥伦比亚游击队攻打伪警营地

1967-02-16
第6版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚游击队攻打伪警营地
    新华社讯　波哥大消息：哥伦比亚游击队最近不断袭击政府军，并缴获敌人的武器来武装自己。
    今年年初，游击队在北桑坦德省萨尔迪纳塔城进攻了警察营地，解除了警察的武装，打伤了当地警察的头子。这支游击队随后在同追击他们的警察的战斗中还打死敌人一名，打伤数名。
    在桑坦德省的苏克雷市，民族解放军的一支游击小队对政府军巡逻队进行了突然袭击，打伤敌人多名，并缴获了不少武器弹药后撤退。
    此外，在北桑坦德省阿尔博莱达斯城，游击队也伏击了警察的一个小队。
