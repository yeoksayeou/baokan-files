Title: The Single Volume Edition of the "Red Flag" Magazine Editorial "On the Struggle for Power by the Revolutionary Faction of the Proletariat" is Published
Author:
Date: 1967-02-02
Edition: 3

The single volume edition of the editorial "On the Struggle for Power by the Revolutionary Faction of the Proletariat" from the third issue of 1967 of the "Red Flag" magazine has been published by the People's Publishing House. Starting from February 3, it will be distributed in Beijing and will be gradually distributed nationwide.

### 《红旗》杂志社论《论无产阶级革命派的夺权斗争》单行本出版

1967-02-02
第3版()
专栏：

    《红旗》杂志社论《论无产阶级革命派的夺权斗争》单行本出版
    新华社一日讯　《红旗》杂志一九六七年第三期社论《论无产阶级革命派的夺权斗争》一文，由人民出版社出版单行本，从二月三日起在北京发行，并将在全国陆续发行。
