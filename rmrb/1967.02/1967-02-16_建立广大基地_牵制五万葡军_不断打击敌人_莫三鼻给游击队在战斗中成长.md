Title: Establishing a Vast Base, Tying Down 50,000 Portuguese Troops, Continuously Striking the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1967-02-16
Edition: 6

	The British newspaper *The Observer* recently published a report acknowledging that the guerrilla forces in Mozambique are growing stronger. Below is a summary of the report:

	The armed forces of the Mozambique Liberation Front began their actions on September 25, 1964. They have now established firm bases in two provinces of Mozambique—Delgado Silva and Niassa—and have started operations in two other provinces—Mozambique and Zambezia.

	At the beginning of the revolution, the Mozambique Liberation Front had 250 trained soldiers, divided into guerrilla groups of five to twelve people. It now claims to have 7,000 soldiers and an additional 3,000 cadres working in various capacities within the guerrilla forces. Among these, 3,000 are in Delgado Silva.

	Despite the Portuguese continuously strengthening their military forces (in the past two years, their troops have nearly quadrupled, reaching over 50,000), the Mozambique Liberation Front has rendered the Portuguese authorities in Delgado Silva and Niassa virtually paralyzed and has established military bases, a temporary administrative structure, and its own jungle hospitals and schools.

	Before 1965, the battles in Mozambique were mainly limited to ambushing Portuguese administrative officials and soldiers. Although ambushes remain an important part of the Mozambique Liberation Front's activities, it now considers itself strong enough to conduct battles in platoon-sized units, deploying 65 to 150 people in a single engagement.

### 建立广大基地  牵制五万葡军  不断打击敌人  莫三鼻给游击队在战斗中成长

1967-02-16
第6版()
专栏：

    建立广大基地  牵制五万葡军  不断打击敌人
    莫三鼻给游击队在战斗中成长
    本报讯　英国《观察家报》最近刊登一篇报道，承认莫三鼻给游击队正在发展壮大。下面是报道的摘要：
    莫三鼻给解放阵线的部队最初是在一九六四年九月二十五日开始采取行动的。他们现在已经在莫三鼻给的两个省——德尔加多角和尼亚萨——建立了牢固的基地并且已经开始在另外两个省——莫三鼻给和赞比西亚——展开行动。
    莫三鼻给解放阵线在开始发动革命时拥有二百五十名受过训练的士兵，分成五到十二人的游击队小组。它现在宣称有七千名士兵和另外以各种身份在游击队中工作的三千名干部。在这些人中，有三千人在德尔加多角。
    尽管葡萄牙人不断加强军事力量（在过去两年中，他们的兵力几乎增加了四倍，达到五万多人），但是莫三鼻给解放阵线已经使在德尔加多角和尼亚萨的葡萄牙当局处于实际上瘫痪的状态，并已建立了军事基地、一个临时性的行政机构和它自己的丛林医院和学校。
    在一九六五年中以前，莫三鼻给的战斗主要局限为伏击葡萄牙的行政官员和士兵。虽然伏击仍然是莫三鼻给解放阵线活动中的一个重要的组成部分，但是现在它认为自己已经强大得足以以连为单位进行作战，在一次战斗中就能出动六十五到一百五十人。（附图片）
