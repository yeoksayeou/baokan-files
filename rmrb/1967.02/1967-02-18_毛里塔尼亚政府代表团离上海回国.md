Title: Mauritania Government Delegation Leaves Shanghai to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1967-02-18
Edition: 5

The delegation from the Islamic Republic of Mauritania, led by Foreign Minister and Planning Minister Bilani Mamadou Wane, concluded their friendly visit to China today. In the afternoon, they boarded a plane in Shanghai to return to their home country. Responsible officials from Shanghai's provisional highest authority and leaders from Shanghai's revolutionary rebel organizations went to the airport to bid them a warm farewell.

In the morning, the Mauritanian guests visited the Shanghai Bicycle Factory and toured the city.

Responsible officials from Shanghai's provisional highest authority hosted a luncheon in honor of the Mauritanian guests today.

### 毛里塔尼亚政府代表团离上海回国

1967-02-18
第5版()
专栏：

    毛里塔尼亚政府代表团离上海回国
    新华社上海十七日电　由毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国外交、计划部长比拉尼·马马杜·瓦尼率领的毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国政府代表团，结束了在我国的友好访问，今天下午乘飞机离开上海回国。上海临时最高权力机构的负责人以及上海无产阶级革命派组织的负责人，前往机场热情欢送。
    上午，毛里塔尼亚客人参观了上海自行车厂，并游览了市容。
    上海临时最高权力机构负责人今天曾设午宴招待毛里塔尼亚客人。
