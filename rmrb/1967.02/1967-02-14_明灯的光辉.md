Title: The Radiance of a Beacon
Author:
Date: 1967-02-14
Edition: 4

The radiance of a beacon

Chairman Mao said: “The heroic people of Albania have become a great beacon of socialism in Europe.”

This great beacon of socialism is shining brilliantly in Europe.

The year 1966 was a year of great strides for the Albanian people in their socialist revolution and construction. Under the call for revolutionization by the Albanian Party of Labour, the Albanian people are full of fighting spirit, with revolutionary enthusiasm, they have launched a new wave of construction, achieving a bumper harvest in both industry and agriculture.

The brilliant achievements of the Albanian people are a great encouragement to the revolutionary people of all countries and a heavy blow to imperialism and modern revisionism.

The revisionist bosses in Moscow—Khrushchev and his followers—initially thought that the Albanian people could not survive without them. They used all kinds of vile means to try to force the Albanian people to submit.

But the Albanian people are heroes who stand tall and proud. They are not afraid of ghosts, not afraid of demons, not afraid of pressure, not afraid of coercion. They hold high the banner of Marxism-Leninism, adhere to the socialist revolutionary direction, and with their actions prove that without those revisionist bosses, their revolutionary and construction work will be even better! They declare with their brilliant achievements the failure of all the conspiracies of the Soviet revisionist leadership!

The heroic Albanian people are fighting and advancing. Let those few revisionist bosses in Moscow weep in the corner!

### 明灯的光辉

1967-02-14
第4版()
专栏：短评

    明灯的光辉
    毛主席说：“英雄的人民的阿尔巴尼亚，成为欧洲的一盏伟大的社会主义的明灯。”
    这盏伟大的社会主义的明灯，正在欧洲放射出灿烂的光辉。
    一九六六年，是阿尔巴尼亚人民在社会主义革命和建设的道路上大踏步前进的一年。在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党提出的革命化的号召之下，阿尔巴尼亚人民斗志昂扬，以革命的干劲，掀起新的建设高潮，夺得了工农业生产的双丰收。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民的辉煌成就，对于各国革命人民是一个巨大鼓舞，对于帝国主义和现代修正主义是一个沉重的打击。
    莫斯科的修正主义老爷们——赫鲁晓夫和他的门徒本来以为，阿尔巴尼亚人民离开了他们就活不下去。他们穷凶极恶地使出各种卑劣手段，企图迫使阿尔巴尼亚人民低头屈服。
    但是，阿尔巴尼亚人民是顶天立地的英雄，他们不怕鬼，不怕魅，不怕压，不怕迫。他们高举马克思列宁主义的革命旗帜，坚持社会主义的革命方向，用自己的行动证明，没有那些修正主义老爷，他们的革命和建设事业会干得更好！他们用自己的光辉成就，宣告了苏修领导集团一切阴谋诡计的破产！
    英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民在战斗，在前进。让莫斯科那一小撮修正主义老爷们向隅而泣吧！
