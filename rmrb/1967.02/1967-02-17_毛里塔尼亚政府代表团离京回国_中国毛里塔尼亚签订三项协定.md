Mauritania Government Delegation Leaves Beijing to Return Home

China and Mauritania Sign Three Agreements

Premier Zhou Attends Signing Ceremony, Vice Premier Chen Yi and Minister Bilani Sign on Behalf of Both Sides

New China News Agency, February 16th—The Minister of Foreign Affairs and Planning of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania, Bilani Mamadou Wani, and the entire delegation of the Mauritania Government led by him, departed Beijing by plane this evening, heading back to their country via Shanghai.

Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council, Chen Yi, and relevant officials, Ji Pengfei, Lu Xiuzhang, Yang Lin, Ding Xilin, and Zhou Yiping, as well as China's Ambassador to Mauritania, Lü Zhixian, and representatives of the revolutionary rebels in the capital, went to the airport to see them off.

Diplomats from various countries stationed in China were also present at the airport to bid farewell.

New China News Agency, Shanghai, February 16th—The Minister of Foreign Affairs and Planning of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania, Bilani Mamadou Wani, and the delegation of the Mauritania Government led by him, consisting of seven members, arrived in Shanghai by special plane from Beijing this evening.

The guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by representatives from the temporary highest authority of Shanghai, the Shanghai Workers' Revolutionary Rebel Headquarters, and the Shanghai Peasants' Revolutionary Rebel Headquarters Preparatory Committee, among other organizations.

New China News Agency, February 16th—The trade agreement, economic and technical cooperation agreement, and cultural cooperation agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania were signed this afternoon in Beijing. The signing of these agreements marks a new development in the friendly relations between China and Mauritania.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the signing ceremony.

Representing the Chinese Government was Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi. Representing the Mauritania Government was the head of the Mauritania Government Delegation and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Planning, Bilani Mamadou Wani.

Present at the signing ceremony were, from the Chinese side, the Minister of the State Commission for Foreign Economic Relations, Fang Yi, Deputy Minister Yang Lin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Lu Xiuzhang, and Deputy Director of the State Commission for Foreign Cultural Relations Zhou Yiping; from the Mauritania side, all members of the Mauritania Government Delegation.

Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi and the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Planning of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania, Bilani Mamadou Wani, signed the agreements on behalf of their respective governments.

### 毛里塔尼亚政府代表团离京回国  中国毛里塔尼亚签订三项协定

1967-02-17
第2版()
专栏：

    毛里塔尼亚政府代表团离京回国
    中国毛里塔尼亚签订三项协定
    周总理参加签字仪式，陈毅副总理和比拉尼部长代表双方签字
    新华社十六日讯　毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国外交、计划部长比拉尼·马马杜·瓦尼，和由他率领的毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国政府代表团全体成员，今天晚上乘飞机离开北京经上海回国。
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，有关方面负责人姬鹏飞、卢绪章、杨琳、丁西林、周一萍，我国驻毛里塔尼亚大使吕志先，以及首都革命造反派代表，前往机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的，还有各国驻中国使节。
    新华社上海十六日电　毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国外交、计划部长比拉尼·马马杜·瓦尼以及由他率领的毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国政府代表团一行七人，今天晚上乘专机由北京到达上海。
    客人们在机场上受到了上海临时最高权力机构的负责人和上海工人革命造反总司令部、上海农民革命造反总司令部筹委会等组织的代表的热烈欢迎。
    新华社十六日讯　中华人民共和国政府和毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国政府贸易协定、经济技术合作协定、文化合作协定，今天下午在北京签订。这些协定的签订，标志了中国毛里塔尼亚两国友好关系的新发展。
    周恩来总理参加了签字仪式。
    代表中国政府签字的是，国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅；代表毛里塔尼亚政府签字的是，毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国政府代表团团长、毛里塔尼亚外交、计划部长比拉尼·马马杜·瓦尼。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有对外经委主任方毅、副主任杨琳，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，外贸部副部长卢绪章，对外文委副主任周一萍；毛里塔尼亚方面有毛里塔尼亚政府代表团全体团员。（附图片）
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅和毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国外交、计划部长比拉尼·马马杜·瓦尼分别代表两国政府在协定上签字。
