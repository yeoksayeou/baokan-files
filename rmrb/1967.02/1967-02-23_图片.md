Title: Students from Asia, Africa, and Latin America Studying in Romania View Exhibits on China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-02-23
Edition: 4

Students from Asia, Africa, and Latin America studying in Romania visited an exhibition of photographs related to China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. They gained a deeper understanding of Chairman Mao's wisdom and greatness, and their faith in Mao Zedong Thought was further strengthened.
