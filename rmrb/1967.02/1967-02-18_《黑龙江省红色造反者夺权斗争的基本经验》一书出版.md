Title: The Basic Experiences of the Red Rebels' Seizure of Power Struggle in Heilongjiang Province
Author:
Date: 1967-02-18
Edition: 2

The book "The Basic Experiences of the Red Rebels' Seizure of Power Struggle in Heilongjiang Province" has been published by the People's Publishing House. Starting from the 17th, it will be distributed in Beijing and will soon be distributed nationwide.

The content of this book includes the basic experiences jointly researched and written by the leading comrades who participated in the seizure of power struggle in Heilongjiang Province, as well as the editorial "A Good Example of the Proletarian Revolutionary Masses' Struggle for Seizure of Power" published in the People's Daily on February 10.

### 《黑龙江省红色造反者夺权斗争的基本经验》一书出版

1967-02-18
第2版()
专栏：

    《黑龙江省红色造反者夺权斗争的基本经验》一书出版
    新华社十六日讯　《黑龙江省红色造反者夺权斗争的基本经验》一书已由人民出版社出版，从十七日起在北京发行，并将在全国陆续发行。
    这本书的内容，除了参加黑龙江省夺权斗争的红色造反者的领导同志共同研究写成的基本经验外，还有《人民日报》二月十日社论《无产阶级革命派夺权斗争的一个好范例》一文。
