Title: Resolved to Arm Oneself with Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1967-02-21
Edition: 4

In Mauritania, the number of people studying Chairman Mao's works is increasing, and some have begun to understand the significance of Mao Zedong Thought in transforming one's subjective world. Recently, the Chinese Embassy in Nouakchott held a small art exhibition. After reading some of Chairman Mao's works at the exhibition, a postal worker wrote in the guest book: "To transform my thinking and establish a proletarian worldview, I believe the most important thing is to arm my mind with Mao Zedong Thought."

### 决心用毛泽东思想武装自己

1967-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

    决心用毛泽东思想武装自己
    在毛里塔尼亚，学习毛主席著作的人越来越多，有些人已开始体会到毛泽东思想对改造自己主观世界的意义。不久前，中国大使馆在努瓦克肖特举办了一个小型美术展览会，一位邮局职员在展览会上阅读了一些毛主席著作后，在留言簿上写道：“为了改造我的思想，树立无产阶级人生观，我认为最主要是用毛泽东思想武装自己的头脑。”
