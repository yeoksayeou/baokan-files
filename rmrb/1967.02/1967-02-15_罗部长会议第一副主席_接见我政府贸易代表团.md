Title: Romanian Deputy Prime Minister Receives Chinese Government Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-02-15
Edition: 6

Romanian Deputy Prime Minister I. Vîrdeţ received the Chinese government trade delegation, which came to participate in the 1967 Sino-Romanian barter and payment agreement negotiations, on February 14. The delegation was led by Jia Shi, with Gao Luo as the deputy leader. Vîrdeţ held friendly talks with them. Present at the meeting were Romanian Minister of Foreign Trade Gheorghe Chiuariu and Deputy Minister Vasile Striobolu, among others.

### 罗部长会议第一副主席  接见我政府贸易代表团

1967-02-15
第6版()
专栏：

    罗部长会议第一副主席
    接见我政府贸易代表团
    据新华社布加勒斯特十四日电　罗马尼亚部长会议第一副主席伊·维尔德茨十四日接见了前来参加一九六七年度中罗换货和付款协定谈判的中国政府贸易代表团团长贾石和副团长高珞，并同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时，在座的有罗马尼亚外贸部部长格·乔拉，副部长瓦·斯特里奥博尔等。
