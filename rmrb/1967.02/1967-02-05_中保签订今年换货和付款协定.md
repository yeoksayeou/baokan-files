Title: China and Bulgaria Sign Agreement for 1967 Trade and Payment
Author:
Date: 1967-02-05
Edition: 6

The agreement for the exchange of goods and payments between China and Bulgaria for 1967 was signed in Beijing on January 31.

Representing the two governments in signing the agreement were Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Jiang Ming from the Chinese side and Head of the Bulgarian Government Trade Delegation and Bulgarian Ambassador to China Stoichev from the Bulgarian side.

### 中保签订今年换货和付款协定

1967-02-05
第6版()
专栏：

    中保签订今年换货和付款协定
    新华社讯　我国和保加利亚一九六七年交换货物和付款的协定于一月三十一日在北京签字。
    代表两国政府在协定上签字的，我国方面是对外贸易部副部长江明，保加利亚方面是保政府贸易代表团团长、保驻华大使斯托伊切夫。
