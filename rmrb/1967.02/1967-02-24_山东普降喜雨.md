Title: Heavy Rainfalls Across Shandong
Author:
Date: 1967-02-24
Edition: 3

According to Xinhua News Agency in Jinan, from 6 a.m. on February 21 to 6 a.m. on February 22, Shandong Province experienced widespread moderate to heavy rain. The regions of Heze, Jining, and Changwei, as well as most of the areas in Linyi, Yantai, and Tai'an, and the cities of Qingdao, Zibo, and Zaozhuang received significant rainfall, ranging from 25 to 35 millimeters, with the highest recorded at 42 millimeters. This rainfall is beneficial for spring plowing, harrowing, and the greening of wheat.

### 山东普降喜雨

1967-02-24
第3版()
专栏：

    山东普降喜雨
    据新华社济南二十二日电　山东省自二月二十一日晨六时至二十二日晨，全省普降中到大雨。菏泽、济宁、昌潍三个专区，临沂、烟台、泰安三个专区的大部地区及青岛、淄博、枣庄三市雨量较大，多为二十五到三十五毫米，其中最大为四十二毫米。这场雨，对春耕春耙和小麦返青都很有利。
