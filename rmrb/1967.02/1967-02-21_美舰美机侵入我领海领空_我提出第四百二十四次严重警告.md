Title: U.S. Ships and Planes Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1967-02-21
Edition: 5

On February 19th at 22:57 until February 20th at 04:52, a U.S. military vessel intruded into our territorial waters near the northeastern region of Pingtan Island, Fujian Province. On February 20th from 09:52 to 10:09, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Dong Island and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding the military provocation by the U.S. military vessel and aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issued the 424th serious warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百二十四次严重警告

1967-02-21
第5版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百二十四次严重警告
    新华社二十日讯　二月十九日二十二时五十七分至二十日四时五十二分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省平潭岛东北地区领海。二月二十日九时五十二分至十时零九分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛、永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百二十四次严重警告。
