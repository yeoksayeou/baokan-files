Title: Spread Mao Zedong Thought to the Fields
Author:
Date: 1967-02-25
Edition: 3

Recently, our troops have responded to Chairman Mao's call to "grasp revolution and promote production." We organized a Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team and spent seven days in the rural areas where we are stationed. We practiced the "four together" method with the community members and actively propagated Chairman Mao's great line of "grasp revolution and promote production," as well as the policies of the Party Central Committee regarding the cultural revolution in rural areas.

Our Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team is composed of personnel from the headquarters, platoons, and families. With deep class affection, we went to more than ten production brigades in two communes to carry out propaganda activities. We mingled with the poor and lower-middle peasants and the revolutionary masses of the proletariat, eating together, studying together, working together, and entertaining together. Wherever we went, we propagated Mao Zedong Thought. We propagated at the dining table, in the fields, during rest, and on the streets, turning everywhere into a classroom for propagating Mao Zedong Thought.

While enthusiastically spreading Mao Zedong Thought to the community members, all the soldiers also actively participated in labor, helping the production brigades to deep plow the land, build terraces, collect and transport fertilizer, develop water conservancy, manage the fields, and irrigate. Everyone became a pioneer in promoting production.

	Driving Unit of the People's Liberation Army in Shandong Province
	Communications Group

### 到地头传播毛泽东思想

1967-02-25
第3版()
专栏：

    到地头传播毛泽东思想
    最近，我们部队响应毛主席关于“抓革命，促生产”的伟大号召，组织了一支毛泽东思想宣传队，利用七天时间，深入驻地农村，和社员实行“四同”，积极宣传毛主席提出的“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针，宣传党中央关于农村无产阶级文化大革命的政策。
    我们的毛泽东思想宣传队是由机关、连队和家属组成的。我们带着深厚的阶级情谊，分别到两个公社的十几个生产队进行宣传活动，和贫下中农、无产阶级革命派打成一片，同吃饭，同学习，同劳动，同娱乐。我们走到哪里，宣传到哪里，人人成为毛泽东思想宣传员。我们吃饭时宣传到炕头，劳动时宣传到地头，休息时宣传到街头，到处成了宣传毛泽东思想的大课堂。
    全体指战员在热情地向社员们传播毛泽东思想的同时，还积极参加劳动，帮助生产队深翻土地，大造梯田，积肥送肥，兴修水利，进行田间管理，浇尿灌浆，个个成为促生产的闯将。
            驻山东省解放军某部
                              通讯组
