Title:  Departure of Albanian Delegations
Author:
Date: 1967-02-08
Edition: 4

On February 7, Comrade Hysni Kapo, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, and Comrade Behar Shytulla, member of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, carrying the highest revolutionary and combatant friendship of the Chinese people towards the Albanian people, departed from Beijing to Shanghai by special plane to return home.

The photo shows Comrade Kapo and Comrade Shytulla, accompanied by Comrade Zhou Enlai, Comrade Kang Sheng, and others, bidding farewell to the soldiers, militia, and Red Guards of the People's Liberation Army of China who came to the airport to see them off.

Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter

On February 7, the Albanian military delegation led by Comrade Beqir Balluku, member of the Political Bureau and Vice Premier and Minister of Defense of the Council of Ministers of the Party of Labour of Albania, and Comrade Hito Çako, member of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania and Director of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army, departed from Beijing to Shanghai by special plane.

The photo shows Comrade Balluku and Comrade Çako, accompanied by Comrade Kang Sheng, Comrade Ye Jianying, and others, bidding farewell to the soldiers, militia, and Red Guards of the People's Liberation Army of China who came to the airport to see them off.

Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter
