Title: Minister Bilani Says China's Victory Belongs to the People of Asia, Africa, and Latin America
Author:
Date: 1967-02-17
Edition: 6

Minister Bilani of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania, who is also the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Planning, expressed his excitement to the revolutionary workers during his visit to the Beijing Second Machine Tool Factory on February 16. He shared his joyful feelings after meeting with the great leader Chairman Mao. He said, "Chairman Mao received us, and we had the opportunity to convey the admiration and support of the Mauritania people to Chairman Mao. We feel extremely honored."

Minister Bilani also told the workers, "We fully understand that the Chinese people, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, not only care about their own happiness but also care about the happiness of other peoples. We fully understand that your efforts and sacrifices are the concretization of Mao Zedong Thought."

Minister Bilani further stated, "Under the wise leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao, the Chinese people have achieved great victories. These victories belong not only to the Chinese people but also to the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America."

When Minister Bilani and the Mauritania government delegation arrived at the Second Machine Tool Factory, the revolutionary rebels and workers of the factory welcomed them with drums and gongs. People waved the red and shining "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and repeatedly shouted, "Long live the great leader Chairman Mao!" "Warmly welcome the Mauritania guests!"

During the discussion with the revolutionary rebels of the Second Machine Tool Factory, Minister Bilani expressed that they were proud of the victories achieved by the Chinese people in their struggle against imperialism and overcoming underdevelopment. He reiterated that the friendship between the Mauritania people and the Chinese people is unbreakable. The Mauritania government and people stand firmly with the Chinese people in the struggle against imperialism and overcoming underdevelopment.

The revolutionary rebels of the Second Machine Tool Factory expressed to the guests that they would definitely hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and strive forward in the direction indicated by Chairman Mao to build a new world that is red and bright. Amidst warm applause, the revolutionary rebels of the Second Machine Tool Factory presented the Mauritania guests with Chairman Mao's works and Chairman Mao's badges.

### 比拉尼部长说中国的胜利属于亚非拉人民  见了毛主席  感到很幸福  北京第二机床厂革命职工热烈欢迎毛里塔尼亚贵宾

1967-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    比拉尼部长说中国的胜利属于亚非拉人民
    见了毛主席  感到很幸福
    北京第二机床厂革命职工热烈欢迎毛里塔尼亚贵宾
    新华社十六日讯　毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国外交、计划部长比拉尼·马马杜·瓦尼，今天在参观北京第二机床厂时，兴奋地向革命职工谈了他们会见伟大领袖毛主席以后的幸福心情。他说，毛主席接见了我们，我们也有机会向毛主席转达毛里塔尼亚人民对中国人民的钦佩和支持，我们因此感到非常荣幸。
    比拉尼部长还向职工们说，我们完全了解，用毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民，不仅关心自己的幸福，而且也关心别国人民的幸福。我们完全懂得，你们的努力和所做的牺牲是毛泽东思想的具体化。
    比拉尼部长还说，中国人民在伟大领袖毛主席的英明领导下，取得了伟大的胜利，这一胜利不仅是中国人民的，而且也是属于亚非拉人民的。
    当比拉尼部长和他率领的毛里塔尼亚政府代表团到达第二机床厂的时候，这个厂的革命造反派和革命职工，敲锣打鼓，热烈欢迎。人们挥动着红光闪闪的《毛主席语录》，不断高呼：“伟大的领袖毛主席万岁！”“热烈欢迎毛里塔尼亚客人！”
    比拉尼部长在和第二机床厂革命造反派代表座谈的时候表示，他们为中国人民取得反帝和克服不发达状态的胜利而感到自豪。他重申，毛里塔尼亚人民和中国人民的友谊是牢不可破的，毛里塔尼亚政府和人民完全和中国人民站在一起，为反帝和克服不发达状态而斗争。
    第二机床厂革命造反派代表向客人们表示，他们一定更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，沿着毛主席指引的方向，为建设一个红彤彤的新世界而奋斗。在热烈的掌声中，第二机床厂革命造反派代表向毛里塔尼亚客人赠送了毛主席著作和毛主席像章。
