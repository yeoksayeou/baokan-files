The Australian "Vanguard" Newspaper Denounces the Shameless Slander by U.S. Imperialism and Soviet Revisionism Against China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is a Heavy Blow to U.S. Imperialism and Soviet Revisionism

	New China News Agency, Melbourne, February 29th

	The first issue of this year of the "Vanguard," the organ of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninists), published an article enthusiastically praising the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution personally initiated and led by Chairman Mao. The article also extols Mao Zedong Thought as the pinnacle of contemporary Marxism-Leninism and a universal truth.

	The article says: "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China and its victory are a heavy blow to U.S. imperialism and its accomplices, the Soviet revisionists, and a great contribution to the world revolutionary cause."

	The article denounces the Soviet revisionist ruling clique for closely collaborating with U.S. imperialism in shamelessly slandering China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The article says: "Indeed, the Soviet revisionists have made Moscow the world center for fabricating lies about China."

	The article points out that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution taking place in China is a fierce class struggle. The article says: "One of Mao Zedong's greatest contributions to Marxism-Leninism is his analysis of the continued existence of class struggle in socialist society."

	The article warmly praises the great role played by the Red Guards in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The article points out, "The Red Guards are a brilliant and advanced part of the Chinese people in the revolution to eliminate bourgeois influence."

	The article highly praises Chairman Mao's support for the revolutionary Red Guards. The article says, only a great Marxist-Leninist could foresee the great power of this movement. Mao Zedong is an outstanding Marxist-Leninist of our time. He has unlimited faith in the people. The article enthusiastically praises the invincible Mao Zedong Thought. The article says, Mao Zedong Thought is the pinnacle of Marxism-Leninism, it is the Marxism-Leninism of the era when imperialism is heading towards total collapse and socialism is advancing towards worldwide victory. ...

	"The Chinese people have proven the correctness of Mao Zedong Thought through many years of great struggle."

	The article emphasizes that "Mao Zedong Thought, which has reached the pinnacle of Marxism-Leninism, is a universal truth."

### 澳《先锋报》谴责美帝苏修对中国文化大革命的无耻诽谤  文化大革命是对美帝苏修的沉重打击

1967-02-26
第5版()
专栏：

    澳《先锋报》谴责美帝苏修对中国文化大革命的无耻诽谤
    文化大革命是对美帝苏修的沉重打击
    新华社墨尔本二十九日电　澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）机关报《先锋报》今年第一期发表文章，热情赞扬由毛主席亲自发动和领导的中国无产阶级文化大革命，并且称颂毛泽东思想是当代马克思列宁主义的顶峰，是普遍真理。
    文章说：“中国无产阶级文化大革命及其胜利，是对美帝国主义及其帮凶苏联修正主义的沉重打击，也是对世界革命事业的巨大贡献。”
    文章谴责苏联修正主义统治集团密切配合美帝国主义对中国的文化大革命进行无耻的诽谤。文章说：“的确，苏联修正主义者已经使莫斯科成为制造关于中国的谎言的世界中心。”
    文章指出，在中国进行的无产阶级文化大革命是一场激烈的阶级斗争。文章说：“毛泽东对马克思列宁主义的最伟大贡献之一，是他的关于社会主义社会中继续存在阶级斗争这个分析。”
    文章热烈赞扬红卫兵在无产阶级文化大革命中发挥的巨大作用。文章指出，“红卫兵是中国人民进行肃清资产阶级影响的革命中的一个光辉而先进的部分。”
    文章高度赞扬毛主席支持造反的红卫兵运动。文章说，只有伟大的马克思列宁主义者才能预见到这一运动的巨大的力量。毛泽东是当代杰出的马列主义者。他无限相信人民。文章热情地称颂战无不胜的毛泽东思想。文章说，毛泽东思想是马克思列宁主义的顶峰，是在帝国主义走向全面崩溃，社会主义走向全世界胜利的时代的马克思列宁主义。……
    “中国人民在多年的巨大斗争的检验中证明了毛泽东思想的正确性。”
    文章强调指出“已达到马克思列宁主义顶峰的毛泽东思想是普遍真理”。
