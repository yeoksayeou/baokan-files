Title: A Delegation from the Islamic Republic of Mauritania Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-02-13
Edition: 4

On the morning of February 12, a delegation from the Islamic Republic of Mauritania, led by Bilani Mamadou Wane, Minister of Foreign Affairs and Planning and a member of the Political Bureau of the People's Party, arrived in Beijing by special plane. The delegation consists of seven people.
