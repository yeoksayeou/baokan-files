Title: Responding to Chairman Mao's Great Call to Launch a Spring Production High Tide
Author: Yang Yiqiū
Date: 1967-02-25
Edition: 6

The revolutionary masses of Chen Village Production Brigade in Malu People's Commune, Jiading County, Shanghai, have united to form a revolutionary and production leadership team with the participation of revolutionary cadres from the brigade. They are seizing the revolution and promoting spring production. This is a scene from the brigade's rally to seize the revolution and promote spring production.

To make the "Letter from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants and Cadres at All Levels in Rural People's Communes Across the Country" known to every household and deeply ingrained in people's hearts, the propaganda team of a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army worked through the night to prepare cultural performances and extensively propagate them in rural areas.

The poor and lower-middle peasants of Xiaowang Production Brigade in Dongfanghong People's Commune, suburban Taiyuan, are studying the "Letter from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants and Cadres at All Levels in Rural People's Communes Across the Country" with great excitement and joy.

The poor and lower-middle peasants of Chengbei Production Brigade in Chengxi People's Commune, Jiading County, with deep class sentiments, are studying and applying Chairman Mao's works in the midst of the Cultural Revolution and the spring production high tide.

The people of Dazhai in Shanxi have shown even greater revolutionary enthusiasm in the Cultural Revolution, seizing the revolution and vigorously promoting production. This is a scene of community members carrying stones to build a dam despite the wind and snow.

The poor and lower-middle peasants of Jiaoqiao Commune in Hai'an County, Jiangsu, have resolutely responded to Chairman Mao's great call to "seize the revolution and promote production," launching a high tide of spring production. This is a scene of community members fertilizing the fields.

The revolutionary masses of the Shanghai Tractor Factory, after seizing power, immediately mobilized the broad revolutionary masses of workers to seize the revolution and promote production, actively supporting spring production by sending high-quality small hand-held tractors to rural areas. (Except for the named photographer, all photos on this page are provided by Xinhua News Agency)

### 响应毛主席伟大号召  掀起春耕生产的高潮（图片）
杨一秋
1967-02-25
第6版()
专栏：

    响应毛主席伟大号召  掀起春耕生产的高潮
    上海市嘉定县马陆人民公社陈村生产大队无产阶级革命派联合起来，建立了由大队革命干部参加的革命、生产两套班子，抓革命，促春耕。这是大队在举行抓革命，促春耕誓师大会。
    为了让《中共中央给全国农村人民公社贫下中农和各级干部的信》家喻户晓、深入人心，解放军某部毛泽东思想宣传队，连夜编排文艺节目，深入农村广泛进行宣传。
                      3150部队报道组供稿
    太原市郊区东方红人民公社小王生产大队的贫下中农社员，怀着万分激动和喜悦的心情，学习《中共中央给全国农村人民公社贫下中农和各级干部的信》。
    嘉定县城西人民公社城北生产大队的贫下中农，怀着深厚的阶级感情，在文化大革命和春耕生产高潮中，坚持活学活用毛主席著作。
    山西大寨人在文化大革命中更加焕发起冲天的革命干劲，狠抓革命，猛促生产。这是社员们冒着风雪在抬石块垒地坝。
    江苏省海安县角斜公社的贫下中农坚决响应毛主席提出的“抓革命，促生产”的伟大号召，掀起了春耕生产的高潮。这是社员们送肥下田的情形。
          杨一秋摄
    上海拖拉机厂无产阶级革命派，在夺权以后，立即发动广大革命职工，抓革命，促生产，积极支援春耕，把质地优良的小型手扶拖拉机运往农村。（本版照片除署名者外均由新华社供稿）
