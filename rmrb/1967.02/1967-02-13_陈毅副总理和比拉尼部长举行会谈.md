Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi and Minister Bilani Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1967-02-13
Edition: 1

Vice Premier Chen Yi and Minister Bilani Hold Talks

New China News Agency, February 12th: Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi and the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Planning of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania, Bilani Mamadou Wani, held talks this afternoon. The talks were conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

Participants from the Chinese side included relevant officials such as Ji Pengfei, Lu Xiuzhang, Yang Lin, Zhou Yiping, and Lü Zhixian. Participants from the Mauritania side included Muhammad Abd al-Rahman Uld Hassan, Hamud Uld Ahmad, Mukhtar Uld Hayba, Abd al-Rahman Uld Sheikh, Muhammad Uld Amar, and Muhammad Mukhtar Uld Muhammad Far.

### 陈毅副总理和比拉尼部长举行会谈

1967-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理和比拉尼部长举行会谈
    新华社十二日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅和毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国外交、计划部长比拉尼·马马杜·瓦尼今天下午举行会谈。会谈在友好的气氛中进行。
    参加会谈的，中国方面有各有关方面负责人姬鹏飞、卢绪章、杨琳、周一萍、吕志先等；毛里塔尼亚方面有穆罕默德·阿卜杜拉希·乌尔德·哈桑、哈穆德·乌尔德·艾哈迈杜、莫克塔·乌尔德·哈伊巴、阿卜杜拉希·乌尔德·谢赫、穆罕默德·乌尔德·阿马尔、穆罕默德·莫克塔·乌尔德·穆罕默德·法尔。
