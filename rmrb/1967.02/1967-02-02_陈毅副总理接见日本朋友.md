Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-02-02
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with Japanese friends today.

	New China News Agency, February 1st: Vice Premier Chen Yi today received the permanent council member of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, Bai Shi Fan, the director of the Secretariat, Bai Tu Wu Fu, the vice chairman and chairman of the Osaka Prefecture (orthodox) branch of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Datong Youzhang and his wife. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

	Present at the meeting were the peace activist Seiichi Saionji, who is currently in Beijing.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本朋友

1967-02-02
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本朋友
    新华社一日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见了日中文化交流协会常任理事白石凡，事务局长白土吾夫，日中友好协会大阪府（正统）总部副会长兼理事长大塚有章夫妇，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有在北京的和平人士西园寺公一。
