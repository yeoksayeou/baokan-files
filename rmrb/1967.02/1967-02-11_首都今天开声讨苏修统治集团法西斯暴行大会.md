Title: Mass Rally in Capital to Denounce Soviet Revisionist Clique's Fascist Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1967-02-11
Edition: 1

The Soviet revisionist ruling clique deliberately created anti-China incidents, repeatedly making crazy provocations against socialist China and the great Chinese people in recent times, which has aroused the extreme indignation of 700 million Chinese people. Revolutionary rebels and revolutionary masses from various sectors in the capital will hold a grand rally on the 11th to denounce the fascist atrocities of the Soviet revisionist ruling clique. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will start broadcasting the live coverage of the rally at 11:55 AM on the 11th using the frequency of the first program. Beijing Television and Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast simultaneously.

### 首都今天开声讨苏修统治集团法西斯暴行大会

1967-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    首都今天开声讨苏修统治集团法西斯暴行大会
    新华社十日讯　苏联修正主义统治集团蓄意制造反华事件，最近一再向社会主义中国和伟大的中国人民进行疯狂挑衅，激起了七亿中国人民的极大愤慨。首都革命造反派和各界革命群众将在十一日举行盛大集会，愤怒声讨苏修统治集团的法西斯暴行。中央人民广播电台将从十一日中午十一时五十五分开始，用第一套节目的频率，转播大会的实况。北京电视台和北京人民广播电台同时转播。
