Title: The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution Will Surely Achieve Victory
Author:
Date: 1967-02-23
Edition: 4

We, Pakistani workers residing in Britain, extend our best wishes to Chairman Mao Zedong, wishing him eternal life.

Chairman Mao Zedong is the great leader of the great Chinese people, the great teacher of the revolutionary people of the whole world, and the contemporary Lenin. Under his leadership, the Communist Party of China has made the decision to launch the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China. We believe that this Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, which is shaking the world, will surely achieve victory.

The more the imperialists shout and the more the revisionists slander the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the more we are convinced that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is a great event of historical significance to the oppressed people of the whole world.

Long live Chairman Mao! Long live the great Communist Party of China! Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

	In Birmingham, United Kingdom
	Pakistani Workers' Federation

### 中国文化大革命必将取得胜利

1967-02-23
第4版()
专栏：国外来信

    中国文化大革命必将取得胜利
    我们旅居英国的巴基斯坦工人，谨向毛泽东主席表示我们最好的祝愿，祝他万寿无疆。
    毛泽东主席是伟大的中国人民的伟大领袖，是全世界革命人民的伟大导师，是当代的列宁。在他的领导下，中国共产党作出了在中国开展无产阶级文化大革命的决定。我们相信，这场震撼全世界的无产阶级文化大革命必将取得胜利。
  帝国主义者愈是大吵大嚷，修正主义者愈是诬蔑无产阶级文化大革命，我们愈是坚信，中国文化大革命，对全世界被压迫人民来说，是具有历史意义的一件大事。
    毛主席万岁！伟大的中国共产党万岁！无产阶级文化大革命万岁！
                在英国伯明翰的巴基斯坦工人联合会
