Title: It Is Necessary to Correctly Handle Cadres
Author:
Date: 1967-02-28
Edition: 1

In provinces and municipalities where power seizure is necessary, a "three-in-one combination" temporary power organ must be established. This "three-in-one combination" temporary power organ is composed of representatives from revolutionary mass organizations that truly represent the broad masses, representatives from the local garrison of the People's Liberation Army, and revolutionary leading cadres.

### 《红旗》杂志社论：《必须正确地对待干部》

1967-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    在需要夺权的省、市，必须建立“三结合”的临时权力机构。这种“三结合”的临时权力机构，由真正代表广大群众的革命群众组织的负责人、人民解放军当地驻军的代表和革命领导干部组成。
