Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Leader Arrives in Romania
Author:
Date: 1967-02-07
Edition: 5

The leader of the Chinese government trade delegation, Jia Shi, who is in Romania to negotiate the 1967 Sino-Romanian trade and payment agreement, arrived here by plane on the 5th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome him included the Romanian Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, Albescu, and others.

The deputy leader of the Chinese trade delegation, Gao Luo, who had arrived earlier, and the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, Li Bin, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 我贸易代表团团长抵罗

1967-02-07
第5版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团团长抵罗
    新华社布加勒斯特五日电  前来参加一九六七年度中罗交换货物和付款协定谈判的中国政府贸易代表团团长贾石，五日乘飞机到达这里。
    前往机场欢迎的有罗马尼亚外贸部副部长阿尔贝斯库等。
    先期到达的中国贸易代表团副团长高珞、中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办李斌等也到机场欢迎。
