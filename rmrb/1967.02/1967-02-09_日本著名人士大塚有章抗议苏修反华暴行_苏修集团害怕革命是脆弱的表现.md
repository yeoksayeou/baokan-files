Title: Japanese Prominent Figure Ōtsuka Ariaki Protests Soviet Revisionist Anti-China Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1967-02-09
Edition: 5

Japanese prominent figure Ōtsuka Ariaki and his wife Ōtsuka Eiko, who are visiting Beijing as the vice-chairman and director of the Osaka Prefecture branch of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox), went to the Soviet Embassy in China today to read a protest letter, strongly condemning the Soviet revisionists' anti-China atrocities.

In the protest letter, Ōtsuka Ariaki said, "Chinese students studying in Europe visited Lenin's tomb, recited Chairman Mao's quotations, and you suddenly committed heinous atrocities. We are extremely indignant about this. Your actions have proven one thing: whether in the Soviet Union or in Japan, all revisionists who have betrayed the world revolution are terrified of Mao Zedong Thought."

He said, "All revisionists fear Mao Zedong Thought, and ultimately, they fear revolution. You in Moscow, and the revisionist leadership of the Japanese Communist Party in Tokyo, are simultaneously obstructing China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. This does not show your strength; instead, it is a manifestation of your anxiety and weakness."

Ōtsuka Ariaki concluded by representing the comrades of the "Mao Zedong Thought Research Association" in Japan, shouting: "Down with the Soviet revisionists and all revisionist leaderships in the world! Long live the great victory of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution! Long live Mao Zedong Thought, the highest peak of world revolutionary theory!"

Foreign experts working in Beijing demonstrated in front of the Soviet Embassy. New China News Agency photographer

### 日本著名人士大塚有章抗议苏修反华暴行  苏修集团害怕革命是脆弱的表现

1967-02-09
第5版()
专栏：

    日本著名人士大塚有章抗议苏修反华暴行
    苏修集团害怕革命是脆弱的表现
    据新华社八日讯　正在北京访问的日中友好协会（正统）大阪府总部副会长兼理事长大塚有章和他的夫人大塚英子，今天到苏联驻中国大使馆门前宣读抗议书，强烈谴责苏修反华暴行。
    大塚有章在抗议书中说，中国留欧学生参谒列宁墓，朗诵毛主席语录，而你们突然施加穷凶极恶的暴行，我们对此感到无比的愤怒。你们的行动又证明了一个问题：不管在苏联还是在日本，凡是背叛了全世界革命的修正主义者，对毛泽东思想都怕得要死。
    他说，所有修正主义者都害怕毛泽东思想，归根结底就是害怕革命。你们在莫斯科，日共的修正主义领导集团在东京，同时阻挠中国无产阶级文化大革命，这并不说明你们强大，相反地正是你们的不安的表现，是脆弱的表现。
    大塚有章最后代表日本“毛泽东思想研究会”的同志们高呼：“打倒苏修和全世界的修正主义领导集团！”“伟大的中国无产阶级文化大革命胜利万岁！”“世界革命理论的最高峰毛泽东思想万岁！”（附图片）
