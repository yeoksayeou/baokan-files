Title: Another Symbol of Sino-Burmese Friendship
Author:
Date: 1967-02-15
Edition: 5

The four construction projects aided by China in Burma are being expedited through the joint efforts of Burmese workers, technicians, and Chinese experts. These four projects are: the Shwebo Paper Mill, the Pyinmana Textile Mill, the Swa Plywood Factory, and the Taikkyi Bridge. All of these projects will be basically completed by the end of this year. This is another symbol of the "Paukphaw" friendship between the peoples of China and Burma.

The Shwebo Paper Mill, with a daily production capacity of 40 tons of paper, will be Burma's first paper mill. The Pyinmana Textile Mill will have 40,000 spindles and 600 looms. This is the second textile mill established with Chinese aid. The Swa Plywood Factory, upon completion, will produce 7,000 cubic meters of plywood daily. The Taikkyi Bridge, a steel truss bridge spanning the Salween River in the southern Shan State, is the second bridge built with Chinese assistance.

### 中缅友谊的又一象征

1967-02-15
第5版()
专栏：

    中缅友谊的又一象征
    据新华社仰光电　中国援助缅甸的四项建设工程正在缅甸工人、技术人员和中国专家的共同努力下加紧进行。这四项工程是：雪当造纸厂、密铁拉纺织厂、斯瓦胶合板厂和太可桥，所有这些工程都将在今年年底基本完成。这是中缅两国人民“胞波”友谊的又一象征。
    日产四十吨纸的雪当造纸厂，将是缅甸的第一家造纸厂。密铁拉纺织厂将拥有四万个纱锭和六百部织布机。这是在中国援助下建立的第二个纺织厂。斯瓦胶合板厂建成后日产七千立方米的胶合板。在掸邦南部的横跨萨尔温江的钢架太可桥是中国帮助建设的第二座桥。
