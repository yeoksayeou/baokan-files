Title: Speech by Comrade Song Renqiong at the Black Dragon River Province Red Rebel Great Alliance and Seizure of Power Rally
Author:
Date: 1967-02-02
Edition: 2

Comrades,

First, let us together wish our greatest and most beloved leader Chairman Mao a long life!

Today, the red revolutionary rebels of Heilongjiang Province are solemnly holding a rally for great unity and great seizure of power. This marks that the Cultural Revolution in Heilongjiang Province has entered a new stage, and it is another great victory for Mao Zedong Thought.

Now, allow me, on behalf of the East China Bureau Secretariat, to extend the warmest congratulations and the most resolute support to this rally you have convened! We fully support the first notice of the Red Rebel Revolutionary Committee and the rally's appeal for seizing the revolution and promoting production. We will fight together with you to the end to achieve the battle tasks proposed by the rally.

Comrades, you red revolutionary rebels have always followed Chairman Mao and the Party Central Committee, and your general direction has always been correct. You have achieved one great victory after another. "It is better to pursue the fleeing enemy with remaining strength than to stop and learn from the example of King Xiang Yu." I hope you will advance victoriously and redouble your efforts to achieve thorough victory in the struggle for great unity and great seizure of power.

I have committed a line error. Under the guidance of Chairman Mao, I will resolutely correct my past mistakes, firmly stand on the side of Chairman Mao's revolutionary line, and resolutely stand with, fight with, and achieve victory with the revolutionary rebels.

Now, let us together shout:

Long live the great alliance of the proletarian revolutionary rebels!

Long live the reasonableness of revolutionary rebellion!

Long live the victory of Chairman Mao's revolutionary line!

Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!

Long live our great leader Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand ten thousand years!

### 在黑龙江省红色造反者大联合大夺权誓师大会上  宋任穷同志的讲话

1967-02-02
第2版()
专栏：

    在黑龙江省红色造反者大联合大夺权誓师大会上
    宋任穷同志的讲话
    新华社哈尔滨一日电　中共中央东北局第一书记宋任穷同志在黑龙江省红色造反者大联合大夺权誓师大会上的讲话，全文如下：同志们：
    首先让我们共同祝愿我们最最伟大的领袖毛主席万寿无疆！
    今天黑龙江省红色革命造反者在这里隆重地举行大联合、大夺权的誓师大会，这标志着黑龙江省文化大革命运动已进入了一个新阶段，这是毛泽东思想的又一伟大胜利。
    现在，请允许我代表东北局书记处，对你们召开的这次大会表示最热烈的祝贺和最坚决的支持！对红色造反者革命委员会第一号通告和大会关于抓革命、促生产的呼吁书表示最坚决的支持，并同你们一起为实现大会所提出的战斗任务而奋斗到底。
    同志们，你们红色革命造反者是紧跟毛主席和党中央的，大方向始终是正确的，取得了一个又一个的伟大胜利。“宜将剩勇追穷寇，不可沽名学霸王”。希望你们乘胜前进，再接再厉，在大联合、大夺权斗争中取得彻底胜利。
    我犯了路线错误，在毛主席的教导下，我要坚决改正过去的错误，坚定不移地站在毛主席的革命路线一边，坚决同革命造反派站在一起，战斗在一起，胜利在一起。
    现在让我们共同高呼：
    无产阶级革命派大联合万岁！
    革命造反有理万岁！
    毛主席的革命路线胜利万岁！
    无产阶级文化大革命万岁！
    战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！
    我们伟大领袖毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！
