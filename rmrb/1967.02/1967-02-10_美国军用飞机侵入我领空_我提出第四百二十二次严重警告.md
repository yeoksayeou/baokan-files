Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Our Airspace
Author:
Date: 1967-02-10
Edition: 5

U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Our Airspace
We Issue the 422nd Serious Warning

Xinhua News Agency, February 9th: At 10:55 AM to 11:17 AM on February 8th, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Dong Island and Yongxing Island in Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 11:55 AM to 12:06 PM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Dong Island in Guangdong Province. At 6:41 AM to 7:20 AM on February 9th, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Dong Island and Yongxing Island in Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 8:43 AM to 8:51 AM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Yongxing Island in Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 11:41 AM to 12:05 PM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Yulin in Guangdong Province. In response to this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on instructions, issues the 422nd serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我领空  我提出第四百二十二次严重警告

1967-02-10
第5版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我领空
    我提出第四百二十二次严重警告
    新华社九日讯　二月八日十时五十五分至十一时十七分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省东岛、永兴岛地区领空。同日十一时五十五分至十二时零六分美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省东岛地区领空。二月九日六时四十一分至七时二十分，美国军用飞机一架侵入我广东省东岛、永兴岛地区领空。同日八时四十三分至八时五十一分美国军用飞机一架侵入我广东省永兴岛地区领空。同日十一时四十一分至十二时零五分美国军用飞机一架侵入我广东省榆林地区领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百二十二次严重警告。
