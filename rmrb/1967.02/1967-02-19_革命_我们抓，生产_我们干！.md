Title: Revolution – We Grasp, Production – We Handle!
Author: Hong Tiewen
Date: 1967-02-19
Edition: 6

Mao Zedong Thought is our compass, Chairman Mao's teachings warm our hearts. "Grasp revolution, promote production," these six characters shine with golden light. Grasp revolution, promote production, countless soldiers rush forward together, red flags flutter and songs resonate, waves surge and roll. We, the broad masses of poor and lower-middle peasants, must be daring fighters. We heed Chairman Mao's words the most and make an ironclad vow: Revolution – we grasp, production – we handle! We must use our iron shoulders to bear the dual burdens of revolution and production! Revolution – we fear no sacrifice, production – we fear no hardship. With Chairman Mao's command, we charge forward, making the revolution roar and the spring plowing blazing hot. Our revolutionary rebels are clear-sighted, seeing through the enemy's tricks and schemes. We are not afraid of your stirring up trouble, inciting discord, or your counter-revolutionary revenge and sabotage of collective property. All counter-revolutionary evil deeds, we will smash them to pieces! Revolution – we grasp, production – we handle. We are the pioneers of the revolution, we are the models of production. We vow to achieve double victories in revolution and production, spreading good news across the five continents and four seas!

### 革命——我们抓，生产——我们干！
洪铁文
1967-02-19
第6版()
专栏：

    革命——我们抓，生产——我们干！
    洪铁文毛泽东思想是方向盘，毛主席的教导暖心间，“抓革命，促生产”，六个大字金光闪。抓革命，促生产，千军万马齐争先，红旗飘扬歌声壮，翻江倒海卷巨澜。我们广大的贫下中农，要当敢打敢冲的战斗员，我们最听毛主席的话，发出钢铁般的誓言：革命——我们抓，生产——我们干！要用一副铁肩，挑起革命、生产两副重担！革命——不怕牺牲，生产——何惧困难，毛主席一声令下，我们就冲锋向前，把革命搞得轰轰烈烈，把春耕搞得热火朝天。我们革命造反派心明眼亮，敌人的阴谋诡计早看穿，不怕你兴风作浪，挑拨离间，不怕你反攻倒算，破坏集体财产，一切反革命的罪恶勾当，坚决把它彻底砸烂！革命——我们抓，生产——我们干，我们是抓革命的闯将，我们是促生产的模范。誓夺革命、生产双胜利，五洲四海捷报传！
