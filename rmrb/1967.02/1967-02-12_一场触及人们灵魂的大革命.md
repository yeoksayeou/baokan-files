Title: A Great Revolution That Touches People's Souls
Author: Mǎlái Yà Shā'ěr Mǎ
Date: 1967-02-12
Edition: 5

A great revolution that touches people's souls

The Chinese Cultural Revolution is a great revolution that touches people's souls. The Chinese bourgeoisie has been overthrown politically and economically, but they still attempt to corrupt the masses and control their thoughts with the old ideas, culture, customs, and habits of the exploiting class, aiming for a comeback.

The Chinese Cultural Revolution is a living example of Chairman Mao's teachings on the existence of class and class struggle throughout the entire socialist period.

We know that in certain stages of the socialist period, class struggle is extremely sharp. If we do not fight against the bourgeois reactionaries and their ideas, culture, customs, and habits, they will gain the upper hand. This is a life-and-death class struggle.

The Cultural Revolution of the proletariat is a comprehensive people's war in the realm of ideology after the establishment of the dictatorship of the proletariat. It vividly illustrates Chairman Mao's teaching: "After the enemies with guns have been wiped out, there still remain enemies without guns. They will inevitably wage a desperate struggle against us, and we must not underestimate these enemies."

The Cultural Revolution of the proletariat also vividly illustrates that only the people are the true creators of history, and only when they grasp Mao Zedong Thought and turn it into material force, are they the true creators of history. Only in this way can they seize power from the "authorities" who take the capitalist road. Therefore, it is necessary to arm the people with Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought from generation to generation.

### 一场触及人们灵魂的大革命
沙尔玛
1967-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    一场触及人们灵魂的大革命
    马来亚  沙尔玛
    中国的文化革命是一场触及人们灵魂的大革命。中国的资产阶级在政治上和经济上被推翻了，但是他们仍旧企图用剥削阶级的旧思想、旧文化、旧风俗和旧习惯来腐蚀群众，控制群众的思想，以图复辟。
    中国文化大革命是毛主席的关于整个社会主义时期存在着阶级和阶级斗争这个教导的活生生的例证。
    我们知道，在社会主义时期某些阶段，阶级斗争是非常尖锐的，如果我们不对资产阶级反动派进行斗争，不对他们的思想、文化、风俗和习惯进行斗争，他们就会占上风。这是一场生死存亡的阶级斗争。
    无产阶级文化大革命，是无产阶级专政建立以后，在思想意识领域里的一场全面的人民战争。它生动地说明了毛主席的教导：“在拿枪的敌人被消灭以后，不拿枪的敌人依然存在，他们必然地要和我们作拚死的斗争，我们决不可以轻视这些敌人。”
    无产阶级文化大革命也生动地说明了只有人民才是真正的历史创造者，而且只有当他们掌握了毛泽东思想，并且把它变为物质力量的时候，他们才是真正的历史创造者。只有这样，他们才能夺取走资本主义道路的“权威们”的权力。因此必须世世代代地以马列主义、毛泽东思想来武装人民。（附图片）
