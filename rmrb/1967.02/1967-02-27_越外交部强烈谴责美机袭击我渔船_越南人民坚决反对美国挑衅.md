Title: Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Strongly Condemns U.S. Air Raids on Our Fishing Boats
Author:
Date: 1967-02-27
Edition: 5

	Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Strongly Condemns U.S. Air Raids on Our Fishing Boats
	Vietnamese People Resolutely Oppose U.S. Provocations

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 25th - The spokesperson for the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on the 25th, strongly condemning the U.S. aircraft's provocative acts of attacking Chinese fishing boats near the coast of Hainan Island on the 20th and 21st.

	The statement said: "For a long time, U.S. aircraft have repeatedly attacked Chinese fishing boats and merchant ships. This time, the heinous act of the U.S. imperialists proves that they have consistently pursued a policy of hostility towards the People's Republic of China and are taking serious steps in their war provocations against China." The statement firmly demands that the United States immediately stop its war provocations against the People's Republic of China.

	The statement said: "The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam fully supports the stern warning issued by the spokesperson of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China on February 21, 1967, against U.S. imperialism." The statement said: "With the support and assistance of the Chinese people and the people of various socialist countries, and with the sympathy and support of the peace-loving people of the world, the Vietnamese people will surely defeat the U.S. imperialist invaders. The Vietnamese people, together with their Chinese brothers, resolutely oppose all U.S. war provocations against the People's Republic of China."

### 越外交部强烈谴责美机袭击我渔船  越南人民坚决反对美国挑衅

1967-02-27
第5版()
专栏：

    越外交部强烈谴责美机袭击我渔船
    越南人民坚决反对美国挑衅
    新华社河内二十五日电　越南民主共和国外交部发言人二十五日发表声明，强烈谴责美国飞机二十日和二十一日袭击在海南岛附近海面上捕鱼的中国渔船的战争挑衅行动。
    声明说：“长期以来，美国飞机曾多次袭击中国的渔船和商船。美帝国主义这一次采取的罪恶行动，证明它一贯推行敌视中华人民共和国的政策，继续在反对中国的战争挑衅阴谋中采取严重步骤。”声明坚决要求美国立即停止对中华人民共和国的战争挑衅行动。
    声明说：“越南民主共和国政府完全支持中华人民共和国政府国防部发言人一九六七年二月二十一日声明中对美帝国主义的有力警告。”声明说：“越南人民在中国人民和各个社会主义国家人民的支持和帮助下，在世界爱好和平人民的同情和支持下，必将打败美帝国主义侵略者。越南人民同兄弟的中国人民一起，坚决反对美国对中华人民共和国的一切战争挑衅行动”。
