    Quotations from Chairman Mao
    The current period is the spring planting season. It is hoped that all leading comrades, cadres, and the masses in the liberated areas will seize the opportunity to grasp the key links in production and achieve even greater successes than last year.

        *From "On the Army's Self-Sufficiency in Production, and the Importance of the Two Movements of Rectification and Production"*
