Title: Ceylonese People Hold Rally to Celebrate Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1967-02-06
Edition: 5

Ceylonese people held a rally here on the 4th to celebrate the 19th anniversary of Ceylon's independence.

They watched a traditional military parade.

Ceylon's Governor-General William Gopallawa, cabinet ministers, and senior Ceylonese military officers, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries, attended the celebration.

### 锡兰人民集会庆祝独立节

1967-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰人民集会庆祝独立节
    新华社科伦坡五日电　锡兰人民四日在这里集会庆祝锡兰独立节十九周年。
    他们观看了传统的阅兵仪式。
    锡兰总督威廉·高伯拉瓦、内阁部长和锡兰的高级军官以及各国外交使节出席了庆祝仪式。
