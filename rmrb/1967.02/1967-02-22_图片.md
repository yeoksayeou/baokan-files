Title: Revolutionary People of Yamaguchi Prefecture Hold Demonstration in Iwakuni
Author:
Date: 1967-02-22
Edition: 5

    The revolutionary people of Yamaguchi Prefecture, Japan, held a demonstration in Iwakuni City, where a U.S. military base is located. They raised high portraits of Chairman Mao and placards with quotations from Chairman Mao, and passionately shouted slogans such as "Long Live Mao Zedong Thought!"
