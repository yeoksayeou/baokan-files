Title: Revolutionary People Love to Read Chairman Mao's Books
Author:
Date: 1967-02-18
Edition: 4

Revolutionary people love to read Chairman Mao's books. Workers in Mali love to read Chairman Mao's works and seek answers to their problems from Chairman Mao's writings. This is a worker from a cigarette factory in Mali who is using Chairman Mao's works to answer questions for his comrades. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 革命人民最爱读毛主席的书（图片）

1967-02-18

专栏：

    革命人民最爱读毛主席的书
    马里工人爱读毛主席的书，遇到问题就从毛主席著作里找答案。这是马里一位卷烟工人拿着毛主席著作在给他的同伴解答问题。　新华社记者摄
