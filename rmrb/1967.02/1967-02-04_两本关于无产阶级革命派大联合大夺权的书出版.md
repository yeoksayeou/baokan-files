Title: Two Books on the Great Alliance and Seizure of Power by the Revolutionary Masses of the Proletariat Published
Author:
Date: 1967-02-04
Edition: 3

	Two books on the great alliance and seizure of power by the revolutionary masses of the proletariat have been published.

	New China News Agency, February 3rd: Over the past half month, the People's Daily, the Liberation Army Daily, and the Wenhui Daily and the Liberation Daily of Shanghai have published a series of editorials on the great alliance and seizure of power by the revolutionary masses of the proletariat. These editorials, along with two documents issued by the Shanxi Revolutionary Rebel Headquarters, have been compiled by the People's Publishing House into two books: "The Revolutionary Masses of the Proletariat Unite and Seize Power from the Capitalist Roaders!" and "The People's Liberation Army Resolutely Supports the Revolutionary Masses of the Proletariat in Seizing Power from the Capitalist Roaders!"

	The first book includes two editorials from the People's Daily: "The Revolutionary Masses of the Proletariat Unite and Seize Power from the Capitalist Roaders!" and "Let Mao Zedong Thought Occupy the Newspaper Battlefield," as well as the editorial "Thousands of Sails Pass by the Sinking Boat" from the Wenhui Daily and the editorial "Seizing Power is Great!" from the Liberation Daily.

	The second book includes two editorials from the Liberation Army Daily: "The People's Liberation Army Resolutely Supports the Revolutionary Masses of the Proletariat" and "Support the Revolutionary Masses of the Proletariat with All Our Might in Deeds," as well as the "First Notice" from the Shanxi Revolutionary Rebel Headquarters, the "Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao" from all revolutionary rebels of the Shanxi Revolutionary Rebel Headquarters, and the editorial "The Great Victory of the Cultural Revolution of the Proletariat in Shanxi Province" from the People's Daily.

	These two books have been distributed in Beijing and will be distributed nationwide in the coming days.

### 两本关于无产阶级革命派大联合大夺权的书出版

1967-02-04
第3版()
专栏：

    两本关于无产阶级革命派大联合大夺权的书出版
    新华社三日讯　《人民日报》《解放军报》和上海《文汇报》《解放日报》近半个月来发表的有关无产阶级革命派大联合、大夺权的部分社论以及山西革命造反总指挥部发出的两个文件，已由人民出版社编成《无产阶级革命派大联合，夺走资本主义道路当权派的权！》《人民解放军坚决支持无产阶级革命派，夺走资本主义道路当权派的权》两书出版。前一本书包括《人民日报》的两篇社论：《无产阶级革命派大联合，夺走资本主义道路当权派的权！》《让毛泽东思想占领报纸阵地》，以及《文汇报》的社论《沉舟侧畔千帆过》和《解放日报》的社论《革命的夺权好得很！》。后一本书包括《解放军报》的两篇社论：《人民解放军坚决支持无产阶级革命派》《用实际行动全力支援无产阶级革命派》，以及《山西革命造反总指挥部第一号通告》、山西革命造反总指挥部全体革命造反派《给毛主席的致敬电》和《人民日报》社论《山西省无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利》。
    这两本书已在北京发行，并将在全国各地陆续发行。
