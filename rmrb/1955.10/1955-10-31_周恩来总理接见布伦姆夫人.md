Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Madame Blum

Author:
Date: 1955-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China received Madame Blum of Belgium, a Standing Committee member and Secretary of the World Peace Council, recipient of the Stalin International Prize for "Strengthening International Peace," and a renowned international peace activist, on the morning of October 30th.

Present at the reception were Li Yimang, a Standing Committee member and Secretary of the World Peace Council, and Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee.

### 周恩来总理接见布伦姆夫人

1955-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见布伦姆夫人
    新华社三十日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在十月三十日上午接见了世界和平理事会常务委员兼书记处书记、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金获得者、国际著名和平人士比利时布伦姆夫人。
    接见时在座的，有世界和平理事会常务委员兼书记处书记李一氓，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一等。
