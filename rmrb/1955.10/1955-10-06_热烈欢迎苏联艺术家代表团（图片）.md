Warmly Welcome the Soviet Artists Delegation (Pictures)

The Soviet Artists Delegation held its first performance at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing on the evening of the 4th, and was warmly welcomed by the Beijing audience. Every program performed by the Soviet artists repeatedly won thunderous applause from the audience. Most of the programs were performed repeatedly, and many new programs were added. Our reporters Na Yi and Gao Liang photographed.

	Mezzo-soprano solo: Warrior's Aria (Selection from "Moscow" Chorus) Composer: Tchaikovsky
	Singer: People's Artist of the Georgian Soviet Socialist Republic and the People's Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, soloist of the Soviet National Grand Opera Theater, Stalin Prize winner V. A. Davydova
	Piano accompaniment: Pianist Ye. N. Kalinkovitskaya
	Feodorova Sisters Quintet: Russian song: Tractor Driver, Nosov-Seryosha
	Singers: A. I. Feodorova, G. I. Feodorova, Ye. I. Feodorova, Na. I. Feodorova, Na. I. Feodorova-Yarivleva
	Accordion accompaniment: F. A. Yakovlev, actor of the Leningrad State Song and Dance Ensemble
	Violin solo: Long Poem Composer: Khachaturian
	Performer: Winner of the Paris International Competition, soloist of the Soviet Touring Performance Bureau Ru. N. Sobolevsky
	Piano accompaniment: Pianist Ye. N. Kalinkovitskaya
	Piano solo: Troika Composer: Tchaikovsky
	Performer: Winner of the All-Union and International Chopin Works Performance Competition, pianist soloist of the Moscow State Conservatory V. K. Merzhanov
	Baritone solo: Georgian Folk Song
	Singer: People's Artist of the Soviet Union, soloist of the Tbilisi National Opera and Ballet Theater P. V. Amiranashvili
	Piano accompaniment: Pianist Ye. N. Kalinkovitskaya
	Acrobatics
	Performers: Acrobats A. I. Artamonov and Mai. V. Olkhovskaya of the Moscow State Song and Dance Ensemble
	Piano accompaniment: K. Ye. Bashkevich, actor of the Leningrad State Song and Dance Ensemble
	Soft Gymnastics
	Performers: N. A. Sulaeva and V. N. Skvortsova, actors of the Leningrad State Song and Dance Ensemble
	Piano accompaniment: K. Ye. Bashkevich, actor of the Leningrad State Song and Dance Ensemble
	Russian Competitive Dance
	Performers: Dancers from the dance group of the Soviet Pyatnitsky State Folk Choir: In. A. Drosdov, A. A. Robshkov, F. I. Markov, F. I. Nikolaeva, A. I. Drosdova, L. S. Gikhamilova
	Accordion accompaniment: F. S. Khabersky, winner of a diploma in the Warsaw Youth Festival competition
	Solo Singing: Soldier's Waltz Composer: Voronov
	Singer: Winner of international competitions, L. G. Kostricha, actor of the Leningrad State Song and Dance Ensemble
	Piano accompaniment: K. Ye. Bashkevich, actor of the Leningrad State Song and Dance Ensemble

After the performance, literary and art workers in the capital presented flowers to the Soviet artists.
