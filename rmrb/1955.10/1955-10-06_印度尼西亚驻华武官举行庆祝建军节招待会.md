Title: Indonesian Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate Army Day
Author:
Date: 1955-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th. Lieutenant Colonel Salim, the Military Attaché of the Indonesian Embassy in China, held a reception on the evening of the 5th to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Indonesian Republic's armed forces.

Among those attending the reception were General Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, and other ministers and deputy ministers, including Ma Wenrui, Zhang Xiruo, Zheng Zhenduo, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Zhang Zongxun, General Xiao Hua, General Yang Chengwu, Admiral Wang Hongkun of the Navy, Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Huang Zhiyong, Major General Zhong Renfang of the Armored Forces, officers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force, and Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Sino-Indonesian Friendship Association.

Also present were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries' embassies in China, as well as military attachés, acting military attachés, and deputy military attachés from various countries' embassies in China.

At the reception, Lieutenant Colonel Salim first delivered a speech, welcoming and thanking the guests attending the reception. General Huang Kecheng then delivered a speech, extending heartfelt congratulations to the Indonesian armed forces on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the friendship between the peoples of the Indonesian Republic and the People's Republic of China, and to the health of the soldiers, officers, and generals of the armed forces of both countries.

After the reception, a dance was held.

### 印度尼西亚驻华武官举行庆祝建军节招待会

1955-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚驻华武官举行庆祝建军节招待会
    新华社五日讯印度尼西亚驻华大使馆武官沙林中校在五日晚举行招待会，庆祝印度尼西亚共和国建军十周年。
    出席招待会的，有中华人民共和国国防部副部长黄克诚大将和其他各部部长、副部长马文瑞、张奚若、郑振铎，外交部部长助理陈家康，中国人民解放军高级将领张宗逊上将、萧华上将、杨成武上将、王宏坤海军上将、王秉璋空军中将、黄志勇中将、钟人仿装甲兵少将等，以及陆、海、空军的军官们，中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之。
    各国驻华使节、外交官员以及各国驻华使馆武官、代理武官及副武官也出席了招待会。
    在招待会上，沙林中校首先致词，对参加招待会的客人们表示欢迎和感谢。黄克诚大将接着致词，他代表中国人民解放军向印度尼西亚军队表示衷心的祝贺。席间，宾主并频频互为印度尼西亚共和国和中华人民共和国两国人民间的友谊，为两国军队的士兵、军官和将官们的健康而干杯。
    招待会结束后，举行了舞会。
