Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Instructs All Levels of the League Committee to Publicize and Implement the Spirit of the National Congress of Youth Activists

Author: 
Date: 1955-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th. The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League issued instructions on the 7th regarding the publicity and implementation of the spirit of the National Congress of Youth Socialist Construction Activists.

The instructions stated that in order to further organize and unleash the enthusiasm and creativity of the broad masses of young people in the cause of socialist construction, to launch a new upsurge of youth across the country striving for socialism, and to further promote the orientation of League organizations at all levels towards the masses and delving into practical work to make the work more practical and concrete, youth league organizations at all levels must widely publicize the achievements of the National Congress of Youth Socialist Construction Activists; continue to vigorously and deeply mobilize youth on all fronts to strive for the implementation of the instructions of the Party Central Committee and the various tasks put forward by the League Central Committee. Strive to fulfill the guarantees made to the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, and strive to be a youth socialist construction activist.

The instructions stated: The content of the publicity should focus on the following points: (1) The care and attention of the Party Central Committee to this conference, the kind teachings and instructions of the Party Central Committee to the younger generation, the guarantees made by the conference to the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, and the support and assistance of all parties to this conference; (2) The great significance of the First Five-Year Plan, and the tasks of young people in the struggle to realize the First Five-Year Plan; (3) The advanced working experience and excellent qualities of the activists, the various proposals put forward by the activists at the conference, calling on young people to strive to learn from the activists, carry forward the spirit of loving the motherland, loving labor, hard work, not being afraid of difficulties, studying diligently, actively creating, learning humbly, and uniting the masses, and strive to make greater achievements in current work and study.

The instructions pointed out: Youth League committees at all levels should convene youth mass meetings in their respective regions or categorized by type, at which responsible comrades of the Youth League Committee should make relatively comprehensive reports, and the activists who participated in the meeting should talk about their gains, feelings, and their action plans. At the meeting, combined with the current work tasks of the region and industry, some specific and feasible proposals or guarantees can be put forward and adopted, so as to guide the enthusiasm of young people to practical work. Provincial, regional, and municipal League committees should regard helping lower levels to hold such meetings well as one of the most important tasks at present.

The instructions also required Youth League committees at all levels to widely organize League members and young people with reading ability to study the conference documents, train a group of propagandists to explain and publicize to League members and young people with lower cultural levels, organize youth and activists' get-togethers, and hold various gatherings with the themes of "Always Be Loyal Children of the Great Motherland," "Strive to Be a Youth Activist," and "Make Contributions to Socialist Construction" to make this publicity more widespread and in-depth.

### 青年团中央指示各级团委  宣传和贯彻全国青年积极分子大会精神

1955-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团中央指示各级团委
    宣传和贯彻全国青年积极分子大会精神
    新华社八日讯　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会七日发出关于宣传和贯彻全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会精神的指示。
    指示说，为了进一步组织和发挥广大青年在社会主义建设事业中的积极性和创造性，掀起全国青年为社会主义而斗争的新高潮，为了进一步推动各级团的组织面向群众，深入实际，把工作做得更加切实具体，各级青年团组织必须广泛宣传全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的成就；继续大张旗鼓地深入地动员各个战线上的青年，为贯彻执行党中央的指示和团中央所提出的各项任务而奋斗。为实现对党中央和毛主席的保证，争取做一个青年社会主义建设积极分子而奋斗。
    指示说：宣传内容应着重以下几点：（一）党中央对这次大会的关怀和重视，党中央对青年一代的亲切教导和指示，大会对党中央和毛主席的保证，以及各方面对这次大会的支持和帮助；（二）第一个五年计划的伟大意义，青年在为实现第一个五年计划而斗争中的任务；（三）积极分子的先进工作经验和他们的优良品质，积极分子在大会上所提出的各种倡议，号召青年努力向积极分子看齐，发扬热爱祖国、热爱劳动、艰苦奋斗、不怕困难、刻苦钻研、积极创造、虚心学习、团结群众的精神，努力在当前的工作、学习中做出更大的成绩。
    指示指出：各级团委应当召开本地区的或分门别类的青年群众大会，由团委负责同志在会上做比较全面的报告，由参加大会的积极分子讲自己的收获、感受和自己的行动计划。在会上可以结合本地区本行业的当前工作任务，提出并通过一些具体可行的倡议或保证书，以便把青年的热情引向到实际工作中去。省、地、市团委应把帮助下级开好这种会议作为当前最重要的工作之一。
    指示还要求各级团委根据条件广泛组织有阅读能力的团员和青年群众学习大会文件，训练一批宣传员到文化水平较低的团员和青年中去进行讲解和宣传，组织青年和积极分子们的联欢活动，举办以“永远做伟大祖国的忠实儿女”“争取做一个青年积极分子”“为社会主义建设立功”为主题的各种集会，使这一宣传更加广泛深入。
