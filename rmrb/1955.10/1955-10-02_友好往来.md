Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1955-10-02
Page: 3

    Friendly Exchanges
    Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation
    The Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation of eight members, led by Chairman Mengadas Nasution and Vice Chairman Indrosugondo, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou on the afternoon of September 30th, at the invitation of the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association to attend the National Day celebrations and visit our country.
    Burmese Postal and Telecommunications Delegation
    The Burmese Postal and Telecommunications Delegation of three members, led by Wu Mei Ang, Deputy Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Transport and Communications, arrived in Beijing by plane the day before yesterday at the invitation to China to discuss postal and telecommunications matters.
    Qiao Weizhong, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, and Su Younong, Director of the General Post Office, were among those who welcomed them at the airport.
    U Maung Maung Toe, First Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, also greeted them at the airport.
    Finnish Cultural Delegation
    The Finnish Cultural Delegation of thirteen members, led by Chairman Avo Henrik Yrjö, arrived in Beijing in batches on the afternoons of September 28th and 30th, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country.
    German Writer Matthias
    The famous German writer Leon Lawrence Matthias, who was invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of September 30th.
    Some Members of the Japanese Diet Members' Delegation to the Soviet Union
    A group of nine members of the Japanese Diet Members' Delegation to the Soviet Union, headed by Masaru Nozoe, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 30th after visiting the Soviet Union and Poland, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to attend the National Day celebrations.
    Reporters from Korea, Germany, Hungary, Bulgaria, etc.
    Korean reporter Li Yongren, German Democratic Republic reporter Walter Franze, Hungarian photojournalist Lev Nicklo, reporter Daboli Bandor, and Bulgarian reporter Georgi Filipov Bokov have successively arrived in Beijing.

### 友好往来

1955-10-02
第3版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    印度尼西亚—中国友好协会代表团
    应中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请前来我国参加国庆观礼及访问的印度尼西亚—中国友好协会代表团一行八人，在团长孟阿达斯·纳苏申及副团长英德罗苏贡多率领下，已在九月三十日下午由广州乘飞机到达北京。
    缅甸邮电代表团
    应邀来中国商谈通邮通电的缅甸邮电代表团一行三人，在缅甸运输和交通部副秘书吴梅昂率领下，前天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有邮电部办公厅副主任乔为中和邮政总局局长苏幼农等人。
    缅甸驻华大使馆一等秘书吴茂茂铎等也到机场迎接。
    芬兰文化代表团
    应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的芬兰文化代表团一行十三人，由团长阿沃·亨利克·于尔拍率领，在九月二十八、三十两日下午分批到达北京。
    德国作家马蒂阿斯
    应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国参观访问的德国著名作家利昂·劳伦斯·马蒂阿斯，已于九月三十日下午到达北京。
    日本国会议员访苏团部分人员
    以野沟胜为首的日本国会议员访苏团部分人员一行九人，在苏联和波兰访问结束后，应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国参加国庆节观礼，在三十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    朝、德、匈、保等国记者
    朝鲜记者李容仁，德意志民主共和国记者华尔特·弗兰策，匈牙利摄影记者列夫·尼克洛、记者达波里·班道尔，保加利亚记者格奥尔基·菲利波夫·勃科夫已先后到达北京。
