Friendly Exchanges

    Japanese Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    At the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association, the Japanese medical delegation of fifteen members, led by Honorary Head Abe Katsuma, Head Domori Yoshio, and Deputy Head Ota Seiichi, arrived in Beijing by train on the 29th. Members include Tasaka Sadataka, Sakakibara Sen, Sasa Manabu, Fuwa Tatsuyo, Nakahara Minoru, Kinoshita Tomotaka, Fujii Atsuo, Suzuki Sanai, Minami Yoshitada, Fukuda Masako, and others.

    Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Nanning for Home
    The Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe has concluded its performance tour in China and left Nanning by train at 8:00 p.m. on the 28th to return home. A farewell ceremony attended by 1,000 people was held at the Nanning Railway Station.

    French Women's Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
    The French women's delegation, headed by Mrs. Marcelle Maguet, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 28th to return home.

    Indonesia-China Friendship Delegation Returns Home
    The Indonesia-China Friendship Association delegation of six members arrived in Guangzhou on the 27th from Beijing and Shanghai respectively and left Guangzhou on the morning of the 29th to return home.

    French Writers Sartre and de Beauvoir Return Home
    The famous French writer Jean-Paul Sartre and female writer Simone de Beauvoir, who were invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China, left Beijing on the 24th to return home.

### 友好往来

1955-10-30
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    日本医学代表团到京
    应中华医学会邀请来中国访问的日本医学代表团一行十五人，在名誉团长阿部胜马、团长堂森芳夫、副团长太田清一率领下，二十九日乘车到达北京。团员有田坂定孝、榊原仟、佐佐学、不破龙登代、中原实、木下友敬、藤井厚男、铃木佐内、南义忠、福田昌子等。
    越南人民歌舞团离南宁回国
    越南人民歌舞团已结束了在中国的访问演出，二十八日晚八时乘车离南宁回国。在南宁市车站上，举行了一千人参加的欢送会。
    法国妇女代表团离京回国
    以玛赛尔·马盖夫人为首的法国妇女代表团，已在二十八日上午乘飞机离开北京返国。
    印度尼西亚—中国友好代表团回国
    印度尼西亚—中国友好协会代表团一行六人，在二十七日分别由北京和上海到达广州，二十九日上午离广州回国。
    法国作家萨特和德波娃回国
    应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国参观访问的法国著名作家让·保罗·萨特和女作家西蒙娜·德波娃，在二十四日离北京返国。
