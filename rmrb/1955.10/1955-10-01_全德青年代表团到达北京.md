Title: All-German Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the All-German Youth Delegation, consisting of seventeen members, arrived in Beijing in batches by plane on the 29th and 30th to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations.

    The delegation is led by Max Stoll-Geboren, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Free German Youth League. The delegation includes ten youth representatives from the German Democratic Republic, including tractor drivers and workers from the Karl Liebknecht Heavy Machinery Factory, and seven youth representatives from the Federal Republic of Germany, including representatives from the Eagle Group, the youth organization of the German Social Democratic Party, as well as representatives from the Hanover City Christian Youth Organization.
    Among those who went to welcome them were Luo Yi, Zhang Ze, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League.

### 全德青年代表团到达北京

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    全德青年代表团到达北京
    新华社三十日讯　应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会邀请来中国访问并参加国庆节观礼的全德青年代表团十七人在二十九日、三十日分批乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团由自由德国青年联盟中央委员会书记马克斯·斯托尔·格博伦率领。代表团包括德意志民主共和国青年代表十人，其中有拖拉机手和卡尔·李卜克内西重型机器厂的工人和德意志联邦共和国青年代表七人，其中有德国社会民主党的青年组织鹰团的代表，还有汉诺威城市基督教青年组织的代表。
    前往欢迎的有中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记罗毅、章泽、胡克实等人。
