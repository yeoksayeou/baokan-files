Title: Sastroamidjojo, Chairman of the Bandung Conference, Points Out That the Spirit of Bandung is Conducive to the Spirit of Geneva
Author:
Date: 1955-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, October 20th: According to the "Independent" newspaper on the 20th, former Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, who served as chairman of the Asian-African Conference in Bandung, said: "The Bandung spirit has proven to be helpful to the Geneva spirit, because it suggests to the world that according to the ten principles adopted by the twenty-nine countries at the Bandung Conference, they should live together peacefully in an atmosphere of mutual trust and tolerance rather than suspicion." The former Prime Minister made this remark when commenting on Dutch Foreign Minister Luns' recent statement that the spirit of Bandung would stifle the spirit of the Geneva Conference.

Sastroamidjojo pointed out: "If Luns is afraid of the anti-colonial spirit of the Bandung Conference, it is understandable, because the Netherlands wants to continue its rule in West Irian."

### 在万隆会议上担任主席的沙斯特罗阿米佐约指出  万隆会议精神有助于日内瓦精神

1955-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    在万隆会议上担任主席的沙斯特罗阿米佐约指出
    万隆会议精神有助于日内瓦精神
    新华社雅加达二十日电　据“独立报”二十日报道，在万隆举行亚非会议时担任主席的前印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约说：“万隆精神已经证明是对日内瓦精神是有帮助的，因为它向世界各国建议：根据二十九个国家在万隆会议上所通过的十项原则在一种相互不存疑惧而是互相信任和容忍的气氛中和平相处。”这位前总理是在评论荷兰外交大臣伦斯最近所说的万隆精神将扼杀日内瓦会议精神的一番话时发表这一意见的。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约指出：“如果伦斯害怕万隆会议的反殖民主义精神，那是可以理解的，因为荷兰想继续它在西伊里安的统治。”
