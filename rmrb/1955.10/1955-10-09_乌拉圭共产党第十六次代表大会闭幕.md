Title: 16th Congress of the Communist Party of Uruguay Concludes

Author:
Date: 1955-10-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, October 7th (TASS): The 16th Congress of the Communist Party of Uruguay concluded in Montevideo. The Congress unanimously adopted resolutions on the reports of the Central Committee and the Inspection Committee, and elected a Central Committee composed of twenty-nine members. The new Central Committee was entrusted with formulating a new party program and constitution, to be submitted to a special party congress for deliberation.

    The Congress abolished the post of General Secretary and elected an Executive Committee composed of eleven members and three alternate members, and a Secretariat composed of five members. Rodney Arismendi was elected First Secretary. The resolutions passed by the Congress stipulated the policy of further improving the political and ideological level of all party members and party cadres.

    The Congress called for continuing the struggle for peace, organizing the masses around the People's Struggle Committee, opposing the government's compromise with US imperialism, and striving to reduce the cost of living. The Congress called for strengthening the unity of the working class and strengthening the worker-peasant alliance, as the basis for organizing a democratic front to liberate the country from the shackles of imperialism and large landowners. The Congress called for full support for organizing peasants into cooperatives, trade unions, and other organizations in rural areas to strengthen the struggle for land reform.

    "Gong Li Bao" wrote that the 16th Congress of the Communist Party of Uruguay demonstrated the party's remarkable, solid unity and demonstrated "its ability to fight for the liberation of our people."

### 乌拉圭共产党第十六次代表大会闭幕

1955-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭共产党第十六次代表大会闭幕
    新华社八日讯　据塔斯社蒙得维的亚七日讯：乌拉圭共产党第十六次代表大会在蒙得维的亚闭幕。大会就中央委员会和检查委员会的报告一致通过了决议，并且选举了由二十九人组成的中央委员会。新的中央委员会受委托制定新党纲和党章，提交特别党代表大会审议。
    代表大会取消了总书记的职务，选出了由十一名委员和三名候补委员组成的执行委员会和由五人组成的书记处。罗德尼·阿里斯门迪被选为第一书记。代表大会通过的决议里规定了进一步提高全体党员和党的干部的政治水平和思想水平的方针。
    大会号召继续展开争取和平的斗争，把群众组织到人民斗争委员会周围，反对政府同美帝国主义妥协，争取减低生活费用。大会号召加强工人阶级的团结和加强工农联盟，作为组织争取把全国从帝国主义和大地主的桎梏下解放出来的民主阵线的基础。大会号召全力支持在农村中把农民组织到合作社、工会和其他组织中去，以加强为实行土地改革而进行的斗争。
    “公理报”写道，乌拉圭共产党第十六次代表大会表现了党的显著的牢固的团结一致，表现了“它为我国人民解放而斗争的能力”。
