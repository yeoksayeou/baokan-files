Title: A Modern Hospital Opens in Xigazê

Author:

Date: 1955-10-20
Page: 3

    A Modern Hospital Opens in Xigazê
    The Xigazê People's Hospital officially opened on the 15th. At the opening ceremony, the Panchen Erdeni presented fifty steel wire beds and five fracture beds as gifts to celebrate the establishment of the hospital. More than 500 people, including officials from the Panchen Kanpo Lhentse Committee, officials from the Tibetan local government stationed in Xigazê, students from the Tibetan Medicine School of the Panchen Kanpo Lhentse Committee, and citizens and lamas of Xigazê, attended to offer their congratulations. In their speeches, Jiantong. Jijinmei, Director of the Panchen Kanpo Lhentse Committee, and other guests unanimously agreed that the establishment of this modern hospital would further ensure the health of the people in the Xigazê area. Everyone thanked the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao for their concern for the Tibetan people.
    The newly built Xigazê People's Hospital has thirteen departments including internal medicine, surgery, obstetrics, X-ray, operating room, and a ward. The hospital has universal operating tables, automatic delivery beds, and electrotherapy equipment, making it the first hospital with modern equipment in the Xigazê area.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 日喀则一所现代化医院开幕

1955-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

    日喀则一所现代化医院开幕
    日喀则人民医院在十五日正式开幕。在开幕典礼上，班禅额尔德尼赠送了五十张钢丝病床和五张折骨病床，作为庆贺这个医院成立的礼物。班禅堪布会议厅委员会官员、西藏地方政府驻日喀则官员、班禅堪布会议厅委员会藏医学校学员和日喀则市民、喇嘛等五百多人前往祝贺。班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任委员詹东·计晋美和其他来宾在讲话中，一致认为这所现代化医院成立后，日喀则地区人民的健康就有了进一步的保障。大家都感谢中国共产党和毛主席对西藏人民的关怀。
    新建的日喀则人民医院设有内科、外科、产科、爱克斯光科、手术室等十三个科室和一个住院部，医院里有万能手术床、自动产床和电疗器，是日喀则地区第一个具有现代化设备的医院。
                                  （新华社）
