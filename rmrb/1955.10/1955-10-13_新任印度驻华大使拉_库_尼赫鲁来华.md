Title: Newly Appointed Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, Arrives in China
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 12th—The newly appointed Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and his wife left New Delhi on the 12th and took a boat to China via Bombay.
    Among those who came to the station to see them off were the Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Pillai, and other government officials.
    Chinese Ambassador to India, Yuan Zhongxian, and members of the diplomatic corps also came to the station to see them off.

### 新任印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁来华

1955-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    新任印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁来华
    新华社新德里十二日电　新任印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和他的夫人十二日离开新德里，取道孟买乘轮船前往中国。
    到车站欢送的有印度外交部秘书长皮莱和其他政府官员。
    中国驻印度大使袁仲贤和外交团人员也到车站欢送。
