Title: Danish Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1955-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 12th. The five-member Danish cultural delegation, led by its head Hans Hansen, left Beijing for home on the 12th, after a visit to China at invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

The other five members of the delegation had previously left Beijing for home on October 2nd.

### 丹麦文化代表团离北京回国

1955-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    丹麦文化代表团离北京回国
    新华社十二日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国参观访问的丹麦文化代表团一行五人，在团长汉斯·汉森率领下在十二日离京返国。
    代表团其他五位代表已先期在十月二日离京返国。
