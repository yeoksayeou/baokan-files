Title: Welcome the Indian National Volleyball Team (Picture)
Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1955-10-31
Page: 3

Welcome the Indian National Volleyball Team

    The opening ceremony for the Indian National Volleyball Team's visit and competition in our country was held on the 26th at the newly built gymnasium in Beijing. The picture shows the Indian National Volleyball Team (left) and the Beijing Volleyball Team (right) at the opening ceremony of the competition. Our reporter Gao Liang photographed.
    A scene from the friendly match between the Indian National Volleyball Team and the Beijing Volleyball Team. Photo by Dong Qing (New Sports Agency Manuscript)
    Female athletes from Beijing present flowers to members of the Indian National Volleyball Team. Our reporter Gao Liang photographed.
