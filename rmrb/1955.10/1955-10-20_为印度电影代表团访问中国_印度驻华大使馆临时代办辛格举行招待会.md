Title: Indian Embassy Chargé d'Affaires in China, Singh, Holds Reception for Visiting Indian Film Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 18th. Bahadur Singh, Chargé d'Affaires of the Indian Embassy in China, held a reception on the evening of the 18th for the visiting Indian film delegation.

The entire Indian film delegation, headed by P.L. Kappar, the head of the delegation, and B.N. Sircar, the deputy head of the delegation, attended the reception.

Among those invited to attend the reception were Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Vice Ministers Zheng Zhenduo and Xia Yan, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations Chen Zhongjing, Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture Wang Lanxi, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhao Yimin, as well as other heads of various people's organizations, Chinese film directors, actors, and other people from the literary and art circles.

During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently proposed toasts to the friendship between the Chinese and Indian people, and to the growing development of cultural exchange and film industries in both countries. After the reception, Indian documentary films such as "Indian Prime Minister Nehru Visits the Soviet Union" were shown. Indian artists also performed wonderful programs.

### 为印度电影代表团访问中国  印度驻华大使馆临时代办辛格举行招待会

1955-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    为印度电影代表团访问中国
    印度驻华大使馆临时代办辛格举行招待会
    新华社十八日讯　印度驻华大使馆临时代办巴哈杜尔·辛格为印度电影代表团访问中国，在十八日晚举行招待会。
    以团长普·拉·卡普尔和副团长布·纳·西尔卡为首的印度电影代表团全体人员出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，有中华人民共和国文化部代部长钱俊瑞，副部长郑振铎、夏衍，外交部部长助理陈家康，对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经，文化部电影事业管理局局长王阑西，中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙，中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏，以及其他各人民团体负责人，中国电影编导、演员和文艺界其他人士。
    席间，宾主频频互为中印两国人民的友谊，为两国的文化交流和两国的电影事业的日益发展而干杯。招待会后，放映了“印度总理尼赫鲁访问苏联”等印度纪录影片。印度艺术家们还表演了精采节目。
