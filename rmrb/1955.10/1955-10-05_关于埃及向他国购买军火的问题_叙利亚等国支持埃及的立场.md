Title: On Egypt's Purchase of Arms from Other Countries: Syria and Other Countries Support Egypt's Position
Author:
Date: 1955-10-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 4th: Syrian Prime Minister Said Ghazi told the parliament on October 3rd that Syria supports Egypt's purchase of arms from any country for self-defense.

Jordanian Acting Prime Minister Hazza' Majali also said in a statement on the 2nd that after Western countries refused to supply Egypt with arms, the step of purchasing arms from Eastern European countries was a natural one.

Majali also said, "Any sovereign country has the right to follow policies that guarantee its sovereignty, if such policies do not contradict its international obligations."

### 关于埃及向他国购买军火的问题  叙利亚等国支持埃及的立场

1955-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    关于埃及向他国购买军火的问题
    叙利亚等国支持埃及的立场
    新华社四日讯　叙利亚总理赛义德·加齐十月三日对议会说，叙利亚支持埃及从任何国家购买军火自卫。
    约旦代理首相哈扎·马加利也在二日发表谈话说，在西方国家拒绝以军火供给埃及以后，从东欧国家购买军火的步骤是一个自然而然的步骤。
    马加利又说，“任何主权国家都有权遵循保证它的主权的政策，如果这种政策并不同它的国际义务相矛盾的话。”
