Title: CPPCC National Committee Holds Peking Opera Evening to Welcome Overseas Chinese Visiting Groups, etc.
Author:
Date: 1955-10-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held a Peking Opera evening on the 6th to welcome overseas Chinese visiting groups from Indonesia, Burma, India, North Korea, and Vietnam, as well as the Hong Kong and Macao National Day Observation Delegations.
    At the beginning of the evening, Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, delivered a welcoming speech, followed by a performance of Peking Opera.

### 政协全国委员会举行京剧晚会  欢迎华侨回国观光团等

1955-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会举行京剧晚会
    欢迎华侨回国观光团等
    新华社六日讯中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在六日晚举行京剧晚会，欢迎印度尼西亚、缅甸、印度、朝鲜、越南等地的华侨回国观光团和港澳国庆观礼团。
    晚会开始时，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李济深致欢迎词，接着演出了京剧。
