Title: Qinghai Province Minority Nationalities Visiting Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency News: A Qinghai Province Minority Nationalities Visiting Group composed of members from the Tibetan, Mongolian, Hui, and Kazakh nationalities, totaling sixty-nine people, led by the head of the group, Dander (Tibetan nationality), arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 25th.

### 青海省少数民族参观团到京

1955-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    青海省少数民族参观团到京
    新华社讯　由藏、蒙古、回、哈萨克等民族组成的青海省少数民族参观团一行六十九人，在团长丹德尔（藏族）率领下，二十五日晚到北京。
