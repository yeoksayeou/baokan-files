Title: Soviet Communist Party Central Committee Issues Slogans for Celebrating the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1955-10-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 24th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has issued slogans for celebrating the 38th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

    The slogans begin by hailing: Long live the 38th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution!

    The slogans state: Working people of all countries! Peace can be maintained and consolidated if the people of all countries take on the cause of defending peace and persevere in it to the end! Strengthen the unity of the people of all countries in the struggle for peace!

    Peace fighters of all countries! Form a strong front of peace defenders against the warmongers, attract broader masses of people to participate in this front, and be vigilant against the conspiracies of the enemies of peace! Hold higher the banner of striving for peace and for peaceful cooperation among all countries!

    Working people of all countries! Strive for the reduction of armaments! Demand the prohibition of atomic weapons, hydrogen weapons, and other weapons of mass destruction! Make atomic energy serve the cause of peace and human progress!

    Strive for lasting peace and the collective security of the people of all European countries!

    Working people of the Soviet Union and the people's democratic countries! Tirelessly strengthen the socialist and democratic camp, develop and strengthen the fraternal friendship and cooperation between the people of our countries!

    We extend fraternal greetings to the working people of the people's democratic countries—Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, Romania, Albania, and the Mongolian People's Republic—who are selflessly fighting for the further development of the national economy and culture, for the continuous improvement of the welfare of the people, and for peace and the construction of socialism!

    We extend fraternal greetings to the great Chinese people who are victoriously struggling to complete the First Five-Year Plan for the development of the national economy, for the industrialization of the country and the collectivization of agriculture, and for the construction of the foundations of socialism! Long live the People's Republic of China, a world power!

    Long live the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the people of the Soviet Union and China, a powerful factor for world peace! May this friendship and cooperation prosper and develop!

    We extend fraternal greetings to the heroic people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the heroic people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam who are struggling for the restoration and further development of the national economy, for peace, and for the reunification of the country on a democratic basis!

    Long live the German Democratic Republic—the fortress of all peace-loving forces in Germany who are fighting against the revival of militarism and for the reunification of the fatherland on a peaceful and democratic basis!

    We pay our respects to the people of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia! May the fraternal amity and cooperation between the peoples of the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia grow stronger and flourish!

    May the friendship and cooperation between the Soviet Union and the Republic of Finland, between the people of the Soviet Union and Austria, and between the peoples of the Soviet Union and India grow stronger and develop!

    We pay our respects to the people of colonial countries and dependent countries who are fighting against imperialist oppression and for their freedom and national independence!

    Long live the friendship and cooperation of the people of Britain, the United States, and the Soviet Union in the struggle for easing international tensions, for the peaceful coexistence of all countries, and for ensuring lasting world peace!

    May the friendship and cooperation between the Soviet people and the people of France and Italy in the struggle for peace, against the revival of German militarism, and for the establishment of collective security in Europe grow stronger!

    We pay our respects to the working people of the Federal Republic of Germany who are fighting against reactionary forces and for peace and the security of the people of all European countries!

    We pay our respects to the people of Japan who are fighting against the revival of militarism, for national independence, and for the democratic development of their country!

    The slogans call upon the soldiers of the Soviet Army and Navy to unswervingly improve their military and political knowledge, improve their combat skills, and master the latest combat technology and modern weapons.

    The slogans call upon the workers in all sectors of the Soviet national economy, Soviet women, young men and women, Communist Party members, and Communist Youth League members to strive for the further development of the Soviet socialist industry and agriculture, the technological progress of the national economy, the continuous improvement of the welfare of the Soviet people, and the construction of communism in the Soviet Union!

### 苏共中央发布庆祝十月革命节口号

1955-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央发布庆祝十月革命节口号
    新华社二十五日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二十四日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会发布了庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十八周年的口号。
    口号首先欢呼：伟大的十月社会主义革命三十八周年万岁！
    口号说：各国的劳动人民！如果各国人民将维护和平的事业担当起来，并且把这一事业坚持到底，和平就能够保持和巩固！加强各国人民在争取和平的斗争中的团结！
    各国的和平战士们！结成一条和平保卫者反对新战争贩子的强大的阵线，吸引更广大的人民群众参加这个阵线，对和平敌人的阴谋提高警惕！更高地举起争取和平和争取各国人民的和平合作的旗帜！
    各国的劳动人民！争取裁减军备！要求禁止原子武器、氢武器和其他大规模毁灭性武器！使原子能为和平和人类进步的事业服务！
    争取持久和平和欧洲各国人民的集体安全！
    苏联和人民民主国家的劳动人民！不倦地加强社会主义和民主阵营，发展和加强我们各国人民之间的兄弟友谊和合作！
    向正在为进一步发展国民经济和文化，为不断提高人民群众的福利，为和平和建设社会主义而忘我奋斗的人民民主国家——波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古人民共和国的劳动人民致以兄弟的敬礼！
    向正在为完成发展国民经济第一个五年计划，为国家工业化和农业合作化，为建设社会主义基础而胜利地进行斗争的伟大的中国人民致以兄弟的敬礼！世界强国——中华人民共和国万岁！
    世界和平的强大因素——苏中两国人民的牢不可破的友谊和合作万岁！祝这种友谊和合作繁荣发展！
    向正在为恢复和进一步发展国民经济，为和平，为在民主的基础上求得全国的统一而奋斗的英雄的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民和英雄的越南民主共和国人民致以兄弟的敬礼！
    德意志民主共和国——为反对复活军国主义、争取在和平、民主的基础上统一祖国而奋斗的全德国和平力量的堡垒万岁！
    向南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国的人民致敬！祝苏联和南斯拉夫两国人民之间的兄弟友谊和合作日益巩固和繁荣！
    祝苏联和芬兰共和国之间、苏联和奥地利两国人民之间、苏联和印度两国人民之间的友谊和合作日益巩固和发展！
    向为反对帝国主义压迫、争取自己的自由和民族独立而奋斗的殖民地和附属国人民致敬！
    英、美、苏三国人民在争取缓和国际紧张局势、争取各国和平共处和保障世界持久和平的斗争中的友谊和合作万岁！
    祝苏联人民同法、意两国人民在争取和平、反对复活德国军国主义、争取建立欧洲集体安全的斗争中的友谊和合作日益巩固！
    向正在为反对反动势力、为争取和平和欧洲各国人民的安全而斗争的德意志联邦共和国的劳动人民致敬！
    向正在为反对复活军国主义、争取民族独立和争取本国的民主发展而斗争的日本人民致敬！
    口号号召苏联陆海军军人坚持不渝地提高军事和政治知识、改进战斗技能、掌握最新的战斗技术和现代武器。
    口号号召苏联国民经济各部门的劳动者，苏联妇女、男女青年、共产党员和共青团员为苏联的社会主义工业和农业的进一步发展、国民经济的技术进步、苏联人民福利的不断提高和在苏联建成共产主义而奋斗！
