Title: Burmese Ambassador Hosts Reception for Burmese Football Delegation's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1955-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 15th: The Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, U Hla Maung, held a reception at 5:00 PM on the 15th for the Burmese National Football Delegation's visit to China.

Invited to attend were He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China and Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation; and responsible persons from the Beijing Branch of the All-China Sports Federation and figures from the capital's sports circles.

Also attending the reception were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

At the reception, U Hla Maung introduced the members of the football delegation to He Long and other guests. The hosts and guests engaged in cordial conversations and raised their glasses to wish each other good health and Sino-Burmese friendship.

### 缅甸大使为缅甸足球代表团访华举行酒会

1955-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸大使为缅甸足球代表团访华举行酒会
    新华社十五日讯　缅甸联邦驻华大使吴拉茂为缅甸国家足球代表团访问中国，在十五日下午五时举行酒会。
    应邀出席的，有中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼体育运动委员会主任贺龙，外交部副部长章汉夫，中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠，以及中华全国体育总会北京市分会的负责人和首都体育界人士。
    出席酒会的，还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    在酒会上，吴拉茂介绍足球代表团人员同贺龙和其他来宾会见。宾主之间进行了亲切的谈话，并举杯互祝健康和中缅友好。
