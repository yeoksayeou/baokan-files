Title: President Pritt of the British Peace Committee Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1955-10-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 11th -- Mr. Pritt, President of the British Peace Committee, President of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, and winner of the Stalin International Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Peoples," and his wife left Beijing for home on the 11th. Zhang Zhiran and Qian Duansheng, Vice Presidents of the China Political Science and Law Association, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Shen Zijiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, were at the airport to see them off.
On the evening of the 10th, Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Shen Junru, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Association, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to Mr. and Mrs. Pritt.

### 英国和平委员会会长普里特离北京返国

1955-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

    英国和平委员会会长普里特离北京返国
    新华社十一日讯　英国和平委员会会长、国际民主法律工作者协会主席、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人普里特和他的夫人在十一日离京返国。中国政治法律学会副主席张志让、钱端升，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一和中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记沈兹九等到机场欢送。
    十日晚，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若和中国政治法律学会副主席沈钧儒曾设宴欢送普里特夫妇。
