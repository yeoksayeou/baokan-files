Title: Soviet and Other Countries' Government Delegations Arrive in Berlin to Attend the Sixth Anniversary Celebration of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1955-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 6th. (TASS dispatch from Berlin, October 5th) The Soviet government delegation, headed by Suslov, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, arrived in Berlin on the 5th to attend the celebration of the sixth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic. The delegation members include Tikhomirov, Minister of the Chemical Industry of the Soviet Union, and Pushkin, Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic.

Suslov said in a statement at the airport: Our delegation has come to Berlin to celebrate with you a great day—the sixth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, and to congratulate your people on their achievements in building a new, free, peaceful, and democratic Germany. Suslov then said that we note with satisfaction that good friendly relations have been established between the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic, and that these relations are becoming stronger year by year. This is of great significance for the further development of the economy and culture of our two countries and for the consolidation of world peace.

The government delegations of Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Hungary, Albania, Mongolia, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, which are participating in the celebration of the sixth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, also arrived in Berlin on the 5th. The Romanian government delegation arrived in Berlin on the 4th.

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th (TASS dispatch from Berlin): Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, received the Soviet government delegation headed by Suslov, member of the Presidium and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union

### 参加德意志民主共和国建国六周年庆典  苏联等国政府代表团到达柏林

1955-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    参加德意志民主共和国建国六周年庆典
    苏联等国政府代表团到达柏林
    新华社六日讯据塔斯社柏林五日讯：以苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员苏斯洛夫为首的苏联政府代表团已在五日抵达柏林，参加德意志民主共和国建国六周年庆祝典礼。代表团团员包括苏联化学工业部部长季霍米罗夫和苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使普希金。
    苏斯洛夫在机场发表声明说：我们代表团来柏林是为了同你们在一起庆祝一个伟大的日子——德意志民主共和国成立六周年，并且庆贺你们人民在建设一个新的自由、和平和民主的德国方面的成就。苏斯洛夫接着说，我们满意地指出，苏联和德意志民主共和国之间已经建立了良好的友好关系，而且这种关系一年比一年巩固。这对于我们两国的经济和文化的进一步发展和对世界和平的巩固都具有重大的意义。
    参加德意志民主共和国成立六周年庆祝典礼的捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、匈牙利、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的政府代表团也已在五日到达柏林。罗马尼亚政府代表团在四日即已到达柏林。
    新华社六日讯　据塔斯社柏林讯：德意志民主共和国总统皮克五日在尼德申豪森宫接见了以苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员兼中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫为首的苏联政府代表团。
    接见时在座的有德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥和德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记、德意志民主共和国副总理乌布利希。
