Title: Shigatse Warmly Celebrates the Opening of the Lhasa-Shigatse and Shigatse-Gyangtse Highways; Tibetan Local Government, the Panchen Kanpo Lhentsog, and Others Express Condolences to the Road Builders
Author:
Date: 1955-10-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, October 21st: On the afternoon of the 20th, people from all walks of life in Shigatse held a grand ceremony in People's Square to celebrate the opening of the Lhasa-Shigatse and Shigatse-Gyangtse highways. The venue was surrounded by trucks laden with goods. Before the meeting, the people of Shigatse presented tens of thousands of white hadas to the portrait of Chairman Mao on the decorated floats.
    More than 20,000 people from all walks of life in Shigatse city and lamas from Tashilhunpo Monastery participated in the celebration, as well as Tibetan and Han model workers and some road construction workers who built the Lhasa-Shigatse and Shigatse-Gyangtse highways. More than 300 representatives of farmers and herdsmen from over 40 dzongs and khalka in the middle and upper reaches of the Yarlung Zangbo River, the northern Tibetan grasslands, and the Himalayan region came with letters of condolence and hadas to participate in the celebration.
    At 1 p.m., the celebration began amidst the sound of salutes. The conference accepted the banners presented to the conference by the Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in Tibet, the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the Panchen Erdeni. The Shigatse Sub-Committee of the Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in Tibet, the Tibetan Local Government, and the Panchen Kanpo Lhentsog presented banners to the conference and the leading organs responsible for building the Lhasa-Shigatse and Shigatse-Gyangtse highways. Then, representatives of mass organizations and elementary school students in Shigatse presented hadas and bouquets to the leading staff and model heroes who built the two highways.
    Liang Xuanxian, Secretary of the Shigatse Sub-Committee of the Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in Tibet, said at the meeting: "The opening of these two highways is another major achievement for the people of Tibet in implementing the country's first five-year plan and the decisions of the State Council. This is a concrete contribution of the Tibetan people in building the common cause of the motherland's big family." Jiantong·Jijinmei, Director of the Panchen Kanpo Lhentsog, said: "The opening of the Lhasa-Shigatse and Shigatse-Gyangtse highways fully demonstrates the State Council's cordial concern for the Tibetan people, making the internal connections of Tibet and the connections between the Tibetan people and the people of all fraternal ethnic groups in the country closer, and consolidating the unity among the Tibetan people and between the Han and Tibetan nationalities, which greatly encourages the Tibetan people's confidence in building the motherland. In the future, we must further strengthen the unity between the Han and Tibetan nationalities and among the Tibetan people, and strive to do a good job in the preparatory work for the establishment of the Tibet Autonomous Region." Renxi Dorje·Ngawang Tsering, the representative of the Tibetan local government who came to participate in the Shigatse opening ceremony, said: "The full opening of the Lhasa-Shigatse and Shigatse-Gyangtse highways is another great happy event for the Tibetan people, which will further consolidate the unity within Tibet. Under the leadership of the State Council, the work of establishing the preparatory committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region is underway. We, the Tibetan people, should actively undertake the task of building Tibet and enter socialist society together with the people of all ethnic groups in the country." Xia Zhongyuan, representative of the Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in Tibet, representatives of the leading departments responsible for building the Lhasa-Shigatse and Shigatse-Gyangtse highways, representatives of model heroes, representatives of officials from the Tibetan local government stationed in Shigatse, representatives of various dzongs and khalka in the Shigatse area, representatives of various mass organizations in Shigatse, and representatives of Tashilhunpo Monastery all spoke at the meeting.
    The conference read out the congratulatory telegram from the Panchen Kanpo Lhentsog to Chairman Mao and the State Council and the letter of condolence to the road builders. In addition, congratulatory telegrams from the Qamdo People's Liberation Committee and the Gyangtse Sub-Committee of the Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in Tibet were also read out.
    After the celebration meeting, a celebration gala began in the square. Famous folk artists, folk troupes, and Tibetan and Han youths in Shigatse performed wonderful Tibetan songs and dances.
    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, October 21st: The Tibetan local government, the Panchen Kanpo Lhentsog, and people from all walks of life in Shigatse jointly expressed their condolences to the personnel who built the Lhasa-Shigatse and Shigatse-Gyangtse highways on the 20th in Shigatse.
    The Tibetan local government and the Panchen Kanpo Lhentsog jointly presented more than 100 packages of condolence items, including highland barley, rice, flour, beef, cigarettes, and towels. The Panchen Kanpo Lhentsog, officials of the Tibetan local government stationed in Shigatse, people from all walks of life in Shigatse, and representatives of various dzongs and khalka in the Shigatse area who participated in the opening ceremony also wrote letters of condolence to the road builders. Mass organizations in Shigatse also performed many songs and dance programs for the road builders who came to Shigatse to participate in the opening ceremony.

### 日喀则热烈庆祝拉日、日江两公路通车  西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会等慰问筑路人员

1955-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    日喀则热烈庆祝拉日、日江两公路通车
    西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会等慰问筑路人员
    新华社拉萨二十一日电　二十日下午，日喀则各界人民在人民广场举行庆祝拉（萨）日（喀则）、日（喀则）江（孜）两条公路通车典礼大会。会场周围排列着满载物资的汽车。开会前，日喀则人民向彩车上的毛主席像献了上万条雪白的哈达。
    参加庆祝大会的，有日喀则市各界人民和札什伦布寺的喇嘛等两万多人，还有修筑拉日、日江两条公路的藏、汉族功臣模范和部分筑路职工。雅鲁藏布江中、上游地区、藏北草原、喜马拉雅山区等地的四十多个宗和谿卡的三百多个农、牧民代表，都带着慰问信和哈达赶来参加庆祝大会。
    下午一时，庆祝大会在礼炮声中开始。大会接受了中共西藏工作委员会、中国人民解放军西藏军区、班禅额尔德尼赠给大会的锦旗，中共西藏工委日喀则分工委、西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会又分别向大会和修筑拉日、日江公路的领导机关赠旗。接着，日喀则市群众团体的代表和小学生把哈达和花束献给了修筑两条公路的领导工作人员和功臣模范。
    中共西藏工委日喀则分工委书记梁选贤在会上讲话说：“这两条公路的通车是西藏人民执行国家第一个五年计划和国务院决定的又一重大成就，这是西藏人民在建设祖国大家庭的共同事业中的具体贡献。”班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任詹东·计晋美说：“拉日、日江公路的通车，充分标志着国务院对西藏人民的亲切关怀，使西藏内部的联系以及西藏人民和全国各兄弟民族人民的联系更加密切，使西藏人民内部以及汉藏民族之间的团结更加巩固，这就大大鼓舞了西藏人民建设祖国的信心。今后，我们要更进一步地加强汉、藏民族之间和西藏人民内部的团结，努力做好成立西藏自治区筹备委员会的工作。”前来参加日喀则通车典礼的西藏地方政府代表仁希多德·阿旺泽仁说：“拉日、日江公路全线通车，是西藏人民的又一件大喜事，这将进一步巩固西藏内部的团结。在国务院领导下，成立西藏自治区筹备委员会的工作正在进行，我们西藏人民应该积极担负起建设西藏的任务，和全国各族人民一道进入社会主义社会。”中共西藏工委代表夏仲远，修筑拉日、日江公路的领导部门的代表，功臣模范代表，西藏地方政府驻日喀则官员代表，日喀则地区各宗、谿卡代表，日喀则各群众团体代表和札什伦布寺代表都在会上讲了话。
    大会宣读了班禅堪布会议厅委员会向毛主席和国务院的致敬电和给筑路人员的慰问信。此外还宣读了昌都地区人民解放委员会、中共西藏工委江孜分工委的贺电。
    庆祝大会结束以后，广场上开始了庆祝联欢会。日喀则市的著名民间艺人、民间剧团和藏、汉族男女青年分别表演了精采的西藏歌舞。
    新华社拉萨二十一日电　西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会和日喀则各界人民二十日在日喀则市联合慰问修筑拉（萨）日（喀则）、日（喀则）江（孜）两条公路的人员。
    西藏地方政府和班禅堪布会议厅委员会联合赠送了青稞、大米、面粉、牛肉、纸烟和毛巾等一百多包慰问品。班禅堪布会议厅委员会、西藏地方政府驻日喀则官员、日喀则各界人民和日喀则地区各宗、各谿卡参加通车典礼的代表，也都分别给筑路人员写了慰问信。日喀则市群众团体还为来日喀则参加通车典礼的筑路人员演出许多歌舞节目。
