Title: German Democratic Republic Brass Band Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 4th: The German Democratic Republic Brass Band, led by its conductor, Kurt Bach, arrived in Beijing by plane at 4:30 PM on the 4th.

Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, representatives from the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Georg Gruttner, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, and many young artists from the capital went to the airport to welcome them.

After disembarking, the German artists warmly shook hands and embraced the welcomers, receiving flowers from the young Chinese artists. Xia Yan delivered a speech welcoming the German artists who had traveled far to visit our country. He said, "Your visit and performances will not only strengthen the cultural exchange between our two countries and enhance the friendly cooperation between our two peoples, but also allow the Chinese people, especially Chinese musicians, to learn many beneficial things from your visit and performances." Bach then delivered a reply, thanking them for their warm welcome.

Xinhua News Agency, October 4th: The German Democratic Republic Sports Delegation concluded its visit to China and left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Chinese gymnasts went to the airport to see them off.

On the eve of the delegation's departure from Beijing, Vice Premier of the State Council He Long hosted a banquet to bid farewell to all members of the delegation.

The delegation visited China for more than a month and held fourteen gymnastics performances in the four cities of Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, and Guangzhou. A total of more than 110,000 Chinese athletes and spectators watched the performances. The delegation also introduced advanced technology and experience in gymnastics to Chinese athletes through seminars and joint practice sessions, and presented educational films on gymnastics techniques to Chinese athletes. The delegation also visited factories, rural areas, and scenic spots in various places.

### 德意志民主共和国铜管乐团到达北京

1955-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国铜管乐团到达北京
    新华社四日讯　德意志民主共和国铜管乐团由团长库·巴赫率领在四日下午四时半乘飞机到达北京。
    中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍、对外文化联络局的代表、德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办葛鲁特纳和首都青年文艺工作者多人前往机场欢迎。
    德国艺术家们下飞机后，和欢迎者亲切地握手拥抱，接受了中国青年文艺工作者的献花。夏衍致词欢迎远道来我国访问的德国艺术家。他说：“你们的来访和演出，不仅会加强两国之间的文化交流，增进两国人民的友好合作，中国人民，——中国音乐工作者们更将从你们的访问演出中，学到很多有益的东西。”巴赫接着致答词，感谢对他们的热烈欢迎。民主德国体育代表团返国
    新华社四日讯　德意志民主共和国体育代表团结束了在中国的访问，四日上午乘飞机离北京回国。中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠和中国体操运动员等到机场送行。
    在代表团离京前夕，国务院副总理贺龙曾设宴欢送代表团全体人员。
    代表团在中国访问了一个多月，曾在北京、天津、上海、广州四个城市举行了十四场体操表演，观看表演的中国运动员和观众共达十一万多人。代表团人员还通过座谈会、共同练习等方式向中国运动员介绍了体操运动方面的先进技术和经验，并把关于体操技术的教育影片赠送给中国运动员。代表团在各地还参观了工厂、农村和游览了名胜古迹。
