Title: Soviet Artists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th. A Soviet artists delegation composed of twenty-five outstanding Soviet artists arrived in Beijing by train at 6:20 PM on the 30th. The head of the delegation is S.F. Nikishkin, and the deputy head is O.S. Soroko-Tsiupa. The delegation members include famous singers, pianists, violinists, soft gymnasts, acrobats, the Feodorova Five Sisters who perform a vocal quintet, and dancers.

More than two hundred people from the capital's literary and art circles and young literary and art workers gathered at the Beijing Railway Station to warmly welcome the arrival of the Soviet artists. Among those welcoming were Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Vice Minister Zheng Zhenduo, Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Wang Kunlun, Vice Mayor of Beijing, Li Zhanwu, Ge Baoquan, and Gu Shuxing, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, as well as Romanjin and Gutikov, Counsellors of the Soviet Embassy.

A grand welcoming ceremony was held at the station, and flowers were presented to the Soviet artists. Zheng Zhenduo delivered a speech on behalf of the Ministry of Culture and the Chinese people, warmly welcoming the Soviet artists delegation. Nikishkin delivered a reply speech, expressing his great pleasure at having the opportunity to visit the great and beautiful China. He also shouted "Long live the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples!"

That evening, Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture, held a banquet to welcome the Soviet artists delegation. (Attached photo)

### 苏联艺术家代表团到达北京

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联艺术家代表团到达北京
    新华社三十日讯　由二十五位卓越的苏联艺术家组成的苏联艺术家代表团在三十日下午六时二十分乘火车到达北京。代表团团长是斯·弗·尼基什金，副团长是奥·斯·索罗科—秋帕，团员中有著名的歌唱家、钢琴家、提琴家、柔软体操家、杂技演员、表演五重唱的费奥多罗娃五姊妹和舞蹈家。
    首都文艺界人士和青年文艺工作者二百多人聚集在北京车站、热烈欢迎苏联艺术家的到来。在欢迎的行列中，有中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞、副部长郑振铎，中国人民解放军总政治部文化部部长陈沂，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，北京市副市长王崑崙，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长李沾吾、戈宝权、顾淑型，以及苏联大使馆参赞罗迈进、顾悌克夫。
    在车站上举行了盛大的欢迎仪式，向苏联艺术家献了花。郑振铎代表文化部和中国人民致词，热烈欢迎苏联艺术家代表团。尼基什金致答词，表示对有机会能来伟大而美丽的中国进行访问感到十分高兴。他并高呼中苏两国人民的伟大友谊万岁。
    当日晚间，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞举行宴会，欢迎苏联艺术家代表团。（附图片）
