Title: Marshal Rokossovsky Sends Telegram to Marshal Zhu De

Author:
Date: 1955-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Marshal Rokossovsky, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic, sent a telegram to Zhu De, Vice-Chairman of the People's Republic of China, congratulating him on being awarded the rank of Marshal of the People's Republic of China. The telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Zhu De, Vice-Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Marshal of the People's Republic of China:

Please accept my most heartfelt congratulations on the occasion of your being awarded the rank of Marshal of the People's Republic of China.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic, Marshal of Poland
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Konstantin Rokossovsky
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Warsaw, September 30, 1955

### 罗科索夫斯基元帅电贺朱德元帅

1955-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    罗科索夫斯基元帅电贺朱德元帅
    新华社二日讯　波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长、波兰元帅罗科索夫斯基致电中华人民共和国副主席朱德，祝贺他荣获中华人民共和国元帅军衔。电文如下：中华人民共和国副主席、中华人民共和国元帅朱德同志：
    在您被授予中华人民共和国元帅军衔之际，请接受我最衷心的祝贺。
            波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长、波兰元帅
　康·罗科索夫斯基
      一九五五年九月三十日于华沙
