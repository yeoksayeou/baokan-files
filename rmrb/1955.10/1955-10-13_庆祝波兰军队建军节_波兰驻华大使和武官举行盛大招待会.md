Title: Polish Ambassador and Military Attaché Hold Grand Reception to Celebrate Polish Army Day
Author:
Date: 1955-10-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 12th. The Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilo and the Military Attaché of the Polish Embassy in China, Lieutenant Colonel Wancze, held a grand reception on the evening of the 12th to celebrate Polish Army Day.

Among those present at the reception were: Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense; General Xiao Ke, Deputy Minister of National Defense; General Li Da; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; and other heads of ministries and commissions; senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Chen Geng, General Xiao Hua, General Wang Hongkun, General Chen Shiju, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Su Jing, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun, Lieutenant General Qiu Chuangcheng, Lieutenant General Tan Jashu, Lieutenant General Li Tianhuan, and generals and officers from various departments, services, and branches of the armed forces, as well as cultural workers from the armed forces, and people from the capital's science, education, literature, and journalism circles.

Also present at the reception were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials of various countries in China, military attachés, acting military attachés, deputy military attachés, as well as heads and representatives of art troupes from various countries visiting and performing in Beijing.

More than 700 people attended the reception.

Before the reception, the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble performed wonderful programs. At the beginning of the performance, Polish artists first sang the national anthems of Poland and China.

At the reception, Kirilo first delivered a speech, extending sincere fraternal greetings to the Chinese people and their heroic People's Liberation Army. Kirilo said: The People's Republic of Poland, which has brotherly friendship with the great Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and all people's democratic countries, and cooperates with all peoples of all countries who long for freedom and peace, is striding forward in the final year of the six-year plan for socialist construction, which is a guarantee of people's welfare, independence, and peace.

Lieutenant Colonel Wancze said in his speech: "Sincere and close friendship unites the Polish army with the armed forces of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and the people's democratic countries, and they are fighting with us to consolidate peace." On behalf of the Polish army, he expressed his gratitude to all peace-loving guests tonight, thanked the military representatives of various countries in Beijing, and hoped to "carry out friendly cooperation" with them.

Marshal Peng Dehuai then delivered a speech. He extended cordial congratulations to the Polish army on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Marshal Peng Dehuai spoke of the good and profound impression he had left during his visit to the Polish People's Army in May this year, saying: "We have seen that the Polish army, with its glorious fighting tradition, is confidently defending its motherland and defending peace; we have also seen that the Polish army has achieved brilliant achievements in its military construction, and today's Polish People's Army is already an army with excellent technical equipment and a high degree of military and political quality. This strengthens our belief that the cause of peace will surely win final victory."

At the reception, the guests and hosts repeatedly rose to toast the health of Comrade Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people, and Comrade Beirut, the leader of the Polish people, the friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Poland, the unbreakable unity between the peoples of the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries and their armed forces, and the health of the soldiers, officers, and generals of the Polish and Chinese armies.

After the reception, a dance was held.

### 庆祝波兰军队建军节  波兰驻华大使和武官举行盛大招待会

1955-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝波兰军队建军节
    波兰驻华大使和武官举行盛大招待会
    新华社十二日讯　波兰驻华大使基里洛克和波兰驻华大使馆武官万采施中校在十二日晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝波兰军队建军节。
    出席招待会的，有国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，国防部副部长萧克上将、李达上将，外交部副部长姬鹏飞和其他各部、委负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领陈赓大将、萧华上将、王宏坤上将、陈士榘上将、萧向荣中将、苏静中将、刘志坚中将、常乾坤中将、邱创成中将、谭家述中将、李天焕中将和各部、各军种、兵种的将军们、军官们以及部队文艺工作者，首都科学、教育、文艺、新闻界人士。
    各国驻华使节、外交官员和各国驻华武官、代理武官、副武官以及在京访问演出的各国艺术团体负责人和代表也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的共有七百多人。
    招待会前，波兰军队歌舞团演出了精采节目。演出开始时，波兰艺术家们首先唱了波兰和中国两国国歌。
    在招待会上，基里洛克首先致词，向中国人民及其英勇的人民解放军致衷心的兄弟的敬礼。基里洛克说：同伟大的苏联、中华人民共和国和各人民民主国家有着兄弟友谊，同一切渴望自由与和平的各国人民进行合作的波兰人民共和国，在实现社会主义建设的六年计划的最后一年中，正大踏步地向前迈进，而六年计划就是人民福利的保证，独立与和平的保证。
    万采施中校在致词中说：“真诚而亲切的友谊将波兰军队和苏联、中华人民共和国和各人民民主国家的武装力量团结在一起，它们和我们在一起为巩固和平而斗争。”他代表波兰军队向今晚全体热爱和平的来宾们致以谢意，向各国驻北京的军事代表们表示感谢，并希望同他们“进行友好合作”。
    彭德怀元帅接着致词。他代表中国人民解放军向波兰军队致亲切的祝贺。彭德怀元帅谈到今年五月间他访问波兰人民军所留下的良好和深刻的印象时说：“我们看到有着光荣战斗传统的波兰军队，正满怀信心地保卫着自己的祖国，保卫着和平；也看到波兰军队在建军事业上获得了辉煌的成就，今天的波兰人民军已经是一支有着优良的技术装备和高度的军事、政治素养的军队。这使我们对和平事业必然获得最后胜利增强了信念。”
    招待会上，宾主不断起立，互为中国人民领袖毛泽东同志和波兰人民领袖贝鲁特同志的健康，为波中两国人民和军队间的友谊，为苏联、各人民民主国家的人民及其武装力量间的牢不可破的团结，为波、中两国军队的士兵们、军官们和将军们的健康干杯。
    招待会结束后，举行了舞会。
