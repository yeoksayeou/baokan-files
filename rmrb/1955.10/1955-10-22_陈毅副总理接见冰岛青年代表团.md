Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Icelandic Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 21st: Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received the Icelandic Youth Delegation, which had come to China for a visit upon invitation, on the afternoon of October 21st. Vice Premier Chen Yi had a cordial conversation with the delegation members and answered the various questions they raised. The delegation presented Vice Premier Chen Yi with a collection of paintings by a famous Icelandic painter as a souvenir.

Present at the meeting were Luo Yi, Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the China New Democratic Youth League, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见冰岛青年代表团

1955-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见冰岛青年代表团
    新华社二十一日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅在二十一日下午接见了应邀来中国访问的冰岛青年代表团。陈毅副总理和代表团人员进行了亲切的交谈，解答了他们提出的各种问题。代表团送给陈毅副总理一本冰岛著名画家的画集作为纪念。
    接见时在座的有中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记罗毅等人。
