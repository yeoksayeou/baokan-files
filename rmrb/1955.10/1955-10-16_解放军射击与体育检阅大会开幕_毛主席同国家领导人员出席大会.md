Title: PLA Shooting and Sports Review Opens; Chairman Mao and Other National Leaders Attend

Author:
Date: 1955-10-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 15th. The Chinese People's Liberation Army Shooting and Sports Review opened at 3:00 PM this afternoon at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium in Beijing.

The great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, attended today's conference. When Chairman Mao appeared on the rostrum, the audience erupted in warm applause, and more than 16,000 spectators in the stands all stood up and cheered for Chairman Mao. Also joining Chairman Mao on the rostrum were Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai.

Also attending today's conference were Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Marshal He Long, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Long Yun, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Huang Kecheng, General Tan Zheng, General Wang Shusheng, General Chen Geng, Admiral Xiao Jingguang, and General Luo Ruiqing.

Also on the rostrum were Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen, and heads of various government departments and people's organizations.

The Myanmar National Football Team, which was invited to visit our country, also attended the conference.

At 3:00 PM, Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the Conference, Senior Colonel Han Futong, announced the opening of the conference. The solemn entrance ceremony began to the majestic music of the "March of the Chinese People's Liberation Army". A team of more than 1,000 shooters and athletes marched past the rostrum in neat steps. Leading the way were the female shooters participating in the conference and the delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers invited to participate in the conference. Behind them were delegations from various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Among the selected representatives were heroic soldiers who had participated in the famous Yan'an Defense War and Huaihai Campaign, model squads and platoons who participated in the construction of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway and the Kangzang Highway, labor models from the agricultural production and construction corps in Xinjiang, and soldiers from the front lines of coastal defense in the southeast. They are all excellent units and individuals selected from various units.

Marshal He Long delivered the opening speech amidst warm applause. He said: The purpose of the conference is to review the results of shooting and physical training of the entire army in 1955, promote shooting and sports movement in the entire army in the future, enhance physical fitness, improve shooting skills, and strengthen the regularization and modernization of our national defense. Marshal He Long said: At present, our country is victoriously carrying out the great socialist construction and socialist transformation, following the general task of the country during the transition period. The glorious task of the Chinese People's Liberation Army is to resolutely defend the achievements of the people's revolution and national construction, defend the sovereignty, territorial integrity and security of the motherland; and strive to strengthen military and political training, and strive to liberate Taiwan. Marshal He Long finally called on all athletes to carry forward the glorious tradition of unity, mutual respect, and mutual learning, and strive to achieve higher scores and create higher records in various subjects.

After the opening ceremony, there were wonderful bayonet fighting, hoop rolling, pole climbing, and motorcycle performances. The Young Pioneers of the "August 1st" Elementary School also performed the "Flower Dance" and dumbbell exercises.

### 解放军射击与体育检阅大会开幕  毛主席同国家领导人员出席大会

1955-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军射击与体育检阅大会开幕
    毛主席同国家领导人员出席大会
    据新华社十五日讯　中国人民解放军射击与体育检阅大会十五日下午三时在北京先农坛体育场开幕。
    中国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东主席出席了今天的大会。当毛主席出现在主席台上时，全场发出热烈的掌声，四面看台上一万六千多名观众全体起立向毛主席欢呼。与毛主席同时登上主席台的还有朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理。
    出席今天大会的还有国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，国务院副总理邓小平，国防委员会副主席贺龙元帅、聂荣臻元帅、张治中、傅作义、龙云，以及黄克诚大将、谭政大将、王树声大将、陈赓大将、萧劲光海军大将、罗瑞卿大将等中国人民解放军高级将领。
    在主席台上还有北京市市长彭真，政府各部门、各人民团体负责人。
    应邀来我国访问的缅甸国家足球代表团也参加了大会。
    三时，大会副总指挥韩复东大校宣布大会开幕。在雄壮的“中国人民解放军进行曲”的乐声中开始了庄严的入场式。一千多人组成的射击手和运动员的队伍踏着整齐的步伐走过主席台。走在最前面的是参加大会的女射击手和应邀参加大会的中国人民志愿军代表队。他们后面是中国人民解放军各部队的代表队。代表队的选手中，有曾参加著名的延安保卫战、淮海战役的英雄战士，有参加修筑成渝铁路和康藏公路的模范班、模范排，有来自新疆的农业生产建设部队的劳动模范和来自东南沿海海防前线的战士。他们都是各部队选拔出来的优秀单位和人物。
    贺龙元帅在热烈的掌声中致开幕词。他说：大会的目的是检阅一九五五年全军射击与体育训练的成果，推动今后全军射击与体育运动，增强体质锻炼，提高射击技术，加强我军正规化现代化国防建设。贺龙元帅说：现在我国正遵循着国家在过渡时期的总任务胜利地进行着伟大的社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业。中国人民解放军的光荣任务就是要坚决保卫人民革命和国家建设的成果，保卫祖国的主权、领土完整和安全；并努力加强军事、政治训练，为解放台湾而奋斗。贺龙元帅最后号召全体运动员发扬团结一致、互相尊重、互相学习的光荣传统，在各项课目中努力争取更高的成绩和创造更高的纪录。
    在大会开幕式结束后，举行了精彩的刺杀、滚圈、爬杆和摩托车表演。“八一”小学的少年先锋队队员们还表演了“花朵舞”和哑铃操。
