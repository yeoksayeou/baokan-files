Title: Friendly Exchanges

Author:

Date: 1955-10-29
Page: 4

Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    The Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition Delegation, led by Vilmos Becsy, Chairman of the Hungarian Geological General Steering Committee, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 26th. Three other members of the delegation had arrived in Beijing earlier.
    
Finnish Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
    The Finnish Cultural Delegation, consisting of nine members and led by Deputy Head Kulo, left Beijing for home on the 26th, having visited China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
    The head of the delegation, Yrjö Ruutu, and two other representatives had already left Beijing for home on the 18th. Another representative, Tuukka, is still visiting China.

British Friends Service Council Delegation Returns Home
    The British Friends Service Council Delegation, which visited China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, returned to their country by plane via Guangzhou on the morning of the 27th. Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Liu GuanYi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Wang Zizhong, Feng Guangjie, Chen Wenrun, and Zheng Ruihua from Chinese religious circles.

British Red Cross Society Representatives Return Home
    Mrs. Raymond Ricke, Vice-Chairman of the British Red Cross Society, and Ms. Barker, Head of the Liaison Department of the British Red Cross Society, who came to China for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Chinese Red Cross Society, left Guangzhou for home on the morning of the 26th.

Belgian Business and Trade Visiting Group Returns Home
    Schuurmans, Head of the Belgian Business and Trade Visiting Group and member of the Belgian House of Representatives, and five other members of the group left Beijing to return home on the morning of the 27th. Ni Weiting, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and others went to the airport to see them off.
    Schuurmans, Head of the Belgian Business and Trade Visiting Group, issued a statement to the press before leaving Beijing.
    The statement first expressed gratitude for the hospitality of the China National Import and Export Corporation.
    The statement said: "We have completed many transactions and conducted discussions in various aspects. Facts have proven that there is great potential for trade in various products between the two countries."
    The statement then said: "There are still some obstacles on the road to comprehensive direct trade between the two countries. Once the visiting group returns to Belgium, the members will make the necessary contacts and will spare no effort to gradually eliminate these obstacles."
                            (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 友好往来

1955-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    匈牙利探矿设备展览会代表团到京
    匈牙利探矿设备展览会代表团在团长、匈牙利地质总指导委员会主席维尔摩什·贝西率领下，在二十六日下午到达北京。另有团员三人已先期到京。
    芬兰文化代表团离京回国
    应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国参观访问的芬兰文化代表团一行九人，在副团长库洛率领下二十六日离京回国
    代表团团长于尔拍和其他两位代表已先期在十八日离京回国。另一位代表图赫卡现在仍在中国访问。
    英国公谊会代表团回国
    应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的英国公谊会代表团，在二十七日晨乘飞机经广州返国。前往机场欢送的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一和中国宗教界人士王梓仲、冯光杰、陈文润、郑瑞华等多人。
    英国红十字会代表回国
    应中国红十字会邀请来中国作友好访问的英国红十字会副主席蕾蒙里克夫人和英国红十字会联络部部长巴克女士，已在二十六日早晨离开广州回国。
    比利时工商界贸易访问团回国
    比利时工商界贸易访问团团长、比利时国会下议院议员舒伦和团员五人在二十七日上午离京返国。前往机场送行的有中国进出口公司副经理倪蔚庭等人。
    比利时工商界贸易访问团团长舒伦在离京前向报界发表声明。
    声明首先对中国进出口公司的招待表示感谢。
    声明说：“我们已经完成了许多交易并就各方面进行了探讨。事实已证明，两国进行多种产品的贸易是有很大的可能性的”。
    声明接着说：“在两国全面进行直接交易的道路上，仍然存在着某些障碍，一旦访问团回到比利时后，团员们就将进行必要的接触，并将不遗余力地促使这些障碍逐步地得到消除。”
                            （据新华社讯）
