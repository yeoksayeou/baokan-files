Title: Burmese National Football Delegation Returns Home After Match in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-10-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 26th. The Burmese National Football Delegation, a group of twenty-five people, who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Sports Federation, left Guangzhou this morning to return home.

    Those who came to the station to see them off included the leaders of the Guangdong Branch of the All-China Sports Federation, the Guangdong Provincial Sports Commission, and the Guangzhou Municipal Sports Commission, as well as athlete representatives from Guangzhou.

    The delegation arrived in Guangzhou on the 24th. The delegation's football team played a friendly match with the Guangzhou Municipal Football Team on the afternoon of the 25th, with the result being a 3-3 draw. The delegation also toured Yuexiu Mountain in Guangzhou.

### 缅甸国家足球代表团在广州比赛后回国

1955-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸国家足球代表团在广州比赛后回国
    据新华社广州二十六日电　应中华全国体育总会邀请来中国访问的缅甸国家足球代表团一行二十五人，今日上午离开广州回国。
    到车站欢送的，有中华全国体育总会广东分会、广东省体育运动委员会、广州市体育运动委员会的负责人和广州市的运动员代表等。
    代表团是二十四日到达广州的，代表团的足球队在二十五日下午，曾同广州市足球队举行了一场友谊比赛，比赛结果为三比三和局。代表团在广州还游览了越秀山。
