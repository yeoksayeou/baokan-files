Title: U.S. to Hold Large-Scale Military Exercises
Author:
Date: 1955-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, October 14th, TASS dispatch: The U.S. Department of Defense announced that from November 1st to December 15th, it will conduct a large-scale army and air force exercise in Louisiana, the largest since World War II. The announcement stated that various missiles would be used during the exercise, and that it would also "practice chemical and bacteriological warfare."

### 美国将举行大规模军事演习

1955-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    美国将举行大规模军事演习
    新华社十五日讯　塔斯社华盛顿十四日讯：美国国防部宣布，从十一月一日到十二月十五日要在路易斯安那州举行一次第二次世界大战以来最大规模的陆空军演习。公告中说，在演习时要使用各种飞弹，并且还“要演习化学和细菌战”。
