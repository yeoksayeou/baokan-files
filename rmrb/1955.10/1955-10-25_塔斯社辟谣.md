Title: TASS Denies Rumors
Author:
Date: 1955-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. TASS, Moscow, October 23rd: Recently, some foreign newspapers published news about the Soviet Union's preparations to supply weapons to Israel. The source of this news was a statement made by US State Department spokesman Mr. Sudanm at a press conference on October 12th, in which he said that the US State Department "has obtained intelligence that the Soviet bloc has taken steps to supply weapons to Israel."

TASS is authorized to declare: These reports are complete fabrications, the purpose of which is obviously to mislead world public opinion.

### 塔斯社辟谣

1955-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    塔斯社辟谣
    新华社二十四日讯　塔斯社莫斯科二十三日讯：最近外国报纸出现了一些消息，就苏联准备向以色列供给武器。这些消息的来源就是美国国务院发言人苏丹姆十月十二日在记者招待会上发表的谈话，他说，美国国务院“获得情报：苏联集团采取了向以色列供给武器的步骤”。
    塔斯社受权声明：这些消息是彻头彻尾的捏造，其目的显然是要迷惑世界舆论。
