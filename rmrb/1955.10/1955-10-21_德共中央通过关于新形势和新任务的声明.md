Title: German Communist Party Central Committee Passes Declaration on New Situation and New Tasks
Author:
Date: 1955-10-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 20th: According to Deutsche Presse-Agentur Dusseldorf: The Central Committee of the German Communist Party held its 21st meeting on October 15th and 16th. After discussing the report "The New Situation and New Tasks of the German Communist Party" made by Lehmann, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the German Communist Party, the meeting passed a declaration on "The New Situation and New Tasks in West Germany."
    The declaration presented the German Communist Party's views to the West German working class and all working people on the path to establishing a pan-European collective security system to achieve lasting peace and on establishing a new democratic order in West Germany. The declaration pointed out that establishing such a democratic order is a necessary prerequisite for restoring German unity into a democratic and peace-loving country.
    The Central Committee of the German Communist Party also issued a call to the German residents of the Saar region, urging them to vote against the Saar Statute.

### 德共中央通过关于新形势和新任务的声明

1955-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    德共中央通过关于新形势和新任务的声明
    新华社柏林二十日电　据德意志通讯社杜塞尔多夫消息：德国共产党中央委员会在十月十五日和十六日举行第二十一次会议。会议在讨论了德国共产党中央委员会第一书记雷曼所作的“新形势和德国共产党的新任务”的报告以后，通过了关于“西德的新形势和新任务”的声明。
    这个声明向西德工人阶级和一切劳动人民提出了德国共产党对建立全欧集体安全体系以获致持久和平的道路和在西德建立新的民主秩序的意见。声明指出，建立这种民主秩序是恢复德国统一成为一个民主和爱好和平国家的必要的先决条件。
    德国共产党中央委员会还向萨尔区德国居民发出号召，要他们投票反对萨尔法规。
