Title: Nenni Returns to Rome After Visiting China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-10-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 20th - Rome dispatch: After visiting China and the Soviet Union, the General Secretary of the Italian Socialist Party, Nenni, returned to Rome by plane on the 20th.
    
    Nenni emphasized in a conversation with reporters at the airport that the Soviet Union is determined to continue implementing its policy of easing international tensions.
    
    Nenni also said that delaying the recognition of New China would be a serious mistake. He believes that China is a good market for Italian industry.

### 南尼访问中国和苏联后回到罗马

1955-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    南尼访问中国和苏联后回到罗马
    新华社布拉格二十日电　罗马消息：意大利社会党总书记南尼在到中国和苏联访问以后已在二十日乘飞机回到罗马。
    南尼在机场上对记者发表谈话时着重指出，苏联决心继续执行它的缓和国际紧张局势的政策。
    南尼又说，延迟承认新中国，将是一个严重的错误。他认为中国是意大利工业的好市场。
