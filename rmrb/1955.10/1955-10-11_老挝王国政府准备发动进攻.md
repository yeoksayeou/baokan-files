Title: Lao Royal Government Prepares to Launch Offensive
Author:
Date: 1955-10-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 10th: According to a Lao dispatch from the Vietnam News Agency on the 10th, the Lao Royal Government is preparing to launch a large-scale offensive against the Pathet Lao Fighting Units. The Royal authorities recently dispatched a battalion to attack and occupy Ban Than, Ban Be, and Na Xam, and also sent a battalion to occupy Phaka near Muong Bun. At the same time, another unit of the Lao Royal Army invaded Muong Sang, 25 kilometers from Sam Neua City, from Hoi Ya. The Royal authorities are now also transferring another battalion to Sam Neua.

### 老挝王国政府准备发动进攻

1955-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝王国政府准备发动进攻
    新华社河内十日电　据越南通讯社十日老挝消息：老挝王国政府正在准备对寮国战斗部队发动大规模的进攻。王国当局最近派遣了一个营来进攻和占领班探、班别和那萨蒙，并且还派遣了一个营来占领芒奔附近的巴卡。同时，老挝王国军队的另一支部队从会雅侵入了距桑怒城二十五公里的孟桑。王国当局现在还正在调遣另一个营赶赴桑怒。
