Title: Burmese National Football Team Arrives in Beijing; Vice Chairman Rong Gaotang of the All-China Sports Federation Hosts Banquet
Author:
Date: 1955-10-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 14th—The Burmese National Football Delegation, consisting of twenty-five members, arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 14th, invited by the All-China Sports Federation for a visit to China. They were warmly welcomed at the station by Chinese sports workers and football players.

The delegation is headed by Situ Subartle, Vice Chairman of the Burmese Football Promotion Committee, with Professor Guo Ge Li, Vice Chairman of the Burmese Amateur Football Association, as the leader of the football team. The observer for the delegation is Wu Wenpei, Chairman of the Burmese Sports Association. The delegation's football team will hold friendly matches with Chinese football teams.

Representatives from the All-China Sports Federation, including Vice Chairman Rong Gaotang, as well as representatives from the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, the Beijing Branch of the All-China Sports Federation, and the Beijing Physical Culture and Sports Commission, were present at the station to welcome them.

Personnel from the Burmese Embassy in China also welcomed the delegation at the station.

Xinhua News Agency, October 14th—Vice Chairman Rong Gaotang of the All-China Sports Federation hosted a banquet on the evening of the 14th to welcome the Burmese National Football Delegation.

Wu Lamo, the Burmese Ambassador to China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Also present at the banquet tonight were Xiao Hua and Dong Shouyi, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Sports Federation, responsible persons and representatives from the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, the Beijing Physical Culture and Sports Commission, the Beijing Branch of the All-China Sports Federation, and the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, as well as figures from the capital's sports circles and well-known athletes and referees.

Rong Gaotang delivered a speech at the banquet, extending a warm welcome to the Burmese guests for their visit to China. He said: The Burmese National Football Delegation's visit to China at our invitation is a further development of cultural exchange between China and Burma and a good start to sports exchange between the two countries. He also said: Burmese football sports have a high level, and through this visit of the delegation, we will not only have the opportunity to observe and learn the outstanding techniques of the Burmese football team, but also make an important contribution to promoting understanding and friendship between the peoples and athletes of China and Burma.

Subsequently, Situ Subartle, head of the Burmese National Football Delegation, delivered a speech expressing his gratitude for the host's hospitality. He said: China and Burma have had a profound friendship since ancient times, and there have been new developments recently. The delegation hopes that this visit to China will further promote and strengthen the development of this friendship.

During the banquet, guests frequently toasted the friendship between the peoples and athletes of Burma and China, the growing development of sports in both countries, and the lasting peace of the Far East and the world.

### 缅甸国家足球代表团到达北京  我全国体育总会副主席荣高棠设宴欢迎

1955-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸国家足球代表团到达北京
    我全国体育总会副主席荣高棠设宴欢迎
    新华社十四日讯　应中华全国体育总会邀请来中国访问的缅甸国家足球代表团一行二十五人，十四日晨乘火车到达北京。他们在车站上受到中国体育工作者和足球运动员们的热烈欢迎。
    代表团由缅甸足球促进委员会副主席西图·苏巴特勒任团长，由缅甸业余足球联合会副主席郭哥礼教授任足球队领队。代表团的观察员是缅甸体育协会主席吴温佩。代表团的足球队将同中国的足球队举行友谊比赛。
    今天到车站欢迎的有中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠和中华人民共和国体育运动委员会、中缅友好协会、中华全国体育总会北京市分会、北京市体育运动委员会等单位的代表。
    缅甸驻中国大使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
    新华社十四日讯　中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠在十四日晚举行宴会，欢迎缅甸国家足球代表团。
    缅甸联邦驻华大使吴拉茂也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席今晚宴会的，有中华全国体育总会副主席萧华、董守义，中华人民共和国体育运动委员会、北京市体育运动委员会、中华全国体育总会北京市分会和中缅友好协会的负责人和代表，还有首都体育界人士和著名的运动员、裁判员。
    荣高棠在宴会上首先致词，对缅甸客人来中国访问表示热烈欢迎。他说：缅甸国家足球代表团应邀来访问中国，是中缅两国文化交流的进一步发展，也是两国体育交流的良好开端。他又说：缅甸足球运动具有高度的水平，通过代表团这次访问，不仅使我们得有机会观摩与学习缅甸足球队的卓越技术，而且对促进中缅两国人民与运动员之间的了解和友谊会有重要贡献。
    接着，缅甸国家足球代表团团长西图·苏巴特勒致词，对主人的盛情招待表示感谢。他说：缅中两国自古就有深厚的友谊，最近又有了新的发展，代表团这次来到中国，也希望能进一步促进和加强这种友谊的发展。
    席间，宾主频频为缅中两国人民和运动员间的友谊，为两国体育运动的日益发展，为远东和世界的持久和平而干杯。
