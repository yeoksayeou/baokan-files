Title: Establishment of the Academic Committee of the Petroleum Institute of the Chinese Academy of Sciences
Author:
Date: 1955-10-20
Page: 3

To implement the resolutions and spirit of the Inaugural Meeting of the Chinese Academy of Sciences' departments, the Petroleum Institute of the Chinese Academy of Sciences recently held a meeting in Lüda City and formally established its Academic Committee. This is the first Academic Committee established among the forty-one existing research institutions of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

The Academic Committee of the Petroleum Institute consists of nineteen scientists with achievements in petroleum science, including engineers and experts from industrial departments, professors from universities, and senior researchers from the Petroleum Institute.

Regarding the development direction of the Petroleum Institute, the meeting determined that, for a certain period, the focus should be on research that supports the establishment of petroleum industrial bases and meets the needs of national defense and industrial products. Simultaneously, basic theoretical research should be appropriately and actively carried out in conjunction with the above work, cultivating scientific research cadres, and actively creating conditions for future research into fundamental scientific theories and solving key scientific problems of great significance to the national economy. The meeting discussed and formulated a long-term plan and a draft outline of the 1956 and 1957 plans for research work on natural petroleum, shale oil, and coal liquefaction by the Petroleum Institute, and reviewed nine research work reports and papers submitted by the Petroleum Institute on fluidized synthesis, petroleum evaluation, crude oil desalting, and chromatographic analysis.

The meeting resolution pointed out that the Academic Committee should, in its future work, implement the future tasks and development direction of the Petroleum Institute, systematically organize and lead academic discussions to ensure the healthy development of scientific work, and should make the greatest efforts to cultivate new forces and actively leverage the petroleum research capabilities of all parties to develop China's petroleum science.

The meeting adopted the key work points of the Academic Committee for the fourth quarter of this year.

### 中国科学院石油研究所学术委员会成立

1955-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

    中国科学院石油研究所学术委员会成立
    中国科学院石油研究所为了贯彻执行中国科学院学部成立大会的决议和精神，最近在旅大市召开会议正式成立了学术委员会。这是中国科学院已有的四十一个研究机构中最先成立的学术委员会。
    石油研究所学术委员会由在石油科学方面著有成就的十九位科学家组成，包括产业部门的工程师、专家，高等学校的教授，以及石油研究所的高级研究人员。
  关于石油研究所的发展方向，会议确定为：在一定时期内把力量重点放在配合石油工业基地的建立，以及满足国防上、工业上产品需要等方面的研究，同时结合上述工作适当地、积极地开展基础理论研究，培养科学研究干部，为今后研究基本的科学理论和解决对于国民经济具有重要意义的关键性科学问题积极创造条件。会议讨论制定了石油研究所关于天然石油、页油岩、煤炼油等方面研究工作的长远规划和一九五六、一九五七年的计划纲要草案，并且审议了石油研究所所提出的流体化合成、石油评价、原油脱盐、色层分析等九篇研究工作报告和论文。
    会议决议中指出，学术委员会要在今后工作中贯彻执行石油研究所今后任务和发展方向，要有计划地组织和领导开展学术讨论，以保证科学工作的健康发展，并且应该用最大努力来培养新生力量，积极发挥各方面的石油研究力量，以发展我国的石油科学事业。
    会议通过了学术委员会今年第四季度的工作要点。
