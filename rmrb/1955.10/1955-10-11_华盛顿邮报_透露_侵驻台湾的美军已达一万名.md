Title: "Washington Post" Reveals: 10,000 US Troops Stationed in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1955-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, October 10th: A column by Pearson published in the October 7th "Washington Post" reveals that "10,000 American troops are stationed in Taiwan, of which 1,500 belong to the U.S. military mission, and the rest are air force personnel."

The article also revealed that the United States not only has a large number of troops stationed in Taiwan but is also strengthening its military deployments in the vicinity of Taiwan. The article said that the United States' "atomic air force" has been deployed near Taiwan.

### “华盛顿邮报”透露  侵驻台湾的美军已达一万名

1955-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    “华盛顿邮报”透露
    侵驻台湾的美军已达一万名
    新华社十日讯　华盛顿消息：据十月七日的“华盛顿邮报”刊载皮尔逊的专栏文章透露，“有一万名美国军队驻在台湾，其中一千五百名属于美国军事代表团，其余的是空军人员。”
    文章还透露，美国不但在台湾驻有大量军队，并且在台湾附近地区加强军事部署。文章说，美国的“原子空军”已经部署在台湾附近。
