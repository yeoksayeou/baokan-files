Title: Chinese and Japanese Business Delegations Sign Trade Contracts
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency

    Chinese and Japanese Business Delegations Sign Trade Contracts
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Japanese business delegation headed by Masao Tajima ended its visit to Beijing and left Beijing by train at 10:10 p.m. on the 15th to visit Shenyang and other places.
    Since arriving in Beijing on September 22, the delegation, together with the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and China's import and export corporations, has concluded more than 290 specific import and export transaction contracts after more than 20 days of negotiations, with a total import and export value of more than 12 million pounds. Both parties also signed a protocol on the balance of the above-mentioned specific transactions, based on the principle of exchange of similar materials and balanced import and export. In addition, a protocol for Category C commodity transactions with a total import and export value of 2 million pounds within three months was signed. The signing ceremony for these two protocols was held in Beijing at 8:30 p.m. on the 15th. Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, signed the protocol on behalf of the Chinese side; Masao Tajima, head of the Japanese business delegation and Vice Chairman of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, signed the protocol on behalf of the Japanese side.
    Before the delegation's departure, Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a farewell banquet.
    During their stay in Beijing, the delegation held symposiums with responsible persons from relevant departments on China's Five-Year Economic Construction Plan, chemical industry, iron and steel hardware industry, machine manufacturing industry, shipbuilding and transportation industry, as well as agriculture and grain issues. The delegation also visited factories, schools, and hospitals in Beijing, and toured the capital's scenic spots and historical sites.

### 我国和日本工商界代表团签订交易合同

1955-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    我国和日本工商界代表团签订交易合同
    据新华社讯　以田岛正雄为首的日本工商界访华代表团结束了在北京的访问后，已在十五日晚十时十分乘火车离开北京去沈阳等地访问参观。
    代表团自九月二十二日到达北京后，同中国国际贸易促进委员会和中国各进出口公司，经过二十多天的谈判；代表团各代表分别同中国各进出口公司签订了二百九十个进出口具体交易合同，进出口总金额达一千二百多万英镑。双方并就上述具体交易的差额，根据同类物资相易、进出口平衡原则签订了议定书。此外，并签订了在三个月内进出口总额二百万英镑的丙类商品交易议定书。这两个议定书的签字仪式，于十五日晚八时半在北京举行。代表中国方面在议定书上签字的，是中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民；代表日本方面在议定书上签字的，是日本工商界访华代表团团长、日本国际贸易促进协会副会长田岛正雄。
    代表团临行以前，中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸曾举行宴会欢送。
    在北京期间，代表团分别和有关各部门的负责人举行了关于中国五年经济建设计划、化学工业、钢铁五金工业、机器制造业、造船运输业以及农业和粮食等问题的座谈会。代表团还在北京参观了工厂、学校和医院，游览了首都的名胜古迹。
