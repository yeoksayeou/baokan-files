Title: China and Myanmar Reach Agreement on Postal and Telecommunications Services

Author: 
Date: 1955-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The Burmese postal and telecommunications delegation of three people, headed by U Myint Aung, Deputy Secretary of the Ministry of Transport and Communications of the Union of Burma, left Beijing by train on the morning of the 27th to return home after completing their negotiation tasks. On their way home, the delegation will visit Nanjing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou and other places.

Those who saw them off at the station included Wang Zigang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China, and others.

U Maung Maung Toe, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of Burma in China, and others also saw them off at the station.

Before the delegation left Beijing, Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, hosted a farewell banquet.

During its stay in Beijing, the delegation held friendly negotiations with the Chinese postal and telecommunications delegation on postal and telecommunications issues between China and Myanmar, and an agreement was successfully reached. The two sides jointly drafted three draft agreements on China-Myanmar postal, telecommunications and postal parcel services. These three draft agreements will be approved by the two governments and formally signed at a later date.

### 中缅两国达成关于通邮通电的协议

1955-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅两国达成关于通邮通电的协议
    新华社二十九日讯　以缅甸联邦运输和交通部副秘书吴梅昂为首的缅甸邮电代表团一行三人，在完成谈判任务后，二十七日晨乘火车离北京回国。代表团在归国途中将到南京、上海、武汉、广州等地参观。
    到车站欢送的有中华人民共和国邮电部副部长王子纲等。
    缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办吴茂茂铎等也到车站欢送。
    在代表团离京前，邮电部部长朱学范曾设宴欢送。
    代表团在京期间，曾同我国邮电代表团就中缅两国间通邮通电问题进行了友好的谈判，并已顺利达成协议。双方共同拟订了中缅邮政、电信和邮政包裹三个协定草案。这三个协定草案将由两国政府核准后，另行约期正式签订。
