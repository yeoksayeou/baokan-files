Title: The First Session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province Closes: Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Declared Established
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-02
Page: 2

    The First Session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province successfully concluded on the 30th after electing the members of the People's Committee of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region. The Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region of the People's Republic of China was simultaneously declared established.

    The meeting, based on the resolution unanimously adopted by all representatives on the 29th regarding supporting the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress to establish the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region and abolish the Xinjiang Province establishment, elected Saifuddin (Uyghur) as the Chairman of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, Gao Jinchun (Han), Iminov (Uyghur), and Patikhan Sugurbayev (Kazak) as Vice-Chairmen, and A. Aysha (Uyghur), Burhan (Uyghur), Jakulin (Kazak), Tao Zhiyue (Han), Ma Zhongcai (Hui), and thirty-two others as members of the Autonomous Region People's Committee through secret ballot.

    The meeting also elected Abulizi Maikhanmaiti (Uyghur) as the President of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Higher People's Court; and elected the presidents of the Intermediate People's Courts in Urumqi and six other regions. When the election results were announced, the band played solemn music, and the hall erupted in thunderous applause. Dozens of literary and art workers and Young Pioneers of all nationalities presented bouquets of flowers to all the elected officials.

    The meeting also heard and approved the report on the examination of proposals and the report on the examination of the budget. It adopted important resolutions on the work report of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government over the past six years, and the report on the first Five-Year Plan for the development of the national economy in Xinjiang. In addition, it also adopted telegrams of respect to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and the State Council.

    At the meeting, the Dalai Lama, represented by Taiji Gasha Qoechyi Nyima, and the Panchen Erdeni, represented by Kanqoin Lobsang Thinley, as well as representatives of various ethnic groups from various regions, delivered congratulatory messages one after another, warmly congratulating the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region.

    After the congress presented gifts to the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao, and presented gifts to representatives of various ethnic groups from various regions, Saifuddin delivered the closing speech. He said: It is only under the leadership of the Communist Party of China that it is possible for us to establish the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region. We must resolutely implement the instructions given to us by Dong Biwu, the representative of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central People's Government, and unite the people of all ethnic groups to advance forever on the basis of the common aspiration and common ideology of building a socialist society. Saifuddin also expressed his gratitude to the representatives from various ethnic autonomous regions and provinces of the motherland, and through them, extended friendly greetings to the people of all ethnic groups in various regions.

    That evening, Saifuddin, Chairman of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, held a grand banquet to celebrate the 6th National Day and the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议闭幕  新疆维吾尔自治区宣告成立

1955-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议闭幕
    新疆维吾尔自治区宣告成立
    新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议，在三十日选出了新疆维吾尔自治区人民委员会组成人员后胜利闭幕。中华人民共和国新疆维吾尔自治区同时宣告成立。
    会议根据二十九日全体代表一致通过的关于拥护全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于成立新疆维吾尔自治区、撤销新疆省建制的决议的决议，以无记名投票方式进行选举，选出了赛福鼎（维吾尔族）为新疆维吾尔自治区主席，高锦纯（汉族）、伊敏诺夫（维吾尔族）、帕提汉·苏古尔巴也夫（哈萨克族）为副主席，阿·艾沙（维吾尔族）、鲍尔汉（维吾尔族）、贾库林（哈萨克族）、陶峙岳（汉族）、马仲才（回族）等三十七人为自治区人民委员会委员。   
   会议还选出了阿不力孜·买汉买提（维吾尔族）为新疆维吾尔自治区高级人民法院院长；并选出了乌鲁木齐等七个地区的中级人民法院院长。当选举结果宣布的时候，乐队奏起了庄严的乐曲，全场响起了暴风雨般的掌声，几十名文艺工作者和各民族少年先锋队员，把一束束鲜花献给了当选的全体人员。
    会议还听取和通过了关于提案审查报告和预算审查报告。通过了关于新疆省人民政府六年来的工作报告、新疆发展国民经济的第一个五年计划的报告等重要决议。此外，还通过了向中国共产党中央委员会和毛主席，全国人民代表大会常务委员会、国务院的致敬电文。
    会上，达赖喇嘛代表台吉噶雪·却吉尼玛和班禅额尔德尼代表堪穷洛桑参勒，以及各地的各民族代表相继在会上致贺词，热烈祝贺新疆维吾尔自治区的成立。
    大会向中央人民政府和毛主席献礼，向各地的各民族代表赠礼以后，由赛福鼎致闭幕词。他说：我们只有在中国共产党领导下，才有可能成立新疆维吾尔自治区。我们必须坚决执行中国共产党中央委员会和中央人民政府代表董必武给我们的指示，团结各族人民在建设社会主义社会这种共同愿望和共同思想的基础上永远前进。赛福鼎并对来自祖国各民族自治区、各省的代表表示谢意，通过他们向各地区的各民族人民致友好的敬礼。
    这天晚上，新疆维吾尔自治区主席赛福鼎举行盛大宴会，欢庆第六届国庆节和新疆维吾尔自治区成立。　（新华社）
