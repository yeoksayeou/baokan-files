Title: Yunnan and Other Provinces' Ethnic Visiting Groups Leave Beijing to Visit Various Places
Author:
Date: 1955-10-17
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    The Yunnan Provincial Ethnic Visiting Group, the Sichuan Provincial Yi Ethnic Visiting Group, and the Guizhou Provincial Ethnic Visiting Group have left Beijing on the 12th, 14th, and 15th, respectively, to visit the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region in Guangxi Province and Wuhan, the Northeast, and other places.

    During their stay in Beijing, the three visiting groups visited the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, the Guanting Reservoir, the Central Institute for Nationalities, Tsinghua University, and agricultural producers' cooperatives in the suburbs of Beijing, and toured famous historical sites such as the Palace Museum.

### 云南等省民族参观团离京去各地参观

1955-10-17
第2版()
专栏：

    云南等省民族参观团离京去各地参观
    云南省各民族参观团、四川省彝族参观团和贵州省各民族参观团，已在十二日、十四日和十五日离开北京，分别前往广西省桂西僮族自治区和武汉、东北等地进行参观。
    三个参观团在京期间，参观了石景山钢铁厂，官厅水库，中央民族学院，清华大学和京郊的农业生产合作社，并游览了故宫等名胜古迹。
                                    （新华社）
