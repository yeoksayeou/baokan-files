Title: Laotian Royal Government Unilaterally Decides on Election Procedures
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 28th: Laotian news: The Laotian Royal Ministry of Interior recently re-announced that general elections will be held on December 25th this year, and stipulated that the list of candidates should be submitted on October 11th, voter registration should end on November 10th, and the election campaign should begin on November 15th.
    At the same time, in the political conferences, the Laotian Royal Government still refused to consider the supplementary opinions proposed by the Lao Fighting Units to make the election law conform to the spirit of the Geneva Accords.

### 老挝王国政府片面决定普选程序

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝王国政府片面决定普选程序
    新华社河内二十八日电　老挝消息：老挝王国内政部最近重新宣布将在今年十二月二十五日举行普选，并且规定在十月十一日提出候选人名单，十一月十日结束选民登记，十一月十五日开始竞选运动。
    同时，在政治会议上，老挝王国政府方面仍旧不肯考虑寮国战斗部队方面所提出的使选举法符合于日内瓦协定精神的补充意见。
