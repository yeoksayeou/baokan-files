Title: Chinese Youth Football Team Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-10-11
Page: 4

    The Chinese Youth Football Team, invited by the Soviet Union's Council of Ministers' Committee on Physical Culture and Sports, and led by team leader Ke Lun, arrived in Kiev, the capital of the Ukrainian Republic, on the 7th from Budapest, the capital of the People's Republic of Hungary.
    On October 9th, the Chinese Youth Football Team played a friendly match against the Kiev Combined Football Team at the Khrushchev Stadium in Kiev. The Kiev Combined Team won with a score of 4-1.
                              (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 我国青年足球队访问苏联

1955-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我国青年足球队访问苏联
    应苏联部长会议体育运动委员会的邀请访问苏联的中国青年足球队，在领队柯轮率领下，已经在七日从匈牙利人民共和国首都布达佩斯到达乌克兰共和国首都基辅。
    十月九日，中国青年足球队同基辅混合足球队在基辅的赫鲁晓夫运动场举行了友谊赛。比赛结果，基辅混合队以四比一获胜。
                              （据新华社讯）
