Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1955-10-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, October 14th: The Sino-US ambassadorial talks held their twenty-first meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 AM this morning. The meeting adjourned at 11:40 AM. The next meeting is scheduled for 10:00 AM on Thursday, October 20th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1955-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦十四日电　中美两国大使级会谈十四日上午十时在国联大厦举行第二十一次会议。会议在十一时四十分休会，下次会议定于十月二十日（星期四）上午十时举行。
