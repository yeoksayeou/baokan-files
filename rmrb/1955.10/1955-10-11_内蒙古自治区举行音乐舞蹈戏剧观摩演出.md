Title: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Holds Music, Dance, and Drama Festival
Author:
Date: 1955-10-11
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency

    The first Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region National Folk Music, Dance, and Drama Festival opened in Hohhot on the 1st. The participating representatives include 860 people from eight ethnic groups: Mongolian, Han, Hui, Manchu, Korean, Solon, Oroqen, and Daur, including professional actors, workers, farmers, herdsmen, and housewives engaged in amateur artistic activities. Among these performers are old artists who have been playing the Yataga (zither) for forty years, a 75-year-old Mongolian woman who is good at female solo singing, and a seven-year-old child playing the Taisho Koto.
    This festival will run until the 20th, with more than 250 programs, including songs, dances, instrumental performances, and sixteen types of dramas. The programs include Mongolian storytelling about the Mongolian national hero "Gada Meilin", the Matouqin solo piece "Zhaomei" that has been popular on the grasslands for more than a century, the "Oroqen Hunter Dance" from the Greater Khingan Mountains forest area, as well as the Mongolian drama "A New Beginning" and the Hui opera "A Well". Six regions, Beijing, Tianjin, Shanxi, Gansu, Wuhan, and Heilongjiang, have sent observation groups to participate in this festival.
                            (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 内蒙古自治区举行音乐舞蹈戏剧观摩演出

1955-10-11
第3版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区举行音乐舞蹈戏剧观摩演出
    内蒙古自治区第一届民族民间音乐舞蹈戏剧观摩演出大会一日在呼和浩特开幕。参加会演的代表包括蒙古、汉、回、满、朝鲜、索伦、鄂伦春、达呼尔八个民族共八百六十多人，其中有职业演员，也有从事业余文艺活动的工人、农民、牧民和家庭妇女。这些表演的人中有弹了四十年牙塔嘎（古筝）的老艺人，擅长女声独唱的七十五岁蒙古族妇女和弹大正琴的七岁儿童。
    这个观摩演出大会要演到二十日，共有二百五十多个节目，包括歌曲、舞蹈、乐器演奏和戏剧等十六个剧种。节目中有歌颂蒙古民族英雄“嘎达梅林”的蒙古说书、流行在草原上百年以上的马头琴独奏曲“召梅”、大兴安岭林区的“鄂伦春猎人舞”，还有蒙古话剧“新的开端”和回族歌剧“一眼井”等。北京、天津、山西、甘肃、武汉、黑龙江六个地区都派有观摩组前来参加这个演出大会。
                            （据新华社讯）
