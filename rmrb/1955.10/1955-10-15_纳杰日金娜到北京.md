Title: Nadezhdina Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: Nadezhda Nadezhdina, artistic director of the Soviet Moscow "Beryozka" Dance Ensemble, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 14th. Xia Yan, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and others welcomed her at the station.

Nadezhdina will leave Beijing for Guangzhou on the 15th to preside over the opening performance of the dance ensemble in Guangzhou.

### 纳杰日金娜到北京

1955-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    纳杰日金娜到北京
    据新华社十四日讯　苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团艺术领导人纳杰日达·纳杰日金娜在十四日晚乘火车到达北京。中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍等到车站欢迎。
    纳杰日金娜将在十五日从北京去广州，主持舞蹈团在广州的开幕演出。
