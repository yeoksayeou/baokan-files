Title: Soviet Union Abolishes the Post of Soviet High Commissioner to Austria
Author:
Date: 1955-10-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 4th. TASS reports: In view of the fact that the State Treaty on the Re-establishment of an Independent and Democratic Austria has come into effect and the withdrawal of Soviet troops from Austria has been completed, the Soviet Council of Ministers has decided to abolish the post of Soviet High Commissioner to Austria as of October 1, 1955.

### 苏联撤销驻奥高级专员

1955-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联撤销驻奥高级专员
    新华社四日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：鉴于关于重新建立一个独立和民主的奥地利的国家条约业已生效和苏联军队业已从奥地利撤退完毕，苏联部长会议决定从一九五五年十月一日起撤销苏联驻奥地利高级专员的职位。
