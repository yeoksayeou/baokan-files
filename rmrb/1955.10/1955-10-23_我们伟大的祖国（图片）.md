    Our Great Motherland
    A new high tide of the agricultural cooperation movement in the suburbs of Beijing has arrived. The Guanyintang Township Agricultural Producers' Cooperative in the eastern suburbs has expanded from the original 332 households to 543 households. This is a scene of new and old members jointly picking cotton in the cooperative's cotton fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京市郊区农业合作化运动的新的高潮已经到来。东郊区观音堂乡农业生产合作社已由原来的三百三十二户扩大到五百四十三户了。这是新社员和老社员共同在合作社的棉田里收摘棉花的情形。
