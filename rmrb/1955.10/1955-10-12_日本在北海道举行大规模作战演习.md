Title: Japan Holds Large-Scale Combat Exercises in Hokkaido
Author:
Date: 1955-10-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 11th: Japanese land, sea, and air forces began a four-day large-scale combat exercise in Hokkaido on the morning of the 10th.

	This comprehensive combat exercise is the largest since the war and another manifestation of the Japanese government's active attempts to reorganize its military. The exercise involves 18,000 troops, 2,500 tanks and military vehicles, more than 50 aircraft, and more than 40 warships. The U.S. Air Force stationed in Japan also participated in the exercise. The U.S. "Military Assistance Advisory Group" stationed in Japan also attended and observed the exercise.
    
Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 11th: The U.S. Atomic Energy Commission announced on the 10th that it will conduct a series of nuclear weapons tests in Nevada starting around November 1st. According to International News Service, this series of tests will continue until December.

### 日本在北海道举行大规模作战演习

1955-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    日本在北海道举行大规模作战演习
    新华社十一日讯　东京消息：日本陆、海、空军从十日早晨在北海道开始举行为期四天的大规模作战演习。
    这次综合作战演习是战后规模最大的一次，是日本政府积极图谋重整军备的又一个表现。参加演习的部队有一万八千人，坦克和军用车共两千五百辆，飞机五十多架，舰艇四十多艘。驻日美国空军也参加了这次演习。美国驻日“军事援助顾问团”并且出席观看演习。
    新华社十一日讯　华盛顿消息：美国政府原子能委员会十日宣布，它将从十一月一日左右开始在内华达州进行一系列的核子武器试验。据国际新闻社报道，这一系列的试验将继续进行到十二月。
