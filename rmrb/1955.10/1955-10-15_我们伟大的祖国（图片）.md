    Our Great Motherland
    The Guanting Hydroelectric Power Station, which is under construction on the Yongding River in Hebei Province, will soon be generating electricity. More than 99 percent of the earth and stone work here has been completed, and other projects are also progressing rapidly. Soon, powerful currents will be supplied from here to meet the needs of urban lighting and industrial power in Beijing and other cities. On the construction site of the power station's factory building, tower cranes are hoisting heavy equipment.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    河北省永定河上正在建设中的官厅水力发电站，发电的日子快要来到了。这里的土方石方工程已有百分之九十九以上完成，其他各项工程也在迅速进展中，不久以后，强大的电流就将要从这里供应北京等地城市照明和工业用电的需要。在发电站的厂房工地上，塔式起重机正在吊运重型器材。
