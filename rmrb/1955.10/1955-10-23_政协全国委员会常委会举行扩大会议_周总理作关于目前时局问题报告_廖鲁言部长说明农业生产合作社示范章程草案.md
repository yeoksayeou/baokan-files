Title: CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting; Premier Zhou Makes Report on Current Situation; Minister Liao Luyan Explains Draft Model Charter for Agricultural Production Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1955-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd—The Standing Committee of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held its seventh enlarged meeting on the afternoon of the 22nd.

At the meeting, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China and Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, made an important report on the current situation; Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, gave an explanatory report on the draft of the "Model Charter for Agricultural Production Cooperatives."

The National Committee of the CPPCC decided to start group discussions on these two reports from next Monday.

### 政协全国委员会常委会举行扩大会议  周总理作关于目前时局问题报告  廖鲁言部长说明农业生产合作社示范章程草案

1955-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常委会举行扩大会议
    周总理作关于目前时局问题报告
    廖鲁言部长说明农业生产合作社示范章程草案
    新华社二十二日讯　中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会常务委员会，在二十二日下午举行第七次扩大会议。
    会上，中华人民共和国国务院总理、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席周恩来作了关于目前时局问题的重要报告；中华人民共和国农业部部长廖鲁言就“农业生产合作社示范章程”的草案作了说明报告。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会决定从下星期一开始分组座谈这两个报告。
