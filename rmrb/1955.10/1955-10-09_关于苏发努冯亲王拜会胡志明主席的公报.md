Title: Communiqué on Prince Souphanouvong's Visit to Chairman Ho Chi Minh
Author:
Date: 1955-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th. The Office of the President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a communiqué on the 8th regarding Prince Souphanouvong's visit to Chairman Ho Chi Minh. The communiqué stated:

　　Prince Souphanouvong paid a visit to Chairman Ho Chi Minh while passing through Hanoi.

　　When Prince Souphanouvong spoke about the problems that still need to be resolved in Laos, Chairman Ho Chi Minh expressed his hope that these problems could be resolved in accordance with the Geneva Accords and the rights of the Lao people.

　　Chairman Ho Chi Minh emphasized that the resolution of the Laotian issue requires the sincerity and efforts of both the Royal Lao Government and the Pathet Lao Fighting Units, and that a peaceful solution should be reached through consultation.

　　Chairman Ho Chi Minh expressed his hope that the upcoming negotiations between Prince Souphanouvong and Prime Minister Katay Don Sasorith would yield good results.

### 关于苏发努冯亲王拜会胡志明主席的公报

1955-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    关于苏发努冯亲王拜会胡志明主席的公报
    新华社河内八日电　越南民主共和国主席府办公厅八日发表关于苏发努冯亲王拜会胡志明主席的公报，公报说：
    苏发努冯亲王在途经河内时曾拜会了胡志明主席。
    在苏发努冯亲王谈到有关目前老挝尚需解决的问题时，胡志明主席表示希望这些问题能够符合日内瓦协议和老挝人民的权利予以解决。
    胡志明主席强调指出，老挝问题的解决需要老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队双方的诚意和努力，要由协商来达成和平解决的办法。
    胡志明主席表示希望苏发努冯亲王和克特首相即将举行的谈判将带来良好的结果。
