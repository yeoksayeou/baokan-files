Title: Prime Minister Nehru Emphasizes India Must Be Economically Independent
Author:
Date: 1955-10-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 18th: According to Delhi newspapers, Indian Prime Minister Nehru, speaking at a student gathering in Calcutta on the 16th, said that if a country, after independence, is so economically dependent on others that other countries can stifle it and force it to do certain things, then there can be no true independence.

Nehru said that there are many such countries in Asia and Europe that are politically independent, but are completely under the economic power of other countries. He said, "If India is to be truly independent and move forward, it must be economically independent."

He pointed out that every country is bound to rely on other countries to some extent, because no country can be absolutely 100% self-sufficient, "but the question is that we should be independent in the main things, so that other countries cannot exert pressure on us and force us to do things against our wishes."

Nehru emphasized the necessity of developing India's basic industries, saying that India will build three more steel plants in the next five years, increasing India's steel production "fourfold again."

He also said, "We hope to achieve rapid economic progress in the country. If we have to buy every machine from other countries, we cannot achieve rapid progress. Therefore, an important task in our second five-year plan is to focus on heavy industry."

### 尼赫鲁总理强调印度必须在经济上独立

1955-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理强调印度必须在经济上独立
    新华社新德里十八日电　据德里报纸报道，印度总理尼赫鲁十六日在加尔各答的学生集会上发表演说时说，如果一个国家在独立之后在经济上是这样依赖人家以致其他国家能够扼杀它和强迫它做某些事情的话，就不可能有真正的独立。
    尼赫鲁说，亚洲和欧洲有许多这种国家，它们在政治上说是独立的，但是它们完全处于其他某些国家的经济势力之下。他说，“如果印度要真正独立和要向前迈进的话，它必须在经济上独立。”
    他指出，每个国家势必在某种程度上依靠其他国家，因为没有一个国家能够绝对地百分之百自给自足，“但是问题在于在主要的东西上我们应该独立，这样其他国家就不能对我们施加压力，强迫我们做违反我们的愿望的事情。”
    尼赫鲁着重指出了发展印度基本工业的必要性，他说，在今后五年中印度将再建造三个钢铁工厂，使印度的钢铁产量“再增加四倍”。
    他又说，“我们希望在国内取得迅速的经济进展。如果我们每部机器都要从其他国家购买的话，我们就不能取得迅速进展。因此，我们在第二个五年计划中的重要任务就是把重点放在重工业上。”
