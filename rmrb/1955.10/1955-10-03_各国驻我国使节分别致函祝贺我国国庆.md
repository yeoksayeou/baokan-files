Title: Foreign Envoys to China Send Letters of Congratulations on China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-10-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd. On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, Polish Ambassador Kirylok, Indian Ambassador Raghunath, Romanian Ambassador Cazan, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Choe Il, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov, Albanian Ambassador Nase, Hungarian Ambassador Siklós, Czechoslovak Ambassador Gregor, Pakistani Ambassador Ahmed, Yugoslav Ambassador Popović, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the German Democratic Republic Grutner sent letters to Chairman Mao Zedong to express their congratulations.
Polish Ambassador Kirylok, Romanian Ambassador Cazan, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Choe Il, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov, Albanian Ambassador Nase, Hungarian Ambassador Siklós, Finnish Ambassador Sundström, Czechoslovak Ambassador Gregor, Pakistani Ambassador Ahmed, Yugoslav Ambassador Popović, Danish Minister Gregerse, Swiss Minister Béguin, Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Sman, British Chargé d'Affaires O'Neill, Norwegian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Giemundsen, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the German Democratic Republic Grutner, and Swedish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Hammarskjöld, also sent letters to Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai to express their congratulations.

### 各国驻我国使节分别致函祝贺我国国庆

1955-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    各国驻我国使节分别致函祝贺我国国庆
    新华社二日讯　在中华人民共和国成立六周年的时候，波兰大使基里洛克、印度大使赖嘉文、罗马尼亚大使郭佐文、蒙古大使奥其尔巴特、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一、苏联大使尤金、保加利亚大使迪莫夫、阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛、匈牙利大使斯克拉丹、捷克斯洛伐克大使格里哥尔、巴基斯坦大使阿哈默德、南斯拉夫大使波波维奇、德意志民主共和国临时代办葛鲁特纳，曾分别致函毛泽东主席表示祝贺。
    波兰大使基里洛克、罗马尼亚大使郭佐文、蒙古大使奥其尔巴特、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一、苏联大使尤金、保加利亚大使迪莫夫、阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛、匈牙利大使斯克拉丹、芬兰大使孙士敦、捷克斯洛伐克大使格里哥尔、巴基斯坦大使阿哈默德、南斯拉夫大使波波维奇、丹麦公使格瑞杰生、瑞士公使贝努义、荷兰代办司曼、英国代办欧念儒、挪威临时代办季茂登、德意志民主共和国临时代办葛鲁特纳、瑞典临时代办哈马孝尔德，并且曾分别致函周恩来总理兼外交部长表示祝贺。
