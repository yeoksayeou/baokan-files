Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Arrives in New Delhi on His Way to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 18th - Burmese Prime Minister U Nu arrived in New Delhi by plane on the morning of the 18th. He arrived here on his way to visit the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet government. He was accompanied by his wife and four staff members.

Indian Prime Minister Nehru and other senior Indian government officials welcomed him at the airport. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to India were also present at the airport to welcome him.

U Nu told reporters that the trend of peace in the international situation is strengthening day by day, especially after the Geneva Four-Power Summit, the atmosphere has become more pleasant. In response to the question of whether he would sign a statement with the Soviet side reaffirming the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, he said, "I am very willing to sign such a statement."

Earlier, while passing through Calcutta, U Nu told reporters that he would discuss world peace issues and issues of common concern to both countries with Soviet leaders. He also said that from an economic point of view, "we must sell our rice, and we will discuss the issue of concluding a trade agreement with the Soviet leaders."

U Nu also said that he was prepared to invite Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Marshal Bulganin, and First Secretary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, to visit Burma.

### 缅甸总理吴努在访苏途中到达新德里

1955-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努在访苏途中到达新德里
    新华社新德里十八日电　缅甸总理吴努十八日上午坐飞机到达新德里。他是在应邀请去苏联访问途中到达这里的，陪同他前往的有他的夫人和四位随员。
    印度总理尼赫鲁和印度政府的其他高级官员到机场欢迎。在机场欢迎的还有各国驻印度的外交使节。
    吴努对新闻记者发表谈话说，国际局势中的和平趋势正在逐日加强，特别是在日内瓦四国政府首脑会议以后，气氛已经变得更令人高兴了。他在答复是否将同苏联方面签署重申五项原则的声明的问题时说：“我十分愿意签署这样一个声明。”
    早些时候，吴努在途经加尔各答时对新闻记者说，他将同苏联领袖讨论世界和平问题和两国共同关心的问题。他又说，从经济方面来说，“我们必须出售我们的大米，我们将同苏联的领袖们讨论缔结贸易协定的问题”。
    吴努还说：他准备邀请苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁元帅和苏联共产党第一书记赫鲁晓夫访问缅甸。
