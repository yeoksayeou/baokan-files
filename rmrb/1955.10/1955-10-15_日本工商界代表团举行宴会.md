Title: Japanese Business Delegation Hosts Banquet
Author:
Date: 1955-10-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: The Japanese business delegation, which was invited to visit China, held a banquet at noon on the 14th, prior to their departure from Beijing.

    Those attending the banquet included Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Vice Chairmen Lei Renmin and Li Zhuchen, Committee Member Lu Xuzhang, and the heads of various specialized corporations under the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, as well as Japanese individuals in Beijing such as Kameda Togo, Kaneko Kenta, and Nakamura Kanemon.

### 日本工商界代表团举行宴会

1955-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    日本工商界代表团举行宴会
    新华社十四日讯　应邀来中国访问的日本工商界访华代表团在即将离开北京前夕，十四日中午举行宴会。
    出席宴会的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸，副主席雷任民、李烛尘，委员卢绪章，以及中华人民共和国对外贸易部各专业公司的负责人，在北京的日本人士龟田东伍、金子健太和中村翫右卫门等。
