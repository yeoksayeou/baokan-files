Title: General Ne Win Visits Marshal Peng Dehuai and Presents Gifts
Author:
Date: 1955-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. General Ne Win, head of the Burmese Military Friendship Delegation, called on Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, on the morning of the 4th, and presented Marshal Peng Dehuai with exquisite silver bowls and silver boxes made by Burmese craftsmen.

In his speech at the presentation, General Ne Win said: "Respected Minister of National Defense and brother officers:

We have successfully laid the foundation for friendship between the armed forces of our two countries, which is the main purpose of our military friendship delegation. Personally, the members of the military friendship delegation have also gained the friendship of the military leaders of the People's Republic of China.

On behalf of the military friendship delegation, please allow me to present this small gift to the respected Minister of National Defense as a permanent memento and symbol of friendship. Please accept it."

After accepting the gift, Marshal Peng Dehuai replied:

"Respected General Ne Win and all the generals and officers:

I am very happy to accept the gift from you, General Ne Win, and the Burmese Military Friendship Delegation you lead. I believe this is not only an expression of the friendship between you and me personally, but also an expression of the friendship between the armed forces of our two countries.

May the friendship between the people and armies of China and Burma grow stronger day by day."

Present at the meeting and gift-giving were: Marshal He Long, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, General Su Yu, General Tan Zheng, Senior General Xiao Hua, Senior General Li Da, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Major General An Dong, and Colonel Jiang Keding, Acting Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense.

Also present was Lieutenant Colonel Maung Maung, Military Attaché of the Burmese Embassy in China.

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Burmese Military Friendship Delegation visited the Tank School of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the 2nd. Senior General Li Da, Deputy Minister of National Defense, and Lieutenant General Xiang Zhonghua of the Armored Forces, accompanied General Ne Win and others on the visit.

The delegation was warmly welcomed by all the personnel of the Tank School. The distinguished guests watched a demonstration of tanks passing through various obstacles on the training ground, and also visited the teaching materials, charts, models, and sand tables used in the classrooms, as well as the students' dormitories, dining halls, clubs, and libraries. In the vehicle display area, the distinguished guests also saw various tanks captured by the Chinese People's Liberation Army during the Liberation War. After the visit, the Tank School presented the delegation with a model tank used for teaching as a souvenir. In his reply, General Ne Win said: This gift will remind me of your friendship to me here at any time, and I will never forget it in the future.

Since arriving in Beijing on September 28, the delegation has not only participated in the National Day celebrations, but also visited Badaling, ascended the Great Wall to view the mountains from afar, and toured the Summer Palace. (Attached picture)

### 奈温中将拜会彭德怀元帅并赠礼

1955-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    奈温中将拜会彭德怀元帅并赠礼
    新华社四日讯　缅甸军事友好代表团团长奈温中将，四日上午拜会了中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅，并以出自缅甸工匠之手的精致的银钵、银匣，向彭德怀元帅赠礼。
    奈温中将在赠礼时致词说：“敬爱的国防部长和军官兄弟们：
    我们已成功的为两国武装部队间的友谊奠定了基础，这是我们军事友好代表团的主要目的。以个人来说，军事友好代表团的团员们，也得到了中华人民共和国军事领袖们的友谊。
    请允许我代表军事友好代表团，将此薄礼赠予敬爱的国防部长作为永久纪念和友好的象征，请您接纳。”
    彭德怀元帅接受礼物后，致答词说：
  “敬爱的总司令奈温将军及各位将军、军官们：
    我很高兴地来接受您，奈温将军及您所率领的缅甸军事友好代表团给我的赠礼，我认为，这不仅是我和您个人之间的友好的表现，同时也是我们两国武装力量之间的友好的表现。
    愿中缅两国人民及其军队的友谊日益发展。”
    拜会和赠礼时在座的有：贺龙元帅、聂荣臻元帅、粟裕大将、谭政大将、萧华上将、李达上将、萧向荣中将、安东少将和国防部外事处代处长蒋克定上校。
    在座的还有缅甸驻华大使馆武官茂茂中校。
    新华社四日讯　缅甸军事友好代表团二日参观了中国人民解放军的坦克学校。国防部副部长李达上将、装甲兵向仲华中将等陪同奈温中将等前往参观。
    代表团受到坦克学校全体人员的热烈欢迎。贵宾们在教练场观看了坦克通过各种障碍物的表演，还参观了各科教室教学所用的实物、图表、模型和沙盘，以及学员们的宿舍、食堂、俱乐部和图书馆。贵宾们在陈列车场内，还看到了中国人民解放军在解放战争时期缴获的各种坦克。参观后，坦克学校赠送了一辆教学用的坦克模型给代表团留念。奈温中将在答谢时说：这个礼物使我随时想起你们在这里对我的友谊，而且以后我也是永远不会忘掉的。
    代表团自九月二十八日到达北京后，除参加国庆观礼外，还游览了八达岭，登上万里长城远眺群山；游览了颐和园。（附图片）
    彭德怀元帅同奈温中将谈话。
                  新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
