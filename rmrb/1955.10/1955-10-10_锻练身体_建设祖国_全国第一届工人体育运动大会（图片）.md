Title: Bodybuilding for National Construction - The First National Workers' Games (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1955-10-10
Page: 5

    Bodybuilding for National Construction
    —The First National Workers' Games
    The Venue of the Games
    The First National Workers' Games concluded on the 9th of this month. Chairman Mao Zedong, along with other Party and state leaders, attended the opening ceremony on the 2nd. Top picture, from left to right: Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers' Committee for Physical Culture and Sports N.N. Romanov, Head of the Soviet Trade Union Sports Delegation A.V. Vasilyev.
          Photos on this page by our reporters Gao Liang, Na Yi, and Wang Zhiyuan, unless otherwise indicated.
    Li Bingcheng, the first place winner in the men's shot put, broke the national record with a throw of 13.34 meters.
    Fu Shenghai, the first place winner in the men's 5,000 meters, broke the national record with a time of 15 minutes 15.6 seconds; he also won the men's 10,000 meters race with a time of 32 minutes 31.5 seconds. This is him crossing the finish line in the men's 10,000 meters final.
    In the women's 3,000-meter cycling final, Li Fengqin (left) won first place, Li Guizhi (center) won second place, and Wu Shuhua (right) won third place. All three of their times broke the national record.
    Soccer: First Machine vs. Light Industry, 2-0, First Machine wins.
                                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Mao Songyou
    Women's Volleyball: Locomotive vs. Education, 3-0, Locomotive wins.
                                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao
    Men's Basketball: Electric Power vs. Education, 99-83, Electric Power wins.
                                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao
    Beijing workers performing group exercises at the Games.　　　　　　　　    Photo by Ma Huamin
    Central Institute of Physical Education students performing group exercises at the Games.
    Wang Yi (left), the first place winner in the women's javelin final, broke the national record with a throw of 34.44 meters. Cai Changhua (right) is the first place winner in the light heavyweight weightlifting competition, with a total score of 310 kg, breaking the national record.

### 锻练身体  建设祖国——全国第一届工人体育运动大会（图片）

1955-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    锻练身体  建设祖国
    ——全国第一届工人体育运动大会
    大会会场
    全国第一届工人体育运动大会已于本月九日闭幕。毛泽东主席同党和国家的领导人员在二日开幕时出席了大会。上图自左至右：毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、苏联部长会议体育运动委员会主席尼·尼·罗曼诺夫、苏联工会体育代表团团长阿·维·华西里耶夫。
          本版照片除署名者外均为本报记者高粮、纳一、王志渊摄
    男子铅球第一名李秉诚，掷球成绩是十三点三十四公尺，打破了全国纪录。
    男子五千公尺第一名傅生海以十五分十五秒六的成绩打破了全国纪录；他参加男子一万公尺赛跑，又以三十二分三十一秒五的成绩获得冠军。这是他在参加男子一万公尺决赛时，跑到终点的情形。
    在女子三千公尺自行车决赛中李凤琴（左）获第一名、李桂芝（中）获第二名、吴淑华（右）获第三名。她们三人的成绩都打破了全国纪录。
    足球：第一机械对轻工业二比零，第一机械胜。
                                        新华社记者　毛松友摄
    女子排球：火车头对教育三比零，火车头获胜。
                                        新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
    男子篮球：电业对教育，九十九比八十三，电业胜。
                                        新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
    大会上的北京工人团体操表演。　　　　　　　　    马化民摄
    大会上的中央体育学院学员团体操表演。
    王毅（左）是女子标枪决赛第一名，她以三十四点四四公尺的成绩打破了全国纪录。蔡昌华（右是次重级举重比赛第一名，他的总成绩是三百一十公斤，打破了全国纪录。
