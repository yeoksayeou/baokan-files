Title: Over 2,800 Key Industrial Enterprises Nationwide Fulfill Third Quarter Gross Output Value Plan

Author:

Date: 1955-10-19
Page: 2

According to statistics from the State Statistical Bureau, 2,817 key industrial enterprises across the country completed 101.5% of their third-quarter gross output value plan. Among them, state-owned enterprises completed 102.8% of the plan; local state-owned enterprises completed 101.5% of the plan; and central joint state-private enterprises completed 102.9% of the plan. Local joint state-private enterprises did not fulfill the plan.

Accumulated from January to September, the total output value of the aforementioned key enterprises increased by 9% compared to the same period last year. Among them, state-owned enterprises increased by 10.4%, local state-owned enterprises increased by 13.7%, central joint state-private enterprises increased by 2.5%, and local joint state-private enterprises decreased by 2%.

Among the 30 important industrial products of the aforementioned key enterprises, 18 products, including raw coal, gasoline, pig iron, steel rolled products, copper, sulfuric acid, nitric acid, caustic soda, soda ash, ammonium sulfate, ammonium nitrate, transformers, metal cutting machine tools, double-furrow plows,麻袋 (ma dai, sacks), paper, flour, and cigarettes, completed their third-quarter plans. Rubber shoes nearly completed their plan. Except for crude oil and generators, other products completed about 90% of their plans.

The labor productivity plans for the aforementioned key enterprises in each month of the third quarter were generally completed, especially in September, with a total completion rate of 105.3%. Among them, state-owned enterprises completed 106.3% of the plan, local state-owned enterprises completed 106.9% of the plan, and central joint state-private enterprises completed 107.4% of the plan. Local joint state-private enterprises did not fulfill the plan.

Enterprises that performed relatively well in completing their production plans in the third quarter included Tianjin Steel Plant, Shanghai Machine Tool Plant, Beijing Power Bureau, Tangshan Power Bureau, Hegang Mining Bureau, Datong Mining Bureau, Petroleum Factory No. 3, Petroleum Factory No. 7, Yanchang Oil Mine, Shixian Paper Mill, Shanghai State-owned Cotton Mill No. 1, Shanghai State-owned Cotton Mill No. 2, Qingdao State-owned Cotton Mill No. 2, and many other enterprises. However, some enterprises completed their plans relatively poorly, such as Jinzhou Power Bureau, Quanjie Coal Mine, Tiantun Coal Mine, Xinjiang Petroleum Company, Huameiao Tungsten Mine, Shanghai Glass Factory, Shenyang High-Voltage Switch Factory, and Jiamusi Textile Factory.

### 全国二千八百多个重点工业企业  完成今年第三季度总产值计划

1955-10-19
第2版()
专栏：

    全国二千八百多个重点工业企业
    完成今年第三季度总产值计划
    根据国家统计局的统计，全国二千八百一十七个重点工业企业完成第三季度总产值计划百分之一百零一点五。其中国营企业完成计划百分之一百零二点八；地方国营企业完成计划百分之一百零一点五；中央公私合营企业完成计划百分之一百零二点九。地方公私合营企业没有完成计划。
    上述重点企业一至九月份累计，总产值比去年同期增长了百分之九。其中国营企业增长了百分之十点四，地方国营企业增长了百分之十三点七，中央公私合营企业增长了百分之二点五，地方公私合营企业降低了百分之二。
    上述重点企业三十种重要工业产品中，有原煤、汽油、生铁、钢压延品、铜、硫酸、硝酸、烧碱、纯碱、硫铵、硝铵、变压器、金属切削机床、双铧犁、麻袋、纸、面粉、卷烟等十八种产品完成了第三季度计划。胶鞋接近完成计划。其他产品除原油、发电机外，都完成计划百分之九十左右。
    上述重点企业第三季度各月的劳动生产率计划总的都完成了，尤以九月份完成得较好，总计完成计划百分之一百零五点三，其中国营企业完成计划百分之一百零六点三，地方国营企业完成计划百分之一百零六点九，中央公私合营企业完成计划百分之一百零七点四。地方公私合营企业则没有完成计划。
    第三季度完成生产计划比较好的有天津钢厂、上海机床厂、北京电业局、唐山电业局、鹤岗矿务局、大同矿务局、石油三厂、七厂、延长油矿、石岘造纸厂、上海国棉一厂、二厂、青岛国棉二厂等许多企业。但也有一些企业完成计划是比较差的，如锦州电业局、全济煤矿、田屯煤矿、新疆石油公司、画眉坳钨矿、上海玻璃厂、沈阳高压开关厂、佳木斯纺织厂等企业。
