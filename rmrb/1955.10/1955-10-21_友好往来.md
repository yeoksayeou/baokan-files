Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1955-10-21
Page: 4
　Friendly Exchanges

　　Our Animal Husbandry Delegation Leaves for the Soviet Union
　　The Chinese Animal Husbandry Delegation, consisting of forty-one members, led by head Wang Enmao and deputy head Yang Heting, left Urumqi by plane on the 18th to visit the Soviet Union. On the 19th, the delegation arrived in Moscow.

　　Romanian Pianist Arrives in Beijing
　　Romanian pianist Cristian Rijica, who is visiting China to perform under the 1955 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 17th.

　　Indian National Volleyball Team Arrives in Guangzhou
　　The Indian National Volleyball Team, consisting of fifteen members, invited by the All-China Sports Federation, arrived in Guangzhou from Shenzhen by train on the afternoon of the 19th. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1955-10-21
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    我国畜牧业参观团前往苏联
    中国畜牧业参观团一行四十一人，在团长王恩茂、副团长杨和亭率领下，十八日乘飞机离开乌鲁木齐市，前往苏联参观访问。十九日，参观团到达莫斯科。
    罗马尼亚钢琴家到京
    根据中罗文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来中国访问演出的罗马尼亚钢琴家克里斯基扬·丽佳，已在十七日乘飞机到达北京。
    印度国家排球队到广州
    应中华全国体育总会邀请来中国访问的印度国家排球队一行十五人，在十九日下午从深圳乘车到达广州。　（据新华社讯）
