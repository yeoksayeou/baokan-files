Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Approves Protocols and Agreements of Soviet-Finnish Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th (TASS): On October 11th, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR studied and approved the following documents, which were adopted by the USSR Council of Ministers and endorsed by the Foreign Affairs Committees of the Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR:

　　1. Protocol between the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the Republic of Finland on the Extension of the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance signed on April 6, 1948.

　　2. Agreement between the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the Republic of Finland on the Soviet Union's Renunciation of the Right to Use the Porkkala-Udd Naval Base and the Withdrawal of Soviet Armed Forces from this Area.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团  批准苏芬会谈的议定书和协定

1955-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    批准苏芬会谈的议定书和协定
    新华社十三日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：十月十一日，苏联最高苏维埃主席团研究并且批准了苏联部长会议决议通过的，并且经苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院的外交委员会赞同的下列文件：
    一、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和芬兰共和国关于延长一九四八年四月六日签订的友好合作互助条约的议定书。
    二、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和芬兰共和国关于苏联放弃使用波卡拉乌德海军基地的权利和从这个地区撤退苏联武装部队的协定。
