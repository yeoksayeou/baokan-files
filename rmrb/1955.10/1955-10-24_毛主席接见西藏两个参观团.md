Title: Chairman Mao Receives Two Tibetan Visiting Groups
Author:
Date: 1955-10-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Lhalu Tsewang Dorje, head of the Tibetan Region Visiting Group, Vice Heads Anqin Dingjie Living Buddha and Rinchen Dondrup, Sangpo Tenzin Dondrup, head of the Tibetan Youth Visiting Group, Vice Heads Tsewang Dodan and Yexi Pingzhuo, and all members of the two visiting groups this afternoon. Also received was Taiji Gashe Queji Nyima, the Dalai Lama's representative attending the inaugural meeting of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region.

Present at the reception were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, Song Qingling, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Chen Yi, and Ulanhu, Vice Premiers of the State Council, Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court, Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Liu Geping, Chairman of the National People's Congress Nationalities Committee, Zhang Jingwu, Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet, Wang Feng, Liu Chun, and Sa Kongliao, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremony of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

Also present at the reception were Dunwang Jianzan Zhaba, Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, and Sunge Badan, Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing.

During the reception, the Tibetan Region Visiting Group, the Tibetan Youth Visiting Group, and Taiji Gashe Queji Nyima, the Dalai Lama's representative attending the inaugural meeting of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, presented Hada scarves to Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen Song Qingling, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, and President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu. Then, Chairman Mao Zedong and others took group photos with the Tibetan Region Visiting Group and the Tibetan Youth Visiting Group.

Chairman Mao Zedong and others also had a cordial two-hour conversation with the heads and main officials of the two visiting groups, as well as Taiji Gashe Queji Nyima, the Dalai Lama's representative attending the inaugural meeting of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, and others. (Attached photos)

### 毛主席接见西藏两个参观团

1955-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见西藏两个参观团
    新华社二十三日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席在二十三日下午接见了西藏地区参观团团长拉鲁·策旺多吉、副团长安钦·定结活佛、仁钦顿珠，西藏青年参观团团长桑颇·登增顿珠、副团长才旺多登、悦希凭卓和两个参观团的全体人员。受接见的还有参加新疆维吾尔自治区成立大会的达赖喇嘛代表台吉噶雪·却吉尼玛。
    接见时在座的，有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、郭沫若、彭真、陈叔通，国务院副总理陈云、邓小平、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫，最高人民法院院长董必武，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国务院秘书长习仲勋，全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任委员刘格平，中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春、萨空了，中华全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志，全国人民代表大会常务委员会典礼局局长余心清。
    接见时，达赖驻京办事处处长顿旺·坚赞札巴、班禅驻京办事处处长孙格巴顿也在座。
    接见时，西藏地区参观团和西藏青年参观团以及参加新疆维吾尔自治区成立大会的达赖喇嘛代表台吉噶雪·却吉尼玛，曾向毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理、副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、郭沫若、彭真、陈叔通、副总理陈云、邓小平、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫和最高人民法院院长董必武敬献哈达。接着，毛泽东主席等同西藏地区参观团和西藏青年参观团一起合影留念。
    毛泽东主席等并同两个参观团的负责人和主要官员，以及参加新疆维吾尔自治区成立大会的达赖喇嘛代表台吉噶雪·却吉尼玛等，作了两小时的亲切谈话。（附图片）
