Title: Welcoming the Myanmar National Football Delegation (Picture)
Author: Na Yi, Liu Dong'ao
Date: 1955-10-17
Page: 3

    Welcoming the Myanmar National Football Delegation
    The opening ceremony of the Myanmar National Football Delegation's visit to China for friendly matches was held on the 16th at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium in Beijing. The picture shows the Myanmar National Football Team (left) and the Beijing Football Team (right) at the opening ceremony of the match. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao
    Myanmar National Football Team goalkeeper La Bao saves a dangerous ball. Xinhua News Agency reporter Jiang Qisheng
The football captains of both sides exchange team flags.
                      Our reporter Na Yi

    欢迎缅甸国家足球代表团
    应邀来我国访问的缅甸国家足球代表团十六日在北京先农坛体育场举行来我国访问比赛的开幕式。图为在比赛开幕式上的缅甸国家足球队（左）和北京足球队（右）。　新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
    缅甸国家足球队守门员拉保正抢救一个险球。　新华社记者　蒋齐生摄双方足球队长交换队旗。
