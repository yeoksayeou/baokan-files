Title: Soviet Moscow "Little Birch" Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Soviet Moscow "Little Birch" Dance Troupe, a group of fifty people led by troupe director Tarumov, arrived in Beijing by train at 6:20 PM on the 7th. Nadezhda Nadezhdina, the artistic director of the dance troupe, has also traveled by train from Moscow to Beijing.

More than 500 people from the capital's literary and art circles and young art workers gathered at the Beijing Railway Station to welcome the dance troupe. Among those welcoming were Liu Zhiming and Xia Yan, Deputy Ministers of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison, Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Liao Jingtian and Gu Shuxing, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Li Haiqiu, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, and Nikishkin, head of the Soviet Artists Delegation.

As the train pulled into the station to the sound of music, students from the Beijing Dance School presented flowers to the Soviet artists. A grand welcoming ceremony was then held at the station. Liu Zhiming delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the Ministry of Culture and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Lyubov Treinova, a member of the dance troupe, delivered a reply on behalf of the dance troupe, thanking everyone for their warm and enthusiastic welcome. She ended by shouting in Chinese: "Long live friendship!"

On the same evening, Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet to welcome the dance troupe. Li Fuchun, Vice Premier of the State Council, and Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General, attended the welcome banquet.

When the dance troupe arrived in Manzhouli, Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, was sent to welcome them.

The Soviet Moscow "Little Birch" Dance Troupe is a female dance troupe formed after the All-Russian Soviet Federal Socialist Republic Collective Farm Amateur Art Performance in 1948. The troupe has thirty-six dancers who perform solo and group dances, as well as solo singers, instrumental soloists, and accordion players. The dances performed by the troupe in China include ancient and elegant Russian circle dances, and cheerful and lively dances depicting Soviet rural life. The dance troupe won first prize at the Second World Youth Festival in 1949 and received great honor during performances in European countries. (Attached picture)

### 苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团到北京

1955-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团到北京
    新华社七日讯　苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团一行五十人在团主任塔路莫夫率领下，七日下午六时二十分乘火车到达北京。舞蹈团艺术领导人纳杰日达·纳杰日金娜也已从莫斯科乘火车前来北京。
    首都文艺界人士和青年文艺工作者五百多人聚集在北京车站欢迎舞蹈团。在欢迎的行列中，有中华人民共和国文化部副部长刘芝明、夏衍，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，北京市副市长王昆仑，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长廖经天、顾淑型，中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记曹孟君，苏联大使馆参赞李海秋，苏联艺术家代表团团长尼基什金等人。
    列车在音乐声中驶进车站，北京舞蹈学校的学生们向苏联艺术家们献了花。在车站上接着举行了盛大的欢迎仪式。刘芝明代表文化部和中苏友好协会总会致欢迎词。舞蹈团团员柳鲍芙·特雷诺娃代表舞蹈团致答词，感谢对他们的亲切热烈的欢迎。她最后用中国话高呼：“友谊万岁”。
    同日晚间，中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞设宴欢迎舞蹈团。国务院副总理李富春，秘书长习仲勋出席了欢迎宴会。
    舞蹈团到达满洲里时，中苏友好协会总会曾派副秘书长李沾吾前去迎接。
    苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团是在一九四八年俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国集体农庄业余艺术观摩会演后组成的女子舞蹈团，团员中有三十六位表演独舞和集体舞的舞蹈家，还有独唱家、器乐独奏家和手风琴手。舞蹈团来中国表演的有古老优雅的俄罗斯环舞，有表现苏联农村生活的欢愉活泼的舞蹈。舞蹈团在一九四九年第二届世界青年联欢节中获得了一等奖，在欧洲各国演出时获得极大的荣誉。（附图片）
