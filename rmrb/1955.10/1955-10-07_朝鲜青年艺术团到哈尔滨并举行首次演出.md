Title: Korean Youth Art Troupe Arrives in Harbin and Gives First Performance
Author:
Date: 1955-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: The Korean Youth Art Troupe, consisting of one hundred and one members, arrived in Harbin by train from the Soviet Union on the afternoon of the 4th, upon the invitation of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, for a performance tour of China. On the evening of the 5th, they gave their first performance at the auditorium of the Northeast Agricultural College in Harbin. The art troupe performed twenty-five beautiful musical and dance programs, which were warmly welcomed.

    Soloist Tian Yu-feng sang the Korean folk songs "Ox! Go Faster!" and "Song of the Pheasant," for which he won the second prize in the national vocal music category at the Fifth World Youth Festival. The art troupe also sang the Chinese song "Long Live Chairman Mao!"

    Korean dancers performed "Kim Chok-ji Dance," "Rose Dance," "In the Apple Orchard," and "Little Drum Dance," and also performed the Chinese folk dance "Picking Tea and Chasing Butterflies."

    Korean youth violin soloist Bai Gaoshan, who won the first prize for instrumental music at the Fifth World Youth Festival, played "Idyll" composed by Ma Sicong and a Spanish dance. At the enthusiastic request of the audience, he also played "My Dove," a song with the theme of praising peace.

    An opening ceremony was held before the performance. Bai Wei, Deputy Director of the Heilongjiang Provincial Bureau of Culture, entrusted by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, welcomed the Korean Youth Art Troupe to visit and perform in China, and wished them a successful performance. Choi Moon-gi, Deputy Head of the Korean Youth Art Troupe, delivered a reply. After the performance, young art workers from Heilongjiang Province and Harbin City presented flowers to the members of the Korean Youth Art Troupe.

### 朝鲜青年艺术团到哈尔滨并举行首次演出

1955-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜青年艺术团到哈尔滨并举行首次演出
    据新华社讯应中华人民共和国文化部和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会邀请来中国访问演出的朝鲜青年艺术团一行一百零一人，在四日下午乘火车由苏联到达哈尔滨，五日晚并在哈尔滨东北农学院礼堂举行首次表演会。艺术团演出了二十五个优美的音乐舞蹈节目，受到热烈欢迎。
    艺术团的独唱演员田雨峰演唱了他在第五届世界青年联欢节上荣获民族声乐二等奖的朝鲜民谣
  “牛啊！快走呀！”和“山鸡之歌”。艺术团还演唱了中国歌曲“万岁！毛泽东”。
    朝鲜舞蹈家表演了“金捉之舞”、“蔷薇舞”、“在苹果园里”和“小鼓舞”等，并表演了中国民间舞蹈“采茶扑蝶”。
    在第五届世界青年联欢节中荣获器乐一等奖的朝鲜青年提琴独奏家白高山演奏了马思聪创作的
  “牧歌”和西班牙的一首舞曲，在观众的热烈要求下，他又演奏一首以歌颂和平为主题的“我的鸽子”。
    在这次演出前举行了开幕式。黑龙江省文化局副局长白韦受中华人民共和国文化部和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的委托，欢迎朝鲜青年艺术团在中国访问演出，并祝贺他们演出成功。朝鲜青年艺术团副团长崔文岐致答词。在演出结束后，黑龙江省和哈尔滨市青年艺术工作者向朝鲜青年艺术团人员献花。
