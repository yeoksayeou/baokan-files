Title: Trade Unions, Women's, Youth, and Friendship Organizations from Various Countries Send Congratulatory Telegrams for China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-10-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: On the eve of National Day, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and its affiliated trade unions received congratulatory telegrams from the World Federation of Trade Unions, the International Federation of Teachers' Unions, the International Textile, Garment and Leather Workers' Union, the International Leather and Shoe Workers' Union, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Poland, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Romania, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Bulgaria, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Albania, the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, the Japan Federation of Steel Industry Workers' Unions, and the Indonesian Sugar Industry Workers' Union congratulating China on National Day. Many telegrams wished the Chinese people further achievements in building socialism and defending peace.

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League received congratulatory telegrams from the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Hungary, the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Mongolian People's Republic, the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, as well as the Hungarian Democratic Women's League, the Romanian Democratic Women's Union, the German Democratic Women's Federation, the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League, and the Vietnam National Salvation Youth League congratulating China on National Day. The telegrams wished the People's Republic of China further achievements in developing the national economy and defending peace in Asia and the world.

The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League received congratulatory telegrams from the World Federation of Democratic Youth, the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union, the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working Youth League, the Central Committee of the Romanian Working Youth League, and the Central Committee of the Dimitrov People's Youth League of Bulgaria celebrating China's National Day. These telegrams all wished the Chinese youth greater contributions in building the motherland and defending peace.

The Central Council of the Soviet-Polish Friendship Association, the Czechoslovak-Soviet Friendship Association, the Central Committee of the Korean-Soviet Cultural Association, and the Council of the Ukrainian Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries also sent telegrams to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, congratulating China on National Day. The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries received congratulatory telegrams from the Britain-China Friendship Association and the Japan-China Friendship Association celebrating China's National Day. Ding Xilin, president of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, also received a telegram from Sundarlal, chairman of the India-China Friendship Association, expressing warm congratulations on the National Day of the People's Republic of China.

### 各国工会、妇女、青年和友协组织电贺我国国庆

1955-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    各国工会、妇女、青年和友协组织电贺我国国庆
    据新华社讯　在国庆节前夕，中华全国总工会及所属工会收到世界工会联合会、教育工会国际、纺织服装工人工会国际、皮革皮毛工人工会国际、全苏工会中央理事会、波兰工会中央理事会、罗马尼亚工会中央理事会、保加利亚工会中央理事会、阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会、朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会、日本钢铁产业工会联合会、印度尼西亚糖业工人工会祝贺中国国庆的贺电。许多贺电中都祝中国人民在建设社会主义和保卫和平的事业中取得进一步的成就。
    中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会分别收到了捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会、匈牙利工会中央理事会、蒙古人民共和国工会中央理事会、越南总工会以及匈牙利民主妇女联盟、罗马尼亚民主妇女联合会、德国民主妇女联合会、朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会和越南救国青年团祝贺我国国庆节的贺电。贺电祝中华人民共和国在发展国民经济和保卫亚洲及全世界和平的斗争中获得进一步的成就。
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会收到世界民主青年联盟、苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会、匈牙利劳动青年联盟中央委员会、罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会、保加利亚季米特洛夫人民青年联盟中央委员会庆祝中国国庆的贺电。这些贺电都祝中国青年在建设祖国和保卫和平中作出更大的贡献。
    波苏友好协会中央理事会、捷苏友好协会、朝苏文化协会中央委员会和乌克兰对外文化协会理事会也打电报给中苏友好协会总会，祝贺我国国庆。中国人民对外文化协会收到了英中友好协会和日中友好协会祝贺中国国庆的贺电。中印友好协会会长丁西林也收到了印中友好协会主席森德拉尔的来电，其中对中华人民共和国国庆表示热烈祝贺。
