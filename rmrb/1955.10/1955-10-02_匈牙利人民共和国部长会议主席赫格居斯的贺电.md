Title: Telegram of Greetings from Chairman Hegedüs of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-02
Page: 3

To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of the great National Day of the People's Republic of China, Premier, please allow me, on behalf of the Hungarian people, the government of the Hungarian People's Republic, and in my own name, to express my heartfelt congratulations to the fraternal Chinese people, the government of the People's Republic of China, and to you personally. In the past six years, the friendship between our two peoples has deepened, and this friendship has made a significant contribution to safeguarding world peace and promoting the prosperity of our two countries. I would like to wish the fraternal People's Republic of China further great achievements in its creative work to build a new socialist China and consolidate true peace in the Far East, and in its tireless struggle for the peaceful liberation of Taiwan.

            András Hegedüs
            Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic

### 匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席赫格居斯的贺电

1955-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席赫格居斯的贺电中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    值此中华人民共和国伟大的国庆节，总理同志，请允许我代表匈牙利人民、匈牙利人民共和国政府，并以我个人名义向兄弟般的中国人民、中华人民共和国政府和您本人表示衷心的祝贺。在过去六年中，我们两国人民之间的友谊加深了，这种友谊对保卫世界和平和促进我们两国的繁荣作出了重大的贡献。我谨祝兄弟般的中华人民共和国在为建设社会主义的新中国和巩固远东的真正的和平而进行的创造性的工作中，在争取和平解放台湾的不倦的斗争中获得进一步的伟大成就。
            匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席　安德拉希·赫格居斯
