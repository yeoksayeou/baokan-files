Title: Lao Administrative Institutions and the Lao Free National United Front, etc., Gather in Sam Neua to Prepare for the Establishment of a New Lao National United Front
Author:
Date: 1955-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 14th -- According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 14th: According to news from Laos, the preparatory meeting for the establishment of a new and broader Lao National United Front opened on October 12th in Sam Neua Province, northern Laos. The meeting will formulate the program and charter of this front and establish an initiating committee responsible for expanding the National United Front.

Participating in the meeting were representatives from the Lao Administrative Institutions, the Lao Free National United Front, various ethnic groups, Buddhists, Catholics, women, and model combatants of the Lao fighting forces.

The meeting unanimously elected Prince Souphanouvong, leader of the Lao fighting forces, and Phoumi Vongvichit as honorary chairmen of the initiating committee for expanding the Lao National United Front, and also elected the presidium of this committee.

### 寮国行政机构和寮国自由民族统一战线等  在桑怒集会筹建新的老挝民族统一战线

1955-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    寮国行政机构和寮国自由民族统一战线等
    在桑怒集会筹建新的老挝民族统一战线
    新华社河内十四日电　据越南通讯社十四日讯：据来自老挝的消息说，为成立一个新的和更广泛的老挝民族统一战线而召开的筹备会议，十月十二日在老挝北部的桑怒省开幕。会议将制定这个战线的纲领和章程，并且设立一个负责扩大民族统一战线的发起委员会。
    参加会议的有寮国行政机构、寮国自由民族统一战线、各民族、佛教徒、天主教徒、妇女和寮国战斗部队模范战士的代表。
    会议一致选出寮国战斗部队领袖苏发努冯亲王和富米为扩大老挝民族统一战线发起委员会的名誉主席，并且选出了这个委员会的主席团。
