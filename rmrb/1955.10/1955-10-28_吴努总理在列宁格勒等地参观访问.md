Title: Premier U Nu Visits Leningrad and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1955-10-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th – According to TASS news: The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his entourage have been visiting Leningrad and Crimea for several days.

　　On the 25th, after arriving in Leningrad, Premier U Nu and his party visited Leningrad for a day. They toured the city of Leningrad, learned about the history of this famous city, and visited the State Antiquities Museum, the Stalin Hardware Factory, and the Leningrad underground tramway under construction. They also visited the famous Smolny Palace, and visited the small room where Lenin once lived and the hall used as the venue for the Second All-Russian Soviet Congress. Premier U Nu praised everything he saw in this great city. Before leaving the city, he expressed his infinite affection and invited representatives from the city of Leningrad to visit Burma.

　　On the 26th, Premier U Nu and his party traveled from Leningrad to the heroic city of Sevastopol on the Black Sea coast, and visited the city of Sevastopol and its urban construction. The guests particularly praised the famous painting "Defense of Sevastopol, 1854-1855" and the new houses built on streets such as Nakhimov Street.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Yalta, October 27th – According to TASS: On October 26th, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, met with the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and exchanged views with him on various issues of mutual concern.

　　Mikoyan and A. Kirichenko attended the meeting.

　　The Burmese side present at the meeting included: the Burmese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, U Wang, the Burmese Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, U Hla Maung, and the Burmese Ambassador to the United Kingdom, U Kin; the Soviet side included: the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Fedrin, and the Soviet Ambassador to Burma, Shibalin.

　　On the same day, Khrushchev also hosted a banquet in honor of U Nu and his entourage.

### 吴努总理在列宁格勒等地参观访问

1955-10-28
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努总理在列宁格勒等地参观访问
    据新华社二十七日讯　据塔斯社消息：缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的随行人员连日在列宁格勒和克里米亚参观访问。
    二十五日，吴努总理等一行到达列宁格勒后在列宁格勒访问了一天。他们参观了列宁格勒的市容，了解了这个名城的历史情况，参观了国家古物博物馆、斯大林五金工厂和正在建设的列宁格勒地下电车道。他们还访问了著名的斯莫尔尼宫，参观了宫内的列宁曾经住过的那个小房间和举行第二次全俄苏维埃代表大会时用作会场的那个大厅。吴努总理盛赞他在这个伟大城市中所见到的一切。在离开这个城市以前，他表示了无限惜别之情，并且邀请列宁格勒市的代表们访问缅甸。
    二十六日，吴努总理等一行从列宁格勒到达黑海沿岸的英雄城市塞瓦斯托波尔，参观了塞瓦斯托波尔的市容和城市建设。客人们对这里保存的名画“一八五四年—一八五五年塞瓦斯托波尔保卫战”和纳希莫夫街等街道上建筑的新房屋备加赞扬。
    新华社二十七日讯　据塔斯社雅尔塔讯：十月二十六日，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫接见了缅甸联邦总理吴努，并且同他进行了会谈，就双方共同关心的各项问题交换了意见。
    米高扬和阿·基里钦科参加了会谈。
    会谈时在座的缅甸方面有：缅甸驻苏联大使吴旺、缅甸驻中华人民共和国大使吴拉茂、缅甸驻英国大使吴钦；苏联方面有：苏联外交部副部长费德林、苏联驻缅甸大使希鲍林。
    在同一天，赫鲁晓夫还设宴招待吴努和他的随行人员。
