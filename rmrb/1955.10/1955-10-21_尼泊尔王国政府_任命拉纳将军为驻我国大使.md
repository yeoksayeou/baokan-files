Title: The Government of the Kingdom of Nepal Appoints General Rana as Ambassador to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1955-10-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 20th—The Government of the Kingdom of Nepal has appointed General Daman Shamsher Jung Bahadur Rana as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Nepal to the People's Republic of China.

### 尼泊尔王国政府  任命拉纳将军为驻我国大使

1955-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔王国政府
    任命拉纳将军为驻我国大使
    新华社二十日讯　尼泊尔王国政府任命达曼·沙姆谢尔·忠格·巴哈杜尔·拉纳将军为尼泊尔驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。
