Title: Minister of Higher Education Yang Xiufeng Hosts Banquet for Indian Teacher and Student Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: Minister of Higher Education Yang Xiufeng hosted a banquet at the New Qiao Hotel on the evening of October 6th to welcome the Indian teacher and student delegation invited to visit China.

Yang Xiufeng, on behalf of the Ministry of Higher Education, extended a heartfelt welcome to the Indian teacher and student delegation. Yang Xiufeng said: China's educational construction has developed rapidly in the six years since the founding of the country. We have achieved some success in reforming the education system, improving teaching, and cultivating cadres suitable for our country's socialist construction. However, there are still many shortcomings in our work. We are willing to introduce our successful experiences, failures, strengths, and weaknesses as much as possible to our friends who have come from afar. We also ask the experienced education experts and outstanding and promising young students in the delegation to offer criticism and help. Yang Xiufeng said, we believe that through this visit of the Indian teacher and student delegation, we can further enhance the profound friendship between China and India and develop cooperation in culture and education between the two countries.

The head of the Indian teacher and student delegation, Ayar, said in his reply that he hoped to increase exchanges between young students from India and China in the future. He suggested that India and China should exchange more professors and students.

Those present at the banquet this evening included Vice Minister of Higher Education Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Education Ye Shengtao, President of Peking University Ma Yinchu, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Chen Hansheng, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation Tian Demin, and representatives of teachers and students from universities in the capital.

Also invited to the banquet were Mr. and Mrs. Bahadur Singh, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy in China.

### 高等教育部部长杨秀峰  设宴招待印度师生代表团

1955-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    高等教育部部长杨秀峰
    设宴招待印度师生代表团
    新华社六日讯高等教育部部长杨秀峰六日晚上在新侨饭店设宴招待应邀来中国的印度师生代表团。
    杨秀峰代表高等教育部向印度师生代表团表示衷心的欢迎，杨秀峰说：中国的教育建设事业，建国六年来有了迅速的发展，在改革教育制度、改进教学工作、培养适合我国社会主义建设要求的干部方面，取得了一些成就。但是我们的工作还存在着许多缺点。我们愿意把工作中一些成功的经验和失败的教训、优点和缺点，尽量介绍给远道来访的朋友们，同时也请代表团中经验丰富的教育专家和优秀有为的青年学生给以批评和帮助。杨秀峰说，我们相信通过这次印度师生代表团的访问，能够进一步增进中印两国间的深厚友谊和发展两国文化教育的合作。
    印度师生代表团团长艾雅尔致答词说，他希望今后要多增加印中两国青年学生之间的往来。他建议印中两国应更多地交换教授和学生。
    今晚出席宴会作陪的有高等教育部副部长刘皑风、教育部副部长叶圣陶、北京大学校长马寅初、中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙、中华全国学生联合会主席田德民以及首都高等学校的教师学生代表等。
    应邀出席宴会的还有印度驻华大使馆临时代办巴哈杜尔·辛格夫妇。
