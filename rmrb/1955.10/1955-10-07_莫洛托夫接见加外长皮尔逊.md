Title: Molotov Receives Canadian Foreign Minister Pearson
Author:
Date: 1955-10-07
Page: 4

    Molotov Receives Canadian Foreign Minister Pearson
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th – According to TASS, Moscow: Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov received Canadian Foreign Minister Lester Pearson on the 5th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th – TASS, Moscow, 5th: Canadian Foreign Minister Mr. Pearson arrived in Moscow on October 5th at the invitation of the Soviet government.
    First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Foreign Minister of the Soviet Union Molotov, other Soviet government officials, and Canadian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Watkins, etc. were at the airport to welcome him.

### 莫洛托夫接见加外长皮尔逊

1955-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫接见加外长皮尔逊
    新华社六日讯据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部长莫洛托夫在五日接见了加拿大外交部长莱·皮尔逊。
    新华社五日讯塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：加拿大外交部长皮尔逊先生应苏联政府的邀请，在十月五日到达莫斯科。
    前往机场欢迎的有苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫、苏联政府其他官员，加拿大驻苏联大使华金斯等。
