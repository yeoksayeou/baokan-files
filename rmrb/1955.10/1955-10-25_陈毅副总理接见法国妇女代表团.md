Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Receives French Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th. Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, received the French Women's Delegation headed by Madame Marcelle Marquis on the morning of the 24th.

Present at the reception were Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Kang Keqing and Cao Mengjun, Secretaries of the Secretariat, and Gong Pusheng, Deputy Director of the International Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理接见法国妇女代表团

1955-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见法国妇女代表团
    新华社二十四日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅，二十四日上午接见了以玛赛尔·马盖夫人为首的法国妇女代表团。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴、书记处书记康克清、曹孟君，外交部国际司副司长龚普生等人。
