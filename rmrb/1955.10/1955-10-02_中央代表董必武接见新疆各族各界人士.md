Title: Central Representative Dong Biwu Receives People from All Ethnic Groups and All Walks of Life in Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1955-10-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency
    Dong Biwu, the representative of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central People's Government who came to congratulate the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, received industrial and animal husbandry labor models and democratic figures and religious figures attending the Second Session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province on September 30. He conveyed Chairman Mao's concern for them and encouraged them to work hard to build the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region.
    On September 23, Dong Biwu also received thirteen heads of the five ethnic autonomous prefectures and three ethnic autonomous counties attending the Second Session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province and listened to reports on the work of the autonomous regions. Dong Biwu expressed his hope that everyone would unite the people of all ethnic groups to do a good job in the construction of the autonomous regions.
    During his stay in Urumqi, Dong Biwu listened to work reports from the heads of the Xinjiang People's Court and the procuratorial departments and gave instructions on their work.

### 中央代表董必武接见新疆各族各界人士

1955-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    中央代表董必武接见新疆各族各界人士
    前来祝贺新疆维吾尔自治区成立的中国共产党中央委员会和中央人民政府的代表董必武，九月三十日分别接见了出席新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议的工业、畜牧业劳动模范和民主人士、宗教界人士，向他们传达了毛主席对他们的关怀，并勉励他们努力建设新疆维吾尔自治区。
    董必武还在九月二十三日接见了出席新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议的五个民族自治州、三个民族自治县的十三名负责人，听取了各自治地方工作情况的汇报。董必武希望大家团结各民族人民共同搞好各自治地方的建设事业。
    董必武在乌鲁木齐期间，听取了新疆人民法院和检察部门负责人的工作汇报，并指示了工作。
                                  （新华社）
