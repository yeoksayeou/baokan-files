Title: Indian Film Workers Delegation Departs to China to Attend "Indian Republic Film Week"
Author:
Date: 1955-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 9th - The Indian film workers delegation, led by the renowned film and stage actor and Indian Member of Parliament, Prithviraj Kapoor, departed for China today to attend the "Indian Republic Film Week" which will be held in twenty cities in China starting on October 17th.

The delegation includes the famous film producer B.N. Sircar, the director of the film "Two Acres of Land," Bimal Roy, and actor Balraj Sahni, whose film will be screened in China, the well-known actress Kamini Kaushal, the film screenwriter and Secretary-General of the Indian Progressive Writers Association, K.A. Abbas, and the film producer and director Chetan Anand. Indian Prime Minister Nehru received the delegation on the morning of the 8th. The Chinese Ambassador to India, Yuan Zhongxian, also hosted a banquet for the delegation on the evening of the same day.

### 应邀来我国参加“印度共和国电影周”  印度电影工作者代表团启程

1955-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加“印度共和国电影周”
    印度电影工作者代表团启程
    新华社新德里九日电　应邀参加从十月十七日开始在中国二十个城市举行的“印度共和国电影周”的印度电影工作者代表团，在著名的电影和舞台演员、印度国会议员普·拉·卡普尔率领下，在九日启程前往中国。
    代表团中包括著名电影制片人布·纳·西尔卡，将在中国放映的影片“两亩地”的导演比马尔·罗伊和演员巴拉吉·萨尼，著名女演员卡米尼·考沙尔，电影剧本作家、印度进步作家协会秘书长克·钱德尔，以及电影制片人和导演契坦·阿南德等。印度总理尼赫鲁在八日上午接见了代表团。中国驻印度大使袁仲贤也曾在同日晚上设宴招待代表团。
