Title: Premier Zhou Sends Condolences to People in Flood-Stricken Areas of India and Pakistan; Prime Minister Nehru and Prime Minister Chaudhri Ali Express Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1955-10-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 25th - On October 18th, Premier Zhou Enlai of our State Council sent telegrams to Indian Prime Minister Nehru and Pakistani Prime Minister Chaudhri Muhammad Ali respectively, expressing sympathy and condolences on behalf of the Chinese government and people to the people in the flood-stricken areas of India and Pakistan. On the 19th, Prime Minister Nehru sent a letter to Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian expressing his gratitude to Premier Zhou Enlai, the Chinese government, and the people for their sympathy and friendship. On the 23rd, Prime Minister Chaudhri Muhammad Ali also sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai to express his thanks.

The full text of Prime Minister Nehru's letter to Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian and Prime Minister Chaudhri Muhammad Ali's telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai are as follows:

Prime Minister Nehru's Letter to Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian

"Thank you for your letter of October 19th, in which you forwarded Premier Zhou Enlai's telegram. I am very grateful for Premier Zhou's telegram, and please convey our deep gratitude to Premier Zhou, the Chinese government, and the people for their expression of sympathy and friendship."

Prime Minister Chaudhri Muhammad Ali's Telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai

"On behalf of the Government and people of Pakistan and in my own name, I express my gratitude to Your Excellency and the Government and people of China for your message of sympathy to those suffering from the serious floods which have occurred in various parts of Pakistan."

### 周总理致电慰问印度、巴基斯坦水灾区人民  尼赫鲁总理和乔德里·阿里总理表示感谢

1955-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理致电慰问印度、巴基斯坦水灾区人民
    尼赫鲁总理和乔德里·阿里总理表示感谢
    新华社二十五日讯　十月十八日我国务院总理周恩来分别致电印度总理尼赫鲁和巴基斯坦总理乔德里·穆罕默德·阿里，代表中国政府和人民向印度和巴基斯坦水灾区人民表示同情和慰问。十九日，尼赫鲁总理函复袁仲贤大使对周恩来总理、中国政府和人民的同情和友谊表示感谢。二十三日，乔德里·穆罕默德·阿里总理也电复周恩来总理致谢。
    尼赫鲁总理致袁仲贤大使复函和乔德里·穆罕默德·阿里致周恩来总理复电全文如下：
    尼赫鲁总理复袁仲贤大使函
    “感谢您十月十九日来函，其中转来周恩来总理电文。对周总理电文我十分感激，请将我们深切的谢意转达给周总理、中国政府和人民，为了他们所表示的同情和友情。”
    乔德里·穆罕默德·阿里总理复周恩来总理电
    “我代表巴基斯坦政府和人民并以我自己的名义向阁下和中国政府和人民表示感激，感谢您同情因巴基斯坦各地所发生的严重水灾而受害人民的慰问电。”
