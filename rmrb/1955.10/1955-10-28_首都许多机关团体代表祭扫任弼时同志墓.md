Title: Representatives of Many Organizations and Groups in the Capital Pay Respects at Comrade Ren Bishi's Tomb
Author:
Date: 1955-10-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 27th. On October 27th, the fifth anniversary of Comrade Ren Bishi's death, representatives from the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League visited Comrade Ren Bishi's tomb in Babaoshan, a suburb of Beijing, this morning to pay their respects.

Also present to sweep the tomb were representatives from the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the People's Republic of China, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and some organizations and groups in Beijing. Ren Bishi's family also went to sweep the tomb today.

In the past few days, representatives of the Youth League organizations of many schools in Beijing and groups of young people have flocked to Ren Bishi's tomb to pay their respects. People have filled Ren Bishi's tomb with flowers and wreaths.

### 首都许多机关团体代表祭扫任弼时同志墓

1955-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    首都许多机关团体代表祭扫任弼时同志墓
    新华社二十七日讯　十月二十七日是任弼时同志逝世五周年纪念日。中国共产党中央委员会办公厅和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的代表在今天上午前往北京西郊八宝山任弼时墓地扫墓。
    前往扫墓的还有中华人民共和国内务部、中华全国总工会和北京市的一些机关、团体的代表。任弼时的家属，今天也前往扫墓。
    这几天，北京市许多学校的青年团组织的代表和成群的青年，纷纷前往任弼时墓地扫墓。人们已在任弼时墓前放满了鲜花和花圈。
