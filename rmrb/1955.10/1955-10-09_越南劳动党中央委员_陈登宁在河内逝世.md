Title: Tran Dang Ninh, Member of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, Dies in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1955-10-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 7th. A communique issued by the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party announced that Tran Dang Ninh, member of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Director of the General Supply Department of the Vietnamese People's Army, died of illness in Hanoi on October 6th. The funeral was scheduled to be held in Hanoi on the afternoon of the 7th.

### 越南劳动党中央委员  陈登宁在河内逝世

1955-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党中央委员
    陈登宁在河内逝世
    新华社河内七日电　越南劳动党中央委员会发表的公报宣布，越南劳动党中央委员会委员、越南人民军总供给局局长陈登宁，因病于十月六日在河内逝世。葬礼定于七日下午在河内举行。
