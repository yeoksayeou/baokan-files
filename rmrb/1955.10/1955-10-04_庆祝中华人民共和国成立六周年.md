Title: Celebrating the Sixth Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1955-10-04
Page: 4

Aidi, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia, Sends a Congratulatory Telegram

Comrade Mao Zedong, Beijing:

On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the founding of the great People's Republic of China, I, on behalf of the Communist Party of Indonesia, extend my congratulations to you, the Chinese Communist Party, and the Chinese people. We are delighted by the achievements made by the Chinese people in economic, cultural, and political cooperation with the people of all countries in the world. Long live the People's Republic of China, an important fortress of peace in Asia!

General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia, D.N. Aidit

Peace, Women, Youth, and Friendship Association Groups from Various Countries Send Telegrams to Congratulate Our National Day

(Xinhua News Agency) The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Preservation has received telegrams from the Soviet Peace Preservation Committee, the Vietnamese Committee for World Peace Preservation, Walter Friedrich, Chairman of the German Peace Committee, Ikio Oyama, a famous Japanese peace fighter, Armando Amastegui, Chairman of the Chilean Peace Committee, and Ivor Montagu and his wife, British peace activists, congratulating the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. These congratulatory telegrams also wish the Chinese people new achievements in the cause of defending world peace.

The All-China Democratic Women's Federation has received telegrams from women's organizations and women leaders from many countries congratulating China's National Day, including: Le Thi Xuyen, President of the Vietnamese Women's Union; the Central Council of the Polish Women's League; the Bulgarian Democratic Women's Committee; the Albanian Women's Union; Kan Kenshiko, a famous Japanese musician; Fujita Hisashi, Nakai Ai, and Tamaki Akiko, representatives of Japan attending the World Mothers' Conference; Durga Khote, President of the Indian People's Theatre Association; and Yona Golan, of the Women's Organization of the Israeli United Workers' Party, etc.

The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League has received telegrams from the Secretariat of the International Student Union, the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, the Czechoslovak Youth League, the Free German Youth League, and the Albanian Labor Youth League, congratulating the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The Sino-Burmese Friendship Association has received a telegram from the Burmese-Chinese Friendship Association, warmly congratulating China's National Day. The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries has also received congratulatory telegrams from Gotu, Vice Chairman of the Australia-China Association, and the Chile-China Cultural Institute, congratulating China's National Day.

### 庆祝中华人民共和国成立六周年

1955-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国成立六周年
    印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会总书记艾地的贺电北京毛泽东同志：
    值此伟大的中华人民共和国成立六周年之际，我代表印度尼西亚共产党，向您、中国共产党和中国人民致贺。我们对于中国人民在经济、文化以及全世界各国人民的政治合作方面所取得的成就感到欢欣鼓舞。亚洲重要的和平堡垒中华人民共和国万岁！
                                      印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会总书记迪·努·艾地各国和平、妇女、青年和友协团体致电祝贺我国国庆据新华社讯中国人民保卫世界和平委员会收到苏联保卫和平委员会、越南保卫世界和平委员会、德国和平委员会主席华·腓特烈、日本著名和平战士大山郁夫、智利和平委员会主席阿迈斯蒂和英国和平人士艾弗·蒙塔古夫妇的电报，祝贺中华人民共和国成立六周年。这些贺电并祝中国人民在保卫世界和平的事业中取得新的成就。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会收到许多国家的妇女组织和妇女领袖祝贺中国国庆的电报，其中包括：越南妇女联合会主席黎氏钏，波兰妇女联盟中央理事会，保加利亚民主妇女委员会，阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会，日本著名音乐家关鉴子，日本出席世界母亲大会代表藤田寿、中井爱、田万明子，印度人民戏剧协会主席杜尔加·柯特夫人，以色列统一工人党妇女组织约纳·戈兰夫人等。
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会收到国际学生联合会书记处、苏联青年反法西斯委员会、捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟、自由德国青年联盟和阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟的电报，祝贺中华人民共和国成立六周年。
    中缅友好协会收到缅中友好协会的来电，热烈祝贺中国国庆。中国人民对外文化协会也收到了澳大利亚—中国协会副主席高脱和智利—中国文化学会的祝贺中国国庆贺电。
