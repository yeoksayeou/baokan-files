Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev to Visit Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1955-10-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 24th—TASS reports: The government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan has invited Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Khrushchev, to visit Afghanistan.
    Bulganin and Khrushchev have accepted the invitation; it is understood that the visit will take place at the end of this year.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫将访问阿富汗

1955-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫将访问阿富汗
    新华社二十五日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二十四日讯：阿富汗王国政府邀请苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫访问阿富汗。
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫已经接受了邀请；据悉，访问时间将在今年年底。
