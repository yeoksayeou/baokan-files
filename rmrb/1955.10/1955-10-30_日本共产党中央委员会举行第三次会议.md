Title: Japanese Communist Party Central Committee Holds Third Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-10-30
Page: 4

    Japanese Communist Party Central Committee Holds Third Meeting
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 29th. According to a report in "Akahata," the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party held a press conference at the Party Headquarters on the morning of October 19th and released a communique regarding the Third Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party's Sixth National Congress held on October 13th.
    The communique stated: Central Committee members, alternate Central Committee members, and inspection committee members attended the meeting.
    Central Committee member Shida Shigeo delivered a "Report on the Discussion of the Sixth National Congress Resolutions and Party Organizational Construction." Based on the report, the meeting discussed the Party-wide discussion of the resolutions and the issue of Party unity and solidarity. The meeting confirmed that the cause of Party unity and solidarity, although differing in various regions, is fundamentally progressing. The meeting decided that, on the basis of completing this task, it would promote Party building and unity in the future, strengthen the ties between the Party and the masses, and that all departments should further formulate concrete policies to accomplish the task of developing a national liberation democratic united front. Central Committee member Konno Yojiro delivered a report on "Regulations for Elections of Party Organs at All Levels." The meeting discussed regulations for the election of leading organs at the local level party conferences based on the new Party Constitution, and made a resolution.
    The communique pointed out that the meeting decided on the basic guidelines for next year's House of Councillors election.
    The communique also stated that the meeting discussed the "Policy on Aiding Victims of Persecution" reported by Central Committee member Shiga Yoshio. The meeting passed a resolution. The resolution expressed opposition to fascist-style trials, strongly protested illegal treatment in prisons; and pointed out that further efforts to demand the release and aid of victims of fascist persecution are major tasks for the entire Party.
    The communique stated that the meeting also discussed and decided on specific measures to strengthen the leadership of Party organs at all levels over the daily activities of "Akahata," based on the report of Central Committee member Miyamoto Kenji.
    First Secretary Nozaka Sanzo made a concluding speech on the matters discussed and passed at the meeting. The meeting then adjourned.

### 日本共产党中央委员会举行第三次会议

1955-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    日本共产党中央委员会举行第三次会议
    新华社二十九日讯　东京消息：据“赤旗报”报道，日本共产党中央委员会常任干部会十月十九日上午在党总部举行记者招待会，发表了关于日本共产党在十月十三日举行第六次全国代表会议第三次中央委员会全体会议的公报。
    公报说：出席这次会议的有中央委员、候补中央委员和监察委员。
    中央委员志田重男在会上作了“关于第六次全国代表会议决议的讨论情况和党组织建设的报告”。会议根据报告，讨论了全党组织讨论决议的情况和党的统一团结问题。会议确认，党的统一和团结的事业，各地虽有不同，但是基本上是在进行着的。会议决定在完成这个事业的基础上，今后要促进党的建设和统一，加强党同群众之间的联系，各部门要进一步拟订具体方针来完成发展民族解放民主统一战线的任务。中央委员绀野与次郎作了关于“党内各级机关选举规定”的报告。会议根据新党章讨论了地方各级党会议选举领导机关的规定，并且作了决议。
    公报指出，会议决定了明年参议院议员选举的基本方针。
    公报还说，会议讨论了中央委员志贺义雄所报告的“关于援救受迫害者的方针”。会议通过了决议。决议表示反对法西斯式的裁判，严重抗议监狱的非法待遇；并指出，进一步努力要求释放和援救遭受法西斯迫害者是全党的重大任务。
    公报说，会议还根据中央委员宫本显治的报告，讨论和决定了加强党的各级机关对“赤旗报”的日常活动的领导的具体措施等事项。
    第一书记野坂参三就会议讨论和通过的事项作了总结发言。会议随后宣告结束。
