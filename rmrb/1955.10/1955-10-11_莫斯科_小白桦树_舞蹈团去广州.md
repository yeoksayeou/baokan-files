Title: Moscow "Beryozka" Dance Ensemble Heads to Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-10-11
Page: 4

    The Moscow "Beryozka" Dance Ensemble, a group of fifty people, accompanied by Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, left Beijing by special train on the morning of the 10th for Guangzhou. The dance ensemble will perform during the Soviet Economic and Cultural Achievement Exhibition held in Guangzhou.

### 莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团去广州

1955-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团去广州
    苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团一行五十人，由中苏友好协会总会副秘书长李沾吾陪同在十日上午乘专车离北京赴广州。舞蹈团将在苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会在广州举行期间进行演出。
