Title: Iranian King to Visit Soviet Union at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran: TASS reported from Tehran that the Iranian Royal Court Department issued a communique in Tehran on the 3rd, stating: In order to strengthen the friendly relations between Iran and the Soviet Union, the King and Queen, in their reply on July 8th to Marshal Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, had accepted with satisfaction the invitation extended by Marshal Voroshilov on June 20th of this year, and decided to visit the Soviet Union. The communique stated that the King and Queen will visit the Soviet Union between May and June of 1956.

### 伊朗国王将应邀访问苏联

1955-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    伊朗国王将应邀访问苏联
    新华社四日讯据塔斯社德黑兰讯：伊朗国王宫廷部三日在德黑兰发表公报说：为了加强伊朗和苏联两国的友好关系，国王和王后在七月八日给苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫元帅的复信中，已经满意地接受了伏罗希洛夫元帅今年六月二十日提出的邀请，决定访问苏联。公报说，国王和王后将在一九五六年五六月间访问苏联。
