Title: Shenyang City Builds a Waste Gas Greenhouse
Author: Zhang Qingfu
Date: 1955-10-21
Page: 2

    Shenyang City Builds a Waste Gas Greenhouse

    On September 15th, the state-owned vegetable farm in Shenyang City began construction on a 400-square-meter waste gas greenhouse next to the Shenyang City Brewery in the Beiguan District of Shenyang. This greenhouse utilizes old iron pipes to collect the industrial waste gas discharged after production by the brewery and introduce it into the greenhouse to replace coal burning for heating. This not only achieves waste utilization and saves coal and stoker expenses, but also greatly improves production technology. People only need to observe the thermometer and turn the gas inlet switch to control the indoor temperature. Because the waste gas greenhouse has good insulation and a uniform temperature, it will create favorable conditions for increasing vegetable yields.

    According to preliminary calculations, the cost of producing vegetables in this type of industrial waste gas greenhouse is one-third lower than that of ordinary greenhouses. It also opens up new avenues for developing winter greenhouse production in the future. (Zhang Qingfu)

### 沈阳市兴建一座废气温室
张庆福
1955-10-21
第2版()
专栏：

    沈阳市兴建一座废气温室
    九月十五日，沈阳市国营蔬菜农场在沈阳北关区沈阳市酿造厂旁开工修建了一座占地四百平方公尺的废气温室。这座温室是利用旧铁管把酿造厂生产后排出的工业废气，收集起来，引进温室，代替烧煤取暖，这样不仅做到了废物利用，节约了煤炭和烧火工的开支，同时在生产技术上，也大大提高了一步。人们只要观察温度表，扭动输气口的开关，就可以掌握室内的温度。由于废气温室的保温条件好，温度均匀一致，将给蔬菜的提高产量创造了有力条件。
    据初步计算，这种利用工业废气进行温室生产的蔬菜，比一般温室生产的蔬菜要降低成本三分之一，同时也为今后发展冬季温室生产开辟了新的途径。　（张庆福）
