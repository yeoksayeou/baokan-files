Title: Czechoslovak Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate Czechoslovak Army Day
Author:
Date: 1955-10-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 6th. Military Attaché of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, Colonel Dvorak, held a grand reception on the evening of the 6th to celebrate Czechoslovak Army Day.

    Among those present at the reception were Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China; General Tan Zheng and Senior General Xiao Ke, Vice Ministers of National Defense; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei and heads of other ministries and commissions; Vice Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping; Senior Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Chen Geng, Li Kenong, Fu Zhong, Xiao Hua, Air Force Senior General Liu Yalou, Senior General Yang Chengwu, Senior General Wang Ping, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Navy Lieutenant General Luo Shunchu, as well as generals and officers from various departments, services, and arms, combat heroes, and literary and artistic workers from the troops, totaling more than 300 people.

    Heads of diplomatic missions, diplomatic officials, military attachés, acting military attachés, and deputy military attachés from various countries also attended the reception.

    At the reception, Colonel Dvorak said in his speech: "Now our people have an army armed with the latest combat equipment and possessing strong combat capabilities, which is defending peace and our socialist construction." He said that the Czechoslovak People's Army cherishes the friendship forged with the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and this friendship is constantly increasing and consolidating. He said that it is the common task of defending peace that has firmly united the armies of our two countries. In his speech, he also expressed special thanks to the Soviet Union and its invincible Soviet army for their selfless assistance to the Czechoslovak people.

    Marshal Peng Dehuai then delivered a speech, on behalf of all the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extending warm congratulations to the soldiers, officers, and generals of the Czechoslovak People's Army. He said that the Czechoslovak People's Army has made great achievements in all aspects and has become an important force in safeguarding European and world peace. He congratulated the Czechoslovak People's Army on achieving even more brilliant achievements in defending the socialist construction of the motherland and safeguarding the cause of peace.

    During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and President Zápotocký, to the unbreakable friendship between the people and armies of China and Czechoslovakia, to the health of all officers and soldiers of the two armies, and to the ever-stronger combat power and friendship of the armies of the Soviet Union and other people's democracies—the most powerful guarantee for defending peace.

    After the reception, literary and artistic workers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army performed wonderful programs, and a dance party was held.

### 捷驻华武官举行招待会庆祝捷建军节

1955-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    捷驻华武官举行招待会庆祝捷建军节
    新华社六日讯捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆武官杜瑞上校六日晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克建军节。
    出席招待会的，有中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，国防部副部长谭政大将、萧克上将，外交部副部长姬鹏飞和其他各部和各委员会负责人，北京市副市长冯基平，中国人民解放军高级将领陈赓大将、李克农上将、傅钟上将、萧华上将、刘亚楼空军上将、杨成武上将、王平上将、萧向荣中将、罗舜初海军中将、以及各部、各军种、各兵种的将军们和军官们，战斗英雄和部队文艺工作者共三百多人。
    各国驻华使节、外交官员和各国驻华武官、代理武官、副武官也出席了招待会。
    在招待会上，杜瑞上校致词说：“现在我国人民已有了一支以最新式战斗装备武装起来的具有强大战斗力的军队，它正在捍卫着和平和我国的社会主义建设。”他说，捷克斯洛伐克人民军非常珍贵和中国人民解放军所结成的友谊，这种友谊正在不断地增进和巩固。他说，这是保卫和平的共同的任务使我们两国军队巩固地团结了起来。在致词中，他还特别感谢苏联及其不可战胜的苏联军队给予捷克斯洛伐克人民的大公无私的援助。
    彭德怀元帅接着致词，他代表中国人民解放军全体将士向捷克斯洛伐克人民军的士兵、军官和将军们致以热烈的祝贺。他说，捷克斯洛伐克人民军在各方面都已取得巨大的成就，成为保卫欧洲和世界和平的一支重要力量。他祝贺捷克斯洛伐克人民军在保卫祖国的社会主义建设和维护和平的事业中获得更加光辉的成就。
    席间，宾主频频互为毛泽东主席和萨波托斯基总统的健康，为中、捷两国人民和两国军队间的牢不可破的友谊，为两国军队的全体将士们的健康，为苏联和各人民民主国家军队的战斗力和友谊——保卫和平的最有力的保证更加强大而干杯。
    招待会结束后，中国人民解放军文艺工作者表演了精彩节目，并举行了舞会。
