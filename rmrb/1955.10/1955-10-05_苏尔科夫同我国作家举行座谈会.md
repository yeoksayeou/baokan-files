Title: Surkov Holds Symposium with Chinese Writers
Author:
Date: 1955-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Surkov, the head of the Soviet cultural delegation invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to participate in National Day celebrations in our country, held a symposium with Chinese writers at 2:00 PM on the 4th. The symposium was presided over by Mao Dun, Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association. Participating were writers Yang Hansheng, Feng Xuefeng, Cao Jinghua, Li Ji, Yuan Shuipai, Kang Zhuo, Yan Wenjing, Zang Kejia, Ruan Zhangjing, Ge Baoquan, Feng Zhi, Ma Feng, Chen Baichen, Yang Shuo, and responsible editors of several publications of the Chinese Writers Association. At the symposium, Surkov introduced to the Chinese writers the Party spirit of Soviet literature, the ideological education of Soviet writers, how to conduct criticism and self-criticism, and the work and experience of the Soviet Writers Association in organizing writers' creative activities.

On the evening of the 4th, Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and Mao Dun, Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, held a banquet to entertain Surkov and his wife, Surkova.

### 苏尔科夫同我国作家举行座谈会

1955-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    苏尔科夫同我国作家举行座谈会
    新华社四日讯　应中苏友好协会总会邀请来我国参加国庆节观礼的苏联文化代表团团长苏尔科夫在四日下午二时同中国作家们举行了座谈会。座谈会由中国作家协会主席茅盾主持。参加座谈的有作家阳翰笙、冯雪峰、曹靖华、李季、袁水拍、康濯、严文井、臧克家、阮章竞、戈宝权、冯至、马烽、陈白尘、杨朔等和中国作家协会几个刊物的负责编辑人员。在座谈会上，苏尔科夫向中国作家们介绍了苏联文学的党性，苏联作家的思想教育，如何进行批评与自我批评以及苏联作家协会在组织作家创作活动方面的工作和经验等问题。
    四日晚，中国文学艺术界联合会主席郭沫若和中国作家协会主席茅盾举行宴会，招待苏尔科夫和他的夫人苏尔科娃。
