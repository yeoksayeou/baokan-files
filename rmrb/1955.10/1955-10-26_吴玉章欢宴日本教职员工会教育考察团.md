Title: Wu Yuzhang Hosts a Banquet for the Japanese Federation of Teachers and Staff Unions Educational Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. Upon the invitation of the National Committee of the Educational Workers Union of China, the Japanese Federation of Teachers and Staff Unions Educational Delegation, headed by Kobayashi Takeshi, Chairman of the Japanese Federation of Teachers and Staff Unions, arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 25th, totaling thirty-five people.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Educational Workers Union of China, as well as heads of various departments, Ma Chungu, Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, representatives of teachers and staff from middle and primary schools in Beijing, and Japanese individuals in Beijing, Kameda Togo and Kaneko Kenta.

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. Wu Yuzhang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Educational Workers Union of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 25th to welcome the entire Japanese Federation of Teachers and Staff Unions Educational Delegation, headed by Chairman Kobayashi Takeshi.

Attending tonight's banquet were Li Jiebo and Dong Xin, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Liu Zijiu, Qian Duansheng, Fang Ming, and standing committee members, Vice Ministers Liu Aifeng of the Ministry of Higher Education and Liu Shi of the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China, Ma Yinchu, President of Peking University, Jiang Nanxiang, President of Tsinghua University, Hu Xikui, Vice President of Renmin University of China, Hu Zhitao and Tao Shufan, heads of Beijing Experimental Middle and Primary Schools, as well as educational workers from the capital, and Japanese individuals in Beijing, Kameda Togo and Kaneko Kenta.

At the beginning of the banquet, Wu Yuzhang delivered a speech, expressing a warm welcome to the Japanese guests on behalf of the Educational Workers Union of China. He said that the arrival of this delegation in China indicates a further development of the friendship between Chinese and Japanese teachers. You can be assured that you will receive assistance and support from the vast number of Chinese teachers and all aspects during this inspection process.

Kobayashi Takeshi, head of the delegation, then delivered a speech, expressing his gratitude to the hosts for their warm hospitality. He said that Japanese educators believe that education should belong to the people and are determined to fight for truth and for peace. Japanese teachers are determined to make the educated children become children friendly to China and children who love world peace. He said that the delegation hopes to learn from the new China through this visit to China.

During the banquet, the guests repeatedly toasted to the friendship between the people and educational workers of China and Japan, to the health of students and young children of the two countries, and to the peace of the Far East and the world.

This morning, Wu Yuzhang also received all members of the Japanese Federation of Teachers and Staff Unions Educational Delegation. Present at the reception were Liu Zijiu and Fang Ming, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Educational Workers Union of China, and Feng Suhai, Secretary-General.

In the afternoon, Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, also received all members of the delegation. Present at the reception were Li Jiebo, Zhang Weizhen, Li Zaiwen, Dong Xin, Secretaries to the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Educational Workers Union of China.

### 吴玉章欢宴日本教职员工会教育考察团

1955-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    吴玉章欢宴日本教职员工会教育考察团
    新华社二十五日讯　应中国教育工会全国委员会的邀请，以团长、日本教职员工会主席小林武为首的日本教职员工会教育考察团一行三十五人在二十五日晨到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的，有中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明以及各部门的负责人，中华全国总工会国际联络部部长马纯古和北京中、小学教职员代表以及在京的日本人士龟田东伍、金子健太。
    新华社二十五日讯　中国教育工会全国委员会主席吴玉章在二十五日晚举行宴会，欢迎以团长小林武为首的日本教职员工会教育考察团全体人员。
    出席今晚宴会的，有中华全国总工会书记处书记李颉伯、董昕，中国教育工会全国委员会副主席刘子久、钱端升、方明和常务委员，中华人民共和国高等教育部副部长刘皑风、教育部副部长柳湜，北京大学校长马寅初，清华大学校长蒋南翔，中国人民大学副校长胡锡奎，北京实验中学和小学负责人胡志涛、陶淑范，以及首都教育工作者，在京的日本人士龟田东伍、金子健太。
    宴会开始，吴玉章致词，代表中国教育工会，向日本客人表示热烈欢迎。他说，这次考察团来到中国，说明中日两国教师的友谊有了进一步的发展。你们可以相信，在这次进行考察的过程中一定会得到中国广大教师和各方面的协助和支持。
    考察团团长小林武接着致词，对主人的盛情招待表示谢意。他说，日本教育工作者都认为教育应属于人民，并决心为真理，为和平而奋斗。日本教师们将决心使受教育的孩子们，都成为同中国友好的孩子，热爱世界和平的孩子。他说，考察团愿通过这次访问中国的机会向新中国学习。
    席间，宾主频频为中日两国人民和教育工作者的友谊，为两国的学生和青年少年儿童们的健康，为远东和世界的和平而干杯。
    今日上午，吴玉章并接见了日本教职员工会教育考察团全体人员。接见时在座的有中国教育工会全国委员会副主席刘子久、方明，秘书长冯宿海。
    在下午，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚也接见了考察团全体人员。接见时，中华全国总工会书记处书记李颉伯、张维桢、栗再温、董昕和中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明也在座。
