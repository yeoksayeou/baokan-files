    Our Great Motherland
    Our country's film projection network will greatly develop during the First Five-Year Plan. By 1957, the film projection teams affiliated with the cultural departments alone will increase to 5,279 teams. The Hebei Provincial Bureau of Culture has held training classes to cultivate projectionists to meet the needs of the developing film projection industry.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国的电影放映网在第一个五年计划内将有很大发展。到一九五七年，仅文化部门所属电影放映队就将增加到五千二百七十九队。河北省文化局举办了训练班，培养放映人员以适应发展中的电影放映事业的需要。
