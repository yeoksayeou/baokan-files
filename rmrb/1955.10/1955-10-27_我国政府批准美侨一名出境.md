Title: Our Government Approves Exit of an American National

Author:
Date: 1955-10-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 26th. The American citizen, Anna Louise Strong (female), residing in Guangzhou, recently applied to our government for permission to leave the country. Our competent authorities approved her request on October 24th, and she is free to leave China at any time.

### 我国政府批准美侨一名出境

1955-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府批准美侨一名出境
    新华社二十六日讯　居住在广州的美国侨民安若素（女），最近向我国政府申请出境，我主管当局已于十月二十四日批准了她的请求，她随时可以离开中国。
