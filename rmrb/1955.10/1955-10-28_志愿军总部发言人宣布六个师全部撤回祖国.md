Title: Volunteer Army Headquarters Spokesperson Announces All Six Divisions Have Withdrawn to the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1955-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 27th. A spokesperson for the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army announced: From October 10th to 26th, the six divisions of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army have all withdrawn from Korea and returned to the motherland. Thus far, including the seven divisions of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army which withdrew from Korea from September 16th to October 3rd, 1954, and the six divisions which withdrew from Korea from March 31st to April 20th, 1955, a total of nineteen divisions of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army have successively withdrawn from Korea and returned to the motherland.

### 志愿军总部发言人宣布六个师全部撤回祖国

1955-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军总部发言人宣布六个师全部撤回祖国
    新华社平壤二十七日电　中国人民志愿军总部发言人宣布：从十月十日起到二十六日止，中国人民志愿军的六个师已经全部撤离朝鲜返回祖国。至此，连同中国人民志愿军在一九五四年九月十六日到十月三日从朝鲜撤出的七个师和一九五五年三月三十一日到四月二十日从朝鲜撤出的六个师在内，中国人民志愿军共有十九个师已先后撤出朝鲜返回祖国。
