Title: Volunteer Army Leading Organ Holds Grand Meeting to Celebrate National Day

Author: 
Date: 1955-10-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd. The leading organ of the Chinese People's Volunteers held a grand meeting on the afternoon of the 1st, with more than 4,000 people attending, to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Also attending the meeting were the Korean People's Delegation, headed by Chen Ban-soo, Minister of Trade of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and personnel from the Korean National Classical Art Theater. Local Korean people, People's Army troops, and overseas Chinese also sent representatives to the meeting to offer congratulations.

Li Zhimin, Political Commissar of the Chinese People's Volunteers, spoke at the meeting. He called on all officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army, while celebrating the sixth anniversary of the founding of the motherland, to redouble their vigilance and strive to improve their combat effectiveness, continue to consolidate and strengthen the friendship and unity with the Korean people and the Korean People's Army, stand shoulder to shoulder with the heroic Korean People's Army to strictly guard the front, and continue to make unremitting efforts to defend the peace of Korea and the security of the motherland, and strive for a peaceful solution to the Korean issue.

Chen Ban-soo, head of the Korean People's Delegation, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting. Subsequently, amid military music, he presented a flag to the Chinese People's Volunteers on behalf of the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front.

After the meeting, actors from the Korean National Classical Art Theater performed the famous drama "Shim Chong."

### 志愿军领导机关隆重集会庆祝祖国国庆

1955-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军领导机关隆重集会庆祝祖国国庆
    新华社平壤二日电　中国人民志愿军领导机关在一日下午隆重举行了四千多人的庆祝中华人民共和国成立六周年大会。参加大会的还有以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁贸易相陈班秀为首的朝鲜人民代表团和朝鲜国立古典艺术剧场的人员。当地的朝鲜人民、人民军部队和华侨也派代表参加大会祝贺。
    中国人民志愿军政治委员李志民在会上讲了话。他号召志愿军全体官兵在庆祝祖国国庆六周年的时候，要更加百倍地提高警惕和努力提高自己的战斗威力，继续不断地巩固和加强同朝鲜人民和朝鲜人民军的友谊和团结，同英雄的朝鲜人民军并肩严守阵地，为保卫朝鲜的和平和祖国的安全，争取朝鲜问题的和平解决继续进行不懈的努力。
    朝鲜人民代表团团长陈班秀在会上致祝词。随后，他在军乐声中代表朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会向中国人民志愿军赠旗。
    大会结束后，朝鲜国立古典艺术剧场的演员们演出了名剧“沈清传”。
