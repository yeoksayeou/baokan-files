Title: East German Historian Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. According to the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Dr. of Philosophy and Lecturer in General History, Mr. J. Nichtweiss of the Department of Philosophy of Humboldt University, East Germany, arrived in Beijing on the 29th to lecture in China.

### 民主德国历史学家到达北京

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国历史学家到达北京
    新华社三十日讯　根据中德文化合作协定来我国讲学的民主德国洪堡大学哲学系哲学博士、普通历史讲师约·尼赫特魏斯在二十九日到达北京。
