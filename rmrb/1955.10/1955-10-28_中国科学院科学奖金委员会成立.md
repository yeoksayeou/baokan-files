Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Science Award Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1955-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th -- The Chinese Academy of Sciences Science Award Committee was established on the 20th.

The committee is chaired by Guo Moruo; the vice-chairmen are Li Siguang, Liang Xi, and Huang Songling. The thirty-five members are: Shi ZhiRen, Liu Xianzhou, Wu Yuzhang, Wu Youxun, Chen Boda, Su Buqing, Wang Huzhen, Bei Shizhang, Zhou Ren, Zhu Kezhen, Zhou Yang, Zhou Peiyuan, Mao Yisheng, Hu Qiaomu, Hou Debang, Hu Sheng, Tao Menghe, Hua Luogeng, Zhuang Changgong, Zhang Jiafu, Yang Shixian, Huang Jiqing, Zeng Zhaolun, Tong Dizhou, Yang Xianzhen, Feng Depei, Yang Zhongjian, Zhao Zhongyao, Pan Zinian, Qian Sanqiang, Qian Xuesen, Qian Chongshu, Dai Fanglan, Zhong Huilan, and Yan Jici.

According to the Provisional Regulations on Science Awards of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the task of this committee is to make a unified review of the scientific research reports or scientific works selected and approved by the various departments of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and to submit them to the Academic Committee of the Chinese Academy of Sciences for discussion and approval before awarding the prizes.

### 中国科学院科学奖金委员会成立

1955-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院科学奖金委员会成立
    新华社二十七日讯　中国科学院科学奖金委员会在二十日成立。
    委员会由郭沫若任主任委员；副主任委员是李四光、梁希、黄松龄。三十五个委员是：石志仁、刘仙洲、吴玉章、吴有训、陈伯达、苏步青、汪胡桢、贝时璋、周仁、竺可桢、周扬、周培源、茅以升、胡乔木、侯德榜、胡绳、陶孟和、华罗庚、庄长恭、张稼夫、杨石先、黄汲清、曾昭抡、童第周、杨献珍、冯德培、杨锺健、赵忠尧、潘梓年、钱三强、钱学森、钱崇澍、戴芳澜、锺惠澜、严济慈。
    根据中国科学院科学奖金暂行条例的规定，这个委员会的任务是对中国科学院各学部评选、通过的科学研究工作报告或科学著作，作统一的审核，提请中国科学院院务委员会讨论通过后授奖。
