Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Indian Film Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received the entire Indian film delegation led by Chairman P. L. Kapur and Vice Chairman B. N. Sircar at 4:00 PM on the 27th.

Present at the reception were Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Culture Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Zhou Yang, Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture Wang Lanxi, and Deputy Director Cai Chusheng.

Also present at the reception was Bahadur Singh, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy in China.

During the meeting, Chairman P. L. Kapur presented Chairman Mao with a garland. (Image attached)

### 毛泽东主席接见印度电影代表团

1955-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见印度电影代表团
    新华社二十七日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东在二十七日下午四时接见了以团长普·拉·卡普尔和副团长布·纳·西尔卡为首的印度电影代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈毅，文化部副部长郑振铎，中国文学艺术界联合会副主席周扬，文化部电影事业管理局局长王阑西、副局长蔡楚生。
    接见时，印度驻华大使馆临时代办巴哈杜尔·辛格也在座。
    会见时，团长普·拉·卡普尔向毛主席献了花环。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席同印度电影代表团团长普·拉·卡普尔（左）谈话。吕厚民摄（新华社稿）
