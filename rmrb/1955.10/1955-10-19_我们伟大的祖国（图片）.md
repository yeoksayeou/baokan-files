Our Great Motherland
Hemp seeds have reached the ripening season. In the Qianjin Agricultural Production Cooperative of Boyan Village, Wu'an County, Hebei Province, the hemp seeds planted along the edges of the fields and slopes are growing full and plump. The members are picking them with joy.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大麻子已到了成熟季节。河北省武安县伯延村前进农业生产合作社利用地边、坡沿点种的大麻子长得颗粒饱满，社员们怀着喜悦的心情正在采摘。
