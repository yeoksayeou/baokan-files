    Our Great Motherland
    In order to develop tropical economic crops, the state has established the first batch of state-owned mechanical farms in Hainan Island, Leizhou Peninsula, and Guangxi. Workers at the Fushan Mechanical Farm in Hainan Island are picking coffee in the lush coffee plantation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了发展热带的经济作物，国家在海南岛、雷州半岛和广西建立了第一批国营机械农场。海南岛福山机械农场的工人正在茂盛的咖啡园内采摘咖啡。
