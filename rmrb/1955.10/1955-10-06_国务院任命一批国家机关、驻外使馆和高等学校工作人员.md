Title: State Council Appoints Personnel to State Organs, Embassies Abroad, and Institutions of Higher Learning
Author:
Date: 1955-10-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    The State Council of the People's Republic of China issued an order on September 16, appointing a batch of personnel to state organs, embassies abroad, and institutions of higher learning. The list is as follows:

    Gu Mu is appointed Deputy Director of the Third Office of the State Council;

    Li Chaobo and Tao Ran are appointed Deputy Directors of the State Statistical Bureau;

    Cui Guang is appointed Deputy Governor of the People's Bank of China;

    Liu Zhuofu is appointed Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agricultural Products Procurement, and Zhao Fasheng is appointed Assistant Minister;

    Zhang Yingwu is appointed Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics;

    Wang Jiefu is appointed Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Hungarian People's Republic, Mo Chenggui is appointed Commercial Counselor, and Lin Ye is appointed Cultural Counselor;

    Gao Shirong is appointed Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Czechoslovak Republic;

    Wang Runsheng is appointed Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the German Democratic Republic; Ye Ke is appointed Cultural Counselor;

    Fu Hao is appointed Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of India, and Lin Lin is appointed Cultural Counselor;

    Cheng Zhiping is appointed Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Union of Burma;

    Wang Ze is appointed Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Lashio, Union of Burma;

    Sima Wensen is appointed Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Indonesia;

    Xu Zhongfu is appointed Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Pakistan;

    Zang Junyu is appointed Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Kingdom of Norway;

    Liang Shangyuan is appointed Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Kingdom of Afghanistan;

    Li Zhuozhi is appointed Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia;

    Li Enqiu and He Wushuang are appointed Vice Presidents of the Foreign Affairs College;

    Chen Yifan is appointed Vice President of Beijing Mining College;

    Chen Yeping is appointed Vice President of Nanjing Agricultural College;

    Liang Tangjin is appointed President of Guangxi Normal College, and Huang Yu is appointed Vice President.

### 国务院任命一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校工作人员

1955-10-06
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院任命一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校工作人员
    中华人民共和国国务院九月十六日发布命令，任命了一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校工作人员。名单如下：
    谷牧为国务院第三办公室副主任；
    李超伯、陶然为国家统计局副局长；
    崔光为中国人民银行副行长；
    刘卓甫为农产品采购部副部长，赵发生为部长助理；
    张映吾为中华人民共和国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟大使馆文化参赞；
    王介福为中华人民共和国驻匈牙利人民共和国大使馆参赞，莫承贵为商务参赞，林耶为文化参赞；
    高仕融为中华人民共和国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使馆商务参赞；
    王润生为中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆商务参赞；叶克为文化参赞；
    符浩为中华人民共和国驻印度共和国大使馆参赞，林林为文化参赞；
    程之平为中华人民共和国驻缅甸联邦大使馆文化参赞；
    王泽为中华人民共和国驻缅甸联邦腊戌总领事；
    司马文森为中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国大使馆文化参赞；
    徐中夫为中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦大使馆文化参赞；
    臧君宇为中华人民共和国驻挪威王国大使馆参赞；
    梁上苑为中华人民共和国驻阿富汗王国大使馆文化参赞；
    李琢之为中华人民共和国驻南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国大使馆商务参赞；
    李恩求、何戊双为外交学院副院长；
    陈一凡为北京矿业学院副院长；
    陈野萍为南京农学院副院长；
    梁唐晋为广西师范学院院长，黄羽为副院长。（新华社）
