Title: Indian Health Minister Mrs. Kaur Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Mrs. Rajkumari Amrit Kaur, the Minister of Health of the Republic of India, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon from Guangzhou. She is visiting China at the invitation of Health Minister Li Dequan to attend the National Day celebrations and for a tour.
    Mrs. Kaur embraced Li Dequan after getting off the plane. They had met in India, so they were particularly close. Then, she shook hands with Vice Minister of Health Cui Yitian, Vice President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Xu Guangping, Vice President and Secretary-General of the Chinese Red Cross Society Hu Lansheng, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Chen Hansheng, representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others who came to welcome her. Representatives of the Overseas Chinese Returnees Visiting Group from India also came to the airport to welcome her.
    The Indian Ambassador to China, Mr. and Mrs. Raghunath, and embassy staff were also at the airport to welcome her.
    In the evening, Mrs. Kaur attended a welcome banquet hosted by Li Dequan.

### 印度卫生部长考尔夫人到京

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    印度卫生部长考尔夫人到京
    新华社三十日讯　印度共和国卫生部长拉·阿·考尔夫人今日下午自广州乘飞机到达北京。她是应卫生部长李德全的邀请来中国参加国庆节观礼和参观访问的。
    考尔夫人下飞机后，就和李德全拥抱。她们曾在印度相识，所以分外亲密。接着，她同来欢迎她的卫生部副部长崔义田、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平、中国红十字会总会副会长兼秘书长胡兰生、中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙、外交部代表等一一握手。印度华侨归国观光团代表也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有印度驻中国大使赖嘉文夫妇和使馆人员。
    晚上，考尔夫人出席了李德全的欢迎宴会。
