Title: Indonesian General Election Vote Counting Underway
Author:
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 30th - Following the voting in Indonesia's first general election on September 29th, vote counting is now underway. According to Mrs. Pudjobuntoro, chairwoman of the Jakarta District Election Committee, who announced on the 30th, the Jakarta District Election Committee will hold a public gathering on October 8th to announce the voting results for the Jakarta electoral district. She said that, according to rough estimates, 75 percent of Jakarta voters participated in the voting on September 29th.

According to a report by the Indonesian News Agency on the 30th, in the total votes counted from 445 polling stations in Jakarta, accounting for approximately one-third of all polling stations, the Masyumi Party received over 91,900 votes, the Indonesian Nationalist Party received over 59,100 votes, the Indonesian Communist Party received over 47,000 votes, the Nahdatul Ulama received over 40,900 votes, and the Indonesian Socialist Party received over 14,000 votes.

According to a report in the "Independent" newspaper on the 30th, the Indonesian Communist Party is leading in the election in Semarang, Central Java. According to preliminary results from 59 of the 190 polling stations in Semarang, the Indonesian Communist Party received over 27,000 votes, the Nahdatul Ulama received 5,300 votes, the Indonesian Nationalist Party received 4,800 votes, the Masyumi Party received 1,900 votes, and the Indonesian Socialist Party received 300 votes.

According to a report in the "Indonesian Torch" newspaper on the 30th, the Indonesian Nationalist Party is leading in other areas of Central Java outside of Semarang.

### 印度尼西亚普选计票工作正在进行

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚普选计票工作正在进行
    新华社雅加达三十日电　印度尼西亚第一次普选在九月二十九日举行投票后，现在正在进行计票工作。据雅加达区选举委员会主席普佐邦托罗夫人三十日宣布，雅加达区选举委员会将在十月八日举行公众集会宣布雅加达选区的投票结果。她说，据粗略估计，雅加达选民中有百分之七十五在九月二十九日参加了投票。
    据印度尼西亚新闻社三十日报道，在雅加达的约占全部投票站三分之一的四百四十五个投票站的总票数中，马斯友美党得九万一千九百多票，印度尼西亚国民党得五万九千一百多票，印度尼西亚共产党得四万七千多票，印度尼西亚回教师联合会得四万零九百多票，印度尼西亚社会党得一万四千多票。
    据“独立报”三十日报道，印度尼西亚共产党在中爪哇三宝垄的选举中领先。据三宝垄一百九十个投票站中的五十九个投票站计票初步结果，印度尼西亚共产党得两万七千多票，印度尼西亚回教师联合会得五千三百票，印度尼西亚国民党得四千八百票，马斯友美党得一千九百票，印度尼西亚社会党得三百票。
    据“印度尼西亚火炬报”三十日报道，印度尼西亚国民党在除三宝垄以外的中爪哇其他地区领先。
