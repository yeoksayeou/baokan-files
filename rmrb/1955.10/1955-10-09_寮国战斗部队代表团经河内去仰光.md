Title: Laotian Fighting Forces Delegation Passes Through Hanoi to Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1955-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 8th: The delegation of the Laotian Fighting Forces, led by Prince Souphanouvong, flew to Rangoon, Burma, and passed through Hanoi at 10:30 AM on October 8th. They will meet with the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, Katay Don Sasorith, in Rangoon to discuss how to fully implement the issues related to both parties as stipulated in the Geneva Accords. This meeting between the two sides was actively arranged with the help of the International Commission for Laos.

The delegation led by Souphanouvong consisted of Phoumi Vongvichit, Nouhak Phoumsavan, and four staff members.

Greeting them at Hanoi Airport were Phan Ke Toai, Vice Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Hung, Minister of the Premier's Office, Ta Quang Buu, Deputy Minister of National Defense, Le Dinh Tham and Cao Chao Phat, representatives of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee, and representatives from all walks of life in Hanoi.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Ha Van Lau, head of the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army to the International Commission for Vietnam, and representatives of the International Commission for Vietnam.

Souphanouvong delivered a written statement at the airport. He paid tribute to the Vietnamese people, the people of Hanoi, and President Ho Chi Minh, and thanked the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Vietnamese people for their sincere welcome.

During his stay in Hanoi, Souphanouvong paid a visit to Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

In the afternoon of the same day, Souphanouvong and his party left Hanoi and continued their journey to Rangoon.

### 寮国战斗部队代表团经河内去仰光

1955-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    寮国战斗部队代表团经河内去仰光
    新华社河内八日电　寮国战斗部队领袖苏发努冯亲王率领的代表团乘飞机去缅甸仰光途中在十月八日上午十时半路过河内。他们将在仰光和老挝王国首相克特会晤以讨论解决如何彻底履行日内瓦协议所规定的有关双方的问题。这次双方会晤是由老挝国际委员会积极帮助安排的。
    苏发努冯率领的代表团是由富米·冯·维希、诺哈·冯沙万和四个工作人员组成的。
    到河内机场欢迎的有越南民主共和国副总理潘继遂、总理府部长范雄、国防部副部长谢光宝、越南祖国战线的代表黎廷探和高朝发、河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴以及河内市各界代表。
    到机场欢迎的还有越南人民军总司令部驻越南国际委员会联络代表团团长何文楼和越南国际委员会的代表。
    苏发努冯在机场上发表了书面谈话。他向越南人民和河内人民以及胡志明主席致敬，并且感谢越南民主共和国政府和越南人民对他的真挚欢迎。
    苏发努冯在停留河内期间，曾经拜会了越南民主共和国主席胡志明。
    当天下午，苏发努冯一行离开河内继续起程前往仰光。
