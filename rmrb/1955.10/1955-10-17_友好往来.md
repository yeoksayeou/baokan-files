Friendly Exchanges

Bulgarian Scientific and Technical Delegation Arrives in Beijing

The first meeting of the Sino-Bulgarian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technical Cooperation is about to be held in Beijing. The Bulgarian scientific and technical delegation of four members, who will participate in the meeting, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 15th. The head of the delegation is Lyu. Vo. Kazakov, Vice-Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the People's Republic of Bulgaria. The members include: Dr. Galabov, Deputy Minister of Health and Social Welfare of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Va. S. Balevsky, Director of the Porcelain and Glass Bureau of the Ministry of Light Industry and Food Industry, and Radi Radjev, Director of the Scientific and Technical Cooperation Department of the State Planning Committee.

Australian Peace Activist Dobbin Arrives in Beijing

Dr. Alex Dobbin, representative to the Helsinki World Peace Conference from Australia, invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and his wife arrived in Beijing on the 15th.

Chinese Trade Union Delegation Visits Slovenia

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions delegation, invited to visit Yugoslavia, arrived in the Republic of Slovenia for a visit accompanied by Vivoda, Secretary of the Central Council Secretariat of the Yugoslav Trade Union Confederation.

The delegation visited the famous agricultural area of the Vojevodina Plain in Yugoslavia, where they visited the agricultural exposition in Novi Sad and the "Jasena" state farm.

Japanese Delegation of the National Council for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union Returns Home

The Japanese delegation of the National Council for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union, headed by Fusanosuke Kuhara, who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, returned home by plane on the morning of the 15th.

Ceylon Government Trade Delegation Returns Home

The Ceylon government trade delegation of eight members, headed by Shirley Corea, Minister of Trade, Commerce and Fisheries of Ceylon, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 15th to return home (another member of the delegation, Amit, had already returned home).

Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies Delegation Returns Home

The delegation of the Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies, invited by the Red Cross Society of China to participate in the National Day celebrations and visit China, returned to Moscow by plane from Beijing on the morning of the 14th.

Soviet Trade Union Sports Delegation Returns Home

The Soviet Trade Union Sports Delegation, including A.V. Vasilyev, etc., a group of six people, who were invited to China to participate in the First National Workers' Sports Games, left Beijing by plane on the 16th to return home.

Burmese Buddhist Delegation Returns Home

The Burmese Buddhist delegation, led by U Htin Maung, the head of delegation, and U Htin Daung, the deputy head of delegation, escorted the Buddha's tooth relic on a special Burmese plane from Guangzhou on the morning of the 15th and returned home. The special envoy of the Buddhist Association of China accompanied the delegation to Yangon to escort the Buddha's tooth relic.

Romanian Musicians Return Home

Romanian musicians Florescu Arta, V. Anna, and Pop Dumitru, who came to China for performances according to the 1955 implementation plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 14th to return home after a month and a half of visit and performance in China.

Belgian Women's Delegation Returns Home

The Belgian Women's Delegation, headed by Francine Lina, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 14th to return home.

Director of the Argentine "Tribune" Returns Home

Luis Goldman, director of the Argentine "Tribune", who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, visited Beijing, Northeast China, and East China, and left Beijing on the 15th to return home.

(Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1955-10-17
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    保加利亚科学技术代表团到京
    中保科学技术合作联合委员会第一届会议即将在北京召开，参加会议的保加利亚科学技术代表团一行四人已在十五日乘飞机抵京。代表团团长是保加利亚人民共和国国家计划委员会副主席柳·沃·卡扎科夫，团员中有：保加利亚人民共和国卫生与社会福利部副部长加拉保夫博士、轻工业及食品工业部瓷器玻璃局局长瓦·斯·巴列夫斯基、国家计划委员会科学技术合作处处长拉季·拉杰夫。
    澳大利亚和平人士多宾到京
    应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国访问的澳大利亚出席赫尔辛基世界和平大会代表、医生亚历克斯·多宾和他的夫人在十五日到达北京。
    中国工会代表团在斯洛文尼亚参观
    应邀访问南斯拉夫的中华全国总工会代表团在南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会书记处书记维沃达陪同下，到达斯洛文尼亚共和国参观访问。
    代表团访问了南斯拉夫著名的农业区伏也央金纳大平原，在那里参观了诺维萨特市的农业博览会，访问了“浅那”国营农场。
    日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议访华团回国
    应中国人民外交学会邀请来中国访问的以久原房之助为首的日本恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议访华团，在十五日晨乘飞机返国。
    锡兰政府贸易代表团回国
    以锡兰贸易、商务和渔业部部长秀莱·科里亚为首的锡兰政府贸易代表团一行八人在十五日晨乘机离京回国（代表团另一团员阿米特已先行回国）。
    苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会代表团回国
    应中国红十字会邀请前来中国参加国庆节观礼和访问的苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会代表团，已在十四日早晨由北京乘飞机返莫斯科。
    苏联工会体育代表团回国
    应邀来我国参加全国第一届工人体育运动大会的苏联工会体育代表团阿·维·华西里耶夫等一行六人，在十六日乘飞机离京回国。
    缅甸联邦佛教代表团回国
    缅甸联邦佛教代表团由团长吴登貌、副团长吴千吞率领，在十五日早晨奉迎着佛牙离开广州乘缅甸专机回国。中国佛教协会特派佛牙护送团陪同代表团前往仰光。
    罗马尼亚音乐家回国
    根据中罗文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来中国访问演出的罗马尼亚音乐家弗洛雷斯库·阿尔塔、维·安娜和波普·杜米特鲁，在中国进行了一个半月的访问演出后，十四日晨乘飞机离北京返国。
    比利时妇女代表团回国
    以弗朗西妮·丽娜为首的比利时妇女代表团，在十四日上午乘飞机离开北京返国。
    阿根廷“论坛报”社长回国
    应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷“论坛报”社长路易斯·戈尔德曼在北京和东北、华东等地参观后，已在十五日离京回国。
                            （据新华社讯）
