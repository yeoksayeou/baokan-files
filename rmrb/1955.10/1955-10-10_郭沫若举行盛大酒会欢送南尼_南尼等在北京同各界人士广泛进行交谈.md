Title: Guo Moruo Holds Grand Reception to Bid Farewell to Nenni; Nenni and Others Conduct Extensive Talks with People from All Walks of Life in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, held a grand reception in Beijing on the evening of the 9th to bid farewell to Pietro Nenni, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, Chairman of the Italian National Peace Council, Secretary-General of the Italian Socialist Party, and his wife, his daughter Luciana Tomassi, Panieri, a member of the Executive Committee of the Italian Socialist Party, and Onsanelli, Secretary of the Italian National Food Workers' Union, who are about to leave Beijing.

At the reception, Guo Moruo presented Nenni and others with gifts such as ivory carvings and Qi Baishi's flower paintings.

Among those present at the reception were Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Zhang Bojun, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Youyu and Wang Kunlun, Vice Mayors of Beijing, also attended the reception.

Also present at the reception were Xing Xiping, Shao Lizi, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Naiqi, Xu Baoju, and Luo Longji, standing committee members of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General, Cai Tingkai, Hu Yuzhi, Li Yimang, Qian Duansheng, Chen Hansheng, and Hou Debang, members of the World Peace Council, Mei Gongbin, Shi Liang, Sun Qimeng, Xu Guangping, Huang Qixiang, Yan Xichun, Xu Deheng, and Li Chunqing, heads of various democratic parties, Zhu Xuefan, Li Dequan, Wu Han, Zhang Xiruo, Yang Hansheng, and Lei Renmin, heads of various people's organizations, as well as people from the capital's science, education, political and legal, literary and art, trade, news, and religious circles, and Japanese peace activists in Beijing.

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Pietro Nenni, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, Chairman of the Italian National Peace Council, Secretary-General of the Italian Socialist Party, and others have been conducting extensive talks with people from all walks of life in Beijing in recent days.

On the 8th, Nenni and Onsanelli, Secretary of the Italian National Food Workers' Union, visited the State Council of the People's Republic of China and held talks on religious affairs with Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, He Chengxiang, Director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs of the State Council, and Xu Ying, Deputy Director. Also present at the talks were Li Junwu, Deputy Bishop of the Beijing Diocese of the Catholic Church, and Jiang Yizhen, Vice-Chairman of the Yanjing Union Theological Seminary of the Christian Church.

On the 5th, Nenni also visited the Ministry of Foreign Trade and discussed the development of Sino-Italian trade with Vice Minister Lei Renmin, Assistant Minister Lu Xuzhang, and Chen Ming, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau; on the 7th, he visited the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and discussed matters relating to Italian nationals in China with Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu, Huang Hua, Director of the Department of Western European and African Affairs, and Qin Lizhen, Deputy Director of the Consular Department.

Prior to this, Nenni and his wife, his daughter Luciana Tomassi, Panieri, a member of the Executive Committee of the Italian Socialist Party, and Onsanelli, Secretary of the Italian National Food Workers' Union, visited the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and held talks with Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairman, and Li Jiebo and Dong Xin, Secretaries of the Secretariat. Panieri also held talks with Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, and Chen Baichen, Secretary-General of the Chinese Writers Association, on Sino-Italian cultural exchanges.

In recent days, they have also visited the Lugou Bridge, the Forbidden City, the High School Affiliated to Beijing Normal University, agricultural producers' cooperatives, cotton textile mills, the Central Institute for Nationalities, Catholic churches, the Yellow River harnessing exhibition, and the Motherland Resources Exhibition, and toured scenic spots such as Beihai Park, the Temple of Heaven, and the Summer Palace.

### 郭沫若举行盛大酒会欢送南尼  南尼等在北京同各界人士广泛进行交谈

1955-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若举行盛大酒会欢送南尼
    南尼等在北京同各界人士广泛进行交谈
    新华社九日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若九日晚在北京举行盛大酒会，欢送即将离开北京的世界和平理事会副主席、意大利全国和平理事会主席、意大利社会党总书记南尼和他的夫人、他的女儿卢契安娜·托马西、意大利社会党执行委员潘齐埃里和意大利全国食品工会书记昂萨奈利。
    酒会上，郭沫若向南尼等赠送象牙雕刻及齐白石的花卉画等礼品。
    出席酒会的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧。
    国务院秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长章汉夫，北京市副市长张友渔、王崑崙也出席了酒会。
    出席酒会的，还有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员邢西萍、邵力子、马寅初、章乃器、许宝驹、罗隆基，秘书长刘贯一，世界和平理事会理事蔡廷锴、胡愈之、李一氓、钱端升、陈翰笙、侯德榜，各民主党派负责人梅龚彬、史良、孙起孟、许广平、黄琪翔、严希纯、许德珩、李纯青，各人民团体负责人朱学范、李德全、吴晗、张奚若、阳翰笙、雷任民，以及首都科学、教育、政法、文艺、贸易、新闻、宗教界人士和在京的日本和平人士。
    新华社九日讯　世界和平理事会副主席、意大利全国和平理事会主席、意大利社会党总书记南尼等连日来在京同中国各界人士广泛进行交谈。
    八日，南尼和意大利全国食品工会书记昂萨奈利访问了中华人民共和国国务院，同国务院秘书长习仲勋，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘、副局长徐盈等，就宗教事务问题进行了交谈。交谈时在座的还有天主教北京教区副主教李君武和基督教燕京协和神学院副主席蒋翼振。
    南尼在五日还访问了对外贸易部，同副部长雷任民、部长助理卢绪章、第三局副局长陈明等交谈了发展中意贸易问题；在七日访问了外交部，同副部长章汉夫、西欧非洲司司长黄华、领事司副司长秦力真等交谈了有关在中国的意大利侨民的事宜。
    在此以前，南尼和他的夫人、他的女儿卢契安娜·托马西、意大利社会党执行委员潘齐埃里、意大利全国食品工会书记昂萨奈利，曾访问了中华全国总工会，同中华全国总工会主席赖若愚、副主席朱学范，书记处书记李颉伯、董昕等交谈。潘齐埃里还同文化部副部长夏衍、中国作家协会秘书长陈白尘就中意文化交流进行了交谈。
    连日来，他们还参观了芦沟桥、故宫、北京师范大学附中、农业生产合作社、棉纺织厂、中央民族学院、天主教堂、治理黄河展览会和祖国资源展览会，游览了北海公园、天坛、颐和园等名胜。
