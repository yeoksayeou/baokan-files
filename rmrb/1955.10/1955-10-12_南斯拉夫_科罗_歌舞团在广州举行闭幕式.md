Title: Yugoslav "Kolo" Dance Troupe Holds Closing Ceremony in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-10-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 11th: The Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Dance Troupe, which has been visiting and performing in our country for a month and a half, held its closing ceremony in Guangzhou on the evening of the 10th.

    The closing ceremony was held in Guangzhou's Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall. In the center of the sky-blue curtain on the stage hung the national flags of China and Yugoslavia. Attending the closing ceremony and watching the performance were He Ximing, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province; Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou; Ding Bo, Director of the Guangzhou Municipal Bureau of Culture; the Soviet and Polish consuls in Guangzhou; and more than 5,000 workers, students, and people from the literary and art circles of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou.

    Zhou Weizhi, Deputy Director of the Arts Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, also attended the closing ceremony.

    At the beginning of the closing ceremony, Zhu Guang first delivered a speech on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, saying: Through the excellent performances of the Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Dance Troupe in China, we have gained a better understanding of the struggles and lives of the Yugoslav people, and the friendly unity between the Chinese and Yugoslav people has grown stronger. May the cultural exchanges between the two countries become closer. Zhu Guang once again thanked the dance troupe for its performances in China on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China.

    The head of the dance troupe, Skovran, spoke to warm applause, expressing her gratitude for the care and hospitality the dance troupe received in China, and finally expressing her willingness to work together to enhance mutual understanding, friendly cooperation, and cultural exchange between the people of China and Yugoslavia.

    After the closing ceremony, the dance troupe gave a wonderful performance, and each of their programs was warmly welcomed by the audience. Finally, literary and art workers from Guangzhou presented flowers to all members of the dance troupe to express their gratitude. He Ximing and Zhu Guang went backstage to express their condolences and congratulations to the dance troupe members.

### 南斯拉夫“科罗”歌舞团在广州举行闭幕式

1955-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫“科罗”歌舞团在广州举行闭幕式
    新华社广州十一日电　来我国访问演出了一个半月的南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团，十日晚上在广州举行闭幕式。
    闭幕式在广州中山纪念堂举行。舞台上天蓝色的帷幕中间悬挂着中南两国的国旗。出席闭幕式并观看演出的，有广东省副省长贺希明，广州市市长朱光，广州市文化局局长丁波，苏联和波兰驻广州的领事，以及广东省、广州市的工人、学生和文艺界人士等共五千多人。
    中华人民共和国文化部艺术事业管理局副局长周巍峙也出席了闭幕式。
    闭幕式开始，朱光首先代表中华人民共和国文化部致词，他说：通过南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团在中国的卓越演出，使我们对南斯拉夫人民的斗争和生活有了更进一步的了解，使中南两国人民的友好团结更加增长。愿两国之间的文化交流更加密切。朱光并再次地代表中华人民共和国文化部感谢歌舞团在中国的演出。
    歌舞团团长斯科夫兰在热烈的掌声中讲话，她对歌舞团在中国受到的关怀和款待表示感谢，最后并表示要为增进中南两国人民之间的相互了解、友好合作和文化交流而共同努力。
    闭幕式以后，歌舞团作了精采的演出，他们的每一个节目都受到观众热烈的欢迎。最后，广州市的文艺工作者向歌舞团全体人员献花致谢。贺希明和朱光到后台向歌舞团人员表示慰问和祝贺。
