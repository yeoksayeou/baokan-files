Title: Indonesian Parliament Foreign Affairs Committee Members Criticize Western Countries for Interfering in Indonesian Cabinet Formation

Author: 
Date: 1955-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, October 10th - According to a report in the Indonesian "People's Daily" on the 9th, members of the Indonesian Parliament's Foreign Affairs Subcommittee criticized Western countries such as the United States and the Netherlands for expressing negative reactions to the preliminary results of the Indonesian general election and for discussing the formation of various coalition cabinets in Indonesia that exclude the Indonesian Communist Party. These individuals stated that this signifies interference by the United States and the Netherlands in Indonesia, and will certainly be opposed by the Indonesian people, who will decide for themselves what kind of coalition cabinet they need.

### 印度尼西亚国会外交委员会人士  抨击西方国家干预印度尼西亚组阁问题

1955-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会外交委员会人士
    抨击西方国家干预印度尼西亚组阁问题
    新华社雅加达十日电　据印度尼西亚“人民日报”九日报道，印度尼西亚国会外交小组委员会人士抨击美、荷等西方国家对印度尼西亚大选的初步的结果发表消极性的反响并且谈论关于印度尼西亚组成排除印度尼西亚共产党在外的各种形色的联合内阁的问题。这些人士说，这意味着美国和荷兰对印度尼西亚的干涉，并且一定会遭到印度尼西亚人民的反对，印度尼西亚人民将自己决定他们需要什么样的联合内阁。
