Title: Britain Protests Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes' Attack on British Freighter
Author:
Date: 1955-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. According to a report from Taipei by a United Press reporter, the British consul in Taiwan lodged a protest with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique on the 5th regarding the Chiang bandit planes' attack on the British freighter "Deigo" near Shantou Port, China, on September 30th.

### 英国抗议蒋贼飞机袭击英国货船

1955-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    英国抗议蒋贼飞机袭击英国货船
    新华社六日讯据美联社记者自台北报道，英国驻台湾领事五日就蒋贼飞机在九月三十日在中国汕头港附近袭击英国货船“德高号”一事向蒋介石卖国集团提出了抗议。
