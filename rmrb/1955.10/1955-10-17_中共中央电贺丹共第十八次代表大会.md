Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulations to the 18th Congress of the Communist Party of Denmark
Author:
Date: 1955-10-17
Page: 4

    CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulations to the 18th Congress of the Communist Party of Denmark

    (Reported by this newspaper) The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sends a congratulatory telegram to the 18th Congress of the Communist Party of Denmark.

    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Denmark, and through them, to all comrades of the 18th Congress of the Communist Party of Denmark:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm, fraternal congratulations to the 18th Congress of the Communist Party of Denmark!

    We sincerely wish the Communist Party of Denmark new achievements in strengthening the united action of the Danish working class, uniting all democratic forces, and struggling to defend European and world peace, and to protect the vital interests of the Danish working class and working people. We wish the congress success.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
            October 14, 1955

### 中共中央电贺丹共第十八次代表大会

1955-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺丹共第十八次代表大会
    本报讯　中国共产党中央委员会给丹麦共产党第十八次代表大会的贺电。丹麦共产党中央委员会并转丹麦共产党第十八次代表大会全体同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向丹麦共产党第十八次代表大会致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺！
    我们谨祝丹麦共产党在加强丹麦工人阶级的一致行动，并团结一切民主力量，为保卫欧洲及世界和平、为保护丹麦工人阶级和劳动人民的切身利益的斗争中，获得新的成就。祝贺大会获得成功。中国共产党中央委员会
            一九五五年十月十四日
