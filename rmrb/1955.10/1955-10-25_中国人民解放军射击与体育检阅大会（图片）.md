Title: Chinese People's Liberation Army Shooting and Sports Review Conference (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1955-10-25
Page: 2

    Chinese People's Liberation Army Shooting and Sports Review Conference
    The various competitions of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Shooting and Sports Review Conference concluded on the 24th.

    The conference reviewed the results of the 1955 all-army shooting and sports training, which will greatly promote the all-army shooting and sports movement, enhance physical fitness, improve shooting skills, and strengthen the regularization and modernization of our army's national defense construction in the future.

    The following photos, unless otherwise credited, were taken by our reporters Gao Liang and Wang Zhiyuan.

    Chairman Mao Zedong and other party and state leaders on the rostrum of the conference. From left to right: Peng Dehuai, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi. Photo by Lü Houmin

    A scene from the soccer match.

    The venue of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Shooting and Sports Review Conference.

    Excellent shooters from various units of the People's Liberation Army and outstanding shooters from the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, who were invited to participate in the conference, exchanged experiences on the shooting range.

    Units such as the Navy, Air Force, Armored Forces, and Cavalry were invited to give wonderful performances such as pole climbing, tiger crawling, motorcycles, and equestrianism at the conference. This is a scene of war horses jumping over obstacles.

    Light machine gun shooting competition.

    A scene from the 1000-meter race.

### 中国人民解放军射击与体育检阅大会（图片）

1955-10-25
第2版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军射击与体育检阅大会
    中国人民解放军射击与体育检阅大会的各项比赛已在二十四日全部结束。
    大会检阅了一九五五年全军射击和体育训练的成果，对今后全军射击和体育运动，增强体质锻炼，提高射击技术，加强我军正规化现代化国防建设有很大推动作用。
    本组照片除署名者外，均为本报记者　高粮　王志渊摄
    毛泽东主席同党和国家的领导人员在大会主席台上。自左至右：彭德怀、周恩来、朱德、毛泽东、刘少奇。　吕厚民摄
    足球比赛的一个场面。
    中国人民解放军射击与体育检阅大会会场。
    解放军各部队的优秀射手们和应邀参加大会的中国人民志愿军的优秀射手在射击场上交流经验。
    海军、空军、装甲兵、骑兵等单位应邀在大会上做了爬杆、虎伏、摩托、马术等精彩表演。这是战马跳越障碍物的情形。
