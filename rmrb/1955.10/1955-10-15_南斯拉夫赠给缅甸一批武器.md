Title: Yugoslavia Presents a Batch of Weapons to Burma
Author:
Date: 1955-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 14th. According to a report from the Yugoslav News Agency in Rangoon, the Yugoslav Ambassador to Burma, Krista Brajic, handed over to the Burmese Minister of Defense, U Ba Swe, on the 12th a batch of equipment for equipping a Burmese army brigade. These weapons were a gift from Yugoslav President Tito to the Burmese government during his visit to Burma in January 1955.

### 南斯拉夫赠给缅甸一批武器

1955-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫赠给缅甸一批武器
    新华社布拉格十四日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社仰光消息，南斯拉夫驻缅甸大使克里斯塔·布拉伊克十二日交给缅甸国防部长吴巴瑞一批供装备缅甸陆军一个旅用的装备。这批武器是南斯拉夫总统铁托在一九五五年一月间访问缅甸时决定赠给缅甸政府的礼物。
