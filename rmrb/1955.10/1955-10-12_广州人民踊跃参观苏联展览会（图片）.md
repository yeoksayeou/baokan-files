Guangzhou People Enthusiastically Visit the Soviet Exhibition

The Soviet Economic and Cultural Achievement Exhibition opened on October 5th in Guangzhou's newly built Sino-Soviet Friendship Building. The exhibition features a total of more than 11,700 exhibits. These exhibits demonstrate the glorious achievements of the Soviet Union's economic and cultural construction over the past thirty years and the incomparable superiority of the Soviet socialist system.

The people of Guangzhou welcomed the opening of the Soviet Exhibition with great interest. People enthusiastically visited the exhibition.

(All photos in this group are provided by Southern Daily, except where noted.)

Academician Yudin, the Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, He Ximing, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, and Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, attended the opening ceremony of the exhibition. The picture below shows Mayor Zhu Guang cutting the ribbon. Photo by Mo Pinli

Representatives of the Second Guangdong Provincial Agricultural Implement Work Conference visit the hydraulically operated lifting universal tractor in the West Square.

Industrial Hall.

Soviet expert Borinov (second from the left) explains the performance and operation of the Y3M-100 universal electron microscope. This microscope can magnify images 40,000 times. A panoramic view of the front of the exhibition.

A rotating display stand in the textile pavilion displays various textiles.

The publications pavilion displays approximately 4,000 kinds of books in Russian, the languages of various Soviet nationalities, Chinese, and other foreign languages.

### 广州人民踊跃参观苏联展览会（图片）

1955-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

    广州人民踊跃参观苏联展览会
    苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会十月五日在广州市新建的中苏友好大厦开幕。展览会的全部展品，共计一万一千七百多件。这些展品显示了苏联三十多年来经济及文化建设的辉煌成就，显示了苏联社会主义制度的无比优越性。
    广州市人民以极大的兴趣欢迎苏联展览会的开幕。参观展览会的人十分踊跃。
                    （本组照片除署名者外，均为南方日报社供稿）
    参加展览会开幕典礼的，有苏联驻我国特命全权大使尤金院士、广东省副省长贺希明和广州市市长朱光等。下图为朱光市长在剪彩。莫品莉摄
    广东省第二次农具工作会议的代表们参观西广场上的油压升降万能型拖拉机。
    工业馆。
    苏联专家鲍里诺夫（左第二人）在讲解y3M—100型万能电子显微镜的性能和操作方法。这种显微镜能把图像放大四万倍。展览会正面全景。
    纺织品分馆里陈列着各种纺织品的转动展出台。
