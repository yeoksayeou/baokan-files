Title: Opening Ceremony of the Soviet "Zenit" Football Team's Visit and Competition Held Yesterday; Chairman Mao Zedong and Others Attended and Watched the Game
Author:
Date: 1955-10-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. The opening ceremony of the Soviet Leningrad "Zenit" Volunteer Sports Association football team's visit and competition in China was attended by Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China this afternoon.

    When Chairman Mao Zedong appeared on the rostrum of the Beijing Xian Nong Tan Stadium, the audience of more than 20,000 people stood up and applauded warmly.

    Also present at the opening ceremony today were Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Zhu De, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Nie Rongzhen, Long Yun, and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun.

    Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, as well as many foreign diplomatic envoys to China, also attended the opening ceremony.

    The banners erected in the stands of the Xian Nong Tan Stadium read: "Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the peoples and athletes of China and the Soviet Union!" "Earnestly learn from the advanced Soviet sports theory and experience!"

    At 2:15 p.m., to the strains of the "Leningrad March" played by the band, the members of the "Zenit" team and the joint team of the Central Institute of Physical Education and the First Machinery Football Team entered the stadium. Then, the band played the national anthems of the Soviet Union and China.

    Cai Shufan, Deputy Director of the Sports Commission, delivered a welcome speech at the opening ceremony. He said that the Soviet sports cause, like other victories achieved in the Soviet communist construction, has greatly encouraged the Chinese people and athletes and set an excellent example for us. This visit of the "Zenit" football team to China will play a significant role in the further development of China's football movement and will surely promote the continued consolidation and strengthening of the friendship and cooperation between the peoples and athletes of China and the Soviet Union.

    The leader of the "Zenit" team, V. S. Deromanov, delivered a speech at the opening ceremony. He said that the "Zenit" football team is proud to be visiting China for the first time and hopes that this visit will be a good start to the traditional competitions between the football movements of the Soviet Union and China. We believe that the upcoming competition will surely promote the further consolidation and development of the friendship and brotherly relations between the peoples of the Soviet Union and China.

    Then, the captains of the "Zenit" team and the joint team exchanged team flags, and the Chinese female athletes presented flowers to all the members of the "Zenit" team.

    After the opening ceremony, the two teams held a friendly match.

    After the wonderful friendly match, Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, and Ambassador Yudin came to the stadium and shook hands with the members of the "Zenit" team and the joint team to express their congratulations. At this time, applause resounded throughout the stadium, and cheers lasted for a long time. (Attached picture)

### 苏联“泽尼特”足球队访问比赛开幕式昨举行  毛泽东主席等出席并观看球赛

1955-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联“泽尼特”足球队访问比赛开幕式昨举行
    毛泽东主席等出席并观看球赛
    新华社三十日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东今天下午出席了苏联列宁格勒“泽尼特”志愿体育协会足球队在中国访问比赛的开幕式。
    当毛泽东主席出现在北京市先农坛体育场的主席台上时，全场两万多观众一致起立，热烈地鼓掌欢呼。
    出席今天开幕式的还有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈云、邓小平、贺龙、陈毅、李富春、李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、彭真，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻、龙云，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧。
    苏联驻中国大使尤金院士以及外国驻中国外交使节多人也出席了开幕式。
    先农坛体育场看台上树立着的标语牌上，写着：“中苏两国人民及运动员之间的永恒的牢不可破的友谊万岁”、“认真学习苏联先进的体育理论与经验”。
    下午二时十五分，在乐队高奏“列宁格勒进行曲”声中，“泽尼特”队同中央体育学院和第一机械足球联合队的队员入场。接着，乐队奏苏、中两国国歌。
    体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩在开幕式上致欢迎词。他说，苏联的体育事业正如苏联共产主义建设中所获得的其他胜利一样，都给中国人民和运动员以极大的鼓舞。为我们树立了卓越的榜样。这次“泽尼特”足球队访问中国，对中国足球运动的进一步发展将起重大的作用，同时必将促进中苏两国人民和运动员之间的友谊合作继续巩固和加强。
    “泽尼特”队领队维·斯·杰洛曼诺夫在开幕式上致词。他说，“泽尼特”足球队以能第一次来中国访问而感到骄傲，希望这次访问，能成为苏中两国足球运动传统性比赛的良好开端。我们相信，即将举行的比赛，必将促进苏中两国人民之间的友谊和兄弟般关系的更进一步巩固和发展。
    接着，“泽尼特”队和联合队的队长相互交换了队旗，中国的女运动员向“泽尼特”队全体队员献花。
    开幕式结束后，两队举行友谊比赛。
    在精采的友谊比赛结束后，毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、周恩来总理和国务院副总理贺龙以及尤金大使来到运动场上，同“泽尼特”队和联合队的队员一一握手致贺。这时全场掌声雷动，欢呼声经久不息。（附图片）
