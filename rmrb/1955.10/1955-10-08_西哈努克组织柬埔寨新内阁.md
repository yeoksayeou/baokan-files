Title: Sihanouk Organizes New Cambodian Cabinet
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-08
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 4th: Phnom Penh dispatch: Former Cambodian King and leader of the People's Socialist Community, Sihanouk, organized Cambodia's new cabinet on October 2nd. The new cabinet is headed by Sihanouk as Prime Minister and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs, Son Sann as Deputy Prime Minister and concurrently Minister of Economy, San Yun as Minister of the Interior, and Colonel Lon Nol as Minister of Defense.

　　Sihanouk was appointed Prime Minister by the new National Assembly elected on September 11th at its first meeting on September 25th. Sihanouk agreed at the time to serve as Prime Minister for three months.

### 西哈努克组织柬埔寨新内阁

1955-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克组织柬埔寨新内阁
    新华社河内四日电　金边消息：柬埔寨前国王、人民社会同盟领袖西哈努克在十月二日组织了柬埔寨的新内阁。新内阁由西哈努克担任首相兼外交大臣，桑·萨里任副首相兼经济大臣，桑扬任内政大臣，朗诺上校任国防大臣。
    西哈努克是由九月十一日选出的新国民议会在九月二十五日它的第一次会议上委任为首相的。西哈努克当时同意担任首相三个月。
