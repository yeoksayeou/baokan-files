Northwest Nationalities Institute
Located at the foot of Lanzhou's western suburban Lanshan Mountain, the Northwest Nationalities Institute was founded in August 1950. Over the past few years, it has trained thousands of ethnic minority cadres for the country. The students come from sixteen ethnic groups, including Hui, Mongolian, Tibetan, Uygur, Kazakh, Dongxiang, Salar, Tu, Manchu, Baoan, Yugu, Uzbek, Tatar, Kirgiz, Xibe, and Han. Among them are on-the-job cadres, peasant and herdsmen activists, and young students.

Currently, the Northwest Nationalities Institute is being expanded to train more outstanding personnel for the various ethnic minorities in the Northwest. Photo by our reporter Na Yi.

    西北民族学院
    位于兰州西郊?兰山麓的西北民族学院，成立于一九五○年八月。几年来它给国家培养了几千名民族工作人员。学员们来自回、蒙、藏、维吾尔、哈萨克、东乡、撒拉、土、满、保安、裕固、乌孜别克、塔塔尔、柯尔克孜、锡伯、汉等十六个民族。其中有在职工作人员、农牧民积极分子、青年学生等。
    现在，西北民族学院正在扩建中，将为西北各少数民族培养更多优秀的工作人员。　本报记者　纳一摄
