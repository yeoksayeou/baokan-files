Title: Peng Zhen, Qian Junrui and Others Separately Receive Leaders of Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency News: Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Mayor of Beijing, received Lieutenant Colonel Z. Wolwa, Colonel T. Ratkowski, and Colonel S. Ruda, leaders of the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble, at 11:00 a.m. on September 29. Present at the meeting were Liu Ren, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Deputy Secretaries Zheng Tianxiang and Chen Peng, and Vice Mayors of Beijing Xue Zizheng and Feng Jiping.

On the same morning, Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, and Yang Chengwu, Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, also separately received the leaders of the song and dance ensemble.

Lieutenant Colonel Wolwa, Colonel Ratkowski, and Colonel Ruda also presented gifts on behalf of the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble to Peng Zhen, Liu Ren, Qian Junrui, Zheng Zhenduo, and Yang Chengwu.

### 彭真钱俊瑞等分别接见波军歌舞团领导人

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    彭真钱俊瑞等分别接见波军歌舞团领导人
    新华社讯　中共北京市委员会书记、北京市市长彭真，在九月二十九日上午十一时接见了波兰军队歌舞团领导人兹·沃尔瓦中校、特·拉特科夫斯基上校、谢·鲁达上校等人。接见时在座的，有中共北京市委员会第二书记刘仁，副书记郑天翔、陈鹏，北京市副市长薛子正、冯基平。
    同日上午，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，中国人民解放军京、津卫戍司令员杨成武也分别接见了歌舞团领导人。
    沃尔瓦中校、拉特科夫斯基上校和鲁达上校并代表波兰军队歌舞团向彭真、刘仁、钱俊瑞、郑振铎、杨成武赠送了礼品。
