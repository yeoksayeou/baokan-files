Title: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser to Visit Poland
Author:
Date: 1955-10-21
Page: 4
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 19th (TASS): An Egyptian Foreign Ministry spokesperson announced that Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser has accepted the invitation of the Polish government and plans to visit the People's Republic of Poland after his visit to the Soviet Union.

### 埃及总理纳赛尔将访问波兰

1955-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及总理纳赛尔将访问波兰
    新华社二十日讯　塔斯社开罗十九日讯：埃及外交部发言人宣布，埃及总理纳赛尔接受了波兰政府的邀请，准备在访问苏联以后，访问波兰人民共和国。
