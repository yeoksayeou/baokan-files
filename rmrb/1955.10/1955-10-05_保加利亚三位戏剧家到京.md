Title: Three Bulgarian Playwrights Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Three Bulgarian playwrights have arrived in Beijing for a visit to China, according to the 1955 executive plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. They are Peter Dimitrov, Chairman of the Bulgarian Artists Association and People's Actor; Stepan Serkakiev, Director of the Krastyo Sarafov People's Theatre; and Lyubomir Detenev, Professor of the Higher Institute of Dramatic Art and Chairman of the Drama and Criticism Department of the Bulgarian Writers Association. Tian Han, Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, and others welcomed them at the airport upon their arrival.

### 保加利亚三位戏剧家到京

1955-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚三位戏剧家到京
    新华社四日讯　根据中保文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来华访问的三位保加利亚戏剧家已经到达北京。他们是保加利亚艺术工作者协会主席、人民演员彼得·季米特洛夫，克勒斯丘·萨拉弗夫人民剧院导演斯切潘·瑟尔卡基耶夫，高等戏剧艺术学院教授、保加利亚作家协会戏剧和批评部主席柳保米尔·德泰涅夫。当他们到达时，中国戏剧家协会主席田汉等曾往机场欢迎。
