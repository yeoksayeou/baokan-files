Title: Syria and Poland Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 13th: Syria and Poland formally signed a trade agreement between the two countries in Damascus on the 10th.

    Syrian Minister of Economy Antaki signed on behalf of the Syrian side, and Maksimovich, head of the Polish economic delegation, signed on behalf of the Polish side.

    According to this agreement, Syria will export agricultural products, wool, cotton, etc. to Poland, and will import industrial machinery from Poland.

### 叙利亚和波兰签订贸易协定

1955-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚和波兰签订贸易协定
    新华社十三日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚和波兰十日在大马士革正式签订了两国之间的贸易协定。
    代表叙利亚方面签字的是叙利亚经济部长安塔基，代表波兰方面签字的是波兰经济代表团团长马克西莫维奇。
    根据这个协定，叙利亚将向波兰输出农产品、羊毛、棉花等，并将从波兰输入工业机器。
