Title: Manturelli Exhibition Well Received

Author: 
Date: 1955-10-31
Page: 2

The "Exhibition of Works by Chilean Painter José Venturelli," sponsored by the Chinese Artists Association, was previewed at the Beijing Art Exhibition Hall on October 19th and officially opened to the public the following day (October 20th). It has been attracting many visitors daily. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Liu GuanYi, Secretary-General, Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress, Committee Member Xu Teli, Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture, Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and peace advocates from various countries in Beijing, including Toogo Kameta, Kan'emon Nakamura, Huang Fengjiu, and Chinese artists in Beijing, have all visited the exhibition.

Visitors highly appreciate José Venturelli's works, considering them to be rich in Chilean national characteristics and possessing his own unique style. Many works reflecting the struggle of the Chilean people and working people of all countries for peace and democracy have been particularly praised by the audience. The People's Fine Arts Publishing House is preparing to select a portion of the works from this exhibition to publish a selection of Venturelli's works.

The exhibition is scheduled to close at 8:00 PM on the afternoon of the 31st of this month.

### 万徒勒里画展受观众欢迎

1955-10-31
第2版()
专栏：

    万徒勒里画展受观众欢迎
    中国美术家协会主办的“智利画家万徒勒里作品展览会”十月十九日在北京美术展览馆预展后，第二天（二十日）起即正式展出，连日参观者很多。中国人民保卫和平委员会主席郭沫若、秘书长刘贯一，第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、陈叔通、委员徐特立，文化部副部长郑振铎，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超和在京的各国和平人士龟田东伍、中村翫右卫门、黄凤九等以及在京的中国美术家们，都参观了这个展览会。
    参观者都很喜爱何塞·万徒勒里的作品，认为他的作品极富智利的民族特色，而且有他自己独特的风格。反映智利人民和各国劳动人民争取和平民主的斗争的许多作品，尤其受到观众的称赞。人民美术出版社已准备在这次展览的作品中选出一部分，出版万徒勒里作品选集。
    展览会定于本月三十一日下午八时闭幕。
