Title: Opening Ceremony of the Indian National Volleyball Team's Visit and Competition Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th. The opening ceremony for the Indian National Volleyball Team's visit and competition in China was held on the evening of the 26th at the newly completed Beijing Gymnasium.

Attending the opening ceremony were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Li Jishen and Luo Ronghuan, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court; Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; Nie Rongzhen, Fu Zuoyi, and Long Yun, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council; Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing; Zhang Wentian and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Cai Tingkai, Cai Shufan, and Huang Qixiang, Vice Chairmen of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; and Xiao Hua, Rong Gaotang, and Dong Shouyi, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Sports Federation.

Bahadur Singh, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy in China, and his wife also attended the opening ceremony.

More than 6,000 people from the capital also attended the opening ceremony.

Inside the competition hall of the Beijing Gymnasium, the national flags of China and India were hung high, and two horizontally hanging banners read: "Welcome the Indian National Volleyball Team" and "Long Live the Friendship Between the People and Athletes of China and India."

At 7:30 PM, after the players of the Indian National Volleyball Team and the Beijing Volleyball Team entered the arena, the band played the national anthems of India and China.

Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, spoke at the opening ceremony. Rong Gaotang said that the friendship and cultural exchanges between the people of China and India have a glorious tradition. This visit by the Indian National Volleyball Team to China fully demonstrates the further development of cultural and sports exchanges between the people of China and India. It is firmly believed that this visit will make an important contribution to consolidating and developing the friendship between the people and athletes of China and India.

Fa. Chan. Arora, the leader of the Indian National Volleyball Team, then delivered a speech. He said, "We are not only here to play volleyball, but we also bring the fraternal friendship of Indian athletes and the Indian people. I believe that this visit will not only help the development of sports in both countries, but will also further strengthen the friendship and ties between the two countries and their people."

Amidst warm applause, the captains of the Indian National Volleyball Team and the Beijing Volleyball Team exchanged team flags, and the players of the Indian National Volleyball Team received flowers presented by Chinese female athletes.

After the opening ceremony, the Indian National Volleyball Team and the Beijing Volleyball Team held a friendly match.

After the game, Zhou Enlai, He Long, and Chen Yi came to the court to shake hands cordially with all members of the Indian National Volleyball Team and took a group photo with the entire Indian National Volleyball Team and the Beijing team. (Attached photo)

### 印度国家排球队访问比赛开幕式在京举行

1955-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    印度国家排球队访问比赛开幕式在京举行
    新华社二十六日讯　印度国家排球队来中国访问比赛开幕式，二十六日晚在新落成的北京体育馆举行。
    参加开幕式的有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、罗荣桓，国务院副总理邓小平、贺龙、陈毅、李富春、李先念，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻、傅作义、龙云，北京市市长彭真，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫，体育运动委员会副主席蔡廷锴、蔡树藩、黄琪翔，中华全国体育总会副主席萧华、荣高棠、董守义。
    印度驻中国大使馆临时代办巴哈杜尔·辛格和他的夫人也参加了开幕式。
    参加开幕式的还有首都人民共六千多人。
    北京体育馆的比赛馆内高悬着中、印两国国旗，两块横悬着的标语牌上写着：“欢迎印度国家排球队”、“中印两国人民及运动员友好万岁”。
    七时半，印度国家排球队和北京排球队的队员入场后，乐队奏印、中两国国歌。
    中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠在开幕式上讲话。荣高棠说，中印两国人民的友谊和文化交流有着光辉的传统。这次印度国家排球队访问中国，充分表现了中印两国人民文化和体育交流的进一步发展。深信通过这次访问，对于巩固和发展中印两国人民和运动员之间的友谊必将作出重要的贡献。
    印度国家排球队领队法·钱·阿罗拉接着致词。他说，我们不仅是来这里比赛排球，我们也带来了印度运动员和印度人民兄弟般的友谊。相信这次访问不仅有助于两国体育事业的发展，也将更进一步加强两个国家和人民之间的友好和联系。
    印度国家排球队队长和北京排球队的队长在热烈的掌声中相互交换了队旗，印度国家排球队队员接受了中国女运动员的献花。
    开幕式结束后，印度国家排球队和北京排球队举行了友谊比赛。
    球赛结束后，周恩来、贺龙、陈毅来到球场上同印度国家排球队全体人员亲切地握手，并和印度国家排球队以及北京队的全体人员合影留念。（附图片）
