Our Great Motherland
A tipping-truck youth team at a mechanical transfer station on the northern section of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway is able to complete nearly 100 tons of sand and soil transportation tasks every day, ensuring construction needs. This is a picture of the two best workers in the team doing record-keeping work.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    宝成铁路北段机械经租站的一个倾卸车青年班，每天都能完成近百吨的沙土运输任务，保证施工需要。这是该班工作最出色的两个人在作记录工作。
