Title: Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, Talks About the Taiwan Issue
Author:
Date: 1955-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 27th. Hong Kong Dispatch: The newly appointed Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, arrived in Hong Kong on the 26th by ship. At a press conference held on board, he said, when talking about the Taiwan issue, "As we see it, the matter is very clear: Taiwan and the coastal islands are part of China. We are confident that the Chinese people hope to resolve this issue peacefully."

R.K. Nehru expressed his hope that the Geneva Four-Power Foreign Ministers' Conference would further ease tensions.

In response to a question about Goa, Nehru said that although Goa is under foreign rule, it is an indivisible part of India. The residents of Goa are Indians, and they certainly hope to be reunited with their motherland. We are confident that this piece of Indian territory under foreign rule will definitely be liberated.

### 印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁谈台湾问题

1955-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁谈台湾问题
    新华社广州二十七日电　香港消息：新任印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁在二十六日乘轮船到达香港。他在船上举行的记者招待会上谈到台湾问题时说，在我们看来，事情是很清楚的：台湾和沿海岛屿是中国的一部分。我们确信中国人是希望和平解决这个问题的。
    拉·库·尼赫鲁表示希望日内瓦四国外长会议将达到进一步缓和紧张局势的目的。
    在回答关于果阿的问题时，尼赫鲁说，果阿虽然处于外国统治下，但是它是印度的一个不可分割的部分，果阿的居民是印度人，他们当然希望同祖国重新统一起来。我们确信，这片为外国所统治的印度的土地是一定会得到解放的。
