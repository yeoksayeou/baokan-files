Title: Volunteer Army Troops Withdrawn from Manpo Port Completely
Author:
Date: 1955-10-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Manpo, October 24th: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army troops withdrawing from Korea through Manpo Port have been completely withdrawn as of the 24th.

At around 7:00 AM (Korean time) on the 24th, a meeting was held at Manpo Station to bid farewell to the Volunteer Army troops returning to the motherland on the last train departing from Manpo. Li Jingyun, Chairman of the Manpo County People's Committee, and representatives of the Volunteer Army troops returning home spoke at the meeting. At the meeting, Li Jingyun, on behalf of the people of Manpo County, presented three silk banners to the representatives of the Volunteer Army troops returning home.

Xinhua News Agency, Manpo, October 24th: The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission stationed at Manpo Port has completed its inspection of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army troops withdrawing from Korea through Manpo Port.

At 8:20 AM (Korean time) on the 24th, the last train carrying the Chinese People's Volunteer Army troops withdrawing from Korea through Manpo Port departed from the Korean border. Liaison officers from the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army stationed at Manpo Port then announced to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission that all the Chinese People's Volunteer Army troops withdrawing from Korea through Manpo Port had been completely withdrawn.

Over the course of the 23rd and 24th, the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission stationed at Manpo Port continued to inspect the Chinese People's Volunteer Army troops withdrawing from Korea on five trains, and conducted spot checks on some of the weapons and ammunition. During each inspection, they did not raise any objections that were inconsistent with the information provided in advance by the liaison officers from the Korean and Chinese sides, nor did they request any re-inspections.

### 志愿军从满浦口岸归国的部队全部撤完

1955-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军从满浦口岸归国的部队全部撤完
    据新华社满浦二十四日电　从满浦口岸撤出朝鲜的中国人民志愿军归国部队，已在二十四日全部撤完。
    二十四日上午七点多钟（朝鲜时间），满浦车站上举行了大会，欢送乘最后一次列车从满浦撤返祖国的志愿军归国部队。满浦郡人民委员会委员长李景云和志愿军归国部队代表都在会上讲了话。会上，李景云代表满浦郡人民把三面锦旗献给志愿军归国部队的代表。
    新华社满浦二十四日电　驻满浦口岸的中立国视察小组对通过满浦口岸撤出朝鲜的中国人民志愿军归国部队的视察工作已经全部结束。
    二十四日上午八时二十分（朝鲜时间），从满浦口岸撤返祖国的中国人民志愿军归国部队的最后一次列车开出了朝鲜国境，朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军驻满浦口岸的联络官随即向中立国视察小组宣布，通过满浦口岸撤出朝鲜的中国人民志愿军归国部队已经全部撤完。
    在二十三、二十四日两天中，驻满浦口岸的中立国视察小组继续对分乘五次列车撤出朝鲜的中国人民志愿军归国部队进行了视察，并曾抽查了部分武器弹药。在历次视察中，他们都没有提出任何同朝中方面联络官事先提供的材料不符的意见，也没有提出复查的要求。
