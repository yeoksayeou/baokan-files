Title: Polish Architecture Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-03
Page: 2

    Polish Architecture Exhibition Opens in Beijing
    The "Polish Architecture Exhibition" opened at the Soviet Exhibition Center in Beijing on the afternoon of October 2nd. This exhibition is hosted in China by the Polish Architects Association at the invitation of the Architectural Society of China.
    Those attending the opening ceremony included: Bo Yibo, Director of the State Construction Commission of the People's Republic of China, Shi Zhiren, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Railways, Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Higher Education, Zhou Rongxin, Chairman of the Architectural Society of China, Liang Sicheng, Vice Chairman, Yang Tingbao, Hou Debang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, Xue Zizheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, as well as responsible persons from relevant urban construction and architectural departments, architects, and engineers.
    Also attending the opening ceremony was the delegation of the Polish Architects Association to China, headed by Wiezybicki.
    Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilo, also attended the opening ceremony.
    Zhou Rongxin, Chairman of the Architectural Society of China, said in his opening speech: With the consolidation and development of the great friendship between the Chinese and Polish people, the mutual exchanges and close friendship between Chinese and Polish architects are also developing day by day. Through this architectural exhibition and the academic reports that the delegation will hold, Chinese architects will surely be inspired, which will be of great help to China's socialist construction.
    Wiezybicki, head of the delegation of the Polish Architects Association to China, pointed out in his speech that the Polish and Chinese people are following a common path—the path of socialist construction. During the delegation's visit to China, Polish and Chinese architects will exchange experiences and discuss common concerns and issues, thereby contributing to the cause of progress and peaceful construction.
    Bo Yibo, Director of the State Construction Commission of the People's Republic of China, cut the ribbon for the exhibition.
    The exhibition displays more than 300 pictures, as well as some books, magazines, and design drawings. These exhibits show the contribution of the Polish working people to architectural art in history and the achievements of the Polish People's Republic after the war in urban planning, industrial buildings, public buildings, residential buildings, and standard designs. After being exhibited in Beijing for ten days, the exhibition will be moved to Shanghai and Guangzhou. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 波兰建筑展览会在北京开幕

1955-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    波兰建筑展览会在北京开幕
    “波兰建筑展览会”十月二日下午在北京苏联展览馆开幕。这个展览会是波兰建筑师协会应中国建筑学会邀请在中国举办的。
    参加开幕典礼的有：中华人民共和国国家建设委员会主任薄一波，铁道部副部长石志仁，高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，中国建筑学会理事长周荣鑫、副理事长梁思成、杨廷宝，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会副主席侯德榜，北京市副市长薛子正以及有关城市建设和建筑部门的负责人和建筑师、工程师们。
    参加开幕典礼的还有：以维日比茨基为首的波兰建筑师协会访华代表团。
    波兰驻华大使基里洛克也参加了开幕典礼。
    中国建筑学会理事长周荣鑫致开幕词说：随着中波两国人民伟大友谊的巩固和发展，中波两国建筑师的互相往来和亲密友谊也正在日益发展。通过这个建筑展览会和代表团即将举行的学术报告，一定会给中国建筑师以启发，这对于中国的社会主义建设事业是很有帮助的。
    波兰建筑师协会访华代表团团长维日比茨基在致词时指出，波兰和中国人民走着共同的道路——社会主义建设的道路。在代表团访华期间，波兰和中国的建筑师们将交流经验，讨论共同关心的事业和问题，而对进步事业与和平建设有所贡献。
    展览会由中华人民共和国国家建设委员会主任薄一波剪彩。
    展览会展出了三百多幅图片以及一部分图书杂志和设计图纸。这些展览品显示出波兰劳动人民在历史上对于建筑艺术的贡献和战后波兰人民共和国在城市规划、工业建筑、公用建筑、居住建筑、标准设计各个方面的成就。这个展览会在北京展出十天后将移往上海和广州展出。（新华社）
