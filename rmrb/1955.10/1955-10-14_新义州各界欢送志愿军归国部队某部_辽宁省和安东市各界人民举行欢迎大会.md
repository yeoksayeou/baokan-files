Title: Xin'yizhou People of All Circles See Off a Certain Unit of the Chinese People's Volunteers Returning Home; People from All Circles in Liaoning Province and Andong City Hold a Welcome Rally
Author:
Date: 1955-10-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Xin'yizhou, 13th. More than 15,000 representatives from all circles in Xin'yizhou, Korea, braved the rain on the 13th to bid farewell to the commanders and fighters of a certain leadership organ of the Chinese People's Volunteers (CPV) returning to their homeland.

	At 10:30 a.m. (Pyongyang time), the train carrying the personnel of a certain leadership organ of the CPV slowly entered the station. Amidst the welcoming music played by the band, the commanders, heroes, model workers, and meritorious individuals of the returning unit disembarked the train. They were accompanied onto the platform by Kim Se-gwang, Vice Chairman of the North Pyongan Province People's Committee of Korea; Zheng Shu-yong, Vice Chairman of the North Pyongan Province Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Kim Ri-jun, Chairman of the Xin'yizhou City Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; and Tian Ji-hong, Vice Chairman of the Xin'yizhou City People's Committee, and others. The medals commemorating the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea and various other medals and military service medals on their chests shone brightly. Other personnel of the leadership organ of this unit also attended the farewell rally.

	Before the farewell rally began, a flower presentation team composed of sixteen Korean girls, representatives of the people from all walks of life in Xin'yizhou, stepped onto the platform and presented huge flower baskets and bouquets to the representatives of the returning CPV unit. Tian Ji-hong, Vice Chairman of the Xin'yizhou City People's Committee of Korea, delivered a speech at the farewell rally. He expressed sincere respect and gratitude to the returning CPV unit on behalf of the people of Xin'yizhou and warmly congratulated the great Chinese people on marching from victory to greater victory in the cause of defending world peace and the great socialist construction. Kang Lin, the representative of the returning CPV unit, delivered a farewell speech. He stated that he would definitely bring the deep friendship of the Korean people towards the CPV back to the motherland and further consolidate and develop the friendship between the people of China and Korea in the future.

	The people of Xin'yizhou presented many gifts to the returning CPV unit at the farewell rally. Among these gifts was a silk banner from the workers of the Xin'yizhou Pulp Factory, embroidered with the words "The heroic deeds of the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers in our country will forever shine brightly." The Xin'yizhou Daily Use Products Factory and other units sent many boat-shaped rubber shoes with Korean national style, embroidered handkerchiefs, bronze cookware, and various exquisite folk handicrafts. Some Korean women also personally tied six silk colored sachets, symbolizing the hearts of the Chinese and Korean people are forever linked, around the waists of the representatives of the returning CPV unit.

	When the commanders and fighters of the returning CPV unit bid farewell to the Korean people and boarded the train, the Xin'yizhou station was in turmoil. A warm get-together activity unfolded simultaneously on and off the train. At the station, people spontaneously formed many dance circles and sang and danced wildly to the cheerful music. In front of many carriages, people were signing each other's names, giving each other photos, or taking group photos together. As the train started amidst the cheers of tens of thousands of people, the people seeing them off and the soldiers on the train still held countless colored paper streamers connecting them, expressing their reluctant feelings of parting with all kinds of affectionate words.

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, 13th. More than 2,500 people from all walks of life in Liaoning Province and Andong City held a grand rally in Andong City on the 13th to warmly welcome the triumphant return of a certain leadership organ and returning troops of the Chinese People's Volunteers.

	Wei Zhi, Secretary of the Andong Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party (CCP); Duan Yongjie, Mayor of Andong City; Wang He and Li Tefu, Deputy Mayors; heads of various people's organizations in Andong City; representatives of the People's Liberation Army (PLA) troops stationed in Andong; and representatives of various organs, schools, and units in Andong City, as well as model workers in industry and agriculture, martyrs, and model family members of military personnel, were at the station to welcome them.

	Zhang Xuexuan, representative of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the CCP and Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the CCP; Zhou Dafu, Director of the Civil Affairs Department of Liaoning Province; Chen Xianzhou, Vice Chairman of the Liaoning Provincial Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; and Guo Kaifeng, representative of the PLA troops stationed in Shenyang, also came to welcome them.

	When the train carrying the returning troops entered the station at 11:08 a.m., people gathered in front of the carriages, shaking hands and embracing the commanders and fighters of the returning CPV unit, and the entire platform resounded with cheers and songs. Young Pioneers presented large bouquets of flowers to the commanders and fighters of the CPV.

	Then, the welcoming rally began amidst the music of the "Song of the Volunteers." Zhang Xuexuan first represented the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the CCP, the Liaoning Provincial People's Committee, and the people of all circles in Liaoning Province, extending a warm welcome and cordial greetings to the triumphant returning CPV unit.

	Zhang Riqing, the representative of the returning CPV unit, delivered a reply speech at the meeting. He expressed gratitude to the welcoming people on behalf of all the soldiers and officers of the returning unit.

	After the welcoming meeting, a banquet was held, and a performance was given for the returning soldiers.

### 新义州各界欢送志愿军归国部队某部  辽宁省和安东市各界人民举行欢迎大会

1955-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    新义州各界欢送志愿军归国部队某部
    辽宁省和安东市各界人民举行欢迎大会
    新华社新义州十三日电　朝鲜新义州市各界人民代表一万五千多人，在十三日冒雨欢送中国人民志愿军归国部队某部领导机关的指挥员和战斗员们返回祖国。
    上午十时三十分（平壤时间），中国人民志愿军某部领导机关人员的列车徐徐进站。在乐队高奏欢迎曲声中，归国部队某部的指挥员和英雄、模范、功臣们走下列车，由朝鲜平安北道人民委员会副委员长金世广、朝鲜劳动党平安北道委员会副委员长郑洙永、朝鲜劳动党新义州市委员会委员长金利俊和新义州市人民委员会副委员长田吉弘等陪同登上主席台。他们胸前佩戴的抗美援朝纪念章和各种勋章、军功章光彩夺目。这支部队的领导机关的其他人员也参加了欢送大会。
    欢送大会开始前，由新义州市各界人民的代表——十六位朝鲜姑娘组成的献花队，走上主席台，把巨大的花篮和花束献给志愿军归国部队的代表。朝鲜新义州市人民委员会副委员长田吉弘在欢送大会上致词。他代表新义州人民向中国人民志愿军归国部队表示诚挚的敬意和感谢，并热烈地祝贺伟大的中国人民在保卫世界和平和伟大的社会主义建设事业中从胜利走向更大的胜利。志愿军归国部队代表康林致告别词。他表示一定把朝鲜人民对志愿军的深情厚意带回祖国去，今后更加巩固和发展中朝两国人民的友谊。
    新义州人民在欢送大会上向志愿军归国部队赠送了许多礼品。这些礼品中有新义州市纸浆工厂职工送来的一幅锦旗，旗上绣有“英雄的中国人民志愿军官兵在我国建立的功勋将永远灿烂光芒”的字样。新义州市日用品工厂等单位送来许多具有朝鲜民族风格的船形胶鞋、绣花手帕、铜饭具和各种精致的民间工艺品。有些朝鲜妇女们还亲自把表示朝中人民永远心连心的六个丝制彩色锦囊系在志愿军归国部队代表的腰间。
    当志愿军归国部队的指战员们向朝鲜人民告别登车时，新义州车站上沸腾起来。车上车下同时展开了热烈的联欢活动。车站上，人们随着欢乐的乐曲，自动组成了许多个舞圈高歌狂舞。在许多车厢前，人们都在互相签名、互赠照片或共同合影留念。当列车在万人欢呼声中开动时，欢送的人们和车上的战士们手里还拉着无数条把他们联结起来的彩色纸带，用各种亲切的话语表示他们依依惜别的心情。
    新华社安东十三日电　辽宁省和安东市各界人民两千五百多人十三日在安东市举行盛大集会，热烈欢迎中国人民志愿军归国的某部领导机关和归国部队凯旋回国。
    到车站欢迎的有中共安东市委员会书记卫之，安东市市长段永杰、副市长王鹤、李特夫，安东市各人民团体负责人，中国人民解放军驻安东部队的代表和安东市各机关、学校单位的代表以及工农业劳动模范、烈士和军人家属模范多人。
    中共辽宁省委员会的代表、中共辽宁省委员会统一战线工作部部长张雪轩，辽宁省民政厅厅长周达夫，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会辽宁省分会副主席陈先舟，中国人民解放军驻沈阳部队代表郭开锋等也特地前来欢迎。
    当载着归国部队的列车在上午十一时零八分进入车站时，人们齐集车厢前，同归国志愿军部队的指挥员和战斗员握手、拥抱，整个月台上响彻了欢呼声和歌曲声。少年先锋队队员们把大束鲜花献给志愿军的指战员。
    接着，欢迎大会在“志愿军战歌”乐声中开始。张雪轩首先代表中共辽宁省委员会、辽宁省人民委员会和辽宁省各界人民，向凯旋归来的中国人民志愿军归国部队表示热烈欢迎和亲切慰问。
    中国人民志愿军归国部队代表张日清在会上致答词。他代表回国部队的全体士兵和军官对欢迎的人们表示谢意。
    欢迎会后举行了宴会，并为归国战士们作了慰问演出。
