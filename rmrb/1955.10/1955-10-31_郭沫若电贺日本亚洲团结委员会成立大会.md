Title: Guo Moruo Sends Telegram of Congratulations to the Inaugural Meeting of the Japan Asia Solidarity Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. The Japan Asia Solidarity Committee will hold its inaugural meeting in Tokyo on October 31st. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, sent a telegram of congratulations to the Preparatory Committee of the Japan Asia Solidarity Committee on the 30th. The text of the telegram is as follows:

"Preparatory Committee of the Japan Asia Solidarity Committee":

We are delighted to learn that the Japan Asia Solidarity Committee will hold its inaugural meeting in Tokyo on October 31st. The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace extends its warm congratulations. We believe that the establishment of the Japan Asia Solidarity Committee will not only further promote friendly cooperation between the Japanese people and the people of Asian countries, and strengthen the solidarity among Asian countries, but will also help to safeguard peace in Asia and the world.

We wish the conference a complete success!

Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
October 30, 1955

### 郭沫若电贺日本亚洲团结委员会成立大会

1955-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若电贺日本亚洲团结委员会成立大会
    新华社三十日讯　日本亚洲团结委员会将于十月三十一日在东京举行成立大会。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若特在三十日致电日本亚洲团结委员会筹备委员会，表示祝贺。电文如下：“日本亚洲团结委员会”筹备委员会：
    欣悉日本亚洲团结委员会将于十月三十一日在东京举行成立大会，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会谨致热烈的祝贺。我们相信日本亚洲团结委员会的成立，不仅将进一步促进日本人民与亚洲各国人民间的友好合作，增强亚洲国家间的团结，并且将有助于保卫亚洲和世界的和平。
    谨祝大会获得圆满的成功！
  中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若
                      一九五五年十月三十日
