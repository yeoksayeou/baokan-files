Title: Italian Socialist Party General Secretary Nenni and Others Leave Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1955-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th – Pietro Nenni, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, Chairman of the Italian National Peace Council, and General Secretary of the Italian Socialist Party, along with his wife, his daughter Lucciana Tomassi, Italian Socialist Party Executive Committee member Panzieri, and Italian National Food Union Secretary Onsanelli, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Li Jishen, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Shutong and Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairmen of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Zhang Bojun, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Ma Yinchu, Xu Baoju, and Luo Longji, Standing Committee members of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Secretary-General Liu Guanyi; Cai Tingkai, Li Yimang, Qian Duansheng, and Chen Hansheng, members of the World Peace Council; Shao Lizi, Hu Yuzhi, Xu Guangping, Sun Qimeng, Huang Qixiang, Xu Deheng, Yan Xichun, and Li Chunqing, responsible persons of various democratic parties; Zhu Xuefan, Li Dequan, and Lei Renmin, responsible persons of various people's organizations; Lei Jieqiong from the political and legal circles; Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Zhang Youyu and Wang Kunlun, Vice Mayors of Beijing; as well as peace figures from various countries in Beijing.

Nenni and his wife, among others, accepted flowers before boarding the plane. They embraced and shook hands warmly with those seeing them off, and waved goodbye frequently from the plane window.

### 意大利社会党总书记南尼等离北京回国

1955-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    意大利社会党总书记南尼等离北京回国
    新华社十日讯　世界和平理事会副主席、意大利全国和平理事会主席、意大利社会党总书记南尼和他的夫人、他的女儿卢契安娜·托马西，意大利社会党执行委员潘齐埃里和意大利全国食品工会书记昂萨奈利在十日晨乘飞机离京返国。
    到机场欢送的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通、廖承志，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员马寅初、许宝驹、罗隆基，秘书长刘贯一，世界和平理事会理事蔡廷锴、李一氓、钱端升、陈翰笙，各民主党派负责人邵力子、胡愈之、许广平、孙起孟、黄琪翔、许德珩、严希纯、李纯青，各人民团体负责人朱学范、李德全、雷任民，政法界人士雷洁琼，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华，北京市副市长张友渔、王昆仑以及在京的各国和平人士。
    南尼和他的夫人等在上飞机前接受了献花。他们和送行者亲切地拥抱、握手，并在机窗里向送行者频频挥手告别。
