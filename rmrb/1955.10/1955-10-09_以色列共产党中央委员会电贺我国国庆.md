Title: Central Committee of the Communist Party of Israel Sends Congratulatory Message on China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-10-09
Page: 4

Reported by this newspaper:

    Central Committee of the Communist Party of Israel Sends Congratulatory Message on China's National Day
    This newspaper reports that the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Israel sent a congratulatory telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the National Day of the People's Republic of China. To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:
    On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the great victory of the Chinese people's revolution and the founding of the People's Republic of China, we extend our warm congratulations and best wishes to you, and through you to the great Chinese people. The achievements of the People's Republic of China under your wise leadership, guaranteeing the increasing prosperity of the Chinese people and the construction of socialism, are a decisive contribution to the cause of peace, freedom and socialism. Long live the great People's Republic of China! Long live the heroic Communist Party of China!
Central Committee of the Communist Party of Israel

### 以色列共产党中央委员会电贺我国国庆

1955-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    以色列共产党中央委员会电贺我国国庆
    本报讯　以色列共产党中央委员会给中国共产党中央委员会祝贺中华人民共和国国庆的贺电。中国共产党中央委员会：
    值此伟大的中国人民革命胜利和中华人民共和国成立六周年之际，我们谨向你们，并且通过你们向伟大的中国人民致热烈的祝贺和最好的祝愿。中华人民共和国在你们的英明领导下所获得的成就，保证中国人民的日益繁荣和社会主义的建设，这是对和平、自由和社会主义事业的决定性的贡献。伟大的中华人民共和国万岁！英勇的中国共产党万岁！以色列共产党中央委员会
