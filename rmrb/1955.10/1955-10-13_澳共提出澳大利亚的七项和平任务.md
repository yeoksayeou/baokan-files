Title: Australian Communist Party Proposes Seven Tasks for Peace in Australia
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, 12th: According to a report in the Australian weekly "Tribune", the General Secretary of the Australian Communist Party, Sharkey, at a meeting of the Central Committee of the Australian Communist Party on October 1, called for acceptance of the proposal made by Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai for the conclusion of a collective peace pact for the Asian and Pacific region.

Based on Australia's specific characteristics, Sharkey put forward the following seven tasks for peace:

    Accept Zhou Enlai's proposal for the conclusion of a collective peace pact for the Asian and Pacific region, including American and Australian nations, and treat it as the foundation for peaceful coexistence and the best way to ensure Australia's security.

    Recall troops from Malaya and support the independence of the Malayans.

    Restore diplomatic relations with the Soviet Union and promote trade and cultural relations with the Soviet Union.

    Recognize the People's Republic of China and support its entry into the United Nations organization.

    Oppose further atomic bomb testing in Australia, and take this as part of the struggle to stop the use of all atomic weapons.

    Return Taiwan to China, and peacefully reunify Korea and Vietnam.

    Support the decisions of the Geneva Conference in order to end the Cold War and ensure peaceful coexistence.

### 澳共提出澳大利亚的七项和平任务

1955-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    澳共提出澳大利亚的七项和平任务
    新华社十二日讯　悉尼消息：据澳大利亚周报“论坛”报道，澳大利亚共产党总书记夏基十月一日在澳大利亚共产党中央委员会会议上要求接受中国总理周恩来提出的关于缔结亚洲和太平洋地区的集体和平公约的建议。
    夏基根据澳大利亚的特点提出了七项和平任务如下：
    接受周恩来提出的缔结包括美洲和澳洲国家在内的亚洲和太平洋地区的集体和平公约的建议，并且把它作为和平共处的基础以及保证澳大利亚安全的最好方法。
    从马来亚召回军队，并且支持马来亚人的独立。
    同苏联恢复外交关系，并且促进同苏联的贸易和文化的关系。
    承认人民中国并且支持它加入联合国组织。
    反对再在澳大利亚作原子弹实验，并且以此作为停止使用一切原子武器的斗争的一部分。
    把台湾交还给中国，并且和平地重新统一朝鲜和越南。
    支持日内瓦会议的决定，以便结束冷战和保证和平共处。
