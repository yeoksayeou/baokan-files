Title: Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe Holds Closing Performance in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1955-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, October 22nd: The Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, which has been visiting and performing in China for nearly seventy days, held its closing performance in Shenyang on October 22nd.

At the closing ceremony, Zhou Huan, head of the leading organ of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Shenyang Garrison, on the behalf of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Ministry Of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and the Chinese people, expressed congratulations and gratitude to the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe for its outstanding performances in China. He said: "The songs and dances performed by the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe not only preserve and promote the glorious national art heritage but also create many works with political, ideological, and artistic merit, faithfully and vividly conveying the heroic struggle of the Albanian people. The magnificent songs and moving dances that you have spread throughout our country will forever remain in our memories."

Lieutenant Colonel Mekur Çela, head of the Song and Dance Troupe, spoke amid enthusiastic applause. He said: "Our visit and performances in major cities in China have been warmly welcomed by the Chinese audience, which has contributed to enhancing the friendship between the people and armies of Albania and China, making us very pleased and happy." He added, "During the visit and performance period, our Song and Dance Troupe had extensive and friendly contact with the Chinese literary and art circles, and we learned a lot from each other. On this, I would like to express my gratitude on behalf of the Song and Dance Troupe."

Following this, the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe performed songs and dances, and each program was warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 阿人民军歌舞团在沈阳举行演出闭幕式

1955-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    阿人民军歌舞团在沈阳举行演出闭幕式
    据新华社沈阳二十二日电　来我国访问演出近七十天的阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团二十二日在沈阳举行访华演出闭幕式。
    在闭幕式上，中国人民解放军沈阳驻军领导机关首长周桓代表中国人民解放军总政治部、中华人民共和国文化部和中国人民向阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团在我国的卓越演出表示祝贺和感谢。他说：阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团所表演的歌舞节目，不仅保存和发扬了光荣的民族艺术遗产，并创造了不少富有政治性、思想性和艺术性的作品，忠诚地生动地传达了阿尔巴尼亚人民英勇斗争业绩。你们在我国到处传播的雄伟歌声、动人的舞蹈将永远地留在我们的记忆里。
    歌舞团团长梅库尔·柴拉中校在热烈的鼓掌声中讲话。他说：我们在中国各大城市的访问演出，受到中国观众的热烈欢迎，对于增进阿中两国人民和军队之间的友谊有所贡献，使我们感到十分愉快和高兴。他又说，在访问演出期间，我们歌舞团和中国的文艺界进行了广泛地亲切地接触，互相学习到了许多东西。对此，我谨代表歌舞团表示感谢。
    接着，阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团演出了歌舞节目，每个节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎。
