Title: Lao Wartime Parties Publish First Joint Communique of Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-10-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 10th - According to TASS Rangoon on the 10th: The delegation of the Lao Fighting Forces and the delegation of the Lao Kingdom Government began talks in Rangoon on the 9th. After the first meeting, a joint communique was issued at the request of the Chairman of the International Supervisory and Control Commission for Laos. The communique stated that the two sides adopted the following agenda: "1. Cessation of hostilities; 2. Restoration of the Kingdom's administrative control in the provinces of Phongsaly and Sam Neua; 3. On general elections; 4. Resolving the status of the Lao Fighting Forces."

Talks will continue on the 10th. The communique pointed out that the meeting on October 9 was conducted in an atmosphere of complete sincerity and mutual understanding.

### 老挝停战双方发表第一次会谈联合公报

1955-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝停战双方发表第一次会谈联合公报
    新华社十一日讯　据塔斯社仰光十日讯：寮国战斗部队代表团和老挝王国政府代表团九日在仰光开始会谈。在第一次会谈之后，根据老挝国际监督和监察委员会主席的请求，发表了联合公报。公报说，双方通过了下列议程：“一、停止敌对行动；二、恢复王国在丰沙里和桑怒两省的行政管理；三、关于普选；四、解决寮国战斗部队的地位问题。”
    十日将继续进行会谈。公报指出，十月九日的会谈是在完全真诚和相互谅解的气氛中进行的。
