Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Receives Overseas Chinese Visiting Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency: Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen received the leaders and other responsible persons, more than 40 in total, of the Overseas Chinese Visiting Delegations from Indonesia, Burma, India, Vietnam, and Korea, who are participating in this year's National Day celebrations, on the morning of September 29th.

Those present at the reception included: Vice Mayors of Beijing, Zhang Youyu, Wu Han, and others.

### 彭真市长接见归国华侨观光团

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    彭真市长接见归国华侨观光团
    新华社讯　北京市长彭真九月二十九日上午接见参加今年国庆典礼的印度尼西亚、缅甸、印度、越南和朝鲜等地华侨回国观光团团长及其他负责人四十多人。
    接见时在座的有：北京市副市长张友渔、吴晗等。
