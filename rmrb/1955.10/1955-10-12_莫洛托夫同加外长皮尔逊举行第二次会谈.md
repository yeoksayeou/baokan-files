Title: Molotov Holds Second Meeting with Canadian Foreign Minister Pearson
Author:
Date: 1955-10-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 11th – TASS reports: Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov held a second meeting with Canadian Foreign Minister Lester Pearson on the 10th, continuing discussions on Soviet-Canadian relations and exchanging views on international issues of mutual interest.

### 莫洛托夫同加外长皮尔逊举行第二次会谈

1955-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫同加外长皮尔逊举行第二次会谈
    新华社十一日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫十日同加拿大外交部长莱·皮尔逊举行了第二次会谈，继续讨论苏联和加拿大的关系问题，并且就双方感到兴趣的国际问题交换了意见。
