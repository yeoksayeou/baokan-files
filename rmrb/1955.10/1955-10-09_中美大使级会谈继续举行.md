Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1955-10-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, October 8th. The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their twentieth meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 a.m. on October 8th. The meeting adjourned at 11:20 a.m. The next meeting is scheduled for 10:00 a.m. on October 14th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1955-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦八日电　中美两国大使级会谈十月八日上午十时在国联大厦举行第二十次会议。会议在十一时二十分休会。下次会议定于十月十四日上午十时举行。
