Title: Deng Xiaoping and Vice Premier Deng Zihui Receive the Korean Agricultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 19th: Vice Premiers of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Deng Xiaoping and Deng Zihui, received the entire Korean Democratic People's Republic Agricultural Delegation, headed by Song Bong-uk, on the morning of the 19th.

    Present at the meeting were Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, and Choi Il, Ambassador of the Korean Democratic People's Republic to China.

    In the evening, Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, held a banquet to bid farewell to the entire delegation, who were about to leave Beijing. Choi Il, the Korean Ambassador to China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

    Also present at the banquet were Liu Ruilong and Yang Xiandong, Vice Ministers of Agriculture, Luo Yuchuan, Vice Minister of Forestry, and Feng Zhongyun, Vice Minister of Water Resources, among others.

    At the banquet, Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, and Song Bong-uk, head of the delegation, delivered speeches successively.

    Prior to the banquet, the delegation presented banners to the Ministry of Agriculture and the Ministry of Water Resources, and the Ministry of Agriculture and the Ministry of Water Resources presented gifts to the delegation.

### 邓小平邓子恢副总理接见朝鲜农业考察团

1955-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    邓小平邓子恢副总理接见朝鲜农业考察团
    新华社十九日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理邓小平、邓子恢，在十九日上午接见了以宋凤郁为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国农业考察团全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有农业部部长廖鲁言和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔一。
    晚间，农业部部长廖鲁言举行宴会，欢送行将离开北京的考察团全体人员。朝鲜驻华大使崔一也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的，有农业部副部长刘瑞龙、杨显东，林业部副部长罗玉川，水利部副部长冯仲云等。
    宴会上，农业部部长廖鲁言和考察团团长宋凤郁先后讲了话。
    宴会以前，考察团分别向农业部和水利部献了锦旗，农业部和水利部并向考察团赠送礼品。
