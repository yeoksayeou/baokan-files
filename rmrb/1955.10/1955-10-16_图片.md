    Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong met with all members of the Japanese Diet Members Delegation to China, led by Uemura Rongichi, on October 15th. This is a group photo taken during the meeting. Front row from left to right: Chen Shutong, Peng Zhen, Shen Junru, Yao Kisaburo, Soong Ching Ling, Miyamoto Kunihiko, Liu Shaoqi, Uemura Rongichi, Mao Zedong, Hara Shigeru, Zhou Enlai, Yamamoto Takeo, Li Jishen, Uemura Tadatsugu, Guo Moruo, Suto Goro, Chen Yi.
                Photo by Hou Bo and Qi Guanshan (Xinhua News Agency manuscript)
