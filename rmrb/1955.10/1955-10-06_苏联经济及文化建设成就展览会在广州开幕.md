Title: Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievement Exhibition Opens in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 5th October. The opening ceremony of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievement Exhibition in Guangzhou was solemnly held at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building at 9:00 am today. More than 9,000 guests attended the opening ceremony.

Academician Yudin, the Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, delivered a speech at the opening ceremony. He said that the exhibition of Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements in Guangzhou is a new proof of the growing consolidation and development of the great friendship between the Soviet and Chinese people. He said that the Soviet people are watching the amazing achievements of the Chinese people with great pleasure and sincerely hope that the Chinese people will successfully realize the great program of building socialism formulated by the Chinese Communist Party.

Subsequently, He Ximing, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, delivered a speech, expressing his deep gratitude on behalf of the people of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City to the Soviet people and all Soviet experts and personnel responsible for the exhibition. He also warmly congratulated the opening of this grand exhibition. He continued, "This exhibition is the best university prepared by the Soviet people for our people to study socialist construction. We will carefully visit and study, and apply the experience and knowledge we have gained to our current practical work, work hard, and strive to complete the great historical task of building a socialist society in our country."

Borisinko, Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievement Exhibition, and Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, spoke successively.

After the speeches, Zhu Guang, the Mayor of Guangzhou, cut the ribbon. Then, the guests entered the magnificent building to begin their visit to the sounds of the melodious music of "Moscow-Beijing."

In the evening, Ambassador Yudin held a grand celebration banquet. He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, He Ximing, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, and heads of various agencies and organizations of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, as well as representatives from all walks of life, totaling more than 600 people, were invited to attend the banquet.

According to a New China News Agency dispatch from Guangzhou on the 4th, to celebrate the exhibition of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements in Guangzhou, He Ximing, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, and Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, held a banquet on the evening of the 4th, on the eve of the opening of the exhibition, to welcome Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China who came to participate in the opening ceremony, Borisinko, Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievement Exhibition, and all the Soviet experts and Soviet staff of the exhibition. He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, who accompanied Ambassador Yudin to Guangzhou, also attended the banquet. At the banquet, He Ximing, Yudin, He Wei, Zhu Guang and Borisinko spoke successively.

### 苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会在广州开幕

1955-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会在广州开幕
    新华社广州五日电在广州举行的苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会五日上午九时在中苏友好大厦隆重举行开幕式，参加开幕式的来宾共有九千多人。
    苏联驻我国特命全权大使尤金院士在开幕仪式上首先致词。他说：苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会在广州的展出，是苏中两国人民伟大友谊日益巩固和发展的新的证明。他说：苏联人民以极大的愉快的心情注视着中国人民的惊人成就，并衷心地希望中国人民顺利地实现中国共产党所制定的建设社会主义的伟大纲领。
    接着由广东省副省长贺希明致词，他代表广东省和广州市人民向苏联人民和负责展出工作的全体苏联专家和人员致以深厚的谢意。并热烈祝贺这个盛大的展览会的开幕。他接着说：这个展览会是苏联人民为我国人民准备的一座学习社会主义建设的最好的大学，我们将认真地参观、学习，并把所得到的体验和知识贯彻到当前各项实际工作中去，刻苦努力，为完成在我国建设社会主义社会这一伟大的历史任务而奋斗。
    苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科、中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎相继讲了话。
    讲话毕，由广州市市长朱光剪彩。接着来宾们在“莫斯科—北京”的悠扬的音乐声中进入宏伟的大厦，开始参观。
    晚上，尤金大使举行了盛大的庆祝宴会，应邀出席宴会的有中华人民共和国外交部部长助理何伟，广东省副省长贺希明，广州市市长朱光，以及广东省、广州市各机关、团体负责人和各界人民代表六百多人。
    据新华社广州四日电为庆祝苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会在广州展出，广东省副省长贺希明和广州市市长朱光在展览会开幕前夕——四日晚举行宴会，欢迎前来参加展览会开幕的苏联驻中国大使尤金院士和苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科以及展览会的全体苏联专家和苏联工作人员。陪同尤金大使前来广州的中华人民共和国外交部部长助理何伟也出席了宴会。宴会上，贺希明、尤金、何伟、朱光和鲍里辛科先后讲了话。
