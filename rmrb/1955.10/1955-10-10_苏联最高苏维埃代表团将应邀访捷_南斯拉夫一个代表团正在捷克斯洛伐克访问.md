Title: Supreme Soviet Delegation of the Soviet Union to Visit Czechoslovakia at Invitation; Yugoslav Delegation Visiting Czechoslovakia

Author:
Date: 1955-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 9th -- TASS reported: The Chairman of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic, Mr. Fierlinger, wrote to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on behalf of the Czechoslovak National Assembly, inviting the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to send a delegation to visit Czechoslovakia and attend a plenary session of the National Assembly.

The Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Mr. Volkov, and the Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, Mr. Lacis, wrote back to Mr. Fierlinger, accepting the friendly invitation extended by Mr. Fierlinger on behalf of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

A delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, headed by the Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, Mr. Tarasov, will depart for Prague on October 10th.

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th -- TASS reported: A delegation composed of representatives from the Yugoslav Chamber of Commerce and Industry Association, and the Secretariat of Foreign Affairs arrived in Brno on the 8th. The Yugoslav delegation will visit the Machine Manufacturing Exhibition and industrial enterprises in Brno.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团将应邀访捷  南斯拉夫一个代表团正在捷克斯洛伐克访问

1955-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团将应邀访捷
    南斯拉夫一个代表团正在捷克斯洛伐克访问
    新华社九日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席费林格写信给苏联最高苏维埃，代表捷克斯洛伐克国民议会邀请苏联最高苏维埃派代表团访问捷克斯洛伐克，并且参加国民议会的一次全体会议。
    苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫和民族院主席拉齐斯写回信给费林格，代表苏联最高苏维埃接受了费林格所提出的友好邀请。
    以苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席、俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团，将在十月十日启程前往布拉格。
    新华社九日讯　据塔斯社报道：由南斯拉夫商会和工业协会以及外交秘书处的代表所组成的一个代表团于八日到达布尔诺。南斯拉夫代表团在布尔诺将参观机器制造业展览会和工业企业。
