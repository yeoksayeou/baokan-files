Title: China Approves Departure of Two US Nationals
Author:
Date: 1955-10-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th -- Two US nationals, Mr. Henckel and Mrs. Henckel, residing in Shanghai, recently applied to the Chinese government to leave the country. The relevant Chinese authorities approved their request on October 12th. They are free to leave China at any time.

### 我国政府批准两名美侨出境

1955-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府批准两名美侨出境
    新华社二十日讯　居住在上海的美侨享克尔及其妻享克尔夫人最近向我国政府申请出境，我主管当局已于十月十二日批准了他们的请求，他们随时可以离开我国。
