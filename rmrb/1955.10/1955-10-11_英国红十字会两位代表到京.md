Title: Two Representatives of the British Red Cross Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-11
Page: 4

Two representatives of the British Red Cross arrived in Beijing

Mrs. Raymond Reick, Vice-Chairman of the British Red Cross, and Ms. Barker, Head of the Liaison Department of the British Red Cross, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 10th via Moscow at the invitation of the Chinese Red Cross Society for a friendly visit to China. Hu Lan-sheng, Vice President and Secretary-General of the Red Cross Society of China, Liu Qingyang, Deputy Secretary-General, Ni Feijun, and Li Jiansheng, Vice President of the Beijing Branch of the Red Cross Society of China, and others welcomed them at the airport.

A representative from the British Chargé d'Affaires also welcomed them at the airport.

### 英国红十字会两位代表到京

1955-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    英国红十字会两位代表到京
    应中国红十字会邀请来中国作友好访问的英国红十字会副主席蕾蒙里克夫人和英国红十字会联络部部长巴克女士十日下午取道莫斯科乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国红十字会总会副会长兼秘书长胡兰生，副秘书长刘清扬、倪斐君和中国红十字会北京市分会副会长李健生等人。
    英国代办处代表也到机场欢迎。
