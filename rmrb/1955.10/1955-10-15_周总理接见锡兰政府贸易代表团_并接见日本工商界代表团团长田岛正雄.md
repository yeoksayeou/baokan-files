Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Ceylon Government Trade Delegation and Receives Japanese Business Delegation Head Masao Tajima
Author:
Date: 1955-10-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation headed by Mr. H. Sri Nissanka Corea, Minister of Trade, Commerce and Fisheries of Ceylon, on the evening of the 14th.
    Present at the reception were Li Zheren, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister, and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Masao Tajima, head of the Japanese business delegation visiting China, on the morning of the 14th.
    Present at the reception were Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Vice Chairmen Lei Renmin and Li Zhuchen, and Committee Member Lu Xuzhang. (Attached picture)
    Above: Premier Zhou shakes hands with Mr. H. Sri Nissanka Corea, Minister of Trade, Commerce and Fisheries of Ceylon.
                                          Xinhua News Agency Reporter Qian Sijie photographed

### 周总理接见锡兰政府贸易代表团  并接见日本工商界代表团团长田岛正雄

1955-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见锡兰政府贸易代表团
    并接见日本工商界代表团团长田岛正雄
    新华社十四日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在十四日晚间接见了以锡兰贸易、商务和渔业部部长秀莱·科里亚为首的锡兰政府贸易代表团。
    接见时在座的，有对外贸易部代理部长李哲人、副部长雷任民，外交部副部长章汉夫。
    新华社十四日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在十四日上午接见了日本工商界访华代表团团长田岛正雄。
    接见时在座的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸，副主席雷任民、李烛尘，委员卢绪章。（附图片）
    上：周总理同锡兰贸易、商务和渔业部部长秀莱·科里亚握手。
                      新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
