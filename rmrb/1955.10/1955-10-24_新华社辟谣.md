Title: Xinhua News Agency Denies Rumor
Author:
Date: 1955-10-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: On October 20th, the United Press International (UPI) issued a message from Suez, Egypt, stating that "one hundred tons of military equipment" had arrived in Egypt from the People's Republic of China on the 20th. Xinhua News Agency learned from relevant sources that this news is completely fabricated.

### 新华社辟谣

1955-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    新华社辟谣
    新华社二十三日讯　十月二十日，美国合众社从埃及苏彝士发出的一则消息说，二十日从中华人民共和国运抵埃及“一百吨军事装备”。新华社从有关方面获悉，此项消息完全是捏造。
