Title: PLA Shooting and Sports Review Conference Closes

Author: 
Date: 1955-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: The closing ceremony of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Shooting and Sports Review Conference was held at the Xiannongtan Stadium in Beijing on October 25th.

Attending the closing ceremony were: Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Xiaoping, Marshal He Long, Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Deputy Minister of National Defense General Huang Kecheng, General Tan Zheng, General Wang Shusheng, General Li Da, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Su Yu, Deputy Chief of General Staff General Chen Geng, General Zhang Zongxun, General Li Kenong, General Peng Shaohui, as well as senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Luo Ruiqing, Air Force General Liu Yalou, responsible persons or representatives of various government departments and people's organizations.

More than 20,000 officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing and people from all walks of life in the capital participated in the conference.

At 1:50 p.m., amidst the music of the "March of the Chinese People's Liberation Army," more than 1,000 male and female shooters and athletes participating in the conference entered the venue in formation.

Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu delivered a closing speech amidst warm applause. He said: The various review results of this shooting and sports review conference are excellent. Several items have broken the original highest records of the troops, and there are many wonderful and outstanding shooting and sports performances. Especially throughout the entire review and performance process, each delegation has carried forward the glorious tradition of unity, mutual respect, mutual learning, and striving for progress, demonstrating the high degree of organization and discipline of our army with socialist education. Su Yu called on the participants of the conference to continue to carry forward their strengths, consolidate their achievements, overcome their shortcomings, guard against arrogance and impetuosity, and under the leadership of the party committees at all levels and leaders at all levels, further strengthen military training and vigorously develop shooting and sports activities in our army. Finally, he gave specific instructions on carrying out shooting and sports activities in the troops in the future.

Subsequently, Deputy Minister of National Defense General Huang Kecheng and General Li Da presented award banners to units that achieved excellent results in the shooting and sports review.

After the closing ceremony, the Chinese Youth Football Team and the Central Institute of Physical Education Football Team held a football demonstration match.

### 解放军射击与体育检阅大会闭幕

1955-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军射击与体育检阅大会闭幕
    新华社二十五日讯　中国人民解放军射击与体育检阅大会二十五日在北京先农坛体育场举行闭幕式。
    出席闭幕式的：有国务院副总理邓小平、贺龙元帅、陈毅元帅，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅，国防部副部长黄克诚大将、谭政大将、王树声大将、李达上将，中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕大将、副总参谋长陈赓大将、张宗逊上将、李克农上将、彭绍辉上将，以及中国人民解放军高级将领罗瑞卿大将、刘亚楼空军上将、政府各部门、各人民团体负责人或代表。
    参加大会的有中国人民解放军驻京部队的军官和士兵、首都各界人民共二万多人。
    下午一时五十分，在“中国人民解放军进行曲”的乐声中，参加大会的一千多名男、女射击手和运动员整队进入会场。
    中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕在热烈的掌声中致闭幕词。他说：这次射击与体育检阅大会的各种检阅成绩是优良的，有若干项突破了原来部队成绩的最高纪录，还有不少的精采和出色的射击与体育项目的表演。尤其在整个的检阅和表演过程中，各个代表队发扬了团结一致、互相尊重、互相学习、竞相进取的光荣传统，显示了我军具有社会主义教养的高度的组织性与纪律性。粟裕号召参加大会的选手们继续发扬优点，巩固成绩，克服缺点，戒骄戒躁，在各级党委和各级首长领导下，进一步加强军事训练，大力展开我军的射击与体育运动。最后，他对今后部队开展射击与体育运动作了具体指示。
    接着，国防部副部长黄克诚大将、李达上将分别把奖旗发给在射击与体育检阅中创造优秀成绩的单位。
    闭幕式结束后，中国青年足球队和中央体育学院足球队举行了足球表演赛。
