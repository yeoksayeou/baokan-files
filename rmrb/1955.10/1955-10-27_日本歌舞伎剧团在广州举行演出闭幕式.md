Title: Japanese Kabuki Troupe Holds Closing Ceremony in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-10-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 26th. The Japanese Kabuki Troupe held the closing ceremony of its visit to China at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in Guangzhou on the evening of the 25th.

Ouyang Shan, Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese Writers Association, delivered a speech on behalf of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries at the closing ceremony. He congratulated the Japanese Kabuki Troupe on the success of its performances and expressed his gratitude, saying, "Mr. Ennosuke Ichikawa and the outstanding performances of the excellent dramatists have left an unforgettable impression on the Chinese people and have been warmly welcomed. Through this kind of artistic contact, it will surely promote cultural exchange and friendship between the people of China and Japan." He hoped that after this performance of the Japanese Kabuki Troupe in China, it will open a glorious path for the connection and cooperation between the art circles of the two countries.

Narasaki Tomio, the acting head of the Japanese Kabuki Troupe, said in his speech: "In China, no matter where we go or where we are, we are welcomed sincerely and warmly. We have seen your country's great construction achievements and cultural development. In these days, we have personally witnessed and deeply felt the Chinese people's love of peace and deep friendship towards us." He believes that through this performance in China, it will be beneficial to the cultural exchange between the two countries and to peace. Finally, he once again expressed his deep gratitude to the Chinese people. Ennosuke Ichikawa then spoke, expressing his gratitude for the warm welcome from the Chinese people.

Before the closing ceremony, the Japanese Kabuki Troupe performed three plays, "Kanjincho," "Keisei Hangonsuko," and "Futari Dōjōji," which were warmly welcomed by the audience. At the end of the performance, all members of the troupe received flowers from Guangzhou literary and art workers.

### 日本歌舞伎剧团在广州举行演出闭幕式

1955-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    日本歌舞伎剧团在广州举行演出闭幕式
    新华社广州二十六日电　日本歌舞伎剧团二十五日晚在广州中山纪念堂举行了访华演出的闭幕式。
    广东省文学艺术工作者联合会主席、中国作家协会广州分会主席欧阳山代表中国人民对外文化协会在闭幕式上致词。他向日本歌舞伎剧团祝贺演出的成功并致以谢意，说：“市川猿之助先生和各位优秀的戏剧家卓越的表演，给中国人民留下了难忘的印象和受到热烈的欢迎。通过这种艺术上的接触，一定会促进中日两国人民的文化交流和两国人民的友好。”他希望经过这次日本歌舞伎剧团来中国的演出，将为两国艺术界的联系合作开辟一条光辉的道路。
    日本歌舞伎剧团代理团长楢崎富男在讲话中说：“我们在中国，无论到任何地方、任何场所，都受到真诚的热烈的欢迎。我们看到了贵国伟大的建设成就和文化的发展情况。在这些日子里，我们亲眼看到，也亲身体会到贵国人民爱好和平和对我们的深厚友谊。”他相信通过这次在中国的演出，将对两国文化的交流和对和平有益。最后，他再次地向中国人民表示深深的谢意。市川猿之助接着讲了话，他对中国人民的热烈欢迎表示感谢。
    在闭幕式举行前，日本歌舞伎剧团演出了“劝进帐”、“倾城返魂香”、“双蝶道成寺”三个剧目，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。演出结束时，剧团全体人员接受了广州市文艺工作者的献花。
