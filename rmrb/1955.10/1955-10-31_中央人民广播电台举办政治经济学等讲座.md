Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Hold Lectures on Political Economy, etc.
Author:
Date: 1955-10-31
Page: 2
The "Political Economy Lectures" and "Fundamentals of Marxism-Leninism Lectures" held by the Central People's Broadcasting Station have been scheduled for broadcast dates and times. The "Political Economy Lectures" are scheduled to begin broadcasting on October 31st. The broadcast time is from 19:30 to 20:00 every Monday and Friday, with rebroadcasts from 7:00 to 7:30 every Tuesday and Saturday. The "Fundamentals of Marxism-Leninism Lectures" are scheduled to begin broadcasting on November 16th. The broadcast time is from 19:30 to 20:00 every Wednesday, with rebroadcasts from 7:00 to 7:30 every Thursday. Both lectures will be broadcast on the station's second program.

### 中央人民广播电台举办政治经济学等讲座

1955-10-31
第2版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台举办政治经济学等讲座
    中央人民广播电台举办的“政治经济学讲座”和“马克思列宁主义基础讲座”已确定广播日期和时间。“政治经济学讲座”定在十月三十一日开始广播。广播时间是每星期一、星期五的十九点三十分到二十点，每星期二、星期六的上午七点到七点三十分重播。“马克思列宁主义基础讲座”定于十一月十六日开始广播，广播时间是每星期三的十九点三十分到二十点，每星期四的上午七点到七点三十分重播。两个讲座都在该台第二种节目中播送。
