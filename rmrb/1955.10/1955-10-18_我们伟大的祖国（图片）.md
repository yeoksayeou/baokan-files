Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-10-18
Page: 1

Our Great Motherland
Thanks to the development of the agricultural cooperation movement and the effectiveness of the Huai River harnessing project, more than ten million mu of rice in the Lixiahe area of Jiangsu Province have achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest. Members of the Xinlian Agricultural Cooperative in Zhahe Township, Gaoyou County, are selling their first batch of 4,000 jin of new grain to the state.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    由于农业合作化运动的开展和治淮工程所起的效益，使江苏省里下河地区一千多万亩水稻获得空前丰收。高邮县闸河乡新联农业合作社的社员正在把第一批四千斤新粮卖给国家。
