Title: Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej Elected First Secretary, Decision Made to Convene Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, October 2nd: The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party held a plenary session on September 30th and October 1st. The first secretaries of the Workers' Party's state committees participated in the plenary session.

After discussion, the plenary session unanimously elected Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej as the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and relieved Apostol of his duties as First Secretary of the Party Central Committee. The plenary session also unanimously elected Chişinevschi as Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party. Apostol was elected Chairman of the Central Council of Romanian Trade Unions by the Central Council of Trade Unions.

The plenary session also decided to convene the Second Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party on December 23rd this year. The agenda of the Congress is as follows:

	I. Summary report of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party – Reporter: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej.
	II. Report of the Central Auditing Commission – Reporter: Guina.
	III. Report of the Second Congress on the directives for the second Five-Year Plan for the development of the national economy from 1956 to 1960 – Reporter: Stoica.
	IV. Report on the revision of the Constitution of the Romanian Workers' Party – Reporter: Ceauşescu.
	V. Election of the party's central organs.

The plenary session also formulated practical measures for the successful completion of the autumn agricultural work in 1955.

### 罗马尼亚工人党中央举行全会  选举乔治乌—德治为第一书记，决定召开党代表大会

1955-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚工人党中央举行全会
    选举乔治乌—德治为第一书记，决定召开党代表大会
    新华社三日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特二日讯：罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会在九月三十日和十月一日举行了全体会议。工人党的各州委会第一书记参加了全体会议。
    全体会议经过讨论后，一致选举乔治乌—德治为罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记，解除阿波斯托尔所担任的党中央委员会第一书记的职务。全体会议还一致选举基辛湼夫斯基为罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会书记。阿波斯托尔被罗马尼亚工会中央理事会选举为工会中央理事会主席。
    全体会议并且决定在今年十二月二十三日召开罗马尼亚工人党第二次代表大会。代表大会的议程如下：
    一、罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会的总结报告——报告人为乔治乌—德治。
    二、中央检查委员会的报告——报告人为古伊纳。
    三、第二次代表大会关于在一九五六年到一九六零年间发展国民经济第二个五年计划的指示的报告——报告人为斯托伊卡。
    四、修改罗马尼亚工人党党章的报告——报告人为齐奥塞斯库。
    五、选举党的中央机构。
    全体会议还制定了有关胜利结束一九五五年秋季农业工作的实际措施。
