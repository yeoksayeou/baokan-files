Title: British Quaker Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    British Quaker Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the British Quaker delegation of six members, led by团长 Duncan Wood, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou on the 5th, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

### 英国公谊会代表团到京

1955-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    英国公谊会代表团到京
    据新华社讯应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国参观访问的英国公谊会代表团一行六人，由团长邓肯·伍德率领，五日由广州乘飞机抵北京。
