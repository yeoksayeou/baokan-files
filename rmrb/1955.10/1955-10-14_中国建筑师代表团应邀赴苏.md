Title: Chinese Architects Delegation Invited to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Architectural Society of China, at the invitation of the Soviet Union of Architects, has sent a delegation of ten architects to visit the Soviet Union. The delegation, led by Wan Li, left for Moscow by plane on the morning of the 13th.

The delegation will visit Moscow, Leningrad, and other major central cities to study the architectural situation and achievements of architects in the Soviet Union.

### 中国建筑师代表团应邀赴苏

1955-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中国建筑师代表团应邀赴苏
    新华社十三日讯　中国建筑学会应苏联建筑师协会邀请，派遣了由十人组成的建筑师代表团去苏联进行访问。代表团在团长万里率领下已在十三日晨乘飞机去莫斯科。
    代表团将访问莫斯科、列宁格勒及其他较大的中心城市，考察苏联方面的建筑情况和建筑师们的成就。
