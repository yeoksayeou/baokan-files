Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Receive Canadian Foreign Minister Pearson
Author:
Date: 1955-10-13
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Yalta Dispatch: According to TASS, Canadian Foreign Minister Pearson and his party, who are currently visiting the Soviet Union, arrived in Miskhor, on the southern coast of Crimea on the Black Sea, by plane from Stalingrad on the 11th.

    Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, and Khrushchev, member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, who are vacationing in Miskhor, received Pearson that day and held talks with him.

    Present at the talks were Canadian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Watkins and Soviet Ambassador to Canada Tuvakin, among others.

    After the talks, Bulganin and Khrushchev hosted a banquet in honor of Pearson.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫接见加外长皮尔逊

1955-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫接见加外长皮尔逊
    新华社十二日讯　据塔斯社雅尔塔讯：正在苏联访问的加拿大外交部长皮尔逊和他的一行人员，十一日从斯大林格勒乘飞机到达克里米亚南部黑海沿岸的米斯赫尔。
    正在米斯赫尔度假的苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫在当天接见了皮尔逊并和他进行了交谈。
    谈话时在座的有加拿大驻苏联大使华金斯和苏联驻加拿大大使楚瓦欣等人。
    谈话之后，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫设宴招待皮尔逊。
