Title: Belgian Business and Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: The Belgian business and trade delegation, a group of nine led by the head of the delegation, Mr. Schullen, a member of the Belgian House of Representatives, arrived in Beijing by train at 6:30 AM on the 6th from Guangzhou.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included Ni Weiting, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and Ding Kejian, Deputy Head of the former Chinese Trade Delegation to Belgium and representative of the China National Import and Export Corporation's Berlin Representative Office.

That evening, Ni Weiting hosted a banquet for all members of the visiting delegation.

### 比工商界贸易访问团到京

1955-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    比工商界贸易访问团到京
    新华社六日讯比利时工商界贸易访问团一行九人由团长、比利时国会下议院议员舒伦率领，在六日晨六时三十分由广州乘车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有中国进出口公司副经理倪蔚庭和前中国赴比利时贸易访问团副团长、中国进出口公司驻柏林代表处代表丁克坚等人。
    当日晚，倪蔚庭宴请访问团全体人员。
