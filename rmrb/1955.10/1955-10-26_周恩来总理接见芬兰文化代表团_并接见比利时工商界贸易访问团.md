Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Finnish Cultural Delegation and Belgian Business and Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Kulo, the deputy head, and ten members, including Kahkonen, of the Finnish Cultural Delegation, which is currently visiting China, on the afternoon of the 25th.

Present at the reception were Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, Yang Hanseng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Huang Hua, the Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also present was the Finnish Ambassador to China, Sundström.

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Mr. Schuurmans, the head of the Belgian Business and Trade Delegation and a member of the Belgian Parliament, and all the members of the delegation, on the afternoon of the 25th. The delegation was invited to visit China.

Present at the reception were Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, Huang Hua, the Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

### 周恩来总理接见芬兰文化代表团  并接见比利时工商界贸易访问团

1955-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见芬兰文化代表团
    并接见比利时工商界贸易访问团
    新华社二十五日讯　中华人民共和国国务院周恩来总理在二十五日下午接见了现在中国访问的芬兰文化代表团副团长库洛和团员卡赫宁等十人。
    接见时在座的，有中华人民共和国农业部长廖鲁言，中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华。
    在座的还有芬兰驻华大使孙士敦。
    新华社二十五日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二十五日下午接见了应邀来中国访问的比利时工商界贸易访问团团长、比利时国会议员舒伦和全体团员。
    接见时在座的，有对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华等人。
