Title: Jakarta "People's Daily" Circulation Increases, Becomes Largest Newspaper in Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1955-10-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta Telegram. According to a report in the Jakarta "People's Daily" of Indonesia on the 3rd, the circulation of the "People's Daily" has recently increased from 38,000 copies to 58,000 copies, becoming the largest newspaper in all of Indonesia.

### 雅加达“人民日报”销行份数增加  已成为印度尼西亚最大报纸

1955-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    雅加达“人民日报”销行份数增加
    已成为印度尼西亚最大报纸
    新华社雅加达电　据印度尼西亚雅加达“人民日报”三日报道，“人民日报”的销行份数最近已经从过去的三万八千份增加到五万八千份，成为全印度尼西亚最大的报纸。
