Title: Jordan and Afghanistan Indicate They Will Not Join the Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1955-10-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, October 25th, via TASS: The Beirut newspaper "Al Hayat" reports from Amman that Jordanian Prime Minister Said al-Mufti has rejected the British government's proposal for Jordan to join the Baghdad Pact.

The Prime Minister told the British Ambassador in Amman that Jordan's participation in such a treaty would be contrary to Jordan's national interests. The Prime Minister said that he would rather resign than agree to Jordan's participation in the Baghdad Pact.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 26th: According to a United Press report from Kabul on the 26th, a spokesman for the Afghan Foreign Ministry said that Afghanistan is not prepared to join the Turkish-Iraqi pact.

### 约旦和阿富汗表示不参加巴格达条约

1955-10-28
第4版()
专栏：

    约旦和阿富汗表示不参加巴格达条约
    新华社二十六日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特二十五日讯：黎巴嫩“明星日报”记者从安曼报道，约旦首相赛义德·穆弗蒂已经拒绝了英国政府向约旦提出的参加巴格达条约的建议。
    首相对驻安曼的英国大使说，约旦参加这种条约是和约旦的民族利益相违背的。这位首相说，他宁愿辞职也不同意约旦参加巴格达条约。
    新华社新德里二十六日电　据合众社二十六日喀布尔消息，阿富汗外交部的一位发言人说，阿富汗不准备参加土耳其—伊拉克条约。
