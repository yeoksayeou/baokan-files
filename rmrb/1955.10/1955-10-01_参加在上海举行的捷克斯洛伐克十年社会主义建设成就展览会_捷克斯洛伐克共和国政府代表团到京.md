Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend the Exhibition of Achievements in Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia Held in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4

    To Attend the Exhibition of Achievements in Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia Held in Shanghai
    Czechoslovak Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Czechoslovak Government Delegation, participating in the Exhibition of Achievements in Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia held in Shanghai, arrived in Beijing by plane from Prague on the 30th.
    The delegation is led by Uher, Minister of the Food Industry of Czechoslovakia. Members of the delegation include the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Grigor (already in Beijing), Commercial Counsellor Šal (already in Beijing), Bužek, Manager of Moto Kovopodnik, Ševr, Deputy Manager of Strojexport, and Molis, Secretary of the delegation.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Yang Weiyu, Vice Minister of Light Industry; Wang Ping, Special Commissioner of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.
    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Grigor, also went to welcome them.
    After participating in our National Day celebrations in Beijing, the delegation will go to Shanghai to attend the opening of the exhibition.
    On the same evening, Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, held a banquet to welcome all members of the delegation.

### 参加在上海举行的捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会  捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团到京

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    参加在上海举行的捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团到京
    新华社三十日讯　参加在上海举行的捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团，在三十日从布拉格乘飞机到京。
    代表团由捷克斯洛伐克食品工业部部长乌赫率领，团员中有捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使格里哥尔（已在京）、商务参赞沙尔（已在京）、摩托五金公司经理布泽克、机器出口公司副经理塞夫尔特和代表团秘书莫利斯。
    到机场欢迎的有：中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸，轻工业部副部长杨卫玉，外交部礼宾司专员王屏等。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使格里哥尔也前往欢迎。
    代表团在北京参加我国国庆典礼以后，就去上海参加展览会的开幕。
    同日晚，中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸举行宴会欢迎代表团全体人员。
