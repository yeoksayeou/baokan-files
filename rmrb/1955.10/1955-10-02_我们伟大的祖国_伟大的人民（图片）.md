Our Great Motherland, Our Great People (Pictures)
    On October 1, 1955, people from all walks of life in the capital held a grand military parade and a grand mass parade in Tiananmen Square to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. This is a magnificent view in front of Tiananmen Square on the great festival. (Special Correspondent of this newspaper, Hou Bo, photographed)
    On the Tiananmen reviewing stand, Chairman Mao Zedong waved to the parading masses. (Special Correspondent of this newspaper, Hou Bo, photographed)
    Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, reviewed the army, navy, and air force units in a review car and extended festive congratulations to them. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Huiru photographed)
    Rocket artillery troops pass through Tiananmen Square. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Jiang Qisheng photographed)
    More than 12,000 people attended the ceremony, these are the foreign guests on the viewing platform. (This newspaper reporter Na Yi photographed)
    Tank troops pass through Tiananmen Square. (Special Correspondent of this newspaper, Hou Bo, photographed)
    Representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Tiananmen viewing platform. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Jingkang photographed)
    Workers carry models and charts representing new achievements on the production front through Tiananmen Square. (This newspaper reporter Gao Liang photographed)
    Farmer teams pass through Tiananmen Square. (This newspaper reporter Yuan Yiping photographed)
    Young students shout "Long Live Chairman Mao!" (This newspaper reporter Na Yi photographed)
    Lively and healthy Young Pioneers pass through Tiananmen Square. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Jiang Qisheng photographed)
    The sports team marches in front of Tiananmen Square. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ling photographed)
    Dance performers in the art troupe perform the lotus lantern dance while passing through Tiananmen Square. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ling photographed)

### 我们伟大的祖国 伟大的人民（图片）

1955-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国 伟大的人民（图片）
    一九五五年十月一日，首都各界人民在天安门广场举行了隆重的阅兵式和盛大的群众游行，庆祝中华人民共和国建国六周年。这是伟大节日天安门前的盛景。          （本报特约记者　侯　波摄）
    在天安门检阅台上，毛泽东主席向游行的群众举手答礼。
