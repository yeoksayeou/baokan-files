Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Nenni
Author: Tan Peizhang
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Pietro Nenni, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, Chairman of the Italian National Peace Council, Secretary-General of the Italian Socialist Party, his wife, his daughter Luciana Tomassi, the Executive Member of the Italian Socialist Party, Panzieri, and the Secretary of the Italian National Food Union, Onsanelli, and other five people, at 11:30 a.m. on the 30th, accompanied by Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

The host and guests exchanged views on the international situation and issues related to Sino-Italian relations, and had lunch together.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace Guo Moruo, and Standing Committee Member Deng Yingchao. (Attached picture)

The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai receiving Nenni. From left to right: Deng Yingchao, Mrs. Nenni, Nenni, Zhou Enlai.
                                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Tan Peizhang

### 周恩来总理接见南尼
谈培章
1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见南尼
    新华社三十日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在三十日上午十一时半接见世界和平理事会副主席、意大利全国和平理事会主席、意大利社会党总书记南尼和他的夫人、女儿卢契安娜·托马西、意大利社会党执行委员潘齐埃里和意大利全国食品工会书记昂萨奈利等五人，由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一陪同前往。
    宾主双方就国际局势和有关中意两国关系等问题交换意见，并共进午餐。
    接见时在座的有国务院副总理陈毅，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、常务委员邓颖超。（附图片）
    图为周恩来总理接见南尼时情形。自左至右：邓颖超、南尼夫人、南尼、周恩来。
                                    新华社记者　谈培章摄
