Title: UN Disarmament Commission to Meet Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1955-10-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New York, October 18th (TASS): According to news from the United Nations Secretariat, the United Nations Disarmament Commission will hold a meeting on October 21.

The commission is being convened at the suggestion of the Soviet Union's Permanent Representative to the United Nations, Sobolev. In a letter to the Chairman of the United Nations Disarmament Commission on October 14, Sobolev requested a meeting of the commission to discuss the working report of the United Nations Disarmament Commission Subcommittee and the report submitted by the United Nations Disarmament Commission to the Security Council and the General Assembly.

### 联合国裁军委员会明日开会

1955-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国裁军委员会明日开会
    新华社十八日讯　塔斯社纽约讯：据联合国秘书处消息，十月二十一日将召开联合国裁军委员会会议。
    委员会是根据苏联常驻联合国代表索波列夫的建议而将召开的。索波列夫在十月十四日在写给联合国裁军委员会主席的信中请求召开委员会会议，讨论联合国裁军委员会小组委员会的工作报告和联合国裁军委员会向安全理事会和大会提出的报告。
