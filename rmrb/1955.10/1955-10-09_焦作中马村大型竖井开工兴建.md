Title: Large Shaft Construction Begins in Zhongmacun, Jiaozuo
Author: Liu Zhipeng
Date: 1955-10-09
Page: 2

    Large Shaft Construction Begins in Zhongmacun, Jiaozuo
    After three years of exploration, the large shaft in Zhongmacun, Jiaozuo Coal Mine, officially commenced construction on September 20th. Soviet experts provided significant assistance in the mine's design, geological exploration, and construction preparation.
    The entire production process of this mine will be mechanized and automated, making it the first modern mine in the Central and Southern regions.
                                (Liu Zhipeng)

### 焦作中马村大型竖井开工兴建
刘志鹏
1955-10-09
第2版()
专栏：

    焦作中马村大型竖井开工兴建
    焦作煤矿中马村大型竖井经过三年的勘探，在九月二十日正式开工兴建。这对矿井的设计、地质勘探和施工准备，都得到了苏联专家的很大帮助。
    这对矿井的全部生产过程将是机械化自动化的，是目前中南地区第一个现代化的矿井。
                                （刘志鹏）
