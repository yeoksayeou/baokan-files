Title: Chinese Envoys in Various Countries Hold Receptions to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-10-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia Wu Xiuquan held a grand reception on October 1st to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Those present at the reception included President of the Federal People's Assembly of Yugoslavia, Pijade; Vice Presidents of the Federal Executive Council, Kardelj, Ranković, and Vukmanović-Tempo; Chief of Staff of the Yugoslav Army, Vucković; members of the Federal Executive Council and other senior military and political leaders; figures from cultural and journalistic circles; and diplomatic personnel from many countries stationed in Yugoslavia. The delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, currently visiting Yugoslavia, also attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Norway Wang Youping held a reception on the evening of the 1st to celebrate China's National Day. Those present at the reception included Norwegian Court Chamberlain Smith-Kielland, Minister of Commerce Skaug, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court Grette, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Skjelsvik, Army Commander Hansteen, people from all walks of life in Norway, and about 300 diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in Norway.

Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China in the United Kingdom Huan Xiang held a reception in London on the evening of the 3rd to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Approximately one thousand people attended, including the Marquess of Reading, Deputy Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs; Lord Boyd-Orr of the British Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and Comptroller General of Customs and Excise, Crawby. Also present were Harry Pollitt, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain; Morgan Phillips, General Secretary of the Labour Party; Labour MPs Aneurin Bevan and his wife; Conservative MP Morrison; Liberal MP Davies; the Chairman of the Britain-China Friendship Association; and other individuals from the aforementioned parties and organizations. Diplomatic officials from the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Sweden, Switzerland, Denmark, Finland, Indonesia, India, Pakistan, Burma, Ceylon, Afghanistan, Saudi Arabia, Cambodia, Laos, and other countries stationed in the United Kingdom also attended the reception.

### 我国驻各国使节举行招待会庆祝国庆

1955-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    我国驻各国使节举行招待会庆祝国庆
    据新华社讯　中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权十月一日举行盛大招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立六周年。出席招待会的有南斯拉夫联邦国民议会议长皮雅杰，联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔、兰科维奇和伏克曼诺维奇—泰波，南斯拉夫陆军参谋长伏茨科维奇，联邦执行委员会委员和其他高级军政领导人员，文化新闻界人士以及许多国家驻南外交人员。现在南斯拉夫访问的中华全国总工会代表团也参加了招待会。
    中国驻挪威大使王幼平在一日晚举行招待会，庆祝中国国庆。出席招待会的有挪威宫廷侍从长史密斯—凯伦特、商务大臣斯卡恩、最高法院院长格莱脱、外交部秘书长斯盖尔斯脱特、陆军司令汉斯汀、挪威各界人士及各国驻挪威外交人员约三百人。
    中华人民共和国驻英国代办宦乡三日晚在伦敦举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国建国六周年。到会的约一千人，其中有英国副外交大臣里丁侯爵、英国国际贸易促进委员会的波伊德—奥尔勋爵、海关税务局局长克劳比；还有英国共产党总书记波立特、工党总书记菲利普斯、工党议员比万夫妇、保守党议员摩里逊、自由党议员戴维斯、英中友好协会主席以及上述各党派团体的其他人士。苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、瑞典、瑞士、丹麦、芬兰、印度尼西亚、印度、巴基斯坦、缅甸、锡兰、阿富汗、沙特阿拉伯、柬埔寨、老挝以及其他国家驻英国外交官员也出席了招待会。
