Title: Guo Moruo Hosts a Banquet to Welcome the British Friends Service Council Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 7th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet on October 7th to welcome the British Friends Service Council Delegation.  Among those attending the banquet were Reverend Wang Zizhong of the North China Congregational Church, Reverend of the Methodist Church, Dr. Li Rongfang, Dean of the Yenching Union Theological Seminary, Zhao Fusan, Vice Dean of the Yenching Union Theological Seminary and Chairman of the Chung Hua Sheng Kung Hui (Chinese Anglican Church), Reverend Feng Guangjie of the Friends Service Council, Reverend Yin Jizeng of the Church of Christ in China, Chen Wenrun, General Secretary of the Beijing YWCA, Zheng Ruihua, Secretary, Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Standing Committee members Luo Longji, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Secretary-General Liu Guanyi, Committee member Zhou Binglin, Qian Duansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Zhang Fusheng, representative of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

The banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 郭沫若设宴欢迎英国公谊会代表团

1955-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若设宴欢迎英国公谊会代表团
    新华社七日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若在十月七日设宴欢迎英国公谊会代表团。参加宴会的有华北公理会王梓仲牧师、卫理公会牧师、燕京协和神学院教务长李荣芳博士、燕京协和神学院副教务长、中华圣公会会长赵复三、公谊会冯光杰牧师、中华基督教会殷继增牧师、北京基督教女青年会总干事陈文润、干事郑瑞华、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、常务委员罗隆基、许德珩、许宝驹、秘书长刘贯一、委员周炳琳，中国人民外交学会副会长钱端升，中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记曹孟君和中国国际贸易促进委员会代表张福生等。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。
