Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1955-10-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 6th. The 19th meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held at the Palace of Nations at 10:00 am on the 5th. The meeting adjourned at 12:20 pm. The next meeting is scheduled for 10:00 am on Saturday, October 8th.

### 中美两国大使继续举行会谈

1955-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使继续举行会谈
    新华社日内瓦六日电中美两国大使级会谈五日上午十时在国联大厦举行第十九次会议。会议在十二时二十分休会，下次会议定于十月八日（星期六）上午十时举行。
