Title: Celebrating the Sixth Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic; Chargé d'Affaires of the German Embassy in China Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1955-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, Mr. Grutner, held a reception on the evening of the 7th to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, attended the reception.

At the beginning of the reception, the band played the national anthems of the German Democratic Republic and the People's Republic of China successively.

At the reception, Mr. Grutner and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, delivered speeches.

Also present at this evening's reception were Li Jishen and Shen Junru, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Yi and Ulanhu, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; heads of various ministries and commissions; senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; heads of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee; heads of various people's organizations; and people from the capital's science, education, literature, art, and journalism circles.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China also attended the reception.

After the reception, German artists performed wonderful programs.

On the afternoon of the 7th, the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the German Democratic Republic, Mr. Grutner, also held a film reception, showing the film "Ernst Thälmann - Leader of His Class" to commemorate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

### 庆祝德意志民主共和国成立六周年  德驻华大使馆代办举行招待会

1955-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝德意志民主共和国成立六周年
    德驻华大使馆代办举行招待会
    新华社七日讯　德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办葛鲁特纳为庆祝德意志民主共和国成立六周年在七日晚举行了招待会。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德出席了招待会。
    招待会开始时，乐队先后奏德意志民主共和国和中华人民共和国两国国歌。
    在招待会上，葛鲁特纳和中华人民共和国国务院副总理邓小平讲了话。
    出席今晚招待会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒，国务院副总理陈毅、乌兰夫，外交部副部长张闻天，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，各部、各委员会负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员，北京市人民委员会负责人，各人民团体负责人，首都科学、教育、文艺、新闻界人士。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也出席了招待会。
    在招待会结束后，德国的艺术家们表演了精采节目。
    七日下午，德意志民主共和国临时代办葛鲁特纳并曾举行电影招待会，放映影片“恩斯特·台尔曼——他的阶级的领袖”，以纪念德意志民主共和国成立六周年。
