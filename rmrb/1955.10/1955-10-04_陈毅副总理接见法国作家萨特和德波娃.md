Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives French Writers Sartre and de Beauvoir
Author:
Date: 1955-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received the famous French writer Jean-Paul Sartre and the writer Simone de Beauvoir at 9:00 a.m. on the 3rd.

Present at the reception were Mao Dun, Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Culture Ministry, Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and writers Lao She and Chen Xue Zhao.

### 陈毅副总理接见法国作家萨特和德波娃

1955-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见法国作家萨特和德波娃
    新华社三日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅在三日上午九时接见了法国著名作家让·保罗·萨特和女作家西蒙娜·德波娃。
    接见时在座的有中国作家协会主席茅盾、文化部副部长夏衍、中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙、作家老舍、陈学昭。
