Title: China's Courts Release Two American Criminals Early
Author:
Date: 1955-10-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 29th: China's courts have, according to law, sentenced two Americans, Milham Reeve and Luke Nien, who committed crimes in China, to imprisonment. Considering that these American criminals have behaved relatively well during their imprisonment, the courts have decided to release them early and deport them.

### 我国法院提前释放两名美籍罪犯

1955-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我国法院提前释放两名美籍罪犯
    新华社二十九日讯　我国法院已依法把在中国犯法的两名美国人密含瑞和卢克念分别判处徒刑。鉴于各该美犯在服刑期间表现尚好，法院决定把他们提前释放，并驱逐出境。
