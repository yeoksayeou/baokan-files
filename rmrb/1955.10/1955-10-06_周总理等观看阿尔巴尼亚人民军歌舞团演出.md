Title: Premier Zhou and Others Watch Performance by Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th: The Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe held a grand performance in Beijing on the evening of the 5th.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Li Jishen and Li Weihan, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council; Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council; Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Shusheng, General of the Ministry of National Defense; Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture; Xiao Hua, General, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; and Chen Yi, Major General, Minister of Culture, attended the performance of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe.

Albanian Ambassador to China, Nase; the Albanian Cultural Delegation headed by Fadil Paçrami; the Polish Army Song and Dance Troupe headed by Lieutenant Colonel Wolva and Colonel Ruda; and the German Democratic Republic Wind Orchestra headed by Paul Kutschbach attended tonight's party.

Also attending the party were members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, heads of various government departments, members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, heads of various people's organizations, staff members of central and Beijing municipal organs, people from the capital's literary and art circles, and local work leaders who are in Beijing for meetings.

At the party, the performances of the artists of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe were warmly welcomed, and each program won continuous applause from the audience.

After the performance, amidst warm applause from the audience, literary and art workers from the capital presented flowers to the performers of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe. Then, Premier Zhou Enlai and others went on stage and shook hands cordially with the Albanian artists. At this time, the Albanian artists cheered "Mao Zedong!" "Long Live!" amidst warm applause from the audience.

### 周总理等观看阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团演出

1955-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理等观看阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团演出
    新华社五日讯阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团五日晚在北京举行了一次盛大的演出晚会。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、李维汉，国务院副总理邓小平，国防委员会副主席张治中，外交部副部长张闻天，国防部副部长王树声大将，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华上将、文化部部长陈沂少将出席观看了阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团的精采演出。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使纳赛，以法迪尔·帕奇拉米为首的阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团和以沃尔瓦中校、鲁达上校为首的波兰军队歌舞团和以保尔·库茨巴赫为首的德意志民主共和国吹奏乐团，出席了今天的晚会。
    出席晚会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，政府各部门负责人，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员，各人民团体负责人，中央和北京市各机关工作人员，首都文艺界人士和正在北京开会的各地地方工作负责人。
    晚会上，阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团艺术家们的表演受到了极大欢迎，每个节目都博得了观众的不断掌声。
    演出结束后，在全场热烈掌声中，首都文艺工作者向阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团的演出人员献花。接着，周恩来总理等上台和阿尔巴尼亚的艺术家们亲切握手。这时，阿尔巴尼亚艺术家们在全场的热烈掌声中欢呼：“毛泽东！”“万岁！”
