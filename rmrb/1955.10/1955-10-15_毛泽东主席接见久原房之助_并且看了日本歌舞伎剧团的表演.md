Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Fusanosuke Kuhara and Watches Performance by Japanese Kabuki Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-10-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Fusanosuke Kuhara, Chairman of the Japanese National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations, on the afternoon of October 2nd. On October 14th, Chairman Mao Zedong again received Fusanosuke Kuhara. Also present at the reception were Masashima Takeshi, Obata Tadashi, Nishikawa Sadaichi, Kuhara Tonozaburo, and Kuhara Fumiko, who came to China with Fusanosuke Kuhara.

Premier Zhou Enlai was present at both receptions.

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: After giving eight wonderful performances for more than 12,000 Beijing audience members, the Japanese Kabuki Troupe concluded its activities in Beijing on the evening of the 14th. Led by Ichikawa Ennosuke, the head of the troupe, and Narasaki Tomio, the deputy head, they left Beijing by special train for a performance tour in Shanghai. The troupe leader, Matsuo Kunizo, will depart from Beijing to return to Japan.

During the performances, Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and other national leaders attended and watched the Kabuki Troupe's performances. After watching the performance, Chairman Mao Zedong also received the troupe leader Matsuo Kunizo, the head of the troupe Ichikawa Ennosuke, the deputy head Narasaki Tomio, and principal actors Ichikawa Chusha, Ichikawa Danshiro, Ichikawa Arajiro, Ichikawa Yaozo, Iwai Hanshiro, Ichikawa Matsutori, Kineya Sajiro, Kineya Sanshiro, and Sakata Senzaburo.

When the Kabuki Troupe left Beijing, those who came to the station to see them off included Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Tian Han, Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, Cao Yu, Zhao Shuli, and other figures from Beijing's literary and art circles, as well as more than 60 famous Peking Opera actors.

During their stay in Beijing, the Kabuki Troupe participated in the joint performances of art groups from various countries at the National Day celebrations and the National Day reception hosted by Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen. Japanese artists also held discussions with people from literary and art circles in Beijing and watched Peking Opera, opera, song and dance, and acrobatics performances. Members of the Kabuki Troupe also visited the Chinese Peking Opera Theatre, the Central Academy of Drama, and the Beijing Dance School. They visited the National Exhibition of Natural Environment and Mineral Resources, the Dunhuang Art Exhibition, and the Rongbaozhai woodblock printing exhibition in Beijing, and toured the Summer Palace, Badaling, and other famous historical sites. ### 毛泽东主席接见久原房之助  并且看了日本歌舞伎剧团的表演

1955-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见久原房之助
    并且看了日本歌舞伎剧团的表演
    新华社十四日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席于十月二日下午接见日本恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议会长久原房之助。十月十四日毛泽东主席再次接见久原房之助，同久原房之助一起来中国访问的马岛僴、小畑忠良、西川贞一、久原统三郎和久原文子也同时受到接见。
    两次接见时，周恩来总理均在座。
    新华社十四日讯　日本歌舞伎剧团为北京观众一万二千多人作了八场精采的表演后，十四日晚结束了在北京的活动，由座长市川猿之助、副团长楢崎富男率领乘专车离北京去上海访问演出。团长松尾国三将从北京启程返回日本。
    在演出中，毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理和其他国家领导人员曾先后出席观看了歌舞伎剧团的表演。毛泽东主席在观剧后，并接见了剧团团长松尾国三，座长市川猿之助，副团长楢崎富男，主要演员市川中车、市川段四郎、市川荒次郎、市川八百藏、岩井半四郎、市川松茑、杵屋佐次郎、杵屋三四郎、坂田仙三郎。
    歌舞伎剧团离京时，到车站欢送的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，中国戏剧家协会主席田汉，北京文艺界人士曹禺、赵树理和著名京剧演员等六十多人。
    歌舞伎剧团在北京期间，参加了庆祝国庆典礼和北京市市长彭真举行的庆祝国庆招待晚会上的各国艺术团体联合演出。日本艺术家在北京还和文艺界人士举行了座谈，观看了京剧、歌剧、歌舞和杂技表演。歌舞伎剧团人员还访问了中国京剧院、中央戏剧学院和北京舞蹈学校，参观了北京的祖国自然环境与矿产资源展览、敦煌艺术展览和荣宝斋木版水印画展览，游览了颐和园、八达岭等名胜古迹。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席接见日本恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议会长久原房之助。
                      新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
