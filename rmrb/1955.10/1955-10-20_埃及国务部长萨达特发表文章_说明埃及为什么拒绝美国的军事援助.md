Title: Egyptian Minister of State Sadat Publishes Article Explaining Why Egypt Rejected US Military Aid
Author:
Date: 1955-10-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 19th (TASS dispatch): Egyptian Minister of State and head of the official "At-Tahrir" publishing house, Anwar Sadat, published an article in "Al-Gomhuria" entitled "Why Egypt Rejected US Military Aid." Sadat wrote that the United States' proposals to provide Egypt with weapons or "military aid" have always been accompanied by many conditions that Egypt cannot accept.

Sadat wrote: "In August 1954, the US government suggested to the Egyptian government that they sign a military assistance agreement. We rejected the US draft of this agreement because the conditions proposed by the US would allow them to establish control over Egypt's military, economy, and politics, thereby undermining Egypt's sovereignty and independence."

Sadat said: "The Americans demanded the right to send a US military mission to Egypt, the purpose of which was clearly to establish US control over the Egyptian armed forces. The United States also demanded that Egypt not sell strategic materials to communist countries, including Egypt's agricultural products; and the sale of agricultural products is of vital importance to Egypt's national economy. Fulfilling these conditions would make Egypt's economy completely subordinate to the United States."

Sadat said: "It is because of this that Egypt rejected the US proposal and demanded that weapons be sold to Egypt on a purely commercial basis, without any conditions attached."

### 埃及国务部长萨达特发表文章  说明埃及为什么拒绝美国的军事援助

1955-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及国务部长萨达特发表文章
    说明埃及为什么拒绝美国的军事援助
    新华社十九日讯　塔斯社开罗十八日讯：埃及国务部长、官方的“阿特·塔林”出版社领导人安瓦尔·萨达特在“共和国报”上发表一篇题为“为什么埃及拒绝美国的军事援助”的文章。萨达特写道，美国关于给予埃及武器或“军事援助”的建议一向附带着许多埃及所不能接受的条件。
    萨达特写道：“一九五四年八月，美国政府向埃及政府建议签订一项军事援助协定。我们拒绝了美国的这个协定的草案，因为美国所提出的条件使得他们可以对埃及军事、经济和政治建立控制，从而损害埃及的主权和独立。”
    萨达特说：“美国人要求得到派遣美国军事使团到埃及的权利，它的目的显然是要建立美国对埃及武装部队的控制。美国还要求，埃及不要把战略物资卖给共产主义国家，其中包括埃及的农产品；而农产品的销售对埃及的国民经济有着切身的重要意义。履行这些条件将会使得埃及的经济完全从属于美国。”
    萨达特说：“就是因为这样，埃及拒绝了美国的建议，而要求在纯粹商业的基础上、不附加任何条件地售卖武器给埃及。”
