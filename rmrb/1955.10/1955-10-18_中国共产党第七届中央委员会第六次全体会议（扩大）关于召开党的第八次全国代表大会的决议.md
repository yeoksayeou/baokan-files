Title: Resolution of the Sixth Plenary Session (Enlarged) of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on Convening the Eighth National Congress of the Party
Author:
Date: 1955-10-18
Page: 1

    The Sixth Plenary Session (Enlarged) of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Resolution on Convening the Eighth National Congress of the Party
    (Adopted on October 11, 1955)
    (1) It is decided to convene the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China in the second half of 1956. The specific time for convening the congress shall be determined by the Political Bureau of the Central Committee.
    (2) The main agenda of this congress is as follows:
      (1) Work report of the Central Committee of the Party;
      (2) Report on the revision of the Party Constitution;
      (3) Instructions on the Second Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy;
      (4) Election of the Central Committee of the Party.
    (3) Delegates to the Eighth National Congress shall be elected by Party congresses convened separately by the Party organizations of each province, autonomous region, and municipality directly under the Central Government, as well as the Party organizations of the organs directly under the Central Committee and the central state organs. The Tibet region may convene a Party representative meeting to elect delegates.
    Delegates from the Party organizations of the Chinese People's Liberation Army shall be elected by the Party Congress of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    (4) The election of delegates shall be conducted in accordance with the provisions of the Party Constitution, using the method of secret ballot.
    (5) The number of delegates shall be calculated according to the following methods:
    (1) One delegate shall be elected for every 10,000 Party members;
    (2) In order to take into account the actual situation of the uneven distribution of Party members in various regions of the country, each electoral unit may separately elect an additional four to eight delegates, and Party organizations in large cities with a population of more than two million may separately elect an additional ten to fifteen delegates. The number of additional delegates for each unit shall be stipulated separately.
    (6) Delegates attending the Eighth National Congress of the Party should be elected before the end of June 1956.

### 中国共产党第七届中央委员会第六次全体会议（扩大）关于召开党的第八次全国代表大会的决议

1955-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党第七届中央委员会第六次全体会议（扩大）关于召开党的第八次全国代表大会的决议
    （一九五五年十月十一日通过）
    （一）决定在一九五六年下半年召开中国共产党第八次全国代表大会，召开这次大会的具体时间由中央政治局规定。
    （二）这次大会的主要议程如下：
      （1）党的中央委员会的工作报告；
      （2）关于修改党章的报告；
      （3）关于发展国民经济的第二个五年计划的指示；
      （4）选举党的中央委员会。
    （三）出席第八次全国代表大会的代表，由各省、自治区、直辖市的党组织以及中共中央直属机关和中央国家机关的党组织分别召开党的代表大会选举。西藏地区可以召开党的代表会议选举。
    中国人民解放军的党组织的代表，由中国人民解放军党的代表大会选举。
    （四）代表的选举，一律按照党章的规定，采取无记名投票的方式进行。
    （五）代表名额按照下列办法计算：
    （1）每一万名党员选代表一名；
    （2）为了照顾到全国各地区党员分布不平衡的实际情况，每个选举单位可以分别增选代表四人至八人，人口在二百万以上的大城市的党组织可以分别另行增选代表十人至十五人。各单位应增选的名额另行规定。
    （六）出席党的第八次全国代表大会的代表，应该于一九五六年六月底以前选出。
