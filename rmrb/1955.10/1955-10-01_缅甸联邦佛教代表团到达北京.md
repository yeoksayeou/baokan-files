Title: Burmese Buddhist Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th. The twelve-member Burmese Buddhist Delegation, which came to China to welcome the Buddha's Tooth Relic, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 30th.

    The delegation was led by U Tun Maung, Vice President of the Burmese Buddhist Association and Chief Justice of the Burmese Supreme Court, as head of the delegation, and U Khin Tun, Judge of the Burmese Supreme Court, Secretary-General of the Burmese Buddhist Association, and Vice President of the World Buddhist Fellowship, as deputy head of the delegation.

    More than 1,000 people, including representatives of the government, Buddhist circles in Beijing, and people's organizations, warmly welcomed the delegation at the airport.

    Zhao Puchu, Vice President and Secretary-General of the Chinese Buddhist Association, delivered a welcome speech, saying that this visit by the delegation will further strengthen the long-standing friendship and ties between Buddhists and the people of China and Burma.

    Delegation leader U Tun Maung delivered a return speech (to be issued separately).

    Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included: Zhang Zhiran, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court of the People's Republic of China, Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, and Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing.

    Also going to the airport to welcome the delegation were: Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, representatives of The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and members of the Burmese Overseas Chinese Returning Home Visiting Group.

    U Hla Maung, Burmese Ambassador to China, and U Than Hlaing, Deputy Head of the Burmese Federal Cultural Delegation, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 缅甸联邦佛教代表团到达北京

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸联邦佛教代表团到达北京
    据新华社三十日讯　前来中国迎接佛牙的缅甸联邦佛教代表团十二人三十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团由缅甸佛教协会副会长、缅甸最高法院院长吴登貌团长和缅甸最高法院法官、缅甸佛教协会秘书长、世界佛教总会副主席吴千吞副团长率领。
    代表团在机场受到政府、北京佛教界人士和人民团体的代表一千余人的热烈欢迎。
    中国佛教协会副会长兼秘书长赵朴初致欢迎词说，代表团这次访问将会更加加强中缅两国佛教徒和两国人民之间已有长久历史的友谊和联系。
    代表团团长吴登貌致答词（另发）。
    前往机场欢迎代表团的有：中华人民共和国最高人民法院副院长张志让、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、外交部部长助理陈家康，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，北京市副市长冯基平。
    前往机场欢迎的还有：中缅友好协会会长郑振铎，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会，中国人民对外文化协会的代表及缅甸华侨归国观光团人员。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂，缅甸联邦文化代表团副团长吴丹盛也到机场欢迎。
