Title: Egyptian Foreign Ministry Spokesman Announces: U.S. Air Attaché Ordered to Leave the Country
Author:
Date: 1955-10-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 26th: An Egyptian Foreign Ministry spokesman announced on the 25th that the U.S. Air Attaché to the U.S. Embassy in Egypt, Major Roe Nicholson, has been declared persona non grata by the Egyptian government and ordered to leave Egypt.

The spokesman said that the U.S. Air Attaché made remarks that damaged Egypt's reputation. He said that this attaché "repeatedly demonstrated a hostile attitude towards Egypt."

### 埃及外交部发言人宣布  美驻埃空军武官已被命令离境

1955-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及外交部发言人宣布
    美驻埃空军武官已被命令离境
    新华社二十六日讯　开罗消息：埃及外交部发言人二十五日宣布，美国驻埃及大使馆的空军武官罗·尼科尔逊少校已被埃及政府宣布为不受欢迎的人，并且被命令离开埃及。
    发言人说，这个美国空军武官说了毁损埃及声誉的话。他说，这个武官“对埃及一再表现敌对态度”。
