Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Delegation Leaves Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1955-10-26
Page: 4

    Friendly Exchanges

    All-China Federation of Trade Unions Delegation Leaves Yugoslavia

    The delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has concluded its visit to Yugoslavia. The head of the delegation, Liu Ningyi, left Belgrade by plane on October 20th to return to China. He was seen off at the airport by V. Vujica, member of the Central Council and Director of the International Department of the Confederation of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia, and Wu Xiuquan, the Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, among others. The other members of the delegation also left Belgrade on the 21st.

    On the eve of its departure from Yugoslavia (October 19th), the delegation held a press conference, at which head of the delegation Liu Ningyi gave his impressions of his visit to Yugoslavia and answered questions from reporters.

    Liu Ningyi said: The delegation visited twenty-three cities in all the republics of Yugoslavia except Montenegro, and was warmly received everywhere. The delegation saw everywhere the Yugoslav people's love for peace and socialist construction. The Yugoslav people's desire to understand China and the Chinese people left a deep impression on the delegation.

    Liu Ningyi emphasized that the Chinese people cared about the Yugoslav people's struggle for liberation in the past, and now care about the Yugoslav people's construction. Liu Ningyi finally expressed the hope that cooperation between the trade unions and people of China and Yugoslavia will be further strengthened.
                              (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 我总工会代表团离开南斯拉夫

1955-10-26
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    我总工会代表团离开南斯拉夫
    中华全国总工会代表团已经结束了在南斯拉夫的访问。代表团团长刘宁一在十月二十日乘飞机离贝尔格莱德回国，到机场送行的有南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会委员、国际部部长维沃达和中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权等人。代表团其他成员也在二十一日离开了贝尔格莱德。
    代表团在离南斯拉夫前夕（十九日）举行了记者招待会，刘宁一团长在会上发表了访问南斯拉夫的观感，并且回答了记者们提出的问题。
    刘宁一说：代表团在南斯拉夫访问了除门的内哥罗以外的各共和国的二十三个城市，到处都受到热情的接待。代表团在各地都看见了南斯拉夫人民对和平和社会主义建设事业的热爱。南斯拉夫人民想了解中国和中国人民的愿望给代表团留下了深刻的印象。
    刘宁一强调指出，中国人民过去关怀南斯拉夫人民争取解放的斗争，现在又关怀着南斯拉夫人民的建设。刘宁一最后表示希望中南两国工会和人民间的合作更为加强。
                              （据新华社讯）
