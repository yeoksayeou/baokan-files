Title: The Advance Troops of the Chinese People's Volunteers Return to An Dong; Korean People's Delegation Bids Farewell to the Returning Chinese People's Volunteers

Author:
Date: 1955-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Sinuiju, October 10th: The advance troops of the third batch of six divisions of the Chinese People's Volunteers returning home left the Korean border city of Sinuiju on the morning of October 10th to return to China. Kim Se-gwang, Vice Chairman of the People's Committee of North Pyongan Province, Kim Ri-jun, Chairman of the Workers' Party of Korea Sinuiju City Committee, Tian Ji-hong, Vice Chairman of the Sinuiju City People's Committee, Choi Cho-deuk, Secretary-General of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland Sinuiju Committee, and more than 4,400 representatives from all walks of life gathered at the station all day to warmly see them off.

A farewell ceremony was held at Sinuiju Station. Tian Ji-hong, Vice Chairman of the Sinuiju City People's Committee, and Zhou Xing-kuan, the representative of the advance troops of the returning Chinese People's Volunteers, spoke at the meeting successively.

The train carrying the advance troops crossed the Yalu River Bridge at 10:35 AM (Pyongyang time) and headed for Andong City.

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, October 10th: The advance troops of the third batch of six divisions of the Chinese People's Volunteers returned to Andong City at 9:45 AM and 2:00 PM on the morning of October 10th.

The returning troops were warmly welcomed by representatives from various people's organizations, institutions, schools, and other units in Andong City. As the train slowly entered the station, the welcoming crowd waved flags and shouted slogans such as "Salute to the heroic and triumphant return of the Chinese People's Volunteers" to greet the returning soldiers. When the train arrived at the station, Young Pioneers presented large bouquets of flowers to the commanders and combatants of the Volunteers. Many dance circles spontaneously formed on the platform, and the welcomers joined the returning heroes in a joyous gathering.

In order to welcome the returning Chinese People's Volunteers, the people of Andong City built a tall triumphal arch at the western end of the Yalu River Iron Bridge a few days ago. On the pillars decorated with red cloth on the station platform, various welcome slogans were written. Staff members of many units in Andong City also rehearsed short and wonderful artistic programs in their spare time, preparing to give consolation performances to the returning Chinese People's Volunteers.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 10th: According to the Korean Central News Agency: A Korean People's Delegation left Pyongyang on the 9th in order to convey the warm gratitude and greetings of the entire Korean people to the commanders and combatants of the Chinese People's Volunteers who are about to return home.

The delegation was led by Jo Seong-mo, President of the Supreme Court. Korean artists accompanied the delegation to express their greetings.

Other people's delegations will also depart one after another.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 10th: A people's delegation of ten people from all walks of life in Korea, led by Jo Seong-mo, President of the Supreme Court of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, arrived at a certain station of the returning Chinese People's Volunteers on the 9th to bid farewell and express their greetings.

At 2:00 PM on the 9th, the leading organs of a certain unit of the returning Chinese People's Volunteers held a grand meeting. At the meeting, the representative of a certain unit of the returning Chinese People's Volunteers delivered a speech, expressing deep gratitude for the care and support given by the Korean people to the Chinese People's Volunteers during the war and the post-armistice period. He wished the Korean people new victories in their future construction and in the struggle for the peaceful reunification of Korea.

Jo Seong-mo, head of the Korean People's Delegation, read the congratulatory letter from the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland to the returning Chinese People's Volunteers at the meeting. Representatives of the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland, the Supreme Command of the Korean People's Army, the People's Committee of a certain province in Korea, and a certain unit of the Korean People's Army presented flags and gifts to a certain unit of the returning Chinese People's Volunteers at the meeting. In the evening, the Korean National Comprehensive Art Troupe, which accompanied the delegation to express their greetings, performed wonderful programs.

### 志愿军归国部队的先头部队回到安东市  朝鲜人民代表团欢送志愿军归国部队

1955-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军归国部队的先头部队回到安东市
    朝鲜人民代表团欢送志愿军归国部队
    新华社新义州十日电　中国人民志愿军第三批六个师归国部队的先头部队已在十日上午离开朝鲜国境城市新义州归国。朝鲜平安北道人民委员会副委员长金世广、朝鲜劳动党新义州市委员会委员长金利俊、新义州市人民委员会副委员长田吉弘、朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线新义州委员会书记长崔初得等和各界人民代表四千四百多人整日齐集在车站上热烈欢送。
    在新义州车站上举行了欢送仪式。新义州市人民委员会副委员长田吉弘和中国人民志愿军归国部队先头部队的代表周性宽在会上相继讲话。
    载着这支先头部队的列车在上午十时三十五分（平壤时间）开过鸭绿江大桥前往安东市。
    新华社安东十日电　中国人民志愿军归国部队第三批六个师先头部队在十日上午九时四十五分和下午二时先后回到了安东市。
    这支归国部队受到安东市各界人民团体、机关、学校等单位代表的热烈欢迎。当列车徐徐进入车站时，欢迎的人群挥动彩旗，高喊着“向英雄凯旋归国的中国人民志愿军致敬”等口号，迎接归国的战士们。当列车到站后，少先队员们把大束鲜花献给志愿军的指挥员和战斗员，月台上自动组成了很多个舞圈，欢迎的人们同归国的英雄们在一起联欢。
    安东市人民为欢迎志愿军归国部队，早几天就在鸭绿江大铁桥西端筑起一所高大的凯旋门。车站月台上用红布装饰的顶柱上，写着各种欢迎标语。安东市许多单位的工作人员，还在业余时间排练了短小而精彩的文艺节目，准备向志愿军归国部队作慰问演出。
    新华社平壤十日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：一个朝鲜人民代表团在九日离开平壤，以便向即将归国的中国人民志愿军指挥员和战斗员传达朝鲜全体人民的热烈的感谢和慰问。
    代表团是由最高裁判所所长赵诚模率领的。朝鲜的艺术家们随同前往慰问。
    其他人民代表团也将陆续出发。
    新华社平壤十日电　朝鲜各界人士组成的人民代表团一行十人，由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高裁判所所长赵诚模率领，九日到达中国人民志愿军归国部队某部驻地进行欢送和慰问。
    九日下午二时，志愿军归国部队某部领导机关举行了盛大的集会。会上，志愿军归国部队某部代表致词，对朝鲜人民在战争时期和停战后的时期内给予中国人民志愿军的关怀和支援表示深切的感谢。他预祝朝鲜人民在为自己祖国今后的建设事业和争取朝鲜和平统一的斗争中获得新的胜利。
    朝鲜人民代表团团长赵诚模在会上宣读了朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会给中国人民志愿军归国部队的欢送信。朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会、朝鲜人民军最高司令部、朝鲜某道人民委员会和朝鲜人民军某部的代表在会上向志愿军归国部队某部赠旗、赠礼。晚上，随代表团前来进行慰问的朝鲜国立综合艺术团演出了精彩的节目。
