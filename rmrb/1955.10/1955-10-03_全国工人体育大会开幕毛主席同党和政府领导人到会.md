Title: National Workers' Games Open; Chairman Mao and Party and Government Leaders Attend
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: The first National Workers' Games grandly opened this afternoon at the Xian Nong Tan (Temple of Agriculture) Stadium in Beijing.
	Chairman Mao Zedong, the most beloved leader and teacher of the Chinese working class and the Chinese people, attended the games today. When Chairman Mao appeared on the rostrum, the entire stadium erupted in thunderous applause and cheers. Athletes and the audience chanted enthusiastically, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live the Chinese Communist Party!"
	Also on the rostrum were members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Peng Zhen, Zhang Wentian, Deng Xiaoping; Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Li Jishen, Luo Ronghuan, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong; Vice-Premiers of the State Council, He Long, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun; Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Lai Ruoyu; Vice-Chairmen of the National Defense Council, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, Long Yun; as well as responsible persons from relevant government departments, people's organizations, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
	Foreign guests invited to participate in the games included N.N. Romanov, Chairman of the Committee for Physical Culture and Sports under the Council of Ministers of the USSR, the entire Soviet Trade Union Sports Delegation headed by Vasilyev, and over 1,200 representatives and delegations from various countries' cultural, youth, sports, trade, women's organizations, and peace figures who came to China to participate in the National Day celebrations and visits.
	Today, the Xian Nong Tan Stadium was decorated solemnly and beautifully, with more than a hundred red flags fluttering in the wind around the stands. On the four plaques erected opposite the rostrum were Chairman Mao's inscription – "Develop sports, strengthen the people's physique" and the inscriptions for the games from Secretaries of the CPC Central Committee Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De. The stands around the stadium were filled with more than 18,000 workers, suburban farmers, students, government employees, soldiers, Young Pioneers in colorful costumes, and representatives attending the National Conference of Active Builders of Socialism.
	At 1:20 PM, the general commander of the games announced the opening of the games. To the strains of a majestic march, a solemn entrance ceremony was held. More than 1,700 male and female worker-athletes wearing sports attire of various colors marched past the rostrum in high spirits, receiving the review of the Party and state leaders. These athletes were selected from one million worker-athletes across the country. They represent the Huoxing (Mars) and Huoche Tou (Locomotive) sports associations, as well as industrial units such as heavy industry, first machinery, coal mining, electricity, textiles, light industry, forestry, construction, highway transportation, agriculture and water conservancy, shop employees, post and telecommunications, and seafarers, and the education and People's Bank systems. Among the athletes were the famous labor model Wang Chonglun, Zhang Xiling, a driver from Dalian Dyestuff Factory and the national record holder for the 10,000-meter long-distance run, Bagmalov, a young Russian drilling worker who came to Beijing for the first time from Xinjiang, Zhang Manhua, a track and field athlete from the People's Bank and a mother of two, He Xiaoxia, a female tractor driver from the state-owned Friendship Farm, and many advanced workers and labor models from the Yichun Forestry Area, the Yangtze River navigation fleet, the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway construction site, and the Kangzang (Sichuan-Tibet) Highway.
	Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered the opening speech at the meeting (see page 3 of today's newspaper).
	Following this, He Long spoke amidst warm applause (see page 3 of today's newspaper). On behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council, he extended congratulations to the games and to the heroic Chinese working class.
	Vasilyev, head of the Soviet Trade Union Sports Delegation, also spoke at the meeting, congratulating the Chinese athletes on their further achievements in developing mass sports and striving for higher sports results. He also presented a gift to the congress on behalf of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, at which point music and enthusiastic applause rang out in the venue.
	After the opening ceremony, there were spectacular group exercises and track and field and football matches.

### 全国工人体育大会开幕毛主席同党和政府领导人到会

1955-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工人体育大会开幕毛主席同党和政府领导人到会
    据新华社二日讯　全国第一届工人体育运动大会今天下午在北京先农坛体育场隆重开幕。
    中国工人阶级和中国人民最敬爱的领袖和导师毛泽东主席今天出席了大会。当毛主席出现在主席台上时，全场发出暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼声。运动员们和全场观众兴奋地高呼“毛主席万岁！”“中国共产党万岁！”
    在主席台上的还有中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、彭真、张闻天、邓小平，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、罗荣桓、郭沫若、陈叔通，国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅、李富春，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅、程潜、张治中、龙云，以及政府有关部门、各人民团体的负责人和中国人民解放军高级将领。
    应邀参加大会的外宾有苏联部长会议体育运动委员会主席尼·尼·罗曼诺夫，以华西里耶夫为首的苏联工会体育代表团全体人员，以及来中国参加国庆观礼和访问的各国文化、青年、体育、贸易、妇女等代表团和代表以及和平人士等共一千二百多人。
    今天，先农坛体育场布置得庄严美丽，看台周围一百多面红旗迎风飘扬。主席台对面竖立着的四面牌匾上，写着毛主席的题词——“发展体育运动，增强人民体质”和中共中央书记处书记刘少奇、周恩来、朱德给大会的题词。体育场四周的看台上坐满了一万八千多名首都的工人、郊区农民、学生、机关工作人员、部队战士、穿着彩色服装的少年先锋队队员和出席全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的代表。
    下午一时二十分，大会总指挥宣布大会开幕。在雄壮的进行曲声中，举行了庄严的入场式。一千七百多名穿着各色运动服装的男女职工运动员，精神百倍地列队走过主席台，接受党和国家领导人的检阅。这些运动员是从全国一百万职工运动员中选拔出来的。他们代表着火星、火车头两个体育协会以及重工业、第一机械、煤矿、电业、纺织、轻工业、林业、建筑、公路运输、农业水利、店员、邮电、海员等产业单位和教育、人民银行系统。在运动员的行列中有著名的劳动模范王崇伦，有大连染料厂汽车司机、一万公尺长跑全国最高纪录保持者张希苓，有第一次从新疆来到北京的俄罗斯族青年钻探工人巴格马洛夫，有人民银行田径运动员、两个孩子的妈妈章曼华，有来自国营友谊农场的女拖拉机手何晓霞，还有来自伊春林区、长江航轮、兰新铁路工地、康藏公路的许多先进工作者和劳动模范。
    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚在会上致开幕词（见今天本报第三版）。
    接着，贺龙在热烈的掌声中讲话（见今天本报第三版）。他代表中国共产党中央委员会和国务院向大会、向英勇的中国工人阶级致贺。
    苏联工会体育代表团团长华西里耶夫也在会上讲话，他祝贺中国运动员们在发展群众性体育运动和争取更高的运动成绩方面取得进一步的成就。他并代表全苏工会中央理事会向大会献礼，这时，会场上响起乐曲声和热烈的掌声。
    在大会开幕式结束后，举行了精采的团体操表演和田径、足球比赛。
