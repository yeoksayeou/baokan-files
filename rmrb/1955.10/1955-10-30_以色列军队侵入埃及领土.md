Title: Israeli Troops Invade Egyptian Territory
Author:
Date: 1955-10-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 29th. The Egyptian government spokesperson said on the 28th that Israeli troops, estimated at battalion strength (approximately 400 men), supported by heavy artillery and mortar fire, attacked the Egyptian barracks at Kontilla, 40 kilometers south of El-Aujah on the evening of the 27th, killing four Egyptian soldiers and taking twenty prisoners.
　　The spokesperson also said, "The Egyptian army then recaptured the outpost."
　　The Egyptian government immediately lodged a protest with the Israel-Egypt Mixed Armistice Commission, requesting an emergency meeting to discuss the Israeli violation.

### 以色列军队侵入埃及领土

1955-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    以色列军队侵入埃及领土
    新华社二十九日讯　开罗消息：埃及政府发言人二十八日说，估计有一个营的兵力（约四百人）的以色列军队，在重炮和迫击炮炮火的支持下，在二十七日晚攻击厄尔—阿雅以南四十公里孔蒂拉的埃及兵营，打死了四个埃及士兵，带走了二十个俘虏。
    发言人又说，“埃及军队随即重新占领了这个前哨”。
    埃及政府马上向以色列—埃及混合停战委员会提出抗议，要求召开一个紧急会议来讨论以色列侵犯问题。
