Title: Premier Zhou Receives Delegation from Six Major Japanese Cities

Author:
Date: 1955-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received the head of the delegation from six major Japanese cities, Mr. Yoshihiko Kitajima, and all its members at 11:30 a.m. on the 2nd.

Present at the reception were Peng Zhen, Liao Chengzhi, Wang Kunlun, and Chai Zemin.

### 周总理接见日本六大城市访华代表团

1955-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本六大城市访华代表团
    新华社二日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二日上午十一时半接见日本六大城市访华代表团团长北岛义彦和全体人员。
    接见时在座的有彭真、廖承志、王崑崙、柴泽民。
