    Our Great Motherland
    After being put into operation, the renovated second magnetic separation plant of Anshan Iron and Steel Company has been operating well for more than ten days. This magnetic separation plant is equipped with various new equipment and has a very high production efficiency. This is the settling tank of the second magnetic separation plant.
