Date: 1955-10-14
Page: 6

On the evening of the 10th, the Bulgarian Artists Delegation held an opening performance at the Tianqiao Theater. Their outstanding performances were warmly welcomed and praised by artists and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Left picture: After the performance of the Bulgarian Artists Delegation, Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Ulanhu, and others went on stage to congratulate the Bulgarian artists and took a group photo.
(Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan)

The photographs in this edition, unless otherwise credited, are by our reporters Gao Liang and Wang Zhiyuan.

Male bass solo: Ivan Susanin's aria from the opera "Ivan Susanin"
Composer: Glinka
Performer: Mikhail Popov, People's Artist of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Dimitrov Prize winner, soloist of the Sofia People's Opera House

Violin solo: Gopak Dance
Composer: Mussorgsky
Performer: Boyan Lechev, Dimitrov Prize winner, winner of the Budapest World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, lecturer at the Sofia Academy of Music

Soprano solo: Lullaby
Composer: Tchaikovsky
Performer: Vasha Radeva, winner of the Second Bulgarian Singers and Instrumentalists Competition, winner of the Warsaw and Bucharest World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, winner of the Prague "Ema Destinnová" Prize, soloist of the Sofia People's Opera House

Male solo: Hey, Golden Dobruja, Bulgarian modern folk song, performer: folk singer Yovcho Karaivanov, soloist of Sofia Radio. Accompaniment "Kaval": Tsvetko Blagoev, soloist of Sofia Radio

Soprano solo: The Maiden's Song
Composer: Hristov
Performer: Yulia Wiener, winner of the Second Bulgarian Singers and Instrumentalists Competition, winner of the Bucharest World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, soloist of the Sofia People's Opera House

Folk instrument "Gaida" solo: Banquet Tune
Performer: Tsvetko Blagoev, soloist of Sofia Radio

Ballet: Meeting in Spring
Composer: Vladigerov
Performers: Luba Kolchakova, Dimitrov Prize winner, winner of the Bucharest World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, dancer of the Sofia People's Opera House, and Asen Gavrilov, winner of the Bucharest World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, dancer of the Sofia People's Opera House
