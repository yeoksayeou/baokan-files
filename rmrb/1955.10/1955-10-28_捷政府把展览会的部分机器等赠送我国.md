Title: The Czechoslovak Government Donates Some Machines, etc. from the Exhibition to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1955-10-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 27th. The government of the Czechoslovak Republic has decided to donate some of the machines and transportation vehicles currently in the "Exhibition of the Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia" being held in Shanghai to the government of the People's Republic of China. These include an “AERO-45” airplane, a glider, a С-6 cigarette-making machine, a Ｄ-7 stone crusher, a hexagonal lathe, an electric welding machine, a broadcasting vehicle, and a tractor. The head of the Czechoslovak government delegation, Jindřich Uher, sent a letter to Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yun before returning to his country. Chen Yun, on behalf of the Chinese government, has replied with a letter of thanks for the gift from the government of the Czechoslovak Republic.

### 捷政府把展览会的部分机器等赠送我国

1955-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    捷政府把展览会的部分机器等赠送我国
    新华社二十七日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府决定把目前在上海举行的捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会中的一部分机器和交通工具，包括“АЭРО—45”飞机、滑翔机、С—6制烟机、Ｄ—7碎石机、六角车床、电焊机、广播汽车和拖拉机等赠送给中华人民共和国政府。捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团团长英德里赫·乌赫在返国前曾致函国务院副总理陈云。陈云代表我国政府对捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府的赠礼已经复函致谢。
