Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives British Friends Service Council Delegation; Yang Xiufeng and Zhang Xiruo Receive Japanese Teachers and Staff Union Education Delegation

Author:
Date: 1955-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China received the British Friends Service Council delegation, headed by Duncan Wood, at 1:30 PM on the 26th. During the meeting, they exchanged opinions on the international situation and the promotion of international peace.

    Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Liu Guan Yi, Secretary-General, Qiao Guanhua, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th: Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education, and Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education of the People's Republic of China, received the Japanese Teachers and Staff Union Education Delegation, headed by Takeshi Kobayashi, on the morning of the 26th. Present at the reception were Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of Higher Education, Liu Shi, Vice Minister of Education, and Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Educational Workers Union of China.

### 周恩来总理接见英国公谊会代表团  杨秀峰张奚若接见日本教职员工会教育考察团

1955-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见英国公谊会代表团
    杨秀峰张奚若接见日本教职员工会教育考察团
    新华社二十六日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二十六日下午一时半接见了以邓肯·伍德为首的英国公谊会代表团全体代表。接见时曾就国际局势和促进国际和平问题交换了意见。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、秘书长刘贯一，中国人民外交学会副会长乔冠华，中国红十字会总会会长李德全等。
    新华社二十六日讯　中华人民共和国高等教育部长杨秀峰和教育部长张奚若，在二十六日上午接见了以小林武为首的日本教职员工会教育考察团全体人员。接见时在座的，有高等教育部副部长曾昭抡、教育部副部长柳湜，中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明。
