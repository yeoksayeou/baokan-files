Title: Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet Executive Committee, Yasnov, Visits London
Author:
Date: 1955-10-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Yasnov, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet of Working People's Deputies, and others arrived in London on the 20th at the invitation of London Mayor Howard and London County Council Chairman Prichard. On the 20th, Yasnov and his companions attended a meeting of the London Municipal Council. London Mayor Howard said in his welcoming speech at the meeting that he himself had visited Moscow in July of this year. That visit had helped to strengthen friendly ties between Britain and the Soviet Union. He expressed his belief that Yasnov's return visit would further consolidate the friendship between the Soviet Union and Britain.

### 莫斯科市苏维埃执委会主席  雅斯诺夫访问伦敦

1955-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科市苏维埃执委会主席
    雅斯诺夫访问伦敦
    据新华社讯　莫斯科市劳动人民代表苏维埃执行委员会主席雅斯诺夫等应伦敦市长霍华德和伦敦郡政委员会主席普里彻德的邀请，在二十日到达伦敦。雅斯诺夫和他的同伴在二十日出席了伦敦市政委员会会议。伦敦市长霍华德在会上致欢迎词时说，他自己在今年七月间曾经访问过莫斯科。那次访问已经有助于加强英、苏两国的友好联系。他表示相信雅斯诺夫的这次回访会更加巩固苏、英两国的友谊。
