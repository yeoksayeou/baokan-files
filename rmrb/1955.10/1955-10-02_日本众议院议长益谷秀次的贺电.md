Title: Telegram of Greetings from Shojiro Yasui, Speaker of the Japanese House of Representatives

Author:
Date: 1955-10-02
Page: 3

Telegram of Greetings from Shojiro Yasui, Speaker of the Japanese House of Representatives

To Mr. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Beijing:
    Congratulations on the sixth anniversary of your country's National Day on October 1st.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shojiro Yasui, Speaker of the House of Representatives
                                September 29, 1955, Tokyo
