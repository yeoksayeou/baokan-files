Title: Dutch Communist Party General Secretary de Groot Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1955-10-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Paul de Groot, General Secretary of the Dutch Communist Party, who came to China for a visit on September 5th, left Beijing by car on the 4th to return to his country with his wife and son. Those who went to the station to see him off included Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, member of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Deputy Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhao Yimin, Li Chuli, and Li Qixin, responsible working personnel of the organs of the CPC Central Committee.

### 荷兰共产党总书记格鲁特离北京回国

1955-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    荷兰共产党总书记格鲁特离北京回国
    新华社四日讯　九月五日前来中国访问的荷兰共产党总书记保罗·格鲁特同他的夫人和儿子，四日乘车离京返国。前往车站送行的有中共中央政治局委员、中共中央秘书长邓小平，中共中央委员王稼祥，中共中央副秘书长杨尚昆，中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏、李初梨、李启新等。
