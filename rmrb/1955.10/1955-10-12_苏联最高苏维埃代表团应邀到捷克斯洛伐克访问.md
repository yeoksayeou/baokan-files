Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Visits Czechoslovakia at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation, invited to visit Czechoslovakia, arrived in Prague by plane on the 10th, led by Tarasov, Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Supreme Soviet and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federal Republic.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团应邀到捷克斯洛伐克访问

1955-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团应邀到捷克斯洛伐克访问
    据新华社讯　应邀到捷克斯洛伐克访问的苏联最高苏维埃代表团，在苏联最高苏维埃副主席、俄罗斯联邦共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫率领下，于十日乘飞机到达布拉格。
