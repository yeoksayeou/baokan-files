Title: Sino-Ceylonese Government Representatives' Trade Talks Communiqué
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-16
Page: 1

    According to the provisions of the "Five-Year Trade Agreement between the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of Ceylon on Rubber and Rice" concluded by the two governments in December 1952, the representatives of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Trade Delegation of the Government of Ceylon, after consultations in Beijing, revised the price of rubber from June 1 to December 31, 1955, and signed a contract for the Government of the People's Republic of China to purchase 50,000 metric tons of rubber from the Government of Ceylon and a contract for the Government of Ceylon to purchase 270,000 metric tons of rice from the Government of the People's Republic of China in 1956. This is the fourth time that the Chinese and Ceylonese governments have signed contracts for buying and selling rubber and rice. Both governments expressed their satisfaction with the implementation of the contracts in the past three years.
    The negotiations were conducted in an extremely friendly and sincere atmosphere, and the agreements reached in the negotiations demonstrated the further consolidation and development of trade and friendly relations between China and Ceylon.
    Xinhua News Agency, 15th - After friendly and sincere negotiations, the representatives of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Trade Delegation of the Government of Ceylon reached an exchange of notes in Beijing on October 14 regarding the revision of the price of rubber from June 1 to December 31, 1955, and separately signed a contract for the Government of the People's Republic of China to purchase 50,000 metric tons of rubber from the Government of Ceylon in 1956 and a contract for the Government of Ceylon to purchase 270,000 metric tons of rice from the Government of the People's Republic of China in 1956.
    Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the exchange of notes on the revision of the rubber price in 1955 on behalf of the Chinese side, and Koliya, head of the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation, signed on behalf of the Ceylon side.
    Liu Guanghan, Deputy Manager of the China National Cereals, Oils and Foodstuffs Export Corporation, signed the 1956 Sino-Ceylonese rice contract on behalf of the Chinese side, and Kaspezu, Director of the Food Supply Bureau of the Ceylon Ministry of Agriculture and Food, signed on behalf of the Ceylon side.
    Ni Weiting, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, signed the 1956 Sino-Ceylonese rubber contract on behalf of the Chinese side, and Gunewardene, Commissioner of Rubber of the Government of Ceylon, signed on behalf of the Ceylon side.
    Li Zhezhi, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and responsible persons from relevant bureaus and corporations of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, etc., attended the signing ceremony on the Chinese side. The entire members of the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation attended on the Ceylon side.
    That evening, Li Zhezhi held a banquet to entertain all members of the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation.

### 中华人民共和国政府代表和锡兰政府贸易代表团贸易谈判公报

1955-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国政府代表和锡兰政府贸易代表团贸易谈判公报
    中华人民共和国政府和锡兰政府双方的代表，根据一九五二年十二月两国政府所缔结的“中华人民共和国中央人民政府与锡兰政府关于橡胶和大米的五年贸易协定”的规定，在北京进行商谈后，修改了一九五五年六月一日至十二月三十一日的橡胶价格并签订了一九五六年中华人民共和国政府向锡兰政府购买橡胶五万公吨的合同和锡兰政府向中华人民共和国政府购买大米二十七万公吨的合同。此次是中锡两国政府第四次签订买、卖橡胶、大米合同，两国政府对三年来双方合同的执行情况均表示十分满意。
    谈判是在极友好和诚恳的空气中进行的，谈判中所达成的各项协议显示了中锡两国间贸易和友好关系进一步的巩固和发展。
    新华社十五日讯　中华人民共和国政府代表和锡兰政府贸易代表团，经过友好诚恳的谈判，十月十四日在北京达成了关于修改一九五五年六月一日至十二月三十一日的橡胶价格的换文，分别签订了一九五六年中华人民共和国政府向锡兰政府购买橡胶五万公吨的合同和锡兰政府向中华人民共和国政府购买大米二十七万公吨的合同。
    代表中国方面在修改一九五五年橡胶价格换文上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长雷任民，代表锡兰方面在这个换文上签字的是锡兰政府贸易代表团团长科里亚。
    代表中国方面在一九五六年中锡大米合同上签字的是中国粮谷油脂出口公司副经理刘光汉，代表锡兰方面在这个合同上签字的是锡兰农业粮食部粮食供应局局长卡斯佩兹。
    代表中国方面在一九五六年中锡橡胶合同上签字的是中国进出口公司副经理倪蔚庭，代表锡兰方面在这个合同上签字的是锡兰政府橡胶专员古涅瓦德尼。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有中华人民共和国对外贸易部代理部长李哲人，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部各有关局、公司的负责人等。锡兰方面有锡兰政府贸易代表团全体团员。
    当日晚，李哲人举行宴会招待锡兰政府贸易代表团全体团员。
