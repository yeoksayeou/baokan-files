Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet to Welcome French Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd—Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 22nd to welcome the French parliamentary delegation headed by Daniel Mayer.
　　At the banquet, President Zhang Xiruo first delivered a speech, extending his welcome to the guests. He said: The Chinese people have always advocated peaceful coexistence and friendly cooperation among the people of all countries. The Chinese people have always held respect and friendship for the French people who love freedom, equality, and fraternity. He believed that this visit to China by the French parliamentary delegation would help enhance mutual understanding and friendly cooperation between the people of China and France. Finally, Zhang Xiruo proposed: A toast to enhancing the friendship between the people of China and France!
　　Head of the delegation Daniel Mayer first expressed his gratitude for the invitation and hospitality of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. He believed that through this visit to China by the delegation, the friendship between the people of China and France would be increased, and this friendship would not only have an impact on the economy and culture, but also on politics.
　　Among those present at the banquet were: Zhou Gengsheng, Qian Duansheng, Qiao Guanhua, Luo Longji, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Zhang Su, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; and Cao Mengjun, Lin Qiaozhi and others, deputies to the National People's Congress.

### 张奚若设宴欢迎法国议员代表团

1955-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    张奚若设宴欢迎法国议员代表团
    新华社二十二日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若在二十二日晚举行宴会欢迎以丹尼埃·麦耶为首的法国议员代表团。
    张奚若会长在宴会上首先致词，向客人们表示欢迎。他说：中国人民一向主张各国人民之间的和平相处和友好合作，中国人民对于爱好自由、平等、博爱的法兰西人民，从来就怀着敬意和友谊。相信法国议员代表团这次来访问中国将有助于中法两国人民的相互了解和友好合作。最后，张奚若建议：为增进中法两国人民的友谊而干杯！
    丹尼埃·麦耶团长首先对中国人民外交学会的邀请和款待表示感谢。他相信通过这次代表团亲自来中国的访问，会增加中法两国人民的友谊，这种友谊不仅会在经济和文化上发生作用，也将会在政治上发生作用。
    出席今晚宴会作陪的有：中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、钱端升、乔冠华、罗隆基，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员张苏，全国人民代表大会代表曹孟君、林巧稚等人。
