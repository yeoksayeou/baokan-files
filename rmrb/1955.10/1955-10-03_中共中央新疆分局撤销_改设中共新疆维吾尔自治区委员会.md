Title: CCP Central Xinjiang Sub-bureau Abolished, Replaced by CCP Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-10-03
Page: 2

    CCP Central Xinjiang Sub-bureau Abolished
    Replaced by CCP Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Committee
    The CCP Central Committee has decided to abolish the CCP Central Xinjiang Sub-bureau and replace it with the Chinese Communist Party Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Committee.
    In accordance with this decision of the CCP Central Committee, the CCP Central Xinjiang Sub-bureau officially announced its abolition on October 1st and was replaced by the Chinese Communist Party Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Committee, which is composed of Comrade Wang Enmao and others.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中共中央新疆分局撤销  改设中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会

1955-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    中共中央新疆分局撤销
    改设中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会
    中共中央决定撤销中共中央新疆分局，改设中国共产党新疆维吾尔自治区委员会。
    中共中央新疆分局遵照中共中央这一决定，在十月一日正式宣布撤销，改设中国共产党新疆维吾尔自治区委员会。由王恩茂同志等组成委员会。
                                  （新华社）
