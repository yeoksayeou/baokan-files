Title: On the Eve of the 12th Anniversary of the Founding of the Polish Army, the General Political Department and the Ministry of Culture of the PLA hosted the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble.
Author:
Date: 1955-10-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. On the eve of the 12th anniversary of the founding of the Polish Army, the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China held a banquet on the 11th to entertain the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble, headed by Lieutenant Colonel Wolwa and Colonel Ratkowski.

Those present at the banquet included General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Director of the Propaganda Department, Major General Chen Yi, Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Xia Yan, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison.

At the banquet, Xiao Hua, Xia Yan and others delivered speeches, congratulating all comrades of the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble on the anniversary of the founding of the Polish Army, and wishing the unbreakable friendship between the people and armies of China and Poland would continue to develop and consolidate.

### 在波兰军队建军十二周年纪念日前夕  解放军总政治部、文化部招待波军歌舞团

1955-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

    在波兰军队建军十二周年纪念日前夕
    解放军总政治部、文化部招待波军歌舞团
    新华社十一日讯　中国人民解放军总政治部和中华人民共和国文化部在十一日——波兰军队建军节十二周年纪念日前夕举行宴会，招待以沃尔瓦中校、拉特科夫斯基上校为首的波兰军队歌舞团。
    出席宴会的，有中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华上将、宣传部部长刘志坚中将、文化部部长陈沂少将，中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经。
    在宴会上，萧华、夏衍等先后致词，向波兰军队歌舞团全体同志祝贺波兰军队建军节，并祝中波两国人民及其军队之间的牢不可破的友谊日益发展和巩固。
