Title: Vietnam-China Friendship Association Holds First Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 7th: The first congress of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, which opened on October 5th, closed on the morning of October 7th.

More than 150 people attended the congress, including the initiators and leaders of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, representatives of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front and various people's organizations, and representatives from various inter-regions, provinces, and cities.

Acting President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Si San, delivered the opening speech, talking about the long-standing friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China and the mutual assistance and support between the two peoples in the revolutionary cause of the two countries. He also briefly introduced the work of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association since its establishment on February 11, 1950.

Si San pointed out that in order to further promote the friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China, the Vietnam-China Friendship Association held this congress to review the work of the past five years, formulate future work plans, revise the charter, adjust the organization, and elect a new executive committee.

Luo Guibo, the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, and Ding Xilin, the head of the Chinese Art Troupe who was visiting Vietnam, were invited to attend the congress and delivered speeches, congratulating the convening of this congress.

At the meeting on the 5th, Sun Guangfa, Secretary-General of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, gave a report to the congress on the five years of work of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association. Chun Shui, a member of the Executive Committee, read out the draft of the new charter of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association to the congress. The representatives attending the congress conducted group discussions on this draft for two days on the 5th and 6th. On the 7th, they unanimously adopted the new charter of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association and elected a new executive committee. The congress elected Bei Ji as the President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Si San, Chun Shui, and Chen Huiliao as Vice-Presidents, and Sun Guangfa as Secretary-General.

The congress also adopted congratulatory telegrams to President Ho Chi Minh and Chairman Mao Zedong.

### 越中友好协会举行第一次代表大会

1955-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    越中友好协会举行第一次代表大会
    新华社河内七日电　十月五日开幕的越中友好协会第一次代表大会，已经在七日上午闭幕。
    出席大会的有越中友好协会的发起人和领导机构人员，越南祖国战线中央委员会和各人民团体的代表，以及来自各联区、各省市的代表等共一百五十多人。
    越中友好协会代理会长诗山致开幕词时谈到越中两国人民的悠久友谊和两国人民在两国革命事业中的互相帮助和支持，并且概括地介绍了越中友好协会从一九五零年二月十一日成立以来的工作。
    诗山指出，为了进一步促进越中两国人民的友谊，越中友好协会举行这次大会来检查过去五年的工作，制定今后的工作计划，修改会章，调整组织并且选出新的执行委员会。
    应邀参加大会的中国驻越南大使罗贵波和正在越南访问的中国艺术团团长丁西林曾在会上致词，对这次代表大会的召开表示祝贺。
    在五日会议上，越中友好协会总书记孙光阀向大会作了越中友好协会五年工作的报告，执行委员会委员春水向大会宣读了越中友好协会新会章的草案。出席大会的代表在五日和六日对这个草案进行了两天分组讨论后，在七日一致通过了越中友好协会的新会章，并且选出了新的执行委员会。大会推举裴纪为越中友好协会会长，诗山、春水、陈辉燎为副会长，孙光阀为秘书长。
    大会还通过了致胡志明主席和毛泽东主席的致敬电。
