Title: Ten Worker-Athletes Awarded Medals for Breaking National Records
Author:
Date: 1955-10-10
Page: 3

Ten worker-athletes were awarded medals for breaking national records at the First National Workers' Games. Athletes Fu Sheng-hai and others broke eight national records in track and field, cycling, weightlifting, and other events, including the men's 5,000 meters, 800 meters, shot put, women's javelin, women's 1,500 meters and 3,000 meters cycling, and weightlifting. The Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China specially awarded Fu Sheng-hai and the other nine athletes medals for breaking national records.

The athletes who broke national records: 1. Men's 5,000-meter race: Fu Sheng-hai, representing the Locomotive team, broke the national record of 15 minutes 21 seconds with a time of 15 minutes 15.6 seconds; 2. Men's 800-meter race: Li Zhong-lin, representing the Locomotive team, broke the national record of 1 minute 57.7 seconds with a time of 1 minute 56 seconds in the preliminary round; 3. Men's shot put: Li Bing-cheng, representing the Education team, broke the "19-year-old" national record of 13 meters 26 centimeters with a throw of 13 meters 34 centimeters; 4. Women's javelin: Wang Yi, representing the Education team, broke the national record of 34 meters 12 centimeters with a throw of 34 meters 44 centimeters; 5. Women's 1,500-meter cycling: Wu Shu-hua, representing the Light Industry team, broke the national record of 3 minutes 1.2 seconds with a time of 2 minutes 59.5 seconds; 6. Women's 3,000-meter cycling: Li Feng-qin of the First Machine team, Li Gui-zhi of the Heavy Industry team, and Wu Shu-hua of the Light Industry team broke the national record of 6 minutes 18 seconds with times of 6 minutes 17.4 seconds, 6 minutes 17.5 seconds, and 6 minutes 17.7 seconds respectively; 7. Middleweight weightlifting: Li Zheng-qing, representing the Light Industry team, broke the single-event national record of 122.5 kilograms with a clean and jerk of 125 kilograms; 8. Light heavyweight weightlifting: Cai Chang-hua, representing the Light Industry team, broke the national record of 300 kilograms with a total of 310 kilograms; 9. Light heavyweight weightlifting: Xu Guo-qing, representing the Light Industry team, broke the national record of 112.5 kilograms with a clean and jerk of 115 kilograms.

### 十名工人运动员获打破全国最高纪录奖章

1955-10-10
第3版()
专栏：

    十名工人运动员获打破全国最高纪录奖章
    全国第一届工人体育运动大会运动员傅生海等十人在田径、自行车、举重等项竞赛中，男子五千公尺、八百公尺，推铅球，女子标枪，女子自行车一千五百公尺、三千公尺及举重等八项成绩，打破各该项全国最高纪录。中华人民共和国体育运动委员会特发给傅生海等十人打破全国最高纪录奖章。
    打破全国纪录的运动员：一、男子五千公尺径赛，火车头代表队运动员傅生海以十五分十五秒六的成绩，打破十五分二十一秒的全国纪录；二、男子八百公尺径赛，火车头代表队运动员李中林，在预赛中以一分五十六秒的成绩，打破一分五十七秒七的全国纪录；三、男子铅球，教育代表队运动员李秉诚以十三公尺三十四公分的成绩，打破“保持十九年之久的”十三公尺二十六公分的全国纪录；四、女子标枪，教育代表队运动员王毅以三十四公尺四十四公分的成绩，打破三十四公尺十二公分的全国纪录；五、女子自行车一千五百公尺，轻工业代表队运动员吴淑华以二分五十九秒五的成绩，打破三分一秒二的全国纪录；六、女子自行车三千公尺，第一机械代表队运动员李凤琴、重工业代表队运动员李桂芝、轻工业代表队运动员吴淑华三人分别以六分十七秒四、六分十七秒五、六分十七秒七的成绩，打破六分十八秒的全国纪录；七、中量级举重，轻工业代表队运动员李正清以一百二十五公斤的成绩，打破两手挺举一百二十二点五公斤的单项全国纪录；八、次重量级举重，轻工业代表队运动员蔡昌华以三百一十公斤的总成绩，打破三百公斤的全国纪录；九、次重量级举重，轻工业代表队运动员徐国庆以一百一十五公斤的成绩，打破两手挺举一百一十二点五公斤的全国纪录。
