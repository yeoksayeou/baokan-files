Title: Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Receives Fusanosuke Kuhara; Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Receives Delegation of All-German Women

Author:
Date: 1955-10-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 14th. Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, received Fusanosuke Kuhara, head of the Japanese Delegation to China of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations, and his son, Tozaburo Kuhara, and daughter, Fumiko Kuhara, on the morning of the 14th.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 14th. Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, received the All-German Women's Delegation, headed by Käthe Kern, on the morning of the 14th.
    Present at the reception were Xu Guangping, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

### 宋庆龄副委员长接见久原房之助  陈毅副总理接见全德妇女代表团

1955-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副委员长接见久原房之助
    陈毅副总理接见全德妇女代表团
    新华社十四日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄，在十四日上午接见了日本恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议访华团团长久原房之助和他的儿子久原统三郎、女儿久原文子。
    新华社十四日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅，在十四日上午接见了以凯特·凯恩为首的全德妇女代表团。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平、书记处书记曹孟君，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等。
