Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Finland
Author:
Date: 1955-10-02
Page: 3

Telegram of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Finland
To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Finland extends its congratulations to your Party and all the Chinese people on the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. At the same time, we, on behalf of our Party and all Finnish friends of the Chinese people, extend our congratulations to you on the achievements made by the People's Republic of China on all the great construction fronts. The new and bold plans for building socialism recently adopted by the Second Session of the National People's Congress have amazed and excited the working people of our country. We wish you further success in building the foundations of socialism and improving the welfare of the people.

    Long live the People's Republic of China, the great warrior for peace in Asia and the world!

    Long live the Communist Party of China, the firm and long-tested leader who leads the Chinese people to a bright future!

  The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Finland

### 芬兰共产党中央委员会的贺电

1955-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    芬兰共产党中央委员会的贺电中国共产党中央委员会：
    芬兰共产党中央委员会向你们的党和全体中国人民祝贺中华人民共和国成立六周年。同时，我们并代表我们的党和芬兰全体中国人民的朋友，为中华人民共和国在各个伟大的建设战线上所取得的成就，向你们表示祝贺。全国人民代表大会第二次会议不久前通过的关于建设社会主义的新的大胆的计划，使我国劳动人民赞叹和兴奋。祝你们在建设社会主义基础和提高人民福利方面取得进一步的成就。
    争取亚洲和全世界和平的伟大战士——中华人民共和国万岁！
    领导中国人民为光明的前途而奋斗的坚定和久经考验的领袖——中国共产党万岁！
  芬兰共产党中央委员会
