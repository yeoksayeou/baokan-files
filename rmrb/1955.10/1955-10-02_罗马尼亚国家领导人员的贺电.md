Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Romanian National Leaders
Author:
Date: 1955-10-02
Page: 3

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China,
Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China,
Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, dear comrades, please allow us to extend our warm congratulations and wishes to you, and through you, to all the Chinese people, on behalf of the Romanian people and the Romanian government.

    We sincerely wish the fraternal Chinese people, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, new and great achievements in the cause of building socialism for the prosperity and happiness of the People's Republic of China and for the consolidation of peace in Asia and the world.

    Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic
        Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic
                  Simion Bughici, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Romanian People's Republic

### 罗马尼亚国家领导人员的贺电

1955-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚国家领导人员的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇同志中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来同志：
    值此中华人民共和国宣告成立六周年之际，亲爱的同志们，请允许我们代表罗马尼亚人民和罗马尼亚政府向你们并且通过你们向全中国人民表示热烈的祝贺和愿望。
    我们衷心地预祝兄弟般的中国人民在中国共产党领导下为中华人民共和国的繁荣和幸福、为巩固亚洲和世界和平而进行的建设社会主义的事业获得新的、巨大的成就。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席　彼得鲁·格罗查博士
        罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席格奥尔基·乔治乌—德治
                  罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长　西米昂·布吉奇
