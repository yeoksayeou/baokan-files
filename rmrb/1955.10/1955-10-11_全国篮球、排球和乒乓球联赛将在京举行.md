Title: National Basketball, Volleyball, and Table Tennis Leagues to be Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-11
Page: 3

The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China has decided to hold the "1955 National Basketball League" and the "1955 National Volleyball League" in Beijing from late October to early December.

The "1955 National Basketball League" is scheduled to begin in late October and end in early November. Participating in the league will be men's and women's basketball teams from fifteen units, including the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the China Railway Sports Association, five physical education institutes (Central, Northeast, East China, Central South, and Southwest), and the provinces and cities of Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Anhui, Jiangxi, Guizhou, Yunnan, Tianjin, and Chongqing.

The "1955 National Volleyball League" is scheduled to begin in late November and end in early December. Participating in the league will be men's and women's volleyball teams from twelve units, including the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the China Railway Sports Association, the Central, Northeast, East China, Central South, and Southwest physical education institutes, and the provinces and cities of Jiangxi, Guangdong, Guizhou, Yunnan, and Chongqing. (Xinhua News Agency)

The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China has decided to hold the "1955 National Table Tennis League for Large and Medium Cities" from late November to mid-December.

This league will be held in two stages. The first stage will begin in late November in three competition zones: Tianjin, Shanghai, and Wuhan. Participating in these three competition zones will be outstanding male and female table tennis players from twenty-seven units, including Tianjin, Beijing, Harbin, Shanghai, Hohhot, Urumqi, Nanning, Chongqing, and Xi'an.

Athletes who win the first stage of the competition will go to Beijing in early December to participate in the second stage of the competition. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国篮球、排球和乒乓球联赛将在京举行

1955-10-11
第3版()
专栏：

    全国篮球、排球和乒乓球联赛将在京举行
    中华人民共和国体育运动委员会决定从十月下旬到十二月上旬在北京举行“一九五五年全国篮球联赛”和“一九五五年全国排球联赛”。
    “一九五五年全国篮球联赛”预定从十月下旬开始举行，到十一月上旬结束。参加联赛的将有中国人民解放军，中国火车头体育协会，中央、东北、华东、中南和西南等五个体育学院，江苏、浙江、安徽、江西、贵州、云南、天津、重庆等省、市共十五个单位的男、女篮球队。
    “一九五五年全国排球联赛”预定从十一月下旬开始举行，到十二月上旬结束。参加联赛的将有中国人民解放军，中国火车头体育协会，中央、东北、华东、中南、西南等体育学院和江西、广东、贵州、云南、重庆等省、市共十二个单位的男、女排球队。　（新华社）
    中华人民共和国体育运动委员会决定从十一月下旬到十二月中旬举行“一九五五年全国大、中城市乒乓球联赛”。
    这次联赛将分两个阶段进行。第一阶段从十一月下旬起在天津、上海、武汉三个竞赛区举行。参加这三个竞赛区比赛的分别有天津、北京、哈尔滨、上海、呼和浩特、乌鲁木齐、南宁、重庆、西安等二十七个单位的优秀男、女乒乓球运动员。
    在第一阶段的比赛中获得优胜的运动员，十二月上旬将到北京参加第二阶段的比赛。（新华社）
