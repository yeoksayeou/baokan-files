Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Head U Win Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1955-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 12th. U Win, head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, along with his wife, daughter, and secretary, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 11th and left Guangzhou for home via Hong Kong on the morning of the 12th. The Burmese Cultural Delegation, led by Deputy Head U Than Shwe, continues its performance tour in various parts of China.

More than 200 people, including Chen Ru Tang, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, Wei Jinfei, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, and Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, went to the Guangzhou Railway Station to see U Win and his party off.

Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, also went to the station to see them off.

U Win and others attended a banquet hosted by Wei Jinfei, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, on the evening of the 11th, watched the Cantonese Opera "Qu Yuan," and visited the Lingnan Cultural Relics Palace.

U Win and others visited Shanghai on their way back to their country.

### 缅甸文化代表团团长吴温离广州回国

1955-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化代表团团长吴温离广州回国
    新华社广州十二日电　缅甸联邦文化代表团团长吴温同他的夫人、女儿和秘书，在十一日下午到达广州，十二日早晨离广州经香港回国。缅甸联邦文化代表团在副团长吴丹盛率领下继续在中国各地进行访问演出。
    到广州车站欢送吴温一行的，有广东省副省长陈汝棠、广州市副市长魏今非、广东省人民委员会外事处处长曹若茗等二百多人。
    中华人民共和国文化部副部长郑振铎也到车站欢送。
    吴温等在十一日晚曾应邀出席了广州市副市长魏今非的宴会，观看了粤剧“屈原”，并参观了岭南文物宫。
    吴温等在返国途中，曾到上海参观。
