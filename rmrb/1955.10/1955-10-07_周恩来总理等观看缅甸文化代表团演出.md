Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Others Watch Performance by Burmese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 6th—This evening, a grand soiree was held in Beijing, featuring a performance of music and dance programs with national characteristics by the Burmese Federal Cultural Delegation.
    Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo and Chen Shutong, Vice-Premiers of the State Council He Long, Chen Yi, and Li Fuchun, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice-Minister Liu Zhiming, Vice-Minister of Culture and President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Chen Yi attended and watched the wonderful performances by the Burmese artists.
    The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maw, the head of the Burmese Federal Cultural Delegation, U Win, and the deputy head, U Than Shwe, attended the soiree and accompanied the audience.
    Also attending the soiree today were members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, heads of various government departments, members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, heads of various people's organizations, staff members of central and Beijing municipal organs, members of the capital's literary and art circles, and local work leaders who are currently meeting in Beijing.
    The Burmese artists' performance began with a "Prelude" consisting of a Burmese national instrumental ensemble and ended with the musical short play "Two Brothers." The fourteen wonderful music and dance programs performed at the soiree were warmly welcomed and praised by the audience.
    At the end of the performance, literary and art workers from the capital presented flowers to the Burmese artists. At this moment, the entire audience stood up, and a storm of applause lasted for a long time. Premier Zhou Enlai and others stepped onto the stage and shook hands cordially with the Burmese artists, expressing their congratulations and appreciation. Upon parting, Premier Zhou Enlai said to them in Burmese: "Goodbye."

### 周恩来总理等观看缅甸文化代表团演出

1955-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理等观看缅甸文化代表团演出
    新华社六日讯六日晚间，在北京举行了一次盛大的晚会，由缅甸联邦文化代表团演出了富有民族特点的音乐舞蹈节目。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、陈叔通，国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅、李富春，秘书长习仲勋，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，文化部部长沈雁冰，副部长刘芝明，文化部副部长、中缅友好协会会长郑振铎，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民解放军总政治部文化部部长陈沂出席观看了缅甸艺术家们的精采表演。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂、缅甸联邦文化代表团团长吴温、副团长吴丹盛出席晚会陪同观看表演。
    出席今天晚会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，政府各部门负责人，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员，各人民团体负责人，中央和北京市各机关工作人员，首都文艺界人士和正在北京开会的各地地方工作负责人。
    缅甸艺术家的表演以缅甸民族乐器合奏的“序曲”开始，以音乐短剧“两兄弟”结束。在晚会上演出的十四个精采的音乐舞蹈节目，受到观众的热烈欢迎和赞赏。
    表演结束时，首都文艺工作者向缅甸艺术家们献花。这时，全场起立，暴风雨般的掌声经久不息。周恩来总理等步上舞台和缅甸艺术家们亲切握手，向他们表示祝贺和慰问。在临别时，周恩来总理用缅甸话和他们说：“再见”。
