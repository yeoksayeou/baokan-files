Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish News of the CCP Central Committee Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1955-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th. Moscow newspapers on the 18th universally published the news of the Sixth Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, which was held from October 4th to 11th.

### 莫斯科各报刊登中共中央全会消息

1955-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报刊登中共中央全会消息
    新华社莫斯科十八日电　莫斯科各报十八日普遍刊载了中国共产党第七届中央委员会在十月四日至十一日举行第六次全体会议的消息。
