Title: He Long Hosts a Banquet for the Indian National Volleyball Team
Author:
Date: 1955-10-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, He Long, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 29th to entertain all members of the Indian National Volleyball Team.

    Bahadur Singh, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy in China, his wife, and embassy staff were invited to attend the banquet tonight.

    Among those attending tonight's banquet were: Cai Tingkai, Cai Shufan, Lu Han, and Huang Qixiang, Vice Chairmen of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Xiao Hua, Rong Gaotang, and Dong Shouyi, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Sports Federation; Huang Zhong, Deputy Secretary-General; Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association; Zhang Zhihuai, Chairman of the Volleyball Association of the People's Republic of China; Feng Jiping, Vice Mayor of Beijing; Chai Zemin, Director of the Beijing Municipal Physical Culture and Sports Commission; representatives of the Beijing Branch of the All-China Sports Federation; and Chinese volleyball players.

    He Long spoke first at the banquet. He said that although the Indian National Volleyball Team's time in Beijing was short, it left a deep impression on the people of Beijing. Your volleyball skills have many aspects worthy of our learning. The friendship you have shown to the Chinese people is profound. I sincerely hope that this friendship can continue to be strengthened, thereby contributing to strengthening the ties between sports circles in Asian countries and the unity of the Asian people, and to consolidating Asian and world peace.

    F.C. Arora, the leader of the Indian National Volleyball Team, then spoke. He thanked the hosts for the hospitality received in Beijing. He said that we both competed and learned from each other on the sports field, and that this visit will undoubtedly further strengthen the friendly ties between the people of the two countries. He congratulated the development of Chinese sports and the growing friendship between the Chinese and Indian people.

    Before the banquet, Rong Gaotang presented gifts to the Indian National Volleyball Team on behalf of the All-China Sports Federation. F.C. Arora presented gifts to He Long, Xiao Hua, and others, and to the All-China Sports Federation on behalf of the Indian National Volleyball Team.

### 贺龙设宴招待印度国家排球队

1955-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙设宴招待印度国家排球队
    新华社二十九日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙，二十九日晚设宴招待印度国家排球队全体人员。
    印度驻中国大使馆临时代办巴哈杜尔·辛格和他的夫人，以及使馆人员应邀出席了今晚的宴会。
    参加今晚宴会的有：体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴、蔡树藩、卢汉、黄琪翔，中华全国体育总会副主席萧华、荣高棠、董守义，副秘书长黄中，中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙，中华人民共和国排球协会主席张之槐，北京市副市长冯基平，北京市体育运动委员会主任柴泽民，以及中华全国体育总会北京市分会的代表和中国的排球运动员等。
    贺龙在宴会上首先讲话。他说，印度国家排球队在北京的时间虽然不长，但在北京人民的心目中留下了深刻的印象。你们的排球技术，许多地方是值得我们学习的。你们对中国人民表示的友谊是深厚的。我深切希望这种友谊能继续不断地加强，从而对加强亚洲国家体育界的联系和亚洲人民的团结，对巩固亚洲和世界和平作出贡献。
    印度国家排球队领队法·钱·阿罗拉接着讲话。他感谢在北京受到的款待。他说，我们在运动场上一面比赛一面相互学习，通过这种访问，无疑地将更能加强两国人民的友好联系。他祝贺中国体育事业和印中两国人民的友谊日益发展。
    宴会前，荣高棠代表中华全国体育总会向印度国家排球队赠送了礼品。法·钱·阿罗拉代表印度国家排球队向贺龙、萧华等和中华全国体育总会赠送礼品。
