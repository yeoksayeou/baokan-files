Title: East Pakistan People's Muslim League Chairman Demands Abolition of US-Pakistan Military Treaty
Author:
Date: 1955-10-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 12th: According to the Indian Press Trust on the 11th, Bhashani, Chairman of the East Pakistan People's Muslim League, at a meeting of the North Bengal People's Muslim League which began on the 9th, demanded the abolition of the military treaty concluded between Pakistan and the United States.
    Bhashani also put forward demands for the realization of complete regional autonomy for East Bengal, the abolition of the landlord system, the distribution of land free of charge, the abolition of the Public Safety Act, and the withdrawal of accusations against political prisoners.

### 东巴基斯坦人民回教联盟主席  要求废除美巴军事条约

1955-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    东巴基斯坦人民回教联盟主席
    要求废除美巴军事条约
    新华社新德里十二日电　据印度报业托辣斯十一日报道，东巴基斯坦人民回教联盟主席巴沙尼在九日开始举行的北孟加拉人民回教联盟的会议上，要求废除巴基斯坦同美国缔结的军事条约。
    巴沙尼还提出了实现东孟加拉的完全的区域自治、取消地主制度、无偿地分配土地、废除公共安全法和撤销对政治犯的控诉等要求。
