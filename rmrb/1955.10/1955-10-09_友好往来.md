	Friendly Exchanges
	General Ne Win Returns Home via Guangzhou
	General Ne Win, leader of the Burmese Military Goodwill Delegation and Commander-in-Chief of the Burmese Armed Forces, Brigadier General Kyaw Win, Director of the Supreme Headquarters Military Affairs Department, and others arrived in Guangzhou by plane from Beijing on the afternoon of the 6th and left Guangzhou by train via Hong Kong on the morning of the 7th to return home.
	Present at the station to see them off were Wen Niansheng, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Guangzhou, Chen Zhifang, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, and others.
	Major General An Dong, representative of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, accompanied General Ne Win to Shenzhen, and Lieutenant Colonel Maung Maung, military attaché of the Burmese Federation to China, accompanied him to Hong Kong.
	On the evening of the 6th, General Ne Win and others visited the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition in Guangzhou.
	China Commodity Exhibition Delegation to Japan Departs for Japan
	The China Council for the Promotion of International Trade's commodity exhibition delegation to Japan, consisting of more than forty people led by Cao Zhongshu, head of the delegation, and Feng Tiecheng and Xiao Xiangqian, deputy heads of the delegation, left Beijing for Japan in September and early October.
	The commodity exhibition to Japan is being held in accordance with the provisions of the Third Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement of 1955, which stipulates that both parties agree to hold separate commodity exhibitions in each other's countries. The exhibition will open in Tokyo in mid-October and then move to Osaka. The entire exhibit consists of more than 3,000 items, most of which are new products from 1954 and 1955, with only a few produced in 1953. The exhibition will introduce China's economic construction achievements in recent years and the ever-increasing new export materials. (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 友好往来

1955-10-09
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    奈温中将经广州回国
    缅甸军事友好代表团团长、缅甸联邦武装部队总司令奈温中将和最高统帅部军务署署长觉温准将等，在六日下午从北京乘飞机到达广州，七日早晨由广州乘火车经香港回国。
    到车站欢送的，有中国人民解放军驻广州部队的首长文年生，广州市副市长陈志方和广东省人民委员会外事处处长曹若茗等。
    中华人民共和国国防部代表安东少将陪送奈温中将到深圳，缅甸联邦驻华武官茂茂中校陪送到香港。
    在六日晚上，奈温中将等曾到广州苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会参观。
    中国赴日本商品展览团去日本
    中国国际贸易促进委员会赴日本商品展览团一行四十多人，在团长曹中枢、副团长冯铁城、萧向前率领下，已在九月和十月上旬先后离京去日本。
    赴日本商品展览会是根据一九五五年第三次中日贸易协定关于双方同意相互在对方国家单独举办商品展览会的规定举办的。展览会将于十月中旬在东京开幕，然后去大阪展出。全部展品共三千多种，其中除少数是一九五三年生产的以外，极大部分是一九五四年和一九五五年的新产品。展览会将介绍中国近几年来经济建设的成就，和不断增加着的新的出口物资。（据新华社讯）
