Title: Responses to the Newspaper's Criticism
Author:
Date: 1955-10-17
Page: 3

Responses to the Newspaper's Criticism

    On May 29th, this newspaper published an article entitled "The Yangquan Rolling Iron Plant Which Ceased Production After Only One Year of Operation," criticizing the Shanxi Provincial Department of Industry for making a rash decision to build the plant without thorough consideration of extremely immature construction conditions, resulting in the Yangquan Rolling Iron Plant ceasing production after only one year of operation. The Shanxi Provincial Department of Industry has sent a letter of self-criticism.
    On July 6th, this newspaper published an article entitled "The Fate of Original Equipment at the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant," criticizing the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant for its rough handling of original equipment, resulting in numerous machine tool accidents. Now, the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant has sent a letter of self-criticism, analyzing the causes of the errors, and proposing methods for improvement.
    On July 17th, this newspaper published an article entitled "The Evil Consequences of Violating the State's Unified Labor Allocation Policy," criticizing the Taiyuan Xishan Coal Mine for violating the state's unified labor allocation policy, randomly recruiting and employing workers, and creating chaotic labor organization. Now, the Xishan Coal Mine Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has sent a letter of self-criticism.

### 对本报批评的反应

1955-10-17
第3版()
专栏：对本报批评的反应

    对本报批评的反应
    五月二十九日本报发表了“建成一年就停止生产的阳泉辗铁厂”一文，批评山西省工业厅在建厂条件极不成熟的情况下，没有做周密的考虑就轻率地决定建厂，致使阳泉辗铁厂建厂只一年就停止生产。山西省工业厅已来信检讨。
    七月六日本报发表了“沈阳第一机床厂原有设备的厄运”一文，批评沈阳第一机床厂对待原有设备采取粗暴的态度，机床事故多。现在，沈阳第一机床厂已来信检讨，分析了错误的原因，并提出了改进方法。
    七月十七日本报发表了“违反国家劳动力统一调配政策的恶果”一文，批评太原西山煤矿违反国家劳动力统一调配政策，乱招乱用工人，劳动组织混乱。现在中共西山煤矿委员会已来信检讨。
