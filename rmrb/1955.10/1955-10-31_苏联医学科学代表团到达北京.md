Title: Soviet Medical Science Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-31
Page: 1

    Soviet Medical Science Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: The Soviet Medical Science Delegation, consisting of ten members, arrived in Beijing by train on the 30th at the invitation of the Ministry of Health of the People's Republic of China for lecturing and visiting China.
    Welcoming them at the station were Cui Yitian, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Health of the People's Republic of China; Shen Qizhen, Director of the Central Institute of Health; Hu Chuankui, Dean of Beijing Medical College; representatives of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, prominent figures in the capital's medical field, and Soviet medical experts.
    The list of delegation members is as follows:
    Professor Klotchkov, Professor of Hygiene, Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences
    Professor Anchelava, Professor of Surgery
    Professor Petrishcheva, Professor of Parasitology
    Professor Ponomareva, Professor of Pediatrics
    Professor Kvasov, Professor of Physiology
    Professor Kavetsky, Professor of Pathophysiology
    Professor Biribin, Professor of Infectious Diseases
    Professor Pishkovsky, Professor of Immunology
    Professor Myasnikov, Professor of Internal Medicine
    Professor Nabokov, Professor of Epidemiology

### 苏联医学科学代表团到达北京

1955-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联医学科学代表团到达北京
    新华社三十日讯　应中华人民共和国卫生部邀请来中国讲学并访问的苏联医学科学代表团一行十人，在三十日乘车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有中华人民共和国卫生部副部长崔义田，中央卫生研究院院长沈其震，北京医学院院长胡传揆以及中苏友好协会总会代表、首都医药界著名人士和苏联医学专家等多人。
    代表团人员名单如下：
    克洛特科夫卫生学教授，苏联医学科学院副院长
    安切拉瓦外科教授
    彼特里谢娃寄生虫学教授
    波诺马罗娃儿科学教授
    克瓦索夫生理学教授
    卡维茨基病理生理学教授
    比里宾传染病学教授
    皮什科夫斯基免疫学教授
    米亚斯尼科夫内科学教授
    纳波科夫流行病学教授
