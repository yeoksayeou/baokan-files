Title: People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Commemorate the Centenary of Michurin's Birth
Author:
Date: 1955-10-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 27th. People from all walks of life in Beijing are conducting various activities to commemorate the centenary of Michurin's birth. On the evening of the 27th, more than 700 children who love natural science held a commemorative gathering. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and other scientists, as well as Beijing agricultural labor models Li Molin and Qin Delin, and the heads of the Beijing Education Bureau, the Beijing Science and Technology Popularization Association, and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and biology teachers from various middle schools were invited to attend the conference. Guo Moruo introduced Michurin's life at the meeting and encouraged the children to learn from Michurin's spirit of loving the motherland, loving the people, loving labor, loving science, and cherishing public property. Various middle schools in Beijing reported on their achievements in biological research to the conference. The children carried their own pickled animal and plant specimens and small animals raised by them to the rostrum, at which time warm applause resounded throughout the venue.

The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization jointly held a report meeting on the afternoon of the 27th. At the meeting, Soviet expert Xie Kong from the Biology Department of Beijing Normal University gave a report on the development of Michurin's theory in the Soviet Union to more than 600 scientific workers in agriculture and biology in Beijing. Chen Fengtong, Director of the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute, introduced methods of applying Michurin's theory in agricultural production.

Beijing Agricultural University will hold a "Michurin Memorial Week" from the 27th to November 3rd. On the 27th, at the memorial meeting attended by more than 2,000 teachers and students, Soviet selection expert Protskov made a report on Michurin's life and career and the development of Michurin's biological science in the Soviet Union. Chen Xiufu, Director of the Crop Genetics and Breeding Teaching and Research Group, introduced the research results of asexual hybridization between tomato varieties based on Michurin's theory for more than four years. Various specimens, objects, and pictures displayed at the Michurin Memorial Exhibition, which opened on the same day, concentratedly showed the achievements of teachers and students in studying Michurin's theory. During this week, the departments of agronomy, horticulture, soil agrochemistry, plant protection, animal husbandry and veterinary medicine will also hold scientific report meetings. Teachers and students from the Biology Department of Peking University and Beijing Forestry College also held commemorative meetings on the 27th. Beijing No. 3 Girls' Middle School will establish two science groups named after Michurin on the 28th.

The Cultural Center of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a "Michurin Centenary Film Show." The Michurin Memorial Exhibition held by the Beijing Library is scheduled to open on the 28th and will exhibit pictures of Michurin's life and career, original Michurin works, and more than 100 kinds of translations in Chinese, Polish, Czech, Hungarian, Bulgarian, English, and French. More than 600 people have already attended the first lecture of the "Achievements of Michurin's Theory in New China" report meeting held by the Beijing Science and Technology Popularization Association, and it will continue to be held in the future.

### 首都各界人民纪念米丘林诞生一百周年

1955-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界人民纪念米丘林诞生一百周年
    新华社二十七日讯　北京市各界人民进行各种活动，纪念米丘林诞生一百周年。二十七日晚，七百多个爱好自然科学的少年儿童举行了纪念集会。应邀参加大会的有中国科学院院长郭沫若和其他科学家，以及北京市农业劳动模范李墨林、秦德林，北京市教育局、北京市科学技术普及协会、中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会负责人和各中学的生物教师。郭沫若在会上介绍了米丘林的生平，并且鼓励少年儿童学习米丘林热爱祖国、热爱人民、热爱劳动、热爱科学和爱护公共财物的精神。北京市各中学向大会汇报了生物研究的成绩。孩子们抬着自己浸制的动、植物标本，饲养的小动物从会场走上了主席台，这时，全场响起了热烈的掌声。
    中苏友好协会总会和中华全国科学技术普及协会在二十七日下午联合举行了报告会，会上北京师范大学生物系苏联专家谢孔向北京六百多农业和生物方面的科学工作者作了关于米丘林学说在苏联的发展的报告，华北农业科学研究所所长陈凤桐介绍了在农业生产中应用米丘林学说的方法。
    北京农业大学从二十七日起到十一月三日举行“米丘林纪念周”，二十七日，在全校二千多名师生参加的纪念会上，苏联选种学专家普罗茨柯夫作了关于米丘林的生平事业和米丘林生物科学在苏联的发展的报告。作物遗传选种教研组主任陈秀夫介绍了四年多来根据米丘林学说进行番茄品种间的无性杂交的研究成果。在同一天开幕的米丘林纪念展览会上陈列的各种标本、实物、图片，集中地表现了师生们学习米丘林学说的成绩。这一周内，农学、园艺、土壤农化、植物保护、畜牧兽医等系还将分别举行科学报告会。北京大学生物系和北京林学院师生也在二十七日举行了纪念会。北京市第三女子中学将在二十八日成立两个以米丘林命名的科学小组。
    北京市中苏友好协会文化馆举办了“纪念米丘林诞生一百周年电影会”。北京图书馆举办的米丘林纪念展览订于二十八日开幕，将展出有关米丘林生平事业的图片，米丘林著作原本以及中国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、保加利亚和英国、法国等国文字的译本一百多种。北京市科学技术普及协会举办的“米丘林学说在新中国的成就”报告会，第一次听讲的已有六百多人，今后还将继续举行。
