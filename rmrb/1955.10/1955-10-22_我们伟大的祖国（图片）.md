    Our Great Motherland
    After the publication of Chairman Mao's report "On the Question of Agricultural Cooperation," the broad masses of peasants in Sichuan Province were overjoyed and further strengthened their confidence in taking the socialist road. When the newspaper arrived in Su Po Township, Wenjiang County, the peasants excitedly read Chairman Mao's report.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    毛主席“关于农业合作化问题”的报告公布后，四川省广大农民欢欣鼓舞，更加坚定了走社会主义道路的信心。当报纸来到了温江县苏坡乡后，农民们兴奋地阅读毛主席的报告。
