Our Great Motherland
Many places in our country are actively studying Michurin's theories and spreading advanced agricultural experiences. Members of the "Michurin Group" of the First Agricultural Production Cooperative in Qunzhong Township, Zhongshan County, Guangdong Province, are using microscopes under the guidance of technical extension station personnel to study the root causes of rice pests and diseases.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国许多地方积极学习米丘林的学说，传播先进的农业经验。广东省中山县群众乡第一农业生产合作社的“米丘林小组”组员，正在技术推广站工作人员指导下用显微镜研究水稻病虫害的根源。
