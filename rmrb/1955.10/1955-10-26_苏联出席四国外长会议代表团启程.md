Title: Soviet Delegation to Four-Power Foreign Ministers Conference Departs
Author:
Date: 1955-10-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. TASS Moscow, October 25th: The Soviet Council of Ministers has approved the Soviet delegation to participate in the Geneva Four-Power Foreign Ministers Conference. The members of the delegation are as follows:

	V.M. Molotov, A.A. Gromyko, Soviet Marshal V.D. Sokolovsky, S.A. Vinogradov, G.M. Pushkin.

	The Council of Ministers also appointed V.S. Semenov, S.K. Tsarapkin, L.F. Ilyichev, I.G. Tunkin, S.G. Lapin, and N.I. Chikhlin as advisors to the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. TASS Moscow, October 25th: The Soviet delegation participating in the Four-Power Foreign Ministers Conference, led by V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs, left Moscow by plane for Geneva on the morning of the 25th.

### 苏联出席四国外长会议代表团启程

1955-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联出席四国外长会议代表团启程
    新华社二十五日讯　塔斯社莫斯科二十五日讯：苏联部长会议已经批准参加日内瓦四国外长会议的苏联代表团，代表团的成员如下：
    维·米·莫洛托夫、安·安·葛罗米柯、苏联元帅瓦·德·索科洛夫斯基、谢·阿·维诺格拉多夫、格·马·普希金。
    部长会议并任命维·斯·凯曼诺夫、谢·克·查拉普金、勒·弗·伊利切夫、伊·格·童金、斯·格·拉宾、纳·伊·契克林为代表团顾问。
    新华社二十五日讯　塔斯社莫斯科二十五日讯：参加四国外长会议的苏联代表团在苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫率领下在二十五日早上乘飞机离开莫斯科赴日内瓦。
