Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Visits Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1955-10-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 4th. According to TASS: A delegation from the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, led by Volkov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet, arrived in Belgrade by plane on the 3rd at the invitation of the Federal People's Assembly of Yugoslavia.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Djesić, Chairman of the Federal Chamber of the Federal People's Assembly of Yugoslavia, Pocišević, Chairman of the Chamber of Producers, Jeković, member of the Federal Executive Council, Bulkić, Deputy Secretary of Foreign Affairs, and others.
    Volkov said in a speech at the airport that the visit of the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation to Yugoslavia was in response to the visit of the Yugoslav National Assembly delegation to the Soviet Union not long ago. The visit of the Yugoslav National Assembly delegation to the Soviet Union means a further expansion and strengthening of the long-standing friendship between the Soviet and Yugoslav peoples. The Soviet people firmly believe that the period of breakdown in our friendly relations is now a thing of the past, and there are now no obstacles that can prevent our two peoples from peacefully and friendly building their happy lives. Volkov expressed his deep conviction that the visit of the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation to Yugoslavia will promote the further development and consolidation of comprehensive political, economic and cultural cooperation between the Soviet and Yugoslav peoples, which will be beneficial to the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia, and to peace and socialism.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团到南斯拉夫访问

1955-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团到南斯拉夫访问
    新华社四日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德讯：苏联最高苏维埃代表团应南斯拉夫联邦国民议会的邀请，在苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫率领下，三日乘飞机到达贝尔格莱德。
    在机场欢迎的有南斯拉夫联邦国民议会联邦院主席捷西维奇、生产者院主席波齐西维奇、联邦执行委员会委员捷科维奇、外交副秘书长布尔基奇等人。
    沃尔科夫在机场发表讲话说，苏联最高苏维埃代表团这次访问南斯拉夫是回答南斯拉夫国民议会代表团前不久对苏联的访问。南斯拉夫国民议会代表团访苏意味着苏南两国人民之间悠久友谊的进一步扩大和加强。苏联人民深信，我们的友善关系破裂的时期现在已经成为过去了，现在不存在任何障碍，可以阻碍我们两国人民和平和友好地建设自己的幸福生活。沃尔科夫表示深信苏联最高苏维埃代表团访问南斯拉夫，将促使苏南两国人民之间的全面的政治、经济和文化合作进一步发展和巩固，这将对苏南两国、对和平和社会主义有利。
