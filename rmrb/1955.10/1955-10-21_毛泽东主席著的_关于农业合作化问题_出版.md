Title: Chairman Mao Zedong's "On the Question of Agricultural Cooperation" Published
Author:
Date: 1955-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Mao Zedong's "On the Question of Agricultural Cooperation" was published by the People's Publishing House on the 20th and simultaneously distributed in bookstores across Beijing. The initial Beijing print run of this book is 800,000 copies, and it will also be printed and distributed in large quantities in 25 regions including Shanghai, Wuhan, Chongqing, Guangzhou, Xi'an, Urumqi, and Shenyang.

Today, the book with the highest sales volume in Beijing bookstores is "On the Question of Agricultural Cooperation." Readers in the city have already pre-ordered more than 56,000 copies of this book from the various outlets of the Xinhua Bookstore Beijing Branch. This afternoon, Xinhua Bookstore convened a meeting of the directors of suburban outlets to study the issue of deeply penetrating rural areas and agricultural production cooperatives to sell this important work.

"Resolution of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the Question of Agricultural Cooperation" will be published on the 23rd, along with Chen Boda's "Explanation on the Draft Resolution on the Question of Agricultural Cooperation".

### 毛泽东主席著的“关于农业合作化问题”出版

1955-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席著的“关于农业合作化问题”出版
    新华社二十日讯　毛泽东著的“关于农业合作化问题”，二十日由人民出版社出版，同时在北京各书店发行。这本书在北京初版印刷八十万册，还将在上海、武汉、重庆、广州、西安、乌鲁木齐和沈阳等二十五个地区大量印刷和发行。
    今天，北京各书店销书量最大的就是“关于农业合作化问题”一书。市内读者已向新华书店北京分店各门市部预购这本书五万六千多册。新华书店在今天下午召集郊区门市部主任开会研究了深入乡村和农业生产合作社销售这本重要著作的问题。
    二十三日将出版“中国共产党中央委员会关于农业合作化问题的决议”，并附有陈伯达“关于农业合作化问题的决议草案的说明”一文。
