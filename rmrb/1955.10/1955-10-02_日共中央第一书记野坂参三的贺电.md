Title: Telegram of Greetings from Sanzo Nosaka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1955-10-02
Page: 3

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China in Beijing:

    On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the founding of thePeople’s Republic of China, the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party extends its heartfelt congratulations to the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people, who are successfully carrying out socialist construction. Learning from the great experience of your party and people, we are determined to continue to fight for peace in Asia and the national independence of our country, consolidate the unity of the party ranks, and strengthen ties with the people.

    Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Japanese people.

                 Sanzo Nosaka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party

### 日共中央第一书记野坂参三的贺电

1955-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    日共中央第一书记野坂参三的贺电北京中国共产党中央委员会：
    值此中华人民共和国成立六周年之际，日本共产党中央委员会谨向成功地进行社会主义建设的中国共产党和中国人民表示衷心的祝贺。学习你们的党和人民的伟大经验，我们决心为亚洲和平和我国的民族独立继续奋斗，巩固党的队伍的团结和加强同人民的联系。
    中国人民和日本人民之间的友谊万岁。
                      日本共产党中央委员会第一书记　野坂参三
