Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Continues to Inspect Returning Chinese People's Volunteers

Author: 
Date: 1955-10-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Xin'yiju, 13th - The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission stationed in Xin'yiju continued to inspect military personnel of the Chinese People's Volunteers returning from Korea and the weapons and ammunition they carried on the 11th and 12th.
　　The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission received full cooperation and assistance from the Liaison Officers of the Korean and Chinese sides and the personnel of the returning Chinese People's Volunteers during their work, and smoothly carried out the inspection. In the three days from the 10th to the 12th, they did not raise any questions to the Liaison Officers of the Korean and Chinese sides regarding any doubts arising during the inspection.

### 中立国视察小组  继续视察志愿军归国部队

1955-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中立国视察小组
    继续视察志愿军归国部队
    新华社新义州十三日电　驻新义州的中立国视察小组十一日和十二日继续视察了撤出朝鲜的中国人民志愿军归国部队的军事人员和他们所携带的武器弹药。
    中立国视察小组在工作中得到朝中方面联络官和中国人民志愿军归国部队人员的充分合作和协助，顺利地进行了视察。从十日到十二日的三天中，他们没有向朝中方面联络官提出任何在视察中发生的疑问。
