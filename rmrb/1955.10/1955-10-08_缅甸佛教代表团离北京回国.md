Title: Burmese Buddhist Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1955-10-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Burmese Buddhist Delegation, after enshrining the Buddha's tooth in Beijing, left Beijing by train on the evening of the 7th to return home. The delegation will visit Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places on its way back.

The China Buddhist Association has specially dispatched a Buddha Tooth Escort Delegation to accompany the delegation to Yangon. The escort delegation is composed of Zhao Puchu, Vice President and Secretary-General of the China Buddhist Association, Zhou Shujia, Executive Director, and others.

There is a special carriage on the train, which is equipped with a Buddhist hall to enshrine the Buddha's tooth for the delegation to worship along the way.

Among those who went to the station to see them off were: Zhang Zhiran, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court of the People's Republic of China, Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the National Affairs Commission, Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and more than 300 people from religious circles in Beijing.

U Hla Maw, Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, also went to the station to see them off.

At 5:30 p.m. on the 7th, the China Buddhist Association, the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, and the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association jointly held a banquet to bid farewell to the delegation.

During their stay in Beijing, the delegation visited the Yonghe Temple, Tongjiao Temple, Big Bell Temple, Reclining Buddha Temple, Biyun Temple, and the old site of the "Portrait Thousand Buddha Pagoda" - Lingguang Temple, where the Buddha's tooth was enshrined, and offered vegetarian food to the monks at Guangji Temple. On the morning of the 7th, the delegation visited Peking University and toured the Summer Palace.

### 缅甸佛教代表团离北京回国

1955-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸佛教代表团离北京回国
    新华社七日讯　缅甸联邦佛教代表团在北京奉迎佛牙以后，七日晚乘火车离北京返国。代表团在归国途中将在南京、上海、杭州等地参观。
    中国佛教协会特派佛牙护送团随同代表团前往仰光。护送团由中国佛教协会副会长兼秘书长赵朴初、常务理事周叔迦等组成。
    在火车上专有一节车厢，内设佛堂，供奉佛牙，以供代表团沿途敬拜。
    前往车站欢送的有：中华人民共和国最高人民法院副院长张志让，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋，外交部部长助理陈家康，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，北京市副市长冯基平及北京宗教界人士等三百余人。
    缅甸联邦驻华大使吴拉茂也到车站欢送。
    七日下午五时半，中国佛教协会、国务院宗教事务局、中缅友好协会联合举行宴会欢送代表团。
    代表团在京期间，曾参拜了雍和宫、通教寺、大钟寺、卧佛寺、碧云寺和供养佛牙的“画像千佛塔”旧址——灵光寺等，并在广济寺斋僧。七日上午，代表团参观了北京大学，游览了颐和园。
