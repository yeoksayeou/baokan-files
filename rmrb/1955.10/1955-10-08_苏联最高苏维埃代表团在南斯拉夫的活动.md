Title: Activities of the Supreme Soviet Delegation of the Soviet Union in Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1955-10-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 6th dispatch from TASS: The Supreme Soviet delegation of the Soviet Union, which was invited to visit Yugoslavia, paid a courtesy call to Pijade, Chairman of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, on the morning of the 4th. During the meeting, Volkov, the head of the Supreme Soviet delegation of the Soviet Union, and Pijade, the Chairman of the Federal National Assembly, both expressed their hope that the contact between the parliamentarians of the two countries would help further strengthen the friendly ties between the two countries and international peace.
    On the afternoon of the 4th, Pijade held a banquet in the Parliament Building to entertain the Supreme Soviet delegation of the Soviet Union.
    In his speech at the banquet, Pijade said that after the Yugoslav parliamentary delegation returned from its visit to the Soviet Union, it gained a deep impression of the great achievements and huge constructive efforts of the Soviet people, and believed that the Soviet people held genuine sympathy for the Yugoslav people and fully supported the policy of cooperation between the two countries. Pijade emphasized the great significance of the Supreme Soviet delegation's visit to Yugoslavia. He also pointed out that the Belgrade Declaration has laid the foundation for friendly cooperation between the two countries and has made a significant contribution to strengthening international peace and security. Pijade welcomed lasting and friendly cooperation between the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia. He said that the Yugoslav people unreservedly support the policy of friendly cooperation with the Soviet Union.
    In his reply, Volkov pointed out that the normalization of relations between the two countries is one of the most important factors in helping to ease international tensions and consolidate world peace and security. Volkov said that the Soviet people sincerely strive to make the friendship between the two countries more widely developed and stronger than ever before.
    On the 5th, the Supreme Soviet delegation of the Soviet Union split into two groups and visited the Atomic Energy Research Institute near Belgrade and the Ivo Lola Ribar Heavy Machinery Manufacturing Plant in Ciresnik, respectively. The Soviet representatives held talks with the factory's responsible personnel and workers for more than two hours.
    The Soviet delegation left Belgrade on the 5th to visit other places.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团在南斯拉夫的活动

1955-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团在南斯拉夫的活动
    新华社六日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德讯：应邀访问南斯拉夫的苏联最高苏维埃代表团在四日上午拜会了南斯拉夫联邦国民议会议长皮雅杰。拜会时，苏联最高苏维埃代表团团长沃尔科夫和联邦国民议会议长皮雅杰都表示希望两国议员间的接触能有助于进一步加强两国间的友好联系和国际和平。
    四日下午，皮雅杰在议会大厦举行宴会，招待苏联最高苏维埃代表团。
    皮雅杰在宴会上致词时说，南斯拉夫议会代表团在访问苏联归来后，对苏联人民的伟大成就和巨大的建设性努力获得很深的印象，并且相信苏联人民对南斯拉夫人民抱有真诚的同情，而且全力支持两国间合作的政策。皮雅杰强调最高苏维埃代表团访问南斯拉夫的重大意义。他还指出，贝尔格莱德宣言已经为两国间的友好合作打下基础。并且已经对加强国际和平和安全作出了重大贡献。皮雅杰欢迎苏联同南斯拉夫间的持久和友好的合作。他说，南斯拉夫人民无保留地支持同苏联友好合作的政策。
    沃尔科夫在答词中指出两国关系正常化是帮助缓和国际紧张局势、巩固世界和平和安全的最重大因素之一。沃尔科夫说苏联人民真诚地努力使两国的友谊得到更广泛的发展，并且比过去更加坚固。
    五日，苏联最高苏维埃代表团分为两批，分别参观了贝尔格莱德附近的原子能研究所和在齐列斯尼克的伊沃·洛拉·里巴尔重型机器制造厂。苏联代表们在厂里同厂内负责人员和工人们进行了两小时以上的交谈。
    苏联代表团已在五日离开贝尔格莱德转赴其他各地参观。
