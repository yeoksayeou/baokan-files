Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1955-10-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 15th: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party held a plenary session on the 12th and 13th.
    The meeting discussed the report made by Comrade Pasek, Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, on the current situation, development, and tasks of the construction industry, and adopted corresponding resolutions.
    The plenary session also discussed the report made by Siroky on certain organizational and personnel matters in the work of the Czechoslovak Republic government, and adopted a resolution. The resolution recommends that K. Polacek serve as Deputy Prime Minister of the Republic; proposes dividing the machine industry into a Ministry of Heavy Machine Industry, a Ministry of Precision Machine Industry, and a Ministry of Automobile and Agricultural Machine Industry; dividing agriculture into a Ministry of Agriculture and a Ministry of State Farms; and proposes the establishment of a National Construction Committee. The resolution also recommended the responsible persons for the above-mentioned ministers and chairman of the committee.

### 捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央举行全体会议

1955-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央举行全体会议
    新华社布拉格十五日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会在十二日和十三日举行了全体会议。
    会议讨论了捷共中央书记帕歇克所作的关于建筑工业的目前情况、发展和任务的报告，并且通过了相应的决议。
    全体会议还讨论了西罗基所作的关于捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府工作中某些组织和人事问题的报告，并且通过了决议。决议中建议卡·波拉契克担任共和国副总理；建议把机器工业部分为重型机器工业部，精密机器工业部，汽车工业和农业机器部；把农业部分为农业部和国营农场部；并且建议设立国家建设委员会。决议还推荐了上述各部部长和委员会主席的负责人。
