Title: Live Broadcast Today of National Day Celebrations in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Central People's Broadcasting Station has decided to broadcast the entire live broadcast of the military parade, march-past, and mass parade celebrating National Day in the capital, starting at 9:40 am on October 1st – the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China – using all wavelengths. People's broadcasting stations across the country will also simulcast.

### 首都庆祝国庆节实况今天广播

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    首都庆祝国庆节实况今天广播
    新华社三十日讯　中央人民广播电台决定在十月一日——中华人民共和国成立六周年纪念日上午九时四十分起，用全部波长广播首都庆祝国庆节的阅兵式、分列式和群众游行的全部实况。全国各地人民广播电台也将同时联播。
