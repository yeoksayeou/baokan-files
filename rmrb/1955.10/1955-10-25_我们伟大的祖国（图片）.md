Author:
Date: 1955-10-25
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Jintan Canal, built by the state for farmers and herdsmen in Jinyintan, Qinghai Province, has been completed and has begun to release water. The canal is more than 14 kilometers long and can provide drinking water for tens of thousands of sheep and irrigate 25,000 mu of land. This is a scene of nearby farmers and herdsmen visiting the Jintan Canal project.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国家为青海省金银滩农民、牧民修建的金滩渠已经完工，开始放水。这条渠长十四公里多，可供数万只羊的饮水和灌溉二万五千亩土地。这是附近的农民、牧民前来参观金滩渠工程。
