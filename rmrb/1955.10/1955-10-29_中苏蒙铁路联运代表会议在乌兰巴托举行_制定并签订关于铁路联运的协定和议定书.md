Title: Sino-Soviet-Mongolian Railway Joint Transport Representatives Conference Held in Ulaanbaatar; Agreement and Protocols on Railway Joint Transport Formulated and Signed
Author:
Date: 1955-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The Sino-Soviet-Mongolian Railway Joint Transport Representatives Conference was held in Ulaanbaatar from September 20th to October 17th.

    The conference discussed issues related to the opening of Sino-Soviet-Mongolian railway joint transport on January 1, 1956. It formulated and signed the Protocol on Matters Related to the Opening of Railway Joint Transport Between the People’s Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics via the Railway of the Mongolian People’s Republic; the Sino-Mongolian Border Railway Agreement and the minutes of talks on the opening of joint passenger and freight transport between Beijing, People's Republic of China, and Ulaanbaatar, Mongolian People's Republic, from January 1, 1956; and the Protocol of the Joint Committee of the Soviet-Mongolian Border Railway.

    The agreements and protocols were signed respectively by: Mi Yong, Head of the Railway Delegation of the People’s Republic of China and Director of the International Joint Transport Bureau of the Ministry of Railways; T.G. Yermakov, Head of the Railway Delegation of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and Director of the East Siberian Railway Administration; and A. Tsogtsaikhan, Head of the Railway Delegation of the Mongolian People’s Republic and Deputy Director of the Ulaanbaatar Railway Administration.

### 中苏蒙铁路联运代表会议在乌兰巴托举行  制定并签订关于铁路联运的协定和议定书

1955-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏蒙铁路联运代表会议在乌兰巴托举行
    制定并签订关于铁路联运的协定和议定书
    新华社二十八日讯　中苏蒙铁路联运代表会议九月二十日到十月十七日在乌兰巴托举行。
    这个代表会议讨论了一九五六年一月一日开办中苏蒙铁路联运的有关问题。制定并签订了关于开办中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟间通过蒙古人民共和国铁路联运有关各项问题的议定书；中蒙国境铁路协定和规定自一九五六年一月一日开办中华人民共和国北京和蒙古人民共和国乌兰巴托间旅客、货物联运的会谈议定书；苏蒙国境铁路联合委员会议定书。
    在协定和议定书上分别签字的有：中华人民共和国铁路代表团团长、铁道部国际联运局局长糜镛；苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟铁路代表团团长、东西伯利亚铁路管理局局长特·格·叶尔马柯夫；蒙古人民共和国铁路代表团团长、乌兰巴托铁路管理局副局长阿·错格特赛汗。
