Title: Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu Receive Nenni
Author:
Date: 1955-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received Nenni, Vice Chairman of the World Peace Council, Chairman of the Italian National Peace Council, General Secretary of the Italian Socialist Party, and his wife, daughter Luciana Tomasi, Executive Member of the Italian Socialist Party, Panzieri, and Secretary of the Italian National Food Workers Union, Onsanelli at 4 p.m. on October 3.

Present at the reception were: Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Guo Moruo, and Vice Chairman, Liao Chengzhi. (Attached picture)
　　Above: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi (first from the right) and Nenni (third from the right), Mrs. Nenni (third from the left), and others in a group photo.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Lu Houmin photographed (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 毛主席和刘委员长接见南尼

1955-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和刘委员长接见南尼
    新华社三日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇在十月三日下午四时接见世界和平理事会副主席、意大利全国和平理事会主席、意大利社会党总书记南尼和他的夫人、女儿卢契安娜·托马西、意大利社会党执行委员潘齐埃里和意大利全国食品工会书记昂萨奈利。
    接见时在座的有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理邓小平、陈毅，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席廖承志。（附图片）
      上图：毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长（右第一人）和南尼（右起第三人）、南尼夫人（左起第三人）等合影。
                        吕厚民摄（新华社稿）
