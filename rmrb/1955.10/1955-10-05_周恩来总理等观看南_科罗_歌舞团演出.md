Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Others Watch Performance by Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Ensemble

Author:

Date: 1955-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Premier of the State Council of the People’s Republic of China Zhou Enlai, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Shen Junru and Chen Shutong, Vice-Premier of the State Council Ulanhu, Vice-Chairmen of the National Defense Council Cheng Qian and Fu Zuoyi, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Vice Ministers of Culture Liu Zhiming and Xia Yan, and Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Gan Siqi, attended a grand performance held by the Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Ensemble in Beijing on the evening of the 4th.

Also attending the evening's event were members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, heads of government departments, members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, heads of various people's organizations, staff members of central and Beijing municipal organs, figures from the capital's literary and art circles, and local work leaders who are currently in Beijing for meetings.

Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, also attended tonight's performance. At the evening party, the eighteen beautiful dances, music, and folk songs performed by the Yugoslav artists were warmly welcomed and appreciated by the audience.

After the performance, literary and art workers from the capital presented flowers to all the performers of the Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Ensemble. Then, Premier Zhou Enlai and others went on stage and warmly shook hands with the Yugoslav artists. At this moment, the entire audience stood up, and a burst of cheers and applause resounded throughout the hall.

### 周恩来总理等观看南“科罗”歌舞团演出

1955-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理等观看南“科罗”歌舞团演出
    新华社四日讯　南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团四日晚在北京举行了一次盛大演出晚会。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、陈叔通，国务院副总理乌兰夫，国防委员会副主席程潜、傅作义，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国务院秘书长习仲勋，文化部副部长刘芝明、夏衍，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将出席观看了南斯拉夫艺术家们的演出。
    出席今天晚会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，政府各部门负责人，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员，各人民团体负责人，中央和北京市各机关工作人员，首都文艺界人士和正在北京开会的各地地方工作负责人。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇也出席观看了今晚的演出。在晚会上，南斯拉夫艺术家们演出的十八个优美的舞蹈、音乐和民歌节目，受到了观众的热烈欢迎和赞赏。
    演出结束以后，首都文艺工作者向南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团的全体演出人员献花。接着，周恩来总理等上台和南斯拉夫的艺术家们亲切握手。这时，全场起立，响起了一片欢呼声和鼓掌声。
