Title: Ji'er Line Gauge Widening Project Completed
Author:
Date: 1955-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency

    Ji'er Line Gauge Widening Project Completed
    In order to facilitate the early opening of through traffic on the Jining-Ulaanbaatar Railway, the gauge widening project on the Ji'er Line was completed at 12:30 on the 12th. At 8:00 a.m. on the 13th, the first wide-gauge locomotive departed from Jining Station and headed straight for Erenhot Station.
    The gauge of the Ji'er Line was widened according to the gauge standard of the Jining-Ulaanbaatar Railway in the territory of the Mongolian People's Republic. During the gauge widening project, the workers responded to the call of the General Headquarters to ensure quality, ensure safety, and improve labor efficiency, and launched a vigorous labor competition. As a result, the gauge widening project was completed two and a half days ahead of schedule. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 集二线轨距加宽工程已完成

1955-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    集二线轨距加宽工程已完成
    为了使集宁—乌兰巴托铁路早日通车联运，集二线轨距的拨宽工程已在十二日十二时三十分完成。十三日上午八时，由集宁开出了第一台宽轨机车，直驶二连车站。
    集二线的轨距是按照集宁—乌兰巴托铁路在蒙古人民共和国境内的轨距标准而加宽的。在拨宽轨距的工程中，工人们响应总指挥部提出的保证质量、保证安全、提高劳动效率的号召，展开了热火朝天的劳动竞赛，结果比计划提前两天半完成了拨宽轨距的工程。（新华社）
