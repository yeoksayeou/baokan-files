Title: More Than 4,000 Workers in Chongqing Hold Mobilization Meeting to Guarantee Completion and Over-Completion of the Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1955-10-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 12th - Chongqing held a city-wide worker mobilization meeting on the afternoon of the 10th with the participation of more than 4,100 people, to guarantee the completion and over-completion of the First Five-Year Plan. Workers from factories, mines, and enterprises throughout the city listened to a live broadcast of the meeting.

Lu Dadong, Deputy Secretary of the Chongqing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, delivered a report at the meeting. He called on the city's workers to actively respond to the "Letter to Workers Across the Country" from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, to mobilize vigorously in the last quarter of this year, redouble their efforts, and guarantee the over-completion of the 1955 national plan, creating conditions for the completion of the First Five-Year Plan. Lu Dadong called on the city's workers to vigorously conduct propaganda and education on the First Five-Year Plan, conscientiously study the experience of combining team-based economic accounting with labor emulation, continuously study and apply advanced experiences, and combine labor emulation with wage rewards. While striving to complete the national plan, it is also necessary to heighten vigilance and fight to the end to eliminate all hidden counter-revolutionaries. Zhang Xianyi, Vice Chairman of the Chongqing Federation of Trade Unions, called on the city's workers at the meeting to actively participate in labor emulation under the leadership of the Party and strive to successfully complete and over-fulfill this year's national plan.

Representatives of industrial labor models, model groups, youth steelmaking teams, worker family members, and trade union workers in Chongqing all spoke at the meeting, unanimously expressing their active response to the call of the Party and the trade union. Many factory, mine, enterprise, and construction site workers called the meeting to present their guarantees, resolutions, and challenges to strive to over-fulfill this year's production tasks after careful discussion. In their guarantee, the workers of Chongqing No. 1 Steel Plant proposed: in addition to completing this year's national plan, they will produce an additional 10,000 tons of high-quality steel and 7,000 tons of steel rails, steel plates, and other rolled steel products for the country, increasing the total value of production by 5 million yuan compared to the national plan, reducing product costs by 3 million yuan compared to the actual cost in 1954, and increasing profits turned over to the state by 3.95 million yuan compared to the national plan. They also guaranteed the successful trial production of six new products. According to statistics, the conference received a total of 157 letters of guarantee from workers in the city's steel, machinery, coal, electricity, textile, railway transportation, inland river shipping, local industrial and mining enterprises, and various enterprises in response to the conference.

### 重庆市职工四千多人举行动员大会  保证完成并超额完成五年计划

1955-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    重庆市职工四千多人举行动员大会
    保证完成并超额完成五年计划
    新华社重庆十二日电　重庆市在十日下午举行有四千一百多人参加的全市职工保证完成和超额完成第一个五年计划的动员大会。全市各厂矿、企业单位的职工都收听了大会的实况广播。
    中共重庆市委员会副书记鲁大东在会上作了报告。他号召全市职工积极响应中华全国总工会的
  “告全国职工书”，在今年最后一个季度紧张地动员起来，加倍努力，保证更多地超额完成一九五五年的国家计划，给完成第一个五年计划创造条件。鲁大东号召全市职工要大力进行第一个五年计划的宣传教育，认真学习班组经济核算和劳动竞赛相结合的经验，不断地学习和运用先进经验，并把劳动竞赛和工资奖励结合起来。在争取完成国家计划的同时，还必须提高警惕，为肃清一切暗藏的反革命分子而斗争到底。重庆市工会联合会副主席张显仪在大会上号召全市职工在党的领导下积极参加劳动竞赛，为胜利完成和超额完成今年国家计划而努力。
    重庆市工业劳动模范代表、模范小组代表、青年炼钢班代表、职工家属代表、工会工作者代表等都在会上发言，一致表示积极响应党和工会的号召。许多厂矿、企业、工地的职工纷纷打电话给大会，提出他们经过仔细讨论的争取超额完成今年生产任务的保证书、决心书和挑战书。重庆第一钢铁厂的职工在保证书中提出：除完成今年国家计划以外，要多为国家生产一万吨优质钢和七千吨钢轨、钢板等钢压延品，生产总值比国家计划增加五百万元，产品成本比一九五四年实际成本降低三百万元，上缴利润比国家计划增加三百九十五万元，还保证试制成功六种新产品。据统计，大会共接到全市钢铁、机械、煤炭、电力、纺织、铁路运输、内河航运、地方工业厂矿和各个企业职工响应大会的保证书共一百五十七件。
