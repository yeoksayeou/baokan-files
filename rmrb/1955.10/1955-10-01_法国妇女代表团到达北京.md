Title: French Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. A French women's delegation of ten people, led by Madame Marcelle Raguet, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 30th at the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Li Dequan and Xu Guangping, vice-chairpersons of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and many women from all walks of life in the capital.

### 法国妇女代表团到达北京

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    法国妇女代表团到达北京
    新华社三十日讯　应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请，前来中国访问并参加国庆节观礼的法国妇女代表团一行十人，由团长玛赛尔·马盖夫人率领，于三十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、许广平及首都各界妇女等多人。
