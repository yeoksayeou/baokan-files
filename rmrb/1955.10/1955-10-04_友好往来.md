Friendly Exchanges

    Tunisian Friendly Delegation to China Returns Home
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Tunisian Friendly Delegation to China, consisting of ten people led by Aleya, the head of the delegation, Standing Committee Member of the Tunisian Peace Committee, and President of the Tunisia-China Association, left Beijing for home on the 3rd.
    The Tunisian Friendly Delegation to China stayed in China for more than a month and visited Changchun, Shenyang, Shanghai, Tianjin, and other places. They also spent China's National Day in Beijing. During their visit, they had contact with people from Chinese economic, cultural, legal, political, religious, and trade union circles. Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, received them.
    Before leaving Beijing, Aleya, the head of the delegation, said: The delegates were deeply impressed by this visit to China. They personally witnessed the thriving construction in all aspects of China and the high degree of enthusiasm and confidence shown by the peace-loving Chinese people in the construction. In particular, China's freedom of religious belief and policies towards ethnic minorities are completely correct. They believe that the Chinese people's fraternal friendship and support for the Tunisian and North African people is a major encouragement to their ongoing struggle to defend peace and strive for national independence. Aleya said that through this visit of the delegation, the friendship between the Chinese and Tunisian people will be further developed.

    Italian Women's Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Italian Women's Delegation returned home by plane on the morning of the 2nd after visiting China for a month and attending the National Day celebrations.
    The delegation visited Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuhan, Guangzhou, and other places, and had extensive contact with Chinese women.
    After returning to Beijing from visits to various places, Premier Zhou Enlai and Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, separately received the delegation, and Peng Zhen hosted a banquet for them.
    Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Xia Yan, Deputy Minister of Culture, Xing Xiping, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, and Yu Yifu also separately received the delegation.
    The Beijing Democratic Women's Federation held a reception for them on September 28.
    In the past week, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a forum for the delegation on issues such as China's First Five-Year Plan for National Economic Development and women's work.

    Yugoslav Deaf Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The delegation of the Yugoslav Deaf Association, invited by the preparatory committee of the Chinese Welfare Association for the Deaf and Mute to visit China, arrived in Beijing on October 2nd. The delegation is led by Te. Vukotic, Chairman of the Yugoslav Deaf Association, and its members include Jo. Odavic, Secretary-General of the Yugoslav Deaf Association, Jo. Komericiki, Chairman of the Croatian Deaf Association, and Professor Jo. Kostic, the interpreter.
    When the delegation arrived, Wu Yansheng and Ma Zhiyuan, deputy directors of the preparatory committee of the Chinese Welfare Association for the Deaf and Mute, Huang Nai, Chen Chi, Hong Xueli, Zhang Qingsong, Li Shihan, and teachers and students of the Beijing School for the Deaf and Mute, and others went to the airport to welcome them.
    Gasparic, Secretary of the Yugoslav Embassy in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 友好往来

1955-10-04
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    突尼斯访华友好代表团回国
    新华社三日讯　突尼斯访华友好代表团十人，在团长、突尼斯和平委员会常务委员、突尼斯—中国协会主席阿莱亚率领下，三日离京回国。
    突尼斯访华友好代表团在中国逗留了一个多月，曾到长春、沈阳、上海、天津等地参观游览，并在北京度过了中国的国庆。他们在访问期间，曾同中国经济、文化、法政、宗教和工会各界人士接触。国务院陈毅副总理曾接见他们。
    代表团团长阿莱亚在离京前说：代表团各位代表对这次访华留下了深刻的印象，他们亲眼看见了中国各方面的欣欣向荣的建设和爱好和平的中国人民在建设中所表现的高度热情和信心，特别是中国的宗教信仰自由和对少数民族的政策，是完全正确的。他们认为，中国人民对突尼斯及北非人民的兄弟友谊和支持，是对他们正在进行的保卫和平和争取民族独立的斗争的重大鼓舞。阿莱亚说：通过这次代表团的访问，中国和突尼斯人民之间的友谊，将得到进一步的发展。
    意大利妇女代表团离京返国
    新华社二日讯　意大利妇女代表团在中国参观访问了一个月，并参加了国庆节观礼以后，二日上午乘飞机返国。
    代表团先后参观了沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、上海、杭州、武汉、广州等地，并和中国妇女界作了广泛的接触。
    代表团在各地参观回到北京后，周恩来总理和全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真曾分别接见，彭真并设宴招待她们。
    中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长雷任民、文化部副部长夏衍和中共中央统一战线工作部副部长邢西萍、于毅夫也分别接见了代表团。
    九月二十八日北京市民主妇女联合会曾举行酒会招待她们。
    在过去一个星期中，中华全国民主妇女联合会曾为代表团举行了关于我国国民经济第一个五年计划及妇女工作等问题的座谈会。
    南聋人协会代表团到达北京
    新华社二日讯　应中国聋哑人福利会筹备委员会邀请来中国访问的南斯拉夫聋人协会代表团在十月二日到达北京。代表团由南斯拉夫聋人协会主席特·伏科第奇率领，团员有南斯拉夫聋人协会秘书长约·奥达维奇、克罗提亚聋人协会主席约·科梅里奇基、翻译乔·科斯第奇教授。
    代表团到达时，中国聋哑人福利会筹备委员会副主任委员吴燕生、马志远，委员黄乃、陈驰、洪雪立、张庆松、李石涵以及北京市聋哑学校师生等多人前往机场欢迎。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使馆秘书加斯铂利也到机场欢迎。
