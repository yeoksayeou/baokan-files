Title: Capital Youth Visit Comrade Ren Bishi Memorial Hall
Author:
Date: 1955-10-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 26th: The 27th is the fifth anniversary of the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and honorary Chairman the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League. Hu Yaobang, Luo Yi, and other secretaries of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, along with more than 200 youth representatives from the capital, visited the Comrade Ren Bishi Memorial Hall on the afternoon of the 26th.

The memorial hall includes a bedroom, office, living room, and auditorium. In the living room, there is a plaster bust of Ren Bishi, and inscriptions commemorating Ren Bishi by Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De. In the rooms surrounding the living room, there are photos and assignments of Ren Bishi from his childhood to his university years, as well as writings and photos from Ren Bishi's revolutionary activities during various revolutionary periods, and many commemorative items.

At 2 PM, the youths stood in silent tribute before Ren Bishi's portrait in the memorial hall's auditorium, and then listened to a speech by Hu Yaobang (see page 3 of tomorrow's newspaper). In his speech, he called on Chinese youth to learn from and inherit the valuable legacy left by Ren Bishi and countless revolutionary martyrs. He said that we should learn from Comrade Ren Bishi's spirit of serving the people wholeheartedly, adhering to principles, and diligently studying, and inherit all the great legacies of Comrade Ren Bishi and all revolutionary martyrs, and strive to fulfill the expectations of the Party and the country for us.

After the speech, representatives of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and many Beijing youths presented flowers to Ren Bishi's plaster bust. Then, with infinite respect, the youths visited the memorial hall and also watched a film about mourning Ren Bishi's death and introducing some of Ren Bishi's life stories in the hall.

### 首都青年瞻仰任弼时同志纪念馆

1955-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    首都青年瞻仰任弼时同志纪念馆
    新华社二十六日讯　二十七日是中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会名誉主席任弼时逝世五周年纪念日。中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记胡耀邦、罗毅等，以及首都二百多名青年代表在二十六日下午瞻仰了任弼时同志纪念馆。
    纪念馆中有寝室、办公室、客厅和礼堂等。客厅中有任弼时的半身石膏像，有毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德的纪念任弼时的亲笔题词。在客厅周围的一些房间里，陈放着任弼时从少年时代到大学时期的照片和作业，还有任弼时在参加革命后历次革命时期写的著作和照片，以及许多纪念物品。
    二时，青年们在纪念馆礼堂内的任弼时遗像前默哀后，听了胡耀邦的讲话（见明天本报第三版）。他在讲话中，号召中国青年要学习和继承任弼时和无数革命先烈留下来的宝贵遗产。他说，我们应该学习任弼时同志全心全意为人民服务、坚持原则和孜孜不倦的学习精神，把任弼时同志和一切革命先烈们的伟大遗产全部继承下来，努力实现党和国家对我们的期待。
    讲话后，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的代表和许多北京市青年纷纷向任弼时石膏像献花。接着，青年们怀着无限崇敬的心情，瞻仰了纪念馆，还在馆内看了关于悼念任弼时逝世和介绍任弼时一些生平事迹的电影。
