Title: China Sends Seven Trade Delegations to Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Recently, the Chinese government dispatched seven trade delegations to the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Romania, and Bulgaria respectively, to conduct negotiations with the governments of these countries on trade agreements for 1956.

### 我国派出七个贸易代表团去各兄弟国家

1955-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我国派出七个贸易代表团去各兄弟国家
    新华社十五日讯　我国政府最近期间派出了七个贸易代表团，分别去苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、罗马尼亚和保加利亚，同这些国家的政府进行一九五六年度贸易协定的谈判。
