Title: China and Romania Sign Protocol on Scientific and Technological Cooperation
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-29
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 27th: Bucharest dispatch: The third session of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation was recently held in Bucharest. After sincere and friendly negotiations, both parties signed a protocol.

　　The protocol stipulates that the Romanian People's Republic will assist the People's Republic of China by providing technical documents related to the petroleum industry, textile industry, cellulose industry, food industry, and various health issues, and will receive Chinese technicians to specifically study crude oil refining issues. The People's Republic of China will assist the Romanian People's Republic by providing technical documents related to the consumer goods industry, health and welfare, construction, and building issues; at the same time, it will receive Romanian experts to study China's experience in manufacturing cellulose and paper from reeds, and to understand the state of China's health undertakings.

### 中罗两国签订科学技术合作的议定书

1955-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗两国签订科学技术合作的议定书
    新华社布拉格二十七日电　布加勒斯特消息：中罗科学和技术合作联合委员会第三届会议最近在布加勒斯特举行。会议经过双方真诚和友好的谈判后，签订了一项议定书。
    议定书规定，罗马尼亚人民共和国将通过提供有关石油工业、纺织工业、纤维素工业、食品工业以及各种保健问题的技术文件的办法援助中华人民共和国，并且将接待中国的技术人员来专门研究原油提炼的问题。中华人民共和国将通过提供有关消费品工业、卫生福利、建设和建筑问题的技术文件来援助罗马尼亚人民共和国；同时将接待罗马尼亚专家研究中国从芦苇制造纤维素和纸的经验，并了解中国卫生事业的情况。
