Title: Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission and Others Host Overseas Chinese Visiting Groups

Author:
Date: 1955-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the China-Indonesia Friendship Association jointly held a banquet on the evening of the 3rd to entertain overseas Chinese visiting groups from Indonesia, Burma, India, Korea, Vietnam, etc., Indonesian-Chinese visiting groups, and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots' National Day observation groups who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations.

Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, He Xiangning, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, Liao Chengzhi, Fang Fang, and Zhuang Xiquan, Vice Chairmen, Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Xu Dixin, Vice Chairman, Yang Hansheng, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and Hu Yuzhi and Ma Yuhuai, Vice Presidents of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, attended the banquet.

At the beginning of the banquet, Vice Premier Chen Yi delivered a speech, conveying congratulations from Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai to everyone. He said: Overseas Chinese have made great contributions to the motherland in the past, and it is hoped that they will make greater contributions to the socialist construction of the motherland in the future. Finally, he invited everyone to raise their glasses and toast to the prosperity of the motherland, the health of Chairman Mao, and the development of the patriotic cause of overseas Chinese unity.

The banquet proceeded in a cheerful and intimate atmosphere throughout.

### 华侨事务委员会等招待华侨回国观光团体

1955-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    华侨事务委员会等招待华侨回国观光团体
    新华社四日讯　中华人民共和国华侨事务委员会、中华全国工商业联合会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中国印度尼西亚友好协会在三日晚联合举行宴会，招待来京参加国庆典礼的印度尼西亚、缅甸、印度、朝鲜、越南等地的华侨回国观光团，印度尼西亚籍华裔观光团和港澳同胞国庆观礼团。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅，华侨事务委员会主任何香凝，副主任廖承志、方方、庄希泉，中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通、副主任委员许涤新，中国文学艺术界联合会秘书长阳翰笙，中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之、马玉槐都出席了宴会。
    宴会开始时，陈毅副总理致词，代表毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理向大家祝贺。他说：国外华侨在过去对祖国有很大的贡献，希望今后在祖国社会主义建设中有更大的贡献。他最后请大家举杯，为祖国的强盛，为毛主席的健康，为国外华侨团结爱国事业的发展干杯。
    宴会始终在欢愉亲密的空气中进行。
