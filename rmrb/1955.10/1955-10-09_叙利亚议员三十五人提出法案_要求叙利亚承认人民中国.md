Title: Thirty-five Syrian Parliamentarians Submit Bill Requesting Syria Recognize People's China
Author:
Date: 1955-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 7th. According to a France-Presse dispatch from Damascus on the 6th, thirty-five Syrian parliamentarians have jointly submitted a bill to the parliament requesting that Syria recognize the People's Republic of China.

At the same time, nineteen parliamentarians have jointly submitted a motion requesting that the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union be invited to send a delegation to visit Syria.

### 叙利亚议员三十五人提出法案  要求叙利亚承认人民中国

1955-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚议员三十五人提出法案
    要求叙利亚承认人民中国
    新华社七日讯　据法新社六日从大马士革发出的消息，有三十五位叙利亚议会议员已经联名向议会提出一项法案，要求叙利亚承认中华人民共和国。
    同时，还有十九位议员联名提出一个动议，要求邀请苏联最高苏维埃派出一个代表团访问叙利亚。
