Title: Thai Minister of Agriculture Receives U.S. Agriculture Officials, Demands U.S. Cease Rice Dumping Policy
Author: 
Date: 1955-10-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, 11th. According to Bangkok newspapers, the Thai Minister of Agriculture, Nai Pote Sarasin, when receiving the Director of the Foreign Agricultural Service of the U.S. Department of Agriculture, Garnett, and the U.S. International Cooperation Administration official, Robert, on the 3rd, demanded that the U.S. cease its policy of dumping rice on the Asian market.
    The report said that Nai Pote expressed concern about the U.S. rice dumping policy. He pointed out that the export of U.S.-produced rice was causing a serious crisis for rice-producing countries in Asia. He hoped that the U.S. would stop this policy to avoid impacting the sales of Thai rice.

### 泰国农业部长接见美国农业官员  要求美国停止大米倾销政策

1955-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    泰国农业部长接见美国农业官员
    要求美国停止大米倾销政策
    新华社十一日讯　曼谷消息：据曼谷报纸报道，泰国农业部长乃屏·春哈旺在三日接见美国农业部对外农业司司长加纳特和美国国际合作署官员罗贝尔时，要求美国停止以大米在亚洲市场倾销的政策。
    消息说，乃屏对美国的倾销大米政策表示忧虑。他指出，美国出产的大米向外倾销，使亚洲的产米国家发生了严重的危机。他希望美国停止这种政策，以免打击泰国大米的销路。
