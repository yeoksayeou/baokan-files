Author:
Date: 1955-10-20

    Our Great Motherland
    This year, rural areas across the country have generally had bumper harvests, and the purchasing power of farmers has increased. The Shanghai Purchasing and Supply Station of the China General Merchandise Company has prepared a large number of industrial products suitable for farmers and transported them to rural areas across the country to meet the needs of farmers. This is the scene of ships loaded with daily necessities leaving the Shanghai pier.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今年各地农村普遍丰收，农民购买力提高了。中国百货公司上海采购供应站准备了大批农民适用的工业品，运往各地农村，供应农民需要。这是满载日用品的船只离开上海码头的情形。
