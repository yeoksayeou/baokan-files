Title: Chinese Envoys to Various Countries Hold Receptions to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-10-04
Page: 4

Chinese envoys to various countries held receptions to celebrate National Day.

On the evening of October 1, Chinese Ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan held a reception in Warsaw. Invited guests included Polish People's Republic Premier Cyrankiewicz, Deputy Premiers Marshal Rokossovsky, Berman, Jóźwiak, Gede, Indrykowski, Polish People's Republic Parliament Speaker Dąbrowski, Minister of Foreign Affairs Skrzeszewski, Minister of Foreign Trade Dąbrowski, Chief of the General Staff and Deputy Minister of National Defense General Bożywolewski, Director of the General Political Department of the Polish People's Army Major General Witaszewski, and other figures from all walks of life, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Poland.

Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Cao Ying and his wife held a reception in Prague on the evening of October 1. Invited to the reception were representatives of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and government, including Ďuriš, Barák, and Kopecký, Secretaries of the CPCz Central Committee Hendrych and Pašek, Deputy Prime Minister Škoďa, and other figures from all walks of life. Chairman of the Slovak Administrative Committee Strechaj, Vice-Chairman Bakula, and Šebesta, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Slovak Communist Party Križ, and the Mayor of Bratislava also came from afar to attend the reception. Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries, as well as representatives of international organizations stationed in Prague and journalists from various countries, also attended the reception. The delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of China to visit the Czechoslovak Machinery Industry Exhibition, headed by Minister Ye Jizhuang, also attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the German Democratic Republic Zeng Yongquan held a reception in the Berlin Ministry Building on the evening of October 1. Among those present at the reception were President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic, Premier Grotewohl, Deputy Premiers Ulbricht, Nuschke, Rau, Loch, Schulz, ministers of various ministries, members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, representatives of people's organizations, and well-known figures from all walks of life; as well as diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, and North Korea to Germany, and the Finnish Commercial Representative to Germany. Also invited to attend were the Acting Head of the Yugoslav Military Mission to Germany, Dimitrijevic, and the Consul of Indonesia in West Berlin, Majuni, who were attending the Chinese National Day reception for the first time. Professor Brandweiner, Chairman of the Austrian Peace Council and delegate to the Fourth Congress of the World Federation of Scientific Workers Associations, was also invited to attend the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to the Romanian People's Republic Ke Bonian held a reception in Bucharest on October 1. Invited to attend the reception were Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly Dr. Petru Groza, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, First Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers Ioan Gh. Maurer, Gheorghe Stoica, Alexandru Moghioroș, member of the Political Bureau of the Workers' Party, Minister of Armed Forces General Emil Bodnăraș and other figures from all walks of life. Diplomatic envoys from various People's Democratic countries and some other countries to Romania were also invited to attend the reception. Also invited to attend the reception were Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic Johannes Dieckmann, Japanese Diet members Haruji Tahara, Masamichi Onishi, and Keita Hoashi, Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions Luigi Grassi, and delegates from various countries attending the first meeting of the Preparatory Committee of the International Conference of Women Workers, who were visiting Romania.

Chinese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Hungary Hao Deqing held a reception in Budapest on the evening of October 1. Among those present at the reception were Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic Dobi, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Hegedüs, Party and government leaders Ács, Gerő, Hideg, Kádár, Mező, Szalai, Bata, Piros, Minister of Foreign Affairs Boldoczki and others. Also present were diplomatic envoys to Hungary from the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, North Korea, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, Yugoslavia, the United Kingdom, Switzerland, the Netherlands, Israel, and Sweden, as well as the Secretary of the Egyptian Embassy in Hungary. The Burmese delegation, led by Minister of Public Works and Reconstruction Ba Swe, who is currently visiting Hungary, was also invited to attend the reception. A total of about 500 people attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria Zhou Zhuan held a reception in Sofia on the evening of October 1. Among those present were Bulgarian government and party leaders Chervenkov, Yugov, Tsankov, Zhivkov, General Mikhailov, Terlekov, Prahov, Panchevsky and others. Ambassadors and ministers of all countries with diplomatic relations with China to Bulgaria were also invited.

Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania Wang Dong held a reception on the evening of October 1. Among those present at the reception were First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Mehmet Shehu, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Spiro Koleka, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Chairman of the State Planning Commission Skender Koleka, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Labor Party Rita Marko, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Maqo Muftiu, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly Haxhi Lleshi and others. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Albania also attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen and his wife held a reception at the embassy on the evening of October 1. Among the 600 distinguished guests who attended the reception were: Indonesian Prime Minister Harahap, Second Deputy Prime Minister Hasono and Minister of Education and Culture, Minister of Agriculture, Minister of Health, Minister of Public Works, Minister of Transportation, as well as Speaker of Parliament Sadono, Mayor of Jakarta Sudiro, and Chief of Naval Staff, Chief of Air Staff, senior army officers, etc.; there were also many well-known figures in political and cultural circles, including former Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo, former First Deputy Prime Minister Wongsonegoro, and former Minister of Foreign Affairs, former Minister of Defense, former Minister of Finance, and former Minister of Education and Culture, as well as Chairman of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association Professor Priyono, President of the University of Indonesia Bahder Djohan, Masjumi Party leader Romlom and Gasman Singodimizo, and General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party Aidit. Among the diplomatic missions, the ambassadors of the Soviet Union, India, Pakistan, Egypt, and the United Kingdom, the chargés d'affaires ad interim of Burma, Saudi Arabia, Switzerland, and Yugoslavia, the Deputy High Commissioner of the Netherlands, and the ministers of Afghanistan and Sweden, the trade representatives of Poland, Romania, Hungary, and the German Democratic Republic attended the reception. Representatives of overseas Chinese and many representatives of the Indonesian press and foreign journalists stationed in Jakarta also attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming and his wife held a reception on the afternoon of October 1. Among the more than 600 guests attending the reception were: Burmese Federal Prime Minister U Nu, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Hkun Hkio, Minister of Foreign Affairs Sao Khun Kyi, Minister of Land Nationalization Tekin Tin, Minister of Justice U Khin Maung Lat, Minister of Relief U San Myaing, Minister of National Unity Duwa Zau Lawn, Acting Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Myint Thein, and other senior Burmese government officials; as well as other figures from all walks of life and representatives of overseas Chinese. Envoys and diplomatic officials from the Soviet Union, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Czechoslovakia, Yugoslavia, India, Indonesia, Pakistan, Israel, the Netherlands, the United Kingdom, and Ceylon to Burma also attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Han Nianlong held a reception on the evening of October 1. Invited to attend the reception were Pakistani Prime Minister Chaudhry Muhammad Ali, senior Pakistani government officials, members of the Pakistani Constituent Assembly, social figures, and figures from cultural, scientific, and business circles. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Pakistan and personnel from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, and other countries participating in the Pakistani International Industrial Exposition also attended the reception. Responsible personnel of the Chinese exhibition group participating in the exposition and representatives of overseas Chinese also attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Ding Guoyu held a reception on the evening of the 1st. Among those invited to the reception were Afghan Prime Minister Sardar Mohammed Daoud Khan, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammed Naim Khan, former Prime Minister Sardar Shah Mahmud Khan, Court Minister Ahmed Ali, cabinet ministers and senior officials, Chairman of the National Assembly Mohammed Nuruze, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Afghanistan, totaling about 100 people.

Chinese Ambassador to Finland Chen Xinren held a reception in Helsinki on October 1. Among those present at the reception were Finnish Prime Minister Kekkonen, Minister of Foreign Affairs Virolainen, Minister of Justice Henriksson, Second Minister of Social Affairs Mrs. Leivo-Larsson, Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces Heiskanen, Secretary of State Seppälä, Deputy Speaker of the Finnish Parliament Kuijala, prominent member of Parliament Kilpi, General Secretary of the Finnish Communist Party Pesy, Chairman of the Finnish Communist Party Aaltonen, Chairman of the Finnish Agrarian League Sukselainen, and other figures from all walks of life. Also present were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to Finland, as well as the Commercial Representative of the German Democratic Republic.

Chinese Ambassador to Sweden Geng Biao held a reception on the 1st. Among those present at the reception were Secretary-General of the Swedish Ministry of Foreign Affairs A. Lundberg, Under-Secretary of the Ministry of the Interior S. A. Eijestan, Under-Secretary of the Ministry of Social Affairs Ekberg, and officials from other government agencies, as well as people from all walks of life in Sweden and diplomatic personnel from various countries to Sweden, totaling more than 240 people.

Chinese Minister to Switzerland Feng Xuan held a reception in Bern on the 1st. Among those present at the reception were Vice-Chairman of the Swiss Federal Council Feldmann, Secretary-General of the Political Department Zenter, Inspector General of Training of the Army Corps Koppold, and other government officials, as well as people from all walks of life in Switzerland, totaling about 300 people. Envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries with diplomatic relations with China to Switzerland, as well as envoys and diplomatic personnel from Israel, Syria, Egypt, Uruguay, and other countries, also attended the reception.

To celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, Chinese Minister to Denmark Chai Chengwen held a reception on September 30. High-ranking officials such as the Speaker of the Danish Parliament, the Minister of Religious Affairs, the Minister of Agriculture, the Minister of Commerce, the Minister of Public Works, and the Commander of the Air Force, as well as well-known social figures and diplomatic personnel from various countries to Denmark, totaling 250 people, attended the reception.

(Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 我国驻各国使节举行招待会庆祝国庆

1955-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我国驻各国使节举行招待会庆祝国庆
    十月一日晚上，中国驻波兰大使王炳南在华沙举行招待会。应邀到会的有波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，部长会议副主席罗科索夫斯基元帅、贝尔曼、约兹维亚克、盖德、英德里霍夫斯基，波兰人民共和国议会议长邓博夫斯基，外交部长斯克热歇夫斯基，对外贸易部长董布罗夫斯基，总参谋长兼国防部副部长博济沃夫斯基上将，波兰人民军总政治部主任维塔谢夫斯基少将以及其他各界人士，还有各国驻波兰使节。
    中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛和夫人，十月一日晚在布拉格举行招待会。应邀出席招待会的有捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府的代表杜兰斯基、巴拉克和柯别茨基，捷共中央书记处书记亨德里赫和帕歇克，共和国副总理什科达以及其他各界人士。斯洛伐克行政委员会主席斯特雷哈伊、副主席巴库拉和谢别斯塔，斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会书记克里日和布拉迪斯拉发市市长也远道前来出席招待会。出席招待会的还有各国外交使节和外交官员，以及驻布拉格的各个国际组织的代表和各国新闻记者。以叶季壮部长为首的中华人民共和国政府参观捷克斯洛伐克机械工业展览会代表团也出席了招待会。
    中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使曾涌泉十月一日晚间在柏林部长会议大厦举行招待会。出席招待会的有德意志民主共和国总统皮克，总理格罗提渥，副总理乌布利希、努舍克、劳、洛赫、舒尔茨，各部部长，德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员，人民团体代表及各界著名人士；还有苏联和波、捷、匈、罗、保、阿、朝各国驻德外交使节以及芬兰驻德商务代办等。应邀出席的还有首次参加中国国庆招待会的南斯拉夫驻德军事使团代理团长季米特里耶维奇、印度尼西亚驻西柏林领事马久尼等。出席世界科学工作者协会第四届代表大会的代表、奥地利和平理事会主席布兰德魏纳教授也应邀出席了招待会。
    中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使柯柏年十月一日在布加勒斯特举行招待会。应邀出席招待会的有罗马尼亚部长会议主席、罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记格·乔治乌—德治，国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查博士，工人党中央委员会政治局委员、部长会议第一副主席约·基辛湼夫斯基、基·斯托伊卡、亚·莫吉奥罗希，工人党中央政治局委员、武装部队部部长埃·波德纳拉希大将以及其他各界人士。应邀出席招待会的还有各人民民主国家及其他一些国家驻罗马尼亚的外交使节。应邀出席招待会的还有正在罗马尼亚访问的德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席约·狄克曼、日本国会议员田原春次、大西正道和帆足计，世界工会联合会书记路易吉·葛拉西和正在罗马尼亚参加国际女工会议筹备委员会第一次会议的各国代表。
    中国驻匈牙利特命全权大使郝德青十月一日晚在布达佩斯举行招待会。出席招待会的有匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比，部长会议主席赫格居斯，党和政府负责人阿奇、格罗、希达什、科瓦奇、麦基什、萨拉伊、巴塔、彼罗什，外交部长波尔多奇基等人。出席招待会的还有苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、朝鲜、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、南斯拉夫、英国、瑞士、荷兰、以色列、瑞典等国驻匈牙利的外交使节和埃及驻匈牙利使馆的秘书。目前正在匈牙利访问的、由公共工程和复建部部长波敏贡率领的缅甸代表团也应邀参加了招待会。出席招待会的共约五百人。
    中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安十月一日晚在索非亚举行招待会。出席招待会的有保加利亚政府和党的领导人契尔文科夫、于哥夫、詹科夫、日夫科夫、米哈伊洛夫大将、特莱科夫、普拉霍夫、潘切夫斯基等人。应邀出席的还有同中国有外交关系的各国驻保加利亚的大使和公使。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王栋十月一日晚举行招待会。出席招待会的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩·霍查，部长会议主席穆·谢胡，部长会议第一副主席希·卡博，部长会议副主席兼国家计划委员会主席斯·科列加，劳动党中央委员会书记里·马尔科，部长会议副主席马·穆夫蒂乌，人民议会主席团主席哈·列希等人。出席招待会的还有各国驻阿外交使节。
    中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇及夫人十月一日晚在大使馆举行招待会。在参加招待会的六百位贵宾中有：印度尼西亚总理哈拉哈普，第二副总理哈索诺和文教部长，农业部长，卫生部长，公共工程部长，交通部长，以及国会议长沙多诺，雅加达市市长苏迪罗，和海军参谋长，空军参谋长，高级陆军军官等；还有许多政界和文化界著名人士，其中包括前总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约，前第一副总理旺梭尼哥罗，和前外交部长，前国防部长，前财政部长和前文教部长，以及印度尼西亚—中国友好协会主席普里约诺教授，印度尼西亚大学校长巴赫德尔·约翰，马斯友美党领袖隆姆和加斯曼·辛戈迪米佐，印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地。外交使团中参加招待会的有苏联、印度、巴基斯坦、埃及和英国的大使们，缅甸、沙特阿拉伯、瑞士和南斯拉夫的临时代办们，荷兰的副高级专员，阿富汗和瑞典的公使们，波兰、罗马尼亚、匈牙利和德意志民主共和国的贸易代表们。参加招待会的还有华侨代表和许多印度尼西亚报界代表及驻雅加达的外国新闻记者们。
    中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和夫人十月一日下午举行招待会。参加招待会的六百多名来宾中有：缅甸联邦总理吴努、民族院议长萧恢塔、外交部长藻昆卓、土地国有化部长德钦阵、司法部长吴钦貌腊、救济部长吴申坦、民族团结部长杜瓦藻朗、代理最高法院首席法官吴敏登和其他缅甸政府高级官员；以及其他各界人士和华侨代表。苏联、越南民主共和国、捷克斯洛伐克、南斯拉夫、印度、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦、以色列、荷兰、英国和锡兰等国驻缅甸的使节和外交官员也参加了招待会。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙十月一日晚举行招待会。应邀出席招待会的有巴基斯坦总理乔德里·穆罕默德·阿里，巴基斯坦政府高级官员，巴基斯坦制宪议会议员，社会名流，文化界、科学界、工商界人士。出席招待会的还有各国驻巴基斯坦外交使节以及苏联、捷克斯洛伐克等国参加巴基斯坦国际工业博览会的展览团人员。中国参加博览会的展览团负责人员和华侨代表也出席了招待会。
    中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰在一日晚间举行招待会。应邀出席招待会的有阿富汗首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德汗，副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗，前首相萨达尔·沙阿·马茂德汗，宫廷大臣艾哈迈德·阿里，内阁大臣和高级官员们，国民议会主席穆罕默德·努鲁塞，以及各国驻阿富汗外交使节等约一百人。

    为庆祝中华人民共和国建国六周年，中国驻丹麦公使柴成文在九月三十日举行招待会。丹麦议会议长、宗教事务大臣、农业大臣、商务大臣、公共工程大臣、空军司令等高级官员，社会知名人士和各国驻丹麦外交人员等二百五十人参加了招待会。
