Title: Qian Junrui Hosts Banquet for Bulgarian Artists Delegation and German Wind Orchestra
Author:
Date: 1955-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th. Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, held a banquet at noon on the 7th to welcome the Bulgarian Artists Delegation headed by Mi. Popov and the three playwrights, Pi. Dimitrov, S. Serkakiyev, and Liu. Deteniev; At the same time, he bid farewell to Bulgarian poet Va. Petrov, painter S. Vinev, and classical artist Va. Chahariiev, who were about to return home.

Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison, and representatives from literary and art circles Ouyang Yuqian, Ma Sicong, Tian Han, Liu Kaiqu, Chen Qitong, and Chen Jinqing attended the banquet.

Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Dimov, also attended the banquet.

At the banquet, Qian Junrui, Dimitrov, Petrov, and Vinev all spoke, feeling that the dispatch of the delegation further strengthened the friendship and cultural exchange between China and Bulgaria. They also wished that the friendship between China and Bulgaria would be increasingly consolidated.

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th. Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet at noon on the 8th to welcome the Wind Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic headed by Paul Kutzbach. Mr. Grutner, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Ma Yanxiang, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Art Affairs, Chen Yi, Minister of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Lu Ming, representative of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison, Wang Kunlun, Vice Mayor of Beijing, Ma Sicong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, Xu Guangping, head of the Chinese Yue Opera Troupe who recently returned from a visit to the German Democratic Republic, and more than 40 people from Beijing's literary and art circles attended the banquet.

At the banquet, Qian Junrui delivered a speech welcoming the Wind Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic and wished them success in their visit and performances in China. Kutzbach delivered a speech in reply.

Grutner also delivered a speech at the meeting, congratulating the increasing development of cultural exchanges between China and Germany.

### 钱俊瑞欢宴保艺术家代表团和德吹奏乐团

1955-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    钱俊瑞欢宴保艺术家代表团和德吹奏乐团
    新华社八日讯　中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞在七日中午举行宴会，欢迎以米·波波夫为首的保加利亚艺术家代表团和三位戏剧家彼·季米特洛夫、斯·瑟尔卡基耶夫、柳·德泰涅夫；同时欢送即将返国的保加利亚诗人瓦·彼得罗夫、画家斯·维涅夫和古典艺术家瓦·查哈里耶夫。
    出席作陪的，有中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙、对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经以及文艺界代表欧阳予倩、马思聪、田汉、刘开渠、陈其通、陈锦清等。
    保加利亚驻华大使迪莫夫也参加了宴会。
    在宴会上，钱俊瑞、季米特洛夫、彼得罗夫和维涅夫都讲了话，都觉得通过代表团的派遣，使中保两国之间的友好和文化交流得到了进一步的加强。他们并祝中保两国的友好日益巩固。
    新华社八日讯　中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，八日中午设宴欢迎以保尔·库茨巴赫为首的德意志民主共和国吹奏乐团。德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办葛鲁特纳也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的，有文化部副部长夏衍、艺术事业管理局副局长马彦祥，中国人民解放军总政治部文化部部长陈沂，对外文化联络局代表鲁明，北京市副市长王昆仑，中国音乐家协会副主席马思聪，最近访问德意志民主共和国归来的中国越剧团团长许广平，以及北京文艺界人士四十多人。
    在宴会上，钱俊瑞致词欢迎德意志民主共和国吹奏乐团，并祝他们在中国的访问演出成功。库茨巴赫致词答谢。
    葛鲁特纳也在会上致词，祝贺中德两国文化交流的日益发展。
