    Our Great Motherland
    The high-yield crop corn planted in Xiqin Village, Shuangcheng County, Heilongjiang Province, has had a bumper harvest, with an average yield of over 3,000 jin per hectare. This is the full corn newly harvested by the Shengren Agricultural Production Cooperative.

    我们伟大的祖国
    黑龙江省双城县希勤村种植的高产作物玉米获得丰收，平均每垧地可产三千多斤。这是胜壬农业生产合作社新收获的饱满的玉米。
