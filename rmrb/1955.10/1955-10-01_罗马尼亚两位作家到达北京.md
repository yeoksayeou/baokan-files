Title: Two Romanian Writers Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th. Romanian writers, Peter Dumitru, recipient of the State Medal and twice the State Prize, and Geo Bogza, winner of the State Prize, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 30th for a visit to China, according to the 1955 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 罗马尼亚两位作家到达北京

1955-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚两位作家到达北京
    新华社三十日讯　根据中罗文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来我国访问的罗马尼亚作家、国家勋章和两次国家奖金获得者彼得·杜米特鲁和国家奖金得奖者日奥·博格扎在三十日乘飞机到达北京。
