Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Approves Treaty on Relations Between the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1955-10-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th. TASS reports: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR decided on October 3rd to approve the Treaty on Relations between the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the German Democratic Republic, which was submitted for approval by the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团  批准苏联和德意志民主共和国关系条约

1955-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    批准苏联和德意志民主共和国关系条约
    新华社四日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团在十月三日决定批准苏联部长会议提请批准的苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和德意志民主共和国关系条约。
