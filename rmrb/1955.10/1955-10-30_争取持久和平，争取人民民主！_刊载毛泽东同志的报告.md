Title: "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" Publishes Comrade Mao Zedong's Report
Author:
Date: 1955-10-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 29th -- Bucharest Dispatch: The forty-third issue of "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" published in Bucharest on October 28th, 1955, carried the full text of Comrade Mao Zedong's report on the question of agricultural cooperation.

### “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”刊载毛泽东同志的报告

1955-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”刊载毛泽东同志的报告
    新华社布拉格二十九日电　布加勒斯特消息：十月二十八日在布加勒斯特出版的“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”一九五五年第四十三期全文刊载了毛泽东同志关于农业合作化问题的报告。
