Title: Lebanese and Iraqi Governments Support the Egyptian-Czech Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-10-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 11th - TASS reported from Cairo on the 10th: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser received Lebanese Foreign Minister Salim Lahoud, who was attending the Arab Foreign Ministers Conference in Cairo, on the 9th. Lahoud told reporters that he "expressed to the Egyptian Prime Minister, on behalf of the President, government, and people of Lebanon, support for all actions regarding the armament of the Egyptian army."

Lahoud then pointed out that the Lebanese government opposes participating in treaties concluded with foreign countries.

Lahoud said: "The only treaty Lebanon agrees to participate in is the Arab States treaty."

According to "Rose El Youssef" weekly, Iraqi Foreign Minister Bashayan, who is also currently in Cairo, expressed to the Egyptian government that the Iraqi government supports the agreement regarding Egypt's purchase of arms from Czechoslovakia.

### 黎巴嫩和伊拉克两国政府支持埃捷协定

1955-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩和伊拉克两国政府支持埃捷协定
    新华社十一日讯　塔斯社开罗十日讯：埃及总理纳赛尔在九日接见了参加在开罗举行的阿拉伯国家外长会议的黎巴嫩外交部长萨利姆·拉霍德。拉霍德向记者说，他“代表黎巴嫩的总统、政府和人民向埃及总理表示，支持关于埃及军队的军备方面的一切做法”。
    拉霍德接着指出，黎巴嫩政府反对参加同外国缔结的公约。
    拉霍德说：“黎巴嫩同意参加的唯一公约就是阿拉伯国家公约。”
    据“鲁兹·优素福”周刊报道，目前也在开罗的伊拉克外交大臣巴沙扬曾向埃及政府表示，伊拉克政府支持关于埃及向捷克斯洛伐克购买军火的协定。
