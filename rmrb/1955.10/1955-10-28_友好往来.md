    Friendly Exchanges

    Soviet TASS Delegation to China Leaves Beijing for Home

    The Soviet TASS delegation to China, a group of six led by delegation head Seliverstov, who came to our country at the invitation of the Xinhua News Agency to attend the National Day celebrations and visit our country, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 26th to return home.

    Among those seeing them off at the airport were Wu Lengxi, Director of the Xinhua News Agency, heads of various departments of the Xinhua News Agency headquarters, and representatives from the "People's Daily" and other units.

    Li Haiqiu, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, and the TASS and Pravda correspondents stationed in Beijing also saw them off at the airport.

    On the afternoon of the 25th, Wu Lengxi, Director of the Xinhua News Agency, hosted a farewell banquet for the delegation.

    On the evening of the same day, Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin held a reception for the delegation's visit to China. Heads of Beijing press circles attended the reception. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1955-10-28
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    苏联塔斯社访华代表团离京回国
    应新华通讯社邀请来我国参加国庆观礼和访问我国的苏联塔斯社访华代表团一行六人，在团长谢利维尔斯托夫率领下，于二十六日上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到飞机场欢送的，有新华通讯社社长吴冷西、新华通讯社总社各部门负责人和“人民日报”等单位的代表。
    苏联大使馆参赞李海秋和塔斯社、真理报驻北京记者也到机场欢送。
    二十五日中午，新华通讯社社长吴冷西设宴为代表团饯行。
    同日晚，苏联驻中国大使尤金为代表团访问中国举行了招待会。参加招待会的有北京新闻界的负责人。　（据新华社讯）
