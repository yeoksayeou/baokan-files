Title: Japanese Army Chief of Staff Goes to Washington to Discuss Expanding the Japanese Army Reserve Personnel
Author:
Date: 1955-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 12th -- A Washington dispatch: The Chief of Staff of the Japanese Ground Self-Defense Force (Army Chief of Staff), Takeo Tsutsui, arrived in Washington on the 10th and began talks on military issues with U.S. Army Chief of Staff Taylor and other senior officers.

According to Reuters, the talks focus heavily on the training and recruitment of Japanese army reserve personnel. U.S. officials are considering a plan to train Japanese reserve forces to replace the Japanese plan, which is considered inadequate.

After the talks, Tsutsui began a tour of U.S. military installations that will last approximately three weeks.

### 日本陆军参谋长去华盛顿  商谈扩充日军后备人员问题

1955-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    日本陆军参谋长去华盛顿
    商谈扩充日军后备人员问题
    新华社十二日讯　华盛顿消息：日本陆上幕僚长（陆军参谋长）筒井竹雄在十日到达华盛顿，并且开始同美国陆军参谋长泰勒等高级军官就军事问题进行会谈。
    据路透社的消息说，会谈非常着重日军后备人员的训练和招募问题。美国官员已经考虑了一个训练日本后备军的计划，以代替被认为不适当的日本计划。
    会谈后，筒井开始了他为期约三周的对美国军事设施的考察旅行。
