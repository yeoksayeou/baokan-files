Title: Another Step in the US's Armed Aggression and Occupation of Taiwan: Announcement of the Establishment of the "US Taiwan Defense Command"
Author:
Date: 1955-10-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: The commander of the US Seventh Fleet and commander of the so-called "Taiwan Liaison Center," Pride, announced on the 22nd that, starting November 1st, the US military's so-called "Taiwan Liaison Center" will be renamed "US Taiwan Defense Command."

The Associated Press reported that Pride's announcement indicated that "the United States has established an organization that will be immediately prepared to assume the duties of a combat command headquarters in the event of hostile actions in the Taiwan area." This is another step by the United States to strengthen its armed aggression and occupation of China's territory, Taiwan.

### 美国武力侵占台湾的又一步骤  宣布成立“美军协防台湾司令部”

1955-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    美国武力侵占台湾的又一步骤
    宣布成立“美军协防台湾司令部”
    新华社二十三日讯　美国第七舰队司令兼所谓“台湾联络中心”司令普赖德在二十二日宣布，从十一月一日起，美军的所谓“台湾联络中心”将改名为“美军协防台湾司令部”。
    美联社记者说，普赖德的宣布表明，“美国已经建立了一个一旦在台湾地区发生敌对行动时就将立即准备好担负起作战指挥总部任务的组织”。这是美国加强它用武力侵占中国领土台湾的又一步骤。
