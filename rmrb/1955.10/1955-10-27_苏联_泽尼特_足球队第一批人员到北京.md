Title: The First Group of Personnel from the Soviet "Zenit" Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The first group of ten members of the Soviet Leningrad "Zenit" Volunteer Sports Association football team, led by team leader V.S. Deromanov, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 26th at the invitation of China.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included representatives from the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Beijing Municipal Physical Culture and Sports Commission, as well as Chinese football players.

Personnel from the Soviet Embassy in China also came to the airport to welcome them.

### 苏联“泽尼特”足球队第一批人员到北京

1955-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联“泽尼特”足球队第一批人员到北京
    新华社二十六日讯　应邀来中国访问的苏联列宁格勒“泽尼特”志愿体育协会足球队第一批人员十人，在领队维·斯·杰洛曼诺夫率领下，二十六日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华人民共和国体育运动委员会、中苏友好协会和北京市体育运动委员会的代表，以及中国的足球运动员等。
    苏联驻中国大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
