Title: Soviet Educator Bafina Gives Report to Women in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1955-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a report meeting this morning, inviting Bafina, a member of the Soviet cultural delegation visiting China, Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, and a famous educator, to give a report to women from all walks of life in the capital. There were more than 1,500 listeners.

    Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, first delivered a welcoming speech. She said: Bafina is an internationally renowned fighter for defending world peace, children's happiness, and women's rights. For her outstanding work, the Soviet government awarded her the Order of the Red Banner of Labor. She is a shining example for Chinese women to learn from.

    In her report, Bafina introduced the great achievements and active role of Soviet women in socialist and communist construction, as well as their contributions to the struggle to defend world peace. She pointed out that the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people and women is a huge force for defending world peace because China and the Soviet Union have 800 million people, and the people of the two countries have the determination and tenacious spirit to defend peace.

    After Bafina finished her report, Deng Yingchao stepped forward and embraced her warmly. Then, representatives of children from Beijing presented her with flowers and put a red scarf on her.

    Furthermore, Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, held a banquet on the afternoon of the 4th to entertain Bafina, a member of the Soviet cultural delegation, Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, and Kosmodemyanskaya, the mother of the Soviet hero Zoya. Xu Guangping and Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Kang Keqing, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children, as well as representatives of women from all walks of life in the capital and female labor models, attended the banquet.

### 苏联教育家巴菲诺娃向首都妇女作报告

1955-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联教育家巴菲诺娃向首都妇女作报告
    新华社五日讯中华全国民主妇女联合会今天上午举行报告会，邀请应邀来中国访问的苏联文化代表团团员、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会副主席、著名的教育家巴菲诺娃向首都各界妇女作报告。听众有一千五百多人。
    全国民主妇联副主席邓颖超首先致欢迎词。她说：巴菲诺娃是国际知名的保卫世界和平、儿童幸福和妇女权利的战士，为了她卓越的劳动，苏联政府曾奖给她以劳动红旗勋章。她是中国妇女学习的光辉榜样。
    巴菲诺娃在她的报告里介绍了苏联妇女在社会主义和共产主义建设中的伟大成就和积极的作用，以及在保卫世界和平的斗争中所作的贡献。她指出，中苏两国人民和妇女的友谊是保卫世界和平的巨大力量。因为中苏两国有八亿人民，而且两国人民有决心和顽强的精神来保卫和平。
    巴菲诺娃报告完毕，邓颖超上前和她热烈拥抱。接着，北京市儿童代表向她献花，并给她戴上红领巾。
    又讯中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅在四日下午举行宴会招待苏联文化代表团团员、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会副主席巴菲诺娃和苏联英雄卓娅的母亲科斯莫杰米扬斯卡娅。出席作陪的有：全国民主妇联副主席许广平、章蕴，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长康克清以及首都各界妇女代表、女劳动模范多人。
