Title: The Soviet Union Presents Fine Horses to President Tito
Author:
Date: 1955-10-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, October 18th. TASS Belgrade, October 18th: According to a report from the Yugoslav News Agency, Yugoslav President Tito received the Soviet Ambassador to Yugoslavia, Firyubin, and the Soviet Trade Representative to Yugoslavia, Krasuk. They presented Tito with gifts selected from the exhibits at the Soviet Pavilion of the Novi Sad International Exhibition, including thoroughbred horses.

### 苏联赠良马给铁托总统

1955-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联赠良马给铁托总统
    新华社十八日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德十八日讯：据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫总统铁托接见了苏联驻南斯拉夫大使费留宾和苏联驻南斯拉夫商务代表克拉苏克。他们把从诺维萨特国际展览会苏联展览馆的展览品中挑选出来的礼物送给铁托，礼物中有良种马。
