Title: Pakistan Communist Leader Zahir and Others Released
Author:
Date: 1955-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 8th. Karachi Dispatch: Pakistan's new Governor-General Iskander Mirza, on the day of his inauguration on October 6th, ordered the release of Major General Akbar Khan, former Chief of Staff of the Pakistan Army, and Zahir, leader of the Pakistan Communist Party, and thirteen others. They were arrested in 1951 and were accused at the time of engaging in a so-called "conspiracy to overthrow the government by violence" at Rawalpindi, the location of the Pakistan Army Headquarters, and were sentenced in 1952 to imprisonment ranging from four to twelve years.

### 巴共领袖查希尔等人被释放

1955-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    巴共领袖查希尔等人被释放
    新华社新德里八日电　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦新任总督伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎在十月六日就职的这一天，下令释放巴基斯坦前陆军参谋长阿克巴尔汗少将和巴基斯坦共产党领袖查希尔等十三人。他们是在一九五一年被捕的，当时曾被控在巴基斯坦陆军总部所在地拉瓦尔品第进行所谓“用暴力推翻政府的阴谋”，并且在一九五二年被判处四年到十二年不等的徒刑。
