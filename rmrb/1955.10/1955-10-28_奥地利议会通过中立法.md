Title: Austrian Parliament Passes Neutrality Law
Author:
Date: 1955-10-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 27th: Vienna dispatch: The Austrian Parliament held a meeting on the 26th and passed the draft of the Federal Constitutional Law on Austrian neutrality. Members of the Austrian People's Party, the Austrian Socialist Party, and the Austrian People's Opposition League voted in favor. The Neutrality Law states: "In order to maintain its external independence and the inviolability of its territory, Austria voluntarily declares that it will maintain permanent neutrality forever and is determined to maintain and defend it by all means within its capabilities. In order to achieve this goal, Austria will never participate in military alliances in the future, and will not allow the establishment of foreign military bases on its territory."

### 奥地利议会通过中立法

1955-10-28
第4版()
专栏：

    奥地利议会通过中立法
    新华社柏林二十七日电　维也纳消息：奥地利议会二十六日举行会议，通过奥地利中立的联邦宪法法律草案。奥地利人民党、奥地利社会党和奥地利人民反对派联盟的议员都投赞成票。中立法案里说：“奥地利为了保持它的对外独立和它的领土的不可侵犯性，兹自愿宣布永远保持中立，并决心用它的能力范围内的一切办法来维持和保卫它。奥地利为了达到这个目的在将来决不参加军事联盟，并不允许在它的领土上建立外国军事基地。”
