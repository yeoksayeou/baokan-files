    A member of the Soviet Cultural Delegation, Kosmodemyanskaya, the mother of Soviet hero Zoya, visited the Beijing Normal University Affiliated Girls' Middle School on October 4th. This is a group photo of Zoya's mother, Kosmodemyanskaya, with the Young Pioneers of the Beijing Normal University Affiliated Girls' Middle School.
