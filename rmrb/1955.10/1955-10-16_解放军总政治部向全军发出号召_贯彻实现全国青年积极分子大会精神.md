Title: General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army Issues Call to the Entire Army to Implement the Spirit of the National Congress of Young Activists

Author:
Date: 1955-10-16
Page: 3

    General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army Issues Call to the Entire Army
    Implement the Spirit of the National Congress of Young Activists
    The General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army recently issued a notice to the entire army regarding the transmission and implementation of the spirit of the National Congress of Young Socialist Construction Activists.
    The notice requested political organs at all levels to carefully study the instructions of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League regarding the publicity and implementation of the spirit of this congress. Based on the instructions of the Party Central Committee on the congress, the report of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League at the congress, the letter of guarantee from the congress to the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, the letter from the congress to the nation's young men and women, and other relevant documents and editorials, they should organize the transmission and implementation of the spirit of the congress among the youth of the troops in a planned manner, in conjunction with the tasks and specific circumstances of their units.
    The notice pointed out: The forms of transmission and publicity activities should be lively and diverse, such as convening youth congresses, League member congresses, organizing report meetings, discussion meetings, newspaper reading, film screenings and picture exhibitions, organizing meetings between young advanced figures and youth, and holding youth evening parties with themes such as "Glorious Post of Defending the Motherland" and "Being an Active Element in Defending Socialist Construction." It is imperative that every young person participate in at least one promotional activity related to the congress, so as to be educated and inspired.
    The notice finally requested the political organs of relevant units to continue to cultivate the young collective units of the troops that were rewarded by the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League at this congress, strengthen the Party's leadership over the youth work of these units, and enable them to always maintain the honor of being advanced youth collective units. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 解放军总政治部向全军发出号召  贯彻实现全国青年积极分子大会精神

1955-10-16
第3版()
专栏：

    解放军总政治部向全军发出号召
    贯彻实现全国青年积极分子大会精神
    中国人民解放军总政治部最近向全军发出了关于传达和贯彻全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会精神的通知。
    通知要求各级政治机关认真研究青年团中央关于宣传和贯彻这次大会精神的指示，根据党中央对大会的指示、团中央在大会上的报告、大会致党中央和毛主席的保证书、大会致全国男女青年书以及其他有关文件和社论，结合本部队的任务和具体情况，有计划地在部队青年中组织传达和贯彻大会精神。
    通知中指出：传达和宣传活动的形式应生动活泼，多种多样，如召开青年大会、团员大会，组织报告会、座谈会、读报、看电影和图片展览，组织青年先进人物和青年的会面，举办以“保卫祖国的光荣岗位”、“做个保卫社会主义建设的积极分子”等为主题的青年晚会，务必使每一个青年都能至少参加一项有关大会的宣传活动，从而受到教育和鼓舞。
    通知最后要求各有关单位的政治机关继续培养在这次大会上受到团中央奖励的部队青年集体单位，加强党对这些单位的青年工作的领导，使他们永远保持青年先进集体单位的荣誉。（新华社）
