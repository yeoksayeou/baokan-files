Title: Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, Recalled to His Country from Beijing

Author:

Date: 1955-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd—Indian Ambassador to our country, His Excellency R.K. Nehru, has been recalled and departed Beijing on the 2nd with his wife to return to his country.  Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs and Director of the Asian Department Chen Jiakang, Director of Protocol Ke Hua, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Chen Hansheng, and Council Member Gong Pusheng went to the station to see them off.  Staff from the Embassy of the Republic of India in China and diplomatic envoys and staff from various countries' embassies in China also went to see them off.

Prior to his departure, Ambassador Nehru paid farewell visits to Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, as well as Vice Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Chen Yi, and responsible persons from relevant departments.

Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a farewell banquet for Ambassador Nehru and his wife at Ziguang Pavilion on the evening of September 26th.

Those attending the banquet included: Vice Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen and Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and Zhang Hanfu, relevant ministers, and heads of various people's organizations.

The wife of Indian archaeologist Chakravarti, Professor Debu Barman, Dean of the Art Institute of Visva-Bharati University, and all diplomatic officers and their wives from the Embassy of the Republic of India in China also attended the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere, and afterwards everyone watched Indian song and dance performances and Chinese song and dance performances performed by the Chinese cultural delegation that had visited India.

On the evening of September 27th, Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, held a farewell banquet for Ambassador Nehru and his wife.

On the afternoon of September 28th, Ambassador Nehru and his wife held a farewell reception, which was attended by Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, relevant ministers, and heads of various people's organizations. Diplomatic envoys and diplomats from various countries in China were also invited to attend.

### 印度驻华大使赖嘉文奉调离北京回国

1955-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    印度驻华大使赖嘉文奉调离北京回国
    新华社三日讯　印度共和国驻我国特命全权大使赖嘉文已奉调于二日偕同他的夫人离京返国。前往车站送行的有外交部部长助理兼亚洲司司长陈家康、礼宾司司长柯华和中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙及理事龚普生。印度共和国驻华大使馆人员和各国驻华使节及使馆人员也前往送行。
    赖嘉文大使在行前曾分别向毛泽东主席，朱德副主席，刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理兼外交部长以及宋庆龄、李济深、郭沫若副委员长，陈云、彭德怀、陈毅副总理和有关部门的负责人进行了辞行拜会。
    在赖嘉文大使及夫人行前，周恩来总理曾于九月二十六日晚在紫光阁设宴为他们饯行。
    出席宴会的有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、郭沫若，国务院副总理陈毅，秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫，各有关部长及各人民团体负责人等。
    印度考古学家查克拉瓦蒂的夫人，印度国际大学艺术院院长德布·巴曼教授及印度共和国驻华大使馆全体外交官夫妇也出席了宴会。
    宴会在充满友好的气氛中进行，会后大家一起观看了曾去印度访问的我国文化代表团演出的印度歌舞节目和中国歌舞节目。
    九月二十七日晚，中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙为赖嘉文大使及夫人举行了饯行宴会。
    九月二十八日下午，赖嘉文大使及夫人举行了辞行酒会，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、郭沫若，国务院副总理陈云、陈毅，外交部副部长张闻天，各有关部长和各人民团体负责人出席了酒会。各国驻华使节及外交官也应邀到会。
