Our Great Motherland

The people of the capital, in high spirits, celebrated the sixth anniversary of the founding of the nation and held a grand parade on October 1st. This is the scene of the vanguard of the parade – the honor guard advancing, holding high a huge banner with the words "Long Live Peace."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    首都各界人民欢欣鼓舞地庆祝建国六周年，十月一日举行了盛大的游行。这是游行队伍的前导——仪仗队高举着“和平万岁”的巨幅标语牌前进的场面。
