Title: Overseas Chinese in Japan Cheerfully Celebrate the Motherland's National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-10-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 2nd dispatch: Overseas Chinese in Japan are celebrating the motherland's National Day with joy and excitement.

    The Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association held a grand celebration meeting for the 6th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China at the Toshima Public Hall in Tokyo on the afternoon of September 30th, on the eve of National Day.

    A total of 1,000 overseas Chinese from Tokyo, Yokohama, and neighboring areas attended the meeting. Feng Tiecheng and Xiao Xiangqian, deputy heads of the Chinese Council for the Promotion of International Trade's Commodity Exhibition Group to Japan, and 36 other members also attended the meeting. Representatives from various sectors and organizations were invited to attend the meeting, including the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, the Sino-Japanese Trade Association, the Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the All Japan Federation of Industrial Organizations, the National Federation for the Protection of the Constitution, the Japanese Communist Party, the Labor-Farmer Party, the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan Federation of Women's Organizations, and the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan.

    Wu Puwen, Acting President of the Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association, said in his opening remarks: Our National Day celebrations are becoming more grand year by year. In particular, this year's celebration has been attended by many representatives from the motherland, which makes us overseas Chinese even happier.

    Kang Mingqiu, a representative of the National People's Congress, proposed at the meeting that all overseas Chinese express their heartfelt gratitude and support to the motherland. This proposal was met with enthusiastic applause from the audience.

    Feng Tiecheng, Deputy Head of the Chinese Commodity Exhibition Group, spoke at the meeting. After introducing the construction achievements of the motherland in the past six years, he said: This time we have come to Japan to hold a commodity exhibition, which is of positive significance for promoting trade relations between China and Japan and enhancing mutual understanding between the peoples of the two countries. His speech aroused enthusiastic applause from the audience.

    Kumaichi Yamamoto, Vice President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, also spoke at the meeting. He said: Only six years have passed since the founding of the People's Republic of China, and now it has truly become a first-class country in the world. He expressed the hope that the people of China and Japan will join hands and work together for peace in Asia and the world.

    Also speaking at the meeting were Kanzo Uchimaya, Vice President of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Han Dezhu, Representative of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan, Nobuo Shioya, Vice Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, Natsu Kawasaki, Vice President of the Japan Federation of Women's Organizations, Koichi Ohno, Secretary-General of the National Federation for the Protection of the Constitution, and Sanzo Nosaka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party. They unanimously praised China's great development in the past six years and emphasized the necessity of friendly exchanges between the peoples of China and Japan.

    That evening, the Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association also held a celebration reception at the Tokyo Kaikan. Takechiyo Matsuda, a Democratic Party member of the Diet and Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, who participated in the Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade, spoke at the reception. He emphasized that the people of China and Japan should take concrete actions to gradually create the reality of friendly exchanges. He also expressed his sincere hope that this Chinese commodity exhibition would be a success. Shozo Murata also spoke at the reception. He emphasized: The unreasonable state between China and Japan should be eliminated as soon as possible. Other guests also spoke at the reception. They unanimously expressed their willingness to work together with the Chinese people to normalize relations between China and Japan.

### 旅日华侨欢欣鼓舞庆祝祖国的国庆节

1955-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    旅日华侨欢欣鼓舞庆祝祖国的国庆节
    据新华社二日讯　东京消息：旅日华侨以欢欣鼓舞的心情，庆祝祖国的国庆节。
    东京华侨总会在国庆节前夕——九月三十日下午，在东京丰岛公会堂举行了中华人民共和国成立六周年庆祝大会。
    参加大会的有东京、横滨和邻近各地的华侨共一千人。中国国际贸易促进委员会赴日本商品展览团副团长冯铁城、萧向前等三十八人也参加了大会。应邀参加大会的还有日中友好协会、日本国际贸易促进协会、中日贸易会、日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟、日本工会总评议会、全日本产业别工会会议、拥护宪法国民联合会、日本共产党、劳农党、日本红十字会、全日本妇女团体联合会、在日朝鲜人总联合会等各界各团体代表。
    东京华侨总会代理会长吴普文致开会词说：我们庆祝国庆节的集会，一年比一年盛大，尤其是今年的庆祝大会，得到许多祖国代表的参加，使我们华侨越发高兴。
    全国人民代表大会代表康鸣球在大会上建议全体华侨向祖国表示衷心的感谢和拥护。这个建议博得全场热烈的掌声。
    中国商品展览团副团长冯铁城在大会上讲话。他在介绍祖国六年来的建设情况后说：这次我们来到日本举办商品展览会，这对促进中日两国的贸易关系和增进两国人民的相互理解，都有着积极的意义。他的讲话引起了全场热烈的掌声。
    日本国际贸易促进协会副会长山本熊一也在大会上讲话。他说：中华人民共和国成立以来，为时不过六年，现在它已名符其实成为世界上第一流的国家。他表示希望中日两国人民携起手来，为亚洲和世界和平而共同努力。
    在大会上讲话的还有日中友好协会副会长内山完造、在日本朝鲜人总联合会代表韩德铢、日本工会总评议会副主席盐谷信雄、全日本妇女团体联合会副会长河崎夏、拥护宪法国民联合会事务局长大野幸一、日本共产党中央第一书记野坂参三等人。他们一致称赞中国六年来的伟大发展，并且强调中日两国人民友好往来的必要性。
    当天晚上，东京华侨总会还在东京会馆举行了庆祝酒会。参加日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟的民主党议员、邮政相松田竹千代在酒会上讲话。他强调中日两国人民以具体的行动来逐步造成友好往来的事实。他并且表示衷心希望这次的中国商品展览会获得成功。村田省藏也在酒会上讲话。他强调说：现在中日两国间的不合理的状态，应该早日消除。其他来宾也纷纷在酒会上讲话。他们一致表示愿意与中国人民共同为使中日两国关系正常化而共同努力。
