Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-10-24
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    A scene of abundant harvest is seen everywhere in the rural areas of the suburbs of Beijing. The Nanjiao Farm and the Yihe Mutual Aid Work Station in the Nanyuan District have harvested the highest rice yield in six years on more than 3,000 mu of paddy fields. Workers at the work station are threshing with threshing machines.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京市郊区农村到处呈现丰收的景象。南苑区南郊农场和义作业站三千多亩水稻获得六年来最高的收成。作业站工人正在用脱粒机打场。
