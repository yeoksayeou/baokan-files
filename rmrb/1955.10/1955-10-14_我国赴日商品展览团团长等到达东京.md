Title: Head of China's Trade Delegation to Japan Arrives in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1955-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 13th: Cao Zhongshu, head of the Chinese Trade Delegation to Japan, and his party of seven arrived in Tokyo on the afternoon of the 12th.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Shonzō Murata, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association; Kumaichi Yamamoto, Vice President; Kesaichi Ito, Director; Katsutoshi Nozoe and Shichiro Hozumi, representatives of the League of Diet Members Promoting Japan-China Trade and members of the left-wing Socialist Party; Kazuo Suzuki, Executive Director of the Japan-China Trade Association; Takeo Ito, Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association; Toru Kobayashi, Standing Director of the Japan Peace Committee; Bunkichi Okada, Central Committee Member of the Japanese Communist Party; Jiajue Zhang, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association; and Chengjia Chen, representative of the Overseas Chinese in Osaka.

Also welcoming them at the airport were Chasovnikov, representative of the Soviet Mission in Tokyo, and Feng Tiecheng and Xiao Xiangqian, deputy heads of the Chinese Trade Exhibition Group who had arrived in Tokyo earlier to prepare for the exhibition.

More than two hundred Overseas Chinese from Tokyo and Yokohama, holding five-star red flags and flowers, welcomed them at the airport.

Shonzō Murata, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, delivered a welcoming speech at the airport, saying, "The Chinese commodity exhibition held in Japan this time is not only significant in terms of trade, but also represents an epitome of your country's great development. We believe that this commodity exhibition will promote friendly relations between China and Japan."

Katsutoshi Nozoe, head of the Second Japanese Diet Members Delegation to the Soviet Union, who had just returned home, delivered a speech expressing his heartfelt welcome to China for holding a commodity exhibition in Japan.

Head of Delegation Cao Zhongshu addressed the audience amidst warm applause. He said that he was very pleased to have the opportunity to exhibit Chinese goods in Japan. He said that our exhibition work has received warm assistance and support from all walks of life in Japan, and on behalf of all members of the delegation, I would like to express my heartfelt thanks.

Cao Zhongshu said: Although China-Japan trade has a long history, commodity exhibitions have not been held frequently in the past. We believe that this exhibition will help further expand economic exchanges and trade relations between China and Japan, thereby enhancing mutual understanding and friendship between the peoples of China and Japan.

Cao Zhongshu finally expressed his respects to the citizens of Tokyo and the people of Japan.

### 我国赴日商品展览团团长等到达东京

1955-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我国赴日商品展览团团长等到达东京
    新华社十三日讯　东京消息：中国赴日本商品展览团团长曹中枢一行七人在十二日下午到达东京。
    到机场欢迎的有日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏、副会长山本熊一、理事伊藤今朝市；日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表、左派社会党议员野沟胜，穗积七郎；中日贸易会专务理事铃木一雄；日中友好协会理事长伊藤武雄；日本拥护和平委员会常任理事小林彻；日本共产党中央委员冈田文吉；东京华侨总会理事会主席张加爵；大阪华侨代表陈承家。
    到机场欢迎的还有苏联驻东京代表团代表查索夫尼科夫和先到东京进行展览会准备工作的中国商品展览团副团长冯铁城、萧向前等。
    东京和横滨两市的华侨等共两百多人举着五星红旗，拿着鲜花到机场欢迎。
    日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏在机场致欢迎词说：“这次在日本举办的中国商品展览会不仅在贸易上有着意义，而且表示着贵国伟大发展的缩影。我们相信这次商品展览会将会促进中日两国的友好关系。”
    刚刚回国的日本国会议员访苏团第二团长野沟胜致词，对于中国在日本举办商品展览会表示衷心的欢迎。
    曹中枢团长在热烈的掌声中致词。他对这次能有机会到日本展出中国的商品，表示感到非常高兴。他说，我们的展出工作得到日本各界人士的热烈协助和支持，我谨代表全团人员表示衷心的感谢。
    曹中枢说：中日贸易虽有悠久的历史，但商品展览会过去举办的不多。我们相信，通过这次展览会将有助于中日两国间的经济交流与贸易关系的进一步扩展，从而增进中日两国人民的相互了解和友谊。
    曹中枢最后表示向东京市民和日本人民致敬。
