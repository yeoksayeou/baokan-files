Title: Sixth Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic: 300,000 Working People in Berlin Gather to Celebrate
Author:
Date: 1955-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 8th: According to TASS, the working people of the German Democratic Republic (GDR) enthusiastically celebrated the sixth anniversary of the republic's founding on the 7th. Mass rallies and parades were held in cities and villages to celebrate this glorious festival.

　　Approximately 300,000 people participated in the celebration rally in the festively decorated Marx-Engels Square and surrounding streets in Berlin. Attending the rally were President Pieck of the GDR, Prime Minister Grotewohl, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Germany Reimann, and government delegations from the Soviet Union and people's democratic countries.

　　Ulbricht spoke at the meeting. He said that the working people of the GDR have made remarkable achievements in peaceful construction over the past six years. Here, workers and farmers have taken the power to govern the country into their own hands. This fact is a historically significant victory that also has an impact on West Germany. Ulbricht pointed out that the GDR is the foundation for building a new peace-loving Germany. In order to further consolidate and strengthen the GDR, it is necessary to make further achievements in peaceful construction work and continue the struggle for a peaceful solution to the German question. He expressed his belief that the cause of the GDR, the cause of the people, will be victorious throughout Germany.

　　The head of the Soviet government delegation, Suslov, then spoke. He said that the Soviet people are sincerely happy for every achievement made by the working people of the GDR in building their new life. The treaty recently signed in Moscow strengthened the friendly relations between the Soviet Union and the GDR. Some Western politicians do not like the existence of the GDR. However, the GDR exists, develops, and has a great future.

　　Suslov stated that the GDR can always count on the assistance and support of the various peoples of the Soviet Union.

　　The head of the Polish government delegation, Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz, the head of the Czechoslovak government delegation, Barak, representatives of the French and Italian Communist Parties, and representatives of West German workers also spoke at the meeting.

### 德意志民主共和国成立六周年  柏林三十万劳动人民集会庆祝

1955-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国成立六周年
    柏林三十万劳动人民集会庆祝
    新华社八日讯　据塔斯社柏林讯：德意志民主共和国劳动人民七日热烈庆祝共和国成立六周年。在各城市和乡村中举行了庆祝这个光荣节日的群众集会和示威游行。
    在节日装饰的宽广的柏林马克思—恩格斯广场上和在广场附近的街道上，约有三十万人参加了庆祝大会。出席大会的有德意志民主共和国总统皮克，总理格罗提渥，德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记、副总理乌布利希，德国共产党中央委员会第一书记雷曼等人，以及苏联和人民民主国家的政府代表团。
    乌布利希在会上讲话。他说，德意志民主共和国的劳动人民在过去六年中在和平建设方面取得了显著的成就。在这里，工人和农民已经把治理国家的权力掌握在自己的手中。这一事实是一个也对西德产生影响的具有历史意义的胜利。乌布利希指出德意志民主共和国是建立一个新的爱好和平的德国的基础。为了进一步巩固和加强德意志民主共和国，就必须在和平建设劳动中取得更进一步的成就，必须继续进行争取和平解决德国问题的斗争。他表示相信德意志民主共和国的事业、人民的事业将会在整个德国获得胜利。
    苏联政府代表团团长苏斯洛夫接着讲话。他说，苏联人民对于德意志民主共和国劳动人民在建设他们的新生活方面取得的每一个成就都感到衷心的高兴。最近在莫斯科签订的条约加强了苏联和德意志民主共和国之间的友好关系。某些西方政治家不喜欢德意志民主共和国的存在。但是，德意志民主共和国存在着，发展着，并且有着伟大的前途。
    苏斯洛夫表示，德意志民主共和国永远可以指靠苏联各族人民的援助和支持。
    波兰政府代表团团长、波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团团长巴拉克，法国和意大利共产党的代表、西德工人代表相继在大会上讲了话。
