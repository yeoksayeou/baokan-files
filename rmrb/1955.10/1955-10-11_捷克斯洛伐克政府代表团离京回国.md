Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation Departs Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1955-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th—The head of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, Uher, and part of the delegation, who participated in the Exhibition of Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia held in Shanghai, left Beijing by plane on the 10th to return home.

Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Yang Weiyu, Vice Minister of Light Industry, and representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs saw them off at the airport.

Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Grigor, and others also went to the airport to see them off.

During their stay in Beijing, Delegation Head Uher and others visited the Ming Tombs, the Summer Palace, the Forbidden City, Beihai Park and other famous historical sites and scenic spots.

### 捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团离京回国

1955-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团离京回国
    新华社十日讯　参加在上海举行的捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团长乌赫和部分团员在十日乘飞机离京回国。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸、对外贸易部副部长雷任民、轻工业部副部长杨卫玉和外交部代表到机场送行。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使格里哥尔等也前往机场送行。
    代表团团长乌赫等在北京游览了明陵、颐和园、故宫、北海等名胜古迹。
