Title: Kim Il-sung and Kim Tu-bong Receive Japanese Diet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-10-22
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 21st: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, received the Japanese Diet delegation visiting Korea on October 20th.

　　The meeting lasted approximately three and a half hours. Throughout the meeting, in an atmosphere filled with mutual understanding and harmony, the two sides exchanged views on a series of issues related to the interests of the Korean and Japanese people. These issues included the establishment of normal diplomatic relations between Korea and Japan, economic and cultural exchanges, guaranteeing the legitimate rights of Korean residents in Japan, the repatriation of Japanese residents in Korea, and the freedom of Korean and Japanese fishermen to fish.

　　During the exchange of views, both sides paid particular attention to issues related to the establishment of diplomatic relations between Korea and Japan, and emphasized that diplomatic relations should be established as soon as possible to actively promote friendship between the people of Korea and Japan.

　　Both sides also assured that, given the necessity of promoting economic and cultural exchanges and that the conditions for taking practical measures in these areas have matured, from the perspective of the interests of the Korean and Japanese people, they should work together to promote the realization of economic and cultural exchanges between the two countries, and should first reach an agreement on the need to hold commodity exhibitions and exchange trade representatives in Korea and Japan.

　　Both sides unanimously expressed regret that the Japanese authorities have failed to guarantee Korean residents in Japan the rights generally recognized by international law, and believe that it is necessary to take practical measures through negotiations between the governments of Korea and Japan to improve the current situation of Korean residents in Japan, guarantee their lives, enable them to receive democratic and national education, and reach an agreement on making joint efforts, first and foremost, to dispatch representatives of the Korean Red Cross to Japan for this purpose.

　　Both sides unanimously expressed their willingness to strive to take practical measures to resolve the issue of repatriating Korean residents in Japan and the free return of nationals of both countries to their home countries as soon as possible. Both sides unanimously agreed to take concrete measures to ensure that Korean and Japanese fishermen can freely fish on the high seas along the coasts of both Korea and Japan; both sides also agreed to work to find effective ways, first of all, to send fishing representatives to each other for this purpose.

　　Both sides unanimously agreed to issue a joint communiqué to affirm the agreements reached in this meeting.

### 金日成金枓奉接见日议员代表团

1955-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成金枓奉接见日议员代表团
    新华社平壤二十一日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉在十月二十日接见了访问朝鲜的日本国会议员代表团。
    这次接见历时约三个半小时，双方在自始至终都充满互相谅解和融洽的空气中就有关朝鲜人民和日本人民的利益的一系列问题交换了意见，这些问题包括建立朝鲜和日本两国之间的正常的外交关系问题、经济和文化交流问题、保证在日本的朝鲜侨民的合法权利问题、送回在朝鲜的日本侨民问题以及朝鲜和日本渔民的捕鱼自由问题。
    在交换意见的过程中，双方特别关心同建立朝鲜和日本之间的外交关系有关的问题，并且强调指出，应该尽速建立外交关系来积极努力促进朝鲜和日本两国人民之间的友谊。
    双方还保证，鉴于有必要促进经济和文化交流而且在这些方面采取实际措施的条件已经成熟，从朝鲜和日本两国人民的利益出发，他们应当共同努力促进两国间经济和文化交流的实现，并且首先就有必要在朝鲜和日本举行商品展览会和互派贸易代表一事达成协议。
    双方一致对日本当局不能保证在日朝侨享受国际法所普遍承认的权利一事表示遗憾，认为有必要通过朝鲜和日本两国政府的谈判来采取实际措施，以改善在日朝侨的目前情况，保证他们的生活，使他们受民主的、民族教育，并且就作出共同努力，首先是为此目的而派遣朝鲜红十字会代表前往日本一事达成协议。
    双方一致表示愿意争取采取实际措施以早日解决关于送回在朝日侨和两国的侨民自由回国的问题。双方一致同意采取具体措施以保证朝鲜和日本渔民可以在朝鲜和日本两国沿海一带的公海上自由捕鱼；双方还同意努力寻求有效的办法，首先是要为此目的两国互派渔民代表。
    双方一致同意发表联合公报把在这次接见中达成的各项协议肯定下来。
