Title: Cai Shufan Hosts Banquet to Welcome Soviet "Zenit" Football Team
Author:
Date: 1955-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: Cai Shufan, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 27th to welcome all members of the Soviet Leningrad "Zenit" Voluntary Sports Association football team.

    Li Haiqiu, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and embassy staff attended the banquet tonight.

    Among those attending tonight's banquet were Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director of the Sports Commission, Chai Zemin, Director of the Beijing Municipal Sports Commission, representatives of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and many Chinese football players.

    Cai Shufan spoke first at the banquet. He extended a warm welcome to the Soviet Leningrad "Zenit" football team, the first football team from the Soviet Union to visit our country. Subsequently, V.S. Deromanov, leader of the Soviet Leningrad "Zenit" football team, delivered a speech. He thanked the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China for their invitation and warm hospitality.

    The second group of fourteen members of the Soviet Leningrad "Zenit" football team arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 蔡树藩设宴欢迎苏联“泽尼特”足球队

1955-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    蔡树藩设宴欢迎苏联“泽尼特”足球队
    新华社二十七日讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩，二十七日晚设宴欢迎苏联列宁格勒“泽尼特”志愿体育协会足球队全体人员。
    苏联驻中国大使馆参赞李海秋和使馆人员出席了今晚的宴会。
    出席今晚宴会的有体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠，北京市体育运动委员会主任柴泽民和中苏友好协会的代表，以及中国的足球运动员等多人。
    蔡树藩在宴会上首先讲话。他对苏联来我国访问的第一个足球队——苏联列宁格勒“泽尼特”足球队表示热烈欢迎。接着，苏联列宁格勒“泽尼特”足球队领队维·斯·杰洛曼诺夫致词。他感谢中华人民共和国体育运动委员会对他们的邀请和热情招待。
    苏联列宁格勒“泽尼特”足球队第二批人员十四人是在今天下午乘飞机到达北京的。
