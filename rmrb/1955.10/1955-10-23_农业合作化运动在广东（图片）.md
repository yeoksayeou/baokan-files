Title: Agricultural Cooperation Movement in Guangdong (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1955-10-23
Page: 2

Agricultural Cooperation Movement in Guangdong
	To support the propaganda work of the agricultural cooperation movement, the People's Cultural Center of Jieyang County, Guangdong Province, produced a large number of slides with agricultural cooperation as the theme and distributed them to districts and townships for screening.
		Photo by Liao Yanqiang (Southern Daily News)
	To welcome the great development of the agricultural cooperation movement, Jieyang County plans to train more than 13,000 core members for cooperative establishment in batches before the autumn harvest. This is a scene where veteran cooperative members give a report to trainees and then answer their questions. Photo by Liao Yanqiang (Southern Daily News)
	The late rice of the Liaoyuan Agricultural Production Cooperative in Meilian Township, Yuhu District, Jieyang County, is growing well. Farmers who are preparing to join the cooperative are visiting the fields of this cooperative.
		Photo by Liao Yanqiang (Southern Daily News)
	Trainees from the cooperative establishment training class in Zhongshan County are visiting the Guangdong Zhongshan Agricultural Machinery Station.
		Photo by Guo Weiqi (Southern Daily News)
	Liu Guanglin (left), a farmer from Maba Township, Qujiang County, is comparing his rice with the rice of the Qunlian Agricultural Production Cooperative in the township and is completely convinced that the rice of the agricultural production cooperative grows better than his. Now Liu Guanglin has signed up to join the cooperative.
		Photo by Guo Weiqi (Southern Daily News)
	Farmers in various parts of Guangdong are joining agricultural production cooperatives one after another. This is a scene of farmers from Jizaiba, Yanggang Township, Qujiang County, happily registering their land certificates to join the cooperative.
		Photo by Guo Weiqi (Southern Daily News)

### 农业合作化运动在广东（图片）

1955-10-23
第2版()
专栏：

    农业合作化运动在广东
    广东省揭阳县人民文化馆，为了配合农业合作化运动的宣传工作，制造了大量以农业合作化为内容的幻灯片，分发到各区、乡放映。
                  廖衍强摄（南方日报社稿）
    为了迎接农业合作化运动大发展，揭阳县计划在秋前分批训练一万三千多名建社骨干。这是老社社员给训练班学员做了报告以后，又给他们解答问题。廖衍强摄（南方日报社稿）
    揭阳县渔湖区梅联乡燎原农业生产合作社晚稻长得好。准备入社的农民都到这个社的田间来参观。
                  廖衍强摄（南方日报社稿）
    中山县建社骨干训练班的学员在参观广东省中山农业机器站。
                  郭伟祺摄（南方日报社稿）
    曲江县第一区马坝乡农民刘光林（左）拿着自己的水稻和乡内群连农业生产合作社的水稻对比，完全信服农业生产合作社的稻比他的长得好。现在刘光林已经报名入社了。
                  郭伟祺摄（南方日报社稿）
    广东各地农民纷纷参加农业生产合作社。这是曲江县第一区阳岗乡鸡仔坝农民，喜悦地拿着土地证登记土地入社。
                  郭伟祺摄（南方日报社稿）
