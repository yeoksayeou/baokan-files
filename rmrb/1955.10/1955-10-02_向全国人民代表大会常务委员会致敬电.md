Title: Telegram of Respect to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-10-02
Page: 2

    Telegram of Respect to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
    To Chairman Liu, Standing Committee of the National People's Congress：
    The resolution of the 21st meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region and the abolition of Xinjiang Province is fully in line with the interests and aspirations of the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang. All the representatives attending the Second Session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province unanimously express their support, and the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region is scheduled to be announced on October 1st. The establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region has further enhanced the patriotic consciousness of the people of all ethnic groups, strengthened the friendly unity of all ethnic groups, and stimulated the enthusiasm of the people of all ethnic groups for production and construction. In the future, we will certainly exercise our autonomous rights actively in accordance with the provisions of the Constitution, give full play to the effectiveness of the autonomous organs, and strive to build the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region and our great socialist motherland. We respectfully send this telegram of respect.
                        The Second Session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province
                                      September 30, 1955

### 向全国人民代表大会常务委员会致敬电

1955-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    向全国人民代表大会常务委员会致敬电全国人民代表大会常务委员会刘委员长：
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十一次会议关于成立新疆维吾尔自治区撤销新疆省建制的决议，是完全符合于新疆各族人民的利益和愿望的，我们出席新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议的全体代表，一致表示拥护，并定于十月一日宣布新疆维吾尔自治区成立。新疆维吾尔自治区的成立，更加提高了各族人民的爱国主义觉悟，增强了各民族的友好团结，激发了各族人民的生产建设积极性。今后，我们一定依照宪法规定，积极行使自治权利，充分发挥自治机关的效能，为建设新疆维吾尔自治区，为建设我们伟大的社会主义祖国而奋斗。谨电致敬。
                        新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议
                                      一九五五年九月三十日
