Title: Soviet Journalist Delegation Visits the United States
Author:
Date: 1955-10-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New York, October 19th: A Soviet journalist delegation, headed by Polevoy, is currently visiting the United States. On the evening of the 17th, the editorial department of "The New York Times" held a reception for the visiting Soviet journalist delegation. On the morning of the 18th, the Soviet journalist delegation visited Columbia University and were guests at a luncheon hosted by Wallace, the dean of the School of International Relations. Subsequently, the Soviet journalist delegation visited the Journalism Institute and had a lively exchange with the institute's director, Ackerman, on the experiences of training journalists in the Soviet Union and the United States.
The Soviet journalist delegation also visited the New York Stock Exchange and Merrill Lynch, one of the largest bankers and brokers in the United States.

### 苏联记者团在美国访问

1955-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联记者团在美国访问
    新华社二十日讯　据塔斯社纽约十九日讯：以波列伏依为首的苏联记者团目前正在美国访问。十七日晚上，“纽约时报”的编辑部为来访的苏联记者团举行了招待会。十八日早晨，苏联记者团访问了哥伦比亚大学，并且在国际关系学院院长华莱士的午餐会上作客。接着，苏联记者团访问了新闻研究所，同研究所所长阿克曼热烈地交流了苏联和美国训练新闻记者的经验。
    苏联记者团还参观了纽约证券交易所和美国最大的公司之一梅里尔·林契银行家与经纪人公司。
