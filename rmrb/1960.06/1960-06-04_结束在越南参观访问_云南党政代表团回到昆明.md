Title: Yunnan Party and Government Delegation Returns to Kunming After Concluding Visit to Vietnam

Author:

Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, June 3rd. The Yunnan Province Party and Government Delegation of our country, invited by the Dai-Miao Autonomous Region of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to participate in the celebration of the fifth anniversary of the establishment of the autonomous region, returned to Kunming by plane from Hanoi at noon on the 2nd, led by Liu Zhuofu, head of the delegation, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Party Committee Secretariat of the Chinese Communist Party, and Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, after concluding its visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The Yunnan Provincial Party and Government Delegation left Kunming for the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on May 15th.

President Ho Chi Minh received all members of the delegation on May 30th. The Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party hosted a banquet for the delegation.

### 结束在越南参观访问  云南党政代表团回到昆明

1960-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在越南参观访问
    云南党政代表团回到昆明
    据新华社昆明3日电　应越南民主共和国傣苗自治区邀请前往参加这个自治区成立五周年庆祝大会的我国云南省党政代表团，结束了在越南民主共和国的参观访问之后，2日中午在团长、中共云南省委书记处书记、云南省副省长刘卓甫率领下，由河内乘飞机回到昆明。
    云南省党政代表团是在5月15日离昆明去越南民主共和国的。
    胡志明主席在5月30日接见了代表团的全体人员。越南劳动党中央曾设宴招待代表团。
