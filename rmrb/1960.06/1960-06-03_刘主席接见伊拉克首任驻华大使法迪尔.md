Title: Chairman Liu Receives First Iraqi Ambassador to China, Fadil
Author:
Date: 1960-06-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd – Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the first Iraqi Ambassador to China, Abdul Haq Fadil, this afternoon and had a friendly conversation. Those present at the meeting included Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and He Ying, Director of the Department of West Asian and African Affairs.

### 刘主席接见伊拉克首任驻华大使法迪尔

1960-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见伊拉克首任驻华大使法迪尔
    新华社2日讯　刘少奇主席今天下午接见了伊拉克首任驻中国大使阿卜杜勒·哈克·法迪尔，并且进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有外交部副部长耿飚，西亚非洲司司长何英等。
