Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Sends Congratulatory Message on Tunisia's National Day
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 31st:
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Tunisian President Bourguiba on May 30th, congratulating him on the National Day of the Republic of Tunisia. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Habib Bourguiba, President of the Republic of Tunisia:
    On the occasion of your country's National Day, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the Tunisian people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish Your Excellency and the Tunisian people new victories in the struggle to safeguard national independence. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness. May the friendly relations between the people of China and Tunisia be further developed.
    Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    May 30, 1960

### 刘少奇主席电贺突尼斯国庆

1960-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席电贺突尼斯国庆
    新华社31日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇30日致电突尼斯总统布尔吉巴，祝贺突尼斯共和国国庆。电文如下：突尼斯共和国总统哈比布·布尔吉巴阁下：值此贵国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和突尼斯人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝阁下和突尼斯人民在维护民族独立的斗争中取得新的胜利。祝贵国繁荣，人民幸福。祝中国和突尼斯两国人民间的友好关系获得进一步的发展。中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇　一九六○年五月三十日
