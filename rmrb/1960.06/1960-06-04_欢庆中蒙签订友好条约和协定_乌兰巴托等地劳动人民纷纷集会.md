Title: Jubilant Celebrations as China and Mongolia Sign Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation: Laboring People in Ulaanbaatar and Other Places Gather in Meetings
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, June 3rd: According to today's report in the Mongolian "Truth Daily," the people of Ulaanbaatar, the capital of Mongolia, and other regions have been holding gatherings to celebrate the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Assistance and the agreement on China's provision of economic and technical assistance to Mongolia.

    Tao Gemide, the Party Committee Secretary of the Ulaanbaatar City Construction Company, said at a gathering held by the company's employees that the great historical significance of China's brotherly and enormous assistance to Mongolia lies in: it further consolidates the eternal friendship between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples and strengthens the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union. On behalf of all construction workers, he expressed his determination to work side by side with the fraternal Chinese construction workers, learn more from them, and strive to complete the buildings quickly, well, and economically according to plan.

    All the staff of the Mongolian National Circus warmly welcomed the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Assistance and the joint statement issued by the two governments. Arildai, the director of the circus, delivered a long speech at the meeting on the signing of the treaty and agreement between Mongolia and China. The actors were pleased that a circus and other buildings would soon be built in Ulaanbaatar. They expressed their determination to make greater achievements in artistic creation.

    At a gathering of teachers and students held at the Ulaanbaatar Normal College, Bai Fanyi, the Party Committee Secretary of the school, explained in detail the content and significance of the Sino-Mongolian Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Assistance and the Sino-Mongolian Agreement on Economic and Technical Assistance.

    After hearing the good news of the signing of the above-mentioned treaty and agreement, the laboring people of Bulgan Province and the staff and workers of the prefabrication factory of wooden structure houses in Sukhbaatar City also held gatherings.

    The Mongolian "Truth Daily" added an editor's note entitled "Symbol of Close Cooperation Between the Mongolian and Chinese Peoples" before reporting the news of mass gatherings in various places, saying: "The friendly visit to our country by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China, has opened a new stage in the fraternal friendship and economic cooperation between the two peoples. All the Mongolian people are delighted with the Chinese government's provision of another huge assistance to our government to accelerate our country's socialist construction and develop its national economy."

### 欢庆中蒙签订友好条约和协定  乌兰巴托等地劳动人民纷纷集会

1960-06-04
第6版()
专栏：

    欢庆中蒙签订友好条约和协定
    乌兰巴托等地劳动人民纷纷集会
    新华社乌兰巴托3日电　据蒙古“真理报”今天报道，蒙古首都乌兰巴托和各地人民纷纷举行集会，庆祝中蒙两国签订友好互助条约和中国给予蒙古经济技术援助的协定。
    乌兰巴托市建筑公司党委书记陶格米德在公司职工举行的集会上讲话说，中国给予蒙古的兄弟般的巨大援助的伟大历史意义在于：它进一步巩固了蒙中人民的永恒友谊，加强了以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结。他代表全体建筑工人表示一定要同兄弟般的中国建筑工人并肩工作，向他们学习更多的东西，争取按计划使建筑物又快、又好、又省地建成。
    蒙古国家马戏院的全体工作人员热烈欢迎中蒙友好互助条约的签订和两国政府发表的联合声明。马戏院的院长阿力尔代在会上就蒙中两国签订条约和协定发表了长篇讲话。演员们对不久将在乌兰巴托市兴建马戏院和其他建筑物感到高兴。他们表示决心要在艺术创作中作出更大的成就。
    在乌兰巴托师范学院举行的师生集会上，学校党委书记白反依详细地介绍了中蒙友好互助条约和中蒙经济技术援助协定的内容和意义。
    布拉干省的劳动人民和苏赫巴托尔市木结构房屋预制厂的职工在听到签订上述条约和协定的喜讯后，也都举行了集会。
    蒙古“真理报”在报道各地举行群众集会的消息前面加上了题为“蒙中人民紧密合作的象征”的编者按语说：“中华人民共和国总理周恩来在我国进行的友好访问，开辟了两国人民兄弟般的友谊和经济合作的新阶段。对于中国政府给予我国政府用以加速我国社会主义建设和发展本国经济的又一次巨大援助，全体蒙古人民感到欢欣鼓舞。
