Title: Japanese Socialist Party Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th: The Japanese Socialist Party Agricultural Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the Chinese Society of Agronomy, arrived in Beijing by train on the 28th for a friendly visit to our country. The delegation consists of ten members and is led by Takechi Takeshi, Director of the Farmers' Department of the Japanese Socialist Party.

Welcoming them at the station were Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Xiao Xiangqian, Deputy Secretary-General, Xi Fengzhou, Secretary-General of the Chinese Society of Agronomy, and others.

Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also at the station to welcome them.

The Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the Chinese Society of Agronomy jointly hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the delegation. Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, delivered a speech at the banquet, welcoming the delegation's visit to our country.

### 日本社会党农业访华代表团到京

1960-06-30
第6版()
专栏：

    日本社会党农业访华代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　应中国人民外交学会和中国农学会邀请前来我国进行友好访问的日本社会党农业访华代表团一行十人，在团长、日本社会党农民部长竹内猛率领下，28日乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪、副秘书长萧向前，中国农学会秘书长席凤洲等。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也到车站欢迎。
    中国人民外交学会和中国农学会今晚共同设宴欢迎代表团。中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之在宴会上致词，对代表团来我国访问表示欢迎。
