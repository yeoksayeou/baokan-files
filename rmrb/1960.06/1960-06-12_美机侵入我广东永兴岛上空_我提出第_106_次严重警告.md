Title: U.S. Military Plane Intrudes into Airspace Over Yongxing Island, Guangdong Province; We Issue 106th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-06-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 11th: At 13:08 to 13:15 on June 11th, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Yongxing Island area of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, a spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 106th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我广东永兴岛上空  我提出第106次严重警告

1960-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我广东永兴岛上空
    我提出第106次严重警告
    新华社11日讯　6月11日十三时零八分至十三时十五分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省永兴岛地区上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百零六次严重警告。
