Title: Jakarta Wartime Authorities Suppress People's Expression of Opinion on the Japan-U.S. Military Treaty; Masses Delivering Statements to the Japanese Embassy Arrested
Author:
Date: 1960-06-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 17th – The Jakarta military authorities announced today that they arrested "a group of people" this morning who were going to deliver a "statement regarding the Japan-U.S. Security Treaty" to the Japanese Embassy in Indonesia.

Antara News Agency quoted Lieutenant Eddy Jajang, a spokesperson for the Jakarta wartime authorities, as saying that these people "violated" regulations restricting political activities. He said that the Jakarta wartime authorities had confiscated the "statement regarding the Japan-U.S. Security Treaty" that was prepared for delivery to the Japanese Embassy.

### 雅加达战时掌权当局  不准人民就日美军事条约表示意见  向日本大使馆递交声明的群众竟遭逮捕

1960-06-19
第6版()
专栏：

    雅加达战时掌权当局
    不准人民就日美军事条约表示意见
    向日本大使馆递交声明的群众竟遭逮捕
    新华社雅加达17日电　雅加达军事当局今天宣布，它在今天上午逮捕了要向日本驻印度尼西亚大使馆递交一份“关于日美安全条约的声明”的“一批人”。
    安塔拉通讯社援引雅加达战时掌权当局发言人埃迪·雅让中尉的话说，这批人“违反”了限制政治活动的条例。他说，雅加达战时掌权者还没收了那些准备递交给日本大使馆的“关于日美安全条约的声明”。
