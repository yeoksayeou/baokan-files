Title: Chen Yi and Liu Lantao Host Banquet to Bid Farewell to Danish Communist Party Chairman Jespersen
Author:
Date: 1960-06-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th June. Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Liu Lantao, alternate secretary of the Secretariat, held a banquet tonight to bid farewell to Knud Jespersen, Chairman of the Danish Communist Party, and his wife, who are about to return home. During the banquet, the hosts and guests had a cordial conversation. Among those present at the banquet were Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, and others. (Attached picture)
Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, held a banquet on June 18 to bid farewell to Jespersen, Chairman of the Danish Communist Party. ### 陈毅刘澜涛设宴欢送丹共主席耶斯佩森

1960-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅刘澜涛设宴欢送丹共主席耶斯佩森
    新华社18日讯　中共中央政治局委员陈毅、书记处候补书记刘澜涛今晚举行宴会，为即将回国的丹麦共产党主席克努德·耶斯佩森夫妇饯行，宴会中，宾主进行了亲切地交谈。出席宴会作陪的，有中央委员刘宁一等人。（附图片）
