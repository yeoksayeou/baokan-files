Title: Premier Zhou Visits Mongolian Lenin Young Pioneer Palace; Mongolian Sports Association Holds Sports Performance to Welcome Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 31st: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi, accompanied by Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, Second Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Tsende, and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Molomjamts, warmly met with children from Ulaanbaatar city at the Mongolian Lenin Young Pioneer Palace this noon. Thousands of children lined up at the entrance of the Young Pioneer Palace, cheering and paying their respects to Premier Zhou.

After the visit, Premier Zhou said to the children amidst warm applause: "Thank you, children, I wish you good health, good studies, and good labor. I wish you a happy Children's Day."

When Premier Zhou arrived at the Young Pioneer Palace, the children presented flowers to Premier Zhou. The children played trumpets and drums, waving the flowers and red flags in their hands, and let out long cheers.

In the reception room of the Young Pioneer Palace, Young Pioneer Tseren, on behalf of all Young Pioneers, paid the highest Young Pioneer salute to Premier Zhou. A group of children rushed forward and tied red scarves on Premier Zhou and each of the honored guest uncles.

Premier Zhou visited each study group and recreational group in the Young Pioneer Palace with great interest. Premier Zhou praised the small tools and embroidery made by the Mongolian children. In the chess room, Premier Zhou, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and the children played chess. In the photography study room, the young photographer Tsetsleger took photos of Premier Zhou, Chairman Tsedenbal, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and others. When Premier Zhou visited the radio study group, a young telegraph operator sent a telegram that read "Long Live Mongolian-Chinese Friendship."

Wherever Premier Zhou went, the children applauded, cheered, and warmly welcomed the Chinese guest uncles. Premier Zhou had cordial conversations with the children and gave them golden Mao Zedong commemorative badges.

Premier Zhou also watched song and dance performances by the Young Pioneers.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 31st: The Central Council of the Mongolian Sports Association held a sports performance at the gymnasium this noon to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai.

Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, Tsedenbal, Second Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Tsende, and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Molomjamts, accompanied the viewing of the sports performance.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 31st: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi, accompanied by First Deputy Chairman Molomjamts and others, visited the Mongolian Central Museum this morning. (Attached picture)

China and Mongolia signed the Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Assistance, the Agreement on Economic and Technical Assistance, and the Joint Statement of the two governments. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai and Chairman Tsedenbal signing the Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Assistance on behalf of their respective governments.

### 周总理参观蒙古列宁少先宫  蒙古体育运动协会举行体育表演欢迎周总理

1960-06-01
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理参观蒙古列宁少先宫
    蒙古体育运动协会举行体育表演欢迎周总理
    新华社乌兰巴托31日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理由蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔、蒙古人民革命党中央第二书记曾德、部长会议第一副主席莫洛姆扎木茨陪同，今天中午在蒙古列宁少先宫亲切地会见了乌兰巴托市的少年儿童们。成千的少年儿童在少先宫门口列队向周总理欢呼致敬。
    周总理参观以后，在热烈的掌声中向儿童们说：“谢谢小朋友们，祝你们身体好，学习好，劳动好，祝你们好好过儿童节。”
    当周总理来到少先宫时，少年儿童向周总理献花。这时儿童们吹号打鼓，摇着手里的鲜花、红旗，发出长时间的欢呼声。
    在少先宫的接待室里，少先队员策伦代表全体少先队员向周总理致以最崇高的少先队敬礼。一群儿童涌上前去，为周总理和每一位贵宾伯伯系上了红领巾。
    周总理很有兴致地参观了少先宫的每个学习小组、文娱小组。周总理赞扬蒙古少年们作的小工具、刺绣品。在象棋室里，周总理、陈毅副总理和少年儿童们进行象棋比赛。在摄影学习室里，小摄影师策策勒格为周总理、泽登巴尔主席、陈毅副总理等照了相。周总理参观无线电学习小组时，小发报员发出了“蒙中友谊万岁”的电报。
    周总理走到那里，那里的少年儿童们就鼓掌、欢呼，热烈欢迎中国贵宾伯伯。周总理同儿童们亲切地谈话，把金色的毛泽东纪念章送给他们。
    周总理还观看了少先队员们的歌舞表演。
    新华社乌兰巴托31日电　蒙古体育运动协会中央理事会今天中午在体育馆举行体育表演，欢迎周恩来总理。
    蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔、蒙古人民革命党中央第二书记曾德、部长会议第一副主席莫洛姆扎木茨陪同观看了体育表演。
    新华社乌兰巴托31日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理由莫洛姆扎木茨第一副主席等陪同，今天上午参观了蒙古中央博物馆。（附图片）
