Title: U.S. Planes and Warships Continue to Provoke Soviet Ships
Author:
Date: 1960-06-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Odessa, June 11th (TASS) -- Further reports have emerged here regarding incidents of U.S. military aircraft and destroyers rudely violating freedom of navigation on the high seas.

According to reports from the Soviet ship "Slavgorod," at 15:35 (Moscow time) on June 8th, a U.S. twin-engine military seaplane, number 128374, flew low over the ship at the southern tip of the Morea Peninsula, nearly striking the ship's mast, before flying towards Greece. In addition, two destroyers flying the U.S. flag chased the Soviet ship "Sukhona" in the Indian Ocean and used light signals to intimidate the Soviet sailors.

### 美机美舰继续向苏联轮船挑衅

1960-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

     美机美舰继续向苏联轮船挑衅
  　　新华社11日讯　塔斯社敖德萨11日讯：这里又公布了关于美国军用飞机和驱击舰粗暴地破坏公海航行自由的事件。
　　据苏联“斯拉夫戈罗德号”轮船报告，6月8日十五时三十五分（莫斯科时间），在摩里亚半岛南端，美国一二八三七四号双引擎军用水上飞机在这艘船上空低空飞行，几乎撞上船上的桅杆，然后向希腊方面飞去。此外，有两艘挂着美国国旗的驱击舰在印度洋追逐苏联“苏霍纳号”轮船，并用灯光讯号恫吓苏联海员。
