Title: After the Closing of the World Federation of Trade Unions Council Meeting, Grishin and Other Delegates Return Home; Toledano and Other Delegates Go to the Northeast for a Tour and Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-06-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 10th June: After attending the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) Council meeting, WFTU Vice Presidents V.V. Grishin (USSR), Benoît Frachon (France), S.A. Dange (India), Loga-Sowinski (Poland), and Pastoriño (Uruguay), WFTU Secretariat Secretaries Grassi (Italy), Theodoroscu (Romania), Brua (France), and representatives and special guests from countries such as the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Romania, Hungary, Haiti, Ecuador, Congo, Zanzibar, Ivory Coast, Portuguese Guinea, Lebanon, France, Italy, and a portion of the representatives and special guests from the Soviet Union, Cuba, Chile, Cameroon, and some WFTU staff members, left Beijing by plane today to return home. Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Vice Chairmen Liu Changsheng and Zhu Xuefan, Secretariat Secretary Li Zaiwen, and others were at the airport to see them off.

Prior to this, representatives and special guests from countries and regions such as Vietnam, Black Africa, Somaliland, Madagascar, Italy, and Luxembourg had already left Beijing by plane to return to their countries.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th June: After attending the WFTU Council meeting, WFTU Vice President Vicente Lombardo Toledano (Mexico) and representatives and special guests from countries such as Brazil, Argentina, Venezuela, Peru, Colombia, Dominican Republic, Costa Rica, Honduras, Martinique, Guadeloupe, Ghana, Sudan, Ubangi-Shari, Mali Federation, Mauritius, Cyprus, Austria, Australia, Spain, and a portion of the representatives and special guests from the Soviet Union, Cuba, Chile, and Cameroon, accompanied by Li Jiebo, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, took a train to the Northeast today for a tour and visit.

### 世界工联理事会议闭幕后  格里申等部分代表回国  托列达诺等代表去东北参观访问

1960-06-11
第6版()
专栏：

    世界工联理事会议闭幕后
    格里申等部分代表回国
    托列达诺等代表去东北参观访问
    新华社10日讯　出席世界工联理事会会议的世界工联副主席格里申（苏联）、贝诺瓦·弗拉商（法国）、丹吉（印度）、洛加—索文斯基（波兰）、巴斯多利诺（乌拉圭），世界工联书记处书记葛拉西（意大利）、戴奥多里斯古（罗马尼亚）、布拉（法国）和德意志民主共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、海地、厄瓜多尔、刚果、桑给巴尔、象牙海岸、葡属几内亚、黎巴嫩、法国、意大利等国家的代表和特邀代表，和苏联、古巴、智利、喀麦隆的一部分代表和特邀代表，以及世界工联一部分工作人员，在今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有中华全国总工会主席刘宁一，副主席刘长胜、朱学范，书记处书记栗再温等。
    在此以前，已有越南、黑非、索马里兰、马达加斯加、意大利、卢森堡等国家和地区的代表和特邀代表分别乘飞机离开北京返国。
    新华社10日讯　出席世界工联理事会会议的世界工联副主席维森特·隆巴多·托列达诺（墨西哥）和巴西、阿根廷、委内瑞拉、秘鲁、哥伦比亚、多米尼加、哥斯达黎加、洪都拉斯、马提尼克、瓜得罗普、加纳、苏丹、乌班沙吉利、马里联邦、毛里求斯、塞浦路斯、奥地利、澳大利亚、西班牙等国的代表和特邀代表，以及苏联、古巴、智利、喀麦隆的一部分代表和特邀代表，在中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯陪同下，今天乘火车去东北参观访问。
