Title: Guo Moruo Receives Heinz Haack
Author:
Date: 1960-06-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received Heinz Haack, member of the German Democratic Republic education delegation and head of the Science Department of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, this morning and discussed strengthening scientific cooperation between the Chinese and German Academies of Sciences in the future.

Xie Xinhe, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Wang Tuo, Director of the Foreign Liaison Bureau, were present at the reception.
