Title: Part of the Congolese Political Party Delegation to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st. A partial delegation, consisting of three members, from the Congolese political party delegation to China, who are visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are the heads of the Congolese General Alliance and the People's Party. The other nine members of the delegation arrived in our country on May 28 and are currently visiting the Northeast.

### 刚果政党访华代表团部分团员到京

1960-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果政党访华代表团部分团员到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的刚果政党访华代表团部分团员共三人，于今日下午乘飞机抵达北京。他们是刚果总联盟和人民党的负责人。代表团的其他成员九人已经在5月28日来到我国，现正在东北访问。
