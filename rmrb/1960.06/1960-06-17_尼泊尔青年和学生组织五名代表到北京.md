Title: Five Representatives of Nepalese Youth and Student Organizations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: Five representatives of Nepalese youth and student organizations, K. P. Shrestha, M. R. Shrestha, D. R. Dhakwa, P. R. Shrachand, and G. N. S. Dawadi, invited by the All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 尼泊尔青年和学生组织五名代表到北京

1960-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔青年和学生组织五名代表到北京
    新华社16日讯　应中华全国青年联合会和中华全国学生联合会邀请前来我国访问的尼泊尔青年和学生组织代表克·普·什雷斯塔、姆·耳·什雷斯塔、德·尔·达克瓦、普·尔·什拉琴德、格·恩·斯·达瓦迪等五人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
