Title: U.S. Warships and Military Aircraft Invade Our Territorial Waters and Airspace; I Issue the 115th Serious Warning

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th: From 20:16 to 22:44 on June 28th, five U.S. warships intruded into the sea area of the Dan Gan and Jia Peng Islands in Guangdong Province. From 07:09 to 07:20 on June 29th, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the sea south of Shanwei, Guangdong Province. Regarding these military provocations by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 115th serious warning.

### 美国军舰和军用飞机侵入我领海领空  我提出第115次严重警告

1960-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军舰和军用飞机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第115次严重警告
    新华社29日讯　6月28日二十时十六分至二十二时四十四分，美国军舰五艘，侵入我广东省担杆、佳蓬列岛地区海域。29日七时零九分至七时二十分美国军用飞机一架侵入我广东省汕尾以南海域上空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百一十五次严重警告。
