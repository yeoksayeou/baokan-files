Title: Guo Moruo Receives Japanese Painter Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 17th: Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the Japanese painter delegation this morning. The delegation was led by Maeda Seison, head of the delegation, and Shirahama Hitoshi, the deputy head, along with all the members of the delegation.
    Present at the meeting were Lian Guan, Ye Qianyu, Lin Lin, Jin Ziming and others.
    Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also present.

### 郭沫若接见日本画家代表团

1960-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本画家代表团
    新华社17日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，今天上午接见了日本画家代表团团长前田青邨、副团长白石凡和全体团员。
    在座的有连贯、叶浅予、林林、金子明等人。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
