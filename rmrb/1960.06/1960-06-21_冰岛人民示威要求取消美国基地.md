Title: Icelandic People Demonstrate, Demanding the Removal of the American Base
Author:
Date: 1960-06-21
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 20th: Reykjavik News: Several hundred citizens of the Icelandic capital held a protest rally and a long march on the 19th, demanding the removal of the American military base in Keflavik, Iceland, and calling for Iceland to withdraw from the North Atlantic Treaty Organization.
    They marched twenty miles from Reykjavik to Keflavik.

### 冰岛人民示威要求取消美国基地

1960-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

    冰岛人民示威要求取消美国基地
    新华社柏林20日电　雷克雅未克消息：冰岛首都好几百个市民19日举行了一次抗议集会和长途游行，要求取消美国在冰岛凯夫拉维克的军事基地，并要求冰岛退出北大西洋集团。
    他们由雷克雅未克步行二十哩游行到凯夫拉维克。
