Title: Demanding Land Allocation to Landless Cultivators: Farmers in Rajasthan, India, Demonstrate; Police Suppress with Tear Gas; Sixty-Eight People Arrested
Author:
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 6

    Demanding Land Allocation to Landless Cultivators
    Farmers in Rajasthan, India, Demonstrate
    Police Suppress with Tear Gas; Sixty-Eight People Arrested
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 28th - According to a report in the "Hindustan Times," farmers in the Bikaner district of Rajasthan, India, held demonstrations to demand land allocation to landless farmers.
    According to reports, in the Hanumangarh sub-division of Bikaner district, when land auctions were being conducted, approximately five hundred members of the Farmers' Association surrounded the Immigration Bureau there on May 24th. They opposed the auction of land and demanded that the land be allocated to landless cultivators. The demonstrators held up signs opposing the restoration of the betterment levy and opposing the auction of land.
    The Sub-Divisional Magistrate of Hanumangarh ordered the police to use tear gas to disperse the demonstrators.
    According to the "Hindustan Times" on the 27th, citing official sources, sixty-eight people were arrested on the 25th following the demonstration.
    The report said that the Hanumangarh Bar Association passed a resolution condemning the police's suppressive measures and demanding a judicial investigation into the incident.

### 要求把土地分配给无地的耕种者  印度拉贾斯坦邦农民示威  警察用催泪弹镇压　六十八名群众被捕

1960-06-01
第6版()
专栏：

    要求把土地分配给无地的耕种者
    印度拉贾斯坦邦农民示威
    警察用催泪弹镇压　六十八名群众被捕
    新华社新德里28日电　据“印度斯坦时报”报道，印度拉贾斯坦邦比加尼尔区的农民曾为要求把土地分配给无地农民举行了示威。
    据报道，在比加尼尔区的哈努曼加尔分区，当进行土地拍卖的时候，大约有五百名农民协会的人员在5月24日包围了那里的移民管理局。他们反对拍卖土地，要求把土地分配给无地的耕种者。示威者高举着标语，表示反对恢复征收改善税，反对拍卖土地。
    哈努曼加尔分区行政长官命令警察使用催泪弹驱散示威者。
    据“印度斯坦时报”27日援引官方消息，在这次示威后，有六十八人在25日被捕。
    消息说，哈努曼加尔律师协会通过决议，谴责警察的镇压手段，并且要求司法机关调查这一事件。
