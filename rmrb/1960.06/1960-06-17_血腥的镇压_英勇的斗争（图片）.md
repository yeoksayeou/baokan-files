Bloody Suppression, Heroic Struggle

On June 15th, the Kishi Nobusuke government created a massive tragedy in Tokyo in which armed police and fascist thugs beat to death and injured demonstrating masses.

The Kishi government's fascist atrocities aroused the anger of the Japanese people. Tokyo masses immediately launched vigorous protest demonstrations.

Six thousand students held a gathering inside the south gate of the House of Representatives, silently mourning their heroic fallen comrades, and singing the Internationale in unison.

From this set of pictures, you can see the bloody crimes of the Kishi Nobusuke government in suppressing the people and the heroic struggle of the Japanese people.

Angry demonstrators attack the south gate of the House of Representatives.

Demonstrators held a protest rally inside the south gate of the House of Representatives.

Students rose up in self-defense and burned police cars.

Police used water cannons to spray demonstrators.

Demonstrating students injured by the police.　　　　　　Tokyo transmitted photographs (Xinhua News Agency)

### 血腥的镇压  英勇的斗争（图片）

1960-06-17
第5版()
专栏：

    血腥的镇压  英勇的斗争
    6月15日，岸信介政府在东京制造了武装警察和法西斯暴徒打死打伤示威群众的大惨案。
    岸政府的法西斯暴行激起了日本人民的愤怒。东京群众立即展开了强有力的抗议示威。
    六千名学生在众议院南便门内举行集会，为英勇牺牲的战友默祷，并且合唱国际歌。
    从这组图片中，可以看到岸信介政府镇压人民的血腥罪行和日本人民英勇斗争的情况。
    愤怒的示威群众攻击众议院的南便门。
    示威群众在众议院南便门内举行了抗议集会。
    学生们奋起自卫，烧毁了警车。
    警察用水龙喷射示威群众。
    被警察打伤的示威学生。　　　　　　东京传真照片（新华社发）
