Title: Turkish Government Deeply in Debt
Author:
Date: 1960-06-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ankara, June 20th: Turkish military government Finance Minister Alican said that Turkey's foreign debt has reached $1,354,000,000. This year's fiscal deficit has reached $178,000,000.

Turkey's massive debt is the evil consequence of the Menderes government's participation in aggressive blocs and following the United States' policy of arms expansion and war preparation.

### 土耳其政府债台高筑

1960-06-23
第6版()
专栏：

    土耳其政府债台高筑
    据新华社20日讯　安卡拉消息：土耳其军政府财政部长阿利坎说土耳其欠外国的国债已达到十三亿五千四百万美元。今年财政赤字达到一亿七千八百万美元。
    土耳其积欠巨额债务是曼德列斯政府参加侵略集团、追随美国的扩军备战政策所造成的恶果。
