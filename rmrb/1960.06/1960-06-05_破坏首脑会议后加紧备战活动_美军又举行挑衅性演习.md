Title: Stepping Up War Preparations After Sabotaging the Summit, US Military Holds Provocative Exercises Again
Author:
Date: 1960-06-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd, Washington Dispatch: U.S. Secretary of Defense Gates revealed yesterday during a closed-door session of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee that U.S. military forces began another "alert exercise" preparing for war on the night of June 1st, which will "last for several days." He also announced that the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff had recommended to him that the United States should "frequently conduct such alert exercises without prior notice."

### 破坏首脑会议后加紧备战活动  美军又举行挑衅性演习

1960-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    破坏首脑会议后加紧备战活动
    美军又举行挑衅性演习
    据新华社3日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国防部长盖茨昨天在参议院外交委员会秘密会议上作证时透露，美国军事部队1日晚上开始又一次举行准备战争的所谓“戒备演习”，这次演习将“进行几天”。他还宣布，美国参谋长联席会议已经向他建议，美国要“经常在事先不通知的情况下进行这种戒备演习”。
