Contemporary Hero

    The workers of the Youth Lathe Team of the Machine Workshop of the Tianjin No. 1 Machine Tool Factory completed more than 550 innovation projects in the first five months of this year, fulfilling the annual production task ahead of schedule. This is them installing a homemade vertical milling head on a horizontal milling machine, making it capable of horizontal milling, vertical milling, and slot milling, achieving multiple uses for one machine.

                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Yinghua photographed
