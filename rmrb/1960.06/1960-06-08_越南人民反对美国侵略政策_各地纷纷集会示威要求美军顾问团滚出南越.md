Title: Vietnamese People Oppose US Aggression Policy, Rallies and Demonstrations Held in Various Places Demanding the US Military Advisory Group Get Out of South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-06-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 7th - The people of Northern Vietnam have continued to hold rallies and demonstrations in recent days, opposing the US imperialists' sabotage of the Four-Power Summit, their expansion of armaments and war preparations in South Vietnam, and the US-Ngo Dinh Diem group's massacre of the people.

According to reports in local newspapers today, 13,000 residents of Yi'an City and its suburbs held a rally on the morning of the 5th. The rally passed a resolution warmly welcoming the Soviet Union's solemn stance at the Four-Power Summit preparatory meeting, strongly condemning the US imperialists' crimes in sabotaging the Four-Power Summit, and demanding that the "US Military Assistance Advisory Group" be driven out of South Vietnam. The participants in the rally also passed a proposal demanding that the International Commission re-examine its inappropriate conclusion regarding South Vietnam's Ordinance No. 10 of 1959.

After the rally, the participants also held a demonstration in the streets of the city.

In the Hong Gai mining area, nearly 1,000 representatives of various organizations and people from all walks of life also held a rally on the 5th, opposing the US imperialists' sabotage of the Four-Power Government Summit, strongly condemning the Ngo Dinh Diem group's promulgation of Ordinance No. 10 of 1959, and demanding that the International Commission rescind its incorrect conclusion regarding this fascist ordinance.

On the 3rd, nearly 1,000 employees of various organizations in Hai Ninh Province also held a similar rally.

### 越南人民反对美国侵略政策  各地纷纷集会示威要求美军顾问团滚出南越

1960-06-08
第7版()
专栏：

    越南人民反对美国侵略政策
    各地纷纷集会示威要求美军顾问团滚出南越
    新华社河内7日电　越南北方各地人民连日继续举行集会和示威游行，反对美帝国主义破坏四国首脑会议、在南越扩军备战以及美国—吴庭艳集团残杀人民。
    据此间报纸今天报道，宜安市和市郊居民一万三千人，5日上午举行大会，大会通过决议，热烈欢迎苏联在四国首脑会议预备会议上的严正立场，强烈谴责美帝国主义破坏四国首脑会议的罪行，并要求把“美国军事援助顾问团”赶出南越。参加大会的人还通过一项建议书，要求国际委员会重新审查它对南越当局1959年第十号法令所作出的不适当的结论。
    大会结束后，参加大会的人还在市内各街道举行游行示威。
    在鸿基矿区，各机关团体和各阶层人民的代表近一千人，也在5日举行集会，反对美帝国主义破坏四国政府首脑会议，强烈谴责吴庭艳集团颁行1959年第十号法令，要求国际委员会取消对这个法西斯法令所作出的不正确的结论。
    3日，海宁省各机关团体的职工近一千人也举行了类似的集会。
