Poetry - A Weapon of Struggle
Gu Gong
    We stand here, we recite poetry.
    Our poetry, is dynamite, is a rocket,
    Is a weapon of struggle,
    Is a torch lighting everyone's eyes,
    Lighting the fire in everyone's heart.
    Our poetry, supports the marching crowds in Tokyo,
    Supports the fighting ranks in Cuba,
    Supports Latin America,
    Supports Algeria,
    Supports the shepherds and coolies of Turkey,
    Supports all miners, all slaves,
    Supports all who want to break free from iron chains,
    Supports the colonies that have suffered hardship and been ravaged!
    Whoever spits, spitting at the scalps of traitors;
    Whoever aims the gun, aiming at the heinous American imperialism,
    Our poetry, will embrace his shoulders,
    And say warmly: Brother, dear brother!
    Our poetry, will knock off the hooves of the invaders,
    Will shoot down U-2 type aircraft,
    Will destroy hydrogen bombs and spy satellites,
    Will eradicate American military bases!
    Our poetry, will fly to the White House and the Pentagon,
    Will dispel the lingering spirits of Hitler,
    Will bury the fascists wandering in Washington.
    We stand here, we recite poetry.
    Our poetry, is truth, is justice,
    Is a weapon of struggle,
    Is a torch lighting everyone's eyes,
    Lighting the fire in everyone's heart!

### 诗——战斗的武器
顾工
1960-06-17
第8版()
专栏：

    诗——战斗的武器
    顾工我们站在这里，我们朗诵诗句。我们的诗句，是炸药，是火箭，是战斗的武器，是点亮每个人的目光点亮每个人心灵的火炬。我们的诗句，支持东京的游行队伍，支持古巴的战斗行列，支持拉丁美洲，支持阿尔及利亚，支持土耳其的牧羊人和苦力，支持一切矿工，一切奴隶，支持一切想挣脱铁索链的人，支持受尽苦难、受尽蹂躏的殖民地！谁把吐沫，吐向卖国贼的头皮；谁把枪口，瞄准万恶的美帝国主义，我们的诗句，便会抱着他的肩膀，热烈地说：兄弟，亲爱的兄弟！我们的诗句，要敲掉侵略者的马蹄，要击落U—2型的飞机，要销毁氢弹和间谍卫星，要铲掉美国的军事基地！我们的诗句，要飞到白宫和五角大楼，要驱散希特勒的阴魂，要埋葬在华盛顿游荡的法西斯蒂。我们站在这里，我们朗诵诗句。我们的诗句，是真理，是正义，是战斗的武器，是点亮每个人的目光，点亮每个人心灵的火炬！
