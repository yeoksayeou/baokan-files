Title: CPC Central Statement Supports the Struggle of the Okinawa People; Protests Eisenhower's "Visit" to Okinawa
Author:
Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 17th: The Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party issued a statement today protesting Eisenhower's planned "visit" to Okinawa and supporting the Okinawa people's protest struggle against Eisenhower's trip to Okinawa.
    The statement pointed out that Eisenhower's visit to Okinawa is to strengthen the American military forces there, build more missile bases on the island, and strengthen American rule over the people of Okinawa.
    The statement strongly protested the behavior of the "Chairman" of the Ryukyu puppet government in prohibiting the people of Okinawa from holding protest rallies and demonstrations against Eisenhower's "visit" to Okinawa.
    The statement said that the Japanese Communist Party wholeheartedly supports the just struggle of the Okinawa people against Eisenhower's "visit." All democratic forces in Japan should lodge strong protests with the U.S. government and the Nobusuke Kishi cabinet regarding Eisenhower's trip to Okinawa.

### 日共中央声明支持冲绳人民斗争  抗议艾森豪威尔“访问”冲绳

1960-06-18
第5版()
专栏：

    日共中央声明支持冲绳人民斗争
    抗议艾森豪威尔“访问”冲绳
    新华社17日讯　东京消息：日本共产党中央委员会今天发表声明，抗议艾森豪威尔计划“访问”冲绳，并且支持冲绳人民为反对艾森豪威尔冲绳之行而举行抗议斗争。
    声明指出，艾森豪威尔到冲绳去，是为了加强那里的美国军事力量，在这个岛上建造更多的导弹基地和加强美国对冲绳岛人民的统治。
    声明强烈抗议琉球傀儡政府“主席”禁止冲绳人民举行反对艾森豪威尔“访问”冲绳的抗议大会和示威游行的行为。
    声明说，日本共产党衷心支持冲绳人民反对艾森豪威尔“访问”的正义斗争。日本一切民主力量应当就艾森豪威尔去冲绳一事向美国政府和岸信介内阁提出强烈的抗议。
