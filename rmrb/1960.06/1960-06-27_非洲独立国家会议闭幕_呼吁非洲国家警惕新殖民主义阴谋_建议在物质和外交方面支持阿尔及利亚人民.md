Title: African Independent States Conference Concludes: Calls on African Countries to be Vigilant Against Neo-Colonialist Plots; Suggests Providing Material and Diplomatic Support to the Algerian People
Author:
Date: 1960-06-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Addis Ababa, June 25th: The Second Conference of Independent African States concluded in Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia, on the evening of the 24th. The conference passed a series of resolutions expressing fidelity to the United Nations Charter, the Declaration of Human Rights, and the Bandung Declaration, and called on African countries to be vigilant against the plots of neo-colonialism.

The conference began on the 14th and was attended by representatives and observers from twenty African countries.

In a resolution on the Algerian question, the conference recommended that all independent African states support the cause of the Algerian people materially and diplomatically, and send delegations to capitals around the world to seek support for the Algerian cause. The conference expressed its opposition to NATO countries supporting France's aggression against Algeria with weapons.

In another resolution, the conference condemned the apartheid policy practiced by the Union of South Africa. The conference called on participating countries to comprehensively boycott the Union of South Africa, such as severing diplomatic relations with it, boycotting all South African goods, and not allowing aircraft belonging to South African companies to fly over their countries' airspace, and so on.

The conference called for measures to be taken to prevent France from testing atomic weapons in the Sahara or other African territories.

The conference also decided to establish a special fund to assist "fighters for freedom" throughout Africa, which would be provided by various independent African countries.

The conference also passed resolutions on accelerating the economic development of Africa, among other things.

### 非洲独立国家会议闭幕  呼吁非洲国家警惕新殖民主义阴谋  建议在物质和外交方面支持阿尔及利亚人民

1960-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    非洲独立国家会议闭幕
    呼吁非洲国家警惕新殖民主义阴谋
    建议在物质和外交方面支持阿尔及利亚人民
    新华社25日讯　亚的斯亚贝巴消息：第二届非洲独立国家会议24日晚间在埃塞俄比亚首都亚的斯亚贝巴闭幕。会议通过一系列决议，表示忠于联合国宪章、人权宣言和万隆宣言，并且呼吁非洲国家警惕新殖民主义的阴谋。
    这次会议是14日开始的，出席会议的有来自非洲二十个国家的代表和观察员。
    会议在关于阿尔及利亚问题的决议中，建议所有非洲独立国家在物质和外交方面支持阿尔及利亚人民的事业，并且派遣代表团到世界各国首都寻求对阿尔及利亚事业的支持。会议表示反对北大西洋公约组织国家以武器支持法国侵略阿尔及利亚。
    会议在另一项决议中谴责了南非联邦实行的种族隔离政策。会议要求与会各国全面抵制南非联邦。如断绝同它的外交关系、抵制一切南非商品、不让属于南非公司的飞机飞越它们国家的领空，等等。
    会议号召采取措施阻止法国在撒哈拉或其他非洲领土上试验原子武器。
    会议还决定建立一笔特别基金来援助非洲各地“为自由而斗争的战士”，这笔基金由各非洲独立国家提供。
    会议还通过关于加速发展非洲经济等决议。
