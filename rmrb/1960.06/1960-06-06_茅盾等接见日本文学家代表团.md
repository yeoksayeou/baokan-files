Title: Mao Dun and Others Receive Delegation of Japanese Writers
Author:
Date: 1960-06-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th June. Mao Dun, Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, and Yang Hansheng, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, met this morning with the Japanese literary delegation, including Hiroshi Noma, Katsuiichiro Kamei, Yoko Matsuoka, Minoru Takeuchi, Ken Kaiko, and Kenzaburo Oe, and held cordial talks.
    Present at the meeting were Lao She, Shao Quanlin, Xie Bingxin, Yan Wenjing, Guo Xiaochuan, Yang Shuo, Wang Yafan, Lou Shiyi, Yuan Shuipai, Li Yingru, Ruan Zhangjing, Ji Xianlin, and Xu Chi.

### 茅盾等接见日本文学家代表团

1960-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    茅盾等接见日本文学家代表团
    新华社5日讯　中国作家协会主席茅盾、中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，今天上午会见了日本文学家代表团野间宏、龟井胜一郎、松冈洋子、竹内实、开高健和大江健三郎，并且进行了亲切的谈话。
    会见时在座的，有老舍、邵荃麟、谢冰心、严文井、郭小川、杨朔、王亚凡、楼适夷、袁水拍、李英儒、阮章竞、季羡林和徐迟等。
