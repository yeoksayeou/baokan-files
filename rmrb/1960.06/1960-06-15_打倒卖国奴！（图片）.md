Title: Down with the Traitors! (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-15
Page: 5

Down with the Traitors!
    On May 24, 30,000 people held a demonstration in Osaka, Japan, protesting against the Kishi Nobusuke clique's illegal and forcible passage of the Japan-U.S. military alliance treaty. The demonstrating masses held a model of Kishi Nobusuke, on which was written: "Bury the traitor!"

### 打倒卖国奴！（图片）

1960-06-15
第5版()
专栏：

    打倒卖国奴！
    五月二十四日，曰本大阪三万人举行示威集会，抗议岸信介集团非法强行通过日美军事同盟条约的暴行。示威群众拿着岸信介的模型，上面写着：“埋葬卖国奴！”
