Title: China's Chief Representative Yao Zhongming and Others Arrive in Rangoon to Attend the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-06-26
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 24th: The Chinese delegation to the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee meeting, led by Chief Representative Yao Zhongming, arrived here by plane today.
    According to the agreement between the Chinese and Burmese governments on the boundary issue between the two countries, the Joint Committee, composed of representatives agreed upon by both China and Burma, is scheduled to hold its first meeting in Rangoon on June 27th.
    Those who welcomed the Chinese delegation at the airport included Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the Burmese Chief Representative to the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, Colonel So Min, Director of the Burmese Border Areas Administration, U Wang, advisor to Prime Minister U Nu, and other senior officials.
    Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang and Burmese Ambassador to China Kyao Winn also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 参加中缅边界联合委员会会议  我国首席代表姚仲明等抵仰光

1960-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    参加中缅边界联合委员会会议
    我国首席代表姚仲明等抵仰光
    新华社仰光24日电　中国参加中缅边界联合委员会会议的代表团在首席代表姚仲明率领下，今天乘飞机到达这里。
    根据中缅两国政府关于两国边界问题的协定，由中缅双方同意所组成的联合委员会，定于6月27日在仰光举行第一次会议。
    到机场欢迎中国代表团的有：参加中缅边界联合委员会的缅甸首席代表、缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将，缅甸边境地区管理局局长苏敏上校，吴努总理顾问吴旺和其他高级官员。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和缅甸驻中国大使叫温也到机场欢迎。
