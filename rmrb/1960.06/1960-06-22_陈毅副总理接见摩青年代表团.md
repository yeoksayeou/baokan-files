Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Moroccan Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-06-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Moroccan Youth League delegation this morning, including the head of the delegation, Abdellah Soussi, and members Brahim Moustakim, Abdellahad El Alaoui, Mouhieddine Omar, and Barada Hamid. They held friendly talks. Present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, and Yang Haibo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League.

### 陈毅副总理接见摩青年代表团

1960-06-22
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见摩青年代表团
    新华社21日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了摩洛哥青年联盟代表团团长阿卜杜拉克·苏西，团员：布拉希姆·穆斯塔金、阿卜杜拉哈德·艾拉乌尼、穆希丁·奥马尔、巴拉达·哈米德，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有：中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，共青团中央书记处书记杨海波。
