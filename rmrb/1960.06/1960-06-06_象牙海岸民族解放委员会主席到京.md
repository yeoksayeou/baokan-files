Title: Chairman of the National Liberation Committee of Ivory Coast Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th - At the invitation of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity, Assi Camille Adam, Chairman of the National Liberation Committee of Ivory Coast, arrived in Beijing today for a visit to our country.

### 象牙海岸民族解放委员会主席到京

1960-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    象牙海岸民族解放委员会主席到京
    新华社5日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请来我国访问的象牙海岸民族解放委员会主席阿西·卡米尔·阿当，在今天到达北京。
