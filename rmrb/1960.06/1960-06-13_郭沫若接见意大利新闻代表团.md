Title: Guo Moruo Receives Italian News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-06-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 12th - Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the Italian journalist delegation headed by Sergio Segre on the morning of the 11th and had a friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Mei Yi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, Zhou You, Secretary of the Secretariat, Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 郭沫若接见意大利新闻代表团

1960-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见意大利新闻代表团
    新华社12日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，11日上午接见了以塞尔乔·塞格莱为首的意大利新闻工作者代表团，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席梅益、书记处书记周游，外交部新闻司副司长康矛召，中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深。
