Title: U.S. Government Slanders Cuban Government and President; Cuba Exposes Another U.S. Aggressive Act
Author:
Date: 1960-06-07
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, June 4th. The threatening note that the U.S. State Department delivered to the Cuban Foreign Ministry today has stirred up strong protests in Cuba. In this note, the U.S. government barbarously attacked the Cuban government for carrying out a so-called "slander campaign" against the United States, and slandered the Cuban President Dorticos's legitimate condemnation of the United States during his visit to South America as "untrue."

The Cuban Foreign Ministry announced this afternoon: "The Cuban government resolutely rejects the various arguments in the press statement issued in Washington today and will respond to each point contained in the note at the appropriate time."

At the same time, an official of the Cuban Foreign Ministry told the press that "this hysterical statement may be a signal for a series of aggressive activities against the Cuban people and the revolutionary government. It should arouse the vigilance of world public opinion." The official said, "The enthusiastic crowds in Latin America welcoming Dorticos were not seen during Eisenhower's visit." He then pointed out that the U.S. authorities are attempting to weaken the impact of President Dorticos's visit to Latin America.

Juan Marinello, Chairman of the Cuban Popular Socialist Party, gave a televised speech tonight on the U.S. note. He said, "The U.S. note is a new attack on Cuba. It will be met with an even stronger counterattack from the people of the whole country. Imperialism may carry out all kinds of aggression in the future. But the revolutionary government will be good at maintaining independence. The note may become more and more aggressive, and the people's response will become more and more resolute."

Havana newspapers strongly condemned the aggressive U.S. note. "Hoy" pointed out in an editorial that the consequences of U.S. imperialism's arrival in Cuba can be briefly summarized as: bringing Cuba to a state of semi-colonial backwardness, hunger, poverty, and humiliation, while the imperialists made a fortune. Now, when Cuba rises up to get rid of its tragic past, the representatives of U.S. imperialism threaten, abuse, and prepare to carry out aggression. "Hoy" emphasized that Cuba does not allow others to slander its president. All of Cuba, like our government, resolutely rejects Washington's arrogant and rude note.

"Combate" believes that the aggressive U.S. note is "a preliminary step in preparation for an armed invasion of Cuba."

### 美国政府诬蔑古巴政府和总统  古巴揭露美国又一侵略性行为

1960-06-07
第7版()
专栏：

    美国政府诬蔑古巴政府和总统
    古巴揭露美国又一侵略性行为
    新华社哈瓦那4日电　美国国务院今天交给古巴外交部的威胁性照会，在古巴激起了强烈的抗议。美国政府在这个照会里蛮横攻击古巴政府进行了所谓对美国的“诽谤运动”，并且诬蔑正在南美访问的古巴总统多尔蒂科斯对美国的正当谴责是“不真实的”。
    古巴外交部今天下午宣布：“古巴政府断然拒绝今天在华盛顿发表的报界声明中的各种论点，并将在适当的时候对照会所包含的各点一一予以回答。”
    同时古巴外交部的一位官员向报界发表谈话说，“这个歇斯底里的声明可能是对古巴人民和革命政府进行一系列侵略活动的讯号。应该引起世界舆论的警惕。”这位官员说，“拉丁美洲欢迎多尔蒂科斯的热情的人群，是在艾森豪威尔访问期间没有出现过的。”他接着指出，因此美国当局企图削弱多尔蒂科斯总统访问拉丁美洲的影响。
    古巴人民社会党主席胡安·马里内略今天晚间就美国的照会发表了电视讲话。他说，“美国的照会是对古巴的新进攻。它将遭到全国人民更有力的回击。帝国主义将来可能进行各种各样的侵略。但是革命政府将善于保持独立。照会可能越来越富于侵略性，而人民的回答将越来越坚决”。
    哈瓦那报纸强烈地谴责美国的这个侵略性的照会。“今日报”在社论中指出，美帝国主义来到古巴的后果可以简短地归结为：给古巴带来的是半殖民地的落后状态、饥饿、贫困和屈辱，而帝国主义者却大发其财。现在，当古巴起来摆脱它的悲惨的过去的时候，美帝国主义的代表人物就进行威胁、辱骂，并且准备进行侵略。“今日报”强调指出，古巴不允许别人诬蔑它的总统。全古巴都像我们的政府一样断然拒绝华盛顿的傲慢无礼的照会。
    “战斗报”认为美国侵略性的照会是“准备对古巴进行的武装侵略的先行步骤”。
