Title: Polish Broadcasting and Television Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 20th -- The Polish Broadcasting and Television Delegation, consisting of three members led by the Chairman of the Polish Radio and Television Committee, Sokolski, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau to visit our country.  Welcoming them at the airport were Mei Yi, Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, Jin Zhao, Deputy Director, and others.

The Commercial Counselor of the Polish Embassy in China, B. Szymanski, and the Acting Military Attaché, Lieutenant Colonel T. Szymanski, were also at the airport to welcome them.

### 波广播电视代表团抵京

1960-06-22
第6版()
专栏：

    波广播电视代表团抵京
    新华社20日讯　应广播事业局邀请来我国访问的波兰广播电视代表团一行三人，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。代表团由波兰广播电视委员会主席索科尔斯基率领。到机场欢迎的有广播事业局局长梅益、副局长金照等。
    波兰驻华使馆商务参赞伯·席曼斯基和代理武官塔·席曼斯基中校也到机场欢迎。
