Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Toledano
Author:
Date: 1960-06-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council received Vicente Lombardo Toledano (Mexico), Vice President of the World Federation of Trade Unions and President of the Latin American Labor Alliance, and his wife this morning.

Present at the reception was Liu Changsheng, Vice President of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. (Attached picture)
