Title: Menderes's Heinous Crimes: Mass Graves and Refrigerated Rooms in Ankara and Other Places Reveal Large Numbers of Corpses

Author:

Date: 1960-06-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ankara, June 2nd: The heinous crimes of the Menderes fascist government of Turkey, which was fostered by U.S. imperialism for a long time and recently overthrown, in brutally slaughtering the people have been exposed.

According to a spokesman for the Turkish Army authorities on June 1st, large numbers of corpses of victims have been found in mass graves and refrigerated rooms in Ankara and Istanbul. He said, "I can't say how they were killed, but corpses have been found in both Istanbul and Ankara, some of which were found in mass graves.

### 曼德列斯罪行滔天  安卡拉等地埋人坑和冷藏室里发现大批尸体

1960-06-03
第6版()
专栏：

    曼德列斯罪行滔天
    安卡拉等地埋人坑和冷藏室里发现大批尸体
    据新华社2日讯　安卡拉消息：美帝国主义长期扶植的、最近被推翻的土耳其曼德列斯法西斯政府残暴屠杀人民的滔天罪行已被揭露出来。
    据土耳其陆军当局的一位发言人1日透露，在安卡拉和伊斯坦布尔的大埋人坑和冷藏室里发现了大批被害者的尸体。他说，“我说不上他们是怎样被杀害的，在伊斯坦布尔和安卡拉都发现了尸体，其中有些尸体是在大埋人坑里发现的。
