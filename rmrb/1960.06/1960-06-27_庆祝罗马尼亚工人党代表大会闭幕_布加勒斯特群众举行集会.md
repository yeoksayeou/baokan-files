Title: Bucharest Mass Rally Celebrates the Closing of the Romanian Workers' Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-06-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 25th. 100,000 working people of Bucharest held a grand rally this afternoon in "August 23rd" Square to celebrate the successful closing of the Romanian Workers' Party "Third Congress." Attending the rally were Romanian party and state leaders Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chivu Stoica, Maurer, and others, as well as delegations and representatives of communist and workers' parties from fifty countries who were invited to participate in the Romanian Workers' Party "Third Congress."

Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej spoke at the meeting. He said that this congress summarized the great achievements made by the hardworking and intelligent Romanian people in socialist construction under the leadership of the Workers' Party. The instructions on the Six-Year Plan and the Fifteen-Year Long-Term Plan adopted by the congress have opened up broad prospects for the complete completion of socialist construction. The resolutions and instructions of the congress will arouse a new upsurge of creative labor among the people of the whole country. We will do everything in our power to promote the realization of these resolutions.

In his speech, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the representatives of the fraternal parties from fifty countries for participating in this congress.

First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee Khrushchev, head of the French Communist Party delegation Leo Figuères, Japanese Communist Party representative Akira Mihar, Italian Communist Party representative Arturo Colombi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Moroccan Communist Party Ali Yata, and Cuban People's Socialist Party representative Carlos Rafael Rodriguez also spoke at the meeting.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚工人党代表大会闭幕  布加勒斯特群众举行集会

1960-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚工人党代表大会闭幕
    布加勒斯特群众举行集会
    新华社布加勒斯特25日电　布加勒斯特市十万劳动人民今天下午在“八月二十三日”广场举行盛大集会庆祝罗马尼亚工人党“三大”胜利闭幕。出席集会的有罗马尼亚党和国家领导人乔治乌—德治、基伏·斯托伊卡、毛雷尔等人，以及应邀参加罗马尼亚工人党“三大”的五十个国家的共产党和工人党代表团和代表。
    乔治乌—德治在会上讲了话。他说，这次代表大会总结了勤劳智慧的罗马尼亚人民在工人党的领导下在社会主义建设中所取得的巨大成就。大会通过的关于六年计划和十五年远景规划的指示，为彻底完成社会主义建设打开了广阔的前景。代表大会的决议和指示将激起全国人民的创造性劳动的新高潮。我们将竭尽一切力量来促使这些决议的实现。
    乔治乌—德治在讲话中表示衷心感谢五十个国家的兄弟党的代表参加这次代表大会。
    苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫、法国共产党代表团团长利奥·斐盖尔、日本共产党代表米原昶、意大利共产党代表阿尔图罗·科隆比、摩洛哥共产党中央第一书记阿里·亚塔、古巴人民社会党代表卡耳西内斯·拉蒙都在会上讲了话。
