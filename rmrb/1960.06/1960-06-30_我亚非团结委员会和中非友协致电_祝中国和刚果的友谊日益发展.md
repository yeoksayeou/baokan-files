Title: Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee and the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association Send Cables Congratulating the Growing Friendship Between China and Congo

Author:

Date: 1960-06-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 28th: The Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee today sent a cable to Patrice Lumumba, Prime Minister of the Republic of the Congo, warmly congratulating the independence of the Congo.

The telegram from the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee stated: "On the occasion of the Congo's declaration of independence, the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, on behalf of the 650 million Chinese people, extends warm congratulations to the people of your country.

"The Congolese people have waged arduous and tenacious struggles to break free from colonial rule and achieve national independence. The Chinese people have always deeply cared for and resolutely supported the just cause of the Congolese people in opposing imperialism and colonialism and striving for national independence. We believe that the Congolese people will further strengthen national unity, struggle to smash the conspiracies of imperialism, consolidate national independence, develop the national economy, and continue to eliminate colonialism, and will continue to achieve new successes.

"May the friendship and unity between the people of China and the Congo grow stronger day by day."

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th: Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, today sent a cable to Patrice Lumumba, Prime Minister of the Republic of the Congo, warmly congratulating the independence of the Congo.

Liu Changsheng's congratulatory telegram stated: "The independence of the Congo is the result of the Congolese people's unyielding struggle. The victorious struggle of the Congolese people has dealt a heavy blow to imperialism and colonialism, inspired the independence movement of the African people, and made an important contribution to the cause of the people of the world against imperialist aggression and in defense of world peace. The Chinese people warmly hail the victory of the Congolese people!

"We wish the Congolese people continuous new victories in strengthening unity and striving for the complete overthrow of colonial rule!"

### 我亚非团结委员会和中非友协致电  祝中国和刚果的友谊日益发展

1960-06-30
第5版()
专栏：

    我亚非团结委员会和中非友协致电
    祝中国和刚果的友谊日益发展
    新华社28日讯　中国亚非团结委员会今天致电刚果共和国总理帕特里斯·卢蒙巴，热烈祝贺刚果独立。
    中国亚非团结委员会的电报说：“值此刚果宣布独立之际，中国亚非团结委员会谨代表六亿五千万中国人民，向贵国人民致以热烈的祝贺。
      “刚果人民为了摆脱殖民主义统治、取得民族独立，进行了艰苦顽强的斗争。中国人民一向深切关怀和坚决支持刚果人民反对帝国主义、殖民主义，争取民族独立的正义事业。我们相信，刚果人民将进一步加强民族团结，为粉碎帝国主义的阴谋、巩固民族独立、发展民族经济和继续消除殖民主义而斗争，并不断取得新的成就。
      “祝中国和刚果两国人民的友谊和团结日益发展。”
    新华社29日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜今天致电刚果共和国总理帕特里斯·卢蒙巴，热烈祝贺刚果独立。
    刘长胜的贺电说：“刚果的独立，是刚果人民不屈不挠地进行斗争的结果。刚果人民的胜利斗争，沉重地打击了帝国主义和殖民主义，鼓舞了非洲人民的独立运动，对全世界人民反对帝国主义侵略，维护世界和平的事业做出了重要的贡献。中国人民热烈欢呼刚果人民的胜利！
    “祝刚果人民在加强团结和争取彻底推翻殖民统治的斗争中不断获得新的胜利！”
