Title: US Spy Diplomats Caught Red-Handed Plotting Conspiracy with Cuban Counter-Revolutionaries; Cuban Government Orders Immediate Expulsion

Author:

Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, June 16th: The Cuban Revolutionary Government announced today that two members of the U.S. Embassy in Cuba—William Friedman and Edwin Sweet—are personae non gratae and have been ordered to leave Cuba within twenty-four hours.

These two U.S. diplomatic officials were caught red-handed while holding a secret meeting here with Cuban counter-revolutionaries to plot a conspiracy.

Cuban Foreign Minister Raúl Roa today notified U.S. Ambassador to Cuba Bonsal of the Cuban government's decision. In its notification, the Cuban government accused these two U.S. spies of "carrying out activities that seriously endanger Cuban national sovereignty and blatantly interfere in Cuban internal affairs."

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, June 14th: The Cuban government recently arrested Chester Lacayo, an American agent and Nicaraguan national. Lacayo, acting on instructions from the U.S. State Department, came to Cuba to fabricate a so-called "military expedition" against Nicaragua, so that the Organization of American States could accuse the Cuban government of "interfering" in the internal affairs of other countries, thereby implementing the imperialist aggression plan of collective intervention against Cuba.

### 同古巴反革命分子策划阴谋  美国间谍外交官当场被捉  古巴政府限令他们立即离境

1960-06-18
第6版()
专栏：

    同古巴反革命分子策划阴谋
    美国间谍外交官当场被捉
    古巴政府限令他们立即离境
    新华社哈瓦那16日电　古巴革命政府今天宣布，美国驻古巴大使馆两名人员——威廉·弗里德曼和埃德温·斯威特为不受欢迎的人，并且限令他们在二十四小时内离开古巴。
    这两个美国外交官员是在此间正同古巴反革命分子举行秘密会议来策划阴谋时被当场捉住的。
    古巴外交部长劳尔·罗亚今天把古巴政府的上述决定通知了美国驻古巴大使邦沙尔。古巴政府在它的通知中指责这两名美国间谍“进行严重危害古巴民族主权和公然干涉古巴内政的活动”。
    新华社哈瓦那14日电　古巴政府最近逮捕了美国特务、尼加拉瓜人切斯特·拉卡约。拉卡约奉美国国务院的指令，到古巴来制造所谓对尼加拉瓜的“军事远征”，以便美洲国家组织借此来控告古巴政府“干涉”他国的内政，从而实行集体干涉古巴的帝国主义侵略计划。
