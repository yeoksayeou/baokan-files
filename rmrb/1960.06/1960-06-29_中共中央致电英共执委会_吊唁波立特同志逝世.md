Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Telegram to British Communist Party Executive Committee, Expressing Condolences on the Passing of Comrade Pollitt
Author:
Date: 1960-06-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 28th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China today sent a telegram to the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain, expressing condolences on the passing of Comrade Pollitt, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
To the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain:

We are shocked to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Pollitt, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain. On behalf of all members of our party and the entire Chinese people, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends its deep condolences to the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain and to the family of Comrade Pollitt. Comrade Pollitt was a respected leader of the British working class and an outstanding fighter in the international proletarian revolutionary movement. ### 中共中央致电英共执委会  吊唁波立特同志逝世

1960-06-29
第6版()
专栏：

    中共中央致电英共执委会
    吊唁波立特同志逝世
    新华社28日讯　中国共产党中央委员会今天致电英国共产党执行委员会，吊唁英国共产党执行委员会主席波立特同志逝世。电报全文如下：英国共产党执行委员会：
    惊悉英国共产党执行委员会主席波立特同志不幸逝世。中国共产党中央委员会谨代表我党全体党员和全体中国人民向英国共产党执行委员会和波立特同志的家属表示深切的哀悼。波立特同志是英国工人阶级的受人尊敬的领袖，是国际无产阶级革命运动的杰出战士，他为革命事业献出了毕生的精力，他的逝世不但是英国共产党和英国工人阶级的损失，而且也是国际无产阶级革命斗争的损失。波立特同志一贯同情和支持中国人民的革命事业，中国人民将永远怀念着他。
      　中国共产党中央委员会
                  1960年6月28日
