Title: Indian "New Age" Weekly Accuses India of its Position in the International Commission for Vietnam, India's Agreement to the US to Send Additional Military Personnel to South Vietnam Violates the Geneva Accords and Endangers Peace

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 23rd June: The editor of the Indian weekly "New Age," Joshi, commented in the latest issue of "New Age" on India's agreement within the International Commission for Vietnam to the US sending additional military personnel to South Vietnam, stating that India's position "seriously violates the Geneva Accords."

The commentary pointed out that the purpose of the Geneva Accords was to stop foreign interference in Indochina and restore peace and unity to Vietnam. The position of the Indian representative and the Canadian representative on the International Commission for Vietnam not only legitimizes the US occupation of South Vietnam but also helps to consolidate this situation.

The commentary said, "This is not a step towards seeking peace, but a step towards undermining peace. This step allows the US imperialists to use and strengthen South Vietnam as their military base, in order to create tension between North and South Vietnam and neighboring countries, and to promote their war preparations, unrestrained by the Geneva Accords."

The commentary said, "Humbly begging the United States for 'assistance' is the reason for this major regression in our (India's) foreign policy. This must be exposed and resisted at all costs. This is not only a matter of national morality but also a matter of national sovereignty. The more our ruling class asks of US imperialism, the closer it will be required to approach the ferocious clutches of US imperialism. This is the prominent historical lesson of today's world."

### 印“新世纪”周报指责印度在越南国际委员会中的立场  印度同意美增派军事人员前往南越是违反日内瓦协议危害和平的行为

1960-06-29
第6版()
专栏：

    印“新世纪”周报指责印度在越南国际委员会中的立场
    印度同意美增派军事人员前往南越    
    是违反日内瓦协议危害和平的行为
    新华社新德里23日电　印度“新世纪”周报主编乔希在最近一期的“新世纪”上就印度在越南国际委员会中同意美国增派军事人员前往南越这件事发表评论说，印度的立场“严重地违反了日内瓦协议”。
    评论指出，日内瓦协议的目的是要制止外国干涉印度支那、并且恢复越南的和平和统一。越南国际委员会中的印度代表和加拿大代表的立场不仅使美国占领南越的行为合法化，而且还帮助使这种状况更加巩固起来。
    评论说，“这不是谋求和平的步骤，而是破坏和平的步骤。这一步骤允许美帝国主义者利用和加强南越作为他们的军事基地，以便在北越和南越以及邻近国家之间制造紧张，并且推行他们的战争准备，而不受日内瓦协议的约束。”
    评论说，“卑躬屈膝地乞求美国‘援助’是我们（印度）外交政策发生这种重大倒退的原因。这必须不惜一切来进行揭露和抵制。这不仅是国家的道德问题，而且也是国家的主权问题。我们的统治阶级对美帝国主义要求愈多，它就会更进一步地被要求接近美帝国主义的穷凶极恶的魔掌。这是今天的世界上突出的历史教训。”
