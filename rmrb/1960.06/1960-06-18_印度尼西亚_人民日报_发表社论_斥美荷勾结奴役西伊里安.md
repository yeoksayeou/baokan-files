Title: Indonesian "People's Daily" Publishes Editorial Condemning US-Dutch Collusion to Enslave West Irian
Author:
Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta – The Indonesian "People's Daily" published an editorial on the 8th, once again exposing the United States' hypocritical neutrality on the West Irian issue. The "People's Daily" said that the United States does not allow Indonesia to use weapons purchased from the United States to resolve the West Irian issue, but has not made the same request to the Netherlands. On the contrary, the United States is helping Dutch warships transport American-made weapons and aircraft from the North Atlantic Treaty Organization to West Irian to carry out threatening and provocative activities.

The editorial demanded that a protest be lodged with the United States regarding the Netherlands' use of American-made weapons and aircraft from the North Atlantic Treaty Organization to enslave the people of West Irian.

### 印度尼西亚“人民日报”发表社论  斥美荷勾结奴役西伊里安

1960-06-18
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚“人民日报”发表社论
    斥美荷勾结奴役西伊里安
    新华社雅加达讯　印度尼西亚“人民日报”8日发表社论，再一次拆穿美国在西伊里安问题上采取伪装中立的面目。“人民日报”说，美国不准印度尼西亚使用购自美国的武器来解决西伊里安问题，但是对荷兰却没有提出同样的要求。相反，美国还帮助荷兰军舰运输北大西洋公约的美制武器和飞机到西伊里安去进行威胁和挑衅活动。
    社论要求就荷兰用北大西洋公约组织的美制武器和飞机来奴役西伊里安人民的做法向美国提出抗议。
