Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Secretary Sugiri and Others Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) Secretary Sugiri (Indonesia) and representatives and invited representatives from the Soviet Union, Burma, Somaliland, and Spain, who attended the WFTU Executive Committee meeting, left Beijing by plane today to return home. Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the Secretariat, were at the airport to see them off.

### 世界工联书记苏吉里等回国

1960-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工联书记苏吉里等回国
    新华社14日讯　出席世界工联理事会会议的世界工联书记苏吉里（印度尼西亚）和苏联、缅甸、索马里兰、西班牙的代表和特邀代表，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜和书记处书记康永和等。
