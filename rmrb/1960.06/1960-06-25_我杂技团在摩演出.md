Title: Chinese Acrobatic Troupe Performs in Morocco
Author:
Date: 1960-06-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Casablanca, June 22nd. The Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe, currently visiting Morocco, performed in Casablanca tonight.

The Governor of Casablanca Province attended the performance and congratulated the Chinese acrobatic artists backstage during the intermission.

### 我杂技团在摩演出

1960-06-25
第6版()
专栏：

    我杂技团在摩演出
    据新华社卡萨布兰卡22日电正在摩洛哥访问的中国杂技艺术团今夜在卡萨布兰卡举行演出。
    卡萨布兰卡省长出席观看了演出，并在休息的时候到后台向中国杂技艺术家们表示祝贺。
