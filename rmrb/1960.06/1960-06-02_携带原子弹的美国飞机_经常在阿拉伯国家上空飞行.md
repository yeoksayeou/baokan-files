Title: U.S. Planes Carrying Atomic Bombs Frequently Fly Over Arab Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, May 31st (TASS): The Lebanese "News Weekly" quoted news agency reports as saying that American military aircraft carrying atomic bombs, taking off from the U.S. Air Force base in Saudi Arabia, frequently fly over the territory of Arab countries.

    The newspaper expressed indignation at this provocative action by the U.S. military aircraft.

### 携带原子弹的美国飞机  经常在阿拉伯国家上空飞行

1960-06-02
第7版()
专栏：

    携带原子弹的美国飞机
    经常在阿拉伯国家上空飞行
    新华社1日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特5月31日讯：黎巴嫩“消息周报”援引通讯社的消息写道，从沙特阿拉伯美国空军基地起飞的载有原子弹的美国军用飞机经常在阿拉伯国家领土上空飞行。
    这家报纸对美国军用飞机的这种挑衅性行动表示愤慨。
