Title: Oppose American Aggression Against Korea; Support the Korean People's Peaceful Reunification of Their Motherland; People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Hold a Meeting Today
Author:
Date: 1960-06-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th—The "Meeting of People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Opposing American Aggression Against Korea and Supporting the Korean People's Peaceful Reunification of Their Motherland" will be solemnly held on the afternoon of the 25th. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of this meeting on its first program channel, starting at 2:50 pm on the 25th. Beijing Television will also broadcast simultaneously.

### 反对美国侵略朝鲜　支持朝鲜人民和平统一祖国  首都各界人民今日举行大会

1960-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    反对美国侵略朝鲜　支持朝鲜人民和平统一祖国
    首都各界人民今日举行大会
    新华社24日讯　“首都各界人民反对美国侵略朝鲜、支持朝鲜人民和平统一祖国大会”，将在25日下午隆重举行。中央人民广播电台将从25日下午两点五十分开始，用第一套节目的频率，转播这次大会的实况。北京电视台也将同时转播。
