Title: China and Mongolia Will Always Cooperate Closely and Advance Hand in Hand: Premier Zhou Enlai Leaves Ulaanbaatar for Home; Mongolian Party and Government Leaders Give a Warm Send-Off; Expressing Gratitude to Chairman Tsedenbal in a Telegram Upon Leaving the Territory of the People's Republic of Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, June 1st: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice-Premier Chen Yi concluded their visit to Mongolia and returned home by plane from Ulaanbaatar at 7:40 this morning.

Chairman Tsedenbal, Chairman Sambuu, and Mongolian Party and government leaders such as Tsende, Molomjamts, Dugersuren, and Jagvaral, as well as Foreign Minister Shagdarjav, and Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, Xie Fusheng, were all at the airport to give Premier Zhou Enlai a warm send-off.

When Chairman Tsedenbal shook hands with Premier Zhou Enlai to bid farewell, he once again thanked Premier Zhou Enlai for his friendly visit and asked Premier Zhou to convey the Mongolian people's respect to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Chairman Zhu De, and other Chinese leaders.

When Premier Zhou Enlai arrived at the airport by car, representatives of working people from all walks of life, who had been waiting at the airport, warmly and for a long time cheered and paid their respects to Premier Zhou.

After the band played the national anthems of China and Mongolia, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice-Premier Chen Yi, accompanied by Chairman Tsedenbal, reviewed the guard of honor.

Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a farewell speech, saying: "We came to Mongolia with the fraternal friendship of the Chinese people, and now we will return to China full of the profound friendship of the Mongolian people. We are very happy about this visit and the results achieved." Premier Zhou said: "Our two countries have signed the historically significant Sino-Mongolian Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Assistance. This will have a significant impact on promoting the fraternal and friendly relations between our two countries. Our two countries, China and Mongolia, will always cooperate closely and advance hand in hand in the socialist family headed by the Soviet Union!"

Chairman Tsedenbal then delivered a farewell speech. In his speech, he wished Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice-Premier Chen Yi, and all the distinguished Chinese guests a safe journey and thanked Premier Zhou Enlai for his good wishes and fraternal friendship towards the Mongolian people.

Tsedenbal said: "The Mongolian people will continue to make every effort to consolidate and enhance the fraternal friendship and cooperation established with the great Chinese people, the heroic Soviet people, and all the people of the socialist family."

Warm applause was constantly heard throughout the venue when Premier Zhou Enlai and Chairman Tsedenbal spoke.

Accompanied by Chairman Tsedenbal, Premier Zhou Enlai shook hands with the Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, members of the Presidium of the Mongolian Great People's Hural, ministers of various departments of the Party and government, senior generals of the People's Army, heads of various mass organizations, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia to bid farewell. When Premier Zhou Enlai bid farewell to the working people, waves of cheers erupted again and again, expressing once again the profound friendship of the Mongolian people towards Premier Zhou and the Chinese people.

At 7:40, the special plane carrying Premier Zhou Enlai took off, and the farewell crowd waved their hands reluctantly, wishing Premier Zhou a safe journey.

Also at the airport today to see off were staff members of the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia, representatives of Chinese employees working in Mongolia, and representatives of overseas Chinese.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, June 1st: Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Chairman Tsedenbal from the plane as he was about to leave the Mongolian border, expressing his gratitude to the Mongolian people. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
Dear Comrade Chairman of the Council of Ministers Tsedenbal:

As we are about to leave the territory of the People's Republic of Mongolia, please allow me to once again extend to you, and through you to the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the Mongolian government and the entire Mongolian people, my sincere thanks. I wish the fraternal People's Republic of Mongolia economic prosperity, national strength and prosperity, and people happiness.

Zhou Enlai
June 1, 1960, 9:00 AM

### 中蒙两国永远亲密合作携手并进  周恩来总理离乌兰巴托回国　蒙古党政领导人热烈欢送  离开蒙古人民共和国国境时致电泽登巴尔主席表示谢意

1960-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    中蒙两国永远亲密合作携手并进
    周恩来总理离乌兰巴托回国　蒙古党政领导人热烈欢送
    离开蒙古人民共和国国境时致电泽登巴尔主席表示谢意
    新华社乌兰巴托1日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理结束了在蒙古的访问，今天上午七时四十分从乌兰巴托乘飞机回国。
  泽登巴尔主席、桑布主席和曾德、莫洛姆扎木茨、杜格苏伦、扎格瓦拉尔等蒙古党政领导人，外交部长沙格达尔苏伦，中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生都到飞机前面热烈欢送周恩来总理。
    泽登巴尔主席同周恩来总理握手告别时，再次感谢周恩来总理的这次友好访问，并请周总理向毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席、朱德委员长和中国其他领导人转达蒙古人民的敬意。
    当周恩来总理乘车来到飞机场的时候，迎候在机场的各界劳动人民代表，热烈地、长时间地向周总理欢呼致敬。
    在乐队奏中、蒙两国国歌以后，周恩来总理、陈毅副总理由泽登巴尔主席陪同，检阅了仪仗队。
    周恩来总理致告别词说：“我们这次带着中国人民的兄弟友情来到蒙古，现在又将满载着蒙古人民的深厚友情回到中国。我们对于这一次的访问和所取得的成果感到十分高兴。”周总理说：“我们两国签订了具有历史意义的中蒙友好互助条约。这对于促进我们两国的兄弟友好关系都将产生重大的作用。我们中蒙两国在以苏联为首的社会主义大家庭中将永远亲密合作，携手并进！”
    泽登巴尔主席接着致欢送词。他在讲话中祝周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和全体中国贵宾一路平安，并感谢周恩来总理对蒙古人民的良好祝愿和兄弟友情。
    泽登巴尔说：“蒙古人民今后仍将为巩固与增进同伟大中国人民和英勇的苏联人民以及同社会主义大家庭全体人民建立的兄弟般的友好合作而作出全部努力。”
    当周恩来总理和泽登巴尔主席讲话时，全场不断发出热烈的掌声。
    周恩来总理由泽登巴尔主席陪同，向到机场送行的部长会议副主席、蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团委员们、党和政府的各部部长们、人民军高级将领、各群众团体负责人以及各国驻蒙古的外交使节握手告别。当周恩来总理向劳动人民群众告别时，人群里又发出经久不息的欢呼声，再次表达蒙古人民对周总理和对中国人民的深厚友谊。
    七点四十分，周恩来总理乘坐的专机起飞，欢送人群依依不舍地不断挥手，祝周总理一路平安。
    今天到飞机场送行的还有中国驻蒙古大使馆的工作人员、在蒙古工作的中国职工代表和华侨代表。
    新华社乌兰巴托1日电　周恩来总理在即将离开蒙古国境时，从飞机上打电报给泽登巴尔主席，向蒙古人民致谢。电报全文如下：亲爱的部长会议主席泽登巴尔同志：
    当我们即将离开蒙古人民共和国国境的时候，请允许我再一次向您，并且通过您向蒙古人民革命党、蒙古政府和全体蒙古人民致以诚挚的谢意。祝兄弟的蒙古人民共和国经济繁荣、国家富强，人民幸福。
                      周恩来
      1960年6月1日上午九时
