Title: Turkish New Government Bans Political Party Activities
Author:
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Ankara, May 31st: The new government formed after the Turkish coup d'état decided at its first meeting on the 30th to absolutely prohibit all political party activities.
    The meeting, presided over by Gürsel, reiterated its readiness to continue to be loyal to Turkey's "allies and friends" and guaranteed to abide by all previously signed agreements with foreign countries.

### 土耳其新政府禁止政党活动

1960-06-01
第6版()
专栏：

    土耳其新政府禁止政党活动
    新华社31日讯　安卡拉消息：土耳其政变后成立的新政府在30日举行的第一次会议上，决定绝对禁止一切政党活动。
    这次由古尔塞勒主持的会议重申，它准备继续忠于土耳其的“盟国和朋友”，并且保证遵守一切以前同外国签订的协定。
