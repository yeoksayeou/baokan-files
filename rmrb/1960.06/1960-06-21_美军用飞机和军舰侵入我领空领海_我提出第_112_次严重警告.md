Title: U.S. Military Aircraft and Warships Intrude into Our Airspace and Territorial Waters; I Issue the 112th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 20th: On June 19th, from 12:57 to 13:04, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island area of the Xisha Islands in Guangdong Province. On June 20th, from 14:20 to 15:07, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of Nanri and Wuqiu areas in Fujian Province. Regarding these military provocations by U.S. military aircraft and warships, a spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 112th serious warning.

### 美军用飞机和军舰侵入我领空领海  我提出第112次严重警告

1960-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    美军用飞机和军舰侵入我领空领海
    我提出第112次严重警告
    新华社20日讯　6月19日十二时五十七分至十三时四分，美国军用飞机一架侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛地区上空。20日十四时二十分至十五时七分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省南日、乌丘地区海域。对于美国军用飞机和军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百一十二次严重警告。
