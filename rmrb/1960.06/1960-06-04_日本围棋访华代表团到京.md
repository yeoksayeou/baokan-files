Title: Japanese Go Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd. The Japanese Go Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by train this morning. The delegation is composed of seven people, led by delegation leader, Honinbo Shusaku (瀬越憲作).

### 日本围棋访华代表团到京

1960-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    日本围棋访华代表团到京
    新华社3日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的日本围棋访华代表团一行七人，在团长濑越宪作率领下，今天上午乘火车到达北京。
