Title: Director of the Cuban National Agrarian Reform Committee, Jiménez, says the United States stopping "aid" will make Cuba happy
Author:
Date: 1960-06-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow dispatch: According to TASS, the head of the Cuban government trade delegation and Director of the Cuban National Agrarian Reform Committee, Jiménez, said in Moscow on June 2nd that U.S. President Eisenhower's announcement last week to halt "aid" to Cuba makes us happy. Jiménez made these remarks during a friendly meeting with Soviet Minister of Foreign Trade Patolichev and Chairman of the Soviet State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations, Skachkov.

Jiménez also said that not long ago, Cuba's natural resources belonged to the Americans. Now, that situation is gone forever. He said that currently, in Cuba's economic construction, we rely not only on the assistance of socialist countries and Latin American countries, but also on the assistance of the people of Asian and African countries, and on the assistance of the American people. Here, we cannot confuse the American people with American imperialism.

The Cuban government trade delegation arrived in Moscow on June 1st.

### 古巴土改全国委员会主任希门尼斯说  美国停止“援助”将使古巴感到幸福

1960-06-08
第7版()
专栏：

    古巴土改全国委员会主任希门尼斯说
    美国停止“援助”将使古巴感到幸福
    新华社讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：古巴政府贸易代表团团长、古巴土地改革全国委员会主任希门尼斯2日在莫斯科说，美国总统艾森豪威尔在上星期宣布决定停止对古巴的“援助”，这使我们感到幸福。希门尼斯这些话是他在同苏联对外贸易部部长帕托利切夫和苏联国家对外经济关系委员会主席斯卡奇科夫进行友好的会谈时说的。
    希门尼斯还说，不久以前，古巴的自然资源还是属于美国人的。现在，这种情况已经一去不复返了。他说，目前，在古巴的经济建设中，我们不仅要靠社会主义国家和拉丁美洲国家的援助，而且要靠亚非各国人民的援助，要靠美国人民的援助。在这里，我们不能把美国人民同美帝国主义混为一谈。
    古巴政府贸易代表团是在6月1日抵达莫斯科的。
