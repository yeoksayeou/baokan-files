Title: Ambassador Wang Youping Hosts Reception for Visit of Chinese Chengdu Basketball Team to Cambodia
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 12th - Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, Wang Youping, held a reception on the evening of the 11th for the visit of the Chinese Chengdu Basketball Team to Cambodia.

Among those attending the reception were Prince Essaro, Chairman of the Cambodian National Sports Committee, and figures from the Cambodian sports community.

At the reception, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted the friendship between China and Cambodia. The reception proceeded in a warm and lively atmosphere.

The Chinese Chengdu Basketball Team arrived in Cambodia on May 22nd at the invitation of the Cambodian Sports Association.

### 为我国成都篮球队访问柬埔寨  王幼平大使举行招待会

1960-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

    为我国成都篮球队访问柬埔寨
    王幼平大使举行招待会
    新华社金边12日电　中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平11日晚上为中国成都篮球队访问柬埔寨举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有柬埔寨国家体育委员会主席埃萨罗亲王和柬埔寨体育界人士。
    在招待会上，宾主不断为中国和柬埔寨的友谊而干杯。招待会在热烈和活跃的气氛中进行。
    中国成都篮球队是应柬埔寨体育协会的邀请在5月22日到达柬埔寨访问的。
