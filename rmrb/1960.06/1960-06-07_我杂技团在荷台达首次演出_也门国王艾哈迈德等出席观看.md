Title: Chinese Acrobatic Troupe Gives First Performance in Hodeida; Yemeni King Ahmad and Others Attend
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-07
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hodeida, 4th June: The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe gave its first performance in Hodeida on the evening of June 3rd. Yemeni King Ahmad watched the performance. Also in attendance were Yemeni Deputy Foreign Minister Abdul Rahman, the King's representative in Hodeida Province and Minister of Public Works, Yehia Abdul Qadir, Health Minister Mohammed Ali Othman, Supreme Court President Mohammed Abdas, and other senior government officials. Accompanying them were Zhang Yue, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Yemen, Wang Jinqian, leader of the Chinese highway expert team, and others. The audience totaled four thousand people.

At the opening ceremony, Abdul Rahman, in his welcoming speech, welcomed the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe to perform in Yemen on behalf of the Yemeni King, Prime Minister Crown Prince Badr, and the Yemeni people. He said that this visit will promote the friendly relations between Yemen and China. He wished the acrobatic troupe success in its performances throughout Yemen.

### 我杂技团在荷台达首次演出  也门国王艾哈迈德等出席观看

1960-06-07
第7版()
专栏：

    我杂技团在荷台达首次演出
    也门国王艾哈迈德等出席观看
    据新华社荷台达4日电　中国杂技团6月3日晚上在荷台达举行首次演出。也门国王艾哈迈德观看了演出。观看演出的还有也门副外交大臣阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼，国王驻荷台达省代表、公共工程大臣叶海亚·阿卜杜勒·卡迪尔，卫生大臣穆罕默德·阿里·乌斯曼，最高法院院长穆罕默德·阿卜达斯和其他高级政府官员。陪同他们观看的有中国驻也门公使馆临时代办张越和中国公路专家小组组长王进前等。观众共有四千人。
    在开幕仪式上，阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼在致欢迎词时，代表也门国王、首相巴德尔王储和也门人民欢迎中国杂技团在也门演出。他说，这次访问将促进也门和中国的友好关系。他祝杂技团在也门各地的演出获得成功。
