Title: Demands for General Election and Establishment of Caretaker Cabinet; Japanese Communist Party Announces List of Candidates for General Election
Author:
Date: 1960-06-07
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 6th: Japanese Communist Party Chairman Sanzo Nosaka and Central Committee Secretariat member Yoshio Shiga announced today at a press conference the list of ninety-four candidates nominated by the JCP to participate in the general election.

    This list was announced by the Japanese Communist Party after putting forward the slogans of overthrowing the Nobusuke Kishi cabinet, establishing a coalition government of parties opposing Nobusuke Kishi, dissolving the Diet, and holding a general election.

    The list includes JCP Central Committee Secretariat members Yoshio Shiga, Shoichi Kasuga, and Katsumi Torao, as well as seven Central Committee members.

    Chairman Nosaka announced that the JCP will nominate a total of one hundred and eighteen candidates to participate in elections in all electoral districts nationwide.

    The second batch of candidate lists will be published soon.

### 要求举行大选成立看守内阁  日共公布大选候选人名单

1960-06-07
第6版()
专栏：

    要求举行大选成立看守内阁
    日共公布大选候选人名单
    新华社6日讯　东京消息：日本共产党主席野坂参三和中央委员会干部会委员志贺义雄今天在记者招待会上公布了日共参加大选的九十四名候选人的名单。
    这个名单是日本共产党在提出打倒岸信介内阁、成立反对岸信介的各党派联合政府、解散国会、举行大选的口号后公布的。
    名单中包括日共中央干部会委员志贺义雄、春日正一和听涛克己以及中央委员七人。
    野坂主席宣布，日共将一共提名一百一十八名候选人参加全国所有选区的竞选。
    第二批候选人名单不久即将发表。
