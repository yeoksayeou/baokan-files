Title: Cultural Work Should Beat the Drums and Cheer for the Great Leap Forward; Representatives from the Cultural System Exchange Experiences in Serving Production
Author:
Date: 1960-06-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Representatives from the cultural system attending the National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education held a meeting today to exchange work experiences.

Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing delivered a speech at the meeting entitled "Continuous Revolution, Striving for Sustained Leap Forward in Cultural and Artistic Work." He said that resolutely opposing U.S. imperialism, thoroughly eliminating the ideological influence of imperialism, and completely smashing the shameless conspiracies of the imperialist running dogs, modern revisionism, is the primary task of China's cultural and artistic circles at present. All our cultural and artistic workers must bring into play the highest enthusiasm in this great struggle and bravely engage in battle. He said that the nationwide upsurge in studying Mao Zedong's works is a great communist ideological revolution movement, and the national cultural and artistic circles must most actively participate in this great ideological revolution movement. Shen Yanbing also proposed that cultural and artistic departments should actively support agriculture, closely cooperate with the movement of running urban people's communes on a large scale, extensively and deeply promote the technical innovation and technical revolution movement, and coordinate with education, health, and sports work to vigorously promote the high tide of cultural revolution.

Fourteen representatives spoke at the meeting today. They were: Yao Meifeng, representative of the Friendly Yue Opera Troupe of Wujiang County, Jiangsu Province; Pan Haoping, representative of Shanghai Library; Ran Maokui, representative of the Logistics Department of the Lushun Troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Chen Zigui, representative of the Amateur Cultural Troupe of Changshi Commune, Dafang County, Guizhou; Cai Songling, representative of Hebei Provincial Drama Troupe; Cheng Shirong, representative of Lanzhou Film Studio, Gansu Province; Zhao Wenqing, representative of Shuangcheng County Film Projection Team, Heilongjiang Province; Meng Xianchong, representative of Tianjin News Cinema. The advanced workers who spoke were You Yanqing, book distributor of Shugang People's Commune Bookstore in Weishi County, Henan Province; Meng San, peasant poet and teacher of a people-run school in Liangcheng Chunhutug Commune, Ulanqab League, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region; Gao Shenglin, actor and deputy head of Wuhan Peking Opera Troupe; Ye Yingmei, blind artist and deputy director of Zhejiang Provincial Quyi Association; Yu Ping, actor of Beijing Chinese Pingju Opera Theater; and Jiang Guiying, graduate student of Hubei Academy of Arts. In their speeches, these representatives vividly introduced their outstanding achievements in various aspects of cultural work, how they resolutely implemented the Party's policy on literary and artistic work, and how they served proletarian politics, production, workers, peasants, and soldiers. In their speeches, the representatives unanimously expressed their determination to hold high the red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, establish lofty ambitions, and make China's cultural work better serve socialist revolution and socialist construction, and strive for the great ideal of realizing communism in the future. ### 文化工作要为大跃进擂鼓助战  文化系统代表交流服务生产经验

1960-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

    文化工作要为大跃进擂鼓助战
    文化系统代表交流服务生产经验
    据新华社10日讯　参加全国文教先进工作者代表大会文化系统的代表，今天举行会议交流了工作经验。
    文化部部长沈雁冰在会上做了题为“不断革命，争取文化艺术工作的持续跃进”的讲话。他说，坚决反对美帝国主义，彻底肃清帝国主义的思想影响，彻底粉碎帝国主义的走狗现代修正主义的无耻阴谋，是当前我国文化艺术界的首要任务。我们的一切文化艺术工作者都要在这一伟大斗争中发挥最高的积极性，勇敢地进行战斗。他说，目前全国轰轰烈烈开展的学习毛泽东著作的热潮，是一个伟大的共产主义思想革命运动，全国文化艺术界必须最积极地投身到这个伟大的思想革命运动中去。沈雁冰还提出，文化艺术部门要积极支援农业；密切配合大办城市人民公社运动；广泛深入地宣传技术革新和技术革命运动；协同和配合教育、卫生、体育工作，大力推进文化革命的高潮。
    今天在会上发言的有十四位代表。他们是：江苏省吴江县友好锡剧团代表姚梅凤、上海图书馆代表潘皓平、中国人民解放军旅顺部队后勤部代表冉茂魁、贵州大方县长石公社业余文工团代表陈子贵、河北省话剧团代表蔡松龄、甘肃省兰州电影制片厂代表程士荣、黑龙江省双城县电影放映队代表赵文清、天津市新闻影院代表孟宪崇，发言的先进工作者有河南省尉氏县竖岗人民公社书店发行员游延清，内蒙古自治区乌兰察布盟凉成春胡图公社农民诗人、民校教师孟三，武汉市京剧团演员兼副团长高盛麟，浙江省盲艺人、省曲协副主任叶英美，北京市中国评剧院演员于萍，湖北省艺术学院研究生蒋桂英。这些代表在发言中，生动地介绍了他们在文化工作的各个方面，怎样坚决贯彻执行党的文艺工作方针，在为无产阶级政治服务、为生产服务、为工农兵服务等方面所作出的优异成绩。代表们在发言中一致表示要更高地举起毛泽东思想的红旗，树雄心、立大志，使我国文化工作更好地为社会主义革命和社会主义建设服务，为在将来实现共产主义的伟大理想而奋斗。（附图片）
