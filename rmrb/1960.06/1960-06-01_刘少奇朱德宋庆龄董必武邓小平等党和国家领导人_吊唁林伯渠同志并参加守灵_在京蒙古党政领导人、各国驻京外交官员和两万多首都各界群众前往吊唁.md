Title: Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Deng Xiaoping and other Party and state leaders mourn Comrade Lin Boqu and participate in the vigil. Mongolian Party and government leaders in Beijing, foreign diplomatic officials in Beijing and more than 20,000 people from all walks of life in the capital went to mourn.
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st — Party and state leaders went to the Working People's Cultural Palace this afternoon to mourn Comrade Lin Boqu, the great warrior of the Chinese people, who was loved by the entire Party and the people of the whole country.

This afternoon, Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Luo Ronghuan, Li Fuchun, Liu Bocheng, Li Xiannian, Zhang Wentian, Lu Dingyi, Bo Yibo, Wang Jiaxiang, Liu Lantao, Luo Ruiqing, etc., came to the Working People's Cultural Palace one after another. They stood in silent tribute before the portrait of Comrade Lin Boqu and participated in the vigil. When leaving the mourning hall, they also expressed their condolences to Comrade Lin Boqu's family. Today, other state leaders and responsible persons from all sides also went to Comrade Lin Boqu's mourning hall to participate in the vigil.

Bargynyam, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia, and Tumur-Ochir, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Secretary of the Secretariat, and member of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia, who are in Beijing, went to Comrade Lin Boqu's mourning hall this afternoon to pay their respects.

### 刘少奇朱德宋庆龄董必武邓小平等党和国家领导人  吊唁林伯渠同志并参加守灵  在京蒙古党政领导人、各国驻京外交官员和两万多首都各界群众前往吊唁

1960-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇朱德宋庆龄董必武邓小平等党和国家领导人
    吊唁林伯渠同志并参加守灵
    在京蒙古党政领导人、各国驻京外交官员和两万多首都各界群众前往吊唁
    新华社31日讯　党和国家的领导人今天下午到劳动人民文化宫，悼念为全党和全国人民爱戴的中国人民的伟大战士林伯渠同志。
    下午，刘少奇、朱德、宋庆龄、董必武、邓小平、彭真、罗荣桓、李富春、刘伯承、李先念、张闻天、陆定一、薄一波、王稼祥、刘澜涛、罗瑞卿等同志，先后来到劳动人民文化宫。他们在林伯渠同志的遗像前静默致哀，并且参加守灵。在离开灵堂的时候，还慰问了林伯渠同志的家属。今天，国家的其他领导人和各方面的负责人也到林伯渠同志灵堂参加守灵。
    正在北京的蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员、蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席巴尔吉尼雅姆，蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员、书记处书记、蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团委员图穆尔—奥奇尔等，今天下午到林伯渠同志灵堂吊唁。
    苏联、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古、德意志民主共和国、阿尔巴尼亚、越南民主共和国、印度、瑞典、丹麦、缅甸、印度尼西亚、瑞士、芬兰、巴基斯坦、挪威、南斯拉夫、阿富汗、阿拉伯联合共和国、锡兰、柬埔寨、伊拉克、英国、荷兰驻中国的大使、代办、临时代办和外交官员，今天上午前往林伯渠同志灵堂吊唁。
    今天上午和下午，前往劳动人民文化宫悼念林伯渠同志的首都各界群众，共计两万多人。（附图片）
