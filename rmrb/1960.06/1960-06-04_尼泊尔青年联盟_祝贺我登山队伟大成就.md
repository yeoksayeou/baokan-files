Title: Nepal Youth League Congratulates Our Mountaineering Team on Great Achievement
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 3rd: The Nepal Youth League warmly congratulated the Chinese mountaineering team on its successful ascent of Mount Everest. In a telegram to Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, signed by Secretary Madan Luhan Singh, the League stated: "We believe that this victory proves that the great Chinese people have made progress in mountaineering, as in other national construction activities."

### 尼泊尔青年联盟  祝贺我登山队伟大成就

1960-06-04
第7版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔青年联盟
    祝贺我登山队伟大成就
    新华社新德里3日电　尼泊尔青年联盟热烈祝贺中国登山队攀登珠穆朗玛峰成功。这个联盟在由秘书马丹·卢汉·辛格署名给中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力的一封电报中说：“我们认为这个胜利证明了伟大的中国人民在登山运动中同在其他的国家建设活动中一样取得了进步。”
