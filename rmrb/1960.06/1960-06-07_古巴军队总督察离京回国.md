Title: Cuban Army Inspector General Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1960-06-07
Page: 2

    Cuban Army Inspector General Leaves Beijing for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, June 6th. After a month-long friendly visit to our country, Cuban Army Inspector General Major William Gálvez Rodríguez and his wife have already left by plane on the 5th, heading home via Moscow. During his visit to our country, Major William Gálvez Rodríguez visited Shenyang, Anshan, Shanghai and other places, and was received by Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi and other leaders.

### 古巴军队总督察离京回国

1960-06-07
第2版()
专栏：

    古巴军队总督察离京回国
    新华社6日讯　古巴军队总督察威廉·加尔维斯·罗德里格斯少校和他的夫人在我国进行了一个月的友好访问后，已经在5日乘飞机取道莫斯科回国。威廉·加尔维斯·罗德里格斯少校在我国访问期间曾到沈阳、鞍山、上海等地参观，并且受到毛泽东主席、周恩来总理、陈毅副总理等领导人的接见。
