Title: Bulgarian People's Bank Governor Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-06-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: The Governor of the Bulgarian People's Bank, N. Nestorov, and the Deputy Director of the Foreign Exchange Administration, Kalinov, left Beijing by plane today to return home after a two-week visit to China at the invitation of Cao Juru, Governor of the People's Bank of China.

On the 23rd, Cao Juru hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the guests. Rong Zihe, Deputy Minister of Finance, and Chen Xiyu and Qiao Peixin, Deputy Governors of the People's Bank of China, attended the banquet. Stoyev, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, and Barov, Commercial Counselor, also attended the banquet.

On the 22nd, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Stoyev held a reception for Governor Nestorov's visit.

### 保人民银行行长离京回国

1960-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    保人民银行行长离京回国
    新华社25日讯　保加利亚人民银行行长?斯托洛夫和外汇管理局副局长卡黎诺夫应中国人民银行行长曹菊如的邀请，在我国进行两周访问后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    23日，曹菊如设宴欢送客人。出席宴会的有财政部副部长戎子和，中国人民银行总行副行长陈希愈、乔培新等。保加利亚驻我国大使馆临时代办斯托耶夫、商务参赞巴洛夫等也出席了宴会。
    22日，斯托耶夫临时代办曾为涅斯托洛夫行长的访问举行了招待会。
