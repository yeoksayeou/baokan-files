Title: Typhoon Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-06-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 28th: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon warning at 12:00 noon today: The third typhoon over the Pacific Ocean has entered the South China Sea. At 2:00 AM this morning, the center of the typhoon was located near 17.7 degrees North latitude and 118.3 degrees East longitude, with the maximum wind force near the center estimated to be force 12. In the future, the typhoon will move in a northwest direction at a speed of 8 to 11 knots per hour. It is expected that the typhoon will make landfall between Yangjiang and Shantou in Guangdong Province on the 29th. Affected by the typhoon, the wind force in the northern part of the South China Sea will gradually increase to force 9 to 12 on the 29th, and the wind force along the central and eastern coasts of Guangdong will increase to force 6 to 9, with wind forces of force 10 to 12 near the area where the typhoon center makes landfall. The wind force along the southern coast of Fujian and the Taiwan Strait will increase to force 6 to 8. Heavy rain to torrential rain is expected in central and eastern Guangdong and southern Fujian.

### 台风警报

1960-06-29
第5版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    据新华社28日讯　中央气象科学研究所今天中午十二时发布台风警报：太平洋上的第三号台风，已经进入南海，今天早晨二时，台风中心位置在北纬十七点七度东经一百一十八点三度附近，中心附近最大风力估计有十二级。未来台风将以每小时八到十一浬的速度，向西北方向移动。预计29日，台风将在广东省的阳江到汕头之间登陆。因受台风影响，29日，南海北部的风力，将逐渐加大到九到十二级，广东中部和东部沿海的风力，将加大到六到九级，其中，台风中心登陆的地区附近，风力将有十到十二级，福建南部沿海、台湾海峡的风力，将加大到六到八级。广东中部和东部、福建南部将有大雨到暴雨。
