Title: U.S. Under Secretary of State Dillon Delivers Speech Promoting the Use of Technology and Financial "Aid" to Strengthen Control over "Underdeveloped Countries"
Author:
Date: 1960-06-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 7th. U.S. Under Secretary of State Dillon, in a speech on the 5th at Williams College, an institution specifically training "talent" for the U.S. ruling class to operate abroad, advocated using so-called aid to strengthen U.S. control over "underdeveloped countries." He said, "Technical and financial aid are the greatest ideas in diplomacy to emerge in the twentieth century." To disguise this sugar-coated bullet, which has long been exposed, he emphasized paying attention to the "manner" in which this "aid" is given. He said, "The manner in which we give aid is as important as the aid itself," and "If aid is given clumsily and unwisely, it will be useless or worse."

Dillon also specifically requested that Western countries "provide military equipment and training to those countries on the periphery of the Sino-Soviet empire (Dillon's slanderous term for the socialist camp)."

### 美国副国务卿狄龙发表演说  鼓吹利用技术和财政“援助”  加强对“不发达国家”的控制

1960-06-08
第7版()
专栏：

    美国副国务卿狄龙发表演说
    鼓吹利用技术和财政“援助”  加强对“不发达国家”的控制
    据新华社7日讯  纽约消息：美国副国务卿狄龙5日在专门为美国统治集团训练到外国去活动的“人才”的威廉斯学院发表的演说中鼓吹利用所谓援助来加强美国对“不发达国家”的控制。他说：“技术和财政援助是二十世纪出现的在外交方面最伟大的主意”。为了掩饰这种早已被揭穿的糖衣炮弹，他强调在给予这种“援助”的时候要注意“方式”。他说：“我们给予援助的方式跟援助本身一样重要”，“如果在给予援助时很笨拙和不明智，这将是毫无用处的，或者会更糟糕。”
    狄龙还特别要求西方国家“向那些在中苏帝国（狄龙对社会主义阵营的诬蔑性称呼）边缘的国家提供军事装备和训练。”
