Title: Zhou Yang Meets Japanese Literary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th. Zhou Yang, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, met with the Japanese literary delegation headed by Hiroshi Noma on the afternoon of the 16th. The hosts and guests had a friendly conversation.

Present at the meeting were Liu Baiyu, Xiao San, Zhao Shuli, Yang Shuo, Wang Yafan, Li Yingru, and Tao Dun. On the afternoon of the 15th and the morning of the 16th, the Japanese literary delegation held discussions with well-known Chinese writers and young writers respectively on issues of Sino-Japanese cultural exchange and literary creation.

### 周扬会见日文学家代表团

1960-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    周扬会见日文学家代表团
    新华社16日讯　中国作家协会副主席周扬，16日下午会见了以野间宏为首的日本文学家代表团。宾主进行了友好的交谈。
    会见时在座的，有刘白羽、萧三、赵树理、杨朔、王亚凡、李英儒、陶钝。15日下午和16日上午，日本文学家代表团分别同中国著名的作家、青年作家，就日中文化交流问题和文学创作问题举行了座谈。
