Title: Comrade Lin Boqu Will Live Forever; Nearly 1,000 People in Linli Gather to Mourn Comrade Lin Boqu
Author:
Date: 1960-06-05
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 3rd. Comrade Lin Boqu, the great revolutionary fighter of the Chinese people, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the people from all walks of life in Linli County, Hunan Province—the hometown of Comrade Lin Boqu—nearly one thousand people, held a meeting at 9:00 this morning to mourn the passing of Comrade Lin Boqu. Guo Xinzhen, First Secretary of the Linli County Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered a eulogy at the memorial meeting. Representatives from all walks of life in Linli County and relatives of Comrade Lin Boqu spoke at the meeting. They unanimously expressed their determination to transform grief into strength, to diligently study and inherit Comrade Lin Boqu's revolutionary will and firm revolutionary spirit, and to mourn Comrade Lin Boqu with practical actions of doing a good job in production and work, and striving for an all-round leap forward.

### 林伯渠同志永垂不朽  临澧近千人集会悼念林伯渠同志

1960-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

    林伯渠同志永垂不朽
    临澧近千人集会悼念林伯渠同志
    新华社长沙3日电　中国人民的伟大革命战士、中国共产党中央政治局委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠同志的故乡——湖南省临澧县各界人民代表近一千人，今天上午九时举行大会追悼林伯渠同志的逝世。中共临澧县委第一书记郭新桢在追悼会上致悼词。临澧县各界代表和林伯渠同志的亲属都在会上讲了话。他们一致表示要化悲痛为力量，努力学习和继承林伯渠同志的革命遗志和坚强的革命精神，以搞好生产、搞好工作，力争全面跃进的实际行动，悼念林伯渠同志。
