Title: Lu Dingyi and Others Receive Representatives of Advanced Workers in Spare-Time Study
Author:
Date: 1960-06-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 6th June: Lu Dingyi, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council; Lin Feng, Member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Wu Yuzhang, Member of the CPC Central Committee and Director of the Chinese Written Language Reform Committee; Xu Teli, Member of the CPC Central Committee; and Zhang Jichun, Member of the CPC Central Committee and Director of the Cultural and Educational Office of the State Council, this afternoon received forty-two representatives of advanced units and advanced workers from industrial and agricultural production units and the People's Liberation Army who have persisted in spare-time theoretical and cultural study for a long time and have achieved outstanding results. Vice Premier Lu and others shook hands with these representatives one by one and took a group photo.
Those present at the reception included Yang Xiufeng, Li Dequan, Qian Junrui, and other responsible persons from central cultural and educational departments.

### 陆定一等接见业余学习先进工作者代表

1960-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    陆定一等接见业余学习先进工作者代表
    新华社6日讯　中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理陆定一，中共中央委员、人大常委会副委员长林枫，中共中央委员、中国文字改革委员会主任吴玉章，中共中央委员徐特立，中共中央委员、国务院文教办公室主任张际春，今天下午接见了四十二位来自工农业生产单位和人民解放军中长期坚持业余理论和文化学习，并获得出色成绩的先进单位和先进工作者代表。陆副总理等和这些代表一一握手，并且合影留念。
    陪同接见的有杨秀峰、李德全、钱俊瑞等中央文教各部门负责人。
