Title: He Long Sends Letter to President of the Royal Geographical Society, Thanking Him for Congratulating the Chinese Mountaineering Team
Author:
Date: 1960-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: He Long, Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, wrote a letter today to Lord Nathan, President of the Royal Geographical Society, thanking him and the Royal Geographical Society for congratulating China's mountaineering athletes on reaching the summit of Mount Everest.

The letter stated: "I was delighted to read your congratulatory letter on the Chinese mountaineers' ascent of the world's highest peak, Mount Everest, from the north side. Prior to this, the Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China in the United Kingdom had already conveyed your Society's congratulations to us. Please allow me, on behalf of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese Mountaineering Association, and the Chinese mountaineering athletes, to express my heartfelt gratitude to you and the Royal Geographical Society for your congratulations."

Nathan's congratulatory letter to He Long was received on June 6th. He extended, on behalf of all members of the Royal Geographical Society, his most heartfelt congratulations to the Chinese mountaineering athletes for their glorious mountaineering achievement.

The letter stated: "China's achievement in reaching the summit of Mount Everest has aroused admiration for the Chinese mountaineers' extraordinary skills and courage, not only in Britain but also throughout the world. This achievement will forever be recorded in history as a milestone in mountaineering exploration."

### 贺龙致信英国皇家地理学会会长  感谢对中国登山队的祝贺

1960-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    贺龙致信英国皇家地理学会会长
    感谢对中国登山队的祝贺
    新华社16日讯　国家体育运动委员会主任贺龙，今天写信给英国皇家地理学会会长内森勋爵，感谢他和皇家地理学会对我国登山运动员登上珠穆朗玛峰的祝贺。
    信中说：“我很高兴地阅读了你对中国登山运动员从北路登上世界最高峰——珠穆朗玛峰——的祝贺信。在此以前，中华人民共和国驻英代办处即曾将贵学会的祝贺转达给我们。请允许我代表中华人民共和国体育运动委员会、中国登山协会及中国登山运动员，向你和英国皇家地理学会的祝贺表示衷心的感谢。”
    内森写信给贺龙的祝贺信，是6月6日收到的。他代表英国皇家地理学会全体会员向中国登山运动员的辉煌的登山成就，致以最衷心的祝贺。
    信中说：“中国登上珠穆朗玛峰的成就，不仅在英国而且在全世界，引起了一切人们对中国登山运动员的卓越的技巧与胆略的钦佩。这一成就将永远作为登山探险上的里程碑而载入史册。”
