Title: Good Start in June: Steel and Coal Achieve High Output; Daily Output Levels of Many Key Steel Plants and Coal Mines Exceed May in the First Week
Author:
Date: 1960-06-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, 9th: Encouraged by the slogan of "Working hard in June, completing half of the annual plan ahead of schedule in the first half of the year," the iron smelting workers of Anshan Iron and Steel Company are launching a battle to seize iron and ensure steel, so that the daily output level of pig iron continues to rise. The average daily pig iron production in the first week of June increased by 6.25% compared with the same period in May, and increased by 3.58% compared with the average daily output level in the entire month of May.

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, 9th: Since entering June, the workers of Taiyuan Iron and Steel Company have launched a battle to seize iron and ensure steel, winning a good start. In the first seven days, pig iron production increased by 0.8% compared with the same period last month, and steel production from the 1st to the 6th increased by 1.5% compared with the same period last month.

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 9th: In the first week of June, the steel output of each steelmaking plant of Chongqing Iron and Steel Company increased by 5.9% compared with the last week of May.

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, 9th: The steel and iron production of Ma'anshan Iron and Steel Company has increased significantly since entering June. From the 1st to the 7th, the average daily steel production increased by 15% compared to the previous month, and iron increased by 9%.

Xinhua News Agency, Tangshan, 9th: The workers of Tangshan Steel Plant fought hard at the beginning of June and reported good news: The average daily output level from the 1st to the 7th increased by 8.7% compared with the last ten days of May, the quality qualification rate increased from 99.5% to 99.67%, and the pig iron consumption per ton of steel decreased by 176.47 kilograms.

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, 9th: After completing the May production plan ahead of schedule, the workers of Dalian Steel Plant have seen a significant increase in production at the beginning of June. In the first seven days, the plant's steel production overfulfilled the production plan by 2%, and the average daily output increased by 1.60% compared with the last ten days of last month. The utilization coefficient of cold charging in electric furnaces was 41.59 tons, continuing to lead the country.

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, 9th: Chengdu Steel Plant had a flying start in the first week of June. The average daily output from the 1st to the 7th increased by 41% in steel and 2.34% in iron compared with the last seven days of May.

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 9th: The coal mines under the jurisdiction of Hegang and Shuangyashan Mining Bureaus in Heilongjiang Province have won initial victories in June production. From the 1st to the 6th, Hegang Coal Mine averaged 42,046 tons of raw coal per day, an increase of more than 1,300 tons compared with the average daily output in the last ten days of May; Shuangyashan Coal Mine averaged 19,392 tons of raw coal per day, an increase of more than 2,200 tons compared with the average daily raw coal output in the last ten days of May. Both bureaus overfulfilled the cumulative daily raw coal production plan for the first week of June.

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 9th: The workers of Hebi Coal Mine, who completed half of the annual plan ahead of schedule and overfulfilled it, implemented the spirit of "using coal to ensure steel." After a complete victory in May, raw coal production at the beginning of June achieved another bumper harvest. From June 1st to 7th, 143.2% of the coal production plan was completed, and the average daily output level increased by 21.1% compared with the last ten days of May.

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 9th: Leading cadres at all levels of Zibo Mining Bureau in Shandong Province continued to go deep into the mines in June to organize large-scale high-yield competitions, achieving "every front has a commander," so that raw coal production from the 1st to the 7th overfulfilled the plan by 9.9%, and the average daily output increased by about 3% compared with the last ten days of May.

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 9th: Yaojie Mining Bureau, one of the key coal mines in Gansu Province, had a daily output level in the first week of June that was 2% higher than the monthly average and 15.2% higher than the same period last month.

### 六月开门红　钢煤齐高产  大批重点钢铁厂和煤矿第一周日产水平超过五月

1960-06-10
第2版()
专栏：

    六月开门红　钢煤齐高产
    大批重点钢铁厂和煤矿第一周日产水平超过五月
    鞍山钢铁公司
    据新华社鞍山9日电　在大干6月、上半年提前完成全年计划一半的口号鼓舞下，鞍山钢铁公司炼铁职工正展开夺铁保钢大战，使生铁的日产水平继续上升。6月第一周平均每天的生铁产量，比5月同期增长6．25％，比5月全月的平均日产水平提高3．58％。
    太原钢铁公司
    据新华社太原9日电　进入6月以来，太原钢铁公司职工展开夺铁保钢大战赢得了开门红。头七天，生铁产量比上月同期增长0．8％，1至6日的钢产量比上月同期增长1．5％。
    重庆钢铁公司
    据新华社重庆9日电　在6月份的第一周中，重庆钢铁公司各炼钢厂的钢产量，比5月份最后一周上升了5．9％。
    马鞍山钢铁公司
    据新华社合肥9日电　马鞍山钢铁公司进入6月份来钢铁产量大幅度增长，1至7日钢比上月平均日产增长15％，铁增长9％。
    唐山钢厂
    据新华社唐山9日电　唐山钢厂职工奋战6月初传捷报：1日至7日的平均日产水平同5月下旬比较，钢锭增长8．7％，质量合格率由99．5％提高到99．67％，每吨钢的生铁消耗减少了一百七十六点四七公斤。
    大连钢厂
    据新华社旅大9日电　大连钢厂职工在提前完成了5月份的生产计划之后，6月初生产又大幅度上升。头七天，全厂钢产量超额2％完成了生产计划，平均日产量比上月下旬提高了1．60％。电炉冷装利用系数为四十一点五九吨，继续在全国领先。
    成都钢铁厂
    据新华社成都9日电　成都钢铁厂6月第一周旗开得胜，1日至7日的平均日产量与5月份最后七天比较，钢增长41％，铁增长2．34％。
    鹤岗和双鸭山两矿务局
    据新华社哈尔滨9日电　黑龙江省鹤岗和双鸭山矿务局所属各煤矿，赢得了6月生产初捷。鹤岗煤矿1日到6日平均日产原煤四万二千四十六吨，比5月下旬平均日产量增长了一千三百多吨；双鸭山煤矿平均日产原煤一万九千三百九十二吨，比5月下旬平均日产原煤产量增长了二千二百多吨。这两个局都超额完成了6月份第一周的原煤日产累计计划。
    鹤壁煤矿
    据新华社郑州9日电　提前超额完成全年计划一半的鹤壁煤矿职工，贯彻“以煤保钢”的精神，在5月全胜之后，6月初的原煤生产又获得丰收。6月1日至7日完成煤炭生产计划143．2％，平均日产水平比5月下旬提高21．1％。
    淄博矿务局
    据新华社济南9日电　山东淄博矿务局各级领导干部，在6月份继续深入矿井组织大面积高产竞赛，做到了“条条战线有帅”，使1日至7日的原煤生产又超额9．9％完成计划，平均日产量比5月下旬增长3％左右。
    窑街矿务局
    据新华社兰州9日电　甘肃省的重点煤矿之一——窑街矿务局，6月第一周的日产水平比上月平均提高2％，比上月同期提高15．2％。
