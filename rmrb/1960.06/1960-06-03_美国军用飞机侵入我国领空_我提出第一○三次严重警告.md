Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into China's Airspace, I Issue 103rd Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd June: At 5:32 a.m. to 5:45 a.m. on June 2nd, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Swatow to Daya Bay area of Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 10:27 a.m. to 10:42 a.m., another U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Swatow to Daya Bay area of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 103rd serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我国领空  我提出第一○三次严重警告

1960-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我国领空
    我提出第一○三次严重警告
    新华社2日讯　6月2日五时三十二分至四十五分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省汕尾至大亚湾地区上空。同日十时二十七分至四十二分又有美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省汕尾至大亚湾地区上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百零三次严重警告。
