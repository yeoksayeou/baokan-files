Title: Party and State Leaders Receive Members of the Presidium of the Conference of Cultural and Educational Heroes

Author:

Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st. Party and state leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Liu Bocheng, Lu Dingyi, Wang Jiaxiang, Liu Lantao, Luo Ruiqing, Xi Zhongxun, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Saifudin, He Xiangning, Lin Feng, Xie Juezai, and Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong and Fu Zuoyi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Li Siguang, and others, this afternoon received all members of the Presidium of the National Congress of Advanced Units and Advanced Workers in Socialist Construction in Education, Culture, Health, Sports, and News. Party and state leaders shook hands with the members of the Presidium one by one and had cordial conversations.

### 党和国家领导人接见文教群英大会主席团成员

1960-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人接见文教群英大会主席团成员
    新华社1日讯　党和国家领导人刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、宋庆龄、董必武、邓小平、彭真、陈毅、李富春、刘伯承、陆定一、王稼祥、刘澜涛、罗瑞卿、习仲勋、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、赛福鼎、何香凝、林枫、谢觉哉以及国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义，政协全国委员会副主席李四光等，今天下午接见了全国教育和文化、卫生、体育、新闻方面社会主义建设先进单位和先进工作者代表大会主席团的全体成员。党和国家领导人和主席团的成员一一握手，并进行了亲切的谈话。
