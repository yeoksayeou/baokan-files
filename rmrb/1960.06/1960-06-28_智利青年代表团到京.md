Title: Chilean Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th – The Chilean Youth Delegation, invited by the All-China Youth Federation for a friendly visit to our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation consists of twelve people, led by Li Yaomin, Head of the Delegation, Executive Director of the Chilean-Chinese Cultural Association, and Secretary-General of the Youth Department.

### 智利青年代表团到京

1960-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

    智利青年代表团到京
    新华社26日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国进行友好访问的智利青年代表团一行十二人，在团长、智中文化协会执行主任兼青年部秘书长李耀民率领下，在今天乘飞机抵京。
