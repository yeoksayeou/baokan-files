Title: Three Distinguished Guests from Cuba Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-06-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: After visiting Shanghai, Hefei, Shenyang, Anshan, and other places, Lopez, Luis, and Susaldí, three figures from the Cuban "July 26th Movement," left Beijing on the 5th to return to their country.

### 古巴三位贵宾回国

1960-06-08
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴三位贵宾回国
    新华社6日讯　古巴“7月26日运动”人士洛佩斯、路易斯、苏萨耳迪等三人赴上海、合肥、沈阳、鞍山等地参观访问后，已经在5日离开北京回国。
