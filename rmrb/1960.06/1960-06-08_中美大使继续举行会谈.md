Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author:
Date: 1960-06-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 7th. The 98th meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw at 2:00 p.m. today. The meeting lasted two hours and fifteen minutes.

After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would be held on Friday, July 15th.

### 中美大使继续举行会谈

1960-06-08
第7版()
专栏：

    中美大使继续举行会谈
    新华社华沙7日电　中美大使级会谈第九十八次会议，今天下午二时在华沙举行。这次会议历时二小时零十五分钟。
    会后双方宣布，下次会谈将在7月15日（星期五）举行。
