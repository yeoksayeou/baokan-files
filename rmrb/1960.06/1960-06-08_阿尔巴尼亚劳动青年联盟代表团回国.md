Title: Albanian Labor Youth League Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-06-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 7th -- The Albanian Labor Youth League delegation of four people, led by Jorkia Blemti, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Youth League, concluded its visit to China and left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. During its stay in China, the delegation visited Beijing, Shenyang, Anshan, Changsha, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai and other cities.
    Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Wang Zhaohua, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and others.
    Luo Yi and Wang Zhaohua, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, hosted a banquet on the 6th to bid farewell to the guests.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟代表团回国

1960-06-08
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟代表团回国
    新华社7日讯　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会书记处书记约勒基亚·布列姆提率领的阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟代表团一行四人，结束了在我国的访问，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。代表团在中国期间，访问了北京、沈阳、鞍山、长沙、广州、杭州、上海等城市。
    到机场欢送的有共青团中央书记处书记王照华等人。
    共青团中央书记处书记罗毅、王照华6日曾设宴欢送客人。
