Title: Strengthen the Fraternal Friendship Between the Albanian and Korean People: Chairman Leshi Arrives in Pyongyang for a Visit; Over 200,000 People in Pyongyang Warmly Welcome the Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-06-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 22nd: At the invitation of Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, arrived in Pyongyang this morning by special plane at the head of a delegation. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and the government, as well as over 200,000 people in Pyongyang.

    The weather was clear today, and the flags of both Korea and Albania were flying throughout Pyongyang.

    After the welcoming ceremony, Lleshi and Choi Yong-kun rode in an open car amidst the cheers of the crowds lining the streets to Kim Il-sung Square, to participate in a mass rally to welcome the distinguished guests.

    At the square, 200,000 people holding flowers and colored flags welcomed the envoys of the heroic Albanian people, holding huge banners that read: "Long Live the Friendship and Solidarity Between the Korean and Albanian People!"

    Chairman Choi Yong-kun first spoke at the rally, extending a warm welcome to Chairman Lleshi and others for their visit. He pointed out that the Albanian people have been working to oppose modern revisionism and defend Marxism-Leninism, and have been fighting to crush Yugoslav modern revisionism. The Korean people highly appraise and support the struggle of the Albanian people.

    Choi Yong-kun praised the achievements of the Albanian people in socialist construction and expressed gratitude to the Albanian people for their assistance to the Korean people. He said that the Korean people will work to further strengthen the solidarity with the Albanian people and strengthen the solidarity of the socialist camp.

    Chairman Lleshi then spoke at the rally, praising the Korean people for their victory over US imperialism during the war and their achievements in socialist construction after the war. He condemned US imperialism for turning South Korea into a military base for its aggression against the socialist camp.

    Chairman Lleshi said: Our Marxist-Leninist party regards the exposure of imperialists and the Yugoslav revisionists fostered by them as a sacred internationalist task.

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 22nd: Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, who is currently visiting Korea, separately met with Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and Premier of the Cabinet, and Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, in Pyongyang today. During the meeting, the two sides held talks in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere.

    Premier Kim Il-sung hosted a luncheon today to entertain Chairman Lleshi.

### 加强阿尔巴尼亚和朝鲜人民兄弟友谊  列希主席到达平壤访问  平壤二十多万群众热烈欢迎贵宾

1960-06-23
第6版()
专栏：

    加强阿尔巴尼亚和朝鲜人民兄弟友谊
    列希主席到达平壤访问
    平壤二十多万群众热烈欢迎贵宾
    据新华社平壤22日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健的邀请，今天上午率领代表团乘专机到达平壤。贵宾们受到朝鲜劳动党和政府领导人以及平壤市二十多万群众的热烈欢迎。
    今天天气晴朗，平壤全城飘扬着朝鲜和阿尔巴尼亚两国的国旗。
    在欢迎仪式之后，列希和崔庸健同乘敞篷汽车在群众的夹道欢呼声中前往金日成广场，参加欢迎贵宾的群众大会。
    在广场上，有二十万群众拿着鲜花和彩旗欢迎英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民的使者，他们举着巨型横幅上写着：“朝鲜人民和阿尔巴尼亚人民之间的友谊团结万岁！”
    崔庸健委员长首先在大会上讲话，对列希主席等的来访表示热烈欢迎。他指出，阿尔巴尼亚人民为反对现代修正主义、保卫马克思列宁主义而努力，一直为粉碎南斯拉夫现代修正主义进行了斗争。朝鲜人民高度地评价和支持阿尔巴尼亚人民的斗争。
    崔庸健赞扬了阿尔巴尼亚人民在社会主义建设中取得的成就，并对阿尔巴尼亚人民对朝鲜人民的援助表示感谢。他说，朝鲜人民将为进一步加强同阿尔巴尼亚人民的团结和加强社会主义阵营的团结而努力。
    列希主席接着在大会上讲话，他赞扬了朝鲜人民在战争时期打败美帝国主义取得的胜利和战后在社会主义建设中取得的成就。他谴责美帝国主义把南朝鲜变成它的侵略社会主义阵营的军事基地。
    列希主席说：我们马克思列宁主义的党，把揭露帝国主义者和由它豢养的南斯拉夫修正主义者当作神圣的国际主义任务。
    新华社平壤22日电　正在朝鲜访问的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希今天在平壤分别拜会了朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长、内阁首相金日成和朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健。拜会时，双方在友好、融洽的气氛中进行了谈话。
    金日成首相在今天设午宴招待了列希主席。
