Title: The International Committee for the Defense of Hélou Issues Statement, Demanding Increased Support for the Hélou Movement
Author:
Date: 1960-06-29
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 25th: The International Committee for the Defense of Hélou issued a statement on the first anniversary of the arrest of Farajallah Hélou, leader of the Lebanese Communist Party and writer, in Damascus, demanding that all Lebanese, all Arabs, and everyone around the world who demands respect for human rights and the restoration of his freedom, strengthen the solidarity movement.
    Today, the "Al Hadaf" newspaper published an editorial paying tribute to Hélou and demanding that the UAR authorities release him or bring him before an open court.

### 保卫赫卢国际委员会发表声明  要求加强声援赫卢的运动

1960-06-29
第6版()
专栏：

    保卫赫卢国际委员会发表声明
    要求加强声援赫卢的运动
    新华社贝鲁特25日电　保卫赫卢国际委员会在黎巴嫩共产党领袖、作家法拉贾拉·赫卢在大马士革被捕一周年的时候发表声明，要求所有的黎巴嫩人、所有的阿拉伯人和全世界所有要求尊重人权和要求恢复他的自由的人加强声援运动。
    “呼声报”今天发表社论，向赫卢致敬，要求阿联当局释放他或者把他提交公开法庭。
