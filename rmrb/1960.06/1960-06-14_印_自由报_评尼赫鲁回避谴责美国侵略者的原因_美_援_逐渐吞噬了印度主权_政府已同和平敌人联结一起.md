Title: Indian "Freedom Daily" Comments on Nehru's Reasons for Avoiding Condemnation of American Aggressors; American "Aid" Gradually Devours Indian Sovereignty; Government Has Linked Itself with Enemies of Peace
Author:
Date: 1960-06-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 11th. The Indian "Freedom Daily" published an editorial on the 7th stating, "The Indian government, through American economic aid, food agreements, and other similar means, has linked itself with the enemies of world peace. Therefore, the Indian government does not have the courage to express a strong attitude towards American leaders and tell the whole truth." "American economic aid and other various agreements are gradually devouring India's political independence and sovereignty. It is wrong if our compatriots forget that the United States has already begun to politically interfere in India."

This newspaper made these comments while reviewing Nehru's recent foreign policy speech at the All India Congress Committee meeting and his attitude towards the Four-Power Summit.

The editorial said that regardless of the normal reaction after the failure of the Four-Power Summit, Nehru remained silent precisely at that time. Even now, Nehru does not consider it necessary to strongly and unequivocally condemn the American leaders. His attitude is extremely dangerous in the current situation and is also extremely dangerous for the efforts to establish world peace. The editorial asked, "What is the argument for thinking that those who violate international law should not be asked to apologize?" The editorial said, "This will only indirectly encourage the schemers of war."

The editorial pointed out that India, like some other countries, has "become so entangled with American imperialism in national development that it has forgotten the great importance of world peace. The leaders of these countries are extremely complimentary to the American leaders."

The editorial said, "Our compatriots must judge this development very profoundly," "We must also remember that the imperialist agents in our country are trying every possible means to drag our country into the American camp." The editorial said, "Fighting without hesitation against the enemies of world peace—the United States—can prevent the schemes of the reactionaries in our country from succeeding."

### 印“自由报”评尼赫鲁回避谴责美国侵略者的原因  美“援”逐渐吞噬了印度主权  政府已同和平敌人联结一起

1960-06-14
第6版()
专栏：

    印“自由报”评尼赫鲁回避谴责美国侵略者的原因
    美“援”逐渐吞噬了印度主权  政府已同和平敌人联结一起
    新华社新德里11日电　印度“自由报”7日发表社论说，“印度政府通过美国的经济援助、粮食协定以及其它类似的途径，使自己同世界和平的敌人联结在一起。所以，印度政府没有勇气对美国领导人表示强烈的态度，讲出全部的真相。”“美国的经济援助和其它各种协定逐渐吞噬了印度的政治独立和主权。如果我国同胞忘记了美国已经开始在政治上在印度插足，那是不对的。”
    这家报纸是在评论尼赫鲁最近在国大党全国委员会会议上的外交政策演说和他对四国首脑会议所采取的态度时这样说的。
    社论说，不管人们在四国首脑会议开不成以后的正常的反应，尼赫鲁恰恰在那个时候保持缄默。甚至到现在，尼赫鲁还不认为有必要强烈地毫不含糊地谴责美国领导人。他的这种态度在当前局势下是极其危险的，而且对于建立世界和平的努力说来也是极其危险的。社论问道，“认为不应该叫破坏国际法的人表示道歉，这种论点是什么论点？”社论说，“这只会间接地鼓励战争的阴谋家们。”
    社论指出，印度和其它有些国家一样，由于“在国家发展方面同美帝国主义纠缠在一起达到这种程度，以致忘记了世界和平的莫大重要性。这些国家的领导人向美国领导人恭维备至。”
    社论说，“我国同胞必须非常深刻地判断这个事态发展”，“我们还必须记住，在我国国内的帝国主义代理人，正在想尽办法把我国拖向美国阵营”。社论说，“毫不犹豫地对世界和平的敌人——美国进行斗争，就有可能使我国国内的反动派的阴谋不能得逞。”
