Title: Accelerate the Development of Spy Satellites and Modern Weapons for Land and Air Forces: US Senate Approves an Additional One Billion Dollars in Military Spending
Author:
Date: 1960-06-13
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 12th June: The Senate Appropriations Committee passed two resolutions on June 10th and 8th respectively, requesting an increase in military appropriations of $218,880,000 to accelerate the development of spy satellites and increase modern weapons for the Army, and an increase of $850 million to fully develop the B-70 bomber system.
    This brings the total US government military appropriation for the 1961 fiscal year to $40,384,897,000, an increase of more than $1 billion over what Eisenhower requested in January of this year.
    Of the huge military expenditure of over $40.38 billion, the three services will receive the following appropriations: Army - $9,556,496,000; Navy - $12,203,465,000; Air Force - $17,433,636,000.
    The House of Representatives also passed a resolution on the 9th, stipulating an appropriation of $50 million to expand US military bases in the Far East region for the Navy, Army, and Air Force.

### 加速发展间谍卫星和陆空军现代武器  美参院增拨十亿元军费

1960-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

    加速发展间谍卫星和陆空军现代武器
    美参院增拨十亿元军费
    新华社12日讯　华盛顿消息：参议院拨款委员会在6月10日和8日分别通过了两项决议，要求增加军事拨款二亿一千八百八十万美元来加速发展间谍卫星和增加陆军的现代化武器，以及增加八亿五千万美元来充分发展B—70轰炸机体系。
    这样就使美国政府1961财政年度的军事拨款总额达到四百零三亿八千四百八十九万七千美元，比艾森豪威尔今年1月间所要求的增加了十亿多美元。
    在四百零三亿八千万美元以上的庞大的军事开支中，三军将分别得到的拨款如下：陆军——九十五亿五千六百四十九万六千美元；海军——一百二十二亿零三百四十六万五千美元；空军——一百七十四亿三千三百六十三万六千美元。
    众议院在9日也通过一项决议，规定拨款五千万美元来扩充美国在远东地区的海、陆、空军事基地。
