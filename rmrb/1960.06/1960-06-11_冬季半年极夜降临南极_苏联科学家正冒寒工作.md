Title: Winter's Half-Year Polar Night Descends on Antarctica - Soviet Scientists Work in the Bitter Cold
Author:
Date: 1960-06-11
Page: 6

    Winter's Half-Year Polar Night Descends on Antarctica
    Soviet Scientists Work in the Bitter Cold
    According to Soviet newspaper reports, as the Northern Hemisphere begins to enter the sweltering summer, Soviet Antarctic explorers are braving temperatures of seventy to eighty degrees below zero, persisting in their scientific work during the long "Antarctic night."
    Winter has now descended on Antarctica, and Antarctica has begun its half-year-long polar night.

### 冬季半年极夜降临南极  苏联科学家正冒寒工作

1960-06-11
第6版()
专栏：

    冬季半年极夜降临南极
    苏联科学家正冒寒工作
    据苏联报纸报道，在北半球开始进入炎热的夏季的时候，苏联南极考察家正在冒着零下七、八十度的严寒，在漫长的“南极夜”中坚持科学工作。
    现在冬季已经降临南极，南极开始了长达半年的极夜。
