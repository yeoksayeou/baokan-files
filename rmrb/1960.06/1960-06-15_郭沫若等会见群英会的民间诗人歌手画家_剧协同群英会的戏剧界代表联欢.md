Title: Guo Moruo and Others Meet Folk Poets, Singers, and Painters of the "Heroes' Gathering"; Drama Association Holds a Joint Celebration with Drama Representatives from the "Heroes' Gathering"
Author:
Date: 1960-06-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 14th Dispatch: The Chinese Folk Literature Research Association held a symposium today at the Lakeside Pavilion in the Summer Palace in the capital. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese Folk Literature Research Association, and Zhou Yang, Vice Chairman, met with folk poets, singers, and painters from all over the country and various ethnic groups who are attending the National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education, and had cordial conversations.

Among the representatives were workers, farmers, soldiers, an old lady who could write poetry and sing, and an eleven-year-old little poet. The folk poets and singers recited and sang their recent works at the meeting, enthusiastically praising the wise leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao, denouncing the aggressive ambitions of U.S. imperialism, and expressing their determination and drive to continuously leap forward. They also improvised poems on the spot. Guo Moruo and Zhou Yang praised and encouraged everyone's works.

Yang Hansheng, Gu Jiegang, Jia Zhi, Lin Shan, Mao Xing, and literary critic Tang Tao, all directors of the Chinese Folk Literature Research Association, also attended today's symposium.

At the meeting, the Chinese Folk Literature Research Association presented each representative with a copy of "Red Flag Ballads," and Guo Moruo and Zhou Yang signed the books as a souvenir.

Xinhua News Agency, 14th Dispatch: The Chinese Dramatists Association held a get-together to warmly welcome the representatives from the drama circles who are attending the National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education.

Tonight, in the auditorium of the Xinqiao Hotel, more than two hundred drama representatives attending the National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education and more than one hundred famous figures and actors from the capital's drama circles talked cordially.

Tian Han, Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, said in his speech that under the brilliant guidance of the Party and Chairman Mao's cultural and educational policies and literary and artistic thoughts, a large number of drama artistic backbones and pacesetters with outstanding achievements have emerged in various regions, fraternal ethnic groups, drama genres, and different drama positions across the country, and have made strong contributions to the socialist revolution and socialist construction of the motherland. He encouraged the representatives to continue to study Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong's works, carry out ideological transformation, actively establish and consolidate the proletarian world outlook, continue to integrate with the working people, and create the newest and most beautiful works with a high degree of ideological and artistic value.

Famous Peking Opera artists Mei Lanfang and Gai Jiaotian successively spoke at the meeting, warmly congratulating the representatives on their achievements in drama work, and hoping that they would continue to work hard, be both red and expert, and make drama art better serve socialist construction.

Then the evening party began. The dance was interspersed with literary and artistic programs. The wonderful performances and singing of Gao Yuanjun, Chen Shufang, Yan Fengying, Li Yilan, Yang Qiuling, Yang Xiaoting, Patam Reiding, and others were warmly welcomed by everyone.

Famous figures and famous actors from the drama circles who participated in the get-together also included Yang Hansheng, Zhang Geng, Ma Shaobo, Zhao Dingxin, Wei Ming, Han Junqing, Guo Xizhu, and Wang Wenjuan.

### 郭沫若等会见群英会的民间诗人歌手画家  剧协同群英会的戏剧界代表联欢

1960-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若等会见群英会的民间诗人歌手画家
    剧协同群英会的戏剧界代表联欢
    新华社14日讯　中国民间文艺研究会今天在首都颐和园湖心亭举行座谈会，中国民间文艺研究会主席郭沫若、副主席周扬，会见了来自全国各地、各民族参加全国文教先进工作者代表大会的民间诗人、歌手、画家，进行了亲切的交谈。
    代表中有工人、农民、战士，还有能诗能歌的老太太和十一岁的小诗人。民间诗人、歌手们在会上朗诵、歌唱了他们最近的新作，热情歌颂党和毛主席的英明领导，痛斥美帝国主义的侵略野心，并且表示自己不断跃进的决心和干劲。他们还当场即兴赋诗。郭沫若、周扬对大家的作品表示称赞和鼓励。
    参加今天座谈会的还有中国民间文艺研究会理事阳翰笙、顾颉刚、贾芝、林山、毛星和文学评论家唐弢等。
    会上，中国民间文艺研究会赠送每位代表“红旗歌谣”一册，郭沫若、周扬并在书上签名留念。
    新华社14日讯　中国戏剧家协会举行联欢晚会，热烈欢迎出席全国文教先进工作者代表大会的戏剧界的代表。
    今晚在新侨饭店的礼堂里，二百多名出席全国文教先进工作者代表大会的戏剧界代表和一百多名首都戏剧界著名人士、演员亲切畅谈。
    中国戏剧家协会主席田汉在会上致词中说，在党和毛主席的文教方针和文艺思想的光辉照耀下，全国各地区、各兄弟民族、各剧种、各个不同的戏剧岗位上，已经涌现出成绩卓著的大批戏剧艺术骨干和标兵，并且对祖国社会主义革命和社会主义建设做出了有力的贡献。他勉励代表们今后继续学习马列主义和毛泽东著作，进行思想改造，积极树立和巩固无产阶级世界观，继续同劳动人民结合，创作出具有高度思想性和艺术性的最新最美的作品。
    著名的京剧艺术家梅兰芳、盖叫天等先后在会上讲话，热烈祝贺代表们在戏剧工作中取得的成绩，希望他们继续努力，做到又红又专，使戏剧艺术更好地为社会主义建设服务。
    接着晚会开始。舞蹈中间穿插着文艺节目，高元钧、陈书舫、严凤英、李忆兰、杨秋玲、杨筱亭、帕他姆热衣丁等人的精彩演唱和表演，受到了大家的热烈欢迎。
    参加联欢晚会的戏剧界著名人士和著名演员，还有阳翰笙、张庚、马少波、赵鼎新、韦明、韩俊卿、郭西珠、王文娟等人。
