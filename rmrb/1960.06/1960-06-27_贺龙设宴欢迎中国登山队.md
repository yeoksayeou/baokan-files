Title: He Long Hosts Banquet to Welcome Chinese Mountaineering Team
Author:
Date: 1960-06-27
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, hosted a banquet in Beijing tonight to celebrate the Chinese mountaineering team's conquest of Mount Everest.

　　Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi spoke at the banquet, congratulating the Chinese mountaineers on their successful ascent to the world's highest peak, writing a new chapter in the history of world mountaineering, and encouraging the mountaineers to continue to carry forward the spirit of daring to think and act combined with solid and hard work, guard against arrogance and impetuosity, and, with the spirit of continuous revolution, make persistent efforts to plant the red flag all over the peaks of our great motherland.

　　Shi Zhanchun, the captain of the Chinese mountaineering team, also spoke at the banquet. He said that the victory of this mountaineering expedition was due to the leadership of the Party and the support of the people of the whole country. He stated that the mountaineers will continue to study Mao Zedong Thought diligently in the future and achieve even greater success in mountaineering.

　　Luo Ruiqing, Vice Premier of the State Council; Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Liu Changsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League; Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the National Women's Federation; Cai Tingkai, Rong Gaotang, and Huang Zhong, Deputy Directors of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Tan Guansan, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China; Chen Kehan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, as well as responsible persons from relevant departments of the government and representatives from relevant units, attended the banquet tonight. After the banquet, a dance was held.

### 贺龙设宴欢迎中国登山队

1960-06-27
第2版()
专栏：

    贺龙设宴欢迎中国登山队
    新华社26日讯　国务院副总理、体育运动委员会主任贺龙，今晚在北京欢宴征服珠穆朗玛峰的中国登山队。
    贺龙、陈毅副总理在宴会上先后讲话，祝贺中国登山队员们胜利地登上了世界第一高峰顶峰，在世界登山史上写下了崭新的一章，并且勉励登山队员们继续发扬敢想敢干和踏实苦干相结合的作风，戒骄戒躁，以不断革命的思想，再接再厉，把红旗插遍我们伟大祖国的高峰。
    中国登山队队长史占春也在宴会上讲话。他说，这次登山的胜利，是由于党的领导和全国人民的支援。他表示，登山队员们今后将继续努力学习毛泽东思想，在登山运动方面取得更大的成就。
    国务院副总理罗瑞卿，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫，中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜，共青团中央书记处书记罗毅，全国妇联副主席李德全，国家体委副主任蔡廷锴、荣高棠、黄中，中共西藏工委副书记谭冠三，中共北京市委书记处书记陈克寒，以及政府各有关部门的负责人和有关单位的代表等，出席了今晚的宴会。宴会结束后，举行了舞会。
