Title: Argentine Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - The Argentine Youth Delegation, invited by the All-China Youth Federation to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 9th. The delegation was led by Miguel Gománz Oussiere, with a total of ten members.

### 阿根廷青年代表团抵京

1960-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    阿根廷青年代表团抵京
    新华社10日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷青年代表团一行十人，在团长米盖尔·哥曼斯·奥斯西埃率领下，已经在9日乘飞机到达北京。
