Title: Xi Zhongxun Receives German Education Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 15th: Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun received the German Democratic Republic Education Delegation led by Mr. Honecker, Vice Minister of National Education of the German Democratic Republic, this afternoon, and had a cordial conversation with them.

Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Education, was present at the meeting.

Wang Gendel, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, was also present.

### 习仲勋接见德教育代表团

1960-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    习仲勋接见德教育代表团
    新华社15日讯　国务院副总理习仲勋今天下午接见了以德意志民主共和国国民教育部副部长霍内克为首的德意志民主共和国教育代表团，并且进行了亲切的交谈。
    陪同接见的有教育部副部长刘皑风。
    德意志民主共和国驻中国大使汪戴尔也在座。
