Title: Grotewohl says during visit to Chinese Pavilion at Agricultural Exhibition: People's Communes in China are changing people and production
Author:
Date: 1960-06-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 12th: The Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic (GDR), Otto Grotewohl, visited the Chinese Pavilion at the Eighth Agricultural Exhibition of the German Democratic Republic on the 11th.

Accompanied by Wang Guoquan, the Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister Grotewohl viewed the exhibits in the Chinese Pavilion. He wrote in the guest book, "China's economy, especially its agricultural economy, is changing the face of the world, a face that will be communist." Prime Minister Grotewohl added, "People's Communes in China are changing people and production."

The exhibits from the "Sino-German Friendship People's Commune" attracted Prime Minister Grotewohl's attention. Wu Wenzhong, the vice-chairman of the People's Commune, who is currently visiting the German Democratic Republic, briefed Prime Minister Grotewohl on the relevant situation of the People's Commune. The Prime Minister asked Wu Wenzhong to convey his greetings to the members of the "Sino-German Friendship People's Commune".

### 格罗提渥参观农展会中国馆时说  中国人民公社改变着人和生产

1960-06-14
第6版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥参观农展会中国馆时说
    中国人民公社改变着人和生产
    新华社柏林12日电　德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥11日在参观德意志民主共和国第八届农业展览会时参观了中国馆。
    格罗提渥总理在中国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权陪同下，参观了中国馆的各项展品。他在贵宾留言簿上写道，“中国的经济，特别是它的农业经济正在改变着世界面貌，这个面貌将是共产主义的”。格罗提渥总理又说，“中国人民公社改变着人和生产”。
    “中德友好人民公社”的各项展品引起了格罗提渥总理的注意。正在德意志民主共和国访问的这个人民公社的副社长吴文仲向格罗提渥总理介绍了人民公社的有关情况。总理请吴文仲转达他对“中德友好人民公社”社员们的问候。
