Title: Albanian Chairman Leshi Departs for China
Author:
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 31st: H. Leshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, left Tirana by plane at 1:00 PM on the 31st for Beijing to pay a friendly visit to China at the invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi.

Accompanying Leshi on his visit to China were L. Belishova, Member of the Presidium of the People's Assembly, Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, L. Goga, Vice-Chairman of the People's Assembly, and V. Natanaeli, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Chinese Ambassador to Albania, Luo Shigao, accompanied Leshi to Beijing.

Present at the airport to see them off were Albanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu, First Deputy Chairman Mufti, and Vice-Chairmen of the Presidium of the People's Assembly Peza and Chuvani, among others.

### 阿尔巴尼亚列希主席启程来华

1960-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚列希主席启程来华
    新华社地拉那31日电　应刘少奇主席邀请前往中国进行友好访问的阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈·列希，在31日下午一时乘飞机离开地拉那前往北京。
    随同列希前往中国访问的有人民议会主席团委员、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、书记处书记利·贝利绍娃，人民议会副主席列·戈加，外交部副部长瓦·纳塔奈利。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高陪同列希前往北京。
    在机场欢送的有阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡、第一副主席穆夫蒂乌，人民议会主席团副主席佩扎和楚瓦尼等。
