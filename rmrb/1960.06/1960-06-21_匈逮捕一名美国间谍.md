Title: Hungary Arrests an American Spy
Author:
Date: 1960-06-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 19th: According to the "People's Freedom" newspaper, the Hungarian Ministry of the Interior arrested Karl Schmidt, a West German citizen engaged in espionage activities in Hungary, on the 13th. This spy was acting under the instructions of the American intelligence agency.

### 匈逮捕一名美国间谍

1960-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

    匈逮捕一名美国间谍
    新华社布达佩斯19日电　据“人民自由报”报道，匈牙利内政部机关13日逮捕了在匈牙利进行间谍活动的西德公民卡尔·施密特，这个间谍是受美国谍报机关指使的。
