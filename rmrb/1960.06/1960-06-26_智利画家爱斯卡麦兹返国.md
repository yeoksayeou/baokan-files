Title: Chilean Painter Ascamez Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-06-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: The Chilean painter Julio Ascamez, who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, left Beijing today to return home.

Chen Jin, Secretary-General of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, was at the airport to see him off.

### 智利画家爱斯卡麦兹返国

1960-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    智利画家爱斯卡麦兹返国
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，前来我国访问的智利画家胡利奥·爱斯卡麦兹，于今日离京返国。
    到机场欢送的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会总干事陈金。
