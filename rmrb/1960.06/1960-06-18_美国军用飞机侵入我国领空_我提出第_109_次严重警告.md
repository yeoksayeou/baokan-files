Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into China's Airspace; 109th Serious Warning Issued

Author:

Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: At 12:41 to 12:47 on June 17th, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island of the Xisha Islands in Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 109th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我国领空  我提出第109次严重警告

1960-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我国领空
    我提出第109次严重警告
    新华社17日讯　6月17日十二时四十一分至十二时四十七分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百零九次严重警告。
