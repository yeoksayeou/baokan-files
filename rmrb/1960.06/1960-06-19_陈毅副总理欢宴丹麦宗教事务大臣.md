Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Hosts Banquet for Danish Minister of Religious Affairs
Author:
Date: 1960-06-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, hosted a banquet at noon today to welcome the Danish Minister of Religious Affairs, Mrs. Bodil Koch, her husband Professor Koch, and their daughter.

Attending the banquet as guests were Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Chu Tunan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; He Chengxiang, Director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs of the State Council; Li Dequan, Vice President of the All-China Women's Federation; Xi Raojiacuo, President of the Chinese Buddhist Association; Da Pongsheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association; Jiang Yizhen, Vice President of the China Theological Seminary, and others.

Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Danish Embassy in China, Cao Ersheng, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 陈毅副总理欢宴丹麦宗教事务大臣

1960-06-19
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理欢宴丹麦宗教事务大臣
    新华社18日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天中午设宴欢迎丹麦王国宗教事务大臣博迪尔·科克夫人和她的丈夫科克教授及女儿。
    出席宴会作陪的有，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长罗贵波，对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，全国妇联副主席李德全，中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，中国神学院副院长蒋翼振等。
    丹麦驻中国大使馆临时代办曹尔生也应邀出席了宴会。
