Title: Creating Tensions, Preparing for Wars of Aggression: Frequent Military Exercises by the North Atlantic Group

Author:

Date: 1960-06-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: According to TASS on June 14th: Troops of the North Atlantic Group stationed on West German territory, as well as the West German Bundeswehr, are conducting various military exercises in different regions of West Germany. Atomic-rocket units at Schleissheim Airport (Bavaria) conducted rocket launching exercises. During the exercise, an atomic explosion was simulated. Officers from West Germany, France, Belgium, and Greece participated in this exercise.

A few days ago, another military exercise was also conducted in the Husum area (Schleswig-Holstein).

In addition, NATO began a three-day exercise in The Hague today to check the preparedness of various staff departments and command posts. Military staff from Belgium, Canada, Denmark, the Netherlands, Portugal, the United Kingdom, and the United States are participating in this exercise.

### 制造紧张局势　准备侵略战争  北大西洋集团军队演习频繁

1960-06-16
第6版()
专栏：

    制造紧张局势　准备侵略战争
    北大西洋集团军队演习频繁
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社14日讯：驻扎在西德领土上的北大西洋集团的军队以及西德国防军，正在西德各地区举行各种军事演习。在施来斯海姆机场（巴伐利亚州）的原子—火箭部队进行了火箭发射演习。在演习时模仿了一个原子爆炸。参加这次演习的有西德、法国、比利时和希腊的军官。
    几天之前，在胡朱姆地区（石勒苏益格—荷尔斯泰因州）还进行了另一次军事演习。
    此外，北大西洋公约组织今天开始在海牙举行为期三天的演习，以检查各参谋部和指挥哨的准备工作。参加这次演习的有比利时、加拿大、丹麦、荷兰、葡萄牙、英国和美国的军事参谋。
