Title: The United States Pays to Expand Laotian Police

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 7

　　Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 30th - According to the Cambodian weekly "Nationalist," the U.S. "Foreign Aid Mission" in Vientiane recently announced that the United States is giving the Laotian government approximately 46 million kips (approximately 80 kips to the U.S. dollar) in "aid," of which 41 million kips will be used to expand and modernize the Laotian police organization.

### 美国出钱扩充老挝警察

1960-06-02
第7版()
专栏：

    美国出钱扩充老挝警察
    新华社金边30日电　据柬埔寨“民族主义者”周刊最近一期透露，万象的美国“援外使团”最近宣布，美国给予老挝政府约四千六百万基普（约八十基普合一美元）的“援助”，其中四千一百万基普用于扩大老挝警察组织，并使之现代化。
