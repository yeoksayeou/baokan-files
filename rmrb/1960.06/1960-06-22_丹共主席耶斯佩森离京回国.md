Title: Danish Communist Party Chairman Jespersen Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. After a month-long visit to our country, Danish Communist Party Chairman Knud Jespersen, his wife, and his entourage of three people, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 21st to return home via Moscow.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Liu Lantao, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee.

### 丹共主席耶斯佩森离京回国

1960-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    丹共主席耶斯佩森离京回国
    新华社21日讯　丹麦共产党主席克努德·耶斯佩森夫妇和随员一行三人，在我国进行了一个月的访问之后，已于21日上午乘飞机经莫斯科回国。
    到机场送行的，有中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛、中央委员刘宁一。
