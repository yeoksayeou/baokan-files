Title: Chairman Leshi Arrives in Moscow, Scheduled to Fly to Beijing Last Night
Author:
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 31st. Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, Leshi, and his entourage, arrived in Moscow on the afternoon of May 31st en route to China for a visit.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Furtseva, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Kozlov, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Filyubin, and other personnel.

Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and embassy staff also welcomed him at the airport.

Chairman Leshi is scheduled to leave Moscow for Beijing by plane on the evening of June 1st.

### 列希主席抵莫斯科定于昨晚乘机来京

1960-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    列希主席抵莫斯科定于昨晚乘机来京
    新华社莫斯科31日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席列希及其随行人员在去中国访问途中于5月31日下午抵达莫斯科。
    到机场欢迎的有苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员福尔采娃、苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席科兹洛夫、外交部副部长费留宾及其他人员。
    中国驻苏联大使刘晓及使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    列希主席定于6月1日晚乘机离莫斯科去北京。
