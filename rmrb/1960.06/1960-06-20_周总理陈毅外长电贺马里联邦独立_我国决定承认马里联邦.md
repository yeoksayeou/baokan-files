Title: Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen Yi Send Telegrams Congratulating the Independence of the Federation of Mali; China Decides to Recognize the Federation of Mali
Author:
Date: 1960-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram today to Prime Minister Keita of the Federation of Mali, congratulating the Federation of Mali on its declaration of independence. The telegram reads as follows:
Federation of Mali
Dakar
His Excellency Mr. Modibo Keita, Prime Minister of the Federation of Mali:

    On the occasion of the declaration of independence of the Federation of Mali, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, I extend my sincere congratulations to the government and people of the Federation of Mali, and wish the people of the Federation of Mali new achievements in the cause of safeguarding national independence. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

          Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                          June 19, 1960, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram today to Prime Minister Keita of the Federation of Mali, congratulating the Federation of Mali on its independence and informing him that the Chinese government has decided to recognize the Federation of Mali. The telegram reads as follows:
Federation of Mali
Dakar
His Excellency Mr. Modibo Keita, Prime Minister of the Federation of Mali:

    I am pleased to learn of the declaration of independence of the Federation of Mali. On behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to the government and people of the Federation of Mali. It is my honor to inform Your Excellency that the government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize The Federation of Mali. I hope that my government's recognition of your country will lead to the further development of friendly relations between our two countries.

            Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
                          June 19, 1960, Beijing

### 周总理陈毅外长电贺马里联邦独立  我国决定承认马里联邦

1960-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈毅外长电贺马里联邦独立
    我国决定承认马里联邦
    新华社19日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给马里联邦总理凯塔，祝贺马里联邦宣告独立，电文如下：马里联邦　达喀尔马里联邦总理莫迪博·凯塔先生阁下：
    在此马里联邦宣告独立的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民，向马里联邦政府和人民致以诚挚的祝贺，祝马里联邦人民在维护民族独立的事业中取得新的成就。祝贵国繁荣，人民幸福。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                          1960年6月19日于北京
    新华社19日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长今天打电报给马里联邦总理凯塔，祝贺马里联邦独立，并且通知他，中国政府已经决定承认马里联邦。电文如下：马里联邦　达喀尔马里联邦总理莫迪博·凯塔先生阁下：
    欣悉马里联邦宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向马里联邦政府和人民致以衷心的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认马里联邦。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将导致我们两国间友好关系的进一步发展。
            中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅
                          1960年6月19日于北京
