    Homemade Trucks Carry Large Loads
    In the movement to reform civilian transport vehicles, cadres, technicians, and workers of the Dalian Transport Joint Company, under the Lüda Transport Company, boldly innovated, put legs on diesel engines, and manufactured homemade trucks.
    This type of homemade truck uses a 25-horsepower diesel engine as its motor, combined with homemade gearboxes, differentials, and chassis. This homemade truck can haul ten tons of goods at a speed of 18 kilometers per hour. This vehicle is suitable for rural transportation and port loading, with one vehicle capable of replacing six horse-drawn rubber-wheeled carts.

### 土汽车装载多

1960-06-06
第2版()
专栏：不断推广  不断提高  不断创造

    土汽车装载多
    在改革民间运输工具的运动中，旅大运输公司所属大连运输联社的干部、技术人员、工人“三结合”，大胆创造，给柴油机安上了腿，制成土汽车。
    这种土汽车用二十五马力柴油机作发动机同土法制造的变速器、差速器、车架子等组成。这种土汽车可拖载十吨货物，时速达十八公里。这种车适用于农村运输以及港站装载，一辆车可顶六辆马拉胶轮车。
