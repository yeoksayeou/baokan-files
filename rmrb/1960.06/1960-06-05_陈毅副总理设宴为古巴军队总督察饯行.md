Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Hosts Farewell Banquet for Cuban Army Inspector General
Author:
Date: 1960-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife hosted a farewell banquet this afternoon for Major William Gálvez Rodríguez, Inspector General of the Cuban Republic Army, and his wife, and had a cordial conversation. (Attached picture)
    Vice Premier Chen Yi and Cuban Army Inspector General Gálvez at the banquet. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Bin.

### 陈毅副总理设宴为古巴军队总督察饯行

1960-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理设宴为古巴军队总督察饯行
    新华社4日讯　国务院副总理陈毅元帅和夫人今天下午设宴为古巴共和国军队总督察威廉·加尔维斯·罗德里格斯少校和夫人饯行，并且进行了亲切的谈话。（附图片）
    陈毅副总理、古巴军队总督察加尔维斯在宴会上。新华社记者　张彬摄
