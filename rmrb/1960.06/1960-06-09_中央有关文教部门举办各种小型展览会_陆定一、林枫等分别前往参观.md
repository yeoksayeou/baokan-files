Title: Central Relevant Cultural and Educational Departments Hold Various Small Exhibitions; Lu Dingyi, Lin Feng, and Others Visit Separately
Author:
Date: 1960-06-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: During the National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education, the relevant central cultural and educational departments held several small exhibitions, including higher education, teaching reform in general education, phonetic literacy, culture and art, eliminating the four pests, promoting hygiene, technical innovation, and technical revolution, for representatives to visit and exchange work experiences. These exhibitions focused on the achievements and experiences of colleges, middle schools, primary schools, spare-time schools, cultural and artistic groups, and medical and health institutions in some parts of the country in carrying out teaching reform, technical innovation, technical revolution movements, scientific research, and language reform. The exhibits included physical objects, models, and charts. Lu Dingyi, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, Lin Feng, Member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Wu Yuzhang, Member of the CPC Central Committee and Chairman of the Chinese Language Reform Committee, and Xu Teli, Member of the CPC Central Committee, visited the general education teaching reform exhibition and the phonetic literacy exhibition on the 6th and 7th, respectively, and received the staff from various provinces and cities who participated in these two exhibitions.

### 中央有关文教部门举办各种小型展览会  陆定一、林枫等分别前往参观

1960-06-09
第3版()
专栏：

    中央有关文教部门举办各种小型展览会
    陆定一、林枫等分别前往参观
    新华社8日讯　在全国文教先进工作者代表大会开会期间，中央有关文教部门举办了高等教育、普通教育教学改革、注音识字、文化艺术和除四害、讲卫生、技术革新、技术革命等几个小型展览会，供代表们参观和交流各项工作经验。这几个展览会集中了全国部分地区的高等学校、中小学校、业余学校、文化艺术团体、医疗卫生机关在开展教学改革工作、技术革新、技术革命运动、科学研究工作、文字改革工作等方面所取得的成就和经验。展览品中有实物、模型和图表。中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理陆定一，中共中央委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫，中共中央委员、中国文字改革委员会主任吴玉章和中共中央委员徐特立，6日和7日分别参观了普通教育教学改革展览会和注音识字展览会，并且接见了各省、市参加这两个展览会的工作人员。
