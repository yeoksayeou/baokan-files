Title: Eisenhower Sees a Doctor (Picture)
Author: Hua Junwu
Date: 1960-06-26
Page: 8

    Eisenhower Sees a Doctor
    Hua Junwu
    "When did the rash start?" "It started the day I arrived in the Philippines."
    "When did you start to lose your hearing?" "When I crossed the Taiwan Strait."
    "When did you start having insomnia?" "Long ago! It started when socialist countries emerged."
    "When did your stomach start to swell?" "It started to bloat the day Nobusuke Kishi advised me to postpone my visit to Japan."
    "When did you lose those two big teeth?" "One fell out the day Syngman Rhee collapsed, and the other the day Menderes collapsed."
    Imperialism's life is not long!

### 艾森豪威尔看病（图片）
华君武
1960-06-26
第8版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔看病
    华君武
    “哪天起的疙瘩？”“到菲律宾那天就起的。”
    “哪天起耳朵就听不见了？”“过台湾海峡的时候。”
    “什么时候开始失眠的？”“早啦！打有社会主义国家时就失眠了。”
    “哪天肚子开始鼓起来的？”“岸信介劝我暂时不要访问日本那天就发胀的。”
    “两个大牙什么时候掉的？”“一个在李承晚垮台那天，一个在曼德列斯垮台那天。”
    帝国主义寿命不长了！
