Title: Japanese Painters Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd — The Japanese Painters Delegation, consisting of eleven members and led by Mr. Maeda Kōzō, arrived in Beijing today for a visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 日本画家代表团到京

1960-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    日本画家代表团到京
    据新华社2日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的日本画家代表团一行十一人，在团长前田康造先生率领下，今天到达北京。
