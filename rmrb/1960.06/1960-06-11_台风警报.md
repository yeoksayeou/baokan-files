Title: Typhoon Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-06-11
Page: 3

    Typhoon Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, June 9th: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon warning at 12:00 noon on the 9th: The South China Sea (1960 No. 1) typhoon made landfall near the Pearl River Estuary around midnight on the 8th. Affected by the typhoon, the northwestern part of the South China Sea and the central coastal areas of Guangdong experienced strong winds of force 9 to 11 in the past 24 hours; the area through which the typhoon center passed experienced winds of force 12. The eastern coastal areas of Guangdong and the northeastern part of the South China Sea experienced winds of force 7 to 8, and heavy rain occurred in the eastern and east-central parts of Guangdong.
    Xinhua News Agency, June 10th: At 10:00 AM today, the Meteorological Science Research Institute of the Central Meteorological Bureau issued a typhoon warning stating: The center of the South China Sea typhoon was located 110 kilometers northwest of Xiamen at 2:00 AM today, at 24.8 degrees north latitude and 117.1 degrees east longitude, with a maximum wind force of 10 near the center. In the past 24 hours, the typhoon center has moved northeastward, and its speed has increased. It is expected that the typhoon center will continue to move northeastward and will move out to sea along the northern coast of Fujian around noon today. In the next 36 hours, the wind force in the coastal areas of Fujian and the southern part of the East China Sea will increase to force 9 to 11; the northern part of the East China Sea, the southern part of the Yellow Sea, the coastal areas of Zhejiang, and the southern coastal areas of Jiangsu will experience strong winds of force 7 to 9; the northern coastal areas of Jiangsu will experience winds of force 6 to 8, and the eastern coastal areas of Guangdong and the Taiwan Strait will still experience winds of force 7 to 9. Fujian, Zhejiang and southern Jiangsu will experience heavy rain, and eastern Guangdong will still experience heavy rain.

### 台风警报

1960-06-11
第3版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    据新华社9日讯　中央气象科学研究所9日中午十二时发布台风警报：南海（1960年一号）台风，已于8日午夜前后在珠江口附近登陆。受台风的影响，过去二十四小时内，南海西北部，广东中部沿海曾有九至十一级大风；台风中心经过的地区，曾出现了十二级大风，广东东部沿海、南海东北部曾有七至八级大风，广东东部和中部偏东地区曾出现暴雨。
    新华社10日讯　今天上午十时，中央气象局气象科学研究所发布台风警报说：南海台风今天上午二时中心位置在厦门西北方一百一十公里的地方，即北纬二十四点八度，东经一百一十七点一度，中心附近最大风力有十级。过去二十四小时内，台风中心向东北方向移动，速度加快。预计未来台风中心将继续向东北方向移动，今天中午前后，将在福建北部沿海出海。未来三十六小时内，福建沿海、东海南部的风力将加大到九至十一级；东海北部、黄海南部、浙江沿海、江苏南部沿海将有七至九级大风；江苏北部沿海将有六至八级大风，广东东部沿海、台湾海峡仍有七至九级大风。福建、浙江和江苏南部将有暴雨，广东东部仍有暴雨。
