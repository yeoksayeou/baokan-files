Title: Comrade Peng Zhen Leads CCP Delegation Back to Beijing; Deng Xiaoping, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Tan Zhenlin and Other Comrades Welcome Them at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-06-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 28th. Peng Zhen, head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation invited to attend the Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party, delegation members Kang Sheng, Wu Xiuquan, and accompanying staff, returned to Beijing by special plane today. Xu Jianguo, member of the delegation and China's ambassador to Romania, also returned on the same plane.

Welcoming them at the airport were Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, and Tan Zhenlin, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Bo Yibo, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, Yang Shangkun and Liu Lantao, alternate secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, and Cai Chang and Liu Ningyi, members of the Central Committee, and others.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Soviet Ambassador to China Chervonenko, Counselor Sudarikov, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, Bashkilo, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 27th. The Chinese Communist Party delegation attending the Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party, led by Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, returned home by plane via Moscow this afternoon.

Greeting and seeing them off at the airport were Mukhitdinov, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Ustinov, First Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the CPSU, Medvedev, Deputy Minister of the Central Committee of the CPSU, Stan, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in the Soviet Union, and Zhang Weilie, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union. The delegation rested and had lunch at the airport on the way.

### 彭真同志率中共代表团返京  邓小平李富春李先念谭震林等同志到机场欢迎

1960-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真同志率中共代表团返京
    邓小平李富春李先念谭震林等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社28日讯　应邀参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长彭真，团员康生、伍修权，以及随行工作人员等，已于今天乘专机回到北京。代表团团员、我国驻罗马尼亚大使许建国也同机返京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央政治局委员李富春、李先念、谭震林，中央政治局候补委员薄一波，中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆、刘澜涛，中央委员蔡畅、刘宁一等。
    到机场欢迎的还有，苏联驻华大使契尔沃年科，参赞苏达利柯夫，罗马尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办巴什基洛等。
    新华社莫斯科27日电　参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会的中国共产党代表团，在中共中央政治局委员彭真率领下，于今天下午经莫斯科乘机返国。
    赴机场迎送的有苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员穆希金诺夫、苏共莫斯科市委第一书记乌斯季诺夫、苏共中央副部长麦特维杰夫、罗马尼亚驻苏联大使馆临时代办斯坦、中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办张伟烈等人。代表团在机场路事休息并进了午餐。
