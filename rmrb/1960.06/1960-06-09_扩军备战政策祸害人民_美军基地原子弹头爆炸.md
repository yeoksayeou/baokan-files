Title: Arms Expansion and War Preparations Policies Harm the People; US Military Base Atomic Warhead Explodes
Author:
Date: 1960-06-09
Page: 7

    Arms Expansion and War Preparations Policies Harm the People
    US Military Base Atomic Warhead Explodes
    Xinhua News Agency, June 8th: New York dispatch: The US government's policy of arms expansion and war preparations has brought disaster to the American people. Yesterday, at McGuire Air Force Base near Trenton, New Jersey, an atomic warhead of a "Bomarc" missile exploded, producing strong radiation and causing deep anxiety among local residents.

### 扩军备战政策祸害人民  美军基地原子弹头爆炸

1960-06-09
第7版()
专栏：

    扩军备战政策祸害人民
    美军基地原子弹头爆炸
    据新华社8日讯　纽约消息：美国政府的扩军备战政策给美国老百姓带来了灾难。昨天，在新泽西州特伦顿附近的麦盖尔空军基地，一颗“鲍马克式”导弹的原子弹头爆炸，爆炸产生了强烈辐射，引起当地居民的深切不安。
