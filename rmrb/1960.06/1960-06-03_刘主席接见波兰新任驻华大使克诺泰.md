Title: Chairman Liu Receives Newly Appointed Polish Ambassador to China, Knothe
Author:
Date: 1960-06-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd—Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the newly appointed Polish Ambassador to China, Jerzy Knothe, this afternoon and had a cordial conversation with him.  Present at the reception were Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and East European Affairs.  Also present at the reception was Mr. Frątczak, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China.

### 刘主席接见波兰新任驻华大使克诺泰

1960-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见波兰新任驻华大使克诺泰
    新华社2日讯　刘少奇主席今天下午接见了波兰新任驻中国大使耶日·克诺泰，并且进行了亲切的谈话。接见时在座的有外交部副部长罗贵波，苏联东欧司副司长余湛等。接见时波兰驻中国大使馆公使衔参赞傅拉托也在座。
