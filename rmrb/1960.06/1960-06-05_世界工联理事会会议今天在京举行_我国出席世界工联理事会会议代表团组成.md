Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Council Meeting Opens in Beijing Today; Composition of the Chinese Delegation to the World Federation of Trade Unions Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th -- The Secretariat of the World Federation of Trade Unions announced in Beijing today that the 11th Council Meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions will be held in Beijing from June 5th to 8th.

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th -- The All-China Federation of Trade Unions delegation to the 11th Council Meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions, which will be held in Beijing soon, has been formed. The members of the delegation are:

Head of Delegation: Liu Changsheng (Vice-President of the World Federation of Trade Unions);

Members: Zhu Xuefan (Council Member of the World Federation of Trade Unions), Kang Yonghe (Executive Committee Member of the World Federation of Trade Unions), Zhao Guoqiang (Alternate Council Member of the World Federation of Trade Unions), Chen Yu (Alternate Council Member of the World Federation of Trade Unions).

### 世界工联理事会会议今天在京举行  我国出席世界工联理事会会议代表团组成

1960-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    世界工联理事会会议今天在京举行
    我国出席世界工联理事会会议代表团组成
    新华社4日讯　世界工会联合会书记处今天在北京宣布：世界工会联合会第十一次理事会会议将在6月5日至8日在北京举行。
    新华社4日讯　出席即将在北京召开的世界工会联合会第十一次理事会会议的中华全国总工会代表团已经组成，代表团的成员是：
    团长：刘长胜（世界工联副主席）；
    团员：朱学范（世界工联理事），康永和（世界工联执行委员），赵国强（世界工联候补理事），陈宇（世界工联候补理事）。
