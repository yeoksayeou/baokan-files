Title: Indian Officials Participating in Sino-Indian Officials' Meeting Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-14
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, June 13th: The Indian officials participating in the Sino-Indian officials' meeting, consisting of the Director of the Northern Division of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, Mehta, the Director of the Historical Division, Gopal, and advisors, a total of five people, arrived in Beijing this afternoon by train from Guangzhou.

Those who greeted them at the station included the Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Wenjin, and the Special Commissioner of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Han Xu, and others. The Indian Ambassador to China, Parthasarathi, also greeted them at the station.

### 参加中印官员会晤的印度官员抵京

1960-06-14
第6版()
专栏：

    参加中印官员会晤的印度官员抵京
    新华社13日讯　参加中印官员会晤的印度官员：印度外交部北方司司长梅达、历史司司长戈帕尔和顾问等一行五人，在今天下午由广州乘火车到达北京。
    到车站迎接的，有外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋，外交部礼宾司专员韩叙等。印度驻中国大使帕塔萨拉蒂也到车站迎接。
