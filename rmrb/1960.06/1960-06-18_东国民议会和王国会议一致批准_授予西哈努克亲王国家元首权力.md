Title: National Assembly and Royal Council Unanimously Approve Granting Prince Sihanouk Powers of Head of State
Author:
Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, June 17th: It is announced here that the Cambodian National Assembly and Royal Council unanimously approved in a joint session this morning the granting of the powers and privileges of Head of State to Prince Sihanouk.

### 东国民议会和王国会议一致批准  授予西哈努克亲王国家元首权力

1960-06-18
第6版()
专栏：

    东国民议会和王国会议一致批准
    授予西哈努克亲王国家元首权力
    新华社金边17日电　这里宣布，柬埔寨国民议会和王国会议今天上午在联席会议上一致批准把国家元首的权力和特权授予西哈努克亲王。
