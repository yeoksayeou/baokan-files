Title: Prominent Argentine Lawyer Alfaro Departs Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1960-06-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th. Mr. Rodolfo Araoz Alfaro, a prominent Argentine lawyer, and his wife, Mrs. Margarita Aguirre, a famous writer, who visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, left Beijing for home today.

Chen Jin, Secretary-General of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, was at the airport to see them off.

### 阿根廷名律师阿尔法罗离京返国

1960-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    阿根廷名律师阿尔法罗离京返国
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，前来我国访问的阿根廷著名律师罗多尔夫·阿拉奥斯·阿尔法罗及其夫人、著名作家玛格丽达·阿盖莱，于今日离京返国。
    到机场欢送的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会总干事陈金。
