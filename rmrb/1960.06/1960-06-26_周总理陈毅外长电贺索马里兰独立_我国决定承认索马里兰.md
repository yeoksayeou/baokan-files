Title: Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen Congratulate Somaliland on its Independence; China Decides to Recognize Somaliland
Author:
Date: 1960-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th—Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China today sent a telegram to Mohamed Haji Ibrahim Egal, Prime Minister of Somaliland, congratulating Somaliland on its declaration of independence. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Mohamed Haji Ibrahim Egal, Prime Minister of Somaliland, Hargeisa, Somaliland:

On the occasion of Somaliland's declaration of independence, I extend my sincere congratulations to the government and people of Somaliland on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China. I wish the people of Somaliland new achievements in their struggle to safeguard national independence and adhere to the path of independent national development. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

            Zhou Enlai
            Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
            June 25, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th—Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, today sent a telegram to Mohamed Haji Ibrahim Egal, Prime Minister of Somaliland, congratulating Somaliland on its independence and informing him that the Chinese government has decided to recognize Somaliland. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Mohamed Haji Ibrahim Egal, Prime Minister of Somaliland, Hargeisa, Somaliland:

I am pleased to learn of Somaliland's declaration of independence, and I extend my sincere congratulations to the government of Somaliland on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China. I am honored to inform Your Excellency that the government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize Somaliland. I hope that our government's recognition of your country will contribute to the further development of friendly relations between China and Somaliland.

            Chen Yi
            Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
            Beijing, June 25, 1960

### 周总理陈毅外长电贺索马里兰独立  我国决定承认索马里兰

1960-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈毅外长电贺索马里兰独立
    我国决定承认索马里兰
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给索马里兰总理穆罕默德·哈吉·易卜拉欣·埃加勒，祝贺索马里兰宣告独立，电文如下：索马里兰　哈尔格萨索马里兰总理穆罕默德·哈吉·易卜拉欣·埃加勒先生阁下：
    值此索马里兰宣布独立的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向索马里兰政府和人民致以诚挚的祝贺。祝索马里兰人民在维护民族独立、坚持民族独立发展道路的斗争中取得新的成就，祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                1960年6月25日
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天打电报给索马里兰总理穆罕默德·哈吉·易卜拉欣·埃加勒，祝贺索马里兰独立，并通知他，中国政府已经决定承认索马里兰。电文如下：索马里兰　哈尔格萨索马里兰总理穆罕默德·哈吉·易卜拉欣·埃加勒先生阁下：
    欣闻索马里兰宣布独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向索马里兰政府致以诚挚的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认索马里兰。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将有助于中、索两国友好关系的进一步发展。
              中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅
                        1960年6月25日于北京
