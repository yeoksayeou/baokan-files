Title: Typhoon Alert
Author:
Date: 1960-06-28
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon warning at 12:00 noon on the 27th: The third typhoon of 1960 in the Pacific Ocean has made landfall in the Philippines. At 2:00 AM this morning, the center was located near 14.7 degrees north latitude and 121.5 degrees east longitude, with a maximum wind force of level 12 or above near the center. The typhoon will enter the South China Sea and move northwest at a speed of 8-11 knots per hour. It is expected that from the afternoon of the 28th to the morning of the 29th, the wind force in the northwestern part of the South China Sea will gradually increase to level 9-12, and the wind force in the northeastern part of the South China Sea and the central and eastern coastal areas of Guangdong will increase to level 6-9. Heavy rain to torrential rain will begin to appear in the central and eastern coastal areas of Guangdong. It is expected that the typhoon will make landfall in the central and eastern coastal areas of Guangdong on the 29th.

### 台风警报

1960-06-28
第7版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    据新华社27日讯　中央气象科学研究所27日中午十二时发布台风警报：太平洋上的1960年第三号台风，已在菲律宾登陆，今天早晨二时中心位置在北纬十四点七度东经一百二十一点五度附近，中心附近最大风力在十二级以上。未来台风将进入南海，并以每小时八—十一浬的速度向西北方向移动，预计28日下午以后到29日早晨，南海西北部的风力将逐渐加大到九—十二级，南海东北部和广东中部东部沿海的风力将加大到六—九级，广东中部东部沿海并将开始出现大雨到暴雨。预计29日台风将在广东中部东部一带沿海登陆。
