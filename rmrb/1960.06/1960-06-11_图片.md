Date: 1960-06-11

    From June 1st, Chongqing Third Steel Plant began to achieve a substantial increase in production. In the week from the 1st to the 7th, the average daily steel output increased by 21% compared to the average daily output in May. In the battle to seize steel, leaders at all levels went deep into the front line of production, setting up camp in the workshops. Li Zhongyue (right), head of the organization department of the factory's Party committee, serves as the general branch secretary in the open-hearth furnace workshop. He is studying production with the workers.

    重庆第三钢铁厂六月一日开始就实现大幅度增产，一日至七日一周，钢的平均日产量比五月份平均日产量增加百分之二十一。在夺钢大战中，各级领导干部深入生产第一线，在车间安营扎寨。工厂党委组织部长李中岳（右）到平炉车间担任总支书记，他正和工人们一起研究生产。　　　新华社记者　刘诗临摄
