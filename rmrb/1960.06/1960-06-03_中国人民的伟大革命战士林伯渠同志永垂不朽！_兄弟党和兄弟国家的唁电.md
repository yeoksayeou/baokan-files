Title: Eternal Glory to Comrade Lin Boqu, Great Revolutionary Fighter of the Chinese People! Telegrams of Condolence from Fraternal Parties and Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-06-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd.

    Eternal Glory to Comrade Lin Boqu, Great Revolutionary Fighter of the Chinese People!
    Telegrams of Condolence from Fraternal Parties and Fraternal Countries
    Telegram of Condolence from Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party
    Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China received a telegram of condolence from Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, on the passing of Comrade Lin Boqu. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:
    I am deeply saddened by the passing of my dear friend, Comrade Lin Boqu. Comrade Lin Boqu was a loyal son of the Chinese people, who dedicated his life's energy to the great cause of communism. His passing is a great loss to the Chinese people and the world proletariat. I extend my deepest condolences to you and, through you, to the family of Comrade Lin Boqu.
                                                Ho Chi Minh
                                          May 31, 1960, Hanoi
    Letter of Condolence from the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party
    Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China received a letter of condolence from the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party on the passing of Comrade Lin Boqu. The full text of the letter is as follows:
    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:
    We were deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Lin Boqu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, and an outstanding veteran communist fighter who had made many contributions to China's national liberation and socialist construction, and a loyal son of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people, on May 29, 1960.
    On behalf of all members of the Vietnamese Labor Party and the Vietnamese people, we extend our deepest condolences to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the family of Comrade Lin Boqu.
                                      Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party
                                      May 29, 1960, Hanoi
    Telegram of Condolence from Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd. Chairman Zhu De received a telegram of condolence from Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on the passing of Comrade Lin Boqu. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China:
    I am deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Lin Boqu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, an old revolutionary and excellent political activist of the Chinese people. On behalf of the National Assembly and its Standing Committee of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I extend my deepest condolences to the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people, as well as the family of Comrade Lin Boqu.
    Comrade Lin Boqu devoted his life to the Party and the people, dedicating himself to the revolutionary cause. This noble example is worthy of emulation by each and every one of us. At this sorrowful moment, the cadres and people of Vietnam share the grief over the unfortunate passing of Comrade Lin Boqu and express deep regret for this great loss to the Chinese people and the People's Republic of China.
                Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
                                      May 31, 1960, Hanoi
    Telegram of Condolence from Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea
    Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd. Chairman Zhu De received a telegram of condolence from Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on the passing of Comrade Lin Boqu. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committe of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China:
    Deeply grieved to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Lin Boqu, a faithful comrade-in-arms of Comrade Mao Zedong, infinitely loyal to the Communist Party of China and the people, and who dedicated his life to the great cause of communism, I extend, in the name of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and myself, my deepest condolences to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China and to you.
  Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
                                        May 31, 1960, Pyongyang
    Telegram of Condolence from the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic
    Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council of the People's Republic of China received a telegram of condolence from the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic on the passing of Comrade Lin Boqu. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China: To the State Council of the People's Republic of China:
    We were deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Lin Boqu, an outstanding state activist of the People's Republic of China, one of the earliest members of the Communist Party of China, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China. The Central Commitee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic extend their deepest condolences.
                                  Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party
                                    Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic
    Telegram of Condolence from Zh. Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, and B. Jargalsaikhan, Chairman of the Great People's Hural
    Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd. Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Chairman Zhu De received a telegram of condolence from Zh. Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, and B. Jargalsaikhan, Chairman of the Great People's Hural, on the passing of Comrade Lin Boqu. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China: To Comrade Zhu De, Chairman the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China:
    It is with extreme sadness that we learned of the passing of Comrade Lin Boqu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China. Comrade Lin Boqu was one of the outstanding political activists of the People's Republic of China, a passionate fighter for the bright future of the great Chinese people. He was an ardent defender of the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples, and of the expansion of ties between the highest state organs of the two countries—the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic and the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China. The Mongolian people and their Great People's Hural will forever cherish the memory of Comrade Lin Boqu.
            Zh. Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic
            B. Jargalsaikhan, Chairman of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic
                                    May 30, 1960, Ulaanbaatar

### 中国人民的伟大革命战士林伯渠同志永垂不朽！  兄弟党和兄弟国家的唁电

1960-06-03
第2版()
专栏：

    中国人民的伟大革命战士林伯渠同志永垂不朽！
    兄弟党和兄弟国家的唁电
    越南劳动党中央委员会主席胡志明的唁电
    新华社2日讯　中国共产党中央委员会收到了越南劳动党中央委员会主席胡志明发来的吊唁林伯渠同志逝世的电报。电报全文如下：中国共产党中央委员会：
    我亲爱的朋友林伯渠同志逝世，使我十分悲痛。林伯渠同志是中国人民的忠诚儿子，他把毕生的精力贡献于伟大的共产主义事业，他的逝世，对中国人民，对世界无产阶级是一种巨大的损失。我谨向您们，并通过您们向林伯渠同志的家属表示深切的哀悼。
                                                胡志明
                                          1960年5月31日河内
    越南劳动党中央委员会的唁信
    新华社2日讯　中国共产党中央委员会收到了越南劳动党中央委员会吊唁林伯渠同志逝世的信件。信的全文如下：中国共产党中央委员会：
    惊闻中国共产党中央政治局委员、中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、曾对中国的民族解放事业和社会主义建设事业作出许多贡献的杰出的老共产主义战士、中国共产党和中国人民忠诚的儿子林伯渠同志于1960年5月29日不幸逝世，我们感到万分痛惜。
    我们谨代表越南劳动党全体党员和越南人民，向中国共产党中央委员会和林伯渠同志的家属致以最深切的哀悼。
                                      越南劳动党中央委员会
                                      1960年5月29日于河内
    越南国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜的唁电
    新华社2日讯　朱德委员长收到了越南民主共和国国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜发来的吊唁林伯渠同志逝世的电报。电文如下：中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德同志：
    惊闻中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国人民的老革命家和优秀的政治活动家林伯渠同志不幸逝世，我感到万分痛惜，谨代表越南民主共和国国会和国会常务委员会，向中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会、中国共产党和中国人民以及林伯渠同志的家属表示深切的哀悼。
    林伯渠同志毕生忠诚于党和人民，把自己的一生献给了革命事业，他这种崇高的榜样值得我们每一个人学习。在这个沉痛的时刻，越南干部和人民对于林伯渠同志的不幸逝世同样感到悲痛，对于中国人民和中华人民共和国这一巨大损失表示深切的惋惜。
                越南民主共和国国会常务委员会委员长　孙德胜
                                      1960年5月31日于河内
    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健的唁电
    新华社2日讯　朱德委员长收到了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健发来的吊唁林伯渠同志逝世的电报。电文如下：中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德同志：
    惊悉毛泽东同志忠实的战友、无限忠于中国共产党和人民并把自己的一生献给共产主义伟大事业的林伯渠同志不幸逝世，我谨以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会和我个人的名义，向中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会和您表示沉痛的哀悼。
