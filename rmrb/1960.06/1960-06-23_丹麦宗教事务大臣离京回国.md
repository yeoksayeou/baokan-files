Title: Danish Minister of Ecclesiastical Affairs Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-06-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st:  The Danish Minister of Ecclesiastical Affairs, Mrs. Bodil Koch, along with her husband, Professor Hal Koch, and daughter, Miss Marianne Koch, left Beijing by plane this morning after visiting Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Wuhan, to return home via Moscow.

Those seeing them off at the airport included: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation Li Dequan, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council He Chengxiang, and Deputy Director of the Western European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Song Zhiguang, among others. Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Danish Embassy in China, Cao Ersen, was also present at the airport to see them off.

On the evening of the 19th, Minister Shen Yanbing hosted a farewell reception for Koch at the Beijing Hotel.

### 丹麦宗教事务大臣离京回国

1960-06-23
第6版()
专栏：

    丹麦宗教事务大臣离京回国
    新华社21日讯　丹麦宗教事务大臣博迪尔·科克夫人和她的丈夫海尔·科克教授、女儿玛丽安娜·科克小姐等，在访问了北京、南京、上海、杭州和武汉等地后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京，取道莫斯科回国。
    到机场送行的有：文化部部长沈雁冰、全国妇联副主席李德全、国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，外交部西欧司副司长宋之光等。丹麦驻华使馆临时代办曹尔生也到机场送行。
    19日晚，沈雁冰部长在北京饭店为科克举行了欢送酒会。
