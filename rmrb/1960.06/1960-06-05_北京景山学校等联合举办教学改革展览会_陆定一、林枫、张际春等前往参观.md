Title: Beijing Jingshan School and Others Jointly Hold Teaching Reform Exhibition; Lu Dingyi, Lin Feng, Zhang Jichun and Others Visit

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-05
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Beijing Jingshan School and the Experimental School Affiliated to Beijing Normal University jointly held a teaching reform exhibition. Lu Dingyi, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Lin Feng, member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, visited the exhibition on the afternoon of June 3rd. These schools are conducting experiments according to the teaching reform plan proposed by Beijing Normal University. After the visit, Lu Dingyi and Lin Feng met with Liu Yongru, First Secretary of the Party Committee of Beijing Normal University, some teachers and students, and some teachers from Beijing Jingshan School and other schools. They praised their hard work and the initial achievements they had made, and believed that this was an important contribution to the teaching reform of primary and secondary schools. They encouraged them to continue their efforts in teaching reform, guard against arrogance and rashness, and combine the spirit of bold experimentation with a down-to-earth work style.

Zhang Jichun, Director of the Cultural and Educational Office of the State Council, Minister of Education Yang Xiufeng, and Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee Zhang Ziyi also visited the exhibition on the afternoon of June 4th. (Attached picture)

Lu Dingyi (first from left) and Comrade Lin Feng visiting the teaching reform exhibition jointly held by Beijing Jingshan School and the Experimental School Affiliated to Beijing Normal University. Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Changyong.

### 北京景山学校等联合举办教学改革展览会  陆定一、林枫、张际春等前往参观

1960-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

    北京景山学校等联合举办教学改革展览会
    陆定一、林枫、张际春等前往参观
    新华社4日讯　中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理陆定一和中共中央委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫，6月3日下午参观了北京景山学校和北京师范大学附属实验学校联合举办的教学改革展览会，这几个学校是按照北京师范大学提出的教学改革方案进行试验的。陆定一和林枫在参观后，接见了北京师范大学党委第一书记刘墉如、部分师生和北京景山学校等校的部分教师，赞扬他们的辛勤劳动和开始取得的成绩，认为这是对中小学教学改革的重要贡献，鼓励他们在教学改革工作中继续努力，戒骄戒躁，把大胆试验的精神和踏踏实实的作风结合起来。
    国务院文教办公室主任张际春，教育部长杨秀峰，中共中央宣传部副部长张子意，6月4日下午也参观了这个展览会。（附图片）
