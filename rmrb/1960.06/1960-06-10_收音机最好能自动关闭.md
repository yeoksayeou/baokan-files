Title: It would be best if radios could automatically shut off
Author: Gan Youwei
Date: 1960-06-10
Page: 2

Letter to the Editor:

    It would be best if radios could automatically shut off
    When we listen to the radio, sometimes we listen for too long, or we forget to turn the radio off, which damages the vacuum tubes. It is hoped that radio factories will find a way to install an automatic control device inside the radio, so that the radio maintains a certain temperature. If the temperature exceeds a certain level, the radio can automatically shut off to avoid damage.
    Gan Youwei

### 收音机最好能自动关闭
甘有为
1960-06-10
第2版()
专栏：来信

    收音机最好能自动关闭
    我们在听收音机的时候，有时听得时间过长，或者忘了关收音机，以致损坏了电子管。希望无线电厂设法在收音机内部安装一个自动控制设备，使收音机保持一定温度，超过一定温度，收音机就可以自动关闭，以免损坏。
    甘有为
