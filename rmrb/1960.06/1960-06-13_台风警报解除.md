Title: Typhoon Warning Lifted
Author:
Date: 1960-06-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The Central Meteorological Science Institute issued a typhoon warning cancellation notice at 12:00 noon today:

　　Typhoon No. 1 of 1960, originating from the South China Sea, went out to sea near the junction of Fujian and Zhejiang provinces yesterday afternoon. At 2:00 AM today, the typhoon center was on the East China Sea, approximately 450 kilometers east of Shanghai. Currently, the typhoon center is accelerating its movement towards the east-northeast direction, heading towards Japan. Except for the East China Sea, which will still experience strong winds of force 6 to 8 today, the impact will weaken thereafter, and the typhoon warning is lifted.

### 台风警报解除

1960-06-13
第3版()
专栏：

    台风警报解除
    新华社11日讯　今天中午十二时中央气象科学研究所发布台风解除警报通知：
    从南海来的（60年一号）台风，于昨天下午在福建、浙江交界附近出海。今天早晨二时，台风中心在上海以东四百五十公里左右的东海海面上。目前，台风中心正在加速向东偏北方向移往日本。除今天白天东海海面仍有六至八级的大风外，以后影响将减弱，台风警报解除。
