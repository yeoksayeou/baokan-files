Inspecting the Great Achievements of the Cultural Revolution; Promoting a New Upsurge of the Cultural Revolution
    National Congress of Cultural and Educational Heroes Grandly Opens
    Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Deng Xiaoping, and others attended the conference
    Lu Dingyi delivered a congratulatory speech on behalf of the CCP Central Committee and the State Council; Lin Feng delivered a report to the conference
    (Xinhua News Agency, June 1st) The National Congress of Advanced Units and Advanced Workers in Socialist Construction in Education, Culture, Health, Sports, and News is grandly opened in the Great Hall of the People this afternoon. This is an unprecedented gathering of heroes on the cultural and educational front since the founding of New China, a general review of the great achievements made in the current upsurge of the cultural revolution in our country, and a mobilization meeting to push our country's cultural revolution to a new upsurge.
    Lu Dingyi, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, pointed out in his congratulatory speech on behalf of the CCP Central Committee and the State Council: The emergence of many advanced units and advanced workers on the cultural and educational front in our country marks the beginning of great achievements in our country's cultural revolution, and marks the initial formation of a contingent of cultural and educational cadres with proletarian intellectuals as the backbone. The Party Central Committee and the State Council believe that such a conference should be convened to commend the advanced, exchange experiences, strengthen unity, muster all our energy, and push our country's cultural revolution to a new upsurge, to cooperate with the great leap forward in industrial and agricultural production in our country, to cooperate with the people's commune movement in rural and urban areas, to cooperate with the technical innovation and technical revolution movement, and greatly promote our country's socialist construction.
    More than 6,000 representatives from 28 provinces, municipalities, autonomous regions, central government organs, and various units of the People's Liberation Army are all vanguards emerging from the high tide of our cultural revolution. They have been valued by the Party and the state and respected by the people of the whole country for their extraordinary achievements in the cause of building and defending the motherland. At today's meeting, everyone's face looked radiant and full of smiles.
    The magnificent Great Hall of the People was filled with joy today because of the presence of this new batch of heroes and heroines of the motherland. On the rostrum, a portrait of the great leader Chairman Mao Zedong was hung, flanked by ten huge red flags, and the front of the stage was covered with flowers and evergreen grass.
    Party and state leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Deng Xiaoping, and others attended today's meeting. When they ascended the rostrum, all the representatives stood up, and a long and thunderous applause erupted in the hall.
    Also seated on the rostrum were Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Liu Bocheng, Members of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee, Lu Dingyi, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CCP Central Committee, Liu Lantao, Alternate Secretary, Luo Ruiqing and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premiers of the State Council, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Saifuddin, He Xiangning, and Lin Feng, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhang Zhizhong and Fu Zuoyi, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council, Li Siguang, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Xie Juezai, President of the Supreme People's Court, and the heads of various departments of the CCP Central Committee, various departments of the central state organs, various people's organizations, and all members of the presidium of the conference.

### 全国文教群英大会隆重开幕

1960-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    检阅文化革命的巨大成就　推动文化革命的新的高潮
    全国文教群英大会隆重开幕
    刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、宋庆龄、董必武、邓小平等出席大会
    陆定一代表中共中央和国务院致祝词　林枫向大会作了报告
    据新华社1日讯　全国教育和文化、卫生、体育、新闻方面社会主义建设先进单位和先进工作者代表大会，今天下午在人民大会堂隆重开幕。这是新中国诞生以来文教战线上一次空前盛大的群英会，是对当前我国文化革命高潮中所取得的巨大成就的一次总检阅，也是把我国文化革命推向新的高潮的一个动员大会。
    中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理陆定一在代表中共中央和国务院向大会致祝词时指出：我国文化教育战线上许多先进单位和先进工作者的出现，标志着我国的文化革命已经开始取得巨大的成绩，标志着以无产阶级知识分子为骨干的文化教育干部队伍已经开始形成。党中央和国务院认为应该召集这样一个大会，表扬先进，交流经验，加强团结，鼓足干劲，把我国的文化革命推向一个新的高潮，来配合我国工农业生产的大跃进，配合乡村和城市的人民公社化运动，配合技术革新和技术革命运动，大大地促进我国的社会主义建设。
    来自二十八个省、市、自治区和中央机关以及人民解放军各部队的六千多名代表，都是我国文化革命高潮中涌现出来的尖兵。他们由于在建设祖国和保卫祖国的事业中，做出了不平凡的成绩而受到党和国家的重视，受到全国人民的尊敬。在今天的大会上，他们每一个人脸上都显得是那样容光焕发，喜笑颜开。
    宏伟的人民大会堂今天由于这又一批祖国的英雄儿女的光临而充满着喜气洋洋的景象。在主席台上，悬挂着伟大领袖毛泽东主席的画像，两旁矗立着十面巨幅的红旗，台前布满着鲜花和常青草。
    党和国家的领导人刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、宋庆龄、董必武、邓小平等出席了今天的大会。当他们登上主席台时，全体代表起立，会场上响起了长时间的暴风雨般的掌声。
    在主席台上就座的还有中共中央政治局委员彭真、陈毅、李富春、刘伯承，政治局候补委员陆定一，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，候补书记刘澜涛，国务院副总理罗瑞卿、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、赛福鼎、何香凝、林枫，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义，政协全国委员会副主席李四光，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，以及中共中央各部门、中央国家机关各部门、各人民团体的负责人和大会主席团的全体成员。
    下午三时半，中共中央委员、国务院文教办公室主任张际春宣布大会开幕。乐队奏起庄严的国歌。
    接着，在六千多人长时间的热烈掌声中，中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理陆定一向大会致祝词。他代表中共中央和国务院向大会致热烈的祝贺，并且通过全体代表，向全国七百多万专业的文化教育工作者和三千多万业余的文化教育工作者致热烈的祝贺（祝词全文见本版）。
    陆定一同志的祝词，给予全体代表以极大的启示和鼓舞。他的祝词不断为全场一阵又一阵的热烈掌声所打断。
    中共中央委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫接着以“大搞文化革命，实现工农群众知识化，知识分子劳动化”为题，向大会作报告。
    林枫在他长达一万六千多字的报告里，向全国文化教育工作者提出：为了在将来实现共产主义这一崇高的目的，文化教育战线的根本任务是，在党中央和毛泽东同志的领导下，在社会主义建设总路线的光辉照耀下，把政治思想战线上的社会主义革命进行到底，彻底肃清资产阶级的政治影响和思想影响，极大地提高全体人民的共产主义思想觉悟和道德品质，普及和提高全民教育，为消灭工农差别、城乡差别、脑力劳动和体力劳动的差别而积极奋斗（报告全文见第三版）。
    林枫的报告在热烈的掌声中结束后，大会宣布休会。
    明天，大会将继续举行。（附图片）
