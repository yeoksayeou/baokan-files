Title: Second Group of Mountaineering Heroes Leaves Base Camp for Xigazê
Author:
Date: 1960-06-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Qomolangma, June 6th. The second group of members of the Chinese mountaineering team today left the mountaineering team's base camp at 5,120 meters above sea level at the foot of Qomolangma and headed to Lhasa via Xigazê.

Just as the battle to conquer Qomolangma was coming to an end, these team members braved the fierce wind and snow of the Qomolangma area, traveling back and forth between steep mountain ridges, transporting various equipment and supplies from the high mountain camps to the base camp below.

### 第二批登山英雄离大本营回日喀则

1960-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    第二批登山英雄离大本营回日喀则
    新华社珠穆朗玛6日电　中国登山队第二批队员，今天离开珠穆朗玛峰下拔海五千一百二十米的登山队大本营，取道日喀则回拉萨。
    在征服珠穆朗玛峰的战斗刚刚结束，这批队员们又冒着珠穆朗玛山区狂暴的风雪，往返在陡峻的山岭间，把高山营地上的各种装备和物资，运到山下大本营。
