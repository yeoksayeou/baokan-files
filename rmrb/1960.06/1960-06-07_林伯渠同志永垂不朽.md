Title: Eternal Glory to Comrade Lin Boqu

Author:
Date: 1960-06-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China today received a telegram of condolence from Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, on the passing of Comrade Lin Boqu. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, dear comrades:

The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany expresses its deepest condolences on the passing of Comrade Lin Boqu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China.

Together with you, we pay tribute to the respected deceased, one of the oldest and earliest members of the Communist Party of China, a passionate patriot, and an outstanding fighter for the socialist construction of the People's Republic of China.

Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany
June 4, 1960

Telegram of Condolence from the Shandong Provincial Committee of the CPC and the Shandong Provincial People's Committee

We were deeply saddened by the news of Comrade Lin Boqu's passing. We extend our deepest condolences on behalf of all Party members and the people of Shandong!

We will actively respond to the call of the Party Central Committee, conscientiously learn from Comrade Lin Boqu's noble revolutionary spirit, the firm stance in safeguarding the Party's correct line, and the style of maintaining close ties with the masses and hard work and simplicity. Under the leadership of the Party Central Committee headed by Comrade Mao Zedong, we will accelerate the various socialist construction undertakings and vow to use practical actions to strive for greater leaps forward to mourn Comrade Lin Boqu!

The Shandong Provincial Committee of the CPC
The Shandong Provincial People's Committee

Telegram of Condolence from the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the CPC

We were deeply saddened to learn of Comrade Lin Boqu's unfortunate passing just as we were holding an enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the Provincial Party Committee.

Comrade Lin Boqu was one of the great revolutionary fighters and outstanding activists of our people. In the long revolutionary struggle, he consistently adhered to the victorious banner of Comrade Mao Zedong and made indelible contributions to the cause of the Party and the people. The passing of Comrade Lin Boqu is a major loss to the Party and the people.

Comrade Lin Boqu has left us forever, but his noble revolutionary spirit will live on forever! All Party members and the people of Zhejiang resolutely respond to the call of the Party Central Committee, learn from Comrade Lin Boqu's great revolutionary fighting spirit, the firm stance in safeguarding the Party's correct line, and the style of maintaining close ties with the masses and hard work and simplicity. Under the leadership of the Party Central Committee headed by Comrade Mao Zedong, we will unite as one and strive to build our country into a powerful socialist country with modern industry, modern agriculture and a modern science and culture.

The Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China
May 30, 1960

Telegram of Condolence from the Third Congress of the CPC Qinghai Provincial Committee

The Third Congress of the Communist Party of China Qinghai Provincial Committee, while in session, was shocked to read the obituary issued by the Party Central Committee regarding the passing of Comrade Lin Boqu, member of the Central Committee, member of the Central Political Bureau, outstanding political activist of China, and great fighter of the Chinese people. All representatives are overwhelmed with grief and are determined to transform grief into strength, learn from Comrade Lin Boqu's revolutionary spirit of lifelong loyalty to the Party and the people, and dedicating his life's energy to the great cause of communism, unite and lead the people of all ethnic groups in the province, and inspire greater revolutionary vigor to strive to accelerate the cause of socialist construction. We hereby send a telegram of condolence. Eternal glory to the respected Comrade Lin Boqu!

The Third Congress of the Communist Party of China Qinghai Provincial Committee
May 30, 1960

### 林伯渠同志永垂不朽

1960-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    林伯渠同志永垂不朽
    德国统一社会党中央委员会  第一书记乌布利希的唁电
    新华社6日讯　中国共产党中央委员会今天收到了德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记瓦·乌布利希发来的吊唁林伯渠同志逝世的电报。电文如下：中国共产党中央委员会敬爱的同志们：
    德国统一社会党中央委员会对中国共产党中央委员会政治局委员、中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠同志的逝世表示深切的悼念。
    我们和您们一起向尊敬的去世者、向中国共产党最老的和最早的党员之一、热忱的爱国者、中华人民共和国社会主义建设的卓越战士致敬。
        德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记　瓦·乌布利希
                                 1960年6月4日
    中共山东省委和省人委的唁电
    林伯渠同志逝世的噩耗传来后，我们感到万分悲痛，谨代表山东全体党员和全省人民深致哀悼！
    我们一定积极响应党中央的号召，认真学习林伯渠同志的崇高的革命精神，维护党的正确路线的坚定立场和密切联系群众、艰苦朴素的作风，在以毛泽东同志为首的党中央的领导下，加速进行各项社会主义建设事业誓以争取更大跃进的实际行动，来悼念林伯渠同志！
                              中共山东省委山东省人民委员会
    中共浙江省委的唁电
    正当我们召开省委常委扩大会议的时候，惊悉林伯渠同志不幸逝世，万分悲痛。
    林伯渠同志是我国人民伟大的革命战士和杰出的活动家之一。他在长期的革命斗争中，一贯坚持毛泽东同志的胜利旗帜，对党对人民的事业作出了不可磨灭的贡献。林伯渠同志的逝世，是党和人民的重大损失。
    林伯渠同志和我们永别了，但是，他的崇高的革命精神，永垂不朽！我们浙江全体党员和全省人民坚决响应党中央的号召，学习林伯渠同志伟大的革命斗争精神，维护党的正确路线的坚定立场和密切联系群众、艰苦朴素的作风，在以毛泽东同志为首的党中央的领导下，团结一致，为把我国建设成为一个具有现代工业、现代农业、现代科学文化的强大的社会主义国家而奋斗。
                                中国共产党浙江省委员会
                                            1960年5月30日
    中共青海省第三届代表大会的唁电
    中国共产党青海省第三届代表大会，正在开会期间，惊读党中央发布的中央委员会、中央政治局委员、中国优秀的政治活动家，中国人民的伟大战士林伯渠同志逝世的讣告，全体代表，不胜沉痛，决心化悲痛为力量，学习林伯渠同志一生对党对人民无限忠诚，把毕生精力贡献于伟大的共产主义事业的革命精神，团结领导全省各族人民，鼓足更大的革命干劲，为加速社会主义建设事业而奋斗。特驰电吊唁。敬爱的林伯渠同志永垂不朽！
                          中国共产党青海省第三届代表大会
                                          1960年5月30日
