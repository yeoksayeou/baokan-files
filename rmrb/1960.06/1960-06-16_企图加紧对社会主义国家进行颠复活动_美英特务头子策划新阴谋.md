Title: Espionage Chiefs of US and UK Plot New Conspiracy, Attempting to Intensify Subversive Activities Against Socialist Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-06-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, June 14: US espionage chief Allen Dulles is currently in London holding secret talks with the British Military Intelligence, in order to intensify subversive activities against socialist countries.

"Daily Mail" today published an article by Stevenson Pue saying that Allen Dulles, who arrived at London Airport without prior announcement, although he said he was here purely for a private visit, "yesterday visited the British espionage chief in the Military Intelligence office in Whitehall." The author said that Allen Dulles' "real purpose of this visit is to discuss a major reorganization of Western intelligence agencies with the British spy chief to make up for the gap caused by the downing of the U-2 aircraft."

The author continued: "The talks currently underway are likely to bring the relationship between the US Central Intelligence Agency and British Military Intelligence closer, and the two agencies are expected to develop a new system for collecting and cross-checking intelligence."

The author pointed out that the United States used U-2 spy planes in the past to gather intelligence on the Soviet Union's military and industrial dynamics.

He said: "For four years, in addition to investigating the development of the Russian rocket forces, U-2 aircraft have also provided target maps to the US Air Force and the British Bomber Command.

"The problem currently facing Allen Dulles and his British colleagues is: how to obtain this valuable intelligence from other sources."

### 企图加紧对社会主义国家进行颠复活动  美英特务头子策划新阴谋

1960-06-16
第6版()
专栏：

    企图加紧对社会主义国家进行颠复活动
    美英特务头子策划新阴谋
    新华社伦敦14日电　美国特务头子艾伦·杜勒斯目前正在伦敦同英国军事情报局举行秘密会谈，以加紧对社会主义国家进行颠复活动。
    “每日邮报”今天发表的史蒂文森·普的一篇报道说，没有事先宣布而到达伦敦机场的艾伦·杜勒斯，尽管他说他到这里来完全是进行私人访问，但是他“昨天在白厅的军事情报局办公室里访问了英国特务头子”。作者说，艾伦·杜勒斯“这次访问的真正目的，是要同英国间谍头子讨论大大改组西方情报机构的问题，以弥补由于U—2型飞机被击落而造成的缺口”。
    作者接着说：“目前正在进行的会谈很可能使美国中央情报局和英国军事情报局的关系更加密切，预料这两个机构将拟订一个关于汇集和互相核对情报的新制度。”
    作者指出，美国过去用U—2型间谍飞机来搜集苏联军事和工业动态的情报。
    他说：“四年来，U—2型飞机除了调查俄国火箭部队的发展情况之外，还向美国空军和英国轰炸机司令部提供了目标地图。
  “艾伦·杜勒斯和他的英国同僚们目前所面临的问题是：如何从其它来源获得这种宝贵的情报。”
