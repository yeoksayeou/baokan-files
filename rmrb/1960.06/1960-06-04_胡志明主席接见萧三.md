Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives Xiao San
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 3rd: This newspaper reported today that Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received the Chinese poet Xiao San, who is currently visiting Vietnam, on June 1st.
    Present at the meeting were Vietnamese poet To Huu and He Wei, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 胡志明主席接见萧三

1960-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席接见萧三
    新华社河内3日电　此间报纸今天报道：越南民主共和国主席胡志明1日接见了正在越南访问的中国诗人萧三。
    接见时，在座的有越南诗人素友和中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使何伟。
