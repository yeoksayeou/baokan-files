Title: Director of the Mongolian Customs Administration Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-12
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Dashzhabu, Director of the Customs Administration of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Mongolian People's Republic, and his party of five, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of our country.
    Hu Renkui, Deputy Director of the General Administration of Customs of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 蒙古海关总署署长到京

1960-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古海关总署署长到京
    新华社10日讯　应邀来我国访问的蒙古人民共和国对外贸易部海关总署署长达什扎布等一行五人，已于今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有我对外贸易部海关总署副署长胡仁奎等人。
