Title: Liu Changsheng Sends Congratulations on Somaliland's Independence; Chinese People Support the Somaliland People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-06-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th. Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, today sent a telegram to Mohamed Haji Ibrahim Egal, Prime Minister of Somaliland, warmly congratulating Somaliland on its independence.

In his congratulatory telegram, Liu Changsheng said: "The Chinese people have always been concerned about and supported the Somaliland people's struggle for national independence and are therefore deeply pleased with the achievements they have made in this struggle. In their struggle to safeguard national independence, the Somaliland people can always count on the resolute support of the 650 million Chinese people. May the friendship between the Chinese and Somaliland people grow stronger day by day!"

### 刘长胜电贺索马里兰独立  中国人民支持索马里兰人民斗争

1960-06-30
第5版()
专栏：

    刘长胜电贺索马里兰独立
    中国人民支持索马里兰人民斗争
    新华社29日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜今天致电索马里兰总理穆罕默德·哈吉·易卜拉欣·埃加勒，热烈祝贺索马里兰独立。
    刘长胜在贺电中说：“中国人民一向关怀和支持索马里兰人民争取民族独立的斗争，因而深深为他们在这个斗争中所取得的成就而高兴。索马里兰人民在维护民族独立的斗争中，可以永远指望得到六亿五千万中国人民的坚决支持。祝中国与索马里兰人民之间的友谊日益发展！”
