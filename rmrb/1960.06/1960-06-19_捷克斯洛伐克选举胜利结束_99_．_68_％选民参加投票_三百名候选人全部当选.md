Title: Czechoslovak Election Ends in Victory; 99.68% of Voters Participate; All 300 Candidates Elected
Author:
Date: 1960-06-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, electric dispatch: The results of the elections for representatives to the Czechoslovak National Assembly, the Slovak National Council, and People's Committees at all levels, held on the 12th, have been released here.

According to election results from various states received by the Czechoslovak Central Election Commission, all 300 candidates were elected as deputies to the National Assembly. In addition, 87 people were elected as deputies to the Slovak National Council, and 203,964 people were elected as members of People's Committees at all levels.

99.68% of all registered voters participated in the National Assembly elections, and 99.86% of the votes were cast for candidates nominated by the National Front. 99.68% of all registered voters participated in the elections for state People's Committees and the Prague City People's Committee, and 99.88% of the votes were in favor of candidates nominated by the National Front.

### 捷克斯洛伐克选举胜利结束  99．68％选民参加投票　三百名候选人全部当选

1960-06-19
第6版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克选举胜利结束
    99．68％选民参加投票　三百名候选人全部当选
    据新华社布拉格电　这里发表了12日举行的选举捷克斯洛伐克国民议会、斯洛伐克民族议会以及各级人民委员会的代表选举结果。
    根据捷克斯洛伐克中央选举委员会收到的各州选举结果，三百名候选人全部当选为国民议会议员，此外，八十七人当选为斯洛伐克民族议会议员，二十万三千九百六十四人被选为各级人民委员会委员。
    在全部登记的选民中有99．68％的选民参加了国民议会的选举，99．86％的选票投给民族阵线提出的候选人。参加州人民委员会和布拉格市人民委员会选举的选民占全部登记选民的99．68％，99．88％的选票都是赞成民族阵线提出的候选人。
