Title: Gathering of Cultural and Educational Elites Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Mountaineering Team
Author:
Date: 1960-06-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd: The National Congress of Advanced Units and Advanced Workers in Socialist Construction in Education, Culture, Health, Sports, and Journalism sent a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese Mountaineering Team. The full text is as follows:

To the Chinese Mountaineering Team:

The National Congress of Advanced Units and Advanced Workers in Socialist Construction in Education, Culture, Health, Sports, and Journalism, after listening to the speech by Comrade Shi Zhanchun, the captain of the Chinese Mountaineering Team, feels extremely uplifted and encouraged. The great victory of the young Chinese Mountaineering Team in scaling Mount Everest from the north slope is a brilliant victory for the Party's General Line for Socialist Construction and a brilliant victory for Mao Zedong Thought. It once again proves the unparalleled superiority of the socialist system. This unprecedented feat proves that the great Chinese people, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, have liberated their minds, broken down superstitions, and, with a bold revolutionary spirit combined with a scientific and pragmatic approach, are able to overcome all difficulties and achieve great victories in mankind's conquest of nature with communist spirit. The congress warmly extends its heartfelt congratulations and cordial greetings to all comrades of the Chinese Mountaineering Team and calls on cultural and educational workers across the country to learn from your lofty aspirations, learn from your spirit of hard work, perseverance, solidarity, and mutual assistance, to push the cultural revolution in our country to a new high tide, to scale the peaks of science and culture, to build our country into a great socialist power, and to strive to realize our great ideal of communism.

### 文教群英会电贺登山队

1960-06-03
第3版()
专栏：

    文教群英会电贺登山队
    新华社2日讯　全国教育和文化、卫生、体育、新闻方面社会主义建设先进单位和先进工作者代表大会，给中国登山队的贺电，全文如下：
    全国教育和文化、卫生、体育、新闻方面社会主义建设先进单位和先进工作者代表大会，听取了中国登山队队长史占春同志的发言，感到极大的振奋和鼓舞。年轻的中国登山队从北坡攀登珠穆朗玛峰的伟大胜利，是党的社会主义建设总路线的光辉胜利，是毛泽东思想的光辉胜利。它又一次证明了社会主义制度的无比优越性。这一史无前例的壮举证明了伟大的中国人民在中国共产党和毛主席的领导下，解放了思想，破除了迷信，以豪迈的革命气概与科学的求实精神相结合的共产主义风格，就能够战胜一切困难，取得人类征服自然的伟大胜利。大会热烈地向中国登山队全体同志表示衷心的祝贺和亲切的慰问，并且号召全国文化教育工作者学习你们的雄心壮志，学习你们的艰苦奋斗、坚韧不拔和团结互助的精神，为把我国的文化革命推向新的高潮，攀登科学文化的高峰，为把我国建设成为伟大的社会主义强国，实现我们的远大理想共产主义而斗争。
