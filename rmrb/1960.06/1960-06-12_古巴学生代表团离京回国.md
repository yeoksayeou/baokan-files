Title: Cuban Student Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-06-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 10th June. The Cuban student delegation of six, who came to China at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

### 古巴学生代表团离京回国

1960-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴学生代表团离京回国
    新华社10日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请前来我国访问的古巴学生代表团一行六人，已经在今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
