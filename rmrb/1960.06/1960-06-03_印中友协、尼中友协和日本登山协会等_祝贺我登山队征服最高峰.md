Title: China-India Friendship Association, Nepal-China Friendship Association, Japan Mountaineering Association, and others congratulate our mountaineering team on conquering the highest peak

Author:

Date: 1960-06-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency—The China-India Friendship Association received a telegram from the India-China Friendship Association in New Delhi on May 28, congratulating our mountaineering team on the success of climbing the world's highest peak.

The telegram said: "We sincerely congratulate your mountaineering team on the great achievement of reaching the summit of Mount Everest."

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 1—The Nepal-China Friendship Association warmly congratulates the Chinese mountaineering team on reaching the summit of Mount Everest, the world's highest peak.

In a letter to Xinhua News Agency reporters here, signed by Purna Bahadur, president of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, it said: "Please convey our sincere and warm congratulations through your agency to the captain and all members of the Chinese mountaineering team who recently climbed Mount Everest, congratulating them on their glorious victory in reaching the summit of Mount Everest for the first time in history from the north slope, which has always been considered impossible to climb. This victory also reflects the great progress made by Chinese mountaineering in a very short period after the glorious founding of the People's Republic of China."

Xinhua News Agency—The Japan Mountaineering Association and British peace activist Montagu, chairman of the International Table Tennis Federation, sent telegrams to our Mountaineering Association and the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, congratulating our mountaineering team on conquering the world's highest peak.

The Japan Mountaineering Association congratulated the Chinese mountaineering team in the telegram for the first successful ascent of Mount Everest from the north side. Montagu said in the telegram, "Congratulations on the glorious achievement of the Chinese mountaineering team in conquering Mount Everest."

### 印中友协、尼中友协和日本登山协会等  祝贺我登山队征服最高峰

1960-06-03
第3版()
专栏：

    印中友协、尼中友协和日本登山协会等
    祝贺我登山队征服最高峰
    新华社讯　中印友好协会收到印中友好协会5月28日由新德里发来的电报，祝贺我国登山队攀登世界最高峰获得成功。
    电报说，衷心地祝贺你们登山队登上珠穆朗玛峰的伟大成就。
    新华社新德里1日电　尼泊尔—中国友好协会热烈祝贺中国登山队登上世界第一高峰珠穆朗玛顶峰。
    尼泊尔—中国友好协会在给这里新华社记者的一封有这个协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔签名的信中说：“请通过贵社把我们诚挚和热烈的祝贺转达给最近攀登珠穆朗玛峰的中国登山队队长和全体队员，祝贺他们在历史上第一次从一直被认为无法攀登的北坡登上了珠穆朗玛峰顶峰的光辉胜利。这个胜利也反映了中国登山运动在中华人民共和国光荣成立后一个很短时期内所取得的巨大进步。”
    新华社讯　日本登山协会和英国和平人士、国际乒乓球联合会主席蒙塔古，分别打电报给我国登山协会和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会，祝贺我国登山队征服了世界最高峰。
    日本登山协会在电报中祝贺中国登山队从北面首次攀登珠穆朗玛峰成功。蒙塔古在电报中说，祝贺中国登山队征服珠穆朗玛峰的辉煌成就。
