Title: Chinese Artists Association Sends Telegram to Support the Just Struggle of the Japanese People; Peking University Student Union Condemns Kishida Nobusuke Clique's Bloody Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1960-06-19
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Chinese Artists Association sent a telegram to the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, condemning the crimes of the U.S.-Japanese reactionaries in their bloody suppression of the Japanese people, and expressing firm support for the Japanese people’s patriotic and just anti-U.S. struggle. The telegram said: On June 15th, 5.8 million heroic Japanese workers launched a large-scale second general strike against U.S. imperialism and the Kishida Nobusuke government. At the same time, people from all walks of life in Japan participated more widely in the 18th National Unified Action struggle. This once again demonstrated the solidarity of the Japanese people and gave the U.S.-Japanese reactionaries a more powerful blow. Chinese artists, together with the Chinese people, firmly support this patriotic and just struggle of the Japanese people.
    The telegram said: In this struggle, the Kishida Nobusuke government has actually carried out bloody suppression of patriotic demonstrators, massacred patriotic young students, and beaten demonstrating cultural figures. We express our great indignation at this. We extend our cordial greetings to the wounded Japanese patriotic students and the families of the deceased. We express our deep condolences to the gloriously sacrificed martyrs.
    The telegram concluded, We firmly believe that the Japanese people’s struggle against the U.S.-Japan military alliance treaty will surely be victorious.

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Peking University Student Union issued a statement today, voicing support for the Japanese students' struggle against the traitorous Kishida Nobusuke government and condemning the Kishida Nobusuke clique, fostered by U.S. imperialism, for its crimes of massacring students in the "June 15th" incident.
    The statement said that the Peking University Student Union, representing all students of the university, strongly condemns the bloody crimes of the Kishida Nobusuke clique fostered by U.S. imperialism, pays high tribute to the Japanese students at the forefront of the struggle and the heroic Japanese people who are persisting in the struggle, expresses deep condolences to the dead martyrs, and extends deep sympathy to the injured students and the families of the victims. It also represents all students of the university in expressing its firm stand with the Japanese students as their strong backing.

### 我美协致电支持日本人民的正义斗争  北京大学学生会谴责岸信介集团血腥暴行

1960-06-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我美协致电支持日本人民的正义斗争
    北京大学学生会谴责岸信介集团血腥暴行
    新华社18日讯　中国美术家协会打电报给日中文化交流协会，谴责美日反动派血腥镇压日本人民的罪行，表示坚决支持日本人民的反美爱国正义斗争。电报说：6月15日，五百八十万英勇的日本工人展开了规模宏大的反对美帝国主义反对岸信介政府第二次总罢工，同时日本全国各界人民也更广泛地参加第十八次全国统一行动的斗争，这再一次显示了日本人民的团结力量，给予美日反动派以更有力的打击。中国美术家和中国人民一起，坚决支持日本人民的这一爱国正义斗争。
    电报说：在这一斗争中，岸信介政府竟对爱国示威群众进行血腥镇压、屠杀爱国的青年学生，殴打示威的文化人士，对此，我们表示极大的愤慨。谨向负伤的日本爱国学生及死难者的家属致以亲切的慰问。向光荣牺牲的烈士表示沉痛的悼念。
    电报最后说，我们坚信，日本人民反对日美军事同盟条约的斗争一定会取得胜利。
    新华社17日讯　北京大学学生会今天发表声明，声援日本大学生反对岸信介卖国政府的斗争，谴责美帝国主义豢养的岸信介集团在“六·一五”事件中屠杀学生的罪行。
    声明说，北京大学学生会代表全校学生严厉谴责美帝国主义豢养的岸信介集团的血腥罪行，向站在斗争前线的日本学生和作坚持斗争的英勇日本人民致以崇高的敬意，向死难的烈士表示沉痛的哀悼，向受伤的学生和受难者的家属表示深切的慰问，并且代表全校学生表示坚决和日本学生站在一起，作为他们坚强的后盾。
