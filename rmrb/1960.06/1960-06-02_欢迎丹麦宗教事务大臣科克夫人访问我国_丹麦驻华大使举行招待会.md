Title: Welcome Madam Kock, Danish Minister of Religious Affairs, on her Visit to China; Danish Ambassador to China Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 1st—Danish Ambassador to China, Mr. Bartelsen, held a reception this afternoon to welcome Madam Bodil Kock, Danish Minister of Religious Affairs, and her husband, Professor Kock, and others, on their visit to China.

Ambassador Bartelsen, Madam Kock, and Minister Shen Yanbing spoke at the reception.

Among those attending the reception were: Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, and prominent figures from educational, cultural, religious, and youth circles in the capital.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries' embassies in China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 欢迎丹麦宗教事务大臣科克夫人访问我国  丹麦驻华大使举行招待会

1960-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    欢迎丹麦宗教事务大臣科克夫人访问我国
    丹麦驻华大使举行招待会
    新华社1日讯　丹麦驻中国大使巴特森今天下午举行招待会，欢迎来我国访问的丹麦宗教事务大臣博迪尔·科克夫人及其丈夫科克教授等。
    巴特森大使、科克夫人和沈雁冰部长在招待会上讲了话。
    出席招待会的有：文化部副部长夏衍，外交部副部长罗贵波，对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南以及首都教育界、文化界、宗教界、青年界等著名人士。
    各国驻中国使节和外交官员也应邀出席了招待会。
