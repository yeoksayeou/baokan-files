Plague God Sweeping the Streets

　　The Plague God Sweeping the Streets, Part Seven: Nostalgia for Seoul

　　Arriving in South Korea, every sight evokes painful memories.
　　At Shangganling, countless corpses were left behind;
　　The Yalu River remains only a pie in the sky;
　　At Panmunjom, they signed the agreement with gritted teeth;
　　At Gyeongmudae, their compliant puppet collapsed.
　　Ah, South Korea, everywhere anti-American flames arise.
　　Alas, this unlucky journey, from beginning to end, is unsatisfactory!

　　Dongfang Jian, poem

专栏：瘟神过街
