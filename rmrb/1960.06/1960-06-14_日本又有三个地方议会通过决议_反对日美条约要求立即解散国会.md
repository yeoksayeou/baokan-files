Title: Japan: Three More Local Assemblies Pass Resolutions Opposing the U.S.-Japan Treaty and Demanding Immediate Dissolution of the Diet
Author:
Date: 1960-06-14
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 13th: Three more local assemblies in Japan have passed resolutions opposing the new U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty," demanding the entire cabinet of Nobusuke Kishi resign, and calling for the immediate dissolution of the Diet.
    These three local assemblies are the Yubari City Assembly in Hokkaido, the General Affairs Committee of the Hokkaido Assembly (an organ with full power to act on behalf of the assembly), and the Mizumaki Town Assembly in Onga District, Fukuoka Prefecture.

### 日本又有三个地方议会通过决议  反对日美条约要求立即解散国会

1960-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

    日本又有三个地方议会通过决议
    反对日美条约要求立即解散国会
    新华社13日讯　东京消息：日本又有三个地方议会通过决议，反对新日美“安全条约”，要求岸信介内阁全体辞职和立即解散国会。
    这三个地方议会是北海道夕张市议会、北海道议会总务委员会（代行议会任务的全权机关）、和福冈县远贺郡水卷町议会。
