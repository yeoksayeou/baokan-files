Title: Mass Rally in Brindisi, Italy, Opposes Establishment of US Military Bases in Italy; Spano Points Out that the Japanese People Should Be Taken as an Example to Launch a Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-06-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 14th: A mass rally protesting the establishment of American military bases on Italian territory was held in Brindisi, Apulia, on the 12th.

Brindisi is one of many Italian cities with American missile bases, and its airport is also one of the bases. This airport has radio contact with the U-2 spy plane shot down in the Soviet Union.

The conference was organized by the Italian Peace Movement, and was attended by officials from the municipal and provincial governments, members of parliament, and people from five provinces in Apulia, representing various political tendencies, including Christian Democrats.

Vittorio Spano, Secretary-General of the Italian Peace Movement and member of the Presidium of the World Peace Council, spoke at the meeting. He emphasized the example of the Japanese people and said that the Italian people must also struggle against the imperialist bloc's establishment of military bases in Italian territory, intended for aggression against the Soviet Union and socialist countries, and must defend Italian sovereignty.

Italian writer and university professor Fiori also addressed the meeting, calling on the people of southern Italy to join the struggle against American bases.

### 意大利布林迪西城群众集会  反对在意大利建立美军基地  斯巴诺指出应以日本人民为榜样展开斗争

1960-06-15
第6版()
专栏：

    意大利布林迪西城群众集会
    反对在意大利建立美军基地
    斯巴诺指出应以日本人民为榜样展开斗争
    新华社14日讯　罗马消息：12日在阿浦利亚区布林迪西举行了抗议在意大利领土上建立美国军事基地的群众大会。
    布林迪西是意大利许多设有美国导弹基地的城市之一，它的机场也是基地之一。这个机场同在苏联被击落的U—2型间谍飞机有无线电的联系。
    大会是由意大利和平运动组织的，参加大会的有市政府和省政府的官员、议员和来自阿浦利亚区五个省的人民，他们代表各种政治倾向，其中包括天主教民主党人。
    意大利和平运动总书记、世界和平理事会主席团委员维利奥·斯巴诺在会上讲了话。他强调指出日本人民的榜样，并且说，意大利人民也必须进行斗争，反对帝国主义集团在意大利领土上建立目的在于侵略苏联和社会主义国家的军事基地，并且必须保卫意大利的主权。
    意大利名作家和大学教授菲奥里也在会上号召意大利南部人民参加反对美国基地的斗争。
