Title: Indonesian Senior Military Officers to Receive Training in the United States
Author:
Date: 1960-06-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 18th: According to an announcement by the Army News Service, Colonel Jonosohardjo and Lieutenant Colonel Mudar Amin, senior officers of the Indonesian Army General Staff, left here yesterday for the United States and will receive approximately one year of training at staff schools in the United States.

Colonel Soehadi, Commander of the Indonesian Marine Corps, left here on the 16th at the invitation of General Shoup, Commandant of the U.S. Marine Corps, to visit the U.S. Marine Corps and facilities stationed in Hawaii and Okinawa.

### 印度尼西亚高级军官  到美国去受训练

1960-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚高级军官
    到美国去受训练
    新华社雅加达18日电　据陆军新闻处宣布，印度尼西亚陆军参谋部的高级军官约诺索禾约上校和穆达尔·阿明中校在昨天离开这里前往美国，并将在美国的参谋学校中受大约一年的训练。
    印度尼西亚海军陆战队司令苏哈迪上校应美国海军陆战队司令休普将军的邀请，于16日离开这里前往夏威夷岛和冲绳岛去参观驻扎在那里的美国海军陆战队和设施。
