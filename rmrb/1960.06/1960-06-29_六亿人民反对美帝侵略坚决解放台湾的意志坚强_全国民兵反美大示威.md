Title: Six Hundred Million People's Will to Oppose US Imperialist Aggression and Resolutely Liberate Taiwan is Firm; National Militia Anti-US Grand Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1960-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report:

    Vast numbers of male and female militia members in major cities across the country, provincial capitals, autonomous region capitals, and on the Fujian front lines, a total of twenty-nine large and medium-sized cities, held massive anti-US demonstration rallies and parades on the 27th, expressing their firm will and determination to resolutely oppose US imperialism's occupation of our country's territory of Taiwan and to liberate Taiwan.

    In Shanghai, 40,000 fully armed militia members held an anti-US demonstration rally. Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and others attended the rally. The powerful militia force was reviewed by the Party and state leaders.

    In the capital, 50,000 male and female militia members held an anti-US demonstration rally. Marshal Chen Yi and others reviewed the militia force. After the meeting, the militia members held a demonstration parade in the rain.

    In the six major cities of Tianjin, Shenyang, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, and Xi'an, more than 270,000 male and female militia members held anti-US grand demonstrations on the 27th.

    The broad masses of militia fighting on the Fujian front separately held demonstrations in Xiamen and Fuzhou.

    The front-line people's commune militia divisions, which grew up in battle, marched at the forefront of the demonstration teams. Over the ten years since US imperialism occupied our country's territory of Taiwan, they have made immortal contributions in coordinating with the People's Liberation Army in shelling Jinmen, attacking US imperialist aggressors, and defending the security of the motherland. In the anti-US armed grand demonstration to drive away the "plague god" Eisenhower with ten thousand cannons, more than 1,000 militia and artillery heroes fought side by side, firing cannons at the "plague god."

    The number of militia members participating in anti-US demonstrations in the provincial capitals and autonomous region capitals were: Nanjing, 30,000; Hangzhou, 10,000; Jinan, 10,000; Hefei, more than 10,000; Taiyuan, more than 12,000; Harbin, more than 20,000; Changchun, more than 10,000; Hohhot, more than 12,000; Lanzhou, 25,000; Urumqi, 25,000; Xining, more than 17,000; Yinchuan, 6,000; Zhengzhou, 10,000; Changsha, 10,000; Nanning, more than 10,000; Chengdu, 20,000; Kunming, more than 10,000; Guiyang, 10,000; and the militia of Nanchang, the heroic city of the "August 1st" Uprising, also held a powerful anti-US demonstration rally.

    At gatherings in various cities, male and female militia members gripped their weapons tightly, sang anti-US songs, and shouted slogans such as "US imperialism get out of Taiwan!" and "We must liberate Taiwan!" (Attached picture)

    More than 10,000 militia members in Nanning held an anti-US demonstration rally. This is a contingent of construction worker militia.

### 六亿人民反对美帝侵略坚决解放台湾的意志坚强  全国民兵反美大示威

1960-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    六亿人民反对美帝侵略坚决解放台湾的意志坚强
    全国民兵反美大示威
    本报讯　据新华社消息：全国各大城市、各省省会、各自治区首府以及福建前线共二十九个大中城市的广大男女民兵，27日举行了声势浩大的反美示威大会和示威游行，表示了坚决反对美帝国主义霸占我国领土台湾、一定要解放台湾的坚强意志和决心。
    上海市有四万名全副武装的民兵举行了反美示威大会。刘少奇主席、周恩来总理等出席了大会。强大的民兵队伍接受了党和国家领导人的检阅。
    在首都，五万名男女民兵举行了反美示威大会。陈毅元帅等检阅了民兵队伍。会后民兵们冒雨举行了示威游行。
    天津、沈阳、武汉、广州、重庆、西安等六大城市，27日共有二十七万多名男女民兵举行反美大示威。
    战斗在福建前线的广大民兵，分别在厦门和福州举行示威。
    在战斗中成长起来的前线人民公社民兵师，走在示威队伍的最前列。他们从美帝国主义霸占我国领土台湾十年来，在配合人民解放军炮击金门、打击美帝国主义侵略者、保卫祖国安全的战斗中，立下了不朽的功勋。在万炮轰逐“瘟神”艾森豪威尔的反美武装大示威中，一千多个民兵和炮兵英雄们并肩上阵，发炮轰“瘟神”。
    在各个省会、自治区首府民兵参加反美示威的人数是：南京市，三万人；杭州市，一万人；济南市，一万人；合肥市，一万多人；太原市，一万二千多人；哈尔滨市，两万多人；长春市，一万多人；呼和浩特市，一万二千多人；兰州市，两万五千人；乌鲁木齐市，二万五千人；西宁市，一万七千多人；银川市，六千人；郑州市，一万人；长沙市，一万人；南宁市，一万多人；成都市，两万人；昆明市，一万多人；贵阳市，一万人；“八一”起义的英雄城市——南昌市的民兵也举行了强大的反美示威大会。
    在各个城市的集会上，男女民兵紧握武器，高唱反美歌曲，高呼“美帝国主义从台湾滚出去！”“我们一定要解放台湾！” 等口号。（附图片）
