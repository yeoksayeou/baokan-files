Title: The Soviet Union Submits Memorandum to the Netherlands Hoping Not to Increase Troops in West Irian and Create Tensions
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 2nd. The Soviet government, in a memorandum to the Dutch government, emphasized that the Dutch government recently moved a considerable number of naval, air, and land forces to the West Irian region, which cannot but deepen the danger of war in Southeast Asia.
    The Soviet government hopes that the Dutch government will not take actions that would endanger peace and create tensions in Southeast Asia.

### 苏联向荷兰提出备忘录  希望不要增兵西伊里安制造紧张局势

1960-06-04
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联向荷兰提出备忘录
    希望不要增兵西伊里安制造紧张局势
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科2日讯：苏联政府在给荷兰政府的一份备忘录中着重指出，不久以前，荷兰政府把相当数量的海军、空军和陆军调到西伊里安地区，这不能不加深东南亚地区的战争危险性。
    苏联政府希望荷兰政府不要采取会危及和平和在东南亚造成紧张局势的行动。
