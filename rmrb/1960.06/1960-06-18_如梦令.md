Title: Like a Dream
Author: Lin Bi
Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 8

Lin Bi's true colors are exposed, who listens to flowery words and sophistry?
Drive out the plague god everywhere, gazing for a return to the White House.
Where? Where?
It is the end of the road and the twilight of the day.

    如梦令
