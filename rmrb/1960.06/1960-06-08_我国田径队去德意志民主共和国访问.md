Title: Chinese Track and Field Team Visits the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1960-06-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 7th June. A Chinese men's and women's track and field team of twenty-five people left Beijing by plane today for a visit to the German Democratic Republic.

The track and field team is led by Dou Lixin, Deputy Director of the Yunnan Provincial Sports Commission, and consists of twenty athletes (seven women and thirteen men). The female athletes are: Zheng Fengrong, Jiang Yumin, Liu Zheng, Chong Xiuyun, Wang Shiyu, Shi Baozhu, He Zufeng; the male athletes are: Chen Jiaquan, Yin Fengwu, Cai Yishu, Hu Zurong, Shi Hongfan, Xuan Yaolun, Zhou Lianli, Zhang Guangbiao, Bi Hongfu, He Yongxian, Su Wenren, Ma Changlu, Fan Dehua.

The track and field team will also visit Czechoslovakia and will be invited to participate in the international track and field competition commemorating Rosicky, which begins on June 18, and will also go to the Soviet Union to participate in the international track and field competition commemorating the Znamensky brothers.

### 我国田径队去德意志民主共和国访问

1960-06-08
第6版()
专栏：

    我国田径队去德意志民主共和国访问
    新华社7日讯　中国男女田径队一行二十五人，今天乘飞机离北京去德意志民主共和国访问。
    田径队由云南省体育运动委员会副主任窦力新率领，有男女运动员二十名（女七人、男十三人）。女运动员是：郑凤荣、姜玉民、刘正、崇秀云、王士玉、石宝珠、贺祖芬；男运动员是：陈家全、殷凤武、蔡艺墅、胡祖荣、史鸿范、宣耀伦、周连立、张光表、毕鸿福、贺永宪、苏文仁、马长路、范德华。
    田径队以后还将访问捷克斯洛伐克，应邀参加6月18日开始的纪念罗西茨基的国际田径比赛，和到苏联参加纪念兹纳门斯基兄弟的国际田径比赛。
