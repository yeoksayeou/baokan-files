Title: Shen Yanbing Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Two Soviet Experts
Author:
Date: 1960-06-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd. Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing hosted a banquet this noon to bid farewell to two Soviet experts who are about to return to their country: Ta. P. Kravchenko, a Soviet piano expert from the Central Conservatory of Music, and P. A. Gusev, a ballet choreographer expert from the Beijing Dance School. Before the banquet, Shen Yanbing, on behalf of Premier Zhou Enlai, presented them with letters of gratitude, thanking them for their important contributions to the development of piano music education and ballet art in our country.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Deputy Director of the State Council Foreign Affairs Office Liao Chengzhi, and Vice Ministers of Culture Qian Junrui and Xia Yan attended the banquet. Counselor Sudarikov of the Soviet Embassy in China and Sevoschiyanov, the head of the Cultural and Educational Expert Office of the Soviet Embassy in China, also attended the banquet.

### 沈雁冰设宴欢送两位苏联专家

1960-06-23
第6版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰设宴欢送两位苏联专家
    新华社22日讯　文化部部长沈雁冰今天中午举行宴会，欢送即将回国的中央音乐学院苏联钢琴专家塔·彼·克拉芙琴柯和北京舞蹈学校芭蕾舞编导专家彼·安·古雪夫。宴会前，沈雁冰代表周恩来总理向他们颁发了感谢状，感谢他们对我国钢琴音乐教育和芭蕾舞艺术事业的发展所作的重要贡献。
    陈毅副总理、国务院外事办公室副主任廖承志和文化部副部长钱俊瑞、夏衍等出席了宴会。苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫和苏驻华大使馆经济参赞办公室文教专家负责人谢沃斯齐雅诺夫也出席了宴会。
