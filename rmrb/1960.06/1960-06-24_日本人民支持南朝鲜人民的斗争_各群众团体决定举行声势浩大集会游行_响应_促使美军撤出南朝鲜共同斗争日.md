Title: Japanese People Support the South Korean People's Struggle; Various Mass Organizations Decide to Hold Large-Scale Rallies and Demonstrations in Response to the "Joint Struggle Day to Promote the Withdrawal of US Troops from South Korea"
Author:
Date: 1960-06-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st, Tokyo Dispatch: Japanese mass organizations, at a joint meeting held today, decided that in response to the Second Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference's call to hold a "Joint Struggle Day to Promote the Withdrawal of US Troops from South Korea" on June 25th, they will hold large-scale rallies and demonstrations in Tokyo on that day, demanding the immediate withdrawal of US troops from South Korea.

	This large demonstration will be hosted by the "Japanese Implementation Committee for the Joint Struggle Day Commemorating the Tenth Anniversary of the Korean War and Demanding the Withdrawal of US Troops from South Korea." Participating in the committee are the Japanese Asian-African Solidarity Committee, the Japanese Peace Committee, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, the National Liaison Council Against Military Bases, as well as representatives from women's organizations, friendship organizations, and representatives from the Socialist Party and the Communist Party.

	The central slogans of the rally are: Demand the immediate withdrawal of US troops from South Korea, Japan, Taiwan, South Vietnam, and Asia; support the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people; demand the immediate return of Okinawa to Japan; remove the black spy planes; and crush the new Japan-US "Security Treaty."

	On June 25th, 100,000 Korean residents in Japan will also hold rallies and demonstrations throughout Japan, demanding the immediate withdrawal of US troops from South Korea and demanding the peaceful reunification of Korea.

### 日本人民支持南朝鲜人民的斗争  各群众团体决定举行声势浩大集会游行  响应“促使美军撤出南朝鲜共同斗争日”

1960-06-24
第6版()
专栏：

    日本人民支持南朝鲜人民的斗争
    各群众团体决定举行声势浩大集会游行
    响应“促使美军撤出南朝鲜共同斗争日”
    新华社21日讯　东京消息：日本各群众团体在今天举行的联席会议上决定，为了响应第二届亚非人民团结大会关于在6月25日举行“促使美军撤出南朝鲜共同斗争日”的号召，将在这一天在东京举行声势浩大的集会和示威游行，要求美军立即撤出南朝鲜。
    这次大示威将由“纪念朝鲜战争十周年要求美军撤出南朝鲜共同斗争日日本实行委员会”主持举行。参加委员会的有日本亚非团结委员会、日本和平委员会、日本工会总评议会、禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会、日本全国反对军事基地联络会议以及妇女团体、友好团体的代表和社会党、共产党的代表。
    大会的中心口号是：要求美军立即撤出南朝鲜、日本、台湾、南越和亚洲；支援南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争；要求立即把冲绳归还日本；撤走黑色间谍飞机；粉碎新日美“安全条约”。
    在6月25日这一天，还有十万名旅日朝侨将在日本各地举行集会和游行，要求美军立即撤出南朝鲜，要求和平统一朝鲜。
