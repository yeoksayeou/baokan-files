Title: National Signature Campaign Launched in Britain Demanding Abolition of U.S. Military Bases in Britain
Author:
Date: 1960-06-21
Page: 6

    Demanding Abolition of U.S. Military Bases in Britain
    National Signature Campaign Launched in Britain
    Xinhua News Agency, London, 19th - Britain today formally began a national signature campaign demanding the abolition of all U.S. military bases in Britain.
    The Peace Congress held here today initiated the national campaign to sign the "Abolish Bases Notice".
    Richard Freeman, Vice-Chairman of the British Peace Committee, said at the conference: "This is not a small issue, but a truly large-scale movement. We can show the world, especially Japan, that the British people are actively taking action to prevent war."
    Freeman suggested that youth "commandos" go to towns without peace movement organizations to engage in propaganda work.
    The "Abolish Bases Notice" states: "We British citizens who sign here believe that the existence of U.S. nuclear rockets and air force bases in Britain constitutes a danger to the British people and increases the danger of war."
    The notice says: "For world peace and the security of Britain, we demand that the United States immediately remove all military bases and facilities on British territory."
    A group of British youths marched to the U.S. Embassy in London over the weekend, demanding an end to U.S. espionage flights and flights by aircraft carrying hydrogen bombs, and the abolition of U.S. bases in Britain.

### 要求废除美国在英国的军事基地  英国展开全国性签名运动

1960-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

    要求废除美国在英国的军事基地
    英国展开全国性签名运动
    新华社伦敦19日电　英国今天正式开始一个全国性的签名运动，要求废除美国在英国的所有军事基地。
    今天在这里举行的和平大会发起在“废除基地通知”上签名的全国性运动。
    英国和平委员会副主席理查德·弗里曼在大会上讲话说：“这不是一个小问题，而是一个真正的大规模运动，我们可以向世界，特别是向日本表示，英国人民正在积极采取防止战争的行动。”
    弗里曼建议青年“突击队”到那些没有和平运动组织的城镇中去，从事宣传工作。
    “废除基地通知”说：“我们在这里签名的英国公民认为，在英国存在美国的核火箭和空军基地，就构成对英国人民的危险，并且增加了战争的危险。”
    通知说：“为了世界和平和英国的安全，我们要求美国立刻撤除在英国领土上的所有军事基地和设施。”
    一批英国青年在周末列队前往美国驻伦敦大使馆，要求停止美国的间谍飞行和载有氢弹的飞机的飞行，废除在英国的美国基地。
