Title: Guo Moruo Receives Albanian News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 31st: Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the Albanian News Delegation led by Ajete Simixhiu this morning and held cordial talks with them.

Those present at the reception included Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Shi Shaohua, Deputy Director of the Xinhua News Agency, An Gang, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, and Wang Mo, Deputy Director of the Newspaper Department of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

### 郭沫若接见阿新闻代表团

1960-06-01
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见阿新闻代表团
    新华社31日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，今天上午接见了以阿耶特·西米季乌为首的阿尔巴尼亚新闻工作者代表团，并且进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的有人大常务委员会副秘书长连贯，新华通讯社副社长石少华，人民日报副总编辑安岗，中共中央宣传部报刊处副处长王谟等。
