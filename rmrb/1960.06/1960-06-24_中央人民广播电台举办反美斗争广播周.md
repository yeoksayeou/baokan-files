Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Holds Anti-American Struggle Broadcasting Week
Author:
Date: 1960-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd: The Central People's Broadcasting Station will hold an Anti-American Struggle Broadcasting Week from June 21st to 27th. During the broadcasting week, both domestic and foreign broadcasts will use various forms to report on our country's massive anti-American struggle and the anti-American upsurge of people around the world, exposing the aggressive nature of U.S. imperialism. During the broadcasting week, the Central Station will invite Zhang Xinyuan, the former regimental commander of a certain volunteer army regiment who participated in the Battle of Shangganling; Li Baocheng, the former company commander of the Iron Eighth Company of a certain volunteer army unit who won a collective special-class merit in the Battle of Shangganling; Wan Fulai, the company commander of the company where martyr Huang Jiguang served before his death; Ma Fuyou, the author of the lyrics of the "Volunteer Army Battle Song"; Wu Shengkai, a second-class combat hero of the former Volunteer Air Force; Zhang Tuanyun and Song Chunyuan, first-class meritorious servicemen who participated in the Battle of Shangganling, to give radio speeches for domestic broadcasts. American friends in China, such as Sidney Rittenberg, will also be invited to speak. For broadcasts to Taiwan, He Xiangning, Zhang Zhizhong, Xu Mengshan, Jiang Guangnai, Zheng Dongguo, Lin Zun, Liao Yunze, and Xie Xuetang and other democratic figures from various democratic parties and non-party democratic people will be invited to broadcast.

During the broadcasting week, the Central Station's literary and artistic broadcasts and television broadcasts will also broadcast literary and artistic programs related to the content of the anti-American struggle.

During the broadcasting week, Beijing Television has also arranged various programs related to the anti-American struggle.

### 中央人民广播电台举办反美斗争广播周

1960-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台举办反美斗争广播周
    据新华社22日讯　中央人民广播电台从6月21日到27日举办反美斗争广播周。在广播周期间，对国内广播和对国外广播，都将用各种形式来报道我国声势浩大的反美斗争，和全世界各地人民的反美怒潮，揭露美帝国主义的侵略本质。在广播周期间，中央台对国内广播，将邀请参加上甘岭战役的前志愿军某团团长张信元、曾在上甘岭战役荣立集体特等功的前志愿军某部钢铁第八连连长李保成、黄继光烈士生前所在连队连长万福来、“志愿军战歌”歌词作者麻扶摇、前志愿军空军二级战斗英雄吴胜凯、上甘岭战役参加者一等功臣张团云、宋春元等作广播讲话。被邀请讲话的，还有在中国的美国朋友李敦白等。对台湾广播，将邀请各民主党派和无党派民主人士何香凝、张治中、徐萌山、蒋光鼐、郑洞国、林遵、廖运泽、谢雪堂等广播。
    广播周期间，中央台的文艺广播和电视广播，还将播送有关反美斗争内容的文艺节目。
    在广播周期间，北京电视台也安排了各种有关反美斗争的节目。
