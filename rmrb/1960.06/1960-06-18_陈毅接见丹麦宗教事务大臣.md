Title: Chen Yi Receives the Danish Minister of Religious Affairs
Author:
Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 17th—Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Danish Minister of Religious Affairs, Mrs. Bodil Koch, her husband Professor Koch, and her daughter this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, and Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, among others. (Attached picture)

The picture shows Vice Premier Chen Yi having a cordial conversation with the Danish Minister of Religious Affairs, Mrs. Bodil Koch. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao)

### 陈毅接见丹麦宗教事务大臣

1960-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见丹麦宗教事务大臣
    新华社17日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了丹麦宗教事务大臣博迪尔·科克夫人及其丈夫科克教授和她的女儿。
    接见时在座的有，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长罗贵波和对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南等。（附图片）
