Title: Lu Dingyi and Others Receive All Teacher Representatives of Jiangsu Agricultural Middle School
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd: Lu Dingyi, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council; Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Jichun, Director of the Cultural and Educational Office of the State Council; and Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Education, received all the teacher representatives of Jiangsu Agricultural Middle School during a break in the National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education this morning and held cordial talks with them.

### 陆定一等接见江苏农业中学全体教师代表

1960-06-04
第7版()
专栏：

    陆定一等接见江苏农业中学全体教师代表
    新华社3日讯　中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理陆定一，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫，国务院文教办公室主任张际春，教育部部长杨秀峰，今天上午在全国文教先进工作者代表大会休息时间，接见了江苏省农业中学的全体教师代表，并和他们进行了亲切的谈话。
