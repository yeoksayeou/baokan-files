Title: Hungarian Party Workers Delegation Visits Zhengzhou After Visiting Harbin and Anshan
Author:
Date: 1960-06-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: After a six-day visit to Harbin and Anshan, the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party workers' delegation, headed by Sándor József, member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, left Anshan by plane on the 23rd to visit Zhengzhou.

The delegation arrived in Harbin by plane from Beijing on the 18th. During their stay in Harbin, the guests visited the Harbin Steam Turbine Factory, the Electric Motor Factory, the Guangming Shoe Buckle Factory, the Jincheng Hardware Factory, the No. 8 District Grain Processing Factory, the Xiangfang People's Commune, and other units, and were warmly welcomed by the workers and commune members of these units. They also held talks with the leaders of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Harbin Municipal Committee.

On the 22nd, the delegation arrived in Anshan from Harbin by train. When the delegation arrived and departed from Harbin and Anshan, the responsible persons of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Anshan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party greeted and saw them off at the airport.

### 在哈尔滨和鞍山访问后  匈牙利党工作人员代表团赴郑州

1960-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    在哈尔滨和鞍山访问后
    匈牙利党工作人员代表团赴郑州
    据新华社24日讯　以匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员山道尔·约瑟夫为首的匈牙利社会主义工人党工作人员代表团，在哈尔滨和鞍山访问六天后，23日乘飞机离鞍山去郑州访问。
    代表团是18日乘飞机由北京到哈尔滨的。客人们在哈尔滨期间，参观了哈尔滨汽轮机厂、电机厂、光明鞋扣厂、金城五金厂、八区粮食加工厂、香坊人民公社等单位，受到了这些单位职工和社员的热烈欢迎。他们还同中共黑龙江省委、哈尔滨市委的领导人员举行了座谈。
    22日，代表团从哈尔滨乘火车到达鞍山。代表团在到达和离开哈尔滨、鞍山两地的时候，中共黑龙江省委和中共鞍山市委的负责人都到机场迎送。
