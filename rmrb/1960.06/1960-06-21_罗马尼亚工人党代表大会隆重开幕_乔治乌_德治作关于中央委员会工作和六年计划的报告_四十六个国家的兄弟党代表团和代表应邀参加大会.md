Title: Romanian Workers' Party Congress Grandly Opens; Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej Delivers Report on the Work of the Central Committee and the Six-Year Plan; Delegations and Representatives from Forty-Six Fraternal Parties Invited to Attend the Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-06-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 20th: The Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party grandly opened at nine o'clock this morning in the newly completed Palace of the Republic in Bucharest.

In the magnificent and solemn hall, representatives from all over the country and from various work positions were seated. When the delegation of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, headed by Khrushchev, and the delegation of the Communist Party of China, headed by Peng Zhen, as well as delegations from other fraternal parties, stepped onto the rostrum, the entire audience stood up and applauded warmly.

Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, announced the opening of the Congress. He extended warm congratulations to all the delegates on behalf of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party.

Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej announced that delegations and representatives from fraternal parties of forty-six countries had been invited to attend the Congress. When he made this announcement, the entire audience applauded warmly. Those present also warmly applauded the delegations from Japan, Africa, and Latin American countries at the forefront of the struggle against imperialism.

The Congress adopted the following agenda: (1) Report on the work of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and the 1960-1965 National Economic Development Plan and Long-Term Economic Development Plan; (2) Report of the Central Auditing Commission; (3) Election of the Central Committee and the Central Auditing Commission.

The meeting then proceeded with the first item on the agenda, with Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej delivering the report.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 20th: Delegations from various fraternal Communist and Workers' Parties invited to attend the Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party have arrived here one after another.

In addition to the Soviet and Chinese Communist Party delegations previously reported, the following have also arrived: the Albanian Party of Labour, the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Czechoslovak Communist Party, the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Korean Workers' Party, the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the Polish United Workers' Party, the Vietnam Workers' Party; the Algerian Communist Party, the Argentine Communist Party, the Belgian Communist Party, the British Communist Party, the Chilean Communist Party, the Colombian Communist Party, the Danish Communist Party, the Finnish Communist Party, the French Communist Party, the Dutch Communist Party, the Indian Communist Party, the Indonesian Communist Party, the Iranian People's Party, the Israeli Communist Party, the Italian Communist Party, the New Zealand Communist Party, the Norwegian Communist Party, the Spanish Communist Party, the Swiss Labour Party, the Syrian Communist Party, the Uruguayan Communist Party, and the United States Communist Party delegations.

### 罗马尼亚工人党代表大会隆重开幕  乔治乌—德治作关于中央委员会工作和六年计划的报告  四十六个国家的兄弟党代表团和代表应邀参加大会

1960-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚工人党代表大会隆重开幕
    乔治乌—德治作关于中央委员会工作和六年计划的报告
    四十六个国家的兄弟党代表团和代表应邀参加大会
    新华社布加勒斯特20日电　罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会，今天上午九时在布加勒斯特新落成的共和国宫大礼堂隆重开幕。
    在辉煌庄严的大厅里，坐满了来自全国各地和各种工作岗位上的代表。当以赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联共产党代表团和以彭真为首的中国共产党代表团以及其他各兄弟党代表团走上主席台时，全场起立，热烈鼓掌。
    罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治乌—德治宣布大会开幕。他代表罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会向全体代表致以热烈的祝贺。
    乔治乌—德治宣布，应邀前来参加大会的有四十六个国家的兄弟党代表团和代表。当他作这样宣布时，全场热烈鼓掌欢迎。到会的人还热烈鼓掌欢迎来自同帝国主义斗争前线的日本、非洲和拉丁美洲国家的代表团。
    大会通过了下列会议议程：（一）关于罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会的工作和1960—1965年国民经济发展计划及经济发展远景规划的报告；（二）中央检查委员会的报告；（三）选举中央委员会和中央检查委员会。
    会议接着进行第一项议程，由乔治乌—德治作报告。
    新华社布加勒斯特20日电　应邀前来参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会的各国兄弟共产党和工人党代表团，已经陆续到达这里。
    除苏联和中国共产党代表团前已报道外，已经到达的还有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、保加利亚共产党、捷克斯洛伐克共产党、匈牙利社会主义工人党、德国统一社会党、朝鲜劳动党、蒙古人民革命党、波兰统一工人党、越南劳动党；阿尔及利亚共产党、阿根廷共产党、比利时共产党、英国共产党、智利共产党、哥伦比亚共产党、丹麦共产党、芬兰共产党、法国共产党、荷兰共产党、印度共产党、印度尼西亚共产党、伊朗人民党、以色列共产党、意大利共产党、新西兰共产党、挪威共产党、西班牙共产党、瑞士劳动党、叙利亚共产党、乌拉圭共产党和美国共产党的代表团。
