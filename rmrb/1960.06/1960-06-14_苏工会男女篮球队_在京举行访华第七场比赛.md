Title: Soviet Trade Union Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Hold Seventh Friendly Match in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-14
Page: 6
    Soviet Trade Union Men's and Women's Basketball Teams
    Hold Seventh Friendly Match in Beijing

    The Soviet trade union system's men's and women's basketball teams held their seventh friendly match in China on the evening of the 12th at the Beijing Gymnasium. The women's team lost to the Chinese women's basketball joint team with a score of 46 to 53; the men's team won against the "August 1st" men's basketball team with a score of 71 to 68.

    Before this, the visiting men's and women's teams had played six matches each with the Chinese men's and women's basketball teams in Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, and Hangzhou. The men's team had a record of five wins and one loss, and the women's team had a record of three wins and three losses. On the evening of June 14, they will hold their last friendly match in Beijing before leaving China.

### 苏工会男女篮球队  在京举行访华第七场比赛

1960-06-14
第6版()
专栏：

    苏工会男女篮球队
    在京举行访华第七场比赛
    苏联工会系统的男女篮球队，十二日晚在北京体育馆比赛馆举行访华第七次友谊比赛，结果女子队以四十六比五十三败给了我国女子篮球联队；男子队以七十一比六十八赢了“八一”男子篮球队。
    客队男女队在这以前，曾先后在北京、天津、上海、杭州同我国的男女篮球队各举行了六场比赛，男子队的战绩是五胜一负，女子队是三胜三负。六月十四日晚，他们将在北京举行访华最后一场友谊比赛。
