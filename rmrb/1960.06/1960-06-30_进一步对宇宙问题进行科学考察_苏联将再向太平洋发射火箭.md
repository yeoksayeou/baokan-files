Title: Further Scientific Exploration of Space: The Soviet Union to Launch Another Rocket into the Pacific
Author:
Date: 1960-06-30
Page: 6
　　
    Further Scientific Exploration of Space
    The Soviet Union to Launch Another Rocket into the Pacific
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 29th — TASS, Moscow, 29th: The Soviet Union is scheduled to test a new type of powerful multi-stage carrier rocket between July 5th and 31st. The carrier rocket, without the final stage, will be launched into the central Pacific Ocean.
    TASS has issued a statement regarding this. The full text of the statement is as follows:
    In the course of its work to conquer outer space, the Soviet Union launched a powerful carrier rocket without the final stage into the Pacific Ocean in January of this year.
    On May 15th of this year, the Soviet Union launched the Earth satellite spaceship, weighing 4,540 kilograms, on the basis of this carrier rocket.
    Now, according to the plan for further scientific exploration of space and experimental design work, Soviet scientists and designers are preparing to test some new types of powerful multi-stage carrier rockets. These carrier rockets, without the final stage, will also be launched into the central Pacific Ocean. The coordinates of the target area are:
    Latitude　Longitude
    2 degrees 05 minutes North 169 degrees 09 minutes West
    7 degrees 53 minutes North 167 degrees 33 minutes West
    5 degrees 26 minutes North 166 degrees 15 minutes West
    4 degrees 38 minutes North 167 degrees 51 minutes West
    In order to carry out the necessary measurements, Soviet special ships equipped with measuring instruments will be dispatched to the area where the penultimate stage of the carrier rocket falls.
    These rockets are scheduled to be launched between July 5th and 31st.
    In order to ensure the safety of navigation and air traffic during the launch of carrier rockets into the central Pacific Ocean, TASS is authorized to announce: The Soviet government notifies the governments of all countries whose ships and aircraft may pass near the area where the penultimate stage of the carrier rocket falls during this period, and requests the relevant authorities to instruct captains and pilots not to enter the Pacific waters and airspace within the above coordinates.

### 进一步对宇宙问题进行科学考察  苏联将再向太平洋发射火箭

1960-06-30
第6版()
专栏：

    进一步对宇宙问题进行科学考察
    苏联将再向太平洋发射火箭
    新华社29日讯　塔斯社莫斯科29日讯：苏联定于7月5日到31日期间试验新型威力强大的多级运载火箭。不带最后一级的运载火箭将向太平洋中部区域发射。
    塔斯社为此发表了公报。公报全文如下：
    苏联在进行征服宇宙空间的工作过程中，曾于今年1月向太平洋水域发射了不带最后一级的威力强大的运载火箭。
    今年5月15日，苏联在这种运载火箭的基础上发射了重达四千五百四十公斤的地球卫星式宇宙飞船。
    现在，苏联科学家和设计师根据进一步对宇宙问题进行科学考察和试验设计工作的计划，准备试验一些新型的威力强大的多级运载火箭。这些不带最后一级的运载火箭也将往太平洋中部区域发射，目标区座标为：
    纬度　经度
    北纬二度零五分西经一百六十九度零九分
    北纬七度五十三分西经一百六十七度三十三分。
    北纬五度二十六分西经一百六十六度十五分。
    北纬四度三十八分西经一百六十七度五十一分。
    为了进行必要的测量，将派遣装备有测量仪器的苏联专用船只前往运载火箭倒数第二级下落地区。
    这些火箭预定在7月5日到31日期间发射。
    为了在往太平洋中部发射运载火箭时保证航海和飞机航行的安全，塔斯社受权宣布：苏联政府通知在这段期间可能有船只和飞机在运载火箭倒数第二级下落区域附近通过的各国政府，请有关当局指示船长和飞机长不要进入上述座标范围内的太平洋水域和空间。
