Title: Some Local Assemblies in Japan Demand Resignation of Kishi Nobusuke Cabinet
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo Dispatch: According to a report in the "Akahata" on May 29th, the Fukuchiyama City Assembly in Kyoto Prefecture passed a resolution on the 28th, protesting the Kishi Nobusuke government's illegal coercion of the House of Representatives to "pass" the new "Security Treaty," and demanding the dissolution of the Diet. Since Kishi Nobusuke dispatched police to force the House of Representatives to "pass" the new "Security Treaty," local assemblies that have passed resolutions demanding Kishi Nobusuke's resignation and dissolution of the Diet include the Hirakata City Assembly, Yao City Assembly, and Ikeda City Assembly in Osaka Prefecture, as well as the Omuta City Assembly in Kyushu and the Maesawa Town Assembly in Iwate Prefecture.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo Dispatch, June 3rd: The Suita City Assembly in Osaka Prefecture, Japan, held a plenary meeting today and passed a resolution demanding the resignation of the Kishi Nobusuke Cabinet and the dissolution of the Diet.
    This resolution was passed by the city assembly based on a petition from the Suita City Joint Struggle Organization Against Revision of the "Security Treaty."
    The Izumiotsu City Assembly in Osaka Prefecture unanimously passed a resolution on May 31st demanding the dissolution of the House of Representatives.

### 日本一些地方议会要岸信介内阁下台

1960-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    日本一些地方议会要岸信介内阁下台
    据新华社讯　东京消息：据“赤旗报”5月29日报道，京都府福知山市议会在28日通过决议，抗议岸信介政府非法强使众议院“通过”新“安全条约”，并且要求解散国会。自从岸信介出动警察迫使众议院“通过”新“安全条约”以来，地方议会通过决议要求岸信介辞职和解散国会的，已经有大阪府的枚方市议会、八尾市议会、池田市议会和九州的大牟田市议会以及岩手县的前泽町议会等。
    新华社3日讯　东京消息：日本大阪府吹田市市议会今天举行全体会议，通过了要求岸信介内阁下台和解散国会的决议。
    这项决议是市议会根据吹田市阻止修改“安全条约”共同斗争组织的请愿而通过的。
    大阪府泉大津市市议会在5月31日一致通过决议，要求解散众议院。
