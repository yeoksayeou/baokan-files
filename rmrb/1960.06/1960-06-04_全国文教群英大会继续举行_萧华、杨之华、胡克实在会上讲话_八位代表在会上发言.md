Title: National Cultural and Educational Heroes Conference Continues: Xiao Hua, Yang Zhihua, and Hu Keshi Speak at Meeting; Eight Representatives Deliver Speeches

Author:

Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd June: The National Conference of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education continued this morning and afternoon.

General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Yang Zhihua, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and Hu Keshi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, spoke at the meeting successively.

Eight representatives spoke at the meeting. They were: Jia Guizhi, representative of Beiguan Primary School in Heishan County, Liaoning Province; Zaiyinaiti Yimin, representative of Yili Girls' Middle School in Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region; Li Ruihuan, Captain of the Youth Carpentry Shock Brigade of the Beijing No. 3 Construction Company; Shen Shuzi, teacher of the Liudong Management Area Kindergarten of the Dehua People's Commune in Helong County, Jilin Province; Ren Jizhou, representative of the Feed Production Teaching and Research Group of Gansu Agricultural University; Zhang Huaiying, representative of Xiyang County, Shanxi Province; Yang Longxiang, representative of Yujiang County, Jiangxi Province; and Liu Fonian, representative of the Shanghai Middle and Primary School Curriculum Reform Committee.

The conference will continue tomorrow.

### 全国文教群英大会继续举行  萧华、杨之华、胡克实在会上讲话　八位代表在会上发言

1960-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    全国文教群英大会继续举行
    萧华、杨之华、胡克实在会上讲话　八位代表在会上发言
    新华社3日讯　全国文教先进工作者代表大会今天上午和下午继续举行。
    中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华上将、全国妇女联合会副主席杨之华、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡克实先后在会上讲了话。
    有八位代表在会上发言。他们是：辽宁省黑山县北关小学代表贾桂枝，新疆维吾尔自治区伊犁女子中学代表在以乃提·伊敏，北京市第三建筑公司木工青年突击队队长李瑞环，吉林省和龙县德化人民公社柳洞管理区幼儿园教养员申淑子，甘肃省农业大学饲料生产教研组代表任继周，山西省昔阳县代表张怀英，江西省余江县代表杨龙祥，上海市中、小学课程革新委员会代表刘佛年。
    明天，大会将继续举行。
