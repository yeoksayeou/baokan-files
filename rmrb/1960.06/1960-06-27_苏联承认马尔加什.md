Title: Soviet Union Recognizes Madagascar

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 26th: TASS, Moscow, June 26th—In a telegram to the President of the Malagasy Republic, Philibert Tsiranana, the President of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Leonid Brezhnev, announced that the Soviet Union recognizes the Malagasy Republic as an independent nation and is willing to establish diplomatic relations with it and exchange diplomatic envoys.

### 苏联承认马尔加什

1960-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联承认马尔加什
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科26日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫在给马尔加什共和国总统齐腊纳纳的电报中宣布，苏联承认马尔加什共和国是独立的国家，并且愿意同它建立外交关系，互派外交使节。
