Title: Soviet Union Recognizes the Mali Federation
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-21
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 20th - TASS, Moscow, June 20th: Soviet Premier Nikita Khrushchev sent a telegram to Modibo Keita, Prime Minister of the Mali Federation, announcing that the Soviet Union recognizes the Mali Federation as an independent nation and is willing to establish diplomatic relations with it, exchanging diplomatic envoys.

    In the congratulatory telegram, Khrushchev said: The Soviet people warmly hope that the people of the Mali Federation will prosper and achieve new successes in building their own country.

    The telegram expressed confidence that the friendly relations and fruitful cooperation already established between the Soviet Union and other African countries will also develop between the Soviet Union and the Mali Federation.

### 苏联承认马里联邦

1960-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联承认马里联邦
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科20日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫致电马里联邦总理莫迪博·凯塔，宣布苏联承认马里联邦是独立国家，并且愿意同它建立外交关系，互派外交使节。
    赫鲁晓夫在这封贺电中说：苏联人民热情地希望马里联邦人民繁荣和在建设自己的国家方面获得新的成就。
    贺电中表示相信，苏联和非洲其他国家已经建立的友好关系和有成效的合作，在苏联和马里联邦之间也将得到发展。
