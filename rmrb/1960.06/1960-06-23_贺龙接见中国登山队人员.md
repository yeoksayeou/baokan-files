Title: He Long Receives Members of the Chinese Mountaineering Team
Author:
Date: 1960-06-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st. Vice Premier of the State Council He Long received the first group of members of the Chinese Mountaineering Team, who returned to Beijing on the 20th, this afternoon. He had a cordial conversation with them and took a group photo together.

Huang Zhong, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, was present at the reception.

### 贺龙接见中国登山队人员

1960-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    贺龙接见中国登山队人员
    新华社21日讯　国务院副总理贺龙今天下午接见了20日返回北京的中国登山队第一批人员，同他们进行了亲切的谈话，并且在一起照了像。
    接见时在座的，有国家体育运动委员会副主任黄中。
