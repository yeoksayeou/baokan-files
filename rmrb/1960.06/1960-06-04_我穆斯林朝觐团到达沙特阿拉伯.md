Title: My Muslim Hajj Delegation Arrives in Saudi Arabia
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 1st: The Chinese Muslim Hajj Delegation, consisting of seventeen members and headed by delegation leader Iminov and deputy delegation leader Liu Kefan, arrived in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, on May 30th. The Hajj Delegation will also visit some countries in the region. The Hajj Delegation includes well-known Islamic religious leaders and Ahongs from China. Since the establishment of the Islamic Association of China, this is the seventh Hajj Delegation from China.

### 我穆斯林朝觐团到达沙特阿拉伯

1960-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    我穆斯林朝觐团到达沙特阿拉伯
    新华社开罗1日电　以团长伊敏诺夫和副团长刘克凡为首的中国穆斯林朝觐团一行十七人，于5月30日到达沙特阿拉伯的吉达。朝觐团还将访问这个地区的一些国家。朝觐团包括中国著名的伊斯兰教教长和阿訇等。自从中国伊斯兰教协会建立以来，这已经是中国的第七次朝觐团。
