Title: U.S. Military Plane Intrudes into Airspace over Yongxing Island, Guangdong; I Issue the 107th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-06-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th June: At 13:45 to 13:53 on June 12th, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Yongxing Island area of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, a spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 107th serious warning.

### 美机侵入广东永兴岛上空  我提出第107次严重警告

1960-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    美机侵入广东永兴岛上空
    我提出第107次严重警告
    新华社12日讯　6月12日十三时四十五分至十三时五十三分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省永兴岛地区上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百零七次严重警告。
