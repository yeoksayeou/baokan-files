Title: Mongolian Customs Administration Director Visits Jining and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1960-06-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, June 28th: Dashdzab, Director of the Customs Administration of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Mongolian People's Republic, and his party of five, who were invited to visit China, left Beijing by train this evening to visit Jining and other places before returning home.
    Hu Renkui, Deputy Director of the Customs Administration of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of China, and others went to the station to see them off.
    Gerserzabu, Commercial Counselor of the Mongolian People's Republic in China, also went to the station to see them off.

### 蒙古海关总署署长去集宁等地参观

1960-06-30
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古海关总署署长去集宁等地参观
    新华社28日讯　应邀来我国参观访问的蒙古人民共和国对外贸易部海关总署署长达什扎布等一行五人，今天晚上乘火车离开北京去集宁等地参观后回国。
    前往车站送行的有我对外贸易部海关总署副署长胡仁奎等人。
    蒙古人民共和国驻中国商务专员格色尔扎布，也去车站送行。
