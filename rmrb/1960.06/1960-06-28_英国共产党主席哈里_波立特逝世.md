Title: British Communist Party Chairman Harry Pollitt Dies
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, June 27th, TASS dispatch from London on June 27th: According to a Reuters dispatch from Sydney, British Communist Party Chairman Harry Pollitt died today on the "Orion" ship while returning to Britain from Australia. He was 69 years old. (Attached picture)

### 英国共产党主席哈里·波立特逝世

1960-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

    英国共产党主席哈里·波立特逝世
    新华社二十七日讯　塔斯社伦敦二十七日讯：据路透社悉尼消息，英国共产党主席哈里·波立特今天自澳大利亚返回英国途中在“奥利翁号”轮船上逝世。享年六十九岁。（附图片）
