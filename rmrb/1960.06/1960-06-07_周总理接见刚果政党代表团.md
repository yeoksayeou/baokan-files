Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Delegation from Congolese Political Parties
Author:
Date: 1960-06-07
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire delegation of Congolese political parties visiting China this afternoon. The delegation included prominent Congolese political leaders such as Alphonse Makwambala, Arthur Mayamba, and Henri Kassongo.
    Present at the meeting were Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Zhang Tiesheng, Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association; He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. ### 周总理接见刚果政党代表团

1960-06-07
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见刚果政党代表团
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理在今天下午接见了刚果政党访华代表团的全体成员，其中有刚果知名的政党领袖阿方斯·马克万巴拉、阿图尔·玛扬巴和亨利·卡松戈等人。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，中国非洲人民友好协会副会长张铁生，外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷，中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达。（附图片）
