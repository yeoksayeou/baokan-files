    Contemporary Heroes
    Ma Yufa (second from right), the section chief of the open hearth furnace section of the Third Steelmaking Plant of Anshan Iron and Steel Company and a Communist Party member, is a well-known steelmaking expert with more than ten years of experience. In the technical innovation and technical revolution movement, he has successively improved three important operational techniques for tapping steel from three spouts, charging materials, and maintaining the furnace body, shortening the smelting time for each furnace of steel by about one hour compared to the original, and expanding the charging capacity by more than fifty tons compared to the original, creating important conditions for a substantial increase in steel production. He has recently been hired as a specially appointed researcher by the Liaoning Branch of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. This is him introducing the experience of rapid steelmaking to his colleagues.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Miao Ming

### 当代英雄（图片）
苗明
1960-06-29
第4版()
专栏：当代英雄

    当代英雄
    鞍钢第三炼钢厂平炉工段段长、共产党员马玉发（右二）是个有十多年经验的著名炼钢能手。在技术革新和技术革命运动中，他先后改进了三槽出钢、装料和维护炉体第三项重要操作技术，使每炼一炉钢的熔炼时间比原先缩短一小时左右，装入量比原先扩大五十吨以上，为大量增加钢产量创造了重要条件。最近他已被中国科学院辽宁分院聘请为特约研究员。这是他在向同行介绍快速炼钢的经验
    　新华社记者　苗明摄
