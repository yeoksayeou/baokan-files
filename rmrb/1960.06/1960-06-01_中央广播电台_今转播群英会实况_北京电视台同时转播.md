Title: Central Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live the Heroes' Meeting; Beijing Television to Broadcast Simultaneously
Author:
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 2

    Central Broadcasting Station
    To Broadcast Live the Heroes' Meeting
    Beijing Television to Broadcast Simultaneously
    Xinhua News Agency, 31st – The Central People’s Broadcasting Station will begin broadcasting live the opening ceremony of the National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education at 3:20 PM on June 1st, using the frequency of its first program. Beijing Television will also broadcast simultaneously.

### 中央广播电台  今转播群英会实况  北京电视台同时转播

1960-06-01
第2版()
专栏：

    中央广播电台
    今转播群英会实况
    北京电视台同时转播
    新华社31日讯　中央人民广播电台将从6月1日下午三时二十分开始，用第一套节目的频率转播全国文教先进工作者代表大会开幕式的实况。北京电视台也将同时转播。
