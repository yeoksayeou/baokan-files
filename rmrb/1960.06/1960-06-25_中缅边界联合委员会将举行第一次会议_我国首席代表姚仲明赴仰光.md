Title: The Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee to Hold First Meeting; China's Chief Representative Yao Zhongming Departs for Rangoon

Author:
Date: 1960-06-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, June 24th. According to the agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Union of Burma on the boundary issue between the two countries, the Joint Committee formed by mutual agreement between China and Burma is scheduled to hold its first meeting in Rangoon on the 27th of this month. The Chinese Chief Representative Yao Zhongming, Representatives Cheng Zhiping, Cheng Xueyu, Chen Yanbin, Huang Fenglin, and a group of thirteen advisors and accompanying personnel, departed from Kunming for Rangoon by plane this morning.

Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Liu Minghui, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province; Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee; Dao Jingban (Dai nationality), Vice Chairman of the Provincial Political Consultative Conference and Governor of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture; Hu Zhonghua (Kawa nationality), member of the Provincial People's Committee and Deputy Commissioner of the Lincang Special District; Hu Yutang (Kawa nationality), Standing Committee Member of the Provincial Political Consultative Conference; Li Ping, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Provincial People's Committee; Senior Colonel Zhu Jiabi, Head of the PLA troops stationed in Kunming, and others.

U Maung Maung So, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 中缅边界联合委员会将举行第一次会议  我国首席代表姚仲明赴仰光

1960-06-25
第6版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合委员会将举行第一次会议
    我国首席代表姚仲明赴仰光
    新华社昆明24日电　根据中华人民共和国政府和缅甸联邦政府关于两国边界问题的协定，由中缅两国双方同意所组成的联合委员会，拟定于本月27日在仰光举行第一次会议。我国首席代表姚仲明、代表程之平、成学俞、陈雁彬、黄凤麟以及顾问和随行人员一行十三人，今天上午乘飞机由昆明前往仰光。
    前往机场送行的，有云南省副省长刘明辉，云南省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，省政协副主席、德宏傣族景颇族自治州州长刀京版（傣族），省人民委员会委员、临沧专区副专员胡忠华（佧佤族），省政协常务委员胡玉堂（佧佤族）、省人民委员会外事处处长李萍，解放军驻昆部队首长朱家璧大校等。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭也到机场送行。
