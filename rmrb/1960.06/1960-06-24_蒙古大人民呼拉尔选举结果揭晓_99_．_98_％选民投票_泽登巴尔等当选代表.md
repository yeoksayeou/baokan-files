Title: Results of the Mongolian Great People's Hural Election Announced: 99.98% of Voters Participated, Tsedenbal and Others Elected as Representatives
Author:
Date: 1960-06-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, June 22nd: According to a report in the Mongolian "Unen" on the 22nd, the Presidium of the Mongolian Great People's Hural decided to convene the first session of the Fourth Great People's Hural on July 5th of this year.

The Mongolian Central Election Commission announced on the 22nd the list of elected representatives for the Fourth Great People's Hural election held on June 19th. Mongolian party and state leaders Tsedenbal, Sambuu, and Tsend were all elected as representatives to the Fourth Great People's Hural. A total of 267 representatives were elected.

According to preliminary statistics from the Mongolian Central Election Commission, 99.98% of voters participated in the election. They all voted for the candidates nominated by the party and non-party alliance.

### 蒙古大人民呼拉尔选举结果揭晓  99．98％选民投票　泽登巴尔等当选代表

1960-06-24
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古大人民呼拉尔选举结果揭晓
    99．98％选民投票　泽登巴尔等当选代表
    新华社乌兰巴托22日电　据蒙古“真理报”22日报道，蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团决定今年7月5日召开第四届大人民呼拉尔第一次会议。
    蒙古中央选举委员会22日公布了在6月19日举行的第四届大人民呼拉尔选举的当选代表名单。蒙古党和国家的领导人泽登巴尔、桑布和曾德等人都被选为第四届大人民呼拉尔代表。当选代表共二百六十七人。
    据蒙古中央选举委员会初步统计，99．98％的选民都参加了这次选举。他们都向党和非党联盟提名的候选人投了票。
