Title: Chairman of the Youth League of the Congolese National Movement Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, June 1st: Mr. Gebabu, Chairman of the Youth League of the Congolese National Movement, who was invited by the All-China Youth Federation to visit our country, has concluded his visit to China and left Beijing by plane today to return home.

### 刚果民族运动党青年联盟主席回国

1960-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果民族运动党青年联盟主席回国
    新华社1日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请前来我国访问的刚果民族运动党青年联盟主席格巴布，结束了在中国的访问，已经在今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
