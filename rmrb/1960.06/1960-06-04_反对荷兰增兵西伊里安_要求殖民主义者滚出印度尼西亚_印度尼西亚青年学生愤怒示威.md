Title: Oppose the Netherlands' Reinforcements in West Irian! Demand Colonialists Get Out of Indonesia! Indonesian Youth and Students Demonstrate in Anger
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 2nd: Following demonstrations by youth and students in several major cities such as Jakarta and Bandung against Dutch provocations, youth and students in Palembang and Solo have recently held demonstrations against the Netherlands sending warships to West Irian and reinforcing troops there.

Hundreds of students and young people in Palembang demonstrated in front of the South Sumatra Governor's Office the day before yesterday. They shouted, "Liberate (West) Irian!" They held up placards with slogans such as "Condemn the Dutch as aggressors," "Confiscate Bataafse Petroleum Maatschappij," "We support the Cooperative Parliament," and so on.

The statement read to the South Sumatra Governor's representative emphasized that South Sumatra students and youth are prepared to cope with any unforeseen events. The statement also appealed to all Indonesian people to form a united front to deal with Dutch provocations.

Students in Solo also held a demonstration in front of the City Hall in mid-May, holding up placards with slogans such as "Drive the colonialists out of Indonesia..." and so on.

### 反对荷兰增兵西伊里安　要求殖民主义者滚出印度尼西亚  印度尼西亚青年学生愤怒示威

1960-06-04
第6版()
专栏：

    反对荷兰增兵西伊里安　要求殖民主义者滚出印度尼西亚
    印度尼西亚青年学生愤怒示威
    新华社雅加达2日电　在雅加达、万隆等几个大城市的青年和学生举行了反对荷兰挑衅的示威以后，巨港和梭罗的青年和学生最近也举行示威，反对荷兰派遣军舰到西伊里安，并向那里增援。
    数以百计的巨港学生和青年前天在南苏门答腊省长办公处前面举行示威。他们高呼：“解放（西）伊里安！”他们高举的标语牌上面写着，“谴责荷兰人是侵略者”，“没收巴达维亚石油公司”，“我们拥护合作议会”等口号。
    向南苏门答腊省长代表宣读的声明强调说，南苏门答腊学生和青年准备应付任何不测事件，声明还向全体印度尼西亚人民呼吁要求他们结成统一战线来对付荷兰的挑衅。
    梭罗的学生也在上月中旬在市政府前面举行示威，他们高举标语牌，上面写着：“把殖民主义者赶出印度尼西亚……”等口号。
