Title: Paraguayan National Liberation United Front Central Leadership Committee Condemns US Interference in Paraguayan Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, 15th: The Central Leadership Committee of the Paraguayan National Liberation United Front, in a letter to Eisenhower, condemned the US government and Eisenhower for supporting dictatorial regimes in Latin America, including Paraguay, and for interfering in the internal affairs of Latin American countries, particularly Cuba.

　　The letter stated that the Paraguayan people are determined to defend their right to self-determination and the right to self-determination of the peoples of Latin American countries with their lives, and to fight for national liberation and against colonialism.

　　The letter pointed out that the United States not only supports the Stroessner dictatorial regime financially, militarily, and diplomatically to oppose the Paraguayan people, but also sends a large number of American officials and military personnel to Paraguay to run rampant, controlling the economic, financial, and military power of Paraguay. The letter also revealed that the Paraguayan government granted extensive concessions to American oil monopolists. The United States is also building a huge jet airport in Paraguay.

　　The letter finally emphasized that despite US support, the Paraguayan people are determined to overthrow the odious dictatorial regime.

### 巴拉圭民族解放联合阵线中央领导委员会  斥美国干涉巴拉圭内政

1960-06-18
第6版()
专栏：

    巴拉圭民族解放联合阵线中央领导委员会
    斥美国干涉巴拉圭内政
    新华社布宜诺斯艾利斯15日电
  巴拉圭民族解放联合阵线中央领导委员会在给艾森豪威尔的一封信里谴责美国政府和艾森豪威尔支持包括巴拉圭在内的拉丁美洲独裁政权和干涉拉丁美洲各国，特别是干涉古巴的内政。
    这封信说，巴拉圭人民决心用生命来保卫他们和拉丁美洲各国人民的自决权，并且为争取民族解放和反对殖民主义而斗争。
    这封信指出，美国不仅在财政上、军事上和外交上支持斯特罗斯纳独裁政权来反对巴拉圭人民，同时还派遣大批美国官员和军事人员到巴拉圭胡作非为，他们掌握着巴拉圭经济、财政和军事的大权。信件还揭露巴拉圭政府给予美国石油垄断资本家以广泛的租让权。美国还在巴拉圭建筑巨大的喷气飞机场。
    信件最后强调说，尽管有美国的支持，巴拉圭人民决心推翻令人痛恨的独裁政权。
