Forest Protection Rhymes Hubei Huangpi
Hubei Huangpi Fan Jinhan

　　The sun has just gotten out of bed,
　　I drive the cattle up the hill,
　　The mountains are full of green grass,
　　Making the cattle wag their tails with joy.
　　Little calf, walk nicely,
　　Don't trample on the small saplings;
　　Big yellow bull, run slowly,
　　Be careful not to scratch the trees.
　　When we come up the mountain next year,
　　Green umbrellas will shade us from the sun.

　　White Poplar
　　Last year, a white poplar was planted,
　　Right at the village entrance,
　　Every day after school, I turn around,
　　Always comparing my height with it,
　　Grow quickly, grow quickly,
　　Grow up together to be pillars.

### 护林谣
范金汉
1960-06-15
第8版()
专栏：

    护林谣湖北黄陂
    湖北黄陂  范金汉太阳刚刚爬起床，我把牛儿赶上岗，满山野草青又青，乐得牛儿尾巴扬。小牛娃、乖乖走，莫要踩坏小树秧；大黄牯、慢慢跑，小心把树来擦伤。等到明年上山来，绿伞给咱们遮太阳。
    小白杨去年栽棵小白杨，恰恰栽在村口上，每天放学回家转，总要跟它比短长，快快长呀快快长，长大一起做栋梁。
