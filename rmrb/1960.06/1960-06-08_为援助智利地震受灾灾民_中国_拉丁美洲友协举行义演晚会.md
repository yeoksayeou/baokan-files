Title: China-Latin America Friendship Association Holds Charity Performance to Aid Chilean Earthquake Victims
Author:
Date: 1960-06-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th -- Several well-known Beijing theatrical troupes jointly held a charity performance tonight to raise funds for Chilean earthquake victims. The charity performance was sponsored by the China-Latin America Friendship Association. Participating troupes included the Mei Lanfang Peking Opera Troupe, the Xun Huisheng Peking Opera Troupe, the Shang Xiaoyun Peking Opera Troupe, the Beijing Opera School, and amateur dance performers from Beijing workers.

Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, spoke at the event. He said that the Chinese people are deeply concerned about the earthquake disaster suffered by the Chilean people in late May. He expressed condolences to the Chilean people on behalf of the China-Latin America Friendship Association.

Chu Tunan said: The Chinese people have not only consistently supported the Chilean people's just struggle against U.S. imperialism to safeguard national independence and fight for democracy, but are also very willing to provide them with brotherly assistance when they encounter misfortune. He concluded by wishing the Chilean people a swift victory over the natural disaster and an early reconstruction of their homes.

Representatives from the nineteen member organizations of the China-Latin America Friendship Association attended the charity performance and made enthusiastic donations.

The Chilean council member attending the World Federation of Trade Unions meeting, the Chilean agricultural delegation, the Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, the famous Chilean painter Venturelli, as well as Latin American foreign guests and experts in Beijing also attended the charity performance.

Also attending the charity performance were Ji Chaoding and Zhou Erfu, Vice Presidents of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, council members of the Friendship Association in Beijing, and well-known figures from the capital including Tian Han, Ma Shaobo, and Qian Liren, totaling over one thousand people.

The program included the Chilean folk dance, Cueca, and the Peking Opera "Legend of the White Snake."

The total proceeds from the evening's performance, along with donations from the China-Latin America Friendship Association and over ten other organizations at the event, amounted to more than 46,000 yuan, which will be remitted to Chile by the China-Latin America Friendship Association in the coming days.

### 为援助智利地震受灾灾民  中国——拉丁美洲友协举行义演晚会

1960-06-08
第6版()
专栏：

    为援助智利地震受灾灾民
    中国——拉丁美洲友协举行义演晚会
    新华社6日讯　北京一些著名剧团今晚为捐助智利地震灾民联合举行义演晚会。这次义演晚会是由中国拉丁美洲友好协会主办的。参加义演的剧团有梅兰芳剧团、荀慧生剧团、尚小云剧团、北京市戏曲学校和北京职工业余舞蹈演员。
    中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南在会上讲话。他说，中国人民十分关怀智利人民5月下旬所受到的地震灾害。他代表中国拉丁美洲友好协会对智利人民表示慰问。
    楚图南说：中国人民不仅一向支持智利人民反对美帝国主义维护民族独立、争取民主的正义斗争，而且当他们遭遇到不幸的时候也极愿给予他们兄弟般的支援。他最后祝智利人民迅速战胜自然灾害，早日重建家园。
    中国拉丁美洲友好协会的十九个团体会员的代表出席了义演晚会，并且热烈捐款。
    智利出席世界工联理事会的理事、智利农业代表团、亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长、智利著名画家万徒勒里以及在北京的拉丁美洲的外宾和专家也出席了义演晚会。
    出席义演晚会的还有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长冀朝鼎、周而复，友协在京理事和首都各界著名人士田汉、马少波、钱李仁等约一千多人。
    晚会上演出了智利民间舞蹈古恩卡舞和京剧白蛇传。
    晚会的全部收入及中国拉丁美洲友好协会等十多个团体在晚会上的捐款共四万六千多元，日内即将由中国拉丁美洲友好协会汇往智利。
