Title: To Coordinate with Eisenhower's Visit, U.S. Troops Invading Taiwan Intensify War Provocations
Author:
Date: 1960-06-09
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 8th dispatch: Taipei news: An Associated Press dispatch from Taipei on the 6th revealed that during U.S. President Eisenhower's "visit" to Taiwan, the U.S. invading forces in the Taiwan Strait area will intensify war activities and carry out military provocations against our people.

The Associated Press dispatch said that in the Taiwan Strait area, "American air and sea power has already made impressive but undisclosed deployments, ready to act when necessary." The dispatch also said, "American planes have begun arriving at the huge Kungkuan Air Force Base in central Formosa (referring to Taiwan) on Monday (the 6th)." A United Press International dispatch from Taipei on June 1, quoting "military sources," said, "During Eisenhower's 24-hour visit here on June 18 and 19, the United States and Nationalist China (referring to the Chiang Kai-shek group) will mobilize the maximum air and sea patrol forces to defend Formosa." The dispatch also said that "missiles, nuclear-armed U.S. Seventh Fleet and Nationalist jet air force" stationed in Taiwan will be in a so-called "high state of alert."

### 配合艾森豪威尔访问  侵台美军加紧战争挑衅

1960-06-09
第7版()
专栏：

    配合艾森豪威尔访问
    侵台美军加紧战争挑衅
    新华社8日讯　台北消息：美联社6日从台北发出一则消息，暴露了美国总统艾森豪威尔“访问”台湾时，在台湾海峡地区的美国侵略军队将加紧进行战争活动，对我国人民进行军事挑衅。
    美联社消息说，现在在台湾海峡地区“美国空、海力量已经做了给人很深印象的但是没有透露的部署，准备在必要时行动”。消息并说，“美国飞机星期一（6日）已开始到达福摩萨（指台湾）中部巨大的公馆空军基地”。美国合众国际社6月1日从台北发出的消息引用“军方人士”的话说，“在艾森豪威尔6月18日到19日到这里作二十四小时访问期间，美国和国民党中国（指蒋介石集团）将动员最大的空、海巡逻力量保卫福摩萨。”消息还说，驻在台湾的“导弹、配备原子武器的美国第七舰队和国民党的喷气机空军”将处于所谓“高度戒备状态”。
