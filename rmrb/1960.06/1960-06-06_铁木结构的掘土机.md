Title: Iron-Wood Structure Excavator
Author:
Date: 1960-06-06
Page: 2

Continuously Promote, Continuously Improve, Continuously Create

    Iron-Wood Structure Excavator
    Ren Changjiu, an old worker at the Third Construction Engineering Company of Changchun City, recently used a combination of indigenous and foreign methods to create an excavator with a mixed iron and wood structure, overcoming a key challenge in earthwork projects.

    Trial use at the construction site showed that this excavator can dig more than one cubic meter of earth per minute, with a minimum of more than four hundred and eighty cubic meters per day. Calculated on the basis of an average daily manual digging of four cubic meters, it can replace the work of one hundred and twenty people.

### 铁木结构的掘土机

1960-06-06
第2版()
专栏：不断推广　不断提高　不断创造

    铁木结构的掘土机
    长春市第三建筑工程公司老工人任长久，最近用土洋结合的方法制成一台铁木混合结构的掘土机，攻下了土方工程的关键。
    在施工现场试用结果，这种掘土机每分钟掘土一立方米以上，每天最低可掘土四百八十多立方米，以人工平均日挖土四立方米计算，可顶一百二十人操作。
