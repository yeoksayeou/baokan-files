Title: Soviet Artists Performing Troupe Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-06-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, June 28th: The Soviet Artists Performing Troupe, led by A. G. Novikov, has left Beijing by plane on the 26th to return to their country after visiting and performing in eight cities including Beijing, Zhengzhou, Wuhan, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Suzhou, Tianjin, and Taiyuan.

The Soviet Artists Performing Troupe came to China for a visiting performance based on the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement. They performed a total of thirty-two shows in various locations, with an audience of more than 70,000 people. Their superb performances were warmly welcomed by the Chinese audience.

Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others went to the airport to see them off.

### 苏联艺术家表演团离京回国

1960-06-30
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联艺术家表演团离京回国
    新华社28日讯　由阿·格·诺维科夫率领的苏联艺术家表演团，在北京、郑州、武汉、上海、杭州、苏州、天津、太原等八个城市进行访问演出后，已经在26日乘飞机离开北京回国。
    苏联艺术家表演团是根据中苏文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国作访问演出的。他们在各地共演出三十二场，观众有七万多人。他们的精湛表演受到了中国观众的热烈欢迎。
    前往机场欢送的有对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊等。
