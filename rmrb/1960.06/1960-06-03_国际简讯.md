    Hungarian Government Decides to Provide Relief to Chilean Earthquake Victims
    Budapest dispatch, June 2nd, "People's Freedom Daily" reports: The Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government has decided to donate medicines, food, blankets, and other supplies worth 300,000 forints (Hungarian currency) through the Hungarian Red Cross to aid Chilean refugees who have recently suffered from the earthquake.
    Swiss Expedition Reaches Summit of Dhaulagiri
    Kathmandu dispatch, according to Western news agencies, the leader of a Swiss expedition, Max Eiselin, announced in Kathmandu on May 26 that six members of his expedition successfully climbed to the summit of Dhaulagiri, which is 26,795 feet (approximately 8,167 meters) above sea level, on May 13.

### 国际简讯

1960-06-03
第6版()
专栏：

    国际简讯
    匈政府决定救济智利地震灾民
    布达佩斯讯　据“人民自由报”2日报道：匈牙利工农革命政府决定通过匈牙利红十字会捐献价值三十万福林（匈币名）的药物、食品和毯子等，救济最近遭受地震之害的智利难民。
    瑞士探险队登上道拉吉里顶峰
    加德满都消息　据西方通讯社报道，一个瑞士探险队的队长马克斯·埃斯森5月26日在加德满都宣布，他的探险队的六个队员在5月13日攀登拔海二万六千七百九十五英尺（约八千一百六十七米）的道拉吉里峰顶峰成功。
