Title: Support You, Japanese Brothers!
Author: Li Guanding
Date: 1960-06-03
Page: 8

    Support You, Japanese Brothers!
    Li Guanding, Student of Peking University

    More violent than a volcanic eruption,
    More surging than ocean waves,
    —What sound
    Is shaking the entire universe?
    The days of May are fiery red,
    The east wind strongly prevails over the west wind,
    Hundreds of millions of oppressed people roar as one:
    Oppose oppression, oppose war!
    Ah, Japan, our brothers!
    You stand at the gateway of the East,
    Launching sixteen offensives against US imperialism and the Kishi Nobusuke clique!
    Sixteen times, sixteen times, ah,
    Each time more violent, each time more successful!
    The seventeenth national mobilization has begun,
    Look, before you, Kishi Nobusuke is trembling,
    Beneath your feet, US imperialism is terrified!
    Well done, Japanese brothers!
    The 650 million Chinese people are your backing.
    We have a common enemy—US imperialism,
    We have a common desire—world peace!
    We stand on the same front,
    Your struggle is our struggle!
    Support you, Japanese brothers!
    Look, fists and arms form a forest before Tiananmen,
    Listen, angry roars shake the sky across the country!
    Ah, the flame of struggle burns in the hearts of 650 million people,
    650 million people fight alongside you!
    We support you,
    Until you achieve final victory!
    With our hands,
    Tear apart the "Japan-US Military Alliance Treaty"!
    With our fists,
    Drive away US imperialism!

### 支持你们，日本兄弟！
李观鼎
1960-06-03
第8版()
专栏：

    支持你们，日本兄弟！
    北京大学学生　李观鼎
    比火山爆发还猛烈，
    比大海波涛还汹涌，
    ——是什么声音
    把整个宇宙震动？
    五月的日子火红，
    东风劲吹压西风，
    千百万被压迫人民一个吼声：
    反对压迫，反对战争！
    呵，日本，我们的兄弟！
    你们站在东方的大门口，
    向美帝国主义和岸信介集团
    发动了十六次进攻！
    十六次，十六次呵，
    一次比一次猛烈，一次比一次成功！
    开始了，第十七次全国总动员，
    看哪，你们面前，岸信介在发抖，
    你们脚下，美帝国主义胆战心惊！
    干得好呵，日本兄弟！
    六亿五千万中国人是你们的后盾。
    我们有共同的敌人——美帝国主义，
    我们有共同的意愿——世界和平！
    我们站在同一条战线，
    你们的斗争就是我们的斗争！
    支持你们，日本兄弟！
    看，天安门前拳臂成林，
    听，全国各地怒吼惊空！
    呵，斗争的火焰燃在六亿五千万颗心头，
    六亿五千万人民同你们一起战斗！
    我们支持你们，
    直到你们取得最后胜利的时候！
    用我们的双手，
    把“日美军事同盟条约”撕碎！
    用我们的拳头，
    把美帝国主义赶走！
