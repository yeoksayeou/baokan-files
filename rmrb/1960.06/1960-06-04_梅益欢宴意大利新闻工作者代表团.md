Title: Mei Yi Hosts Banquet for Italian Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd: Mei Yi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, hosted a banquet on the evening of June 1st to welcome the Italian Journalists Delegation headed by Sergio Segre, Secretary-General of the Italian Center for the Study of Development and Economic and Cultural Relations with China.

### 梅益欢宴意大利新闻工作者代表团

1960-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    梅益欢宴意大利新闻工作者代表团
    据新华社2日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席梅益1日晚设宴欢迎以意大利发展与中国经济和文化关系研究中心秘书长塞尔乔·赛格莱为首的意大利新闻工作者代表团。
