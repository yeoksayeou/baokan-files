Title: China's Acrobatic Troupe Leaves Guinea for Morocco
Author:
Date: 1960-06-07
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, 4th: The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, led by its head Qu Wu, concluded its two-month visit to the Republic of Guinea and left Conakry this morning for Morocco. Among those at the airport to see them off were Diallo Alpha, Secretary-General of the Guinean Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Doumkara Tibou, Supervisor of the African Youth Democratic Union, as well as Ke Hua, the Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, and personnel from the Chinese Embassy.

### 我国杂技艺术团离几内亚赴摩洛哥

1960-06-07
第7版()
专栏：

    我国杂技艺术团离几内亚赴摩洛哥
    据新华社科纳克里4日电　中国杂技艺术团结束了它在几内亚共和国的两个月访问，今天上午在团长屈武率领下离开科纳克里前往摩洛哥。到机场欢送的有几内亚外交部秘书长迪亚洛·阿尔法，非洲青年民主联盟总监杜恩卡拉·蒂布以及中国驻几内亚大使柯华和中国大使馆人员等。
