Title: Khrushchev Leads Delegation to Romania to Attend Romanian Workers' Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-06-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 18th: The delegation of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, led by First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU, Khrushchev, arrived here by plane today to attend the Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party.

The following Romanian party and government leaders were at the airport to welcome them: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chivu Stoica, Maurer, and others.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Romania and members of the fraternal parties' delegations who came to attend the "Third Congress" of the Romanian Workers' Party were also at the airport to welcome them.

### 参加罗马尼亚工人党代表大会  赫鲁晓夫率代表团抵罗

1960-06-19
第6版()
专栏：

    参加罗马尼亚工人党代表大会
    赫鲁晓夫率代表团抵罗
    新华社布加勒斯特18日电　前来参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会的苏联共产党代表团，在苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫的率领下，今天乘飞机到达这里。
    到机场欢迎的有：乔治乌—德治、基伏·斯托伊卡、毛雷尔等罗马尼亚党政领导人。
    各国驻罗马尼亚外交使节以及前来参加罗马尼亚工人党“三大”的各兄弟党代表团人员也到机场欢迎。
