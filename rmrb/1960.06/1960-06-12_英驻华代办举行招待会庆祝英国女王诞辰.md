Title: British Chargé d'Affaires in China Holds Reception to Celebrate the Queen's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1960-06-12
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, June 11th. British Chargé d'Affaires in China, Shidi Hua, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the birthday of Queen Elizabeth II of Great Britain.

Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, were invited to attend.

Ambassadors and diplomatic officials from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 英驻华代办举行招待会庆祝英国女王诞辰

1960-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    英驻华代办举行招待会庆祝英国女王诞辰
    新华社11日讯　英国驻中国代办施棣华今天下午举行招待会，庆祝英国女王伊丽莎白二世诞辰。
    应邀出席的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若和外交部副部长罗贵波等。
    各国驻中国大使和外交官员也出席了招待会。
