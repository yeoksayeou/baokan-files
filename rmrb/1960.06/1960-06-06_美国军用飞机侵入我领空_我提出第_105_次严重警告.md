Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Our Airspace, We Issue the 105th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-06-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th June: On June 5th, from 16:48 to 17:00, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Pinghai to Shanwei area of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 105th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我领空  我提出第105次严重警告

1960-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我领空
    我提出第105次严重警告
    新华社5日讯　6月5日十六时四十八分至十七时，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省平海至汕尾地区上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百○五次严重警告。
