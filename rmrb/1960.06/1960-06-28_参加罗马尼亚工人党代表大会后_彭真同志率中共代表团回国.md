Title: Comrade Peng Zhen Leads CPC Delegation Home After Attending Romanian Workers' Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-06-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 27th: The Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Comrade Peng Zhen, which attended the Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party, left Bucharest by special plane this afternoon to return home.

Xu Jianguo, Chinese Ambassador to Romania, also returned home with the delegation.

Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Apostol, member of the Political Bureau of the Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee, and Koliu and Salajan, alternate members of the Political Bureau.

Wang Luming, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, embassy staff, and representatives of Chinese experts and students working in Romania also saw the delegation off at the airport.

### 参加罗马尼亚工人党代表大会后  彭真同志率中共代表团回国

1960-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    参加罗马尼亚工人党代表大会后
    彭真同志率中共代表团回国
    新华社布加勒斯特27日电　参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会的中国共产党代表团，在彭真同志率领下，于今天下午乘专机离布加勒斯特回国。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使许建国也随代表团回国。
    到机场送行的有罗马尼亚工人党中央政治局委员阿波斯托尔、政治局候补委员柯柳、萨拉扬等人。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办王鲁明和使馆工作人员以及在罗马尼亚工作的中国专家和留学生的代表也到机场送行。
