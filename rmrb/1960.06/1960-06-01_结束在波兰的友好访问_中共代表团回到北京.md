Title: CCP Delegation Returns to Beijing After Friendly Visit to Poland

Author:
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 31st -- The Chinese Communist Party Delegation, headed by Bo Yibo, and including Li Baohua, Liu Xiufeng, Huo Shilian, Xu Daoqi, and five staff members, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after visiting Poland at the invitation of the Polish United Workers' Party.

Those who greeted them at the airport included Li Fuchun, member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat; Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee; Gong Zirong, a responsible staff member of the CCP Central Committee; Gu Mu and Wang Xin, vice chairmen of the State Economic Commission; Song Yuhe, Vice Minister of Construction Engineering; Qian Zhengying and Cheng Mingsheng, Vice Ministers of Water Resources and Electric Power, etc. Polish Ambassador to China, Jerzy Knothe, Counselor Furato, and other diplomats from the embassy also greeted them at the airport.

### 结束在波兰的友好访问  中共代表团回到北京

1960-06-01
第6版()
专栏：

    结束在波兰的友好访问
    中共代表团回到北京
    新华社31日讯　中国共产党代表团团长薄一波，团员李葆华、刘秀峰、霍士廉、许道琦和随员五人，应波兰统一工人党邀请访问波兰后，已于今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场迎接的有中共中央政治局委员李富春，书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员伍修权，中共中央机关负责工作人员龚子荣，国家经济委员会副主任谷牧、王新三，建筑工程部副部长宋裕和，水利电力部副部长钱正英、程明升等。波兰驻华大使耶日·克诺泰、参赞傅拉托和大使馆其他外交官也到机场迎接。
