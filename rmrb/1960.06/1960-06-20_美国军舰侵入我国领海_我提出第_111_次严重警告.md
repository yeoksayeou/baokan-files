Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into China's Territorial Waters: 111th Serious Warning Issued
Author:
Date: 1960-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. A U.S. warship intruded into the waters of the Haitan and Wuqiu areas of Fujian Province from 18:16 to 22:35 on June 18th. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 111th serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我国领海  我提出第111次严重警告

1960-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我国领海
    我提出第111次严重警告
    新华社19日讯　6月18日十八时十六分至二十二时三十五分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省海坛、乌丘地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百一十一次严重警告。
