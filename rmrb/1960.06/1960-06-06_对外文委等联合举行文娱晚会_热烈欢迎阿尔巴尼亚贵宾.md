Title: The Foreign Cultural Commission and Others Jointly Hold a Cultural and Entertainment Evening to Warmly Welcome Distinguished Albanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-06-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Ministry of Culture, and the Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association jointly held a cultural and entertainment evening at the CPPCC Auditorium today, warmly welcoming Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, and other distinguished guests.

　　At the evening party, the Shanghai Experimental Opera House performed the historical dance drama "The Small Swords Society" in seven acts, reflecting the resolute struggle of the people of Shanghai a century ago against imperialism and feudal rule.

　　Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen Guo Moruo and Saifudin, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Ding Xilin and Luo Jun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and President of the Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association Jiang Nanxiang, accompanied the distinguished guests to watch the performance.

　　After the performance was completely over, Chairman Lleshi and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Chairman Dong Biwu and others, stepped onto the stage, presented flower baskets to the actors, and warmly shook hands with the actors to congratulate them on their successful performance.

### 对外文委等联合举行文娱晚会  热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚贵宾

1960-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    对外文委等联合举行文娱晚会
    热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚贵宾
    新华社5日讯　对外文化联络委员会、文化部、中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会今天在政协礼堂联合举行文艺晚会，热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希和其他贵宾。
    晚会上，上海实验歌剧院演出了反映一百年前上海人民为反对帝国主义和反对封建统治而进行的一场坚决斗争的七场历史舞剧——“小刀会”。
    董必武副主席，习仲勋副总理，郭沫若、赛福鼎副委员长，文化部部长沈雁冰，对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林、罗俊，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会会长蒋南翔，陪同贵宾们观看演出。
    演出全部结束后，列希主席等贵宾由董必武副主席等陪同走上舞台，向演员们赠花篮，并且同演员们亲切握手，祝贺他们演出成功。
