Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Our Airspace: 108th Serious Warning Issued

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: On June 14th, from 11:27 to 11:38, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 108th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵犯我领空  我提第108次严重警告

1960-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵犯我领空
    我提第108次严重警告
    新华社14日讯　6月14日十一时二十七分至十一时三十八分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百零八次严重警告。
