Title: Chairman Liu, Premier Zhou, Foreign Minister Chen Send Telegrams Congratulating Congo on its Declaration of Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-06-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram on the 26th to the Head of State of the Republic of the Congo, Joseph Kasavubu, congratulating Congo on its declaration of independence. The telegram reads as follows:

Congo, Leopoldville
To His Excellency Mr. Joseph Kasavubu, Head of State of the Congo:

On the occasion of Congo's declaration of independence, I, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of the Congo. I wish the people of the Congo further achievements in their struggle to strengthen national unity and uphold national independence. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
June 26, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram on the 26th to the Prime Minister of the Republic of the Congo, Patrice Lumumba, congratulating Congo on its declaration of independence. The telegram reads as follows:

Congo, Leopoldville
To His Excellency Mr. Patrice Lumumba, Prime Minister of the Congo:

I am delighted to learn of Congo's declaration of independence. On behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and, through you, to the government and people of the Congo. Congo's independence is a great victory for the Congolese people's heroic struggle for national independence. This victory will help strengthen the struggle of the people of African countries against imperialism and colonialism, and for national independence and its preservation.

The Chinese government and the Chinese people have always warmly sympathized with and resolutely supported the just struggle of the Congolese people for national independence. I am deeply convinced that the friendship established between the Chinese and Congolese peoples in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism will continue to develop and be consolidated.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
June 26, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, sent a telegram on the 26th to the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of the Congo, congratulating Congo on its declaration of independence, and informing him that the Chinese government has decided to recognize the Republic of the Congo. The telegram reads as follows:

Congo, Leopoldville
To His Excellency the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Congo:

I am pleased to learn that Congo has declared its independence. On behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to the Congolese government. I am honored to inform Your Excellency that the government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize the Congo. I hope that my government's recognition of your country will help further develop friendly relations between China and the Congo.

Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
Beijing, June 26, 1960

### 刘主席　周总理　陈外长  电贺刚果宣告独立

1960-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

    刘主席　周总理　陈外长
    电贺刚果宣告独立
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇26日打电报给刚果共和国国家元首约瑟夫·卡萨武布，祝贺刚果宣告独立。电文如下：刚果　利奥波德维尔刚果国家元首约瑟夫·卡萨武布先生阁下：
    在此刚果宣告独立的时候，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义，向阁下和刚果人民致以热烈的祝贺。祝刚果人民在增强民族团结、坚持民族独立的斗争中取得进一步的成就。祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
                            中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                                         1960年6月26日
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来26日打电报给刚果共和国总理帕特里斯·卢蒙巴，祝贺刚果宣告独立。电文如下：刚果　利奥波德维尔刚果总理帕特里斯·卢蒙巴先生阁下：
    欣闻刚果宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民，向阁下并通过阁下向刚果政府和人民表示诚挚的祝贺。刚果的独立是刚果人民为争取民族独立而进行的英勇斗争的伟大胜利。这一胜利将有助于加强非洲各国人民反对帝国主义和殖民主义、争取和维护民族独立的斗争。
    中国政府和中国人民一贯热烈同情和坚决支持刚果人民争取民族独立的正义斗争。我深信，中、刚两国人民在反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争中建立起来的友谊，必将日益发展和巩固。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                      1960年6月26日
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅在26日打电报给刚果共和国外交部长，祝贺刚果宣告独立，并通知他，中国政府已经决定承认刚果共和国。电文如下：刚果　利奥波德维尔刚果外交部长阁下：
    欣悉刚果宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向刚果政府致以衷心的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认刚果。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将有助于中、刚两国友好关系的进一步发展。
                  中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                                1960年6月26日于北京
