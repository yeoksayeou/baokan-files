Title: Chairman Leshi Visits and Tours Hangzhou; Staff of the Dū Jǐnshēng Silk Weaving Factory Warmly Welcome Albanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-06-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, June 8th. Comrade Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Wu Xian, Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, today visited the Dū Jǐnshēng Silk Weaving Factory, which was awarded the title of National Red Flag Factory in 1959, in Hangzhou.

    During the visit, the distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by Qiú Nán'ān, Director of the Dū Jǐnshēng Silk Weaving Factory, and Chén Shànlián, the female worker-turned-Deputy Director, as well as the male and female workers. The distinguished guests carefully observed the production process of various silk fabrics. During the visit, they inquired with concern about the factory's production situation. Upon saying goodbye, Chairman Lleshi said to Director Qiú Nán'ān: "Congratulations on your greater victories in production." Politburo Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, Comrade Belishova, said to Chén Shànlián: "We wish you to maintain the glorious title of Red Flag Factory. Every achievement you make will strengthen the power of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union."
    This morning and afternoon, the distinguished guests also enjoyed visiting scenic spots and historical sites on West Lake such as Three Pools Mirroring the Moon, the Flower Harbor Viewing Fish Park, Yuquan, Lingyin Temple, and the Six Harmonies Pagoda in the rain, and took a boat trip on West Lake to appreciate the lake and mountain scenery.
    In the evening, Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province, hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished Albanian guests.

### 列希主席在杭州参观游览  都锦生丝织厂职工热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚贵宾

1960-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    列希主席在杭州参观游览
    都锦生丝织厂职工热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚贵宾
    新华社杭州8日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希和其他贵宾，今天由中共浙江省委书记处书记、浙江省副省长吴宪等负责人陪同，在杭州参观了1959年获得全国红旗厂称号的都锦生丝织厂。
    贵宾们在参观时，受到都锦生丝织厂厂长裘南安和女工人出身的副厂长陈善莲以及男女工人们的热烈欢迎。贵宾们在这里仔细地参观了各种丝织品的生产过程。参观中，贵宾们关切地询问了丝织厂的生产情况。在告别时，列希主席向厂长裘南安说：“祝贺你们在生产上取得更大胜利。”阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员贝利绍娃向陈善莲说：“祝你们保持红旗厂的光荣称号，你们的每一项成就，都将增强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的力量。”
    今天上午和下午，贵宾们还兴致勃勃地冒雨游览了三潭印月、花港观鱼公园、玉泉、灵隐寺、六和塔等西湖名胜古迹，并且泛舟西湖欣赏了湖光山色。
    晚间，浙江省省长周建人设宴招待阿尔巴尼亚贵宾。
