Title: Sino-Soviet Academies of Medical Sciences Sign Agreement on Cooperation in Medical Science
Author:
Date: 1960-06-11
Page: 6

    Sino-Soviet Academies of Medical Sciences
    Sign Agreement on Cooperation in Medical Science
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The delegations of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences and the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences signed the "Scientific Cooperation Agreement between the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences and the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences on Major Medical Issues" in Beijing tonight.
    Huang Jia驷, President of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences, signed on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences; Academician V.D. Timakov, First Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences, signed on behalf of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences.
    Li Dequan, Minister of Public Health, Vice Ministers He Biao and Qian Xinzhong, and Zhang Yunzhi, Deputy Director of the International Cooperation Bureau of the Science and Technology Commission of the People's Republic of China, also attended the signing ceremony.
    Sudalikov, Cultural Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, First Secretary Sang Feilou, members of the delegation of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences, and Soviet experts working at the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences attended the signing ceremony.
    After the signing ceremony, the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences held a banquet to entertain the delegation of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences.

### 中苏两国医学科学院  签订医学科学合作协定

1960-06-11
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏两国医学科学院
    签订医学科学合作协定
    新华社10日讯　中国医学科学院和苏联医学科学院代表团今晚在北京签订了“中国医学科学院和苏联医学科学院在重大医学问题方面的科学合作协定”。
    代表中国医学科学院签字的是中国医学科学院院长黄家驷；代表苏联医学科学院签字的是苏联医学科学院第一副院长弗·得·季马科夫院士。
    参加签字仪式的还有卫生部部长李德全，副部长贺彪、钱信忠，中华人民共和国科学技术委员会国际合作局副局长张韵之等。
    苏联驻华大使馆文化参赞苏达利柯夫、一等秘书桑飞楼，苏联医学科学院代表团团员，以及在中国医学科学院工作的苏联专家参加了签字仪式。
    签字仪式以后，中国医学科学院举行宴会，招待苏联医学科学院代表团。
