Title: Wu Lengxi Hosts Farewell Banquet for Albanian News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st. The Albanian news delegation headed by Ajet Simixhiu, Director of the Albanian Telegraph Agency, concluded its friendly visit to our country and departed from Beijing by plane this morning to return home. Before their departure, Wu Lengxi, Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, and Director of the Xinhua News Agency, hosted a farewell banquet. Responsible persons from the capital's press circles attended the banquet. Albanian Ambassador to China, Pllambeti Pllifti, was also invited to the banquet.

### 吴冷西为阿新闻代表团饯行

1960-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    吴冷西为阿新闻代表团饯行
    新华社1日讯　以阿尔巴尼亚通讯社社长阿耶特·西米季乌为首的阿尔巴尼亚新闻工作者代表团结束了在我国的友好访问于今晨乘飞机离京返国。行前由中华全国新闻工作者协会主席、人民日报总编辑、新华通讯社社长吴冷西设便宴欢送。出席宴会的有首都新闻界负责人士。阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使帕里夫蒂也应邀参加了宴会。
