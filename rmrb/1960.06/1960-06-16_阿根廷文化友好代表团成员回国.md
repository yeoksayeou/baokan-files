Title: Members of the Argentine Cultural Friendship Delegation Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-06-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Members of the Argentine Cultural Friendship Delegation who visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association: Doctor Ernesto Malbec, Mr. and Mrs. Jose Chudnovsky, and writer Carlos Luis D’audet, have already left Beijing by plane and train on the 14th and 15th respectively to return to their country.

### 阿根廷文化友好代表团成员回国

1960-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷文化友好代表团成员回国
    新华社15日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷文化友好代表团成员：医生埃尔内斯托·马耳贝克、何塞·楚德诺夫斯基夫妇和作家卡洛斯·路易斯·达乌德特，已经在14、15日分别乘飞机和火车离开北京回国。
