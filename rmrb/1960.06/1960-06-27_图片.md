    The People's Liberation Army Zhan You (Combat Friend) Acrobatic Troupe performed the drama "Friendship" in Beijing, which depicts the fighting friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples. This is a scene from the drama. To save a reconnaissance platoon leader of the Chinese People's Volunteers, the Korean woman Jin Shunyu detonated a grenade and fought heroically with the enemy, sacrificing herself gloriously. (Xinhua News Agency)
