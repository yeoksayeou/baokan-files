Title: Peng Zhen Leads CCP Delegation to Attend the Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party

Author:
Date: 1960-06-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th - At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has sent a Chinese Communist Party delegation headed by Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, to attend the Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party. The delegation left Beijing by plane this morning for Bucharest.

The other members of the delegation are: Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Wu Xiuquan, member of the CPC Central Committee, and Xu Jianguo, Chinese Ambassador to Romania.

Comrade Xu Jianguo is already in Romania.

Those who saw the delegation off at the airport were Liu Lantao, alternate secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, members of the Central Committee, and Gong Zirong and other responsible personnel from the organs of the CPC Central Committee.

Also present at the airport to see them off were Soviet Ambassador to China Chervonenko, Counselor Sudarikov, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, Bashkirov.

### 应邀参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会  彭真同志率中共代表团启程

1960-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会
    彭真同志率中共代表团启程
    新华社16日讯　中国共产党中央委员会应罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会的邀请，派遣以中共中央政治局委员彭真为首的中国共产党代表团参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会。代表团一行，已于今天上午乘飞机离开北京，前往布加勒斯特。
    代表团的其他成员是：中共中央政治局候补委员康生，中共中央委员伍修权，中国驻罗马尼亚大使许建国。
    许建国同志已经在罗马尼亚。
    到机场送行的，有中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛，中央委员廖承志、刘宁一，中共中央机关负责工作人员龚子荣等。
    到机场送行的，还有：苏联驻华大使契尔沃年科，参赞苏达利柯夫，罗马尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办巴什基洛。
