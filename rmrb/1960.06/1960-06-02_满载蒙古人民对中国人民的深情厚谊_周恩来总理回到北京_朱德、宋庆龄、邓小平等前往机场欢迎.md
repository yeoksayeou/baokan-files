Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Returns to Beijing, Full of the Profound Friendship of the Mongolian People Towards the Chinese People; Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, Deng Xiaoping and Others Go to the Airport to Welcome Him
Author:
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 1st. After concluding a friendly visit to the Mongolian People's Republic, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and their entourage returned to Beijing by special plane from Ulaanbaatar at 11:15 this morning, carrying with them the profound friendship of the fraternal Mongolian people towards the Chinese people.

Party and state leaders Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Li Xiannian and others went to the airport to welcome them.

When Premier Zhou Enlai stepped out of the cabin, those waiting at the airport burst into warm applause. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi warmly shook hands with the welcomers.

Children from China and Mongolia presented flowers to Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi.

Present at the airport to welcome them were Balzhinnyam, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, and Tumur-Ochir, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat, and member of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, who are currently in Beijing.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China.

Also present at the airport to welcome them today were Vice Premiers of the State Council Luo Ruiqing and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, and Saifudin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee Wang Jiaxiang, alternate secretary Liu Lantao, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Siguang, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong and Fu Zuoyi, President of the Supreme People's Court Xie Juezai, and Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and Geng Biao.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were responsible persons from various government departments, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, responsible persons from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and Beijing Municipality, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and representatives of minority nationalities in Beijing.

### 满载蒙古人民对中国人民的深情厚谊  周恩来总理回到北京  朱德、宋庆龄、邓小平等前往机场欢迎

1960-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    满载蒙古人民对中国人民的深情厚谊
    周恩来总理回到北京
    朱德、宋庆龄、邓小平等前往机场欢迎
    新华社1日讯　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和随行人员，结束了在蒙古人民共和国的友好访问之后，满载兄弟的蒙古人民对中国人民的深情厚谊，于今天上午十一时十五分乘专机由乌兰巴托回到北京。
    党和国家的领导人朱德、宋庆龄、邓小平、彭真、李先念等，前往机场欢迎。
    周恩来总理走出机舱时，迎候在机场上的人们发出热烈掌声。周恩来总理和陈毅副总理同欢迎的人们亲切握手。
    中蒙两国的儿童向周总理和陈毅副总理献了鲜花。
    正在北京的蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员、蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席巴尔吉尼雅姆，蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员、书记处书记、蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团委员图穆尔—奥奇尔，到飞机场欢迎。
    到飞机场欢迎的，还有各国驻中国的外交使节和外交官员。
    今天，到飞机场欢迎的，还有国务院副总理罗瑞卿、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、郭沫若、赛福鼎，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，候补书记刘澜涛，政协全国委员会副主席李四光，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，外交部副部长罗贵波、耿飚。
    到飞机场欢迎的，还有政府各部门的负责人，全国人民代表大会常务委员会的委员，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会的委员，各民主党派、各人民团体和北京市的负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，在北京的少数民族的代表等。
