Title: The 8th German Agricultural Exhibition Opens; German Party and Government Leaders Visit the Chinese Pavilion; Heinrich Rau Says the Chinese Pavilion Showcases the Achievements of the People's Communes and the Great Leap Forward
Author:
Date: 1960-06-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The 8th German Democratic Republic Agricultural Exhibition opened in Leipzig on the 3rd. Those attending the opening ceremony included Heinrich Rau, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Deputy Prime Minister; Grünenberg, alternate member of the Politburo of the Central Committee, and other party and government leaders of the German Democratic Republic, government delegations from various socialist countries, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to the German Democratic Republic.

The main content of this exhibition is achievements related to consolidating agricultural production cooperatives and developing animal husbandry and agriculture. The Socialist Unity Party and the government are treating this exhibition as a school for promoting advanced experiences and further consolidating and developing the cooperative movement. Cooperatives members, agricultural workers, and workers in industrial sectors related to agriculture from all over the country are preparing to visit the exhibition to learn and study advanced experiences.

The exhibition covers an area of 132 hectares and will last for more than 40 days. The exhibition also includes pavilions of socialist countries such as the Soviet Union, China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, and Mongolia. China and Mongolia are participating in the German Democratic Republic Agricultural Exhibition for the first time. The main content of the Chinese pavilion's exhibits includes three parts: the people's communes, the Great Leap Forward, and the "Eight-Point Charter" for agriculture. This is the first time that China's agriculture has been exhibited in Europe.

Heinrich Rau and other party and government leaders visited some of the main pavilions of the German Democratic Republic and the pavilions of the Soviet Union, China, and other countries as the first group of distinguished guests. After visiting the Chinese pavilion, Heinrich Rau wrote in the guestbook: "The Chinese exhibition shows the Great Leap Forward in the People's Communes! I wish you further and greater achievements!"

On the 4th, Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, visited the Chinese exhibition pavilion while visiting the 8th German Democratic Republic Agricultural Exhibition.

The information on China's water conservancy construction exhibited in the Chinese exhibition hall aroused the interest of the distinguished guests. Ulbricht said that the exhibition shows the great people's initiatives carried out in China under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party. He wrote in the guestbook, "Sincere thanks for this interesting exhibition. We congratulate the glorious Chinese people on further achievements in building socialism."

Accompanying Ulbricht today were Grünenberg, alternate member of the Politburo of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Secretariat, Deputy Prime Minister Scholz, and others.

### 德国第八届农展会开幕  德国党政领导人参观中国馆  海因里希·劳说中国馆展示了人民公社大跃进成就

1960-06-06
第6版()
专栏：

    德国第八届农展会开幕
    德国党政领导人参观中国馆
    海因里希·劳说中国馆展示了人民公社大跃进成就
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：德意志民主共和国第八届农业展览会3日在来比锡开幕。参加开幕式的有德国统一社会党中央政治局委员、副总理海因里希·劳，中央政治局候补委员格吕纳贝格等德意志民主共和国党政领导人、各社会主义国家政府代表团以及各国驻德意志民主共和国的外交使节。
    这次展览会展出的主要内容是有关巩固农业生产合作社、发展畜牧业和农业等方面的成就。统一社会党和政府把这次展览会当作一次推广先进经验、进一步巩固和发展合作化运动的学校。全国各地准备组织合作社社员、农业工人和与农业有关的工业部门的工人到展览会参观，学习和研究先进经验。
    这次展览会面积达一百三十二公顷，历时四十多天。展览会中还设有苏联、中国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、蒙古等社会主义国家展览馆。中国和蒙古都是第一次参加德意志民主共和国农业展览会。中国馆展品的主要内容有人民公社、大跃进和农业“八字宪法”等三个部分，这是我国农业第一次在欧洲展出。
    海因里希·劳等党政领导人作为第一批贵宾，参观了德意志民主共和国的一些主要展览馆和苏联、中国等展览馆。海因里希·劳参观了中国馆后，在留言簿上写道：“中国展览馆显示了人民公社里的大跃进！祝进一步取得更多的成就！”
    德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希4日在参观德意志民主共和国第八届农业展览会时，参观了中国展览馆。
    在中国展览馆展出的有关中国水利建设的情况，引起贵宾的兴趣。乌布利希说，展览表明了在中国共产党领导下在中国进行的人民伟大创举。他在留言簿上写道，“衷心感谢这个有兴趣的展览会，我们祝贺光荣的中国人民在建设社会主义中进一步取得成就。”
    今天陪同乌布利希参观的，有统一社会党中央政治局候补委员、中央书记处书记格吕纳贝格、副总理绍尔茨等。
