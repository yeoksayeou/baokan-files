Title: US Army, Navy and Air Force Intensify War Preparations
Author:
Date: 1960-06-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch from Washington: The US ruling clique is carrying out various deployments and intensifying preparations for war.

    The US Army announced yesterday that a special combat team composed of two thousand paratroopers equipped with modern combat equipment will depart today aboard the transport ship "General Breckinridge" for Okinawa to reinforce US forces there. It was revealed that this unit has received special training and is being sent to Okinawa in preparation for "small wars" in the Western Pacific region.

    The US Air Force Department also issued a statement yesterday announcing that its Strategic Air Command will soon begin implementing a "regular dispersal training program" to enable the personnel of its B-47 bombers, which carry nuclear weapons, to "familiarize themselves with operations from civil airports or other US military bases." It also announced that, "when needed," the US Strategic Air Command's B-52 heavy bombers will also acquire "airborne alert capability."

    The day before this, the Air Force also launched a "Quail" missile at Eglin Air Force Base in Florida to test the performance of the "Bomarc" missile.

    The Navy's war preparations are not lagging behind. On the same day that the Army announced the dispatch of troops to Okinawa and the Air Force announced "dispersal training," the US State Department revealed that the United States and its eight NATO "allies" have completed the establishment of a "research center" in preparation for submarine warfare. It was announced that this institution will be located in La Spezia, Italy, and will be attended by the United States, the United Kingdom, West Germany, France, Canada, Italy, the Netherlands, Denmark, and Norway.

    At the same time, the US Senate Appropriations Subcommittee also passed a resolution on the 8th, requesting that military appropriations for the 1960-61 fiscal year be increased to $40 billion, which is more than $660 million more than the military expenditure in the original budget.

### 美国海陆空三军加紧备战活动  陆军：两千名伞兵特种部队开往冲绳准备“小型战争”  空军：宣布将开始执行使轰炸机加强活动的训练计划  海军：准备同北大西洋集团各国加紧研究进行潜艇战

1960-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    美国海陆空三军加紧备战活动
    陆军：两千名伞兵特种部队开往冲绳准备“小型战争”
    空军：宣布将开始执行使轰炸机加强活动的训练计划
    海军：准备同北大西洋集团各国加紧研究进行潜艇战
    新华社10日讯　华盛顿消息：美国统治集团正在进行种种部署，加紧准备战争。
    美国陆军方面昨天宣布，由两千名有现代战斗装备的伞兵组成的一个特种战斗队将在今天乘运输舰“布列金里治——将军”号去冲绳，以便增强在那里的美国兵力。据透露，这支部队曾经受过专门训练，是准备在西太平洋地区进行“小型战争”而派到冲绳去的。
    美国空军部昨天也发表声明，宣布它的战略空军司令部即将开始执行一个“定期的疏散训练计划”，以便使它的携带核武器的B—47型轰炸机的机上人员“熟悉从民航机场或美国其他军事基地进行活动的情况”。它还宣布，在“需要的时候”，将使美国战略空军司令部的B—52型重型轰炸机也获有“空中戒备能力”。
    在这前一天，空军方面还在佛罗里达州德格林基地发射了一个“鹌鹑”式导弹，以便试验“鲍马克”式导弹的性能。
    海军方面的备战活动也不落后。就在陆军宣布派兵去冲绳和空军宣布进行“疏散训练”的同一天，美国国务院透露，美国和它的北大西洋集团的八个“盟国”已经完成了关于成立准备进行潜艇战的“研究中心”。据宣布，这个机构将设在意大利的斯培西亚，参加的除美国外，还有英国、西德、法国、加拿大、意大利、荷兰、丹麦和挪威。
    与此同时，美国参议院拨款小组委员会也在8日通过决议，要求把1960—61财政年度的军事拨款增加到四百亿美元，比原预算案中的军费开支还多六亿六千多万美元。
