Title: Exchange of Instruments of Ratification of the Sino-German Treaty of Commerce and Navigation
Author:
Date: 1960-06-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 18th: The instruments of ratification of the Treaty of Commerce and Navigation between the People's Republic and the German Democratic Republic were exchanged in Berlin today.

Representing the two sides in the exchange of instruments of ratification were Wang Guoquan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic, and Lothar Bolz, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic.

This treaty was signed in Beijing on January 18 this year. The treaty comes into effect upon the exchange of instruments of ratification.

### 中德通商航海条约互换批准书

1960-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

    中德通商航海条约互换批准书
    新华社柏林18日电　中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国通商航海条约今天在柏林互换批准书。
    代表双方交换批准书的是：中国驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使王国权和德意志民主共和国外交部长博尔茨。
    这一条约是在今年1月18日在北京签订的。交换批准书后，条约即生效。
