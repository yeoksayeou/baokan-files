Title: Hungarian Light Industry Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 17th. The Hungarian Light Industry Delegation of twelve people, headed by Mrs. Nagy József, Minister of Light Industry of the Hungarian People's Republic, concluded its visit to our country and left Beijing by plane this morning for home. Jiang Guangnai, Minister of Textile Industry, Li Zhongyi, Vice Minister, Deng Jie and Wang Xinyuan, Vice Ministers of Light Industry, saw them off at the airport.

The delegation visited our country on May 22nd according to the decision of the Fifth Session of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee. They visited textile and light industry technology in Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuxi, Tianjin and other places.

### 匈牙利轻工业代表团离京回国

1960-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利轻工业代表团离京回国
    新华社17日讯　以匈牙利人民共和国轻工业部部长纳吉·约瑟夫夫人为首的匈牙利轻工业代表团一行十二人，结束了在我国的访问，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。纺织工业部部长蒋光鼐、副部长李中一，轻工业部副部长邓洁、王新元到机场欢送。
    代表团是根据中匈科学技术合作委员会第五届会议的决议，在5月22日到我国访问的。他们曾先后在北京、上海、杭州、无锡、天津等地考察了我国的纺织和轻工业技术。
