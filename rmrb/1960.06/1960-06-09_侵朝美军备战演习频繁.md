Title: U.S. Troops Invading Korea Frequently Hold War Preparations Exercises
Author:
Date: 1960-06-09
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Seoul dispatch: The U.S. military command invading Korea announced on the 4th that the U.S. military invading Korea would begin a series of new provocative so-called "training exercises" in South Korea from that day on, and ordered all troops in South Korea to be in a "state of alert."

    The U.S. military command invading Korea blatantly clamored that this was a measure to prepare for war, saying that the series of "exercises" were conducted to "maintain constant readiness."

### 侵朝美军备战演习频繁

1960-06-09
第7版()
专栏：

    侵朝美军备战演习频繁
    据新华社讯　汉城消息：侵朝美军司令部4日宣布，侵朝美军从这一天开始在南朝鲜举行一系列新的挑衅性的所谓“训练演习”，并且命令在南朝鲜的所有部队处于“警戒状态”。
    侵朝美军司令部公然叫嚣这是一种准备战争的措施，它说进行这一系列“演习”是为了“保持经常不断的准备状态”。
