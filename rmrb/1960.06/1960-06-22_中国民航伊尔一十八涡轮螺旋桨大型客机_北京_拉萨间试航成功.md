Title: Chinese Civil Aviation's Ilyushin Il-18 Turboprop Airliner Successfully Completes Trial Flight Between Beijing and Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1960-06-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, June 21st. Chinese Civil Aviation's Ilyushin Il-18 turboprop airliner has successfully completed a trial flight on the Beijing-Lhasa route.

This Il-18 airliner flew from Beijing to Lhasa on the morning of the 16th. When the plane landed at the airport near Lhasa, it was warmly welcomed by the people of Tibet.

On the afternoon of the 17th, more than 1,000 people from all walks of life in Lhasa held a gathering in Norbulingka to celebrate the successful trial flight of the aircraft.

Panchen Erdeni, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, spoke at the meeting. He said: The successful trial flight of the Il-18 airliner between Beijing and Lhasa further strengthens the connection between the Tibet region and the motherland's capital, as well as various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions, and further strengthens the unity between fraternal ethnic groups. It demonstrates the Party and Chairman Mao's infinite concern for the people of Tibet, as well as the strong support of the people of all fraternal ethnic groups in the motherland for Tibet's construction.

The Tibet Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and the Tibet Military Region presented silk banners to all the personnel who participated in the trial flight. The banner from the Working Committee read: "Chairman Mao's eagles bring happiness to the people of Tibet."

The trial flight personnel presented trial flight commemorative badges and a Chinese Civil Aviation Decade Picture Album to Panchen Erdeni and Zhang Guohua and Tan Guansan, Deputy Secretaries of the Tibet Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. Panchen Erdeni, Zhang Guohua, Tan Guansan, and others accompanied all the trial flight personnel to watch Tibetan folk dances performed by Lhasa art troupes in the flower-filled Norbulingka.

### 中国民航伊尔一十八涡轮螺旋桨大型客机  北京—拉萨间试航成功

1960-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中国民航伊尔一十八涡轮螺旋桨大型客机
    北京—拉萨间试航成功
    据新华社拉萨21日电　中国民航伊尔—十八涡轮螺旋桨大型客机，已在北京—拉萨航线试航成功。
    这架伊尔—十八大型客机，是在16日上午从北京飞到拉萨的。当飞机降落到拉萨附近机场时，受到了西藏人民的热烈欢迎。
    17日下午，拉萨市各界人民一千多人在罗布林卡举行集会，庆祝这架飞机试航成功。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼在会上讲了话。他说：伊尔—十八大型客机在北京和拉萨间试航成功，进一步密切了西藏地区和祖国首都以及各省市、自治区之间的联系，进一步加强了各兄弟民族之间的团结。它表明党和毛主席对西藏人民的无限关怀，以及祖国各兄弟民族人民对西藏建设事业的大力支援。
    中共西藏工委、西藏自治区筹备委员会和西藏军区向参加试航的全体人员赠送了锦旗。工委的锦旗上写着“毛主席的雄鹰给西藏人民带来了幸福”。
    试航人员向班禅额尔德尼和中共西藏工委副书记张国华、谭冠三等赠送了试航纪念章和中国民航十年画册。班禅额尔德尼、张国华和谭冠三等陪同全体试航人员，在百花盛开的罗布林卡观看了拉萨文艺团体表演的西藏民间舞蹈。
