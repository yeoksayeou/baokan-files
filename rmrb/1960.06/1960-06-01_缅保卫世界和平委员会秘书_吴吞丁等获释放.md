Title: Myanmar Peace Committee Secretary U Tun Tin and Others Released
Author:
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Yangon: According to a report in the "Guardian" on the 29th, the Myanmar authorities released three people on the 28th: U Tun Tin, Secretary of the Myanmar Peace Committee; U Hla Done, former member of parliament of the National United Front of Shwebo County; and Tekin Mya Maung, former editor of the "People's Monthly".

### 缅保卫世界和平委员会秘书  吴吞丁等获释放

1960-06-01
第6版()
专栏：

    缅保卫世界和平委员会秘书
    吴吞丁等获释放
    据新华社仰光电　据29日的“卫报”报道，缅甸当局在28日释放了缅甸保卫世界和平委员会秘书吴吞丁、竖磅县的前缅甸民族团结阵线议员吴拉东，前“人民月刊”编辑德钦妙丹等三人。
