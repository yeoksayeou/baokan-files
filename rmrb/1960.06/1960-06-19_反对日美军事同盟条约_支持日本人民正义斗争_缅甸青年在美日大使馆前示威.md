Title: Oppose the Japan-U.S. Military Alliance Treaty; Support the Just Struggle of the Japanese People Burmese Youth Demonstrate in Front of the U.S. and Japanese Embassies
Author:
Date: 1960-06-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 16th - About fifty members of the Burmese Youth Vanguard held a demonstration in front of the U.S. Embassy and the Japanese Embassy in Rangoon this afternoon, expressing strong opposition to the ratification of the Japan-U.S. Military Alliance Treaty.

They shouted slogans such as "The Japan-U.S. Military Alliance Treaty must be abolished!", "No bullying the Japanese people!", "Support the just struggle of the Japanese people!".

### 反对日美军事同盟条约　支持日本人民正义斗争  缅甸青年在美日大使馆前示威

1960-06-19
第5版()
专栏：

    反对日美军事同盟条约　支持日本人民正义斗争
    缅甸青年在美日大使馆前示威
    据新华社仰光16日电　缅甸青年先锋的成员五十人左右今天下午在仰光的美国大使馆和日本大使馆前面举行示威，表示强烈反对批准日美军事同盟条约。
    他们高呼“日美军事同盟条约应当废除！”、“不许欺侮日本人民！”、“支持日本人民的正义斗争！”等口号。
