Title: Two Polish Playwrights Leave Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-06-12
Page: 6

    Two Polish Playwrights Leave Beijing for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, June 10th According to the 1960-1961 execution plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, two Polish playwrights, Jasinski and Jurandot, who came to China for a visit, left Beijing by plane today to return to their country.

### 波兰两戏剧家离京回国

1960-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰两戏剧家离京回国
    新华社10日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1960—1961年执行计划来我国访问的波兰两位戏剧家雅辛斯基和尤兰道特，已经在今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
