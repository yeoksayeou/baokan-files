Title: Chairman Lezh visits Mongolia, Passing Through Beijing, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu and others welcome him at the airport

Author:

Date: 1960-06-29
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 28th - Albanian People's Assembly Presidium Chairman Haxhi Lleshi and other Albanian distinguished guests arrived in Ulaanbaatar by special plane this afternoon. They are invited to pay a three-day friendly visit here.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, as well as other leaders of the Party and the state, ministers of government departments, and heads of mass organizations. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia also welcomed them at the airport.

Lleshi, accompanied by Sambuu and Tsedenbal, reviewed the guard of honor. Sambuu and Lleshi spoke at the airport successively.

On behalf of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia and the Mongolian people, Sambuu extended a warm welcome to the Albanian distinguished guests. He said that the brilliant achievements made by the Albanian people in socialist construction are inspiring the Mongolian people who are building socialism. He expressed his conviction that the visit of Lleshi and other distinguished guests to Mongolia would make new and great contributions to the development and consolidation of the fraternal friendship and comprehensive cooperation between the two peoples.

In his speech, Lleshi expressed his gratitude for the warm invitation from the Mongolian people. He said that the principles of Marxism-Leninism and the friendly cooperation of the people of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union unite us. We believe that through this visit, we will further understand the life of the Mongolian people and further enhance the fraternal friendship and solidarity and cooperation between the Albanian and Mongolian peoples.

When Lleshi, accompanied by Sambuu, Tsedenbal, and others, entered the urban area of Ulaanbaatar, he was warmly welcomed by the citizens lining the streets.

That afternoon, Lleshi paid a visit to Sambuu and Tsedenbal. Subsequently, Sambuu hosted a banquet in honor of Lleshi and other Albanian distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, 28th - Albanian People's Assembly Presidium Chairman Haxhi Lleshi and members of the Albanian People's Assembly Presidium, member and secretary of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, Liri Belishova and other distinguished guests, arrived in Beijing by special plane at noon today while en route from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Mongolian People's Republic for a visit.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman Saifudin, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Albania Luo Shigao, Vice Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping, President of the China-Albania Friendship Association Jiang Nanxiang, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen, and others.

Albanian Ambassador to China Parifti was at the airport to greet them. Also at the airport to welcome them were the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China Li Yongho, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China Sharab, and others.

Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman Saifudin, and others had lunch with Chairman Lleshi, Comrade Belishova, and others at the airport.

At 1:50 p.m., Chairman Lleshi and other distinguished guests left Beijing by special plane for a friendly visit to the Mongolian People's Republic.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 28th - At the invitation of Choe Yong-gon, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, and his entourage, who came to visit Korea, left Pyongyang by special plane this morning after a week-long visit to Korea, via China, to visit the Mongolian People's Republic.

Choe Yong-gon, Park Chung-ae, Jung Il-yong, and other leaders of the Korean Party and government, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea, were at the airport to see them off.

In his farewell speech at the airport, Chairman Lleshi expressed his gratitude for the warm hospitality of the Korean people. He said that he had a very good impression during his visit to Korea, and that the Korean people are not only a heroic people in wartime, but also a heroic people in construction.

During his visit to Korea, Chairman Lleshi visited factories, agricultural cooperatives, schools, and irrigation projects in Pyongyang, Hamhung, Wonsan, and other places, and was warmly welcomed by the Korean people everywhere. In addition to the rally held in Pyongyang with the participation of more than 200,000 people to welcome Chairman Lleshi, mass rallies of 50,000 and 30,000 people were also held in Hamhung and Wonsan respectively to warmly welcome Chairman Lleshi.

### 列希主席到蒙古访问  途中路经北京，董必武副主席等到机场迎送

1960-06-29
第7版()
专栏：

    列希主席到蒙古访问
    途中路经北京，董必武副主席等到机场迎送
    新华社乌兰巴托28日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希和其他阿尔巴尼亚贵宾，今天下午乘专机到达乌兰巴托。他们应邀将在这里作三天的友好访问。
    到飞机场欢迎的有蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔，以及党和国家的其他领导人，政府各部部长和群众团体的负责人等。各国驻蒙古的外交使节也到机场欢迎。
    列希在桑布、泽登巴尔陪同下，检阅了仪仗队。桑布和列希先后在机场上讲了话。
    桑布代表蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团和蒙古人民向阿尔巴尼亚贵宾表示热烈欢迎。他说，阿尔巴尼亚人民在社会主义建设中取得的辉煌成就鼓舞着建设社会主义的蒙古人民。他表示确信，列希及其他贵宾在蒙古的访问，对发展和巩固两国人民的兄弟友谊和全面合作，必将作出新的巨大的贡献。
    列希在讲话中对蒙古人民的盛情邀请表示感谢。他说，马克思列宁主义的原则以及以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国人民的友好合作把我们联结在一起，我们相信通过这次访问将进一步了解蒙古人民的生活，进一步增进阿蒙两国人民的兄弟友谊和团结合作。
    列希由桑布、泽登巴尔等陪同进入乌兰巴托市区时，沿途受到市民的夹道欢迎。
    当天下午，列希拜会了桑布和泽登巴尔。随后，桑布设宴招待列希和阿尔巴尼亚其他贵宾。
    新华社28日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希和阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团委员、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、书记处书记利丽·贝利绍娃等贵宾，在由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国前往蒙古人民共和国访问的途中，今天中午乘专机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有董必武副主席，陈毅、习仲勋副总理，赛福鼎副委员长，外交部副部长曾涌泉，我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高，北京市副市长冯基平，中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会会长蒋南翔，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文等。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使帕里夫蒂到机场迎接。到机场欢迎的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐，蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使沙拉布等。
    董必武副主席，陈毅、习仲勋副总理，赛福鼎副委员长等在机场同列希主席、贝利绍娃等同志共进午餐。
    下午一时五十分，列希主席和其它贵宾乘专机离开北京，前往蒙古人民共和国进行友好访问。
    新华社平壤28日电　应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健的邀请前来访问的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希等一行，在朝鲜进行了一周的访问以后，今天上午乘专机离开平壤，经中国前往蒙古人民共和国进行访问。
    到机场欢送的有崔庸健、朴正爱、郑一龙等朝鲜的党和政府的领导人以及各国驻朝鲜外交使节等。
    列希主席在机场上致告别辞时说，他对朝鲜人民的热情款待表示感谢。他说，在朝鲜访问期间得到了很好的印象，朝鲜人民不仅在战争时期是英雄的人民，在建设时期也是英雄的人民。
    列希主席在朝鲜访问期间，先后在平壤、咸兴、元山等地参观了各地的工厂、农业社、学校和灌溉工程等，到处受到了朝鲜人民的热烈欢迎。除平壤市举行了有二十多万群众参加的大会欢迎列希主席以外，在咸兴和元山也分别举行了五万人和三万人的群众大会，热烈欢迎列希主席。
