Title: UAR Foreign Minister Summons U.S. Ambassador to Protest U.S. Senator's Interference in Internal Affairs; "Al-Ahram" Says Senator Keating's Slander of Soviet Embassy in UAR as a "Spy Center" is an Excuse for U.S. Espionage Activities
Author:
Date: 1960-06-07
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd, TASS, Cairo: The Middle East News Agency reports that the Foreign Minister of the United Arab Republic, Fawzi, summoned the U.S. Ambassador to the UAR, Reinhardt, on June 2nd and demanded that he explain the slanderous remarks made by U.S. Senator Keating against the Soviet Embassy in Cairo.

Fawzi believes that the U.S. Senator's remarks are an interference in the UAR's internal affairs, which the UAR cannot agree to under any circumstances.

Reportedly, Keating slandered the Soviet Embassy in Cairo during a U.S. Senate debate on U.S. foreign aid as one of the most important "centers of espionage and subversion" on the African continent.

A political commentator for "Al-Ahram" wrote in an article that Senator Keating should follow the example of some other senators and study the reasons for the failure of a series of U.S. policies, abandon the attempt to justify U.S. espionage activities and U-2 spy plane activities by slandering the Soviet Embassy, and abandon the attempt to drag Cairo into the Cold War between East and West.

The article hopes that U.S. congressmen will pay attention to the following questions: Where are the fatal weaknesses of U.S. policy? Why does this policy fail one after another? Why do U.S. friends collapse one after another? Why have those who pandered to the United States in concluding the Baghdad Pact, such as Said, Menderes, and Bayar, collapsed one after another? Why have Syngman Rhee and Chiang Kai-shek in the Far East failed? Why did they suffer such a fate despite U.S. spiritual and material support?

### 阿联外长召见美国大使  反对美参议员干涉内政  美参议员库契尔诽谤苏联驻阿联大使馆是“间谍中心”，“金字塔报”说这是为美国间谍活动辩解

1960-06-07
第7版()
专栏：

    阿联外长召见美国大使
    反对美参议员干涉内政
    美参议员库契尔诽谤苏联驻阿联大使馆是“间谍中心”，“金字塔报”说这是为美国间谍活动辩解
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社开罗讯：中东通讯社报道，阿拉伯联合共和国外交部长法齐2日召见美国驻阿联大使莱因哈特，并要求他解释美国参议员库契尔对苏联驻开罗大使馆的诽谤言论。
    法齐认为美国参议员的言论是对阿联内政的干涉，阿联无论如何不能同意这一点。
    据报道，库契尔在美国参议院一次关于美国对外援助的辩论中曾诬蔑苏联驻开罗大使馆是所谓对非洲大陆的最重要的“间谍和颠覆活动中心之一”。
    “金字塔报”的政治评论员在一篇文章中写道，库契尔参议员应当追随某些其他议员之后，去研究研究美国一系列政策失败的原因，放弃那种以诬蔑苏联大使馆的手法来为美国间谍活动和U—2型间谍飞机的活动进行辩解的企图，放弃把开罗拖进东西方冷战的企图。
    文章希望美国议员注意这样一些问题：美国政策的致命弱点何在？为什么这种政策一个接着一个地失败？为什么美国的朋友一个跟着一个垮台？为什么那些在缔结巴格达条约方面趋奉美国的人，如赛义德、曼德列斯和拜亚尔等人一个接着一个地垮台了？为什么远东的李承晚和蒋介石遭到了失败？为什么尽管美国以精神力量和物质力量支持他们，而他们还是遭到这样的命运？
