Title: U.S. Nuclear Submarine Explodes
Author:
Date: 1960-06-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Honolulu, June 15th: The U.S. nuclear submarine "Thresher" caught fire and then experienced a powerful explosion on the evening of the 14th while docked at Pearl Harbor in the Hawaiian Islands. Because the fire was too intense to extinguish, the submarine had to be scuttled. The U.S. Navy announced that the "Thresher" has been severely damaged.

### 美国一原子潜艇爆炸

1960-06-17
第6版()
专栏：

    美国一原子潜艇爆炸
    据新华社15日讯　檀香山消息：停泊在夏威夷群岛珍珠港的美国原子潜艇“棘鬣鱼”号14日晚上失火，接着发生了强烈爆炸。由于火势很大，无法扑灭，只得使这艘潜水艇下沉。美国海军方面宣布，“棘鬣鱼”号已经大大损毁。
