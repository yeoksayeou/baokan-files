Title: Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union Secretaries, etc., Send Telegram to Liu Ningyi to Congratulate Our Mountaineering Team on Reaching the Highest Peak
Author:
Date: 1960-06-05
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Secretary of the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union, Shevchenko, and the Chairman of the Soviet Trade Union Sports Association, Zubkov, sent a telegram to Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, at the end of May, congratulating the Chinese mountaineering team on conquering Mount Everest.

Shevchenko said in his telegram, "Congratulations to your mountaineers Wang Fuzhou, Qu Yinhua, and Gonbu for brilliantly ascending the world's highest peak from the north slope. Please accept our warm congratulations. We wish them good health and new achievements in labor and sports."

Zubkov said in his telegram, "The athletes and mountaineers of the Soviet Trade Union Sports Association warmly congratulate our Chinese friends on their brilliant victory in conquering the world's highest peak—Mount Everest."

### 全苏工会中央理事会书记等致电刘宁一  祝贺我登山队登上最高峰

1960-06-05
第7版()
专栏：

    全苏工会中央理事会书记等致电刘宁一
    祝贺我登山队登上最高峰
    新华社4日讯　苏联工会中央理事会书记谢夫钦科和苏联工会体育协会理事会主席祖布科夫，5月底打电报给中华全国总工会主席刘宁一，祝贺中国登山队征服珠穆朗玛峰的成就。
    谢夫钦科在电报中说，祝贺你们的登山运动员王富洲、屈银华、贡布出色地从北路登上世界最高峰，谨向他们致以热烈的祝贺。祝贺他们身体健康，在劳动和体育中取得新的成就。
    祖布科夫在电报中说，苏联工会体育协会的运动员和登山运动员，热烈祝贺我们的中国朋友以辉煌的胜利征服了世界最高峰——珠穆朗玛峰。
