Title: German Education Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 17th: The German Democratic Republic education delegation of six people, headed by Honecker, Deputy Minister of National Education of the German Democratic Republic, left Beijing by plane today to return home after a month-long visit to our country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Liu Aifeng and Dong Chuncai, Vice Ministers of Education, and Zhu Kezhen, President of the Sino-German Friendship Association.

Wang Daer, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 德教育代表团离京回国

1960-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    德教育代表团离京回国
    新华社17日讯　以德意志民主共和国国民教育部副部长霍内克为首的德意志民主共和国教育代表团一行六人，在我国考察一月后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有教育部副部长刘皑风，董纯才，中德友好协会会长竺可桢等。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔也到机场送行。
