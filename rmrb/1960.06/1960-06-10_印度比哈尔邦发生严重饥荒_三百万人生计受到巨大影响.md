Title: Serious Famine in Bihar, India; Three Million People's Livelihoods Greatly Affected
Author:
Date: 1960-06-10
Page: 7

    Serious Famine in Bihar, India
    Three Million People's Livelihoods Greatly Affected
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 8th - According to the Press Trust of India, Congress Party member of the Bihar Legislative Assembly, Oistra, said in Patna on the 7th that "terrible scenes of famine" involving three million people have already appeared in certain counties of Bihar.
    He said that during his visit to the disaster area, he encountered hundreds of emaciated and ragged starving people.
    According to the "Times of India," Indian Minister of Food and Agriculture Patil revealed at a press conference in New Delhi on the 7th that India's grain production in 1959-1960 may decrease by one to two million tons compared to 1958-1959.

### 印度比哈尔邦发生严重饥荒  三百万人生计受到巨大影响

1960-06-10
第7版()
专栏：

    印度比哈尔邦发生严重饥荒
    三百万人生计受到巨大影响
    新华社新德里8日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道，印度比哈尔邦立法会议国大党议员奥伊斯特拉7日在巴特那说，在比哈尔邦的某些县已经出现了三百万人“闹饥荒的可怕情景”。
    他说，在他访问灾区期间，他遇到了数以百计的骨瘦如柴、衣衫褴褛的饥民。
    据“印度时报”报道，印度粮食和农业部长帕蒂尔7日在新德里记者招待会上透露，印度1959—1960年的粮食产量可能比1958—1959年下降一百万到二百万吨。
