Title: The Party's Sunshine
Author: Kou Lixiang
Date: 1960-06-05
Page: 8

    The Party's Sunshine
    (Bai Nationality) Kou Lixiang
    The Party's sunshine,
    The Party's brilliant sunshine,
    Shines all over the motherland,
    From Tiananmen Square,
    To our borders.
    The hearts of the people of all ethnic groups on the border,
    Forever run towards the sun,
    The hearts of the people of all ethnic groups on the border,
    Are forever illuminated by the sun.
    One layer higher than the other
    Poems can't write enough,
    Songs can't sing enough,
    Our great motherland,
    Is everywhere like flowers and brocade.
    Five-Year Plan,
    Completed in two years,
    The socialist building,
    One layer higher than the other.

### 党的阳光
寇丽祥
1960-06-05
第8版()
专栏：

    党的阳光
    （白族）寇丽祥
    党的阳光
    党的灿烂的阳光，
    照遍祖国的地方，
    从天安门广场上，
    照到我们的边疆。
    边疆各族人民的心，
    永远奔向太阳，
    边疆各族人民的心，
    永远给太阳照亮。
    一层高一层
    诗写不尽，
    歌唱不赢，
    我们伟大的祖国，
    处处如花似锦。
    五年计划，
    两年完成，
    社会主义的高楼，
    一层高一层。
