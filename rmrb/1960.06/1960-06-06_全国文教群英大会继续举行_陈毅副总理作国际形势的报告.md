Title: National Congress of Outstanding Workers in Culture and Education Continues; Vice Premier Chen Yi Delivers Report on the International Situation
Author:
Date: 1960-06-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 5th June. The National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education continued today.

    At the morning session, Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, delivered a report on the current international situation.

    In the afternoon, Xu Bing, Deputy Head of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, spoke on the issue of the transformation of intellectuals.

    Two representatives spoke at today's meeting: Zhang Zigao, professor at Tsinghua University, and Gu Chaohao, representative of the Department of Mathematics at Fudan University.

    The congress will continue tomorrow.

### 全国文教群英大会继续举行  陈毅副总理作国际形势的报告

1960-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    全国文教群英大会继续举行
    陈毅副总理作国际形势的报告
    新华社5日讯　全国文教先进工作者代表大会今天继续举行。
    在上午的大会上，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅就目前国际形势问题作了报告。
    下午，中共中央统战部副部长徐冰在会上就知识分子的改造问题讲了话。
    有两位代表在今天的会上发言。他们是：清华大学教授张子高，复旦大学数学系代表谷超豪。
    明天，大会将继续举行。
