Title: United Arab Republic Issues Decree, Pardons Personnel of Former Dynasty
Author:
Date: 1960-06-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 12th: A decree was promulgated here, announcing the pardon of certain public officials of former Egyptian King Farouk who were convicted in the early days of the Egyptian Revolution, and permitting them to reassume positions in the government.

### 阿联颁布法令  赦免前王朝的人员

1960-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联颁布法令
    赦免前王朝的人员
    据新华社开罗12日电　这里颁布了一项法令，宣布赦免埃及革命初期判罪的前埃及国王法鲁克的某些公职人员的罪行，并准许他们在政府中重新担任职务。
