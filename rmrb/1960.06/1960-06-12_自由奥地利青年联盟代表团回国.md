Title: Free Austrian Youth League Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-06-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 10th — The five-member delegation of the Free Austrian Youth League, invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China to visit our country, has concluded its visit and left Beijing by plane today to return home.

### 自由奥地利青年联盟代表团回国

1960-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    自由奥地利青年联盟代表团回国
    新华社10日讯　应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会邀请前来我国访问的自由奥地利青年联盟代表团一行五人，结束了在我国的访问，已经在今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
