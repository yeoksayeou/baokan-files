Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Waters off Guangdong Province; 114th Serious Warning Issued
Author:
Date: 1960-06-29
Page: 1
	
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th. On June 28th, from 08:50 to 13:52, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area south of Honghai Bay, Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 114th serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我广东省海域  我提出第114次严重警告

1960-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我广东省海域
    我提出第114次严重警告
    新华社28日讯　6月28日八时五十分至十三时五十二分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省红海湾以南海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百一十四次严重警告。
