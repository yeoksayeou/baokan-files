Title: Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Staff Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Shanghai and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1960-06-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th: The thirteen-member Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party staff delegation, led by Sándor József, member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, returned to Beijing by plane today from Shanghai after visiting and touring Zhengzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai, and other places.

　　The delegation arrived in Zhengzhou from Anshan on the 23rd. They visited the Red Flag People's Commune, the Eastern Suburb People's Commune, and the Zhengzhou No. 1 State-owned Cotton Factory in Zhengzhou. On the evening of the 24th, Li Li, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, held a discussion with all members of the delegation. On the 25th, the delegation flew from Zhengzhou to Hangzhou, where they toured the scenic spots and historical sites of West Lake. On the 26th, the delegation arrived in Shanghai from Hangzhou. They visited the Shanghai Revolutionary History Memorial Hall, the Shanghai Machine Tool Factory, the Jianshe Machinery Factory, the Huguang Scientific Instrument Factory, Fudan University, and a street in Zhangmiao.

### 匈牙利社会主义工人党工作人员代表团  在上海等地参观后回到北京

1960-06-30
第6版()
专栏：

    匈牙利社会主义工人党工作人员代表团
    在上海等地参观后回到北京
    新华社29日讯　由匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员山多尔·约瑟夫率领的匈牙利社会主义工人党工作人员代表团一行十三人，在郑州、杭州、上海等地参观访问后，今天从上海乘飞机回到北京。
    代表团是23日由鞍山到达郑州的。他们在郑州参观了红旗人民公社、东郊人民公社、郑州国棉一厂。24日晚间，中共河南省委书记处书记李立和代表团的全体人员举行了座谈。25日，代表团乘飞机从郑州到达杭州，在杭州游览了西湖名胜古迹。26日，代表团从杭州到达上海。他们在上海参观访问了上海革命历史纪念馆、上海机床厂、建设机器厂、沪光科学仪器厂、复旦大学和张庙的一条街。
