Title: In Memory of Comrade Lin Boqu
Author: Xie Juezai
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 2

Lin Lao:

　　You toiled for the revolution your whole life.

　　You endured the long and arduous years of the revolution,

　　You experienced the tortuous and heroic path of the revolution.

　　When the reactionary tide surged against the revolution, you were a staunch fighter;

　　When opportunism eroded our Party, you were a model for defending the Party.

　　Your shouts echoed in the sound of war spreading across the land,

　　Your image was present in the sea of red flags.

　　Your achievements are forever written in the history of the people,

　　Your spirit will forever live in the hearts of the people.

　　A wise man has passed away, the wind howls and the rain weeps;

　　A model is immortal, the mountains are high and the waters are clear.

　　Lin Lao: Rest in peace!

### 悼林伯渠同志
谢觉哉
1960-06-02
第2版()
专栏：

    悼林伯渠同志
    谢觉哉
    林老：
    你为革命劳瘁了一生。
    你忍受了漫长的革命艰苦岁月，
    你经历了迂回的革命英勇路程。
    当反动的浪潮冲激革命时，你是一个坚强的战士；
    当机会主义侵蚀我党时，你又是一个保卫党的标兵。
    战火燎原的声中有你的呼喊，
    红旗如海的影里有你的仪容。
    你的功绩永写在人民的史上，
    你的精神永活在人民的心中。
    哲人云亡，风号雨泣；
    典型不朽，山高水清。
    林老：你安息罢！
