Title: CSAFCA President Xia Yan Arrives in Prague to Attend the Czechoslovak Second National Games

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 29th — Xia Yan, President of the Chinese-Czechoslovak Friendship Association (CSAFCA) and Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Culture, arrived in Prague this morning at the invitation of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Education and Culture and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, to attend the Czechoslovak Second National Games. Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Bušniak and Deputy Minister of Education and Culture Hučko were at the airport to welcome him. Staff from the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia were also at the airport to welcome him.
　　
Xia Yan left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 28th.

### 应邀参加捷第二届全国运动会  中捷友协会长夏衍抵布拉格

1960-06-30
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀参加捷第二届全国运动会
    中捷友协会长夏衍抵布拉格
    新华社布拉格29日电　中捷友好协会会长、中国文化部副部长夏衍应捷克斯洛伐克教育和文化部及外交部的邀请，于今日上午到达布拉格，前来参加捷克斯洛伐克第二届全国运动会。到机场欢迎的有捷克斯洛伐克外交部副部长布希尼亚克和教育和文化部副部长胡奇科。中国驻捷使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    夏衍是在28日上午乘飞机离开北京的。
