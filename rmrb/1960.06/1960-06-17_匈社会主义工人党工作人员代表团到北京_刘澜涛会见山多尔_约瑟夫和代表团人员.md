Title: Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Liu Lantao Meets with Sándor József and Delegation Members
Author:
Date: 1960-06-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party delegation of thirteen members, led by Sándor József, member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included: An Ziwen and Liu Ningyi, members of the Central Committee of the CPC, and Zhang Panshi, a responsible staff member of the central organs of the CPC.

Szigeti Károly, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and József Bencsik, Counselor, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

That evening, Liu Lantao, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, met and hosted a banquet for all members of the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th. Liu Lantao, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, met tonight with the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party delegation headed by Sándor József, member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party.

Present at the meeting were An Ziwen and Liu Ningyi, members of the Central Committee of the CPC.

### 匈社会主义工人党工作人员代表团到北京  刘澜涛会见山多尔·约瑟夫和代表团人员

1960-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    匈社会主义工人党工作人员代表团到北京
    刘澜涛会见山多尔·约瑟夫和代表团人员
    新华社16日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请前来我国访问的匈牙利社会主义工人党工作人员代表团一行十三人，在团长、匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员山多尔·约瑟夫率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中共中央委员安子文、刘宁一和中共中央机关负责工作人员张磐石等人。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂·卡洛伊、参赞白诺克·约瑟夫也到机场迎接。
    当日晚，中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛会见并宴请代表团全体成员。
    新华社16日讯　中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛今晚会见了以匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员山多尔·约瑟夫为首的匈牙利社会主义工人党工作人员代表团。
    会见时在座的有中共中央委员安子文、刘宁一等人。
