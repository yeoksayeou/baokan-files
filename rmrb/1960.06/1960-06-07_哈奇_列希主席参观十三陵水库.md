Title: Haxhi Lleshi Visits the Ming Tombs Reservoir
Author:
Date: 1960-06-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, and other distinguished guests, visited the Ming Tombs Reservoir in the suburbs of Beijing this morning.

After being accompanied to the Ming Tombs Reservoir by Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Luo Shigao, our country's ambassador to Albania, the distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the person in charge and staff of the reservoir management office. The distinguished guests listened to an introduction on the reservoir's construction, strolled on the dam to admire the scenery of the water and mountains, and also viewed the inscriptions engraved on the buildings by our Party and state leaders for the Ming Tombs Reservoir.

Afterwards, the distinguished guests traveled by car to Dingling and visited the underground palace. This afternoon, Chairman Lleshi and Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Political Bureau member Belishova and other distinguished guests visited the Palace Museum and the Cultural Palace of Nationalities respectively. Ambassador Luo Shigao accompanied them on their visits.

### 哈奇·列希主席参观十三陵水库

1960-06-07
第2版()
专栏：

    哈奇·列希主席参观十三陵水库
    新华社6日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希和其他贵宾，今天上午参观了北京近郊的十三陵水库。
    贵宾们由全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、外交部副部长姬鹏飞、我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高陪同到十三陵水库以后，受到水库管理处负责人和工作人员的热情欢迎。贵宾们听取了水库修建情况的介绍，并且在大坝上漫步，欣赏水光山色，还观看了镌刻在建筑物上的我国党和国家领导人为十三陵水库的题字。
    随后，贵宾们乘车到定陵，参观了地下宫殿。今天下午，列希主席和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员贝利绍娃等贵宾，先后分别参观了故宫博物院和民族文化宫。罗士高大使陪同他们参观。
