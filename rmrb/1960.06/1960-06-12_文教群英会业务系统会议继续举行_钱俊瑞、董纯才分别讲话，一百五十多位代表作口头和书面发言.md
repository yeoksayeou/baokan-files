Title: The National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education Continues its Business System Meetings; Qian Junrui and Dong Chuncai Deliver Speeches Respectively; Over One Hundred and Fifty Representatives Make Oral and Written Statements
Author:
Date: 1960-06-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education continued its meetings this morning, divided by different business systems.

At these meetings, a total of seventy-eight representatives spoke, and another seventy-seven representatives made written statements.

At the meetings of the cultural system and the spare-time education system, Vice Minister of Culture Qian Junrui and Vice Minister of Education Dong Chuncai delivered speeches respectively.

At the meetings held yesterday and this morning according to the business systems, a total of two hundred and seventy-four representatives made oral and written statements.

Including the oral statements of fifty representatives and the written statements of thirty-seven representatives at the Congress, during this Congress, a total of two hundred and forty-seven representatives made oral statements, and one hundred and fourteen representatives made written statements at the Congress and at the meetings held separately according to business systems. The meetings of some business systems will continue after the closing of this Congress.

### 文教群英会业务系统会议继续举行  钱俊瑞、董纯才分别讲话，一百五十多位代表作口头和书面发言

1960-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    文教群英会业务系统会议继续举行
    钱俊瑞、董纯才分别讲话，一百五十多位代表作口头和书面发言
    新华社11日讯　全国文教先进工作者代表大会今天上午继续按不同的业务系统分别举行会议。
    在这些会议上，一共有七十八位代表发了言，另外还有七十七位代表作了书面发言。
    在文化系统和业余教育系统的会议上，文化部副部长钱俊瑞和教育部副部长董纯才分别讲了话。
    在昨天和今天上午按业务系统分别举行的会议上，一共有二百七十四位代表作了口头发言和书面发言。
    连同在大会上五十位代表的口头发言和三十七位代表的书面发言在内，在这次大会期间，在大会和按业务系统分别举行的会议上，一共有二百四十七位代表作了口头发言，一百一十四位代表作了书面发言。有些业务系统的会议，在这次大会闭幕以后还要继续举行。
