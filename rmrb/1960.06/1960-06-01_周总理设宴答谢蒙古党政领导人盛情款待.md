Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet to Thank Mongolian Party and Government Leaders for Their Hospitality

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 31st. Premier Zhou Enlai held a banquet at the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia tonight to thank Chairman Tsedenbal and other Mongolian party and government leaders for the hospitality and warm welcome they extended to him and Vice Premier Chen Yi during their visit to the Mongolian People's Republic.

The following were invited to the banquet: Chairman Tsedenbal, Chairman Sambuu, other Mongolian People's Republic party and government leaders Tsende, Molomjamts, Durasuren, Jagvaral, Lhamsuren, Maidar, and heads of government departments.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, other members of Premier Zhou Enlai's delegation, and Xie Fusheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, also attended the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in a very cordial and warm atmosphere, with guests and hosts chatting and laughing. During the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai, Chairman Tsedenbal, Chairman Sambuu, and Vice Premier Chen Yi, among others, raised their glasses and toasted the unbreakable friendship between China and Mongolia, the solidarity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, world peace, and the health of the leaders of both countries.

### 周总理设宴答谢蒙古党政领导人盛情款待

1960-06-01
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理设宴答谢蒙古党政领导人盛情款待
    新华社乌兰巴托31日电　周恩来总理今晚在中国驻蒙古大使馆举行宴会，答谢以泽登巴尔主席为首的蒙古党和政府领导人在他和陈毅副总理访问蒙古人民共和国期间所给予的盛情款待和热烈欢迎。
    应邀出席宴会的有：泽登巴尔主席、桑布主席，蒙古人民共和国的其他党政领导人曾德、莫洛姆扎木茨、杜洛苏伦、扎格瓦拉尔、拉姆苏伦、迈达尔，以及政府各部门的负责人。
  陈毅副总理和周恩来总理的其他随行人员及中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生也出席了宴会。
    宴会进行的非常亲切、热烈，宾主谈笑风生。席间，周恩来总理、泽登巴尔主席、桑布主席和陈毅副总理等，都举杯致词，祝中蒙两国牢不可破的友谊和以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结，祝世界和平，祝两国领导人的健康。
