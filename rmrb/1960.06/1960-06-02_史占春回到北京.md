Title: Shi Zhanchun Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st. Shi Zhanchun, the captain of the Chinese Mountaineering Team, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon from Lhasa. He rushed back to attend the National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education, which has already opened.

    As the leader of the Chinese Mountaineering Team stepped off the plane, the welcoming crowd at the airport immediately rushed forward and presented him with bouquets of flowers.

    Shi Zhanchun appeared very excited, and he cordially shook hands and embraced Huang Zhong, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Li Shubin, Chairman of the Chinese Mountaineering Association, and others. Representatives from organizations such as the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Peking University, and Beijing Institute of Geology, as well as Shi Zhanchun's family members, came to the airport to welcome him today.

### 史占春回到北京

1960-06-02
第2版()
专栏：

    史占春回到北京
    新华社1日讯　中国登山队队长史占春，今天下午乘飞机由拉萨来到北京。他是赶来参加已经开幕的全国文教先进工作者代表大会的。
    当这位中国登山队的领导者走下飞机以后，机场上的欢迎人群立刻拥上前去，把一束束的鲜花献给了他。
    史占春显得十分兴奋，他同国家体委副主任黄中，中国登山协会主席栗树彬等人亲切地一一握手和拥抱。今天，中华全国总工会、北京大学、北京地质学院等单位的代表和史占春的家属都到机场欢迎。
