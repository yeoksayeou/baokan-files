Title: Japanese Communist Party, Socialist Party, and Others Issue Statements on General Strike, Japanese People Determined to Strengthen Unity and Persist in Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-06-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Japanese Communist Party, Socialist Party, General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, and the "National Council to Prevent Revision of the Security Treaty" issued statements on June 4 regarding the general strike.

### 日本共产党、社会党等就总罢工发表声明  日本人民决心加强团结坚持斗争

1960-06-05
第3版()

    日本共产党、社会党等就总罢工发表声明
