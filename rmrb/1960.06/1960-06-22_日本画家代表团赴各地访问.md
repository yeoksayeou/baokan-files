Title: Japanese Painting Delegation Visits Various Places

Author:
Date: 1960-06-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 20th -- The Japanese Painting Delegation, led by Deputy Head Shiraishi Bon, left Beijing by train tonight to visit other places.

Those who saw them off at the station included Lin Lin and Jin Zi Ming, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ye Qianyu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, and Hua Junwu, Secretary-General.

Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a banquet on the 19th to bid farewell to the Japanese Painting Delegation.

### 日本画家代表团赴各地访问

1960-06-22
第5版()
专栏：

    日本画家代表团赴各地访问
    新华社20日讯　日本画家代表团在副团长白石凡率领下今天晚上乘火车离开北京赴外地访问。
    到车站欢送的有中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长林林、金子明，中国美术家协会副主席叶浅予、秘书长华君武等。
    中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙19日举行宴会欢送日本画家代表团。
