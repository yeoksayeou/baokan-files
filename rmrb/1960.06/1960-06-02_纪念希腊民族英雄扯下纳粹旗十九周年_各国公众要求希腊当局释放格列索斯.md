Title: Commemorating the Nineteenth Anniversary of Greek National Hero's Tearing Down of the Nazi Flag, Public Figures in Various Countries Demand Greek Authorities Release Glezos
Author:
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency report: May 31 marked the nineteenth anniversary of the day the Greek national hero Manolis Glezos tore down the swastika flag of fascist Germany in Athens. As public figures in various countries commemorate Glezos's heroic act, they once again strongly demand that the Greek authorities release Glezos.

The International Committee for the Defense of Glezos sent a telegram to the King of Greece, stating that on the nineteenth anniversary of Glezos's heroic feat of tearing down the Nazi flag that desecrated the Acropolis of Athens, all those who admire the heroic deeds of resistance fighters, all those who still remember the struggle for freedom and defense of the motherland, call on the King of Greece to release Glezos.

The Committee for the Defense of Democratic Tradition, composed of prominent Greek social figures, issued a statement saying that Glezos's actions are an example of heroism and selfless sacrifice, and a paragon of the Greek national tradition of struggle.

On May 31, people from all walks of life in Moscow held a meeting at the Central House of Journalists, demanding the immediate release of Glezos and other Greek patriots.

Many British Labour Party members of Parliament also sent telegrams to Greek Prime Minister Karamanlis, demanding the release of Glezos.

### 纪念希腊民族英雄扯下纳粹旗十九周年  各国公众要求希腊当局释放格列索斯

1960-06-02
第7版()
专栏：

    纪念希腊民族英雄扯下纳粹旗十九周年
    各国公众要求希腊当局释放格列索斯
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：5月31日是希腊民族英雄格列索斯在雅典扯下法西斯德国的卐字旗的十九周年纪念日。当各国公众纪念格列索斯这一英勇行动的时候，他们再次强烈地要求希腊当局释放格列索斯。
    国际保卫格列索斯委员会打电报给希腊国王说，在格列索斯扯下污辱雅典城堡的纳粹旗的英雄功绩十九周年纪念的时候，一切对抵抗运动战士的英勇事迹表示敬佩的人，一切还记得争取自由和保卫祖国的斗争的人，都呼吁希腊国王释放格列索斯。
    由希腊著名社会人士组成的保卫民主传统委员会发表声明说，格列索斯的行动是英雄主义和忘我牺牲的榜样，是希腊民族传统的斗争的俊杰。
    莫斯科各界人士5月31日在中央新闻工作者之家举行集会，要求立即释放格列索斯和其他的希腊爱国人士。
    英国许多工党议员也打电报给希腊首相卡拉曼利斯，要求释放格列索斯。
