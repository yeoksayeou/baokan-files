Title: Plague God Crosses the Street
Author: Dongfang Jian
Date: 1960-06-20
Page: 8

    Plague God Crosses the Street
    Five, "Salvoes" of welcome, the shock still lingers, then Taiwanese movies appear,
  "Salvoes" roar mightily, making eyes blur and heads spin. Filled with sorrow, setting foot on Keelung, only to come and go in haste, most panic-stricken and disoriented, two days of artillery bombardment.
    Dongfang Jian poem

### 瘟神过街
东方剑
1960-06-20
第8版()
专栏：

    瘟神过街
    五，“礼炮”迎送惊魂未定，又见台湾影，
  “礼炮”声声动地来，打得眼花头晕。含愁踏上基隆，只能来去匆匆，最是心慌意乱，两天大炮轰轰。
    东方剑诗
