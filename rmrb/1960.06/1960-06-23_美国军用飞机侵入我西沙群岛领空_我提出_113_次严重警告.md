Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Our Xisha Islands' Airspace; I Issue the 113th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd: On June 22nd, from 11:33 to 11:43 AM, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Shidao and Yongxing Island of our Xisha Islands in Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, a spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 113th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我西沙群岛领空  我提出113次严重警告

1960-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我西沙群岛领空
    我提出113次严重警告
    新华社22日讯　6月22日十一时三十三分至十一时四十三分，美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的石岛、永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百一十三次严重警告。
