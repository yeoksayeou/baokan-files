Title: Indonesian Cooperation Parliament Sworn In
Author:
Date: 1960-06-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 25th. The Indonesian Cooperation Parliament, organized by President Sukarno, held a swearing-in ceremony at the Presidential Palace this morning. The ceremony was presided over by President Sukarno.

Sukarno announced that Zainul Arifin of the Islamic Teachers Association and Arudji Kartawinata of the Islamic Union Party would be temporarily appointed as acting Speaker and Deputy Speaker, respectively, pending the formal appointment of a Speaker. Both were former Deputy Speakers of the Parliament.

### 印度尼西亚合作议会宣誓就职

1960-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚合作议会宣誓就职
    新华社雅加达25日电　苏加诺总统组织的印度尼西亚合作议会，今天上午在总统府举行宣誓就职仪式。仪式由苏加诺总统主持。
    苏加诺宣布，伊斯兰教师联合会的蔡努耳·阿里芬和伊斯兰联盟党的阿鲁季·卡塔威纳塔将暂时被任命为代理议长和副议长，以待正式任命议长。两人都是议会的前副议长。
