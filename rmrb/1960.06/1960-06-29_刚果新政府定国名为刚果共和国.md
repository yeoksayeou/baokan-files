Title: New Congolese Government Names Country the Republic of the Congo
Author:
Date: 1960-06-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Léopoldville, June 27th: The newly formed first government of the Congo decided on the 26th to name the new African nation, which will declare its independence on June 30th, the Republic of the Congo.

As previously reported, Kasavubu, leader of the ABAKO Party of the Congo, has been elected as the head of state of the Congo, and Patrice Lumumba, leader of the Congolese National Movement, has been elected as the first Prime Minister of the Congo.

### 刚果新政府定国名为刚果共和国

1960-06-29
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果新政府定国名为刚果共和国
    新华社27日讯　利奥波德维尔消息：最近刚成立的刚果第一届政府26日决定把这个将在6月30日宣布独立的非洲新国家定名为刚果共和国。
    前已报道，刚果阿巴科党领袖卡萨武布已经当选为刚果国家元首，刚果民族运动党领袖卢蒙巴当选为刚果第一届政府总理。
