Title: Ninth Time Setting Women's High Jump World Record, Balas Jumps 1.85 Meters
Author:
Date: 1960-06-11
Page: 6

Ninth Time Setting Women's High Jump World Record
    Balas Jumps 1.85 Meters

Romanian athlete Iolanda Balaș set a new women's high jump world record for the ninth time on the 6th in Bucharest. She jumped 1.85 meters, a result that improved her own world record by one centimeter.

### 第九次创女子跳高世界纪录  巴拉斯跳过1．85米

1960-06-11
第6版()
专栏：

    第九次创女子跳高世界纪录
    巴拉斯跳过1．85米
    罗马尼亚运动员约·巴拉斯6日在布加勒斯特第九次创造女子跳高世界纪录。她跳过了一·八五米，这个成绩比她本人保持的世界纪录又提高了一厘米。
