Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Three Guests from Cuba
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd. Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received three members of the Cuban "July 26th Movement" who are currently visiting China this morning: Fernando López Muñío, Director of Secondary Education of the Cuban Ministry of Education; Fernando Luis, Mayor of Santiago de Cuba; and Manuel Susarridi, Financial Secretary of the "July 26th Movement."

Present at the meeting were Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理接见古巴三位客人

1960-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见古巴三位客人
    新华社3日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天上午接见了正在我国访问的三位古巴“7月26日运动”人士：古巴教育部中等教育司司长费尔南多·洛佩斯·穆尼奥，古巴圣地亚哥市市长费尔南多·路易斯，“7月26日运动”财务秘书曼努埃尔·苏萨耳迪。
    接见时在座的有：外交部美澳司司长申健，中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达。
