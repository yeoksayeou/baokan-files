Title: Central Committee Member of the Indonesian Communist Party, Njoto, Wounded in Assassination Attempt; Aidit Sends Condolences
Author:
Date: 1960-06-17
Page: 6
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 15th: The Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party announced today that at 9:00 PM on June 14th, someone threw several grenades at the residence of Njoto, a member of the People's Representative Council (DPR) and member of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, in Palembang. Njoto's foot was wounded by shrapnel, but his life is not in danger.
　　D.N. Aidit, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, sent a telegram to Njoto to express his condolences. The telegram stated that this incident serves as another warning that Communists and genuine democrats should continue to heighten their political vigilance.

### 印度尼西亚共产党中央委员  努恩季克遇刺受伤  艾地致电表示慰问

1960-06-17
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党中央委员
    努恩季克遇刺受伤
    艾地致电表示慰问
    据新华社雅加达15日电　印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会书记处今天宣布，6月14日晚上九点钟，有人向合作国会议员、印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会委员努恩季克在巨港的住宅投了几颗手榴弹。努恩季克的一只脚被弹片炸伤，但是没有生命危险。
    印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会主席艾地打电报给努恩季克表示慰问。电报说，这一事件再次提出警告，共产党人和真正的民主人士应该继续提高他们的政治警惕性。
