Title: Our Artillery Wounds a Chiang Kai-shek Warship
Author:
Date: 1960-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, June 6th: The People's Liberation Army Fujian Front artillery units have wounded a Chiang Kai-shek warship.

　　At 5:56 PM on the 4th, a Chiang Kai-shek submarine chaser was carrying out harassment activities in the sea near Weitou, Fujian Province. Our Fujian Front artillery units immediately opened fire and wounded it. The wounded Chiang Kai-shek warship fled in a panic towards Kinmen Island.

### 我炮兵击伤蒋军舰一艘

1960-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我炮兵击伤蒋军舰一艘
    新华社福建前线6日电　中国人民解放军福建前线炮兵部队击伤蒋军兵舰一艘。
  4日下午五时五十六分，蒋军驱潜舰一艘在福建省围头附近海面进行骚扰活动，我福建前线炮兵部队当即开炮轰击把它击伤。受伤的蒋军兵舰仓慌地向金门岛方向狼狈逃窜。
