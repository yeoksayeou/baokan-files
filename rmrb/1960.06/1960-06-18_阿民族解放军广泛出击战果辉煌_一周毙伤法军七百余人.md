Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Launches Wide Offensive with Brilliant Results: Over 700 French Troops Killed or Wounded in One Week
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 17th - According to a military communiqué released today by the headquarters of the Algerian National Liberation Army, between June 7th and 13th, the Algerian National Liberation Army killed 449 French soldiers and wounded 293.

The communiqué stated that they also shot down 19 military aircraft and 10 other aircraft, destroyed a train, destroyed 46 armored vehicles, and seized 175 weapons. The communiqué said that the Algerian National Liberation Army ambushed French troops and convoys 15 times, engaged in combat with enemy forces in 19 locations, attacked 38 French strongholds, and destroyed most of them. Algerian commando units launched 46 attacks on French military camps and police outposts. The communiqué stated that during this period, 13 French soldiers defected to the Algerian National Liberation Army.

### 阿民族解放军广泛出击战果辉煌  一周毙伤法军七百余人

1960-06-18
第6版()
专栏：

    阿民族解放军广泛出击战果辉煌
    一周毙伤法军七百余人
    新华社开罗17日电　据阿尔及利亚民族解放军总部今天发表的军事公报，在6月7日到13日，阿尔及利亚民族解放军共打死四百四十九名法军，打伤二百九十三名。
    公报说，他们还击落十九架军用飞机和十架其它飞机，炸毁一列火车，击毁四十六辆装甲车，缴获一百七十五件武器。公报说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军伏击法军和运输队十五次，在十九个地方同敌军进行了战斗，袭击法军据点三十八处，并破坏了其中大部分据点。阿尔及利亚突击队对法军军营和警察派出所发动了四十六次进攻。公报说，在这期间，有十三名法军向阿尔及利亚民族解放军投诚。
