Title: Tibet Holds First Women's Congress; Decides to Establish Tibet Autonomous Region Women's Federation; Panchen Erdini and Others Attend to Offer Congratulations and Instructions
Author:
Date: 1960-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 16th: The first women's congress of Tibet was held in Lhasa from the 8th to the 15th. The congress summarized the women's work in Tibet over the past year, proposed new tasks for 1960, and decided to establish the Tibet Autonomous Region Women's Federation.

During the congress, Panchen Erdini, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, Zhang Guohua, Tan Guansan, Zhou Renshan, and Guo Xilan, Deputy Secretaries of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, attended to offer congratulations and gave instructions.

This congress was convened under the excellent situation where the democratic reform in the entire region had achieved a great victory, the vast number of working women had been liberated, and they were bravely advancing on the agricultural, pastoral, and other fronts. More than 300 women representatives and observers from various regions and strata of Tibet attended the congress, the vast majority of whom were liberated working women. At the meeting, they denounced the heinous feudal serf system and the crimes committed by the three major estate-holders against working women, and at the same time, sang praises of the happy life of today with incomparable joy.

The congress held that in the past few years, under the leadership of the Party, the broad masses of women in Tibet actively participated in the anti-imperialist and patriotic struggle, and their class consciousness has been greatly enhanced. In the struggle to quell the rebellion, the broad masses of women in Tibet actively supported the People's Liberation Army's units in quelling the rebellion. In the democratic reform and patriotic production increase movement in the past year, women have fully demonstrated their strong power. Thousands of women advanced producers and advanced workers have emerged throughout the region. Eighty-five advanced individuals and advanced collectives have been awarded the honorary titles of "March 8th Red Banner Holder" and "March 8th Red Banner Collective" by the All-China Women's Federation.

The congress held that with the fundamental changes in the situation in Tibet, the preparatory committee of the Tibet Patriotic Women's Association has completed its historical mission. In order to accurately reflect the nature and tasks of the women's federation organization under the new situation, and to better mobilize the enthusiasm of women, the congress unanimously discussed and adopted the resolution on the establishment of the Tibet Autonomous Region Women's Federation. At the meeting, sixty-three members were elected to form the Tibet Autonomous Region Women's Federation, and Aphe Tseten Dolkar and Huang Jingbo were elected as chairman and vice chairman respectively.

The congress also discussed and approved the decision to convene a regional conference of women activists in Lhasa in January 1961 to build a new people's democratic and socialist Tibet. At this conference, three hundred women advanced figures and advanced collectives from all fronts will be commended.

The congress also adopted a letter to women throughout the region, a congratulatory telegram to the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, and a congratulatory letter to the CPC Tibet Working Committee. In the letter to women throughout the region, women throughout the region are called upon to unite closely around the Party and strive to become women activists in building a new socialist Tibet.

During the congress, congratulatory telegrams and pennants were received from the All-China Women's Federation, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, as well as Shanghai, Sichuan, Guizhou, and Yunnan.

### 西藏举行妇女第一次代表大会  决定成立西藏自治区妇女联合会  班禅额尔德尼等到会祝贺并作指示

1960-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    西藏举行妇女第一次代表大会
    决定成立西藏自治区妇女联合会
    班禅额尔德尼等到会祝贺并作指示
    新华社拉萨16日电　西藏妇女第一次代表大会8日到15日在拉萨举行。大会总结了一年来西藏全区的妇女工作，提出了1960年新的工作任务，并决定成立西藏自治区妇女联合会。
    大会期间，西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼，中共西藏工委副书记张国华、谭冠三、周仁山、郭锡兰等都到会祝贺，并作了指示。
    这次大会，是在全区民主改革取得伟大胜利，广大劳动妇女获得翻身，并在农业、牧业以及其他各条战线上奋勇前进的大好形势下召开的。参加大会的有来自西藏各地、各阶层的妇女代表和列席代表三百多人，其中绝大多数都是翻身后的劳动妇女。她们在会上控诉了万恶的封建农奴制度和三大领主对劳动妇女犯下的罪行，同时以无比欢乐的心情歌颂了今天的幸福生活。
    大会认为，过去几年来，西藏广大妇女在党的领导下，积极参加了反帝爱国斗争，阶级觉悟有了很大提高。在平定叛乱的斗争中，西藏广大妇女积极支援了人民解放军平叛部队。在一年来的民主改革和爱国丰产运动中，妇女们又充分显示了她们的强大力量，全区涌现出了成千上万的妇女先进生产者和先进工作者，有八十五名先进个人和先进集体被全国妇联授予“三八红旗手”和“三八红旗集体”的光荣称号。
    大会认为，随着西藏形势的根本变化，西藏爱国妇女联谊会筹备委员会已完成了历史任务，为了确切反映出新形势下妇联组织的性质和任务，以及更好地调动妇女的积极性，大会一致讨论通过了关于成立西藏自治区妇女联合会的决议。会上选出了六十三名委员组成西藏自治区妇女联合会，阿沛·才旦卓噶和黄静波被分别选为主任和副主任。
    大会还讨论通过了关于1961年1月在拉萨召开全区建设人民民主和社会主义新西藏妇女积极分子大会的决定，在这个大会上将表彰三百名各条战线上的妇女先进人物和先进集体。
    大会还通过了告全区妇女书，给党中央、毛主席的致敬电和给中共西藏工委的致敬信。告全区妇女书中，号召全区妇女紧密地团结在党的周围，争取成为建设社会主义新西藏的妇女积极分子。
    大会期间，收到了全国妇女联合会、内蒙古自治区和新疆维吾尔自治区以及上海、四川、贵州、云南发来的贺电和送来的锦旗。
