Title: National Conference of Outstanding Workers in Culture and Education Continues; Vice Premier Li Xiannian Delivers Report on Current Economic Situation

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th - The National Conference of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education continued this morning.

　　Li Xiannian, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, delivered a report to the conference on the current economic situation.

　　In the afternoon, the conference broke into group discussions. In the two group meetings held this afternoon and on the morning of the 2nd, the representatives enthusiastically discussed Lu Dingyi's congratulatory message, Lin Feng's report, and Li Xiannian's report. The conference will continue tomorrow.

### 全国文教群英大会继续举行  李先念副总理向大会作关于当前经济情况的报告

1960-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    全国文教群英大会继续举行
    李先念副总理向大会作关于当前经济情况的报告
    新华社4日讯　全国文教先进工作者代表大会今天上午继续举行。
    中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李先念向大会作了关于当前经济情况的报告。
    下午，大会分组进行讨论。在今天下午和2日上午先后举行的两次小组会上，代表们热烈地讨论了陆定一的祝词、林枫的报告和李先念的报告。明天，大会将继续举行。
