Title: Chinese Communist Party Delegation En Route to Moscow to Attend the Romanian Workers' Party Third Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-06-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 16th. The Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, en route to attend the Romanian Workers' Party Third Congress, arrived in Moscow by special plane on the afternoon of the 16th.

    Present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Mukhitdinov, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union; Ustinov, First Secretary of the Moscow City Committee; Bobrovnikov, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet; Medvedev, Deputy Director of the Central Committee of the CPSU, and others.

    Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Stan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in the Soviet Union; Zhang Weilie, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, and embassy staff.

### 参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会  中共代表团路经莫斯科

1960-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会
    中共代表团路经莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科16日电　前往参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会的中国共产党代表团，由中共中央政治局委员彭真率领，已于16日下午乘专机抵达莫斯科。
    前往机场欢迎的有苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员穆希金诺夫。莫斯科市委员会第一书记乌斯季诺夫、莫斯科市苏维埃主席波布罗夫尼科夫、苏共中央副部长麦德维杰夫等人。
    前往机场欢迎的还有罗马尼亚驻苏联大使馆临时代办斯坦、我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办张伟烈及使馆人员。
