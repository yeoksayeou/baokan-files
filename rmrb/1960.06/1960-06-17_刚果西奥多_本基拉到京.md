Title: Congo's Theodore Bengila Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th—Theodore Bengila, member of the National Steering Committee and Deputy Secretary-General of the Congolese African Solidarity Party, arrived in Beijing this afternoon. He is visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs for a friendly visit.

### 刚果西奥多·本基拉到京

1960-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果西奥多·本基拉到京
    新华社16日讯　刚果非洲团结党全国指导委员会委员、副总书记西奥多·本基拉，今天下午到达北京。他是应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国进行友好访问的。
