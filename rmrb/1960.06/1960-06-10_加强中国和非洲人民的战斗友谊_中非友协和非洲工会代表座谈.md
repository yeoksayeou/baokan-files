Title: Strengthen the Militant Friendship Between the Chinese and African People: Sino-African Friendship Association and African Trade Union Representatives Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-06-10
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, June 9th. The Chinese-African People's Friendship Association held a friendly symposium today with African trade union representatives who are here attending the 11th Council Meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions.

    The guests attending the symposium came from ten countries and regions including Ghana, Congo, Cameroon, the Mali Federation, Somaliland, Mauritius, Portuguese Guinea, Zanzibar and Ubangi-Shari.

    Nan Hanchen and Zhang Tiesheng, vice presidents of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, executive director, and He Gongkai and Li Huanong, directors of the association in Beijing, attended today's symposium.

    At the symposium, the guests and hosts sincerely exchanged views on issues such as further strengthening the militant friendship between the Chinese and African peoples in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism, and expanding cultural and economic ties.

### 加强中国和非洲人民的战斗友谊  中非友协和非洲工会代表座谈

1960-06-10
第7版()
专栏：

    加强中国和非洲人民的战斗友谊
    中非友协和非洲工会代表座谈
    新华社9日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会今天和在这里参加世界工联第十一次理事会会议的非洲工会代表们举行了一次友好的座谈会。
    出席这次座谈会的客人们，来自加纳、刚果、喀麦隆、马里联邦、索马里兰、毛里求斯、葡属几内亚、桑给巴尔和乌班吉沙立等十个国家和地区。
    中国—非洲人民友好协会副会长南汉宸、张铁生，常务理事丁西林，以及该会在京的理事何功楷和李华农等出席了今天的座谈会。
    座谈会上，宾主之间就进一步加强中国和非洲人民在反对帝国主义和殖民主义中的战斗友谊和扩大文化和经济联系等问题，诚恳地交换了意见。
