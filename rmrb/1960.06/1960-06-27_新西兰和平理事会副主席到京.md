Title: Vice Chairman of New Zealand Peace Council Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th. Harold Wilfred Youren, Vice Chairman of the New Zealand Peace Council, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country at the invitation of the China Peace Committee.
    Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, welcomed the guests at the airport.

### 新西兰和平理事会副主席到京

1960-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    新西兰和平理事会副主席到京
    新华社26日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的新西兰和平理事会副主席哈罗德·韦尔弗雷德·约伦和他的夫人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长王明远到机场欢迎客人。
