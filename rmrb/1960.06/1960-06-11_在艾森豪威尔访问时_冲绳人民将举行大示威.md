Title: Okinawa People to Hold Large-Scale Demonstration During Eisenhower's Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-06-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Tokyo Dispatch: According to a report in today's "Asahi Shimbun" of Japan, the Okinawa Prefecture Reversion to the Homeland Council, composed of seventeen mass organizations, decided on the 8th to organize a large-scale demonstration on the day of US President Eisenhower's arrival in Okinawa on the 19th, demanding that the United States return Okinawa to Japan and opposing the United States bringing missiles into Okinawa.

    The council also called on all the people of Okinawa to hang the Japanese flag in front of every house on the 19th.

    According to the newspaper, youth groups in the central part of Okinawa Island are preparing to mobilize one thousand young people to demonstrate around Eisenhower's residence on the same day, demanding that the United States fully compensate the people of Okinawa for the losses suffered as a result of the crash of a US jet in Ishikawa City in June of last year.

    Youth groups in Ishikawa City called on all citizens to actively participate in this demonstration.

### 在艾森豪威尔访问时  冲绳人民将举行大示威

1960-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    在艾森豪威尔访问时
    冲绳人民将举行大示威
    新华社10日讯　东京消息：据日本“朝日新闻”今天报道，由十七个群众团体组成的冲绳县重归祖国协议会8日决定，在美国总统艾森豪威尔19日到达冲绳的那一天，将组织大规模的示威游行，要求美国把冲绳归还日本，反对美国把导弹运进冲绳。
    协议会同时呼吁全体冲绳人民，19日在每一家门前悬挂日本国旗。
    据这家报纸报道，冲绳岛中部地区的青年团体准备动员一千名青年，在同一天到艾森豪威尔的住所周围举行示威，要求美国对美国喷气式飞机去年6月在石川市坠落而使冲绳人民遭受的损失给予全部赔偿。
    石川市的青年团体呼吁全体市民积极参加这次示威游行。
