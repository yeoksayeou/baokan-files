Title: Chairman Lezsi Arrives in Kunming for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-06-12
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 11th: The Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, Haxhi Lleshi, and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Liri Belishova, and other distinguished guests, concluded their visit to Shanghai and left Shanghai by special plane at 9:00 am today for Kunming, accompanied by Saifudin, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Luo Shigao, our country's Ambassador to Albania. Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Mayor of Shanghai, and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premier of the State Council, and others saw the distinguished guests off at the airport.
    Consuls General and Consuls of various countries stationed in Shanghai, and Albanian interns in Shanghai, also went to the airport to see them off.
    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, June 11th: The Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, Haxhi Lleshi, and member of the Political Bureau and Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Liri Belishova, and other distinguished guests arrived in Kunming by special plane from Shanghai at noon today.
    The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Yu Yichuan, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Governor, Ma Jikong, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Minghui, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Governor, Zhang Tianfang and Dao Jingban (Dai nationality), Vice Chairmen of the Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Sun Keji and Ding Rongchang, leaders of the Kunming-based troops, Zhao Zengyi, First Secretary of the Kunming Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, as well as responsible persons of local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and over 1,500 people from all ethnic groups in Yunnan Province.
    Also at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests today were Vu Van Huong, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, and U Hla Tun, Vice Consul of the Union of Burma in Kunming.
    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, June 11th: This afternoon, the Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, Haxhi Lleshi, and member of the Political Bureau and Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Liri Belishova, and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Yu Yichuan, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Governor, and other responsible persons, visited the Kunming Bleaching and Dyeing Factory and the Yunnan Nationalities Institute.
    At the Bleaching and Dyeing Factory, the distinguished guests carefully observed the entire production process in the bleaching and finishing and printing workshops.
    The distinguished guests received a warm welcome at the Yunnan Nationalities Institute from Wang Yizhong, the President, Jia Dunfang, the Party Secretary, and over six hundred students of more than twenty nationalities of this institute. The distinguished guests watched song and dance performances by students of various nationalities here. The distinguished guests also toured Kunming's famous scenic spot, the Western Hills, and admired the historical sites there.
    In the evening, Yu Yichuan hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished Albanian guests. Saifudin, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Luo Shigao, Chinese Ambassador to Albania, also attended the banquet. Accompanying the guests at the banquet were: Ma Jikong, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Minghui, Secretary of the Provincial Committee and Deputy Governor, Sun Yuting, Secretary of the Provincial Committee, Cun Shusheng, Zhang Tianfang, and Dao Jingban (Dai nationality), Vice Chairmen of the Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Sun Keji and Ding Rongchang, Major Generals of the Kunming-based troops, Zhao Zengyi, First Secretary of the Kunming Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, and others.
    Vu Van Huong, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, and U Hla Tun, Vice Consul of the Union of Burma in Kunming, were invited to attend the banquet.

### 列希主席到达昆明参观访问

1960-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    列希主席到达昆明参观访问
    据新华社上海11日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员利丽·贝利绍娃等贵宾，结束了在上海的访问，今天上午九时由全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长赛福鼎、外交部副部长姬鹏飞、我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高陪同，乘专机离开上海去昆明。中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施和国务院副总理习仲勋等到机场欢送贵宾。
    各国驻上海总领事、领事，阿尔巴尼亚在上海的实习生，也到机场欢送。
    据新华社昆明11日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、书记处书记利丽·贝利绍娃等贵宾，今天中午乘专机由上海到达昆明。
    贵宾们在飞机场上受到中共云南省委书记处书记、省长于一川，中共云南省委书记处书记马继孔，中共云南省委书记处书记、副省长刘明辉，省政协副主席张天放、刀京版（傣族），驻昆明部队首长孙克骥、丁荣昌，中共昆明市委第一书记赵增益，昆明市市长潘朔端以及各民主党派、各人民团体地方组织负责人和云南省各族各界人民群众一千五百多人的热烈欢迎。
    今天到机场欢迎贵宾的，还有越南民主共和国驻昆明领事武文享、缅甸联邦驻昆明副领事吴拉吞。
    据新华社昆明11日电　今天下午，阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、书记处书记利丽·贝利绍娃等贵宾，在中共云南省委书记处书记，省长于一川等负责人陪同下，参观了昆明漂染厂和云南民族学院。
    在漂染厂，贵宾们仔细地观看了漂染整理、印花两个车间的全部生产过程。
    贵宾们在云南民族学院受到了院长王以中、党委书记贾敦芳和这个学院二十多个民族的六百多学生的热烈欢迎。贵宾们在这里观看了各民族学生的歌舞表演。贵宾们还游览了昆明著名的风景区——西山，欣赏了这里的名胜古迹。
    晚上，于一川设宴招待阿尔巴尼亚贵宾。人大常委会副委员长赛福鼎，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高也出席了宴会。出席宴会作陪的有：中共云南省委书记处书记马继孔，书记处书记、副省长刘明辉，书记处书记孙雨亭，省政协副主席寸树声、张天放、刀京版（傣族），驻昆明部队首长孙克骥、丁荣昌少将，中共昆明市委第一书记赵增益、市长潘朔端等。
    越南民主共和国驻昆明领事武文享和缅甸联邦驻昆明副领事吴拉吞应邀出席了宴会。
