Title: U.S. Warships Invade China's Territorial Waters, I Issue the 110th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-06-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th: From 02:06 to 04:32 on June 18th, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of ​​Baiquan and Haitan in Fujian Province. On the same day, from 11:18 to 16:31, another U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of ​​Wuqiu and Haitan in Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warships, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 110th serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我国领海  我提出第110次严重警告

1960-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我国领海
    我提出第110次严重警告
    新华社18日讯　6月18日二时零六分至四时三十二分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省白犬、海坛地区海域。同日十一时十八分至十六时三十一分又有美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省乌丘、海坛地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百一十次严重警告。
