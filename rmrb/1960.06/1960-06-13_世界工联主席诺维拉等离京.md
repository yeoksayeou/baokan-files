Title: World Federation of Trade Unions President Novella and Others Leave Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) President Novella (Italy), who attended the WFTU Council meeting, left Beijing by plane today to return to his country.

World Federation of Trade Unions Vice President Herbert Warnke, along with trade union representatives from the German Democratic Republic, left Beijing by plane today for a visit to Rangoon, Burma.

Suzuki Seiichi, Head of the Dispute Countermeasures Department of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), a special representative attending the WFTU Council meeting, left Beijing by plane today for a visit to Pyongyang, North Korea.

Also leaving Beijing by plane today to return to their countries were: WFTU Secretary Zakaria (Sudan), representatives and special representatives from Bulgaria, North Korea, and Panama, as well as some WFTU staff.

Those who went to the airport to see them off today included Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Vice Chairman Liu Changsheng, and Secretary of the Secretariat, Li Zaiwen, and others.

### 世界工联主席诺维拉等离京

1960-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

    世界工联主席诺维拉等离京
    新华社12日讯　出席世界工联理事会会议的世界工联主席诺维拉（意大利），今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    世界工联副主席赫伯特·瓦恩克同德意志民主共和国的工会代表，今天乘飞机离开北京前往缅甸仰光访问。
    出席世界工联理事会会议的特邀代表、日本工会总评议会争议对策部长铃木诚市，今天乘飞机前往朝鲜平壤访问。
    今天乘飞机离开北京回国的还有：世界工联书记扎卡里亚（苏丹），保加利亚、朝鲜、巴拿马的代表和特邀代表，以及世界工联一部分工作人员。
    今天到机场欢送的，有中华全国总工会主席刘宁一、副主席刘长胜和书记处书记栗再温等。
