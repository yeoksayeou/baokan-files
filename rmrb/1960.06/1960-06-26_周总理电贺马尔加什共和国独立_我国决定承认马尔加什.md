Title: Premier Zhou Sends Congratulations on the Independence of the Malagasy Republic; China Decides to Recognize Madagascar

Author:
Date: 1960-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram today to the President of the Malagasy Republic, congratulating the independence of the Malagasy Republic and informing him that the Chinese government has decided recognize the Malagasy Republic. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency the President of the Malagasy Republic, Tananarive, Malagasy Republic:

On learning of the declaration of independence of the Malagasy Republic, I extend my sincere congratulations to the people of the Malagasy Republic on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, and wish your people new achievements in the struggle to safeguard national independence. I am also honored to inform Your Excellency that the government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize the Malagasy Republic. I hope that my government's recognition of your country will lead to the development of friendly relations between China and Madagascar.

　　　　　　　　　　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
                                　June 25, 1960

### 周总理电贺马尔加什共和国独立  我国决定承认马尔加什

1960-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺马尔加什共和国独立
    我国决定承认马尔加什
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给马尔加什共和国总统，祝贺马尔加什共和国独立，并且通知他，中国政府已经决定承认马尔加什共和国。电文如下：马尔加什共和国塔那那利佛马尔加什共和国总统阁下：
    欣悉马尔加什共和国宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民，向马尔加什共和国人民致以诚挚的祝贺，祝贵国人民在维护民族独立的斗争中取得新成就。我并且荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认马尔加什共和国。我希望我国政府对贵国的承认将导致中、马友好关系的发展。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                1960年6月25日
