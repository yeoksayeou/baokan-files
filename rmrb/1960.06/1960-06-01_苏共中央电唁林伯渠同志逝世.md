Title: Telegram of Condolences from the CPSU Central Committee on the Passing of Comrade Lin Boqu
Author:
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China today received a telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Lin Boqu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China,

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union expresses its condolences to you, dear comrades, on the death of Comrade Lin Boqu, one of the oldest members of the Communist Party of China, one of its prominent activists, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress—who dedicated his life and all his activities to the Chinese people's struggle for liberation and the victorious construction of socialism in the People's Republic of China.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
May 31, 1960

### 苏共中央电唁林伯渠同志逝世

1960-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央电唁林伯渠同志逝世
    新华社31日讯　中国共产党中央委员会今天收到了苏联共产党中央委员会发来的吊唁中共中央政治局委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠同志逝世的电报，电报全文如下：中国共产党中央委员会
    苏联共产党中央委员会，对于中国共产党的一位最老的党员、它的著名活动家之一、中国共产党中央政治局委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠同志——他把自己的一生和全部活动都献给了中国人民争取解放的斗争和在中华人民共和国胜利建设社会主义的事业——的逝世，谨向你们，亲爱的同志们，表示哀悼。
        苏联共产党中央委员会
        一九六○年五月三十一日
