Title: Typhoon Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-06-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th – The Central Meteorological Science Institute issued a typhoon warning on the 26th:

    A typhoon (1960 No. 3) has formed in the Pacific Ocean east of the Philippines. At 2:00 AM this morning, the typhoon's center was located near 14 degrees North latitude and 124 degrees East longitude. The maximum wind force at the typhoon's center is above level 12. Currently, the typhoon center is moving west-northwest at a speed of 11-13 knots per hour. It is expected that the typhoon will cross the Philippines and enter the South China Sea on the 27th, after which it will turn to the northwest. From the evening of the 27th to the morning of the 28th, the wind force in the central part of the South China Sea will increase to levels 10-12, the northern part of the South China Sea will have strong winds of levels 6-8, and the central and eastern parts of Guangdong and the eastern part of Hainan Island will have strong winds of levels 5-7.

    Please relevant units listen to the weather forecast broadcasts from local meteorological stations (sites).

### 台风警报

1960-06-27
第3版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    新华社26日讯　中央气象科学研究所26日发布台风警报：
    在菲律宾以东的太平洋上有台风（1960年三号）形成。今天早晨二时台风中心位置在北纬十四度东经一百二十四度附近，台风中心位置最大风力在十二级以上。目前，台风中心正以每小时十一—十三浬的速度向西偏北方向移动，预计27日台风将越过菲律宾进入南海，以后将转向西北方向移动。27日晚上到28日上午，南海中部风力将加大到十至十二级，南海北部将有六至八级大风，广东中部东部和海南岛东部将有五至七级大风。
    请有关单位收听各当地气象台（站）天气预报广播。
