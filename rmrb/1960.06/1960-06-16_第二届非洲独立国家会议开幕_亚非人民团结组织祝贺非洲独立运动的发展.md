Title: Second Conference of Independent African States Opens; Asian-African People's Solidarity Organization Congratulates the Development of African Independence Movement
Author:
Date: 1960-06-16
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Addis Ababa, June 15th: The Second Conference of Independent African States opened on the 14th in Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia.

It is reported that the main agenda items for the conference include: the complete liberation of all African peoples from colonialism, the removal of foreign military bases from African territory, the future of Algeria and assistance to Algerian nationalists, racial discrimination in South Africa and the boycott of South African goods, etc.

Ethiopian Emperor Haile Selassie I presided over the opening ceremony of the conference. In his address, he called for an end to the era of colonialism and urged African countries to eliminate all obstacles and strive to achieve their ultimate goal: the independence and complete freedom of all African peoples from colonial oppression. He said, "Developing our economic resources is the best foundation for maintaining our independence." He emphasized that if African countries want to develop their economies more quickly, they must cooperate.

It is reported that representatives and observers from twenty countries and regions in Africa attended the conference.

The conference will be held in secret and is scheduled to end on the 25th.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 14th: The Secretariat of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Organization sent a telegram warmly congratulating the opening of the Second Conference of Independent African States and expressed strong support for its goals.

In its congratulatory telegram to the conference, the Secretariat pointed out that the African national independence movement is advancing at an astonishing rate and is crushing all forces that are trying to obstruct it.

The telegram said that at the time of the First Conference of Independent African States, there were only eight independent African countries, while this conference includes seventeen independent African countries, which is of great significance.

The Secretariat expressed its belief that Africans are united as one in the struggle for freedom, independence, and sovereignty. In this struggle, they are receiving strong support from the great movement of Asian-African solidarity.

### 第二届非洲独立国家会议开幕  亚非人民团结组织祝贺非洲独立运动的发展

1960-06-16
第6版()
专栏：

    第二届非洲独立国家会议开幕
    亚非人民团结组织祝贺非洲独立运动的发展
    新华社15日讯　亚的斯亚贝巴消息：第二届非洲独立国家会议14日在埃塞俄比亚首都亚的斯亚贝巴开幕。
    据报道，会议将讨论的主要议程包括：非洲各族人民摆脱殖民主义而取得完全解放的问题、从非洲领土上撤除外国军事基地问题、阿尔及利亚前途和援助阿尔及利亚民族主义者问题、南非种族歧视和抵制南非货物问题等。
    埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世主持了会议的开幕仪式。他在致词中呼吁结束殖民主义时代，并要求非洲国家排除一切障碍，为争取实现它们的最后目标——非洲各族人民从殖民压迫下取得独立和完全的自由而努力。他说，“开发我们的经济资源是维护我们独立的最好基础。”他强调指出，非洲国家如果要更快地发展经济，就必须进行合作。
    据报道，出席会议的有来自非洲二十个国家和地区的代表和观察员。
    会议将秘密进行，预定在25日结束。
    新华社开罗14日电　亚非人民团结组织书记处打电报热烈祝贺第二届非洲独立国家会议的召开，并表示大力支持它的目标。
    书记处在给会议的贺电中指出，非洲民族独立运动正在以惊人的速度前进着，正在粉碎拦阻它的各种力量。
    贺电说，在第一届非洲独立国家会议召开的时候，只有八个独立的非洲国家，而这一届会议已包括十七个独立非洲国家，这是意义重大的事情。
    书记处表示相信，非洲人在争取自由、独立和主权的斗争中团结得像一个人。在这个斗争中，他们得到亚非团结的巨大运动的有力支持。
