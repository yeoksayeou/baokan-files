Title: Lin Feng and Others Receive Representatives of Advanced Workers in Amateur Education
Author:
Date: 1960-06-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 11th: Lin Feng, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Wu Yuzhang and Xu Teli, members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Zhang Jichun, Director of the Cultural and Educational Office of the State Council; Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Education; and Xu Zhizhen, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, received 130 representatives of advanced units and advanced workers who have long adhered to running amateur education and adhering to amateur learning during a break in the amateur education system meeting of the National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education this morning.

### 林枫等接见业余教育的先进工作者代表

1960-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    林枫等接见业余教育的先进工作者代表
    新华社11日讯　中共中央委员、人大常委会副委员长林枫，中共中央委员吴玉章、徐特立，国务院文教办公室主任张际春，教育部部长杨秀峰，全国总工会副主席许之桢，今天上午在全国文教先进工作者代表大会的业余教育系统会议休息时间，接见了长期坚持办业余教育和坚持业余学习的先进单位和先进工作者代表一百三十人。
