Title: Chen Yi Receives Japanese Go and Painting Delegations
Author:
Date: 1960-06-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the entire members of the Japanese Go Delegation to China led by Se Kosaku this afternoon.
	Those present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Yang Hansheng, Zhou Shiguan, Lin Lin, and Zhang Lianhua.
	Also present at the reception was Japanese peace figure Kinkazu Saionji.
	On the evening of the 4th, Yang Hansheng hosted a banquet to welcome all members of the delegation.
    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the entire members of the Japanese Painting Delegation led by Seison Maeda this evening.
	Those present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Yang Hansheng, Ye Qianyu, Hua Junwu, Lin Lin, and others.
	Also present at the reception was Japanese peace figure Kinkazu Saionji.
	On the evening of the 2nd, Yang Hansheng held a welcome banquet for the delegation.

### 陈毅接见日本围棋和画家代表团

1960-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见日本围棋和画家代表团
    新华社5日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了以濑越宪作为首的日本围棋访华代表团全体成员。
    接见时在座的有廖承志、阳翰笙、周士观、林林和张联华等。
    接见时在座的还有日本和平人士西园寺公一。
    4日晚上，阳翰笙曾设宴欢迎代表团的全体成员。
    新华社5日讯　陈毅副总理今晚接见了以前田青邨为首的日本画家代表团全体成员。
    接见时，在座的有廖承志、阳翰笙、叶浅予、华君武、林林等。
    接见时在座的还有日本和平人士西园寺公一。
    2日晚上，阳翰笙为代表团举行了欢迎宴会。
