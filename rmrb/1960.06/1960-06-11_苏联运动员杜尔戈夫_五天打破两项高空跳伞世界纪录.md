Title: Soviet Athlete Durgov Breaks Two World High Altitude Parachuting Records in Five Days
Author:
Date: 1960-06-11
Page: 6

    Soviet Athlete Durgov
    Breaks Two World High Altitude Parachuting Records in Five Days
    Soviet parachutist Pyotr Durgov recently broke two world high-altitude parachuting records in five days.

    The forty-year-old Durgov jumped from a supersonic jet at an altitude of 12,974 meters on the night of June 3. This altitude exceeded the world record of 12,000 meters for men's night high-altitude parachuting set by Bulgarian athlete Karapchiev in November 1958.

    On June 7, Durgov made another high-altitude parachute jump during the day, reaching an altitude of 14,835 meters. This altitude also exceeded the world record of 13,738 meters for daytime high-altitude parachuting held by Karapchiev. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏联运动员杜尔戈夫  五天打破两项高空跳伞世界纪录

1960-06-11
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联运动员杜尔戈夫
    五天打破两项高空跳伞世界纪录
    苏联跳伞运动员彼·杜尔戈夫最近一个人在五天中打破了两项高空跳伞世界纪录。
    四十岁的杜尔戈夫6月3日夜间乘一架超音速的喷气飞机在一万二千九百七十四米高空跳伞。这个高度超过了保加利亚运动员卡拉普契耶夫1958年11月间创造的男子夜间高空跳伞一万二千米的世界纪录。
    杜尔戈夫在6月7日白天又进行了一次高空跳伞，高度达到一万四千八百三十五米。这个高度也超过了卡拉普契耶夫所保持的日间高空跳伞的一万三千七百三十八米的世界纪录。　　（据新华社讯）
