Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi Pay Respects to Comrade Lin Boqu; Over 50,000 People in the Capital Pay Their Respects Over Three Days

Author:
Date: 1960-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, and Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, and others, after returning to Beijing from a visit to the Mongolian People's Republic, went directly from the Capital Airport to the Working People's Cultural Palace this noon to stand in silent tribute before the portrait of Comrade Lin Boqu and participate in the vigil.

Since the opening of the mourning hall on the 30th, workers, peasants, soldiers, students, and cadres from organizations in the capital have come to the Working People's Cultural Palace in an endless stream every day, silently passing before the portrait of Comrade Lin Boqu. By today, Comrade Lin Boqu's mourning hall and the three-tiered stone platform in front of the main hall of the Working People's Cultural Palace were filled with wreaths. The elegies written on the wreaths expressed people's deep sorrow and the will to transform grief into strength; determined to learn from Comrade Lin Boqu's revolutionary spirit and strive for the cause of communism.

### 周总理陈毅副总理吊唁林伯渠同志  三天来首都五万多群众前往吊唁致哀

1960-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈毅副总理吊唁林伯渠同志
    三天来首都五万多群众前往吊唁致哀
    新华社1日讯　今天是吊唁林伯渠同志逝世的第三天。三天以来，党和国家的领导人和首都五万多名各界群众和代表，到劳动人民文化宫，悼念把毕生的精力贡献于伟大的共产主义事业的林伯渠同志。
    今天中午，访问蒙古人民共和国之后回到北京的中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅等人，由首都飞机场直接来到劳动人民文化宫，在林伯渠同志的遗像前静默致哀，并且参加守灵。
    从30日开吊以来，首都工人、农民、士兵、学生、机关干部，每天都络绎不绝地来到劳动人民文化宫，默默地从林伯渠同志的遗像前走过。到今天，林伯渠同志的灵堂内和劳动人民文化宫大殿前的三层石台上，都摆满了花圈。花圈上写的悼词，表达了人们的沉痛心情和把悲痛化为力量的意志；决心学习林伯渠同志的革命精神，为共产主义事业而奋斗。
