Anti-American Demonstrations by Militias Across the Country

    Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Marshal, reviews the capital militia contingent participating in the anti-American demonstration rally.
                                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Bihua photo

    More than 30,000 militiamen in Shenyang held an anti-American demonstration rally in Zhongshan Square. This is the militia shouting slogans. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhu Ying photo

    More than 10,000 militiamen of all ethnic groups in Kunming held an anti-American demonstration rally on the 27th.
　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Lü Huachang photo

    On the 27th, 40,000 militiamen in Shanghai held a demonstration rally against American imperialism. The picture shows the militia contingent participating in the demonstration.
        Xinhua News Agency reporter Cao Xinghua photo (facsimile photo)

    On the 27th, more than 40,000 militiamen in Wuhan held an anti-American demonstration rally. The militia artillery units participating in the rally are shouting slogans.
    Li Deyi photo (Xinhua News Agency draft) (facsimile photo)

    On the 27th, Guangzhou militia held an anti-American imperialism demonstration rally. Female militia are shouting slogans.
        Xinhua News Agency reporter Cai Zhongzhi photo (facsimile photo)

    25,000 young people of all ethnic groups in Urumqi held an anti-American demonstration. Militiamen from Xinjiang Normal College shouted the slogan: American imperialism get out of Asia. Xinhua News Agency reporter Mei Nawaer photo

    10,000 militiamen in Hangzhou held an anti-American imperialism demonstration rally on the morning of the 27th, and then held a demonstration parade.
            Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Shenming photo (facsimile photo)

    On the morning of the 27th, 30,000 soldiers and civilians in Tianjin held an anti-American demonstration rally at Minyuan Stadium. The picture shows the rally venue.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Yinghua photo
