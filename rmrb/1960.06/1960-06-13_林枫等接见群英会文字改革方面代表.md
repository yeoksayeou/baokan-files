Title: Lin Feng and Others Receive Representatives of the National Conference of Heroes in Cultural and Educational Fields Regarding the Reform of the Written Language

Author:
Date: 1960-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Lin Feng, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Jichun, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Director of the Cultural and Educational Office of the State Council; Wu Yuzhang, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman of the Chinese Written Language Reform Committee; Xu Teli, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Xie Juezai, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Education; and Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, this morning received the seventy-seven representatives of advanced collectives, advanced workers, and specially invited representatives in the field of writing reform who attended the National Conference of Heroes in Cultural and Educational Fields.

Also present at the reception were Hu Yuzhi, Wei Que, Ding Xilin, Ye Laishi, and others, all vice chairmen of the Chinese Written Language Reform Committee.

### 林枫等接见群英会文字改革方面代表

1960-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    林枫等接见群英会文字改革方面代表
    新华社12日讯　中共中央委员、人大常委会副委员长林枫，中共中央委员、国务院文教办公室主任张际春，中共中央委员、中国文字改革委员会主任吴玉章，中共中央委员徐特立和中共中央后补委员谢觉哉，教育部部长杨秀峰，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇，今天上午接见了出席全国文教群英会的七十七位文字改革方面的先进集体代表、先进工作者和特邀代表。
    陪同接见的还有中国文字改革委员会副主任胡愈之、韦悫、丁西林、叶籁士等人。
