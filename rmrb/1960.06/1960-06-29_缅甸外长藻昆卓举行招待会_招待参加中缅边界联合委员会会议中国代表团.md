Title: Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hko Hosts Reception for Chinese Delegation Attending Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-06-29
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 27th. Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hko hosted a reception here tonight to welcome the Chinese delegation, led by Yao Zhongming, attending the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee meeting.

Among those attending the reception were Burmese Federal Supreme Court Chief Justice U Myint Thein, Brigadier General Aung Gyi, chief representative of the Burmese delegation to the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee, Kachin State Affairs Minister Duwa Sinwa Nawng, former Foreign Minister U Chan Tun Aung, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs James Barrington, and other senior officials from the Burmese Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yi-mang and diplomatic officials from the Chinese Embassy were also invited to attend the reception.

The hosts and guests repeatedly toasted Sino-Burmese friendship and the final and comprehensive settlement of the Sino-Burmese boundary issue.

### 缅甸外长藻昆卓举行招待会  招待参加中缅边界联合委员会会议中国代表团

1960-06-29
第7版()
专栏：

    缅甸外长藻昆卓举行招待会
    招待参加中缅边界联合委员会会议中国代表团
    新华社仰光27日电　缅甸外交部长藻昆卓今晚在这里举行招待会，招待以姚仲明为首的参加中缅边界联合委员会会议的中国代表团。
    出席招待会的有缅甸联邦最高法院首席法官吴敏登、中缅边界联合委员会的缅甸代表团首席代表昂季准将、克钦邦事务部长杜瓦信瓦瑙、前外交部长吴千吞昂、外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿以及缅甸外交部的其它高级官员。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓以及中国大使馆的外交官员也应邀出席了招待会。
    宾主一再为中缅友谊和最后全面解决中缅边界问题干杯。
