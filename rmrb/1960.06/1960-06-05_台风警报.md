Author:
Date: 1960-06-05
Page: 6

    Typhoon Alert
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Institute of Meteorological Science of the Central Meteorological Bureau issued a typhoon alert at 12:00 noon today, stating: The tropical depression over the South China Sea has strengthened into a weak typhoon. At 2:00 AM today, the center was located in the central part of the South China Sea, at approximately 16 degrees north latitude and 114 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind force near the center is estimated to be level 7-8. In the future, the typhoon will move in a west-northwest direction at a speed of 8-11 knots per hour and will continue to strengthen. In the next 48 hours or so, the wind force on the sea near Hainan Island and the southern part of the Beibu Gulf will increase to level 8-10; Hainan Island will experience heavy rain. The Leizhou Peninsula, Hainan Island, the western coastal areas of Guangdong, and the northern part of the Beibu Gulf will also be affected by the typhoon. The wind force will increase to level 6-8.

### 台风警报

1960-06-05
第6版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    新华社4日讯　今天中午十二时，中央气象局气象科学研究所发布台风警报说：南海上的热带低压，已经加强为弱台风，今天早晨二时，中心位置在南海中部北纬十六度、东经一百一十四度附近，中心附近最大风力估计有七——八级。未来台风将以每小时八——十一浬的速度向西偏北方向移动，并将继续加强。未来四十八小时前后，海南岛附近的南海海面和北部湾南部海面风力将加大到八——十级；海南岛将有暴雨。雷州半岛、海南岛、广东西部沿海和北部湾北部亦将受到台风影响。风力将加大到六——八级。
