Title: To Attend the World Federation of Trade Unions Council Meeting, WFTU Vice-President Grishin Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, May 31st. To attend the World Federation of Trade Unions Council meeting, World Federation of Trade Unions Vice-President Grishin (USSR) and council members and representatives from countries and regions including Cameroon, Black Africa, the Mali Federation, Somaliland, Mauritius, Brazil, Venezuela, Panama, Martinique, Guadeloupe, Indonesia, Cyprus, and Japan arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.  Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Vice-Chairmen Liu Changsheng and Zhu Xuefan, and Secretariat Secretary Kang Yonghe were among those who welcomed them at the airport.

### 出席世界工联理事会会议  世界工联副主席格里申等到京

1960-06-01
第6版()
专栏：

    出席世界工联理事会会议
    世界工联副主席格里申等到京
    新华社31日讯　出席世界工联理事会会议的世界工联副主席格里申（苏联）和喀麦隆、黑非、马里联邦、索马里兰、毛里求斯、巴西、委内瑞拉、巴拿马、马提尼克、瓜达罗普、印度尼西亚、塞浦路斯、日本等国家和地区的理事和代表，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会主席刘宁一，副主席刘长胜、朱学范，书记处书记康永和等。
