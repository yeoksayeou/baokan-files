Title: First Group of Mountaineering Heroes Arrive in Xigazê, Welcomed by Over 10,000 People Lining the Streets
Author:
Date: 1960-06-06
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Xigazê, 5th – The first group of members of the Chinese mountaineering team, who victoriously conquered the world's highest peak, arrived in Xigazê on the afternoon of the 4th and received a grand welcome from the local people.

    On this day, colorful "triumphal arches" were erected on the outskirts of Xigazê city's highway, with huge embroidered tents propped up along the roadside and various welcome slogans posted on the buildings along the streets. More than 15,000 people, holding flowers and "Hada," lined the streets to welcome the mountaineering heroes. Party and military leaders of the Xigazê region personally welcomed the mountaineering heroes on the highway. Wang Ting, Deputy Secretary of the Xigazê Sub-Committee of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, delivered a welcome speech, representing the people of all sectors in the Xigazê region in welcoming the mountaineering heroes back. Xu Jing, Deputy Captain of the mountaineering team, expressed gratitude on behalf of all the mountaineering team members to the people of Xigazê for their welcome and pledged to create even greater and better achievements to repay the care of the people of the motherland.

### 首批登山英雄抵日喀则  一万多群众夹道欢迎

1960-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    首批登山英雄抵日喀则
    一万多群众夹道欢迎
    新华社日喀则5日电　胜利征服了世界第一高峰的中国登山队第一批队员，在4日下午抵达日喀则，受到当地人民隆重的欢迎。
    这一天，日喀则市郊公路上，搭起了五彩缤纷的“凯旋门”，路旁支撑着巨大的镶花帐幕，街道的建筑物上张贴着各种欢迎标语。一万五千多名群众捧着鲜花和“哈达”，站在公路两旁夹道欢迎。日喀则地区党、军负责人亲自到公路上欢迎登山英雄们。中共西藏工委日喀则分工委副书记王挺致欢迎词，代表日喀则地区各界人民欢迎登山英雄们归来。登山队副队长许竞代表全体登山队员对日喀则人民的欢迎表示感谢，并且保证要创造出更大更好的成绩，报答祖国人民的关怀。
