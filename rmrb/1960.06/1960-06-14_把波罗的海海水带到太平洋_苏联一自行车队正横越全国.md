Title: Bringing Baltic Sea Water to the Pacific - Soviet Cycling Team Crosses the Country
Author:
Date: 1960-06-14
Page: 6

    Bringing Baltic Sea Water to the Pacific
    Soviet Cycling Team Crosses the Country
    TASS, Sverdlovsk: A cycling team from the Lithuanian Soviet Socialist Republic is undertaking a long-distance journey from west to east across the country. The trip starts in Klaipeda, Lithuania, in the west and ends in Vladivostok in the Far East.
    The team set off from Klaipeda on May 1st and, after forty days, had travelled 3,000 kilometers, arriving in Sverdlovsk. They are carrying Baltic Sea water with them, which they will pour into the Pacific Ocean upon arrival at their destination.

### 把波罗的海海水带到太平洋  苏联一自行车队正横越全国

1960-06-14
第6版()
专栏：

    把波罗的海海水带到太平洋
    苏联一自行车队正横越全国
    据塔斯社斯维德洛夫斯克讯：苏联立陶宛共和国一个自行车旅行队正在进行着从西往东横越全国的长途旅行。这次行程西起立陶宛的克莱佩达，东迄远东的海参崴市。
    这个旅行队是在五月一日从克莱佩达启程的，四十天后已经走了三千公里，抵达斯维德洛夫斯克市。他们随身携带着波罗的海的海水，抵达终点时将把它倒入太平洋。
