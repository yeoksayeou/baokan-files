Title: Italian Journalist Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-06-01
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 31st. An Italian journalist delegation of five, led by Sergio Segre, head of the Italian Research Center for Development and Economic and Cultural Relations with China, arrived in Beijing by plane, today, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and All-China Journalists Association.

### 意大利新闻工作者代表团到京

1960-06-01
第6版()
专栏：

    意大利新闻工作者代表团到京
    新华社31日讯　意大利新闻工作者代表团一行五人应中国人民对外文化协会和中华全国新闻工作者协会的邀请，在团长、意大利发展与中国经济和文化关系研究中心秘书长塞尔乔·赛格莱率领下于今日飞抵北京。
