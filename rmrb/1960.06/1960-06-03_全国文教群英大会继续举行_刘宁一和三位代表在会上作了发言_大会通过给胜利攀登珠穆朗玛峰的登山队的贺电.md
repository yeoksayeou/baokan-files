Title: National Congress of Outstanding Workers in Culture and Education Continues; Liu Ningyi and Three Representatives Speak at the Meeting; Congress Passes a Congratulatory Telegram to the Mountaineering Team that Victoriously Scaled Mount Everest
Author:
Date: 1960-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd. The National Congress of Advanced Workers in Culture and Education continued this afternoon.

    Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, spoke at the meeting, introducing the situation of the broad masses of workers and staff across the country studying Marxism-Leninism, studying Mao Zedong's works, vigorously running spare-time education for workers and staff, and carrying out cultural, health, and sports activities.

    Three representatives spoke at the meeting. They were: Wang Furu, specially invited representative of a guard unit of the People's Liberation Army who has made remarkable achievements in studying culture under the personal care and guidance of the great leader Chairman Mao; Shi Zhanchun, leader of the Chinese mountaineering team who has just returned from Tibet after leading the victorious ascent of Mount Everest; and Yi Yaowen, First Secretary of the Jishan County Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in Shanxi Province, a red banner in national rural health work.

    The congress unanimously passed a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese mountaineering team that victoriously scaled Mount Everest.

    This morning, the congress held group discussions. The congress will continue tomorrow.

### 全国文教群英大会继续举行  刘宁一和三位代表在会上作了发言  大会通过给胜利攀登珠穆朗玛峰的登山队的贺电

1960-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    全国文教群英大会继续举行
    刘宁一和三位代表在会上作了发言
    大会通过给胜利攀登珠穆朗玛峰的登山队的贺电
    新华社2日讯　全国文教先进工作者代表大会今天下午继续举行。
    中华全国总工会主席刘宁一在会上讲话，介绍了全国广大工人群众学习马克思列宁主义、学习毛泽东著作，大办职工业余教育，以及开展文化、卫生、体育活动的情况。
    有三位代表在会上发言。他们是：在伟大领袖毛主席亲自关怀和指导下学习文化卓有成就的解放军某警卫部队特邀代表王福如，领导胜利攀登珠穆朗玛峰刚刚从西藏归来的中国登山队队长史占春，全国农村卫生工作的一面红旗——山西省稷山县中共县委第一书记仪耀文。
    大会一致通过了给胜利攀登珠穆朗玛峰的中国登山队的贺电。
    今天上午，大会分组举行讨论。明天，大会将继续举行。
