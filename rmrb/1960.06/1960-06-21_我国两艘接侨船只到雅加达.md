Title: Our Two Ships Arrive in Jakarta to Pick Up Compatriots

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 19th: The "Hai Huang" ship, dispatched by the Chinese government to pick up displaced overseas Chinese who wish to return to their homeland, arrived in Jakarta today. Another ship for picking up overseas Chinese, the "Da Bao Kang," arrived here on the 18th. They will transport more than a thousand displaced overseas Chinese from various parts of Java back to China.

### 我国两艘接侨船只到雅加达

1960-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

    我国两艘接侨船只到雅加达
    新华社雅加达19日电　中国政府派遣前来接运流离失所的华侨回国的“海皇号”今天到达雅加达。另一艘接运华侨的船只“大宝康号”已在18日到达这里。它们将把爪哇各地的一千多名流离失所的华侨接运回中国。
