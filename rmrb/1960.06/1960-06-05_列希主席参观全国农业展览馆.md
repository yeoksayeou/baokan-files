Title: Chairman Leshi Visits the National Agricultural Exhibition Center
Author:
Date: 1960-06-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: Albanian People's Assembly Presidium Chairman Haxhi Lleshi and other distinguished guests visited the National Agricultural Exhibition Center this morning.

Red flags were flying in front of the National Agricultural Exhibition Center, surrounded by greenery. Chairman Lleshi, Comrade Belishova, and other distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by Vice Premier Chen Yi, Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, and staff members of the exhibition center upon their arrival.

Accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and Minister Liao Luyan, the distinguished guests spent more than two hours visiting the exhibition center's Comprehensive Hall, Water Conservancy Hall, Measures Hall, and People's Commune Industry Hall. During the visit, Vice Premier Chen Yi explained to the distinguished guests in front of many exhibits, models, and charts.

Chairman Lleshi and other distinguished guests expressed their admiration for the development of the People's Communes and various production and construction endeavors. In the farmers' life exhibition section of the Comprehensive Hall, after Chairman Lleshi viewed the typical comparison between the old and new lives of farmers, he said, "This is truly a world of difference!" Vice Premier Chen Yi said: "We are not good enough yet, so we need a Great Leap Forward." Chairman Lleshi said: "Albania is the same. Our development should be endless."

Before leaving the exhibition center, Minister Liao Luyan and the person in charge of the exhibition center presented the distinguished guests with commemorative albums of the exhibition center. Chairman Lleshi, Belishova, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Minister Liao Luyan, Chinese Ambassador to Albania Luo Shigao, and Albanian Ambassador to China Plevti, who accompanied the visit, also took a group photo with the staff of the exhibition center. ### 列希主席参观全国农业展览馆

1960-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

    列希主席参观全国农业展览馆
    新华社4日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希和其他贵宾，今天上午参观了全国农业展览馆。
    绿树丛中的全国农业展览馆前面，今天红旗招展。列希主席，贝利绍娃同志等贵宾到达那里时，受到陈毅副总理，农业部部长廖鲁言等负责人和展览馆工作人员的热烈欢迎。
    贵宾们在陈毅副总理、廖鲁言部长的陪同下，用了两个多小时的时间，先后参观了展览馆的综合馆、水利馆、措施馆和人民公社工业馆。参观过程中，陈毅副总理在展出的许多实物、模型和图表前面，向贵宾们讲解。
    列希主席等贵宾对人民公社和各项生产建设的发展表示赞许。在综合馆农民生活展览部分，列希主席看了典型的农民新旧生活对比以后说：“这真是天壤之别！”陈毅副总理说：
  “我们还不够，因此我们需要大跃进。”列希主席说：“阿尔巴尼亚也是一样。我们的发展应该是无止境的。”
    离开展览馆前，廖鲁言部长和展览馆负责人，向贵宾们赠送了展览馆的纪念册。列希主席和贝利绍娃等贵宾，陈毅副总理，廖鲁言部长，陪同来访的中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使帕里夫蒂等，还一起同展览馆的工作人员合影留念。（附图片）
