Title: U.S. Aircraft Again Intrudes into Our Airspace; We Issue the 104th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-06-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: On June 4th, from 9:55 AM to 10:04 AM, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island, Shidao Island, Beidao Island, and other islands of the Xisha Islands in Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 10:03 AM to 10:25 AM, another U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Shanwei to Pinghai area of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesman of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 104th serious warning.

### 美机又侵入我领空  我提出第104次严重警告

1960-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    美机又侵入我领空
    我提出第104次严重警告
    新华社4日讯　6月4日九时五十五分至十时零四分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、石岛、北岛等岛屿上空。同日十时零三分至十时二十五分又有美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省汕尾至平海地区上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百零四次严重警告。
