Title: Chen Yi and Others Receive Advanced Cultural and Educational Workers with Over 30 Years of Service

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council; Lu Dingyi, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council; Lin Feng, member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Xu Teli, member of the CPC Central Committee; and Zhang Jichun, Director of the Cultural and Educational Office of the State Council, received this morning over 190 advanced workers with more than 30 years of service attending the National Congress of Heroes in Culture and Education, and had cordial conversations with them.

Accompanying them were Yang Xiufeng, Li Dequan, Qian Junrui, Zhang Ziyi and other heads of central cultural and educational departments.

These veteran educators, cultural workers, health workers, sports workers, and journalists who were received have been engaged in cultural and educational undertakings for decades, and have made outstanding achievements, winning the love of the broad masses. (Picture attached)

At the National Congress of Heroes in Culture and Education, Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, Lu Dingyi, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, Lin Feng, member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Xu Teli, member of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhang Jichun, Director of the Cultural and Educational Office of the State Council, and other comrades received over 190 advanced workers with more than 30 years of service. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Qingrui

### 陈毅等接见30年以上工龄文教先进工作者

1960-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅等接见30年以上工龄文教先进工作者
    新华社5日讯　中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅，中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理陆定一，中共中央委员、人大常委会副委员长林枫，中共中央委员徐特立，国务院文教办公室主任张际春，今天上午接见了出席全国文教群英会的一百九十多名有三十年以上工龄的先进工作者，并且和他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    陪同接见的有杨秀峰、李德全、钱俊瑞、张子意等中央文教各部门负责人。
    被接见的这些老教育工作者、文化工作者、卫生工作者、体育工作者和新闻工作者，数十年如一日地从事文化教育事业，并且作出了出色成绩，受到广大群众的爱戴。（附图片）
    在全国文教群英会上，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅，中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理陆定一，中共中央委员、人大常委会副委员长林枫，中共中央委员徐特立，国务院文教办公室主任张际春等同志接见了一百九十多名有三十年以上工龄的先进工作者　新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
