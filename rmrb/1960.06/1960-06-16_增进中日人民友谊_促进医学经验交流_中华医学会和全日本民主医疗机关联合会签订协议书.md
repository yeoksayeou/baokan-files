Title: Enhance Sino-Japanese People's Friendship, Promote Medical Experience Exchange: Chinese Medical Association and All-Japan Federation of Democratic Medical Institutions Sign Agreement

Author:

Date: 1960-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Chinese Medical Association and the All-Japan Federation of Democratic Medical Institutions signed an agreement in Beijing this afternoon. The agreement stated that they will work together to enhance friendship between the people of China and Japan, oppose American imperialism and Japanese militarism, and safeguard lasting peace in Asia and the world.

The agreement was signed by Fu Lianzhang, President of the Chinese Medical Association, and Su Tian Zhu Bahachiro, head of the Japanese medical delegation currently visiting China. Cui Yitian, Vice President of the Chinese Medical Association, Qian Xinzhong, Deputy Minister of Health, and well-known figures in Beijing's medical circles attended the signing ceremony.

The agreement stated: "The Chinese Medical Association expresses its sincere support and high respect for the Japanese people's persistent and unyielding patriotic and just struggle against American imperialism, and fully supports the democratic medical activities carried out by the All-Japan Federation of Democratic Medical Institutions to protect the health of the Japanese working people and unite Japanese medical personnel."

The agreement believes that this friendly visit has promoted mutual understanding between the two sides, strengthened the ties and exchange of experience between the medical circles of China and Japan, thereby enhancing the friendship between the two peoples.

The Japanese medical delegation arrived in our country on April 29. They have successively visited medical institutions, medical education, scientific research institutions, factories, and people's communes in Beijing, Wuhan, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places.

### 增进中日人民友谊  促进医学经验交流  中华医学会和全日本民主医疗机关联合会签订协议书

1960-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    增进中日人民友谊  促进医学经验交流
    中华医学会和全日本民主医疗机关联合会签订协议书
    新华社15日讯　中华医学会和全日本民主医疗机关联合会，今天下午在北京签订协议书。协议书表示要为增进中日两国人民间的友谊，反对美帝国主义和日本军国主义，维护亚洲和世界持久和平而共同奋斗。
    协议书是由中华医学会会长傅连暲和正在中国访问的日本医学代表团团长须田朱八郎签字的。参加签字仪式的，有中华医学会副会长崔义田，卫生部副部长钱信忠，和北京医学界著名人士。
    协议书说：“中华医学会对日本人民再接再厉不屈不挠地反对美帝国主义的爱国正义斗争，表示衷心的支持和崇高敬意，并完全支持全日本民主医疗机关联合会为了保障日本劳动人民的健康，团结日本医学界人士而进行的民主医疗活动。”
    协议书认为：这次友好访问促进了双方互相了解，加强了中日两国医学界的联系和经验交流，从而增进了两国人民之间的友谊。
    日本医学代表团是在4月29日来到我国的。他们先后参观访问了北京、武汉、上海、杭州等地的医疗机构，医学教育、科学研究机构、工厂和人民公社。
