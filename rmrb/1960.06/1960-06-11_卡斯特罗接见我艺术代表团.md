Title: Castro Receives Chinese Art Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-06-11
Page: 6

(Comprehensive report from our newspaper) According to Xinhua News Agency: Cuban Prime Minister Castro received Chen Zhongjing, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, and other leaders at his residence on the 9th, and had a cordial two-hour conversation with them. Zeng Tao, director of the Xinhua News Agency's Havana branch, was present at the meeting.

Chen Zhongjing, on behalf of all members of the art troupe, expressed to Prime Minister Castro their great admiration for the Cuban people's indomitable struggle against imperialism under his leadership. Chen Zhongjing also thanked Prime Minister Castro for watching the performance of the Chinese Art Troupe.

Castro warmly praised the performance of the Chinese Art Troupe. He said that people on the streets of Havana were talking about Chinese art. He congratulated the art troupe on its greater success in its performances in Cuba. Prime Minister Castro said that he loves Chinese art, just as he loves China.

Chen Zhongjing, head of the art troupe, presented gifts to Castro on behalf of Premier Zhou Enlai. Castro carefully examined the gifts and expressed his deep gratitude.

Castro invited the Chinese guests to view the construction blueprints and models of Cuba's "City of Schools," and he also explained to the Chinese guests in detail Cuba's current construction plans and future prospects.

### 卡斯特罗接见我艺术代表团

1960-06-11
第6版()
专栏：

    卡斯特罗接见我艺术代表团
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：古巴总理卡斯特罗9日在他的官邸接见了中国艺术团团长陈忠经及其他领导人，并同他们进行了两小时的亲切的谈话。接见时在座的有新华社哈瓦那分社社长曾涛。
    陈忠经代表艺术团的全体团员向卡斯特罗总理表示，他们非常钦佩古巴人民在他的领导下所进行的不屈不挠的反对帝国主义的斗争。陈忠经还对卡斯特罗总理观看了中国艺术团的演出表示感谢。
    卡斯特罗热烈赞扬了中国艺术团的演出。他说，哈瓦那街上的人都在谈论中国的艺术。他祝贺，艺术团在古巴的演出取得更大的成就。卡斯特罗总理说，他喜爱中国的艺术，正像他喜爱中国一样。
    艺术团团长陈忠经代表周恩来总理向卡斯特罗赠送了礼物。卡斯特罗仔细地观看了这些礼物，并且表示深切的感谢。
    卡斯特罗请中国客人观看了古巴“学校之城”的施工蓝图和模型，他还向中国客人详细地解释了古巴目前的建设计划和将来的远景。
