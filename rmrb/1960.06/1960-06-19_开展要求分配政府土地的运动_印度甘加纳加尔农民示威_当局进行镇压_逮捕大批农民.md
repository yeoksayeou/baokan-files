Title: Launching a Campaign to Demand Allocation of Government Land: Indian Farmers in Ganganagar Demonstrate; Authorities Suppress, Arresting Large Numbers of Farmers
Author:
Date: 1960-06-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 17th. According to a report in today's "Hindustan Standard," farmers in the Ganganagar district of Punjab, India, are launching a campaign against the auctioning of government land, demanding that this land be allocated to landless farmers. Applications from farmers requesting allocation of government land have reached approximately ten thousand.

The report states that the District Farmers' Association of Ganganagar held a protest demonstration on the 15th in front of the Bhakra office in the county.

The authorities suppressed the movement. The number of farmers arrested has reached seventy-five. Fifteen people were arrested on the day of the demonstration on the 15th.

Prior to this, in Mohan Garh village, Punjab, a struggle took place between tenant farmers and landlords over the seizure of tenancies. Local police carried out a bloody suppression, killing and wounding dozens.

According to a recent issue of the "New Age" weekly, the Punjab organization of the All India Kisan Sabha called on the masses to hold a rally on June 26 to protest the police killing of farmers in Mohan Garh village. The Secretariat of the Punjab State Committee of the Communist Party of India also issued a statement condemning the police for opening fire to suppress tenant farmers in order to help landlords seize tenancies, resulting in a serious tragedy. (Attached picture)

On June 12, 20,000 Sikhs held a demonstration in Delhi, the capital of India, demanding the establishment of a separate Punjabi-speaking state. The picture shows armed police clashing with Sikhs.

### 开展要求分配政府土地的运动  印度甘加纳加尔农民示威  当局进行镇压　逮捕大批农民

1960-06-19
第6版()
专栏：

    开展要求分配政府土地的运动
    印度甘加纳加尔农民示威
    当局进行镇压　逮捕大批农民
    新华社新德里17日电　据“印度斯坦旗报”今天报道，印度旁遮普邦甘加纳加尔县的农民正在开展反对拍卖政府的土地、要求把这些土地分配给无地的农民的运动。农民要求分配政府土地的申请书已达到一万份左右。
    消息说，甘加纳加尔的县农会15日在这个县的巴克拉办事处前面曾举行了抗议示威。
    当局对这个运动进行了镇压。被捕的农民已达七十五名。在15日举行示威的那天就有十五人被捕。
    在这以前，在旁遮普邦的莫汉加尔村发生了佃农反对地主夺佃的斗争，当地警察进行了血腥的镇压，打死、打伤了数十人。
    据最近一期的“新世纪”周报报道，全印农民协会旁遮普邦组织号召群众在6月26日举行集会，抗议警察杀害莫汉加尔村农民的行为。印度共产党旁遮普邦委员会书记处也发表声明，谴责警察为帮助地主夺佃开枪镇压佃农，因而造成了严重的惨案。（附图片）
    6月12日在印度首都德里两万锡克教族人举行要求单独成立旁遮普语言邦的示威，图为武装警察和锡克教族人发生冲突的情形。
