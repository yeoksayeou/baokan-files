Title: Romanian Party Congress Continues; Khrushchev Delivers Greetings on Behalf of the CPSU Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-06-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 21st. The Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party continued its session on the afternoon of the 21st.

　　At the beginning of the meeting, Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, delivered greetings to the congress on behalf of the CPSU Central Committee. He also read out the congratulatory telegram from the CPSU Central Committee to the congress. The greetings and telegram were warmly welcomed by the entire audience.

　　Then, the delegates continued to speak, discussing Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej’s report and the report of the Central Auditing Commission.

　　Minister of the Petrochemical Industry, M. Florescu, spoke first. He said that in the process of discussing the draft Six-Year Plan and the long-term plan put forward by the Party Central Committee, the workers and staff of factories and enterprises under the Ministry of Petrochemical Industry guaranteed to complete the tasks stipulated in the Six-Year Plan ahead of schedule.

　　He also said that the Ministry of Petrochemical Industry has strengthened drilling work in accordance with the instructions of the Party. Electric turbine drilling will begin in 1961, and drilling of 6,000-meter deep wells will begin this year. The employees of the petroleum industry are determined to take measures to greatly strengthen the utilization of crude oil and reduce losses.

　　Speaking of the chemical industry, he pointed out that many tasks formulated by the Second Congress of the Romanian Workers’ Party have been overfulfilled. Romania will vigorously develop the inorganic chemical industry, vigorously develop chemical fertilizers, especially nitrogen fertilizer production, and the production of pesticides for the prevention and control of diseases and insect pests will also be developed. The plastics and synthetic fiber industry will use petroleum as raw materials; in addition, the sources of other raw materials for synthetic fibers will be expanded.

　　Petru Furdului, First Secretary of the Hunedoara County Party Committee, and P. Alexandru, a representative of the Ploiesti County Party organization, also spoke at the meeting. In their speeches, they talked about the industrial development of their respective counties.

　　Finally, Vasili Vîlcu, a representative of the Credentials Committee of the Congress, reported on the situation of the delegates attending the congress. He said that a total of 946 official delegates and 207 alternate delegates attended the congress; 384 more than the previous congress. Among all the delegates, 60% are recipients of medals and orders, and 16 are heroes of socialist labor.

　　Today's meeting ended at 8 p.m., and the meeting will continue on the morning of the 22nd.

### 罗马尼亚党代表大会继续举行  赫鲁晓夫代表苏共中央致贺词

1960-06-23
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚党代表大会继续举行
    赫鲁晓夫代表苏共中央致贺词
    新华社布加勒斯特21日电　罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会在21日下午继续开会。
    会议开始时，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫代表苏共中央向大会致贺词。他并且宣读了苏共中央给代表大会的贺电。贺词和贺电受到了全场的热烈欢迎。
    然后，大会代表继续发言，讨论乔治乌—德治的报告和中央检查委员会的报告。
    石油化学工业部长米·弗洛里斯库首先发言。他说，在讨论党中央提出的六年计划和远景规划草案的过程中，石油化学工业部所属的工厂企业的职工们保证提前完成六年计划所规定的任务。
    他还说，石油化学工业部根据党的指示加强了钻探工作。1961年将开始电力涡轮钻井，今年将开始六千米深井的钻探。石油工业部门的职工决心采取措施大大加强原油的利用，减少损耗。
    在谈到化学工业时，他指出，罗马尼亚工人党“二大”制订的许多任务都已超额完成。罗马尼亚将大力发展无机化学工业，大力发展化学肥料、特别是氮肥生产，防治病虫害的药品的生产也将得到发展。塑料和人造纤维工业将以石油为原料；此外，还将扩大人造纤维的其他原料来源。
    在会上发言的还有洪尼多阿拉州党委第一书记彼得鲁·富尔杜伊和普罗什蒂州党组织的代表波·阿莱克桑德鲁。他们在发言中分别谈到了他们州里的工业发展情况。
    最后，大会代表资格审查委员会的代表瓦西里·维耳库报告了出席这次大会的代表的情况。他说，出席这次大会的共有九百四十六名正式代表和二百零七名候补代表；比上届大会多三百八十四名。在全部代表中，60％是勋章和奖章获得者，有十六人是社会主义劳动英雄。
    今天的会议在晚八时结束，22日上午将继续开会。
