Title: Typhoon Emergency Alert
Author:
Date: 1960-06-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon emergency alert at 5:00 PM on the 29th: The third typhoon of 1960 in the South China Sea was centered about 150 kilometers south of Taishan at 2:00 PM today, near 20.7 degrees North latitude and 112.8 degrees East longitude. The maximum wind force near the center is Force 12. Currently, the typhoon center is moving northwest-west at a speed of 8 to 10 knots per hour. It is estimated that the typhoon center will make landfall tonight or tomorrow morning in the area between the Leizhou Peninsula and Taishan in Guangdong. Affected by the typhoon, there will be Force 9 to 12 winds in the northwestern part of the South China Sea, Force 8 to 11 winds along the central and western coasts of Guangdong, the Leizhou Peninsula, the Beibu Gulf, and the northern part of Hainan Island in the next 24 hours. There will be Force 12 winds near the typhoon center's landfall point, Force 7 to 10 winds in the central and western inland areas of Guangdong, and Force 6 to 8 winds in the southern part of Hainan Island. There will also be heavy to torrential rain in central and western Guangdong, the northern part of Hainan Island, and southern Guangxi. All relevant units are requested to pay attention to listening to the weather forecast broadcasts from local meteorological observatories (stations).

### 台风紧急警报

1960-06-30
第3版()
专栏：

    台风紧急警报
    新华社29日讯　中央气象科学研究所29日下午五时发布台风紧急警报：南海上的1960年第三号台风，今天下午二时中心位置在台山以南一百五十公里左右的海面上，就是北纬二十·七度东经一一二·八度附近。中心附近最大风力有十二级。目前台风中心正以每小时八到十浬的速度向西北偏西方向移动，估计台风中心将于今夜到明晨在广东的雷州半岛到台山之间的地区登陆。由于受台风的影响，未来二十四小时内，南海西北部海面有九到十二级的大风，广东中部和西部沿海、雷州半岛、北部湾、海南岛北部将有八到十一级大风。台风中心登陆地点附近有十二级大风，广东中部和西部内陆将有七到十级大风，海南岛南部将有六到八级大风。广东中部西部、海南岛北部、广西南部并将有大雨到暴雨。请各有关单位注意收听各当地气象台（站）的天气预报广播。
