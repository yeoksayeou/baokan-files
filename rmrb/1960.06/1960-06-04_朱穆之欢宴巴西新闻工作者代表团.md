Title: Zhu Muzhi Hosts Banquet for Brazilian Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd. Zhu Muzhi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Brazilian Journalists Delegation headed by Costa Pinto, head of the Brazilian National Federation of Professional Journalists. The delegation arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of June 1st at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 朱穆之欢宴巴西新闻工作者代表团

1960-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    朱穆之欢宴巴西新闻工作者代表团
    新华社2日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之今晚设宴欢迎以巴西全国职业新闻工作者联合会负责人科斯塔·平托为首的巴西新闻工作者代表团。代表团是应中华全国新闻工作者协会的邀请，在1日下午到京的。
