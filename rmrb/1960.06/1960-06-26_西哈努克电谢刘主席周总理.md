Title: Sihanouk Sends Telegrams to Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou to Express Thanks
Author:
Date: 1960-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th. Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk sent a telegram to Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China on the 24th, thanking Chairman Liu Shaoqi for his congratulations on Prince Sihanouk's assumption of the office of Head of State of the Kingdom of Cambodia on the 20th. The telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Mr. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

I am very grateful for your congratulatory telegram on my assumption of the office of Head of State. Mr. Chairman, please accept my most sincere gratitude for this high honor to me personally. I reiterate to Your Excellency, to your government, and to the great Chinese people my most ardent thanks for this renewed and warm affirmation of the unbreakable friendship between our two countries, linked by a common love of peace and freedom.

Norodom Sihanouk
Head of State of Cambodia
June 24, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th. Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China on the 24th, thanking Premier Zhou Enlai for his congratulations on Prince Sihanouk's assumption of the office of Head of State of the Kingdom of Cambodia on the 20th. The telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China:

Your congratulatory telegram and your wishes on my assumption of the office of Head of State, at the unanimous request of our people, have deeply moved me, both in terms of the sublimity of the sentiments expressed and in terms of the indelible impression left by your and Vice Premier Chen Yi's recent visit. In expressing my most sincere and profound gratitude to you, I must reiterate to you, Mr. Premier, to the Chinese government, and to the Chinese people my most ardent thanks for their constant support and precious encouragement of Cambodia's struggle to safeguard freedom and for the prosperity of its people under all circumstances. I am convinced that the unbreakable unity of our two peoples, with common aspirations, will continue to develop in the interests of our peoples, and that this unity will be the most reliable guarantee of peace and happiness in this region of the world. Finally, please allow me to extend my warmest congratulations to you. I wish you new and lasting achievements in fulfilling your noble mission and in achieving the great tasks undertaken by your government.

Norodom Sihanouk
Head of State of Cambodia
June 24, 1960

### 西哈努克电谢刘主席周总理

1960-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    西哈努克电谢刘主席周总理
    新华社25日讯　柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王24日打电报给中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，感谢刘少奇主席20日对西哈努克亲王就任柬埔寨王国国家元首的祝贺。电文如下：中华人民共和国主席刘少奇先生阁下：
    非常感谢您在我出任国家元首时给我的贺电。主席先生，请您接受我十二万分的谢意，感谢这种对我个人的崇高声誉。我向阁下，向贵国政府，向伟大的中国人民重申我最热忱的谢意，感谢这又一次地，热情地肯定了联系其有共同爱好和平和自由思想的贵我两国的牢不可破的友谊。
    柬埔寨国家元首　诺罗敦·西哈努克　1960年6月24日
    新华社25日讯　柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王24日打电报给中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，感谢周恩来总理20日对西哈努克亲王就任柬埔寨王国国家元首的祝贺。电文如下：中华人民共和国总理周恩来先生阁下：
    在我按照我国人民一致要求下，出任国家元首之际，您的贺电和您的祝愿，无论是从表达的情感的崇高方面或者从回忆您和副总理陈毅元帅阁下最近一次的访问所留下的不可磨灭的印象方面都使我非常感动。在向您表示我最真诚、最深切谢意的同时，我仍需向您，总理先生，向中国政府，向中国人民重申我最热忱的谢意，感谢其在任何情况下，经常地支持和珍贵地鼓励柬埔寨进行为维护自由和为其人民的富强而斗争。我深信具有共同愿望的贵我两国人民的牢不可破的团结将本着我们人民的利益继续发展，这种团结将成为世界这一地区的和平和幸福的最可靠的保证。最后，请允许我向您致以最热烈的祝贺。祝在完成您崇高的使命中以及在实现贵国政府所进行的伟大任务中获得新的和持久的成就。
    柬埔寨国家元首诺　罗敦·西哈努克　1960年6月24日
