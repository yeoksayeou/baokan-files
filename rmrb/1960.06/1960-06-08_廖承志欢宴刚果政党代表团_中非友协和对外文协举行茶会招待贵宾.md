Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts a Banquet for Congolese Political Party Delegation; Sino-African Friendship Association and the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Host a Tea Party for Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-06-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome all members of the Congolese political party delegation currently visiting China, including prominent Congolese political leaders Alphonse Makwambala, Arthur Mayamba, and Henri Kassongo.

In his speech at the banquet, Liao Chengzhi extended a heartfelt welcome to the Congolese friends. He said that the Chinese people have always held the heroic struggle of the Congolese people for national independence and against colonialism in admiration and sympathy. He congratulated the Congolese people on their impending achievement of national independence after a long struggle. He also wished the Congolese people further success in safeguarding national independence and developing the national economy. Liao Chengzhi concluded by raising his glass to the victory of all African people in their struggle against imperialism, and to the friendship between the Chinese and Congolese people.

Present at the banquet this evening were: Mao Dun and Li Dequan, Vice-Chairmen of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity; Hu Yuzhi, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Nan Hanchen and Zhang Tiesheng, Vice-Presidents of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and Ding Xilin, Vice-President, as well as people from all walks of life: Meng Yongqian, Yang Shuo, Zheng Senyu, Wu Maosun, Deng Gang, Wu Xueqian, Zhang Mingyang, Wang Zhaohua, Yu Yixuan, and others.

### 廖承志欢宴刚果政党代表团  中非友协和对外文协举行茶会招待贵宾

1960-06-08
第6版()
专栏：

    廖承志欢宴刚果政党代表团
    中非友协和对外文协举行茶会招待贵宾
    新华社7日讯　中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志今晚设宴欢迎正在我国访问的刚果政党代表团全体成员，其中有刚果知名的政党领袖阿方斯·马克万巴拉、阿图尔·玛扬巴和亨利·卡松戈等人。
    廖承志在宴会上致词时对刚果的朋友们表示衷心的欢迎。他说，中国人民对刚果人民为争取民族独立和反对殖民主义者所作的英勇斗争是始终抱着敬佩和同情之心的。他祝贺刚果人民在经过长期斗争以后即将获得民族的独立。他还祝刚果人民在维护民族独立和发展民族经济等方面取得进一步的成就。廖承志最后举杯为全非洲人民反对帝国主义斗争的胜利，为中国和刚果人民之间的友谊，干杯。
    出席今晚宴会的有：中国亚非团结委员会副主席茅盾、李德全，中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，中国非洲人民友好协会副会长南汉宸、张铁生，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长丁西林，以及社会各界人士：孟用潜、杨朔、郑森禹、吴茂荪、邓岗、吴学谦、张明养、王照华、喻宜萱等。
    据新华社7日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会和中国人民对外文化协会今天下午联合举行茶会，招待正在我国访问的刚果政党访华代表团的全体成员，其中有刚果知名的政党领袖阿方斯·马克万巴拉、阿图尔·玛扬巴和亨利·卡松戈等人。
