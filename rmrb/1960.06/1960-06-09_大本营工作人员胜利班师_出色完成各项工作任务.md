Title: Base Camp Staff Triumphantly Return After Successfully Completing Various Tasks

Author: 
Date: 1960-06-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Qomolangma [Mount Everest] , 8th. The base camp staff of the Chinese Mountaineering Team, having completed their tasks, all left the base camp at the foot of Qomolangma at an altitude of 5,120 meters above sea level on the 7th, and returned to Lhasa via Xigazê.

This group of base camp staff arrived at the foot of Qomolangma first, undertaking the work of establishing camps and transporting materials, and are now escorting various materials of the mountaineering team, leaving here last. Among them are general affairs personnel, radio operators, medical personnel, cooks, and staff from various other professions.

### 大本营工作人员胜利班师  出色完成各项工作任务

1960-06-09
第3版()
专栏：

    大本营工作人员胜利班师
    出色完成各项工作任务
    新华社珠穆朗玛8日电　中国登山队的大本营工作人员，7日全部离开珠穆朗玛峰下拔海五千一百二十米的大本营，取道日喀则返回拉萨。
    这批大本营工作人员最先到达珠穆朗玛峰下，担负起建立营地和运输物资的工作，现在又押运着登山队的各种物资，最后离开这里。他们当中，有总务人员、无线电报务员、医务人员、炊事员和其他各行各业的工作人员。
