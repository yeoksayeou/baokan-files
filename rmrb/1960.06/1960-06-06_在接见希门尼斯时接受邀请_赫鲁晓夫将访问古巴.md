Title: Accepting Invitation During Meeting with Jiménez, Khrushchev to Visit Cuba
Author: 
Date: 1960-06-06
Page: 6

Accepting Invitation During Meeting with Jiménez
Khrushchev to Visit Cuba

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 3rd: According to TASS, Moscow, June 3rd: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, met with the head of the economic delegation of the government of the Republic of Cuba, Director of the National Agrarian Reform Institute of Cuba, Jiménez, on June 3rd.

Jiménez conveyed the greetings of the Prime Minister of the Cuban Revolutionary Government, Castro, to the Soviet people and to Khrushchev personally, and presented souvenirs to the head of the Soviet government.

Khrushchev expressed his gratitude to Jiménez and asked him to convey his own and the Soviet people's wishes to the Cuban people and Castro, wishing them success in their struggle for Cuba's freedom and independence. During the conversation, Jiménez, on behalf of Castro, invited the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union to visit Cuba. Khrushchev accepted the invitation and expressed his gratitude. The time of Khrushchev's visit to Cuba will be further negotiated.

### 在接见希门尼斯时接受邀请  赫鲁晓夫将访问古巴

1960-06-06
第6版()
专栏：

    在接见希门尼斯时接受邀请
    赫鲁晓夫将访问古巴
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科3日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫3日接见了古巴共和国政府经济代表团团长、古巴土改全国委员会主任希门尼斯。
    希门尼斯向苏联人民和赫鲁晓夫本人转达了古巴革命政府卡斯特罗总理的问候，并把纪念品赠予苏联政府首脑。
    赫鲁晓夫对希门尼斯表示感谢并请他转达苏联人民和他本人的祝愿，祝古巴人民和卡斯特罗在为古巴的自由和独立而进行的斗争中取得成就。谈话时，希门尼斯代表卡斯特罗邀请苏联部长会议主席到古巴进行访问。赫鲁晓夫接受了这个邀请并表示感谢。赫鲁晓夫访问古巴的时间将进一步协商决定。
