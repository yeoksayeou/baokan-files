Title: Sino-Indian Officials Begin Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-06-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 15th—Chinese and Indian officials began meeting in Beijing this afternoon. This meeting is being held in accordance with the provisions of the joint communiqué issued by the Prime Ministers of China and India in New Delhi.
    Participating in the meeting on the Chinese side were: Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yang Gongsu, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and advisors.
    Participating in the meeting on the Indian side were: J. S. Mehta, Director of the Northern Division of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, Gopal, Director of the Historical Division, and advisors.
    This morning, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, received the Indian officials J. S. Mehta and others who are participating in the Sino-Indian officials' meeting.

### 中印官员开始会晤

1960-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中印官员开始会晤
    新华社15日讯　中国和印度的官员今天下午在北京开始会晤。这次会晤是根据中印两国总理在新德里发表的联合公报的规定举行的。
    参加会晤的，中国方面有：外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋、西藏自治区筹备委员会外事处处长杨公素和顾问等。
    印度方面有：印度外交部北方司司长梅达、历史司司长戈帕尔和顾问等。
    今天上午，外交部副部长章汉夫接见了参加中印官员会晤的印度官员梅达等人。
