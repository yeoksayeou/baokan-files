Title: U.S. State Department Spokesman Publicly Declares the U.S. Will Continue to Occupy Okinawa
Author:
Date: 1960-06-20
Page: 5

    U.S. State Department Spokesman Publicly Declares
    The U.S. Will Continue to Occupy Okinawa
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, June 19th: On the eve of Eisenhower's arrival in Okinawa, U.S. officials and bourgeois propaganda organs are clamoring that the U.S. will continue to occupy Okinawa as a base to promote U.S. aggression and expansion policies in the Far East.
    A State Department spokesman, in a statement to reporters on the 18th, used the excuse of the so-called "increased tensions in the Far East" to clamor that the current situation "has reduced the chances of returning Okinawa to Japan in the foreseeable future." He said that Eisenhower will reaffirm U.S. policy in Okinawa.
    A dispatch from Washington by United Press International on the 18th clearly elaborated the logic of the U.S. aggressors, stating that the anti-U.S. struggle of the people of Asia, especially Japan, "increases the need for the United States to maintain strong military bases in the Far East." "Okinawa is a vital and powerful link in the chain of Western bases on the Asian arc extending from Alaska to the Philippines. This island bridges the important gap between Japan and Taiwan."

### 美国务院发言人公然宣布  美国要继续霸占冲绳

1960-06-20
第5版()
专栏：

    美国务院发言人公然宣布
    美国要继续霸占冲绳
    据新华社19日讯　华盛顿消息：在艾森豪威尔抵达冲绳前夕，美国官方和资产阶级宣传机关叫嚷美国要继续霸占冲绳作基地，推行美国在远东的侵略扩张政策。
    国务院发言人18日对新闻记者发表谈话时，借口所谓“远东紧张局势加剧”，叫嚣目前“已减少了在可以预见的将来把冲绳归还日本的机会”。他说，艾森豪威尔在冲绳将重申美国的政策。
    合众国际社18日从华盛顿发出的一条消息显然阐述了美国侵略者的逻辑，它说，亚洲、特别是日本人民的反美斗争“增加了美国在远东保持强大军事基地的需要”。“冲绳是从阿拉斯加伸延到菲律宾的亚洲弧线上的西方基地锁链的极重要和强大的一环。这个岛屿弥合了日本和台湾之间的重要缺口。”
