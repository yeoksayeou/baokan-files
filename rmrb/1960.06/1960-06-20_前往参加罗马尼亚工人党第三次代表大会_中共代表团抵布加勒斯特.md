Title: Chinese Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Bucharest to Attend the Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party
Author:
Date: 1960-06-20
Page: 1

    To Attend the Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party
    Chinese Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Bucharest
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 19th: The Chinese Communist Party delegation, invited to attend the Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party and led by Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, arrived here this morning by special plane.
    Greeting them at the airport were Gheorghe Apostol, member of the Political Bureau of the Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee, Central Committee members Si. Bughici, Du. Coliuver Danarak, Li. Salagian and others. Xu Jianguo, the Chinese Ambassador to Romania, embassy staff, and representatives of students also went to the airport to welcome them.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 19th: The Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, which is going to Romania to attend the Third Congress of the Workers' Party, left Moscow by plane this morning for Romania.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off were Mukhitdinov, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Ustinov, First Secretary of the Moscow City Committee, Medvedev, Deputy Minister of the Central Committee of the CPSU, Stan, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in the Soviet Union, and Zhang Weilie, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, and others.

### 前往参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会  中共代表团抵布加勒斯特

1960-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    前往参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会
    中共代表团抵布加勒斯特
    新华社布加勒斯特19日电　应邀前来参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会的中国共产党代表团，在中共中央政治局委员彭真率领下，今天上午乘专机到达这里。
    前往机场欢迎的有罗马尼亚工人党中央政治局委员格·阿波斯托尔、中央委员西·布吉奇、杜·柯柳弗·达纳拉克、利·萨拉扬等。中国驻罗马尼亚大使许建国和使馆人员、留学生代表也前往机场欢迎。
    新华社莫斯科19日电　前往罗马尼亚参加工人党第三次代表大会的中国共产党代表团，由中国共产党中央政治局委员彭真率领，已于今天上午离开莫斯科乘机去罗马尼亚。
    前往机场送行的有苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员穆希金诺夫、莫斯科市委员会第一书记乌斯季诺夫、苏共中央副部长麦德维杰夫、罗马尼亚驻苏使馆临时代办斯坦，以及中国驻苏使馆临时代办张伟烈等人。
