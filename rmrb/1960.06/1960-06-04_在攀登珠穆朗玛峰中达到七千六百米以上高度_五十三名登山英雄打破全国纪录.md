Title: Fifty-Three Mountaineering Heroes Break National Record by Reaching Heights Above Seven Thousand Six Hundred Meters in the Ascent of Mount Everest
Author:
Date: 1960-06-04
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Qomolangma [Mount Everest], 3rd. According to final statistics, during the process of conquering Mount Everest, a total of fifty-three athletes from the Chinese mountaineering team reached heights above seven thousand six hundred meters above sea level, breaking the national record of seven thousand five hundred and ninety meters for the highest altitude reached by Chinese men in mountaineering. This record was set by athletic champions Shi Zhanchun and five others in 1957 during the conquest of Mount Gongga in Sichuan.

The Chinese Mountaineering Team was formed at the end of last year, with a total of two hundred and fourteen members (including logistics and other staff). Some of them participated in the mountaineering activities. In addition to three people reaching the summit of Mount Everest at eight thousand eight hundred and eighty-two meters above sea level, twenty-five others reached heights above eight thousand one hundred meters above sea level. This is a rare achievement in the history of world mountaineering.

The fifty-three athletes who broke the national record this time and the heights they reached are:

Three people reached eight thousand eight hundred and eighty-two meters above sea level: Wang Fuzhou, Qu Yinhua, Gongbu (Tibetan).

One person reached eight thousand seven hundred meters above sea level: Liu Lianman.

Two people reached eight thousand six hundred meters above sea level: Shi Zhanchun, Wang Fengtong.

Twelve people reached eight thousand five hundred meters above sea level: Xu Jing, Duo Jia (Tibetan), Shi Jing, Laba Cairen (Tibetan), Wu Zongyue, Qunbei Jianzan (Tibetan), Suonam Duoji (Tibetan), Mima (Tibetan, soldier), Yun Deng (Tibetan), Ci Ren (Tibetan), Mima Zhaxi (Tibetan), Que Jia (Tibetan).

Ten people reached eight thousand one hundred meters above sea level: Zhang Junyan, Liu Dayi, Heng Hulin, Cheng Tianliang, Ma Baochang, Duojifu (Tibetan), Xie Wucheng (Tibetan), Tamujun (Tibetan), Zhaxi (Tibetan, thirty-three years old), Mima (Tibetan, stonemason).

Twenty-five people reached seven thousand six hundred meters above sea level: Chen San, Wang Fubao, Zeng Shusheng, Lei Yaorong, Duoji (Tibetan), Suolang (Tibetan), Luobu (Tibetan), Danzeng (Tibetan), Luosang (Tibetan, soldier), Sangzhu (Tibetan), Zhaxi Wangdui (Tibetan), Gajiong Qiongbei (Tibetan), Dawa Ciren (Tibetan), Zhaxi (Tibetan, twenty-five years old), Zhaxi (Tibetan, twenty-seven years old), Zhaxi (Tibetan, thirty-four years old), Qiongda (Tibetan), Dawa Tongzhu (Tibetan), Luosang (Tibetan, farmer), Bian Anmin, Damuzhen (Tibetan), Zhang Xiaolu, Bianba Ciren (Tibetan), Ci Wang (Tibetan), Zhawang (Tibetan).

### 在攀登珠穆朗玛峰中达到七千六百米以上高度  五十三名登山英雄打破全国纪录

1960-06-04
第7版()
专栏：

    在攀登珠穆朗玛峰中达到七千六百米以上高度
    五十三名登山英雄打破全国纪录
    新华社珠穆朗玛3日电　根据最后统计，中国登山队在征服珠穆朗玛峰的过程中，一共有五十三名运动员到达了拔海七千六百米以上的高度，打破了七千五百九十米的我国男子登山高度的最高纪录，这个纪录是由运动健将史占春等六人1957年在征服四川贡嘎山时创造的。
    中国登山队是在去年年底组成的，全队共二百一十四人（包括后勤等工作人员），其中一部分参加登山活动，除有三人达到珠穆朗玛峰拔海八千八百八十二米的顶峰外，还有二十五人达到了拔海八千一百米以上的高度，这是世界登山史上罕见的成就。
    这次打破全国纪录的五十三名运动员和他们所达到的高度是：
    到达拔海八千八百八十二米的共三人：王富洲、屈银华、贡布（藏族）。
    到达拔海八千七百米一人：刘连满。
    到达拔海八千六百米二人：史占春、王凤桐。
    到达拔海八千五百米十二人：许竞、多加（藏族）、石竞、拉八才仁（藏族）、邬宗岳、群贝坚赞（藏族）、索南多吉（藏族）、米马（藏族，战士）、云登（藏族）、茨仁（藏族）、米马扎西（藏族）、却加（藏族）。
    到达拔海八千一百米十人：张俊岩、刘大义、衡虎林、成天亮、马保昌、多吉甫（藏族）、谢武成（藏族）、塔木君（藏族）、扎西（藏族，三十三岁）、米马（藏族、石匠）。
    到达拔海七千六百米二十五人：陈三、王富葆、曾曙生、雷耀荣、多吉（藏族）、索郎（藏族）、罗布（藏族）、丹增（藏族）、洛桑（藏族，战士）、桑珠（藏族）、扎西旺堆（藏族）、嘎吉穷白（藏族）、达娃茨仁（藏族）、扎西（藏族，二十五岁）、扎西（藏族、二十七岁）、扎西（藏族，三十四岁）、穷达（藏族）、达娃同珠（藏族）、洛桑（藏族，农民）、边安民、搭木真（藏族）、张小路、边巴茨仁（藏族）、茨旺（藏族）、扎旺（藏族）。
