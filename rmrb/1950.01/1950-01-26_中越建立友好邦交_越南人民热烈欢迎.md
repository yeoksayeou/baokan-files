Title: China and Vietnam Establish Friendly Diplomatic Relations; The Vietnamese People Warmly Welcome It

Author:

Date: 1950-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 24th: The Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, reported on the 23rd that the news of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has been warmly welcomed by people throughout Vietnam. Residents and people's organizations in Hanoi, Thai Binh, Tourane, Ha Tinh, and other provinces have sent telegrams in recent days supporting the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Vietnam.

### 中越建立友好邦交  越南人民热烈欢迎

1950-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中越建立友好邦交
    越南人民热烈欢迎
    【新华社北京二十四日电】越南通讯社北越二十三日讯：中华人民共和国与越南民主共和国之间建立外交关系的消息，受到越南各地人民的热烈欢迎。河内、太平、土伦、河静及其他省份的居民和人民团体，这几天纷纷致电拥护中越建立邦交。
