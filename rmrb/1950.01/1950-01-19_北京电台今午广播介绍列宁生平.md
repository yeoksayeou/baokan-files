Title: Beijing Radio to Broadcast Lenin's Biography This Afternoon
Author:
Date: 1950-01-19
Page: 3

    Beijing Radio to Broadcast Lenin's Biography This Afternoon
    [This newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the following programs today (the 19th): 17:30, Women's Time: Correspondence "They Started Making Tofu"; 18:30, Popular Science Lecture: Academician Vavilov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences' paper "The Alliance of Labor and Science in the Stalin Era"; 18:00, First Literary Program: Lianhualuo "Granny Li Watches Opera"; 21:00, Second Literary Program: New Ping Opera "New Dog Beating Persuades Husband". 18:45 New Songs: "Ode to Leader Mao Zedong", "Songs of the Liberated Areas", etc.
    [In addition] Beijing People's Radio, 12:30, Workers' Program: 1. Trade Union Work: Xu Ping talks about "The Situation of Implementing Trade Unions by Everyone at the Jingwei Weaving Factory"; 2. Worker Services: ① Introduction to the life of Lenin, the great revolutionary teacher of the world, ② Answering the question of why Peking Opera is not prohibited in factories now. 13:00 Listener Services: ① Answering questions about the People's Victory Indexed Bonds, ② Answering questions about indexed savings, ③ Answering questions about how to prevent electric wires from catching fire, ④ Notice from the Music Correspondence Department of the National Association of Musicians, National Central Conservatory of Music. 16:30 Children's Program: (1) Current Affairs Talk, (2) Children from the Central National Primary School in the Seventh District Sing. 17:00 Teaching New Songs "Bright Red Lantern". 19:15 Voice of the Municipality: ① The Statistics Office of the Bureau of Public Health introduces "Beijing's Vital Statistics Work", ② "Fire Prevention Knowledge".

### 北京电台今午广播介绍列宁生平

1950-01-19
第3版()
专栏：

    北京电台今午广播介绍列宁生平
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（十九）天十七点三十分妇女时间：通讯“她们做起豆腐来了”。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫的论文“斯大林时代劳动与科学的联盟”。十八点第一次文艺节目：莲花落《李大娘看戏》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：新评戏《新打狗劝夫》。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《歌颂领袖毛泽东》、《解放区的歌声》等。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：一、工会工作：许平讲“经纬织布工厂实行大家办工会的情形”，二、工人服务：①介绍全世界伟大革命导师列宁的生平，②回答现在工厂里为什么不禁止演京戏的问题。十三点听众服务：①答关于人民胜利折实公债的问题，②答关于折实储蓄的问题，③答怎样防止电线走火的问题，④全国音协国立中央音乐学院音乐问题通讯部通告。十六点三十分儿童节目：（一）时事讲话，（二）七区中心国民小学小朋友们唱歌。十七点教唱新歌《鲜红的灯》。十九点十五分市政之声：①公共卫生局统计室介绍“北京市的生命统计工作”，②“防火常识”。
