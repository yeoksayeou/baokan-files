Title: Our Army Liberates Sanmen Pass Between Aotou and Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1950-01-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 15th. A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army, in coordination with local armed forces, liberated Sanmen Pass, an important island between Aotou and Hong Kong, on the 6th, completely annihilating the remnants of the bandit Ling Bingquan, totaling 250 bandits including bandit regiment leader Liang Guoxing; seizing 8 machine guns of various types, 212 rifles, a motorboat, and a portion of other military supplies.

### 澳头香港间我军解放三门关

1950-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    澳头香港间我军解放三门关
    【新华社上海十五日电】人民解放军某部在地方武装配合下，于六日解放澳头至香港间的重要岛屿三门关，全歼海匪凌炳权残部，计匪团长梁国兴以下二百五十人；缴各式机枪八挺，步枪二百十二支，电船一艘，其他军用品一部。
