Title: Zhou Enlai's Telegram to the United Nations: Prominently Published in Warsaw Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1950-01-12
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th - According to the Polish News Agency, Warsaw: On the 9th, Warsaw newspapers prominently published, with top billing, a telegram from Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, to UN General Assembly President Romulo, UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie, and the member states of the Security Council, demanding the expulsion of the Kuomintang clique's representatives from the Security Council. Warsaw newspapers ran headlines spanning four columns, stating: The presence of Kuomintang representatives in the Security Council is illegal.

### 周外长致联合国电文  华沙各报均显著刊载

1950-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    周外长致联合国电文
    华沙各报均显著刊载
    【新华社北京十一日电】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：华沙各报九日以首要地位，刊载中国中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长致联合国大会主席罗慕洛、联大秘书长赖伊及安全理事会各理事国要求从安理会中开除国民党集团代表的电文。华沙报占四栏的标题称：国民党代表出席安理会是非法的。
