Author:

    [This newspaper dispatch] Central People's Broadcasting Station tonight (11th) 17:30 Youth Time: (1) Have a good winter vacation, (2) How rural Youth League cadres and members should conduct winter training. 18:30 Natural Science Common Sense Lecture: Common Sense of Electricity, Lecture 3, "Induction Charging." 18:00 First Literary Program: Lianhualuo "Mother Ma Investigates Spies." 21:00 Second Literary Program: New Peking Opera "The Third Attack on Zhujiazhuang," Part 2 of the Third Attack. 18:45 Broadcasting new songs: "The Army Marches Forward," "Singing of Chairman Mao," etc. 19:45 Broadcasting Peking Opera: "Guangtaizhuang," etc.
    [Further dispatch] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Staff Program: (1) Continue teaching the song "Support the Issuance of People's Victory Indexed Bonds," (2) Production situation of the porcelain factory of Dahua Pottery Company: "Work hard and build a new China." 13:00 Youth Program: Correspondence from the Revolutionary University "Gains from Studying Internationalism." 16:30 Children's Program: Sun Jingxiu tells the Soviet fairy tale "The Story of Three Daughters." 17:00 Music Program: Municipal No. 8 Middle School sings new songs.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-01-11
第3版()
专栏：

    中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（十一）晚十七点三十分青年时间：（一）过好寒假，（二）农村青年团干部和团员怎样进行冬训。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：电学常识第三讲《感应带电》。十八点第一次文艺节目：莲花落《马大娘探子》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：新京剧《三打祝家庄》第三打第二部分。十八点四十五分播送新歌曲：《军队向前进》，《歌唱毛主席》等。十九点四十五分播送京剧：《广太庄》等。
