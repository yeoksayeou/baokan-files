Title: Democratic National Construction Association Beijing Branch New Knowledge Forum: Ai Siqi to Speak on Learning Issues Today

Author: 
Date: 1950-01-27
Page: 3

    Democratic National Construction Association Beijing Branch New Knowledge Forum
    Ai Siqi to Speak on Learning Issues Today
    [Our Newspaper] The New Knowledge Forum of the Beijing Branch of the Democratic National Construction Association will hold its 43rd gathering at the Europe and America Alumni Association at 7:00 PM today.  Professor Ai Siqi of the Marx-Lenin Academy has been specially invited to speak on learning issues. The focus of this lecture will be: 1. What is learning? 2. Why learn? 3. How to learn?

### 民建京分会新知识座谈会  今请艾思奇讲学习问题

1950-01-27
第3版()
专栏：

    民建京分会新知识座谈会
    今请艾思奇讲学习问题
    【本报讯】民主建国会北京市分会新知识座谈会订今日午后七时在欧美同学会举行第四十三次集会，特邀请马列学院教授艾思奇讲述学习问题，此次讲述重点是：一、什么叫学习？二、为什么学习？三、如何学习？
