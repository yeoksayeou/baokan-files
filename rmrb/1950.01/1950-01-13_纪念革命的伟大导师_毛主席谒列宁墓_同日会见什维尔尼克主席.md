Title: Commemorating the Great Teacher of the Revolution, Chairman Mao Visits Lenin's Mausoleum; Meets Chairman Shvernik on the Same Day
Author:
Date: 1950-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th: TASS, Moscow Dispatch: On January 11th, Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited Lenin's Mausoleum and laid a wreath. The inscription on the wreath was written in Chinese and Russian as follows:

    Dedicated to Lenin – The Great Teacher of the Revolution.
    Mao Zedong, January 11, 1950

Mao Zedong was accompanied on his visit to the mausoleum by Wang Jiaxiang, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and Professor Chen Boda.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th: According to a TASS Moscow dispatch on January 11th, Nikolai Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, received Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, on January 11th. Also present were Professor Chen Boda, Wang Jiaxiang, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and Gorkin, the Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

### 纪念革命的伟大导师  毛主席谒列宁墓  同日会见什维尔尼克主席

1950-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念革命的伟大导师
    毛主席谒列宁墓
    同日会见什维尔尼克主席
    【新华社北京十二日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：一月十一日，中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东谒列宁墓，并献花圈，花圈上以中俄文题字如下：
    献给列宁——革命的伟大导师。
    毛泽东  一九五○年一月十一日
    陪同毛泽东前往谒墓的，有中华人民共和国驻苏大使王稼祥及陈伯达教授。
    【新华社北京十二日电】据塔斯社莫斯科十一日电，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克于一月十一日邀见中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东。当时在座者尚有陈伯达教授、中华人民共和国驻苏联大使王稼祥和苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金。
