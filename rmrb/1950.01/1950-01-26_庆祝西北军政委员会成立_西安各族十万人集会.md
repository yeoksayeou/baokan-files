Title: Ten Thousand People of All Ethnic Groups in Xi'an Gather to Celebrate the Establishment of the Northwest Military and Political Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-01-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 25th. Xi'an News: 100,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Xi'an City solemnly gathered on the 19th to celebrate the establishment of the Northwest Military and Political Committee. The venue was decorated with colorful flags, and the surrounding area was plastered with huge banners written in the five ethnic languages of Han, Uyghur, Tibetan, Hui, and Mongolian. After the chairman, Xi'an Deputy Mayor Fang Zhongru, delivered the opening speech, Chairman Peng Dehuai of the Northwest Military and Political Committee stepped onto the stage amidst warm applause. He pointed out that the liberation of the Great Northwest should be attributed to Chairman Mao's wise and correct leadership, the heroic struggle of the People's Liberation Army, and the active support of the people of the whole country. He then stated that the Great Northwest has now entered a period of construction and that a large-scale production movement should be launched, coupled with cultural and educational construction, to improve the people's living standards and cultural level, and to build a democratic, free, ethnically united, prosperous, and happy new Northwest. Vice Chairman Xi Zhongxun then spoke, calling on Communist Party members to become models in implementing various policies. Vice Chairman Zhang Zhizhong, in his speech, expressed his hope that people of all ethnic groups and all walks of life would provide supervision and assistance to the Northwest Military and Political Committee and governments at all levels at any time. Other speakers included Uyghur member Bao Erhan, Tibetan member Xirao Jiacuo, Hui member Ma Hongbin, and Mongolian member Bai Haifeng, all members of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, who unanimously called on people of all ethnic groups to unite and cooperate to jointly build the Great Northwest. Workers' representative Li Delin and farmers' representative Wang Pinxian also firmly stated at the meeting that they would strengthen industrial and agricultural production as a gift to celebrate the establishment of the Northwest Military and Political Committee.

### 庆祝西北军政委员会成立  西安各族十万人集会

1950-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝西北军政委员会成立
    西安各族十万人集会
    【新华社北京二十五日电】西安讯：西安市各族各界十万人民，于十九日隆重集会庆祝西北军政委员会成立。会场上彩旗招展，周围贴着用汉、维、藏、回、蒙五族文字写成的巨幅标语。主席西安市副市长方仲如致开幕词后，西北军政委员会彭德怀主席在热烈掌声中登台讲话。他指出大西北的解放，要归功于毛主席英明正确的领导，人民解放军的英勇奋战及全国人民的积极支援。继称，今日大西北已转进建设的时期，要发动大生产运动，并配合文化教育建设，以提高人民的生活和文化水平，建设民主、自由、民族团结、繁荣和幸福的新西北。习仲勋副主席继起讲话，他号召共产党员要成为执行各项政策的模范。张治中副主席在讲话中，希望各族各界人民随时给西北军政委员会和各级人民政府以督促和帮助。其他讲话者还有西北军政委员会维族鲍尔汉委员、藏族喜饶嘉错委员、回族马鸿宾委员、蒙族白海风委员，他们一致号召各族人民团结合作，共同建设大西北。工人代表李德林、农民代表王品贤亦在会上坚决表示，将以加强工农生产，作为庆祝西北军政委员会成立的礼物。
