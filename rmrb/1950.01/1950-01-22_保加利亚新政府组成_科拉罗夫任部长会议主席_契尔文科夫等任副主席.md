Title: Bulgarian New Government Formed, Kolarov Appointed Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Chervenkov and Others Appointed Vice-Chairmen
Author:
Date: 1950-01-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia: TASS reported that the Bulgarian National Assembly's meeting on the 19th unanimously approved the list of government members proposed by Kolarov. The new government list is as follows: Chairman of the Council of Ministers Kolarov, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chervenkov, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs Poptomov, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers Damyanov, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers Traykov, Chairman of the State Planning Commission Lukanov, Chairman of the State Control Commission Titchev, Minister of Internal Affairs Khristozov, Minister of Education Dr. Dramaliyev, Minister of Finance Lazarov, Minister of Justice Najdenov, Minister of People's Defense Lieutenant General Damyanov, Minister of Domestic Trade Bilovski, Minister of Foreign Trade Ganev, Minister of Agriculture Chernokolev, Minister of Forestry Popov, Minister of Construction and Highways Ivanov, Minister of Public Utilities and Urban Development Kamenov, Minister of Transport Zhankov, Minister of Communications Dragoicheva, Minister of Industry and Acting Minister of Mining and Natural Resources Yugov, Minister of Electrification and Land Reclamation Georgiev, Minister of Public Health Kolarov, Minister of Labor and Social Insurance Tebishev, Chairman of the Committee on Science, Arts and Culture Professor Ganovski. When the new government list was approved, members of the National Assembly responded with stormy applause. The members of the new government also took the oath.

### 保加利亚新政府组成  科拉罗夫任部长会议主席  契尔文科夫等任副主席

1950-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚新政府组成
    科拉罗夫任部长会议主席
    契尔文科夫等任副主席
    【新华社北京二十一日电】塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚国民议会十九日的会议一致通过了科拉罗夫所提出的政府组成名单。新政府名单如下：部长会议主席科拉罗夫，部长会议副主席契尔文科夫，部长会议副主席及外交部长波普托莫夫，部长会议副主席达姆扬诺夫，部长会议副主席特莱科夫，国家计划委员会主席卢卡诺夫，国家监察委员会主席季切夫，内政部长克里斯托佐夫，教育部长德拉马里也夫博士，财政部长拉查罗夫，司法部长纳杰诺夫，人民国防部长达米扬诺夫中将，国内贸易部长比洛夫斯基，对外贸易部长加涅夫，农业部长切尔诺科列夫，森林部长波波夫，建设与公路部长伊凡诺夫，公用事业与城市发展部长卡门诺夫，运输部长詹科夫，交通部长德拉哥伊切娃，工业部长及代理矿业与天然资源部长于哥夫，电气化与垦拓部长乔吉也夫，公共卫生部长柯拉洛夫，劳动与社会保险部长特比舍夫，科学艺术文化委员会主席加诺夫斯基教授。新政府名单通过时，国民议会议员们报以暴风雨般的掌声。新政府的委员们并举行了宣誓。
