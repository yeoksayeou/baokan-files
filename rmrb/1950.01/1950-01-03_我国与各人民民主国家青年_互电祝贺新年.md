Title: Chinese and People's Democratic Countries' Youth Exchange New Year Greetings
Author:
Date: 1950-01-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 2nd: The youth of China and various new democratic countries have exchanged New Year's greetings. The telegrams are as follows:

(1) Telegram from the Polish Youth Union to the All-China Democratic Youth Federation:
	We, on behalf of the Polish youth, extend our heartfelt greetings to you and wish you a Happy New Year. We wish you more brilliant victories in the battle to finally defeat the reactionary armed forces in your country, and wish you success in building a new and happy People's Republic of China under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and your great leader Comrade Mao Zedong.
	Long live the heroic Chinese youth!
	Long live the solidarity of progressive youth around the world!
			Polish Youth Union, December 29th

(2) Telegram from the All-China Democratic Youth Federation to the Polish Youth Union:
	Chinese youth celebrate your fruitful New Year and wish you success in your work towards socialism.
		All-China Democratic Youth Federation, Liao Chengzhi, December 31st

(3) Telegram from the Central Committee of the Czech Youth Union to the All-China Democratic Youth Federation:
	Happy New Year, and wish you great success in the important work of consolidating world peace.
			Central Committee of the Czech Youth Union, December 23rd

(4) Telegram from the All-China Democratic Youth Federation to the Central Committee of the Czech Youth Union:
	Chinese youth wish you a Happy New Year and wish you success in building socialism to consolidate the forces of world peace.
		All-China Democratic Youth Federation, Liao Chengzhi, December 31st

(5) Telegram from the Bulgarian Dimitrov Youth League to the All-China Democratic Youth Federation:
	On behalf of the Bulgarian Dimitrov Youth League, we extend our fighting salute to you and wish you a Happy New Year. We wish you success in your work and wish the people's regime under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party increasingly consolidated, in order to finally defeat the Kuomintang group, the running dogs of American and British imperialism.
	Long live the People's Republic of China!
	Long live international youth solidarity!
	Long live Stalin, the great leader of the Soviet Union and progressive mankind!
		Bulgarian Dimitrov Youth League, December 28th

(6) Telegram from the All-China Democratic Youth Federation to the Bulgarian Dimitrov Youth League:
	Chinese youth celebrate the victorious New Year of the heroic Dimitrov Youth and wish you success in your successful entry into socialism.
		All-China Democratic Youth Federation, Liao Chengzhi, December 31st

(7) Telegram from the All-China Democratic Youth Federation to the Hungarian People's Youth League:
	Chinese youth wish you a Happy New Year and wish you greater success in your journey towards socialism.
		All-China Democratic Youth Federation, Liao Chengzhi, December 31st

(8) Telegram from the Central Committee of the Romanian Labor Youth Union to the All-China Democratic Youth Federation:
	On behalf of the labor youth of the Romanian People's Republic, we extend our warm greetings to you and wish you a Happy New Year. We sincerely wish the heroic people and youth of the People's Republic of China complete victory next year, and wish you brilliant success in the peaceful construction work for the happiness and joy of the Chinese working people.
		Central Committee of the Romanian Labor Youth Union, December 28th

(9) Telegram from the All-China Democratic Youth Federation to the Central Committee of the Romanian Labor Youth Union:
	Chinese youth warmly wish you a Happy New Year and wish you success in your successful entry into socialism.
		All-China Democratic Youth Federation, Liao Chengzhi, December 31st

(10) Telegram from the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Youth League to the All-China Democratic Youth Federation:
	Albanian labor youth wish the heroic Chinese youth a Happy New Year and wish you success in the struggle to liberate all of China, success in the cause of building the foundation of socialism, and success in the cause of defending world peace.
		Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Youth League, December 29th

(11) Telegram from the All-China Democratic Youth Federation to the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Youth League:
	Chinese youth extend our fighting salute to you and wish you a Happy New Year and wish you success in building socialism.
		All-China Democratic Youth Federation, Liao Chengzhi, December 31st

(12) Telegram from the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League to the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League:
	To Comrade Feng Wenbin of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League:
	As we welcome the New Year full of light and hope, I am honored to extend fraternal greetings to you and the millions of democratic youth under your leadership with the brotherhood of all Korean youth.
	The growth of the world's youth forces and the victory of the Chinese youth have inspired us and filled us with the confidence of victory to welcome the New Year, and we are determined to strengthen the struggle for national reunification and democratic development, and hope that you who are fighting for a new, democratic, prosperous and strong People's Republic of China will achieve more brilliant victories and further consolidate the friendship between the youth of China and North Korea.
		Hyon Jung-min, Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League
		December 28, 1949

(13) Telegram from the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League to the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League:
	Thank you for your telegram. As we welcome the arrival of 1950, on behalf of the Chinese youth, I would like to wish the Korean youth a Happy New Year and wish the Korean youth greater victories in the struggle for national reunification, democracy and economic construction.
		Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, December 31st

### 我国与各人民民主国家青年  互电祝贺新年

1950-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    我国与各人民民主国家青年
    互电祝贺新年
    【新华社北京二日电】我国和各新民主主义国家的青年，互致新年贺电。电文如下：
            （一）波兰青年联合会致中华全国民主青年联合总会的贺电：
    我们代表波兰青年向你们致衷心的敬礼，恭贺新禧，祝你们在最后击败你们国内反动武装力量的战斗中，获得更辉煌的胜利。并祝你们在中国共产党与你们国家伟大的领袖毛泽东同志的领导下，胜利地建设一个新的幸福的中华人民共和国。
    英勇的中国青年万岁！
    全世界进步青年团结万岁！
                    波兰青年联合会  十二月二十九日
   （二）中华全国民主青年联合总会致波兰青年联合会的贺电：
    中国青年庆祝你们成绩丰富的新年，祝你们走向社会主义的工作成功。
          中华全国民主青年联合总会廖承志  十二月三十一日
  （三）捷克青年联合会中央委员会致中华全国民主青年联合总会的贺电：
    恭贺新禧，并祝你们在巩固全世界和平的重要工作中获得很大的成功。
                    捷克青年联合会中央委员会  十二月二十三日
  （四）中华全国民主青年联合总会致捷克青年联合会中央委员会的贺电：
    中国青年向你们恭贺新禧，并祝建设社会主义成功，以巩固世界和平力量。
            中华全国民主青年联合总会廖承志  十二月三十一日
   （五）保加利亚季米特洛夫青年团致中华全国民主青年联合总会
        的贺电：
    我们谨代表保加利亚季米特洛夫青年团，向你们致战斗的敬礼，恭贺新禧，祝你们工作成功，并祝在中国共产党领导下的人民政权日益巩固，以期最后击败美英帝国主义走狗国民党集团。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    国际青年团结万岁！
    苏联及进步人类的伟大领袖斯大林万岁！
               保加利亚季米特洛夫青年团  十二月二十八日
    （六）中华全国民主青年联合总会致保加利亚季米特洛夫青年团
        的贺电：
    中国青年庆贺英勇的季米特洛夫青年胜利的新年，并祝你们成功地进入社会主义。
           中华全国民主青年联合总会廖承志  十二月三十一日
    （七）中华全国民主青年联合总会致匈牙利人民青年联盟的贺电：
    中国青年祝贺你们新年，祝你们在走向社会主义中获得更大成功。
          中华全国民主青年联合总会廖承志  十二月三十一日
    （八）罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会致中华全国民主青年联
        合总会的贺电：
    我们谨代表罗马尼亚人民共和国劳动青年向你们致热烈的敬礼，恭贺新禧。我们衷心地祝中华人民共和国英勇的人民和青年，明年获得彻底胜利，并祝你们在为中国劳动人民的幸福与快乐所进行的和平建设事业中，获得辉煌的成功。
             罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会  十二月二十八日
   （九）中华全国民主青年联合总会致罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟中央
        委员会的贺电：
    中国青年热烈地向你们恭贺新禧，祝你们成功地进入社会主义。
          中华全国民主青年联合总会廖承志  十二月三十一日
    （十）阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会致中华全国民主青年
        联合总会的贺电：
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年向英勇的中国青年恭贺新禧，祝你们解放全中国的斗争成功，建设社会主义基础的事业成功，保卫世界和平的事业成功。
          阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会  十二月二十九日
    （十一）中华全国民主青年联合总会致阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟
        中央委员会的贺电：
    中国青年向你们致战斗的敬礼，恭贺新禧，并祝你们成功地建设社会主义。
         中华全国民主青年联合总会廖承志  十二月三十一日
    （十二）北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会致中国新民主主义青年
        团中央委员会的贺电：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会冯文彬同志：
    在迎接充满着光明与希望的新年之际，谨以全体朝鲜青年兄弟之谊，向您和您所领导的数百万民主青年，致以兄弟的祝贺，我感到非常的光荣。
    世界青年力量的成长和中国青年的胜利鼓舞了我们，使我们充满了胜利的信心来迎接新年，并决心加强为祖国统一与民主发展的斗争，并希望为新生的、民主富强的中华人民共和国而斗争的您们得到更辉煌的胜利，更进一步巩固朝中两国青年之间的友谊。
                 北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会委员长玄正民
                               一九四九年十二月二十八日
    （十三）中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会致北朝鲜民主青年同
        盟中央委员会的贺电：
    谢谢你们的贺电，在迎接一九五○年来到时，谨代表中国青年向朝鲜青年庆贺新年，并祝朝鲜青年在争取祖国统一、民主及进行经济建设中，取得更大的胜利。
         中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会  十二月三十一日
