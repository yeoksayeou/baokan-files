Title: Increased Operating Hours for Trolleybuses on Lines 1 and 2 in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-01-01
Page: 2

    Increased Operating Hours for Trolleybuses on Lines 1 and 2 in Beijing
    [Beijing News] The Electric Trolleybus Company, in order to celebrate the first New Year after the founding of the new China, has decided to advance and extend the departure and return times of the trolleybuses on Lines 1 and 2 by half an hour, starting from New Year's Day. The first trolleybus will leave the depot at 5:30 in the morning, and the last trolleybus will return to the depot at 10:30 in the evening.

### 京一、二路电车增加行驶时间

1950-01-01
第2版()
专栏：

    京一、二路电车增加行驶时间
    【北京讯】电车公司为了庆祝新中国诞生后的第一个新年，决定自元旦起，将第一、第二路电车的出入厂时间，分别提前和延长半点钟，首车早晨五点半出厂，末车晚十点半入厂。
