Title: Beijing Telecommunications Bureau Employees Purchase Over Ten Thousand Victory Bonds
Author:
Date: 1950-01-18
Page: 4

    The Beijing Telecommunications Bureau employees have launched a wave of purchasing Victory Bonds, exceeding 12,000 subscriptions to date. Many comrades in Telephone Bureau No. 7 deposited their salaries in the People's Bank last month in preparation for buying the bonds. The bureau's more than 3,500 employees subscribed to over 12,400 copies in the first phase of issuance, averaging more than four shares per person. The No. 3 Bureau's branch union averaged more than five shares purchased per person.
                                   (Telecommunications Bureau Correspondent Group)

### 京电信局职工买公债逾万份

1950-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    京电信局职工买公债逾万份
    【本报讯】北京电信局职工掀起了购买胜利公债的热潮，截至目前为止已突破一万二千分。电话七局的许多同志们在上月发薪后，即将钱预存入人民银行，准备买公债。该局三千五百多职工，在第一期发行中就认购了一万二千四百多分，平均每人合四分多。三局分会平均每人购到五分以上。
                                   （电信局通联组）
