Title: An Engineering Regiment of the Second Field Army Participates in the Construction of a Railway Bridge

Author:

Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, January 27th: An engineering regiment of the Special Forces Column of the People's Liberation Army's Second Field Army is enthusiastically responding to Chairman Mao's call and actively participating in national economic construction. They arrived at a certain location on the 16th of this month to participate in the construction of a railway bridge. After receiving orders from their superiors to collaborate with the Central and Southern China Highway Bureau in constructing a permanent large railway bridge, all commanders and soldiers regarded participating in the construction of the new China as a supreme honor and were in high spirits. They had boundless confidence in completing the new task, and actively made plans and challenged each other. On the 10th of this month, the units directly under the Special Forces Column held a grand send-off meeting, and the local people also enthusiastically saw them off. After the unit arrived in the engineering area, they were warmly welcomed by the Central and Southern China Highway Bureau, the Bridge Construction Project Office, and the Bridge Workers' Team. All commanders and soldiers have now begun intensive work.

### 第二野战军某工兵团参加修建铁桥工程

1950-01-29
第2版()
专栏：

    第二野战军某工兵团参加修建铁桥工程
    【新华社重庆二十七日电】人民解放军第二野战军特种兵纵队某工兵团热烈响应毛主席号召，积极参加国家经济建设，已于本月十六日到达某地参加修建铁桥工程。该团接奉上级命令协同华中南公路局修建一座永久性大铁桥后，全体指战员对参加新中国建设工作引为无上光荣，情绪极高，并对新任务之完成具有无限信心，纷纷订计划，互相挑战应战。本月十日，特种兵纵队直属单位召开大会欢送，驻地群众也热烈送行。该部抵达工程地区后，受到华中南公路局、修桥工程处及桥工队的热烈欢迎。全体指战员现已开始紧张工作。
