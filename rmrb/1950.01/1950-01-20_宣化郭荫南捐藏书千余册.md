Title: Xuanhua's Guo Yinnan Donates Over a Thousand Volumes of Books
Author:
Date: 1950-01-20
Page: 3
    [This Newspaper] News: Guo Yinnan of Xuanhua County, Chahar Province, recently donated 1,039 books from his family collection to Xuanhua Middle School to enrich the school's library. The donated books include the complete Twenty-Four Histories and commentaries on the Thirteen Classics. Guo Yinnan is currently sixty-two years old and has served as a teacher at Xuanhua Middle School twice, for a total of twelve years. Regarding this donation, Xuanhua Middle School has requested commendation from higher-level authorities.

### 宣化郭荫南捐藏书千余册

1950-01-20
第3版()
专栏：

    宣化郭荫南捐藏书千余册
    【本报讯】察哈尔省宣化县郭荫南，最近将其家藏图书一千零三十九本捐赠宣化中学，以充实该校图书。所捐书籍内有全部二十四史及十三经注疏等。郭荫南现年六十二岁，曾两度任宣化中学教员，历十二年。此次捐书，宣化中学已呈请上级机关予以褒扬。
