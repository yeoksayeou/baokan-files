Title: Cultural and Educational Briefs
Author:
Date: 1950-01-09
Page: 3

    *Chuan Nan Daily* was launched in Zigong City on the 23rd. The Sichuan South Branch of Xinhua News Agency was established at the same time.
    *Guangzhou Worker*, a newspaper run by the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Federation of Trade Unions, and *South China Youth*, a publication run by the South China Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, were both published on New Year's Day.
    The Shandong Xinhua Bookstore Main Store has recently printed twelve kinds of New Year pictures and one kind of New Year calendar, and will publish a variety of books such as *New Year Joy* for New Year propaganda.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th]

### 文化教育简讯

1950-01-09
第3版()
专栏：

    文化教育简讯
    《川南日报》二十三日在自贡市创刊。新华社川南分社同时成立。
    广州市总工会筹委会所办的《广州工人》报及新民主主义青年团华南工委会所办刊物《华南青年》均于元旦出版。
    山东新华书店总店新近印制了新年画十二种和新日历一种，并将出版《新年乐》等图书多种，以供年节宣传之用。
    【新华社北京七日电】
