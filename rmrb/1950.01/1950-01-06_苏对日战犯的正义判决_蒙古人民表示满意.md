Title: The Soviet Union's Just Verdict on Japanese War Criminals: Mongolian People Express Satisfaction
Author:
Date: 1950-01-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 5th: The working people of the Mongolian People's Republic have expressed great satisfaction with the just verdict of the Soviet Union's Primorsky Krai Military Tribunal on former Japanese military personnel who prepared and used bacteriological weapons, according to a TASS report from Ulaanbaatar on the 4th. The Mongolian people once again express their immense gratitude to the Soviet Union and the heroic Soviet army for saving humanity (including the Mongolian people) from the cruel fate of annihilation.

### 苏对日战犯的正义判决  蒙古人民表示满意

1950-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    苏对日战犯的正义判决
    蒙古人民表示满意
    【新华社北京五日电】塔斯社乌兰巴托尔四日讯：蒙古人民共和国劳动人民，对苏联滨海军区军事法庭对准备并使用细菌武器的前日军军人的正义判决，表示非常满意。蒙古人民对于苏联以及把人类（包括蒙古人民在内）从残酷的灭亡的厄运中拯救出来的英勇苏军，再次表示极大的感激。
