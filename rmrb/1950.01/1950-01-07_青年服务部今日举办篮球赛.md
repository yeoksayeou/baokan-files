Title: Youth Service Department to Hold Basketball Tournament Today
Author:
Date: 1950-01-07
Page: 3

    Youth Service Department to Hold Basketball Tournament Today
    [Beijing News] The winter basketball tournament organized by the Youth Service Department, comprehensive section (including factories, military units, organizations, and groups), has been accepting registrations since December 27th of last year. As of yesterday (the 6th), three women's teams and forty-nine men's teams have registered. The first matches are scheduled to take place today at 2:00 PM at the Youth Service Department court on East Chang'an Street. Participating units are: North China People's Hospital vs. the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government, and the Beijing Municipal People's Government Education Bureau vs. the Beijing Municipal Post Office. Subsequent matches will be held every Saturday afternoon and Sunday at the Youth Service Department, Normal University, and Fu Jen Catholic University.

### 青年服务部今日举办篮球赛

1950-01-07
第3版()
专栏：

    青年服务部今日举办篮球赛
    【北京讯】青年服务部举办的冬季篮球赛综合部分（包括工厂、部队、机关团体），自去年十二月二十七日开始报名以来，截至昨（六）日止，女子组报名的已有三队，男子组已有四十九队。首次比赛定今日下午二时在东长安街青年服务部球场举行，参加单位为：华北人民医院对中央人民政府财政部，北京市人民政府教育局对北京市邮局。以后每星期六下午及星期日分别在青年服务部、师范大学、辅仁大学三处举行。
