Title: Consolidating Diplomatic Ties and Strengthening Friendly Cooperation: China and Vietnam Establish Diplomatic Relations; Foreign Minister Zhou Sends Telegram to Vietnamese Foreign Minister Expressing Welcome
Author:
Date: 1950-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 18th:
Consolidating Diplomatic Ties and Strengthening Friendly Cooperation
China and Vietnam Establish Diplomatic Relations
Foreign Minister Zhou Sends Telegram to Vietnamese Foreign Minister Expressing Welcome

[Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 18th] Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, received a note from Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on January 15, expressing the willingness to establish diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China and exchange ambassadors. The full text of the note is as follows:

The government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, based on the declaration of the government of the People's Republic of China on October 1, 1949, announce their recognition of the government of the People's Republic of China led by Chairman Mao Zedong.

In order to enhance the friendship and cooperation between the Chinese and Vietnamese peoples, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has decided to establish formal diplomatic relations with the government of the People's Republic of China and exchange ambassadors.

Hoang Minh Giam
Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
January 15, 1950

[Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 18th] Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, today replied to Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, with a note welcoming the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Vietnam. The full text of the note is as follows:

To His Excellency Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

I am honored to receive your Excellency's telegram of January 15, 1950, requesting the establishment of diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China. I now notify your Excellency that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China considers the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to be the legitimate government representing the will of the Vietnamese people.  The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China is willing to  establish  diplomaitc relations with the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and exchange ambassadors in order to consolidate the ties between the two countries and strengthen friendship and cooperation. I hereby reply by telegram and hope you will acknowledge it.

Zhou Enlai
Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
January 18, 1950

### 巩固邦交加强友好合作  中越建立外交关系  周外长电复越外长表示欢迎

1950-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    巩固邦交加强友好合作
    中越建立外交关系
    周外长电复越外长表示欢迎
    【新华社北京十八日电】中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来，接获越南民主共和国外交部长黄明鉴一月十五日照会一件，表示愿与中华人民共和国建立外交关系并交换大使。照会全文如下：
    越南民主共和国的政府与人民，根据中华人民共和国政府一九四九年十月一日宣言，宣布承认毛泽东主席所领导的中华人民共和国政府。
    为了增强中国与越南两民族之间的友谊与合作，越南民主共和国政府决定与中华人民共和国政府建立正式外交关系，并交换大使。
                                越南民主共和国外交部长  黄明鉴
                                        一九五○年一月十五日
    【新华社北京十八日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部长周恩来，今日以照会一件答复越南民主共和国外交部长黄明鉴，欢迎中越两国建立外交关系。照会全文如下：越南民主共和国政府外交部部长黄明鉴先生：
    我很荣幸地接到贵部长一九五○年一月十五日要求和中华人民共和国建立外交关系的电报。我现在通知贵部长，中华人民共和国中央人民政府认为越南民主共和国政府是代表越南人民意志的合法政府，中华人民共和国中央人民政府愿意和越南民主共和国政府建立外交关系并互派大使，借以巩固两国邦交，加强两国的友好和合作。特此电复并希察照为幸。
                   中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长  周恩来
                                      一九五○年一月十八日（附图片）
    越南民主共和国政府主席胡志明
