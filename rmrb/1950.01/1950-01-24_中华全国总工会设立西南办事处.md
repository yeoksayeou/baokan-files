Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Establishes Southwest Office
Author:
Date: 1950-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 23rd: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, in order to unify the leadership of trade union work in the Southwest provinces, decided at its third enlarged standing committee meeting on the 19th to establish the Southwest Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions in Chongqing, with Cai Shufan as Director and Zhang Linzhi and Peng Guangwei as Deputy Directors.

### 中华全国总工会设立西南办事处

1950-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会设立西南办事处
    【新华社北京二十三日电】中华全国总工会为统一领导西南各省工会工作，于十九日经第三次常委扩大会议决定，在重庆设立中华全国总工会西南办事处，并以蔡树藩为主任，张霖之、彭光伟为副主任。
