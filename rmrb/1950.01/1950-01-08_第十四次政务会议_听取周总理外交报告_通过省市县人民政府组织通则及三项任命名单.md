Title: The 14th Government Administration Council Meeting: Listened to Premier Zhou's Report on Foreign Affairs, Approved the General Rules of Organization for Provincial, Municipal, and County People's Governments and Three Lists of Appointments

Author:

Date: 1950-01-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th. The Central People's Government Administration Council held its 14th meeting yesterday afternoon at 3:00 PM, listening to Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's report on foreign affairs. Minister of Education Ma Xulun, Vice Minister of Communications Li Yunchang, Minister of Railways Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Zhu Xuefan, and Minister of Trade Ye Jizhuang respectively reported to the meeting on the National Education Work Conference, the National Shipping and Highway Conference, the Ministry of Railways' 1950 Plan, the National Urban Supply Conference, and the various postal and telecommunications agreements between China and Korea. The meeting approved the current policy guidelines of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government Committee. In addition, yesterday's meeting revised and approved the three organizational rules for provincial, municipal, and county people's governments and approved three lists of appointments.

### 第十四次政务会议  听取周总理外交报告  通过省市县人民政府组织通则及三项任命名单

1950-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    第十四次政务会议
    听取周总理外交报告
    通过省市县人民政府组织通则及三项任命名单
    【新华社北京七日电】中央人民政府政务院昨日下午三时举行第十四次政务会议，听取周恩来总理兼外交部长关于外交问题的报告。教育部马叙伦部长、交通部李运昌副部长、铁道部滕代远部长、邮电部朱学范部长和贸易部叶季壮部长分别向会议作了关于全国教育工作会议、全国航务公路会议、铁道部一九五○年计划、全国城市供应会议和中国朝鲜通邮通电各项协定的报告。会议批准了新疆省人民政府委员会目前施政方针。此外，昨天的会议修正通过了省、市、县人民政府的三个组织通则并通过了三项任命名单。
