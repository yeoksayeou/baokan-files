Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and All-China Association of Literary Workers Send Telegram of Congratulations to the All-Soviet Writers' Association Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-01-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 30th. The 13th Plenary Session of the Soviet Writers' Association opened in Moscow on the 25th. The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the All-China Association of Literary Workers sent a joint telegram of congratulations. The telegram reads as follows: To Comrade Fadeyev of the Soviet Writers' Association in Moscow, and to all writer comrades:
    We are delighted to learn of the opening of the 13th Plenary Session of your Association. We extend our warm comradely greetings to all the delegates and wish the conference a great success!
                                    All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles
                                    All-China Association of Literary Workers
                                                  January 30th

### 我全国文联和文协电贺苏作家协会全会

1950-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我全国文联和文协电贺苏作家协会全会
    【新华社北京三十日电】苏联作家协会第十三次全体会议于二十五日在莫斯科开幕，我中华全国文联及中华全国文协特联电致贺，电文如下：莫斯科苏联作家协会法捷耶夫同志并转全体作家同志们：
    欣闻贵会第十三次全体会议开幕，谨向全体代表们致热烈的同志的敬礼，并祝贺大会的成功！
                                    中华全国文学艺术界联合会	                                中华全国文学工作者协会
                                                  一月三十日
