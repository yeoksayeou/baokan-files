Title: Chengdu 30,000 Students March to Celebrate Liberation
Author:
Date: 1950-01-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 7th. Over 30,000 students from 49 middle schools and higher education institutions in Chengdu held a parade on the 3rd to celebrate the liberation of Southwest China and conducted street-level propaganda.

### 成都三万学生游行庆祝解放

1950-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    成都三万学生游行庆祝解放
    【新华社重庆七日电】成都四十九所中等以上学校的学生三万余人，三日举行庆祝西南解放大游行，并进行街头宣传。
