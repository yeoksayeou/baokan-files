Title: Stalin Replies to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou, Thanking Them for Birthday Greetings on His Seventieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1950-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 9th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Premier Zhou Enlai sent telegrams to Chairman Stalin of the Soviet Council of Ministers to congratulate him on his seventieth birthday. Now, they have received telegrams from Stalin expressing his gratitude. The texts of the two telegrams are as follows:

(I) Telegram from Chairman Stalin to Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China
To His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

Mr. Chairman, I would like to express my heartfelt thanks for your congratulations on my birthday.
                                                    J. Stalin

(II) Telegram from Chairman Stalin to Premier Zhou Enlai
To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administrative Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

Thank you, Mr. Premier, for your congratulations and wishes on my birthday.
                                                  J. Stalin

### 斯大林主席电复毛主席周总理  感谢对七十寿辰的祝贺

1950-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    斯大林主席电复毛主席周总理
    感谢对七十寿辰的祝贺
    【新华社北京九日电】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席、周恩来总理在苏联部长会议主席斯大林七十寿辰时，曾致电祝贺，现已分别收到斯大林表示谢意的复电。两电电文如下：
            （一）斯大林主席复毛泽东主席电中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    主席先生，当我生辰之时，承蒙庆贺，谨此致衷心的谢意。
                                                    约·斯大林
            （二）斯大林主席复周恩来总理电
    北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来先生阁下：
    我感谢你，总理先生，对我的生日的祝贺和愿望。
                                                  约·斯大林
