Title: All-Union Widely Expanding Socialist Emulation Drive, Grandly Welcoming Supreme Soviet Elections, Workers Guarantee Completion of First Quarter Production Plan Before Elections

Author:

Date: 1950-01-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 15th: TASS Moscow dispatch: The people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union are welcoming the election of the Supreme Soviet with the pride of the epoch-making victory of socialism. Throughout the vast Soviet land, in the cities and villages of Moscow and Leningrad, in the Far East and Siberia, in Ukraine and Belarus, in the Baltic Republics and Central Asia, the Soviet people are enthusiastically beginning their preparations. In all factories, offices, educational institutions, and collective farms, working people are studying the election regulations. Propagandists are telling audiences about the Soviet election law, which is based on the truly democratic principles of the Stalin Constitution.

Every Soviet citizen hopes to commemorate the solemn day of electing people's representatives with significant and wonderful achievements. Meetings are being held everywhere, and participants are resolving to welcome the election day with further labor victories. A special Stakhanovite movement has been launched by working people across the country. Personnel at various enterprises are guaranteeing to complete production plans ahead of schedule and supply the state with as many goods as possible. Workers and staff in many departments are guaranteeing to complete the first quarter's production plan for this year before the election day (March 12th).

Soviet farmers have decided to welcome the election day with new achievements. The competition to prepare for spring plowing in an outstanding manner and to complete the rush repairs of agricultural machinery ahead of schedule is expanding in collective farms, state farms, and machine and tractor stations. At a conference of advanced agricultural workers in the Kharkiv region of Ukraine, participants put forward even greater guarantees for 1950 and decided to complete preparations for spring sowing ahead of schedule.

### 全苏广泛扩展社会主义竞赛  隆重迎接最高苏维埃选举  工人保证选举日前完成第一季生产计划

1950-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    全苏广泛扩展社会主义竞赛
    隆重迎接最高苏维埃选举
    工人保证选举日前完成第一季生产计划
    【新华社北京十五日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联各族人民以社会主义的划时代胜利的自豪，来迎接苏联最高苏维埃选举。在广阔的苏维埃国土上，在莫斯科和列宁格勒，在远东和西伯利亚的城市和乡村里，在乌克兰和白俄罗斯，在波罗的海各共和国和中亚细亚，苏联人民都热情地开始进行筹备工作。在所有的工厂、机关和教育机关以及集体农场里，劳动人民正在学习着选举条例。宣传工作者向听众讲述着关于以斯大林宪法中真正民主的原则为基础的苏维埃选举法。
    每一个苏联公民都希望以重大和美好的事物来纪念选举人民代表的隆重的日子。各处都举行了集会，与会者决定以进一步的劳动胜利来迎接选举日。全国各地的劳动人民都发起了特别的斯塔哈诺夫运动。各企业的人员保证提前完成生产计划，并供给国家以尽可能多的货物。许多部门中的工人和职员们，都保证在选举日（三月十二日）以前，提前完成今年第一季的生产计划。
    苏联农民决定以新的成就来迎接选举日。卓越地准备春耕和提早完成抢修农业机器的竞赛，正在集体农场、国营农场和机器与拖拉机站上扩展中。在乌克兰哈尔科夫州先进的农业工作者的会议上，与会者提出了一九五○年的更大保证，并决定提前完成春季播种的准备。
