Title: Correct Methods for the Central People's Radio Broadcasting Union to Promote Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1950-01-22
Page: 3

[News from this newspaper] Today (22nd) at 18:45, Chen Qiyuan will give a speech to overseas Chinese via Central People's Radio. 17:30, Staff Time: "Correct Methods for the Trade Union to Promote Public Bonds". 18:30, Natural Science Lectures: "The Great Chemist Mendeleev". 18:00, First Literary and Artistic Program: Leting Drum "Train Driver Zhang Fulin". 21:00, Second Literary and Artistic Program: Revolutionary Story Song "Singing Liu Zhidan". 20:30, Weekend Art: Yangko Opera "Literacy for Husband and Wife".

[Additional News] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Staff Program: (1) Entertainment: Opera "Contending for the Red Flag", (2) News: ① The situation of the "Telegraph Contracting System" implemented by the Beijing Telegraph Office of the Tianjin Railway Bureau, ② Production report of the private Cixing Iron Factory. 13:00 Listener Service: ① Answering the question of whether engaging in commerce counts as production, ② Introducing Vietnamese people's leader Ho Chi Minh, ③ Enrollment brochure for the Training Class for Staff of the Ministry of Heavy Industry. 16:30 Children's Program: Sun Jingxiu tells several stories of Lenin. 17:00 Soviet Music. 19:15 Introducing the People's Democratic countries of Romania and Bulgaria.

### 中央人民电台广播工会推销公债的正确办法

1950-01-22
第3版()
专栏：

    中央人民电台广播工会推销公债的正确办法
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（二十二）天十八点四十五分：陈其瑗向海外华侨广播演讲。十七点三十分职工时间：“工会推销公债的正确办法”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：“大化学家门德列也夫”。十八点第一次文艺节目：乐亭大鼓“火车司机张福林”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：革命故事歌《歌唱刘志丹》。八点三十分星期文艺：秧歌剧《夫妻识字》。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：（一）文娱：歌剧《争红旗》，（二）通讯：①天津铁路局北京电报所实行“电报包台制”的情形，②私营慈型铁工厂生产报导。十三点听众服务：①回答关于从事商业算不算生产的问题，②介绍越南人民领袖胡志明，③重工业部工作人员训练班招生简章。十六点三十分儿童节目：孙敬修讲几个列宁的故事。十七点苏联音乐。十九点十五分介绍人民民主国家罗马尼亚和保加利亚。
