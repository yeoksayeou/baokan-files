Title: Sweden Wishes to Establish Diplomatic Relations with Us; Foreign Minister Zhou Replies, Hoping It Will Sever Relations with the Remaining Bandits and Send Representatives to Beijing for Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1950-01-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th. Swedish Foreign Minister Östen Undén telegraphed our Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai on the 14th, stating that the Swedish government is willing to establish diplomatic relations with the Central People's Government of China. In his reply, Minister Zhou, on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, stated that after the Swedish government severs relations with the remnants of the Kuomintang reactionaries, our government is willing to establish diplomatic relations between the two countries on the basis of equality, mutual benefit, and mutual respect for territorial sovereignty, and hopes that the Swedish government will send representatives to Beijing to negotiate on this matter.

### 瑞典愿与我建外交关系  周外长复电望其与残匪  断绝关系后派代表来京谈判

1950-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典愿与我建外交关系
    周外长复电望其与残匪
    断绝关系后派代表来京谈判
    【新华社北京十九日电】瑞典政府外交部长奥斯丁·安登于十四日电我外交部周恩来部长，谓瑞典政府愿与我中央人民政府建立外交关系。周部长于复电中代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府称，在瑞典政府与国民党反动派残余断绝关系之后，我政府愿在平等、互利及互相尊重领土主权的基础上建立两国之间的外交关系，并望瑞典政府派遣代表来京，就此项问题进行谈判。
