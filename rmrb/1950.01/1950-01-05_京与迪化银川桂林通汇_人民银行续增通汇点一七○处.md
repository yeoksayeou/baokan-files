Title: Beijing Commences Remittance Services with Dihua, Yinchuan, and Guilin; People's Bank Continues to Add 170 Remittance Points
Author:
Date: 1950-01-05
Page: 2

    [This newspaper report] The Beijing Branch of the People's Bank is further developing its domestic remittance business. In December of last year, in addition to commencing remittance services with the provincial capitals of Dihua in Xinjiang Province, Yinchuan in Ningxia Province, and Guilin in Guangxi Province, it has also added 170 remittance points: Hebei Province: Hangu, Wuqiang.
