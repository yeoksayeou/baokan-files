Introduction to the Administrative Divisions of the North China Region (Continued and Completed)

            Chahar Province
    Chahar Province – The Provincial People's Government is stationed in Zhangjiakou, administering three special districts and three province-administered municipalities. The three special districts administer 32 counties (including two newly established counties). The total population of the province is 3,994,303, with 23,950,213 mu of land.

    Yanbei Special District – The special commissioner's office is stationed in Datong City, administering 12 counties: Ying County, Guangling County, Lingqiu County, Hunyuan County, Yanggao County, Datong County, Shuo County, Shanyin County, Huairen County, Zuoyun County, Youyu County, Pinglu County.

    Chanan Special District – The special commissioner's office is stationed in Xuanhua City, administering 12 counties: Wei County, Huaian County, Zhuolu County, Tianzhen County, Yangyuan County, Xuanhua County, Wanquan County, Sihai County (southwestern part of Fengning County, Rehe, a newly established county), Yanqing County, Chicheng County, Longguan County, Huailai County.

    Chabei Special District – The special commissioner's office is stationed in Zhangbei, administering eight counties: Zhangbei County, Kangbao County, Baoyuan County (formerly Baocheng and Guyuan merged), Duolun County, Chongli County, Shangyi County, Shangdu County, Huade County.

    Three province-administered municipalities – Zhangjiakou City, Datong City, Xuanhua City.

            Suiyuan Province
    Suiyuan Province – The Provincial People's Government is stationed in Guisui City, administering 22 counties (including six newly established counties), two province-administered municipalities, and 18 Mongolian banners. The total population of the province is 2,255,869.

    22 counties – Fengzhen County, Jining County, Liangcheng County, Taolin County, Xinghe County, Longsheng County (formed from parts of Fengzhen, Jining, Liangcheng, and Taolin counties), Guisui County, Wudong County (eastern part of Wuchuan), Helin County, Qinghe County, Tuo County (i.e., Tokto), Wuxi (western part of the original Wuchuan County), Sa County (i.e., Sarachi), Baotou County, Guyang County, Wuyuan County, Linhe County, Anbei County, Langshan County (newly established county by the Kuomintang during the Anti-Japanese War), Micang County (newly established county by the Kuomintang during the Anti-Japanese War), Yanjiang County (newly established county by the Kuomintang during the Anti-Japanese War).

    Two province-administered municipalities – Guisui City, Baotou City.

    18 Mongolian banners – Bordered Red Banner, Bordered Blue Banner, Plain Yellow Banner, Plain Red Banner, Tumet Independent Banner, East Gong Banner, Central Gong Banner, West Gong Banner, Maoming'an Banner, Darhan Banner, Siziwang Banner, Jungar Banner, Dalat Banner, Uxin Banner, Fosaq Banner, Etok Banner, Junwang Banner, Hanggin Banner.

            Two Central Government-Administered Municipalities
    Beijing City  Population: 1,940,290.
    Tianjin City  Population: 1,785,813.

    华北区行政区划介绍（续完）
            察哈尔省
    察哈尔省——省人民政府驻张家口，共辖三个专区，三个省辖市。三个专区分辖三二个县（内二个新设县）。全省人口三、九九四、三○三人，地亩二三、九五○、二一三亩。
    雁北专区——专署驻大同市，辖一二个县：应县、广灵县、灵邱县、浑源县、阳高县、大同县、朔县、山阴县、怀仁县、左云县、右玉县、平鲁县。
    察南专区——专署驻宣化市，辖一二个县：蔚县、怀安县、涿鹿县、天镇县、阳原县、宣化县、万全县、四海县（热河丰宁县西南部分，新设县）、延庆县、赤城县、龙关县、怀来县。
    察北专区——专署驻张北，辖八个县：张北县、康保县、宝源县（原宝昌、沽源合并）、多伦县、崇礼县、尚义县、商都县、化德县。
    三个省辖市——张家口市、大同市、宣化市。
            绥远省
    绥远省——省人民政府驻归绥市，共辖二二个县（内有六个新设县），二个省辖市，一八个蒙旗。全省人口二、二五五、八六九人。
    二二个县——丰镇县、集宁县、凉城县、陶林县、兴和县、龙胜县（由丰镇、集宁、凉城、陶林四县之各一部分合成）、归绥县、武东县（武川东部）、和林县、清河县、托县（即托克托）、武西（原武川县西部）、萨县（即萨拉齐）、包头县、固阳县、五原县、临河县、安北县、狼山县（抗战期间国民党新设县）、米仓县（抗战期间国民党新设县）、宴江县（抗战期间国民党新设县）。
    二个省辖市——归绥市、包头市。
    一八个蒙旗——镶红旗、镶蓝旗、正黄旗、正红旗、土默特独立旗、东公旗、中公旗、西公旗、茂明安旗，达尔汉旗、四子王旗、准格尔旗、达拉特旗、乌审旗、佛萨克旗、鄂托克旗、郡王旗、杭锦旗。
            两个中央直辖市
    北京市  人口一、九四○、二九○人。
    天津市  人口一、七八五、八一三人。
