Title: Strengthening the Unity of Our People with the People of All Countries: English "People's China" Launched; Inaugural Issue Carries Important Works by Chairman Mao and Others
Author:
Date: 1950-01-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 31st: The English-language bi-monthly magazine "People's China" will be launched in the capital on New Year's Day. The inaugural message of the magazine states: "The purpose of 'People's China' is to strengthen the connection and unity between the Chinese people and the people of the Soviet Union, the People's Democratic countries, and the people of all capitalist countries, and to support the anti-imperialist and anti-feudal struggles of the Asian people." The main contents of the inaugural issue include: Mao Zedong's "Stalin is a Friend of the Chinese People," Guo Moruo's "Ode to Stalin, The United Front of Literature and Art," Zou Yan's "The Historic Meeting in Moscow," Tian Huai's "On the Status of the National Bourgeoisie in the Chinese Revolution," Torildano's "I Saw New China" and other important works. In addition, there are columns such as "Fortnightly Chronicle," "Current Affairs Pictures," and "Cultural World." The magazine is published on the 1st and 16th of each month, and is distributed domestically by Sanlian Bookstore.

### 加强我人民与各国人民团结  英文《人民中国》创刊  创刊号载毛主席等重要论著

1950-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    加强我人民与各国人民团结
    英文《人民中国》创刊           
    创刊号载毛主席等重要论著
    【新华社北京三十一日电】英文的《人民中国》半月刊，将于明年元旦在首都创刊。该刊发刊词中说：《人民中国》的主旨在于加强中国人民和苏联、人民民主国家以及各资本主义各国的人民间的联系和团结，并支持亚洲人民反帝反封建的斗争。创刊号主要内容有：毛泽东作：斯大林是中国人民的朋友，郭沫若作：斯大林颂、文学和艺术的统一战线，邹彦作：莫斯科历史性的会晤，天怀作：关于民族资产阶级在中国革命中的地位问题，托里达诺作：我看见了新中国等重要论著多篇，此外并有《半月大事记》、《时事图画》和《文化世界》等栏。该刊每月一日、十六日出版，国内由三联书店总经售。
