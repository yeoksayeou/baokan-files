Title: CPC Keshan County Party Congress Sends Respects to Chairman Mao Zedong, Guaranteeing Unity, Improving Production and Cultural Construction, and Completing the Task of Purchasing People's Victory Inflation-Adjusted Bonds
Author:
Date: 1950-01-09
Page: 1

    【Report from this newspaper in Shenyang】The First Congress of the Communist Party of China in Keshan County, Heilongjiang Province, sent a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao Zedong on the 23rd of last month. The original telegram is as follows: Beloved and Great, Wise Leader of the Party, Chairman Mao:
    Our First Congress of the CPC Keshan County has successfully concluded today. We, on behalf of all Party members, extend our highest respect to the Party! The Congress summarized and reviewed the experiences and lessons of our past work in a spirit of criticism and self-criticism, and formulated the work tasks for next year. We guarantee to unite as one, under your glorious banner, to resolutely complete the instructions given to us by the higher-level Party organizations: to truly improve agricultural production one step further, to improve cooperative work one step further, to strengthen cultural and educational work, to guarantee the completion of the purchase of the People's Victory Inflation-Adjusted Bonds issued by the Central People's Government, and to make every effort to ensure more grain production, support the restoration and development of industry, and support the rapid and complete victory of the national liberation war!
                                  The First Congress of the CPC Keshan County
                                December 23, 1949

### 中共克山县党员代表大会  电毛泽东主席致敬  保证团结一致提高生产及文化建设并完成购销人民胜利折实公债任务

1950-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中共克山县党员代表大会
    电毛泽东主席致敬
    保证团结一致提高生产及文化建设并完成购销人民胜利折实公债任务
    【本报沈阳讯】中国共产党黑龙江省克山县第一届党员代表大会于上月二十三日致电毛泽东主席致敬，原电如下：亲爱的党的伟大的英明领袖毛主席：
    我们克山县第一届党员代表大会于今天胜利闭会。我们谨代表全体党员向党致最崇高的敬礼！大会以批评自我批评的精神总结与检讨了我们过去工作的经验与教训，并制定了明年的工作任务。我们保证团结一致，在您光辉的旗帜下，坚决完成上级党给予我们的指示：确实把农业生产提高一步，把合作社工作提高一步，加强文化教育工作，保证完成中央人民政府人民胜利折实公债的购买任务，并尽一切努力为保证多打粮食，支援工业的恢复与发展，支援全国解放战争迅速全部胜利而奋斗！
                                   克山县首届党代表大会
                                一九四九年十二月二十三日
