Title: Lenin is Our Sun
Author: Jié bā yě cì
Date: 1950-01-21
Page: 6

    Lenin is Our Sun
    By Jebaits
    Translated by Zhifang

    I am a pilgrim from Estonia
    To visit the great mausoleum on Red Square in Moscow,
    Where the Kremlin's stars shine like gold,
    For all the Soviet people to behold.
    This mausoleum stands upright in the heart of the land of freedom,
    As rivers inevitably flow into the sea,
    People gather from all directions to the mausoleum,
    Their hearts filled with reverence, their eyes filled with mourning:
    Tajiks, Kazakhs, Olutes, and Bashkirs come to visit,
    Followed by Afghans, Turks, and Lesgions.
    They look at Lenin like soldiers, like sons;
    I go with them—my blood boils,
    I see Lenin lying affectionately as before,
    He looks so intelligent, so serene,
    The military flag lowers before him with love,
    The people's ranks pass by, yet he doesn't even blink an eyelid.
    He cannot hear me—a stooped, white-haired man,
    Whispering with filial reverence,
    Solemnly vowing to live according to Lenin's will:
    To think, work, and struggle like Lenin,
    No matter what, I vow to use poetry
    To tell my descendants and all people:
    Lenin is our sun, we revolve around him; Stalin's sharp eyes flash with his genius.

            (Reprinted from the poetry collection "Lenin is Our Sun" by Dalian Haiyan Bookstore)

### 列宁是我们的太阳
杰巴也次  之方
1950-01-21
第6版()
专栏：

    列宁是我们的太阳
    杰巴也次  作  之方  译
    我是一个从额萨斯坦来的拜谒者
    来拜谒莫斯科红场上的伟大的陵墓，
    在那里克里姆林宫的星辰像黄金一样地闪烁，
    让全苏维埃的人们观望。
    这座陵墓直立在自由之地的中心，
    好像河流必然地流入海中，
    人们从四面八方集中到陵墓，
    他们的心里充满着敬爱，他们的眼里充满着哀悼：
    来拜谒的有塔斋克人，哈萨克人，奥利兀惕人，和巴什克尔人，
    跟着的还有阿富尔人，土耳其人和烈述人。
    他们像兵士，像儿子一样地去看列宁；
    我跟着他们一块儿去——我的血沸腾了，
    我看到列宁慈爱地躺着像从前一样，
    他看上去是那般聪明，那般静穆，
    军旗怀着爱在他面前低下了，
    人民的队伍经过，然而他连眼皮也不动一动。
    他听不到我——一个弯背白发的人，
    是怎样地用孝顺的敬爱在低语。
    低低地发誓要遵照列宁的意志而生活：
    像列宁一样地思想，工作和斗争，
    无论怎样我发誓要用诗歌
    来向我的子孙和一切的人们诉说：
    列宁是我们的太阳，我们绕着他转动；斯大林的锐利的眼睛闪射着他的天才。
            （转载自大连海燕书店诗集《列宁是我们的太阳》）
