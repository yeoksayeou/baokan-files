Title: Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government Centralizes Management of Railway Finances
Author: 
Date: 1950-01-10
Page: 1

    Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government
    Centralizes Management of Railway Finances
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th] In order to strengthen the business operations of national railways, the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government has implemented centralized management of national railway finances starting from January 1st. The business income of the Northern and Southern Railway Administrations is uniformly handed over to the General Treasury of the Ministry of Railways for management. The daily business proceeds of each administration are immediately settled and remitted to the Ministry of Railways by the local People's Bank of China via telegraphic transfer. The Ministry of Railways then allocates funds according to the budget and settles accounts daily. The finances of the Northeast Railway Administrations are uniformly the responsibility of the Northeast General Administration to the Ministry of Railways. Furthermore, starting from January 1st, the national railways have uniformly implemented the new railway accounting system and the provisional railway budget regulations formulated by the Ministry of Railways.

### 中央人民政府铁道部  统一管理铁路财政

1950-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府铁道部
    统一管理铁路财政
    【新华社北京九日电】为了加强全国铁路企业经营，中央人民政府铁道部已自一月一日起实行全国铁路财政的统一管理。北方、南方各铁路管理局营业收入统一交铁道部总金库掌管，各局营业进款当日即结交当地中国人民银行电汇铁道部，铁道部再根据预算拨款，逐日清算。东北各铁路管理局财政则统一由东北总局向铁道部负责。此外，自一月一日起全国铁路已统一实行了铁道部新拟订的铁路会计制度和铁路预算暂行规程。
