Title: Shantou Shoots Down Enemy Plane

Author: 
Date: 1950-01-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shantou, 16th. The Shantou air defense forces shot down an invading Kuomintang bandit plane on the 15th. Since January 8th, bandit planes have continuously and indiscriminately bombed Shantou city. Two to four enemy planes have attacked each day, resulting in the deaths and injuries of 15 citizens according to incomplete statistics. The entire city's military and civilians are filled with extreme hatred for the Kuomintang bandit gang's dying struggle of frantically slaughtering the people, and their heinous crimes.

### 汕头击落敌机一架

1950-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    汕头击落敌机一架
    【新华社汕头十六日电】汕头空防部队十五日击落来袭的国民党匪机一架。自一月八日至今，匪机连续滥炸汕头市。每日来袭敌机两架至四架不等，据不完全统计炸死炸伤市民十五人。全市军民对国民党匪帮垂死挣扎疯狂屠杀人民之万恶罪行，愤恨万分。
