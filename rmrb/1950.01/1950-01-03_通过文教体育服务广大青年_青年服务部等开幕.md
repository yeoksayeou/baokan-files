    Serving the Broad Masses of Youth Through Cultural, Educational, and Sports Services:
    Opening of the Youth Service Department, etc.
    [Beijing News] The Youth Service Department, jointly organized by the Social Service Department of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, along with the Youth Publishing House and the Youth Cultural Service Agency, organized by the Publishing Committee of the Central Committee of the Youth League, simultaneously held their opening ceremony at the auditorium of the Youth Service Department on New Year's Day afternoon. Guests attending included representatives from units such as the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, the Beijing Municipal Joint Association of Primary and Secondary School Teachers, and the YMCA, as well as representatives from various factories and schools in Beijing, totaling more than 200 people. Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, and Ma John from the sports community also attended. At the meeting, Rong Gaotang, Minister of the Social Service Department of the Central Committee of the Youth League, first delivered an opening speech, explaining that the purpose of the Youth Service Department, the Youth Publishing House, and the Youth Cultural Service Agency is to serve the broad masses of youth through various cultural education, sports, and recreational activities, thereby helping them improve their political and cultural levels. He expressed the hope that people from all walks of life would provide assistance. Jiang Ling, Director of the Social Education Department of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, delivered a speech, hoping that the Youth Service Department would pay special attention to the learning of general out-of-school, unemployed, and employed youth. After the meeting, there were events such as basketball games, skating performances, ice hockey games, and costume skating performances.

### 通过文教体育服务广大青年  青年服务部等开幕

1950-01-03
第3版()
专栏：

    通过文教体育服务广大青年
    青年服务部等开幕
    【北京讯】新民主主义青年团中央委员会社会服务部与青年团北京市工委会共同举办的青年服务部、青年团中央出版委员会举办的青年出版社和青年文化服务社三单位于元旦下午在青年服务部礼堂同时举行开幕式。出席来宾有中央人民政府教育部、全国妇联、全国青联、全国学联、中苏友好协会、青年团北京市工委会、北京市中小学教联、基督教青年会等单位及北京市各工厂、学校代表共二百余人。青年团中央副书记蒋南翔、体育界马约翰等也都到会。会上首由团中央社会服务部部长荣高棠致开幕词，说明青年服务部、青年出版社、青年文化服务社的宗旨在通过文化教育和体育康乐的各种活动来为广大青年服务，借以帮助他们提高政治与文化水平，他希望各界人士能予以协助。中央人民政府教育部社会教育司司长江凌致词，希望青年服务部能特别照顾一般失学失业及在职青年的学习。会后举行篮球赛、溜冰表演、冰球赛、化装溜冰表演等节目。
