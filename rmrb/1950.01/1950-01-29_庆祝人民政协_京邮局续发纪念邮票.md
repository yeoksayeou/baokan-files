Title: Celebrating the People's Political Consultative Conference: Beijing Postal Bureau Continues Issuance of Commemorative Stamps
Author:
Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 4

    Celebrating the People's Political Consultative Conference
    Beijing Postal Bureau Continues Issuance of Commemorative Stamps
    [Beijing News] In celebration and commemoration of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Directorate General of Posts had prepared two sets of stamps: one for celebration and one for commemoration. The celebration set was issued at post offices in Beijing before the New Year, and now the intaglio commemorative set is being issued. This set of commemorative stamps features two designs: the Xinhua Gate and a portrait of Chairman Mao, with denominations of 50 yuan (red), 100 yuan (blue), 300 yuan (purple), and 500 yuan (green). Beijing post offices will begin selling them on February 1st.

### 庆祝人民政协  京邮局续发纪念邮票

1950-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝人民政协
    京邮局续发纪念邮票
    【北京讯】邮政总局为庆祝及纪念中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议，曾筹印庆祝及纪念邮票各一组。除庆祝邮票一组，已于年前在京市各邮局发行外，现续发行凸版纪念票一组。此组纪念邮票计有新华门及毛主席像两种图案，票值分五十元（红色），一百元（蓝色），三百元（紫色），五百元（绿色）四种。京市各邮局定于二月一日开始发售。
