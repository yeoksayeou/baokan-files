Title: First Installment of National Bonds Officially Issued Today
Author:
Date: 1950-01-22
Page: 4

    [Beijing News] The first installment of the People's Victory Parity Unit Public Bonds has been printed and will be officially issued starting today (the 22nd). The Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China has distributed the bonds to its subordinate departments, and people from all walks of life can directly purchase them from these departments. Those who previously purchased public bonds but have not yet received their bond certificates can bring their temporary receipts to the original purchasing location to exchange them for bond certificates from January 24th to 31st.

### 第一期公债票今起正式发行

1950-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    第一期公债票今起正式发行
    【北京讯】人民胜利折实公债第一期公债票已印制完成，自今（二十二）日起正式发行。中国人民银行北京分行已将公债票分发所属各部处，各界人士可直接到各部处购买。前购买公债而没有领到债票的，在本月二十四日至三十一日可持临时收据到原购买处换取债票。
