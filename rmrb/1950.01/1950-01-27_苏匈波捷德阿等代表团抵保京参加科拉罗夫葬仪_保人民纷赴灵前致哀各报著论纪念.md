Title: Soviet, Hungarian, Polish, Czech, German, Albanian and Other Delegations Arrive in Bulgarian Capital to Attend Kolarov's Funeral; Bulgarian People Flock to Pay Respects Before the Casket; Newspapers Publish Articles in Commemoration
Author:
Date: 1950-01-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 26th: TASS, Sofia, January 25th: The Soviet government delegation, headed by Voroshilov, to attend the funeral of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Kolarov, upon arriving in Sofia, immediately went to the Bulgarian National Assembly to lay a wreath before Kolarov's remains. The wreath was inscribed: "To our dear friend V. Kolarov. Respectfully presented by the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union." Voroshilov, along with members of the Soviet delegation, Soviet Ambassador to Bulgaria, Podurov, and the Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Chervenkov, and others, stood guard beside Kolarov's remains.

Government delegations from various People's Democratic countries have also successively arrived in Sofia to attend Kolarov's funeral. The Hungarian delegation is led by Foreign Minister Károly Gyula, the Polish delegation is led by the Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, the Czechoslovakian delegation is led by Deputy Prime Minister Fierlinger, the German Democratic Republic delegation is led by Deputy Prime Minister Nuschke of the Provisional Government, and the Albanian delegation is led by Albanian trade union leader Nushi.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 26th: According to TASS, Sofia: An endless stream of people is flocking to the National Assembly Building to pay respects before the remains of their beloved leader, Kolarov. Newspapers have published essays on Kolarov's life and work. The Central Committee of the Dimitrov Youth League wrote in a letter to the youth: "Kolarov devoted his glorious life to serving the working class and the working people selflessly. His heroic life and struggle are a shining example that has inspired and continues to inspire the younger generation to fight for the victory of socialism and communism in our country." The Sofia City People's Council held a special meeting on the 24th to mourn Kolarov.

### 苏匈波捷德阿等代表团抵保京参加科拉罗夫葬仪  保人民纷赴灵前致哀各报著论纪念

1950-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏匈波捷德阿等代表团抵保京参加科拉罗夫葬仪
    保人民纷赴灵前致哀各报著论纪念
    【新华社北京二十六日电】塔斯社索非亚二十五日讯：以伏罗希洛夫为首的参加保加利亚部长会议主席科拉罗夫葬礼的苏联政府代表团，于抵达索非亚后，即前往保加利亚国民议会，在科拉罗夫灵前敬献花圈。花圈上写着：“献给亲爱的朋友Ｖ·科拉罗夫。联共（布）中央委员会及苏联部长会议敬献。”伏罗希洛夫与苏联代表团团员，苏驻保大使保德罗夫及保加利亚部长会议副主席契尔文科夫等人，都在科拉罗夫灵旁守灵。
    各人民民主国家的政府代表团亦先后抵达索非亚，参加科拉罗夫的葬礼。匈牙利代表团由外长卡莱·古拉率领，波兰代表团由部长会议主席西伦凯维兹率领，捷克斯洛伐克代表团由副总理费林格率领，德意志民主共和国代表团由临时政府副总理努舍克率领，阿尔巴尼亚代表团由阿尔巴尼亚工会领袖努什率领。
    【新华社北京二十六日电】据塔斯社索非亚讯：无穷尽的人流涌往国民议会大厦，在他们热爱的领袖科拉罗夫的灵前致哀。各报刊载关于科拉罗夫的生活与工作的论文。季米特洛夫青年联盟中央委员会在告青年书中写道：“科拉罗夫以其辉煌的一生，忘我地为工人阶级和劳动人民服务。他的英勇的生活和斗争，是曾经鼓舞并继续鼓舞着为社会主义和共产主义在我国的胜利而斗争的年青一代的光辉范例。”索非亚市人民议会于二十四日举行特别会议，追悼科拉罗夫。
