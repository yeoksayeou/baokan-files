Title: The 15th Government Administration Council Meeting Approves Instructions on Agricultural Land Issues in Old Liberated Areas and Suburban Districts, and Six Items on Appointment Lists

Author: 
Date: 1950-01-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th—The Central People's Government Administration Council held its 15th meeting at 3:00 PM today, discussed and approved the instructions on handling agricultural land issues in old liberated areas and suburban districts. Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, and He Changgong, Deputy Minister of Heavy Industry, attended the meeting and reported on the National Postal Conference and the Iron and Steel Conference, respectively. Finally, the meeting approved six items on the appointment lists.

### 第十五次政务会议  通过老区市郊农业土地  问题指示及任命名单六项

1950-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    第十五次政务会议
    通过老区市郊农业土地
    问题指示及任命名单六项
    【新华社北京十三日电】中央人民政府政务院今天下午三时举行第十五次政务会议，讨论并通过了关于处理老解放区市郊农业土地问题的指示。邮电部朱学范部长和重工业部何长工副部长列席会议分别作了关于全国邮政会议和钢铁会议的报告。会议最后通过任命名单六项。
