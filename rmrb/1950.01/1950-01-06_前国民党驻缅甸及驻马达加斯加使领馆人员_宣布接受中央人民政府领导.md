Title: Former Kuomintang Personnel in Burmese and Madagascar Consulates Announce Acceptance of Leadership of the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1950-01-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 5th:

    Former personnel from the Kuomintang reactionary government's embassy in Burma and consulate general in Madagascar have successively announced their departure from the Kuomintang bandit gang and acceptance of the leadership of the Central People's Government. Tu Yuntan, the former Kuomintang government's ambassador to Burma, along with all members of the embassy, sent a telegram to Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of our Central People's Government, on the 17th of last month, stating that they would sever relations with the Kuomintang government from that day forward and accept the leadership of the Central People's Government. Furthermore, all Chinese staff members of the former Kuomintang Consulate General in Antananarivo, the capital of Madagascar in Africa, also issued a declaration on the 11th of last month announcing their departure from the Kuomintang reactionary government, stating in brief: Now that our motherland has been liberated under the leadership of the great people's leader Chairman Mao, and has embarked on a new path, we hereby announce our departure from the Kuomintang reactionary government from this day forward. After receiving the telegrams, Foreign Minister Zhou replied separately to commend them, and instructed them to unite as one, stand fast at their posts, maintain the status quo, and be responsible for the safekeeping of all public property and documents, pending further instructions.

### 前国民党驻缅甸及驻马达加斯加使领馆人员  宣布接受中央人民政府领导

1950-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    前国民党驻缅甸及驻马达加斯加使领馆人员
    宣布接受中央人民政府领导
    【新华社北京五日电】前国民党反动政府驻缅甸大使馆及驻马达加斯加总领事馆全体人员，先后宣布脱离国民党匪帮，接受中央人民政府领导。前国民党政府驻缅甸大使涂允檀率大使馆全体馆员于上月十七日电呈我中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长，表示即日起与国民党政府脱离关系，接受中央人民政府领导。又，前国民党驻非州马达加斯加岛首府安塔那那利佛总领事馆全体中国馆员，亦于上月十一日发表脱离国民党反动政府宣言，略称：现在我们的祖国已经在伟大的人民领袖毛主席领导之下获得解放，从此走上新生的道路，我们从即日起宣布脱离国民党反动政府。周外长接电后，已分别复电嘉勉，并令他们团结一致，坚守岗位，维持现状，负责保管一切公物文件，以待后命。
