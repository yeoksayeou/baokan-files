Title: Beijing New Year's Celebration to Open Tomorrow at 8:30 AM, Held in Zhongshan Park and Beihai Park

Author:

Date: 1950-01-01
Page: 3

[Beijing News] The "Beijing New Year's Celebration," organized by the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Municipal Education Bureau, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Beijing Municipal Student Federation, and over twenty other organizations and groups, is scheduled to take place simultaneously in Zhongshan Park and Beihai Park tomorrow (2nd). The opening ceremony will be held at 8:30 AM. From 9:00 to 11:00 AM, both locations will hold a "Competition to Search for Reactionaries and Achieve Meritorious Service," where participants will search for paper slips hidden in the park bearing the names of the chief war criminals Chiang Kai-shek, Soong Tzu-wen, H.H. Kung, Chen Li-fu, and Yan Xishan. From 11:00 AM to 4:30 PM, there will be group games and dances. In addition, Zhongshan Park will have a song and dance entertainment (from 1:00 to 4:30 PM in the Music Hall), a new folk art entertainment (from 1:00 to 4:30 PM in the Zhongshan Hall), and an exhibition of photographs of workers and students (in the Water Pavilion). Beihai Park will have a cross-country race and an ice sports meet. The ice sports meet will be held from 10:30 AM to 4:30 PM and will include competitions, skill demonstrations, and costume performances. In the evening, entertainment galas will be held in the auditoriums of the National Academy of Fine Arts and Fu Jen Catholic University.

### 京市新年大联欢明晨八时半开幕  分在中山公园北海公园举行

1950-01-01
第3版()
专栏：

    京市新年大联欢明晨八时半开幕
    分在中山公园北海公园举行
    【北京讯】由北京市总工会筹委会、市教育局、青年团北京市工委会、市学联等二十余机关、团体筹办的“北京市新年大联欢”，定明（二）日在中山公园和北海公园同时举行。上午八点半举行开幕式。九点至十一点两处均为“搜索反动派立功比赛”，由参加者寻找藏在园内的首要战犯蒋介石、宋子文、孔祥熙、陈立夫、阎锡山等的纸片。十一点至下午四点半为集体游戏及舞蹈。此外，中山公园还有歌舞游艺会（下午一点至四点半，在音乐堂），新曲艺游艺会（下午一点至四点半，在中山堂），工人、学生的照片展览（在水榭）等。北海公园有越野登高比赛及冰上运动会。冰上运动会从上午十点半到下午四点半，有竞赛、技巧表演和化装表演等。晚上，在国立美术学院和辅仁大学礼堂举行游艺晚会。
