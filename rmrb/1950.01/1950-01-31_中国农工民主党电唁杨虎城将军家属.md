Title: China National Revolutionary Committee Sends Condolences to General Yang Hucheng's Family
Author:
Date: 1950-01-31
Page: 4

    China National Revolutionary Committee Sends Condolences to General Yang Hucheng's Family
    [This newspaper] The Central Committee of the China National Revolutionary Committee sent a telegram of condolence to General Yang Hucheng's eldest son, General Yang Zhengmin, stating: Now all the national territory except Tibet, Hainan Island, and Taiwan has been completely liberated, and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has been established. All democratic parties have participated in the government. General Yang's grand aspirations have not only been fully realized but have also far exceeded expectations. General Yang can smile in his grave. General Yang is a hero of the people, and his heroic name will be immortal. We hereby send our condolences.

### 中国农工民主党电唁杨虎城将军家属

1950-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    中国农工民主党电唁杨虎城将军家属
    【本报讯】中国农工民主党中央委员会电唁杨虎城将军长子杨拯民将军称：现在全国领土除西藏、海南岛及台湾外均已完全解放，中华人民共和国中央人民政府已经成立，各民主党派均已参加政权，杨将军的宏愿不但已完全实现，而且已远远超过，杨将军可以含笑于泉下。杨将军是人民的英雄，他的英名将永垂不朽。谨电致唁。
