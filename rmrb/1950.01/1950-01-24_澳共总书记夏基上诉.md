Title: Australian Communist Party General Secretary Sharkey Appeals
Author: 
Date: 1950-01-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd: TASS, Sydney, 22nd: Australian Communist Party General Secretary Sharkey has, through his defense lawyer, filed an appeal to the New South Wales Court of Appeal against the three-year prison sentence he received for making the statement "The Australian people will never fight against the Soviet Union."

### 澳共总书记夏基上诉

1950-01-24
第4版()
专栏：

    澳共总书记夏基上诉
    【新华社北京二十三日电】塔斯社悉尼二十二日讯：澳大利亚共产党总书记夏基已经由辩护律师向新南威尔士上诉院提出上诉，反对因发表“澳大利亚人民将决不与苏联作战”的声明，而被判处三年徒刑的判决。
