Title: All Korean Workers Voice Support for Italian Workers Protesting the Modena Bloody Incident
Author:
Date: 1950-01-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th. All Korean workers unanimously support the heroic struggle of the Italian workers and express their anger and protest against the Gasperi reactionary government's slaughter of workers in Modena. The North Korean Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Italian General Confederation of Labor on the 23rd, stating: "The working class of North Korea is extremely angry upon hearing the news of the Gasperi reactionary government's brutal slaughter of workers in Modena who were demanding bread and freedom. We extend to you our class respect and militant solidarity."

The National Council of Korean Labor Unions in South Korea also sent a telegram of support on the same day, stating: "All workers in South Korea protest with great anger the Gasperi government's brutal slaughter of workers in Modena. We firmly believe that the struggle of the Italian working class against Fascism and war, under the leadership of the Italian Communist Party, will be victorious."

### 全朝工人声援意工人抗议摩登纳血腥事件

1950-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    全朝工人声援意工人抗议摩登纳血腥事件
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】全朝鲜工人一致声援意大利工人的英勇斗争，并对加斯贝利反动政府屠杀摩登纳工人的暴行，表示愤怒和抗议。北朝鲜职业总同盟二十三日致电意大利总工会称：“北朝鲜的工人阶级，在听到加斯贝利反动政府残酷屠杀要求面包和自由的摩登纳工人的消息后，极为愤怒。我们谨向你们致以阶级的敬意和战斗的声援。”
    南朝鲜的朝鲜劳动组合全国评议会，也于同日发出声援的电报称：“南朝鲜全体工人以极大的愤怒，抗议加斯贝利政府屠杀摩登纳工人的暴行。我们坚信，反对法西斯主义和战争的意大利工人阶级的斗争，在意大利共产党的领导下，一定会胜利”。
