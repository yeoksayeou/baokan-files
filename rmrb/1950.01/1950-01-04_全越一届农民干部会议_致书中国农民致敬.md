Title: First All-Vietnam Conference of Peasant Cadres Sends Message of Respect to Chinese Peasants
Author:
Date: 1950-01-04
Page: 4

    First All-Vietnam Conference of Peasant Cadres
    Sends Message of Respect to Chinese Peasants
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 3rd] The first All-Vietnam Conference of Peasant Cadres sent a message of respect to Chinese peasants on the 23rd of last month. The original text reads:
    Chinese peasant brothers and sisters:
    The first All-Vietnam Conference of Peasant Cadres opened on November 28th and closed on December 7th. The content of this conference was to review past work and determine the guiding principles for post-conference activities, in order to strive for an early victory in the Vietnamese war of resistance against the French, and unanimously resolved to extend to you our greetings of solidarity and affection. The founding of the People's Republic of China fills us with infinite joy. We firmly believe that under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, your unity with people of all strata will be increasingly consolidated in the cause of thoroughly annihilating reactionary forces and maintaining world peace and democracy. Under the Vietnam National United Front, we are determined to fight resolutely for national independence and lasting world peace. The Vietnamese war of resistance will surely be victorious.
    Long live the People's Republic of China!
    Long live the Democratic Republic of Vietnam!
    Long live Chairman Mao!
    Long live President Ho!
    Long live Vietnamese-Chinese friendship!

### 全越一届农民干部会议  致书中国农民致敬

1950-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    全越一届农民干部会议
    致书中国农民致敬
    【新华社北京三日电】越南全国第一次农民干部会议于上月二十三日致书中国农民致敬，原书称：
    中国农民兄弟姐妹们：
    越南全国第一次农民干部会议开幕于十一月二十八日，闭幕于十二月七日。这次会议的内容，是检讨过去工作和确定会后活动方针，以争取越南抗法战争的早日赢得胜利，并一致决议向你们致以团结和亲爱的敬礼。中华人民共和国的成立，使我们感到无限的快慰。我们坚信在毛主席和中国共产党的英明领导下，你们与各阶层人民的团结，在彻底歼灭反动力量和保持世界和平与民主的事业中，将日益巩固起来。我们在越南民族统一战线下，誓必坚决为民族的独立和世界的永久和平而战斗，越南抗战一定胜利。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    越南民主共和国万岁！
    毛主席万岁！
    胡主席万岁！
    越华友谊万岁！
