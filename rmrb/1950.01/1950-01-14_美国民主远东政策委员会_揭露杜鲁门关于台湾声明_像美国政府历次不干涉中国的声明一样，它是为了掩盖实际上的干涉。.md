Title: US Democratic Far East Policy Committee Exposes Truman's Statement on Taiwan: Like previous US government statements on non-interference in China, it is intended to cover up actual interference.
Author:
Date: 1950-01-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th - TASS, New York, 9th: The American Democratic Far East Policy Committee issued a statement to the press, exposing Truman's statement on Taiwan, saying: Like previous US government statements on non-interference in China, this statement is intended to cover up actual interference. The statement pointed out that Truman had stated in 1945 that the United States was not prepared to militarily intervene in the Chinese civil war, but after this statement, the United States immediately provided unprecedented military and economic "aid" to the Kuomintang government, spending more than six billion US dollars.

The statement pointed out that, as the New York Times recently reported, 250 tanks and more than 100 American so-called "retired" military officers have been sent to Taiwan.

The statement pointed out: Truman's statement of non-interference in Taiwan and the fanatical remarks of American imperialist representatives such as Senators Nolan, Smith, Taft, and former President Hoover, advocating for the US military to invade and occupy Taiwan, are in fact merely a division of labor among those who share the same goal - namely, to prevent or delay US recognition of the People's Republic of China.

The committee declared that it believed that an increasing number of Americans favored a policy of "finally abandoning all efforts to obstruct and undermine the democratic movements of colonial peoples...recognizing the new People's Republic of China and trading with it in a friendly manner, and Taiwan should obtain its rightful place in this republic. This policy requires strict non-interference in the internal affairs of all Far Eastern countries and the restoration of a friendly and mutually beneficial policy."

### 美国民主远东政策委员会  揭露杜鲁门关于台湾声明  像美国政府历次不干涉中国的声明一样，它是为了掩盖实际上的干涉。

1950-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    美国民主远东政策委员会
    揭露杜鲁门关于台湾声明
    像美国政府历次不干涉中国的声明一样，它是为了掩盖实际上的干涉。
    【新华社北京十三日电】据塔斯社纽约九日电：美民主远东政策委员会向报界发表声明，揭露杜鲁门关于台湾的声明说：像美国政府历次不干涉中国的声明一样，这个声明是为了掩盖实际上的干涉。声明指出：杜鲁门在一九四五年曾声明说，美国不准备对中国内战进行军事干涉，但在这声明之后，美国迅即对国民党政府进行空前的军事与经济“援助”，化了六十亿美元以上。
    声明指出，如纽约时报最近报道的，已有二百五十辆坦克和一百多名美国的所谓“退休”军官送到台湾。
    声明指出：杜鲁门的不干涉台湾的声明，和美参议员诺兰、史密斯、塔夫脱和前总统胡佛之类美国帝国主义的狂妄代表的主张由美国出兵侵占台湾的热狂言论，事实上只是那些怀有同一目的——即阻止或拖延美国承认中华人民共和国——的人中间的一种分工。
    委员会宣称它相信，愈来愈多的美国人赞成这样一种政策，即“最后放弃一切阻挠和破坏殖民地人民的民主运动的努力……承认新的中华人民共和国并与之友好贸易，台湾应在这个共和国中获得其应有的地位。这种政策要求严格不干涉一切远东国家的内政并恢复友好互助政策”。
