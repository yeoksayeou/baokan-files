Title: 26th Anniversary of Lenin's Death: Korean People Prepare to Commemorate

Author:
Date: 1950-01-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 11th: January 21st marks the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death. The people of North Korea are preparing to hold lectures, symposiums, and photo exhibitions on Lenin's life to commemorate this great proletarian revolutionary teacher, the creator of the Bolshevik Party and the Soviet state. The Korean-Soviet Cultural Association will hold a memorial meeting for Lenin's death at that time, and theaters will also screen films such as "Lenin in October."

### 列宁逝世廿六周年  朝鲜人民准备纪念

1950-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    列宁逝世廿六周年
    朝鲜人民准备纪念
    【新华社平壤十一日电】一月二十一日为列宁逝世二十六周年纪念日，北朝鲜人民准备举办有关列宁生平的讲演会，座谈会和照片展览会，以纪念这位伟大的无产阶级革命导师、布尔什维克党和苏维埃国家的创造者。朝苏文化协会届时将举行列宁逝世纪念会，各影院也将放映《列宁在十月》等影片。
