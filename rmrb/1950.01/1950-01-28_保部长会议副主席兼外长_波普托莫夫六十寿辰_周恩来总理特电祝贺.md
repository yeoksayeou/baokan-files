Title: Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Foreign Minister of Bulgaria, Pop Tomov, Celebrates Sixtieth Birthday; Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Telegram of Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1950-01-28
Page: 1

    Bulgarian Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Foreign Minister
    Pop Tomov Celebrates Sixtieth Birthday
    Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Telegram of Congratulations
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 27th] Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to congratulate Vladimir Poptomov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Bulgaria, on his sixtieth birthday. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Vladimir Poptomov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Bulgaria:
    On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to you and wish you good health.
                        Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
                                        January 25, 1950

### 保部长会议副主席兼外长  波普托莫夫六十寿辰  周恩来总理特电祝贺

1950-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    保部长会议副主席兼外长
    波普托莫夫六十寿辰
    周恩来总理特电祝贺
    【新华社北京二十七日电】中华人民共和国总理兼外交部长周恩来电贺保加利亚部长会议副主席兼外交部长波普托莫夫六十寿辰，电文如下：
    保加利亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼外交部长乌拉季米尔·波普托莫夫部长先生：
    欣逢阁下六十大庆，谨在此向阁下致热诚的贺意，并祝阁下康健。
                        中华人民共和国总理兼外交部部长  周恩来
                                        一九五○年一月二十五日
