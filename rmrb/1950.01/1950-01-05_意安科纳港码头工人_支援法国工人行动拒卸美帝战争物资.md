Title: Italian Ancona Dockworkers Support French Workers' Action, Refuse to Unload US Imperialist War Materials
Author:
Date: 1950-01-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th: Rome dispatch: Dockworkers in the port of Ancona, Italy, have recently supported the movement of dockworkers in Dunkirk, Marseilles, Saint-Nazaire, and other ports in France to refuse to unload American war materials. The Italian Communist Party's newspaper, *Unità*, reported: At a general meeting, Ancona dockworkers passed a telegram to French dockworkers stating: "We approve of your lofty peace movement and have decided to take the same action. In our port, all munitions transported from the United States and other countries, prepared for use in new wars of aggression, will be refused unloading. The warmongers say: if we unload munitions, we will have work and bread, but we would rather starve than take wages from executioners. We will unite all Italian dockworkers, learn from your example, and insist that all Italian ports only be used for loading and unloading materials that benefit the people and national economic recovery, and also for peaceful endeavors."

### 意安科纳港码头工人  支援法国工人行动拒卸美帝战争物资

1950-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    意安科纳港码头工人
    支援法国工人行动拒卸美帝战争物资
    【新华社北京四日电】罗马讯：意大利安科纳港码头工人顷支持法国敦刻尔克、马赛、圣那最尔及其他港口码头工人拒绝起卸美国战争物资的运动。意共机关报团结报报道：安科纳码头工人在大会上通过致法国码头工人的电文称：“我们赞成你们崇高的和平运动，并且决定采取同样的行动。在我们的港口里，所有自美国和其他国家运来准备为新的侵略战争使用的军火，将一概不予起卸。战争贩子说：起卸军火，我们就有了工作和面包，但我们宁愿饿死，也不从刽子手那里拿工钱。我们要团结全意大利的码头工人，学习你们的榜样，并坚持把所有意大利港口只用于装卸一切为谋人民福利与国家经济复兴同时也是用于和平事业的物资。”
