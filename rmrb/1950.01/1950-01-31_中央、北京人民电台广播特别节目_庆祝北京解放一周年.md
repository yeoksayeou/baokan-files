Central, Beijing People's Radio Broadcast Special Program
Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Liberation of Beijing

    [Our Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a special program celebrating the first anniversary of the liberation of Beijing from 20:20 to 20:50 today (31st): (1) Actual recording of the People's Liberation Army's entry into the city on February 3, 1949; (2) "Changes in Workers' Thoughts in the Year Since the Liberation of Beijing as Seen from Workers' Poems." 17:30 Employee Time: "Introduction to the Chinese Workers' Pictorial" First Issue. 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: General Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 9, "Electric Arc and Electric Furnace". 18:00 First Literary Program: Single-string "Great Production." 21:00 Second Literary Program: Songs Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Liberation of Beijing. 18:45 New Songs: "Singing of Chairman Mao" etc.
    [Further News] Beijing People's Radio, 12:30: Radio Gala Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Liberation of Beijing. 16:30 Children's Program: Children from the North China Yucai Primary School Perform the Third and Fourth Acts of the Opera "Little Red Riding Hood Escapes Danger." 17:00 Continue Teaching "The Sun Comes Out, the Sky is Red." 19:15 Municipal Voice: Deputy Director Zhang Minghe of the Public Security Bureau gives a lecture on "Public Security Work in the Past Year."

### 中央、北京人民电台广播特别节目  庆祝北京解放一周年

1950-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    中央、北京人民电台广播特别节目
    庆祝北京解放一周年
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（三十一）日二十点二十分到二十点五十分播送庆祝北京市解放一周年特别节目：（一）一九四九年二月三日人民解放军入城式的实况录音，（二）“从工人的诗歌看北京解放一年来工人思想的变化”。十七点三十分职工时间：“介绍中国工人画刊”第一期。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：电学常识第九讲“电弧与电熔炉”。十八点第一次文艺节目：单弦“大生产”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：庆祝北京市解放一周年歌曲。十八点四十五分新歌曲：“歌唱毛主席”等。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分：庆祝北京解放一周年广播联欢会。十六点三十分儿童节目：华北育才小学小朋友们演唱歌剧《小红帽脱险记》第三幕、第四幕。十七点继续教唱《太阳一出满天红》。十九点十五分市政之声：公安局张明河副局长讲演“一年来的公安工作”。
