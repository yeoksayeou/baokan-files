Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Issues Order: Employees of Former Kuomintang Government Agencies in Hong Kong Must Protect State Property and Await Takeover

Author:
Date: 1950-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 9th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government issued an order today, instructing all employees of former Kuomintang central and local government agencies stationed in Hong Kong to be responsible for protecting state property and awaiting takeover. The full text of the order is as follows:

Order of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China

To the responsible personnel and all employees of all political, diplomatic, financial, economic, cultural, educational, overseas Chinese affairs, and other agencies of the former Kuomintang central and local governments stationed in Hong Kong:

You must all remain at your posts, protect state property and archives, and await takeover, not allowing reactionaries to engage in any theft, destruction, transfer, or concealment. All original employees may be employed according to their abilities. Those who have contributed to the protection of state property will be rewarded. Those who engage in theft, destruction, transfer, or concealment will be prosecuted.

						Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government Zhou Enlai
						January 9, 1950, in Beijing

### 周恩来总理发布命令  驻港原国民党政府机构员工务须保护国家财产听候接收

1950-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理发布命令
    驻港原国民党政府机构员工务须保护国家财产听候接收
    【新华社北京九日电】我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理于本日发布命令，令驻在香港的原国民党中央政府和地方政府的一切机构的员工，负责保护国家财产，听候接收。命令全文如下：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院命令
    令驻在香港的原属国民党中央政府和地方政府的一切政治、外交、财政、经济、文化、教育、侨务等机构的主管人员及全体员工：
    你们务须各守岗位，保护国家财产档案，听候接收，不让反动分子有任何偷窃、破毁、转移、隐匿等情事。原有员工，均可量才录用。其中保护国家财产有功者，将予以奖励。其有偷窃、破毁、转移、隐匿等情者，必予究办。
                                中央人民政府政务院总理  周恩来
                                 一九五○年一月九日于北京
