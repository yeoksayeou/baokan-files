Title: Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party in Beijing Mobilizes Beijing Party Members to Encourage Purchase of Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1950-01-13
Page: 1

    The Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party in Beijing
    Mobilizes Beijing Party Members to Encourage Purchase of Public Bonds
    [This newspaper] After receiving the call from the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party to solicit the People's Victory Parity Unit Public Bonds, the Beijing Preparatory Committee of the Party convened a meeting of party members in the city yesterday afternoon to mobilize all party members to carry out publicity and solicitation work for the People's Victory Parity Unit Public Bonds. It was decided to establish an organization for soliciting public bonds within the party and to launch various groups to carry out publicity, discussions, and solicitation activities.

### 中国农工民主党京市整委会  动员京市党员劝购公债

1950-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中国农工民主党京市整委会
    动员京市党员劝购公债
    【本报讯】中国农工民主党北京市整理委员会接到该党中央发出劝募胜利折实公债的号召后，于昨日下午在该会召开党员大会，动员全市党员，进行人民胜利折实公债的宣传和劝募工作。决定在党内设立劝募公债组织及发动各小组展开宣传讨论及劝募活动。
