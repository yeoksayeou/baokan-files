Title: Growing Watermelons—New Folk Song
Author: Keming
Date: 1950-01-28
Page: 6
Growing Watermelons
—New Folk Song
Keming recording

Little boy, goes to Nanwa
Digs a pit, plants a watermelon
First grows leaves, then blooms flowers
Leaves are green, flowers are yellow
Who gets to taste the watermelon first when it grows?
Send it to Chairman Mao to taste
Why?
He led us to liberation
Divided land and houses

### 种西瓜——新民歌
克明
1950-01-28
第6版()
专栏：

    种西瓜
    ——新民歌
    克明记
    小小子  上南洼
    刨个坑  种西瓜
    先长叶  后开花
    叶绿  花黄
    长了西瓜谁先尝
    送给毛主席尝
    为什么
    他领导咱们翻了身
    分了地和房
