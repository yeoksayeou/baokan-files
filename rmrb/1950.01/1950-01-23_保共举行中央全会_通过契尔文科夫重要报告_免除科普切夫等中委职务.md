Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Holds Central Plenary Session; Passes Important Report by Chervenkov; Removes Kopchev and Others from Central Committee Positions
Author:
Date: 1950-01-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd: TASS, Sofia - The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party held a plenary session here on the 16th and 17th. The plenary session heard a report by Chervenkov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, on the party's pressing tasks and the lessons learned from the struggle to expose and defeat the Kostov gang, as well as the shortcomings in the party's activities. The report sparked heated debate, after which the plenary session unanimously adopted a resolution on the report.

The plenary session also discussed some organizational issues and adopted corresponding resolutions on these issues: Boris Kopchev, Hristo Dobrev, Gocho Grozev, Ivan Bachvarov, Velko Gotev, Hristo Stoichev and others were removed from their positions as members of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party because they had proven unable to fulfill their duties and had shown a lack of vigilance; Dobri Terpeshev was removed from his position as a member of the Politburo of the Party Central Committee; Todor Zhivkov (former Chairman of the Sofia City People's Council) and Georgi Tsankov (General Secretary of the Bulgarian General Trade Union) were elected as Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party.

### 保共举行中央全会  通过契尔文科夫重要报告  免除科普切夫等中委职务

1950-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

    保共举行中央全会
    通过契尔文科夫重要报告
    免除科普切夫等中委职务
    【新华社北京二十二日电】塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚共产党中央委员会于十六、十七两日在此间举行全体会议。全会听取了保加利亚共产党中央委员会书记契尔文科夫关于党的迫切任务和从揭露并击败科斯托夫伙帮的斗争中、以及党的活动之缺点中所得到的教训的报告。该报告引起了热烈的辩论，辩论完毕后，全会一致通过了一项关于该报告的决议。
    全会也讨论了组织方面的一些问题，并对这些问题通过了相应的决议：免除鲍里斯·科普切夫，克利斯提奥·多布列夫，哥卓·哥罗塞夫，伊凡·巴赤瓦罗夫，维尔科·哥捷夫，克利斯提奥·斯托伊捷夫等的保加利亚共产党中央委员的职务，因为他们业已证明不能胜任他们的工作，而且表现出缺乏警觉性；免除了道布利·特比舍夫的党中央政治局委员的职务；选举了托多尔·吉夫科夫（前索非亚市人民议会主席）和格奥尔吉·詹科夫（保加利亚总工会总书记）为保加利亚共产党中央委员会书记。
