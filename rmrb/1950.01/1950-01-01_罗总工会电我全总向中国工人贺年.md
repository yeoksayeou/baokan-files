Title: Romanian General Confederation of Labor Cables All-China Federation of Trade Unions to Extend New Year Greetings to Chinese Workers
Author:
Date: 1950-01-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 31st: Apostol, Chairman of the Romanian General Confederation of Labor, recently sent the following New Year's telegram to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on behalf of the Romanian workers: On the occasion of the New Year 1950, we extend our congratulations to the heroic Chinese workers and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on the victory of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in liberating the Chinese mainland from the enslavement of imperialism and the Kuomintang. The workers, engineers, and staff of the Romanian People's Republic respectfully wish you further victories in the struggle to liberate all of China's territory and to build a strong China, making it an outstanding force in the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the invincible socialist country of the Soviet Union, led by the great genius of mankind, Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin.
       G. H. Apostol, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Romanian People's Republic General Confederation of Labor

### 罗总工会电我全总向中国工人贺年

1950-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    罗总工会电我全总向中国工人贺年
    【新华社布拉格三十一日电】罗马尼亚总工会主席阿波斯托尔，顷代表罗马尼亚工人向中华全国总工会致新年贺电如下：值此一九五零年新年，我们谨向英勇的中国工人及中华全国总工会，庆贺中国人民解放军，从帝国主义与国民党的奴役下解放中国大陆的胜利。罗马尼亚人民共和国的工人、工程师和职员，敬祝你们在解放全中国领土的斗争中，获得进一步的胜利，并建设强大的中国，使之成为以人类伟大天才约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林所领导的不可战胜的社会主义国家苏联为首的和平、民主与社会主义阵线中的杰出力量。
       罗马尼亚人民共和国总工会中央委员会主席Ｇ·Ｈ·阿波斯托尔
