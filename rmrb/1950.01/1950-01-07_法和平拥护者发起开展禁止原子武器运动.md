Title: French Peace Advocates Launch Campaign to Ban Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1950-01-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 6th (Telegraph from TASS): The campaign to ban atomic weapons, launched by French peace advocates, began on the 5th. Farge, Joliot-Curie, Brizon, and Dillon held a press conference in Paris on the 4th. Farge explained the goals and tasks of the campaign, stating: "The movement to ban atomic weapons is the initial stage of a universal disarmament movement. We demand that all parliamentarians issue public statements taking a clear stance on the movement to ban atomic weapons." Vigier stated: "Many city councils have passed resolutions declaring the need to declare atomic weapons illegal."

### 法和平拥护者发起开展禁止原子武器运动

1950-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    法和平拥护者发起开展禁止原子武器运动
    【新华社北京六日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：法国和平拥护者发动的禁止原子武器运动，已于五日开始。法奇、约里奥——居里、布立埃和狄隆等于四日在巴黎举行记者招待会，法奇说明这一运动的目的与任务称：禁止原子武器的运动，是普遍解除军备运动的初步阶段，我们要求一切议员发表公开声明，对禁止原子武器的运动采取明确的态度。维基尼称：许多市议会已通过决定，声明需要宣布原子武器为非法。
