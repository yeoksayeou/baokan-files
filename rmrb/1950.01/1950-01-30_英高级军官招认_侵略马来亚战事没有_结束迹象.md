Title: High-ranking British Officer Admits Malayan Invasion Shows No "Signs of Ending"
Author:
Date: 1950-01-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 29th - According to a report from the Telecommunications Agency: British Commander of the Army in the Far East, Harding, stated in Singapore on the 27th that the Malayan war currently shows no signs of ending. At the same time, due to the increasing activity of the Malayan People's Liberation Army, the rubber production of the British colonialists in Malaya has greatly decreased. Last year's rubber production decreased by 26,000 tons compared to the previous year.

### 英高级军官招认  侵略马来亚战事没有“结束迹象”

1950-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    英高级军官招认
    侵略马来亚战事没有“结束迹象”
    【新华社北京二十九日电】据电通社消息：英国驻远东陆军司令哈丁二十七日在新加坡发表谈话，承认目前马来亚战争尚无结束迹象。同时，由于马来亚人民解放军的日益活跃，英国殖民者在马来亚的橡胶产量，已大大降低。去年橡胶产量已较前年减少了二万六千吨。
