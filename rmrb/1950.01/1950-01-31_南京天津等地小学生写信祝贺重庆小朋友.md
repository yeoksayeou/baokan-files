Title: Primary School Students in Nanjing, Tianjin, and Other Places Write Letters to Congratulate Children in Chongqing

Author:

Date: 1950-01-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, January 30th: Primary school students in Chongqing recently received more than 40 letters from primary school students in cities such as Nanjing, Tianjin, and Jinan, congratulating the children in Chongqing for gaining a free and happy life after liberation. A primary school student named Shumin in Nanjing happily said in her letter: "You are now living a free life like us." Bai Ping, a child from Tianjin, wrote on the letter: "We people on the Bohai coast celebrate your liberation and rejoice for the liberation of the motherland." All the students of Li Qingxiang Street Primary School in Jinan sent a letter to the primary school students in Chongqing to congratulate them on their liberation, filled with excitement, writing: "Now we are all happy children of the Mao Zedong era."

### 南京天津等地小学生写信祝贺重庆小朋友

1950-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    南京天津等地小学生写信祝贺重庆小朋友
    【新华社重庆三十日电】重庆的小学生最近收到南京、天津、济南等城市的小学生寄来的四十多封信，祝贺重庆的小朋友们在解放以后获得了自由快乐的生活。南京市一个名叫淑敏的小学生在信中愉快地说：“你们现在也和我们一样过着自由的日子了。”天津的小朋友白萍在信上写着：“我们远在渤海边的人，为你们获得解放而庆祝，为祖国获得解放而欢欣。”济南市立庆祥街小学全体同学向重庆小学生祝贺解放的信中充满了兴奋的情绪写着：“现在我们都是毛泽东时代幸福的儿童。”
