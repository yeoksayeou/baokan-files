Title: First North Korean Ambassador to China, Li Zhouyuan, Departs for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th. Li Zhouyuan, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the People's Republic of China, along with Counselor Cui Ying, Jin Guofu, and over twenty staff members and their families, departed from Pyongyang by train at 1:00 PM on the 18th for Beijing. Among those who saw them off at the station were Park Heon-yeong, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Heo Heon, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly, and Hong Nam-pyo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and other important government officials.

### 朝鲜驻华首任大使李周渊启程来京

1950-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜驻华首任大使李周渊启程来京
    【新华社平壤十九日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国派驻中华人民共和国首任特命全权大使李周渊，偕参事崔英、金国辅及随员眷属等一行二十余人，于十八日午后一时自平壤乘车赴北京。到车站欢送的，有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议副主席兼外交部长朴宪永、最高人民会议议长许宪、最高常任委员会副委员长洪南杓等政府要员。
