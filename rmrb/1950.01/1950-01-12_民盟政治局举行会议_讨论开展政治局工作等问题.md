Title: League of Democratic Parties' Political Bureau Holds Meeting to Discuss Issues such as Carrying Out the Work of the Political Bureau
Author:
Date: 1950-01-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 10th

    The Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League held a plenary meeting on January 8th. Zhang Lan, Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Luo Longji, Shi Liang, Zhou Xinmin, Ma Xulun, Peng Zemin and others attended. The meeting discussed issues such as actively carrying out the work of the Political Bureau, leadership of league affairs, and mastery of policies. Zhang Bojun was also appointed to draft various work plans. In addition, the first joint meeting of the headquarters of the China Democratic League was held on January 9th. Zhang Lan, Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Zhou Jianren, Xu Guangping, Zhou Jingwen, Liu Wang Liming, Zhou Xinmin and other 21 people attended. The meeting decided that the various committees should be established within this month, formulate work guidelines and plans, and immediately start work. Each department will report every half month, and a joint meeting will be held with the Central Standing Committee once every half month.

### 民盟政治局举行会议  讨论开展政治局工作等问题

1950-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    民盟政治局举行会议
    讨论开展政治局工作等问题
    【新华社北京十日电】中国民主同盟中央政治局于一月八日举行全体委员会议，到张澜、沈钧儒、章伯钧、罗隆基、史良、周新民、马叙伦、彭泽民等。会议中讨论了积极开展政治局工作、盟务的领导、推动和政策的掌握等问题。并推定章伯钧起草各项工作方案。又，民盟总部各处会首次联席会议于一月九日举行。到张澜、沈钧儒、章伯钧、周建人、许广平、周鲸文、刘王立明、周新民等二十一人。会议讨论决定各委员会应于本月内成立，拟定工作方针计划，并即展开工作，各处会每半月举行汇报一次，每半月与中常会举行联席会议一次。
