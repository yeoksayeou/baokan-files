Title: Vietnamese Student Congress Sends Greetings to the All-China Students' Federation

Author:
Date: 1950-01-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th - Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 4th: The Vietnamese Student Congress held last December sent greetings to the All-China Students' Federation and the International Union of Students. In a telegram to the All-China Students' Federation, the Vietnam Students' Union expressed its deep admiration for the heroic struggle of Chinese students and pledged to unite closely with Chinese students to eliminate foreign colonialism and achieve national independence.

The Vietnam Students' Union also warmly responded to the call of the International Union of Students and the World Federation of Democratic Youth to organize youth and students worldwide to fight imperialism with all their strength. The telegram stated that Vietnamese students have been fighting against French colonialism for four years, and they are convinced that their struggle will help establish world peace and democracy.

### 越南学生代表大会电我全国学联致敬

1950-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    越南学生代表大会电我全国学联致敬
    【新华社北京五日电】越南通讯社北越四日讯：去年十二月举行的越南学生代表大会，曾致电中华全国学生联合会和国际学生联合会致敬。在给中华全国学生联合会的电报中，越南学生联合会对中国学生的英勇斗争表示深切钦佩，并保证与中国学生亲密地团结起来，以消灭外国殖民主义，获得国家的独立。
    越南学生联合会还热烈响应国际学生联合会和世界民主青年联盟关于组织全世界青年和学生对帝国主义作全力斗争的号召。电报称，越南学生曾对法国殖民主义者作战四年，他们确信他们的斗争将有助于建立世界和平与民主。
