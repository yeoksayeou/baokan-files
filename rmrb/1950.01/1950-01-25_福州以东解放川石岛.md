Title: Liberation of Chuanshi Island East of Fuzhou
Author:
Date: 1950-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 24th. A unit of the People's Liberation Army on the Fujian front crossed the sea on the evening of the 9th of this month and captured Chuanshi Island, directly east of Fuzhou, capturing 58 bandits and seizing some weapons.

### 福州以东解放川石岛

1950-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    福州以东解放川石岛
    【新华社福州二十四日电】福建前线人民解放军一部，于本月九日黄昏渡海攻占福州正东的川石岛，俘匪五十八名，缴获武器一部。
