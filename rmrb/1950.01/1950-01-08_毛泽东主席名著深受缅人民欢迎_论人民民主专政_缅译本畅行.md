Title: Chairman Mao's Famous Works Are Deeply Popular Among the Burmese People; Burmese Translation of "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" Widely Circulated
Author:
Date: 1950-01-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th. According to a report from Rangoon cited by the Thai-Chinese newspaper Quan Min Bao on December 2nd of last year: The famous work of the Chinese people's leader, Chairman Mao Zedong, "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," was translated into Burmese and published three months ago, and it has been greatly welcomed by the Burmese people, selling out instantly. Now, a new translation has appeared in Rangoon bookstores to meet the needs of readers.

### 毛泽东主席名著深受缅人民欢迎  “论人民民主专政”缅译本畅行

1950-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席名著深受缅人民欢迎
    “论人民民主专政”缅译本畅行
    【新华社北京七日电】据泰国华侨报纸全民报去年十二月二日引仰光消息称：中国人民领袖毛泽东主席的名著《论人民民主专政》，已于三月前被译成缅文本出版，大受缅甸人民的欢迎，瞬即售完。现在，仰光书店中已出现一种新译本，以适应读者的需要。
