Title: Shanghai-Guangzhou Express Through Train

Author: 
Date: 1950-01-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 8th. The Shanghai-Guangzhou direct express train has successfully completed its trial run. The first train departed from Shanghai at 3:20 PM on the 5th and arrived in Guangzhou at 8:30 PM on the 8th, taking 76 hours. The train's operational performance was good. The direct service between the two cities is scheduled to officially begin on the 12th.

### 上海广州快车直达

1950-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    上海广州快车直达
    【新华社广州八日电】上海广州直通快车试车成功。首次列车系于五日下午三时二十分由上海开出，至八日下午八时三十分抵广州，历时七十六小时，驶行情况良好。两地直达通车已定于十二日正式开始。
