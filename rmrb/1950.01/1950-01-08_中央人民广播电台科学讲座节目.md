Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Science Lecture Program
Author:
Date: 1950-01-08
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th -- The Central People's Broadcasting Station's natural science lecture program for the week beginning on the 8th is as follows:

    8th: How was Penicillin Invented? (Wen Jize)
    9th: Electricity and Daily Life (General Knowledge of Electricity, Part 1), (Shen Keqi)
    10th: Static Electricity from Friction (General Knowledge of Electricity, Part 2), (Xue Qinfang)
    11th: Static Electricity from Induction (General Knowledge of Electricity, Part 3). (Ibid.)
    12th: Kaolin. (Tao Shilong)
    13th: Limestone. (Zhao Pengda)

    All of the above programs will be broadcast at 16:30 every evening. Among them, General Knowledge of Electricity is a systematic lecture series, collectively compiled by Professor Xue Qinfang of the Physics Department of Peking University, etc., totaling more than twenty lectures, which will be broadcast successively.

### 中央人民广播电台科学讲座节目

1950-01-08
第3版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台科学讲座节目
    【新华社北京七日电】中央人民广播电台从八日起一星期的自然科学讲座节目如下：
    八日  青霉素是怎样发明的？（温济泽）
    九日  电和日常生活（电学常识之一），（沈克琦）
    十日  摩擦带电（电学常识之二），（薛琴访）
    十一日  感应带电（电学常识之三）。（仝上）
    十二日  高岭土。（陶世龙）
    十三日  石灰石。（赵鹏大）
    以上各节目，在每晚十六点三十分播送。其中电学常识是有系统的讲座，由北京大学物理系薛琴访教授等集体编撰，共二十余讲，将陆续广播。
