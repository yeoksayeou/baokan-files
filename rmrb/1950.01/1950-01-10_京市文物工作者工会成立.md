Title: Beijing Municipal Cultural Relics Workers' Union Established
Author: Li Hui
Date: 1950-01-10
Page: 3

    Beijing Municipal Cultural Relics Workers' Union Established
    [This newspaper reports] The Beijing Municipal Cultural Relics Workers' Union, after convening its first representative assembly on December 20th of last year and electing Hang Chenggen and twelve others as executive committee members, held its official establishment meeting at 6:30 PM on the 7th of this month in the Shenwumen Tower Auditorium of the Palace Museum branch. Over nine hundred members participated. Xiao Ming, director of the preparatory committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, attended and spoke, pointing out the relationship between the union and the administration, and encouraging everyone to ensure the completion of administrative tasks and to do a good job in the cultural relics work of the people's capital. Wang Yejiu, Deputy Director of the Cultural Relics Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, spoke, hoping that everyone would raise their class consciousness, overcome the employee mentality, and strive to complete the 1950 cultural relics work tasks of the capital.
                                                  (Li Hui)

### 京市文物工作者工会成立
黎晖
1950-01-10
第3版()
专栏：

    京市文物工作者工会成立
    【本报讯】北京市文物工作者工会自去年十二月二十日召开第一届代表大会，选出杭承艮等十三人为执委后，于本月七日下午六时半在故宫分会神武门楼礼堂召开正式成立大会，参加会员九百余人。市总工会筹委会主任萧明出席讲话，指出工会和行政的关系，勉励大家保证行政任务的完成，搞好人民首都的文物工作。文化部文物局副局长王冶秋讲话中，希望大家提高阶级觉悟，克服雇佣观点，为完成一九五○年度首都的文物工作任务而努力。
                                                  （黎晖）
