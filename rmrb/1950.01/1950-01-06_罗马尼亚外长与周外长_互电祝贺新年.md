Title: Romanian Foreign Minister and Foreign Minister Zhou Exchange Greetings for the New Year
Author:
Date: 1950-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 4th: Romanian People's Republic Foreign Minister Anna Pauker sent a telegram to Chinese Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai on December 31st of last year to congratulate him on the New Year. Minister Zhou has replied with a telegram of thanks. The exchange of telegrams is as follows:

(1) Telegram from Romanian People's Republic Foreign Minister Pauker: To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    On this New Year's occasion, please accept my sincere congratulations and wishes to you, congratulating the People's Republic of China on achieving new victories, together with the socialist and democratic forces of peace throughout the world under the leadership of the great Soviet Union, in the struggle against imperialists and instigators of war.
                  Anna Pauker, Foreign Minister of the Romanian People's Republic
                       Bucharest, December 31, 1949

(2) Minister Zhou Enlai's reply: To Minister Anna Pauker, Foreign Ministry of the Romanian People's Republic, Bucharest:

    Your telegram is acknowledged. I sincerely thank Your Excellency for your New Year's greetings and warmly wish the Romanian People's Republic and its people even greater achievements in the coming year.
                          Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China

### 罗马尼亚外长与周外长  互电祝贺新年

1950-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚外长与周外长  互电祝贺新年
    【新华社北京四日电】罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长安娜·波克于去年十二月三十一日致电我国外交部部长周恩来祝贺新年，周部长已复电致谢。来往电文如下：
            （一）罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长波克来电：北京中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来阁下：
    当此新年之际，请您接受对您的诚恳的祝贺和愿望，祝贺中华人民共和国与伟大苏联领导下的全世界社会主义和民主的和平力量一道，在进行反对帝国主义与战争挑拨者的斗争中得到新的胜利。
                    罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长  安娜·波克
                      一九四九年十二月三十一日于布加勒斯特
            （二）周恩来部长复电：布加勒斯特罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部安娜·波克部长：
    来电敬悉。衷心感谢  阁下之新年贺电，并热诚预祝罗马尼亚人民共和国及其人民在本年内获得更伟大的成就。
                            中华人民共和国中央人民政府
                                  外交部部长周恩来
