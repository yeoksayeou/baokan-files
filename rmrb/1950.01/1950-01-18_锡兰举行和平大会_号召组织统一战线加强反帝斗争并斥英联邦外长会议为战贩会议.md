Title: Ceylon Holds Peace Conference, Calls for United Front to Strengthen Anti-Imperialist Struggle and Condemns British Commonwealth Foreign Ministers Meeting as a War-Mongering Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-01-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th—The Ceylon National Peace Conference was held in Colombo on the 14th. According to the Indian newspaper *Bharat* cited by TASS: Two thousand people attended the All-Ceylon Peace Conference hosted by the Ceylon Communist Party. The conference called on all the people of Ceylon to organize a united front to carry out the struggle against imperialism. According to a Reuters dispatch from Colombo: Pictures of Lenin, Stalin, and Mao Zedong were hung on the platform at the opening of the conference. The conference condemned the British Commonwealth Foreign Ministers' Meeting as a meeting of warmongers and called on the people of Ceylon to mobilize against the imperialist war plans.

### 锡兰举行和平大会  号召组织统一战线加强反帝斗争并斥英联邦外长会议为战贩会议

1950-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰举行和平大会
    号召组织统一战线加强反帝斗争并斥英联邦外长会议为战贩会议
    【新华社北京十七日电】锡兰全国和平大会于十四日在科伦坡举行。据塔斯社引印度《巴拉特》报消息：有两千人出席锡兰共产党主持的全锡兰和平大会。大会曾号召锡兰全体人民组织统一战线，以进行反对帝国主义的斗争。另据路透社科伦坡消息：在大会开幕时，讲台上悬挂了列宁、斯大林及毛泽东的像片。大会曾斥责不列颠联邦外长会议为战争贩子的会议，并号召锡兰人民动员起来，反对帝国主义的战争计划。
