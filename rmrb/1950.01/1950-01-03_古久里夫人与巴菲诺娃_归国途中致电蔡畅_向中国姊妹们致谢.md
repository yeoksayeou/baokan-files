Title: Mrs. Gouri and Babfenova, en route home, called Cai Chang to thank Chinese sisters

Author:
Date: 1950-01-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 2nd: Mrs. Gouri, Secretary-General of the International Democratic Women's Federation, and Babfenova, head of the Soviet delegation to the Asian Women's Representative Conference, sent telegrams from Chita to Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, while en route home, to thank Chinese sisters. The telegram reads as follows:
To Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Beijing:
	On behalf of the International Women's Federation and all the delegates of the conference, I extend my warm thanks to you for the convenience you have provided for holding the conference in Beijing and for your sincere hospitality. We will work more resolutely to implement the resolutions of the conference. Long live the unity of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and the women of the world united in the International Democratic Women's Federation!
					Mrs. Gouri
					December 29th

To Comrade Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Beijing:
	On behalf of the Soviet women, I warmly thank you for the attention you have given us during our stay in China. Our joint work will always remain in our memory. This is beneficial to further consolidate the friendship between the women of China and the Soviet Union. I sincerely wish you and your comrades good health and the ability to complete the tasks before you.
					Babfenova
					December 29th

### 古久里夫人与巴菲诺娃  归国途中致电蔡畅  向中国姊妹们致谢

1950-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    古久里夫人与巴菲诺娃
    归国途中致电蔡畅
    向中国姊妹们致谢
    【新华社北京二日电】出席亚洲妇女代表会议的国际民主妇联总书记古久里夫人和苏联代表团团长巴菲诺娃，在离京归国途中，由赤塔分别致电中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，向中国姊妹们致谢，电文如下：北京中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅：
    谨代表国际妇联和大会全体代表向你致热烈的谢意，为了你所给予我们在北京召开大会的方便和为了你的诚恳的招待。我们要更坚决地为执行大会的决议而工作。中华全国民主妇联及联合于国际民主妇联中的全世界妇女的团结万岁！
                                            古久里夫人
                                           十二月二十九日北京中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅同志：
    谨代表苏联妇女热烈地向你致谢，为了你在我们停留于中国的日子里所给予我们的关注。我们共同的工作将永远留于我们的记忆。这对于更加大大地巩固中苏两国妇女之间的友谊，是有好处的。衷心地祝贺你及你的同志们健康和完成摆在你们面前的任务之能力。
                                                      巴菲诺娃
                                              十二月二十九日
