Title: Central People's Radio Station's Special Program for Tibet Today
Author:
Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 4

    Central People's Radio Station's Special Program for Tibet Today
    [This Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a special program for Tibet in Chinese, Tibetan, and English at 22:00 today (the 29th). The content includes three items: speeches by Tibetan democratic figures in Beijing, Ngawang Kesang, Lobsang, and Dorje Tseten, to Tibetan compatriots. Three Tibetan folk songs sung in Tibetan will also be broadcast: 1. Heaven and Earth, 2. Song of Democracy, 3. Song of Auspiciousness. Other programs are as follows: 17:30 Workers' Time: All-China Federation of Trade Unions' notice regarding the 27th anniversary of the "February 7th" Strike. 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: Scientists' Story "The Father of Soviet Aviation, Zhukovsky." 20:20 International Current Affairs: "The Great Achievements of the Mongolian People's Republic." 20:30 Comprehensive Report: "How American Imperialism Deceives Its People." 18:00 First Cultural Program: Leting Drum "Chunyun's Divorce." 21:00 Second Cultural Program: Broadcasting "Mongolian Dance Music" and six other instrumental pieces. 20:30 Sunday Art and Literature: Military Songs.
    [Another News Item] Beijing People's Radio Station 12:30 Workers' Program: 1. Culture and Entertainment: Beijing Postal Administration Orchestra playing new songs, 2. Communication: "Apprenticeship Movement Universally Improves the Technical Level of Young Workers." 13:00 Listener Services: (1) Answering the question of how many countries and how many people are there in the world, (2) The Mudanjiang People's Government and Mudanjiang Enterprise Company's announcement of recruitment of technical personnel. 13:30 Miscellaneous Operas: New Beating the Dog to Persuade Husband. 16:30 Children's Program: Sun Jingxiu continues to tell several stories of Lenin. 17:00 Broadcasting new songs. 19:15 Introduction to Soviet agricultural production, animal husbandry, and food industry.

### 中央人民电台今日广播对西藏特别节目

1950-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中央人民电台今日广播对西藏特别节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台在今（二十九）天二十二点用中文、藏文和英文三种语言播送对西藏的特别节目。内容一共有三项：包括在北京的藏族民主人士昂旺格桑。罗旺和多杰才旦对藏族同胞的讲话。并同时播送三首用藏语歌唱的西藏民歌：一、天上人间、二、民主之歌，三、吉祥之歌。其他节目如下：十七点三十分职工时间：全总关于“二七”罢工二十七周年纪念的通知。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：科学家的故事“苏联航空之父楚可夫斯基”。二十点二十分国际时事：“蒙古人民共和国的伟大成就”。二十点三十分综合报导：“美帝国主义怎样欺骗他的人民”。十八点第一次文艺节目：乐亭大鼓“春云离婚”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：播送“蒙古舞曲”等六支器乐曲。八点三十分星期文艺：部队歌曲。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：一、文化娱乐：北京邮政管理局乐队演奏新歌曲，二、通讯：“拜师运动普遍提高了青年工人技术水平”。十三点听众服务：（一）回答全世界有多少国家和有多少人口的问题，（二）牡丹江人民政府和牡丹江企业公司招聘技术人员的启事。十三点三十分杂曲：新打狗劝夫。十六点三十分儿童节目：孙敬修继续讲几个列宁的故事。十七点播送新歌曲。十九点十五分介绍苏联的农业生产，牧畜业和食品工业。
