Title: Zhanjiang Military Control Commission Established
Author:
Date: 1950-01-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 8th. The Zhanjiang City (formerly Guangzhouwan) Military Control Commission of Guangdong Province was officially established on the 20th of last month, with Liu Tianfu as Director and Wu Qiang and Shen Bin as Deputy Directors. Since the liberation of Zhanjiang City, public security has been good. Schools and shops have resumed classes and business, postal and telecommunications are open, and land and water transportation is gradually recovering.

### 湛江市军管会成立

1950-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    湛江市军管会成立
    【新华社广州八日电】广东湛江市（即广州湾）军事管制委员会已于上月二十日奉命成立，刘田夫为主任，邬强、沈斌为副主任。湛江市解放后，治安良好，学校商店均已复课复业，邮电畅通，水陆交通渐次恢复。
