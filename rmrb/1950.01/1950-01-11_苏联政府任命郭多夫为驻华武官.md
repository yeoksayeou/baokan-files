Title: The Soviet Government Appoints Kudov as Military Attaché to China
Author:
Date: 1950-01-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th—The government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics has appointed Lieutenant General Kudov as Military Attaché to the Embassy of the Soviet Union in the People's Republic of China. Lieutenant General Kudov formally met with Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, on the afternoon of the 8th of this month, and paid courtesy calls to General Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and General Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff, on the morning of the 10th.

### 苏联政府任命郭多夫为驻华武官

1950-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联政府任命郭多夫为驻华武官
    【新华社北京十日电】苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府任命郭多夫中将为驻中华人民共和国大使馆武官。郭多夫中将于本月八日下午正式拜会中华人民共和国外交部周恩来部长，并于十日上午分别拜会人民革命军事委员会副主席朱德将军及代理总参谋长聂荣臻将军。
