Title: Polish-Soviet Friendship Association and Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Exchange New Year's Greetings, Encouraging Each Other to Strengthen Work on Soviet-Chinese Friendship
Author:
Date: 1950-01-08
Page: 1

    Polish-Soviet Friendship Association and Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Exchange New Year's Greetings, Encouraging Each Other to Strengthen Work on Soviet-Chinese Friendship
    [Reported by this newspaper] The Polish-Soviet Friendship Association and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters exchanged telegrams to congratulate each other on the New Year. The Polish-Soviet Friendship Association's telegram reads: To the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters, Beijing:

    The Polish-Soviet Friendship Association extends New Year's greetings to you and wishes you further success in strengthening your work on Soviet-Chinese friendship for the happiness of the people of your country and the consolidation of world peace.
                                  President Swiatkowski
                                 Secretary-General Turingneo
                              December 31, 1949

    The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters replied as follows: To the Polish-Soviet Friendship Association, Warsaw:

    We sincerely thank you for your New Year's greetings and wish you a happy New Year. We hope that you will continue to consolidate your friendship with the Soviet Union and work together to defend world peace.
                                Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters, President Liu Shaoqi
                                            Secretary-General Qian Junrui
                                                January 3

### 波苏友协中苏友协互贺新年  共勉加强对苏友好工作

1950-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    波苏友协中苏友协互贺新年
    共勉加强对苏友好工作
    【本报讯】波苏友好协会与中苏友好协会总会互相致电祝贺新年。波苏友好协会来电称：北京中苏友好协会总会：
    波苏友好协会向您们敬贺新年，并为贵国人民的幸福和全世界和平的巩固，敬祝您们加强对苏友好工作的进一步的成功。
                                  会长  斯维奥脑脱柯夫斯基
                                 秘书长  都灵涅奥
                              一九四九年十二月三十一日
    中苏友好协会总会复电如下：华沙波苏友好协会：
    衷心感谢您们新年的贺电，并祝您们年禧，希望您们继续巩固与苏联的友好并共同为保卫世界和平而努力。
                                中苏友好协会总会  会长刘少奇
                                            总干事钱俊瑞
                                                一月三日
