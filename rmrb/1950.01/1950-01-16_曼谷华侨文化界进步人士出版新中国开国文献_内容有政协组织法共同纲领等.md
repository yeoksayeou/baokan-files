Title: Progressive Figures in Bangkok's Overseas Chinese Cultural Circles Publish Founding Documents of New China, Including the Organic Law of the CPPCC and the Common Program
Author:
Date: 1950-01-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th. According to a report in the Bangkok Quanmin Bao on December 8th of last year, progressive figures in Bangkok's overseas Chinese cultural circles have compiled and published a "Special Collection of Founding Documents of the People's Republic of China," which includes various documents related to the establishment of the People's Republic of China. The book is in a sixteenmo format, totaling over 130 pages, and includes three major documents: "Organic Law of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference," "Organic Law of the Central People's Government," and "Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference." It also contains a list of representatives from various units attending the First Plenary Session of the CPPCC, important resolutions passed by the CPPCC, speeches by representatives from various units, and a complete list of personnel of the Central People's Government. In addition, it includes brief biographies of important parties and figures.

### 曼谷华侨文化界进步人士出版新中国开国文献  内容有政协组织法共同纲领等

1950-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    曼谷华侨文化界进步人士出版新中国开国文献
    内容有政协组织法共同纲领等
    【新华社北京十五日电】据曼谷全民报去年十二月八日消息：曼谷华侨文化界进步人士，已将有关中华人民共和国成立期间的各项文件编纂成册，出版《中华人民共和国开国文献专辑》。该书为十六开本，全书一百三十余页，内容有“人民政治协商会议组织法”、“中央人民政府组织法”、“人民政治协商会议共同纲领”三大文件，人民政协首届全体会议各单位出席代表名单、人民政协通过的各项重要决议、各单位代表的讲话以及中央人民政府全部人选名单等。此外还附有各重要党派和人物的略历。
