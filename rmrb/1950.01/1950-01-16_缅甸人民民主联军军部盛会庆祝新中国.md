Title: The People's Democratic Allied Forces of Burma Held a Grand Celebration of the New China at Military Headquarters

Author:

Date: 1950-01-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th: According to a Rangoon dispatch in the Bangkok Quanmin Bao on December 3rd of last year: The military headquarters of the People's Democratic Allied Forces of Burma, stationed in Prome (Pyay) northwest of Rangoon, held a grand meeting at the public stadium on October 25th of last year to celebrate the founding of the People's Republic of China. Representatives from more than 20 Burmese, Chinese, and Indian ethnic groups attended. At the beginning of the meeting, students from Zhongxing School and all the overseas Chinese present sang the national anthem of the People's Republic of China. Mao Qi Mao, chairman of the meeting and secretary-general of the First Military Region, introduced the great victory of the Chinese people's revolutionary struggle under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong in his speech. Several others continued to speak. The meeting also read out the telegram of congratulations from the department to Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. Finally, the representative of the overseas Chinese gave a speech, thanking the conference on behalf of all overseas Chinese. A parade was held after the meeting. On that day, all overseas Chinese shops hung flags to celebrate.

That same evening, Zhongxing School held a tea party and concert to entertain local people from all walks of life. Mao Xi Xie, commander-in-chief of the People's Democratic Allied Forces, delivered a speech at the meeting, stating that he believed the bright days of the Burmese people would arrive soon. The victory of the Chinese revolution has strengthened the confidence of Burmese revolutionaries. He said: "Today's celebration of China's victory is also a celebration of a part of the world revolutionary movement. The current era is an era of struggle between democracy and anti-democracy, and there is no 'neutrality' in between."

### 缅甸人民民主联军军部盛会庆祝新中国

1950-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸人民民主联军军部盛会庆祝新中国
    【新华社北京十二日电】据去年十二月三日曼谷全民报载仰光通讯：驻于仰光西北卑谬（即普罗姆）的缅甸人民民主联军军部，于去年十月二十五日在公共体育场举行大会，庆祝中华人民共和国的成立。到会者有缅、中、印各民族二十余团体的代表。大会开始时由中兴学校学生及全体到会华侨高唱中华人民共和国国歌。大会主席、第一军部秘书长帽七貌在致辞中介绍了中国人民在毛泽东主席领导下进行的革命斗争的伟大胜利。继续发言者尚有多人。大会并宣读该部致毛主席及中华人民共和国中央人民政府的贺电。最后由华侨代表演讲，代表全体华侨向大会致谢。会后并举行游行。是日，华侨商店皆悬旗志庆。
    同日晚，中兴学校举行茶会及音乐会，招待当地各界人士。人民民主联军总司令帽西耶在会上发表演说称：他相信缅甸人民的光明日子，不久将会到来。中国革命的胜利，增强了缅甸革命者的信心。他说：“今天庆祝中国的胜利，也是庆祝世界革命运动的一部分的胜利。目前时代是民主与反民主斗争的时代，在这中间没有‘中立’存在。”
