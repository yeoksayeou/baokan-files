Title: Premier Zhou Visits Lenin's Mausoleum, Accompanied by Li Fuchun, Wang Jiaxiang, and Others
Author:
Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, currently in Moscow, visited Lenin's Mausoleum on January 27 and laid a wreath. Accompanying Mr. Zhou Enlai were Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government; Wang Jiaxiang, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union; and Mr. Ouyang Qin, Mr. Wu Xiuquan, and Mr. Lai Yali.

### 周总理谒列宁墓  李富春王稼祥等同往

1950-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理谒列宁墓
    李富春王稼祥等同往
    【新华社北京二十八日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：现在莫斯科的中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，一月二十七日谒列宁墓，并献花圈。随同周恩来先生前往者尚有东北人民政府副主席李富春、中华人民共和国驻苏大使王稼祥以及欧阳钦、伍修权、赖亚力先生等。
