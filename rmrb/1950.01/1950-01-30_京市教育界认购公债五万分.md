Title: Beijing Education Community Subscribes to 50,000 Shares of Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1950-01-30
Page: 4

    Beijing News: Teachers and staff of major and secondary schools in Beijing have subscribed to a total of 50,000 shares of public bonds. After the official issuance of the public bonds, the preparatory committee of the Beijing Education Workers' Trade Union formed a public bond sales branch, calling on trade unions of various colleges, schools, and districts to form sales sub-branches, launch public bond publicity and sales work. Under the principle of voluntary participation within one's capacity, a surge of subscription enthusiasm has been set off in various schools. Up to now, the subscription amount has reached 50,000 shares.

### 京市教育界认购公债五万分

1950-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    京市教育界认购公债五万分
    【北京讯】北京各大、中、小学校教职员工警认购公债总数已达五万分。在公债正式发行后，市教育工作者工会筹委会即组成了公债推销分会，号召各院、校、区工会组成推销支会，展开公债宣传和推销工作，在自愿量力的原则下，各校掀起了认购的热潮，到最近为止，认购额已到达五万分。
