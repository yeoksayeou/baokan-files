Title: Czechoslovakia Passes Law Guaranteeing Holiday Pay

Author: 
Date: 1950-01-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 6th: TASS, Prague -- The Czechoslovak government has passed a law regarding the payment of wages during holidays for the working people of Czechoslovakia in 1950. This year, the basic paid holiday is two weeks, but workers under the age of 18 and over the age of 50, as well as those who have worked in the same enterprise for more than five years, will have their holiday extended by one week. Working people who have worked in a certain enterprise for 15 years will receive four weeks of vacation. Miners will also receive an additional week of vacation.

### 捷克通过法案假日工资照付

1950-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克通过法案假日工资照付
    【新华社北京六日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克政府已通过关于一九五○年捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民假日工资照付的法案。今年一年中基本的工资照付的假期是两周，但十八岁以下和五十岁以上的工人以及凡在同一企业工作五年以上的，其假期得延长一周。凡在某一企业中工作达十五年的劳动人民，则可得到四星期的假期。矿工也可多得一星期的休假日。
