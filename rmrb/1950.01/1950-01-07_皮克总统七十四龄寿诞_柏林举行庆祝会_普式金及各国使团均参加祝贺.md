Title: President Pieck's Seventy-Fourth Birthday Celebrated in Berlin; Pushkin and Diplomatic Missions from Various Countries Participate in Congratulating Him
Author:
Date: 1950-01-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th - TASS, Berlin dispatch: A celebration was held at the Friedrichstadt-Palast in Berlin on the 3rd to celebrate the seventy-fourth birthday of Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic. Representatives of the government of the German Democratic Republic, representatives of mass organizations, and representatives of the people of the Republic and Berlin attended the meeting. Pushkin, head of the Soviet diplomatic mission to the German Democratic Republic, and members of the diplomatic missions of various People's Democratic countries participated in the congratulations. Pieck received a warm welcome from the audience at the meeting, and Lehmann, chairman of the West German Communist Party, was also welcomed when he arrived. Ebert, the mayor of Greater Berlin, Nuschke, chairman of the executive committee of the Christian Democratic Union, Heymann, chairman of the Free Democratic Party, Bolz, chairman of the executive committee of the National Democratic Party, Goldenbaum, chairman of the executive committee of the Democratic Farmers' Party, and a female representative of the Free German Trade Union Federation all delivered speeches at the meeting. Ulbricht, a member of the Political Bureau of the German Socialist Unity Party, extended congratulations to Pieck on behalf of the party's executive committee. The speakers all wished Pieck good health and victory in the struggle for peace, for the unity of Germany, and for promoting and expanding friendly relations with the Soviet Union. The congratulatory speeches evoked stormy applause.

### 皮克总统七十四龄寿诞  柏林举行庆祝会  普式金及各国使团均参加祝贺

1950-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    皮克总统七十四龄寿诞
    柏林举行庆祝会
    普式金及各国使团均参加祝贺
    【新华社北京六日电】塔斯社柏林讯：柏林弗烈德里斯塔德宫于三日举行庆祝会，庆贺德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克的七十四龄寿诞。与会的有德意志民主共和国政府的代表，群众团体的代表，共和国和柏林人民的代表等。苏联驻德意志民主共和国外交使团团长普式金，各人民民主国家外交使团的团员均参加祝贺。皮克在会上受到会众热烈的欢迎，西德共产党主席雷曼到场时也受到欢迎。大柏林市长艾柏特，基督教民主党执委会主席努舍克，自由民主党主席海曼，国家民主党执委会主席博尔茨，民主农民党执委会主席戈登堡姆，及自由德国工会联合会的女代表均在会上发表演说。德国统一社会党政治局委员乌布利希代表该党执委会向皮克致贺。演讲者都祝贺皮克健康，并祝他在争取和平，争取德国的统一及促进和扩大对苏友好关系的斗争中胜利。祝词引起了暴风雨般的掌声。
