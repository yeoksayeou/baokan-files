Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author: 
Date: 1950-01-27
Page: 3

    [This newspaper's dispatch] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (27th) 17:30 Workers' Time: Recorded broadcast "Notice Regarding Preparations for Convening the National Military Industry Trade Union and Iron and Steel Trade Union Member Representative Congresses." 18:30 Natural Science Lectures: "Answering Comrade Wang Xinghua's Questions Regarding Michurin's Theory." 20:45: Feature: "The Underwater Heroes Building the Huai River Bridge." 18:00 First Literary and Art Program: Qinshu Ballad "Dong Cunrui Sacrifices Himself to Blow Up the Bunker." 21:00 Second Literary and Art Program: Peking Opera: "The River God Takes a Bride" Part 2.

    [Another dispatch] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Workers' Program: 1. Culture and Entertainment: Renli Carpet Factory Workers' Choir Singing, 2. Feature: The process of the No. 1 blast furnace of Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant creating a new record. 13:00 Youth Program: An article from the Young Communist League Truth Newspaper "American Advanced Youth Fight for Peace and Democracy." 16:30 Children's Program: Singing by children from the Central areas of the Second, Fourth, and Ninth Districts, the Qiaochang of the Fifth District, and the Dongbajiao National Elementary School of the Eighth District. 17:00 Music Program: New songs performed by the Jiaotong University Beijing Management Institute Workers' Night School Choir.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-01-27
第3版()
专栏：

    中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（二十七）天十七点三十分职工时间：记录广播《关于筹备召开全国军工工会及钢铁工会会员代表大会的通知》。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：《答王兴华君关于米邱林学说问题》。二十点四十五分：通讯《修建淮河桥的潜水英雄们。》十八点第一次文艺节目：琴书《董存瑞舍身炸碉堡》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：京剧：《河伯娶妇》第二部分。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：一、文化娱乐：仁立地毡工厂工人歌咏队唱歌，二、通讯：石景山钢铁厂第一号炼铁炉创造新纪录的经过。十三点青年节目：少共真理报的一篇文章《美国先进青年为和平民主而斗争》。十六点三十分儿童节目：二区、四区、九区中心，五区厂桥，八区东八角国民小学小朋友们唱歌。十七点音乐节目：交大北京管理学院工人夜校歌咏团演唱新歌曲。
