Title: Albanian Labor Party Activists' Meetings Held Across Regions, Resolution of Information Bureau Widely Conveyed
Author:
Date: 1950-01-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 7th – Albanian News Agency reports: Workers' Party activists' conferences have been held throughout Albania to convey the resolution of the Communist and Workers' Parties Information Bureau meeting held in Hungary in the latter half of November last year, as well as the reports submitted by Comrades Jakova, Shehu, and Spahiu, Secretaries of the Albanian Labor Party, to the Fourth Special Plenary Session of the Workers' Party Central Committee. At the conclusion of these conferences, Party activists, based on the Information Bureau resolution and the resolutions of the Fourth Special Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, have planned the Party's tasks.

### 阿工人党各地积极分子集会  普遍传达情报局决议

1950-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    阿工人党各地积极分子集会
    普遍传达情报局决议
    【新华社北京七日电】阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚全国各地举行了工人党积极分子大会，传达去年十一月下半月在匈牙利举行会议的共产党和工人党情报局的决议，以及阿尔巴尼亚工人党书记雅科瓦、谢胡、斯巴修诸同志向工人党中央第四次特别全体会议提出的报告。在这些大会结束时，党的积极分子们都依据情报局决议和阿尔巴尼亚工人党中央委员会第四次特别全会的决议规划了党的任务。
