Title: Ode to 1950
Author: Feng Zhi
Date: 1950-01-08
Page: 5

    Ode to 1950
    Feng Zhi
    Nineteen fifty,
    We sing your praises:
    No year in the past,
    Can compare to you.
    You have people who have stood up,
    You have liberated land,
    Your three hundred and sixty-five days
    Are filled with the efforts of four hundred million people.
    You have the people's country
    On our Chinese land,
    You raise the victorious five-star flag
    In the center of the twentieth century.
    Look at those fields, mountains, and rivers,
    All revealing a new atmosphere,
    Look at those cities, villages,
    All unleashing a new strength!
    Nineteen fifty,
    We sing your praises:
    In the past, no year
    Could compare to you.
    The bridges and factories destroyed by bandits,
    You will restore and grow them;
    The countless disaster victims starved by bandits,
    You will give them food and clothing;
    You let the highlands and islands awaiting liberation
    Soon see the red flag flying:
    One flag on the coast of Taiwan,
    One flag on the Himalayas.
    You will overcome numerous difficulties,
    Your year is one of victory,
    The people's victory is extraordinary,
    What day does not produce miracles?
    Nineteen fifty,
    We sing your praises:
    Many happy years in the future
    Will compare themselves to you year after year.

### 一九五零年颂
冯至
1950-01-08
第5版()
专栏：

    一九五零年颂
    冯至
    一九五零年，
    我们歌颂你：
    过去的任何一年，
    都不能和你相比。
    你有翻了身的人民，
    你有解放了的土地，
    你的三百六十五天
    天天有四万万人的努力。
    你有了人民的国家
    在我们中华的土地上，
    你竖起胜利的五星旗
    在二十世纪的中央。
    看那些田野，山河，
    都显露出新的气象，
    看那些城市，村庄，
    都发挥出新的力量！
    一九五零年
    我们歌颂你：
    在过去没有一年
    能够和你相比。
    匪帮们破坏的桥梁，工厂，
    你要使它们恢复，生长；
    匪帮们冻馁了无数灾民，
    你要给他们粮食和衣裳；
    你让待解放的高原，海岛
    不久会看见红旗飘扬：
    一面旗在台湾的海滨，
    一面旗在喜马拉雅山上。
    你要克服重重的困难，
    你的年号是胜利，
    人民的胜利不平常，
    哪一天不产生奇迹？
    一九五零年，
    我们歌颂你：
    将来的许多幸福年
    年年要和你相比。
