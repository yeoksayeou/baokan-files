Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to Italian General Confederation of Labour to Support the Heroic Struggle of Italian Workers, Protesting the Reactionary Massacre of Modena Workers
Author: 
Date: 1950-01-23
Page: 1

    Protesting the Reactionary Massacre of Modena Workers
    All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to Italian General Confederation of Labour to Support the Heroic Struggle of Italian Workers

    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Italian General Confederation of Labour and all Italian workers, both male and female, regarding the Modena incident, expressing sympathy and support for their heroic struggle. The telegram reads as follows:
    To the World Federation of Trade Unions, forwarded to the Italian General Confederation of Labour and all Italian workers, both male and female!

    We, the Chinese working class, feel boundless indignation at the massacre of our Modena worker brothers by the Marshall Plan-ized reactionary rulers of Italy. The Chinese working class is willing to unite with the Italian working class and all peace-loving and democratic people of the world to resolutely protest the barbarous atrocities of the Italian reactionary rulers and to assist you in your heroic struggle for democracy and freedom.
                                        All-China Federation of Trade Unions
                                        January 21, 1950

### 抗议反动派屠杀摩登纳工人  全总电意大利总工会支援意工人英勇斗争

1950-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    抗议反动派屠杀摩登纳工人
    全总电意大利总工会支援意工人英勇斗争
    【新华社北京二十二日电】中华全国总工会为摩登纳事件致电意大利总工会和意大利全体男女工人，表示同情与支援他们的英勇斗争。电文如下：世界工联转意大利总工会与意大利全体男女工人们！
    我们中国工人阶级，对于摩登纳工人弟兄被马歇尔化的意大利反动统治者所屠杀，感到无限的愤慨。中国工人阶级愿与意大利工人阶级以及全世界一切爱好和平民主的人民团结一致，坚决抗议意大利反动统治者的野蛮暴行，援助你们争取民主自由的英勇斗争。
                                        中华全国总工会
                                        一九五○年一月二十一日
