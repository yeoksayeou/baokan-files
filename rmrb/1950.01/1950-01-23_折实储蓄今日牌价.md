Title: Commodity Unit Savings Exchange Rate Today

Author: 
Date: 1950-01-23
Page: 2

    Commodity Unit Savings Exchange Rate Today
    The Beijing Branch Savings Department of the People's Bank of China announces that today's (23rd) standard commodity unit exchange rate for commodity unit savings is 3,745.18 yuan.
