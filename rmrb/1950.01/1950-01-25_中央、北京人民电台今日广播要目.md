Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-01-25
Page: 3

    [This newspaper reports] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (25th) 17:30 Youth Time: Two short essays, "Need to Prescribe the Right Medicine" and "Should Secondary Schools Have Exams?". 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: Zhou Jianren gives "Natural Science and Marxism-Leninism, Mao Zedong Thought". 18:00 First Literary Program: "Jiangnan Envoy," a Meihua Dagu performance. 21:00 Second Literary Program: New Peking Opera "Zhengqi Tu" (Portrait of Righteousness). 18:45 New songs: "Women's Freedom Song," etc. 12:00 National Music: "Melancholy," etc.
    [Further news] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Workers' Program: (1) Singing lesson: "Bright Red Lantern", (2) Newsletter: Zhou Ran from Fengtai Engineering Section talks about "We Have Learned New Road Maintenance Methods." 13:00 Youth Program: "The Study and Work of Soviet Youth". 16:30 Children's Program: Sun Jingxiu tells several stories of Lenin. 17:00, Broadcasting new songs.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-01-25
第3版()
专栏：

    中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（二十五）天十七点三十分青年时间：短文两篇《要对症下药》、《中等学校要不要考试》。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：周建人作《自然科学与马列主义、毛泽东思想》。十八点第一次文艺节目：梅花大鼓《江南使者》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：新京剧《正气图》。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《妇女自由歌》等。十二点国乐：《幽思》等。
