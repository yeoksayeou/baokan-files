Title: Soviet Union, Mongolia, and China Youth Exchange New Year's Greetings
Author:
Date: 1950-01-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 3rd: Youth and sports organizations from the Soviet Union, Mongolia, and China exchanged New Year's greetings via telegram. The telegrams are as follows:

(1) Telegram from the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee to the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth:

    Happy New Year! We wish you success in uniting the strength of youth to strive for the final victory of the Chinese people and the consolidation of the world peace camp.

                                Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee
                              December 31, 1949

(2) Reply telegram from the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth to the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee:

    Thank you for your New Year's greetings. We wish you success in building communism and may the Soviet Union, the bulwark of world peace and democracy, grow stronger day by day.

                        All-China Federation of Democratic Youth
                              January 3, 1950

(3) Telegram from the All-Soviet Sports Committee to the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation:

    We wholeheartedly and warmly congratulate you on the upcoming New Year. We hope that Chinese athletes will achieve great victories in the new year of 1950. Let us consolidate our sporting friendship for the sake of world peace!

                          All-Soviet Sports Committee
                            Moscow, December 31, 1949

(4) Reply telegram from the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation:

    Thank you for your New Year's greetings. We wish you greater success in the development of labor and national defense sports.

                        Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation
                                             Director: Feng Wenbin
                                             Secretary-General: Rong Gaotang
                                                January 3

(5) Telegram from the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League to the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China:

    To the Secretary of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China: On behalf of all Mongolian youth, I extend my New Year's greetings to you and, through you, to the heroic youth of the friendly People's Republic of China. We warmly wish you further new successes in the heroic struggle to develop national youth unity and in the struggle to win world peace.

                        Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League: Danga Xiaolun

(6) Reply telegram from the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China:

    Thank you for your New Year's greetings. We wish you progress in the New Year and success in completing the construction of 1950.

                          Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China
                                            January 3

### 苏联蒙古和我国青年  新年互电致贺

1950-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联蒙古和我国青年
    新年互电致贺
    【新华社北京三日电】苏联青年反法西斯委员会、全苏联体育委员会、蒙古革命青年团中央委员会与我国青年及体育团体新年互致贺电，电文如下：
    （一）苏联青年反法西斯委员会致中华全国民主青年联合总会的贺电：
    恭贺新禧，并祝胜利地团结青年力量，为中国人民的最后胜利，为全世界和平阵营的巩固而奋斗。
                            苏联青年反法西斯委员会
                          一九四九年十二月三十一日
    （二）中华全国民主青年联合总会致苏联青年反法西斯委员会的复电：
    谢谢你们的新年祝贺，祝你们胜利地建设共产主义，并祝世界和平民主堡垒苏联日益强大。
                        中华全国民主青年联合总会
                              一九五○年一月三日
    （三）全苏联体育委员会致中华全国体育总会筹委会的贺电：
    我们衷心热烈祝贺即将到来的新年，希望中国体育者在新的一九五○年有巨大的胜利：让我们为着全世界的和平，巩固我们体育的友谊吧！
                          全苏联体育委员会
                            一九四九年十二月三十一日于莫斯科
    （四）中华全国体育总会筹备委员会的复电：
    谢谢你们新年的祝贺，并祝你们在发展的劳动与国防的体育事业中，获得更大的成功。
                        中华全国体育总会筹备委员会  主任冯文彬
                                             秘书长荣高棠
                                                一月三日
    （五）蒙古革命青年团中央委员会致中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的贺电：
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记：谨代表全体蒙古青年向你、并通过你向友好的中华人民共和国的英勇青年，恭贺新禧，热忱地祝你们在发展全国青年团结的英勇斗争中，在争取全世界和平的斗争中，获得更进一步的新的成功。
                        蒙古革命青年团中央委员会书记丹格校伦
    （六）中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的复电：
    谢谢你们的新年祝贺，祝你们新年进步，并胜利地完成一九五○年的建设。
                          中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
                                            一月三日
