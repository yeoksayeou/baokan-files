Title: General Yang Hucheng's Coffin Shipped from Chongqing to Native Place
Author:
Date: 1950-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, January 17th: General Yang Hucheng's coffin was loaded onto a ship in Chongqing on the 16th to be transported back to his native place. Approximately two thousand representatives from all walks of life in Chongqing went to mourn and see him off. The coffin will be stopped along the way and will be heavily mourned by citizens.

### 杨虎城将军灵柩由渝启运回原籍

1950-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    杨虎城将军灵柩由渝启运回原籍
    【新华社重庆十七日电】杨虎城将军灵柩十六日由渝登轮启运回原籍，重庆各界代表约两千人前往哀送。灵柩沿途停留，备受市民祭奠。
