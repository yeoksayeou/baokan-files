Title: Guiding Political and Legal Work, Sharing Experience: Central Political and Legal Gazette Inaugurated
Author:
Date: 1950-01-21
Page: 2

    Guiding Political and Legal Work · Sharing Experience
    Central Political and Legal Gazette Inaugurated
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th] In order to strengthen guidance on political and legal work and exchange work experience, the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council has published the Central Political and Legal Gazette (bi-monthly). The inaugural issue was published on the 19th. In addition to publishing policies, laws, resolutions, and instructions on political law promulgated by the Central People's Government and the Government Administration Council, its contents also include regulations, rules, resolutions, orders, etc. promulgated by various departments under the guidance of the Political and Legal Committee. The gazette will be distributed to governments at all levels above the county level throughout the country for cadres to use as a reference for work, study and research.

### 指导政法工作·交流经验  中央政法公报创刊

1950-01-21
第2版()
专栏：

    指导政法工作·交流经验
    中央政法公报创刊
    【新华社北京二十日电】政务院政治法律委员会为加强对政法工作的指导及交流工作经验，特出版中央政法公报（半月刊），创刊号已于十九日出版。其内容除刊载中央人民政府及政务院颁订的有关政治法律的政策、法令、决议及指示外，并包括政治法律委员会指导的各部门颁布的条例章则、决议、命令等。该公报将发给全国县以上各级政府，供干部工作参考及学习研究。
