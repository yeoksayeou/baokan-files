Title: Ministry of Interior Instructs Local Governments to Launch Spring Festival Support-the-Army and Give Preferential Treatment to Families of Soldiers Movement
Author:
Date: 1950-01-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 29th: The Ministry of Interior of the Central People's Government issued instructions to the People's Governments of major administrative regions and provinces and cities on the 26th, instructing local People's Governments to launch a movement to support the people's army and give preferential treatment to the families of revolutionary soldiers during the Spring Festival. The instructions also called for publicizing the victory of the People's Liberation War and the tasks of liberating Taiwan, Hainan Island, and Tibet. The instructions pointed out that local People's Governments should organize the people to write letters of慰问 to the soldiers at the front, strengthen the work of 代耕 for the families of revolutionary martyrs and revolutionary soldiers, and help the families of martyrs and soldiers solve their livelihood difficulties. The instructions also pointed out that the People's Governments of major administrative regions and provinces and cities can, based on local conditions and past experience, make specific instructions to their subordinates.

### 内务部指示各地人民政府  春节展开拥军优属运动

1950-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

    内务部指示各地人民政府
    春节展开拥军优属运动
    【新华社北京二十九日电】中央人民政府内务部，于二十六日向各大行政区及省市的人民政府发出指示，令各地人民政府于春节展开拥护人民军队、优待革命军人家属的运动，并宣传人民解放战争的胜利，和解放台湾、海南岛和西藏的任务。指示指出：各地人民政府应组织人民给前方将士写慰问信，加强为革命烈士和革命军人家属代耕的工作，帮助烈士和军人家属解决生活困难。指示并指出：各大行政区和各省、市的人民政府，可以根据当地具体情况和过去的经验，向其下级作出具体指示。
