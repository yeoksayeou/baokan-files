Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and Beijing-Tianjin Organizations Hold Memorial Meeting for Lenin's Death Anniversary Yesterday
Author: Li Wen
Date: 1950-01-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 21st: Commemorative meetings were held in Beijing and Tianjin to mark the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death. The headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a memorial evening at the Sino-French University auditorium tonight, with Qian Junrui giving a lecture on Lenin's life. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association branch of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions was established at the Lenin Memorial Meeting held by the Federation, and Xu Zhizhen and four others were elected as members of the committee. Various organizations and groups in Tianjin also held commemorative meetings separately. Xinhua Bookstore, Life, Reading, New Knowledge, and Sanlian Bookstores in Beijing and Tianjin jointly held a one-day discount, selling Lenin's works and related books about Lenin at 20% off.

The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association of the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government held a meeting in the auditorium of the ministry at 8:00 AM yesterday to commemorate the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death. Minister Li Dequan reported on Lenin's life and his contributions to the revolution. She called on everyone to learn from Lenin, learning his work style and revolutionary spirit. Subsequently, the Academic Committee announced that they would study Lenin's "On the Work Attitude of Government Personnel" to commemorate Lenin.

### 中苏友协总会及京津各机关  昨集会纪念列宁忌辰
李文
1950-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会及京津各机关
    昨集会纪念列宁忌辰
    【新华社北京二十一日电】京津两地集会纪念列宁逝世二十六周年。中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友协分会，本日晚在中法大学礼堂举行纪念晚会，由钱俊瑞主讲列宁生平。全国总工会中苏友好协会支会，在该会举行的列宁逝世纪念大会上宣告成立，选出许之祯等五人为委员。天津各机关，团体也分别集会举行纪念。京津两市新华书店、生活、读书、新知、三联等书店举行联合减价一天，将列宁著作及有关列宁书籍，一律八折出售。
    【本报讯】中央人民政府卫生部中苏友好协会，于昨日上午八时在该部大礼堂举行纪念列宁逝世二十六周年大会，由李德全部长报告列宁的生平、及其对革命的贡献。她号召大家向列宁学习，学习他的工作作风与革命精神。接着学委会宣布以学习列宁所著《论政府机关人员的工作态度》以纪念列宁。
                                                （李文）
