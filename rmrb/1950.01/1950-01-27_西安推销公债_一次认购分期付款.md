Title: Xi'an Promotes Public Bonds: Lump-Sum Subscription with Installment Payments
Author:
Date: 1950-01-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, January 26th: The Xi'an Municipal Public Bond Promotion Committee held its second plenary session on the 18th to discuss specific methods for promoting public bonds. The meeting decided to adopt a method of lump-sum subscription with installment payments. In the city's public bond quota, 700,000 shares were allocated to private industrial and commercial enterprises, and 400,000 shares to wealthy households and wealthy retired civil and military officials; government agencies, schools, organizations, troops, general citizens, small merchants, and self-employed individuals were to subscribe voluntarily.

### 西安推销公债  一次认购分期付款

1950-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    西安推销公债
    一次认购分期付款
    【新华社西安二十六日电】西安市公债推销委员会于十八日召开第二次全体委员会议讨论推销公债的具体办法。会议决定采取一次认购，分期付款的办法。在全市公债配额中，分配私营工商业七十万分，殷实富户和富有的退职文武官员四十万分；机关、学校、团体、部队、一般市民、小商人及自由职业者自愿认购。
