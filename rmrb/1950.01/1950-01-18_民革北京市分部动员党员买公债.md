Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Beijing Branch Mobilizes Members to Buy Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1950-01-18
Page: 4

    Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Beijing Branch Mobilizes Members to Buy Public Bonds
    [Beijing News] In response to the government's call to expand the sale of People's Victory Inflation-Adjusted Bonds, the Beijing Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang convened a general meeting of all members in the city on the 15th of this month to mobilize all members to actively purchase bonds. Li Jishen, Chairman of the committee, Mei Gongbin, Secretary-General, and Xu Baoju, Director of the Propaganda Department, attended and spoke at the meeting.

### 民革北京市分部动员党员买公债
北京讯
1950-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    民革北京市分部动员党员买公债
    【北京讯】中国国民党革命委员会北京市分部为响应政府扩大推销人民胜利折实公债的号召，于本月十五日召开市全体党员大会，动员全体党员踊跃认购公债，并请该会主席李济深、秘书长梅龚彬、宣传部长许宝驹出席讲话。
