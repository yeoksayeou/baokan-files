Title: Documents Concerning Victory Bonds to be Broadcast by Beijing Radio Tonight
Author:
Date: 1950-01-03
Page: 3

Documents Concerning Victory Bonds
Beijing Radio Broadcasts Tonight

[This Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will rebroadcast a special music program for Chinese listeners from Moscow Broadcasting Station from 17:20 to 18:00 this evening (the 3rd). At 18:30, a lecture on common sense in natural science: "Common Sense of the Calendar." At 18:00, the first literary program: Leting Drum (a type of folk singing) "Li Xiujuan Sells Tofu". At 21:00, the second literary program: New Songs.

[Additional News] Beijing People's Radio at 12:30, a program for workers and staff: Peng Qingzhao, head of the Education Section of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions' Department of Culture and Education, will lecture on political lessons. At 13:00, a women's program: Women's songs. At 16:30, a children's program: Children from Baojia Street Elementary School in the Second District will perform "Celebrating the New Year Song and Dance". At 17:00, a music program: Songs celebrating the New Year. At 19:15, Voice of the City: (1) The State Council's directive on the issuance of the 1950 First Phase People's Victory Indexed Bonds, (2) The Ministry of Finance's announcement entrusting the People's Bank of China to issue Victory Bonds, (3) The People's Bank of China's measures for the staggered subsidy interest on the first phase of Victory Bonds, (4) The People's Bank of China's measures for entrusting the sale of the first phase of Victory Bonds.

### 有关胜利公债文件  北京电台今晚广播

1950-01-03
第3版()
专栏：

    有关胜利公债文件
    北京电台今晚广播
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（三）晚十七点二十分到十八点转播莫斯科广播电台特别对中国听众播送的音乐节目。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“历法的常识”。十八点第一次文艺节目：乐亭大鼓《李秀娟卖豆腐》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：新歌曲。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：全总文教部教育科长彭庆昭讲授政治课。十三点妇女节目：妇女歌曲。十六点三十分儿童节目：二区鲍家街小学小朋友们演唱《庆祝新年大歌舞》。十七点音乐节目：庆祝新年歌曲。十九点十五分市政之声：（１）政务院关于发行一九五零年第一期人民胜利折实公债的指示，（２）财政部委托人民银行发行胜利公债的通告，（３）人民银行关于认购第一期胜利公债分期贴补利息办法，（４）人民银行第一期胜利公债委托代销办法。
