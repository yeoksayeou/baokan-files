Title: Overseas Chinese Enthusiastically Subscribe to Government Bonds
Author:
Date: 1950-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 12th: The movement for overseas Chinese to subscribe to government bonds has already begun. In recent days, various circles, entrusted by overseas Chinese, have been inquiring with the Bank of China here about the procedures for overseas remittances to purchase government bonds. On the 10th, the bank received the first overseas remittance of 9,900 Hong Kong dollars, which was converted to purchase 2,485 shares of government bonds.

### 侨胞踊跃认购公债

1950-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    侨胞踊跃认购公债
    【新华社上海十二日电】国外侨胞认购公债运动业已展开。连日来各界因受华侨委托向此间中国银行询问侨汇购买公债手续者甚多。该行十日已收到首笔侨汇港币九千九百元，折兑购买公债二千四百八十五分。
