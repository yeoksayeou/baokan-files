Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1950-01-28
Page: 3

    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (28th) 17:30 Youth Time: Introduction to "Chinese Youth" Issue 31. 20:45 Communication: "The National Flag Flies on the Border of the Motherland". 18:00 First Literary Program: Local Opera "Hou Zhaoyin Destroys the Enemy to Save Her Daughter." 21:00 Second Literary Program: Chinese Music. 18:30 Broadcast of "Don River Fighters" and other five Soviet songs.
    Furthermore, the station will broadcast a special program on Tibet at 22:00 in Chinese, Tibetan, and English: First, the Foreign Ministry Spokesperson's statement on the issue of the Lhasa authorities in Tibet planning to send a so-called "Goodwill Mission"; Second, the speech by Sangji Yexi (Tianbao), the Tibetan representative of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, on the above issue; Third, speeches by Tibetan democratic figures in Beijing, Angwang Ge, Luowang, and Duojie Cairen, to Tibetan compatriots.
    [Another Dispatch] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Staff Program: Continuing to teach singing "Bright Red Lantern". 13:00 Women's Program: Women's health knowledge "Why Not Have Children". 16:30 Children's Program: Singing and speaking by the children of Fengtai Fulun Elementary School. 17:00 Continuing to teach singing "The Sun Rises Red All Over the Sky". 18:30 Broadcast Lecture: Miao Peishi speaks on "Talking to Young Workers and Students About Writing". 19:30 Voice of the Municipality: "Report on the Inspection Work of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Tax Bureau".

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-01-28
第3版()
专栏：

    中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（二十八）天十七点三十分青年时间：介绍《中国青年》第三十一期。二十点四十五分通讯：“国旗飘扬在祖国的边疆”。十八点第一次文艺节目：牌子戏《侯昭银毁敌救女记》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：国乐。十八点三十分播送《顿河战士》等五支苏联歌曲。
    又，该台在二十二点，用中文、藏文和英文三种语言播送对西藏的特别节目：第一、外交部发言人关于西藏拉萨当局将要派出所谓“亲善使团”问题的谈话；第二、中国人民政治协商会议藏族代表桑吉悦希（天宝）对上述问题的谈话；第三、在北京的藏族民主人士昂旺格、罗旺和多杰才旦对藏族同胞讲话。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：继续教唱“鲜红的灯”。十三点妇女节目：妇女卫生常识“为什么不生孩子”。十六点三十分儿童节目：丰台扶轮小学小朋友们唱歌、讲话。十七点继续教唱“太阳一出满天红”。十八点三十分广播讲演：苗培时讲“和青年工人学生谈写稿”。十九点三十分市政之声：“北京市人民政府税务局稽查工作的报导”。
