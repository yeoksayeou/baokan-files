Title: The Fourteenth Government Affairs Council Approves Three Appointment Lists
Author:
Date: 1950-01-08
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th. The Fourteenth Government Affairs Council of the Government Administration Council approved the following three appointment lists:
(1) List of personnel directly under the Government Administration Council:
	Director of the Secretariat of the General Office: Liao Hanfei, formerly Deputy Secretary-General of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
	Counselor: Yang Fuqing, formerly Deputy Director-General of the China Industrial Cooperative Association.
(2) List of Deputy Directors of the General Office of the Ministry of Light Industry of the Central People's Government:
	Deputy Director of the General Office: Li Zaiyun, formerly Deputy Manager of the Foreign Trade Corporation of the Trade Department of the East China Financial and Economic Committee.
(3) List of personnel of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government:
	Secretary-General: Yun Guangying (concurrent)
	Deputy Secretary-General: Zuo Hongtao, currently Director of the Political Department of the People's Liberation Army Yueganxiang Column.
	Director of the Department of Civil Affairs: Gu Dacun (concurrent)
	Director of the Department of Finance: Yi Xiuxiang (concurrent)
	Deputy Director: Zhang Yongli, formerly Director of the Salt Affairs Bureau of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government.
		Wu Hanjie, formerly Director of the Financial Administration Division of the Finance Department of the Northeast People's Government.
		Li Shizhong, formerly Director of the Taxation Bureau of the Northeast People's Government.
	Director of the Department of Culture and Education: Du Guoxiang (concurrent)
	Deputy Director: Xiao Junying (concurrent)
		Qin Yuanbang, currently a member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang.
	Director of the Department of Industry: Liu Xiangsan, formerly Director of the Northeast Coal Mine Administration.
	Deputy Director: Zhang Yongqing, formerly Deputy Director of the Northeast Textile Administration.
		Luo Zicai, former factory director of the Kuomintang government's arsenal.
		Huang Xichuan, formerly Chief of the Engineering Planning Section of the Military Industry Department of the People's Liberation Army Northeast Military Region.
	Director of the Department of Agriculture and Forestry: Qiu Zhe (concurrent)
	Deputy Director: Lin Meinan (concurrent)
		Li Boqiu (concurrent)
	Director of the Department of Trade: Zhu Jingzhi, formerly Director of the Commercial Bureau of the Shenyang Municipal People's Government.
	Deputy Director: Deng Wenzhao, formerly Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Hong Kong China Business Daily.
		Li Jianxing, formerly a member of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central South China Bureau of the Chinese Communist Party.

	Deputy Director: Zhang Li, Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang.
