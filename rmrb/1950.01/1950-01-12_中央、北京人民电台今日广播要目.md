Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-01-12
Page: 3

    Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
    [Our Newspaper] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (12th) at 17:30 Women's Time: All-China Democratic Women's Federation's letter to all provincial and municipal women's federations and sisters in all cities across the country. 18:30 Natural Science Common Knowledge Lecture: "Kao" Ling Earth. 18:00 First Literary Program: Card Play "New Five Saints 朝天 [Chao Tian]". 18:45 New Songs: "Fānshendào Qíng" [Emancipated Taoist ballad], etc. 21:00 Second Literary Program: New Peking Opera "Three Attacks on Zhujiazhuang", Part 3, Third Attack.
    [Further News] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Staff Program: (1) Trade Union Work: Shi Yu talks about "How the Yongfeng Chemical Factory's Trade Union Promotes Production." (2) Worker Services: Answers to questions about how to run a blackboard newspaper well; whether joining the trade union means joining the Communist Party; whether it is reasonable for the factory to employ temporary workers and sign contracts without going through the trade union; whether labor discipline should be observed by staff as well. 13:00: Listener Services: ① Recruitment announcement for technical personnel by the North China Coal Mine Administration Bureau, ② Enrollment brochure for the Cadre School of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government, ③ Recruitment announcement for Russian translators by the Translation Bureau of the Publishing Administration of the Central People's Government. ④ Notice from the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government regarding the registration of senior health specialists and the collection of information on health care, ⑤ Recruitment announcement for staff by the Beijing Coal and Iron Branch Company, ⑥ Several telegrams claimed by the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau. 13:30 Storytelling: Zhao Shuli's novella "Fu Gui". 16:30 Children's Program: Children from the Central National Elementary School of the Twelfth District sing. 17:00 New Song Practice: Singing "Support the Issuance of the People's Victory Discount Bond." 19:15 Voice of the Municipality: (1) The Telecommunications Bureau broadcasts "How to Use Long-Distance Calls", (2) Notice from the Commerce Bureau on surveying the quantity of grain brought into, sold, and stored by public and private grain merchants in Beijing to ensure the city's grain supply.
