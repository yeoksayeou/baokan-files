Title: This Week's Literary and Art Lecture Announcement
Author:
Date: 1950-01-20
Page: 4
    【Our Newspaper News】The Beijing Mass Literary and Art Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Agency are jointly organizing this week's literary and art lecture. This week, they have invited Li Bozhao, Deputy Director of the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education, to speak on "Several Issues in Current Writing." The entertainment will feature Li Xinghai and Zhou Yintian performing the drum ballad "The Blue Bridge Sorrow." The location is still the Soviet Union's Foreign Cultural Association on Nanheyan Street, and the time is 9:30 AM. Listeners will be admitted with long-term or temporary admission tickets. Temporary admission tickets can be collected from the Youth Service Department on Saturday.

### 星期文艺讲座预告

1950-01-20
第4版()
专栏：

    星期文艺讲座预告
    【本报讯】京市大众文艺创作研究会与青年服务社联合举办的星期文艺讲座，本星期邀请京市文教局副局长李伯钊讲“目前写作中的几个问题”。余兴由李兴海、周荫田演唱大鼓“蓝桥恨”。地点仍在南河沿苏联对外文化协会，时间为上午九点半。听众凭长期或临时入场券入场。临时入场券可于星期六到青年服务部领取。
