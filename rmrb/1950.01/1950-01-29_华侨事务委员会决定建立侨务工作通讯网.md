Title: Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission Decides to Establish Overseas Chinese Affairs Work Communications Network
Author:
Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th -- In order to maintain regular contact and policy guidance with overseas Chinese affairs departments in various regions, and to report and reflect the situation of overseas Chinese affairs at home and abroad in a timely manner, the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government has decided to establish an overseas Chinese affairs work communications network. The Commission has stipulated that the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commissions of Guangdong and Fujian provinces each establish a communications team, and overseas Chinese affairs bureaus and overseas Chinese service stations in various regions establish correspondents under the leadership of the communications teams. The Commission has also notified the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commissions of Guangdong and Fujian provinces, as well as overseas Chinese affairs bureaus and stations in various regions, to regard communications work as a regular and important business, formulate specific work plans, and designate specific personnel to be responsible for its implementation.

### 华侨事务委员会决定建立侨务工作通讯网

1950-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    华侨事务委员会决定建立侨务工作通讯网
    【新华社北京二十八日电】中央人民政府华侨事务委员会为了与各地侨务部门作经常的联系与政策指导并及时报道和反映海内外侨务情况，决定建立侨务工作通讯网。该会已规定广东、福建两省侨务委员会各设一通讯组，各地侨务局和华侨服务站设通讯员，归通讯组领导。该会并通知广东、福建两省侨务委员会和各地侨务局、侨务站等，都要以通讯工作为经常重要业务之一，并定出具体工作计划，指定专人负责进行。
