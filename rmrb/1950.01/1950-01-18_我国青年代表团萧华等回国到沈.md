Title: China Youth Delegation Led by Xiao Hua Returns Home to Shenyang
Author: 
Date: 1950-01-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 17th. Xiao Hua, head of the Chinese Democratic Youth Delegation attending the Second World Democratic Youth Congress, along with deputy head Han Tianshi, secretary-general Chen Jiakang, and two others, returned to Shenyang from abroad at 1:30 PM on the 16th. Youth workers from the Northeast Preparatory Committee of the Youth League, the Shenyang Municipal Committee, the Northeast Student Federation, and the Shenyang Democratic Youth Federation welcomed them at the station. Xiao Hua and his party will stay in Shenyang for two or three days before returning to Beijing. Note: After leaving the country on July 22nd of last year to participate in the Second World Democratic Youth Congress and the International Youth Festival and International Student Festival in Budapest, the capital of Hungary, Xiao Hua and his group spent two months visiting cities in the Soviet Union such as Kiev, Stalingrad, and Tbilisi, the hometown of Marshal Stalin, participating in celebrations for the October Revolution and Stalin's 70th birthday. In addition, they visited the tomb of Dimitrov in Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, and visited the new democratic countries of Hungary, Albania, Romania, Czechoslovakia, and Poland, receiving warm welcome and reception from the people of those countries.

### 我国青年代表团萧华等回国到沈

1950-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我国青年代表团萧华等回国到沈
    【新华社沈阳十七日电】出席世界民主青年第二届代表大会的中国民主青年代表团团长萧华和副团长韩天石、秘书长陈家康等一行五人，十六日下午一时半从国外回到沈阳。青年团东北筹委会和沈阳市委会、东北学生联合会、沈阳民主青年联合会等青年工作者都到车站欢迎。萧华等在沈阳逗留二、三日后即返京。按：萧华等一行自去年七月二十二日出国到匈牙利京城布达佩斯参加世界民主青年第二届代表大会和国际青年节、国际学生节以后，曾以两个月的时间访问了苏联的基辅、斯大林格勒、斯大林大元帅的故乡梯比里斯等城，参加了庆祝十月革命节和斯大林七十寿辰等盛典。此外并曾在保加利亚首都索菲亚拜谒季米特洛夫的陵墓，访问了匈牙利、阿尔巴尼亚、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰等新民主主义国家，受到各国人民热烈欢迎和接待。
