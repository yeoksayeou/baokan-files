Title: Southwest of Hanoi, Hoa Binh Region: Vietnamese Liberation Army Annihilates 800 Enemies, Destroys 18 French Military Strongholds, Liberating 1,000 Square Kilometers
Author:
Date: 1950-01-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th: Vietnam News Agency, Hoa Binh Region, 9th: In a recent offensive in the Hoa Binh region southwest of Hanoi, the Vietnamese Liberation Army destroyed 18 French military strongholds, killed 420 French soldiers, wounded 158 French soldiers, and captured 218 French soldiers. The Vietnamese army also seized 38 machine guns, 200 rifles, 13 mortars, and 200 tons of ammunition and military equipment.
    The recent offensive by the Vietnamese Liberation Army in the Hoa Binh region has liberated 1,000 square kilometers of territory west of Hanoi.

### 河内西南和平地区  越南解放军歼敌八百  摧毁法军据点十八处  一千方公里地区已获得解放

1950-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    河内西南和平地区
    越南解放军歼敌八百  摧毁法军据点十八处
    一千方公里地区已获得解放
    【新华社北京十日电】越南通讯社和平地区九日讯：越南解放军最近在河内西南和平地区的攻势中，摧毁法军十八个据点，打死法军四百二十名，打伤法军一百五十八名，俘获法军二百一十八名。越南军并缴获机枪三十八挺、步枪二百支、迫击炮十三门及弹药和军事装备二百吨。
    越南解放军最近在和平地区的攻势，已使河内以西一千方公里的地区，获得解放。
