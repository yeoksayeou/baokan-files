Title: Ghost, Buffoon, Jiang Tingfu
Author: Ma Fantuo
Date: 1950-01-20
Page: 6

    Ghost, Buffoon, Jiang Tingfu
    Ma Fantuo
    Jiang Tingfu, thick-skinned,
    Made a big fool of himself at the Security Council,
    Despised and scolded, without a hint of shame,
    Clinging on and refusing to leave.
    The dead pulling at the living,
    Jiang Tingfu pulling at his godfather;
    The living clutching at the dead,
    Truman clutching at this utterly rotten
    "Kuomintang" concubine!
    The Chinese people unanimously shout "Expel him!"
    "Political zombie," subjected to international ridicule and abuse,
    American imperialism, caught between laughter and tears, hugging this buffoon,
    Unable to keep him, yet unwilling to discard him!
    The Soviet Union's statement is like a thunderclap:
    Jiang Tingfu cannot represent anyone!
    Jiang Tingfu is a vague and ethereal thing!
    Jiang Tingfu is a ghost!
    Who can deny this fact?
    Only Britain, shamelessly two-faced and opportunistic;
    Only France, saying "Regarding the situation in China,
    we have not yet reached a conclusion."
    Imperialists, if you cannot reach a conclusion,
    the conclusion will find you!
    "Imperialism and its running dogs are bound to perish!"
    —This conclusion is already knocking on your door!
                                            January 18, 1950

### 幽灵  活宝  蒋廷黻
马凡陀
1950-01-20
第6版()
专栏：

    幽灵  活宝  蒋廷黻
    马凡陀
    蒋廷黻，脸皮厚，
    安理会上大出丑，
    遭唾骂，不知羞，
    死拉活扯不肯走。
    死的拉着活的，
    蒋廷黻拉着他干爹；
    活的扯住死的，
    杜鲁门扯住这腐烂透顶的
    “国民党”小妾！
    中国人民一致喊道“开除他！”
    “政治僵尸”，受尽国际上的笑骂，
    美帝哭笑不得，抱着这个活宝，
    留又留不住，丢又丢不下！
    苏联的声明好比一声雷：
    蒋廷黻不能代表任何人！
    蒋廷黻是一个虚无飘渺的东西！
    蒋廷黻是一个幽灵！
    这事实谁能否认？
    只有英国，厚颜无耻两面逢迎；
    只有法国，说什么“对中国形势，
    我们还没有得出结论”。
    帝国主义者们，你们得不出结论，
    结论会找到你们！
    “帝国主义及其走狗一定灭亡！”
    ——这结论已经在敲你们的大门！
                                            一九五○、一、十八
