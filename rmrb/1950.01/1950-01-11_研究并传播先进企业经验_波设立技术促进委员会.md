Title: Poland Establishes Technology Promotion Committee to Study and Disseminate Advanced Enterprise Experience
Author:
Date: 1950-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th – According to a TASS report from Warsaw on the 9th: In view of the needs of implementing the Six-Year Plan, the Economic Committee of the Polish Council of Ministers has decided to establish a Technology Promotion Committee, composed of representatives from various scientific and technological departments, with the task of implementing mechanization and automation in industry, transportation, agriculture, and construction.

    The Technology Promotion Committee will supervise the practical application of the results obtained by various research institutes and other scientific organizations, as well as workers' rationalization proposals and inventions, and disseminate the experience of advanced enterprises.

    The Economic Committee also decided to establish a Scientific and Technological Documentation Institute. The institute will collect and disseminate information and scientific works on technology, science, and related economic issues, and will maintain contact and cooperation with research institutions abroad, especially in the Soviet Union and the People's Democracies.

### 研究并传播先进企业经验  波设立技术促进委员会

1950-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    研究并传播先进企业经验
    波设立技术促进委员会
    【新华社北京十日电】据塔斯社华沙九日讯：波兰部长会议经济委员会鉴于实行六年计划的需要，已决定成立技术促进委员会由各个科学与技术部门的代表组成，其任务为使工业、运输、农业和建设中实行机械化和自动化。
    该技术促进委员会，将监督各研究所与其他科学机关所获成果以及工人的合理化建议和发明的实际运用，并传播先进企业的经验。
    经济委员会并决定成立科学技术文献所。该所将搜集和传播技术、科学和有关经济问题等方面的情报和科学著作，并将和国外特别是苏联及人民民主国家的研究机关保持联系和合作。
