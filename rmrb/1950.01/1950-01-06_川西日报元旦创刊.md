Title: "Western Sichuan Daily" Inaugurated on New Year's Day
Author:
Date: 1950-01-06
Page: 1

    "Western Sichuan Daily" Inaugurated on New Year's Day
    [Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, 4th] The "Western Sichuan Daily," as the mouthpiece of the people of Western Sichuan, was inaugurated on New Year's Day, 1950, the third day after the People's Liberation Army entered the city. The Chengdu Branch of the Xinhua News Agency was also established on the same day.

### 川西日报元旦创刊

1950-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    川西日报元旦创刊
    【新华社成都四日电】作为川西人民喉舌的川西日报，已于解放军入城后之第三日，即一九五○年元旦创刊。新华社成都分社亦于同日成立。
