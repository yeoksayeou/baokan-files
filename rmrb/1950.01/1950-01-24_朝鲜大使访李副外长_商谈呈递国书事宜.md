Title: Korean Ambassador Visits Vice Foreign Minister Li to Discuss Presenting Credentials
Author:
Date: 1950-01-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd—Li Ju-yeon, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, accompanied by Counselor Choi Young and Kim Kuk-bo, visited Vice Foreign Minister Li Ke-nong at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 4:00 PM today (the 23rd) to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. Also present were Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 朝鲜大使访李副外长  商谈呈递国书事宜

1950-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜大使访李副外长
    商谈呈递国书事宜
    【新华社北京二十三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华首任特命全权大使李周渊偕参事崔英及金国辅于今日（二十三日）下午四时赴外交部访问李克农副部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有办公厅王炳南主任及交际处王倬如处长。
