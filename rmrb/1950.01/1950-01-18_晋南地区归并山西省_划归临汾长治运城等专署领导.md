Title: Southern Shanxi Region Merged into Shanxi Province, Placed Under the Leadership of the Linfen, Changzhi, and Yuncheng Special Districts
Author:
Date: 1950-01-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th: The Southern Shanxi Region, originally part of Shanxi Province under the Northwest Region, has been merged into Shanxi. The handover between the two sides was completed in late December of last year, and it was decided that it would be placed under the leadership of Shanxi Province starting from January 1, 1950. The Yicheng Temporary Special District, originally under Shanxi, was also abolished on December 31, 1949, and replaced with the Yuncheng Special District. The former Southern Shanxi Region has now been divided and placed under the leadership of the Linfen, Changzhi, and Yuncheng Special Districts of Shanxi Province.

### 晋南地区归并山西省  划归临汾长治运城等专署领导

1950-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    晋南地区归并山西省
    划归临汾长治运城等专署领导
    【新华社北京十七日电】西北区所属原山西省的晋南地区，已归并山西。去年十二月下旬双方即办理交待，并决定从一九五○年一月一日起划归山西省领导。山西原属之翼城临时专署，亦已于一九四九年十二月三十一日撤消，改设运城专署。原晋南地区刻已分别划归山西省临汾、长治、运城等专署领导。
