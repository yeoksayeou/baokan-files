Title: *Nanyang Siang Pau* Celebrates Third Anniversary, Publishes Inscriptions by Chairman Mao and Others; Over 5,000 People Gather to Loyally Support the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1950-01-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 7th: According to a Singapore dispatch published in the *Guangming Daily* here today, the Singapore *Nanyang Siang Pau* published inscriptions by leaders of the Central People's Government of the motherland on November 20th of last year, its third anniversary, and held a celebration rally. Chairman Mao's inscription was: "Serve the interests of overseas Chinese." Premier Zhou Enlai's inscription was: "Strive to publicize the Common Program of New Democracy, and strive to protect the legitimate rights and interests of overseas Chinese." The *Nanyang Siang Pau*'s third anniversary celebration was held at the Great World Amusement Park in Singapore, with more than 5,000 people attending, expressing warm support for the Central People's Government of the motherland. During the meeting, in order to express its infinite loyalty and support to the motherland, the newspaper presented the Central People's Government with a silk banner embroidered with the signatures of all staff members and coconut trees, which was accepted by Zhuang Xiquan, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, on behalf of the government. After the meeting, recreational programs were held, including the Yangge dance, which was introduced to Southeast Asia for the first time.

### 《南侨日报》创刊三周年  刊载毛主席等题词  五千余人集会忠诚拥护祖国

1950-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    《南侨日报》创刊三周年
    刊载毛主席等题词
    五千余人集会忠诚拥护祖国
    【新华社北京七日电】据此间光明日报今日所载新加坡通讯，新加坡《南侨日报》于去年十一月二十日即该报创刊三周年纪念日，刊出祖国中央人民政府各首长的题词，并举行了庆祝大会。毛主席的题词是：“为侨民利益而服务”，周恩来总理的题词是：“为宣传新民主主义的共同纲领而奋斗，为保护国外华侨的正当权益而奋斗。”该报创刊三年庆祝会于新加坡大世界游艺场举行，到会者五千余人，热烈表示对祖国中央人民政府的拥护。会中该报为表示对祖国的无限忠诚和拥戴，献给中央人民政府一面绣有全体工作人员签名和椰树的锦旗，由中央人民政府华侨事务委员会副主任委员庄希泉代表接受。会后并举行游艺节目，其中有第一次被介绍到南洋的秧歌舞。
