Title: Publications of the Information Bureau Published in Swedish; Communist Parties of Sweden and Norway Publish Articles Welcoming the Publication, Believing it Will Strengthen the Peace and Democratic Forces of Both Countries

Author:
Date: 1950-01-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th - Telecommunication Agency Copenhagen, 10th: The Swedish Communist Party's newspaper *Ny Tid* published an article welcoming the publication of the Swedish version of the Information Bureau's newspaper, *For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy*, stating: "Swedish Communists can now more easily learn the working methods and experiences of communist parties in various countries. This will powerfully improve the work of the Swedish Communist Party and strengthen the forces of our country's peace and democracy front. Regularly studying the contents of the Information Bureau's newspaper should be an important task for every Communist Party member."
    The Norwegian Communist Party's newspaper *Friheten* stated: "Thoroughly studying the contents of the Information Bureau's newspaper is necessary for organizing the working people of Norway and all democratic patriotic elements to strive for peace, oppose warmongers, and strive for democracy and national independence."

### 情报局刊物瑞典文版出版  瑞共挪共报纸均著论欢迎  认为将加强两国和平民主力量

1950-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    情报局刊物瑞典文版出版
    瑞共挪共报纸均著论欢迎
    认为将加强两国和平民主力量
    【新华社北京十二日电】电通社哥本哈根十日讯：瑞典共产党机关报《新时代》报著论欢迎共产党情报局机关报《争取持久和平、争取人民民主》瑞典文版出版称：“瑞典共产党员今后可以更便利地学习各国共产党的工作方法和经验。这将有力地改进瑞典共产党的工作，加强我国和平民主阵线的力量。经常地研究情报局机关报的内容，应当是每一个共产党员的重要工作。”
    挪威共产党机关报自由报称：“透彻地研究情报局机关报的内容，对于组织挪威的劳动人民和一切民主爱国分子以争取和平、反对战争贩子、争取民主和民族独立，是必要的。”
