Title: Wuhan People's Delegation Visits Wounded Soldiers in Hospitals
Author:
Date: 1950-01-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 8th. The Wuhan Municipal People's Support-the-Army Committee sent a delegation to the Wuhan Hospital of the Central South Military Region and the directly subordinate hospital of the Health Department of the Fourth Field Army on the 6th of this month to visit wounded and sick soldiers. A total of 4,000 representatives from all walks of life participated in the visit. The delegation presented banners and gifts to the wounded and sick soldiers in each hospital and performed programs, creating an exceptionally enthusiastic atmosphere.

### 武汉各界慰问团赴医院慰问伤员

1950-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    武汉各界慰问团
    赴医院慰问伤员
    【新华社汉口八日电】武汉市各界拥军委员会，本月六日派慰问团赴中南军区武汉医院及第四野战军司令部卫生处直属医院慰问伤病员。参加慰问的各界代表共四千人。慰问团曾向各院伤病员献旗献礼，表演节目，情绪异常热烈。
