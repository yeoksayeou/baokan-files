Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-01-05
Page: 3

    [This newspaper's report] Central People's Broadcasting Station tonight (5th) 17:30 Women's Time: "Introduction to Beijing Women's Production and Education Center". 18:30 Natural Science Knowledge Lecture: "Surface Tension of Water". 18:00 First Literary Program: Fengtiao Dagushu "Wei Bingyi Returns Home". 21:00 Second Literary Program: Shanghai Opera and Yangzhou Opera.
    [Another news] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Staff Program: (1) Trade Union Work: Zu Tian Gong conveys the notice on holding "Workers' Radio Education" and talks about the importance of "Workers' Radio Education"; (2) Worker Service: ① Answers the question "Which is more important, factory administration or trade union?", ② Answers the question of how to write manuscripts. 13:00 Listener Service: ① Answers the question of why people's victory discount bonds should be issued, ② Admission brochure of the Central Cooperative Enterprise Administration Cooperative Cadre School. 16:30 Children's Program: Singing by children from the Central National Primary School in the Fourth District. 17:00 New Song Practice: Teaching to sing "Support the Issuance of People's Victory Bonds". 19:15 Voice of the City: ① The Health Bureau calls on citizens to pay attention to preventing smallpox and get vaccinated as soon as possible, ② The Public Security Bureau's notice "Please citizens to be vigilant and prevent fires".

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-01-05
第3版()
专栏：

    中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（五）晚十七点三十分妇女时间：“介绍北京市妇女生产教养院”。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“水的表面张力”。十八点第一次文艺节目：奉调大鼓“魏炳义回家”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：沪剧和维扬戏。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：（一）工会工作：祖田工传达关于举办“工人广播教育”的通知，并讲“工人广播教育”的重要性；（二）工人服务：①回答“关于工厂行政重要还是工会重要”的问题，②回答怎么写稿子的问题。十三点听众服务：①回答关于为什么要发行人民胜利折实公债的问题，②中央合作事业管理局合作干部学校招生简章。十六点三十分儿童节目：四区中心国民小学小朋友们唱歌。十七点新歌练习：教唱“拥护发行人民胜利公债”。十九点十五分市政之声：①卫生局号召市民注意预防天花快种牛痘，②公安局“请市民提高警惕严防火灾”的通知。
