Title: Czechoslovak Ambassador Visits Foreign Minister Zhou, Foreign Minister Zhou Personally Receives Him
Author:
Date: 1950-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 9th. Dr. Weisskopf, the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, after arriving in Beijing, formally visited Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at 6:00 PM today with his secretary, Mr. Rode. Minister Zhou personally received them. Deputy Minister Zhang Hanfu and Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru were present. They had a long and cordial conversation and took their leave at 7:40 PM.

### 捷大使拜访周外长  周外长亲自接见

1950-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    捷大使拜访周外长
    周外长亲自接见
    【新华社北京九日电】捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使魏斯柯普夫博士抵京后，于今日下午六时偕秘书罗戴正式拜访我外交部长周恩来，当由周部长亲自接见，在座者有副部长章汉夫及交际处长王倬如，畅谈甚久，至七时四十分告辞。
