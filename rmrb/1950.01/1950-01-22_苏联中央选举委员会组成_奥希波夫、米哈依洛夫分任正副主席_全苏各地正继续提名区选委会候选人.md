Title: Composition of the Soviet Central Election Commission: Osipov and Mikhailov Appointed Chairman and Vice-Chairman Respectively; Candidate Nominations for District Election Committees Continue Throughout the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1950-01-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 21st: TASS Moscow, January 20th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has approved the composition of the Central Election Commission to oversee the elections to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. Osipov, representing the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, has been approved as Chairman of the Central Election Commission. Mikhailov, representing the Lenin Communist Youth League, has been approved as Vice-Chairman, and Afanasy Dedov, representing the Trade Union of Workers in Political Education Organs, has been approved as Secretary. All members of the commission were nominated at numerous mass meetings of working people, in accordance with the Law on Elections to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.
    At the same time, nomination meetings for candidates to the district election committees for the elections to the Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities are continuing throughout the Soviet Union.

### 苏联中央选举委员会组成  奥希波夫、米哈依洛夫分任正副主席  全苏各地正继续提名区选委会候选人

1950-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联中央选举委员会组成
    奥希波夫、米哈依洛夫分任正副主席
    全苏各地正继续提名区选委会候选人
    【新华社北京二十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团已批准主持苏联最高苏维埃选举事宜的中央选举委员会的组成。代表全苏工会中央理事会的奥希波夫，已被批准为中央选举委员会主席，代表列宁共产主义青年团的米哈依洛夫被批准为副主席，代表政治教育机关职工工会的阿法纳西·杰多夫被批准为秘书。委员会的所有委员，都是按照苏联最高苏维埃选举法在许多劳动人民的大会上提名的。
    与此同时，全苏各地正在继续举行苏维埃联邦院和苏维埃民族院选举的区选举委员会候选人的提名大会。
