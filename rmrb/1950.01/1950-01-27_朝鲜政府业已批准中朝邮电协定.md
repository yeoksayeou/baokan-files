Title: Korean Government Approves Sino-Korean Postal and Telecommunications Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1950-01-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th. According to the Korean Central News Agency, Pyongyang, 24th: The Cabinet of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea approved three agreements and two additional protocols between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China regarding postal services, telegraph and telecommunications, and wired telephone communications on January 21st. The various agreements on mutual postal and telecommunications between Korea and China were signed in Beijing on December 25, 1949, and will come into effect on February 1, 1950, after being approved by both governments.

### 朝鲜政府业已批准中朝邮电协定

1950-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府业已批准中朝邮电协定
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社平壤二十四日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议，已于一月二十一日批准朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和中华人民共和国间关于通邮、电报电讯和有线电话通讯的三个协定和两个附加议定书。朝中两国间互通邮通电的各项协定，系于一九四九年十二月二十五日在北京签定，经双方政府批准后，自一九五零年二月一日起生效。
