Title: RCCK Second Standing Committee Resolution: Expulsion of Zhao Kangmin from the Party

Author:

Date: 1950-01-13
Page: 1

    RCCK Second Standing Committee Resolution
    Expulsion of Zhao Kangmin from the Party
    [Our Newspaper] The Second Standing Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang recently resolved to expel Zhao Kangmin, a member of the RCCK Shanghai Provisional Working Committee, from the party and announce it to the public. The announcement stated: Zhao Kangmin's ideology is vile, and his behavior is despicable. He has engaged in swindling activities, severely violating discipline. In order to enforce party discipline and strengthen the organization, the Second Standing Committee of the RCCK has decided to expel Zhao Kangmin from the party.

### 民革二次中常会决议  开除赵康民党籍

1950-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    民革二次中常会决议
    开除赵康民党籍
    【本报讯】中国国民党革命委员会第二次中常会日前决议将民革上海临时工作委员会所属会员赵康民开除党籍，并向社会公告。该公告称：赵康民思想恶劣，行为卑鄙，曾在外招摇撞骗，严重违反纪律，为严整党纪，健全组织，民革二次中常会决将赵康民开除出党。
