Title: Drama Improvement Bureau Prepares Public Theater, Opening Set for the 20th

Author:
Date: 1950-01-14
Page: 3

    The Drama Improvement Bureau Prepares Public Theater
    Opening Set for the 20th
    [This newspaper's report] The Public Theater (former site of Huale Theater), organized by the Ministry of Culture's Drama Improvement Bureau after many days of preparation, is scheduled to open on the 20th of this month. The opening performance will be "Three Attacks on the Zhu Family Village" by the Experimental Opera Troupe.

### 戏曲改进局筹办大众剧场  定廿日开业

1950-01-14
第3版()
专栏：

    戏曲改进局筹办大众剧场
    定廿日开业
    【本报讯】文化部戏曲改进局经多日筹组之大众剧场（华乐剧院旧址）订于本月二十日开幕。开锣第一个剧为戏曲实验剧团之《三打祝家庄》。
