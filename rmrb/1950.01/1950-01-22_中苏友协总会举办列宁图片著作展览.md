Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Holds Lenin Photo and Works Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1950-01-22
Page: 4
(News from Beijing) To commemorate the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death, the headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has organized an exhibition of photos and works related to Lenin. The content is divided into the following six parts: 1. Lenin's Biography; 2. Lenin's Works; 3. How Lenin Cared for Soviet Children; 4. How Lenin Valued Cultural and Educational Work; 5. Lenin's Legacy in Today's Soviet Union; 6. Lenin Library and Museum. It officially opened yesterday at the Association's location at 2 Yuheqiao. The exhibition is scheduled to last for three days, from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM each day. Many organizations and groups have enthusiastically signed up to visit.

### 中苏友协总会举办列宁图片著作展览

1950-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会举办列宁图片著作展览
    【北京讯】中苏友好协会总会，为纪念列宁逝世二十六周年，特举办有关列宁的图片及其著作展览。其内容共分下列六个部分：一、列宁书传，二、列宁著作，三、列宁怎样关心苏联儿童，四、列宁怎样重视文教工作，五、列宁遗志在今日的苏联，六、列宁图书馆及博物馆。已于昨日在御河桥二号会所正式开始。展览时间预定三天，每天自上午九时至下午七时，各机关团体前往报名参观者颇为踊跃。
