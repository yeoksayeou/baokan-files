Title: The 16th Government Administration Council Approved the Henan Land Reform Regulations; Passed the Hebei Provincial Government List for Submission for Appointment
Author:
Date: 1950-01-21
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th—The Central People's Government Administration Council held its 16th meeting at 3:00 PM today, approving the "Henan Land Reform Regulations" and the work plans of the departments and committees under the Political and Legal Affairs Committee of the Government Administration Council. The meeting approved the list of the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and members of the Hebei Provincial People's Government and submitted it to the Central People's Government Committee for appointment. It also approved the appointments of Xu Zirong as Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government, Li Guozhang as Deputy Director; and Yan Dingchu as Deputy Director of the Personnel Bureau of the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government.
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th—The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government recently announced that Wu Shaoshu is appointed as Counselor of the Ministry of Transportation of the Central People's Government, and Yu Yong is appointed as a Specialist Committee Member of the Ministry of Transportation of the Central People's Government.

### 第十六次政务会议批准河南省土地改革条例  通过河北省府名单提请任命

1950-01-21
第2版()
专栏：

    第十六次政务会议批准河南省土地改革条例
    通过河北省府名单提请任命
    【新华社北京二十日电】中央人民政府政务院今天下午三时举行第十六次政务会议，批准了《河南省土地改革条例》和政务院政治法律委员会所属各部、会的工作计划。会议通过了河北省人民政府主席、副主席和委员的名单，提请中央人民政府委员会任命，并通过任命徐子荣为中央人民政府公安部办公厅主任，李国章为副主任；阎定础为中央人民政府公安部人事局副局长。
    【新华社北京二十日电】中央人民政府政务院顷发表吴绍澍为中央人民政府交通部参事，俞墉为中央人民政府交通部专门委员。
