Title: Military Tribunal of the Soviet Primorsky Military District Sentences Japanese Bacteriological Warfare Criminals to Two to Twenty-Five Years of Labor Reform; Representatives of the Working People of Khabarovsk Express Satisfaction with the Verdict
Author:
Date: 1950-01-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd: TASS, Khabarovsk dispatch: The Military Tribunal of the Primorsky Military District pronounced the verdict on the former Japanese military personnel accused of preparing and using bacteriological weapons on the afternoon of December 30th. The court sentenced defendants Otozō Yamada, Ryūji Kajitsuka, Takaatsu Takahashi and Kiyoshi Kawashima to 25 years of labor reform camp, Shunji Satō and Tomio Karasawa to 20 years of labor reform camp, Toyohiko Nishi to 18 years, Katsuo Mitomo to 15 years, Masao Onoue to 12 years, Zensaku Hirazakura to 10 years, Shigeji Kurushima to 3 years, and Jōzō Kikuchi to 2 years. Representatives of the working people of Khabarovsk who attended the court were extremely satisfied with the verdict.

### 苏滨海军区军事法庭  判决日本细菌战犯  分处二年至廿五年劳动改造  伯力劳动人民代表对判决表示满意

1950-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏滨海军区军事法庭
    判决日本细菌战犯
    分处二年至廿五年劳动改造
    伯力劳动人民代表对判决表示满意
    【新华社北京二日电】塔斯社伯力讯：滨海军区军事法庭于十二月三十日下午宣读对被控犯准备及使用细菌武器罪的前日本军军人的判决书。法庭判决被告山田乙三、梶塚龙二、高桥高笃及川岛清入劳动改造营二十五年，佐藤春二及唐泽富雄入劳动改造营二十年，西丰彦十八年，三友胜雄十五年，尾上正雄十二年，平樱善作十年，久留岛重治三年，菊池乘三两年。列席法庭的伯力劳动人民代表对这一判决书，极为满意。
