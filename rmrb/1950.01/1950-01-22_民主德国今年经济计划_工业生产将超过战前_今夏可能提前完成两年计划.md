Title: East Germany's Economic Plan for This Year: Industrial Production to Exceed Pre-War Levels; Two-Year Plan Likely to be Completed Ahead of Schedule This Summer
Author:
Date: 1950-01-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 21st: TASS, Berlin, January 20th: Heinrich Rau, Minister of Planning of the Provisional Government of the German Democratic Republic, reported on the 1950 national economic plan at a meeting of the Provisional People's Chamber. Rau declared: The Republic's two-year plan, which began on January 1, 1949, has all the prerequisites for completion in the summer of 1950. In 1950, industrial production will be 21 percent higher than in 1949. Many industries, such as coal mining, chemicals, power, and machinery, will greatly exceed the levels of 1936. Steel production in 1950 will be 36 percent higher than in 1949, electrical equipment 35 percent higher, precision and optical instruments 32 percent higher, machinery industry 30 percent higher, building materials 29 percent higher, chemicals 21 percent higher, textiles 21 percent higher, food industry 20 percent higher, and light industry 17 percent higher. In terms of power, 48 million marks will be invested in 1950 to build power plants. Lignite production in 1950 will increase by 132 million tons, exceeding the 1936 level by 30 percent. The food industry sector, including the fish industry, will produce a total of 3.8 billion marks worth of products in 1950, compared to 3.1 billion marks in 1949.
    The sown area this year will be increased to pre-war levels. By the end of 1950, the total number of cattle will reach 3.6 million, and the total number of pigs will reach 5.7 million, reaching pre-war levels.
    Due to the improvement of the Republic's economic situation, it is possible to increase the wages of the working people by 15 percent in 1950.
    Heinrich Rau continued, "Our economic plan, on the one hand, plans to increase production, and on the other hand, plans to increase the people's purchasing power." Commodity transactions in 1950 will exceed or at least equal 144 percent of 1949. Foreign trade will increase by 90 percent.
    Heinrich Rau finally pointed out that the plan of the German Democratic Republic serves the cause of peace and German unity.

### 民主德国今年经济计划  工业生产将超过战前  今夏可能提前完成两年计划

1950-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国今年经济计划
    工业生产将超过战前
    今夏可能提前完成两年计划
    【新华社北京二十一日电】塔斯社柏林二十日讯：德意志民主共和国临时政府计划部长亨利·劳氏，在临时人民议会会议上报告了一九五○年的国民经济计划。劳氏宣称：从一九四九年一月一日开始的共和国两年计划，有在一九五○年夏季完成的一切前提条件。一九五○年，工业生产将较一九四九年高百分之二十一。许多工业，如采煤、化学、动力及机器工业等，将大大超过一九三六年的水平。一九五○年的钢铁生产，将较一九四九年高百分之三十六，电工器材将高百分之三十五，精确仪器及光学仪器将高百分之三十二，机器业百分之三十，建筑材料百分之二十九，化学品百分之二十一，纺织品百分之二十一，食品工业百分之二十，轻工业百分之十七。在动力方面，一九五○年将投资四千八百万马克，以建设动力厂。褐煤的产量：一九五○年要增加一亿三千二百万吨，超过一九三六年水平的百分之三十。包括鱼类工业在内的食品工业部门，在一九五○年中将总共生产价值三十八亿马克的产品，一九四九年中的产品总值为三十一亿马克。
    今年的播种面积将提高到战前水平。一九五○年底，牛的总头数将达三百六十万，猪的总头数达五百七十万，达到战前水平。
    由于共和国经济情况的改善，一九五○年中可能增加劳动人民的工资百分之十五。
    亨利·劳氏继称：“我们的经济计划，一方面筹划了增加生产，同时又筹划了提高人民的购买力。”一九五○年的货物交易，将超过或至少等于一九四九年的百分之一百四十四。对外贸易将增加百分之九十。
    亨利·劳氏最后指出，德意志民主共和国的计划，是服务于和平和德国统一的事业的。
