Title: Central, Beijing People's Broadcasting Station Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-01-08
Page: 3

    Central People's Broadcasting Station today (8th) 17:30 in the evening, Workers' Time: All-China Federation of Trade Unions "Investigation Outline on the Work of the Electrical Workers' Union." 18:30 Natural Science Knowledge Lecture: "How was Penicillin Discovered?" 20:30 Saturday Art and Literature: Yangko Opera "Red Cloth Strip." 18:00 First Art Program: Paizi Opera "Great Production." 21:00 Second Art Program: New Songs (1) Iron Tree Blossoms, (2) Liberation Construction Flower Drum, (3) Selection of Songs for Loving the People on the Front Lines.
    [Another News] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station 12:30 Workers' Program (1) Entertainment: Allegro Opera "Sino-Soviet Friendship," (2) News: Railway workers in Fengtai Changxindian and other places in Beijing enthusiastically purchase People's Victory Public Bonds. 13:00 Listener Service (1) Answering the question of why the government still allows private banks and financial institutions to exist, (2) Recruitment announcement from the North China Coal Mine Management Bureau for technical personnel, (3) Recruitment announcement from the Anshan Iron and Steel Company of the Northeast Industrial Department for cost accounting and statistics personnel. 16:30 Children's Program: Sun Jingxiu continues to tell the Soviet fairy tale "Kind Umar." 17:00 Music Program: Sino-Soviet Friendship Songs. 19:15 Introduction to Soviet Education.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-01-08
第3版()
专栏：

    中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（八）日晚十七点三十分职工时间：全总《电业工会工作情况调查大纲》。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：《青霉素（盘尼西林）是怎样发明的？》八点三十分星期文艺：秧歌剧《红布条》。十八点第一次文艺节目：牌子戏《大生产》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：新歌曲（一）铁树开花，（二）解放建设花鼓，（三）火线爱民选曲。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目（一）文娱：快板剧《中苏友好》，（二）通讯：北京市丰台长辛店等地铁路职工热烈购买人民胜利公债。十三点听众服务（一）回答政府为什么还准私营行庄存在的问题，（二）华北煤矿管理总局招聘技术人员的启事，（三）东北工业部鞍山钢铁公司招收成本会计统计人员的启事。十六点三十分儿童节目：孙敬修继续讲苏联童话《好心的乌马尔》。十七点音乐节目：中苏友谊小唱。十九点十五分介绍苏联的教育事业。
