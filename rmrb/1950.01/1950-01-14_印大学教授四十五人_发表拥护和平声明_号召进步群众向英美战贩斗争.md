Title: Forty-Five Indian University Professors Issue Statement Supporting Peace, Calling on Progressive Masses to Struggle Against Anglo-American Warmongers
Author:
Date: 1950-01-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th: The "Naya Sarira" newspaper in Delhi published a statement by forty-five professors of Allahabad University supporting peace and people's democracy, expressing their firm dedication to the cause of world peace. The statement said: We know that the causes of modern war lie in capitalism and imperialism. Therefore, we welcome the victory of socialism in the Soviet Union and the victory of people's democracy in Eastern European countries and in China. The statement further pointed out that the Indian government has approved a huge military budget, cutting back on the meager funds for health, education, and other social undertakings. The professors finally called on the progressive masses to unite and fight against the Anglo-American warmongers and their accomplices in India.

### 印大学教授四十五人  发表拥护和平声明  号召进步群众向英美战贩斗争

1950-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    印大学教授四十五人
    发表拥护和平声明
    号召进步群众向英美战贩斗争
    【新华社北京十三日电】塔斯社德里十二日讯：《纳雅萨里拉》报刊载阿拉哈巴德大学四十五位教授拥护和平与人民民主的声明，表示坚决献身于世界和平事业。声明说：我们知道近代战争的原因是在于资本主义和帝国主义。因此，我们欢迎社会主义在苏联的胜利和人民民主主义在东欧国家和在中国的胜利。声明进一步指出，印度政府批准了庞大的军事预算，削减了少得可怜的卫生、教育及其他社会事业的经费。教授们最后号召进步的群众团结起来，对英美战争贩子和他们在印度的帮凶者进行斗争。
