Title: Central People's Radio Station Today's Broadcast Program
Author:
Date: 1950-01-21
Page: 3

    Central People's Radio Station Today's Broadcast Program
    [This newspaper's report] The Central People's Broadcasting Station's special program at 20:20 today (21st) commemorating the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death: Qian Junrui will broadcast "Lenin – Immortal Leader, Mentor, and Friend." 17:30 Youth Time: The story of Lenin's childhood, "The Student Who Ranked First." 18:00 First arts and literature program: Qinshu storytelling "Shi Yaozong's Transformation." 21:00 Second arts and literature program: Soviet music. 18:45, broadcasting seven new songs including "Fighting Production" and "Young Petrel."
    [Another news item] Beijing People's Radio Station 12:30 Workers' program: Teaching to sing "Bright Red Lantern." 13:00 Women's program: Women's hygiene knowledge "Causes and Treatment of Leukorrhea." 16:30 Children's program: Children from the No. 6 District Central National Primary School perform dialogues, songs, and speeches. 17:00 Continuing to teach singing "Bright Red Lantern." 18:30 Youth Lecture Broadcast Lecture: Wang Shigu speaks on "About Lenin's Stories." 19:30 Municipal Voice: Report on "Post Offices Universally Accepting and Sending International Mail." 19:45 Sun Fuling speaks on "How to Overcome Ideological Concerns to Purchase Public Bonds."

### 中央人民电台今日广播节目

1950-01-21
第3版()
专栏：

    中央人民电台今日广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（二十一）日二十点二十分纪念列宁逝世二十六周年特别节目：钱俊瑞播讲《列宁——不朽的领袖导师和朋友》。十七点三十分青年时间：列宁少年时代的故事《考第一的学生》。十八点第一次文艺节目：琴书《史耀宗转变》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：苏联音乐。十八点四十五分，播送《战斗生产》、《年青的海燕》等七支新歌曲。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：教唱《鲜红的灯》。十三点妇女节目：妇女卫生常识《白带病发生的原因和治疗的方法》。十六点三十分儿童节目：六区中心国民小学小朋友们演播对话、唱歌和讲话。十七点继续教唱《鲜红的灯》。十八点三十分青年讲演会广播讲演：王士谷讲《关于列宁的故事》。十九点三十分市政之声：《邮局普遍收寄国际邮件》的报导。十九点四十五分孙孚陵讲《如何打通思想上的顾虑来购买公债》。
