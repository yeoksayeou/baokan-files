Title: The CCP Central Committee Issues Notice: Party Organizations on Railways and Along Railway Lines Must Guarantee Completion of Ministry of Railways' Plan
Author:
Date: 1950-01-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 30th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China issued a notice on January 29th regarding guaranteeing the implementation of the Ministry of Railways' 1950 work plan. The full text is as follows:

    The Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government's 1950 work plan has been approved by the Government Administration Council and has been put into effect. The CCP Central Committee agrees with this plan. The CCP Central Committee directs all Party organizations and all Party members on the railways to first initiate the study and discussion of this plan among workers, staff, and engineering and technical personnel, and to ensure the complete fulfillment and surpassing of this plan with high enthusiasm and diligent work. All CCP Central Bureaus and Provincial Committees, and their subordinate local Party organizations, in areas through which railway lines pass, should discuss this plan and provide the Ministry of Railways with all possible assistance in various aspects.

### 中共中央发出通知  铁路上及沿线党的组织要保证完成铁道部计划

1950-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央发出通知
    铁路上及沿线党的组织要保证完成铁道部计划
    【新华社北京三十日电】中国共产党中央委员会于一月二十九日发出关于保证实施铁道部一九五○年工作计划的通知，全文如下：
    中央人民政府铁道部一九五○年工作计划已经政务院批准，并已付之实施。中共中央同意这个计划。中共中央责成铁路上所有党的组织与全体党员首先发起在工人、职员和工程技术人员中研究与讨论这个计划，并保证以高度的热情和努力的工作全部完成和超过这个计划。凡属铁路线经过地区的中共中央局和省委、及其以下的地方党的组织，均应讨论这个计划，并在各方面给铁道部以尽可能的协助。
