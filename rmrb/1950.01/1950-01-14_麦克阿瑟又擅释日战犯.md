Title: MacArthur Again Arbitrarily Releases Japanese War Criminals
Author:
Date: 1950-01-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 13th: In order to foster the revival of Japanese militarism, MacArthur has recently arbitrarily released another group of Japanese war criminals. According to a United Press dispatch from Tokyo on the 10th: Three Japanese war criminals, namely, Lieutenant General Shigeru Sawada, former commander of the Japanese 13th Expeditionary Army, Captain Okada, and Tatsuta, who were sentenced to five years' imprisonment in 1949 for trying eight captured American pilots during the war and executing three of them, have been released for "good behavior." This is the third group of Japanese war criminals that MacArthur has arbitrarily released since December 25th of last year.

### 麦克阿瑟又擅释日战犯

1950-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    麦克阿瑟又擅释日战犯
    【新华社北京十三日电】麦克阿瑟为了扶植日本军国主义的复活，最近又擅自释放一批日本战犯。据合众社东京十日讯：曾因审判战时美国八名被俘飞行员并枪决其中三名而于一九四九年被判处五年徒刑的三名日本战犯，已因“行为良好”而获释放。该三人为前日本第十三派遣军指挥官泽田茂中将，冈田大尉和辰田。这是麦克阿瑟自去年十二月二十五日以来，擅自释放的第三批日本战犯。
