Title: Kim Il-sung Sends Condolences

Author:

Date: 1950-01-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Pyongyang newspapers on the 25th prominently published the news of the death of Vulko Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria. Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Central Committees of the North and South Korean Workers' Parties, sent telegrams of condolence to the Bulgarian government and the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party on the 24th, respectively. Kim Il-sung's telegram reads as follows:

"The government and people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea express the same grief as the beloved Bulgarian people for the death of Vulko Chervenkov, an old revolutionary of Bulgaria and close comrade-in-arms of Dimitrov. The death of Chervenkov is not only a loss for the Bulgarian people but also for the people of all countries fighting for peace and democracy. Chervenkov, who dedicated his life to the cause of democracy and peace, his glorious achievements will be immortal!"

### 金日成电唁

1950-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成电唁
    【新华社讯】平壤各报二十五日均以显要地位刊载保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席科拉罗夫逝世的消息。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议主席金日成及南、北劳动党中央委员会，二十四日分别致电保加利亚政府和保加利亚共产党中央致唁。金日成电文如下：
    “朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和人民，对保加利亚的老革命家季米特洛夫的亲密战友、保加利亚部长会议主席科拉罗夫的逝世，表示与亲爱的保加利亚人民一样的哀悼。科拉罗夫的逝世，不仅是保国人民的损失，也是为和平民主而斗争的一切国家人民的损失。终身为民主和平事业而斗争的科拉罗夫，其光辉业迹，永垂不朽！”
