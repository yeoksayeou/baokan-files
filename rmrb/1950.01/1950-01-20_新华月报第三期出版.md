Title: Xinhua Monthly, Issue 3 Published
Author:
Date: 1950-01-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th. The third issue (New Year Edition) of Volume 1 of "Xinhua Monthly", compiled and printed by the "Xinhua Monthly" office of the Central People's Government Publishing Administration, has been published. It contains over two hundred documents and materials, the main contents of which include: four pieces, including the New Year's Message of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Xinhua News Agency editorial; three pieces about Chairman Mao's visit to the Soviet Union; thirteen pieces celebrating Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday; twenty pieces on the general budget of financial revenue and expenditure and the Victory Discount Public Bonds; fifteen pieces on the Asian Women's Congress. The cover is a color cartoon featuring the national flags of eleven countries in the socialist and people's democratic camp. The pictorial contains photos of the presidium of the Moscow Grand Theatre celebrating Stalin's 70th birthday, in which Marshal Stalin and Chairman Mao stand shoulder to shoulder.

### 新华月报第三期出版

1950-01-20
第3版()
专栏：

    新华月报第三期出版
    【新华社北京十九日电】中央人民政府出版总署《新华月报》社所编印的《新华月报》第一卷第三期（新年号）业已出版，所收文件资料达二百余篇，主要内容有：中共中央新年文告和新华社社论等四篇，毛主席访苏三篇，庆祝斯大林大元帅七十寿辰十三篇，关于财政收支概算和胜利折实公债二十篇，亚洲妇代会十五篇。封面是彩色漫画，有社会主义和人民民主阵营十一个国家的国旗。画刊中有莫斯科大戏院庆祝斯大林七十寿辰大会主席团的照片，其中斯大林大元帅和毛主席并肩而立。
