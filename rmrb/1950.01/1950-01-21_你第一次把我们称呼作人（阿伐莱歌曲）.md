Title: The First Time You Called Us Humans (Avar Song)
Author: Ming Jian
Date: 1950-01-21
Page: 6

    The First Time You Called Us Humans
    (Avar Song)
    Translated by Ming Jian
    When the dawn rises in the east,
    All the stars, like ice, turn white and melt.
    Compared to you, all the scholars in the world
    Pale in comparison, like stars to the dawn.
    The first time you came to us, calling us humans,
    And placed weapons of victory in our hands,
    The wicked people hid in the cracks,
    Like geese avoiding the eagle's call.
    (Note) This is a short poem by the Avar people of the Soviet Union (also known as the Obri) commemorating Lenin.

### 你第一次把我们称呼作人（阿伐莱歌曲）
明鉴
1950-01-21
第6版()
专栏：

    你第一次把我们称呼作人
    （阿伐莱歌曲）    明鉴译
    当曙光升在东方，
    所有的星星，像冰块，变白并融化。
    全世界的学者和你相比，
    像星星比曙光，黯然失色。
    你第一次跑过来，把我们称呼作人，
    还把胜利的武器放到我们手上，
    万恶的人们躲进隙缝，
    像鹅鸟躲避雄鹰的叫声。
    （注）这是苏联阿伐莱（又称奥勃莱）族人民纪念列宁的一首小诗。
