Title: Albanian and Mongolian Women's Organizations Exchange New Year's Greetings with All-China Women's Federation
Author:
Date: 1950-01-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 17th: The Albanian Women's Union and the Central Committee of the Mongolian Working Women and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation exchanged New Year's greetings. The telegrams are as follows:

(1) Telegram from the Albanian Women's Union and reply from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
All-China Democratic Women's Federation:

On the occasion of the New Year 1950, we sincerely wish the Chinese comrades great success in consolidating the great victory of the Chinese people and strengthening the forces of peace and the democratic cause of the world.

Albanian Women's Union
Albanian Women's Association:

Thank you very much for your kind New Year's greetings and your wishes to us. We will certainly work tirelessly to consolidate our victory and strengthen the forces of world peace and democracy. With the wise leadership of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, we will certainly be able to overcome all difficulties and complete the construction of our great new China. We also hope that you will achieve success in your construction work and smoothly enter the construction of socialism.

With everlasting friendship and New Year's greetings.

All-China Democratic Women's Federation
Chairman Cai Chang

(2) Telegram from the Central Committee of the Mongolian Working Women and reply from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation:
To Madame Cai Chang, Chairman of the Women's Committee of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

On behalf of all the working women of the Mongolian People's Republic, I extend New Year's greetings to you and the working women of the People's Republic of China. We hope that your heroic Chinese women, under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, will achieve greater success in the cause of promoting your free country and building a new and happy life.

Chairman of the Central Committee of the Laboring Women, Udeval
December 31, 1949

To Comrade Udeval, Chairman of the Central Committee of Mongolian Working Women:

We deeply appreciate your New Year's greetings and kindness. We have full confidence that under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, we will strive to complete the task of building a new China. We hope that we will receive the cooperation of all friendly neighbors in our struggle against the common enemies threatening world peace.

All the women of China join me in wishing you a Happy New Year and everlasting and solid friendship.

All-China Democratic Women's Federation
Chairman Cai Chang

### 阿尔巴尼亚及蒙古妇女团体  与我妇联互贺新年

1950-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚及蒙古妇女团体
    与我妇联互贺新年
    【新华社北京十七日电】阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会及蒙古劳动妇女中央委员会和中华全国民主妇女联合会互致新年贺电。电文如下：
            （一）阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会来电及中华全国民主妇女联合会复电中华全国民主妇女联合会：
    借一九五○年新年的机会，我们热诚的祝福中国同志在他们巩固中国人民的伟大胜利，加强和平力量与世界民主事业中得到伟大的成功。
                                                阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会阿尔巴尼亚妇女会：
    很感谢你们的新年祝贺的厚意和你们对我们的愿望。我们一定努力不懈，为巩固我们的胜利和加强世界和平民主的力量而奋斗。我们有共产党和毛泽东主席的贤明领导，我们一定能够克服一切的困难，能够完成我们伟大的新中国的建设。我们同样希望你们在建设工作中，得到成功而顺利的进入社会主义的建设。
    此致恒古不渝的友谊和新年的祝贺
                             中华全国民主妇女联合会
                                                主席蔡畅
            （二）蒙古劳动妇女中央委员会来电及中华全国民主妇女联合会
        复电：北京中华人民共和国妇女委员会主席蔡畅夫人：
    我代表蒙古人民共和国全体劳动妇女，向你和中华人民共和国的劳动妇女致新年的祝贺。我们希望你们英勇的中国妇女在中国共产党贤明的领导之下，为繁荣你们的自由的国家、为建设新的幸福生活的事业得到更大的成功。
                              劳动妇女中央委员会主席乌得伐尔
                                    一九四九年十二月三十一日蒙古劳动妇女中央委员会主席乌得伐尔同志：
    我们深深感谢你们的新年祝贺和厚意，我们具有充分的信心，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席贤明的领导下，我们行将努力完成建设新中国的任务。我们希望我们反对威胁世界和平的共同敌人的斗争中得到所有友好邻邦的合作。
    全中国妇女与我一道，你们新年快乐并致恒久巩固的友谊。
                                 中华全国民主妇女联合会
                                             主席蔡畅
