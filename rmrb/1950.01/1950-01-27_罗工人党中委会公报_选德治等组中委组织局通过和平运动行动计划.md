Title: Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee Communiqué: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej Elected to Head Central Committee Organizational Bureau; Action Plan for Peace Movement Approved

Author:

Date: 1950-01-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th. TASS, Bucharest dispatch: The Romanian Workers' Party newspaper, "Spark," published the following communiqué:

    "The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party held a plenary session on the 23rd and 24th. Several district party committee secretaries and secretaries of party organizations in major industrial enterprises also attended the meeting.
    The meeting passed the following resolutions: 1. The plenary session discussed and approved the report of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party on the organizational tasks of the Party. 2. The plenary session approved the action plan for the peace movement proposed by Chișinevschi. 3. The plenary session elected the Organizational Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, composed of seventeen people including Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Georgescu, Ana Pauker, and Luca. 4. The plenary session decided to remove Nicolae Radovanovici from his position as a member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party because he concealed his past political history and was unfit to serve as a member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party. 5. The plenary session elected Petrescu and Bujik, alternate members of the Central Committee, as full members."

### 罗工人党中委会公报  选德治等组中委组织局通过和平运动行动计划

1950-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    罗工人党中委会公报
    选德治等组中委组织局通过和平运动行动计划
    【新华社北京二十六日电】塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚工人党机关报火花报刊登下列公报：
    “罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会于二十三、二十四日举行全体会议。若干区党委会书记及大工业企业党组织的书记，亦列席会议。
    会议通过决议如下：一、全会讨论通过罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会政治局关于党的组织任务的报告。二、全会通过契希涅夫斯奇所提和平运动的行动计划。三、全会选出罗马尼亚工人党中委会组织局，由乔治乌·德治、乔治斯库、安娜·波克与卢加等十七人组成。四、全会决定把罗马尼亚工人党中央委员尼可拉·拉多瓦诺维奇撤职，因为他隐瞒过去的政治历史，不能担任罗马尼亚工人党中央委员。五、全会选举候补中央委员彼特里斯古及布吉克为中央委员”。
