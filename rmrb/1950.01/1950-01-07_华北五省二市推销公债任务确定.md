Title: Public Bond Sales Targets Determined for Five Provinces and Two Municipalities in North China
Author:
Date: 1950-01-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 6th. The public bond sales targets for the five provinces and two municipalities in North China directly under the Central People's Government have been determined. The Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council convened a meeting of responsible persons from various provinces and cities on the 5th to discuss the matter and, based on the specific circumstances of each province and city, made the following allocations: Tianjin Municipality: 7.3 million shares; Beijing Municipality: 2.9 million shares; Hebei Province: 3 million shares; Shanxi Province: 1 million shares; Pingyuan Province: 500,000 shares; Suiyuan Province: 400,000 shares; Chahar Province: 400,000 shares. A total of 15.5 million shares, exceeding the original quota of 15 million shares stipulated by the Government Administration Council by 500,000 shares.

### 华北五省二市推销公债任务确定

1950-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    华北五省二市推销公债任务确定
    【新华社北京六日电】中央人民政府直辖之华北五省二市公债推销任务，业已确定。政务院财经委员会于五日召集各省市负责人会商，并依据各省市情况具体分配如下：天津市七百三十万分；北京市二百九十万分；河北省三百万分；山西省一百万分；平原省五十万分；绥远省四十万分；察哈尔省四十万分。共计一千五百五十万分，超过政务院原规定之数额五十万分。
