Title: Vyshinsky Receives Afghan Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1950-01-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 27th: TASS reports: Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky received Sultan Ahmad Khan, the Afghan Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, on the 25th, to discuss matters concerning the ambassador's upcoming presentation of his credentials to the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

### 维辛斯基接见阿富汗大使

1950-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    维辛斯基接见阿富汗大使
    【新华社北京二十七日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部长维辛斯基，于二十五日接见阿富汗驻苏特命全权大使苏丹·阿哈德可汗，商讨该大使行将向苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席呈递国书事宜。
