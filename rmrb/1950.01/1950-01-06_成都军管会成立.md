Title: Chengdu Military Control Commission Established
Author:
Date: 1950-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, January 4th: The Chengdu Military Control Commission was established on New Year's Day, 1950, upon the order of Liu Bocheng, Commander, and Deng Xiaoping, Political Commissar, of the Second Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Director Li Jingquan, Deputy Director Zhou Shidi, and responsible personnel from various departments have all taken office and are working.

### 成都军管会成立

1950-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    成都军管会成立
    【新华社成都四日电】成都市军事管制委员会奉中国人民解放军第二野战军刘伯承司令员、邓小平政委电令，已于一九五○年元旦日成立。军管会主任李井泉、副主任周士第以及各部门负责人员均已到职工作。
