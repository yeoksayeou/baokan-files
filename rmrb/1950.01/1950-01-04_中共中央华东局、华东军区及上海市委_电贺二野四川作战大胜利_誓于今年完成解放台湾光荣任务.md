Title: CCP Central Committee East China Bureau, East China Military Region, and Shanghai Municipal Committee Send Telegram of Congratulations on the Great Victory of the Second Field Army in Sichuan, Vowing to Complete the Glorious Task of Liberating Taiwan This Year
Author:
Date: 1950-01-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 3rd. The CCP Central Committee East China Bureau, the East China Military Region Headquarters, Political Department, and the CCP Shanghai Municipal Committee have sent a telegram to Commander Liu Bocheng, Political Commissar Deng Xiaoping, and Deputy Political Commissar Zhang Jichun of the Second Field Army, congratulating them on the great victory of liberating Chengdu and completely annihilating the Hu Zongnan bandit troops. The telegram reads as follows:

    Commander Liu, Political Commissar Deng, Deputy Political Commissar Zhang, and all the commanders and fighters of the Second Field Army:

    We are delighted to hear of your great victory in completely annihilating the Hu Zongnan bandit troops and liberating Chengdu, the capital of Sichuan Province. As we celebrate the New Year, we extend our warmest congratulations to you. The annihilation of the Hu Zongnan main force of the Kuomintang bandit gang and the liberation of Chengdu, the capital of Sichuan Province, mark the complete victory of the Chinese People's Liberation War on the Chinese mainland and the reactionary rule of imperialism and the Kuomintang has been overthrown forever. All the people of East China and Shanghai are extremely excited and encouraged by this. We pledge to cooperate with all fraternal units in 1950 to resolutely complete the glorious combat task of finally liberating Taiwan and thoroughly eliminating the remaining Chiang bandit forces. We hereby send our congratulations.

    CCP Central Committee East China Bureau, CCP Shanghai Municipal Committee, East China Military Region Headquarters, Political Department, January 3rd.

### 中共中央华东局、华东军区及上海市委  电贺二野四川作战大胜利  誓于今年完成解放台湾光荣任务

1950-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央华东局、华东军区及上海市委
    电贺二野四川作战大胜利
    誓于今年完成解放台湾光荣任务
    【新华社上海三日电】中共中央华东局、华东军区司令部、政治部、中共上海市委会顷致电第二野战军刘伯承司令员、邓小平政委、张际春副政委，祝贺解放成都与全歼胡宗南匪部的伟大胜利。电文如下：刘司令员、邓政委、张副政委并转二野全体指战员同志们：
    欣闻你们全歼胡宗南匪部，解放四川省会——成都的伟大胜利，我们于欢度新年之际，以极热烈的心情向你们祝贺。国民党匪帮胡宗南主力的被歼灭和四川省会成都获得解放，标志着中国人民解放战争在中国大陆上业已获得完全胜利，帝国主义国民党的反动统治已被永远推翻。全华东、全上海人民无不对此感到极大兴奋和鼓励。我们誓于一九五○年配合各兄弟部队，坚决完成最后解放台湾、彻底消灭蒋匪残余力量的光荣战斗任务。谨此电贺。
    中共中央华东局、中共上海市委员会、华东军区司令部、政治部一月三日
