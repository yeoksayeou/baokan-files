Title: Long Yun Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-01-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: Long Yun, member of the Central People's Government and member of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, arrived in Beijing from Hong Kong on the 18th.

### 龙云抵京

1950-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    龙云抵京
