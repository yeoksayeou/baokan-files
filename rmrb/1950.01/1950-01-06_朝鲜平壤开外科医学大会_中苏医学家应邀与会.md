Title: Pyongyang, Korea Holds Surgical Medical Conference; Chinese and Soviet Medical Experts Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1950-01-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 5th: The first Korean National Surgical Medical Conference was held in Pyongyang from December 25th to 27th of last year. More than 150 medical experts, university professors and health workers attended the conference. Soviet medical expert Professor Arujunon, who is in Korea, and five Chinese surgical medical experts headed by Zhou Zezhao, director of surgery at Beijing Hospital, were also invited to attend the conference. The central topics of the conference were: the fight against cancer; research and treatment of gastric and duodenal ulcers; trauma, osteomyelitis and its treatment, etc.

### 朝鲜平壤开外科医学大会  中苏医学家应邀与会

1950-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜平壤开外科医学大会
    中苏医学家应邀与会
    【新华社平壤五日电】朝鲜全国第一届外科医学大会，于去年十二月二十五日至二十七日在平壤举行。到会医学家、大学教授和保健事业工作人员共一百五十余人。在朝鲜的苏联医学家阿鲁朱诺恩教授及以北京医院外科主任周泽昭为首的中国外科医学家五人，亦应邀与会。大会的中心议题为：与癌症的斗争；胃溃疡与十二指肠溃疡的研究与治疗；外伤、骨髓炎及其治疗等。
