Title: Afghan Ambassador to the Soviet Union Presents Credentials
Author:
Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th: TASS Moscow dispatch: The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Shvernik, received Sultan Ahmad Khan, the Afghan Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary, at the Kremlin on the 26th to accept his credentials. Ambassador Sultan Ahmad Khan was accompanied by Second Secretaries of the Embassy, Mukhmmad Hishm Khan and Mukhmmad Yaeshia Khan. Both were introduced to Shvernik. Those attending the ceremony also included Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zorin, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Gorkin, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Molochkov, and Acting Director of the Near East and Middle East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Maksimov. After the ceremony, Shvernik and Ambassador Sultan Ahmad Khan held talks, with Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zorin also present.

### 阿富汗驻苏大使呈递国书

1950-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗驻苏大使呈递国书
    【新华社北京二十八日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克，于二十六日在克里姆林宫接见呈递国书的阿富汗特命全权大使苏丹·阿哈米德·可汗。苏丹·阿哈米德·可汗大使率领大使馆二等秘书姆希麦德·希施姆·可汗和姆希麦德雅叶希亚·可汗同来。并将两人介绍给什维尔尼克。参加典礼者尚有苏联外交部副部长佐林、苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金、苏联外交部交际司司长莫洛赤科夫、苏联外交部近东与中东司代理司长马克西莫夫。礼成后，什维尔尼克和苏丹·阿哈米德·可汗大使会谈，苏联外交部副部长佐林亦在座。
