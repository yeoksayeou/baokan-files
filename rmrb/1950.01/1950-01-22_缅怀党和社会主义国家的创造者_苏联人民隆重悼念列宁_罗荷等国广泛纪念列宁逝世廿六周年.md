Title: Remembering the Creator of the Party and Socialist State: Soviet People Solemnly Commemorate Lenin; Romania, Holland, and Other Countries Widely Commemorate the 26th Anniversary of Lenin's Death
Author:
Date: 1950-01-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 21st: TASS, Moscow dispatch: The Soviet people solemnly commemorate Lenin, the founder of the Bolshevik Party and the creator of the world's first socialist state.

On the eve of the 26th anniversary of the death of this proletarian revolutionary genius, memorial meetings have been held in towns and villages throughout the Soviet Union. Memorial meetings have been held in Moscow, Leningrad, Kiev, Arkhangelsk, Frunze, and other places. Exhibitions of Lenin's writings and manuscripts are being held at the Lenin Library in Moscow and in factories and plants in the capital. From 1917 to 1949, Lenin's works during his lifetime were published in more than 188.8 million copies in 77 languages of the Soviet Union and many foreign languages. Tens of thousands of working men and women in the capital often visit the Central Lenin Museum in groups.

In Lenin's birthplace, Ulyanovsk, employees of factories and institutions, middle school students, and university students continuously visit the house where the Ulyanov family once lived. Lectures and seminars on Lenin's life are held in enterprises, institutions, and educational institutions in the city. Under the Soviet regime, this formerly remote city has become a huge industrial and cultural center, and the people of the city are beautifying this place, which is linked to Lenin's name, with special love and care.

On the 20th, Moscow newspapers devoted much space to publishing news and articles commemorating the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death. The "Pravda" emphasized the immortality of Lenin's thought in its long essay.

The "Izvestia" editorial discussed that Soviet democracy represents the victory of Leninism.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 21st: TASS, Bucharest, January 20th: The Romanian working people widely commemorate the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death. Memorial meetings and seminars on Lenin's life are being held throughout the country. In the Dej district alone, more than 300 cultural centers and clubs held memorial meetings. Cinemas in Bucharest and other provinces are showing the documentary "Vladimir Ilyich Lenin". Newspapers in Bucharest published articles entitled "Lenin - the banner of the struggle for the victory of communism" and "Lenin's doctrine of imperialism - the guiding principle in the anti-imperialist struggle."

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 21st: TASS, The Hague: The Dutch working people commemorate the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death. A memorial meeting was held in Amsterdam on the 19th, where Hoekstra, a member of the Central Committee of the Dutch Communist Party, reported on Lenin's life and work. He emphasized that Lenin and Stalin, the leaders of the world proletariat, have pointed out the road to future happiness for all peoples. Memorial meetings are scheduled to be held in Rotterdam, The Hague, and other places on the 21st and 22nd.

### 缅怀党和社会主义国家的创造者  苏联人民隆重悼念列宁  罗荷等国广泛纪念列宁逝世廿六周年

1950-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    缅怀党和社会主义国家的创造者
    苏联人民隆重悼念列宁
    罗荷等国广泛纪念列宁逝世廿六周年
    【新华社北京二十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联人民隆重悼念列宁——布尔什维克党的创立者、世界上第一个社会主义国家的缔造者。
    在这位无产阶级革命天才逝世二十六周年的前夕，苏联的城镇与乡村都纷纷举行纪念大会。莫斯科、列宁格勒、基辅、阿尔汗吉尔斯克、伏龙芝和其他各地，都已举行了纪念大会。莫斯科列宁图书馆及首都的制造厂与工厂，都举行了展览会。展览列宁的著述及文墨等。从一九一七至一九四九年，列宁生前的著作已用苏联境内各族人民的七十七种语文及多种外国语文出版了一亿八千八百余万册。首都千千万万的劳动男女、经常成群结队地往访中央列宁博物馆。
    在列宁的出生地乌里扬诺夫斯克城，工厂和机关的员工，中、小学生和大学生，络绎不绝地往访乌里扬诺夫一家一度居住过的房子。该城企业、机关和教育机关所举行的演讲会与座谈会上，都讲述着列宁的生平事迹。在苏维埃政权下，这个原先是偏僻的城市，现在已变成巨大的工业与文化中心，该城的人民正以特别的心爱和关怀来美化与列宁的名字相连的这个地方。
    二十日，莫斯科各报都以许多篇幅来刊登纪念列宁逝世二十六周年的消息与文章。真理报在其长篇论文中着重指出列宁思想的永垂不朽。
    消息报的专文则论述苏维埃民主代表着列宁主义思想的胜利。
    【新华社北京二十一日电】塔斯社布加勒斯特二十日讯：罗马尼亚劳动人民广泛纪念列宁逝世二十六周年，全国各地正举行纪念会和列宁生平事迹的座谈会。仅第贺尔一区，就有三百余座文化之家和俱乐部举行了纪念会。布加勒斯特和各省的电影院，都放映纪录片“乌拉第米尔·伊里奇·列宁”。布加勒斯特各报刊登了题为“列宁——争取共产主义胜利的斗争旗帜”、“列宁关于帝国主义的学说——反帝国主义斗争中的指导原则”等文章。
    【新华社北京二十一日电】塔斯社海牙讯：荷兰劳动人民纪念列宁逝世二十六周年。阿姆斯特丹于十九日举行纪念会，荷兰共产党中央委员霍查克在会上报告了列宁的生平和事业。他强调说，全世界无产阶级的领袖——列宁和斯大林，已向各族人民指出了走向未来幸福的道路。鹿特丹、海牙和其他各地定于二十一日和二十二日举行纪念会。
