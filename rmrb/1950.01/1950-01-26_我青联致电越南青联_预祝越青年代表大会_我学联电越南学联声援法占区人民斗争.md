Title: All-China Youth Federation Sends Telegram to Vietnam Youth Federation to Congratulate the Vietnam Youth Congress; All-China Students' Federation Sends Telegram to Vietnam Students' Federation to Express Support for the Struggle of the People in the French-Occupied Area

Author:
Date: 1950-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 25th: The All-China Federation of Democratic Youth sent a telegram to the Vietnam Youth League, congratulating the convening of the Vietnam Youth Congress. The text is as follows:

Friends of the Vietnam Youth League:

The Chinese democratic youth warmly congratulate you on the success of the Youth Congress to be held in February.

We believe that this conference will strengthen the Vietnamese youth movement, unite the broad masses of Vietnamese youth, and carry out a victorious struggle to defeat the aggression of French imperialism, eliminate the puppet Bao Dai regime, and liberate all of Vietnam's territory.

Now that China and Vietnam have established diplomatic relations, the unity of Chinese and Vietnamese youth will be further strengthened. Let us join hands and strive for the common interests of the people of China and Vietnam and the cause of world peace and democracy!

                                    All-China Federation of Democratic Youth
                                          January 25th

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 25th: The All-China Students' Federation sent a telegram to the Vietnam National Student Union on the 24th, expressing support for the just actions of patriotic workers, shop assistants, and students in the French-occupied areas of Vietnam in resisting French imperialism and the Bao Dai puppet regime. The telegram reads as follows:

Vietnam National Student Union:

We strongly condemn the atrocities of French imperialism and its running dog, the Bao Dai puppet government, in shooting patriotic students on January 9th, and express our respect to the patriotic workers, shop assistants, and students in the French-occupied areas of Vietnam who are on strike, market strike, and class strike.

                                        All-China Students' Federation
                                    January 24, 1950

### 我青联致电越南青联  预祝越青年代表大会  我学联电越南学联声援法占区人民斗争

1950-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我青联致电越南青联
    预祝越青年代表大会
    我学联电越南学联声援法占区人民斗争
    【新华社北京二十五日电】中华全国民主青年联合总会致电越南青年联盟，祝贺越南青年代表大会的召开，文如下：越南青年联盟的朋友们：
    中国民主青年热烈庆贺你们将在二月召开的青年代表大会的成功。
    我们相信这次大会将会加强越南的青年运动，团结广大的越南青年，为击败法国帝国主义的侵略，消灭保大傀儡政权，及解放越南全部国土而进行胜利的斗争。
    现在中越两国已建立了邦交，中越青年的团结，将更加强。让我们携起手来，为中越两国人民共同利益和世界和平民主事业而奋斗！
                                    中华全国民主青年联合总会
                                          一月二十五日
    【新华社北京二十五日电】中华全国学生联合会二十四日致电越南全国学联，声援越南法占区爱国工人、店员、学生反抗法帝国主义及保大傀儡政权的正义行动，电文如下：越南全国学联：
    我们严厉谴责一月九日法帝国主义及其走狗保大傀儡政府枪杀爱国学生的暴行，并对罢工、罢市、罢课的越南法占区爱国工人、店员、学生表示敬意。
                                        中华全国学生联合会
                                    一九五○年一月二十四日
