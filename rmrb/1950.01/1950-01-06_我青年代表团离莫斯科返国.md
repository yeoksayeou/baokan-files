Title: Our Youth Delegation Departs Moscow for Home

Author:

Date: 1950-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 5th: TASS, Moscow dispatch: The Chinese Youth Delegation, led by Xiao Hua, member of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, departed Moscow for China on January 4th. During their visit to the Soviet Union, the delegation visited Kyiv, Stalingrad, Tbilisi, and Gori. Representatives from the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, and Moscow youth went to the station to see them off.

### 我青年代表团离莫斯科返国

1950-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我青年代表团离莫斯科返国
    【新华社北京五日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会委员萧华率领的中国青年代表团，于一月四日离莫斯科返中国。在访苏期间，代表团曾访问过基辅、斯大林格勒、第比利斯及哥里。赴车站送行的，有苏联共产主义青年团中央委员会、苏联青年反法西斯委员会与莫斯科青年的代表们。
