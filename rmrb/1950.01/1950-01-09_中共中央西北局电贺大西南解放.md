Title: CCP Central Committee Northwest Bureau Sends Congratulations on the Liberation of Southwest China
Author: 
Date: 1950-01-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 7th. The CCP Central Committee Northwest Bureau, the Headquarters and Political Department of the Northwest Military Region, and the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government have sent telegrams to Commanders Liu Bocheng and He Long, Political Commissar Deng Xiaoping, Deputy Political Commissar Zhang Jichun, and others, congratulating them on the victory of completely annihilating the Hu bandit troops and liberating Southwest China. The telegram reads as follows:

    Commanders Liu, He, Political Commissar Deng, Deputy Political Commissar Zhang, and all commanders and fighters under your command:
    We warmly congratulate you on the great victory of continuing to rapidly liberate Chongqing and Chengdu after liberating Guiyang and Hanzhong, completely annihilating the main force of Hu Zongnan's bandit troops, and liberating Southwest China. The entire military and civilians of the Northwest will forever thank you and continue to work hard to support your struggle to eliminate the remaining enemies and liberate Tibet.

### 中共中央西北局电贺大西南解放

1950-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央西北局
    电贺大西南解放
    【新华社西安七日电】中共中央西北局、西北军区司令部政治部、陕甘宁边区政府顷致电刘伯承、贺龙两司令员，邓小平政委、张际春副政委等庆贺全歼胡匪，解放大西南的胜利。电文如下：
    刘、贺司令员、邓政委、张副政委暨所率全体指战员同志们：
    热烈庆贺你们在解放贵阳、汉中之后，继续迅速解放重庆、成都，全歼胡宗南匪部主力，解放大西南的伟大胜利。西北全体军民将永远感谢你们，并继续努力支援你们肃清残敌和解放西藏的斗争。
