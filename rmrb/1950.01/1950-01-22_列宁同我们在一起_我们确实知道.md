Title: Lenin Is With Us - We Know It's True
Author: Xialiba
Date: 1950-01-22
Page: 5

    Lenin Is With Us - We Know It's True
    Soviet Folk Song Translated by Xialiba
    Who says Lenin is dead?
    He lives!
    In every new generation
    He lives,
    In our thriving youth
    He lives,
    In the unity of the proletariat
    He lives,
    In the victorious constitution
    He lives,
    In the revolutions of the whole world
    He lives,
    In our victorious truth
    He lives,
    In Stalin's immortal oath
    He lives,
    In the ranks of the Red Army
    He lives,
    In the wise Stalin's reports
    He lives,
    In every new day in the world
    He lives,
    In the conversations of honest travelers
    He lives,
    In the sound of bugles
    He lives,
    In every victory of Stalin
    He lives,
    In the people's good intentions
    He lives,
    In the mechanical tractor stations, in the factories
    He lives,
    In our hearts, in resounding songs
    He lives,
    Lenin is with us - we know it's true—
    He lives!
                    (Translated from Soviet Women, January 1949 issue)

    列宁同我们在一起——我们确实知道
    苏联民歌  夏里巴  译
    谁说列宁死了？
    他活着！
    在每一个新的世代里
    活着，
    在我们茁壮的青春里
    活着，
    在无产阶级的团结里
    活着，
    在胜利的宪法里
    活着，
    在全世界的革命里
    活着，
    在我们胜利的真理里
    活着，
    在斯大林的不朽宣誓里
    活着，
    在红军部队里
    活着，
    在英明的斯大林的报告里
    活着，
    在世间上每一个新日子里
    活着，
    在诚实的旅行者的谈话里
    活着，
    在号角声里
    活着，
    在每一个斯大林的胜利里
    活着，
    在人民的美善的意念里
    活着，
    在机械拖拉机站、在工厂里
    活着，
