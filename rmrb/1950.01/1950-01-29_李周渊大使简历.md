Title: Biography of Ambassador Li Zhouyuan
Author:
Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th. Ambassador Li Zhouyuan is 47 years old. He is from Hamgyong-namdo Province, Korea. He is currently a delegate to the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and a member of the Central Inspection Committee of the Workers' Party of North Korea. Ambassador Li Zhouyuan has participated in the anti-Japanese struggle since 1923, and has served as a standing executive member of the Central Committee of the New Gan Association of the Korean Anti-Japanese National United Front and an executive member of the Central Committee of the Korean Farmers' General Alliance. He was one of the leaders of the Gwangju Student Movement and the Dancheon Peasant Riot. In 1931, he was arrested and imprisoned by the Japanese rulers. After being released from prison in 1937, he continued to engage in the anti-Japanese underground struggle until the liberation of Korea in 1945. After the liberation of Korea in August 1945, he served as the chairman of the South Pyongan Provincial People's Committee, the Minister of General Affairs of the North Korean People's Committee, and the chief representative of the Korean trade delegation to Moscow.

### 李周渊大使简历

1950-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    李周渊大使简历
    【新华社北京二十八日电】李周渊大使，现年四十七岁。朝鲜咸镜南道人。现任朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议代议员。北朝鲜劳动党中央检察委员。李周渊大使自一九二三年以来即参加反日斗争，历任朝鲜反日民族统一战线新干会中央常务执行委员和朝鲜农民总同盟中央执行委员，是光州学生运动及端川农民暴动领导者之一。一九三一年被日本统治者检举入狱。一九三七年出狱后，直至一九四五年朝鲜解放前，继续作反日地下斗争。一九四五年八月朝鲜解放后，历任朝鲜平安南道人民委员会委员长，北朝鲜人民委员会总务部长，驻莫斯科朝鲜贸易使节团首席代表。
