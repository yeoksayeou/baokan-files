Title: Korean Ambassador to China, Li Zhouyuan, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-01-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 21st: The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Li Zhouyuan, along with Counselor Cui Ying, Jin Guofu, and over twenty staff members, arrived in China's capital, Beijing, at 10:00 PM today. Those who went to the station to welcome him included Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of China; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Wang Changjiang, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command; Czechoslovak Ambassador Weisskopf; Counselor Chikhvin of the Soviet Embassy; Deputy Military Attaché Lieutenant Colonel Fumin; Secretary Ji Mofei; and Xie Liejin, Acting Consul General of the Soviet Union in Beijing, and more than ten others. When Ambassador Li Zhouyuan passed through Shenyang, Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, hosted a banquet in his honor. Zhao Anbo, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Division of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Northeast People's Government, also accompanied Ambassador Li to Beijing.

### 朝鲜驻我国大使李周渊抵京

1950-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜驻我国大使李周渊抵京
    【新华社北京二十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国首任特命全权大使李周渊，偕同参事崔英、金国辅及随员等二十余人，于今日下午十时抵达我国首都北京。赴车站欢迎的有我国外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部办公厅主任王炳南，交际处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔，京津卫戍司令部副参谋长王长江，捷克大使魏斯柯普夫，苏联大使馆参事齐赫文，副武官富敏克中校，秘书纪沫费，苏联驻北京代理总领事谢列金等十余人。李周渊大使过沈阳时，东北人民政府副主席林枫曾设宴招待。东北人民政府外事局外事处副处长赵安博并陪同李大使到京。
