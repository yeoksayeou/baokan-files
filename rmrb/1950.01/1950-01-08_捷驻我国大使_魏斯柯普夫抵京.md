Title: Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Weiskopf, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-01-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th. Dr. F.C. Weiskopf, the Czechoslovak Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, and his party of twelve, accompanied by Yang Zuoqing, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Harbin Municipal People's Government, arrived in Beijing at 3:05 PM today from Manchuria by special train. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Tang Yanjie, Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command, Mr. Vasikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, Mr. Birochi, Chargé d'Affaires of the Polish Embassy, and Mr. Hegil, Principal of the Czechoslovak Embassy.

### 捷驻我国大使  魏斯柯普夫抵京

1950-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    捷驻我国大使  魏斯柯普夫抵京
    【新华社北京七日电】捷克斯洛伐克驻中华人民共和国大使魏斯柯普夫博士（Dr.F.C.Weiskopf）一行十二人由哈尔滨市人民政府外侨事务处杨佐青处长陪同于今日下午三时零五分乘专车自满洲里抵京。赴站欢迎者有我外交部章汉夫副部长，办公厅王炳南主任，交际处王倬如处长，北京市张友渔副市长，京津卫戍司令部唐延杰参谋长，苏联大使馆华司考参事，波兰大使馆毕罗奇代办，及捷克大使馆黑吉尔主事等。
