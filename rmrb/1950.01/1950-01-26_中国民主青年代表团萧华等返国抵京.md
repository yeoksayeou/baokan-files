Title: Chinese Democratic Youth Delegation Led by Xiao Hua Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-01-26
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 25th: Xiao Hua, head of the Chinese Democratic Youth Delegation attending the Second Congress of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, deputy head Han Tianshi, secretary-general Chen Jiakang, and three others arrived in Beijing today. Feng Wenbin, secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, deputy secretaries Liao Chengzhi and Jiang Nanxiang, secretary-general Rong Gaotang, Li Bo Zhao of the Municipal Cultural Committee, Wu Xue and Jin Shan, deputy directors of the Youth Art Theater, and Zhang Fan, director of education of the Central Youth League School, were at the station to welcome them. Xiao Hua told reporters: We were warmly welcomed and received by youth organizations and people of the Soviet Union and other new democratic countries during our half-year stay abroad, for which we are very grateful and extend our sincere thanks. The Chinese Democratic Youth Delegation was the first delegation to engage in activities abroad after the founding of the People's Republic of China. Due to the great victory of the Chinese revolution, the delegation enjoyed supreme honor abroad. This is the glory of the Chinese people's leader, Chairman Mao, the Chinese people, and the Chinese youth. He said: We visited Hungary, Albania, Bulgaria, Romania, Czechoslovakia, Poland, and other new democratic countries, and visited Moscow, Kiev, Stalingrad, and Tbilisi in the Soviet Union, as well as Gori, the hometown of Marshal Stalin. The main purpose of the visit was to understand the experience of youth movements in various countries and the experience of post-war recovery and economic construction, to strengthen friendly ties with youth organizations in various countries, and to introduce the struggle of Chinese youth. Xiao Hua emphasized: The Soviet Union's experience in socialist construction and the experience of the youth movement are very rich and valuable, and we should learn from the Soviet Union well.

### 中国民主青年代表团萧华等返国抵京

1950-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中国民主青年代表团萧华等返国抵京
    【新华社北京二十五日电】出席世界民主青年联盟第二次代表大会的中国民主青年代表团团长萧华、副团长韩天石、秘书长陈家康等一行五人，于今日抵达北京。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬、副书记廖承志、蒋南翔，秘书长荣高棠，市文委李伯钊，青年艺术剧院副院长吴雪、金山，中央团校教育长张凡等都到车站欢迎。萧华告诉记者说：在国外半年，备受苏联及新民主主义国家的青年团体和人民的热诚欢迎和接待，令人十分感激，谨向他们致深切的谢意。中国民主青年代表团是中华人民共和国成立后第一个在国外活动的代表团，由于中国革命的伟大的胜利，代表团在国外享受到无上荣誉，这是中国人民领袖毛主席、中国人民和中国青年的光荣。他说：在国外曾先后访问了匈牙利、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰等新民主主义国家，并参观苏联的莫斯科、基辅、斯大林格勒和梯夫里斯以及斯大林大元帅的故乡戈里等城市。参观的主要目的，是要了解各国青年运动的经验及战后恢复和建设经济事业的经验，加强与各国青年团体的友谊联系，并介绍中国青年的斗争情况。萧华着重地说：苏联社会主义的建设经验和青年运动的经验非常丰富和宝贵，我们应该很好地向苏联学习。
