Title: The Fifth Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee Heard Reports on the Situation in the Northwest and Diplomatic Issues, Approved Agreements on Postal Services Between China and North Korea, and Appointments
Author:
Date: 1950-01-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th: The Central People's Government Committee held its fifth meeting at 7:30 this afternoon, presided over by Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi. The following thirty-three people were in attendance: Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Li Lisan, Lin Boqu, He Xiangning, Peng Dehuai, Wu Yuzhang, Peng Zhen, Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Zhou Enlai, Dong Biwu, Xu Teli, Chen Yun, Ma Xulun, Guo Moruo, Shen Junru, Shen Yanbing, Chen Shutong, Situ Meitang, Li Xijiu, Huang Yanpei, Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Li Zhuchen, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Xiruo, Tan Pingshan, Zhang Nanxian, and Liu Yazi, Zhang Dongsun. Over sixty people were present as observers, including members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Vice Presidents of the Supreme People's Court, Deputy Procurators-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, members of the Government Administration Council, and leading officials from various committees, ministries, commissions, academies, banks, and administrations under its jurisdiction. The meeting heard a report on the work situation in the Northwest by Chairman Peng Dehuai of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, who arrived in Beijing from Xinjiang on the 28th of last month, and a report on diplomatic issues by Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai. The meeting approved three agreements and two additional protocols regarding postal, telegraph, and wired telephone communications between China and North Korea. The meeting approved the appointment of Zhang Weiyi as Deputy Director of the General Intelligence Department, Zhang Hanying as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Water Resources, approved the appointment of the Chairman, Vice Chairmen, and members of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, approved the appointment of Guo Renzhi as Deputy Secretary-General of the People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council, Wu Kejian as Secretary-General of the East China Military and Political Committee, Fu Zihe as a member of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, Ma Yinchu as President of Zhejiang University, and approved the resignation of Shui Zi from his position as a member of the Northwest Military and Political Committee.

### 中央人民政府委员会五次会议  听取西北情况和外交问题的报告  批准中朝通邮等协定及任命事项

1950-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府委员会五次会议
    听取西北情况和外交问题的报告 
    批准中朝通邮等协定及任命事项
    【新华社北京七日电】中央人民政府委员会今天下午七时半在刘少奇副主席主持下举行第五次会议。出席朱德、刘少奇、李济深、张澜、李立三、林伯渠、何香凝、彭德怀、吴玉章、彭真、薄一波、聂荣臻、周恩来、董必武、徐特立、陈云、马叙伦、郭沫若、沈钧儒、沈雁冰、陈叔通、司徒美堂、李锡九、黄炎培、蔡廷锴、彭泽民、李烛尘、章伯钧、张奚若、谭平山、张难先、柳亚子、张东荪等三十三人。列席者有中国人民政协全国委员会常务委员、最高人民法院副院长、最高人民检察署副署长、政务院政务委员及所属各委、部、会、院、行、署负责首长等六十余人。会议听取了上月二十八日由新疆抵京的西北军政委员会彭德怀主席关于西北工作情况的报告和外交部周恩来部长关于外交问题的报告。会议批准了中国和朝鲜间通邮、电报通讯和有线电话通讯等三个协定和两个附加议定书。会议通过任命张唯一为情报总署副署长，张含英为水利部副部长，批准任命新疆省人民政府主席、副主席和委员，批准任命郭任之为政务院人民监察委员会副秘书长，吴克坚为华东军政委员会秘书长，傅子和为青海省人民政府委员，马寅初为浙江大学校长，并批准西北军政委员会委员水梓辞职。
