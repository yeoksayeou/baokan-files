Title: The 15th Government Administration Council Approves Six Appointment Lists
Author:
Date: 1950-01-14
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 13th. The 15th meeting of the Government Administration Council approved the following six appointment lists:

    I. List of personnel for the People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council:
        Director of the General Office: Zhang Pengtu, formerly Deputy Director of the Department of Civil Affairs of the Hubei Provincial People's Government.
        Deputy Director of the Second Bureau: Lu Fu, formerly a member of the Supervisory Committee of the People's Supervisory Yuan of the North China People's Government.

    II. List of personnel for the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Government Administration Council:
        Director of the Research Office: Lu Xinyuan, formerly a Standing Committee member of the Preparatory Committee of the Returned Overseas Chinese Association.
        Deputy Director: Peng Guanghan, formerly a researcher at the Second Division of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
        Deputy Director of the Liaison Office: Chen Manyun (female), overseas Chinese affairs worker.
