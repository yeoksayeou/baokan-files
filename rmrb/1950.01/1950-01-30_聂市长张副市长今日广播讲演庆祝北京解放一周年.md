Title: Mayor Nie and Deputy Mayor Zhang to Give Radio Addresses Today Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Liberation of Beijing

Author:
Date: 1950-01-30
Page: 3

Mayor Nie and Deputy Mayor Zhang to Give Radio Addresses Today Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Liberation of Beijing

[Report from our newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station and the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a special program celebrating the first anniversary of the liberation of Beijing today from 8:20 PM to 9:00 PM: (1) Radio address by Nie Rongzhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government: "Commemorating the First Anniversary of the Liberation of Beijing"; (2) Radio address by Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government: "Municipal Construction in Beijing in the Year Since Liberation."

[Further News] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will continue to broadcast a special program for Tibetan compatriots in Chinese, Tibetan, and English languages at 10:00 PM today (30th). The content is the same as yesterday's (29th) broadcast.

[Further News] The Central People's Broadcasting Station's women's program at 5:30 PM today will introduce the women's literacy classes in the eight districts of Beijing. The natural science lecture at 6:30 PM is entitled: "Should Those Studying Natural Sciences Study Marxism-Leninism?" The first arts program at 6:00 PM will feature a new form of Quyi: Henan Zhui Zi "Three Heroes Pushing a Boat Across the River." The second arts program at 9:00 PM will feature Soviet music. At 12:45 AM: Beijing Opera: "Opposing Rongyang," "Sacrificing to the Yangtze River," "Beheading the Yellow Robe." New songs at 6:45 PM: (1) "Annihilating the Bandit Security First Brigade," (2) "Mobilization Order." Beijing Opera at 7:45 PM: "The Story of Red拂," "The Empty City Stratagem," "Lian Jin Feng."

[Further News] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station's workers' program at 12:30 PM: The draft of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions charter proposed by the preparatory committee of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions. Youth program at 1:00 PM: (1) A comprehensive report from the Beijing Youth Society, "A Year of the Capital's Youth League," (2) An article by Peng Peiyun and He Dongchang, students at Tsinghua University, "Are You Going Back to Your Hometown? Let Me Say Goodbye." Children's program at 4:30 PM: Children from the Huabei Yucai Elementary School will perform the opera "Little Red Riding Hood Escapes Danger." Music program at 5:00 PM: Chongzi Girls' High School sings new songs.

### 聂市长张副市长今日广播讲演庆祝北京解放一周年

1950-01-30
第3版()
专栏：

    聂市长张副市长今日广播讲演庆祝北京解放一周年
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台和北京市人民广播电台，今天二十点二十分到二十一点，播送庆祝北京市解放一周年的特别节目：（一）北京市人民政府聂荣臻市长广播讲演：《纪念北京市解放一周年》；（二）北京市人民政府张友渔副市长广播讲演：《北京市解放一年来的市政建设。》
    【又讯】中央人民广播电台在今（三十）日二十二点，继续用中文，藏文和英文三种语言播送对藏族同胞的特别节目。内容和昨（二十九）日播送的相同。
    【又讯】中央人民广播电台今天十七点三十分妇女时间：介绍北京市八区妇女职字班。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：《学习自然科学的人是否要学马列主义》。十八点第一次文艺节目——新曲艺：河南坠子《三勇士推船渡江》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：苏联音乐。十二点四十五分京剧：《反荣阳》、《祭长江》、《斩黄袍》。十八点四十五分新歌曲：（一）歼灭匪保安一旅、（二）动员令。十九点四十五分京剧：《红拂传》、《空城计》、《廉锦枫》。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：北京市总工会筹委会提出的北京市总工会章程草案。十三点青年节目：（一）北京青年社的一篇综合报导《首都青年团的一年》，（二）清华大学彭佩云、何东昌二位同学写的一篇文章《你要回家乡去吗，让我向你道别》。十六点三十分儿童节目：华北育才小学小朋友们演唱歌剧《小红帽脱险记》。十七点音乐节目：崇兹女中演唱新歌曲。
