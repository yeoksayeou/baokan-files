Title: The All-Union Communist Party, All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, and other organizations nominate candidates to participate in the Central Electoral Commission and request the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet to approve them
Author:
Date: 1950-01-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 17th: The Communist Party of the Soviet Union, trade unions, and other mass organizations have all held meetings to nominate candidates for the Central Election Commission, which will preside over the elections to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions nominated Osipov, the secretary of the council, as a candidate. The Moscow Committee of the All-Union Communist Party nominated Khrushchev as a candidate, the Trade Union of Workers in Political Education Institutions nominated Shkiryatov as a candidate, the Trade Union of Workers in Higher Education Institutions and Scientific Institutions nominated Vavilov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, as a candidate, and the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League nominated Mikhailov as a candidate. The organizations that nominated the candidates have requested the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to approve these candidates as members of the Central Election Commission.

### 联共、全苏工会等提候选人参加中央选举委会  请求最高苏维埃主席团批准

1950-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    联共、全苏工会等提候选人参加中央选举委会
    请求最高苏维埃主席团批准
    【新华社北京十八日电】塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：苏联共产党、工会及其他群众组织均纷纷集会，提出主持苏联最高苏维埃选举事宜的中央选举委员会的候选人。全苏工会中央理事会提名该理事会书记奥希波夫为候选人。联共莫斯科委员会提名赫鲁舍切夫为候选人，政治教育机关职工工会提名施基利亚托夫为候选人，高等学校及科学机关职工工会提名苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫为候选人，苏联共产主义青年团中央委员会提名米哈依洛夫为候选人。提出候选人的各组织，请求苏联最高苏维埃主席团批准这些候选人为中央选举委员会委员。
