Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Station Weekend Broadcast Schedule

Author:

Date: 1950-01-07
Page: 3

[This Newspaper News] The Central People's Broadcasting Station's youth time tonight (7th) at 17:30: "Several ways for the Harbin Municipal No. 2 Middle School to spend League Day." First art program at 18:00: Qinshu "Good Couple." Second art program at 21:00: National Music. Also: Today is the weekend, the station will broadcast Soviet music at 18:30: (1) Borodin, (2) Susanin's Ditties, (3) Evening Bells.

[Also News] Beijing People's Radio Station's employee program at 12:30: Singing "Support the Issuance of People's Victory Parity Unit Public Bonds." Women's program at 13:00: Women's health knowledge "Postpartum Care." Children's program at 16:30: Children from Nanxinhua Street Elementary School in the 9th District singing. New song practice at 17:00: Teaching to sing "Support the Issuance of People's Victory Parity Unit Public Bonds." Youth Service Department's Khrushchev speaks at 18:30: "How to Love the People's Motherland." Municipal Voice at 19:30: Beijing Postal Administration introduces postal parcel services.

### 中央、北京人民电台周末广播要目

1950-01-07
第3版()
专栏：

    中央、北京人民电台周末广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（七）晚十七点三十分青年时间：“哈尔滨市立二中过团日的几个方式”。十八点第一次文艺节目：琴书《好夫妻》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：国乐。又：今日为周末，该台特在十八点三十分播送苏联音乐：（一）葆罗丁诺，（二）苏三宁小曲，（三）晚钟。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：教唱“拥护发行人民胜利折实公债歌”。十三点妇女节目：妇女卫生常识《产后的护理》。十六点三十分儿童节目：九区南新华街小学小朋友们唱歌。十七点新歌练习：教唱“拥护发行人民胜利折实公债”。十八点三十分青年服务部赫鲁讲：“怎样热爱人民的祖国”。十九点三十分市政之声：京邮政管理局介绍邮政包裹业务。
