Title: Premier Zhou's Speech: No Force Can Stop China and the Soviet Union from Advancing Hand in Hand
Author:
Date: 1950-01-22
Page: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech:
    No Force Can Stop China and the Soviet Union from Advancing Hand in Hand
    Upon arriving in Moscow, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered the following speech through a loudspeaker: Dear Soviet comrades and friends:
    I have come to Moscow this time on the instructions of Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to participate in consultations on consolidating the friendly relations between China and the Soviet Union. I consider this mission a great honor. The Soviet government's consistent expression of friendship towards the Chinese people is based on Lenin and Stalin's consistent policy of supporting and assisting the liberation struggles of all oppressed peoples in the world. Now that the great revolutionary war of the Chinese people has achieved victory, the further friendship and unity between China and the Soviet Union will undoubtedly have significant implications for the cause of peace and progress in the world and the Far East. Under the guidance of Marshal Stalin's correct international policy and Chairman Mao Zedong's firm policy of alliance with the Soviet Union, no force can stop our two great nations, China and the Soviet Union, from advancing hand in hand. Let us repeat Chairman Mao's words here: Long live Sino-Soviet friendship and cooperation!

### 周总理发表演说  没有任何力量能阻止中苏两大国携手前进

1950-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理发表演说
    没有任何力量能阻止中苏两大国携手前进
    在抵达莫斯科时，周恩来总理在扩音器前发表演说如下：亲爱的苏联同志和朋友：
    我这次奉了中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席的指示，来到莫斯科，参加关于巩固中苏两大国邦交的会商。这个使命我认为是很荣幸的。苏联政府历来对于中国人民所作的各种友谊的表示，是根据列宁斯大林拥护和援助世界上一切被压迫人民解放斗争的一贯政策。现在在中国人民的伟大革命战争已经取得胜利的时候，中苏两大国家进一步的友谊与团结对于世界和远东的和平进步事业毫无疑义将有重大的意义。而在斯大林大元帅正确的国际政策与毛泽东主席的坚定的联苏政策指导之下，没有任何力量可以阻止我们中苏两大国携手前进。让我们在这里重复毛主席的话：中苏友好和合作万岁！
