Title: Malayan Guerrillas Actively Attack British Aggressor Troops
Author:
Date: 1950-01-20
Page: 4
    
    Malayan Guerrillas Actively Attack British Aggressor Troops
    [This newspaper's dispatch] According to a Xinhua dispatch: On the afternoon of December 3rd last year, Malayan guerrillas attacked a British colonial army motorcade in the area between Kelantan and Kota Bharu, killing three government troops. Due to the very active nature of the guerrillas in northern Malaya, the Thai authorities have ordered the Thai southern military police to be on high alert at all times and have mobilized military police to reinforce defenses along key routes.

### 马来亚游击队积极袭击英侵略军

1950-01-20
第4版()
专栏：

    马来亚游击队积极袭击英侵略军
    【本报讯】据新华电：马来亚游击队去年十二月三日下午，在吉连丹至哥洛之间的地区袭击英殖民军一汽车队，击毙政府军三人。由于马来亚北部游击队非常活跃，泰国当局已下令泰国南部军警应随时严密戒备，并调动军警前往各要道增防。
