    Tianma Painting
    Wei Ran Poem
    A living person chairs a meeting at the Security Council, while a dead ghost presides,
    "Security Council," what utter nonsense!
    Quickly carry away this skeleton!
    Let him represent his underworld,
    Don't come to the human world and spout ghostly nonsense!

专栏：

    天马画
    未冉诗
    安理会上活人开会，死鬼作主席，
    “安理会”，岂有此理？
    快快抬开这一架骷髅吧！
    让他代表他的阴曹地府去，
    不要跑到人世说鬼话！
