Title: Our Qiongyai Army Liberates Tunchang City; Two Attacks Annihilate Over Two Hundred Bandits
Author:
Date: 1950-01-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Qiongyai, 9th: The Qiongyai People's Liberation Army liberated Tunchang City, an important stronghold of the remnant bandits in Xinmin County (newly established county, formerly parts of Qiongshan and Chengmai), on the 4th of this month. The entire bandit Qiongshan Security Third Battalion headquarters and its subordinate Second Company, as well as four puppet township offices and their affiliated self-defense forces, which relied on strong fortifications to resist stubbornly, were completely annihilated. Our army took advantage of the victory to remove the Datong bandit stronghold near Tunchang, annihilating a portion of the bandits. On the northern Qiong area, our army entered Sanjiang City, Qiongshan County, on the 29th of last month, completely annihilating the bandit Sanjiang and Xinling puppet township offices and their affiliated self-defense forces stationed in the city. In the aforementioned battles, our army captured 198 officers and soldiers, including Cai Pei, the company commander of the Second Company of the Qiongshan Security Third Battalion, and wounded 32 officers and soldiers, including Zhen Heling, the battalion commander of the Qiongshan Security Third Battalion. It seized 11 various machine guns, 2 grenade launchers, and 234 long and short guns. There were many other military supplies.

### 琼崖我军解放屯昌市  两次出击歼匪二百余

1950-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    琼崖我军解放屯昌市
    两次出击歼匪二百余
    【新华社琼崖九日电】琼崖人民解放军本月四日解放新民县属（新设县，原琼山、澄迈各一部）残匪重要据点屯昌市，全歼凭借强固工事顽抗之匪琼山保安第三营营部及其所属第二连，和四个伪乡公所及所属自卫队。我军乘胜拔除屯昌附近之大同匪据点，歼匪一部。琼北方面我军于上月二十九日，攻入琼山县三江市，全歼盘据该市之匪三江、新陵两个伪乡公所及其所属自卫队。在上列各次战斗中，我军俘匪琼山保安第三营第二连连长蔡培等以下官兵一百九十八名，伤匪琼山保安第三营营长甄鹤龄以下官兵三十二名；缴获各式机枪十一挺，掷弹筒两个，长短枪二百三十四枝。其他军用物资甚多。
