Title: TASS Refutes Fabrications by French and Swiss Newspapers
Author: 
Date: 1950-01-26
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th: TASS, Moscow, 23rd: In view of the provocative reports recently published by French and Swiss newspapers, alleging that the Soviet Union is conducting "trade" with Franco's Spain through certain countries, and that the Soviet Union has engaged in so-called "direct trade negotiations" with Spain, etc., TASS is authorized to state the following: All such reports are inconsistent with the facts. These are all fabrications by scoundrels and swindlers.

### 塔斯社斥法瑞报纸造谣

1950-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    塔斯社斥法瑞报纸造谣
    【新华社北京二十四日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十三日讯：鉴于法国和瑞士报纸最近发表的挑拨性报道，指称苏联正通过某些国家与佛朗哥西班牙进行“贸易”，并且苏联已经和西班牙进行所谓“直接的贸易谈判”等等，塔斯社奉命声明如下：所有这类报道均与事实不符。这些全是无赖和骗子们的捏造。
