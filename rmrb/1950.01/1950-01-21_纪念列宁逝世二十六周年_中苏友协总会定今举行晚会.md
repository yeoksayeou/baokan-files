Title: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold Evening Party Today to Commemorate the 26th Anniversary of Lenin's Death
Author:
Date: 1950-01-21
Page: 1

    Commemorating the 26th Anniversary of Lenin's Death
    The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold Evening Party Today
    [Beijing News] The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will hold a lecture and film evening party in the auditorium of the Sino-French University tonight (the 21st) to commemorate the 26th anniversary of the death of the great revolutionary teacher Lenin. Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, will give a lecture on Lenin's life, and the film "The Man with a Gun" will be shown.
    [Further News] The Beijing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, in order to commemorate the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death, has organized sixteen propagandists from the Central Youth League School, who are scheduled to go to various cinemas in Beijing tonight to talk about Lenin's life before the film screenings.
    [Shenyang News] The Shenyang Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is widely carrying out commemorative activities. All branches and sub-branches will hold meetings and invite experts to make special reports on Lenin's life, career, revolutionary activities, and contributions to the world proletariat, especially his help to the Chinese revolution. The City Friendship Association is scheduled to hold a commemorative evening party today.

### 纪念列宁逝世二十六周年  中苏友协总会定今举行晚会

1950-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念列宁逝世二十六周年
    中苏友协总会定今举行晚会
    【北京讯】中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友协分会为了纪念伟大的革命导师列宁逝世二十六周年，将于今（二十一日）晚在中法大学礼堂举行演讲电影晚会。会上由中苏友好协会总干事钱俊瑞主讲列宁生平，并放映电影《带枪的人》。
    【又讯】中苏友好协会北京市分会为纪念列宁逝世二十六周年，组织了中央团校十六名宣传员，定今晚到京市各影院，在电影放映前讲述列宁生平。
    【沈阳讯】沈阳市中苏友好协会，广泛展开纪念活动。各支会、支分会将召开大会，并请专人作有关列宁生平事业、革命活动及对世界无产阶级的贡献，特别是对中国革命的帮助的专题报告，市友协定今日举行纪念晚会。
