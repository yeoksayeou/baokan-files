Title: Uzbek People Celebrate National Day, Tashkent Holds Grand Military Parade, Kaganovich and Others Participate in Review
Author:
Date: 1950-01-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th. TASS, Tashkent: A grand military parade and a march of 350,000 people were held in Tashkent, the capital of the Uzbek Republic, on the 17th to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the republic's founding. The parade took place on Red Square in Tashkent. Participants included Kaganovich, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, as well as representatives of various fraternal republics. Kaganovich, who personally assisted in the construction and development of the Uzbek Republic, was greeted with stormy applause in Red Square.

### 乌兹贝克人民庆祝国庆  塔什干盛大阅兵  卡冈诺维奇等参加检阅

1950-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    乌兹贝克人民庆祝国庆
    塔什干盛大阅兵
    卡冈诺维奇等参加检阅
    【新华社北京十八日电】据塔斯社塔什干讯：乌兹贝克共和国首都塔什干于十七日举行盛大阅兵典礼与三十五万人的大游行，庆祝该共和国成立二十五周年纪念。阅兵系在塔什干的红场举行。参加者有联共中央政治局委员、苏联部长会议副主席卡冈诺维奇及各兄弟共和国的代表。卡冈诺维奇曾亲自帮助乌兹贝克共和国的建设和发展，他在红场上受到暴风雨般的欢呼。
