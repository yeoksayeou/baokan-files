Title: Soviet Union Appoints Beria as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1950-01-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 25th: TASS Moscow - The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed Mikhail Beria as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and relieved him of his position as Minister of the Chemical Industry; Sergei Tikhomirov has been appointed Minister of the Chemical Industry; Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Malyshev, has been appointed Minister of the Shipbuilding Industry, and Ivan Nosenko as First Deputy Minister; Yuri Maksarev has been appointed Minister of Transport Machine Building.

### 苏联任命别尔乌辛为部长会议副主席

1950-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联任命别尔乌辛为部长会议副主席
    【新华社北京二十五日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命米哈伊尔·别尔乌辛为部长会议副主席，并解除其化学工业部部长职；任命塞尔盎·提贺米尔罗夫为化学工业部部长；任命部长会议副主席马利雪夫为造船工业部部长，伊凡·诺森科为第一副部长；任命尤里·马克萨列夫为运输机器制造部部长。
