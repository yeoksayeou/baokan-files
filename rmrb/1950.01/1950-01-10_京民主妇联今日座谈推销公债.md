Title: Beijing Democratic Women's Federation to Hold Symposium Today to Promote Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1950-01-10
Page: 1

    Beijing Democratic Women's Federation to Hold Symposium Today to Promote Public Bonds
    [Beijing News] The Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, in order to call on women from all walks of life in the city to respond to the purchase of the People's Victory Indexed Public Bonds, is scheduled to hold a symposium at 2:00 pm today (the 10th) in the auditorium of the European and American Alumni Association on Nanheyan Street. Those invited to attend include people from the city's industrial and commercial and educational circles, as well as housewives, workers, and students from all walks of life. The meeting will discuss the significance of the government issuing Victory Public Bonds and the specific methods for women in Beijing to publicize and promote Victory Public Bonds.

### 京民主妇联今日座谈推销公债

1950-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    京民主妇联今日座谈推销公债
    【北京讯】本市民主妇女联合会，为号召本市各阶层妇女响应购买人民胜利折实公债，定于今（十）日下午二时在南河沿欧美同学会礼堂举行座谈会。被邀出席的有本市工商、教育界人士及各阶层家庭妇女、工人、学生等。会上将讨论政府发行胜利公债的意义及京市妇女宣传和推销胜利公债的具体办法。
