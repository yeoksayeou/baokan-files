Title: Reactionary Authorities in Hyderabad, India, Forced to Postpone Murder of Peasant Leaders
Author:
Date: 1950-01-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th — TASS, London, 24th: Under the pressure of world public opinion, the authorities of Hyderabad Province, India, have had to postpone the execution of the death sentences on peasant leaders. The execution was originally scheduled for the 22nd. The peasant leaders will now be tried by the "Hyderabad Judicial Committee," which is composed entirely of large landowners.

### 印度海德拉巴反动当局被迫延缓杀害农民领袖

1950-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    印度海德拉巴反动当局被迫延缓杀害农民领袖
    【新华社北京二十六日电】电通社伦敦二十四日讯：在世界舆论的压力下，印度海德拉巴省当局，不得不延缓执行对于农民领袖死刑的判决。该判决原定二十二日执行。各农民领袖现将受“海德拉巴司法委员会”的审判，该委员会完全由大地主所组成。
