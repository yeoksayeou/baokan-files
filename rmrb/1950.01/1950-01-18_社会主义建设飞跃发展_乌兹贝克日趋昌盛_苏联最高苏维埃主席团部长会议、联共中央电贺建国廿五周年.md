    Socialist Construction Leaps Forward
    Uzbekistan Flourishes
    USSR Supreme Soviet Presidium, Council of Ministers, and the CPSU Central Committee Send Congratulations on the 25th Anniversary of the Founding of the Republic
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th] According to a TASS report from Moscow on the 16th: January 17th marks the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Uzbek Republic. The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, the Council of Ministers, and the Central Committee of the CPSU have all sent congratulatory messages.

    On the eve of the anniversary, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Uzbek Republic, Abdurakhmanov, told a TASS correspondent: Before the October Revolution, the Uzbek people did not have their own national state; they were divided and lived unhappy lives. The October Revolution liberated all nationalities from the exploitation of Tsarist Russia and guided the Uzbek people onto the road of political, economic, and cultural progress. Within a quarter of a century, Uzbekistan has become a prosperous socialist republic, enjoying equal status with all republics within the powerful Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.

### 社会主义建设飞跃发展  乌兹贝克日趋昌盛  苏联最高苏维埃主席团部长会议、联共中央电贺建国廿五周年

1950-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    社会主义建设飞跃发展
    乌兹贝克日趋昌盛
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团部长会议、联共中央电贺建国廿五周年
    【新华社北京十七日电】据塔斯社莫斯科十六日讯：一月十七日，是乌兹贝克共和国成立二十五周年纪念。苏联最高苏维埃主席团，部长会议和联共中央，均曾致电庆贺。
    纪念日前夕，乌兹贝克共和国部长会议主席阿布杜拉赫马诺夫特对塔斯社记者发表谈话称：在十月革命以前，乌兹贝克人民没有本民族的国家，他们被四分五裂，过着不幸的生活。十月革命从沙俄的剥削中解放了一切民族，把乌兹贝克人民引导到政治、经济和文化进步的大道。乌兹贝克在四分之一的世纪内变成了繁昌的社会主义共和国，在强大的苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟之中与各共和国同处于平等地位。
