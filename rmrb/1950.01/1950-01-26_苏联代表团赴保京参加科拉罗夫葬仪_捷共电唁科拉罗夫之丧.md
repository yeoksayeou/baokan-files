Title: Soviet Delegation Travels to Bulgarian Capital to Attend Kolarov's Funeral; Czechoslovak Communist Party Sends Condolences on Kolarov's Death
Author:
Date: 1950-01-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 25th: TASS, Moscow: A Soviet government delegation, headed by Voroshilov, left for Sofia on the 24th to attend the funeral of Vasil Kolarov, an outstanding leader of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 25th: TASS, Prague, 23rd: The Czechoslovak people deeply mourn the death of Bulgarian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Kolarov. Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, is flying flags at half-mast, with black borders. The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party sent a telegram of condolence to the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, stating: "Our party, the working class, and the working people, together with you, are saddened by the death of Kolarov, the great fighter and leader of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the People's Republic of Bulgaria. He was a great friend of the Czechoslovak people. The heavy loss you have suffered is also a loss to the entire international labor movement."

### 苏联代表团赴保京参加科拉罗夫葬仪  捷共电唁科拉罗夫之丧

1950-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团赴保京参加科拉罗夫葬仪
    捷共电唁科拉罗夫之丧
    【新华社北京二十五日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：以伏罗希洛夫为首的苏联政府代表团，于二十四日前赴索非亚，参加保加利亚共产党卓越领袖、保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席华西尔·科拉罗夫的葬礼。
    【新华社北京二十五日电】塔斯社布拉格二十三日讯：捷克斯洛伐克人民对保加利亚部长会议主席科拉罗夫的逝世，深致哀悼。捷京布拉格下半旗志哀，旗帜镶以黑边。捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会电唁保加利亚共产党中央委员会称：“我们的党、工人阶级与劳动人民，同你们一起，对保加利亚共产党与保加利亚人民共和国的伟大战士与领袖科拉罗夫的逝世，感到悲痛。他是捷克斯洛伐克人民的伟大朋友。你们所遭受的沉重损失，也是整个国际劳工运动的损失”。
