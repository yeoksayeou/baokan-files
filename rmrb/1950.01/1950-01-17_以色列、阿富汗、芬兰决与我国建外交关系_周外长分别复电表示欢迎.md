Title: Israel, Afghanistan, and Finland Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations with Our Country; Foreign Minister Zhou Sends Separate Telegrams of Welcome
Author:
Date: 1950-01-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 16th:
    Israeli Minister of Foreign Affairs Moshe Sharett, Afghan Minister of Foreign Affairs Ali Mohammed, and Finnish Minister of Foreign Affairs Carl Enckell, on behalf of their respective governments, telegraphed Minister Zhou Enlai of our country's Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the 9th, 12th, and 13th of this month, expressing their decision to establish diplomatic relations with our country and extending their congratulations. Minister Zhou has sent separate replies, welcoming the decisions of the respective governments on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and expressing his gratitude for their congratulations.

### 以色列、阿富汗、芬兰决与我国建外交关系  周外长分别复电表示欢迎

1950-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    以色列、阿富汗、芬兰决与我国建外交关系
    周外长分别复电表示欢迎
    【新华社北京十六日电】以色列政府外交部部长摩西·萨尔特，阿富汗政府外交部部长阿里·穆罕莫德与芬兰政府外交部部长卡尔·恩开尔，先后于本月九日，十二日，十三日代表各该国政府致电我国外交部周恩来部长，表示决定与我国建立外交关系，并致祝贺，周部长已分别复电，对各该政府的决定，代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府表示欢迎，对他们的祝贺表示谢意。
