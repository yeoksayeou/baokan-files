Title: Qiongya Our Army Wins in Attack; Remnants of Bandit Troops Surrender En Masse
Author:
Date: 1950-01-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Qiongya, January 24th: A unit of the Qiongya People's Liberation Army attacked the bandit stronghold of Qixingpo in Shude Township, Qiongya County, on the 5th of this month, annihilating the entire Shude Township Public Office and "Self-Defense Force." They seized three machine guns, two grenade launchers, 121 rifles and other military supplies.

    Furthermore, on December 30th of last year, the people's local armed forces of Wenchang County routed bandit troops who were looting grain near Chongxing City, killing, wounding, and capturing 12 bandits, including a platoon leader.

    Xinhua News Agency, Qiongya, January 24th: Officers and soldiers of the Kuomintang remnant bandit troops in Qiongya are flocking to the People's Liberation Army to surrender. In the past month, entire units of bandit regular troops defecting have included a platoon and a squad from the 765th Regiment of the 255th Division of the 32nd Army, a squad from the 798th Regiment of the 266th Division, two squads from the 764th Regiment of the 252nd Division, and a squad from the 477th Regiment of the 159th Division of the 64th Army. More than 30 others have surrendered individually. These personnel brought with them nine various machine guns, one carbine, one grenade launcher, two rifle grenades, and 58 rifles.

### 琼崖我军出击获胜  残余匪军官兵纷纷投诚

1950-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    琼崖我军出击获胜
    残余匪军官兵纷纷投诚
    【新华社琼崖二十四日电】琼崖人民解放军一部本月五日攻击琼崖县树德乡七星坡匪据点，歼匪树德乡乡公所及“自卫队”全部，缴获机枪三挺，掷弹筒二个，长短枪一百二十一枝，其他军用品一部。
    又文昌县人民地方武装，于去年十二月三十日，在重兴市附近，击溃抢粮匪军，毙伤俘匪排长以下十二名。
    【新华社琼崖二十四日电】琼崖国民党残余匪军官兵，纷纷投奔人民解放军。近一月来，匪正规军集体投奔的，计有三十二军二五五师七六五团一个排又一个班，二六六师七九八团一个班，二五二师七六四团两个班，六十四军一五九师四七七团一个班。其他零星个别投诚者，亦有三十余人。以上人员并带来各式机枪九挺，卡宾枪一枝，掷弹筒一个，枪榴筒二个，长短枪五十八枝。
