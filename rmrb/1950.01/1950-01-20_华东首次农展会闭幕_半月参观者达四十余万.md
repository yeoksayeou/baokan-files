Title: East China's First Agricultural Exhibition Closes; Over 400,000 Visitors in Half a Month
Author:
Date: 1950-01-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 19th. The first Agricultural Exhibition of the East China Region, held in Shanghai, closed on the 15th after lasting half a month. At the closing ceremony, Li Renfeng, Deputy Director of the Department of Agriculture, Forestry and Water Conservancy of the East China Financial and Economic Committee, gave a speech, in which he requested the labor heroes and agricultural science workers attending the meeting to bring the achievements of the exhibition to the masses, so as to promote the completion of the East China region's 1950 agricultural production increase plan. According to reports, during the exhibition period, visitors were extremely enthusiastic, with a total of more than 420,000 people visiting in half a month. The Beijing and Shanghai film studios have already filmed the exhibition into a documentary newsreel.

### 华东首次农展会闭幕  半月参观者达四十余万

1950-01-20
第2版()
专栏：

    华东首次农展会闭幕
    半月参观者达四十余万
    【新华社上海十九日电】在上海举行的华东区第一次农业展览会，历时半月，已于十五日闭幕。在闭幕时，华东区财经委员会农林水利部副部长李人凤出席讲话，他要求各地与会劳动英雄、农业科学工作者把展览会的成果带到群众中去，以推动各地完成华东区一九五○年的农业增产计划。按此次展览会在展览期间，参观者极为踊跃，半月来共达四十二万余人。北京、上海两电影制片厂已将展览会情况拍摄成纪录新闻片。
