Title: Circular from the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government on Exempting Public Debt Interest Income Tax
Author:
Date: 1950-01-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th—In order to encourage the people to actively purchase public bonds, the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government has issued a circular to the Finance Departments and Tax Bureaus of all major administrative regions and the Finance Bureaus and Tax Bureaus of all municipalities directly under the Central Government, exempting public bond interest (including interest subsidies) from income tax. It also ordered the subordinate finance authorities at all levels to implement it accordingly.

### 中央人民政府财政部通令免征公债利息所得税

1950-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府财政部
    通令免征公债利息所得税
    【新华社北京十二日电】中央人民政府财政部为鼓励人民踊跃认购公债，特通令全国各大行政区财政部、税务局及直辖各省市财政厅、局和税务局，一律免征公债利息（包括贴息）所得税，并饬转令各级财政机关一体运行。
