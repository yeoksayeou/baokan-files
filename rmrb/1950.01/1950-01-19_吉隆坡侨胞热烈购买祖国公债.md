Title: Kuala Lumpur Overseas Chinese Enthusiastically Purchase National Bonds

Author:
Date: 1950-01-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 18th: Hong Kong news: Overseas Chinese in Kuala Lumpur launched a movement to purchase national people's bonds at a banquet held at the Huizhou Association on the 10th of this month. Overseas Chinese Huang Tianyuan immediately subscribed 2,000 Straits dollars (approximately 700 shares of People's Victory Discount Bonds), which was enthusiastically responded to by the overseas Chinese present.

### 吉隆坡侨胞热烈购买祖国公债

1950-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    吉隆坡侨胞热烈购买祖国公债
    【新华社广州十八日电】香港消息：吉隆坡侨胞本月十日在惠州会馆举行的宴会上，发起购买祖国人民公债运动。侨胞黄天远当场认购叻币二千元（约合人民胜利折实公债七百余分），得到与会侨胞的热烈响应。
