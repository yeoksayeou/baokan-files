Title: Direct Shanghai-Guangzhou Train Service Officially Begins Passenger and Freight Operations; Guilin to Liuzhou Section of the Xiang-Gui-Qian Railway Opens to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1950-01-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 14th:
    Following the successful trial run of the direct train between Shanghai and Guangzhou, passenger and freight services officially began on January 9th. The direct Shanghai-Guangzhou train runs twice a week in both directions, departing from Shanghai on Mondays and Thursdays, and departing from Guangzhou on Tuesdays and Fridays.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 14th:
    The Guilin-Liuzhou section of the Xiang-Gui-Qian Railway was repaired and opened to traffic on the 9th of this month. Now, from Hengyang, via Guilin and Liuzhou, it is possible to reach Baipeng, and west of Liuzhou, it is possible to reach Celing near Nandan. Of the 1,077-kilometer-long Xiang-Gui-Qian Railway, 777 kilometers are now open to traffic.

### 上海广州间直达车正式办理客货运营业  湘桂黔路桂林到柳州通车

1950-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    上海广州间直达车正式办理客货运营业
    湘桂黔路桂林到柳州通车
    【新华社北京十四日电】上海、广州间直达车试车成功后，已于一月九日正式办理客货运营业。上海、广州直达车每星期对开两次，星期一、四从上海开出，星期二、五从广州开出。
    【新华社北京十四日电】湘桂黔铁路桂林、柳州段已于本月九日修复通车。现在由衡阳经桂林、柳州可通至百朋，柳州以西可通至南丹附近的侧岭。全长一千零七十七公里的湘桂黔路，已有七百七十七公里通车。
