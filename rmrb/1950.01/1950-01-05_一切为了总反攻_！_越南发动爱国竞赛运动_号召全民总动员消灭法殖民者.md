Title: "Everything for the General Counter-Offensive"! Vietnam Launches Patriotic Emulation Campaign, Calls for National Mobilization to Eliminate French Colonialists
Author:
Date: 1950-01-05
Page: 4

    "Everything for the General Counter-Offensive"!
    Vietnam Launches Patriotic Emulation Campaign
    Calls for National Mobilization to Eliminate French Colonialists
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 31st] Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, December 31st: The Central Committee for the Promotion of Patriotic Emulation in Vietnam will launch another patriotic emulation campaign in the first few weeks of 1950. The general slogan of this movement is "Everything for the General Counter-Offensive." In this movement, the Vietnamese people will realize four historical tasks: 1. The Vietnamese people will carry out general mobilization to prepare for the final offensive against French colonialism; 2. Make every effort to step up agricultural production; 3. Completely eliminate illiteracy in Vietnam; 4. The entire Vietnamese people will accumulate material and financial resources for the counter-offensive.
    Official sources point out that this new patriotic emulation campaign is underway throughout Vietnam, and 1950 will see the victory of the Vietnamese people over foreign aggression, famine and illiteracy, so that national construction work can begin on a solid foundation.

### “一切为了总反攻”！  越南发动爱国竞赛运动  号召全民总动员消灭法殖民者

1950-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    “一切为了总反攻”！
    越南发动爱国竞赛运动
    号召全民总动员消灭法殖民者
    【新华社北京四日电】越南通讯社北越十二月三十一日讯：越南促进爱国竞赛运动中央委员会，将在一九五○年头几个星期中发动另一次爱国竞赛运动。这一运动的总口号是“一切为了总反攻”。越南人民将在这个运动中实现四项历史任务：一、越南人民进行总动员，以准备最后进攻法国殖民主义者；二、全力加紧农业生产；三、完全消灭越南文盲；四、全越南人民积蓄物资与财政资源，以便反攻。
    官方人士指出，这个新的爱国竞赛运动正在全越进行中，一九五○年将看到越南人民战胜外国侵略、饥馑和文盲的胜利，国民建设工作因而可能在稳固的基础上开始。
