Title: General Administration of Customs Revises the Names of Various Customs Offices
Author: 
Date: 1950-01-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th - The General Administration of Customs of the Central People's Government has recently revised the names of all customs offices and their subordinate branch offices nationwide. It is stipulated that the names of the customs offices should be based on the current local names of their locations. Customs offices located on the coast are called "海关" (Hai Guan, Sea Customs), while customs offices located on land borders are simply called "关" (Guan, Customs). Previously, the names of customs offices nationwide were inconsistent, with some named after provinces and regions, some named after feudal prefectures, and some named after the cities where they were located. Some land border customs offices were also called "海关" (Hai Guan, Sea Customs), which was inappropriate. The revised names of the customs offices are listed below:

### 海关总署改订各关名称

1950-01-21
第2版()
专栏：

    海关总署改订各关名称
    【新华社北京二十日电】中央人民政府海关总署顷改订全国各关及所属各分支关名称，规定均以驻在地的现地名为关名，沿海之关称为海关，陆地边境之关则单称为关。按过去全国各关名称不统一，有以省区为名，有以封建州府为名，有以驻在地的城市为名，有些陆地边境的关也称海关，皆属不当。兹开列各关改订名称如下：
