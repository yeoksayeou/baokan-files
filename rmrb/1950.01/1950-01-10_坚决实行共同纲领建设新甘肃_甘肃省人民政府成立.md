Title: Resolutely Implement the Common Program and Build a New Gansu: The Establishment of the Gansu Provincial People's Government

Author: 
Date: 1950-01-10
Page: 1

    Resolutely Implement the Common Program and Build a New Gansu
    The Establishment of the Gansu Provincial People's Government
    [Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 9th] The Gansu Provincial People's Government was formally established on the 8th. At the inauguration ceremony, Chairman Deng Baoshan stated in his speech that the Gansu Provincial People's Government will resolutely implement the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, strengthen unity and mutual assistance among all ethnic groups, and strive to build a new Gansu for the people. Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, Gan Siqi, Director of the Political Department of the First Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, and Zhao Shoukong of the Gansu Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party also attended and spoke.

### 坚决实行共同纲领建设新甘肃  甘肃省人民政府成立

1950-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    坚决实行共同纲领建设新甘肃
    甘肃省人民政府成立
    【新华社兰州九日电】甘肃省人民政府已于八日正式成立。在成立典礼中，邓宝珊主席致词称，甘肃省人民政府将坚决实行中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领，加强各民族团结互助，为建设人民的新甘肃而奋斗。西北军政委员会副主席张治中，人民解放军第一野战军政治部主任甘泗淇，中共甘肃省委赵守攻亦均出席讲话。
