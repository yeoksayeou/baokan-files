Title: Studying Lenin: Letter to the Soviet "Worker's Newspaper" on the Anniversary of Lenin's Death
Author: Sīdálín (Stalin)
Date: 1950-01-21
Page: 4

    Studying Lenin
    Letter to the Soviet "Worker's Newspaper" on the Anniversary of Lenin's Death
    Stalin
    Remember, love, and study our teacher, our leader Ilyich.
    Oppose and defeat enemies at home and abroad as Ilyich did.
    Build a new life, new customs, and a new culture as Ilyich did.
    In your work, never reject small things, because great things are made up of small things—this is one of Ilyich's important legacies.

### 学习列宁  在纪念列宁逝世一周年时给苏联《工人报》的信
斯大林
1950-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    学习列宁
    在纪念列宁逝世一周年时给苏联《工人报》的信
    斯大林
    要记着，要爱戴、要学习我们的导师，我们的领袖伊里奇。
    要照伊里奇那样去反对、去战胜国内国外的敌人。
    要照伊里奇那样去建设新生活、新风俗和新文化。
    在工作中无论何时都不要拒绝小事情，因为大事是由小事积成的，——这是伊里奇的重要遗训之一。
