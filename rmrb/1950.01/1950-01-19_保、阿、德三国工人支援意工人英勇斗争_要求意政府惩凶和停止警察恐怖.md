Title: Workers in Bulgaria, Albania, and Germany Support Italian Workers' Valiant Struggle, Demanding the Italian Government Punish the Culprits and Stop Police Terror
Author:
Date: 1950-01-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th: Following workers in Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania and other countries, workers in Bulgaria, Albania, and the German Democratic Republic have successively voiced their support for Italian workers, protesting the Gasperi government's massacre of workers in Modena. According to reports from TASS and Telepress: The Central Committee of the Bulgarian General Trade Union sent a protest telegram demanding an immediate end to police terror and punishment of the murderers. The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Anti-Fascist Fighters League also sent a telegram to Italian President Einaudi expressing their protest. The Albanian Trade Union sent a telegram to the Italian General Confederation of Labor, expressing its full support for the heroic struggle of the Italian workers. The Miners' Union of Democratic Germany, representing 200,000 miners, pointed out to workers all over the world in its protest letter that the terror events in Italy "are directed by the main enemy of the international working class – American monopoly capital." The 165,000 organized metalworkers in Saxony-Anhalt, in their protest declaration, promised to stand with the World Federation of Trade Unions and support the struggle of the Italian workers with all means. The Executive Committee of the Berlin Railway Workers' Union, representing 200,000 members, sent a telegram to Italian President Einaudi expressing their protest.

### 保、阿、德三国工人支援意工人英勇斗争  要求意政府惩凶和停止警察恐怖

1950-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    保、阿、德三国工人支援意工人英勇斗争
    要求意政府惩凶和停止警察恐怖
    【新华社北京十八日电】继波、捷、罗等国工人之后，保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚和德意志民主共和国的工人，又相继声援意大利工人，抗议加斯贝利政府屠杀摩登纳工人的暴行。据塔斯社与电通社报道：保加利亚总工会中央委员会发出抗议电报，要求立即停止警察恐怖，并惩办凶手。保加利亚反法西斯战士联盟中央委员会，亦致电意总统伊诺第表示抗议。阿尔巴尼亚工会致电意大利总工会，表示完全支援意大利工人的英勇斗争。民主德国矿工工会代表二十万矿工在其抗议书中向全世界工人指出：意大利的恐怖事件，“是由国际工人阶级的主要敌人——美国独占资本所导演的”。萨克森——安哈特州十六万五千有组织的金属工人，在其抗议宣言中允诺与世界工联在一起，以一切手段支持意大利工人的斗争。柏林铁路工人工会执行委员会代表二十万会员，致电意总统伊诺第表示抗议。
