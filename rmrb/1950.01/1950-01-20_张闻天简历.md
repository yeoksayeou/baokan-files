Title: Zhang Wentian's Biography
Author:
Date: 1950-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 19th: Zhang Wentian, who was appointed by the Central People's Government today as the chief representative of the People's Republic of China to attend meetings of the United Nations and its Security Council and participate in its work, is 50 years old and a native of Nanhui County, Jiangsu Province. He is currently a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, a member of the Politburo of the Central Committee, a standing committee member of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and a member of the Northeast People's Government. Zhang Wentian joined the Communist Party of China in 1925. After 1931, he participated in the work of the central leading organs of the Party. During the famous 25,000-li Long March led by the CPC Central Committee leading the Chinese People's Revolutionary Army from the winter of 1934 to the winter of 1935, Zhang Wentian was a member of the leadership organization of this Long March. He was re-elected as a member of the Central Committee at the Seventh National Congress of the Communist Party of China in 1945.

### 张闻天简历

1950-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    张闻天简历
    【新华社北京十九日电】今日为中央人民政府任命为中华人民共和国出席联合国及其安全理事会会议并参加其工作的代表团首席代表张闻天，现年五十岁，江苏省南汇县人。现任中国共产党中央委员和中央政治局委员，中共中央东北局常委，和东北人民政府委员。张闻天于一九二五年参加中国共产党。一九三一年以后，参加党中央领导机关的工作。在一九三四年冬至一九三五年冬中共中央领导中国人民革命军队举行著名的二万五千里长征时，张闻天是这一长征的领导组织中的一员。一九四五年中共第七次代表大会上继续当选为中央委员。
