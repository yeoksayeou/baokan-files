Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Notifies on Promoting the Victory Bonds

Author:
Date: 1950-01-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 31st: The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League's Notification Regarding Promoting the People's Victory Indexed Bonds (December 21, 1949):

    "The People's Victory Indexed Bonds recently decided to be issued by the Central People's Government are one of the powerful weapons to overcome the current national financial difficulties, strive for price stability, and consolidate the national victory.
    All members of the Youth League and patriotic youths should support this measure. During the bond issuance period, Youth League members should actively conduct publicity and persuasion to purchase bonds among the broad masses of youth and the people. They should also carry out this work through their families, relatives, and friends. Youth League members with the ability to purchase bonds should purchase them individually or collectively. The Youth League committees of all cities should regard the publicity of bond purchases as one of the central items of current publicity work, should compile popular publicity materials on persuading people to purchase bonds, and should compose and perform yangko dances, short plays, and clapperdoge, etc. on promoting bonds. The Youth League publications of all cities should publish articles publicizing the significance of bonds and reports on bond subscription."

### 青年团中央委员会通知推行胜利公债

1950-01-01
第2版()
专栏：

    青年团中央委员会通知推行胜利公债
    【新华社北京三十一日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会关于推行人民胜利折实公债的通知（一九四九年十二月二十一日）：
    “最近中央人民政府所决定发行的人民胜利折实公债，是战胜当前国家财政困难，争取物价稳定，巩固全国胜利的有力武器之一。
    全体青年团员和爱国青年们都应当拥护这一措施。在公债发行期间，青年团员应在广大青年群众和人民群众中积极地进行关于公债的宣传和劝购。并应通过自己的家庭、亲友等关系进行这一工作。凡有购买公债能力的青年团员，应当单独地或集体地购买。各城市团委应当将购买公债的宣传作为当前宣传工作的重心项目之一，应当编印关于劝购公债的通俗宣传品，并编演关于推行公债的秧歌、活报、快板等。各城市的团刊，应登载宣传公债意义和认购公债情况的文告。”
