Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Decides to Elect the Supreme Soviet on March 12

Author:
Date: 1950-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th – TASS Moscow, 9th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has issued a decree on the election of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. The decree points out that the term of the Second Supreme Soviet of the USSR will expire on February 10, 1950. Article 54 of the Constitution of the USSR stipulates that the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR must announce new elections within two months before the expiration of the term of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. Article 72 of the Electoral Law of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR stipulates that the date of the election of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR shall be announced two months before the election and shall be held on a non-working day. Therefore, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics hereby orders: The election of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR will be held on March 12, 1950 (Sunday).

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团  决定三月十二日选举最高苏维埃

1950-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    决定三月十二日选举最高苏维埃
    【新华社北京十日电】塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团顷发表关于苏联最高苏维埃选举的法令。法令指出：第二届苏联最高苏维埃任期至一九五○年二月十日即告满期。苏联宪法第五十四条规定，苏联最高苏维埃主席团须于苏联最高苏维埃任满前两个月以内，宣布举行新选举。苏联最高苏维埃选举法第七十二条规定，苏联最高苏维埃的选举日期，应在选举前两个月宣布，并应在非工作日举行选举。为此，苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团特下令：在一九五○年三月十二日（星期日）举行苏联最高苏维埃选举。
