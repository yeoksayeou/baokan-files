Title: Soviet Steelworkers and Engineers Receive Service Seniority Bonuses; Approximately 60 Million Rubles Received Last Year
Author:
Date: 1950-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow dispatch, January 10th: Engineers and technicians of enterprises under the Soviet Ministry of the Iron and Steel Industry received approximately 600 million rubles in service seniority bonuses last year. These bonuses are issued according to the individual's length of service, at a rate of 10% to 30% of their annual salary. Workers and engineers with more than 15 years of service can receive the maximum bonus amount, equivalent to 30% of their annual salary. Last year, steelmaking, blast furnace, and rolling mill workers at the Magnitogorsk Iron and Steel Works received nearly 30 million rubles in service seniority bonuses, while employees of the Kuznetsk Iron and Steel Works received over 25 million rubles in bonuses.

### 苏钢铁工人和工程师获服务资历奖金  去年约领到六万万卢布

1950-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏钢铁工人和工程师获服务资历奖金
    去年约领到六万万卢布
    【新华社北京十日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联钢铁工业部所属各企业的工程师和技师，在去年约领到六万万卢布的服务资历奖金，这种奖金是依各人服务的年限而按其年薪的百分之十至百分之三十发给的。凡服务十五年以上的工人和工程师，可得到合年薪百分之三十的最高额奖金。在去年，马格尼托高尔斯克钢铁工厂的炼钢、鼓风炉和辗机工人，得到将近三千万卢布的服务资历奖金，库兹涅茨克钢铁厂的员工得到二千五百余万卢布的奖金。
