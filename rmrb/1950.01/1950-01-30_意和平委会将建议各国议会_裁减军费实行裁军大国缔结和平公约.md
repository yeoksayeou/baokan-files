Title: Italian Peace Committee to Propose to National Parliaments: Reduce Military Spending, Implement Disarmament, Major Powers Conclude Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1950-01-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 29th: TASS, Rome, January 27th: Nenni, Chairman of the Italian Peace Committee, recently stated to the press that members of the Italian Peace Committee will submit a peace proposal to the Italian Parliament in February, and will simultaneously send it to the parliaments of other countries. The recommendations include: reducing military budgets and armed forces, prohibiting atomic weapons, conducting direct negotiations under international supervision to end military intervention in other countries, ceasing suppression of peace advocates, and major powers concluding a peace pact within the framework of the United Nations.

The Italian Communist Party newspaper, L'Unità, stated: While the Gasperi government is currently in Washington signing an agreement to supply Italy with armaments and formally include Italy in the group of imperialist warmongers, the proactive battle of peace advocates is of particular and major significance to Italy.

### 意和平委会将建议各国议会  裁减军费实行裁军大国缔结和平公约

1950-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    意和平委会将建议各国议会
    裁减军费实行裁军大国缔结和平公约
    【新华社北京二十九日电】塔斯社罗马二十七日讯：意大利和平委员会主席南尼，顷向报界发表谈话称：意大利和平委员会的议员，将在二月向意大利议会提出和平建议，并将同时送交其他国家的议会。建议内容包括：减少军事预算和军队，禁止原子武器，在国际监督下进行直接谈判以结束对其他国家的军事干涉，停止对和平拥护者的镇压，各大国缔结在联合国机构内的和平公约。
    意共机关报团结报称：当加斯贝利政府目前正在华盛顿签署以军火供给意大利，并使意大利正式参加帝国主义战争贩子集团的协定时，拥护和平人士的主动作战，对意大利实有特殊的主要意义。
