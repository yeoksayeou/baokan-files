Title: German Provisional People's Chamber Approves This Year's Economic Plan
Author:
Date: 1950-01-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 23rd: TASS reported from Berlin on the 21st that the Provisional People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic held its ninth plenary session in Berlin on the 20th. After hearing the report by Wissel, Chairman of the Economic Committee of the Chamber, the Chamber unanimously approved the national economic plan for the German Democratic Republic for 1950 proposed by the government.

### 德临时人民议会批准今年经济计划

1950-01-24
第4版()
专栏：

    德临时人民议会批准今年经济计划
    【新华社北京二十三日电】塔斯社二十一日讯：德意志民主共和国临时人民议会，二十日在柏林举行第九次全体会议，听取了议会经济委员会主席威塞尔的报告，一致通过政府所提出的德意志民主共和国一九五○年国民经济计划。
