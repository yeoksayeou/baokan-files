Title: General Yang Hucheng's Coffin Passes Through Wuhan; General Lin Biao and Others Personally Greet the Casket
Author:
Date: 1950-01-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 25th—The coffins of General Yang Hucheng and eight others, who were brutally murdered by the Kuomintang reactionaries, arrived in Hankou at 4:30 PM on the 24th, escorted by Yang Zhengmin, the eldest son of General Yang and Commander of the Dali Sub-district of the Northwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Those who went to greet the coffins included leading figures from the Party, government, and military, such as Lin Biao, Secretary of the CCP Central South Bureau and Commander of the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army concurrently, as well as representatives from all walks of life, totaling more than 3,000 people. Funeral wreaths and floral tributes formed a vast expanse. Amidst the low strains of mourning music, the coffins of General Yang Hucheng, Martyr Huang Xiansheng, Martyr Song Qiyun (General Yang's secretary), Martyr Yang Zhengzhong (General Yang's third son), Martyr Zhang Xingmin, Martyr Yan Jiming (both bodyguards of General Yang), Martyr Xu Lifang (Martyr Song Qiyun's wife), and her daughter Song Yusheng (born in prison, only six years old, and murdered alongside her parents), as well as the ash urn of General Yang's wife, were successively moved into the prepared memorial hall. A public memorial service will be held by all circles in Wuhan at 10:00 AM today, and the coffins will be sent north at 1:00 PM this afternoon.

### 杨虎城将军灵柩过汉  林彪将军等亲往迎灵

1950-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    杨虎城将军灵柩过汉
    林彪将军等亲往迎灵
    【新华社汉口二十五日电】惨遭国民党反动派杀害的杨虎城将军等八人灵柩，由杨将军长公子、中国人民解放军西北军区大荔分区司令员杨拯民护侍，二十四日下午四时半自渝运抵汉口。前往迎灵者有中共中央中南局书记、中南军区兼第四野战军司令员林彪等党政军诸首长及各界代表共三千余人。挽联花圈，环成一片。哀乐低鸣中，杨虎城将军、黄显声烈士、宋绮云烈士（杨将军秘书）、杨拯中烈士（杨将军三子）、张醒民烈士、阎继明烈士（均为杨将军之随身副官）、徐丽芳烈士（宋绮云烈士夫人）及其女宋狱生（出生狱中，年仅六岁，与其父母同时被害）等八人之灵柩及杨将军夫人之骨灰瓷瓶，相继升入预先布置之灵堂。今日上午十时武汉各界举行公祭，下午一时送灵北上。
