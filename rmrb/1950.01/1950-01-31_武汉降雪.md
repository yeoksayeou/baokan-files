Title: Wuhan Snowfall
Author:
Date: 1950-01-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 30th—Yesterday, starting from 1:30 PM, Wuhan experienced its first widespread snowfall of the season, which continued until late at night. Since the beginning of winter, Wuhan has been lacking snow, leading to the emergence of infectious diseases such as meningitis within the city. With the snowfall, the climate is expected to normalize, bringing relief and joy to the entire city.

### 武汉降雪

1950-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    武汉降雪
    【新华社汉口三十日电】昨日下午一时半起，武汉市首次普降瑞雪，直至深夜始停。入冬以来，武汉因缺雪而脑膜炎等传染病开始在市内发现；雪后，气候可望正常，全市人民莫不额手称庆。
