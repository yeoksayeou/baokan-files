Title: Former CCP Central Hebei-Chahar-Jehol-Liaoning Sub-Bureau Member Comrade Gao Zili Passes Away in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1950-01-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, January 11th: Comrade Gao Zili, former member of the CCP Central Hebei-Chahar-Jehol-Liaoning Sub-Bureau and former Deputy Director of the Hebei-Chahar-Jehol-Liaoning Office of the Northeast Administrative Committee, passed away in Shenyang on the 9th due to illness. The Northeast Bureau of the CCP Central Committee and the Northeast People's Government have organized a funeral committee and decided to hold a memorial service in Shenyang at 10:00 AM on the 11th. Comrade Gao Zili was from Pingxiang, Jiangxi, and was 51 years old this year. He came from a shop assistant background. He joined the Chinese Communist Party in 1926 and joined the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in July of the following year, successively serving as secretary of the Party committee of a company, detachment, division, and army, Minister of Land of the Central Government of the Chinese Soviet Republic, Director of the Department of Civil Affairs and Director of the Department of Construction of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government, and Deputy Chairman.

### 前中共中央冀察热辽分局委员  高自立同志病逝沈阳

1950-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    前中共中央冀察热辽分局委员
    高自立同志病逝沈阳
    【新华社沈阳十一日电】前中共中央冀察热辽分局委员、前东北行政委员会冀察热辽办事处副主任高自立同志于九日在沈阳病逝。中共中央东北局及东北人民政府已组织治丧委员会，并决定于十一日上午十时在沈举行追悼会。高自立同志系江西萍乡人，今年五十一岁。店员出身。一九二六年加入中国共产党，翌年七月加入中国工农红军，历任连、支队、师、军党委书记，中华苏维埃中央政府土地部长，陕甘宁边区政府民政厅长、建设厅长、副主席等职。
