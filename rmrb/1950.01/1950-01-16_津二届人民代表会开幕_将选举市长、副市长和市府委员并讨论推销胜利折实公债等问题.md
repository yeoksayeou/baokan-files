Title: Tianjin Second People's Representative Conference Opens: To Elect Mayor, Deputy Mayors, and Municipal Government Committee Members, and to Discuss Issues Such as Promoting the People's Victory Discounted Bonds

Author: 
Date: 1950-01-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 15th: The second Tianjin Municipal People's Representative Conference ceremoniously opened today—the anniversary of the liberation of Tianjin. This conference will exercise the functions of a People's Congress, elect the mayor, deputy mayors, and members of the municipal government committee, and discuss issues such as promoting the People's Victory Discounted Bonds. A total of 424 representatives from all sectors attended. Mayor Huang Jing of the Municipal People's Government gave a brief report in his opening speech on the work done in the three months since the last People's Representative Conference and presented to the conference his opinions on issues such as developing production, promoting bonds, fiscal austerity, and municipal construction in Tianjin in the future. The conference began discussions this afternoon.

### 津二届人民代表会开幕  将选举市长、副市长和市府委员并讨论推销胜利折实公债等问题

1950-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    津二届人民代表会开幕
    将选举市长、副市长和市府委员并讨论推销胜利折实公债等问题
    【新华社天津十五日电】天津市第二届各界人民代表会议，于今日——津市解放周年纪念日隆重揭幕。本届会议将行使人民代表大会职权，选举市长、副市长和市府委员，并讨论推销人民胜利折实公债等问题。出席各界代表共四百二十四人。市人民政府黄敬市长在开幕词中扼要报告了上届各界代表会后三个月的各项工作情形，并向大会提出关于今后津市发展生产、推销公债、财政节约、市政建设等问题的意见。下午大会即进行讨论。
