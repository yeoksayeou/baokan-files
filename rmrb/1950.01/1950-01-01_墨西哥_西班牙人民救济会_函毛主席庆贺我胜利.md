Title: Mexican "Spanish People's Relief Society" Sends Letter to Chairman Mao Congratulating Us on Victory
Author:
Date: 1950-01-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 30th. The Mexican "Spanish People's Relief Society" Committee sent a letter to Chairman Mao Zedong congratulating our people on their victory and the establishment of the People's Republic of China. The full text of the letter is as follows:

Respected Chairman Mao:

The "Spanish People's Relief Society" is delighted to extend its most sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Chinese people on the great victory won by the People's Liberation Army and the formal establishment of the People's Republic of China.

The democratic victory of the Chinese people is a great help to the Spanish people's struggle against fascism, reactionary forces, and imperialism.

We respectfully wish the Chinese people and their new government prosperity, and wish Your Excellency happiness and success in everything.

Mexican "Spanish People's Relief Society" Committee: Umasge, Margas, Gasdailote, Espinos, Maz, Luaermu, Liskano, Falango, Luano.

October 21, 1949

### 墨西哥“西班牙人民救济会”  函毛主席庆贺我胜利

1950-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    墨西哥“西班牙人民救济会”
    函毛主席庆贺我胜利
    【新华社北京三十日电】墨西哥“西班牙人民救济会”委员会致函毛泽东主席庆贺我国人民的胜利和中华人民共和国的成立。该函全文如下：可敬的毛主席：
    “西班牙人民救济会”欣喜地谨向阁下和中国人民致最诚恳的贺忱，以庆祝解放军所获得的伟大的胜利和中华人民共和国的正式成立。
    中国人民的民主胜利，对于西班牙人民反抗法西斯主义，反抗反动势力和反抗帝国主义的斗争，是一个伟大的助力。
    敬颂中国人民和他的新政体蒸蒸日上，敬祝阁下和社会百事如意。
    墨西哥“西班牙人民救济会”委员会乌马斯葛、马尔葛斯、加斯戴略特、爱斯比诺思、马兹、卢爱尔木、李斯加诺、法朗戈、卢阿诺。
                            一九四九年十月二十一日
