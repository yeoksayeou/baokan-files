Title: The Financial and Economic Committee Decides: All Imported Foreign Cotton Through Ports Within the Customs Area to be Exempt from Tax for Three Months

Author:

Date: 1950-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th. In order to supply the raw cotton needed by public and private textile industries nationwide and to encourage public and private manufacturers to organize the import of foreign cotton, the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council decided on the 4th of this month: From January 1, 1950, to the end of March, all foreign cotton imported through ports within the customs area by public and private manufacturers with approved applications shall be exempt from import tax.

### 财经委员会决定  关内各港口进口外棉一律准予免税三个月

1950-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    财经委员会决定
    关内各港口进口外棉一律准予免税三个月
    【新华社北京五日电】为供应全国公私纺织业所需原棉，鼓励公私厂商组织外棉进口，政务院财政经济委员会于本月四日决定：自一九五○年一月一日起至三月底止，凡公私厂商经申请批准经过关内各港口进口之外棉，一律准予免税进口。
