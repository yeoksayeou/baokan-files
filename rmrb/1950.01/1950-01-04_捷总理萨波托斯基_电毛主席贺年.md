Title: Czechoslovak Premier Zápotocký Sends New Year's Greetings to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1950-01-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd - Czechoslovak Premier Antonín Zápotocký sent a telegram to Chairman Mao to congratulate the New Year. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of this New Year, on behalf of the Czechoslovak government and myself, I extend my congratulations to Your Excellency and all the people of your country for further success in your construction efforts after your country has rid itself of domestic and foreign exploiters. The working people of the whole world are fervently celebrating the great victory of the Chinese people in the past year, which marks the victory of the powerful fortress of the world's peace camp.

                                Antonín Zápotocký
                                              December 31st

### 捷总理萨波托斯基  电毛主席贺年

1950-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    捷总理萨波托斯基
    电毛主席贺年
    【新华社北京三日电】捷总理安东尼·萨波托斯基致电毛主席祝贺新年，电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    当此新年之际，谨代表捷克斯洛伐克政府与本人恭祝阁下和贵国全体人民在贵国摆脱了内外剥削者之后的建设努力中更进一步的成功。全世界劳动人民都狂热地庆祝中国人民一年来的伟大胜利，它标志着全世界和平阵营强大堡垒的胜利。
                               安东尼·萨波托斯基
                                              十二月三十一日
