Title: Korean Ambassador to China, Li Zhouyuan, Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Liu
Author:
Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 28th. Li Zhouyuan, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, accompanied by Cui Yi, Administrative Counselor of the Embassy, Cui Ying, Cultural Counselor, and Jin Guofu, Trade Counselor, presented his credentials to Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government of China, at 4:30 PM today. Ambassador Li Zhouyuan delivered a eulogy to Vice Chairman Liu when presenting his credentials. Vice Chairman Liu then delivered a reply. Also present were Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government, Li Kenong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director, and Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government.

### 朝鲜驻华大使李周渊向刘副主席呈递国书

1950-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜驻华大使李周渊向刘副主席呈递国书
    【新华社北京二十八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国第一任驻我国特命全权大使李周渊率大使馆行政参事崔一、文化参事崔英、贸易参事金国辅等，于今日下午四时半向我中央人民政府刘少奇副主席呈递国书。李周渊大使于呈递国书时向刘副主席致颂词。刘副主席继即致答词。在场者尚有中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，外交部副部长李克农，人民解放军代总参谋长聂荣臻，外交部办公厅主任王炳南，副主任阎宝航，中央人民政府典礼局长余心清。
