Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author: 
Date: 1950-01-24
Page: 4

    [This Newspaper News] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (24th) at 17:30, during workers' time, will "Talk About a Few Basic Understandings that Trade Unions Should Have Regarding the Signing of Collective Contracts." At 18:30, natural science lecture: Electrical Knowledge Lecture 8 "Resistance." At 18:00, the first art program: the Beijing opera "New Journey to the Capital". At 21:00, the second art program: Soviet songs. At 18:45, new songs will be broadcast: "Chairman Mao" and others. At 12:00, Chinese music: "Spring Scenery in April" and others.
