Title: US Strike Numbers Surge Last Year; 1948: 34 Million Work Hours Lost; 1949: 53 Million Work Hours Lost
Author:
Date: 1950-01-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th -- TASS, New York: The number of strikes in the United States sharply increased in 1949. According to estimates from the Bureau of Labor Statistics of the US Department of Labor, the US lost 34 million work hours due to strikes in 1948, while in 1949 this figure rose to 53 million work hours. The strikes hit major industrial sectors such as steel and coal mining. Workers in these enterprises were struggling to win better living and working conditions.

### 美去年罢工人数激增  一九四八：损失三千四百万工时  一九四九：损失五千三百万工时

1950-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    美去年罢工人数激增
    一九四八：损失三千四百万工时         
    一九四九：损失五千三百万工时
    【新华社北京六日电】塔斯社纽约讯：一九四九年，美国罢工人数锐增。据美国劳工部劳工统计局估计，一九四八年美国因罢工而损失三千四百万工时，而一九四九年则为五千三百万工时，罢工打击了主要的产业部门如钢业和煤矿业。这些企业的工人是为了争取较好的生活条件与工作条件而进行斗争的。
