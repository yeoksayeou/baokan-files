Title: Togliatti Delivers Speech, Denounces Modena Police Crimes, Calls for Thorough Investigation of Interior Minister's Relationship with the Atrocity
Author:
Date: 1950-01-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th: TASS Rome, 12th: Italian Communist Party General Secretary Togliatti recently issued a statement on the Modena police shooting of workers, stating: Apart from the terrorist crimes of the Fascists, the Modena shooting of workers is the most tragic and barbaric atrocity seen in Italy only since the shooting of Milan workers by General Bava Beccaris' government in 1898. There is no doubt that some people deliberately used bloody suppression to overwhelm the heroic and tenacious fighting spirit of the Modena workers.

Togliatti calls on the entire Italian people to pay attention: in less than three months, there have been four incidents of the Gasperi government's personnel shooting working people in Italy. This is unprecedented in Italian history. Togliatti demands a thorough investigation into the relationship between Italian Interior Minister Scelba and these shootings.

### 托里亚蒂发表谈话  痛斥摩登纳警察罪行  主张彻查内政部长与暴行关系

1950-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    托里亚蒂发表谈话  痛斥摩登纳警察罪行
    主张彻查内政部长与暴行关系
    【新华社北京十三日电】塔斯社罗马十二日讯：意大利共产党总书记托里亚蒂顷就摩登纳警察枪杀工人事件，发表谈话称：除了法西斯的恐怖罪行以外，摩登纳的枪杀工人事件，是自一八九八年贝洛克斯将军的政府枪杀米兰工人事件以来，在意大利所仅见的最悲惨最野蛮的大暴行。无可怀疑，这次某些人蓄意用血腥镇压手段，来压倒摩登纳工人英勇顽强的战斗精神。
    托里亚蒂提请全意人民注意：不到三个月的时间内，意大利发生了四次加斯贝利政府人员枪杀劳动人民的事件。这在意大利历史上是空前的。托里亚蒂要求彻底调查意大利内政部长谢尔巴与这些枪杀事件的关系。
