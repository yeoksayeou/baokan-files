Title: Preparing to Commemorate Lenin's Death: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Plans Activities

Author:
Date: 1950-01-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th. January 21st marks the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death. To commemorate this great proletarian revolutionary teacher, the creator of the Bolshevik Party and the Soviet state, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters will carry out the following activities: (1) On the 21st, a lecture and film commemorative evening will be held in the auditorium of the Sino-French University. Qian Junrui, the general secretary of the association, will report on Lenin's life and his theoretical doctrines; (2) From the 21st to the 23rd, a three-day commemorative exhibition of Lenin's pictures and works will be held at the association; (3) "Lenin's Life" will be broadcast on the Central People's Broadcasting Station three days before the anniversary; (4) A commemorative supplement will be published in the People's Daily on the 21st.

### 准备纪念列宁逝世  中苏友协总会拟定活动项目

1950-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    准备纪念列宁逝世
    中苏友协总会拟定活动项目
    【新华社北京十六日电】一月二十一日为列宁逝世二十六周年纪念日。中苏友好协会总会为纪念这位伟大的无产阶级革命导师、布尔什维克党和苏维埃国家的创造者，届时将进行下列活动：（一）于二十一日在中法大学大礼堂举办讲演电影纪念晚会，由该会总干事钱俊瑞报告列宁生平及其理论学说；（二）从二十一日起至二十三日止，在该会举行有关列宁图片和著作纪念展览三天；（三）于纪念日前三天在中央人民广播电台广播“列宁生平”；（四）在二十一日人民日报上出版纪念专刊。
