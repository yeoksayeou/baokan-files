Title: Prasad Elected President of India; Chairman Mao Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th—Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram congratulating Rajendra Prasad on his election as President of the Republic of India. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Rajendra Prasad, President of the Republic of India:
    On the happy occasion of Your Excellency's election as the first President of India, I, on behalf of the Central People's Government and the people of the People's Republic of China, extend my congratulations.

                        Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
                                      January 28, 1950

### 普拉沙德当选印度总统  毛主席致电庆贺

1950-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    普拉沙德当选印度总统
    毛主席致电庆贺
    【新华社北京二十八日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席电贺印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德当选，电文如下：印度共和国拉金德拉·普拉沙德总统阁下：
    欣值阁下当选为印度第一任总统之时，我谨代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府与人民表示庆贺。
                        中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席  毛泽东
                                      一九五○年一月二十八日
