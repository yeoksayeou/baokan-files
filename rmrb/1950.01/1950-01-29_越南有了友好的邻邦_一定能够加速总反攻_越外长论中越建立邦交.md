Title: Vietnam Has a Friendly Neighbor, Surely Able to Accelerate the General Counter-Offensive - Vietnamese Foreign Minister on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between China and Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th: According to Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 27th: When interviewed by "Voice of Vietnam" reporters yesterday, Huang Mingjian, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, commented on the significance of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, stating: "This is a natural action by the two governments, because the New China is a good friend of Vietnam. We must strengthen the friendship and cooperation between the two countries. This is the reason for the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Vietnam."

Huang continued: "Now we have a friendly neighbor. The appearance of such a friend will greatly encourage our people and army. They will become stronger day by day and will surely be able to realize Chairman Ho Chi Minh's call to accelerate preparations for the general counter-offensive."

### 越南有了友好的邻邦  一定能够加速总反攻  越外长论中越建立邦交

1950-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    越南有了友好的邻邦
    一定能够加速总反攻
    越外长论中越建立邦交
    【新华社北京二十八日电】据越南通讯社北越二十七日讯：越南民主共和国外交部长黄明鉴昨日接见“越南之声”记者时，对中华人民共和国与越南民主共和国建立外交关系的意义，发表谈话称：“这是两国政府的一种自然的行动，因为新中国是越南的好朋友。我们必须加强两国之间的友谊与合作。这就是中国与越南之间建立外交关系的原因。”
    黄氏继称：“现在我们有了一个友好的邻邦。这样一个朋友的出现，将大大地鼓舞我们的人民和军队。他们将一天天地变得更加强大，并一定能够实现胡志明主席所提出的加速准备总反攻的号召。”
