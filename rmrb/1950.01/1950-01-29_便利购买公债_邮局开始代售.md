Title: Convenient Purchase of Public Bonds: Post Offices Begin Selling on Commission
Author:
Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 4

    Convenient Purchase of Public Bonds
    Post Offices Begin Selling on Commission
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The Beijing Postal Administration's branches and the Postal Savings and Remittance Bureau and its offices, entrusted by the People's Bank, will begin selling Victory Parity Unit Public Bonds on commission from January 30th. People from all walks of life who wish to purchase public bonds can go to the nearest post office or savings and remittance bureau to buy them. (Postal and Remittance Bureau Communications Team)
    [This newspaper's dispatch] To facilitate the purchase of Victory Parity Unit Public Bonds by people from all walks of life, the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China, in addition to the original 17 units such as the Savings Department that continue to act as agents for issuance, has added seven sub-offices to act as agents for issuance from the 27th: Qianmen, Meishi Street, Xiliushu Well, Xidan, Dongsi, Dongdan, and Tsinghua University.

### 便利购买公债  邮局开始代售

1950-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    便利购买公债
    邮局开始代售
    【本报讯】北京邮政管理局所属各支局及邮政储金汇业局和办事处，受人民银行委托，于一月三十日起，代售胜利折实公债，各界民众如欲购买公债，可向就近各邮局或储汇局去买。（邮汇局通讯组）
    【本报讯】中国人民银行北京分行为便利各界购买胜利折实公债，除原设储蓄部等十七个单位继续代理发行外。于二十七日起增加：前门、煤市街、西柳树井、西单、东四、东单、清华大学等七个分理处代理发行。
