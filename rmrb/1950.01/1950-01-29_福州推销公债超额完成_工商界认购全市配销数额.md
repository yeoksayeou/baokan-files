Title: Fuzhou Overfulfills Public Bond Sales Target; Industrial and Commercial Circles Subscribe to Entire City Quota
Author:
Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 28th. The task of promoting the Victory Inflation-Adjusted Public Bonds in Fuzhou City has been overfulfilled. After the Fujian Provincial People's Government announced a distribution of 300,000 shares of Victory Public Bonds for Fuzhou, the city held its second People's Representatives Conference on the 20th of this month to discuss the issue of subscribing to the Victory Public Bonds. Representatives from the industrial and commercial sectors announced at the meeting that they would subscribe to the entire public bond allocation of 300,000 shares for Fuzhou City. According to People's Bank statistics, since the issuance of public bonds, the city has sold 8,714 shares, with subscriptions of over 300,200 shares yet to be paid, exceeding the city's allocated quota. Currently, all sectors are still enthusiastically purchasing.

### 福州推销公债超额完成  工商界认购全市配销数额

1950-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    福州推销公债超额完成
    工商界认购全市配销数额
    【新华社福州二十八日电】福州市胜利折实公债推销任务已超额完成。在福建省人民政府公布福州配销胜利公债三十万分后，本月二十日，本市召开第二次各界人民代表会议讨论认购胜利公债问题。工商界代表在会上宣布认购全福州市公债配销数三十万分。据人民银行统计，本市自发行公债以来共已售出八千七百十四分，认购未缴者三十万二千余分，已超过本市配销数额。目前各界仍热烈购买中。
