Title: Czechoslovak Ambassador Pays Respects to Our Government Leader Vice-Chairman Liu, Hosts Banquet for Czechoslovak Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1950-01-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 17th: Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Weisskopf, paid respects to Vice-Chairman Zhu De and Vice-Chairman Li Jishen on the 16th. On the 17th, he paid respects to Vice-Chairman Zhang Lan and Vice-Premier Dong Biwu, and visited Acting Chief of the General Staff and Mayor Nie Rongzhen, and Deputy Mayor Zhang Youyu. Mr. Weisskopf will continue to visit other leaders of our government in the coming days.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 17th: Vice-Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet for Czechoslovak Ambassador Weisskopf and Secretary Rodai at 8:00 PM on the 17th. Those invited to attend included Vice-Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government, Vice-Chairman Li Jishen, Vice-Chairman Zhang Lan, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Vice-Premier Dong Biwu of the Government Administration Council, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Li Kenong, Vice-Minister Zhang Hanfu, Director Wang Bingnan, and Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government Yu Xinqing, and others.

### 捷大使谒我政府首长刘副主席欢宴捷大使

1950-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    捷大使谒我政府首长刘副主席欢宴捷大使
    【新华社北京十七日电】捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使魏斯柯普夫于十六日谒见朱德副主席和李济深副主席，十七日分谒张澜副主席、董必武副总理，并分访聂荣臻代总参谋长兼市长、张友渔副市长。魏氏将于日内续访我政府其他首长。
    【新华社北京十七日电】中央人民政府刘少奇副主席于十七日下午八时宴捷克斯洛伐克魏斯柯普夫大使及罗戴秘书。应邀作陪者有中央人民政府朱德副主席、李济深副主席、张澜副主席、林伯渠秘书长，政务院董必武副总理，人民革命军事委员会聂荣臻代总参谋长，外交部李克农副部长，章汉夫副部长，王炳南主任，中央人民政府典礼局余心清局长等。
