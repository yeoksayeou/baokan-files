Title: British Workers Overwhelmingly Vote Against Government Wage Freeze

Author:

Date: 1950-01-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th. British workers overwhelmingly oppose the wage freeze policy of the British Labour government, which is supported by the Trades Union Congress controlled by labor traitors. According to a TASS report from London on the 10th, following the National Union of Railwaymen and the Amalgamated Engineering Union, 133 branches of the National Union of Mineworkers in Scotland, as well as branches in Yorkshire, Lancashire, and other regions, have all voted against the wage freeze. The London Daily Express estimates that when the executive committees of all unions affiliated with the Trades Union Congress meet on the 12th, the National Union of Mineworkers will cast 610,000 votes against, bringing the total number of votes against the wage freeze to three million.

### 英国广大职工投票反对政府冻结工资

1950-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    英国广大职工投票反对政府冻结工资
    【新华社北京十二日电】英国广大职工群众反对为工贼所控制的全国职工大会支持的英国工党政府的冻结工资的政策。据塔斯社伦敦十日讯，继全国铁路工人工会和机器工人工会之后，全国矿工联盟在苏格兰的一百三十三个分会和约克郡、兰开厦及其他地区的分会，均已投票反对冻结工资。伦敦每日快报估计在十二日职工大会所属全体工会执行委员会开会时，全国矿工联盟将投六十一万反对票，因此反对冻结工资的总票数将达三百万。
