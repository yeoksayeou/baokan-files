Title: The Hague Women's Rally Calls to Defend Peace, Protests US Imperialism Providing Weapons to the Netherlands
Author:
Date: 1950-01-21
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th -- TASS, The Hague, 19th: A grand women's rally in support of peace was held in Amsterdam, Netherlands. Mrs. Eslanda Robeson (wife of the famous Black leader Paul Robeson), who recently attended the Asian Women's Representative Conference in Beijing; the wife of Indonesian leader Soelipno, who was murdered by imperialist agents; and Dutch women's representative Averink all gave speeches at the meeting. Eslanda Robeson said: "Progressive groups and people across the United States are fighting for lasting peace." Speakers called on all Dutch women to intensify the struggle to defend peace, strengthen the unity of the working class, and more bravely thwart the plans of warmongers. The meeting passed a resolution demanding the withdrawal of Dutch troops from Indonesia, granting freedom to the Indonesian people, protesting the United States' provision of weapons to the Netherlands, and demanding that the Dutch government withdraw from the aggressive North Atlantic Treaty Organization.

### 荷京妇女集会号召保卫和平  抗议美帝给荷兰武器

1950-01-21
第3版()
专栏：

    荷京妇女集会号召保卫和平
    抗议美帝给荷兰武器
    【新华社北京二十日电】塔斯社海牙十九日讯：荷兰阿姆斯特丹举行盛大的妇女拥护和平的集会。不久以前出席北京亚洲妇女代表会议的伊斯兰达·罗伯逊夫人（著名黑人领袖保罗·罗伯逊的夫人），被帝国主义者的代理人所残害的印尼领袖苏利普诺的夫人，荷兰妇女代表阿维伦克等，均在会上发表演说。伊斯兰达·罗伯逊说：“美国的进步团体和全国人民一起，正在为持久和平而战。”演说者号召荷兰全体妇女加紧保卫和平的斗争，增强工人阶级的团结，更为勇敢地挫败战争鼓动者的计划。会议通过决议，要求从印尼撤回荷兰军队，给印尼人民以自由，决议抗议美国给荷兰以武器，要求荷兰政府退出侵略性的北大西洋公约。
