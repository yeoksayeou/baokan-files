Title: Central People's Radio Station Natural Science Lectures: Programs from January 29th to Next Month 3rd
Author:
Date: 1950-01-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 28th: The Central People's Broadcasting Station's natural science lecture program for the week starting January 29th is as follows:

    January 29th: Zhukovsky, the Father of Soviet Aviation (Scientist Stories)
    January 30th: Should those who study natural science learn Marxism-Leninism? (Pei Wenzhong)
    January 31st: Electric Arc and Electric Furnace (Basics of Electricity) Lecture 9 (Qin Zhuhao)
    February 1st: Electric Lights (Basics of Electricity Lecture 10) (Guo Yizeng)
    February 2nd: Electrolysis and Electroplating (Basics of Electricity Lecture 11) (Liang Sirui)
    February 3rd: How to view Newton's Laws of Motion from a Materialist Dialectical Perspective? (Science Q&A)
    All of the above programs will be broadcast at 6:30 PM each evening.

### 中央人民电台自然科学讲座  二十九日至下月三日节目

1950-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中央人民电台自然科学讲座
    二十九日至下月三日节目
    【新华社北京二十八日电】中央人民广播电台从二十九日起一星期的自然科学讲座节目如下：
    二十九日  苏联航空之父楚可夫斯基（科学家故事）
    三十日  学习自然科学的人是否要学马列主义？（裴文中）
    三十一日  电弧与电熔炉（电学常识）第九讲（秦祝浩）
    二月一日  电灯（电学常识第十讲）（郭沂曾）
    二日   电解与电镀（电学常识第十一讲）（梁思睿）
    三日  怎样用唯物辩证法观点看牛顿运动定律？（科学问答）
    以上各节目于每晚六时三十分播送。
