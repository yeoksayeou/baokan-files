Title: All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions and Others Extend New Year's Greetings to Chinese Workers

Author:
Date: 1950-01-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 2nd: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has received New Year's greetings from V.V. Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, as well as from Kulabko, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Railway Workers' Union, Milev, Chairman of the Bulgarian Newspaper Workers' Union, Demitrov, Secretary, and Kirilova, Chairman of the Bulgarian Textile and Garment Workers' Union, addressed to various industrial unions in China.

Kuznetsov's telegram stated: "To Comrade Chen Yun, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions: The All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, on behalf of the Soviet working people, extends joyful New Year greetings to the Chinese working class and laboring people. We celebrate the successes in building a new life for the welfare of all workers of the People's Republic of China."

Kulabko's telegram stated: "The Central Committee of the Soviet Railway Workers' Union wishes you and all Chinese railway workers a happy New Year and further success in your struggle to rapidly restore and develop railway transportation for the prosperity of the People's Republic of China."

Milev's and others' telegram stated: "The Bulgarian Newspaper Workers' Union respectfully extends New Year's greetings to our comrade brothers and congratulates you on the great victory of completely overthrowing the reactionary Kuomintang government."

Kirilova's telegram stated: "We hope that this year you will completely eliminate all reactionary bandits in the country, turn to the road of building a new China, and become peaceful defenders of the oppressed people of the Far East colonies."

### 全苏职工会中央委员会等向我国工人贺年

1950-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    全苏职工会中央委员会等向我国工人贺年
    【新华社北京二日电】中华全国总工会顷收到全苏联职工会中央委员会主席库兹涅佐夫及苏联铁路总工会中央委员会书记库拉勃柯、保加利亚报业工会主席米列夫、书记得米特洛夫、保加利亚纺织缝纫工会主席基里洛瓦分致我国各业工会祝贺新年的电文。
    库兹涅佐夫来电称：“中华全国总工会主席陈云同志：全苏工会中央委员会代表苏联劳动人民向中国工人阶级和劳动人民祝贺快乐的新年。我们庆祝为了中华人民共和国全体工人的福利在建设新生活上的成功。”
    库拉勃柯电文称：“苏联铁路总工会中央委员会向你们和全中国铁路工人祝快乐的新年，并祝你们为了中华人民共和国的兴盛而迅速恢复及发展铁路交通的斗争进一步地成功。”
    米列夫等电文称：“保加利亚报业工会敬贺同志兄弟们新年，并祝贺您们完全打倒了反动的国民党政府的伟大胜利。”
    基里洛瓦电文称：“希望你们在今年完全消灭国内一切反动匪帮，转向建设新中国的大路，成为远东殖民地被压迫人民的和平保卫者。”
