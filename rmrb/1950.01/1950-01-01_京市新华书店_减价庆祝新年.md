Title: Beijing Xinhua Bookstore Offers New Year Discounts
Author:
Date: 1950-01-01
Page: 3

    Beijing Xinhua Bookstore
    Discounted Prices to Celebrate the New Year
    [This newspaper report] To celebrate the first New Year's Day of the People's Republic of China, the Beijing branch of Xinhua Bookstore is responding to the Beijing New Year's Celebration by setting up mobile supply teams at Shuanghong Pavilion East in Beihai Park and Xili Pavilion in Zhongshan Park on January 2nd. Readers can enjoy a 20% discount on all Mao Zedong's works, Stalin's works, youth reading materials, magazines and newspapers.

### 京市新华书店  减价庆祝新年

1950-01-01
第3版()
专栏：

    京市新华书店
    减价庆祝新年
    【本报讯】新华书店北京分店为庆祝中华人民共和国第一个元旦，响应京市新年大联欢，于一月二日在北海公园双虹榭东及中山公园习礼亭设立流动供应队。毛主席著作、斯大林著作、青年读物、杂志报刊一律八折优待读者。
