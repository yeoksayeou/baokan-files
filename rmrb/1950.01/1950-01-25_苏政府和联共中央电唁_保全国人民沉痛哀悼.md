Title: Soviet Government and CPSU Send Condolences; People of Bulgaria Mourn with Deep Sorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 24th: TASS Moscow reports: The Soviet government and the Communist Party of the Soviet Union have sent condolences on the passing of Vasil Kolarov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria and Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party.

    Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Shvernik, sent a telegram to Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Najdenov, stating: "On behalf of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and myself, I express my sincere condolences on the passing of Vasil Kolarov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria. The memory of Kolarov, the outstanding leader of the Bulgarian People's Democratic movement, the founder of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and a loyal friend of the Soviet Union, will forever live in the hearts of our people."

    The Council of Ministers of the USSR sent a telegram to the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria stating: "The passing of Comrade Kolarov has deprived the Bulgarian people of a well-tested and staunch fighter for the cause of peace, democracy, and socialism."

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union sent a telegram of condolence to the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, stating: "Comrade Kolarov, a loyal friend of the Soviet Union and the closest comrade-in-arms of Dimitrov, consistently upheld the cause of internationalism, fighting unyieldingly against the reactionary imperialists and their lackeys. The glorious memory of Comrade Kolarov will forever live in the hearts of all fighters of the international labor and communist movement."

    Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky sent a telegram of condolence to Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Pop Tomov.

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 24th: TASS Sofia, January 23rd: Bulgaria is in national mourning for the passing of Vasil Kolarov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

    All government offices and residences in Sofia are flying flags at half-mast as a sign of mourning. Portraits of Kolarov are displayed in windows. The working people of the entire country are deeply grieved.

    Memorial services have been held at the Sofia Twelfth Mechanical Plant, the Dimitrov Factory, and other enterprises and institutions throughout the country.

    The afternoon and evening editions of the capital's newspapers have published announcements from the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Council of Ministers regarding the passing of Kolarov, messages of condolence from various organizations, and news of memorial services held in Sofia and other places and enterprises. The government's Kolarov Funeral Committee announced that Kolarov's body will lie in state at the National Assembly Building to allow the people of the entire country to pay their last respects.

    The funeral will be held on January 25th.

### 苏政府和联共中央电唁  保全国人民沉痛哀悼

1950-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    苏政府和联共中央电唁  保全国人民沉痛哀悼
    【新华社北京二十四日电】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府、苏联共产党顷电唁保加利亚部长会议主席、保共书记科拉罗夫的逝世。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克致电保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席奈捷夫称：“谨代表苏联最高苏维埃主席团和我个人，对保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席科拉罗夫的逝世，表示真诚的哀悼。对保加利亚人民民主运动的杰出的领袖、保加利亚人民共和国的建立者、苏联的忠诚友人科拉罗夫的忆念，将永远活在我国人民的心中。”
    苏联部长会议致电保加利亚人民共和国部长会议称：“科拉罗夫同志的逝世，使保加利亚人民失去了为和平、民主与社会主义事业而斗争的历尽锻炼的坚贞斗士。”
    苏联共产党中央委员会电唁保加利亚共产党中央委员会称：“苏联的忠诚朋友和季米特洛夫最亲密的战友科拉罗夫同志，始终如一地维护着国际主义的事业，不屈不挠地和帝国主义反动派及其走狗斗争。对科拉罗夫同志的光辉忆念，将永远活在国际劳工和共产主义运动的一切斗士的心中。”
    苏联外交部长维辛斯基致电保加利亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼外交部长波普托莫夫，表示吊唁。
    【新华社北京二十四日电】塔斯社索非亚二十三日讯：保加利亚举国哀悼部长会议主席华西尔·科拉罗夫的逝世。
    索非亚所有政府机关与住宅都下半旗志哀。窗户上挂着科拉罗夫的像。全国劳动人民都深为哀痛。
    索非亚第十二机械厂、季米特洛夫工厂以及国内其他企业与机关，都举行了追悼会。
    首都报纸的午晚刊，都登载了保加利亚共产党中央委员会和部长会议关于科拉罗夫逝世的公告、各团体的唁电、以及索非亚和其他各地各企业举行追悼会的消息。政府的科拉罗夫治丧委员会宣布：科拉罗夫遗体停放国民议会大厦，以便全国人民致最后的告别。
    葬仪将在一月二十五日举行。
