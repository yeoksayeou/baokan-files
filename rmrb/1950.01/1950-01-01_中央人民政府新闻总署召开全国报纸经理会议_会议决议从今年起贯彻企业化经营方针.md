Title: The General Administration of News of the Central People's Government Convened a National Newspaper Managers Conference; The meeting resolved to implement the policy of enterprise management starting this year.
Author:
Date: 1950-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 31st: In order to solve the serious loss problem of national public and private newspapers, the General Administration of News of the Central People's Government convened a National Newspaper Managers Conference in Beijing from December 17th to 26th. Twenty-seven units of public and private newspapers above the provincial and municipal levels attended, and six units attended as observers. These newspapers are distributed in a vast area from Inner Mongolia in the north to Wuhan in the south, from Xi'an in the west to Shanghai in the east. The meeting decided that in 1950, national public and private newspapers must implement and carry out the policy of enterprise management, and overcome the unreasonable phenomena in the operation of many newspapers in the past. The meeting conducted research on issues such as paper usage, quotation, distribution, advertising, staffing, and accounting, and made decisions accordingly. During the conference, Bo Yibo, Minister of Finance of the Central People's Government, and Vice Minister Rong Zihe reported on the current financial situation, and Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, reported on factory management and trade union work. After the meeting, representatives of newspapers in North China, under the leadership of the General Administration of News, promoted the spirit of mutual assistance and adjusted thirteen letterpress printing presses for the Pingyuan Daily, Chahar Daily, Suiyuan Daily, and Qinhuangdao Workers' Daily, which lacked machines.

### 中央人民政府新闻总署召开全国报纸经理会议  会议决议从今年起贯彻企业化经营方针

1950-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府新闻总署召开全国报纸经理会议
    会议决议从今年起贯彻企业化经营方针
    【新华社北京三十一日电】为了解决全国公营与私营报纸的严重赔耗问题，中央人民政府新闻总署于十二月十七日至二十六日在北京召开了全国报纸经理会议。出席的有省市以上公营私营报社二十七个单位，列席的有六个单位。这些报纸，分布在北至内蒙古，南至武汉，西至西安，东至上海的广大地区。会议决定：一九五○年全国公私营报纸都须实行与贯彻企业化的经营方针，克服以往许多报纸经营中的不合理现象。对于用纸、报价、发行、广告、编制、会计等问题，会议都分别进行了研究，并作出了决定。在会议进程中，中央人民政府财政部薄一波部长、戎子和副部长报告了当前的财政情况，中华全国总工会李立三副主席报告了工厂管理及工会工作。会议结束后，华北各报代表在新闻总署领导下，发扬互助精神，给缺乏机器的平原日报、察哈尔日报、绥远日报、秦皇岛工人报等调剂了十三部平版印刷机。
