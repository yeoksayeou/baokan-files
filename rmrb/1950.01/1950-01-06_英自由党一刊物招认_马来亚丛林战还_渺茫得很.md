Title: British Liberal Party Publication Admits Malayan Jungle War Still "Very Dim"
Author:
Date: 1950-01-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 5th: According to Reuters, quoting the British Liberal Party's weekly "Time and Tide": the British colonial authorities in Malaya, in their proclamation to intensify the "mopping up" of guerrillas in 1950, displayed an "almost desperate tone." The weekly wrote: "The fact is this: British troops have made no progress in the operation against well-equipped, mobile, and well-trained guerrillas in the Malayan jungle. For a year, the losses in both lives and money have been enormous, and the hope of ending this tenacious jungle war seems very dim."

### 英自由党一刊物招认  马来亚丛林战还“渺茫得很”

1950-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    英自由党一刊物招认
    马来亚丛林战还“渺茫得很”
    【新华社北京五日电】据路透社引英国自由党的《时与潮》周刊消息：马来亚英国殖民当局在所谓一九五○年加紧清剿游击队的文告中，表现了一种“几乎绝望的声调”。该周刊写道：“事实如此：英国军队对在马来亚丛林中装备良好、机动而训练有素的游击队的作战，毫无进展。一年来，在生命和金钱两方面的损失巨大，而结束这一坚强固执的丛林战的希望，似乎还渺茫得很。”
