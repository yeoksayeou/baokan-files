Title: Our Army Shot Down an Enemy Plane Over the Leizhou Peninsula
Author:
Date: 1950-01-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, South China Front, 7th. On December 29th, in a certain area of the Leizhou Peninsula in southern Guangdong, a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army shot and wounded an enemy advanced trainer aircraft strafing our army's positions. After being wounded, the plane was forced to land, and the pilot, Sun Chongbin, a staff officer of the 1st Section of the 5th Squadron of the bandit air force, was captured by our forces.

### 雷州半岛我军击落敌机一架

1950-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    雷州半岛我军击落敌机一架
    【新华社华南前线七日电】人民解放军某部十二月二十九日，在粤南雷州半岛某地，击伤向我军阵地扫射的敌高级教练机一架。该机负伤后，被迫降落，驾驶员匪空军第五大队一科参谋孙重斌被我俘获。
