Title: Cultivating Revolutionary Cadres: South China University Opens in Guangzhou; Currently Has Over 3,000 Students in Three Colleges; Workers' College to be Added Soon
Author:
Date: 1950-01-06
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 4th: South China University, an institution for cultivating revolutionary cadres, commenced classes on the 1st of this month, and instruction began on the 3rd. The university is currently divided into three departments: the College of Education, the College of Administration, and the College of Finance and Economics, with a total of more than 3,700 students. The president is Ye Jianying, and the vice-presidents are Chen Weishi and Luo Ming. The university will also establish a Workers' College and a Research Department in the near future. Furthermore, National Sun Yat-sen University also officially commenced classes on the 4th, with a total of 2,255 students reporting and registering.

### 培养革命干部  南方大学在穗开课  现有三院学生三千余人  短期内将添设工人学院

1950-01-06
第3版()
专栏：

    培养革命干部
    南方大学在穗开课
    现有三院学生三千余人  短期内将添设工人学院
    【新华社广州四日电】培养革命干部的南方大学已于本月一日开学，三日开始上课。该校现在分为教育学院、行政学院和财政经济学院三部，共有学生三千七百多人。校长叶剑英、副校长陈唯实、罗明。该校并将在短期内添设工人学院和研究部。又：国立中山大学也在四日正式上课，报到注册的学生共二千二百五十五人。
