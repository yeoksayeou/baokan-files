Title: Laboring People Around the World Call Out to the Great Lenin with Love and Hope in Various National Languages; People in Romania and Other Countries Hold Meetings to Commemorate Him
Author:
Date: 1950-01-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th: According to TASS, Moscow: On the 26th anniversary of the death of the great Lenin, Soviet newspapers have devoted considerable space to commemorative articles, reporting on the commemoration meetings held on the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death, and news from China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, Albania, Romania, and other countries commemorating Lenin.

    The editorial in *Pravda* stated: Laboring people everywhere in the world call out Lenin's name with love and hope, in all the languages ​​of the world. Lenin's immortal ideas illuminate the path to freedom and happiness for all those who still suffer under capitalist enslavement. Under Lenin's banner, the Soviet people are building a communist society, and the laboring people of various people's democratic countries are advancing along the road of socialist construction.

    *Pravda* continued, "Now, the forces of the people of all countries who support peace, democracy, and socialism are growing stronger. Our great socialist motherland is the leading force of the world's democratic camp. Under the banner of the all-conquering Lenin-Stalin thought, more and more millions of laborers in various countries are rising up against capitalism and imperialist war. Lenin's banner inspires us to fight for new victories, and the great Stalin leads us to the supremely blissful realm of radiant communism."

    The editorial in *Izvestia* stated: All progressive humanity attributes the achievements of the Great October Socialist Revolution of 1917 to Lenin. Our Soviet state, our great Soviet people, are confidently advancing along the Leninist path under the leadership of the Bolshevik Party, under the leadership of Comrade Stalin. The Soviet people believe that the wise leadership of the Bolshevik Party is the source of all our victories.

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th: The people of Romania and Bulgaria commemorate the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death. According to the Romanian News Agency: The Romanian-Soviet Friendship Association held a commemoration meeting on the 22nd. The speaker at the meeting recounted Lenin's life and deeds, and an exhibition of photos and Lenin's works was held. Workers at the "Vulcan" factory in Bucharest declared at the meeting: "Lenin's name has become a symbol of the flourishing life of this country." Participants unanimously stated: "The greatest respect for Lenin is to constantly study and apply his brilliant teachings."

    TASS reported: The Bulgarian Workers' Cause newspaper published an editorial entitled "The Sun of Revolutionary Thought and Revolutionary Action" and published excerpts from Dimitrov's essay on Lenin. Bulgarian newspapers all published articles commemorating Lenin.

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th: Comprehensive TASS news: The laboring people of Belgium, the Netherlands, Italy, Finland, and Austria held meetings on the 22nd to commemorate the 26th anniversary of the death of the great Lenin.

    More than 4,000 people in Brussels participated in a memorial meeting hosted by the Belgian-Soviet Friendship Association. After the meeting, the film *Ilyich Lenin* was screened, with representatives of the diplomatic corps, members of parliament, and representatives of various groups participating. Whenever Lenin and Stalin appeared on the screen, there was thunderous applause.

    The laboring people of the Netherlands held Lenin Commemoration meetings in many towns and cities. At the memorial meeting in The Hague, Schalker, a member of the Politburo of the Dutch Communist Party, spoke about Lenin's life and screened a film about Lenin's life. The Dutch Communist Party's newspaper *Truth* published a special article commemorating Lenin.

    The Italian Communist Party held city-wide meetings of party activists and branch meetings in various cities to commemorate Lenin and to celebrate the 29th anniversary of the founding of the Italian Communist Party.

    The Finnish-Soviet Friendship Association held a Lenin Commemoration meeting in Helsinki, with President Paasikivi, cabinet ministers, and diplomatic personnel from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, and other people's democratic countries participating. In his speech, Association Chairman Kil stressed that Lenin often safeguarded Finland's freedom during his lifetime and was supported by the Finnish working class.

    At the memorial meeting in Vienna, Malik, a member of the Central Committee of the Austrian Communist Party, reported on Lenin's life and Leninism. Major factories in Austria also held memorial ceremonies.

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 25th: TASS reports: A mass meeting commemorating the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death, hosted by the Delhi Standing Committee for Peace, various trade unions, and the Delhi Students' Union, has been held in Delhi. Speakers at the meeting expressed the Indian people's great love for Lenin and Stalin, the successor to Lenin's cause. The speakers declared that by following the teachings of Lenin and Stalin, the Soviet people had eliminated all systems of exploitation in their country, and that the Soviet Union is now the world's bastion of peace. Thousands of participants sang the praises of Lenin and Stalin, the greatest leaders of the world's working class and laboring people, and praised the Soviet Union, the powerful defender of peace and democracy.

    The Pakistan-Soviet Cultural Association held a mass meeting in Karachi on the 21st to commemorate the 26th anniversary of Lenin's death. Writer Mamdaz reported on Lenin's life at the meeting, and poets recited poems dedicated to the laboring people and praising the achievements of the Soviet Union. The meeting resolved to "maintain closer friendship with the Soviet Union, which defends world peace and democracy." The association also sponsored a photo exhibition commemorating Lenin.

### 全世界劳动人民怀着热爱与希望用各民族语言呼唤着伟大的列宁  罗比等国人民集会纪念

1950-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    全世界劳动人民怀着热爱与希望用各民族语言呼唤着伟大的列宁
    罗比等国人民集会纪念
    【新华社北京二十四日电】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：在伟大的列宁逝世二十六周年纪念日，苏联各报均以极大篇幅刊登纪念文章，报道列宁逝世二十六周年纪念大会的情形，和中、波、捷、匈、保、阿、罗等国纪念列宁的消息。
    真理报社论称：全世界各地的劳动人民怀着热爱与希望，以全世界各民族的语言呼唤着列宁的名字。列宁不朽的思想给一切仍然遭受资本主义奴役的人们，照明了走向自由与幸福的道路。在列宁的旗帜下，苏联人民正建设着共产主义社会，各人民民主国家的劳动人民，正沿着社会主义建设的道路前进。
    真理报继称，现在各国人民拥护和平、民主与社会主义的力量，正在增长壮大。我们伟大的社会主义祖国是世界民主阵营的领导力量。在战胜一切的列宁斯大林思想的旗帜下，各国正有越来越多的千百万劳动者，起来反对资本主义与帝国主义战争。列宁的旗帜鼓舞我们为新的胜利而斗争，伟大的斯大林领导我们走向光芒万丈的共产主义的极乐境界。
    消息报社论称：全体进步人类把一九一七年十月伟大社会主义革命中的成就，归功于列宁。我们的苏维埃国家、我们伟大的苏维埃人民，满怀信心地沿着在布尔什维克党、在斯大林同志领导下的列宁主义道路前进。苏联人民认为布尔什维克党的英明领导，是我们一切胜利的源泉。
    【新华社北京二十四日电】罗马尼亚、保加利亚人民纪念列宁逝世二十六周年。据罗马尼亚通讯社讯：罗苏友好协会二十二日举行纪念大会，报告人在会上讲述列宁的生平事迹，并举行照片和列宁遗作展览。布加勒斯特“火神”制造厂的工人在集会上宣称：“列宁的名字已成了这个国家欣欣向荣的生活的象征”，与会者一致表示：“对列宁最大的敬意，就是经常学习和运用他的英明的训示。”
    塔斯社讯：保共工人事业报以“革命思想与革命行动的太阳”为题发表社论，并刊出季米特洛夫关于列宁的论文的摘录。保国各报均刊登纪念列宁的文章。
    【新华社北京二十四日电】综合塔斯社消息：比、荷、意、芬、奥各国劳动人民，于二十二日纷纷举行集会，纪念伟大的列宁逝世二十六周年。
    比京有四千余人参加在比苏友好协会主持下的纪念会。会后放映电影《伊里奇·列宁》，参加者有外交团代表、国会议员及各团体代表。每当列宁和斯大林出现在银幕上时，掌声如雷。
    荷兰劳动人民在许多城镇举行纪念列宁大会。在海牙的纪念会上由荷共政治局委员夏尔克讲述列宁生平，并放映关于列宁生平的电影。荷共机关报《真理》特载纪念列宁的论文。
    意共在各城市举行全城规模的党的积极分子和支部会议，纪念列宁，同时庆祝意共建党二十九周年。
    芬苏友好协会在芬京举行纪念列宁大会，与会者有总统巴锡基维，各部部长和苏联及捷克斯洛伐克等人民民主国家外交人员。协会主席基尔在演说中着重指出，列宁生前经常维护芬兰的自由，并得到芬兰工人阶级的拥戴。
    在维也纳纪念会上，奥共中委马利克报告列宁生平和列宁主义。奥国各大工厂也举行了纪念仪式。
    【新华社北京二十五日电】塔斯社讯：由德里和平常设委员会，各职工会及德里学生联合会主持的纪念列宁逝世二十六周年群众大会，已在德里召开。大会讲演者表示了印度人民对列宁及列宁事业的继承者——斯大林的极大爱戴。讲演者宣称：由于遵行列宁与斯大林的教义，苏联人民已在他们的国内消灭了一切剥削制度，苏联目前是全世界人民的和平堡垒。数以千计的与会者，歌颂世界工人阶级和劳动人民最伟大的领袖列宁与斯大林，并歌颂和平与民主的强大保卫者——苏联。
    巴基斯坦——苏联文化协会二十一日在喀喇蚩召开纪念列宁逝世二十六周年的群众大会。作家马姆达滋在会上报告了列宁的生平事迹，诗人们朗诵献给劳动人们及颂扬苏联成就的诗篇。大会决议要“与保卫世界和平民主的苏联保持更亲密的友谊”。该会并主办了纪念列宁的照片展览会。
