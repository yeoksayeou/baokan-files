Title: Chairman Mao Returns to Moscow; Gromyko, Roshchin, and Others Greet Him at the Station
Author:
Date: 1950-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 18th: TASS Moscow Dispatch: On January 17th, Mr. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, along with Mr. Wang Jiaxiang, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and Professor Chen Boda, returned to Moscow from Leningrad.

    Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Gromyko, Soviet Ambassador to the People's Republic of China Roshchin, Acting Director of the First Far Eastern Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry Kurdyukov, Acting Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry Bushuyev, and personnel from the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Moscow went to the station to welcome Mr. Mao Zedong.

    The national flags of the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union were hung at the station.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 18th: TASS Moscow, January 17th Dispatch: All newspapers published reports on Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China visiting Leningrad on January 15th and 16th. Newspapers also published news from the People's Republic of China reporting that the Chinese people demanded the immediate expulsion of the representatives of the Kuomintang clique from the Security Council. Newspapers also reported that representatives from the Soviet Union and the People's Democratic countries had left various committees of the United Nations in protest against the illegal attendance of representatives of the Kuomintang clique in these committees.

### 毛主席返抵莫斯科  葛罗米柯罗申等赴车站欢迎

1950-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席返抵莫斯科
    葛罗米柯罗申等赴车站欢迎
    【新华社北京十八日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：一月十七日，中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生，偕中华人民共和国驻苏联大使王稼祥先生及陈伯达教授自列宁格勒返抵莫斯科。
    苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯、苏联驻中华人民共和国大使罗申、苏联外交部第一远东司代理司长库尔久科夫、苏联外交部交际司代理司长布舒也夫和中华人民共和国驻莫斯科大使馆的人员，均前往车站欢迎毛泽东先生。
    车站悬挂着中华人民共和国的国旗和苏联国旗。
    【新华社北京十八日电】塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：所有报纸都发表了报道中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席在一月十五、十六两日访问列宁格勒的消息。报纸又刊出来自中华人民共和国的消息，报道中国人民要求立即将国民党集团的代表开除出安理会。报纸也报道了苏联及人民民主国家的代表纷纷离开联合国各委员会，以抗议国民党集团代表之非法出席这些委员会。
