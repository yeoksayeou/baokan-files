Title: Secretariat of the National Front of Democratic Germany to be Established
Author:
Date: 1950-01-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 13th (TASS dispatch): The Secretariat of the German People's Congress, based on the declaration of the Third German People's Congress, which was produced by universal suffrage, and the declaration of the German People's Council regarding the National Front, has resolved to establish a secretariat for the National Front of Democratic Germany. The resolution points out that this measure is in accordance with the requirements of the National Front Committee of Democratic Germany. The state, district, and local committees of the National Front of Democratic Germany will make every effort to build a peace-loving, democratic Germany.

### 民主德国全国阵线书记处将成立

1950-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国全国阵线书记处将成立
    【新华社北京十四日电】塔斯社柏林十三日讯：德国人民代表大会书记处，根据由普选产生的第三届德国人民代表大会的宣言及德国人民委员会关于全国阵线的宣言，决议成立民主德国全国阵线书记处。决议指出：这一措施符合民主德国全国阵线委员会的要求。各州、区、地方民主德国全国阵线委员会，将用一切努力建立爱好和平的民主德国。
