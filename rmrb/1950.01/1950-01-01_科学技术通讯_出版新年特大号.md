Title: Science and Technology Newsletter Publishes New Year's Special Issue
Author:
Date: 1950-01-01
Page: 3

The *Science and Technology Newsletter* has published a special New Year's issue. The contents include a selection of New Year's greetings sent to Chairman Mao by worker readers from various regions.
