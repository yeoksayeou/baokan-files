Title: Premier Zhou's Arrival in Moscow: Prominently Published in Soviet Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1950-01-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 22nd: TASS, Moscow, January 21st: All newspapers prominently reported the news of Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, arriving in Moscow, and published Zhou Enlai's speech upon arrival in Moscow.

### 周总理抵苏京消息  苏联各报显著刊载

1950-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理抵苏京消息  苏联各报显著刊载
    【新华社北京二十二日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十一日讯：所有一切报纸均以显著地位报道中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外长周恩来抵达莫斯科的消息，并刊登周恩来在抵达莫斯科时所发表的演说。
