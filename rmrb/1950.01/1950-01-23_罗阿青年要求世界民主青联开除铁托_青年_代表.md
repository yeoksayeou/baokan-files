Title: Romanian and Albanian Youth Demand World Federation of Democratic Youth Expel Tito's "Youth" Representatives
Author:
Date: 1950-01-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd: Romanian and Albanian labor youth have successively demanded that the executive committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, currently meeting in Bucharest, expel the representatives of the so-called "Yugoslav People's Youth."

According to TASS, Bucharest: Romanian labor youth, currently holding mass meetings in factories, government offices, and educational institutions across the country, unanimously believe that the "Yugoslav People's Youth" is a tool in the hands of the fascist Tito-Ranković clique, and they demand that the World Federation of Democratic Youth expel them.

According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, Tirana: The Central Committee of the Albanian Youth League has sent a telegram to the executive committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, demanding the expulsion of the representatives of the "Yugoslav People's Youth." The telegram points out: "Washing these lackeys of imperialism out of the World Federation of Democratic Youth will help to further strengthen the international youth movement fighting for peace, led by the Soviet youth."

### 罗阿青年要求世界民主青联开除铁托“青年”代表

1950-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

    罗阿青年要求世界民主青联开除铁托“青年”代表
    【新华社北京二十二日电】罗马尼亚和阿尔巴尼亚的劳动青年，纷纷要求现在布加勒斯特开会的世界民主青年联盟执委会，开除所谓“南斯拉夫人民青年”的代表。
    据塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚的劳动青年，刻在全国各地工厂、机关及教育机关举行的群众大会上，一致认为“南斯拉夫人民青年”是法西斯铁托—兰科维奇集团手中的工具，他们要求世界民主青联把它开除出去。
    又据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚青年联盟中央委员会顷致电世界民主青联执委会，要求开除“南斯拉夫人民青年”的代表。电文指出：“把这批帝国主义的走狗从世界民主青联中清洗出去，将有助于进一步加强苏联青年所领导的为和平而斗争的国际青年运动。”
