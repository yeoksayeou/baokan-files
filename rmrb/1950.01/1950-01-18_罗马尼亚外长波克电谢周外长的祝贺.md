Title: Romanian Foreign Minister Pauker Cables Thanks to Foreign Minister Zhou for Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1950-01-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th—Romanian People's Republic Foreign Minister Anna Pauker sent a cable to thank Chinese Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai for his congratulations on the second anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic on December 30th of last year. The text of the cable is as follows:

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    I express my warm gratitude for Your Excellency's congratulations and wishes to the Romanian people and government on the second anniversary of the proclamation of the Romanian People's Republic.

                    Anna Pauker
                    Foreign Minister of the Romanian People's Republic

### 罗马尼亚外长波克电谢周外长的祝贺

1950-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚外长波克电谢周外长的祝贺
    【新华社北京十六日电】罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长安娜·波克电谢我国外交部周恩来部长在去年十二月三十日对罗马尼亚人民共和国成立二周年的祝贺。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来阁下：
    我对阁下在罗马尼亚人民共和国宣告成立第二周年时，对罗马尼亚人民与政府的祝贺与愿望，谨表示热烈的感谢。
                    罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长  安娜·波克
