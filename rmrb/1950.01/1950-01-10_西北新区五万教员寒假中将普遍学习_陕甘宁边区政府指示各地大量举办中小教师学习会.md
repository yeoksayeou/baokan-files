Title: Northwest New Area's 50,000 Teachers to Receive Universal Training During Winter Vacation; Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government Directs Various Regions to Hold Numerous Primary and Secondary Teacher Training Sessions
Author:
Date: 1950-01-10
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 7th. The Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government, in order to utilize the schools' winter vacation to reform and improve the political ideology of teachers in newly liberated areas, instructed various regions on the 5th to focus on holding primary and secondary school teacher training sessions. This is to universally provide revolutionary education to the more than 50,000 teaching staff in the more than 30,000 old primary and secondary schools in the Northwest newly liberated areas (excluding Xinjiang). The instructions, based on the specific conditions of each region, stipulated that Xi'an and Lanzhou cities, the Gansu Administrative Office, Qinghai Provincial Government, Ningxia Provincial Government, Shaanan Administrative Office, Yulin Administrative Office, and various districts in Shaanxi and Gansu provinces, should separately hold middle school and primary school teacher training sessions. The instructions also stipulated that teacher enrollment should be based on the principle of voluntarism, with the content of study divided into political and professional aspects. The duration of study should be three to four weeks, with the ratio of time spent on political study to professional study being three to one. The organizing agencies must send capable cadres to preside over this work.

### 西北新区五万教员寒假中将普遍学习  陕甘宁边区政府指示各地大量举办中小教师学习会

1950-01-10
第3版()
专栏：

    西北新区五万教员寒假中将普遍学习
    陕甘宁边区政府指示各地大量举办中小教师学习会
    【新华社西安七日电】陕甘宁边区政府为利用学校的寒假，改造和提高新解放区教师的政治思想，于五日指示各地有重点地举办中小学教师学习会，以便对西北新解放区（新疆除外）旧有中小学三万多所的教职员五万多人，普遍地施以革命教育。指示根据各地具体情况，规定西安、兰州二市，甘肃行署、青海省府、宁夏省府、陕南行署、榆林专署以及陕甘两省各分区，分别举办中学和小学的教师学习会。指示并规定教师入学以自愿为原则，学习内容，分政治和业务两方面；学习时间为三周到四周，政治学习与业务学习所占时间为三与一之比，各主办机关须派有力干部主持这一工作。
