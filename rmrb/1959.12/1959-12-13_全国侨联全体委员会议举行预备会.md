Title: National Overseas Chinese Federation Holds Preparatory Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-12-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 12th. The first session, fourth plenary (enlarged) meeting of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese began its preparatory meeting in Shanghai on the 10th.

Attending the preparatory meeting were Vice Chairmen of the National Overseas Chinese Federation: Fang Fang, You Yangzu, Wang Yuanxing, Zhuang Xiquan, Zhuang Mingli, Luo Lishi, Huang Changshui, and Yi Meihou.

Also attending the preparatory meeting were standing committee members, committee members, and representatives of advanced figures from returned overseas Chinese and relatives of overseas Chinese from all walks of life. (Attached picture)

The returned overseas Chinese attending the National Overseas Chinese Federation meeting support Foreign Minister Chen Yi's serious protest against Indonesia's anti-Chinese and discriminatory activities and his three proposals for resolving the issue of overseas Chinese. This is Huang Zenghui, an Indonesian returned overseas Chinese student at the Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel, speaking at the meeting. Sun Zhihe (telephoto).

### 全国侨联全体委员会议举行预备会

1959-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    全国侨联全体委员会议举行预备会
    新华社上海12日电　中华全国归国华侨联合会一届四次全体委员（扩大）会议，10日起在上海举行预备会议。
    参加预备会议的，有全国侨联副主席方方、尤扬祖、王源兴、庄希泉、庄明理、罗理实、黄长水、蚁美厚。
    参加预备会议的，还有常务委员、委员和归侨、侨眷中各方面的先进人物代表。（附图片）
    出席全国侨联会议的归国华侨，支持陈毅外长对印度尼西亚反华排华活动所提出的严重抗议和解决华侨问题的三点建议。这是印度尼西亚归侨、北京钢铁学院学生黄增辉在会上发言。　孙智和摄（传真照片）
