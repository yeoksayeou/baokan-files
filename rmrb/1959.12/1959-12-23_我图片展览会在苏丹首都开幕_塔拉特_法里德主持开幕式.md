Title: Chinese Photo Exhibition Opens in Sudanese Capital; Talat Farid Presides Over Opening Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1959-12-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, December 22nd: A Chinese Photo Exhibition of the People's Republic of China opened in Khartoum, the capital of Sudan, on the afternoon of the 20th. Major General Talat Farid, member of the Supreme Council of the Sudanese Armed Forces and Sudanese Minister of Information and Labor, presided over the opening ceremony of the exhibition. He visited the exhibition in the company of Wang Yutian, the Chinese Ambassador to Sudan. Major General Farid praised the achievements made by the People's Republic of China in the ten years since its founding, as demonstrated by the photos.
Among the two hundred guests attending the opening ceremony were also Sudanese Minister of Finance Ali Abdul Ahmed, Minister of Health Mohammed Ahmed Abu and other senior government officials, prominent figures from economic, journalistic, cultural and artistic circles, diplomatic envoys and cultural counselors from various countries stationed in Khartoum, and others.
The exhibition is sponsored by the Chinese Embassy in Sudan and will last for twelve days.

### 我图片展览会在苏丹首都开幕  塔拉特·法里德主持开幕式

1959-12-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　我图片展览会在苏丹首都开幕
    塔拉特·法里德主持开幕式
    新华社22日讯  喀土穆消息：中华人民共和国图片展览会20日下午在苏丹首都喀土穆开幕。苏丹武装部队最高委员会委员、苏丹宣传和劳工部长塔拉特·法里德少将主持了展览会的开幕式。他在中国驻苏丹大使王雨田陪同下参观了展览会。法里德少将对图片显示出来的中华人民共和国成立十年以来所获得的成就表示赞扬。
    参加开幕仪式的两百位客人中还有苏丹财政部长阿里杜勒·艾哈迈德，卫生部长穆罕默德·艾哈迈德·阿卜和其他政府高级官员，经济界、报界、文化和艺术界的著名人士，各国驻喀土穆的外交使节和文化参赞等。
    这次展览会是由中国驻苏丹大使馆主办的。展览会将举行十二天。
