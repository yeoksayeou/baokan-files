Title: Singing of Construction Sites and Factories
Author: 
Date: 1959-12-20
Page: 1
Construction sites and factories are truly good,
They produce dozens of machines.
Clumsy tools are all discarded,
Saving manpower and increasing efficiency.
	Discard the dull hoe,
	Shovel soil with a straight back;
	Discard the hard carrying pole,
	Run alongside the cart;
	Discard the clumsy stone rammer,
	Happily operate the machine with your feet.
	(Originally published in Hubei Daily)

### 歌唱工地工厂

1959-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    歌唱工地工厂
  工地工厂真正好，
  造出机器几十套。
  笨重工具都丢掉，
  省人省力工效高。
  丢掉钝挖锄，
  铲土直着腰；
  丢掉硬扁担，
  跟着车子跑；
  丢掉笨石硪，
  脚踩机器乐逍遥。
      （原载湖北日报）
