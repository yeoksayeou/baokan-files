Title: Rockefeller Again Advocates Military Strength
Author:
Date: 1959-12-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 19th (New York): Some important figures in the American ruling class continue to issue calls for a "Cold War" and adherence to a "position of strength" policy.

The tycoon of monopoly capital, New York Governor Rockefeller, in a speech in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, on the 15th, clamored that the Soviet Union and China are America's "most serious and most terrible threats." He said that the United States must possess "maximum military power" and that "only from this position of strength can negotiations be conducted with the Communists."

Rockefeller is conducting a so-called "understanding the people's hearts" tour to prepare for his presidential campaign next year. He also advocated that while the United States pursues a "position of strength" policy, it should adopt some deceptive new methods to make the United States a "welcome guest."

On the same day, CIA Director Allen Dulles, in a speech at the "New York Salvation Army Association" annual meeting, advocated that the United States "resolutely maintain deterrents and military strength." He also stated that the United States cannot tolerate the people of Eastern European countries choosing the socialist path.

### 洛克菲勒又鼓吹军事实力

1959-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    洛克菲勒又鼓吹军事实力
    新华社19日讯　纽约消息：美国统治集团中的一些重要人物继续发出“冷战”的和坚持“实力地位”政策的叫嚣。
    垄断资本巨擘、纽约州州长洛克菲勒15日在威斯康星州密尔窝基城发表演说时，叫嚣苏联和中国是美国的“最严重和最可怕的威胁”。他说，美国必须具备“最大限度的军事威力”，“只有从这种实力地位出发，才能同共产党进行谈判。”
    洛克菲勒正在进行所谓“了解人心”的旅行，来为他明年参加总统竞选进行准备。他还鼓吹美国在推行“实力地位”政策的同时，要采取一些欺骗性的新作法，使美国成为一个“受欢迎的客人”。
    美国中央情报局局长艾伦·杜勒斯同一天在“纽约救世军协会”年会上发表演说时，主张美国“坚决保持威慑手段和军事实力”。他还表示，美国不能容忍东欧国家的人民选择社会主义道路。
