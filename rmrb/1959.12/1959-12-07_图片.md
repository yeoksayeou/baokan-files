    The residents of Xiaoying Lane in Hangzhou, which Chairman Mao visited, have created a new park covering ten mu (approximately 1.65 acres) on the basis of improving environmental sanitation, transforming the original breeding ground for mosquitoes and flies into a beautiful garden.
