Title: Chilean Railway Workers' Union Rally Grandly Welcomes Our Trade Union Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-12-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, December 3rd: The leading committee of the Chilean Railway Workers' Union held a rally yesterday to warmly welcome the Chinese trade union delegation, which was invited to attend the Second National Congress of the Chilean Workers' United Trade Union.

### 智利铁路工人联合会集会  隆重欢迎我工会代表团

1959-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    智利铁路工人联合会集会
    隆重欢迎我工会代表团
    据新华社圣地亚哥3日电　智利铁路工人联合会领导委员会昨天举行集会，隆重欢迎应邀参加智利工人统一工会第二次全国代表大会的中国工会代表团。
