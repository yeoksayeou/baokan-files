Rockefeller, Merchant of Death
Yuan Shuipai Poem, Hua Junwu Cartoon

　　Missile profits rise higher and higher,
　　Missile merchants laugh in their hearts,
　　Eagerly awaiting the early arrival of a new war!
　　Once there was a Hitler,
　　Who wanted to paint the Earth black,
　　Playing with fire and burning himself in the basement!
　　Now there is you, Rockefeller,
　　When you launch a war,
　　That will be the day capitalism goes to its grave!

### 死亡贩子洛克菲勒
袁水拍　华君武
1959-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

    死亡贩子洛克菲勒
    袁水拍诗　华君武画导弹利润节节高，导弹商人心里笑，巴不得新战争早来到！从前有一个希特勒，想把地球涂成黑色，玩火自焚地下室！如今有你这洛克菲勒，当你发动战争之时，也就是资本主义归天之日！（附图片）
