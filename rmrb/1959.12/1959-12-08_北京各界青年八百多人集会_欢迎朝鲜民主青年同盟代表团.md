Title: More Than 800 Youths from All Circles in Beijing Gather to Welcome the Delegation of the Korean Democratic Youth League
Author:
Date: 1959-12-08
Page: 4

Beijing, December 7th (News Report) More than 800 youths from all walks of life in Beijing gathered tonight to welcome the delegation of the Korean Democratic Youth League headed by Choi Chan-joon, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League.

Hu Keshi and Liang Buting, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, attended the welcome meeting.

Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and Choi Chan-joon, head of the Korean Democratic Youth League delegation, spoke at the meeting. Wang Zhaohua and Choi Chan-joon both pointed out that U.S. imperialism is the common enemy of the people of China and Korea, and U.S. imperialism must get out of Taiwan and South Korea! The youths of China and Korea, united in the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, will advance hand in hand forever! At the meeting, Wang Zhaohua introduced the achievements of Beijing youth in continuing the Great Leap Forward, and expressed his infinite excitement about the "Chollima Work Team" movement currently being vigorously carried out by the youth of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to "work, study, and produce in the spirit of communism." He said that he firmly believes that the friendship of the broad masses of Korean youth brought by the delegation will encourage the youth of Beijing to advance even more bravely in the continued Great Leap Forward. Choi Chan-joon introduced the significant contributions of the youth of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in changing the face of the country. At the end of his speech, he shouted "Long live the unbreakable friendship between the youths of Korea and China!"

Afterwards, the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, the Beijing Municipal Committee, and the Korean Democratic Youth League delegation exchanged gifts. After the meeting, they watched the movie "Nameless Island" together.

The Korean Democratic Youth League delegation came to our country for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League. The delegation of seven people arrived in Beijing on the 3rd of this month. They will visit Beijing, Wuhan, Changsha, Guangzhou, Shanghai, Jilin and other places. (Attached photo)
When the delegation of the Korean Democratic Youth League entered the venue, they were warmly welcomed by the youth of Beijing. On the front right is Choi Chan-joon, Vice Chairman of the Korean Democratic Youth League.

### 北京各界青年八百多人集会  欢迎朝鲜民主青年同盟代表团

1959-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    北京各界青年八百多人集会
    欢迎朝鲜民主青年同盟代表团
    本报讯　北京各界青年八百多人今晚集会欢迎以朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会副委员长崔缵浚为首的朝鲜民主青年同盟代表团。
    中国共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡克实、梁步庭参加了欢迎会。
    中国共产主义青年团北京市委员会第一书记王照华和朝鲜民主青年同盟代表团团长崔缵浚在会上讲了话。王照华和崔缵浚都指出美帝国主义是中朝两国人民共同的敌人，美帝国主义一定要从台湾和南朝鲜滚出去！团结在以苏联为首的社会主义阵营中的中朝两国青年将永远携手前进！王照华在会上介绍了北京青年继续跃进的事迹，并对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国青年目前正在蓬勃开展的“按共产主义精神工作、学习、生产”的“千里马作业班”运动表示无限兴奋。他说，他深信，由代表团带来的朝鲜广大青年的友谊，将鼓舞北京的青年在继续跃进中更加奋勇前进。崔缵浚在会上介绍了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国青年在改变国家面貌中的重大贡献。他在讲话的最后高呼朝、中两国青年牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    接着，中国共产主义青年团中央委员会、北京市委员会和朝鲜民主青年同盟代表团互相赠送了礼品。会后一起看了电影“无名岛”。
    朝鲜民主青年同盟代表团是应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会的邀请来我国进行友好访问的。代表团一行七人，于本月3日到达北京。他们将在北京、武汉、长沙、广州、上海、吉林等地参观访问。（附图片）
    朝鲜民主青年同盟代表团进入会场时，受到北京青年的热烈欢迎。前右是朝鲜民主青年同盟副委员长崔缵浚
          　新华社记者　塔吉古勒摄
