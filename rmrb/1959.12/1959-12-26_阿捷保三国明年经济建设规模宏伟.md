Title: Albania, Czechoslovakia, and Bulgaria Plan Ambitious Economic Construction for Next Year
Author:
Date: 1959-12-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 24th: The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party held a plenary meeting on December 22nd and 23rd. The meeting was chaired by First Secretary of the Central Committee, Hoxha. The plenary session heard and discussed the report by Chairman of the Council of Ministers and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, Shehu, on the implementation of the national budget and national plan for 1956-1959, as well as the national plan and national budget for 1960. The plenary session unanimously adopted the aforementioned report and made corresponding resolutions.

According to the plenary session's statistics, the total industrial output value for 1956-1959 completed 95.9 billion leks (Albanian currency), that is, 100% completed the expansion plan stipulated by the Party. The 1959 industrial production plan is expected to be completed at 101%. The total industrial output value for 1960 is projected to be 34.214 billion leks, an increase of 11% compared to 1959.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 24th: The fourth session of the fourth Albanian People's Assembly opened in Tirana at 10:00 am today.

The meeting began today with the first item on the agenda. The Vice-Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers and Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Tiodosi, delivered a report at the morning session on Albania's 1960 national economic development plan. Tiodosi said that Albania's industrial output value this year will complete the annual plan at 101%, an increase of 18% compared to last year; agricultural output value will increase by 27% compared to last year. Currently, land cultivated by agricultural cooperatives accounts for 82% of the country's total cultivated land area.

Tiodosi pointed out that in 1960, Albania's total industrial output value will increase by 11% compared to 1959; among them, the production of means of production and labor productivity will both increase by 12%, and the production of consumer goods will increase by 10%. This means that the Second Five-Year Plan (1956-1960), which was revised and expanded last year, will be completed half a year ahead of schedule in four and a half years.

He also pointed out that in 1960, agricultural output value will increase by 33% compared to this year; the sown area will increase by 11%. Albania plans to reclaim 33,000 hectares of new land in the coming year.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 24th: According to Czechoslovak newspapers: According to the draft national plan discussed and approved by the Czechoslovak government at its meeting on December 21st, Czechoslovakia's total industrial output value will increase by 10.2% next year.

The draft plan stipulates that the production of means of production should grow faster than the production of consumer goods. Next year, the output of the machinery industry will increase by 14%, and the proportion of the machinery industry in the total industrial output value will reach more than 30%. The output of the metallurgical industry will increase by 13%. The output of the chemical industry will increase by 12.2%, and the proportion of machinery and equipment in industrial construction will reach 53%.

Due to the great enthusiasm shown by workers in discussing the 1960 targets of various industrial enterprises and the specific guarantees put forward, the total industrial output value stipulated in the 1960 draft national plan was raised by 2.242 billion crowns, or 1.3%, compared to the original instructions.

The Czechoslovak government also discussed and approved the 1960 draft national budget at this meeting. The total revenue in the draft budget will reach 103.6 billion crowns, an increase of 7.4 billion crowns compared to this year.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia: Bulgarian Minister of Finance Lazarov delivered a report on the draft national budget for 1960 at a meeting of the National Assembly on the 21st. Lazarov said that the total revenue of the 1960 national budget will be 31.2473 billion leva, and the total expenditure will be 30.8269 billion leva.

He pointed out that more than 90% of the total budget revenue will come from the socialist economy. In the total budget expenditure for next year, appropriations for developing the national economy will reach 19.995 billion leva, and appropriations for social and cultural facilities will reach 6.605 billion leva, which together account for more than 87% of the total budget expenditure. The country's administrative expenses will be 702 million leva, and defense expenses will account for 5.8% of the total expenditure.

He said that in the budget expenditure for developing the national economy, the appropriation for industry is 4.1 billion leva (1 billion leva more than this year); the investment in capital construction is 5.6 billion leva (1.5 billion leva more than this year); and the appropriation for agriculture is 3.3 billion leva (500 million leva more than this year).

### 阿捷保三国明年经济建设规模宏伟

1959-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　阿捷保三国明年经济建设规模宏伟
　　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央全会通过决议，明年工业产值增长11％
　　捷克斯洛伐克制定明年发展经济计划，工业产值将增长10.2％
　　保加利亚财长在议会提出明年预算，工农业的拨款占主要地位
    新华社地拉那24日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会在12月22日和23日举行了全体会议。这次会议是由中央委员会第一书记霍查主持的。全会听取和讨论了部长会议主席、中央政治局委员谢胡所作的关于1956至1959年国家预算和国家计划的执行情况以及1960年国家计划和国家预算的报告。全会一致通过了上述报告并作出了相应的决议。
    根据全会的统计，1956至1959年的工业总产值完成了九百五十九亿里克（阿币名），即百分之百地完成了党所规定的扩大计划。1959年的工业生产计划预计将完成101％。1960年工业总产值预计将为三百四十二亿一千四百万里克，将比1959年增加11％。
    据新华社地拉那24日电　阿尔巴尼亚第四届人民议会第四次会议，24日上午十时在地拉那开幕。
    会议今天开始进行第一项议程。阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席、国家计划委员会主席蒂奥多西在上午会议上就阿尔巴尼亚1960年发展国民经济计划作了报告。蒂奥多西说，阿尔巴尼亚今年的工业产值将以101％完成年度计划，比去年增长18％；农业产值将比去年增长27％。目前，农业合作社耕种的土地已占全国耕地面积的82％。
    蒂奥多西指出，1960年阿尔巴尼亚的工业总产值将比1959年增长11％；其中生产资料的生产和劳动生产率都将增长12％，消费资料的生产将增长10％。这就是说，经过去年修订扩大的第二个五年计划（1956—1960），将提前半年以四年半的时间完成。
    他又指出，1960年农业产值将比今年增长33％；播种面积将增加11％。阿尔巴尼亚计划在明年一年开垦新地三万三千公顷。
    据新华社布拉格24日电　据捷克斯洛伐克报纸报道：根据捷克斯洛伐克政府在12月21日的会议上讨论和通过的国家计划草案，捷克斯洛伐克明年的工业总产值将增加10.2％。
    计划草案规定，生产资料生产要比消费品生产增长更快。明年机械工业的产量将增加14％，机械工业在工业总产值中所占的比例将达到30％以上。冶金工业产量将增加13％。化学工业的产量将增加12.2％，机器和装备在工业建设中的比例将达53％。
    由于工人在讨论各工业企业的1960年指标中所表现的巨大积极性和提出的具体保证，1960年国家计划草案规定的工业总产值比原来的指示提高了二十二亿四千二百万克朗，即提高1.3％。
    捷克斯洛伐克政府在这次会上还讨论和通过了1960年国家预算草案。预算草案中的总收入将达一千零三十六亿克朗，比今年增加七十四亿克朗。
    据新华社索非亚电　保加利亚财政部长拉扎罗夫21日在国民议会会议上作了关于1960年国家预算草案的报告。拉扎罗夫说，1960年国家预算总收入将为三百一十二亿四千七百三十万列弗，总支出为三百零八亿二千六百九十万列弗。
    他指出，预算总收入的90％以上将来自社会主义经济。在明年的预算总支出中，发展国民经济的拨款将达一百九十九亿九千五百万列弗，社会和文化设施的拨款将达六十六亿零五百万列弗，两者共占预算总支出的87％以上。国家的行政经费将为七亿零二百万列弗，国防费用将占总支出的5.8％。
    他说，在发展国民经济的预算支出中，用于工业的拨款是四十一亿列弗（比今年多十亿列弗）；基本建设投资是五十六亿列弗（比今年多十五亿列弗）；农业拨款是三十三亿列弗（比今年多五亿列弗）。
