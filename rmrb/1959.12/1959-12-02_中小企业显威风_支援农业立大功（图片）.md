Title: Small and Medium Enterprises Show Great Power, Making Great Contributions to Support Agriculture (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1959-12-02
Page: 2

Small and Medium Enterprises Show Great Power
Making Great Contributions to Support Agriculture

    1. Staff and workers of the Xuchang Comprehensive Chemical Factory in Henan Province, using indigenous methods, have built and are building a batch of superphosphate fertilizer furnaces and defluorinated phosphate fertilizer furnaces, actively producing chemical fertilizers needed for local agriculture.
                                Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Qingyun photo

    2. Wuhan General Machinery Factory produced a large number of small-sized universal tractors to support agricultural production. This is the workers inspecting the products. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Chengjian photo

    3. A large number of pesticides produced by the Zhengzhou Pesticide Factory are ready to be shipped out. Photo by Sha Sha (Xinhua News Agency draft)

    4. The farm tool factory of the Chengdong People's Commune in Jiading County, Shanghai, has manufactured a power thresher. Commune members have already used this machinery, and the work efficiency is more than ten times higher than manual threshing.
                                Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhao Cheng photo

### 中小企业显威风  支援农业立大功（图片）

1959-12-02
第2版()
专栏：

    中小企业显威风
    支援农业立大功
    1．河南省许昌综合性化学工厂职工，用土法建成和正在建设一批过氟磷肥炉及脱氟磷肥炉，积极生产当地农业需要的化肥。
                                新华社记者　张青云摄
    2．武汉通用机器厂生产了大批小型万能拖拉机支援农业生产。这是工人们在检查产品。　新华社记者　于澄建摄
    3．郑州农药厂生产的大批农药准备外运。　沙莎摄（新华社稿）
    4．上海嘉定县城东人民公社的农具厂制造出了动力脱粒机。社员们已使用这种机械，工作效率比人工脱粒高十倍以上。
                                 新华社记者　赵成摄
