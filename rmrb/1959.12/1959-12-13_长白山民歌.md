Changbai Mountain Folk Songs

　　Stepping onto Changbai Mountain

　　Stepping onto Changbai Mountain,
　　Making my home deep in the white clouds,
　　Raising high the mountain-splitting axe,
　　Waving the mountain-driving whip,
　　Scaring the Mountain God into offering treasures,
　　Making the people of the mountain area beam with joy.

　　Thousands of Red Flowers from a Single Root

　　Thousands of red flowers from a single root,
　　Ten thousand people sing with a single voice,
　　The people sing of Chairman Mao,
　　Chairman Mao's kindness is deeper than the sea.

### 长白山民歌

1959-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

    长白山民歌
    一步跨上长白山
    一步跨上长白山，
    白云深处把家安，
    高举劈山斧，
    挥动赶山鞭，
    吓的山神献百宝，
    山区人民乐开颜。
    千朵红花一个根
    千朵红花一个根，
    万民歌唱一个音，
    人民歌唱毛主席，
    毛主席恩情比海深。
