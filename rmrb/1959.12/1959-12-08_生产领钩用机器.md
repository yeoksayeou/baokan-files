Title: Machine for Producing Collar Hooks
Author:
Date: 1959-12-08
Page: 2

    Machine for Producing Collar Hooks
    Workers at the Dadong No. 2 Hardware Products Factory in Shenyang, which produces small commodities, have successfully trial-manufactured a machine for producing collar hooks based on the principle of a stapler. This machine is easy to use and requires only one person to operate. Its production efficiency is more than 100 times higher than manual operation.

### 生产领钩用机器

1959-12-08
第2版()
专栏：

    生产领钩用机器
    沈阳市生产小商品的大东第二五金制品厂工人，根据订书机的原理试制成功一台生产领钩的机器。这台机器，使用灵便，只需一人操纵，生产效率比手工操作提高一百倍以上。
