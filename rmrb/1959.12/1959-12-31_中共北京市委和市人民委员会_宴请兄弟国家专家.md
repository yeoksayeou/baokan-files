Title: Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Municipal People's Committee Host Banquet for Experts from Brotherly Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-12-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee held a New Year's reception tonight at the Beijing Hotel, hosting experts from the Soviet Union, the German Democratic Republic, Poland and other countries who came to help with the construction of our capital, as well as their wives and children.

Also invited to the banquet were Antonov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, Wandel, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Furato, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, and Fumin, head of the Soviet economic, cultural and educational experts.

Peng Zhen, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Mayor of Beijing, presided over the banquet.

Present at tonight's banquet were Liu Ren, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Secretaries of the Secretariat Fan Rusheng and Chen Kehan, and Vice Mayors of Beijing Wan Li, Feng Jiping, Wang Kunlun, Cheng Hongyi, Jia Tingsan, and Le Songsheng, as well as heads of various departments of Beijing.

### 中共北京市委和市人民委员会  宴请兄弟国家专家

1959-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

　　中共北京市委和市人民委员会
    宴请兄弟国家专家
    新华社30日讯　中共北京市委员会和北京市人民委员会今晚在北京饭店举行新年联欢宴会，招待前来帮助我国首都建设的苏联、德意志民主共和国、波兰等国家的专家以及他们的夫人、孩子们。
    应邀出席宴会的，还有苏联驻华大使馆临时代办安东诺夫、德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔、波兰驻华大使馆临时代办傅拉托和苏联经济、文教专家负责人符明等。
    中共北京市委第一书记、北京市市长彭真主持了宴会。
    出席今晚宴会的，有中共北京市委第二书记刘仁、书记处书记范儒生、陈克寒，和北京市副市长万里、冯基平、王昆仑、程宏毅、贾庭三、乐松生，以及北京市各部门的负责人。
