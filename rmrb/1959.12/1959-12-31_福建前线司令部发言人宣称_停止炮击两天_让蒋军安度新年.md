Title: Fujian Front Command Spokesperson Announces: Ceasefire for Two Days to Allow Chiang's Troops to Peacefully Celebrate the New Year
Author:
Date: 1959-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 30th—A spokesperson for the Fujian Front Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army announced: In order to allow compatriots and Chiang's soldiers on the islands of Greater Kinmen, Lesser Kinmen, Greater Dadan, and Lesser Dadan to celebrate the New Year quietly and happily, the artillery units of the People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front are ordered to cease shelling from December 31, 1959, to January 1, 1960, as a sign of concern.

### 福建前线司令部发言人宣称  停止炮击两天  让蒋军安度新年

1959-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　福建前线司令部发言人宣称
    停止炮击两天  让蒋军安度新年
    新华社福建前线30日电　中国人民解放军福建前线司令部发言人宣称：为了使大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的同胞和蒋军官兵，能够安静愉快地欢度新年，福建前线人民解放军炮兵部队，奉命在1959年12月31日至1960年1月1日停止炮击，以示关怀。
