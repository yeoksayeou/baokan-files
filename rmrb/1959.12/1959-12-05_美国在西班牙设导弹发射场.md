Title: U.S. Establishes Missile Launch Site in Spain
Author:
Date: 1959-12-05
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 30th—TASS, Paris, 30th: According to French newspapers, the United States has begun building missile launch sites in the Spanish Pyrenees.

### 美国在西班牙设导弹发射场

1959-12-05
第6版()
专栏：

    美国在西班牙设导弹发射场
    新华社30日讯　塔斯社巴黎30日讯：据法国报纸报道，美国在西班牙比利牛斯山脉开始建立导弹发射场。
