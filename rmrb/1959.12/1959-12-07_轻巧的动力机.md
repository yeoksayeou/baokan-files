Title: Lightweight Power Machine
Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 2

Supporting Agricultural Technological Transformation

    Lightweight Power Machine
    An alcohol-fueled, air-cooled engine, small, light, and suitable for use in the vast rural areas, has been successfully trial-produced at the Shijiazhuang Tractor Factory.
    This engine weighs only eighty-six kilograms, and the total amount of alloy steel used in the entire machine is only over twenty kilograms, saving 75% to 80% of steel compared to a general diesel engine. It has a simple structure and is easy to start. It can be used as fixed power, and it can also be used as a vehicle tractor engine.

### 轻巧的动力机

1959-12-07
第2版()
专栏：支援农业技术改造

    轻巧的动力机
    一种小巧、轻便，适合广大农村使用的酒精风冷发动机，在石家庄市拖拉机厂试制成功。
    这种发动机体重只有八十六公斤，全部机器所用的合金钢材只有二十多公斤，比一般柴油机节约钢材75％到80％。它的构造简单，起动容易，既可作固定动力用，又可作为车辆拖拉机引擎。
