Title: Soviet-British Sign Cultural Exchange Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-12-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, London, December 1st: The talks between the Soviet and British government cultural delegations concluded in London on December 1st. Both sides signed an agreement on cultural, scientific, educational, and technological exchanges between the two countries from April 1, 1960, to April 1, 1961.

The Soviet side attending the signing ceremony included Zhukov, Chairman of the State Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; the British side included Alan, Assistant Under-Secretary of the British Foreign Office.

The cultural exchange agreement stipulates that both sides will exchange scientists and experts in the industrial, transportation, and construction sectors, scientific personnel and professors in agriculture, graduate students, teachers, and medical personnel.

### 苏英签订文化交流协定

1959-12-04
第5版()
专栏：

    苏英签订文化交流协定
    据新华社2日讯　据塔斯社伦敦1日讯：苏联和英国政府文化代表团的会谈1日在伦敦结束，双方签订了一项自1960年4月1日至1961年4月1日两国间文化、科学、教育和技术交流的协定。
    出席协定签字仪式的苏联方面有国家对外文化委员会主席茹科夫；英国方面有英国外交部助理次官艾伦。
    文化交流协定规定双方互派工业、运输业和建筑部门的科学家和专家、农业方面的科学工作人员和教授、研究生、教师和医务人员。
