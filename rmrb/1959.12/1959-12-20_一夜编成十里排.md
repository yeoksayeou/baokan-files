Title: Ten Li of Rafts Woven Overnight
Author:
Date: 1959-12-20
Page: 6

Ten Li of Rafts Woven Overnight
Red lanterns and torches like the sun,
Busy weaving rafts on Dongting Lake,
Ten Li of rafts woven overnight,
Ten thousand giant dragons rush to the Yangtze River.
