Title: Wenzel Believes the German National Question Cannot Be Completely Solved by Foreign Countries; The German Democratic Republic Must Participate in the High-Level Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-12-26
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 23rd: TASS reported that the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Wenzel, in an interview with "Neues Deutschland" reporters, emphasized the necessity of the German Democratic Republic's participation in the highest-level conference. He said that only with the presence of Germans can the highest-level conference discuss the German peace treaty and the West Berlin issue.

    He said that the government of the German Democratic Republic cannot, under any circumstances, completely allow foreign countries to resolve the major national issues of the German people.

    Wenzel emphasized that West Germany's atomic armament is the main obstacle to the unification of Germany and a major danger to peace in Europe. Therefore, the German people hope that the highest-level conference can take measures to stop West Germany's atomic armament. Wenzel said that, according to the vital interests of our nation, we advocate that the highest-level conference, according to the disarmament proposals put forward by Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Khrushchev at the United Nations General Assembly, should find a way to reach a genuine agreement on comprehensive and complete disarmament.

### 文策尔认为德国民族问题不能完全让外国来解决  德意志民主共和国必须参加高级会议

1959-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　文策尔认为德国民族问题不能完全让外国来解决
    德意志民主共和国必须参加高级会议
    新华社24日讯  据塔斯社柏林23日讯：德意志民主共和国外交部第一副部长文策尔在向“新德意志报”记者发表谈话时，强调指出了德意志民主共和国参加最高级会议的必要性。他说，只有德国人在场，最高级会议才能讨论对德和约和西柏林问题。
    他说，德意志民主共和国政府在任何情况下都不能完全让外国来解决德国人民的重大民族问题。
    文策尔着重指出，西德的原子武装是统一德国道路上的主要障碍，是欧洲和平的主要危险。因此，德国人民希望最高级会议能采取措施，制止西德原子武装。文策尔说，根据我们民族的切身利益，我们主张，最高级会议根据苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在联合国大会会议上提出的裁军建议来寻找达成关于全面和彻底的裁军的真正协议的道路。
