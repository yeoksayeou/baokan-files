Title: Nan Hanchen Departs Damascus for Return to China
Author:
Date: 1959-12-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, December 18th: Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, left Damascus this morning to return to China. He was here to attend the Second Meeting of the Consultative Committee of the Afro-Asian Economic Cooperation Organization, which has already concluded.

### 南汉宸离大马士革回国

1959-12-20
第3版()
专栏：

    南汉宸离大马士革回国
    新华社大马士革18日电　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今天上午离开大马士革回国。他是来参加已经在这里闭幕的亚非经济合作组织协商委员会第二次会议的。
