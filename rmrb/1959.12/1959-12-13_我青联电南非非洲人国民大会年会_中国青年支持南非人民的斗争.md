Title: All-China Youth Federation Sends Telegram to African National Congress of South Africa Annual Meeting: Chinese Youth Support the Struggle of the South African People
Author:
Date: 1959-12-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: The All-China Youth Federation today sent a telegram to the Youth Department of the African National Congress of South Africa, wishing the 47th Annual Meeting of the African National Congress of South Africa a success.

The telegram said: "Chinese youth extend their heartfelt respect to the South African people and youth who are fighting heroically against colonialism, racial discrimination, and for democratic freedom. The 130 million Chinese youth have always held deep sympathy and firm support for your just struggle."

The telegram said, "At present, the strength of the peace-loving people of the world has far surpassed the strength of imperialism. The people of the whole world stand with you and support your just struggle. Although imperialism still wants to make a dying struggle, it is all in vain. Final victory will surely belong to the united and struggling South African people and youth!"

The telegram concluded by expressing the hope that "the friendship between Chinese and South African youth will develop day by day!"

### 我青联电南非非洲人国民大会年会  中国青年支持南非人民的斗争

1959-12-13
第3版()
专栏：

    我青联电南非非洲人国民大会年会
    中国青年支持南非人民的斗争
    新华社12日讯　中华全国青年联合会今天打电报给南非非洲人国民大会青年部，预祝南非非洲人国民大会第四十七届年会成功。
    电报说：“中国青年谨向为反对殖民主义、反对种族歧视、争取民主自由而英勇斗争的南非人民和青年致以衷心的敬意。一亿三千万中国青年对你们的正义斗争始终寄予深切的同情和坚决的支持。”
    电报说，“目前，全世界爱好和平的人民的力量已经大大超过帝国主义的力量。全世界人民都和你们站在一起、支持你们的正义斗争。尽管帝国主义还想作垂死的挣扎。但都是枉费心机的。最后胜利必定属于团结一致、坚持斗争的南非人民和青年！”
    电报最后希望“中国和南非青年之间的友谊日益发展！”
