Title: Korean Architectural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-12-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. A fifteen-member architectural delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Kim Jung-hee, Vice-Chairman of the State Construction Committee, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of November 29th at the invitation of Liu Xiufeng, Minister of the Ministry of Construction Engineering of China, for a visit.

　　On the morning of the 30th, Liu Xiufeng, Minister of the Ministry of Construction Engineering, met with all members of the delegation.

### 朝鲜建筑考察团到北京

1959-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜建筑考察团到北京
    新华社30日讯　由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国家建设委员会副委员长金正熙率领的建筑考察团一行十五人，应我国建筑工程部部长刘秀峰的邀请来中国访问，在11月29日下午到达北京。
    30日上午，建筑工程部部长刘秀峰会见了考察团的全体人员。
