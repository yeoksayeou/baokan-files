Title: Little Girl Washing Clothes
Author: Wang Fatang, Jiang Cheng
Date: 1959-12-20
Page: 8
Newsreel Window

    Little girl washing clothes
    Little girl loves washing clothes
    Water splashes, bubbles smell sweet
    Sleeves rolled up, eyes bright
    Rub carefully, hang gently
    Washed so white, washed so fragrant
    Dirty grease all washed away
    Love labor, improve health
    Pay attention to hygiene, strong body
    Everyone praises the little girl
    Wang Fatang, Photography
    Jiang Cheng, Poem
