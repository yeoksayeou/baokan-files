Title: Reception Held by Our Ambassador to Mongolia for Visits of Two Chinese Delegations to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1959-12-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 24th: Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Xie Fusheng held a reception tonight to celebrate the friendly visits and negotiations in Mongolia by the Chinese Agricultural Delegation headed by Liao Luyan and the Chinese Financial Delegation headed by Qiao Peixin.
    Among those attending the reception were Mongolian First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Molomjamts, Deputy Chairmen Regvaalal and Luvsan, and Foreign Minister Shagdar Suran.

### 为我两代表团访问蒙古  我驻蒙大使举行招待会

1959-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　为我两代表团访问蒙古
    我驻蒙大使举行招待会
    据新华社乌兰巴托24日电　中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生，为以廖鲁言为首的中国农业代表团和以乔培新为首的中国财政金融代表团在蒙古进行友好访问和谈判，在今天晚上举行了招待会。
    出席招待会的有蒙古部长会议第一副主席莫洛姆扎木茨、副主席热格瓦拉尔、鲁布桑和外交部长沙格达尔苏伦等人。
