Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Receives Chinese Journalists Delegation; Ambassador Qiao Xiaoguang Holds Banquet for Delegation's Visit to Korea
Author:
Date: 1959-12-25
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 24th: Premier of the Cabinet of North Korea, Kim Il-sung, received the Chinese journalists delegation headed by Hu Jiwei, deputy editor-in-chief of the People’s Daily, this morning. Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, Qiao Xiaoguang, accompanied the delegation.

Present at the meeting on the Korean side were: Kim Chang-man, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; Park Sung-chul, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Kim Do-man, Director of the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; and Hyun Pil-hoon, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Journalists Union.

A cordial and friendly conversation took place during the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 24th: Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, Qiao Xiaoguang, held a banquet today for the Chinese journalists delegation's visit to North Korea.

Among those present at the banquet were: Ha Ang-cheon, Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association; Li Il-gyeong, Minister of Education and Culture; Song Young, Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations; Kim Do-man, Director of the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; Park Yong-guk, Director of the International Department; Hyun Pil-hoon, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Journalists Union; and heads of various Korean newspapers and news agencies.

Ambassador Qiao Xiaoguang, Chairman Hyun Pil-hoon, and Hu Jiwei, head of the Chinese journalists delegation, delivered speeches at the banquet.

### 金日成首相接见我新闻工作者代表团  乔晓光大使为代表团访朝举行宴会

1959-12-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　金日成首相接见我新闻工作者代表团
    乔晓光大使为代表团访朝举行宴会
    新华社平壤24日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成今天上午接见了以人民日报副总编辑胡绩伟为首的中国新闻工作者代表团。中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光陪同代表团前往。
    接见时朝鲜方面在座的有：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长金昌满、外务相朴成哲、劳动党中央宣传鼓动部部长金道万、朝鲜记者同盟中央委员会委员长玄弼勋。
    接见时进行了亲切的友好的谈话。
    新华社平壤24日电　中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光今天为中国新闻工作者代表团访问朝鲜举行宴会。
    出席宴会的有：朝中友好协会委员长河仰天，教育文化相李一卿，对外文化联络委员会委员长宋影，朝鲜劳动党中央宣传鼓动部部长金道万、国际部部长朴容国，朝鲜记者同盟中央委员会委员长玄弼勋和朝鲜各报社、通讯社的负责人。
    乔晓光大使、玄弼勋委员长和中国新闻工作者代表团团长胡绩伟先后在宴会上讲了话。
