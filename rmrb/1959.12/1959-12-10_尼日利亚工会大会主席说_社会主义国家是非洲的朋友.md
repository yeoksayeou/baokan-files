Title: President of the Nigerian Trade Union Congress Says Socialist Countries are Friends of Africa
Author:
Date: 1959-12-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Accra, December 8th: The Lagos "Daily News" reported on the 7th that the President of the Nigerian Trade Union Congress, Imoudu, said in Lagos that socialist countries are friends of Africa.

Imoudu had just returned to Lagos after visiting China and the Soviet Union. He attended the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China in Beijing.

Speaking at a rally of the Nigerian Textile Workers' Union on December 5th, he said that he had made contact with the people in China and the Soviet Union, and he personally felt that the Chinese people and the Soviet people are "our friends."

He said that Western capitalist countries "drain us dry day by day, exploit us, and take our money to build their empires."

He said, "I believe that only socialism can accelerate the progress of this country, and only the working class can make this country an efficient country." He also said, "Socialism is our way out."

### 尼日利亚工会大会主席说  社会主义国家是非洲的朋友

1959-12-10
第5版()
专栏：

    尼日利亚工会大会主席说
    社会主义国家是非洲的朋友
    新华社阿克拉8日电　拉各斯“每日新闻”7日报道，尼日利亚工会大会主席伊穆杜在拉各斯说，社会主义国家是非洲的朋友。
    伊穆杜是在访问中国和苏联以后刚回到拉各斯的。他曾经在北京参加中华人民共和国建国十周年的庆祝典礼。
    他12月5日在尼日利亚纺织工人工会的一次集会上讲话时说，他在中国和苏联同人民进行了接触，他亲身体会到，中国人民和苏联人民是“我们的朋友”。
    他说，西方资本主义国家“一天天地把我们吸干，对我们进行剥削，并且拿走了我们的钱以建造他们的帝国”。
    他说，“我相信，只有社会主义才能加速这个国家的进步，只有工人阶级才能使这个国家成为有效率的国家。”他又说，“社会主义是我们的出路。”
