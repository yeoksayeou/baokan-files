Title: Western Pacific Fisheries Research Committee Meeting Concludes; China, Soviet Union, Vietnam, North Korea, and Mongolia Decide to Expand Cooperation in Aquatic Science Research
Author:
Date: 1959-12-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 10th. The fourth plenary session of the Western Pacific Fisheries Research Committee, composed of the Soviet Union, China, Vietnam, North Korea, and Mongolia, concluded here this afternoon. The meeting opened on the 3rd.

Deputy Prime Minister and Director of the State Science Committee of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Truong Chinh, Deputy Director of the State Science Committee, Ta Quang Buu, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry, Nghiem Xuan Yem, and the diplomatic envoys of China, the Soviet Union, North Korea, and Mongolia to Vietnam were invited to attend today's meeting.

At today's plenary session, the meeting unanimously adopted the work report for 1958-1959 made by the committee's secretary, Shi Min, the suggestions for cooperation in fisheries, oceanography, and limnology research between China, the Soviet Union, Vietnam, North Korea, and Mongolia for 1959-1960, the work plans for each professional group for 1959-1960, and the resolutions of the committee secretariat regarding funding and staffing.

The meeting also re-elected the chairman, vice-chairman, and secretary of the committee, and approved the list of group leaders and deputy group leaders for each professional group. Xu Deheng, head of the Chinese delegation and Minister of Aquatic Products, was re-elected as the chairman of the committee.

The head of the Soviet delegation, Deputy Director of the All-Union Research Institute of Marine Fisheries and Oceanography and Vice-Chairman of the Committee, P.A. Moiseyev, gave a summary of the academic reports of the fourth plenary session of the committee at today's meeting. He pointed out that the delegations of various countries presented a total of twenty-seven academic reports during the meeting, and these creative papers demonstrated the continuous development of each country's fisheries scale and new achievements in scientific research.

The head of the Vietnamese delegation, Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Forestry, and Vice-Chairman of the Committee, Le Duy Trinh, delivered the closing speech. He said that the fourth plenary session of the committee had achieved great success, marking a new step in the mutual assistance and cooperation among our socialist countries in fisheries, oceanography, and limnology. He expressed his belief that after this meeting, aquatic science workers from the five fraternal countries will achieve even greater successes. Today's meeting also decided to hold the fifth plenary session of the committee in Beijing in 1960 and adopted the draft agenda for the fifth plenary session.

### 太平洋西部渔业研究委员会会议闭幕  中苏越朝蒙决定扩大水产科学研究合作

1959-12-11
第5版()
专栏：

    太平洋西部渔业研究委员会会议闭幕
    中苏越朝蒙决定扩大水产科学研究合作
    新华社河内10日电　由苏联、中国、越南、朝鲜、蒙古五国组成的太平洋西部渔业研究委员会的第四次全体会议今天下午在这里闭幕。这次会议是在3日开幕的。
    越南民主共和国副总理兼国家科学委员会主任长征，国家科学委员会副主任谢光宝，农林部部长严春庵以及中、苏、朝、蒙四国驻越南外交使节应邀出席了今天的会议。
    在今天的全体会议上，一致通过了委员会秘书史敏所作的1958至1959年度的工作报告，中、苏、越、朝、蒙五国1959至1960年度在渔业、海洋学和湖沼学研究方面合作的建议和各专业组1959至1960年度的工作计划，以及委员会秘书处关于经费和人员编制等项决议。
    会议还改选了委员会主席、副主席、秘书，并通过了各专业组组长、副组长名单。中国代表团团长、水产部部长许德珩再次当选为委员会的主席。
    苏联代表团团长、全苏海洋渔业和海洋学研究所副所长、委员会副主席彼·阿·莫伊谢也夫在今天的会议上作了委员会第四次全体会议学术报告的总结。他指出，各国代表团在会议期间共提出了二十七篇学术报告，这些具有创造性的论文，表明了各国在渔业规模上的不断发展和科学研究方面的新的成就。
    越南代表团团长、农林部副部长、委员会副主席黎维贞致闭幕词。他说，委员会第四次全体会议获得了巨大的成绩，它标志着我们几个社会主义国家在渔业、海洋学和湖沼学等方面的互助合作迈出了新的一步。他表示相信，经过这次会议以后，五个兄弟国家的水产科学工作者将会取得更大的成绩。今天的会议还决定1960年在北京举行委员会第五次全体会议，并通过了第五次全体会议的议程草案。
