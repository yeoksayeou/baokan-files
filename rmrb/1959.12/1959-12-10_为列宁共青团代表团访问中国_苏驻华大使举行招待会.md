Title: Soviet Ambassador to China Holds Reception for Visit of All-Union Leninist Young Communist League Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1959-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th: Soviet Ambassador to China, Chervonenko, held a reception this evening for the visit of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League (Komsomol) delegation to China.

In his speech, Ambassador Chervonenko stated that the Soviet people and youth highly value the great victories achieved by the Chinese people and youth under the leadership of the great Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao in socialist construction. This visit to China by the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League delegation will further consolidate and strengthen the friendship between the peoples and youth of the Soviet Union and China. Ambassador Chervonenko wished for the continuous development of the friendly relations between the peoples and youth of the Soviet Union and China.

Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, in his speech, warmly thanked the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League delegation for bringing the Chinese youth lofty internationalist friendship, rich experience, and tremendous encouragement. He emphasized that the Chinese youth have in the past, do now, and will in the future regard the Soviet youth as an example to learn from. He concluded by proposing a toast to the Leninist Young Communist League and the Soviet youth, who were educated by the glorious Soviet Communist Party.

Murtazaev, head of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League delegation, said in his speech that the delegation saw everywhere in China the high enthusiasm and great vigor of the Chinese youth in building socialism, and felt everywhere the fraternal friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and China. He wished for the continuous strengthening of the friendship between the youth of the Soviet Union and China.

Invited to the reception this evening were Luo Yi, Hu Keshi, and Liang Buting, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League; Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League; as well as representatives of young activists on the production front in Beijing.

### 为列宁共青团代表团访问中国  苏驻华大使举行招待会

1959-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    为列宁共青团代表团访问中国
    苏驻华大使举行招待会
    新华社9日讯　苏联驻华大使契尔沃年科为苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团访问中国，在今天晚间举行了招待会。
    契尔沃年科大使在致词中说，苏联人民和青年十分重视中国人民和青年在伟大的中国共产党和毛主席领导下在社会主义建设中所取得的巨大胜利。苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团这次访问中国，将使苏中两国人民和青年之间的友谊进一步得到巩固和加强。契尔沃年科大使祝苏中两国人民和青年之间的友谊关系不断发展。
    中国共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处第一书记胡耀邦在致词中，热烈感谢苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团给中国青年带来了崇高的国际主义友谊、丰富的经验和巨大的鼓舞。他强调说，中国青年过去、现在和将来都把苏联青年当做自己学习的榜样。他最后提议为光荣的苏联共产党教导出来的列宁共产主义青年团和苏联青年干杯。
    苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团团长穆尔塔扎耶夫在致词中说，代表团这次在中国到处看到了中国青年建设社会主义的高度热情和巨大干劲，到处感受到了苏中两国人民的兄弟情谊。他祝苏中两国青年的友谊不断加强。
    应邀出席今晚招待会的，有共青团中央书记处书记罗毅、胡克实、梁步庭，共青团北京市委第一书记王照华，以及北京市生产战线上的青年积极分子的代表。
