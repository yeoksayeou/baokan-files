Title: Ministry of Foreign Affairs Holds Reception for Envoys from Various Countries to Welcome the Upcoming Year 1960
Author:
Date: 1959-12-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th – The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China tonight invited ambassadors, chargés d'affaires, and all diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China, along with their wives, to a reception to welcome the upcoming year 1960.

    Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, attended the reception and delivered a speech, wishing the guests a happy New Year 1960. The Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Booker, delivered a response on behalf of the diplomatic envoys attending the reception. He proposed a toast to the prosperity of the People's Republic of China and to peace and happiness.

    After the reception began, the hosts and guests had dinner together, and then the hosts and guests enjoyed a cultural performance.

    Also attending the reception were the heads of units from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the State Council's Foreign affairs Office, the Ministry of National Defense, the Ministry of Foreign Trade, the Commission for Cultural Relations with foreign Countries, the Ministry of Culture and other units.

### 迎接即将来临的1960年  我外交部邀请各国使节联欢

1959-12-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　迎接即将来临的1960年
    我外交部邀请各国使节联欢
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国外交部今晚邀请各国驻中国的大使、代办和全体外交官员以及他们的夫人，举行联欢晚会迎接即将来临的1960年。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长出席了联欢晚会，他在晚会上致词，祝客人们1960年新年快乐。瑞典驻中国大使布克代表出席联欢晚会的外交使节致答词，他提议为中华人民共和国的繁荣、为和平和幸福干杯。
    联欢晚会开始后，宾主一起共进晚餐，然后宾主又欣赏了文艺表演。
    出席联欢晚会的，还有外交部、国务院外事办公室、国防部、对外贸易部、对外文化联络委员会和文化部等单位的负责人。
