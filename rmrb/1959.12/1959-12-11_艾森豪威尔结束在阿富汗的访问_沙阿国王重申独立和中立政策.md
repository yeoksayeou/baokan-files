Title: Eisenhower Ends Visit to Afghanistan; King Shah Reaffirms Policy of Independence and Neutrality
Author:
Date: 1959-12-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th, Kabul Dispatch: U.S. President Eisenhower left Kabul for New Delhi today after a brief five-hour visit to Afghanistan.

During his stay in Kabul, Eisenhower held talks with Afghan King Zahir Shah and other national leaders. A joint communique issued after the talks stated that, in the course of discussing issues of mutual interest, King Zahir Shah clarified Afghanistan's independent and neutral policy.

King Zahir Shah expressed his high hopes for the upcoming summit meeting of heads of state, including President Eisenhower, and expressed his hope that Eisenhower's visit to the Soviet Union would yield concrete results.

Eisenhower arrived in Kabul by plane from Karachi this morning.

According to New York reports, "The New York Times" and the New York "Daily News" emphasized in today's editorials that Eisenhower's visit to Afghanistan was related to the country's "strategic position." "The New York Times" stated, "For strategic and economic reasons, modern Afghanistan, situated at the crossroads of Central Asia, remains an important country. This is why the U.S. President is visiting Kabul today on his way to India."

### 艾森豪威尔结束在阿富汗的访问  沙阿国王重申独立和中立政策

1959-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔结束在阿富汗的访问
    沙阿国王重申独立和中立政策
    新华社9日讯　喀布尔消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔今天在阿富汗作了五小时的短暂访问后，离开喀布尔前往新德里。
    在喀布尔停留期间，艾森豪威尔同阿富汗国王查希尔·沙阿和其他国家领导人举行了会谈。会谈后发表的联合公报说，在就双方感兴趣的问题举行会谈的过程中，查希尔·沙阿国王阐明了阿富汗的独立的和中立的政策。
    查希尔·沙阿国王表示，他对艾森豪威尔总统和其他国家政府首脑即将举行的最高级会议寄予很大希望，并希望艾森豪威尔访问苏联会带来具体结果。
    艾森豪威尔是在今天上午从卡拉奇乘机到达喀布尔的。
    据纽约消息，“纽约时报”和纽约“每日新闻”在今天的社论中强调艾森豪威尔访问阿富汗与这个国家所处的“战略地位”有关。“纽约时报”说，“由于战略和经济上的原因，仍然使这个处在中亚的十字路口的现代阿富汗成为一个重要的国家。这就是美国总统今天在去印度的中途要访问喀布尔的原因。”
