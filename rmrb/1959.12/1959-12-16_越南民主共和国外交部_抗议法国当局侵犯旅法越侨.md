Title: Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Protests French Authorities' Infringement on the Rights of Overseas Vietnamese in France
Author:
Date: 1959-12-16
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 14th: According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency today, Hoang Bao Son, Deputy Director of the Department of Western Europe and the Americas of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, representing the Vietnamese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, met with Albert Chambon, the acting representative of the Republic of France in Hanoi, on December 12th. He protested against the French government's unreasonable actions, which included ordering the dissolution of the Union of Overseas Vietnamese in France, closing the mutual aid restaurants run by overseas Vietnamese in Paris, prohibiting the distribution of the Vietnamese-run French newspaper "Vietnamese Current Affairs" throughout France, and prohibiting the distribution of Vietnamese publications such as "People's Daily" and "Unity Daily" in France.
    Hoang Bao Son reiterated the views expressed in the letter from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the French Ministry of Foreign Affairs on October 21st of this year.
    Hoang Bao Son also once again demanded that the French government immediately rescind its arbitrary decisions against overseas Vietnamese in France and its unfriendly attitude towards the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Otherwise, the French government must bear full responsibility for all the consequences arising therefrom.

### 越南民主共和国外交部  抗议法国当局侵犯旅法越侨

1959-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国外交部
    抗议法国当局侵犯旅法越侨
    新华社河内14日电　据越南通讯社今天报道，越南民主共和国外交部西欧和美洲司副司长黄宝山代表越南外交部在12日接见法兰西共和国驻河内代表的代理阿尔贝·香邦，抗议法国政府在下令解散旅法越侨联合会之后，又封闭了旅居巴黎越侨经营的互助性的饭店、禁止越侨办的法文报纸“越南时事报”在法国全境发行和禁止越南出版的“人民报”、“统一报”等在法国发行等无理行为。
    黄宝山重申了越南民主共和国外交部今年10月21日写给法国外交部的信中的观点。
    黄宝山并且再一次要求法国政府立即取消对旅法越侨的专横的决定和对越南民主共和国不友好的态度，否则法国政府必须对这一切造成的后果负全部责任。
