Title: International Table Tennis Federation Announces This Year's World's Outstanding Players; Chinese Player Rong Guotuan Ranks Among the Best
Author:
Date: 1959-12-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, December 12th: The International Table Tennis Federation announced here on the 12th the rankings of outstanding world table tennis players for 1959.

    Men:
    1. Rong Guotuan (China) 2. Sidó (Hungary) 3. Ogimura Ichiro (Japan) 4. Murakami Teruo (Japan) 5. Berczik (Hungary) 6. Le Van Tiet (South Vietnam) 7. Miles (United States) 8. Wang Chuanyao (China) 9. Harangozó (Yugoslavia) 10. Marković (Yugoslavia) 11. Yang Ruihua (China) 12. Mai Van Hoa (South Vietnam) 13. Štípek (Czechoslovakia).

    In addition, there are four outstanding players who were not ranked: Freundorfer (West Germany), Larsson (Sweden), Hoshino Tatsuya (Japan), and Zhuang Jiafu (China).

    Women:
    1. Matsuzaki Kimiyo (Japan) 2. Eguchi Fujie (Japan) 3. Kóczián (Hungary) 4. Qiu Zhonghui (China) 5. Haydon (Great Britain) 6. Lantos (Hungary) 7. Cho Kyung-ja (South Korea) 8. Simon (Netherlands) 9. Namba Taeko (Japan) 10. Choi Kyung-ja (South Korea) 11. Sun Meiying (China) 12. Mosoczy (Hungary) 13. Yamaizumi Kazuko (Japan).

### 国际乒乓球联公布今年世界优秀选手名次  我国选手容国团名列前茅

1959-12-14
第5版()
专栏：

    国际乒乓球联公布今年世界优秀选手名次
    我国选手容国团名列前茅
  新华社伦敦12日电　国际乒乓球联合会12日在这里公布了1959年世界乒乓球优秀选手名次。
    男子
    一、容国团（中国）二、西多（匈牙利）三、荻村伊智朗（日本）四、村上辉夫（日本）五、别尔切克（匈牙利）六、黎文节（南越）七、迈耳斯（美国）八、王传耀（中国）九、哈兰高佐（南斯拉夫）十、马科维奇（南斯拉夫）十一、杨瑞华（中国）十二、梅文和（南越）十三、斯蒂佩克（捷克斯洛伐克）。
