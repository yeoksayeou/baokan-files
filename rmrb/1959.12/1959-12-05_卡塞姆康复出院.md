Title: Qasim Discharged from Hospital
Author:
Date: 1959-12-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, December 3rd: Iraqi Prime Minister Qasim has recovered his health and left the "Peace House" hospital at noon today. He was guarded by armored vehicles when he left the hospital. The crowd gathered outside the hospital applauded him warmly.

Qasim was admitted to the hospital after being wounded in an assassination attempt on October 7.

### 卡塞姆康复出院

1959-12-05
第6版()
专栏：

    卡塞姆康复出院
    新华社巴格达3日电　伊拉克总理卡塞姆已经恢复健康，今天中午离开“和平之家”医院。他出院时由装甲车警卫着。聚在医院外面的群众向他热烈鼓掌。
    卡塞姆是在10月7日被刺受伤后进入医院的。
