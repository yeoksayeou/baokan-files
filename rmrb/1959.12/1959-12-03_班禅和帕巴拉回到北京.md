Title: Panchen and Pabala Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-12-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd—Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Pabala Gelleg Namgyal, Vice Chairman, returned to Beijing after visiting Northeast China and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, accompanied by Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, and Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee.
    Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, Yang Jingren, Xie Hechou and others, Deputy Directors of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, greeted them at the station.

### 班禅和帕巴拉回到北京

1959-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅和帕巴拉回到北京
    新华社2日讯　西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰，在中共中央统战部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋和中共西藏工委副书记周仁山陪同下，在参观访问了东北和内蒙古自治区以后返回北京。
    政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，民族事务委员会副主任刘春、萨空了、杨静仁、谢鹤筹等都到车站迎接。
