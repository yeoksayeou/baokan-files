Title: Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Holds New Year's Reception, Wishing Foreign Friends in Beijing a Happy New Year

Author:

Date: 1959-12-30
Page: 5

　　Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Holds New Year's Reception
    Wishing Foreign Friends in Beijing a Happy New Year
    Xinhua News Agency, December 29th. The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries invited cultural figures, peace activists, experts, journalists, and students from Asia, Africa, Europe, Latin America, and other regions who are visiting or residing in Beijing to a get-together tonight to welcome the upcoming 1960. Before the reception began, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, delivered a speech, wishing foreign friends a happy New Year.
    Among those attending the reception were Ding Xilin, Yang Hansheng, Xia Yan, and Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Presidents of the Association, as well as well-known figures from all walks of life in the capital.

### 我对外文化协会举行联欢晚会  祝贺在京外国朋友新年愉快

1959-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　我对外文化协会举行联欢晚会
    祝贺在京外国朋友新年愉快
    新华社29日讯　中国人民对外文化协会今天晚间邀请正在北京访问和在京的亚洲、非洲、欧洲、拉丁美洲等地区的文化人士、和平人士、专家、记者和留学生一起联欢，迎接即将来临的1960年。在联欢晚会开始前，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南致词，向外国朋友们祝贺新年愉快。
    参加联欢晚会的有对外文协副会长丁西林、阳翰笙、夏衍、张致祥以及首都各界知名人士。
