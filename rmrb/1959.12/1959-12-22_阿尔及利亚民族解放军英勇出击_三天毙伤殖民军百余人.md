Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Strikes Courageously, Killing or Wounding Over 100 Colonial Soldiers in Three Days
Author:
Date: 1959-12-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 19th: The headquarters of the Algerian National Liberation Army announced here today a communique regarding military actions from December 15th to 17th, stating that during this period, the Algerian National Liberation Army killed seventy French soldiers and wounded seventy-eight. The communique stated that the National Liberation Army ambushed French troops and military convoys a total of five times, engaged the enemy in thirteen battles, and attacked French military and police strongholds eleven times. The communique stated that the Algerian National Liberation Army also destroyed fifteen French military vehicles, blew up two French military trains, seized thirty-one weapons and a large quantity of ammunition, and captured one French soldier. Fifteen French soldiers defected with all their equipment.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放军英勇出击  三天毙伤殖民军百余人

1959-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿尔及利亚民族解放军英勇出击
    三天毙伤殖民军百余人
    新华社开罗19日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军总部今天在这里发表的关于12月15日到17日军事行动的公报说，在这期间，阿尔及利亚民族解放军打死法军七十名，打伤七十八名。公报说，民族解放军共伏击法军和军事运输队五次，同敌人进行十三次战斗，进攻法军和警察据点十一次。公报说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军还击毁十五辆法国军用车辆，炸毁两列法国军用火车，缴获三十一件武器和大量弹药，俘掳了一名法国士兵。十五名法国士兵携带全副装备投诚。
