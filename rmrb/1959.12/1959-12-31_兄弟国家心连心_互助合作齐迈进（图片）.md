Title: Brotherly Countries Heart to Heart, Mutual Assistance and Cooperation March Forward Together (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1959-12-31
Page: 5

The friendly cooperation within the socialist camp, headed by the Soviet Union, is developing and strengthening day by day. This fraternal mutual support and friendly cooperation have effectively promoted the common prosperity of the socialist camp and will further accelerate the economic power of socialist countries to leap to a leading position in the competition with capitalist countries.

　　Socialist countries' students studying in the Radio Department of the Moscow Power Institute are doing experiments with Soviet students. TASS draft (Xinhua News Agency)

　　Workers at the Hungarian Klement Gottwald Electrical Factory are rushing to produce power generation equipment for our country. Hungarian News Agency draft (Xinhua News Agency)

　　Korean agronomist Che Lijin (transliteration) is teaching members of the Lenin Agricultural Production Cooperative in Stara Zagora Province, Bulgaria, how to cultivate cotton seedlings. Bulgarian News Agency draft (Xinhua News Agency)

　　The first batch of 789 sheep and camels donated by the Mongolian People's Republic to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam arrived at Gia Lam Railway Station in Vietnam. Vietnam News Agency draft (Xinhua News Agency)

　　Romania, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, and Czechoslovakia are jointly building a reed processing complex in Kiskunhalas, Romania. Xinhua News Agency draft

### 兄弟国家心连心　互助合作齐迈进（图片）

1959-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

　　兄弟国家心连心　互助合作齐迈进
    以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的友好合作，正在日益发展和加强。这种兄弟般的相互支援和友好合作，已经有效地促进了社会主义阵营的共同繁荣，并将进一步促使社会主义国家的经济威力在同资本主义国家的竞赛中更快地跃向领先地位。
　　在莫斯科动力学院无线电系学习的社会主义国家的留学生们正在跟苏联同学一起做实验。　　塔斯社稿（新华社发）
　　匈牙利克利门特·哥特瓦尔德电器厂的工人在为我国赶制发电设备。
            匈牙利通讯社稿（新华社发）
　　朝鲜的农学家车利镇（译音）在给保加利亚斯塔拉萨哥拉州列宁农业生产合作社的社员们讲授培育棉花秧子的经验。
          保加利亚通讯社稿（新华社发）
　　蒙古人民共和国赠送给越南民主共和国的第一批七百八十九头羊和骆驼运到了越南嘉林火车站。　越南通讯社稿（新华社发）
　　罗马尼亚、德意志民主共和国、波兰和捷克斯洛伐克正在罗马尼亚的基什干尼合建一座芦苇加工联合厂。　　　　新华社稿
