Title: Australian Barton Couple Leave Guangzhou for Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-12-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 14th: Mrs. Malvina Lillian Barton, Executive Committee Member of the Australian Peace Committee and former National President of the Australian Federation of Women, and her husband, Robert John Barton, who were invited by the All-China Women's Federation to visit China, concluded their visit to China and left Guangzhou for home via Hong Kong on the 13th.

The Barton couple arrived in Beijing by train from Moscow on November 17th. During their stay in China, they visited Beijing, Zhengzhou, Wuhan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Guangzhou and other places.

### 澳大利亚巴顿夫妇离广州回国

1959-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚巴顿夫妇离广州回国
    新华社广州14日电　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请前来我国参观访问的澳大利亚和平委员会执行委员、前任澳大利亚妇联全国委员会主席玛尔维娜·莉莉安·巴顿夫人和她的丈夫罗伯特·约翰·巴顿，结束了在我国的参观访问，13日离广州经香港回国。
    巴顿夫妇是11月17日自莫斯科乘车到达北京的。他们在我国期间曾在北京、郑州、武汉、南京、上海、广州等地进行了参观访问。
