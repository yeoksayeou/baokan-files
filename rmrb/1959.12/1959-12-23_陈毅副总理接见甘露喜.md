Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Ganlu Xi
Author:
Date: 1959-12-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Ganlu Xi, President of the Nepal Buddhist Revival Association and Vice President of the World Fellowship of Buddhists, this afternoon. The host and guest engaged in friendly talks.

Present at the reception were He Chengxiang, Director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs of the State Council, Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见甘露喜

1959-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见甘露喜
    新华社22日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了尼泊尔王国佛教复兴会会长、世界佛教徒联谊会副主席甘露喜，宾主进行了友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初等。
