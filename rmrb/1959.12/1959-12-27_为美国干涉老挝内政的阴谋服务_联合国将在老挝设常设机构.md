Title: Serving the US Conspiracy to Interfere in Laos' Internal Affairs - The United Nations Will Establish a Permanent Agency in Laos
Author:
Date: 1959-12-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 25th: Vientiane Dispatch: According to Roberto Heurtematte, United Nations Technical Assistance Commissioner, in Vientiane on the 24th, the United Nations will soon establish a permanent agency in Vientiane to coordinate various activities of the United Nations that influence and interfere in Laos' internal affairs.

Heurtematte revealed that a large number of personnel belonging to different specialized agencies of the United Nations will arrive in Vientiane soon to work under the direction of a senior United Nations official to be determined.

Heurtematte recently arrived in Vientiane. It is reported that his activities in Vientiane are to discuss with the Lao Phoui Sananikone government how to implement the suggestions made by Tumioja, the special representative of United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld stationed in Laos, concerning interference in Laos' internal affairs.

### 为美国干涉老挝内政的阴谋服务  联合国将在老挝设常设机构

1959-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　为美国干涉老挝内政的阴谋服务
    联合国将在老挝设常设机构
    新华社河内25日电　万象消息：据联合国技术援助专员罗伯托·埃乌特马特24日在万象透露，联合国不久将在万象设立一个常设机构，以协调联合国影响和干涉老挝内政的各种活动。
    埃乌特马特透露，一大批属于联合国不同专门机构的人员最近将到达万象，在一名有待确定的联合国高级官员的指挥下工作。
    埃乌特马特是在最近到达万象的。据报道，他到万象进行活动，是为了同老挝培·萨纳尼空政府讨论如何实施联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德派驻老挝的特别代表杜米奥雅提出的干涉老挝内政的建议。
