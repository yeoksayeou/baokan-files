Title: Chinese Sichuan Opera Troupe Returns to Beijing After Visiting Four Brotherly Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-12-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Chinese Sichuan Opera Troupe, a group of sixty-four people led by troupe leader Ming Lang, returned to Beijing by train tonight after visiting and performing in Poland, Czechoslovakia, East Germany, and Bulgaria. The Sichuan Opera Troupe performed sixty-nine shows in thirty-five cities in the four countries and made several radio and television performances, which were warmly welcomed by the vast audience.

### 中国川剧团访问四个兄弟国家后回京

1959-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中国川剧团访问四个兄弟国家后回京
    新华社18日讯　赴波、捷、德、保访问演出的中国川剧团一行六十四人，在团长明朗率领下，今天晚上乘火车回到北京。川剧团在四个国家的三十五个城市演出了六十九场，并且作了数次广播电视演出，受到广大观众的热烈欢迎。
