    What is a Power Distribution System

    A power distribution system is the general term for a power supply system composed of a series of equipment that distributes electrical energy. It includes three main components: high-voltage distribution lines, distribution transformers, and low-voltage distribution lines.

    Generally speaking, high-voltage distribution lines refer to 3,300 volt, 6,300 volt, and 10,000 volt overhead power lines and cable lines; distribution transformers are the transformers we commonly see on utility poles or in transformer kiosks; low-voltage distribution lines refer to 110 volt, 220 volt, and 380 volt overhead power lines and cable lines.

### 什么叫配电系统

1959-12-07
第2版()
专栏：工业常识

    什么叫配电系统
    配电系统就是由分配电能的一系列的设备组成的一个供电系统的总称。它包括高压配电线，配电变压器，和低压配电线等三个主要组成部分。
    一般来讲高压配电线是指三千三百伏、六千三百伏、一万伏的架空电线和电缆线，配电变压器就是我们常见的放在电柱上的变压器或在变电亭里的变压器；低压配电线是指一百一十伏、二百二十伏、三百八十伏的架空电线和电缆线路。
