Title: Advancing Towards New Peaks
Author: Hai Leng
Date: 1959-12-29
Page: 8

　　Advancing Towards New Peaks
　　Hai Leng

　　Forward, forward, forward!
　　Raise the red flag and advance towards new peaks!
　　Let the leaping red arrows rise day by day,
　　Let the torrent of life forever surge onward.
　　The heroes of the era utter their heroic vows—
    “We must walk ahead of time!
　　Hand in hand, heart to heart, helping and encouraging each other,
　　Forever leading in the long-distance race of socialism.”

　　Battle after battle,
　　Victory after victory,
　　Every second is crucial for deciding victory or defeat,
　　Every step must not have the slightest relaxation or complacency.
　　Let steel floods, iron rivers, and oil flow into thousands of rivers,
　　Let grain, cotton, and coal pile up into ten thousand mountains,
　　Producing one more ton of steel, producing one more bushel of grain,
　　Is adding one more point of strength to defend peace.

　　Let Mao Zedong Thought nurture us to be even stronger,
　　Let the flowers of life bloom even more fragrantly and brilliantly,
　　Open the magnificent treasure trove of human culture and knowledge,
　　Let us climb to the peak of world science.

　　Let sunshine, air, earth, and water offer more wealth,
　　Let every element emit its maximum energy for us,
　　Let every stream flow into the great sea of socialism,
　　Let every road lead to the paradise of communism.

　　Let all deserts be transformed into oases and fertile fields,
　　Let the wilderness build up colorful towns, factories, and gardens,
　　Use our skillful hands to dress up the motherland's landscape even more beautifully,
　　Let us build a fairy tale wonderland in the world.

　　Let 600 million people ignite an even more ardent flame of youth,
　　Cross the threshold of time with the stride of giants,
　　Lift the lever of the Great Leap Forward and the People's Communes,
　　Carry the burden of creating history on our shoulders.

　　The motherland is precisely the morning of the rising sun,
　　Humanity is entering a new era of conquering outer space,
　　Let the declining classes sing dirges for the doomsday of the old world,
　　Let us use enthusiastic cheers and hope to praise the new world!

　　Present the bountiful fruits to our dear Party and Leader,
　　Present the flowers of victory to the heroes of the era,
　　Ride the east wind, drive the rocket,
　　Use high speed to welcome the eleventh extraordinary spring.

　　Let the imperialists and their running dogs bark madly,
　　Let the frogs in the well laugh at the blue sky.
　　See who laughs last, laughs best,
　　The giant of history will make a fair judgment.

　　Forward, forward, forward!
　　Raise the red flag and advance towards new peaks!
　　Let everything we do conform to the line of more, faster, better, and more economical,
　　Let the torrent of life forever roll forward!

### 向新的高峰前进
海棱
1959-12-29
第8版()
专栏：

　　向新的高峰前进
　　海棱
　　前进，前进，前进！
　　高举红旗向新的高峰前进！
　　让跃进的红色箭头日日上升，
　　让生活的巨流永远滚滚奔腾。
　　时代的英雄们发出豪迈的誓言——
    “我们一定要走在时间前面！
　　手携手，心连心，互助互勉，
　　在社会主义竞赛的长跑中永远领先。”
　　一个战斗接着一个战斗，
　　一个胜利接着一个胜利，
　　一分一秒都是决定胜负的关键，
　　每一步都不能有半点儿松懈自满。
　　让钢洪铁水石油流成千条河，
　　让粮食棉花煤炭堆成万重山，
　　多炼一吨钢，多产一斗粮，
　　就是多增加一分保卫和平的力量。
　　让毛泽东思想把我们哺育得更加坚强，
　　让生活的花朵开得更加芬芳灿烂，
　　打开人类文化知识的辉煌宝库，
　　让我们攀登上世界科学的峰巅。
　　让阳光空气大地和水献出更多的财富，
　　让每种元素为我们发出最大的能量，
　　让每条小溪都流归社会主义的大海，
　　让条条道路都通向共产主义的天堂。
　　让所有的沙漠都变成绿洲良田，
　　让荒野建起万紫千红的市镇、工厂、花园，
　　用我们的巧手把祖国江山打扮得更加娇艳，
　　让我们把童话般的仙境建在人间。
　　让六亿人民燃起更炽烈的青春火焰，
　　用巨人的步伐跨过时间的门限，
　　举起大跃进和人民公社的杠杆，
　　把创造历史的重担挑在我们双肩。
　　祖国正是旭日东升的早晨，
　　人类正跨入征服宇宙空间的新纪元，
　　让没落阶级去哀唱旧世界末日的挽歌，
　　让我们用热情的欢呼和希望把新世界礼赞！
　　把丰硕的果实献给亲爱的党和领袖，
　　把胜利的鲜花献给时代的英雄，
　　乘东风，驾火箭，
　　用高速度迎接第十一个不平凡的春天。
　　让帝国主义和他的叭儿狗们去狂吠，
　　让井底的青蛙去嘲笑青天。
　　看谁笑得最后，笑得最好，
　　历史的巨人会作出公正的裁判。
　　前进，前进，前进！
　　高举红旗向新的高峰前进！
　　让我们的一切都符合多快好省的路线，
　　让生活的巨流永远滚滚向前！
