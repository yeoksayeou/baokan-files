Title: Our Country's Table Tennis Team B Returns Victorious

Author:

Date: 1959-12-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 14th. China's Table Tennis Team B, led by Du Qian, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Table Tennis Association, returned to Beijing this afternoon after a friendly visit and competition in Hungary, the United Kingdom, and Sweden.

China's Table Tennis Team B consists of five male athletes and three female athletes, with an average age of only nineteen. They demonstrated excellent sportsmanship and achieved outstanding results during their visits and competitions abroad. At the International Table Tennis Championships held in Budapest, Hungary, they won one championship and two runner-up titles out of the five events. Subsequently, in London, Oxford, and other places in the United Kingdom, they achieved a record of five wins and one draw. Finally, at the 5th Scandinavian Table Tennis Championships held in Borås, Sweden, they achieved even more outstanding results, winning three championships and two runner-up titles in six events.

This afternoon, Huang Zhong, Vice Minister of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Zhang Qingji, Director of the Beijing Municipal Sports Commission, Lu Zhao, Director of the Military Sports Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Chen Xian, Chairman of the Chinese Table Tennis Association, as well as famous Chinese table tennis players, welcomed them at the airport.

### 我国乒乓球乙队胜利归来

1959-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我国乒乓球乙队胜利归来
    新华社14日讯　中国乒乓球乙队在匈牙利、英国和瑞典进行了友好访问比赛之后，已经于今天下午在中国乒乓球协会副主席杜前率领下回到北京。
    中国乒乓球乙队包括男运动员五名、女运动员三名，平均年龄只有十九岁。他们在国外访问和比赛中表现了良好的作风，取得优秀成绩。在匈牙利布达佩斯举行的国际乒乓球锦标赛的五项冠军中取得了一项冠军和两项亚军。接着在英国伦敦、牛津等地又取得五胜一平的战绩。最后在瑞典博腊斯举行的第五届斯堪的纳维亚国家乒乓球锦标赛中，获得更出色的成绩，在六个项目中取得三项冠军和两项亚军。
    今天下午，到机场欢迎的有国家体委副主任黄中，北京市体委主任张青季，团中央军体部部长鲁钊，中国乒乓球协会主席陈先，以及我国著名乒乓球运动员等。
