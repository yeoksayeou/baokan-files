Title: Grand Gathering in Moscow Commemorates the Centenary of Katayama Sen's Birth
Author:
Date: 1959-12-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 4th: According to TASS, a grand meeting was held in Moscow on the 3rd by people from all walks of life to commemorate the centenary of the birth of Katayama Sen, the founder and leader of the Japanese Communist Party. The meeting was attended by scientists and people from all walks of life in Moscow, as well as old Bolsheviks and university students.
    Rostislav Ulyanovsky, Deputy Director of the Institute of Oriental Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, said in his opening remarks that, like the Japanese people, the Soviet people will forever remember Katayama Sen as an activist who embodied the desire for friendship between our two peoples. In his legacy, he regarded the friendship between the Soviet and Japanese peoples as the foundation for lasting peace in the Far East.
    Gennady Obichkin, Director of the Institute of Marxism-Leninism, gave a report at the meeting on the life of this great son of the Japanese people. He emphasized that throughout his life, Katayama Sen consistently followed the Leninist principles of proletarian internationalism and was an irreconcilable fighter for the cause of communism, against imperialist war, and against fascism and colonial oppression.
    At the meeting, several veteran communists recalled their meetings with Katayama Sen.
    Professor Kiyoshi Inoue, a member of the Japanese Committee for Commemorating the Centenary of Katayama Sen's Birth, also spoke at the meeting.

### 莫斯科盛会纪念片山潜诞生百周年

1959-12-05
第6版()
专栏：

    莫斯科盛会纪念片山潜诞生百周年
    据新华社莫斯科4日电　据塔斯社报道：莫斯科各界人士3日举行大会，隆重纪念日本共产党创始人和领袖片山潜诞生一百周年。参加大会的有莫斯科的科学家和各界人士，还有老布尔什维克和高等学校的学生。
    苏联科学院东方学研究所副所长罗斯季斯拉夫·乌里扬诺夫斯基在致开会词时说，苏联人同日本人民一样，将把片山潜作为体现了我们两国人民友谊愿望的活动家而永远记在心里。他在遗训中把苏日两国人民的友谊看作是远东持久和平的基础。
    马克思列宁主义研究院院长根纳吉·奥比奇金在会上报告了这位日本人民的伟大儿子的生平事迹。他强调指出，片山潜在一生的活动中一贯遵循着无产阶级国际主义的列宁主义原则，是争取共产主义事业、反对帝国主义战争、反对法西斯主义和殖民主义压迫的不调和的战士。
    会上，有几位老共产党员追述了他们同片山潜会晤的情况。
    在大会上讲话的还有日本纪念片山潜诞生一百周年委员会委员井上清教授。
