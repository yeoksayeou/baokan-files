Title: Savitsky Says Widespread Use of Isotopes in Industry Will Save a Lot of Money at Opening of World's First Isotope Store
Author:
Date: 1959-12-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 23rd. TASS reports: The world's first isotope store opened in Moscow on the 22nd. A press conference was also held at the opening.

　　Savitsky, Director of the Soviet Isotope Production and Application Administration, said at the reception that the widespread application of control and automation instruments based on radioactive isotopes in industry would save the Soviet Union five to six billion rubles by the end of the Seven-Year Plan (1959-1965). No such store has ever been opened anywhere in the world. The store has three tasks: to promote the sale of radioactive isotopes, to maintain closer contact with users, and to introduce the application of radioactive materials under absolutely safe conditions.

　　Chichkin, a representative of the Soviet State Chemistry Committee, also spoke at the press conference. He said that the new store will supply customers with more than 500 kinds of stable isotopes and such isotope compounds, 490 kinds of radioactive compounds, and 75 kinds of radioactive isotopes. All varieties currently produced by Soviet industry are available here. Currently, the Soviet government also plans to open similar stores in Kiev, Leningrad, and Novosibirsk.

### 萨维茨基在世界第一个同位素商店开幕时说  工业广泛利用同位素将节约大量资金

1959-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

　　萨维茨基在世界第一个同位素商店开幕时说
    工业广泛利用同位素将节约大量资金
    新华社莫斯科23日电　据塔斯社报道：世界第一个同位素商店22日在莫斯科开幕。开幕时还举行了记者招待会。
    苏联同位素生产和应用管理局局长萨维茨基在招待会上说，在工业中广泛应用以放射性同位素为基础的控制仪器和自动化仪器，使苏联在七年计划（1959—1965年）终了时可以节约五、六十亿卢布。世界上任何地方还没有开设过这种商店。商店的任务有三：推销放射性同位素、保持与用户的更密切联系、在绝对安全的条件下介绍应用放射性物质的情况。
    苏联国家化学委员会代表契奇金也在记者招待会上发表讲话。他说，新商店将供应顾客五百多种稳定性同位素和这种同位素化合物，四百九十种放射性化合物和七十五种放射性同位素。苏联工业目前所生产的一切品种这里应有尽有。目前，苏联政府还计划在基辅、列宁格勒和新西伯利亚城开设同类商店。
