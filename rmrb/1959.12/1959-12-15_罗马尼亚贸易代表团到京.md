Title: Romanian Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-12-15
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, December 14th. A seven-member Romanian government trade delegation, led by Mr. Ciobanu, chairman of the Romanian Chamber of Commerce and Director of the Romanian Ministry of Foreign Trade, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

    Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Lin Haiyun, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, and Xiao Fangzhou, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade. Mr. Başchirotto, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, and Mr. Vicianu, Economic Counselor, also went to the airport to meet the delegation.

### 罗马尼亚贸易代表团到京

1959-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚贸易代表团到京
    新华社14日讯　罗马尼亚政府贸易代表团一行七人，在团长、罗马尼亚商会主席、罗马尼亚对外贸易部部务委员乔巴努率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有我对外贸易部副部长林海云，中国国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长萧方洲等人。罗马尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办巴什基洛和经济参赞维强努也到机场迎接。
