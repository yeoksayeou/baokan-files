Title: Secretary of the New South Wales Branch of the Australian Building Workers' Union Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-12-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 1st. Clancy, Secretary of the New South Wales Branch of the Australian Building Industry Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing by plane on December 1st at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Building Workers' Union. Zhang Tianmin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Building Workers' Union, and others welcomed him at the airport.

### 澳建筑工人工会新南威尔士州分会书记到京

1959-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    澳建筑工人工会新南威尔士州分会书记到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国建筑工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的澳大利亚建筑产业工人工会新南威尔士州分会书记克兰西，1日乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国建筑工会全国委员会主席张天民等。
