Title: German Socialist Unity Party Central Committee Plenary Session Issues Communiqué Calling for Maximizing the Commodity Rate of Agricultural Products
Author:
Date: 1959-12-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 13th. The German News Agency published on the 13th the communiqué of the Seventh Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party.

The communiqué said that the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party held a meeting from December 10th to 13th. This Central Plenary Session, with the participation of more than one hundred agricultural scientists, outstanding breeders and production team leaders, party and state cadres, and non-party personages, studied the issues related to completing the agricultural tasks stipulated in the Seven-Year Plan.

Hans Reichelt, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry of the German Democratic Republic, delivered a report on "Tasks in Animal Husbandry" at the meeting. B. Grünert and M. Steffan respectively reported on the implementation of agricultural resolutions in the Schwerin and Neubrandenburg districts. The plenary session put forward important suggestions while discussing these reports. Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party, made a concluding speech at the meeting.

The communiqué pointed out that the main issue facing every agricultural production unit in the German Democratic Republic is to maximize the output of commodity products. The Central Committee entrusted the Central Politburo to formulate a document for the next Central Plenary Session, stipulating the steps to complete the agricultural tasks of the Seven-Year Plan. The Central Committee suggested that the Central Politburo strengthen the Agricultural Commission and fundamentally improve its working methods.

The communiqué said that the plenary session also listened to and approved the work report of the Politburo made by G. Grünberg, Secretary of the Party Central Committee. The plenary session unanimously elected Grünberg as an alternate member of the Politburo.

### 德国统一社会党中央全会发表公报  号召最大限度提高农产品商品率

1959-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    德国统一社会党中央全会发表公报
    号召最大限度提高农产品商品率
    新华社柏林13日电　德意志通讯社13日发表了德国统一社会党中央委员会第七次全体会议的公报。
    公报说，德国统一社会党中央委员会在12月10日到13日举行了会议。这次中央全会在有一百多名农业科学家、优秀饲养员和生产队长、党和国家干部以及非党人士的参加下，研究了与完成七年计划规定的农业任务有关的问题。
    德意志民主共和国农林部长汉斯·赖希尔特在会上作了“关于畜牧业方面的任务”的报告。本·格吕纳特和马·斯特芬分别作了什末来和新勃兰登堡两个专区在贯彻执行农业方面的决议的情况的报告。全会在讨论这些报告时，提出了重要的建议。统一社会党中央第一书记乌布利希在会上作了总结发言。
    公报指出，德意志民主共和国每一个农业生产单位面临的主要问题是，最大限度地增加商品产品的产量。中央委员会委托中央政治局为下一次中央全会制定一项文件，规定出完成七年计划农业方面的任务的步骤。中央委员会建议中央政治局加强农业委员会，从根本上改进它的工作方法。
    公报说，全会还听取和批准了党中央书记处书记格·格吕纳贝格作的政治局工作报告。全会一致选举格吕纳贝格为政治局候补委员。
