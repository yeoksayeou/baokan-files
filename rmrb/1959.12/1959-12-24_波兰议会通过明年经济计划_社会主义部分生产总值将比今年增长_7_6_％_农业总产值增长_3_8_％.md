Title: Polish Parliament Approves Next Year's Economic Plan: Socialist Sector Output to Increase by 7.6% Over This Year; Total Agricultural Output to Increase by 3.8%
Author: 
Date: 1959-12-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 22nd: The Parliament of the Polish People's Republic passed laws on December 21 regarding the 1960 National Economic Plan and the National Budget. The law concerning the National Economic Plan points out that 1959 was a year in which Poland achieved great success in developing the national economy, as demonstrated by increased industrial production, significantly improved labor productivity, accelerated investment, and housing construction, among other things.

The 1960 National Economic Plan stipulates that the total output value of the socialist sector (calculated at comparable prices) will increase by 7.6% compared to 1959.

The plan stipulates that in 1960, 29 billion kilowatt-hours of electricity will be produced (9% more than in 1959), as well as 103 million tons of coal and approximately 6.5 million tons of steel. In 1960, the production of machinery manufacturing products will significantly increase. Compared to this year, the production of steam turbines will increase by 86.5%, seagoing vessels by over 40%, trucks by 30.9%, and electric locomotives by 33.3%.

The plan stipulates that in 1960, the total agricultural output value will increase by 3.8% compared to 1959. Among these, crop production will increase by 5%, and livestock production will increase by 2%.

The law concerning the 1960 National Budget points out that the 1960 budget revenue is approximately 196 billion zlotys, and expenditures are approximately 195 billion zlotys. Compared to this year, the 1960 budget expenditures will increase by 6.5%.

Within the budget expenditures, over 100 billion zlotys are allocated for developing the national economy, over 10 billion zlotys for education, 33 billion zlotys for science, culture, art, and healthcare, over 24 billion zlotys for social security and other social needs, and approximately 15.5 billion zlotys for national defense.

The state budget's investments are primarily used to further develop production. Basic investments in 1960 will increase by 6.4% compared to this year.

### 波兰议会通过明年经济计划  社会主义部分生产总值将比今年增长7.6％　农业总产值增长3.8％

1959-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

　　波兰议会通过明年经济计划
    社会主义部分生产总值将比今年增长7.6％　农业总产值增长3.8％
    新华社华沙22日电　波兰人民共和国议会在21日通过了关于1960年国民经济计划和国家预算的法律。关于国民经济计划的法律中指出，1959年是波兰在发展国民经济方面取得巨大成就的一年，这表现在工业生产增长了，劳动生产率大大提高了，投资和住宅建筑的速度加快了，等等。
    1960年国民经济计划规定，社会主义部分的生产总值（按比较价格计算）将比1959年增长7.6％。
    计划规定，1960年将生产电力二百九十亿度（比1959年多9％），煤——一亿零三百万吨，钢——六百五十万吨左右。1960年，机器制造业产品的生产将大大增加。同今年相比，汽轮机的生产将增加86.5％，海船——40％以上，载重汽车——30.9％，电力机车——33.3％。
    计划规定，1960年农业总产值将比1959年增长3.8％。其中，农作物的生产将增长5％，畜牧业生产将增长2％。
    关于1960年国家预算的法律指出，1960年预算收入约为一千九百六十亿兹罗提，支出约为一千九百五十亿兹罗提。同今年相比，1960年预算支出将增加6.5％。
    在预算支出中，用于发展国民经济的拨款为一千亿兹罗提以上，用于教育方面的拨款为一百亿兹罗提以上，用于科学、文化、艺术和卫生保健方面的拨款为三百三十亿兹罗提，用于社会赡养和其他社会需要方面的开支为二百四十多亿兹罗提，用于国防方面的经费为一百五十五亿兹罗提左右。
    国家预算的投资主要用于进一步发展生产。1960年的基本投资将比今年增加6.4％。
