Title: Gale and Temperature Drop Alert
Author:
Date: 1959-12-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a gale and temperature drop alert at 7:30 PM on the 9th: Affected by cold air, it is expected that in the next thirty-six hours, western and central Inner Mongolia, Ningxia, northern Gansu, northern Shaanxi, and northern Shanxi will experience northwesterly winds of force six or seven. Within twenty-four hours after the cold air front passes, the temperature in these areas will drop by about 8 to 10 degrees Celsius. The Bohai Sea and the Yellow Sea will have northeasterly winds of force six or seven, and the northern and central East China Sea will have easterly winds turning to northerly winds of force six or seven.

### 大风和降温警报

1959-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    大风和降温警报
    新华社9日讯　中央气象科学研究所9日下午七时半发布大风和降温警报：受冷空气影响，预计未来三十六小时内，内蒙古西部和中部、宁夏、甘肃北部、陕西北部、山西北部有六、七级西北风。冷空气前锋过后二十四小时内，这些地区的气温将下降摄氏八至十度左右。渤海、黄海有六、七级偏东北风，东海北部和中部有六、七级偏东风转偏北风。
