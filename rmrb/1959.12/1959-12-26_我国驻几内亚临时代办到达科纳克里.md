Title: China's Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Arrives in Conakry

Author:

Date: 1959-12-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, December 24th - Zhao Yuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Guinea, and other members of the embassy arrived here today. Sassoon, Director of the Protocol Department of the Guinean Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet and Bulgarian Embassies in Guinea, and the Commercial Representative of the German Democratic Republic in Guinea were present at the airport to welcome them.

### 我国驻几内亚临时代办到达科纳克里

1959-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　我国驻几内亚临时代办到达科纳克里
    新华社科纳克里24日电　中华人民共和国驻几内亚共和国大使馆临时代办赵源和使馆其他人员今天到达这里。前往机场迎接的有几内亚外交部礼宾司司长萨松、苏联和保加利亚驻几内亚大使馆的临时代办以及德意志民主共和国驻几内亚商务代表等。
