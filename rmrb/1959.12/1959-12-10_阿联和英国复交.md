Title: United Arab Republic and Britain Resume Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1959-12-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo—The United Arab Republic and Britain resumed diplomatic relations on the 1st, three years after severing them.

A joint statement issued by both sides on the matter stated that the government of the United Arab Republic and the government of the United Kingdom have agreed to re-establish diplomatic relations at the chargé d'affaires level.

### 阿联和英国复交

1959-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联和英国复交
    据新华社开罗讯　阿联和英国在断绝外交关系三年以后，1日恢复了外交关系。
    双方就这件事发表的一项联合声明中说，阿联政府和联合王国政府已经同意在代办一级上重新建立外交关系。
