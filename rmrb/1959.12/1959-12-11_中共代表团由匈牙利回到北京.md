Title: CCP Delegation Returns to Beijing from Hungary
Author:
Date: 1959-12-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 10th: The Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Tan Zhenlin, head of the delegation, and Wang Congwu, member of the delegation, along with five accompanying personnel, returned to Beijing by plane today after attending the Seventh Congress of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party.

    Li Fuchun, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and secretary of the Secretariat, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee, were among those who welcomed them at the airport.

    Szigeti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and Beno, Counselor, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 中共代表团由匈牙利回到北京

1959-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中共代表团由匈牙利回到北京
    新华社10日讯　参加了匈牙利社会主义工人党第七次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长谭震林和团员王从吾及随员共五人，今天乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场迎接的，有中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李富春，中央委员伍修权等。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂、参赞白诺克等也到机场迎接。
