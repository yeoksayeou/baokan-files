Title: Indonesian Ministry of Information Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-12-01
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Indonesian Ministry of Information Delegation, which came to China for a visit at the invitation of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, left Beijing by plane today to visit Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou and other places. The delegation will return home after visiting these places.

### 印度尼西亚情报部代表团离京

1959-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚情报部代表团离京
    新华社30日讯　应对外文化联络委员会邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚情报部代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京去上海、武汉、广州等地访问。代表团将在访问这些地方以后回国。
