Title: Voroshilov to Visit India in January Next Year
Author:
Date: 1959-12-06
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th dispatch from TASS: Kliment Voroshilov, Flor Kozlov, and Yekaterina Furtseva will visit India in the second half of January next year and attend the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the Republic of India. They will also visit various states and learn about the life of the Indian people.

Here, in the officially published communique, it is pointed out that the Soviet state leaders gratefully accepted the invitation to visit India.

At the end of 1958, Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, received an invitation from Indian President Rajendra Prasad to pay a friendly visit to India. However, Voroshilov did not go due to illness.

In November 1959, President Rajendra Prasad again invited Kliment Voroshilov. At the same time, the Indian government also invited Flor Kozlov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Yekaterina Furtseva, a representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, to visit India together with Voroshilov.

### 伏罗希洛夫明年一月访问印度

1959-12-06
第3版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫明年一月访问印度
    新华社5日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：克利门特·伏罗希洛夫、弗罗尔·科兹洛夫和叶卡捷琳娜·福尔采娃将在明年1月的下半月访问印度并参加印度共和国国庆十周年典礼。他们还将访问各个邦和了解印度人民的生活。
    这里，在正式发表的公报中指出，苏联国家领导人怀着感激的心情接受了访问印度的邀请。
    在1958年末，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克利门特·伏罗希洛夫曾接到印度总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德希望他到印度去进行亲善访问的邀请。但是，伏罗希洛夫因病没有前去。
    1959年11月，拉金德拉·普拉沙德总统再次邀请克利门特·伏罗希洛夫。同时，印度政府还邀请了苏联部长会议第一副主席弗罗尔·科兹洛夫和苏联最高苏维埃代表叶卡捷琳娜·福尔采娃同伏罗希洛夫一起去印度访问。
