Title: Heavy Snowfall in Areas South of the Great Wall

Author:
Date: 1959-12-19
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency report: Snow began falling in Beijing at 8:00 AM on the 18th, and by noon, the accumulated snow was already an inch thick.

    Currently, widespread areas of China south of the Great Wall are experiencing rain and snow. Snowfall began in Gansu, Shaanxi, and other regions on the morning of the 17th. On the morning of the 18th, widespread areas from the Northwest and North China to the north of the Huai River successively experienced snowfall. The snow-covered area extends from the central and southern parts of Inner Mongolia in the north to the Qinling Mountains, Dabie Mountains, and the Huai River basin in the south; from eastern Gansu in the west to coastal areas in the east. The snowfall was heavier in the southeast, generally moderate to heavy snow. On the 18th, areas south of the Huai River and north of the Nanling Mountains, from Sichuan in the west to coastal areas in the east, experienced rainfall. Sporadic rainfall occurred in some areas south of the Nanling Mountains.

### 长城以南地区普降瑞雪

1959-12-19
第3版()
专栏：

    长城以南地区普降瑞雪
    据新华社讯　北京从18日上午八时开始飞雪，中午积雪已经盈寸。
    目前，我国长城以南广大地区都有雨、雪。17日上午，甘肃、陕西等地开始下雪。18日晨，西北、华北直到淮河以北的广大地区都陆续降雪。雪区北起内蒙古的中南部，南到秦岭、大别山和淮河流域，西起甘肃东部，东到沿海地区。东南部雪量较大，一般是中雪或大雪。18日，淮河以南、南岭以北，西起四川东到沿海地区都下了雨。南岭以南部分地区零星下了雨。
