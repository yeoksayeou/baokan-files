Title: Secretary-General of the Iraqi Peace Partisans National Committee Condemns CENTO for Creating Tension
Author:
Date: 1959-12-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, December 6th. Aziz Sharif, Secretary-General of the Iraqi Peace Partisans National Committee, in a statement to Xinhua News Agency reporters, condemned the CENTO countries for frantically creating international tension.

    He said, "Unfortunately, we still see warmongers carrying out frantic activities in escalating world tension, especially the activities of the CENTO countries."

    Sharif accused CENTO of discussing the situation in the Middle East, particularly in Iraq, at meetings in Washington and Tehran. During these two meetings, the armies of the organization's member states also held military "exercises." The two meetings also discussed establishing a joint command and measures to strengthen military bases in the region.

    Sharif said that the Iraqi peace movement warmly welcomes the resolution passed by the Political Committee of the UN General Assembly on the disarmament proposals put forward by the Soviet Union and the United States, as well as the UN's appeal to France to stop its planned atomic tests in the Sahara.

    He stated that the Iraqi peace movement warmly supports the call by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, that the Geneva Accords must be implemented to stop the civil war in Laos.

### 伊和平战士全国委员会秘书长  谴责中央条约组织制造紧张

1959-12-08
第6版()
专栏：

    伊和平战士全国委员会秘书长
    谴责中央条约组织制造紧张
  据新华社巴格达6日电　伊拉克和平战士全国委员会秘书长阿齐兹·谢里夫对新华社记者发表谈话，谴责中央条约组织国家疯狂地制造国际紧张局势。
    他说，“不幸的是，我们仍然看到战争贩子在加剧世界紧张局势方面进行疯狂的活动，特别是中央条约组织国家的活动。”
    谢里夫指责说，中央条约组织在华盛顿和德黑兰举行的会议讨论了中东、特别是伊拉克的局势。在这两次会议期间，这个组织的成员国的军队还举行了军事“演习”。这两次会议还讨论了建立联合司令部，加强这个地区内的军事基地的措施。
    谢里夫说，伊拉克和平运动热烈欢迎联合国大会政治委员会就苏美两国提出的裁军建议所通过的决议以及联合国要求法国停止计划中的撒哈拉原子试验的呼吁书。
    他表示，伊拉克和平运动热烈支持中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若的呼吁，必须执行日内瓦协议以停止在老挝的内战。
