Title: Machine-Blown Glass Cups
Author:
Date: 1959-12-08
Page: 2

Machine-Blown Glass Cups

    In the midst of the great technological innovation movement, the Tianjin No. 1 Glass Factory, focusing on weak links in production, worked in cooperation with the Tianjin Daily Use Machinery Research Institute to create a set of cup-blowing machines to replace manual blowing, increasing production efficiency by 54% compared to manual labor.

    Glass cups, which had always been blown manually, have thus undergone a fundamental change. This reform has also paved the way for the further realization of mechanization in the blowing of other products.

### 机器吹制玻璃杯

1959-12-08
第2版()
专栏：

    机器吹制玻璃杯
    天津市第一玻璃厂在大闹技术革新运动中，针对生产中的薄弱环节，和天津市日用机械研究所共同配合，创制了一套代替人工吹制的吹杯机，生产效率比人工提高54％。
    一向用人工吹制的玻璃杯，从此实现了根本的改变。这项改革还为进一步实现其他产品的吹制机械化开辟了途径。
