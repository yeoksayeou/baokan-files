Title: Zhu De, Lin Boqu and Other Comrades Receive Ethnic and Religious Visiting Groups from Five Provinces

Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 6th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Lin Boqu, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, and Wang Feng, Alternate Member of the Central Committee, received this afternoon ten ethnic visiting groups and one religious visiting group from Inner Mongolia, Gansu, Shandong, Jilin, and Heilongjiang provinces, comprising fourteen ethnic groups.

### 朱德、林伯渠等同志接见五省区的民族宗教参观团

1959-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

    朱德、林伯渠等同志接见五省区的民族宗教参观团
    新华社6日讯　中国共产党中央委员会副主席朱德、中央政治局委员林伯渠和中央候补委员汪锋，今天下午接见了内蒙古、甘肃、山东、吉林、黑龙江等省区的包括十四个民族的十个各民族参观团和一个宗教界参观团。
