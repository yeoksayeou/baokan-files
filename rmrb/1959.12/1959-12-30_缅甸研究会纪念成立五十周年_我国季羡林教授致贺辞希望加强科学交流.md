Title: Burmese Research Society Celebrates Fiftieth Anniversary; Chinese Professor Ji Xianlin Sends Congratulatory Message Hoping to Strengthen Scientific Exchange
Author:
Date: 1959-12-30
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 28th: The fiftieth anniversary celebration of the Burmese Research Society opened today at the University of Rangoon under the direction of Burmese Minister of Culture U Chit Thoung.

　　Approximately two hundred Burmese scholars, as well as scholars from the People's Republic of China, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, India, and Thailand, attended the commemorative meeting. The Chinese scholars invited to attend were Ji Xianlin, member of the Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and director of the Department of Oriental Languages ​​at Peking University, and Jian Zhuopo, member of the Comprehensive Survey Committee of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

　　In his congratulatory message to the meeting, Professor Ji Xianlin expressed his hope to strengthen friendly cooperation between Chinese and Burmese scientists and to strengthen scientific exchanges between our two countries.

### 缅甸研究会纪念成立五十周年  我国季羡林教授致贺辞希望加强科学交流

1959-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　缅甸研究会纪念成立五十周年
    我国季羡林教授致贺辞希望加强科学交流
    据新华社仰光28日电　缅甸研究会五十周年纪念会今天在缅甸文化部长吴漆东主持下在仰光大学开幕。
    大约二百名缅甸学者以及来自中华人民共和国、越南民主共和国、印度和泰国的学者参加了纪念会。应邀出席的中国学者是中国科学院哲学社会科学部学部委员、北京大学东方语文系主任季羡林，中国科学院综合考察委员会委员简焯坡。
    季羡林教授在向会议致贺词时表示希望加强中国和缅甸科学家之间的友好合作，加强我们两国之间的科学交流。
