Title: Exchange of Instruments of Ratification for Sino-Soviet Consular Treaty

Author: 
Date: 1959-12-21
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 19th: China and the Soviet Union exchanged instruments of ratification for the Sino-Soviet Consular Treaty in Moscow on the 19th. Representing the Chinese side in the exchange of instruments of ratification was Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao, and representing the Soviet side was Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Firyubin.

　　The Sino-Soviet Consular Treaty was signed in Beijing on June 23, 1959. The treaty comes into effect from the date of the exchange of instruments of ratification.

### 中苏互换领事条约批准书

1959-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　中苏互换领事条约批准书
    新华社莫斯科19日电　中国和苏联双方19日在莫斯科互换了中苏领事条约批准书。代表中国方面互换批准书的是中国驻苏联大使刘晓，代表苏联方面的是苏联外交部副部长费留宾。
    中苏领事条约是1959年6月23日在北京签字的。条约从互换批准书之日起生效。
