Title: US Military Creates Domestic Tensions; Suggests Bombers Carrying Hydrogen Bombs Patrol Day and Night
Author:
Date: 1959-12-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th. New York dispatch: The "New York Post" revealed on December 3rd that the US military plans to intensify the creation of domestic tensions in order to incite war hysteria.

	The newspaper said in an article that the US Air Force has suggested that the United States maintain a 24-hour, round-the-clock so-called "airborne alert," keeping B-52 bombers constantly flying overhead, and also loaded with "hydrogen bombs to wipe the Soviet Union off the face of the earth." The article said that this dangerous plan would cost more than ten billion US dollars annually.

### 美国军方制造国内紧张气氛  建议带氢弹的轰炸机日夜巡逻

1959-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    美国军方制造国内紧张气氛
    建议带氢弹的轰炸机日夜巡逻
    新华社9日讯　纽约消息：12月3日的“纽约邮报”透露，美国军方计划加紧在国内制造紧张气氛以煽动战争歇斯底里。
    这家报纸在一篇文章中说，美国空军建议，美国保持二十四小时日夜不断的所谓“空中警戒”，让B—52型轰炸机不断在上空飞行，而且还装着“把苏联从地面上消灭的氢弹”。文章说，这项危险计划每年要花掉十多亿美元。
