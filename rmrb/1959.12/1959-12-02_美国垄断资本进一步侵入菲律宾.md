Title: U.S. Monopoly Capital Further Encroaches on the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1959-12-02
Page: 6

    Manila News: U.S. monopoly capital is further encroaching on the Philippines.
    The Philippine International Harvester Company, belonging to the United States, recently invested another 3.5 million pesos in the Philippines to build a new factory.
    First National City Bank of New York bought back the Ayala Building in Binondo, worth 5.9 million pesos, which the American bank sold in 1940.
    The Philippine Resources and Development Company, which first discovered oil in Cebu Province in July this year, said in early November that Americans not residing in the Philippines had already controlled the $2.5 million worth of shares issued by the company in the United States. ### 美国垄断资本进一步侵入菲律宾

1959-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    美国垄断资本进一步侵入菲律宾
    马尼拉消息：美国垄断资本正在进一步侵入菲律宾。
    属于美国的菲律宾国际收割机公司最近又在菲律宾投资三百五十万比索，建造一座新的工厂。
    纽约第一花旗银行买回在比囊多的一座价值五百九十万比索的阿亚拉大厦，这座大厦是这家美国银行在1940年卖掉的。
    今年7月在宿务省第一次发现石油的菲律宾资源和开发公司在11月初说，不居住在菲律宾的美国人已经控制了这家公司在美国发行的二百五十万美元的股票。（附图片）
