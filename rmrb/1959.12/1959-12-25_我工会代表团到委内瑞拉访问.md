Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Visits Venezuela
Author:
Date: 1959-12-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Caracas, December 23rd: The Chinese trade union delegation currently visiting Latin America arrived in the Venezuelan capital today from Uruguay. The delegation is visiting Venezuela at the invitation of the Confederation of Venezuelan Workers. Prominent figures from Venezuelan trade unions, political and cultural organizations, including Bernardo Pérez Salinas, Deputy of the Democratic Action Party (the ruling party) and International Relations Secretary of the Confederation of Venezuelan Workers, Martín Ramírez, Communist Party Deputy and Vice Chairman of the Confederation of Venezuelan Workers, as well as overseas Chinese in Caracas, welcomed the Chinese trade union delegation at Maiquetía International Airport.

### 我工会代表团到委内瑞拉访问

1959-12-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　我工会代表团到委内瑞拉访问
    据新华社加拉加斯23日电　正在拉丁美洲访问的中国工会代表团今日从乌拉圭到达委内瑞拉首都。代表团是应委内瑞拉工人联合会的邀请到委内瑞拉访问的。到迈凯蒂亚国际机场欢迎中国工会代表团的有委内瑞拉工会、政治及文化团体的著名人士，其中有执政党民主行动党众议员、委内瑞拉工人联合会国际关系书记贝尔纳多·佩雷兹·萨利纳斯，共产党众议员、委内瑞拉工人联合会副主席马丁·拉米雷斯以及在加拉加斯的华侨。
