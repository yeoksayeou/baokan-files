Title: All-China Youth Federation and All-China Students' Federation Send Telegram Supporting Japanese Youth's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1959-12-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: The All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation today sent a telegram to the Democratic Youth League of Japan, and requested it to forward the message to the Central Joint Struggle Conference of Japanese youth and students to prevent the revision of the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty," expressing full support for the tenth unified action held by the Japanese people and youth and students to oppose the revision of the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty." The telegram said: "The heroic struggle of the Japanese people and youth and students is not only to defend the interests and security of the Japanese nation and prevent the revival of Japanese militarism, but also to defend peace in Asia and the world. We sincerely wish the Japanese youth, students, and the broad masses of the Japanese people to continue to strengthen unity, take unified action, and achieve greater victories in the struggle for national independence, peace, democracy, and neutrality."

### 我国青联和学联发出电报  支持日本青年的斗争

1959-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

　　我国青联和学联发出电报
    支持日本青年的斗争
    据新华社22日讯　中华全国青年联合会和中华全国学生联合会今天打电报给日本民主青年联盟，并请它转致日本青年学生阻止修改日美“安全条约”中央共同斗争会议，表示全力支持日本人民和青年、学生为反对修改日美“安全条约”而举行的第十次统一行动。电报说：“日本人民和青年学生的英勇斗争，不仅是为了保卫日本民族的利益和安全，制止日本军国主义的复活，而且也是为了保卫亚洲和世界的和平。我们衷心祝日本青年、学生和广大日本人民一起，继续加强团结，统一行动，在争取祖国独立、和平、民主和中立的斗争中获得更大的胜利。”
