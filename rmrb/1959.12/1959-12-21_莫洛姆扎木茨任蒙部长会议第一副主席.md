Title: Molomjamts Appointed First Deputy Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers
Author: 
Date: 1959-12-21
Page: 5

　　Molomjamts Appointed First Deputy Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 19th: According to today's "Truth Daily" of Mongolia, the Presidium of the Mongolian Great People's Hural appointed Molomjamts as the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 莫洛姆扎木茨任蒙部长会议第一副主席

1959-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　莫洛姆扎木茨任蒙部长会议第一副主席
    新华社乌兰巴托19日电　据蒙古“真理报”今天报道，蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团任命莫洛姆扎木茨为蒙古人民共和国部长会议第一副主席。
