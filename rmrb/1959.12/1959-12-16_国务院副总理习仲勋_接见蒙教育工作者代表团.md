Title: Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun Receives Mongolian Educational Workers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-12-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 15th. Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, received the Mongolian educational workers delegation led by the First Deputy Minister of Education of the Mongolian People's Republic, Herrlo, this morning.

Those present at the meeting included Dong Chuncai, Vice Minister of Education, and others.

### 国务院副总理习仲勋  接见蒙教育工作者代表团

1959-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院副总理习仲勋
    接见蒙教育工作者代表团
    新华社15日讯　国务院副总理习仲勋今天上午接见了以蒙古人民共和国教育部第一副部长赫尔洛为首的蒙古教育工作者代表团。
    接见时，在座的有教育部副部长董纯才等。
