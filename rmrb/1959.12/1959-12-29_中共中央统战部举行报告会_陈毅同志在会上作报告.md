Title: United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee Holds Report Meeting; Comrade Chen Yi Delivers Report at the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-12-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 28th: The United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee held a report meeting this morning. Comrade Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, delivered a report at the meeting. Those attending the report meeting included representatives attending the Second National Congress of the China Democratic National Construction Association and the Third Member Representative Congress of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, as well as deputies to the National People's Congress in Beijing, members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, members of various democratic parties, non-party democrats, people's representatives of Beijing Municipality, and members of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC.

### 中共中央统战部举行报告会  陈毅同志在会上作报告

1959-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央统战部举行报告会
    陈毅同志在会上作报告
    新华社28日讯　中共中央统一战线工作部今天上午举行报告会，中共中央政治局委员陈毅同志在会上作了报告。参加报告会的，有出席中国民主建国会第二次全国代表大会和中华全国工商业联合会第三届会员代表大会的代表，还有在京的全国人民代表大会代表、政协全国委员会委员、各民主党派的成员和无党派民主人士，北京市人民代表、政协委员等。
