Title: Sea Trial
Author: Sha Bai
Date: 1959-12-22
Page: 8

　　Sea Trial
　　Sha Bai
　　The waves on the Huangpu River
　　Beat like ten thousand hands clapping,
　　Shanghai stands on both banks,
　　Peering on tiptoes.
　　Every ship and vessel
　　Blasts its whistle.
　　This is the motherland's new freighter
　　Setting off on its sea trial along the river.
　　The tall, silver bow
　　Ploughs through vast waves,
　　As the decorated hull passes,
　　Patches of rosy clouds fall upon the river.
　　Goodbye, city!
　　Goodbye, factories!
　　Ahead are thousands of miles,
　　The sea is waiting impatiently.
　　Sailing across the chest of the East Sea,
　　The sun sends a golden cloak,
　　Gazing boundlessly,
　　The motherland is bold and broad.

### 试航
沙白
1959-12-22
第8版()
专栏：

　　试航
　　莎白
　　黄浦江上浪花
　　鼓起万双手掌，
　　上海站在两岸，
　　踮着足尖张望。
　　每只轮船舰艇
　　都把汽笛拉响。
　　这是祖国新海轮
　　踏着江路去试航。
　　高高银色船头
　　犁开万顷波浪，
　　结采的船身过处，
　　片片朝霞落江上。
　　再见吧，城市！
　　再见吧，工厂！
　　前面千万里路，
　　大海等得心慌。
　　驶过东海的胸脯，
　　太阳送来金色大氅，
　　纵目无边无际，
　　祖国豪迈宽广。
