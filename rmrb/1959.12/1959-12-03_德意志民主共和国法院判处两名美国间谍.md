Title: German Democratic Republic Court Sentences Two American Spies
Author:
Date: 1959-12-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 28th. According to a report by the German News Agency: The Cottbus Court of the German Democratic Republic concluded the trial of American spies Brochwitz and Reba.

The court confirmed that they had committed military and economic espionage crimes and therefore sentenced them to fifteen years of imprisonment.

### 德意志民主共和国法院判处两名美国间谍

1959-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国法院判处两名美国间谍
    新华社柏林28日电　据德意志通讯社报道：德意志民主共和国科特布斯法院结束了对美国间谍布罗赫维茨和雷巴的审讯。
    法庭确认，他们犯有军事上和经济上的间谍罪行，因此判处十五年徒刑。
