Title: Chinese and Japanese People Fight Side-by-Side Against US Aggressive War Policy; All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegram Congratulating the National Conference to Prevent Revision of the "Security Treaty"
Author:
Date: 1959-12-25
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 24th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram on the 23rd to the National Conference to Prevent Revision of the US-Japan "Security Treaty," warmly congratulating the convocation of the Third National Congress of the National Conference, and wishing the congress complete success.
    The All-China Federation of Trade Unions said in this telegram, which was forwarded to the National Conference through the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan: "The Japanese people's struggle against the revision of the US-Japan 'Security Treaty' is growing stronger, powerfully demonstrating the Japanese people's will and determination to oppose the revival of militarism and oppose the US-Japan military alliance."
    The telegram said: "Chinese workers will stand closely with you to jointly fight against the aggressive and war policies of US imperialism and to defend peace in the Far East and the world." The telegram said: "We believe that through the convening of your conference, the Japanese people will be more united and achieve new and greater success in the struggle against the revision of the US-Japan 'Security Treaty'."

### 中日两国人民并肩反对美国侵略战争政策  我全总电贺日本阻止修改“安全条约”国民会议代表大会

1959-12-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　中日两国人民并肩反对美国侵略战争政策
    我全总电贺日本阻止修改“安全条约”国民会议代表大会
    新华社24日讯　中华全国总工会23日打电报给日本阻止修改日美“安全条约”国民会议，热烈祝贺国民会议第三次全国代表大会的召开，并祝大会圆满成功。
    中华全国总工会在这封通过日本工会总评议会转致国民会议的电报中说：“日本人民反对修改日美‘安全条约’的斗争日趋壮大，这有力地显示出日本人民反对复活军国主义、反对日美军事同盟的意志和决心。”
    电报说：“中国工人将同你们紧密地站在一起，为反对美帝国主义的侵略政策和战争政策、保卫远东和世界和平而共同奋斗。”电报说：“我们相信，通过贵大会的召开，日本人民将会更加团结一致，在反对修改日美‘安全条约’的斗争中获得新的更大的成就。”
