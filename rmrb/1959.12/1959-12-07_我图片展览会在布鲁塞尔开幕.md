Title: Chinese Photo Exhibition Opens in Brussels
Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, December 5th. A Chinese photo exhibition opened this morning at the Central Station Exhibition Hall here in Brussels.
    More than one hundred prominent figures attended the opening ceremony, including representatives from the arts, politics, and business, as well as cultural attachés from some countries' embassies and legations in Belgium. Mrs. Blum, a member of the Presidium of the World Peace Council, was also present.
    Mr. Rocher, secretary of the Economic Committee of the Belgian-Chinese Association, said in his opening speech that he hoped the exhibition would help the Belgian people understand the remarkable achievements China has made in the past decade. He also expressed his hope that this exhibition would help further strengthen cultural exchanges and mutual understanding between the peoples of China and Belgium.
    The Chinese photo exhibition is sponsored by the Belgian-Chinese Association. It will be on display for a total of three weeks.

### 我图片展览会在布鲁塞尔开幕

1959-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    我图片展览会在布鲁塞尔开幕
    新华社布鲁塞尔5日电　中国图片展览会今天上午在这里的中央车站展览厅开幕。
    一百余位著名人士参加了开幕式，他们之中包括艺术家、政界和商界的代表以及一些国家驻比利时大使馆和公使馆的文化专员。出席的还有世界和平理事会主席团委员布伦姆夫人。
    比中协会经济委员会书记罗歇在开幕辞中说，希望展览会能帮助比利时人民了解中国在过去十年里所取得的惊人成就。他还表示希望这一展览会能有助于进一步加强中比两国人民之间的文化交流和相互了解。
    中国图片展览会是由比中协会主办的。它一共将展出三星期。
