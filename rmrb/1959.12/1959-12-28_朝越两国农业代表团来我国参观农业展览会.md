Title: Agricultural Delegations from North Korea and Vietnam Arrive in China to Visit Agricultural Exhibition

Author:

Date: 1959-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: The Korean agricultural delegation of two members, headed by Hong Yeon-gui, Deputy Minister of Agriculture of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the agricultural delegation of three members, headed by Nguyen Tao, Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Forestry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, who attended the Second Congress of Agricultural and Pastoral Cooperatives in the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of December 27th at the invitation of Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture of China, to visit the National Agricultural Exhibition.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Cai Ziwei, Vice Minister of Agriculture, and Li Qiangfen, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Danzangsuren, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in China, Ma Dongshan, Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China, and Pham Binh, Counselor of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 朝越两国农业代表团来我国参观农业展览会

1959-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝越两国农业代表团来我国参观农业展览会
    新华社27日讯　赴蒙古人民共和国参加第二届农牧业合作社代表大会的，以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国副农业相洪渊龟为首的朝鲜农业代表团二人，以越南民主共和国农林部副部长阮造为首的农业代表团三人，在参加会议以后，应我国农业部部长廖鲁言的邀请，于12月27日下午乘飞机到达北京，前来我国参观全国农业展览会。
    到机场欢迎的，有农业部副部长蔡子伟，外交部第二亚洲司副司长李强奋等人。
    蒙古驻华大使馆临时代办丹格苏伦，朝鲜驻华大使馆参赞马东山，越南驻华大使馆参赞范平也到机场欢迎。
