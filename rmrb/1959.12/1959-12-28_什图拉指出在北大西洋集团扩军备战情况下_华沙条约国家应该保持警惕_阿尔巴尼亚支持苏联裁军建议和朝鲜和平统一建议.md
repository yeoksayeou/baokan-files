Title: Shtylla Points Out that Warsaw Pact Countries Should Remain Vigilant in Face of NATO Arms Expansion; Albania Supports Soviet Disarmament Proposals and Korean Peaceful Reunification Proposals
Author:
Date: 1959-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 26th: The Fourth Session of the Fourth People's Assembly of Albania concluded on the 25th after two days of discussions.

　　At the meeting on the 25th, all the representatives unanimously approved Albania's 1960 National Economic Development Plan and the 1960 National Fiscal Budget. The meeting also unanimously adopted resolutions supporting the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union's appeal to the parliaments of all countries on the issue of striving for complete and thorough disarmament, and supporting the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea's letter to the parliaments of all countries on the peaceful reunification of Korea.

　　At the meeting on the 25th, Foreign Minister Shtylla delivered a report on the current international situation and Albania's foreign policy. Shtylla said that facts have proven that Lenin's policy of peaceful diplomacy has overcome capitalism's policy of war, and the war policies and plans implemented by countries headed by U.S. imperialism have failed. He pointed out that Albania supports all initiatives made by the Soviet Union to safeguard peace, supports the Soviet Union's proposal for complete and thorough disarmament, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's proposal for the peaceful reunification of Korea. Shtylla said that the Albanian people resolutely support the People's Republic of China's legitimate right to liberate Taiwan and other Chinese islands from the armed occupation of the United States. The Albanian government unreservedly supports the positive efforts made by the People's Republic of China to consolidate and expand cooperation among Asian countries in accordance with the spirit of the Bandung Principles. We believe that efforts in this spirit, such as the recent letter from Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai to Indian Prime Minister Nehru, will resolve the temporary and unpleasant border disputes between the two friendly neighbors (China and India). Shtylla condemned the United Nations for not determining China's legitimate status under U.S. intervention. The Albanian government, together with all friendly governments that take a correct and realistic stand on this issue, strongly condemns this unreasonable and egregious position. He will continue to fight for the recognition of China's legitimate rights in the United Nations.

　　Shtylla said that in the face of the existence and arms expansion of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, the Warsaw Pact countries should remain vigilant. He also condemned the U.S. State Department's arrogant clamor for the so-called "liberation" of Albania.

### 什图拉指出在北大西洋集团扩军备战情况下  华沙条约国家应该保持警惕  阿尔巴尼亚支持苏联裁军建议和朝鲜和平统一建议

1959-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　什图拉指出在北大西洋集团扩军备战情况下
    华沙条约国家应该保持警惕
    阿尔巴尼亚支持苏联裁军建议和朝鲜和平统一建议
    新华社地拉那26日电　阿尔巴尼亚第四届人民议会第四次会议经过两天的讨论后在25日闭幕。
    在25日的会议上，全体代表一致通过了阿尔巴尼亚1960年发展国民经济计划和1960年国家财政预算。会议还一致通过决议，支持苏联最高苏维埃就争取全面彻底裁军问题告世界各国议会书和支持朝鲜最高人民会议就和平统一朝鲜问题给世界各国议会的信。
    在25日的会议上，外交部长什图拉作了关于目前国际形势和阿尔巴尼亚外交政策的报告。什图拉说，事实证明，列宁的和平外交政策已胜过资本主义的战争政策，以美国帝国主义为首的国家所执行的战争政策和计划已经失败了。他指出，阿尔巴尼亚支持苏联为维护和平所作出的一切倡议，支持苏联提出的全面彻底裁军的建议和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国提出的和平统一朝鲜的建议。什图拉说，阿尔巴尼亚人民坚决地支持中华人民共和国为从美国的武装强占下解放台湾和中国其他岛屿的法定权利。阿尔巴尼亚政府无保留地支持中华人民共和国为根据万隆原则的精神巩固和扩大亚洲国家的合作所作出的积极的努力。我们相信根据这一精神的努力，中国国务院总理周恩来致印度尼赫鲁总理最近的一封信将使两个友好的邻国（中国和印度）之间的暂时的令人不愉快的边境纠纷获得解决。什图拉谴责在美国的干预下联合国还没有确定中国的合法地位。阿尔巴尼亚政府和所有对待这一问题采取正确和现实立场的友好政府在一起，强力谴责这一不合理的恶劣的立场。他将继续为承认中国在联合国的法定权利而斗争。
    什图拉说，在北大西洋公约组织的存在和扩军备战的情况下，华沙条约国家应保持警惕。他并谴责了美国国务院发出的所谓“解放”阿尔巴尼亚的狂妄叫嚣。
