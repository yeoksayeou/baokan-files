Title: Chairman Zhu De Receives All Members of the Algerian Football Team
Author:
Date: 1959-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 21st: Chairman Zhu De received all members of the Algerian Republic National Football Team this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were: Zhang Su, member and deputy secretary-general of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Rong Gaotang, vice chairman of the Sports Commission, and Zhang Lianhua, secretary-general of the All-China Sports Federation.

During the meeting, the leader of the football team, Mustapha, presented their team flag to Chairman Zhu De.

### 朱德委员长接见阿尔及利亚足球队全体人员

1959-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　朱德委员长接见阿尔及利亚足球队全体人员
    新华社21日讯　朱德委员长今天下午接见了阿尔及利亚共和国国家足球队全体人员。
    在座的有：人大常委会委员、副秘书长张苏，体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠，中华全国体育总会秘书长张联华。
    接见时，足球队领队穆斯塔法把他们的队旗献给了朱德委员长。
