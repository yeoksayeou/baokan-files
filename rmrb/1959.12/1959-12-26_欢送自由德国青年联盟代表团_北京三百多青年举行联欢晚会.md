Title: Welcoming the Free German Youth League Delegation: Over 300 Beijing Youth Hold Evening Gala

Author: 
Date: 1959-12-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: Beijing youth held a welcoming evening gala for the Free German Youth League delegation from the German Democratic Republic who are about to return home tonight.

Hu Keshi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, attended the gala.

The Free German Youth League delegation arrived in our country for a visit on December 1st at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League. After visiting Harbin, Nanjing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other places, they returned to Beijing this afternoon.

### 欢送自由德国青年联盟代表团  北京三百多青年举行联欢晚会

1959-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　欢送自由德国青年联盟代表团
    北京三百多青年举行联欢晚会
    据新华社25日讯　今晚，北京市三百多名青年为欢送即将返国的德意志民主共和国自由德国青年联盟代表团举行联欢晚会。
    中国共青团中央委员会书记处书记胡克实、共青团北京市委员会第一书记王照华参加了晚会。
    自由德国青年联盟代表团是应中国共青团中央委员会的邀请，在12月1日到达我国访问的。他们在访问了哈尔滨、南京、上海、广州、武汉等地以后，今天下午回到北京。
