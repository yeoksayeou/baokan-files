Title: Korean Youth League Delegation to Return Home After Visiting Jilin Province
Author:
Date: 1959-12-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency - The delegation of the Korean Democratic Youth League, which came to China for a visit at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, left Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 25th to visit Jilin Province. After visiting Jilin Province, the delegation will return home via Shenyang. Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, were among those who saw them off at the station. During their stay in China, the delegation visited Beijing, Wuhan, Changsha, Guangzhou, Shanghai and other cities, and received a warm welcome from the youth of various places.

### 朝鲜青年同盟代表团到吉林省访问后将回国

1959-12-29
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜青年同盟代表团到吉林省访问后将回国
    新华社讯　应中国共青团中央委员会邀请来我国访问的朝鲜民主青年同盟代表团，25日下午乘火车离开北京去吉林省访问。代表团在吉林省访问以后，将经过沈阳回国。到车站送行的，有共青团中央书记处书记罗毅，共青团北京市委第一书记王照华等。这个代表团在中国期间，曾先后访问了北京、武汉、长沙、广州、上海等城市，受到了各地青年的热烈欢迎。
