Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Sends Telegram to the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos Strongly Protesting the Lao Army's Invasion of Territory
Author:
Date: 1959-12-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 9th: Premier Pham Van Dong of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam today sent a telegram to Phoui Sananikone, Prime Minister of the Government of the Kingdom of Laos, strongly protesting the serious invasion of the territory of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam by the army of the Kingdom of Laos. The telegram stated that at 4:00 a.m. on December 6, 1959, a unit of the army of the Kingdom of Laos crossed the Lao border and invaded the territory of Bien Fa Village, Ghenh Do Commune, Xiangyang County, Yi'an Province, of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, killing five people, wounding three, and burning two natural villages in Bien Fa Village; destroying all houses and grain of the local people.

The telegram pointed out: "The above-mentioned actions of the army of the Kingdom of Laos seriously violated the sovereignty and territory of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, caused loss of life and property to the people in the border area of Vietnam, and damaged the neighborly relations between Vietnam and Laos."

The telegram said: "The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam strongly protests the above-mentioned extremely serious actions of the army of the Kingdom of Laos, and demands that the Government of the Kingdom of Laos immediately take measures to prevent similar actions from happening again, and punish those responsible for the above-mentioned serious acts."

The telegram concluded: "The Government of the Kingdom of Laos must bear full responsibility for the above-mentioned losses and consequences caused by the army of the Kingdom of Laos."

### 范文同总理致电老挝王国首相  强烈抗议老挝军队侵犯领土

1959-12-10
第5版()
专栏：

    范文同总理致电老挝王国首相
    强烈抗议老挝军队侵犯领土
    新华社河内9日电　越南民主共和国政府总理范文同今天打电报给老挝王国政府首相培·萨纳尼空，强烈抗议老挝王国军队严重侵犯越南民主共和国领土的行为。电报说，1959年12月6日早晨四点钟，老挝王国军队一支部队越过老挝边界，侵入了越南民主共和国宜安省相杨县更都乡边发村的领土，开枪打死五人，打伤三人，焚烧了边发村的两个自然村；烧毁了当地人民的全部房屋和粮食。
    电报指出：“老挝王国军队的上述行动严重地侵犯了越南民主共和国的主权和领土，给越南边界地区的人民造成了生命和财产的损失，损害了越老两国之间的邻邦关系。”
    电报说：“越南民主共和国政府强烈抗议老挝王国军队上述极其严重的行动，要求老挝王国政府立即采取措施，防止类似行动的再次发生，并且惩治上述严重行为的人员。”
    电报最后说：“老挝王国政府必须对老挝王国军队制造的上述损失和后果负全部责任。”
