Title: Bainqen Erdini, etc. Leave Shanghai for Nanning to Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-12-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 28th: Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, along with their entourage, accompanied by Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, and Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, concluded their visit in Shanghai and left Shanghai by special train at 4:00 PM today for Nanning.

This morning, Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain and Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal bid farewell to Ke Qingshi, First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Mayor of Shanghai, as well as leaders of the Party, government, and army in Shanghai, and had a cordial conversation with Ke Qingshi. Ke Qingshi hosted a banquet at noon to see them off.

Those who saw them off at the station today included Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Deputy Major of Shanghai; Lieutenant-General Liu Fei, Deputy Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Shu Xincheng, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC; as well as Shanghai Vice Mayors Niu Shucai, Song Richang, Song Jiwen, and Jin Zhonghua, etc.

Bainqen Erdini and Pagbalha arrived in Shanghai on the 18th. During their stay in Shanghai, they visited the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition, Shanghai Electric Motor Factory, Shanghai No. 2 Cigarette Factory, Huafu Gold Pen Factory, Shanghai Watch Factory, Synthetic Fiber Factory, Shanghai No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory, the Light Industry Research Institute, and the Fine Arts Film Studio, etc. On the 26th, Bainqen Erdini and Pagbalha also attended the Second Plenary Session of the Second Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, which is currently being held, and Bainqen Erdini also spoke at the meeting.

### 班禅额尔德尼等  离上海去南宁参观访问

1959-12-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　班禅额尔德尼等
    离上海去南宁参观访问
    新华社上海28日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和政协全国委员会副主席、西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰以及他们的随行人员，在中共中央统战部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋，中共西藏工委副书记周仁山陪同下，结束了在上海的参观访问，今天下午四时乘专车离开上海前往南宁。
    今天上午，班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和帕巴拉·卓列朗杰向中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施及上海市党、政府和军队的领导人辞行，并和柯庆施进行了亲切交谈。柯庆施在中午举行宴会欢送他们。
    今天到车站欢送的有中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，中国人民解放军上海警备区副司令员刘飞中将，政协上海市委员会副主席舒新城，以及上海市副市长牛树才、宋日昌、宋季文、金仲华等。
    班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉是18日到达上海的。在上海期间，曾参观了上海工业展览会、上海电机厂、上海卷烟二厂、华孚金笔厂、上海手表厂、合成纤维厂、上海印染一厂、轻工业研究所和美术电影制片厂等单位。26日，班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉还出席了正在举行的政协上海市第二届委员会第二次全体会议，班禅额尔德尼还在会上讲了话。
