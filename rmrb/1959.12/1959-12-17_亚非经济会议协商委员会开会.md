Title: Afro-Asian Economic Conference Consultative Committee Meets
Author:
Date: 1959-12-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, December 14th: The second meeting of the Afro-Asian Economic Conference Consultative Committee opened here this afternoon. Representatives from the member committees of China, India, Indonesia, Iraq, Japan, Pakistan, Ghana, Libya, Sudan, and the United Arab Republic attended today's opening meeting. The representative from Ethiopia has not yet arrived. The first meeting of this committee was held in Bandung in April this year.

　　The second meeting of the committee will study the draft charter of the Afro-Asian Economic Cooperation Organization, review the Secretary-General's report on the activities of the Cairo office of the Afro-Asian Economic Cooperation Organization, approve the financial budget, and decide on the location and date of the next Afro-Asian Economic Conference.

　　This meeting will last until the 18th of this month.

### 亚非经济会议协商委员会开会

1959-12-17
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非经济会议协商委员会开会
    新华社大马士革14日电　亚非经济会议协商委员会第二次会议今天下午在这里开幕。来自中国、印度、印度尼西亚、伊拉克、日本、巴基斯坦、加纳、利比亚、苏丹和阿拉伯联合共和国等国的会员委员会的代表出席了今天的开幕会议。埃塞俄比亚的代表还没有到达。这个委员会的第一次会议是今年4月在万隆举行的。
    委员会的第二次会议将研究亚非经济合作组织的章程草案，审查秘书长所作的关于亚非经济合作组织开罗办事处的活动的报告，批准财政预算和决定下次亚非经济会议的地点和日期。
    这次会议将开到本月18日。
