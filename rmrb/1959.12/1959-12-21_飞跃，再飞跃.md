Title: Leap Forward, Again Leap Forward
Author: Gao Fang, Shi Zuocai
Date: 1959-12-21
Page: 2

　　Wax plum buds are forming,
　　Winter snow hasn't yet fallen,
　　Good news spreads from the industrial front!
　　Nineteen sixty, you are late!
　　Good! Good!
　　The red flag of the General Line flies high,
　　Listen! The Xin'an River water laughs merrily,
　　Look! The Half-Mountain Iron and Steel Plant's furnace fire dyes the clouds red
　　Weaving girls joyfully watch the shuttles fly,
　　Colorful clouds shine brilliantly;
　　This song of the Great Leap Forward,
　　How beautifully it sings!
　　Good! Whip the fast horse again,
　　So that this year's fruits are bigger,
　　And next year's flowers bloom earlier,
　　The working class's slogan:
　　"Leap forward! Again leap forward!"
               Zhejiang　Gao Fang　Shi Zuocai

### 飞跃，再飞跃
高钫　施佐才
1959-12-21
第2版()
专栏：

    飞跃，再飞跃
　　腊梅含苞，
　　冬雪未飘，
　　工业战线传捷报！
　　一九六○年，你来迟了！
　　好啊！好啊！
　　总路线的红旗飘得高，
　　听！新安江水哗哗笑，
　　看！半山钢铁厂炉火染云霄
　　织女喜看梭子飞，
　　彩霞万丈闪闪耀；
　　这一支跃进曲，
　　唱得多美妙！
　　好啊！快马再加一鞭，
　　叫今年果实更大，
　　催来年花开更早，
　　工人阶级的口号：
　　“飞跃！再飞跃！”
               浙江　高钫　施佐才
