Title: Hu Yaobang Meets Soviet Communist Youth League Delegation; Over 800 Beijing Youth Gather with Delegation

Author:

Date: 1959-12-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat, met with the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League delegation led by Murtazaev this evening, and had a cordial conversation with them. After the meeting, Hu Yaobang and others had dinner with the delegation.

More than 800 youths from all walks of life in Beijing also held a get-together with the delegation this evening.

The Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League delegation arrived in Beijing on November 19th. The delegation left Beijing on November 24th to visit Hefei, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Wuhan, and returned to Beijing today.

### 胡耀邦会见苏共青团代表团  北京八百多青年同代表团联欢

1959-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    胡耀邦会见苏共青团代表团
    北京八百多青年同代表团联欢
    新华社8日讯　中国共产主义青年团中央委员会第一书记胡耀邦、书记处书记罗毅，今天晚间会见了以穆尔塔扎耶夫为首的苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团，并且同他们进行了亲切的交谈。会见后胡耀邦等并和代表团共进晚餐。
    北京市各界青年八百多人今天晚上还和代表团举行了联欢晚会。
    苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团是在11月19日到达北京的。代表团于11月24日离开北京到合肥、上海、广州、武汉进行了访问，在今天回到北京。
