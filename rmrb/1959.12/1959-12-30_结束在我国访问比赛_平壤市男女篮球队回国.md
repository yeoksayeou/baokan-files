Title: Pyongyang Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Return Home After Concluding Visit and Competition in Our Country
Author: 
Date: 1959-12-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 29th: The Pyongyang Men's and Women's Basketball Teams from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, having concluded their visit and competition in our country, left Shenyang by train on the 29th to return home.

### 结束在我国访问比赛  平壤市男女篮球队回国

1959-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在我国访问比赛
    平壤市男女篮球队回国
    据新华社沈阳29日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国平壤市男女篮球队，已经结束在我国的访问比赛，29日乘火车离开沈阳回国。
