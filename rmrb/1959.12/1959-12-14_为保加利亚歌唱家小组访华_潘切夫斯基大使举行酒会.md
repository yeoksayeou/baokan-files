Title: Ambassador Panchevski Holds Reception for Visit to China by Bulgarian Vocal Group
Author:
Date: 1959-12-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Ambassador Panchevski of Bulgaria in China held a reception tonight for the Bulgarian vocal group visiting China. The Bulgarian vocal group includes five singers and one pianist: mezzo-soprano soloist and Dimitrov Prize winner Na. Bao. Afiyan, bass soloist and Dimitrov Prize winner A. Bi. Milkovsky, tenor soloist Ni. Ji. Ztelafkov, soprano soloist La. Bi. Kanferti, soprano soloist and winner of the Second Bulgarian Singing and Instrumental Music Competition Li. Ji. Koshrukova, and pianist Bao. Kang. Kostov. Led by Mi. Liu. Katzalov, director of the China Section of the Bulgarian Committee for Friendly Relations with Foreign Countries and Cultural Relations, they arrived in Beijing on November 9 and have held many concerts in Beijing and some cities in our country, receiving a warm welcome from the audience. Tonight, except for mezzo-soprano soloist Na. Bao. Afiyan who was unwell, the other Bulgarian singers all attended the reception.
    Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Weizhi, Director of the Art Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, Xia Ting, Secretary-General of the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association, Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, Feng Jiaping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and Yuyi Xuan and other people from the capital's music circles were invited to attend the reception.
    Chinese and Bulgarian singers performed wonderful programs at the reception.

### 为保加利亚歌唱家小组访华  潘切夫斯基大使举行酒会

1959-12-14
第5版()
专栏：

    为保加利亚歌唱家小组访华
    潘切夫斯基大使举行酒会
    新华社13日讯　保加利亚驻中国大使潘切夫斯基今晚为保加利亚歌唱家小组访问中国举行酒会。保加利亚歌唱家小组包括五位歌唱家和一位钢琴家，他们是：女中音独唱家、季米特洛夫奖金获得者娜·包·阿菲扬，男低音独唱家、季米特洛夫奖金获得者阿·彼·米尔科夫斯基，男高音独唱家尼·季·兹特拉夫科夫，女高音独唱家拉·彼·康弗尔梯，女高音独唱家、第二届保加利亚歌唱和器乐比赛获奖者莉·基·科什鲁科娃和钢琴家包·康·科斯托夫。他们由保加利亚对外友好与文化联络委员会中国科科长米·留·卡察洛夫率领，在11月9日到达北京后，曾在北京和我国一些城市举行了多次音乐会，受到听众的热烈欢迎。今晚除女中音独唱家娜·包·阿菲扬因身体不好外，其他的保加利亚歌唱家们都参加了酒会。
    对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、外交部苏欧司副司长陈伯清、文化部艺术局局长周巍峙、中保友协秘书长夏霆、中国音乐家协会主席吕骥、北京市副市长冯基平和首都音乐界人士喻宜萱等应邀参加了酒会。
    中国歌唱家和保加利亚歌唱家们在酒会上演出了精彩节目。
