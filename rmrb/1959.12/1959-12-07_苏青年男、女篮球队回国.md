Title: Soviet Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. After visiting our country for more than three weeks, the Soviet National Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams returned to their country by plane from Beijing today.

During this visit, the guest team held a total of ten friendly matches and twenty games with our country's men's and women's basketball teams in Beijing, Tianjin, Nanjing, and Shanghai, with nearly 60,000 spectators. Their men's team won seven, drew two, and lost one; the women's team won eight and lost two. The guest team members' skilled basketball techniques, tenacious fighting will, and good moral character left a deep impression on our country's audiences.

During this visit to our country, the guest team also visited factories, schools, and people's communes, and toured scenic spots and historical sites. On November 23, when they visited the Shengfang People's Commune in the suburbs of Tianjin, they also participated in voluntary labor on a cultural center under construction. This cultural center has been decided to be named "China-Soviet Friendship Cultural Center."

### 苏青年男、女篮球队回国

1959-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏青年男、女篮球队回国
    新华社6日讯　苏联国家青年男、女篮球队在我国访问了三个多星期以后，已经在今天乘飞机离北京回国。
    客队这次先后在北京、天津、南京、上海同我国的男、女篮球队共举行了十次二十场友谊比赛，观众近六万人。他们的男子队十战七胜、二平、一负；女子队十战八胜、二负。客队队员熟练的篮球技巧、顽强的战斗意志和良好的道德作风，给我国观众留下了深刻的印象。
    客队这次在我国还参观了工厂、学校、人民公社，游览了名胜古迹。11月23日他们在天津参观郊区胜芳人民公社时，还参加了正在修建的一座文化馆的义务劳动。这座文化馆已决定命名为“中苏友好文化馆”。
