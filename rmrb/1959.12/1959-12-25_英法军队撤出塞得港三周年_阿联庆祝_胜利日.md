Title: United Arab Republic Celebrates "Victory Day" on the Third Anniversary of the Withdrawal of British and French Troops from Port Said
Author:
Date: 1959-12-25
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 23rd: The United Arab Republic today celebrated its "Victory Day," the third anniversary of the withdrawal of British and French troops from Port Said following the failure of the tripartite aggression against Egypt by Britain, France, and Israel in 1956.

　　UAR President Nasser and the vice presidents today went to Port Said to review the army, navy, air force, and people's resistance forces stationed there. Nasser also delivered a speech there.

　　Nasser said that the UAR's foreign policy is "to be friendly to countries that are friendly to us and hostile to countries that are hostile to us."

　　He expressed his hope that the relations between the UAR and the Soviet Union would be normal and friendly. He said: "We do not believe that ideological differences are an obstacle to cooperation and friendship."

　　Regarding relations with the United States, Nasser said: "We welcome its friendship."

　　Regarding Britain, Nasser stated that the UAR is "ready to forget the past."

　　In his speech, Nasser also vehemently attacked Iraqi Prime Minister Kassem.

　　Speaking of domestic affairs in the UAR, Nasser said that Vice President Amir, who is authorized to handle all affairs in the Syrian region, is facing a huge task that needs to be accomplished in a short period of time.

### 英法军队撤出塞得港三周年  阿联庆祝“胜利日”

1959-12-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　英法军队撤出塞得港三周年
    阿联庆祝“胜利日”
    新华社开罗23日电　阿拉伯联合共和国今天庆祝它的“胜利日”，这一天是1956年英、法和以色列三国侵略埃及失败后英法军队撤出塞得港的三周年。
    阿联总统纳赛尔和副总统们今天前往塞得港检阅了那里的陆海空军部队和人民抵抗部队。纳赛尔并且在那里发表了演说。
    纳赛尔说，阿联的外交政策是，“同对我们友好的国家友好，同敌视我们的国家敌对”。
    他希望阿联同苏联的关系正常和友好。他说：“我们不认为，意识形态的分歧是走向合作和友好的障碍。”
    关于同美国的关系，纳赛尔说：“我们欢迎它的友谊”。
    对于英国，纳赛尔表示，阿联“准备忘掉过去”。
    纳赛尔在演说中还猛烈攻击了伊拉克总理卡塞姆。
    纳赛尔在谈到阿联国内事务时说，他授予全权处理叙利亚地区事务的阿密尔副总统正面临着一个需要在短时间内实现的巨大任务。
