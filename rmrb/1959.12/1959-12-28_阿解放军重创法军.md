Title: Algerian Liberation Army Heavily Defeats French Army
Author:
Date: 1959-12-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 26th - The Supreme Command of the Algerian National Liberation Army announced in a military communique today that Algerian armed forces killed 113 French soldiers and wounded 31 during military operations from December 18th to 21st. They also destroyed 17 French military vehicles and a large number of tanks. Algerian demolition teams destroyed many enemy military facilities throughout the country.

The communique said that Algerian troops damaged power grids on the eastern and western borders during the offensive and opened a gap in the French "Morice Line."

The communique said that during this period, Algerian troops engaged in seven battles and six ambushes. They also attacked six French strongholds, some of which were completely destroyed.

### 阿解放军重创法军

1959-12-28
第5版()
专栏：

　　阿解放军重创法军
    新华社开罗26日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军最高司令部今天发表的军事公报说，阿尔及利亚武装部队在12月18日至21日期间的军事行动中，打死法军一百一十三名，打伤三十一名，并且击毁十七辆法国军用车辆和大量坦克。阿尔及利亚的爆破队在全国各地炸毁了很多敌人的军用设施。
    公报说，阿尔及利亚军队在进攻中破坏了东部和西部边境的电网，并且在法国人的“莫里斯防线”上打开了一个缺口。
    公报说，在此期间，阿尔及利亚军队进行了七次战斗和六次伏击。他们还攻击了六处法军据点，有些据点被全部摧毁。
