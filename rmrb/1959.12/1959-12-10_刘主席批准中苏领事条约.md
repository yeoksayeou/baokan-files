Title: Chairman Liu Approves Sino-Soviet Consular Treaty
Author:
Date: 1959-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th December.  Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China, in accordance with the decision of the 11th meeting of the Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress, approved on December 8, 1959, the Consular Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, signed in Beijing on June 23, 1959.

### 刘主席批准中苏领事条约

1959-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席批准中苏领事条约
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国刘少奇主席根据第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第十一次会议的决定，在1959年12月8日批准了1959年6月23日在北京签订的中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟领事条约。
