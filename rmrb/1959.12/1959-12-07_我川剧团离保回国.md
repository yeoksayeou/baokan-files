Title: Our Sichuan Opera Troupe Leaves Bulgaria for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, December 5th. The Chinese Sichuan Opera Troupe concluded its visit and performances abroad and left Sofia for home tonight. Chairman Avramova and Vice-Chairman Petrov of the Bulgarian Committee for Friendly and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and others went to the station to see them off. Zhu Qiwen, the Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, and other staff members of the embassy also went to the station to see them off.

The Chinese Sichuan Opera Troupe left Beijing on July 30 for visits abroad, and successively visited and performed in Poland, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, and Bulgaria for a period of four months.

### 我川剧团离保回国

1959-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    我川剧团离保回国
    新华社索非亚5日电　中国川剧团结束了在国外的访问演出，今晚离开索非亚回国。保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会主席阿弗拉莫娃、副主席彼得罗夫等曾到车站欢送。到车站欢送的还有中国驻保加利亚大使朱其文和使馆其他工作人员。
    中国川剧团是7月30日离开北京出国访问的，先后在波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国和保加利亚作了为期四个月的访问演出。
