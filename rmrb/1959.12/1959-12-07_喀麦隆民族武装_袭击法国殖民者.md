Title: Cameroonian National Armed Forces Attack French Colonialists
Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 6

    Cameroonian National Armed Forces
    Attack French Colonialists
    Xinhua News Agency, Yaoundé, December 5th: Cameroonian nationalists recently attacked a police post in Bamendu, western Cameroon. According to officials of the French colonial authorities on the 3rd, a "large group" of armed nationalists carried out the attack.

### 喀麦隆民族武装  袭击法国殖民者

1959-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

    喀麦隆民族武装
    袭击法国殖民者
    据新华社5日讯　雅温得消息：喀麦隆民族主义者最近袭击了喀麦隆西部巴曼杜的警察据点。据法国殖民当局官方人士3日透露，从事这次袭击的有“一大队”武装的民族主义者。
