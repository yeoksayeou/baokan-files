Title: Further Expanding Cultural Cooperation Between the Two Countries: China and Bulgaria Sign Next Year's Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1959-12-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, December 6th: China and Bulgaria signed the 1960 Implementation Plan for the Cultural Cooperation Agreement in Sofia on the 5th.

The Implementation Plan stipulates that the two countries will further expand brotherly cooperation and exchange experiences in science, health, education, literature, art, film, news, publishing, broadcasting, television, sports, and other fields next year.

Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Avramova, Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, signed the Implementation Plan.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, December 6th: Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, left Sofia by plane on the 6th to return to China.

Petrov, Vice Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Zhu Qiwu, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, and others saw him off at the airport.

### 进一步扩大两国文化方面合作  中保签订明年执行计划

1959-12-08
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步扩大两国文化方面合作
    中保签订明年执行计划
    新华社索非亚6日电　中国和保加利亚5日在索非亚签订了文化合作协定1960年执行计划。
    执行计划规定，两国明年将在科学、卫生、教育、文学、艺术、电影、新闻、出版、广播、电视、体育等方面进一步扩大兄弟般的合作和交流经验。
    在执行计划上签字的是：中国对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会主席阿弗拉莫娃。
    新华社索非亚6日电　中国对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，6日乘飞机离开索非亚回国。
    保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会副主席彼特罗夫、中国驻保加利亚大使朱其文等曾到机场送行。
