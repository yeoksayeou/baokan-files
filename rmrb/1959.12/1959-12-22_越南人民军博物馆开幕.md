Title: Vietnam People's Army Museum Opens
Author:
Date: 1959-12-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 21st: The Vietnam People's Army Museum solemnly opened in Hanoi today.

Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, Chairman Ton Duc Thang, Deputy Prime Ministers Truong Chinh and Phan Ke Toai, General Vo Nguyen Giap, senior generals of the People's Army, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam attended today's opening ceremony. After General Van Tien Dung, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Army, delivered the opening speech, Prime Minister Pham Van Dong cut the ribbon for the museum.

The museum is located near Ba Dinh Square in Hanoi, a place of historical significance. The museum is divided into three parts: before the August Revolution, the period of the War of Resistance, and after the restoration of peace. The exhibits of physical objects, charts, and photographs comprehensively introduce the history of the birth, growth, and strengthening of the People's Army. (Picture attached)

### 越南人民军博物馆开幕

1959-12-22
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南人民军博物馆开幕
    据新华社河内21日电　越南人民军博物馆今天在河内隆重开幕。
    范文同总理，孙德胜委员长，长征、潘继遂副总理，武元甲大将和人民军高级将领以及各国驻越南的外交使节参加了今天的开幕典礼。人民军总参谋长文进勇上将致开幕词后，范文同总理为博物馆剪采。
    博物馆的馆址设在具有历史意义的河内巴亭广场附近。博物馆分为八月革命前、抗战时期、和平恢复后三个部分。馆中展出的实物、图表和照片全面地介绍了人民军诞生、成长和壮大的历史。（附图片）
