Title: High-Strength Suspension Insulator Successfully Trial-Produced
Author:
Date: 1959-12-01
Page: 2

1959 New Products

	High-Strength Suspension Insulator Successfully Trial-Produced
	The Xi'an High-Voltage Electrical Porcelain Factory, in cooperation with the Electrical Porcelain Research Institute and Tsinghua University, has successfully trial-produced a high-strength suspension insulator for use on high-voltage lines.
	This product weighs 40% less and is 25% shorter in height than ordinary suspension insulators with the same technical specifications. Therefore, it can reduce the height of transmission towers and save a large amount of steel. It provides favorable conditions for China to develop ultra-high voltage and large-capacity transmission systems.

### 高强度悬式绝缘子试制成功

1959-12-01
第2版()
专栏：1959年的新产品

    高强度悬式绝缘子试制成功
    西安高压电瓷厂和电瓷研究所、清华大学合作试制成功一种高压线路上使用的高强度悬式绝缘子。
    这种产品比技术条件相同的普通悬式绝缘子重量减轻了40％，高度缩短了25％。因而可以降低输电杆塔的高度，节约大量钢材。它为我国发展超高压、大容量的输电系统提供了有利条件。
