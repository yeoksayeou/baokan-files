Title: Chinese Communist Party Delegation Visits Budapest
Author:
Date: 1959-12-06
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 4th. The Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Tan Zhenlin, which was invited to attend the Seventh Congress of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, has been visiting Budapest in recent days.

　　Accompanied by Ács Alajos, a member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, the delegation visited the Budapest "Emag" Agricultural Machinery Factory and the Nagytétény Pig Farm near Budapest on the 2nd and 3rd respectively. The delegation received a warm welcome during its visit to these two units.

　　The delegation, accompanied by Fehér Lajos, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, also visited the Michurin Agricultural Production Cooperative near Budapest on November 28. On the morning of the 29th, the delegation, accompanied by Ács Alajos, visited the city of the capital and the National Gallery, and visited workers' families in the new workers' residential area.

### 中共代表团在布达佩斯参观

1959-12-06
第3版()
专栏：

    中共代表团在布达佩斯参观
    新华社布达佩斯4日电　应邀前来参加匈牙利社会主义工人党第七次代表大会的以谭震林为首的中国共产党代表团，连日来在布达佩斯进行了参观访问。
    代表团在匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员阿策耳的陪同下，于2日和3日分别参观了布达佩斯“埃马格”农业机器厂和布达佩斯近郊的纳吉特特尼养猪场。代表团在参观这两个单位时受到了热烈的欢迎。
    代表团还曾在11月28日由匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员费赫尔陪同，参观了布达佩斯附近的米丘林农业生产合作社。29日上午，代表团在阿策耳的陪同下参观了首都的市容和民族画廊，并到新的工人住宅区访问了工人家庭。
