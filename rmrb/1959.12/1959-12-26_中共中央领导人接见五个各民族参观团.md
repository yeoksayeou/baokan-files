Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Visiting Groups from Five Ethnic Groups
Author:
Date: 1959-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th:  Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Comrades Zhu De, Chen Yi, and He Long, members of the Political Bureau, and Comrade Lu Dingyi, alternate member of the Political Bureau, received all members of the visiting groups from the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture in Jilin Province, the Baicheng Special District in Jilin Province, Chengde City in Hebei Province, and Benxi City in Liaoning Province this afternoon.

### 中共中央领导人接见五个各民族参观团

1959-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央领导人接见五个各民族参观团
    新华社25日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇、朱德，政治局委员陈毅、贺龙，政治局候补委员陆定一，在今天下午接见了宁夏回族自治区、吉林省延边朝鲜族自治州、吉林省白城专区、河北省承德市、辽宁省本溪市等五个各民族参观团的全体成员。
