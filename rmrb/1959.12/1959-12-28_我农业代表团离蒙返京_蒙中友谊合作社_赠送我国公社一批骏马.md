Title: Our Agricultural Delegation Leaves Mongolia for Beijing; "Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Cooperative" Presents a Batch of Fine Horses to Our Communes

Author:

Date: 1959-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 27th: The Chinese agricultural delegation headed by Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan left here by plane today to return to China. The delegation attended the Second Mongolian Agricultural and Pastoral Cooperatives Congress at the invitation of the Mongolian Ministry of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry.

Mongolian Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry Regvaral and Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Xie Fusheng saw them off at the airport.

On the 26th, the delegation visited the "Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Cooperative" in the Mongolian pastoral area. The cooperative expressed to the delegation its willingness to establish contact with a people's commune in Hebei Province, exchange production experience with each other, and present this people's commune with one hundred and fifty horses. The delegation stated that it would convey the cooperative's good intentions to a people's commune in Hebei Province.

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: The Chinese agricultural delegation of four people, headed by Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of December 27 after being invited to the Mongolian People's Republic to attend the Second Mongolian Agricultural and Pastoral Cooperatives Congress.

Among those who went to the airport to greet them were Cai Ziwei, Vice Minister of Agriculture, and Li Qiangfen, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Danggasuren, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 我农业代表团离蒙返京  “蒙中友谊合作社”赠送我国公社一批骏马

1959-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　我农业代表团离蒙返京
    “蒙中友谊合作社”赠送我国公社一批骏马
    新华社乌兰巴托27日电　以农业部长廖鲁言为首的中国农业代表团今天离开这里乘飞机回国。代表团应蒙古农牧业部的邀请参加了蒙古第二届农牧业合作社代表大会。
    蒙古部长会议副主席、农牧业部长热格瓦拉尔等和中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生到机场送行。
    代表团26日曾到蒙古牧区参观访问了“蒙中友谊合作社”。这个合作社向代表团表示愿和中国河北省的一个人民公社建立联系，相互交流生产经验，并赠送这个人民公社一百五十匹马。代表团表示，将向河北省的一个人民公社转达合作社的盛意。
    新华社27日讯　以我国农业部部长廖鲁言为首的中国农业代表团一行四人，应邀去蒙古人民共和国参加蒙古第二届农牧业合作社代表大会以后，在12月27日下午乘飞机回到北京。
    前往机场迎接的，有农业部副部长蔡子伟，外交部第二亚洲司副司长李强奋等人。
    蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办丹格苏伦，也前往机场迎接。
