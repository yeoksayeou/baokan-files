Title: Romanian Grand National Assembly Approves Next Year's Budget; Issues Statement Fully Supporting Soviet Disarmament Proposals
Author:
Date: 1959-12-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest -- The eighth session of the Romanian Grand National Assembly, which opened on the 25th, closed on the 29th.

　　After the meeting on the 25th approved the supplementary list of deputies to the Grand National Assembly, Finance Minister Viroel made a report on the implementation of the 1959 national budget and the 1960 national budget.

　　Viroel said that according to preliminary statistics, the budget revenue for 1959 was 50,675 million lei, and the expenditure was 47,996 million lei.

　　The draft national budget for 1960 stipulates that the budget revenue will be 56.8 billion lei, an increase of 12.1% compared with 1959, and the expenditure will be 55.93 billion lei. 93.2% of the budget revenue comes from the socialist economic sector.

　　When talking about the expenditure of the 1960 national budget, Viroel said that the total investment in 1960 will be 23.5 billion lei, of which industrial investment accounts for 59%; agricultural investment will be 4.4 billion lei, an increase of 25% compared with 1959. Among agricultural investments, the loans provided by the state to collective farms, co-farming cooperatives, and agricultural production cooperatives will increase by 40% compared with 1959. Appropriations for social and cultural facilities will be more than 13.4 billion lei, more than 1.3 billion lei more than in 1959. Defense expenditure accounts for 8.3% of the total budget expenditure.

　　After two days of discussion on the 28th and 29th, the meeting approved the 1960 national budget.

　　The meeting also passed a statement expressing full support for the appeal of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to the parliaments of all countries in the world on the issue of complete and thorough disarmament. The statement also stated that Romania will contribute to the struggle to achieve universal disarmament.

　　The meeting also approved the decrees formulated by the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly since the last meeting.

　　Romanian Foreign Minister Bunaqiu delivered a speech on the international situation at the meeting.

　　Bunaqiu said that at present, it is urgent to eliminate the "Cold War" from international life. Implementing the disarmament proposals of the Soviet government is a guarantee to achieve this goal. The upcoming summit meeting of the Soviet Union, Britain, the United States, and France is an event of great significance in strengthening cooperation and consolidating peace, and is warmly welcomed by the people of all countries in the world.

　　Bunaqiu said that the main obstacle to disarmament is that the cold warriors who start from a position of "strength" do not want to recognize the reality of the situation, and ignore the dangers of the "Cold War" and the arms race policy.

　　Bunaqiu said that Romania is closely united with other countries in the socialist camp and resolutely implements the policy of improving relations with all countries on the basis of peaceful coexistence.

　　The Romanian government is working to expand bilateral ties between countries in the Balkan region, develop multifaceted cooperation and collective security, which is beneficial to all Balkan countries.

### 罗大国民议会通过明年预算  发表声明完全支持苏联裁军建议

1959-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

　　罗大国民议会通过明年预算
    发表声明完全支持苏联裁军建议
    据新华社布加勒斯特电　从25日开幕的罗马尼亚大国民议会第八次会议于29日闭幕。
    25日的会议通过了大国民议会补选议员名单后，财政部长维若利作了关于1959年国家预算执行情况和1960年国家预算的报告。
    维若利说，据初步统计，1959年预算收入为五百零六亿七千五百万列伊，支出为四百七十九亿九千六百万列伊。
    1960年的国家预算草案规定，预算收入为五百六十八亿列伊，比1959年增加12.1％，支出为五百五十九亿三千万列伊。预算收入的93.2％来自社会主义经济部门。
    维若利在谈到1960年国家预算的支出时说，1960年总投资额为二百三十五亿列伊，其中工业投资占59％；农业投资为四十四亿列伊，比1959年增长25％。在农业投资中，国家给集体农庄、共耕社和农业生产合作社的贷款将比1959年增加40％。社会文化设施的拨款为一百三十四亿多列伊，比1959年多十三亿列伊以上。国防支出占整个预算支出的8.3％。
    会议经过28日和29日两天讨论后，通过了1960年国家预算。
    会议还通过了一项声明，表示完全支持苏联最高苏维埃就全面彻底裁军问题给世界各国议会的呼吁书。声明并且表示，罗马尼亚将在争取实现普遍裁军的斗争中作出贡献。
    会议还通过了大国民议会主席团自上次会议以来所制定的法令。
    罗马尼亚外交部长布纳丘在会上作了关于国际局势的发言。
    布纳丘说，目前，迫切需要从国际生活中消除“冷战”。实现苏联政府的裁军建议是达到这一目的的保证。即将举行的苏、英、美、法四国最高级会谈，是在加强合作与巩固和平方面具有重大意义的事件，受到世界各国人民的热烈欢迎。
    布纳丘说，裁军的主要障碍是，从“实力”地位出发的冷战分子不想承认现实局势，不顾“冷战”和军备竞赛政策的危险性。
    布纳丘说，罗马尼亚同社会主义阵营其他各国紧密团结在一起，坚决执行在和平共处基础上改善同各国关系的政策。
    罗马尼亚政府正在为在巴尔干地区国家间扩大双边联系、发展多方面合作和集体安全而努力，这对巴尔干各国都是有利的。
