Title: Title and Content Mismatch
Author: Yang Chengzhi
Date: 1959-12-13
Page: 8

    Title and Content Mismatch
    The experience of Qinhuangdao implementing a coordinated "one-dragon" transportation system, published on page nine of the People's Daily's November 9th "Heroes Gathering" page, discusses how railways and ports cooperate and collaborate with each other. However, the title is marked as "Railway-Mine Integration," which does not match the content.
              Hainan Island, Yang Chengzhi
