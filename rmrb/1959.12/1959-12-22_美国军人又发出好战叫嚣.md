Title: American Military Figures Again Issue War Cries
Author:
Date: 1959-12-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 21st: American high-ranking military officers are constantly issuing cries advocating a "position of strength" policy and preparing for war.

American Atlantic Submarine Commander Ward said in a speech in the United States on the 19th that the combined naval and air power of the United States "can support our country's policies in any possible conflict situation." He also said that this force is applicable to "cold war, limited war, or all-out war."

On the same day, a spokesman for the Commander-in-Chief of the U.S. Pacific Forces, Felt, also clamored that the U.S. troops stationed in the Far East are constantly "on alert" for possible so-called "aggression," regardless of where this "aggression" may occur in the Pacific region, including "Korea, the Taiwan Strait, and Laos."

### 美国军人又发出好战叫嚣

1959-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国军人又发出好战叫嚣
    新华社21日讯　华盛顿消息：美国高级军官不断发出鼓吹“实力地位”政策和进行备战的叫嚣。
    美国驻大西洋潜水艇司令华德19日在美国发表的一次讲话中说，美国的海军和空军力量加在一起“能够在任何可能发生冲突的情况下支持我们国家的政策”。他还说，这种力量适用于“冷战、有限战争或全面战争”。
    同一天，美国太平洋军队总司令费耳特的发言人也叫嚷说，美国驻远东的军队经常对可能发生的所谓“侵略”“保持警戒”，不管这种“侵略”可能发生在太平洋地区哪个地方，其中“包括朝鲜、台湾海峡和老挝”。
