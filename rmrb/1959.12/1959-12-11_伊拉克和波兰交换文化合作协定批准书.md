Title: Iraq and Poland Exchange Instruments of Ratification of Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-12-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, December 9th - Polish Ambassador to Iraq, Jaroszek, and Iraqi Foreign Minister Jawad, today exchanged here the instruments of ratification of the cultural cooperation agreement signed by the two countries in Warsaw in April of this year.

    In his speech at the exchange of instruments ceremony, Jaroszek pointed out the mutually beneficial nature of this agreement and noted that some of its provisions were already being implemented in practice.

    In his reply, Jawad expressed the hope that this agreement would be the beginning of better cooperation between the two countries.

### 伊拉克和波兰交换文化合作协定批准书

1959-12-11
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克和波兰交换文化合作协定批准书
    新华社巴格达9日电　波兰驻伊拉克大使雅罗谢克和伊拉克外交部长贾瓦德今天在这里交换了两国今年4月在华沙签订的文化合作协定的批准书。
    雅罗谢克在交换批准书仪式上致词时，指出了这项协定的互利性质，并且指出，其中一些条款实际上早就在实行了。
    贾瓦德在致答词时表示希望这项协定将是两国之间更好的合作的开始。
