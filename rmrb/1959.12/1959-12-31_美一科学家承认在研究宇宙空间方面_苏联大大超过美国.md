Title: U.S. Scientist Admits Soviet Union Far Surpasses United States in Space Exploration
Author:
Date: 1959-12-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th. According to TASS, New York, December 28th: United Press International reported that Dr. Neil, Assistant Director of the U.S. National Aeronautics and Space Administration, said, "Russia's obvious advantage in rocket technology will enable it to greatly surpass the United States in space exploration."

According to Neil, the progress of the two countries in exploring the lower layers of space is roughly the same. As for going deeper into space, the Soviet Union is undoubtedly ahead. This is directly related to its obvious advantage in rocket technology.

Neil believes that the Soviet Union's achievements in sending animals into space "greatly exceed" those of the United States. Its rockets have superior power, and the containers for scientific instruments are very excellent. Neil believes that Americans are "just beginning" to study the moon, while the Soviet Union has already taken a big step in this regard.

### 美一科学家承认在研究宇宙空间方面  苏联大大超过美国

1959-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

　　美一科学家承认在研究宇宙空间方面
    苏联大大超过美国
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社纽约28日讯：据合众国际社记者报道，美国全国星际和空间航行局局长助理尼耳博士说，“俄国在火箭技术方面的明显的优势，将使它在研究宇宙空间方面能够大大超过美国”。
    据尼耳说，两国在研究宇宙空间的低层方面的进展大致是一样的。至于谈到深入宇宙空间，那么苏联无疑走在前面。这与它在火箭技术方面的明显的优势直接有关。
    尼耳认为，苏联在投送动物到宇宙空间方面的成就“大大超过”美国。它的火箭的威力和装置科学仪器的容器非常优越。尼耳认为，美国人在研究月球方面“才刚刚开始”，而苏联在这方面却已迈进了很大一步。
