Title: North Atlantic Group Builds Missile Launch Pad in Norway
Author:
Date: 1959-12-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Oslo, December 28th: The Nordic Command of the North Atlantic Group announced on the 27th that the missile launch pads established by the group in Norway have been completed this year. According to reports, American-made "Nike" surface-to-air missiles will be installed on these launch pads next spring.

The Norwegian people have consistently opposed the establishment of North Atlantic Group missile bases on their soil.

### 北大西洋集团在挪威建成导弹发射台

1959-12-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　北大西洋集团在挪威建成导弹发射台
    新华社28日讯　奥斯陆消息：北大西洋集团北欧司令部27日宣布，这个集团在挪威设立的导弹发射台已经在今年建成。据报道，美国造的“奈克”式地对空导弹将在明年春天安装在这些发射台上。
    挪威人民一直反对在他们的国土上设置北大西洋集团的导弹基地。
