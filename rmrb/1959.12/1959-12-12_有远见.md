Foresight

    Various localities have achieved great success in using wild plants to manufacture indigenous pesticides. Due to extensive collection, the issues of resource protection and cultivation have arisen. Santai County, in the utilization of wild medicinal material resources, implements the principle of combining collection and protection, and combining exploitation and cultivation, to open up new sources of medicine in the future. This is a forward-looking approach and is worthy of learning by all localities.

### 有远见

1959-12-12
第3版()
专栏：编后

    有远见
    各地利用野生植物制造土农药已经取得很大成绩。由于大量的采集，就发生了资源的保护和培植的问题。三台县在野生药材的资源利用方面，贯彻执行采集和保护相结合，挖掘和培植相结合的原则，为今后开辟新的药源，这是一种有远见的作法，值得各地学习。
