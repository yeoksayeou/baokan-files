Title: Secretary-General of the British-China Friendship Association, Deri Berin, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-12-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 14th – At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Secretary-General of the British-China Friendship Association, Deri Berin, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country.

Zhang Zhixiang, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 英中友协秘书长德里本到京

1959-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    英中友协秘书长德里本到京
    新华社14日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的英中友好协会秘书长德里本和他的夫人今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长张致祥、副秘书长朱伯深等。
