Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Rodriguez
Author:
Date: 1959-12-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th - Premier Zhou Enlai received Rodriguez, a figure from the Uruguayan cultural circles who was invited to visit our country, this afternoon and had a friendly conversation. Xia Yan, Zhang Zhixiang, Yao Zhen, Zhou Erfu, and others were present at the reception.

### 周恩来总理接见罗德里格斯

1959-12-20
第3版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见罗德里格斯
    新华社19日讯　周恩来总理在今天下午接见了应邀来我国访问的乌拉圭文化界人士罗德里格斯，进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有夏衍、张致祥、姚溱、周而复等人。
