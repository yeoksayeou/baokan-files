Title: Panchen Erdeni and Others to Visit East China

Author: 
Date: 1959-12-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 14th: Panchen Erdeni Chökyi Gyaltsen, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Vice-Chairman Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, accompanied by Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, left Beijing by special train today to visit East China and other places.

Bao Erhan, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, Yang Jingren, Xie Hechou, and other Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, saw them off at the station.

### 班禅额尔德尼等去华东参观

1959-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼等去华东参观
    新华社14日讯　西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰，在中共中央统战部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋和中共西藏工委副书记周仁山陪同下，今天乘专车离北京到华东等地参观访问。
    政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，民族事务委员会副主任刘春、萨空了、杨静仁、谢鹤筹等，都到车站送行。
