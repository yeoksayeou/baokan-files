Title: Matsumura Reports to Kishi Nobusuke on China Visit Conclusions, Requests Efforts to Restore Japan-China Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1959-12-16
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 15th: Liberal Democratic Party advisor Kenzō Matsumura today reported his conclusions from his visit to China to Japanese Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi. According to Kyodo News, Matsumura told Kishi Nobusuke: "I deeply feel that we should not only restore trade (with China) but also strive to restore diplomatic relations from the standpoint of the overall situation."
    He also told Kishi Nobusuke that China is developing a modern industry, "therefore, we cannot ignore China in pursuing diplomacy."

### 松村向岸信介报告访华结论  要求设法恢复日中邦交

1959-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

    松村向岸信介报告访华结论
    要求设法恢复日中邦交
    新华社15日讯　东京消息：日本自由民主党顾问松村谦三今天向日本首相岸信介报告了他的访华结论。据共同社报道，松村谦三对岸信介说：“我痛切地感到，我们不仅要（同中国）恢复贸易，而且要从大局的立场出发设法恢复邦交”。
    他还告诉岸信介说，中国正在发展现代化工业，“因此不能无视中国来推行外交”。
