　　Guangxi Folk Songs
    Harvest Ballad

　　The General Line is like a red sun,
　　Shining on the mountains and changing their appearance;
　　Grain-deficient counties become grain-surplus counties,
　　Good news reported to Huairentang.
            (Dong ethnic group, Sanjiang)

    The July 1st Commune is One Year Old

　　After the birth of the July 1st Commune,
　　It's like a hillside of lingzhi grass,
　　The Party's policies are like spring rain,
　　The lingzhi grows five feet tall every day.

　　The July 1st Commune is one year old,
　　It's like Mount Tai standing in the suburbs,
　　Lingzhi covers the mountains, and fruit fills the trees,
　　A hundred birds sing harvest ballads joyfully.
              (Huang Dairong)
    　　Note: The July 1st Commune is in the suburbs of Quanzhou

　　White Smoke Rises from the Waist of the Green Mountain

　　White smoke rises from the waist of the green mountain,
　　The white smoke rushes over the other side of the mountain,
　　The shepherd boy sees it and laughs heartily,
　　He says it's a cloud in the sky.

　　Little brother, little brother, don't talk nonsense,
　　This is smoke from the factory burning ore.
　　        (Li Youxiang, Nandan)

### 广西民歌

1959-12-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　广西民歌
    丰收谣
　　总路线好像红太阳，
　　照得山区变了样；
　　缺粮县变成余粮县，
　　喜讯报到怀仁堂。
            （三江侗族）
    七一公社一岁了
　　七一公社诞生后，
　　好比一坡灵芝草，
　　党的政策如春雨，
　　灵芝日长五尺高。
　　七一公社一岁了，
　　好比泰山立城郊，
　　满山灵芝满山果，
　　百鸟欢唱丰收谣。
              （黄代荣）
    　　注：七一公社在全州城郊
　　青山腰间冒白烟
　　青山腰间冒白烟，
　　白烟冲过山那边，
　　牧童见了哈哈笑，
　　他说这是云上天。
　　弟弟弟弟别乱说，
　　这是工厂烧矿烟。
　　        （南丹李友湘）
