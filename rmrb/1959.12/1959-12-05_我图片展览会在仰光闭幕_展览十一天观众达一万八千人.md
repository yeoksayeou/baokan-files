Title: My Photo Exhibition Closes in Rangoon; 18,000 Visitors in Eleven Days
Author:
Date: 1959-12-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 3rd. The Chinese Photo Exhibition, which opened here on November 23, closed here tonight.

The Chinese Photo Exhibition was originally scheduled to end on November 30, but was later extended by three days at the request of the public. In the past eleven days, 18,000 Rangoon citizens visited the exhibition.

### 我图片展览会在仰光闭幕  展览十一天观众达一万八千人

1959-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我图片展览会在仰光闭幕
    展览十一天观众达一万八千人
    新华社仰光3日电　11月23日在这里开幕的中国图片展览会今晚在这里闭幕。
    中国图片展览会原定在11月30日结束，后来应公众的要求，延长了三天。在过去十一天里，有一万八千名仰光市民参观了这个展览会。
