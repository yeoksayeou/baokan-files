Title: Soviet Komsomol Delegation Ends Visit to China and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-12-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, December 11th: The Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League delegation left Beijing by plane today to return home after concluding its visit to China. The delegation arrived in China on November 19th at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League.

Luo Yi and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and others went to the airport to see the guests off.

### 结束在我国访问  苏联共青团代表团回国

1959-12-13
第3版()
专栏：

    结束在我国访问
    苏联共青团代表团回国
    新华社11日讯　苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团今天乘飞机离开北京回国。代表团是应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会的邀请，在11月19日来到我国访问的。
    中国共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅、胡克实等到机场欢送客人。
