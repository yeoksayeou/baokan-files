Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Fujian Waters for Provocation; I Issue the 76th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-12-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: On December 3rd, from 9:57 to 17:49, a U.S. warship intruded into the waters of the Baiquan and Haitan areas of Fujian Province, China. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 76th serious warning.

### 美舰侵入我福建海域挑衅  我提出第76次严重警告

1959-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    美舰侵入我福建海域挑衅
    我提出第76次严重警告
    新华社3日讯　12月3日九时五十七分至十七时四十九分，美国军舰一艘侵入我国福建省白犬、海坛地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第七十六次严重警告。
