Title: Ten Soviet Republics Eradicated Malaria
Author:
Date: 1959-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th - TASS reports: Ten of the fifteen Soviet republics have completely eradicated malaria, including Ukraine, Belarus, Lithuania, Armenia, Estonia, Turkmenistan, etc. In the Russian Federation, forty-five provinces and territories have also eradicated this infectious disease. In the first half of this year, three million people were blood-tested, and only nineteen were found to have malaria parasites.

Professor Sergeyev wrote in "Medical Workers' Newspaper": The Soviet Union has all the conditions to completely eradicate malaria, and this task should be completed in 1960.

### 苏联十个加盟共和国消灭了疟疾

1959-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联十个加盟共和国消灭了疟疾
    新华社莫斯科26日电　据塔斯社报道；苏联的十五个加盟共和国中已经有十个共和国完全消灭了疟疾，其中包括乌克兰、白俄罗斯、立陶宛、亚美尼亚、爱沙尼亚、土库曼等。在俄罗斯联盟也已经有四十五个州和边疆区消灭了这一传染病。在今年的上半年，对三百万人进行了验血，只发现了十九个人染有疟疾菌。
    谢尔基叶夫教授在“医务工作者报”上撰文写道：在苏联已经具备了全部消灭疟疾的条件，这一任务在1960年应该完成。
