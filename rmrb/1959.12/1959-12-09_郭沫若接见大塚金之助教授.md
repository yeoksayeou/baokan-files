Title: Guo Moruo Receives Professor Otsuka Kinnosuke
Author: 
Date: 1959-12-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th December: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, today received the Japanese economist Professor Otsuka Kinnosuke and his wife.

### 郭沫若接见大塚金之助教授

1959-12-09

    郭沫若接见大塚金之助教授
