Title: "Socialist Victory" Art Photography Exhibition Opens in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-12-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 19th: A "Socialist Victory" art photography exhibition featuring works from twelve socialist countries officially opened today at the "Friendship House of the Peoples of All Countries" in Moscow.

　　The opening ceremony was presided over by Popova, Chairman of the Council of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. In her opening remarks, she said that the exhibition vividly demonstrates the achievements of the twelve socialist countries in building communist and socialist societies, as well as the solid unity between the socialist countries. The exhibition features more than four hundred works.

### “社会主义胜利”艺术摄影展览在莫斯科开幕

1959-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　“社会主义胜利”艺术摄影展览在莫斯科开幕
    新华社莫斯科19日电　十二个社会主义国家的“社会主义胜利”艺术摄影展览今天在莫斯科“各国人民友谊之家”正式开幕。
    苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会理事会主席波波娃主持开幕式。她在开幕词中说，这个展览显明地表现了社会主义阵营十二个国家在建设共产主义社会和社会主义社会中的成就，以及社会主义阵营各国间的巩固的团结。这次展览共展出四百多件作品。
