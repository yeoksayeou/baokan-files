Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Holds Central Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1959-12-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, December 18th: It was announced here today that the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee held its Fourth Plenary Session. The plenary session was held on the 16th and 17th.

The communique said that the plenary session listened to and discussed the report made by the First Secretary of the People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee, Tsedenbal, on the summary of the collectivization of individual herding households and some important measures to consolidate agricultural and pastoral cooperatives. The plenary session made detailed resolutions on the issues discussed.

The communique said that some agricultural and pastoral cooperative chairmen, heads of county and commune party organizations, and stationmasters of animal husbandry machine stations participated in the meeting.

The communique said that the plenary session also elected Molomjamts, alternate member of the Central Political Bureau and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, as a member of the Party Central Political Bureau.

### 蒙古人民革命党举行中央全会

1959-12-19
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党举行中央全会
    新华社乌兰巴托18日电　此间今天公布了蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第四次全体会议的公报。这次全会是在16、17日举行的。
    公报说，全会听取并讨论了人民革命党中央第一书记泽登巴尔所作的关于个体牧户合作化的总结和巩固农牧业合作社的若干重要措施的报告。全会就所讨论的问题作出了详细的决议。
    公报说，某些农牧业合作社的社长，县、社党组织的负责人，畜牧业机器站的站长参加了这次会议。
    公报说，全会还选举了中央政治局候补委员、部长会议副主席莫洛姆扎木茨为党中央政治局委员。
