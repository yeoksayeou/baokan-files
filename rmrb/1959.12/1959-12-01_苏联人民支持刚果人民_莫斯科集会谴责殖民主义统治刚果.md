Title: Soviet People Support the Congolese People: Moscow Rally Condemns Colonialist Rule in the Congo
Author:
Date: 1959-12-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 27th: The people of the Soviet capital held a rally on the 26th to support the just struggle of the Congolese people. The resolution passed at the meeting stated that the Soviet people wholeheartedly support the Congolese people's struggle to abolish colonial rule, and firmly protest the brutal persecution of the Congolese people by the Belgian colonialists.
	This rally was organized in response to the call by the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council to hold a "Congo Day." Participants included figures from the cultural and scientific circles, workers, and African students studying in the Soviet Union.
	Professor Oshanin, a member of the Presidium of the Soviet Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, and textile worker Borisov, among others, spoke at the meeting, expressing the Soviet people's fraternal solidarity with the Congolese people who are fighting for freedom and independence. They demanded that the Congolese people's right to self-determination be recognized in accordance with the principles of the United Nations Charter.
	Elyanges, a Congolese student at Moscow University, said in his speech: We have suffered enough from the colonial system. We can no longer allow Belgian capitalists to use our people as victims of their insatiable greed. No matter what, we will fight for our independence.

### 苏联人民支持刚果人民  莫斯科集会谴责殖民主义统治刚果

1959-12-01
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联人民支持刚果人民
    莫斯科集会谴责殖民主义统治刚果
    新华社莫斯科27日电　苏联首都公众在26日举行了声援刚果人民的正义斗争的集会。会议在决议中说，苏联人民衷心地支持刚果人民为废除殖民主义统治而进行的斗争，并坚决抗议比利时殖民主义者对刚果人民的残酷迫害行为。
    这个集会是为了响应亚非人民团结理事会关于举行“刚果日”的号召而组织的。到会的有文化和科学界人士、工人和在苏联学习的非洲学生。
    苏联亚非团结委员会主席团委员鄂山荫教授和纺织工人波里索夫等在会上讲话，表达了苏联人民声援为自由和独立而斗争的刚果人民的兄弟情谊，他们要求根据联合国宪章原则承认刚果人民的自决权。
    莫斯科大学的刚果留学生埃连格斯在大会上发言说：殖民主义制度的苦头，我们是受够了。我们再不能允许比利时资本家把我们的人民作为他们的贪得无厌的牺牲品。不管怎样，我们要为我们的独立而斗争。
