Title: Bulgarian National Assembly Approves National Economic Plan and Budget for Next Year: Bulgaria's Socialist Construction Continues to Accelerate; Total Industrial Output Value Will Increase by 15.2 Percent Compared to This Year, and Total Agricultural Output Value Will Increase by 32 Percent
Author:
Date: 1959-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, December 26th: Bulgarian newspapers today published the Law on the National Economic Development Plan for 1960 and the State Budget Law, which were approved by the Fourth Special Session of the Bulgarian National Assembly. The Plan Law stipulates that Bulgaria's total industrial output value next year (calculated at factory prices as of April 1, 1956) will increase by 15.2% compared to this year.

Next year, Bulgaria will produce 4.532 billion kilowatt-hours of electricity; 17.39 million tons of coal; 146,400 tons of iron for steelmaking; 305,000 tons of steel; 242,000 tons of non-ferrous rolled metal; 44,000 tons of lead; and 18,000 tons of zinc. Next year, meat production will reach 166,200 tons; canned vegetables 120,100 tons; and sugar 267,600 tons.

According to the Plan Law, the total value of Bulgaria's agricultural products in 1960 (calculated at comparable prices from 1955) will increase by 32% compared to the estimated figures for this year.

Next year, the volume of transportation will increase by 9.9% compared to this year. The total retail trade volume (calculated at comparable prices) will increase by 14.2% compared to this year. The total amount of capital construction investment within the limits for next year will be 7.6505 billion levs.

In terms of culture, education, and health, next year the country will enroll 18,090 full-time and correspondence students in institutions of higher education; and 34,400 full-time and correspondence students in secondary technical schools. The number of hospital beds nationwide will increase by 2,500 compared to this year.

Next year, industrial labor productivity will increase by 5.7%.

The State Budget Law for 1960, approved by the Assembly, stipulates that total budget revenue will be 31.247278 billion levs, and total expenditure will be 30.926913 billion levs.

The total budget expenditure is allocated as follows: expenditure for further accelerating the development of industry, agriculture, transportation, housing, and municipal construction, etc., is 20.221695 billion levs; expenditure for social welfare and cultural undertakings is 6.60624 billion levs. (Photo Attached)

### 保国民议会通过明年国民经济计划和预算  保加利亚社会主义建设继续加速发展  工业总产值将比今年增长百分之十五点二农业总产值增长百分之三十二

1959-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　保国民议会通过明年国民经济计划和预算
    保加利亚社会主义建设继续加速发展
    工业总产值将比今年增长百分之十五点二农业总产值增长百分之三十二
    新华社索非亚26日电　保加利亚报纸今天公布了保加利亚国民议会第四次特别会议批准的1960年国民经济发展计划法和国家预算法。计划法规定，明年保加利亚的工业总产值（按1956年4月1日工厂价格计算）将比今年增长15.2％。
    明年保加利亚将生产电力四十五亿三千二百万度；煤一千七百三十九万吨；炼钢用铁十四万六千四百吨；钢三十万五千吨；有色压延金属二十四万二千吨；铅四万四千吨；锌一万八千吨。明年肉类生产将达十六万六千二百吨；蔬菜罐头十二万零一百吨；砂糖二十六万七千六百吨。
    按照计划法，1960年保加利亚农产品总值（按1955年比较价格计算）将比今年预计完成的数字增长32％。
    明年运输量将比今年增加9.9％。零售贸易总额（按比较价格计算）将比今年增长14.2％。明年的限额基本建设投资总额为七十六亿五千零五十万列弗。
    在文教卫生方面，明年全国将招收一万八千零九十名高等学校的正式生和函授生；三万四千四百名中等技术学校的正式生和函授生。全国医院的病床数目将比今年增加二千五百张。
    明年工业劳动生产率将提高5.7％。
    议会批准的1960年国家预算法规定，预算总收入为三百一十二亿四千七百二十七万八千列弗，总支出为三百零九亿二千六百九十一万三千列弗。
    预算总支出是这样分配的：用于进一步加速发展工业、农业、交通运输业、住宅和市政建设等方面的支出为二百零二亿二千一百六十九万五千列弗；用于社会福利和文化事业方面的为六十六亿零六百二十四万列弗。（附图片）
