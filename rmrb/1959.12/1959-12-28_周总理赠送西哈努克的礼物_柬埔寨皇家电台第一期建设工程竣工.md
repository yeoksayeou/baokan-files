Title: Premier Zhou's Gift to Sihanouk: Completion of the First Phase of Construction of the Cambodian Royal Radio Station
Author:
Date: 1959-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, December 25th: The first phase of construction of the Cambodian Royal Radio Station has recently been completed.

　　This radio station is a gift from Premier Zhou Enlai to Prince Sihanouk. The first phase of the radio station's construction mainly included the installation of two transmitters and one generator, and the construction of antenna towers. The first 20-kilowatt medium-wave transmitter was installed in August this year, and the second transmitter was installed at the end of last month. Test broadcasts of the second 15-kilowatt short-wave transmitter show that the Chinese-made transmitter functions very well. Radio broadcasts from this station can be heard clearly in the border areas of Prey Veng Province and Stung Treng Province, where residents are hearing their own country's radio programs for the first time.

　　With the help of Chinese experts, Cambodian technicians are currently learning to operate the various machines of the radio station.

### 周总理赠送西哈努克的礼物  柬埔寨皇家电台第一期建设工程竣工

1959-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　周总理赠送西哈努克的礼物
    柬埔寨皇家电台第一期建设工程竣工
    新华社金边25日电　柬埔寨皇家电台的第一期建设工程最近已经完成。
    这个电台是周恩来总理送给西哈努克亲王的礼物。电台的第一期工程主要包括安装两部发射机和一部发电机，建筑天线塔。第一部二十瓩的中波发射机已经在今年8月安装好，第二部发射机在上月底安装好。第二部功率为十五瓩的短波发射机试播情况表明，中国制造的发射机的功能很好，在柴桢省和桔井省的边界地区收听这个电台的广播，声音都很清晰，那里的居民第一次听到自己国家的广播节目。
    在中国专家的帮助下，柬埔寨技术人员目前正在学习操作这个电台的各种机器。
