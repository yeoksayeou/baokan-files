Title: Gale Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-12-04
Page: 4

    Gale Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a gale warning at 7:00 PM today: The cold air mass that has invaded our country has weakened. It is expected that in the next 24 hours, there will still be northerly winds of force 5 to 6 in the central and eastern parts of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Shandong Peninsula, and the Liaodong Peninsula; the Bohai Sea and the Yellow Sea will still have northwesterly winds of force 6 to 7. Please pay attention to listening to the forecast broadcasts of the local meteorological stations.

### 大风警报

1959-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    大风警报
    新华社3日讯　中央气象科学研究所今日下午七点发布大风警报：侵袭我国的冷空气已经减弱，预计未来二十四小时内，内蒙古自治区中部和东部，山东半岛和辽东半岛，仍有五到六级的偏北风；渤海和黄海仍有六、七级的偏西北风。请注意收听当地气象台的预报广播。
