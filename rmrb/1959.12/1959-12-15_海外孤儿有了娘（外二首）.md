Overseas Orphans Have a Mother (and two other poems)
　　Li Qing, Indonesian Overseas Chinese
　　China's liberation, the East is bright,
　　Overseas orphans have a mother;
　　Gloom and bad luck swept away,
　　Faces beaming with joy.

　Old Man Returns Home for Sightseeing
　　Old man returns home for sightseeing,
　　The way he talks has changed;
　　Before, no one listened to him,
　　Now his words are like honey.

　Department Store Window
　　The overseas department store window,
　　Is a mirror to observe the motherland.
　　Yesterday it was -
　　Suzhou and Hangzhou silk rivaling colorful clouds,
　　Exquisite painted fans, beautiful and refined,
　　Ivory carvings are the best in the world,
　　Moutai Guanyin is famous far and wide.
　　Today it is -
　　"Love" brand ink rivals Parker,
　　Fragrant printed cloth fascinates people,
　　Automobile charts are posted in the window,
　　Mechanical samples are placed at the heart of the window.
　　The overseas department store window,
　　Is a mirror of the motherland's leap forward.
