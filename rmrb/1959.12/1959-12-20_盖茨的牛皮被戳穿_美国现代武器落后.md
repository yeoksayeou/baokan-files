Title: Gates' Bluff Exposed: American Modern Weapons Lag Behind
Author:
Date: 1959-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 19th:  Senate Democratic Deputy Leader Mansfield punctured the boast of the new American Defense Secretary Gates at the North Atlantic Treaty Organization Council meeting on the 16th. Gates had comforted America's allies at the meeting by saying that the United States "still leads the Soviet Union" in nuclear weapons and nuclear weapon delivery vehicles.

Mansfield expressed disbelief in Gates' statement in a radio talk on the 16th. He said that the Soviet Union is "ahead of us (the United States)" not only in the production of intercontinental ballistic missiles but also in artificial satellites and lunar rockets.

Mansfield also said that the United States is "not as modern as the Russians" in submarines. He advocated that the United States accelerate its arms race.

### 盖茨的牛皮被戳穿  美国现代武器落后

1959-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    盖茨的牛皮被戳穿  美国现代武器落后
    新华社19日讯　华盛顿消息：美国参议院民主党副领袖曼斯菲尔德16日戳穿了美国新任国防部长盖茨在北大西洋集团理事会会议上的吹牛。盖茨在会上安慰美国的盟国说，美国在核武器和核武器载运工具方面“仍然走在苏联前面”。
    曼斯菲尔德16日在一次广播谈话中，对盖茨的说法表示不相信。他说，苏联不仅在洲际弹道导弹生产方面而且在人造卫星和月球火箭方面，“都走在我们（美国）前面”。
    曼斯菲尔德还说，美国在潜水艇方面“不像俄国人那样现代化”。他鼓吹美国加紧扩军备战。
