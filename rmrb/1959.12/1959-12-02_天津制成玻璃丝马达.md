Title: Tianjin Makes Glass Filament Motor
Author:
Date: 1959-12-02
Page: 2

The youth workers of the Electrical Maintenance Team of Tianjin State No. 3 Cotton Mill recently manufactured a kind of glass filament motor which is heat-resistant, highly efficient and has high insulation performance. Fourteen units have already been made.

　　The glass filament motor is converted from an ordinary ten-horsepower motor. The insulator of an ordinary ten-horsepower motor is made of cotton rope, which has relatively low heat resistance; while the motor of the same horsepower made of glass filament as an insulator has greatly improved heat resistance and doubled the power, equivalent to the effect of twenty horsepower.
                                (Xinhua News Agency)

### 天津制成玻璃丝马达

1959-12-02
第2版()
专栏：

    天津制成玻璃丝马达
    天津国棉三厂电气保全小组的青年工人，最近制成一种耐高温、效率大、绝缘性能高的玻璃丝马达。现在已经做出十四台。
    玻璃丝马达是用普通的十马力的电动机改制成的。普通的十马力电动机的绝缘体，是用棉绳做的，耐温能力比较低；而采用玻璃丝做绝缘体制成的同等马力的电动机，耐温能力大大提高，功率增加一倍，相当于二十马力的作用。
                                （新华社讯）
