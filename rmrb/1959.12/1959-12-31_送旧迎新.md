Title: Seeing off the Old Year and Ushering in the New
Author: Fang Zixing
Date: 1959-12-31
Page: 1

　　Seeing off the Old Year and Ushering in the New
　　Worker of the Golden Star Golden Pen Factory Fang Zixing
　　The whole world celebrates with golden gongs ringing,
　　Everywhere together enjoys bronze chimes sounding.
　　Medals for overtaking Britain hang full on the chest,
　　The old man of '59 bids farewell!
　　Miracles in a series release extraordinary splendor,
　　Epic poems in volumes flash with glory.
　　Heroic sons and daughters display their supernatural powers,
　　Riding the rocket, soaring forever!
　　Battle drums rumble across mountains and plains,
　　Firecrackers explode in thousands of households.
　　Holding countless bouquets of fresh flowers,
　　The '60 child's face contains a smile!
　　After the year, opening the door brings a hall full of red,
　　New tides surge from all corners of the land.
　　Flowers of innovation bloom in vibrant splendor,
　　And see, the new year's spring is even more charming!

### 送旧迎新
方孜行
1959-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　送旧迎新
    金星金笔厂工人  方孜行
    普天同庆金锣鸣，
    遍地共乐铜磬敲。
    赶英勋章满胸挂，
    五九老人辞别了！
    奇迹连串放异彩，
    史诗成卷闪光耀。
    英雄儿女显神通，
    驾着火箭永飞跃！
    漫山遍野擂战鼓，
    千家万户燃鞭炮。
    手捧鲜花无数丛，
    六○童孩脸含笑！
    年后开门满堂红，
    五湖四海涌新潮。
    革新花儿朵朵艳，
    且看新岁春更娇！
