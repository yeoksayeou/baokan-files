Title: Nyasaland People Continue to Struggle for Independence Outside the "Central African Federation"
Author:
Date: 1959-12-22
Page: 6

　　Striving for Independence Outside the "Central African Federation"
    Nyasaland People Continue to Struggle
    Xinhua News Agency, Johannesburg, December 20th: Chiume, the publicity secretary of the Nyasaland African National Congress, stated that Nyasaland's struggle to break away from the British-created "Central African Federation" and achieve independence will continue, no matter how long the struggle lasts and what difficulties are faced.
    Chiume demanded that the British rulers release Banda, the chairman of the Nyasaland African National Congress, and other detained individuals, and recognize Nyasaland's right to independence outside the "Central African Federation." He pointed out that the entire foundation of the "Central African Federation" is the rule of Europeans over Africans.
    He emphasized that there is no room for compromise on the issue of seceding from the "Federation" and achieving independence.
    The British government can only postpone, but can never prevent, the realization of freedom. Chiume also stated that when Nyasaland is no longer a police state, the Nyasaland African National Congress is determined to continue the struggle, according to non-violent principles, for Nyasaland's independence from the "Central African Federation."
    Chiume refuted the fallacy that Nyasaland is too poor to be independent outside the "Central African Federation." He said that independence can inspire tremendous capacity and enthusiasm for national development. He said that Nyasaland's economic potential has not yet been fully realized.

### 争取在“中非联邦”外独立  尼亚萨兰人民继续斗争

1959-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　争取在“中非联邦”外独立
    尼亚萨兰人民继续斗争
    据新华社20日讯　约翰内斯堡消息：尼亚萨兰非洲人国民大会宣传秘书丘梅发表谈话指出，尼亚萨兰要求脱离英国人制造的“中非联邦”而实现独立的斗争将继续下去，不论斗争要持续多久和面临什么样的困难。
    丘梅要求英国统治者释放尼亚萨兰非洲人国民大会主席班达及其他被拘捕的人，并且承认尼亚萨兰有权在“中非联邦”之外独立。他指出，“中非联邦”的整个基础就是欧洲人统治非洲人。
    他强调说，就脱离“联邦”和独立的问题来说，没有什么妥协办法。
    英国政府所能做的只不过是推迟而决不能阻挡自由的实现。丘梅还表示，在尼亚萨兰不再是一个警察国家的时候，尼亚萨兰非洲人国民大会决心根据非暴力的方针，为尼亚萨兰脱离“中非联邦”获得独立而继续斗争。
    丘梅驳斥了所谓尼亚萨兰太穷不能在“中非联邦”之外独立的谬论。他说，独立可以鼓舞巨大的能力和热情用于国家的发展。他说，尼亚萨兰的经济潜力还没有充分发挥。
