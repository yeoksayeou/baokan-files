Title: Japanese Foreign Minister, Ignoring Domestic Opposition, Shouts About Signing New Japan-U.S. Military Treaty in January Next Year
Author:
Date: 1959-12-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 4th: Following U.S. President Eisenhower's statement on December 2nd urging the Kishi Nobusuke government to quickly sign a new Japan-U.S. "security treaty," Japanese Foreign Minister Aiichiro Fujiyama declared to reporters today that January 19th next year is an "appropriate day" to sign this new treaty.

　　Fujiyama also said at a press conference after the cabinet meeting that this day is also an "appropriate day" for Japanese Prime Minister Kishi Nobusuke to hold talks with Eisenhower. He said that he and Japanese Ambassador to the United States Koichiro Asakai will represent the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to go to Washington to attend the signing ceremony of this treaty.

　　According to today's "Mainichi Shimbun" report, Kishi Nobusuke will represent the Japanese government in Washington to sign this military alliance treaty that is hostile to China, the Soviet Union, and the people of Asia. Those attending the signing ceremony will also include Vice President of the Liberal Democratic Party Banboku Ohno and Chairman of the Policy Research Council, Tanaka Funada.

### 日本外相不顾国内人民反对  叫嚷明年一月签订新日美军事条约

1959-12-05
第6版()
专栏：

    日本外相不顾国内人民反对
    叫嚷明年一月签订新日美军事条约
    新华社4日讯　东京消息：紧接着美国总统艾森豪威尔在2日发表声明催促岸信介政府迅速签订新的日美“安全条约”以后，日本外务相藤山爱一郎今天向新闻记者宣称，明年1月19日是签订这个新条约的“适当日子”。
    藤山在内阁会议后的记者招待会上还说，这一天也是日本首相岸信介同艾森豪威尔举行会谈的“适当日子”。他说，他和日本驻美国大使朝海浩一郎将代表外务省去华盛顿参加签订这个条约的仪式。
    据“每日新闻”今天报道，岸信介将代表日本政府去华盛顿签订这个同中国、苏联和亚洲人民为敌的军事同盟条约。参加签字仪式的还将包括自由民主党副总裁大野伴睦和政策审议会会长船田中。
