Title: Panchen and Others Visit Nanjing, Then Go to Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-12-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 18th: Panchen Erdeni Choskyi Gyaltsen, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Vice Chairman Phagpalha Geleg Namgyal, accompanied by Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Work Committee, arrived in Nanjing by special train from Beijing on the morning of the 15th for a visit. They left Nanjing at noon today for Shanghai.

During their stay in Nanjing, Panchen Erdeni Choskyi Gyaltsen and Phagpalha Geleg Namgyal, accompanied by Zhou Yifeng, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, Chen Yang, Mayor of Nanjing, and others, visited the Nanjing Radio Factory, the Purple Mountain Observatory, and a military school. They also laid wreaths at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery. They visited the martyrs' historical materials exhibition room in Yuhuatai. In addition, they toured scenic spots such as Xuanwu Lake.

The Jiangsu Provincial People's Committee and the Nanjing Municipal People's Committee held banquets to welcome Acting Chairman Panchen Erdeni and Vice Chairman Phagpalha Geleg Namgyal to Nanjing for a visit.

When Panchen Erdeni and his party arrived and left Nanjing, Hui Yuyu, Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPC and Governor of Jiangsu Province, Xu Jiatun, Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPC, Peng Chong, First Secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the CPC, Chen Yang, Mayor of Nanjing, and Xu Shiyou, General of the Nanjing Garrison, and other responsible persons went to the station to welcome and see them off.

### 班禅等在南京访问后去上海

1959-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

    班禅等在南京访问后去上海
    新华社南京18日电　西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰，在中共中央统战部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋和中共西藏工委副书记周仁山陪同下，于15日上午乘专车从北京到达南京参观，今天中午离开南京去上海。
    班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和帕巴拉·卓列朗杰等在南京期间，由江苏省副省长周一峰、南京市市长陈扬等陪同，参观了南京无线电厂、紫金山天文台和一所军事学校，并到中山陵和雨花台人民革命烈士墓献了花圈。他们在雨花台参观了烈士史料陈列室。此外，他们还游览了玄武湖等名胜。
    江苏省人民委员会和南京市人民委员会曾举行宴会，欢迎班禅额尔德尼代理主任委员和帕巴拉·卓列朗杰副主任委员来南京访问。
    在班禅额尔德尼等一行到达和离开南京的时候，中共江苏省委书记、江苏省省长惠浴宇，中共江苏省委书记许家屯，中共南京市委第一书记彭冲，南京市市长陈扬，以及南京驻军负责人许世友上将等，都到车站欢迎和欢送。
