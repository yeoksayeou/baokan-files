Title: Schematic Diagram of Indigenous Mechanization of Pig Raising Process in Wujiapo
Author:
Date: 1959-12-29
Page: 4

To the Editor:

　　Since the People's Daily published the news on December 1st that Wujiapo Management Area in our county reformed its breeding tools, with only sixty-seven people raising more than four thousand six hundred pigs, a reduction of one hundred and ninety-three people compared to the past, people from all over the country have come to visit. Currently, there are a total of five thousand seven hundred and ninety people from sixty-seven counties in seven provinces including Henan, Hebei, and Jilin, most of whom are county party secretaries and commune party secretaries. Recently, many special districts, counties, and cities from provinces and regions such as Guangxi, Fujian, and Jiangsu have sent telegrams requesting materials from us. We believe this is a very good phenomenon and it also has a great promoting and supervisory effect on our work. In order to meet the needs of various places as much as possible, and also to reduce the time and expenses of comrades traveling back and forth, we are now sending a batch of "Schematic Diagrams of Simplified Mechanization of Pig Raising Process" for publication, for reference by various places.

　　Greetings!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Communist Party Committee of Fushan County, Shanxi Province (Attached image)

### 吴家坡养猪过程土机械化示意图

1959-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　吴家坡养猪过程土机械化示意图编辑同志：
    自12月1日人民日报发表我县吴家坡管理区改革饲养工具，四千六百多头猪只有六十七人饲养，比过去减少了一百九十三人的消息以后，全国各地纷纷派人前来参观。目前来自河南、河北、吉林等七省六十七个县的共计五千七百九十多人，大部分都是县委书记、公社党委书记。最近又有广西、福建、江苏等省、区很多专、县、市打来电报，向我们要资料。我们认为这是十分好的现象，对我们的工作也有很大的推动和督促作用。为了尽量满足各地的要求，同时也减少各地同志往返的时间和费用，现在我们送去一批“养猪过程简易机械化示意图”请发表，供各地参考。
    敬礼！
