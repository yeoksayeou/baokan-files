Title: The Ministry of Foreign Trade Holds New Year's Party to Entertain Foreign Friends in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-12-31
Page: 5
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: The Ministry of Foreign Trade held a New Year's party this evening. Invited to attend the party were commercial representatives, commercial counselors, commercial staff and their wives from various countries stationed in China, as well as trade delegations from various countries' governments currently in Beijing, and science and technology representatives from socialist countries stationed in China.
    Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin delivered a speech at the party, wishing everyone a Happy New Year.
    Those attending the party included Vice Ministers of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, Lu Xuzhang, Jiang Ming, and heads of relevant departments of the Central Committee.

### 我对外贸易部举行新年联欢晚会  招待在北京的外国朋友

1959-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

　　我对外贸易部举行新年联欢晚会
    招待在北京的外国朋友
    新华社30日讯　对外贸易部今天晚间举行新年联欢晚会。被邀请一起参加晚会的有各国驻华商务代表、商务参赞、商务工作人员和他们的夫人，以及正在北京的各国政府贸易代表团，各社会主义国家驻华科技代表。
    对外贸易部副部长雷任民在晚会上致词祝贺新年快乐。
    参加晚会的有对外贸易部副部长林海云、卢绪章、江明以及中央各有关部门的负责人等。
