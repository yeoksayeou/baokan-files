Title: Czechoslovak Academy of Agricultural Sciences Awards Scientist Ding Ying Certificate and Medal
Author:
Date: 1959-12-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 4th: The Czechoslovak Academy of Agricultural Sciences, at its fifteenth plenary meeting held on the 2nd and 3rd of this month, awarded certificates and medals to a group of foreign scientists who were elected honorary academicians of the Czechoslovak Academy of Agricultural Sciences at the previous meeting. Among them was Ding Ying, President of the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences, who arrived here a few days ago to attend this meeting, as well as scientists from the Soviet Union, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Bulgaria, and Romania.

### 捷克斯洛伐克农业科学院  授予我科学家丁颖奖状奖章

1959-12-06
第3版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克农业科学院
    授予我科学家丁颖奖状奖章
    据新华社布拉格4日电　捷克斯洛伐克农业科学院在2日和3日举行的第十五次全体会议上，授予一批在上次会议上被选为捷克斯洛伐克农业科学院荣誉院士的外国科学家以奖状和奖章。其中有数日前到达这里出席这次会议的中国农业科学院院长丁颖，以及苏联、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、波兰、保加利亚和罗马尼亚的科学家。
