Title: Finnish Ambassador Holds National Day Reception, Chairman Zhu De and Others Attend to Offer Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th — Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Varmaani, held a reception this evening to celebrate the 42nd anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Finland.

    Chairman Zhu De attended the reception and delivered a speech, wishing the Republic of Finland prosperity and its people happiness, wishing the President of the Republic of Finland, Mr. Kekkonen, good health, and wishing the further consolidation and development of the friendly relations between China and Finland.

    In his speech, Ambassador Varmaani wished Chairman Liu Shaoqi good health and the Chinese people happiness.

    Among those present at this evening's reception were Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, President of the Supreme People's Court Xie Juezai, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Luo Guibo, and people from various circles, as well as diplomatic envoys of various countries to China.

### 芬兰大使举行国庆招待会  朱德委员长等到会祝贺

1959-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰大使举行国庆招待会
    朱德委员长等到会祝贺
    新华社6日讯　芬兰驻华大使瓦尔万尼今天晚间举行招待会，庆祝芬兰共和国成立四十二周年。
    朱德委员长出席了招待会并且在招待会上致词，祝芬兰共和国繁荣和人民幸福，祝芬兰共和国总统吉科宁身体健康，祝中芬两国间的友好关系进一步巩固和发展。
    瓦尔万尼大使在致词中祝刘少奇主席身体健康，祝中国人民幸福。
    出席今晚招待会的，有人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，国务院副总理习仲勋，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，外交部副部长章汉夫、罗贵波和各方面的人士，以及各国驻华使节。
