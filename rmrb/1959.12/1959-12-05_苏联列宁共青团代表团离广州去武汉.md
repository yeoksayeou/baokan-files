Title: Soviet Leninist Komsomol Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1959-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 4th. The Soviet Leninist Young Communist League delegation, led by Murtazayev, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Komsomol, left Guangzhou by plane today for a visit to Wuhan. Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Tian Xin, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Tan Zhuofen, Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League.

　　The delegation arrived in Guangzhou on the 1st and was warmly welcomed by the members of the Communist Youth League and young people in Guangzhou. More than 1,200 young people from all walks of life in Guangzhou held a soiree on the evening of the 3rd to celebrate with the delegation. The head of the delegation, Murtazayev, was invited to speak at the meeting. He praised the vigor of Chinese youth in socialist construction and introduced the selfless labor of Soviet youth in communist construction. Young actors from the South China Song and Dance Troupe and the Guangzhou Orchestra performed wonderful programs for the guests. Many young workers presented the products they produced to the guests, and young students also presented carefully selected gifts to the guests. Members of the delegation also had cordial conversations with advanced young producers in Guangzhou at the meeting.

　　During its stay in Guangzhou, the delegation also held discussions with the heads of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Guangzhou Municipal Committee, and the district committees of the Communist Youth League in Guangzhou on the work of the League, and visited and toured the suburban people's communes, the Guangzhou Institute of Physical Education, the former site of the Guangzhou Peasant Movement Training Institute, and the Guangzhou Commune Uprising Martyrs' Cemetery.

### 苏联列宁共青团代表团离广州去武汉

1959-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联列宁共青团代表团离广州去武汉
    新华社广州4日电　苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团，在团长、苏联共青团中央书记穆尔塔扎耶夫率领下，今天乘飞机离开广州去武汉访问。到机场欢送的有共青团广东省委书记田心、共青团广州市委书记谭卓芬等。
    代表团在一日来到广州后，受到了广州市的共青团员和青年们的热烈欢迎。广州市各界青年一千二百多人曾在3日晚举行晚会，同代表团联欢。代表团团长穆尔塔扎耶夫应邀在会上讲了话。他赞扬我国青年在社会主义建设中的干劲，介绍了苏联青年在共产主义建设中忘我劳动的情况。华南歌舞团、广州乐团的青年演员为贵宾们表演了精彩节目。许多青年工人把自己生产的产品赠给贵宾，青年学生们也把自己精心选购的礼物赠给贵宾。代表团成员在会上还同广州市的青年先进生产者进行了亲切的交谈。
    代表团在广州期间，还曾经同共青团广东省委和广州市委、广州市各区团委负责人座谈了团的工作，访问和参观了郊区人民公社、广州体育学院、广州农民运动讲习所旧址和广州公社起义烈士陵园等。
