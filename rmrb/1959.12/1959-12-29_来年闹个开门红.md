Title: Get a Flying Start Next Year
Author: Li Youqi
Date: 1959-12-29
Page: 1

    Get a Flying Start Next Year
    Ride the horse and whip the rocket,
    The indicators soar and break through the sky.
    Leap forward flowers bloom in the millions,
    Get a Flying Start Next Year.
　　　　　　Worker of Hudong Shipyard, Li Youqi
　　(Originally published in Shanghai News Daily) (With picture)
