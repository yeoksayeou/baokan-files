Title: Juvenile Delinquency Rampant
Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi—According to a report in the South Vietnamese "Liberty News" on November 2, juvenile crime in South Vietnam has been increasing since last year, and has become a "serious social illness."

    The newspaper said that the number of juvenile delinquents in 1958 increased by 40% compared to 1957, and by 123% compared to 1955.

    The newspaper pointed out that in Saigon, where American yellow films are rampant, the number of juvenile delinquents is six times higher than the average number of juvenile delinquents in the whole of South Vietnam.

### 少年犯罪成灾

1959-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

    少年犯罪成灾
    据新华社河内讯　南越“自由报”在11月2日登载的一篇文章中承认，去年以来，南越少年儿童犯罪事件日益增多，已经形成了“严重的社会病”。
    这家报纸说，1958年的犯罪少年比1957年增加了40％，比1955年增加了123％。
    这家报纸指出，在美国黄色电影泛滥成灾的西贡市，犯罪少年比全南越的犯罪少年的平均数字多六倍。
