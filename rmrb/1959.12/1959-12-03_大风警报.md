Title: Gale Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-12-03
Page: 3

    Gale Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd — The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a gale warning at 8 p.m. on the 2nd: Affected by cold air, it is expected that in the next 24 hours, the Bohai Sea, western and central Inner Mongolia will experience westerly winds turning to northerly winds of force 6-7; the northern Yellow Sea will experience northwesterly winds of force 6-7; the Taiwan Strait and the northeastern part of the South China Sea will experience northeasterly winds of force 6-7; the Shandong Peninsula, the Liaodong Peninsula, the Beibu Gulf and the northwestern part of the South China Sea will experience northerly winds of force 5-6. In the next 24 to 48 hours, western and central Inner Mongolia will experience northerly winds of force 6-7; the Northeast Plain and the southern Yellow Sea, and the East China Sea will experience northerly winds of force 5-6; northern Hebei and northern Shanxi will experience northwesterly winds of around force 6. Relevant units and coastal fishermen are requested to pay special attention.

### 大风警报

1959-12-03
第3版()
专栏：

    大风警报
    新华社2日讯　中央气象科学研究所2日下午八时发布大风警报；受冷空气的影响，预计未来二十四小时内渤海、内蒙古西部和中部有六、七级的偏西风转偏北风；黄海北部有六、七级的偏西北风；台湾海峡、南海东北部有六到七级的东北风；山东半岛、辽东半岛、北部湾和南海西北部有五、六级的偏北风。未来二十四小时到四十八小时内，内蒙古西部和中部有六到七级的偏北风；东北平原和黄海南部、东海有五、六级的偏北风；河北北部、山西北部有六级左右的西北风。请有关单位和沿海渔民特别注意。
