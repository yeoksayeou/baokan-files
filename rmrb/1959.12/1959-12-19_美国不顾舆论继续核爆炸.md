Title: US Ignores Public Opinion and Continues Nuclear Explosions
Author:
Date: 1959-12-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 17th (Tass): The United States Atomic Energy Commission announced that the United States has conducted two small nuclear explosion tests in salt wells in Louisiana. The commission described the two explosions as being for the purpose of studying whether underground nuclear weapons tests can be detected.

The commission plans to conduct a total of ten similar explosions.

### 美国不顾舆论继续核爆炸

1959-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    美国不顾舆论继续核爆炸
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社华盛顿17日讯：美国政府原子能委员会宣布，美国在路易斯安那州的盐井里进行了两次不大的核爆炸试验。委员会把这两次爆炸说成是为了研究地下核武器试验是否能够被发觉。
    委员会计划一共要进行十次类似的爆炸。
