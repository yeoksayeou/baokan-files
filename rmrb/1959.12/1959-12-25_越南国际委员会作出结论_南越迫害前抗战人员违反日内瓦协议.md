Title: Vietnam International Commission Concludes: South Vietnam's Persecution of Former Resistance Personnel Violates Geneva Accords
Author:
Date: 1959-12-25
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 23rd: Newspapers here reported today that the Chairman of the International Commission for Supervision and Control in Vietnam, Ansari, wrote to General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, on December 9th, in response to General Vo Nguyen Giap's memorandum of March 6th of this year regarding the South Vietnamese authorities' serious violations of the Geneva Accords, particularly Article 14(c) concerning the prohibition of retaliation and discrimination against former resistance personnel.

　　In his reply, Ansari stated that the International Commission "will endeavor to persuade the South Vietnamese authorities to change their attitude."

　　The letter also stated that the International Commission's conclusions regarding the South Vietnamese authorities' violation of Article 14(c) of the Geneva Accords have been notified to the Liaison Delegation of the Vietnam People's Army; the International Commission will promptly notify the Liaison Delegation of the People's Army of these conclusions after it has reached conclusions on the incidents under investigation.

### 越南国际委员会作出结论  南越迫害前抗战人员违反日内瓦协议

1959-12-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南国际委员会作出结论
    南越迫害前抗战人员违反日内瓦协议
    新华社河内23日电　此间报纸今天报道：越南国际监察和监督委员会主席安萨里12月9日写信给越南人民军总司令武元甲大将，答复武元甲大将在今年3月6日就南越当局严重违反日内瓦协议，特别是违反协议第十四条丙款关于不得对前抗战人员进行报复和歧视的规定的行为给他的备忘录。
    安萨里在复信中说，国际委员会“将努力说服南越当局，促使它改变态度。”
    信中还说，国际委员会就南越当局违反日内瓦协议第十四条丙款作出的结论已经通知越南人民军联络代表团；国际委员会在它对正在调查的事件作出结论后，将把这些结论及时通知人民军联络代表团。
