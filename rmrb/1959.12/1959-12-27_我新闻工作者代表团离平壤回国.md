Title: Our Journalists' Delegation Returns Home from Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1959-12-27
Page: 5
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th - The Chinese journalists' delegation, headed by Hu Jiwei, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the "People's Daily," left Pyongyang at noon today to return home after a month-long visit to Korea.

　　Those who saw the delegation off at the station included Hyun Pil-hoon, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Journalists' Union, Park Kwang-seon, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Choi Ki-cheol, Vice Chairman of the Korean Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Park Pal-yang, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Writers' Union, and the heads of various newspapers and news agencies in Pyongyang. Qiao Xiaoguang, the Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, also went to the station to see them off.

　　The Chinese journalists' delegation arrived in Pyongyang on November 27th at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Korean Journalists' Union. During their stay in Korea, the delegation visited Pyongyang, Kaesong, Wonsan, Hamhung, Chongjin and other places, visited many factories, enterprises and agricultural cooperatives, and interviewed the workers and farmers of Korea "riding the Chollima."

### 我新闻工作者代表团离平壤回国

1959-12-27
第5版()
专栏：

　　我新闻工作者代表团离平壤回国
    新华社平壤26日电　以“人民日报”副总编辑胡绩伟为首的中国新闻工作者代表团，在朝鲜进行了一个月的访问以后，今天中午离开平壤回国。
    到车站欢送的有朝鲜记者同盟中央委员会委员长玄弼勋、外务省副相朴光善、朝鲜对外文化联络委员会副委员长崔基哲、朝鲜作家同盟中央委员会副委员长朴八阳和平壤各报社、通讯社的负责人。中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光也到车站送行。
    中国新闻工作者代表团是应朝鲜记者同盟中央委员会的邀请在11月27日到达平壤访问的。代表团在朝鲜期间，曾先后访问了平壤、开城、元山、咸兴、清津等地，参观了许多工厂企业和农业社，访问了“跨上千里马”的朝鲜的工人和农民。
