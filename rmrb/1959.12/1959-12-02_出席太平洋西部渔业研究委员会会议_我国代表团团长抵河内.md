Title: Head of Chinese Delegation Arrives in Hanoi for Pacific Western Fisheries Research Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-12-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 1st: Xu Deheng, Minister of Aquatic Products and head of the Chinese delegation to the Fourth Meeting of the Pacific Western Fisheries Research Committee, arrived in Hanoi by train this afternoon.

Those who welcomed him at the station included Nghiêm Xuân Yêm, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry of Vietnam, Lê Duy Trinh, Deputy Minister, Tạ Quang Bửu, Deputy Director of the Vietnam National Science Committee, and He Wei, the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam.

Members of the Chinese delegation and the Korean delegation participating in this meeting arrived in Hanoi on November 28. The Pacific Western Fisheries Research Committee is composed of five countries: the Soviet Union, China, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Mongolian People's Republic. The committee holds a plenary meeting once a year.

### 出席太平洋西部渔业研究委员会会议  我国代表团团长抵河内

1959-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

    出席太平洋西部渔业研究委员会会议
    我国代表团团长抵河内
    新华社河内1日电　出席太平洋西部渔业研究委员会第四次会议的中国代表团团长、水产部部长许德珩今天下午乘火车到达河内。
    到车站欢迎的有越南农林部部长严春庵、副部长黎维贞，越南国家科学委员会副主任谢光宝以及中国驻越南大使何伟等。
    参加这次会议的中国代表团团员和朝鲜代表已经在11月28日到达河内。太平洋西部渔业研究委员会由苏联、中国、越南民主共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国五国组成。这个委员会每年举行全体会议一次。
