Title: Ma Likun
Author: Ma Likun
Date: 1959-12-21
Page: 2

This is a photograph of the workers at the converter steelmaking experimental plant of Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company posting their letters of determination, having comprehensively overfulfilled the annual national plan by twenty-five days ahead of schedule. They are determined to produce more high-quality steel for the country by the end of the year, and strive to win the national red flag in competitive indicators such as converter furnace life and utilization coefficient.

　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency Special Correspondent Ma Likun photo
