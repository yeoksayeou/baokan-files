Title: Free German Youth League Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-12-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: The delegation of the Free German Youth League of the German Democratic Republic, which came to China for a visit at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, concluded its tour and visit in our country and left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 27th to return home.

### 自由德国青联代表团回国

1959-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　自由德国青联代表团回国
    新华社27日讯　应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会邀请，来我国访问的德意志民主共和国自由德国青年联盟代表团，结束了在我国的参观访问，已于27日上午乘飞机离京回国。
