Title: China Sends Experts to Rabat to Assist Morocco in Tea Planting Research
Author:
Date: 1959-12-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, December 27th—Chinese tea expert Liu Guoshi and soil expert Chen Yuping arrived here today at the invitation of the Moroccan government. Representatives from the Moroccan Ministry of Foreign Affairs Protocol Department, the Moroccan National Tea Monopoly Bureau, and the Director of the Agricultural Research Institute of the Ministry of Agriculture welcomed them at the airport.

The two Chinese experts will work with relevant Moroccan research institutions to study the possibility of planting tea trees in Morocco. The Moroccan government has expressed its hope to plant tea trees in Morocco.

Last year, Morocco imported a total of 17,000 tons of tea, worth 7.82 billion Moroccan francs. Most of Morocco's tea imports come from China.

### 帮助摩洛哥研究植茶  我国两专家抵拉巴特

1959-12-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　帮助摩洛哥研究植茶
    我国两专家抵拉巴特
    新华社拉巴特27日电　中国茶业专家刘国士和土壤专家陈禹平应摩洛哥政府的邀请今天到达这里。到机场欢迎他们的有摩洛哥外交部礼宾司和摩洛哥全国茶叶专卖局的代表，农业部农业研究所所长等。
    两位中国专家将和摩洛哥有关研究机构一起研究在摩洛哥种植茶树的可能性。摩洛哥政府表示希望在摩洛哥种植茶树。
    摩洛哥去年共进口一万七千吨茶叶，价值七十八亿二千万摩洛哥法郎。摩洛哥进口的茶叶大部分来自中国。
