Title: Makarios Elected President of Cyprus
Author:
Date: 1959-12-19
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Nicosia, December 15th: Archbishop Makarios was elected as president in the first presidential election held in Cyprus on the 13th.
    According to an official announcement yesterday, Makarios received 144,000 votes, exceeding half of the approximately 240,000 registered voters on the island, and was elected president according to the election system. John Clerides, the candidate from the left-wing "Democratic Union" party who competed with him, also received a large number of votes—71,000 votes.

### 马卡里奥斯当选塞浦路斯总统

1959-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    马卡里奥斯当选塞浦路斯总统
    新华社15日讯　尼科西亚消息：在13日举行的塞浦路斯第一届总统选举已经揭晓，马卡里奥斯大主教当选为总统。
    据官方昨天宣布，马卡里奥斯得了十四万四千票，超过全岛已登记选民约二十四万人的半数，按选举制度规定当选为总统。同他竞争的左翼政党“民主联盟”的候选人约翰·克莱里德斯也得到很大数量的选票——七万一千票。
