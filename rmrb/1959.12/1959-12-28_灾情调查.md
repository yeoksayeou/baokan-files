Title: Disaster Survey
Author: Wei Qimei, Li Cun
Date: 1959-12-28
Page: 8

　　Disaster Survey
    Wei Qimei (painting), Li Cun (poem)
　　The flood washed away the Dragon King Temple,
　　Washing away the Bodhisattva,
　　Imperialists and reactionaries,
　　Chattering noisily:
　　"China is a land of famine,
　　Communism is in a mess."
　　The flood washed away the Dragon King Temple,
　　People's communes build high-rise buildings,
　　The reservoir has clear waves,
　　Crops are green everywhere,
　　Let the Dragon King Temple be destroyed,
　　No one is looking for the washed-away Bodhisattva.
　　Hear this, imperialist reactionaries,
　　This is a disaster survey form:
　　Mud Bodhisattvas, Dragon King Temples,
　　Landlords, bureaucrats, all together,
　　The cold wind and withered leaves signal the end,
　　Watch as you all find it hard to survive. (Attached picture)
　　"This is the only destruction caused by this year's flood that has not been restored, and we don't want to restore it!"

### 灾情调查
韦启美  李村
1959-12-28
第8版()
专栏：

　　灾情调查
    韦启美画  李村诗
　　洪水冲毁龙王庙，
　　把菩萨冲走了，
　　帝国主义，反动派，
　　七嘴八舌哇哇叫：
　　“中国赤地千里，
　　共产主义乱糟糟。”
　　洪水冲毁龙王庙，
　　人民公社建高楼，
　　水库清波浪滔滔，
　　庄稼遍地绿油油，
　　龙王庙毁了任它倒，
　　冲走的菩萨无人找。
　　叫声帝国主义反动派，
　　这是一张灾情调查表：
　　泥菩萨，龙王庙，
　　地主，官僚一古脑，
　　寒风败叶末日到，
　　眼看你们通通都难保。（附图片）
　　“这是今年洪水造成的破坏中唯一没有恢复的，我们也不想恢复它了！”
