Title: Albanian Party and State Leadership Jointly Hold Grand Banquet to Celebrate the Fifteenth Anniversary of Liberation
Author: 
Date: 1959-12-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, November 30th. The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, the Presidium of the People's Assembly, and the Council of Ministers jointly held a grand banquet at the Partisan Palace in Tirana on the evening of the 29th to celebrate the fifteenth anniversary of Albania's liberation. Albanian Party and state leaders Hoxha, Shehu, Lleshi, and others attended the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were the Soviet delegation headed by Spiridonov, writers and journalists from fraternal countries, and returning overseas Chinese. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Albania were also invited to attend the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere, and a splendid cultural program was performed after the banquet.

The residents of Tirana also gathered with relatives and friends on this day to celebrate this national holiday, toasting the glorious achievements of their motherland. In the evening, the citizens watched the festive fireworks display at Skanderbeg Square in the city center.

### 阿尔巴尼亚党和国家领导机构  联合举行盛宴庆祝解放十五周年

1959-12-01
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚党和国家领导机构
    联合举行盛宴庆祝解放十五周年
    新华社地拉那30日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会、人民议会主席团和部长会议29日晚在地拉那游击队之宫联合举行盛大宴会，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年。阿尔巴尼亚党政领导人霍查、谢胡、列希等都出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有以斯皮里多诺夫为首的苏联代表团和各兄弟国家的作家、记者以及归国参观的侨民等。各国驻阿外交使节也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会在热烈、友好气氛中进行，宴会后演出了精彩的文娱节目。
    地拉那的居民们在这一天也都和亲友们欢聚在一起，庆祝这一全民节日，为祖国的光辉成就而干杯。夜晚，市民们在市中心斯坎德培广场观看了节日的焰火。
