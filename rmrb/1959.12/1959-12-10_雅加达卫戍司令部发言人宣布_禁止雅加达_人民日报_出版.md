Title: Jakarta Military Garrison Command Spokesperson Announces Ban on Publication of Jakarta's "People's Daily"
Author:
Date: 1959-12-10
Page: 6

    Jakarta Military Garrison Command Spokesperson Announces
    Ban on Publication of Jakarta's "People's Daily"

    This newspaper reports that the spokesperson for the Jakarta Military Garrison Command in the Indonesian capital announced on the 8th that the publication, distribution, and sale of the Jakarta "People's Daily" would be prohibited starting from the 9th.

    The spokesperson stated that because the "People's Daily" published a report by the Second Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, Yudo, on November 24th, and recently published other news and statements, the Jakarta wartime authorities met with the负责人(responsible person) of the "People's Daily" on the 8th and decided to prohibit the publication, distribution, and sale of the "People's Daily" starting from the 9th until the ban is lifted.

    Yudo delivered a report on his visit to China at a meeting of the Jakarta City Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party on November 23rd. This report included three parts: impressions of his visit to China, the Sino-Indian border issue, and the Overseas Chinese issue.

### 雅加达卫戍司令部发言人宣布  禁止雅加达“人民日报”出版

1959-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

    雅加达卫戍司令部发言人宣布
    禁止雅加达“人民日报”出版
    本报讯　印度尼西亚首都雅加达卫戍司令部发言人8日宣布，从9日起禁止雅加达“人民日报”出版、发行和销售。
    发言人说，由于“人民日报”在11月24日刊载了印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会第二副主席约多的报告，最近又刊载了其他一些消息和声明，雅加达战时掌权者8日接见了“人民日报”的负责人，并决定从9日起禁止“人民日报”的出版、发行和销售，直到这个禁令取消时为止。
    约多曾经在印度尼西亚共产党雅加达市委员会11月23日的一个集会上作了关于他访问中国的报告。这个报告包括访华观感、关于中印边界问题和华侨问题等三个部分。
