Title: Female Party Secretary of the Commune
Author: Guan Hua
Date: 1959-12-26
Page: 8

　　Female Party Secretary of the Commune
　　Guan Hua
　　She, draped in a red jacket, climbs the steep mountain peaks,
　　Like a red flag flying into the high, distant sky.
　　The patches of white clouds trod upon,
　　Float on the rocks beneath her feet.
　　The commune's people behind her,
　　Are like a dragon soaring into the air.
　　She waves her hand to the long sky,
　　And immediately continuous explosions erupt;
　　Smoke rolls out from between the rocks,
　　Like thick clouds carrying rumbling thunder.
　　A silver river rolls straight down from the sky,
　　Rushing along the canyon towards the arid land,
　　Crashing against the rocks along the way,
　　Making joyous roars.

### 公社女书记
管桦
1959-12-26
第8版()
专栏：

　　公社女书记
　　管桦
　　她披着红袄攀登峻峭的山峰，
　　像一面红旗飞上高远的苍空。
　　踩下的片片白云，
　　在脚底岩石上浮动。
　　她后面公社的人群，
　　像一条腾空而起的长龙。
　　她向长天挥一下手，
　　立刻发出连续的爆炸声；
　　烟雾从山石间滚卷出来，
　　就像浓云带着轰响的雷鸣。
　　一道银河直从天上滚下，
　　沿着峡谷朝干旱的土地飞奔，
　　一路上冲打岩石，
　　发出欢乐的吼声。
