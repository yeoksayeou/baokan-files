Title: Displaying the Artistic Achievements of the Korean People and Sino-Korean Friendship: Korean Fine Arts Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-12-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: A Korean Democratic People's Republic Fine Arts Exhibition opened today at the Wenhua Hall in the Palace Museum, Beijing.

　　The exhibition displays over 200 pieces of various art works with Korean national characteristics, including Korean paintings, oil paintings, sculptures, propaganda posters, satirical drawings, prints, and various exquisite handicrafts. These include works by dozens of artists such as Korean Meritorious Artists Sunwoo Dam, Jung Kwan-cheol, and Moon Seok-oh. The vast majority of these works were created after the establishment of the Korean Democratic People's Republic, demonstrating the achievements of Korean artists in resolutely implementing the Korean Workers' Party's principle that "literature and art must serve the people." Works depicting Sino-Korean friendship include the oil paintings "Luo Shengjiao" and "Farewell to the Volunteers," and the Korean painting "Farmer Park Jae-geun." Ancient Korean art, such as mural paintings from ancient tombs, can also be seen at the exhibition. Chung Jong-yeo, a standing committee member of the Korean Fine Arts Artists Alliance and a painter who accompanied the exhibits, gave a detailed introduction to the exhibits.

　　The Korean Democratic People's Republic Fine Arts Exhibition has toured many countries, including the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, and Hungary, and has received high praise.

　　The exhibition is sponsored by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Artists Association. Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Liu Kaiqu, Ye Qianyu, Vice Chairmen of the Chinese Artists Association, Lao She, Vice President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, and more than 500 people from the capital's art circles attended today's opening ceremony. Ma Dong-san, Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Korean Democratic People's Republic in China, and cultural officials from various embassies in China also attended the opening ceremony.

　　After being exhibited in Beijing for three weeks, the exhibition will also be held in Shanghai.

### 展示朝鲜人民艺术成就和中朝友谊  朝鲜造型艺术展览会在京开幕

1959-12-13
第3版()
专栏：

    展示朝鲜人民艺术成就和中朝友谊
    朝鲜造型艺术展览会在京开幕
    新华社12日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国造型艺术展览会，今天在北京故宫文华殿开幕。
    展览会展出了具有朝鲜民族特色的各种美术作品二百多件，其中有朝鲜画、油画、雕塑、宣传画、讽刺画、版画及各种精致的手工艺品，包括朝鲜功勋艺术家鲜于澹、郑宽澈、文锡五等几十位美术家的作品。这些作品绝大部分是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立后创作的，显示了朝鲜美术家坚决执行朝鲜劳动党提出的“文艺必须为人民服务”方针的成就。表现中朝友谊的作品有油画“罗盛教”、“欢送志愿军”，朝鲜画“农民朴在根”等。朝鲜的古代艺术古墓壁画也可在展览会上看到。随同展品前来的朝鲜美术家同盟常务委员、画家郑钟汝，对展品作了详细介绍。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国造型艺术展览，曾在苏联、保加利亚、匈牙利等许多国家巡回展出，获得很高的评价。
    展览会是对外文化联络委员会、中国美术家协会主办的。参加今天开幕式的有对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊、中国美术家协会副主席刘开渠、叶浅予、中朝友好协会副会长老舍及首都美术界人士五百多人。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华使馆代办马东山和各国驻华使馆的文化官员也出席了开幕式。
    展览会在北京展出三周后，还要在上海展出。
