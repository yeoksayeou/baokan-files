Title: Pyongyang Rally Welcomes Returning Korean Residents from Japan; Premier Kim Il-sung Receives Representatives of Returning Residents and Holds Cordial Talks

Author:
Date: 1959-12-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 21st: A mass rally of 7,000 people from all walks of life in Pyongyang was held this evening at the Korean People's Army Gymnasium to warmly welcome Korean residents returning from Japan. Premier Kim Il-sung of the Cabinet of North Korea attended the rally.

When Premier Kim Il-sung and the representatives of the returning Korean residents from Japan entered the venue together, all the first group of returning residents and the masses attending the rally stood up and applauded enthusiastically. The returning Korean residents presented flowers to their beloved leader Premier Kim Il-sung, who cordially shook hands with those presenting the flowers and waved to all the returning residents present.

After the rally began, rows of neatly arranged children from Pyongyang entered the venue carrying colorful flags, flowers, and balloons. The children welcomed and congratulated their grandfathers, grandmothers, uncles, and aunts returning from overseas.

Wonderful song and dance performances were presented at the rally.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 21st: Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea received over fifty representatives of the first group of Korean residents returning from Japan, including Lee Soon-young and Choi Byung-sik, this afternoon.

Premier Kim Il-sung held cordial talks with the representatives. He expressed a warm welcome to the returning Korean residents from Japan for participating in socialist construction, and expressed gratitude to people from all walks of life, political parties, and social organizations in Japan for supporting the return of Korean residents.

The returning Korean residents expressed their eagerness to return to their homeland and their joy and excitement upon seeing the vigorous development of socialist construction in their homeland after returning. They expressed their gratitude to the Republic government for its care for overseas compatriots.

Present at the reception were: Choe Yong-geon, Kim Il, Hong Myung-hee, Park Jung-ae, Park Kim-chul, and Chung Il-yong, leaders of the Korean party and government, as well as leaders of other Korean political parties and social organizations.

### 平壤集会欢迎旅日朝侨归国  金日成首相接见归侨代表并进行了亲切的谈话

1959-12-22
第5版()
专栏：

　　平壤集会欢迎旅日朝侨归国
    金日成首相接见归侨代表并进行了亲切的谈话
    据新华社平壤21日电　平壤市各界人民七千人今天晚上在朝鲜人民军体育馆举行群众大会，热烈欢迎由日本归国的朝鲜侨民。朝鲜内阁首相金日成参加了大会。
    当金日成首相同回国的旅日朝侨代表一同走进会场时，第一批归国的全体旅日朝侨和参加大会的群众一齐起立，并且热烈鼓掌欢呼。归国的旅日朝侨们捧着鲜花，献给他们的敬爱的领袖金日成首相，金日成首相亲切地同献花的人握手，并且向到会的全体旅日朝侨招手致意。
    大会开始后，排着整齐队列的平壤市少年儿童拿着彩旗、鲜花和汽球走入会场，孩子们向从海外归来的爷爷、奶奶、叔叔、婶婶们表示欢迎和祝贺。
    会上演出了精彩的歌舞节目。
    新华社平壤21日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成今天下午接见了从日本归国的第一批旅日朝侨代表李舜英、崔秉植等五十多人。
    金日成首相同代表们进行了亲切的谈话。他对旅日朝侨归国参加社会主义建设表示热烈欢迎，并对支持朝侨归国的日本各界人民、政党和社会团体表示感谢。
    归国的旅日朝侨代表谈到他们渴望回到祖国以及归国后看到祖国的社会主义建设的蓬勃发展而感到欢欣鼓舞的心情，他们对共和国政府对海外侨胞的关怀表示感谢。
    接见时在座的有：崔庸健、金一、洪命熹、朴正爱、朴金喆、郑一龙等朝鲜党政领导人以及朝鲜其他政党和社会团体的负责人。
