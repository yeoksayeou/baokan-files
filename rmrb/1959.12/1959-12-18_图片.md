Title: Malta People Demand Independence

Author: 
Date: 1959-12-18
Page: 6

When British Colonial Secretary Iain Macleod arrived in Valletta, the capital of Malta, on December 8, the Maltese people gathered in the streets holding huge banners demanding independence, waving their fists and shouting "British get out!"
