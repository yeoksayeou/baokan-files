Academic Trends

Soviet Scholar Kozlov Suggests
Improving the System of Political Economy Textbooks

The Soviet journal "Kommunist" published an article by Professor G. Kozlov in its 1959, No. 16 issue entitled "On the Scientific Textbook of Socialist Political Economy." Professor Kozlov also recently published an article "On the Structure of Political Economy Textbooks" (reprinted in "Economic Translations" 1959, No. 6). Both articles suggest that the current system of political economy textbooks needs further improvement. In his recently published article for discussion, the author believes that the current system separates the study of economic laws from the development process of the socialist mode of production as a whole, placing each economic law in a separate chapter, which is a shortcoming. As a result, the political economy textbook fails to reflect the complete development process of the socialist mode of production, the qualitative changes in production relations, and weakens the connection between theory and practice. It also makes it impossible to study the actual process of interaction between various economic laws in the development of the socialist mode of production. In addition, socialist production, as the most important area of the national economy, is discussed in the textbook system in many chapters that have little connection with each other, which is also inappropriate.

The author believes that a scientific textbook of political economy should reflect the complete development process of the socialist mode of production as a unity of production relations and productive forces. Therefore, the political economy textbook system should include the following four parts. The first part, as before, still studies the emergence, development, and victory of the socialist mode of production. The second part analyzes socialist production and related distribution issues and can be called "Socialist Production and Distribution." The third part analyzes the reproduction of socialist enterprises and can be called "Circulation of Funds and Income of Socialist Enterprises" (Socialist Enterprise Reproduction). Only after analyzing the relationship between each enterprise and society and the reproduction of each enterprise can we turn to the analysis of the entire social reproduction. Therefore, the fourth part studies social reproduction and the reproduction of total product and production relations. The author believes that the issue of the gradual transition from socialism to communism should also be included in the fourth part because socialist expanded reproduction creates a material and technical foundation for communism.

Soviet Economic Circles Discuss
How to Treat Econometrics Issues

On July 2nd of this year, the editorial department of the Soviet magazine "Statistical Communications" held a discussion on the use of mathematical methods in economic research and how to treat econometrics issues (abstracts of reports and speeches at the meeting are published in "Economic Translations" No. 12).

The vast majority of participants agreed that it is very necessary to use mathematical methods in Soviet planning, statistics, and economic research. Furthermore, they all believed that the "rational factors" in econometrics must be utilized. However, there were different views on how to understand and treat econometrics issues. The presenter of the discussion, Boyarsky, regarded econometrics as an independent school of bourgeois economics, saying that it is mainly based on the theory of marginal utility and uses mathematical and statistical methods to study the equilibrium problem in exchange. The presenter quoted the definition of econometrics representatives - only the synthesis of economics, mathematics, and statistics constitutes econometrics, believing that the significance of this synthesis lies in replacing these sciences. The presenter also believed that establishing "Soviet econometrics" is impossible.

Many speakers raised different views on some of the arguments in this report. Lifshitz said that it is unfounded to believe that the synthesis of the three methods and means of economics, statistics, and mathematics can "replace" or exclude other sciences and form an independent new science. In the Soviet Union, many problems closely related to economics, planning, and statistics have arisen, but these problems cannot be solved by mathematics and statistics alone, nor can they be found ready-made answers in political economy. Therefore, it is necessary to comprehensively use mathematical methods, mathematical statistics methods, and economic statistics methods on the basis of Marxist political economy. He defined Soviet econometrics as follows: comprehensively using mathematical, economic, and statistical methods on the basis of Marxist political economy, and using the latest electronic computing technology to accurately measure complex categories, quantitative relationships, and regularities in the national economy.

Some comrades believe that the mathematical methods used in economic and planning work can form an independent teaching subject or practical subject.

Many comrades believe that whether or not to call it "econometrics" is a secondary issue. The important thing is to select and develop mathematical methods that are beneficial to our economy and to quickly adopt these methods and use electronic computers in planning and statistical work. (Dong Lin)

专栏：学术动态

　　学术动态
    苏联科兹洛夫撰文建议
　　改进政治经济学教程的体系
    苏联“共产党人”1959年第十六期上刊登了格·科兹洛夫教授的“论社会主义政治经济学的科学教程”一文。科兹洛夫教授不久前还发表过一篇“论政治经济学教程的结构”的文章（载“经济译丛”1959年第六期）。这两篇文章都认为目前采用的政治经济学教程体系还有待进一步改进。作者在新近发表的这篇供大家讨论的文章里认为，目前的政治经济学教程体系把经济规律的研究同作为整体的社会主义生产方式的发展过程分开，把每个经济规律放在专门一章里，这是一个缺点。其结果使政治经济学教程不能反映社会主义生产方式的完整的发展过程，不能反映生产关系的质的变化，同时削弱了理论与实践的联系，也无从研究社会主义生产方式发展过程中各种经济规律的相互作用的实际过程。另外，作为国民经济最重要领域的社会主义生产，在教程体系里被分散在许多相互间没有多大联系的章节里论述，这也是不合适的。
    作者认为，政治经济学的科学教程应该反映作为生产关系与生产力统一体的社会主义生产方式的完整发展过程。因此，政治经济学教程体系应包括下列四篇。第一篇同以前一样，仍然研究社会主义生产方式的发生、发展和胜利。第二篇分析社会主义生产和有关的分配问题，可以称为“社会主义生产与分配”。第三篇分析社会主义企业的再生产问题，因而不妨称为“社会主义企业的基金周转和收入”（社会主义企业再生产）。只有分析了每个企业同社会的关系和每个企业的再生产以后，才可以转入整个社会再生产的分析。因此，第四篇研究社会再生产，研究总产品和生产关系的再生产。作者认为，从社会主义逐步过渡到共产主义的问题也应该列入第四篇里，因为社会主义扩大再生产就是为共产主义创立物质技术基础。
    苏联经济学界讨论
　　怎样对待度量经济学问题
    今年7月2日苏联“统计通讯”杂志编辑部召开了关于在经济研究中运用数学方法和如何对待度量经济学问题的讨论会（会上的报告和发言的译文摘要载“经济译丛”第十二期）。
    绝大多数与会者都认为，在苏联的计划工作、统计工作和经济研究中运用数学方法是非常必要的。并且，他们都认为必须利用度量经济学中的“合理因素”。但是，在如何认识和对待度量经济学问题上却有不同看法。讨论会的报告人波雅尔斯基把度量经济学看成是资产阶级经济学的一个独立学派，说它主要是以边际效用论为基础，运用数学方法和统计方法来研究交换中的平衡问题。报告人引证了度量经济学的代表人物的定义——只有经济、数学和统计的综合才形成度量经济学，认为这种综合的意义在于代替这些科学。报告人还认为建立“苏维埃度量经济学”是不可能的。
    许多发言人对这个报告中的一些论点提出不同看法。利弗希茨说，认为把经济、统计和数学的三种方法和手段综合起来可以“代替”或排挤其他科学而形成一门独立的新科学，这是毫无根据的。在苏联已产生许多同经济、计划和统计有密切关系的问题，但是这些问题既不能单独用数学和统计来解决，又不能在政治经济学里找到现成答案。因此，需要在马克思主义政治经济学基础上综合运用数学方法、数理统计方法和经济统计方法。他对苏联度量经济学下了这样一个定义：在马克思主义政治经济学基础上综合运用数学的、经济的和统计的方法，并借助于最新的电子计算技术来精确地计量复杂的范畴、数量关系和国民经济中的规律性。
    有些同志认为，在经济和计划工作中运用的数学方法可以形成一门独立的讲授学科或实用学科。
    许多同志认为，叫不叫“度量经济学”这个名称是一个次要的问题，重要的是应该选择和发展对我国经济有利的数学方法，并在计划和统计工作中迅速采用这种方法和采用电子计算机。　（东林）
