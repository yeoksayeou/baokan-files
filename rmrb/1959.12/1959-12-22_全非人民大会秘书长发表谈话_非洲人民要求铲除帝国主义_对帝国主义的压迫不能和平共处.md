Title: Secretary-General of the All-African People's Conference Issues Statement: African People Demand Eradication of Imperialism - No Peaceful Coexistence with Imperialist Oppression
Author:
Date: 1959-12-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 19th - Diallo, Secretary-General of the All-African People's Conference and Guinean Minister Resident in Ghana, told the press today that what Africans are asking for is not reform of imperialism, but eradication of it.

He said that the people of all African countries are peace-loving, but there can be no peaceful coexistence with imperialist oppression.

Diallo denounced the imperialist conspiracy of "divide and rule" in Africa. He emphasized the necessity of strengthening unity and friendly cooperation among African countries.

He called for closer cooperation between the All-African People's Conference and the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference.

Explaining the agenda for the next session of the All-African People's Conference to be held in Tunis, Diallo said that the conference will not only discuss the political independence of African countries, but also the issue of ending the economic domination of imperialist countries.

Diallo left here tonight after a five-day visit to the United Arab Republic. Diallo came here to discuss the next session of the All-African People's Conference and the African trade union movement with responsible authorities and various organizations.

### 全非人民大会秘书长发表谈话  非洲人民要求铲除帝国主义  对帝国主义的压迫不能和平共处

1959-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　全非人民大会秘书长发表谈话
    非洲人民要求铲除帝国主义
    对帝国主义的压迫不能和平共处
    新华社开罗19日电　全非人民大会秘书长、几内亚常驻加纳部长迪亚洛今天向报界发表谈话时说，非洲人所要求的不是对帝国主义进行改革，而是铲除帝国主义。
    他说，非洲各国人民是爱好和平的，但是对帝国主义的压迫不能有和平共处。
    迪亚洛指责帝国主义在非洲的“分而治之”的阴谋。他着重指出非洲国家加强团结和友好合作的必要性。
    他要求全非人民大会和亚非人民团结大会之间有更密切的配合。
    迪亚洛在解释将在突尼斯举行的全非人民大会下届会议的议程时说，会议不仅将讨论非洲国家的政治独立问题，而且也将讨论结束帝国主义国家的经济统治问题。
    迪亚洛在阿联访问五天以后，今天晚上离开这里。迪亚洛到这里来是为了同负责当局和各个组织讨论有关全非人民大会下届会议和非洲工会运动的问题。
