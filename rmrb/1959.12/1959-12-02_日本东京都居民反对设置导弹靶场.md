Title: Tokyo Residents Oppose Missile Range Construction
Author:
Date: 1959-12-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 30th: According to a report in today's "Mainichi Shimbun", more than 300 residents of Niijima, which belongs to Tokyo, went to the Niijima Village Office today to demand that the chairman of the village council refuse the "Defense Agency's" plan to set up a missile range in Niijima.

According to reports, the residents who went to the village office shouted in unison against the establishment of a missile range, demanding that the chairman of the village council reject the plan.

### 日本东京都居民反对设置导弹靶场

1959-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    日本东京都居民反对设置导弹靶场
    据新华社30日讯　东京消息：据“每日新闻”今天报道，属于东京都的新岛居民三百多人，今天到新岛村公所，要求村议会议长拒绝接受“防卫厅”在新岛设置导弹靶场的计划。
    据报道，到村公所的居民们齐声高呼反对设置导弹靶场，要求村议会议长拒绝这项计划。
