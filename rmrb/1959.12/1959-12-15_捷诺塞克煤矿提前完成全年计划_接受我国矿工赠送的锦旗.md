Title: The Jenoschek Coal Mine Completes Annual Plan Ahead of Schedule; Receives Banner Presented by Chinese Miners
Author:
Date: 1959-12-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 13th: The Nossek Coal Mine in the Kladno coal district of Czechoslovakia received the all-Czechoslovakia mobile red banner—a banner presented by Chinese miners—on the 13th, due to its outstanding production performance in the third quarter.

That afternoon, a grand banner presentation ceremony was held at the coal mine. Representatives from the Czechoslovak Ministry of Fuel Industry attended the ceremony.

Wang Yimu, First Secretary of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, was also invited to participate in the ceremony.

The Nossek Coal Mine is known throughout Czechoslovakia for initiating a competition movement to win the title of "Socialist Labor Team."

The mine's director, Dvořák, announced at the ceremony that the Nossek Coal Mine had already completed its entire annual production task for this year on December 9th, ahead of schedule.

### 捷诺塞克煤矿提前完成全年计划  接受我国矿工赠送的锦旗

1959-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    捷诺塞克煤矿提前完成全年计划
    接受我国矿工赠送的锦旗
    新华社布拉格13日电　捷克斯洛伐克克拉德诺煤区的诺塞克煤矿，由于第三季度卓越的生产成绩，在13日接受了全捷流动红旗——中国矿工赠送的锦旗。
    这天下午这个煤矿隆重举行了授旗仪式。捷克斯洛伐克燃料工业部的代表，出席了授旗仪式。
    中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆一等秘书王一木也应邀参加了仪式。
    诺塞克煤矿曾以发起争取“社会主义劳动队”称号竞赛运动而闻名全捷克斯洛伐克。
    这个煤矿的矿长德尔伏塔在仪式上宣布，诺塞克煤矿已经在12月9日提前完成了今年全年的生产任务。
