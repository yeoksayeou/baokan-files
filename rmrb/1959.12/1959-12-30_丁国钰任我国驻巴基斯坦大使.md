Title: Ding Guoyu Appointed as China's Ambassador to Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1959-12-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th—Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Ding Guoyu as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Pakistan. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has removed Geng Biao from his position as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Pakistan.

### 丁国钰任我国驻巴基斯坦大使

1959-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　丁国钰任我国驻巴基斯坦大使
    新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命丁国钰为中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦特命全权大使。中华人民共和国主席刘少奇免去耿飚的中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦特命全权大使的职务。
