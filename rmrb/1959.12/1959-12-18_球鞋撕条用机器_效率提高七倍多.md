Title: Shoe Sole Strip Cutting Machine: Efficiency Increased More Than Sevenfold
Author:
Date: 1959-12-18
Page: 2

    Shoe Sole Strip Cutting Machine
    Efficiency Increased More Than Sevenfold
    The making of shoe welts has always been done manually. Recently, Chen Zhiqing and other workers at the Tianjin Da Zhonghua Rubber Factory created an "automatic strip cutting machine" that can cut more than four thousand pairs per hour, increasing efficiency more than sevenfold, and also improving quality.

### 球鞋撕条用机器  效率提高七倍多

1959-12-18
第2版()
专栏：

    球鞋撕条用机器
    效率提高七倍多
    制做球鞋沿条，历来都是手工操作。最近，天津大中华橡胶厂工人陈志清等创制了一台“自动撕条机”，一小时能撕四千双以上，效率提高七倍多，质量也有所提高。
