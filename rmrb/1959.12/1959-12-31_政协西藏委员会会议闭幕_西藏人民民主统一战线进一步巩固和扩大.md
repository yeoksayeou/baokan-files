Title: CPPCC Tibet Committee Meeting Concludes: The People's Democratic United Front of Tibet is Further Consolidated and Expanded
Author:
Date: 1959-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, December 30th: The first plenary session of the First Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Tibet Committee concluded on the 27th.

The meeting approved the candidates for chairman, vice-chairmen, secretary-general, and standing committee members of the First Committee of the CPPCC Tibet Committee. Tan Guansan was elected as chairman. Ngagwang Jigme Ngawang Lobsang Tenpey Gyaltsen, Zhou Renshan, Langdun Gongga Wangqiu, Sampo Tenzin Rinchen, Yao Xi Gongbo Caidan, Tubdain Nima, Bonda Doje, Sampo Doje Pamba (female) were elected as vice-chairmen. Dingjie Doje Gyaltsen and thirty-eight others were elected as standing committee members.

Amidst warm applause, the meeting unanimously adopted a letter of respect to Chairman Mao.

The meeting also adopted a resolution on the tasks of the CPPCC Tibet Committee during the democratic reform movement in Tibet. The resolution stated that under the firm leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, we must diligently study various policies, strive to enhance our political awareness, strengthen national unity, consolidate and expand the people's democratic united front, mobilize all positive factors, and work hard to achieve the tasks of 1960. The resolution also mentioned strengthening political consultation on the current central work and major issues concerning the entire region; strengthening ties with democratic figures from all walks of life through inspection tours and various political activities, encouraging them to continue to promote patriotism, assisting state organs in publicizing policies and laws, and promptly reflecting opinions and suggestions from all sides to state organs; and organizing and promoting theoretical and political studies among democratic figures from all walks of life on a voluntary basis.

The meeting opened on the 20th, and the spirit of democracy, consultation, and unity was implemented throughout. The committee members and representatives attending the meeting came from various regions, ethnic groups, sectors, and classes of Tibet. In group discussions and plenary speeches, they freely expressed their opinions and fully discussed the current situation in Tibet, democratic reforms, and issues related to production and construction. More than forty committee members and representatives spoke at the meeting or submitted written statements. All committee members and representatives expressed their determination to unite more closely around the Chinese Communist Party and the great leader of all ethnic groups, Chairman Mao, continue to strengthen unity under the leadership of the Party, wage unremitting struggle against all conspiracies and sabotage activities of enemies at home and abroad, and strive to consolidate the unity of the motherland and national unity, build a democratic and socialist new Tibet, and build a great socialist motherland.

At the closing session on the last day, Langdun Gongga Wangqiu, Vice-Chairman of the First Committee of the CPPCC Tibet Committee, delivered a closing speech. He said: This meeting has been very successful. In their speeches in groups and at the general assembly, all participants, in the spirit of unity, progress, democratic consultation and free speech, fully discussed the current situation in Tibet and many major issues such as democratic reform and development of production, and also reflected from many aspects the great achievements made in various tasks with the victory of democratic reform, and in the spirit of mutual assistance and common progress, consciously carried out criticism and self-criticism, distinguished right from wrong, and improved understanding and policy level, thus further strengthening unity.

Langdun Gongga Wangqiu said: "This meeting fully demonstrates that under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, the people's democratic united front in the Tibet region has been further consolidated and expanded. It also demonstrates the unprecedented great unity of the Tibetan people. The basis of this great unity is the common desire to build a democratic and socialist new Tibet. Therefore, this meeting is a meeting of unity and progress."

### 政协西藏委员会会议闭幕  西藏人民民主统一战线进一步巩固和扩大

1959-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　政协西藏委员会会议闭幕
    西藏人民民主统一战线进一步巩固和扩大
    新华社拉萨30日电　中国人民政治协商会议西藏委员会第一届第一次全体会议已经在27日闭幕。
    会议通过了政协西藏委员会第一届委员会主席、副主席、秘书长和常务委员的人选。谭冠三当选为主席，噶丹赤巴·土登滚噶、周仁山、朗顿·滚噶旺秋、桑颇·才旺仁增、尧西·贡保才旦、土丹尼玛、邦达多吉、桑颇·多吉帕姆（女）等八人当选为副主席。丁杰·多吉坚赞等三十九人当选为常务委员。
    会议在热烈的掌声中，一致通过了向毛主席的致敬电。
    会议还通过了政协西藏委员会在西藏民主改革运动期间的任务的决议。决议说，我们坚决在中国共产党的领导下，很好地学习各项政策，努力提高政治认识，加强民族团结，巩固和扩大人民民主统一战线，动员一切积极因素，为实现1960年的任务而努力。决议还提到要加强对当前中心工作以及有关全区性的重大问题的政治协商工作；通过视察工作和各种政治活动，密切同各界民主人士的联系，推动他们继续发挥爱国主义精神，协助国家机关宣传政策法令，及时地向国家机关反映各方面的意见和建议；以及组织与推动各界民主人士，在自愿的基础上，进行理论、政治学习。
    这次会议是在20日开幕的，会议自始至终贯彻了民主、协商和团结的精神。出席会议的委员和列席代表来自西藏各地区、各民族、各界、各阶层，他们在小组讨论和大会发言中，畅述自己的意见，充分讨论了西藏当前的形势、民主改革和有关生产建设等问题。有四十多名委员和列席代表在会上发言或作了书面发言。所有委员和代表都表示要更加紧密地团结在中国共产党和各族人民的伟大领袖毛主席的周围，在党的领导下，继续加强团结，对国内外敌人的一切阴谋破坏活动坚持不懈地斗争，为巩固祖国的统一和民族团结，建设民主和社会主义的新西藏，建设伟大的社会主义祖国而努力。
    在最后一天会上，政协西藏委员会第一届委员会副主席朗顿·滚噶旺秋致闭幕词。他说：这次会议开得很好。与会的全体人员在小组和大会发言中，本着团结进步、民主协商和畅所欲言的精神，充分讨论了西藏当前的形势和民主改革、发展生产等很多重大问题，也从多方面反映了随着民主改革的胜利，在各项工作上所取得的重大成就，并本着互相帮助、共同进步的精神，自觉地进行了批评和自我批评，分清了是非界线，提高了认识和政策水平，从而进一步加强了团结。
    朗顿·滚噶旺秋说：“这次会议充分表明了在中国共产党领导下，西藏地区的人民民主统一战线进一步巩固和扩大了。同时也表明了西藏人民空前大团结。这种大团结的基础就是建设民主和社会主义新西藏的共同愿望。因此，这次会议是一次团结进步的会议。”
