Title: Beijing Youth Gather to Welcome the Chinese Table Tennis Team B
Author:
Date: 1959-12-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 18th: Over 1,600 young people in Beijing gathered at the Beijing Hotel last night to welcome the Chinese Table Tennis Team B, which recently returned home after achieving excellent results in international competitions in Europe.

Zhang Jinlin, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, congratulated the Chinese Table Tennis Team B on their achievements on behalf of the youth of Beijing. He then announced that, in response to the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League's call to widely organize table tennis activities among young people, the Beijing Municipal Sports Commission, the Education Bureau, and the Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League have jointly decided to launch a winter sports exercise movement centered on table tennis among young people throughout the city from now until March next year, planning to organize 200,000 young people to participate in table tennis competitions.

Huang Zhong, Vice Chairman of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and Hu Keshi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, spoke at the meeting one after the other, welcoming the victorious return of the Chinese Table Tennis Team B, and encouraging young people to strengthen their physical fitness and actively participate in exercise in order to study better.

### 北京青少年集会欢迎中国乒乓球乙队

1959-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

    北京青少年集会欢迎中国乒乓球乙队
    据新华社18日讯　北京市青少年一千六百多人昨晚在北京饭店集会欢迎最近在欧洲国际比赛中取得优秀成绩后回国的中国乒乓球乙队。
    共青团北京市委第二书记张进霖代表北京市青少年祝贺中国乒乓球乙队所取得的成就，随后宣布说，为了响应团中央关于在青少年中广泛组织乒乓球活动的号召，北京市体委、教育局和团市委已经联合决定，从现在开始到明年3月止，在全市少年中开展一个以乒乓球为中心的冬季体育锻炼运动，计划组织二十万少年参加乒乓球比赛。
    国家体委副主任黄中和共青团中央书记处书记胡克实相继在会上讲了话，欢迎中国乒乓球乙队胜利归来，并且勉励青少年为了更好学习，要增强体质，积极参加锻炼。
