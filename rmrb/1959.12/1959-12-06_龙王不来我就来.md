Title: If the Dragon King Doesn't Come, I Will
Author:
Date: 1959-12-06
Page: 6

If the Dragon King doesn't come, I will.
When the sun rises, a hundred flowers bloom,
Dry fields can also transplant seedlings;
Don't worry that the land is high and the water is shallow,
If the Dragon King doesn't come, I will.
(Pumping station worker)

### 龙王不来我就来

1959-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    龙王不来我就来太阳升起百花开，旱田也能把秧栽；莫愁地高水又浅，龙王不来我就来。
      （抽水机工人）
