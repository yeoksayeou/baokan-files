Title: Inexhaustible Oil Rolls In
Author: Wei Qimei, Chen Tao

Inexhaustible Oil Rolls In
Wei Qimei (painting), Chen Tao (poem)
井架Forests of derricks stand tall,
汽车扬起灰尘, Cars kick up dust,
平地波涛起伏, Waves undulate on the flat land,
但见石油滚滚！ All one sees is oil rolling in!
从前荒山野地, Formerly barren mountains and wild lands,
而今变作新城. Now transformed into a new city.
黄羊奔走相告: The gazelles run to tell each other:
另寻住处安身！ Find another place to settle down! (with picture)
黄羊：“亲家，我们马上搬家吧，看来再不能在这儿来往了！”Gazelle: "In-law, let's move immediately, it seems we can no longer come and go here!"

　　另寻住处安身！（附图片）
