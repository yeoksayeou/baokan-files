Title: New Songs from the Borderland (and one other poem)
Author: Li Ying
Date: 1959-12-23
Page: 8

　　New Songs from the Borderland (and one other poem)
　　Li Ying
　　To the borderland I won't take a train,
　　I wish to walk every inch of the land,
　　Even if there are mirages in the desert depths,
　　I yearn for the whistle there.
　　Striding forward are iron towers,
　　Gurgling along are irrigation canals,
　　Wind and sand cannot hide the brilliance of the new city,
　　Another batch of drilling rigs is brought in by the convoy.
　　The old man telling wild stories is telling me history,
　　Saying: "Under the moon, border passes, the sound of the reed pipe is urgent..."
　　Suddenly a horse gallops past me,
　　The lasso tightly locks yesterday, flying to the horizon.
　　At this time, a solemn image,
　　Suddenly rises high in front of me:
　　The great Gobi is vowing to the motherland to leap forward,
　　The forest belts are slogans, the wisps of smoke are flags.
　　A Glimpse
　　The car speeds across the Loess Plateau,
　　Endless wind and sand fill the eyes;
　　Wind, sand, wind, sand, wind, sand,
　　Between the lines of wind and sand,
　　Suddenly flashes a green wall.
　　Like a rain, like a mist,
　　Yet it is clearly discernible:
　　— Twigs of tender green willow,
　　Are dipping into the yellow of the plateau,
　　Writing the poems of spring...

### 塞上新曲（外一首）
李瑛
1959-12-23
第8版()
专栏：

　　塞上新曲（外一首）
　　李瑛
　　到塞上去不坐火车，
　　我愿一步步走遍大地，
　　纵然沙海深处有幻景，
　　我却向往那里的汽笛。
　　大踏步走去的是铁塔，
　　琤琤的流来的是水渠，
　　风沙遮不住新城的风彩，
　　车队又载来一批钻探机。
　　那讲野史的老人正向我讲历史，
　　说：“月下边关笳声急……”
　　突然从我身边驰过一匹马，
　　套马杆紧锁着昨天向天边飞去。
　　这时一个庄严的形象，
　　忽然在我面前高高耸起：
　　大戈壁正向祖国誓师跃进，
　　林带是标语、烟缕是旗。
　　一瞥
　　车驰过黄土高原，
　　望不断风沙满眼；
　　风沙，风沙，风沙，
　　在风和沙的线条间，
　　忽然闪出绿墙一面。
　　像一阵雨，像一片烟，
　　却又是清晰可辨：
　　——一枝枝嫩绿的柳丝，
　　正蘸着高原的黄色，
　　写春的诗篇……
