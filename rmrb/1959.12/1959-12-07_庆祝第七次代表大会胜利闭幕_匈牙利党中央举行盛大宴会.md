Title: Hungarian Party Central Committee Holds Grand Banquet to Celebrate the Victorious Conclusion of the Seventh Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 5th. The Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party held a grand banquet in the Parliament Building on the evening of December 5th to celebrate the victorious conclusion of the Seventh Congress.

Leaders of the Socialist Workers' Party, such as Kádár, Münnich, and Marosán, attended the banquet. The Soviet Communist Party delegation headed by Khrushchev, the Chinese Communist Party delegation headed by Tan Zhenlin, and delegations and representatives from other fraternal parties attending the Seventh Congress were all invited to the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in a very friendly and warm atmosphere until late at night.

### 庆祝第七次代表大会胜利闭幕  匈牙利党中央举行盛大宴会

1959-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝第七次代表大会胜利闭幕
    匈牙利党中央举行盛大宴会
    新华社布达佩斯5日电　匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会5日晚在国会大厦举行盛大宴会，庆祝第七次代表大会胜利闭幕。
    社会主义工人党领导人卡达尔、明尼赫和马罗山等出席了宴会。以赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联共产党代表团、以谭震林为首的中国共产党代表团、参加七大的其他兄弟党代表团和代表都应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会在非常友好热烈的气氛中一直进行到深夜。
