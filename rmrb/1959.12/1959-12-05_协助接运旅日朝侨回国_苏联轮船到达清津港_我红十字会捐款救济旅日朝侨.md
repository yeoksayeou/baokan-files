Title: Soviet Ships Arrive at Chongjin Port to Assist in Repatriating Pro-North Korean Residents of Japan; Chinese Red Cross Donates Funds to Aid Pro-North Korean Residents of Japan

Author:
Date: 1959-12-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 4th December – According to the Korean Central News Agency, the Soviet ships “Krilion” and “Topolsk,” assisting the Korean Red Cross in repatriating pro-North Korean residents of Japan, have arrived at Chongjin Port in northern Korea. This repatriation effort is based on the agreement signed on August 13th between the delegation of the Red Cross Society of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the delegation of the Japanese Red Cross Society.

Those welcoming the Soviet ships at the dock included leaders from the Central Committee for the Reception of Repatriated Pro-North Korean Residents of Japan, the Central Committee of the Korean Red Cross Society, the North Hamgyong Province Committee for the Reception of Repatriated Pro-North Korean Residents of Japan, and local party and government officials, as well as many workers and local residents.

The captain of the "Krilion" expressed to the welcomers that all the personnel of the Soviet ships were pleased to carry out this mission of going to Japan to pick up Korean citizens.

It is also reported that the two Soviet ships are scheduled to arrive at Niigata Port in Japan on December 12th.

Xinhua News Agency, 4th December – The Chinese Red Cross Society received a telegram on the 1st from the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan, expressing gratitude for the Chinese Red Cross Society's donation to aid pro-North Korean residents of Japan.

The Chinese Red Cross Society sent 100,000 yuan in RMB to the Japanese Red Cross Society on October 27th to aid pro-North Korean residents of Japan who were experiencing hardship due to obstruction of their repatriation. After receiving this donation, the Japanese Red Cross Society transferred the funds to the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan for their disposal on November 28th.

### 协助接运旅日朝侨回国  苏联轮船到达清津港  我红十字会捐款救济旅日朝侨

1959-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    协助接运旅日朝侨回国
    苏联轮船到达清津港
    我红十字会捐款救济旅日朝侨
    新华社平壤4日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，协助朝鲜红十字会去日本接运旅日朝侨归国的苏联轮船“克里利昂号”和“托波尔斯克号”已经到达朝鲜北部的清津港。这次接运旅日朝侨归国工作是根据今年8月13日朝鲜民主主义人民共和国红十字会代表团同日本红十字会代表团签订的协议进行的。
    到码头上欢迎苏联船只的有接待旅日朝侨中央委员会、朝鲜红十字会中央委员会、咸镜北道接待旅日朝侨委员会和当地党政机关的负责人，以及许多工人和当地的居民。
    “克里利昂号”船长向欢迎者表示，苏联船只的全体人员都对执行这次去日本接运朝鲜公民的任务感到高兴。
    另据报道，这两艘苏联船只预定在12月12日到达日本的新潟港。
    新华社4日讯　中国红十字会在1日收到了旅日朝鲜人总联合会发来的表示感谢中国红十字会捐款救济旅日朝鲜侨民的电报。
    中国红十字会曾经在10月27日寄给日本红十字会人民币十万元，救济因回国受到阻挠而生活困难的旅日朝侨。日本红十字会在收到这笔捐款后，在11月28日将这笔捐款转交旅日朝鲜人总联合会支配。
