Title: Marshal Lin Biao Sends a Telegram to General Vo Nguyen Giap to Congratulate the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnamese Army
Author:
Date: 1959-12-22
Page: 1

　　Marshal Lin Biao Sends a Telegram to General Vo Nguyen Giap
    Congratulates the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnamese Army
    Xinhua News Agency, December 21st: Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to General Vo Nguyen Giap, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, to congratulate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Vietnamese People's Army. The text of the telegram is as follows:
To Comrade General Vo Nguyen Giap, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hanoi:
    On the occasion of the glorious 15th anniversary of the founding of the Vietnamese People's Army, I, on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and in my own name, extend warm and fraternal congratulations to you and, through you, to all comrades of the Vietnamese People's Army.
    For the past fifteen years, under the leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party, the heroic Vietnamese People's Army has made outstanding contributions in the struggle to win national independence, democracy, and unification, to defend the achievements of socialist construction, and to safeguard peace in Indochina and Southeast Asia. Your faithful comrades-in-arms, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, firmly believe that the glorious cause of the Vietnamese People's Army will achieve new and great accomplishments in the future.
    Today, the strength and unity of the socialist camp are the most powerful guarantee for defending and consolidating world peace. May the unbreakable friendship between the armies of China and Vietnam and the armies of all socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union last forever!
              Lin Biao, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
                            December 21, 1959, in Beijing

### 林彪元帅致电武元甲大将  祝贺越南建军十五周年

1959-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　林彪元帅致电武元甲大将
    祝贺越南建军十五周年
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅致电越南民主共和国副总理兼国防部长武元甲大将，祝贺越南人民军建军十五周年。电文如下：河内越南民主共和国副总理兼国防部长武元甲大将同志：
    值此越南人民军光荣的建军十五周年之际，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体官兵，并以我个人的名义，向您，并通过您向越南人民军全体同志致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    十五年来，英勇的越南人民军，在越南劳动党的领导下，为争取祖国的独立、民主和统一，保卫社会主义建设成果，维护印度支那和东南亚的和平的斗争中，已作出了卓越的贡献。您们忠实的战友——中国人民解放军坚信：越南人民军的光荣事业，今后还将取得新的伟大成就。
    今天，社会主义阵营的力量和团结是保卫和巩固世界和平最有力的保证，祝中越两国军队和以苏联为首的社会主义各国的军队之间牢不可破的友谊万岁！
              中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长　林彪
                            一九五九年十二月二十一日于北京
