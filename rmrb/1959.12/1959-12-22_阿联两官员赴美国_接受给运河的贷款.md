Title: Two Officials from the United Arab Republic to the United States to Accept Loan for the Canal
Author:
Date: 1959-12-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 16th: Mahmoud Younis, a board member of the Suez Canal Authority, and Abdul Galil Emery, the president of the United Arab Republic National Bank, left here today for Washington. They will sign an agreement in Washington with the World Bank regarding the bank's loan for the Suez Canal project.

### 阿联两官员赴美国  接受给运河的贷款

1959-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿联两官员赴美国
    接受给运河的贷款
    据新华社开罗16日电　苏伊士运河管理局董事会董事马哈茂德·尤尼斯和阿联国家银行总裁阿卜杜勒·格利勒·艾默里，今天离开这里前往华盛顿。他们将在华盛顿同世界银行签订关于这家银行给予苏伊士运河工程贷款的协定。
