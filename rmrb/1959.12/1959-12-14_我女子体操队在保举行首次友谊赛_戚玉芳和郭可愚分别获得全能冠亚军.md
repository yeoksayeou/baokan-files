Title: Our Women's Gymnastics Team Holds First Friendly Match in Bulgaria, Qi Yufang and Guo Keyu Win All-Around Gold and Silver Medals Respectively
Author:
Date: 1959-12-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, December 13th. The Chinese women's gymnastics team, currently visiting Bulgaria, held its first friendly match with the Bulgarian women's gymnastics team on December 12th at the "Nevsky" Stadium in Sofia. Chinese athletes Qi Yufang and Guo Keyu won the all-around gold and silver medals in the competition, respectively (37.80 points and 37.65 points). The Bulgarian team's total score was 186.4 points, winning the team championship by a margin of 0.2 points.

### 我女子体操队在保举行首次友谊赛  戚玉芳和郭可愚分别获得全能冠亚军

1959-12-14
第5版()
专栏：

    我女子体操队在保举行首次友谊赛
    戚玉芳和郭可愚分别获得全能冠亚军
    据新华社索非亚13日电　正在保加利亚访问的中国体操队女子队，12日在索非亚市的“涅夫斯基”体育场和保加利亚女子体操队举行了第一场友谊赛。中国队员戚玉芳和郭可愚分别获得了这次比赛的全能冠军和亚军（三十七点八○分和三十七点六五分）。保加利亚队团体总分为一百八十六点四分，以○点二分的优势获团体冠军。
