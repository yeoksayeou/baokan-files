Title: Bourguiba Delivers Speech Before Talks with Eisenhower Opposing US Control Over the Use of "US Aid"
Author: 
Date: 1959-12-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, December 13th: Tunisian President Bourguiba, in a nationwide broadcast on the 10th, expressed his opposition to the United States controlling the use of "US Aid."

    He said that the question of how Tunisia uses "US Aid" is not the concern of the United States. He also said that Tunisia would not accept any orders on this matter, and if necessary, it would seek assistance elsewhere.

    Referring to the US delegation in Tunisia's view that some of the Tunisian government's plans are unfeasible, Bourguiba pointed out that this issue is not the business of the Americans, "Tunisia has its own standards for judging things, which may be different from American standards."

    In his speech, Bourguiba stated that Tunisia is in great need of foreign aid. However, he stated that foreign aid "must not be subject to any preconditions that we deem unacceptable." But he also believes that US aid has not reached the level of aid that France cut off in 1956.

    Western news agencies believe that these remarks, made by Bourguiba a week before his meeting with US President Eisenhower, are of great significance.

### 布尔吉巴在突美会谈前发表演说  反对美国控制使用“美援”

1959-12-14
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔吉巴在突美会谈前发表演说
    反对美国控制使用“美援”
    新华社13日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯总统布尔吉巴10日在他向全国的广播演说中，表示反对美国控制“美援”的使用。
    他说，突尼斯如何使用“美援”的问题，同美国没有关系。他又说，突尼斯在这个问题上不会接受任何命令，如果有必要的话，它将向其他地方寻求援助。
    布尔吉巴在谈到美国驻突尼斯的代表团认为突尼斯政府的某些计划是不可行的问题时指出，这个问题不是美国人的事情，“突尼斯有它自己判断事物的标准，这可能与美国的标准有所不同”。
    布尔吉巴在讲话中表示突尼斯很需要外国援助。但是他表示，外国援助“必须不附带任何我们认为不能接受的先决条件”。但他又认为美国援助还没有达到法国在1956年所断绝的援助的水平。
    西方通讯社认为，布尔吉巴在将要与美国总统艾森豪威尔会谈前一星期所发表的这些谈话有重要意义。
