Title: Central Treaty Organization Holds Another Military Exercise
Author:
Date: 1959-12-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 5th: The joint naval and air force exercise held by the Central Treaty Organization in Karachi ended on the 4th. This naval and air force joint exercise began on November 16th, with the participation of the United States, Britain, Turkey, Iran, and Pakistan. During the three weeks of exercises, Karachi was shrouded in an artificial atmosphere of war.

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 6th: According to an official announcement here tonight, the Central Treaty Organization is scheduled to hold a joint headquarters and transportation liaison exercise from December 13th to 19th. This exercise, planned by the staff of the member countries of this military bloc and the Central Treaty Organization's Joint Military Planning Staff, is part of the recent frequent military exercises conducted by this military bloc.

### 中央条约组织又举行军事演习

1959-12-09
第6版()
专栏：

    中央条约组织又举行军事演习
    据新华社卡拉奇5日电　中央条约组织在卡拉奇举行的海空军联合演习4日结束。这次海空军联合演习是从11月16日开始的，参加国有美国、英国、土耳其、伊朗和巴基斯坦。在过去三星期举行的演习期间，卡拉奇笼罩着一种人为的战争气氛。
    据新华社卡拉奇6日电　据官方今晚在这里宣布，中央条约组织订于12月13日到19日举行联合总部和交通联络演习。由这个军事集团成员国的参谋人员和中央条约组织联合军事计划参谋部计划的这次演习，是这个军事集团最近举行的频繁的军事演习的一部分。
