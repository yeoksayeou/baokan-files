Title: All-African People's Conference Agenda Announced
Author:
Date: 1959-12-16
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, 15th. The agenda for the Second All-African People's Conference was announced in Tunis on the 14th. The agenda includes: (1) Organizations and means to achieve African independence. (2) The question of breaking free from colonialism. (3) Africa's economic and social development. (4) African unity.

The All-African People's Conference will be held in Tunis from January 25th to 29th, 1960. Invitations to attend the conference have been sent to African political organizations and trade unions.

Diallo, Secretary-General of the Standing Secretariat of the All-African People's Conference, pointed out at a press conference held in Tunis on the 14th that this meeting will promote the independence of African countries. He said that the conference should demand that African countries give more assistance to the Algerian people.

He accused some representatives attending the United Nations meetings of talking about "principles of freedom" on the one hand, but not accepting the proposition that there should be no colonies.

### 全非人民大会议程宣布

1959-12-16
第6版()
专栏：

    全非人民大会议程宣布
    新华社15日讯　突尼斯消息：第二届全非人民大会的议程14日在突尼斯宣布。议程包括：（一）达到非洲独立的组织和手段。（二）摆脱殖民主义问题。（三）非洲的经济和社会发展。（四）非洲团结。
    全非人民大会将在1960年1月25日到29日在突尼斯举行。已经向非洲的政治组织和工会发出参加大会的邀请。
    全非人民大会常设秘书处秘书长迪亚洛14日在突尼斯举行的记者招待会上指出，这次会议将促进非洲国家的独立。他说，会议应该要求非洲各国给予阿尔及利亚人民更多的援助。
    他指责出席联合国会议的一些代表一方面谈论“自由原则”，然而不接受不应该有殖民地的主张。
