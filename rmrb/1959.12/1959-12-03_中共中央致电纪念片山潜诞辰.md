Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Telegram to Commemorate the Centenary of Katayama Sen's Birth
Author:
Date: 1959-12-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Japanese conference commemorating the centenary of Katayama Sen's birth on December 1st. The full text of the telegram is as follows: To the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, forwarded to the Japanese conference commemorating the centenary of Katayama Sen's birth:

    December 3rd of this year marks the centenary of Comrade Katayama Sen's birth. Comrade Katayama Sen was an outstanding Japanese Marxist and an outstanding leader of the proletarian revolutionary movement, and also one of the earliest Marxists in the East. He dedicated his life, without reservation, to the Japanese proletarian revolutionary movement.

    Comrade Katayama Sen was a loyal friend of the Chinese people's cause of liberation. He always praised the revolutionary spirit of the Chinese working class with great enthusiasm, cared about the Chinese revolutionary movement, and repeatedly appealed to the people of the world and the Japanese people to support the Chinese revolution in his work in the Communist International. We feel infinitely grateful for this revolutionary friendship between the Chinese and Japanese proletariat, and express infinite respect for Comrade Katayama Sen's noble spirit of internationalism.

    Now, the Japanese Communist Party, together with the broad masses of the Japanese people, is waging a heroic struggle to get rid of American imperialism's rule over Japan, to oppose the Japanese reactionaries' revision of the Japan-U.S. Security Treaty, and to abolish this treaty, to establish a peaceful, independent, democratic, and neutral Japan. In commemorating the centenary of Comrade Katayama Sen's birth, we express brotherly concern and support for the great struggle of the Japanese people; and we firmly believe that the heroic Japanese proletariat will surely inherit Comrade Katayama Sen's revolutionary spirit to complete the glorious revolutionary cause of the Japanese people.

                  Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                      December 1, 1959

### 中共中央致电纪念片山潜诞辰

1959-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央致电纪念片山潜诞辰
    支持日本人民争取和平独立民主中立的伟大斗争
    深信日本无产阶级一定能够完成光荣的革命事业
    新华社2日讯　中国共产党中央委员会在12月1日致电日本纪念片山潜诞生一百周年大会。电报全文如下：日本共产党中央委员会转日本纪念片山潜诞生一百周年大会：
    今年12月3日，是片山潜同志诞生一百周年。片山潜同志是日本杰出的马克思主义者和无产阶级革命运动的卓越领袖，而且也是东方最早的马克思主义者之一。他把自己的一生，毫无保留地贡献给日本无产阶级的革命运动。
    片山潜同志是中国人民解放事业的忠实的朋友。他始终以极大的热情，讴歌中国工人阶级的革命精神，关怀中国革命运动，并且在其参加共产国际工作的活动中，一再向世界人民和日本人民大声疾呼，支持中国革命。我们对于中日两国无产阶级的这种革命友谊，感到无限珍贵，对于片山潜同志的这种崇高的国际主义精神表示无限敬意。
    现在日本共产党同广大的日本人民一道，为摆脱美帝国主义对日本的统治，反对日本反动派修改日美安全保障条约，并进而废除这个条约，为建立一个和平、独立、民主、中立的日本，正在进行着英勇的斗争。在纪念片山潜同志诞生一百周年的时候，我们对于日本人民的伟大斗争，谨表示兄弟般的关怀和支持；并且深信，英勇的日本无产阶级，一定能够继承片山潜同志的革命精神，来完成日本人民的光荣的革命事业。
                  中国共产党中央委员会
                                      一九五九年十二月一日
