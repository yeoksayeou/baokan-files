Title: Every Set is a Priceless Treasure
Author: Cao Jian
Date: 1959-12-21
Page: 2

　　Every Set is a Priceless Treasure
　　Everyone innovates, racing the spring tide,
　　Tides surge through thousands of paths;
　　Every path of experience is assembled into a set,
　　Every set is a priceless treasure.
  　　　Cao Jian, Committee member of the Northern Machinery Repair Factory Party Committee of Anshan Iron and Steel Company

### 套套都是无价宝
曹建
1959-12-21
第2版()
专栏：来信

　　套套都是无价宝
　　人人革新赛春潮，
　　潮潮涌经千万条；
　　条条经验配成套，
　　套套都是无价宝。
  　　　鞍钢北部机修厂党委会干事  曹建
