Title: Polish Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 6th -- A five-member Polish government trade delegation, led by Mr. Kolar, the deputy head of the delegation and Director of the Planning Department of the Polish Ministry of Foreign Trade, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

	Mr. Jia Shi, Director of the Second Bureau of our Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

	Mr. Szymanski, Commercial Counselor of the Polish Embassy in China, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 波兰政府贸易代表团到京

1959-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社6日讯　波兰政府贸易代表团一行五人，在副团长、波兰对外贸易部计划司司长柯拉尔率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有我对外贸易部第二局局长贾石等人。
    波兰驻华大使馆商务参赞席曼斯基也去机场迎接。
