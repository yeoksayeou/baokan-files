Title: First Volume of Mao Zedong's Selected Works Published in Iceland
Author:
Date: 1959-12-23
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 21st -- Copenhagen dispatch: The first volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" has recently been translated into Icelandic by the Icelandic "Language and Culture" Publishing House and published in Reykjavik. The selected works consist of 270 pages, including five great works: "On Practice," "On Contradiction," "On New Democracy," "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art," and "On Coalition Government."

### 冰岛出版毛泽东选集第一卷

1959-12-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　冰岛出版毛泽东选集第一卷
    新华社柏林21日讯　哥本哈根消息：毛泽东选集第一卷最近已由冰岛“语言与文化”出版社译成冰岛文，在雷克雅未克出版。选集共二百七十页，包括“实践论”、“矛盾论”、“新民主主义论”、“在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话”和“论联合政府”等五篇伟大著作。
