Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Our Fujian Territorial Waters; I Issue the Seventy-Seventh Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-12-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 9th: A U.S. warship intruded into the waters of the Haitan area of Fujian Province from 11:45 to 15:41 on December 9th. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the seventy-seventh serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建领海  我提出第七十七次严重警告

1959-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我福建领海
    我提出第七十七次严重警告
    新华社9日讯　12月9日11时45分至15时41分，美国军舰一艘侵入我国福建省海坛地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第七十七次严重警告。
