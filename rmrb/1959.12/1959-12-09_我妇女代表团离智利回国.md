Title: Our Women's Delegation Leaves Chile for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-12-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, 7th: The Chinese Women's Delegation, invited to attend the Latin American Women's Congress, departed for home today.

During their two weeks stay in Chile, the delegation made contact with Chilean women, political figures, and labor leaders, and visited industrial sectors and workers' residential areas. On the day before their departure, the delegation also accepted an invitation from Pedro Vuskovic, leader of the People's Action Front, to visit the port of San Antonio and the coastal city of Cartagena.

### 我妇女代表团离智利回国

1959-12-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我妇女代表团离智利回国
    新华社圣地亚哥7日电　应邀参加拉丁美洲妇女代表大会的中国妇女代表团今天启程回国。
    代表团在逗留智利的二星期间与智利妇女、政界及工人领袖等进行了接触，参观了工业部门和工人住宅区。在启程回国的前一天，代表团还接受了人民行动阵线领导人佩德罗·丰塞亚的邀请，到圣安东尼奥港和沿海城市喀他享那访问。
