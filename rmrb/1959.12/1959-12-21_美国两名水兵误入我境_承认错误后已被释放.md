Title: Two American Sailors Mistakenly Enter Our Territory, Released After Admitting Error
Author:
Date: 1959-12-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 20th: American sailors Jurley Lee Mensorait and Roy Dunford Bashour mistakenly entered our territory on the night of December 16th and were detained by our local police. After we explained the situation, they admitted their error in crossing the border. During their detention, Mensorait and the other sailors were given due treatment. They were released in Shenzhen on December 20th.

### 美国两名水兵误入我境  承认错误后已被释放

1959-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　美国两名水兵误入我境
    承认错误后已被释放
    新华社广州20日电　美国水兵裘理·利·门错雷特、罗埃·顿福特·巴休尔，于12月16日夜误入我境，被我当地民警拘留，经我方说明情况后，已承认越境错误。在拘留期间，我对门错雷特等水兵给以应有待遇。已于12月20日在深圳地方予以释放。
