Title: This Side and That Side (Picture)
Author: Jiang Yousheng Fang Cheng
Date: 1959-12-29
Page: 8

　　This Side and That Side
　　Jiang Yousheng Fang Cheng
　　We stick them up straight on this side, they stick them up crooked on that side.
　　On this side, happily lining up in order; on that side, frowning and lining up horizontally.
　　On this side, feeling relaxed and happy; on that side, trembling with fear.
　　On this side, the sounds of gongs and drums fill the sky; on that side, the shouts shake the earth.
