Title: Celebrating the Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnamese People's Army, General Vo Nguyen Giap Holds Grand Reception, President Ho Chi Minh and Prime Minister Pham Van Dong Congratulate All Commanders and Soldiers

Author: 
Date: 1959-12-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 23rd: Newspapers here reported today that Vo Nguyen Giap, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense of Vietnam, and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, held a grand reception on the evening of the 22nd to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Vietnamese People's Army.

　　President Ho Chi Minh attended the reception. Also present at the reception were Chairman Ton Duc Thang, Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, Le Duan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, and members of the Central Committee, Deputy Prime Ministers and Ministers and Deputy Ministers of various departments, representatives of various organizations and groups, as well as heroes, model workers, army cadres, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam.

　　President Ho Chi Minh congratulated all commanders and combatants of the Vietnamese People's Army on behalf of the Party Central Committee and the government at the meeting. Prime Minister Pham Van Dong also expressed the government's praise to all commanders and soldiers and wished the People's Army more victories.

　　General Vo Nguyen Giap delivered a speech at the beginning of the banquet. He said that the Vietnamese People's Army is a truly people's army. It was born and grew up in the revolutionary struggle of the Vietnamese people under the leadership of the former Indochinese Communist Party headed by President Ho Chi Minh and the present Vietnamese Labor Party.

　　He then narrated the great achievements of the Vietnamese People's Army before and after the August Revolution, in promoting the peace of the Indochinese branch, and in defending North Vietnam, striving for national reunification, and building socialism after the restoration of peace. He said that the victory of the Vietnamese People's Army is the victory of our entire party and people, and the victory of the combination of Marxism-Leninism with the practice of the Vietnamese revolutionary struggle and the construction of revolutionary armed forces.

　　He said that the Vietnamese People's Army always obeys the peaceful diplomatic policy and the policy of peaceful reunification of the country of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, strictly implements the Geneva Agreement, so as to maintain peace on the basis of respecting the independence, unity, sovereignty and territorial integrity of the Indochinese countries.

### 庆祝越南人民军建军节  武元甲大将举行盛大招待会  胡志明主席和范文同总理向全体指战员祝贺

1959-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

　　庆祝越南人民军建军节
    武元甲大将举行盛大招待会
    胡志明主席和范文同总理向全体指战员祝贺
    新华社河内23日电　此间报纸今天报道：越南副总理兼国防部长、越南人民军总司令武元甲大将，22日晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝越南人民军建军十五周年。
    胡志明主席出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有孙德胜委员长，范文同总理，越南劳动党中央政治局委员黎笋和中央委员们，副总理和各部正副部长，各机关团体代表以及英雄、劳模、军队干部和各国驻越南外交使节等。
    胡志明主席在会上代表党中央和政府向越南人民军全体指挥员和战斗员祝贺。范文同总理也向全体指战员表达了政府对他们的赞扬，并祝人民军取得更多的胜利。
    武元甲大将在宴会开始时发表讲话。他说，越南人民军是一支真正的人民军队，它是在以胡志明主席为首的过去的印度支那共产党和今天的越南劳动党的领导下，在越南全国人民的革命斗争中诞生和长大起来的。
    他接着叙述了越南人民军在八月革命前后、在促成印度支部和平以及和平恢复后在保卫越南北方、争取祖国统一和建设社会主义的事业中的巨大功勋。他说，越南人民军的胜利是我们全党、全民的胜利，是马克思列宁主义同越南革命斗争和建设革命武装力量的实践相结合的胜利。
    他说，越南人民军时刻服从越南民主共和国政府的和平外交政策和和平统一国家的主张，严正履行日内瓦协议，以便在尊重印度支那各国的独立、统一、主权和领土完整的基础上来维护和平。
