Title: International Women's Congress for Disarmament Concludes; Head of Chinese Delegation Exposes US Intensification of Tensions in Pacific Region
Author:
Date: 1959-12-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, December 13th: The International Women's Congress for Disarmament concluded on the 12th in Gothenburg, Sweden, after holding three days of meetings.

Participating in the conference were women representatives and individuals from twenty-six countries. In addition, there were observers. They included women with differing political views.

The conference heard and discussed reports on four issues, including disarmament and the threat of modern weapons to humanity. Twenty-eight people spoke at the conference. Speakers expressed a common desire for peace, disarmament, and opposition to modern weapons. Some representatives, based on the situations in their own countries, exposed the US imperialists' continued schemes to create tension.

Ling Sha, head of the Chinese women's delegation, elaborated on the Chinese government's consistent foreign policy of peace in her speech. She pointed out that the relaxation of international tensions is a result of the strength of the world's people.

She exposed a series of recent US activities in the Pacific region to create tension. She said that the people of all countries must maintain full vigilance against the imperialists.

She said that promoting the realization of the Soviet disarmament proposal is an important aspect of defending peace at present, and Chinese women must work tirelessly with women from all countries for this great cause concerning the bright future of mankind.

The conference adopted three documents: a letter to women's groups in various countries, a letter to women in various countries, and a letter to the heads of the four major powers.

### 国际妇女争取裁军大会闭幕  我代表团长揭露美国在太平洋地区加剧紧张局势

1959-12-15
第6版()
专栏：

    国际妇女争取裁军大会闭幕
    我代表团长揭露美国在太平洋地区加剧紧张局势
    新华社13日讯　斯德哥尔摩消息：国际妇女争取裁军大会在举行了三天会议后于12日在瑞典的哥德堡闭幕。
    参加大会的有二十六个国家的妇女代表和个人。此外还有观察员。她们中间包括具有不同政治见解的妇女。
    大会听取和讨论了关于裁军及现代武器对人类的威胁等四个问题的报告。有二十八人在大会上发言。发言者表示了争取和平、裁军和反对现代武器的共同愿望。有的代表从自己国家所处的情况，揭露了美帝国主义继续制造紧张局势的勾当。
    中国妇女代表团团长凌莎在发言中申述了中国政府一贯的和平外交政策。她指出，国际紧张局势的缓和是由于世界人民力量强大的结果。
    她揭露美国最近在太平洋地区的一系列制造紧张局势的活动。她说各国人民对帝国主义者必须保持充分的警惕。
    她说，促进实现苏联裁军建议是当前保卫和平的一个重要方面，中国妇女一定要和各国妇女一起，为这一有关人类光明前途的重大事业进行不懈的努力。
    大会通过了告各国妇女团体书、告各国妇女书以及致四大国首脑的信等三个文件。
