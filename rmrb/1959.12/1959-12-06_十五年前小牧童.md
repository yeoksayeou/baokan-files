Title: Fifteen Years Ago, A Little Cowherd

Author:

Date: 1959-12-06
Page: 6

A dragon flew into the city fifteen years ago, sending water straight to the rice fields; The hero who subdued the dragon is me, a little cowherd fifteen years ago.
    (Electric plant worker)

### 十五年前小牧童

1959-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    十五年前小牧童城里飞来一条龙，直向稻田把水送；降龙英雄就是我，十五年前小牧童。
    （电厂工人）
