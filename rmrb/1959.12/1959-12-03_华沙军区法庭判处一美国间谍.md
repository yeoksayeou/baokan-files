Title: Warsaw Military District Court Sentences American Spy
Author:
Date: 1959-12-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 1st. The Warsaw Military District Court today sentenced American spy, Jadwiga Płucińska, former director of the Polish Newsreel Film Studio, to seven years of imprisonment, according to a report by the Polish Press Agency.
    The trial showed that Płucińska was bought by American intelligence agencies while staying in the Federal Republic of Germany. After returning from West Germany, she sent information about the deployment of troops on Polish territory to American intelligence agencies.

### 华沙军区法庭判处一美国间谍

1959-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    华沙军区法庭判处一美国间谍
    新华社华沙1日电　据波兰通讯社报道，华沙军区法庭今天判处美国间谍、波兰新闻纪录电影制片厂前导演亚德维加·普卢欣斯卡娅七年徒刑。
    审判表明，普卢欣斯卡娅在德意志联邦共和国逗留时，被美国谍报机关收买。她从西德回来后，曾把有关波兰领土上的军队配置的情报寄给美国谍报机关。
