Title: China Peking Opera Troupe Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-12-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 8th: The Chinese Peking Opera Troupe, a group of 59 people led by the head of the troupe Qian Jingren, returned to Beijing by train tonight after participating in the 7th World Youth Festival and visiting and performing in Norway, Sweden, Finland, Denmark, and Iceland.

The Peking Opera Troupe performed sixty-five shows in twenty cities in the five countries, and also made five television and two radio performances, which were warmly welcomed by the general audience.

### 中国京剧团回京

1959-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    中国京剧团回京
    新华社8日讯　在参加了第七届世界青年联欢节后赴挪威、瑞典、芬兰、丹麦、冰岛五国访问演出的中国京剧团一行五十九人，在团长钱静人率领下，今天晚上乘火车回到北京。
    京剧团在五个国家的二十个城市演出了六十五场，并且作了五场电视和两次广播演出，受到了广大观众的热烈欢迎。
