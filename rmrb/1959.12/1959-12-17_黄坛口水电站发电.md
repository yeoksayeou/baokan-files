Title: Huangtankou Hydropower Station Generates Electricity
Author:
Date: 1959-12-17
Page: 2

The first medium-sized hydropower station in Zhejiang Province, the Huangtankou Hydropower Station, has recently been completed and is generating electricity. This power station stands on the banks of the Wu River and can generate 160 million kilowatt-hours of electricity annually, supplying industrial electricity for the Qu County area and construction electricity for the Xin'anjiang Hydropower Station and the Hunanzhen Hydropower Station, which are under construction.

### 黄坛口水电站发电

1959-12-17
第2版()
专栏：

    黄坛口水电站发电
    浙江省第一座中型水力发电站——黄坛口水电站，已在最近建成并发电。这个电站矗立在乌溪畔，每年可发电一点六亿度，供应衢县地区工业用电和正在建设的新安江电站、湖南镇电站的施工用电。
