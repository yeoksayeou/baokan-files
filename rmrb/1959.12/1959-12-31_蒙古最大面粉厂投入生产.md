Title: Mongolia's Largest Flour Mill Goes Into Production
Author:
Date: 1959-12-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 29th: Mongolia's Ulaanbaatar Flour Mill was completed and officially put into production today.

　　This flour mill was built with the assistance of the Soviet Union. The factory has a total area of 47,700 square meters. According to its designed capacity, it can process and produce 25,000 tons of flour annually. This is currently Mongolia's largest flour mill. Construction of the plant began in June 1957.

　　A mass celebration rally was held at the flour mill this afternoon. Mongolian party and government leaders Tsedenbal, Molomjamts and others attended the rally.

### 蒙古最大面粉厂投入生产

1959-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

　　蒙古最大面粉厂投入生产
    新华社乌兰巴托29日电　蒙古乌兰巴托面粉厂今天建成并正式投入生产。
    这个面粉厂是在苏联的援助下修建的。工厂全部面积达四万七千七百平方米。根据设计能力，每年可以加工生产面粉二万五千吨。这是蒙古目前最大的面粉厂。这个厂是在1957年6月开始建设的。
    今天中午在面粉厂举行了群众庆祝集会。蒙古党政领导人泽登巴尔、莫洛姆扎木茨等出席了集会。
