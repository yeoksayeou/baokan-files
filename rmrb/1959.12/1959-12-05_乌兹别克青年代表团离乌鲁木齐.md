Title: Uzbek Youth Delegation Leaves Urumqi
Author:
Date: 1959-12-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, December 4th. The five-member youth delegation from the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic of the Soviet Union, led by Hamitijan Muminov, First Secretary of the Bukhara Oblast Committee of the Lenin Communist Youth League of Uzbekistan, having been invited by the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Youth League to visit the autonomous region, left Urumqi on the 3rd after a week of sightseeing. They will visit Dushanzi, Shihezi, Karamay and other places before returning home.

During their stay in Urumqi, the delegation visited the Bayi Iron and Steel Plant, the July 1st Cotton Textile Factory, the Hongguang People's Commune in the suburbs, the Xinjiang Bayi Agricultural College, the Xinjiang Medical College, and held discussions with cadres from the Autonomous Region Committee and the Urumqi Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League. Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Saifuddin, Secretary of the Autonomous Region Committee and Chairman of the Autonomous Region, met with all members of the delegation.

### 乌兹别克青年代表团离乌鲁木齐

1959-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    乌兹别克青年代表团离乌鲁木齐
    新华社乌鲁木齐4日电　应共青团新疆维吾尔自治区委员会邀请到自治区访问的苏联乌兹别克共和国青年代表团一行五人，在团长、苏联列宁共产主义青年团乌兹别克共和国布哈拉州委第一书记哈米提江·莫敏诺夫率领下，在乌鲁木齐参观访问一个星期后，于3日离开乌鲁木齐。他们将到独山子、石河子、克拉玛依等地参观访问后回国。
    代表团在乌鲁木齐期间，先后访问了八一钢铁厂、七一棉纺厂、郊区红光人民公社、新疆八一农学院、新疆医学院等，并同共青团自治区委、共青团乌鲁木齐市委工作干部举行了座谈。中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会第一书记王恩茂，自治区党委书记、自治区主席赛福鼎曾经接见代表团全体成员。
