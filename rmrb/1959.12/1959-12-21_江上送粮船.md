Title: Sending Grain Boats on the River
Author: Wang Dongxue, Zhao Zhifang
Date: 1959-12-21
Page: 8

　　Sending Grain Boats on the River
　　Clear water,
　　Blue sky,
　　The surging river passes three checkpoints.
　　Under the white clouds,
　　Waves churn,
　　Emerald waves and red light in eighteen bends;
　　Bamboo forests and tangerine trees on both banks,
　　A beautiful scene of a good land,
　　Grain boats come down the river.
　　Fishermen's songs,
　　Sails on the river,
　　Green water, red sun, golden and bright.
　　Leap Forward song,
　　Leap Forward year,
　　A high-spirited song of the General Line;
　　Double oars stirring the water like琴弦,
　　The melodious singing spreads on the river,
　　Grain boats pass through the heart of the river.
　　High sails,
　　Boat after boat,
　　Tens of thousands of shi of grain on the boats.
　　Night after night transporting,
　　Day after day sending,
　　Sending until the sun sets;
　　The bright moon takes over the shift from the eastern mountain,
　　Dragon boats on the river stretch boundlessly,
　　All are grain-transporting boats.
　　The people of the commune,
　　The boats of the commune,
　　The commune's red flag flutters in the wind.
　　The Party plants flowers,
　　Each bloom fresh,
　　Boat flowers, each petal a sail.
　　The red thread of the people's commune is long,
　　The Party's leadership is the steel needle tip,
　　Sewing together the grain boats on the river.
    　　  Wang Dongxue poem Zhao Zhifang painting (with picture)

### 江上送粮船
王冬雪  赵志方
1959-12-21
第8版()
专栏：

　　江上送粮船
　　清清的水，
　　蓝蓝的天
　　滔滔江水过三关。
　　白云下，
　　浪花翻，
　　碧波红光十八湾；
　　两岸竹林橘子树，
　　一幅美景好江山，
　　江上下来了运粮船。
　　渔家的歌，
　　江上的帆，
　　绿水红阳金灿灿。
　　跃进歌，
　　跃进年，
　　一曲高歌总路线；
　　双桨荡水琴弦动，
　　歌声委婉江上传，
　　江心过来了运粮船。
　　高高的帆，
　　船连着船，
　　船上的粮食千万石。
　　夜夜运，
　　日日送，
　　送得太阳落了山；
　　明月值班东山起，
　　江上龙舟望无边，
　　尽是运粮的船。
　　公社的人，
　　公社的船，
　　公社红旗迎风展。
　　党栽花，
　　朵朵鲜，
　　船花朵朵片片帆。
　　人民公社的红线长，
　　党的领导就是钢针尖，
　　穿起了江上的运粮船。
    　　  王冬雪诗  赵志方画（附图片）
