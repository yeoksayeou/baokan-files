Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Receives Ambassador Chervonenko
Author:
Date: 1959-12-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th - Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, received the newly appointed Ambassador of the Soviet Socialist Republics to China, Chervonenko, this evening. Present at the reception was Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zeng Yongquan.

### 刘主席接见契尔沃年科大使

1959-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见契尔沃年科大使
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天晚上接见了苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟新任驻华大使契尔沃年科。接见时在座的有外交部副部长曾涌泉。
