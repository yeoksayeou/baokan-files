Scientific Research Institutes Widely Study Agricultural Problems; Formulate Agricultural Production Systems for Non-Black Soil Areas

The Agricultural Science Research Institute of the Central Region of the Soviet Union's Non-Black Soil Zone, near Moscow, is conducting extensive research into many agricultural problems. These problems include agricultural technology, breeding, seed selection, agricultural economics, and production knowledge.

Based on the results of their research and the achievements of advanced agricultural workers, the institute's scientific staff has formulated a system for agricultural production in the non-black soil zone, the most important component of which is correct crop rotation and fertilization methods.

The institute maintains close ties with many collective farms and state farms, providing them with agronomic and organizational guidance. It has also helped collective farms and state farms cultivate nineteen improved varieties of grains, legumes, and feed crops.

### 科学研究所广泛研究农业问题  制定非黑土地带农业生产制度

1959-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

　　科学研究所广泛研究农业问题  制定非黑土地带农业生产制度
    莫斯科附近的苏联非黑土地带中央区农业科学研究所，对很多农业问题进行着广泛的研究工作。这些问题包括农业技术、育种、选种、农业经济和生产知识等。
    研究所的科学工作人员，根据研究的成果和农业先进工作者的成就，制定了在非黑土地带进行农业生产的制度，其中最重要的组成部分就是正确的轮种和施肥方法。
    这个研究所和许多集体农庄和国营农场保持着紧密的联系。对它们进行农艺和组织上的指导。并帮助集体农庄和国营农场培植了谷类、豆类和饲料作物等十九种优良品种。
