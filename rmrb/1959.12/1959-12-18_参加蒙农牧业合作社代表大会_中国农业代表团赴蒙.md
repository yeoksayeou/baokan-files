Title: Chinese Agricultural Delegation to Mongolia to Attend Mongolian Agricultural and Pastoral Cooperative Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-12-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 17th. A four-member Chinese agricultural delegation headed by Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, left Beijing by train for the Mongolian People's Republic on December 17th. They were invited by N. Zhagvaral, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry of the Mongolian People's Republic, to attend the Second Congress of Agricultural and Pastoral Cooperatives of the Mongolian People's Republic.

    Cai Ziwei, Vice Minister of Agriculture, Li Qiangfen, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others went to the station to see them off. Dangaasuren, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian People's Republic's Embassy in China, also went to see them off.

### 参加蒙农牧业合作社代表大会  中国农业代表团赴蒙

1959-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    参加蒙农牧业合作社代表大会
    中国农业代表团赴蒙
    新华社17日讯　以农业部部长廖鲁言为首的中国农业代表团一行四人，于12月17日乘车离京赴蒙古人民共和国。他们是应蒙古人民共和国部长会议副主席兼农牧业部部长尼·热格瓦拉尔的邀请，前去参加蒙古人民共和国第二届农牧业合作社代表大会的。
    前往车站欢送的有农业部副部长蔡子伟、外交部第二亚洲司副司长李强奋等人，蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办丹格苏伦也前往欢送。
