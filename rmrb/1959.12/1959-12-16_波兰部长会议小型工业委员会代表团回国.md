Title: Polish Ministry of Small Industries Committee Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-12-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 15th: The Polish Ministry of Small Industries Committee delegation of five people, headed by Polish Ministry of Small Industries Committee Vice-Chairman M. Hoffman, concluded their friendly visit to our country and left Beijing for home today. Among those at the airport to see them off were Wang Xinyuan, Vice Minister of Light Industry, and others. Fultato, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, was also at the airport to see them off.

### 波兰部长会议小型工业委员会代表团回国

1959-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰部长会议小型工业委员会代表团回国
    新华社15日讯　以波兰部长会议小型工业委员会副主席米·霍夫曼为首的波兰部长会议小型工业委员会代表团一行五人，结束了在我国的友好访问，今天离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有轻工业部副部长王新元等多人。波兰驻华大使馆临时代办傅拉托也到机场欢送。
