Title: Shehu Receives Chinese Writer Yang Shuo
Author:
Date: 1959-12-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 1st: Albanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu received Chinese writer Yang Shuo today. Yang Shuo was invited by the Albanian Writers and Artists Association to participate in the celebrations for the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Albania. Shehu also received guests from the German Democratic Republic, Iraq, Lebanon, and Luxembourg at the same time.

### 谢胡接见我国作家杨朔

1959-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    谢胡接见我国作家杨朔
    新华社地拉那1日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡今天接见了中国作家杨朔。杨朔是应阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会的邀请前来参加阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年庆祝活动的。谢胡并同时接见了来自德意志民主共和国、伊拉克、黎巴嫩和卢森堡的客人们。
