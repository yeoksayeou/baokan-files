Title: Ministry of Internal Affairs Issues Notice on Carrying Out Spring Festival Activities to Support the Army and Give Preferential Treatment to Families of Servicemen

Author:

Date: 1959-12-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: The Ministry of Internal Affairs today issued a notice to the Civil Affairs Departments and Bureaus of all provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government regarding carrying out activities to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of servicemen during the 1960 Spring Festival.

　　The notice requires all localities, under the leadership of the local Party committees, to adopt appropriate forms around the 1960 Spring Festival to conduct comfort visits or get-togethers for families of martyrs, families of servicemen, disabled soldiers, retired military officers, as well as residents of sanatoriums for families of martyrs and chronic disease sanatoriums for demobilized soldiers, and to extend New Year greetings to them. Through comfort visits and various activities to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of servicemen, the people should be educated to cherish the People's Army, support national defense construction, and receive revolutionary tradition education and education on supporting the army and giving preferential treatment to families of servicemen. The work of supporting the army and giving preferential treatment to families of servicemen during the Spring Festival should play a role in boosting the morale of the troops, enhancing national defense strength, and stimulating the patriotic thinking of the masses and promoting the spirit of communism. At the same time, attention should also be paid to educating the families of martyrs, families of servicemen, honorary soldiers, and demobilized soldiers on maintaining and carrying forward their honor and socialist ideology, so that they become models in observing and implementing government laws and decrees, and contribute even greater strength to the cause of socialist construction.

　　The notice also requires the civil affairs departments in all localities to summarize and check the preferential treatment and demobilization resettlement work in their regions through the activities of supporting the army and giving preferential treatment to families of servicemen during the Spring Festival. They should work with the conscription departments and other relevant departments to do a good job in handling the retirement of conscripts in 1960.

### 内务部通知开展春节拥军优属工作

1959-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　内务部通知开展春节拥军优属工作
    新华社22日讯　内务部今天向各省、自治区、直辖市的民政厅、局发出了关于1960年春节期间进行拥军优属工作的通知。
    通知要求各地在1960年春节前后，在当地党委的领导下，采取适当的形式，对烈属、军属、残废军人、退休军官，以及烈属养老院和复员军人慢性病疗养院的住院人员，进行慰问或联欢活动，向他们拜年贺节。通过慰问和各项拥军优属活动，对人民群众进行爱护人民军队、支援国防建设的教育以及进行革命传统教育和拥军优属教育。要使春节期间的拥军优属工作，起到鼓舞部队士气、增进国防力量和激发群众的爱国主义思想、发扬共产主义精神的作用。同时，还应注意对烈属、军属和荣誉军人、复员军人进行保持光荣、发扬光荣和社会主义思想的教育，使他们成为遵守并执行政府法令的模范，在社会主义建设事业中贡献更大的力量。
    通知还要求各地民政部门通过春节期间的拥军优属活动，总结和检查本地区的优抚和复员安置工作，应当和兵役部门以及其他有关部门一起，做好1960年义务兵退伍的处理工作。
