Title: Free German Youth League Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-12-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st. A delegation of six members from the Free German Youth League of the German Democratic Republic, led by Hans Modrow, Central Secretary of the Free German Youth League, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League.

Welcoming them at the airport were Hu Keshi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Standing Committee member Yang Haibo, and Qian Liren, Director of the International Liaison Department, among others.

The delegation will stay in our country for three weeks. In addition to Beijing, they will also visit Harbin, Dalian, Nanjing, Shanghai and other cities.

### 自由德国青年联盟代表团到京

1959-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    自由德国青年联盟代表团到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会邀请来我国访问的德意志民主共和国自由德国青年联盟代表团一行六人，在团长、自由德国青年联盟中央书记汉斯·莫德洛夫率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有共青团中央书记处书记胡克实、常委杨海波、国际联络部部长钱李仁等。
    代表团将在我国逗留三个星期。除北京外，还将去哈尔滨、大连、南京、上海等城市访问。
