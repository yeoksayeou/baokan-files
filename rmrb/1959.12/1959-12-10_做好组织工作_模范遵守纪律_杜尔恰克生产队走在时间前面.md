Title: Good Organization, Exemplary Discipline: Durczak Production Team Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1959-12-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 9th: The Durczak production team of the Peter Cinger Coal Mine in the famous Ostrava-Karvina coal district of Czechoslovakia has completed the tasks stipulated in the Second Five-Year Plan (1956-1960) thirteen months ahead of schedule.

This production team, composed of thirty miners, has mined 180,671 tons of coal and successfully reduced the production cost per ton of coal by 1.15 crowns (Czech currency).

The Durczak production team has achieved such outstanding results mainly due to good work organization and exemplary adherence to labor discipline by all team members.

In a letter reporting their victorious news to the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, the production team stated that they would participate in the competition for the title of "Socialist Labor Team" and strive to create even better results.

### 做好组织工作  模范遵守纪律  杜尔恰克生产队走在时间前面

1959-12-10
第5版()
专栏：

    做好组织工作  模范遵守纪律
    杜尔恰克生产队走在时间前面
    新华社布拉格9日电　捷克斯洛伐克著名的俄斯特拉发—卡尔维那煤区的彼得钦格尔煤矿，有一个生产队已经提前十三个月完成了第二个五年计划（1956—1960年）规定的任务。
    这个由三十名矿工组成的杜尔恰克生产队已经开采了十八万零六百七十一吨煤，并且成功地使每吨煤的生产成本降低一点一五克朗（捷币）。
    杜尔恰克生产队所以能获得这种杰出的成就，主要是由于工作组织得好和全体队员的模范地遵守劳动纪律。
    这个生产队在向捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会汇报他们的胜利的喜讯的信中说，他们要参加争取“社会主义劳动队”称号的竞赛，争取创造更好的成绩。
