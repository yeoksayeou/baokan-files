Title: China's Women's Delegation to Attend International Women's Congress for Disarmament Goes to Sweden
Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 6th. Hu Naiqiu, a member of the Chinese delegation to the International Women's Congress for Disarmament, left Beijing by plane this morning for Sweden.

The International Women's Congress for Disarmament will be held in Gothenburg, Sweden, from December 10th to 13th. The Chinese women's delegation consists of Ling Sha and Hu Naiqiu. Ling Sha, the head of the delegation, is currently abroad.

### 参加国际妇女争取裁军大会  我妇女代表团去瑞典

1959-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    参加国际妇女争取裁军大会
    我妇女代表团去瑞典
    新华社6日讯　中国参加国际妇女争取裁军大会的代表团团员胡耐秋，今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往瑞典。
    国际妇女争取裁军大会将于12月10日到13日在瑞典哥德堡举行。中国妇女代表团由凌莎、胡耐秋二人组成，团长凌莎现在国外。
