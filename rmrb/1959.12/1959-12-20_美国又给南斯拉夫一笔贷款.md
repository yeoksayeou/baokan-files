Title: The US Again Gives Yugoslavia a Loan
Author:
Date: 1959-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, December 17th: The Yugoslav government announced on the 17th that the US government has decided to give Yugoslavia another loan of 15 million US dollars. The agreement on this loan was signed in Washington on the 17th.

This loan will be used to purchase hydroelectric power station equipment in the United States. The loan will be repaid within 25 years, with an interest rate of 3.5%.

According to incomplete statistics, since the beginning of this year, Yugoslavia has received 66.65 million US dollars in loans from the United States.

### 美国又给南斯拉夫一笔贷款

1959-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    美国又给南斯拉夫一笔贷款
    新华社贝尔格莱德17日电　南斯拉夫政府17日宣布，美国政府又决定给南斯拉夫一千五百万美元的贷款。关于这笔贷款的协定17日已在华盛顿签订。
    这笔贷款将用来在美国购买水电站设备。贷款将在二十五年内还清，利息为3.5％。
    据不完全统计，今年以来，南斯拉夫已从美国获得六千六百五十万美元的贷款。
