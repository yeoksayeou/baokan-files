Title: Gale and Temperature Drop Alert
Author:
Date: 1959-12-14
Page: 6

    Gale and Temperature Drop Alert
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th -- The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a gale and temperature drop alert at 4 p.m. on the 13th: Affected by cold air, it is expected that in the next 36 hours, there will be northerly winds of force 6 to 8 in the Shandong Peninsula, the Yellow Sea, the East China Sea, and the Taiwan Strait, and northerly winds of about force 6 on the river and lake surfaces of the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River and the Huai River basin. In the 24 hours after the cold air passes through the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River and the Huai River basin and other regions, the temperature will drop by 5 to 7 degrees Celsius.

### 大风和降温警报

1959-12-14
第6版()
专栏：

    大风和降温警报
    新华社13日讯　中央气象科学研究所13日下午四时发布大风和降温警报：受冷空气影响，预计未来三十六小时内，山东半岛、黄海、东海和台湾海峡有六到八级的偏北风，长江中下游和淮河流域的江面湖面上有六级左右的偏北风。长江中下游和淮河流域等地区在冷空气过后的二十四小时内，温度下降摄氏五到七度。
