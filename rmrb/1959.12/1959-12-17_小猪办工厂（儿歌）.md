Title: Little Pig Runs a Factory (Nursery Rhyme)
Author: Chen Tao
Date: 1959-12-17
Page: 8

Little pig, so capable,
Humming and hawing, runs a factory.
No machines, no buildings,
Everything we need is on our own bodies.
What kind of factory? A fertilizer factory.
Plenty of raw materials, busy production,
Helping the commune to grow more grain.
Many sisters, all running factories,
Good, good, strong, strong,
Thousands and thousands of factories all over the farm.

### 小猪办工厂（儿歌）
陈涛
1959-12-17
第8版()
专栏：

    小猪办工厂（儿歌）
    陈涛小小猪，本领强，哼呀哼，办工厂。没机器，没厂房，这些都在咱身上。什么厂？肥料厂。原料足，生产忙，帮助公社多打粮。姊妹多，都办厂，好呀好，强呀强，千厂万厂遍农庄。
