Title: A Couple獻 Treasures
Author: Wei Huali
Date: 1959-12-21
Page: 2

　　Technical collaboration is running high,
　　A new thing has spread:
　　Cao Enfu works on collaboration under the lamp,
　　Shi Guixia smiles holding her baby;
　　The couple brainstorms until the fifth watch,
　　Each watch brings a plan, a treasure of gold.
　　　  Worker of the steel casting workshop Wei Huali

### 夫妻献宝
未华利
1959-12-21
第2版()
专栏：来信

    夫妻献宝
　　技术配套热潮高，
　　一桩新事传开了：
　　曹恩福灯下搞配套，
　　石桂霞怀抱婴儿笑；
　　夫妻动脑五更敲，
　　一更一套献金宝。
　　　  铸钢车间工人　未华利
