Title: Gale and Cooling Alert
Author:
Date: 1959-12-20
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a gale and cooling alert at 8 p.m. on the 19th:

    Affected by low pressure and cold air, it is expected that in the next 24 hours, there will be northerly winds of force 6 to 8 in the eastern Bohai Sea, the Yellow Sea, the East China Sea, the Taiwan Strait, and the northeastern South China Sea; there will be northerly winds of force 6 to 7 in the western Bohai Sea, the Shandong Peninsula, the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River and the lake surfaces of the lower reaches of the Huai River, as well as the northwestern South China Sea and the Beibu Gulf; there will be northerly winds of about force 5 in the provinces of the Jianghuai region and Guangdong and Guangxi. In the above areas, the temperature will continue to drop. It is expected that from the 20th to the morning of the 22nd, the minimum temperature in northern Sichuan, northern Guizhou, and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River will drop to 0 degrees Celsius or below successively; the minimum temperature in central Sichuan, southern Guizhou, northern Guangdong and Guangxi, western and northern Fujian, southern Jiangxi, and coastal areas of southern Zhejiang will drop to 4 degrees Celsius or below successively, with frost expected in most areas; the minimum temperature in southern Guangdong, southern Guangxi, and southern Fujian will drop to 6 to 9 degrees Celsius.

### 大风和降温警报

1959-12-20
第2版()
专栏：

    大风和降温警报
    新华社19日讯　中央气象科学研究所19日下午八时发布大风和降温警报：
    受低气压和冷空气影响，预计未来二十四小时内，渤海东部、黄海、东海、台湾海峡、南海东北部有六到八级偏北风；渤海西部、山东半岛、长江流域中下游和淮河下游的江面湖面上以及南海西北部、北部湾，有六、七级的偏北风；江淮流域各省和广东、广西有五级左右偏北风。以上地区，温度将继续下降，预计20日到22日早晨，四川北部、贵州北部、长江流域中下游，最低气温将先后降到摄氏零度和零度以下；四川中部、贵州南部、两广北部、福建西部北部、江西南部、浙江南部沿海，最低气温将先后降低到摄氏四度和四度以下，大部地区有霜冻出现；广东南部、广西南部、福建南部，最低气温将降低到摄氏六度到九度。
