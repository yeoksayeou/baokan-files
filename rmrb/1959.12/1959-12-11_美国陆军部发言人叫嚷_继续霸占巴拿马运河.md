Title: U.S. Army Department Spokesman Shouts to Continue Occupying the Panama Canal
Author:
Date: 1959-12-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 9th. A U.S. Army Department spokesman clamored on the 7th that the United States should continue to occupy the Panama Canal in order to serve U.S. military plans.

The spokesman said that the Panama Canal is "an extremely important tool" for the United States both economically and militarily. He pointed out that during the U.S. war of aggression against Korea, more than 1,000 ships carrying munitions and supplies for the invading Korean national forces passed through the Panama Canal. He also said that the United States must "effectively control this canal, keep it open and prevent sabotage."

### 美国陆军部发言人叫嚷  继续霸占巴拿马运河

1959-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    美国陆军部发言人叫嚷
    继续霸占巴拿马运河
    新华社9日讯　华盛顿消息：美国陆军部发言人7日叫嚷美国要继续霸占巴拿马运河，以便为美国的军事计划服务。
    这个发言人说，巴拿马运河在经济和军事上对美国都是“一个极为重要的工具”。他指出，在美国侵朝战争期间，就有一千多条船载运着供给侵朝国家部队的军火和供应品通过巴拿马运河。他还说，美国必须“实实在在控制这条运河，保持运河的开放和防止破坏活动”。
