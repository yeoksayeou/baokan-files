Title: Moroccan Istiqlal Party Passes Resolution Demanding Withdrawal of All Foreign Troops
Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, December 3rd. The Moroccan Istiqlal Party, at a meeting of party cadres in Fez on November 29th, passed a resolution demanding the withdrawal of all foreign troops, and expressed its determination to struggle until this urgent national demand is realized. The Istiqlal Party believes that the presence of foreign troops from France, Spain, and the United States is a violation of Morocco's sovereignty and constitutes a constant provocation.

The meeting was held to prepare for the congress scheduled for January of next year. This congress will be the first congress held by the Istiqlal Party since its split in January of this year.

### 摩洛哥独立党通过决议  要求一切外国军队撤出

1959-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥独立党通过决议
    要求一切外国军队撤出
    据新华社拉巴特3日电　摩洛哥独立党11月29日在非斯举行的党的干部会议上，通过决议，要求撤退一切外国军队，并表示决心进行斗争，直到这个迫切的民族要求得到实现为止。独立党认为，法国、西班牙和美国等外国军队的留驻，是侵犯摩洛哥的主权，同时形成经常的挑衅。
    这次会议是为了预定明年1月召开的代表大会进行准备。而这次大会将是独立党在今年1月发生分裂后第一次召开的代表大会。
