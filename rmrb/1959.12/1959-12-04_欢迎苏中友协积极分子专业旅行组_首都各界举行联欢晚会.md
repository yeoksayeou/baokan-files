Title: Capital Circles Hold Joint Soiree to Welcome the Activist Professional Touring Group of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association

Author: 
Date: 1959-12-04
Page: 4

    Welcoming the Activist Professional Touring Group of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    Capital Circles Hold Joint Soiree
    Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd. This evening, people from all walks of life in the capital held a friendship soiree with comrades from the Activist Professional Touring Group of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, who are visiting our country, at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Hall in Beijing.
    This professional touring group came to our country on December 1st for a tour and visit in accordance with the provisions of the cooperation agreement between the Sino Soviet Friendship Association and the Union of Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Soviet-China Frienship Society. The activists of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association from major cities and republics of the Soviet Union include professors, researchers, writers, composers, playwrights, designers, editors, collective farm leaders, and people from other fields. At tonight's soiree, people from education, literature and art, publishing, trade unions, women's federations, and other fields in the capital gathered with Soviet friends, chatting, exchanging gifts, and extending best wishes and brotherly sentiments.
    The soiree was held by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and others were invited to attend the soiree.
    In addition to visiting Beijing, the professional tour group will also visit Shanghai, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Nanjing and other cities.

### 欢迎苏中友协积极分子专业旅行组  首都各界举行联欢晚会

1959-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    欢迎苏中友协积极分子专业旅行组
    首都各界举行联欢晚会
    新华社3日讯　今天晚间，首都各界人士同来我国访问的苏中友好协会积极分子专业旅行组的同志们，在北京中苏友好馆进行了友谊联欢。
    这个专业旅行组是根据中苏友好协会总会同苏联对外友好和文化联系协会联合会、苏中友好协会合作协定的规定，于12月1日来到我国旅行访问的。来自苏联各大城市和各加盟共和国的苏中友好协会的积极分子们，包括有教授、研究员、作家、作曲家、戏剧家、设计师、编辑、集体农庄负责人和其他方面的人士。在今晚的联欢会上，首都教育、文学艺术、出版、工会、妇女等方面的人士同苏联朋友们欢聚一堂，他们分别围在一起畅谈，互赠礼品，互道美好的祝贺和表达兄弟般的情谊。
    联欢晚会是由中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会举行的。苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫等应邀出席了联欢晚会。
    专业旅行组除访问北京外，还将访问上海、广州、杭州、南京等城市。
