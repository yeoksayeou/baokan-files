Title: Emphasize Recycling Metal Boxes and Tubes
Author: Wang Suwen
Date: 1959-12-10
Page: 2

Suggestion:

    Emphasize Recycling Metal Boxes and Tubes
    Oil paints, shoe polish, ointments, and toothpaste sold on the market are all packaged in metal boxes or tubes. After the contents are used up, some of these metal boxes or tubes can be recycled and reused, while others can be processed to produce recycled lead. According to statistics, each ton of waste lead tubes can be made into 53,000 toothpaste tubes, or about 4,000 battery plates.
    However, some waste recycling departments currently only recycle toothpaste tubes and do not recycle other waste metal boxes or tubes. It is hoped that relevant departments will pay attention to the role of this scrap metal and organize its recycling and utilization.
              (Hefei Wang Suwen)

### 重视回收金属盒管
王素文
1959-12-10
第2版()
专栏：建议

    重视回收金属盒管
    市场上出售的油彩色膏、鞋油、药膏和牙膏等，都是采用的金属盒或管包装。这些金属盒或管，在油膏用完后，有的可以回收再用，有的经过加工处理，可以制成再生铅。据统计，每吨废铅管可以制成牙膏管五万三千支，或者制造四千张左右的蓄电池片。
    可是现在有些废品回收部门只回收牙膏管，不回收其他废金属盒或管，希望有关部门重视这项废金属的作用，组织回收和利用。
              （合肥　王素文）
