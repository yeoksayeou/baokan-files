Title: U.S. Military Conducts Missile Test in Okinawa Again
Author:
Date: 1959-12-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 13th: U.S. forces stationed in Okinawa test-fired two "Nike" missiles at Cape Zampa in central Okinawa Island on the 12th. This is the twelfth "Nike" missile launch exercise conducted by the U.S. military in Okinawa. The continuous missile launches by the United States in Okinawa is a measure to create tension in the Far East. Fifty representatives from the Okinawa Anti-Atomic and Hydrogen Bomb Council held a rally at the missile explosion site on the same day to protest the U.S. military's missile launch.

### 美军又在冲绳试射导弹

1959-12-15
第6版()
专栏：

    美军又在冲绳试射导弹
    据新华社13日讯　东京消息：驻在冲绳的美军12日在冲绳岛中部残波岬试射了两枚“奈克”式导弹。这是美军在冲绳举行的第十二次发射“奈克”导弹演习。美国连续在冲绳岛发射导弹是它在远东制造紧张局势的一个措施。冲绳禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会的五十位代表当天在导弹的爆炸现场举行了演说会，抗议美军发射导弹。
