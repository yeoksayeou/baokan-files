Title: Iraqi Government Issues Decree Announcing Abolition of the Turkish-Iraqi Treaty
Author:
Date: 1959-12-25
Page: 5

　　Iraqi Government Issues Decree
　　Announcing Abolition of the Turkish-Iraqi Treaty

　　Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, December 22nd: "Al-Zaman" reported today that the Iraqi government has promulgated a republican decree announcing the abolition of the Iraqi-Turkish "Mutual Cooperation Pact" signed in Baghdad on February 24, 1955, as well as all agreements and annexes related to this pact.

　　The "Turkish-Iraqi Mutual Cooperation Pact" was the predecessor of the Baghdad Pact Organization. With the successive participation of Britain, Pakistan, and Iran in this pact, the Baghdad Pact Organization was formally announced in November 1955.

　　The decree pointed out that, considering Iraq's continued participation as a party to the "Turkish-Iraqi Mutual Cooperation Pact" is not in line with the policy of positive neutrality and the nature of the July 14 Revolution, Iraq has therefore abolished this treaty. This decree came into effect on March 24, 1959, the date of Iraq's withdrawal from the Baghdad Pact.

### 伊拉克政府颁布法令  宣布废除土—伊条约

1959-12-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　伊拉克政府颁布法令
    宣布废除土—伊条约
    新华社巴格达22日电　“时代报”今天报道，伊拉克政府颁布了一项共和国法令，宣布废除1955年2月24日在巴格达签订的伊拉克和土耳其“互助合作公约”，以及和这个公约有关的一切协定和附件。
    “土—伊互助合作公约”是巴格达条约组织的前身，随着英国、巴基斯坦和伊朗先后参加这个公约，巴格达条约组织便于1955年11月正式宣告成立。
    法令指出，鉴于伊拉克继续作为“土—伊互助合作公约”的一方是不符合积极中立政策和7月14日革命的性质的，因此伊拉克废除这个条约。这项法令从1959年3月24日伊拉克退出巴格达条约之日起生效。
