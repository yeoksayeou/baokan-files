Qinggang Oak

Bark is light grayish-brown. Leaves are two to four inches long, deep green on the surface and gray-green on the back. The fruit is spherical, brown and glossy. Dried and crushed leaves can be used to feed pigs. After shelling the fruit, it can be soaked in water, ground, and used as concentrated feed. (Attached picture)

### 青刚栎

1959-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　青刚栎
　　树皮淡灰褐色。叶长二寸至四寸，表面深绿色，背面灰绿色。果实球形，褐色有光泽。叶子晒干打碎可以喂猪，果实去壳后用水浸泡磨碎，可以作精饲料。（附图片）
