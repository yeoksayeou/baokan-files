Title: Beijing Holds Memorial Meeting for L. L. Zamenhof, Founder of Esperanto
Author:
Date: 1959-12-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, December 13th:

    Beijing held a memorial meeting for L. L. Zamenhof, the Polish founder of Esperanto, on the occasion of the 100th anniversary of his birth, which falls on December 15th this year. The All-China Esperanto League and the Sino-Polish Friendship Association held the memorial meeting in Beijing on the 13th. Hu Yuzhi, Chairman of the All-China Esperanto League, presided over the meeting. He Chengxiang, Vice President of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, was also present.

    Ye Laishi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Esperanto League, reported on Zamenhof's life and the struggles he went through to create Esperanto, as well as his participation in the 44th International Esperanto Congress held in Warsaw at the beginning of August this year.

    Polish Chargé d'affaires ad interim to China, Mr. and Mrs. Futor and Embassy Counsellor Dumbnitzki were invited to attend the memorial meeting. Futor spoke at the meeting, expounding on the significance of Zamenhof's creation of Esperanto. Cen Qixiang, professor of the Chinese Department of Peking University, and Ye Junjian, director of the All-China Esperanto League, also spoke at the meeting.

    Also attending the memorial meeting were Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, members of the All-China Esperanto League and the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, and people from various sectors such as culture, education, science, diplomacy, and news, as well as universities and colleges.

### 北京举行世界语创始人柴门霍夫纪念会

1959-12-14
第6版()
专栏：

    北京举行世界语创始人柴门霍夫纪念会
    新华社13日讯　今年12月15日是世界语创始人波兰柴门霍夫诞生一百周年纪念日。中华全国世界语协会和中国波兰友好协会13日在北京举行了纪念会。纪念会由中华全国世界语协会理事长胡愈之主持。中国波兰友好协会副会长何成湘也在座。
    中华全国世界语协会副理事长叶籁士在会上报告了柴门霍夫生平和柴门霍夫创始世界语的奋斗过程，以及他参加今年8月初在华沙举行的第四十四次国际世界语大会的情况。
    波兰驻华大使馆临时代办傅拉托夫妇和大使馆参赞邓布尼茨基应邀参加了纪念会。傅拉托在会上讲话，他阐述了柴门霍夫创始世界语的重要意义。北京大学中文系教授岑麒祥、中华全国世界语协会理事叶君健也在会上讲了话。
    参加纪念会的还有对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊、中华全国世界语协会和中国波兰友好协会会员以及文化、教育、科学、外交、新闻等部门和高等学校等各方面人士。
