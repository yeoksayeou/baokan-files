Title: Irresistible Force (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1959-12-04
Page: 6

Irresistible Force
   On November 27th, during the eighth national unified action of the Japanese people against the revision of the "Security Treaty," 100,000 people from all walks of life in Tokyo held a massive demonstration and rally.

    (Top) Defying the Kishida Nobusuke government's dispatch of police to suppress them, demonstrators in Tokyo bravely surged into the Diet building to submit petitions.

    (Middle) Violence cannot intimidate the Japanese people, who are determined to defend national independence and oppose the military collusion between Japan and the United States. Demonstrators in Tokyo finally broke through the police cordon.

    (Bottom) The Kishida government even dispatched armed police to suppress the masses petitioning the Diet. All sectors of Japanese society have strongly protested against this.

### 不可抗拒的力量（图片）

1959-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

    不可抗拒的力量
   11月27日，在日本人民举行反对修改“安全条约”的第八次全国统一行动中，东京各阶层人民十万人举行声势浩大的示威集会。
    （上）东京示威群众不顾岸信介政府出动警察镇压，奋勇地涌进国会大厦递交请愿书。
    （中）暴力吓不倒坚决保卫民族独立、反对日美军事勾结的日本人民。东京示威者终于冲开了警察的警戒线。
    （下）岸政府竟出动武装警察，镇压向国会请愿的群众。日本各界对此提出强烈抗议。
