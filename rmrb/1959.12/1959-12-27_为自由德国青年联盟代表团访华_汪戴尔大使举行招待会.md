Title: Ambassador Wang Daël Hosts Reception for Free German Youth League Delegation's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1959-12-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 26th—German Democratic Republic Ambassador Wang Daël hosted a reception this evening for the Free German Youth League delegation's visit to China.

Luo Yi, Hu Keshi, and Liang Buting, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, were invited to attend the reception.

Ambassador Wang Daël first delivered a speech at the reception, congratulating the development of friendship between the people and youth of Germany and China. He said that the friendship between the people and youth of Germany and China is based on international proletarian solidarity and is an eternal friendship. He proposed a toast to the friendship between the people and youth of Germany and China, to the achievements of the work of the Chinese Communist Party and its assistant, the Chinese Communist Youth League, and to the health of Chairman Mao, the great leader of the Chinese people and youth.

Luo Yi then delivered a speech, saying that the youth of China and Germany have had close exchanges for a long time, and the Chinese youth have received a lot of help from the youth of the German Democratic Republic.

Hans Modrow, head of the Free German Youth League delegation, also spoke at the reception.

Also invited to attend the reception were heads of various departments of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, workers from Beijing, members of the China-Germany People's Friendship Commune, student representatives, and young artists.

The delegation will leave Beijing tomorrow to return home.

### 为自由德国青年联盟代表团访华  汪戴尔大使举行招待会

1959-12-27
第5版()
专栏：

　　为自由德国青年联盟代表团访华
    汪戴尔大使举行招待会
    据新华社26日电　德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔今天晚间为自由德国青年联盟代表团访华举行招待会。
    共青团中央书记处书记罗毅、胡克实、梁步庭，共青团北京市委第一书记王照华等应邀参加了招待会。
    汪戴尔大使在会上首先致词，祝贺德中两国人民和青年友谊的发展。他说，德中两国人民和青年的友谊是以国际无产阶级团结为基础的，是永恒的友谊。他建议为德中两国人民和青年的友谊，为中国共产党和它的助手——中国共产主义青年团的工作成就、为中国人民和青年的伟大领袖毛主席的健康而干杯。
    罗毅接着致词说，长久以来中德两国青年有着密切的来往，中国青年从德意志民主共和国青年那里取得了很多的帮助。
    自由德国青年联盟代表团团长汉斯·莫德洛夫也在会上讲了话。
    应邀参加招待会的，还有共青团中央各部门负责人，北京市工人，中德人民友好公社社员、学生代表和青年艺术家等。
    代表团将在明天离开北京回国。
