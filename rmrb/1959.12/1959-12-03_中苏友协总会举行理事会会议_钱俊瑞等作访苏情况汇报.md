Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Council Meeting; Qian Junrui and Others Report on Visit to Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-12-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held its Second Meeting of the Third Council in Beijing this morning.

　　Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and head of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association delegation that went to the Soviet Union to participate in the 42nd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, gave a report at the meeting. He pointed out that the friendship between the brotherly Chinese and Soviet peoples is constantly consolidating and developing. He reported on the process of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association signing a cooperation agreement with the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association. Qian Junrui said that the purpose of signing the cooperation agreement is to carry out close cooperation and mutual assistance between China and the Soviet Union on the basis of proletarian internationalism, and to fully promote the development and consolidation of the unbreakable friendship and fraternal cooperation between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union. Qian Junrui also talked about his impressions of visiting the Soviet Union and the delegation's participation in the 42nd anniversary celebration of the October Socialist Revolution. Liu Shuzhou, deputy head of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association delegation, reported on the delegation's visits to Moscow, Leningrad, Tallinn, Kiev, Tashkent and other cities. He said that the delegation was warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in the Soviet Union, and the victory of the Soviet Union in building communism gave the delegation a deep impression and great encouragement.

　　Shen Junru, Li Dequan, Shao Lizi, Li Zhuchen, vice presidents of the Friendship Association, and members of the council attended today's meeting.

### 中苏友协总会举行理事会会议  钱俊瑞等作访苏情况汇报

1959-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会举行理事会会议
    钱俊瑞等作访苏情况汇报
    新华社2日讯　中苏友好协会总会今天上午在北京举行第三届理事会第二次会议。
    友协总会秘书长、赴苏参加十月社会主义革命四十二周年庆祝典礼的中苏友好协会代表团团长钱俊瑞，在会议上作了汇报，他指出：现在兄弟的中苏两国人民之间的友谊正在不断巩固和向前发展。他汇报了中苏友好协会总会同苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会、苏中友好协会签订合作协定的经过。钱俊瑞说，签订合作协定的目的，就是为了中苏两方在无产阶级国际主义的基础上进行密切的合作和互助，全力促进发展和巩固中苏两国人民之间牢不可破的友谊和兄弟般的合作。钱俊瑞还谈述了访苏观感和代表团参加十月社会主义革命四十二周年庆祝典礼的情况。中苏友好协会代表团副团长刘述周汇报了代表团访问莫斯科、列宁格勒、塔林、基辅、塔什干等城市的情况。他说代表团到处受到苏联各界人民的热烈欢迎，苏联建设共产主义的胜利给了代表团深刻的印象和巨大的鼓舞。
    出席今天会议的，有友协总会副会长沈钧儒、李德全、邵力子、李烛尘和理事会的理事。
