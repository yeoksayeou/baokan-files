Title: Hungarian Party Congress Begins Discussion of Kádár and Fock's Reports; Unanimously Supports the Party Central Committee's Policies of the Past Three Years; Khrushchev and Tan Zhenlin Extend Warm Congratulations to the Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-12-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 1st: The Seventh Congress of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party continued at 4:30 PM on November 30th.

	Jeno Fock, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party, delivered a report at the meeting on "Solving Economic Tasks and Formulating the Guidelines for the Second Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy." The report concluded at 8:00 PM.

	The summary of the work of the Party Central Committee and the report on the Party's tasks, delivered by First Secretary Kádár at the opening meeting, concluded at 2:30 PM in the afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 1st: The Seventh Congress of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party continued at 8:30 AM today.

	After Executive Chairman Károly Kiss announced the opening of the meeting, he first invited Nikita Khrushchev, head of the Soviet Communist Party delegation and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU, to speak.

	As Khrushchev walked to the podium, the entire audience stood up and welcomed him with a long and enthusiastic applause. In his speech, Khrushchev extended warm congratulations to the Congress. His speech was repeatedly interrupted by enthusiastic applause. At the end of the speech, the entire audience stood up again and expressed their respect with a long, thunderous applause.

	Then, the meeting began to discuss the reports made by Kádár and Fock.

	Ferenc Münnich, Member of the Political Bureau of the Socialist Workers' Party Central Committee and Prime Minister of the Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, spoke at the meeting on the government's national and economic leadership work.

	Speakers at the morning meeting also included Nemes, secretary of the Party Committee of the Lenin Iron and Steel Works; Németi, First Secretary of the Csongrád County Party Committee; Tatár, Chairman of the Hajdú-Bihar County Council; Szidasi, representative of the Danube River Coal Mine United Enterprise; Dezső Kiss, Second Secretary of the Budapest City Party Committee; and Dalabos, First Secretary of the Zala County Party Committee. In their speeches, they all unanimously expressed their support for the policies implemented by the Party Central Committee since November 1956, and talked about the laboring masses' support for the Party and their high enthusiasm for building socialism.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 1st: The Seventh Congress of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party continued this afternoon.

	After Németh Dezső, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Party Central Committee, and Executive Chairman of the congress, announced the meeting was open, he first invited Tan Zhenlin, head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and secretary of the Secretariat, to address the Congress.

	When Tan Zhenlin walked onto the podium, the entire audience stood up and applauded enthusiastically for several minutes.

	Comrade Tan Zhenlin's speech and the congratulatory message from the CPC Central Committee that he read were greeted with prolonged applause from the entire audience.

### 匈牙利党代表大会开始讨论卡达尔和福克的报告  一致支持党中央三年来的政策  赫鲁晓夫和谭震林向大会表示热烈祝贺

1959-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利党代表大会开始讨论卡达尔和福克的报告
    一致支持党中央三年来的政策
    赫鲁晓夫和谭震林向大会表示热烈祝贺
    新华社布达佩斯1日电　匈牙利社会主义工人党第七次代表大会11月30日下午四时半继续举行。
    党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记福克·耶诺在会上作了“解决经济任务和制订发展国民经济第二个五年计划的方针”的报告。报告在下午八时结束。
    党中央第一书记卡达尔在开幕会上作的党中央委员会的工作总结和党的任务的报告，是在下午二时半结束的。
    新华社布达佩斯1日电　匈牙利社会主义工人党第七次代表大会今天上午八时半继续举行。
    大会执行主席基什·卡罗伊宣布会议开始后，首先请苏联共产党代表团团长、苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫讲话。
    当赫鲁晓夫走向讲台时，全场起立，以长时间的、热烈的掌声欢迎他。赫鲁晓夫在讲话中向代表大会表示了热烈的祝贺。他的讲话不断为热烈的掌声所打断。讲话结束时，全场再次起立，以经久不息的、暴风雨般的掌声向他表示敬意。
    接着，会议开始讨论卡达尔和福克所作的报告。
    社会主义工人党中央政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫在会上就政府的国家和经济领导工作发了言。
    在上午会议上发言的还有列宁钢铁联合企业党委会书记内梅什，琼格拉德州党委会第一书记内迈特，海都比哈尔州议会主席塔塔尔，多瑙河流域煤矿联合企业的代表希达西，布达佩斯市党委会第二书记基什·德热，佐洛州党委会第一书记达拉博什等。他们在发言中都一致表示支持党中央自从1956年11月以来所执行的政策，并且谈到了劳动群众拥护党以及他们热情高涨地建设社会主义的情况。
    新华社布达佩斯1日电　匈牙利社会主义工人党第七次代表大会今天下午继续开会。
    大会执行主席、党中央政治局候补委员内梅什·德热宣布会议开始后，首先请中国共产党代表团团长、中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记谭震林向大会致词。
    当谭震林走上讲台时，全场起立，热烈鼓掌达数分钟之久。
    谭震林同志的致词和他宣读的中共中央的贺电，受到了全场长时间的鼓掌欢迎。
