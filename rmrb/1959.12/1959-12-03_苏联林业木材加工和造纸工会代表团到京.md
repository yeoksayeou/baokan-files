Title: Soviet Forestry, Timber Processing, and Paper Industry Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-12-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: At the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Agricultural and Forestry Trade Union, the four-member Soviet Forestry, Timber Processing, and Paper Industry Trade Union Delegation, led by Novikov, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Forestry, Timber Processing, and Paper Industry Trade Union, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Welcoming them at the airport were He Yingcai, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Agricultural and Forestry Trade Union, and Vice-Chairmen Song Chuan and Fan Jixian, Du Yanqing, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Light Industry Trade Union, and others.

### 苏联林业木材加工和造纸工会代表团到京

1959-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联林业木材加工和造纸工会代表团到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国农林工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的苏联林业木材加工和造纸工会代表团一行四人，在团长、苏联林业木材加工和造纸工会中央委员会主席诺维科夫率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国农林工会全国委员会主席何英才，及副主席宋川、范继先，中国轻工业工会全国委员会主席杜延庆等。
