Title: Japanese Economist Kan'nosuke Otsuka Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-12-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st. At the invitation of Pei Lisheng, Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Japanese economist Professor Kan'nosuke Otsuka and his wife, along with polymer chemist Professor Minoru Imoto and a delegation of five, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country.

Responsible persons from relevant departments of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and scientists were at the airport to welcome them. Japanese peace advocate Mr. Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 日本经济学家大塚金之助等到京

1959-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    日本经济学家大塚金之助等到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国科学院秘书长裴丽生邀请，来我国访问的日本经济学家大塚金之助教授夫妇和高分子化学家井本稔教授等一行五人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国科学院有关部门负责人及科学家。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一先生也到机场欢迎。
