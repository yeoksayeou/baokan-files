Title: US-Li Clique Intensifies Military Expansion
Author:
Date: 1959-12-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 25th: According to a Seoul dispatch from the Korean Central News Agency: Song Yangchuan, the "Army Chief of Staff" of the South Korean Li puppet army, said at a press conference on the 23rd that the Syngman Rhee clique will import surface-to-air missiles from the United States in 1960 to strengthen the South Korean army.

The Dongyang News Agency of South Korea reported from Seoul that Song Yangchuan also announced: South Korea will import new-type tanks, transportation equipment, and various weapons from the United States next year.

### 美李集团加紧扩军

1959-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美李集团加紧扩军
    据新华社平壤25日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社汉城消息：南朝鲜李伪军的“陆军参谋总长”宋扬川23日在一次记者招待会上说，李承晚集团1960年将从美国运入地对空导弹来加强南朝鲜的军队。
    南朝鲜的东洋通讯社从汉城报道，宋扬川还宣布：南朝鲜明年将从美国运入新型坦克、交通设备和各种武器。
