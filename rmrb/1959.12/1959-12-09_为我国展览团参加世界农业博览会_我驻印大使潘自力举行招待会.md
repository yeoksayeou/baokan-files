Title: Ambassador Pan Zili Holds Reception for Chinese Exhibition Delegation Participating in World Agricultural Exposition
Author: 
Date: 1959-12-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 7th – Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili held a reception this evening for Ji Chao Ding, head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation attending the World Agricultural Exposition, and other members of the delegation.

Attending the reception were senior officials from the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, and officials from the World Agricultural Exposition.

Mrs. Uma Nehru, Chairperson of the All-India Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association, Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, Chairperson of the Indian Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, Mr. Sokhey, Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, as well as Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew and Mrs. Ali Asaf were also invited to attend the reception.

Diplomatic envoys from socialist countries and Asian-African countries in Delhi also attended the reception.

A newsreel of the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China was shown at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 7th – Ji Chao Ding, head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation participating in the World Agricultural Exposition held in New Delhi, arrived in New Delhi on the evening of the 6th. Wu Zhen, deputy head and curator of the Chinese Pavilion, and other members of the delegation had arrived earlier.

The opening ceremony of the World Agricultural Exposition will be held on December 11th. The Chinese Pavilion at the exposition is one of the largest exhibition halls. The exhibition hall is nearing completion.

The members of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation include experts in wheat cultivation, rice cultivation, and animal husbandry, as well as model workers and directors of people's communes.

### 为我国展览团参加世界农业博览会  我驻印大使潘自力举行招待会

1959-12-09
第5版()
专栏：

    为我国展览团参加世界农业博览会
    我驻印大使潘自力举行招待会
    新华社新德里7日电　中国驻印度大使潘自力今晚为前来参加世界农业博览会的中国展览团团长冀朝鼎和展览团的其他成员举行了招待会。
  出席招待会的，有印度外交部、工商部的高级官员和世界农业博览会的官员。
    印中友协全国执行委员会主席乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人、印度亚非团结委员会主席拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人、全印和平理事会副主席索克以及赛福丁·克其鲁博士和阿里·阿萨夫夫人也应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有社会主义国家和亚非国家在德里的外交使节。
    招待会上，放映了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝活动的新闻纪录片。
    新华社新德里7日电　参加在新德里举行的世界农业博览会的中国展览团团长冀朝鼎6日晚间到达新德里。副团长兼中国馆馆长吴振和展览团的其他成员已在早些时候到达。
    世界农业博览会的开幕式将在12月11日举行。博览会的中国馆是最大的展览馆之一。展览馆即将落成。
    中国展览团的团员有小麦培植、水稻培植和畜牧方面的专家，还有劳动模范和人民公社的社长。
