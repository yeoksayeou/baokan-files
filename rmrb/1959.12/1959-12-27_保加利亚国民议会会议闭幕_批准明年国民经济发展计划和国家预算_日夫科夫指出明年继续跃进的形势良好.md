Title: Bulgarian National Assembly Session Closes: Approves National Economic Development Plan and State Budget for Next Year; Zhivkov Points Out Favorable Situation for Continued Leap Forward Next Year
Author:
Date: 1959-12-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, December 25th: The Fourth Special Session of the Bulgarian National Assembly closed here this afternoon.

The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Todor Zhivkov, spoke at today's meeting. In his speech, he talked about Bulgaria's achievements in the economic leap forward this year and the favorable domestic and international situation for continued leap forward next year. Zhivkov's speech was warmly welcomed by the deputies.

The meeting lasted for a total of five days. After heated and serious discussions, the meeting approved the National Economic Development Plan and the State Budget for 1960.

The meeting also approved the recommendations proposed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Yugov, regarding some changes in the Bulgarian government agencies.

According to Yugov's recommendation, the current Ministry of Trade will be divided into the Ministry of Foreign Trade and the Ministry of Domestic Trade; a Committee on Technological Progress will be established, and the current Committee on Industry and Technological Progress will be renamed the Committee on Industry; a Committee on Construction and Urban Construction will be established; L. Damyanov will be relieved of his concurrent position as Minister of Trade so that he can concentrate on his work as First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Stanislav Todorov, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, will be appointed Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Chairman of the State Planning Committee; Z. Zhivkov will be relieved of his position as Minister of Education and Culture and appointed Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Georgiev will be relieved of his position as Chairman of the Committee on Construction and Architecture and appointed Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

### 保加利亚国民议会会议闭幕  批准明年国民经济发展计划和国家预算  日夫科夫指出明年继续跃进的形势良好

1959-12-27
第5版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚国民议会会议闭幕
    批准明年国民经济发展计划和国家预算
    日夫科夫指出明年继续跃进的形势良好
    新华社索非亚25日电　保加利亚国民议会第四次特别会议今天下午在这里闭幕。
    保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记托多尔·日夫科夫在今天的会议上讲了话。他在讲话中谈到了保加利亚今年经济跃进的成就和明年继续跃进的良好的国内和国际形势。日夫科夫的讲话受到议员们的热烈欢迎。
    这次会议一共进行了五天。会议经过热烈而认真的讨论后，批准了1960年的国民经济发展计划和国家预算。
    会议还批准了部长会议主席于哥夫提出的关于保加利亚政府机构的一些变动的建议。
    根据于哥夫的建议，现在的贸易部将分成对外贸易部和国内贸易部；成立技术进步问题委员会，现在的工业和技术进步委员会改为工业委员会；成立建筑和城市建设委员会；解除拉·达米扬诺夫兼任的贸易部长职务，以便他集中全力于部长会议第一副主席的工作；任命保共中央政治局候补委员斯·托多罗夫为部长会议副主席兼国家计划委员会主席；解除日·日夫科夫的教育和文化部长职务，任命他为部长会议副主席；解除格奥尔基耶夫的建设和建筑委员会主席的职务，任命他为部长会议副主席。
