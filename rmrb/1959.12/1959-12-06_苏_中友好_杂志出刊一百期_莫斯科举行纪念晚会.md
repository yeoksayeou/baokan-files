Title: "Sino-Soviet Friendship" Magazine Celebrates 100th Issue with Commemorative Soiree in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-12-06
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 4th: The editorial department of the "Sino-Soviet Friendship" magazine held a commemorative soiree tonight at the Moscow Central House of Journalists to celebrate the magazine's 100th issue.

	"Sino-Soviet Friendship" Deputy Editor-in-Chief Potapov, in his speech at the event, introduced the magazine's work over the past two years, its mission, and future plans. He stated that the magazine's readership is growing, and its connection with readers is strengthening. The editorial department strives to use the magazine to explain the achievements of the Soviet Union's communist construction in various aspects to Chinese readers. A primary task of the magazine is to develop and strengthen the great Sino-Soviet friendship.

	Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao attended the soiree and delivered a speech, saying: "The names of China's 'Friendship' weekly and the Soviet Union's 'Sino-Soviet Friendship' weekly vividly illustrate that the solidarity between our two countries is the core of the solidarity of all countries in the socialist camp, a pillar of the world peace movement, and the most powerful weapon to stop war maniacs. Now, peace-loving people all over the world see and understand the immeasurable significance of Sino-Soviet friendship and solidarity, and see that the great unity and friendship between our two countries are being unprecedentedly consolidated and developed in the continuous victory of our common cause and common struggle." Liu Xiao said, "I am very pleased to see that the 'Sino-Soviet Friendship' magazine has used various forms to introduce the advanced experience of Soviet communist construction, disseminate the latest achievements of Soviet culture, science, and technology, and promote the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. It has played a positive role in helping our people understand the great achievements of Soviet construction and learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union. The 'Sino-Soviet Friendship' magazine is widely welcomed by our people. It has made significant contributions to the cause of consolidating and developing the great friendship between China and the Soviet Union. I believe that with the continuous development of the great unity and friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, your magazine must achieve even greater success in the future."

	Representatives from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Soviet State Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others also spoke to congratulate the magazine on its achievements over the past two years. (Attached picture)

	The above picture is the cover of the "Sino-Soviet Friendship" magazine's 48th issue in 1959 (the 100th issue overall).

### “苏  中友好”杂志出刊一百期  莫斯科举行纪念晚会

1959-12-06
第3版()
专栏：

    “苏  中友好”杂志出刊一百期
    莫斯科举行纪念晚会
    新华社莫斯科4日电　“苏中友好”杂志编辑部为纪念杂志出刊一百期，今晚特在莫斯科中央新闻工作者之家举行纪念晚会。
    “苏中友好”副总编辑波塔波夫在会上致词时，介绍了这家杂志两年来的工作情况、杂志的任务和今后的计划。他说，杂志的读者日益增多，和读者联系日益加强了。编辑部力图通过杂志，来向中国读者阐述苏联共产主义建设的各方面的成就，杂志的一个主要的任务就是发展和加强伟大的苏中友谊。
    中国驻苏联大使刘晓出席了晚会，并致词说：中国的“友好”周刊和苏联的“苏中友好”周刊——这两个杂志的名字，很形象地说明了我们两国的团结是社会主义阵营各国团结的核心，是世界和平运动的支柱，是制止战争狂人的最有力的武器。现在全世界爱好和平的人民，都看到和懂得了中苏友好团结的无法估量的意义，看到我们两国伟大的团结和友谊正在我们共同事业和共同斗争的不断胜利中，得到空前的巩固和发展。刘晓说，我很高兴地看到“苏中友好”杂志运用各种形式介绍了苏联共产主义建设的先进经验，传播苏联文化、科学、技术的最新成就，宣传和促进了中苏两国人民的友谊。对于我国人民了解苏联建设的巨大成就和通过它学习苏联的先进经验起了良好的作用。“苏中友好”杂志受到我国人民的普遍欢迎。它在巩固和发展中苏两国伟大友谊的事业中作出了重大的贡献。我相信：随着中苏两国人民伟大团结和友谊的不断发展，你们的杂志今后必须取得更大的成就。
    苏中友好协会、苏联国家对外文化委员会的代表等人也讲话祝贺杂志两年来的成绩。（附图片）
