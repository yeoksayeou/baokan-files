Title: A Soviet Retired Man Walks Thousands of Miles Around Various Places
Author:
Date: 1959-12-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 5th: According to TASS, an old man of sixty, Polikarpov, retired from Omsk city in Siberia, is undertaking a long journey of thousands of miles on foot. On September 16, he set off from his hometown and spent fifty-six days of walking, traveling 1,900 kilometers to the city of Ulyanovsk on the Volga River. On the 4th, he left Lenin's hometown again for Moscow, and the old man is preparing to welcome the New Year in the capital.

At the end of January 1960, Polikarpov will arrive in Leningrad, and then he will travel around the Estonian, Latvian, and Lithuanian republics along the Baltic Sea. Finally, he will return to Omsk via Belarus, Ukraine, and the southern part of the Russian Federation. The plan is to end this long journey on September 16, 1960, lasting exactly one year.

### 苏联一位退休老人徒步万里周游各地

1959-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联一位退休老人徒步万里周游各地
    新华社莫斯科5日电　据塔斯社消息：西伯利亚鄂木斯克城有一位退休的六十岁老人波利卡尔波夫正进行着徒步万里旅行。9月16日他从故乡出发，用了五十六个步行日，走了一千九百公里，抵达了伏尔加河畔的乌里扬诺夫斯克城。4日他又离开了这列宁的故乡往莫斯科行进，老人准备在首都迎接新年。
    1960年1月底，波利卡尔波夫将到达列宁格勒，然后他将周游沿波罗的海的爱沙尼亚、拉脱维亚、立陶宛等共和国，最后经白俄罗斯、乌克兰、俄罗斯联邦南部返回鄂木斯克城。计划在1960年9月16日结束这漫长的旅程，为期正好一年。
