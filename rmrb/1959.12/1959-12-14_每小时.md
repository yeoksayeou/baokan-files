Title: Per Hour...
Author:
Date: 1959-12-14
Page: 8

　　A person can only dig one cubic meter of earth per hour with a shovel. A horse-drawn scraper can dig two cubic meters of earth in the same time. A tractor-drawn scraper can dig 40 cubic meters. A bulldozer can dig 40-50 cubic meters. A multi-bucket excavator can dig 45 cubic meters. A road-building elevating grader can dig 400 cubic meters. A single-bucket excavator that digs four cubic meters of earth at a time can dig 500 cubic meters. A single-bucket excavator that digs fifteen cubic meters of earth at a time can dig 1,000 cubic meters. The Soviet Union's newest "3ФM—3000" soil loosener can dig 3,000 cubic meters of earth per hour. [Compiled by Zhineng]

### 每小时……

1959-12-14
第8版()
专栏：

     每小时……
    人用铁锹每小时只能挖土一立方米，马拉刮土机在同样的时间内可挖土二立方米，拖拉式刮土机是四十立方米，推土机是四十—五十立方米，多挖斗掘土机是四十五立方米，筑路提升机是四百立方米，一次挖土四立方米的单挖斗掘土机是五百立方米，一次挖土十五立方米的单挖斗掘土机是一千立方米，苏联最新式的“3ФM—3000”号松土机每小时可挖土三千立方米。〔治能编译〕
