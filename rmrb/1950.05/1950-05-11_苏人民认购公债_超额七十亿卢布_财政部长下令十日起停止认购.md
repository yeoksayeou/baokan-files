Title: Soviet People Oversubscribe to Public Bonds by 7 Billion Rubles; Minister of Finance Orders Subscription to Cease from the 10th

Author:
Date: 1950-05-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th (TASS dispatch): The Soviet Ministry of Finance recently issued a communiqué stating that the total amount of the fifth post-war Soviet National Economic Restoration and Development State Bonds, issued on May 3rd this year, was originally set at 20 billion rubles. As of the 8th, the total amount subscribed by the people had reached 27 billion 3.608 million rubles, greatly exceeding the original target. According to the instructions of the Soviet Council of Ministers, the Soviet Ministry of Finance has ordered all regions to cease subscribing to public bonds from the 10th. The communiqué was signed by Soviet Minister of Finance Zverev.

### 苏人民认购公债　超额七十亿卢布　财政部长下令十日起停止认购

1950-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏人民认购公债
    超额七十亿卢布
    财政部长下令十日起停止认购
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：苏联财政部顷发表公报称：今年五月三日发行的战后第五次苏联国民经济恢复与发展的国家公债，原定总额为二百亿卢布，到八日为止，人民已认购的总额为二百七十亿零三百六十万八千卢布，已大量超过原定总额。苏联财政部根据苏联部长会议的指示，已下令各地从十日起，停止认购公债。公报由苏联财政部长兹维列夫签署。
