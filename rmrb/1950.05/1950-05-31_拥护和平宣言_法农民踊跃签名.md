Title: Declaration in Support of Peace: French Farmers Actively Sign

Author:
Date: 1950-05-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 28th (TASS dispatch): The movement to collect signatures for the World Peace Council's Peace Declaration is unfolding extensively in the French countryside and attracting more and more rural residents.

According to statistics from the farmers' weekly "La Terre," as of May 24, 90 to 100 percent of farmers in thousands of villages, large and small, across the country had already signed the Stockholm Appeal. All residents of eleven villages, large and small, in the Haute-Vienne department and all residents of twelve villages in the Charente department had signed.

The following departments have seen 90 to 100 percent of their rural residents sign the peace declaration: Saône-et-Loire, Vosges, Isère, Hautes-Pyrénées, Vienne, Creuse, Dordogne, Seine-et-Oise, Var, Gironde, Pas-de-Calais, Gard, Charente, Aude, Lot, Lot-et-Garonne, Aube, Corsica.

To expand the movement to collect signatures for the peace declaration, more peace committees are being established in rural areas.

### 拥护和平宣言  法农民踊跃签名

1950-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　拥护和平宣言
    法农民踊跃签名
    【新华社三十日讯】据塔斯社巴黎二十八日讯：为和大常委会和平宣言征集签名的运动，正在法国农村中广泛展开，并吸引着越来越多的农村居民。
    据农民周刊“大地”统计，截至五月二十四日止，国内数千大小村庄中百分之九十至百分之百的农民，已经在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名。上维也纳省的十一个大小村庄的全体居民和夏朗德省十二个村庄的全体居民都签了名。
    下列各省的百分之九十至百分之百的农村居民都签署了和平宣言：索恩——罗亚尔、瓦斯、伊塞尔、上比利牛斯、维也纳、克勒斯、多马山、塞纳——瓦斯、瓦尔、吉伦特、加来海峡、加尔、夏朗特、奥德、罗特、罗特——加隆、奥伯、科西嘉。
    为了展开征集和平宣言签名运动，乡间正在成立更多的和平委员会。
