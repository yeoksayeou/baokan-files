Title: Soviet Union Decides to Establish State Construction Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 4

　　Soviet Union Decides to Establish
　　State Construction Committee
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th] TASS Moscow, 10th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has recently decided to establish a State Construction Committee under the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联决定设立　国家建设委员会

1950-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联决定设立
　　国家建设委员会
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团顷决定在苏联部长会议之下，设立国家建设委员会。
