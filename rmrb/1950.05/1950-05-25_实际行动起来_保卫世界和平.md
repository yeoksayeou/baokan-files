Title: Take Real Action to Defend World Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-05-25
Page: 4

　　Take Real Action to Defend World Peace

　　The entire group of cadres from the Sixth District of Pingshan County, Hebei Province, wrote on the peace signature book: "Under the leadership of the Communist Party, the people here once drove away the Japanese devils, eliminated the Kuomintang reactionaries, and carried out land reform. Now, we are stepping up production and living a good life. We resolutely oppose imperialism instigating a new war and resolutely demand a ban on the use of atomic weapons. We guarantee: we must work harder to lead the people of the entire district to complete the 1950 plan to increase production. We will use this practical action to oppose war instigators and defend world peace."
　　The Fourth Bureau of the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government held a signature rally to defend world peace. Female comrade Zeng Ouqiu said: "The victory of the cause of peace is assured, but we cannot relax our vigilance because of this."
　　Gong Maoqiu, a first-class agricultural labor hero from Lushun-Dalian, said: "This signature campaign really suits my heart. It is not enough to just say you support peace, you have to show it sincerely. This time, I will not only sign my name, but also promote it in the village."

### 实际行动起来  保卫世界和平

1950-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　实际行动起来  保卫世界和平
    河北省平山县六区全体干部在和平签名书上写道：“我们这里的人民在共产党的领导下，曾经打跑了日本鬼子，消灭了国民党反动派，进行了土地改革；现在，我们正加劲生产，过好光景。我们坚决反对帝国主义挑拨新战争，坚决要求禁止使用原子武器。我们保证：一定要更加努力领导全区人民，完成一九五○年增加生产的计划。我们就用这个实际的行动，来反对战争挑拨者，保卫世界和平。”
    中央人民政府公安部四局曾召开保卫世界和平签名大会，女同志曾藕秋说：“和平事业的胜利是有把握的，但我们不能因此而松弛警惕。”
    旅大一等农业劳动英雄公茂秋说：“这次签名真合乎我心眼，平常只口头上说拥护和平是不行的，要实心实意的表示出来才成。我这次不但要签名，还要在村里宣传。”
