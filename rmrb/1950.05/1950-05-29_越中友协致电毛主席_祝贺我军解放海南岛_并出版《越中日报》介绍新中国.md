Title: Vietnam-China Friendship Association Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao Congratulating Our Army on the Liberation of Hainan Island and Publishes "Vietnam-China Daily" to Introduce New China
Author:
Date: 1950-05-29
Page: 1

　　Vietnam-China Friendship Association Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao
    Congratulating Our Army on the Liberation of Hainan Island
    And Publishes "Vietnam-China Daily" to Introduce New China
    [Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 27th] Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 26th: Ho Tung Mau, President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, recently sent a telegram to Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, congratulating the liberation of Hainan Island. The telegram reads as follows:
    "The Vietnamese people are overjoyed to hear of the complete liberation of Hainan Island. On behalf of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, I warmly celebrate this glorious victory of the Chinese people and ask you to convey our heartfelt congratulations to the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army under your and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's wise leadership."
    [Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 27th] Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 26th: The Vietnam-China Friendship Association has recently published the "Vietnam-China Daily." The purpose of this newspaper is to enable the Vietnamese people to have a deeper understanding of the new China of the people and to enable them to understand the construction work of the new China, in order to promote the increasingly close friendship between the people of China and Vietnam. The newspaper will also regularly introduce the situation of the Soviet Union and Eastern European People's Democratic countries in considerable detail, in order to promote the friendship between the Vietnamese people and the people of other friendly countries.

### 越中友协致电毛主席  祝贺我军解放海南岛  并出版《越中日报》介绍新中国

1950-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　越中友协致电毛主席
    祝贺我军解放海南岛         
    并出版《越中日报》介绍新中国
    【新华社二十七日讯】越南通讯社北越二十六日讯：越中友好协会会长胡松茂顷致电中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东，祝贺海南岛的解放。电文如下：
    “越南人民欣闻海南岛全部解放，不胜欢愉。我谨代表越中友好协会，热烈欢庆中国人民的这一辉煌胜利，并请你向在你及朱德总司令英明领导下的英勇的中国人民解放军转致我们衷心的贺忱。”
    【新华社二十七日讯】越南通讯社北越二十六日讯：越中友好协会顷出版《越中日报》。该报创办目的在于使越南人民对人民的新中国有更深刻的认识，并使他们了解新中国的建设工作，以促进中越两国人民间的日益紧密的友谊。该报并将经常地以相当篇幅介绍苏联及东欧人民民主国家的情况，以便促进越南人民与其他友邦人民的友谊。
