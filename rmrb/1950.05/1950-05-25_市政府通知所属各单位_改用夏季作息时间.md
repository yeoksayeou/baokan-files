The Municipal Government Notifies All Subordinate Units to Adopt Summer Work Hours

[Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing Municipal People's Government, in accordance with the notification from the General Office of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council regarding the revised office hours for Beijing's various agencies, has formulated summer work hours for the municipal government and issued an order for all municipal units to implement them starting May 22nd. The summer work hours stipulate that the morning office hours will be from 8:30 AM to 12:00 PM, and the afternoon office hours will be from 2:30 PM to 6:30 PM. On Sundays and holidays, the past practice will still be followed, with each unit assigning dedicated personnel to be on duty.

### 市政府通知所属各单位  改用夏季作息时间

1950-05-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　市政府通知所属各单位
    改用夏季作息时间
    【京市新闻处讯】京市人民政府遵照中央人民政府政务院秘书厅改订京市各机关办公时间的通知，已制定市府夏季作息时间，通令市属各单位自五月二十二日起遵照实行。夏季作息时间规定上午办公时间自八时半开始至十二时止，下午办公时间自二时半开始至六时半止，星期日及假日仍依过去办法，由各单位指派专人负责值班。
