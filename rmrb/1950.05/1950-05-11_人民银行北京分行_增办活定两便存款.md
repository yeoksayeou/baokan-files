Title: People's Bank of China Beijing Branch Adds Current and Fixed Deposit Convenience

Author:
Date: 1950-05-11
Page: 3

【Beijing News Agency Dispatch】In order to expand deposit business, organize idle social funds into production, and seek the interests and convenience of depositors, the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China will add current and fixed deposit convenience services starting from the 15th of this month. The regulations stipulate that the minimum opening amount is 50,000 yuan, and the maximum term is one month; withdrawals can be made at any time, and interest is calculated based on the length of the deposit term, providing both the convenience of current deposits and the benefits of fixed deposits. All departments of the bank in the city and suburbs can handle this. In addition, in order to facilitate citizens' inquiries about deposit and remittance regulations and procedures, the bank has set up two telephone service stations: the deposit service station is at 3-1175, and the remittance service station is at 3-0210.

### 人民银行北京分行  增办活定两便存款

1950-05-11
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　人民银行北京分行
　  增办活定两便存款
    【京市新闻处讯】中国人民银行北京分行，为了开展存款业务、组织社会闲散资金投向生产，并谋存户利益与存取方便起见，自本月十五日起增办活期定期两便存款。规定开户最低金额为五万元，期限最长为一个月；随时可以提取，按存期长短计息，既有活期的方便又有定期的利益，该行所属市内和郊区各部处均能办理。又：该行为便利市民询问存款、汇款章则手续，特设立电话服务台两座：存款服务台为三局一一七五号、汇款服务台为三局○二一○号。
