Title: Sunday Literary Lecture: Ye Shengtao to Give a Lecture

Author:
Date: 1950-05-11
Page: 3

Sunday Literary Lecture
　  Ye Shengtao to Give a Lecture
    [Our Paper's News] The Sunday Literary Lecture, jointly organized by the Popular Literature Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department, has invited Ye Shengtao, Deputy Director of the Publishing Administration, to give a lecture this week. The topic is "The Only Tool." After the lecture, Ma Xinming and Zhou Yintian will perform the Leting Drum performance "Old Zhang Plants Cotton." The address is at the Youth Palace. The time is Sunday morning at 9:30 AM. Admission is by long-term ticket or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be picked up at the Youth Service Department from Saturday.
                                         (Popular Literature Creation and Research Association)

### 星期文艺讲座  请叶圣陶讲演

1950-05-11
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　星期文艺讲座
　  请叶圣陶讲演
    【本报讯】大众文艺创研会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座，本周聘请出版总署副署长叶圣陶主讲，讲题为“唯一的工具”。会后由马鑫铭、周荫田表演乐亭大鼓“张老汉种棉花”。地址在青年宫。时间是星期日上午九时半，凭长期券或临时券入场，临时券可于星期六至青年服务部领取。
                                         （大众文艺创研会）
