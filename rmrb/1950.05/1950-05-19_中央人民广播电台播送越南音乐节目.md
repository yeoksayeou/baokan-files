Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Vietnamese Music Program
Author:
Date: 1950-05-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th. To celebrate the 60th birthday of President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the 19th, the Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a program of Vietnamese music from 22:00 to 22:30 tomorrow evening. The program will include songs such as "Long Live Ho Chi Minh," "Song of the Vietnamese Liberation Army," "New Life," "Song Commemorating the August Revolution in Vietnam," and "March of the Vietnamese Youth."

### 中央人民广播电台播送越南音乐节目

1950-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台播送越南音乐节目
    【新华社十八日讯】十九日是越南民主共和国胡志明主席六十寿辰，中央人民广播电台为了表示庆贺，在明天晚间二十二点到二十二点三十分播送越南音乐节目。内容包括胡志明万岁、越南解放军歌、新生命、越南八月革命纪念歌及越南青年进行曲等歌曲。
