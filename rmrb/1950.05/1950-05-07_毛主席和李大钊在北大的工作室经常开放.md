Title: Chairman Mao and Li Dazhao's Studios at Peking University are Regularly Open to the Public
Author:
Date: 1950-05-07
Page: 3

　　[Beijing News]
Chairman Mao's studio at Peking University and the memorial hall of the martyr Li Dazhao will be regularly open to the public from now on, from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. daily.

### 毛主席和李大钊在北大的工作室经常开放

1950-05-07
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　毛主席和李大钊在北大的工作室经常开放
    【本报讯】毛主席在北京大学的工作室和先烈李大钊的纪念室，今后经常开放，时间为每日上午九时至下午五时。
