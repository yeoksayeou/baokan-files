Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Calls on Workers Nationwide to Sign Peace Appeal

Author: 
Date: 1950-05-11
Page: 1

　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions Calls on Workers Nationwide to Sign Peace Appeal

    【Xinhua News Agency, 10th】 The All-China Federation of Trade Unions yesterday sent a letter to the General Trade Unions of major administrative regions and the General Trade Unions of provinces and cities, calling on all localities to mobilize the broad masses of workers to enthusiastically respond to the resolution passed at the anniversary of the China Peace Preservation Congress—to sign the World Peace Congress Standing Committee's Third Session (Stockholm Conference) appeal to ban atomic weapons. The letter calls on all localities to use this signature campaign to deepen the workers' understanding of the current world situation and to strengthen internationalist education.

### 全总号召全国工人　在和平呼吁书上签名

1950-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总号召全国工人
    在和平呼吁书上签名
    【新华社十日讯】中华全国总工会于昨日致函各大行政区总工会、各省市总工会，号召各地发动广大工人群众热烈响应中国保卫和平大会周年纪念大会上通过的决议——在世界和平大会常设委员会第三次会议（斯德哥尔摩会议）禁用原子武器呼吁书上签名。该函号召各地通过这次签名运动，以加深工人群众对当前世界形势的认识，以及国际主义的教育。
