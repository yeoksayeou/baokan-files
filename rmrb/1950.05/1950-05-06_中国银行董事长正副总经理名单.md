Title: List of Chairman, General Manager, and Deputy General Managers of the Bank of China
Author:
Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th—The State Council has recently appointed Nan Hanchen as Chairman of the Bank of China, Gong Yinbing as General Manager of the Bank, and Ji Chaoding and Zhan Wu as Deputy General Managers of the Bank.

### 中国银行董事长正副总经理名单

1950-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国银行董事长正副总经理名单
    【新华社五日讯】政务院顷任命南汉宸为中国银行董事长，龚饮冰为该行总经理，冀朝鼎、詹武为该行副总经理。
