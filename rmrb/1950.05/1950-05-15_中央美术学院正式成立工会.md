Title: Central Academy of Fine Arts Formally Establishes Trade Union
Author:
Date: 1950-05-15
Page: 3

　　Beijing News

　　Central Academy of Fine Arts Formally Establishes Trade Union
　　[Report from this newspaper] The trade union of the Central Academy of Fine Arts was established on the 14th. The inaugural meeting began with an opening speech by President Xu Beihong, representative of the presidium. Representatives from the Beijing Education Workers' Trade Union, Yu Mingchuan, and Peking University Trade Union, Qian Duansheng, then spoke. Shen Baoji of the academy reported on the preparations for the trade union. Finally, elections were held. More than 200 members elected 19 executive committee members. The meeting concluded successfully in a solemn and joyful atmosphere.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Academy of Fine Arts Correspondent Group)

### 中央美术学院正式成立工会

1950-05-15
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　中央美术学院正式成立工会
    【本报讯】中央美术学院工会于十四日成立。成立大会由主席团代表徐悲鸿院长致开幕词。北京市教育工作者工会代表俞铭传、北京大学工会代表钱端升等相继讲话，该院沈宝基报告工会筹备经过。最后进行选举。全体会员二百余数十人，选出执委十九名。大会在严肃而愉快的气氛中胜利结束。
                                               （美院通讯组）
