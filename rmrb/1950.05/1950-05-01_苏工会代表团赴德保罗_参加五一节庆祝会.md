Title: Soviet Trade Union Delegation Goes to Germany to Participate in May Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1950-05-01
Page: 4

　　Soviet Trade Union Delegation Goes to Germany
    to Participate in May Day Celebrations

    [Xinhua News Agency, 29th] A compilation of TASS dispatches: The Soviet Trade Union Delegation departed on the 28th for the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, and Romania to participate in May Day celebrations.

### 苏工会代表团赴德保罗  参加五一节庆祝会

1950-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏工会代表团赴德保罗
    参加五一节庆祝会
    【新华社二十九日讯】综合塔斯社消息：苏联工会代表团已于二十八日分别首途，前赴德意志民主共和国、保加利亚和罗马尼亚参加五一节庆祝会。
