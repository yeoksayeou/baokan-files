Title: Representatives of Tibetan People in Xikang, Wangjia and Others, Send Letter to the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Respectfully Wishing Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Good Health
Author:
Date: 1950-05-17
Page: 1

　　Representatives of Tibetan People in Xikang, Wangjia and Others
　　Send Letter to the Nationalities Affairs Commission
　　Respectfully Wishing Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Good Health
    【Xinhua News Agency, 16th】When the Xikang Tibetan representatives Wangjia, Bozhi, and Cailiang were on their way back to Xikang via Chongqing, they sent a letter to the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, respectfully wishing good health to the people's leaders Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and thanking them for their warm hospitality during their stay in Beijing. The letter also stated: "After we return to Xikang, we must use various methods to thoroughly convey to all Tibetan people the friendly and mutually supportive situation of all ethnic groups in the country united in the national family of the People's Republic of China, which we personally tasted, saw, and heard during this trip."

### 西康藏民代表旺嘉等　致函民族事务委员会　敬祝毛主席朱总司令健康

1950-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　西康藏民代表旺嘉等
　　致函民族事务委员会         
　　敬祝毛主席朱总司令健康
    【新华社十六日讯】西康藏民代表旺嘉、伯志、蔡良等三人在返康路经重庆时，曾致函中央人民政府民族事务委员会敬祝人民领袖毛主席、朱总司令的健康，并感谢在京期中对他们的热情款待。信中并称：“我们回到西康后，一定要把这次亲自尝到的、见到的和听到的国内各民族团结在中华人民共和国的民族大家庭中，友爱互助的情形，通过各种方式，详细的传达给所有的藏族人民。”
