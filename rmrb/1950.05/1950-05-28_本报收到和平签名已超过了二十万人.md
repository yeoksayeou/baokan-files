Title: This newspaper has received over 200,000 peace signatures
Author:
Date: 1950-05-28
Page: 4

　　[News from this newspaper] Yesterday, this newspaper received peace signatures from 25,529 people from this city and other regions. Together with the previous twelve days, the total is 200,000,1852 people. Guards platoon soldiers of a certain detachment of the Third Field Army's South China Sea Troops wrote from the Zhejiang East Front, expressing their will to defend world peace. Many readers wrote letters expressing their willingness to take advantage of the "June 1st" International Children's Day celebration to mobilize more parents who care about themselves and other people's children to participate in the signature campaign.

### 本报收到和平签名已超过了二十万人

1950-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　本报收到和平签名已超过了二十万人
    【本报讯】本报昨天收到本市及各地人民二万五千五百二十九人的和平签名，连前十二天共计二十万零一千八百五十二人。三野南海部队某支队警卫班战士自浙东前线写来他们的签名书，表示他们保卫世界和平的意志。许多读者来信表示愿借“六一”国际儿童节庆祝会的机会，发动更多关心自己和别人的孩子的父母参加签名。
