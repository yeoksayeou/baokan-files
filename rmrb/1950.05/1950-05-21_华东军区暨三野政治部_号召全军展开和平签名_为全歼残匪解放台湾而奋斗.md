Title: East China Military Region and Third Field Army Political Department Call on the Entire Army to Launch Peace Signatures, Striving to Annihilate Remaining Bandits and Liberate Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1950-05-21
Page: 4

　　East China Military Region and Third Field Army Political Department
    Call on the Entire Army to Launch Peace Signatures
    Striving to Annihilate Remaining Bandits and Liberate Taiwan
    [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 20th] The East China Military Region of the People's Liberation Army and the Political Department of the Third Field Army, in response to the call of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, have issued instructions calling on all officers and soldiers of the army to launch a peace signature campaign, and to conduct in-depth education throughout the army, to encourage all officers and soldiers to thoroughly eliminate the Kuomintang remnant bandits supported by American imperialism, to complete the liberation of Taiwan and Jinmen, and to strive to defend world peace.
    After the instructions were issued, they were warmly received by the officers and soldiers of various units. At a hero and model conference held by a certain army, all eighty-four people's heroes and work models at all levels signed their names first. The peace signature campaign in other units is also being carried out enthusiastically.

### 华东军区暨三野政治部  号召全军展开和平签名  为全歼残匪解放台湾而奋斗

1950-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　华东军区暨三野政治部
    号召全军展开和平签名     
    为全歼残匪解放台湾而奋斗
    【新华社南京二十日电】人民解放军华东军区和第三野战军政治部，为响应世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的号召，特发出指示，号召全军指战员展开和平签名运动，并在全军进行深入教育，鼓舞全体指战员为彻底消灭美帝国主义支持下的国民党残余匪帮，完成解放台湾、金门，保卫世界和平而奋斗。
    指示发出后，已获得各部指战员的热烈响应。某军召开的英雄模范大会上，全体八十四位各级人民英雄和工作模范首先签了名。其他各部的和平签名运动已在热烈开展中。
