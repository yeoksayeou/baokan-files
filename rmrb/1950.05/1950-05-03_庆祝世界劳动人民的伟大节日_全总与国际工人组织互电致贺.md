Title: Celebrating the Great Festival of the World's Working People: ACFTU and International Workers' Organizations Exchange Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1950-05-03
Page: 3

World Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegram

[Xinhua News Agency, 2nd] Louis Saillant, Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, telegraphed Liu Shaoqi, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, to congratulate him on the first "May Day" Labor Day after the founding of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Liu Shaoqi of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

On the occasion of May Day, I would like to extend my brotherly friendship to you and all members of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

You are celebrating the first May Day Labor Day of the new People's Republic of China, and we sincerely share your joy and hope.

Regarding the construction of the new China, we would like to express our great confidence to the Chinese people and their leader, Comrade Mao Zedong, and congratulate you on your complete victory.

Louis Saillant
April 29, 1950, Paris

Reply to the World Federation of Trade Unions

[Xinhua News Agency, 2nd] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram of respect to the World Federation of Trade Unions on "May Day" Labor Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Saillant, Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions:

We firmly support the call issued by the World Federation of Trade Unions in the "May Day" declaration: "Turn the 'May Day' of 1950 into a worldwide festival in which the working people of all countries actively express their desire to establish a solid and lasting peace in the world." In order to respond to the call of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, we have decided to launch a peace signature campaign on "May Day" and commemorate the first "May Day" International Labor Day of the People's Republic of China with this practical action. We salute the World Federation of Trade Unions, which leads us in the struggle to defend world lasting peace and the liberation of the working class.

All-China Federation of Trade Unions
May 1st

Congratulating the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions

[Xinhua News Agency, 2nd] Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, telegraphed Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions, to congratulate him on "May Day" Labor Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions:

As we rejoice and celebrate the first "May Day" International Labor Day after the founding of the People's Republic of China, we would like to extend our lofty and warm respect and congratulations to the Soviet Trade Unions and all the Soviet working class brothers on behalf of the Chinese working class!

We thank the Soviet working class brothers for their constant care and assistance to us in the course of the Chinese revolution, and thank the trade union workers' delegation headed by Comrade Kolbanov for their assistance. The Chinese working class will always follow the Soviet working class and unite closely under the great banner of Stalin to fight to the end for world lasting peace and the cause of people's democracy.

All-China Federation of Trade Unions
Vice Chairman Li Lisan, May 1st

Reply Telegram from the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions

[Xinhua News Agency, 2nd] Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions, telegraphed the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to congratulate them on "May Day" Labor Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

On the occasion of May Day, the festival of unity of the working people of the world, the festival of unity of workers and brothers of all countries, the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions extends its congratulations to the working people of the People's Republic of China.

The Soviet working people are extremely concerned about and sympathetic to all the achievements of the Chinese people. They have won a historically significant victory in the struggle against imperialism and its Kuomintang lackeys.

We sincerely hope that the workers of democratic China will achieve all victories in the struggle to build socialism, strengthen national unity, and defend world peace.

Long live the firm, fraternal friendship between the workers of China and the Soviet Union!

Chairman of the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions
V. Kuznetsov
April 29, 1950, Moscow

### 庆祝世界劳动人民的伟大节日  全总与国际工人组织互电致贺

1950-05-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　庆祝世界劳动人民的伟大节日
    全总与国际工人组织互电致贺
    世界工联来电
    【新华社二日讯】世界工会联合会书记路易·赛扬电中华全国总工会名誉主席刘少奇，祝贺中华人民共和国成立后的第一个“五一”劳动节。电文如下：中华全国总工会刘少奇同志：
    欣逢五一劳动节之际，我谨向您和中华全国总工会全体会员致以兄弟之忱。
    您们是度着新的中华人民共和国的第一个五一劳动节，我们热诚地分享您们的愉快和希望。
    对新中国的建设，我们谨向中国人民及其领导者——毛泽东同志，表示我们的巨大信心，并祝贺您们的完全胜利。
    路易·赛扬
    一九五○年四月二十九日  巴黎
     电复世界工联
    【新华社二日讯】中华全国总工会于“五一”劳动节致电世界工会联合会致敬。电文如下：世界工联书记赛扬同志：
    我们坚决拥护世界工联在“五一”宣言中所发出的号召：“把一九五○年的‘五一’节变成各国劳动人民积极表示要在全世界建立巩固而持久的和平的世界性的节日”。为了响应世界拥护和平大会常委会的号召，我们已决定在“五一”节展开和平签名运动，并以这种实际行动来纪念中华人民共和国的第一个“五一”国际劳动节。谨向领导我们为保卫世界持久和平与工人阶级解放的世界工联致敬。
     中华全国总工会
     五月一日
     电贺全苏工会
    【新华社二日讯】中华全国总工会副主席李立三致电全苏工会中央理事会主席库兹涅佐夫，祝贺“五一”劳动节。电文如下：全苏工会中央理事会主席库兹涅佐夫同志：
    当我们欢欣鼓舞庆祝中华人民共和国成立后第一个“五一”国际劳动节的时候，我们谨代表中国工人阶级向苏联职工会及全苏联工人阶级兄弟致崇高的热烈的敬意与祝贺！
    我们感谢苏联工人阶级弟兄，在中国革命过程中对于我们的经常关怀与帮助，并感谢苏联职工会所派遣的以科勒班诺夫同志为首的工会工作者代表团给予我们的帮助。中国工人阶级将永远跟随苏联工人阶级一起，紧密地团结在伟大的斯大林旗帜下为世界持久和平与人民民主事业奋斗到底。
     中华全国总工会
     副主席李立三  五月一日
     全苏工会复电
    【新华社二日讯】全苏工会中央理事会主席库兹涅佐夫致电中华全国总工会祝贺“五一”劳动节。电文如下：中华全国总工会：
    欣逢五一劳动节——全世界劳动人民团结的节日，一切国家工人兄弟团结的节日——之际，全苏工会中央理事会谨向中华人民共和国的劳动人民致祝贺之忱。
    苏联劳动人民极其关心并同情中国人民的一切成就。他们在反对帝国主义及其国民党走狗的斗争中，已经赢得了具有历史意义的胜利。
    我们热诚希望民主中国的工人在为建立社会主义，加强全国统一以及在保卫世界和平等斗争中的一切胜利。
    中苏两国工人坚固的兄弟般的友谊万岁！
    全苏工会中央理事会主席
     Ｖ·库兹涅佐夫
     一九五○年四月二十九日莫斯科
