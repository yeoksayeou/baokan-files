Title: JCP 19th Central Committee Plenum Decision: The Whole Party Unites to Strike Against Reactionaries
Author:
Date: 1950-05-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, dispatch of the 3rd: According to a Tokyo dispatch, the 19th Central Committee Plenary Session of the Japanese Communist Party was held in Tokyo from April 28th to 30th. JCP spokesman Ritsu Ito issued a statement on May 1st saying: The 19th Central Committee Plenary Session of our party has decided that the entire party will unite as one and resolutely counter the conspiracies of domestic and foreign reactionaries who are taking various measures to undermine our party. Ito Ritsu said: The Central Committee also decided to vigorously oppose the colonization of Japan and the transformation of Japan into a military base, and to carry out the struggle for national independence and world peace.

### 日共第十九次中央全会决定  全党团结打击反动派

1950-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　日共第十九次中央全会决定
    全党团结打击反动派
    【新华社三日讯】据东京电讯称，日本共产党第十九次中央委员会全体会议，自四月二十八日至三十日在东京举行。日本共产党发言人伊藤律于五月一日发表声明说：我们党的第十九次中央委员会全体会议决定全党团结一致，对国内外反动派正在采取的用各种手段破坏我们党的阴谋，予以坚决的反击。伊藤律说：中央委员会同时决定要强有力地反对使日本殖民地化及将日本变为军事基地，并进行争取民族独立和世界和平的斗争。
