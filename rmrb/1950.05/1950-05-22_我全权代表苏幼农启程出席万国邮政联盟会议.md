Title: I, Fully Representing Su Younong, Depart to Attend the Universal Postal Union Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-05-22
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, May 21st: The Executive and Liaison Committee of the Universal Postal Union convened in Montreux, Switzerland, on May 15th. Su Younong, the plenipotentiary representative appointed by the State Council of the Central People's Government to attend the committee, and his accompanying staff member, Xu Chuanxian, departed for the meeting on the 21st. Advisor Ge Baoquan will join Su and Xu when they pass through Moscow.
    It is reported that the Secretary-General of the Universal Postal Union, Hess, telegraphed Minister Zhou of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our Central People's Government on the 16th of this month, stating: "The Executive and Liaison Committee of the Universal Postal Union has decided to recognize the representative of the People's Republic of China as the sole qualified representative of China to attend the meeting currently being held in Montreux." He also requested that we telegraph the date of arrival of Director Su Younong, which our Ministry of Foreign Affairs replied to on the 20th.

### 我全权代表苏幼农启程出席万国邮政联盟会议

1950-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　我全权代表苏幼农启程出席万国邮政联盟会议
    【新华社二十一日讯】万国邮政联盟执行及联络委员会于五月十五日在瑞士蒙特罗开会。我中央人民政府政务院任命出席该委员会之全权代表苏幼农及随员徐传贤二人已于二十一日启程赴会。顾问戈宝权将于苏、徐二人经莫斯科时随同前往。
    按万国邮政联盟秘书长赫斯，曾于本月十六日致电我中央人民政府外交部周部长称：“万国邮政联盟执行及联络委员会业已决定承认中华人民共和国之代表为中国出席现在蒙特罗举行之会议的唯一合格代表。”并请我电告苏幼农局长到达之日期，我外交部已于二十日电复。
