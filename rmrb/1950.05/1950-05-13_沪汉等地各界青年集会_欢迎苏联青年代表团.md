Title: Shanghai, Wuhan and Other Places Youth Gatherings Welcome Soviet Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1950-05-13
Page: 1

　　Shanghai, Wuhan and Other Places Youth Gatherings
　　Welcome Soviet Youth Delegation
    [Reported by this newspaper] More than 10,000 youths from all walks of life in Shanghai held a grand welcoming meeting on the afternoon of the 10th to welcome the Soviet Youth Delegation. Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, Vice Chairman Su Yu, Shanghai Mayor Chen Yi, heads of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, heads of various parties and organizations, and representatives of Soviet expatriates were all invited to participate. Li Chang, Secretary of the East China Working Committee of the Youth League, delivered a welcome speech. He extended warm revolutionary greetings on behalf of the 700,000 Youth League members and the vast number of youths in East China to the distinguished guests from afar and to Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, who accompanied the Soviet Youth Delegation to Shanghai. Feng Wenbin said in his speech: The path taken by the Soviet youth in the past is our example. Mikhailov, head of the Soviet Youth Delegation, delivered a reply. He said: Soviet youth have always been concerned about the Chinese people and Chinese youth. They are always faithful to their internationalist obligations. When he said that the people under the leadership of the Communist Party can overcome all difficulties, thunderous applause erupted throughout the venue. After the meeting, the Soviet Youth Art Troupe performed singing, dancing, drumming, soft gymnastics and other programs. In the evening, Shanghai youths from all walks of life held a literary and artistic welcome party to entertain the delegation.
    [Another report] 30,000 youths from all walks of life in Wuhan held a conference on the afternoon of the 10th to welcome the ten members of the Soviet Youth Delegation led by Pesliak, a member of the Central Committee of the Soviet Youth League. At the meeting, General Tao Zhu, Director of the Political Department of the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, presented commemorative medals to the Soviet Youth Delegation on behalf of the field army. The Central China Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League and the Wuhan Municipal Working Committee of the Youth League presented exquisite porcelain statues of Marx, Engels, Lenin, Stalin, and Chairman Mao, and a large red flag embroidered with "Long Live the Solidarity of Chinese and Soviet Youth" as gifts. Youths from all walks of life also presented souvenirs.
    [Another report] Soviet Youth Delegation member Peter Masherov and his party of four arrived in Changsha on the afternoon of the 10th. Five thousand youths and representatives from all walks of life in Changsha went to the station to welcome them.
    Masherov and the other three, in Changsha, introduced the achievements and experiences of Soviet workers, youth and students in completing the post-war five-year plan at gatherings of workers, staff, and students in universities and middle schools, and visited the Chengnan Academy and the Antimony Factory, where Chairman Mao studied in his early years in Changsha. They took a train to Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 11th.

### 沪汉等地各界青年集会　欢迎苏联青年代表团

1950-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪汉等地各界青年集会
　　欢迎苏联青年代表团
    【本报讯】上海市各界青年一万余人于十日下午举行盛大欢迎会，欢迎苏联青年代表团。华东军政委员会主席饶漱石、副主席粟裕、上海市长陈毅、人民解放军驻沪部队、各党派团体负责人和苏侨代表等都应邀参加。青年团华东工作委员会书记李昌致欢迎词，他代表华东七十万青年团员和广大青年向远道而来的嘉宾们及陪同苏联青年代表团来沪的青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬致热烈的革命敬礼。冯文彬致词说：苏联青年过去所走的道路，就是我们的榜样。苏联青年代表团团长米哈依洛夫致答词。他说：苏联青年始终关怀着中国人民和中国青年。他们无论什么时候都忠实于国际主义的义务的。当他说到共产党领导下的人民对一切困难都能克服的时候，全场掀起雷动的掌声。会后，苏联青年文艺工作团表演歌唱、舞蹈、击鼓、柔软体操等节目。晚间，上海各界青年举行文艺欢迎晚会，招待代表团。
    【又讯】武汉市各界青年三万人于十日下午举行大会，欢迎以苏联青年团中央委员裴斯良克为领队的苏联青年代表团的十位团员。会上中国人民解放军第四野战军政治部主任陶铸将军代表该野战军向苏联青年代表团赠送纪念章。新民主主义青年团中南区工作委员会和青年团武汉市工作委员会分别以马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林和毛主席的精致瓷像和绣着“中苏青年团结万岁”的大红旗作为赠礼。各界青年亦纷纷赠送纪念品。
    【又讯】苏联青年代表团团员彼得·马舍洛夫一行四人，于十日下午到达长沙。长沙青年及各界代表五千人前往车站欢迎。
    马舍洛夫等四人，在长沙市分别在职工、大中学生的集会上，介绍了苏联职工、青年学生在完成战后五年计划中的成就和经验，并参观了毛主席早年在长沙读书的城南书院和锑品厂。十一日下午由长沙乘车赴广州。
