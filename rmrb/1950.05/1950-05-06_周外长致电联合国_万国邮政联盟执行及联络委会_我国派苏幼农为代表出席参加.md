Title: Zhou Enlai Sends Telegram to the United Nations: China Appoints Su Younong as Representative to Attend the Executive and Liaison Committee of the Universal Postal Union

Author:
Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 1

　　Zhou Enlai Sends Telegram to the United Nations
    China Appoints Su Younong as Representative to Attend the Executive and Liaison Committee of the Universal Postal Union
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 5th] Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, sent telegrams on May 5th, with the same content, to both United Nations Secretary-General Trygve Lie and Dr. Hess, Secretary-General of the Executive and Liaison Committee of the Universal Postal Union, notifying them that China was appointing Su Younong, Director-General of the Postal Administration, as its representative to participate in the Executive and Liaison Committee of the Universal Postal Union and to attend the Committee's meeting to be held in Montreux.
    The text of the telegram to Lie is below: To Mr. Trygve Lie, Secretary-General of the United Nations at Lake Success, New York:
    The Central People's Government of the People's of Republic of China is the sole legal government representing the Chinese people. The so-called "representatives" of the remnant clique of the Chinese Kuomintang reactionaries are no longer qualified to participate in the Universal Postal Union. On behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, in addition to having notified Dr. Hess, Secretary-General of the Executive and Liaison Committee of the Universal Postal Union, I hereby formally notify you that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has appointed Su Younong, Director-General of the Postal Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, as the representative to participate in the Executive and Liaison Committee of the Universal Postal Union and to attend the Committee's meeting to be held in Montreux, Switzerland, starting on May 15th of this year. Please take note and reply by telegram, and please notify the Universal Postal Union to arrange for my representative's visa to enter Switzerland.
    Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
    May 5, 1950

### 周外长致电联合国  万国邮政联盟执行及联络委会  我国派苏幼农为代表出席参加

1950-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　周外长致电联合国
    万国邮政联盟执行及联络委会  我国派苏幼农为代表出席参加
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长于五月五日就我国派邮政总局局长苏幼农为代表，参加万国邮政联盟执行及联络委员会，并出席该委员会将在蒙特罗举行的会议事，以同样内容分别致电通知联合国秘书长赖伊及万国邮政联盟执行及联络委员会秘书长赫斯博士。
    致赖伊电文如下：纽约成功湖联合国秘书长赖伊先生：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府是代表中国人民的唯一合法政府，中国国民党反动派残余集团的所谓“代表”，现在已没有参加万国邮政联盟的资格。我代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府，除已通知万国邮政联盟执行及联络委员会秘书长赫斯博士外，特正式通知阁下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府业已任命我邮电部邮政总局局长苏幼农为参加万国邮政联盟执行及联络委员会的代表，并出席该委员会本年五月十五日起在瑞士蒙特罗举行的会议，请予查照电复，并通知万国邮政联盟为我代表办理进入瑞士之签证为荷。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府
    外交部部长周恩来
    一九五○年五月五日
