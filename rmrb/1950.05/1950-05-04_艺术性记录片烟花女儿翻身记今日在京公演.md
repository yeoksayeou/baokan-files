Title: Artistic Documentary "The Turnaround of the Prostitutes" Premieres Today in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-05-04
Page: 3

　　Beijing News

　　Artistic Documentary "The Turnaround of the Prostitutes" Premieres Today in Beijing
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The Beijing Municipal People's Government closed all brothels in the city and punished the owners according to the law. More than a thousand liberated women, after undergoing transformation, have participated in productive labor. This event has been filmed by the Beijing Film Studio of the Central Film Bureau into an artistic documentary, "The Turnaround of the Prostitutes." This film will be screened in Beijing starting today. The documentary "Asian Women's Representative Conference," filled with the spirit of international women's solidarity and struggle, will be shown before the main feature.

### 艺术性记录片烟花女儿翻身记今日在京公演

1950-05-04
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　艺术性记录片烟花女儿翻身记今日在京公演
    【本报讯】北京市人民政府封闭全市妓院并依法处罚了领家老板；一千多被解放的妇女经过改造，参加了生产劳动。这一件事已由中央电影局北京电影制片厂摄制成为一部艺术性的纪录片《烟花女儿翻身记》。这部影片将于今日起在北京献映。片前加演《亚洲妇女代表会议》记录片，片中充满了国际妇女团结斗争的精神。
