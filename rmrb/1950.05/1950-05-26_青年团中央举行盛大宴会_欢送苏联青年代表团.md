Title: Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Holds Grand Banquet to See Off Soviet Youth Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1950-05-26
Page: 1

　　Youth League Central Committee Holds Grand Banquet
    To See Off Soviet Youth Delegation
    [This News] According to Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League held a grand banquet on the evening of May 25th to see off the Soviet youth delegation, which had come to participate in China's Youth Day, as they returned home. Those present included Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, Deputy Secretaries Liao Chengzhi and Jiang Nanxiang, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Wu Yuzhang, President of Renmin University of China, Tang Yongtong and Qian Duansheng of Peking University, Ye Qisun and Zhang Xiruo of Tsinghua University, Zhou Yang of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Shuai Mengqi and Kang Keqing of the All-China Women's Federation, Qian Sanqiang and Wu Han of the All-China Youth Federation, as well as combat heroes, labor heroes, student representatives, and youth league work cadres from Beijing, totaling more than 300 people. Soviet Ambassador Roschin and his wife were also invited to attend. At the beginning of the banquet, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, delivered a farewell speech (full text to be published separately), followed by a reply speech by Mikhailov, head of the Soviet youth delegation (full text to be published separately). The meeting was filled with an atmosphere of friendship between Chinese and Soviet youth. Guests and hosts frequently toasted Marshal Stalin, Chairman Mao Zedong, and the unity of the Chinese and Soviet peoples and youth.

### 青年团中央举行盛大宴会  欢送苏联青年代表团

1950-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央举行盛大宴会
    欢送苏联青年代表团
    【本报讯】据新华社二十五日讯：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会二十五日晚举行盛大宴会欢送前来参加中国青年节的苏联青年代表团回国。到会者有中国新民主主义青年团中央书记冯文彬、副书记廖承志、蒋南翔，中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民大学校长吴玉章，北京大学汤用彤、钱端升，清华大学叶企荪、张奚若，全国文联周扬，全国妇联帅孟奇、康克清，全国青联钱三强、吴晗，以及北京的战斗英雄、劳动英雄、学生代表和青年团工作干部等三百余人。苏联大使罗申夫妇亦应邀与会。宴会开始时，由新民主主义青年团中央书记冯文彬致欢送词（全文另发），继由苏联青年代表团团长米哈依洛夫致答词（全文另发）。会上，充满着中苏青年的友谊气氛，宾主频频为斯大林大元帅、毛泽东主席、中苏两国人民及青年的团结而干杯。
