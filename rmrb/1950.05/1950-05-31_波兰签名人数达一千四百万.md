Title: 14 Million Signatures Collected in Poland
Author:
Date: 1950-05-31
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th. According to reports from the Polish News Agency: Poland is universally carrying out the peace signature campaign in various ways. As of the 26th, 14 million people across Poland had signed the peace declaration. In factories, schools and villages in the Silesia province, more than 1,300 meetings were held. More than 150,000 young people expressed their determination to fight for peace at the meetings. Rawa and Mazowiecka organized 387 "three-person teams" to promote and collect peace signatures among farmers.

### 波兰签名人数达一千四百万

1950-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰签名人数达一千四百万
    【新华社三十日讯】综合波兰通讯社消息：波兰正以各种方式普遍开展和平签名运动。到二十六日为止，波兰全国已有一千四百万人在和平宣言上签了名。在西里西亚省的工厂、学校和村落中，举行了一千三百多次集会。十五万以上的青年在会上表示其争取和平的决心。拉瓦及马佐维也斯卡两地组织了三百八十七个“三人队”，到农民中间宣传和征集和平签名。
