Title: Reports from the Peace Front

Author: 
Date: 1950-05-16
Page: 4

　　Reports from the Peace Front
　　*L'Unità*, a Roman newspaper, reported that the workers of the Ballem Shipyard have passed a resolution, firmly rejecting all construction work for war purposes, and declaring their will to work only for the interests of peace.
　　At a mass rally of working people in Amsterdam to strive for peace and oppose warmongers, dockworkers from Amsterdam and Rotterdam resolutely swore not to unload war materials. Berta de Valis, the wife of a port worker in Amsterdam, addressed the meeting and said: "We have seen the horrors of the last war, so we fervently need peace. We must save our children and our future."
　　In Wushan Village, Incheon District, Korea, all adult residents have signed the peace appeal. Guo Haifang, a farmer who was the first to sign, said: "We must win peace. To achieve victory in our struggle for peace, we must continue to strengthen the democratic camp. The signature campaign is an important contribution to the struggle for peace."
　　At the Second Canadian National Peace Congress held in Toronto, when youth representatives from Montreal presented the "Peace Torch" relayed from Montreal to the chairman of the Congress, Wen Youzhang, it was met with enthusiastic cheers from all the representatives. Wen Youzhang pointed out that this "Peace Torch" was delivered despite the obstruction of the government authorities, so it is not only a symbol of peace, but also a symbol of the will to struggle for peace.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Compiled by this newspaper)

### 来自和平战线的报道

1950-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　来自和平战线的报道
    罗马团结报报道：巴莱姆船坞工人已通过决议，坚决拒绝一切为战争目的的建造工作，宣布他们只为和平利益而工作的意志。
    在阿姆斯特丹劳动人民争取和平反对战争贩子的群众大会中，阿姆斯特丹与鹿特丹码头工人坚决宣誓不起卸战争物资。一位阿姆斯特丹港口工人之妻贝塔·德·瓦丽丝在会上致词称：“我们已看到上次战争的可怖，因此我们热烈需要和平。我们必须拯救我们的孩子与我们的将来。”
    朝鲜仁川区的五山村（译音），全部成年居民都在和平呼吁书上签了名。最先签名的一个农民郭海方（译音）说：“我们必须赢得和平，要取得我们争取和平斗争的胜利，必须继续加强民主阵营，签名运动是对和平斗争一个重要的贡献。”
    在多伦多举行的加拿大第二次全国拥护和平大会中，当蒙特利的青年代表向大会主席文幼章呈献自蒙特利接力传递来的“和平火炬”时，获得全体代表热烈的欢呼，文幼章指出：这“和平火炬”是在政府当局的阻难下传递到来的，因此它不仅是和平的标志，而且成了和平斗争意志的标志。
                                                   （本报辑）
