Title: Children's Reading Garden Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1950-05-08
Page: 3

Beijing News

	Children's Reading Garden Opens Today
	[This newspaper dispatch] The Children's Reading Garden, jointly organized by the Beijing Library and the Youth Service Department, will open at 9:00 am today (the 8th). The address is inside the Beijing Library at 1 Wenjin Street. The daily opening hours are from 9:00 am to 12:00 noon and from 1:30 pm to 5:30 pm. There are currently 2,000 copies of various new books and magazines such as fairy tales, new comic strips, novels, Children of China, Children of Beijing, Children, Chinese Youth and Juvenile Natural Science Series. Young children are welcome to enter the garden to read.

### 少年儿童阅览园今日开放

1950-05-08
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　少年儿童阅览园今日开放
    【本报讯】北京图书馆，青年服务部合办的少年儿童阅览园，订于今（八）日上午九时开放，地址在文津街一号北京图书馆内，每日开放时间为上午九时至十二时，下午一时半至五时半，现有各种新书，杂志刊物如童话，新连环画，小说故事，中国少年儿童，北京少年儿童，小朋友，中华少年及少年自然科学丛书等二千册，欢迎少年儿童入园阅览。
