Title: Fujian, Zhejiang Exceed Targets for Actual Sales of Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1950-05-08
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 6th. As of the end of April, Fujian and Zhejiang provinces had exceeded their original allocated sales quota for the first phase of public bonds. Fujian Province has actually sold 1,093,504 shares, and Zhejiang Province has actually sold 4,091,224 shares, each exceeding the original allocated quota by over 90,000 shares.

### 福建、浙江超额完成实销公债

1950-05-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　福建、浙江超额完成实销公债
    【新华社上海六日电】截至四月底止，福建、浙江两省实销第一期公债分数均已超过原配销额。福建省已实销一百零九万三千五百零四分，浙江省已实销四百零九万一千二百二十四分，各超过原配销额九万多分。
