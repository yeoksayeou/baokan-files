Title: Resolution of the Central Political Bureau Meeting of the China Democratic League: Mobilize League Members to Aid Unemployed Workers
Author:
Date: 1950-05-20
Page: 1

　　Resolution of the Central Political Bureau Meeting of the China Democratic League
　　Mobilize League Members to Aid Unemployed Workers
    [Reported by This Newspaper] The Central Political Bureau of the China Democratic League held its tenth meeting on May 13th. Regarding the relief of unemployed workers, it was resolved that the headquarters and local organizations should mobilize all League members to actively donate. At the meeting, donations of 460,000 RMB were immediately pledged. Headquarters staff also voluntarily pledged 143,000 RMB based on their ability, totaling 603,000 RMB pledged. Donations are still continuing.

### 民盟中央政治局会议决议　发动盟员援救失业工人

1950-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　民盟中央政治局会议决议
　　发动盟员援救失业工人
    【本报讯】中国民主同盟中央政治局于五月十三日召开第十次会议，对于救济失业工人，决议：由总部及各地方组织发动全体盟员踊跃认捐。当场即分别认捐人民券四十六万元，总部工作人员亦均量力认捐人民券十四万三千元，共认捐人民券六十万三千元，现仍在继续认捐中。
