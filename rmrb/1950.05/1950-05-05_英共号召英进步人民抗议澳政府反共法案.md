Title: British Communist Party Calls on British Progressive People to Protest Australian Government's Anti-Communist Bill
Author:
Date: 1950-05-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, London, 4th. According to a Reuters dispatch from London: The Political Committee of the British Communist Party issued a statement on April 28th, calling on every democrat and labor movement member in Britain to protest the Australian government's legislation outlawing the Communist Party, and demanding the revocation of the Menzies government's proposal.

　　The statement pointed out that this bill is a heinous attack on civil liberties, unprecedented since the Nazi regime in Germany. This attack is also part of the Australian ruling class's savage war preparations and is encouraged by American warmongers.

### 英共号召英进步人民抗议澳政府反共法案

1950-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　英共号召英进步人民抗议澳政府反共法案
    【新华社四日讯】据路透社伦敦消息：英国共产党政治委员会于四月二十八日发布声明，号召英国每一个民主分子和劳工运动者抗议澳大利亚政府规定共产党为非法的立法，并要求撤消孟席斯政府的建议。
    声明指出，这个法案是自德国纳粹政权以来，从没有见过的对公民自由权利的罪恶昭著的攻击。这个攻击也是澳大利亚统治阶级野蛮备战的一部分，而且是受美国战争贩子所鼓舞的。
