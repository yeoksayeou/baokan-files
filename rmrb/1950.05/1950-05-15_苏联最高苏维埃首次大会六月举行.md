Title: First Session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR to be Held in June
Author:
Date: 1950-05-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th: TASS, Moscow, 13th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, in accordance with Article 55 of the Constitution of the USSR, has decided to convene the first session of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics of the current term on June 12, 1950, in Moscow.

### 苏联最高苏维埃首次大会六月举行

1950-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃首次大会六月举行
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十三日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团，顷根据苏联宪法第五十五条，定于一九五○年六月十二日在莫斯科召开苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟本届最高苏维埃的第一次大会。
