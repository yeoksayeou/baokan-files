Title: Seventeen New Newspapers and Periodicals Approved for Publication by the Beijing Military Control Commission
Author:
Date: 1950-05-05
Page: 3

[Beijing News Agency] The Beijing Municipal People's Government's News and Publication Department investigated and studied the newspaper and periodical registration applications submitted in Beijing. In April, the Military Control Commission approved the registration and issued temporary registration certificates to 17 units: (1) One daily newspaper: *Beijing News Draft*; (2) Two weekly periodicals: *Health News*, *World Knowledge*; (3) Eight monthly periodicals: *Beijing Worker*, *Beijing Broadcasting*, *World Youth* (Chinese version), *New Sports*, *People's China Report* (Esperanto), *People's Education*, *China Agriculture News*, *Private Hospital Trade Union Newsletter*; (4) Two bi-monthly periodicals: *Science Workers*, *Science Communication*; (5) Two quarterly periodicals: *Chinese Science*, *Chemistry*; (6) Two irregular periodicals: *Tax Worker Bulletin*, *Journal of the Chinese New Legal Research Institute*. As of the end of April this year, the city has approved the registration of a total of 127 newspapers, periodicals, and news agencies, including 22 that have ceased publication or moved to other locations, and 11 that have not yet been published.

### 新报刊十七种  京军管会批准出版

1950-05-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　新报刊十七种
    京军管会批准出版
    【京市新闻处讯】京市申请登记的报纸期刊，经市人民政府新闻出版处调查研究，于四月份内，先后呈奉军管会批准登记并发给临时登记证的计有十七个单位：（一）日刊一种：京市新闻稿；（二）周刊二种：健康报、世界知识；（三）月刊八种：北京工人、北京广播、世界青年（中文版）、新体育、人民中国报导（世界语）、人民教育、中国农报、私立医院工会通讯；（四）双月刊二种：科学工作者、科学通讯；（五）季刊二种：中国科学、化学；（六）不定期刊二种：税工通报、中国新法学研究院院刊。至本年四月底止，本市已批准登记的报刊通讯社共有一百二十七种，其中包括已停刊或迁至外埠出版的二十二种以及尚未出刊的十一种。
