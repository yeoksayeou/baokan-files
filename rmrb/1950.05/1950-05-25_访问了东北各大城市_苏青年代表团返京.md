Title: Soviet Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Major Cities in Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1950-05-25
Page: 1
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, 24th
    The Soviet Youth Delegation and the Soviet Youth Art Troupe returned to Beijing from Shenyang yesterday and today. The delegation visited major cities in Northeast China and participated in 35 gatherings, including lectures and symposiums, introducing the work achievements and happy life of Soviet youth, as well as the work experience of the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League, to more than 300,000 Chinese youth attendees. The Soviet Youth Art Troupe performed eight times in Northeast China, with an audience of 215,000 people.

### 访问了东北各大城市  苏青年代表团返京

1950-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　访问了东北各大城市
    苏青年代表团返京
    【新华社二十四日讯】苏联青年代表团和苏联青年文艺工作团已于昨今两日先后由沈阳返抵北京。代表团曾访问了东北各大城市，共参加了演讲会、座谈会等各种集会三十五次，向到会的三十余万中国青年群众介绍了苏联青年的工作成就和幸福生活以及苏联列宁共产主义青年团的工作经验。苏联青年文艺工作团在东北共演出八次，观众达二十一万五千人。
