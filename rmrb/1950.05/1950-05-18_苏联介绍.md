　　Introduction to the Soviet Union
    [Report from this newspaper] The 12th lecture of the "Introduction to the Soviet Union" lecture series, sponsored by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, is scheduled to be held at 19 Nanheyan Street on May 19th. Tao Dayong will speak on "A Comparison of Three Economies: Socialism, New Democracy, and Capitalism." All those holding long-term or temporary lecture passes from the association are welcome to attend.
                                    (Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association)

　　苏联介绍
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会主办的“苏联介绍”讲座第十二讲，定于五月十九日在南河沿十九号举行，请陶大镛讲“社会主义、新民主主义、资本主义三种经济的比较”，凡持有该会长期或临时听讲证者届时均可前往听讲。
