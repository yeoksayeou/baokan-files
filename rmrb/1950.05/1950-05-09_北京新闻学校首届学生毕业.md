Title: First Graduates of Beijing Journalism School

Author: 
Date: 1950-05-09
Page: 3

Beijing City News

　　First Graduates of Beijing Journalism School
    [Xinhua News Agency] The first class of the Beijing Journalism School graduated on the 8th. A total of 272 students graduated and will soon be assigned to work in various locations. The allocation of this graduating class will prioritize meeting the needs of news organizations in the capital, and will also focus on addressing the areas most lacking in news cadres. The enrollment regulations for the school's second class are being prepared. The second class will implement a more standardized education than the first.

### 北京新闻学校首届学生毕业

1950-05-09
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　北京新闻学校首届学生毕业
    【新华社讯】北京新闻学校第一期已于八日毕业。毕业学员共二百七十二名，即将分发各地工作。这一期毕业学生的分配除尽先满足首都各新闻机关的需要，并有重点地照顾新闻干部最感缺乏的地区。该校第二期招生章程已在准备中。第二期将实施较第一期更正规的教育。
