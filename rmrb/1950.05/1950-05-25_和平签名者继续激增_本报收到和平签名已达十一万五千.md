Title: Peace Signatures Continue to Surge: Our Newspaper Receives 115,000 Peace Signatures
Author:
Date: 1950-05-25
Page: 1

　　Peace Signatures Continue to Surge
    Our Newspaper Receives 115,000 Peace Signatures
    [Our Newspaper] The signature campaign for the Declaration for the Defense of World Peace is underway across the country. Up until recently, all organizations and groups in the capital have universally launched peace signature campaigns. Over 280,000 people in Tianjin have signed, 94,310 people in Xi'an have signed, and nearly 100,000 people in Qingdao have signed. Other cities such as Shenyang, Wuhan, Jinan, Kaifeng, Yinchuan, Dihua, Hangzhou, Kunming, Zhangjiakou, Changsha, Suzhou, Shijiazhuang, Dalian, and provinces such as Hubei, Hebei, Chahar, Suiyuan, Gansu, and Hunan have successively held peace signature rallies involving thousands to tens of thousands of people. They are also conducting propaganda in various ways, responding to the calls of the World Peace Council and the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and against American Aggression, and enthusiastically launching peace signature campaigns.
    [Another News] Yesterday, our newspaper received 10,419 peace signatures from this city and other places, bringing the total to 115,994.

### 和平签名者继续激增  本报收到和平签名已达十一万五千

1950-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　和平签名者继续激增
    本报收到和平签名已达十一万五千
    【本报讯】在保卫世界和平宣言上的签名运动正在全国各地展开。截至最近止，首都各机关团体已经普遍展开和平签名运动。天津已有二十八万多人签名，西安已有九万四千三百一十人签名，青岛已有近十万人签名。其他各地如沈阳、武汉、济南、开封、银川、迪化、杭州、昆明、张家口、长沙、苏州、石家庄、大连等市及湖北、河北、察哈尔、绥远、甘肃、湖南等省都已先后举行了数千人至数万人参加的和平签名运动大会，并以各种方式进行宣传，响应世界和大与我国和大的号召，热烈展开和平签名运动。
    【又讯】昨日本报收到本市及外埠和平签名一万零四百一十九人，连前共计十一万五千九百九十四人。
