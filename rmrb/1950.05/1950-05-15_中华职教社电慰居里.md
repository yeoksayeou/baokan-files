Title: China Vocational Education Society Sends Telegram of Condolences to Joliot-Curie
Author:
Date: 1950-05-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The China Vocational Education Society sent a telegram of condolence to the French scientist Professor Joliot-Curie, protesting the French reactionary government's dismissal of him from his position as a member of the French Atomic Energy Commission.

### 中华职教社电慰居里

1950-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华职教社电慰居里
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中华职业教育社致电慰问法国科学家约里奥——居里教授，并抗议法国反动政府解除他的法国原子能委员会委员的职务。
