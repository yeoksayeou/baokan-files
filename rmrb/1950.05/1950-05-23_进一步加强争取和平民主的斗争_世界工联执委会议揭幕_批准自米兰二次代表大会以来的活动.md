Title: Further Strengthen the Struggle for Peace and Democracy: World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Committee Meeting Opens, Approving Activities Since the Second Congress in Milan
Author:
Date: 1950-05-23
Page: 4

　　Further Strengthen the Struggle for Peace and Democracy
    World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Committee Meeting Opens
     Approving Activities Since the Second Congress in Milan
    [Xinhua News Agency, 22nd] According to TASS reports: The Executive Committee meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) opened on the 19th in Budapest, the capital of Hungary.

    Giuseppe Di Vittorio, President of the WFTU, emphasized in his opening speech that this meeting would find the correct direction for further opposing war preparations and striving for peace, democracy, and human happiness.

    Louis Saillant, General Secretary of the WFTU, then briefly explained the WFTU's activities since the Second Congress held in Milan from the end of June to the beginning of July 1949. Saillant pointed out that the WFTU continued to grow in the struggle against the splitters of the labor movement. It now has 78.3 million members, compared with only 64 million members when the WFTU was founded in 1945, and 71 million at the Milan meeting. Saillant also reported on the WFTU's active activities in striving for peace and opposing imperialist preparations for a new war, as well as the major tasks of this meeting.

    The meeting then launched a heated discussion on Saillant's report. Representatives from Iran, Australia, the People's Republic of China, Africa, the Soviet Union, Ceylon, Czechoslovakia, Tunisia, and Romania spoke successively. After the discussion, the meeting unanimously passed a resolution approving the WFTU's activities since the Second Congress in Milan.

### 进一步加强争取和平民主的斗争  世界工联执委会议揭幕  批准自米兰二次代表大会以来的活动

1950-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　进一步加强争取和平民主的斗争
    世界工联执委会议揭幕
     批准自米兰二次代表大会以来的活动
    【新华社二十二日讯】综合塔斯社消息：世界工会联合会执行委员会会议，已于十九日在匈牙利首都布达佩斯揭幕。
    世界工联主席维特里奥在揭幕词中强调指出：这次会议将为进一步反对战争准备和争取和平、民主及人类幸福的斗争，找出正确的方向。
    世界工联总书记路易·赛扬继而扼要地说明世界工联自一九四九年六月底至七月初在米兰举行第二次代表大会以来的活动情况。赛扬指出：世界工联在对劳工运动分裂分子的斗争中，继续成长着。它现在已拥有会员七千八百三十万人，而在一九四五年世界工联成立时仅有会员六千四百万人，在米兰会议时则为七千一百万人。赛扬并报告了世界工联在争取和平、反对帝国主义准备新战争的积极活动和这次会议的重大任务。
    会议随即对赛扬的报告展开热烈讨论，相继发言的有：伊朗、澳大利亚、中华人民共和国、非洲、苏联、锡兰、捷克斯洛伐克、突尼斯和罗马尼亚的代表们。会议在讨论结束之后一致通过决议，批准世界工联自米兰第二次代表大会以来的活动。
