Title: Special Exhibition of Labor Reform Images at the Water Pavilion of Zhongshan Park
Author: 
Date: 1950-05-14
Page: 3

Kyoto News

Special Exhibition of Labor Reform Images at the Water Pavilion of Zhongshan Park

[News from the Kyoto Municipal News Agency] The "Exhibition of Labor Reform Special Agent Pictures" organized by the Kyoto Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau was inaugurated at 2:00 PM on the 12th in the Water Pavilion of Zhongshan Park. Even before the opening, many visitors were waiting in the Water Pavilion teahouse. After the opening, visitors were extremely enthusiastic. As of 7:30 PM on the same day, in just over five hours, more than 1,100 people had visited. The exhibition is divided into three parts: the first part is farm labor (ditch digging, spring plowing, etc.); the second part is the daily life of the special agents, including political education, cultural entertainment, reading newspapers, medical care, and other daily life; the third part is the construction of the farm and the 1950 rice production schedule chart drawn by the special agents themselves.
(Publicity Department)

### 劳动改造特别的图片在中山公园水榭展览
公宣
1950-05-14
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　劳动改造特别的图片在中山公园水榭展览
    【京市新闻处讯】京市人民政府公安局举办的“劳动改造特务图片展览”已于十二日下午二时在中山公园水榭揭幕，揭幕前即有许多观众在水榭茶社等候，开放后参观者极为踊跃，截至当日下午七时半，仅五个多小时，即有一千一百余人前往参观。该展览计分三个部分：第一部分为农场劳动（挖渠、春耕等工作）；第二部分为特务们的日常生活，包括政治教育、文化娱乐、读报、医疗及其他日常生活等，第三部分是农场的建设和特务们自己绘制的一九五○年水稻生产进度图等。
                                                （公宣）
