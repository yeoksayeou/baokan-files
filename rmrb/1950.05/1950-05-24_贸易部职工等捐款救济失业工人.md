Title: Ministry of Trade Employees and Others Donate to Relieve Unemployed Workers
Author:
Date: 1950-05-24
Page: 2

　　Ministry of Trade Employees and Others Donate to Relieve Unemployed Workers
    [This newspaper] The employees of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government actively donated their salaries to relieve unemployed workers in Shanghai, donating a total of 1,048,600 yuan.
    [Also reported] All members of the China National Democratic League in Beijing donated money to relieve unemployed workers, donating 511,000 yuan in a few days.

### 贸易部职工等捐款救济失业工人

1950-05-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　贸易部职工等捐款救济失业工人
    【本报讯】中央人民政府贸易部职工积极捐献薪资，救济上海失业工人，共捐出一百零四万八千六百元。
    【又讯】中国农工民主党北京全体党员，捐款救济失业工人，数日内即捐出五十一万一千元。
