Title: Northwest Bureau Holds党员干部大会, Xi Zhongxun Speaks on "Opposing Bureaucratism," Points Out the Great Significance of Rectifying Cadres
Author:
Date: 1950-05-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 21st. The Northwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China convened a党员干部大会on the 20th, and invited non-Party democratic figures to participate. Xi Zhongxun, Secretary of the Northwest Bureau, delivered a speech entitled "Opposing Bureaucratism," using concrete facts to analyze and criticize in detail the harmfulness of bureaucratism and commandism, the biggest obstacles in the current work of the Northwest, and pointed out the great significance of rectifying cadres and transforming work styles at present.

### 西北局召开党员干部大会  习仲勋讲《反对官僚主义》  指出整训干部的重大意义

1950-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　西北局召开党员干部大会
    习仲勋讲《反对官僚主义》  指出整训干部的重大意义
    【新华社西安二十一日电】中共中央西北局于二十日召开党员干部大会，并邀请党外民主人士参加。西北局书记习仲勋以《反对官僚主义》为题发表讲话，以具体事实，详尽地分析和批判了当前西北工作中最大的障碍——官僚主义与命令主义的危害性，指出目前整训干部、改造作风的重大意义。
