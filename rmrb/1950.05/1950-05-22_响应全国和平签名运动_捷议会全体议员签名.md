Title: Czech Parliament Members Sign in Response to the National Peace Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1950-05-22
Page: 4

　　Responding to the National Peace Signature Campaign
    Czech Parliament Members Sign
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 20th] TASS, Prague: The Czechoslovak National Assembly held a special meeting on the 18th, attended by government members and representatives of the Central Action Committee of the Democratic Front. The meeting passed a resolution in response to the national peace signature campaign.

    The National Assembly resolution stated: "Relying on the hundreds of millions of people around the world fighting for peace, relying on the people of all countries who are building socialism, relying on the people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union who are the most powerful guarantee of peace, and recognizing that leading the struggle for peace is Marshal Stalin – the true genius leader of all progressive mankind, we are convinced that we will bring the struggle to defend peace to a victorious conclusion."

    All members of parliament unanimously signed the declaration (formerly translated as appeal) of the World Peace Congress Committee.

    A comprehensive peace signature campaign is underway throughout the Czech Republic. The city of Prague has collected over 201,000 signatures, and the Prague district has collected 460,000 signatures.

### 响应全国和平签名运动  捷议会全体议员签名

1950-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　响应全国和平签名运动
    捷议会全体议员签名
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克国民议会于十八日举行特别会议，出席者有政府委员和民主阵线中央行动委员会的代表们。会议通过一项响应全国和平签名运动的决议。
    国民议会决议称：“倚靠着全世界千百万为和平而奋斗的人民，倚靠着正在建设社会主义的各国人民，倚靠着成为和平之最有力的保障的苏联的各族人民，并且认识到领导着和平斗争的乃是斯大林大元帅——一切进步人类真正的天才领袖，我们深信我们必将使保卫和平的斗争，达到胜利的结果。”
    议会中的全体议员一致在世界拥护和平大会党委会的宣言（前译呼吁书）上签了名。
    捷克全国正在进行着全面的和平签名运动。布拉格市已经征集了二十万一千余人签名，布拉格区已经征集了四十六万人签名。
