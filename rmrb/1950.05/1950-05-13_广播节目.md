　　Radio Programs
    Central People's Broadcasting Station, 20:00, People's Forum: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, will speak on "Consolidating Revolutionary Victory and Defending Lasting Peace." 19:00, Social Science Lecture: Zhang Geng will speak on "The Content of the New Opera." 18:45, Natural Science Lecture: The station's lecture department will speak on "Regarding the Treatment of Blindness" (Further answers to questions from Zhang Shulin, etc.). 12:45, Book Review: Introducing the book "The Factory of Three Medals" published by the Era Publishing House. 20:30, First Literary and Art Program: The Central Academy of Drama's Drama Troupe will broadcast the drama "Walking Towards a Road." 22:00, Second Literary and Art Program: Ye Yangxi will sing a section from the Kunqu Opera "The Palace of Eternal Life."
    Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, 10:00, Opening Music: The New Democracy Theory Radio Learning Lecture, sponsored by the Higher Education Department of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government: The lecture topic is "Answers to Questions on New Democratic Politics," written by Professor Wang Dagang of the Renmin University of China and broadcast by a comrade from the Ministry of Education. 16:00, Teachers and Staff Radio Learning Program: Broadcasting a recording of the report by Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, entitled "The Historical Characteristics of the Chinese Revolution." 17:30, Children's Program: Children from Niujie Elementary School will sing the opera "Singing the Praises of Sino-Soviet Friendship." 18:00, Workers' Program: Continuing to teach the song "World Democratic Youth March." 18:00, Voice of the City: Broadcasting the Beijing Municipal People's Government News Office's report on the issue of convening People's Representative Conferences at the district level. 21:30, Women's Program: Chen Xiuling, Head Nurse of the Municipal Second Hospital, will speak on: How to commemorate the "May 12th" Nurses' Day.

### 广播节目

1950-05-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　广播节目
    中央人民广播电台今天二十点人民讲坛：中国保卫世界和平大会委员会主席郭沫若讲《巩固革命胜利与保卫持久和平》。十九点社会科学讲座：张庚讲《新歌剧的内容问题》。十八点四十五分自然科学讲座：该台讲座科讲《关于医治盲眼问题》（再答张树林等问题）。十二点四十五分书刊介绍：介绍时代出版社出版的《三个勋章的工厂》一书。二十点三十分第一次文艺节目：中央戏剧学院话剧团演播话剧《走向一条路》。二十二点第二次文艺节目：叶仰曦演唱昆曲《长生殿》中之一段。
    北京市人民广播电台今天十点开始曲：由中央人民政府教育部高等教育司主办的新民主主义论广播学习讲座：播讲的题目是《关于新民主主义政治的问题解答》由中国人民大学王大纲教授撰稿教育部同志播讲。十六点教职员广播学习节目：播送中央人民政府教育部钱俊瑞副部长报告的录音，题目是《中国革命的历史特点》。十七点三十分儿童节目：由牛街小学小朋友们演唱歌剧《说唱中苏友好》。十八点工人节目：继续教唱《世界民主青年进行曲》。十八点市政之声：播送北京市人民政府新闻处关于召开区各界人民代表会议的问题的报导。二十一点三十分妇女节目：市立第二医院护士长陈秀玲讲：怎样纪念“五一二”护士节。
