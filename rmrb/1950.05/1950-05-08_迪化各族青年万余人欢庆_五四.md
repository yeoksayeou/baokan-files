Title: More Than 10,000 Youths of All Ethnic Groups in Dihua Celebrate "May Fourth"
Author:
Date: 1950-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 6th: Dihua News: On May 4th, more than 10,000 young men and women of all ethnic groups and from all walks of life in Dihua City gathered to warmly celebrate the "May Fourth" Youth Day. In their speeches, representatives of the youths of all ethnic groups unanimously stated that they are willing to study Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought diligently under the leadership of the Communist Party and the People's Government, strive to produce and save, consolidate national unity, master professional skills, and struggle for the construction of a new China. On the same day, the city's youth also held ball games and various recreational activities.

### 迪化各族青年万余人欢庆“五四”

1950-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　迪化各族青年万余人欢庆“五四”
    【新华社西安六日电】迪化讯：迪化市各族各界男女青年、学生万余人于五月四日集会，热烈庆祝“五四”青年节。各族青年代表在讲话中，一致表示愿在共产党和人民政府的领导下，努力学习马列主义和毛泽东思想，努力生产节约，巩固民族团结，精通业务技术，为建设新中国而奋斗。同日全市青年并举行球赛及各种文娱活动。
