Title: The Soviet Youth Cultural Troupe Holds Concert of Songs and Dances in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The Soviet Youth Cultural Troupe, headed by Soviet artist and Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Arts Committee, Anisimov, held a concert of songs and dances at Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai tonight. Over 700 people attended, including Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman Li Jishen, and Premier Zhou Enlai. Performers included Smolienskaya, soloist of the Soviet State Grand Theatre and winner of the Budapest World Youth Festival, and Zvagina, dancer of the Soviet State Grand Theatre and winner of the Budapest World Youth Festival, who were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 苏联青年文工团在京举行歌舞音乐会

1950-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联青年文工团在京举行歌舞音乐会
    【新华社三日讯】以苏联艺术家、苏联艺术委员会副主席阿尼西莫夫为首的苏联青年文艺工作团，今晚在中南海怀仁堂举行歌舞音乐会，到有中央人民政府朱德副主席，刘少奇副主席，宋庆龄副主席，李济深副主席，周恩来总理等七百余人。会上表演的有苏联国立大戏院独唱家、布达佩斯世界青年联欢大会得奖者斯莫林斯卡亚，苏联国立大戏院舞蹈家，布达佩斯世界青年联欢大会得奖者兹娃纪娜等，受到观众热烈欢迎。
