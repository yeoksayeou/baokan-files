Title: Southern Fujian Coast: Our Army Liberates Dongshan Island, Leaving the Kinmen Remnant Bandits Isolated
Author:
Date: 1950-05-16
Page: 1

　　Southern Fujian Coast
　　Our Army Liberates Dongshan Island
　　Leaving the Kinmen Remnant Bandits Isolated
    [Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, May 15th] A unit of the People's Liberation Army's Third Field Army liberated Dongshan Island, located at the junction of the Minnan coastal area and Guangdong, at dawn on the 12th. The Liberation Army crossed the sea starting at 7:00 PM on the 11th, and with the support of powerful artillery fire, successfully seized the beachhead around 10:00 PM that night. They then launched attacks on the enemy with multiple spearheads, and successfully concluded the battle at 4:00 AM on the 12th. The remnant bandits, except for a portion who escaped by boat, were completely annihilated. The results of the battle are still being tallied. Dongshan Island and Kinmen Island are the two major islands in the southeastern coastal area of Fujian occupied by the Kuomintang remnant bandits. The liberation of Dongshan Island has left the Kinmen remnant bandits isolated.

### 福建南部沿海　我军解放东山岛　使金门残匪陷于孤立

1950-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　福建南部沿海
　　我军解放东山岛
　　使金门残匪陷于孤立
    【新华社福州十五日电】人民解放军第三野战军一部，于十二日拂晓解放闽南沿海闽粤交界处的东山岛。解放军于十一日晚七时起渡，在强大炮火配合下，于当晚十时左右胜利抢占滩头阵地，随即以多路箭头对敌展开攻击，至十二日拂晓四时，胜利结束战斗。残匪除一部乘船逃窜外，其余全部就歼，战果正在清查中。按东山岛与金门岛为福建东南沿海国民党残匪盘踞的两大岛屿。东山岛的解放，使金门残匪陷于孤立。
