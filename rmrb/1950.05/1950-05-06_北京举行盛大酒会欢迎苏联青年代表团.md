Title: Grand Reception Held in Beijing to Welcome Soviet Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the State Council of the Central People's Government, and the Beijing Municipal People's Government jointly held a grand reception tonight at 7 p.m. to welcome the Soviet Youth Delegation and the Soviet Youth Art Troupe. Attendees included Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, and Li Jishen; Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, and Chen Shutong; Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Huang Yanpei; Mayor of Beijing Municipal People's Government Nie Rongzhen, and Deputy Mayors Zhang Youyu and Wu Han. Invited guests included heads of ministries, commissions, departments, and administrations of the Central People's Government, leaders of the Chinese Communist Party and various democratic parties, leaders of the New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the All-China Students' Federation, the All-China Federation Of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and other people's organizations, totaling over 600 people. Soviet Ambassador Roshchin and envoys from various people's democratic countries were also invited to attend. Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a welcoming speech at the reception. Head of the Soviet Youth Delegation Mikhailov delivered a reply. Guo Moruo and Soviet youth writer Gonchar also delivered short speeches. The reception proceeded in a cordial and warm atmosphere, with hosts and guests frequently shouting slogans such as "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong," "Long live Marshal Stalin," and "Long live the friendship between the youth of China and the Soviet Union." After the reception, the Soviet Art Troupe performed artistic programs.

### 北京举行盛大酒会欢迎苏联青年代表团

1950-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京举行盛大酒会欢迎苏联青年代表团
    【新华社五日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中央人民政府政务院、北京市人民政府于今晚七时联合举行盛大酒会，欢迎苏联青年代表团和苏联青年文艺工作团。出席者计有中央人民政府副主席朱德、宋庆龄、李济深，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席沈钧儒、郭沫若、陈叔通，中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来、副总理黄炎培，北京市人民政府市长聂荣臻，副市长张友渔、吴晗。应邀作陪的计有中央人民政府各院部会署行首长，中国共产党及各民主党派领袖，新民主主义青年团、中华全国民主青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中苏友好协会总会以及其他人民团体的领袖等六百余人。苏联大使罗申及各人民民主国家的使节亦应邀参加。周恩来总理在酒会上致欢迎词。苏联青年代表团团长米哈依洛夫致答词。郭沫若及苏联青年作家龚查尔并发表简短演说。酒会在亲切热烈的气氛中进行，宾主频频高呼“毛泽东主席万岁”、“斯大林大元帅万岁”、“中苏两国青年友谊万岁”等口号。会后苏联文艺工作团曾表演艺术节目。
