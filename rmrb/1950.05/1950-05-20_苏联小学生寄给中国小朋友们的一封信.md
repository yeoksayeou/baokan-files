Title: A Letter from Soviet Elementary School Students to Chinese Children

Author: 
Date: 1950-05-20
Page: 4

To the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association of Beijing, People's Republic of China:

Dear Chinese Children!

    We, the elementary school students and Young Pioneers of the Bulonduqiao Seven-Year School in the Ponazlyev District of Kostroma Province, with extreme excitement, read your letter published in the 15th issue of the "Young Pioneer Truth" newspaper on February 21, 1950.

    We send you fraternal Young Pioneer greetings. We wholeheartedly rejoice for you in building a new and happy life under the leadership of your great leader, Mao Zedong.

    Now that the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance has been signed between our two countries, your every achievement encourages us even more. A few days ago, when the great leader of the Chinese people, Mao Zedong, returned to your motherland, the train he was on passed through our station.

    Our country, under the leadership of the Communist Party and the great leader of the Soviet people, Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin, is now implementing seven years of free education. Every child in our country should complete seven years of schooling. The doors of all higher education institutions are open to us. Recently in geography class, we talked about the topic of climate.

    It's warm here now.

    It snows a lot. The average temperature here in February is minus 8.53 degrees Celsius, and the snow depth reaches 0.73 meters.

    How is the weather there?

    Do you conduct weather observations?

    How are your schools preparing for tests and exams?

    We look forward to receiving your reply and hope that we will always maintain friendly communication in the future.

    Students of Bulonduqiao Seven-Year School, Ponazlyev District, Kostroma Province, Soviet Union: Glinnevich, Verkov, Beloborodov, Telitsotkin, Tomagayevsky, Giselev, Zabokin, Beloborodov, Dubonov.

### 苏联小学生寄给中国小朋友们的一封信

1950-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联小学生寄给中国小朋友们的一封信中华人民共和国北京市中苏友好协会转亲爱的中国小朋友们！
    我们，科斯特洛姆省波纳兹列夫区布隆都契奥七年制学校的小学生和少年先锋队员们，带着极度兴奋的情感，读到一九五○年二月二十一号《少先真理报》十五期所发表的你们的来信。
    我们向你们致兄弟般的少先队员的敬礼。我们为了你们在你们的伟大领袖毛泽东领导下建立新的幸福的生活而衷心地欢欣。
    现在我们两国之间签订了中苏友好同盟互助条约，你们的一切成就使得我们更加鼓舞。几天以前，当中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东返回你们祖国去的时候，他所乘的列车曾从我们的车站经过。
    我们国家，在共产党和苏维埃人民伟大的领袖约瑟夫·维萨里昂诺维奇·斯大林领导之下，现在实施着七年免费教育。我们国家每一个儿童都应当完成七年的学业。所有高等学校的校门都对我们敞开着。最近在地理课上我们讲过了气候这个题目。
    现在我们这儿正是暖和的天气。
    雪下得很多。我们这儿二月份平均温度是零下八·五三度，雪深度达零·七三公尺。
    你们那里天气怎样？
    你们是否进行天气的观测？
    你们的学校如何准备测验与考试？
    盼望得到你们的回信，并希望今后我们永远保持友谊的通信。
    苏联科斯特洛姆省波纳兹列夫区布隆都契奥七年制学校学生格林聂维赤、维尔科夫、别洛伯洛多夫、特利错特金、托马盖耶夫斯基、吉谢廖夫、扎伯金、别洛伯洛多夫、杜宝诺夫。
