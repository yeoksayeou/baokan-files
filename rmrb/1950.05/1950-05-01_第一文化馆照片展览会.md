Title: First Cultural Center Photo Exhibition
Author: Tie Jun
Date: 1950-05-01
Page: 3

Beijing News

First Cultural Center Photo Exhibition
[This newspaper reports] To celebrate the first "May Day" Labor Day after the founding of New China, the Beijing First Cultural Center recently borrowed a large number of photos, pictures, and real objects from the Central People's Government's Ministry of Heavy Industry, Ministry of Water Resources, Ministry of Agriculture, Beijing Industrial Bureau, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and other units. Together with the serial pictures made overnight by the amateur art group led by the center, they are holding a "Comprehensive Photo and Picture Exhibition Celebrating May 1st International Labor Day". The exhibition will be held from May 2nd to May 16th in Diwai Gulou. The time is from 10:00 am to 5:30 pm daily.

(Tie Jun)

### 第一文化馆照片展览会
铁军
1950-05-01
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　第一文化馆照片展览会
    【本报讯】京市第一文化馆为庆祝新中国成立后第一个“五一”劳动节，日前向中央人民政府重工业部、水利部、农业部、京市工业局、中苏友协等单位借到大批照片、图片、实物，连同该馆领导的业余美工组连夜赶制的连环画一起，举办“庆祝五一国际劳动节照片画片综合展览”。该项展览，自五月二日起至五月十六日在地外鼓楼举行。时间每日上午十时至下午五时半。
                                                        （铁军）
