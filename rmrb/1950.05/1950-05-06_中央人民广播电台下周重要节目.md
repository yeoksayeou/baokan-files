Title: Important Programs of the Central People's Broadcasting Station Next Week
Author:
Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 3
　　
Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: The following are the important evening programs of the Central People's Broadcasting Station from May 7th to 13th:

　　Natural Science Lectures (broadcast every evening from 18:45 to 19:00):
　　  8th, 9th, 10th Dr. Huang Shuze will lecture on: Vomiting and Diarrhea in Children
　　  11th Peng Dahui from the Nutrition Research Laboratory of the Central Hygiene Experimental Institute will lecture on: The Impact of Cooking Methods on the Nutrients in Rice and Flour
　　  12th The lecture department of this station will lecture on: The Creator of the World's First Airplane
　　  13th On the Issue of Treating Blindness (Further Answers to Questions from Zhang Shulin, etc.)

　　Social Science Lectures (broadcast every evening from 19:00 to 20:00):
　　  8th Ai Siqi will lecture on: Socialist Revolution (Chapter 3, Lecture 3 of the History of Social Development)
　　  9th Ai Siqi will lecture on: New Democratic Revolution (Chapter 3, Lecture 4 of the History of Social Development)
　　  10th Ai Siqi will lecture on: What is Politics? (Chapter 4, Lecture 1 of the History of Social Development)
　　  11th, 12th Yu Guangyuan will lecture on: Currency (Lecture 7 of Political Economy)
　　  13th Zhang Geng will lecture on: Content Issues of the New Opera

　　Sunday Literary Program (7th, 18:45 to 20:00)
　　  Drama: The Beijing Women's Production and Education Center presents: "The Thousand-Year Glacier Thaws" (Collectively created by the students of the Fifth Institute of the Beijing Women's Production and Education Center)

　　People's Forum (broadcast every evening from 20:00 to 20:30):
　　  Wednesday is a program sponsored by the Ministry of Education. Other times will feature programs sponsored by the Chinese Committee for the Defense of World Peace and lectures on the New Marriage Law. Detailed content will be announced separately.

　　Literary Programs (broadcast every evening from 20:30 to 21:00)
　　  7th Western Music: Introduction to Tchaikovsky's Music
　　  8th Opera Excerpts: Central Academy of Drama Opera Troupe performs excerpts from the operas "Good Military Dependents" and "The Whole Family is Honored."
　　People's Art Theater performs excerpts from the opera "Brother and Sister Reclaiming Wasteland."
　　  9th Soviet Military Songs: Army Chorus - "Singing the Red Army," "Artillery March," "Cavalry March," "Howitzer Gunners," "Don Cossacks,"
　　"Brave Song," "Marching Onward"
　　  10th Literary Works Recitation: Li Yang recites the narrative poem: "The Messenger Girl" (by Li Ji)
　　  11th Film Introduction Recording: Northeast Film Studio film: "Spring in Inner Mongolia"
　　  12th Western Music: Introduction to Mozart and his works
　　  13th Drama:
　　Central Academy of Drama Drama Troupe presents: "Walking Towards One Road" (written by Lin Song)
