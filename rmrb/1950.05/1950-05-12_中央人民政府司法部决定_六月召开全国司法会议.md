Title: The Ministry of Justice of the Central People's Government Decides to Convene a National Judicial Conference in June
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 1

　　Central People's Government Ministry of Justice Decides
　　To Convene National Judicial Conference in June
    [Xinhua News Agency, 11th] In order to summarize the experiences of the people's judicial work, strengthen the policy thinking of judicial work cadres, and solve the urgent and solvable problems at present, the Ministry of Justice of the Central People's Government has decided to jointly convene the first National Judicial Conference in Beijing in June this year with the Supreme People's Court, the Supreme People's Procuratorate, and the Legislative Affairs Committee. The preparatory committee for the conference has been formed. Minister of Justice Shi Liang is the director of the preparatory committee. The committee has notified local judicial organs to send representatives. In order to fully prepare for and hold this conference well, the preparatory committee has formed judicial work inspection groups to inspect the Northeast and North China regions. The Northeast inspection group, led by Minister of Justice Shi Liang and Vice President of the Supreme People's Court Wu Gaizhi as the head and deputy head of the group, departed on the 7th of this month. The North China inspection group will also depart in mid-May.

### 中央人民政府司法部决定　六月召开全国司法会议

1950-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府司法部决定
　　六月召开全国司法会议
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府司法部为了总结人民司法工作的经验，加强司法工作干部的政策思想，解决目前急需与可能解决的问题，决定会同最高人民法院、最高人民检察署及法制委员会于今年六月间在北京联合召开首次全国司法会议。会议的筹备委员会已经组成。司法部史良部长为筹委会主任。该会已通知各地司法机关选派代表。筹委会为充分准备，开好此次会议起见，已组成司法工作视察团，分赴东北和华北两地区视察。东北视察团由司法部部长史良、最高人民法院副院长吴溉之任正副团长，已于本月七日出发。华北视察团亦将于本月中旬启程。
