Title: British Veterans Demonstrate in Front of U.S. Consulate, Shouting "Warmonger Acheson Get Out!"
Author:
Date: 1950-05-17
Page: 4

　　British Veterans Demonstrate in Front of U.S. Consulate
　　Shouting "Warmonger Acheson Get Out!"
    [Xinhua News Agency, London, 16th] TASS, London, 15th: London newspapers reported pro-peace activities in various parts of Britain. On the 13th, a group of veteran men and women held a parade and demonstration in front of the U.S. Consulate in Edinburgh, holding signs that read: "Warmonger Acheson Get Out," "Defending Peace is Defending Britain," and "Ban the Atomic Bomb." The Nottingham Conference, organized by the Nottingham Area Peace Committee, passed a resolution supporting the demand to ban atomic weapons.

### 英退伍军人在美领馆前示威　高呼“战贩艾奇逊滚蛋！”

1950-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　英退伍军人在美领馆前示威
　　高呼“战贩艾奇逊滚蛋！”
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社伦敦十五日讯：伦敦报纸报道了英国各地拥护和平的活动。十三日在爱丁堡美国领事馆门前，有一群退伍男女举行游行示威，他们手持标语，上面写着：“战争贩子艾奇逊滚蛋”，“保卫和平就是保卫英国”和“禁止原子弹”。由诺丁罕地区和平委员会所组织的诺丁罕大会，通过了拥护禁止原子武器要求的决议。
