Title: Famous Opera "The White-Haired Girl" Performed to Host Soviet Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1950-05-17
Page: 3

　　Beijing News

　　Famous Opera "The White-Haired Girl" Performed to Host Soviet Youth Delegation
    [Report from this newspaper] The preparatory committee for welcoming the Soviet Youth Delegation has scheduled an evening party at the Beijing Theater at 7:30 PM on the 17th. The Opera Troupe of the Central Academy of Drama will perform the opera "The White-Haired Girl" to entertain the Soviet Youth Delegation. All tickets for "The White-Haired Girl" on the 17th that were pre-sold by the Beijing Theater will be valid for the evening performance on the 19th.

### 上演名剧《白毛女》　招待苏青年代表团

1950-05-17
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　上演名剧《白毛女》招待苏青年代表团
    【本报讯】欢迎苏联青年代表团筹委会，定在十七日晚七时半假北京剧院举行晚会，由中央戏剧学院歌剧团演出歌剧《白毛女》招待苏联青年代表团。所有由北京剧院预售的十七日《白毛女》客票，一律改在十九日晚场继续有效。
