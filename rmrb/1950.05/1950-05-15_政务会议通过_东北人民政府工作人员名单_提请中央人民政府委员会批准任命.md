Title: Political Affairs Council Passes List of Northeast People's Government Staff Members to be Submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for Approval and Appointment
Author:
Date: 1950-05-15
Page: 3

　　Political Affairs Council Passes
　  List of Northeast People's Government Staff Members
　　To be Submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for Approval and Appointment
Xinhua News Agency, 13th — The Political Affairs Council of the Central People's Government Administration Council has passed the list of Northeast People's Government staff members to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval and appointment, as follows:
    Director of the People's Supervisory Committee: Zhang Xiushan, current Secretary-General of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
        Deputy Director: Zhang Mingyuan, current Deputy Secretary-General of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
            Chen Bocun, current Deputy Director of the Organization Department of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
          Committee Member: Zhang Weizhen (concurrent)
            Zhou Huan (concurrent)
            Han Tianshi (concurrent)
            An Jianping (female), current Deputy Secretary of the Women's Work Committee of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
            Li Huang, current President of the Northeast Daily.
            Guan Mengjue, current Secretary-General of the Northeast General Branch of the China Democratic League.
            Wang Zhixi (concurrent)
            Tan Kaiyun, current Director of the Organization Department of the Political Department of the Northeast Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
    (The above was passed by the 27th Political Affairs Council meeting)
            Guo Mingqiu (female), current member of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Northeast People's Government.
    (The above was passed by the 32nd Political Affairs Council meeting)
