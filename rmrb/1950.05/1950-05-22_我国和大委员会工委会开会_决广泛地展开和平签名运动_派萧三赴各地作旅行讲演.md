Title: China Peace Committee Working Committee Holds Meeting; Decides to Widely Launch Peace Signature Campaign; Sends Xiao San to Give Traveling Lectures in Various Places
Author:
Date: 1950-05-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The Working Committee of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace held its fourth meeting on the 21st to discuss launching a wider signature campaign for the defense of peace in various parts of the country. The committee members present included Xiao San, Ding Zan, Tian Han, Li Dequan, Cao Jinghua, Xu Guangping, etc. The meeting began with Ding Zan reporting that branches of the World Peace Congress had been established in Shenyang, Tianjin, Shanghai, Chongqing, Kunming, Dalian, Harbin, Zhengzhou, Taiyuan and other cities, and that signature campaigns for the defense of world peace had been launched. In Shanghai, 400,000 people had signed in half a month, and signature campaigns had been launched in various agencies, organizations, schools, and factories in Beijing. The meeting then discussed the issue of launching a signature campaign throughout the country.

　　The meeting considered it necessary to conduct extensive internationalist education simultaneously with the launch of the signature campaign for the defense of world peace, to publicize the growth of world peace forces, to point out the necessity of strengthening the solidarity of peace-loving people around the world, and to explain that the plans of the warmongers can definitely be smashed, in order to raise the consciousness and initiative of the broad masses of the people to actively participate in the signature campaign. The meeting decided to issue a publicity outline to mobilize cultural, artistic, and press circles in various places to use various forms of publicity to promote this movement.

　　The meeting decided to notify the local branches to report to the general assembly on the situation and experience of the signature campaign for the defense of peace for reference in various places. The meeting decided to send Comrade Xiao San to give traveling lectures throughout the country. He will leave Beijing within this month for Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Changsha, Wuhan, Xi'an and other places to give traveling lectures. His task is to convey the resolutions of the Stockholm meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, to strengthen the connection between the General Assembly of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace and the local branches, and to elaborate on the significance of the signature campaign for the Declaration on the Defense of World Peace.

　　The meeting also discussed the work of launching a campaign to solicit applications for the World Peace Prize and the Stalin Peace Prize, and decided to recommend Tian Han, Cao Jinghua, Yuan Muzhi, Ai Qing and other seven members to jointly form a committee with the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the China-Soviet Friendship Association and other groups to handle it.

　　The meeting also heard a report on the meeting of the Executive Bureau of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace to be held in London at the end of May. Our country has sent Liu Ningyi as a representative to attend the meeting.

### 我国和大委员会工委会开会  决广泛地展开和平签名运动  派萧三赴各地作旅行讲演

1950-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国和大委员会工委会开会
    决广泛地展开和平签名运动
    派萧三赴各地作旅行讲演
    【新华社二十一日讯】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会工作委员会二十一日举行第四次会议，讨论在全国各地更广泛展开保卫和平签名运动。到会的有萧三、丁瓒、田汉、李德全、曹靖华、许广平等委员，会议首先由丁瓒报告了沈阳、天津、上海、重庆、昆明、大连、哈尔滨、郑州、太原等城市都已经建立了保卫世界和平大会分会，并都已展开保卫世界和平签名运动，上海在半月之内已经有四十万人签名，北京各机关、团体、学校、工厂都展开了签名。接着会议对于在全国展开签名运动的问题加以讨论。
    会议认为：与展开保卫世界和平签名运动同时，必需配合进行广泛的国际主义教育，宣传世界和平力量的增长，指出全世界爱好和平人民的加强团结的必要，并说明定能粉碎战争贩子的计划，以提高广大人民的觉悟性与自动性，来积极参加签名。会议决定制发宣传大纲，发动各地文化、艺术、新闻界运用各种宣传形式，来促进这一运动。
    会议决定通知各地分会向总会汇报展开保卫和平签名运动的情况和经验，以供各地参考。会议决定派萧三委员赴全国各地作旅行讲演。他将于本月内离京赴南京、上海、杭州、广州、长沙、武汉、西安等作旅行讲演，其任务为传达世界拥护和平大会常设委员会斯德哥尔摩会议的决议，加强中国保卫世界和平大会总会与各地分会之联系，阐述关于保卫世界和平宣言签名运动的意义。
    会议又讨论了发起关于世界和平奖金及斯大林和平奖金的应征运动的工作，并决定推田汉、曹靖华、袁牧之、艾青等七委员会同中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中苏友好协会总会等团体合组委员会办理。
    会议又听取了关于世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局五月底将在伦敦举行会议的报告。我国已派刘宁一代表出席该会议。
