Title: Fifth Trolleybus Route Resumes Service Today
Author:
Date: 1950-05-03
Page: 3

　　Fifth Trolleybus Route Resumes Service Today
　　[Beijing News Agency] The Beijing Trolleybus Company, with the continuous increase in vehicles produced from the factory, in order to strengthen the city's transportation operation and facilitate citizens' travel, will resume the fifth route (Chongwenmen to Xuanwumen) from today (the 3rd). The ticket price will be the same as the current sectional fare system for each route.

### 五路电车今起恢复

1950-05-03
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　五路电车今起恢复
    【京市新闻处讯】北京电车公司以出厂车辆续有增加，为加强市内交通运转，便利市民乘车起见，自今（三）日起恢复第五路行车（崇文门至宣武门），票价与现行各路分段制度相同。
