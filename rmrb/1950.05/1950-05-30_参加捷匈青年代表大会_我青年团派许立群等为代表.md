Title: To attend the Czechoslovak and Hungarian Youth Congresses, the Communist Youth League sent Xu Liqun and others as representatives.
Author:
Date: 1950-05-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 27th - The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League recently received telegrams from the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Youth League and the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Youth League, inviting them to send representatives to attend the Czechoslovak Youth Congress to be held on June 7th and the Hungarian Youth Unity Congress to be held on June 18th. The Central Committee of the Youth League has decided that Xu Liqun, Wu Xueqian, and Li Guoying, who are currently abroad, will attend the Czechoslovak Youth Congress; Wu Xueqian and Liang Geng will attend the Hungarian Youth Unity Congress.

### 参加捷匈青年代表大会  我青年团派许立群等为代表

1950-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加捷匈青年代表大会
    我青年团派许立群等为代表
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会，日前收到捷克青年联盟中央委员会和匈牙利人民青年联盟主席团的来电，邀请派遣代表参加在六月七日召开的捷克青年代表大会和在六月十八日召开的匈牙利青年统一代表大会。青年团中央顷决定由现在国外的许立群、吴学谦、李国英前往参加捷克青年代表大会；吴学谦、梁畊参加匈牙利青年统一代表大会。
