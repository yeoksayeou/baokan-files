Title: This Newspaper Has Received Peace Signatures Exceeding 260,000
Author:
Date: 1950-05-31
Page: 4

　　This Newspaper Has Received Peace Signatures Exceeding 260,000

　　[This Newspaper] This newspaper continued to receive letters containing peace signatures from various regions yesterday, totaling 21,142 people. This includes all officers and soldiers of the 205th Division of the People's Liberation Army, the delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions attending the Moscow "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations, and 822 farmers mobilized by the Anyang County Sixth Complete Primary School. Combined with the previous total, there are 261,392 names.

### 本报收到和平签名已达二十六万余人

1950-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　本报收到和平签名已达二十六万余人
    【本报讯】本报昨日继续收到各地和平签名来信，总计二万一千一百四十二人。其中包括人民解放军二○五师全师指战员、中华全国总工会参加莫斯科“五一”国际劳动节庆典代表团，以及安阳县立第六完全小学发动的八百二十二名农民的签名。连前共计二十六万一千三百九十二名。
