Title: Cultural and Educational Committee and Other Units Host Soviet Youth Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1950-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles jointly hosted a banquet on the evening of the 18th for the Soviet Youth Delegation. Present were the Soviet Youth Delegation, headed by Mikhailov, and the Soviet Youth Cultural Troupe, totaling thirty-eight people. Also invited were Fedlin and Chikhvin from the Soviet Embassy in China, Seleznev, the representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, and Feng Wenbin, Liao Chengzhi, and Jiang Nanxiang from the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League. Attending from the host side were Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Shen Yanbing, Qian Junrui, Xiao San, Li Dequan, Ding Zan, Zhou Yang, and others. Chairman Guo Moruo and Head of Delegation Mikhailov exchanged toasts during the banquet. The guests and hosts frequently rose to toast "The friendship between the great peoples of China and the Soviet Union," "Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people," "Marshal Stalin, the great leader of the working people of the world," "Eternal friendship and cooperation between China and the Soviet Union," and "Lasting world peace." The banquet proceeded in an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere. After the banquet, an evening party was held, with the Beijing People's Art Theatre performing music and dance programs. The guests and hosts enjoyed themselves until 9:30 p.m. Some members of the Soviet Youth Delegation took a car to Shenyang and other places in Northeast China for sightseeing after the meeting that night. ### 文教委员会等单位欢宴苏联青年代表团

1950-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　文教委员会等单位欢宴苏联青年代表团
    【新华社十八日讯】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会、中苏友好协会总会、中国保卫世界和平大会委员会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会于十八日晚联合欢宴苏联青年代表团。出席的有以米哈依洛夫为首的苏联青年代表团和苏联青年文工团三十八人；应邀作陪的有苏联驻华大使馆参事费德林、齐赫文，苏联对外文化协会驻华代表谢列金及中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会冯文彬、廖承志、蒋南翔等。主人方面出席的有李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、沈雁冰、钱俊瑞、萧三、李德全、丁瓒、周扬等。席间郭沫若主任委员和米哈依洛夫团长曾互致祝词；宾主并频频起立，为“中苏两国伟大人民的友谊”、“中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东主席”、“世界劳动人民伟大领袖斯大林大元帅”、“中苏两国永远友好合作”、“世界持久和平”干杯。宴会在极热烈友好的气氛中进行。欢宴后举行晚会，由北京人民艺术剧院表演音乐、舞蹈节目。至夜九时半宾主始尽欢而散。苏联青年代表团一部分人员，当晚于会后搭车赴东北沈阳等地参观。（附图片）
