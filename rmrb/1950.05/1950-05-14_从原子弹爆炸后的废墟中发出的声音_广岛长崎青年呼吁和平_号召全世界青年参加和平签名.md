Title: Voices from the Ruins of the Atomic Bomb: Hiroshima and Nagasaki Youth Appeal for Peace, Calling on Youth Worldwide to Participate in Peace Signatures
Author:
Date: 1950-05-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 11th (TASS): The World Federation of Democratic Youth has submitted an appeal from the youth of Hiroshima and Nagasaki (the two cities bombed by atomic bombs during the war) to various newspapers for publication.

The appeal states: "We are issuing an appeal to the youth of the world from the ruins left by the atomic bomb. Five years have passed since this land was scorched by the atomic bomb. Although the flames and smoke rising after the atomic bomb explosion have disappeared, a hatred for the atomic bomb, even stronger than in the past, continues to exist in our hearts. The atomic bomb suddenly killed our parents, brothers, and sisters all at once."

The appeal continues: "If we do not take resolute action, if the people of all countries around the world do not preemptively defeat the imperialists' conspiracies, the world will experience the suffering of Hiroshima and Nagasaki."

The youth of Hiroshima and Nagasaki appeal to young men and women around the world, asking them to sign the World Peace Congress Standing Committee's peace appeal.

### 从原子弹爆炸后的废墟中发出的声音　广岛长崎青年呼吁和平　号召全世界青年参加和平签名

1950-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　从原子弹爆炸后的废墟中发出的声音
　　广岛长崎青年呼吁和平
　　号召全世界青年参加和平签名
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社巴黎十一日讯：世界民主青年联盟已将日本广岛和长崎两个城市（按该两城在战时曾受原子弹轰炸）的青年的呼吁书送交各报发表。
    呼吁书称：“我们从原子弹爆炸后所留下的废墟中，向全世界的青年们发出呼吁。自从这块土地被原子弹轰炸焚焦以来，已经过去了五年。原子弹爆炸后上升的烈焰和乌烟虽然已经消逝，然而在我们的心坎中继续存在着一种甚至比过去更加强烈的对原子弹的痛恨。原子弹突然把我们的父母、兄弟和姊妹们一齐杀死了。”
    呼吁书继称：“如果我们不坚决地采取行动，如果全世界各国人民不预先击破帝国主义者的阴谋，世界就会经历到广岛和长崎的苦难。”
    广岛和长崎的青年们，向全世界的男女青年呼吁，要求他们在世界拥护和平大会常委会的和平呼吁书上签名。
