Title: First National Higher Education Conference Scheduled to Open on June 1st; Preparatory Meetings to Be Held Today and Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-05-30
Page: 1

　　First National Higher Education Conference
    Scheduled to Open on June 1st
    Preparatory Meetings to Be Held Today and Tomorrow
    [Report from this newspaper] The first National Higher Education Conference, convened by the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, began registration on the 28th. Representatives from the Northeast, East China, Central South, Southwest, and units such as Peking University, Tsinghua University, Agricultural University, Yenching University, Central Academy of Drama, and the Central People's Government News Administration in Beijing have already registered.

    Preparatory meetings are scheduled for the 30th and 31st, during which representatives from various regions will report on problems and opinions in higher education work. The conference will open at 9:00 AM on June 1st, with an opening address by Ma Xulun, Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, and speeches by attending leaders and guests. In the afternoon, Vice Minister Qian Junrui will deliver a report. On the 2nd, the opening address and report will be discussed in regional groups. From the 3rd to the 6th, conference proposals will be discussed in groups. On the 7th and 8th, the results of group discussions will be submitted to the conference for discussion. On the morning of the 9th, a summary report will be given, and the conference will adjourn in the afternoon.

### 首次全国高等教育会议订六月一日开幕  今明两日开预备会

1950-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　首次全国高等教育会议
    订六月一日开幕
    今明两日开预备会
    【本报讯】中央人民政府教育部即将召开的第一次全国高等教育会议，于二十八日开始报到，报到者已有东北、华东、中南、西南等地区及京市北大、清华、农大、燕京、中央戏剧学院、中央人民政府新闻总署等单位代表。
    预备会预定三十、三十一两日举行，由各地区代表报告高等教育工作中的问题及意见。六月一日上午九时大会开幕，将由中央人民政府教育部马叙伦部长致开幕词及到会首长来宾讲话，下午由钱俊瑞副部长作报告；二日将按地区分组讨论开幕词及报告；三日至六日分组讨论大会提案，七日至八日将各组讨论结果提交大会讨论；九日上午总结报告，下午闭会。
