Title: First Quarter Production Target Exceeded by Beijing Postal District
Author:
Date: 1950-05-10
Page: 3

　　Beijing Postal District Exceeds First Quarter Production Target
　　[Report from this newspaper] The original production target for Beijing Postal District for the first quarter of this year was 5,742,498,020 yuan. After the specific figures were allocated to each bureau, the bureaus successively formulated plans to guarantee the completion of the task. According to statistics from the Beijing Postal Administration, the first quarter's production task was not only successfully completed but also exceeded the original plan. The amount reached 8,827,899,259.29 yuan, exceeding the original amount by 53.7 percent (the amount of rice reached 9,032,860.1 jin, exceeding the original amount by 10.1 percent).
　　(Beijing Postal Bureau Social Service Department)

### 第一季生产任务北京邮区超额完成

1950-05-10
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　第一季生产任务北京邮区超额完成
    【本报讯】北京邮区本年度第一季生产任务原订为五、七四二、四九八、○二○元，自将具体数字分配到各局去以后，各局纷纷拟订计划，保证完成任务。兹经北京邮政管理局统计，第一季生产任务不但顺利完成，而且超过原订计划，计款数达成八、八二七、八九九、二五九·二九元，较原数额超过百分之五三·七（米数达成九、○三二、八六○·一斤，较原数额超过百分之十·一）。
      （北京邮局社会服务股）
