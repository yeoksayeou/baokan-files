Title: Second Session of the People's Representative Conference of All Circles: Consultation Committee Holds Meeting, Passes Draft of Organizational Rules for People's Representative Conferences of All Circles
Author:
Date: 1950-05-08
Page: 3
　　
　　Beijing City News
　　
　　Second Session of the People's Representative Conference of All Circles:
    Consultation Committee Holds Meeting
    Passes Draft of Organizational Rules for People's Representative Conferences of All Circles

    [Beijing City News Agency Dispatch] The Consultation Committee of the Second Session of the People's Representative Conference of All Circles in Beijing held its fifth meeting yesterday (the 7th). The meeting was presided over by Peng Zhen, Chairman of the Consultation Committee. According to the authority granted by the Second Session of the Second Representative Conference, the committee discussed and revised the "Draft Organizational Rules for People's Representative Conferences of All Circles in the Urban Area of Beijing," which was drafted by the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and it was unanimously approved by all members. The meeting then heard a summary report on the promotion of public bonds by Vice Mayor Wu Han, Director of the Beijing Municipal Public Bond Promotion Committee, and a report on the work of clearing accumulated cases by Wang Feiran, President of the Beijing Municipal People's Court. After reporting and analyzing the current situation of accumulated cases in the court, President Wang stated: At present, approximately 200 cadres have been transferred from various departments, and it is decided that general accumulated cases will be cleared within one month. Finally, the meeting exchanged views on the "Draft Sentencing Standards for Various Civil and Criminal Cases" drafted by the Beijing Municipal People's Court.

### 第二届各界人民代表会  协商委会举行会议  通过各界人代会组织通则草案

1950-05-08
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　第二届各界人民代表会
    协商委会举行会议
    通过各界人代会组织通则草案
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市第二届各界人民代表会议协商委员会昨（七）日举行第五次会议，会议由协商委员会主席彭真主持，根据第二届第二次代表会议所授权力，讨论和修正了北京市人民政府所拟订的《北京市区各界人民代表会议组织通则草案》，并经全体委员一致通过。会议继听取了北京市公债推销委员会主任委员吴晗副市长关于推销公债的总结报告和京市人民法院王斐然院长关于清理积案工作的报告。王院长报告和分析了目前法院积案的情况后称：目前已从各方面抽调干部约二百人，决于一个月内将一般积案清理完毕。最后会议就京市人民法院所拟订的《各种民事、刑事案件处刑标准草案》交换了意见。
