Title: Cooperative Handicraft Products on Display at Zhongshan Park
Author: Wang Yi
Date: 1950-05-23
Page: 3

　　[News from the Beijing Municipal News Agency] In order to broadly gather opinions from all sides and further improve technology and quality, the Beijing Municipal Production Cooperatives and Women's Handicraft Factories jointly held a handicraft product exhibition at the Altar of Land and Grain in Zhongshan Park starting on the 21st. Products are also being sold at low prices, including shoes, clothing, electrical appliances, towels, cotton textiles, knitted goods, drawnwork embroidery, carpets, toothbrushes, carved toys, etc. The exhibition is scheduled to last for one week. (Wang Yi)

### 合作社手工业产品在中山公园展览
王绎
1950-05-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　合作社手工业产品在中山公园展览
    【京市新闻处讯】京市各生产合作社、妇女手工厂，为广泛吸取各方面的意见，进一步改进技术，提高质量，二十一日起在中山公园社稷坛联合举行手工业产品展览会，并廉价销售产品，种类有鞋、服装、电器、毛巾、棉织品、编织品、挑补花、地毯、牙刷、雕刻玩具等。预定展览一周。（王绎）
