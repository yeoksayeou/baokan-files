Title: Automobile Company Recruits Drivers
Author:
Date: 1950-05-19
Page: 3

　　Automobile Company Recruits Drivers
    [This Newspaper's News] The business of the Beijing Public Bus Company has gradually expanded, with the number of vehicles and routes increasing compared to last month. In order to complete the plan of one hundred vehicles on the road in the short term, it has been decided to recruit forty more drivers. Registration will take place on the 19th and 20th of this month, and examinations will begin on the 21st. Any unemployed driver under the age of forty, in good health, and with a formal driver's license may register and take the examination with a letter of introduction from the District People's Government, except for those who have already taken the examination and were not admitted. They should go to the company's office at Donghuamen to register for the examination.
                                        (Automobile Company Communications Group)

### 汽车公司招考司机

1950-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　汽车公司招考司机
    【本报讯】北京市公共汽车公司业务已逐渐开展，出车数量及行驶路线均已较上月增加。为要在短期内完成出车百辆计划决定再行添招司机四十余名，定于本月十九日及二十日报名，二十一日起开始考试。凡年龄不满四十岁体格健全有正式驾驶执照的失业司机，除已参加过考试未曾录取者不准报名外，其余均可持区人民政府介绍信前往东华门该公司报名应考。
                                        （汽车公司通讯组）
