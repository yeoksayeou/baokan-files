Title: Beijing Holds Cadre Conference to Celebrate May Day; Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi Speaks at the Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1950-05-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 30th—To celebrate the May 1st International Labor Day, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference convened a cadre commemoration meeting in Beijing at 3:30 PM on April 29th, with more than 4,500 people attending. At the top of the rostrum of the meeting hall hung portraits of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, with a portrait of Chairman Mao Zedong and the national flag of the People's Republic of China in the center. The meeting was presided over by Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai of the National Committee. At the beginning of the meeting, the band played the national anthem of the People's Republic of China and the Internationale. Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Central People's Government delivered a long speech at the meeting.

### 北京举行干部大会庆祝五一劳动节  刘少奇副主席在会上讲话

1950-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京举行干部大会庆祝五一劳动节
    刘少奇副主席在会上讲话
    【新华社三十日讯】为庆祝五一国际劳动节，中国人民政协全国委员会在四月二十九日下午三时半，在北京召集了四千五百余人的干部纪念大会。大会主席台上端高悬马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林画像，正中悬有毛泽东主席像和中华人民共和国国旗。大会由全国委员会周恩来副主席任主席。大会开始时，乐队高奏中华人民共和国国歌和国际歌。中央人民政府刘少奇副主席在会上作了长篇讲话。
