Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Other Organizations Telegraph Australian Federal Parliament to Protest Australian Government's Anti-Communist Bill
Author:
Date: 1950-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation have jointly telegraphed the Australian Federal Parliament to protest the anti-Communist bill submitted by the Australian Prime Minister for discussion. The telegram is as follows: Speaker of the House of Representatives of the Australian Federal Parliament, Mr. Cameron:
	We firmly protest the bill submitted by Australian Prime Minister Menzies on April 27 for discussion in Parliament, which declares the Australian Communist Party "illegal," and demand that you reject its passage. The passage of this bill will subject the Australian government and Parliament to the condemnation of the entire democratic world.
		All-China Federation of Trade Unions
		All-China Federation of Democratic Youth
		All-China Democratic Women's Federation
		May 6, 1950

### 全总等团体电澳联邦议会  抗议澳政府反共法案

1950-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总等团体电澳联邦议会
    抗议澳政府反共法案
    【新华社六日讯】中华全国总工会、中华全国民主青年联合会、中华全国民主妇女联合会顷共同致电澳大利亚联邦议会，抗议澳总理提交该会讨论的反共法案。电文如下：澳大利亚联邦议会下院卡米郎议长：
    我们坚决抗议澳总理孟席斯四月二十七日提交议会讨论的宣布澳共“非法”的法案，并要求你们拒绝通过它。通过这一法案将使澳政府和议会受到整个民主世界的责难。
    中华全国总工会
    中华全国民主青年联合会
    中华全国民主妇女联合会
    一九五零年五月六日
