Title: National Women's Federation Schedules August Meeting to Discuss Urban Women's Work
Author:
Date: 1950-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The Standing Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation has decided to convene the Third Executive Committee and a joint conference of directors of women's federations from major administrative regions, provinces, and cities in Beijing this August. The central task of the meeting is to summarize the experiences and lessons learned from urban women's work over the past year, and to discuss the specific guidelines, tasks, and working methods for urban women's work in the future. Discussions will focus on the following aspects: 1. The central content of urban women's work and the issues of working methods. 2. The issue of continuing to consolidate and expand the democratic united front of women from all sectors. 3. The organizational forms of urban women's mass organizations. In addition to all members and alternate members of the National Democratic Women's Federation Executive Committee, heads of women's federations at all levels in major administrative regions, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, administrative offices, provinces, and cities, as well as women's organizations such as the Chinese Women's Friendship Association, the National Association of the Chinese Young Women's Christian Association, and the Chinese Women's Temperance Union, will each send one person to participate. In addition, the National Women's Federation has also decided that all units participating in the meeting must summarize their work based on the aforementioned agenda, submit their opinions, and send them in writing to the National Women's Federation by the end of June. Among them, the women's federations of Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang, Shanghai, Xi'an, as well as the Lushun-Dalian area, Henan, and Chahar provinces, in addition to conducting work summaries, have also been assigned by the National Women's Federation to focus on several issues that must be studied in depth.

### 全国妇联定八月召开会议讨论城市妇女工作

1950-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国妇联定八月召开会议讨论城市妇女工作
    【新华社十七日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会常委会已决定今年八月在北京召开第三次执行委员会及大行政区、省、市妇联主任联席会议。会议中心任务为总结一年来大、中城市妇女工作的经验教训，讨论今后城市妇女工作的具体方针任务和工作作法。讨论将着重于下列几方面：一、城市妇女工作的中心内容及工作的方式方法问题。二、继续巩固和扩大各界妇女的民主统一战线问题。三、城市妇女群众的组织形式问题。出席会议的除全国民主妇联全体执委及后补执委外，各大行政区、内蒙自治区、各行署区、省、市各级妇联以及中国妇女联谊会、中华基督教女青年会全国协会、中华妇女节制会等妇女团体将各派负责人一人参加。此外，全国妇联并决定：凡参加会议的各单位必须根据前述议程总结工作，并提出意见，于六月底前书面汇寄全国妇联，其中北京、天津、沈阳、上海、西安等市及旅大区、河南、察哈尔省妇联，除进行工作总结外，并由全国妇联分别指定了若干必须着重研究的问题。
