Title: Municipal People's Government Holds Garden Party for Cadres
Author:
Date: 1950-05-19
Page: 3

　　The Municipal People's Government Holds Garden Party for Cadres
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] In order to strengthen the cultural and recreational activities of cadres, the General Club of the Beijing Municipal People's Government has decided to hold a garden party for cadres of the Municipal Government at Zhongshan Park from 2:00 PM to 8:00 PM on the 21st (Sunday). All staff members of units directly under the Municipal Government can enter the venue upon presentation of their credentials. The activity center will be located in the Shejitan (Altar of Earth and Grain) and Xingjianhui sports field in the park. The program includes short performances, basketball and tug-of-war competitions, games, singing and dancing, and other activities.
                    (Municipal Government Correspondent Group)

### 市人民政府举行干部游园会

1950-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　市人民政府举行干部游园会
    【本报讯】北京市人民政府总俱乐部为加强干部文娱活动，定于二十一日（星期日）下午二时至八时在中山公园举行市府干部游园会，凡市府直辖各单位工作人员届时均可凭证章入场，活动中心在公园社稷坛及行健会球场等地，内容有精短节目表演，篮球、拔河等项比赛，游戏、歌舞等。
                    （市府通讯组）
