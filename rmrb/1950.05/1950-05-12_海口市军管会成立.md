Title: Haikou Military Control Commission Established
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 1

　　Haikou Military Control Commission Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, Qiongya, 11th] The Haikou City Military Control Commission of the Hainan Administrative Region was formally established on May 1st upon the order of the Guangdong Military Region of the People's Liberation Army. Various military control tasks are underway.

### 海口市军管会成立

1950-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　海口市军管会成立
    【新华社琼崖十一日电】海南行政区海口市军事管制委员会已奉人民解放军广东军区的命令，于五月一日正式成立。各项军管工作正进行中。
