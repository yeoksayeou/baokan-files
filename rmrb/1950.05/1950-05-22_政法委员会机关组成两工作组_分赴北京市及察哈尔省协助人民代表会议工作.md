Title: Political and Legal Affairs Committee Organizes Two Work Groups to Assist People's Representative Conferences in Beijing and Chahar Province
Author:
Date: 1950-05-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 21st: The Political and Legal Affairs Committee of the Government Administration Council, in accordance with the resolutions of the fifth and eighth committee meetings, has determined that its central task for the second quarter of this year is to inspect, research, and guide the work of urban and rural People's Representative Conferences or People's Congresses. To this end, the various organs under the Political and Legal Affairs Committee have formed two work groups (comprising 71 members from the Political and Legal Affairs Committee, 14 from the Ministry of Internal Affairs, 3 from the Ministry of Justice, 8 from the Ministry of Public Security, and 4 from the Nationalities Affairs Commission, totaling 100 members), which will go to Beijing and Chahar Province, respectively, to participate in and assist in the work of the People's Representative Conferences or People's Congresses, focusing on key areas in conjunction with local cadres. The urban group, consisting of 40 people, went to the sixth and seventh districts of Beijing on the 14th to begin work; the rural group, consisting of 60 people, departed for Chahar on the 20th.

### 政法委员会机关组成两工作组  分赴北京市及察哈尔省协助人民代表会议工作

1950-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　政法委员会机关组成两工作组
    分赴北京市及察哈尔省协助人民代表会议工作
    【新华社二十一日讯】政务院政治法律委员会依照第五次委员会议及第八次委务会议的决议，拟定本年第二季中心工作为检查、研究、指导城乡人民代表会议或人民代表大会的工作。为此，政治法律委员会所属各机关已组成两个工作组（由政法委员会七十一人、内务部十四人、司法部三人、公安部八人、民族事务委员会四人，共一百人组成），分赴北京市及察哈尔省，结合地方干部，有重点地参加和协助人民代表会议或人民代表大会的工作。城市组共四十人已于十四日前往京市六、七两区开始工作；乡村组共六十人，已于二十日赴察。
