Title: "China Month" Exhibits Set Off for Swift Transportation

Author: 
Date: 1950-05-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. Fifty boxes of all the exhibits from our country's participation in Czechoslovakia's "China Month" at the invitation of the Czechoslovak government, were dispatched on the 22nd, with personnel from the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government responsible for transporting them to Czechoslovakia. The exhibits are divided into seven categories: pictures, modern art, special handicrafts, books and periodicals, films, records, and classical art.

### “中国月”展览品启程运捷

1950-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　“中国月”展览品启程运捷
    【新华社二十二日讯】我国应捷克斯洛伐克之请参加该国“中国月”之全部展览品五十箱，于二十二日由中央人民政府文化部对外文化联络事务局派员负责运送赴捷。该项展览品分：图片、现代美术品、特种手工艺品、书籍报刊、电影片、唱片、古典艺术品七大类。
