Title: Film "The Lyuliang Mountains" to Reshoot Battle Scenes - Please Do Not Be Alarmed by Gunfire
Author:
Date: 1950-05-18
Page: 3

　　Film "The Lyuliang Mountains" to Reshoot Battle Scenes
　　Please Do Not Be Alarmed by Gunfire
　　[Our Newspaper] The Beijing Film Studio's feature film "The Lyuliang Mountains" still requires several battle scenes to be reshot in the city from the 18th to the 24th of this month. Gunfire may be heard at that time. We hope the public will not be alarmed.

### 影片《吕梁山上》补拍战斗场面  闻枪声勿惊扰

1950-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　影片《吕梁山上》补拍战斗场面
　  闻枪声勿惊扰
    【本报讯】北京电影制片厂摄制之艺术片《吕梁山上》，尚有若干战斗场面须于本月十八日至二十四日夜在本市进行拍摄，届时可闻枪声，望勿惊扰。
