Title: Four Million People in North Korea Have Signed Peace Petition; Mongolia and India Vigorously Launch Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1950-05-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 12th: The peace signature campaign is being vigorously carried out in various parts of North Korea. As of May 7th, 3.97 million people out of North Korea's 13 million population have signed the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. Among them, over 238,000 people in Pyongyang alone have signed. Participants include workers, farmers, staff, students, professors, scientific workers, writers, artists, cultural workers, and pastors. Simultaneously with the signature campaign, leaders of various democratic parties and social organizations in Korea, as well as well-known model workers, have made speeches supporting the peace appeal, unanimously believing that the movement to defend world peace is closely linked to the struggle for Korean reunification, independence, and national sovereignty.

Xinhua News Agency, May 12th: According to TASS, Ulaanbaatar, May 10th: The campaign to sign the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress is proceeding smoothly in Mongolia amidst a great political upsurge. In various enterprises and educational institutions in the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, 85 mass meetings have been held since the beginning of May. Attendees unanimously expressed their determination to fight together with the powerful peace camp headed by the Soviet Union for peace, democracy, and socialism. The faculty and students of the Mongolian State University and the Advanced Cadre School of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the miners and other employees of the Nalaikh Coal Mine, the workers of the Stalin Meat Packing Plant, and railway employees have all signed the appeal. In the capital, more than 30,000 people have signed the appeal. Mongolian herders are also enthusiastically participating in the signing. Peace committees have been established in various parts of the country.

Xinhua News Agency, May 12th: According to TASS, Delhi: The Standing Peace Committee of the United Provinces of India recently announced the results of the provincial peace propaganda week, which ended on May 1st, stating that the All-India Trade Union Congress, women's groups, and intellectuals actively participated in this movement for peace. During the Peace Propaganda Week, four mass meetings were held in Delhi, with approximately 25,000 people signing the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. Mass meetings were also held in Lucknow, where 2,500 people signed the peace appeal. In many parts of the United Provinces, peace advocates have been persecuted by the police. (Attached picture)

### 北朝鲜四百万人已参加和平签名　蒙、印热烈展开签名运动

1950-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜四百万人已参加和平签名
　　蒙、印热烈展开签名运动
    【新华社平壤十二日电】北朝鲜各地的和平签名运动，正在热烈开展中。截至五月七日止，北朝鲜的一千三百万人口中，已有三百九十七万人在世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的呼吁书上签了名。其中，仅平壤市签名者即达二十三万八千余人。参加签名者包括：工人、农民、职员、学生、教授、科学工作者、作家、艺术家、文化工作者及牧师等人。与签名运动同时，朝鲜各民主党派社会团体的领袖和著名的模范工人，曾发表拥护和平呼吁书的谈话，他们一致认为保卫世界和平运动是与争取朝鲜统一、独立和国家主权的斗争密切联系着的。
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托尔十日讯：蒙古各地正在伟大的政治高潮中顺利进行着在世界和平大会常委会呼吁书上签名的运动。在蒙古人民共和国首都的各企业与教育机关中，五月初以来已举行了八十五次群众大会，到会者一致表示，决心与以苏联为首的强大的和平阵营一起，共同为和平、民主与社会主义而奋斗。蒙古国立大学和蒙古人民革命党中央委员会主办的高级干部学校的全体师生、那莱哈煤矿的矿工和其他职工、斯大林肉类包装厂的工人以及铁路员工等，都已在呼吁书上签了名。在首都，已有三万多人在呼吁书上签名。蒙古的畜牧者也热情地参加了签名。全国各地均已纷纷成立了和平委员会。
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社德里讯：印度联合省常设和平委员会顷发表于五月一日结束的该省和平宣传周的成绩说：全印职工大会、妇女团体、知识分子都积极地参加了这一争取和平的运动。在和平宣传周中，德里曾举行了四次群众大会，约有二万五千人在世界拥护和平大会常委会的呼吁书上签了名。在勒克瑙也同样举行了群众大会，并有二千五百人在和平呼吁书上签名。在联合省的许多地方，和平拥护者受到了警察的迫害。（附图片）
