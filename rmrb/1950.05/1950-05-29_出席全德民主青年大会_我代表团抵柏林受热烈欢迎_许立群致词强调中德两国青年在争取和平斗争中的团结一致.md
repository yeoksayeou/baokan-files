Title: Attending the All-German Democratic Youth Congress, Our Delegation Arrives in Berlin and Receives a Warm Welcome; Xu Liqun Delivers a Speech Emphasizing the Solidarity of Chinese and German Youth in the Struggle for Peace
Author: 
Date: 1950-05-29
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 27th: The Chinese youth delegation attending the All-German Democratic Youth Congress has arrived in Berlin and received an extremely warm welcome from German youth. Representatives of the Free German Youth League personally drove four new cars on the dark night of the 24th from Berlin to Bad Schandau, the first station on the German-Czech border, to welcome the Chinese youth delegation; and on the evening of the 25th, they brought them to Berlin.

　　When the five-member Chinese youth delegation passed through the cities of Pirna and Dresden, thousands of German youths gathered and shouted "Congratulations to the Youth of the Victorious New China." The mayors of the two cities and the chairmen of the Socialist Unity Party committees also held receptions to welcome them. Young Pioneers and farmers lined the roads to pay tribute to the delegation, cheering "Mao Zedong" and throwing flowers into the representatives' cars.

　　On the border of Greater Berlin, twenty kilometers from the city center, Erich Honecker, Chairman of the Free German Youth League, and the secretaries of the Central Secretariat of the Free German Youth League came to greet the delegation. Honecker delivered a welcoming speech, saying: "The Free German Youth will never act as cannon fodder for the war-mongering American and British imperialists. The youth of the German Democratic Republic under the leadership of President Pieck, and the democratic youth of occupied West Germany, will fight for the democracy and unification of Germany and will always stand with the Soviet Union." Xu Liqun, head of the Chinese youth delegation, gave a reply, emphasizing the solidarity of Chinese and German youth in the struggle for peace.

　　It is reported that more than 30 fraternal delegations will attend the All-German Democratic Youth Congress. A 13-member delegation from the Soviet Komsomol and a Soviet youth art troupe of about 200 people will soon arrive in Berlin.

### 出席全德民主青年大会  我代表团抵柏林受热烈欢迎  许立群致词强调中德两国青年在争取和平斗争中的团结一致

1950-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　出席全德民主青年大会
    我代表团抵柏林受热烈欢迎
    许立群致词强调中德两国青年在争取和平斗争中的团结一致
    【新华社柏林二十七日电】出席全德民主青年大会的中国青年代表团，现已抵达柏林，受到德国青年极其热情的欢迎。自由德国青年联盟的代表们，在二十四日漆黑的晚上，曾亲自驾驶着四辆新车，从柏林赶赴德捷边境的第一个车站杉道温泉去欢迎中国青年代表团；并于二十五日傍晚时分将他们接到柏林。
    当中国青年代表团一行五人，途经皮尔纳和德累斯顿两城时，数千德国青年曾聚集起来，高呼“祝贺新中国胜利的青年”。两城的市长和统一社会党委员会的主席，也举行酒会欢迎他们。青年、少年先锋队员和农民曾排列在公路两旁，向代表团致敬，他们欢呼着“毛泽东”，并把鲜花投入行进中的代表们的坐车中。
    在大柏林市区的边境上，离市中心尚有二十公里的地方，自由德国青年联盟主席昂纳克及自由德国青年联盟中央书记处的书记们都来迎接代表团。昂纳克并致欢迎词说：“自由德国青年永远不会替美英帝国主义战争贩子充当炮灰。在皮克总统领导下的德意志民主共和国青年，以及在占领下的西德的民主青年，要为德国的民主、统一而奋斗，并将永远和苏联站在一起。”中国青年代表团团长许立群致答词，他着重指出中德两国青年在争取和平斗争中的团结一致。
    据悉：出席全德民主青年大会的，将有三十余国的兄弟代表团。苏联共青团的十三人代表团和约二百人的苏联青年艺术演出队，即将来到柏林。
