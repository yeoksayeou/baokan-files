Title: Trolleybuses on All Routes Leave Depots Earlier
Author:
Date: 1950-05-08
Page: 3

Beijing News

　　Trolleybuses on All Routes Leave Depots Earlier
    [News from the Beijing News Agency] Recently, the city's trolleybus company, due to the gradual lengthening of daylight hours, decided to adopt a summer timetable starting from May 7th. All vehicles on all routes will leave the depot earlier at 5 o'clock. The departure times for the first and last buses on each route are as follows:
    Route 1
    Tianqiao: First bus 5:20 AM, Last bus 12:39 AM;
    Xizhimen: First bus 5:03 AM, Last bus 12:18 AM.
    Route 2
    Tianqiao: First bus 5:22 AM, Last bus 11:53 PM;
    Beixinqiao: First bus 5:36 AM, Last bus 12:13 AM.
    Circular Route
    Ping'anli: First bus 5:11 AM, Last bus 11:47 PM;
    Dongdan: First bus 5:56 AM, Last bus 12:29 AM.
    Route 5
    Xuanwumen: First bus 5:57 AM, Last bus 11:07 PM;
    Chongwenmen: First bus 5:58 AM, Last bus 11:08 PM.
    Route 6
    Chongwenmen: First bus 6:12 AM, Last bus 10:56 PM;
    Caishikou: First bus 5:53 AM, Last bus 11:29 PM.
    Route 7
    Tianqiao: First bus 5:50 AM, Last bus 10:46 PM;
    Yongdingmen: First bus 5:57 AM, Last bus 10:53 PM.

### 各路电车提早出厂

1950-05-08
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　各路电车提早出厂
    【京市新闻处讯】本市电车公司近以日咎渐长，决定自五月七日起改用夏季行车时间，所有各路车辆一律提早于五点出厂。各路首、末车开行时间如下：
    第一路
    天桥：首车五点廾分，末车廾二点卅九分；
    西直门：首车五点零三分，末车廾二点十八分。
    第二路
    天桥：首车五点廾二分，末车廾一点五十三分；
    北新桥：首车五点卅六分，末车廾二点十三分。
    环行路
    平安里：首车五点十一分，末车廾一点四十七分；
    东单：首车五点五十六分，末车廾二点廾九分。
    第五路
    宣武门：首五五点五十七分，末车廾一点○七分；
    崇文门：首车五点五十八分，末车廾一点○八分。
    第六路
    崇文门：首车六点十二分，末车廾点五十六分；
    菜市口：首车五点五十三分，末车廾一点廾九分。
    第七路
    天桥：首车五点五十分，末车廾点四十六分；
