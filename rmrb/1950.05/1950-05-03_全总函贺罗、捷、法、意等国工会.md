Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Letters of Congratulations to Trade Unions in Romania, Czechoslovakia, France, Italy, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1950-05-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent letters of congratulations on May 1st Labor Day to the trade unions of Romania, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Poland, Albania, Bulgaria, Mongolia, France, Italy, Japan, India, Pakistan, Ceylon, Korea, and Vietnam.

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions' congratulatory letter to the trade unions of Romania, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Poland, Albania, Bulgaria, and Mongolia stated: As the Chinese working people welcome their first May Day International Labor Day after the establishment of their own country with unparalleled joy, we extend our warmest fraternal greetings to you! Every achievement you make in production and construction will be a model for Chinese workers to learn from, a source of strength for the world's peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, and a serious blow to war provocateurs. We wish our common struggle for lasting peace and people's democracy new victories!

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions' congratulatory letter to the trade unions of France, Italy, Japan, India, Pakistan, Ceylon, Korea, and Vietnam stated: On this festival when the working people of the world review their strength, we extend our militant greetings to you! The liberated Chinese working class is watching every victory in your struggle with unparalleled concern. These victories will powerfully strike our common enemy—the international reactionaries—and strengthen the worldwide democratic and peaceful camp headed by the Soviet Union. Today, the Chinese working class must step up production and actively build its new country, so that the prosperity and strength of the motherland will enhance our strength in striving for lasting peace and opposing war provocateurs. May our common struggle for lasting peace and people's democracy win new victories.

### 全总函贺罗、捷、法、意等国工会

1950-05-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　全总函贺罗、捷、法、意等国工会
    【新华社二日讯】中华全国总工会在“五一”劳动节分别致函罗马尼亚、捷克、匈牙利、波兰、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、蒙古等国工会和法国、意大利、日本、印度、巴基斯坦、锡兰、朝鲜、越南等国工会祝贺。
    全国总工会致罗、捷、匈、波、阿尔巴尼亚、保、蒙古等国工会的贺函说：中国劳动人民以无比欢欣迎接建立自己国家后第一个五一国际劳动节的时候，谨向你们致热烈的兄弟的致礼！你们在生产建设中的每一个成就将是中国工人学习的榜样、将是全世界以苏联为首的和平民主阵营力量增长的源泉，将是对战争挑拨者的严重打击。祝我们争取持久和平与人民民主的共同斗争赢得新的胜利！
    全国总工会致法、意、日、印、巴基斯坦、锡兰、朝鲜、越南等国工会的贺函说：在这全世界劳动人民检阅自己力量的节日，谨向你们致战斗的敬礼！解放了的中国工人阶级正以无比的关切注视着你们斗争中的每一个胜利，这些胜利将强有力地打击我们共同的敌人——国际反动派，将加强以苏联为首的全世界民主和平阵营。中国的工人阶级今天当加紧生产，积极建设自己的新国家，以祖国的繁荣强大，增强我们争取持久和平反对战争挑拨者的力量。愿我们争取持久和平与人民民主的共同斗争赢得新的胜利。
