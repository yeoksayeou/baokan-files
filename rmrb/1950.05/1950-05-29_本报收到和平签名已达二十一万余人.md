Title: This Newspaper Has Received Peace Signatures From Over 210,000 People
Author:
Date: 1950-05-29
Page: 4

　　This Newspaper Has Received Peace Signatures From Over 210,000 People
    [This newspaper's dispatch] This newspaper received peace signatures from 14,062 people from various regions yesterday, bringing the total to 215,914 people.

### 本报收到和平签名已达二十一万余人

1950-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　本报收到和平签名已达二十一万余人
    【本报讯】本报昨天收到各地一万四千零六十二人的和平签名，连前共二十一万五千九百一十四人。
