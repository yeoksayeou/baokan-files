Title: Soviet Army and Navy Ministers Issue Orders Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of Victory Over Fascist Germany
Author:
Date: 1950-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th. TASS: Soviet Minister of the Army, Vasilevsky, issued an order on the 9th celebrating the fifth anniversary of the victory over Fascist Germany. The full text is as follows:

    Comrades, soldiers, sergeants, officers, and generals of the Soviet Army!

    Five years ago, the Soviet people and their armed forces, led by the Bolshevik Party, the great leader and brilliant strategist Comrade Stalin, achieved a landmark victory over German imperialism, safeguarding the freedom and independence of our socialist motherland, and rescuing the peoples of Europe from fascist tyranny.

    On this glorious fifth anniversary of the victory over Fascist Germany, I extend my respects and congratulations to you.

    To commemorate Victory Day, I order: Today, May 9th, in our homeland's capital, Moscow, in the capitals of the various Union Republics, and in the heroic cities of Kaliningrad, Rostov, Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, a thirty-gun salute shall be fired simultaneously.

    Long live the Soviet people and their heroic Soviet Army, who safeguard peace and the security of our motherland!

    Long live our Soviet government!

    Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union – our inspirer and organizer of victory!

    Long live our beloved leader and strategist, Generalissimo of the Soviet Union, Comrade Stalin!

    Eternal glory to the heroes who sacrificed their lives in the fight for the freedom and independence of our motherland!

    Minister of the Army of the Soviet Union

    Marshal of the Soviet Union Vasilevsky (signed)

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th. TASS: Soviet Minister of the Navy, Yumashev, issued an order on the 9th celebrating the fifth anniversary of the victory over Fascist Germany. The full text is as follows:

    Comrades, soldiers and sergeants of the Soviet Navy! Comrades, officers and generals of the Navy!

    Five years ago, the Soviet people and their armed forces, led by the Bolshevik Party, the great leader and brilliant strategist Comrade Stalin, won the greatest epoch-making victory over German imperialism, defended the freedom and independence of our socialist motherland, and liberated the peoples of Europe from the shackles of fascism.

    On this glorious fifth anniversary of the victory over Fascist Germany, I extend my respects and congratulations to you.

    Long live the great Soviet people and their Navy!

    Long live our Soviet government!

    Long live the party of Lenin and Stalin, our inspirer and organizer of victory!

    Long live our great leader, teacher, and strategist, Generalissimo of the Soviet Union, Comrade Stalin!

    Eternal glory to the heroes who sacrificed their lives in the fight for the freedom and independence of our motherland!

    Minister of the Navy of the Soviet Union

    Admiral Yumashev (signed)

### 苏陆军海军两部长发布命令庆祝战胜法西斯德国五周年

1950-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏陆军海军两部长发布命令庆祝战胜法西斯德国五周年
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联陆军部部长华西列夫斯基九日发布庆祝战胜法西斯德国五周年纪念日的命令，全文如下：苏联陆军的战士、士官、校尉和将军同志们！
    五年以前，由布尔什维克党、伟大的领袖和天才的战略家斯大林同志所领导的苏联人民及其武装部队，划时代地战胜了德国帝国主义，维护了我们社会主义祖国的自由与独立，并且把欧洲各国人民从法西斯暴政之下拯救出来。
    际此光辉的战胜法西斯德国的五周年纪念日，我谨向你们致敬并表示庆贺。
    为纪念胜利日，我命令：今天、五月九日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科，在各加盟共和国的首都，同时，在加里宁格勒、罗夫、列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、悉瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨等英雄城市鸣放礼炮三十响。
    守卫着和平与我们祖国的安全的苏联人民及其英勇的苏军万岁！
    我们的苏联政府万岁！
    苏联共产党——我们的胜利的感召者与组织者万岁！
    我们爱戴的领袖和战略家、苏联大元帅斯大林同志万岁！
    在为我们祖国的自由与独立的战斗中牺牲的英雄们永垂不朽！
    苏联陆军部部长
    苏联元帅华西列夫斯基（签字）
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联海军部部长尤马谢夫九日发布庆祝战胜法西斯德国五周年纪念日的命令，全文如下：苏联海军的战士和士官同志们！海军的校尉和将军同志们！
    由布尔什维克党、伟大的领袖和天才的战略家斯大林同志领导的苏联人民及其武装部队，五年以前，对德国帝国主义赢得了最伟大的划时代的胜利，保卫了我们社会主义祖国的自由和独立，并使欧洲的各国人民从法西斯主义的束缚下获得解放。
    际此光辉的战胜法西斯德国五周年纪念日，我谨向你们致敬，并表示庆贺。
    伟大的苏联人民及其海军万岁！
    我们的苏联政府万岁！
    我们的胜利的感召者和组织者—列宁和斯大林的党万岁！
    我们的伟大领袖、导师和战略家、苏联大元帅斯大林同志万岁！
    在为我们祖国的自由与独立而战斗中牺牲的英雄们永垂不朽！
    苏联海军部部长
    海军上将尤马谢夫（签字）
