Title: Beijing Second Cultural Center Exhibition: Wang Xiuluan, Liu Hulan Comic Strips
Author:
Date: 1950-05-18
Page: 3

　　Beijing Second Cultural Center Exhibition
　　Wang Xiuluan, Liu Hulan Comic Strips
    [From Our Newspaper] The Beijing Second Cultural Center is exhibiting comic strips of female labor hero Wang Xiuluan and female Communist Party member Liu Hulan, totaling over eighty pieces, starting from the 17th. The hours are from 9:30 AM to 5:00 PM. People from all walks of life are welcome to visit the center at the Qianmen Arrow Tower.
                                            (Second Cultural Center Communications Group)

### 京第二文化馆展览  王秀鸾刘胡兰连环画

1950-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　京第二文化馆展览
　  王秀鸾刘胡兰连环画
    【本报讯】京市第二文化馆自十七日起，展览女劳动英雄王秀鸾及女共产党员刘胡兰连环画，共计八十余副。时间为上午九时半至下午五时。欢迎各界人民前往前门箭楼该馆参观。
                                            （二馆通讯组）
