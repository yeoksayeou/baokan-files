Title: Our Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Czechoslovakia to Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of Czech Liberation; Received Warmly in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1950-05-07
Page: 4

　　Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of Czech Liberation
    Our Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Czechoslovakia
    Received Warmly in Moscow
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 6th] The Chinese delegation, led by Liu Ningyi, to participate in the celebrations for the Fifth Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia, arrived in Prague at 10:30 AM on the 5th, along with delegations from Korea, Mongolia, and Vietnam. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Czech Prime Minister Zápotocký, Deputy Prime Ministers Fierlinger and Svoboda, Minister of Foreign Trade Gregor, Minister of Domestic Trade Krajcir, Minister of Transport Petr, Minister of Health Projhaska, Assistant Secretary of the Czech Communist Party, Ťmíndel, Deputy Speakers of the National Assembly David and Spurna-Hodinova (who is also the Chairman of the Women's Federation), Vice Chairman of the Trade Union Confederation, Kořsky, and Chairman of the Youth League, Hrzal, among other representatives from various sectors, numbering fifty in total. The Czech Prime Minister delivered a welcoming speech. The heads of the Chinese, Mongolian, Korean, and Vietnamese delegations delivered replies in succession. The Internationale was then played, and the guard of honor was reviewed, creating an extremely solemn and enthusiastic atmosphere.
    Chinese representatives who had also gone to Poland and the German Democratic Republic to participate in the May Day celebrations arrived in Prague on the 5th as well.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th] After participating in the May Day celebrations in Moscow, the Chinese trade union delegation, led by Wang Yiqing with nine representatives, departed for the Hero City of Leningrad on the 3rd for sightseeing. Liu Ningyi, leading seven representatives, departed for Prague on the 4th to participate in the Fifth Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia on May 9th. During their stay in Moscow, the delegation received a warm welcome from the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions and the Soviet Textile Workers' Union, among other organizations, and visited various places and attended theatrical performances. On April 30th, during a visit to the Pavlovich Morozov Young Pioneer Palace, they saw a wall newspaper specifically researching China and a Young Pioneer group that publishes wall newspapers. These children requested that the representatives convey their heartfelt greetings from the Young Pioneers to Mao Zedong, Zhu De, and the children of China. On the eve of their departure for Prague, the delegation also attended a banquet at the Czechoslovak Embassy in the Soviet Union.

### 庆祝捷克解放五周年  我工会代表团抵捷  过莫斯科时曾备受欢迎

1950-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝捷克解放五周年
    我工会代表团抵捷
    过莫斯科时曾备受欢迎
    【新华社布拉格六日电】参加捷克斯洛伐克解放五周年庆典的中国代表团在团长刘宁一率领下，已于五日上午十时半与朝鲜、蒙古、越南等国代表团飞抵布拉格。赴机场欢迎的有捷总理萨波托斯基、副总理费林格与斯沃波达、对外贸易部长格里高尔、国内贸易部长克拉尹契、运输部长彼得、卫生部长普洛伊哈、捷共助理书记季明德尔、国民议会副议长达维德与副议长兼妇联会主席斯普尔纳——荷第诺娃、总工会副总书记科尔斯基、青年联盟主席赫兹勒等各界代表五十人。捷总理致欢迎词。中、蒙、朝、越各代表团团长相继致答词。旋即奏国际歌并检阅仪仗队，情绪极为庄严热烈。
    又分赴波兰及德意志民主共和国参加五一劳动节大会的中国代表，亦于五日先后抵布拉格。
    【新华社莫斯科六日电】中国工会代表团在参加莫斯科五一节庆典后，由王亦清率领的九名代表已于三日赴列宁格勒英雄城观光。刘宁一率领的七位代表于四日赴布拉格，参加五月九日捷克斯洛伐克解放五周年庆典。代表团在逗留莫斯科期间，曾受到全苏工会中央理事会及苏联纺织工人工会等团体的热烈欢迎，并曾往各处参观及观剧。代表团于四月三十日参观巴佛里克莫罗佐少先宫时，曾看到一个专门研究中国的墙报及出版墙报的少先小组。这些儿童们请求代表们把少先队的由衷的敬礼，转达毛泽东、朱德和中国的儿童们。代表团在赴布拉格前夕，并曾出席捷克斯洛伐克驻苏大使馆的宴会。
