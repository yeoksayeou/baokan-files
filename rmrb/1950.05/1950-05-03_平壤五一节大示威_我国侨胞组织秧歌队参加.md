Title: Pyongyang May Day Grand Parade: Our Overseas Chinese Organize Yangko Team to Participate

Author:

Date: 1950-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 1st. A total of 350,000 working people in Pyongyang, the capital of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, participated in today's parade and demonstration. At 10:00 a.m., a mass rally of 150,000 people was held in Kim Il-sung Square in the city center, celebrating the festival of the working people of the world with jubilation. Republic party, government, military, and civilian leaders, headed by Kim Il-sung, the leader of the Korean people, stood on the reviewing stand and accepted the cheers of the masses. Kim Chaek, Vice-Chairman of the Korean Cabinet, Choe Kyung-deok, Chairman of the General Federation of Trade Unions of North Korea, and Han Seol-ya, Chairman of the North Korean National Committee for the Defense of World Peace, successively delivered speeches, unanimously extending congratulations to all Korean people who are fighting for the unification, independence, and defense of world peace of their motherland, and calling on the Korean people to further launch the struggle against the reactionary rule of the US imperialists and their lackeys, the Syngman Rhee bandit gang, in South Korea.

After the speeches, the parade and demonstration began. The marching contingents of workers, students, staff, citizens, and suburban farmers held high the national flags and portraits of leaders of Korea, the Soviet Union, China, and other new democratic countries, and cheered the "May Day" slogans issued by the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front: "Long live the world peace camp headed by the Soviet Union!" "Long live the Democratic People's Republic of Korea!" "Long live General Kim Il-sung!" "Long live Marshal Stalin!"

More than 200 overseas Chinese in Pyongyang also organized a Yangko team to participate in the parade.

### 平壤五一节大示威  我国侨胞组织秧歌队参加

1950-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　平壤五一节大示威
    我国侨胞组织秧歌队参加
    【新华社平壤一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首都平壤，今天有三十五万劳动人民参加游行示威的行列。上午十时在市中心区金日成广场，举行了十五万人的群众大会，狂欢庆祝全世界劳动人民的节日。以朝鲜人民领袖金日成总理为首的共和国党政军民首长，站在检阅台上接受群众的欢呼。朝鲜部长会议副主席金策、北朝鲜职业总同盟委员长崔璟德、北朝鲜拥护世界和平全国委员会委员长韩雪野，相继发表演说，一致向为争取祖国统一、独立与保卫世界和平而斗争的全朝鲜人民致贺，并号召朝鲜人民进一步展开反对美帝国主义及其走狗李承晚匪帮在南朝鲜的反动统治的斗争。
    演说毕，游行示威即开始。工人、学生、职员、市民及郊区农民的游行队伍，高举着朝鲜、苏联、中国及其他新民主主义国家的国旗与领袖肖像，欢呼着朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会所发布的“五一”节口号：“以苏联为首的世界和平阵营万岁！”、“朝鲜民主主义人民共和国万岁！”“金日成将军万岁！”“斯大林大元帅万岁！”
    平壤市的我国侨胞二百余人，也组织了秧歌队参加游行。
