Title: China Peace Committee to Hold Mass Meeting Tomorrow to Launch Peace Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1950-05-13
Page: 1

　　China Peace Committee to Hold Mass Meeting Tomorrow
　　Launch Peace Signature Campaign
    【Xinhua News Agency, 12th】 The China Peace Committee, in response to the call of the Third Plenary Session of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, in addition to issuing a notice at the end of last month calling on the people of the whole country to widely launch a signature campaign on the appeal of the Peace Committee to ban atomic weapons, has now decided to hold a mass meeting at 1 p.m. on the 14th of this month in Zhongshan Park, Beijing, hosted by Guo Moruo, chairman of the committee, to actively launch a signature campaign in all circles of the capital.

### 我国和大委员会明天召开群众大会  开展和平签名运动

1950-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国和大委员会明天召开群众大会
　  开展和平签名运动
    【新华社十二日讯】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会为响应世界拥护和平大会常委会第三次全会的号召，除已于上月底发出通告号召全国人民广泛发动在和大常委会要求禁止原子武器的呼吁书上签名的运动以外，现又决定于本月十四日下午一时，由该委员会主席郭沫若主持，在北京中山公园召开群众大会，积极开展首都各界的签名运动。
