Title: Military and Government Personnel in Southwest China Donate to Unemployed Worker Brothers
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 10th. Military and government personnel in various parts of Southwest China have enthusiastically responded to the call of the three leaders, Liu Bocheng, He Long, and Deng Xiaoping, and have been actively donating cash and materials to help unemployed workers. According to incomplete statistics, at the beginning of this month, staff in Chongqing, Guiyang, Chengdu, and southern and northern Sichuan have donated over 270 million yuan and over 15,000 jin of rice. All staff of the Chongqing Municipal People's Government units are saving on food for a week, eating two meals of porridge and one meal of dry rice each day. Many cadres on the supply system have donated their full month's or half month's allowance. The Guizhou Provincial Government Military Region Logistics Department and more than twenty other people's organizations have unanimously decided to save one to two liang of rice per person per day from late April to the end of July to help unemployed workers. In addition, the Southwest Finance and Economics Committee has approved the Guizhou Provincial People's Government's allocation of one million jin of rice for organizing unemployed workers to renovate the Guiyang sewer system.

### 西南各地军政人员　捐助失业工人兄弟

1950-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　西南各地军政人员
　　捐助失业工人兄弟
    【新华社重庆十日电】西南各地军政机关工作人员，热烈响应刘伯承、贺龙、邓小平三首长的号召，纷纷捐献现款、实物援助失业工人。据不完全统计，本月初重庆、贵阳、成都和川南、川北等地的工作人员，共已捐款两亿七千余万元和食米一万五千余斤。重庆市人民政府各单位全体工作人员，均节食一星期，每日改吃两餐稀饭和一餐干饭。许多供给制干部捐出全月或半月的津贴。贵州省政府军区后勤部及其他人民团体二十余单位，一致决定自四月下旬起至七月底止，每人每日节约食米一两至二两，来救济失业工人。此外，西南财政经济委员会并已批准贵州省人民政府拨大米一百万斤，作为组织失业工人翻修贵阳下水道之用。
