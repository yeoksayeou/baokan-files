Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Secret Service Deploys, Plans to Strengthen Sabotage Activities on the Mainland
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 11th: According to documents seized and confessions from prisoners during the Hainan Campaign, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit secret service agency, "Bureau of Investigation and Statistics," convened a "Bureau of Investigation and Statistics Guangdong Province Senior Cadre Meeting" in Haikou from April 1st to 7th this year, to deploy secret agents and bandits for subversive activities on the mainland. Xu Zhidao, one of Chiang Kai-shek's secret service chiefs, presided over the meeting. The meeting decided that the main modes of operation for secret agents and bandits in the future would be: (1) Long-term dormancy, avoiding exposure. (2) Infiltrating industrial and mining enterprises to sabotage production. (3) Organizing landlords and local bullies to launch armed riots. (4) Establishing communication liaison points. (5) Organizing bombings and assassinations. (6) Establishing contact with available foreigners.

### 蒋匪特务机关布置　图加强大陆破坏活动

1950-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒋匪特务机关布置
　　图加强大陆破坏活动
    【新华社广州十一日电】据海南战役中缴获文件及俘虏口供暴露：蒋匪特务机关“保密局”曾于今年四月一日至七日在海口召开“保密局粤省高干会议”，布置特务匪徒在大陆上之潜伏破坏工作。该会由蒋匪特务头子之一徐志道主持。会议决定特务匪徒今后活动方式主要为：（一）长期潜伏，避免暴露。（二）潜入工矿企业，破坏生产。（三）组织地主、恶霸举行武装暴动。（四）建立通讯联络据点。（五）组织爆破、暗杀。（六）与可利用之外国人建立联系。
