Title: Federation of Literary and Art Circles Inaugural Meeting Rescheduled to the Seventeenth
Author:
Date: 1950-05-14
Page: 3

Beijing News

　　Federation of Literary and Art Circles Inaugural Meeting Rescheduled to the Seventeenth
　　[This Newspaper] The inaugural meeting of the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Circles, originally scheduled for 1:00 PM today, has been changed to Wednesday, May 17th at 1:00 PM, still at the Beijing People's Art Theater, 137 Dongsi South Street, due to a scheduling conflict with the mass rally convened by the Chinese Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

### 文联发起人大会改于十七日举行

1950-05-14
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　文联发起人大会改于十七日举行
    【本报讯】北京市文联发起人大会，原定于今日下午一时举行，因与中国保卫世界和平大会委员会召开之群众大会时间冲突，特改至十七日（星期三）下午一时仍在东四南大街一三七号北京人民艺术厅举行。
