Title: Ho Chi Minh Cables Chairman Mao in Reply, Thanking Him for His Sixtieth Birthday Greetings, and Cables Thanks to the CPC Central Committee and the Central Committee of the Youth League, etc.
Author:
Date: 1950-05-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 30th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam cabled replies to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, thanking them for their congratulations on his sixtieth birthday. The telegrams are as follows:
    (1) To Chairman Mao, Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    On my sixtieth birthday, I am deeply moved and grateful for the congratulations from Your Excellency and your government, as well as the government and people of all Vietnam. We believe that the unity of the Chinese and Vietnamese people will surely defeat the invaders and their running dogs, and the cause of peace in Asia will surely succeed. Wishing you good health.
                               Ho Chi Minh, May 20th
   (2) To the CPC Central Committee:
    I and all Vietnamese comrades are very happy and grateful for your telegram congratulating me on my sixtieth birthday.
                          Ho Chi Minh, May 20th
    (3) To the Central Committee of the Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth: To the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth:
    Thank you for your telegram congratulating me on my sixtieth birthday. The Vietnamese youth were also very happy to see the telegram. I firmly believe that with the efforts of the Vietnamese people and youth themselves, coupled with the help of our Chinese brothers, our resistance war will surely be victorious, and democracy and peace in Asia will surely be guaranteed. I wish you success in your efforts for world's new democracy.
                        Ho Chi Minh, May 20th

### 胡志明电复毛主席  感谢对他六十寿辰的祝贺  并电谢中共中央和青年团中央等

1950-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　胡志明电复毛主席
    感谢对他六十寿辰的祝贺       
    并电谢中共中央和青年团中央等
    【新华社三十日讯】越南民主共和国胡志明主席分别电复我中央人民政府毛泽东主席，中国共产党中央委员会，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会及中华全国民主青年联合总会，感谢对他的六十寿辰的祝贺。电文如下：
    （一）致毛主席电中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛主席阁下：
    在我六十岁时，蒙阁下及贵政府电贺，志明和敝政府及全越人民不胜欣感。我们相信，中越人民团结一致，一定会战胜侵略者及其走狗，而亚洲和平事业一定会成功。敬祝您健康。
                               胡志明  五月二十日
   （二）致中共中央电中共中央鉴：
    蒙你们电贺我六十岁，我和越南全体同志非常高兴，并感谢你们。
                          胡志明  五月二十日
    （三）致青年团中央及全国民主青联电中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合总会：
    你们电贺我六十岁，多谢你们。越南青年看了来电，也非常高兴。我深信越南人民和青年自己的努力，加上中国兄弟的帮忙，我们的抗战一定胜利，亚洲的民主与和平一定有保证。祝你们为世界的新民主主义而努力。
                        胡志明  五月二十日
