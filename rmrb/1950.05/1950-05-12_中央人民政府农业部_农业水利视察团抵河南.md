Title: The Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government Agricultural and Water Conservancy Inspection Group Arrives in Henan
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 1

　　Central People's Government Ministry of Agriculture
　　Agricultural and Water Conservancy Inspection Group Arrives in Henan
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 11th] The Agricultural and Water Conservancy Inspection Group of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, led by Minister Li Shucheng, consisting of twenty-six people, arrived in Kaifeng on the 10th. That evening, more than 4,000 people from provincial and municipal organizations and groups held a welcome reception. The purpose of the group's visit to Henan is to investigate agricultural water conservancy in the province and inspect the recovery and construction situation in the Yellow River flood area.

### 中央人民政府农业部　农业水利视察团抵河南

1950-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府农业部
　　农业水利视察团抵河南
    【新华社开封十一日电】中央人民政府农业部李书城部长率领的农业水利视察团二十六人，已于十日抵达开封。当晚省市各机关团体四千余人举行欢迎晚会。该团此次来豫目的，是调查本省农业水利与视察黄泛区恢复建设情况。
