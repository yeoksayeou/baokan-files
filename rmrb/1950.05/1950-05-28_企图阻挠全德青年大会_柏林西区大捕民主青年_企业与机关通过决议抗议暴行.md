Title: Attempting to Obstruct the All-German Youth Congress, West Berlin Arrests Democratic Youth En Masse; Enterprises and Organizations Pass Resolutions Protesting Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1950-05-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 27th: TASS Berlin reports: According to "Deutsche Nachrichten," on the 23rd, West Berlin's riot police unreasonably arrested thirteen members of the Free German Youth League and Young Pioneer teams. On the 22nd, eleven people were also arrested in West Berlin. The newspaper pointed out that the police in West Berlin, in their helpless frustration, arrested all German democratic youth wearing blue shirts who appeared in the West Berlin area (note: blue shirts are the uniforms worn by participants of the All-German Youth Congress). Many enterprises and organizations in Berlin have passed resolutions protesting this new atrocity by the West Berlin police.

### 企图阻挠全德青年大会  柏林西区大捕民主青年  企业与机关通过决议抗议暴行

1950-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　企图阻挠全德青年大会
    柏林西区大捕民主青年     
    企业与机关通过决议抗议暴行
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：据“德意志新闻”报道，二十三日，柏林西区突击警察无理地逮捕了十三位自由德国青年联盟的盟员和少年先锋队的队员。二十二日，柏林西区亦曾有十一人被捕。该报指出，柏林西区的警察在无可作为的气恼之下，逮捕所有穿着蓝衫在西区出现的德国民主青年（按蓝衫是全德青年大会参加者所穿的制服）。柏林的许多企业和机关都已通过决议，抗议柏林西区警察的这种新暴行。
