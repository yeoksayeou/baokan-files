Title: Peace Propaganda Troupe Passes Through Nanjing En Route to Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1950-05-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 27th -- The Peace Propaganda Troupe, organized by the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace and headed by Xiao San, arrived in Nanjing on the night of the 25th. Those who went to the Xiaguan Railway Station to welcome them included Jin Shanbao and Deng Haoming, vice chairmen of the Nanjing Branch of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, as well as representatives from the Nanjing Municipal People's Government, the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and the Nanjing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. On the afternoon of the 26th, members of the propaganda troupe participated in two symposiums held jointly by the Peace Protection Branch and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Branch, and by the Nanjing Federation of Literary and Art Circles. Troupe leader Xiao San conveyed at the meeting the resolution of the World Peace Council Standing Committee on the prohibition of atomic weapons and the defense of world peace. More than 3,000 representatives from all walks of life in Nanjing held a grand gathering on the evening of the same day to welcome the Peace Propaganda Troupe's arrival in Nanjing. On the 27th, members of the propaganda troupe attended three lecture meetings and delivered speeches. The propaganda troupe left Nanjing on the evening of the 27th for Shanghai.

### 和平宣传团过南京赴沪

1950-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　和平宣传团过南京赴沪
    【新华社南京二十七日电】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会组织的以萧三为首的保卫世界和平宣传团已于二十五日夜抵达南京。前往下关车站欢迎者有中国保卫世界和平大会委员会南京分会副主席金善宝、邓昊明及南京市人民政府、中共南京市委会、中苏友好协会南京分会等单位代表。二十六日下午，宣传团团员分别参加了保卫世界和平分会、中苏友协分会联合举行的及南京文联举行的两个座谈会。萧三团长在会上传达了世界和大常委会关于禁用原子武器、保卫世界和平的决议。南京市各界代表三千余人于同日晚举行盛大集会，欢迎保卫世界和平宣传团的来宁。二十七日，宣传团团员分别出席三个演讲会演讲。宣传团已于二十七日晚离宁赴沪。
