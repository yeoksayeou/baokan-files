Title: Tianjin Launches Peace Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1950-05-16
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 15th. Twenty-one units, including democratic parties and people's organizations in Tianjin, have jointly issued a call to launch a Peace Signature Campaign Week, requesting that people in organized groups first sign the Declaration for the Defense of World Peace, and publicize and organize people from all walks of life to carry out a wide-ranging signature campaign. The Peace Signature Campaign Week has now begun on the 15th. The Tianjin Branch of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace has issued 500,000 signature books to various groups and published the signature book style in newspapers, calling on people from all walks of life to sign in support of defending world peace.

### 天津发起和平签名

1950-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津发起和平签名
    【新华社天津十五日电】天津市各民主党派、各人民团体共二十一个单位，顷联合发出号召，发起和平签名运动周，要求各团体有组织的人民，首先在保卫世界和平宣言上签字，并宣传与组织各界人民，开展广泛的签名运动。现和平签名运动周已于十五日开始。中国保卫世界和平大会委员会天津分会已向各团体发出五十万人的签名书，并于报端刊载签书样式，号召各界人民签名拥护保卫世界和平。
