Title: Hoping to Establish a Theater in Changxindian
Author: Tu Ke Shi Tang Song Defang Chen Jian
Date: 1950-05-08
Page: 4
People's Garden

　　Hoping to Establish a Theater in Changxindian

Comrade Editor:

　　We live in Changxindian. There are many workers, troops, and residents in Changxindian and the surrounding areas, but there is not a single entertainment venue. We propose that the government establish a theater in Changxindian to regularly screen movies and perform dramas, so that workers, troops, and the general public can have the opportunity to enjoy entertainment.

　　Military Region ×× Training Regiment Tu Ke Shi Tang Song Defang Chen Jian

### 希望在长辛店设立戏院
涂克　石塘　宋德芳　陈健
1950-05-08
第4版()
专栏：人民园地

　　希望在长辛店设立戏院编辑同志：
    我们住在长辛店。长辛店及附近地区工人、部队、居民甚多，但一个娱乐场所也没有。我们提议政府在长辛店设立一个戏院，经常放映电影并演出戏剧，使工人、部队及广大居民有享受娱乐的机会。
    军区××教导团  涂克  石塘  宋德芳  陈健
