Title: Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Široký Sends Telegram to Foreign Minister Zhou, Thanking Him for Congratulations on Czechoslovak Liberation Day
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Czechoslovak Republic Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Viliam Široký sent a telegram in reply to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai’s congratulations on the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia by the Soviet Army. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

Thank you for your congratulations on the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia by the Soviet Army. I extend my warmest congratulations to the free Chinese people and hope that they will have even greater success in building their great motherland.

Viliam Široký, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic
May 11, 1950

### 捷代总理兼外长　西罗基电复周外长　感谢对捷克解放纪念日的祝贺

1950-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷代总理兼外长
　　西罗基电复周外长
　　感谢对捷克解放纪念日的祝贺
    【新华社十一日讯】捷克共和国代理总理兼外交部长威廉·西罗基电复周恩来外交部长对苏军解放捷克五周年纪念日的祝贺，电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部周恩来阁下：
    感谢您对于苏军解放捷克五周年纪念日的庆贺。我对自由的中国人民表示最热诚的祝贺，并且希望他们在建设自己伟大祖国的事业中，有更大的成功。
                   捷克共和国代理总理兼外交部长  威廉·西罗基
                                        一九五○年五月十一日
