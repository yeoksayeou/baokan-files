Title: Resolutely Standing on the Side of the Soviet Union to Strive for Peace: German People Celebrate Liberation Day; Rallies and Demonstrations Held in Berlin and Throughout the Republic
Author:
Date: 1950-05-11
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 10th: According to TASS, Berlin, the capital of the German Democratic Republic, celebrated the day of the liberation of the German people on the 8th. Large and small commemorative meetings were held in factories and government offices. Early in the morning, people flocked to Treptow Park, where the Soviet War Memorial is built to commemorate the Soviet soldiers who sacrificed themselves in the struggle to liberate all mankind from the plague of the Hitler bandits. The procession to the park was led by Wilhelm Pieck, Chairman of the Provisional People's Chamber, and Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, followed by representatives of various democratic parties, the National Front of Democratic Germany, the Free German Trade Union Federation, the Democratic Women's League, the League of Victims of Nazi Persecution, the German-Soviet Friendship Association, the Free German Youth League, and other public organizations.
　　A grand memorial service was held in front of the Soviet War Memorial. Deputy Prime Minister Professor Kask, on behalf of the German people, expressed eternal gratitude to the Soviet people and the Soviet government for liberating the German people and helping to establish a democratic order. He pointed out that the best way to express this gratitude is to stand on the side of the Soviet Union, to fight to guarantee peace, and to fight to strengthen friendship with the great Soviet people.
　　Dahlem, representing the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, expressed the same gratitude and pointed out that the main task at present is to attract all Germans to the struggle for peace.
　　Hundreds of thousands of demonstrators on this day expressed their gratitude and friendship to the Soviet Union, and solemnly guaranteed to stand firmly on the side of the Soviet Union to strive for peace, progress, and democracy.
　　Meetings and demonstrations were also held in towns and cities throughout the German Democratic Republic on this day. (Attached picture)

### 坚决站在苏联一边争取和平　德人民欢庆解放纪念日　柏林及共和国各地均集会示威

1950-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　坚决站在苏联一边争取和平
    德人民欢庆解放纪念日 
    柏林及共和国各地均集会示威
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：德意志民主共和国首都柏林于八日欢庆德国人民的解放日。各工厂和机关都举行了大大小小的纪念会。一清早，人们就涌向特列泡公园，这里建筑有苏军阵亡将士纪念碑，以纪念在从希特勒匪徒的瘟疫中争取解放全人类的斗争中牺牲的苏军战士。走向公园的游行行列以临时议会主席团主席狄克曼及总理格罗提渥率先，其后为各民主党派，民主德国全国阵线，自由德国工会联合会，民主妇女联盟，受纳粹迫害者联盟，德苏友好协会，自由德国青年联盟及其他公共团体的代表。
    苏军战士纪念碑前举行了盛大的纪念会。副总理卡斯的教授代表德国人民表示永远感谢苏联人民与苏联政府解放德国人民与帮助建立民主秩序的恩情。他指出，表达这种感激的最好办法，就是站在苏联一边，为保证和平而斗争，及为加强和伟大苏联人民的友谊而斗争。
    达赖姆代表德国统一社会党中央委员会表示了同样的感谢，并指出：当前的主要任务，是把所有的德国人都吸引到争取和平的斗争中来。
    几十万的示威者在这一天表示了他们对苏联的感激和友谊，并庄严保证坚决站在苏联一边，争取和平、进步与民主。
    德意志民主共和国全境的各城镇，在这一天也都举行了集会和示威。（附图片）
