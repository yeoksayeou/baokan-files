Title: Soviet Youth Delegation Members Continue to Attend Symposiums and Give Lectures at Invitation

Author: 
Date: 1950-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: Members of the Soviet Youth Delegation today continued to attend symposiums or give lectures at the invitation of various Beijing agencies, organizations, factories, and military units. The head of the delegation, Mikhailov, gave a report at the Central Youth League School, giving a very detailed explanation of the leadership issues of the Youth League. Gidayeva, the secretary of the Lenin Communist Youth League Committee of the Soviet city of Saratov, attended a symposium on education work hosted by the Ministry of Education; the Soviet youth writer Gonchar attended a symposium for young writers hosted by the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Dumanova, the editor of the Soviet "Pioneer Truth" newspaper, attended a symposium on youth newspapers and publications hosted by the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Youth League; Soviet youth representatives Peslyak, Melgolyan, and Zizina gave lectures to students at the North China University Industrial College, Jiaotong University, and Fu Jen Catholic University, respectively, introducing the development of Soviet literature and art, education, publishing, science, and other undertakings, as well as the Soviet Communist Party and the Soviet government's love and cultivation of the younger generation.

In addition, Sizov, the secretary of the Moscow Lenin Communist Youth League Committee, and others gave lectures to railway workers in Changxindian; Masherov, the secretary of the Belorussian Lenin Communist Youth League, and the Soviet hero Kozhedub gave lectures to students at the North China Military and Political University.

### 苏青年代表团各代表继续应邀座谈或讲演

1950-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏青年代表团各代表继续应邀座谈或讲演
    【新华社五日讯】苏联青年代表团各代表今日继续应邀出席北京各机关、团体、工厂、部队的座谈会或讲演会。代表团团长米哈依洛夫到中央团校作报告，对青年团的领导问题，有很详细的解说。苏联萨拉多拉城列宁共产主义青年团团委书记吉大也娃出席教育部主持的教育工作座谈会，苏联青年作家龚查尔出席全国文联主持的青年作家座谈会，苏联《少先真理报》的编辑杜曼诺娃出席青年团中央宣传部主持的青年报刊和出版工作座谈会，苏联青年代表裴斯良克、梅尔戈亮和吉金娜对华北大学工学院、交通大学和辅仁大学等校学生讲演，分别介绍了苏联的文艺、教育、出版以及科学等事业的发展，和苏联共产党与苏联政府对青年一代的爱护与培养。
    此外，莫斯科列宁共产主义青年团团委书记西佐夫等到长辛店对铁路工人讲演，白俄罗斯列宁共产主义青年团书记马舍洛夫与苏联英雄阔日杜布对华北军政大学学生讲演。
