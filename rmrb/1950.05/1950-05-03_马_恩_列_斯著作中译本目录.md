　　Catalog of Chinese Translations of the Works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin

A few notes: First, the catalog only lists books currently available in bookstores; old versions that are out of print are not included. Second, excerpted and edited works are not included, except for "Marx and Engels on China." Third, important essays that are included in collections but also published as separate books are listed separately. For example, "On the State" is included in the two-volume "Selected Works of Lenin," but since it was also published as a separate book, it is listed in the catalog. Readers are requested to point out any omissions or errors in this survey.
