Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Cables the Women's International Democratic Federation and the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee to Celebrate "June 1st" International Children's Day
Author:
Date: 1950-05-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation sent a cable to the Women's International Democratic Federation and the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee to celebrate "June 1st" International Children's Day. The text of the cable is as follows:

(1) To the Women's International Democratic Federation, and forwarded to the International Children's Day Preparatory Committee and all member organizations:

　　On the occasion of celebrating the first International Children's Day, we extend warm congratulations to mothers and children of all countries; and for the safety and happiness of children, we will work together with all people in all countries who care about children to defend children's rights and strive for lasting world peace.
                         All-China Democratic Women's Federation
                                    May 22

(2) To the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee:

　　On the occasion of celebrating the first International Children's Day, we extend warm congratulations to the happy mothers and children of the Soviet Union. The beautiful life of Soviet mothers and children is the direction and goal of the efforts of women and children all over the world. It gives great encouragement to those who defend children's rights and strive for lasting world peace.
                          All-China Democratic Women's Federation
                            May 22

### 我民主妇联电国际民主妇联及苏联妇女反法西斯委员会  庆贺“六一”国际儿童节

1950-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　我民主妇联电国际民主妇联及苏联妇女反法西斯委员会         
    庆贺“六一”国际儿童节
    【新华社二十七日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会为庆祝“六一”国际儿童节，特致电国际民主妇联及苏联妇女反法西斯委员会祝贺。电文如下：
    （一）致国际民主妇联电国际民主妇女联合会并转国际儿童节筹备委员会及各会员团体：
    在庆祝第一个国际儿童节之际，我们向各国的母亲和儿童致热烈的祝贺；并为儿童的安全和幸福，与各国一切关心儿童的人们，共同为保卫儿童权利、争取世界持久和平而奋斗。
                         中华全国民主妇女联合会
                                    五、二十二
    （二）致苏联妇女反法西斯委员会电苏联妇女反法西斯委员会：
    在庆祝第一个国际儿童节的时候，我们向苏联幸福的母亲和儿童致热烈的祝贺。苏联母亲与儿童的美好生活是全世界妇女儿童努力的方向与目标。它给予了为保卫儿童权利、争取世界持久和平的人们以极大的鼓励。
                          中华全国民主妇女联合会
                            五、二十二
