Title: Czechoslovak People Celebrate Liberation Day; Grand Parades Held in Prague and Other Cities; Gottwald's Speech Thanks Soviet Aid
Author:
Date: 1950-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: The working people of the Czechoslovak Republic celebrated the fifth anniversary of the Soviet Army's liberation of Czechoslovakia with great enthusiasm. According to a TASS report from Prague: On the 8th, military parades of army units, national security forces, and militias were held in cities such as Prague and Bratislava. In all towns and villages, gatherings were held to celebrate the fifth anniversary of liberation.

　　In Prague, the Wenceslas Square, where the parade was held, and the surrounding streets and alleys, were crowded with hundreds of thousands of Prague citizens and tens of thousands of people from all over the republic who came to participate in the celebrations from early morning. Attendees warmly welcomed the Soviet delegation headed by Bulganin. After the meeting, a parade of hundreds of thousands of working people was held.

　　Czech President Gottwald delivered a speech at the parade, stating that May 9th is Czechoslovakia's solemn and greatest national holiday, and it was on this day that the Czechoslovak people opened a new era in their history. He compared the thriving situation of Czechoslovakia in the five years since liberation with the situation of Western and Southern European countries enslaved by American imperialism, illustrating the decisive role played by the victory of the Soviet Army and the fraternal assistance of the Soviet Union in the recovery and development of Czech. Gottwald declared that the Czech people will tirelessly fight in the ranks of the world peace front.

### 捷人民庆祝解放日  布拉格等地举行盛大游行  哥特瓦尔德演说感谢苏联援助

1950-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷人民庆祝解放日
    布拉格等地举行盛大游行       
    哥特瓦尔德演说感谢苏联援助
    【新华社九日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国的劳动人民，以巨大的热情庆祝苏军解放捷克斯洛伐克的五周年。据塔斯社布拉格讯：八日，在布拉格、布拉的斯拉发等城市，都举行了陆军部队、国家保安队与民兵的检阅，在一切城镇和乡村中，都举行了庆祝解放五周年的联欢大会。
    在布拉格，举行检阅的瓦特斯拉瓦广场和附近的大街小巷上，从清晨就簇拥着数十万布拉格市民及数万由共和国各地前来参加庆祝盛典的人民。与会者热烈地欢迎以布尔加宁为首的苏联代表团。会后举行了数十万劳动人民的游行。
    捷总统哥特瓦尔德曾在检阅时发表演说称：五月九日是捷克斯洛伐克隆重与最伟大的国庆日，捷克斯洛伐克人民就在这一天打开了他们历史上的新纪元。他以捷克斯洛伐克在解放后五年来的欣欣向荣的景况，和西欧与南欧国家受美国帝国主义奴役的情况加以对比，说明由于苏军的胜利和苏联的兄弟般的援助，对捷克的复兴与发展所起的决定性作用。哥特瓦尔德宣称：捷克人民决将在世界和平阵线的行列中不倦地斗争。
