Title: "Observation" reorganized as "New Observation": A comprehensive domestic current affairs publication
Author:
Date: 1950-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The semi-monthly journal "Observation" has announced its termination with Volume VI, Issue 14. The original staff of "Observation" will join the newly reorganized semi-monthly journal "New Observation." "New Observation" will be a comprehensive domestic current affairs publication, a sister publication to the international current affairs publication "World Knowledge." It will be published and distributed by Xinhua Bookstore. Its content will include: 1. Commentary on domestic current affairs; 2. Research materials on domestic current affairs; 3. Investigative records of construction and reform work; 4. Digest of national newspapers; 5. Local correspondence, travel correspondence; 6. Book and newspaper reviews; 7. Mailbox; 8. Current affairs pictorial. The first issue of "New Observation" will be published on July 1st.

### 《观察》改组为《新观察》　性质为综合性国内时事刊物

1950-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　《观察》改组为《新观察》
　　性质为综合性国内时事刊物
    【新华社十七日讯】《观察》半月刊于第六卷第十四期宣布结束。原《观察》社同人将加入从新组织的《新观察》半月刊工作。《新观察》半月刊是一种综合性的国内时事刊物，与国际时事刊物《世界知识》为姊妹刊。由新华书店出版和发行。其内容为：一、关于国内时事的评论；二、关于国内时事的研究资料；三、关于建设工作和改革工作的调查纪录；四、全国报纸文摘；五、地方通讯、旅行通讯；六、书报评介；七、信箱；八、时事画刊。《新观察》第一期将于七月一日出版。
