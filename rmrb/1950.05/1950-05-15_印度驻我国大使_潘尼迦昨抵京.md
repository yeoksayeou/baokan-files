Title: Indian Ambassador to China, Pannikar, Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-05-15
Page: 1

　　Indian Ambassador to China
　　Pannikar Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] The first Indian Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Pannikar, his wife and entourage of four people, accompanied by Mr. Ma Muming, Section Chief of the Foreign Affairs Section of the Tianjin Municipal People's Government, and Indian Chargé d'Affaires Mr. Yan Yuesen, arrived in Beijing at 6:25 p.m. today. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Qiao Guanhua, Acting Director of the Asian Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Tang Yanjie, Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command, Zikhivin, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, Birowsky, Chargé d'Affaires of the Polish Embassy, Fekete, Counselor of the Hungarian Legation, Mentianu, Secretary of the Romanian Embassy, Hutchinson, Representative of the British Government, Hou Weimin, Representative of the Dutch Government, and Wu Bi, Representative of the Burmese Government.

### 印度驻我国大使　潘尼迦昨抵京

1950-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度驻我国大使
　　潘尼迦昨抵京
    【新华社十四日讯】印度驻我国首任特命全权大使潘尼迦大使夫妇及随员等一行四人，由天津市人民政府外侨事务处交际科马牧鸣科长及印度雁谒森代办陪同，于今日下午六时二十五分抵京。赴站欢迎者有我外交部办公厅主任王炳南、亚洲司代司长乔冠华、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，京津卫戍司令部参谋长唐延杰，苏联大使馆参事齐赫文，波兰大使馆代办毕罗奇，匈牙利公使馆参事费凯特，罗马尼亚大使馆秘书孟天努，英国政府代表胡阶森，荷兰政府代表侯卫民，缅甸政府代表吴辟等。
