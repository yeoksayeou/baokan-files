Title: All-China Journalists Association Protests British Imperialist Oppression of Nanyang Siang Pau
Author:
Date: 1950-05-08
Page: 1

　　All-China Journalists Association
    Protests British Imperialist Oppression of Nanyang Siang Pau
    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] The All-China Journalists Association today sent a telegram to Gimson, the Governor of Singapore, stating: "We protest the Singapore British government's unreasonable oppression of the Nanyang Siang Pau and demand the immediate release of Zhu Qizhuo, the newspaper's advertising director, who has been detained for two months."

### 全国新闻工作者协会  抗议英帝压迫南侨日报

1950-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国新闻工作者协会
    抗议英帝压迫南侨日报
    【新华社七日讯】中华全国新闻工作者协会本日致电新加坡总督金森称：“我们抗议新加坡英政府无理压迫南侨日报，并要求立即释放已被拘禁两月之该报广告主任朱奇卓。”
