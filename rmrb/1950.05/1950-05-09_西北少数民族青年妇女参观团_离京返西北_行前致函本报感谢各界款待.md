Title: Northwest Minority Youth and Women's Visiting Group Leaves Beijing for Northwest; Sends Letter to This Newspaper Before Departure, Thanking All Sectors for Their Hospitality
Author:
Date: 1950-05-09
Page: 3

　　Northwest Minority Youth and Women's Visiting Group
    Leaves Beijing for Northwest
    Sends Letter to This Newspaper Before Departure, Thanking All Sectors for Their Hospitality
    [Xinhua News Agency, 6th] The Northwest Minority Youth and Women's Visiting Group left Beijing this morning to return to the Northwest. Representatives from the Central People's Government Nationalities Affairs Commission, the Central Committee of the Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, and the Beijing Municipal Women's Federation went to the station to see them off. Since arriving in Beijing on March 26th, the Northwest Youth and Women's Visiting Group has received a warm welcome and reception from various agencies, organizations, and people from all walks of life in the capital. They visited the Beijing Clothing Factory, the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, Tsinghua University, the Mongolian-Tibetan School, and the National Hui Institute, among other factories and schools, and participated in symposiums hosted by the Central Committee of the Youth League and the All-China Women's Federation. On April 26th, the representatives met Chairman Mao with extreme excitement. Before leaving Beijing, they unanimously stated that during this visit, they deeply experienced the care of the Central People's Government, the Chinese Communist Party, and the people of the whole country for the minority nationalities, and at the same time greatly increased their love for Chairman Mao and their confidence in building the motherland. They all promised to respond to everyone's expectations by strengthening the unity among all nationalities and working hard.
    [This Newspaper] Before leaving Beijing, the Northwest Minority Youth and Women's Visiting Group sent a letter to this newspaper, expressing their gratitude to the leaders of various agencies and the enthusiastic hospitality of youth friends from various places, and attached a signed paper from all the members.

### 西北少数民族青年妇女参观团  离京返西北  行前致函本报感谢各界款待

1950-05-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　西北少数民族青年妇女参观团
    离京返西北
    行前致函本报感谢各界款待
    【新华社六日讯】西北少数民族青年、妇女参观团于今日晨离京返西北。前往车站欢送的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会、青年团中央、全国妇联、全国青联、全国学联、青年团北京市工作委员会、北京市妇联等单位的代表。西北青年、妇女参观团自三月二十六日抵京后，受到首都各机关、团体及各界人民的热烈欢迎和接待。他们参观了北京被服厂、石景山钢铁厂、清华大学、蒙藏学校、国立回民学院等工厂学校，并参加了青年团中央和全国妇联等所主持的座谈会。代表们以极端兴奋的心情于四月二十六日谒见了毛主席。离京前，他们一致表示在这次参观中深深地体验了中央人民政府、中国共产党和全国人民对少数民族人民的关怀，同时格外增加了对毛主席的热爱和对建设祖国的信心。他们都保证要以加强全国各民族间的团结、努力工作来回答各方对他们的期望。
    【本报讯】西北少数民族青年、妇女参观团离京前致函本报，对各机关首长及各地青年朋友的热情招待表示感谢，并附有全体团员签名纸一张。
