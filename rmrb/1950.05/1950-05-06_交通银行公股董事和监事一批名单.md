Title: List of Public Directors and Supervisors of the Bank of Communications

Author:

Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Government Administration Council recently appointed a batch of public directors and supervisors of the Bank of Communications. The list is as follows:
    Public Directors: Nan Hanchen, Hu Jingyun, Cao Juru, He Changgong, Zhang Bojun, Wang Shao'ao, Yang Weiyu, Wu Jingnan, Qian Zhiguang, Qian Changzhao, Chen Mu, Zhang Pingzhi, Li Zhongchu;
    Public Supervisors: Zhu Xuefan, Chen Yu, Sun Yueqi, He Songting, Wang Lei.

### 交通银行公股董事和监事一批名单

1950-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　交通银行公股董事和监事一批名单
    【新华社五日讯】政务院顷任命交通银行公股董事和监事一批，名单如下：
    公股董事：南汉宸，胡景澐，曹菊如，何长工，章伯钧，王绍鏊，杨卫玉，武竞天，钱之光，钱昌照，陈穆，张平之，李钟楚；
    公股监事：朱学范，陈郁，孙越崎，何松亭，王磊。
