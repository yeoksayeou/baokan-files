Title: Shanghai-Moscow Wireless Telephone Service Opens

Author:

Date: 1950-05-03
Page: 1

　　Shanghai-Moscow Wireless Telephone Service Opens

　　[Xinhua News Agency, 2nd] The Shanghai International Radio Station of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government officially opened wireless telephone service with Moscow on April 29th. The call time is from 4:30 PM to 5:30 PM daily. The price is calculated in RMB converted from gold francs, with the basic fee for ordinary calls being 24.60 gold francs for every three minutes, and an additional 8.20 gold francs for each additional minute. Government calls are charged at half the rate of ordinary calls. When the Shanghai-Moscow wireless telephone service officially opened, Huang Ping, Director of the East China Telecommunications Branch Bureau of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, first spoke with the Director of the Moscow Radio Bureau, exchanging congratulations.

### 上海莫斯科间开放无线电话

1950-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海莫斯科间开放无线电话
    【新华社二日讯】中央人民政府邮电部上海国际电台，已于四月二十九日起正式开放与莫斯科之间的无线电话业务。通话时间为每日下午四时半至五时半。价目系按金法郎折合人民币计算，计寻常通话基本费每次三分钟二四·六○金法郎，逾时每分钟加收八·二○金法郎；政务通话照寻常通话价目折半收费。上海与莫斯科无线电话正式开放时，邮电部华东电信总分局黄萍局长曾与莫斯科无线电局长首先通话，互致祝贺。
