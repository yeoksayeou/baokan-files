Title: Literary and Art Workers Representative Conference - List of Presidium Members
Author:
Date: 1950-05-30
Page: 3

　　Literary and Art Workers Representative Conference
    List of Presidium Members
    [News Agency of Beijing City] The list of the Presidium of the Beijing Municipal Literary and Art Workers Representative Conference is as follows: Lao She, Ouyang Yuqian, Li Bozhao, Wang Yaping, Yang Zhensheng, Lian Kuoru, Xu Beihong, Zhao Shuli, Feng Zi, Lao Zhicheng, Li Guangtian, Shang Xiaoyun, Miao Peishi, Hu Man, Qi Baishi, Yu Pingbo, Luo Changpei, Wang Yaoliao, Wen Jiasi, Zhang Shirong, Dai Ailian, Jiao Juyin, Cao Baolu, Tian Jian, Zhou Weizhi, Duanmu Hongliang, Tian Fang, Han Shichang, Tian Han, Hong Shen, Li Guoquan, Bai Yunpeng, Xiao Changhua, Ding Li, Ruan Zhangjing, Zhong Jingwen, Jin Shan, Wu Zuxiang.
    The Presidium mutually elected Lao She as the Chairman of the Presidium, Li Bozhao and Zhao Shuli as Vice-Chairmen, and Wang Yaping as Secretary-General.

### 文艺工作者代表大会  大会主席团名单

1950-05-30

专栏：

　　文艺工作者代表大会
    大会主席团名单
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市文艺工作者代表大会主席团名单如下：老舍、欧阳予倩、李伯钊、王亚平、杨振声、连阔如、徐悲鸿、赵树理、凤子、老志诚、李广田、尚小云、苗培时、胡蛮、齐白石、俞平伯、罗常培、王瑶聊、闻家驷、张世荣、戴爱莲、焦菊隐、曹宝禄、田间、周巍峙、端木蕻良、田方、韩世昌、田汉、洪深、黎国荃、白云鹏、萧长华、丁里、阮章竞、钟敬文、金山、吴组缃。
    主席团互推老舍为主席团主席，李伯钊、赵树理为副主席，王亚平为秘书长。
