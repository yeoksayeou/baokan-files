Title: National Congress of Electrical Workers' Union Postponed to July 7th
Author:
Date: 1950-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The first National Congress of the Chinese Electrical Workers' Union, originally scheduled to be held on May 30th, has been postponed to July 7th as decided by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions due to the need for more preparation time. The agenda for the congress has also been revised to include: (1) Reports: A, Work summary of the Electrical Workers' Union Working Committee and future work guidelines and tasks; B, Task of the Ministry of Fuel Industry for electricity production in the second half of 1950 (July to December); (2) Draft Constitution of the Chinese Electrical Workers' Union and draft regulations for grassroots organizations; (3) Draft collective contract; (4) Draft resolution on issues of amateur education and cultural entertainment activities; (5) Model reports; (6) Election of members of the National Committee of the Chinese Electrical Workers' Union and members of the Finance Review Committee. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has issued a notice requiring Northeast, North China, Central South, Southwest, and Shanghai regions to send one representative each to Beijing before June 1st to participate in the preparatory work.

### 电业工会代表大会延至七月七日召开

1950-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　电业工会代表大会延至七月七日召开
    【新华社十六日讯】原定于五月三十日召开的中国电业工会第一次全国代表大会，因准备需时，已经中华全国总工会决定延至七月七日召开。大会议程也已重新拟定为：（一）报告：甲、电业工会工作委员会工作总结及今后工作方针与任务，乙、燃料工业部一九五○下半年（七月到十二月）电业生产的任务；（二）中国电业工会章程草案和基层组织的各种条例草案；（三）集体合同草案；（四）业余教育和文化娱乐活动问题的决议草案；（五）典型报告；（六）选举中国电业工会全国委员会委员及经费审查委员会委员。全国总工会为此已发出通知，要求东北、华北、中南、西南及上海等地区，应先派代表一人于六月一日前到北京参加筹备工作。
