Title: Congress Sends Condolences to Joliot-Curie
Author:
Date: 1950-05-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 14th: The Beijing Conference for the Signature Campaign for the Declaration to Defend World Peace sent a telegram of condolence to Professor Joliot-Curie, protesting the reactionary French government's dismissal of Professor Curie from his positions as High Commissioner for Atomic Energy and member of the Atomic Energy Commission. The telegram reads as follows: Professor Joliot-Curie:

Today, representatives from all walks of life in Beijing are convening the signature campaign conference for the Declaration to Defend World Peace. On behalf of the people of Beijing, we express our indignant protest against the absurd behavior of the reactionary French government in dismissing you from your positions as a member of the Atomic Energy Commission and High Commissioner for Atomic Energy, and extend our cordial greetings to you – the heroic fighter for the defense of world peace. We will stand with you and strive for lasting world peace and people's democracy.

The Beijing Conference for the Signature Campaign for the Declaration to Defend World Peace
May 14, 1950

### 大会致电慰问居里

1950-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　大会致电慰问居里
    【新华社十四日讯】北京市保卫世界和平宣言签名运动大会通电慰问约里奥——居里教授，并抗议法国反动政府解除居里教授所担任的原子能高级专员和原子能委员会委员职务。电文如下：约里奥——居里教授：
    今天北京各界代表在这里召开保卫世界和平宣言签名运动大会，我们代表北京各界人民，对法国反动政府解除您的原子能委员会委员和原子能高级专员的悖谬行为，表示愤怒的抗议，并向您——保卫世界和平的英勇斗士致亲切的慰问。我们一定跟您站在一起，为世界持久和平与人民民主而努力奋斗。
    北京市保卫世界和平宣言签名运动大会
                                        一九五○年五月十四日
