Title: East China Military Region and Third Field Army Units Donate to Unemployed Worker Brothers
Author:
Date: 1950-05-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 3rd: The East China Military Region and all units of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army are enthusiastically donating money to relieve unemployed workers in Shanghai and other places. Personnel from units directly under the Military Region not only donated their allowances, but some even donated their health care funds and disability pensions. According to preliminary statistics from four units including the Cipher Department of the Military Region Headquarters, donations have reached more than 3.9 million yuan. Many comrades also wrote letters to express their sympathy to the unemployed workers in Shanghai, expressing their firm support for them to overcome their current difficulties. Workers and staff in the rear service department factories and repair shops of the Special Column who are paid on a salary system also generally launched a campaign to donate one day's wages; workers and staff on the supply system saved one meal to help unemployed workers. Employees of a certain clothing factory have donated 9,000 units of equivalent value, averaging one and a half day's wages per employee. Many factory employees also volunteered to donate a day's work on Sundays.

### 华东军区及三野各部  捐助失业工人兄弟

1950-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　华东军区及三野各部
    捐助失业工人兄弟
    【新华社南京三日电】人民解放军华东军区暨第三野战军各部热烈捐款救济上海及各地失业工人。军区直属队各单位人员不但捐出津贴费，有的还捐出了保健费和残废金。据军区司令部机要处等四个单位初步统计捐款已达三百九十余万元。很多同志并写信慰问上海失业工人，表示坚决支援他们渡过目前困难。后勤部各工厂及特种纵队修配厂实行薪给制待遇的职工，也普遍开展捐献一天工资运动；供给制的职工人员则节约一餐饭救济失业工人。某被服厂职工已捐出九千个折实单位，平均每个职工捐出一天半工资。很多工厂职工并自动在星期日捐工一天。
