Title: Our Delegation Arrives in Czechoslovakia to Participate in May Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1950-05-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 1st. The Chinese delegation, including Xu Liqun, Wang Laowu, Li Yonglu, and Li Guoying, arrived in Prague by plane on the morning of the 30th to attend the Czechoslovak May Day celebration. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included J. Mender, Assistant Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Kipro, Acting Chairman of the Czechoslovak Trade Union Federation, and Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs Bořek and Toval. Liu Ningyi and others, who are coming to Czechoslovakia to participate in the celebration of Czechoslovakia's Liberation Day on May 9th, will come to Czechoslovakia from Moscow after May Day.

### 我代表团抵捷参加五一庆祝

1950-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　我代表团抵捷参加五一庆祝
    【新华社布拉格一日电】来此参加捷克斯洛伐克五一庆祝大会的中国代表团许立群、王老五、李永禄、李国英等一行，于三十日上午飞抵捷京。赴机场欢迎者有捷共中央助理书记季明德尔，捷总工会代理主席祁普罗，外交部副部长包列克和陶伐尔等人。来捷参加捷克解放日——五月九日庆祝会的中国代表刘宁一等人，将于五一后自苏京来捷。
