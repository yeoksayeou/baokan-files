Title: Declaration of Support and Great Appeal by the Representatives Conference of the Chinese People's Relief Effort
Author:
Date: 1950-05-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, dated 3rd:

　　The Chinese People's Relief Representative Conference
    Declaration of Support and Great Appeal

    Today, the Chinese People's Relief Representative Conference successfully concluded in Beijing. Since our task is disaster relief, we deeply understand the source of the disasters. Our current disasters stem from Japan's eight years of aggression, the Kuomintang bandits' anti-people war, and the ongoing atrocities and poisonous schemes of the remaining bandits, supported by American imperialism, bombing cities and blockading seaports.

    Japan's aggression had already placed the Chinese people in dire straits, and after the War of Resistance Against Japan, American imperialism supported the Kuomintang bandits with 600 million US dollars to launch an anti-people war. It allowed the Kuomintang bandits to act perversely, breaching dikes and setting fires. During sieges, American-made bombs rained down, and in field battles, American tanks charged forward. The evils of war left families broken, with widows and orphans destitute. Land was barren, people were sick, and floods caused famine—all hallmarks of the war. After the trauma, our government led the people of the whole country to quickly eliminate the Kuomintang remnants and launched a production and disaster relief campaign to promote the great cause of building a peaceful new China.

    As we strive to relieve the disasters created by American imperialism today, we cannot forget that American imperialism is actively preparing to create even larger-scale disasters tomorrow.

    To realize its arrogant plan to dominate the world, American imperialism is mass-producing atomic weapons, preparing for a new world war. All our representatives, adhering to the principle of cherishing peace and opposing aggression, warmly support the call issued by the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress to defend peace: demanding the unconditional prohibition of atomic weapons, demanding the establishment of strict controls, and declaring the government that dares to use this weapon first as a war criminal.

    We believe that the struggle of the people of the world against a new war can prevent a new war. The two major allied countries, China and the Soviet Union, are both determined to fight to defend world peace and oppose imperialist aggression. The power of the 700 million people of these two major allied countries is the backbone of the world peace camp. This power is invincible, and their spirit of loving peace and opposing war cannot be intimidated by American imperialist atomic weapons.

    All Representatives of the Chinese People's Relief Representative Conference

### 中国人民救济代表会议  拥护和大号召宣言

1950-05-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国人民救济代表会议
    拥护和大号召宣言
    今天中国人民救济代表会议在北京胜利的闭幕了。我们的任务既是救灾，所以我们深知道灾难的来源。我们今日的灾难是来自日本的八年侵略，蒋匪的反人民战争及现在还进行着的美帝支援下的残匪的轰炸城市和封锁海口等暴行毒计。
    日本的侵略已置中国人民于水深火热中，而美帝复于抗日战争结束后以六十万万金元支持蒋匪，发动反人民战争，一任蒋匪倒行逆施，决河纵火，攻城则美制造炸弹如雨，野战则美国坦克冲锋，战争的罪恶，使人民家破人亡，寡妇孤儿流离无靠。地荒人病，水泛粮绝，也是战争的烙印。创痛之余，我们的政府领导全国人民迅速消灭蒋匪残余，并发动生产救灾运动，以促成建设和平的新中国的伟业。
    当我们努力于救济美帝国主义所制造的今天的灾难的时候，我们不能忘记美帝国主义还在积极准备着制造明天的更大规模的灾难。
    美帝为了实现称霸全世界的狂妄侵略计划，正在大量制造原子武器，准备新的世界战争。我们全体代表本爱护和平，反对侵略的宗旨，热烈地拥护世界保卫和平大会常设委员会所发出的保卫和平的号召：要求无条件地禁止原子武器，要求建立严格的管制，宣布首先胆敢使用这武器的政府为战争罪犯。
    我们相信：全世界人民反对新战争的奋斗能够阻止新战争。中苏两大盟国都决心为保卫世界和平，反对帝国主义的侵略而奋斗；这两大盟邦的七万万人民的力量，是世界和平阵营的中坚，这个力量是无可征服的，他们的爱和平反战争的精神不是美帝原子武器所能吓倒的。
    中国人民救济代表会议全体代表
