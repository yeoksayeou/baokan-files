Title: China Democratic League Headquarters Calls on Members to Respond to Peace Signature Campaign; Joint Office of Various Research Associations of Chinese Social Sciences Notifies Affiliates to Launch Signatures
Author:
Date: 1950-05-16
Page: 1
　　
　　China Democratic League Headquarters Calls on Members to Respond to Peace Signature Campaign
　　Joint Office of Various Research Associations of Chinese Social Sciences Notifies Affiliates to Launch Signatures

　　[Xinhua News Agency, 15th] In response to the nationwide signature campaign launched by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace in support of the World Peace Congress' Standing Committee Declaration, the headquarters of the China Democratic League, in addition to mobilizing central committee members in Beijing and all headquarters staff to sign, issued a circular on the 15th, calling on local organizations of the China Democratic League in various provinces and cities to enthusiastically respond to this movement.

　　[Xinhua News Agency, 15th] In response to the call from the Stockholm meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, the Joint Office of Various Research Associations of Chinese Social Sciences has notified the Chinese New Philosophy Research Association, the Chinese New History Research Association, the Chinese New Economics Research Association, the Chinese New Political Science Research Association, and the Chinese New Legal Science Research Association to call on their affiliated branches and all members to widely launch a signature campaign to ban atomic weapons.

### 民盟总部号召盟员响应和平签名运动　中国社会科学各研究会联合办事处通知所属展开签名

1950-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　民盟总部号召盟员响应和平签名运动
　　中国社会科学各研究会联合办事处通知所属展开签名
    【新华社十五日讯】中国民主同盟总部为响应中国保卫世界和平大会所发动的全国人民在世界拥护和平大会常设委员会宣言上签名的运动，除已发动留京中委及总部全体工作人员签名外，十五日特发出通告，号召民盟各省市地方组织热烈响应这一运动。
    【新华社十五日讯】中国社会科学各研究会联合办事处，为响应世界拥护和平大会常设委员会斯德哥尔摩会议的号召，特通知中国新哲学研究会、中国新史学研究会、中国新经济学研究会、中国新政治学研究会、中国新法学研究会等五学会，号召所属分会及全体会员，广泛展开禁用原子武器的签名运动。
