Title: Five Thousand Youths Gather in Zhongshan Park to Warmly Welcome the Soviet Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1950-05-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd of May - More than 5,000 active youths from all walks of life in the capital, including soldiers, workers, students, cadres and Young Pioneers, gathered in the Zhongshan Park Music Hall on the morning of the 1st to hold a welcoming meeting for the Soviet Youth Delegation. After Liao Chengzhi, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, delivered the opening remarks, Mikhailov, head of the Soviet Youth Delegation, introduced the Soviet youth representatives to the participating youth. When these heroic figures, nurtured under communist education, including combat heroes, labor heroes, model workers, writers, scholars, and artists, appeared in front of the rostrum, thunderous cheers and applause erupted from the audience. Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, in his welcoming speech, called on Chinese youth to learn from the Soviet youth and the Youth League, to learn their patriotism and internationalism, noble qualities and heroic deeds, scientific knowledge and technology, and excellent work style and valuable experience. In his reply, Mikhailov first paid tribute to the victorious Chinese people and youth, and then introduced the brilliant achievements of the Soviet people and youth in the cause of peace, democracy and socialist construction. Kozhedub, who shot down 62 enemy planes in the Great Patriotic War and won three Combat Hero medals, and Gigina, who won the title of Socialist, each described their struggles at the meeting.
Before the meeting ended, various groups presented gifts to the Soviet Youth Delegation. Feng Wenbin and Liao Chengzhi presented a banner on behalf of the Central Committee of the Youth League, which read: "We salute the youth of the Stalin generation, and we will learn from your glorious example and consolidate the unbreakable unity between China and the Soviet Union!" Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, presented a flag of the China New Democratic Youth League on behalf of all League members. Xiao Hua, member of the Central Committee of the Youth League and youth general, presented three sets of military uniforms on behalf of all young soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Representatives of the Chinese Young Pioneers also presented flowers to the delegation.

### 五千青少年集会中山公园   热烈欢迎苏青年代表团

1950-05-03
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　五千青少年集会中山公园
     热烈欢迎苏青年代表团
    【新华社二日讯】首都各界青年积极分子，包括战士、工人、学生、干部及少年儿童队队员等五千余人，于一日上午在中山公园音乐堂召开欢迎苏联青年代表团大会。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会副书记廖承志致开会词后，苏联青年代表团团长米哈依洛夫向与会青年介绍苏联的青年代表。当这些在共产主义教育下培养出来的战斗英雄、劳动英雄、模范工作者、作家、学者及艺术家等英雄人物出现在主席台前时，台下响起了春雷般的欢呼与鼓掌。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬致欢迎词时，号召中国青年向苏联青年及青年团学习，学习他们的爱国主义和国际主义精神、高尚品质与英雄行为、科学知识与技术、以及优良作风与宝贵验经。米哈依洛夫在答词中首先向胜利的中国人民和青年致敬，继即介绍了苏联人民与青年在为和平民主与建设社会主义事业中的光辉成绩。在卫国战争中击落敌机六十二架、荣获三次战斗英雄奖章的阔日杜布和获得社会主义称号的吉金娜，在会上分别叙述了自己的奋斗经历。
    会议结束前，各团体纷纷向苏联青年代表团献礼。冯文彬和廖承志代表青年团中央献锦旗一面，上面写着“我们向斯大林一代的青年致敬，并要学习你们的光辉榜样，巩固中苏两国牢不可破的团结！”青年团中央副书记蒋南翔代表全国团员赠送中国新民主主义青年团团旗一面。青年团中央委员、青年将领萧华代表中国人民解放军全体青年战士赠送军服三套。中国少年儿童队代表也向代表团献花。
