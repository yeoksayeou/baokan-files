Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Food Industry Working Committee Notice: Convene Salt Industry Trade Union Representative Symposium to Prepare for the Establishment of a Unified Salt Industry Trade Union
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 1

　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions Food Industry Working Committee Notice:
　　Convene Salt Industry Trade Union Representative Symposium
　　Preparing to Establish a Unified Salt Industry Trade Union
    [Xinhua News Agency, 11th] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions Food Industry Working Committee issued a notice on the 11th, deciding to convene a symposium of salt industry trade union representatives from all over the country in Beijing from June 10th to 25th this year to discuss the work of salt industry trade unions in various regions and prepare for the establishment of a unified national salt industry trade union organization.

### 全总食品工作委员会通知　召开盐业工会代表座谈会　筹备建立统一的盐业工会

1950-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总食品工作委员会通知
　　召开盐业工会代表座谈会 
    筹备建立统一的盐业工会
    【新华社十一日讯】中华全国总工会食品工作委员会于十一日发出通知，决定今年六月十日至二十五日在北京召开全国各地盐业工会代表座谈会，讨论各地盐业工会工作和筹备建立全国统一的盐业工会组织等问题。
