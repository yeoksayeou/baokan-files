Title: Switzerland Sends Representative to Beijing to Negotiate Establishment of Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1950-05-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 16th. In order to negotiate the establishment of diplomatic relations with the Central People's Government of our country, the Swiss government has sent Mr. Sven Stiner, designated as Chargé d'affaires ad interim, as a negotiating representative. Mr. Stiner arrived in Beijing at 1:30 this afternoon. Han Xu, Section Chief of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to the station to greet him.

### 瑞士派遣代表来京谈判建立外交关系

1950-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　瑞士派遣代表来京谈判建立外交关系
    【新华社十六日讯】为与我国中央人民政府就建立外交关系问题进行谈判，瑞士政府已派遣被指定为临时代办的司文·司丁纳先生为谈判代表。司丁纳氏已于今日下午一时半抵京。我外交部派交际处科长韩叙赴站迎接。
