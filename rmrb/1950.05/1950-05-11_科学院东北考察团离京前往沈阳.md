Title: Academy of Sciences Northeast Investigation Group Departs Beijing for Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1950-05-11
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Northeast Investigation Group organized by the Chinese Academy of Sciences departed Beijing for Shenyang on the 9th. The investigation group includes experts in physics, chemistry, metallurgy, agricultural climate, agricultural insect pests, etc., and is headed by Vice President Zhu Kezhen. After arriving in the Northeast, the group will go to relevant production departments to conduct on-the-spot research on the technical problems existing in production and construction.
　　In addition, the sixteen people in the Physics Mining Training Class sponsored by the Academy and the eleven researchers from the Academy's Institute of Geology will also go to the Northeast within days to engage in mineral surveying and exploration.

### 科学院东北考察团离京前往沈阳

1950-05-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　科学院东北考察团离京前往沈阳
    【新华社九日讯】中国科学院组织的东北考察团已于九日离京前往沈阳。考察团包括物理、化学、冶金、农业气候、农业虫害等方面的专家，由竺可桢副院长任团长。该团到东北后，将分别到有关生产部门去实地研究生产建设中在技术方面所存在的问题。
    又该院主办的物理采矿训练班十六人和该院地质研究所研究人员十一人亦将于日内到东北去从事矿产的测勘工作。
