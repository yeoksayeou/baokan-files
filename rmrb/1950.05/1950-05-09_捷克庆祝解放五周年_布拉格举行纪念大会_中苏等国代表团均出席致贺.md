Title: Czechoslovakia Celebrates the Fifth Anniversary of Liberation; Commemorative Meeting Held in Prague; Delegations from China, the Soviet Union and Other Countries Attended to Offer Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1950-05-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Eighth Dispatch from Prague: A commemorative meeting celebrating the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia by the Soviet Army was held in Prague on the 6th. Attendees included Czechoslovak President Gottwald, Prime Minister Zápotocký, and representatives from all sectors of society. The Soviet government delegation, led by Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Marshal Bulganin, the Chinese delegation, delegations from various people's democratic countries, and delegations from other countries were also in attendance.

　　Zápotocký delivered a long speech at the meeting. When Bulganin spoke on behalf of the Soviet government, the audience cheered for a long time for the great Soviet Union and Stalin, the teacher of the working people of the world. The head of the Chinese delegation and the heads of other delegations also delivered congratulatory speeches to the meeting. The meeting also passed a telegram of salute to Marshal Stalin.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Seventh Dispatch from Prague, Fifth Dispatch: The Soviet government delegation has arrived in Prague to participate in the celebration of the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia. The delegation is led by Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Marshal Bulganin, and includes Suslov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party, Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zorin, and Lieutenant General Dimchev.

　　Those who went to the airport to welcome them included government members led by Czech Prime Minister Zápotocký, Czech Communist Party General Secretary Slánský, and others.

### 捷克庆祝解放五周年  布拉格举行纪念大会  中苏等国代表团均出席致贺

1950-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷克庆祝解放五周年
    布拉格举行纪念大会         
    中苏等国代表团均出席致贺
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：捷京于六日举行庆祝苏军解放捷克斯洛伐克五周年纪念大会。出席大会者有捷总统哥特瓦尔德，总理萨波托斯基及各界代表。以苏联部长会议副主席布尔加宁元帅为首的苏联政府代表团，中国代表团，各人民民主国家的代表团及其他国家的代表团亦均出席。
    萨波托斯基在会上发表长篇演说。当布尔加宁代表苏联政府演说之际，会众经久不息地为伟大苏联和世界劳动人民的导师斯大林而欢呼。中国代表团团长及各国代表团团长亦分别向大会致贺词。大会并通过向斯大林大元帅致敬电。
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社布拉格五日讯：苏联政府代表团顷已抵达布拉格，参加捷克斯洛伐克解放五周年的庆祝大会。代表团系由苏联部长会议副主席、苏联元帅布尔加宁率领，团员有联共中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫，苏联外交部副部长佐林和迪姆采夫中将。
    赴机场欢迎的有以捷总理萨波托斯基为首的政府委员，捷共总书记斯兰斯基等人。
