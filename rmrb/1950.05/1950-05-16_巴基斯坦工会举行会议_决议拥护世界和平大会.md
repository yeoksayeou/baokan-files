Title: Pakistani Trade Union Holds Meeting, Resolves to Support World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-05-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Dateline Moscow, May 15th, via TASS from PTI Bombay: The Pakistan Federation of Trade Unions recently held a meeting in Lahore. The meeting passed resolutions demanding the nationalization of all foreign-owned industries and major industries; and demanding the abolition of landlord privileges without compensation, giving land to those who cultivate it. The resolution supports the World Peace Congress and condemns the war plans of British and American imperialists. The resolution also demands the cessation of aid to the reactionary Burmese government and recognition of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Ibrahim, Chairman of the Pakistan Federation of Trade Unions, pointed out in his speech at the meeting: British imperialists extract 80 million rupees (approximately US$24 million) annually from their investments in Pakistani railways and waterways. He pointed out that more and more workers have organized under the banner of the Federation of Trade Unions, and unorganized workers are also forced to take militant action to protect their minimum living standards.

### 巴基斯坦工会举行会议　决议拥护世界和平大会

1950-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦工会举行会议
　　决议拥护世界和平大会
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社十二日电引电通社孟买消息：巴基斯坦总工会最近在拉合尔举行会议。会议通过各项决议，要求将所有外国人拥有的工业和主要的工业收归国有；要求无偿的废止地主的特权，把土地给予耕种者。决议拥护世界和平大会，抨击英美帝国主义者的战争计划。决议并要求停止援助反动的缅甸政府，要求承认越南民主共和国。
    巴基斯坦总工会的主席伊布拉新在会议的发言中指出：英国帝国主义者每年要从他们在巴基斯坦铁路和水道的投资中榨取八千万卢比（约合二千四百万美元）。他指出越来越多的工人已在工会联合会的旗帜下组织起来，没有组织起来的工人也被迫采取战斗性的行动，以保障他们最低限度的生活标准。
