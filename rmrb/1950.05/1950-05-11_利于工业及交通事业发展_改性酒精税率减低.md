Title: Reduced Tax Rate on Denatured Alcohol to Benefit Industrial and Transportation Development
Author:
Date: 1950-05-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 10th -- The Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government has submitted to and been approved by the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council to reduce the tax rate on denatured alcohol to 20 percent ad valorem. The Ministry of Finance has instructed the State Taxation Administration to notify tax authorities across the country to implement this. Denatured alcohol is a necessary raw material in some industries and can be used as a power fuel to replace gasoline, which is beneficial to the development of industry and transportation. Therefore, the Ministry of Finance has reduced the tax rate accordingly. The tax rate for ordinary alcohol will remain at 100 percent.

### 利于工业及交通事业发展　改性酒精税率减低

1950-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　利于工业及交通事业发展
    改性酒精税率减低
    【新华社十日讯】中央人民政府财政部已呈准政务院财政经济委员会将改性酒精税率减低为从价百分之二十。财政部已令税务总局通知全国各地税务机关遵照执行。按改性酒精系若干工业中必需的原料，且能代替汽油用作动力燃料，有利于工业及交通事业之发展，故财政部在税率方面予以减低。至普通酒精税率则仍按百分之百征收。
