Title: Adjusting Taxation to Improve Tax Work: Second National Tax Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1950-05-30
Page: 2

　　Adjusting Taxation to Improve Tax Work
    Second National Tax Conference Opens
    [Xinhua News Agency, 27th] The second National Tax Conference, convened by the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government, opened in Beijing on the 27th. With the exception of representatives from the Southwest region who are still en route, 42 directors of tax bureaus from major administrative regions, provinces, and cities across the country, as well as 46 representatives from industrial and commercial circles from various regions recommended by local industrial and commercial sectors and hired by the government, attended the conference. The purpose of the conference is to adjust taxation, review the shortcomings existing in the current tax work, and prescribe methods for correcting these shortcomings, so that taxation is more in line with the principles of simplifying the tax system and rational burden. The conference will first hear reports from representatives from various regions, and then conduct discussions in several groups by region; at the same time, three specialized groups on industrial and commercial tax, goods tax, and local tax will be organized to study various plans separately.

### 调整税收改进税务工作  二届全国税务会议开幕

1950-05-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　调整税收改进税务工作
    二届全国税务会议开幕
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府财政部所召开的第二届全国税务会议已于二十七日在京开幕。除西南区代表尚在途中外，出席会议的有全国各大行政区、各省市税务局局长四十二人及由各地工商界推选政府聘请的各地工商业界代表四十六人。会议的目的在于调整税收，检讨目前税收工作中所存在的缺点，并规定改正缺点的办法，使税收更加合乎简化税制和合理负担的原则。会议首先听取各地代表报告，然后将按地区分若干小组进行讨论；同时，并组织工商业税、货物税、地方税三个专门小组分别研究各种方案。
