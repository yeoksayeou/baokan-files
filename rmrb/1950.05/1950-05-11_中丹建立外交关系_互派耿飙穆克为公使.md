Title: China and Denmark Establish Diplomatic Relations; Geng Biao and Mook Appointed as Ministers

Author:

Date: 1950-05-11
Page: 1

　　China and Denmark Establish Diplomatic Relations
    Mutual Appointment of Geng Biao and Mook as Ministers
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 11th] The Danish government announced on January 9th of this year its willingness to establish diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China. Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs replied to Foreign Minister Rasmussen of the Danish government on January 14th, expressing the same desire and requesting the Danish government to send a representative to Beijing to negotiate the preliminary and procedural matters concerning the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Denmark. Mr. Mook, the Chargé d'affaires ad interim dispatched by the Danish government, arrived in Beijing on February 26th and immediately began these negotiations, which have now concluded successfully. China and Denmark have also agreed that the Danish government will appoint Mook as Minister to the People's Republic of China, and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China will appoint General Geng Biao as Minister to Denmark.

### 中丹建立外交关系　互派耿飙穆克为公使

1950-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　中丹建立外交关系
    互派耿飙穆克为公使
    【新华社十一日讯】丹麦政府于本年一月九日宣布愿与中华人民共和国建立外交关系，我外交部周恩来部长当于一月十四日电复丹麦政府外交部部长拉斯木森，表示同样愿望，并请丹麦政府派遣代表前来北京，就中丹两国建立外交关系的初步与程序问题进行谈判。丹麦政府派遣的谈判代表穆克临时代办于二月二十六日到达北京，随即开始进行此项谈判，现谈判已顺利结束。中丹两国并已同意：丹麦政府派遣穆克为驻中华人民共和国公使，中华人民共和国中央人民政府派遣耿飙将军为中华人民共和国驻丹麦公使。
