Title: Southwest People's Revolutionary University Chongqing Main Campus Officially Opens

Author:
Date: 1950-05-18
Page: 1

　　Southwest People's Revolutionary University
　　Chongqing Main Campus Officially Opens
    [Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 17th] The Southwest People's Revolutionary University Chongqing Main Campus, after more than a month of preparation, officially opened on the 14th. Liu Bocheng serves concurrently as the president of the university, and Zhang Ziyi serves as the vice president. The first class has 6,000 students.

### 西南人民革命大学　重庆总校正式开学

1950-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　西南人民革命大学
　　重庆总校正式开学
    【新华社重庆十七日电】西南人民革命大学重庆总校，经月余筹备，已于十四日正式开学。该校由刘伯承兼任校长，张子意任副校长。第一期学员六千人。
