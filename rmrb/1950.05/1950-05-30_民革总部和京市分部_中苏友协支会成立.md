Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Headquarters and Beijing Municipal Branch: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Branches Established
Author:
Date: 1950-05-30
Page: 3

　　Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Headquarters and Beijing Municipal Branch
    Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Branches Established
    [Report from this newspaper] The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association branch jointly organized by the Headquarters and the Beijing Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held its inaugural meeting at 9:00 AM on the 28th in the hall of the association. Over a hundred people attended, including Li Jishen, Shao Lizi, Ning Wu, Zhou Fanwen, Xu Baoqian, Weng Shiwu, Su Congzhou, and Fu Xuewen. At the meeting, Ning Wu first presided and reported on the preparations and organizational charter. Li Jishen spoke on behalf of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, addressing the work done since the establishment of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and future work plans. Shao Lizi spoke on "Understanding the Soviet Union, Learning from the Soviet Union." Finally, Ning Wu and Fu Xuewen were elected as the chairman and vice-chairman, respectively, and Xu Jinfan, Ren Jifu, and Zhao Renqing were appointed as organizers, service personnel, and clerks, respectively.

### 民革总部和京市分部  中苏友协支会成立

1950-05-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　民革总部和京市分部
    中苏友协支会成立
    【本报讯】中国国民党革命委员会总部和北京市分部联合组织的中苏友好协会支会，于二十八日上午九时在该会礼堂开成立大会，出席李济深、邵力子、宁武、周范文、许宝騤、翁世五、苏从周、傅学文等百余人，大会先由宁武主席并报告筹备经过和组织章程；李济深代表中苏友协讲话，对于中苏友协成立后的工作及今后的工作计划；邵力子讲“认识苏联、学习苏联”。最后推选宁武、傅学文为正副主任，胥锦帆、任吉甫、赵任情分任组织、服务、文书干事。
