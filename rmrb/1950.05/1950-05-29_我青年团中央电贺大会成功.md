Title: Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League Sends Congratulations on Successful Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League recently received a telegram from the Free German Youth League, inviting them to send representatives to attend the All-German Democratic Youth Congress held in Berlin on May 26th. The Central Committee of the Youth League has sent Xu Liqun, Wu Xueqian, Liang Geng, Li Guoying, and others to attend, and sent a telegram of congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows: Free German Youth League, Berlin:

The Chinese youth warmly congratulate the success of the All-German Democratic Youth Congress. We firmly believe that the united action of the German youth will powerfully strengthen the German people's struggle for national reunification and national independence, and make a powerful contribution to the cause of defending world peace.

                            Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League
                                       May 27th

### 我青年团中央电贺大会成功

1950-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　我青年团中央电贺大会成功
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会日前收到自由德国青年联盟来电，邀请派遣代表参加在五月二十六日在柏林举行的全德民主青年大会。青年团中央已派许立群、吴学谦、梁畊、李国英等前往出席，并致电祝贺。电文如下：柏林自由德国青年联盟：
    中国青年热烈地祝贺全德民主青年大会的成功。深信德国青年的团结行动将有力地加强德国人民争取国家统一、民族独立的斗争，并予保卫世界和平事业以有力贡献。
                            中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
                                       五月二十七日
