Title: Central South Region Flood Control Headquarters Established
Author:
Date: 1950-05-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, May 29th: The Central South Region Flood Control Headquarters has been formally established. Deng Zihui, Vice Chairman of the Central South Military and Political Committee, will serve as the Director, Minister of Water Resources Liu Fei, and Zhao Erlu, Chief of Staff of the Central South Military Region, will serve as Deputy Directors. The main tasks of the current flood control work are: to repair and defend the main dikes of the Yangtze and Han Rivers, to ensure that the Yangtze River does not breach even when it rises to the highest water level of 1949, and that the Han River does not breach when the flow at the Nianpanshan station does not exceed 12,000 cubic meters per second, thereby protecting 20 million mu of farmland along the Yangtze and Han Rivers from flooding.

### 中南区防汛指挥部成立

1950-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　中南区防汛指挥部成立
    【新华社汉口二十九日电】中南区防汛指挥部已正式成立，由中南军政委员会邓子恢副主席任主任，水利部刘斐部长，中南军区参谋长赵尔陆任副主任。目前防汛工作的主要任务为：修防长江和汉水的干堤，保证长江涨至一九四九年最高水位时不致溃决，汉水在碾盘山流量不超过一万二千秒立方公尺时不溃口，借以保障沿长江、汉水的二千万市亩农田免受水灾。
