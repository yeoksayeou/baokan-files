Title: Demonstrating Determination to Ensure Lasting Peace: Signature Drives Unfold in Poland and Czechoslovakia; Hungarian and Bulgarian Peace Signature Campaigns Successfully Completed
Author:
Date: 1950-05-17
Page: 4

　　Demonstrating Determination to Ensure Lasting Peace
　　Signature Drives Unfold in Poland and Czechoslovakia
　　Hungarian and Bulgarian Peace Signature Campaigns Successfully Completed
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 16th: TASS reports: The signature campaign for the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace continues to unfold across Poland.

    In Łódź alone, 100,000 people signed on May 14th. In Kraków, tens of thousands of activists from the Women's League, the Polish Youth League, and other organizations are participating in the mass signature campaign. Catholic priests are also participating in the peace signature campaign.
    Xinhua News Agency: TASS, Prague, 14th: On May 14th, Czechoslovakia launched a campaign to collect signatures on the appeal of the Stockholm meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress amidst a national upsurge.

    A state peace conference was held in Pilsen, attended by representatives from all districts of the state. The Chairman of the Czechoslovak Peace Committee, Ms. Hrdinová-Spurná, delivered a speech at the conference, urging peace fighters to redouble their efforts and emphasizing the great significance of collecting signatures of millions on the appeal of the World Congress and the Standing Committee.

    By signing this appeal, the working people of Czechoslovakia have demonstrated their determination to ensure lasting peace.
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Comprehensive TASS report: The working people of Hungary and Bulgaria warmly support the appeal of the Stockholm meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace.

    The Hungarian National Peace Committee announced at its meeting on the 13th that the work of collecting signatures and establishing local peace committees has been successively completed throughout the country. Out of the national population of 9.2 million, 7,231,329 people have signed the appeal of the Stockholm meeting. Twenty-seven thousand local peace committees have been established in various places.

    The peace signature campaign in Bulgaria is also nearing completion. According to the preliminary estimates of the Bulgarian National Peace Committee, out of the national population of 7 million, 6,144,560 people have signed the appeal.

### 表示确保持久和平的决心　波捷各地展开签名运动　匈保和平签名工作胜利完成

1950-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　表示确保持久和平的决心
　　波捷各地展开签名运动
　　匈保和平签名工作胜利完成
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社华沙讯：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会呼吁书的签名运动继续在波兰全国各地展开。
    仅五月十四日一天内，在罗兹就有十万人签名。在克拉科，数以万计的妇女联盟、波兰青年联盟和其他团体的积极分子，都正在参加群众性的签名运动。天主教教士也参加了和平签名运动。
    【新华社讯】塔斯社布拉格十四日电：五月十四日，捷克斯洛伐克在全国高潮中开始了征求在世界和平大会常设委员会斯德哥尔摩会议呼吁书上签名的运动。
    比尔森曾举行州和平大会，出席者有全州各区的代表。捷克斯洛伐克和平委员会主席荷第诺娃——斯普尔纳曾在大会上发表演说，她敦促和平战士们加倍努力，并且强调指出征求数百万人在世界和大常委会呼吁书上签名的重大意义。
    捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民由于在这个呼吁书上签了名，就表示了他们要确保持久和平的决心。
    【新华社十六日讯】综合塔斯社消息：匈牙利与保加利亚的劳动人民热烈拥护世界拥护和平大会常设委员会斯德哥尔摩会议的呼吁书。
    据匈牙利全国和平委员会十三日会议宣布：关于征求签名和成立地方和平委员会的工作，已在全国各地相继完成。在全国九百二十万人口中，已有七百二十三万一千三百二十九人在斯德哥尔摩会议的呼吁书上签了名。各地已成立了二万七千处地方和平委员会。
    保加利亚的和平签名运动亦将完成。根据保加利亚全国和平委员会的初步估计：全国七百万人口中，已有六百十四万四千五百六十人在呼吁书上签了名。
