　　Publisher's Statement
    Liberation Press
    We believe that Comrade Wang Ruoshui's criticism of the Chinese translation of "Ludwig Feuerbach and the End of Classical German Philosophy," published by our press, is fundamentally correct. With the exception of Comrade Zhang Zhongshi, who will provide a separate response, we have decided to re-proofread "Ludwig Feuerbach and the End of Classical German Philosophy" for a new edition. We welcome readers to offer similar criticisms and suggestions regarding other books published by our press.

### 出版者的声明

1950-05-31

专栏：

　　出版者的声明
    解放社
    我们认为王若水同志对本社出版的《费尔巴哈与德国古典哲学的终结》中译本的批评意见，是基本上正确的。除张仲实同志另作答复外，我们决定将《费尔巴哈与德国古典哲学的终结》一书重新校正，以便再版。对于本社所出版的其他书籍，欢迎读者同样提出批评与意见。
