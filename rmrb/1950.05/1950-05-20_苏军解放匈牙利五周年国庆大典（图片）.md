Title: Celebration of the Fifth Anniversary of the Liberation of Hungary by the Soviet Army (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1950-05-20
Page: 5

On the evening of April 3rd, the Hungarian government held a celebration in the Budapest Opera House, the Hungarian capital. Delegations from various countries and envoys to Hungary attended the grand ceremony. In the picture, from right to left: Chairman István Dobi of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic, Lieutenant General Bian Zhangwu, head of the Chinese People's Republic delegation, Mátyás Rákosi, General Secretary of the Hungarian Workers' Party, Kliment Voroshilov, Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Marshal of the Soviet Union, and Hungarian model workers.
