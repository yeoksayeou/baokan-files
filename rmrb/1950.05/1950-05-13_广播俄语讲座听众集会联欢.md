Title: Broadcast Russian Language Lecture Listener Gathering and Get-Together

Author:

Date: 1950-05-13
Page: 3

Beijing News Agency

　　Broadcast Russian Language Lecture Listener Gathering and Get-Together
    [Report from this newspaper] In order to provide listeners with the opportunity to exchange experiences and socialize, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station's broadcast Russian language lecture series will hold a listener get-together this Sunday (the 14th) at 7:00 PM in the auditorium of the Liberal Arts College of the Teachers' University on Shifuma Street. Approximately 500 listeners are expected to attend, with listeners from areas such as Fengtai traveling long distances to attend. In addition to brief speeches by responsible comrades from the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Broadcasting Station, a film will be shown for entertainment.

### 广播俄语讲座听众集会联欢

1950-05-13
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻处

　　广播俄语讲座听众集会联欢
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会和北京市人民广播电台合办的广播俄语讲座，为使听众有机会交流经验和联欢，定于本星期日（十四日）下午七时在石驸马大街师范大学文学院礼堂举行广播俄语讲座听众联欢会。届时将有五百左右的听众参加，丰台等地区的听众也将远道赶来。会上除去北京市中苏友协和电台的负责同志做简单的讲话以外，并演出电影助兴。
