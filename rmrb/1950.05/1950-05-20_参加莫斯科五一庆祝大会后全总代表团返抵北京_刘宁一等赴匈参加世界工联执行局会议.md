Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Delegation Returns to Beijing After Attending Moscow May Day Celebrations; Liu Ningyi and Others Go to Hungary to Attend World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-05-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions delegation, invited by the Central Committee of the Soviet Union Central Council of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebration in Moscow, returned to Beijing on the 19th. The delegation was led by Wang Yiqing, Deputy Director of the Organization Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and included Zhao Guoyou, a famous labor hero from the Northeast, Hang Huilan (female), director of the trade union of Shanghai No. 1 Textile Factory, Wang Wenbin, worker-turned-deputy director of the Beijing Electric Power Plant, Xu Xin (female), vice chairman of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, Jin Ziming, vice chairman of the Nanjing Federation of Trade Unions, and Zhang Fang, director of the Planning Department of the Ministry of Heavy Industry, a total of seven people. Xu Zhizhen, Secretary-General and Director of the General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Yi Lirong, Minister of Labor Protection, Hou Yutian, Minister of Labor Insurance, and Feng Suhai, Deputy Minister of Culture and Education, and many others went to welcome the delegation. According to Wang Yiqing, the deputy head of the delegation: In addition to attending the "May Day" celebration, the delegation stayed in Moscow, Leningrad and other places for three weeks, visited many factories, and received a warm welcome from Soviet workers and people from all over. Everywhere the delegates went, they witnessed the high tide of socialist construction and the enthusiasm of Soviet workers in striving to build and defend world peace. The delegates will soon tell the staff and workers in various places everything they saw in the Soviet Union.
Furthermore, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Zhang Qi, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, who originally led the delegation abroad, went to Budapest to attend the World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Committee meeting after attending the Moscow "May Day" celebration.

### 参加莫斯科五一庆祝大会后全总代表团返抵北京　刘宁一等赴匈参加世界工联执行局会议

1950-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加莫斯科五一庆祝大会后全总代表团返抵北京
　　刘宁一等赴匈参加世界工联执行局会议
    【新华社十九日讯】应苏联总工会中央委员会邀请去莫斯科参加“五一”庆祝大会的中华全国总工会代表团已于十九日返抵北京。代表团系由全国总工会组织部副部长王亦清率领，包括东北著名劳动英雄赵国有、上海中纺一厂工会主任杭惠兰（女）、工人出身的北京电业厂副厂长王文斌、中国邮电工会副主席徐欣（女）、南京总工会副主席金子明、重工业部计划司司长张方等共七人。前往欢迎代表团的有全国总工会秘书长兼办公厅主任许之桢、劳动保护部部长易礼容、劳动保险部部长侯玉田、文教部副部长冯宿海等多人。据代表团副团长王亦清谈：代表团除参加“五一”庆祝大会外，并在莫斯科、列宁格勒等地停留了三周，参观了很多工厂，受到苏联工人和各地人民的热烈欢迎。代表们所到的地方都表现着社会主义建设的高潮，以及苏联工人为保卫世界和平努力建设的热诚。代表们将在最近把在苏联亲眼看到的一切情形告诉给各地职工。
    又原率领代表团出国的中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一和上海总工会副主席张祺，在参加莫斯科“五一”节庆祝大会后，即前往布达佩斯参加世界工联执行局会议。
