Title: North Korea and Vietnam Welcome International Children's Day; Prepare to Build Nurseries and Children's Camps
Author:
Date: 1950-05-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency report: North Korea and Vietnam are preparing to welcome International Children's Day on June 1st.

　　The Korean Democratic Women's League will expand the equipment of nurseries and children's parks in various places around Children's Day, and plans to establish seasonal nurseries, kindergartens, and the like. It is also preparing to organize慰问teams to visit the schools for children of revolutionary martyrs and the dormitories of various factories to comfort the children. The various organizations of the Korean Democratic Women's League will hold celebrations on June 1st. In addition, the local Democratic Youth Leagues will also organize demonstrations and parades of Young Pioneers on the same day.

　　The fathers of Vietnamese children are building children's camps and raising funds for the children of soldiers. Many mothers' literacy classes and children's parks will also be established on this day.

### 北朝鲜和越南  迎接国际儿童节  筹建保育院儿童营

1950-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜和越南
    迎接国际儿童节
    筹建保育院儿童营
    【本报讯】据新华社消息：北朝鲜和越南正筹备迎接六月一日国际儿童节。
    北朝鲜民主妇女同盟将于儿童节前后，扩充各地托儿所和儿童公园的设备，并计划创办季节托儿所、保育院、幼稚园等。并准备组织慰问队前往革命烈属子弟学校和各工厂的宿舍，慰问儿童。北朝鲜民主妇女同盟的各级组织，均将于六月一日举行庆祝会。此外，各地民主青年同盟亦将于同日组织少年团的示威游行。
    越南儿童的父亲们正在筹建儿童营，并为战士的孩子们募款。许多母亲补习班和儿童公园，也将在这天成立。
