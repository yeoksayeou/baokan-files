Title: The Thinking and Feelings of Progressive People All Over the World Unanimously Turn to the Peace Bastion of the Soviet Union; Pravda Publishes Congratulatory Letters from Representatives of People's Democratic Countries
Author:
Date: 1950-05-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th (via TASS): Pravda recently published letters from delegations of various People's Democratic countries, which vividly illustrate that the thoughts and feelings of all progressive mankind are directed towards the Soviet Union—the powerful bastion of peace, democracy, and socialism.

　　Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, wrote: During our stay in Moscow—the capital of all progressive mankind—the joy and excitement of the members of our Chinese delegation are beyond description. For the Chinese working people, Moscow is extraordinarily familiar and dear. Anything associated with these words is welcomed and loved in the People's Republic of China.

　　This year's May Day is of particularly great significance to the Chinese working people. We are celebrating this festival for the first time in the free People's Republic of China. The gift of this festival is the first great labor achievement of hundreds of millions of people, who are building a new and happy life under the leadership of our leader Mao Zedong. Whether we are laboring or fighting, we are inspired by the great example of the Soviet Union. On behalf of the 475 million Chinese people, we extend warm greetings to the people of Moscow and congratulate them on May Day.

　　Polish artist Szpinski wrote: All the creative efforts of Polish artists are inspired by the example of the Soviet people.

　　Bulgarian writer Polyanov said: We, the Bulgarian people, have never felt so strong and proud as we do today, because we are supported by the friendly assistance of the great Soviet Union in building our new life.

　　The appeal of Shifket Musaraj, Chairman of the Albanian Writers Union, is: Fight for universal peace, for democracy, for socialism. He said: The Albanian people feel for the first time that they are equal to all countries in the world. With Stalin at our side, we are invincible!

### 世界进步人类思想感情一致归向和平堡垒苏联  真理报刊载人民民主国家代表贺信

1950-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界进步人类思想感情一致归向和平堡垒苏联     
    真理报刊载人民民主国家代表贺信
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科三十日讯：真理报顷刊载各人民民主国家代表团的信件，这些信件生动地说明了全体进步人类的思想和感情都归向于苏联——和平、民主及社会主义的强大堡垒。
    中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一的信说：在逗留莫斯科——全体进步人类的首都——的期间，我们中国代表团团员的高兴和激动是难以形容的。对于中国劳动人民来说，莫斯科是异常熟悉而亲热。任何和这几个字相联系的事物，都在中华人民共和国里受到欢迎和热爱。
    今年的五一对中国劳动人民来说，是具有特别重大的意义的。我们第一次在自由的中华人民共和国内庆祝这个节日。这一个节日的礼物，是成百万人民的第一次巨大劳动成就，他们正在我们的领袖毛泽东的领导之下建设起新的幸福生活。无论我们是在劳动或是斗争，我们都从苏联的伟大范例得到了鼓舞。我们代表四万万七千五百万中国人民，向莫斯科人致热烈的敬意，并向他们庆贺五一节。
    波兰艺术家赛平斯基的信说：波兰艺术工作者的一切创造性努力，都得到了苏联人民范例的鼓舞。
    保加利亚作家波利扬诺夫说：我们保加利亚人民从未感到像今天这样的强大和骄傲，这是由于我们从伟大苏联的友好协助中得到支持，建立了我们的新生活。
    阿尔巴尼亚作家联盟主席希夫开特·穆萨莱的号召是：为普遍和平、为民主、为社会主义而斗争。他说：阿尔巴尼亚人民第一次感到他们和全世界国家是平等的。和斯大林在一齐，我们是无敌的！
