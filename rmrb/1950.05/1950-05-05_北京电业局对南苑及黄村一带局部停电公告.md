Title: Beijing Electricity Bureau Notice Regarding Partial Power Outage in Nanyuan and Huangcun Area
Author:
Date: 1950-05-05
Page: 1

Beijing Electricity Bureau Notice Regarding Partial Power Outage in Nanyuan and Huangcun Area
    (50) Jing Gong Zi No. 0840
    The bureau is carrying out work to repair and replace knife switches on the Nanyuan and Huangcun area power distribution lines. A power outage will occur in the Nanyuan and Huangcun area (including Nanyuan Airport, Huangcun, and Dahongmen Radio Station) from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM on May 5th. We hope users will understand and forgive this inconvenience.
