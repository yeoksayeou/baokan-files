Title: Indonesian, Burmese, and Filipino Laboring People Hold Celebrations

Author:

Date: 1950-05-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch: Laboring people in Southeast Asian countries such as Indonesia, Burma, and the Philippines enthusiastically celebrated International Labor Day.
    In Indonesia, according to a dispatch from Antara News Agency, Jakarta, approximately 80,000 Indonesian workers held a mass rally in Jakarta's largest Freedom Square on the 1st, carrying huge portraits of Marx, Lenin, Stalin, and Mao Zedong to celebrate May Day. Representatives of various trade unions delivered speeches successively. They unanimously emphasized the great significance of May Day for workers around the world and Indonesian workers, and pointed out the significant role played by the laboring people in the struggle for Indonesia's freedom.
    In Burma, according to a dispatch from Associated Press: Thousands of Burmese workers held a May Day demonstration in Yangon on the 1st. The demonstration队伍 carried portraits of Lenin, Stalin, and Mao Zedong, and held high banners demanding that Britain end its control of Burmese oil companies.
    According to a dispatch from United Press Manila: Hundreds of thousands of Filipino workers also celebrated Labor Day under the "strict guard" of the Quirino government.

### 印尼·缅甸·菲律宾劳动人民开庆祝会

1950-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　印尼·缅甸·菲律宾劳动人民开庆祝会
    【新华社二日讯】东南亚印尼、缅甸与菲律宾等国的劳动人民，热烈庆祝五一国际劳动节。
    在印尼，据安尼塔社雅加达讯，印尼工人约八万名于一日抬着马克思、列宁、斯大林和毛泽东等人的巨幅画像，在雅加达最大的自由广场召开庆祝五一节的群众大会。各工会组织的代表先后发表演说。他们一致强调五一节对世界工人及印尼工人的重大意义，并指出在为印尼自由的斗争中，劳动人民所起的重大作用。
    在缅甸，据美联社讯：数以千计的缅甸工人一日在仰光举行五一节示威游行。游行队伍抬着列宁、斯大林和毛泽东的画像，并且高举要求英国终止控制缅甸石油公司的旗帜。
    另据合众社马尼拉讯：菲律宾数十万工人，亦在季里诺政府“严加戒备”的情况下，庆祝了五一劳动节。
