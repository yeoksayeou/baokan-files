Title: Celebrating "June 1st" International Children's Day - Preparatory Committee Decides on Seven Activity Plans
Author:
Date: 1950-05-15
Page: 3

Beijing News

　　Celebrating "June 1st" International Children's Day
　  Preparatory Committee Decides on Seven Activity Plans
    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] "June 1st" International Children's Day is fast approaching. The Capital Democratic Women's Federation, the Communist Youth League Municipal Working Committee, and the Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education, along with more than twenty other organizations and groups, responded to the call of the All-China "June 1st" International Children's Day Preparatory Committee and formally established the "Capital Celebrating June 1st International Children's Day Preparatory Committee" on the 13th at the Municipal Democratic Women's Federation. The committee is preparing to organize diverse and widespread activities so that the children of the capital can spend their holiday joyfully; and through the Children's Day activities, inspire children's internationalism and patriotism. The preparatory committee decided on the following activity plans: ㈠ Hold celebration meetings (not exceeding two hours) in primary schools, kindergartens, nurseries, and districts, inviting leaders, labor heroes, and writers to meet with the children; ㈡ Hold a city-wide representative celebration meeting and a get-together with international children in Beijing; ㈢ Consult with relevant agencies to provide preferential treatment for children to watch movies, visit parks, buy toys and books, and ride trams and buses around "June 1st"; ㈣ Conduct focused health check-ups for children; ㈤ Hold mothers' meetings; ㈥ Invite prominent figures from all walks of life to sign an appeal for protecting children's rights; ㈦ Call on children to voluntarily write letters to children in the Soviet Union, people's democratic countries, and colonial countries.
    The preparatory committee elected Vice Mayor Wu Han, director of the Beijing Municipal Maternal and Child Welfare Committee, as the director, and Zhang Xiaomei, chairman of the Municipal Democratic Women's Federation, Yang Bozhen, deputy secretary of the Communist Youth League Municipal Working Committee, Weng Dujian, director of the Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education, and Yan Jingqing, director of the Public Health Bureau, as deputy directors.

### 庆祝“六一”国际儿童节  筹委会决定七项活动计划

1950-05-15
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　庆祝“六一”国际儿童节
　  筹委会决定七项活动计划
    【京市新闻处讯】“六一”国际儿童节快到了。首都市民主妇女联合会、青年团市工委会及市文教局等二十余机关团体响应中华全国“六一”国际儿童节筹委会的号召，于十三日在市民主妇联正式成立“首都庆祝六一国际儿童节筹委会”。准备组织分散的广泛的各种活动，使首都的孩子们能在欢乐中度过自己的节日；并通过儿童节活动，启发儿童的国际主义与爱国主义思想。筹委会决定活动计划如下：㈠以小学、幼稚园、托儿所、区为单位举行庆祝会（不超过二小时），约请首长、劳动英雄和作家与儿童见面；㈡举行一个全市代表性的庆祝会，和在京的国际小朋友联欢；㈢洽请有关机关于“六一”前后优待儿童看电影，游公园，买玩具和图书，乘坐电车、公共汽车等；㈣有重点地进行儿童健康检查；㈤举行母亲会；㈥请各界有名望人士在保卫儿童权利的呼吁书上签名；㈦号召儿童自愿地给苏联、人民民主国家，及殖民地国家小朋友写信。
    筹委会推选了北京市妇幼福利委员会主任委员吴晗副市长为主任，市民主妇联主席张晓梅，团市工委副书记杨伯箴，市文教局局长翁独健，公共卫生局局长严镜清等为副主任。
