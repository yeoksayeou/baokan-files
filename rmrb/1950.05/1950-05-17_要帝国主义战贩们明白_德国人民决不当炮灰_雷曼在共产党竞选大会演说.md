Title: Make the Imperialist Warmongers Understand: The German People Will Never Be Cannon Fodder – Speech by Lehmann at Communist Election Rally
Author:
Date: 1950-05-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, dated 16th. TASS dispatch citing Deutsche Nachrichten-Agentur (German News Agency): The Chairman of the Communist Party of West Germany, Lehmann, delivered the opening speech on May 12th in Düsseldorf during the election campaign of the Communist Party of North Rhine-Westphalia. Attending the meeting were 1,400 people, including Communist Party representatives from various regions, worker representatives, and Communist Party state assembly members. In his speech, Lehmann pointed out: The task of participating in the state assembly election campaign this time is to make the imperialist warmongers and their accomplices in Germany understand: The German people and German youth are unwilling to die in war; to make them understand: The German people demand the establishment of a unified, democratic, and peace-loving Germany, and demand the establishment of friendly relations with all countries.

### 要帝国主义战贩们明白　德国人民决不当炮灰　雷曼在共产党竞选大会演说

1950-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　要帝国主义战贩们明白
　　德国人民决不当炮灰       
    雷曼在共产党竞选大会演说
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社引德意志通讯社讯：西德共产党主席雷曼于五月十二日在杜塞尔多夫市内举行的北莱茵——威斯特伐利亚州共产党竞选运动中，作开幕演说。参加会议的有各地共产党代表、工人代表、共产党州议员等一千四百人。雷曼于会中致词时指出：这次参加州议会竞选运动的任务，是要使帝国主义战争贩子和他们在德国的帮凶们明白：德国人民和德国青年，不愿意在战争中送命；使他们明白：德国人民要求成立一个统一的、民主的、爱好和平的德国，并要求与一切国家建立友好关系。
