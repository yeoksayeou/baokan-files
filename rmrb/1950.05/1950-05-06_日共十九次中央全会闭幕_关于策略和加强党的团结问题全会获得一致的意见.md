Title: 19th Central Committee Plenum of the Japanese Communist Party Concludes: Unanimous Agreement Reached on Strategy and Strengthening Party Unity
Author:
Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: According to a TASS dispatch on the 4th, citing news published in the Japanese Communist Party's newspaper *Akahata*, the 19th Central Committee Plenum of the Japanese Communist Party concluded on April 30th.

　　The plenum unanimously adopted the general report of Tokuda Kyuichi, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party; the report of Shiino Etsuro, Chairman of the Central Supervisory Committee; the statement of Shiga Yoshio, who called for strengthening party unity and ceasing factional activities; and a statement on the successful conclusion of the plenum.

　　The statement of the Central Committee Plenum of the Japanese Communist Party pointed out that the plenum reached unanimous agreement on the issue of strategy and strengthening party unity. The statement continued: Domestic and foreign reactionaries are preparing to strike against the Japanese Communist Party. The plenum's statement called for a struggle to win Japan's complete independence, to oppose the construction of military bases on Japanese territory, and to oppose war.

### 日共十九次中央全会闭幕  关于策略和加强党的团结问题全会获得一致的意见

1950-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　日共十九次中央全会闭幕
    关于策略和加强党的团结问题全会获得一致的意见
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社四日电引日本共产党机关报《赤旗报》刊载的消息称：日本共产党第十九次中央委员会全会已于四月三十日闭幕。
    全会一致通过了日本共产党总书记德田球一的总报告；中央监察委员会主席椎野悦郎的报告；志贺义雄的声明，他曾号召加强党的团结并停止宗派的活动；以及关于全会圆满结束的声明。
    日本共产党中央委员会全会的声明指出：关于策略和加强党的团结问题，全会获得一致的意见。声明继称：国内外的反动派，正在准备打击日本共产党。全会的声明号召为争取日本的完全独立、为反对在日本领土上建设军事基地、为反对战争而斗争。
