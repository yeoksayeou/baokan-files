Title: "People's China" Society Sends Condolences on the Death of Agnes Smedley
Author:
Date: 1950-05-11
Page: 1

　　"People's China" Society
　　Condolences on the Death of Agnes Smedley
    [Xinhua News Agency, 10th] After the news of the death of the famous American female writer and journalist Agnes Smedley, the English-language bi-monthly magazine "People's China" Society sent a telegram to the American Committee for a Democratic Far Eastern Policy to express their condolences.

### “人民中国”社　唁史沫特莱逝世

1950-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　“人民中国”社
　　唁史沫特莱逝世
    【新华社十日讯】美国著名女作家与记者史沫特莱病逝消息传来后，此间英文半月刊“人民中国”社特致电美国远东民主政策委员会表示悼念。
