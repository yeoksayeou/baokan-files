Title: Part of Soviet Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing; Warmly Welcomed by Chinese Youth Wherever They Went; Soviet Professor Baudrev Leaves Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-05-18
Page: 1

　　Part of Soviet Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing
　　Warmly Welcomed by Chinese Youth Wherever They Went
　　Soviet Professor Baudrev Leaves Beijing Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] The Soviet Youth Delegation, headed by Mikhailov, consisting of 14 members, and the entire Soviet Youth Art Troupe, accompanied by Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, visited youth in East China for eleven days and returned to Beijing on the 17th.
    The delegation visited Tianjin, Xuzhou, Shanghai, Nanjing, and Jinan, and on the way back, also visited Qufu, the hometown of Confucius, and was warmly welcomed by local youth. Young people in major cities along the Beijing-Shanghai route gathered at the stations to welcome the delegation as they passed through or arrived.
    The delegation participated in 33 lecture meetings and 18 symposiums in East China, introducing the achievements of Soviet youth in the Soviet Patriotic War and in the post-war socialist economic construction, as well as the situation of the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League to 200,000 Chinese youth. The Soviet Youth Art Troupe performed cultural programs a total of 13 times, with an audience of 200,000 people.
    Chinese youth and organizations from various places presented the Soviet Youth Delegation with gifts or local products of commemorative significance, expressing their sincere friendship.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] Professor Baudrev, a Soviet Master of Economics, who was invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to lecture in our country, departed from Beijing at 10:10 PM on the 17th. Those who went to the station to see him off included Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, as well as Nan Hanchen, Wang Shao'ao, Ding Guitang, and others. Jabou and Ledov of the Soviet Embassy, and Seleznev, the representative of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association in China, also went to the station to see him off.
    In addition, Seleznev, the representative of the Beijing Soviet Foreign Cultural Association in Beijing, held a reception at 6:00 PM today to bid farewell to Professor Baudrev. A film was also shown after the meeting.

### 苏青年代表团一部返京　所到之处受到我国青年热烈欢迎　苏联教授包德列夫昨日离京

1950-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏青年代表团一部返京
　　所到之处受到我国青年热烈欢迎
　　苏联教授包德列夫昨日离京
    【新华社十七日讯】以米哈依洛夫为首的苏联青年代表团一行十四人及苏联青年文艺工作团全体人员，由中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬陪同访问华东各地青年，历时十一日，十七日返抵北京。
    代表团先后访问了天津、徐州、上海、南京和济南，归途中顺道访问了孔子故里曲阜受到各地青年的热烈欢迎。京沪沿路主要城市的青年在代表团经过或到达时，相率齐集车站欢迎。
    代表团在华东各地共参加演讲会三十三次，座谈会十八次，向到会的二十万中国青年群众介绍了苏联青年在苏联卫国战争中及战后的社会主义经济建设中的成绩和苏联列宁共产主义青年团的情形。苏联青年文艺工作团前后共表演文艺节目十三次，观众达二十万人。
    各地中国青年和团体，纷纷以富有纪念意义的礼品或土产送给苏联青年代表团，表示亲切的友情。
    【新华社十七日讯】应中苏友协总会邀请来我国讲学的苏联经济学硕士包德列夫教授十七日晚十时十分，已启程离京。赴车站欢送者有中苏友协总会副会长吴玉章、总干事钱俊瑞、及南汉宸、王绍鏊、丁贵堂等。苏联大使馆贾布候、列朵夫斯基、苏联对外文化协会驻华代表谢列金等也均赴站欢送。
    又北京苏联对外文化协会驻京代表谢列金今日下午六时曾举行酒会，欢送包德列夫教授。会后并放映电影。
