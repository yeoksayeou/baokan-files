　　Lecture Announcement
　  Science Lectures
    [This Newspaper's Report] The "Science Lectures" sponsored by the Chinese Academy of Sciences have been scheduled for the fourth and fifth lectures to be held at 4:00 PM on the 20th (Saturday) and 21st respectively. Professor Ge Tingsui of the Department of Physics at Tsinghua University will lecture on "The Strength of Metals," and Professor Feng Zhaolin of the Department of Soil Science at Agricultural University will lecture on "Soil Science and Agricultural Construction." Both lectures will be held in the auditorium of the Sino-French University on Donghuangchenggen.

### 讲座预告  科学讲座

1950-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　讲座预告
　  科学讲座
    【本报讯】中国科学院主办之“科学讲座”，已决定于二十日（星期六）及二十一日下午四时，分别举行第四讲及第五讲，先后由清华大学物理学系葛庭燧教授主讲“金属的强度问题”及农业大学土壤学系冯兆林教授主讲“土壤科学与农业建设”。地点皆在东皇城根中法大学礼堂。
