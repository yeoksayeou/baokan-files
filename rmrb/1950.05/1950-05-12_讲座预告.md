　　Kyoto News

　　Lecture Announcement
　　　　Introducing the Soviet Union
    [Reported by our newspaper] The eleventh lecture in the "Introducing the Soviet Union" series, sponsored by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, will be held at 7:30 PM on Friday, May 12th, at the Beijing Soviet Foreign Cultural Association at 19 Nanheyan Street. Professor Baudrev, a Soviet finance expert who is visiting China to give lectures, will speak on "The Difference Between the Soviet Financial System and the Financial Systems of Capitalist Countries." A film will be shown after the meeting. All those holding long-term or temporary lecture passes from the Association are welcome to attend.
                                          (Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association)
            Popular Current Events
    [Reported by our newspaper] The sixth lecture in the Popular Current Events series, sponsored by the Youth Service Department, will be held at 3:00 PM on Saturday, May 13th, at the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association (19 Nanheyan Street). Ling Qing will lecture on: "Recent Developments in America's Overall Diplomacy." All circles are welcome to attend. Tickets can be collected at the Youth Service Department from 1:30 PM to 5:00 PM and from 6:00 PM to 8:30 PM on May 12th. Group tickets can be collected upon presentation of an introduction letter.
                                             (China Youth Society)

### 讲座预告

1950-05-12
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　讲座预告
　　　　苏联介绍
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会主办的“苏联介绍”讲座第十一讲，定于五月十二日（星期五）下午七时半在南河沿十九号北京苏联对外文化协会举行，请此次来华讲学的苏联财政学专家包德列夫教授主讲《苏联财政制度与资本主义国家财政制度的区别》。会后并放映电影。凡持有该会长期或临时听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。
                                          （北京中苏友好协会）
            大众时事
    【本报讯】青年服务部主办的大众时事讲座第六讲定于五月十三日（星期六）下午三时，在苏联对外文化协会（南河沿十九号）举行，由凌青主讲：讲题是《最近美国总体外交的发展》，欢迎各界前往听讲。入场券于五月十二日下午一时半至五时，六时至八时半到青年服务部领取，团体票凭介绍信领取。
                                             （中国青年社）
