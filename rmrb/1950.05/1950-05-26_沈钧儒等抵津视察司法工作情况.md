Title: Shen Junru and Others Arrive in Tianjin to Inspect Judicial Work

Author:

Date: 1950-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 25th: Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government, Li Liuru, Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Chen Shaoyu, Chairman of the Legislative Affairs Committee, and a group of over thirty people arrived in Tianjin today. They will inspect the judicial administration, trials, prisons, and law enforcement in Tianjin. President Shen and his party plan to stay in Tianjin for several days before going to Tangshan and Cangzhou for inspections.

### 沈钧儒等抵津视察司法工作情况

1950-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　沈钧儒等抵津视察司法工作情况
    【新华社天津二十五日电】中央人民政府最高人民法院院长沈钧儒、最高人民检察署副署长李六如、法制委员会主任委员陈绍禹等一行三十余人，于今日抵津。他们将在津视察津市司法行政、审判、监狱、法律执行等方面的情形。沈院长等一行拟在津逗留数日，然后赴唐山、沧州等地视察。
