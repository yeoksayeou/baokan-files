　　Cultural and Educational Briefs
　　▲ The Central and Southern China District Sports Workers Representative Conference, held in Wuhan on the 4th of this month, concluded on the 7th. At the conference, a preparatory committee for the Central and Southern China Branch of the All-China Sports Federation was established, with Zhu Yujin, Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central and Southern China Working Committee of the Youth League, as the director.
　　▲ The Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government launched the "Health News" weekly on the 4th. The inaugural issue included an article by Minister Li Dequan entitled "Undertaking the New Tasks of Health Work."

### 文化教育简讯

1950-05-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　文化教育简讯
    ▲本月四日在武汉举行的中南区体育工作者代表会已于七日闭幕。会上成立了中华全国体育总会中南分会筹备委员会，由青年团中南区工作委员会宣传部长朱语今任主任。
    ▲中央人民政府卫生部于四日创刊“健康报”周刊。创刊号有李德全部长所作“担负起卫生工作的新任务”一文。
