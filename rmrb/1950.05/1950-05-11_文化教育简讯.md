Date: 1950-05-11

　　Cultural and Educational Briefs
　　The Northwest China Wu Gong Agricultural College and the East China Jinan Agricultural College have recently each dispatched professors and lecturers to Beijing Agricultural University to observe and study. The study items include the New Democratic agricultural education policy, Michurin's biological sciences, and teaching methods that combine theory with practice.
　　　　　　　　　　　　(Agricultural University Correspondent Group)
　　The Beijing Agricultural University's staff and workers' spare-time literacy school has officially opened. The students include 138 low-literacy staff members of the university and nearby farmers, workers, and out-of-school children, who are divided into senior high school, junior high school, higher primary school, and junior primary school classes.
　　　　　　　　　　　　(Agricultural University Correspondent Group)
　　Since May of last year, the Jinan City First People's Cultural Center has sent people deep into factories and schools to provide books and help workers and students organize study. Up to now, it has assisted in organizing 224 reading groups, with more than 1,200 group members, of which workers account for 30 percent. The center has also established a workers' club to assist workers in organizing recreational activities.
　　　　　　　　　　　　(Yuan Biguang)

### 文化教育简讯
袁碧光
1950-05-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　文化教育简讯
    西北区武功农学院和华东区济南农学院，最近各派遣教授、讲师来北京农业大学观摩学习。学习项目包括新民主主义农业教育方针、米邱林生物科学以及理论结合实际的教学方法等。
                          （农业大学通讯组）
    北京农业大学的员工业余补习学校已正式开学。学员包括该校文化程度低的职工和附近农民、工人、失学儿童等一百三十八人，分高中、初中、高小、初小等班上课。
                              （农大通讯组）
    济南市第一人民文化馆自去年五月以来，派人深入工厂、学校供给图书，帮助工人、学生组织学习。到现在为止，已协助组织了二百二十四个读书小组，有组员一千二百余人，其中工人占百分之三十。该馆并建立了工人俱乐部，协助工人组织文娱活动。
                            （袁碧光）
