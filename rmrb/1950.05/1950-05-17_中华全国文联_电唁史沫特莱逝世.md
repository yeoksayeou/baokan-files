Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Sends Condolences on the Death of Agnes Smedley
Author:
Date: 1950-05-17
Page: 3

　　All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles
　　Sends Condolences on the Death of Agnes Smedley

    [Xinhua News Agency, 15th] The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles sent a telegram to the American Committee for a Democratic Far Eastern Policy, expressing condolences on the death of the famous American writer Agnes Smedley. The telegram reads as follows:

    Upon hearing the news of the death of Ms. Agnes Smedley, we deeply mourn the loss of a democratic fighter and progressive writer. At the same time, the Chinese people have also lost a good friend.

                      All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles
                                Guo Moruo
                                Mao Dun
                                Zhou Yang
                              May 13th

### 中华全国文联　电唁史沫特莱逝世

1950-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　中华全国文联
　　电唁史沫特莱逝世
    【新华社十五日讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会电美国民主远东政策委员会，对美国名作家史沫特莱的逝世，表示悼唁。电文如下：
    惊闻史沫特莱女士逝世，我们深切地哀悼着失去了一位民主战士、进步作家，同时中国人民也失去了一位好友。
                      中华全国文学艺术界联合会
                                郭沫若
                                茅盾
                                周扬
                              五月十三日
