Title: Government Administration Council Passes Two Appointment Cases
Author:
Date: 1950-05-15
Page: 3
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The 32nd Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government passed the following two appointment cases:
    1. Supplementary List of Staff Members of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government
        Deputy Director of the Asian Department: Chen Jiakang, currently a member of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League.
    2. List of Commissioners and Deputy Commissioners of the General Administration of Customs of the Central People's Government
        Commissioner of Tianjin Customs: Zhu Jianbai, formerly Director of the Import and Export Department of the Industry and Commerce Department of the North China People's Government.
        Commissioner of Shanghai Customs: Jia Zhenzhi, formerly Special Commissioner for the Takeover of the Shanghai Jianghai Customs.
          Deputy Commissioner: Zhang Yongnian, currently Acting Deputy Commissioner of Shanghai Customs.

### 政务会议通过任命案两项

1950-05-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　政务会议通过任命案两项
    【新华社十三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第三十二次政务会议通过任命案两项如下：
    一、中央人民政府外交部工作人员补充名单
    亚洲司副司长  陈家康  现任中国新民主主义青年团中央委员。
    二、中央人民政府海关总署关长、副关长名单
    天津海关关长  朱剑白  曾任华北人民政府工商部出入口处处长。
    上海海关关长  贾振之  曾任上海江海关接管专员。
      副关长  张勇年  现任上海海关代理副关长。
