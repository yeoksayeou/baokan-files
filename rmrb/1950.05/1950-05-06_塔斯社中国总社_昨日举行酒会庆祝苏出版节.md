Title: TASS China Branch Holds Reception to Celebrate Soviet Publishing Festival
Author:
Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 1

　　TASS China Branch
    Held Reception Yesterday to Celebrate Soviet Publishing Festival
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th] Rogov, director of the TASS China Branch, held a reception at 5:00 PM today to celebrate the Soviet Publishing Festival. Many people from cultural, press, and publishing circles in the capital were invited to participate in the celebration. Soviet Ambassador Roshchin and Czech Ambassador Weiskopf also attended the meeting.

### 塔斯社中国总社  昨日举行酒会庆祝苏出版节

1950-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　塔斯社中国总社
    昨日举行酒会庆祝苏出版节
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯电讯社中国总社社长罗果夫，为庆祝苏联出版节，今日下午五时举行酒会。首都中外文化界、新闻界、出版界人士多人应邀前往参加庆祝。苏联大使罗申、捷克大使魏斯科普夫均曾赴会。
