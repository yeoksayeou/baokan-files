Title: Soviet, Romanian Central Cooperative Alliances Exchange May Day Greetings with Our Cooperative Administration

Author: 
Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 4

　　Soviet, Romanian Central Cooperative Alliances Exchange May Day Greetings with Our Cooperative Administration
　　[This newspaper dispatch] On the eve of the May First International Labor Day, the Central Cooperative Alliance of the Soviet Union and the Central Cooperative Alliance of Romania respectively sent telegrams to the Central Cooperative Affairs Administration of our country to express their congratulations. The Central Cooperative Affairs Administration has respectively replied with telegrams of thanks.

### 苏、罗中央合作社联盟与我合作管理局互贺“五一”

1950-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏、罗中央合作社联盟与我合作管理局互贺“五一”
    【本报讯】苏联中央合作社联盟、罗马尼亚中央合作社联盟于五一国际劳动节前夕分别致电我国中央合作事业管理局表示祝贺，中央合作事业管理局已分别复电致谢。
