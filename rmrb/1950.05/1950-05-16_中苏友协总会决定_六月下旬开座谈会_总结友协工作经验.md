Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters Decides to Hold Symposium in Late June to Summarize Friendship Association Work Experience
Author:
Date: 1950-05-16
Page: 3

　　Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters Decides
　　To Hold Symposium in Late June
　　To Summarize Friendship Association Work Experience
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] In order to summarize and exchange the work experience of local Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations over the past six months, so as to further develop the work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the National Headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has decided to convene a symposium on the work of local Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in Beijing in late June, mainly to discuss the basic work content of the Friendship Association and the establishment and development of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association organization. The association has notified local Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations to send representatives to participate at that time, and to prepare the following materials: (1) The establishment and development of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association organization and its experience. (2) The routine work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the work of grassroots organizations, and the organizational situation and experience. (3) Better typical materials.

### 中苏友协总会决定  六月下旬开座谈会  总结友协工作经验

1950-05-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协总会决定
　　六月下旬开座谈会
　  总结友协工作经验
    【新华社十二日讯】中苏友好协会总会为了总结及交流各地中苏友协半年来的工作经验，以便更进一步开展中苏友协工作，将于六月下旬在京召开各地中苏友好协会工作座谈会，主要是讨论友好协会基本工作内容和中苏友好协会组织的建立、发展等问题。该会已分别通知各地中苏友好协会届时派员参加，并准备下列材料：（一）中苏友好协会组织的建立和发展情况及经验。（二）中苏友好协会的经常工作情况，基层组织的工作情况和组织情况及经验。（三）较好的典型材料。
