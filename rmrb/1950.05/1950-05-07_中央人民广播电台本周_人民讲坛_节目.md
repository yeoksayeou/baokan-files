Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station's "People's Forum" Program This Week
Author:
Date: 1950-05-07
Page: 3

　　Kyoto News

　　Central People's Broadcasting Station's "People's Forum" Program This Week
　　[This Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station's "People's Forum" program from the 7th to the 13th, from 20:00 to 20:30 each evening, will consist of the following:
　　7th (1) Yuan Zhen, Chairman of the National Tsinghua University Women's Association, will introduce the work of the Tsinghua Women's Association; (2) Wu Shaowen, a member of the Tsinghua University Women's Association and a female domestic worker, will speak on: "As long as you are determined, you can learn culture well."
　　8th Zheng Zesen, Deputy Director of the Trade Union of Tianjin No. 3 Cotton Mill, will speak on: "How Tianjin No. 3 Cotton Mill Implements Democratic Management."
　　9th (1) Lu Cheng, Preparatory Committee Member of the Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Association and former Chairman of the Malayan National Trades Union Congress, will speak on: "The Heroic Struggle of the Malayan Working Class"; (2) Answering letters from listeners such as Jiang Tieying regarding improving the content and form of broadcasting.
　　10th Program organized by the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government: Jiang Ling, Deputy Director of the Department of Social Education of the Ministry of Education, will speak on: "Strengthening the Leadership of Workers' and Peasants' Spare-Time Education."
　　11th (1) Tang Xiaochun, Director of Studies at the Beijing Experimental Workers' and Peasants' Rapid Middle School, will speak on: "Overview of the Beijing Experimental Workers' and Peasants' Rapid Middle School"; (2) Introduction to the Beijing Labor People's Cultural Palace.
　　12th (1) Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court, will speak on: "Guaranteeing the Implementation of Our Marriage Law"; (2) Zhang Zhirang, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, will speak on: "The Marriage Law that Takes into Account Both the Old and the New."
　　13th Program organized by the Chinese Committee for the Defense of World Peace: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for the Defense of World Peace, will speak on: "Consolidating Revolutionary Victory and Defending Lasting Peace."

### 中央人民广播电台本周“人民讲坛”节目

1950-05-07
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

    中央人民广播电台本周“人民讲坛”节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台七日到十三日每晚二十点到二十点三十分“人民讲坛”节目的内容如下：
    七日  （一）国立清华大学家庭妇女会主席袁震介绍清华家庭妇女会的工作；（二）清华大学家庭妇女会会员、家庭女工吴少文讲：“只要有决心，就能学好文化”。
    八日  天津中纺三厂工会副主任郑泽森讲：“天津中纺三厂是怎样实行民主管理的”。
    九日  （一）北京归国华侨联谊会筹备委员前马来亚各民族各业总工会主席卢成讲：“马来亚工人阶级的英勇斗争”；（二）回答姜铁英等听者关于改进广播内容和广播形式的来信。
    十日  中央人民政府教育部主办节目：教育部社会教育司副司长江陵讲：“加强工农业余教育的领导”。
     十一日  （一）北京市实验工农速成中学教导主任唐孝纯讲：“北京市实验工农速成中学概况”；（二）介绍北京市劳动人民文化宫。
     十二日  （一）最高人民法院院长沈钧儒讲：“保证执行我们的婚姻法”；（二）最高人民法院副院长张志让讲：“新旧兼顾的婚姻法”。
    十三日  中国保卫世界和平大会委员会主办节目：中国保卫世界和平大会委员会主席郭沫若讲：“巩固革命胜利与保卫持久和平”。
