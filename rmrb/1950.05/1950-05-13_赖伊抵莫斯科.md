Title: Lie Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1950-05-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th. TASS, Moscow, 12th: The Secretary-General of the United Nations, Mr. Lie, arrived in Moscow on the 11th. Accompanying him were Assistant Secretary-General Mr. Sinhenko and the head of the United Nations Information Centre in Prague, Mr. Ritt. Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Mr. Gromyko, the acting director of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs' United Nations Affairs Department, Mr. Rosin, the Executive Secretary of the United Nations Economic Commission for Europe currently in Moscow, Mr. Myrdal, the head of the United Nations Information Centre in Moscow, Mr. Vavilov, the Swedish Ambassador, Mr. Schulman, and the Norwegian Acting Chargé d'Affaires, Mr. Aakre, went to the Vnukovo Airport to welcome Mr. Lie.

### 赖伊抵莫斯科

1950-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　赖伊抵莫斯科
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十二日电：联合国组织秘书长赖伊于十一日抵达莫斯科。随他前来的有助理秘书长辛琴柯和联合国驻布拉格新闻处处长里特。赴富努柯伏机场欢迎赖伊的有苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯，苏联外交部联合国事务司代理司长罗辛，还有刻在莫斯科的联合国欧洲经济委员会执行秘书密达尔，联合国驻莫斯科新闻处处长华维洛夫，瑞典大使舒尔曼及挪威代理代办阿克雷。
