Title: UN Commission on the Status of Women: Soviet Union Refuses to Attend Fourth Session, Protesting the Illegal Attendance of Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Gang Representatives
Author:
Date: 1950-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, May 7th: TASS, New York, May 5th: The United Nations Secretariat released a message to reporters stating that the UN Secretary-General had received a letter from Soviet representative Malik, stating that the Soviet Union would not participate in the work of the fourth session of the UN Commission on the Status of Women, scheduled to begin on May 8th, because representatives of the Chinese Kuomintang clique would illegally attend the meeting.

Malik's letter also stated that the Soviet Union would not recognize the legitimacy of any resolutions passed at this session attended by Kuomintang representatives.

### 联合国妇女地位委会  苏拒绝参加四届会议  抗议蒋匪帮代表非法出席

1950-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　联合国妇女地位委会
    苏拒绝参加四届会议         
    抗议蒋匪帮代表非法出席
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社纽约五日电：联合国秘书处对各报记者发表消息说：联合国秘书长已接到苏联代表马立克的信，其中说，苏联将不参加定于五月八日举行的联合国妇女地位委员会第四届会议的工作，因为中国国民党集团的代表将非法出席会议。
    马立克的信中又说：苏联将不承认有国民党代表参加的这届会议中所通过的任何决议的合法性。
