Title: Soviet Union's Spring Planting Progresses Smoothly; Most Crops Nearing Completion of Sowing
Author:
Date: 1950-05-13
Page: 4

　　Soviet Union's Spring Planting Progresses Smoothly
　　Most Crops Nearing Completion of Sowing
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th] According to TASS, Moscow: The Pravda reports that, according to the Ministry of Agriculture's estimates, as of May 5th, collective farms have sown 14 million hectares more of spring crops than in the same period last year. Collective farmers have exceeded the cotton planting plan, and the sowing of sugar beets, sunflowers, and many other grains and industrial crops is nearing completion.
    Pravda's editorial commented on this matter, pointing out that these achievements are the result of the Soviet state's enormous assistance to collective farms, state farms, and machine and tractor stations, as well as the excellent organization and selfless efforts of the personnel of these institutions.

### 苏联春播顺利进展　大部分作物即将播种完毕

1950-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联春播顺利进展
　　大部分作物即将播种完毕
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报载：据农业部估计，截至五月五日止，集体农场已较去年同期多播种了一千四百万公顷的春季作物。集体农民已超过植棉计划，甜菜、向日葵以及其他许多谷物和工业作物，即将播种完毕。
    真理报社论于评论此事时指出：这些成就，是苏维埃国家给予集体农场、国营农场和机器与拖拉机站的巨大帮助，以及这些机构的人员组织优良和忘我地努力的成果。
