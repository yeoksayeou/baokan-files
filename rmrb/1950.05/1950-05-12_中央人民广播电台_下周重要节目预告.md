Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Next Week's Important Program Preview
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The important broadcast programs for the week of the Central People's Broadcasting Station from the 14th to the 20th of this month are as follows:

	Natural Science Lectures 18:45 to 19:00
	14th (Replaced by a literary program)
	15th, 16th, 17th Huang Shuze speaks on: Sore Throat
	18th Lecture Department of this station speaks on: The Creator of the World's First Airplane
	19th Lecture Department of this station speaks on: The Professor Who Can Make the Blind See Again
	20th Lecture Department of this station speaks on: Regarding the Treatment of Bone Tuberculosis and Others (Answering questions from Meng Yi, Meng Jingzhi, etc.)
	Social Science Lectures 19:00 to 20:00
	14th (Replaced by a literary program)
	15th Ai Siqi speaks on: What is the State (Chapter 4, Lecture 2 of the History of Social Development)
	16th Ai Siqi speaks on: Two Kinds of States (Chapter 4, Lecture 3 of the History of Social Development)
	17th Ai Siqi speaks on: Answering Listeners' Questions Regarding the History of Social Development
	18th Yu Guangyuan speaks on: Answering Listeners' Questions Regarding Chapter 3 of Political Economy
	19th Wang Huide speaks on: Surplus Value (Lecture 8 of Political Economy)
	20th Zhang Geng speaks on: The Script Problem of the New Opera
	18:45 - 20:00 Sunday Literary Program Drama
		The Beijing Women's Production and Education Institute Art Troupe performs: The Millennium Glacier Thaws (Collective creation by the students of the Fifth Institute of the Beijing Women's Production and Education Institute)
	People's Forum 20:00 to 20:30
	14th Beijing Municipal People's Government Taxation Bureau: "Work Experience in Collecting Industrial and Commercial Taxes in Beijing"
	15th Program hosted by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation: (1) Questions and Answers on the Marriage Law; (2) Zhang Qi, Chief of the Accounting Section of the Beijing Electric Power Administration Bureau, speaks on: "The Female Comrades of the Accounting Section of the Electric Power Bureau Have Implemented the Quota Management System"
	16th Introducing the Anti-Feudal Labor Struggle of Porters in Beijing
	17th Program hosted by the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government: Cheng Jinwu, Deputy Director of the Supervision Department of the Ministry of Education, speaks on: "The Political Theory Study of Teachers"
	18th Program hosted by the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League: (1) She Shiguang, Minister of Propaganda of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, speaks on: "How We Established Propagandists"; (2) The Cultural Learning Activities of the Youth League in Dongliangma Village, Shulu County, Hebei Province
	19th Some Preliminary Experiences in Building Regimental Organizations in Companies by the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in the Past Four Months
	20th (1) How the Cultural Learning of the Seventh Company of a Certain Unit of the People's Liberation Army Has Been Activated; (2) How Liu, the Battalion Commander of a Certain Unit of the People's Liberation Army, Studies Culture
	Literary Programs 20:30 to 21:00
	14th Movie Introduction Recording: An Ordinary Soldier (Translated film by Northeast Film Studio)
	15th Opera Excerpts: Central Academy of Drama Opera Troupe sings excerpts from the opera "The White-Haired Girl"
	16th Soviet Dance Music National Dance Music Russian Joy Collective Farm Dance Spring Dance Lively Polka Happy Brothers Soldier Dance Russian Dance Ural Peasant Round Dance
	17th Singing Long Zai: Good Friends, Good Treaty
	18th Recitation of Literary Works: Broadcasting Group of the Central Academy of Drama Theater Troupe recites poems and folk tales: I Want His Grain Piled High as Mountains (by Zhang Mingquan) Rural Scenery (by Zhang Qingtian) Flowers for Flowers (by Zhang Mingquan) Labor Heroes Return to the Village (by Zhang Mingquan) Tian Benling Learned Four Characters (by Jian Qing)
	19th Western Music: Introducing Beethoven and His Works
	20th Opera: North China People's Revolutionary University Art Troupe sings: Report of Merit (Written by Hu Qi, Music by the North China People's Revolutionary University Art Troupe)
