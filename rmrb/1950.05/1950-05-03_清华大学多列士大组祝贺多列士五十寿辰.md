Title: Tsinghua University Thorez Group Congratulates Thorez on His Fiftieth Birthday
Author: Li Luotong, Sun Zhaoshu, Guo Zhaode, Zheng Jishen, Wang Chuanying, Guan Dinghua, Chen Yongcun, Luo Zongkuang, Zhou Tijian
Date: 1950-05-03
Page: 4
People's Garden

　　The Thorez Group of Tsinghua University Congratulates Comrade Thorez on His Fiftieth Birthday
Respected Comrade Thorez:
    On the occasion of your fiftieth birthday, all members of our "Thorez Group" extend to you our warmest and most cordial congratulations!
    On March 7, 1950, the "Thorez Group" was established at Tsinghua University in China. This demonstrates our infinite respect and love for the French people and their leader—dear Comrade Thorez.
    We care about the struggle of the French people and humbly learn from the experiences of these struggles. We extend our boundless sympathy to the strike struggles of the French workers. We angrily protest the unreasonable expulsion of the Secretary of the Youth League, Williams, by the French reactionary government, and we are infinitely delighted by the development, expansion, and consolidation of the forces for peace and democracy in France.
    We warmly support the resolutions of the 12th National Congress of the French Communist Party. This meeting once again proved the unity of the French people and clearly pointed out the direction of the French people's struggle.
    As the latter half of the twentieth century dawns, the broad masses of people all over the world are standing on the same front. Let us strengthen the unity of the Chinese and French people, as well as the people of the world, and bravely advance in this great era to strive for the beautiful world that is about to be realized.
    May you hear our warm cheers in faraway France:
    Long live the friendship and unity between China and France!
    Long live the victory of the Chinese and French people!
    Long live respected Comrade Thorez!
    All members of the Thorez Group of Tsinghua University
    Li Luotong, Sun Zhaoshu, Guo Zhaode, Zheng Jishen, Wang Chuanying, Guan Dinghua, Chen Yongcun, Luo Zongkuang
    Zhou Tijian
    April 22, 1950

### 清华大学多列士大组祝贺多列士五十寿辰
李洛童　孙钊枢　郭肇德　郑吉申　王传英　关定华　陈勇存　罗宗贶　周体健
1950-05-03
第4版()
专栏：人民园地

　　清华大学多列士大组祝贺多列士五十寿辰敬爱的多列士同志：
    欣逢您五十寿辰的来临，我们“多列士大组”全体组员，谨向您致最热烈最亲切的祝贺！
    一九五○年三月七日，在中国清华大学成立了“多列士大组”。这表现了我们对法国人民以及他们的领袖——亲爱的多列士同志的无限的崇敬和热爱。
    我们关心着法国人民的斗争，虚心学习这些斗争的经验。我们对法国工人的罢工斗争，寄予无限的同情。我们为法国反动政府无理强迫青年联盟书记威廉士出境而愤怒抗议，我们为法国和平民主力量的发展扩大和巩固而感到无限欢欣。
    我们热烈的拥护法共十二次全代会的决议。这次会议，再度证明了法国人民的团结，明确地指出了法国人民斗争的方向。
    在这二十世纪后半世纪揭幕前，全世界广大的人民已站在同一阵线上了，让我们更加紧中法人民以及全世界人民的团结，在这伟大的时代中，为争取行将实现的美好世界而奋勇前进。
    愿您在遥远的法国能听到我们热烈的欢呼：
    中法两国友谊团结万岁！
    中法两国人民胜利万岁！
    敬爱的多列士同志万岁！
    清华大学多列士大组全体组员
    李洛童  孙钊枢  郭肇德  郑吉申  王传英 关定华  陈勇存  罗宗贶
    周体健
    一九五○年四月二十二日
