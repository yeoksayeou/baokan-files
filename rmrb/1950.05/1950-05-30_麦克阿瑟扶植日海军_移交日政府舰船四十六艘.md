Title: MacArthur Supports the Japanese Navy: Transfers Forty-Six Ships to the Japanese Government
Author:
Date: 1950-05-30
Page: 4

　　MacArthur Supports the Japanese Navy
    Transfers Forty-Six Ships to the Japanese Government
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 29th] According to TASS on May 28th, citing Japan's Jiji Press, individuals associated with the Japanese Ministry of Transportation stated that MacArthur Headquarters has transferred forty-six former Japanese Navy ships to the Japanese government. It is said that these ships will be used as "scrap metal."
    According to a Reuters report, these forty-six ships all sank during the war, including the famous battleship "Mutsu," the cruiser "Nagara," and the "Tatsuta."

### 麦克阿瑟扶植日海军  移交日政府舰船四十六艘

1950-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　麦克阿瑟扶植日海军
    移交日政府舰船四十六艘
    【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社二十八日电引日本时事通讯社消息：据与日本运输省有关的人士称，麦克阿瑟总部已把四十六艘前日本海军舰船移交给日本政府。据说这些舰船将充作“废金属”之用。
    另据路透社消息，这四十六艘舰船都是在战时沉没的，其中包括著名的战舰“陆奥”号，巡洋舰“长良”号和“龙田”号。
