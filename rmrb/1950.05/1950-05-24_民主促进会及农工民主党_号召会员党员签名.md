Title: China Association for Promoting Democracy and Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Call on Members and Party Members to Sign

Author:

Date: 1950-05-24
Page: 4

China Association for Promoting Democracy and Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party
Call on Members and Party Members to Sign
[Report from this newspaper] The China Association for Promoting Democracy has launched a vigorous peace signature campaign among members across the country and has instructed its branches to specifically arrange this work. The headquarters staff of the association and members in Beijing have launched a signature campaign in accordance with the above points. They are taking the signature books and going door-to-door to mobilize the masses to sign, and are explaining the significance of the signature to the masses at any time. The Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party has issued instructions to party organizations across the country, calling on party members across the country to enthusiastically participate in the peace signature campaign. Currently, the Beijing Party Affairs Consolidation Committee of the party has not only mobilized all party members to sign, but also instructed party members to encourage their relatives, friends, and neighbors to participate in the signing.

### 民主促进会及农工民主党  号召会员党员签名

1950-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　民主促进会及农工民主党
    号召会员党员签名
    【本报讯】中国民主促进会发动全国各地会员热烈进行和平签名运动，并指示各分会具体布置这一工作。该会总部工作人员及在京会员，已遵照以上各点展开签名运动，他们拿着签名册，挨家挨户去发动群众签名，并随时向群众解释签名的意义。中国农工民主党中央委员会，对全国各地党的组织发出指示，号召全国党员热烈参加和平签名运动。现该党北京市党务整理委员会，除已发动全体党员签名外，并指示党员推动其亲友邻居参加签名。
