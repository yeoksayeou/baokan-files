Title: US, British, and French Foreign Ministers' Meeting Begins in London
Author: 
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 11th: News from various sources: US Secretary of State Acheson, after meeting with French Foreign Minister Schuman, flew to London with his entourage on the morning of the 9th and held two days of talks with British Foreign Secretary Bevin. Schuman also flew to London on the 10th with the Secretary-General of the French Foreign Ministry, Parrot, the French Ambassador to the UK, Massigli, the French High Commissioner to Germany, Poncet, and other experts. The US, British, and French Foreign Ministers' meeting is scheduled to begin in London today.

### 美英法外长会已在伦敦开始

1950-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　美英法外长会已在伦敦开始
    【新华社十一日讯】各方消息：美国国务卿艾奇逊在与法国外交部长舒曼会谈之后，已偕随员于九日上午飞抵伦敦，并与英外相贝文举行两日会谈。舒曼亦已于十日偕法国外交部秘书长巴罗弟、法驻英大使马西格里、法驻德高级专员庞赛及其他专家飞抵伦敦。美英法三国外长会议定今日起在伦敦开始举行。
