Title: Ambassador Wang Jiaxiang Meets with Trygve Lie; Indian Ambassador Visits Foreign Minister Zhou to Discuss Presenting Credentials
Author:
Date: 1950-05-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 17th: On May 16th, at 12:00 noon, Ambassador Wang Jiaxiang, the Plenipotentiary Ambassador of our country to the Soviet Union, met with United Nations Secretary-General Trygve Lie for about half an hour at the latter's request.
    Xinhua News Agency, May 17th: The first Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the Republic of India to our country, K.M. Panikkar, visited Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 5:30 PM today to discuss matters related to presenting his credentials. Also present were Qiao Guanhua, Acting Director of the Asian Affairs Department; Chen Jiakang, Deputy Director; and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 王稼祥大使与赖伊会谈　印大使拜会周外长谈呈递国书事

1950-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　王稼祥大使与赖伊会谈
　　印大使拜会周外长谈呈递国书事
    【新华社莫斯科十七日电】五月十六日正午十二时，我驻苏特命全权大使王稼祥应联合国秘书长赖伊之请会谈约半小时。
    【新华社十七日讯】印度共和国驻我国首任特命全权大使潘尼迦于今日下午五时三十分赴外交部拜会周恩来部长，就呈递国书问题有所商谈。在座者尚有亚洲司代司长乔冠华、副司长陈家康，交际处长王倬如。
