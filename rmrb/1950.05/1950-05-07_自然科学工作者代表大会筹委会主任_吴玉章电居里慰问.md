Title: Preparatory Committee Director of the National Congress of Natural Science Workers, Wu Yuzhang, Sends Condolences to Curie
Author:
Date: 1950-05-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: Wu Yuzhang, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the First National Congress of Natural Science Workers of China, today sent a telegram to French scientist Joliot-Curie, stating: Professor Joliot-Curie:
    Chinese scientific workers express their highest respect for your struggle for peace. Under your leadership, the Stockholm meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace demanded the unconditional prohibition of atomic weapons and declared the government that first uses atomic weapons to be a war criminal. We are determined to follow you and fight for lasting peace. We condemn the reactionary measures of the French government in removing you from your position as a member of the French Atomic Energy Commission and extend our sincere condolences to you.
    Wu Yuzhang
    Director of the Preparatory Committee of the First National Congress of Natural Science Workers of China
    May 5th

### 自然科学工作者代表大会筹委会主任  吴玉章电居里慰问

1950-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　自然科学工作者代表大会筹委会主任
    吴玉章电居里慰问
    【新华社五日讯】中华全国第一次自然科学工作者代表大会筹备委员会主任吴玉章本日致电法国科学家约里奥——居里，电称：约里奥——居里教授：
    中国的科学工作者对于你为和平的奋斗表示崇高的敬意。在你的主持之下，世界拥护和平大会常设委员会斯德哥尔摩会议要求无条件禁止原子武器，并宣布首先使用原子武器的政府为战犯。我们誓愿追随你的后面，为持久和平而奋斗。我们谴责法国政府免去你的法国原子能委员会委员职务的反动措施，并向你致恳切的慰问。
    中华全国第一次自然科学工作者代表大会筹备委员会主任
    吴玉章
    五月五日
