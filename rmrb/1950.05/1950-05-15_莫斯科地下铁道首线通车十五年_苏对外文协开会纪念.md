Title: 15th Anniversary of the Opening of the First Moscow Metro Line: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Commemorative Meeting

Author:

Date: 1950-05-15
Page: 3

Beijing News

　　15th Anniversary of the Opening of the First Moscow Metro Line
　  Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Commemorative Meeting
    [Reported by this newspaper] The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries will hold a commemorative meeting at 7:30 PM this evening (the 15th) at the Beijing Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, 19 Nanheyan Street, to commemorate the 15th anniversary of the opening of the first Moscow Metro line. Mr. Tsveitkov will speak on "The Moscow Metro." A scientific film will also be shown after the meeting. All those holding admission tickets and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's "Introduction to the Soviet Union" long-term lecture passes are welcome to attend.
                                       (Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association)

### 莫斯科地下铁道首线通车十五年  苏对外文协开会纪念

1950-05-15
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　莫斯科地下铁道首线通车十五年
　  苏对外文协开会纪念
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会与北京苏联对外文化协会为纪念莫斯科地下铁道第一线通车十五周年，定于今（十五）日下午七时半在南河沿十九号北京苏联对外文化协会举行纪念会。请茨外特科夫讲“莫斯科地下铁道”。会后并放映科学电影。凡持有入场券和北京市中苏友好协会“苏联介绍”长期听讲证者，均可前往参加。
                                       （北京市中苏友好协会）
