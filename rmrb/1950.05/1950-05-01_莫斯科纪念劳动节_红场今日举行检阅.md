Title: Moscow Celebrates May Day: Red Square Hosts Parade Today
Author:
Date: 1950-05-01
Page: 4
　　
    Moscow Celebrates May Day
    Red Square Hosts Parade Today

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th (TASS): May 1, 1950—International Labor Day, the Moscow garrison will hold a parade in Red Square. Military academies, military schools, and armed forces stationed in Moscow will participate. The parade will be reviewed by Chief of General Staff Shtemenko, and commanded by Lieutenant General Artemyev, Commander of the Moscow Military District and Commander of the Moscow Garrison.

### 莫斯科纪念劳动节  红场今日举行检阅

1950-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科纪念劳动节
    红场今日举行检阅
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科三十日电：一九五○年五月一日——国际劳动节，莫斯科卫戍部队将在红场举行检阅。军事学院、军事学校以及驻扎在莫斯科的武装部队将行参加。阅兵式将由参谋总长施蒂门柯检阅，而由莫斯科军区司令兼莫斯科卫戍司令阿尔杰米也夫准上将担任指挥。
