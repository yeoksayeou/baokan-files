Title: Youth Movement Historical Materials Exhibition Extended to Today

Author:
Date: 1950-05-11
Page: 3

　　Youth Movement Historical Materials Exhibition
　　Extended to Today
    [From Our Newspaper] The "Chinese Youth Movement Historical Materials Exhibition" organized by the Central Committee of the Youth League, the All-China Youth Federation, and the All-China Student Federation, among other units, attracted 20,000 visitors in the five days from its opening on the 4th to the 8th. Visitors included leading cadres from various organizations, young workers, young cadres, students, soldiers, and the Soviet youth delegation. Everyone unanimously agreed that this exhibition was of great educational significance to young people. Young people from the Soviet Union, Indonesia, and the United Kingdom who visited the exhibition also expressed their respect for the glorious struggle history of Chinese youth. The exhibition was originally scheduled to close on the 10th, but in response to requests from all sides, it has been decided to extend it for one more day, today (the 11th).

### 青运史料展览  延至今天截止

1950-05-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　青运史料展览
　  延至今天截止
    【本报讯】青年团中央、全国青联、全国学联等单位举办的“中国青年运动史料展览”，自四日开幕至八日止五天中参观者达二万人，参观者包括各机关首长、青年工人、青年干部、学生、战士以及苏联青年代表团等。大家一致认为此次展览对青年的教育意义很大。看过展览的苏联、印尼、英国青年，也表示了他们对中国青年光荣斗争史迹的尊敬。该展览会原定十日截止，现应各方面要求，决定今（十一）日延期一天。
