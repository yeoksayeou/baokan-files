Title: Higher Education Conference Postponed
Author:
Date: 1950-05-08
Page: 3

　　Higher Education Conference Postponed
    [Xinhua News Agency, 4th] The Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government originally scheduled the first National Higher Education Conference to be held from May 16th to 30th. It has now been decided to postpone it to June 1st due to incomplete preparations.

### 高等教育会议延期

1950-05-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　高等教育会议延期
    【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府教育部原定于五月十六日至三十日召开第一次全国高等教育会议，现因准备工作尚未完成，决定延至六月一日举行。
