Title: Peace Signature Campaign Unfolds in Shanghai, Tianjin, Baoding, etc.; 400,000 Signatures in Shanghai in Half a Month
Author: 
Date: 1950-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 19th: The people of Shanghai are enthusiastically engaged in the signature campaign to defend world peace. From May 1st to the 15th, according to incomplete statistics, over 400,000 people have signed. Newspapers have printed blank signature forms to mobilize signatures among readers. Ta Kung Pao, Joint Publishing, and other stores have set up signature books at their storefronts, and over 10,000 people have signed. Farmers in the suburbs, housewives, office workers, and shop clerks have been actively creating their own signature pages and sending them to the peace sub-committee. To support this significant movement, the Shanghai Postal Administration has changed the postage for signature mails to the printed matter rate.

Xinhua News Agency: The people of Tianjin are also universally and enthusiastically engaged in the signature campaign to defend world peace. Following the distribution of 500,000 signature forms, the Tianjin branch of the World Peace Congress issued an additional 240,000 forms on the 16th and 17th in response to requests from various agencies and organizations. Agencies, factories, and schools have widely launched publicity efforts, and many units have held mobilization meetings. Workers, staff, and students are eagerly signing. Various newspaper offices, the People's Radio Station, district People's Cultural Centers, and some theaters have set up signature locations, and many people have gone to sign in recent days.

Xinhua News Agency: Over 60,000 people in Xi'an have signed the peace declaration. Currently, various agencies, organizations, and industries are planning to coordinate with the anniversary of Xi'an's liberation (May 20th) to widely launch publicity and organizational work.

Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, 17th: The people of Hebei Province are launching a signature campaign to ban atomic weapons and defend world peace. The Hebei Provincial People's Government, various people's organizations, the Hebei branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the preparatory committee of the Hebei branch of the Journalists Association simultaneously issued a call today, urging all workers, farmers, youth, students, and women in the province to sign the peace declaration. Li Xijiu, Vice Chairman of the Provincial People's Government, broadcast on the Baoding People's Radio Station on the 16th, calling on the 30 million people of the province to join the signature campaign to defend world peace. The Hebei Daily also launched a peace signature campaign among its readers.

### 上海天津保定等地  和平签名运动展开  上海半个月四十万人签名

1950-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　上海天津保定等地
    和平签名运动展开
    上海半个月四十万人签名
    【本报讯】据新华社上海十九日电：上海人民保卫世界和平签名运动正在热烈展开中。自“五一”到十五日止半个月中，据不完全统计，已有四十余万人签了名。各报均在报端刊印空白签名书，在广大读者中发动签名。大公报、三联书店等并在门市部设置签名册，签名的达万余人。近郊农民、家庭妇女、机关职员、商号店员们纷纷自订册页，将签名寄递和平分会。上海邮政管理局为拥护此一重大意义的运动，特将签名邮件改作印刷品收费寄递。
    又讯：津市各界人民保卫世界和平签名运动，已普遍热烈展开。和大天津分会继发出五十万份签名书后，十六、十七两日又应各机关、团体的要求，续发出二十四万余份。各机关、工厂、学校广泛展开宣传，许多单位召开了动员大会。工人、职员和学生争相签名。各报社、人民电台、各区人民文化馆及部分戏院均设立代签处，连日前往签名者非常踊跃。
    另讯：西安市各界在和平宣言上签名者已达六万余人。现各机关、团体、行业，正筹商配合西安解放周年纪念日（五月二十日）广泛展开宣传与组织工作。
    又据新华社保定十七日电：河北省人民正展开禁用原子武器、保卫世界和平的签名运动。河北省人民政府、各人民团体、中苏友好协会河北分会、新闻工作者协会河北分会筹委会，于今日同时发出号召，号召全省工人、农民、青年、学生、妇女，在和平宣言上签名。省人民政府李锡九副主席十六日曾在保定人民广播电台播讲，号召全省三千万人民，投入保卫世界和平签名运动。河北日报亦发起广大读者进行和平签名运动。（附图片）
