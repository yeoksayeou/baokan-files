Title: Shantou City Federation of Trade Unions Established
Author:
Date: 1950-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 11th: The Shantou City First Workers' Representative Congress, which lasted for four days, concluded on the 4th, and the Shantou City Federation of Trade Unions was simultaneously announced. The congress elected Fan Jinfu, Chen Peizhi and 29 others as the first executive committee members of the City Federation of Trade Unions. Shantou City already has more than 14,000 union members, accounting for more than one-third of the city's total of more than 40,000 workers.

### 汕头市总工会成立

1950-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　汕头市总工会成立
    【新华社广州十一日电】历时四天的汕头市第一届工人代表大会，已于四日闭幕，市总工会同时宣告成立。大会选举范劲夫、陈培志等三十一人为市总工会首届执行委员。按汕市已有工会会员一万四千余人，占全市四万余工人总数的三分之一以上。
