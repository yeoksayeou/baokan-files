Author: Zhang Ruifu Wang Chengjun Tian Y茂 De Lin
Date: 1950-05-19
Page: 2

　　Industrial Briefs

　　In order to shorten the stop time of locomotives and increase transportation time, the workers of Kouquan Station of Datong Railway Sub-bureau, under the leadership of the stationmaster and on the basis of the joint labor group, organized 16 employees to replace locomotives with manpower to shunt and weigh cars. From March 5th to April 5th, a total of 150,000 catties of millet were saved.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Zhang Ruifu)

　　The management committee of Pingwang Station of Datong Railway Sub-bureau organized 31 employees to collect materials in the surrounding villages. Within a week, they collected 1,573 small steel rails and more than 200 tons of other materials. It is estimated to be worth more than 120,000 catties of millet.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Wang Chengjun, Tian Y茂)

　　The workers of the Yongjiabao work area of the repair team of the Zhangjiakou Engineering Section, in the new road maintenance work, implemented a system of everyone setting plans and everyone contributing ideas. The work efficiency of each worker per day has increased to more than six meters, and the quality also meets the standards. After this work experience was promoted to all work areas of the entire section, the "Red May" competition has generally exceeded the 6-meter standard. The Yongjiabao work area has exceeded 7 meters.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 (De Lin)

### 工业简讯
张瑞福　王承君　田雨茂　德麟
1950-05-19
第2版()
专栏：工业简讯

　　工业简讯
    大同车务段口泉站工友，为了缩短机车停站时间，增加运输时间，在站长的领导和联合劳动小组的基础上，组织了职工十六名，以人力代替机车调车过磅，从三月五日至四月五日，共节省小米十五万斤。
                                                 （张瑞福）
    大同车务段平旺站管委会，组织了三十一名职工，分赴四乡收集器材，在一星期内即收集了小型钢轨一五七三根，其他器材二百多吨。估计可值小米十二万多斤。                  （王承君、田雨茂）
    张家口工务段包修班永嘉堡工区的工友，在新养路包修工作中，实行大家订计划，大家出主意，每天每工的工作效率提高到六公尺多，质量也合乎规格。此种工作经验推广到全段各工区后，在红五月竞赛中，均已普遍超过六公尺标准，永嘉堡工区则已超过七公尺。           
             （德麟）
