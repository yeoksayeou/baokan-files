Title: Celebrating International Children's Day, the Municipal People's Broadcasting Station Holds Story Competition

Author:
Date: 1950-05-20
Page: 3

To celebrate the first International Children's Day in the new China, the Beijing Municipal People's Broadcasting Station will hold a story competition. The rules are as follows: 1. Story content: Must have the meaning of mutual love; 2. Time: Each story must not exceed ten minutes; 3. Registration date: From the 23rd to the 25th of this month; 4. Registration procedure: Submit a written copy of the story to the Beijing Municipal People's Broadcasting Station at No. 3 West Chang'an Street, and include your name, age, school name, grade, and mailing address.

### 庆祝六一国际儿童节  市人民广播电台举办故事比赛会

1950-05-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　庆祝六一国际儿童节
　  市人民广播电台举办故事比赛会
    【本报讯】北京市人民广播电台为了庆祝新中国第一个六一国际儿童节，将举办故事比赛会，办法如下：一、故事内容：有互爱的意义；二、时间：每个故事不得超过十分钟；三、报名日期：本月二十三日到二十五日；四、报名手续：将故事写一份送到西长安街三号北京市人民电台，并且写上姓名、年龄、学校名字、年级，和通讯地点。
