Title: Oppose Wall Street Tycoons and Their Lackeys; Churches in Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, and Romania Issue Appeal Supporting Peace

Author: 

Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 11th dispatch: Churches in Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, and Romania support the World Peace Congress Standing Committee's appeal to ban atomic weapons. TASS reported: The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Clergy Union recently passed a resolution endorsing the message of Moscow Patriarch Alexius in early April calling for support of the Stockholm Conference resolutions. It urged all believers, all evangelical denominations, and Muslim followers in the country to participate in the struggle for peace and oppose the warmongering Wall Street tycoons and their lackeys.

　　The Czechoslovak Orthodox Church also issued a declaration signed by Archbishop Elevfery, urging all believers to support the appeal of the World Peace Congress Standing Committee.

　　Representatives of Catholic clergy and believers in Romania recently passed a resolution at a meeting in Targu Mures, condemning all actions aimed at using the Church to peddle war, and calling on all clergy and believers to support the decisions of the Stockholm Conference.

### 反对华尔街大亨及其走狗　保捷罗三国教会　拥护和平呼吁书

1950-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　反对华尔街大亨及其走狗
　　保捷罗三国教会         
　　拥护和平呼吁书
    【新华社十一日讯】保、捷、罗三国教会拥护世界拥护和平大会常委会要求禁止原子武器的呼吁书。综合塔斯社消息：保加利亚教士联盟中央委员会最近通过决议，对于莫斯科总主教阿利修斯于四月上旬号召拥护斯德哥尔摩会议决议的文告，表示拥护，并敦促国内一切教徒、一切福音教派和回教的教徒们参加进行争取和平的斗争，反对好战的华尔街大亨及其走狗。
    捷克斯洛伐克正教会亦发表由埃列夫菲里大主教签署的宣言，敦促全体教徒拥护和大常委会的呼吁书。
    罗马尼亚天主教的教士与教徒的代表，最近在塔古尔谟勒斯举行会议时通过决议，斥责一切旨在利用教会以贩卖战争的行动，并号召一切教士与教徒拥护斯德哥尔摩会议的决定。
