Introduction to the Soviet Youth Art Troupe

　　(1) Anisimov - Honorary Artist of the Soviet Union, Vice Chairman of the Soviet Art Committee, Professor of the Moscow State Conservatory, and Head of the Soviet Youth Art Troupe.
　　(2) Gefukonia Smolinskaya - Singer of the Soviet Socialist State Grand Theater, awarded the Order of Lenin, and received a diploma at the World Youth Festival singing competition in Budapest.
　　(3) Leisha Struzhkova - Famous dancer of the Soviet Socialist State Grand Theater, awarded the Order of Lenin, and won a medal at the World Youth Festival song and dance competition in Budapest.
　　(4) Alexander Lababuri - Famous dancer of the Soviet Socialist State Grand Theater, awarded the Order of Lenin, and won a medal at the World Youth Festival song and dance competition in Budapest.
　　(5) Susanna Zvagina - Famous dancer of the Soviet Socialist State Grand Theater, awarded the Order of Lenin.
　　(6) Elizavida Tsaftal - Singer of the Kiev National "Shevchenko Opera and Ballet Theater," who received a diploma at the World Youth Festival singing competition in Budapest.
　　(7) Viktor Bikaikin - Student of the Gnessin Music School, winner of the Braga "Yana" International Violin Competition.
　　(8) Yevgeny Malinin - Winner of the Warsaw International Chopin Piano Competition, and recipient of a diploma at the World Youth Festival piano competition in Budapest.
　　(9) Kalia Ismanova - Famous dancer of the Uzbek Republic Alexander Opera House, awarded the Order of Lenin, winner of the Stalin Prize, Honorary Artist of the Uzbek Republic; recipient of a diploma at the World Youth Festival singing competition.
　　(10) Jonit Kostricha - Actor of the Leningrad National Theater, who won a prize at the All-Soviet singing competition.
　　(11) Kliuri - Student of the National Tchaikovsky Conservatory.
　　(12) Balayev - Musician of the Uzbek Republic Alexander Opera House, winner of a diploma at the World Youth Festival in Budapest.
　　(13) Danilina - Famous actress of the Pyatnitsky People's Choir.
　　(14) Sorokin - Famous actor of the Pyatnitsky People's Choir.
　　(15) Svetlova - Famous actress of the Pyatnitsky People's Choir.
　　(16) Klimov - Famous actor of the Pyatnitsky People's Choir.
　　(17) Nifedov - Accordion expert from Moscow.
　　(18) Anikin - Famous actor of the Pyatnitsky People's Choir, winner of a diploma at the World Youth Festival in Budapest.
　　(19) Dashigwa - Famous actor of the Pyatnitsky People's Choir, famous actor of the Moscow Circus.
　　(20) Yerokin - Piano expert from Moscow, famous actor of the Pyatnitsky People's Choir.
　　(21) Motveyev - Representative of the Soviet Art Committee.
　　(22) Honin - Central organizer of the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League.
　　(23) Sharogin - Translator.
[Xinhua News Agency]

### 苏联青年文艺工作团介绍

1950-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联青年文艺工作团介绍
    （一）阿尼西莫夫——荣誉的苏联艺术家，苏联艺术委员会副主席，莫斯科国立音专教授，苏联青年文艺工作团团长。
    （二）格富科尼亚·斯莫林斯卡亚——荣获列宁勋章的苏维埃社会主义国家大戏院歌唱家，曾在布达佩斯举行的世界青年节歌唱竞赛会上荣获该会奖状。
    （三）莱沙·斯特鲁兹柯娃——荣获列宁勋章的苏维埃社会主义国家大戏院名舞蹈家，曾在布达佩斯世界青年节歌舞比赛会中获得奖章。
    （四）亚历山大·拉巴屋里——荣获列宁勋章的苏维埃社会主义国家大戏院名舞蹈家，曾在布达佩斯世界青年节歌舞比赛会中获得奖章。
    （五）苏萨那·兹娃纪娜——荣获列宁勋章的苏维埃社会主义国家大戏院的名舞蹈家。
    （六）叶利扎维达·蔡夫达尔——基辅国立“石夫琴果歌舞剧院”的歌唱家，曾在布达佩斯世界青年节歌唱比赛会荣获该会奖状者。
    （七）维克托·毕开金——格聂西内音乐学校的学生，布拉哥“牙娜”国际提琴比赛荣获冠军。
    （八）耶夫哥尼·玛利尼——华沙国际萧邦钢琴比赛会的荣冠者，布达佩斯世界青年节钢琴会比赛荣获该会奖状者。
    （九）卡利亚·依斯曼罗娃——荣获列宁勋章的乌兹别克共和国亚历塞尔歌剧院名舞蹈家，斯大林奖金的荣获者，乌兹别克共和国荣誉演员；布达佩斯世界青年节歌唱竞赛奖状获得者。
    （十）乔尼特·克斯特利查——列宁格勒国家戏院演员，曾在全苏维埃歌唱竞赛会上荣获奖金。
    （十一）克里乌里——国立柴以果夫斯基音专学生。
    （十二）巴来也夫——乌兹别克共和国亚历塞尔歌剧院音乐家，布达佩斯世界青年节荣获奖状者。
    （十三）达妮莉娜——毕亚蒂尼斯基人民歌舞团的名演员。
    （十四）索罗金——毕亚蒂尼斯基人民歌舞团的名演员。
    （十五）斯维利托娃——毕亚蒂尼斯基人民歌舞团的名演员。
    （十六）克利莫夫——毕亚蒂尼斯基人民歌舞团的名演员。
    （十七）尼费多夫——莫斯科手风琴专家。
    （十八）阿尼金——毕亚蒂尼斯基人民歌舞团的名演员，布达佩斯世界青年节荣获奖状者。
    （十九）达石格瓦——毕亚蒂尼斯基人民歌舞团的名演员，莫斯科马戏团名演员。
    （二十）叶罗金——莫斯科钢琴专家，毕亚蒂尼斯基人民歌舞团的名演员。
    （二十一）莫特维也夫——苏联艺术委员会代表。
    （二十二）霍宁——苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央组织工作者。
