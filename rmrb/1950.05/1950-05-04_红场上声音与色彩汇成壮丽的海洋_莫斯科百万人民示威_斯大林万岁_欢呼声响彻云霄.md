Title: A Magnificent Ocean of Sound and Color on Red Square: A Million People Demonstrate in Moscow; Cheers of "Long Live Stalin" Resound Through the Sky

Author:

Date: 1950-05-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Comprehensive report from TASS: Over a million people in Moscow participated in the May Day demonstration, lasting more than eight hours. On Red Square, sounds and colors merged into a magnificent ocean. Demonstrators carried brilliant bouquets of flowers, tens of thousands of flags fluttered in the air, and the band played joyful songs from beginning to end, interweaving with the Ura cheers for Stalin, resonating throughout the sky.

　　Marshal Stalin personally stood on the platform of the Lenin Mausoleum, warmly welcoming the demonstrators.

　　Workers from the Moscow Transformer Factory held up banners stating they had completed their Five-Year Plan on April 30th. Exquisitely patterned textiles fluttered in the ranks of the textile workers. The Stalin Automobile Factory, Hammer and Sickle Factory, Electric Bulb Factory, and various other Moscow enterprises proudly carried their trademarks, with huge billboards proclaiming their achievements in the May Day competition.

　　Foreign delegations expressed their impressions of attending the grand ceremony: Xu Xin, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union of China, said: The ranks of the demonstrators left an indelible impression. The great love of the Soviet people for our people was fully demonstrated. When the Soviet people passed in front of our delegation, they cheered us: "Glory to the Chinese people!" "Glory to Mao Zedong!" We saw demonstrators carrying portraits of our leader Mao Zedong. Now, close friendship binds us together, and no one can destroy it!

　　Hang Huilan, a female textile worker from Shanghai, said: Today my joy is boundless, because I met Stalin on Red Square in Moscow. The grand demonstration made me feel the profound internationalist spirit of the Soviet people.

### 红场上声音与色彩汇成壮丽的海洋  莫斯科百万人民示威  “斯大林万岁”欢呼声响彻云霄

1950-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　红场上声音与色彩汇成壮丽的海洋
    莫斯科百万人民示威
    “斯大林万岁”欢呼声响彻云霄
    【新华社三日讯】综合塔斯社消息：五一节莫斯科一百余万人民参加示威游行，历时八小时以上。在红场上，声音与色彩汇集成壮丽的海洋。示威者抱着灿烂的花束，千万面旗帜在空中翻飞，乐队自始至终，演奏着欢乐的歌曲，和为斯大林欢呼的乌拉声交织成一片，响彻了云霄。
    斯大林大元帅亲在列宁陵墓的方台上，热烈欢迎示威者。
    莫斯科变压器工厂的工人高举着标语，写明他们已在四月三十日完成五年计划。花样精致的纺织物在纺织工人的行列上飘扬着。斯大林汽车工厂，锤子与镰刀工厂，电灯泡制造工厂以及莫斯科各企业都自豪地携带着他们的商标，巨大的标语牌上写明他们在五一竞赛中获得的成就。
    外国代表团发表了参加盛典的观感：中国邮电工会全国委员会副主席徐欣称：示威者的行列给人以不可磨灭的印象。苏联人民对我国人民的伟大的爱在示威中充分表现出来。当苏联人民走过我们代表团前面的时候，他们向我们欢呼：“光荣归于中国人民！”“光荣归于毛泽东！”我们看到了示威者擎着我们领袖毛泽东的画像。现在，亲密的友谊把我们联结在一起，谁也不能破坏它！
    上海纺织女工杭惠兰说：今天我的高兴是无比的，因为我在莫斯科的红场上见到了斯大林。盛大的示威使我感到苏联人民的深厚的国际主义精神。
