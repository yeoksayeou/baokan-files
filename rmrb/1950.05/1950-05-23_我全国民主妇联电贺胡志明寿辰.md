Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Cables Congratulations to Ho Chi Minh on His Birthday
Author:
Date: 1950-05-23
Page: 4

The All-China Democratic Women's Federation Cables Congratulations to Ho Chi Minh on His Birthday
[Reported by this newspaper] The All-China Democratic Women's Federation sent a telegram to President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram reads as follows:

Dear Comrade Ho Chi Minh:

On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, on behalf of all Chinese women, I extend warm congratulations to you. We congratulate you with great joy on the complete victory you will achieve in leading the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people and women for national liberation.

For the happiness of Vietnamese women and children, and for the close friendship between the people of China and Vietnam, I wish you good health and longevity!

                    All-China Democratic Women's Federation

### 我全国民主妇联电贺胡志明寿辰

1950-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　我全国民主妇联电贺胡志明寿辰
    【本报讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会致电越南民主共和国胡志明主席，祝贺他的六十寿辰。电文如下：亲爱的胡志明同志：
    在您六十寿辰之际，谨代表全中国妇女向您致热烈的祝贺。我们谨以极大的欢欣祝贺你在领导越南人民与妇女所进行的民族解放的英勇斗争中将获得完全的胜利。
    为了越南妇女与儿童的幸福，为了中越两国人民亲密的友谊，谨祝你健康长寿！
                    中华全国民主妇女联合会
