Title: Feng Wenbin Delivers Farewell Speech
Author:
Date: 1950-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: Feng Wenbin's farewell speech at the banquet for the Soviet Youth Delegation: Dear Comrades,

　　Tonight we are holding a grand farewell party here to bid farewell to our dearest friends—the Soviet Youth Delegation led by Comrade Mikhailov.

　　The brilliant cause of the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League has had a profound impact on the history of the Chinese youth movement. The organization of advanced Chinese youth—the Socialist Youth League first established in 1920, the Communist Youth League during the period before the War of Resistance Against Japan, and today's New Democratic Youth League—all take the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League as our best example.

　　This time, Comrade Mikhailov personally led the most outstanding youth representatives of the Soviet Union to visit China with a high degree of internationalist enthusiasm, and they worked tirelessly and selflessly in many important cities in China. This is a great encouragement and help to Chinese youth. On behalf of the youth of the whole country, I express my heartfelt thanks.

　　Please, Comrade Mikhailov and the comrades of the delegation, convey to all the Soviet youth league members, Young Pioneers, and young friends that Chinese youth will always be the most loyal friends of the Soviet youth. We must unswervingly move forward along the path they are taking—the path of socialism and communism!

　　I ask everyone to fill their glasses to thank the Soviet Youth Delegation, to thank the Lenin Communist Youth League, to thank all the Soviet youth, to thank Comrade Stalin, the mentor of the working people and democratic youth of the world, and to wish them success in their endeavors and good health. Cheers!

### 冯文彬致欢送词

1950-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　冯文彬致欢送词
    【新华社讯】冯文彬在欢送苏联青年代表团宴会上的演词：亲爱的同志们：
    今晚我们在这里举行盛大的欢送晚会，来欢送我们最亲切的朋友——以米哈依洛夫同志为首的苏联青年代表团。
    苏联列宁共产主义青年团的光辉事业，对于中国青年运动的历史，是有着深刻的影响。中国先进青年的组织——一九二○年第一次建立的社会主义青年团，抗日战争以前时期的共产主义青年团，今天的新民主主义青年团——都是以苏联的列宁共产主义青年团，作为我们最好的榜样。
    这次，米哈依洛夫同志亲自率领苏联最优秀的青年代表，以高度国际主义的热情，访问了中国，他们并以不知疲倦的忘我的精神，分别在中国许多重要城市进行工作。对于中国青年，这是一种莫大的鼓舞和帮助。我代表全国青年，表示衷心的感谢。
    请米哈依洛夫同志及代表团同志们，转告全苏联的青年团员、少先队员、青年朋友们，中国青年永远是苏联青年最忠实的朋友，我们要坚定不移地向着他们所走的道路——社会主义、共产主义的道路前进！
    我要求大家斟满酒杯，为了感谢苏联青年代表团，感谢列宁共产主义青年团，感谢全苏联青年，感谢全世界劳动人民和民主青年的导师斯大林同志，并祝福他们事业成就，身体健康，干杯。
