Title: Exhibition of Historical Materials on the Chinese Youth Movement Opens Today at Zhongshan Park
Author:
Date: 1950-05-04
Page: 3

Beijing News

	Exhibition of Historical Materials on the Chinese Youth Movement Opens Today at Zhongshan Park
	[Report from this newspaper] To celebrate the first Chinese Youth Day since the founding of the People's Republic of China and the first anniversary of the founding of the China New Democratic Youth League, the "Exhibition of Historical Materials on the Chinese Youth Movement," jointly organized by the Central Committee of the Youth League, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Student Federation, and other units, will be held in the Zhongshan Hall of Zhongshan Park from May 4th to 10th, from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM daily.
	(China Youth Press)

### 中国青年运动史料展览今日起在中山公园举行

1950-05-04
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　中国青年运动史料展览今日起在中山公园举行
    【本报讯】为庆祝中华人民共和国诞生后的第一个中国青年节及中国新民主主义青年团成立一周年，青年团中央、全国青联、全国学联等单位联合举办的“中国青年运动史料展览”由五月四日起至十日每天上午十时至下午六时在中山公园中山堂举行。
    （中国青年社）
