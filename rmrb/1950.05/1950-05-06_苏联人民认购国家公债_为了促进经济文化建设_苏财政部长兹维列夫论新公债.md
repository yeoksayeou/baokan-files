Title: Soviet People Subscribe to National Bonds To Promote Economic and Cultural Construction Soviet Finance Minister Zverev on the New Bonds
Author:
Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 4

　　Soviet People Subscribe to National Bonds To Promote Economic and Cultural Construction
    Soviet Finance Minister Zverev on the New Bonds
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 4th] According to TASS, Moscow, May 4th: Soviet Finance Minister Zverev recently published an article in the newspaper on the issuance of the fifth post-war Soviet National Economic Recovery and Development Bonds, stating that in bourgeois countries, the issuance of bonds is to make up for budget deficits during the general crisis of capitalism, while in the Soviet Union, the issuance of bonds is for construction purposes. Subscribing to Soviet national bonds is to expand and promote the scale and speed of economic and cultural construction, increase the wealth of the people, and guarantee the peaceful labor of the Soviet people. The internal capital accumulation of socialist enterprises is the main and decisive source of capital accumulation in the Soviet state; at the same time, the savings of the Soviet people in subscribing to national bonds and other aspects constitute another huge source of capital accumulation.

### 苏联人民认购国家公债  为了促进经济文化建设  苏财政部长兹维列夫论新公债

1950-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联人民认购国家公债  为了促进经济文化建设
    苏财政部长兹维列夫论新公债
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科四日讯：苏联财政部长兹维列夫顷就发行战后第五次苏联国民经济恢复与发展公债，在报上发表专文称：在资产阶级的国家内，发行公债是为了弥补资本主义总危机时期内的预算赤字，而在苏联，发行公债是为了建设的目的。认购苏联国家公债就是为了扩大和促进经济与文化建设的规模及速度，增加人民的财富和保障苏联人民的和平劳动。社会主义各企业的内部资本积累，是苏维埃国家的资本积累的主要的和具有决定性的来源；同时，苏联人民在认购国家公债及其他方面的储蓄，则构成了资本积累的另一巨大来源。
