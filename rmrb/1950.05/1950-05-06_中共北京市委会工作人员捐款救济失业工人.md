Title: CCP Beijing Municipal Committee Staff Donate to Aid Unemployed Workers
Author: Tan Dengyun
Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 3

　　CCP Beijing Municipal Committee Staff Donate to Aid Unemployed Workers

　　[This newspaper's dispatch] To aid unemployed workers in Shanghai, all staff members of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China launched a donation drive of two "jin" of rice (for those on a contract supply system) and one day's salary (for those on a salary system). As a result, 391 people donated a total of 3,279 "jin" of millet.
　　(Tan Dengyun)

### 中共北京市委会工作人员捐款救济失业工人
谭登云
1950-05-06
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　中共北京市委会工作人员捐款救济失业工人
    【本报讯】中国共产党北京市委会全体工作人员为了救济上海失业工人开展二斤米（包干供给制的）和一日薪金（薪金制的）捐输。结果三百九十一人共捐献小米三千二百七十九斤。
    （谭登云）
