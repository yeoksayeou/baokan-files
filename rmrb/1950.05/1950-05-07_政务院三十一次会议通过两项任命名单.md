Title: Government Administration Council's Thirty-First Meeting Passes Two Appointment Lists
Author:
Date: 1950-05-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: The Government Administration Council passed the following two appointment lists at its 31st administrative meeting:

　　I. Supplementary List of Deputy Directors of the General Office of the Ministry of Interior of the Central People's Government

　　Deputy Director of the General Office: Yue Song - Formerly served as Director of the Administrative Office of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government.

　　II. List of Staff Members of the General Administration of Customs of the Central People's Government

　　Chief of the Tariff Division: Wu Yaoqi - Formerly a Taotai of the Shanghai Jianghai Customs of the Kuomintang government.

　　Deputy Chief of the Cargo Supervision Division: Li Changzhe - Formerly a Taotai of the Shanghai Jianghai Customs of the Kuomintang government.

　　Shi Yunjie - Formerly served as Director of the Liaodong Tax Bureau.

　　Chief of the Smuggling Investigation Division: Yin Zhiqian - Formerly served as Deputy Inspector of the Shanghai Jianghai Customs.

　　Deputy Chief: Zhuo Guanchao - Formerly a Deputy Taotai of the Chongqing, Lanzhou, and Shanghai Customs of the Kuomintang government.

　　Deputy Chief of the Maritime Affairs Division: Dong Mingqi - Formerly Deputy Director of the Northeast Navigation Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Communications of the Kuomintang government.

　　Chief of the Finance Division: Chen Tiebao - Formerly served as Secretary to the Military Representative of the Shanghai Jianghai Customs.

　　Chief of the Statistics Division: Lin Daqi - Formerly Director of the Wenjindu Sub-branch of the Kowloon Customs of the Kuomintang government.

　　Deputy Chief of the Inspection Division: Wu Jingfang - Formerly a member of the takeover team for the Chongqing Customs.

　　Chief of the Personnel Division: Xu Ming (female) - Formerly Secretary of the Fushun Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Secretary of the Women's Work Committee.

　　Deputy Chief: Sun En'yuan - Formerly Director of the Printing Office of the Shanghai Jianghai Customs.

　　Deputy Chief of the General Affairs Division: Chen Qiongkun - Formerly a Taotai of the Shanghai, Chongqing, Hankou, and Zhanjiang Customs of the Kuomintang government.

　　Director of the Northeast Customs Administration: Zhang Huadong - Currently Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Northeast People's Government.

　　Deputy Director: Sun Chun - Formerly Deputy Director of the Northeast Customs Administration.
