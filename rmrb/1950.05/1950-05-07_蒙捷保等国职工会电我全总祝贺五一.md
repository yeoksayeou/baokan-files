Title: Trade Unions of Mongolia, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria and other countries send telegrams to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to congratulate May Day
Author:
Date: 1950-05-07
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, dated the 6th: The Chairman of the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Mongolian People's Republic, Adligbish, sent a telegram to Chen Yun, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on April 29 to congratulate "May Day." The telegram stated: On this "May Day" International Day, a day to review the fighting strength of workers around the world, I would like to extend warm fraternal congratulations to you and, through you, to the free Chinese working class and the workers of your country.
　　
The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Trade Union sent a telegram to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on the 2nd, stating: We sincerely hope that you will achieve the greatest success in further construction.
　　
The Chairman of the General Trade Union of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Pulanov, sent a telegram to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on April 28, stating: The Chinese working class's construction of the People's Republic of China is a powerful factor in strengthening the socialist peace and democracy camp. We wish you full success!

### 蒙捷保等国职工会电我全总祝贺五一

1950-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　蒙捷保等国职工会电我全总祝贺五一
    【本报讯】据新华社六日消息：蒙古人民共和国全国职工会中央会议主席阿德利比什四月二十九日致电我全国总工会主席陈云，祝贺“五一”劳动节。贺电中称：值此“五一”国际日，检阅全世界劳动者战斗力量的日子，谨向您并通过您向自由的中国工人阶级与贵国的劳动者转致兄弟的热烈祝贺。
    捷克工会中央委员会于二日致我全国总工会的贺电中称：切望你们在进一步的建设当中得到最大的成就。
    保加利亚人民共和国总工会主席浦拉诺夫于四月二十八日致我全国总工会的贺电中称：中国工人阶级建设中华人民共和国是加强社会主义和平民主阵线的有力因素。我们谨祝你们充分成功！
