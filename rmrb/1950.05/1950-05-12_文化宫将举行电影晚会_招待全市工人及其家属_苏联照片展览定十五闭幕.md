Title: Cultural Palace to Hold Film Evening for Workers and Families; Soviet Photo Exhibition to Close on the 15th
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 3

Beijing News

　　Cultural Palace to Hold Film Evening
　　Inviting Workers and Their Families from Across the City

    Soviet Photo Exhibition to Close on the 15th
    [Our Newspaper] The Labor People's Cultural Palace and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Film Team will hold a workers' film evening at 8:00 PM on Saturday, the 13th, at the Cultural Palace Square. They will show the Soviet film "International Court" (in Chinese narration) and colorful short films, inviting workers and their families from across the city. (Cultural Palace)
    [Our Newspaper] The Soviet Photo Exhibition, sponsored by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, has been held at the Labor People's Cultural Palace since May 1st, and has been visited by over 40,000 people to date. As the Association plans to move these photos to suburban areas and factories for touring exhibitions, the exhibition at the Cultural Palace will close on the 15th. (Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association)

### 文化宫将举行电影晚会　招待全市工人及其家属　苏联照片展览定十五闭幕

1950-05-12
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　文化宫将举行电影晚会
　　招待全市工人及其家属     
    苏联照片展览定十五闭幕
    【本报讯】劳动人民文化宫及北京市中苏友好协会电影队定于十三日（星期六）晚八时在文化宫广场举办工人电影晚会——放苏联影片《国际法庭》（华语解说）及五彩短片，招待全市工人及工人家属。（文化宫）
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会主办的苏联照片展览，自五月一日在劳动人民文化宫开始展览，参观人数迄今已达四万余人。因该会准备把这些照片分别去郊区、工厂流动展览，在文化宫的展览，定于十五日闭幕。（北京市中苏友好协会）
