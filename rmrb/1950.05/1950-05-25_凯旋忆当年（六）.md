Triumphant Return, Remembering the Past (6)
Xi Hong (Lyrics), Xi Ye (Painting)

　　That time when we attacked Jinzhou,
　　The artillery roared, shaking the sky;
　　Obstructed during the street fighting,
　　I took on the heavy demolition task.

　　The main street was tightly blockaded,
　　Bullets formed a dense web of fire;
　　Leg pain intensified my hatred,
　　I crawled and rolled towards the building's base.

　　A thunderous explosion, black smoke rose,
　　The broken building buried the dog enemies;
　　Completing the task filled me with joy,
　　I forgot the blood streaming down my right arm.

　　A stretcher carried me to the rear,
　　Doctor Zhang at the hospital was kept busy;
　　Cutting open the flesh for surgery,
　　Turned out it was an American bullet that wounded me. (Attached picture)

### 凯旋忆当年（六）
西虹词  西野
1950-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    凯旋忆当年（六）
    西虹词  西野画
    那一回攻打锦州，
    炮火轰隆隆震天响；
    巷战当中受阻碍，
    重量爆破我承担。
    大街之上封锁紧，
    枪弹密密成火网；
    腿伤发疼仇火并，
    匍匐滚向大楼跟。
    一声雷响黑烟腾，
    破楼埋藏了狗敌人；
    完成任务心中喜，
    忘掉了右臂血淋淋。
    担架送我去后方，
    忙坏了病院张医生；
    开刀破肉动手术，
    原来是美式子弹伤我身。（附图片）
