Title: Beijing Adds Twenty-Three More Telegraph Offices for Foreign Communication
Author:
Date: 1950-05-28
Page: 3

　　Beijing Adds Twenty-Three More Telegraph Offices for Foreign Communication
    [This Newspaper News] Since April 26th to the 26th of this month, the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau has added twenty-three more locations for sending telegrams abroad. These twenty-three locations are: Jiucheng, Anguo, and Shexian in Hebei Province; Jiaozuo, Puyang, Liaocheng, Daokou, and Heze in Pingyuan Province; Shangnan, Shenmu, and Shilibu in Shaanxi Province; Shashi and Jiangling in Hubei Province; Dinghai in Zhejiang Province; Zhoukou in Henan; Haikou in Guangdong; and Huali, Ningnan, Yanyuan, Zhaojue, Yuexi, Yanbian in Xikang Province; and Sanshenggong in Ningxia Province. Please note and utilize them.

### 北京对外通电报又增加二十三处

1950-05-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京对外通电报又增加二十三处
    【本报讯】北京电信局对外通报地点自四月二十六日到本月廾六日又增加了二十三处，这二十三处是：河北的旧城、安国、涉县，平原省的焦作、濮阳、聊城、道口、菏泽，陕西省的商南、神木、十里铺，湖北省的沙市、江陵，浙江省的定海，河南周口，广东海口及西康省的会理、宁南、盐源、昭觉、越西、盐边，及宁夏省三盛公等地，请各界注意利用。
