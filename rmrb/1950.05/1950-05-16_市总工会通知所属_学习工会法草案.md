Title: Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Notifies Affiliates to Study the Draft Trade Union Law
Author: Gong Zhi Ren
Date: 1950-05-16
Page: 3

Beijing News

　　Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Notifies Affiliates
　  Study the Draft Trade Union Law
    [This Newspaper's Report] The Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions has printed and distributed the draft Trade Union Law and the May 1st Workers' Daily editorial to all factories, enterprises, industry trade unions, and district offices of the Federation. It is also notified that after all units have completed studying Liu Shaoqi's speech at the May Day celebration cadre meeting on the 15th of this month, they will all begin studying the draft Trade Union Law and the May 1st Workers' Daily editorial. The notification states that at the beginning of extensively mobilizing the worker masses to discuss and study, trade union cadres must first conduct their own study and give enlightening reports to the masses. After in-depth discussion and study, each unit will report its learning progress, discussion opinions, and discovered problems to the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions before the 25th of this month, which will then be reported to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
    [Another Report] Liu Laifu, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, explained the content and spirit of the draft Trade Union Law to all cadres of the organization at 9:00 am on the 16th. (Gong Zhiren, Cultural and Educational Department of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions)

### 市总工会通知所属  学习工会法草案
龚之人
1950-05-16
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　市总工会通知所属
　  学习工会法草案
    【本报讯】北京市总工会已将工会法草案和四月三十日工人日报社论印发各工厂企业、各行业工会和总工会各区办事处。并通知在本月十五日，各单位将刘少奇在庆祝“五一”节干部大会上的演说学习结束后，一律开始学习工会法草案及四月三十日工人日报社论，通知在广泛发动工人群众讨论研究之初，工会干部要先进行学习，并向群众作启发报告。在深入讨论研究后，于本月二十五日以前，各单位将学习情况，讨论意见和发现的问题，汇报市总工会，再由市总工会统一汇报全总。
    【又讯】北京市总工会副主席刘莱夫在十六日上午九时，向本机关全体干部讲解工会法草案的内容及精神。（北京市总工会文教部龚之人）
