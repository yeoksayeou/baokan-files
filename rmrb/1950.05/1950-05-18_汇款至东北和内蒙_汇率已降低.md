Title: Remittances to Northeast and Inner Mongolia: Exchange Rate Reduced
Author:
Date: 1950-05-18
Page: 3

　　Kyoto City News

　　Remittances to Northeast and Inner Mongolia
　　Exchange Rate Reduced
    [Kyoto City News Agency] According to regulations from the People's Bank of China and the Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the Northeast Bank, the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China has lowered the exchange rate from Beijing to Northeast China and Inner Mongolia, effective May 16th. The exchange rate is calculated per thousand yuan, and is reduced by two to six yuan compared to the previous rate. The current exchange rates are: Jinzhou, three yuan; Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Benxi, four yuan; Changchun, Jilin, Andong, Harbin, Mudanjiang, Jiamusi, five yuan; Ulanhot, Qiqihar, Chengde, six yuan; Hailar, Manzhouli, seven yuan.

### 汇款至东北和内蒙  汇率已降低

1950-05-18
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　汇款至东北和内蒙
　  汇率已降低
    【京市新闻处讯】中国人民银行北京分行根据中国人民银行和东北银行山海关联合办事处的规定，自五月十六日起降低北京对东北和内蒙古的汇率。汇率按每千元计算，比以前降低二元到六元，现在汇率是：锦州三元；沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、本溪四元；长春、吉林、安东、哈尔滨、牡丹江、佳木斯五元；乌兰浩特、齐齐哈尔、承德六元；海拉尔、满洲里七元。
