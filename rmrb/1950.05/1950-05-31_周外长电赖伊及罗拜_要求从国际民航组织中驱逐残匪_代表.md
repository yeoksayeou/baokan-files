Title: Foreign Minister Zhou Cables Trygve Lie and Roper, Demanding the Expulsion of the Remnant Bandit "Representatives" from the International Civil Aviation Organization
Author:
Date: 1950-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 30th— Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, sent telegrams of identical content to Trygve Lie, Secretary-General of the United Nations, and Dr. Roper, Secretary-General of the International Civil Aviation Organization, on May 30th, notifying them that the so-called "representatives" of the remnant Kuomintang reactionary clique are now completely unqualified to participate in the International Civil Aviation Organization and must be expelled from all its institutions and meetings (including the fourth session of the Assembly being held in Montreal, Canada on May 30th of this year). In the telegrams, Foreign Minister Zhou requested them to reply and to inform all parties concerned.

### 周外长电赖伊及罗拜  要求从国际民航组织中驱逐残匪“代表”

1950-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　周外长电赖伊及罗拜
    要求从国际民航组织中驱逐残匪“代表”
    【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长于五月三十日以同样内容的电文分别致电联合国秘书长赖伊及国际民用航空组织秘书长罗拜博士，通知他们说：中国国民党反动派残余集团的所谓“代表”现已完全没有参加国际民用航空组织的资格，必须将其从该组织的各项机构和会议（包括今年五月三十日在加拿大蒙特累尔召开的第四届代表大会）中驱逐出去。周外长在电文中要求他们答复并转知有关各方。
