Title: American Spies Penetrate Thailand to Investigate Progressive Forces
Author:
Date: 1950-05-16
Page: 4

　　American Spies Penetrate Thailand
　　Engaged in Reconnaissance of Progressive Forces
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 15th] The Far East spy network of American imperialism has recently penetrated deeply into Thailand. According to a report in the Singapore Nanyang Siang Pau on April 13th, quoting the Thai newspaper "Lak Chai": The Asian spy agency led by MacArthur, the U.S. Commander in Japan, has recently sent spies disguised as businessmen to infiltrate Thailand, and has begun to contact and cooperate with figures in Thai political circles and retired military officers. The main task of these spies is not only to engage in reconnaissance activities against progressive forces in Thailand, but also to be responsible for reconnaissance of Thailand's state secrets.

### 美间谍深入泰国　从事侦查进步势力

1950-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　美间谍深入泰国
　　从事侦查进步势力
    【新华社十五日讯】美帝国主义的远东间谍网最近已深入泰国。据新加坡南侨日报四月十三日引泰国“叻猜报”消息：美驻日统帅麦克阿瑟所领导的亚洲间谍机构，最近已派出间谍，伪装商人，潜入泰国，并与泰国政界人物、退职军官开始联系合作。这些间谍的主要任务除了从事侦查泰国进步势力的活动外，并将负责侦察泰国的国家机密。
