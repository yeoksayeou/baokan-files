　　Brief Reply to Comrade Wang Wenyu:
    Peking University, Tsinghua University, Normal University, Sino-French University, Fu Jen Catholic University (Private), Yenching University (Private), and Peking Union Medical College (Private) were all established before liberation and are now directly under the leadership of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government.
    Renmin University and Agricultural University were established after liberation and are also directly under the leadership of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government.
    Beijing Railway Management Institute (Ministry of Railways), North China Institute of Technology (Ministry of Heavy Industry), Central Academy of Drama (Ministry of Culture), Central Academy of Fine Arts (Ministry of Culture), and Peking University School of Medicine (Ministry of Health) are not directly under the leadership of the Ministry of Education.
