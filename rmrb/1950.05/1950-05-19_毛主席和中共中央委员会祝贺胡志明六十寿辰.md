Title: Chairman Mao and the CCP Central Committee Congratulate Ho Chi Minh on His Sixtieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1950-05-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th: This month, May 19th, marks the sixtieth birthday of President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China has sent a telegram of congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows: To His Excellency President Ho Chi Minh of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:
　　On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, I extend to you, on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and myself, my warm congratulations. I celebrate the victory of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, under your leadership and with the support of all patriotic Vietnamese people, over foreign invaders and their running dogs, and I celebrate the eternal unity of the Chinese and Vietnamese peoples in jointly dedicating themselves to the cause of peace in Asia.
　　　　　　　　　Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　May 12, 1950

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to Ho Chi Minh, congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday. The text of the telegram is as follows: Dear Comrade Ho Chi Minh:
　　On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, I wish you good health and longevity, and I wish you complete victory in leading the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in its struggle for national liberation.
　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　May 12, 1950

### 毛主席和中共中央委员会祝贺胡志明六十寿辰

1950-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席和中共中央委员会祝贺胡志明六十寿辰
    【新华社十八日讯】本月十九日为越南民主共和国胡志明主席六十寿辰。我中央人民政府毛泽东主席特电祝贺。电文如下：越南民主共和国政府胡志明主席阁下：
    值此阁下六十寿辰之际，谨代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府和我个人向您致热烈的祝贺。我庆祝越南民主共和国在您的领导和全越南爱国人民的支持之下，战胜外国侵略者及其走狗，并且庆祝中越两国人民永远团结一致，共同致力于亚洲的和平事业。
                          中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东
                                         一九五○年五月十二日
    【新华社十八日讯】中国共产党中央委员会致电胡志明，祝贺他六十寿辰。电文如下：亲爱的胡志明同志：
    在你六十寿辰之际，谨祝你健康和长寿，并祝你在领导越南民主共和国进行民族解放的斗争中得到完全胜利。
                          中国共产党中央委员会
                          一九五○年五月十二日
