Title: Shanghai Commemorates the "May Thirtieth" Incident: Old Comrades-in-Arms of Gu Zhenghong Present Wreaths to Martyr Gu

Author:
Date: 1950-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 30th. Today is the 25th anniversary of the "May Thirtieth" Shanghai Nanjing Road Massacre. Shanghai newspapers have published articles commemorating this great day of the Chinese people's heroic struggle against imperialism. Workers from the Second Factory of the China Textile Construction Company, where the martyr Gu Zhenghong worked before his death, held a special memorial meeting. Old workers who had worked and fought alongside Martyr Gu and are now production heroes presented wreaths at the site of his martyrdom to express their condolences.

### 上海纪念“五卅”  顾正红的老战友给顾烈士献花圈

1950-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海纪念“五卅”
    顾正红的老战友给顾烈士献花圈
    【新华社上海三十日电】今天是“五卅”上海南京路惨案廿五周年纪念日，沪各报均发表文章，纪念这一中国人民与帝国主义英勇斗争的伟大日子。死难烈士顾正红生前工作的中国纺织建设公司第二厂的工人们特举行纪念大会，曾与顾烈士一同工作和斗争而今日是生产功臣的老工人们，并在烈士殉难处献花圈致哀。
