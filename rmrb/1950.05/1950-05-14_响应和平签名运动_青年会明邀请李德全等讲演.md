Title: Responding to the Peace Signature Campaign: YMCA to Invite Li Dequan and Others to Speak Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-05-14
Page: 3

　　Responding to the Peace Signature Campaign
　　YMCA to Invite Li Dequan and Others to Speak Tomorrow
　　[Reported by this newspaper] The YMCA of this city has specially invited Minister Li Dequan, representative of the Committee for the Defense of World Peace Congress, and Mr. Wu Yaozong and Mr. Liu Liangmo, representatives of the religious community of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, to speak at the YMCA at 4:00 PM on May 14th (Sunday) on the topic of "The Signature Campaign to Defend World Peace."

### 响应和平签名运动　青年会明邀请李德全等讲演

1950-05-14
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　响应和平签名运动
　　青年会明邀请李德全等讲演
    【本报讯】本市青年会定五月十四日（星期日）下午四时特请保卫世界和平大会委员会代表李德全部长及人民政协宗教界代表吴耀宗先生、刘良模先生在该会讲：“保卫世界和平签名运动”。
