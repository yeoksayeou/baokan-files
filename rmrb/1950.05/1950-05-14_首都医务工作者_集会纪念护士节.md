Title: Capital Medical Workers Gather to Commemorate Nurses Day
Author:
Date: 1950-05-14
Page: 3
Capital Medical Workers
　　Gather to Commemorate Nurses Day
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th — Over a thousand medical workers in the capital held a grand commemoration of "May 12th" Nurses Day on the 12th. The commemoration was jointly hosted by the labor unions of public and private hospitals in Beijing, the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, the Beijing Medical Workers Association, and the Beijing Municipal People's Government Public Health Bureau. In addition to medical workers from the capital, Minister of Health of the Central People's Government Li Dequan, Minister of Children's Welfare of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Kang Keqing, and Minister of Social Services Cao Mengjun also attended. Minister Li Dequan and Minister Kang Keqing both encouraged medical workers in their speeches to strengthen their studies, transform their thinking, establish a fine style of people's medical work, and wholeheartedly serve the people. Finally, Ruan Xuehua, who won the title of Model Medical Worker in Yan'an during the Anti-Japanese War, spoke, and was warmly welcomed by the attendees. After the meeting, the film "Michurin" was shown.

### 首都医务工作者　集会纪念护士节

1950-05-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　首都医务工作者
　　集会纪念护士节
    【新华社十二日讯】首都医务工作者一千多人十二日举行“五一二”护士节的盛大纪念会。纪念大会由北京市公立及私立医院工会、北京市民主妇女联合会、北京医务工作者协会、北京市人民政府公共卫生局联合主持。到会的除首都医务工作者外，尚有中央人民政府卫生部部长李德全、中华全国民主妇女联合会儿童福利部长康克清、社会服务部长曹孟君等。李德全部长和康克清部长在讲话中都勉励医务工作者要加强学习，改造思想，树立人民医务工作的优良作风，全心全意为人民服务。最后由抗日战争时期在延安荣获模范医务工作者称号的阮雪华讲话，受到与会者的热烈欢迎。会后放映影片《米邱林》。
