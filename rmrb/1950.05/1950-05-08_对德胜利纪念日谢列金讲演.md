Title: Shelepin's Speech on Victory Over Germany Day
Author:
Date: 1950-05-08
Page: 3

Beijing News

　　Shelepin's Speech on Victory Over Germany Day
    [Report from this newspaper] The Beijing Soviet Foreign Culture Association and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association have scheduled a commemorative meeting for the fifth anniversary of Victory Over Germany Day at 7:30 PM on May 8th at 19 Nanheyan Street. Mr. Shelepin, representative of the Soviet Foreign Culture Association in China, will give a speech entitled "Fifth Anniversary of the Defeat of Fascist Germany." A film will be shown after the meeting. Anyone holding an admission ticket or a long-term listening pass from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association for Soviet introductions is welcome to attend. Branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association requiring admission tickets may obtain them by presenting a letter of introduction to the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association at 2 Yuheqiao before 2:00 PM today. (Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association)

### 对德胜利纪念日谢列金讲演

1950-05-08
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　对德胜利纪念日谢列金讲演
    【本报讯】北京苏联对外文化协会、北京市中苏友好协会为纪念对德胜利日五周年，定于五月八日下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行纪念会，请苏联对外文化协会驻中国代表谢列金讲“击溃法西斯德国五周年纪念”。会后并放映电影。凡持有入场券和中苏友协苏联介绍长期听讲证者，均可前往参加。中苏友协各支会如需入场券者，可于本日下午二时以前持介绍信往御河桥二号北京市中苏友好协会索取。                  （北京市中苏友好协会）
