Title: Government Administration Council's 33rd Meeting Passes Directive on Relief for Unemployed Workers
Author:
Date: 1950-05-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th

　　The Government Administration Council's 33rd Meeting Passes Directive on Relief for Unemployed Workers
    [Xinhua News Agency, 19th dispatch] The 33rd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held at 3:30 PM on the 19th. The meeting heard reports on disaster conditions and work from Liu Jingfan, head of the Anhui-North and Pingyuan inspection groups of the Central Disaster Relief Inspection Team; Wu Xinyu, head of the Jiangsu-North and Shandong inspection groups; Chao Zhefu, Chairman of the People's Government of Pingyuan Province; and Yang Xiufeng, Chairman of the People's Government of Hebei Province, specially dispatched by the Government Administration Council to assist in disaster relief work in Anhui-North. After hearing a report from Li Lisan, Minister of Labor of the Central People's Government, on the issue of relief for unemployed workers, the meeting discussed and approved the directive on relief for unemployed workers and approved the interim measures of the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government regarding relief for unemployed workers. In addition, the meeting approved several appointments and submitted them to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院卅三次会议通过救济失业工人指示

1950-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院卅三次会议通过救济失业工人指示
    【新华社十九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第三十三次政务会议于十九日下午三时半举行。会议听取了中央救灾视察团皖北、平原视察组组长刘景范，苏北、山东视察组组长武新宇，平原省人民政府主席晁哲甫及政务院特派前往皖北协助救灾工作的河北省人民政府主席杨秀峰等关于各该地区的灾情和工作报告。会议在听取了中央人民政府劳动部部长李立三关于救济失业工人问题的报告后，讨论和通过了救济失业工人的指示，并批准了中央人民政府劳动部关于救济失业工人的暂行办法。此外，会议通过任命及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命多起。
