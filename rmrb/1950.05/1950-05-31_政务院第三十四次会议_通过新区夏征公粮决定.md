Title: Government Administration Council's 34th Meeting Passes Decision on Summer Grain Tax in Newly Liberated Areas
Author:
Date: 1950-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 30th. The 34th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held at 8:00 PM on the 30th. After hearing a report by Bo Yibo, Vice Chairman of the Financial and Economic Commission of the Government Administration Council and Minister of Finance, on the issue of the summer grain tax, the meeting discussed and passed the decision on the 1950 summer grain tax in the newly liberated areas.

### 政务院第三十四次会议  通过新区夏征公粮决定

1950-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第三十四次会议
    通过新区夏征公粮决定
    【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第三十四次政务会议于三十日下午八时举行。会议在听取了政务院财政经济委员会副主任兼财政部部长薄一波关于夏征公粮问题的报告后，讨论并通过了关于一九五○年新解放区夏征公粮的决定。
