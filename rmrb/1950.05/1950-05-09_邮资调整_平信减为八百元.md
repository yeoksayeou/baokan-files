Title: Postage Adjustment: Ordinary Mail Reduced to 800 Yuan
Author:
Date: 1950-05-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, in order to make postage rates suitable for the current price situation, has instructed the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government to adjust the current domestic postage rates from the 11th of this month as follows: Ordinary mail 800 yuan, postcards 400 yuan, news dispatches 600 yuan, priority mail 1600 yuan, single registered mail 3200 yuan, double registered mail 5600 yuan, registered priority mail 4000 yuan; printed matter 240 yuan per 100 sq cm, ordinary newspapers 100 yuan per 50 sq cm, securities newspapers 160 yuan per 100 sq cm, bulk newspapers 200 yuan per kilogram. Postage in the Northeast will remain at the original rates. With the exception of bulk newspapers, which were not changed due to postal transportation costs, the above postage rates for all types of mail have been restored to the tariff standards before March 10 of this year.

### 邮资调整  平信减为八百元

1950-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　邮资调整  平信减为八百元
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会为使邮资适应于目前物价情势，已令中央人民政府邮电部将现行国内邮资自本月十一日起分别调整如下：平信八○○元、明信片四○○元、新闻稿件六○○元、平快信一六○○元、单挂号信三二○○元、双挂号信五六○○元、挂号快信四○○○元；印刷品每一百公分二四○元、普通新闻纸每五十公分一○○元、立券新闻纸每一百公分一六○元、总包新闻纸每一公斤二○○元。东北邮资仍维原数。以上各类邮件资费，除总包新闻纸因邮政运输成本关系未予变动外，其他均已恢复本年三月十日以前的资费标准。
