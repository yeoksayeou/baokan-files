Title: Greeting the Supreme Soviet Representative Meeting: Soviet Workers Launch Competition, Moscow Tire Factory's Daily Production Exceeds Quota

Author:

Date: 1950-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 23rd, TASS dispatch: Workers in various parts of the Soviet Union have launched socialist competitions to greet the Supreme Soviet Representative Meeting, which will open in Moscow on June 12th. The Moscow Tire Factory's daily production exceeds its plan. Workers at the Moscow "Dynamo" factory have increased their production guarantees. Miners of the Moscow Basin "Red Guard" coal mine trust completed their five-month coal production plan eleven days ahead of schedule.

　　In the various departments of the Kuznetsk Metallurgical Combine, blast furnace workers have successfully adopted the Stakhanovite method of work, producing hundreds of tons of pig iron daily above the plan.

　　Miners at the Voroshilov mine in Krivoy Rog are producing ore according to the seventh month's plan. Due to the large-scale application of the most advanced labor methods, their production has exceeded last year's by 62 percent.

　　In the Urals, thousands of miners, metallurgical workers, and machine-building workers have begun to adopt the Stakhanovite method of work. Personnel at the "Ural Electrical Equipment" factory have decided to complete the six-month plan before June 15th and also to work for four days in May and June using only the raw materials, fuel, and electricity saved.

### 迎接最高苏维埃代表会议  苏联工人展开竞赛  莫斯科车胎厂每日生产量均超过定额

1950-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　迎接最高苏维埃代表会议
    苏联工人展开竞赛       
    莫斯科车胎厂每日生产量均超过定额
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十三日讯：苏联各地工人纷纷展开社会主义竞赛，以迎接六月十二日在莫斯科揭幕的苏联最高苏维埃代表会议。莫斯科车胎工厂每日的生产都超过计划。莫斯科“狄纳莫”工厂的工人已提高了生产保证。莫斯科盆地“赤卫队”煤矿托辣斯的矿工，已比规定时间早十一天完成了五个月的产煤计划。
    在库兹涅茨克的冶金联合企业各部门中，高炉的司炉工人们成功地采用了斯塔哈诺夫式工作方法，他们每天都超计划地生产数百吨铣铁。
    在克里佛伊·罗格的伏罗希洛夫矿坑的矿工，正按照第七个月的计划生产矿砂。由于大规模应用最先进的劳动方法，他们的生产已超过去年百分之六十二。
    在乌拉尔，数千矿工、冶金工人和机器制造工人，已开始采用斯塔哈诺夫式工作方法。“乌拉尔电工器材”厂的人员已决定在六月十五日以前完成六个月计划，还要在五月份与六月份只用节省下来的原料、燃料和电力工作四天。
