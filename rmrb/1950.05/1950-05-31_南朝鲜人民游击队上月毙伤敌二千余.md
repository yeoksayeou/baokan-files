Title: South Korean People's Guerrillas Killed or Wounded Over 2,000 Enemies Last Month
Author:
Date: 1950-05-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th: The South Korean Labor News published the following summary of the South Korean People's Guerrillas' combat results in April: In April, the South Korean People's Guerrillas fought 1,423 battles against puppet troops and police, mobilizing 65,005 people. They killed or wounded 2,868 puppet soldiers and police, captured 3 mortars, and a total of 244 weapons including machine guns and rifles, more than 250 mortar shells, and more than 280,000 bullets.

　　In mid-April, the proactive counterattacks of the People's Guerrillas in the Odaesan area dealt a serious blow to two regiments of the puppet army's 6th Division that were carrying out "suppression" campaigns.

### 南朝鲜人民游击队上月毙伤敌二千余

1950-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜人民游击队上月毙伤敌二千余
    【新华社平壤三十日电】南朝鲜劳力者报发表南朝鲜人民游击队四月份综合战果如下：南朝鲜人民游击队在四月一个月中，与伪军警作战一千四百二十三次，动员人数达六万五千零五人。毙伤伪军警二千八百六十八名，缴获迫击炮三门，机枪、步枪等武器共二百四十四支，迫击炮弹二百五十余发，子弹二十八万余发。
    在四月中，五台山地区的人民游击队的主动反击，予进行“讨伐”的伪军第六师团的两个联队以严重打击。
