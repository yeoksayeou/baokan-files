Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Continues to Hold Science Lectures
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 3

　　Chinese Academy of Sciences
　　Continues to Hold Science Lectures
    [By our reporter] The Chinese Academy of Sciences has scheduled two science lectures this weekend. On May 13 (Saturday) at 4:00 PM, Professor Zhang Longxiang of the Chemistry Department of Peking University will give a lecture on "The Application of Isotopes in Biochemistry"; on the 14th at 4:00 PM, Professor Li Jitong of the Biology Department of Tsinghua University will give a lecture on "Introduction to the Work of Michurin." Both lectures will be held in the auditorium of the Sino-French University on Donghuangchenggen, Beijing. Professor Zhang Longxiang's lecture will be supplemented with slides. The academy held its first lecture on the 6th, given by Professor Hua Luogeng of the Mathematics Department of Tsinghua University, on "The Contributions of Soviet Mathematicians to Famous Problems in Mathematics." More than 800 people attended.

### 中国科学院　连续举办科学讲座

1950-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国科学院
　　连续举办科学讲座
    【本报讯】中国科学院定本周末举办科学讲座二次。十三日（星期六）下午四时由北京大学化学系张龙翔教授主讲《同位素在生物化学上的应用》；十四日下午四时由清华大学生物系李继侗教授主讲《米邱林的工作介绍》。地点均在北京市东皇城根中法大学大礼堂。张龙翔教授之讲演并将用幻灯辅助说明。按该院曾于六日举办第一次讲座，由清华大学数学系华罗庚教授主讲《苏联数学家对于数学上著名问题的贡献》。听讲者达八百余人。
