Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Sends Condolences on the Death of Agnes Smedley
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 1

　　All-China Democratic Women's Federation Sends Condolences on the Death of Agnes Smedley
    [Report from this newspaper] The All-China Democratic Women's Federation sent a telegram to the American Council for a Democratic Far East on the 11th, expressing condolences on the death of the famous American writer and journalist Agnes Smedley. The telegram is as follows: American Council for a Democratic Far East:
    We deeply mourn the death of Ms. Agnes Smedley, a friend of the Chinese people, and express our extreme indignation at the American imperialist government's animosity towards her, which forced her into exile in England, ultimately leading to her death. We are willing to unite with all friends who are loyal to the cause of world peace and democracy to jointly defend lasting world peace and strive for people's democracy to commemorate this revolutionary comrade who persisted in struggle.
                                    All-China Democratic Women's Federation
                                    Chairman Cai Chang
                             Vice-Chairmen Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, Xu Guangping

### 全国民主妇联电唁史沫特莱逝世

1950-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国民主妇联电唁史沫特莱逝世
    【本报讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会于十一日致电美国远东民主政策委员会，对美国著名作家与记者史沫特莱的逝世，表示哀悼，电文如下：美国远东民主政策委员会：
    我们深深哀悼中国人民的朋友史沫特莱女士的逝世，对于美帝国主义政府对她的仇视，并迫使她流亡英国，终致于死，表示异常的愤憾。我们愿意团结一切忠诚于世界和平民主事业的朋友，共同捍卫世界持久和平，争取人民民主来纪念这位坚持奋斗的革命战友。
                                    中华全国民主妇女联合会
                                    主席  蔡畅
                             副主席  邓颖超  李德全  许广平
