Title: Wave of Peace Signature Campaign Unfolds; Mass Meetings Held in Various Places; President Bolesław Bierut of Poland Leads in Signing

Author: 
Date: 1950-05-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch: Warsaw dispatch: A nationwide campaign to sign the Appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress is being widely launched in Poland. The Polish peace signature campaign began in Warsaw, the capital of Poland. President Bolesław Bierut, Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz, and famous figures from Warsaw's shock workers, science, culture, and the arts, were the first to sign the Great Appeal. Warsaw residents warmly welcomed the signature collectors and conducted the signing in a heated atmosphere. People all over Poland also held meetings to denounce the imperialist warmongers and express their support for the Great Appeal of the Standing Committee.

    At the same time, new committees for the defense of peace are being established throughout Poland. In the city and province of Łódź alone, more than 500 village residential areas and street peace committees were established in just three days from the 2nd to the 4th. Women and young people account for a large percentage of the committee members. Many clergymen also participated in the committee. In rural areas, agricultural workers and farmers work side-by-side in the peace committees.

### 波展开和平签名运动　各地纷纷开群众大会　贝鲁特总统率先签名

1950-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　波展开和平签名运动
　　各地纷纷开群众大会         
　  贝鲁特总统率先签名
    【新华社十三日讯】华沙讯：波兰全国已广泛展开在世界拥护和平大会常委会呼吁书上签名的运动。波兰的和平签名运动系自波兰首都华沙开始。共和国总统贝鲁特、总理西伦凯维兹，以及华沙突击工人和科学、文化、艺术界的著名人物，首先在和大呼吁书上签了名。华沙居民热诚地接待征集签名的人员，并在热烈的气氛中进行签名。波兰各地的人民，亦纷纷举行会议，斥责帝国主义战争贩子，表示拥护和大常委会的呼吁书。
    与此同时，波兰全国各地正在建立新的拥护和平委员会。仅在二日至四日三天内，在罗兹市与罗兹省，就成立了五百多个乡村居民区与街市的拥护和平委员会。妇女与青年在委员会委员中所占的百分比很大。许多教士也参加了委员会。在农村中，农业工人和农民们肩并肩地在和平委员会里工作。
