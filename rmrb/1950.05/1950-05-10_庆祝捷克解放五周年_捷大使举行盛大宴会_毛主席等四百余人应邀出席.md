Title: Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia, the Czech Ambassador Holds a Grand Banquet; Chairman Mao and More Than 400 Others Attend
Author:
Date: 1950-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch: To celebrate the fifth anniversary of the liberation of the Czechoslovak Republic, Czech Ambassador to China, Weisskopf, held a grand banquet at 8:00 PM today. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China was invited to attend. More than 400 other invitees included Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, and Li Jishen, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Premier of the State Council and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and heads of various ministries, academies, commissions, administrations, and banks. Foreign diplomatic envoys invited to attend included Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Mrs. Lee Joo-yeon, wife of the Korean Ambassador, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Hungarian Minister Szaffránkó, Polish Chargé d'Affaires Birochi, Indian Chargé d'Affaires Haksar, Swedish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Gullers, and others. The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝捷克解放五周年  捷大使举行盛大宴会  毛主席等四百余人应邀出席

1950-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

  　庆祝捷克解放五周年
    捷大使举行盛大宴会         
    毛主席等四百余人应邀出席
    【新华社九日讯】捷克驻我国大使魏斯柯普夫为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克共和国解放五周年纪念日，特于今日下午八时举行盛大宴会。中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席应邀出席。其他应邀与会的四百余人中有中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深，秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，副总理陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培，及各部、院、会、署、行首长。各国外交使节应邀出席的有苏联大使罗申，朝鲜李周渊大使夫人，罗马尼亚大使鲁登科，匈牙利公使夏法朗柯，波兰代办毕罗奇，印度代办雁谒森，瑞典临时代办高罗士等。宴会在热烈的友好气氛中进行。
