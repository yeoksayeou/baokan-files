　　New Knowledge Forum: Xu Dixin's Speech

[Our newspaper's report] The Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association is scheduled to hold its 70th New Knowledge Forum at 7:00 PM on the 19th. The location is the Physicians' Association at No. 4 Dongdan Santiao. Mr. Xu Dixin, Deputy Director of the East China Military Region Finance and Economics Committee and concurrently Director of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Industry and Commerce, will be the speaker, lecturing on "Urban Economy."

### 新知识座谈会许涤新讲演

1950-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会许涤新讲演
    【本报讯】民主建国会北京市分会定于十九日下午七时举行第七十次新知识座谈会，地点是在东单三条四号医师公会，这次是请华东军区财经委员会副主任兼上海市工商局长许涤新先生主讲“城市经济”。
