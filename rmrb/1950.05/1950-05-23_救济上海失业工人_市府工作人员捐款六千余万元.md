Title: Relief for Unemployed Workers in Shanghai: City Government Staff Donates Over 60 Million Yuan
Author:
Date: 1950-05-23
Page: 3

　　Relief for Unemployed Workers in Shanghai
    City Government Staff Donates Over 60 Million Yuan
    [Beijing News Agency] Staff members of various units under the Beijing Municipal People's Government enthusiastically donated money to aid unemployed workers in Shanghai. The entire staff donated a total of 62,230,298 yuan, which has been sent to the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions for transfer to the unemployed worker brothers in Shanghai.

### 救济上海失业工人  市府工作人员捐款六千余万元

1950-05-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　救济上海失业工人
    市府工作人员捐款六千余万元
    【京市新闻处讯】京市人民政府所属各单位工作人员，热烈捐款救济上海失业工人。全体工作人员共捐款六千二百二十三万零二百九十八元，已送市总工会转给上海失业工人弟兄。
