Title: More Than 100,000 Working People in Pyongyang Celebrate May Day at Kim Il-sung Square
Author:
Date: 1950-05-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th - The working people here are enthusiastically welcoming the "May 1st" International Labor Day. Commemorative slogans are hung everywhere in the streets of Pyongyang. Groups of young men and women, wearing bright clothes and carrying musical instruments, go to the Minqing Club to rehearse celebration programs. Flower shops and poster shops are doing particularly well. From the 25th to the 30th, factories, agencies, and schools held report meetings one after another. On the evening of the 30th, a memorial meeting for all circles of the Republic will be held under the auspices of the Pyongyang Municipal People's Committee. On the morning of "May 1st," more than 100,000 working people will gather in Kim Il-sung Square in the city center to review their strength, and will hold parades and various sports competitions.

### 平壤十余万劳动人民今在金日成广场庆祝五一

1950-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　平壤十余万劳动人民今在金日成广场庆祝五一
    【新华社平壤三十日电】此间劳动人民热情地迎接“五一”国际劳动节。平壤的街头到处悬挂着纪念标语。成群结队的男女青年，穿着鲜艳的服装、携带着乐器，到民青俱乐部去排演庆祝节目。鲜花店和广告画店的生意格外兴隆。工厂、机关、学校，从二十五日到三十日，纷纷举行报告会。三十日晚将在平壤市人民委员会的主持下，举行共和国各界纪念会。在“五一”的早晨，十余万的劳动人民，将集结在市中心区的金日成广场，检阅自己的力量，并将游行示威和举行各种体育竞赛。
