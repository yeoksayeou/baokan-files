Title: The 31st Government Affairs Council Meeting Approves the Organizational Outline of the Northeast People's Government; The Government Administration Council Announces Two Appointments
Author:
Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: The Government Administration Council held its 31st meeting at 3:00 PM today. The council heard a report from Minister of Justice Shi Liang regarding the Ministry's work plan for the second quarter of this year, approved the Organizational Outline of the Northeast People's Government, and passed two lists of appointments.

### 第三十一次政务会议  批准东北人民政府组织大纲  政务院发表两项任命

1950-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　第三十一次政务会议
    批准东北人民政府组织大纲
    政务院发表两项任命
    【新华社五日讯】政务院今日下午三时举行第三十一次政务会议，听取了司法部史良部长关于该部本年第二季工作计划的报告，批准了东北人民政府组织大纲，并通过任命名单两项。
