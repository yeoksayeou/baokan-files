Title: China Peace Committee: Tianjin, Dalian, and Xi'an Branches Established
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 1

　　China Peace Committee
　　Tianjin, Dalian, and Xi'an Branches Established

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 11th. The Tianjin branch of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace was officially established on the 10th. Huang Songling was elected as the chairman of the branch, Li Zhuchen and twelve others as vice-chairmen, and Wang Kangzhi and one hundred and three others as committee members. The committee decided to launch a city-wide signature campaign to defend peace, with the 15th to 22nd designated as a propaganda week.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, 11th. The Dalian branch of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace was officially established on the 8th. The chairman of the standing committee of the branch is Guo Shushen (Chairman of the Dalian General Labor Union), and the vice-chairmen are Qiao Chuanjue (President of the Dalian Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association), Wu Yanan (Chairman of the Dalian Democratic Women's Federation), and Wu Shanchang (Deputy Director of the Dalian People's Daily). The branch has decided to launch a broad signature campaign demanding the unconditional prohibition of atomic weapons and the establishment of strict controls.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 11th. More than 500 representatives from 330 organizations of all ethnic groups in Xi'an held a meeting on the 10th in response to the call of the Permanent Committee of the World Peace Congress. The Xi'an branch of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace was established on the spot, and it was decided to widely and deeply launch a signature campaign to ban atomic warfare. The conference elected Yang Mingxuan as chairman and passed a telegram of condolences to Curie, Chairman of the Permanent Committee of the World Peace Congress.

### 中国和大委员会　天津大连西安分会成立

1950-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国和大委员会
　　天津大连西安分会成立
    【新华社天津十一日电】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会天津分会于十日正式成立，选出黄松龄为分会会长，李烛尘等十二人为副会长，王亢之等一百零三人为委员。该会决定开展全市保卫和平签名运动，定十五日至二十二日为宣传周。
    【新华社大连十一日电】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会大连分会于八日正式成立。分会常设委员会主席为郭述申（旅大总工会主席），副主席为乔传珏（中苏友好协会旅大分会会长）、吴亚南（旅大民主妇联主席）、吴善昌（旅大人民日报副社长）。该分会已决定展开广泛的要求无条件禁止原子武器、建立严格管制的签名运动。
    【新华社西安十一日电】西安市各族各界三百三十个团体的代表五百余人，为响应世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的呼吁，于十日举行集会。当场成立中国保卫世界和平大会委员会西安分会，并决定广泛深入地展开禁止原子战争的签名运动。大会选出杨明轩为主席，并通过致电慰问世界拥护和平大会常设委员会主席居里。
