　　Cultural and Educational Briefs
    The "Science Bulletin," published by the Chinese Academy of Sciences, was launched on the 15th. The first issue includes an inaugural message from President Guo Moruo; an overview of the Academy of Sciences; reports on domestic scientific work; an overview of the research institutions under the Academy of Sciences; domestic and foreign science briefs; and developments in the scientific community. The bulletin is distributed by Xinhua Bookstore.
    The Southwest People's Broadcasting Station officially began operations in Chongqing on the 5th of this month. Medium wave shortwave wavelength: 27.27 meters, 11000 kHz; morning and evening wavelength: 49 meters, 6154 kHz.
    In order to cultivate intellectuals from worker and peasant backgrounds, the Shanxi Provincial People's Government has established a Worker-Peasant Accelerated Secondary School in Taiyuan. The school has decided that the first half of this year will be a trial period, temporarily enrolling 160 worker-peasant students, and plans to enroll up to 1,000 secondary school students in the second half of the year.
    The Chongqing National Southwest People's Library was established on the 24th of last month. The library was originally a part of the puppet "Central Library," with a collection of over 110,000 volumes.
                                            [Xinhua News Agency]

### 文化教育简讯

1950-05-19
第3版()
专栏：文化教育简讯

　　文化教育简讯
    中国科学院出版的《科学通报》于十五日创刊。该刊第一期有郭沫若院长的发刊词；科学院概况；国内科学工作报道；科学院所属各研究机构概况；国内外科学简讯和科学界动态等。该刊由新华书店总经售。
    西南人民广播电台已于本月五日在重庆市正式成立。中午短波波长：二七·二七公尺，一一○○○千周；早晨和晚间波长：四九公尺，六一五四千周。
    山西省人民政府为培养工农出身的知识分子，已在太原创办工农速成中学一所。该校决定今年上半年为试办时期，暂收工农学员一百六十名，下半年计划招收中学学员至一千名。
    重庆市国立西南人民图书馆已于上月二十四日成立。该馆原为伪“中央图书馆”之一部，藏书十一万余册。
                                            【新华社】
