Title: Professor Baodelev Lectures at Renmin University
Author:
Date: 1950-05-14
Page: 3

　　Professor Baodelev Lectures at Renmin University
　　[This Newspaper's dispatch] China Renmin University invited Professor Baodelev, a Soviet economic master who had just returned to Beijing from Tianjin, to give a lecture at the university on the afternoon of the 11th, entitled "The Difference Between the Soviet Financial System and the Capitalist Financial System." Professor Baodelev first analyzed the essential differences between capitalist and socialist countries, and thus established two different financial systems. Then, regarding the income and expenditure of the fiscal budget, the purpose and repayment of national bonds, and the taxation departments, he specifically explained the differences between the two financial systems.
                                            (Renmin University Communications Group)

### 包德列夫教授在人民大学讲演

1950-05-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　包德列夫教授在人民大学讲演
    【本报讯】中国人民大学于十一日下千约请新由津返京的苏联经济学硕士包德列夫教授在该校讲演《苏联财政制度与资本主义财政制度的区别》。包德列夫教授首先分析了资本主义国家与社会主义国家在本质上的不同，因此也就建立起来两种不同的财政制度；继就财政预算的岁出、岁入两方面，国家发行公债的目的与偿还，以及税收等三部门，具体地说明了两种财政制度的区别。
                                            （人民大学通讯组）
