Title: China and Sweden Establish Diplomatic Relations; Geng Biao and Ahmansson Appointed as Ambassadors

Author:
Date: 1950-05-09
Page: 1

　　China and Sweden Establish Diplomatic Relations
    Mutual Appointment of Geng Biao and Ahmansson as Ambassadors
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 9th] The Swedish government announced on January 14th of this year its willingness to establish diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China. On January 18th, Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of our ministry, replied to Mr. Östen Undén, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Swedish government, expressing the same desire and requesting the Swedish government to send representatives to Beijing to negotiate the preliminary and procedural matters regarding the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Sweden. Mr. Kaoru Ros, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Swedish government, arrived in Beijing on February 26th and immediately began these negotiations. The negotiations have now been successfully concluded. The governments of China and Sweden have also agreed that the Swedish government will appoint Mr. Ahmansson as Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China will appoint General Geng Biao as Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Sweden.

### 中瑞建立外交关系  互派耿飚阿马斯顿为大使

1950-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　中瑞建立外交关系
    互派耿飚阿马斯顿为大使
    【新华社九日讯】瑞典政府于本年一月十四日宣布愿与中华人民共和国建立外交关系。我外交部周恩来部长当于一月十八日电复瑞典政府外交部部长奥斯丁·安登，表示同样愿望，并请瑞典政府派遣代表前来北京，就中瑞两国建立外交关系的初步与程序问题进行谈判。瑞典政府派遣的谈判代表高罗士临时代办，于二月二十六日到达北京，随即开始进行此项谈判。现谈判已顺利结束。中瑞两国政府并已同意：瑞典政府派遣阿马斯顿为驻中华人民共和国大使，中华人民共和国中央人民政府派遣耿飚将军为中华人民共和国驻瑞典大使。
