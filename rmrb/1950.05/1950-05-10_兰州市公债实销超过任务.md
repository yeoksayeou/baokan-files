Title: Lanzhou City Public Bonds Sales Exceed Target

Author:
Date: 1950-05-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 9th. The Lanzhou City People's Victory Real Value Public Bond sales task has been overfulfilled. As of May 2nd, the city's payment figure has reached 394,635 portions, exceeding the original allocation by 54,635 portions.

### 兰州市公债实销超过任务

1950-05-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　兰州市公债实销超过任务
    【新华社西安九日电】兰州市人民胜利折实公债推销任务已超额完成。截至五月二日止，该市缴款数字已达三十九万四千六百三十五分，超过原分配额五万四千六百三十五分。
