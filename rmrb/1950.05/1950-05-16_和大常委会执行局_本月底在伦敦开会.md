Title: Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council to Meet in London at the End of This Month
Author:
Date: 1950-05-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris: TASS reports that the Executive Bureau of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress will hold a plenary meeting in London on May 31 to discuss how to advance the global movement to collect signatures for the World Peace Congress appeal. The Executive Bureau meeting will also discuss preparations for the Second World Peace Congress, scheduled to be held in the fourth quarter of this year.

### 和大常委会执行局　本月底在伦敦开会

1950-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　和大常委会执行局
　　本月底在伦敦开会
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社巴黎讯：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局将于五月卅一日在伦敦召开全体会议，讨论如何推进全世界的运动，以便为世界和大常委会呼吁书征求签名的问题。执行局会议亦将讨论定于今年第四季召开的第二次世界拥护和平大会的筹备工作。
