Title: 1950 International Peace Prize

Author:

Date: 1950-05-11
Page: 5

When the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress held its third meeting in Stockholm, it selected the judging committee for the 1950 International Peace Prize. This committee will conduct the selection in October of this year and award three International Peace Prizes to the best works of art, film, and literature that have made significant contributions to the cause of peace. As the selection period is approaching, Chinese cultural and artistic workers are encouraged to actively apply.

The full list of the judging committee is as follows:

### 一九五○年国际和平奖金

1950-05-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　一九五○年国际和平奖金
    世界拥护和平大会常设委员会在斯德哥尔摩召开第三次会议时，选出了一九五○年国际和平奖金的评议委员会，该委员会将在本年十月实行评选，并颁发三项国际和平奖金，发给对和平事业具有重大贡献的最优良的艺术、电影与文学作品。现在评选期近，望中国文化艺术工作者踊跃应征。
