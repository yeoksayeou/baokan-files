Title: Absorbing the People's Financial Resources to Further Develop the National Economy. The Soviet Union Issues the Fifth National Bond for a Total of 20 Billion Rubles for a Term of 20 Years. People Throughout the Soviet Union Hold Rallies to Enthusiastically Subscribe.
Author:
Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 4

　　Absorbing the People's Financial Resources to Further Develop the National Economy
    The Soviet Union Issues the Fifth National Bond for a Total of 20 Billion Rubles for a Term of 20 Years
    People Throughout the Soviet Union Hold Rallies to Enthusiastically Subscribe
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th] TASS Moscow, 3rd: To absorb the people's financial resources to further develop the Soviet national economy, the Soviet Council of Ministers has decided to issue the Fifth Soviet National Economic Recovery and Development Bond. The total amount of the bond is 20 billion rubles, with a term of 20 years. Bonds and their proceeds, including interest and bonuses, are exempt from all national and local taxes.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th] Comprehensive TASS News: The Soviet people are enthusiastically subscribing to the bonds, and mass rallies held in various places have become a grand demonstration of Soviet patriotism.
    Workers, peasants, staff, scientific workers, etc., unanimously stated that the new bonds will further promote the consolidation of Soviet power. Many workers and peasants said that the savings from their labor are invested by the government every year to build new factories, schools, and recreation centers. In Donets, in many factories and rural areas of Ukraine, in Latvia, and in many parts of the Soviet Union, the working people of some enterprises and collective farms have completed the subscription of bonds allocated to them within two hours.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th] TASS Moscow, 3rd: The Presidium of the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions has recently passed a resolution on trade union organizations participating in the promotion of the Fifth Soviet National Economic Recovery and Development Bond. The decision stated that the bonds issued according to the government's decision are of great importance for the further development of all sectors of the national economy, the strengthening of the Soviet state, and the further improvement of the material welfare and culture of the Soviet people. The Presidium of the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions instructed all trade union organizations to actively promote the Fifth Soviet National Economic Recovery and Development Bond among workers, staff, intellectuals, and all working people of the Soviet Union.
    The Presidium of the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions urged all male and female workers in industry and transportation, agricultural workers, staff, engineers and technicians, scientific and artistic workers, and all working people of the Soviet Union to subscribe to the bonds with three to four weeks of income.
    The Soviet people will go all out to subscribe to the bonds, thereby demonstrating their infinite love and loyalty to the socialist motherland, to the Communist Party, to the Soviet government, and to the great leader Stalin.

### 吸收人民财力进一步发展国民经济  苏发行第五次国家公债  总额二百亿卢布为期二十年  全苏各地人民集会热烈认购

1950-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　吸收人民财力进一步发展国民经济
    苏发行第五次国家公债  总额二百亿卢布为期二十年
    全苏各地人民集会热烈认购
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：苏联部长会议为了吸收人民的财力以进一步发展苏联国民经济，已决定发行第五次苏联国民经济恢复与发展的国家公债。公债总额为二百亿卢布，为期二十年。公债券及其收益部分，包括利息及奖金，一律免征国家及地方捐税。
    【新华社五日讯】综合塔斯社消息：苏联人民热烈认购公债，各地召开的群众大会，已成为表现苏维埃爱国精神的大示威。
    各地的工人、农民、职员、科学工作者等一致表示：新公债将进一步促进苏联威力的巩固。许多工人农民表示：他们劳动所得的节余，每年都由政府投资建设起新的工厂、学校与休息所。在顿尼兹，在乌克兰的许多工厂和农村，在拉脱维亚和苏联许多地方，若干企业和集体农庄的劳动人民，已在两小时之内，把分配到的公债，签名认购完毕。
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：全苏工会中央理事会主席团顷通过一项关于工会组织参加推销第五次苏联国民经济恢复与发展的国家公债的决定。决定称：根据政府决定所发行的公债，对于国民经济一切部门的进一步发展、苏联国家的加强，及苏联人民的物质福利与文化的进一步增进，具有极大的重要性。全苏工会中央理事会主席团训令一切工会组织，在工人、职员和知识分子中、在苏联一切劳动人民中积极推销第五次苏联国民经济恢复与发展的国家公债。
    全苏工会中央理事会主席团敦促工业与运输业的全体男女工人、农业工人、职员、工程师和技师、科学与艺术工作者、苏联全体劳动人民一致以三——四星期的收入来认购公债。
    苏联人民将以全力认购公债，借以表现出他们对社会主义祖国、对共产党、对苏维埃政府、和对伟大的领袖斯大林的无限热爱与忠诚。
