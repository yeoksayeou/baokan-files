Title: Zhoushan Yellow Croaker Arrives in Ningbo
Author:
Date: 1950-05-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, May 25th: The first batch of large yellow croakers has arrived in Ningbo since the liberation of Zhoushan. On the 19th of this month, the first batch of 25,000 jin of ice-fresh large yellow croakers transported from Dinghai was put on the market in Ningbo. After unloading the fish, the fishing boat exchanged them for food, glue, jute, netting, and other supplies to transport back to Dinghai. Currently, the Ningbo Native Produce Company has sent people to Dinghai to purchase 30,000 dan of fresh fish.

### 舟山黄鱼运抵宁波

1950-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　舟山黄鱼运抵宁波
    【新华社杭州二十五日电】舟山解放后，首批大黄鱼已运抵宁波。本月十九日，自定海运来的第一批冰鲜大黄鱼二万五千斤在宁波上市。该鱼船脱货后，即换购食粮、胶、麻、网线等物资运返定海。现宁波土产公司已派人赴定海收购鲜鱼三万担。
