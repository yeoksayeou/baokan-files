Title: Foreign Minister Zhou Calls Lai and Others, Demanding the Expulsion of Chiang Kai-shek Bandit "Representatives" from Four International Organizations
Author:
Date: 1950-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th—Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, sent telegrams on the 12th to UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie, FAO Director-General Norris E. Dodd, WHO Director-General Brock Chisholm, UNESCO Director-General Jaime Torres Bodet, and World Meteorological Organization Secretariat Director Sverre Petterssen, informing them that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China is the sole legal government representing the Chinese people, and the so-called "representatives" of the remnant group of the Chinese Kuomintang reactionaries are now completely unqualified to participate in these organizations and must be expelled from all institutions and meetings of these organizations. Foreign Minister Zhou requested them to reply and inform all parties concerned in the telegrams. The aforementioned meetings include the Ninth Session of the FAO Council held in Rome on May 8th, the Third World Health Assembly held in Geneva on May 8th, and the Fifth General Conference of UNESCO.

### 周外长致电赖伊等　要求由四个国际组织中开除蒋匪“代表”

1950-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　周外长致电赖伊等
　　要求由四个国际组织中开除蒋匪“代表”
    【新华社十三日讯】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长于十二日分别致电联合国秘书长赖伊，联合国粮食及农业组织总干事陶德，世界卫生组织总干事戚任姆，联合国教育、科学及文化组织总干事鲍台特，及世界气象组织秘书处主任斯渥波达，通知他们说：中华人民共和国中央人民政府是代表中国人民的唯一合法政府，中国国民党反动派残余集团的所谓“代表”现已完全没有资格参加各该组织，必须将这些非法“代表”从各该组织的各项机构和会议中驱逐出去。周外长在电文中要求他们答复并转知有关各方。上述会议，包括今年五月八日在罗马召开的联合国粮食及农业组织的第九届理事会，五月八日在日内瓦召开的第三届世界卫生大会，以及联合国教育、科学及文化组织的第五届大会。
