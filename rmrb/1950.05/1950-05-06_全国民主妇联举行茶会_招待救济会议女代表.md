Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Holds Tea Party to Host Female Representatives of Relief Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-05-06
Page: 3

Beijing News

    All-China Democratic Women's Federation Holds Tea Party
    To Host Female Representatives of Relief Conference
    [By Our Newspaper] The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a tea party at 3:00 PM on April 30th to host the female representatives attending the China People's Relief Conference. A total of over fifty guests and hosts attended. The tea party was presided over by Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the Federation. She expressed a warm welcome to everyone and briefly introduced the work of the All-China Women's Federation over the past year, hoping that everyone would strengthen ties with the Federation and assist in promoting women's work. Representatives Li Fuqing, Xu Zhuan, Yao Shuwen, Chen Wenxian, Rao Guomo, and others successively introduced the general situation of the women's movement in their respective regions and put forward some opinions to the All-China Women's Federation regarding women's culture, education, health and other work. Finally, Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, reported on the process of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace's Standing Committee initiating a peace signature campaign, hoping that everyone would support this call, which received a unanimous and enthusiastic response from the audience.

### 全国民主妇联举行茶会  招待救济会议女代表

1950-05-06
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　全国民主妇联举行茶会
    招待救济会议女代表
    【本报讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会于四月三十日下午三时举行茶会，招待出席中国人民救济会议的女代表。到宾主共五十余人。茶会由该会邓颖超副主席主持，她向大家表示热烈的欢迎，并简要地介绍了全国妇联一年来的工作，希望大家和妇联加强联系，协助推进妇女工作。代表李馥清、徐篆、姚淑文、陈文仙、饶国模等相继介绍了各该地妇运的概况，并向全国妇联提出了一些有关妇女文教、卫生等工作的意见。最后，全国妇联李德全副主席报告关于世界拥护和平大会常委会发起和平签名运动的经过，希望大家拥护这个号召，得到全场一致热烈响应。
