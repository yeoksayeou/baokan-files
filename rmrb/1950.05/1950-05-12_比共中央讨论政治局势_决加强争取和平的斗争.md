Title: Belgian Communist Party Central Committee Discusses Political Situation; Decides to Strengthen the Struggle for Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-05-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, May 9th: The Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party held a meeting to discuss the political situation and the tasks of the Communist Party in the upcoming election campaign. Maurice Horlait, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party, emphasized in his report the increasing war crisis and the fascization of government policies. The Belgian Communist Party has defined its tasks as launching a resolute struggle for peace and for the improvement of the workers' conditions, increasing activities against unloading and transporting American military supplies, and strengthening the campaign to solicit signatures on the Stockholm Appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Partisans for Peace regarding the prohibition of atomic weapons.

### 比共中央讨论政治局势　决加强争取和平的斗争

1950-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　比共中央讨论政治局势
　　决加强争取和平的斗争
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社布鲁塞尔九日讯：比利时共产党中央委员会的会议，讨论了政治局势以及共产党在未来竞选运动中的任务。比利时共产党中央政治局委员何里克，在他的报告中强调指出日益增涨的战争危机与政府政策的法西斯化。比利时共产党规定了自己的任务，要为和平并为改善劳动者的状况发动坚决的斗争，增加反对起卸与运输美国军火的活动，并且加强征求在世界拥护和平大会常设委员会斯德哥尔摩会议关于禁止原子武器的呼吁书上签名的运动。
