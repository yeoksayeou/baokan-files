Title: Government Administration Council's Financial and Economic Committee Approves Domestically Produced Canned Goods, Bone Meal, and Salt as Permissible Barter Export Goods
Author:
Date: 1950-05-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. The Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government Administration Council today approved the request of the North China Foreign Trade Administration to add three types of goods, domestically produced canned goods, bone meal, and salt, as permissible barter export goods, and notified the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government to forward the announcement to the bureau for implementation.

### 政务院财委会批准  国产罐头、骨粉、盐为准许易货出口物资

1950-05-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　政务院财委会批准
    国产罐头、骨粉、盐为准许易货出口物资
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会今日批准华北对外贸易管理局的请求，增加国产罐头、骨粉、盐三种物资为准许易货出口物资，并通知中央人民政府贸易部转该局公布执行。
