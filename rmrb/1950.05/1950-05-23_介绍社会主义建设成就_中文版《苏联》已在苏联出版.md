Title: Introducing Achievements of Socialist Construction: Chinese Edition of "Soviet Union" Published in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1950-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 22nd: The first issue of the new Soviet magazine "Soviet Union" monthly has been published, replacing the original magazine "Soviet Union in Construction." The "Soviet Union" monthly is published in Russian, Chinese, English, French, German, and Spanish, and publishes achievements of socialist construction, achievements in Soviet economy, science, technology, culture, and other aspects, as well as major events in the Soviet Union.

### 介绍社会主义建设成就  中文版《苏联》已在苏联出版

1950-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　介绍社会主义建设成就
    中文版《苏联》已在苏联出版
    【新华社二十二日讯】莫斯科航讯：苏联新杂志《苏联》月刊创刊号已出版，代替原来的《在建设中的苏联》杂志。《苏联》月刊以俄文、中文、英文、法文、德文和西班牙文出版，登载社会主义建设的成就，苏联经济、科学、技术、文化等方面的成就，以及苏联的大事。
