Title: Two Soviet Experts in Philosophy and Law, Chesnokov and Askelov, Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-05-29
Page: 1

　　[Xinhua News Agency, 28th] At the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, following the arrival of Nuzhin and others to lecture in China, Chesnokov, Vice President of the Philosophy Institute of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and Editor-in-Chief of the journal "Problems of Philosophy," and Askelov, Head of the Law Department and Professor of Law at Moscow State University, arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 28th. Those present to welcome them included: Professor Ai Siqi, a famous Chinese philosopher; Professor He Sijing, a legal scholar; Professor Zheng Xin, Head of the Department of Philosophy at Peking University; Wang Zhixiang, a member of the Political and Legal Affairs Committee of the Central People's Government; as well as Zhang Zhongshi and Zeng Ping from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Zhu Zisi from the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; and Seleznev, the representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, who also came to welcome them.

### 苏联哲学、法学两专家  车斯诺柯夫阿斯凯洛夫抵京

1950-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联哲学、法学两专家
    车斯诺柯夫阿斯凯洛夫抵京
    【新华社二十八日讯】应中苏友好协会总会之邀，继努日金等来我国讲学之苏联学者苏联科学院哲学院副院长、《哲学问题》杂志总编辑车斯诺柯夫与苏联国立莫斯科大学法律系主任、法学讲座主任阿斯凯洛夫于二十八日上午抵京。到场欢迎者有：我国著名哲学家艾思奇教授、法学家何思敬教授、北京大学哲学系主任郑昕教授、中央人民政府政法委员会委员王之相以及中苏友协总会张仲实、北京市中苏友好协会曾平，对外文化联络局朱子思等，苏联对外文化协会驻中国代表谢列金也前往欢迎。
