Title: Chairman Mao Cables Gottwald to Congratulate Czechoslovakia on the Fifth Anniversary of Liberation; Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Cables Czechoslovak Foreign Minister Siroky to Extend Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1950-05-09
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, May 8th: May 9th marks the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia. Chairman Mao Zedong and Minister Zhou Enlai sent telegrams to President Gottwald of the Czechoslovak Republic and Foreign Minister Siroky of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, respectively, to express their congratulations. The texts of the two telegrams are as follows:
   (1) Chairman Mao's telegram to President Gottwald
   To His Excellency President Gottwald:
    On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia, on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Czechoslovakia, and wish the Czechoslovak Republic growing prosperity and strength under Your Excellency's leadership.
    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
    May 3, 1950, Beijing
   (2) Minister Zhou's telegram to Minister Siroky
   To His Excellency Minister Siroky:
    On the happy occasion of the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia, I sincerely wish the Czechoslovak people happiness and progress.
     Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
     May 3, 1950, Beijing

### 毛主席致电哥特瓦尔德  祝贺捷克解放五周年  周恩来外长电捷外长西罗基致贺

1950-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席致电哥特瓦尔德
    祝贺捷克解放五周年       
    周恩来外长电捷外长西罗基致贺
    【新华社八日讯】五月九日为捷克斯洛伐克解放五周年纪念日，毛泽东主席和周恩来部长分别致电捷克斯洛伐克共和国哥特瓦尔德总统和外交部西罗基部长，表示祝贺。两电电文如下：
   （一）毛主席致哥特瓦尔德总统电哥特瓦尔德总统阁下：
    我代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府及中国人民，值此捷克斯洛伐克解放五周年之际，谨向阁下及捷克斯洛伐克人民致热烈的祝贺，并祝捷克斯洛伐克共和国在阁下领导下日益繁荣壮大。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东
    一九五○年五月三日于北京
   （二）周部长致西罗基部长电西罗基部长阁下：
    欣逢捷克斯洛伐克解放五周年之际，我热诚祝贺捷克斯洛伐克人民的幸福与进步。
     中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来
     一九五○年五月三日于北京（附图片）
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统哥特瓦尔德
