Title: Peace Will Conquer War - Soviet Film Premieres in Beijing Theaters on May 5th
Author:
Date: 1950-05-07
Page: 4

　　Good Movie Recommendation

　　Peace Will Conquer War
    Soviet Film
    Premiering on May 5th at the Red Star, Peace, Central, and Zhonghe Theaters in Beijing Simultaneously.
    This is a documentary depicting the global peace movement of peace-loving people, showcasing the concrete facts of people worldwide striving for peace and the frantic war-mongering of American imperialists.
    The film unfolds a contrasting scene between the Soviet Union and American imperialism. From the film, we can see that peace-loving people all over the world are united around the Soviet Union, striving for world peace. After World War II, the Soviet Union exposed the conspiracies of instigators of new wars and signed treaties of mutual assistance and friendship with many countries. The World Peace Congress and the Women's Congress are all depicted in this film. On the other hand, we can see that the United States is suffering from a severe economic crisis, and they are struggling in their death throes. The Marshall Plan and the Atlantic Pact greedily plunder the fruits of the laboring people of various countries. The film concludes with the World Peace Congress. Finally, representatives of peace appear in the film, and with unparalleled excitement, unanimously passed a letter of salute to Comrade Stalin, the great leader of the peace camp.

### 和平战胜战争  苏联影片  五月五日起在京市红星、平安、中央、中和诸影院同时上演

1950-05-07
第4版()
专栏：好电影介绍

　　和平战胜战争
    苏联影片
    五月五日起在京市红星、平安、中央、中和诸影院同时上演
    这是一部描写全世界爱好和平人民争取和平运动的纪录片，表现了全世界人民争取和平与美国帝国主义者在疯狂制造战争的具体事实。
    影片展开了苏联和美国帝国主义两个国家的对比场面，我们从影片中可以看出全世界爱好和平的人民都团结在苏联周围，为争取世界和平而努力。苏联在二次大战后，揭露了新战争挑拨者的阴谋，并与许多国家签定了互助友好条约。保卫世界和平大会、妇女大会，在这个电影里都一一演出。另一方面，我们可以看出美国闹着严重的经济危机，他们在作垂死的挣扎，马歇尔计划、大西洋公约，贪婪地掠夺了各国劳动人民的果实。影片是以保卫世界和平大会作结束的。最后，影片中出现了和平的代表们，以无比的兴奋心情一致通过了向和平阵营伟大的领袖斯大林同志的致敬书。
