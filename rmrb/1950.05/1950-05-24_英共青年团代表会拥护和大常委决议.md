Title: British Communist Youth League Congress Supports the Resolution of the World Peace Congress and the Standing Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-05-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, May 23rd: The British Communist Youth League Congress closed on the 21st. The delegates discussed the report of Brookes, the National Secretary of the Youth League. All speakers declared that British working youth would not fight the Soviet Union and the People's Democracies for the American and British plutocrats. The congress unanimously adopted a resolution supporting the World Peace Congress and the Standing Committee's resolution on banning atomic weapons. The delegates to the congress pledged to strive to be the best peace fighters. A youth rally was also held after the congress closed.

### 英共青年团代表会拥护和大常委决议

1950-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　英共青年团代表会拥护和大常委决议
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社伦敦讯：英国共产主义青年团代表大会，已于二十一日闭幕。代表们讨论了青年团全国书记布罗克斯的报告。所有发言的人都宣称：英国劳动青年将不替美英富豪们对苏联及人民民主国家作战。大会一致通过拥护和大常委会关于禁用原子武器的决议。大会代表们保证要争取作最优秀的和平战士。大会闭幕之后还举行了青年大会。
