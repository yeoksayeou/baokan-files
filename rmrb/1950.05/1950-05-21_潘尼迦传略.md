Title: A Brief Biography of Panikkar
Author:
Date: 1950-05-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 20th: A Brief Biography of Indian Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, Mr. Panikkar:

　　Ambassador Panikkar was born on June 3, 1895. He studied at Madras University in India and Oxford University in England. Subsequently, he served as a professor at Aligarh University, editor of the Delhi "Hindustan Times," and worked for many Indian princely states for a long time. In July 1944, he became the Prime Minister of Bikaner State. From 1947 to 1948, he served as a member of the Indian Constituent Assembly and a Member of Parliament. In 1947, he was a member of the Indian delegation to the United Nations General Assembly. From 1948 to 1949, he was the Indian Ambassador to the Nationalist Government.

### 潘尼迦传略

1950-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　潘尼迦传略
    【新华社二十日讯】印度驻中华人民共和国大使潘尼迦传略：
    潘尼迦大使生于一八九五年六月三日，曾就读于印度的马德拉斯大学及英国的牛津大学。其后，他曾任亚立加尔大学教授、德里《印度斯坦时报》编辑，曾长期供职于许多印度土邦。一九四四年七月他任毕肯奈土邦的首相。一九四七年至一九四八年出任印度制宪大会议员及国会议员。一九四七年他任印度出席联合国大会代表团团员。一九四八年至一九四九年，他是印度驻国民党政府大使。
