Title: Sujiatun Abrasive Wheel Factory Implements Production Responsibility System, Exceeds Task and Reduces Costs in April
Author: Shan Lire
Date: 1950-05-25
Page: 2

　　Sujiatun Abrasive Wheel Factory Implements Production Responsibility System
    Exceeds Task and Reduces Costs in April
    [Our Newspaper, Shenyang] After the Shenyang Sujiatun Abrasive Wheel Factory universally established the production responsibility system, production exceeded the quota in April, and quality was improved, rejects were reduced, and costs were lowered. The pressing group of the factory first established the production responsibility system, then promoted it to various teams, and launched a comprehensive competition campaign. As a result of the continuous improvement of tools and operating methods by the factory's pressing team, rejects were reduced by 0.31 percent, and working time was shortened by 31 percent. The number of rejects in the rough grinding team has been reduced from an average of 4.5 pieces per day in March to the current 1.2 pieces per day. The product quality of the mixing team has increased by 80 percent compared to March. Since April, the cost of each team has been generally reduced. The cost price per kilogram of six-inch oilstones produced by the pressing team was 69,829 yuan (Northeast currency) in the middle ten days of the month, but was reduced to 63,940 yuan in the last ten days of the month. Compared with the first ten days of the month, the semi-finished abrasive wheel products produced by the mixing team were reduced by 5,000 yuan per kilogram in the last ten days of the month.
                                （Shan Lire）

### 苏家屯砂轮工厂贯彻生产责任制  四月份超过任务降低成本
单力人
1950-05-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏家屯砂轮工厂贯彻生产责任制
    四月份超过任务降低成本
    【本报沈阳讯】沈阳苏家屯砂轮工厂普遍建立生产负责制后，四月份生产超额完成，并且作到质量提高、废品减少、成本降低。该厂压榨组首先建立了生产负责制，接着推广到各班组，并展开了全面的竞赛运动。该厂压榨班连续改进工具与操作方法的结果，使废品减低了百分之○·三一，缩短了百分之三十一的工作时间。粗磨组废品已由三月份平均每天产生四·五块，减少到现在每天产生一·二块。搅拌组的产品质量比三月份提高百分之八十。各班组成本从四月份起，已普遍降低。压榨班生产的六寸油石，中旬每公斤成本价格为六九、八二九元（东北币），下旬则降低为六三、九四○元。揽拌组生产的砂轮半成品，上旬与下旬比较，每公斤降低了五千元。
                                （单力人）
