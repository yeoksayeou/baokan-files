Title: Soviet Youth Delegation Passes Through Jinan on Return to Beijing; Warmly Welcomed During Visits to Shanghai and Nanjing; Another Group of Ten Delegation Members Return to Hankou from Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1950-05-17
Page: 1

　　The Soviet Youth Delegation Passes Through Jinan on Return to Beijing
　　Warmly Welcomed During Visits to Shanghai and Nanjing
　　Another Group of Ten Delegation Members Return to Hankou from Guangzhou
    [Report from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The Soviet Youth Delegation left Shanghai on the 14th and arrived in Nanjing. After a one-day visit in Nanjing, they left Nanjing at 11 PM on the same day to return to Beijing. On the 15th at 12 PM, they arrived in Jinan, where more than 1,200 people from all walks of life and youth representatives went to the station to welcome them. During their short stay in Nanjing, the delegation received a warm welcome from the youth and people of Nanjing. More than 50,000 young commanders and fighters from the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, along with more than 3,000 youth representatives from all walks of life in Nanjing, held grand welcoming rallies on the afternoon of the 14th. The head of the delegation, Mikhailov, introduced the achievements and happy life of Soviet youth in socialist construction to the youth of Nanjing.
    Wei Laiguo, a first-class People's Hero of East China, and Li Landing, a model female medical worker, presented a banner to the delegation on behalf of all the young commanders and fighters of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, and presented them with medals commemorating the Huaihai Campaign and the victory of the Crossing the River Campaign.
    During their five-day stay in Shanghai, the Soviet Youth Delegation attended 14 lecture meetings and 14 symposiums of Shanghai youth from all walks of life. The Soviet Youth Art Troupe performed a total of six art programs, with a total audience of more than 100,000 people.
    The youth of East China and Shanghai presented various gifts and souvenirs to the delegation. Ten combat heroes from the Shanghai Garrison of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army presented the Soviet youth representatives with medals commemorating the Crossing the River Campaign.
    [Another report] A team of ten members of the Soviet Youth Delegation, led by Pesliank, a member of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union and Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, arrived in Guangzhou from Hankou on the afternoon of the 12th. More than 5,000 youth representatives from all sectors, including leading cadres and youth representatives from factories, schools, troops, and organizations, went to the station to welcome them.
    On the morning of the 13th, more than 5,000 young people from all walks of life held a grand welcoming meeting. Soviet Youth Delegation representative Masherov delivered a speech amidst warm applause. He first expressed the warm concern and respect of Soviet youth for Chinese youth and introduced the great achievements of the Soviet people in healing the wounds of war and restoring and developing production after the war. The South China Working Committee of the Communist Youth League and the Guangzhou Municipal Working Committee presented the delegation with an ivory ball as a souvenir, and youth from all walks of life also presented the delegation with banners and flowers. In the afternoon, members of the Soviet Youth Delegation were invited to visit and give speeches at South China University, the Guangdong Branch of the Military and Political University, Sun Yat-sen University, the South China League School, and the Waterworks, where they were warmly welcomed by students and workers.
    The delegation left Guangzhou by train and returned north on the morning of the 15th. More than 6,000 representatives from all walks of life and youth representatives went to the station to see them off.

### 苏青年代表团过济返京　在沪宁访问受到热烈欢迎　另代表团员十人由广州返汉口

1950-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏青年代表团过济返京
　　在沪宁访问受到热烈欢迎   
    另代表团员十人由广州返汉口
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：苏联青年代表团于十四日离沪抵宁，在宁访问一日，已于当日晚十一时离宁返京，十五日晚十二时抵达济南，各界及青年代表一千二百余人赴车站欢迎。该团在留宁短短的时间内，受到了南京各界青年及人民的热烈欢迎。华东军区暨第三野战军五万多青年指战员，与南京市各界青年代表三千余人于十四日下午分别举行了盛大的欢迎会。代表团团长米哈依洛夫向南京青年介绍苏联青年在社会主义建设中的成就和幸福的生活。
    华东一级人民英雄魏来国、模范女医务工作者李蓝丁代表华东军区和第三野战军全体青年指战员，向代表团献旗，并赠送淮海战役与渡江胜利纪念章。
    苏联青年代表团此次留沪五天中，曾先后出席上海各界青年的十四次讲演会和十四次座谈会；苏联青年文艺工作团共演出艺术节目六次，观众共计十余万人。
    华东及上海各界青年曾向代表团赠献各种礼物和纪念品，人民解放军第三野战军驻沪部队的十个战斗英雄以渡江纪念章赠给苏联青年代表们。
    【又讯】由苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员、苏联青年反法西斯委员会副主席裴斯良克领队的苏联青年代表团一队十人，于十二日下午由汉口抵达广州。前往车站欢迎的有各界首长及各工厂、学校、驻军、机关团体等青年代表五千余人。
    十三日上午各界青年五千余人举行盛大欢迎会。苏联青年代表团代表马舍洛夫在热烈的掌声中发表演说。他首先表示苏联青年对中国青年的热烈关怀和敬意，并介绍苏联人民在战后医治战争创伤恢复发展生产的伟大成绩。青年团华南工委会及广州市工委会向代表团赠送象牙球一具作为纪念，各界青年亦向代表团献旗、献花。下午，苏联青年代表团的团员们，分别应邀到达南方大学、军政大学广东分校、中山大学、华南团校及自来水厂等处参观及作演讲，受到学生、工人们的热烈欢迎。
    代表团于十五日晨乘车离广州北返。各界代表和青年代表共六千余人前往车站欢送。
