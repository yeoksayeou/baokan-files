Title: Tianjin's Industrial and Commercial Tax Collection for the Second Half of Last Year Basically Completed
Author:
Date: 1950-05-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 13th: Tianjin's industrial and commercial tax collection for the second half of 1949 has been basically completed. As of the 10th, the number of taxpayers who have paid into the treasury has reached 36,254, accounting for 90 percent of the total number of taxable households; the tax revenue paid into the treasury is over 83 billion yuan, equivalent to over 71 million jin of millet. During the period of tax payment, although it coincided with a time of temporary difficulty due to sluggish sales of goods for general industrial and commercial households, the majority of manufacturers were able to demonstrate patriotic enthusiasm and pay their taxes on time. The remaining factories and households are continuing to pay.

### 津去年下半年工商业税　征收工作基本完成

1950-05-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　津去年下半年工商业税
　　征收工作基本完成
    【新华社天津十三日电】天津市一九四九年下半年工商业税征收工作基本完成。截至十日止纳库户数共达三万六千二百五十四户，占应纳税总户数百分之九十；纳库税款八百三十余亿元，折合小米七千一百余万斤。在纳库缴款期间，虽值一般工商业户货物滞销暂时困难之际，但大部厂商均能发挥爱国热忱，按期缴清税款。其余厂户正在继续缴纳中。
