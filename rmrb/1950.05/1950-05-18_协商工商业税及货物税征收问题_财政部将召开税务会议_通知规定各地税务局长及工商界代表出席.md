Title: Negotiating Industrial and Commercial Tax and Goods Tax Collection Issues: Ministry of Finance to Convene Tax Conference; Notice Stipulates Attendance of Local Tax Bureau Directors and Representatives from the Industrial and Commercial Sectors

Author:
Date: 1950-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 17th: To improve national taxation work, make the burden more fair and reasonable, and the procedures simple and easy to implement, so as to achieve the goal of taking into account both public and private interests and jointly developing production, the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government recently issued a notice deciding to convene a tax conference on May 24th with local tax bureau directors and representatives from the industrial and commercial sectors to negotiate issues related to the collection of industrial and commercial tax and goods tax. The notice stipulates that the attendees will be: 1. Directors of tax administration bureaus in major administrative regions. 2. Directors of tax bureaus in major cities such as Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Qingdao, Shenyang, Harbin, Chongqing, Xi'an, and Lüda (Dalian). 3. Representatives from the industrial and commercial sectors in major cities (three each from Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou, and Shenyang; one or two each from Chongqing, Qingdao, Harbin, Xi'an, and Lüda). 4. Three or four people selected by major administrative regions from directors of provincial tax bureaus, directors of tax bureaus in small and medium-sized cities, and representatives from the industrial and commercial sectors. All of the above representatives must arrive in Beijing on May 24th to attend the meeting.

The notice requires major cities to collect in advance opinions and materials on various aspects such as tax laws, tax items, tax rates, collection methods and procedures, tax revenue tasks and the ability of the industrial and commercial sectors to bear the burden, tax measures, and the work style of cadres, and to sort and organize them by category to bring to Beijing.

### 协商工商业税及货物税征收问题　财政部将召开税务会议　通知规定各地税务局长及工商界代表出席

1950-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　协商工商业税及货物税征收问题
　　财政部将召开税务会议
　　通知规定各地税务局长及工商界代表出席
    【新华社十七日讯】为改善全国税收工作，使负担更加公平合理，手续简便易行，以便达到公私兼顾、共同发展生产的目的，中央人民政府财政部顷发出通知，决定于五月二十四日召集各地税务局长及工商界代表举行税务会议，协商工商业税及货物税的征收问题。通知规定与会人员为：一、各大行政区税务管理局局长。二、京、津、沪、汉、穗、青、沈、哈、渝、西安、旅大等城市税务局局长。三、各大城市工商界代表（京、津、沪、汉、穗、沈各三人，渝、青、哈、西安、旅大各一人或二人）。四、各大区在省税务局局长、中小城市税务局局长及工商界代表中选派三人或四人。以上与会代表均须于五月二十四日到京出席会议。
    通知要求各大中城市预先收集有关对税法、税目、税率，征收方法与手续，税收任务及工商界负担能力，征税措施及干部作风等各方面的意见与材料，并分类整理带京。
