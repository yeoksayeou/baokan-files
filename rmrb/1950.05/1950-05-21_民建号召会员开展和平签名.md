Title: China Democratic National Construction Association Calls on Members to Launch Peace Signature Campaign
Author: 
Date: 1950-05-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce (China Democratic National Construction Association) has issued instructions to enthusiastically respond to the call of the Chinese Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace Standing Committee and the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, mobilizing members across the country to actively launch a peace signature campaign. The instructions require that where local members are divided into groups, each group should compete with each other. The instructions also require local branches to send the signature books to the local receiving institutions, and report the number of signatures to the General Association. In places where no receiving institution is established, the signature books should be sent directly to the General Association for forwarding before June 15.

### 民建号召会员开展和平签名

1950-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　民建号召会员开展和平签名
    【本报讯】据新华社十七日讯：民主建国会总会为热烈响应世界拥护和平大会常设委员会中国保卫和平大会委员会的号召，特发出指示，发动全国各地会员积极展开和平签名运动。指示要求各地会员分编有小组的，各小组应彼此竞赛。指示并要求各地分会将签名书送达当地的收件机构，另将签名人数报告总会，未设收件机构的地方，应于六月十五日以前，将签名书迳寄总会汇转。
