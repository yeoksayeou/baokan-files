Title: China Association for Promoting Democracy Holds National Congress; Accepts the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference as its Program
Author:
Date: 1950-05-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The China Association for Promoting Democracy (CAPD) held its first National Congress after liberation in Beijing on April 15th. More than 40 representatives from Shanghai, Guangzhou, Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang and representatives of the association participating in the CPPCC attended. The meeting lasted eleven days and victoriously concluded on April 25th.

The meeting adopted a political report, a report on association affairs, and a declaration, formulated its charter, accepted the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference as the program of the association, and unanimously and solemnly resolved to do everything possible to unite with all democratic parties and people of the country under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and to strive for the complete realization of the Common Program. The congress adopted important resolutions such as "On Accepting the Leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, Learning from the Spirit of the 'CPC's Strengthening of Unity and Cooperation with Non-Party Masses and Democratic Personages' to Consolidate and Expand the People's Democratic United Front," "On Supporting the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, Consolidating the Permanent Unity and Cooperation of the Three Great Nationalities of China and the Soviet Union," and "On Defending World Peace, Consolidating and Expanding the Forces of the Struggle for Peace." The congress also unanimously passed messages of respect to Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government, Commander-in-Chief Zhu of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the People's Liberation Army on the Hainan Front.

During the congress, all the representatives attended a reception held by the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and listened to a speech by Premier Zhou Enlai. In addition to providing a detailed analysis of the current domestic and international situation, Premier Zhou offered sincere advice on the future direction of the association's efforts. The congress invited Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Deputy Premier Chen Yun, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC Peng Zhen, Deputy Minister of Education Qian Junrui, and Deputy Director of the Press Administration Fan Changjiang to give special lectures on the Common Program of the CPPCC, financial and economic work, the transformation of intellectuals, and Sino-Soviet friendly relations. At the opening of the congress, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong of the National Committee of the CPPCC and Vice Minister Xu Bing of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee attended and spoke.

During the congress, all the participating representatives held several group discussions and launched heated discussions based on the political report, the report on association affairs, and various motions proposed by the Central Council, as well as the special lectures. The representatives all used the weapon of criticism and self-criticism to conduct earnest reviews, not only gaining a correct assessment of the achievements and shortcomings of past work, but also gaining a clear understanding of the association's tasks and specific work steps in the construction of the new China as one of the constituent elements of the people's democratic united front, thereby fully demonstrating the spirit of unity and achieving an unprecedented level of ideological consistency.

At the end of the congress, Ma Xulun, Wang Shao'ao, Xu Guangping, Zhou Jianren, Xie Renbing, Chen Sisheng, Xu Boxin, Zhao Puchu, Mei Dajun, Lin Handa, Lei Jieqiong, Ge Zhicheng, Yan Jingyao, Chen Qiuan, Feng Shaoshan, Dong Qiusi, Feng Binfu, Ke Ling, Meng Qiujiang, Li Pingxin, Cao Hongzhu, Liang Chunfu, Zhou Xuliang, Jin Zhixuan, and Mi Yiqun were elected as members of the Central Council, and Zhang Jiyuan, Yu Zhijie, Chen Ludi, Wen Chongshi, Wang Xingsheng, Zhu Jingqing, Wu Qiyao, and Xie Jiayin were elected as alternate members of the Central Council.

After the congress, the Third Central Council held its first plenary session on the 26th, and mutually elected Ma Xulun as Chairman, Wang Shao'ao as Vice Chairman, Xu Guangping as Secretary-General, and Zhou Jianren, Lin Handa, Xu Boxin, and Ge Zhicheng as Standing Directors.

### 民主促进会举行全国代表大会  接受人民政协共同纲领为该会纲领

1950-05-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　民主促进会举行全国代表大会
    接受人民政协共同纲领为该会纲领
    【新华社二日讯】中国民主促进会于四月十五日在北京举行解放后第一届全国代表大会，出席上海、广州、北京、天津、沈阳各地代表及该会参加人民政协代表共四十余人。会议共举行了十一天，至四月二十五日宣告胜利闭幕。
    此次会议通过了政治报告，会务报告和宣言，制定了章程，接受了中国人民政协共同纲领为该会纲领，并一致郑重决议，将竭尽全力在中国共产党领导之下与各民主党派和全国人民团结一致，为彻底实现共同纲领而奋斗。大会通过了《关于接受中国共产党的领导，学习“中共加强与非党群众及民主人士团结合作”的精神以巩固扩大人民民主统一战线》、《关于拥护中苏友好同盟互助条约，巩固中苏三大民族永久团结合作》、《关于保卫世界和平，巩固和扩大和平斗争力量》等项重要决议。大会并一致通过向中央人民政府毛主席，中国人民解放军朱总司令，海南前线人民解放军的致敬电。
    大会期间，全体代表参加了中共中央统战部举行的招待会，听了周恩来总理的讲话。周总理除对当前国内外形势作了详尽的分析外，并对该会今后的努力方向，提供了恳切的意见。大会曾邀请朱总司令、陈云副总理、中共北京市委员会书记彭真、教育部钱俊瑞副部长、新闻总署范长江副署长等分别就人民政协共同纲领、财经工作、知识分子的改造及中苏友好关系等问题作了专题讲演。大会开幕时，人民政协全国委员会陈叔通副主席，中共中央统战部徐冰副部长均出席讲话。
    全体与会代表在大会期间曾举行多次分组讨论，根据中央理事会所提出的政治报告、会务报告和各项议案以及专题演讲，展开了热烈的讨论。代表们都运用批评与自我批评的武器，认真地进行检讨，不但对过去工作的成就与缺点有了正确的估计，并且对于该会作为人民民主统一战线的组成分子之一，在新中国建设中的任务和具体工作步骤，有了明确的认识，从而充分地发挥了团结的精神，并达到了思想上空前的一致。
    大会最后选出了马叙伦、王绍鏊、许广平、周建人、谢仁冰、陈巳生、徐伯昕、赵朴初、梅达君、林汉达、雷洁琼、葛志成、严景耀、陈秋安、冯少山、董秋斯、冯宾符、柯灵、孟秋江、李平心、曹鸿翥、梁纯夫、周煦良、金芝轩、宓逸群等二十五人为中央理事、张纪元、余之介、陈芦荻、温崇实、王幸生、朱镜清、吴企尧、谢加因等七人为候补中央理事。
    大会结束后，第三届中央理事会于二十六日召开了第一次全体会议，互推马叙伦为主席，王绍鏊为副主席，许广平为秘书长，周建人、林汉达、徐伯昕、葛志成为常务理事。
