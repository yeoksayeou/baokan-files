Title: Soviet Youth Delegation Arrives in Shanghai; Over Two Thousand Representatives from All Walks of Life Welcome Them at the Train Station
Author:
Date: 1950-05-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 10th. The Soviet Youth Delegation, led by Mikhailov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, and the Soviet Youth Art Troupe, a group of thirty-six people, accompanied by Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China, arrived in Shanghai at 2:00 PM on the 9th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Li Chang, Secretary of the East China Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, Deputy Secretary Liu Daosheng, Zhang Ben, Secretary of the Shanghai Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, Deputy Secretary Lu Ke, Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, Chen Yi, Mayor of Shanghai, Liu Xiao, Second Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chen Wangdao, Vice President of the Shanghai Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and representatives from all walks of life, including workers, youth, and women, totaling over two thousand five hundred people. Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, Vladimirov, Soviet Trade Representative in Shanghai, Obraztsov, Chairman of the Soviet Compatriot Association, Tsibunovsky, and Head of the Shanghai Branch of TASS, Yeshamin, also went to welcome them. As Mikhailov and others alighted from the train, the band played the "Anthem of the World Federation of Democratic Youth," and forty children from the Shanghai Railway Bureau Employees' Children's School presented flowers to the delegation. Subsequently, the welcoming crowd held a brief welcoming meeting at the North Railway Station Plaza. Zhang Ben, Secretary of the Shanghai Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, delivered a welcoming speech, and Soviet youth representatives Richia and Jikina delivered speeches in response.

　　The Soviet Youth Delegation was warmly welcomed by youth groups and people from all walks of life in Nanjing on the morning of the 9th as they passed through the city.

### 苏联青年代表团抵沪　各界代表二千余人赴车站欢迎

1950-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联青年代表团抵沪
    各界代表二千余人赴车站欢迎
    【新华社上海十日电】以苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记米哈依洛夫为首的苏联青年代表团和苏联青年文艺工作团一行三十六人，由中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬陪同，于九日下午二时抵沪。前往车站欢迎的有新民主主义青年团华东工作委员会书记李昌、副书记刘导生，新民主主义青年团上海工作委员会书记张本、副书记鲁克，华东军政委员会主席饶漱石、上海市长陈毅、中共上海市委第二书记刘晓，中苏友好协会上海分会副会长陈望道和工人、青年、妇女等各界代表共二千五百余人。苏联驻沪总领事符拉基米洛夫、苏联驻沪商务代表奥不拉磋夫、苏联侨民协会主席齐布诺夫斯基、塔斯社上海分社社长叶夏明等亦前往欢迎。当米哈依洛夫等下车时，乐队齐奏世界民主青年进行曲，并有上海铁路局员工子弟学校少年儿童队四十人向代表团献花。接着，欢迎群众在北站广场举行简短的欢迎会。新民主主义青年团上海工作委员会书记张本致欢迎词，苏联青年代表里奇亚、吉金娜等致答词。
    苏联青年代表团一行于九日上午路经南京时，曾受到该市青年团体及各界人士热烈欢迎。
