Title: All-China Women's Federation and Beijing Women's Federation Hold Peace Signature Rally
Author:
Date: 1950-05-21
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation and its member organizations, as well as directly affiliated schools and institutions, held a signature rally in support of world peace on the 20th. Deng Yingchao and Li Dequan, vice-chairpersons of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, standing committee members Kang Keqing and Cao Mengjun, Liu Qingyang, vice-chairperson of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, representatives from various women's organizations, cadres, and female students, totaling more than 600 people, attended. At the rally, Li Dequan, vice-chairperson of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, first reported on the significance of defending world peace. She pointed out: The forces defending peace in all countries around the world are growing stronger day by day, and the peace camp is unprecedentedly powerful. Women have contributed tremendous strength to the movement to defend world peace. In order to win lasting world peace, women should strengthen their unity, stand in the peace camp headed by the Soviet Union, and persist in the struggle against imperialist warmongers. Liu Qingyang of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation then spoke. She called on every woman who loves peace and democracy to sign the declaration of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace. Beijing women should inherit the fine revolutionary tradition of defending peace in the past and take the lead in this signature campaign. After the speeches, the signing began. Everyone signed their names on the declaration made of red satin with excitement, expressing their hatred for imperialist warmongers and their infinite determination to defend world peace.

### 全国妇联及京市妇联召开和平签名大会

1950-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国妇联及京市妇联召开和平签名大会
    【新华社二十日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会与北京市民主妇联及所属会员团体及直属学校机关，于二十日召开拥护世界和平签名大会。到全国民主妇联副主席邓颖超、李德全，常委康克清、曹孟君，北京市民主妇联副主席刘清扬，各妇女团体代表、干部及女学生等六百余人。会上首由全国民主妇联李德全副主席报告保卫世界和平的意义。她指出：全世界各国保卫和平的力量正日益增涨，和平阵营空前强大。妇女在保卫世界和平运动中曾贡献了巨大的力量。为了赢得世界的持久和平，妇女应加强团结，站在以苏联为首的和平阵营中，坚持对帝国主义战争贩子作斗争。继由北京市民主妇联刘清扬讲话。她号召每一个爱好和平民主的妇女，都应在世界拥护和平大会常设委员会宣言上签名。北京市妇女应继承过去保卫和平的优良革命传统，在这次签名运动中起带头作用。讲话后，即开始签名，大家都带着兴奋的心情，在红缎制成的宣言上签下了自己的名字，表示了对帝国主义战争贩子的憎恨，对保卫世界和平的无限的决心。
