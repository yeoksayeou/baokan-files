Title: Zhoushan Islands Military Control Commission Established; Dinghai People's Government Begins Office Work
Author:
Date: 1950-05-24
Page: 1
    The Zhoushan Islands Military Control Commission Established
    Dinghai People's Government Begins Office Work
    [Xinhua News Agency, Dinghai, 22nd] The Zhoushan Islands Military Control Commission and the Zhoushan Islands Garrison Headquarters were established today upon the order of the Zhejiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 23rd] The Dinghai County People's Government was established on the 22nd upon the order of the Zhejiang Provincial People's Government and has begun office work.

### 舟山群岛军管会成立   定海人民政府开始办公

1950-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　舟山群岛军管会成立
    定海人民政府开始办公
    【新华社定海二十二日电】舟山群岛军事管制委员会及舟山群岛警备司令部奉中国人民解放军浙江军区命令于今日成立。
    【新华社杭州二十三日电】定海县人民政府已奉浙江省人民政府命令，于二十二日成立，并开始办公。
