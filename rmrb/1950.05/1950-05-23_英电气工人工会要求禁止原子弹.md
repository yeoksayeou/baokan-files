Title: British Electrical Workers' Union Demands Ban on Atomic Bombs
Author:
Date: 1950-05-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, London, May 21st. TASS from London on May 21st: The annual meeting of the British Electrical Workers' Union passed a resolution by 325 votes to 30 votes, demanding that the British government work to ban atomic bombs, destroy existing stockpiles, and establish an international inspection system for atomic energy plants. The resolution emphasized that this step is necessary to lay the foundation for world peace.
    The annual meeting also passed the following resolutions: demanding a reduction in military spending, calling for support for a collective policy of jointly opposing aggression with other countries; urging the government to increase trade with the Soviet Union, China, and the People's Democratic countries; and demanding that British troops be withdrawn from Malaya for world peace.

### 英电气工人工会要求禁止原子弹

1950-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　英电气工人工会要求禁止原子弹
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十一日讯：英国电气工人工会的年会，以三二五票对三○票通过一项决议，要求英国政府努力禁止原子弹，销毁现有的存货，并且对原子能的工厂确立国际检查制度。决议着重指出：这一步骤是奠定世界和平所必需的。
    年会并通过了下列几项决议：要求削减军费，呼吁支持一种与其他国家共同反对侵略的集体政策；敦促政府增加与苏联、中国及人民民主国家的贸易；要求为了世界和平，将英军撤出马来亚。
