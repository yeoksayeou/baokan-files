Title: Turning Britain into an Air Base: US to Build New Airport in Oxford
Author:
Date: 1950-05-30
Page: 4

　　Turning Britain into an Air Base
    US to Build New Airport in Oxford
    [Xinhua News Agency, New York, 29th] According to a TASS dispatch from New York, a Baltimore Sun reporter, Nolton, citing reliable sources in London, stated that the U.S. Air Force will begin constructing three permanent bomber bases near Oxford within the next three weeks. Because British officials fear anti-war demonstrations when U.S. engineering teams arrive, the locations of the bases will be kept secret. The U.S. engineering teams are en route to Britain.
    In addition to these three bases under intense construction, the U.S. is also considering building a fourth base in the Oxford area. U.S. bomber squadrons currently occupy three airfields in Lancashire, England.

### 变英国为空军基地  美将在牛津建筑新机场

1950-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　变英国为空军基地
    美将在牛津建筑新机场
    【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社纽约讯，巴尔的摩太阳报记者诺尔顿自伦敦引可靠消息称：美国空军在今后三星期内，将在牛津附近开始建筑三个永久的轰炸机基地。由于英国官方害怕美国工程队到来时发生反战示威游行，基地的位置将被保守秘密。美国的工程队正在赴英途中。
    除了这三个克日建筑的基地外，美国还正在考虑在牛津区建设第四个基地。美国轰炸机队刻在英国已占有兰开夏的三个飞机场。
