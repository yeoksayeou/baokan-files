Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Establish Tibetan Language Broadcast Program
Author:
Date: 1950-05-13
Page: 1

　　Central People's Broadcasting Station
　　Will Establish Tibetan Language Broadcast Program
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will add a Tibetan language broadcast program starting May 22nd, tentatively three times a week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday). Wavelength: 428.57 meters, 700 kilocycles; 29.24 meters, 10260 kilocycles; 19.92 meters, 15060 kilocycles. Broadcast time: 23:30 to 24:00 Beijing time.

### 中央人民广播电台　将设藏语广播节目

1950-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台
　　将设藏语广播节目
    【新华社十二日讯】中央人民广播电台从五月二十二日起增设西藏语广播节目，暂定每周三次（星期一、三、五）。波长：四二八·五七公尺，七○○千周；二九·二四公尺，一○二六○千周；一九·九二公尺，一五○六○千周。播音时间：北京时间二十三点三十分至二十四点。
