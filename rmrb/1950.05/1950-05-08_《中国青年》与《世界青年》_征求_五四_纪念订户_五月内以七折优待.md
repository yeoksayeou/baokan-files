Title: "China Youth" and "World Youth" Seeking "May Fourth" Commemorative Subscribers, 30% Discount in May

Author:

Date: 1950-05-08
Page: 3

《China Youth》 and 《World Youth》
    Seeking "May Fourth" Commemorative Subscribers
     30% Discount in May
    [Our News] To celebrate the "May Day" Labor Day, "May Fourth" Youth Day, and the anniversary of the founding of the Youth League, the China Youth Publishing House is launching a "May Fourth" commemorative subscription drive to expand subscriptions to 《China Youth》 and 《World Youth》. All subscriptions to both magazines in May will receive a 25% discount (30 copies or more, 30% discount). The publishing house's retail department will also offer discounts to readers from May 2nd to 7th.
    [Our News] The fourth lecture in the popular science lecture series co-organized by the Youth Service Department and the Science Popularization Bureau of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Culture is scheduled for 3:00 PM on Saturday, May 6th, at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association (19 Nanheyan Street). Experts from the Meteorological Bureau will speak on: "The Climate of North China and Life." All sectors are welcome to attend. Admission tickets can be picked up at the Youth Service Department from 1:30 PM to 5:00 PM and 6:00 PM to 8:30 PM on Friday, May 5th. Groups can pick up tickets with a letter of introduction.

### 《中国青年》与《世界青年》  征求“五四”纪念订户  五月内以七折优待

1950-05-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　《中国青年》与《世界青年》
    征求“五四”纪念订户
     五月内以七折优待
    【本报讯】为庆祝“五一”劳动节、“五四”青年节及青年团成立周年，青年出版社特举办“五四”纪念订户，扩大征求订阅《中国青年》及《世界青年》。凡于五月内订阅两种杂志的，一律以七五折优待（三十份以上七折）。该社门市部并于五月二日至七日减价优待读者。
    【本报讯】青年服务部及中央人民政府文化部科学普及局合办的大众科学讲座第四讲，定于五月六日（星期六）下午三时在苏联对外文化协会（南河沿十九号）举行，请气象局的专家主讲：《华北的气候与人生》。欢迎各界听讲。入场券于五月五日（星期五）下午一时半至五时，六时至八时半到青年服务部领取，团体票凭介绍信领取。
