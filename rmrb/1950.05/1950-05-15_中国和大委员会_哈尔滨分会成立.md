Title: Establishment of the Harbin Branch of the China and the World Peace Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-05-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 13th — The Harbin Branch of the China Committee for the Defence of World Peace was formally established on the 12th. The inaugural meeting elected Feng Zhongyun (Chairman of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government) as Chairman of the Standing Committee of the branch, and resolved to widely launch a peace signature campaign demanding the prohibition of atomic weapons and war.

### 中国和大委员会　哈尔滨分会成立

1950-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国和大委员会
　　哈尔滨分会成立
    【新华社哈尔滨十三日电】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会哈尔滨分会于十二日正式成立。成立大会选出冯仲云（松江省人民政府主席）为分会常委会主席，并决议广泛展开要求禁止原子武器战争的和平签名运动。
