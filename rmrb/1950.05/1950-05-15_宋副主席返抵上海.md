Title: Vice Chairman Soong Returns to Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1950-05-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 14th—Vice Chairman Soong Ching-ling of the Central People's Government, who went to Beijing a month ago to attend the seventh meeting of the Central People's Government Committee and the China People's Relief Representative Conference, has completed her official duties and returned to Shanghai on the morning of the 12th. Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, Chen Yi, Mayor of Shanghai, and Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor, were among those who went to the station to welcome her.

### 宋副主席返抵上海

1950-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　宋副主席返抵上海
    【新华社上海十四日电】中央人民政府宋庆龄副主席，于月前赴北京参加中央人民政府委员会第七次会议及中国人民救济代表会议，业已公毕，于十二日晨返沪。前往车站欢迎的有华东军政委员会饶漱石主席、上海市陈毅市长及潘汉年副市长等。
