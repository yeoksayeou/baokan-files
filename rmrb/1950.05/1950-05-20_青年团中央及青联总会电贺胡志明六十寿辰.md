Title: Youth League Central Committee and All-China Youth Federation Send Congratulatory Message on Ho Chi Minh's Sixtieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1950-05-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth sent a telegram to President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram reads as follows:

Dear Comrade Ho Chi Minh:

On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, we, on behalf of all members of the China New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, extend our sincere and warm congratulations to you.

Under your leadership, the Vietnamese people and youth have achieved unprecedented victories in the struggle against enslavement and aggression by the imperialists. The Chinese people and youth are following the victorious struggle of their friends, the Vietnamese people and youth, and the upcoming liberation of all the Vietnamese people, with great joy.

For the happiness of all the Vietnamese people, for the friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese people, and for the cause of world peace, we wish you good health and longevity.

                            Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
                            All-China Federation of Democratic Youth
                                                  May 16th

### 青年团中央及青联总会电贺胡志明六十寿辰

1950-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央及青联总会电贺胡志明六十寿辰
    【新华社十九日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合总会致电越南民主共和国胡志明主席，祝贺他六十寿辰。电文如下：亲爱的胡志明同志：
    当您六十寿辰之际，谨代表中国新民主主义青年团以及中华全国民主青年联合总会的全体团员及会员，向您致衷心的、热烈的祝贺。
    在您的领导下，越南人民和青年在反对帝国主义者奴役侵略的斗争中，获得了空前的胜利。中国人民和青年正以极大的欢欣关注着他们的朋友——越南人民和青年的胜利斗争，以及即将实现的全越南人民的解放。
    为了全体越南人民的幸福，为了中越两国人民的友谊和世界和平的事业，我们谨祝您健康长寿。
                            中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
                            中华全国民主青年联合总会
                                                  五月十六日
