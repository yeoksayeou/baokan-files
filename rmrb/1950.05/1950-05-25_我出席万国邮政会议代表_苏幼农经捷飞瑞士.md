Title: My Representative to the Universal Postal Congress, Su Younong, Flies to Switzerland via Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1950-05-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 24th: My plenipotentiary representative to the Executive and Liaison Committee of the Universal Postal Union, Su Younong, advisor Ge Baoquan, and attendant Xu Fuxian, arrived in Prague from Moscow on the 23rd. After a short rest at the airport, they transferred to a flight to Switzerland to attend the meeting. Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Segnerova, and Deputy Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, Leimose, were among those who personally welcomed them at the airport.

### 我出席万国邮政会议代表  苏幼农经捷飞瑞士

1950-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　我出席万国邮政会议代表
    苏幼农经捷飞瑞士
    【新华社布拉格二十四日电】我出席万国邮政联盟执行及联络委员会的全权代表苏幼农、顾问戈宝权及随员徐傅贤一行三人，已于二十三日由莫斯科飞抵布拉格，在机场稍事休息后，即换机飞往瑞士赴会。捷克斯洛伐克外交部副部长赛格妮诺娃，邮政部副部长莱伊莫赛等均曾亲往机场欢迎。
