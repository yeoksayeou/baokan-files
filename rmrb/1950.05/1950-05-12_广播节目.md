Author:
Date: 1950-05-12

　　Broadcast Programs
    Central People's Broadcasting Station, 19:00 today, Social Science Lecture: Yu Guangyuan speaks on "Currency" (Part 2). 20:00 People's Forum: (1) President of the Supreme People's Court, Shen Junru, speaks on "Guaranteeing the Implementation of Our Marriage Law"; (2) Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, Zhang Zhizhirang, speaks on "The Marriage Law, Taking into Account Both the Old and the New". 18:45 Natural Science Lecture: The station's lecture department speaks on "Several Issues in Optics" (answering Ye Zhigong, et al.). 12:45 Commemorating the "May 12th" International Nurses Day, introducing the books "The Nightingales of the Liberated Areas of China" and "Learning from Bethune". 18:00 Folk Art: Gu Rongfu, Yin Fulai perform the traditional opera "Don't Be Superstitious". 20:30 First Literary and Artistic Program: Western music "Introducing Mozart and His Works". 22:00 Second Literary and Artistic Program: Henan Opera "Wang Gui and Li Xiangxiang" recording.
    Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, 16:30 today, Youth Program: (1) School News, (2) Cultural Entertainment: Peking University Pre-Medical students sing a clapper talk "The Story of Love" (3) Excerpts broadcast from Cheng Jinwu's article in China Youth No. 38, entitled "Establishing a Correct View of Love". 17:00 Time signal; Entertainment Program: (1) Soldiers' Entertainment: Soldiers from the Third Division of the North China Military Region Garrison sing (2) Instrumental Music: (1) Mei Hu Joint Performance (2) Serenade (3) Sen Jide Ma. 17:30 Children's Program: (1) Talking about International Nurses Day (2) The story of Li Landing, the Nightingale of China. 18:00 Workers' Program: (1) Communication Speech: Sun Boling of the newly built ice factory speaks: "Find a Way to Repair the Cold Storage". (2) Cultural Entertainment: The Telecommunications Bureau Workers' Choir sings. 18:30 Municipal Voice: The First Division of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Public Health Bureau broadcasts public health knowledge to citizens, entitled "Illness Enters Through the Mouth". 21:30 Listener Service: (1) Broadcasting the call of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles to respond to the launch of the peace signature campaign, (2) Broadcasting the notice of the Preparatory Committee Organization Department of the First National Congress of Natural Science Workers of the People's Republic of China for handling the registration of natural science workers in Beijing, (3) Broadcasting several unclaimed telegrams from the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau.

### 广播节目

1950-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　广播节目
    中央人民广播电台今天十九点社会科学讲座：于光远讲《货币》（下）。二十点人民讲坛：（一）最高人民法院院长沈钧儒讲《保证执行我们的婚姻法》；（二）最高人民法院副院长张志让讲《新旧兼顾的婚姻法》。十八点四十五分自然科学讲座：该台讲座科讲《关于光学上的几个问题》（答叶志工等）。十二点四十五分纪念“五·一二”国际护士节，介绍《中国解放区的南丁格尔们》和《学习白求恩》两本书。十八点曲艺：顾荣甫、尹福来演唱牌子戏《别迷信》。二十点三十分第一次文艺节目：西乐《介绍莫札特和他的作品》。二十二点第二次文艺节目：河南梆子《王贵与李香香》录音。
    北京市人民广播电台今天十六点三十分青年节目：（一）学校新闻，（二）文化娱乐：北大医预系同学演唱快板《恋爱的故事》（三）摘要播送中国青年三十八期上程今吾写的一篇文章，题目是《建立正确的恋爱观》。十七点对时；文娱节目：（一）战士文娱：由华北军区警备营三处战士唱歌（二）器乐：（１）郿鄠联奏（２）塞外舞曲（３）森吉得马。十七点三十分儿童节目：（一）讲讲国际护士节（二）中国的南丁格尔李蓝丁的故事。十八点工人节目：（１）通讯讲话：新建制冰厂孙伯苓讲：《想办法修好冷藏库》。（２）文化娱乐：电信局工人歌咏队唱歌。十八点三十分市政之声：由北京市人民政府公共卫生局第一科广播市民卫生常识，题目是《病从口入》。二十一点三十分听众服务：（１）播送中华全国文学艺术界联合会为响应展开和平签名运动的号召，（２）播送中华全国第一次自然科学工作者代表大会筹备委员会组织部为办理北京市自然科学工作者登记的通告，（３）播送北京电信局几件招领的电报。
