Title: Mass Rally of 20,000 Youths Held in Tianjin to Grandly Commemorate Youth Day; Soviet Youth Delegation Receives Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1950-05-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 7th: Over 20,000 youths from all walks of life in Tianjin held a grand rally at Ningyuan this morning to commemorate the "May Fourth" Youth Day, celebrate the first anniversary of the founding of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and welcome the Soviet Youth Delegation. Soviet Consul General in Tianjin, Gidofov, and many Soviet citizens in Tianjin were also invited to participate. Zhang Huaisan, the chairman of the meeting, delivered a speech first, representing the youth of the city in extending a warm welcome to the Soviet Youth Delegation. Subsequently, Huang Jing, Mayor of the Tianjin Municipal People's Government, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and Mikhailov, head of the Soviet Youth Delegation, delivered speeches in succession. Mayor Huang Jing, after narrating the historical significance of the "May Fourth" Movement, called on the youth of the city to strive to learn culture, science, and technology, to learn the spirit of patriotism and internationalism from Soviet youth, and to shoulder the great responsibility of building a new China. In his speech, Mikhailov, on behalf of all Soviet youth, expressed his respect to the Chinese people and youth, and detailed the glorious achievements of Soviet youth in the Patriotic War and socialist construction. The entertainment program began with the Soviet Youth Art Troupe performing wonderful programs such as the "Russian Dance." After the meeting, representatives of the Tianjin youth presented flags and flowers to the Soviet Youth Delegation.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 7th: In addition to attending the grand welcome meeting of all circles of youth in Tianjin today, the member of the Soviet Youth Delegation, Soviet hero Kozhedub, attended the welcome meeting of the commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Tianjin this morning. In the afternoon, Mikhailov, head of the Soviet Youth Delegation, attended a welcome rally of more than 2,000 cadres from the Tianjin Municipal People's Government, the Chinese Communist Party, and the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and delivered a speech. Soviet youth writer Gonchar and youth artist Homiliko attended a symposium of cultural and artistic circles in Tianjin. Xie Weili, Melgeliang, and Jidaeva attended a lecture meeting for more than 1,400 students at Nankai University. Rozhneva and Sizov participated in a youth work symposium.

### 各界青年两万人集会  津市盛大纪念青年节  苏联青年代表团受热烈欢迎

1950-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　各界青年两万人集会
    津市盛大纪念青年节
    苏联青年代表团受热烈欢迎
    【新华社天津七日电】天津市各界青年两万余人，今晨在宁园举行盛大集会，纪念“五四”青年节，庆祝中国新民主主义青年团成立一周年及欢迎苏联青年代表团。苏联驻津总领事吉多福和天津苏侨多人亦应邀参加。大会首由主席张淮三致词，他代表全市青年，向苏联青年代表团表示热烈欢迎。继由天津市人民政府市长黄敬、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬和苏联青年代表团团长米哈依洛夫相继讲话。黄敬市长在叙述了“五四”的历史意义之后，号召全市青年努力学习文化、科学、技术，学习苏联青年爱国主义和国际主义的精神，负起建设新中国的巨任。米哈依洛夫在演说中，代表全苏青年向中国人民及青年表示敬意，并详细介绍了苏联青年在卫国战争及社会主义建设中的光辉成就。游艺节目开始，由苏联青年文艺工作团演出“俄罗斯舞”等精彩节目。会后，天津市各界青年代表向苏联青年代表团献旗、献花。
    【新华社天津七日电】苏联青年代表团人员今日除出席天津市各界青年的盛大欢迎会外，该团团员苏联英雄阔日杜布上午出席了人民解放军驻津部队指战员的欢迎会。下午，苏联青年代表团团长米哈依洛夫出席了津市人民政府、中国共产党和中国新民主主义青年团干部二千余人的欢迎大会，并发表演说。苏联青年作家龚查尔、青年艺术家合米里科出席了津市文化艺术界的座谈会。谢维里、梅尔格亮、吉大也娃出席了南开大学一千四百余学生的讲演会。罗日聂娃、西佐夫参加了青年工作座谈会。
