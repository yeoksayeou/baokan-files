Author: 
Date: 1957-11-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Manuscript:

Before launching artificial satellites, the Soviet Union repeatedly sent animals to high altitudes using rockets for experiments. The left picture shows the rocket starting to ascend after launch. The right picture shows a puppy, prepared for space flight, being placed in a sealed box and then put into the experimental rocket. The sensitive instruments in the box will record the animal's body temperature, blood pressure, breathing, and pulse rate at high altitudes. This puppy traveled at an altitude of one hundred kilometers and then returned safely to the ground.

### 图片

1957-11-10

专栏：

    苏联在发射人造卫星以前，曾多次用火箭把动物送上高空，进行实验。左图是火箭发射后开始上升时的情况。右图是准备去作宇宙航行的小狗被装在一个封闭的盒子里，然后放入实验的火箭。盒子里的灵敏的仪器将记录着动物在高空的体温、血压、呼吸和脉搏的次数。这个小狗在一百公里高空旅行后，又平安地回到了地面。　（新华社稿）
