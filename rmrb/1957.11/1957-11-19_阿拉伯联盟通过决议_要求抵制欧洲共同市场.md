Title: Arab League Passes Resolution Demanding Boycott of European Common Market
Author:
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, November 18th Dispatch from Cairo: The Arab League Council meeting concluded on the 17th, passing a resolution demanding the promotion of Arab economic unity and the boycott of the European Common Market.

    The resolution demands that Asian and African countries ruled by the West not participate in the European Common Market.

    In another resolution, the Council decided to instruct Arab diplomatic officials stationed in Bonn to pay close attention to West Germany's relations with Israel and to continue warning West German officials to be mindful of the adverse consequences that would arise from establishing diplomatic relations with Israel.

### 阿拉伯联盟通过决议  要求抵制欧洲共同市场

1957-11-19
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟通过决议
    要求抵制欧洲共同市场
    据新华社18日讯　开罗消息：阿拉伯联盟理事会会议17日在闭幕时通过决议，要求促进阿拉伯经济团结，抵制欧洲共同市场。
    这项决议要求被西方统治的亚洲和非洲国家不要参加欧洲共同市场。
    理事会在另一项决议中决定指示阿拉伯驻波恩外交官员密切注意西德同以色列的关系，并且继续警告西德官员们，要他们注意同以色列建立外交关系后将产生的不利后果。
