Title: The Spirit of Ah Q
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1957-11-28
Page: 6

    The Spirit of Ah Q
    The spirit of Ah Q has spread to the Americas,
    The heroes are roaring at the man-made satellite:
    "We need more guns, less butter!" (Note 1)
    In their roars there is clearly some trembling.
    "Shoot it down, that red aerial spy!
    We must quickly manufacture interstellar missiles to interfere!
    The moon and Mars are about to turn red,
    There will be fewer stars added to the future Stars and Stripes."
    However, missile experiments have repeatedly failed,
    The "Vanguard" satellite has become a backward pioneer. (Note 2)
    The atomic blackmail policy has clearly gone bankrupt,
    The heroes are still clamoring for war, showing off their power.
    It would be great to compete in science,
    If it is for peace, everyone will raise their hands. What's so bad about peaceful coexistence?
    Still bluffing and blustering, after all, just Ah Q.
                          (November 16, 1957)

  (Note 1) On November 13, US Secretary of Commerce Weeks emphasized a principle at the "Defense Preparedness" conference: "Less butter, more guns."
  (Note 2) The United States once boasted that it would manufacture the first artificial earth satellite, named "Vanguard."

### 阿Q精神
郭沫若
1957-11-28
第6版()
专栏：

    阿Q精神	郭沫若
    阿Q的精神已经传到了美洲，
    豪杰们在望着人造卫星怒吼：
    “我们要多一些大炮，少一些黄油！”（注一）
    在他们的吼声中显然有些发抖。
    “打下来，把那对赤色的空中侦探！
    要赶快制造出干扰的星际导弹！
    月球和火星眼看就快要赤化了，
    未来的花旗上会少添几个星点。”
    然而导弹实验却屡次都不成功，
    “先锋号”卫星已成为后进的先锋。（注二）
    原子讹诈政策分明已经破了产，
    豪杰们还在叫嚣战争，显示威风。
    要比赛科学自然再好也没有，
    如果为了和平，人人都会举手。V和平共处到底有什么不好呢？
    总还要虚张声势，毕竟只是阿Q。
                          （1957年11月16日）
  （注一）11月13日美国商务部长威克斯在“国防后备”会议上强调的一个原则：“少一些黄油，多一些大炮。”
  （注二）美国曾自吹必将制造出第一个人造地球卫星，命名为“先锋号”。
