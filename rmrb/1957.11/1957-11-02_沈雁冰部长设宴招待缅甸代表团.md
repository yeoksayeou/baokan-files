Title: Minister Shen Yanbing Hosts Banquet for Burmese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st - Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing held a banquet at noon on the 1st to host the Burmese delegation headed by Thakin Chit Maung, Minister of Information of Burma.

The five-member delegation was invited to the Soviet Union to attend the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. They arrived here yesterday and are preparing to visit Beijing before going to Moscow.

### 沈雁冰部长设宴招待缅甸代表团

1957-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰部长设宴招待缅甸代表团
    新华社1日讯　文化部部长沈雁冰1日午间举行宴会，招待以缅甸宣传部部长德钦漆貌为首的缅甸代表团。
    这个由五人组成的代表团，是应邀到苏联参加庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年纪念活动的。他们昨天到达这里，准备在北京参观、游览后，再去莫斯科。
