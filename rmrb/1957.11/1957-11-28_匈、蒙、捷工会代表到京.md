Title: Hungarian, Mongolian, and Czechoslovak Trade Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Mihály Somogyi, Chairman of the National Federation of Free Trade Unions of Hungary, D. Balajinnim, Chairman of the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Mongolian People's Republic, and F. Kahal, Secretary of the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 25th and 27th respectively to attend the Eighth National Congress of the Trade Unions of China.

Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 匈、蒙、捷工会代表到京

1957-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    匈、蒙、捷工会代表到京
    新华社27日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请，前来参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会的匈牙利自由工会全国联合会主席米·绍莫吉，蒙古人民共和国工会中央理事会主席德·巴拉吉尼姆和捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会书记弗·卡哈，分别在25日和27日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜，书记处书记董昕等人。
