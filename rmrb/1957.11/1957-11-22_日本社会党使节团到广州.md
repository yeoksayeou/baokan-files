Title: Japanese Socialist Party Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 21st. A thirteen-member delegation from the Japanese Socialist Party, headed by Yoshio Suzuki, arrived in Guangzhou from Hong Kong this afternoon and was welcomed by Du Guoxiang, Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and others. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. Yoshio Suzuki, the head of the delegation, told Xinhua reporters that the purpose of the delegation's visit was to promote the speedy restoration of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations and to explore the possibility of resuming negotiations on the fourth Sino-Japanese trade agreement.

### 日本社会党使节团到广州

1957-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

    日本社会党使节团到广州
    新华社广州21日电　以铃木义男为首的日本社会党使节团一行十三人，今天下午自香港到达广州，受到中国人民外交学会理事杜国庠等人的欢迎。使节团是应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的。使节团团长铃木义男对新华社记者说，代表团此行的目的是促进中日邦交迅速恢复，并就恢复商谈中日第四次贸易协定问题进行探讨。
