Title: Chairman Fierlinger of the Czechoslovak Parliament Cables Chairman Liu, Expressing Warm Thanks for the Attention Received During His Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1957-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Zdeněk Fierlinger, head of the Czechoslovak National Assembly delegation, sent a cable to Chairman Liu Shaoqi after returning home. The text of the cable is as follows: Respected Chairman Comrade:

Now, as our parliamentary delegation and the delegation of the Central People's Committee of Prague have visited your beautiful country and returned to Czechoslovakia, during the meeting, it was unanimously expressed that I should once again extend to you, dear Chairman Comrade, and other comrades of the Standing Committee, our warm thanks for the attention you and other comrades have given our delegation. All of us express our deep appreciation for your achievements, and your achievements and experiences are a source of high enthusiasm for our people and, to a large extent, help to further consolidate our common great socialist camp. The impressions brought back by our representatives from your country will be introduced to our people in various states, so that our visit will further strengthen and consolidate the unbreakable friendship and brotherly relations between our two peoples.

Zdeněk Fierlinger

### 捷议会主席费林格电刘委员长  热烈感谢访问我国时所受到的关注

1957-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    捷议会主席费林格电刘委员长
    热烈感谢访问我国时所受到的关注
    新华社12日讯　捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团团长兹德尼克·费林格回国后给刘少奇委员长的电报。电文如下：敬爱的委员长同志：
    现在，当我们议会代表团和布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团访问了你们美丽的国家，回到捷克斯洛伐克后的会见中，一致表示希望我向您，亲爱的委员长同志和常委会其他同志们又一次地对您和其他同志所给予我们代表团的关注致以热烈的感谢。我们所有的人对你们所取得的成就深表赞意，你们的成就和经验是我国人民热情高涨的源泉，并且在很大程度上，有助于我们共同的伟大社会主义阵营的进一步巩固。我们的代表们从贵国带回的印象将在各州介绍给我国人民，以便使我们这次访问将能进一步加强和巩固我们两国人民之间牢不可破的友好和兄弟般的关系。　　　　　　　　　　兹德尼克·费林格
