Title: Second Satellite is Safe and Sound, Has Orbited the Earth Sixty-Eight Times
Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th: TASS reports: On November 7th, ground telemetry stations continued to record the animal's basic physiological functions, observations of solar radiation and cosmic rays, and data on the temperature of the second satellite's surface and interior. By 6:00 AM Moscow time on November 8th, the second satellite had orbited the Earth sixty-eight times. The carrier rocket has traveled two and a half orbits more than the first satellite, and by the morning of November 8th, it will be forty-seven minutes ahead of the first satellite.

### 第二个卫星一路平安  已绕地球转了六十八圈

1957-11-09
第6版()
专栏：

    第二个卫星一路平安
    已绕地球转了六十八圈
    据新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科电：地面各遥测站11月7日继续纪录动物的基本生理机能情况、对太阳辐射、宇宙线的观测结果和有关第二个卫星表面和内部的温度的资料。到11月8日莫斯科时间六点正为止，第二个卫星已经绕地球运行了六十八圈。运载火箭已经比第一个卫星多走了两圈半，在11月8日上午将比第一个卫星领先四十七分钟。
