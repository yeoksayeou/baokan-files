Title: Soviet Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home; Delegation Leader Andreyev Writes Letter Expressing Gratitude to Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1957-11-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The Soviet delegation of various circles, headed by Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, concluded its visit to China and returned home by plane on the morning of the 27th.

At the farewell ceremony held at Nanyuan Airport, Chairman Soong Ching Ling and delegation leader Andreyev both emphasized in their speeches that the unbreakable great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people is an indestructible pillar of world peace.

Chairman Soong Ching Ling said in her farewell speech at the airport, "In the past twenty days, you have worked tirelessly in Beijing and other parts of our country, participating in various commemorative activities held by our people to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. Your enthusiastic encouragement and the deep friendship of the Soviet people for the Chinese people that you have brought will forever burn in our hearts."

Soong Ching Ling added, "Friendship is the source of our great strength. The Chinese people cherish the friendship of the Soviet people extremely. The great and unbreakable unity between the Chinese and Soviet people is of great significance, it is an indestructible pillar of world peace. For our people's socialist construction, this unity is not only a spiritual encouragement but also a material force. The Chinese people will always unite with the Soviet Union and strive for the construction of a socialist society and the defense of world peace."

Delegation leader Andreyev then delivered a farewell speech. He said, "Today we are about to leave your great country. During the three weeks of our visit to the People's Republic of China, we had the opportunity to witness firsthand how the Chinese people have achieved extremely important socialist transformations in the economy, culture, and the material life of the people in a very short historical period. The Soviet people, together with you, are delighted and encouraged by every victory and every progress you make."

Delegation leader Andreyev said, "In your country, the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution has become a national demonstration, a true triumphal song of the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. This great friendship is a strong foundation for the unity and cooperation of all socialist countries and a powerful fortress of world peace."

Andreyev finally thanked all the leaders of the People's Republic of China, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and all the Chinese people for their warm hospitality and brotherly care for the Soviet delegation.

After delegation leader Andreyev finished his speech, a group of young women ran up to present flowers to the distinguished guests. Subsequently, delegation leader Andreyev and the members of the delegation boarded the plane in the warm applause of the welcomers.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and secretary of the Secretariat, Li Jishen and Huang Yanpei, vice chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, He Long, vice premier of the State Council, Zhang Zhizhong, vice chairman of the National Defense Council, Zhang Wentian, vice minister of Foreign Affairs, Wu Yuzhang, Liao Chengzhi, and Shao Lizi, vice presidents and directors of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

Staff from the Soviet Embassy also went to the airport to see them off.

This newspaper reports: Andreyev, the head of the Soviet delegation of various circles participating in the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Revolution, wrote a letter to the editorial department of this newspaper on the 26th, expressing his gratitude to the Chinese people and to all the organizations and individuals who sent letters and gifts to the delegation.

The full text of the letter is as follows: To the Editorial Department of the People's Daily, Dear Comrades:

The Soviet delegation of various circles came to the People's Republic of China this time to participate in the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. During our visit to your great country, we received many friendly letters and precious gifts from state organs, social organizations, and individuals. In these letters, our Chinese friends expressed their love and friendly feelings for the Soviet people and expressed their heartfelt hope that the Soviet Union would achieve further success in its work. These letters once again prove the unbreakable unity of our two peoples in the struggle for peace and the victory of socialism.

Please allow us, through your newspaper, to express our deep gratitude to the Chinese people, to all the organizations and individuals who sent us letters and gifts.

Andreyev, on behalf of all members of the delegation
November 26, 1957

### 苏联各界代表团离北京回国  安德烈耶夫团长写信向我国人民表示感谢

1957-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联各界代表团离北京回国
    安德烈耶夫团长写信向我国人民表示感谢
    新华社27日讯　以苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫为首的苏联各界代表团结束了在中国的访问，27日上午乘飞机离北京回国。
    在南苑机场举行的欢送仪式上，宋庆龄会长和安德烈耶夫团长在讲话中一致强调中苏两国人民的牢不可破的伟大友谊，是世界和平的坚不可摧的支柱。
    宋庆龄会长在机场上致欢送词说，“在过去的二十天里，你们不辞辛劳地，在北京以及我国各地，参加了我国人民庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年的各项纪念活动，你们的热情鼓励和你们所带来的苏联人民对于中国人民的深厚友谊，将会永远燃烧在我们的心中。”
    宋庆龄又说，“友谊是我们伟大力量的源泉。中国人民是异常珍贵苏联人民的友情的。中苏两国人民的伟大的牢不可破的团结，具有重大的意义，它是世界和平的坚不可摧的支柱。对于我国人民建设社会主义来说，这种团结不仅是精神上的鼓舞，而且是物质的力量。中国人民将永远和苏联团结一致，为建设社会主义社会，为保卫世界和平而奋斗。”
    安德烈耶夫团长接着发表了告别讲话。他说，“今天我们就要离开你们伟大的国家了。在中华人民共和国访问的三个星期中，我们有机会亲眼见到，中国人民怎样在极短的历史期间实现了在经济、文化和人民物质生活方面的极其重要的社会主义改造。苏联人民和你们一起，为你们的每一个胜利，为你们的每一个进步而感到欢欣鼓舞。”
    安德烈耶夫团长说，“在你们国家里，十月革命四十周年的庆祝活动，已成为全民性的大示威，已成为中苏两国人民伟大友谊的真正凯歌，这个伟大的友谊是所有社会主义国家团结合作的坚强基础，是世界和平的强大堡垒。”
    安德烈耶夫最后感谢中华人民共和国的全体领导同志，中苏友好协会和全体中国人民对苏联各界代表团的热情款待和兄弟般的关怀。
    安德烈耶夫团长讲话结束后，一群女青年跑上前来向贵宾们献了花。随后，安德烈耶夫团长和团员们在欢送者的热烈掌声中登上飞机。
    到飞机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、黄炎培，国务院副总理贺龙，国防委员会副主席张治中，外交部副部长张闻天，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、廖承志、邵力子和理事们，各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人。
    苏联大使馆人员也到机场欢送。
    本报讯　苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝伟大十月革命四十周年纪念代表团团长安德烈耶夫26日写信给本报编辑部，向中国人民，向所有寄给代表团信件和礼物的团体和个人表示感谢。
    这封信的全文如下：人民日报编辑部亲爱的同志们：
    苏联各界代表团这次来访中华人民共和国，是为了参加中国人民庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年。在访问你们这个伟大国家的期间，我们收到了很多国家机关和社会团体以及个人的友好信件和珍贵礼品。我们的中国朋友在这些信件中表示出他们对苏联人民的热爱和友好的感情，并表示衷心希望苏联在工作中进一步取得成就。这些信件再一次证明了我们两国人民在争取和平事业和社会主义的胜利的斗争中坚如磐石般的团结。
    请允许我们通过贵报向中国人民，向所有寄给我们信件和礼物的团体和个人表示深深的感谢。
  安德烈耶夫代表全体代表团成员
