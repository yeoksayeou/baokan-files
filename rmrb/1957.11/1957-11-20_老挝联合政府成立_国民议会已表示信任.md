Title: Lao Coalition Government Established; National Assembly Expresses Confidence

Author:

Date: 1957-11-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 19th: According to a Vientiane radio broadcast tonight, the Lao coalition government, headed by Prince Souvanna Phouma as Prime Minister and with representatives from the Lao Fighting Forces, was proclaimed established today. This marks the end of the years-long division in Laos.

The Lao National Assembly, which opened yesterday, held a lengthy discussion today on Prince Souvanna Phouma's report on the coalition government. Prince Souvanna Phouma and Prince Souphanouvong answered numerous inquiries raised by the deputies.

In the final vote, all deputies present with voting rights cast votes of confidence in the coalition government. A total of thirty-seven deputies attended today, of whom ten deputies holding government ministerial positions had no voting rights.

Tomorrow, members of the coalition government will be sworn in at the That Luang Temple in Vientiane.

### 老挝联合政府成立  国民议会已表示信任

1957-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝联合政府成立
    国民议会已表示信任
    新华社河内19日电　据万象电台今天晚上广播，由富马亲王担任首相并有寮国战斗部队代表参加的老挝联合政府今天宣告成立。老挝多年来的分裂状态至此结束。
    昨天开幕的老挝国民议会，今天就富马亲王所作关于联合政府的报告进行了长时间的讨论。富马亲王和苏发努冯亲王对许多议员提出的质询分别作了答复。
    最后付表决时，全体到会的有投票权的议员都对联合政府投了信任票。今天到会的议员共三十七名，其中十名担任政府部长职务的议员无投票权。
    明天，联合政府成员将在万象大佛寺宣誓就职。
