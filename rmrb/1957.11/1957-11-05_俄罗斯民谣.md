Russian Folk Song

    You, my brothers,
    Comrades, let us go together!
    To fight the White bandits,
    Defend the free Soviet regime.

    There are armored cars on the mountains,
    There are cadets below the mountains,
    Our Red Army is advancing,
    Wiping them out completely.

    Note: Cadets were counter-revolutionary reserve officers trained by the Tsar.
                (Translated by Cao Nianming)

### 俄罗斯民谣

1957-11-05
第8版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯民谣
    你们，我的兄弟们，
    同志们，我们一齐走吧！
    和白匪去作战，
    保卫自由的苏维埃政权。
    山上有装甲车，
    山下有士官生（注），
    我们的红军在前进，
    把他们消灭干净。
    注：士官生是沙皇训练的反革命后备军官。
                （曹念明译）
