Title: Albanian Ambassador Holds Press Conference to Introduce Albanian Independence and Liberation Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 25th November. The Albanian Ambassador to China, Mr. Bali Li, held a press conference in Beijing on the afternoon of the 25th to introduce the upcoming 45th anniversary of Albanian Independence (28th) and the 13th anniversary of Liberation (29th).

The Albanian nation is one of the oldest nations in the Balkan Peninsula. Due to the importance of Albania's geographical location, invaders have repeatedly invaded and devastated the country. In the 14th century, Turkey invaded Albania. The Albanian people waged an unyielding struggle against foreign invaders and declared independence on November 28, 1912. In 1939, the Albanian traitor Zog sold the motherland to fascist Italy. After fascist Italy surrendered in 1943, German fascists sent troops to occupy Albania. Under the leadership of the Albanian Communist Party, the Albanian people launched a widespread guerrilla war against the Italian and German fascist invaders. On November 29, 1944, under the historical victory of the Soviet army defeating the fascist invaders, the Albanian people finally drove the fascist German occupying forces out of the country and liberated the entire territory.

Ambassador Bali Li pointed out that the Albanian people finally achieved victory after arduous struggles, and are now smoothly building a socialist society with the help of the great Soviet Union, People's China, various people's democratic countries, and other friendly countries. Ambassador Bali Li especially mentioned the great and loyal friendship between the Soviet and Albanian peoples. He pointed out that the Soviet Union has been and will always be the closest friend of the Albanian people.

An Albanian documentary film was shown at the press conference, and a cocktail reception was held afterwards.

### 介绍阿尔巴尼亚独立和解放纪念日  阿大使举行记者招待会

1957-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    介绍阿尔巴尼亚独立和解放纪念日
    阿大使举行记者招待会
    新华社25日讯　阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使巴利里25日下午在北京举行记者招待会，介绍即将到来的阿尔巴尼亚独立四十五周年（28日）和解放十三周年（29日）两个纪念日。
    阿尔巴尼亚民族是巴尔干半岛上最古老的民族之一。由于阿尔巴尼亚所处地理位置的重要性，侵略者曾多次侵略和破坏了这个国家。十四世纪时土耳其入侵阿尔巴尼亚。阿尔巴尼亚人民为反对外国侵略者进行了不屈的斗争，并在1912年11月28日宣告独立。1939年，阿尔巴尼亚的叛徒索古把祖国出卖给了法西斯意大利。1943年法西斯意大利投降后，德国法西斯又出兵侵占了阿尔巴尼亚。阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚共产党领导下，向意、德法西斯侵略者发动了广泛的游击战争。1944年11月29日，阿尔巴尼亚人民终于在苏军击溃法西斯侵略军的历史性的胜利条件下，把法西斯德国占领军逐出国境，解放了全部国土。
    巴利里大使指出，阿尔巴尼亚人民经过艰苦的斗争终于获得了胜利，现在在伟大的苏联、人民中国、各人民民主国家以及其他友好国家的帮助下，正在顺利地建设社会主义社会。巴利里大使特别提到苏联和阿尔巴尼亚人民间伟大和忠诚的友谊。他指出，苏联在过去和将来永远是阿尔巴尼亚人民最亲密的朋友。
    招待会上放映了阿尔巴尼亚的纪录电影，招待会以后举行了酒会。
