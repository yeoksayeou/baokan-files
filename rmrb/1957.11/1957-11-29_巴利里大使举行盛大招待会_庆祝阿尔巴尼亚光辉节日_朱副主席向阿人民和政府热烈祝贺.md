Title: Albanian Ambassador Balili Holds Grand Reception to Celebrate Albania's Glorious Festival; Vice Chairman Zhu Extends Warm Congratulations to the Albanian People and Government
Author:
Date: 1957-11-29
Page: 1
		
Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Ambassador of Albania to China, Balili, held a grand reception on the evening of the 28th to celebrate the 45th anniversary of Albania's declaration of national independence and the 13th anniversary of Albania's liberation.

Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai attended the reception.

Vice Chairman Zhu De spoke at the reception, extending sincere and warm congratulations to the Albanian people and the Albanian government on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese government. Vice Chairman Zhu De said that in the thirteen years since liberation, under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party and the Albanian People's Republic government, the Albanian people have made great achievements in the cause of building socialism, making Albania a socialist industrial and agricultural country. The Chinese people are delighted by the great achievements made by the fraternal Albanian people, because these achievements are an inseparable part of the entire achievements of the socialist camp.

Vice Chairman Zhu De also said that the Albanian People's Republic, as a strong member of the socialist camp, has made unremitting efforts to further consolidate and develop the unity of socialist countries and to safeguard international security and world peace. The international status of the Albanian People's Republic is constantly improving.

Vice Chairman Zhu De concluded by saying that in recent years, the profound friendship and close cooperation between the Chinese people and the fraternal Albanian people have been continuously developed, and the friendly exchanges between our two countries are increasing day by day. The Chinese people will continue to strengthen friendship and cooperation with the fraternal Albanian people in the future, and make joint efforts for the close unity of socialist countries and world peace.

Ambassador Balili spoke at the beginning of the reception. After outlining the struggle for Albanian independence and liberation, he said that today, with the assistance of the Soviet Union, China and other friendly countries, Albania is victoriously building socialism. In the thirteen years of peaceful life, under the leadership of its party and government, the Albanian people have transformed Albania from a backward agricultural country into an agricultural-industrial country. Today, Albania's industrial production has increased fourteen times compared with pre-war levels, and agriculture has also rapidly formed a great socialist mechanized agriculture. Culture has greatly developed, and people's lives are improving day by day.

The Ambassador said that the friendship between Albania and China is growing and developing, and it will be greatly consolidated in the future. The Albanian people have made and will continue to make every effort to further consolidate this friendship. Similarly, the Albanian people have made and will continue to make every effort to consolidate the great peaceful socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and world peace.

At the end of their speeches, Vice Chairman Zhu De and Ambassador Balili mutually wished the prosperity and strength of the two countries, the development of friendship between the two peoples, and the good health of the leaders of the two countries.

Tonight, those present at the reception also included Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Vice Premiers Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, responsible persons from various departments of state organs, people from all walks of life in the capital, diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China, and guests from many countries who are visiting Beijing, totaling more than 600 people.

### 巴利里大使举行盛大招待会  庆祝阿尔巴尼亚光辉节日  朱副主席向阿人民和政府热烈祝贺

1957-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    巴利里大使举行盛大招待会
    庆祝阿尔巴尼亚光辉节日
    朱副主席向阿人民和政府热烈祝贺
    新华社28日讯　28日是阿尔巴尼亚宣布民族独立四十五周年，29日是阿尔巴尼亚解放十三周年，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使巴利里在28日晚间举行盛大招待会，庆祝这两个节日。
    朱德副主席和周恩来总理等出席了招待会。
    朱德副主席在招待会上讲话，代表中国人民和中国政府向阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚政府表示衷心的、热烈的祝贺。朱德副主席说，在解放后的十三年中间，阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府的领导下，在建设社会主义的事业上取得了巨大的成就，使阿尔巴尼亚已经成为一个社会主义的工农业国家。中国人民为兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民所取得的巨大成就而欢欣鼓舞，因为这些成就是社会主义阵营全部成就中不可分割的一部分。
    朱德副主席又说，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国作为社会主义阵营中坚强的一员，在进一步巩固和发展社会主义国家的团结和维护国际安全和世界和平方面，进行了不懈的努力。阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的国际地位正在不断地提高。
    朱德副主席最后说，近年来，中国人民和兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民间的深厚友谊和亲密合作获得了不断的发展，我们两国之间的友好往来正在一天比一天地增加。中国人民今后将继续加强同兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民的友谊和合作，为社会主义国家的紧密团结和世界和平进行共同的努力。
    巴利里大使在招待会开始的时候讲话。他在概述了阿尔巴尼亚独立和解放的斗争经过以后说，今天，阿尔巴尼亚在苏联、中国和其他各友好国家的援助下，正在胜利地建设着社会主义。在和平生活的十三年中，阿尔巴尼亚人民在自己的党和政府的领导下，已经把阿尔巴尼亚从一个落后的农业国变成了一个农业—工业国。今天，阿尔巴尼亚的工业生产比战前增加到十四倍，农业方面也迅速地形成了一个伟大的社会主义机械化的农业。文化有了很大的发展，人民生活日益在提高。
    大使说，阿中两国的友谊日益在增长和发展，将来它还将大大地巩固。阿尔巴尼亚人民在过去曾作过，今后也还将继续作一切努力，以进一步巩固这一友谊。同样，过去阿尔巴尼亚人民曾作过，而今后也将继续作一切努力，来巩固以苏联为首的伟大的和平社会主义阵营以及世界和平。
    朱德副主席和巴利里大使在讲话结束时，互祝两国的繁荣和富强，祝两国人民友谊的发展，祝两国领导人的身体健康。
    今晚，出席招待会的，还有副委员长李济深、黄炎培、彭真，副总理陈毅、李先念，外交部副部长曾涌泉，国家机关各部门的负责人，首都社会各界人士，各国驻中国的使节和外交官员，以及正在北京访问的许多国家的来宾，共六百多人。
