Title: Two Artificial Satellites Revolving in the Sky; Soviet Scientists Continue Measurements

Author:

Date: 1957-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th dispatch: The Soviet Union's first artificial Earth satellite and its carrier rocket have been orbiting in space for more than a month. It has been nearly a week since the launch of the second artificial Earth satellite.

Scientists are carefully studying the observed and measured data. Through research, it may be possible to determine the characteristics of the satellite's orbital parameters and the effects of atmospheric drag in the upper atmosphere. According to the planned schedule, scientists are continuing to conduct scientific measurements.

As of November 9th, the first artificial satellite's orbital period was 94.72 minutes, shortening by 2.94 seconds per day. The maximum altitude of the first artificial satellite's orbit was 810 kilometers. The orbital period of the first artificial satellite's carrier rocket was 93.48 minutes. Because it orbits at a lower altitude (maximum altitude of 695 kilometers), its orbital period is shortening faster than that of the first satellite, at approximately 6.3 seconds per day.

The orbital period of the second artificial Earth satellite is 103.52 minutes, and this period is shortening at an extremely slow rate. It shortens by 2.3 seconds per day. The maximum altitude of its orbit is 1,650 kilometers.

The second satellite passed over Beijing at 8:51 AM on November 10th; over Shanghai at 8:53 AM; over Urumqi at 10:32 AM; over Kunming at 10:39 AM; and will pass over Beijing at 0:01 AM on November 11th.

### 两颗人造卫星天上转  苏联科学家正继续进行测量

1957-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    两颗人造卫星天上转
    苏联科学家正继续进行测量
    新华社10日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联第一颗人造地球卫星和它的运载火箭已经在世界上空飞行一个多月了。自从发射第二颗人造地球卫星到现在，已经将近一个星期了。
    科学家正在仔细研究观察和测量得到的材料。经过研究，可能确定卫星运行轨道参数和大气上层阻力所产生的力的影响的特征。根据预定的计划，科学家正在继续进行科学测量。
    到11月9日为止，第一颗人造卫星运转一周的时间为九十四·七二分钟。每昼夜缩短了二·九四秒。第一颗人造卫星轨道的最大高度为八百一十公里。第一颗人造卫星的运载火箭环绕地球一周的时间是九十三·四八分钟。因为它运行的轨道较低（最大高度六百九十五公里），所以它运行一周的时间缩短的趋向要比第一颗卫星快，每昼夜约缩短六·三秒。
    第二颗人造地球卫星运行一周的时间是一○三·五二分钟，这个时间正在以极缓慢的速度缩短中。它一昼夜缩短二·三秒钟。它的运行轨道的最大高度是一千六百五十公里。
    第二颗卫星11月10日八点五十一分经过北京；八点五十三分经过上海，十点三十二分经过乌鲁木齐，十点三十九分经过昆明；11日零点一分经过北京。
