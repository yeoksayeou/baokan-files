Title: The Soviet Union Sends a Government Delegation to Czechoslovakia to Attend the President's Funeral
Author:
Date: 1957-11-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th -- TASS reported: The Soviet Union has decided to send a government delegation headed by Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, to Prague to attend the funeral of Czechoslovak President Antonín Zápotocký.

### 苏联派政府代表团赴捷参加总统葬礼

1957-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联派政府代表团赴捷参加总统葬礼
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科13日讯：苏联已经决定派出以苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫为首的政府代表团，前往布拉格参加捷克斯洛伐克总统安·萨波托斯基的葬礼。
