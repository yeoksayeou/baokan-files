Title: Purple Mountain Observatory Releases Time for Second Satellite to Pass Over China
Author:
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 8

    Purple Mountain Observatory Release:
    Time for Second Satellite to Pass Over China
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 5th: The Purple Mountain Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences announced: The Soviet Union's second artificial satellite will fly over western China on the morning of the 7th. At 6:05 AM Beijing time, the satellite will fly in from the southwest, passing between Kunming and Tengchong; at 6:07 AM, it will pass over Chengdu; at 6:08 AM, it will pass over Tianshui, Gansu, and Baoji, Shaanxi; at 6:10 AM, it will fly towards Hohhot. The satellite will look like an orange star, with a brightness of about magnitude 1, moving slowly across the sky, easily visible to the naked eye.

### 紫金山天文台发布  第二颗卫星经过我国时间

1957-11-06
第8版()
专栏：

    紫金山天文台发布
    第二颗卫星经过我国时间
    新华社南京5日电　中国科学院紫金山天文台发布：苏联第二颗人造卫星7日晨将飞过我国西部上空。北京时间六点○五分，卫星从西南飞来，经过昆明腾冲之间，六点○七分过成都，六点○八分过甘肃天水，陕西宝鸡，六点十分飞往呼和浩特。卫星像一颗橙色的亮度约为一等的星，在天空缓缓移动，普通肉眼很容易看到。
