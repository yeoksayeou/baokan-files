Title: The Fruitful Result of Sino-Soviet Friendly Cooperation: Romania Manufactures Cyclotron
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, November 11th: According to local newspaper reports, a Romanian cyclotron capable of accelerating particles to 12.5 million electron volts has been manufactured and is entering the final adjustment phase before use. It can provide accelerated particles necessary for scientific research, produce radioactive isotopes that cannot be obtained in atomic reactor reactions, and help understand the properties of the nuclei of the substances being studied. The manufacture of the accelerator is a powerful testament to the Romanian Party and government's support for the development of science and technology, and a vivid embodiment of friendly cooperation between Romania and the Soviet Union.

The Romanian Institute of Atomic Physics was established not long ago. In addition to this cyclotron, the institute is expected to install an experimental atomic reactor.

### 罗苏友好合作的结晶  罗马尼亚制成回旋加速器

1957-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    罗苏友好合作的结晶
    罗马尼亚制成回旋加速器
    据新华社布加勒斯特11日电　据这里报纸报道，罗马尼亚一座可以把粒子加速到一千二百五十万电子伏特的回旋加速器已经制成，并且进入了使用前总的调整阶段。它可以提供科学研究工作所必需的加速粒子，生产在原子反应堆的反应中无法获得的放射性同位素，有助于了解被研究物质的原子核的性质。加速器的制成是罗马尼亚党和政府支持科学技术发展的有力证据，也是罗苏友好合作的生动体现。
    罗马尼亚原子物理研究所是不久以前建立的。这个研究所预计安装的除这台回旋加速器外，还有一个试验型原子反应堆。
