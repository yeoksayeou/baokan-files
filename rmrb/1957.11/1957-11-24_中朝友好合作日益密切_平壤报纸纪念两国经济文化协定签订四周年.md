Title: China-North Korea Friendly Cooperation Growing Closer: Pyongyang Newspapers Commemorate Fourth Anniversary of Economic and Cultural Agreement

Author:

Date: 1957-11-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 23rd. Pyongyang newspapers today published editorials commemorating the fourth anniversary of the signing of the China-North Korea Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

	"Rodong Sinmun" editorial stated that the China-North Korea Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement is a new milestone in the development of friendly cooperative relations between the two peoples. The Korean people are pleased to point out that over the past four years, this agreement has made tremendous contributions to further consolidating and developing the friendship and unity between the two peoples, expanding and strengthening long-term cooperative relations in the economic and cultural fields, safeguarding the vital interests of the two peoples, and defending peace in the Far East.

	The editorial said that the friendship, unity, and economic and cultural cooperation between the Chinese and Korean peoples are one of the powerful guarantees for the Korean people to realize the peaceful reunification of their motherland and build socialism in northern Korea. The Korean people deeply realize that the reason they have been able to achieve victory in their struggles is because they are united within the socialist family. The Korean people are determined to forever unite with them.

	"Minju Choson" editorial stated that this agreement laid the foundation for economic and cultural cooperation between China and North Korea and contributed to strengthening the unity of the socialist camp.

	The editorial said that the Chinese people have not only assisted Korea during the war and after the war, but have also made consistent efforts to peacefully resolve the Korean issue. This unwavering stance of the 600 million Chinese people is a powerful encouragement to the Korean people.

### 中朝友好合作日益密切  平壤报纸纪念两国经济文化协定签订四周年

1957-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    中朝友好合作日益密切
    平壤报纸纪念两国经济文化协定签订四周年
    据新华社平壤23日电　平壤各报今天发表社论，纪念中朝经济及文化合作协定签订四周年。
    “劳动新闻”的社论说，中朝经济及文化合作协定是发展两国人民友好合作关系的新的里程碑。朝鲜人民满意地指出，四年以来，这项协定对进一步巩固和发展两国人民的友谊团结，扩大和加强两国在经济和文化方面长期合作的关系，保障两国人民的切身利益和保卫远东和平作出了巨大的贡献。
    社论说，朝中两国人民的友谊团结和经济文化合作是朝鲜人民实现祖国的和平统一和在朝鲜北部建设社会主义的有力保证之一。朝鲜人民深刻地认识到，他们在自己的斗争中所以能够取得胜利，是由于他们团结在社会主义大家庭之中。朝鲜人民决心永远同他们团结在一起。
    “民主朝鲜报”的社论说，这个协定奠定了朝中两国经济文化合作的基础，对加强社会主义阵营团结作出了贡献。
    社论说，中国人民不仅在战争中和战后援助了朝鲜，而且为和平解决朝鲜问题进行了始终一贯的努力。六亿中国人民的这种坚定不移的立场是对朝鲜人民有力的鼓舞。
