Title: Afghan Olympic Committee Chairman Saraj Leaves Guangzhou to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 11th. Saraj, Chairman of the Afghan Olympic Committee, left Guangzhou this morning, the 11th, to return home. Qin Yuanbang, Chairman of the Guangdong Branch of the All-China Sports Federation, Dong Shiyang, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch, and others saw him off at the station.

Saraj arrived in Guangzhou from Hangzhou on the 8th. In Guangzhou, he visited gymnasiums and sports fields, learned about the sports activities carried out at Zhongshan University, and visited the Guangzhou Commune Martyrs Cemetery, the Huanghuagang Martyrs Cemetery, and the Qingzhen Mosque, which has a thousand-year history.

### 阿富汗奥林匹克委员会主席  萨拉季离广州回国

1957-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗奥林匹克委员会主席
    萨拉季离广州回国
    新华社广州11日电　阿富汗奥林匹克委员会主席萨拉季11日晨离广州回国。中华全国体育总会广东省分会主席秦元邦、广州市分会副主席董世扬等人到车站欢送。
    萨拉季是在8日自杭州到达广州的。在广州参观了体育馆、运动场，了解了中山大学开展体育运动的情况，并参谒了广州公社烈士陵园、黄花岗烈士陵墓和已有千年历史的清真寺。
