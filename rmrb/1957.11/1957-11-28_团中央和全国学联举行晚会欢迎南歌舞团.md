Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and the All-China Students' Federation Hold a Soiree to Welcome the Yugoslav Dance Troupe

Author:

Date: 1957-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th: To welcome the "Branko Krsmanović" Yugoslav student dance troupe, composed of Yugoslav university students, on their visit to China, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China and the All-China Students' Federation held a get-together in Beijing on the evening of November 27th, attended by over six hundred Chinese university students.

Miloš Nikolić, head of the dance troupe, member of the Central Committee of the Yugoslav Youth League, and chairman of the Yugoslav Student Union, and Miotrag Pavlović, deputy head of the troupe and vice chairman of the "Branko Krsmanović" student art society of Yugoslavia, as well as all members of the dance troupe, were invited to attend the soiree.

Also invited to the soiree were Milicevic, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Yugoslav Embassy in China, and embassy staff.

At the beginning of the soiree, Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and the All-China Students' Federation, expressed a warm welcome and gratitude to the dance troupe for visiting China and performing tirelessly in various places.

Miloš Nikolić, head of the dance troupe, then spoke. He expressed his gratitude for the opportunity to meet with Chinese youth.

After the speeches, a dance was held. The "Branko Krsmanović" dance troupe and the Beijing University Student Amateur Art Troupe also performed many wonderful song and dance programs.

### 团中央和全国学联举行晚会欢迎南歌舞团

1957-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    团中央和全国学联举行晚会欢迎南歌舞团
    据新华社27日讯　为欢迎由南斯拉夫大学生组成的南斯拉夫“伯朗柯·克里斯曼诺维奇”歌舞团来中国访问，中国共产主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国学生联合会27日晚在北京举行了有六百多名中国大学生参加的联欢晚会。
    歌舞团团长、南斯拉夫青年联盟中央委员会委员、南斯拉夫学生联合会主席米洛什·尼柯里奇和副团长、南斯拉夫“伯朗柯·克里斯曼诺维奇”学生文艺社副主席米奥特拉格·巴夫洛维奇，以及歌舞团全体团员应邀出席了晚会。
    应邀出席晚会的还有南斯拉夫驻华大使馆临时代办米里切维奇和使馆人员。
    晚会开始时，中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立代表共产主义青年团中央委员会和全国学联，对歌舞团来中国访问和不辞辛劳地到各地演出，表示热烈欢迎和感谢。
    歌舞团团长米洛什·尼柯里奇接着讲话。他对能有机会同中国青年见面，表示感谢。
    在讲话以后，举行了舞会。“伯朗柯·克里斯曼诺维奇”歌舞团和北京市大学生业余文工团还表演了许多精采的歌舞节目。
