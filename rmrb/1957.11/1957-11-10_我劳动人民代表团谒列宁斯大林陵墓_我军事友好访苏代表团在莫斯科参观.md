Title: Our Labor People's Delegation Pays Respects at the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum; Our Military Friendship Delegation Visits Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-11-10
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th: The Chinese Labor People's Delegation and other labor people's delegations from various countries, invited to the Soviet Union to participate in the 40th Anniversary Celebration of the October Revolution, paid respects at the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum on the morning of the 9th and laid wreaths.

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th: The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to the Soviet Union, led by Deputy Head of the Delegation, Marshal Ye Jianying, visited the rooms and conference halls in the Kremlin where Lenin once worked and lived on the 8th. The delegation also visited the armory in the palace.

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th: The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to the Soviet Union visited the Soviet Agricultural and Industrial Exhibition on the 9th. Skobkin, Director of the Exhibition Propaganda Department, welcomed the Chinese guests on behalf of the exhibition and presented the delegation with commemorative badges and pictures of the exhibition. Deputy Head of the Delegation, Marshal Ye Jianying, thanked Skobkin on behalf of all members of the delegation and presented a gift – Xu Beihong's painting "Galloping Horse." Subsequently, the delegation toured the agricultural and industrial exhibition halls in detail. The delegation members visited the replica of the world's first artificial satellite displayed in the Science Hall with great interest.

### 我劳动人民代表团谒列宁斯大林陵墓  我军事友好访苏代表团在莫斯科参观

1957-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我劳动人民代表团谒列宁斯大林陵墓
    我军事友好访苏代表团在莫斯科参观
    新华社莫斯科9日电　应邀到苏联参加十月革命四十周年庆典的中国劳动人民代表团和其他各国劳动人民代表团，9日上午拜谒了列宁斯大林陵墓，并献了花圈。
    据新华社莫斯科9日电　中国军事友好访苏代表团8日在副团长叶剑英元帅率领下参观了克里姆林宫内列宁曾经工作和居住过的房间和会议室。代表团还参观了宫内的兵器陈列室。
    据新华社莫斯科9日电　中国军事友好访苏代表团9日参观了苏联农业和工业展览会。展览会宣传处处长斯科布金代表展览会欢迎中国客人，并且向代表团赠送了纪念章和关于展览会的图片。副团长叶剑英元帅代表全体团员向斯科布金致谢，并且献了礼物——徐悲鸿画的“奔马”。接着，代表团详细参观了农、工业的陈列馆。团员们以极大的兴趣参观了科学馆内陈列的世界上第一颗人造卫星的复制品。
