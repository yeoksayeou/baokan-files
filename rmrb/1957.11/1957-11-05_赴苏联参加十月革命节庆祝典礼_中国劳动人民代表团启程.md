Title: Chinese Labor People's Delegation Departs for Soviet Union to Attend October Revolution Celebration

Author:
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: The Chinese Labor People's Delegation, led by its head Liu Ningyi, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 4th to go to Moscow to attend the grand celebration of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, heads of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, leaders of relevant organizations, and representatives from the scientific, literary, and artistic fields all went to the airport to see them off.

G. Tikhvinski, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, leaders of the Soviet Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theatre and others were also at the airport to welcome them.

On the afternoon of the 3rd, Comrade Zhou Enlai received all members of the Chinese Labor People's Delegation.

### 赴苏联参加十月革命节庆祝典礼  中国劳动人民代表团启程

1957-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    赴苏联参加十月革命节庆祝典礼
    中国劳动人民代表团启程
    新华社4日讯　中国劳动人民代表团由团长刘宁一率领在4日上午乘飞机离开北京，去莫斯科参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的庆祝典礼。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通和中苏友好协会总会以及各有关团体的负责人，科学、文学、艺术界的代表，都到机场送行。
    苏联驻华大使馆临时代办顾悌克夫、苏联新西伯利亚歌舞剧院芭蕾舞团的领导人等，也到机场欢送。
    3日下午，周恩来同志接见了中国劳动人民代表团的全体人员。
