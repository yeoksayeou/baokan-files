Title: Princess Norodom Pâpha Devi Holds Reception to Welcome Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-11
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 10th—Princess Norodom Pâpha Devi held a reception at Prince Sihanouk's "Mulberry Garden" villa to welcome the Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation headed by Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Culture. Four princes including Norodom Sihanouk, Cambodian Minister of National Education Mao Chai, Secretary of State for Information and Tourism Tan Dong, dancers from the Royal Cambodian Ballet, and personnel from the Chinese Economic Delegation to Cambodia attended the reception.

　　Princess Norodom Pâpha Devi delivered a welcoming speech, saying that through cultural exchanges, the friendship between Cambodia and China has become as solid as a rock.

　　At the suggestion of Princess Norodom Pâpha Devi, the guests and hosts danced the Cambodian folk dance "Rom Vong" together at the reception. Later, Princess Norodom Pâpha Devi, accompanied by four Cambodian girls, sang a song from the "Tea Picking and Butterfly Catching Dance," and the guests and hosts danced the Chinese "Yangge" dance together.

### 帕花·黛维公主举行招待会  欢迎我文化艺术代表团

1957-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    帕花·黛维公主举行招待会
    欢迎我文化艺术代表团
    据新华社金边10日电　帕花·黛维公主在西哈努克亲王的“桑园”别墅举行招待会，欢迎以中国文化部副部长丁西林为首的中国文化艺术代表团。夏卡朋等四位王子、柬埔寨国民教育大臣茅柴、新闻和旅行事务国务秘书坦东、柬埔寨王家舞蹈团的舞蹈家们以及中国驻柬埔寨经济代表团人员出席了招待会。
    帕花·黛维公主致欢迎词说，通过文化的交流，柬埔寨和中国的友谊已经像磐石那样巩固。
    在帕花·黛维公主的建议下，宾主们在招待会上相偕跳起柬埔寨的民间舞蹈“南旺”舞。后来，帕花·黛维公主偕同四位柬埔寨女郎唱了“采茶扑蝶舞”中的歌曲，宾主又相偕跳起中国的“秧歌”舞。
