Title: Jinan Meat Processing Plant Officially Begins Production
Author:
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 4

The Jinan Meat Processing Plant, constructed with Soviet assistance, officially began production on the 1st. This factory will produce 800 tons of frozen meat in November, which will be processed into canned goods by relevant departments for export.

This factory includes sections for feeding, slaughtering, processing, freezing, and storage. Most of the production process is mechanized. The pork slaughter workshop is designed to slaughter approximately 2,500 live pigs per day.

### 济南肉类联合加工厂正式投入生产

1957-11-05
第4版()
专栏：

    济南肉类联合加工厂正式投入生产
    在苏联帮助下建设的济南肉类联合加工厂，1日正式投入生产。这座工厂在11月份将生产八百吨冻肉，经有关部门加工成罐头后出口。
    这个工厂包括饲养、宰杀、复制、冷冻、储藏等部分，整个生产过程大部采用机器操作。宰猪的肉脂车间，按设计能力每天能宰生猪二千五百头左右。
