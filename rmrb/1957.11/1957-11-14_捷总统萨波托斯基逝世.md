Title: Czechoslovak President Zápotocký Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1957-11-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 13th: Radio Prague broadcast today an announcement from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and the Czechoslovak government stating that the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic deeply announce that Comrade Antonín Zápotocký, member of the Politburo of the CPCz Central Committee and President of the Republic, unfortunately passed away this morning at 5:00 AM (Prague time).

According to successive communiqués issued by the Czechoslovak State Sanatorium, President Zápotocký had been making good progress since suffering a cardiovascular embolism on November 4th, but his condition suddenly deteriorated last night. After doctors took various emergency measures, his condition temporarily improved. However, at 3:00 AM this morning, severe circulatory failure occurred, and he could not be saved, passing away at 5:00 AM.

Zápotocký was 73 years old. He participated in the revolution in his early years and was one of the founders of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia. His lifelong activities are inextricably linked to the history of the Czechoslovak workers' movement.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 13th: The Politburo of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and the Presidium of the Czechoslovak Government held an emergency joint meeting today for the passing of President Zápotocký. The meeting decided on:

\t1. An obituary to the Czechoslovak people;
\t2. A period of national mourning from the 13th until the day of the funeral, the 18th. During the period of national mourning, theaters and cinemas will adjust their programs, and banquets, sports games, and other competitions will be suspended;
\t3. The formation of a funeral committee for President Zápotocký. The chairman of the committee is Novotný, and the members are: Široký, Barák, Šupka, Plojhár, Šlechta, and Strechaj.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 13th: According to a report from the Czechoslovak News Agency this morning, the report from the medical board of Czechoslovak President Antonín Zápotocký is as follows:

After President's condition temporarily improved in the hours after the evening of the 12th, his condition deteriorated sharply at 3:00 AM on the 13th. An uncontrollable circulatory failure occurred. Comrade Antonín Zápotocký, President of the Republic, passed away at 5:00 AM.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 13th: According to a report from the Czechoslovak News Agency, the medical report on President Zápotocký's health issued by the State Sanatorium on November 12th stated that after a stable and normal course of disease development in the first week, a sudden new case of myocardial insufficiency occurred today. There are signs of heart failure. The pulse is 120 beats per minute, which is normal. Blood pressure is 105-70 mm.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 13th: Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Cao Ying paid a visit to Czechoslovak Acting Foreign Minister Gregor and Director of the Presidential Office Novák this morning to express condolences and sympathy for the passing of President Zápotocký, and asked them to convey condolences and sympathy to President Zápotocký's wife on his behalf. ### 捷总统萨波托斯基逝世

1957-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    捷总统萨波托斯基逝世
    新华社布拉格13日电　布拉格电台今天广播捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会和捷克斯洛伐克政府的公告说，捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府沉痛地宣布，捷共中央政治局委员、共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基同志不幸于今晨五时（布拉格时间）逝世。
    据捷克斯洛伐克国家疗养院先后发表的公报，萨波托斯基总统从11月4日患心脏血管栓塞症以来，最初经过良好，但是昨晚病情突然恶化，经医生采取各种急救措施以后，曾暂时获得改善，今晨三时又发生严重的血液循环衰竭，无法挽救，到上午五时逝世。
    萨波托斯基享年七十三岁，早年参加革命，是捷克斯洛伐克共产党创始人之一。他的毕生的活动同捷克斯洛伐克工人运动的历史有不可分割的联系。
    新华社布拉格13日电　捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会政治局、捷克斯洛伐克政府主席团今天为萨波托斯基总统的逝世举行了紧急的联席会议，会议决定了：
    一、致捷克斯洛伐克人民的讣告；
    二、从13日起至18日举行葬礼之日为止，全国志哀。在全国志哀期间，各剧院、电影院将调整节目，宴会、体育比赛以及其他比赛将停止举行；
    三、组成萨波托斯基总统治丧委员会。委员会的主席是诺沃提尼，委员是：西罗基、巴拉克、苏普卡、普洛伊哈、斯列赫塔和斯特雷哈伊。
    新华社布拉格13日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社今晨消息，捷克斯洛伐克总统安托宁·萨波托斯基医师委员会的报告如下：
    总统的病情在12日傍晚后几小时暂时好转之后，13日上午三时情况急剧恶化。出现了无法控制的血液循环衰弱现象。共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基同志于上午五时逝世。
                              大学教授赫勒斯博士
                              大学教授约纳什
                              卡尔比西克博士
                            国家疗养院院长扎沃德尼博士
    新华社布拉格13日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，国家疗养院11月12日发表的关于萨波托斯基总统健康情况的医疗报告说，经过第一周的平稳而正常的病情发展过程，今天突然新出现了心肌不健全的情况。有心脏衰弱的病征。脉搏每分钟一二○次，正常。血压一○五——七○毫米。
    新华社布拉格13日电　中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛今天上午拜会了捷克斯洛伐克代理外交部长格里哥尔和总统办公厅主任诺瓦克，为萨波托斯基总统逝世向他们表示哀悼和慰问，并请他们代向萨波托斯基总统夫人表示哀悼和慰问。（附图片）
