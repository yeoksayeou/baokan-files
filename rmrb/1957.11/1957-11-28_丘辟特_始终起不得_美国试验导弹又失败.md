Title: "Jupiter" Still Can't Get Off the Ground: Another US Missile Test Failure
Author:
Date: 1957-11-28
Page: 6

    "Jupiter" Still Can't Get Off the Ground
    Another US Missile Test Failure
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 27th: The US Department of Defense announced that the United States failed again in testing the "Jupiter" medium-range missile at the Florida missile test range on the evening of the 26th. The US Department of Defense said: "Due to technical obstacles, the missile did not complete its intended flight."

### “丘辟特”始终起不得  美国试验导弹又失败

1957-11-28
第6版()
专栏：

    “丘辟特”始终起不得
    美国试验导弹又失败
    据新华社27日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国防部宣布，美国26日晚上在佛罗里达州导弹试验场试验“丘辟特”式中程导弹又失败了。美国国防部说：“由于技术上的障碍，导弹没有完成预定的飞行。”
