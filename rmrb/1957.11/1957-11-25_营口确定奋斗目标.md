Title: Yingkou Sets Striving Goal
Author:
Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang: Yingkou County, Liaoning Province, strives to have 90% of the county's agricultural cooperatives reach or exceed the production level of well-to-do middle peasants by next year, and all agricultural cooperatives by 1959. The county recently held a three-level cadre meeting to determine this goal. Yingkou County has seen a leap in agricultural production in the past two years. Currently, 60% of the county's agricultural cooperatives have exceeded the production level of local well-to-do middle peasants, and 20% have reached the grain production targets required by the revised draft of the National Agricultural Development Program ahead of schedule. According to preliminary plans, next year not only can 90% of the county's agricultural cooperatives exceed the production level of well-to-do middle peasants, but also the county's grain output will increase by 27.4% compared to this year.

### 营口确定奋斗目标

1957-11-25
第2版()
专栏：

    营口确定奋斗目标
    据新华社沈阳电　辽宁省营口县争取明年全县90％的农业社和1959年所有农业社都赶上或超过富裕中农的生产水平。这个县最近举行三级干部会议，确定了这个奋斗目标。营口县两年来农业生产有了飞跃的发展。目前全县已有60％的农业社超过了当地富裕中农的生产水平，已有20％的农业社提前达到了全国农业发展纲要修正草案所要求的产粮指标。据初步计划，明年全县不但可以使90％的农业社超过富裕中农的生产水平，而且全县的粮食产量将比今年提高27．4％。
