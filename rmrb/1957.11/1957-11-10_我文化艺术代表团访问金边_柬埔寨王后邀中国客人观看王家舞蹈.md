Title: My Cultural and Art Delegation Visits Phnom Penh, Cambodian Queen Invites Chinese Guests to Watch Royal Dance
Author:
Date: 1957-11-10
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh Dispatch: The Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, led by Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xilin, arrived in Phnom Penh on the 6th. The delegation is visiting at the invitation of the Royal Government of Cambodia, in return for the visit of the Cambodian Cultural and Art Delegation to China in October.

On the 6th, Pochentong Airport was filled with people. Princess Norodom Buppha Devi, Deputy Secretary of State for Education of Cambodia, Fang Bingzhen, head of the Cambodian cultural and art delegation to China, and other members of the reception committee, Secretary of State for News Tan Dong, Deputy Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs Wu Chun, and head of the Chinese Economic Delegation to Phnom Penh Ye Jinghao welcomed the Chinese artists at the airport. Many representatives of overseas Chinese also came to the airport to welcome them. According to people familiar with the situation, this is the first time that a foreign art delegation has been welcomed so warmly here.

On the evening of the 7th, the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, at the invitation of the Queen of Cambodia, attended a dance evening held in the Royal Palace.

Three Cambodian Royal dance programs were performed at the evening party. Their wonderful and beautiful performances won the Chinese artists' long and continuous applause.

On the 7th, the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation was invited to the floating palace on Tonle Sap Lake to participate in the Water Festival celebration in Cambodia.

### 我文化艺术代表团访问金边  柬埔寨王后邀中国客人观看王家舞蹈

1957-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我文化艺术代表团访问金边
    柬埔寨王后邀中国客人观看王家舞蹈
    据新华社金边电  由文化部副部长丁西林率领的中国文化艺术代表团在6日抵达金边。代表团应柬埔寨王国政府的邀请，答聘10月间访问中国的柬埔寨文化艺术代表团。
    坡成东机场6日人山人海。帕花·黛维公主和柬埔寨教育副国务秘书、柬埔寨文化艺术访华代表团团长方秉祯为首的接待委员会人员、新闻国务秘书坦东、外交副国务秘书乌春和中国驻金边经济代表团团长叶景灏在机场迎接中国艺术家们。许多华侨代表也到机场欢迎。据熟悉情况的人说，这样热烈欢迎一个外国文艺代表团，在这里还是第一次。
    中国文化艺术代表团7日晚上应柬埔寨王后的邀请，参加了在王宫中举行的一个舞蹈晚会。
    晚会演出了三个柬埔寨王家舞蹈节目。他们精彩优美的表演博得了中国艺术家们经久不息的掌声。
    7日，中国文化艺术代表团应邀到洞里湖的水上浮宫参加柬埔寨的送水节庆祝典礼。
