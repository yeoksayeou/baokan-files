Title: Premier Zhou Receives Vietnamese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-10
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Cultural Delegation this afternoon, including head Qu Huy Can, deputy head Dao Duy Ky, and members Luu Trong Lu, Bi Ngoc Bao, Nguyen Bac, Nguyen Cao Thương, Tran Van Hai, Phung Thi, Dinh Tinh, and Tran Quang Bich.

    Present at the reception were Xia Yan, Chen Zhongjing, and others.

    Nguyen Khang, Ambassador of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic to China, was also present at the reception.

    After the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai also took a photo with all members of the delegation.

    The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Cultural Delegation has been visiting Beijing for nearly three weeks and has had extensive contact with responsible personnel from our cultural departments. They will leave Beijing tomorrow to visit Shenyang, Anshan, and other places.

### 周总理接见越南文化考察团

1957-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见越南文化考察团
    新华社9日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了越南民主共和国文化考察团团长瞿辉近、副团长陶维其和团员刘仲卢、闭玉宝、阮北、阮高仓、陈文海、冯市、丁静、陈光碧。
    接见时，在座的有夏衍、陈忠经等人。
    接见时，越南民主共和国驻中国大使阮康也在座。
    接见后，周恩来总理还同考察团全体人员在一起照了像。
    越南民主共和国文化考察团已经在北京访问了近三个星期，同我国文化部门的一些负责工作人员进行了广泛的接触。他们将在明天离开北京去沈阳、鞍山等地访问。
