Title: The Soviet Film Delegation Leaves Shanghai for Hangzhou After Visit
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 14th: The head of the Soviet Film Delegation, Soviet Deputy Minister of Culture Surin, and members: Naumov, director of the film "Pavel Korchagin," and Kuznetsova, the actress in the film "High Calling," left for Hangzhou on the afternoon of the 14th after participating in the Soviet Film Week activities and visiting Shanghai.

### 苏电影代表团在上海访问后去杭州

1957-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    苏电影代表团在上海访问后去杭州
    据新华社上海14日电　苏联电影代表团团长、苏联文化部副部长苏林和团员：“保尔·柯察金”影片导演纳乌莫夫、“天职”影片的女演员库兹涅佐娃，在上海参加了苏联电影周活动和进行了参观访问以后，14日下午去杭州。
