Title: Congratulations on the First Anniversary of Egypt's Anti-Aggression Struggle: Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association and Other Organizations Send Telegrams to Nasser
Author:
Date: 1957-11-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd November – The Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association and the China Islamic Association sent a telegram to Egyptian President Nasser on the 1st, saying that the Chinese people and all Chinese Muslims express their heartfelt admiration for the great heroic spirit of the Egyptian people and the Arab nation in opposing colonialism and striving for national independence.

The Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association and the China Islamic Association said this in a telegram to President Nasser congratulating the Egyptian and Arab people on the first anniversary of their victorious struggle against the Anglo-French-Israeli aggression. The telegram also said that the Chinese people "will always firmly support this just struggle to safeguard national independence and oppose colonialism."

### 祝贺埃及反侵略斗争一周年  中埃友协等团体致电纳赛尔

1957-11-04
第2版()
专栏：

    祝贺埃及反侵略斗争一周年
    中埃友协等团体致电纳赛尔
    新华社2日讯　中埃友好协会和中国伊斯兰教协会在1日打电报给埃及总统纳赛尔说，中国人民和全中国穆斯林对埃及人民和阿拉伯民族反对殖民主义、争取民族独立的伟大英勇气概表示衷心的敬佩。
    中埃友好协会和中国伊斯兰教协会是在打电报给纳赛尔总统祝贺埃及和阿拉伯人民胜利地反对英、法、以侵略斗争一周年的时候这样说的。电报中还说，中国人民“将永远坚决支持这种维护民族独立及反殖民主义的正义斗争”。
