Title: Premier Zhou Receives Vietnamese Ha Ninh Province Ethnic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — Premier Zhou Enlai received the Ha Ninh Province Ethnic Representatives Visiting Group from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the morning of the 12th. The group included the Head of the Delegation, Shen Yizhong, Deputy Head, Lin Zhongshu, Secretary-General, Zheng Bohui, and members: Le Qi, Zhao Longgui, Wei Tinglong, Pan Wenxing, Su Tianshun, Zhang Guang Kui, and Xiang Youquan.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Wang Feng and Xie Hechou, and Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Li Chen.

Also present at the reception was the Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Nguyen Khang.

### 周总理接见越南海宁省民族参观团

1957-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见越南海宁省民族参观团
    新华社12日讯　周恩来总理在12日上午接见了越南民主共和国海宁省民族代表赴华参观团团长沈奕忠，副团长林仲书，秘书长郑伯辉，团员：黎七、赵隆贵、韦廷隆、盘文兴、苏添顺、张光奎、项有全。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理陈毅，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、谢鹤筹，外交部亚洲司副司长李晨。
    接见时，越南民主共和国驻中国大使阮康也在座。
