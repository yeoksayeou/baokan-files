Title: Declaration of Fraternal Parties Congress; Soviet Publication of Separate Edition
Author:
Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 24th - The Soviet State Political Literature Publishing House has published a booklet containing the full texts of the "Declaration of the Meeting of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of Socialist Countries" and the "Peace Manifesto."
The booklet also contains the communique of the meeting of representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of Socialist countries, as well as the communique of the meeting of Communist and Workers' Parties.

### 兄弟党代表会议宣言  苏联出版单行本

1957-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    兄弟党代表会议宣言
    苏联出版单行本
    新华社莫斯科24日电　苏联国家政治书籍出版局出版了一本收集有“社会主义国家共产党和工人党代表会议宣言”和“和平宣言”全文的小册子。
    小册子中还刊载了社会主义国家共产党和工人党代表会议公报以及共产党和工人党代表会议公报。
