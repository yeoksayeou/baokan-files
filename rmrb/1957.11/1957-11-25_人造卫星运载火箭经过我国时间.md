Title: Time When the Artificial Satellite Carrier Rocket Passes Through China

Author:
Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, November 24th dispatch: TASS reported that the Soviet Union's first artificial satellite's carrier rocket will pass through China on the 24th, over areas including Changchun, Shenyang, Xi'an, Wuhan, and Lhasa.

The first artificial satellite's carrier rocket will fly from Chita, Soviet Union, at 17:18 Beijing time on the 24th, pass Changchun at 17:20, Shenyang at 17:21, Xi'an at 18:55, Wuhan at 18:56, Lhasa at 20:27, and reach Bandung, Indonesia at 20:37.

### 人造卫星运载火箭经过我国时间

1957-11-25
第8版()
专栏：

    人造卫星运载火箭经过我国时间
    据新华社24日讯　据塔斯社讯：苏联第一个人造卫星的运载火箭24日将经过我国的长春、沈阳、西安、武汉、拉萨等地。
    第一个人造卫星的运载火箭24日北京时间十七点十八分从苏联的赤塔飞来，十七点二十分过长春，十七点二十一分过沈阳，十八点五十五分过西安，十八点五十六分过武汉，二十点二十七分过拉萨，二十点三十七分到印度尼西亚的万隆。
