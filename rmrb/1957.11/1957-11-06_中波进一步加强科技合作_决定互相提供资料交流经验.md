Title: China and Poland Further Strengthen Scientific and Technological Cooperation, Decide to Provide Each Other with Information and Exchange Experience
Author:
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. November.　Communique of the Fourth Session of the Joint Standing Committee for Technical and Scientific-Technical Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Poland.

The fourth session of the Joint Standing Committee for Technical and Scientific-Technical Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Poland concluded in Beijing on November 4th. The meeting proceeded in an atmosphere of friendly cooperation.

The committee discussed the implementation of the resolutions of past sessions and considered that both sides had fulfilled their obligations well.

In addition, a series of resolutions were adopted regarding the mutual provision of technical and scientific-technical experience in the future.

According to the provisions of the signed protocol: In 1958, the People's Republic of China will provide the People's Republic of Poland with technical data on the petroleum industry, food industry, and metal analysis. At the same time, it will receive Polish experts to inspect technical achievements in the chemical industry, geology, refractory materials, and food industry. During this period, the People's Republic of Poland will provide the People's Republic of China with design and research data on the chemical industry, as well as technical data on construction, shipbuilding, coal, light industry, and other sectors of the national economy. Simultaneously, it will receive Chinese experts and interns to inspect technical achievements in non-ferrous metals, chemical, and forestry industries. Furthermore, it will dispatch experts to China to provide technical assistance in antibiotics and metallurgy. According to the committee's resolution, the two countries will conduct direct scientific and technological cooperation between relevant ministries and scientific research institutions.

Zhu Lizhi, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the People's Republic of China, signed the protocol on behalf of the People's Republic of China. Z. Koi, Chairman of the Polish Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the People's Republic of Poland, signed the protocol on behalf of the People's Republic of Poland.

### 中波进一步加强科技合作  决定互相提供资料交流经验

1957-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    中波进一步加强科技合作
    决定互相提供资料交流经验
    新华社5日讯　中华人民共和国和波兰人民共和国技术和科学技术合作联合常任委员会会议公报
    中波技术和科学技术合作联合常任委员会第四届会议于11月4日在北京闭幕。会议是在充满友好合作的气氛中进行的。
    委员会讨论了过去几届会议的决议执行情况，并认为双方已经很好地完成了自己的义务。
    此外，并通过了一系列的有关今后互相提供技术和科学技术经验的决议。
    根据所签订的议定书中规定：中华人民共和国在1958年将供给波兰人民共和国石油工业、食品工业、金属分析方面的技术资料，同时并接待波兰专家考察化学工业、地质、耐火材料和食品工业方面的技术成就；波兰人民共和国在这个期间将供给中华人民共和国化学工业的设计研究资料，供给建筑、造船、煤炭、轻工业方面和国民经济其他部门的技术资料，同时并接待中国专家和实习生考察有色金属、化学、森林工业方面的技术成就，此外，并派遣专家到中国在抗生素和冶金方面给予技术援助。根据委员会决议规定：两国在相应的部和科学研究机构之间将进行直接的科学技术合作。
    代表中华人民共和国方面在议定书上签字的是委员会中国组主席、中华人民共和国交通部副部长朱理冶，代表波兰人民共和国方面在议定书上签字的是委员会波兰组主席、波兰人民共和国重工业部副部长兹·科伊。
