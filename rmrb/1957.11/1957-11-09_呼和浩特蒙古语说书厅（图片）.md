Title: Hohhot Mongolian Storytelling Hall (Picture)
Author: Baoyin Chaoketu
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 8

    Hohhot Mongolian Storytelling Hall
    A Mongolian-style yurt storytelling hall in Mongolian has been completed in Hohhot, with an area of more than 300 square meters. This storytelling hall makes the cultural life of the Mongolian people more active.
    The picture shows the exterior view of the magnificent Mongolian storytelling hall and the famous Mongolian old artist Mao Yihan telling stories in the newly built storytelling hall.
      Xinhua News Agency reporter Baoyin Chaoketu

### 呼和浩特蒙古语说书厅（图片）
宝音朝克图
1957-11-09
第8版()
专栏：

    呼和浩特蒙古语说书厅
    一座蒙古包形式的蒙古语说书厅已经在呼和浩特市落成，它的面积有三百多平方公尺。这座说书厅使蒙古族人民的文化生活更加活跃。
    图为金碧辉煌的蒙古语说书厅外景及著名的蒙古族老艺人毛依罕在新建的说书厅里说书。
      新华社记者　宝音朝克图摄
