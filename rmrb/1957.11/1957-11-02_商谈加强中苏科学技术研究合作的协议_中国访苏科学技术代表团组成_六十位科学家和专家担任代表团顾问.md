Title: Negotiations on Agreement to Strengthen Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Research Cooperation; Formation of Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union; Sixty Scientists and Experts Serve as Delegation Advisors
Author:
Date: 1957-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The People's Republic of China Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union has been formed, and most of the members have arrived in Moscow in batches.

The tasks of the delegation are: to solicit the opinions of Soviet scientists on China's twelve-year long-term plan for scientific and technological development; to discuss with the Soviet government an agreement to further strengthen scientific and technological research cooperation between China and the Soviet Union.

The head of the Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union is Guo Moruo, and the members of the delegation are as follows (in order of the number of strokes in the surname): Wang Xinyuan, Liu Xiyao, Liu Bin, Wang Daohan, Du Runsheng, Zhu Kezhen, Zhou Peiyuan, Zhou Rongxin, Fan Changjiang, Xu Jie, Huang Songling, Zhang Yufan, Yang Shixian, Yang Xiandong, Qian Xinzhong.
Secretary-General: Fan Changjiang
Deputy Secretary-Generals: Du Runsheng, Wang Shuntong

Sixty scientists and experts from various fields serve as advisors to the delegation. They are: Ma Rongzhi, Zhao Jiuzhang, Lu Wu, Hou Defeng, Cheng Yuqi, Sun Hongru, Wang Zhizhi, Li Xun, Chen Da, Liu Fang, Qin Tongluo, Zhang Dayu, Hou Xianglin, Weng Wenbo, Chai Huazhou, Wang Zhuquan, Liu Wanxi, Jin Shiqu, Chu Yinghuang, Wu Zhonghua, Tao Hengxian, Zhang Zuomei, Ding Shunnian, Wang Daheng, Lin Hua, Wang Dexi, Wang Baoren, Wang Dajun, Deng Encheng, Zhang Zilin, Zhang Guangdou, Zhang Changling, Chen Lingfeng, Zhang Naifeng, Dai Songen, Zhu Shuping, Huang Jiasi, Zhang Changshao, Zhang Xiaoqian, Bai Min, Xia Jianbai, Yin Zijia, Zhang Wenzhi, Zhou Mai, Zhang Zhao, Hou Zhibu, Shi Ruwei, Wang Zhuxi, Wu Xuezhu, Lu Jiaxi, Bei Shizhang, Feng Depei, Lin Rong, Xu Zhuoran, Ge Chunlin, Ge Mingsong, Qian Wenji, Min Naida, Luo Peilin, Huang Wuhan.

Thirty-two members of the delegation and advisors are members of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 商谈加强中苏科学技术研究合作的协议  中国访苏科学技术代表团组成  六十位科学家和专家担任代表团顾问

1957-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    商谈加强中苏科学技术研究合作的协议
    中国访苏科学技术代表团组成
    六十位科学家和专家担任代表团顾问
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国访苏科学技术代表团已经组成，大部分成员已经分批到达莫斯科。
    代表团的任务是：征求苏联科学家对我国十二年科学技术发展远景规划的意见；同苏联政府商谈进一步加强中苏两国科学技术研究合作的协议。
    访苏科学技术代表团由郭沫若任团长，团员如下（以姓氏笔划为序）：王新元　刘西尧　刘　彬　汪道涵　杜润生竺可桢　周培源　周荣鑫　范长江　许　杰黄松龄　张雨帆　杨石先　杨显东　钱信忠秘书长：范长江副秘书长：杜润生、王顺桐
