Title: Soviet Delegations from All Walks of Life Arrive in Beijing to Participate in Chinese People's Celebrations of the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution; Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Hosts a Banquet for All Delegation Members
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 7th November: Soviet delegations from all walks of life participating in the Chinese people's celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution arrived in Beijing from Moscow by jet aircraft at noon on the 7th. The delegation of thirteen people was headed by Andreyev, member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association. Members of the delegation included participants in the October Revolution, workers, collective farm chairmen, scientists, writers, composers, and trade union and Communist Youth League workers.

Wu Yuzhang, Deputy Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese People's Celebrations for the 40th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution and Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered a welcome speech at the airport, thanking the Soviet delegations from all walks of life for bringing the warm friendship of the fraternal Soviet people to the Chinese people. Wu Yuzhang said that we are pleased to see that among the Soviet delegations from all walks of life, there are outstanding representatives from all walks of life in the Soviet Union, especially the old Bolsheviks who participated in the October Revolution, whose personal experiences will greatly inspire the Chinese people.

In his speech, delegation leader Andreyev conveyed warm, fraternal greetings from the Soviet people to the Chinese people. Andreyev said that for forty years, the people of all nationalities in the Soviet Union, the world's first socialist worker-peasant state, have often felt the fraternal friendship and support of the Chinese people. The glorious sons of the Chinese people once took up arms to participate in the October Revolution and defended the great achievements of the Russian proletariat. The friendship between our two peoples was established and consolidated in the struggle to realize the great ideals of the October Revolution, and this friendship is eternal and unbreakable. Andreyev concluded by wishing the Chinese people further success in the struggle for peace, progress, and socialism under the leadership of the glorious Communist Party.

Also present at the airport today were Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, Li Jishen, Chen Shutong, Bao Erhan, Shi Liang, Zhang Wentian, Li Zhuchen, Liao Chengzhi, Cao Mengjun, Cai Tingkai, Liu Ren, Zhang Youyu, and Le Songsheng, Vice Presidents and Directors of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Gutikov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, and embassy staff members also came to the airport to welcome the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th November: Wu Yuzhang, Deputy Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese People's Celebrations for the 40th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution and Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, held a banquet at noon on the 7th to welcome Andreyev, head of the Soviet delegations from all walks of life participating in the Chinese people's celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, and all delegation members. Gutikov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

In his speech at the banquet, Wu Yuzhang said that the Chinese people have always held feelings of respect, love, and gratitude towards the Soviet Union. These feelings are particularly evident in the upsurge of celebrating the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. The delegation will personally witness this feeling of the Chinese people, see the Chinese people's firm determination to be forever united with the Soviet people, and see the Chinese people's enthusiasm in earnestly studying the Soviet experience in construction. At the same time, through the delegation's extensive contact with the Chinese working people, it will surely make a great contribution to strengthening the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Soviet peoples. Wu Yuzhang proposed a toast to the 40th anniversary of the great October Revolution and to the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples.

In his speech, Andreyev thanked them for their warm reception, saying that friendship is the most beautiful and sublime feeling, and friendship can bring great strength. The friendship that unites our two peoples, China and the Soviet Union, is a great force. Andreyev proposed a toast to the noble activities of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and to the unbreakable great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples.

Present at the banquet today were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Liao Chengzhi, Shao Lizi, and Li Zhuchen, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, members of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese People's Celebrations for the 40th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, and leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations in China, and people from all walks of life. ### 参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念活动  苏联各界代表团到京  中苏友协总会副会长吴玉章欢宴全体团员

1957-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

    参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念活动
    苏联各界代表团到京
    中苏友协总会副会长吴玉章欢宴全体团员
    新华社7日讯　苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团7日中午乘喷气客机从莫斯科到达北京。代表团一行十三人由苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员、苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫担任团长，团员中有十月革命的参加者、工人、集体农庄主席、科学家、作家、作曲家、工会和共青团工作者。
    中国人民庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年筹备委员会副主任委员、中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章在机场致欢迎词，感谢苏联各界代表团给中国人民带来了兄弟的苏联人民的热烈的友情。吴玉章说，我们感到高兴的是，在苏联各界代表团中，有苏联各界的优秀的代表人物，特别是其中的参加十月革命的老布尔什维克，他们的亲身经历，将给中国人民以极大鼓舞。
    安德烈耶夫团长接着讲话，他代表苏联人民向中国人民致以热烈的兄弟般的敬礼。安德烈耶夫说，四十年以来，世界上第一个社会主义工农国家的苏联各族人民经常体会到中国人民的兄弟友谊和支持。中国人民的光荣儿子曾经拿起武器来参加十月革命并且保卫俄国无产阶级的伟大成果。我们两国人民的友谊是在为实现十月革命的伟大理想的斗争中建立和巩固起来的，这种友谊是永远的、牢不可破的。安德烈耶夫最后祝中国人民在光荣的共产党的领导下，在为和平、进步和社会主义的斗争中获得进一步的成功。
    今天到机场欢迎的，还有中共中央政治局委员彭真，国务院副总理陈毅，中苏友好协会总会副会长和理事、首都各界人士李济深、陈叔通、包尔汉、史良、张闻天、李烛尘、廖承志、曹孟君、蔡廷锴、刘仁、张友渔、乐松生等人。
    苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办顾悌克夫和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    新华社7日讯　中国人民庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年筹备委员会副主任委员、中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章7日中午举行宴会，欢迎苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团团长安德烈耶夫和全体团员。苏联驻华大使馆临时代办顾悌克夫也应邀出席了宴会。
    吴玉章在宴会上致词说，中国人民对苏联一贯地抱着尊敬、爱戴和感激的心情。这种心情在庆祝十月革命四十周年的热潮中特别明显地表现出来。代表团将会亲眼看到中国人民的这种感情，看到中国人民坚定不移地同苏联人民永远团结一致的决心，看到中国人民认真地努力学习苏联建设经验的热情。同时，通过代表团同中国劳动人民的广泛接触，一定会对加强中苏两国人民的友好和团结作出巨大的贡献。吴玉章提议为伟大的十月革命四十周年，为中苏两国人民的牢不可破的友谊干杯。
    安德烈耶夫讲话感谢对他们的热情接待，他说，友谊是最美好最崇高的感情，友谊可以带来伟大的力量。把我们中苏两国人民团结在一起的友谊就是一种伟大的力量。安德烈耶夫提议为中苏友好协会崇高的活动，为中苏两国人民牢不可破的伟大友谊干杯。
    出席今天宴会的，有国务院副总理陈毅，中苏友好协会总会副会长李济深、黄炎培、廖承志、邵力子、李烛尘，中国人民庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年筹备委员会委员以及中国各民主党派，各人民团体的负责人和社会各界人士。（附图片）
    中共中央领导人接见苏联各界代表团时合影。前排左起第六人是团长安德烈耶夫、左起第四人是苏联驻华大使馆临时代办顾悌克夫。
