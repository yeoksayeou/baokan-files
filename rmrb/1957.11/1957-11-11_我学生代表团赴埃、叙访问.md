Title: My Student Delegation Visits Egypt and Syria
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-11
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Chinese student delegation, consisting of six members and led by delegation leader Zhang Ding, left Beijing by plane on the 10th for Damascus to visit Egypt and Syria in accordance with the Sino-Egyptian Cultural Cooperation Agreement and the Sino-Syrian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 我学生代表团赴埃、叙访问

1957-11-11
第2版()
专栏：

    我学生代表团赴埃、叙访问
    新华社10日讯　执行中埃文化合作协定、中叙文化合作协定赴埃及和叙利亚访问的中国学生代表团一行六人，在团长张定率领下于10日乘机离京赴大马士革。
