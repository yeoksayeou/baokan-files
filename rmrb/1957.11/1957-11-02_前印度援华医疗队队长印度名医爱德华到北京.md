Title: Former Head of Indian Medical Aid Team to China, Renowned Indian Doctor Edward, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: Edward, the former head of the Indian Medical Aid Team to China, and a renowned Indian doctor, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of October 31st at the invitation of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association. Edward is a famous Indian doctor and vice chairman of the All India and [unclear].

Those who went to the airport to greet him included Wu Huazhi, Secretary-General of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Xu Baoju from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Wei Yizhai, Deputy Director of the Education Department of the Ministry of Health, and others.

### 前印度援华医疗队队长印度名医爱德华到北京

1957-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    前印度援华医疗队队长印度名医爱德华到北京
    新华社1日讯　应中印友好协会邀请来我国访问的前印度援华医疗队队长爱德华在10月31日下午到达北京。爱德华是印度名医生，全印和大副主席。
    前往机场迎接的有中印友好协会总干事吴化之、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会许宝驹、卫生部教育司副司长魏一斋等。
