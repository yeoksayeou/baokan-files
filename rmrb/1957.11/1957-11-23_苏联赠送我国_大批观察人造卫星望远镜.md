Title: The Soviet Union Donates a Large Number of Telescopes for Observing Artificial Satellites to China
Author:
Date: 1957-11-23
Page: 8

News Common Knowledge

    The Soviet Union Donates a Large Number of Telescopes for Observing Artificial Satellites to China

    Xinhua News Agency: The Astronomical Committee of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has donated 165 wide-angle telescopes (11-degree field of view) to China to facilitate our observation of the artificial Earth satellites launched by the Soviet Union. One hundred of these have already been airlifted to Beijing today. The Chinese Academy of Sciences will soon distribute these instruments to observation stations in Beijing, Nanjing, Lanzhou, Kunming, Lhasa, Guangzhou, Xi'an, and other locations.

### 苏联赠送我国  大批观察人造卫星望远镜

1957-11-23
第8版()
专栏：新闻常识

    苏联赠送我国
    大批观察人造卫星望远镜
    新华社讯　苏联科学院天文委员会赠我一百六十五架广角望远镜（视野十一度），以便我们观察苏联发射的人造地球卫星。其中一百架已在今日空运到北京。中国科学院即将把这批仪器分送给北京、南京、兰州、昆明、拉萨、广州、西安等观察站。
