Title: Our Military Friendship Delegation to the Soviet Union Departs for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-11-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th. At the invitation of the Soviet Ministry of Defense, the Military Friendship Delegation of the People's Republic of China left Beijing at 8:00 this morning aboard a jet aircraft for a friendly visit to the Soviet Union.
    The head of the Military Friendship Delegation to the Soviet Union is Marshal Peng Dehuai (already in the Soviet Union), and the deputy head is Marshal Ye Jianying. The members are: General Su Yu, General Tan Zheng, Admiral Xiao Jingguang, General Xu Guangda, General Liu Yalou of the Air Force, General Hong Xuezhi, General Chen Xilian, General Liu Zhen of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong.
    Among those who saw the delegation off at the airport were Senior Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Huang Kecheng, General Chen Geng, General Li Da, General Zhang Zongxun, General Xiao Hua, General Xiao Ke, General Gan Siqi, General Peng Shaohui, General Chen Shiju, and General Yang Chengwu.
    Also seeing the delegation off at the airport was Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zeng Yongquan.
    Military attachés from various countries stationed in China also saw the delegation off at the airport.
    On the afternoon of the 5th, Premier Zhou Enlai received all the members of the Military Friendship Delegation to the Soviet Union.

### 我军事友好访苏代表团赴苏

1957-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    我军事友好访苏代表团赴苏
    新华社6日讯　应苏联国防部的邀请，中华人民共和国军事友好访苏代表团于今天上午八时乘喷气式专机离开北京去苏联进行友好访问。
    军事友好访苏代表团的团长是彭德怀元帅（已在苏联），副团长是叶剑英元帅。团员有：粟裕大将、谭政大将、萧劲光海军大将、许光达大将、刘亚楼空军上将、洪学智上将、陈锡联上将、刘震空军上将、刘志坚中将、萧向荣中将。
    到机场送行的有黄克诚大将、陈赓大将、李达上将、张宗逊上将、萧华上将、萧克上将、甘泗淇上将、彭绍辉上将、陈士榘上将、杨成武上将等中国人民解放军高级将领。
    到机场送行的还有外交部副部长曾涌泉。
    各国驻华武官也到机场送行。
    5日下午，周恩来总理接见了军事友好访苏代表团的全体人员。
