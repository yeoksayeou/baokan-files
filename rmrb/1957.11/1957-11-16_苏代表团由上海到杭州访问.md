Title: Soviet Delegation Visits Hangzhou from Shanghai
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, November 15th: The Soviet delegation to China, led by Andrei Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, which is participating in the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, arrived in Hangzhou by special train from Shanghai at 9:00 a.m. on the 15th. Accompanying the delegation were Liao Chengzhi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Cao Mengjun, Director, and Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the station today included Huo Shilian, Deputy Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Deputy Governor of Zhejiang Province, and President of the Zhejiang Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Wu Xian, First Secretary of the Hangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Mayor of Hangzhou, and President of the Hangzhou Municipal Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

Today, the guests toured West Hill Park and Pinghu Qiuyue, and visited the "Spraying Fish Wash" and the Hangzhou 8th Chrysanthemum Exhibition.

In the afternoon, the guests also visited the famous Meijiawu October Tea Production Cooperative in the suburbs of Hangzhou, visited the cooperative's tea frying workshop, and visited the family of cooperative member Zheng Xinye.

In the evening, Huo Shilian, President of the Zhejiang Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Wu Xian, President of the Hangzhou Municipal Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, hosted a banquet at Lou Wai Lou to entertain the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 14th: Ke Qingshi, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and President of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, hosted a banquet at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building on the evening of the 14th to bid farewell to the Soviet delegation to China, led by Andrei Andreyev, which is participating in the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, which is about to leave Shanghai.

### 苏代表团由上海到杭州访问

1957-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    苏代表团由上海到杭州访问
    新华社杭州15日电　以苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫为首的苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团，15日上午九时从上海乘专车到达杭州。陪同代表团前来的有中苏友好协会总会副会长廖承志、理事曹孟君和副秘书长戈宝权。
    今天到车站欢迎的有中共浙江省委副书记、浙江省副省长、浙江省中苏友好协会会长霍士廉，中共杭州市委第一书记、杭州市市长、杭州市中苏友好协会会长吴宪等。
    客人们今天在杭州游览了西山公园、平湖秋月，参观了“喷水鱼洗”和杭州市第八届菊花展览会。
    下午，客人们还访问了杭州市郊区著名的梅家坞十月茶叶生产合作社，参观了这个社的炒茶工场，访问了社员郑鑫业的一家。
    晚间，浙江省中苏友好协会会长霍士廉及杭州市中苏友好协会会长吴宪在楼外楼设宴招待贵宾。
    据新华社上海14日电　中共上海市委员会第一书记、上海市中苏友好协会会长柯庆施14日晚在中苏友好大厦设宴欢送即将离开上海的以安德烈耶夫为首的苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团。
