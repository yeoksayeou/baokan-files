Title: Immortal Glory to the Martyrs of the October Revolution: Moscow Mass Rally Commemorates Heroes
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 11th: According to TASS Moscow, veterans who participated in the October Revolution, as well as representatives from Moscow's factories, Party organizations, and social groups, held a rally in Red Square on the 10th to commemorate the heroes who sacrificed themselves in the October 1917 battles and laid wreaths at the martyrs' tombs.

    Orlov, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the CPSU, delivered the opening speech. He said: The people of the entire Soviet Union are commemorating with reverence the heroes who sacrificed themselves in the struggle for the victory of the great Marxist-Leninist ideology and the happy life of the working people. The images of the heroes will live on forever, just like the great revolutionary cause for which they sacrificed themselves.

    Representatives of veteran revolutionaries, the working people of Moscow, and young workers also spoke at the mass rally.

### 十月革命烈士永垂不朽  莫斯科群众集会悼英雄

1957-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    十月革命烈士永垂不朽
    莫斯科群众集会悼英雄
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：曾参加过十月革命的老战士以及莫斯科的工厂、党组织和社会团体的代表，10日在红场举行大会，追悼1917年10月战斗中牺牲的英雄，并且向烈士墓献了花圈。
    苏共莫斯科市委员会书记奥尔洛夫致开幕词。他说：全苏联人民都怀着虔诚的心情追悼在争取伟大的马克思列宁主义思想胜利和劳动人民幸福生活的斗争中牺牲的英雄们。英雄们的形象将像他们为之牺牲的伟大革命事业一样永世长存。
    在群众大会上讲话的还有革命老战士、莫斯科劳动人民和青年工人的代表。
