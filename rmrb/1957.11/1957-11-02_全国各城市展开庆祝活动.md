Title: Celebrations Unfold in Cities Across the Nation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency: Celebrations for the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution are being widely展开ed in major, medium, and small cities across the country.

    Posters, exhibition galleries, celebratory slogans in shop windows, and various reports and exhibitions have created a festive atmosphere in major, medium, and small cities across the country. Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Xi'an, Lanzhou, Changchun, Chengdu, Kunming, Changsha, Jinan, Hohhot, Nanjing, Xining, Hangzhou, Harbin, Fuzhou, Guiyang, Taiyuan, Luoyang, Hefei, Lüda, and other places have all held various reports, exhibitions, and other celebrations. Gui Linqi, Secretary of the Anhui Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; Ma Jikong, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee; Li Erzhong, Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee; Chen Puru, Deputy Governor of Guizhou Province, and other Party and government leaders, have all given reports to cadres and the masses on the October Revolution and the achievements of Soviet construction. In Beijing, 400,000 people have already heard reports on the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution and participated in various celebrations. Yunnan Province and Kunming City have held more than 40 reports since mid-October, with more than 60,000 people of all ethnic groups and officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army listening to the reports. In Hohhot, 3,000 Mongolian, Han, and Hui speakers have gone deep into factories, mines, construction sites, and street organizations, using symposiums, discussion groups, and other methods to展开宣传活动 among the masses. More than 300 speakers in Guiyang City have already given reports on celebrating the October Revolution to 100,000 citizens. More than 600 speakers in Nanjing have also gone to various units to hold reports, and it is estimated that 400,000 people will hear reports on the October Revolution and the achievements of Soviet construction before and after the festival.

    The Soviet Union's launch of the first artificial earth satellite and other great achievements in science and technology have aroused great interest among the people of the whole country. Reports on this aspect have been warmly welcomed by audiences everywhere. The Shanghai Science and Technology Popularization Association has recently been holding more than a dozen reports every night to introduce artificial satellites and Soviet scientific and technological achievements. According to statistics from six cities, Kunming, Chongqing, Lanzhou, Guangzhou, Guiyang, and Hohhot, 110,000 people have already listened to special reports in this area. In Kunming, 26 professors and scientists have recently given 37 special reports to people of all ethnic groups on the achievements of Soviet science and technology, with an audience of more than 50,000 people. In Chongqing, more than 20,000 people have listened to reports on artificial satellites, and more than 40 units, including the Shizitan Hydropower Station, have specifically invited the Municipal Science and Technology Popularization Association to give reports in this area to their units before and after the festival. Engineering and technical personnel from the First Design Institute and the First Engineering Bureau of the Ministry of Railways in Lanzhou also invited Soviet experts, who are helping China build the Lanzhou-Urumqi-Aktogay railway, to report to everyone on the achievements of Soviet construction.

    All localities have paid attention to using various ways to conduct celebrations in a lively and active manner. The grassroots trade unions of more than 100 factories and mining enterprises in Qinghai Province will use wired broadcasting, blackboards, big-character posters, and propaganda cards to宣传活动 to employees. Taiyuan City will hold a poetry recital related to the October Revolution. Students from Dalian Institute of Technology launched a symbolic long-distance race from Dalian to Moscow, and Dalian Foreign Languages College held an essay contest among students on Sino-Soviet friendship.

### 全国各城市展开庆祝活动

1957-11-02
第3版()
专栏：

    全国各城市展开庆祝活动
    据新华社讯　全国各大、中、小城市中，正在广泛展开庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年的活动。
    招贴画、展览画廊、商店橱窗里的庆祝标语和各种报告会、展览会，使全国其他各大、中、小城市中出现了一片节日的景象。北京、上海、天津、沈阳、武汉、广州、重庆、西安、兰州、长春、成都、昆明、长沙、济南、呼和浩特、南京、西宁、杭州、哈尔滨、福州、贵阳、太原、洛阳、合肥、旅大等地，都举行了各种报告会、展览会和其他庆祝活动。中共安徽省委书记桂林栖、云南省委书记马继孔、武汉市委书记李尔重、贵州省副省长陈璞如等党、政负责人，都向干部、群众作了有关十月革命节和苏联建设成就的报告。北京市已有四十万人听到了关于十月社会主义革命四十周年的报告和参加了各种庆祝活动。云南省和昆明市从10月中旬起已经举行了四十多次报告会，有六万多名各族人民和人民解放军官兵听了报告。呼和浩特市有三千名蒙古、汉、回族报告员，深入到各厂矿、建筑工地和街道组织，运用座谈会、讨论会等方式，向群众展开宣传活动。贵阳市的三百多个报告员，已经向十万市民作了庆祝十月革命节的报告。南京市的六百多名报告员也已经分别深入各单位举行报告会，估计将有四十万人在节日前后听到有关十月革命节和苏联建设成就的报告。
    苏联发射的第一颗人造地球卫星和苏联在科学技术上的其他伟大成就引起了全国人民的极大兴趣。有关这一方面的报告受到了各地听众的热烈欢迎。上海市科学技术普及协会最近每天晚上都要举行十多个报告会，介绍人造卫星和苏联科学技术上的成就。据昆明、重庆、兰州、广州、贵阳、呼和浩特六个城市统计，已经有十一万人听了这一方面的专题报告。昆明市有二十六位教授和科学家最近向各族人民作了三十七次介绍苏联科学技术成就的专题报告，听众已达五万多人。重庆市已经有二万多人听了关于人造卫星的报告，狮子滩水电站等四十多个单位还专门约请市科学技术普及协会在节日前后到本单位去作这一方面的报告。兰州市的铁道部第一设计院和第一工程局的工程技术人员，还邀请帮助我国建筑兰州—乌鲁木齐—阿克斗卡铁路的苏联专家向大家报告苏联的建设成就。
    各地都注意运用各种方式生动活泼地来进行庆祝活动。青海全省一百几十个厂矿企业的基层工会将运用有线广播、黑板报、大字报、鼓动牌等来向职工进行宣传。太原市将要举办有关十月革命节的诗歌朗诵会。大连工学院的学生们发起了大连—莫斯科的象征性长跑，大连外国语专科学校在学生中举办了中苏友好征文。
