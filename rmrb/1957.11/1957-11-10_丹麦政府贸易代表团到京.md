Title: Danish Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-10
Page: 2

    Danish Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th November. The Danish government trade delegation, headed by Count Knud-Vinterfeldt, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 9th. This is the first Danish government trade delegation to visit our country. During its visit to our country, the delegation will discuss trade agreements between the two governments with relevant Chinese departments.
    Zhu Jianbai, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others welcomed the delegation at the airport. Personnel from the Danish Embassy in China also greeted them at the airport.

### 丹麦政府贸易代表团到京

1957-11-10
第2版()
专栏：

    丹麦政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社9日讯　以克努—温特番尔特伯爵为首的丹麦政府贸易代表团一行六人，9日下午乘飞机到达北京。这是来我国访问的第一个丹麦政府贸易代表团。代表团在我国访问期间，将同我国有关方面商谈两国政府间的贸易协定问题。
    到机场欢迎的，有对外贸易部第三局副局长朱剑白等人。丹麦驻我国大使馆人员也到机场迎接。
