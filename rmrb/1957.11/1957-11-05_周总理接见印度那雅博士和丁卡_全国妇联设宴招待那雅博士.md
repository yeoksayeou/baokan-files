Title: Premier Zhou Receives Indian Dr. Nayar and Dinkar; All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Dr. Nayar
Author:
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Vice-President of the All-India Village Women's Federation, Dr. Sushila Nayar, and the Indian poet Ramdhari Dinkar this afternoon.
    Also present at the reception were Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, and the poet Li Ji.
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The All-China Women's Federation hosted a banquet on the evening of the 4th in honor of Dr. Sushila Nayar, Vice-President of the All-India Village Women's Federation. Among those present at the banquet were Shi Liang, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, members of the Presidium, and heads of various departments.
    Dr. Sushila Nayar arrived in Beijing on October 30th at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation.

### 周总理接见印度那雅博士和丁卡  全国妇联设宴招待那雅博士

1957-11-05
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印度那雅博士和丁卡
    全国妇联设宴招待那雅博士
    新华社4日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了印度全国乡村妇女联合会副主席苏希拉·那雅博士，印度诗人拉姆德哈利·丁卡。
    接见时在座的，有全国妇联书记处书记曹孟君和诗人李季。
    新华社4日讯　全国妇联4日晚设宴招待印度全国乡村妇女联合会副主席苏希拉·那雅博士。出席宴会的有全国妇联副主席史良和主席团委员、各部门负责人等。
    苏希拉·那雅博士是应全国妇联邀请来我国访问，在10月30日到达北京的。
