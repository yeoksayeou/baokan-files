Title: China First Machine-Building Trade Union Delegation Leaves for Japan

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: The China First Machine-Building Trade Union delegation, at the joint invitation of the All-Japan Shipbuilding Labor Union and the Japan Vehicle Industry Labor Union Federation, departed from Beijing by plane on the morning of the 11th for a visit to Japan. The head of the delegation is Li Yunchuan, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the China First Machine-Building Trade Union.

### 中国第一机械工会  访日代表团启程

1957-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    中国第一机械工会
    访日代表团启程
    新华社11日讯　中国第一机械工会访日代表团应全日本造船劳动组合和日本车辆产业劳动组合联合会的联合邀请，11日早晨由北京乘飞机去日本访问。代表团团长是中国第一机械工会全国委员会副主席李云川。
