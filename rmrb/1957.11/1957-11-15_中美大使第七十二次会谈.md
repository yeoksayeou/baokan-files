Title: The Seventy-Second Sino-US Ambassadorial Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-11-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 14th -- The Chinese and American ambassadors held their seventy-second meeting here today at the Palace of Nations. The meeting began at 10:00 AM and ended at 11:00 AM. The next meeting is scheduled for December 12th.

### 中美大使第七十二次会谈

1957-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使第七十二次会谈
    新华社日内瓦14日电　中美两国大使今天在这里的国联大厦举行了第七十二次会谈。会谈十点钟开始，十一点结束。下次会谈订在12月12日举行。
