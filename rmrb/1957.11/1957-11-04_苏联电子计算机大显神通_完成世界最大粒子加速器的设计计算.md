Title: Soviet Electronic Computers Show Great Power: Completing the Design Calculations for the World's Largest Particle Accelerator
Author:
Date: 1957-11-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd dispatch from TASS: The calculations required in the design process of the world's largest particle accelerator, which is being designed in the Soviet Union, have been completed. The calculations for this accelerator, which can accelerate particles to an energy of 50 billion electron volts, were performed by a Soviet-made "M-2" type computer.

This computer is installed in the Soviet Institute of Control Machines and Control Systems. It can perform calculations according to dozens of engineering plans and then select the best design. One such computer can replace the labor of 10,000 calculators. Soviet scientists also used this computer to calculate natural gas extraction methods.

The director of the institute, Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Mr. Brook, said that this machine can also be used to control combined power systems. A program is currently being developed to enable the machine to perform this task. According to the most conservative estimates, using a computer to control a combined power system can save hundreds of millions of rubles each year.

### 苏联电子计算机大显神通  完成世界最大粒子加速器的设计计算

1957-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联电子计算机大显神通
    完成世界最大粒子加速器的设计计算
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联正在设计的世界上最大的粒子加速器在设计过程中所需的计算已经完成。这个能把粒子加速到具有五百亿电子伏特能量的加速器的计算工作，是由苏联制的“M—2”型计算机进行的。
    这台计算机安装在苏联控制机器和控制系统实验所内。它可以按照一项工程的几十种方案进行计算，然后选择出一种最好的设计方案。一台这样的计算机就可以代替一万名计算员的劳动。苏联科学家们还用这台计算机进行了天然煤气开采方法的计算。
    这个实验所的主任、苏联科学院通讯院士勃鲁克说，这种机器还可以用来操纵联合动力系统。现在正在编制使这种机器从事此项工作所需要的工作大纲。按最保守的估计，利用计算机控制联合动力系统，每年可以节约数亿卢布。
