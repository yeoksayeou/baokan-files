Title: Chairman Mao Sends Congratulations on Yemen's National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 24th: Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to King Ahmad of Yemen on November 23rd to congratulate him on Yemen's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Majesty Imam Ahmad bin Yahya Hamidaddin, King of Yemen:

On the occasion of the Kingdom of Yemen's National Day, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of your country on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish Yemen prosperity and its people happiness.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, November 23, 1957

### 毛主席电贺也门国庆

1957-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺也门国庆
    新华社24日讯　毛泽东主席23日致电也门国王艾哈迈德祝贺也门国庆，电文如下：也门国王伊马姆·艾哈迈德·本·雅赫亚·哈米德丁陛下：
    欣逢也门王国国庆，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人名义，向陛下和贵国人民表示热诚的祝贺。祝也门国家繁荣、人民幸福。
                中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1957年11月23日
