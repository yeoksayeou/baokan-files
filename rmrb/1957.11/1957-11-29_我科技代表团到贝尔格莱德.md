Title: Our Science and Technology Delegation Arrives in Belgrade
Author:
Date: 1957-11-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, November 27th - The Chinese science and technology delegation headed by Shen Hong, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Yugoslav Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, arrived in Belgrade by plane on the afternoon of November 26th. The delegation is here to attend the second meeting of the Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee held here.

### 我科技代表团到贝尔格莱德

1957-11-29
第5版()
专栏：

    我科技代表团到贝尔格莱德
    据新华社贝尔格莱德27日电以中南科学技术合作委员会中国组主席沈鸿为首的中国科学技术代表团，11月26日下午乘飞机到达贝尔格莱德。代表团是前来参加此间举行的科学技术合作委员会第二次会议的。
