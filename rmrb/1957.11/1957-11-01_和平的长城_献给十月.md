Title: The Peaceful Great Wall – Dedicated to October
Author: Gan Yongbai
Date: 1957-11-01
Page: 8

    The Peaceful Great Wall
    — Dedicated to October
    Gan Yongbai
    One bright May morning, I took off my hat before General Vatutin's [1] tomb. I felt profound
              sorrow,
  and unparalleled
              respect! Sunlight
    like a spring
              washed
                    Kyiv, the streets,
    buildings
        crystal-like
                  transparent. Dnieper River, oh,
          your gentle
                    flow, reflects
      the mountain city's
          majestic figure! So many thoughts
        surged in
              my mind, and what first made me think of was
                    war! Because I was thinking of Vatutin,
  thinking of the tens of thousands
        of heroic
                  blood, that nurtured this
          flowery and brocade-like
  glorious city! Oh!
  Suffering,
        happiness,
              war,
                    peace, in forty years
        this heroic land has borne
      so many deep
                  imprints! In order to
        end
            war forever, and
  suffering
      will be buried like fallen leaves in
                        passing water; Soviet! Oh,
  your sons and daughters
            labor,
                fight,
                    and sing, even offering
          the most precious
                  life. I recited Mayakovsky's
                verses,
  and gave my
        highest respect
                  to General Vatutin
            and all Soviet people who, for the sake of
    humanity's tomorrow,
                generously faced death – "Comrades, rest quietly... Your
      young country, with each
            spring
              becomes more glorious and splendid, it is powerful and majestic,
                  growing stronger
                    day by day." [2] What is being strengthened is
            not only
              the Soviet land, how many
      kind
              people around the world surround
      the peaceful Great Wall
                  and cheer! In November 1956, when you
    halted
            the iron hooves of
      the robbers
                invading Egypt, and also severed
        the black
                devil's hand stretching
        towards
            our fraternal country Hungary, I
    in the ports of
                southern China
    saw
    how many people with tears in their eyes
                    excitedly
                        called out the name of the Soviet Union!
  They came from Mumbai, Nagasaki, Cairo, they came from Liverpool, Marseille, Naples,
    they came
  from Ostend, Vladivostok,
    Gdańsk... They cheered
          as the flag of peace
                was raised again on the high seas!
              And they
                    will embrace their homeland! Glory
    to
        the people of the
                  world who defend peace! Glory
      to
          the peaceful Great Wall,
                      the Soviet people!
    [1] General Vatutin was a famous general in the Soviet Patriotic War who recovered Kyiv. He died in the battle to pursue Hitler's bandit army and was buried in Kyiv.
    [2] Sentences from the eighteenth chapter of Mayakovsky's long poem "Good!".

### 和平的长城——献给十月
甘永柏
1957-11-01
第8版()
专栏：

    和平的长城
    ——献给十月
    甘永柏
    一个明朗的五月的早晨，我在瓦杜丁将军〔一〕墓前脱下了自己的帽子。我感到深重的
              沉哀，
  又有无比的
              钦敬！阳光
    泉水一般地
              冲洗着
                    基辅，市街
    房屋
        水晶似的
                  透明。德聂伯河呵
          您悠悠的
                    水流，映照着
      山城
          雄壮的姿影！多少思潮
        澎湃在
              我的脑海，而最先使我想起的是
                    战争！因为我在想着瓦杜丁，
  想着他所代表的千千万万
        英雄的
                  热血，培育了这座
          如花似锦的
  光辉的名城！呵！
  苦难，
        幸福，
              战争，
                    和平，四十年间
        这英雄的国土打下了
      多少深刻的
                  烙印！为了把
        战争
            永远结束，而
  苦难
      将像败叶似地埋入
                        逝水；苏维埃呵！
  你的儿女
            劳动
                战斗
                    又歌唱，甚至献出
          最珍贵的
                  生命。我背诵着马雅可夫斯基的
                诗句，
  把我的
        最高的敬礼
                  献给瓦杜丁将军
            和一切为了
    人类的明天
                慷慨赴难的苏维埃人——“同志们，静静地安息吧……你们的
      年少的国家，随着每一个
            春天
              变得更为光辉灿烂，它有力而又雄壮，
                  一天天地巩固起
                    来”。〔二〕巩固起来的
            不只是
              苏维埃的国土，全世界
      有多少
              善良的人民环绕着
      和平的长城
                  欢呼！一九五六年十一月，当你
    喝住了
            侵入埃及的
      强盗们的
                铁蹄，又斩断了
        伸向
            我们兄弟国家匈牙利的
        黑色的
                魔手，我在
    中国南方的
                港口看到
    多少人噙着眼泪
                    激动地
                        呼唤苏维埃的名字！
  他们是从孟买、长崎、开罗来的，他们是从利物浦、马赛、那不勒
    斯来的，
  他们是从奥斯坦德、海参崴、革
    但斯克来的……他们欢呼
          公海上
                重新扬起和平的旗帜！
              而他们
                    将拥抱自己的家园！光荣
    归于
        保卫和平的
                  全世界人民！光荣
      归于
          和平的长城
                      苏维埃人！
    〔一〕瓦杜丁将军是苏联卫国战争中收复基辅的名将，在追击希特勒匪军的战役中牺牲，葬于基辅城。
    〔二〕马雅可夫斯基长诗“好”第十八章中的句子。
