Title: The Soviet Union Dispatches a Government Delegation to Escort Preoteasa's Remains Back Home; Comrades Deng Xiaoping and Others Once Kept Vigil and Paid Respects
Author:
Date: 1957-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch: TASS, Moscow dispatch: The hearse carrying the remains of Preoteasa, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and Secretary of the Central Committee, left Moscow for Bucharest on the 10th.

The platform of the Kiev Station was decorated with the Romanian and Soviet flags, each bearing a black ribbon. An honor guard was lined up on the platform, and the national anthems of Romania and the Soviet Union were played.

The Soviet Council of Ministers and the CPSU Central Committee dispatched a government delegation consisting of Comrade Brezhnev, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, and other comrades to escort Comrade Preoteasa's remains and participate in the funeral in Bucharest.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th dispatch: According to TASS reports: The Soviet "Pravda" and "Izvestia" published today the announcement of the Soviet Council of Ministers and the CPSU Central Committee, stating that the Soviet Council of Ministers and the CPSU Central Committee have established a government committee to handle the affairs of escorting the remains of Comrade Preoteasa, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and Secretary of the Central Committee, and is responsible for escorting the remains to Romania. This committee is composed of Comrades Brezhnev, Tishchenko, Patolichev, and Bobrovnikov.

Preoteasa unfortunately sacrificed himself on November 4th due to an accident when the plane carrying the Romanian delegation landed in Moscow.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th dispatch: Members of the Chinese delegation Deng Xiaoping, Li Xiannian, Ulanhu, and Liu Xiao, on behalf of the Chinese delegation, went to the Hall of Columns of the Trade Union Building this morning to present wreaths to the coffin of Comrade Preoteasa, member of the Romanian delegation, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and Secretary of the Central Committee, and kept vigil and paid their respects before the coffin.

### 苏联派遣政府代表团  护送普列奥蒂亚萨遗体回国  邓小平等同志曾经守灵致敬

1957-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联派遣政府代表团
    护送普列奥蒂亚萨遗体回国
    邓小平等同志曾经守灵致敬
    据新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：装运罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会政治局候补委员、中央委员会书记普列奥蒂亚萨遗体的灵车10日离开莫斯科开往布加勒斯特。
    基辅车站的站台上挂着镶有黑带的罗马尼亚和苏联的国旗。站台上排列了仪仗队，奏了罗马尼亚和苏联的国歌。
    苏联部长会议和苏共中央派遣了由苏共中央主席团委员、苏共中央书记勃列日涅夫等同志组成的政府代表团护送普列奥蒂亚萨同志的遗体，并且参加在布加勒斯特举行的葬礼。
    新华社莫斯科9日电　据塔斯社报道：苏联“真理报”和“消息报”今天发表的苏联部长会议和苏共中央的公告说，苏联部长会议和苏共中央成立了处理护送罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会政治局候补委员和中央委员会书记普列奥蒂亚萨同志遗体事宜的政府委员会，负责把遗体护送往罗马尼亚。这个委员会由勃列日涅夫、季申科、帕托利切夫、波布罗夫尼科夫四位同志组成。
    普列奥蒂亚萨是在11月4日因罗马尼亚代表团所乘飞机在莫斯科着陆时发生事故而不幸牺牲的。
    新华社莫斯科10日电　中国代表团团员邓小平、李先念、乌兰夫、刘晓今天上午代表中国代表团前往工会大厦圆柱大厅，向罗马尼亚代表团团员、罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会政治局候补委员和中央委员会书记普列奥蒂亚萨同志的灵柩献花圈，并在灵柩前守灵致敬。
