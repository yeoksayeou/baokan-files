Title: Beijing "Italian Film Week" Ends
Author:
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th. The "Italian Film Week" held in Beijing concluded today. Over the past seven days, the three Italian films featured in the film week were screened nearly 500 times in Beijing, with an audience of over 300,000 people.

The "Italian Film Week," which began on October 4th this year, was held in batches in ten major cities in China. Italian films such as "The Policeman and the Thief" were warmly welcomed in various locations.

### 北京“意大利电影周”结束

1957-11-06
第8版()
专栏：

    北京“意大利电影周”结束
    新华社5日讯　在北京举行的“意大利电影周”今天结束。七天来，参加电影周的三部意大利影片在北京共映出了近五百场，观众达三十万人次以上。
    从今年10月4日开始，“意大利电影周”曾分批在我国十个大城市中举行。“警察与小偷”等意大利影片，在各地受到了热烈欢迎。
