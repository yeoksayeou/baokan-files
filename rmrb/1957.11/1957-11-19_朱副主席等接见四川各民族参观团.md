Title: Vice Chairman Zhu and Others Receive Sichuan Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Vice Chairman Zhu De, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen and Shen Junru, and Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi received all members of the Sichuan Province Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation this afternoon.

### 朱副主席等接见四川各民族参观团

1957-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    朱副主席等接见四川各民族参观团
    新华社18日讯　朱德副主席，李济深、沈钧儒副委员长，贺龙、陈毅副总理，在今天下午接见了四川省各民族参观团的全体人员。
