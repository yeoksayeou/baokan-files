Title: Chen Yi and Others Watch the Performance of the Yugoslav Dance Ensemble and Meet with the Head and Deputy Head of the Ensemble and the Choir Conductor
Author:
Date: 1957-11-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Vice Premier Chen Yi watched the last performance of the Yugoslav "Branko Krsmanović" Dance Ensemble in Beijing on the evening of the 19th. The performance was warmly welcomed by more than 1,400 audience members. After the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Culture Liu Zhiming, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League Hu Keshi and others went to the stage to congratulate the Yugoslav actors on their successful performance. Actors from the Central National Song and Dance Ensemble also presented flowers to the guests. The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Yugoslav Embassy in China, Milicevic, also accompanied them on stage.
    During the intermission, Vice Premier Chen Yi also met with the head of the dance ensemble, Miloš Nikolić, the deputy head, Miotrag Pavlović, and the choir conductor, Dimitrije Stefanović.
    This dance ensemble, composed of Yugoslav university students, gave four performances in Beijing. More than 8,000 audience members enjoyed their beautiful songs, dances, and musical programs. The delegation will leave Beijing on the evening of the 20th to visit Changchun. (Attached picture)

### 陈毅等观看南歌舞团的演出  并接见歌舞团团长副团长和合唱队指挥

1957-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅等观看南歌舞团的演出
    并接见歌舞团团长副团长和合唱队指挥
    新华社19日讯　陈毅副总理19日晚观看了南斯拉夫“伯朗柯·克里斯曼诺维奇”歌舞团在北京的最后一次演出。这次演出受到了一千四百多名观众的热烈欢迎。在演出结束以后，陈毅副总理，文化部副部长刘芝明，中国共产主义青年团中央书记处书记胡克实等人都到舞台上祝贺南斯拉夫演员们演出成功。中央民族歌舞团的演员们还向客人们献了花。南斯拉夫驻华大使馆临时代办米里切维奇也陪同上台。
    在演出休息时，陈毅副总理还接见了歌舞团团长米洛什·尼柯里奇，副团长米奥特拉格·巴夫洛维奇和合唱队指挥第米特里耶·斯蒂方诺维奇。
    由南斯拉夫大学生们组成的这个歌舞团，在北京共举行了四次演出。有八千多观众欣赏了他们表演的优美的歌舞和音乐节目。代表团将在20日晚离开北京去长春访问。（附图片）
