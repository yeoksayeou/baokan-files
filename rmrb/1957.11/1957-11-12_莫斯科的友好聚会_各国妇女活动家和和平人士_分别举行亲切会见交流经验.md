Title: Friendly Gatherings in Moscow: Women Activists and Peace Advocates from Various Countries Hold Cordial Meetings to Exchange Experiences
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 5
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 10th: Soviet women representatives held a meeting in Moscow on the afternoon of the 10th with prominent women social activists from the delegations of socialist countries.
　　Song Qingling, member of the Chinese delegation, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and the wife of Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao attended the meeting.
　　Dragoycheva, member of the Bulgarian delegation, member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Chairman of the Bulgarian Democratic Women's Committee, and the wife of Sukhbaatar, member of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and member the Presidium of the Great People’s Hural of Mongolia, also attended the meeting, as well as women leaders from other countries.
　　The Soviet women attending the meeting included: Gagarina, Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Women's Committee, Petrova, Executive Secretary, Kovrigina, Minister of the Soviet Ministry of Health, and others.
　　Gagarina, Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Women's Committee, on behalf of Soviet women, extended a warm welcome to the women social activists from various countries who came to the Soviet Union to participate in the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. When she introduced Song Qingling, the entire audience stood up and applauded, shouting "Long live the People's Republic of China."
　　Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: According to TASS, Moscow: Representatives of peace movements from various countries and members of the World Peace Council delegation who came to Moscow to participate in the 40th anniversary celebration of the October Revolution met with members of the Soviet Peace Committee on the 10th in Moscow.
　　During the meeting, in a friendly conversation, the participants exchanged views on the tasks facing the peace movement, and each introduced their experiences in working towards the peaceful resolution of issues of concern to the people, and in banning nuclear weapons and stopping nuclear weapons testing.
　　Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, warmly welcomed the guests.
　　The meeting fully demonstrated the unanimous determination of the representatives of peace movements from various countries to expand and strengthen the international peace movement and to cooperate with all organizations and kind people who safeguard world peace.

### 莫斯科的友好聚会  各国妇女活动家和和平人士  分别举行亲切会见交流经验

1957-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    莫斯科的友好聚会
    各国妇女活动家和和平人士
    分别举行亲切会见交流经验
    据新华社莫斯科10日电　苏联妇女界代表10日下午在莫斯科举行了同社会主义国家代表团中的著名妇女社会活动家的会见。
    中国代表团团员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中苏友好协会总会会长、全国妇女联合会名誉主席宋庆龄和驻苏大使刘晓的夫人出席会见。
    保加利亚代表团团员、保共中央委员、保加利亚民主妇女委员会主席德拉戈伊切娃和蒙古人民革命党中央委员、蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团委员苏赫—巴托尔夫人也出席了会见，以及其他国家妇女界领袖。
    苏联妇女界出席会见的有：苏联妇女委员会副主席加加里娜、执行书记彼得罗娃、苏联卫生部部长科夫里金娜等。
    苏联妇女委员会副主席加加里娜代表苏联妇女对前来苏联参加十月社会主义革命四十周年的各国妇女社会活动家们表示热烈欢迎，当她介绍宋庆龄时，全场起立鼓掌，高呼“中华人民共和国万岁”。
    据新华社11日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：来莫斯科参加十月革命四十周年庆典的各国和平运动的代表和世界和平理事会代表团人员，10日在莫斯科同苏联保卫和平委员会委员们会见。
    参加会见的人在友好的谈话中，就和平运动所面临的任务交换了意见，并且各自介绍了在和平解决人民所关心的问题方面、在禁止核武器和停止核武器试验方面进行工作的经验。
    苏联保卫和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫热烈地欢迎了客人们。
    会见充分地表现了各国和平运动的代表有一致的决心，要扩大和加强国际和平运动，并且同一切维护世界和平的组织和善良人们进行合作。
