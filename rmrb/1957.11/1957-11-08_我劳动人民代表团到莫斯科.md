Title: Chinese Labor People's Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-11-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 7th: The Chinese Labor People's Delegation, which is going to the Soviet Union to participate in the celebrations for the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, arrived in Moscow in batches on the afternoon of the 6th.

The head of the Chinese Labor People's Delegation is Liu Ningyi, and the deputy heads are Qian Junrui, Xu Guangping, Lao She, Mei Lanfang, and Qian Sanqiang.

Among those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation were B. Lev, Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions; Kimilov, Director of the International Department of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions; Zverev, Secretary of the Central Council of the Soviet Machine Building Industry Trade Union; and Altabayev, Deputy Director of the Oriental Department of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Gong Ting, First Secretary of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 我劳动人民代表团到莫斯科

1957-11-08
第3版()
专栏：

    我劳动人民代表团到莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科7日电　前往苏联参加十月社会主义革命四十周年庆祝典礼的中国劳动人民代表团，6日下午分批到达莫斯科。
    中国劳动人民代表团团长是刘宁一，副团长是钱俊瑞、许广平、老舍、梅兰芳、钱三强。
    前往机场欢迎代表团的有全苏工会中央理事会书记布?列夫，全苏工会中央理事会国际部主任基米罗夫，苏联机器制造业工会中央理事会书记兹维列夫，苏联对外文化协会东方部副主任阿尔塔巴耶夫等人。
    中国驻苏联大使馆一等秘书宫亭也到机场欢迎。
