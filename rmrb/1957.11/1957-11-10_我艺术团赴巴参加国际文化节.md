Title: Our Art Troupe Goes to Pakistan to Participate in International Cultural Festival
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Chinese Art Troupe, invited by Pakistan to participate in the International Cultural Festival to be held in Karachi in mid-November, flew to Pakistan from Beijing today.

　　The Chinese Art Troupe has seventy-one members, including Peking Opera, song and dance, and folk instrumental performers. The head of the delegation is Ma Yuhuai, director of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 我艺术团赴巴参加国际文化节

1957-11-10
第2版()
专栏：

    我艺术团赴巴参加国际文化节
    新华社8日讯　应巴基斯坦邀请参加11月中旬在卡拉奇举行的国际文化节的中国艺术团，今天由北京飞往巴基斯坦。
    中国艺术团有京剧、歌舞和民间器乐演员共七十一人。团长是中国人民对外文化协会理事马玉槐。
