Title: Hungary Rewards Meritorious Personnel, Bestows Medals on Over Two Hundred People
Author:
Date: 1957-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 10th: The Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, on the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution and the first anniversary of the establishment of the Hungarian government, awarded the Order of the Red Banner and the Order of the Red Banner of Labor, the Hungarian Freedom Medal, and the Order of the Red Star to over two hundred people who have made outstanding contributions in the struggle to suppress counter-revolutionaries and in the work of restoring the national economy.

### 匈奖励一批有功人员  授予二百多人勋章

1957-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    匈奖励一批有功人员
    授予二百多人勋章
    新华社布达佩斯10日电　匈牙利人民共和国主席团在十月革命四十周年和匈牙利政府成立一周年的时候，以红旗勋章和劳动红旗勋章、匈牙利自由勋章和红星勋章分别奖给了二百多名在镇压反革命的斗争和恢复国民经济工作中有卓著功绩的人。
