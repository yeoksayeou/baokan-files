Author:
Date: 1957-11-30
Page: 1

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    The Organizational Regulations of the People's Congress and the People's Committee of the Qian Gorlos Mongol Autonomous County of Jilin Province were approved by the 86th meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on November 29, 1957, and are hereby promulgated.
           Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, November 29, 1957

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1957-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    吉林省前郭尔罗斯蒙古族自治县人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于一九五七年十一月二十九日第八十六次会议批准，现予公布。
           中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东	　一九五七年十一月二十九日
