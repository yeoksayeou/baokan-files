Title: The Second Round of the National Football League "A" Division Tournament: Ten Matches Held in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1957-11-03
Page: 8

Football League "A" Division Tournament Second Round
Ten Matches Held in Beijing District
    (Our newspaper reports) The second round of the 1957 National Football League "A" Division tournament in the Beijing competition zone began on November 1st with the first match. The Beijing team defeated the Changchun team with a score of 2-1.
    In addition to the match held on November 1st, there will be nine more matches in the Beijing competition zone. The schedule is as follows:
    November 7th: Beijing Team vs. "August 1st" Team
    November 10th: Shenyang Military Region Team vs. "August 1st" Team
    First Machine Sports Association vs. Beijing Team
    November 13th: First Machine Sports Association vs. "August 1st" Team
    Shenyang Military Region Team vs. Beijing Team
    November 17th: Changchun Team vs. "August 1st" Team
    Shenyang Institute of Physical Education vs. Beijing Team
    November 21st: Shenyang Institute of Physical Education vs. "August 1st" Team
    November 24th: Beijing Team vs. Tianjin Team
    In addition, competition zones in Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing and others will hold competitions this month.

### 足球甲级队联赛第二次循环赛  北京赛区举行十场比赛

1957-11-03
第8版()
专栏：

    足球甲级队联赛第二次循环赛
    北京赛区举行十场比赛
    本报讯　1957年全国足球甲级队联赛第二循环北京竞赛区，11月1日举行了第一场比赛。北京队以二比一胜长春队。
    北京竞赛区除1日举行的这场比赛以外，还有九场竞赛，程序如下：
    11月7日：北京队—“八一”队
    11月10日：沈阳部队队—“八一”队
    第一机械体育协会—北京队
    11月13日：第一机械体育协会—“八一”队
    沈阳部队队—北京队
    11月17日：长春队—“八一”队
    沈阳体育学院—北京队
    11月21日：沈阳体育学院—“八一”队
    11月24日：北京队—天津队
    此外，天津、上海、南京等竞赛区，本月都将举行竞赛。
