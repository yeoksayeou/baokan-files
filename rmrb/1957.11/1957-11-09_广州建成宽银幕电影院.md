Title: Guangzhou Builds Widescreen Cinema
Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 8

Column: Stage and Screen

    Guangzhou Builds Widescreen Cinema
    The Xinhua Widescreen Cinema in Guangzhou held its completion ceremony on the morning of the 7th. At the same time, the opening ceremony of "Soviet Film Week" was held, and the Soviet color widescreen stereophonic film "Prelude to Revolution" was screened. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 广州建成宽银幕电影院

1957-11-09
第8版()
专栏：舞台和银幕

    广州建成宽银幕电影院
    广州的新华宽银幕电影院，7日上午举行落成典礼，同时举行“苏联电影周”开幕式，放映了苏联彩色宽银幕立体声艺术片“革命的前奏”。（新华社）
