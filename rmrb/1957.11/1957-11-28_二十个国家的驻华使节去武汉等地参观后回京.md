Title: Ambassadors and Envoys from Twenty Countries Return to Beijing After Visiting Wuhan and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1957-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th — Ambassadors, chargés d'affaires, and diplomatic officials from twenty countries, along with the head of the Cambodian Royal Economic Delegation and their wives, accompanied by Vice Foreign Minister Zeng Yongquan and his wife, left Beijing on November 17th. They visited Wuhan, Changsha, Guilin, and other places, where they toured the Yangtze River Bridge and several factories, agricultural producers' cooperatives, schools, and scenic spots. They returned to Beijing tonight.

### 二十个国家的驻华使节去武汉等地参观后回京

1957-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    二十个国家的驻华使节去武汉等地参观后回京
    新华社27日讯　二十个国家的驻华大使、代办和外交官员以及柬埔寨王国经济代表团团长和他们的夫人等，在外交部副部长曾涌泉和夫人的陪同下，11月17日离开北京先后访问了武汉、长沙、桂林等地，参观了长江大桥和一些工厂、农业生产合作社、学校和名胜古迹。他们已经在今天晚上回到北京。
