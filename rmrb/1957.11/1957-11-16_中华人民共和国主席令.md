Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 1

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China

    The "Regulations on the Appointment and Removal of State Organ Personnel by People's Committees at and above the County Level" was adopted by the 83rd meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on November 6, 1957, and is hereby promulgated.

                                  Mao Zedong
                                  Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                        November 6, 1957

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1957-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    县级以上人民委员会任免国家机关工作人员条例已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于一九五七年十一月六日第八十三次会议通过，现予公布。
                                  中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                        一九五七年十一月六日
