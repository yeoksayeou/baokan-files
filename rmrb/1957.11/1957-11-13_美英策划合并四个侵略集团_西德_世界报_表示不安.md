Title: US-UK Plot to Merge Four Aggression Blocs, West German "Die Welt" Expresses Concern
Author:
Date: 1957-11-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 12th: The influential West German newspaper "Die Welt" has expressed unease over the US-UK plot to organize a global military alliance composed of approximately fifty countries. The newspaper stated that doing so would "produce absurd results" and would drag countries like West Germany and Norway into Southeast Asian disputes that have nothing to do with them. Based on decisions made at the US-UK talks in Washington, the US and UK are studying and will propose at the upcoming North Atlantic Council meeting next month a plan to piece together the North Atlantic Treaty, the Baghdad Pact, the Manila Treaty, and the Pan-American Union into a global military bloc.

### 美英策划合并四个侵略集团  西德“世界报”表示不安

1957-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    美英策划合并四个侵略集团
    西德“世界报”表示不安
    据新华社12日讯　柏林消息：西德有影响的“世界报”对美英策划组织一个全球性的、由约五十个国家组成的军事联盟的计划表示不安。该报说，这样做会“产生荒谬的结果”，会把西德和挪威这样的国家拖入到同它们毫无关系的东南亚纠纷中去。根据美英华盛顿会谈的决定，美英两国正在研究并将在下月举行的北大西洋理事会会议上提出一个计划，把北大西洋公约、巴格达条约、马尼拉条约和泛美联盟拼凑成一个全球性的军事集团。
