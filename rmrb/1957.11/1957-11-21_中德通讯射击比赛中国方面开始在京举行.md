Title: China Begins Holding Sino-German Correspondence Shooting Competition in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-21
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, 20th – China began holding the Chinese portion of the Sino-German correspondence shooting competition today at the Beijing shooting range.

Based on today's on-site results (final scores, verified after target inspection, will be announced tomorrow), it appears that in the men's 25-meter rapid-fire pistol event at the first stage team competition, the renowned shooter Zhang Hong achieved the best score, hitting 291 rings out of 30 shots (the full ring is 300). If Zhang Hong can hit 297 rings out of 30 shots in tomorrow's second stage competition, his total score will surpass the Olympic record of 587 rings achieved by the first-place winner last year.

It is noteworthy that in today's first stage individual championship of the men's rapid-fire pistol event, another shooter, Dong Ruiguang, also achieved a score of 291 rings. This is better than the scores of the other four athletes participating in the aforementioned team competition: Li Jie (290 rings), Li Peilin (283 rings), Zhang Yan (281 rings) and Han Guobin (260 rings).

In both the men's and women's free-choice small-bore rifle 50-meter distance prone, kneeling, and standing positions events, only the prone and kneeling positions were held today. The standing position event will be held tomorrow.

Results of the prone and kneeling positions events: Among the five shooters participating in the men's team competition, Zhao Shanjun had the best score, hitting a total of 783 rings out of 80 shots (the full ring for prone and kneeling is 800 rings), with 396 rings in the prone position (full ring 400 rings) and 387 rings in the kneeling position (full ring 400 rings). Among the four shooters participating in the men's individual championship, Meng Jisheng had the best score, hitting a total of 767 rings out of 80 shots (389 rings in the prone position and 378 rings in the kneeling position). Among the five shooters participating in the women's team competition, Cao Jingfen achieved the best score, hitting a total of 780 rings out of 80 shots (392 rings in the prone position and 388 rings in the kneeling position), only three rings less than Zhao Shanjun's score in the men's team. Among the two shooters participating in the women's individual championship, Zhang Hongzhi is in the lead, hitting a total of 761 rings out of 80 shots (384 rings in the prone position and 377 rings in the kneeling position).

Today, the military attaché of the German Democratic Republic in China, Weinhold, served as the deputy chief judge of the competition. The chief judge is Zhang Weihan from the Chinese side. (Photo attached)

This is the scene of the men's and women's small-bore rifle kneeling position competition in the Sino-German correspondence shooting competition.

### 中德通讯射击比赛中国方面开始在京举行

1957-11-21
第8版()
专栏：

    中德通讯射击比赛中国方面开始在京举行
    新华社20日讯　中国和德意志民主共和国之间的通讯射击比赛，今天在北京射击场开始举行中国方面的比赛。
    根据今天的现场成绩（最后成绩经验靶后将在明天公布）看来，在男子小口径手枪二十五公尺距离人像靶速射第一阶段队际的比赛中，著名射手张鈜取得了最好的成绩，他三十发子弹共命中了二九一环（满环是三○○环）。在明天的第二阶段比赛中，如果张鈜能用三十发子弹命中二九七环，他的总成绩将超过去年奥林匹克运动会第一名五八七环的纪录。
    引人注意的是，今天在男子小口径手枪人像靶速射第一阶段的个人冠军赛中，另一名射手董瑞光的成绩也达到了二九一环。这比其他四名参加上述队际比赛的选手李杰（二九○环）、李培林（二八三环）、张炎（二八一环）和韩国斌（二六○环）的成绩都好。
    男子和女子自选小口径步枪五十公尺距离卧、跪、立姿比赛，今天都只举行了卧姿和跪姿比赛。立姿比赛将在明天举行。
    卧姿和跪姿比赛结果：参加男子队际比赛的五名射手中，成绩最好的是赵善俊，他八十发子弹共命中了七八三环（卧姿和跪姿的满环是八○○环），其中卧姿命中三九六环（满环四○○环），跪姿命中三八七环（满环四○○环）。参加男子个人冠军赛的四名射手中，孟继胜的成绩最好，他八十发子弹共命中七六七环（卧姿命中三八九环，跪姿命中三七八环）。参加女子队际比赛的五名射手中，曹靖芬取得了最好的成绩，她八十发子弹共命中七八○环（卧姿命中三九二环，跪姿命中三八八环），比男子队赵善俊的成绩只少三环。参加女子个人冠军赛的两名射手中，成绩领先的是张宏智，她八十发子弹共命中了七六一环（卧姿三八四环，跪姿三七七环）。
    今天，德意志民主共和国驻华武官魏恩浩特担任了这次比赛的副总裁判长。总裁判长是中国方面的张维汉。（附图片）
