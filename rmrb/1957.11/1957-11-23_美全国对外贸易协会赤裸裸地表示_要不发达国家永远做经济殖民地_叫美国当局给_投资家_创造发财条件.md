Title: U.S. National Foreign Trade Council Nakedly States That Underdeveloped Countries Should Forever Be Economic Colonies and Calls on the U.S. Authorities to Create Opportunities for "Investors" to Make a Fortune
Author:
Date: 1957-11-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, November 22nd – According to a TASS dispatch from New York: The 44th annual meeting of the U.S. National Foreign Trade Council concluded in New York on the 21st. At the meeting on the 20th, a declaration was passed, demanding that the U.S. government exert pressure on underdeveloped countries to create favorable conditions for U.S. investors in these countries. The declaration stated, "The U.S. government should, in the future, use improvements in our trade agreement system and other appropriate methods to establish conditions that encourage and protect private foreign investment. It must be guaranteed to prevent nationalization or any other encroachment on the private property of citizens of other countries."

The declaration shows that the U.S. ruling clique is trying its best to keep underdeveloped countries as semi-colonies that are forever their source of raw materials. Participants in the meeting opposed the U.S. participation in the "Special Fund for Economic Development" planned by the United Nations, and advocated that U.S. government technical "assistance" to other countries, either directly or through the United Nations, should be limited to "health care, education, sanitation facilities, and agricultural technology," and should not be used for "economic or industrial development plans."

In a chapter entitled "Ensuring Strategic and Other Raw Material Sources," the declaration stated more directly that the main purpose of U.S. foreign policy should be to assist in ensuring that underdeveloped countries continue to be the source of strategic and other important raw materials needed by Western countries.

### 美全国对外贸易协会赤裸裸地表示  要不发达国家永远做经济殖民地  叫美国当局给“投资家”创造发财条件

1957-11-23
第5版()
专栏：

    美全国对外贸易协会赤裸裸地表示
    要不发达国家永远做经济殖民地
    叫美国当局给“投资家”创造发财条件
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社纽约消息：美国全国对外贸易协会第四十四届年会21日在纽约闭幕。在20日的会议上通过了一项宣言，要求美国政府对不发达的国家施加压力，以便在这些国家中为美国投资家创造有利条件。宣言说，“美国政府今后应该用改善我国贸易协定制度和其他合适的方式来建立鼓励和保护私人在国外投资的条件。必须保证防止国有化或对其他国家公民的私人财产有任何其他侵占行为”。
    宣言表明，美国统治集团竭力想保持不发达国家永远作为他们原料来源的半殖民地。参加会议的人反对美国参加联合国所筹划的“经济发展特种基金”，主张美国政府直接或者通过联合国给予其他国家的技术“援助”应限制于“保健、教育、卫生设施和农业技术方面”，而不应该用于“经济或工业的发展计划”。
    在题为“保证战略和其他原料来源”的一章中，宣言更直截了当地指出，美国外交政策的主要目的应当在于协助保证不发达国家继续成为西方国家所需要的战略和其他重要原料的来源地。
