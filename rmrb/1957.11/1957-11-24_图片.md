Author: Li Ziyou

    A warm tide of provincial-level cadres in Guizhou Province requesting to go up to the mountains and down to the countryside to participate in agricultural production has formed. This is Du Huaju, Director of the Physical Education Department of Guiyang Normal College (the one holding flowers on the vehicle), bidding farewell to the faculty, staff, and students who are seeing him off. Photo by Li Ziyou (Xinhua News Agency draft)
