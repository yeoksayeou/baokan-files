Title: Delegations from Various Countries Go to the Soviet Union to Participate in the 40th Anniversary Celebrations of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1957-11-04
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 3rd: The delegation of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Kim Il-sung, left Pyongyang at 10:00 a.m. today on a "Tu-104" jet plane to attend the celebrations for the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution in Moscow.
    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 3rd: The Bulgarian delegation, headed by Zhivkov, left Sofia by special train on the evening of the 2nd for Moscow.
    The delegation was invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers to go to Moscow to participate in the October Revolution celebrations.
    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 3rd: A party and government delegation composed of six people including Kardelj and Rankovic, members of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia and Vice-Presidents of the Federal Executive Council, left Belgrade by train on the evening of the 2nd to go to Moscow to participate in the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.
    Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd: TASS, Moscow: The Italian Communist Party delegation, headed by General Secretary Togliatti, arrived in Moscow on the 2nd to participate in the 40th anniversary celebrations of the October Revolution.
    Among those who welcomed the delegation at the airport were members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the CPSU, Mikoyan and Furtseva.
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 3rd: The delegation of the working people of the Mongolian People's Republic left here by plane this afternoon to participate in the 40th anniversary celebrations of the Great October Socialist Revolution in Moscow. The head of the delegation is Damdin, First Secretary of the Ulaanbaatar City Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party. The delegation includes 25 representatives of advanced workers, herdsmen and people from all walks of life.

### 参加十月革命四十周年庆典  各国代表团纷纷赴苏

1957-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

    参加十月革命四十周年庆典
    各国代表团纷纷赴苏
    据新华社平壤3日电　以金日成为首的朝鲜劳动党中央委员会和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团今天上午十时乘“图—一○四”喷气式飞机离开平壤，前往莫斯科参加十月革命四十周年的庆祝典礼。
    新华社索非亚3日电　以日夫科夫为首的保加利亚代表团，2日晚乘专车离开索非亚前往莫斯科。
    代表团是应苏联共产党中央委员会和部长会议的邀请去莫斯科参加十月革命庆祝典礼的。
    新华社贝尔格莱德3日电　由南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会执行委员会委员和联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔和兰科维奇等六人组成的党和政府代表团，2日晚由贝尔格莱德乘车去莫斯科参加十月革命四十周年庆典。
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：以总书记陶里亚蒂为首的意大利共产党代表团在2日到达莫斯科，参加十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有苏共中央主席团委员米高扬和福尔采娃等人。
    新华社乌兰巴托3日电　蒙古人民共和国劳动人民代表团今天下午从这里坐飞机去莫斯科参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年庆祝典礼。代表团团长是蒙古人民革命党乌兰巴托市委员会第一书记达姆丁。代表团包括先进工人、牧民和各阶层人民代表共二十五人。
