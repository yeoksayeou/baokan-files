Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Farewell Reception for the British Ballet Rambert

Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 2

    Chu Tunan Hosts Farewell Reception for the British Ballet Rambert
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th – Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a luncheon reception on the 8th to bid farewell to the British Ballet Rambert. The ballet troupe, after visiting Wuhan, Nanjing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou, returned to Beijing yesterday and will return to their country tomorrow.
    The Deputy Director of the ballet troupe, David Ellis, said at today's reception that they had received a warm welcome in China. He also said that the joint orchestra of the Central Philharmonic Orchestra and the Central Experimental Opera House, which accompanied them, had cooperated very well with them.
    The Director of the British Ballet Rambert, Marie Rambert, had returned to her country a month earlier.

### 楚图南为英国蓝伯特芭蕾舞团饯行

1957-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    楚图南为英国蓝伯特芭蕾舞团饯行
    新华社8日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南8日午间举行酒会欢送英国蓝伯特芭蕾舞剧团。舞剧团访问了武汉、南京、上海和杭州以后，在昨天回到北京，他们将在明天回国。
    舞剧团副团长大伟·埃利斯在今天的酒会上说，他们在中国受到了热情的招待。他还说，为他们伴奏的中央乐团和中央实验歌剧院联合管弦乐队同他们合作得非常好。
    英国蓝伯特芭蕾舞剧团团长玛丽·蓝伯特，在一个月以前已先期回国。
