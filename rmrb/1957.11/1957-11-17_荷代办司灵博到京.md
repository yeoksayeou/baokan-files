Title: Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Slingeboer Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The newly appointed Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. Slingeboer, and his wife arrived in Beijing this afternoon by car from Tianjin.

Mr. Slingeboer arrived in Tianjin today by ship from Hong Kong.

### 荷代办司灵博到京

1957-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    荷代办司灵博到京
    新华社16日讯  荷兰新任驻我国代办司灵博先生和他的夫人在今天下午由天津乘汽车到达北京。
    司灵博先生是从香港乘船在今天到达天津的。
