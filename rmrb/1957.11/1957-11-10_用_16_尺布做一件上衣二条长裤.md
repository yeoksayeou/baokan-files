    Making One Jacket and Two Pairs of Pants with 16 Feet of Cloth
    Explanation: Usually, 16 feet of cloth can only make one uniform. Here, we introduce a tailoring method for making one jacket and two pairs of pants with 16 feet of cloth. Using 75 cm wide (equivalent to 2.25 feet) denim, you can make a men's notched-collar uniform jacket and two pairs of pants by following the attached size chart and cutting pattern. The advantages of this tailoring method are: utilizing the slightly wider cloth surface, rationally arranging the cloth usage for each part of the clothing, minimizing scrap cloth as much as possible, and fully utilizing all usable scraps. Furthermore, while not affecting the appearance and durability of the clothing, removing the pocket flaps and narrowing the facing can also save some cloth.

### 用16尺布做一件上衣二条长裤

1957-11-10
第8版()
专栏：

    用16尺布做一件上衣二条长裤
    说明：通常，用16尺布只能做一身制服。这里，我们介绍一种用16尺布做一件上衣二条长裤的裁剪方法。用宽75公分（合2．25尺）的劳动布，按照所附尺寸表和裁剪图样，就可以做成男翻领制服一件上衣和两条长裤。这种裁剪方法的优点是：利用稍宽一点的布面，合理地安排衣服各部分的用布，尽量减少碎布，并且把一切可以利用的零碎布充分利用起来；此外，在不影响衣服的美观和耐穿的原则下，减去口袋盖，把贴边改窄，也可以节省一些用布。
