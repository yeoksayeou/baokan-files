Title: National Basketball Athletes Gather in Guangzhou; Grade A League Matches to Begin on the 14th
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 11th – The annual National Basketball Grade A League matches will begin on the 14th in Guangzhou. All twenty-three Grade A men's and women's basketball teams participating in this league (the China Zhong Sheng Sports Association women's team withdrew) have arrived in Guangzhou.

　　The competition will be conducted using a single round-robin system. From the 14th to the 17th, these men's and women's basketball teams will be divided into three groups each to hold preliminary rounds. Then, the top two teams from each group will advance to the finals starting on the 18th, to compete for the top six positions in the men's and women's groups, respectively. The third and fourth-place teams from each group will be placed into another group to hold finals, competing for the seventh to twelfth positions in the men's and women's groups, respectively. The entire competition is expected to conclude on the 24th.

　　The matches between the Beijing team and the Shanghai Red team in the men's group, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Red team and the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shenyang team, will be held on the 14th and 15th, respectively. According to local sports figures, these two matches will be the most eye-catching in the preliminary rounds.

### 全国篮球健将会师广州  十四日将举行甲级队联赛

1957-11-12
第8版()
专栏：

    全国篮球健将会师广州
    十四日将举行甲级队联赛
  　据新华社广州11日电　一年一度的全国篮球甲级队联赛，将从14日开始在广州举行。参加这次联赛的二十三个甲级男、女篮球队（中国钟声体育协会女子队弃权）已经全部到达广州。
  　比赛采取单循环制竞赛办法进行。从14日到17日，这些男女篮球队将各分三个组分别举行预赛。然后取各组的前两名，从18日开始举行决赛，分别争夺男子组和女子组的前六名名次。各组的第三和第四名将另分一组进行决赛，分别男子组和女子组第七至第十二名的名次。全部比赛预计到24日结束。
  　男子组北京队对上海红队、中国人民解放军“八一”红队对中国人民解放军驻沈阳部队队的比赛，将分别在14日和15日举行。据此间体育界人士认为，这两场比赛将是预赛中最引人注意的。
