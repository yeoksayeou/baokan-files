Title: Our Military Delegation Visits Soviet Army Central Museum
Author:
Date: 1957-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th - The Chinese military goodwill delegation to the Soviet Union visited the Soviet Army Central Museum on the 10th. The museum's director, Major General Ustiyantsev, briefed the delegation on the museum's overview. Accompanied by the director, the delegation toured the exhibits in detail. The delegation members expressed great admiration for the heroic deeds of the Soviet army in the domestic war and the Great Patriotic War. Major General Ustiyantsev presented the delegation with a photo album of the Soviet Army House. Ye Jianying, the deputy head of the delegation, on behalf of the head of the delegation Peng Dehuai and all the members, thanked Ustiyantsev and presented him with a Song Dynasty painting album and traditional Chinese paintings.

### 我军事代表团参观苏军中央博物馆

1957-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团参观苏军中央博物馆
    新华社莫斯科10日电　中国军事友好访苏代表团10日参观了苏军中央博物馆。馆长乌斯吉扬采夫少将向代表团介绍了博物馆概况，代表团在馆长陪同下详细参观了馆内的陈列品，团员们对苏军在国内战争和卫国战争中所表现的英勇事迹表示了极大的钦佩。乌斯吉扬采夫少将向代表团赠送了苏军之家相册，代表团副团长叶剑英元帅代表彭德怀团长和全体团员向乌斯吉扬采夫致谢并且赠送了宋人画册和国画。
