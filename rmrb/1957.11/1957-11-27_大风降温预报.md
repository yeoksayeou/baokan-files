Title: Gale and Cooling Forecast
Author:
Date: 1957-11-27
Page: 8

    Gale and Cooling Forecast
    Cold air is moving into our country from the Mongolian People's Republic. In the next 24 hours, there will be northerly winds of about force 6 in the central and eastern parts of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, northern Hebei, northern Shanxi, and the Northeast Plain. From tomorrow morning, there will also be northerly winds of force 5 or 6 in the middle and lower reaches of the Yellow River, the Huai River basin, and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River. After the cold air invades, the temperature in the above areas will drop by 6 to 10 degrees. There will also be northerly winds of force 6 or 7 in the Bohai Sea, the Yellow Sea, and the Taiwan Strait.

### 大风降温预报

1957-11-27
第8版()
专栏：

    大风降温预报
    有冷空气从蒙古人民共和国移入我国。未来二十四小时内，内蒙古自治区的中部和东部、河北北部、山西北部、东北平原有六级左右的偏北风。明天早晨起，黄河中下游、淮河流域、长江中下游也有五、六级的偏北风。以上各地在冷空气侵入以后气温将下降六至十度。渤海、黄海、台湾海峡也有六、七级的偏北风。
