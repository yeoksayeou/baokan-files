Title: East German Self-Funded Travelers Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd. The first group of ten self-funded travelers from the German Democratic Republic arrived in Beijing by plane on the 2nd. They will visit and tour Beijing, Wuhan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Wuxi for twenty-one days before returning to their country.

### 民主德国自费旅行者到京

1957-11-04
第2版()
专栏：

    民主德国自费旅行者到京
    新华社2日讯　德意志民主共和国的第一批自费旅行者一行十人2日乘飞机到达北京。他们将在北京、武汉、南京、上海、杭州和无锡等地参观、游览二十一天后回国。
