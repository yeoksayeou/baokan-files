Title: State Council Issues Orders Promulgating Two Regulations on Improving the Industrial and Commercial Management System
Author:
Date: 1957-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th—The State Council issued an order:

    The State Council's Regulations on Improving the Industrial Management System have been principally approved by the 84th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on November 14, 1957, and are now promulgated and will be implemented from 1958.
                Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai November 15, 1957

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th—The State Council issued an order:

    The State Council's Regulations on Improving the Commercial Management System have been principally approved by the 84th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on November 14, 1957, and are now promulgated and will be implemented from 1958.
                Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai November 15, 1957
                                        (Both sets of regulations are published on page 3)

### 国务院发布命令  公布改进工商管理体制两规定

1957-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院发布命令
    公布改进工商管理体制两规定
    新华社17日讯　国务院命令：
    国务院关于改进工业管理体制的规定，已经经过全国人民代表大会常务委员会一九五七年十一月十四日第八十四次会议原则批准，现在予以公布，自一九五八年起施行。
                国务院总理　周恩来　一九五七年十一月十五日
    新华社17日讯　国务院命令：
    国务院关于改进商业管理体制的规定，已经经过全国人民代表大会常务委员会一九五七年十一月十四日第八十四次会议原则批准，现在予以公布，自一九五八年起施行。
                国务院总理　周恩来　一九五七年十一月十五日
                                        （两项规定登在第三版）
