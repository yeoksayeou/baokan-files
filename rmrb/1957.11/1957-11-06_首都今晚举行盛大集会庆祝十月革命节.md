Title: The Capital Will Hold a Grand Rally Tonight to Celebrate the October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a grand rally on the evening of November 6 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of the celebration rally on its first program, starting at 18:45 on November 6.

### 首都今晚举行盛大集会庆祝十月革命节

1957-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    首都今晚举行盛大集会庆祝十月革命节
    新华社5日讯　首都各界人民将在11月6日晚上举行盛大集会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年。中央人民广播电台将从11月6日十八点四十五分开始，在第一种节目里转播庆祝大会的实况。
