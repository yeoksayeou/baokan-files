Title: Tomb Sweeping for Ten Members of the Art Troupe Who Died Last Year
Author:
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, 25th: Yesterday marked the one-year anniversary of the death of ten members of the Chinese Art Troupe who visited South America last year. Heads of the Ministry of Culture, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and other units, figures from literary and art circles and Peking opera circles, and family members of the martyrs went to Babaoshan to sweep the tombs. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, presented a wreath in front of the tomb.

### 祭扫去年遇难的艺术团十位团员墓

1957-11-26
第8版()
专栏：

    祭扫去年遇难的艺术团十位团员墓
    新华社25日讯　昨天是去年赴南美访问的中国艺术团十位团员遇难一周年。文化部、中国人民对外文化协会等单位负责人，文艺界、京剧界人士和烈士家属，前往八宝山扫墓。中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南在墓前献了花圈。
