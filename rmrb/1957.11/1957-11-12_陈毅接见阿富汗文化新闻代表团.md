Title: Chen Yi Receives Afghan Cultural and News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the head of the Cultural and News Delegation of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Abdul Sattar Shalizai, Deputy Director of the News Agency of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and members Abdul Rauf Bennawa, Director of Kabul Radio Station, as well as photographer Sultan Hamid and film photographer Akbar Shalizai on the morning of November 11th.
	Present at the reception were Zhang Zhixiang and Wang Yunsheng.
	During the meeting, Delegation Leader Abdul Sattar Shalizai also presented gifts to Vice Premier Chen Yi.
	The Cultural and News Delegation of the Kingdom of Afghanistan returned to Beijing on the 10th after visiting Urumqi.

### 陈毅接见阿富汗文化新闻代表团

1957-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见阿富汗文化新闻代表团
    新华社11日讯　陈毅副总理11日上午接见了阿富汗王国文化新闻代表团团长、阿富汗王国新闻署副署长阿卜杜勒·萨塔尔·沙利扎伊和团员、喀布尔广播电台台长阿卜杜勒·拉奥夫·本纳瓦，以及摄影师苏尔丹·哈米德和电影摄影师阿克巴·沙利扎伊。
    接见时，在座的有张致祥和王芸生。
    接见时，代表团团长阿卜杜勒·萨塔尔·沙利扎伊还向陈毅副总理赠送了礼品。
    阿富汗王国文化新闻代表团到乌鲁木齐访问后，10日回到北京。
