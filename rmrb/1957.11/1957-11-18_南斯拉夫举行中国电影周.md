Title: Yugoslav Film Week of Chinese Films Held

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, November 17th: "Chinese Film Week" grandly opened in Belgrade on the evening of the 16th. At the opening ceremony, the Chinese widescreen color news documentary "Festivals of May" and the famous feature film "The New Year Sacrifice" were screened for the first time to Yugoslav audiences.

Attending the opening ceremony were Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice President Djurakovic, Federal National Assembly Vice Presidents Simic and Šentjurc, and more than 800 people from all walks of life.

Charge d'affaires Zhou Qiuye and his wife from the Chinese Embassy in Yugoslavia were also invited to attend.

At the opening ceremony, Secretary-General Skrjabin of the Yugoslav Film Workers Association and Sheng Yaowu, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Yugoslavia, delivered speeches respectively.

"Chinese Film Week" will continue to be held in the capitals of the six republics of Yugoslavia for a total of twenty days. The films to be screened include the feature films "The New Year Sacrifice," "The White Haired Girl," "Chicken Feather Letter," "Mother," "Why are Crows Black," and "The Magic Brush," as well as four newsreels.

### 南斯拉夫举行中国电影周

1957-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫举行中国电影周
    据新华社贝尔格莱德17日电“中国电影周”16日晚在贝尔格莱德隆重开幕，开幕式上，首次向南斯拉夫观众放映了中国宽银幕五彩新闻纪录片“五月的节日”和著名故事片“祝福”。
    出席开幕式的有南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席乔拉科维奇、联邦国民议会副主席西米奇和申丘尔茨以及各界人士共八百多人。
    中国驻南斯拉夫临时代办周秋野夫妇也应邀出席。
    在开幕式上，南斯拉夫电影工作者协会秘书长斯克里京和中国驻南斯拉夫大使馆文化参赞盛耀武先后讲了话。
    “中国电影周”将继续在南斯拉夫六个共和国的首府举行，历时共二十天。放映的影片有“祝福”、“白毛女”、“鸡毛信”、“母亲”、“乌鸦为什么是黑的”、“神笔”这六部故事片以及四部新闻片。
