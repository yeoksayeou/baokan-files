Title: State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Announces List of Athletes of Master Rank in Football and Volleyball

Author:
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, 18th - The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China today announced the first list of forty-eight athletes of master rank in football and volleyball. Among them are twenty-two football athletes of master rank and twenty-six volleyball athletes of master rank (twelve men and fourteen women). The list is as follows:

    Football Athletes of Master Rank: Zhang Junxiu, Xu Fusheng, Zhang Jingtian, Chen Chengda, Piao Wanfu, Shi Wanchun, Nian Weisi, Fang Renqiu, Sun Fucheng, Zhang Honggen, Wang Lu (the above eleven are from the Beijing team), Chen Fulai, Gao Junshi, Ren Bin, Zhou Xing, Wang Xiwen, Wang Xinsheng, Xing Guifu, Ha Zengguang, Xian Dixiong (the above nine are from the "August 1st" team), Cong Anqing (First Machinery Sports Association team), Jin Changji (Tianjin team).

    Male Volleyball Athletes of Master Rank: Jiang Zhenhong, Wu Limin, Li Ceda, Xie Lie, Ye Zhuo, Chen Lixian (the above six are from the "August 1st" team), Cai Qiao, Ma Junyao, Zhu Ci, Wang Zuhong, Mei Tingchang, Mei Shounan (the above six are from the Beijing team).

    Female Volleyball Athletes of Master Rank: Zhang Guizhen, Wang Liuru, Zhang Xiaoxia, Liu Chongyang, Lin Hongzhu, Shang Yuhua (the above six are from the "August 1st" team), Wang Zishu, Hua Guiqing, Ma Renhua, Jin Dongxi (the above four are from the Beijing team), Tan Xin, Yin Yulian, Cui Huishu (the above three are from the Shenyang Institute of Physical Education team), Wang Defen (Chengdu Institute of Physical Education team).

### 国家体委公布一批足球排球运动健将名单

1957-11-19
第8版()
专栏：体育新闻

    国家体委公布一批足球排球运动健将名单
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会今天公布了第一批四十八名足球、排球运动健将名单。其中足球运动健将二十二名，排球运动健将二十六名（男十二名，女十四名）。名单如下：
    足球运动健将：张俊秀、徐福生、张京天、陈成达、朴万福、史万春、年维泗、方纫秋、孙福成、张宏根、王陆（以上十一名是北京队的）、陈复赉、高筠时、任彬、周兴、王锡文、王新生、邢桂福、哈增光、冼迪雄（以上九名是“八一”队的）、丛安庆（第一机械体育协会队）、金昌吉（天津队）。
    男子排球运动健将：江振洪、伍理民、李策大、谢烈、叶灼、陈立贤（以上六名是“八一”队的）、蔡翘、马俊耀、朱慈、王祖洪、梅庭昌、梅寿南（以上六名是北京队的）。
    女子排球运动健将：张桂珍、王六如、张晓霞、刘冲阳、林宏珠、尚玉华、（以上六名是“八一”队的）、王子淑、花桂卿、马纫华、金东熙（以上四名是北京队的）、谭心、尹玉莲、崔惠淑（以上三名是沈阳体育学院队的）、王德芬（成都体育学院队）。
