Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting, Passes 1958 National Construction Bonds Regulations
Author:
Date: 1957-11-10
Page: 1

    NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting
    Passes 1958 National Construction Bonds Regulations
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch The 83rd meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress was held on the morning of the 6th.
    After listening to explanations from Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Minister of Finance Wu Bo, the meeting discussed and approved the "1958 National Economic Construction Bonds Regulations." These regulations stipulate that the total amount of bonds for 1958 will be 630 million yuan, to be issued starting in January of next year; bond interest will be paid in full at the time of principal repayment, without compounding.
    After listening to an explanation from Zhang Yibai, Director of the State Council Personnel Bureau, the meeting approved the "Regulations on the Appointment and Removal of State Organ Personnel by People's Committees Above the County Level."
    The meeting also approved several appointment and removal cases.

### 人大常委会举行会议  通过1958年建设公债条例

1957-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行会议
    通过1958年建设公债条例
    新华社讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会第八十三次会议在6日上午举行。
    会议在听了周恩来总理和财政部副部长吴波的说明后，经过讨论，通过了“1958年国家经济建设公债条例”。这个条例规定1958年的公债总额为六亿三千万元，从明年1月份开始发行；公债利息在偿还本金时一次付给，不计复利。
    会议听取了国务院人事局局长章夷白的说明后，通过了“县级以上人民委员会任免国家机关工作人员条例”。
    会议还通过了几项任免案。
