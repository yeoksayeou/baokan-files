Title: History Society Holds Academic Report

Author:
Date: 1957-11-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency report: The Chinese History Society will hold an academic report meeting in Beijing on November 2nd to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the great October Revolution. Six academic papers written by historical workers to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution will be distributed and read at the meeting. The main contents of these papers are about the dictatorship of the proletariat, the Soviet Union's contribution to defending world peace, the friendly relations between China and Russia, the development of Sino-Soviet friendship, the impact of the October Revolution on Chinese advanced elements, and the impact of the October Revolution on the Chinese revolution.

Approximately 500 people are expected to attend this academic report meeting, including members of the Chinese History Society in Beijing, staff members of the historical research institutes of the Academy of Sciences, teachers from the history departments of Beijing universities, history teachers from some middle schools in Beijing, and relevant personnel from other units. This meeting will be a grand academic gathering for the historical community in Beijing.

The History Society has elected Fan Wenlan, Pan Zinian, Jian Bozan and eleven others to form the presidium to preside over the conference.

### 史学会举行学术报告

1957-11-01
第2版()
专栏：

    史学会举行学术报告
    据新华社讯　中国史学会为了庆祝伟大的十月革命四十周年，将于11月2日在北京举行学术报告会。历史工作者为庆祝十月革命四十周年所写的学术论文六篇将在会上散发并宣读。这些论文的主要内容是关于无产阶级专政、苏联对保卫世界和平的贡献、中俄友好关系、中苏友谊的发展、十月革命对中国先进分子的影响及十月革命对中国革命的影响等。
    准备参加这个学术报告会的有中国史学会在京会员、科学院各历史研究所的工作人员、北京各高等学校历史系教师、北京市部分中学历史教师及其他单位的有关人员共约五百余人。这个会将是北京史学界一次盛大的学术性的集会。
    史学会已推选范文澜、潘梓年、翦伯赞等十一人组成主席团主持大会。
