Title: Egypt Efficiently Manages the Canal: Unprecedented Record of Passing Vessels
Author:
Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 24th: The Egyptian Suez Canal Authority announced on November 23rd that a total of sixty-four ships passed through the Suez Canal on November 22nd. This is the highest daily traffic record since the canal opened in 1869. Before Egypt nationalized the canal, the daily traffic volume was generally forty-four ships.

Egyptian sources pointed out that this new record fully demonstrates the high efficiency of the Egyptians in managing the canal themselves. After Egypt nationalized the canal in July last year, Western countries slandered that the Egyptians were incapable of managing the canal.

### 埃及管理运河有效率  通过船只创空前纪录

1957-11-25
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及管理运河有效率  通过船只创空前纪录
    新华社开罗24日电　埃及苏伊士运河管理局11月23日宣布，共有六十四艘船只在11月22日通过苏伊士运河。这是运河自从1869年通航以来最高的每天通航纪录。在埃及把运河收归国有以前，运河每天的交通量一般是四十四艘。
    埃及人士指出，这个新纪录充分证明了埃及人自己管理运河的高度效率。在去年7月间埃及把运河收归国有以后，西方国家曾经诬蔑埃及人没有能力管理运河。
