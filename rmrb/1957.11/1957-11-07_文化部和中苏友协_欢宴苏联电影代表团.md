Title: Ministry of Culture and Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Host Banquet for Soviet Film Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Ministry of Culture and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a banquet at noon on the 6th in Beijing to welcome the Soviet Film Delegation.

The head of the delegation, Soviet Deputy Minister of Culture Surin, delegation members and famous Soviet film actress Kuznetsova, and delegation members and Soviet film directors Gigan and Naumov all attended the banquet.

Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Shen Junru, Deputy Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Director of the Film Bureau Wang Lanxi, Chairman of the China Film Workers Association Cai Chusheng, and many other well-known figures from China's film industry attended the banquet.

Sudalikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 文化部和中苏友协  欢宴苏联电影代表团

1957-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    文化部和中苏友协
    欢宴苏联电影代表团
    新华社6日讯　文化部和中苏友好协会总会6日午间在北京举行宴会，欢迎苏联电影代表团。
    代表团团长、苏联文化部副部长苏林，团员、苏联电影名演员库兹涅佐娃，团员、苏联电影导演吉甘和纳乌莫夫都出席了宴会。
    中苏友好协会总会副会长沈钧儒，文化部副部长夏衍、电影事业管理局局长王阑西，中国电影工作者联谊会主席蔡楚生和我国电影界著名人士等多人出席了宴会。
    苏联驻中国大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫也应邀出席了宴会。
