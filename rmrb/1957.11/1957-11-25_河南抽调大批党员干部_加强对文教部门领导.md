Title: Henan Transfers Large Numbers of Party Cadres to Strengthen Leadership over Cultural and Educational Departments
Author:
Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Henan has transferred more than 1,000 outstanding Communist Party leading cadres to cultural, educational, and news departments to strengthen the political and ideological leadership of these departments.

Among these cadres are more than 90 bureau, department, and section chiefs.

### 河南抽调大批党员干部  加强对文教部门领导

1957-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    河南抽调大批党员干部
    加强对文教部门领导
    新华社讯　河南已有一千多优秀的共产党员领导干部、调往文化、教育、新闻部门，以加强这些部门的政治思想领导。
    这批干部中包括九十多名厅、局、处长。
