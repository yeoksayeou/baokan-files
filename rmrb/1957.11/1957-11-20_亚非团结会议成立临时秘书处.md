Title: Asian-African Solidarity Conference Establishes Provisional Secretariat
Author:
Date: 1957-11-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 19th—Preparations for the Asian-African Solidarity Conference, scheduled for December 26, are actively underway. A provisional secretariat for the conference has now been established by representatives from Egypt, Syria, India, Indonesia, Burma, China, Japan, Ceylon, Algeria, Sudan, and the Soviet Union.

According to the "Al-Ahram" newspaper, representatives from nearly fifty countries will participate in the conference in Cairo.

### 亚非团结会议成立临时秘书处

1957-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非团结会议成立临时秘书处
    新华社19日讯　开罗消息：预定在12月26日举行的亚非团结会议的筹备工作，正在积极进行中。现在已由埃及、叙利亚、印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸、中国、日本、锡兰、阿尔及利亚、苏丹和苏联的代表成立了会议临时秘书处。
    据“金字塔报”报道，将有近五十个国家的代表参加在开罗举行的这个会议。
