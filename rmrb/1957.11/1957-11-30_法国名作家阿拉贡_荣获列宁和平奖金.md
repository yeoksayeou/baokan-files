Title: Famous French Writer Aragon Awarded Lenin Peace Prize
Author:
Date: 1957-11-30
Page: 5

    French Writer Aragon
    Wins Lenin Peace Prize
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 27th: According to Soviet newspapers, the "International Lenin Peace Prize for Strengthening International Peace" Committee held a meeting from November 5th to 7th. The meeting discussed the list of candidates for the award and decided to award the 1956 Lenin International Peace Prize.
    According to the pre-determined regulations, the committee's decision will be published on January 1, 1958.
    To celebrate the sixtieth birthday (November 11th) of the famous French writer and social activist Louis Aragon and to commend his great contributions to the struggle for maintaining and strengthening peace, the committee passed a resolution to award him the Lenin International Peace Prize.

### 法国名作家阿拉贡  荣获列宁和平奖金

1957-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    法国名作家阿拉贡
    荣获列宁和平奖金
    新华社莫斯科27日电　据苏联报纸报道：“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金委员会11月5日到7日举行了会议。会议讨论了得奖候选人的名单，决定颁发1956年列宁国际和平奖金。
    根据事先决定的章程，委员会的决议将在1958年1月1日发表。
    为祝贺法国著名作家、社会活动家路易·阿拉贡的六十岁生日（11月11日）和表彰他在维护和加强和平的斗争中的巨大功绩，委员会通过了授予他列宁国际和平奖金的决定。
