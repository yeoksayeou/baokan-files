Title: Khrushchev Invites Chairman Mao to See "Swan Lake"
Author:
Date: 1957-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th - Chairman Mao Zedong, at the invitation of Comrade Khrushchev, watched Tchaikovsky's ballet "Swan Lake" at the Soviet Grand Theatre on the 15th.

Accompanying Chairman Mao Zedong to the performance were Comrade Khrushchev, Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, and members of the Chinese delegation.

Chairman Mao Zedong and Comrade Khrushchev were warmly welcomed by the audience in the theatre.

### 赫鲁晓夫请毛主席看“天鹅湖”

1957-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫请毛主席看“天鹅湖”
    新华社莫斯科16日电  毛泽东主席应赫鲁晓夫同志的邀请，15日在苏联大剧院观看了柴可夫斯基的芭蕾舞剧“天鹅湖”。
    陪同毛泽东主席观剧的有赫鲁晓夫同志，苏联驻中国大使尤金和中国代表团人员。
    毛泽东主席和赫鲁晓夫同志受到剧院观众的热烈欢迎。
