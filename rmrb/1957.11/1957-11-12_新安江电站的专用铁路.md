Title: Special Railway Line for Xin'an River Hydropower Station

Author:

Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 3

Construction News:

    The special Lanxi-Tongguan railway line, built to support the Xin'an River Hydropower Station project, has recently been laid to the construction site and has begun operations. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 新安江电站的专用铁路

1957-11-12
第3版()
专栏：建设动态

    新安江电站的专用铁路
  　为支援新安江水力发电站工程而修建的兰（溪）铜（官）铁路专用线，已经在最近铺轨到工地，开始通车。　（据新华社讯）
