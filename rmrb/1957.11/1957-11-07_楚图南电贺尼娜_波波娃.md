Title: Chu Tunan Sends Telegram of Congratulations to Nina Popova
Author:
Date: 1957-11-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, sent a telegram on the 6th to Nina Popova, Chairman of the Board of the Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, congratulating her on the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.

The telegram stated, "As the working people of the world celebrate the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, please allow me, on behalf of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, to extend my warm congratulations to you and, through you, to the Soviet people, and to express my highest respect for the great and glorious achievements of the Soviet Union in science, technology, and culture over the past forty years. May the fraternal friendship and cooperation between cultural workers of China and the Soviet Union continue to grow, and may the cultural exchanges between China and the Soviet Union be constantly consolidated and developed. Let the victorious banner of the great October Socialist Revolution forever illuminate our path forward!"

### 楚图南电贺尼娜·波波娃

1957-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    楚图南电贺尼娜·波波娃
    新华社6日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南6日致电苏联对外文化协会理事会主席尼娜·波波娃，祝贺十月革命四十周年。
    电报说，“值此全世界劳动人民欢庆伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年之际，请允许我代表中国人民对外文化协会向您并通过您向苏联人民致以热烈的祝贺，并对于四十年来苏联在科学技术文化工作上的伟大光辉的成就，表示崇高的敬意。祝中苏两国文化工作者的兄弟友谊和合作不断增进，中苏两国文化交流工作不断巩固和发展。让伟大的十月社会主义革命的胜利的旗帜永远照耀着我们前进！”
