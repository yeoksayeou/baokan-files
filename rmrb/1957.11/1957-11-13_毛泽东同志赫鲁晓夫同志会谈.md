Title: Comrade Mao Zedong Meets with Comrade Khrushchev
Author:
Date: 1957-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th -- Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Mao Zedong, held talks with First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Comrade Khrushchev, at the Central Committee Building of the CPSU from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM (Moscow time) on the afternoon of the 11th. The atmosphere of the talks was extremely warm and friendly.

The meeting was attended by Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Fedelin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

### 毛泽东同志赫鲁晓夫同志会谈

1957-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东同志赫鲁晓夫同志会谈
    新华社莫斯科12日电　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东11日下午五时到八时（莫斯科时间）同苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫在苏共中央大厦举行了会谈。谈话的气氛极为热忱友好。
    会谈时在座的有中国共产党中央委员会总书记邓小平和苏联外交部副部长费德林。
