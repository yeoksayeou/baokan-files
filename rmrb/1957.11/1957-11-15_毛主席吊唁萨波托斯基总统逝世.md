Title: Chairman Mao Mourns President Zápotocký's Death
Author:
Date: 1957-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 14th: Chairman Mao Zedong went to the Czechoslovakian Embassy in the Soviet Union at 3:30 PM (Moscow time) on the 14th to mourn the death of Czechoslovakian President Antonín Zápotocký, and laid a wreath in front of President Zápotocký's portrait.

Accompanying Chairman Mao Zedong to the mourning were Chinese delegation members Soong Ching Ling, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Dehuai, Guo Moruo, Li Xiannian, Ulanhu, Lu Dingyi, Chen Boda, Shen Yanbing, Yang Shangkun, Hu Qiaomu, Liu Xiao, and Saifudin.

Chairman Mao Zedong, accompanied by Czechoslovakian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Vosahlik, observed a moment of silence in front of President Zápotocký's portrait. Chairman Mao Zedong requested Ambassador Vosahlik to convey his deep condolences on the death of President Zápotocký.

### 毛主席吊唁萨波托斯基总统逝世

1957-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席吊唁萨波托斯基总统逝世
    新华社莫斯科14日电　毛泽东主席14日下午三时三十分（莫斯科时间）前往捷克斯洛伐克驻苏联大使馆，吊唁捷克斯洛伐克总统安托宁·萨波托斯基同志的逝世，并且在萨波托斯基总统的遗像前献花圈。
    随同毛泽东主席前往吊唁的有中国代表团团员宋庆龄、邓小平、彭德怀、郭沫若、李先念、乌兰夫、陆定一、陈伯达、沈雁冰、杨尚昆、胡乔木、刘晓、赛福鼎。
    毛泽东主席在捷克斯洛伐克驻苏联大使沃萨格里克陪同下，在萨波托斯基总统遗像前默哀。毛泽东主席面请沃萨格里克大使转达对萨波托斯基总统逝世的沉痛悼念。
