Title: Soviet Party and Government Leaders Receive Delegations from East Germany, North Korea, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Yugoslavia; Mikoyan and Others Hold Talks with Egyptian Defense Minister Amer
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, November 11th - According to a TASS report, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, on November 10th, separately received the delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Ulbricht, the delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea headed by Kim Il-sung, the delegation of Czechoslovakia headed by Novotny, and the delegation of Yugoslavia headed by Kardelj, who were participating in the celebrations for the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.
    Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, also separately received the delegations of the German Democratic Republic, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Poland, and Yugoslavia on the same day.
    Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, also separately received the delegations of East Germany, North Korea, Poland, and Yugoslavia on the same day.
    Xinhua News Agency, November 11th, Moscow dispatch: Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Mikoyan, Soviet Minister of Defense Marshal Malinovsky, and Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Semenov received Egyptian Defense Minister General Amer, who was invited to visit the Soviet Union, on the 10th, and held friendly and frank talks with him on issues of Soviet-Egyptian relations.
    General Amer and his entourage also visited the Central "Shooting" Military Training Class on the same day.

### 苏联党和政府领导人  接见德朝波捷南代表团  米高扬等和埃及国防部长阿密尔会谈

1957-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联党和政府领导人
    接见德朝波捷南代表团
    米高扬等和埃及国防部长阿密尔会谈
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社报道，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫11月10日分别接见了参加十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼的以乌布利希为首的德意志民主共和国代表团、以金日成为首的朝鲜代表团、以诺沃提尼为首的捷克斯洛伐克代表团和以卡德尔为首的南斯拉夫代表团。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫在这一天也分别接见了德意志民主共和国代表团、朝鲜代表团、波兰代表团和南斯拉夫代表团。
    苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁同一天也分别接见了民主德国、朝鲜、波兰和南斯拉夫代表团。
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议副主席米高扬、苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅和苏联外交部副部长谢苗诺夫在10日接见了应邀来苏联访问的埃及国防部长阿密尔将军，并且就苏埃关系问题同他进行了友好坦率的交谈。
    阿密尔将军和随行人员这一天还参观了中央“射击”军事训练班。
