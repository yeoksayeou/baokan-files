Title: Soviet "Sputnik" Documentary Premieres in Beijing on the 4th

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 8

    Soviet "Sputnik" Documentary
    Premieres in Beijing on the 4th

    Xinhua News Agency, November 4th. More than a thousand Beijing audience members saw the Soviet documentary film "Sputnik" for the first time today. Currently, the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio is rushing to translate this documentary. This film will be available to the general public in Beijing on November 8th.

    The film documents the preparations made by the Soviet Union before launching the first artificial satellite, schematic diagrams of the artificial satellite orbiting in the air after its launch, the observations of the artificial satellite in various parts of the Soviet Union, and congratulatory telegrams sent to the Soviet Union from all over the world.

    When the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture and the China Film Workers Association held a celebration today to commemorate the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, this untranslated documentary was screened.

### 苏联“人造卫星”纪录片  4日在北京首次上演

1957-11-05
第8版()
专栏：

    苏联“人造卫星”纪录片
    4日在北京首次上演
    新华社4日讯　一千多名北京观众今天第一次看到了苏联纪录影片“人造卫星”。目前，中央新闻纪录电影制片厂正在突击译制这部纪录片。11月8日这部影片就可以同北京广大观众见面。
    影片纪录了苏联在发射第一颗人造卫星以前的准备工作，人造卫星发射以后在空中运行的示意图，苏联各地观测人造卫星的情形，和世界各地拍给苏联的贺电等。
    文化部电影事业管理局和中国电影工作者联谊会今天举行庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年纪念大会时，放映了这部未经译制的纪录片。
