Title: Cambodian King Signs Decree Confirming Cambodia as a Neutral Country
Author:
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 18th. According to the Cambodian News Agency, the King of Cambodia signed a decree on November 6th, promulgating the law on neutrality that was passed for the second time by the National Assembly on September 11th of this year.

    The law stipulates: 1. The Kingdom of Cambodia is a neutral country, it does not establish any military or ideological alliance with foreign countries, and does not invade any other countries; 2. When Cambodia is subjected to foreign aggression, it reserves the following rights: to defend itself with arms, to appeal to the United Nations, and to appeal to a friendly major power; 3. This law shall take effect immediately.

### 柬国王签署法令确定  柬埔寨是中立国家

1957-11-19
第6版()
专栏：

    柬国王签署法令确定
    柬埔寨是中立国家
    新华社金边18日电　据柬埔寨通讯社报道，柬埔寨国王在11月6日签署了一项法令，颁布国民议会在今年9月11日第二次通过的关于中立的法律。
    法律规定：一、柬埔寨王国是一个中立国家，它不同外国建立任何军事或意识形态上的联盟，不侵略任何其它国家；二、当柬埔寨受到外国侵略的时候，保留下列权利：自己用武装自卫，向联合国发出呼吁，向一个友好的大国发出呼吁；三、这项法律即时生效。
