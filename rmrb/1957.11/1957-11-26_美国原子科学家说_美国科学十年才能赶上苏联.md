Title: American Atomic Scientist Says: U.S. Science Will Need Ten Years to Catch Up with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th. According to a United Press Washington dispatch, the famous American atomic scientist Dr. Teller said in a television broadcast that the United States would need ten years to catch up with the Soviet Union, which is now in the leading position in scientific development.

### 美国原子科学家说  美国科学十年才能赶上苏联

1957-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    美国原子科学家说
    美国科学十年才能赶上苏联
    新华社25日讯　据合众社华盛顿消息，美国著名的原子科学家戴勒博士在电视广播中说，美国需要十年才能赶上现在在科学发展上居于首位的苏联。
