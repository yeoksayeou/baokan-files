Title: Cambodian King and Queen Host Banquet for Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-17
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 16th -- King Norodom Suramarit and Queen Kossamak of Cambodia held a cold buffet in the Moon Hall of the Royal Palace on the evening of the 15th to entertain the Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation.

Those attending the reception included Cambodian princesses and princes, members of the Royal High Council, Nhiek Tioulong and Saukham Khoy, Prime Minister Sim Var and all the ministers, and members of the Cambodian Royal Dance Troupe and the National Theatre. Ye Jinghao, head of the Chinese Economic Delegation to Cambodia, and some members of the delegation also attended the reception.

At the reception, the King and Queen of Cambodia presented the gifts for Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Zhou Enlai and his wife, and Marshal He Long and his wife to the Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation, and also presented gifts to all members of the delegation.

At the reception, the head and deputy head of the Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation, Ding Xilin and Wu Qing, were invited to sit with the King and Queen. The participants at the reception danced happily in the magnificent hall to the accompaniment of Cambodian and Chinese music. The hall was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere.

At the end of the reception, a documentary film about the visit of the Cambodian Cultural and Artistic Delegation to China and a documentary film about the family life of the King and Queen of Cambodia were shown.

The reception lasted for six hours, ending at 2:00 AM on the 16th.

The Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation will continue to perform in Phnom Penh for two days and then go to the provinces to perform. The work of entertaining the Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation in the provinces is in full swing.

### 柬埔寨国王和王后  宴请我文化艺术代表团

1957-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国王和王后
    宴请我文化艺术代表团
    据新华社金边16日电  柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特和王后15日晚间在王宫里的月厅举行冷餐会，招待中国文化艺术代表团。
    出席招待会的有柬埔寨的公主们和王子们、皇廷最高会议的成员兰·涅特和桑云、首相沈法和全体大臣们、柬埔寨王家舞蹈团和国家剧团的团员们。中国驻柬埔寨经济代表团团长叶景灏和一些团员也出席了招待会。
    在招待会上，柬埔寨国王和王后把赠给毛泽东主席、周恩来总理和夫人、贺龙元帅和夫人的礼品交给中国文化艺术代表团，并且向代表团全体团员赠送了礼物。
    在招待会上，中国文化艺术代表团正副团长丁西林和吴青都被邀请同国王和王后坐在一起。招待会的参加者在这个装饰华丽的大厅里，在柬埔寨和中国音乐的伴奏下快乐地跳舞。大厅里充满热烈友好的气氛。
    招待会最后放映了关于柬埔寨文化艺术代表团访问中国情况的记录影片和有关柬埔寨国王和王后家庭生活的记录影片。
    这个招待会进行了六个小时，直到16日早上两点钟才结束。
    中国文化艺术代表团将在金边继续表演两天，然后到各省去演出。各省招待中国文化艺术代表团的工作正在热烈进行。
