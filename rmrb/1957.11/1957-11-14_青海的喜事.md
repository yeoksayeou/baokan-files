Title: Qinghai's Happy Events
Author:
Date: 1957-11-14
Page: 3
Construction Updates:

    Qinghai Province's local state-owned Huangzhong County People's Power Plant and the joint state-private Lusha'er Flour Mill were put into production simultaneously on the 7th. Zha Xi Wang Xu (Tibetan), Secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Deputy Governor of Qinghai Province, Ma Xingtai (Hui), Vice Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and lamas from the Ta'er Monastery attended the celebration and commissioning ceremony.
    The power generation capacity of the Huangzhong County People's Power Plant is 240 kilowatts. The Lusha'er Flour Mill was jointly invested in and built by industrialists and the state. This flour mill can produce about 100,000 jin of flour per day.
                    (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 青海的喜事

1957-11-14
第3版()
专栏：建设动态

    青海的喜事
    青海省地方国营湟中县人民电厂和公私合营鲁沙尔面粉厂，于7日同时投入生产。中共青海省委书记、青海省副省长扎喜旺徐（藏族），青海省政协副主席马兴泰（回族）和塔尔寺喇嘛等都参加了庆祝开车典礼。
    湟中县人民电厂的发电能力为二百四十瓩。鲁沙尔面粉厂是由工商业者等和国家共同投资兴建的。这个面粉厂每天可生产面粉十万斤左右。
                    （据新华社讯）
