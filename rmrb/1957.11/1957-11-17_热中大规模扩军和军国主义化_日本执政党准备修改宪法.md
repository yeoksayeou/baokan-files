Title:  Obsessed with Large-Scale Military Expansion and Militarization: Japanese Ruling Party Prepares to Revise the Constitution
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th:  Japanese Parliamentary Vice-Minister for Foreign Affairs Takazo Matsumoto openly declared in a radio broadcast today that the ruling Liberal Democratic Party intends to revise the current constitution next year, which prohibits Japan from rearming.

Matsumoto believes that Article 9 of the current constitution is problematic. This clause stipulates that Japan forever renounces war and the use of force as means of settling international disputes, and that it shall not maintain land, sea, and air forces, or other war potential. Matsumoto said that this clause hinders Japan's "security." "We want to revise the constitution in order to eliminate this clause on war issues and other clauses."

He said that in order to achieve the goal of revising the constitution, the Liberal Democratic Party will try to win two-thirds of the seats in the Diet in next year's general election, which is necessary to amend the constitution.

In order to revise the constitution, carry out large-scale military expansion and militarization according to the requirements of the United States, the Kishi Nobusuke Cabinet established a "Constitution Investigation Committee" this August, which no previous cabinet had ever established, to carry out preparations for revising the constitution.

### 热中大规模扩军和军国主义化  日本执政党准备修改宪法

1957-11-17
第6版()
专栏：

    热中大规模扩军和军国主义化
    日本执政党准备修改宪法
    据新华社16日讯  东京消息：日本外务省政务次官松本泷藏今天在广播谈话中公开宣称，执政的自由民主党要在明年修改规定禁止日本重整军备的现行宪法。
    松本认为，现行宪法中成问题的是第九条。这一条款规定日本永远放弃用战争和武力手段来解决国际争端，并且不得保有陆、海、空军以及其他作战力量。松本说，这一条款妨碍日本的“安全”。“我们要修改宪法以便取消这一有关战争问题的条款和其他条款”。
    他说，为了达到修改宪法的目的，自由民主党要设法在明年举行的大选中争取在国会里获得修改宪法所必需的三分之二的议席。
    为了根据美国的要求来修改宪法，实行大规模的扩军和军国主义化，岸信介内阁已经在今年8月成立了历届前任内阁都没有成立的“宪法调查会”，进行修改宪法的准备工作。
