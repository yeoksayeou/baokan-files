Title: Picture
Author: Fu Jun
Date: 1957-11-29
Page: 8

    November 24th, more than 400 students from the Beijing Institute of Geology, treading on the white snow, went to the Qiansheng Agricultural Cooperative in Haidian District, Beijing, to dig irrigation ditches, helping the agricultural cooperative transform a piece of dry land into paddy fields. Xinhua News Agency reporter Fu Jun photographed.
