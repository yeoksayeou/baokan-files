Title: Jilin Province Delegation to Vladivostok
Author:
Date: 1957-11-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, November 2nd: A ten-member delegation from Jilin Province, led by Guan Shanfu, Secretary of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, departed from Changchun on the 2nd at the invitation of the Soviet Communist Party's Primorsky Krai Committee and the Primorsky Krai Soviet, to attend the Soviet Primorsky Krai and Vladivostok laboring people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution in the city of Vladivostok, Soviet Union.

### 吉林省代表团去海参崴

1957-11-03
第2版()
专栏：

    吉林省代表团去海参崴
    新华社长春2日电　吉林省代表团一行十人在中共吉林省委书记关山复率领下，应苏联共产党沿海边区党委和沿海边区苏维埃的邀请，2日自长春出发，前往苏联海参崴市参加苏联沿海边区和海参崴市劳动人民庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年大会。
