Title: Dutch Submarine Sails to Indonesia; Indonesian Military Expresses Vigilance Against All Provocations
Author:
Date: 1957-11-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 28th. According to a report in today's "Indonesia Torch" in Jakarta, quoting a reliable source, the Netherlands has sent its submarine to Indonesian territorial waters.

Dutch banks in Indonesia have also withdrawn loans to Indonesian national capitalists.

The Indonesian Army Headquarters spokesman told "Indonesia Torch" that the authorities are always paying attention and are vigilant against all provocative actions from any source that disrupt social order and national independence.

### 荷兰潜艇开到印度尼西亚  印度尼西亚军方表示警惕一切挑衅行动

1957-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    荷兰潜艇开到印度尼西亚
    印度尼西亚军方表示警惕一切挑衅行动
    新华社雅加达28日电　据雅加达“印度尼西亚火炬报”今天引述一位权威人士透露的消息说，荷兰已经把它的潜水艇派到印度尼西亚的领海来。
    在印度尼西亚的荷兰银行也收回了贷给印度尼西亚民族资本家的贷款。
    印度尼西亚陆军总部发言人对“印度尼西亚火炬报”发表谈话说，当局永远注意和警惕来自任何方面的破坏社会秩序和国家独立的一切挑衅行动。
