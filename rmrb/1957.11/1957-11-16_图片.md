\
    About 100,000 youths in Jakarta "held a torchlight parade" on October 28 to commemorate "Youth Pledge Day," the day Indonesian youth called for independence in 1928. This is a picture of the procession passing in front of the Dutch diplomatic representative's residence, setting fire to the embodiment of Dutch colonialism (made of woven straw mats). Photo by Zhao Qingfu (Xinhua News Agency draft)

专栏：

    雅加达青年约十万人在10月28日“举行了火炬大游行”，纪念印度尼西亚青年在1928年要求独立的节日——“青年宣誓日”。这是队伍行至荷兰外交代表住宅前，点火焚烧荷兰殖民主义者的化身（用草席编扎）。赵庆福摄（新华社稿）
