Title: New Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China Presents Letter of Credence to Foreign Minister Zhou
Author:
Date: 1957-11-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th - Mr. Slingenberg, the new Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China, paid a visit to Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, on the afternoon of the 19th and presented his letter of credence as Chargé d'Affaires.

Present at the meeting were Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 荷兰新任驻华代办  向周外长递交委任书

1957-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    荷兰新任驻华代办
    向周外长递交委任书
    新华社19日讯　荷兰新任驻我国代办司灵博先生19日下午拜会中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来，并递交代办委任书。
    在座的有外交部西欧司司长黄华，礼宾司副司长马振武。
