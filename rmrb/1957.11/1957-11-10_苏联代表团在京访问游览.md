Title: Soviet Delegation Visits and Tours Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: The Soviet delegation of representatives from all sectors, participating in the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, visited relevant Chinese organizations today and met with leaders of various Chinese people's organizations.

The head of the delegation, Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, accompanied by Gutikov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, visited the headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association this afternoon. Vice President Wu Yuzhang and others warmly received the distinguished Soviet guests and introduced them to the grand celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution in China. Chairman Andreyev also visited the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center of Beijing and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Gallery on Xidan Street today.

Delegation member Cherednichenko, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Railway Workers' Union, met this afternoon with Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Minister of Railways Teng Daiyuan, and Wang Zhijie and Xiao Hongqi, leaders of the Chinese Railway Union; the three female members of the delegation, Volkova, Andreyeva, and Bulgina, visited Yang Zhihua and Kang Keqing, Vice-Presidents of the All-China Women's Federation, this afternoon; Kozlovsky, Secretary of the Kuibyshev Regional Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, visited Hu Kezhi at the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League; Umarov, President of the Tajik Republic Academy of Sciences, held discussions with Vice-Presidents Wu Youxun and Zhang Jingfu and Chinese scientists at the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Novgorodsky, Deputy Secretary-General of the Soviet Asian Solidarity Committee, visited Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee; Old Bolshevik and participant in the Civil War, Kislenko, was invited by Marshal Nie Rongzhen to dinner. Composer Muradeli met with Chinese musicians Lü Ji, Ma Ke, and Qu Xixian at the Chinese Musicians Association and participated in sales work at the open-air book market in the Working People's Cultural Palace.

This morning, the entire delegation, accompanied by Vice Minister of Culture Zhang Zhixiang, toured the Forbidden City.

### 苏联代表团在京访问游览

1957-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团在京访问游览
    新华社9日讯　苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团今天分别访问了中国各有关团体，会晤了中国各人民团体的负责人。
    代表团团长、苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫由苏联驻华大使馆临时代办顾悌克夫陪同，在今天下午访问了中苏友好协会总会。总会副会长吴玉章等热情地接待了苏联贵宾，并且向他们介绍了中国庆祝十月革命四十周年的盛况。安德烈耶夫团长今天还参观了北京市中苏友协文化馆和西单街头的中苏友好画廊。
    代表团团员、苏联铁路工会中央委员会主席切烈德尼钦科今天下午先后会见了中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，铁道部部长滕代远和中国铁路工会负责人王志杰、萧洪启；代表团的三位女团员沃尔科娃、安德烈耶娃和布尔金娜，今天下午访问了全国妇联副主席杨之华和康克清；全苏列宁共产主义青年团古比雪夫州委员会书记科兹洛夫斯基到共青团中央委员会访问了胡克实；塔吉克共和国科学院院长乌玛罗夫到中国科学院同副院长吴有训、张劲夫和中国科学家进行了座谈；苏联亚洲团结委员会副秘书长诺夫哥罗德斯基访问了中国亚洲团结委员会副主席廖承志；老布尔什维克、国内战争参加者基斯连科应聂荣臻元帅的邀请共进了晚餐。作曲家穆拉德里到中国音乐家协会会见了中国音乐家吕骥、马可和瞿希贤，并且到劳动人民文化宫的露天书市参加售卖工作。
    今天上午，代表团全体由文化部副部长张致祥陪同游览了故宫。
