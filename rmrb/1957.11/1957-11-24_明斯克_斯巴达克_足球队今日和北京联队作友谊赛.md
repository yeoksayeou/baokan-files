Author:

    Minsk "Spartak" Football Team to Play Friendly Match with Beijing Team Today
    (Our newspaper dispatch) After visiting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Soviet Belarusian Minsk "Spartak" football team, passing through our country, will play a friendly match with the Beijing Team at the Beijing Xian Nong Tan Stadium at 2:30 pm on the 24th. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the match live on the second program frequency starting at 2:30 pm. The starting lineups for both sides are as follows:
