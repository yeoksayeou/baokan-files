Title: Egyptian Defense Minister Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-11-03
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 2nd. TASS, Moscow: Egyptian Defense Minister General Abdel Hakim Amer, at the invitation of Soviet Defense Minister Malinovsky, arrived in Moscow on the 1st, leading a group of Egyptian army generals.
    Marshal Malinovsky and senior Soviet military officers were at Vnukovo Airport to welcome them. Egyptian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Kouni and Military Attaché Tohami also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 埃国防部长抵莫斯科

1957-11-03
第6版()
专栏：

    埃国防部长抵莫斯科
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：埃及国防部长阿卜杜勒·哈基姆·阿密尔将军应苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基的邀请，1日率领一批埃及军队的将官到达莫斯科。
    前往伏努科夫机场欢迎的有马利诺夫斯基元帅和苏军高级将领。埃及驻苏联大使库尼和武官托哈米也前往机场欢迎。
