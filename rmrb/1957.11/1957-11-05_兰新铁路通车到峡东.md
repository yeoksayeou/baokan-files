Title: Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway Opens to Xiadong
Author:
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 4

    The Lanzhou-Urumqi-Aktogay Railway, within China, has been opened for 1,024 kilometers to Xiadong Station.
    On the 1st, the first passenger trains ran between Lanzhou and Xiadong, and through transport with the national railway system also began.
    Xiadong is located at the western end of the Hexi Corridor in Gansu Province, only 130 kilometers away from the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region to the west. To the south, crossing the Qilian Mountains, lies the Qaidam Basin, with a highway connecting to Lhasa.

### 兰新铁路通车到峡东

1957-11-05
第4版()
专栏：

    兰新铁路通车到峡东
    兰州—乌鲁木齐—阿克斗卡铁路我国境内段，已经通车一千零二十四公里到峡东车站。
    1日，从兰州和峡东两地第一次对开了一列客车，这段铁路和全国铁路的联运也同时开始。
    峡东在甘肃省河西走廊的西端，西距新疆维吾尔自治区只有一百三十公里，向南翻过祁连山就是柴达木盆地，并有公路同拉萨衔接。
