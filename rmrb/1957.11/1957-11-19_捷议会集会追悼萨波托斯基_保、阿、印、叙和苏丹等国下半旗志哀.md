Title: Czechoslovak Parliament Holds Memorial Gathering for Zápotocký; Bulgaria, Argentina, India, Syria, and Sudan Lower Flags in Mourning

Author:

Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 18th: The Czechoslovak National Assembly held a special meeting in Prague today to commemorate President Zápotocký.

Czechoslovak Communist Party and government leaders such as Bacílek, Barák, Ďuriš, Fierlinger, Kopecký, Novotný, Široký, and Jankovcová attended the meeting.

National Assembly Chairman Fierlinger spoke at the meeting, emphasizing Zápotocký's outstanding role in establishing a people's democratic Czechoslovakia and in building socialism in Czechoslovakia. Fierlinger said that Zápotocký came from the people and dedicated everything to the prosperity, progress, and bright future of the people.

Compiled report: According to Xinhua News Agency, November 18th marked the funeral of Czechoslovak President Zápotocký. On this day, the Councils of Ministers of Bulgaria and Albania declared national mourning. All government buildings in New Delhi, the capital of India, lowered their flags to half-mast in mourning. The Syrian Prime Minister's Office announced that flags would be flown at half-mast throughout the country. The governments of Sudan and Argentina declared this day a national day of mourning.

The Czechoslovak Communist Party and government continue to receive telegrams of condolence from around the world on the death of President Zápotocký. The CPC Central Committee received condolences from the Communist Parties of the United States, the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, Syria and Lebanon, Iraq, Jordan, Luxembourg, and other countries. The Government of the Czechoslovak Republic received condolences from Lebanese President Chamoun, Bolivian President Hernán Siles Zuazo, U.S. President Eisenhower, Japanese Emperor Hirohito, and Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan.

The National Assembly received condolences from Indonesian Parliamentary Speaker Sardjono.

Delegations from the Soviet Union, Poland, East Germany, Romania, Hungary, Bulgaria, Albania, Yugoslavia, Vietnam, Korea, and Mongolia, who are attending President Zápotocký's funeral, have arrived in Prague successively.

At 4:00 PM on the 17th, Comrade Voroshilov, the head of the delegation, and delegation members Ignatov, Konev, Korotchenko, and Grishin participated in the vigil.

The Austrian government delegation, led by Deputy Minister of the Interior Grubelhofer, as well as Burmese representative Tekin Chit Maung and Cambodian representative Tiao Long, have also arrived in Prague.

In addition, the governments of France, the United States, Egypt, the United Kingdom, Iceland, Finland, Norway, Afghanistan, Switzerland, Iran, Belgium, Greece, and Italy have sent their ambassadors and ministers to Czechoslovakia to attend President Zápotocký's funeral.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 18th: On the afternoon of the 17th, the people of Czechoslovakia continued to go to Prague Castle to bid farewell to President Zápotocký.

Prague welcomed thousands of people from all over the republic. They entered Prague Castle to pay their last respects to President Zápotocký. They were silent, deeply mourning, signing their names in the condolence book, and solemnly expressing their determination before the President's remains. They expressed their firm resolve to follow the President's example to strengthen the country's strength and defend world peace through national construction.

The Czechoslovak Writers' Association held a memorial service on the 17th to mourn President Zápotocký.

Foreign diplomatic representatives stationed in Prague also went to the Spanish Hall to bid farewell to President Zápotocký. (With Pictures)

### 捷议会集会追悼萨波托斯基  保、阿、印、叙和苏丹等国下半旗志哀

1957-11-19
第5版()
专栏：

    捷议会集会追悼萨波托斯基
    保、阿、印、叙和苏丹等国下半旗志哀
    新华社布拉格18日电　捷克斯洛伐克国民议会今天在布拉格举行特别会议，追悼萨波托斯基总统。
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府的领导人巴契列克、巴拉克、杜兰斯基、费林格、柯别茨基、诺沃提尼、西罗基和扬科夫卓娃等都出席了会议。
    国民议会主席费林格在会上讲了话，他强调指出萨波托斯基在建立人民民主的捷克斯洛伐克，在捷克斯洛伐克建设社会主义中所起的杰出的作用。费林格说，萨波托斯基来自人民，为人民的繁荣、进步和美好的未来贡献了一切。
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：11月18日是捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基安葬的日子，在这一天，保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚两国部长会议宣布全国哀悼，印度首都新德里所有的政府机关大厦都下半旗表示哀悼，叙利亚总理办公厅宣布全国各地下半旗志哀，苏丹和阿根廷两国政府决定这一天为全国哀悼日。
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府继续收到来自世界各地的吊唁萨波托斯基总统逝世的电报。捷共中央收到了美国共产党、加拿大劳工进步党、叙利亚和黎巴嫩、伊拉克、约旦、卢森堡等国共产党的唁电。捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府收到了黎巴嫩总统夏蒙、玻利维亚总统埃南·西拉斯·萨乌索、美国总统艾森豪威尔、日本天皇裕仁、印度副总统拉达克里希南的唁电。
    国民议会收到了印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺的唁电。
    参加萨波托斯基总统葬仪的苏联、波兰、民主德国、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、南斯拉夫、越南、朝鲜、蒙古代表团已经陆续到达布拉格。
    17日下午四时，伏罗希洛夫团长和团员伊格纳托夫、科涅夫、科罗特钦科和格里申参加守灵。
    到达布拉格的还有以内政部次长格鲁布霍弗尔为首的奥地利政府代表团、缅甸代表德钦漆貌和柬埔寨代表刁隆。
    另外，法国、美国、埃及、英国、冰岛、芬兰、挪威、阿富汗、瑞士、伊朗、比利时、希腊和意大利等国的政府，委派它们驻捷克斯洛伐克的大使和公使参加萨波托斯基总统的葬礼。
    据新华社布拉格18日电　捷克斯洛伐克人民17日下半天继续到布拉格宫向萨波托斯基总统告别。
    布拉格接待着成千的来自共和国各地的人们。他们走进布拉格宫，最后一次瞻仰萨波托斯基总统的遗容。他们静默，深深地哀悼，在悼念册上签上自己的名字，在总统灵前庄严表示自己的决心。他们表示坚决遵循总统的范例，以完成国家建设来加强本国力量和保卫世界和平。
    捷克斯洛伐克作家协会在17日召开了追悼会，悼念萨波托斯基总统。
    驻在布拉格的各国外交代表也到西班牙大厅向萨波托斯基总统告别。（附图片）
