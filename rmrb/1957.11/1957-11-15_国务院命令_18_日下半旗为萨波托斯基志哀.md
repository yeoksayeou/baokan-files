Title: State Council Orders Half-Mast on the 18th to Mourn Zápotocký
Author:
Date: 1957-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 14th. The State Council of the People's Republic of China issued an order on November 14th, stating that on the 18th—the day of the funeral of Comrade Antonín Zápotocký, President of the Czechoslovak Republic—all agencies, troops, factories, mines, enterprises, schools, and people's organizations located in the people's committees of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government, as well as the people's committees of cities, shall lower their flags to half-mast for one day of mourning.

### 国务院命令18日下半旗为萨波托斯基志哀

1957-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院命令18日下半旗为萨波托斯基志哀
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国国务院11月14日发出命令，在18日——捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基同志殡葬的日子，全国各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会和各市人民委员会所在地的机关、部队、厂矿、企业、学校和人民团体一律下半旗一日志哀。
