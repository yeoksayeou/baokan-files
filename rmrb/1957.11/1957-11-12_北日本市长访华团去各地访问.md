Title: North Japanese Mayors Delegation Visits Various Places in China
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The North Japanese Port City Mayors Delegation, headed by Suzuki Tomi, left Beijing by train on the morning of the 9th to visit various places in China.
    Zhang Youyu and Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayors of Beijing, and others were at the station to see them off.

### 北日本市长访华团去各地访问

1957-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    北日本市长访华团去各地访问
    据新华社9日讯　以铃木东民为首的北日本港湾城市市长访华团，9日上午乘火车离开北京去我国各地访问。
    到车站欢送的有北京市副市长张友渔、王昆仑等人。
