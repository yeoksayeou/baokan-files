Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the head of the Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission, Yoshio Suzuki, on the morning of the 28th. Also present were mission members Shozo Oya, Yonosuke Kikuchi, Shinichi Satake, Haruki Satake, Haruji Tahara, Shoichi Miyake, Shin Chiba, Yoshinobu Yamashita, and Sadao Furuya, as well as the mission's accompanying staff. Present at the meeting were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General; Liao Chengzhi, Xie Nangguang, Zhao Anbo, Council Members; and Xiao Xiangqian, Deputy Secretary-General.

### 周恩来总理接见日本社会党亲善使节团

1957-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见日本社会党亲善使节团
    新华社28日讯　周恩来总理28日上午接见了日本社会党亲善使节团团长铃木义男，团员大矢省三、菊地养之辅、佐竹新市、佐竹晴记、田原春次、三宅正一、千叶信、山下义信、古屋贞雄，以及使节团的随行人员。接见的时候，在座的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，秘书长吴茂荪，理事廖承志、谢南光、赵安博，副秘书长萧向前。
