Author:
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 5

    Brief News

    First Volume of "Soviet Encyclopedia Yearbook" Published

    The first volume of the "Soviet Encyclopedia Yearbook," which the Soviet Union began publishing this year, has been released. This yearbook includes information on Soviet and international social, political, scientific, and cultural life. The "Yearbook" also includes specially compiled information on new discoveries and major achievements in science and technology.

### “苏联大百科全书年鉴”第一册问世

1957-11-19
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    “苏联大百科全书年鉴”第一册问世
    苏联从今年开始出版的“苏联大百科全书年鉴”第一册已经问世。这部年鉴包括苏联国内外社会、政治、科学、文化生活等方面的资料。“年鉴”中还特别编入一些有关科学技术方面的新发现和重大成就的资料。
