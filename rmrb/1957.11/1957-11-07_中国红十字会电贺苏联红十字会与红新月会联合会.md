Title: China Red Cross Sends Congratulatory Message to the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the USSR
Author:
Date: 1957-11-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Red Cross Society of China sent a telegram on the 6th to the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the USSR, congratulating them on the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

The telegram said, "On the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, we extend our heartfelt and warmest congratulations to your society, and through your society to all members of the Soviet Red Cross and the people, for the achievements you have made under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. As the Red Cross Society of the world's first socialist country, you have set a brilliant example for us in socialist humanitarian service. We wish you even greater achievements in the cause of maintaining world peace and protecting the health of the Soviet people.

Long live the unity and fraternal friendship between the Red Cross Societies of China and the Soviet Union and between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union!"

### 中国红十字会电贺苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会

1957-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会电贺苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会
    新华社6日讯　中国红十字会6日致电苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会，祝贺十月社会主义革命四十周年。
    电报说，“值此伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年，谨向贵会，并通过贵会向全体苏联红十字会员和人民为你们在苏联共产党的领导下所获得的成就致以衷心的和最热烈的祝贺。做为世界上第一个社会主义国家的红十字会，贵会在社会主义的人道主义服务中为我们树立了光辉的榜样。祝贵会在维护世界和平和保卫苏联人民的健康的事业中获得更大的成就。
    中苏两国红十字会和中苏两国人民间的团结和兄弟般的友谊万岁！”
