Title: Sing for the Socialist Countryside!
Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 8

The climax of celebrating the fortieth anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution has passed. We thank the many authors who participated with unparalleled passion to offer their tributes to the great festival. Poetry, in particular, can be described as a bumper harvest. Up to the day before yesterday, a total of thirty-five poems (groups), comprising approximately 1,700 lines, were published in the supplementary "Ode to October" column. The number of submissions we received but did not publish was hundreds of times greater than this. We thank you once again and eagerly await your continued support!

At a time when the socialist debate is unfolding in the countryside, the Forty Articles of the Program for Agricultural Development in our country (revised draft) have been published. The implementation of this program will bring about a fundamental change in the face of our countryside. Now, from the grasslands of Inner Mongolia to Hainan Island, from the coast of the East China Sea to the foot of the Tianshan Mountains, five hundred million peasants, under the leadership of the Party, are striding forward heroically along the road of socialism, building their new lives with enthusiastic action! Isn't this change an inexhaustible source of writing for poets, writers, and the vast number of authors?

In the coming period, the supplement plans to dedicate more space to reflecting the rich life of the socialist countryside, reflecting the rapidly changing face of the countryside, and especially to singing the praises of the ordinary people who are working diligently to build the socialist countryside. We hope that poets and writers will immerse themselves in the boiling life, and we hope that authors in the countryside will also take up their pens and sing for the socialist countryside with great enthusiasm!

### 为社会主义的农村歌唱吧！

1957-11-16
第8版()
专栏：

    为社会主义的农村歌唱吧！
    庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的高潮过去了。我们感谢许多作者们以无比的激情参加对伟大节日的献礼。特别是诗，称得上是一次丰收。到前天为止，在副刊“十月颂歌”栏内，一共发表了三十五首（组）约一千七百多行的诗篇。我们收到而没有发表的更是百倍于这个数目。我们再一次地感谢你们，并渴望着你们新的支持！
    正值农村展开社会主义大辩论的时候，发展我国农业的四十条纲要（修正草案）公布了。这个纲要的实行，将要使我们的农村的面貌起根本的变化。现在，从内蒙古草原到海南岛，从东海之滨到天山脚下，五亿农民在党的领导下，正跨着豪迈的脚步，沿着社会主义的道路，以热烈的行动来建设自己的新生活！这个变化，岂不正是诗人们、作家们和广大的作者们无穷无尽的写作源泉吗？
    今后一个时期内，副刊准备以较多的篇幅反映社会主义农村丰富的生活、反映农村急速变化着的面貌、特别是歌颂那些为了建设社会主义农村而辛勤劳动着的普通人。我们希望诗人们、作家们投身到沸腾的生活中去，希望在农村的作者们也拿起笔来，以巨大的热情为社会主义的农村歌唱吧！　编　者
