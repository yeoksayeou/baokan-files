Title: Singing the Heroic Struggle of the Soviet People: Grand Festival Performance Held in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-11-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 9th: A grand performance was held on the evening of November 8th at the Luzhniki Palace of Sports in Moscow to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. It was a very brilliant performance. About a thousand people participated in the performance, including famous Soviet musicians, actors, and choirs.

Soviet Party and government leaders, delegations from communist and workers' parties of various countries, other delegations, representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and people from all walks of life in Moscow watched the performance.

The prelude to the performance was "The Friendly Big Family of All Nationalities of the Soviet Union," performed by representatives of the literary and art circles of the fifteen republics and famous choirs from all over the Soviet Union. The actors appeared in colorful national costumes, singing the majestic and solemn song "My Beloved Motherland" in unison.

The first part of the performance ran through the heroic struggles of the Soviet people for forty years.

Subsequently, the scenes of the Soviet civil war, socialist construction, electrification, agricultural collectivization, and the Great Patriotic War were shown in the performances of opera, ballet, recitation, and drama.

Hundreds of Young Pioneers walked onto the stage and performed songs and dances for the guests.

The last program was a dance of the various republics, choreographed by Soviet People's Artist Moiseyev, symbolizing the friendship and cooperation of all nationalities.

At the end of the performance, the entire audience sang the Internationale in unison.

### 歌唱苏联人民的英雄斗争  莫斯科举行盛大节日演出

1957-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    歌唱苏联人民的英雄斗争
    莫斯科举行盛大节日演出
    据新华社莫斯科9日电　11月8日晚间在莫斯科卢日尼基体育宫举行了盛大的演出，庆祝十月革命四十周年。这是一场十分精采的演出。参加表演的有苏联著名的音乐家、演员和合唱团，共约一千人。
    苏联党和政府领导人、各国共产党和工人党代表团以及其他代表团、苏联最高苏维埃代表、莫斯科各界人民，观看了这次演出。
    演出的前奏曲是由十五个加盟共和国文艺界代表和苏联全国各著名合唱团表演的“苏联各民族友好大家庭”。演员们穿着鲜艳的民族服装登场，齐声合唱雄伟庄严的歌曲“我的可爱的祖国”。
    演出的第一部分贯穿着苏联人民四十年英勇斗争的事迹。
    随后，在歌剧、巴蕾舞、朗诵和话剧的表演中，显现了苏联国内战争、社会主义建设、电气化、农业集体化和伟大卫国战争的情景。
    上百名少年先锋队员走上舞台，为客人们表演了歌曲和舞蹈。
    最后的一个节目是由苏联人民演员莫伊塞耶夫排演的、象征各民族友爱合作的各加盟共和国的舞蹈。
    演出结束时，全场齐唱国际歌。
