Title: Chairman Mao Cables Congratulations to Novotny on Election as Czechoslovak President
Author:
Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 21st November. Chairman Mao Zedong cabled Antonín Novotný on the 21st to congratulate him on his election as President of the Czechoslovak Republic. The cable reads:

Dear Comrade Antonín Novotný,

I am delighted to learn of your election as President of the Czechoslovak Republic, and I extend to you, on behalf of the Chinese people and myself, my heartfelt congratulations.

The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, headed by you, has made outstanding contributions to the cause of building socialism, consolidating the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and safeguarding world peace. The Czechoslovak people's awarding you the highest state position proves the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the people's infinite trust and love for you.

I wish you great success in leading the Czechoslovak Republic in the great cause of socialist construction and safeguarding world peace.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, 21st November, 1957

### 毛主席电贺诺沃提尼当选捷总统

1957-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺诺沃提尼当选捷总统
    新华社21日讯　毛泽东主席21日致电祝贺安托宁·诺沃提尼当选为捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统。电文如下：亲爱的安托宁·诺沃提尼同志：
    欣悉你当选为捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统，谨以中国人民和我个人名义向你表示衷心的祝贺。
    以你为首的捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会在建设社会主义、巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结和维护世界和平的事业中作出了卓越贡献。捷克斯洛伐克人民授予你最高国家职位，证明捷克斯洛伐克共产党和人民对你无限的信任和爱戴。
    祝你在领导捷克斯洛伐克共和国的社会主义的建设和维护世界和平的伟大事业中取得巨大的成就。
             中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1957年11月21日
