Title: China-Soviet Friendship Cup: A Masterpiece Crafted by Soviet Ivory Carvers
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: TASS reported on November 10th that a group of ivory carvers in Kholmogory, Arkhangelsk Oblast, in the northwest of the Russian Federation, collectively created a magnificent ivory carving cup themed on Sino-Soviet friendship.

　　This cup was collectively created by a group of carvers from the famous Lomonosov Ivory Art Carving Workshop in the city. Shtang, who has participated in international exhibitions many times, was responsible for leading the work.

　　The cup is forty-five centimeters high. The national emblems of China and the Soviet Union are carved in the center of the cup. On both sides of the national emblems are carvings of Red Square, the Kremlin, and Tiananmen Square in Beijing. The lower part of the cup is carved with images of workers, farmers, and intellectuals, embodying the friendship between the people of the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union. The lid of the cup is carved with the Earth and two small stars.

　　This cup has been sent to Moscow for display at the All-Union Art Exhibition celebrating the fortieth anniversary of the October Revolution.

### 中苏友谊之杯  苏联象牙雕刻家精心杰作

1957-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏友谊之杯
    苏联象牙雕刻家精心杰作
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社10日讯：俄罗斯联邦西北部阿尔汉格尔州霍尔莫戈尔城的一批象牙雕刻家们，集体创作了一只以苏中友谊为主题的精美的象牙雕刻杯。
    这只杯是这个城市著名的洛蒙诺索夫象牙艺术雕刻社的一批雕刻家集体创作的，负责领导这个工作的是曾经多次参加过国际展览会的什坦格。
    这只杯有四十五公分高。杯的中央刻有苏中两国的国徽。国徽的两边刻有红场、克里姆林宫和北京的天安门广场。杯的下部刻的是体现苏中两个伟大国家人民友谊的工人、农民和知识分子像。杯盖上刻着地球，还有两颗小星星。
    这个杯子已经送往莫斯科的庆祝十月革命四十周年的全苏艺术展览会上展出。
