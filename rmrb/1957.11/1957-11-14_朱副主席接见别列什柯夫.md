Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Receives Belyayev
Author:
Date: 1957-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. This afternoon, Vice Chairman Zhu De received Belyayev, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Soviet magazine "New Times," who is currently visiting our country.

Present at the meeting was Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 朱副主席接见别列什柯夫

1957-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    朱副主席接见别列什柯夫
  新华社13日讯　今天下午，朱德副主席接见了正在我国访问的苏联“新时代”杂志副总编辑别列什柯夫。
    接见时在座的，有外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
