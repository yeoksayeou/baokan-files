Title: Czechoslovakian Embassy Sends Letter of Thanks to Beijing Circles for Condolences on President Zápotocký

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Xinhua News Agency has received a letter of thanks from the Czechoslovakian Embassy in China to the people of all circles in Beijing. The full text of the letter is as follows: "In these days of deepest sorrow for the Czechoslovakian people following the sudden death of the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Mr. Zápotocký, tens of thousands of people from all circles in Beijing came to the Embassy to express their sincere sympathy and condolences and to pay their last respects to President Zápotocký. The Czechoslovakian Ambassador to China hereby extends his heartfelt thanks to all those who came to offer their condolences. This sincere grief and mourning once again proves the profound friendship existing between the people of China and Czechoslovakia. In the future, the people of our two countries will continue to share joy and sorrow."

### 捷大使馆函谢北京各界吊唁萨波托斯基总统

1957-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    捷大使馆函谢北京各界吊唁萨波托斯基总统
    新华社20日讯　新华通讯社收到了捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆向北京各界人民的鸣谢信。这封信的全文如下：“在捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基突然逝世，捷克斯洛伐克人民感到最悲痛的日子里，北京各界人民数万人前来大使馆表达了真诚的同情和哀悼，并向萨波托斯基总统致最后的敬意。捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使在此谨向所有前来吊唁者表示深切的谢意。这种真诚的悲痛和哀悼再次证明，中捷两国人民之间存在着深厚的友谊。今后两国人民仍将共享欢乐和悲痛。”
