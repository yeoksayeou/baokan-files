Title: Yang Xiufeng Hosts a Banquet for the Polish Higher Education Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd November. A six-member Polish Higher Education Delegation, led by the Minister of Higher Education of Poland, S. Żukrowski, arrived in Beijing on the 2nd. This delegation is visiting China in accordance with the 1957 Executive Plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Welcoming the delegation at the airport were Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education of the People's Republic of China, Vice Minister Liu Aifeng, and representatives from the Ministry of Culture, the Ministry of Education, and relevant universities.

Polish Ambassador to China, Kiryluk, Counselor Dumbnitzky, and others also greeted the delegation at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd November. Minister of Higher Education Yang Xiufeng hosted a banquet today to welcome the Polish Higher Education Delegation headed by S. Żukrowski. Attending the banquet were Vice Ministers of Higher Education Zhou Jianren, Liu Aifeng, Liu Zizai, Vice Minister of Culture Liu Zhiming, President of Peking University Ma Yinchu, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, and renowned professors from universities in the capital, such as Jian Bozan and Yang Hui.

Also present at the banquet were Polish Ambassador to China Kiryluk and embassy staff.

### 杨秀峰欢宴波兰高等教育代表团

1957-11-04
第2版()
专栏：

    杨秀峰欢宴波兰高等教育代表团
    新华社2日讯　波兰高等教育代表团一行六人，在团长、波兰高等教育部部长斯·茹凯夫斯基率领下在2日到北京。这个代表团是根据中波文化合作协定1957年执行计划来我国访问的。
    到机场欢迎代表团的，有中华人民共和国高等教育部部长杨秀峰、副部长刘皑风和文化部、教育部以及有关高等学校的代表。
    波兰驻华大使基里洛克、参赞邓布尼茨基等也到机场迎接。
    新华社3日讯　高等教育部部长杨秀峰今日设宴欢迎以斯·茹凯夫斯基为首的波兰高等教育代表团。参加宴会的有高等教育部副部长周建人、刘皑风、刘子载，文化部副部长刘芝明，北京大学校长马寅初，北京市副市长吴晗，以及首都高等学校著名教授翦伯赞、杨晦等。
    参加宴会的还有波兰驻华大使基里洛克和使馆人员。
