Title: International Students from Brotherly Countries in Beijing Hold Joint Celebration

Author:

Date: 1957-11-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd: International students from various brotherly countries held a joint celebration at the Moscow Restaurant of the Soviet Exhibition Center in Beijing on the 2nd to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

International students from seven countries, including India, Egypt, Italy, and the Republic of Iceland, were invited to participate in the evening. More than 300 people attended the party.

In his speech, a student from the German Democratic Republic, Fei Lu, said that the path of the October Revolution is the path to happiness for the working people of the world, and it is also our firm direction. Socialism will surely triumph.

At this moment, Ai Dayan, Director of the International Student Management Department of the Ministry of Higher Education, walked to the center of the hall and shouted: "Long live the unbreakable friendship between China and the Soviet Union! Long live the great festival of the working people of the world!"

Amidst applause, two Chinese girls read aloud a congratulatory letter from the Chinese Communist Youth League members of the Chinese Language Training Class at Peking University to the Soviet Communist Youth League members at Peking University. Soviet international student Lyuba Bacheqiueva also spoke on behalf of the Soviet Communist Youth League members to express her gratitude. She said in Chinese: We sincerely thank the Chinese Communist Youth League members for their congratulations and wish you greater success in your studies.

Warm applause resounded in the hall, and a rich and varied program of entertainment began. After German international students performed a wonderful chorus, Vietnamese international student Gao Jian performed a solo male vocal. Chinese teachers and students sang the famous Soviet song "Lenin Hills" and some folk songs respectively.

In the lounge next to the hall, international students from various countries and their Chinese classmates gathered together for cordial conversations. Several Soviet and Polish international students raised their glasses to wish each other happiness and joy on the holiday.

### 兄弟国家在京留学生举行联欢晚会

1957-11-03
第2版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家在京留学生举行联欢晚会
    据新华社2日讯　来自各兄弟国家的留学生，2日在北京苏联展览馆莫斯科餐厅举行联欢晚会，庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年。
    应邀参加晚会的客人有印度、埃及、意大利和冰岛共和国等七个国家的留学生。参加联欢晚会的共三百多人。
    德意志民主共和国留学生费路在致辞的时候说，十月革命的道路是全世界劳动人民走向幸福的道路，也是我们的坚定方向。社会主义一定胜利。
    这时，高等教育部留学生管理司司长艾大炎走到大厅中央，高呼：“中苏牢不可破的友谊万岁！全世界劳动人民的伟大节日万岁！”
    两位中国姑娘在掌声中宣读了北京大学中国语文专修班中国共青团员写给在北京大学的苏联共青团员的节日贺信。苏联留学生柳巴·巴切秋也娃代表苏联共青团员讲话致谢。她用中国话说：我们衷心感谢中国共青团员们的祝贺，祝你们在学习上取得更大的成绩。
    大厅里响起了热烈的掌声，丰富多采的联欢节目开始了。德国留学生刚刚表演了精采的合唱，越南留学生高坚又表演了男声独唱。中国教师和学生分别演唱了苏联著名歌曲“列宁山”和一些民歌。
    在大厅旁的休息室里，各国留学生和他们的中国同学围在一起亲切交谈，几位苏联和波兰的留学生举起酒杯互祝节日幸福和愉快。
