Title: Premier Zhou Receives Delegation of Mayors from Port Cities of Northern Japan
Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, November 8th - Premier Zhou Enlai received the delegation of mayors from port cities of Northern Japan this afternoon, including the head of the delegation, Suzuki Tomin, the deputy head, Sakurai Tatsuji, and all members of the delegation.
    Present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Zhao Anbo, Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Youyu, Vice-Mayor of Beijing.

### 周总理接见北日本港湾城市市长访华团

1957-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见北日本港湾城市市长访华团
    新华社8日讯　周恩来总理8日下午接见了北日本港湾城市市长访华团团长铃木东民、副团长樱井辰治和访华团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、中国人民外交学会理事赵安博和北京市副市长张友渔。
