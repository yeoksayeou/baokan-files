Title: Soviet Delegation Visits Guangzhou and Then Proceeds to Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1957-11-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 19th: The Soviet delegation to China, led by Andrei Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, and composed of representatives from various sectors of Soviet society participating in the Chinese people's celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, visited factories, schools, trade unions, and Communist Youth League organizations in Guangzhou on the 19th. They also held discussions with writers and musicians.

Andreyev and others visited the Guangzhou Paper Mill on the morning of the 19th and had cordial conversations with several Chinese employees of the factory who had returned from studying in Gorky City, Soviet Union. Prior to the visit, more than 600 people, including the factory's Party Committee Secretary, Director, and employee representatives, gathered to welcome the distinguished guests.

Cherednichenko, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Railway Workers' Union, and others visited the General Machinery Factory, where the factory's employees also held a grand rally to warmly welcome the distinguished guests.

In the afternoon, Andreyev visited the South China Agricultural College to see subtropical crops unique to the South.

The delegation's distinguished guests also visited the Guangzhou Railway Workers' Union, Guangdong Textile Factory, Zhongshan University and Zhongshan Medical College, Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and other units. The Soviet writer and Editor-in-Chief of "Znamya" magazine, Kozhevnikov, and the composer of the song "Moscow-Beijing," Muradeli, held separate discussions with Guangzhou writers and musicians.

Kislanko, a member of the delegation who had served as an advisor at the Whampoa Military Academy more than thirty years ago, revisited the old site of the military academy today. He said emotionally to Zhao Qimin, Vice Admiral of the South Sea Fleet, who accompanied him on the visit: "I always feel that a part of my heart remains in China, especially here."

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 20th: The Soviet delegation to China, led by Andrei Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, and composed of representatives from various sectors of Soviet society participating in the Chinese people's celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, accompanied by Liao Chengzhi, Cao Mengjun, and Ge Baoquan, left Guangzhou by train at 8:05 tonight to visit Wuhan.

Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Wang De, First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, Du Guoxiang, Vice President of the Guangdong Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and other prominent figures from all walks of life in Guangdong and Guangzhou went to the station to see off the distinguished guests.

Balkin and Lobno, Deputy Consuls of the Soviet Consulate in Guangzhou, also went to the station to see them off.

This morning, Andreyev visited the Wenchong Agricultural Cooperative on the outskirts of Guangzhou. The cooperative members presented the distinguished guests with large baskets of freshly picked fruit. In the afternoon, Andreyev and others visited the Chinese Export Commodities Fair and the Guangzhou Children's Palace. In the evening, Tao Zhu and Chen Yu jointly hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the distinguished guests.

### 苏代表团在广州参观访问后去武汉

1957-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    苏代表团在广州参观访问后去武汉
    新华社广州19日电　以苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫为首的苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团人员，19日在广州访问了工厂、学校、工会和共青团组织，并同作家和音乐工作者座谈。
    安德烈耶夫等19日早上访问了广州造纸厂，并同这个厂的几位从苏联高尔基城学习回来的中国职工恳切地交谈。参观以前，这个厂的党委书记、厂长和职工代表六百多人集会欢迎贵宾。
    苏联铁路工会中央委员会主席切烈德尼钦科等访问了通用机器厂，这个厂的职工也举行盛大集会热烈欢迎贵宾。
    下午，安德烈耶夫到华南农学院参观了南方特有的亚热带作物。
    代表团的贵宾们还分别到广州区铁路工会、广东纺织厂、中山大学和中山医学院、共青团广州市委员会、中苏友好协会等单位访问，苏联作家、“旗帜”杂志总编辑科热夫尼柯夫和“莫斯科——北京”歌曲作者穆拉德里分别同广州作家和音乐工作者座谈。
    代表团团员、三十多年前曾在黄埔军校担任顾问的基斯连科，今天重游了这个军校的旧址。他激动地对陪同参观的南海舰队首长赵启民中将说：“我时刻感觉到有一部分心留在中国，尤其是留在这里”。
    新华社广州20日电　以苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫为首的苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团，由廖承志、曹孟君和戈宝权陪同，今晚八时五分乘火车离开广州去武汉访问。
    中共广东省委第一书记陶铸、省长陈郁、中共广州市委第一书记王德、市长朱光、广东省中苏友好协会副会长杜国庠以及广东省、广州市各界著名人士都到车站欢送贵宾。
    苏联驻广州领事馆副领事巴尔金和罗布诺也到车站送行。
    今晨，安德烈耶夫访问了广州市郊文冲农业社，社员们把大筐刚刚摘下来的水果送给贵宾。下午，安德烈耶夫等参观了中国出口商品交易会，并访问了广州少年儿童宫。傍晚，陶铸、陈郁联合设宴为贵宾饯行。
