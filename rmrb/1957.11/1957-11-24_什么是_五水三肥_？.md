What is "Five Waterings and Three Fertilizations"?

　　"Five Waterings and Three Fertilizations" is part of the wheat yield increase technology measures in Meng County, Henan Province. "Five Waterings" refers to irrigating the wheat fields five times. Local farmers, based on the timing and effect of the irrigation, call these five irrigations: base moisture watering, winter watering, greening watering, jointing watering, and grain filling watering. "Three Fertilizations" refers to applying fertilizer to the wheat fields three times, namely, one basal fertilizer application and two top dressing applications.

### 什么是“五水三肥”？

1957-11-24
第2版()
专栏：名辞解释

    什么是“五水三肥”？
    “五水三肥”是河南省孟县小麦增产技术措施的一部分。“五水”是指对麦田灌溉五次。当地农民根据灌溉的时间和作用，分别叫这五次灌溉的水为：底墒水、冬水、返青水、拔节水和灌浆水。“三肥”是指向麦田施肥三次，即一次基肥、两次追肥。
