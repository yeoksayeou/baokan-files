Title: Czechoslovak National Assembly to Elect President Today
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 5

    Czechoslovak National Assembly
    To Elect President Today
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 18th. According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency, the Czechoslovak National Assembly will hold a meeting on November 19th to elect a president.

### 捷国民议会  今日选举总统

1957-11-19
第5版()
专栏：

    捷国民议会
    今日选举总统
    新华社布拉格18日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克国民议会将在11月19日举行会议，选举总统。
