Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Delegation to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The Chinese Academy of Sciences has formed a delegation to go to the Soviet Union to discuss scientific and technological cooperation between the two academies. The head of the delegation is Guo Moruo. The members of the delegation are Zhu Kezhen, Du Runsheng, Feng Depei, Wu Xuezhou, Liu Daosheng, and Yu Guangyuan. Apart from Guo Moruo, they have already departed for the Soviet Union.

### 中国科学院代表团赴苏

1957-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院代表团赴苏
    新华社1日讯　中国科学院组成代表团，前往苏联与苏联科学院商讨两国科学院科学技术合作问题。代表团团长是郭沫若。团员竺可桢、杜润生、冯德培、吴学周、刘导生、于光远，除郭沫若外，他们已经先后前往苏联。
