Title: Chairman Mao's Speech in Moscow Receives Welcome from Syrian Officials and Public Opinion
Author:
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, November 4th: Major newspapers here, including "Al-Nour," "Al-Rai al-Aam," "Al-Nasr," "Alif Baa," and "Al-Youm," all published Chairman Mao's speech on November 2nd in Moscow, expressing the Chinese people's support for the Syrian struggle, on their front pages on the 3rd.

The Syrian Minister of State for Information and Propaganda, Aqil, commented on Chairman Mao's speech on the 3rd, saying, "This speech demonstrates the true policy of People's China and its purpose of safeguarding world peace."

He also said, "This friendly new statement by Chairman Mao does not surprise us, because he and the entire Chinese people have fought against the imperialists and driven them out."

### 毛主席在莫斯科的讲话  叙官方和舆论表示欢迎

1957-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席在莫斯科的讲话
    叙官方和舆论表示欢迎
    新华社大马士革4日电　这里包括“光明报”、“舆论报”、“胜利报”、“阿利夫巴报”和“今天报”在内的主要报纸3日都在第一版刊载了毛主席11月2日在莫斯科发表的表示中国人民支持叙利亚斗争的讲话。
    叙利亚负责新闻和宣传事务的国务部长阿基勒3日就毛主席的这篇讲话发表意见说：“这篇讲话表明了人民中国真正的政策和它维护世界和平的目的。”
    他还说：“毛主席的这个友好的新的声明并不使我们感到意外，因为他和全体中国人民曾经和帝国主义者进行斗争，并且把帝国主义者赶了出去。”
