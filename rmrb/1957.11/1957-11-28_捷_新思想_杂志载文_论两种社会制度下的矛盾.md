Czechoslovakian "New Ideas" Magazine Publishes Article on Contradictions Under Two Social Systems

The Czechoslovakian magazine "New Ideas" published an article in its October issue on "Antagonistic Contradictions of Capitalism and Non-Antagonistic Contradictions of Socialism." This article provides a relatively specific and in-depth analysis and argumentation of these two different types of contradictions, especially the non-antagonistic contradictions in socialist society.

The author of this article, Comrade Dragoslav Slejchka, first raises the question of the necessity of revealing contradictions. He says that in the rapid development of socialist society, many contradictions arise, and the resolution of these contradictions creates conditions for further development. Otherwise, under certain social conditions, they may develop into dangerous conflicts. Moreover, if we do not analyze these contradictions from a Marxist point of view, our enemies, the revisionists, will analyze and utilize them in a distorted manner.

In concretely analyzing contradictions, Slejchka said, "The development of anything, including the development of society, is a process of the emergence, development, and resolution of contradictions. Under different conditions, these general laws have different manifestations. For example, after the victory of the socialist revolution, the role of general dialectics takes on a new character."

In analyzing antagonistic and non-antagonistic contradictions, the author believes that non-antagonistic contradictions play a fundamental role and are a phenomenon unique to socialist society. During the transition period from capitalism to socialism, the antagonistic struggle between capitalism and socialism continues, and may even be very sharp even when antagonism has been overcome from a global perspective. However, "even during this transition period, new socialist internal contradictions are increasingly emerging. At this time, it would be a serious political error not to distinguish between these two essentially different contradictions."

In the part analyzing the internal contradictions of socialism, the author says that under the socialist system, there is a phenomenon in which the relations of production lag behind the productive forces, hindering the development of production, but the relationship between the two is fundamentally different from antagonistic contradictions, and this relationship is one of mutual assistance. Under the socialist system, the development of productive forces does not destroy or weaken the relations of production, but rather forces it to develop more rapidly to adapt to the new productive forces. After arguing for the unity of contradictions between the centralization of the socialist economy and the initiative of the working masses, he said that this contradiction is the economic foundation of socialist centralization and socialist democracy.

He believes that Comrade Mao Zedong's article "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People" "raises the question of socialist contradictions very boldly and creatively." He said, "The concept of 'contradictions among the people' is much broader than the concept of 'socialist contradictions'; and said, "If we consider to what extent the contradictions analyzed by Comrade Mao Zedong are unique to the Chinese situation, and to what extent they are universal phenomena that also exist in other countries, then it is necessary to remember the diversification of contradictions among the people." It is a dogmatic understanding to attempt to apply all contradictions to other countries, or to attempt to exclude the possibility that these contradictions also play a role in other countries.

Slejchka said that antagonistic contradictions and non-antagonistic contradictions are only the basic types of social contradictions, so it cannot be considered that the issue of the particularity of contradictions does not exist. Here, he especially mentioned the chapter on "The Particularity of Contradictions" in Comrade Mao Zedong's "On Contradiction," saying that within the basic types, contradictions also have qualitative differences, so each contradiction must be specifically analyzed. He said that resolving the non-antagonistic contradictions of socialism is not a painless process either. As for resolving these contradictions, the party's principle of democratic centralism is the main guiding principle.

The author believes that the issue of socialist contradictions is playing an increasingly prominent role in the internal development of socialist countries. But from the perspective of the entire historical process of mankind, the antagonistic contradiction between capitalism and socialism is still the basic role in the present and for a long time to come. Capitalist forces are trying to use all possible means to take advantage of socialist contradictions to benefit themselves. The Hungarian incident is an example of this. The Chinese bourgeois rightists also tried to use the mass movement (the task of this mass movement was originally to resolve contradictions among the people and eliminate obstacles to development, so that socialist forces could develop more rapidly). The Chinese Communist Party promptly recognized this danger and counterattacked the bourgeois rightists. However, the mass movement to improve working styles continues.

### 捷“新思想”杂志载文  论两种社会制度下的矛盾

1957-11-28
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    捷“新思想”杂志载文
    论两种社会制度下的矛盾
    捷克斯洛伐克的“新思想”杂志十月号，发表了一篇论“资本主义的对抗性矛盾和社会主义的非对抗性矛盾”的文章。这篇文章对这两种不同性质的矛盾，特别是对社会主义社会的非对抗性矛盾，作了比较具体和深入的分析和论证。
    这篇文章的作者德腊戈斯拉夫·斯列伊希卡同志，首先提出揭露矛盾的必要性问题。他说，在社会主义社会迅速发展的过程中，产生了许多矛盾，这些矛盾的解决，就造成进一步发展的条件。否则，在一定的社会条件下，它们就可能发展成为危险的冲突。并且，如果我们不从马克思主义的观点来分析这种矛盾，那末我们的敌人，修正主义者就会用歪曲的方法分析、利用这种矛盾。
    在具体分析矛盾的时候，斯列伊希卡说，“任何事物的发展，包括社会的发展，都是矛盾的产生、发展和解决的过程。而在不同的条件下，这些一般的规律，就有不同的表现。比如社会主义革命胜利后，一般辩证法的作用，就具有新的性质。”
    在分析对抗性和非对抗性的矛盾时，作者认为，非对抗性的矛盾起着根本作用，是社会主义社会所特有的现象。由资本主义到社会主义的过渡时期，资本主义和社会主义的对抗性斗争是在继续着的，甚至在从全局上说来对抗性已克服了的情况下，也还可能非常尖锐。不过“就是在这个过渡时期，新的社会主义的内部矛盾也已经越来越多地出现了。这个时候，如果不区分这两种本质不同的矛盾，就会犯严重的政治错误。”
    作者在分析社会主义内部矛盾的部分时说，在社会主义制度下，生产关系落后于生产力，阻碍生产发展的现象是存在的，但两者的关系和对抗性矛盾根本不同，这种关系是相互帮助的关系。在社会主义制度下，生产力的发展并不破坏或削弱生产关系，而是强迫它更快地发展以适应新的生产力。在论证了社会主义经济的集中和劳动群众的主动性之间的矛盾的统一性之后，他说，这种矛盾就是社会主义集中和社会主义民主的经济基础。
    他认为毛泽东同志的“关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题”一文，“是非常大胆地和创造性地提出了社会主义的矛盾问题。”他说，“人民内部矛盾”这个概念，比“社会主义矛盾”这个概念要广泛得多；并说，“如果考虑到毛泽东同志所分析的矛盾在何种程度上是中国的情况所特有的，在何种程度上是其他国家也存在的普遍现象，那末就有必要记住，人民内部矛盾中的多样化。”而企图使一切矛盾运用于其他国家，或企图排斥这种矛盾也在其他国家中起作用的可能性，都是教条主义的理解。
    斯列伊希卡说，对抗性矛盾和非对抗性矛盾仅仅是社会矛盾的基本类型，因此不能认为矛盾的特殊性问题就不存在了。这里，他特别提到了毛泽东同志的“矛盾论”中的“矛盾的特殊性”一章说，在基本类型里面，矛盾也具有质的区别，因此必须具体分析各个矛盾。他说，解决社会主义的非对抗性矛盾，也不是毫无痛苦的过程。至于在解决这些矛盾的时候，党的民主集中制原则是主要的指针。
    作者认为，社会主义矛盾的问题，在社会主义国家内部发展中越来越占显著地位了。但从人类整个历史进程来看，现在和将来很长时期内，基本起作用的还是资本主义和社会主义之间的对抗性矛盾。资本主义势力正在企图使用一切可能的方法利用社会主义矛盾使自己得到好处。如匈牙利事件就是这样。中国资产阶级右派也曾设法利用群众运动（这个群众运动的任务，原来是为了解决人民内部矛盾，消除发展中的障碍，以便社会主义力量更迅速地发展）。中国共产党及时了解到这种危险，对资产阶级右派分子进行了反击。然而，改进工作作风的群众运动则继续进行着。
