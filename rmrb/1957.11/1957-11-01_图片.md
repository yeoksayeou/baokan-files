Author: Lü Xiangyou
Date: 1957-11-01
Page: 2

    To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, the Symphony Orchestra of the Novosibirsk State Opera and Ballet Theatre of the Soviet Union, the Orchestra of the Central Experimental Opera Theatre, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army Military Band jointly held a grand Sino-Soviet friendship symphony concert at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing on the evening of October 31.
　　Our reporter Lü Xiangyou photographed it.
