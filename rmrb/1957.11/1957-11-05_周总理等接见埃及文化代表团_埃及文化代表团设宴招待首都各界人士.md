Title: Premier Zhou and Others Receive Egyptian Cultural Delegation; Egyptian Cultural Delegation Hosts Banquet for People from All Walks of Life in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, November 4th. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Egyptian Cultural Delegation headed by Dr. El Sayed Mustafa El-Said, President of Alexandria University, on the morning of November 4th.

    Present at the reception were Xia Yan, Liu Aifeng, Liu Shi, Chu Tunan, and Chen Zhongjing, among others.

    Also present at the reception was the Egyptian Ambassador to China, Ragab.

    Following the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi also took a photograph with all members of the Egyptian Cultural Delegation.
    Xinhua News Agency, November 4th. The Ministry of Higher Education and the Ministry of Education held a luncheon banquet in Beijing on November 4th to welcome the Egyptian Cultural Delegation headed by El Sayed Mustafa El-Said, President of Alexandria University.

    Among those attending the banquet were Zhou Jianren, Vice Minister of Higher Education, Liu Shi, Vice Minister of Education, and many prominent professors from Beijing.

    The Egyptian Ambassador to China, Ragab, was invited to attend the banquet.
    Xinhua News Agency, November 4th. The Egyptian Cultural Delegation, which is currently visiting China, held a banquet at the Beijing Hotel tonight to entertain people from all walks of life in the capital, Beijing.

    Among those invited to attend the banquet tonight were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Higher Education Zhou Jianren, Vice Minister of Education Liu Shi, President of the Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association Burhan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Vice Chairmen of the China Islamic Association Da Puchen and Zhang Jie, President of Peking University Ma Yinchu, and more than one hundred other people from all walks of life.

### 周总理等接见埃及文化代表团  埃及文化代表团设宴招待首都各界人士

1957-11-05
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理等接见埃及文化代表团
    埃及文化代表团设宴招待首都各界人士
    新华社4日讯　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理4日上午接见了以亚历山大大学校长爱尔赛德·穆斯塔法·赛义德博士为首的埃及文化代表团。
    接见时，在座的有夏衍、刘皑风、柳湜、楚图南、陈忠经等人。
    接见时，埃及驻中国大使拉加卜也在座。
    接见以后，周恩来总理、陈毅副总理还同埃及文化代表团全体人员在一起照了像。
    新华社4日讯　高等教育部和教育部4日午间在北京举行宴会，欢迎以亚历山大大学校长爱尔赛德·穆斯塔法·赛义德为首的埃及文化代表团。
    出席宴会的有高等教育部副部长周建人，教育部副部长柳湜，以及北京著名教授多人。
    埃及驻中国大使拉加卜应邀出席了宴会。
    据新华社4日讯　正在中国访问的埃及文化代表团今晚在北京饭店举行宴会，招待首都北京各界人士。
    今晚应邀出席宴会的有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，高等教育部副部长周建人，教育部副部长柳湜，中埃友好协会会长包尔汉，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生、张杰，北京大学校长马寅初，和其他各界人士共一百多人。
