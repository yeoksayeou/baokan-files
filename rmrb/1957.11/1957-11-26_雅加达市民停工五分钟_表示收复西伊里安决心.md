Title: Jakarta Citizens Stop Work for Five Minutes to Show Determination to Recover West Irian
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 25th: At exactly 12 noon today, the bells and drums of all mosques in Jakarta rang out in unison, and the whistles of factories and locomotives sounded simultaneously. All citizens stopped work for five minutes to express their determination to recover West Irian.

　　The ongoing National Construction Conference was temporarily adjourned. State-owned and private enterprises temporarily suspended work. Government agencies temporarily ceased operations. Traffic on the roads temporarily halted.

　　At this time, President Sukarno said at the National Construction Conference venue that this was a manifestation of the determination of the Indonesian people. He hoped the whole world would pay attention to the demands of the Indonesian people.

### 雅加达市民停工五分钟  表示收复西伊里安决心

1957-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    雅加达市民停工五分钟  表示收复西伊里安决心
    新华社雅加达25日电　今天中午十二点正，雅加达全市清真寺的钟鼓齐鸣，工厂和机车的汽笛同时鸣放，全体市民停止工作五分钟，来表示他们收复西伊里安的决心。
    正在举行的全国建设会议暂时休会。国营和私营企业暂时停工停业。政府机关暂时停止工作。马路上车辆暂时停止通行。
    在这时候，苏加诺总统在全国建设会议会场上说，这是印度尼西亚人民的决心的表现。他希望全世界都会注意印度尼西亚人民的要求。
