Title: Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Holds Report Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-11-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd -- To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the headquarters of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League (TDSL) and its Beijing branch jointly held a report meeting at the CPPCC Cultural Club on the evening of November 1st.

    The meeting was attended by members of the League and Taiwan compatriots in Beijing, as well as returned overseas Chinese from Taiwan.

    Li Chunqing, vice chairman of the TDSL, made a report entitled "The Great Significance of the October Revolution" at the meeting.

    Li Hemin, a student from Taiwan Province who studied in the Soviet Union and the chief technician of the Biological Products Inspection Institute of the Ministry of Health, reported on "Experiences of Studying in the Soviet Union".

    The TDSL will also hold a celebration party and a photo exhibition around the time of the October Revolution anniversary.

### 台盟举行报告会

1957-11-03
第2版()
专栏：

    台盟举行报告会
    新华社2日讯　为了庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年，台盟总部及北京市支部1日晚在政协文化俱乐部联合举办报告会。
    到会的有在北京市的盟员和台湾同胞以及台湾籍归侨。
    台盟副主席李纯青在会上做了题为“十月革命的伟大意义”的报告。
    台湾省籍的留苏学生，卫生部生物制品检定所主任技师李河民报告了“在苏联学习的体会”。
    台盟还将在十月革命节前后举行庆祝联欢晚会和图片展览会。
