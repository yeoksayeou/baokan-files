Title: Imam of Oman Rejects Unconditional Ceasefire, Resolves to Continue Struggle Until Final Victory
Author:
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 5th: The Oman Imam's office in Cairo, citing a telegram from the Persian Gulf region on the 4th, announced that the Imam of Oman, Ghalib, has rejected the Sultan of Muscat's proposal for an unconditional ceasefire.

Ghalib said that Oman has decided to continue the struggle until final victory is achieved.

The so-called peace proposal from the Sultan of Muscat was delivered to the Imam of Oman by the Sultan's envoy.

### 阿曼教长拒绝无条件停火  决心继续斗争到最后胜利

1957-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    阿曼教长拒绝无条件停火  决心继续斗争到最后胜利
    新华社开罗5日电　阿曼教长驻开罗办事处4日援引来自波斯湾地区的电报宣布，阿曼教长加利卜已经拒绝了马斯喀特苏丹所提出的实行无条件停火的建议。
    加利卜说，阿曼已经决定继续斗争，直到取得最后胜利为止。
    马斯喀特苏丹的所谓和平建议是由苏丹的使者递交给阿曼教长的。
