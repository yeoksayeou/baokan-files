Title: Fencing and Gymnastics Championships Conclude: Beijing and Tianjin Win Two Team Titles Each
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 8
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 10th. The 1957 Seventeen City Fencing and Gymnastics Championships concluded this evening. Fu Yushan, Director of the大会竞赛委员会 (Congress Competition Committee), announced the results of the five-day competition.

    The Beijing team won the fencing team championship, and the Xi'an team won the fencing team runner-up.

    The Tianjin team won the gymnastics team championship, and the Shenyang team won the gymnastics team runner-up.

    The results of the individual events are:

    Men's Foil: Champion Tao Jinhan (Beijing), Runner-up Li Zhixiong (Xi'an).

    Women's Foil: Champion Wang Enzhu (Beijing), Runner-up Huo Shiru (Tianjin).

    Men's Sabre: Champion Li Zhixiong (Xi'an), Runner-up Wang Shougang (Beijing).

    Men's Individual Gymnastics: Champion Li Yuemin (Tianjin), Runner-up Lü Zongguang (Shenyang).

    Women's Individual Gymnastics: Champion Ying Weiru (Hangzhou), Runner-up Zhang Yanyun (Harbin).

    Men's Pair Strength Exercises: Champions Tianjin Team (Gao Fulin, Zhao Shiqing), Runner-up Harbin Team (Guo Shuzeng, Bai Zhanshan).

    Mixed Doubles Gymnastics: Champions Harbin Team (Zhang Yanyun, Guo Shuzeng), Runner-up Tianjin Team (Yang Bozi, Zhao Shiqing).

     Men's Three-Person Team Compulsory Exercises: Champions Tianjin Team (Gao Fulin, Yu Chunxi, Zhao Shiqing), Runner-up Shenyang Team (Lü Zongguang, Li Wenbin, Wang Zhongbai). (Attached picture)

    Small football players in the Dapailou Hutong, Dongdan District, Beijing, are playing football. Our reporter Liu Changzhong took the picture.

### 击剑、技巧运动锦标赛结束  北京、天津分获两项团体冠军

1957-11-12
第8版()
专栏：

    击剑、技巧运动锦标赛结束
    北京、天津分获两项团体冠军
    新华社天津10日电　1957年十七城市击剑、技巧运动锦标赛今晚结束。大会竞赛委员会主任傅玉珊宣布了五天来的全部比赛结果。
    北京队获得了击剑团体冠军，西安队获得击剑团体亚军。
    获得技巧团体冠军的是天津队，沈阳队获得技巧团体亚军。
    单项比赛的结果是：
    男子轻剑：冠军陶金汉（北京），亚军李志雄（西安）。
    女子轻剑：冠军王恩珠（北京），亚军霍士儒（天津）。
    男子花式剑：冠军李志雄（西安），亚军王守刚（北京）。
    男子单人技巧：冠军李月民（天津），亚军吕宗广（沈阳）。
    女子单人技巧：冠军应蔚茹（杭州），亚军张艳云（哈尔滨）。
    男子双人用力动作：冠军天津队（高福林、赵士卿），亚军哈尔滨队（国书增、白占山）。
    男女双人混合动作：冠军哈尔滨队  （张艳云、国书增），
    亚军天津队（杨波紫、赵士卿）。
     男子三人团体规定动作：冠军天津队（高福林、于春喜、赵士卿），亚军沈阳队（吕宗广、李文斌、王仲白）。（附图片）
