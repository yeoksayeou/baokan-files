Title: Mass Rally in New York Celebrates October Revolution Anniversary Demanding Establishment of Friendly Relations Between the U.S. and Soviet Union, Celebratory Gatherings Held by People in Sweden, Denmark, and Other Countries

Author:

Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: According to TASS, New York, a mass rally was held at "Carnegie Hall" in New York, hosted by the American-Soviet Friendship Association, to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution and the 24th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the United States and the Soviet Union. Several thousand representatives from all walks of life in the United States attended the rally.

Speaking at the rally were Rockwell Kent, Chairman of the National Committee of the American-Soviet Friendship Association and renowned artist; Paul Robeson, renowned black singer; Professor Du Bois; writers Albert Kahn and Anna Louise Strong. They all pointed out the achievements of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Union's struggle for world peace, and demanded the establishment of friendly relations between the United States and the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: According to TASS, Stockholm, Sweden's capital held an evening gathering on the evening of the 7th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Great October Revolution. Lager, Chairman of the Stockholm organization of the Swedish Communist Party, spoke at the meeting. The speeches by Soviet guests, participants in the 1917 revolution, old Bolsheviks Miroshnikov and Vinogradov, were warmly welcomed.

At the celebration party held in Gothenburg, Nikitin, a participant in the storming of the Winter Palace in 1917, reviewed the circumstances of the October Revolution.

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: According to TASS, Copenhagen, the capital of Denmark, Copenhagen, held an evening gathering on the 7th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The evening gathering was initiated by the Copenhagen organization of the Danish Communist Party. Alfred Jensen, Vice Chairman of the Danish Communist Party, described the tremendous achievements made by the Soviet Union in the past forty years in his report at the meeting.

Those attending the celebration party adopted a congratulatory letter to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

Finally, a grand festive concert was held. Soviet actors who came to Denmark to participate in the Danish-Soviet Friendship Month participated in the concert.

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: According to TASS, Montevideo, Uruguay's capital, Montevideo's central square held a mass rally on the 7th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. Members of the Central Committee of the Uruguayan Communist Party, trade union leaders, cultural workers, and others made speeches on the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution at the meeting.

Mass celebration rallies were also held in many other cities in Uruguay.

### 纽约举行庆祝十月革命节群众大会  要求美苏之间建立友好关系  瑞典、丹麦等国人民举行庆祝集会

1957-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    纽约举行庆祝十月革命节群众大会
    要求美苏之间建立友好关系
    瑞典、丹麦等国人民举行庆祝集会
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社纽约11日讯：在纽约的“卡内基音乐厅”举行了由美苏友好协会主持的群众大会，庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年和美苏建立外交关系二十四周年。参加这次大会的有美国社会各界代表数千人。
    在会上讲话的有美苏友好协会全国委员会主席、著名艺术家罗克威尔·肯特，著名黑人歌唱家保罗·罗伯逊，杜波依斯教授，作家艾伯特·卡恩和安娜·路易斯·斯特朗。他们都指出了苏联的成就和苏联为世界和平进行的斗争，并且要求在美国和苏联之间建立友好关系。
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩讯：瑞典首都在7日晚举行了庆祝伟大十月革命四十周年的晚会。瑞典共产党斯德哥尔摩组织的主席拉格尔在会上讲了话。苏联客人、1917年革命的参加者、老布尔什维克米罗施尼科夫和维诺格拉多夫的讲话受到热烈欢迎。
    在哥德堡举行的庆祝晚会上，1917年攻打冬宫的参加者尼基京回顾了十月革命的情况。
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社哥本哈根讯：丹麦首都哥本哈根在7日举行了庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年的晚会。晚会是由丹麦共产党哥本哈根组织倡议举办的。丹麦共产党副主席阿尔弗雷德·任生在会上所作的报告中叙述了苏联在过去四十年中所取得的巨大成就。
    参加庆祝晚会的人通过了致苏联共产党中央委员会的一封贺信。
    最后举行了盛大的节日演奏会。到丹麦参加丹苏友好月的苏联演员参加了演奏会。
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社蒙得维的亚讯：乌拉圭首都蒙得维的亚中央广场在7日举行了群众大会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年。乌拉圭共产党中央委员会委员、工会领导人、文化工作者等在会上作了关于十月革命四十周年的演说。
    乌拉圭其它许多城市也举行了群众庆祝大会。
