Title: Exploring the Mysteries of the Microscopic World, Promoting the Peaceful Use of Nuclear Energy: Scientists from Socialist Countries Expand Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: According to TASS reports, plenipotentiary representatives of the twelve member governments of the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research held a meeting in Dubna, near Moscow, on the 25th and 26th to discuss expanding cooperation among member states, the activities of the institute, its organization, and its budget.

Professor Blokhintsev, the director of the institute, delivered a report on the institute's activities over the past year, announcing some of the most significant results of scientific activities related to research on high energy and atomic nuclei. He particularly emphasized the achievements in microphotography of nuclear reaction traces, as well as a series of major research achievements by the Theoretical Physics Laboratory and other laboratories.

The meeting approved the report and passed a resolution to further expand the cooperation of scientists from the twelve countries in exploring the mysteries of the microscopic world. The resolution pointed out that the achievements of this work will promote the rapid development of the era of peaceful use of nuclear energy.

The meeting approved the institute's 1957 budget execution report and the 1958 budget draft. The allocations of various participating countries for the institute's research work and expansion work will be greatly increased.

### 探究微观世界奥秘　促进和平利用核能  社会主义国家科学家扩大合作

1957-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    探究微观世界奥秘　促进和平利用核能
    社会主义国家科学家扩大合作
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社报道：联合原子核研究所十二个成员国政府的全权代表25日和26日在莫斯科附近的杜布纳举行会议，讨论了扩大会员国间的合作、研究所的活动、机构和预算等问题。
    研究所所长勃洛欣采夫教授在会上作了关于研究所一年来活动的报告，宣布了有关高能量和原子核问题研究的科学活动的一些最重大的成果。他着重谈到了在核反应踪迹的显微照相方面的成就以及理论物理实验室及其他一些实验室所进行的一系列主要研究成果。
    会议通过了这个报告，并且通过决议，决定进一步扩大十二国科学家在探究微观世界奥秘方面的合作。决议中指出，这一工作的成就将促进和平利用核能时代的迅速发展。
    会议批准了研究所1957年预算执行报告和1958年预算草案。各参加国对研究所的研究工作和扩建工作的拨款将大大增加。
