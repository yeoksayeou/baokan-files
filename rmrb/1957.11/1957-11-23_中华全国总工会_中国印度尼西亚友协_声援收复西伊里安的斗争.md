Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions, China-Indonesia Friendship Association Voice Support for the Struggle to Recover West Irian
Author:
Date: 1957-11-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions telegraphed the Central Organization of All-Indonesia Trade Unions on the 22nd, supporting the Indonesian people's struggle to recover West Irian. The telegram is as follows:
    To the Central Organization of All-Indonesia Trade Unions:
    On behalf of the 24 million male and female workers of China, we express our full support for the just struggle of the Indonesian people to recover West Irian. The Chinese workers and people have always believed that West Irian is an inseparable part of Indonesian territory. The just struggle of the Indonesian people to recover West Irian is not only to oppose colonialism and safeguard Indonesia's territorial sovereignty and integrity, but also to defend peace in Asia and the world. This just struggle of the Indonesian people will be supported by workers and justice-loving people all over the world. Therefore, your just struggle will surely be victorious.
        All-China Federation of Trade Unions
        November 22, 1957
    Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd. Bao Erhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, telegraphed Professor Priyono, President of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association, on the 22nd, supporting the Indonesian people's struggle to recover West Irian. The telegram is as follows:
    To Dr. Priyono, President of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association:
    The entire membership of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association and the Chinese people will resolutely support the just struggle of the Indonesian people to bravely oppose colonialism and recover West Irian with practical actions. Recovering West Irian is not only to safeguard the integrity of Indonesian territory and sovereignty, but also to defend peace in Asia and the world. It will be supported by people all over the world. We wish you final victory in your just struggle.
    President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association
    Bao Erhan
    November 22, 1957

### 中华全国总工会　中国印度尼西亚友协  声援收复西伊里安的斗争

1957-11-23
第5版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会　中国印度尼西亚友协
    声援收复西伊里安的斗争
    新华社22日讯　中华全国总工会22日致电全印度尼西亚工会中央组织，支持印度尼西亚人民收复西伊里安的斗争。电文如下：
    全印度尼西亚工会中央组织：
    我们代表全中国二千四百万男女职工对印度尼西亚人民收复西伊里安的正义斗争表示全力支持。中国工人和人民一向认为，西伊里安是印度尼西亚领土不可分割的一部分。印度尼西亚人民收复西伊里安的正义斗争，不仅是为了反对殖民主义和维护印度尼西亚领土主权完整，而且也是为了保卫亚洲和世界的和平。印度尼西亚人民的这一正义斗争，将得到全世界工人和维护正义的人民的支持。因此，你们的正义斗争一定能够得到胜利。
                中华全国总工会
                  1957年11月22日
    新华社22日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉22日致电印度尼西亚中国友好协会会长普里约诺，支持印度尼西亚人民收复西伊里安的斗争。电文如下：
    印度尼西亚中国友好协会会长普里约诺博士：
    对于印度尼西亚人民英勇反对殖民主义、收复西伊里安的正义斗争，中国印度尼西亚友好协会全体会员和中国人民定用实际行动坚决支持你们。收复西伊里安，不仅是为了维护印度尼西亚领土和主权的完整，也是为了保卫亚洲和世界的和平，它将得到全世界人民的拥护和支持。祝你们正义斗争获得最后的胜利。
    中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长
                      包尔汉
                    1957年11月22日
