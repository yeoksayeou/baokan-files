Title: Vietnamese National Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th. The Vietnamese Democratic Republic National Delegation has arrived in China for a visit at the invitation of the Nationalities Affairs Commission.

The delegation, led by Head of Delegation Shen Yizhong and Deputy Head of Delegation Lin Zhongshu, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of November 4th.  Those present at the station to welcome them included Xie Hechou, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission.  Tran Cao Thanh, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in our country, was also at the station to greet them.

On the afternoon of the 5th, Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, hosted a banquet to welcome all members of the delegation.

### 越南民族访问团到北京

1957-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    越南民族访问团到北京
    新华社5日讯　越南民主共和国民族访问团应民族事务委员会邀请来中国访问。
    访问团由团长沈奕忠、副团长林仲书率领，于11月4日下午到达北京。到站欢迎的有民族事务委员会副主任谢鹤筹等。越南民主共和国驻我国大使馆一等秘书陈高诚也到车站迎接。
    5日下午，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋设宴欢迎访问团全体人员。
