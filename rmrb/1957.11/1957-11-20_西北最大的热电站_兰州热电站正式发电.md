Title: Northwest's Largest Thermal Power Plant, Lanzhou Thermal Power Plant, Officially Generates Electricity
Author:
Date: 1957-11-20
Page: 3

    Northwest's Largest Thermal Power Plant
    Lanzhou Thermal Power Plant Officially Generates Electricity
    Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, November 18th: Lanzhou Thermal Power Plant, the main power base of Gansu's industry, officially generated electricity on the 16th.

    A grand completion ceremony was held before the power generation. At the meeting, Wang Yaohua and Deng Baoshan, on behalf of the Gansu Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Gansu Provincial People's Committee respectively, expressed their heartfelt thanks to the Soviet experts who assisted in the construction of the thermal power plant, and extended cordial greetings to all the workers who participated in the construction.

    At the completion ceremony, representatives from the Lanzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Lanzhou Municipal People's Committee, and the Lanzhou Thermal Power Plant also presented gifts and banners to the Soviet experts and the units participating in the construction of the thermal power plant.

    Lanzhou Thermal Power Plant is currently the largest thermal power plant in the Northwest, and its machinery and equipment are the most advanced in the world. After its completion, in addition to supplying electricity to various construction and production units, it can also supply a large amount of chemically purified water, hot water for heating, and steam at the same time. ### 西北最大的热电站  兰州热电站正式发电

1957-11-20
第3版()
专栏：

    西北最大的热电站
    兰州热电站正式发电
    据新华社兰州18日电　甘肃工业的主要动力基地——兰州热电站16日正式发电。
    发电前，举行了盛大的落成典礼。会上，王耀华、邓宝珊分别代表中共甘肃省委、甘肃省人民委员会向帮助热电站建设的苏联专家表示衷心的谢意，向参加建设的全体职工表示亲切的慰问。
    在落成典礼大会上，中共兰州市委、兰州市人民委员会、兰州热电站的代表，还分别向苏联专家和参加热电站建设的单位赠礼、赠旗。
    兰州热电站是目前西北最大的热电站，它的机器设备是世界上最先进的。它建成以后，除了向各建设生产单位供电外，还能同时大量供应化学净水、取暖用热水和蒸气。（附图片）
