Title: Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:
Date: 1957-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 13th: The fourteen-member delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association, led by Acting Head of Delegation Kiyoshi Murase, left Guangzhou for home on the morning of the 13th.

### 日中友好协会访华团离广州回国

1957-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友好协会访华团离广州回国
    据新华社广州13日电　日本日中友好协会访华代表团一行十四人由代理团长村濑清率领，13日早晨离开广州回国。
