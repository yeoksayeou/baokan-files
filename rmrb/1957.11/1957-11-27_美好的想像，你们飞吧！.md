Title: Beautiful Imagination, Fly On!
Author: Liyang
Date: 1957-11-27
Page: 8

What song makes the strings of our hearts tremble so?
What flower attracts our imagination to bloom?
It is our ears, our hearts that hear again
The second artificial satellite flying through the blue sky.

	Carrying the pride of the victory of communism;
	Carrying our endless joy and congratulations;
Beautiful imagination, fly on, fly on!
In this moonlit, starlit night.
Also at a speed of eight thousand meters per second,
Along the beautiful trajectory of the "Red Moon,"
Fly on! From this clear and limpid azure sky,
Fly towards distant space, distant centuries.

Our ancestors used labor and wisdom,
To hollow wood into boats, creating ships for us,
Enabling us to cross deep and wide rivers;
Enabling us to cross turbulent floods.

Our ancestors also used labor and wisdom,
To create rolling vehicles for us,
Enabling us to trek across one plain after another,
Not with slow steps, but with spinning wheels.

Who sat on that first vehicle?
Who sat on that first ship?
No matter who it was, we, generation after generation,
Have endless gratitude for the creator.

In the near future, perhaps tomorrow,
Communists will again use labor and wisdom,
To create a ship for us that sails to the moon,
One that doesn't need sails or paddles.

At that time, we will travel to various planets,
Just like traveling to various cities within the country;
Perhaps, we will take a trip to the moon,
Just like going to the park for a stroll.

On the moon, we will first meet
That Wu Gang who chops wood year-round;
We will also visit Chang'e in the Moon Palace,
And greet her with a smile, saying "Good morning."

If they have already become
The Adam and Eve of the moon, then,
Please have them lead their numerous descendants,
Back to the human world to join our communist paradise.

During our long journey,
We will also surely meet that celestial couple—
The Cowherd grazing in the west of the river,
And the Weaver Girl weaving in the east of the river.

I don't know if the Yangtze River is a reflection of the Milky Way?
Or if the Milky Way is a shadow of the Yangtze River?
If the Milky Way is a shadow of the Yangtze River, then,
Construction in the heavens should also follow the arrangement of the human world.

Why are the Cowherd and Weaver Girl so unwise,
Always separated by a mere stream, sighing year after year,
Only on the night of the seventh day of the seventh month,
Do they cross the magpie bridge and meet each other once?

Why not rebuild a steel bridge,
Like the Yangtze River Bridge, connecting the east and west of the river?
We can supply all the steel materials;
Soviet experts will also design it for them.

Cowherd! Weaver Girl! Model laborers of the heavens!
If you are still individual farmers,
You will inevitably suffer from disasters, poverty, and hardship for thousands of years,
Then, please join our agricultural producers' cooperative...

Our beautiful imaginations are many, many more,
But a burst of ugly sounds shattered them:
War-mongers, seeing the emergence of this benefactor of humanity,
Do not rejoice, but emit envious cries.

Beautiful things, praise them a thousand times,
Praise them ten thousand times, it's not an exaggeration;
Ugly things, even if you look at them once,
Listen to them once, it's enough to make you sick.

We want to dedicate the flower of flowers to
Humanity's creative and peaceful labor;
We want to use the song of songs to praise
The great achievements of communism.

We don't have time to scold now,
When we fly to the moon,
Let's look back at the war-mongers on Earth,
And spit a star's worth of saliva on their esteemed faces.

### 美好的想像，你们飞吧！
力扬
1957-11-27
第8版()
专栏：

    美好的想像，你们飞吧！	力扬
    是什么歌使我们底心弦如此震动？
    是什么花朵吸引着我们底想像开放？
    是我们底耳朵，我们底心又听见
    第二颗人造卫星在蓝色的高空飞过。
    带着共产主义胜利的自豪；
    带着我们无尽的喜悦和祝贺；
    美好的想像，你们飞吧，飞吧！
    在这月色如水、星光灿烂的良夜。
    也以一秒钟八千公尺的速度，
    沿着美丽的“红色月亮”底轨迹，
    你们飞吧！从这明净澄澈的青空，
    飞向遥远的空间、遥远的世纪。
    我们底祖先用劳动和智慧，
    刳木为舟，给我们创造了船只，
    使我们渡过水深浪阔的江河；
    使我们渡过波涛汹涌的洪水。
    我们底祖先又用劳动和智慧，
    给我们创造了能够滚动的车子，
    使我们跋涉过一个原野又一个原野，
    不是用缓慢的脚步，而是用飞旋着的轮子。
    是谁坐上那第一辆的车子？
    是谁坐上那第一艘的船只？
    不管是谁，我们世世代代的人，
    都对着创造者产生无穷无尽的感激。
    在不久的未来，也许就在明天，
    共产主义者又会用劳动和智慧，
    给我们创造一种航向月亮的船只，
    它用不着风帆，也用不着桨子。
    那时候，我们来往各个星球，
    就好像来往国内各个都市；
    也许，我们去月球旅行一遭，
    就好像到公园里去游玩一次。
    在月球上，我们会首先遇见
    那个终年伐木的吴刚；
    我们也会去访问广寒宫里的嫦娥，
    用微笑向她请一次“早安”。
    如果，他们早就已经成为
    月球上的亚当和夏娃，那么，
    就请他们带领着蕃衍的子孙，
    回到人间，参加我们共产主义的乐园。
    在我们这种漫长的旅途中，
    我们也一定会遇见那天上情侣——
    那在河西放牧的牛郎，
    和那在河东纺织的织女。
    我不知道长江是天河的写照？
    或者天河是长江的影子？
    如果天河是长江的影子，那么，
    天上的建设也应该按照人间的布置。
    为什么牛郎、织女如此的不智，
    老是隔着盈盈一水而终年太息，
    只有在七月七日的那个晚上，
    才渡过了鹊桥，彼此见面一次？
    为什么不改建一座钢铁的桥梁，
    像长江大桥一样，从河东通到河西？
    我们可以供给全部的钢材；
    苏联专家也会替他们设计。
    牛郎！织女！天上的模范劳动者！
    如果你们还是单干的农户，
    几千年来也免不了灾害、贫穷和疾苦，
    那么，就请加入我们底农业生产合作社。……
    我们美好的想像，还有很多很多，
    一阵丑恶的声音却把它们搅破：
    战争贩子们看见这人类福星的出现，
    没有欢笑，却发出嫉妒的啼哭。
    美好的东西，歌颂它一千遍，
    歌颂它一万遍，都不算过分；
    丑恶的东西，即使看它一眼，
    听它一次，也尽够叫人恶心。
    我们要用花中之花献给
    人类底创造性的和平劳动；
    我们要用歌中之歌来赞美
    共产主义的伟绩丰功。
    我们现在没有工夫来唾骂，
    等到我们飞上月亮的日子，
    再回头看一眼地球上的战争贩子们，
    给他们底尊容吐上一星星口水。
