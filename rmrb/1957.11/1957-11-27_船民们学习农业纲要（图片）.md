Title: Boat People Study the Agricultural Program (Picture)
Author: Wang Mianzhi
Date: 1957-11-27
Page: 2

    Boat People Study the Agricultural Program

    After the promulgation of the National Agricultural Development Program (Revised Draft), the boat people along the Xin'an River in Anhui Province were greatly encouraged. This is a scene of members of the Tunxi City Advanced Wooden Sailboat Production Cooperative enthusiastically studying the National Agricultural Development Program on the bow of their boat.
      Photo by Wang Mianzhi
      (Xinhua News Agency Draft)

### 船民们学习农业纲要（图片）
王勉之
1957-11-27
第2版()
专栏：

    船民们学习农业纲要
    全国农业发展纲要（修正草案）公布后，安徽省新安江畔的船民受到极大的鼓舞。这是屯溪市先进木帆船生产合作社社员们在船头热烈学习全国农业发展纲要的情形。
      王勉之摄
      （新华社稿）
