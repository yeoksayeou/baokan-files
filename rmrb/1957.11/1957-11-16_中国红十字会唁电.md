Title: Condolence Telegram from the Chinese Red Cross

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Red Cross Society of China sent a telegram to the Red Cross Society of Czechoslovakia on the 14th, mourning the death of President Antonín Zápotocký.
    The telegram said, "We were shocked to hear of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Antonín Zápotocký, President of the Czechoslovak Republic. On behalf of the members of the Chinese Red Cross Society, we extend our deepest condolences to you, and through you, to the members of the Czechoslovak Red Cross Society and all the people."

### 中国红十字会唁电

1957-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会唁电
    新华社15日讯　中国红十字会14日致电捷克斯洛伐克红十字会，哀悼安托宁·萨波托斯基总统逝世。
    电报说，“惊闻捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基同志不幸逝世，我们谨代表中国红十字会会员向你们，并通过你们，向捷克斯洛伐克红十字会会员和全体人民致以深切的哀悼。”
