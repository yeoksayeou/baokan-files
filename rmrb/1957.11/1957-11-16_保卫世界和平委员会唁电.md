Title: Condolence Telegram from the China Peace Committee

Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 4

Condolence Telegram from the China Peace Committee

To the Czechoslovak Peace Committee:

We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Antonín Zápotocký, President of Czechoslovakia. We extend our deepest condolences to the comrades of the Czechoslovak Peace Committee and the people of Czechoslovakia.

Comrade Antonín Zápotocký devoted his life to making outstanding contributions to the cause of world peace and socialism. His passing is a great loss not only for the people of Czechoslovakia but also for the people of socialist countries and progressive forces around the world.

At this sorrowful moment, the comrades of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, together with the Chinese people, express to you: we will forever strengthen solidarity with the Czechoslovak people, work together to promote the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Czech people, consolidate the socialist forces led by the Soviet Union, and strive for lasting world peace.

The great spirit and glorious cause of President Antonín Zápotocký will be immortal!

The Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace
November 14, 1957

### 保卫世界和平委员会唁电

1957-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    保卫世界和平委员会唁电捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会：
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克总统安托宁·萨波托斯基同志不幸逝世，我们以十分沉痛的心情向捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会的同志们，向捷克斯洛伐克人民，表示深切的哀悼。
    安托宁·萨波托斯基同志的一生为世界和平和社会主义事业作出了卓越的贡献。他的逝世不仅是捷克斯洛伐克人民，而且是社会主义各国人民和世界进步力量的重大损失。
    在这沉痛的时刻，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的同志们和中国人民一起，向你们表示：我们永远和捷克斯洛伐克人民加强团结，为促进中捷人民的兄弟友谊，为巩固以苏联为首的社会主义力量和争取世界持久和平而共同努力。
    安托宁·萨波托斯基总统的伟大精神和光荣事业永垂不朽！
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
                      1957年11月14日
