Title: U.S. Sends Additional Missile Units to Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1957-11-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Washington dispatch: The U.S. Department of Defense announced on the 18th that the U.S. will dispatch a "Matador" missile unit to be stationed in Taiwan within the next two weeks.

This is the second batch of missile units transferred to Taiwan after the U.S. dispatched a batch of "small missile units" to be stationed in Taiwan in May of this year.

A United Press International report admitted that these missile units are precisely aimed at air force bases on the Chinese mainland.

### 美国向台湾增派导弹部队

1957-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    美国向台湾增派导弹部队
    新华社19日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国防部18日宣布，美国将在最近两个星期内派遣一支“斗牛士”导弹部队前往台湾驻扎。
    这是美国在今年5月派遣一批“小导弹部队”驻扎台湾以后第二批调到台湾的导弹部队。
    合众社消息承认，这些导弹部队的矛头正是指向中国大陆上的空军基地。
