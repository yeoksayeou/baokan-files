Title: Soviet Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou; 5,000 People from All Circles in Guangdong Hold Rally to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 18th: The Soviet delegation composed of representatives from all circles to attend the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, headed by Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-China Friendship Association, arrived in Guangzhou by train at 10:30 this morning from Hangzhou, accompanied by Liao Chengzhi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Cao Mengjun, Council Member, and Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General.

Those who welcomed the distinguished guests at the station included Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province; Qu Mengjue, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and President of the Guangzhou Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and leading figures from various circles. Soviet Consul in Guangzhou, Orlov, also greeted the delegation at the station.

At noon, Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Governor Chen Yu, Wang De, First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Mayor Zhu Guang had a cordial conversation with the distinguished guests of the delegation headed by Andreyev at the Yingbin Hotel.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 18th: Five thousand people from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City gathered tonight to welcome the Soviet delegation composed of representatives from all circles to attend the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, headed by Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-China Friendship Association.

Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Wang De, First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, and people from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City attended the welcoming rally. Liao Chengzhi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Cao Mengjun, Council Member, and Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General, who accompanied the delegation to Guangzhou, also attended the rally.

Andreyev spoke at the rally. He praised the Chinese people's spirit of unyielding socialist construction under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party. He said, "What particularly moves our delegation is the sincere friendship and love of the Chinese people for the Soviet people." His speech was interrupted by enthusiastic applause from time to time.

At the beginning of the meeting, Tao Zhu delivered a speech, repeatedly expressing a warm welcome to the distinguished guests.

In the afternoon, the distinguished guests paid homage to the Guangzhou Commune Martyrs' Cemetery and laid a wreath. Members of the delegation also visited the water dwellers on the Pearl River.

### 苏联各界代表团到广州  广东各界五千人集会欢迎

1957-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联各界代表团到广州
    广东各界五千人集会欢迎
    新华社广州18日电　以苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫为首的苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团，在中苏友好协会总会副会长廖承志、理事曹孟君和副秘书长戈宝权陪同下，今天早晨十时三十分自杭州乘火车到达广州。
    到车站欢迎贵宾的有广东省省长陈郁，中共广东省委书记区梦觉，广州市市长、中共广州市委书记、广州市中苏友好协会会长朱光和各界负责人。苏联驻广州领事鄂洛夫也到车站迎接。
    中午，中共广东省委第一书记陶铸、省长陈郁、中共广州市委第一书记王德、市长朱光在迎宾馆同以安德烈耶夫为首的代表团贵宾们进行了亲切的交谈。
    新华社广州18日电　广东省和广州市各界人民五千人今晚集会，欢迎以苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫为首的苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团。
    中共广东省委第一书记陶铸、广东省省长陈郁、中共广州市委第一书记王德、广州市市长朱光和广东省、广州市各界人士出席了欢迎大会。陪同代表团来到广州的中苏友好协会总会副会长廖承志、理事曹孟君和副秘书长戈宝权也出席了大会。
    安德烈耶夫在大会上讲话。他赞扬中国人民在中国共产党领导下百折不挠地进行社会主义建设的精神。他说：“特别使我们代表团感动的，是中国人民对于苏联人民的真挚友情和热爱”。他的讲话不时被热烈的掌声所打断。
    大会开始时，陶铸致词一再向贵宾们表示热烈的欢迎。
    下午，贵宾们拜谒了广州公社烈士陵墓，并献了花圈。代表团团员们还访问了珠江上的水上居民。
