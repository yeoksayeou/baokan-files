Title: Suez Canal Revenue Increases
Author:
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 25th - Egyptian Minister of Finance, Keshunni, stated on November 23rd at the Cairo Chamber of Commerce Club that it is estimated that from June 1957 to June 1958, the Suez Canal's revenue will be between 35 million and 40 million Egyptian pounds. Before the Suez Canal was nationalized by Egypt, its revenue was only 30 million Egyptian pounds. He said that this estimate is based on the canal's revenue since it reopened to traffic in May of this year.

### 苏伊士运河收入增加

1957-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    苏伊士运河收入增加
    据新华社开罗25日电　埃及财政部长凯舒尼11月23日在开罗的商会俱乐部发表演说时说，估计从1957年6月到1958年6月，苏伊士运河的收益将为三千五百万到四千万埃镑；而苏伊士运河在被埃及收归国有以前收益仅为三千万埃镑。他说，这项估计是根据运河自从今年5月恢复通行以后的收入作出的。
