Title: Egyptian and Syrian Presidents Agree: Imperialist Aggression Against Syria Will Fail
Author:
Date: 1957-11-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 2nd: Egyptian President Nasser and Syrian President Quwatli stated on November 1st that any imperialist aggression against Syria would meet the same shame and failure as the attack on Egypt last year.
    The Egyptian and Syrian heads of state exchanged letters and telegrams on the anniversary of the Anglo-French and Israeli aggression against Egypt. In his reply to Quwatli, Nasser expressed his hope that Syria would achieve final victory in the current struggle against imperialism.
    Syrian President Quwatli said in his telegram to Nasser, "Today, imperialism is staging a comeback. But our position is the same as a year ago, and we are even more convinced of the righteousness of our cause, the strength of our brotherhood, and our united strength."

### 埃叙两国总统一致表示  帝国主义侵叙将遭到失败

1957-11-03
第6版()
专栏：

    埃叙两国总统一致表示
    帝国主义侵叙将遭到失败
    据新华社开罗2日电　埃及总统纳赛尔和叙利亚总统库阿特利11月1日表示，帝国主义对叙利亚的任何侵略将遭到像去年进攻埃及一样的羞耻和失败。
    埃及和叙利亚的元首在英法和以色列侵略埃及一周年之际互致函电。纳赛尔在给库阿特利的复电中表示希望叙利亚在当前反帝国主义的斗争中获得最后的胜利。
    叙利亚总统库阿特利在给纳赛尔的电报中说，“今天帝国主义又在卷土重来。但是我们的立场和一年前一样，并且更加深信我们的事业的正义性和我们的兄弟关系以及我们团结一致的强大力量。”
