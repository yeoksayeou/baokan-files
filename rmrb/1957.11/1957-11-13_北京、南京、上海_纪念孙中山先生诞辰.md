Title: Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai Commemorate Sun Yat-sen's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1957-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 12th - Today is the 91st anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. More than sixty people, including the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Standing Committee members, and Committee members of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, visited the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall at Biyun Temple in Xiangshan.

The visit ceremony was held at 10:30 am. Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, presented flowers to the statue of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Subsequently, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Zhizhong, Vice-Chairmen of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and Zhu Yunshan, Shao Lizi, Chen Qiyuan, Weng Wenhao, Mei Gongbin, Jiang Guangnai, and other Standing Committee members, stood in silence before the statue of Mr. Sun Yat-sen to pay their respects.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, November 12th - Today, on the 91st anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, representatives from various circles in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City held a memorial ceremony at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum this morning, presented wreaths to the portrait of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, and paid their respects at Mr. Sun Yat-sen's mausoleum.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 12th - To commemorate the 91st anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, Chen Pixian and Wei Wenbo, Secretaries of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Cao Diqiu, Xu Jianguo, Jin Zhonghua, Liu Shuzhou, Song Richang, Zhao Zukang, Sheng Pihua, and Rong Yiren, Vice Mayors of Shanghai, and Hu Juewen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, visited the former residence of Sun Yat-sen on Xiangshan Road this morning and presented wreaths.

### 北京、南京、上海  纪念孙中山先生诞辰

1957-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    北京、南京、上海
    纪念孙中山先生诞辰
    据新华社12日讯　今天是孙中山先生诞生九十一周年的纪念日。中国国民党革命委员会主席、副主席、中常委、中委等六十多人到香山碧云寺晋谒孙中山纪念堂。
    晋谒仪式在上午十时半举行。民革主席李济深向孙中山先生像献鲜花。随后，民革副主席蔡廷锴、张治中，中常委朱蕴山、邵力子、陈其瑗、翁文灏、梅龚彬、蒋光鼐等人在孙中山先生像前肃立致敬。
    据新华社南京12日电　在孙中山先生诞生九十一周年的今天，江苏省、南京市各界代表上午在中山陵举行纪念仪式，向孙中山先生遗像敬献花圈，并晋谒中山先生陵寝。
    新华社上海12日电　为纪念孙中山先生诞生九十一周年，12日上午，中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显、魏文伯，上海市副市长曹荻秋、许建国、金仲华、刘述周、宋日昌、赵祖康、盛丕华、荣毅仁，市政协副主席胡厥文，前往香山路瞻仰了中山故居并敬献花圈。
