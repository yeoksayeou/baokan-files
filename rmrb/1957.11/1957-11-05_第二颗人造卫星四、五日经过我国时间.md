Title: The Second Artificial Satellite Passes Over China on the 4th and 5th

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: According to TASS news agency: The Soviet Union's second artificial satellite and the carrier rocket of the first artificial satellite will pass over Chengdu, Lanzhou, Shenyang, Changchun, Chongqing, Foshan, Lhasa, Guangzhou, Nanjing, Yumen, Qiqihar, Hailun, Huma, Tacheng and other places in China on the 4th and 5th respectively.

The second satellite will fly in from Yangon at 7:11 am Beijing time on the 4th, pass Chengdu at 7:15 am, pass Lanzhou at 7:16 am, and arrive in Ulaanbaatar at 7:19 am. At 2:30 pm, it will fly in from Ulan-Ude (Soviet Union), pass Shenyang and Changchun at 2:34 pm, and arrive in Pyongyang at 2:35 pm. At 4:05 pm, it will fly in from Arkhangelsk, pass Lanzhou at 4:21 pm, pass Chongqing and Chengdu at 4:23 pm, pass Foshan at 4:25 pm, and arrive in Melbourne at 4:47 pm. At 6:01 pm, it will fly in from Almaty, pass Lhasa at 6:06 pm, and arrive in Yangon at 6:11 pm. At 4:35 am on the 5th, it will fly in from Caracas (Venezuela), pass Guangzhou at 5:41 am, pass Nanjing at 5:43 am, pass Shenyang at 5:47 am, and arrive in Magadan (Soviet Union) at 5:53 am. At 7:25 am, it will fly in from Calcutta, pass Yumen at 7:30 am, and arrive in Ulaanbaatar at 7:32 am.

The carrier rocket of the first satellite will fly in from Montevideo at 2:23 pm on the 4th, pass Qiqihar and Hailun at 3:13 pm, pass Huma at 3:14 pm, and arrive in Okhotsk at 3:18 pm. At 5:41 pm, it will fly in from Graham Land (Antarctica), pass Tacheng at 6:21 pm, and arrive in Karaganda (Soviet Union) at 6:22 pm.

### 第二颗人造卫星四、五日经过我国时间

1957-11-05
第8版()
专栏：

    第二颗人造卫星四、五日经过我国时间
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联第二个人造卫星和第一个人造卫星的运载火箭4日、5日将分别经过我国的成都、兰州、沈阳、长春、重庆、佛山、拉萨、广州、南京、玉门、齐齐哈尔、海伦、呼玛、塔城等地。
    第二个卫星在4日北京时间七点十一分从仰光飞来，七点十五分过成都，七点十六分过兰州，七点十九分到乌兰巴托。十四点三十分从乌兰乌德（苏联）飞来，十四点三十四分过沈阳、长春，十四点三十五分到平壤。十六点五分从阿尔汉格尔飞来，十六点二十一分过兰州，十六点二十三分过重庆、成都，十六点二十五分过佛山，十六点四十七分到墨尔本。十八点一分从阿拉木图飞来，十八点六分过拉萨，十八点十一分到仰光。5日四点三十五分从加拉加斯（委内瑞拉）飞来，五点四十一分过广州，五点四十三分过南京，五点四十七分过沈阳，五点五十三分到马加丹（苏联）。七点二十五分从加尔各答飞来，七点三十分过玉门，七点三十二分到乌兰巴托。
    第一个卫星的运载火箭在4日十四点二十三分从蒙得维的亚来，十五点十三分过齐齐哈尔、海伦，十五点十四分过呼玛，十五点十八分到鄂霍次克。十七点四十一分从格雷姆地（南极）来，十八点二十一分过塔城，十八点二十二分到卡拉干达（苏联）。
