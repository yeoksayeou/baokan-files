Title: Second Day of Sino-German Correspondence Shooting Competition: Chinese Women's Team Achieves Excellent Results
Author:
Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 8

Sports News

    Second Day of Sino-German Correspondence Shooting Competition
    Chinese Women's Team Achieves Excellent Results

    (By our staff reporter) The Sino-German correspondence shooting competition concluded its third event for the Chinese side on the 21st.

    In the men's small-bore rifle 50-meter distance prone, kneeling, and standing team competition, the Chinese team, composed of Zhao Shanjun, Xu Yuhua, and others (each person shooting forty bullets in the prone, kneeling, and standing positions), achieved a total score of 5,614 rings. In the prone position competition, Xu Yuhua scored 399 rings with forty bullets (the full score being 400 rings), breaking the national record of 398 rings. Tan Binggong, who participated in the individual championship competition for this event, was the best shooter among the four, scoring a total of 1,113 rings.

    The women's small-bore rifle 50-meter distance prone, kneeling, and standing competition (each person shooting forty bullets in the prone, kneeling, and standing positions) was a formal competition for our country for the first time. However, our shooters – Hu Zhi, Lu Guizhen, Cao Jingfen, Li Suping, Fu Junjie, and others – all performed very well, achieving an excellent score of 5,667 rings. This score is 53 rings higher than the men's team. Zhang Hongzhi and Li Sufen, who participated in the individual championship competition for this event, scored 1,107 rings and 1,093 rings, respectively.

    In the eye-catching event – the men's small-bore pistol 25-meter distance silhouette target rapid-fire competition – the team competition resulted in the Chinese team hitting a total of 298 silhouette targets, scoring 2,831 rings. Participating in the competition were renowned Chinese shooters and athletes Zhang Hong and Li Jie, among others. Zhang Hong did not achieve ideal results this time. Li Jie's score was better than Zhang Hong's, as he scored 583 rings, making him the best shooter in this rapid-fire pistol competition. Dong Ruiguang, who participated in the individual championship competition for pistol rapid-fire, hit 59 targets out of 60 bullets, scoring 566 rings; Zhao Guorui and Yang Yanpu hit 60 targets, scoring 563 rings, and 59 targets, scoring 545 rings, respectively.

### 中德通讯射击比赛第二天  中国方面女子队成绩很好

1957-11-22
第8版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中德通讯射击比赛第二天
    中国方面女子队成绩很好
    本报讯　中国和德意志民主共和国之间的通讯射击比赛，到21日止，中国方面已经举行完三个项目的比赛。
    这次比赛的男子小口径步枪五十公尺距离卧、跪、立姿比赛的队际比赛，中国队由赵善俊、徐雨华等五人参加
  （每人在卧、跪、立姿中各射四十发子弹），总成绩是五千六百一十四环。徐雨华在卧姿比赛中，他四十发子弹打了三百九十九环（满环是四百环），这个成绩打破了三百九十八环的全国最高纪录。参加这项比赛个人冠军赛的谭炳贡，是四个人中打得最好的一个，他一共打了一千一百一十三环。
    女子小口径步枪五十公尺距离卧、跪、立姿比赛（每人在卧、跪、立姿中各射四十发子弹），这个项目的正式比赛，在我国还是第一次。可是我们的射手们——胡智、陆桂珍、曹靖芬、李素萍、傅均洁等都打得很好，她们取得了五千六百六十七环的优异成绩。这个成绩比男子队还多五十三环。参加这项比赛个人冠军赛的张宏智和李素芬分别取得一千一百零七环和一千零九十三环的成绩。
    引人注意的项目——男子小口径手枪二十五公尺距离人像靶速射比赛，队际比赛的结果是，中国队共命中二百九十八个人像靶，二千八百三十一环。参加比赛的有我国著名射手、运动健将张鈜和李杰等。这次张鈜没有打出理想的成绩。李杰的成绩比张鈜好，他打了五百八十三环，他是这次手枪速射比赛中打得最好的一个。参加手枪速射个人冠军赛的董瑞光，是六十发子弹，命中五十九个靶，五百六十六环；赵国瑞和杨延朴分别中了六十个靶、五百六十三环和五十九个靶、五百四十五环。
