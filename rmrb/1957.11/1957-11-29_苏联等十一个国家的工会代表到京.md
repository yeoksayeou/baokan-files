Title: Trade Union Representatives from Eleven Countries Including the Soviet Union Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Trade union representatives from eleven more countries arrived in Beijing this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the Eighth National Congress of the Trade Unions of China.

They are: the Soviet trade union delegation headed by P.T. Pimenov, Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Australian Council of Trade Unions Vice-President Kenny and his wife, Free German Trade Union Federation Presidium member Rudi Haeponel, Finnish Trade Union Central Federation Second Chairman Vihtori Rantanen, Japanese Trade Union General Council Vice-Chairman Mitsuo Fujioka, Moroccan Labour Union Treasurer Mohammed Abderrazak, Bulgarian Trade Union Central Council Secretary Nikola Tsaichev, Burmese Trade Union Congress Chairman Tekin Lun, Albanian Trade Union Central Council Secretary Sojil Camberi, Argentine trade union representative and Buenos Aires Province Leader of the Movement for Trade Union Democracy and Independence Ignacio Alba, and Korean Vocational General League Central Committee Chairman Han Sang-doo.

Those who went to welcome them included Liu Changsheng and Zhu Xuefan, vice-chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Dong Xin and Zhang Xiuzhu, secretaries of the Secretariat.

### 苏联等十一个国家的工会代表到京

1957-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联等十一个国家的工会代表到京
    新华社28日讯　又有十一个国家的工会代表应中华全国总工会邀请在今天下午分别到达北京，前来参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会。
    他们是：以全苏工会中央理事会书记普罗霍罗夫为首的苏联工会代表团，澳大利亚工会理事会副主席肯尼及夫人，自由德国工会联合会主席团委员鲁迪·海波涅尔，芬兰工会中央联合会第二主席维赫里·朗塔宁，日本工会总评议会副议长藤冈三男，摩洛哥劳工联盟司库穆罕默德·阿布得拉扎克，保加利亚工会中央理事会书记尼古拉·蔡契夫，缅甸工会大会主席德钦伦，阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会书记索吉尔·坎伯里，阿根廷工会代表、布宜诺斯艾利斯省争取工会民主独立运动领导人依纳西奥·阿尔巴，朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会委员长韩相斗。
    前往欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜、朱学范，书记处书记董昕，张修竹等人。
