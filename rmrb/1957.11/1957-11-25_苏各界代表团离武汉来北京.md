Title: Soviet Delegation Departs Wuhan for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, November 23rd: The distinguished Soviet guests from all walks of life, who are part of the delegation commemorating the 40th anniversary of the Great October Revolution with the Chinese people, visited factories and schools and met with people from all walks of life in Wuhan today.

　　Head of the delegation, Comrade Andreyev, and some delegation members, accompanied by Li Erzhong, Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, visited the Wuhan Iron and Steel Complex in the morning and were warmly welcomed by the Party Committee Secretary, General Manager, and one thousand employees of the factory.

　　The distinguished guests visited Wuhan University, Wuchang No. 1 Cotton Mill, Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant, Wuhan Medical College, and a certain Senior Infantry School in the morning. In the afternoon, some of the distinguished guests held discussions with the heads of the Wuhan Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and met with young people.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, November 24th: The Soviet delegation, led by Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, which came to China to participate in the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, left Wuhan by train on the evening of the 23rd to return to Beijing after concluding its two-day visit to Wuhan.

　　Before their departure, Li Erzhong, President of the Hubei Province and Wuhan City Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations and Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, hosted a banquet to see the distinguished guests off.

### 苏各界代表团离武汉来北京

1957-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    苏各界代表团离武汉来北京
    据新华社武汉23日电　苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝伟大十月革命四十周年纪念代表团的贵宾们，今天在武汉参观工厂、学校并同武汉各界人士会见。
    安德烈耶夫团长和部分团员，上午由中共武汉市委书记李尔重陪同到武汉钢铁联合企业参观时，受到这个厂的党委书记、总经理和一千职工的热情欢迎。
    贵宾们上午分别访问了武汉大学、武昌第一纱厂、武汉重型机床厂、武汉医学院和某高级步兵学校。下午，部分贵宾同武汉市中苏友好协会负责人座谈，并和青少年会见。
    新华社武汉24日电　以苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫为首的苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年纪念代表团，结束了在武汉的两天访问后，23日晚上乘火车离开武汉回北京。
    离别前，湖北省及武汉市中苏友好协会会长、中共武汉市委书记李尔重设宴为贵宾们饯行。
