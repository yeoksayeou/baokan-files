Title: Conspiracy of Subversion by US-Dutch Colonialists Attempts to Change Indonesia's Independent Foreign Policy
Author:
Date: 1957-11-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 14th: According to a report in the "Indonesian Torch" on the 14th, certain major powers are plotting actions against Indonesia in Singapore, with the aim of changing the Indonesian government's active and independent foreign policy and dragging Indonesia into a certain bloc.

The newspaper pointed out that these major powers are using the "Islamic Army" and Indonesian businessmen and socialists in Singapore to achieve the above-mentioned goals. Indonesian socialists are working hard to undermine Indonesia's grain supply efforts.

"Indonesia News" also pointed out on the 14th that some countries are obstructing Indonesia from implementing an independent policy and a policy of friendship with newly established countries. The countries that are carrying out these obstructions are of course countries in the Manila Treaty Organization, led by the United States and including Dutch imperialism.

### 妄想改变印度尼西亚独立外交政策  美荷殖民者制造颠覆阴谋

1957-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    妄想改变印度尼西亚独立外交政策
    美荷殖民者制造颠覆阴谋
    新华社雅加达14日电　据“印度尼西亚火炬报”14日报道，某些大国正在新加坡策划对付印度尼西亚的行动，目的是改变印度尼西亚政府的积极和独立自主的外交政策，并且把印度尼西亚拉入某个集团。
    这家报纸指出，这些大国正在使用“伊斯兰教军”以及在新加坡的印度尼西亚商人和社会党人来达到上述目的。印度尼西亚社会党人正在竭力破坏印度尼西亚的谷物供应工作。
    “印度尼西亚新闻”在14日也指出，有一些国家阻挠印度尼西亚执行独立的以及和新建国家友好的政策，这些进行阻挠的国家当然是以美国为首的并且包括荷兰帝国主义在内的马尼拉条约组织中的国家。
