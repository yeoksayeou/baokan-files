Title: Eisenhower's Popularity Declines

Author:

Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 20th: The "U.S. News & World Report" on November 8th stated that Eisenhower's "prestige" among the American people had fallen in October to its lowest point since he became president in 1953.

The magazine said that, according to the Gallup poll, nearly eighty out of every hundred people surveyed approved of Eisenhower's actions in January of this year. However, by October, this percentage had dropped to fifty-seven.

### 艾森豪威尔行情见落

1957-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔行情见落
    据新华社20日讯　11月8日的“美国新闻与世界报道”说，艾森豪威尔在美国人民中的“威信”在10月份已经降到了他1953年任总统以来的最低点。
    这家杂志说，据盖洛普民意测验，今年1月份，在被测验的人中每一百个人将近有八十个对艾森豪威尔的做法表示赞同。但是，到10月份这个百分比已经降到了五十七。
