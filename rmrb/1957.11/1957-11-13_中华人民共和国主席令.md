Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1957-11-13
Page: 1

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    The "Regulations on the 1958 National Economic Construction Bonds" were adopted by the 83rd meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on November 6, 1957, and are hereby promulgated.
                                  Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                       November 6, 1957

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1957-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    一九五八年国家经济建设公债条例已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于一九五七年十一月六日第八十三次会议通过，现予公布。
                                  中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                       一九五七年十一月六日
