A Little Girl's Coat

This little coat is suitable for girls aged three to four. Its advantage is that it saves fabric and is comfortable and beautiful. Just 3.24 chi of 2.4 chi wide material is enough to make one. Corduroy or ribbed fabric is best. If you use light-colored fabric for the clothes, you can embroider dark-colored flowers and leaves; conversely, embroider light-colored flowers and leaves. The style and cutting method of this coat were designed by the Research Institute of the Beijing Clothing Production Cooperative Union.

Coat dimensions: Body length 46 cm (1.38 chi); shoulder width 28 cm (0.84 chi); sleeve length 34 cm (1.02 chi); neckline 29 cm (0.87 chi) (with picture)

Cutting diagram description: 1. Front piece, 2. Back piece, 3. Front piece armhole, 4. Back piece armhole, 5. Sleeve piece, 6. Sleeve placket, 7. Sleeve cuff, 8. Collar facing, 9. Collar lining, 10. Front piece facing, 11. Pocket

### 小女孩的外衣

1957-11-17
第8版()
专栏：

    小女孩的外衣
    这件小外衣适合三、四岁的女孩穿。它的优点是既省布，又舒适、美观。用二点四尺宽，三点二四尺长的料子，就够做一件。料子最好用灯芯绒或罗纹布。若用浅色料子作衣服，可以镶深色的花和叶子；反之，则镶浅色的花和叶子。这件外衣的式样和裁剪方法，是由北京市服装生产合作社联合社研究所设计的。
    外衣的尺寸：身长四十六公分（1．38尺）；肩宽  二十八公分（0．84尺）；袖长  三十四公分（1．02尺）；领口  二十九公分（0．87尺）（附图片）
