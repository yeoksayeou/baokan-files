Title: The CPSU Central Committee Presidium Hosts Delegations from Various Countries: Comrades Khrushchev, Mao Zedong, and Duclos Speak at the Banquet

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 18th: The Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union hosted a banquet in the Kremlin on November 17th to welcome the party and government delegations and party delegations from various countries who attended the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. Attending the banquet were the heads and members of delegations from various countries, members of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, members of the CPSU Central Committee, and ministers of the Soviet government.
    Comrade Khrushchev, Comrade Mao Zedong, and Comrade Duclos spoke at the banquet.
    The banquet was filled with an atmosphere of sincerity and fraternal friendship.

### 苏共中央主席团招待各国代表团  赫鲁晓夫同志、毛泽东同志和杜克洛同志在宴会上讲了话

1957-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央主席团招待各国代表团
    赫鲁晓夫同志、毛泽东同志和杜克洛同志在宴会上讲了话
    新华社莫斯科18日电　苏联共产党中央委员会主席团11月17日在克里姆林宫设宴招待参加伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年庆祝典礼的各国党、政府代表团和党代表团。出席宴会的有各国代表团团长和团员、苏共中央主席团委员、苏共中央委员和苏联政府各部部长。
    赫鲁晓夫同志、毛泽东同志和杜克洛同志在宴会上讲了话。
    宴会充满了诚挚和兄弟友好的气氛。
