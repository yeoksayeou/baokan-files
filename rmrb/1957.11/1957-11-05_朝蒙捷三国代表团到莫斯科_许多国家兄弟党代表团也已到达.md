Title: Delegations from North Korea, Mongolia, and Czechoslovakia Arrive in Moscow; Delegations from Brother Parties of Many Countries Also Arrive
Author:
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 6
Comprehensive report from this newspaper: According to Xinhua News Agency reports: The delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Kim Il-sung, the delegation from the Mongolian People's Republic, and the delegation from Czechoslovakia, headed by Novotny, arrived in Moscow on the 4th, having been invited to participate in the celebrations of the October Revolution Festival.
Xinhua News Agency, dated the 4th, reports from TASS, Moscow: The delegation of the Indonesian Communist Party, headed by Sudisman, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, arrived in Moscow on the 3rd to participate in the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.
The following have also arrived in the Soviet capital: The delegation of the Japanese Communist Party, headed by Shiga Yoshio, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee; the delegation of the Spanish Communist Party, headed by General Secretary Ibarruri; the delegation of the Argentine Communist Party, headed by Party Central Committee Secretary Codovilla; the delegation of the Mexican Communist Party, headed by Party Central Committee General Secretary Encina; the delegation of the Ecuadorian Communist Party, headed by Party Central Committee General Secretary Saad; the delegation of the Uruguayan Communist Party, led by Party Central Committee General Secretary Arismendi; the delegation of the Norwegian Communist Party, headed by Party Central Committee Chairman Lovlien; and the delegation of the Luxembourg Communist Party, headed by Party Central Committee General Secretary Urbany.
The Australian Communist Party delegation has also arrived in Moscow, including Australian Communist Party Chairman Dixon and General Secretary Sharkey.

### 朝蒙捷三国代表团到莫斯科  许多国家兄弟党代表团也已到达

1957-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    朝蒙捷三国代表团到莫斯科
    许多国家兄弟党代表团也已到达
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：应邀参加十月革命节庆祝典礼的以金日成为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团、蒙古人民共和国代表团和以诺沃提尼为首的捷克斯洛伐克代表团，分别于4日到达莫斯科。
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：以印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会政治局委员苏蒂斯曼为首的印度尼西亚共产党代表团在3日到达莫斯科，来参加十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼。
    已经到达苏联首都的还有：以中央常任干部会委员志贺义雄为首的日本共产党代表团，以总书记伊巴露丽为首的西班牙共产党代表团，以党中央书记柯都维亚为首的阿根廷共产党代表团，以党中央总书记恩西那为首的墨西哥共产党代表团，以党中央总书记萨德为首的厄瓜多尔共产党代表团，同党中央总书记阿里斯门迪一起前来的乌拉圭共产党代表团，以党中央主席洛夫林为首的挪威共产党代表团，以党中央总书记欧班尼为首的卢森堡共产党代表团。
    澳大利亚共产党代表团也已经到达莫斯科，其中包括澳共主席狄克逊和总书记夏基。
