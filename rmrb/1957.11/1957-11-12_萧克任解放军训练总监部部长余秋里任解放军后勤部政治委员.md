Title: Xiao Ke Appointed as Minister of the PLA Training Supervision Department, Yu Qiuli Appointed as Political Commissar of the PLA General Logistics Department
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, according to the decision of the 83rd meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, appointed Xiao Ke as Minister of the Training Supervision Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Yu Qiuli as Political Commissar of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 萧克任解放军训练总监部部长余秋里任解放军后勤部政治委员

1957-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    萧克任解放军训练总监部部长余秋里任解放军后勤部政治委员
    新华社11日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第八十三次会议的决定，任命萧克为中国人民解放军训练总监部部长，余秋里为中国人民解放军总后方勤务部政治委员。
