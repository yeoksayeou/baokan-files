    Gale Forecast
    At 8 p.m. on November 17, the Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a gale forecast:
    The cold air mass moving from western Siberia has moved to the central part of the Mongolian People's Republic and will continue to move eastward. Starting on the night of the 17th, the southwest wind of about force six in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region will turn to a northwest wind of about force six. The Hexi Corridor will have a northwest wind of force five or six. During the day of the 18th, the entire northeastern region, the Bohai Sea, the northern Yellow Sea, and the Shandong Peninsula will change from southwest winds of about force six to northwest winds of about force six. Starting on the morning of the 18th, northern Hebei and northern Shanxi will have northerly winds of force five or six.
    On the morning of the 18th, the temperature in northern Xinjiang and the Hexi Corridor will drop by 8-10 degrees. On the morning of the 19th, the temperature in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and western Northeast China will also drop by 8-10 degrees.

### 大风预报

1957-11-18
第8版()
专栏：气象台

    大风预报
    11月17日下午8时中央气象科学研究所发布大风预报：
    由西伯利亚西部移来的冷空气已经移到蒙古人民共和国中部，并将继续向东移动。17日夜间起，内蒙古自治区由六级左右的西南风转为六级左右的西北风。河西走廊有有五、六级的西北风。18日白天，东北全区，渤海、黄海北部，山东半岛由六级左右的西南风转为六级左右的西北风。18日上午起河北北部和山西北部有五、六级的偏北风。
    18日早晨，新疆北部河西走廊的气温将下降8—10度。19日早晨内蒙古自治区和东北西部的气温也将下降8—10度。
