Title: Telegram of Condolence from the Central Committee of the Jiu San Society

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 4

Telegram of Condolence from the Central Committee of the Jiu San Society to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia:

    We were shocked to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Antonín Zápotocký, President of the Republic of Czechoslovakia. With deep sorrow, we, on behalf of all members of the Jiu San Society, extend our deepest condolences to you.
            Central Committee of the Jiu San Society
                      November 14, 1957

### 九三学社中央唁电

1957-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    九三学社中央唁电捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会：
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基同志不幸逝世。我们怀着十分悲痛的心情代表九三学社全体社员向你们致以深切的哀悼。
            九三学社中央委员会
                      1957年11月14日
