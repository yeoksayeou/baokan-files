Title: North Atlantic Group and Turkey Hold Exercises Threatening Syria
Author:
Date: 1957-11-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Istanbul, November 1st: North Atlantic Group forces and Turkish troops began large-scale exercises on October 31st along the southwestern coast of Turkey and near the Syrian border.

    The sea, land, and air forces of the North Atlantic Group's Southern European Command are conducting "Red Era" exercises on the southwestern coast of Turkey. Participants include the U.S. Sixth Fleet, the British Air Force, and North Atlantic Group army forces conducting landing exercises. These exercises will continue until November 2nd. Concurrently, Turkish troops are conducting exercises near the Syrian border.
    Following this, the Baghdad Pact nations will hold exercises in the Indian Ocean in late November.

### 北大西洋集团和土耳其举行威胁叙利亚的演习

1957-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    北大西洋集团和土耳其举行威胁叙利亚的演习
    新华社1日讯　伊斯坦布尔消息：北大西洋集团的部队和土耳其军队10月31日开始在土耳其西南沿海和叙利亚边境附近举行大规模的演习。
    北大西洋集团南欧部队的海、陆、空三军在土耳其西南沿海举行“红色时代”演习。参加演习的包括美国第六舰队、英国空军和举行登陆演习的北大西洋集团陆军部队。这次演习将继续到11月2日。同时，土耳其军队在叙利亚边境附近举行演习。
    在这以后，巴格达条约国家将于11月下旬在印度洋举行演习。
