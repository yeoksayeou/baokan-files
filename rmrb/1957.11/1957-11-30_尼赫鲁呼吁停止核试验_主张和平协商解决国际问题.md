Title: Nehru Calls for Halt to Nuclear Tests, Advocates Peaceful Negotiation to Resolve International Issues
Author:
Date: 1957-11-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 28th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru today appealed to the leaders of major powers, especially the leaders of the United States and the Soviet Union, to peacefully resolve international issues. He also called on the leaders of the United States and the Soviet Union to "halt all nuclear explosion tests" and to "begin effective disarmament."

Nehru said, "The only way is peaceful coexistence, despite the differences." "Only through peaceful direct contact and reaching agreements can these differences be resolved."

Nehru read his appeal at a press conference held in New Delhi. Nehru pointed out, "Today, the United States and Russia possess a large number of nuclear weapons, and Britain also has them. Tomorrow, other countries may have them as well, and even the ability to control these weapons will be beyond human capabilities."

He said, "Morality and ethics demand an end to this race, and practical common sense points in this direction."

He said, "Without a doubt, I believe this can be done. Without a doubt, I believe that the United States and Russia have the power to eliminate this terror that endangers the entire world and casts a shadow on our minds and our future."

### 尼赫鲁呼吁停止核试验  主张和平协商解决国际问题

1957-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁呼吁停止核试验
    主张和平协商解决国际问题
    据新华社新德里28日电　印度总理尼赫鲁今天上午向各大国领导人，特别是美、苏两国领导人呼吁和平解决国际问题。他还呼吁美国和苏联的领导人“停止一切核爆炸试验”，并且“着手进行有效的裁军”。
    尼赫鲁说：“唯一的办法就是和平共处，尽管存在着分歧”。“只有通过和平的方式进行直接的接触，并且取得协议，才能解决这些分歧”。
    尼赫鲁是在新德里举行的一次记者招待会上宣读他的呼吁书的。尼赫鲁指出，“今天，美国和俄国拥有大量核武器，而英国也有。明天，别的国家也可能有，甚至于控制这些武器的能力也将超乎人力所能及的范围以外”。
    他说：“道义和伦理要求停止这种竞赛，而实际的常识更是指出了这一方向。”
    他说：“毫无疑问，我相信这一点是能够做到的。毫无疑问，我认为美国和俄国有力量来消除这个危及整个世界和对我们的心灵和我们的前途投下阴影的恐怖。”
