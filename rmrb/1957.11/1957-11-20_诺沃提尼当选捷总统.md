Title: Novotny Elected President of Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1957-11-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 19th: The Czechoslovak National Assembly held a plenary session at 12:00 noon on the 19th to elect the new President of the Czechoslovak Republic. Antonín Novotný was elected President.

    The presidential election was held in the Vladislav Hall of the Presidential Palace on the 19th, according to tradition.

    After announcing the opening of the meeting, National Assembly Chairman Fierlinger welcomed Soviet Supreme Soviet Presidium Chairman Voroshilov, Polish Council of State Chairman Zawadzki, Albanian People's Assembly Presidium Chairman Lechi, and other foreign representatives who were attending the meeting.

    National Assembly member Široký, representing the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the National Front, nominated Antonín Novotný as a presidential candidate to the National Assembly. He said that it is appropriate for the positions of president and first secretary of the party to be held by the same person at present. Antonín Novotný, through his long-term selfless work in the workers' movement and in his leading positions, has demonstrated his loyalty to the Czechoslovak people, to proletarian internationalism, to the Soviet Union, and to peace and friendship among nations.

    National Assembly members unanimously elected Antonín Novotný as President. After Chairman Fierlinger announced the election results, the entire audience stood up, cheered, and applauded for a long time.

    After the election, the inauguration ceremony of the new President was held. (Attached picture)

### 诺沃提尼当选捷总统

1957-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    诺沃提尼当选捷总统
    新华社布拉格19日电　捷克斯洛伐克国民议会19日中午十二时举行全体会议，选举捷克斯洛伐克共和国新总统。安托宁·诺沃提尼当选总统。
    总统的选举19日按照传统在总统府符拉吉斯拉夫大厅举行。
    国民议会主席费林格在宣布开会以后，对列席会议的苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基、阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席列希以及其他外国代表表示欢迎。
    国民议会议员西罗基代表捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会和民族阵线向国民议会提名安托宁·诺沃提尼为总统候选人。他说，目前，总统的职务和党的第一书记的职务由同一人担任，是适当的。安托宁·诺沃提尼由于在工人阶级运动中和在他的领导职务中长期的无私工作，显示了他对于捷克斯洛伐克人民、对于无产阶级国际主义、对于苏联、对于和平和各国之间的友谊的忠诚。
    国民议会议员一致选举安托宁·诺沃提尼为总统。在主席费林格宣布选举结果以后，全场起立欢呼并长久地鼓掌。
    选举以后，举行了新总统的宣誓就职仪式。（附图片）
