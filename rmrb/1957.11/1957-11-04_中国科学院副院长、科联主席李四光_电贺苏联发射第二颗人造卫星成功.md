Title: Li Siguang, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology, Sends Congratulations on the Successful Launch of the Second Soviet Artificial Satellite
Author:
Date: 1957-11-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: Li Siguang, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology, sent a telegram on the 3rd to Soviet Academy of Sciences President Nesmeyanov, All-Union Trade Union Chairman of Higher Education and Scientific Workers Shutskov, and Chairman of the All-Union Scientific and Technical Association Committee Siruyanov, congratulating them on the successful launch of the second Soviet artificial satellite. The telegram reads:
    As we and the working people of the world jubilantly celebrate the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the successful launch of the second, larger, and heavier Soviet artificial Earth satellite into outer space has filled us with great excitement.
    The successful launch of the first Soviet artificial Earth satellite on October 4th was warmly cheered by people all over the world. This time, the artificial satellite carries more scientific instruments, which will undoubtedly provide more valuable scientific data for the study of outer space and the upper atmosphere. Its successful launch and normal operation powerfully demonstrate that the development of Soviet science and technology has taken the lead in the world.
    The artificial satellite launched this time carries a higher nervous system organism, which is an extremely important event. This new achievement has pushed the cause of scientific and technological research to a new stage, taking humanity a big step forward into a new era of space exploration.
    On behalf of Chinese scientists, I would like to once again express my highest respect and heartfelt congratulations to you and all Soviet scientists and engineers for this new contribution and great achievement in promoting human progress and world peace.

### 中国科学院副院长、科联主席李四光  电贺苏联发射第二颗人造卫星成功

1957-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院副院长、科联主席李四光
    电贺苏联发射第二颗人造卫星成功
    新华社3日讯　中国科学院副院长、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会主席李四光3日致电苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫、全苏高等教育与科学工作者工会主席舒茨可夫、全苏科学技术协会委员会主席西鲁亚诺夫，祝贺苏联发射第二颗人造卫星成功，电文如下：
    正当我们和全世界劳动人民一起欢腾庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的时候，苏联第二颗更大更重的人造地球卫星又成功地飞向宇宙空间，这一重大消息，使我们感到十分兴奋。
    10月4日苏联第一颗人造地球卫星的发射成功，受到全世界人民的热烈欢呼，而这次人造卫星携带了更多的科学仪器，无疑将对宇宙空间和高空大气层的研究获得更多的珍贵科学资料。它的发射成功与运行的正常，有力地证明了苏联科学技术发展已经走在全世界的最前面。
    这次发射的人造卫星携带了高级神经系统的生物，这是一个极为重大的事件，这一新的成就，又为科学技术研究事业推向新的阶段，使人类在进入宇宙空间的新纪元上，又向前迈进了一大步。
    我谨代表中国科学工作者向您和全苏科学家工程师这一促进人类进步与世界和平的新的贡献和伟大成就，再一次地致以崇高的敬意与衷心的祝贺。
