Title: Chen Yi Receives Polish Higher Education Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th — Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received the Polish Higher Education Delegation, headed by S. Żukrowski, Minister of Higher Education of Poland, on the morning of the 26th, along with delegation members: Bukowski, Libera, Wadoś, Bencki, and others.

Present at the meeting were Minister of Higher Education Yang Xiufeng, Vice Minister of Education Liu Shi, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, President of Peking University Ma Yinchu, Vice President of Beijing Institute of Aeronautics Shen Yuan, and others.

Also present were Polish Embassy in China Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Frątczak, Counselor Dąbnicki, and others. (Attached photo)
    Group photo of Vice Premier Chen Yi receiving the Polish Higher Education Delegation. Sixth from the left in the front row is Żukrowski, head of the Polish Higher Education Delegation. Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao.

### 陈毅接见波兰高等教育代表团

1957-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见波兰高等教育代表团
    新华社26日讯　国务院副总理陈毅26日上午接见了波兰高等教育代表团团长、波兰高等教育部部长斯·祖凯夫斯基，代表团团员：布科夫斯基、利倍拉、瓦多什、本茨基等。
    接见时在座的有高等教育部部长杨秀峰，教育部副部长柳湜，文化部副部长夏衍，北京大学校长马寅初，北京航空学院副院长沈元等。
    在座的还有波兰驻华大使馆公使衔参赞傅拉托，参赞邓布尼茨基等。（附图片）
    陈毅副总理接见波兰高等教育代表团时合影。前排左第六人为波兰高等教育代表团团长祖凯夫斯基。新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
