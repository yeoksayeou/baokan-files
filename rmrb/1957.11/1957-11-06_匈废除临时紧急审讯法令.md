Title: Hungary Abolishes Provisional Emergency Trial Decree
Author:
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 4th: The Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic has promulgated a decree deciding to abolish the decree on provisional emergency trials issued last year. Pending cases in the provisional courts will be transferred to formal courts for trial.

### 匈废除临时紧急审讯法令

1957-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    匈废除临时紧急审讯法令
    据新华社布达佩斯4日电　匈牙利人民共和国主席团颁布了一个法令，决定废除去年颁布的关于临时紧急审讯的法令。临时法庭未决案件即移交正式法院审理。
