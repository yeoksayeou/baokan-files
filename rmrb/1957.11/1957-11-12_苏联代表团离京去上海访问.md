Title: Soviet Delegation Leaves Beijing to Visit Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th. The Soviet delegation, led by Andrei Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, and including representatives from all walks of life in the Soviet Union who participated in the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, left Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 11th, accompanied by Liao Chengzhi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Cao Mengjun, Council Member, to visit Shanghai and other places.

Those who saw them off at the station included Li Jishen and Shao Lizi, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Chen Shutong, Bao Erhan, Chu Tunan, Cai Tingkai, Cao Jinghua, Liu Ren, Zhang Youyu, Wang Kunlun, Le Songsheng and other responsible persons from relevant organizations and people from all walks of life.

G.A. Goutikov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, also saw them off at the station.

### 苏联代表团离京去上海访问

1957-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团离京去上海访问
    新华社11日讯　以苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫为首的苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团由中苏友好协会总会副会长廖承志和理事曹孟君陪同，在11日下午乘火车离开北京去上海等地访问。
    到车站送行的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长李济深、邵力子，外交部副部长曾涌泉，以及各有关团体负责人和各界人士陈叔通、包尔汉、楚图南、蔡廷锴、曹靖华、刘仁、张友渔、王昆仑、乐松生等人。
    苏联驻华大使馆临时代办顾悌克夫也到车站送行。
