Title: Socialist Countries' Scientists Meet to Study Further Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-13
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th November: According to TASS, a working conference on Wilson cloud chambers, diffusion cloud chambers, and bubble chambers (instruments used to record and study nuclear reactions), chaired by Chinese scientist Wang Ganchang, opened on the 11th in Dubna, the location of the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research near Moscow.
    Representatives from Bulgaria, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, China, North Korea, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, and Czechoslovakia are participating in the conference. The scientists will discuss the development of methods for conducting research using track chambers, summarize findings, and formulate pathways for further developing experimental techniques in high-energy physics. More than forty reports will be heard at this conference.
    Wang Ganchang, chairman of the organizing committee, said when announcing the opening of the conference that this meeting is another manifestation of the cooperation of scientific forces from many countries around the world to solve the most important problems in modern nuclear physics.
    The successful resolution of these problems will ultimately promote the widespread application of atomic energy to peace, progress, and human prosperity.
    In the morning, the representatives attending the conference also held an informal meeting to establish personal contacts between relevant physicists and engineers.

### 研究进一步和平利用原子核能  社会主义国家科学家开会

1957-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    研究进一步和平利用原子核能
    社会主义国家科学家开会
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社讯：威尔逊云雾室、扩散式云雾室和气泡室（用来记录和研究核反应的仪器）工作会议在中国科学家王淦昌主持下11日在莫斯科市郊联合原子核研究所所在地杜布诺开幕。
    参加这次会议的有保加利亚、民主德国、匈牙利、中国、朝鲜、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联和捷克斯洛伐克的代表。科学家们将讨论利用径迹室进行研究的方法的发展，并且做出总结，拟定进一步发展高能物理学实验技术的途径。这次会议将听取四十多个报告。
    组织委员会主席王淦昌在宣布会议开幕时说，这次会议是世界上许多国家的科学力量为了解决现代原子核物理学中最重大的问题进行合作的又一表现。
    顺利解决这些问题最后必然会促进原子核能被广泛应用于和平、进步和人类繁荣方面。
    上午，出席这次会议的代表还举行了非正式的会见，以便在有关物理学家和工程师之间建立个人接触。
