Title: Soviet Leaders Hold Fruitful Talks with Egyptian Defense Minister
Author:
Date: 1957-11-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 19th: According to TASS, Soviet government leaders held talks with Egyptian President's representative, Defense Minister General Amer, in the Kremlin on the 19th.

The Soviet side participating in the talks included: Khrushchev, member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union; Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Mikoyan, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Gromyko, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; Bierousin, Chairman of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; and Marshal Malinovsky, Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, and others.

The Egyptian side participating in the talks included General Amer, Egyptian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Kouni, and Brigadier General Ismail, Chief of Staff, and others.

The talks were conducted in a spirit of complete understanding and friendship. The two sides discussed issues related to political and economic cooperation between the two countries, as well as military issues.

Both sides expressed satisfaction with the results obtained from the talks.

### 苏联领导人同埃及国防部长  举行会谈结果圆满

1957-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联领导人同埃及国防部长
    举行会谈结果圆满
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府领导人同埃及总统代表、国防部长阿密尔将军19日在克里姆林宫举行了会谈。
    苏联方面参加会谈的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫、苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、苏联部长会议副主席米高扬、苏联外交部长葛罗米柯、苏联部长会议国家对外经济关系委员会主席别尔乌辛和苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅等。
    埃及方面参加会谈的有阿密尔将军、埃及驻苏联大使库尼和参谋长伊斯梅尔准将等人。
    会谈在完全谅解和友好的精神中进行。双方讨论了两国在政治和经济合作方面的问题，也讨论了军事问题。
    双方对会谈所得到的结果表示满意。
