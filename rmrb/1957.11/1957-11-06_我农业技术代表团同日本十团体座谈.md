Title: Our Agricultural Technology Delegation Holds Symposium with Ten Japanese Groups
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The head of the Chinese Agricultural Technology Delegation, Wang Zhen, and all members of the delegation, currently visiting Japan, simultaneously met with leaders of ten Japanese groups including those for peace, Sino-Japanese friendship, and Sino-Japanese trade on the afternoon of the 4th and held a symposium with them.
    In the evening, the Chinese Agricultural Technology Delegation was invited to attend a reception hosted by Kazuo Koeda, Chairman of the Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries Committee of the Japanese Diet. Japanese Minister of Agriculture and Forestry Munenori Akagi, Parliamentary Vice-Ministers of Agriculture and Forestry Mitsuo Setoyama and Takebu Honna, Administrative Vice-Minister of Agriculture and Forestry Tomonosuke Shiomi, and Chairman of the Asian Agricultural Exchange Association Hisayoshi Muramatsu, along with more than twenty others attended the banquet.

### 我农业技术代表团同日本十团体座谈

1957-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    我农业技术代表团同日本十团体座谈
    据新华社5日讯　正在日本进行访问的中国农业技术代表团团长王震和全体团员4日下午同时会见了日本和平、日中友好、日中贸易等十个团体的领导人，并且同他们举行了座谈会。
    晚上，中国农业技术代表团应邀出席了由日本国会农林水产委员会委员长小枝一雄举行的招待宴会。日本政府农林相赤城宗德，农林省政务次官濑户山三男、本名武，事务次官盐见友之助和亚细亚农业交流协会会长村松久义等二十多人出席了宴会。
