Title: President Novotný Reviews Capital Garrison; Premier Široký Hosts Banquet for Foreign Delegations
Author:
Date: 1957-11-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 20th: On November 19th, after the closing of the plenary session of the Czechoslovak National Assembly, a traditional grand military parade was held in the courtyard of Prague Castle, where the new President of the Republic, Novotný, reviewed the Prague Garrison.

Members of the Czech National Assembly and foreign dignitaries who attended the plenary session of the National Assembly that day also participated in the review.

At the beginning of the military parade, President Novotný first listened to a report by Minister of National Defense, General Lomský. Then, as the Commander-in-Chief of the Czechoslovak People's Army, accompanied by members of the National Assembly and dignitaries, he reviewed the Prague Garrison. During the parade, formations of jet fighters flew over Prague Castle.

After the parade, President Novotný, according to tradition, delivered a short speech from the reviewing stand to those who participated in the parade.

On the same day, President Novotný also received leading members of the Czechoslovak government, foreign government delegations, delegations from fraternal Communist parties, foreign diplomatic envoys, and delegations from organizations of the Czechoslovak National Front and worker representatives from various enterprises at Prague Castle.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 20th: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, Premier Široký hosted a banquet on the evening of the 18th at the Czernin Palace for the various national delegations who attended President Zápotocký's funeral.

All foreign delegations, delegations from various Communist and Workers' Parties, and foreign diplomatic envoys attended the reception.

On the Czechoslovak side, leaders of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and government, leaders of the parties and organizations of the National Front, and social figures attended the reception. (Attached image)

### 诺沃提尼总统检阅首都部队  西罗基总理设宴招待各国代表团

1957-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

    诺沃提尼总统检阅首都部队
    西罗基总理设宴招待各国代表团
    新华社布拉格20日电　11月19日，捷克斯洛伐克国民议会全体会议闭幕以后，布拉格宫内的庭院中举行了传统的盛大阅兵式，由共和国新总统诺沃提尼检阅布拉格卫戍部队。
    捷国民议会议员和出席当天国民议会全体会议的外国贵宾们也参加了这次检阅。
    阅兵式开始时，诺沃提尼总统先听取了国防部长洛姆斯基上将的报告。然后以捷克斯洛伐克人民军总司令的身份在国民议会议员和贵宾们的陪同下检阅了布拉格卫戍部队。阅兵时，一队队的喷气式飞机从布拉格宫上空飞过。
    阅兵式结束以后，诺沃提尼总统根据传统在检阅台上向参加阅兵的人们发表了简短的演说。
    同一天，诺沃提尼总统还在布拉格宫接见了捷克斯洛伐克政府的领导人员、外国政府代表团、各兄弟共产党代表团、各国外交使节以及捷克斯洛伐克民族阵线组织的代表团和各企业的工人代表团。
    新华社布拉格20日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，西罗基总理18日晚上在策尔宁宫招待参加萨波托斯基总统葬礼的各国代表团。
    所有外国代表团、各个共产党和工人党代表团以及各国外交使节出席了这个招待会。
    捷克斯洛伐克方面参加招待会的有捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府领导人，民族阵线的各党派、团体的负责人以及社会人士。（附图片）
