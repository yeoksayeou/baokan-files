Title: Chinese Heavy Industry Trade Union Delegation Visits Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-11-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th - A delegation of eight members from the Chinese Heavy Industry Trade Union departed from Beijing on the 12th for a visit to the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Soviet Metallurgical Workers' Union. The head of the delegation is Zhao Guoquan, Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese Heavy Industry Trade Union.

### 中国重工业工会组代表团赴苏访问

1957-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    中国重工业工会组代表团赴苏访问
    新华社12日讯　中国重工业工会访苏代表团一行八人，应苏联冶金工会中央委员会邀请，在12日由北京启程，前往苏联访问。代表团团长是中国重工业工会全国委员会秘书长赵国权。
