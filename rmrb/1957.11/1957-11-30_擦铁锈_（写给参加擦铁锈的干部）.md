Author: Zhang Shizhao
Date: 1957-11-30
Page: 8

    Scrubbing Rust
    (Written for Cadres Participating in Rust Scrubbing)	Henan Hebi No. 1 Mine Zhang Shizhao
    The wire brush rasps against the iron bucket,
    Scrubbing away the rust to make it shine,
    Labor is also a wire brush!
    Scrubbing away your non-working-class thoughts.

### 擦铁锈  （写给参加擦铁锈的干部）

1957-11-30

专栏：

    擦铁锈
    （写给参加擦铁锈的干部）	河南鹤碧一矿　张世照
    铁刷子在铁桶上沙沙响，
    擦干净铁锈放亮光，
    劳动也是一把铁刷子啊！
    刷掉你非工人阶级的思想。
