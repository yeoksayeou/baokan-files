Title: Xincun Agricultural Cooperative Achieves Rice Yield of Over 1,200 Jin Per Mu
Author: Qing Yang
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 4

    Xincun Agricultural Cooperative Achieves Rice Yield of Over 1,200 Jin Per Mu
    (Reported by this newspaper) The Xincun Agricultural Cooperative in Pingxiang County, Jiangxi Province, led by national rice production model Peng Guangxian, achieved another bumper harvest this year. Although the cooperative suffered a severe drought of more than fifty days without rain this year, it fully utilized the superiority of the cooperative system and vigorously built water conservancy projects to overcome the drought. The total of 611 mu of double-cropping rice fields in the cooperative achieved an average yield of 1,222 jin per mu, setting a new record for the entire province. The total rice production increased by 10% compared to last year. Due to the increase in the cooperative's production, the income of the members has also increased. If the income from the side occupations of the members' families is added, the average annual income per person in the cooperative is 105 yuan. All members generally reached the living standards of prosperous middle peasants. (Qing Yang)

### 新村农业社水稻亩产一千二百多斤
庆杨
1957-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    新村农业社水稻亩产一千二百多斤
    本报讯　全国水稻丰产模范彭光贤领导的江西省萍乡县新村农业合作社今年又获得了大丰收。今年虽然遭遇了五十多天没有下雨的严重旱灾，但由于这个合作社充分发挥了合作制度的优越性，大力兴修水利，战胜了旱灾。全社六百一十一亩双季稻田，每亩平均产量一千二百二十二斤，创造了全省最高新纪录。水稻总产量较去年增产10％。由于合作社的生产增加，社员也增加了收入，如果加上社员家庭副业收入，全社每人全年平均收入有一百零五元，全体社员一般都达到富裕中农生活水平。　（庆杨）
