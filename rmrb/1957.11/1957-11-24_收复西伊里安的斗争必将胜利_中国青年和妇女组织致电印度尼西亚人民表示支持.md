Title: The Struggle to Recover West Irian Will Surely Be Victorious: Chinese Youth and Women's Organizations Send Telegrams to the Indonesian People to Express Support

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd: The All-China Youth Federation sent a telegram to the Indonesian Youth Military Cooperation Central Committee on the 23rd, supporting the Indonesian people's struggle to recover West Irian. The telegram reads as follows:

To the Indonesian Youth Military Cooperation Central Committee and to all the youth of Indonesia:

Recently, we have been watching with great excitement the firm and vigorous struggle waged by the Indonesian youth and the people of the whole country to recover West Irian. On behalf of 120 million Chinese youth, we express our full support for your just struggle. West Irian is the territory of the Republic of Indonesia. The imperialists occupied West Irian in order to use it as a base for subversive activities and war preparations. The heroic struggle of the Indonesian people will give a powerful blow to this shameless conspiracy of imperialism, thereby safeguarding Indonesia's sovereignty and territorial integrity and world peace. Your struggle will inevitably receive the widespread support of the youth of Asian and African countries and all peace-loving youth in the world. Chinese youth firmly support all actions conducive to the Indonesian people's recovery of West Irian. Dear friends, unity is strength! The great unity of the Indonesian people will surely drive the colonialists out of West Irian!

		All-China Youth Federation
			November 23, 1957

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd: The All-China Women's Federation sent a telegram to the Indonesian Women's Congress on the 23rd, supporting the Indonesian people's struggle to recover West Irian. The telegram reads as follows:

To the Indonesian Women's Congress

Indonesian Sisters:

Chinese women fully support the Indonesian people's just struggle to recover West Irian. West Irian is the sacred and inalienable territory of Indonesia. Your struggle is not only to oppose colonialism and safeguard the integrity of national territory and sovereignty, but also a part of defending peace in Asia and the world. We believe that the colonialists will surely be defeated, and your struggle will surely achieve final victory.

		All-China Women's Federation
			November 23, 1957

### 收复西伊里安的斗争必将胜利  中国青年和妇女组织致电印度尼西亚人民表示支持

1957-11-24
第6版()
专栏：

    收复西伊里安的斗争必将胜利
    中国青年和妇女组织致电印度尼西亚人民表示支持
    新华社23日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会23日致电印度尼西亚青年军事合作中央委员会，支持印度尼西亚人民收复西伊里安的斗争。电文如下：
    印度尼西亚青年军事合作中央委员会并转全印度尼西亚的青年们：
    最近，我们以极大兴奋的心情注视着印度尼西亚青年和全国人民一道为收复西伊里安所进行的坚定而有力的斗争。我们代表一亿二千万中国青年对你们的正义斗争表示全力支持。西伊里安是印度尼西亚共和国的领土，帝国主义窃据西伊里安是为了要把它作为进行颠覆活动和战争准备的基地。印度尼西亚人民的英勇斗争将给帝国主义这一无耻的阴谋以有力的打击，从而维护印度尼西亚的主权完整和世界和平。你们的斗争必然会得到亚非各国青年和全世界一切热爱和平的青年的广泛支持。中国青年坚决支持一切有利于印度尼西亚人民收复西伊里安的行动。亲爱的朋友们，团结就是力量，印度尼西亚人民的大团结，是一定能把殖民主义者赶出西伊里安去！
          中华全国民主青年联合会
                      1957年11月23日
    新华社23日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会23日致电印度尼西亚妇女大会，支持印度尼西亚人民收复西伊里安的斗争。电文如下：
    印度尼西亚妇女大会转
    印度尼西亚姊妹们：
    中国妇女全力支持印度尼西亚人民收复西伊里安的正义斗争。西伊里安是印度尼西亚神圣不可分割的领土。你们的斗争不仅为了反对殖民主义、维护国家领土和主权的完整，同时也是保卫亚洲和世界和平的一部分。我们相信，殖民主义者一定要失败，你们的斗争必将取得最后的胜利。
    中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会
                      1957年11月23日
