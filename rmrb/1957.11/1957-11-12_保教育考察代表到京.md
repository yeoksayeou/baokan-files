Title: Bulgarian Education Inspection Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 10th – Ivan Koleff Traganoff, head of the Secondary Education Division of the Ministry of Education and Culture of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and a member of the Bulgarian People's Republic education inspection delegation, arrived in Beijing by plane at 3:25 p.m. on the 9th of this month. He is visiting to inspect the state of national education in accordance with the 1957 implementation plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement; he will stay in our country for six to eight weeks.
    Hou Junyan, Director of the Secondary Education Department of the Ministry of Education, and others welcomed him at the airport.

### 保教育考察代表到京

1957-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    保教育考察代表到京
    新华社10日讯　保加利亚人民共和国教育考察代表、保加利亚人民共和国教育与文化部中等教育处处长伊万·科莱夫·特拉干诺夫于本月9日下午三时二十五分乘飞机抵北京。他是根据中保文化合作协定1957年执行计划前来考察国民教育情况的；将在我国停留六至八周。
    教育部中学教育司司长侯俊岩等到机场欢迎。
