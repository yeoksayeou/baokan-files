Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution: The Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Meets Today; Grand Parade and Procession to be Held in Moscow Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th, Moscow Dispatch: According to TASS, Moscow, November 5th: The joint session of the Union Council and the National Council of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, celebrating the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, will open at 10:00 AM on November 6th at the Luzhniki Stadium.

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th, Moscow Dispatch: According to TASS, Moscow, November 4th: On November 7th, the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, a military parade and mass procession will be held on Red Square in Moscow. The Moscow garrison will be reviewed by Marshal Malinovsky, Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union. The commander of the parade will be Marshal Moskarenko, commander of the Moscow Military District. After the parade, a grand procession of the working people of Moscow will take place.

### 庆祝十月革命四十周年  苏联最高苏维埃今日开会  莫斯科明日举行盛大阅兵和游行

1957-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月革命四十周年
    苏联最高苏维埃今日开会
    莫斯科明日举行盛大阅兵和游行
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科5日讯：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院庆祝十月革命四十周年的联席会议，将于11月6日上午十时在卢日尼基体育宫开幕。
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科4日讯：在11月7日——十月革命四十周年这一天，莫斯科红场上将举行阅兵式和群众游行。莫斯科卫戍部队将接受苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅的检阅。阅兵指挥是莫斯科军区司令莫斯卡连科元帅。阅兵后将举行莫斯科劳动人民大游行。
