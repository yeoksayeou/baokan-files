Title: Chinese Red Cross Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th—The head of the Chinese Red Cross delegation, Li Dequan, delegation members, and some members of the government delegation who attended the 19th International Red Cross Conference returned to Beijing on the 14th.

### 我国红十字会代表团回到北京

1957-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    我国红十字会代表团回到北京
    新华社15日讯　出席十九届国际红十字大会的我国红十字会代表团团长李德全和团员以及政府代表团部分团员，已经在14日回到北京。
