Title: Two Peaceful Satellites Continue to Fly as Usual; The Launch Vehicle of the First Satellite Will Burn Up Next Month

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th - TASS reports from Moscow: As of 6:00 AM Moscow time on November 26th, the second artificial satellite had orbited the Earth 320 times, while the first artificial satellite and its launch vehicle had orbited the Earth 790 times and 798 times respectively.

In clear weather, the second artificial Earth satellite can be seen with the naked eye before sunrise in areas between 58 and 64 degrees north latitude, and after sunset in areas between 27 and 57 degrees south latitude.

The launch vehicle of the first satellite can be seen after sunset in areas between 0 and 60 degrees north latitude, and before sunrise in areas between 0 and 65 degrees south latitude.

At 5:11 PM Moscow time on November 26th, the launch vehicle of the first satellite will fly over the area west of Moscow from northwest to southeast.

According to measurements and calculations, the launch vehicle of the first satellite will enter the Earth's atmosphere with extremely high density and burn up in early December.

### 两颗和平卫星飞行照常  第一个卫星的运载火箭下月将陨灭

1957-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    两颗和平卫星飞行照常
    第一个卫星的运载火箭下月将陨灭
    据新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：到11月26日莫斯科时间六点正为止，第二个人造卫星已经绕地球转了三百二十圈，第一个人造卫星及其运载火箭则已经分别绕地球转了七百九十圈和七百九十八圈。
    天气晴朗的时候，北纬五十八度到六十四度之间地区在日出之前，南纬二十七度到五十七度之间地区在日落之后，可以用肉眼看见第二个人造地球卫星。
    北纬○度到六十度之间地区在日落之后，南纬○度到六十五度之间地区在日出之前可以看见第一个卫星的运载火箭。
    11月26日莫斯科时间十七点十一分第一个卫星的运载火箭将从西北向东南飞过莫斯科以西地区上空。
    据测量和计算表明，第一个卫星的运载火箭将在12月上旬进入密度极大的地球大气层而陨灭。
