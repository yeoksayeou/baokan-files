Title: North Atlantic Group Decides to Hold "Top-Level" Meeting as Scheduled
Author:
Date: 1957-11-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, November 28th: The North Atlantic Group's Council of Permanent Representatives met here today and decided that the group's "top-level" meeting will be held in Paris on December 16 as originally planned.

### 北大西洋集团决定  如期举行“最高级”会议

1957-11-29
第6版()
专栏：

    北大西洋集团决定
    如期举行“最高级”会议
    据新华社28日讯　巴黎消息：北大西洋集团常任代表理事会今天在这里开会，决定这个集团的“最高级”会议将按原定计划，于12月16日在巴黎召开。
