Title: The Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions is About to be Held; ACFTU Executive Committee Meeting Discusses Relevant Matters
Author:
Date: 1957-11-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 29th dispatch: The Seventh Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) held its Sixth Plenary Session (expanded) this afternoon to discuss relevant matters concerning the upcoming Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. More than 100 people attended the meeting, including the members of the 7th Executive Committee, alternate members of the 7th Executive Committee, and heads of various departments of the ACFTU and various industrial trade unions who attended as observers. The meeting discussed and principally approved the reports and documents to be submitted to the Eighth National Congress of Trade Unions for discussion, namely the report on the work of the trade unions, the Constitution of the Chinese Trade Unions (draft), the report on the revision of the Constitution of the Chinese Trade Unions, and the report on the financial work of the trade unions. The meeting also elected Lai Ruoyu, Xu Zhizhen, and Li Zaiwen to represent the Seventh Executive Committee in making the above reports to the Eighth National Congress.

The meeting also approved the proposed list of the Presidium of the Congress, the list of the Credentials Committee of the Congress, the list of the Proposals Examination Committee of the Congress, and the list of the Secretary-General and Deputy Secretaries-General of the Congress, which will be recommended to the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. The meeting discussed and approved the draft agenda of the Congress.

The Seventh Financial Review Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions also held a meeting this afternoon to discuss and approve the report on the financial review of trade union funds to be submitted to the Congress for discussion.

### 中国工会第八次全国代表大会即将举行  全总执委会会议讨论有关事项

1957-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中国工会第八次全国代表大会即将举行
    全总执委会会议讨论有关事项
    新华社29日讯　中华全国总工会第七届执行委员会今天下午举行第六次全体会议（扩大），讨论了即将举行的中国工会第八次全国代表大会的有关事项。出席会议的七届执行委员、七届候补执行委员，以及列席会议的全总各部门和各产业工会负责人，共一百多人。会议讨论并原则通过了即将提交工会第八次全国代表大会讨论的报告和文件，这就是关于工会工作报告、中国工会章程（草案）和关于修改中国工会章程的报告、关于工会财务工作的报告。会议并推选出赖若愚、许之桢、栗再温代表七届执行委员会分别向第八次全国代表大会作上述报告。
    会议还通过了向中国工会第八次全国代表大会建议的大会主席团名单、大会代表资格审查委员会名单、大会提案审查委员会名单和大会秘书长、副秘书长名单。会议讨论并通过了大会会议日程（草案）。
    中国工会第七届经费审查委员会也在今天下午举行会议，讨论并通过了提交大会讨论的关于工会经费审查的报告。
