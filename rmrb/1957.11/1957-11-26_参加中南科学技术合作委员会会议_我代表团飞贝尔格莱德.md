Title: Delegation Flies to Belgrade to Attend Sino-Yugoslav Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 25th. The Chinese Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation, consisting of six people (one of whom is already in Moscow), led by Shen Hong, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Yugoslav Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, flew to Belgrade today to attend the second session of the Committee.

### 参加中南科学技术合作委员会会议  我代表团飞贝尔格莱德

1957-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    参加中南科学技术合作委员会会议
    我代表团飞贝尔格莱德
    新华社25日讯　参加中南科学技术合作委员会第二届会议的中国科学技术合作代表团一行六人（其中一人已在莫斯科），在委员会中国组主席沈鸿率领下，今天乘飞机前往贝尔格莱德。
