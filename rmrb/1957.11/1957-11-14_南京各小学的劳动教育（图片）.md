Title: Labor Education in Nanjing Elementary Schools (Photos)
Author: Xu Bihua
Date: 1957-11-14
Page: 8

    Nanjing Elementary Schools' Labor Education
    Recently, elementary schools in Nanjing have begun regularly leading children to the countryside for labor, to educate them to love agricultural production and the new socialist countryside.

    The children are preparing to plant vegetables on this wasteland.

    Why can corn grow so big? - Old Wang Yunjun, a senior member of the Jinhua Agricultural Cooperative, is answering this question for the children.
             Xinhua News Agency Reporter Xu Bihua

### 南京各小学的劳动教育（图片）
许必华
1957-11-14
第8版()
专栏：

    南京各小学的劳动教育
    南京市各小学最近开始定期地带领儿童到农村进行劳动，以教育儿童们热爱农业生产，热爱社会主义的新农村。
    孩子们准备在这块荒地上种蔬菜。
    玉米为什么能长这么大呢？——近华农业社的老社员王允俊老爷爷正在给孩子们解答这个疑问。
              新华社记者　许必华摄
