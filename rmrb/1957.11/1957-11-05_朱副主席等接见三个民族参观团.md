Title: Vice-Chairman Zhu and Others Receive Three Ethnic Minority Visiting Groups
Author:
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Vice-Chairman Zhu De, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, Vice-Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Bao Erhan received representatives of various ethnic groups from the Tibet Visiting Group, the Gansu Province Ethnic Minority Visiting Group, and the Heilongjiang Province Ethnic Minority Visiting Group on the afternoon of the 4th.
    During the reception, Tibetan representatives from the visiting groups presented Hada scarves to Vice-Chairman Zhu De and others, who in turn presented Hada scarves to them. Premier Zhou and others received the Egyptian Cultural Delegation.

### 朱副主席等接见三个民族参观团

1957-11-05
第2版()
专栏：

    朱副主席等接见三个民族参观团
    新华社4日讯　朱德副主席，李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、陈叔通副委员长，贺龙、陈毅副总理和人民政协副主席包尔汉，在4日下午接见了西藏参观团、甘肃省各民族参观团和黑龙江省各民族参观团的各兄弟民族的代表。
    在接见的时候，参观团中的藏族代表向朱德副主席等献了哈达，朱德副主席等向他们回敬了哈达。周总理等接见埃及文化代表团
