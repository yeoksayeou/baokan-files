Title: Turkish Troops Massing Near Syrian Border
Author:
Date: 1957-11-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, November 15th (TASS): According to the "Al-Manar" newspaper, a large number of Turkish troops, equipped with nearly 300 aircraft and 500 tanks, are massing in the Isliahie region (near the Syrian border).

Nearly 10,000 Turkish troops are massing in the Kunaitra region.

### 在叙利亚边境附近  土耳其集结大批军队

1957-11-17
第6版()
专栏：

    在叙利亚边境附近
    土耳其集结大批军队
    新华社16日讯  塔斯社大马士革15日讯：据“灯塔报”报道，在伊斯利亚谢（在叙利亚边境附近）地区集结着大批土耳其军队，配备有将近三百架飞机和五百辆坦克。
    在孔塔地区集结着将近一万名土耳其军队。
