Title: Shanxi's Large-Area Demonstration Cotton Fields Achieve New Record Harvest

Author:

Date: 1957-11-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, November 9th: Shanxi Province's large-area high-yield cotton fields cultivated this year have achieved many new records in high yields. Among them, 650,000 mu of large-area cotton demonstration fields in the Jinnan and Jinzhong plains achieved an average yield of over 63 jin of ginned cotton per mu, an increase of 20% to 100% compared to the unit yield of ordinary local cotton fields. Agricultural cooperatives led by national famous cotton planting models Qu Yaoli and Wu Chunan, achieved a bumper cotton harvest after overcoming various natural disasters. The Xianfeng Agricultural Cooperative in Yicheng County, where Wu Chunan is located, has over 1,100 mu of cotton fields, with an average yield of over 93 jin of ginned cotton per mu. The Youyi Agricultural Cooperative in Anyi County has 50 mu of cotton fields, yielding 812 jin of seed cotton per mu (three jin of seed cotton equals one jin of ginned cotton).

### 山西大面积示范棉田  获得丰产新纪录

1957-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

    山西大面积示范棉田
    获得丰产新纪录
    据新华社太原9日讯　山西省今年培植的大面积丰产棉田，有许多获得高额丰产新纪录。其中，晋南、晋中平原上的六十五万亩大面积增产示范棉田，每亩平均产皮棉六十三斤多，比当地一般棉田的单位产量增加20％到百分之一百。全国著名植棉模范曲耀离、吴春安领导的农业社，在战胜了各种自然灾害后棉花获得了丰收。吴春安所在的翼城县先锋农业社一千一百多亩棉田，每亩平均产皮棉九十三斤以上。安邑县友谊农业社有五十亩棉田，每亩产籽棉八百一十二斤（三斤籽棉合一斤皮棉）。
