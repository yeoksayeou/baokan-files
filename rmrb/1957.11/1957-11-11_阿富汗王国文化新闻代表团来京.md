Title: Afghanistan Kingdom Cultural and News Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, 9th - The four-member Cultural and News Delegation from the Kingdom of Afghanistan, which came to visit the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, left Urumqi for Beijing on the 8th.

The guests arrived in Urumqi on the 5th. They visited some factories, schools, and water conservancy projects, and visited farmers in the suburbs and urban residents of Uyghur, Kazakh, Uzbek, and other ethnic groups. The delegation also introduced the production and construction and the people's lives of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to some cultural and news workers in the autonomous region through color documentaries they brought with them.

Before the delegation left, the Cultural Department of the Autonomous Region hosted a banquet for the guests.

### 阿富汗王国文化新闻代表团来京

1957-11-11
第2版()
专栏：

    阿富汗王国文化新闻代表团来京
    新华社乌鲁木齐9日电　前来新疆维吾尔自治区参观访问的阿富汗王国文化新闻代表团一行四人，8日离开乌鲁木齐去北京。
    客人们是在5日到乌鲁木齐的。他们在这里曾参观了一些工厂、学校和水利工程，访问了郊区农民和维吾尔、哈萨克、乌孜别克等族城市居民。代表团还通过带来的彩色纪录片，向自治区一些文化和新闻工作者介绍了阿富汗王国的生产建设和人民生活的概况。
    代表团临行前，自治区文化厅曾设宴招待客人。
