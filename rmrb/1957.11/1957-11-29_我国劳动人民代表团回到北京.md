Title: Chinese Labor Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The Chinese Labor Delegation, after attending the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union, returned to Beijing from Moscow by jet plane on the afternoon of the 28th, led by Liu Ningyi, the head of the delegation, and Qian Junrui, Xu Guangping, Lao She, and Mei Lanfang, the deputy heads of the delegation.
    Deputy head Qian Sanqiang and some members of the delegation are still in Moscow for other business and will return to China successively.
    Among those who welcomed them at the airport were Li Dequan, Kang Keqing, Zhao Yimin, Dong Xin, Xia Yan, and other heads of organizations such as the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Communist Youth League of China, the All-China Women's Federation, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, as well as literary, artistic, and scientific groups in the capital.
    Also welcoming them at the airport were Soviet Embassy in China Counselor Gutikov, Sudarikov, Wei Lixiajin and other embassy staff.

### 我国劳动人民代表团回到北京

1957-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我国劳动人民代表团回到北京
    新华社28日讯　中国劳动人民代表团到苏联参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的庆祝典礼以后，由团长刘宁一，副团长钱俊瑞、许广平、老舍、梅兰芳率领，乘喷气式飞机在28日下午从莫斯科回到北京。
    副团长钱三强和部分团员因事现仍在莫斯科，将陆续回国。
    到机场欢迎的有，中苏友好协会总会、中华全国总工会、中国共产主义青年团、全国妇女联合会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民对外文化协会，以及首都文学、艺术、科学团体的负责人李德全、康克清、赵毅敏、董昕、夏衍等。
    到机场欢迎的还有，苏联驻华大使馆参赞顾悌克夫、苏达利科夫、魏立夏金等使馆工作人员。
