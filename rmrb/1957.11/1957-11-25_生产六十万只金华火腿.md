Title: Production of 600,000 JinHua Hams
Author: Fang Ruyu
Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 3

　　Production of 600,000 JinHua Hams

　　The 1958 production year for the famous "JinHua Ham," known at home and abroad, has already begun. It is estimated that the output in 1958 will reach 600,000 hams, an increase of nearly one-third compared to this year.

　　In recent years, the quality of JinHua Ham has significantly improved. The percentage of qualified hams has gradually increased from 81.43% in 1952 to 97.36% in 1957.

　　Currently, JinHua Ham is not only selling well in major cities across the country, but is also exported to the Soviet Union and other People's Democratic countries, as well as Southeast Asian countries and countries like England and France.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Fang Ruyu)

### 生产六十万只金华火腿
方如玉
1957-11-25
第3版()
专栏：

    生产六十万只金华火腿
    驰名国内外的“金华火腿”1958年生产年度已经开始。预计1958年产量可达六十万只，比今年增产近三分之一。
    近几年来，金华火腿的质量有了显著的提高，火腿正品率已由1952年的81．43％，逐步提高到1957年的97．36％。
    目前，金华火腿不仅畅销国内各大城市，还远销苏联和各人民民主国家，以及东南亚各国和英、法等国。
                          （方如玉）
