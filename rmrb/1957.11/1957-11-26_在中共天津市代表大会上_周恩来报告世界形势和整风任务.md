Title: At the CCP Tianjin Municipal Congress, Zhou Enlai Reports on the World Situation and Rectification Tasks
Author:
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 25th. Comrade Zhou Enlai, Vice-Chairman of the CCP Central Committee, delivered a report titled "The World Situation and Our Rectification Tasks" to more than two thousand delegates and observers attending the Second Congress of the CCP Tianjin Municipal Committee at its second meeting today.

The second meeting of the Second Congress of the CCP Tianjin Municipal Committee opened on November 23rd. On the opening day, Huang Huoqing, First Secretary of the CCP Tianjin Municipal Committee, delivered a "Report on the Rectification Movement."

Comrade Zhou Enlai arrived in Tianjin yesterday. Yesterday afternoon, he convened the heads of seven factories and enterprises to solicit opinions on four draft documents, including the State Council's provisional regulations on the handling of retirement for workers and staff.

### 在中共天津市代表大会上  周恩来报告世界形势和整风任务

1957-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    在中共天津市代表大会上
    周恩来报告世界形势和整风任务
    新华社天津25日电　中共中央副主席周恩来同志25日在中共天津市第二届代表大会第二次会议上向两千多名出席会议的代表和列席人员等作了“世界形势和我们整风任务”的报告。
    中共天津市第二届代表大会第二次会议是11月23日开幕的。在开幕的当天，中共天津市委第一书记黄火青作了“关于整风运动的报告”。
    周恩来同志是昨天到达天津的，昨天下午他曾召集七个工厂企业单位的负责人，就国务院关于工人、职员退休处理的暂行规定等四个草案征求意见。
