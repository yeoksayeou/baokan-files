Title: Thirteen Socialist Countries Co-Produce Film “The Light of October” to be Released Soon
Author:
Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 24th: According to TASS, the color documentary "The Light of October," co-produced by filmmakers from thirteen countries, is about to be released in the Soviet Union. The filmmakers participating in the production of this color documentary are from Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, Vietnam, the German Democratic Republic, China, North Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, and Yugoslavia.

Filming for this movie began in February of this year. The theme of the film is to reflect the international significance of the October Revolution and the role it played in the history of various countries.

The first part of the film – the revolutionary history part – is composed of authentic materials preserved in the film archives of various countries. Some of these materials are being brought to the screen for the first time. From this part of the film, the audience will see the Russian October Revolution, the German Revolution of 1918, the establishment of the Hungarian Republic, the development of revolutionary movements in China and Bulgaria, the events in Spain in 1937, and other situations.

The second part of the film reflects the arrangement of peaceful life after the war, the achievements of the People's Democratic countries in industry, agriculture, science and culture, and the struggle of the people of all countries for peace and friendship.

### 十三个社会主义国家合拍影片  “十月的光芒”即将上映

1957-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    十三个社会主义国家合拍影片
    “十月的光芒”即将上映
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：十三个国家的电影工作者合拍的彩色纪录影片“十月的光芒”即将在苏联上映。参加拍摄这部彩色纪录片的有阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、越南、民主德国、中国、朝鲜、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联、捷克斯洛伐克和南斯拉夫的电影工作者。
    这部影片是从今年2月开始拍摄的。影片的主题是反映十月革命的国际意义和它在各国历史上所起的作用。
    影片的第一部分——革命史部分是根据各国电影档案库中保存的真实材料组成的。其中有些材料是首次搬上银幕的。观众将从影片的这一部分看到俄国十月革命、1918年德国革命、匈牙利共和国的建立、中国和保加利亚革命运动的发展、1937年西班牙事件等情况。
    影片的第二部分反映了战后和平生活的安排、人民民主国家在工业、农业、科学和文化方面的成就、各国人民争取和平和友谊的斗争。
