Title: Chen Yi Receives British Pathologist Willis
Author:
Date: 1957-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received Professor Willis, a renowned British pathologist, and his wife, who are currently visiting China, on the 20th.

Present at the meeting were Minister of Public Health Li Dequan, Acting President of the Chinese Medical Association Cui Yitian, and Professor of Pathology Hu Zhengxiang.

### 陈毅接见英国病理学家韦利斯

1957-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见英国病理学家韦利斯
    新华社20日讯　国务院副总理陈毅20日接见了正在我国访问的英国著名病理学家韦利斯教授和他的夫人。
    接见时在座的有卫生部部长李德全，中华医学会代会长崔义田，病理学家胡正详教授。
