Title: Vietnam and North Korea Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 21st. The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea signed their first cultural cooperation agreement in Hanoi on the 20th.

According to this agreement, both sides will further strengthen cooperation in science, education, literature, art, film, radio, mass culture, etc., and achieve the purpose of cultural exchange by exchanging delegations, students and interns, exchanging books, photographs, films and various materials.

Representing their respective governments in signing the agreement were Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Culture of Vietnam, and Seo Cheol, Ambassador of North Korea to Vietnam.

### 越朝签订文化合作协定

1957-11-22
第6版()
专栏：

    越朝签订文化合作协定
    新华社河内21日电　越南民主共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府20日在河内签订了第一个文化合作协定。
    根据这项协定，双方将进一步加强科学、教育、文学、艺术、电影、广播、群众文化等方面的合作，并且通过互派代表团、留学生和实习生，交换书籍、照片、影片以及各种材料等方式，达到文化交流的目的。
    代表双方政府参加签字的是越南文化部部长黄明鉴和朝鲜驻越南大使徐哲。
