Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Issues Communiqué, Voices Support for the Struggle of the Indonesian People
Author:
Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 21st. The World Federation of Trade Unions issued a communiqué on the 20th, voicing support for the demand of the Indonesian people that Dutch troops withdraw from West Irian.

The World Federation of Trade Unions' communiqué stated that the Secretariat of the WFTU, in a telegram to the President of the United Nations General Assembly on November 16, said: "The World Federation of Trade Unions welcomes the resolution on the West Irian issue, unanimously adopted at the Fourth World Trade Union Congress by representatives of 105 million workers of different faiths and belonging to different trade unions. It requests the 12th session of the General Assembly to recognize, in accordance with the principles of the United Nations Charter, the legitimate demand of the Indonesian people to liberate and recover the territory of West Irian occupied by Dutch imperialists."

The Secretary of the WFTU, Gras, wrote to the Committee for the Liberation of West Irian on the 19th, expressing the WFTU's support for the just struggle of the Indonesian people and government. The letter stated that the Indonesian people's struggle will certainly receive the unconditional support of the WFTU and the support of workers around the world and justice-loving people of all countries.

### 世界工联发表公报  声援印度尼西亚人民的斗争

1957-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    世界工联发表公报
    声援印度尼西亚人民的斗争
    新华社布拉格21日电　世界工会联合会20日发表公报，声援印度尼西亚人民要荷兰军队撤出西伊里安的要求。
    世界工联的公报中写道，世界工联书记处在11月16日致电联合国大会主席说：“世界工会联合会欢迎代表着一亿零五百万具有不同信仰和属于不同工会的劳动人民的代表，在世界工会第四次代表大会上所一致通过的关于西伊里安问题的决议。它要求联大第十二届会议根据联合国宪章的原则，承认印度尼西亚人民解放和收回被荷兰帝国主义者侵占的西伊里安领土的合法要求。”
    世界工联书记葛拉西在19日写信给解放西伊里安委员会，表示世界工联声援印度尼西亚人民和政府的正义斗争。信中说，印度尼西亚人民的斗争一定会得到世界工联的无条件的声援和全世界工人以及各国维护正义的人民的支持。
