Title: Lunar Eclipse to Occur on the Evening of the 7th
Author:
Date: 1957-11-03
Page: 8

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 2nd. The Zijinshan Astronomical Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences announced a lunar eclipse message saying: A total lunar eclipse will occur on the evening of the 7th. Beijing time for the first contact (start of the partial eclipse) is 20:43, totality (start of the total eclipse) is 22:11, greatest eclipse (the height of the total eclipse) is 22:27, the end of totality (end of the total eclipse) is 22:42, and the end of the partial eclipse (end of the partial eclipse) is 0:10 on the 8th. The total duration of this lunar eclipse is 3 hours and 27 minutes, with a total eclipse of 31 minutes. If there are no clouds on that day, the eclipse will be visible across China.

### 七日晚将发生月全蚀

1957-11-03
第8版()
专栏：

    七日晚将发生月全蚀
    新华社南京2日电　中国科学院紫金山天文台发布月食消息说：7日晚将发生月全食，北京时间初亏（偏食开始）是二十点四十三分，食既（全食开始）是二十二点十一分，食甚（全食最甚的时候）是二十二点二十七分，生光（全食终了）是二十二点四十二分，复圆（偏食终了）是在8日零时十分。这次月食共历时三点二十七分，全食有三十一分钟。我国各地当天如无阴云，都可看到这次月食。
