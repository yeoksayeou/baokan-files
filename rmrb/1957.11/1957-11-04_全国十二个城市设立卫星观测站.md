Title: Twelve Cities Nationwide Establish Satellite Observation Stations
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd -- The Chinese Academy of Sciences is setting up observation stations in twelve locations across the country to observe Soviet artificial satellites. The Soviet Union will soon supply China with one hundred and twenty wide-field (11 degrees) astronomical telescopes for observing artificial satellites. Powerful receivers capable of receiving signals from 20 MHz to 40 MHz have begun to be transported to various observation stations. Each observation station is equipped with specialized scientific and technical personnel.

The locations of these twelve observation stations are: Beijing, Nanjing, Lanzhou, Kunming, Lhasa, Wuhan, Changchun, Guangzhou, Xi'an, Urumqi, Tianjin, and Shanghai.

### 全国十二个城市设立卫星观测站

1957-11-04
第2版()
专栏：

    全国十二个城市设立卫星观测站
    新华社3日讯　中国科学院正在全国十二个地点布置观测苏联人造卫星的观测站。苏联即将供给我国一百二十架宽视野（十一度）的天文望远镜作为观察人造卫星之用。能够收听二十兆赫到四十兆赫的强力收报机已经开始运往各地观测站。各观测站配备了专门观测的科学技术人员。
    这十二个观测站的所在地是：北京、南京、兰州、昆明、拉萨、武汉、长春、广州、西安、乌鲁木齐、天津和上海。
