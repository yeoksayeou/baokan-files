Title: Premier Zhou Receives Chief Editor of "Süddeutsche Zeitung"
Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th - Premier Zhou Enlai received Josef Kapschky, chief editor of the Federal Republic of Germany's "Süddeutsche Zeitung," who is currently visiting our country, on the afternoon of the 14th.

Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the reception.

### 周总理接见“南德日报”主笔

1957-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见“南德日报”主笔
    新华社14日讯　周恩来总理14日下午接见了正在我国访问的德意志联邦共和国“南德日报”主笔凯普斯基。
    接见时在座的，有外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
