Title: Waste Gas Turns into Light Oil
Author:
Date: 1957-11-12
Page: 3

Construction Dynamics

	Waste Gas Turns into Light Oil
	The light oil recovery workshop built by Fushun Petroleum Factory No. 1 and No. 2 was fully put into production on November 2nd.
	This workshop was built to recover light oil from waste gas produced during the refining of crude oil from oil shale, and can recover more than 70,000 tons of light oil annually. This oil can be turned into high-grade gasoline after simple processing. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 废气变成轻质油

1957-11-12
第3版()
专栏：建设动态

    废气变成轻质油
    抚顺石油一、二厂兴建的轻质油回收车间，到11月2日已经全部投入生产。
    这个车间是为从油母页岩炼制原油时所产生的废瓦斯气体中回收轻质油而兴建的，每年约可回收七万多吨轻质油。这种油经过简单加工后就可以变成高级汽油。（据新华社讯）
