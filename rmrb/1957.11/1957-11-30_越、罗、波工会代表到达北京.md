Title: Vietnamese, Romanian, and Polish Trade Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th - Hoang Quoc Viet, President of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, the Romanian trade union delegation headed by Bascu Nicolae, Secretary of the Central Council of the Romanian Trade Unions, and Polish trade union representative Bolesław Gebert, who were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, arrived in Beijing today.
　　
Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat, and others went to welcome them.

### 越、罗、波工会代表到达北京

1957-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    越、罗、波工会代表到达北京
    新华社29日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请前来参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会的越南总工会主席黄国越、以罗马尼亚工会中央理事会书记巴斯库·尼古拉为首的罗马尼亚工会代表团和波兰工会代表波莱斯拉夫·吉伯特，今天分别到达北京。
    前往欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜、书记处书记董昕等人。
