Title: Chen Yi Receives Japanese Socialist Party Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received the Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Delegation leader Yoshio Suzuki and the delegation members on the morning of the 25th. Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, Liao Chengzhi, Qiao Guanhua, Wu Maosun, Xie Nanguang, Zhao Anbo, and Xiao Xiangqian.

### 陈毅接见日本社会党使节团

1957-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见日本社会党使节团
    新华社25日讯　国务院陈毅副总理在25日上午接见了日本社会党亲善使节团团长铃木义男和使节团团员。接见时在座的，有张奚若、廖承志、乔冠华、吴茂荪、谢南光、赵安博和萧向前。
