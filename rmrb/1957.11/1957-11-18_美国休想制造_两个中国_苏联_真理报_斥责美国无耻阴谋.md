Title: The U.S. Will Never Succeed in Creating "Two Chinas" - Soviet "Pravda" Denounces Shameless U.S. Conspiracy
Author:
Date: 1957-11-18
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, November 17th - The Soviet newspaper "Pravda" published an article on the 16th, attacking the U.S. conspiracy to create "two Chinas." The article said that the United States stubbornly refuses to recognize the existence of the People's Republic of China, which accounts for a quarter of the world's population. When the situation forces it to consider this reality, it deliberately concocts the conspiracy of "two Chinas," attempting to say that the People's Republic of China is not the only China in the world, and that the Chiang Kai-shek clique, eking out a living on the island of Taiwan, is the so-called "Republic of China."

The article said that at the International Red Cross Conference held recently in New Delhi, the Americans, as usual, made a geographical "discovery." The U.S. delegation to the conference pressured its Western lackey countries and actually passed a proposal to invite Chiang Kai-shek elements to participate in the conference, which was about to close. This act of undermining the spirit of international cooperation aroused the reasonable indignation of the conference participants and the general public. The delegation of the People's Republic of China protested against this political provocation by the United States and immediately withdrew from the meeting. The Soviet Union, the people's democratic countries, India, Indonesia, Syria, and Egypt supported the Chinese delegation.

The article pointed out that the growing international prestige of the People's Republic of China makes certain Americans resentful. Their nonsense about "two Chinas" aims to maintain and prolong the occupation of Taiwan, the territory of the Chinese people since ancient times. Soviet public opinion firmly supports the position of the People's Republic of China. All forces defending peace cannot but condemn this shameless U.S. conspiracy against the sovereignty and territorial integrity of great China.

### 美国休想制造“两个中国”  苏联“真理报”斥责美国无耻阴谋

1957-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

    美国休想制造“两个中国”
    苏联“真理报”斥责美国无耻阴谋
    新华社17日讯　苏联“真理报”16日发表文章抨击美国制造“两个中国”的阴谋。文章说，美国顽固地不承认占世界人口四分之一的中华人民共和国的存在。当形势迫使它不得不考虑这个现实的事实的时候，它就故意制造出“两个中国”的阴谋，企图说中华人民共和国不是世界上唯一的中国，而在台湾岛上苟延残喘的蒋介石集团是所谓“中华民国”。
    文章说，不久前在新德里举行的国际红十字大会上，美国人照例做了一次地理“发现”。参加这次大会的美国代表团对它的西方仆从国家施加压力，竟然通过了一项邀请蒋介石分子参加这个就要闭幕的大会的提案。这种破坏国际合作精神的行为激起了大会参加者和广大公众的合理的愤慨。中华人民共和国代表团对美国代表这种政治挑衅表示抗议，立即退出了会场。支持中国代表团的有苏联、各人民民主国家、印度、印度尼西亚、叙利亚和埃及。
    文章指出，中华人民共和国的国际威信的日益提高，使得某些美国人士怀恨在心。他们瞎说有“两个中国”，目的是企图保持和长期侵占中国人民自古以来的领土台湾。苏联公众坚决支持中华人民共和国的立场。各国维护和平的力量不能不谴责美国反对伟大中国的主权和领土完整这种恬不知耻的阴谋。
