Scientific Film "Birth Control"

Shanghai Science and Education Film Studio is producing a popular science film on contraception called "Birth Control" (tentative title). It uses the form of a contraception exhibition to introduce a variety of effective contraceptive tools, drugs and their methods of use, and briefly explains the significance of contraception, and briefly tells the audience the physiological knowledge of childbirth. Using film to publicize and guide contraceptive knowledge is a fresh and convenient way, and it is expected to receive greater results. A scene of "private visit" in "Empty Seal Box".

### 科教片“节制生育”

1957-11-16
第8版()
专栏：舞台和银幕

    科教片“节制生育”
    上海科学教育电影制片厂正在摄制一部有关避孕问题的科学普及影片“节制生育”（暂名）。它用避孕展览会的形式，介绍了多种有效的避孕工具、药品及其使用的方法，并扼要地说明了避孕的意义，和简略的告诉观众们生孩子的生理知识。用电影来宣传和指导避孕常识，是又新鲜又便利的好方式，预料会收到较大效果。“空印盒”中“私访”的一个场面。
