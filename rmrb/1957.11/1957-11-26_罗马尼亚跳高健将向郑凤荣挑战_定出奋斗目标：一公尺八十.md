Title: Romanian High Jumper Challenges Zheng Fengrong; Sets Goal: 1.80 Meters
Author:
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 25th. The world women's high jump record of 1.77 meters set by Chinese female high jumper Zheng Fengrong has stirred her Romanian counterparts. A Romanian News Agency sports reporter wrote that Zheng Fengrong's achievement has had a profound impact in Romania, surprising and delighting colleagues worldwide.

The reporter said that to learn the opinions of Romanian high jumpers on this achievement, he recently visited Soger, the coach of Romanian female high jumper Balas, who has a record of 1.75 meters. Soger told the reporter that after McDaniel (American female high jumper, who once set a world record of 1.76 meters) and Balas set the world's highest record in women's high jump, China's Zheng Fengrong surpassed them in a very short period. The coach said: "Zheng Fengrong's achievement did not surprise me because I know that Chinese high jumpers are talented, and once they master advanced technology, they will reach the world's highest record."

He also said that China's Zheng Fengrong may be able to devote all her time to practice, regardless of the winter cold. He also mentioned that Balas will also begin winter training. Balas will strive to jump over a height of 1.80 meters, because the height of 1.77 meters is likely to be surpassed by Chinese and Soviet athletes.

Soger finally said that even if Balas jumps over 1.80 meters, China's Zheng Fengrong will not fall behind her.

### 罗马尼亚跳高健将向郑凤荣挑战  定出奋斗目标：一公尺八十

1957-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚跳高健将向郑凤荣挑战
    定出奋斗目标：一公尺八十
    新华社布加勒斯特25日电　中国女子跳高健将郑凤荣所创造的一公尺七十七公分的世界女子跳高纪录，惊动了她的罗马尼亚的同行们。罗马尼亚通讯社体育记者报道说，郑凤荣的成就在罗马尼亚产生了深刻的影响，它也使全世界的同行们感到惊讶和高兴。
    这位记者说，为了了解罗马尼亚跳高家对这一成就的意见，他最近访问了有着一公尺七十五公分的纪录的罗马尼亚女子跳高健将巴拉斯的指导索捷尔。索捷尔对这位记者说，在麦克丹尼尔（美国女子跳高健将，她曾创一公尺七十六公分的世界女子跳高纪录）和巴拉斯创造了女子跳高的世界最高纪录以后，中国的郑凤荣在很短期间内就超过了她们。这位指导说：“郑凤荣的成就并没有出乎我的意料之外，因为我知道中国的跳高家是有才能的，他们一旦掌握了先进技术，就会达到世界最高纪录。”
    他又说，中国的郑凤荣有可能不顾冬天的寒冷、用全部时间来从事练习。他还谈到，巴拉斯也将开始冬季练习。巴拉斯将争取跳过一公尺八十公分的高度，因为一公尺七十七公分的高度是有可能被中国和苏联的运动员超过的。
    索捷尔最后表示，即使巴拉斯跳过一公尺八十公分，中国的郑凤荣也不会落在她的后边。
