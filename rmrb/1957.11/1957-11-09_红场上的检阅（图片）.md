Author: Hou Bo
Date: 1957-11-09

    Parade on Red Square
    The parade is dancing forward on Red Square. TASS telephoto (Xinhua News Agency)
    On November 7th, a grand military parade and mass procession were held on Red Square in Moscow. This is the marching contingent of working people being reviewed on Red Square. TASS telephoto (Xinhua News Agency)
    Tracked transporters carrying rocket weapons move across Red Square. TASS telephoto (Xinhua News Agency)
    Trucks carrying rocket weapons drive past the reviewing stand. TASS telephoto (Xinhua News Agency)
    The marching contingent of working people being reviewed.
