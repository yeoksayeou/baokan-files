Title: Jinghan and Yuehan Railways Renamed Beijing-Guangzhou Railway

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 10th - The Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge has connected the Jinghan and Yuehan Railways. Therefore, the Ministry of Railways recently issued an order to merge and rename these two railways as the "Beijing-Guangzhou Railway."

The trunk line starts from Fengtai in Beijing and ends at Guangzhou South Station, with a total length of 2,331 kilometers.

### 京汉、粤汉铁路更名为京广线

1957-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

    京汉、粤汉铁路更名为京广线
    新华社武汉10日电　武汉长江大桥已经把京汉、粤汉两条铁路连结起来，因此铁道部在最近发出命令，将这两条铁路合并命名为“京广线”。
    这条干线起点是北京丰台，终点是广州南站，全线里程二千三百三十一公里。
