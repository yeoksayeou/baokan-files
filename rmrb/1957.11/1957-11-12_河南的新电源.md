	Henan's New Power Source
	The Zhengzhou Thermal Power Plant has been put into production. In addition to supplying electricity to various factories in Zhengzhou and heat to the textile factory, this plant will also connect with the Luoyang Thermal Power Station through the Zheng (zhou), Luo (yang), and San (menxia) high-voltage transmission lines to form a power supply system, providing power to important industrial cities in Henan.
	This plant was built under the guidance of experts from the German Democratic Republic. The main equipment of this plant was also supplied by the German Democratic Republic. (Attached picture)
	The picture above shows German experts Schulz (right) and Baumann (center) guiding Chinese technicians in their work. Photo by Wei Dezhong (Henan Daily Press)

### 河南的新电源

1957-11-12
第3版()
专栏：建设动态

    河南的新电源
    郑州热电厂已经投入生产，这个厂除供给郑州各厂用电和纺织厂用热外还将通过郑（州）、洛（阳）、三（门峡）高压输电线，和洛阳热电站连成一个供电系统，供应河南重要工业城市。
    这个厂是在德意志民主共和国专家指导下建成的。这个厂的主要设备也是由德意志民主共和国供应的。（附图片）
    上图是德国专家舒尔茨（右）、鲍尔曼（中）在指导我国技术员进行工作。　魏德忠摄（河南日报社稿）
