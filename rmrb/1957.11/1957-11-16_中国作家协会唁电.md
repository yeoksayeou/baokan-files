Chinese Writers Association's Condolence Telegram to the Czechoslovak Writers Association

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Antonín Zápotocký, President of the Czechoslovak Republic. Chinese writers extend their deepest condolences to the writers and people of Czechoslovakia. His death is a great loss not only for the writers and people of Czechoslovakia, but also for the people of all socialist countries, the working people of the world, and the cause of socialist literature. Comrade Antonín Zápotocký was not only an outstanding son and distinguished leader of the Czechoslovak people, but also an accomplished writer. His lifelong dedication to the socialist construction of Czechoslovakia and his literary creations will be immortal!

                Chinese Writers Association
                  November 14, 1957, Beijing

### 中国作家协会唁电

1957-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    中国作家协会唁电捷克斯洛伐克作家协会
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基同志不幸逝世，中国的作家们谨向捷克斯洛伐克的作家和人民表示最沉痛的哀悼。他的逝世不仅对于捷克斯洛伐克的作家和人民，而且对于社会主义各国的人民和全世界的劳动人民、对于社会主义文学事业都是极大的损失。安托宁·萨波托斯基同志不仅是捷克斯洛伐克人民的优秀的儿子和杰出的领袖，而且也是一位出色的作家，他终生所献身的捷克斯洛伐克社会主义建设事业和他的文学创作永垂不朽！
                中国作家协会
                  1957年11月14日北京
