Title: Cambodia Celebrates Fourth Anniversary of Independence
Author:
Date: 1957-11-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, November 10th: Phnom Penh, the capital of Cambodia, held a military parade on November 9th to warmly celebrate the fourth anniversary of Cambodia's independence. Cambodian troops, including mechanized units, marched past the Royal Palace and were reviewed by the King.

Cambodian Prime Minister Sim Var delivered a speech, calling on the Cambodian people to devote all their energy and talent to building the country. Sim Var reiterated Cambodia's adherence to a policy of active neutrality.

Leaders of the Cambodian government and army, Ye Jinghao, head of the Chinese Economic Delegation to Cambodia, and diplomatic representatives of various countries to Cambodia attended the military parade.

### 柬埔寨庆祝独立四周年

1957-11-11
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨庆祝独立四周年
    据新华社金边10日电　柬埔寨首都金边11月9日举行阅兵典礼，热烈庆祝柬埔寨独立四周年。包括有机械化部队的柬埔寨部队列队经过王宫前面，接受了国王的检阅。
    柬埔寨首相沈法发表了讲演，号召柬埔寨人民以全部精力和才能来建设国家。沈法重申柬埔寨采取积极的中立政策。
    柬埔寨政府和军队的领袖、中国驻柬埔寨经济代表团团长叶景灏和各国驻柬埔寨的外交代表等参加了阅兵典礼。
