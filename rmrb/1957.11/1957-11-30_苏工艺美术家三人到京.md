Title: Three Soviet Applied Art Artists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Soviet applied art artists V.D. Lipitsky, A.V. Kovalev, and T.M. Shulipina arrived in Beijing this afternoon. They are visiting China according to the 1957 implementation plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 苏工艺美术家三人到京

1957-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    苏工艺美术家三人到京
    新华社29日讯　苏联工艺美术家维·得·利皮茨基、阿·维·科瓦廖夫、塔·米·舒利皮娜今天下午到北京。他们是根据中苏文化合作协定1957年执行计划前来中国访问的。
