Title: Readjustment of Guangming Daily; Yang Mingxuan and Chen Cisheng Appointed as President and Vice President Respectively
Author:
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Guangming Daily’s board of directors held a meeting on the 11th, inviting the heads of various democratic parties. The meeting unanimously resolved: to remove the rightist Zhang Bojun from his position as President of Guangming Daily and the rightist Chu Anping from his position as Editor-in-Chief of Guangming Daily. The meeting also decided to appoint Yang Mingxuan as President of Guangming Daily, Chen Cisheng as Vice President and Editor-in-Chief, and Mu Xin and Gao Tian as Deputy Editors-in-Chief.

The following individuals attended the meeting: Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Wang Kunlun and Chen Cisheng, members of the Central Standing Committee; Shen Junru, Chairman of the China Democratic League; Shi Liang, Vice Chairman; Hu Yuzhi and Yang Mingxuan, members of the Central Standing Committee; Huang Yanpei, Chairman of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Sun Xiaocun, member of the Central Standing Committee; non-party democrats Chen Shutong, Zhang Xiruo, and Ma Yinchu; Wang Shao'ao and Zhou Jianren, Vice Chairmen of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Ji Fang and Guo Zechen, members of the Central Standing Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Chen Qiyou, Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party; Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Jiusan Society; Li Chunqing, Vice Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League; and Sa Kongliao, Secretary-General of Guangming Daily.

Also attending the meeting were Xing Xiping, Ping Jiesan, and Yu Yifu, Deputy Ministers of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee.

Yang Mingxuan, Chen Cisheng, Mu Xin, and Gao Tian assumed their posts on the 12th. All employees of Guangming Daily held a welcoming meeting that day. At the meeting, Yang Mingxuan encouraged all employees to strive to make Guangming Daily a newspaper that truly serves the people and to make it a tool for the propaganda and organization of socialist construction. In his speech, Chen Cisheng pointed out that the long-term basic task of Guangming Daily is to implement the intentions of the CPC Central Committee and the Central People's Government and to promote the cause of socialism. Representatives of the newspaper's staff spoke at the meeting, making assurances and expressing their determination to work hard under the leadership of the Communist Party, thoroughly eliminate the impact of the rightists' alteration of the political direction of Guangming Daily, and run this socialist newspaper well.

### 光明日报社改组  杨明轩陈此生分任正副社长

1957-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    光明日报社改组
    杨明轩陈此生分任正副社长
    新华社18日讯　光明日报社务委员会在11日邀请各民主党派中央负责人举行会议。会议一致决议：撤销右派分子章伯钧的光明日报社社长职务和右派分子储安平的光明日报总编辑职务。会议并决定任杨明轩为光明日报社社长，陈此生为副社长兼总编辑，穆欣、高天为副总编辑。
    出席这次会议的有中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深、中央常务委员王昆仑、陈此生，中国民主同盟主席沈钧儒、副主席史良、中央常务委员胡愈之、杨明轩，中国民主建国会主任委员黄炎培、中央常务委员孙晓村，无党派民主人士陈叔通、张奚若、马寅初，中国民主促进会副主席王绍鏊、周建人，中国农工民主党中央常务委员季方、郭则沉，中国致公党主席陈其尤，九三学社主席许德珩，台湾民主自治同盟副主席李纯青，光明日报社秘书长萨空了。
    出席这次会议的还有中共中央统一战线工作部副部长邢西萍、平杰三、于毅夫，中共中央宣传部副部长周扬。
    杨明轩、陈此生、穆欣、高天已经在12日到职。光明日报全体职工当天举行了欢迎大会。杨明轩在会上勉励全体职工努力把光明日报办成一张真正为人民服务的报纸，使它成为建设社会主义的宣传和组织工具。陈此生在讲话中指出，光明日报长期的基本任务是贯彻中共中央和中央人民政府的意图，宣扬社会主义的事业。报社工作人员的代表在会上发言，纷纷提出保证，表示要在共产党的领导下，努力工作，彻底清除右派分子篡改光明日报政治方向期间所造成的影响，办好这张社会主义的报纸。
