Title: Representatives of Party History Research Institutions from Various Countries Gathered in Czechoslovakia to Discuss the Influence of the October Revolution and Study Issues of Scientific Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 25th: Representatives of Party history research institutions from Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries held an international conference in Prague from the 19th to the 23rd. Delegations from Albania, Bulgaria, China, France, Italy, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Hungary, Mongolia, Romania, the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Austria, Poland, and the German Democratic Republic participated in the meeting.

The conference discussed the great October Socialist Revolution's impact on the working-class movement and various countries, and also studied the issues of scientific cooperation among party history research institutions of various countries. The conference emphasized the importance of the resolutions of the 20th Congress of the CPSU, the 8th National Congress of the CPC, and the congresses, conferences, and central plenary sessions of the Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries for studying the history of the revolutionary struggle of the working class.

At the conference, Shatagin, Deputy Director of the Institute of Marxism-Leninism of the CPSU Central Committee, elaborated on the comprehensive influence of the October Revolution on world history. Li Shu, Academic Committee Member of the Third Institute of the Institute of History of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, spoke about the influence of the October Revolution on the Chinese revolution and the form of the Chinese revolution that arose from China's specific conditions. Academician Park Si-hyung, Director of the Institute of History of the Academy of Sciences of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Ochir, Director of the Institute of Party History of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and Professor Daniszewski, Director of the Institute of Party History of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, spoke about the influence of the October Revolution on the revolutionary movements in their respective countries.

The conference entrusted the Institute of Party History of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party with the final compilation of the documents on "The Influence of the October Socialist Revolution on Central European Countries."

### 各国党史研究机构代表在捷集会  讨论了十月革命影响并研究了科学合作的问题

1957-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    各国党史研究机构代表在捷集会
    讨论了十月革命影响并研究了科学合作的问题
    新华社布拉格25日电　各国共产党和工人党党史研究机构的代表，从19日到23日在布拉格举行了国际会议。参加会议的有阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、中国、法国、意大利、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、匈牙利、蒙古、罗马尼亚、苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、奥地利、波兰和民主德国的代表团。
    会议讨论了伟大的十月社会主义革命对工人阶级运动和各国的影响，还研究了各国党史研究机构进行科学合作的问题。会议强调指出了苏共第二十次代表大会、中共第八次全国代表大会以及各国共产党、工人党的代表大会、代表会议和中央全会的决议对于研究工人阶级革命斗争历史的重要性。
    在会议上，苏共中央马克思列宁主义研究院副院长夏塔金论述了十月革命对世界历史的全面影响。中国科学院历史研究所第三所学术委员黎澍谈到了十月革命对中国革命的影响以及从中国具体条件产生的中国革命的形式。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国科学院历史研究所所长朴时亨院士、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会党史研究所所长奥奇尔、波兰统一工人党中央委员会党史研究所所长达尼谢夫斯基教授，分别谈到了十月革命对这些国家的革命运动的影响。
    会议委托匈牙利社会主义工人党党史研究所对“十月社会主义革命对中欧各国的影响”的文献进行最后的编纂工作。
