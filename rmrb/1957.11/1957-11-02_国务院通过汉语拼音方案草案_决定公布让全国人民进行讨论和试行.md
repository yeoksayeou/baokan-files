Title: State Council Approves Draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme, Decides to Publish for Nationwide Discussion and Trial Implementation
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The sixtieth plenary session of the State Council held on the 1st discussed and approved the draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme, and decided to publish this scheme (draft) for nationwide discussion and trial implementation, before submitting it to the National People's Congress for discussion and decision.

The meeting also approved the State Council's Interim Provisions on the Handling of Retirement for Workers and Staff (draft). This draft will be issued to factories and mining enterprises for discussion after being approved by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and then promulgated for implementation after being revised by the State Council. The meeting also approved the State Council's proposal to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for approval to amend the number of times provincial and municipal people's congresses are held each year.

The meeting agreed to accept the 1948 International Regulations for Preventing Collisions at Sea and decided to submit it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and decision. The meeting also discussed and approved the draft agreement between China and the Soviet Union on merchant shipping on the national borders and connecting rivers and lakes. It was decided to submit it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval.

The meeting discussed and approved the issue of the proportion of agricultural tax surcharges in economic crop areas, and decided to submit this issue to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval.

The meeting also approved the list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院通过汉语拼音方案草案  决定公布让全国人民进行讨论和试行

1957-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院通过汉语拼音方案草案
    决定公布让全国人民进行讨论和试行
    新华社1日讯　1日举行的国务院全体会议第六十次会议，讨论和通过了汉语拼音方案（草案），并决定公布这个方案（草案）让全国人民进行讨论和试行，然后再提请全国人民代表大会讨论决定。
    会议还通过了国务院关于工人、职员退休处理的暂行规定（草案），这个草案将经过全国人民代表大会常务委员会通过后，发到厂矿企业进行讨论，然后再由国务院修改后公布执行。会议又通过了国务院关于提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会批准修改省、直辖市人民代表大会每年举行会议次数的议案。
    会议同意接受1948年国际海上避碰章程，并决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议决定。会议还讨论通过了中苏两国国境及相通河流和湖泊商船通航协定草案。并决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议讨论通过了关于经济作物地区农业税附加比例幅度问题，并决定将这个问题提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议还通过了任免名单。
