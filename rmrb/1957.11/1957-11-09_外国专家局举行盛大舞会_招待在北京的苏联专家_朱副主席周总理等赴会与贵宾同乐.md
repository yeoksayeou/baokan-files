Title: Foreign Experts Bureau Holds Grand Ball to Entertain Soviet Experts in Beijing; Vice Chairman Zhu, Premier Zhou, and Others Attend and Enjoy Themselves with Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The State Council's Foreign Experts Bureau held a grand ball at the Beijing Hotel on the evening of the 8th to entertain Soviet experts and their wives in Beijing.

    Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Li Fuchun, and Vice Minister Zhang Wentian attended the ball tonight.

    The Soviet delegation from all walks of life, headed by Andreyev, attending the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, G.T. Titov, and other Soviet guests currently visiting Beijing were invited to attend the ball.

    Also invited were diplomatic envoys to China from various people's democratic countries, as well as experts from various people's democratic countries in Beijing.

    Actors of all ethnic groups from the capital's literary and art circles performed many wonderful programs at the ball.

### 外国专家局举行盛大舞会  招待在北京的苏联专家  朱副主席周总理等赴会与贵宾同乐

1957-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    外国专家局举行盛大舞会
    招待在北京的苏联专家
    朱副主席周总理等赴会与贵宾同乐
    新华社8日讯　国务院外国专家局8日晚在北京饭店举办盛大舞会，招待在北京的苏联专家和他们的夫人。
    朱德副主席，周恩来总理，陈毅、李富春副总理和张闻天副部长出席了今晚的舞会。
    以安德烈耶夫为首的苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团、苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办顾悌克夫和正在北京访问的其他苏联客人应邀出席舞会。
    应邀出席的，还有各人民民主国家驻中国的使节，以及各人民民主国家在北京的专家。
    首都文艺界的各族演员们在舞会上表演了许多精采节目。
