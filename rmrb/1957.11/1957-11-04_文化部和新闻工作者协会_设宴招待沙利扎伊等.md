Title: Ministry of Culture and Journalists Association Host Banquet for Shalizi et al.
Author:
Date: 1957-11-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd - The Ministry of Culture and the China Journalists Association held a luncheon banquet on the 2nd to entertain Abdul Sattar Shalizi, Deputy Director of the Department of News and Publications of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and Abdul Rauf Bennawa, Director of Kabul Radio Station, and others.

Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Culture, Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the China Journalists Association, Wen Jize, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, and others attended the banquet.

Shalizi and Bennawa accompanied Afghan Prime Minister Sardar Mohammed Daoud on his visit to our country on October 22. They will continue their tour and visit in our country.

### 文化部和新闻工作者协会  设宴招待沙利扎伊等

1957-11-04
第2版()
专栏：

    文化部和新闻工作者协会
    设宴招待沙利扎伊等
    新华社2日讯　文化部和中国新闻工作者协会2日午间举行宴会，招待阿富汗王国新闻和出版署副署长阿卜杜勒·萨塔尔·沙利扎伊和喀布尔广播电台台长阿卜杜勒·拉奥夫·本纳瓦等人。
    文化部副部长张致祥和中国新闻工作者协会副会长王芸生，广播事业管理局副局长温济泽等人出席了宴会。
    沙利扎伊和本纳瓦是随同阿富汗首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德在10月22日来我国访问的。他们将在我国继续参观访问。
