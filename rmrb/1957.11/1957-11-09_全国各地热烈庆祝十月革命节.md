Title: Nationwide Celebrations of the October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 3

Nationwide Celebrations of the October Revolution Day

    Xinjiang
    More than 800 people from all ethnic groups in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region and Urumqi City, together with Soviet experts, solemnly held a celebration meeting on the morning of November 7 to commemorate the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.
    Urumqi City was decorated with lanterns and colored hangings, and the streets were bustling with activity. Employees of many units held celebrations, and employees of units with Soviet experts working to assist them held get-togethers with the Soviet experts. The Soviet Film Week has also begun.
    Lanzhou
    Lanzhou City held a meeting of more than 1,600 people on November 7 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.
    Soviet experts who are assisting Lanzhou City in its construction also attended the celebration meeting.
    Zhang Zhongliang, First Secretary of the Gansu Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke at the meeting. He called on the people of the province to humbly and earnestly learn from the spirit of the Soviet people's hard work, arduous struggle, and building the country with diligence and thrift. He expressed heartfelt thanks to the Soviet experts who are helping our country's construction.
    During this great festival, the streets and alleys of Lanzhou City were filled with banners, slogans, and colorful flowers celebrating the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. Crowds thronged in front of every window displaying pictures of Soviet construction achievements and Sino-Soviet friendship. Cultural and artistic units held a music appreciation evening featuring the music of the great Russian composer Glinka, and performed Soviet dramas and song and dance programs. The "Soviet Film Week" also began on November 7.
        Qinghai
    More than 1,200 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Qinghai Province solemnly gathered at the People's Auditorium in Xining City on the evening of November 6 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.
    Soviet experts in Xining were invited to attend the celebration meeting.
    Gao Feng, First Secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered a report at the meeting.
    On November 6, about 20 government agencies, factories, enterprises, and agricultural cooperatives in Xining City held celebrations, get-togethers, song and dance evenings, and various other forms of evening parties. The opening ceremony of the "Soviet Film Week" in Xining City was also held on the morning of November 6.
      Hohhot
    More than 1,200 people from all ethnic groups in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Hohhot City solemnly gathered at the Ulanqab Theatre (Red Theatre) in Hohhot City on the evening of November 6. Wang Duo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke at the meeting. Also attending the celebration meeting were Tse. Dash, Consul General of the Mongolian People's Republic in Hohhot, members of the National Music and Drama Theatre Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic who had just arrived in Hohhot City for a visit on the evening of the 6th, and Mongolian experts in Inner Mongolia.
        Guizhou
    The mountain city in the southwest of the motherland—Guiyang City, with its 500,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life, solemnly celebrated the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. On the eve of the festival, more than 1,000 people from all walks of life in Guizhou Province and Guiyang City held a celebration meeting. Soviet experts who came to Guizhou to help with work were also invited to attend. On the morning of November 7, more than 1,600 teachers and students from 23 ethnic groups at the Guizhou Provincial Nationalities College held a celebration meeting.
        Yunnan
    On the evening of November 6, more than 1,700 representatives from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Yunnan Province and Kunming City solemnly celebrated the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. Xie Fuzhi, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke at the meeting. He said: We must earnestly learn from the experience of the Soviet Union. The spirit of the Soviet people overcoming difficulties, working hard, and building the country with diligence and thrift is the best example for us to learn from. Only through hard work and building the country with diligence and thrift can we build our country into a socialist industrial power. Soviet expert Masnov also spoke at the meeting. He said that we will do our best to help you build socialist China and make China's construction proceed faster and more smoothly.
        Lhasa
    More than 1,000 people from all walks of life in Lhasa solemnly gathered at the Lhasa Auditorium on November 7 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.
    The celebration meeting was presided over by Sokang Wangqinggele. On behalf of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Local Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, he extended warm congratulations to this great festival of the working people of the world, and then reported on the construction achievements of the Soviet Union.
    Fan Ming, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region, spoke at the meeting.
    Apei Awang Jinmei, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, warmly congratulated the great festival on behalf of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region. In his speech, he warmly praised the unparalleled superiority of the socialist system. He said that only by following the socialist road can the people of Tibet achieve prosperity and happiness. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国各地热烈庆祝十月革命节

1957-11-09
第3版()
专栏：

    全国各地热烈庆祝十月革命节
    新疆
    新疆维吾尔自治区和乌鲁木齐市各族各界人民八百多人，和苏联专家一起，7日上午隆重举行了十月社会主义革命四十周年庆祝大会。
    乌鲁木齐市到处张灯结彩，大街上热闹异常。许多单位的职工举行了庆祝会，有苏联专家帮助工作的单位的职工和苏联专家一起举行了联欢会。苏联电影周也已经开始。
     兰州
    兰州市7日举行了一千六百多人的大会，庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年。
    帮助兰州市进行建设的苏联专家也参加了庆祝大会。
    中共甘肃省委第一书记张仲良在会上讲了话。他号召全省人民虚心地认真学习苏联人民艰苦奋斗、勤俭建国的精神。他向帮助我国建设的苏联专家们表示衷心的感谢。
    在这个伟大的节日里，兰州市大街小巷挂满了庆祝十月革命四十周年的横幅、标语和彩花，街头每个贴有苏联建设成就和表现中苏友谊等图片的橱窗前，都拥挤着人群。文化艺术单位举行了俄国大音乐家格林卡的音乐欣赏晚会，演出了苏联话剧和歌舞节目。“苏联电影周”也在7日开始。
        青海
    青海各族各界一千二百多人6日晚上在西宁市人民礼堂隆重集会，庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年。
    在西宁的苏联专家应邀参加了庆祝大会。
    中共青海省委第一书记高峰在会上作了报告。
    6日，西宁市约二十多个机关、工厂企业和农业社分别举行了庆祝会、联欢会、歌舞晚会和各种形式的晚会。西宁市“苏联电影周”也在6日上午举行了开幕式。
      呼和浩特
    内蒙古自治区及呼和浩特市各族各界一千二百多人，6日晚在呼和浩特市乌兰卡特（红色剧院）隆重举行大会。中共内蒙古自治区委员会书记处书记王铎在会上讲了话。参加庆祝大会的，还有蒙古人民共和国驻呼和浩特总领事策·达什，和6日晚上刚到呼和浩特市访问的蒙古人民共和国国家音乐话剧院艺术团的团员，以及在内蒙古的蒙古专家。
        贵州
    祖国西南方的山城——贵阳市的五十万各族各界人民隆重庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年。节日前夕，贵州省、贵阳市各界人民一千多人举行了庆祝会。前来贵州帮助工作的苏联专家也应邀参加。7日上午，贵州省民族学院包括二十三种民族的一千六百多名师生举行了庆祝会。
        云南
      6日晚，云南省和昆明市各族各界代表一千七百多人隆重庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年。中共云南省委第一书记谢富治在会上讲话。他说：我们一定要认真学习苏联的经验。苏联人民克服困难、艰苦奋斗、勤俭建国的精神，是我们最好的学习榜样，只有艰苦奋斗，勤俭建国，才能把我国建成为一个社会主义工业国。苏联专家马斯诺夫也在会上讲了话。他说，我们将尽力帮助你们建设社会主义的中国，使中国的建设进行得更快更顺利。
        拉萨
    拉萨各界一千多人7日在拉萨大礼堂隆重集会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年。
    庆祝大会由索康·旺清格勒主持。他代表政协西藏地方筹备委员会，向这个全世界劳动人民伟大的节日致热烈的祝贺，并且接着报告了苏联的建设成就。
    中共西藏工委副书记、西藏军区副政治委员范明在会上讲了话。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美代表西藏自治区筹备委员会，热烈祝贺这个伟大的节日。他在讲话中热烈赞扬社会主义制度的无比优越。他说，西藏人民只有走社会主义道路，才能得到繁荣、幸福。　（据新华社讯）
