Title: Russian Folk Song
Author: Cao Nianming
Date: 1957-11-02
Page: 8

A public notice of a Russian folk song came to the village, this is Lenin's order: Return all the landlords' land to the peasants. Not for God, not for the Tsar, we bravely go to the battlefield, for the land, for the factories, for the peasants and workers. The white snow melts quickly, the stream flows in the river. The White bandits are desperately fleeing, the Red Army is advancing rapidly.
        (Translated by Cao Nianming)

### 俄罗斯民谣
曹念明
1957-11-02
第8版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯民谣公文来到村庄，这是列宁的命令：把地主的土地，全都还给农民。不为上帝，不为沙皇，我们勇敢地走上战场，为了土地，为了工厂，为了农民和工人。白雪很快地溶化了，流水在小河里奔腾。白匪拚命地逃跑了，红军迅速地前进。
        （曹念明译）
