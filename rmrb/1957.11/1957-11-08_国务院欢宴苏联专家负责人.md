Title: State Council Hosts Banquet for Heads of Soviet Experts

Author:

Date: 1957-11-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 7th November: The State Council held a banquet at noon on the 7th to entertain the heads of Soviet experts working in China, celebrating the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

Li Fuchun, Vice Premier of the State Council, extended warm congratulations to the heads of Soviet economic, cultural, and educational experts and their assistants, as well as their wives, on behalf of the State Council. Li Fuchun said, "The achievements of China's construction work over the past eight years are inseparable from the creative labor of the Soviet expert comrades who have been helping in various departments in our country. In the future, we should continue to learn humbly from the Soviet expert comrades." In his speech, he expressed his high respect and heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet experts.

Soviet economic expert Galinin said in his speech that today is the greatest festival of mankind, and it is the most precious moment we feel. What makes us even more happy and proud is that 30,000 Chinese people once participated in the struggle of the October Revolution together with the Soviet people. This shows that the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people is deep and unbreakable, and it has already begun. He proposed a toast to this profound friendship.

Nie Rongzhen, Vice Premier of the State Council, and Soviet expert Sudarikov also spoke at the banquet, congratulating the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution and the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people.

More than 100 people attended the banquet, including the heads of Soviet experts Sudarikov, Galinin, Levakin, Rakiyanov, and other experts, as well as their wives.

Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, and the heads of various offices of the State Council were also present at the banquet.

The various departments of the State Council also held banquets and get-togethers today to celebrate the festival with the experts from the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries who are helping in the various departments.

### 国务院欢宴苏联专家负责人

1957-11-08
第3版()
专栏：

    国务院欢宴苏联专家负责人
    新华社7日讯　国务院在7日中午举行宴会招待在我国工作的苏联专家的负责人，庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年。
    国务院副总理李富春在宴会上代表国务院，向苏联经济、文教专家的负责人及其助手人员和他们的夫人，致热烈的祝贺。李富春说：“中国八年来的建设工作的成就，是和在我国各部门帮助工作的苏联专家同志们的创造性劳动分不开的。今后我们应当继续虚心地向苏联专家同志们学习。”他在讲话中表示向苏联专家们致崇高的敬意和衷心的感谢。
    苏联经济专家加里宁讲话说，今天是人类最伟大的节日，是我们感到的最宝贵的时刻。更使我们高兴和骄傲的是有三万名中国人民曾经和苏联人民一道参加了十月革命的斗争。这说明中国和苏联两国人民的友谊是深厚而又牢不可破的，是早已开始了的。他建议为这种深厚的友谊干杯。
    国务院副总理聂荣臻和苏联专家苏达利科夫也在宴会上讲话，祝贺十月社会主义革命四十周年和中苏人民的友谊。
    参加宴会的苏联专家负责人有苏达利科夫、加里宁、列瓦金、拉基扬诺夫和其他专家以及他们的夫人一百多人。
    在宴会上作陪的还有国务院秘书长习仲勋，国务院各办公室的负责人。
    国务院各部门今天也分别举行宴会和联欢活动，同在各部门帮助工作的苏联和各人民民主国家的专家们欢度节日。
