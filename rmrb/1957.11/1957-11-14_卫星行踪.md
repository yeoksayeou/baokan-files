Title: Satellite's Whereabouts
Author:
Date: 1957-11-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 13th: By 6:00 AM Moscow time on November 13th, the second artificial satellite had orbited the Earth 138 times. The first artificial satellite and its carrier rocket had orbited the Earth 591 and 595 times, respectively.

    When the weather is clear, the second satellite can be seen before sunrise between 54 and 68 degrees north latitude, and after sunset between 35 and 68 degrees south latitude. The carrier rocket of the first satellite can be seen after sunset between 62 and 65 degrees north latitude, and before sunrise between 48 and 64 degrees south latitude.

### 卫星行踪

1957-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    卫星行踪
    据新华社13日讯　到11月13日莫斯科时间早晨六点钟为止，第二个人造卫星已经绕地球转了一百三十八圈。第一个人造卫星及其运载火箭分别绕地球转了五百九十一圈和五百九十五圈。
    天晴时，北纬五十四度到六十八度之间在日出前，南纬三十五度到六十八度之间在日落后可以看到第二个卫星。北纬六十二度到六十五度之间在日落后，南纬四十八度到六十四度之间在日出前可以看到第一个卫星的运载火箭。
