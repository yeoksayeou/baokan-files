Title: All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Condolence Message
Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 4

    To Mr. Bušiňák, Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic:
    We were deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Antonín Zápotocký, President of the Czechoslovak Republic. This is not only a great loss for the people of Czechoslovakia and the socialist countries, but also a great loss for all people around the world who love peace, democracy, and socialism. On behalf of the Chinese national industrial and commercial circles, I extend my deepest condolences to Your Excellency the Ambassador, and through you, to the people of Czechoslovakia!
          All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce
                      November 15, 1957

### 全国工商联唁电

1957-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    全国工商联唁电布希尼亚克大使先生：
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基同志不幸逝世，这不仅是捷克斯洛伐克人民和社会主义各国人民的重大损失，而且也是全世界爱好和平、民主与社会主义的人民的重大损失。谨代表中国全国工商界向大使先生，并通过您向捷克斯洛伐克人民表示深切的哀悼！
          中华全国工商业联合会
                      1957年11月15日
