Title: Indonesian Farmers' Front Demands Elimination of Foreign Monopoly Capital
Author:
Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 21st: The Central Committee of the Indonesian Farmers' Front issued a statement saying that only by eliminating foreign monopoly capital, especially Dutch monopoly capital in Indonesia, can Indonesia's construction plans be realized, state-owned enterprises be further strengthened and expanded, national private enterprises be protected, and the people's lives be gradually improved.

The announcement expressed hope that the Indonesian National Construction Conference, which opens on November 25, will focus on discussing the drafting and restoration of the national economic plan.

The announcement stated that Indonesia is rich in natural resources and has sufficient manpower. If the government makes full use of resources and stipulates that foreign capitalists, landlords, and millionaires in Indonesia should purchase government bonds and nationalize all Dutch enterprises in Indonesia, it will have sufficient capital for construction.

The announcement expressed disagreement with the method of increasing foreign capital investment to achieve construction plans.

### 印度尼西亚农民阵线  要求消灭外国垄断资本

1957-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚农民阵线
    要求消灭外国垄断资本
    新华社雅加达21日电　印度尼西亚农民阵线中央委员会发表公告说，只有消灭了外国的垄断资本，特别是荷兰在印度尼西亚的垄断资本，印度尼西亚的建设计划才能实现，国营企业才能进一步加强和扩大，民族的私营企业才能得到保护，人民生活才能得到逐步改善。
    公告表示希望11月25日开幕的印度尼西亚全国建设会议集中讨论起草和恢复国民经济计划的问题。
    公告说，印度尼西亚自然资源丰富，人力充足，如果政府充分利用资源，并且规定外国在印度尼西亚的大资本家以及地主和百万富翁应该购买公债，把荷兰在印度尼西亚的一切企业收归国有，它将有足够的资本来进行建设。
    公告表示不同意用增加外国资本的投资的办法来实现建设计划。
