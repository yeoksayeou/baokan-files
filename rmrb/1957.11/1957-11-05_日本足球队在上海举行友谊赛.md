Title: Japanese Football Team Holds Friendly Match in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 8

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 4th. The Japanese national football team tied a friendly match with the Shanghai football team in Shanghai on the 3rd with a score of 1-1. This was the fifth friendly match for the Japanese national football team after arriving in China.
    In this match, both sides played very bravely, attacking each other and fighting for every inch of ground, winning continuous applause and praise from more than 50,000 spectators.
    The Japanese national football team is scheduled to hold another friendly match with the Red Flag Sports Association football team on the 5th.

### 日本足球队在上海举行友谊赛

1957-11-05
第8版()
专栏：

    日本足球队在上海举行友谊赛
    据新华社上海4日电　日本国家足球队3日在上海同上海足球队举行的友谊比赛中，以一比一踢成和局。这是日本国家足球队来中国后的第五场友谊比赛。
    这场比赛，双方都踢得非常勇猛，互相进攻，寸土必争，赢得五万多观众不断鼓掌称赞。
    日本国家足球队预定5日再同红旗体育协会足球队举行友谊比赛。
