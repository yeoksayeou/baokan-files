Title: Chairman Mao Cables Thanks to Soviet Leaders
Author:
Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Irkutsk, 21st. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram today to Comrades Khrushchev, Voroshilov, and Bulganin, expressing his gratitude as he left Soviet territory. The telegram reads as follows:

    Dear Comrades Khrushchev, Voroshilov, and Bulganin:
    As we are about to leave the land of the great Soviet Union, I, on behalf of all comrades of the Chinese delegation, once again express our heartfelt gratitude to you, and through you to the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Soviet government, and the Soviet people. Thank you for inviting us to participate in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution and to visit your great country, thank you for your very kind reception, and thank you for the deep brotherly friendship you have shown to us and all the Chinese people.
    Please allow me, on behalf of the Chinese delegation and all the Chinese people, and in my own name, to wish the leading comrades of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government good health, to wish the Soviet people even more brilliant achievements under the leadership of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union in the cause of building communism and safeguarding world peace, and to wish the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples eternal consolidation and continuous development.
            Mao Zedong
            November 21, 1957
            Irkutsk

### 毛主席电谢苏联领导人

1957-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电谢苏联领导人
    新华社伊尔库次克21日电　毛泽东主席今天在离开苏联国境的时候，打电报给赫鲁晓夫、伏罗希洛夫和布尔加宁同志表示感谢。电文如下：亲爱的赫鲁晓夫同志、伏罗希洛夫同志、布尔加宁同志：
    当我们将要离开伟大苏联的国土的时候，我代表中国代表团全体同志再一次向你们，并且通过你们向苏联共产党、苏联政府和苏联人民，表示衷心的感谢。感谢你们邀请我们参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的庆祝典礼和访问你们的伟大的国家，感谢你们对我们非常亲切的接待，感谢你们对我们和全体中国人民所表示的深厚的兄弟情谊。
    请允许我代表中国代表团和全体中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，祝苏联共产党和苏联政府的领导同志们身体健康，祝苏联人民在苏联共产党中央委员会的领导下，在建设共产主义和维护世界和平的事业中取得更加辉煌的成就，祝中苏两国人民的伟大友谊永远巩固和不断发展。		毛泽东　1957年11月21日　伊尔库次克
