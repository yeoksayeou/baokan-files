Title: Resisting the US and British "Atomic Hegemony": Western European Union Parliamentary Committee on Armaments Advocates Joint Weapons Production by Member States
Author:
Date: 1957-11-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 29th, Paris dispatch: The Western European Union Parliamentary Defense and Armaments Committee met in Paris on November 28th. Based on a report by French representative Deschamps, the meeting passed a proposal advocating that Western European Union countries unite to produce weapons for the North Atlantic Group, in order to "relieve the United States of part of the burden of producing weapons for this alliance." The committee chairman, Italian Lucifero, said that this proposal prevents the United States and Britain from playing a "leading role" in the North Atlantic Group.

This proposal will be submitted to the North Atlantic Group heads of government meeting scheduled to be held in Paris in December for consideration.

### 抵制美英的“原子霸权”  西欧联盟议会军备委员会  主张成员国联合生产武器

1957-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    抵制美英的“原子霸权”
    西欧联盟议会军备委员会  主张成员国联合生产武器
    据新华社29日讯　巴黎消息：西欧联盟议会防御和军备委员会11月28日在巴黎开会。会议根据法国代表戴尚的报告，通过了一项建议，主张西欧联盟各国联合起来为北大西洋集团生产武器，以“解除美国为这个联盟生产武器的一部分负担”。委员会主席、意大利人卢契费罗说，这个建议使美英两国不能在北大西洋集团里起“领导作用”。
    这项建议将提交给定在12月在巴黎举行的北大西洋集团政府首脑会议考虑。
