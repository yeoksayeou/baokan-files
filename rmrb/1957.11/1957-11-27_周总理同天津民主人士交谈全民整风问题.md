Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Discusses the National Rectification Campaign with Democratic Figures in Tianjin

Author:
Date: 1957-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 26th – Premier of the State Council, Zhou Enlai, met with over twenty democratic figures from various democratic parties and non-party democrats in Tianjin, including Zhou Shutao, Yang Yizhou, Zhang Guofan, and Liu Xiying, at approximately 7:00 PM on the 25th. He discussed the issue of the national rectification campaign with them, hoping that they would actively participate in this national rectification work, support state personnel going to the countryside to engage in labor training, and transform the overall social atmosphere.

### 周总理同天津民主人士交谈全民整风问题

1957-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同天津民主人士交谈全民整风问题
    新华社天津26日电　国务院周恩来总理在25日下午七时许会见了天津市各民主党派和无党派民主人士周叔弢、杨亦周、张国藩、刘锡瑛等二十余人，同他们交谈了全民整风的问题，并且希望他们积极参加这一全民整风的工作，支持国家工作人员下乡实行劳动锻炼，转变整个社会的风气。
