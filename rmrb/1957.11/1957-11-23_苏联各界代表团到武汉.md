Title: Soviet Delegation Arrives in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1957-11-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, November 22nd: The Soviet delegation from all walks of life participating in the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, headed by Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, arrived in Wuhan by train from Guangzhou at 11:36 PM on the 21st. Liao Chengzhi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Cao Mengjun, Director, and Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General, accompanied them.

　　Zhang Tixue, Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Governor of Hubei Province, Li Erzhong, Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and President of the Wuhan Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Liu Huinong, Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Wuhan, and responsible persons from all walks of life, were at the station to welcome the distinguished guests.

　　More than 40 Soviet experts in Wuhan, including Andryniuk, also came to the station to greet them.

### 苏联各界代表团到武汉

1957-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联各界代表团到武汉
    新华社武汉22日电　以苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫为首的苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团，21日晚十一时三十六分乘火车由广州到达武汉。中苏友好协会总会副会长廖承志、理事曹孟君和副秘书长戈宝权陪同前来。
    到车站欢迎贵宾的，有中共湖北省委书记、湖北省省长张体学，中共武汉市委书记、武汉市中苏友好协会会长李尔重，中共武汉市委书记、武汉市市长刘惠农和各界负责人。
    在武汉的苏联专家安德尼丘克等四十余人，也到站迎接。
