Title: Syrian People's Resistance Army Will Exist for a Long Time
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-30
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 29th - Colonel Salah Bizri, Commander-in-Chief of the People's Resistance Army, said on the 28th that the establishment of the People's Resistance Army is not a temporary measure. He said that the Syrian people will always remain armed in order to resist any possible foreign aggression.

He also said that if the enemy dares to invade Syria, he will find that Syria is not so easy to swallow.

### 叙人民抗敌军将长期存在

1957-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    叙人民抗敌军将长期存在
    据新华社大马士革29日电　人民抗敌军总司令萨拉赫·比兹里上校28日说，建立人民抗敌军并不是临时性的措施。他说，叙利亚人民将永远保持武装状态以便抵抗任何可能发生的外国侵略。
    他又说，敌人如果竟然敢于侵犯叙利亚的话，那他将发现叙利亚不是那么容易吞没的。
