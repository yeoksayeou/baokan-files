Title: Mourning the Death of President Zapotocky: Czechoslovak National Assembly Decides to Hold Memorial Service on the 18th; Party and Government Leaders of Various Countries Send Condolences on the Death of the Czechoslovak President
Author:
Date: 1957-11-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 16th: On the 15th, crowds of workers, farmers, women, children, students, and other residents of Prague continued to flock to the Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee building to pay their final respects to the late President Zapotocky.

Inside the mourning hall, a large portrait of President Zapotocky, framed in black, hung high, and many wreaths presented by various enterprises and organizations in Prague were displayed. People offering condolences mourned silently in front of the portrait with tears in their eyes. Among them were old classmates from Zakolany village near Kladno, President Zapotocky’s birthplace, and veteran workers from major factories in Prague. They paid their revolutionary respects to their "Tonik" (as they usually called him). On the 14th, a total of more than seven thousand people went to the Central Committee building to bid farewell to Zapotocky, and by noon on the 15th, approximately three thousand five hundred more people had gone there. In addition, thousands of people went to other halls in Prague where memorial services were held to pay their respects.

The Party Central Committee has so far received eight thousand telegrams and letters of condolence from all over the country, indicating that the people of Czechoslovakia feel extremely grieved by the great loss they have suffered. About sixty telegrams of condolence have also been sent by fraternal parties and party leaders around the world.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 16th: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency on the 15th, the Czechoslovak National Assembly will hold a memorial service for President Zapotocky on November 18th.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 15th: The people of Korea deeply mourn the death of Czechoslovak President Zapotocky. Pyongyang newspapers yesterday and today published photographs of President Zapotocky and news of his death.

"Rodong Sinmun" published an editorial today stating that the Korean people express their deep condolences on the death of Comrade Zapotocky. The Korean people are very familiar with the name of Comrade Zapotocky, an outstanding fighter for the construction of socialism in Czechoslovakia and standing at the forefront of the international workers' movement and communist movement. He had made efforts to strengthen the friendship and cooperation between the peoples of Korea and Czechoslovakia.

Xinhua News Agency, November 16th: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the government continue to receive telegrams of condolence from all over the world mourning the death of President Zapotocky.

The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party received telegrams of condolence from the Communist Party of Australia, the Communist Party of Chile, the Communist Party of Mexico, the Swiss Labor Party, the Communist Party of Spain, and the Communist Party of Uruguay.

The Czechoslovak government received telegrams of condolence from Indian President Prasad and Prime Minister Nehru, Burmese President U Win Maung, Indonesian President Sukarno, Egyptian President Nasser, Cambodian Prince Sihanouk, Pakistani Acting President Wahab Khan, Afghan King Mohammed, and Prime Minister Daud Khan.

### 悼念萨波托斯基总统逝世  捷国民议会决定十八日举行追悼大会  各国党和政府领导人电唁捷总统逝世

1957-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    悼念萨波托斯基总统逝世
    捷国民议会决定十八日举行追悼大会
    各国党和政府领导人电唁捷总统逝世
    新华社布拉格16日电  15日，成群结队的工人、农民、妇女、儿童、学生和布拉格其他市民继续涌向捷共中央委员会大厦，向已故的萨波托斯基总统致最后的敬礼。
    灵堂里高悬着镶有黑边的萨波托斯基总统的巨幅画像，陈列着布拉格各企业和机关团体所献的许多花圈。吊唁的人们含着眼泪在画像的前面默哀。他们当中有来自克拉德诺附近萨波托斯基同志出生地扎科拉尼村的老同学们、布拉格各大工厂的老工人们。他们向自己的“托尼克”（他们通常总是这样称呼他）致以革命的敬礼。14日共有七千多人到中央委员会大厦向萨波托斯基告别，到15日中午为止，又有大约三千五百人到那里去。另外还有成千的人前往布拉格其他举行追悼仪式的大厅致敬。
    党中央委员会到目前为止已经从全国各地收到八千份吊唁的电报和信件，这些电报和信件表明，捷克斯洛伐克人民对于他们所蒙受的重大损失感到极其哀痛。全世界各兄弟党和党的领导人也拍来了大约六十份唁电。
    新华社布拉格16日电  据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社15日报道，捷克斯洛伐克国民议会将在11月18日举行萨波托斯基总统的追悼大会。
    新华社平壤15日电  朝鲜人民沉痛哀悼捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基的逝世。昨天和今天的平壤报纸都登载了萨波托斯基总统的相片和逝世的消息。
      “劳动新闻”今天发表社论说，朝鲜人民对萨波托斯基同志的逝世表示深深地哀悼。朝鲜人民对捷克斯洛伐克建设社会主义的卓越的斗士、站在国际工人运动和共产主义运动的前列的萨波托斯基同志的名字是很亲切的。他曾对加强朝捷两国人民的友谊合作作了努力。
    据新华社16日讯  据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府继续收到来自世界各地悼念萨波托斯基总统逝世的唁电。
    捷共中央收到澳大利亚共产党、智利共产党、墨西哥共产党、瑞士劳动党、西班牙共产党和乌拉圭共产党的唁电。
    捷克斯洛伐克政府收到了印度总统普拉沙德和总理尼赫鲁、缅甸总统吴貌温、印度尼西亚总统苏加诺、埃及总统纳赛尔、柬埔寨西哈努克亲王、巴基斯坦代理总统瓦哈卜汗、阿富汗国王穆罕默德和首相达乌德汗的唁电。
