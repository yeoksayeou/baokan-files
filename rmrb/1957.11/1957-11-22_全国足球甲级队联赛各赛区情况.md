Title: National Football League Division Situation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 8
Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The second round of the 1957 National Football League Division matches saw two more games played in the Beijing and Tianjin competition zones on the 21st. The final two games in this competition zone for November are scheduled to be held in Tianjin and Beijing on the 22nd and 28th, respectively.

    The "August 1st" team, after defeating Changchun team three to zero on the 17th, defeated the Shenyang Institute of Physical Education team four to one in Beijing on the 21st. A match was also held in Tianjin on the 21st. The match between the Tianjin team and the First Machinery Sports Association team was quite tense, ending in a one-to-one draw.
    The second round of the 1957 National Football League Division matches in the Shanghai and Nanjing competition zones also saw one game each on the 20th. In Shanghai, the China Red Flag Sports Association team defeated the Wuhan Institute of Physical Education team six to one. In Nanjing, the Shanghai Institute of Physical Education First Team played to a zero-to-zero draw with the Nanjing Military Team. The final two games in the Shanghai and Nanjing competition zones for November will be held in Shanghai and Nanjing on the 24th, respectively.

### 全国足球甲级队联赛各赛区情况

1957-11-22
第8版()
专栏：体育新闻

    全国足球甲级队联赛各赛区情况
    据新华社21日讯　1957年全国足球甲级队联赛第二循环11月份的比赛，21日在北京、天津竞赛区又举行了两场。这个竞赛区11月的最后两场比赛，预定22日和28日在天津和北京分别举行。
      “八一”队继17日以三比零胜长春队后，21日在北京又以四比一胜了沈阳体育学院队。21日在天津也举行了比赛。天津队对第一机械体育协会队的比赛相当紧张，结果以一比一战和。
    1957年全国足球甲级队联赛第二循环11月份上海、南京竞赛区的比赛，20日也各赛了一场。在上海，中国红旗体育协会队以六比一胜武汉体育学院队。在南京，上海体育学院第一队以零比零和南京部队队踢成平局。上海、南京竞赛区11月份的最后两场比赛，24日将在上海和南京分别举行。
