Title: Gale Forecast
Author:
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 8

    Gale Forecast
    (This newspaper dispatch) According to a gale forecast released by the Central Meteorological Science Institute at 7:00 PM on the 18th: Influenced by cold air, the Bohai Sea, Yellow Sea, Liaodong Peninsula, and Shandong Peninsula will experience northeasterly winds of around force six within the next twenty-four hours; Shanxi, Hebei, Henan, and Shandong will experience northerly winds of around force five. On the 19th, the Huai River basin, the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River, and the northern part of the East China Sea will experience northerly winds of around force five, with maximum wind force reaching force six on river and lake surfaces.
  　In the aforementioned areas, the temperature will drop significantly within twenty-four hours after the passage of the cold air front. On the morning of the 20th, the minimum temperature in various parts of the Huai River basin will drop to 1 to 3 degrees Celsius, and there will be frost.

### 大风预告

1957-11-19
第8版()
专栏：

    大风预告
    本报讯　中央气象科学研究所18日下午七时发布大风预告：受冷空气影响，未来二十四小时内，渤海、黄海、辽东半岛和山东半岛，将有六级左右的东北风；山西、河北、河南和山东将有五级左右的偏北风。19日，淮河流域、长江中下游和东海北部将有五级左右的偏北风，江面、湖面最大风力将达六级。
  　以上地区在冷空气前锋过后，二十四小时以内，气温将有显著的下降。20日早晨，淮河流域各地最低气温将下降到摄氏一至三度，并将有霜冻。
