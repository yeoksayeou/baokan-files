Title: Polish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 18th - A three-member Polish cultural delegation arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China in accordance with the 1957 executive plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

The delegation members are: A. Markovski, Director of the Department of Foreign Cultural Cooperation of the Polish Ministry of Culture; Jan Cybis, Chairman of the Polish Artists Association; and the writer K. Wikka.

The head of the delegation, S. Żółkiewski, Minister of the Polish Ministry of Higher Education, had arrived in China earlier.

### 波文化代表团到京

1957-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    波文化代表团到京
    新华社18日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1957年执行计划来中国访问的波兰文化代表团一行三人，今天坐飞机到达北京。
    代表团成员是：波兰文化部对外文化合作司司长艾·马可夫斯基，波兰美术家协会主席扬·齐比斯和作家卡·维卡。
    代表团团长、波兰高等教育部部长斯·茹凯夫斯基已先期来中国。
