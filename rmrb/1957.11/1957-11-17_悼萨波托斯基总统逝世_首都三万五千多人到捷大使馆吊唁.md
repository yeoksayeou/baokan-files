Title: Mourning President Zapotocky's Death: Over 35,000 People in the Capital Visit the Czechoslovakian Embassy to Pay Respects
Author:
Date: 1957-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Another ten thousand people from the capital visited the Czechoslovakian Embassy in China today to mourn the death of President Zapotocky.

Over the past four days, a total of more than 35,000 people from all walks of life in the capital have gone to the embassy to pay their respects. Those who paid their respects presented more than 350 wreaths to President Zapotocky's portrait.

Today was the last day to pay respects at the embassy. Among those who paid their respects were workers, government officials, officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army, students, professors, and citizens from all walks of life.

### 悼萨波托斯基总统逝世  首都三万五千多人到捷大使馆吊唁

1957-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    悼萨波托斯基总统逝世
    首都三万五千多人到捷大使馆吊唁
    新华社16日讯  今天又有一万多首都人民到捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆，吊唁萨波托斯基总统逝世。
    四天以来，前往大使馆吊唁的首都各界人民共有三万五千多人。吊唁的人们向萨波托斯基总统遗像献了三百五十多个花圈。
    今天是到大使馆吊唁的最后一天。吊唁的人们中有工人、机关干部，解放军军官和士兵，学生，教授和市民等各界人士。
