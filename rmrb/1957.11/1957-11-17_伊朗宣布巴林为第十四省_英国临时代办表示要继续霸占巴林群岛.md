Title: Iran Announces Bahrain as its Fourteenth Province; British Chargé d'Affaires Expresses Intention to Continue Occupying the Bahrain Islands
Author:
Date: 1957-11-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, 15th (TASS): The Iranian Cabinet met to discuss the bill regarding the division of Iranian administrative regions. The Shah of Iran also attended the meeting. With the Shah's consent, the Cabinet decided to establish four new provinces: Kurdistan, Luristan, Seistan, and Bahrain.

According to the "Knowledge Daily," following the Iranian government's decision to declare the Bahrain Islands as Iran's fourteenth province, the British Chargé d'Affaires in Iran, Russell, visited the Iranian Foreign Minister, Ardalan, and told him that the Bahrain Islands are "under British protection."

### 伊朗宣布巴林为第十四省  英国临时代办表示要继续霸占巴林群岛

1957-11-17
第6版()
专栏：

    伊朗宣布巴林为第十四省
    英国临时代办表示要继续霸占巴林群岛
    据新华社16日讯  塔斯社德黑兰15日讯：伊朗内阁会议讨论了关于伊朗行政区的划分法案。伊朗国王也出席了这个会议。在国王的同意下，内阁决定建立四个新省份，即：库尔德斯坦、卢里斯坦、塞伊斯坦和巴林。
    据“知识报”报道，由于伊朗政府决定宣布巴林群岛为伊朗的第十四个省份，英国驻伊朗临时代办罗素拜会了伊朗外交大臣阿达兰，并且对他说，巴林群岛“受英国的保护”。
