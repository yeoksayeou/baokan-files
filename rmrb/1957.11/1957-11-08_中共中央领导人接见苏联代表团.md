Title: Central Committee Leaders of the CPC Receive Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th: Leaders of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Peng Zhen, and Chen Yi, received this afternoon the Soviet delegation to the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, led by Andrei Andreyevich Andreyev, and delegation members Cherednichenko, Volkova, Andreyeva, Umarov, Bulkina, Kozlovsky, Muradeli, Novgorodsky, and Kislenko, as well as accompanying staff members Kalmanov and Yerofeyev.

Present at the reception were Wu Yuzhang, Qi Yanming, Zeng Yongquan, Zhang Youyu, Ge Baoquan, and Lin Lang.

Also present at the reception was the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, G.F. Goutikov.

### 中共中央领导人接见苏联代表团

1957-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见苏联代表团
    新华社7日讯　中共中央领导人刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、彭真、陈毅，今天下午接见了苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团团长安德烈耶夫和团员切烈德尼钦科、沃尔科娃、安德烈耶娃、乌玛罗夫、布尔金娜、科兹洛夫斯基、穆拉德里、诺夫哥罗德斯基、基斯连科，代表团随行人员卡尔曼诺夫、叶罗费耶夫。
    接见时在座的，有吴玉章、齐燕铭、曾涌泉、张友渔、戈宝权、林朗。
    接见时，苏联驻华大使馆临时代办顾悌克夫也在座。
