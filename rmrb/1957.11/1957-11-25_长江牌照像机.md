Title: Changjiang Brand Camera
Author: Xie Shucheng
Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 3

    Changjiang Brand Camera
    Chongqing Changjiang Electric Factory recently trial-produced its first Changjiang brand camera and is preparing for mass production.
    The Changjiang brand camera is trial-produced in imitation of the Soviet "Смена" (Smena) camera. It is small and exquisite in appearance, beautiful and elegant in style, and convenient to carry, making it suitable for photography enthusiasts and travelers.
                          (Xie Shucheng) (Attached picture)

### 长江牌照像机
谢书城
1957-11-25
第3版()
专栏：

    长江牌照像机
    重庆长江电工厂最近试制出了第一台长江牌照像机，并准备大量生产。
    长江牌照像机是仿照苏联斯密纳（CMEHA）照像机试制的，外形小巧玲珑，式样美观大方，携带便利，适合于摄影爱好者和旅行家们采用。
                          （谢书城）（附图片）
