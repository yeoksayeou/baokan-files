Title: Communiqué of the Meeting of Communist and Workers' Parties
Author:
Date: 1957-11-23
Page: 1

    The delegations of the Communist and Workers' Parties participating in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution decided to take advantage of the opportunity in Moscow to hold a friendly meeting and discuss issues of concern to the various parties.

    A meeting was held in Moscow from November 16 to 19, attended by representatives of the following parties: Communist Party of Australia, Communist Party of Austria, Albanian Labor Party, Algerian Communist Party, Communist Party of Argentina, Communist Party of Belgium, Communist Party of Bolivia, Bulgarian Communist Party, Brazilian Communist Party, Communist Party of Great Britain, Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, Communist Party of Venezuela, Vietnam Workers' Party, Guatemalan Labor Party, German Communist Party, Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Communist Party of Honduras, Communist Party of Greece, Communist Party of Denmark, People's Socialist Party of the Dominican Republic, Communist Party of Israel, Communist Party of India, Communist Party of Indonesia, Communist Party of Jordan, Communist Party of Iraq, Communist Party of Spain, Italian Communist Party, Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, Communist Party of China, Communist Party of Colombia, Korean Workers' Party, People's Vanguard Party of Costa Rica, People's Socialist Party of Cuba, Communist Party of Luxembourg, Malayan Communist Party, Moroccan Communist Party, Mexican Communist Party, Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Communist Party of the Netherlands, Communist Party of New Zealand, Communist Party of Norway, Communist Party of Panama, Communist Party of Paraguay, Communist Party of Peru, Polish United Workers' Party, Portuguese Communist Party, Romanian Workers' Party, Communist Party of San Marino, Communist Party of Syria and Lebanon, Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Communist Party of Thailand, Communist Party of Tunisia, Communist Party of Turkey, Communist Party of Uruguay, Communist Party of Finland, French Communist Party, Communist Party of Ceylon, Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, Communist Party of Chile, Swiss Labor Party, Communist Party of Sweden, Communist Party of Ecuador, League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Communist Party of Japan.

    The meeting participants exchanged views on pressing issues in the current international situation.

    The representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties decided to issue a peace manifesto to the workers and peasants of all countries, to the men and women of the whole world, to all people of goodwill, the full text of which will be published later.

    The meeting was conducted in an atmosphere of close cooperation and sincerity, reflecting the mutual relations of the fraternal parties united by the unity of Marxist-Leninist thought and the principles of proletarian internationalism.

### 共产党和工人党代表会议公报

1957-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    共产党和工人党代表会议公报
    参加庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年的各共产党和工人党代表团，决定利用在莫斯科的机会作一次友好的会见和讨论各党所关心的问题。
    11月16日到19日在莫斯科举行了会议，参加这次会议的有下列党的代表：澳大利亚共产党、奥地利共产党、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、阿尔及利亚共产党、阿根廷共产党、比利时共产党、玻利维亚共产党、保加利亚共产党、巴西共产党、英国共产党、匈牙利社会主义工人党、委内瑞拉共产党、越南劳动党、危地马拉劳动党、德国共产党、德国统一社会党、洪都拉斯共产党、希腊共产党、丹麦共产党、多米尼加共和国人民社会党、以色列共产党、印度共产党、印度尼西亚共产党、约旦共产党、伊拉克共产党、西班牙共产党、意大利共产党、加拿大劳工进步党、中国共产党、哥伦比亚共产党、朝鲜劳动党、哥斯达黎加人民先锋党、古巴人民社会党、卢森堡共产党、马来亚共产党、摩洛哥共产党、墨西哥共产党、蒙古人民革命党、荷兰共产党、新西兰共产党、挪威共产党、巴拿马共产党、巴拉圭共产党、秘鲁共产党、波兰统一工人党、葡萄牙共产党、罗马尼亚工人党、圣马力诺共产党、叙利亚和黎巴嫩共产党、苏联共产党、泰国共产党、突尼斯共产党、土耳其共产党、乌拉圭共产党、芬兰共产党、法国共产党、锡兰共产党、捷克斯洛伐克共产党、智利共产党、瑞士劳动党、瑞典共产党、厄瓜多尔共产党、南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟、日本共产党。
    会议参加者就当前国际局势中的迫切问题交换了意见。
    共产党和工人党的代表决定向各国工人和农民、向全世界的男女们、向一切善良的人们发出和平宣言，全文发表于后。
    会议是在密切合作和真诚的气氛中进行的，这种情况表明了以马克思列宁主义思想的一致，以无产阶级国际主义原则联合在一起的各兄弟党的相互关系。
