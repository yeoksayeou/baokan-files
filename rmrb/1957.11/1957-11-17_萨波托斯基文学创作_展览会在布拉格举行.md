Title: Sapotocky Literary Works Exhibition Held in Prague
Author:
Date: 1957-11-17
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 16th. An exhibition of Sapotocky's literary works, commemorating the literary contributions of the late Czechoslovak President Sapotocky, opened in Prague on the 15th.

### 萨波托斯基文学创作  展览会在布拉格举行

1957-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    萨波托斯基文学创作
    展览会在布拉格举行
    新华社布拉格16日电  为纪念捷克斯洛伐克故总统萨波托斯基在文学上的贡献而举办的萨波托斯基文学创作展览会，15日在布拉格开幕。
