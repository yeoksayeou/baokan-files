Title: Pictures
Author: Lin Hui, Wang Zhiyuan
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 8

    To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, Beijing theatrical workers are staging famous Soviet plays such as "Far Away." The left picture shows a scene from the Soviet drama "Far Away" performed by the Directing Teacher Training Class of the Central Academy of Drama. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Lin Hui).
    The upper right picture shows a scene from "The Storm of Revolution" performed by the Experimental Theater of the Central Academy of Drama; the right picture shows a scene from "A Real Man" performed by the Drama Troupe of the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army. 本报记者 Wang Zhiyuan.
