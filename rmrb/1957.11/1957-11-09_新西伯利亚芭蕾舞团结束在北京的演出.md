Title: Novosibirsk Ballet Troupe Concludes Performances in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 8th -- The ballet troupe of the Soviet State Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theatre gave its final performance in Beijing at the Tianqiao Theatre on the evening of the 8th. Over a thousand Beijing audience members congratulated the success of the performances with repeated, long-lasting, and enthusiastic applause.

The performance tonight was the three-act ballet "Giselle," lasting over two hours. After the performance, the audience stood up and applauded enthusiastically. Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing, and Andreyev, head of the Soviet delegation representing various circles participating in the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, accompanied by Gu Tikefu, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, went to the stage to shake hands and congratulate the actors of the ballet troupe.

The entire British Rambert Ballet troupe, currently visiting Beijing, also went backstage to congratulate the Soviet ballet dancers.

During the intermission, Peng Zhen also received the head of the ballet troupe, Ligachev, and the deputy head, Zelimanov.

Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, and others also watched tonight's performance.

Members of the Soviet delegation representing various circles participating in the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution also watched tonight's performance.

The ballet troupe of the Soviet State Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theatre will leave Beijing on the 14th to visit and perform in Guangzhou. Since October 2nd, they have given a total of thirty-eight performances in Beijing, with an audience of 70,000 people.

### 新西伯利亚芭蕾舞团结束在北京的演出

1957-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    新西伯利亚芭蕾舞团结束在北京的演出
    新华社8日讯　苏联国立新西伯利亚歌剧芭蕾舞剧院芭蕾舞团8日晚在天桥剧场作在北京的最后一次演出。一千多名北京观众以多次长时间的热烈掌声，祝贺这次演出的成功。
    今晚演出的是历时两个多钟头的三幕芭蕾舞剧“吉赛尔”。演出结束后，观众起立热烈鼓掌。全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真和苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团团长安德烈耶夫等人，由苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办顾悌克夫陪同，到舞台上同芭蕾舞剧团的演员们握手祝贺。
    正在北京访问的英国蓝伯特芭蕾舞剧团全体人员也到后台，向苏联芭蕾舞演员们祝贺。
    在演出休息时，彭真还接见了芭蕾舞团团长利加乔夫和副团长泽利马诺夫。
    外交部副部长姬鹏飞，文化部副部长夏衍等人也观看了今晚的演出。
    观看今晚演出的还有苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念代表团的团员们。
    苏联国立新西伯利亚歌剧芭蕾舞剧院芭蕾舞团将在14日离开北京，到广州访问演出。从10月2日以来，他们在北京共演出了三十八场，观众达七万人。
