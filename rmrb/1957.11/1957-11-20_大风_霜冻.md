Title: Gale · Frost

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-20
Page: 8

　　Gale · Frost
　　This newspaper reports that the Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a gale and frost forecast at 12:00 noon on the 19th: Affected by southward-moving cold air, in the next 24 hours, the Huai River basin and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River basin will experience northerly winds of force 5-6. The Yellow Sea, East China Sea, and Taiwan Strait will experience northerly winds of force 6-7. The wind force in the Bohai Sea and Yellow River basin will gradually weaken.
　　Frost will appear in the Huai River basin on the mornings of the 20th and 21st.

### 大风·霜冻

1957-11-20
第8版()
专栏：气象台

    大风·霜冻
    本报讯　中央气象科学研究所19日中午十二时发布大风霜冻预告：受南下冷空气影响，未来二十四小时内，淮河流域、长江流域中下游将有五、六级的偏北风，黄海、东海、台湾海峡将有六、七级的偏北风，渤海、黄河流域的风力将逐渐减弱。
    20日和21日早晨淮河流域将有霜冻出现。
