Title: Exhibition of Russian Classical Paintings Opens in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th: An "18th-20th Century Russian Painting Exhibition" opened on the morning of November 5th at the Cultural Hall of the Soviet Exhibition Center in Beijing.

　　The exhibition displays sixty-three original oil paintings by forty-five famous painters, attracting great interest from the audience. Many viewers stood for a long time in front of the original paintings by familiar artists such as Repin, appreciating their works. These original works are part of the collection of the Leningrad State Russian Museum in the Soviet Union.

　　At the opening ceremony of the exhibition, Liu Zhiming, Vice Minister of Culture, and V.V. Vilishchagin, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, spoke.

　　Puskarev, Director of the Leningrad State Russian Museum in the Soviet Union, who came to China to prepare for this exhibition, also spoke.

　　The exhibition also introduced the famous Soviet Leningrad State Russian Museum through one hundred and thirty-six pictures. This museum, built between 1819 and 1825, now has 250,000 exhibits.

### 北京展出俄罗斯古典绘画作品

1957-11-06
第8版()
专栏：

    北京展出俄罗斯古典绘画作品
    据新华社5日讯　“十八—二十世纪俄罗斯绘画展览会”，5日上午在北京苏联展览馆文化馆开始展出。
    展览会展出的四十五位著名画家的六十三幅油画原作，引起了观众极大兴趣。许多观众长时间站在他们所熟悉的画家列宾等的原画前，欣赏他们的作品。这些原作是苏联列宁格勒国立俄罗斯博物馆收藏的一部分。
    在展览会的开幕式上，文化部副部长刘芝明和苏联驻中国大使馆参赞维利夏金先后讲了话。
    来我国筹备这次展览会的苏联列宁格勒国立俄罗斯博物馆馆长普斯卡列夫也讲了话。
    展览会还通过一百三十六幅图片对著名的苏联列宁格勒国立俄罗斯博物馆作了介绍。这个建于1819到1825年的博物馆，现在拥有二十五万件展品。
