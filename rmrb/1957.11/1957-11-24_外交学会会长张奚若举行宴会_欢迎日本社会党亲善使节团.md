Title: President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Xiruo, Holds Banquet to Welcome Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission
Author:
Date: 1957-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet on the evening of the 23rd to welcome Yoshio Suzuki, head of the Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission, and all members of the delegation.

Zhang Xiruo spoke at the banquet, welcoming the Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission. He said that the visit of Japanese friends to China would help enhance understanding and friendship between the Chinese and Japanese peoples. The Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs has had close contact with friends from the Japanese Socialist Party in recent years. This visit of the mission will definitely contribute to enhancing the understanding and friendship between the two peoples.

Yoshio Suzuki then spoke, saying that the mission would contribute to the early restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China, in accordance with the spirit of the resolution of the Japanese Socialist Party Congress, and would work to eliminate obstacles to the restoration of diplomatic relations and the promotion of trade between Japan and China.

At the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the friendship between the Chinese and Japanese peoples and the early restoration of diplomatic relations between China and Japan.

Those present at the banquet included Qiao Guanhua, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Wu Defeng, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Association, and Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation.

### 外交学会会长张奚若举行宴会  欢迎日本社会党亲善使节团

1957-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    外交学会会长张奚若举行宴会
    欢迎日本社会党亲善使节团
    新华社23日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若在23日晚间举行宴会，欢迎日本社会党亲善使节团团长铃木义男和全体团员。
    张奚若在宴会上讲话，欢迎日本社会党亲善使节团。他说，日本朋友们的来中国访问，有助于增进中日两国人民的了解和增进中日两国人民的友谊。中国人民外交学会在这几年来同日本社会党的朋友们有着密切的接触，这次使节团来中国访问，定将对增进两国人民的了解和两国人民的友谊作出贡献。
    铃木义男接着讲话说，使节团将按照日本社会党大会决议中促进早日恢复日中邦交的精神，在这一方面贡献力量，并且为消除恢复日中邦交和促进日中贸易的障碍而努力。
    在宴会上，宾主们一再为中日两国人民友好和中日邦交早日恢复而干杯。
    出席宴会作陪的，有中国人民外交学会副会长乔冠华，中国红十字会总会会长李德全，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民，中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰，中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠等人。
