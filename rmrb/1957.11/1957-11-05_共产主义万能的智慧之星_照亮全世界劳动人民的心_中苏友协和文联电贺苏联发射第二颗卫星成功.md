Title: The Omnipotent Star of Wisdom of Communism Illuminates the Hearts of the Working People of the World; Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Literary and Art Circles Send Telegrams Congratulating the Soviet Union on the Successful Launch of the Second Satellite
Author:
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 4th. Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, sent a telegram on the 4th to Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, and Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of the second artificial earth satellite.

The telegram stated that as the Chinese people and the working people of the world are jubilantly celebrating the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, the Soviet Union has successfully launched the second artificial earth satellite, adding immense excitement and joy to this great festival.

The successful launch of the Soviet artificial earth satellite once again proves that the October Socialist Revolution not only brought about fundamental changes in the political and material lives of mankind but also brought about an unprecedentedly brilliant prospect for mankind's cultural and scientific endeavors. At the same time, this extraordinary achievement vividly demonstrates the superiority of the socialist system and the tremendous growth of the Soviet Union's overall national strength. People fully understand the great significance of the Soviet Union's prosperity and strength in safeguarding world peace.

On behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, we extend our warm congratulations to all members of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, all Soviet scientific workers, and all Soviet people with incomparable excitement.

Xinhua News Agency, November 4th. The China Federation of Literary and Art Circles sent a telegram on the 4th to the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and all the Soviet people, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of the second artificial earth satellite.

The telegram stated that just as the working people of the world are jubilantly celebrating the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the Soviet Union's second fully equipped "Red Moon" has successfully flown into the "highest heavens." This star, symbolizing the omnipotent wisdom of communism, illuminates the hearts of the working people of the world, severely strikes warmongers, and more vividly demonstrates the beginning of a new era for mankind to further conquer nature. This thrilling good news that has spread throughout the world makes us feel extremely excited. We Chinese literary and art workers warmly and sincerely congratulate the Soviet scientists and engineering technicians and pay our highest respect to the Soviet people, the Soviet government, and the Soviet Communist Party. (Attached picture)

Beijing Observatory staff are preparing multiple observation telescopes to observe the second artificial satellite. Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jilu.

### 共产主义万能的智慧之星  照亮全世界劳动人民的心  中苏友协和文联电贺苏联发射第二颗卫星成功

1957-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    共产主义万能的智慧之星  照亮全世界劳动人民的心
    中苏友协和文联电贺苏联发射第二颗卫星成功
    新华社4日讯　中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，秘书长钱俊瑞4日致电苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫和苏联科学院院长?斯米扬诺夫，祝贺苏联发射第二颗人造地球卫星成功。
    电文说，当中国人民和全世界劳动人民一道，正在欢欣鼓舞庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的高潮声中，苏联又成功地发射了第二颗人造地球卫星，给人们在这个伟大的节日中增添了莫大的兴奋和喜悦。
    苏联人造地球卫星的又一次发射成功，再度证明了十月社会主义革命不仅使人类的政治和物质生活起了根本的变化，并且使人类的文化科学事业出现了空前光辉灿烂的远景。同时，这个非凡的成就，也生动地显示了社会主义制度的优越性和苏联整个国力的巨大增长。人们完全了解，苏联的繁荣和强大，对于保卫世界和平事业具有何等重大的意义。
    我们谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员，以无比兴奋的心情，向苏中友好协会全体成员和苏联全体科学工作者以及全体苏联人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    新华社4日讯　中国文学艺术界联合会4日致电苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联全体人民，祝贺苏联发射第二颗人造地球卫星成功。
    电文说，正当全世界劳动人民欢腾庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的时候，苏联第二个装备齐全的“红色月亮”又成功地飞于“九重天上”。这颗象征着共产主义万能的智慧之星，照亮了全世界劳动人民的心，狠狠地打击了好战分子，更鲜明地展示了人类进一步征服自然界的新纪元的开始。这一传遍全世界的动人心弦的喜讯，使我们感到万分兴奋。我们中国文学艺术工作者，热烈地衷心地向苏联科学家和工程技术人员祝贺并向苏联人民、苏联政府和苏联共产党致以崇高的敬意。（附图片）
    北京天文台的工作人员正在准备多架观测镜，以便观测第二颗人造卫星。　新华社记者　李基禄摄
