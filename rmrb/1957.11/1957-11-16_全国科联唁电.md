Title: All-China Federation of Scientific Societies Telegram of Condolence

Author:

Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 4

All-China Federation of Scientific Societies Telegram of Condolence to the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic:

We are deeply saddened to learn of the passing of the esteemed President Zápotocký due to overwork. This is not only a loss for the people of Czechoslovakia but also for the working people of the world. We, on behalf of the scientific and technological workers of the People's Republic of China, express our deepest condolences.

            All-China Federation of Natural
                      Science Societies
                      November 14, 1957
