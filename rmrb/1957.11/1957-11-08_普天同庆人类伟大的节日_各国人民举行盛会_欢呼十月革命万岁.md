Title: People All Over the World Celebrate the Great Festival of Mankind: People of All Countries Hold Grand Gatherings, Cheering "Long Live the October Revolution!"
Author:
Date: 1957-11-08
Page: 4

Comprehensive report from our newspaper based on Xinhua News Agency dispatches: The atmosphere of commemorating the October Revolution Day permeates the world, and people of all countries have entered a celebration craze.

Korea
　　More than 150,000 people in Pyongyang held a grand celebration meeting in Kim Il Sung Square on the morning of the 7th. Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Hong Myung-hui, Park Ui-wan, Jeong Il-ryong, Jeong Jun-taek, Deputy Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul and other party and government leaders, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to North Korea attended the meeting.
　　Vice Premier Kim Il of the Korean Cabinet, Soviet Ambassador to North Korea Puzanov, and laboring people from all walks of life spoke at the meeting.

Vietnam
　　More than 9,000 people from all walks of life in Vietnam held a grand gathering at the Hanoi People's Theater on the evening of the 6th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.
　　The presidium of the meeting included 31 people, including Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam, Truong Chinh, Secretary-General of the Labor Party, Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, Soviet Ambassador Dyminyan, and Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee.
　　After the band played the national anthems of the Soviet Union and Vietnam, Ton Duc Thang delivered the opening speech.
　　Nguyen Duy Trinh, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, then gave a long report.

Czechoslovakia
　　People in Prague held a grand rally in Old Town Square on the afternoon of the 6th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. Czechoslovak party and state leaders Fierlinger, Kopetsky, Šimůnek, etc., and Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Grishin attended the meeting.
　　Kopetsky, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, delivered a report at the meeting.

Bulgaria
　　Representatives from all walks of life in Bulgaria and the laboring people of Sofia held a grand gathering in Sofia on the evening of the 6th to celebrate the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution.
　　Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Raiko Damyanov, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Fatherland Front, Staykov, and other party and government leaders, as well as Soviet Ambassador to Bulgaria, Prijodov, and diplomatic envoys from other countries attended the celebration meeting.
　　Damyanov gave a report on the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

Mongolia
　　80,000 working people in the Mongolian capital held a rally and parade on the morning of the 7th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.
　　At ten o'clock in the morning, the celebration meeting began. Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, delivered a congratulatory message at the meeting.

Yugoslavia
　　On the evening of November 6, Belgrade held a celebration meeting for the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. Attending the celebration meeting were Yugoslav state and party leaders Stambolic, Vukmanovic, Djurakovic, Gosnjak, etc., as well as representatives from all walks of life and diplomatic envoys from various countries, totaling about 2,000 people.
　　Velinasev, member of the Central Executive Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia and Chairman of the Serbian National Assembly, gave a report on the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.

India
　　The Delhi State Committee of the Communist Party of India organized a mass meeting in Shadara, near Delhi, on the evening of the 5th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.
　　Farooqi, a Communist leader in Delhi, addressed the meeting.

Indonesia
　　The Indonesia-Soviet Union Cultural Relations Association held a grand evening party on the evening of the 6th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. Among the 3,000 guests attending the evening party were Indonesian Minister of Education and Culture Priyono, Air Force Chief of Staff Major General Suryadama, representatives of the Army Chief of Staff, members of parliament, General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party Aidit, and Deputy General Secretary Yodo.
　　Also attending the evening party were Chinese Ambassador Huang Zhen and ambassadors from other socialist countries.

### 普天同庆人类伟大的节日  各国人民举行盛会　欢呼十月革命万岁

1957-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    普天同庆人类伟大的节日
    各国人民举行盛会　欢呼十月革命万岁
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：纪念十月革命节的气氛弥漫全世界，各国人民已进入庆祝热潮。朝鲜
    平壤市十五万以上的群众7日上午在金日成广场隆重举行庆祝大会。朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健，内阁副首相洪命熹、朴义琓、郑一龙、郑准泽，劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金哲等党和政府的领导人和各国驻朝鲜使节参加了大会。
    朝鲜内阁副首相金一、苏联驻朝鲜大使普扎诺夫和各界劳动人民在大会上讲了话。越南
    越南各界人民九千多人6日晚上在河内人民剧场举行盛大集会，庆祝十月革命四十周年。
    大会的主席团包括越南国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜、劳动党书记长征、总理范文同、苏联大使季米亚宁和河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴等三十一人。
    在乐队演奏苏越两国国歌后，孙德胜致开幕词。
    越南劳动党中央政治局委员阮维桢接着作了长篇的报告。捷克斯洛伐克
    布拉格人民6日下午在旧城广场举行盛大集会，庆祝十月革命四十周年。捷克斯洛伐克党和国家领导人费林格、柯别茨基、西穆湼克等以及苏联驻捷克斯洛伐克大使格里申都参加了会议。
    捷共中央政治局委员柯别茨基在会上作了报告。
  保加利亚保加利亚社会各界代表和索非亚的劳动人民6日晚间在索非亚举行盛大集会庆祝十月革命四十周年。
    保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员、部长会议第一副主席拉·达米扬诺夫、祖国阵线全国委员会常务委员会主席斯塔伊科夫和其他党政负责人，以及苏联驻保加利亚大使普里霍多夫和其他国家的外交使节都出席了庆祝大会。
    达米扬诺夫作了关于十月社会主义革命四十周年的报告。蒙古
    蒙古首都八万劳动人民7日上午举行集会和游行，庆祝十月革命四十周年。
    上午十时，庆祝大会开始。蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布在会上致了节日的祝词。南斯拉夫
    11月6日晚间，贝尔格莱德举行了十月革命四十周年庆祝大会。出席庆祝大会的有南斯拉夫国家和党的领导人斯坦鲍利奇、伏克曼诺维奇、乔拉柯维奇、戈什尼亚克等，以及各界代表、各国外交使节共约二千人。
    南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央执行委员会委员、塞尔维亚国民议会主席维林塞诺夫在会上作了关于十月革命四十周年的报告。印度
    印度共产党德里邦委员会5日晚间在德里近郊沙达拉组织了一个庆祝十月革命四十周年的群众大会。
    德里的共产党领袖法鲁基在会上致了词。印度尼西亚
    印度尼西亚—苏联文化关系协会6日晚上举行盛大晚会庆祝十月革命四十周年。在参加晚会的三千来宾中有印度尼西亚文教部长普里约诺、空军参谋长苏里亚达马少将、陆军参谋长的代表、国会议员、印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地和副总书记约多。
    参加晚会的还有中国大使黄镇和其他社会主义国家的大使。
