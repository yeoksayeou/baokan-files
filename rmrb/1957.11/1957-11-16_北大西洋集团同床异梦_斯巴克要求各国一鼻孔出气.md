Title: North Atlantic Group's Discord: Spaak Demands Unified Voice
Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, November 15th: North Atlantic Treaty Organization Secretary-General Spaak told parliamentarians from fifteen countries attending the group's parliamentary meeting on the 14th that he believes the group's countries must coordinate their foreign policies in order for the group to be "saved."

Spaak said that in order to formulate a common foreign policy, the foreign policies of the group's large and small partners must undergo "significant adjustments" and consultations must be conducted in advance.

### 北大西洋集团同床异梦  斯巴克要求各国一鼻孔出气

1957-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    北大西洋集团同床异梦
    斯巴克要求各国一鼻孔出气
    据新华社15日讯　巴黎消息：北大西洋公约组织秘书长斯巴克14日向参加这个集团议员会议的十五个国家议员说，他认为这个集团各国要协调它们的外交政策，这样这个集团才会“得救”。
    斯巴克说，要制订共同的外交政策，这个集团的各大小伙伴的外交政策就都要作“重大的调整”，要事先进行协商。
