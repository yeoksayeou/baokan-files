Title: The coffin of Tofik Yazd departs from Tianjin for Syria

Author: 
Date: 1957-11-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 12th. The coffin of Mr. Tofik Yazd, head of the Syrian Journalists Delegation to China and Director of the Information Department of the Syrian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was transported from Beijing to Tianjin Xingang on the 12th, and shipped back to Syria on the "Paul Lickmai" ship.

    Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, Wang Yi, Secretary-General, Shao Hongye, President of the Tianjin Journalists Association, and heads of various news organizations in Tianjin went to Tianjin Xingang to accompany the coffin onto the ship. Adib Shalan, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Syrian Embassy in China, also accompanied the coffin onto the ship.

### 托菲克·雅兹的灵柩从天津启运回叙利亚

1957-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    托菲克·雅兹的灵柩从天津启运回叙利亚
    新华社天津12日电　叙利亚新闻工作者访华代表团团长、叙利亚外交部新闻司司长托菲克·雅兹先生的灵柩，12日从北京运到天津新港，由“保罗利克麦”号轮船启运回叙利亚。
    中国新闻工作者协会副会长王芸生、秘书长王揖，天津市新闻工作者协会会长邵红叶和天津各新闻单位的负责人前往天津新港陪送灵柩上船。陪送灵柩上船的还有叙利亚驻中国大使馆临时代办阿迪卜·沙蓝。
