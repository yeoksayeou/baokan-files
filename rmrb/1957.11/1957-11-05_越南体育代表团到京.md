Title: Vietnamese Sports Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, November 4th. The sports delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, invited by the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by train tonight. They will visit Beijing, Shenyang, Tianjin, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other cities for a period of one and a half months.
    The sports delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam consists of nine members. The head of the delegation, Wang Biwang, has arrived in Beijing earlier.

### 越南体育代表团到京

1957-11-05
第2版()
专栏：

    越南体育代表团到京
    新华社4日讯　应体育运动委员会邀请来我国访问的越南民主共和国体育代表团，今晚坐火车到达北京。他们将在北京、沈阳、天津、广州、武汉等城市作为时一个半月的访问。
    越南民主共和国体育代表团由九人组成，团长王碧旺已先期到达北京。
