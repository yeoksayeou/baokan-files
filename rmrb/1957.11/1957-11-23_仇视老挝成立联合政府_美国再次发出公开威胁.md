Title: Opposing the Establishment of a Coalition Government in Laos, the United States Issues Another Public Threat
Author:
Date: 1957-11-23
Page: 5

    Hating the Establishment of a Lao Coalition Government
    The United States Issues Another Public Threat
    Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd dispatch from Washington: U.S. government officials issued another threat on the 21st, saying that the United States hopes that the "strongly worded statement" issued by the U.S. State Department regarding the establishment of a coalition government in Laos "will prompt the Lao Royal Government to recognize the dangers of this approach." These U.S. officials said that if Laos "moves in the wrong direction," the United States will reconsider its "aid" to Laos.
    U.S. State Department spokesman White, in a statement to reporters on the 20th, said that the United States "is deeply concerned" about the establishment of a coalition government in Laos, thereby achieving national unity.

### 仇视老挝成立联合政府  美国再次发出公开威胁

1957-11-23
第5版()
专栏：

    仇视老挝成立联合政府
    美国再次发出公开威胁
    新华社22日讯　华盛顿消息：美国政府官员21日再次发出威胁说，美国希望美国国务院就老挝成立联合政府这件事发表的“措辞强硬的声明”，“会促使老挝王国政府认识到这一做法的危险”。这些美国官员说，如果老挝“走向错误的方向”，美国将重新考虑它对老挝的“援助”。
    美国国务院发言人怀特在20日曾对新闻记者发表声明说，美国对老挝成立联合政府从而实现国家统一这件事“感到严重的忧虑”。
