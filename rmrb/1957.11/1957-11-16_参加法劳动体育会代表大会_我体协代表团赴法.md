Title: Attending the French Labor Sports Federation Congress, Our Sports Association Delegation Goes to France
Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 5

    Attending the French Labor Sports Federation Congress
    Our Sports Association Delegation Goes to France
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, November 14th: The delegation of the All-China Sports Federation, invited to attend the 13th Congress of the French Labor Sports Federation, arrived in Paris on the 13th. The head of our delegation is Li Shubin, and the members are Cai Jintao and Wang Yugui.
    This conference is held in Strasbourg. Sports delegations from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Democratic Germany, Hungary, Italy, the Netherlands, and Belgium, as well as representatives from the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the World Workers Sports Federation, are also invited to attend the conference.
    The French Labor Sports Federation was established in 1934 and is the largest and oldest workers' sports organization in France, maintaining close ties with sports organizations in many countries.

### 参加法劳动体育会代表大会  我体协代表团赴法

1957-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    参加法劳动体育会代表大会
    我体协代表团赴法
    新华社巴黎14日电　应邀参加法国劳动体育联合会第十三次代表大会的中华全国体育总会代表团13日到达巴黎。我国代表团团长为栗树彬，团员为蔡锦涛及王玉贵。
    这次大会在斯特拉斯堡举行，应邀参加大会的还有苏、捷、民主德国、匈、意、荷、比等国的体育代表团和世界民主青联、世界工人体育联合会的代表。
    法国劳动体育联合会成立于1934年，是法国影响最大、历史最久的工人体育组织，和许多国家的体育组织保持着密切联系。
