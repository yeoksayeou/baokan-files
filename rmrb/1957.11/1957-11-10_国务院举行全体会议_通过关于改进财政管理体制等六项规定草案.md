Title: State Council Holds Plenary Session, Approves Draft Regulations on Improving the Financial Management System and Six Other Items
Author:
Date: 1957-11-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The State Council held its sixty-first plenary meeting on the afternoon of November 8.
    The meeting discussed and approved: the State Council's regulations on improving the financial management system (draft), the State Council's regulations on improving the industrial management system (draft), the State Council's regulations on improving the commercial management system (draft), the State Council's interim regulations on the vacation and wage benefits for workers and staff returning home to visit relatives (draft), the State Council's interim regulations on the length of apprenticeship and living allowances for apprentices in state-owned, joint state-private, cooperative-run, and individually-operated enterprises and institutions (draft), and the State Council's interim regulations on the wage benefits for ordinary workers and odd-job laborers in enterprises, institutions, and state organs (draft). The meeting decided to submit these drafts to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation.

### 国务院举行全体会议  通过关于改进财政管理体制等六项规定草案

1957-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    通过关于改进财政管理体制等六项规定草案
    新华社讯　国务院8日下午举行了第六十一次全体会议。
    会议讨论和通过了：国务院关于改进财政管理体制的规定（草案），国务院关于改进工业管理体制的规定（草案），国务院关于改进商业管理体制的规定（草案），国务院关于工人、职员回家探亲的假期和工资待遇的暂行规定（草案），国务院关于国营、公私合营、合作社营、个体经营的企业和事业单位的学徒的学习期限和生活补贴的暂行规定（草案），国务院关于企业、事业和国家机关中普通工和勤杂工的工资待遇的暂行规定（草案）。会议决定将这些草案提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。
