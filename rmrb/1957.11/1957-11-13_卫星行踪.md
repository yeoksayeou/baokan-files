Title: Satellite's Trajectory
Author:
Date: 1957-11-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th -- According to TASS, by 6:00 AM Moscow time on November 12th, the second artificial satellite had completed one hundred and twenty-four orbits around the Earth. The first artificial satellite and its carrier rocket had completed five hundred and seventy-six and five hundred and seventy-nine orbits around the Earth respectively. In clear weather, the second artificial satellite can be seen with the naked eye before sunrise between 54 and 68 degrees north latitude, and after sunset between 30 and 70 degrees south latitude.

### 卫星行踪

1957-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    卫星行踪
    据新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：到11月12日莫斯科时间早上六点为止，第二个人造卫星已经绕地球转了一百二十四圈。第一个人造卫星及其运载火箭已经分别绕地球转了五百七十六圈和五百七十九圈。天气晴朗时，北纬五十四度到六十八度之间在日出以前，南纬三十度到七十度之间在日落以后，用肉眼可以看到第二个人造卫星。
