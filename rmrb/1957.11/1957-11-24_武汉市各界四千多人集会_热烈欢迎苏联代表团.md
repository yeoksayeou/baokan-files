Title: More Than 4,000 People From All Walks of Life in Wuhan Gathered to Warmly Welcome the Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 22nd - More than 4,000 people from all walks of life in Wuhan gathered at the Wuhan Gymnasium tonight to welcome the Soviet delegation from all walks of life, led by Andrei Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, to participate in the Chinese people's celebration of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Revolution.

Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Li Erzhong, President of the Wuhan Municipal Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, attended the meeting tonight. Soviet experts in Wuhan also attended the meeting.

Liao Chengzhi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Cao Mengjun, Director, and Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General, who accompanied the distinguished guests to Wuhan, and Jakov, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, also attended the meeting.

In his speech at the meeting, Andreyev elaborated on the great achievements made in the 40 years since the October Socialist Revolution and the superiority of the Soviet system. He said that during their stay in China, the delegation was deeply moved by the sincere friendship and love of the Chinese people for the Soviet people. He also praised the indomitable spirit shown by the Chinese people in building socialism.

Umarov, member of the delegation and President of the Academy of Sciences of the Tajik Republic, and Muradeli, member of the delegation, composer of the song "Moscow-Beijing," also spoke at the meeting.

The speeches of the distinguished guests were repeatedly greeted with thunderous applause.

At the beginning of the meeting, Wang Renzhong delivered a welcome speech. On behalf of the people of Hubei Province and Wuhan City, he expressed a warm welcome to all members of the delegation headed by Andreyev and expressed his gratitude for the great Soviet assistance in China's construction. Wang Renzhong said that the tremendous construction achievements in Wuhan over the past eight years, and the early completion and opening of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, are inseparable from the great and selfless assistance of the Soviet Union.

After the meeting, songs, dances, and acrobatics were performed.

The distinguished guests also toured the East Lake scenic area and the urban area of Wuhan today, and visited the Wuhan Sino-Soviet Friendship Palace and the Yangtze River Bridge. At the Hanyang bridgehead of the Yangtze River Bridge, the distinguished guests met with more than 1,000 workers, and a group of female workers pinned commemorative badges of the bridge on the chests of the distinguished guests.

### 武汉市各界四千多人集会  热烈欢迎苏联代表团

1957-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    武汉市各界四千多人集会
    热烈欢迎苏联代表团
    新华社武汉22日电　武汉市各界四千多人今晚在武汉体育馆集会，欢迎以苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫为首的苏联各界参加中国人民庆祝伟大十月革命四十周年纪念代表团。
    中共湖北省委第一书记王任重、湖北省省长张体学、中共武汉市委第一书记宋侃夫、武汉市中苏友好协会会长李尔重、武汉市市长刘惠农参加了今晚的大会。参加大会的还有在武汉的苏联专家。
    陪同贵宾们来到武汉的中苏友好协会总会副会长廖承志、理事曹孟君、副秘书长戈宝权和苏联驻华大使馆一等秘书贾柯夫也参加了大会。
    安德烈耶夫在大会讲话中阐述了十月社会主义革命四十年来取得的伟大成就和苏维埃制度的优越性。他说，在中国逗留期间，使代表团深受感动的是中国人民对苏联人民的真挚友情和热爱。他并赞扬了中国人民在建设社会主义事业中所表现出的不屈不挠的精神。
    代表团团员、塔吉克共和国科学院院长乌玛罗夫，团员、歌曲“莫斯科—北京”作者、作曲家穆拉德里也在大会上讲了话。
    贵宾们的讲话一再获得雷鸣般的掌声。
    在大会开始时，王任重致欢迎词，他代表湖北省和武汉市人民向以安德烈耶夫为首的代表团全体人员表示热烈欢迎，对伟大苏联援助我国建设表示感谢。王任重说，武汉地区八年来各方面的巨大建设成就，武汉长江大桥能够提前建成通车，都是和苏联伟大的、无私的援助分不开的。
    大会结束后，演出了歌舞和杂技。
    贵宾们今天还游览了武汉东湖风景区和市区，并参观了武汉中苏友好宫和长江大桥。在长江大桥汉阳桥头，贵宾们同一千多职工会了面，一群女职工把大桥纪念章佩在贵宾们的胸前。
