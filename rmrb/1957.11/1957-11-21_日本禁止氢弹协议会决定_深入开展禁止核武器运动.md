Title: Japan Anti-Hydrogen Bomb Council Decides to Deepen the Movement to Ban Nuclear Weapons
Author:
Date: 1957-11-21
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs decided at a meeting of its standing directors yesterday afternoon to actively carry out activities at home and abroad to further promote the movement to ban nuclear weapons.
    According to the decision of the meeting, the Council will call on all political parties to support the demand for an immediate and unconditional ban on nuclear weapons when the Diet convenes in December; the Council will also send Council Director Ikuo Yasui and others to participate in the Afro-Asian Solidarity Conference to be held in Cairo on December 26 to call for a comprehensive ban on nuclear weapons and oppose other countries transporting nuclear weapons to Asian and African countries and other activities in preparation for atomic warfare.
    The meeting also decided to launch activities at home and abroad in the spring of next year that are larger in scale than the World Unified Action Day for the Prohibition of Nuclear Weapons in November of this year, and to prepare to hold the Fourth World Conference for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs in an Asian-African country in August next year.

### 日本禁止氢弹协议会决定  深入开展禁止核武器运动

1957-11-21
第5版()
专栏：

    日本禁止氢弹协议会决定
    深入开展禁止核武器运动
    新华社20日讯　日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会在昨天下午举行的常任理事会会议上，决定在国内外积极展开活动，来进一步开展禁止核武器运动。
    根据会议的决定，协议会将于12月国会开会的时候呼吁所有政党支持立即无条件禁止核武器的要求；协议会还将派遣协议会理事长安井郁等人参加将在12月26日在开罗举行的亚非团结会议，呼吁全面禁止核武器，反对其他国家向亚洲和非洲国家运进核武器等准备原子战争的活动。
    会议还决定在明年春季要在国内和国外发动比今年11月的禁止核武器世界统一行动日规模更大的活动，并且准备在明年8月在一个亚非国家中举行第四届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会。
