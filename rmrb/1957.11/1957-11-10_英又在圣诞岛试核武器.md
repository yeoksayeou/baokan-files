Title: Britain Again Tests Nuclear Weapons on Christmas Island
Author:
Date: 1957-11-10
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch from London: The British Ministry of Supply announced on the 8th that Britain had conducted another nuclear weapon explosion test in the Christmas Island test area in the Central Pacific on the 8th. This marks the beginning of the second batch of tests announced by Britain not long ago this year. This summer, Britain exploded three hydrogen bombs in a series of tests on Christmas Island.

### 英又在圣诞岛试核武器

1957-11-10
第6版()
专栏：

    英又在圣诞岛试核武器
    新华社9日讯　伦敦消息：英国军需供应部8日宣布，英国8日在中太平洋圣诞岛试验区上空又进行了一次核武器爆炸试验。这是英国不久前宣布的今年第二批试验的开始。今年夏季英国曾在圣诞岛的一连串试验中爆炸了三颗氢弹。
