Title: Our Military Delegation Continues Visit to the Soviet Union: Returns to Moscow After Visiting Kiev and Tbilisi
Author:
Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 24th: The Chinese military goodwill delegation led by Marshal Peng Dehuai has returned to Moscow on the 23rd after visiting Kiev and Tbilisi.

When the delegation arrived in Kiev on the 19th, they were welcomed at the airport by the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic, Marshal Chuikov, Commander of the Kiev Military District, leading party and government officials of Kiev, and representatives of the working people. The delegation reviewed the guard of honor and received flowers presented by students of the Suvorov Military School.

On the same day, the delegation paid a visit to Marshal Chuikov. Marshal Chuikov warmly welcomed the delegation from the People's Liberation Army of China and hosted a banquet for the delegation members.

After the banquet, the delegation, accompanied by Marshal Chuikov, visited an aviation unit. The delegation members spoke with the pilots and praised the unit's sophisticated equipment and the pilots' skills.

On this day, the delegation also visited Kiev's cityscape and cultural monuments. In the evening, they attended a soiree held at the House of Officers.

The delegation left Kiev by plane on the 20th and arrived in Tbilisi on the morning of the 21st. At the airport, the delegation was warmly welcomed by Mzhavanadze, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Georgia, Javakhishvili, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Georgian Soviet Socialist Republic, Marshal Rokossovsky, Commander of the Transcaucasian Military District, and others.

In the afternoon of the same day, Marshal Rokossovsky hosted a banquet for the delegation. Afterwards, the delegation visited the cityscape. In the evening, the Georgian Folk Dance Troupe and the Military District Song and Dance Troupe held a soiree to entertain the delegation. The delegation left Tbilisi on the 22nd.

After returning to Moscow, the delegation visited the Artillery Academy and will continue its visit in Moscow.

### 我军事代表团继续在苏访问  参观了基辅和第比利斯后回到莫斯科

1957-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团继续在苏访问
    参观了基辅和第比利斯后回到莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科24日电　由彭德怀元帅率领的中国军事友好访苏代表团在参观了基辅和第比利斯以后，已经在23日回到莫斯科。
    代表团在19日到达基辅时，在机场上受到了乌克兰部长会议第一副主席、基辅军区司令崔可夫元帅，基辅的党政领导人员以及劳动人民代表的欢迎。代表团检阅了仪仗队，并且接受了苏沃洛夫军事学校学生的献花。
    当天，代表团拜会了崔可夫元帅。崔可夫元帅对来自人民中国军队的代表团表示热烈的欢迎，并且设宴招待了代表团人员。
    宴会结束后，代表团在崔可夫元帅陪同下前往参观了某航空部队，代表团人员同飞行员们谈了话，他们对部队装备的精良和飞行员的技术极为称赞。
    这一天，代表团还参观了基辅市容和文化古迹，晚间出席了在军官之家举办的晚会。
    代表团在20日乘飞机离开基辅，在21日晨到达第比利斯。在机场上，代表团受到格鲁吉亚共产党中央委员会第一书记姆日阿瓦纳泽，格鲁吉亚部长会议主席贾瓦希什维利，南高加索军区司令罗科索夫斯基元帅等人的热烈欢迎。
    当天下午，罗科索夫斯基元帅设宴招待代表团。随后，代表团参观了市容。晚上，格鲁吉亚民间舞蹈团和军区歌舞团举行晚会，招待了代表团。代表团在22日离开第比利斯。
    代表团回到莫斯科后，参观了炮兵学院，并将继续在莫斯科参观访问。
