Title: Indonesian Maluku Art Troupe Holds Closing Performance in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 12th: The Indonesian Maluku Art Troupe held its closing performance at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in Guangzhou on the evening of November 12th.

　　Before the performance, Sain Nasution, head of the art troupe and Secretary-General of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association, spoke at the closing ceremony. He emphasized the friendly relations between Indonesia and China and praised China's peaceful construction. He said that as everyone knows, the Maluku Islands are the front line for the liberation of West Irian, and the struggle of the Chinese people for the liberation of Taiwan and the struggle of the Indonesian people for the liberation of West Irian are the same.

　　Yang Kanghua, Vice President of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, thanked the art troupe for bringing friendship and rich and colorful dance art in his speech. He also said that the Chinese people will resolutely support the Indonesian people's struggle to liberate West Irian until victory.

　　The art troupe, a group of thirty-nine people, arrived in Guangzhou from Hangzhou on the 11th. The art troupe has visited and performed in Beijing, Shenyang, Anshan, Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places, and has been warmly welcomed by tens of thousands of Chinese audiences.

　　The art troupe is scheduled to leave Guangzhou for home on the 14th.

### 印度尼西亚马鲁古艺术团在广州举行闭幕演出

1957-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚马鲁古艺术团在广州举行闭幕演出
    新华社广州12日电　印度尼西亚马鲁古艺术团12日晚在广州中山纪念堂举行闭幕演出。
    演出前，艺术团团长、印度尼西亚—中国友好协会总会秘书长赛因·纳苏蒂安在闭幕式上讲了话。他强调了印度尼西亚和中国之间的友好关系，并称赞中国的和平建设。他说，如众所知，马鲁古群岛是解放西伊里安的前线，中国人民为解放台湾的斗争和印度尼西亚人民为解放西伊里安的斗争是一样的。
    中国人民对外文化协会广州分会副会长杨康华在讲话中感谢艺术团带来的友谊和丰富多采的舞蹈艺术。他还说，中国人民将坚决支持印度尼西亚人民解放西伊里安的斗争一直到胜利。
    艺术团一行三十九人是在11日自杭州到达广州的。艺术团曾先后在北京、沈阳、鞍山、天津、南京、上海、杭州等地访问演出，受到了数以万计的中国观众的热烈欢迎。
    艺术团定14日离广州回国。
