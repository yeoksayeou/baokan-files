Title: China and the Soviet Union Negotiate Next Year's Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th. Negotiations between China and the Soviet Union regarding cultural cooperation for 1958 began in Moscow on the 15th. The leader of the Chinese negotiation delegation is Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, and the leader of the Soviet delegation is Minister of Culture Mikhailov. Both sides expressed satisfaction with the further strengthening of cultural ties between the two countries in 1957. During this negotiation, a plan for cultural cooperation between the two countries for 1958 will be formulated. The negotiations are proceeding in a friendly and warm atmosphere. During its stay in the Soviet Union, the Chinese cultural delegation will visit various cultural and scientific institutions and meet with Soviet literary, artistic, and film workers.

### 中苏谈判明年文化合作协定

1957-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏谈判明年文化合作协定
    新华社莫斯科15日电　中国和苏联关于1958年文化合作的谈判15日在莫斯科开始举行。领导中国谈判代表团的是文化部长沈雁冰，领导苏联代表团的是文化部长米哈伊洛夫。双方对1957年两国文化联系的进一步加强感到满意。在这次谈判中，将制定1958年两国文化合作计划。谈判是在友好热忱的气氛中进行的。中国文化代表团在苏联停留期间，将访问各种文化科学机关，同苏联文学艺术和电影工作者会见。
