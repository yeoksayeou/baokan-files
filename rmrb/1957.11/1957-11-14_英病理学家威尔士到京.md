Title: British Pathologist Wells Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th — British pathologist Professor Rupert Wells, accompanied by his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of November 13th at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association to visit and lecture in China.

### 英病理学家威尔士到京

1957-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    英病理学家威尔士到京
    新华社13日讯　应中华医学会邀请前来我国访问和讲学的英国病理学家鲁珀特·威尔士教授偕同夫人于11月13日下午乘飞机到达北京。
