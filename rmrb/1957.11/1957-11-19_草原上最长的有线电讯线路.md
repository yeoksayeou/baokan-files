Title: The Longest Wired Telecommunications Line on the Grassland

Author:

Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 3

The longest wired telecommunications line on the grassland in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region was completed on the Xilin Gol Grassland on the 13th.

The Xilin Gol Grassland is one of the largest natural pastures in our country. The line is 367 kilometers long and went into full operation on the 13th. Next year, a machine room will be built on this line, and three carrier machines and amplifiers will be installed. By then, Xilinhot will be able to talk directly to all parts of the country.

Construction of this telecommunications line began in September this year, and it is another new construction project in the autonomous region during the first five-year plan. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 草原上最长的有线电讯线路

1957-11-19
第3版()
专栏：建设动态

    草原上最长的有线电讯线路
    内蒙古自治区草原上最长的一条有线电讯线路，13日在锡林郭勒草原建成。
    锡林郭勒草原是我国最大的天然牧场之一。这条线路全长三百六十七公里，从13日起全线通话。在这条线路上明年还要兴建机房，安装三条载波机和增音机等机械，届时锡林浩特就可和全国各地直接通话。
    这条电讯线路是今年9月开始动工兴建的，这是自治区在第一个五年计划期间，又一项新的建设。　　（据新华社讯）
