Title: Czechoslovak President Zápotocký Falls Ill
Author:
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 5th. Prague National Sanatorium announced in a communique on the evening of the 4th that Comrade Antonín Zápotocký, President of the Republic, suddenly fell seriously ill today and has been admitted to the National Sanatorium for treatment. His condition tonight is satisfactory.

### 捷总统萨波托斯基患病

1957-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    捷总统萨波托斯基患病
    新华社布拉格5日电　布拉格国家疗养院4日晚发表公报说，共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基同志今天突然患重病，已经进入国家疗养院医治。他今晚的健康情况令人满意。
