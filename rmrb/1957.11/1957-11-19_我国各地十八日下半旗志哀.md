Title: Our Country Pays Respects with Half-Mast Flags on the 18th in Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th: On the 18th, the day of the funeral of the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Antonín Zápotocký, all organs, troops, factories, mines, enterprises, schools and people's organizations located in the people's committees of each province, autonomous region and municipality and the people's committees of each city lowered their flags to half-mast to express mourning.

### 我国各地十八日下半旗志哀

1957-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我国各地十八日下半旗志哀
    据新华社18日讯　18日是捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基殡葬的日子。全国各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会和各市人民委员会所在地的机关、部队、工厂、矿山、企业、学校和人民团体都一律下了半旗，表示哀悼。
