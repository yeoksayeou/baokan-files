Title: Competition
Author: Tu'an
Date: 1957-11-23
Page: 8

    Competition
    Tu'an
    The small moon and the big moon,
    Compete to see who is bigger,
    The big moon says: "I am big!"
    The small moon says: "I am big,
    My bigness is not in volume,
    But in the immense significance of man's triumph over nature."
    The small moon and the sun compete to see who is brighter,
    The sun says: "I am bright!"
    The small moon says: "I am bright,
    Because I radiate
    The immense light of communism."

### 比赛
屠岸
1957-11-23
第8版()
专栏：

    比赛
    屠岸
    小月亮跟大月亮，
    比赛谁大，
    大月亮说：“我大！”
    小月亮说：“我大，
    我大不是体积大，
    是人定胜天的意义巨大。”
    小月亮跟太阳比赛谁亮，
    太阳说：“我亮！”
    小月亮说：“我亮，
    因为我身上发射着
    共产主义的巨大光芒。”
