Title: China's Largest Newsprint Mill Expansion Completed
Author:
Date: 1957-11-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency report:

    China's largest newsprint mill, the Guangzhou Paper Mill, has completed its expansion project and held an opening ceremony on the morning of the 6th.

    After the expansion, the Guangzhou Paper Mill will produce more than 60,000 tons of newsprint and other paper annually, more than six times the amount produced in 1952 before the expansion.

    This factory is the first large-scale integrated pulp and paper factory designed and built independently by Chinese designers. Its equipment is relatively complete, and it uses advanced production technology.

    Soviet experts provided enthusiastic assistance in the design and production processes of this factory.

    Song Naide, Vice Minister of Light Industry, Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Feng Baiju, Deputy Governor, Soviet expert A. Ye. Poliyakov, and others attended the opening ceremony, warmly congratulating the completion of this newsprint mill.

### 我国最大的新闻纸厂扩建完工

1957-11-13
第3版()
专栏：

    我国最大的新闻纸厂扩建完工
    据新华社讯　我国目前最大的新闻纸厂——广州造纸厂扩建完成，6日上午举行了开工典礼。
    扩建后的广州造纸厂，年产新闻纸和其他纸张六万多吨，比扩建前的1952年多六倍以上。
    这个厂是中国的设计人员第一次自行设计和施工的大型制浆造纸综合工厂。它的设备比较完善，并且采用了先进的生产技术。
    苏联专家在这个厂的设计和生产过程中，都给了热情的帮助。
    轻工业部副部长宋乃德，广东省省长陈郁、副省长冯白驹，苏联专家阿·叶·波里雅柯夫等参加了开工典礼，热烈祝贺这个新闻纸厂的建成。
