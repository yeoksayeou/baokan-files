Title: Romanian Newly Appointed Ambassador to China, Rudenko, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: Romania's newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Rudenko, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane on the 15th.

Li Huichuan, Deputy Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, were at the airport to welcome them. The Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, Iesan, and all embassy staff also went to the airport to welcome them.

Diplomatic envoys and other diplomatic officials from various countries to China also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 罗新任驻华大使鲁登科到京

1957-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    罗新任驻华大使鲁登科到京
    新华社15日讯　罗马尼亚新任驻华特命全权大使鲁登科和他的夫人15日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有外交部苏欧司副司长李汇川，礼宾司副司长马振武。罗马尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办耶桑和全体使馆人员也前往机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有各国驻华使节和其他外交官员。
