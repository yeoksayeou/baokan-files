Title: Mongolian National Musical Drama Theatre Troupe Holds Closing Performance in Baotou
Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baotou, November 14th. The Mongolian People's Republic National Musical Drama Theatre Troupe, which came to China for a performance tour according to the 1957 executive plan of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Agreement, held a closing performance in Baotou City on the evening of the 14th, and was warmly welcomed by more than a thousand spectators.

　　Lkhagvabanzhid, winner of the Gold Medal at the 6th World Youth and Students Peace and Friendship Festival, performed the Mongolian folk song "Beautiful Khangai", full of grassland life, leading people into the boundless realm of the vast grassland. At the audience's repeated applause and request, Lkhagvabanzhid had to add new programs. Tonight, the art troupe also performed Hebei Bangzi "Great Dengdian" and Chinese folk songs "Aobao Meeting" and "Spring Comes to the Tea Mountain", which aroused great interest among the audience. After the performance, Buhe (Mongolian nationality), Director of the Cultural Bureau of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, delivered a speech on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Art Troupe.

　　The head of the art troupe, I. Tsegmid, also spoke at the meeting.

　　The art troupe will return home via Erenhot on the 17th.

### 蒙古国家音乐话剧院艺术团  在包头举行闭幕演出

1957-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古国家音乐话剧院艺术团
    在包头举行闭幕演出
    新华社包头14日电　根据中蒙文化协定1957年执行计划来中国访问演出的蒙古人民共和国国家音乐话剧院艺术团14日晚在包头市举行闭幕演出，受到了一千多观众的热烈欢迎。
    第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节金质奖获得者洛尔布班吉德演出的富有草原生活气息的蒙古民歌“美丽的杭盖”，把人们引进了辽阔无边的大草原的境界。在观众再三鼓掌要求下，洛尔布班吉德不得不加演了新的节目。今晚艺术团还演出了河北梆子“大登殿”和我国民歌“敖包相会”、“春到茶山”等，引起观众极大兴趣。演出结束后，内蒙古自治区文化局局长布和（蒙古族）代表中华人民共和国文化部和内蒙古自治区艺术工作队致词。
    艺术团团长衣·策格米德也在会上讲话。
    艺术团将在17日经二连回国。
