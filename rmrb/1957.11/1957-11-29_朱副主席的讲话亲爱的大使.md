Title: Vice Chairman Zhu's Speech to the Dear Ambassador

Author:
Date: 1957-11-29
Page: 4

Vice Chairman Zhu's Speech
Dear Ambassador, Comrades, and Friends:

    Today is the 45th anniversary of Albania's declaration of national independence, and tomorrow is the 13th anniversary of Albania's liberation. Please allow me, on behalf of the Chinese people and the government of the People's Republic of China, to extend heartfelt and warm congratulations to the fraternal Albanian people and the government of the People's Republic of Albania.
    The Chinese people greatly admire the heroic struggle of the Albanian people for their national independence 45 years ago. On November 28, 1912, the Albanian people declared their independence, delivering a heavy blow to foreign imperialists. This historically significant event marked the victory of the Albanian people's heroic struggle for national liberation against occupiers and traitors.
    November 28, 1912, marked the beginning of a new stage in Albanian history. Coveting Albania's important geographical location and rich underground resources, the imperialists, while superficially recognizing Albania's independence, simultaneously declared Albania under their protection, thereby invading and ruling Albania. Only under the victorious conditions of the Great Soviet Union's defeat of fascism, under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party, did the Albanian people achieve complete liberation and gain genuine independence and freedom in 1944.
    In the thirteen years since liberation, the Albanian people, under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party and the government of the People's Republic of Albania, have made tremendous achievements in the cause of building socialism. Albania has become a socialist industrial and agricultural country. This year, the Albanian people completed the annual agricultural collectivization plan three months ahead of schedule, and have already realized agricultural collectivization in rural areas accounting for more than half of the country's cultivated land. This year, the Albanian government founded the first people's university in Albanian history - the National Tirana University. Due to the rapid development of the national economy, Albania completely abolished the rationing system this year and lowered the prices of industrial products and food, thereby increasing the actual income of the working people. The Albanian state planning agency has formulated a fifteen-year long-term plan for the development of the national economy this year. The Chinese people are overjoyed by the tremendous achievements made by the fraternal Albanian people, because these achievements are an inseparable part of the entire achievements of the socialist camp.
    As a strong member of the socialist camp, the People's Republic of Albania has made unremitting efforts to further consolidate and develop the unity of socialist countries and to safeguard international security and world peace. The international status of the People's Republic of Albania is constantly improving.
    In recent years, the deep friendship and close cooperation between the Chinese people and the fraternal Albanian people have continuously developed, and our two countries' friendly exchanges are increasing day by day. In January of this year, the delegation of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, headed by Comrade Peng Zhen, paid a friendly visit to Albania. In May of this year, the delegation of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, headed by Comrade Rita Marko, visited our country. These friendly visits have further promoted mutual understanding and friendly relations between our two peoples. The Chinese people will continue to strengthen friendship and cooperation with the fraternal Albanian people in the future and make joint efforts for the close unity of socialist countries and world peace.

    Allow me to propose a toast:
    To the prosperity of the People's Republic of Albania,
    To the fraternal and unbreakable friendship between China and Albania,
    To the health of Comrade Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania!

### 朱副主席的讲话亲爱的大使

1957-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    朱副主席的讲话亲爱的大使同志、同志们和朋友们：
    今天是阿尔巴尼亚宣布民族独立的四十五周年，明天就是阿尔巴尼亚解放的十三周年，请允许我代表中国人民和中华人民共和国政府向兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府表示衷心的，热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民非常钦佩阿尔巴尼亚人民在四十五年前为自己的民族独立所进行的英勇斗争。1912年11月28日，阿尔巴尼亚人民宣布了独立，给外国帝国主义者一个沉重的打击。这个具有历史意义的事件，标志着阿尔巴尼亚人民争取民族解放，反对占领者和卖国贼而进行的英勇斗争的胜利。
    1912年11月28日是阿尔巴尼亚历史上的一个新阶段的开始。由于垂涎阿尔巴尼亚的重要地理位置和丰富的地下资源，帝国主义者虽然表面上承认了阿尔巴尼亚的独立，但是同时又宣布阿尔巴尼亚归他们保护，进而侵占和统治了阿尔巴尼亚。阿尔巴尼亚人民只是在伟大的苏联击溃法西斯的胜利条件下，在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，才在1944年获得了彻底的解放，得到了真正的独立和自由。
    在解放后的十三年中间，阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府的领导下，在建设社会主义的事业上取得了巨大的成就，阿尔巴尼亚已经成为一个社会主义的工农业国家。今年，阿尔巴尼亚人民提前三个月完成了本年度的农业合作化计划，已经在占有全国耕地一半以上的农村实现了农业合作化。今年，阿尔巴尼亚政府创办了阿尔巴尼亚历史上第一个人民的大学——国立地拉那大学。由于国民经济的迅速发展，阿尔巴尼亚今年完全取消了配给制，并且降低了工业品和食品的价格，从而增加了劳动人民的实际收入。阿尔巴尼亚的国家计划机关今年制订了发展国民经济的十五年远景计划。中国人民为兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民所取得的这些巨大成就而欢欣鼓舞，因为这些成就是社会主义阵营全部成就中不可分割的一部分。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国作为社会主义阵营中坚强的一员，在进一步巩固和发展社会主义国家的团结和维护国际安全和世界和平方面，进行了不懈的努力。阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的国际地位正在不断地提高。
    近年来，中国人民和兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民间的深厚友谊和亲密合作获得了不断的发展，我们两国之间的友好往来正在一天比一天地增加。今年1月间，以彭真同志为首的中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会代表团在阿尔巴尼亚进行了友好访问，今年5月间，以里塔·马尔科同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团访问了我国。这些友好访问进一步促进了我们两国人民的互相了解和友好关系。中国人民今后将继续加强同兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民的友谊和合作，为社会主义国家的紧密团结和世界和平进行共同的努力。
    请允许我建议：
    为阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的繁荣，
    为中阿两国兄弟的、牢不可破的友谊，
    为阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希同志的健康，干杯！
