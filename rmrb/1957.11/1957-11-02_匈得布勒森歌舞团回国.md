Title: Hungarian Debrecen Song and Dance Troupe Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th - The Hungarian Debrecen Folk Song and Dance Troupe concluded its visit to China and left Beijing by train on the evening of the 30th to return home. Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Minister of Culture, and others saw them off at the station. The song and dance troupe visited China for two months, giving successful performances to more than 55,000 audience members in nine major cities.

### 匈得布勒森歌舞团回国

1957-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    匈得布勒森歌舞团回国
    据新华社30日讯　匈牙利得布勒森民间歌舞团结束了在中国的访问，30日晚乘火车离开北京回国。文化部副部长张致祥等到车站欢送他们。歌舞团在中国访问了两个月，在九大城市为五万五千多名观众作了成功的演出。
