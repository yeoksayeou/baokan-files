Title: Lebanese Newspapers Welcome the Establishment of the Egyptian-Syrian Federation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, November 20th: The "Political Newspaper" commentator, commenting on the resolution passed by the joint session of Syrian and Egyptian parliamentarians regarding the establishment of the Egyptian-Syrian Federation, stated that the foundation of the Arab motherland was laid at the joint session of Syrian and Egyptian parliamentarians. They have thus initiated a new history and realized what Arabs have hoped for generations.

"National News" wrote: "The union of Syria and Egypt into a federation will be a powerful blow to imperialism and its policies in the Middle East."

### 黎巴嫩报纸  欢迎埃叙成立联邦

1957-11-23
第5版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩报纸
    欢迎埃叙成立联邦
    据新华社21日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特20日讯：“政治报”评论员就叙利亚和埃及议员联席会议通过关于建立埃—叙联邦问题的决议发表评论说，在叙利亚和埃及议员联席会议上奠定了阿拉伯母国的基础，它们就此开创了新的历史，实现了阿拉伯人世世代代以来所盼望的事。
    “国家报”写道：“叙利亚和埃及联合成为一个联邦将是对帝国主义和它在中东的政策的有力打击。”
