Title: Watanabe Photography Exhibition Opens
Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The opening ceremony of the "Exhibition of Photographic Works by Yoshio Watanabe, Vice President of the Japan Photographers Association," was held on the morning of the 15th at the Handicraft Hall of the Soviet Exhibition Center in Beijing. The exhibition, scheduled to last three weeks, is jointly sponsored by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Photographic Society.

    Shi Shaohua, Chairman of the Chinese Photographic Society, said in his opening speech that the pictures in this exhibition were taken by Yoshio Watanabe, an outstanding Japanese realist photographer, during his visit to the Soviet Union, China, Mongolia, Vietnam and India in 1956. He said that this exhibition is the beginning of further friendly cooperation between the photographic circles of China and Japan.

    Nakajima Kenzo, Executive Director of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, who is currently visiting Beijing, said at the opening ceremony that he hoped that the Chinese people would understand the Japanese people's views on things through this exhibition. He said that photography as an art will help deepen the understanding between the peoples of Japan and China.

    After the opening ceremony, Nakajima Kenzo, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Shi Shaohua, Chairman of the Chinese Photographic Society, and others viewed the exhibition, which featured more than 560 pictures. They were joined by more than 300 Chinese photographers, artists, and others.

### 渡边摄影作品展览开幕

1957-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    渡边摄影作品展览开幕
    新华社15日讯　“日本写真家协会副会长渡边义雄摄影作品展览”，15日上午在北京苏联展览馆手工艺馆举行开幕式。这个预定举行三个星期的展览，是由中国人民对外文化协会和中国摄影学会联合举办的。
    中国摄影学会主席石少华在致开幕词时说，这次展览的图片，是日本优秀的现实主义派摄影师渡边义雄1956年访问苏联、中国、蒙古、越南和印度时拍摄的。他说，这次展览是中日两国摄影界进一步友好合作的开端。
    正在北京访问的日本中国文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏在开幕式上说，他希望中国人民通过这次展览了解日本人民对事物的看法。他说，摄影这种艺术将有助于日中两国人民了解的加深。
    开幕式后，中岛健藏和中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、中国摄影学会主席石少华等人，观看了这个有五百六十多幅图片的展览。和他们一起观看的还有中国的摄影工作者和美术工作者等共三百多人。
