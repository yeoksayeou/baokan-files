Title: "U.S. Delegation Damaged the Red Cross" Indian Newspapers Angrily Condemn U.S. Sabotage
Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 8th. All newspapers in Delhi today used front-page, full-width headlines to publish news of the many delegations withdrawing from the International Red Cross Conference last night.

Political and press circles in the Indian capital expressed deep indignation at the U.S. delegation's stubborn insistence on forcing the conference to pass the U.S. proposal to allow elements of the Chiang Kai-shek clique to participate in the meeting. They blamed the United States for ending the conference in this situation. The Hindi newspaper "Hindustan Daily" sharply stated when commenting on the voting situation of the U.S. proposal today, "This is really too bad." Another Hindi daily, "Nava Bharat Times," wrote under the title "Foolish": "The U.S. delegation has spared no effort in destroying the good reputation of the Red Cross."

The "National Herald" editorial said: "The U.S. delegation should apologize and explain to all people who believe in the Red Cross." It added: "The U.S. delegation damaged the Red Cross."

The newspaper continued: "The United States does not recognize China, does not recognize the facts, because it wants people to recognize two Chinas for its own purposes."

It said: "What the U.S. delegation, supported by some other delegations against the Chinese delegation, attempted to do is an insult to this country (referring to India)."

### “美国代表团损害了红十字会”  印度报纸愤怒抨击美国的破坏行动

1957-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    “美国代表团损害了红十字会”
    印度报纸愤怒抨击美国的破坏行动
    新华社新德里8日电　德里今天所有的报纸都用第一版横贯全页的大标题发表了许多代表团昨晚退出国际红十字大会的消息。
    印度首都政界和报界人士对美国代表团强使大会通过让蒋介石集团分子参加会议的美国提案的顽固态度，表示深深的愤慨。他们指责美国使得大会在这种情况下结束。印地文报纸“印度斯坦日报”今天在评论美国提案的表决情况时尖锐地表示，“这实在太糟糕了”。另一家印地文日报“新印度时报”以“愚蠢”为题写道：“美国代表团一直不遗余力地在毁坏红十字会的好名声。”
    “国民先驱报”社论说：“美国代表团应当向相信红十字会的一切人们道歉和解释。”它又说：“美国代表团损害了红十字会。”
    这家报纸继续说：“美国不承认中国，不认识事实，因为它为了自己的目的想要人们承认两个中国。”
    它说：“美国代表团在其它一些反对中国代表团支持下所企图作的事是侮辱这个国家（指印度）的事情。”
