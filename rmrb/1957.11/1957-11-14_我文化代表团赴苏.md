Title: Our Cultural Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Chinese cultural delegation, invited by the Soviet Ministry of Culture to discuss the 1958 implementation plan for the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement, departed for Moscow today. The head of the delegation is Shen Yanbing, the deputy heads are Huang Songling and Yang Hansheng, and the members are Qian Xinzong, Mei Yi, Zhang Yingwu (the above six are already in the Soviet Union), Wang Lanxi, Wang Yi, and Zhang Dongchuan.

### 我文化代表团赴苏

1957-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团赴苏
    新华社13日讯　应苏联文化部邀请前往商谈中苏文化合作协定1958年执行计划的中国文化代表团，今天启程前往莫斯科。代表团团长是沈雁冰，副团长是黄松龄、阳翰笙，团员有钱信忠、梅益、张映吾（以上六人已在苏联）、王阑西、王益、张东川。
