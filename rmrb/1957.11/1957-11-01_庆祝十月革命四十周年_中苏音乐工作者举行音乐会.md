Title: Chinese and Soviet Musicians Hold Concert to Celebrate the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author: 
Date: 1957-11-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 31st. To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, more than 150 Chinese and Soviet musicians successfully held a Sino-Soviet Friendship Symphony Concert at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing on the evening of the 31st. This was the first time that so many people have performed symphony music in Beijing.

    When the Symphony Orchestra of the Novosibirsk State Opera and Ballet Theatre of the Soviet Union and the Orchestra of the Central Experimental Opera Theatre of China and the Military Band of the Chinese People's Liberation Army jointly performed the last piece - Tchaikovsky's solemn "1812" Overture, more than a thousand audience members stood up and applauded warmly for them for as long as three minutes. M.A. Bukhbinder, Honored Artist of the Russian Republic and General Conductor of the Novosibirsk State Opera and Ballet Theatre of the Soviet Union, accepted the flowers. Li Delun, the Chinese conductor who had studied in the Soviet Union, and soprano Zheng Xingli also participated in the performance tonight. Accompanied by the orchestra conducted by Li Delun, Zheng Xingli sang in Russian an excerpt from Tchaikovsky's opera "Eugene Onegin", a section of Tatyana's letter. At the end of the performance, the ballerinas of the Novosibirsk State Opera and Ballet Theatre of the Soviet Union also presented them with flowers.

    Tonight, the symphony orchestras of China and the Soviet Union performed a total of five programs, lasting two hours.

### 庆祝十月革命四十周年  中苏音乐工作者举行音乐会

1957-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月革命四十周年
    中苏音乐工作者举行音乐会
    新华社31日讯　为庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年，一百五十多名中国和苏联的音乐工作者，31日晚在北京天桥剧场成功地举行了一次中苏友谊交响乐音乐会。这样多人演出交响乐，在北京还是第一次。
    当苏联新西伯利亚国立歌舞剧院交响乐团和我国中央实验歌剧院管弦乐团、中国人民解放军军乐团联合演奏完最后一个曲子——柴可夫斯基的“一八一二”庄严序曲时，一千多听众曾经起立向他们热烈鼓掌祝贺达三分钟之久。俄罗斯共和国功勋艺术家、苏联新西伯利亚国立歌舞剧院总指挥米·阿·布赫宾德尔接受了献花。曾经在苏联学习过的我国的乐队指挥李德伦和女高音郑兴丽，今晚也参加了演出。在李德伦指挥的乐队伴奏下，郑兴丽用俄文独唱了柴可夫斯基的歌剧“叶夫根尼·奥涅金”的选曲，塔姬雅娜书信的一段。当演出结束时，苏联新西伯利亚国立歌舞剧院的芭蕾舞女演员，也向他们献了花。
    今晚，中苏两国的交响乐团共演出了五个节目，历时两个钟头。
