Title: Shanghai and Guangdong Party and Government Leaders Visit the Czechoslovak Consulate to Mourn the Death of the Czechoslovak President
Author:
Date: 1957-11-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 13th: Ke Qingshi, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Secretariat; Cao Diqiu and Jin Zhonghua, Vice Mayors of Shanghai; and Liang Yufan, Acting Director of the Foreign Affairs Office, visited the Czechoslovak Consulate General in Shanghai this evening to express their condolences to Consul General Felinge on the death of Antonín Zápotocký, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and President of the Republic.
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 13th: At 10:20 p.m. tonight, Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Wang De, First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, and Major General Gao Tigian, Commander of the Guangzhou Garrison, visited the Czechoslovak Consulate in Guangzhou together to mourn the death of President Antonín Zápotocký and presented wreaths.

    More than ten responsible comrades from the provincial and municipal Party and government also went to the consulate to mourn together with Tao Zhu and others.

    Soviet Consul in Guangzhou, Olov, Chargé d'affaires of the Consulate General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Guangzhou, Lê Chung Thủy, and representative of the Polish Consulate General in Guangzhou, Nowaczyk, also visited the Czechoslovak Consulate in Guangzhou tonight to express their condolences.

### 上海、广东党政领导人  到捷领事馆吊唁捷总统逝世

1957-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    上海、广东党政领导人
    到捷领事馆吊唁捷总统逝世
    新华社上海13日电　中共上海市委第一书记柯庆施、书记处书记陈丕显，上海市副市长曹荻秋、金仲华和外事处代处长梁于藩，今天晚上为捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央政治局委员、共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基逝世，到捷克斯洛伐克驻上海总领事馆向总领事费林格表示哀悼。
    新华社广州13日电　今晚十时二十分，中共广东省委第一书记陶铸、广东省省长陈郁、中共广州市委第一书记王德、广州市市长朱光和广州驻军首长高体乾少将一起到捷克斯洛伐克驻广州领事馆吊唁安·萨波托斯基总统逝世，并献上花圈。
    和陶铸等一起到领事馆吊唁的，还有省、市党政负责人十多人。
    苏联驻广州领事鄂洛夫、越南民主共和国驻广州代总领事黎终始和波兰驻广州总领事馆代表诺华奇克，今晚也都到捷克斯洛伐克驻广州领事馆吊唁。
