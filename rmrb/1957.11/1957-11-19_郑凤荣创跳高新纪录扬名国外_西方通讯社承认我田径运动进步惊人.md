Title: Zheng Fengrong Sets New High Jump Record, Gains Fame Abroad; Western News Agencies Acknowledge China's Astonishing Progress in Athletics
Author:
Date: 1957-11-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Zheng Fengrong's creation of a world record in the women's high jump has spread to every corner of the world. The Associated Press, in a dispatch from London on the 17th, said: A twenty-year-old Chinese girl has warned the world of athletics with a powerful jump, "Six hundred million Chinese will not be also-rans forever."

The Associated Press dispatch also said that Zheng Fengrong's record is the first world record created by China in athletics. It is also the first world record in athletics created by an Asian since 1936, when Tajima Naoto set the world record for the triple jump in 1936.

A Reuters reporter reported that this powerfully illustrates the astonishing and comprehensive improvement of Chinese athletics. The improvement in Chinese sports achievements in the past eighteen months has been so remarkable that it is conceivable that they may become a huge force in the Olympic Games in the future.

### 郑凤荣创跳高新纪录扬名国外  西方通讯社承认我田径运动进步惊人

1957-11-19
第6版()
专栏：

    郑凤荣创跳高新纪录扬名国外
    西方通讯社承认我田径运动进步惊人
    新华社18日讯　郑凤荣创造女子跳高世界纪录的消息已经传向世界各个角落。美联社17日从伦敦发出的电讯中说：一位二十岁的中国姑娘在北京以有力的一跳警告世界田径界，“六亿中国人不会永远是落选选手了。”
    美联社的这条电讯还说，郑凤荣的纪录是中国在田径方面创造的第一个世界纪录。这也是1936年以来亚洲人创造的第一个田径运动的世界纪录，1936年田岛直人曾经创造了三级跳远的世界纪录。
    路透社记者报道说，这件事有力说明了中国田径运动有了惊人的全面提高。在过去十八个月中中国体育运动方面的成绩的提高是那么突出，以至可以设想，他们可能成为今后奥林匹克运动会上的一支巨大力量。
