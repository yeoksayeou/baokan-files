Title: Sukarno Meets with Hatta
Author:
Date: 1957-11-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 19th: Indonesian President Sukarno visited former Vice President Hatta yesterday and held an hour and a half of talks with Hatta. This was their first meeting since Hatta resigned from the position of Vice President. No details of the meeting were released.

### 苏加诺同哈达会谈

1957-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺同哈达会谈
    据新华社雅加达19日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺昨天访问了前副总统哈达，同哈达进行了一小时半的会谈。这是他们两人在哈达辞去副总统职位以后的第一次会谈。会谈情况没有发表。
