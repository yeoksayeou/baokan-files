Title: Wu Youxun, Zhou Peiyuan, and Qian Sanqiang Send Telegrams to Congratulate Li Zhengdao and Yang Zhenning
Author:
Date: 1957-11-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 1st. Chinese renowned physicists Wu Youxun, Zhou Peiyuan, and Qian Sanqiang, on October 31st, on behalf of the Chinese Physical Society, sent telegrams to Chinese physicists Li Zhengdao and Yang Zhenning, who are residing in the United States, congratulating them on winning the Nobel Prize in Physics for their research on the theory of atomic nuclei and elementary particles. The telegram stated, "Chinese physicists are proud of this gratifying event."

### 吴有训　周培源　钱三强电贺李政道杨振宁

1957-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    吴有训　周培源　钱三强电贺李政道杨振宁
    新华社1日讯　中国著名物理学家吴有训、周培源和钱三强10月31日代表中国物理学会打电报给我国留美物理学家李政道和杨振宁，祝贺他们由于对原子核和基本粒子理论的研究获得了诺贝尔物理学奖金。电文中说：“中国物理学家对这一可喜的事件感到自豪”。
