Title: Czechoslovakian Musicians Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th:

    The Czechoslovakian musicians delegation, comprised of ten members, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 9th. The delegation is visiting China according to the 1957 executive plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement, and will hold its first performance in Beijing on the 11th.

    The delegation is led by Mr. F. Hushka, Head of the Art Department of the Slovak Ministry of Education and Culture, and Ms. M. Knotkova, Deputy Head, Professor of the Prague National Conservatory of Music, and pianist.

### 捷音乐家代表团到京

1957-11-11
第2版()
专栏：

    捷音乐家代表团到京
    据新华社9日讯　由十人组成的捷克斯洛伐克音乐家代表团，9日下午坐飞机到达北京。代表团是根据中捷文化合作协定1957年执行计划来我国访问的，11日将在北京举行首次演出。
    代表团由团长、斯洛伐克教育文化部艺术司司长弗·胡希卡和副团长、布拉格国家音乐学院教授、钢琴家玛·克诺特柯娃率领。
