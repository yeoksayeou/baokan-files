Title: Japanese Business Circles Advocate Accepting China's Draft to Strive for Conclusion of a New Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: According to reports, representatives from three organizations – the Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade, Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and Japan-China Import and Export Association – decided at a meeting on the 18th to negotiate with the government through trade circles, urging them to strive to sign the Fourth Japan-China Trade Agreement within this year.

The meeting believed that the Japanese side should accept the trade agreement draft proposed by the Chinese side, including a memorandum.

The National Council of the Wuhan and Guangzhou Japanese Goods Exhibition held a meeting in Tokyo on the afternoon of the 19th. After listening to a report by Chairman Eichi Shukutani on the negotiations regarding holding Japanese goods exhibitions in China, it decided that the organizations participating in the exhibition and the industrial and commercial circles would do their utmost to jointly appeal to the Japanese government to ensure the immediate signing of the Fourth Japan-China Trade Agreement and the early realization of the Chinese goods exhibitions in Nagoya and Fukuoka.

At the same time, the Osaka Chamber of Commerce and Industry also suggested to the government on the 19th to immediately resume Japan-China trade negotiations and requested the immediate conclusion of the Fourth Japan-China Trade Agreement.

### 日工商界主张接受中国草案  争取缔结新贸易协定

1957-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    日工商界主张接受中国草案
    争取缔结新贸易协定
    本报综合报道  据新华社讯：日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟、日本国际贸易促进协会、日中输出入协会三个团体的代表在18日举行的会议上，决定要由贸易界向政府进行交涉，促使争取在今年内签订第四次日中贸易协定。
    会议认为，日本方面应该接受中国方面提出的包括一项备忘录在内的贸易协定草案。
    武汉广州日本商品展览会全国理事会19日下午在东京举行的会议上听取了理事长宿谷荣一的关于在中国举行日本商品展览会的谈判经过的报告以后，决定由参加展览会的团体和工商业界竭尽全力一致向日本政府提出要求，使第四次日中贸易协定能立即签订，在名古屋和福冈举行的中国商品展览会能早日实现。
    同时，大阪商工会议所也在19日向政府建议立即恢复日中贸易谈判，并且要求立即缔结第四次日中贸易协定。
