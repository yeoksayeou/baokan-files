Title: The Road to the Stars: New Soviet Science Film
Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 6

    The Road to the Stars
    Soviet Union Produces New Science Film
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th dispatch: According to TASS reports: The Soviet Union recently produced a new science film, "The Road to the Stars." This film vividly narrates the principles of interstellar flight, the history of rocket technology development in the Soviet Union and foreign countries, and the work of the great Soviet scientist Tsiolkovsky.
    Audiences will see on the screen how the first spaceship—a three-stage rocket with passengers—flies into the high altitude and enters a predetermined orbit, circling the earth. Audiences can also see the construction of the first interstellar transportation station. This large ring-shaped interstellar transportation station is powered by atomic energy. Fantasy images of conquering the moon also appear on the screen.

### 通往星球的道路  苏联拍制新科学影片

1957-11-09
第6版()
专栏：

    通往星球的道路
    苏联拍制新科学影片
    据新华社8日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联最近摄制了一部新的科学影片“通往星球的道路”。这部影片形象地叙述了星际飞行的原理，苏联和外国火箭技术发展的历史，以及伟大的苏联科学家齐奥尔科夫斯基的劳动。
    观众将在银幕上看到第一艘宇宙飞船——带有乘客的三级火箭如何飞上高空，并且进入预定的轨道，围绕地球飞行的情景。观众们还可以看到建设第一座星际交通站的情况。这个大圆环形的星际交通站是用原子能推动的。银幕上还出现征服月球的幻想图景。
