Title: National Basketball League Concludes: "August 1st" Team Red Wins Men's Division, Beijing Wins Women's Division
Author:
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 25th. The 1957 National Basketball League, lasting ten days, held its closing ceremony today at the Guangzhou Gymnasium.

The league began on the 14th, with twenty-three Class A men's and women's basketball teams holding a total of eighty-eight intense and exciting matches, determining the rankings of each team. During the competition, the teams demonstrated good performance in terms of tactical application and sportsmanship. More than twenty visiting groups composed of basketball instructors, referees, and athletes from all over the country observed this league.

According to Xinhua News Agency, after the "August 1st" men's basketball red team defeated its last opponent, the Shenyang team, by a score of 73 to 64 in Guangzhou today, it won first place in the 1957 National Basketball League men's division with a record of five wins and zero losses. Second place went to the Beijing team, who defeated the "August 1st" yellow team today by a score of 67 to 59. The first place in the women's division went to the Beijing team, who defeated their last opponent, the "August 1st" team, today by a score of 52 to 28. The "August 1st" team took second place.

### 全国篮球甲级队联赛闭幕  男子组八一队红队　女子组北京队得第一

1957-11-26
第8版()
专栏：

    全国篮球甲级队联赛闭幕
    男子组八一队红队　女子组北京队得第一
    据新华社广州25日电　历时十天的1957年全国篮球甲级队联赛，今天在广州体育馆举行闭幕式。
    这一届联赛从14日开始，二十三个甲级男、女篮球队共举行了八十八场紧张激烈的比赛，评定了各队的名次。在比赛中，各队在战术运用和道德作风等方面，都有良好的表现。由全国各地的篮球指导、裁判员和运动员组成的二十多个参观团，观摩了这次的联赛。
    据新华社讯　“八一”男子篮球红队今天在广州以七十三比六十四战胜了最后一个对手沈阳队以后，以五战五胜的成绩获得了1957年全国篮球甲级队联赛男子组的第一名。第二名是北京队，他们今天以六十七比五十九胜“八一”黄队。女子组的第一名是北京队，她们今天以五十二比二十八胜最后一个对手“八一”队。“八一”队得第二名。
