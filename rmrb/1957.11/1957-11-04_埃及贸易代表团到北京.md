Title: Egyptian Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd: An Egyptian trade delegation of fourteen, led by Mustafa Khalifa, Undersecretary of the Egyptian Ministry of Commerce, arrived in Beijing by plane today. This delegation is in China to sign the 1958 barter protocol according to the Sino-Egyptian trade agreement.

Welcoming them at the airport were Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Ragab, the Egyptian Ambassador to China, and commercial personnel from the Vietnamese and Korean embassies in China.

Mustafa Hausney, First Secretary of the Egyptian Embassy in Japan, who arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou yesterday, and two officials from the Egyptian Embassy in China will also participate in this trade negotiation.

### 埃及贸易代表团到北京

1957-11-04
第2版()
专栏：

    埃及贸易代表团到北京
    新华社2日讯　埃及贸易代表团一行十四人在埃及商业部次长穆斯塔法·哈利法率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。这个代表团是根据中埃贸易协定来我国签订1958年换货议定书的。
    在机场欢迎的有对外贸易部副部长雷任民，埃及驻华大使拉加卜和越南、朝鲜驻华大使馆的商务人员。
    昨天从广州乘飞机到达北京的埃及驻日大使馆一等秘书穆斯塔法·豪斯奈和埃及驻华大使馆的两位官员也将参加这次贸易谈判。
