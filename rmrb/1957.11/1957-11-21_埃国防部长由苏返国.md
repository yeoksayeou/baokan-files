Title: Egyptian Defense Minister Returns Home from Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-11-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th: TASS reports: The representative of the Egyptian President, Defense Minister General Amer, today flew from Moscow back to Cairo. General Amer visited the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet government and Defense Minister Marshal Malinovsky.

### 埃国防部长由苏返国

1957-11-21
第5版()
专栏：

    埃国防部长由苏返国
    新华社20日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：埃及总统代表、国防部长阿密尔将军今天从莫斯科乘飞机回开罗。阿密尔将军是应苏联政府和国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅的邀请来苏联访问的。
