Title: Soviet Party and Government Leaders Receive Chinese Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-11-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 27th: Soviet Communist Party and government leaders Khrushchev, Voroshilov, and Bulganin received the Chinese military friendship delegation headed by Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai at the Kremlin at 11:00 a.m. today (Moscow time).

Accompanying Marshal Peng Dehuai were the deputy head of the delegation, Marshal Ye Jianying, and delegation members Senior General Su Yu, Senior General Tan Zheng, Senior General Xiao Jingguang (Navy), Senior General Xu Guangda, Air Force Senior General Liu Yalou, Senior General Hong Xuezhi, Senior General Chen Xilian, Air Force Senior General Liu Zhen, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong.

Present from the Soviet side during the reception were: Soviet Minister of Defense Marshal Malinovsky, Chief of the General Staff of the Soviet Army Marshal Sokolovsky, Soviet Minister of Foreign Affairs Gromyko, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Soviet Army General Antonov, and Lieutenant General Trufanov.

Also present at the reception were: Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao and Military Attache of the Embassy, Lieutenant General Han Zhenji.

### 苏联党和政府领导人  接见我军事代表团

1957-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联党和政府领导人
    接见我军事代表团
    新华社莫斯科27日电　苏联共产党和政府领导人赫鲁晓夫、伏罗希洛夫和布尔加宁今天上午十一时（莫斯科时间）在克里姆林宫接见了以国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好访苏代表团。
    随同彭德怀元帅受接见的有代表团副团长叶剑英元帅，团员粟裕大将、谭政大将、萧劲光海军大将、许光达大将、刘亚楼空军上将、洪学智上将、陈锡联上将、刘震空军上将、刘志坚中将、萧向荣中将。
    接见时苏联方面在座的有：苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅、苏军总参谋长索科罗夫斯基元帅、苏联外交部长葛罗米柯、苏军副总参谋长安东诺夫大将、特鲁法诺夫上将。
    接见时在座的还有：中国驻苏联大使刘晓和大使馆武官韩振纪中将。
