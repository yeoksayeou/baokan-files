Title: Syrian Army Commander-in-Chief Cables Egyptian Defense Minister to Congratulate on Positive Results of Visit to Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-11-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 22nd: The Commander-in-Chief and Chief of Staff of the Syrian Armed Forces, Bizri, sent a telegram to the Egyptian Minister of Defense, General Amer, on November 21, congratulating him on the positive results achieved during his visit to the Soviet Union.

Bizri's telegram also stated: "I hope you will soon complete the alliance of the Syrian and Egyptian armies."

### 叙军总司令电埃国防部长  祝贺访苏取得积极结果

1957-11-23
第5版()
专栏：

    叙军总司令电埃国防部长
    祝贺访苏取得积极结果
    新华社开罗22日电　叙利亚武装部队总司令兼参谋长比兹里11月21日打电报给埃及国防部长阿密尔将军，对他在苏联访问时所取得的积极结果表示祝贺。
    比兹里的电报还说：“我希望你不久将完成叙埃两国军队的联盟。”
