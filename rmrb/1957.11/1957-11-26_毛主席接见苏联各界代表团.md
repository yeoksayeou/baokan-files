Title: Chairman Mao Receives Soviet Delegations from All Walks of Life
Author:
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 25th: Chairman Mao Zedong received Andrei Andreyev, head of the Soviet delegation from all walks of life, as well as delegation members Cherednichenko, Volkova, Kozhevnikov, Andreyeva, Umarov, Bulkina, Kozlovsky, Muradeli, Novgorodsky, Kislenko, and delegation staff members Kalmanov and Yerofeyev on the evening of the 25th.

Present at the reception were: Soong Ching Ling, Wu Yuzhang, Liao Chengzhi, Yu Xinqing, Ge Baoquan, and Cao Mengjun.

Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin was also present at the reception. (Attached pictures)

### 毛主席接见苏联各界代表团

1957-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见苏联各界代表团
    新华社25日讯　毛泽东主席25日晚间接见了苏联各界代表团团长安德烈耶夫，团员切烈德尼钦科、沃尔科娃、科热夫尼柯夫、安德烈耶娃、乌玛罗夫、布尔金娜、科兹洛夫斯基、穆拉德里、诺夫哥罗德斯基、基斯连科，代表团随行人员卡尔曼诺夫和叶罗费耶夫。
    接见的时候，在座的有：宋庆龄、吴玉章、廖承志、余心清、戈宝权、曹孟君。
    接见时，苏联驻中国大使尤金也在座。（附图片）
