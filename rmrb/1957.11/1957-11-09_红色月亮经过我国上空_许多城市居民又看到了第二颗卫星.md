Title: Red Moon Passes Over China; Residents of Many Cities See Second Satellite Again
Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 8
Xinhua News Agency report: On the 6th and 7th, many people in Kunming, Xi'an, Taiyuan, Beijing, Hohhot, Baoding, Chengdu, Lhasa, Urumqi, and other places in China saw the second artificial Earth satellite again.

On the morning of the 7th, two teachers from the Physics Department of Kunming Normal College saw the artificial satellite at 6:05 (Beijing time, same below) using binoculars. At around 6:00, the people of Xi'an saw the second artificial Earth satellite with the naked eye for the first time. More than ten staff members of the Shanxi Provincial Agricultural Construction Department in Taiyuan also saw this artificial satellite at this time. The Beijing Planetarium observation team saw the artificial satellite with a wind measuring theodolite and the naked eye at around 6:00, and photographed the passage of the artificial satellite. At 6:06:30, tens of thousands of people of all ethnic groups in Hohhot stood on rooftops, high-rise buildings, ancient city walls, and suburban squares and saw this large and bright satellite with the naked eye. Citizens of Baoding, and more than ten teachers and students from Chengdu Institute of Technology also saw the second artificial Earth satellite at around 6:00.

On the 6th, observers from the Lhasa Geophysical Observatory and the Tibet Meteorological Bureau, as well as office workers and citizens of Urumqi, saw the second artificial satellite.

### 红色月亮经过我国上空  许多城市居民又看到了第二颗卫星

1957-11-09
第8版()
专栏：

    红色月亮经过我国上空
    许多城市居民又看到了第二颗卫星
    综合新华社讯  6、7两日，在我国的昆明、西安、太原、北京、呼和浩特、保定、成都、拉萨、乌鲁木齐等地，又有许多人看到第二颗人造地球卫星。
    7日晨，昆明师范学院物理系的两位教师在六点零五分（北京时间，下同），用双筒望远镜看到人造卫星。六时许，西安人民第一次用肉眼看到了第二颗人造地球卫星。太原市的山西省农业建设厅工作人员十多人，也在这一时间看到了这颗人造卫星。北京天文馆观察队六时许用测风经纬仪和肉眼看到了这颗人造卫星，并拍摄了人造卫星经过的情况。六点零六分三十秒，呼和浩特市有成千上万的各族人民站在屋顶、高楼、古城墙和郊外广场上用肉眼看到了这颗又大又亮的卫星。保定市民、成都工学院十多名教师和学生在六时许也看到了第二颗人造地球卫星。
    在6日，看到第二颗人造卫星的，有拉萨地球物理观象台和西藏气象处的观测员，还有乌鲁木齐市的机关工作人员和市民。
