Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 8

Track and Field Athletes Set Two New Records

On the afternoon of the 7th, at the track and field performance competition held at Beijing Normal University to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, female javelin thrower Wang Yi achieved an excellent result of 47.34 meters. This result surpassed her previous national record of 44.62 meters, which she achieved on October 9th of this year at the 1957 National Track and Field Games, and also exceeded the standard of 45 meters for Chinese sports athletes.

The variable distance relay team, composed of Huang Qiaoshu, Lin Tao, Yu Shangwu, and Li Zhonglin, set a new record of 3 minutes 20 seconds in the 1,500-meter variable distance relay. This broke the previous national record of 3 minutes 27.8 seconds. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 田径选手又创两项新成绩

1957-11-09
第8版()
专栏：

    田径选手又创两项新成绩
    7日下午，在北京师范大学为庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年而举行的田径表演赛中，女子标枪选手王毅创造了四十七公尺三十四公分的优异成绩。这个成绩超过了她今年10月9日在1957年全国田径运动会上创造的四十四公尺六十二公分的全国新成绩，也超过了我国运动健将四十五公尺的标准。
    由黄巧叔、林涛、余尚武和李忠林四人组成的异程接力队，创造了一千五百公尺异程接力三分二十秒的新成绩。打破了三分二十七秒八的全国纪录。　（据新华社讯）
