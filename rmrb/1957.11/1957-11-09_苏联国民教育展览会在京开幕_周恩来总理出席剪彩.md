Title: Soviet National Education Exhibition Opens in Beijing; Premier Zhou Enlai Attends Ribbon-Cutting Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1957-11-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 8th. The Soviet National Education Exhibition opened today at the Soviet Exhibition Hall in Beijing.

Premier of the State Council, Zhou Enlai, attended the opening ceremony and cut the ribbon for the exhibition.

Minister of Education Zhang Xiruo spoke at the opening ceremony, first expressing, on behalf of the Ministry of Education and the nation's educators, heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet government for holding this exhibition according to the cultural cooperation agreement between the two countries. He said that this exhibition comprehensively and systematically introduces the great achievements of Soviet national education and is a rare opportunity for us to deeply and comprehensively learn from advanced Soviet experience. We should modestly and conscientiously learn from advanced Soviet experience to improve our education work.

The head of the Soviet National Education Exhibition Working Group, A. A. Sergeyenko, said at the meeting that the purpose of holding this exhibition is to enable education workers from both the Soviet Union and China to exchange experiences, learn from each other, and make greater achievements in the field of national education in both countries.

Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy, G.T. Kitikov, said in his speech that during the years of Soviet power, the Soviet Union transformed from an agricultural, economically backward, and uncultured country into an advanced industrial power, a country where everyone is literate, and a country with highly developed culture and education. The tasks put forward by the Soviet Party and government regarding the promotion of universal secondary education, comprehensive technical education, and the construction of new-type schools—boarding schools—are being successfully completed. He hopes that this exhibition will arouse the interest of Chinese friends.

Vice Mayor of Beijing, Wu Han, also spoke at the meeting.

Also attending the opening ceremony were Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long and Chen Yi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Xu Teli, university professors, outstanding teachers, and more than 400 other people.

Counselors of embassies of various countries in China also attended the opening ceremony.

The exhibition displays various pictures, charts, and books, introducing in detail and comprehensively the organization, content, and methods of Soviet teaching and education, and pointing out the path of comprehensive technical education in Soviet schools. Among the exhibits are also Soviet students' classroom assignments, specimens, drawings, and various teaching aids and children's toys.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, and others, visited all the exhibits in detail and frequently asked questions of the guides, expressing great interest in the children's toys and teaching aids on display.

### 苏联国民教育展览会在京开幕  周恩来总理出席剪彩

1957-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联国民教育展览会在京开幕
    周恩来总理出席剪彩
    新华社8日讯　苏联国民教育展览会今天在北京苏联展览馆开幕。
    国务院总理周恩来出席了开幕式并为展览会剪彩。
    教育部部长张奚若在开幕式上首先代表教育部和全国教育工作者对苏联政府根据两国文化合作协定举办的这个展览会表示衷心的谢意。他说，这个展览会全面地系统地介绍了苏联国民教育事业的巨大成就，对我们深入全面地学习苏联先进经验是个难得的机会。我们应该虚心地认真地学习苏联的先进经验，来改进我们的教育工作。
    苏联国民教育展览会工作团团长阿·阿·谢尔吉茵柯夫在会上讲话说，举办这个展览会的目的是使苏中两国教育工作者互相交流经验，互相学习，并使两国在国民教育领域中取得更大的成就。
    苏联大使馆临时代办顾悌克夫在讲话中说，在苏维埃政权的年代里，苏联从一个农业的、经济落后的、无文化的国家变成了先进的工业强国，成了人人识字的国家，具有高度发展的文化和教育的国家。苏联党和政府所提出的关于推行普及中等教育、综合技术教育以及建设新型学校——寄宿学校的任务正在顺利地完成着。他希望这次展览会能引起中国朋友的兴趣。
    北京市副市长吴晗在会上也讲了话。
    出席开幕式的还有国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，中共中央委员徐特立，大学教授和优秀教师等四百多人。
    各国驻华大使馆参赞也参加了开幕式。
    展览会展出了各种图片、表格、书籍，详尽而全面地介绍了苏联教学与教育的组织、内容和方法，指出了苏维埃学校综合技术教育的途径。展品中还有苏联学生的课堂作业、标本、图画以及各种教具和儿童玩具等。
    周恩来总理，贺龙、陈毅副总理等，详细地参观了全部展品，并不时向讲解员提出问题，对展出的儿童玩具和教具表现了浓厚的兴趣。
