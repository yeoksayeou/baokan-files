Title: Peng Zhen Receives North Japan Port City Mayors Delegation

Author:
Date: 1957-11-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd: Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen met this afternoon with the entire North Japan Port City Mayors Delegation headed by Suzuki Tomin.

Present at the meeting were Beijing Vice Mayors Zhang Youyu and Wang Kunlun, and Beijing Municipal People's Committee Secretary-General Jia Xingwu, among others.

During their visit to Beijing, the North Japan Port City Mayors Delegation has toured scenic spots and historical sites such as the Temple of Heaven and the Great Wall, and visited the Beijing Children's Hospital, the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory, and the Beijing Gymnasium.

### 彭真接见北日本港湾城市市长访华团

1957-11-04
第2版()
专栏：

    彭真接见北日本港湾城市市长访华团
    新华社3日讯　北京市市长彭真今天下午接见了以铃木东民为首的北日本港湾城市市长访华团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有北京市副市长张友渔、王昆仑，北京市人民委员会秘书长贾星五等。
    北日本港湾城市市长访华团这几天在北京游览了天坛、长城等名胜古迹，参观了北京市儿童医院、国营北京第二棉纺织厂、北京体育馆等。
