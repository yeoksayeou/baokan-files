Title: Ministry of Agriculture Announces Second List of Model Agricultural Increase Awards
Author:
Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 14th dispatch: The Ministry of Agriculture, according to the "Provisional Regulations on Rewarding Model Agricultural Increase", recently reviewed and announced the second list of 1956 annual agricultural increase model awards.

Included in this list are one hundred and seven cooperatives, tractor stations, agricultural technology extension stations, and state-owned farms from Anhui, Hebei, Gansu, Jiangxi, Heilongjiang, Guizhou, Shanxi, Liaoning, Yunnan, Henan, Jiangsu, Guangdong, Sichuan, Hunan, Qinghai, Zhejiang, Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, and Tianjin.

The cooperatives being rewarded include ninety agricultural, forestry, animal husbandry, tea, vegetable, and pastoral cooperatives. For example, the Qianjin Agricultural Production Cooperative in Beiguan Township, Yicheng County, Shanxi Province (Cooperative Director Ma Fangting), has ninety households and 411.9 mu of cultivated land. Among them, grain crops cover 105.44 mu, with an average yield of 1,086.4 jin per mu, exceeding the local yield by 534.39% and increasing by 186.95% compared to 1955; cotton covers 108.94 mu, with an average yield of 449.76 jin of seed cotton per mu, exceeding the local yield by 263.44%. The Jinxing Agricultural, Forestry, and Animal Husbandry Production Cooperative, led by the province's famous agricultural labor models Li Shunda and Shen Jilan (female), also received an award.

Also receiving awards are: Zhangye County, Gansu Province (County Party Secretary Guo Fayong, County Head Huang Zhenwu, Li Weixian), Xingzi County, Jiangxi Province (County Party Secretary Li Chunduo), Shangrao County Farm (Farm Director Zhou Hanzhi), Jinshan County, Jiangsu Province (County Party Secretary Wen Hanguang, County Head Wang Xiangchen), Changshi Township, Dading County, Guizhou Province (Township Head Zhang Taiming), Wu'an County Tractor Station, Shanxi Province (Station Director Zhao Qin'an), Shenyang Tractor Station (Station Director Zhang Fabin), Ningbian Township, Zhaotong County, Yunnan Province (Township Party Branch Secretary An Deyun), Yucheng County Farm, Henan Province (Pig raising worker Li Youxuan), etc.; Individual award recipients also include Wang Zaisan, vegetable technician of the Xinchun Vegetable Production Cooperative in Harbin. These award-winning units and individuals have achieved remarkable achievements in grain, vegetables, and other areas. For example, Zhangye County has 40,017 households and 781,497 mu of cultivated land. Among them, wheat covers 353,521 mu, with a yield of 381 jin per mu, exceeding the province's average yield by 122.68% and increasing by 2.45% compared to 1955.

The Ministry of Agriculture has decided to issue a patriotic bumper harvest certificate to the awarded bumper harvest counties, townships, tractor stations, agricultural technology extension stations, and farms; issue a patriotic bumper harvest certificate and a bonus of 200 yuan to the bumper harvest agricultural cooperatives; and award a patriotic bumper harvest medal to the heads of each increase unit and individual models.

(Editor's note: The Ministry of Agriculture issued the first list of 1956 annual agricultural increase models on August 29th of this year, with sixty-eight units and individuals receiving awards, as reported in this newspaper on September 3rd.)

### 农业部公布第二批农业增产模范奖励名单

1957-11-16
第2版()
专栏：

    农业部公布第二批农业增产模范奖励名单
    据新华社14日讯　农业部根据“关于奖励农业增产模范的暂行规定”，最近审定并公布1956年度农业增产模范第二批奖励名单。
    在这批名单中，有安徽、河北、甘肃、江西、黑龙江、贵州、山西、辽宁、云南、河南、江苏、广东、四川、湖南、青海、浙江、新疆维吾尔自治区和天津市等一百零七个合作社、拖拉机站、农业技术推广站和国营农场。
    合作社受到奖励的，包括农业、农林牧、茶叶、蔬菜、牧业等九十个社。例如，山西省翼城县北关乡前进农业生产合作社（社主任马芳庭），全社九十户，耕地四百一十一点九亩。其中粮食作物一百零五点四四亩，平均每亩产粮一千零八十六点四斤，高出当地产量534．39％，较1955年增产186．95％；棉花一百零八点九四亩，每亩产籽棉四百四十九点七六斤，高出当地产量263．44％。这个省著名的农业劳动模范李顺达、申纪兰（女）所领导的金星农林牧生产合作社也得了奖。
    受到奖励的还有：甘肃省张掖县（县委书记郭发永，县长黄振武、李维先）、江西省星子县（县委书记李春铎）、上饶县农场（场长周含志）、江苏省金山县（县委书记文汉光、县长王相臣）、贵州省大定县长石乡（乡长张太明）、山西省武安县拖拉机站（站长赵钦安）、沈阳拖拉机站（站长张发斌）、云南省昭通县宁边乡（乡支部书记安德云）、河南省虞城县农场（养猪工人李友轩）等；个人受奖的还有哈尔滨市新春蔬菜生产合作社蔬菜技术员王在善。这些受奖的单位和个人，都在粮食、蔬菜等方面取得了显著的成就。例如，张掖县全县四万零一十七户，耕地七十八万一千四百九十七亩。其中小麦三十五万三千五百二十一亩，每亩产三百八十一斤，高出全省平均产量122．68％，较1955年增产2．45％。
    农业部决定给得奖的丰产县、乡、拖拉机站、农业技术推广站和农场，发给爱国丰产奖状一张；发给丰产的农业社爱国丰产奖状一张，奖金二百元；发给各增产单位负责人、个人模范爱国丰产奖章一枚。
      （编者注：农业部曾于本年8月29日发出奖励1956年度第一批农业增产模范名单，受奖者有六十八个单位和个人，见本报9月3日报道。）
