Title: Our Military Friendship Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-11-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 7th: At the invitation of the Soviet Ministry of Defense, the Military Friendship Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Deputy Leader Marshal Ye Jianying, arrived in Moscow on the afternoon of the 6th aboard a "Tu-104" jet airliner.

The leader of the Military Friendship Delegation is Marshal Peng Dehuai (who arrived in Moscow earlier). The deputy leader is Marshal Ye Jianying. The members of the delegation are: General Su Yu, General Tan Zheng, Naval General Xiao Jingguang, General Xu Guangda, Air Force General Liu Yalou, General Hong Xuezhi, General Chen Xilian, Air Force General Liu Zhen, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport were: First Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal K.S. Moskalenko, Chief of the General Staff of the Soviet Army Marshal Sokolovsky, Marshal Moskalenko, Air Force Chief Marshal Zhigarev, Air Force Marshal Vershinin, Artillery Marshal Varentsov, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Soviet Army General Antonov, Admiral Gorshkov, General Vinogradov, and General Poluboyarov.

Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, Lieutenant General Han Zhenji, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

After Marshal Ye Jianying disembarked, he reviewed the guard of honor accompanied by Marshal K.S. Moskalenko.

### 我军事友好代表团到莫斯科

1957-11-08
第3版()
专栏：

    我军事友好代表团到莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科7日电　应苏联国防部的邀请，中华人民共和国军事友好访苏代表团在副团长叶剑英元帅的率领下，6日下午乘“图一一○四”喷气专机到达莫斯科。
    军事友好访苏代表团的团长是彭德怀元帅（先期到达莫斯科）。副团长是叶剑英元帅。团员有：粟裕大将、谭政大将、萧劲光海军大将、许光达大将、刘亚楼空军上将、洪学智上将、陈锡联上将、刘震空军上将、刘志坚中将、萧向荣中将。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有：苏联国防部第一副部长科?夫元帅，苏军总参谋长索科洛夫斯基元帅，莫斯卡连科元帅，空军主帅日加列夫，空军元帅维尔希宁，炮兵元帅瓦连佐夫，苏军副总参谋长安东诺夫大将，海军上将戈尔什科夫，维诺格拉多夫上将，波卢比亚罗夫上将。
    中国驻苏联大使馆武官韩振纪中将也到机场欢迎。
    叶剑英元帅下飞机后，在科?夫元帅的陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
