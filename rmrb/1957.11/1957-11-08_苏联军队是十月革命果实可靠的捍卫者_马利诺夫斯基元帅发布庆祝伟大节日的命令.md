Title: The Soviet Army is a Reliable Defender of the Achievements of the October Revolution Marshal Malinovsky Issues Order Celebrating the Great Festival
Author:
Date: 1957-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th November (TASS): Soviet Minister of Defense, Soviet Marshal Malinovsky, issued an order on the 7th of November celebrating the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The full text is as follows:
	Comrade Soldiers and Non-Commissioned Officers of the Army and Navy!
	Comrade Officers and Generals of the Army and Navy!

	Today, the Soviet people and their armed forces are solemnly celebrating the national holiday – the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

	Our country is greeting this significant anniversary with outstanding achievements in realizing Lenin's communist construction plan. Under the leadership of the Communist Party, the Soviet people have transformed our motherland into one of the most powerful countries in the world, a socialist agricultural country with highly developed industry and large-scale production. On this basis, great achievements have been made in improving the material and cultural life of the people. The Soviet Union is advancing on the path of rapid progress in science and technology.

	The Soviet Union, dedicated to construction, resolutely and consistently pursues a policy of peace, which is warmly supported by peace-loving people of all countries. However, the Soviet people cannot but take into account that the reactionary imperialist bloc is pursuing a "position of strength" policy, which intensifies the international situation.

	The Soviet armed forces are reliable defenders of the achievements of the October Revolution. Thanks to the constant concern of the Communist Party and the Soviet government for strengthening our country's national defense, the Soviet Army and Navy have been equipped with the latest weapons and technology and have reached the high level required to defend the interests of the Soviet state.

	It is the duty of the members of the Soviet armed forces to fully master new combat technologies and weapons, strengthen the discipline and organization of the army, and tirelessly improve the combat readiness of the troops and fleets.

	I congratulate the soldiers, non-commissioned officers, officers, and generals of the Army and Navy on the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. On the occasion of celebrating this national holiday, I order:

	Today, November 7, in Moscow, the capital of our motherland, in the capitals of the union republics, in the hero cities: Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, Odessa, in Lviv, Kaliningrad, Murmansk, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok and Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky, each fire a forty-gun salute.

	Long live the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution!

	Long live the heroic Soviet people and their glorious armed forces!

	Long live the Communist Party, the leader of the October Revolution, and the inspirer and organizer of our socialist and communist construction!

		Soviet Minister of Defense, Soviet Marshal R. Malinovsky

### 苏联军队是十月革命果实可靠的捍卫者  马利诺夫斯基元帅发布庆祝伟大节日的命令

1957-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联军队是十月革命果实可靠的捍卫者
    马利诺夫斯基元帅发布庆祝伟大节日的命令
    新华社7日讯　塔斯社莫斯科7日电：苏联国防部长、苏联元帅马利诺夫斯基7日发布庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年的命令，全文如下：陆海军士兵同志和士官同志们！陆海军军官和将军同志们！
    今天，苏联人民和他们的武装部队正在隆重地庆祝全民的节日——伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年。
    我们国家正以实现列宁的共产主义建设计划的卓越成就来迎接这个意义重大的纪念日。苏联人民在共产党领导下已经把我们祖国改变成世界上最强大的国家之一，改变成拥有高度发达的工业和大规模生产的社会主义农业的国家。在这个基础上，在提高人民的物质生活和文化生活方面取得了巨大的成就。苏联正在科学技术飞快进步的道路上前进。
    致力于建设工作的苏联，坚决地一贯地执行为全世界各国爱好和平的人民所热烈拥护和支持的和平政策。但是，苏联人民不能不考虑到，反动的帝国主义集团奉行“实力地位”政策使得国际局势紧张化。
    苏联武装部队是十月革命果实的可靠的捍卫者。由于共产党和苏联政府不断关怀加强我国的国防力量，苏联陆海军都已经用最新式的武器和技术装备起来，并且达到了保卫苏维埃国家的利益所要求的高度水平。
    苏联武装部队成员的职责是：充分掌握新的战斗技术和武器、加强军队的纪律性和组织性、不倦地提高部队和舰队的战斗准备。
    我向陆海军士兵、士官、军官、将军们祝贺伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年。在庆祝这一全民节日的时候，我命令：
    今天，11月7日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科，在各加盟共和国首都，在英雄城：列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、塞瓦斯托波尔、敖德萨，在里沃夫、加里宁格勒、摩尔曼斯克、伯力、海参崴和彼得罗巴夫罗夫斯克——堪察加，各鸣礼炮四十响。
    伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年万岁！
    英勇的苏联人民及其光荣的武装部队万岁！
    十月革命的领导者、我国社会主义建设和共产主义建设的鼓舞者和组织者——共产党万岁！
                  苏联国防部长、苏联元帅罗·马利诺夫斯基
