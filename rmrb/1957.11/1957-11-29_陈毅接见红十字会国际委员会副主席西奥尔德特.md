Title: Chen Yi Receives ICRC Vice-President Siordet
Author:
Date: 1957-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council received Mr. Siordet, Vice-President of the International Committee of the Red Cross, and Mr. Paul Boissier, Secretary of the Presidential Office of the International Committee of the Red Cross, this morning for a conversation that lasted over an hour.

Present at the reception were Li Dequan and Wu Yunfu.

### 陈毅接见红十字会国际委员会副主席西奥尔德特

1957-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见红十字会国际委员会副主席西奥尔德特
    新华社28日讯　国务院副总理陈毅在今天上午接见了红十字会国际委员会副主席西奥尔德特和红十字会国际委员会主席团秘书鲍新杰，进行了一个多小时的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有李德全，伍云甫。
