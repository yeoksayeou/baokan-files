Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Visits Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th - Chairman Mao Zedong and all members of the Chinese delegation visited the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum in Red Square at 12:00 noon (Moscow time) on the afternoon of the 5th, and laid a wreath.

Accompanying the delegation were Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Fedrin, Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, Director of the Far East Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Gudev, and Lieutenant General Vedyenin, Commandant of the Kremlin Garrison. (Attached picture)

Chairman Mao Zedong and all members of the Chinese delegation paid respects to the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum and laid wreaths on the 5th. TASS facsimile photo (Sent by Xinhua News Agency)

### 毛泽东主席谒列宁斯大林陵墓

1957-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席谒列宁斯大林陵墓
    新华社莫斯科5日电　毛泽东主席和中国代表团全体团员，5日下午十二时（莫斯科时间）前往红场拜谒了列宁斯大林陵墓，敬献了花圈。
    陪同代表团谒陵的有苏联外交部副部长费德林、苏联驻中国大使尤金、外交部远东司司长顾德夫、克里姆林宫卫戍司令维杰宁中将。（附图片）
