Title: More Locations Receiving Signals from the Second Artificial Satellite
Author:
Date: 1957-11-06
Page: 8

    More Locations Receiving Signals from the Second Artificial Satellite
    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. As of the 4th and 5th, more locations in our country have received signals from the Soviet Union's second artificial satellite.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th. The time that Xinhua News Agency Radio Station in Beijing received the signal on the 5th was 2:44 PM Beijing time. The Chongqing Telecommunications Bureau's receiving station also began to receive radio signals from the satellite again at 4:34 PM on the 5th. The First Receiving Station of the Shanghai Municipal Radio Management Office, the Purple Mountain Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences in Nanjing, the Chengdu Telecommunications Bureau Radio Station, and the listening and observation group of the Jinan Municipal Posts and Telecommunications Bureau all received radio signals from the satellite on the 4th and 5th.

### 又有一些地方收听到第二颗人造卫星的讯号

1957-11-06
第8版()
专栏：

    又有一些地方收听到第二颗人造卫星的讯号
    据新华社5日讯　4日和5日我国又有一些地方收到苏联第二颗人造卫星的讯号。
    北京新华社电台5日收到讯号的时间是北京时间下午二时四十四分。重庆电信局收讯台5日下午四时三十四分又开始收听到卫星发出的无线电讯号。上海市无线电管理处第一收讯台、南京中国科学院紫金山天文台、成都电信局电台以及济南市邮电局收听、观察小组都在4日和5日收听到卫星所发出的无线电讯号。
