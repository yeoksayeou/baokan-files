Title: U.S. Conspires with Israel and Jordan to Betray Palestinian Refugees
Author:
Date: 1957-11-11
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 10th: The U.S.-orchestrated conspiracy to eliminate the Palestinian refugee problem has ignited outrage among Palestinian refugees and Arab people. Palestinian refugees in Jericho, Nablus, and Ramallah in Jordan have recently held demonstrations. Palestinian refugees currently in Jordan, Egypt, and Syria have consistently demanded to return to their homeland.

    Sources here say that Jordanian King Hussein, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Rifai, and Economic Minister Huri, along with Israeli Prime Minister Ben-Gurion, Foreign Minister Golda Meir, and in the presence of the U.S. Ambassador to Jordan, held a secret meeting on October 15 in the Nablus region. The U.S. Ambassador to Jordan has been visiting Tel Aviv, Israel, almost daily recently. The U.S. Secretary of Agriculture of Jewish origin, Benson, also made a sudden visit to Jordan and Israel. These developments indicate that the U.S.-engineered conspiracy regarding the Palestinian refugee problem is actively underway.

    According to a recent expose in the Egyptian "People's Daily," the Jordanian government has accepted a ceasefire plan formulated by Rifai and Ben-Gurion in 1950. Under this plan, Jordan agrees to accept $30 million in compensation from U.S. aid funds paid by Israel to Jordan, and relinquish the right of Arab refugees to return to their Palestinian homeland.

    Political figures here point out that the main reason for the U.S.'s actions is that it considers the long-standing Arab-Israeli dispute and the Palestinian refugee problem to be a major obstacle to the expansionist policies the U.S. is currently pursuing in the Middle East. The reason it is actively pursuing this now is in the hope that this conspiracy will succeed because Arab public opinion is currently focused on the Turkish-Syrian border issue.

### 美国策动以色列和约旦共谋出卖巴勒斯坦难民

1957-11-11
第6版()
专栏：

    美国策动以色列和约旦共谋出卖巴勒斯坦难民
    据新华社大马士革10日电　美国正在策划的取消巴勒斯坦难民问题的阴谋已经激起了巴勒斯坦难民和阿拉伯人民的愤慨，约旦的杰里科、纳布鲁斯和拉马拉等地的巴勒斯坦难民最近都举行了示威游行。目前在约旦、埃及和叙利亚等地的巴勒斯坦难民一直坚决要求返回故乡。
    据这里得到的消息说，约旦国王侯赛因、副首相兼外交大臣里法伊、经济大臣扈利，同以色列总理本—古里安、外交部长梅厄夫人，在美国驻约旦大使的参加下，10月15日曾经在纳布鲁斯地区举行了秘密会谈。美国驻约旦大使最近几乎每天都去以色列的特拉维夫。美国的犹太籍农业部长本逊最近也突然去约旦和以色列访问。这些都表明了美国所策划的关于巴勒斯坦难民问题的阴谋正在积极进行中。
    据埃及“人民报”最近揭露，约旦政府已经接受了由里法伊和本—古里安在1950年制订的一项休战计划。根据这项计划，约旦同意接受由以色列从美援基金中付给约旦三千万美元的赔偿费，而放弃阿拉伯难民重回巴勒斯坦故乡的权利。
    这里的政界人士指出，美国所以这样做的主要原因是：美国认为，长期悬而未决的阿拉伯—以色列纠纷和巴勒斯坦难民问题是美国现在在中东推行的扩张政策所面临的一个重要障碍。它所以在目前积极进行，是希望这个阴谋会由于阿拉伯舆论的注意力现在都集中在土叙边境问题上而得以成功。
