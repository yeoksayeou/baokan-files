Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Condemns Dutch Unreasonable Occupation of West Irian at the UN Political Committee; Soviet Representative Supports Indonesia's Demand for the Return of Territory
Author:
Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 21st, New York Dispatch: The United Nations General Assembly Political Committee began discussing the West Irian issue on November 20th.

    The representative of Ceylon, on behalf of the delegations of eighteen countries in the Asia-Africa region and Bolivia in Latin America, a total of nineteen countries, submitted a proposal. The proposal suggests that the United Nations General Assembly invite the parties in dispute, in the spirit of the principles of the United Nations Charter, to seek a solution to this issue. The proposal also entrusts United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld to provide assistance and take measures he deems appropriate to implement this resolution and report the results to the next General Assembly.

    Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio spoke first at the meeting, accusing the Netherlands of forcibly separating West Irian from Indonesia, a situation that threatens the peace and stability of the entire Asia. Subandrio emphasized that West Irian is an indivisible part of Indonesian territory.

    He said that Indonesia is prepared to accept a UN resolution with "an appropriate procedure and mechanism to assist both parties in finding a peaceful solution to the West Irian issue." He also condemned Australia for supporting the Dutch colonialist stance.

    Ceylon representative Gunawardene said that the West Irian issue is purely a colonial issue. He expressed regret at the Netherlands' attempts to maintain its imperial remnants.

    Soviet representative Pevy said in his speech that the West Irian issue is one of the products of the colonialist system that urgently needs to be resolved.

    Pevy said that there should be no doubt about Indonesia's sovereignty over West Irian. The Soviet Union fully supports Indonesia's legitimate demand to return West Irian to its homeland - Indonesia.

    Jordanian representative Heikal also supported Indonesia's position on the West Irian issue.

    Dutch representative Schurmann, in his speech, defended the Netherlands' continued occupation of West Irian and said that the Netherlands refused to hold any form of negotiations with Indonesia on the West Irian issue.

### 印度尼西亚外长在联大政委会上  谴责荷兰无理强占西伊里安  苏联代表支持印度尼西亚收回领土要求

1957-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外长在联大政委会上
    谴责荷兰无理强占西伊里安
    苏联代表支持印度尼西亚收回领土要求
    据新华社21日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会政治委员会11月20日开始讨论西伊里安问题。
    锡兰代表以亚非地区十八个国家和拉丁美洲的玻利维亚共十九个国家的代表团的名义，提出一项提案。提案建议联合国大会邀请争执双方以符合联合国宪章原则的精神，寻求对这个问题的解决办法。提案还责成联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德给予协助，采取他认为是适合的办法使这项决议付之实现，并把结果向下届大会报告。
    印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约首先在会上发言，他指责荷兰硬把西伊里安和印度尼西亚割开，这种情况威胁到整个亚洲的和平和稳定。苏班德里约强调西伊里安是印度尼西亚领土不可分割的一部分。
    他说，印度尼西亚准备接受联合国的一项有“一个适当的程序和机构来协助双方寻找和平解决西伊里安问题的决议案”。他还谴责澳大利亚支持荷兰的殖民主义立场。
    锡兰代表古纳瓦地尼说，西伊里安问题纯粹是殖民地问题。他对荷兰企图保持它的帝国残余的做法表示遗憾。
    苏联代表佩维在发言中说，西伊里安问题是急需解决的、属于殖民主义制度产物的问题之一。
    佩维说，印度尼西亚对西伊里安的主权是不容有任何怀疑的。苏联完全支持印度尼西亚提出的把西伊里安交还自己的祖国——印度尼西亚的合法要求。
    约旦代表赫卡勒也支持印度尼西亚在西伊里安问题上的立场。
    荷兰代表舒尔曼在发言中，为荷兰继续占领西伊里安进行辩解，并且说荷兰拒绝就西伊里安问题和印度尼西亚举行任何形式的谈判。
