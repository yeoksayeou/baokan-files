Title: Italian Film Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-11-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 22nd. The five-member Italian film delegation left Guangzhou this morning, November 22nd, to return home.

　　The delegation met with more than 1,500 moviegoers in Guangzhou on the evening of the 18th and was warmly welcomed.

　　Since the Italian film "Bicycle Thieves" and other films were screened in Guangzhou, the number of moviegoers in Guangzhou who have seen Italian films has reached more than 2 million by the end of the Italian Film Week.

### 意大利电影工作代表团回国

1957-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利电影工作代表团回国
    新华社广州22日电　意大利电影工作者代表团一行五人，22日晨离广州回国。
    这个代表团的成员18日晚上曾和广州的一千五百多电影观众见面，受到观众们的热情欢迎。
    自从意大利电影“偷自行车的人”等片在广州放映以来，到这次举办的意大利电影周结束时止，广州市看过意大利电影的观众共达二百多万人次。
