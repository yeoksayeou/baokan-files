Title: Artificial Lighting Can Promote Plant Growth
Author: Xu Zisen
Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 7

    Artificial Lighting Can Promote Plant Growth
    It was previously thought that artificial lighting would prevent plants from growing normally. Now, experiments have proven that electrical lighting can make plants grow even better than under natural sunlight. Recent experiments led by Professor Mashkov at the Physical Optics Laboratory of the Leningrad Institute of Agricultural Physics have demonstrated this point through multiple trials.
    For example, in a specially equipped illuminated room, tomatoes mature in just sixty days (from the germination period). Under normal circumstances, it takes 120 to 130 days for them to mature. Generally, twelve kilograms of tomatoes can be harvested per square meter of land. In the illuminated room, over seventy kilograms of tomatoes can be harvested per square meter of land annually. Tomatoes grown with electrical lighting have a higher nutritional value than ordinary tomatoes. If small-fruited varieties are planted, they can bear fruit thirty-five to forty days after germination. All of this proves that artificial lighting has a beneficial effect on plant growth. (Xu Zisen)

### 人工照明能促进植物生长
许子森
1957-11-22
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    人工照明能促进植物生长
    以前人们都认为人工照明，会使植物不能正常地生长。现在试验证明，用电气照明，可以使植物生长得比受天然的阳光照射还好。在列宁格勒农业物理研究所物理光学实验室里，最近玛什科夫教授领导下进行的多次实验证明了这一点。
    例如，在有专门设备的照明室内，蕃茄只需六十天就成熟（从萌芽期算起）。而在一般情况下，它需要一百二十到一百三十天才能成熟。每一平方公尺面积的地上，一般能收获十二公斤蕃茄。在照明室内每一平方公尺地上每年可收获七十多公斤蕃茄。用电气照明培植的蕃茄，营养价值超过一般的蕃茄。如果栽种小果品种，自发芽后，三十五到四十天就可以结果。这都证明人工照明对植物的生长有良好的效果。　　　　　　　　（许子森）
