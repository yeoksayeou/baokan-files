Title: Czechoslovak Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 25th. The Czechoslovak Republic education delegation, led by the Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Education and Culture of Czechoslovakia, Jan Kotoč, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation members arriving on the same plane included: Maria Kotikova, Josef Novotny, Bohumil Kujal, and interpreter Aldrich Prohazka. Another member, Ervin Kukal, had arrived earlier in Beijing.

Liu Shi, Vice Minister of Education, and Bailide, Cultural Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, welcomed the delegation at the airport.

The delegation will conduct a six-week investigation of China's education.

### 捷教育代表团到京

1957-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    捷教育代表团到京
    新华社25日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国教育代表团在团长、捷教育和文化部副部长杨·科托奇率领下，今日下午乘飞机到达北京。同机到京的有团员：玛丽亚·科提科娃、约瑟夫·诺沃提尼、波胡米尔·库亚尔和译员阿尔德里赫·普罗哈兹卡。另一团员埃尔文·库卡尔已先期到京。
    教育部副部长柳湜和捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆文化参赞白利德到机场欢迎。
    代表团将对我国教育事业进行为期六周的考察。
