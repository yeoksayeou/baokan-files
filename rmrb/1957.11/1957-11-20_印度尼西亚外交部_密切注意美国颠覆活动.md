Title: Indonesian Foreign Ministry Closely Watches U.S. Subversive Activities
Author:
Date: 1957-11-20
Page: 6

    Indonesian Foreign Ministry
    Closely Watches U.S. Subversive Activities
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 19th: Indonesian Foreign Ministry Secretary-General Suwito Kusumowidagdo told the press on the 18th that the Indonesian Foreign Ministry is closely watching news about the U.S. Ambassador to Indonesia being implicated in activities opposing the Indonesian government. He said that a careful study of the matter is underway.

### 印度尼西亚外交部  密切注意美国颠覆活动

1957-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外交部
    密切注意美国颠覆活动
    据新华社雅加达19日电　印度尼西亚外交部秘书长苏维托·库索莫维达格多在18日对报界说，印度尼西亚外交部正在密切注意关于美国驻印度尼西亚大使同反对印度尼西亚政府的活动有牵连的消息。他说，正在对这件事进行仔细的研究。
