Title: Egyptian Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-11-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 21st. The Egyptian cultural delegation, headed by Dr. El-Sayed Mustafa Said, President of Alexandria University, departed Guangzhou this morning for home. Zhang Ruiquan, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, and Ding Bo, Director of the Guangzhou Municipal Bureau of Culture, saw them off at the railway station.

During their stay in China, the Egyptian cultural delegation visited Beijing, Shenyang, Anshan, Wuhan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places. They arrived in Guangzhou from Hangzhou on the 19th.

Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, and Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, held a farewell banquet on the 19th to bid farewell to Dr. El-Sayed Mustafa Said and all members of the delegation. The delegation held a farewell luncheon on the 20th.

### 埃及文化代表团回国

1957-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及文化代表团回国
    新华社广州21日电　以埃及亚历山大大学校长爱尔赛德·穆斯塔法·赛义德博士为首的埃及文化代表团一行八人，今晨离广州回国。广州市副市长张瑞权，广州市文化局局长丁波到车站欢送。
    埃及文化代表团在中国期间，访问了北京、沈阳、鞍山、武汉、南京、上海、杭州等地，19日自杭州到达广州。
    广东省省长陈郁、广州市市长朱光于19日举行饯别晚宴，为爱尔赛德·穆斯塔法·赛义德博士和代表团全体团员饯行。20日，代表团举行了告别午宴。
