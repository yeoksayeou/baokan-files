Title: Ho Chi Minh Leads Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-11-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd - TASS reports: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the CPSU and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by President Ho Chi Minh, to attend the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, arrived in Moscow by special plane on the evening of the 1st.

Welcoming the distinguished Vietnamese guests at Vnukovo Airport were: Voroshilov, Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Furtseva, Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, Pervukhin, Alternate Member, as well as representatives of the working people of Moscow and diplomatic envoys of various countries to Moscow.

President Ho Chi Minh spoke at the airport. He emphasized that the October Revolution was a turning point in the history of the world's liberation movement. He said that the October Revolution caused an unprecedented upsurge in the national liberation movements in colonies and dependent countries. The Vietnamese people have always regarded, and continue to regard, the October Revolution and the Soviet state it established as an inspiring example and a symbol of a bright future.

### 胡志明率代表团抵莫斯科

1957-11-03
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明率代表团抵莫斯科
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：应苏共中央和苏联部长会议的邀请，参加十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼的越南民主共和国代表团，在胡志明主席率领下，1日晚间坐专机抵达莫斯科。
    到伏努科夫机场欢迎越南贵宾的有：苏共中央主席团委员、苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，苏共中央主席团委员福尔采娃，候补委员别尔乌辛，以及莫斯科劳动人民的代表和各国驻莫斯科的外交使节。
    胡志明主席在机场上讲了话。他强调指出，十月革命是全世界解放运动发展史中的转折点。他说，十月革命引起了殖民地和附属国的民族解放运动的空前高涨。越南人民过去和现在都一直把十月革命和它所建立起来的苏维埃国家看成是鼓舞自己的榜样和光明未来的象征。
