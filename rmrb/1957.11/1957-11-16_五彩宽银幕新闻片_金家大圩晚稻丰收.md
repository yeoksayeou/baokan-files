Title: Colorful Widescreen Newsreel – Golden Harvest of Late Rice at Jinjia Great Embankment

Author:

Date: 1957-11-16
Page: 8

Stage and Screen

    Colorful Widescreen Newsreel
    – Golden Harvest of Late Rice at Jinjia Great Embankment
    The good news of a bumper harvest of over 30,000 mu of late rice at Jinjia Great Embankment in Changshu County, Jiangsu Province, has reached Beijing. The Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio sent people to the area on November 1st to film a colorful widescreen newsreel. Soon, audiences will be able to see the joyful commune members harvesting the abundant late rice.

### 五彩宽银幕新闻片——金家大圩晚稻丰收

1957-11-16
第8版()
专栏：舞台与银幕

    五彩宽银幕新闻片
    ——金家大圩晚稻丰收
    江苏常熟县金家大圩三万多亩晚稻大片丰收的喜讯传到了北京，中央新闻纪录电影制片厂在11月1日派人来到当地拍摄五彩宽银幕新闻片。不久，观众就能看到满怀喜悦的社员们收割着丰硕的晚稻的景象。
