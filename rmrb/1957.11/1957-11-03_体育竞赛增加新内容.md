Title: Sports Competitions Add New Content
Author:
Date: 1957-11-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: This reporter learned from relevant departments on November 1st that, in order to more enthusiastically celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, the capital's sports competition activities will add new content.

　　The four men's basketball teams, the famous Beijing, "August 1st," Tianjin, and Shenyang Military Region teams, which have gone to Guangzhou to prepare for the 1957 National Basketball League, will especially fly from Guangzhou to Beijing within the next few days. On the evening of the 6th, they will hold two exciting exhibition matches at the lighted court next to the Beijing Gymnasium, with the Beijing team playing against the Shenyang Military Region team, and the "August 1st" team playing against the Tianjin team.

　　On the 6th, at the football field next to the Beijing Gymnasium, the first evening football exhibition match will also be held, with the Shenyang Institute of Physical Education team playing against the Changchun team. Currently, powerful lighting equipment is being installed in this football field.

　　On the evening of the 6th, exciting volleyball, table tennis, water polo, swimming, diving, and parachuting performances will also be held in the Beijing Gymnasium and on the sports field next to the Beijing Gymnasium.

### 体育竞赛增加新内容

1957-11-03
第2版()
专栏：

    体育竞赛增加新内容
    新华社1日讯　新华社记者1日从有关部门获悉：为了更热烈地庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年，首都的体育竞赛活动将增加新的内容。
    已去广州准备参加1957年全国篮球甲级队联赛的著名的北京、“八一”、天津、沈阳部队队等四个男子篮球队，将在日内特地从广州乘飞机赶来北京，他们将在6日晚间在北京体育馆旁的灯光球场举行两场精采的表演赛，由北京队对沈阳部队队；“八一”队对天津队。
    6日，在北京体育馆旁的足球场上，还将第一次举行在晚间进行的足球表演赛，由沈阳体育学院队对长春队。目前，这个足球场正在安装强力的照明设备。
    6日晚上，在北京体育馆里和北京体育馆旁的体育场上，还将有精采的排球、乒乓球、水球、游泳、跳水和跳伞等表演。
