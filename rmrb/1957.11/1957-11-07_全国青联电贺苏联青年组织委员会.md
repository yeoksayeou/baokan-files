Title: All-China Youth Federation Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the Committee of Soviet Youth Organizations

Author:
Date: 1957-11-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The All-China Youth Federation sent a telegram on the 4th to the Committee of Soviet Youth Organizations, representing China's 120 million young people in congratulating all Soviet youth on the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.

The telegram said: "In celebrating the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, Chinese youth once again express their unwavering determination to forever advance alongside Soviet youth along the glorious path of the October Revolution, and to diligently learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, learning the spirit of the Soviet people and Soviet youth in overcoming all difficulties to build communism, so as to accelerate our country's socialist construction. We wholeheartedly congratulate Soviet youth on achieving new and greater successes in the cause of building communism and defending world peace."

### 全国青联电贺苏联青年组织委员会

1957-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    全国青联电贺苏联青年组织委员会
    新华社6日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会4日致电苏联青年组织委员会，代表中国一亿二千万青年向全苏联青年祝贺十月革命四十周年。
    电报说：“在庆祝十月革命四十周年的时候，中国青年再一次表示他们坚定不移的决心，永远同苏联青年沿着十月革命的光辉道路前进，并努力学习苏联的先进经验，学习苏联人民和苏联青年克服一切困难建设共产主义的精神，以加速我国的社会主义建设。我们衷心祝贺苏联青年在建设共产主义和保卫世界和平的事业中取得新的更大的成就。”
