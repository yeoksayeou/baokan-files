Title: Yugoslav and Ceylonese Trade Union Chairmen Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions

Author:
Date: 1957-11-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd. Djuro Salaj, Chairman of the Central Council of the Yugoslav Trade Union Confederation, and Mandis, Chairman of the Ceylon Trade Union Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 22nd at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Vice-Chairman Liu Changsheng, Secretary Dong Xin, and others went to welcome them.

### 应邀参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会  南斯拉夫和锡兰工会主席到京

1957-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会
    南斯拉夫和锡兰工会主席到京
    新华社22日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请前来参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会的南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会主席德尤络·萨拉伊和锡兰工会联合会主席曼迪斯，22日下午分别乘飞机到达北京。前往欢迎的有中华全国总工会主席赖若愚、副主席刘长胜、书记处书记董昕等人。
