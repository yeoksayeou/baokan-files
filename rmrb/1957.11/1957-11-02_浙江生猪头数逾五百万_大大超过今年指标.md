Title: Zhejiang's Hog Population Exceeds Five Million, Greatly Surpassing This Year's Target
Author:
Date: 1957-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, November 1st: Zhejiang Province's hog population has reached 5.09 million, completing and exceeding this year's production target of 4.5 million hogs ahead of schedule.

The Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Zhejiang Provincial People's Committee recently issued instructions, requiring all localities to seize the time before and after the autumn harvest to further lead farmers in developing hog production.

### 浙江生猪头数逾五百万  大大超过今年指标

1957-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    浙江生猪头数逾五百万
    大大超过今年指标
    新华社杭州1日电　浙江省今年养猪数量已达到五百零九万口，提前和超额完成了今年养猪四百五十万口的生产指标。
    中共浙江省委和浙江省人民委员会最近又发出指示，要求各地抓紧秋收前后的时间，进一步领导农民发展养猪生产。
