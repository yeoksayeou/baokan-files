Title: The Soviet Union Will Publish an Important Work of Modern History
Author: Lian Rou
Date: 1957-11-07
Page: 7

Academic Trends

    The Soviet Union Will Publish an Important Work of Modern History

    To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, the Institute of History of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and the State Political Book Publishing House are preparing to publish a work of modern history, "Soviet Russia and the Capitalist World, 1917-1923." The main material for this book is taken from Volume VIII of the ten-volume "World History."

    This book discusses the main events of the Russian October Revolution and the revolutionary climax in the capitalist world after the First World War. The author demonstrates the true role of Russia in the course of world history with a large amount of practical material.

    The main part of the book elaborates on the history of the great socialist revolution and the establishment of the Soviet state. The victory of the Russian October Revolution opened a new era in human history.

    The book devotes considerable space to commenting on the First World War launched by the imperialists. At the end of the First World War, revolutionary situations appeared in many countries in Europe and Asia. In Russia, the signs of mature revolutionary conditions were particularly obvious. The Bolshevik Party successfully utilized the revolutionary situation, established Soviet power, and issued a call for world revolution and just peace to all the peoples of the world. The working masses of Germany, Austria-Hungary, Britain, France, Italy, and other countries waged heroic struggles in support of the peace without annexation and indemnity advocated by Soviet Russia. The author elaborates in detail on the turbulent revolutionary upsurge and the national liberation movements of the oppressed nations in the capitalist world.

    The revolutionary wave from Europe to the Americas is a clear sign of the overall crisis of capitalism not only in the economic, class composition, and national relations, but also in the ideological aspects. During the period of 1917-1923, communist parties were established in most countries. The proletariat is becoming the decisive force guaranteeing the development of society towards democracy, peace, and socialism. The Russian working class played a vanguard role in the struggle of working people around the world to overthrow capitalism.

    The book is permeated with the idea of proletarian international solidarity as an important condition for the victory of revolutionary struggles.

    The author shows that the main content of the historical process after the First World War is the struggle between two systems - capitalism and socialism.

    The last part of the book mainly elaborates on the establishment of the Soviet socialist system and the formation of the first worker-peasant state in the world - the Soviet Union.

    Scientific institutions and scholars from brotherly countries such as Bulgaria, Hungary, China, Poland, Romania, Czechoslovakia, and Yugoslavia have provided great assistance to the authors of this book in correctly and objectively solving many problems in the historical period under discussion.
                        (Lian Rou)

### 苏联将出版一本现代史的重要著作
练柔
1957-11-07
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    苏联将出版一本现代史的重要著作
    为了庆祝十月革命四十周年，苏联科学院历史研究所和国家政治书籍出版社正准备出版一本现代史的著作——“1917—1923年的苏维埃俄国与资本主义世界”。这本书的主要材料取自十卷本“世界通史”的第八卷。
    在这本书中，谈到了俄国十月革命以及第一次世界大战后资本主义世界中革命高潮时期的主要事件。作者以大量的实际材料表明了俄国在世界历史进程中的真正的作用。
    该书主要篇幅是阐述伟大的社会主义革命和苏维埃国家成立之初的历史。俄国十月革命的胜利开始了人类历史的新纪元。
    这本书以相当多的篇幅评述了帝国主义者所发动的第一次世界大战。第一次世界大战结束时，欧洲和亚洲许多国家出现了革命的形势。在俄国，革命的条件业已成熟的迹象尤加显著。布尔什维克党成功地利用了革命的形势，建立了苏维埃政权，并向全世界各族人民发出了世界革命、正义和平的号召。德国、奥匈帝国、英国、法国、意大利和其他国家的劳动群众，为支持苏维埃俄国所倡议的不割地、不赔款的和平进行了英勇的斗争。作者详尽地阐述了汹涌澎湃的革命高潮和资本主义世界被压迫民族的民族解放运动。
    从欧洲到美洲的革命浪潮，是资本主义总危机不仅在经济、阶级构成、民族关系方面，而且在思想意识方面的鲜明的标志。在1917—1923年这一时期里，大多数国家都建立了共产党。无产阶级正在成为保证社会走向民主、和平和社会主义发展的决定性的力量。俄国工人阶级在全世界劳动者推翻资本主义的斗争中，起着先锋队的作用。
    书中贯串着作为胜利的革命斗争重要条件的无产阶级国际团结的思想。
    作者表明，第一次世界大战后历史进程中的主要内容，就是存在着两种制度——资本主义与社会主义的斗争。
    该书最后一部分主要是阐述苏维埃社会主义制度的确立和世界上第一个工农国家——苏联的形成。
    保加利亚、匈牙利、中国、波兰、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、南斯拉夫等兄弟国家的科学机关和学者们，对本书作者在正确地、客观地解决所探讨的这一历史时期许多问题方面，曾给予很大的帮助。
                        （练柔）
