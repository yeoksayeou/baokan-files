Title: "Friendly News" Reader Symposium
Author:
Date: 1957-11-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th: TASS reports: The Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a reader symposium for the "Friendly News," the official publication of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, on the 18th. Participants in the symposium included Perevertailo, Vice Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association; Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhang Yingwu, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union; and representatives from all walks of life in Moscow.

At the symposium, Perevertailo expressed hope that the magazine that the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association will soon publish for China will fully utilize the valuable experience of the Chinese "Friendly News." Attendees at the symposium welcomed the first few issues of the "Friendly News" after it became a weekly publication.

Finally, Yang Hansheng expressed his gratitude to the Soviet readers for their concern for the "Friendly News."

### “友好报”读者座谈

1957-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

    “友好报”读者座谈
    据新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联对外文化协会18日举行了中苏友好协会总会机关刊物“友好报”的读者座谈会。参加座谈会的有：苏中友好协会理事会副主席佩雷维尔塔伊洛、中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙和中国驻苏联大使馆文化参赞张映吾，以及莫斯科的各界代表。
    佩雷维尔塔伊洛在座谈会上表示，希望苏中友好协会即将为中国出版的杂志充分地利用中国“友好报”的宝贵经验。参加座谈会的人们都对“友好报”改为周刊后的头几期表示欢迎。
    最后，阳翰笙对于苏联读者对“友好报”的关切表示感谢。
