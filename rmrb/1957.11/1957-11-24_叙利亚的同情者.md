Title: Sympathizer of Syria
Author: Shen Yinmo
Date: 1957-11-24
Page: 8

    The winds and clouds of the Middle East are constantly surging towards Damascus, the capital of Syria, once praised by Muhammad as a paradise on earth.
    Oil has been continuously fueling the desires of profiteers. Colonialists want to use the magic hand of their partners—the dream of the Ottoman Empire's restoration—to spread the seeds of fire, with wishful thinking, adopting a blitzkrieg approach, starting from Syria, stirring up Arab countries, so as to loot a burning house, and further burn to the places on earth they think need to be burned.
    However, the Syrian people are not weak and easily bullied, nor are they isolated and helpless. Just listen to the neat steps of the joint Egyptian-Syrian army, the Soviet Union's loud warning, the People's Republic of China's supportive statements, and the voices of justice from other countries.
    Those who play with fire, be sober! A powerful fire extinguisher in the socialist camp—the first Earth satellite—has appeared in the sky, circling the Earth day and night, fully loaded with the sincere desire to maintain lasting peace and unparalleled strong force, ready to serve human peace and eliminate the flames of war. October 18, 1957

### 叙利亚的同情者
沈尹默
1957-11-24
第8版()
专栏：

    叙利亚的同情者
    沈尹默
    中近东的风云，不断地涌向大马士革，这是叙利亚的首都，曾经为穆罕默德所赞礼，它是地球上的天堂。
    石油一直在灌浇着暴利贪图者的欲火，殖民主义者想借用他们的伙伴——奥斯曼帝国复辟的梦想的魔手，来散播火种，打着如意算盘，采取闪电方式，从叙利亚烧起，烧乱阿拉伯国家，好趁火打劫，再进一步烧到地球上他们认为须要烧掉的地方。
    可是，叙利亚人民本不是软弱可欺的，也不是孤立无助的，试听听叙埃联合军队的整齐步伐，苏维埃联盟的宏亮警告，中华人民共和国的支持言论，其他国家里的正义呼声。
    玩火者们清醒些吧！社会主义阵营里一架强大灭火机——第一颗地球卫星已经出现在天空中，昼夜不停地环绕着地球巡礼，它满载着维护永久和平的真挚意愿和无比坚强的力量，准备着为人类和平服务，消灭战火。1957年10月18日
