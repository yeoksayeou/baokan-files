Title: Asian-African Legal Workers Conference Holds Group Discussions
Author:
Date: 1957-11-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, November 9th: The Asian-African Legal Workers Conference on November 8th divided into five subcommittees to discuss the following main issues: nationalization, imperialism, public freedoms and natural rights, aggression and the legal consequences of aggression, and world peace.

	Chen Shouyi, deputy head of the Chinese delegation, delivered a report on the issue of world peace at the fifth subcommittee discussing world peace. In his report, Chen Shouyi emphasized the importance of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, disarmament, expanding trade, and developing cultural exchange and economic cooperation. He also strongly condemned the "Eisenhower Doctrine" and emphasized the importance of supporting the just struggles of Arab countries, especially the recent struggle of Syria against American conspiracies.

	At the meeting held on the afternoon of the 7th, representatives from Algeria, Cyprus, Vietnam, Cameroon, Libya, Lebanon, Tunisia, Morocco, Kuwait, as well as representatives from the Arab League and the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, spoke at the meeting. Most of the representatives expressed their full support for Syria's struggle. The representative of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, Vassilev, proposed a suggestion on behalf of the association, advocating the dispatch of an international committee of legal workers to investigate the atrocities of the imperialists in Algeria.

### 亚非法律工作者会议  进行小组讨论

1957-11-10
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非法律工作者会议
    进行小组讨论
    据新华社大马士革9日电　亚非法律工作者会议11月8日分成五个小组委员会讨论下列各项主要问题：国有化、帝国主义、公众自由和天赋权利、侵略和侵略的法律后果以及世界和平。
    中国代表团副团长陈守一在讨论世界和平问题的第五小组委员会上作了关于世界和平问题的报告。陈守一在报告中强调指出了和平共处的五项原则、裁军、扩展贸易、发展文化交流和经济合作的重要性。他还强烈谴责“艾森豪威尔主义”，强调支持阿拉伯国家的正义斗争、特别是最近叙利亚反对美国阴谋的斗争的重要性。
    在7日下午举行的会议上，来自阿尔及利亚、塞浦路斯、越南、喀麦隆、利比亚、黎巴嫩、突尼斯、摩洛哥、科威特的代表，以及阿拉伯联盟和国际民主法律工作者协会的代表都曾经在会上发了言，其中大部分代表都表示充分支持叙利亚的斗争。国际民主法律工作者协会代表瓦西列夫代表协会提出一项建议，主张派遣国际法律工作者委员会前往调查帝国主义者在阿尔及利亚的暴行。
