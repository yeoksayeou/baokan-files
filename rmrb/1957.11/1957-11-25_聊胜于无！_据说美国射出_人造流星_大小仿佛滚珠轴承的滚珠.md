Title: Better Than Nothing! The US is Said to Have Launched "Artificial Meteors" the Size of Ball Bearings

Author:

Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 24th: Bedford, Massachusetts dispatch: As the far-reaching impact of the Soviet satellite launch becomes increasingly apparent, the US Air Force local research center announced on the 22nd that it had launched "artificial meteors" on October 16th - several small aluminum balls about the size of ball bearings.

These aluminum balls were launched by rockets at an altitude of fifty-four miles above the Earth. According to Mr. Dubin, an expert who participated in the launch of these small spheres: "We cannot control the flight direction of these aluminum balls after the explosion."

The "New York Herald Tribune" published an editorial entitled "Small Spheres". The editorial said: "We should thank these small spheres, because they bring us comfort. But, the Air Force announcing that they launched small spheres the size of ball bearings is a trivial matter... The bad thing is that these small spheres are invisible, while the Soviet satellites can be seen."

### 聊胜于无！  据说美国射出“人造流星”大小仿佛滚珠轴承的滚珠

1957-11-25
第6版()
专栏：

    聊胜于无！
    据说美国射出“人造流星”大小仿佛滚珠轴承的滚珠
    新华社24日讯　马萨诸塞州培德福德市消息：在苏联人造卫星发射成功的深远影响越来越显露的时候，美国空军地方研究中心在22日宣布，它在10月16日曾经发射了“人造流星”——同滚珠轴承里的滚珠一般大小的几颗铝制小球。
    这些铝制小球是在离地球五十四英里的高空由火箭发射出去的。据参加发射这些小圆球的一位专家杜宾说：“我们无法控制爆炸以后的这些铝制小球的飞行方向。”
    “纽约先驱论坛报”发表了题为“小圆球”的社论。社论说：“我们应该感谢这些小圆球，因为它们给我们带来了慰借。但是，空军宣布他们发射了同滚珠轴承的滚珠大小相仿的小球，这种消息真是一件微不足道的事……糟糕的是这些小球看不见，而苏联的卫星却可以看见。”
