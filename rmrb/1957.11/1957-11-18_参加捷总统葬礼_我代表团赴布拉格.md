Title: Our Delegation Travels to Prague to Attend Czechoslovak President's Funeral
Author:
Date: 1957-11-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 17th: Li Xiannian, head of the Chinese government delegation, and delegation member Saifudin, left Moscow by plane on the morning of the 17th for Prague to attend the funeral of Czechoslovak President Sapotocky.

Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, saw them off at the airport.

Delegation member Cao Ying is currently in Czechoslovakia.

### 参加捷总统葬礼  我代表团赴布拉格

1957-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

    参加捷总统葬礼
    我代表团赴布拉格
    新华社莫斯科17日电　参加捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基同志葬礼的中国政府代表团团长李先念和团员赛福鼎，17日上午乘飞机离开莫斯科前往布拉格。
    中国驻苏联大使刘晓前往机场送行。
    代表团团员曹瑛现在捷克斯洛伐克。
