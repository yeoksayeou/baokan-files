Title: Zhengzhou No. 5 State-owned Cotton Mill Goes Into Production
Author:
Date: 1957-11-05
Page: 4

    Zhengzhou No. 5 State-owned Cotton Mill, the fourth cotton textile factory newly built by the state in Zhengzhou during the First Five-Year Plan, officially went into production on the 1st.
    The scale of this factory is the same as that of Zhengzhou No. 4 State-owned Cotton Mill, which went into production in May of this year, with a total of more than 83,000 spindles and 3,700 automatic looms. All the cotton yarn spun by this factory is supplied to the factory's weaving; the cotton cloth woven each year can provide more than 21 million people with new clothes.
                  (According to Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 郑州国棉五厂投入生产

1957-11-05
第4版()
专栏：

    郑州国棉五厂投入生产
    第一个五年计划期间国家在郑州新建的第四座棉纺织厂——国营郑州第五棉纺织厂，已在1日正式投入生产。
    这个厂的规模和今年5月投入生产的郑州国棉四厂相同，共拥有八万三千多枚纱锭和三千七百台自动布机。这个工厂纺成的棉纱全部供给本厂织布；每年织成的棉布可供二千一百多万人各做一身新衣。
                  （据新华社讯）
