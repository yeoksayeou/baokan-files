Title: May First Brand Watch
Author: Wu Songcao
Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 3

    New Products

    May First Brand Watch
    The first batch of 300 "May First" brand new product seventeen-jewel waterproof watches produced by the Tianjin Watch Factory have been test-marketed in Tianjin. "May First" brand watches are available in both full steel and semi-steel versions, with a thin movement and large three hands. The design is beautiful and elegant. The "May First" brand logo is on the top of the dial, and the words "China Tianjin" are at the bottom. To meet consumer needs, the annual output will gradually increase to more than 240,000 pieces in the future.
                        (Wu Songcao)

### 五一牌手表
吴松操
1957-11-26
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    五一牌手表
    天津市手表厂生产的第一批“五一”牌新产品十七钻防水手表三百只，已在天津试销。“五一”牌手表分全钢半钢两种，细机、大三针，式样设计得美观大方，在表盘的正上方有“五一”牌图案，下端有“中国天津”四字。为了满足消费者的需要，将来年产量可逐步提高到二十四万余只。
                        （吴松操）
