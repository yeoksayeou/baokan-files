Title: State Council Appointments and Removals

Author:

Date: 1957-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency

    State Council Appointments and Removals

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The State Council, at its 60th plenary meeting on November 1st, approved the appointment and removal of a number of government and school staff members. The list is as follows:

    Appointed:
    Shen Hong as Vice Minister of the Coal Industry Ministry;
    Gui Peng as Assistant Minister of the Railways Ministry;
    Zhu Yuanding as President of Shanghai Fisheries College.

    Removed:
    Shen Hong from the post of Vice Minister of the Electrical Machinery Manufacturing Industry Ministry;
    Mao Cheng from the post of President of Dalian Maritime Institute;
    Liu Lie夫 from the post of Vice President of Changchun Automobile Tractor Institute;
    Peng Weisan from the post of President of Shandong Normal College;
    Lu Changshan from the post of President of Henan Medical College;
    Liu Ruilin from the post of President of Jiangxi Normal College;
    Liang Tangjin from the post of President of Guangxi Normal College;
    Bai Xiqing, Chen Yingqian, and Que Senhua from the posts of Vice Presidents of China Medical University.

### 国务院任免名单

1957-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社讯　国务院在11月1日第六十次全体会议上通过任免一批政府、学校工作人员，名单如下：
    任命沈鸿为煤炭工业部副部长；
    桂蓬为铁道部部长助理；
    朱元鼎为上海水产学院院长。
    免去沈鸿的电机制造工业部副部长职务；
    茅珵的大连海运学院院长职务；
    刘列夫的长春汽车拖拉机学院副院长职务；
    彭畏三的山东师范学院院长职务；
    卢长山的河南医学院院长职务；
    刘瑞霖的江西师范学院院长职务；
    梁唐晋的广西师范学院院长职务；
    白希清、陈应谦、阙森华的中国医科大学副校长职务。
