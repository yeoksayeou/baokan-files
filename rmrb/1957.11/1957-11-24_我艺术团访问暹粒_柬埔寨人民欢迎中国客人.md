Title: Our Art Troupe Visits Siem Reap: Cambodian People Welcome Chinese Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-11-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Siem Reap, 22nd: The Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, which is currently on a performance tour in Cambodia, arrived in Siem Reap on the 21st from Kampong Cham. The delegation performed two shows in Kampong Cham, winning enthusiastic applause from 12,000 spectators.

The delegation received a warm welcome from Cambodian local authorities, residents, and overseas Chinese along the way to Siem Reap. The Governor of Kampong Thom Province, Phan Sang, invited the Chinese delegation to a lunch personally prepared by his wife.

### 我艺术团访问暹粒  柬埔寨人民欢迎中国客人

1957-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我艺术团访问暹粒
    柬埔寨人民欢迎中国客人
    据新华社暹粒22日电　正在柬埔寨作访问演出的中国文化艺术代表团21日从磅湛到达暹粒。代表团曾经在磅湛演出了两场，赢得了一万二千名观众的热烈欢迎。
    代表团在去暹粒的沿途受到柬埔寨地方当局、居民和华侨的热烈欢迎。磅同省省长潘桑曾经请中国代表团吃了一顿由他的妻子亲自准备的午餐。
