Title: Indonesian "Youth Daily" Reveals: American Ships Repeatedly Smuggle Arms to Riau; US-Chiang Kai-shek Agents Use Singapore as Headquarters for Subversive Activities
Author:
Date: 1957-11-25
Page: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 23rd: The Indonesian "Youth Daily" reported today that the "Wild Bull Council" of Central Sumatra discovered four hundred boxes of brand new arms marked as supplies for Burma when searching an American ship in Riau in mid-November and has "confiscated" them.
    The newspaper wrote that only the "Wild Bull Council" knows whether this is a genuine "confiscation" or a pre-arranged unloading. The same "confiscation" incident has happened many times.
    The "Youth Daily" said that Singapore has become the headquarters for a third party to carry out subversive activities against Indonesia. There, one of the "richest countries" in the world provides money to Chiang Kai-shek clique elements, who have spent hundreds of thousands of dollars to carry out propaganda attacks on President Sukarno and the Indonesian government. Chiang Kai-shek clique elements are also in contact with the Sumitro faction of the Indonesian Socialist Party.

### 印度尼西亚“青年报”揭露  美国船屡向廖内私运军火  美蒋特务利用新加坡作为颠覆活动总部

1957-11-25
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚“青年报”揭露
    美国船屡向廖内私运军火
    美蒋特务利用新加坡作为颠覆活动总部
    据新华社雅加达23日电　印度尼西亚“青年报”今天报道，中苏门答腊“雄牛师委员会”11月中在廖内搜查一艘美国船时发现了四百箱写有供应缅甸字样的崭新的军火，并且已经加以“没收”。
    报纸写道，这是真正的“没收”抑或是预先有意安排在那里卸下的，那只有“雄牛师委员会”才知道。同样的“没收”事件已经发生过多次了。
    “青年报”说，新加坡已经成为第三方面对印度尼西亚进行颠覆活动的总部的所在地。在那里，世界上的一个“最富有的国家”供应金钱给蒋介石集团分子，他们已经花了数十万美元来进行攻击苏加诺总统和印度尼西亚政府的宣传活动。蒋介石集团分子也同印度尼西亚社会党苏米特罗派分子接触。
