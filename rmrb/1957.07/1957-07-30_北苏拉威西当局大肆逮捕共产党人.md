Title: North Sulawesi Authorities Arrest Communists En Masse
Author:
Date: 1957-07-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 27th: Antara News Agency reports that large-scale arrests occurred in Manado and Minahasa, North Sulawesi, a few days ago. Approximately thirty people were arrested, the majority of whom were local leaders of the Indonesian Communist Party, as well as members of the Indonesian Nationalist Party.

Among those arrested were Kareh Soebit, Secretary of the East Indonesian Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party and member of the Constitutional Assembly, Tuuyuwalie, Indonesian Communist Party member of the Minahasa Regional Council, and Warokka, former Deputy Mayor of Manado and member of the Indonesian Communist Party.

### 北苏拉威西当局大肆逮捕共产党人

1957-07-30
第6版()
专栏：

    北苏拉威西当局大肆逮捕共产党人
    据新华社雅加达27日电　安塔拉通讯社消息说，北苏拉威西的万鸦老和米那哈沙在几天前发生了大规模的捕人事件。被捕的约有三十人，其中大半是当地的印度尼西亚共产党领袖，也有印度尼西亚国民党人士。
    印度尼西亚共产党东印度尼西亚委员会书记、制宪议会议员卡雷·苏比特，米那哈沙地方议会的印度尼西亚共产党议员土尤瓦利和前万鸦老副市长、印度尼西亚共产党员瓦罗科都在被捕之列。
