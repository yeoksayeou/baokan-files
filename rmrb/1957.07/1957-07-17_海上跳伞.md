Title: Sea Parachuting
Author: Wen Xiong, An Yi
Date: 1957-07-17
Page: 8

    Sea Parachuting
    Riding on clouds of white,
    Drifting in the sky.
    Scattering clusters of jade blossoms,
    Blooming on the sea.
    Above, ten thousand miles of floating clouds,
    Below, ten thousand fathoms of roaring waves.
    The ocean opens its warm embrace,
    The fleet speeds over,
    To give us a fond embrace.
          Wen Xiong, An Yi

### 海上跳伞
文雄　安禕
1957-07-17
第8版()
专栏：

    海上跳伞
    驾着一朵朵白云
    在天际飘摇
    撒下一簇簇琼花
    盛开在海上
    头上是万里云飘
    脚下是万丈波涛
    海洋张开了温暖的怀抱
    舰队飞驰过来
    向我们亲切的拥抱
          文雄　安禕
