Title: Nie Rongzhen Receives Mihai et al. Su Yu Hosts Japanese Ex-Servicemen Delegation

Author:
Date: 1957-07-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 15th. Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen received today, in the morning, the Hungarian group chairman of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, Sofinecz Mihai, and committee members Ujházy László, Bandó Gábor, etc., and held cordial talks on issues related to Sino-Hungarian scientific and technological friendly cooperation.
    Di Jingxiang, the Chinese group chairman of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, and committee member Li Xuan were also present at the meeting.

    Xinhua News Agency, July 16th. General Su Yu, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, held a banquet tonight to host the Japanese ex-servicemen delegation headed by Saburo Endo.

### 聂荣臻接见米海依等  粟裕宴请日前军人访华团

1957-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻接见米海依等
    粟裕宴请日前军人访华团
    新华社15日讯　国务院副总理聂荣臻在今天上午接见了中匈科学和技术合作委员会匈牙利组主席索菲涅茨·米海依，委员乌依哈茨·拉斯洛、班多·加波尔等，并且就中匈科学和技术友好合作方面的问题进行了亲切的交谈。
    接见时，中匈科学和技术合作委员会中国组主席狄景襄和委员李轩也在座。
    据新华社16日讯中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕大将今天晚上举行宴会，招待以远藤三郎为首的日本前军人访华团。
