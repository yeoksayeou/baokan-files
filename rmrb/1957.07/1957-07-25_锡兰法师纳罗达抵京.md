Title: Ceylon Monk Narada Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-07-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: Ceylon Monk Narada arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Buddhist Association of China.

    Venerable Narada has lectured on Buddhism around the world five times. He visited China in 1935.

    Also arriving in Beijing on the same plane was the Chinese Buddhist scholar, Venerable Liao Can, who studied Buddhism in Ceylon for eleven years. He will return to China to teach at the Chinese Buddhist Academy and continue his work in researching and translating Buddhist scriptures.

### 锡兰法师纳罗达抵京

1957-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰法师纳罗达抵京
    据新华社24日讯　应中国佛教协会邀请到中国访问的锡兰纳罗达法师，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    纳罗达法师曾五次周游世界讲授佛学。他在1935年来过中国。
    同机到达北京的还有曾经在锡兰研究佛学十一年的中国佛学家了参法师。他这次回国将在中国佛学院教课，并继续研究和翻译佛经的工作。
