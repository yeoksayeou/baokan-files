Title: Japanese Salt Industry Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Japanese Salt Industry Delegation, consisting of eleven members and led by head of the delegation, Hideo Tanaka, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

    The delegation will consult with relevant departments in our country on matters relating to the salt industry, and will visit and inspect salt production and the soda-making industry.

### 日本盐业访华代表团抵京

1957-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    日本盐业访华代表团抵京
    新华社19日讯　日本盐业访华代表团一行十一人，在团长田中秀男率领下今天飞抵北京。
    代表团将同我国有关部门协商有关盐业方面的问题，并将参观访问食盐的生产和制碱工业。
