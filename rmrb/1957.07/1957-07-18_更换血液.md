Title: Changing Blood

Author:

Date: 1957-07-18
Page: 6

    Changing Blood
    In Casablanca, a newborn infant had bled to death, but was revived six minutes later.
    Because the infant's parents had conflicting blood types, doctors decided to replace the infant's blood to relieve his suffering. During the blood change, the infant's heart stopped beating, but after the new blood was injected, it started beating again.

### 更换血液

1957-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

    更换血液
    在卡萨布兰卡地方，有一个刚出生的婴孩已经流尽血死了，但在六分钟后又被救活了。
    由于这个婴孩的父母的血型互相矛盾，医生们决定给这个婴孩更换他身上的血以解除他的痛苦。在更换血液期间，这个婴孩的心脏停止了跳动，可是在注射了新的血液之后，它又跳动起来了。
