Title: Please Bring the Friendship of the Chinese People to the People of Pakistan - Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet to See Off Pakistan Parliamentary Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-07-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 12th. Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing, hosted a banquet in Beijing this evening to bid farewell to the Pakistan Parliamentary Delegation.

    Peng Zhen delivered a speech at the beginning of the reception. He said that the delegation's visit would further enhance mutual understanding and deepen friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan. Peng Zhen, on behalf of the people of Beijing and the 600 million people of China, expressed heartfelt gratitude for the delegation's visit and asked them to bring the friendship of the people of Beijing and the 600 million people of China to the citizens of Karachi and the entire Pakistani people.

    Head of the delegation, Rahman, then delivered a speech, saying, "We are about to leave China, and we are leaving with the following feeling: the Chinese people want peace, and the Chinese people want to live in peace with the people of Pakistan and the people of the world." Rahman said, "Your country is developing very well, and your development is so rapid that any Asian country would be proud of it." Rahman also stated that the delegation would bring the good wishes and greetings of the Chinese people to the Pakistani people.

    Also present at the banquet this evening were Saifuddin, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Liu Geping, President of the Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association, as well as leading members of various people's organizations and well-known figures from all walks of life in the capital, totaling more than ninety people.

    Pakistan's Ambassador to China, A. Al Haj M. Ahmed and his wife, and China's Ambassador to Pakistan, Geng Biao and his wife, also attended the banquet.

### 请把中国人民的友谊带给巴基斯坦人民  彭真设宴欢送巴基斯坦议会代表团

1957-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    请把中国人民的友谊带给巴基斯坦人民
    彭真设宴欢送巴基斯坦议会代表团
    新华社12日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真今天晚上在北京设宴欢送巴基斯坦议会代表团。
    彭真在酒会开始的时候致词。他说，通过代表团的这次访问将进一步增进中巴两国人民之间的相互了解和加深中巴两国人民之间的友谊。彭真代表北京市和全中国人民向代表团的访问表示衷心的感谢，并请他们把北京市人民和全中国六万万人民的友谊带给卡拉奇的市民和全巴基斯坦人民。
    拉赫曼团长接着致词说，我们就要离开中国了，我们是带着下边这样的心情离开的：中国人民是要和平的，中国人民是要同巴基斯坦人民以及世界人民和平相处的。拉赫曼说，你们的国家发展得很好，你们的发展是这样迅速，以致任何的亚洲国家都引以为骄傲。拉赫曼还表示，代表团将把中国人民良好的祝愿和问候带给巴基斯坦人民。
    出席今晚宴会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长赛福鼎，国务院秘书长习仲勋，中巴友好协会会长刘格平，以及各人民团体的负责人和首都各界知名人士共九十多人。
    巴基斯坦驻华大使阿哈默德及夫人，中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飙及夫人也出席了宴会。
