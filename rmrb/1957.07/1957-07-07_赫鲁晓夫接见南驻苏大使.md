Title: Khrushchev Receives Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1957-07-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 5th (TASS): The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union, V. Mićunović, on July 5th at his request and spoke with him.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见南驻苏大使

1957-07-07
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见南驻苏大使
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社莫斯科5日电：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫在7月5日接见了要求会见他的南斯拉夫驻苏联大使维·米丘诺维奇，同他谈了话。
