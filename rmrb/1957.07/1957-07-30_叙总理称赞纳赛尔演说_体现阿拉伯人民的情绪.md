Title: Syrian Prime Minister Praises Nasser's Speech as Reflecting the Sentiments of the Arab People
Author:
Date: 1957-07-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 29th. Syrian Prime Minister al-Asali said on July 27th that President Nasser's speech at the mass rally in Alexandria expressed the sentiments of all free Arab residents of Arab countries.

Referring to the issue of a federation between Syria and Egypt, he said that Arab nationalist forces are currently making their best efforts to once again realize the unity between Syria and Egypt. He believes that "this effort will be successful." He expressed "the hope that the two countries can achieve their common goals as soon as possible."

### 叙总理称赞纳赛尔演说  体现阿拉伯人民的情绪

1957-07-30
第6版()
专栏：

    叙总理称赞纳赛尔演说  体现阿拉伯人民的情绪
    新华社大马士革29日电　叙利亚总理阿萨利7月27日说，纳赛尔总统在亚历山大港的群众大会上发表的演说，表示了阿拉伯各国一切自由的阿拉伯居民的情绪。
    在提到叙利亚和埃及的联邦问题时，他说，阿拉伯民族主义力量目前正在尽最大的努力再一次实现叙利亚和埃及两国之间的团结。他相信，“这种努力将取得成功。”他表示“希望两国能够尽快实现它们的共同目标。”
