Title: All-India Anti-Nuclear Weapons Congress Issues Appeal, Hoping London Disarmament Conference Will Break Deadlock, Our Delegation Sets Off for Japan to Attend World Conference Against Hydrogen Bombs
Author:
Date: 1957-07-30
Page: 5

    All-India Anti-Nuclear Weapons Congress Issues Appeal
    Hoping London Disarmament Conference Will Break Deadlock
    Our Delegation Sets Off for Japan to Attend World Conference Against Hydrogen Bombs
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 29th: The All-India Anti-Nuclear Weapons Congress, which closed on July 28th, issued an appeal calling for an immediate agreement on a long-term cessation of nuclear weapons testing.
    At its final meeting last night, the Congress also adopted a resolution on disarmament, urging the United Nations to take measures to end the stalemate in the Disarmament Subcommittee. The resolution called for an increase in the number of member states in the Disarmament Subcommittee, allowing governments of countries that can help resolve differences between nuclear powers to participate in the Subcommittee.
    Xinhua News Agency, July 29th: The Chinese delegation to the Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament, led by Cai Tingkai, left Beijing by plane for Japan today.

### 全印反对核武器代表大会发出呼吁  希望伦敦裁军会议打开僵局  我代表团启程赴日参加禁止氢弹世界大会

1957-07-30
第5版()
专栏：

    全印反对核武器代表大会发出呼吁
    希望伦敦裁军会议打开僵局
    我代表团启程赴日参加禁止氢弹世界大会
    据新华社新德里29日电　7月28日闭幕的全印反对核武器代表大会发出呼吁，要求立即就长期停止核爆炸试验问题达成协议。
    大会在昨晚举行的最后一次会议上还通过了关于裁军问题的决议，吁请联合国采取措施结束裁军小组委员会的僵持状态。决议要求增加裁军小组委员会的会员国，让能够帮助解决原子核国家之间的分歧的国家的政府参加小组委员会。
    据新华社29日讯　出席第三届禁止原子弹、氢弹和争取裁军世界大会的中国代表团，在团长蔡廷锴率领下今天乘飞机离开北京去日本。
