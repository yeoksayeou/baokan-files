Title: Bianliang Watermelons
Author: Liu Miao
Date: 1957-07-11
Page: 6

Market Inspection

    Bianliang Watermelons
    Kaifeng County is a nationally renowned production area for Bianliang watermelons. On the evening of June 30th, the first truckload of Bianliang watermelons was shipped to Beijing. This month, 33.5 million jin of watermelons will be transported to 33 regions, including Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Inner Mongolia, Shanxi, and the Northeast.

    In order to allow more people to enjoy Bianliang watermelons, Kaifeng County has expanded its watermelon planting area from 20,000 mu last year to 32,000 mu, with an estimated output of 80 million jin.
    ·Liu Miao·

### 汴梁西瓜
刘淼
1957-07-11
第6版()
专栏：市场巡礼

    汴梁西瓜
    开封县是全国驰名的汴梁西瓜产地。6月30日晚，第一车汴梁西瓜已运往北京。本月内，将有三千三百五十万斤西瓜运往北京、上海、天津、内蒙古、山西、东北等三十三个地区。
    为了让更多的人吃到汴梁西瓜，开封县已将西瓜播种面积由去年的二万亩扩大到三万二千亩，估计可产瓜八千万斤。　　·刘淼·
