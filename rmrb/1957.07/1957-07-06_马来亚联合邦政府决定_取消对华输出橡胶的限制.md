Title: The Government of the Federation of Malaya Decides to Remove Restrictions on Rubber Exports to China
Author:
Date: 1957-07-06
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, July 5th, Kuala Lumpur dispatch: The Minister of Commerce and Industry of the Federation of Malaya, Ismail, said on July 4th that the government of the Federation of Malaya has decided to remove all restrictions on rubber exports to China.
    He said: Since Britain relaxed trade restrictions on China, Malaya has been awaiting details of this decision from the British Colonial Office. Now, the Federation government has decided to follow Britain's lead.
    The Minister of Commerce and Industry said that the provision to lift restrictions on rubber exports to China will remain in effect until August 31st this year, when the Federation of Malaya declares its independence. He did not specify what policies would be adopted after independence.
    The Federation of Malaya had announced a conditional lifting of the "embargo" on rubber exports to China in June of last year.

### 马来亚联合邦政府决定  取消对华输出橡胶的限制

1957-07-06
第7版()
专栏：

    马来亚联合邦政府决定
    取消对华输出橡胶的限制
    新华社5日讯　吉隆坡消息：马来亚联合邦工商部长伊斯梅尔7月4日说，马来亚联合邦政府已经决定取消对中国输出橡胶的一切限制。
    他说：自从英国放宽对中国贸易的限制以后，马来亚方面一直期待着英国殖民地部发来关于这项决定的细节。现在，联合邦政府已经决定仿效英国的做法。
    这位工商部长说，取消对中国输出橡胶的限制这一规定将继续生效到今年8月31日马来亚联合邦宣告独立为止。他没有说明独立后将采取什么政策。
    马来亚联合邦曾经在去年6月份宣布有条件地解除对中国输出橡胶的“禁运”。
