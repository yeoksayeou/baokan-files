Title: He is like...
Author: Guan Hua
Date: 1957-07-27
Page: 8

Poetry Handbill

    He is like...
    Guan Hua
    Like a fox in human clothing,
    He comes to the countryside,
    To express "compassion" to the peasants.
    Like a poisonous tree stretching its branches,
    In a field full of flowers,
    Dripping foul poison.
    Like a sharp-beaked owl,
    Squatting in a dark tree hollow,
    Cursing the light of day.
    Like an evil sorcerer,
    Blowing a horn to the azure sky,
    Calling forth the dark clouds of capitalism.

### 他像……
管桦
1957-07-27
第8版()
专栏：诗传单

    他像……
    管桦
    像狐狸披着人的外衣，
    他来到乡村，
    向农民表白“怜悯”之心。
    像伸张枝叶的毒树，
    在开满鲜花的原野，
    滴下恶臭的毒汁。
    像长着利嘴的猫头鹰，
    蹲在阴暗的树洞，
    诅咒白昼的光明。
    像罪恶的巫师，
    向碧蓝的天空吹起牛角，
    呼唤资本主义的阴云。
