Title: Khrushchev Receives Ho Chi Minh
Author: 
Date: 1957-07-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 17th - TASS reports that the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on July 17th, who was passing through Moscow on his way to visit European countries. They had a cordial and friendly conversation together.

Those present at the meeting included Hoang Van Hoan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Nguyen Van Kinh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, Andropov, responsible staff member of the Central Committee of the CPSU, and Zimyannin, Soviet Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见胡志明

1957-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见胡志明
    新华社莫斯科17日电  据塔斯社报道，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫7月17日接见了在前往欧洲国家访问途中路过莫斯科的越南民主共和国主席胡志明。他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    谈话时在座的有越南劳动党中央政治局委员黄文欢、越南民主共和国驻苏大使阮文镜、苏共中央委员会负责工作人员安德罗波夫和苏联驻越南民主共和国大使齐米亚宁。
