Title: Ask Him
Author: Zhang Zhimin
Date: 1957-07-17
Page: 8

Ask Him
Zhang Zhimin
　　I recently came from the countryside,
　　Bringing some peasant talk:
　　Who says we are not living better than before?
　　Those with eyes should see it:
　　No more beggars roam the streets,
　　No more lifting the pot to find no rice,
　　New Year's Eve
　　No more landlords逼死人 [逼死人 means "forcing people to death"],
　　In the dead of winter
　　No more wearing only a tattered jacket,
　　The son of the long-term laborer went to university,
　　The pig feeder wears a "felt gown"
　　…
　　Ask him—
　　If this is not called improvement,
　　Could it be called "decline"!
　　Who says the cooperative is not good?
　　If you haven't seen it, you should have heard it:
　　More pigs, more sheep, more grain,
　　Fewer poor, fewer illnesses, fewer lazy people,
　　The elderly and lonely are cared for,
　　There are solutions for disasters,
　　If individuals farm alone,
　　Who can afford a tractor?
　　For generations,
　　Who has made a "plan" for our village?
　　This year, one mu yields five shi [石, a unit of dry measure],
　　Next year, one mu will yield eight shi…
　　Ask him—
　　If this is not called superior,
　　Could it be called "collapse"!
　　So to speak—
　　Only the house of "Huang Shiren" [a notorious landlord in Chinese revolutionary opera] agrees.

### 问问他
张志民
1957-07-17
第8版()
专栏：

    问问他
    张志民
    最近我从农村来，
    带来几句庄稼话：
    谁说我们过得没以前好？
    有眼的就该瞧见吧：
    再没叫花子满街串，
    再不掀锅没米下，
    三十儿晚上
    再没账主子逼死人，
    十冬腊月
    再不只披件破单褂，
    扛长工的儿子上了大学，
    喂猪的穿上了“毡疙疸”
    ……
    问问他——
    这不叫提高，
    难道叫“低啦”！
    谁说合作社不行？
    没瞧见也该听见吧：
    猪多啦，羊多啦，粮食多啦，
    穷少啦，病少啦，懒汉少啦，
    孤老有人管，
    灾荒有办法，
    个人单干
    谁种地使得起拖拉机？
    祖祖辈辈
    谁给俺们村作过“规划”？
    今年一亩打石五，
    明年一亩要打石八……
    问问他——
    这不叫优越，
    难道叫“垮啦”！
    那么说——
    只有“黄世仁”家。
