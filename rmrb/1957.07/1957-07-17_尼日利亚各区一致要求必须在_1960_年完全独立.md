Title: Nigerian Regions Unanimously Demand Complete Independence by 1960
Author:
Date: 1957-07-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Lagos, 16th - News: The Action Group, the ruling party of the Western Region of Nigeria, decided at its third representative conference to strive for Nigeria's complete independence by April 2, 1960.

The Eastern, Western, and Northern regions of Nigeria have consistently demanded complete independence in 1960. However, at the recent conference in London on the issue of Nigerian independence, the British refused to specify a date for granting independence, although they stated that they agreed in principle to Nigerian independence.

At the same time, the Eastern Region Government Party of Nigeria, as well as the National Council of Nigeria and the Cameroons, have also announced their intention to work towards achieving Nigeria's independence in 1960.

### 尼日利亚各区一致要求必须在1960年完全独立

1957-07-17
第7版()
专栏：

    尼日利亚各区一致要求必须在1960年完全独立
    新华社16日讯　拉各斯消息：尼日利亚西部地区执政党行动派的第三次代表大会决定争取在1960年4月2日获得尼日利亚的完全独立。
    尼日利亚东部、西部和北部地区一直要求在1960年获得完全独立。但是，在不久前结束的在伦敦举行的关于尼日利亚独立问题的会议上，英国拒绝规定给予独立的具体日期，虽然它表示在原则上同意尼日利亚独立。
    同时，尼日利亚东部地区政府党以及尼日利亚和喀麦隆国民会议也宣布要为尼日利亚在1960年实现独立而努力。
