Title: To a Friend
Author: Xie Mian
Date: 1957-07-27
Page: 8

Poetry leaflet

    To a Friend
    Xie Mian
    Brothers who have taken the wrong path, quickly wipe away your tears
    A Communist Youth League member's responsibility is not just repentance
    Our socialist motherland needs us to defend it
    Come, quickly join the ranks of the anti-Rightist struggle

### 给朋友
谢冕
1957-07-27
第8版()
专栏：诗传单

    给朋友
    谢冕
    走过错路的兄弟，快擦干泪水
    共青团员的责任不仅是忏悔
    社会主义祖国要我们保卫
    来，快跟上反右派斗争的大队
