Title: Sentinel's Morning Song
Author: Sheng Xian
Date: 1957-07-13
Page: 8

    Sentinel's Morning Song
  　Sheng Xian
    The eastern sky has already been burned red by the rosy clouds,
    The western sky still hangs with drowsy stars,
    Moonlight has just plated a layer of silver on my bayonet,
    The morning sun has smeared a layer of gold on this silver blade.
    Good morning, hometown!
    Good morning, mother!
    May this sunlight interwoven with the love of your sentinel,
    Kiss the villages and towns of the motherland.
          1957.4.8, Dawn in Ichon, Korea

### 哨兵晨歌
省先
1957-07-13
第8版()
专栏：

    哨兵晨歌
  　省先
    东半天已被霞光烧红，
    西半天还挂着困倦了的星星，
    月光刚在我刺刀上镀了一层银，
    朝阳又在这银刀上抹了一层金。
    早啊，故乡！
    早啊，母亲！
    愿这阳光交织着我哨兵的爱情，
    去亲吻祖国的乡村城镇。
          1957、4、8、黎明于朝鲜伊川
