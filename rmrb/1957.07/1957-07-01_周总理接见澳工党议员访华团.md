Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Australian Labor Party Delegation

Author:
Date: 1957-07-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Premier Zhou Enlai received the Australian Labor Party delegation, headed by Mr. Haylen, this afternoon.

Zhang Xiruo, Qiao Guanhua, Wu Maosun, and Yu Peiwen were also present at the reception. (Attached photo)

Premier Zhou Enlai in conversation with the head of the delegation, Mr. Les Haylen. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Chengjian photo.

### 周总理接见澳工党议员访华团

1957-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见澳工党议员访华团
    新华社30日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以海伦为首的澳大利亚工党议员访华团。
    接见时，张奚若、乔冠华、吴茂荪、俞沛文也在座。（附图片）
    周总理和团长里斯利·海伦在交谈。　新华社记者　淤澄建摄
