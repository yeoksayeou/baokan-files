Title: National Defense Commission Holds Plenary Session, Discusses Report on Military Construction Overview and Two Drafts
Author:
Date: 1957-07-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 17th—The National Defense Commission of the People's Republic of China held its third plenary session on July 16th and 17th. A total of 69 vice chairmen and members attended the meeting.

At the meeting, attendees heard and discussed the Ministry of National Defense's report on the overview of military construction of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and discussed the draft "Discipline Regulations of the Chinese People's Liberation Army" and the "Provisional Regulations on Preferential Treatment for Retired Officers."

### 国防委员会举行全体会议  讨论了军事建设概况汇报和两个草案

1957-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    国防委员会举行全体会议
    讨论了军事建设概况汇报和两个草案
    新华社17日讯  中华人民共和国国防委员会在7月16日到17日举行了第三次全体会议，到会的有副主席、委员共六十九人。
    会上听取和讨论了国防部关于中国人民解放军军事建设概况的汇报，并讨论了中国人民解放军纪律条令和关于军官退休优待办法的暂行规定两个草案。
