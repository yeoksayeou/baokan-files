Title: Leningrad Holds Grand Parade Celebrating 250th Anniversary of Founding

Author: 
Date: 1957-07-09
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Leningrad, July 8th: According to TASS, Leningrad held a grand parade on July 7th with 700,000 residents celebrating the 250th anniversary of the city's founding.

	The parade began at ten o'clock in the morning. The marching columns passed in front of the Winter Palace, a famous landmark of the October Revolution. They enthusiastically cheered Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kozlov, Kuusinen, Furtseva, Khrushchev, Shvernik, and the leaders of the Leningrad city and region, who were standing on the reviewing stand.

	Among the parading contingents were workers from the Putilov factory (now the Kirov factory), renowned for its revolutionary traditions; workers from the metal factory manufacturing giant water turbines and steam turbines; workers from the Nevsky Machine Building Plant, the "Elektrosila" Electrical Equipment Plant, and other enterprises. People held red banners with slogans calling for universal peace. Many slogans expressed support for the decision of the June Plenum of the CPSU Central Committee regarding the anti-Party group of Malenkov, Kaganovich, and Molotov.

	At twelve noon, the Peter and Paul Fortress fired a salute.

	The parade in Leningrad lasted three hours, demonstrating the strength and power of the Soviet people, their unity around the CPSU Central Committee, and their determination to strive for new economic and cultural development.

### 庆祝建城二百五十周年  列宁格勒举行大游行

1957-07-09
第7版()
专栏：

    庆祝建城二百五十周年
    列宁格勒举行大游行
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社列宁格勒讯：列宁格勒的七十万居民7月7日举行了盛大的游行，庆祝这个城市建立二百五十周年。
    游行在早上十点钟开始。游行队伍经过了十月革命的著名的纪念地冬宫的前面。他们向站在检阅台上的布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、科兹洛夫、库西宁、福尔采娃、赫鲁晓夫、什维尔尼克，以及列宁格勒市和州的领导人热烈欢呼。
    游行队伍中有以革命传统著称的普梯洛夫工厂（现名基洛夫工厂）的工人、制造巨型水轮机和汽轮机的五金工厂的工人，?瓦斯基机器制造厂、“电力”电工器材厂和其他企业的工人。人们举着红色的横幅，上面写着呼吁普遍和平的口号。很多标语都表示赞同苏共中央六月全会关于马林科夫、卡冈诺维奇、莫洛托夫反党集团的决议。
    正午十二时，彼得罗巴夫罗夫斯克堡垒鸣了礼炮。
    列宁格勒的游行历时三小时，显示了苏联人民的强大和威力，显示了他们在苏共中央周围的团结一致以及争取经济和文化的新发展的决心。
