Title: Discussion of the Resolutions of the June Plenary Session of the CPSU Central Committee: Party Activist Meetings Continue to Be Held Across the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-07-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 6th dispatch: According to TASS, party organization activists in many large Soviet cities have recently held meetings one after another to discuss the resolution of the June Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union regarding the anti-Party group of Malenkov, Kaganovich, and Molotov.

Party activist meetings have been held in Vilnius, the capital of Lithuania, Ashgabat, the capital of Turkmenistan, as well as Kharkov, Stalino, Gorky, Khabarovsk, Rostov, and Stavropol.

At the meeting held in Vilnius, Sniečkus, member of the CPSU Central Committee and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Lithuanian Communist Party, reported on the results of the CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session. Those who spoke at the meeting agreed with the resolutions of the CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session. The meeting unanimously passed a resolution firmly condemning the activities of the anti-Party group of Malenkov, Kaganovich, and Molotov.

The meeting held in Kharkov heard a report from Titov, member of the CPSU Central Committee and First Secretary of the Kharkov Oblast Committee of the Ukrainian Communist Party. Thirty-one people spoke at the meeting. They unanimously supported the resolutions of the CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session and condemned the anti-Party views and actions of the Malenkov, Kaganovich, and Molotov group.

In addition, the Presidium of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions also met to discuss the resolutions of the CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session, and passed a resolution stating that the Presidium of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions fully supported the resolutions of the CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session and believed that the timely exposure of the anti-Party group was a great achievement of the CPSU Central Committee.

In a resolution passed at a party meeting, Ukrainian writers fully supported the resolutions of the CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session, firmly condemned the sectarian activities of the anti-Party group, and assured the Party that they would unite their ranks more closely under the Party's invincible banner.

### 讨论苏共中央六月全会决议  苏联各地继续举行党积极分子会议

1957-07-07
第6版()
专栏：

    讨论苏共中央六月全会决议
    苏联各地继续举行党积极分子会议
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社报道，苏联许多大城市党组织积极分子最近几天陆续举行会议，讨论苏联共产党中央委员会6月全体会议关于马林科夫、卡冈诺维奇和莫洛托夫反党集团的决议。
    立陶宛首都维尔纽斯、土库曼首都阿什哈巴德以及哈尔科夫、斯大林诺、高尔基、伯力、罗斯托夫、斯塔夫罗波尔等城市都举行了党的积极分子会议。
    在维尔纽斯举行的会议上，苏共中央委员、立陶宛共产党中央委员会第一书记斯涅奇库斯报告了苏共中央全体会议的结果。在会上发言的人都赞同苏共中央全会的决议。会上一致通过了一项决议，坚决谴责马林科夫、卡冈诺维奇和莫洛托夫反党集团的活动。
    哈尔科夫举行的会议听取了苏共中央委员、乌克兰共产党哈尔科夫州委员会第一书记季托夫的报告。三十一个人在会上发言。他们一致拥护苏共中央全会的决议，谴责马林科夫、卡冈诺维奇和莫洛托夫集团的反党观点和行动。
    此外，全苏工会中央理事会主席团也开会讨论了苏共中央全体会议的决议，并且通过决议指出，全苏工会中央理事会主席团完全拥护苏共中央全体会议的决议，并且认为及时揭露反党集团是苏共中央的极大的功绩。
    乌克兰作家在党的会议上通过的一项决议中完全拥护苏共中央全体会议的决议，坚决谴责反党集团的宗派活动，并且向党保证他们将在党的不可战胜的旗帜下更紧密地团结他们的队伍。
