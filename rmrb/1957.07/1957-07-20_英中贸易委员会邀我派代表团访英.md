Title: Sino-British Trade Council Invites Chinese Delegation to Visit Britain
Author:
Date: 1957-07-20
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, London, July 19th: The Sino-British Trade Council announced on the 18th that it had invited China to send a technical delegation, or a series of delegations composed of various experts, to visit Britain in early autumn. After the first Chinese technical delegation arrives and concludes its investigations in Britain, the issue of British entrepreneurs visiting China in return will be discussed.

The Sino-British Trade Council announced the above after preliminary exchanges of views with the Chinese Commercial Counsellor here on the issue of promoting Sino-British trade. This council is composed of the British Association of Chambers of Commerce, the Federation of British Industries, the National Union of Manufacturers, and major British trade groups related to trade with China.

### 英中贸易委员会邀我派代表团访英

1957-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    英中贸易委员会邀我派代表团访英
    新华社伦敦19日电  英中贸易委员会18日宣布，它已经邀请中国在初秋时派遣一个或一系列由各种专家组成的技术代表团前来访问英国。在第一个中国技术代表团到达并结束了在英国的调查工作以后，将讨论英国企业家回访中国的问题。
    英中贸易委员会在就促进英中贸易问题同这里的中国商务参赞初步交换意见之后，宣布了上面这件事。这个委员会是由英国全国总商会、英国工业联合会、全国制造商联盟以及同对华贸易有关的英国主要贸易团体组成的。
