Title: A Huge Oil Painting (and Another Poem)
Author: Gù Gōng
Date: 1957-07-25
Page: 8

    A Huge Oil Painting (and Another Poem)
    Gu Gong
    In the mess hall of a warship
    Hangs a huge oil painting,
    Is this a sailor's practice work?
    Or from the brush of a master...
    Huge waves are engulfing the sun
    The sea has overflowed into the sky
    A small boat with flames spurting from its stern
    Is smashing through water columns, piercing through fire nets
    Rushing towards the black enemy ship, rushing onward...
    The gazes of the Red Army soldiers on the small boat
    Shine like lightning
    Erupt like bullets
    Every time I put down my spoon and gaze at the oil painting
    I feel waves of fever, burning
    As if a brave storm blows out of the painting
    Stirring up towering waves in the sea of my heart...

    To Our Seaplanes
    You are the seagull's close friend
    You are the dove's companion
    The entire ocean is your plaza
    The entire ocean is your runway
    Every time you land
    White waves embrace you tightly
    You drop fierce bombs
    On the rampant enemy ships
    You drop life-saving rubber rings
    To the overturned fishing boats
    You are the seagull's close friend
    You are the dove's companion

### 一幅巨大的油画（外一首）
顾工
1957-07-25
第8版()
专栏：歌唱解放军

    一幅巨大的油画（外一首）
    顾工
    在军舰的餐厅里面
    挂着一幅巨大的油画，
    这是一位水兵的习作？
    还是出自那位大师的手笔……
    骇浪在吞卷着太阳
    海水泛滥到了天上
    一艘尾部冒着火光的小艇
    在撞碎水柱，穿进火网
    向黑色的敌舰冲去，冲去……
    小艇上几个红军士兵的目光
    像雷电一样闪亮
    像子弹一样迸放
    我每次放下汤匙，凝望油画
    就会感到一阵阵的发热、发烧
    好像画中吹出一股勇猛的风暴
    掀起我心海中的万丈波涛……
    致我们的水上飞机
    你是海鸥的密友
    你是白鸽的同伴
    整个大海，都是你的广场
    整个大海，都是你的跑道
    每当你降落的时候
    白色的浪花就把你团团拥抱
    你对横行的敌舰
    投下一枚枚烈性的炸弹
    你对倾复的渔船
    投下一个个救生的皮圈
    你是海鸥的密友
    你是白鸽的同伴
