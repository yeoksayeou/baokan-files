    High-Energy Physics Conference
    The seventh annual Rochester Conference on High-Energy Physics was held at the University of Rochester in the United States from April 15th to 19th, 1957. Approximately three hundred nuclear physicists were invited to attend, including four Nobel Prize winners and eighty-five leaders from prominent research institutions in twenty-five countries outside the United States. These countries included the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Yugoslavia, Canada, the United Kingdom, France, India, Italy, Japan, and others. After the conference, the organizers arranged a two-week tour for the foreign representatives to visit several high-energy facilities across the United States.
    Last year, three Soviet scientists participated in the Rochester conference for the first time. They presented the progress of nuclear physics research in the Soviet Union at the conference. Following that conference, fourteen American scientists accepted the invitation of the Soviet Academy of Sciences to attend the High-Energy Physics Conference held in Moscow last May. The American scientists reported that, with the full facilities provided by their hosts, they visited some Soviet scientific installations and obtained a great deal of information about the activities and achievements of the Soviet Union in science and technology over the past thirty years.
    The purpose of the Rochester conference is to promote basic research in high-energy nuclear physics by enabling members of the world scientific community to engage in open and informal discussions and exchanges of ideas. Of course, the issues discussed at the conference were all non-confidential.

### 高能物理学会议

1957-07-18
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    高能物理学会议
    1957年4月15日到19日，美国罗彻斯特大学召开了罗彻斯特高能物理学会议第七次年会。被邀请参加会议的核子物理学家约三百多人，其中包括诺贝尔奖金获得者四人和八十五个从美国以外二十五个国家来的著名研究机关的领导人。这些国家是苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、南斯拉夫、加拿大、英国、法国、印度、意大利、日本等。会后，会议主持者还为外国代表们组织了一个为时两星期的参观，使他们参观了美国各地的一些高能的装置。
    去年有三个苏联科学家曾经第一次参加了罗彻斯特的会议。他们在会上介绍了苏联的核子物理学研究工作的进展情况。在那次会议以后，有十四个美国科学家接受了苏联科学院的邀请，参加了去年5月在莫斯科召开的高能物理学会议。美国科学家曾经报告说：他们在主人所给予的充分便利条件下，参观了苏联的一些科学装置，并且得到了许多关于苏联近三十多年来在科学技术方面的活动和成就的资料。
    罗彻斯特的会议的目的，是要使世界科学界的成员们能够通过公开的和非正式的讨论和交换意见，以促进高能核子物理学的基本研究。当然，他们在会上所讨论的问题全是属于非保密性的。  （程）
