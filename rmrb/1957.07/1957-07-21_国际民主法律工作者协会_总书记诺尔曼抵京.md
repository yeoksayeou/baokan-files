Date: 1957-07-21

Secretary-General of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, Nordmann, Arrives in Beijing

(Our Newspaper) Beijing—Nordmann, Secretary-General of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, and his wife arrived in Beijing on the 20th at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Association.

### 国际民主法律工作者协会  总书记诺尔曼抵京

1957-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    国际民主法律工作者协会
    总书记诺尔曼抵京
    本报讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来华访问的国际民主法律工作者协会总书记诺尔曼和他的夫人，已于二十日抵达北京。
