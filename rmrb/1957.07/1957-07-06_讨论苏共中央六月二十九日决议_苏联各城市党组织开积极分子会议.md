Title: Discussion of the CPSU Central Committee Resolution of June 29th: Party Organizations in Soviet Cities Hold Meetings of Activists
Author:
Date: 1957-07-06
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, July 5th: TASS reports that Communist Party organizations in Moscow and other Soviet cities have held meetings of party activists in the past two days to discuss the resolution of the Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union regarding the anti-Party group of Malenkov, Kaganovich, and Molotov.

The party organizations of Moscow City and Moscow Oblast held meetings of party activists on July 2nd and 3rd, respectively. Bulganin, Khrushchev, Furtseva, Pervukhin, and Moskatov, Chairman of the Auditing Commission of the CPSU Central Committee, participated in these two meetings. Khrushchev delivered a lengthy speech at both meetings. Bulganin and Pervukhin also spoke at the meeting in Moscow City.

At these two meetings, Furtseva, Secretary of the Moscow City Party Committee, and Kapitonov, First Secretary of the Moscow Oblast Party Committee, delivered reports on the resolution of the CPSU Central Committee. During the discussion of their reports and the resolution of the CPSU Central Committee, speakers unanimously pointed out that under the leadership of the CPSU Central Committee, the Soviet people, implementing the resolutions of the 20th Party Congress, have made tremendous achievements in economic and cultural construction. They unanimously agreed with the resolution of the Party Central Committee and condemned the activities of the anti-Party group of Malenkov, Kaganovich, Molotov, and Shepilov, who joined their faction. The meetings passed resolutions, making guarantees to the CPSU Central Committee, stating that the Communists of Moscow City and Oblast will consolidate their unity around the CPSU Central Committee and strive to solve the tasks of communist construction. At the same time, more than eight thousand party organizations in industrial enterprises, construction sites, and institutions in Moscow City held meetings to discuss the resolution of the CPSU Central Committee. Aristov, Brezhnev, Voroshilov, Mikoyan, Furtseva, Shvernik, and Pospelov gave reports at the meetings. A total of about 60,000 people spoke during the discussion of the reports, expressing their satisfaction with the resolution of the CPSU Central Committee.

Party organizations in cities such as Leningrad, Kiev, Minsk, Alma-Ata, Tashkent, Yerevan, Tbilisi, and Riga also held meetings of party activists. These meetings were also conducted in an atmosphere of unity within the party ranks. All meetings unanimously approved the resolution of the CPSU Central Committee and condemned the activities of the anti-Party group of Malenkov, Kaganovich, and Molotov.

### 讨论苏共中央六月二十九日决议  苏联各城市党组织开积极分子会议

1957-07-06
第7版()
专栏：

    讨论苏共中央六月二十九日决议
    苏联各城市党组织开积极分子会议
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社报道：莫斯科和苏联其他一些城市的共产党组织这两天来举行了党积极分子会议，讨论苏联共产党中央委员会全体会议关于马林科夫、卡冈诺维奇和莫洛托夫反党集团的决议。
    莫斯科市和莫斯科州的党组织先后在7月2日和3日举行了党的积极分子会议。布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫、福尔采娃、别尔乌辛和苏共中央检查委员会主席莫斯卡托夫分别参加了这两次会议。赫鲁晓夫在这两次会议上发表了长篇讲话。布尔加宁和别尔乌辛等也在莫斯科市的会议上讲了话。
    在这两个会议上，莫斯科市党委会书记福尔采娃和莫斯科州党委会第一书记卡皮托诺夫分别就苏共中央的决议作了报告。在讨论他们的报告和苏共中央决议的时候，发言的人一致指出，苏联人民在苏共中央的领导下，执行着第二十次党代表大会的决议，在经济和文化建设方面都取得了巨大的成就。他们一致赞同党中央的决议，并且谴责了马林科夫、卡冈诺维奇、莫洛托夫以及参加他们一伙的谢皮洛夫的反党集团的活动。会议通过决议，向苏共中央提出保证，表示莫斯科市和州的共产党员将巩固地团结在苏共中央的周围为解决共产主义建设的任务而奋斗。和这同时，莫斯科市的工业企业、建筑工地和机关还有八千多个党组织举行了会议，讨论苏共中央的决议。阿里斯托夫、勃列日?夫、伏罗希洛夫、米高扬、福尔采娃、什维尔尼克和波斯伯洛夫在会议上作了报告。在讨论报告时发言的总共约有六万人，他们对苏共中央的决议表示满意。
    列宁格勒、基辅、明斯克、阿拉木图、塔什干、埃里温、第比利斯、里加等城市的党组织也举行了党的积极分子会议。这些会议也是在党的队伍团结一致的气氛中进行的。所有的会议都一致赞成苏共中央的决议，并且谴责马林科夫、卡冈诺维奇、莫洛托夫反党集团的活动。
