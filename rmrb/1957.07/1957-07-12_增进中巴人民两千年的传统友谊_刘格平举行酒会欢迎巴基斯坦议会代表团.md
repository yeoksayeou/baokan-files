Title: Enhance the 2000-Year Traditional Friendship Between the Chinese and Pakistani People: Liu Geping Holds Reception to Welcome Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-07-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 11th—Liu Geping, President of the Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association, held a reception at the Xinqiao Hotel in Beijing tonight to welcome the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation.

    Premier Zhou Enlai attended the reception.

    Liu Geping said at the beginning of the reception that the delegation had traveled a long distance, visiting many cities in our country, spreading the seeds of Sino-Pakistani friendship, and promoting mutual understanding and friendship between the people of China and Pakistan. He said that the purpose of the Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association is to enhance mutual understanding between the two peoples and develop friendly relations between China and Pakistan. He hoped that the Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association and the Pakistan-China Friendship Association would strengthen their ties and work towards a common goal. He also hoped that the delegation would bring back the Chinese people's friendship towards the Pakistani people.

    Mujibur Rahman, head of the delegation, said that after visiting many places in great China for three weeks, we are about to embark on our journey home. The warm hospitality of the Chinese government and people will never be forgotten. He said that China is a symbol of human progress and has greatly enriched human knowledge in science, culture, art, and philosophy. The world is undergoing tremendous changes, and China is also undergoing tremendous reforms. Your national leaders want to build China with modern technology. We are happy to see the great achievements you have made in industry and agriculture, and we wish you even greater achievements in the future. He concluded by saying that the people of Pakistan and China have a traditional friendship of two thousand years, we will always support you, and we will always pay attention to you.

    Liu Geping and Mujibur Rahman also presented gifts to each other on behalf of the Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association and the Pakistan-China Friendship Association.

    Pakistan's Ambassador to China, A. Ahmed, and China's Ambassador to Pakistan, Geng Biao, both attended the reception. At the meeting, Mujibur Rahman, head of the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation, presented Premier Zhou with a documentary film of Premier Zhou's visit to Pakistan.

    After the reception, the guests watched a song and dance performance.

### 增进中巴人民两千年的传统友谊  刘格平举行酒会欢迎巴基斯坦议会代表团

1957-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    增进中巴人民两千年的传统友谊
    刘格平举行酒会欢迎巴基斯坦议会代表团
    新华社11日讯　中巴友好协会会长刘格平今晚在北京新侨饭店举行酒会，欢迎巴基斯坦议会代表团。
    周恩来总理出席了酒会。
    刘格平在酒会开始时讲话说，代表团不辞劳苦，长途跋涉，访问了我国不少城市，散布了中巴友好的种子，促进了中巴两国人民之间的相互了解和友谊。他说，中巴友好协会的宗旨是要增进两国人民的互相了解，发展中巴两国的友好关系。他希望中巴友好协会和巴中友好协会加强联系，为共同的目标而努力。并希望代表团将中国人民对巴基斯坦人民的友谊带回去。
    代表团团长穆吉布尔·拉赫曼讲话说，三个星期来访问了伟大中国的许多地方，我们即将踏上归途。中国政府和人民对我们的热情招待是永远不会忘记的。他说，中国是人类进步的象征，在科学、文化、艺术和哲学等方面大大丰富了人类的知识。今天的世界正在进行着巨大变化，中国也在进行巨大的改革，你们国家的领导要用现代化的技术把中国建设起来。我们为能够看到你们在工、农业方面已取得的伟大成就而高兴，预祝你们在今后取得更巨大的成就。他最后说，巴中两国人民有着二千年的传统友谊，我们永远支持你们，而且永远关注着你们。
    刘格平和穆吉布尔·拉赫曼还代表中巴友好协会和巴中友好协会互相赠送礼品。
    巴基斯坦驻华大使阿哈默德和我国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飙都出席了酒会。会上，巴基斯坦议会代表团团长穆吉布尔·拉赫曼送给周总理一部周总理访问巴基斯坦的纪录影片。
    酒会结束后，宾主观看了歌舞表演。
