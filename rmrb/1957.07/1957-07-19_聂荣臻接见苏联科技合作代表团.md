Title: Nie Rongzhen Receives Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-07-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen today received N.M. Silyanov, Chairman of the Soviet Group of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, and all members. They are in China to attend the sixth meeting of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee.

Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen and Silyanov exchanged views on further developing scientific and technological cooperation between China and the Soviet Union in the future.

Present at the reception were Li Qiang, Acting Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, and Wang Dongnian, Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

### 聂荣臻接见苏联科技合作代表团

1957-07-19
第6版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻接见
    苏联科技合作代表团
    新华社18日讯  国务院副总理聂荣臻今天接见了中苏科学技术合作委员会苏联组主席尼·米·西露扬洛夫和全体委员。他们是来中国参加中苏科学技术合作委员会第六届会议的。
    聂荣臻副总理和西露扬洛夫等交换了有关进一步发展今后中苏两国间的科学技术合作方面的意见。
    接见时在座的有中苏科学技术合作委员会中国组代主席李强和对外贸易部技术合作局局长王东年。
