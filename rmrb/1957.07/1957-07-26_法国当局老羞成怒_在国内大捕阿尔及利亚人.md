Title: French Authorities Enraged, Mass Arrests of Algerians in France

Author:

Date: 1957-07-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th, Paris dispatch: "France-Soir" reports that Lyon authorities mobilized more than 1,200 military police on the evening of the 23rd to conduct a large-scale search and arrest operation targeting North African residents. At least 700 Algerians were arrested. This is the largest of a series of search and arrest operations conducted in mainland France since the National Assembly passed a bill on the 19th of this month granting the government "emergency powers" over Algerians in Algeria and mainland France.

### 法国当局老羞成怒  在国内大捕阿尔及利亚人

1957-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    法国当局老羞成怒
    在国内大捕阿尔及利亚人
    新华社25日讯　巴黎消息：“法兰西晚报”报道，里昂市当局23日晚间出动了军警一千二百多人，对北非籍居民进行了大规模的搜捕。至少有七百多名阿尔及利亚人被逮捕。这是自从本月19日国民议会通过授予政府在阿尔及利亚和法国本土对阿尔及利亚人的“紧急处置权”以来，在法国本土所进行的一系列搜捕活动中最大的一次。
