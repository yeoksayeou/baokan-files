Title: Regarding the Resolution of the CPSU Central Committee on June 29, the CPC Central Committee Sends a Telegram to the CPSU Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1957-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 5th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on the 5th. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union:

Thank you for informing us, through Comrade Yudin, of the resolution made by the CPSU Central Committee at its plenary session held from June 22 to 29 regarding the anti-Party group of Malenkov, Kaganovich, and Molotov. We believe that this resolution, unanimously adopted by the CPSU Central Committee plenary session, will help to further unite and consolidate the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The Communist Party of China will unswervingly unite closely with the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and jointly strive to continuously strengthen the great fraternal alliance between China and the Soviet Union, to win lasting world peace, and to win the victory of the cause of Marxism-Leninism.

          The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, July 5, 1957

### 就苏共中央六月二十九日的决议  中共中央致电苏共中央

1957-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    就苏共中央六月二十九日的决议
    中共中央致电苏共中央
    新华社5日讯　中国共产党中央委员会5日打电报给苏联共产党中央委员会，电报全文如下：苏联共产党中央委员会：
    感谢你们经过尤金同志把苏共中央在6月22日至29日举行的全体会议所作的关于马林科夫、卡冈诺维奇和莫洛托夫反党集团的决议通知我们。我们相信，苏共中央全会所一致通过的这一决议，将有助于苏联共产党的进一步的团结和巩固。中国共产党将坚定不渝地和苏联共产党亲密地团结在一起，为继续加强中苏两国的伟大的兄弟同盟，为争取世界持久和平，为争取马克思列宁主义事业的胜利而共同奋斗。
          中国共产党中央委员会　1957年7月5日
