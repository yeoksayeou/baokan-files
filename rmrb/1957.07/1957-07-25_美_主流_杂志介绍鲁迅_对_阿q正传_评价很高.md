Title: US "Mainstream" Magazine Introduces Lu Xun, Highly Praises "The True Story of Ah Q"
Author:
Date: 1957-07-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 24th: The July issue of the "Mainstream" magazine published a lengthy article of over ten thousand words introducing the Chinese writer Lu Xun. The author, Charles Humboldt, said: "Lu Xun strived throughout his life to tell people the truth about their lives and the reasons why they could not obtain freedom." The author stated that because the American bourgeoisie is unwilling to introduce Chinese literature, Americans have little opportunity to know Lu Xun. Only recently, with the People's Republic of China translating Lu Xun's works into English, have American readers had the opportunity to learn more about Lu Xun.

Humboldt believes that among Lu Xun's novels, there is a category that depicts the various images of people from different classes in the face of the anti-feudal bourgeois democratic revolution. These works are highly political. "The True Story of Ah Q" is a representative work of this category. Humboldt highly praised "The True Story of Ah Q," saying: "Ah Q possesses the conditions of a universally typical character that we like to talk about. All of us possess some of the characteristics he exhibits, and some of us even possess all the characteristics he exhibits."

The author pointed out that Lu Xun accepted historical materialism, eventually becoming a Marxist, and he supported the October Revolution and the program of the Chinese Communist Party.

### 美“主流”杂志介绍鲁迅  对“阿Q正传”评价很高

1957-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    美“主流”杂志介绍鲁迅
    对“阿Q正传”评价很高
    据新华社24日讯　纽约消息：7月份的“主流”杂志刊登了一篇一万多字的介绍中国作家鲁迅的长文。文章的作者查尔斯·亨鲍尔特说：“鲁迅一生力求向人们述说他们的生活的真相以及他们不能获得自由的原因。”作者说，由于美国资产阶级不愿介绍中国的文学，美国人很少有机会知道鲁迅。直到最近，中华人民共和国把鲁迅的著作译成英文，美国读者才有机会更多地了解鲁迅。
    亨鲍尔特认为，在鲁迅的小说中，有一类是描写在反封建的资产阶级民主革命面前不同阶级的人们的各种形象的。这类作品的政治性很强。“阿Q正传”就是这类的代表作。亨鲍尔特对“阿Q正传”评价很高，他说：“阿Q具备了我们所喜欢说的普遍性典型的条件。我们所有的人都具有他身上所表现的某些性格，我们之中有些人还具有他身上所表现的所有性格。”
    作者指出，鲁迅接受了历史唯物论，他最后成了一个马克思主义者，他支持了十月革命和中国共产党的纲领。
