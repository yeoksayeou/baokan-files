Title: Mujibur Rahman Holds Banquet; Chairman Liu Shaoqi Congratulates the Pakistani People on Their New Achievements; Vice-Chairman Song Qingling Receives Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-07-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 10th: Mujibur Rahman, head of the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation, held a banquet tonight for the delegation's visit to China.

　　Chairman Liu Shaoqi attended the banquet.

　　In his speech at the banquet, Chairman Rahman said that during our visit to China, we had the opportunity to see the tremendous achievements made by your people in just eight years. Our impression is that the Chinese people are a hardworking and diligent people under wise leadership. We want to take this impression back with us. At the same time, I assure you that in all your endeavors, you will have the sincere wishes of the Pakistani people.

　　He said that although our two countries have different social systems and forms of government, the people of our two countries are working for a common purpose, which is to work for the welfare of the people in social, cultural, political, and other aspects. Moreover, we believe that there are more avenues for exchanges between our two countries in promoting the happiness and welfare of the people of our two countries. I have had the opportunity to exchange views freely and frankly with your great leaders, and I have no doubt that the cooperative relationship between China and Pakistan will become closer and closer. Chairman Rahman said: We abide by the principles of the United Nations Charter and the letter and spirit of the Bandung Conference resolutions, and I would like to express once again my hope that all countries in the world, especially Asian countries, can abide by the letter and spirit of these lofty principles and resolutions for the common good and the progress of mankind.

　　In his speech, Chairman Liu Shaoqi said that in a short period of time, the friends of the delegation visited many places in our country, visited many of our industrial and agricultural construction projects, and spent the Islamic Eid al-Adha in Urumqi. Your friendly visit to our country has further deepened the friendship between the people of China and Pakistan.

　　The Chinese people have always cherished the friendship with the Pakistani people, because this not only conforms to the interests of the people of China and Pakistan, but also is conducive to the cause of peace in Asia and the world.

　　Chairman Liu Shaoqi said, dear distinguished guests, you will soon end your visit to our country, we would like to ask the gentlemen of the delegation to bring the friendship of the 600 million Chinese people to the Pakistani people, and sincerely congratulate the Pakistani people on their new achievements in maintaining national independence and national construction!

　　Also present at the banquet were Li Jishen, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice-Premiers He Long and Bo Yibo, Bao Erhan, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Hanfu, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs, Liu GePing of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association, and Geng Biao, our country's ambassador to Pakistan, and more than one hundred other people.

　　Pakistan's Ambassador to China, Ahmed, also attended the banquet.

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 10th: Vice-Chairman Song Qingling received the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation this afternoon.

　　Zhang Su and Zhang Bangying were also present at the reception.

　　During the reception, all members of the delegation and Mrs. Salma Tasadduq Hussain, a member of the delegation, presented gifts to Vice-Chairman Song Qingling.

### 穆吉布尔·拉赫曼举行宴会  刘少奇委员长祝贺巴基斯坦人民获得新的成就  宋庆龄副委员长接见巴基斯坦议会代表团

1957-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    穆吉布尔·拉赫曼举行宴会
    刘少奇委员长祝贺巴基斯坦人民获得新的成就
    宋庆龄副委员长接见巴基斯坦议会代表团
    新华社10日讯　巴基斯坦议会代表团团长穆吉布尔·拉赫曼在今晚为代表团访问中国举行宴会。
    刘少奇委员长出席了宴会。
    拉赫曼团长在宴会上致词说，在我们访华期间，我们有机会看到贵国人民在短短八年中所取得的巨大成就。我们的印象是：中国人民是在英明的领导下勤劳而努力工作的人民。我们要把这个印象带回去。同时，我向你们保证，在你们一切的努力中，你们将得到巴基斯坦人民真诚的祝愿。
    他说，虽然我们两国的社会制度和政府形式并不相同，但我们两国人民在为一个共同的目的而工作，那就是为人民在社会、文化、政治等方面的福利而工作。而且，我们相信，在促进两国人民的幸福与福利方面，我们两国之间还存在着更多的往来的途径。我这次有机会和你们伟大的领袖们自由而坦率地交换了意见，我毫不怀疑，中巴两国之间的合作关系将会更加日益密切。拉赫曼团长说：我们遵守联合国宪章的原则和万隆会议决议的文字与精神，我愿再一次表示这样的希望，即世界各国，特别是亚洲各国，为了共同的利益和人类的进步，都能遵守这些崇高的原则和决议的文字与精神。
    刘少奇委员长在致词中说，代表团的各位朋友在短短的时间，访问了我国不少的地方，参观了我国不少的工农业建设，并在乌鲁木齐欢度了伊斯兰教的古尔邦节。你们在我国的友好访问，进一步加深了中巴两国人民的友谊。
    中国人民一向珍视同巴基斯坦人民之间的友谊，因为这不仅符合中巴两国人民的利益，而且也有利于亚洲和世界的和平事业。
    刘少奇委员长说，敬爱的贵宾们，你们不久就要结束在我国的访问，我们愿意请代表团各位先生们把六万万中国人民的友谊带给巴基斯坦人民，并且衷心地祝贺巴基斯坦人民在维护民族独立和国家建设工作中获得新的成就！
    出席宴会的还有人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、彭真、陈叔通，副总理贺龙、薄一波，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长章汉夫，中巴友好协会会部刘格平和我国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚等一百多人。
    巴基斯坦驻华大使阿哈默德也出席了宴会。
    新华社10日讯　宋庆龄副委员长今天下午接见了巴基斯坦议会代表团。
    接见时，张苏、张邦英也在座。
    接见时，代表团全体人员和代表团团员萨尔玛·塔萨杜克胡森夫人分别向宋庆龄副委员长赠送了礼品。
