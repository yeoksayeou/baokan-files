Title: Climate Unfavorable for Flight: Ho Chi Minh Postpones Trip Abroad
Author:
Date: 1957-07-06
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 5th. According to the Vietnamese President's Office, Chairman Ho Chi Minh did not depart from Hanoi today for his foreign visit because the climate was unfavorable for flight.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh was originally scheduled to leave Hanoi today for Beijing, and then proceed to North Korea for an official visit.

An official from the President's Office told a Xinhua News Agency reporter this morning that if the weather is good, Chairman Ho Chi Minh will depart for Beijing tomorrow.

### 气候不宜于飞行  胡志明延期出国访问

1957-07-06
第7版()
专栏：

    气候不宜于飞行
    胡志明延期出国访问
    新华社河内5日电　据越南主席府消息，因为气候不宜于飞行，胡志明主席今天没有从河内启程作出国访问。
    胡志明主席原定在今天离开河内去北京，然后转赴朝鲜作正式访问。
    据主席府有关人士今晨告诉新华社记者说，如果天气良好，胡志明主席将在明天启程去北京。
