Title: A Soldier's Heart
Author: Wang Liaosheng
Date: 1957-07-30
Page: 8

    A Soldier's Heart

    A soldier's heart is a peculiar heart,
    Sometimes soft, sometimes hard,
    Soft as a gentle breeze,
    Afraid of hurting the life of a small grass;
    Hard as a roaring fire,
    Showing no mercy to the enemies of the motherland.

### 兵士的心
王辽生
1957-07-30
第8版()
专栏：

    兵士的心
    王辽生
    兵士的心是奇特的心，
    有时很软，有时很硬，
    软时一如娓娓轻风，
    害怕伤了小草的生命；
    硬时一如团团大火，
    对祖国的敌人毫不留情。
