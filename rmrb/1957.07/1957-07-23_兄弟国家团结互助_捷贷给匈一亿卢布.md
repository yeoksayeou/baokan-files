Title: Fraternal Countries Unite and Help Each Other: Czechoslovakia Loans Hungary 100 Million Rubles
Author:
Date: 1957-07-23
Page: 5

    Fraternal Countries Unite and Help Each Other
    Czechoslovakia Loans Hungary 100 Million Rubles
    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, July 21st: According to TASS, the long-term credit agreement signed in Budapest between Hungary and Czechoslovakia stipulates that Czechoslovakia will provide Hungary with a loan of 100 million rubles, with an annual interest rate of 2%.
    Starting this year, Czechoslovakia will supply Hungary with coal, coke, clay, chemical raw materials, textiles, shoes, refrigerators, bicycles, wooden furniture, utensils and other goods.

### 兄弟国家团结互助  捷贷给匈一亿卢布

1957-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家团结互助
    捷贷给匈一亿卢布
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯21日讯：在布达佩斯签字的匈牙利和捷克斯洛伐克长期信贷协定规定，捷克斯洛伐克将供给匈牙利一亿卢布的贷款，年息二厘。
    捷克斯洛伐克从今年开始把煤、焦炭、陶土、化学原料、纺织品、鞋、冷藏器、自行车、木器家具、器皿和其他货物供给匈牙利。
