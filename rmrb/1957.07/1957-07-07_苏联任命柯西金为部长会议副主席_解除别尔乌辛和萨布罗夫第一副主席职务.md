Title: The Soviet Union Appoints Kosygin as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Relieves Pervukhin and Saburov of Their Positions as First Deputy Chairmen
Author:
Date: 1957-07-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 6th (TASS): The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed A. N. Kosygin as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

The Presidium relieved M. G. Pervukhin and M. Z. Saburov of their posts as First Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联任命柯西金为部长会议副主席  解除别尔乌辛和萨布罗夫第一副主席职务

1957-07-07
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联任命柯西金为部长会议副主席
    解除别尔乌辛和萨布罗夫第一副主席职务
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社莫斯科5日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命阿·尼·柯西金为苏联部长会议副主席。
    主席团解除了米·格·别尔乌辛和马·扎·萨布罗夫的苏联部长会议第一副主席的职务。
