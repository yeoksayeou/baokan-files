Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Representatives from All Circles in Shanghai (Picture)
Author: Hou Bo
Date: 1957-07-11
Page: 1

    Chairman Mao Meets with Representatives from All Circles in Shanghai
    Chairman Mao met with representatives from Shanghai's science, education, literature, art, and industrial and commercial circles at the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Building on July 7. The picture shows Chairman Mao having a cordial conversation with them.　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo (transmitted by radio facsimile)
