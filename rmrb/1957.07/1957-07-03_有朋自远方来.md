Guests from Afar

    Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, July 1st  A Soviet scientific and technological cooperation delegation of eleven people, headed by Siluyanov, arrived in Beijing a few days ago. The delegation is here to discuss scientific and technological cooperation between China and the Soviet Union.

    Japanese Meteorologists Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, July 1st  At the invitation of Zhu Kezhen, Chairman of the National Committee of the International Geophysical Year of the People's Republic of China, and Tu Changwang, Director of the Central Meteorological Bureau, Japanese meteorologists Dr. Kisho Kanzaburo, Dr. Sakanishi Ikio, and Mori Shigeo, and their party of three, who are here to lecture and compare meteorological instruments, arrived in Beijing by train today.

    Hokkaido Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, July 1st  The Japanese Hokkaido Agricultural Delegation, a total of five people, who are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Society of Agronomy, arrived in Beijing this evening under the leadership of the head of the delegation, Heizo Shionoya.

### 有朋自远方来

1957-07-03
第7版()
专栏：有朋自远方来

    有朋自远方来
    苏联科技合作代表团抵京
    新华社1日讯　由西露扬洛夫率领的苏联科学技术合作代表团一行十一人，已在日前到达北京。代表团是前来商谈中苏两国科学技术合作问题的。
    日本气象学家抵京
    新华社1日讯　应中华人民共和国国际地球物理年国家委员会主席竺可祯、中央气象局局长涂长望邀请，前来我国讲学和进行气象仪器比较的日本气象学家岸保勘三郎博士、佐贯亦男博士、毛利茂男等一行三人，于今日乘火车抵达北京。
    北海道农业访华团到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国农学会邀请来我国访问的日本北海道农业访华代表团共五人，在团长盐野谷平藏率领下在今天晚上到达北京。
