    Rocket Traveling Passengers
    Three passengers who traveled by rocket to an altitude of one hundred kilometers have returned to Moscow. They participated in a press conference held in Moscow on the 18th. They are three little pugs named Katzyavka, Linda, and Maleshka (left to right). (Xinhua News Agency)

### 乘火箭旅行的旅客（图片）

1957-07-06
第7版()
专栏：花絮

    乘火箭旅行的旅客
    三个乘火箭到一百公里高空旅行的乘客，已经回到了莫斯科。它们于18日参加了在莫斯科举行的一次记者招待会。它们是三只小哈巴狗，名叫卡齐亚夫卡、林达和马列什卡（左到右）。（新华社）
