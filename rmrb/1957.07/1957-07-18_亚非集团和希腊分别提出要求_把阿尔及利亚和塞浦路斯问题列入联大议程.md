Title: Asian-African Group and Greece separately request that the Algerian and Cypriot issues be included in the UN General Assembly agenda
Author:
Date: 1957-07-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th, New York dispatch: The Asian-African group of nations in the United Nations decided on the 16th to request that the Algerian issue be included in the agenda of the twelfth session of the United Nations General Assembly. This session will convene in mid-September.
    The Greek representative to the United Nations also formally requested on the 15th that the Cyprus issue be included in the General Assembly agenda. The items proposed by the Greek representative are: (1) The issue of implementing the principles of national rights equality and national self-determination among the people of Cyprus Island under the auspices of the United Nations; (2) The issue of the British colonial authorities infringing upon human rights and committing atrocities against local residents.

### 亚非集团和希腊分别提出要求  把阿尔及利亚和塞浦路斯问题列入联大议程

1957-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非集团和希腊分别提出要求
    把阿尔及利亚和塞浦路斯问题列入联大议程
    新华社17日讯  纽约消息：联合国亚非集团国家16日决定要求把阿尔及利亚问题列入联合国大会第十二届会议议程。这一届会议将在9月中旬召开。
    希腊驻联合国代表也已经在15日正式要求把塞浦路斯问题列入大会议程。希腊代表提出的项目是：（一）在联合国的赞助下在塞浦路斯岛人民中实行民族权利平等和民族自决原则的问题；（二）英国殖民当局侵犯人权和对当地居民干下暴行的问题。
