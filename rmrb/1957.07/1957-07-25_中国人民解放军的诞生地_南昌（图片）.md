Title: The Birthplace of the Chinese People's Liberation Army – Nanchang (Photos)
Author: Lu Zhiguang
Date: 1957-07-25
Page: 3

    The Birthplace of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
    ——Nanchang

    Comrade Zhou Enlai's residence at the time. Here, he and his comrades decided on the uprising plan and the time of the uprising.

    Exterior view of the General Headquarters during the "August 1st" Uprising. It is now the "August 1st" Memorial Hall in Nanchang.

    During the "August 1st" Uprising, Comrades Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, He Long, Ye Ting, and others often held important meetings here. It was the command center of the entire armed struggle.

      All photos in this set are taken by Lu Zhiguang (Manuscript from Jiangxi Daily)

### 中国人民解放军的诞生地——南昌（图片）
陆之光
1957-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军的诞生地
    ——南昌
    周恩来同志当时的住所。在这里，他和战友们决定了起义计划和起义时间。
    “八一”起义时的总指挥部外景。现为南昌市“八一”纪念馆。
    “八一”起义期间，周恩来、朱德、贺龙、叶挺等同志经常在这里召开重要会议，它是整个武装斗争的指挥中心。
      本组照片均为陆之光摄（江西日报社稿）
