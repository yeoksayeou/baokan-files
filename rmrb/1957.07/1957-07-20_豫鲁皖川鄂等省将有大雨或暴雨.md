Title: Heavy Rain or Rainstorms Expected in Henan, Shandong, Anhui, Sichuan, Hubei and Other Provinces
Author:
Date: 1957-07-20
Page: 4

This newspaper article contains information transmitted by:

The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a heavy rain forecast at 12:00 noon on the 19th: It is expected that in the next 24 hours, there will be rainstorms in eastern Henan, southern and central Shandong, and northern Anhui. There will be heavy rain to rainstorms in western Henan, eastern Sichuan, northern Jiangsu, and northern Hubei.

### 豫鲁皖川鄂等省将有大雨或暴雨

1957-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    豫鲁皖川鄂等省将有大雨或暴雨
    本报讯  19日中午十二点，中央气象科学研究所发布暴雨预告：预计未来二十四小时内，河南东部、山东南部、中部、安徽北部将有暴雨。河南西部、四川东部、江苏北部、湖北北部将有大雨到暴雨。
