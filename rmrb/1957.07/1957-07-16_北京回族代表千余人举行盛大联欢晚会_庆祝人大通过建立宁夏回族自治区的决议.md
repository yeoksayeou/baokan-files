Title: Beijing Hui Representatives Hold Grand Soiree Celebrating the NPC's Resolution to Establish the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1957-07-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 15th. The China Islamic Cultural Association and the Beijing Civil Affairs Bureau jointly invited more than 1,700 representatives from all walks of life in Beijing's Hui community to a grand soiree on the 14th to celebrate the resolution passed by the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress to establish the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region. Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council and Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, and Hui representatives attending the Fourth Session of the National People's Congress, attended the soiree. Ulanhu spoke at the meeting and expressed his congratulations.
    During the soiree, Hui actors from various art troupes in Beijing and some Han actors performed various wonderful programs.

### 北京回族代表千余人举行盛大联欢晚会  庆祝人大通过建立宁夏回族自治区的决议

1957-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    北京回族代表千余人举行盛大联欢晚会
    庆祝人大通过建立宁夏回族自治区的决议
    新华社15日讯　中国回民文化协进会和北京市民政局于14日联合邀请北京各界回族代表一千七百多人，举行盛大的联欢晚会，庆祝第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议通过建立宁夏回族自治区的决议。国务院副总理兼民族事务委员会主任乌兰夫、中共中央统战部部长李维汉和参加全国人民代表大会第四次会议的回族代表，都出席了这个晚会。乌兰夫在会上讲了话，并表示祝贺。
    晚会中，北京各艺术团体的回族演员和部分汉族演员表演了各种精采节目。
