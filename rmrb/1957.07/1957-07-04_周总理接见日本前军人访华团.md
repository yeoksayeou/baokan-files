Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Japanese Ex-Servicemen Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1957-07-04
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Japanese Ex-Servicemen Delegation this afternoon.

    Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, Liao Chengzhi, Xie Nanguang, Zhao Anbo, and Xiao Xiangqian.

### 周总理接见日本前军人访华团

1957-07-04
第7版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本前军人访华团
    新华社3日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了日本前军人访华团。
    接见时，在座的有张奚若、廖承志、谢南光、赵安博、萧向前。
