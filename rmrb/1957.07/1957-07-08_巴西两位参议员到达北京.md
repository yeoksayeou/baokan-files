Title: Two Brazilian Senators Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-07-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th -- Two Brazilian senators, Vice-President of the Brazilian Socialist Party, Vilas-Boas and his wife, and Brazilian Senator Baranão and his wife, a total of four people, arrived in Beijing by train tonight.

They were invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit China after attending the World Peace Council meeting in Colombo. Since arriving in Guangzhou on June 27, they have visited Hangzhou, Shanghai, Suzhou, Nanjing, and other places.

### 巴西两位参议员到达北京

1957-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    巴西两位参议员到达北京
    新华社7日讯　巴西参议员、巴西社会党副主席维拉斯古和夫人，巴西参议员巴拉纽和夫人等四人今天晚上乘火车到达北京。
    他们是在参加世界和平理事会科伦坡会议后，应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国访问的。他们自6月27日到达广州后，曾先后访问了杭州、上海、苏州和南京等地。
