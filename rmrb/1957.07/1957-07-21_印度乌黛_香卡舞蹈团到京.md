Title: Indian Uday Shankar Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th – The world-renowned Indian Uday Shankar Dance Troupe arrived in Beijing on the night of the 19th. The dance troupe will perform here the unique Indian "shadow play" and various styles of Indian dance, which Chinese audiences have not seen before.

The dance troupe has come to China for a six-week visit and performance at the invitation of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association. This dance troupe has a history of twenty-seven years and has visited many countries in Europe, the Americas, and Asia. The troupe has a total of twenty-seven members, bringing together India's first-class dancers and musicians.

The leader of the dance troupe, fifty-six-year-old Uday Shankar, told Xinhua reporters at the train station that they would present the best of Indian art to Chinese audiences this time. Uday Shankar is one of India's most accomplished contemporary dancers, known as the "Mei Lanfang of India." He has developed the Indian "shadow play" and has made many contributions to creating new forms of Indian dance.

Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association and Vice Minister of Culture, Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and more than one hundred people from Beijing's art circles came to the station to welcome the Indian artists.

Singh, Cultural Counselor of the Indian Embassy in China, also came to welcome the dance troupe.

After performing in Beijing, the dance troupe will also visit the Northeast, East China, and South China regions. (Attached picture)

### 印度乌黛·香卡舞蹈团到京

1957-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    印度乌黛·香卡舞蹈团到京
    据新华社20日讯　世界驰名的印度乌黛·香卡舞蹈团19日夜间来到北京。舞蹈团将在这里演出中国观众没有看过的印度特有的“影子戏”和各派印度舞蹈。
    舞蹈团这次应中印友好协会邀请来中国作六个星期的访问演出。这个舞蹈团已经有二十七年历史，曾访问欧洲、美洲、亚洲许多国家，全团成员共二十七人，集中了印度第一流的舞蹈演员和音乐家等。
    舞蹈团的领导人、五十六岁的乌黛·香卡在火车站对新华社记者说，他们这次将把印度艺术最好的东西献给中国观众。乌黛·香卡是印度当代最有成就的舞蹈家，曾被誉为“印度的梅兰芳”，他发展了印度“影子戏”，并且在创造新的印度舞蹈形式方面做出不少贡献。
    中印友好协会会长、文化部副部长丁西林，中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深以及北京艺术界人士一百多人来到车站欢迎印度艺术家们。
    来欢迎舞蹈团的还有印度驻华大使馆文化参赞辛格等。
    舞蹈团在北京演出后，还将到东北、华东、华南各地访问。（附图片）
