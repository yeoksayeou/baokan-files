Title: Tunisia Abolishes Monarchy, Establishes Republic; Bourguiba Elected First President
Author:
Date: 1957-07-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, 26th: The Tunisian Constituent Assembly unanimously passed a resolution on July 25th to abolish the Tunisian monarchy and declare Tunisia a republic. It also elected the current Prime Minister and leader of the New Constitution Party, Bourguiba, as the first president, thus ending the rule of the Hussein Dynasty in Tunisia since 1705. Large crowds gathered outside the Parliament House in Bardo and cheered the decision.

Prior to the vote, Bourguiba delivered a speech to the Assembly, fiercely attacking the Tunisian monarchy. He said that the Hussein Dynasty had failed to prevent the country from becoming a French protectorate. It also failed to arouse a spirit of resistance among the people. Even when France allowed Tunisia to control its own defense forces, the King opposed it.

The government announced that July 26th would be a holiday to celebrate the founding of the republic.

### 突尼斯废黜王室改建共和  布尔吉巴当选首任总统

1957-07-27
第6版()
专栏：

    突尼斯废黜王室改建共和
    布尔吉巴当选首任总统
    据新华社26日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯制宪议会7月25日一致通过废黜突尼斯国王，宣布突尼斯为共和国，并且选出现任首相、新宪政党领袖布尔吉巴为第一任总统，从而结束了1705年以来侯赛因王朝在突尼斯的统治。聚集在议会会场巴尔多宫外面的大批群众对这个决定发出了欢呼。
    在投票之前，布尔吉巴在议会上发表演说，猛烈攻击突尼斯王室。他说，侯赛因王朝没有能够使国家不沦为法国的保护国。它也没有能够在人民中间唤起抵抗精神。甚至当法国允许突尼斯掌握自己的国防军时，国王还表示反对。
    政府宣布：7月26日为庆祝共和国成立的节日。
