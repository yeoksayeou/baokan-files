Title: Li Jishen and Guo Moruo Receive Rahman, Rahman Visits Peng Zhen and Li Dequan
Author:
Date: 1957-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th — Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Mujibur Rahman, head of the Pakistan parliamentary delegation, this morning and afternoon respectively.

Mujibur Rahman paid a visit to Mayor Peng Zhen this evening. Zhang Bangying and Feng Jiping were also present during the visit.

Mujibur Rahman also paid visits to Li Dequan and Zhang Bangying this morning.

### 李济深、郭沫若接见拉赫曼  拉赫曼拜会彭真和李德全

1957-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    李济深、郭沫若接见拉赫曼  拉赫曼拜会彭真和李德全
    新华社11日讯　李济深副委员长和郭沫若副委员长在今天上午和下午分别接见巴基斯坦议会代表团团长穆吉布尔·拉赫曼。
    穆吉布尔·拉赫曼今晚拜会了彭真市长。拜会时，张邦英、冯基平也在座。
    穆吉布尔·拉赫曼还在今天上午先后拜会了李德全和张邦英。
