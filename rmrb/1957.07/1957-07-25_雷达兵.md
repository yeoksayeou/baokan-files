Title: Radar Soldier
Author: Li Mingzhou
Date: 1957-07-25
Page: 8

    Radar Soldier
    Li Mingzhou
    The sea water beats against the shore,
    The radar antenna is rotating.
    The radar soldier's eyes emit a hidden light,
    Patrolling the motherland's vast airspace.
    The black night churns up waves.
    The radar soldier reports to the command post:
    "An enemy plane is sneaking across the border,
    Flying very low, hugging the sea surface."
    On the command post's plotting board,
    A red line and a black line are in battle,
    The black line turns its head east, desperately fleeing,
    The red line chases closely behind.
    A few minutes later, the red line catches up with the black line,
    Like an eagle catching a chick,
    The warplane is about to aim and fire,
    Suddenly the enemy plane disappears into the clouds.
    The prey nearly caught escapes,
    The commander frowns.
    But, no matter how cunning you are,
    You can't escape the radar soldier's net of heaven and earth.
    The enemy plane sways left and right,
    Like a fish caught by the tail.
    A string of sparks, a ball of fire,
    It plunges to the bottom of the sea to find its companions.
    The sea water beats against the shore,
    The radar antenna is rotating.
    The radar soldier's eyes emit a hidden light,
    Patrolling the motherland's vast airspace.

### 雷达兵
李名洲
1957-07-25
第8版()
专栏：

    雷达兵
    李名洲
    海水拍击着海岸，
    雷达天线在转动。
    雷达兵两眼发射出隐光，
    巡逻着祖国辽阔的领空。
    漆黑的夜里波浪卷起。
    雷达兵向指挥所报告：
    “一架敌机偷越国境，
    飞得很低紧贴着海面。”
    在指挥所标图板上，
    一根红线一根黑线在战斗，
    黑线扭转头朝东拼命逃窜，
    红线在后紧紧地追击。
    几分钟后红线追上了黑线，
    就像老鹰抓住了小鸡，
    战鹰正要瞄准射击，
    突然敌机消失在云里。
    刚到口的猎物被逃走，
    指挥员皱起了眉头。
    但是，任凭你怎么狡猾，
    逃不出雷达兵的地网天罗。
    敌机左摆又右摆，
    像一条被抓住尾巴的鱼。
    一串火花，一团火花，
    它栽下海底去找寻伴侣。
    海水拍击着海岸，
    雷达天线在转动。
    雷达兵两眼发射出隐光，
    巡逻着祖国辽阔的领空。
