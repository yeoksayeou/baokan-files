Title: Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation Departs Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1957-07-14
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The Pakistani parliamentary delegation left Beijing by special plane this morning to return home.

Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Liu Geping, President of the Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association, Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and more than 100 leaders from various people's organizations and democratic parties saw them off at the airport.

Geng Biao, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, and A.H.B. Tyabji, Pakistani Ambassador to China, also came to see off the delegation.

Before the distinguished guests boarded the plane, Vice Chairman Li Jishen delivered a speech, thanking the delegation for their visit and wishing them a safe journey.

In his speech, delegation leader Rahman once again praised the tremendous achievements made by the Chinese people in various constructions.

Delegation leader Rahman also said in Chinese: "Long live peace!"

Delegation leader Rahman and all members of the delegation accepted flowers presented by Chinese girls.

### 巴议会代表团离京回国

1957-07-14
第7版()
专栏：

    巴议会代表团离京回国
    据新华社13日讯　巴基斯坦议会代表团今天早晨坐专机离开北京回国。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，国务院秘书长习仲勋，中巴友好协会会长刘格平，北京市副市长乐松生，以及许多人民团体、民主党派的负责人共一百多人到机场送行。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飙以及巴基斯坦驻中国大使阿哈默德也来欢送代表团。
    李济深副委员长在贵宾登机以前讲了话，他感谢代表团的访问，并祝贵宾们一路平安。
    拉赫曼团长在讲话中，再一次赞扬了中国人民在各项建设中取得的巨大成就。
    拉赫曼团长又用中国话说：“和平万岁！”
    拉赫曼团长和全体团员接受了中国姑娘们的献花。
