Title: Delegation of the CPSU Primorsky Krai Committee Visits Heilongjiang Province
Author:
Date: 1957-07-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, July 18th: At the invitation of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the delegation of the CPSU Primorsky Krai Committee, consisting of ten members and led by Shtikov, First Secretary of the CPSU Primorsky Krai Committee, arrived in Harbin by special train this morning for a friendly visit to Heilongjiang Province.

Ouyang Qin, First Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Governor of the Province, and others welcomed the guests at the station.

Comrade Shtikov, the head of the delegation, was formerly a General of the Soviet Army, participated in the war against Japan, and visited major cities in Northeast China.

### 苏共沿海边区代表团到黑龙江省访问

1957-07-19
第6版()
专栏：

    苏共沿海边区代表团到黑龙江省访问
    据新华社哈尔滨18日电  应中共黑龙江省委员会邀请来黑龙江省作友好访问的苏共沿海边区代表团一行十人，在团长、苏共沿海边区委员会第一书记施蒂可夫率领下今天早上乘专车到达哈尔滨。
    中共黑龙江省委第一书记、省长欧阳钦等都在车站上迎接客人。
    施蒂可夫团长原来是苏联军队的上将，曾参加过对日战争，到过东北各大城市。
