Title: Karlovy Vary Film Festival Closes; "Blessing" Wins Special Prize
Author:
Date: 1957-07-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 22nd: The 10th Karlovy Vary International Film Festival in Czechoslovakia closed on July 21st.

　　The Chinese color feature film "Blessing" won a Special Prize from the jury at this film festival. The Special Prize is second only to the Grand Prize of the film festival. The Grand Prize of the film festival was awarded to the Indian film "Under the Cover of Night." The Hungarian film "Professor Hannibal," the Soviet film "Labor and Love," and the German Democratic Republic film "Lissy" won the main prizes of the film festival.

### 卡罗维发利电影节闭幕  “祝福”获得特别奖

1957-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    卡罗维发利电影节闭幕
    “祝福”获得特别奖
    新华社布拉格22日电　在捷克斯洛伐克举行的第十届卡罗维发利国际电影节7月21日闭幕。
    中国彩色故事片“祝福”在这届电影节上获得了评奖委员会的特别奖。特别奖仅次于电影节的大奖。获得电影节大奖的是印度影片“在夜幕下”。匈牙利影片“哈尼巴耳教授”、苏联影片“劳动与爱情”和德意志民主共和国影片“利西”获得了电影节的主要奖。
