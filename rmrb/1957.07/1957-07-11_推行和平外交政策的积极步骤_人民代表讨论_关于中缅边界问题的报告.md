Title: Positive Steps in Pursuing a Peaceful Foreign Policy: People's Representatives Discuss "Report on the Sino-Burmese Boundary Question"
Author:
Date: 1957-07-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th—The Fourth Session of the National People's Congress held group meetings this morning to discuss the "Report on the Sino-Burmese Boundary Question" made by Premier Zhou Enlai, concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs.

During the discussions, the various representative groups expressed their agreement with the report. The representatives emphasized that the Chinese government's negotiation with the Burmese government, based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, to resolve the boundary issue between the two countries is an active and positive step in implementing China's peaceful foreign policy, resolving specific problems, enhancing friendly relations with neighboring countries, and easing international tensions. The representatives believe that handling the Sino-Burmese boundary issue in accordance with the principles proposed by Premier Zhou Enlai in the report is fair and reasonable, not only safeguarding China's national interests but also promoting Sino-Burmese friendship and the unity of the people of Asian and African countries.

The Yunnan Province representative group, which is directly related to the Sino-Burmese border, held a heated discussion today. Many representatives pointed out that the Chinese government's handling of the Sino-Burmese boundary issue is appropriate and thorough. They expressed their support for the report and expressed their willingness to live in greater friendship and peace with their neighbor, Burma, in the future.

### 推行和平外交政策的积极步骤  人民代表讨论“关于中缅边界问题的报告”

1957-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    推行和平外交政策的积极步骤
    人民代表讨论“关于中缅边界问题的报告”
    新华社10日讯　全国人民代表大会第四次会议今天上午举行小组会，讨论了周恩来总理兼外交部长所作的“关于中缅边界问题的报告”。
    各代表小组在讨论中表示同意这个报告。代表们强调指出，我国政府同缅甸政府根据和平共处的五项原则商谈解决两国边界问题，是我国推行和平外交政策，解决具体问题、增进同邻国的友好关系、和缓国际局势的一个主动的积极的步骤。代表们认为，按照周恩来总理在报告中提出的方针来处理中缅边界问题是公平合理的，不但维护我国的民族利益，同时也促进中缅友谊和亚非各国人民的团结。
    与中缅边界直接有关的云南省的代表小组今天进行了热烈的讨论。许多代表指出，我国政府对中缅边界问题的处理是适当的、周到的，他们表示拥护这个报告，今后并且愿意同邻邦缅甸更加友好地和平相处。
