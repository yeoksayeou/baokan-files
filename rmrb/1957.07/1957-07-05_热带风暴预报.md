    Tropical Storm Forecast
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. At 12:00 noon on the 4th, the Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a tropical storm forecast:
    The tropical storm discovered in the South China Sea was estimated to be centered at 8:00 a.m. today at about 200 kilometers east of Yulin Port, Hainan Island, near 18 degrees north latitude and 112 degrees east longitude.
    It is expected that in the next 24 hours, the storm center will move slowly in a west-northwest direction and strengthen slightly. Affected by it, the northwest of the South China Sea and the Qiongzhou Strait will have strong winds of force 6-7; the Leizhou Peninsula, Hainan Island, the central coast of Guangdong, and the Beibu Gulf will have strong winds of force 5-7.

### 热带风暴预报

1957-07-05
第3版()
专栏：

    热带风暴预报
    新华社4日讯　4日中午十二时中央气象科学研究所发布热带风暴预报：
    在南海发现的热带风暴，今天上午八时中心位置估计在海南岛榆林港以东约二百公里左右的海面上，就是北纬十八度东经一一二度附近。
    预计未来二十四小时内，风暴中心将缓慢地向西偏北方向移动，并稍加强。受它的影响，南海西北部琼州海峡将有六——七级大风；雷州半岛、海南岛、广东中部沿海、北部湾将有五——七级的大风。
