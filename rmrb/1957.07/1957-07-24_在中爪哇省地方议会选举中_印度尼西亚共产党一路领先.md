Title: Indonesian Communist Party Leads in Central Java Provincial Council Elections
Author:
Date: 1957-07-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 22nd - According to incomplete information, the Indonesian Communist Party is leading in the elections for local councils in counties and cities in Central Java Province, with a significant increase in votes compared to the last general election.

According to a report in today's "People's Daily" in Jakarta, the Indonesian Communist Party has already obtained more than 1,719,000 votes in the regions of Semarang, Kedu, Solo, and Pati in Central Java Province. In the last parliamentary elections, the Indonesian Communist Party received more than 1,659,000 votes in these four regions, and more than 1,690,000 votes in the Constituent Assembly elections.

According to unofficial statistics, in the Semarang city council election, the largest city in Central Java Province, the total number of voters was over 192,000, with the Indonesian Communist Party receiving over 101,000 votes; the Nationalist Party over 25,000 votes; the Nahdlatul Ulama over 20,200 votes; and the Masyumi Party over 7,800 votes. According to this voting result, out of the thirty-five seats in the Semarang city council, the Indonesian Communist Party may get twenty seats, the Nationalist Party four seats, the Nahdlatul Ulama three seats, the National Consultative Assembly two seats, and the Masyumi Party only one seat.

According to unofficial final statistics, in the Solo city council election, the second largest city in Central Java Province, the Indonesian Communist Party received over 84,000 votes, the Nationalist Party over 28,000 votes, the Masyumi Party over 13,000 votes, and the Nahdlatul Ulama over 1,800 votes.

Local council elections have not yet been held in two other regions in Central Java Province and the special region of Yogyakarta at the provincial level.

### 在中爪哇省地方议会选举中  印度尼西亚共产党一路领先

1957-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    在中爪哇省地方议会选举中
    印度尼西亚共产党一路领先
    新华社雅加达22日电　据不完全的材料，印度尼西亚共产党在中爪哇省各县市地方议会的选举中一路领先，所得票数比上次大选时有显著增加。
    据雅加达“人民日报”今天报道，在中爪哇省的三宝垅州、吉都州、梭罗州和巴地州，印度尼西亚共产党已经获得了一百七十一万九千多选票。在上次国会选举中，印度尼西亚共产党在这四个州得到了一百六十五万九千多票，在制宪议会的选举中得到了一百六十九万多票。　
    据非官方统计，中爪哇省第一大城市三宝垅的市议会选举的投票总人数是十九万二千多人，印度尼西亚共产党得到十万一千多票；国民党二万五千多票；伊斯兰教师联合会二万零二百多票；马斯友美党七千八百多票。按这个投票结果，三宝垅市议会的三十五个席位中，印度尼西亚共产党可能得到二十席，国民党四席，伊斯兰教师联合会三席，国籍协商会二席，而马斯友美党只得到一席。
    另据非官方的最后统计，在中爪哇省第二大城市梭罗的市议会选举中，印度尼西亚共产党得到八万四千多票，国民党二万八千多票，马斯友美党一万三千多票，伊斯兰教师联合会一千八百多票。
    中爪哇省还有两个州和省一级的日惹特别区还没有举行地方议会选举。
