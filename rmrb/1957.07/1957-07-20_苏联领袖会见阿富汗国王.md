Title: Soviet Leaders Meet with King of Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1957-07-20
Page: 5

    Soviet Leaders Meet with King of Afghanistan
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th - TASS reports that Soviet state leaders Voroshilov, Khrushchev, Bulganin, Mikoyan, and Zhukov met with King Zahir Shah of Afghanistan in the Kremlin on the 19th.
    Zahir Shah held a banquet at the Grand Kremlin Palace today to host Soviet state leaders. This morning, Zahir Shah also paid separate calls to Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Zhukov, the Soviet Minister of Defense.
    Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, hosted a banquet for the King of Afghanistan at the Grand Kremlin Palace on the morning of the 18th. The King of Afghanistan arrived in Moscow on the 17th for a visit.

### 苏联领袖会见阿富汗国王

1957-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联领袖会见阿富汗国王
    据新华社莫斯科19日电  据塔斯社报道，苏联国家领导人伏罗希洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁、米高扬和朱可夫等19日在克里姆林宫会见了阿富汗国王查希尔·沙阿。
    查希尔·沙阿今天在大克里姆林宫举行宴会，招待苏联国家领导人。查希尔·沙阿今天上午还分别拜会了苏共中央委员会第一书记、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫和苏联国防部长朱可夫。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫18日上午在大克里姆林宫设宴招待阿富汗国王。阿富汗国王是17日到达莫斯科进行访问的。
