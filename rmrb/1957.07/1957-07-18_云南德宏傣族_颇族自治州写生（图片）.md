Sketches of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture, Yunnan

At the beginning of this year, the Chinese Artists Association organized ten artists and young art workers, including Shao Yu, Cheng Shifa, and Li Chenlan, from Kunming, Chongqing, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Shanghai, Xi'an, and Beijing to experience life in the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture, Yunnan. From early March to the end of May this year, they traveled almost all over the autonomous prefecture and made many sketches. From July 6 to July 14, the Chinese Artists Association sponsored their sketch exhibition, which was held at the Beijing Art Exhibition Hall. These works vividly reflect the living conditions and landscapes of the fraternal nationalities. At the preview of the exhibition, Han Guichen, a Dai student from the Central Institute for Nationalities, wrote: "This is the first time I have seen an art exhibition with our nationality as the subject. This has further increased our love for the Party, because only under the leadership of the great Chinese Communist Party can there be artistic works that reflect the lives of our Dai people."

Due to space limitations, this newspaper introduces eight works by six artists to readers here.

### 云南德宏傣族?颇族自治州写生（图片）

1957-07-18
第8版()
专栏：

    云南德宏傣族?颇族自治州写生
    中国美术家协会在今年初，组织了昆明、重庆、武汉、广州、上海、西安和北京地区的美术家和青年美术工作者邵宇、程十发、李晨岚等十人，到云南德宏傣族?颇族自治州体验生活。从今年3月初到5月底，他们几乎走遍了这一自治州的地区，作了许多写生。7月6日至7月14日，中国美术家协会主办了他们的写生画展，在北京美术展览馆展出。这些作品，对兄弟民族的生活状况和山川风貌都作了生动的反映。在展览会的预展会上，中央民族学院傣族学生罕贵琛写道：“这是我第一次看到以我们民族为题材的美术作品展览。由此更增进了我们对党的热爱，因为只有在伟大的中国共产党领导下，才可能有反映我们傣族人民生活的美术作品出现。”
