Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Meets with Catholic Representatives
Author:
Date: 1957-07-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: Vice Chairman Zhu De, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, and Chen Shutong, and President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, met with all the representatives of the Chinese Catholic Representative Conference this morning. Vice Chairman Zhu De also had a brief conversation with the members of the conference's presidium. He expressed his hope that the representatives attending the conference would unite Chinese Catholic believers, promote patriotism, and actively participate in the socialist construction of the motherland.

### 朱副主席接见天主教代表

1957-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席接见天主教代表
    新华社24日讯　朱德副主席，林伯渠、李济深、陈叔通副委员长，董必武院长，今天上午接见了中国天主教代表会议的全体代表。朱德副主席还同会议主席团的组成人员作了简短谈话。他希望出席会议的代表团结中国天主教教友，发扬爱国精神，积极参加祖国的社会主义建设。
