Title: Mexican Folk Ballet Dancers and Musicians Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The five-member delegation of the renowned Mexican Folk Ballet dancers and musicians, on their first invited visit to China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 8th.

During their visit to China, they will engage with Chinese musicians and dancers, and will also visit various places.

### 墨西哥民间芭蕾舞舞蹈家和演奏团到京

1957-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    墨西哥民间芭蕾舞舞蹈家和演奏团到京
    新华社9日讯　首次应邀来中国访问的墨西哥著名民间芭蕾舞舞蹈家和演奏团一行五人，在8日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    他们在中国访问期间，将和中国音乐、舞蹈家进行接触；并将到各地参观访问。
