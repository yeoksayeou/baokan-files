Title: Typhoon Emergency Alert
Author:
Date: 1957-07-17
Page: 5

    Typhoon Emergency Alert
    (Our report) The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon emergency alert at 6:00 PM on the 16th: At 2:00 PM today, the typhoon's center was located near 21 degrees North latitude and 116.5 degrees East longitude, approximately 50 kilometers northwest of Dongsha Island. The typhoon center is currently moving northwest at a speed of about 12 nautical miles per hour. It is expected to make landfall near Shanwei, Guangdong, around midnight tonight. The wind force in the coastal areas between Shantou and Hong Kong and the nearby sea will reach level 10-12. The wind force in the Taiwan Strait, eastern and central coastal areas of Guangdong, northeastern and northwestern parts of the South China Sea, and the middle and lower reaches of the Dongjiang River basin will increase to level 6-9. Heavy rain is expected in eastern Guangdong.

### 台风紧急警报

1957-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    台风紧急警报
    本报讯　16日下午六时，中央气象科学研究所发布台风紧急警报：今天下午两点，台风中心位置在北纬二十一度、东经一百十六点五度附近，即东沙岛西北方约五十公里的海面上。目前台风中心正以每小时十二海里左右的速度向西北方向移动，预计今天午夜前后将在广东的汕尾附近登陆，汕头至香港之间的沿海地区及附近海面上，风力可达十——十二级，台湾海峡、广东东部、中部的其他沿海地区，南海的东北部、西北部、东江流域的中下游等地区风力将增大至六——九级，广东东部将有暴雨出现。
