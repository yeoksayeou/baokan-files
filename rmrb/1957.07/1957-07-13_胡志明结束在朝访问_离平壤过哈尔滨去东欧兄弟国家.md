Title: Ho Chi Minh Ends Visit to North Korea, Departs Pyongyang, Passes Through Harbin to Eastern European Brother Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-07-13
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 12th: President Ho Chi Minh and his party concluded their visit to Korea and left Pyongyang by plane this morning.

　　It was drizzling in Pyongyang, and many people braved the rain to go to the airport to see them off with bouquets and flags. Before boarding the plane, President Ho Chi Minh reviewed the guard of honor, and then warmly embraced and said goodbye to Chairman Kim Du-bong, Premier Kim Il-sung, and Foreign Minister Nam Il, who came to see him off.

　　President Ho Chi Minh delivered a farewell speech at the airport, thanking the Korean people for their warm welcome and reception. He said that we had profound impressions during our short visit, and we personally witnessed the brilliant achievements of the Korean people, which encouraged the Vietnamese people.

　　President Ho Chi Minh said that we believe that this visit will help further develop the friendship and unity between the people of Vietnam and Korea, and we wish the Korean people greater achievements in the peaceful reunification of their motherland and socialist construction.

　　President Ho Chi Minh finally said in Korean: "Comrades, goodbye!"

　　The plane took off at 7:20.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, July 12th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and his entourage passed through Harbin on their way from Pyongyang, Korea, to visit Eastern European brother countries. Due to bad weather, they stayed here for a day.

　　During President Ho Chi Minh's stay in Harbin, he was accompanied by Qiang Xiaochu, Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Ren Zhongyi, First Secretary of the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, to tour the city of Harbin.

### 胡志明结束在朝访问  离平壤过哈尔滨去东欧兄弟国家

1957-07-13
第7版()
专栏：

    胡志明结束在朝访问
    离平壤过哈尔滨去东欧兄弟国家
    据新华社平壤12日电　胡志明主席一行结束了在朝鲜的访问，今天早晨乘飞机离开平壤。
    平壤下着小雨，许多群众拿着花束和旗帜冒雨到机场上欢送。胡志明主席临上飞机前检阅了仪仗队，然后同前来欢送的金枓奉委员长、金日成首相、南日外务相热烈拥抱作别。
    胡志明主席在机场上致告别词，他对朝鲜人民的热情欢迎和接待表示感谢，他说，我们在时间很短的访问中得到了深刻的印象，我们亲眼看到了朝鲜人民取得的光辉成就，这些成就鼓舞了越南人民。
    胡志明主席说，我们相信，我们这次访问将有助于越朝两国人民友谊和团结的进一步发展，我们预祝朝鲜人民在和平统一祖国和社会主义建设事业中取得更大的成就。
    胡志明主席最后用朝鲜话说：“同志们，再见！”
    飞机在七时二十分起飞。
    据新华社哈尔滨12日电　越南民主共和国胡志明主席和随行人员，从朝鲜平壤前往东欧各兄弟国家访问途中经过哈尔滨，因为天气不佳，在这里停留了一天。
    胡志明主席在哈尔滨停留期间，由中共黑龙江省委书记强晓初和中共哈尔滨市委第一书记任仲夷陪同，游览了哈尔滨市容。
