Title: Marshal Peng Dehuai Receives Japanese Ex-Servicemen Delegation

Author:
Date: 1957-07-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 1st. Marshal Peng Dehuai received the Japanese ex-Servicemen Delegation this morning.

When receiving the delegation, Admiral Wang Hongkun of the Navy, Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, and Zhang Xiruo and Liao Chengzhi were also present.

### 彭德怀元帅接见日本前军人访华团

1957-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀元帅接见日本前军人访华团
    新华社1日讯　彭德怀元帅今天上午接见了日本前军人访华团。
    接见时，王宏坤海军上将、常乾坤空军中将、萧向荣中将和张奚若、廖承志也在座。
