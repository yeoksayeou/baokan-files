    Wrote Over Ten Thousand Words on a Postcard
    A Viennese has created a record by handwriting ten thousand four hundred and twenty-three words on a postcard of ordinary size (13 centimeters long, 9 centimeters wide). The creator of the record is Anton Parka.
        (Xinhua News Agency)

### 在一张明信片上写一万多字

1957-07-22
第3版()
专栏：花絮

    在一张明信片上写一万多字
    一位维也纳人创造了在一张普通大小（十三公分长、九公分宽）的明信片上手写一万零四百二十三个字的纪录。纪录的创造人是安东·帕尔卡。
        （新华社）
