Title: Guatemalan Dictator Armas Assassinated by Guards

Author:

Date: 1957-07-29
Page: 3

　　Guatemalan Dictator
　　Armas Assassinated by Guards
　　Xinhua News Agency, July 28th: Guatemalan President Carlos Castillo Armas was assassinated by one of his guards at 9 p.m. on the 26th.
　　After Armas' assassination, Vice President Luis Arturo González López assumed the presidency on the morning of the 27th. He told reporters that he would continue to implement Armas' anti-communist and anti-democratic policies.

### 危地马拉独裁者  阿马斯被卫兵刺死

1957-07-29
第3版()
专栏：

    危地马拉独裁者
    阿马斯被卫兵刺死
    据新华社28日讯　危地马拉总统卡洛斯·卡斯蒂略·阿马斯26日晚九时被他的一名卫兵刺死。
    阿马斯被刺身死后，副总统路易斯·阿图罗·冈萨雷斯—洛佩斯已经在27日清晨接任总统职务。他对记者表示，他将继续执行阿马斯的反共、反民主政策。
