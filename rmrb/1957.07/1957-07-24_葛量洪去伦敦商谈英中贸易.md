Title: Grantham to London to Discuss Sino-British Trade
Author:
Date: 1957-07-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from London: The British Governor of Hong Kong, Alexander Grantham, arrived in London by plane on the 21st. He said he would probably discuss trade issues between Britain and China with the British Colonial Secretary, Lennox-Boyd. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 葛量洪去伦敦商谈英中贸易

1957-07-24
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    葛量洪去伦敦商谈英中贸易
    伦敦消息：英国驻香港总督葛量洪21日乘飞机到达伦敦。他说，他大概会同英国殖民地大臣伦诺克斯—波伊德讨论英国同中国的贸易问题。　（据新华社讯）
