Title: The State Council Approves the "Report on Issues Concerning Guidance and Funding for the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce"
Author:
Date: 1957-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - The State Council approved the "Report on Issues Concerning Guidance and Funding for the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce" from the Central Administration for Industry and Commerce on June 12th, and forwarded it to the People's Committees of all provinces, cities, and autonomous regions for reference and implementation.

The content of this report is as follows: (1) The People's Committees of all localities should designate a department (industry and commerce administration or industry, commerce, and other departments) to be responsible for guiding the work of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. If other relevant departments need the assistance of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce in carrying out their work, they should consult with the department responsible for guiding the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce in advance and consult with the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce to ensure that the work can be arranged in a unified manner. (2) When the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce encounters difficulties in collecting membership dues, the department responsible for guiding the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce should assist in resolving them. The financial expenditures of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce do not need to go through examination and approval procedures. (3) When the People's Committees at all levels and their subordinate departments convene meetings related to the work of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, they may invite relevant cadres of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce to participate; when issuing documents related to the work of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, it is necessary to send copies to the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce.

### 国务院批准“有关对工商联的指导工作和经费开支问题的报告”

1957-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院批准“有关对工商联的指导工作和经费开支问题的报告”
    新华社6日讯　国务院于6月12日批准中央工商行政管理局“有关对工商联的指导工作和经费开支问题的报告”，并把它转发给各省、市、自治区人民委员会参照执行。
    这个报告的内容是：（一）各地人民委员会应该指定一个部门（工商行政或工业、商业等部门）负责指导工商联的工作，其他各有关部门如需要工商联协助进行工作时，应事先与负责指导工商联的部门取得联系，并与工商联协商，使工作能够统一安排。（二）工商联征收会费遇有困难时，负责指导工商联的部门，应协助其解决，对工商联的经费开支，不需要采取审查批准的手续。（三）各级人民委员会及其所属部门，召开和工商联工作有关的会议时，可以邀请工商联的有关干部参加；发出和工商联工作有关的文件时，需要抄送工商联。
