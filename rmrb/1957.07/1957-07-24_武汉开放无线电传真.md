Title: Wuhan Opens Radio Facsimile Service
Author:
Date: 1957-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 23rd. The Wuhan-Beijing and Wuhan-Shanghai radio facsimile services officially opened today. Photographs, design drawings, reports, and other materials can be transmitted. Transmissions abroad can be relayed through the above two locations. Thus far, four major cities in the country—Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Wuhan—have long-distance telephone bureaus handling radio facsimile services.

The Wuhan-Guangzhou radio facsimile service is also scheduled to open soon.

### 武汉开放无线电传真

1957-07-24
第4版()
专栏：东鳞西爪

    武汉开放无线电传真
    新华社武汉23日电　武汉——北京、武汉——上海间今天起正式开放无线电传真业务，照片、设计图和表报等都可以传送；发往国外的可以经过上面两地转传。至此，全国已有四个大城市——北京、上海、广州和武汉的长途报话局经办了无线电传真业务。
    武汉——广州间的无线电传真业务也要在最近开放。
