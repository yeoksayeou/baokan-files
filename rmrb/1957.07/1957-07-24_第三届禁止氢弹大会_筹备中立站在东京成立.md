Title: Third Conference Against Hydrogen Bombs: Preparatory Neutral Station Established in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1957-07-24
Page: 5

    Third Conference Against Hydrogen Bombs
    Preparatory Neutral Station Established in Tokyo
    Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd: The International Preparatory Center Station for the Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs was formally established in Tokyo on July 22nd.
    Participating in this preparatory organization are representatives from China, the United Kingdom, Australia, and Ceylon, who have already arrived in Japan, and representatives from the Soviet Union, the United States, and India, who have not yet arrived in Japan, as well as Japanese representatives, totaling twenty representatives from eight countries. Ikuo Yasui, Director of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, and William Morrow, Director of the World Peace Council and representative of Australia, were elected as Chairman and Secretary-General of the Preparatory Center Station, respectively.

### 第三届禁止氢弹大会  筹备中立站在东京成立

1957-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

    第三届禁止氢弹大会
    筹备中立站在东京成立
    据新华社23日讯　第三届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会国际筹备中心站7月22日在东京正式成立。
    参加这个筹备组织的有已经到达日本的中国、英国、澳大利亚、锡兰的代表和还没有到达日本的苏联、美国、印度代表，以及日本代表等八个国家的代表二十人。日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会理事长安井郁和世界和平理事会理事、澳大利亚代表威廉·莫罗分别被选为筹备中心站的议长和事务总长。
