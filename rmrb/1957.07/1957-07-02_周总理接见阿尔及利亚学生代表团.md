Title: Premier Zhou Receives Algerian Student Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-07-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Algerian student delegation this afternoon.

During the reception, Liu Xiyuan and Hu Qili were also present.

### 周总理接见阿尔及利亚学生代表团

1957-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见阿尔及利亚学生代表团
    新华社1日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了阿尔及利亚学生代表团。
    接见时，刘西元、胡启立也在座。
