Title: Hu Yaobang and Others Arrive in Moscow
Author: 
Date: 1957-07-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: Hu Yaobang, head of the Chinese delegation to the World Youth Festival, and deputy heads Xiang Nan and Wu Xueqian arrived in Moscow at 7:15 PM on the 22nd by a "Tu-104" aircraft. The other two deputy heads of the delegation, Ma John and Zhou Weizhi, had arrived earlier.

### 胡耀邦等到达莫斯科

1957-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

    胡耀邦等到达莫斯科
    据新华社讯　莫斯科消息：世界青年联欢节中国代表团团长胡耀邦和副团长项南、吴学谦22日下午七时一刻乘“图一○四”式飞机飞抵莫斯科。代表团其他二位副团长马约翰、周巍峙已先期到达。
