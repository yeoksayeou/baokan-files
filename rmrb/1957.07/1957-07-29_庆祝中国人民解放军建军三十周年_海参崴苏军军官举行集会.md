Title: Soviet Military Officers in Vladivostok Hold Gathering to Celebrate the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1957-07-29
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, July 28th, Vladivostok dispatch: According to TASS, the Soviet Naval Officers' Club in Vladivostok held a celebration on July 27th to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Some Chinese soldiers were also invited to attend the celebration. Naval officers from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea were also present.
    On the stage hung many velvet Soviet red flags and the five-star flag of the People's Republic of China. The hall was hung with many banners with slogans written in Russian and Chinese: "Long Live the Unbreakable Sino-Soviet Friendship!".
    Soviet Navy Captain Kuz' Zov spoke at the meeting, recalling the friendly visit of Soviet Pacific sailors to Shanghai last year. He heartily congratulated the representatives of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Army present at the meeting.
    Following this, two Chinese naval soldier representatives spoke.

### 庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十周年  海参崴苏军军官举行集会

1957-07-29
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十周年
    海参崴苏军军官举行集会
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社海参崴讯：苏联海参崴海军军官之家7月27日举行庆祝会庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十周年。一些中国的士兵也应邀出席了庆祝会。到会的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的一些海军军官。
    主席台上挂着许多天鹅绒的苏联红旗和中华人民共和国的五星国旗。大厅里挂着许多横幅标语，上面分别用俄文和中文写着：“牢不可破的中苏友谊万岁！”。
    苏联海军大尉库兹?佐夫在会上讲话，他回忆了去年苏联太平洋水兵去上海作友好访问的情景。他衷心向到会的中国人民革命军队的代表祝贺。
    接着，两位中国海军士兵代表讲了话。
