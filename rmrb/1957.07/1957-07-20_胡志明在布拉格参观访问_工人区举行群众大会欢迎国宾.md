Title: Ho Chi Minh Visits Prague: Mass Rally Held in Workers' District to Welcome State Guest
Author:
Date: 1957-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 19th: On July 18th, Czechoslovak President Zápotocký met with President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam at Prague Castle, and they held cordial talks.

Subsequently, President Ho Chi Minh and his entourage visited the Old Town Hall in Prague.

Near noon, President Ho Chi Minh, accompanied by President Zápotocký, visited the Škoda Sokolovo Machine Factory in Prague.

Following this, they attended a mass rally held in Prague's workers' district of Vysočany, at People's Militia Square. At the rally, President Ho Chi Minh said that in order to unify Vietnam on a democratic basis, it is necessary to strive to maintain peace, to cooperate closely within the socialist camp, and to resist the U.S. imperialists. He stated definitively that Vietnam, Korea, and Germany would one day achieve unification.

President Zápotocký introduced the liberation movement of the Vietnamese people and President Ho Chi Minh's role in this movement to the assembled masses. He emphasized that currently, when world imperialism continues to incite new wars, the most important task of socialist countries is to strengthen the close cooperation of all socialist countries, led by the Soviet Union.

### 胡志明在布拉格参观访问  工人区举行群众大会欢迎国宾

1957-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明在布拉格参观访问
    工人区举行群众大会欢迎国宾
    据新华社布拉格19日电  7月18日，捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基在布拉格宫会见了越南民主共和国主席胡志明，进行了亲切的交谈。
    随后，胡志明主席和随行人员参观了布拉格的旧城市政厅。
    将近中午的时候，胡志明主席在萨波托斯基总统陪同下访问了布拉格的斯克德索科洛沃机器工厂。
    接着，他们参加了在布拉格工人区维索查尼的民警广场上举行的群众大会。胡志明主席在会上说，为了在民主基础上统一越南，必须努力维护和平，必须在社会主义阵营中密切进行合作，必须抵抗美帝国主义者。他肯定地表示，越南、朝鲜和德国总有一天会实现统一。
    萨波托斯基总统向到会群众介绍了越南人民的解放运动和胡志明主席在这个运动中的作用。他着重指出，目前，当世界帝国主义继续在煽动新战争的时候，社会主义国家最重要的工作是加强以苏联为首的一切社会主义国家的密切合作。
