Title: Development Seen Through Numbers: Romania's Rapid Economic and Cultural Progress in Industrial and Agricultural Production
Author:
Date: 1957-07-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: According to a statistical material recently released by the Romanian News Agency, by 1956, the production volume of Category I industries in Romania accounted for 57.6% of the entire industry; while in 1938 before liberation, this proportion was 45.5%.

　　In 1956, total industrial output increased 2.3 times compared to 1938, while the production volume of Category I industries increased 3.1 times.

　　Statistical materials show that in 1956, the cultivated land area of the socialist sector of agriculture accounted for 38.4% of the total cultivated land area of the country, recently reaching 41%, while in 1950 this proportion was 23.6%.

　　In Culture and Education
　　In culture and education, during the 1938-1939 academic year, there were 33 departments in national institutions of higher learning, with 26,489 university students. In the 1956-1957 academic year, the number of departments in institutions of higher learning increased to 112, and the number of university students increased to 81,206. The number of libraries nationwide increased from 15,485 in 1949 to 24,440 in 1956. The collection of books in public libraries alone was 5,641,000 in 1949, and reached 27,731,000 in 1956.

　　In 1949, there were 83 newspapers nationwide, increasing to 125 in 1956; during the same period, the annual circulation of newspapers increased from 510 million copies to 813,969,000 copies. The number of magazines increased from 117 to 293, and the annual circulation increased from 33,478,000 copies to 56,727,000 copies.

　　In Healthcare
　　In medical and healthcare services, the number of hospitals nationwide increased from 413 in 1938 to 563 in 1956; the number of maternity hospitals increased from 370 in 1948 to 1,465 in 1956. In 1940, the national population growth rate was 7.1 per thousand, and in 1956 it was 14.3 per thousand. The infant mortality rate has decreased from 17.9% in 1938 to 8.1% in 1956.

### 从数字看发展  罗马尼亚经济文化进步很快  在工农业生产方面

1957-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    从数字看发展
    罗马尼亚经济文化进步很快
    在工农业生产方面
    新华社19日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社报道：最近发表的一项统计材料表明，到1956年罗马尼亚工业中第一部类的生产量在整个工业中的比重已经达到了57.6％；而在解放前的1938年，这个比重是45.5％。
    1956年，全部工业产量比1938年增加了二点三倍，而第一部类工业的生产量增加了三点一倍。
    统计材料表明，1956年农业社会主义部分的耕地面积占全国总耕地面积38.4％，最近已经达到了41％，而1950年这个比重是23.6％。
    在文化教育方面
    在文化教育方面，1938—1939学年，全国高等院校有三十三个科系，有大学生两万六千四百八十九人，在1956—1957学年，高等院校科系的数目增加到一百一十二个，大学生增加到八万一千二百零六人。全国图书馆的数目从1949年的一万五千四百八十五个增加到1956年的二万四千四百四十个。单是公共图书馆的藏书1949年是五百六十四万一千册，1956年就达到了二千七百七十三万一千册。
    1949年全国有八十三家报纸，1956年增加到了一百二十五家；在同一时期，报纸的每年发行量从五亿一千万份增加到八亿一千三百九十六万九千份。杂志数目从一百十七种增加到了二百九十三种，每年的发行量从三千三百四十七万八千份增加到了五千六百七十二万七千份。
    在医疗保健方面
    在医疗和保健事业方面，1938年全国医院是四百十三家，到1956年增加到了五百六十三家；产科医院1948年是三百七十家，1956年就增加到了一千四百六十五家。1940年，全国人口的增加率是千分之七点一，1956年是千分之十四点三。婴儿死亡率已经从1938年的17.9％降低到1956年的8.1％。
