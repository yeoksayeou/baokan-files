Title: Cui Yong Enters Military Academy to Study
Author:
Date: 1957-07-24
Page: 5
Brief News

    Pyongyang dispatch: Cui Yong, the Korean youth who was saved by martyr Luo Shengjiao at the cost of his own life, has recently entered the Korean People's Army Military Academy to study. He is now twenty-three years old. He joined the Korean People's Army two years ago and has become a model soldier.
