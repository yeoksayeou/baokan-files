Title: East German Government Decides to Streamline Institutions and Improve Work
Author:
Date: 1957-07-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 28th. The East German Council of Ministers discussed on the 26th and passed measures to implement the resolution of the 32nd Central Plenary Session of the Socialist Unity Party on streamlining state institutions and improving the working methods of state organ staff. Prime Minister Grotewohl pointed out at the meeting that the resolution of the 32nd Central Plenary Session of the Socialist Unity Party on streamlining state institutions and improving the working methods of state organ staff pointed out the direction for further strengthening the ties between state organs and the masses and consolidating the worker-peasant regime. He said that in order to enable the working people to better exert their enthusiasm and creativity in socialist construction, it is necessary to change the current working forms and methods of state institutions.

### 民主德国政府决定  精简机构改进工作

1957-07-29
第3版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府决定  精简机构改进工作
    据新华社柏林28日电　民主德国部长会议在26日讨论并且通过采取什么措施来实现统一社会党第三十二次中央全会关于精简国家机构和改进国家机关工作人员工作方法的决议。格罗提渥总理在会上指出，统一社会党第三十二次中央全会关于精简国家机构和改进国家机关工作人员工作方法的决议，为进一步加强国家机关和群众的联系以及巩固工农政权指出了方向。他说，为了使劳动人民在社会主义建设中能更好地发挥积极性和创造性，必须改变目前国家机构的工作形式和方法。
