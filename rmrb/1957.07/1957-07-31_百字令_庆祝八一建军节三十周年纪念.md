Title: Hundred-Character Poem: Celebrating the 30th Anniversary of the August 1st Army Day
Author: Li Jishen
Date: 1957-07-31
Page: 2

Red flags are unfurled,
    Again we happily meet the August 1st Army Day holiday.
Modern national defense is scientific and technological,
    Strengthening the ranks of the three armed forces.
Submarines dive,
    Airplanes jet,
    Possessing even newer power.
Thirtieth anniversary celebration,
    Opens a brilliant page in history.
Recalling the Nanchang Uprising,
    The Long March northward,
    Heroes gather in Yan'an.
From then on, the banner waved in all directions,
    Internal and external ferocious enemies were annihilated.
Protecting peace,
    Supporting construction,
    Still relying on great achievements.
All directions celebrate together,
    The mighty army is invincible wherever it goes!

### 百字令  庆祝八一建军节三十周年纪念
李济深
1957-07-31
第2版()
专栏：

    百字令
    庆祝八一建军节三十周年纪念
    李济深
    红旗招展，
    又欣逢、八一建军令节。
    现代国防科技化，
    壮大三军行列。
    海艇潜航，
    空机喷气，
    更具新威力。
    卅年纪念，
    别开灿烂史页。
    回忆起义南昌，
    长征北上，
    俊杰延安集。
    从此干旄麾十面，
    内外凶残并灭。
    保卫和平，
    支援建设，
    还仗丰功烈。
    万方同庆，
    雄师所向无敌！
