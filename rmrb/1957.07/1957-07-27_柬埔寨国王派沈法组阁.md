Title: Cambodian King Appoints Sim Var to Form Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1957-07-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 26th — Cambodian National Radio broadcast today that King Suramarit has appointed National Assembly Speaker Sim Var to form a new cabinet to lead the new government on behalf of the King until Prince Sihanouk recovers.

Recently, people in Phnom Penh and the provinces of Cambodia have held many gatherings demanding that Sihanouk resume his rule. They also demanded that the King personally lead the government until Sihanouk is able to resume his rule.

### 柬埔寨国王派沈法组阁

1957-07-27
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国王派沈法组阁
    新华社金边26日电　柬埔寨国家电台今天广播，苏拉玛里特国王已经委派王国会议议长沈法筹组新内阁，以便在西哈努克亲王恢复健康以前代表国王领导新的政府。
    最近，金边和柬埔寨各省的人民举行了许多次集会，要求西哈努克重新执政。他们还要求国王在西哈努克能够重新执政以前亲自领导政府。
