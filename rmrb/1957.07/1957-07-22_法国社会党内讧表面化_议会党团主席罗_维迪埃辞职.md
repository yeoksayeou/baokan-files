Title: French Socialist Party Internal Strife Surfaces: Parliamentary Group Chairman Guy Mollet Resigns
Author:
Date: 1957-07-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st, Paris dispatch: The chairman of the French Socialist Party's parliamentary group, Guy Mollet, resigned on July 20th. This action further publicizes the serious differences within the Socialist Party regarding the Algerian issue.

Sources say that Guy Mollet is a member of the "opposition" faction within the Socialist Party that opposes Mollet and Lacoste's Algerian policy. This "opposition" also includes Jules Moch, the chief French representative to the Disarmament Conference, Daniel Mayer, the chairman of the National Assembly's Foreign Affairs Committee, and Alain Savary, the former Under-Secretary of State for Tunisian and Moroccan Affairs.

Sources say that when the National Assembly held a vote of confidence on July 19th regarding whether to grant the government special powers in Algeria and mainland France, at least twenty of the twenty-six Socialist Party members who abstained belonged to this "opposition."

### 法国社会党内讧表面化  议会党团主席罗·维迪埃辞职

1957-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    法国社会党内讧表面化
    议会党团主席罗·维迪埃辞职
    据新华社21日讯　巴黎消息：法国社会党议会党团主席罗·维迪埃在7月20日提出辞职。这一行动使得社会党内部在阿尔及利亚问题上的严重分歧更进一步公开化了。
    有消息说，维迪埃是社会党内反对摩勒和拉戈斯特的阿尔及利亚政策的“反对派”成员之一。这个“反对派”还包括法国出席裁军会议首席代表莫克、国民议会外交委员会主席丹尼埃·麦耶和前外交部专管突尼斯摩洛哥事务的次长阿兰·沙瓦里。
    消息说，当国民议会在7月19日举行关于是否授予政府在阿尔及利亚以及在法国本土以特殊权力的信任投票时，弃权的二十六名社会党议员中至少有二十位属于这个“反对派”。
