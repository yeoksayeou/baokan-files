Title: Syria Lifts National Martial Law; Jordanian Patriotic Officers Call on Army to Oppose Traitors
Author:
Date: 1957-07-24
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 23rd: The Syrian government announced on July 22nd that the national martial law has been lifted, except for several areas bordering Syria and Israel. Martial law was implemented after the tripartite aggression against Egypt by Britain, France, and Israel in November of last year.
    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 23rd: According to reports in the local newspapers, a booklet distributed among the Jordanian army by patriotic Jordanian officers on July 21st stated: "Our brothers in the army must oppose the traitors who are selling our people to American imperialists." The booklet called on the army to unite and prepare to fight for freedom.
    The booklet said: "Hussein has gone mad. He believes that everyone in his country wants to kill him. Therefore, he has begun arresting any soldiers or civilians he wants to arrest and accusing them of plotting against him."

### 叙利亚解除全国戒严令  约旦爱国军官号召陆军反对卖国贼

1957-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚解除全国戒严令
    约旦爱国军官号召陆军反对卖国贼
    新华社大马士革23日电　叙利亚政府在7月22日宣布，除叙利亚和以色列边境的几个地区外，全国戒严令已经解除。戒严令是在去年11月英、法、以三国发动侵埃战争之后实行的。
    新华社大马士革23日电　据这里的报纸报道，约旦爱国军官7月21日在陆军中散发的一本小册子，说：“我们陆军弟兄们必须反对把我国人民出卖给美帝国主义者的卖国贼”。小册子号召陆军团结起来，准备为自由进行斗争。
    小册子说：“侯赛因变得疯狂了，他认为他的国中每一个人都要杀他，因此，他开始逮捕他想逮捕的任何军人或平民，并且控告他们阴谋反对他。”
