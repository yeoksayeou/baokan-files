Title: Heavy Rain Today
Author:
Date: 1957-07-11
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th -- The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a heavy rain forecast at 6 p.m. today as follows:
    It is expected that in the next twenty-four hours, most of Shandong, southern Hebei, northeastern Henan, and the northern end of Anhui will experience heavy rain or torrential rain.

### 今天有暴雨

1957-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    今天有暴雨
    新华社10日讯　今天下午六点，中央气象科学研究所发布暴雨预告如下：
    预计未来二十四小时内，山东大部、河北南部、河南东北部、安徽北端等地区将有大雨或暴雨出现。
