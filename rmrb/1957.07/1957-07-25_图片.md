Author:
Date: 1957-07-25
Page: 4

    Agricultural cooperatives in Shancheng Township, Nanjing County, Fujian Province, use dragon-bone waterwheels and pumps to draw water to high places. The water then flows into the mountainous areas through water channels that resemble overpasses. These kinds of water channels are common in the southern Fujian mountain region. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xiang Zhi took this picture.
