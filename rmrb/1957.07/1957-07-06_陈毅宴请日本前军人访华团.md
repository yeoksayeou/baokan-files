Title: Chen Yi Hosts Dinner for Japanese Ex-Servicemen Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1957-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a dinner today for the entire Japanese ex-servicemen delegation. Those present at the dinner included Zhang Xiruo, Liao Chengzhi and others.

### 陈毅宴请日本前军人访华团

1957-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅宴请日本前军人访华团
    新华社5日讯　陈毅副总理今天邀请日本前军人访华团全体人员共进晚餐。出席作陪的有张奚若、廖承志等人。
