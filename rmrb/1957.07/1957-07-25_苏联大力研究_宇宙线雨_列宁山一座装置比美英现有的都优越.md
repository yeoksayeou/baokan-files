Title: Soviet Union Vigorously Researches "Cosmic Ray Showers": Lenin Hills Installation Superior to Existing US and UK Ones
Author:
Date: 1957-07-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 24th: The world's largest installation for obtaining and studying so-called cosmic ray showers has begun operation in the Soviet Union, according to a TASS report. This installation is technically superior to existing similar installations in the United Kingdom and the United States. It is located on Lenin Hills in Moscow and is affiliated with Moscow University.

Currently, the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research in the Soviet Union uses a synchrophasotron to artificially provide the world's largest particle energy, equivalent to ten billion electron volts. However, the energy of the particles contained in cosmic rays is millions of times greater. When these cosmic particles collide with atomic nuclei in the air, they form so-called cosmic ray showers. Studying these cosmic ray showers is a major problem in modern physics.

The installation occupies an area of hundreds of thousands of square meters. Its main experimental equipment consists of Geiger-Müller counters and one hundred and fifty ionization chambers that record cosmic ray showers. The entire operation of this equipment is automated.

### 苏联大力研究“宇宙线雨”  列宁山一座装置比美英现有的都优越

1957-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联大力研究“宇宙线雨”
    列宁山一座装置比美英现有的都优越
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：世界上最大的获取和研究所谓宇宙线雨的装置已经在苏联开始工作。这座装置在技术上比英国和美国现有的同类装置都优越。它设立在莫斯科列宁山上，是莫斯科大学附设的。
    目前，设在苏联的联合核子研究所靠同步稳相加速器用人工方法提供的世界上最大的粒子能，相当于一百亿电子伏特。但是，宇宙线中所含粒子的能量却要大数百万倍。当这些宇宙粒子同空气中的原子核相碰撞时就形成所谓宇宙线雨，研究这种宇宙线雨是现代物理学的一个重大问题。
    这座装置所占的面积达数十万平方公尺。它的主要实验设备是一些盖革—弥勒计数器和一百五十个记录宇宙线雨的电离室。这些设备的全部工作过程都是自动化的。
