Title: Premier Zhou Receives Indian Statistician Mahalanobis; Nan Hanchen Hosts Banquet for Japanese Mayor and Local Assembly Member Delegation

Author:
Date: 1957-07-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 9th: Premier Zhou Enlai and his wife received Professor P. C. Mahalanobis, Statistical Advisor to the Indian Cabinet, Director of the Indian Statistical Institute, and Chairman of the United Nations Statistical Commission, and his wife, as well as Indian sampling survey expert Professor Lahiri, and invited them to dinner tonight.
    Present at the reception were Xue Muqiao, Director of the State Statistical Bureau, and Wang Sihua, Deputy Director.
    Also present at the reception were Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and his wife.
    Xinhua News Agency, July 9th: Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Japanese Mayor and Local Assembly Member Delegation to China.
    Nan Hanchen and Yamaya Yachio, the head of the Japanese Mayor and Local Assembly Member Delegation to China, delivered speeches at the banquet, mutually wishing that trade and friendship between China and Japan would grow day by day.
    Among those present at the banquet were Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; Lei Renmin, Li Zhuchen, and Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见印统计学家马哈拉诺比斯  南汉宸欢宴日本市长、地方议员访华团

1957-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印统计学家马哈拉诺比斯
    南汉宸欢宴日本市长、地方议员访华团
    新华社9日讯　周恩来总理和夫人今晚接见了印度内阁统计顾问、印度统计学院院长、联合国统计委员会主席马哈拉诺比斯教授和夫人，及印度抽样调查专家拉希瑞教授，并且邀请他们共进晚餐。
    接见时在座的有国家统计局局长薛暮桥、副局长王思华。
    接见时，印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁夫妇也在座。
    新华社9日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今晚设宴欢迎日本市长、地方议员访华团。
    南汉宸和日本市长、地方议员访华团团长山屋八万雄先后在宴会上致词，互祝中日两国贸易和友谊日益增长。
    出席宴会作陪的，有北京市副市长乐松生，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、李烛尘、冀朝鼎，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪等人。
