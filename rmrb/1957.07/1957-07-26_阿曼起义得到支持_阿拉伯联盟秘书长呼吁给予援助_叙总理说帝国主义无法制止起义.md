Title: Oman Uprising Receives Support; Arab League Secretary-General Calls for Aid; Syrian Premier Says Imperialism Cannot Stop the Uprising
Author:
Date: 1957-07-26
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 25th: According to Cairo Radio, Arab League Secretary-General Hassouna called on Arab League countries on July 24th to support the Oman uprising in order to "restore the country's independence."

In a memorandum he sent to member states of the League, Hassouna said that British troops were about to brutally attack the Omani people's uprising and massively suppress this nationalist movement demanding the restoration of independence. All Arab countries have an unshirkable duty to support this country.

The memorandum pointed out that Oman had been an independent country until it was occupied by British troops in November 1955.

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: Damascus Dispatch: Syrian Prime Minister al-Assali said on July 24th that imperialism can no longer stop the "anti-colonial outbreak" of Arab nationalism in Oman.

He said: "Throughout the Arab homeland, Arab nationalism will eventually triumph over all colonialist attempts, no matter what means imperialism takes to maintain its rule."

Al-Assali made these remarks when commenting on the British ultimatum to the Omani insurgents.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 25th: The representative of the Imam of Oman in Cairo, Muhammad al-Kharsi, said today that British aircraft have so far only attacked innocent civilians in Oman and have not found armed insurgents. Al-Kharsi also said that the insurgents now control all of Oman's territory, including the capital, Nizwa.

### 阿曼起义得到支持  阿拉伯联盟秘书长呼吁给予援助  叙总理说帝国主义无法制止起义

1957-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    阿曼起义得到支持
    阿拉伯联盟秘书长呼吁给予援助
    叙总理说帝国主义无法制止起义
    据新华社开罗25日电　据开罗电台广播，阿拉伯联盟秘书长哈苏纳7月24日吁请阿拉伯联盟各国支援阿曼的起义，以“恢复这个国家的独立”。
    哈苏纳在他发给联盟各成员国的一份备忘录中说，英国军队正要残酷地进攻阿曼的人民起义，大举镇压这个要求恢复独立的民族主义运动。所有阿拉伯国家都应该义不容辞地去支援这个国家。
    这份备忘录指出，阿曼在1955年11月被英国军队侵占以前一直是一个独立国家。
    据新华社25日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总理阿萨利7月24日说，帝国主义不再能制止阿拉伯民族主义在阿曼的“反殖民主义的爆发”。
    他说：“在阿拉伯人祖国各地，阿拉伯民族主义终将战胜一切殖民主义企图，不管帝国主义采取什么手段来保持它的统治。”
    阿萨利是在评论英国向阿曼起义者发出最后通牒时说这番话的。
    据新华社开罗25日电　阿曼教长驻开罗代表穆罕默德·哈尔赛今天说，英国飞机迄今只是袭击了阿曼的无辜老百姓，而并没有找到武装起义者。哈尔赛还说，起义者现在已经控制了包括首都尼兹瓦在内的阿曼全部领土。
