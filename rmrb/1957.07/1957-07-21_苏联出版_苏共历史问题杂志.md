Title: Soviet Union Publishes "Problems of History of the CPSU" Magazine
Author:
Date: 1957-07-21
Page: 5

    Soviet Union Publishes
    "Problems of History of the CPSU" Magazine
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 19th (TASS): The first issue of "Problems of History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union," the organ of the Institute of Marxism-Leninism of the Central Committee of the CPSU, has been published.
    This issue contains the communique of the June Plenary Session of the CPSU Central Committee, the resolution of the CPSU Central Committee on the anti-Party group of Malenkov, Kaganovich, and Molotov, and newly discovered documents of Marx, Engels, and Lenin. The essays published in this issue include: "On the Party's Tactical Issues in March-April 1917," "The Development of the Leninist Principle of Democratic Centralism in the Socialist Economic Sector," "The October Revolution and the British Workers' Movement," etc.
    The magazine also has a special column, which in this issue contains information on the complete works of Lenin, information on newly discovered documents of Marx and Engels, information on the communist movement in various capitalist countries, and information on the Eighth National Congress of the CPC.
    This issue of the magazine also contains reviews of many party history books and memoirs.

### 苏联出版  苏共历史问题杂志

1957-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联出版
    苏共历史问题杂志
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科19日讯：苏共中央马克思—列宁主义研究院的机关刊物“苏联共产党历史问题”第一期已经出版。
    这一期刊载了苏共中央今年六月全会的公报，苏共中央全会关于马林科夫、卡冈诺维奇、莫洛托夫反党集团的决议以及新发现的马克思、恩格斯、列宁的文件。这一期刊载的论文有：“关于1917年3—4月党的策略问题”“民主集中制的列宁主义原则在社会主义经济部门的发展”，“十月革命和英国工人运动”等。
    杂志还辟有专栏，这期的专栏里刊载了关于列宁全集的资料，关于新发现的马克思和恩格斯的文件的资料，关于各资本主义国家共产主义运动的资料以及关于中共第八次代表大会的资料等。
    这期杂志中还有对许多党史书籍和回忆录的评论。
