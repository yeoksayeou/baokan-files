Title: Sastroamidjojo Speaks in Moscow, Advocates Holding Second Asian-African Conference Next Year
Author:
Date: 1957-07-28
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, July 27th. TASS reports: Former Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo stated in Moscow on the 26th that it is necessary to convene a second Asian-African conference. He said, "I hope this conference can be held in early next year."

He further said that if someone proposes holding this conference in Egypt, he would only welcome such a decision.

This Indonesian politician is currently visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet government. He spoke about his views on the Asian-African Conference at a press conference. He said that the declaration signed by representatives of twenty-nine Asian and African countries at the Bandung Conference is being implemented. The conference had adopted resolutions on economic, political, and cultural cooperation. He said that these issues could continue to be discussed. However, the discussion of international situation issues should occupy the most important place on the agenda of the second conference.

Sastroamidjojo expressed his full agreement with the Soviet proposal to immediately and unconditionally cease nuclear weapon testing.

Regarding the West Irian issue, he said that Indonesia will raise this issue again at the recent United Nations meeting and hopes that freedom-loving people of all countries will support the just demands of the Indonesian people.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约在莫斯科发表谈话  主张明年开第二次亚非会议

1957-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    沙斯特罗阿米佐约在莫斯科发表谈话
    主张明年开第二次亚非会议
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社报道：印度尼西亚前总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约26日在莫斯科认为，需要召开第二次亚非会议。他说，我希望这次会议能够在明年年初召开。
    他又说，如果有人提议希望在埃及召开这次会议，那么他只会欢迎这种决定。
    这位印度尼西亚政治家应苏联政府的邀请正在苏联访问。他在记者招待会上谈到了他对亚非会议的看法。他说，二十九个亚非国家的代表在万隆会议上签署的宣言正在实施中。这次会议曾经就经济、政治和文化方面的合作通过了决议。他说，这些问题还可以继续讨论。但是，国际局势问题的讨论应当在第二次会议议程上占最重要的地位。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约表示完全赞同苏联提出的立即无条件停止试验核武器的建议。
    关于西伊里安问题，他说，印度尼西亚将在联合国最近举行的会议上重新提出这个问题，并且希望爱好自由的各国人民支持印度尼西亚人民的正义要求。
