Title: Deng Xiaoping Receives Vietnamese Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received the Vietnamese women's delegation headed by Nguyen Thi Thap at 9:00 AM today. Present at the reception were Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and Vice Chairmen Deng Yingchao and Zhang Yun.

### 邓小平接见越南妇女代表团

1957-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    邓小平接见越南妇女代表团
    新华社25日讯　中国共产党中央委员会总书记邓小平，在今天上午九时接见了以阮氏十为首的越南妇女访华代表团。接见时在座的有中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，全国妇联主席蔡畅、副主席邓颖超、章蕴等。
