Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Receives Speaker Wilopo
Author:
Date: 1957-07-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling received Indonesian Constitutional Assembly Speaker Wilopo and his wife, and First Deputy Speaker Prawoto Mangkusasmito and his wife this afternoon.

    Those present at the reception were Zhang Su and Zhang Yun.

    Indonesian Ambassador to China Wilopo Pranoto and his wife were also present.

### 宋庆龄副委员长接见韦洛坡议长

1957-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副委员长接见韦洛坡议长
    新华社30日讯　宋庆龄副委员长今天下午接见了印度尼西亚制宪议会议长韦洛坡和夫人、第一副议长普拉沃托·曼库沙斯米托和夫人。
    接见的时候，在座的有张苏、章蕴。
    印度尼西亚驻中国大使维约普拉诺托和夫人也在座。
