Title: Syria-Saudi Relations Tend Towards Normalization; Saudi Embassy in Syria Resumes Work
Author:
Date: 1957-07-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 29th: The Saudi Arabian embassy in Syria resumed its normal operations on July 28th, after being closed for three weeks. This indicates that the strained relations between Syria and Saudi Arabia have returned to normal.

It is reported that the Saudi Arabian ambassador is scheduled to return to Syria on July 29th.

Responsible political figures here, in a statement to "Al-Jamahir" newspaper on July 26th, indicated that the recent crisis between Syria and Saudi Arabia has been amicably resolved in accordance with the policy of positive neutrality followed by Syria. However, this person pointed out that, contrary to the statement of the Saudi Arabian ambassador to Lebanon, the Syrian government did not express any apology in its letter to the King of Saudi Arabia.

### 叙沙两国关系趋于正常化  沙驻叙大使馆恢复工作

1957-07-30
第6版()
专栏：

    叙沙两国关系趋于正常化
    沙驻叙大使馆恢复工作
    新华社大马士革29日电　沙特阿拉伯驻叙利亚大使馆在停闭了三星期以后，7月28日恢复了它的正常工作。这表明，叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯两国之间一度紧张的关系已经恢复正常。
    有消息说，沙特阿拉伯大使预定在7月29日回到叙利亚。
    这里的负责的政界人士7月26日在向“群众报”发表的声明中表明，最近叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯两国之间的危机，已按照叙利亚遵循的积极的中立政策得到了友好解决。但是，这位人士指出，同沙特阿拉伯驻黎巴嫩大使的说法相反，叙利亚政府给沙特国王的信件中没有表示任何歉意。
