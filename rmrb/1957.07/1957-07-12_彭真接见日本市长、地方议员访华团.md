Title: Peng Zhen Receives Japanese Mayors and Local Councilors Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1957-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Mayor Peng Zhen received the Japanese Mayors and Local Councilors Delegation this evening.

	Vice Mayors Feng Jiping, Jia Tingsan, and others were present at the reception.

### 彭真接见日本市长、地方议员访华团

1957-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真接见日本市长、地方议员访华团
    新华社11日讯　彭真市长今天晚上接见了日本市长、地方议员访华团。
    接见时，冯基平、贾庭三副市长等也在座。
