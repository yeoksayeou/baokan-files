　　Brief News
　　Poland's Grain Bumper Harvest
　　Warsaw News: Poland has a bumper grain harvest this year. Almost every city is now sending large numbers of laborers to help rural areas, especially state-owned farms, harvest grain.
　　U.S. Dissatisfied with Japan's Relaxation of "Embargo"
　　Beijing News: According to a United Press report, government officials in Washington are unhappy with Japan's recent decision to relax trade with China. They believe that Japan's move away from U.S. policy on this major issue is wrong, and they oppose Japan's practice. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 简讯

1957-07-19
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    波兰谷物丰收
    华沙讯：波兰今年谷物丰收。现在几乎每一个城市都在送出大批劳动力帮助农村、特别是国营农场收割谷物。
    美国不满日本放宽“禁运”
    北京讯：据合众社报道，华盛顿的政府人士对不久前日本放宽对中国贸易的决定感到不痛快。他们认为日本在这个重大问题上离开美国政策的行动是错误的，他们反对日本的作法。  （据新华社讯）
