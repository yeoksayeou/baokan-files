Title: PLA Headquarters and Beijing Garrison Hold Medal Conferral Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1957-07-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: The headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, various military branches, and the Beijing Garrison jointly held a medal conferral ceremony at 3:00 PM today.

    Li Da, Deputy Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, read the order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China to award medals to meritorious personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army during the Chinese People's Revolutionary War.

    He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of Chairman Mao Zedong, awarded medals to Yang Chengwu and 394 other meritorious personnel who participated in the revolutionary war during the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army period and the War of Resistance Against Japan.

    Those attending the ceremony today to receive medals were Beijing-based personnel from the second batch of award recipients approved by the 74th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. These individuals were awarded First, Second, and Third Class August 1st Medals, Independence and Freedom Medals, and Liberation Medals, respectively.

### 解放军总部和驻京部队举行授勋典礼

1957-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军总部和驻京部队举行授勋典礼
    新华社27日讯　中国人民解放军总部、各军种兵种和驻京部队，今天下午三时联合举行授勋典礼。
    中华人民共和国国防部副部长李达上将宣读了中华人民共和国主席授予中国人民解放军在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员勋章的命令。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙元帅代表毛泽东主席把勋章授予杨成武上将等三百九十四名在中国工农红军时期、抗日战争时期，参加革命战争的有功人员。
    今天参加典礼接受勋章的是全国人民代表大会常务委员会第七十四次会议通过的第二批授予勋章人员名单中的在京人员。这些人员分别获得了一、二、三级八一勋章、独立自由勋章和解放勋章。
