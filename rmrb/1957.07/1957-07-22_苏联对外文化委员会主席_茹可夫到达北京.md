Title: Chairman of the Soviet Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhukov, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-07-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency: Georgy Zhukov, Chairman of the State Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries under the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Tikhvinsky, Director of the Far East Department of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 20th at the invitation of the Ministry of Culture to visit China.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister of Culture, and others.

### 苏联对外文化委员会主席  茹可夫到达北京

1957-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联对外文化委员会主席
    茹可夫到达北京
    据新华社讯　应文化部邀请来中国访问的苏联部长会议国家对外文化委员会主席格奥尔基·茹可夫和对外文化委员会远东部主任齐赫文斯基，二十日乘坐飞机到北京。
    前往机场欢迎者有文化部副部长钱俊瑞等。
