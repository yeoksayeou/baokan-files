Title: Prince Souvanna Phouma Accepts Task of Forming Cabinet
Author: 
Date: 1957-07-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 26th: Vientiane Radio broadcasted tonight that the former Prime Minister of Laos, Prince Souvanna Phouma, telegraphed the King of Laos today, stating that he accepted the task of forming a new cabinet.

The Royal Palace Office of Laos telegraphed Prince Phouma in Vientiane yesterday from Luang Prabang, informing him that the King had decided to entrust him with the organization of a new cabinet.

In the telegram, Phouma stated that he would immediately begin soliciting opinions from all parties in order to form a new government.

### 富马亲王接受组阁任务

1957-07-27
第6版()
专栏：

    富马亲王接受组阁任务
    新华社河内26日电　据万象电台今晚广播：老挝前任内阁首相梭发那·富马亲王今天打电报给老挝国王，表示接受筹组新内阁的任务。
    老挝皇廷办公厅在昨天从琅勃拉邦打电报给在万象的富马亲王，通知他说国王已经决定授命他组织新内阁。
    富马在电文中表示，他将立即开始征询各方面的意见，以便成立新政府。
