Title: Japanese Mayors and Local Councilors Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-07-09
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, July 8th:

    Japanese Mayors and Local Councilors Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    A delegation of twenty-five Japanese mayors and local councilors, led by Chairman Yamaya Yamazo and Vice Chairmen Yagi Jutarō and Uchida Yuzo, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 8th.
    The delegation includes the mayors of four cities, including Moji, Nobeoka, Ikeda, and Denkyo, and representatives of local councils from sixteen regions of Japan. They are visiting China at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.
    Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, were among those who welcomed them at the station.
    Before coming to Beijing, the delegation visited Wuhan.

### 日本市长、地方议员访华团到京

1957-07-09
第7版()
专栏：

    日本市长、地方议员访华团到京
    新华社8日讯　日本市长、地方议员访华团一行二十五人，在团长山屋八万雄，副团长八木重太郎、内田雄三率领下，在8日晚乘火车到达北京。
    访华团包括门司、延冈、池田、传桥等四市市长，和日本十六个地区的地方议会代表。他们是应中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请来我国访问的。
    到车站欢迎的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、北京市副市长乐松生等人。
    访华团来北京以前，曾在武汉市进行了参观访问。
