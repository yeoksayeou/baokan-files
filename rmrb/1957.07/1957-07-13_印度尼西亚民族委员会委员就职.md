Title: Indonesian National Council Members Inaugurated
Author:
Date: 1957-07-13
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 12th: The members of the Indonesian National Council were sworn in today under the auspices of President Sukarno.

President Sukarno is the chairman of this council. Among the forty-five members of this council, five did not attend today's swearing-in ceremony, including representatives from the Catholic Church and other sectors who have not yet been selected.

President Sukarno said in his speech at the ceremony that the role of this council is to assist the cabinet, enhance the cabinet's prestige, and serve as a bridge between society and the cabinet. The task of this council is to provide suggestions to the cabinet on constitutional and social issues at the request of the cabinet or on its own initiative. He emphasized that the establishment of the National Council would not undermine the status of the cabinet and parliament.

### 印度尼西亚民族委员会委员就职

1957-07-13
第7版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚民族委员会委员就职
    据新华社雅加达12日电　印度尼西亚民族委员会的委员今天在苏加诺总统的主持下宣誓就职。
    苏加诺总统是这个委员会的主席。在这个委员会的四十五名委员中，有五名委员没有参加今天的宣誓就职典礼，缺席的委员中包括还没有产生的天主教等方面的代表。
    苏加诺总统在典礼上发表讲话的时候说，这个委员会的作用是：协助内阁，提高内阁的威信并且做社会和内阁之间的桥梁。这个委员会的任务是应内阁的要求，或者自己主动地向内阁提出有关宪法和社会问题的建议。他强调指出，成立民族委员会不会损害内阁和国会的地位。
