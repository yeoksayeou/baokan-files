Title: Belukhin Appointed Chairman of the Soviet Committee for Foreign Economic Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-07-26
Page: 6

    Belukhin Appointed Chairman of the
    Soviet Committee for Foreign Economic Relations
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 25th—TASS, Moscow, July 24th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union issued an order to reorganize the Central Bureau of Economic Relations for Economic Ties with People's Democratic Countries into the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.
    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union appointed Comrade M.G. Belukhin as Chairman of this committee, relieving him of his post as Minister of Medium Machine Building.

### 别尔乌辛被任命为  苏对外经济关系委会主席

1957-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    别尔乌辛被任命为
    苏对外经济关系委会主席
    据新华社25日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科24日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布命令，把同人民民主国家进行经济联系的中央经济关系局改组为苏联部长会议国家对外经济关系委员会。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命米·格·别尔乌辛同志为这个委员会主席，解除他的中型机器制造部长的职务。
