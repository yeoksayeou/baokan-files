Title: Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan Signed
Author:
Date: 1957-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 19th. The 1957 Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan between China and Hungary was signed in Budapest on the 18th.

The plan stipulates cooperation projects between the two countries in science, education, and culture. In terms of culture and education, both sides will send writers, journalists, artists, song and dance troupes or puppet troupes, and educational delegations to visit each other this year.

### 中匈文化合作执行计划签字

1957-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    中匈文化合作执行计划签字
    新华社布达佩斯19日电  中国和匈牙利1957年文化合作执行计划18日在布达佩斯签字。
    计划中规定了两国在科学、教育、文化等方面进行合作的项目，在文化教育方面，双方在今年将互派作家、记者、造型艺术家、歌舞团或木偶剧团、教育代表团等进行访问。
