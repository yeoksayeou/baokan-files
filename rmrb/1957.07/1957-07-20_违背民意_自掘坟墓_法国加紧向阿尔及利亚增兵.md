Title: Defying Public Opinion, Digging its Own Grave: France Intensifies Troop Deployment to Algeria
Author:
Date: 1957-07-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th dispatch from TASS, Paris, July 18th: The French government, disregarding the firm opposition of people from all walks of life, is continuously reinforcing its troops in Algeria.

Recently, the French government has transported an increasing number of troops and military technical equipment to Algeria. According to "Echo," the French army currently has fifteen divisions stationed in Algeria. There are also plans to transfer four divisions from Morocco and Tunisia to Algeria. In addition, the French command is recruiting new soldiers from among the Muslim residents, and the total number of Muslim soldiers participating in the suppression of the Algerian national liberation movement has now reached six thousand. Furthermore, several Algerian infantry regiments that were transferred to France and West Germany last year have also been recalled to Algeria.

"L'Humanité" reported that on the 17th, four naval transport ships carrying 7,500 soldiers and a large amount of military technical equipment sailed from France to the port of Algiers in Algeria.

### 违背民意  自掘坟墓  法国加紧向阿尔及利亚增兵

1957-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

    违背民意  自掘坟墓
    法国加紧向阿尔及利亚增兵
    新华社19日讯  据塔斯社巴黎18日讯：法国政府正不顾各阶层人民的坚决反对，不断向阿尔及利亚增援兵力。
    最近，法国政府把越来越多的军队和军事技术装备运到了阿尔及利亚。据“回声报”报道，目前法军在阿尔及利亚有十五个师的兵力。最近还准备从摩洛哥和突尼斯抽调四个师去阿尔及利亚。此外，法国统帅部还在穆斯林居民中招募新兵，现在参加镇压阿尔及利亚民族解放运动的穆斯林士兵的总数已达六千人。另外，去年调到法国和西德的几个阿尔及利亚步兵团也已被调回阿尔及利亚。
    “人道报”报道说，17日有四艘海军运输舰载着七千五百名士兵和大量的军事技术装备从法国开到了阿尔及利亚的阿兰港。
