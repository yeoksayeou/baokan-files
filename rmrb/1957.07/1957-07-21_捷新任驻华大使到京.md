Title: New Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th – The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, Jan Bušniak, and his wife arrived in Beijing this morning.

### 捷新任驻华大使到京

1957-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    捷新任驻华大使到京
    据新华社20日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国新任驻华特命全权大使杨·布希尼亚克及夫人在今日上午到达北京。
