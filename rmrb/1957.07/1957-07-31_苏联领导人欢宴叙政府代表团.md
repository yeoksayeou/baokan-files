Title: Soviet Leaders Fete Syrian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-07-31
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th. TASS reported from Moscow: Bulganin and Khrushchev hosted a banquet in the Grand Kremlin on July 29th in honor of the Syrian Republic government delegation headed by State Minister and Defense Minister Azem.
    Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Kuzmin also attended the banquet.

### 苏联领导人欢宴叙政府代表团

1957-07-31
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联领导人欢宴叙政府代表团
    新华社30日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫7月29日在大克里姆林宫设宴招待以国务部长兼国防部长阿泽姆为首的叙利亚共和国政府代表团。
    苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅和部长会议副主席库兹明等也参加了宴会。
