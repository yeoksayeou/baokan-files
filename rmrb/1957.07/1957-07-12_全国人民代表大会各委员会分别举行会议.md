Title: Various Committees of the National People's Congress Hold Meetings Separately
Author: 
Date: 1957-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 11th: The Legislative Affairs Committee of the National People's Congress held a meeting this afternoon to discuss the draft resolutions on the election of deputies to the Second National People's Congress and on the sentencing or approval of death penalty cases by the Supreme People's Court, which were submitted by the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress. These two draft resolutions will be passed by the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress.

　　The Legislative Affairs Committee also held a meeting on June 28 to review the initial draft of the Criminal Law of the People's Republic of China.

　　The Budget Committee of the National People's Congress also held a meeting on the morning of July 6 to review the 1956 National Final Accounts and the 1957 National Budget.

　　The Proposals Examination Committee of the Fourth Session of the National People's Congress held its second, third, and fourth meetings on the 8th, 9th, and 10th respectively, to deliberate on each proposal item by item.

### 全国人民代表大会各委员会分别举行会议

1957-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会各委员会分别举行会议
    新华社11日讯　全国人民代表大会法案委员会今天下午举行会议，讨论了第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议关于第二届全国人民代表大会代表选举问题的决议草案和关于死刑案件由最高人民法院判决或者核准的决议草案。这两个决议草案将由第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议通过。
    法案委员会在6月28日还曾举行会议审查了中华人民共和国刑法草案初稿。
    全国人民代表大会预算委员会也在7月6日上午举行了会议，对1956年国家决算和1957年国家预算进行了审查。
    全国人民代表大会第四次会议提案审查委员会在8日、9日和10日分别举行了第二次、第三次和第四次会议，对各项提案逐条进行了审议。
