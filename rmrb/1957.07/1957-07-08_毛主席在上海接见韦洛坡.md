Title: Chairman Mao Meets Wilopo in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-07-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 7th. Chairman Mao Zedong met with Wilopo, Speaker of the Indonesian Constitutional Assembly, and his wife, along with all the Indonesian guests who came to visit China, in Shanghai on the evening of the 6th.

	Chairman Mao Zedong had a cordial conversation with the distinguished guests. During the meeting, Speaker Wilopo conveyed Indonesian President Sukarno's greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong and presented Chairman Mao Zedong with a letter written by President Sukarno.

	Present at the meeting were Chen Qiyu, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Wang Lifu, Deputy Director of the General Office, who accompanied the distinguished guests on their visit to Shanghai, as well as Cao Diqiu, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, and Ke Qingshi, Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

	Suwanto, Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy in China, was also present at the meeting.
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, July 6th. Wilopo, Speaker of the Indonesian Constitutional Assembly, and his wife, along with a party of ten, left Nanjing for Shanghai by special plane at 8:00 a.m. today.

	The guests arrived in Nanjing by special plane from Tianjin at 5:00 p.m. on the 5th, accompanied by Chen Qiyu, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and others. Upon arriving in Nanjing, the guests went directly to the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum to pay their respects. In the memorial hall of the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, the guests presented wreaths to the statue of Mr. Sun Yat-sen and paid their respects, and entered the tomb chamber to admire the reclining statue of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. After leaving the memorial hall, Speaker Wilopo spoke with Wang Quan, an elderly guard who had followed Mr. Sun Yat-sen for more than twenty years, and took a group photo in front of the memorial hall.

	The guests also toured the ancient relics of the stone statue road in front of the Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum and Xuanwu Lake. In the evening of the 5th, Hui Yuyu, Governor of Jiangsu Province, and Peng Chong, Mayor of Nanjing, hosted a banquet to entertain Speaker Wilopo and others.

### 毛主席在上海接见韦洛坡

1957-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席在上海接见韦洛坡
    新华社上海7日电　毛泽东主席6日晚上在上海接见了印度尼西亚制宪议会议长韦洛坡和夫人以及同来访问中国的全体印度尼西亚客人们。
    毛泽东主席同贵宾们进行了亲切的交谈。接见时，韦洛坡议长曾转达印度尼西亚苏加诺总统对毛泽东主席的问候，并将苏加诺总统写给毛泽东主席的一封信交给了毛泽东主席。
    接见时在座的有陪同贵宾们前来上海访问的全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员陈其瑗和办公厅副主任王黎夫，还有上海市副市长曹荻秋、政协上海市委员会主席柯庆施。
    接见时，印度尼西亚驻华大使馆参赞苏万托也在座。
    新华社南京6日电　印度尼西亚制宪议会议长韦洛坡和夫人等一行十人，今天上午八时乘专机离开南京去上海。
    客人们是在5日下午五时由全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员陈其瑗等陪同，从天津乘专机到达南京的。客人们到南京后，一下飞机就前往中山陵谒陵。在中山陵灵堂里，客人们向孙中山先生的坐像献花圈致敬，并步入墓室陵寝瞻仰孙中山先生卧像。走出灵堂后，韦洛坡议长同曾经跟随孙中山先生二十多年的卫士、安陵老人王全进行了交谈，并在灵堂前合影留念。
    客人们还游览了明孝陵前石像路的古迹和玄武湖。5日晚间，江苏省省长惠浴宇和南京市市长彭冲曾设宴招待韦洛坡议长等。
