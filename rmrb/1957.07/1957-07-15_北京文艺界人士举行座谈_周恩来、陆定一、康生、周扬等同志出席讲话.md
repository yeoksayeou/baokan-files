Title: Beijing Literary and Art Circles Hold Symposium; Comrades Zhou Enlai, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, Zhou Yang, etc., Attend and Speak

Author:
Date: 1957-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee, the Ministry of Culture, and the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles convened a symposium in Beijing today for literary and art figures attending the National People's Congress and those in Beijing.

The symposium was presided over by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles.

Premier Zhou Enlai, alternate members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Lu Dingyi and Kang Sheng, and Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee Zhou Yang attended the symposium and delivered speeches.

After the meeting, Premier Zhou had dinner with the literary and art figures attending the symposium.

Zhang Jichun, Acting Director of the Second Office of the State Council, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister, and Liu Zhijian, Director of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, also attended the symposium.

About two hundred literary and art figures attended the symposium, including Lao She, Ba Jin, Yang Hansheng, Zheng Zhenduo, Ye Shengtao, Xia Yan, Tian Han, Li Jieren, Cai Chusheng, Ma Sicong, Liang Sicheng, Hua Junwu, Yu Fei'an, Zhou Xinfang, Xiao Changhua, Lan Ma, Shu Xiuwen, Chen Bohua, and Shi Shaohua.

### 北京文艺界人士举行座谈  周恩来、陆定一、康生、周扬等同志出席讲话

1957-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    北京文艺界人士举行座谈
    周恩来、陆定一、康生、周扬等同志出席讲话
    新华社14日讯　中共中央宣传部、文化部、中国文学艺术界联合会今天在北京邀集出席全国人民代表大会及在北京的文艺界人士举行了座谈会。
    座谈会由中国文联主席郭沫若主持。
    周恩来总理，中共中央政治局候补委员陆定一、康生，中共中央宣传部副部长周扬出席了座谈会并讲了话。
    会后，周总理和出席座谈会的文艺界人士共进晚餐。
    国务院第二办公室代主任张际春，文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长钱俊瑞，中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部部长刘志坚也出席了座谈会。
    出席座谈会的文艺界人士有老舍、巴金、阳翰笙、郑振铎、叶圣陶、夏衍、田汉、李劼人、蔡楚生、马思聪、梁思成、华君武、于非闇、周信芳、萧长华、蓝马、舒绣文、陈伯华、石少华等约二百人。
