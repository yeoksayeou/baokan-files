    Editorial Department Apology
    In this newspaper's July 1st editorial, "The Bourgeois Orientation of Wenhui Bao Should Be Criticized," in the twelfth line of the second to last paragraph, "repeated admonitions but no change" was mistakenly printed as "repeated admonitions but no admonition." This error was repeated in reprints and citations elsewhere. We hereby issue this correction, and hope readers will take note.

### 本报编辑部启事

1957-07-15

专栏：

    本报编辑部启事
    本报7月1日社论“文汇报的资产阶级方向应当批判”内，倒数第二段第十二行中，“累戒不改”误排为“累戒不戒”，各地转载引用时亦以讹传讹，特此更正，希读者注意。
