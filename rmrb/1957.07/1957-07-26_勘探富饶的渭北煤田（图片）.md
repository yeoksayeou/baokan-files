Exploring the Rich Weibei Coalfield

The Loess Plateau of Weibei, Shaanxi, is rich in coal deposits. Currently, large-scale exploration activities are underway here. According to existing data, the coal reserves here amount to 8.5 billion tons, making it one of the largest coalfields discovered in China so far.

Below: On this undulating Weibei Loess Plateau, inexhaustible black gold – coal – is hidden.

Right: In order to accurately understand the thickness of the underground coal seams, dozens of drilling rigs are distributed throughout the Weibei Coalfield, drilling day and night.　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Yao Zongyi Photo

### 勘探富饶的渭北煤田（图片）
姚宗仪
1957-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    勘探富饶的渭北煤田
    陕西渭北的黄土高原，蕴藏着丰富的煤矿。目前，这里正在进行大规模的勘探活动。据现有资料证明，这里煤的储藏量达八十五亿吨，是我国目前发现的最大煤田之一。
    下图：在这个起伏不平的渭北黄土高原上，蕴藏着取之不尽的黑色金子——煤。
    右图：为了精确的了解地下煤层厚度，有数十台钻机分布在渭北煤田上日夜不停地进行钻探。　新华社记者　姚宗仪摄
