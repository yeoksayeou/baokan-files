Title: British Planes Bomb Southern Yemen Again
Author:
Date: 1957-07-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 26th: The Yemeni Embassy in Cairo announced on the 25th that British planes bombed southern Yemen again on July 22nd, dropping incendiary bombs.
　　The embassy received a telegram from the Yemeni government stating that the British plane bombing caused serious material losses, and the number of deaths and injuries has not yet been counted.

### 英机再次轰炸也门南部

1957-07-27
第6版()
专栏：

    英机再次轰炸也门南部
    新华社开罗26日电　也门驻开罗公使馆25日宣布，英国飞机在7月22日再次轰炸也门南部，投掷燃烧弹。
    公使馆收到也门政府发来的电报说，英国飞机的轰炸造成严重的物资损失，死伤人数还没有统计。
