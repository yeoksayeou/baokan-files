A Folk Rhyme
　　National treasure, national treasure,
　　Who will you follow?
　　If you sing the opposite tune,
　　We don't want you!
　　(Originally published in Tsinghua University's "Street Poetry")

### 民谣一首

1957-07-27
第8版()
专栏：诗传单

    民谣一首
    国宝，国宝，
    跟着谁跑？
    若唱反腔，
    不要！
    （原载清华大学“街头诗”）
