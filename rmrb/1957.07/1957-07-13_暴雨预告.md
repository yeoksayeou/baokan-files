Title: Rainstorm Forecast
Author:
Date: 1957-07-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued the following rainstorm forecast at 7 p.m. today: It is expected that in the next thirty-six hours, there will be heavy rain and rainstorms in southern Shaanxi, southern Shanxi, northern Henan, southern Hebei, northern Anhui, the northern end of Jiangsu, and most of Shandong.

### 暴雨预告

1957-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

    暴雨预告
    新华社12日讯　今天下午7时，中央气象科学研究所发布暴雨预告如下：预计未来三十六小时内，陕西南部、山西南部、河南北部、河北南部、安徽北部、江苏北端和山东大部等地区将有大雨和暴雨。
