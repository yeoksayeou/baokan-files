Friendly Exchanges and Cultural Exchanges

    Dürer's Works of Art Exhibited in Beijing
    The "Exhibition of Works by the 15th-16th Century Art Master Dürer of the German Democratic Republic," organized by the Bureau for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of China, opened on the 15th of this month at the Palace Museum in Beijing.
    The exhibits include more than 400 oil paintings, watercolors, prints, and sketches. After the exhibition, the German Democratic Republic will donate all the works to the Beijing Chinese Painting Academy in the name of the Berlin German Academy of Arts.

    Neruda and the Amados Arrive in Beijing
    Chilean progressive poet Neruda and his wife, and Brazilian writer Amado and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 15th. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.  (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来与文化交流

1957-07-18
第5版()
专栏：友好往来与文化交流

    友好往来与文化交流
    艺术大师丢勒作品在京展览
    中国对外文化联络局举办的“德意志民主共和国十五—十六世纪艺术大师丢勒作品展览”于本月15日在京故宫博物院开幕。
    展品包括油画、水彩画、版画、速写等四百余幅。展览完毕后德意志民主共和国将以柏林德国艺术院的名义将全部作品赠给北京中国画院。
    聂鲁达和亚马多夫妇到北京
    智利进步诗人聂鲁达夫妇和巴西作家亚马多夫妇15日下午坐飞机到北京。他们是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，来中国进行友好访问的。      （据新华社讯）
