Title: Ho Chi Minh Leaves Poland to Visit East Germany; Poland Awards Ho Chi Minh the Order of Polonia Restituta, First Class
Author:
Date: 1957-07-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 25th: After a four-day visit to Poland, President Ho Chi Minh and his entourage left Warsaw for Berlin today.

When President Ho left Warsaw, Chairman of the Polish Council of State Zawadzki and Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz went to the airport to see him off. After reviewing the guard of honor, President Ho waved to the thousands of people who came to see him off.

Many children at the airport enthusiastically cheered for President Ho, waving Polish and Vietnamese flags and shouting "Grandpa Ho Chi Minh, good health! Greetings to the Vietnamese children!" President Ho kissed and hugged them, and gave the children a flower. Chairman Zawadzki acted as President Ho's Russian interpreter for the children.

Finally, as the distinguished guests were about to leave, President Ho Chi Minh said to the send-off party in Polish: "Long live Polish-Vietnamese friendship!" "Goodbye!" "Thank you!"

President Ho Chi Minh's four-day visit to Poland left a deep impression on the Polish people.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 25th: According to the Polish Press Agency, President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received the First Class Order of Polonia Restituta in Warsaw on the 24th.

Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, and other Polish party and state leaders, as well as President Ho Chi Minh's entourage, attended the award ceremony.

In his speech on behalf of the Polish Council of State, Zawadzki pointed out that the decision to award President Ho the medal was to express the fraternal relations between Poland and Vietnam and to commend President Ho Chi Minh for his contributions to the Vietnamese people's liberation struggle, to the peaceful construction of socialism in Vietnam, and to the strengthening of the sincere friendship and cooperation between the people of Poland and Vietnam.

President Ho Chi Minh expressed his gratitude in a speech after receiving the medal. Then, on behalf of the Vietnamese people, he presented the Polish people with a banner with the words "Long live Polish-Vietnamese friendship" written in both Polish and Vietnamese.

Ho Chi Minh held a reception that day. Those present included Polish leaders such as Zawadzki and Cyrankiewicz, representatives from all walks of life in Poland, and envoys from some countries to Warsaw.

### 胡志明离波兰去民主德国访问  波兰赠予胡志明一级复兴勋章

1957-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    胡志明离波兰去民主德国访问
    波兰赠予胡志明一级复兴勋章
    新华社华沙25日电　胡志明主席和他的随行人员在波兰访问四天以后已经在今天离开华沙前往柏林。
    在胡主席离开华沙的时候，波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基和部长会议主席西伦凯维兹曾经前往机场为他送别。在检阅仪仗队以后，胡主席特地向欢送的几千群众招手致意。
    机场上还有许多儿童不断向胡主席热情欢呼，他们挥动着手里的波越两国国旗高呼“胡志明爷爷健康！向越南小朋友致敬！”胡主席亲一个抱一个，并且送给孩子们一朵花，萨瓦茨基主席在一旁充当胡主席和孩子们的俄语翻译。
    最后当贵宾们快要离开的时候，胡主席用波兰话向送行者说“波越友谊万岁！”“再见！”“谢谢！”
    胡志明主席在波兰的四天访问给波兰人民留下了深刻的印象。
    新华社华沙25日电　据波兰通讯社报道，越南民主共和国主席胡志明24日在华沙接受了一级波兰复兴勋章。
    萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹等波兰党和国家领导人以及胡志明主席的随行人员参加了授勋典礼。
    萨瓦茨基代表波兰国务委员会致词时指出，决定授予胡主席勋章是为了表示波越两国的兄弟关系，表彰胡志明主席在越南人民的解放斗争中、在越南和平建设社会主义过程中以及在加强波越两国人民的真诚友谊和合作中所作的贡献。
    胡志明主席在接受勋章后讲话表示感谢。接着，他代表越南人民赠给波兰人民一幅旗帜，上面用波越两国文字写着：“波越友谊万岁”。
    胡志明在这天举行了招待会。出席的有萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹等波兰领导人员、波兰各界代表和一些国家的驻华沙使节。
