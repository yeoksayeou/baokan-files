Title: Premier Zhou Receives Chilean and Japanese Peace Activists and Receives Japanese Mayors and Local Councilors Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1957-07-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon Martinez Gonsada, the head of the Chilean delegation attending the World Peace Council Colombo Conference, and Japanese peace activists Yoro Ohno and Manabu Hattori.

Present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, and Xu Baoju, Standing Committee member.

Xinhua News Agency, July 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai received this evening the Japanese Mayors and Local Councilors Delegation headed by Yatsuo Yamaya.

Present at the meeting were Guo Moruo, Nan Hanchen, Lei Renmin, Li Zhuchen, and Liao Chengzhi.

The delegation presented gifts to Premier Zhou.

### 周总理接见智利、日本和平人士并接见日本市长、地方议员访华团

1957-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见智利、日本和平人士并接见日本市长、地方议员访华团
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了出席世界和平理事会科伦坡会议的智利代表团团长马尔土涅斯·甘萨达，日本和平人士大野阳郎、服部学。
    接见的时候，在座的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，常务委员许宝驹。
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上接见了以山屋八万雄为首的日本市长、地方议员访华团。
    接见时，在座的有郭沫若、南汉宸、雷任民、李烛尘、廖承志。
    代表团向周总理赠送了礼品。
