Title: Egyptian Defense Minister Visits Saudi Arabia
Author:
Date: 1957-07-06
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 5th: Egyptian Armed Forces Commander-in-Chief and Defense Minister General Amer flew to Saudi Arabia on the morning of July 4 to meet with the Saudi King. General Amer is one of President Nasser's closest colleagues. Therefore, observers here are closely watching the outcome of his visit.

### 埃及国防部长赴沙特阿拉伯

1957-07-06
第7版()
专栏：

    埃及国防部长赴沙特阿拉伯
    据新华社5日讯　开罗消息：埃及武装部队总司令兼国防部长阿密尔将军在7月4日上午乘飞机前往沙特阿拉伯会晤沙特国王。阿密尔将军是纳赛尔总统最亲密的同僚之一。因此，这里观察家正在密切注视他的访问结果。
