Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Visits President Pieck
Author:
Date: 1957-07-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 27th. Chairman Ho Chi Minh, after arriving in Berlin on the 25th, paid a visit to President Pieck.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh is visiting the German Democratic Republic at the invitation of President Pieck.

On the morning of the 26th, Chairman Ho Chi Minh successively visited the Mayor of Greater Berlin, Ebert, and the Chairman of the People's Chamber, Dieckmann, in Berlin. Chairman Ho invited Dr. Dieckmann to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

On the afternoon of the 26th, Chairman Ho Chi Minh and his entourage traveled by car from Berlin to Stalinstadt to visit the largest iron and steel complex in the German Democratic Republic, where they were warmly welcomed by the factory's workers. Chairman Ho addressed the workers in the blast furnace workshop, expressing the fraternal greetings and congratulations of the Vietnamese working class. Afterwards, he also toured the city.

### 胡志明主席拜会皮克总统

1957-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席拜会皮克总统
    新华社柏林27日电　胡志明主席在25日到达柏林后拜会了皮克总统。
    胡志明主席是应皮克总统的邀请到民主德国访问的。
    26日上午，胡志明主席在柏林先后拜会了大柏林市长艾柏特和人民议院主席狄克曼。胡主席邀请狄克曼博士前往越南民主共和国访问。
    胡志明主席和他的随员在26日下午从柏林乘车到斯大林斯塔特参观了民主德国最大的钢铁联合企业，受到了全厂职工的热烈欢迎。胡主席在高炉车间向职工们讲话，表达了越南工人阶级的兄弟般的问候和祝贺，随后，还参观了这个城市的市容。
