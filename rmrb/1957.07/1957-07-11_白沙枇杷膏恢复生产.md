Title: White Sand Loquat Paste Resumes Production
Author:
Date: 1957-07-11
Page: 6

Market Report:

    White Sand Loquat Paste Resumes Production
    Loquat paste from Dongting Mountain in Taihu Lake has resumed production, with output increasing eightfold compared to the highest pre-war annual production.
    Dongting Mountain's loquat paste is made from the famous local White Sand loquats and nourishing medicinal ingredients such as Glehnia root and Fritillaria bulbs. It has the effects of moistening the lungs, promoting body fluids, and relieving coughs. The taste is fresh, sweet, and delicious.
                      ·Xinhua News Agency·

### 白沙枇杷膏恢复生产

1957-07-11
第6版()
专栏：市场巡礼

    白沙枇杷膏恢复生产
    太湖洞庭山的枇杷膏恢复生产，产量比战前最高年产量增加八倍。
    洞庭山的枇杷膏，是用当地有名的白沙枇杷和中药沙参、川贝等营养药料加工制成，有润肺、生津、止咳等功效。味道鲜甜可口。
                      ·新华社·
