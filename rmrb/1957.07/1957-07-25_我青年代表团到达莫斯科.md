Title: Our Youth Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-07-25
Page: 5

    Our Youth Delegation Arrives in Moscow
    (Our newspaper's Moscow dispatch on the 24th) The Chinese youth delegation participating in the World Youth Festival arrived in Moscow today and received a grand and warm welcome from the youth of Moscow. According to the ancient Russian custom of welcoming distinguished guests, the youth of Moscow presented bread and salt to the Chinese youth delegation.
    Today, Yaroslavsky Station once again appeared as an ocean of flags and flowers. At 10:30 in the morning, the train pulled into the station, and the Chinese youth representatives alighted amidst the warm welcome of the Moscow youth. The Moscow youth accompanied them to a welcome meeting held in the station square. At the welcome meeting, Semichastny, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Komsomol, first delivered a speech, welcoming the arrival of the Chinese youth delegation and congratulating the Chinese youth on achieving the greatest success at the festival. Then, Polischuk, Secretary of the Komsomol of the Proletarian District of Moscow, delivered a speech on behalf of the Moscow youth, welcoming the Chinese youth guests. Finally, Hu Yaobang, head of the Chinese youth delegation, spoke, thanking the Soviet people and youth for their warm welcome to the Chinese youth delegation. He said that under the leadership of the united Soviet Communist Party Central Committee, the Soviet people and youth will surely achieve a great new victory of communism.

### 我青年代表团到达莫斯科

1957-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    我青年代表团到达莫斯科
    本报莫斯科24日电　参加世界青年联欢节的中国青年代表团今天到达莫斯科，受到莫斯科青年隆重和热烈的欢迎。莫斯科青年按照俄罗斯民族迎接贵宾的古老风俗，向中国青年代表团献了面包和盐。
    今天雅罗斯拉夫车站又一次出现了一个旗帜和鲜花的海洋。上午十点半钟列车开进了车站，中国青年代表们在莫斯科青年们的热烈欢迎中下了车。莫斯科青年陪同着他们出席了在车站广场上举行的欢迎会。在欢迎会上，苏联共青团中央委员会书记谢米察斯特尼首先致词，欢迎中国青年代表团的到来，并且祝贺中国青年们在联欢节上取得最大的成就。接着莫斯科无产阶级区共青团书记波立树克代表莫斯科青年致词，欢迎中国青年客人。最后，中国青年代表团团长胡耀邦讲话，感谢苏联人民和青年对中国青年代表团的热烈欢迎。他说，苏联人民和青年在团结一致的苏共中央的领导下，一定能取得伟大的新的共产主义的胜利。
