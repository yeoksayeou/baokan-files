Title: Restore Peace in Algeria, Don't Allow Fascism to Succeed - French Working People Demonstrate on National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-07-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, July 16th dispatch from Tass, Paris: The broad masses of French working people commemorated the French National Day on July 14th by further strengthening unity and taking practical actions to strive for democratic rights and restore peace in Algeria.

Workers and citizens of Marseille responded to the call of the "Committee for the Defense of Justice and Freedom" and held a massive demonstration. The marching crowds shouted: "Restore Peace in Algeria!" "Don't Allow Fascism to Succeed!"

The working people of Toulon also responded to the call of the local Communist Party, Socialist Party, Radical Socialist Party, Progressive Republican Alliance, and some democratic social groups and held a demonstration.

The "Committee for Unity of Left-Wing Forces" organized a gathering in Villemomble, a suburb of Paris. Communist Party member of parliament Jacques Duclos, Radical Socialist Party member of parliament Ernoult, as well as representatives of the Human Rights League and other Republican organizations attended the gathering. Duclos and Ernoult spoke at the meeting, calling on left-wing forces to unite in the struggle against the danger of fascism.

### 恢复阿尔及利亚和平　不许法西斯主义得逞  法劳动人民在国庆节示威

1957-07-17
第7版()
专栏：

    恢复阿尔及利亚和平　不许法西斯主义得逞
    法劳动人民在国庆节示威
    据新华社16日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：法国广大劳动群众用进一步加强团结，用争取民主权利和恢复阿尔及利亚和平的实际行动，纪念了7月14日法国国庆节。
    马赛的工人和市民响应了“维护正义与自由委员会”的号召，举行了规模巨大的示威游行。游行队伍高呼：“恢复阿尔及利亚和平”，“不许法西斯主义得逞”。
    土伦的劳动人民也响应了当地共产党、社会党、激进社会党、进步共和联盟和一些民主社会团体的号召，举行了示威游行。
    “争取左翼力量团结委员会”在巴黎近郊维勒蒙布里组织了一个集会。共产党议员雅克·杜克洛，激进社会党议员艾尔努，以及人权联盟和其他一些共和主义组织的代表都出席了这次集会。杜克洛和艾尔努在会上讲了话，他们号召左翼力量在反对法西斯危险的斗争中团结起来。
