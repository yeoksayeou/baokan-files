Title: Hong Kong Compatriots Eagerly Purchase Mao Zedong's Speech in Booklet Form
Author:
Date: 1957-07-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, 30th. Hong Kong compatriots are eagerly buying the booklet of Chairman Mao's speech "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People." As of last night, the Hong Kong New Democratic Publishing House, the distribution agent for the book, had already wholesaled 10,000 copies, breaking the highest wholesale figure for books in Hong Kong in recent years.

This booklet began to be sold at retail in major bookstores in Hong Kong yesterday. Citizens who purchased the book were very enthusiastic. The Hong Kong Joint Publishing (Sanlian) Bookstore alone sold more than 3,000 copies within a few hours.

### 香港同胞争购毛主席讲演单行本

1957-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    香港同胞争购毛主席讲演单行本
    新华社香港30日电　香港同胞争购毛主席的“关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题”的讲演的单行本。截至昨天晚上为止，发行这本书的代理书店香港新民主出版社已经批发出一万本，打破了香港书籍几年来批发数字的最高纪录。
    这本单行本昨天在香港各大书店开始零售，购买这本书的市民非常踊跃，仅香港三联书店在几个小时之内就卖出了三千本以上。
