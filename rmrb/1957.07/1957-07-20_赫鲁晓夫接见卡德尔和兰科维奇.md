Title: Khrushchev Receives Kardelj and Ranković
Author:
Date: 1957-07-20
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 19th -- TASS: Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU, received Kardelj and Ranković, Vice Presidents of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia and members of the Central Executive Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, who are currently in the Soviet Union for recuperation, on July 18th. He held talks with them in a frank and friendly atmosphere.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见卡德尔和兰科维奇

1957-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见卡德尔和兰科维奇
    据新华社19日讯  塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫7月18日接见了正在苏联休养的南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席、南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央执行委员会委员卡德尔和兰科维奇，并且同他们在坦率友好的气氛中谈了话。
