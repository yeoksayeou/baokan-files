Title: The Nationalities Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China's Review Report on the Proposals for the Establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region
Author: Liu Geping
Date: 1957-07-16
Page: 2

 The Nationalities Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
 Review Report on the Proposals for the Establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region
 (Adopted by the Nationalities Committee on July 12, 1957)
 Chairman of the Nationalities Committee of the First National People's Congress: Liu Geping
 The Nationalities Committee of the First National People's Congress, after listening to the report made by Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council and Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, to the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress regarding the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, convened an enlarged meeting on July 6 to review the two proposals for the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region. It unanimously agreed that the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region is in accordance with the provisions of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China and is in line with the interests and demands of the people of all nationalities.
 The Nationalities Committee suggests that the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress approve the two proposals put forward by the State Council for the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region.

### 中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会民族委员会  关于成立广西僮族自治区和成立宁夏回族自治区的议案的审查报告
刘格平
1957-07-16
第2版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会民族委员会
    关于成立广西僮族自治区和成立宁夏回族自治区的议案的审查报告
    （1957年7月12日民族委员会通过）
    第一届全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任委员　刘格平
    第一届全国人民代表大会民族委员会听了国务院副总理兼民族事务委员会主任乌兰夫向第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议所作的关于建立广西僮族自治区和宁夏回族自治区的报告以后，于7月6日召开了扩大会议，对成立广西僮族自治区和成立宁夏回族自治区的两个议案进行了审查。一致认为，成立广西僮族自治区和成立宁夏回族自治区是符合中华人民共和国宪法的规定的，是符合各民族人民的利益和要求的。
    民族委员会建议第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议批准国务院提出的成立广西僮族自治区和成立宁夏回族自治区的两个议案。
