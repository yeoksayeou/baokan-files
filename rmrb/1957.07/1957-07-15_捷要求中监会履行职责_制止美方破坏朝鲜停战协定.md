Title: Czechoslovakia Demands that the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Fulfill its Responsibilities and Stop the US from Undermining the Korean Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-07-15
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 14th: The Korean Central News Agency reported today from Prague that the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic recently sent similar memoranda to the Swiss and Swedish governments regarding the US undermining of the Korean Armistice Agreement.
    The memorandum points out: The introduction of new war materials into South Korea and the refusal of the United Nations Command to report this situation to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission is a blatant and serious violation of the most important provisions of the Armistice Agreement, and makes the special importance of the mission of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, as an international neutral body, even more critical.
    In the memorandum, the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic reiterated the special significance of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in maintaining the Korean armistice and maintaining peace in the Far East.
    The memorandum emphasizes that the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic believes that it is the responsibility of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, in the current situation, to take a stand consistent with the provisions of the Armistice Agreement.
    The memorandum expresses confidence that the representatives of Switzerland and Sweden will also act in accordance with the obligations of member states of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission.

### 捷要求中监会履行职责  制止美方破坏朝鲜停战协定

1957-07-15
第3版()
专栏：

    捷要求中监会履行职责
    制止美方破坏朝鲜停战协定
    新华社平壤14日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社今天报道，据来自布拉格的消息说，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府最近就美方破坏朝鲜停战协定的行为分送给瑞士政府和瑞典政府内容类似的备忘录各一份。
    备忘录指出：新的作战物资运入南朝鲜和联合国军司令部方面拒绝把这种情况报告给中立国监察委员会，是明目张胆地严重违反停战协定中的最重要的规定，而且使得作为一个国际的中立机构的中立国监察委员会的使命所具有的特别重要性变得更为极其重要。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府在备忘录中重申中立国监察委员会在维持朝鲜停战和维持远东和平方面的特殊意义。
    备忘录强调说，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府相信，中立国监察委员会在目前的局势下，采取同停战协定的规定相一致的立场，乃是它的责任。
    备忘录表示相信，瑞士和瑞典的代表们也将按照中立国监察委员会成员国的义务行事。
