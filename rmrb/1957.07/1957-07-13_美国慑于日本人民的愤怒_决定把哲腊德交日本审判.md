Title: The United States, Afraid of the Japanese People's Anger, Decides to Hand Over Girard to Japan for Trial
Author:
Date: 1957-07-13
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 12th: The U.S. Supreme Court authorized the U.S. government on the 11th to hand over U.S. soldier Girard, who killed Japanese farmer Mrs. Sakai at a shooting range in Japan, to Japan for trial.

After the U.S. government decided to hand over Girard to Japan not long ago, U.S. Federal Judge McGarrakin ruled on June 18 that the U.S. government should not hand over Girard to the Japanese authorities for trial. This ruling aroused anger in Japanese and world public opinion, and the U.S. Department of Justice had to ask the Supreme Court on June 20 to change the decision of the Federal Court.

### 美国慑于日本人民的愤怒  决定把哲腊德交日本审判

1957-07-13
第7版()
专栏：

    美国慑于日本人民的愤怒
    决定把哲腊德交日本审判
    据新华社12日讯　华盛顿消息：美国最高法院11日授权美国政府把在日本的一个射击场上杀死日本农妇坂井中的美国士兵哲腊德交给日本审判。
    在美国政府不久以前决定把哲腊德交给日本以后，美国联邦法官麦克格拉基又曾经在6月18日宣判，美国政府不得把哲腊德交给日本当局审判。这个宣判引起了日本和世界舆论的愤怒，美国司法部不得不于6月20日要求最高法院改变联邦法院的决定。
