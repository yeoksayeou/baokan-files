Title: Egypt Sincerely Seeks to Resolve Canal Dispute; Conditionally Accepts International Court of Justice Ruling
Author:
Date: 1957-07-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd dispatch from New York: The United Nations announced on July 23rd that Egypt has formally expressed its agreement to accept the binding rulings that the International Court of Justice may issue in the future regarding the Suez Canal dispute, on the condition that such rulings apply to both parties in the dispute and that no special agreement is signed.
According to the announcement, Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi wrote to the United Nations Secretary-General's office on July 18th, clarifying the above position.

### 埃及诚心解决运河纠纷  有条件接受国际法院裁决

1957-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及诚心解决运河纠纷
    有条件接受国际法院裁决
    据新华社23日讯　纽约消息：联合国7月23日宣布，埃及正式表示同意接受国际法院对苏伊士运河纠纷今后作出的强制性裁决，条件是：这种裁决必须对争执的双方都起作用，并且不签订特别协定。
    据宣布，埃及外交部长法齐已经在7月18日写信给联合国秘书长办公处，申明上述立场。
