Title: Japanese Minister of International Trade and Industry Says Time is Ripe to Relax Trade Restrictions on China
Author:
Date: 1957-07-14
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 13th. According to a report in the "Japan Economic News" on the 12th, the new Japanese Minister of International Trade and Industry, Shigesaburo Maeo, stated in an interview with the newspaper's reporter that, for Japan, the time has come to reduce the items on the "COCOM" embargo list that "control" trade with China to the level of the Paris Coordinating Committee's "embargo" on the Soviet Union.

Shigesaburo Maeo also said that he agreed that Japan should follow Britain's example in relaxing trade restrictions on China.

Matsuo Kanekura, Director of the Trade Bureau of the Ministry of International Trade and Industry, revealed at a press conference on the 12th that Japan will formally announce the relaxation of "embargo" restrictions on China within the next week.

### 日本通商产业相说  放宽对华贸易限制时候已到

1957-07-14
第7版()
专栏：

    日本通商产业相说
    放宽对华贸易限制时候已到
    新华社13日讯　东京消息：据“日本经济新闻”12日报道，新任日本通商产业相前尾繁三郎在接见这家报纸的记者时表示，对日本说来，把“管制”对中国贸易的“中国委员会”的“禁运”货单中的项目削减到巴黎统筹委员会对苏联的“禁运”水平的时候已经到来。
    前尾繁三郎还说，他同意日本在放宽对中国贸易限制的问题上效法英国。
    通商产业省通商局局长松尾金藏12日在记者招待会上透露，日本将在下周内正式宣布放宽对中国的“禁运”限制。
