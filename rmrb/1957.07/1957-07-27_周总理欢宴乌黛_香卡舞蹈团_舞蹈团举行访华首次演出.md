Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts Banquet for Uday Shankar Dance Troupe; Dance Troupe Holds First Performance in China

Author: 
Date: 1957-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th – Premier Zhou Enlai held a reception in Beijing on the evening of the 25th to welcome all members of the Indian Uday Shankar Dance Troupe.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai spoke at the beginning of the reception, saying: Today, in the very hot Beijing, we extend a warm welcome with warm hearts to our enthusiastic Indian friends. We have been to India, where it is hot all year round, and everywhere we felt the enthusiastic welcome given to us by the warm people. Today, we thank them for performing such outstanding Indian dances in such hot weather, expressing such love for China.

　　Uday Shankar, in his speech, expressed gratitude for the welcome from Chinese friends.

　　The Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and his wife were also invited to attend the reception.

　　Vice Premier He Long and others also attended the reception.

　　At the reception, Premier Zhou introduced famous Chinese artists to meet with the Indian artists.

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 25th – The Indian Uday Shankar Dance Troupe gave its first performance in China at the Beijing People's Theater on the evening of the 25th.

　　Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and others attended the opening ceremony and watched the performance. After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai and others went on stage to shake hands with Uday Shankar and all the artists of the troupe, and took a group photo as a souvenir.

　　The Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, his wife, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China also came to watch the performance.

　　The Indian artists performed classical Bharatanatyam dance, Kathakali dance, folk dances, and Indian dances with collective performances created and developed by Uday Shankar, all of which received enthusiastic praise from the audience.

　　Starting from the 30th, the Uday Shankar Dance Troupe will formally present the Indian "Shadow Play" in Beijing.

### 周总理欢宴乌黛·香卡舞蹈团  舞蹈团举行访华首次演出

1957-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理欢宴乌黛·香卡舞蹈团
    舞蹈团举行访华首次演出
    据新华社25日讯　周恩来总理25日晚上在北京举行酒会，欢迎印度乌黛·香卡舞蹈团全体人员。
    周恩来总理在酒会开始的时候讲话，他说：今天我们在很热的北京，以热烈的心情，欢迎热情的印度朋友。我们到过印度，在印度四时都是热天，处处感到热情的人民给我们以热烈的欢迎。今天我们感谢他们在这样热的天，表演了这样出色的印度舞蹈，表现了对于中国这样的热爱。
    乌黛·香卡在讲话中，对中国朋友们的欢迎表示感谢。
    印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和他的夫人也应邀出席了酒会。
    副总理贺龙等也出席酒会。
    酒会上，周总理介绍中国著名艺术家们同印度艺术家们见面。
    据新华社25日讯　印度乌黛·香卡舞蹈团25日晚在北京人民剧场举行访问中国的第一场演出。
    国务院总理周恩来、副总理贺龙等都出席了开幕式并观看了演出。在演出结束以后，周恩来总理等上台同乌黛·香卡以及全团的艺术家们握手，并且合影留念。
    印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁及他的夫人以及各国驻华使节也来观看演出。
    印度艺术家们今晚演出了古典婆罗多舞、卡塔卡利舞、民间舞和经过乌黛·香卡创造并发展了的集体表演的印度舞蹈，都受到观众的热烈赞美。
    从30日起，乌黛·香卡舞蹈团将在北京正式献演印度“影子戏”。
