Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Mayor Peng Zhen Give Reports to University Graduates in the Capital

Author:

Date: 1957-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: The Beijing Municipal People's Committee, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the Beijing Municipal Student Federation jointly held the "Beijing Higher Education Graduates Report Meeting" on the evening of the 16th at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace. Premier Zhou Enlai and Mayor Peng Zhen attended the meeting and gave reports to more than 12,000 graduates of the current graduating class from twenty-nine universities in Beijing.

The report meeting was presided over by Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing. Heads of various universities in Beijing also attended the meeting.

### 周恩来总理和彭真市长  向首都高等学校毕业生作报告

1957-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理和彭真市长
    向首都高等学校毕业生作报告
    新华社17日讯  北京市人民委员会、共青团北京市委员会、北京市学生联合会16日晚上在北京市劳动人民文化宫联合举行“北京市高等学校毕业生报告会”，周恩来总理和彭真市长到会向北京市二十九所高等学校本届毕业生一万二千多人作了报告。
    报告会是由北京市副市长吴晗主持的，北京各高等院校的负责人也出席了报告会。
