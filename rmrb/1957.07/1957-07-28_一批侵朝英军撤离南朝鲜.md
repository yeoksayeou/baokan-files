Title: Batch of British Troops Invading Korea Withdraw from South Korea
Author:
Date: 1957-07-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 27th: Seoul dispatch: The last batch of 1,200 British Commonwealth soldiers invading Korea who were withdrawing from South Korea left Incheon by transport ship on the 27th.

### 一批侵朝英军撤离南朝鲜

1957-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    一批侵朝英军撤离南朝鲜
    据新华社平壤27日电　汉城消息：最后一批撤离南朝鲜的侵朝英联邦士兵一千二百人27日坐运输舰离开仁川。
