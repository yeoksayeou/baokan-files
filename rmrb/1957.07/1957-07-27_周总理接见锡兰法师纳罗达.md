Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Ceylonese Monk Narada
Author:
Date: 1957-07-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th. Premier Zhou Enlai received Ceylonese Monk Narada tonight. He Chengxiang, Zhao Puchu, and Zhang Wenjin were also present at the reception.

### 周总理接见锡兰法师纳罗达

1957-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见锡兰法师纳罗达
    新华社26日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上接见了锡兰法师纳罗达。接见时，何成湘、赵朴初、章文晋也在座。
