Title: Ho Chi Minh to Visit India Next Year
Author:
Date: 1957-07-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 15th - It has been announced here that President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has accepted the invitation from the Indian government and will visit India in the spring of next year.

The Vietnamese President's Office also announced when issuing the above communique: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan has accepted President Ho Chi Minh's invitation and will visit Vietnam in mid-September this year.

### 胡志明明年访问印度

1957-07-16
第7版()
专栏：

    胡志明明年访问印度
    新华社河内15日电　此间宣布，越南民主共和国主席胡志明接受了印度政府的邀请，将在明年春天到印度进行访问。
    越南主席府在发表上述公报时另外又宣布：印度副总统拉达克里希南已经接受了胡志明主席的邀请，将在今年9月中旬到越南进行访问。
