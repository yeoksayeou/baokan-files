Title: Li Xiannian Receives Delegation from Mongolian Handicraft Cooperative

Author:
Date: 1957-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 4th. Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian received the delegation from the Handicraft Cooperative of the Mongolian People's Republic, led by Ji. Demqi Ge, member of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, this afternoon. During the meeting, Sonoman Luvsan, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, and Bai Rubing, Director of the Central Handicraft Administration Bureau of our country, were also present.

### 李先念接见蒙古手工艺合作社代表团

1957-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    李先念接见蒙古手工艺合作社代表团
    新华社4日讯　国务院副总理李先念今天下午接见了由蒙古人民革命党中央委员济·德木齐格领导的蒙古人民共和国手工艺合作社访华代表团。接见的时候，蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使索诺曼·鲁布桑和我国中央手工业管理局局长白如冰也在座。
