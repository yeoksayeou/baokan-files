Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Leaves for Moscow to Attend World Youth Festival
Author:
Date: 1957-07-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency Comprehensive Report, July 16th: The Chinese Youth Delegation, participating in the Sixth World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship, left Beijing by train today for Moscow.

The youth delegation consists of sixty-seven members, selected from youth from all walks of life in China.

The head of the delegation is Hu Yaobang, and the deputy heads are Ma John, Zhou Weizhi, Xiang Nan, and Wu Xueqian.

The members of the delegation (in order of stroke count of surnames) are: Ding Lizhun, Ding Mingzhi, Yu Deshui, Wan Taihe, Wang Yunhu, Wang Baojing, Wang Jin, Fang Yanlong, Qiu Zhengyi, Ye Zhishan, Shi Kunbin, Bing Gengye, Shen Xiaozeng, Gude Xiangqian, Jiang Wenhan, Bi Wenting, Yibulayingu Rouzi, Tuohutuerhan, Qi Shengshan, Liu Ying, Wu Juetian, Chen Sheng, Li Xiaofang, Li Tingshun, Lu Jun, Di Shuxun, Li Chun, Li Zhiguo, Song Chengxi, Li Minhua, Shama Wuzi, Su Shunan, Chen Qingquan, Zhou Shixian, Yue Shangwu, Zhou Zhenyuan, Lin Ping, Lin Xuan, Jin Zhangyu, Wu Xiufu, Wu Peixin, Wu Ailian, Qiabag Sang Wangdui, Zheng Zhenyu, Tu Shihua, Zhao Yucheng, Rong Hongren, Guo Yongjian, Weng Kemin, Yin Meidi, Ma Liesun, Tang Xinpeng, Chang Cheng, Zhang Shiqian, Zhang Jiyuan, Yan Xia, Qian Liren, Ji Minxiu, Sai Chi, Xiao Jian, Nie Hong, Tan Zhuofen, Tan Yuling, Tan Mingzhang.

### 参加世界青年联欢节  我国青年代表团赴莫斯科

1957-07-17
第6版()
专栏：

    参加世界青年联欢节
    我国青年代表团赴莫斯科
    本报综合报道　据新华社16日讯，参加第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的中国青年代表团，今天乘火车离开北京前往莫斯科。
    青年代表共六十七人，他们是中国各阶层青年中选派出来的。
