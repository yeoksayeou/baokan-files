Title: Premier Zhou Receives Japanese Agricultural Technology Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1957-07-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Japanese Agricultural Technology Delegation headed by Hisayoshi Muramatsu this morning.

Present at the meeting were Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, and Yang Xiandong, Chairman of the China Agricultural Society.

### 周总理接见日本农业技术访华团

1957-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本农业技术访华团
    新华社8日讯　周恩来总理今天上午接见了以村松久义为首的日本农业技术访华团。
    接见的时候，在座的有农业部部长廖鲁言、中国农学会理事长杨显东。
