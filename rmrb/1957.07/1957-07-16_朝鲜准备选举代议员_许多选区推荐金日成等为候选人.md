Title: Korea Prepares for Election of Deputies; Many Electoral Districts Recommend Kim Il-sung, etc., as Candidates
Author:
Date: 1957-07-16
Page: 7

    North Korea Prepares for Election of Deputies
    Many Electoral Districts Recommend Kim Il-sung, etc., as Candidates
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 15th – According to North Korean newspapers, voters throughout North Korea began holding meetings on July 14th to recommend candidates for deputies to the Supreme People's Assembly. The North Korean Supreme People's Assembly will hold elections for deputies on August 27th of this year.
    The Sadong electoral district in Pyongyang, and many electoral districts in South Pyongan Province such as Mundok and Sungho, yesterday recommended Marshal Kim Il-sung as a candidate. Kim Tu-bong, Choi Yong-kun, Hong Myung-hui, Kim Il, Pak Chong-ae, Kim Chang-man, and other leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and the Republic's government have also been recommended by voters in various electoral districts as candidates for deputy.
    The candidates recommended by voters in various regions also include social activists, production innovators, literary and art workers, and patriotic people from all walks of life in the Republic.

### 朝鲜准备选举代议员  许多选区推荐金日成等为候选人

1957-07-16
第7版()
专栏：

    朝鲜准备选举代议员
    许多选区推荐金日成等为候选人
    据新华社平壤15日电　据朝鲜报纸报道，朝鲜北部各地选民从7月14日起开始集会，推荐最高人民会议代议员的候选人。朝鲜最高人民会议将在今年8月27日举行代议员的选举。
    平壤市寺洞选区和平安南道文德、胜湖等许多选区昨天已经推荐金日成元帅为候选人。金枓奉、崔庸健、洪命熹、金一、朴正爱、金昌满等朝鲜劳动党和共和国政府的领导人也都被各选区的选民推荐为代议员候选人。
    各地选民推荐的候选人中还包括共和国的社会活动家、生产革新者、文艺工作者和各阶层爱国人士。
