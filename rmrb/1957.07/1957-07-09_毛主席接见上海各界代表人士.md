Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Representatives from All Circles in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-07-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 8th: Chairman Mao Zedong met with representatives from Shanghai's scientific, educational, literary, artistic, and industrial and commercial circles at the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Building on the evening of the 7th, and had cordial conversations with them.

Chairman Mao Zedong talked with everyone around the table for about two hours.

Those who participated in the conversation included Qi Qisheng, Tan Jiazhen, Da Yijin, Sun Huairen, Zhou Xuliang, Li Ruifu, Yin Hongzhang, Wang You, Ying Yunwei, Shen Fu, Zheng Junli, Zhao Dan, Jin Yan, Huang Zongying, Chen Liting, Zhang Jinyi, Luo Ziknan, Ding Shande, Chen Daxie, Wu Hexuan, Qian Baojun, Fu Yuchen, Ding Chen, Xu Ziwei, Jiang Xuemo, Liu Nianyi, Wu Zhongyi, Li Guohao, Wang Yuanmei, Huang Chen, Su Delong, Chen Mingshan, Ye Baoshan, Nie Chuanxian, Shu Shicheng, and Shi Mukang, totaling thirty-six people.

Ke Qingshi, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Secretaries Chen Pixian, Cao Diqiu, Wei Wenbo, and Ma Tianshui also accompanied the meeting. After the conversation, Chairman Mao Zedong watched a Yue Opera performance with everyone.

### 毛主席接见上海各界代表人士

1957-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见上海各界代表人士
    新华社上海8日电　毛泽东主席7日晚在上海中苏友好大厦，接见了上海科学、教育、文学、艺术和工商界的代表人士，并和他们进行了亲切的交谈。
    毛泽东主席和大家围桌谈话约两小时。
    参加这次谈话的有漆琪生、谈家桢、笪移今、孙怀仁、周煦良、李锐夫、殷宏章、汪猷、应云卫、沈浮、郑君里、赵丹、金焰、黄宗英、陈鲤庭、章靳以、罗稷南、丁善德、陈大燮、武和轩、钱宝钧、傅于琛、丁忱、徐子威、蒋学模、刘念义、吴中一、李国豪、王元美、黄晨、苏德隆、陈铭珊、叶宝珊、聂传贤、束世泟、史慕康等共三十六人。
    中共上海市委第一书记柯庆施，书记陈丕显、曹荻秋、魏文伯、马天水等也陪同参加了这次谈话。谈话后，毛泽东主席并和大家一起观看了越剧。
