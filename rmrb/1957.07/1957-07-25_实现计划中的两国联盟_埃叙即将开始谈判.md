Title: Planned Two-Nation Union to be Realized: Egypt and Syria to Start Negotiations Soon
Author:
Date: 1957-07-25
Page: 6

    Realizing the Planned Two-Nation Union
    Egypt and Syria to Start Negotiations Soon
    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 24th: Syrian Prime Minister al-Asali said on July 23 that Syria and Egypt will start negotiations as soon as possible to realize the planned two-nation union.
    Al-Asali told reporters that the planned union will be realized in four stages: political union, military union, economic union, and cultural union.
    Al-Asali made these remarks when commenting on Nasser's speech to the National Assembly on the 22nd. In that speech, Nasser said that Egypt would respond to Syria's desire to form an alliance with Egypt.

### 实现计划中的两国联盟  埃叙即将开始谈判

1957-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

    实现计划中的两国联盟
    埃叙即将开始谈判
    新华社24日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总理阿萨利7月23日说，叙利亚和埃及将尽速开始谈判以实现计划中的两国联盟。
    阿萨利对记者说，计划中的联盟将分四个阶段——政治联盟、军事联盟、经济联盟和文化联盟——来实现。
    阿萨利是在评论纳赛尔22日在国民议会的演说时说这番话的。纳赛尔在那篇演说中说，埃及将响应叙利亚所主张的同埃及结成联盟的愿望。
