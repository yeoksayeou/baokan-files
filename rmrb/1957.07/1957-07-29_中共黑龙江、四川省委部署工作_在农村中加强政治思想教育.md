Title: Heilongjiang and Sichuan Provincial Committees of the CPC Deploy Work: Strengthening Political and Ideological Education in Rural Areas
Author:
Date: 1957-07-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Harbin. The meeting of the CPC Heilongjiang Provincial Committee, prefectural committee and county committee secretaries, which concluded on the 20th, deployed rural work for the second half of the year in Heilongjiang Province. Based on the current agricultural production situation, the meeting decided to immediately launch a mass movement for increasing production and ensuring harvest in the province's rural areas.

The meeting also decided to continue the work of rectifying and consolidating agricultural cooperatives during the slack farming season and after the autumn harvest, while simultaneously rectifying Party grassroots organizations. After analyzing the current ideological state of the people of all strata in rural areas, the meeting decided to further strengthen the Party's political and ideological education work in rural areas, and launch a socialist propaganda movement both inside and outside the Party.

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu. From the 10th to the 20th, the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the CPC convened a provincial mountainous area production conference. The meeting discussed how to implement the policy of developing diversified operations with increased grain production as the center.

The meeting also agreed that: at present, ideological education work for wealthy peasants should be strengthened; a socialist ideological education should continue to be carried out in depth in rural areas, combined with criticism of right-wing remarks, to educate the peasant masses to maintain vigilance, strengthen supervision of landlords, rich peasants, and counter-revolutionaries, and resolutely sanction the few who engage in sabotage activities.

### 中共黑龙江、四川省委部署工作  在农村中加强政治思想教育

1957-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中共黑龙江、四川省委部署工作
    在农村中加强政治思想教育
    据新华社哈尔滨电　20日结束的中共黑龙江省地委、县委书记会议，部署了黑龙江省下半年农村工作，会议根据当前农业生产的情况，决定立即在全省农村中开展一个群众性的增产保收运动。
    会议还决定在挂锄期间和秋收以后继续进行整顿巩固农业社的工作，同时整顿党的基层组织。在分析了当前农村中各阶层人民的思想状况以后，会议决定进一步加强党在农村中的政治思想教育工作，在党内外开展一个社会主义的宣传运动。
    据新华社成都电　从10日到20日，中共四川省委员会召开了全省性的山区生产会议。会议讨论了怎样贯彻以增产粮食为中心来发展多种经营的方针。
    会议还同意：当前应加强对富裕农民的思想教育工作；在农村中继续深入地进行一次社会主义思想教育，结合批判右派言论，教育农民群众保持警惕，加强对地主、富农、反革命分子的监督，对少数有破坏活动的，应该坚决加以制裁。
