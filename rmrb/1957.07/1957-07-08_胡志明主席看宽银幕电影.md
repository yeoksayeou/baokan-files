Title: President Ho Chi Minh Watches Widescreen Movies
Author:
Date: 1957-07-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th—President Ho Chi Minh and his entourage, accompanied by Vice-Chairman Zhu De, watched the widescreen movies "May Day Celebrations" and "Prelude to the Revolution" this evening.

Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Luo Guibo, and Vietnamese Ambassador to China Nguyen Khang also attended the screening.

### 胡志明主席看宽银幕电影

1957-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席看宽银幕电影
    新华社7日讯　胡志明主席和他的随行人员由朱德副主席陪同，在今晚观看了宽银幕电影“五月的节日”和“革命的前奏”。
    文化部副部长夏衍，中国驻越南大使罗贵波和越南驻中国大使阮康都陪同观看。
