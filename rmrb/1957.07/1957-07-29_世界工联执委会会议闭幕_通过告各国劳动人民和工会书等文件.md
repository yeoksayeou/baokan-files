Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Committee Meeting Concludes, Adopts Appeal to Working People and Trade Unions of All Countries and Other Documents
Author:
Date: 1957-07-29
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 28th: The 14th session of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Trade Unions concluded on the 27th, after four days of meetings.

    On the final day, the meeting adopted an appeal from the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Trade Unions to the working people and trade unions of all countries regarding the working class's struggle for its immediate demands and the preparations for the Fourth World Trade Union Congress. It also adopted a letter to the members of the United Nations Disarmament Subcommittee on the issue of halting the production, use, and testing of atomic and hydrogen weapons, as well as a declaration on the Algerian question.

    During the continued discussion on Seydou’s report on the first item on the agenda at the meeting on the 27th, speeches were delivered by World Federation of Trade Unions Vice President and Latin American Labor Federation Vice President Paniagua, Chairman of the Central Council of Bulgarian Trade Unions Prahov, General Secretary of the Central Council of the National Federation of Free Trade Unions of Hungary Gáspár, World Federation of Trade Unions Vice President Luchesi, and Chairman of the Central Council of Albanian Trade Unions Pilišti.

    World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Seydou made a summary speech on the first item on the agenda. He suggested that before the congress is convened, an outline of the report on this agenda item should be published to facilitate discussions within the central organizations of trade unions in various countries.

    During the discussion on the second item on the agenda—amendments to the charter of the World Federation of Trade Unions—speeches were delivered by World Federation of Trade Unions President Vittorio, World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Seydou, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Liu Changsheng, as well as representatives from trade unions in the Soviet Union, France, India, and other countries, and representatives from some of the trade union internationals affiliated with the World Federation of Trade Unions.

    The meeting also heard and discussed the report by World Federation of Trade Unions Secretary Jourdain on the preparations for the Fourth World Trade Union Congress, which was the third item on the agenda.

    On the day the meeting closed, all participants visited the mausoleum of Lenin and Stalin and laid wreaths on behalf of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Trade Unions.

### 世界工联执委会会议闭幕  通过告各国劳动人民和工会书等文件

1957-07-29
第3版()
专栏：

    世界工联执委会会议闭幕
    通过告各国劳动人民和工会书等文件
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：世界工会联合会执行委员会第十四次会议进行四天后，已经在27日闭幕。
    最后一天的会议通过了世界工联执行委员会就工人阶级为切身要求而进行的斗争以及第四次世界工会代表大会的筹备情形告各国劳动人民和工会书，通过了就停止生产、使用和试验原子武器和氢武器问题给联合国裁军小组委员会委员的一封信，还通过了关于阿尔及利亚问题的宣言。
    在27日的会议继续讨论赛扬就第一项议程所作的报告时，发言的有世界工联副主席、拉丁美洲劳工联盟副主席佩尼，保加利亚工会中央理事会主席普拉霍夫，匈牙利自由工会全国联合会中央理事会总书记加什帕尔，世界工联副主席卢切西，阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会主席皮里斯特利。
    世界工联总书记赛扬就第一项议程作了总结性发言。他建议在召开代表大会前夕发表关于这项议程的报告提纲，以便在各国工会中央组织中展开讨论。
    这天的会议在进行第二项议程——对世界工联章程的修正案的讨论时发言的有世界工联主席维多里奥、世界工联总书记赛扬、中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜以及苏联、法国、印度等国工会的代表和世界工联所属的一些工会国际的代表。
    会议还听取和讨论了世界工联书记儒丹就第三项议程所作的关于第四次世界工会代表大会的筹备情形的报告。
    全体与会者在会议闭幕当天谒了列宁和斯大林的陵墓，并且代表世界工联执行委员会献了花圈。
