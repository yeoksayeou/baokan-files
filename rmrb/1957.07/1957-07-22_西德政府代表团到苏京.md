Title: West German Government Delegation Arrives in Soviet Capital
Author:
Date: 1957-07-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st. The government delegation of the Federal Republic of Germany, led by Envoy Lahr, arrived in Moscow today. This delegation will negotiate with the Soviet government on issues related to the development of relations between the two countries.

### 西德政府代表团到苏京

1957-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    西德政府代表团到苏京
    新华社莫斯科21日电　由拉尔特使率领的德意志联邦共和国政府代表团今天到达莫斯科。这个代表团将同苏联政府谈判有关发展两国关系的问题。
