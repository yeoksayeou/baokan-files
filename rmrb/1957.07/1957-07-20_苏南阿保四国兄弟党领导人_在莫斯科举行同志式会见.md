Title: Leaders of the Soviet, Yugoslav, Albanian and Bulgarian Brother Parties Hold Comradely Meeting in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th (TASS): On July 18th, the leaders of the brotherly Communist and Workers' Parties of the Soviet Union, Yugoslavia, Albania, and Bulgaria held a comradely meeting at a suburban villa in Moscow.

    Participating in the meeting were Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, who is currently recuperating in the Soviet Union; Comrade Todor Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party; Comrade Edvard Kardelj and Comrade Aleksandar Ranković, Vice Presidents of the Federal Executive Council of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia and members of the Executive Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia; Comrade Vesselinov, member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia; and Comrade Kreacic, Deputy Secretary of State of the Secretariat of National Defense of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

    On the Soviet side, the following comrades of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union participated in this comradely meeting: Comrade N.S. Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU; Comrades Aristov, Belyayev, Brezhnev, Kirichenko, Kuusinen, Furtseva, members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the CPSU; and Comrade Pospelov, alternate member.

    Also participating in the meeting were Comrades Serov, Zamchevsky, and Andropov, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to the Soviet Union, Comrade Prifti, and Ambassador of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia to the Soviet Union, Comrade Micunovic. Family members of the leaders of the fraternal parties also attended this comradely meeting.

    The meeting took place in a warm and sincere atmosphere. Comrades Khrushchev, Kuusinen, Kardelj, Ranković, Hoxha, and Zhivkov exchanged friendly conversations during the meeting.

### 苏南阿保四国兄弟党领导人  在莫斯科举行同志式会见

1957-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    苏南阿保四国兄弟党领导人
    在莫斯科举行同志式会见
    新华社19日讯  塔斯社莫斯科19日电：7月18日，苏联、南斯拉夫、阿尔巴尼亚和保加利亚四国兄弟共产党和工人党的领导人在莫斯科的郊外别墅里举行了同志式的会见。
    参加这次会见的有目前正在苏联休养的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查同志，保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫同志，南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国联邦执行委员会副主席、南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会执行委员会委员卡德尔同志和兰科维奇同志，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会委员维塞林诺夫同志，南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国国防国务秘书处副秘书长克雷阿契奇同志。
    苏联共产党方面参加这次同志式的会见的有：苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫同志，苏共中央主席团委员阿里斯托夫同志、别利亚耶夫同志、勃列日涅夫同志、基里钦科同志、库西宁同志、福尔采娃同志和候补委员波斯伯洛夫同志。
    参加这次会见的还有谢罗夫同志、扎姆切夫斯基同志和安德罗波夫同志，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻苏联大使普里夫蒂同志和南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国驻苏联大使米丘诺维奇同志。各兄弟党领导人的家属也参加了这次同志式的会见。
    这次会见是在热烈诚挚的气氛中进行的。赫鲁晓夫、库西宁、卡德尔、兰科维奇、霍查、日夫科夫同志在会见中互相作友好的谈话。
