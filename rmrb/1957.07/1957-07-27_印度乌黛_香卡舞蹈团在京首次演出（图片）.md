Title: The Indian Uday Shankar Dance Troupe Performs for the First Time in Beijing (Photos)
Author: Gao Liang, Wang Zhiyuan
Date: 1957-07-27
Page: 5

    The Indian Uday Shankar Dance Troupe Performs for the First Time in Beijing
    The Indian Uday Shankar Dance Troupe gave its first performance in China at the Beijing People's Theater on the evening of the 25th. Premier Zhou Enlai and artist Mei Lanfang attended the opening ceremony and watched the performance. After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai and others went on stage to shake hands with the artists and take photos.
    "Indra" Dance
    Performer: Uday Shankar
    "Krishna Leela" Dance
    Performer: Amala Shankar
    "Labor and Machine" Dance Performed by: All members of the troupe
    Our reporters Gao Liang and Wang Zhiyuan took these pictures

### 印度乌黛·香卡舞蹈团在京首次演出（图片）
高粮　王志渊
1957-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    印度乌黛·香卡舞蹈团在京首次演出
    印度乌黛·香卡舞蹈团25日晚在北京人民剧场举行访问中国的第一场演出。周恩来总理和艺术家梅兰芳等出席了开幕式并观看了演出。演出结束后，周恩来总理等上台同艺术家们握手，并合影
    “因陀罗”舞
    表演者：乌黛·香卡
    “克里希里尼”舞
    表演者：亚玛娜·香卡
    “劳力与机器”舞　表演者：全团演员
    本报记者　高粮　王志渊摄
