Title: Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet for Colombo Municipal Council Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-07-31
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, July 30th. Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen held a banquet this evening to welcome the Colombo Municipal Council Goodwill Delegation, headed by Colombo Mayor Sugathadasa.

    In his speech at the banquet, Mayor Peng Zhen warmly welcomed the distinguished guests from Ceylon. He said that the people of China and Ceylon have a long-standing friendship. As early as the first century AD, our two countries began economic and cultural exchanges. Not long ago, our two countries established diplomatic relations and exchanged ambassadors. Our understanding and friendship are growing stronger day by day. Peng Zhen concluded by expressing his belief that the friendship and understanding between the people of Beijing and Colombo will deepen with continuous mutual exchanges, and will also help strengthen and develop the friendship between the people of China and Ceylon.

    In his speech, Mayor Sugathadasa first conveyed the friendship of the people of Ceylon to the people of China, and expressed his sincere gratitude for the warm welcome and hospitality they had received. He said that friendly cooperation between China and Ceylon is beneficial to both countries. He firmly believes that the friendship between China and Ceylon is growing stronger, and that the delegation's visit will promote the unity, friendship, and cooperation between the two countries. He expressed his hope that mutual visits between China and Ceylon will increase day by day, in order to further enhance the understanding between the people of the two countries and promote the common interests of the two peoples.

    Among those present at the banquet were Beijing Vice Mayors Wu Han, Feng Jiping, Cheng Hongyi, Jia Tingsan, and Le Songsheng, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Liu Ren, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Li Chen, and heads of various departments of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee and various people's organizations.

    Prior to the banquet, Mayor Peng Zhen met with all members of the delegation. Ceylon Ambassador to China Pereira was also present at the meeting.

### 彭真宴科伦坡市政议会代表团

1957-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真宴科伦坡市政议会代表团
    新华社30日讯　北京市市长彭真今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎以科伦坡市长苏加塔达萨为首的科伦坡市政议会访华友好代表团。
    彭真市长在宴会上讲话，热烈欢迎锡兰贵宾。他说，中锡两国人民有着悠久的友谊。远自公元一世纪起，我们两国就开始了经济、文化的接触。不久前，我们两国又建立了外交关系，互派了大使，我们之间的了解和友谊正在日益加强。彭真最后表示相信北京和科伦坡两个城市人民的友谊与了解，将会随着不断的相互交往而日益加深，同时也将有助于加强和发展中锡两国人民的友谊。
    苏加塔达萨市长在讲话开始时，首先转达了锡兰人民对中国人民的友谊，并且对他们所受到的热烈欢迎和招待表示真诚的感谢。他说，中国和锡兰两国之间的友好合作，是对两国都有利的。他深信中锡两国的友谊正在日益增进，代表团的这次访问，将促进两国的团结、友好和合作。他希望中锡两国之间的相互访问日益增加，以更进一步增进两国人民的了解，增进两国人民的共同利益。
    出席宴会作陪的，有北京市副市长吴晗、冯基平、程宏毅、贾庭三、乐松生，政协北京市委员会主席刘仁，外交部亚洲司副司长李晨，以及北京市人民委员会各部门和各人民团体负责人。
    在宴会以前，彭真市长接见了代表团全体人员。接见时，锡兰驻华大使佩雷拉也在座。
