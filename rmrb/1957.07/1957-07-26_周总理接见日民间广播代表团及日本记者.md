Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Japanese Private Broadcasting Delegation and Japanese Journalists
Author:
Date: 1957-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Japanese Private Broadcasting Association Delegation headed by Yasumasa Tajiri, as well as reporters from Kyodo News and Asahi Shimbun in Beijing this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Mei Yi, and Wen Jize.

### 周总理接见日民间广播代表团及日本记者

1957-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日民间广播代表团及日本记者
    新华社25日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以田尻泰正为首的日本民间广播联盟代表团，以及日本共同社、朝日新闻在北京的记者。
    接见时在座的，有廖承志、梅益和温济泽。
