Title: "Crossing the River Reconnaissance" Praised in Belgrade
Author:
Date: 1957-07-23
Page: 5

    "Crossing the River Reconnaissance"
    Praised in Belgrade
    Belgrade Dispatch, July 22nd – The Chinese revolutionary film "Crossing the River Reconnaissance" has been showing in six cinemas in Belgrade for the past week. The thrilling plot and the bravery and resourcefulness of the reconnaissance personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as their endearing image of loyalty to the people's cause, have aroused great interest among the audience. During the screening, the audience was completely captivated by the tense scenes in the film. Applause and laughter were frequently heard from the audience when touching scenes appeared. Through this film, the Yugoslav audience has gained a deeper understanding of the Chinese people's revolutionary struggle.

### “渡江侦察记”  在贝尔格莱德受到赞赏

1957-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    “渡江侦察记”
    在贝尔格莱德受到赞赏
    本报贝尔格莱德22日专电　我国革命故事影片“渡江侦察记”最近一周来在贝尔格莱德市六家电影院上演。影片中的惊险情节和我人民解放军侦察人员的勇敢机智，忠于人民事业的可爱形象，引起观众的很大兴趣。在放映过程中，观众完全为影片中的紧张场面所吸引。看到动人的镜头时，从观众中不时传出掌声和笑声。通过这部影片，南斯拉夫观众对中国人民的革命斗争有了更进一步的了解。
