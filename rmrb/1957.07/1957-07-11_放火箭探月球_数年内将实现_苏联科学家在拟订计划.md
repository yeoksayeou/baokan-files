Title: Launching Rockets to Explore the Moon: To Be Realized Within Several Years, Soviet Scientists are Drafting Plans
Author:
Date: 1957-07-11
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 10th Dispatch: According to a TASS report, Soviet scientists have recently drafted a plan to launch cosmic rockets to the moon. Soviet scientists believe that in the next five to ten years, people will be able to conquer the moon from Earth using radio-controlled rockets and small tank-like laboratories.

    The plan for launching cosmic rockets drafted by Soviet Deputy Doctor of Technical Sciences, Hlebceвич, is as follows: after a three-stage rocket weighing dozens of tons is launched from a mountainous airfield, many powerful radio positioning stations on the ground will closely monitor the rocket. At the same time, the automatic instruments on the rocket will continuously emit radio signals, and scientists will use electronic computers to calculate these signals and continue to direct the flight of the cosmic rocket.

    The fuel carried by the cosmic rocket will be used up before it reaches the moon. At this time, another rocket with supplementary fuel, launched from the ground, will catch up with it and replenish the fuel into the cosmic rocket through a special device.

    The cosmic rocket continues to fly and escapes the Earth's gravitational pull, entering the moon's gravitational pull.

    According to radio commands from Earth, the rocket turns around, points its tail at the moon, and slowly descends onto the moon.

    A small tank-like laboratory automatically drives out of the rocket onto the moon and automatically erects an antenna and radio transmitter. The laboratory, while moving on the moon under the control of radio signals from Earth, transmits the measurement results on the moon back to Earth.

    In this way, scientists from all over the world can observe everything happening on the moon through powerful radio televisions and other equipment.

### 放火箭探月球  数年内将实现  苏联科学家在拟订计划

1957-07-11
第7版()
专栏：

    放火箭探月球  数年内将实现
    苏联科学家在拟订计划
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联科学家最近拟订了向月球放射宇宙火箭的计划。苏联科学家们认为，在今后五年和十年的时间内，人们就可以在地球上用无线电指挥火箭和小型坦克式实验室来征服月球。
    苏联技术科学副博士赫列勃采维奇拟订放射宇宙火箭的计划是这样的：几十吨重的三级火箭在一个山地飞机场放射后，地面上许多强大的无线电定位站就紧紧地控制住火箭，同时，火箭上的自动仪器也不断地发出无线电讯号，科学家就根据这些讯号用电子计算机加以计算，继续指挥宇宙火箭的飞行。
    宇宙火箭携带的燃料在飞上月球以前就要用完，这时，从地面上放出的另一个补充燃料的火箭，追上了它，通过一种特殊的设备，把燃料补充进宇宙火箭。
    宇宙火箭继续飞行，就脱离了地心吸力的范围，而进入了月球吸力的范围。
    根据地球上的无线电指挥，火箭就调转身来，用尾部对着月球，缓缓地降到月球上。
    一辆小型坦克式实验室自动地从火箭里驶上月球，并且自动地架起天线和无线电发报机，实验室一边根据地球上无线电讯号的操纵在月球上行动，一边把在月球上测量到的结果发送到地球上来。
    这样，世界各国的科学家都可以通过强大的无线电电视机和其他设备观察到月球上发生的一切事情了。
