Title: CPSU and PCI Hold Talks, Agreeing on Various Issues of the Communist Movement
Author:
Date: 1957-07-29
Page: 3

    CPSU and PCI Hold Talks
    Agreeing on Various Issues of the Communist Movement
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th - According to TASS, representatives of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (CPSU) held talks on the 27th with a delegation from the Italian Communist Party (PCI).
    The PCI delegation arrived in the Soviet Union on July 16th for a visit.
    The CPSU was represented at the talks by Khrushchev, Suslov, Kuusinen, Pospelov, and Ponomarev; the PCI was represented by Longo, Sereni, Alicata, and others.
    During the long and sincere talks, both sides exchanged information about the work of their respective parties and exchanged views on various issues of the communist movement. The exchange of views demonstrated that both sides have a consistent view on these issues.
    The PCI delegation expressed satisfaction with the great achievements made by the Soviet Union in all aspects of national life since the 20th Congress of the CPSU, in ensuring the full implementation of the line of the 20th Congress of the CPSU.

### 苏共和意共举行会谈  双方对共产主义运动各种问题看法一致

1957-07-29
第3版()
专栏：

    苏共和意共举行会谈
    双方对共产主义运动各种问题看法一致
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会的代表27日同意大利共产党代表团举行了会谈。
    意共代表团是在7月16日到达苏联进行访问的。
    参加会谈的，苏共方面有赫鲁晓夫、苏斯洛夫、库西宁、波斯伯洛夫、波诺马烈夫；意共方面有隆哥、塞伦尼、阿利卡塔等。
    在长时间的真诚会谈中，双方交换了两党的工作情报，就共产主义运动的各种问题交换了意见。交换意见的结果证明，双方在这些问题上具有一致的看法。
    意共代表团人员对苏联在苏共第二十次代表大会以后，在国家生活的各个方面为保证充分实现苏共第二十次代表大会的路线所获得的巨大成就，表示满意。
