Title: Soviet Leaders to Visit East Germany
Author:
Date: 1957-07-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 23rd. "German Radio" in the Berlin Democratic Zone broadcast on the evening of the 22nd that a Soviet Party and Government delegation headed by Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and Nikolai Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, will visit East Germany at the beginning of August at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED), and the Government of The German Democratic Republic.

### 苏联领袖将访问民主德国

1957-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联领袖将访问民主德国
    新华社柏林23日电　柏林民主区的“德意志广播电台”22日晚上广播说：以苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫和部长会议主席布尔加宁为首的苏联党和政府代表团应德国统一社会党中央委员会和德意志民主共和国政府的邀请，将在8月初访问民主德国。
