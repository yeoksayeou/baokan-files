Title: Mongolia's Third Great People's Hural Holds Meeting; Sambu Elected and Re-elected Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural; Adoption of the List of Members of the Council of Ministers Headed by Tsedenbal
Author:
Date: 1957-07-09
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 8th: The first session of Mongolia's Third Great People's Hural opened on the afternoon of July 7th. The Third Great People's Hural was elected on June 16th.

The meeting elected Sürenjav as Chairman of the Great People's Hural, and Danaajav and Natsuk as Vice-Chairmen. At the same time, the meeting also adopted the list of members of the various committees of the Great People's Hural. Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, delivered a report on the talks between the Mongolian and Soviet governments at the meeting. In his report, he pointed out that the Soviet government would help Mongolia carry out three years of construction. He said that Mongolia's construction plan for 1958 to 1960 stipulates that the number of livestock will increase by 5.6%, the sown area will increase by 2.3 times, and industrial construction will increase by 40%. The total investment in the three-year plan will be twice as much as the second five-year plan.

When talking about the international situation, Tsedenbal said that the people of the world are launching a movement to consolidate peace and oppose the arms race, the socialist camp has always pursued a policy of peace, and the Mongolian people have decided to join the people of the world in the struggle to defend peace.

Tsedenbal, on behalf of the Council of Ministers, submitted his resignation at the meeting, as the term of the current Council of Ministers has expired. The Congress decided that Tsedenbal would propose the list of the next Council of Ministers, and the current Council of Ministers would continue to perform its duties until the new Council of Ministers is formed.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 8th: The first session of Mongolia's Third Great People's Hural closed on the afternoon of July 8th.

The meeting elected Sambu to continue to serve as Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural, Sharab as Secretary-General, and Damba and four others as members of the Presidium.

The meeting also adopted the list of members of the Council of Ministers. Tsedenbal was re-elected Chairman of the Council of Ministers; the first Vice-Chairman was Tsende, and the Vice-Chairmen were Molomjamts, Ragchaa, Aveircid and Maidar. The meeting approved the appointment of Choiizamts as State Prosecutor General.

A delegation from the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union was invited to attend the meeting. The head of the delegation, Khokhlov, spoke at the meeting. On behalf of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, he congratulated the conference on its success and congratulated the Mongolian people on their greater achievements in socialist construction, and presented gifts to the conference.

### 蒙古第三届大人民呼拉尔举行会议  桑布当选连任大人民呼拉尔主席团主席  通过泽登巴尔为首的部长会议成员名单

1957-07-09
第7版()
专栏：

    蒙古第三届大人民呼拉尔举行会议
    桑布当选连任大人民呼拉尔主席团主席
    通过泽登巴尔为首的部长会议成员名单
    新华社乌兰巴托8日电　蒙古第三届大人民呼拉尔第一次会议7月7日下午开幕。第三届大人民呼拉尔是在6月16日选出的。
    会议选出了苏伦扎布为大人民呼拉尔主席，丹娜扎布、那楚克为副主席。同时，会议还通过了大人民呼拉尔各个委员会组成人员的名单。蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔在会上作了关于蒙苏两国政府会谈的报告。他在报告中指出，苏联政府将要帮助蒙古进行三年建设。他说，蒙古从1958年到1960年的建设计划规定，牲畜头数将增加5.6％，播种面积将增加两点三倍，工业建设将增加40％。三年计划的投资总额将比第二个五年计划多两倍。
    泽登巴尔在谈到国际局势的时候说，世界人民展开巩固和平、反对扩军备战的运动，社会主义阵营始终奉行着和平政策，蒙古人民决定和世界人民一道，进行保卫和平的斗争。
    泽登巴尔代表部长会议在会上提出辞职，本届部长会议的任期已经届满。大会决定由泽登巴尔提出下届部长会议的名单，在新的部长会议未产生以前，本届部长会议继续执行职务。
    新华社乌兰巴托8日电　蒙古第三届大人民呼拉尔第一次会议在7月8日下午闭幕。
    会议选举了桑布连任大人民呼拉尔主席团主席，沙拉布为秘书长，达姆巴等五人为主席团委员。
    会议还通过了部长会议的成员名单。泽登巴尔连任部长会议主席；第一副主席是曾德、副主席是莫洛姆扎木茨、热格瓦拉尔、阿维尔齐德和迈达尔。会议通过任命乔依扎木茨为国家总检察长。
    苏联最高苏维埃代表团应邀出席了会议。代表团团长霍赫洛夫在会上讲话。他代表苏联最高苏维埃祝贺大会成功，祝贺蒙古人民在社会主义建设中获得更大的成就，并且向大会赠送了礼品。
