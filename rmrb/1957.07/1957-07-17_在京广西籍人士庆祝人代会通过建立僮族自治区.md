Title: Guangxi Natives in Beijing Celebrate the People's Congress Passing the Establishment of Zhuang Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1957-07-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Guangxi natives from all walks of life in Beijing held a joint celebratory soiree at the Peace Hotel today to celebrate the First National People's Congress's Fourth Session passing the resolution to establish the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Mo Naiqun, Vice Governor of Guangxi Province, and Qin Yingji, Vice Governor of Guangxi Province and Governor of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture, all participated in the soiree. Ulanhu, on behalf of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, extended congratulations to the people of all ethnic groups in Guangxi. There were also cultural performances during the soiree.

### 在京广西籍人士庆祝人代会通过建立僮族自治区

1957-07-17
第2版()
专栏：

    在京广西籍人士庆祝人代会通过建立僮族自治区
    新华社16日讯　在京的广西籍各界人士今天在和平宾馆举行联欢晚会，庆祝第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议通过建立广西僮族自治区的决议。全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、国务院副总理兼民族事务委员会主任乌兰夫、广西省副省长莫乃群、副省长兼桂西僮族自治州州长覃应机等都参加了这次晚会。乌兰夫代表民族事务委员会向全广西各族人民表示祝贺。晚会中并有文娱节目的表演。
