Title: Most US Newspaper Editors Advocate Lifting Ban on Journalist Visits to China
Author:
Date: 1957-07-13
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, San Francisco, July 12th: According to the "San Francisco News," among the approximately 250 editors attending the American Society of Newspaper Editors annual meeting in San Francisco, over 92% advocated that the State Department should lift the ban preventing American journalists from visiting China.

### 美国大多数报纸编辑  主张取消记者访华禁令

1957-07-13
第7版()
专栏：

    美国大多数报纸编辑
    主张取消记者访华禁令
    据新华社12日讯　旧金山消息：据“旧金山新闻”统计，在旧金山参加美国报纸编辑协会年会的约二百五十名编辑中，有92％以上主张国务院应当取消不许美国记者访问中国的禁令。
