Title: Double-crop Early Rice Grows Well in Anhui Province
Author: 
Date: 1957-07-07
Page: 5

The 4.4 million mu of double-crop early rice planted in Anhui Province this year is generally growing well. If there are no unexpected disasters, a bumper harvest is entirely possible. This is a picture of members of the Fozi Ridge Agricultural Cooperative in Huoshan County weeding in the rice paddies.
