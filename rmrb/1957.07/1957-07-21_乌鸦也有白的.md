Title: Even Crows Can Be White

Author: 
Date: 1957-07-21
Page: 6

Anecdotes

    Even Crows Can Be White
    In Toyosato Village, Inukami County, Shiga Prefecture, Japan, a beekeeper found a white baby crow in a crow's nest on April 10th this year and has raised it. All of its feathers, as well as its beak and feet, are snow-white.

### 乌鸦也有白的

1957-07-21
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    乌鸦也有白的
    在日本滋贺县犬上郡豊乡村，有个专门养蜜蜂的人，今年4月10日在乌鸦窠里捉到一只白色的小乌鸦，目前已喂大了。它全身羽毛以至嘴和脚都是雪白的。
