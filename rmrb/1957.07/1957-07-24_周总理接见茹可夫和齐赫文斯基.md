Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Zhukov and Chikhvinsky
Author:
Date: 1957-07-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd. Premier Zhou Enlai received Zhukov, Chairman of the Soviet State Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, and Chikhvinsky, Director of the Committee's Department of Asian Socialist Countries, on the morning of the 23rd.

Present at the reception were Qian Junrui, Gong Peng, Chen Zhongjing, and Lin Lang. The Soviet Embassy in China's Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Abrasimov, was also present. A luncheon was held after the meeting.

### 周总理接见茹可夫和齐赫文斯基

1957-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见茹可夫和齐赫文斯基
    新华社23日讯　周恩来总理在23日上午接见了苏联国家对外文化委员会主席茹可夫和委员会的亚洲社会主义国家部主任齐赫文斯基。
    接见时在座的有钱俊瑞、龚澎、陈忠经、林朗。苏联驻华使馆临时代办阿布拉西莫夫也在座。接见后并共进午餐。
