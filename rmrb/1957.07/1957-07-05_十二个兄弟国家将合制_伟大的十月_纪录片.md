Title: Twelve Brother Countries to Co-Produce Documentary “The Great October”
Author: 
Date: 1957-07-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 4th: Documentary filmmakers from the Soviet Union and eleven other socialist countries will jointly produce a large-scale color documentary titled "The Great October" to commemorate the 40th anniversary of the Great October Revolution. The film will depict the lives of all socialist countries today. The film is expected to be completed in October this year.

### 十二个兄弟国家将合制  “伟大的十月”纪录片

1957-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

    十二个兄弟国家将合制
    “伟大的十月”纪录片
    新华社莫斯科4日电　苏联和十一个社会主义国家的纪录电影工作者将共同摄制一个以“伟大的十月”为题的大型彩色纪录片，以纪念伟大十月革命四十周年。影片将描写所有社会主义国家今天的生活。预计这部影片将在今年十月摄制完成。
