Title: Premier Zhou Receives Delegation from Burmese Cooperative

Author: 
Date: 1957-07-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire Burmese Cooperative Study Delegation headed by U Ni Ni this afternoon at Huairen Hall.

Present at the reception were Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture; Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister; and Wu Ba Maung, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy in China.

### 周总理接见缅甸合作社代表团

1957-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见缅甸合作社代表团
    新华社8日讯　周恩来总理今天下午在怀仁堂接见了以吴尼尼为首的缅甸合作社考察代表团的全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有农业部部长廖鲁言、副部长刘瑞龙，以及缅甸驻中国大使馆临时代办吴巴茂。
