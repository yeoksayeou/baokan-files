Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-07-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 14th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and his entourage arrived in Moscow at 6:00 PM on the 13th aboard a Tu-104 jet airliner. Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Belyayev, Brezhnev, Kuusinen, Shvernik, Pospelov, Kosygin, and others, as well as personnel from various countries' diplomatic missions in the Soviet Union and representatives of the masses, totaling several hundred people.

After disembarking from the plane, Chairman Ho Chi Minh shook hands with each of the welcomers, accepted flowers presented by Young Pioneer members, and waved to the welcoming masses.

He will rest here for a short period before visiting Eastern European People's Democratic countries.

### 胡志明主席飞抵莫斯科

1957-07-15
第3版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席飞抵莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科14日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明和随行人员13日下午六时乘“图—一○四”式喷气专机飞抵莫斯科。前往机场欢迎的有别利亚耶夫、勃列日涅夫、库西宁、什维尔尼克、波斯伯洛夫、柯西金等人以及一些国家驻苏外交使团人员和群众代表共数百人。
    胡志明主席下机后和欢迎人员一一握手，接受了少年先锋队队员的献花，并且向欢迎的群众招手致意。
    他在此地休息一个短的时期后将到东欧人民民主国家访问。
