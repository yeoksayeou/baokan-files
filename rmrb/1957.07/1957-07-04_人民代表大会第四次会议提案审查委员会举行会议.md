Title: Proposal Review Committee of the National People's Congress Held Fourth Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1957-07-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd. The Proposal Review Committee of the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress held its first meeting on the morning of July 3rd.
    The meeting discussed and approved the work plan of the Proposal Review Committee, and approved the group list of the Proposal Review Committee.
    The meeting was presided over by Li Xuefeng, Chairman of the Committee. At the meeting, Li Zhuchen and Guan Wenwei were elected as Deputy Chairmen of the Proposal Review Committee.

### 人民代表大会第四次会议提案审查委员会举行会议

1957-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    人民代表大会第四次会议提案审查委员会举行会议
    新华社3日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议提案审查委员会3日上午举行了第一次会议。
    会议讨论并通过了提案审查委员会工作方案，通过了提案审查委员会分组名单。
    会议由李雪峰主任委员主持。会上推选了李烛尘、管文蔚二人为提案审查委员会副主任委员。
