Title: Disarmament Subcommittee Continues Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-07-12
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, London, July 11th: The United Nations Disarmament Subcommittee continued its meeting on July 10th. At the meeting, Soviet representative Zorin requested that Western countries express their opinions on the proposals previously put forward by the Soviet Union, and also requested that they continue to clarify some of their viewpoints.

### 裁军小组会继续开会

1957-07-12
第7版()
专栏：

    裁军小组会继续开会
    据新华社11日讯　伦敦消息：联合国裁军小组委员会7月10日继续开会。苏联代表佐林在会上要求西方国家对苏联前些时候提出的建议表明意见，并且要求它们继续澄清他们的一些观点。
