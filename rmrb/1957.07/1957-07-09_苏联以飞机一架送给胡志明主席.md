Title: The Soviet Union Presents Chairman Ho Chi Minh with an Airplane
Author:
Date: 1957-07-09
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: Soviet Embassy Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Abrasimov, on behalf of the Soviet government, presented Chairman Ho Chi Minh with an Ilyushin Il-14 aircraft at the Beijing airport this afternoon. Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Nguyen Khang, accepted the aircraft on behalf of Chairman Ho Chi Minh.

A ceremony was held at the Beijing West Suburb Airport this afternoon. Abrasimov and Nguyen Khang both delivered speeches, congratulating the friendly unity of the Soviet and Vietnamese people. The plane also flew around the airport once.

Last year, the Soviet government also presented Chairman Ho Chi Minh with an airplane in Beijing.

### 苏联以飞机一架送给胡志明主席

1957-07-09
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联以飞机一架送给胡志明主席
    新华社8日讯　苏联大使馆临时代办阿布拉西莫夫今天下午在北京机场代表苏联政府向胡志明主席赠送了一架伊尔—十四型飞机，越南驻中国大使阮康代表胡志明主席接受了这架飞机。
    今天下午在北京西郊机场举行了仪式，阿布拉西莫夫和阮康都讲了话，祝贺苏越两国人民的友好团结。飞机还在机场上空飞行了一周。
    去年，苏联政府也曾在北京赠送给胡志明主席一架飞机。
