Title: Nasser Expresses Willingness to Discuss Canal Management with Canal Users
Author:
Date: 1957-07-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, 16th - Egyptian President Nasser told a "Extra-Bladet" reporter stationed in Cairo that Egypt is willing to negotiate with Suez Canal users on canal management issues, but opposes foreign interference in Egyptian affairs.

Nasser's remarks were published in yesterday's Copenhagen newspapers.

Nasser said: "We are willing to negotiate, but we do not agree to foreign interference in our affairs. We do not recognize the Suez Canal Users' Association, but we are willing to negotiate with canal users."

When talking about the arbitration issue, Nasser said: "We have stated that Egypt is willing to submit any issues related to the Suez Canal to the International Court of Justice in The Hague, but the other party must also guarantee to abide by the decisions of this court before Egypt accepts these decisions."

When talking about Eisenhower's comments that the Soviet Union's sale of submarines to Egypt created an unhealthy situation in the Mediterranean, Nasser said: "Our policy is only to purchase weapons for self-defense, to deal with possible aggressors."

Nasser said that the United States had agreed to supply Egypt with weapons in 1953, but on the condition that an American military delegation be stationed in Egypt. Egypt refused.

When a reporter asked him if Egypt wanted to improve relations with the United States, Nasser said: "We are willing to do anything except give up our independence."

### 纳赛尔表示愿同运河使用者  讨论运河管理问题

1957-07-17
第7版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔表示愿同运河使用者
    讨论运河管理问题
    新华社16日讯　哥本哈根消息：埃及总统纳赛尔对丹麦“号外报”驻开罗记者说，埃及愿意同苏伊士运河使用者就运河管理问题进行商谈，但是反对外国干涉埃及事务。
    纳赛尔的谈话刊登在昨天丹麦首都哥本哈根的报纸上。
    纳赛尔说：“我们愿意进行商谈，但是不同意外国干涉我国的事务。我们不承认苏伊士运河使用国协会，但是我们愿意同运河使用者商谈。”
    纳赛尔在谈到仲裁问题时说：“我们已经声明过，埃及愿意把任何同苏伊士运河有关的问题提交海牙国际法院，但是对方也必须保证遵守这个法院的决定，然后埃及才接受这些决定。”
    在谈到艾森豪威尔所谓苏联把潜水艇卖给埃及在地中海造成了不健康情势的谈话时，纳赛尔说：“我们的政策仅仅是购买武器进行自卫，对付可能的侵略者。”
    纳赛尔说，美国在1953年曾经答应对埃及供给武器，但是条件是在埃及派驻一个美国军事代表团。埃及拒绝了。
    当记者问他埃及是否想改善同美国的关系时，纳赛尔说：“我们愿意做除了放弃我们的独立以外的任何事情。”
