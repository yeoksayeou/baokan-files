Title: Three Silica Sand Mines Discovered in the Horqin Grassland of Inner Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1957-07-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tongliao, 12th. Recently, three more silica sand mines with very rich reserves have been discovered in the Horqin Grassland of Inner Mongolia, providing reliable resources for a large increase in silica sand production this year.

Silica sand has a wide range of uses and is an indispensable material for glass, ceramics, metallurgical casting, and the chemical industry.

### 内蒙古科尔沁草原发现三处矽砂矿

1957-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

    内蒙古科尔沁草原发现三处矽砂矿
    据新华社通辽12日电　内蒙古科尔沁草原上最近又发现三处蕴藏量很丰富的矽砂矿，为今年大量增产矽砂提供了可靠资源。
    矽砂用途很广，它是玻璃、陶瓷、冶金铸造和化学工业不可缺少的材料。
