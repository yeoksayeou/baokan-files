Title: DKP and CPCz Leaders Meet, Decide to Strengthen Party Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-07-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 4th. According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency on the 3rd, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the German Communist Party, Max Reimann, recently visited Czechoslovakia at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia.
  During the visit, he met with the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, Antonín Novotný, and exchanged views on some of the most important issues concerning the further opposition to the danger of German militarism. Also participating in the talks was the Secretary of the CPCz Central Committee, Jiří Hendrych.
    During the talks, the representatives of the two parties also exchanged information on the internal and domestic political issues of their respective parties. On all the issues discussed, both sides expressed completely consistent views and expressed their unwavering will to strengthen the mutual relations between the two parties for the solidarity of the international revolutionary working class movement and for the consolidation of world peace.

### 德共与捷共领袖会谈  决定加强两党关系

1957-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

    德共与捷共领袖会谈
    决定加强两党关系
    新华社布拉格4日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社3日报道，德国共产党中央委员会第一书记雷曼应捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会邀请，最近访问了捷克斯洛伐克。
  在访问期间，他会见了捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记诺沃提尼，就进一步反对德国军国主义危险的一些最重要问题交换了意见。参加会谈的还有捷共中央书记亨德里赫。
    在会谈中，两党代表还相互交换了关于党内和国内政治问题的情况。在所有讨论的问题中，双方表示了完全一致的看法，并且表示了要为国际革命工人阶级运动的团结、为巩固世界和平而加强两党之间相互关系的不可动摇的意志。
