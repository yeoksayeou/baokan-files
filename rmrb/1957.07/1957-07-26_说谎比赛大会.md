Title: Lie Telling Competition

Author:

Date: 1957-07-26
Page: 6

    Lie Telling Competition
    The nationwide lie telling competition in Abbottabad, northwestern Pakistan, began on July 12. The competition is judged by five judges, lawyers, engineers, doctors, and journalists, and will continue until the end of this month. During this period, the judges will evaluate all the lies presented, and the winner of the competition will be awarded a prize.
    The Pakistan National Lie Telling Competition is held once a year.

### 说谎比赛大会

1957-07-26
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    说谎比赛大会
    7月12日起，巴基斯坦西北部的阿堡特巴德开始举行了全国说谎比赛大会。大会由五位法官、律师、工程师、医生、新闻记者担任裁判，会期将继续到本月底止。在此期间，裁判员将对所有提出的谎言进行评定，比赛优胜者将给予奖金。
    巴基斯坦全国说谎比赛大会每年举行一次。
