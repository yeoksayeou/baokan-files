Title: Soviet Party and Government Delegation Departs for Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1957-07-09
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th (TASS) - A Soviet Communist Party and government delegation departed from Moscow today for Prague.

The delegation consists of the following personnel: First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev; Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin; First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Patolichev; Secretary of the Central Committee of the Ukrainian Communist Party, Ivashchenko; Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, V. Grishin; and Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, I. Grishin.

### 苏联党政代表团启程访捷

1957-07-09
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联党政代表团启程访捷
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社莫斯科8日讯：苏联共产党和政府代表团今天从莫斯科启程前往布拉格。
    代表团由下列人员组成：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫、苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、苏联外交部第一副部长帕托利切夫、乌克兰共产党中央委员会书记伊瓦申科、全苏工会中央理事会主席维·格里申和苏联驻捷克斯洛伐克大使伊·格里申。
