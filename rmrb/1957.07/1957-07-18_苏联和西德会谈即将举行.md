Title: Soviet-West German Talks Soon to Be Held
Author:
Date: 1957-07-18
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 17th: TASS reported that the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs notified the Embassy of the Federal Republic of Germany in Moscow on July 16th, expressing agreement that delegations from the two governments would begin negotiations on July 22nd regarding the development of relations between the two countries. The notification pointed out that the agreement to hold negotiations was reached through an exchange of letters between the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, and the Chancellor of the Federal Republic of Germany, Adenauer, and through an exchange of notes between the two governments.

### 苏联和西德会谈即将举行

1957-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和西德会谈即将举行
    新华社17日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部7月16日通知德意志联邦共和国驻莫斯科大使馆，表示同意两国政府代表团在7月22日开始就发展两国关系问题进行谈判。通知指出，举行谈判的协议是通过苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和德意志联邦共和国总理阿登纳互换信件和两国政府互换照会方式达成的。
