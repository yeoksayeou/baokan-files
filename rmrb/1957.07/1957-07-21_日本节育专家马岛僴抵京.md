Title: Japanese Family Planning Expert Masujima Kan Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Japanese family planning expert, Masujima Kan, Secretary-General of the National Council for Promoting the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations, arrived in Beijing today by plane from Moscow.

### 日本节育专家马岛僴抵京

1957-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    日本节育专家马岛僴抵京
    新华社20日讯　日本节育专家、日本促进日中日苏邦交恢复国民会议事务局长马岛僴，今天从莫斯科乘气机到达北京。
