Title: President Pieck on Vacation, Dieckmann Acts as Deputy

Author:
Date: 1957-07-11
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 10th: “Neues Deutschland” reported today that President Pieck is now beginning his vacation near Berlin. During his vacation, his presidential duties will be performed by the Chairman of the People's Chamber, Dieckmann.

### 皮克总统度假  狄克曼代理职务

1957-07-11
第7版()
专栏：

    皮克总统度假
    狄克曼代理职务
    新华社柏林10日电　“新德意志报”今天报道：皮克总统现在开始在柏林附近度假期。在他度假期间，总统职务由人民议院主席狄克曼代理。
