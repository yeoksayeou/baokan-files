Title: Londoners Suffer in Scorching Heat; Eggs Fried on Stadium Stands
Author:
Date: 1957-07-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London, 30th: Yesterday was London's hottest day in ten years, with temperatures reaching 95 degrees Fahrenheit (35 degrees Celsius).

At an international tennis match held in the London suburbs, two hundred spectators fainted from heatstroke on the stands. Two spectators even fried eggs on the cement surface baked by the scorching sun.

Tens of thousands of Londoners tried to escape the city's heat, flocking to the seaside, causing traffic jams on the roads. Many people fainted from heatstroke in cars crawling along like snails.

### 酷暑热煞伦敦人  球场看台煎鸡蛋

1957-07-01
第3版()
专栏：

    酷暑热煞伦敦人
    球场看台煎鸡蛋
    新华社30日讯　伦敦消息，昨天是伦敦十年来最热的一天，温度达到华氏九十五度（摄氏三十五度）。
    在伦敦郊区举行的国际网球比赛的看台上，有二百个观众当场中暑昏倒。有两个观众居然在烈日炙晒下的水门汀上煎起鸡蛋来了。
    成千成万的伦敦人想逃避城市中的炎热，纷纷涌往海边，使得公路交通堵塞。在像蜗牛似地爬行的汽车中，许多人中暑昏倒。
