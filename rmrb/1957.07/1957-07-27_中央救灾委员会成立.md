Title: Central Disaster Relief Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1957-07-27

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The State Council meeting approved the list of the Central Disaster Relief Committee as follows:

    Deputy Directors: Xi Zhongxun, Xie Juezai
    Members: Fu Zuoyi, Li Dequan
    Liao Luyan, Niu Peicong, Wang Shoudao
    Yuan Renyuan, Rong Zihe, Chen Guodong
    Zhang Qilong
