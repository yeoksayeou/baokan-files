Title: Japanese Ex-Servicemen Delegation Visits Changchun First Automobile Factory
Author:
Date: 1957-07-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, July 14th: The Japanese ex-servicemen delegation visited the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant and the Changchun Film Studio in Changchun yesterday, and left for Shenyang that night.

    The guests carefully toured various machine equipment and worker welfare facilities in the casting and assembly workshops of the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant. The guests believed that this factory was a technologically advanced, large-scale, world-class automobile manufacturing plant. The guests also visited the film production situation at the Changchun Film Studio and appreciated the film "Shangganling."

    The Japanese ex-servicemen delegation arrived in Changchun on the 12th.

### 日本前军人访华团参观长春第一汽车厂

1957-07-15
第3版()
专栏：

    日本前军人访华团
    参观长春第一汽车厂
    据新华社长春14日电　日本前军人访华团昨天在长春参观了第一汽车制造厂和长春电影制片厂后，当夜离开长春去沈阳。
    客人们在第一汽车制造厂的铸工、装配等车间中，仔细参观了各种机器设备和工人福利设施。客人们认为这个工厂是一个技术先进、规模宏大、世界上第一流的汽车制造厂。客人们还在长春电影制片厂参观了影片摄制情形，并且欣赏了影片“上甘岭”。
    日本前军人访华团是12日来到长春的。
