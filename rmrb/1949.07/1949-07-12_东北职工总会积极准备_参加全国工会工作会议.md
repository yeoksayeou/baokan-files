Title: Northeast Federation of Trade Unions Actively Prepares to Participate in the National Trade Union Work Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-07-12
Page: 2

　　Northeast Federation of Trade Unions Actively Prepares
  　To Participate in the National Trade Union Work Conference
    [Reported by this newspaper] The Northeast Federation of Trade Unions is actively preparing to participate in the National Trade Union Work Conference. On June 20th, the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions issued a notice to the trade union federations of various provinces and industrial trade unions in the Northeast, calling on local trade union federations to "regard the preparation for this conference as the main and central task at present." It was also decided to convene the first Northeast Trade Union Work Conference in Shenyang on July 11th to summarize the experience of the Northeast workers' movement and elect representatives to attend the National Trade Union Work Conference.

### 东北职工总会积极准备　参加全国工会工作会议

1949-07-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北职工总会积极准备
  　参加全国工会工作会议
    【本报讯】东北职工总会积极准备参加全国工会工作会议。六月二十日，东北职工总会曾向东北各省总工会与产业总工会发出通知，号召各地职工总会“把准备此次会议作为目前主要和中心的工作”。并决定七月十一日在沈阳召开第一次东北工会工作会议，以总结东北职工运动的经验，并选出出席全国工会工作会议的代表。
