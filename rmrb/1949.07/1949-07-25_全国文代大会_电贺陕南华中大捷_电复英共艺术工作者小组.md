Title: National Literary and Art Congress Sends Telegrams Congratulating on Great Victories in Southern Shaanxi and Central China; Replies to British Communist Party Artists' Group
Author:
Date: 1949-07-25
Page: 2

　　National Literary and Art Congress Sends Telegrams Congratulating on Great Victories in Southern Shaanxi and Central China; Replies to British Communist Party Artists' Group
    To all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army on the Southern Shaanxi and Central China Front:
    As the congress victoriously closes, we are delighted to hear of the great victories on the front lines. We send this telegram to express our congratulations.
    　　National Congress of Literary and Art Workers of China
    　　July 22nd
    To Poulden, Chairman, and Niven, Vice-Chairman, of the British Communist Party Artists' Group:
    Your congratulations have given us great encouragement. In the future, we are willing to unite consistently under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Mao Zedong, learn from the excellent literary and artistic traditions of the Chinese people and the people of the world, and strive to complete the literary and artistic construction of the new China!
    National Congress of Literary and Art Workers of China
    July 22nd

### 全国文代大会　电贺陕南华中大捷　电复英共艺术工作者小组
中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会
1949-07-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国文代大会　电贺陕南华中大捷　电复英共艺术工作者小组
    陕南暨华中前线人民解放军全体指战员：
    正当大会胜利闭幕之际，欣闻前线大捷，特电祝贺。
    　　中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会
    　　七月二十二日
    英国共产党艺术工作者小组主席普尔敦，副主席尼文：
    你们的祝贺给了我们很大的鼓励。今后我们愿意一致团结在中国共产党和毛泽东的领导下，学习中国人民和世界人民的优良的文学艺术传统，为完成新中国的文学艺术建设事业而奋斗！
    中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会
    七月二十二日
