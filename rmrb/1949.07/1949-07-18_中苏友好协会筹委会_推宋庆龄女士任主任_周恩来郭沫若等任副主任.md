Title: Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Appoints Madame Soong Ching Ling as Director; Zhou Enlai, Guo Moruo, et al. as Deputy Directors
Author:
Date: 1949-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th -- The Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has recently appointed Madame Sun Yat-sen, Soong Ching Ling, as Director, and Zhou Enlai, Guo Moruo, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Zhang Lan, Huang Yanpei, etc., as Deputy Directors. The executive committee of the association decided to appoint Qian Junrui as the Secretary-General, and Xiao San, Huan Xiang, Shen Zhiyuan, and Lai Yali as Deputy Secretaries-General.

### 中苏友好协会筹委会  推宋庆龄女士任主任  周恩来郭沫若等任副主任

1949-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会筹委会
    推宋庆龄女士任主任
    周恩来郭沫若等任副主任
    【新华社北平十七日电】中苏友好协会筹备委员会顷推定孙夫人宋庆龄女士担任主任，周恩来、郭沫若、李济深、沈钧儒、张澜、黄炎培等担任副主任。该会干事会决定以钱俊瑞为总干事，萧三、宦乡、沈志远、赖亚力为副总干事。
