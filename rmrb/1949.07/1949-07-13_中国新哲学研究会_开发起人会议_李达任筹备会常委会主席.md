Title: China New Philosophy Research Association Initiating Person Conference, Li Da Appointed Chairman of Preparatory Committee Standing Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-07-13
Page: 1

[Ping City News] The China New Philosophy Research Association, initiated by philosophy workers from all over the country (list of initiators attached), held an initiating person conference in Beiping on the 8th of this month. The meeting discussed the draft of the preparatory committee's organizational rules and provisional regulations, and elected Li Da, Ai Siqi, He Sijing, Jin Yuelin, Zhang Dongsun, Tang Yongtong, Zheng Xin, He Ganzhi, Ma Te, Hu Sheng, and Xia Kangnong as 11 standing committee members of the preparatory committee, forming the standing committee of the preparatory committee, and mutually elected Li Da as chairman, Ai Siqi and Zheng Xin as vice-chairmen, and Hu Sheng and Ma Te as secretaries, responsible for carrying out all preparatory work. The meeting unanimously recognized that the significance of organizing the China New Philosophy Research Association is to unite philosophy workers across the country and disseminate Marxism-Leninism philosophy and Mao Zedong Thought, with a view to correctly understanding the laws of development of China's new democratic society, and to critically absorb the old philosophical heritage, and launch a criticism of various erroneous ideologies on the cultural and ideological front.

The list of initiators of the China New Philosophy Research Association is as follows (in order of number of strokes in the surname):

Yu Guangyuan, Wang Ziye, Shi Zhaotang, Ai Siqi, Li Da, Shen Zhiyuan, Wu Liping, Du Shousu, Du Renzhi, He Feng, He Sijing, He Ganzhi, Zhou Yang, Jin Yuelin, Hu Sheng, Hou Wailu, Xia Kangnong, Xu Teli, Ma Te, Chen Shaoyu, Chen Boda, Chen Weishi, Xu Deheng, Zhang Zhirang, Zhang Ruxin, Zhang Tianlin, Zhang Dongsun, Zhang Tiesheng, Cao Baohua, Fu Tong, Peng Kang, Tang Yongtong, Feng Wenqian, Chu Tunan, Yang Xianzhen, Zhao Pingsheng, Cai Yi, Pan Zinian, Zheng Xin, Zheng Yili, Luo Keting.

### 中国新哲学研究会　开发起人会议　李达任筹备会常委会主席

1949-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国新哲学研究会
  　开发起人会议
  　李达任筹备会常委会主席
    【平市讯】由全国各地哲学工作者发起组织的中国新哲学研究会（发起人名单附后），本月八日于北平召开发起人会议。会上讨论了筹备会组织规程和暂行简章草案，并选出李达、艾思奇、何思敬、金岳霖、张东荪、汤用彤、郑昕、何干之、马特、胡绳、夏康农等十一人为筹备会常务委员，组成筹备会常务委员会，并互推李达为主席，艾思奇、郑昕为副主席，胡绳、马特为秘书，负责进行一切筹备工作。会议一致认定：组织中国新哲学研究会的意义，是团结全国哲学工作者和传播马列主义哲学及毛泽东思想，以期正确认识中国新民主主义社会发展的规律，并批判吸收旧哲学遗产，在文化思想战线上展开对于各种错误思想意识的批判。
