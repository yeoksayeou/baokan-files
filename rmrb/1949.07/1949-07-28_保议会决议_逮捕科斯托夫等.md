Title: Bulgarian Parliament Resolution: Arrest of Kostov and Others

Author:

Date: 1949-07-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 27th: TASS, Sofia: The Bulgarian National Assembly special session passed the resolution of the Presidium of the National Assembly: (1) Authorize the Prosecutor General to arrest former Deputy Prime Minister Kostov and initiate prosecution of his crimes. (2) Authorize the Prosecutor General to arrest and interrogate former National Assembly member and Bulgarian National Agrarian Union member Helchev, because he engaged in espionage for a certain foreign country.

### 保议会决议　逮捕科斯托夫等

1949-07-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　保议会决议
  　逮捕科斯托夫等
    【新华社北平二十七日电】塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚国民议会特别会议通过了国民议会主席团的决议：（一）授权检察长逮捕前副总理科斯托夫，并着手对他的犯罪提出起诉。（二）授权检察长逮捕并审讯前国民议会议员、保加利亚全国农民联盟盟员海尔切夫，因为他为某外国作间谍活动。
