Title: Victory in Suppressing Bandits by Local Armed Forces in the Daye Sub-district, Outskirts of Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1949-07-04
Page: 3

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 2nd] The people's local armed forces in the Daye sub-district of Hubei Province, on the outskirts of Wuhan, have achieved remarkable results in coordinating with the military in suppressing bandits and vigorously launching political offensives. In the past month or so, local Kuomintang bandit forces in Yangxin, Wuchang, Echeng, Xianning, Chongyang, Tongshan, Tongcheng and other counties in the area have surrendered their weapons one after another, including the puppet county governments of Chongyang, Tongshan, and Tongcheng counties, as well as the Tongcheng puppet Security Regiment, the Yangxin County puppet Self-Defense Headquarters, and the puppet Pacification Regiment, among others. According to incomplete statistics, a total of one mortar, forty light and heavy machine guns, and more than three thousand long and short guns were seized.

### 武汉外围大冶分区  地方武装剿匪胜利

1949-07-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　武汉外围大冶分区  地方武装剿匪胜利
    【新华社汉口二日电】武汉市外围的湖北省大冶分区人民地方武装，配合军事剿匪，大力开展政治攻势，已获得显著成绩。月余来该区阳新、武昌、鄂城、咸宁、崇阳、通山、通城等县国民党地方匪军纷纷缴械投降，其中包括崇阳、通山、通城三县伪县政府及通城伪保安团、阳新县伪自卫总队、伪绥靖团等部。据不完全统计，共缴获迫击炮一门，轻重机枪四十挺，长短枪三千余支。
