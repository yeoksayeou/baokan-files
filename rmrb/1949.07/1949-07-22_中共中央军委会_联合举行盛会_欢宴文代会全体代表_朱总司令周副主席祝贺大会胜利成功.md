Title: CCP Central Military Commission Jointly Holds Grand Gathering to Fete All Delegates to the Literary and Art Congress; Commander-in-Chief Zhu and Vice Chairman Zhou Congratulate the Success of the Congress
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-07-22
Page: 1

　　CCP Central Military Commission
  　Jointly Holds Grand Gathering
  　To Fete All Delegates to the Literary and Art Congress
    Commander-in-Chief Zhu and Vice Chairman Zhou Congratulate the Success of the Congress
    [Reported by This Newspaper] After the successful conclusion of the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers, yesterday (21st) at 5:00 PM, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission jointly hosted a banquet at the Beijing Hotel to entertain all the delegates. At the banquet, comrades such as Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai, and Lu Dingyi congratulated the successful conclusion of the congress. All the delegates to the Literary and Art Congress stood up in unison and cheered, wishing Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Vice Chairman Zhou good health. During the meal, delegates such as Zhou Xiaoyan, Tang Rongmei, Zhang Lu, Li Shaochun, and Zhou Xinfang performed programs to add to the festive atmosphere.
    (Bai Sheng)

### 中共中央军委会　联合举行盛会　欢宴文代会全体代表  朱总司令周副主席祝贺大会胜利成功
柏生
1949-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央军委会
  　联合举行盛会
  　欢宴文代会全体代表
    朱总司令周副主席祝贺大会胜利成功
    【本报讯】全国文代大会胜利闭幕后，昨（二十一）日下午五时，中国共产党中央委员会、中国人民革命军事委员会联合在北京饭店设宴招待全体代表。席上，朱总司令、周恩来副主席、陆定一等同志祝贺大会的胜利成功。文代大会全体代表一致起立欢呼，敬祝毛主席、朱总司令、周副主席的健康。餐中并有周小燕、唐荣枚、张鲁、李少春、周信芳等代表表演节目助兴。
    （柏生）
