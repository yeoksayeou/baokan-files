Title: Employee Salary Rice Equivalent Priced at Sixty Yuan
Author:
Date: 1949-07-15
Page: 2

　　Employee Salary Rice Equivalent Priced at Sixty Yuan
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] The municipal Federation of Trade Unions and other relevant departments have assessed the rice equivalent price for employees' salaries in the first half of July in Ping City, and it will be issued at sixty yuan per jin.

### 职工薪资米折价六十元

1949-07-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　职工薪资米折价六十元
    【本报讯】平市七月份上半月职工薪资小米折价，经市总工会等有关部门评定，按每斤六十元发给。
