Title: Youth Activity Briefs
Author:
Date: 1949-07-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, July 25th: The Youth Workers Department of the Shanghai Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League held a youth worker study class on the 16th. The study period is ten days, with more than 100 students participating. After the youth workers studied, they requested to join the League.

　　　×　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　×

More than 12,000 students from 54 secondary schools and above in Tianjin (accounting for more than 60% of the total number of secondary and above students) participated in summer activities. More than 700 students from universities and colleges participated, and classes have already started on the 14th. Nearly 10,000 students from various middle schools participated in tutoring classes or tutoring schools. Clerk tutoring classes have been established, and worker study classes are being planned. Recently, more than 50 students will be organized to go to 30 factories, such as Zhongfang, to teach literacy and organize recreational activities.

　　×　　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　×

Students from Wuhan Yude Girls' Middle School organized a women's visiting team during the summer vacation. Students from Hubei Agricultural College organized a rural visiting team to go to the countryside to understand the living conditions of farmers and carry out propaganda. Teachers, students, and staff members remaining at the college jointly organized a reclamation team to engage in productive labor.

During the summer vacation, students from middle schools in Wenzhou City, Zhejiang Province, received material assistance from the Cultural and Educational Department of the city's Military Control Commission and organized two tutoring classes to provide free education for unemployed children of workers, farmers, and the poor. More than 300 children are currently enrolled in the classes.

### 青年活动简报

1949-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　青年活动简报
    【新华社北平二十五日电】新民主主义青年团上海工作委员会青年工人部，十六日开办青工学习班。学习时间是十天，参加学员共一百多人。青年工人学习后，纷纷要求入团。
    　　　×　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　×
    天津市五十四所中等以上学校学生一万二千余人（占中等以上学生总数百分之六十以上），参加了暑期活动。大学学园参加的学生有七百多人，十四日已经开学。各中学同学近万人参加了补习班或补习学校的工作。店员补习班已成立，工人学习班正筹设中。最近还将组织五十多位同学到中纺等三十个工厂去教字和组织文娱活动。
    　　×　　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　×
    武汉育德女中学生暑期组织妇女访问队。湖北农学院的同学组织农村访问队下乡了解农民生活情况，并进行宣传。该院留校的师生工友则共同组织垦荒队，从事生产劳动。
    　　×　　　　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　　　　×
    浙江温州市中学学生利用暑期，得到该市军管会文教部的物质帮助举办了两个补习班，供工人农民和贫民的失学子女免费入学，现有儿童三百多名入班学习。
