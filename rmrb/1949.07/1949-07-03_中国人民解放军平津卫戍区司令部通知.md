Date: 1949-07-03

Notice from the Headquarters of the Pingjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army

The artillery unit of this Headquarters is scheduled to conduct live ammunition exercises in the western suburbs after 3:00 PM daily from July 3rd to 6th. This is to notify you.

July 3rd
