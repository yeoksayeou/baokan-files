Title: Mao Zedong's "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" To Be Studied By All Members Following French Communist Party Central Committee Notice; Organ Publications of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and American Communist Party Publish Full Text

Author: 
Date: 1949-07-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 29th: The Central Committee of the French Communist Party has notified all party members to study Chairman Mao's "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship." This notice was published on the front page of the July 22nd edition of *l'Humanité*. The notice stated: "The Central Committee hereby notifies all party branches to study Chairman Mao Zedong's 'On the People's Democratic Dictatorship' at their next meeting." This document by Chairman Mao was published in full in *l'Humanité* on the 21st.

　　The theoretical weekly of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, "*Creation*," published the full text of Chairman Mao's document on the 20th of this month. The American *Daily Worker* published the main points of the article in early July, and then published a detailed account on the 24th, accompanied by a photograph of Chairman Mao and photographs of a large number of American-made howitzers captured by the People's Liberation Army.

### 毛主席“论人民民主专政”法共中央通知全党学习  捷共美共机关刊物全文刊载

1949-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席“论人民民主专政”法共中央通知全党学习
    捷共美共机关刊物全文刊载
    【新华社布拉格二十九日电】法国共产党中央委员会通知全党学习毛主席“论人民民主专政”一文。这一通知刊登于二十二日人道报第一版上。该通知说：“中央委员会通知全党各支部于下一次集会上学习中共中央主席毛泽东论人民民主专政一文”。毛主席的这一文献，已于二十一日在人道报全文刊载。
    捷共中央理论周刊“创造”，本月二十日全文刊载毛主席的这一文献，美国工人日报七月初曾刊登该文要点，二十四日又详为刊载，并附毛主席照片及人民解放军缴获的大批美造榴弹炮的照片。
