Title: Military Control Commission of Beiping Issues Order to Cease Activities of the U.S. Information Service
Author:
Date: 1949-07-20
Page: 2

    Military Control Commission of Beiping Issues Order
    to Cease Activities of the U.S. Information Service
    [This newspaper's report] The Military Control Commission of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in Beiping Municipality yesterday (the 19th) ordered the U.S. Information Service in Beiping to cease its activities. The notification was delivered at 3:00 PM yesterday to the U.S. Information Service at 1 Dazhou Hutong, Nanchizi. The original text is as follows:
    "It has been investigated that the U.S. Information Service is a part of the U.S. State Department. Since the People's Government has no diplomatic or consular relations with the United States, the U.S. Information Service shall cease its activities from this date, including the printing and distribution of news releases, the exhibition and gifting of books and newspapers, the posting of pictures, the screening of movies, the convening of concerts, and all other external activities. It is hoped that you will comply without fail! Ye Jianying, Director of the Military Control Commission of Beiping Municipality, July 19, 1949."

### 平军管会发出命令  停止美新闻处活动

1949-07-20
第2版()
专栏：

    平军管会发出命令
    停止美新闻处活动
    【本报讯】中国人民解放军北平市军事管制委员会昨（十九日）已命令北平美国新闻处停止活动。该项通知于昨日下午三时送达南池子大苏州胡同一号美国新闻处，其原文如下：
    “兹查美国新闻处为美国国务院组织之一部分，人民政府既与美国无外交的及领事的关系，故美国新闻处应自即日起，停止活动，包括新闻稿之印刷及散发，书报之展览与赠送，图片之张贴，电影之放映，音乐会之召开，以及其他一切对外活动。望即遵照毋违！北平市军事管制委员会主任叶剑英一九四九年七月十九日。”
