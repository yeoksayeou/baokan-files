Title: US Industrial Production Declines for Seven Consecutive Months, Reaching Lowest Level in Three Years
Author:
Date: 1949-07-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, July 27th: TASS, New York, July 26th: The Federal Reserve Board reports that US industrial production in June experienced its seventh consecutive month of decline, dropping 13 percent since last November, reaching its lowest level since May 1946. The industrial production index for June was 169, compared to 174 in May and 195 last November. The production of light industry in June was approximately 10 percent lower than last November. The production of heavy industry decreased by 15 percent, and the production of mining decreased by approximately 16 percent.

### 美工业生产　连续下跌七个月　达三年以来最低水平

1949-07-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　美工业生产
  　连续下跌七个月
  　达三年以来最低水平
    【新华社北平二十七日电】塔斯社纽约二十六日讯：联邦准备局报告：六月份美国工业生产作第七个月的连续下降，比去年十一月下降了百分之十三，为一九四六年五月以来的最低水平。六月份的工业生产指数为一六九，五月份为一七四，去年十一月为一九五。轻工业六月份的生产比去年十一月约低百分之十。重工业的生产降低了百分之十五，矿业的生产降低了百分之十六左右。
