Title: Reply Telegram from the Congress to the Soviet Composers Association
Author:
Date: 1949-07-11
Page: 2

Telegram from the Congress in Reply to the Soviet Composers Association

Soviet Composers Association:
    We are very pleased and excited to receive your congratulatory telegram. We are determined, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, to strive to serve the people and learn from your work experience, create works with high ideological and artistic quality. We hope that writers from China and the Soviet Union will unite more closely and work together to fight for lasting world peace and people's democracy. We extend our brotherly greetings to you.
  　　　　　　　All-China Congress of Literary and Art Workers

### 大会电复苏联作曲家协会

1949-07-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　大会电复苏联作曲家协会苏联作曲家协会：
    接到你们的贺电，我们感到非常愉快和兴奋。我们决心在中国共产党领导之下，努力为人民服务，并学习你们的工作经验，创造有高度思想性和艺术性的作品，我们希望中苏两国的作家，更加团结，共同为争取全世界的持久和平与人民民主而奋斗。谨以兄弟的情谊向你们致敬。
  　　　　　　　中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会
