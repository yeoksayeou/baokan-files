Title: Philippine People's Anti-Japanese Army Commander Taruc Sternly Condemns the Pacific Union; Emphasizes Solidarity with the Chinese People to Destroy Imperialist Conspiracies
Author:
Date: 1949-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th -- Associated Press dispatch: The Manila Chronicle published a statement by Taruc, Commander of the Philippine People's Anti-Japanese Army, on the 25th, fiercely criticizing the proposed Pacific Union. Taruc declared: "The Pacific Union, as a supplement to the North Atlantic Treaty, is an imperialist delusion attempting to crush the Asian people's movement to save itself." Taruc emphasized that the Filipino people must consolidate their unity with the Chinese people who have driven out the Kuomintang butchers. Taruc's statement concluded: "The Pacific Union will be destroyed by the armed people."

### 菲民抗军总司令塔鲁克　严斥太平洋联盟  强调与中国人民团结摧毁帝国主义者阴谋

1949-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　菲民抗军总司令塔鲁克
  　严斥太平洋联盟
    强调与中国人民团结摧毁帝国主义者阴谋
    【新华社北平二十六日电】美联社讯：马尼拉纪事晚报二十五日发表菲民抗军总司令塔鲁克的声明，猛烈抨击拟议中的太平洋联盟。塔鲁克宣称：“作为北大西洋公约之补充的太平洋联盟，是帝国主义者企图粉碎亚洲人民运动以拯救它自己的妄想”。塔鲁克强调菲律宾人民必须与驱逐了国民党屠夫的中国人民巩固地团结。塔鲁克声明结语称：“太平洋联盟将被武装起来的人民所摧毁”。
