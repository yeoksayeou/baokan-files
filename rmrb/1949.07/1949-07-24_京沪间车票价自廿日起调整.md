Author:
Date: 1949-07-24
Page: 2

Ticket Prices Between Beijing and Shanghai Adjusted Starting from the 20th

[This newspaper report] With the opening of traffic between Beiping and Shanghai, the ticket price between Nanjing and Shanghai will be calculated at 20 yuan per person per kilometer starting from the 20th (originally 4 yuan per person per kilometer). The new ticket prices are listed as follows:

(1) Third-class express train ticket price from Beiping to Shanghai: 11,010 yuan
　　　Third-class express train ticket price from Beiping to Suzhou: 9,570 yuan
　　　Third-class express train ticket price from Beiping to Wuxi: 8,670 yuan
　　　Third-class express train ticket price from Beiping to Zhenjiang West Station: 6,490 yuan
　　　Third-class express train ticket price from Beiping to Nanjing: 5,000 yuan

(2) Third-class express train ticket price from Tianjin to Shanghai: 10,710 yuan
　　　Third-class express train ticket price from Tianjin to Suzhou: 9,270 yuan
　　　Third-class express train ticket price from Tianjin to Wuxi: 8,370 yuan
　　　Third-class express train ticket price from Tianjin to Zhenjiang West Station: 6,100 yuan
　　　Third-class express train ticket price from Tianjin to Nanjing: 4,700 yuan
