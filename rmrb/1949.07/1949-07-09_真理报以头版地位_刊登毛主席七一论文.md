Title: Pravda Publishes Chairman Mao's July 1st Article Prominently on Front Page
Author:
Date: 1949-07-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, July 7th: TASS, Moscow, 6th: Pravda published Chairman Mao's long essay "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," commemorating the 28th anniversary of the Chinese Communist Party, prominently on the front page.
