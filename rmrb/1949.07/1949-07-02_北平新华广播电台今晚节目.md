Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-02
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This newspaper dispatch] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast a speech program from 19:50 to 20:10 tonight, replaying the recording of Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army broadcasting to the entire country in commemoration of the 28th anniversary of the founding of the Party. During the youth time from 19:30 to 19:50, two commentaries from the 10th issue of "Chinese Youth" will be broadcast: the first is "The People's Festival"; the second is "Celebrating the Establishment of the New Political Consultative Conference Preparatory Committee". From 22:20 to 22:50, the literary and artistic program will feature a small-scale Yangko opera performed by the Second Cultural Troupe of the Political Department of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army: "Quickly Grind Victory Rice".

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，重播中国人民解放军朱德总司令为纪念党的二十八周年生日对全国广播录音。十九点三十分到十九点五十分青年时间，播送“中国青年”第十期的两篇评论：第一篇是“人民的节日”；第二篇是“庆祝新政协筹备会成立”。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由人民解放军第三野战军政治部第二文工团演播小型秧歌剧：“快碾胜利米”。
