Title: Czechoslovak Foreign Trade Breaks Record Last Month
Author:
Date: 1949-07-23
Page: 3

　　Czechoslovak Foreign Trade
  　Breaks Record Last Month
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd: Prague News: According to official statistics, Czechoslovakia's total foreign trade in June broke the predicted record, totaling 8,261,100,000 crowns (equivalent to 165,220,000 US dollars), including 4,331,100,000 crowns in imports and 3,930,000,000 crowns in exports. The total foreign trade of Czechoslovakia in the first six months of this year is 40,344,000,000 crowns (equivalent to 806,880,000 US dollars). The trade deficit in the first six months of last year was 4 billion crowns, while the current figure is only 226 million crowns.

### 捷克对外贸易　上月打破预定纪录

1949-07-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷克对外贸易
  　上月打破预定纪录
    【新华社北平二十二日电】布拉格讯：据官方统计，捷克斯洛伐克六月份对外贸易总额已打破预定的纪录，总数为八十二亿六千一百万克郎（等于一亿六千五百二十二万美元），包括进口四十三亿三千一百万克郎，出口三十九亿三千万克郎。捷克斯洛伐克本年度头六个月的对外贸易总额，计为四百另三亿四千四百万克郎（等于八亿零六百八十八万美元）。去年头六个月的入超四十亿克郎，而现在的数目仅为二亿二千六百万克郎。
