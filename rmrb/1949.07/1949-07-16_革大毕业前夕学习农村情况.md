Title: Learning About Rural Conditions Before Graduation from the Revolutionary University
Author: Liu Baibi
Date: 1949-07-16
Page: 2

(Ping City News) As the graduation date for students at the Revolutionary University draws near, and with the possibility that some will be assigned to work in rural areas, the Academic Affairs Office has arranged for all classes to use time outside of regular classes this week to familiarize themselves with rural situations and working methods. Class cadres and students will discuss the following points: 1. Rural organizational structure. 2. Land reform and production. 3. Rural culture, education, and hygiene. 4. Mass work concerning militia, youth, women, and children. 5. Farmers' customs and rural investigation and research.

### 革大毕业前夕学习农村情况
刘白笔
1949-07-16
第2版()
专栏：

    革大毕业前夕学习农村情况
    【平市讯】革大同学离毕业期已不远了，可能有一部分人到农村去工作，为了使同学们对农村的情况和工作方法多熟习一点起见，教务处特布置全校各班在本周内利用上正课以外的时间，由班干部与同学们讲下面几点问题：一、农村组织情况。二、土地改革与生产。三、农村文化、教育、卫生。四、关于民兵、青年、妇女、儿童等群众工作。五、农民的风俗习惯与农村的调查研究。
            （刘白笔）
