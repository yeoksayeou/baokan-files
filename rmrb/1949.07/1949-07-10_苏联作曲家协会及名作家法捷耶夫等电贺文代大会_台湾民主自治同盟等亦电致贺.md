Title: Soviet Composers Association, Renowned Writer Fadeyev, and Others Send Congratulatory Telegrams to the Literary and Art Congress; Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League and Others Also Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1949-07-10
Page: 1

[This Dispatch]

　　Soviet Composers Association, Renowned Writer Fadeyev, and Others Send Congratulatory Telegrams to the Literary and Art Congress
    Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League and Others Also Send Congratulatory Telegrams
    [Our Dispatch] The National Literary and Art Congress has recently received congratulatory telegrams from the Soviet Soviet Composers Association and the famous Soviet writer Fadeyev, among others. The original telegrams are as follows: To the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers of China:
    The Soviet composers warmly congratulate the Congress of Literary and Art Workers of Free China.
    We believe that literary and art workers will make valuable contributions to the construction of a free and democratic China.
    We sincerely hope that you will achieve new successes in your efforts to create works of high ideological content that serve the cause of peace and progress.
    　　　　　　　　　　Soviet Soviet Composers Association, July 7, 1949, To the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers of China: Dear Friends:
    Soviet writers warmly congratulate the Congress of Writers and Art Activists of Free China.
    You are meeting during the great days of victory for the Chinese people, a victory achieved under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and its leader, Mao Zedong.
    Literature and art, and its activists, as educators of the people, play a huge and responsible role.
    We sincerely wish you, dear friends and colleagues, complete success in the cause of uniting all Chinese democratic writers and art activists, and in creating literary and artistic works that are closely linked to the lives of the Chinese people and the struggle to build a new China.
    Fadeyev, Sholokhov, Simonov, Tikhonov, Vishnevsky, Surkov, Sofronov, Gorbatov, Panfilov, Leonov, Korniychuk, Koshevnikov.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 7, 1949.
    [Another Dispatch] The Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League and the All-China Democratic Youth Federation have successively sent congratulatory telegrams to the Literary and Art Congress. The Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, on behalf of the seven million people of Taiwan, sent its congratulations, stating: "We are confident that in the future, on the arduous road of New Democratic construction, you will also hold high Chairman Mao's banner and correctly, sincerely, and enthusiastically indicate to the construction fighters of the whole country the path they should take." The congratulatory telegram from the All-China Youth Federation stated: "In the future, in the work of New Democratic construction, we believe that under the banner of Chairman Mao, Chinese progressive literary and art workers will certainly be able to unite more closely and struggle on the cultural front to complete the great task of cultural construction."

### 苏联作曲家协会及名作家法捷耶夫等电贺文代大会  台湾民主自治同盟等亦电致贺

1949-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联作曲家协会及名作家法捷耶夫等电贺文代大会
    台湾民主自治同盟等亦电致贺
    【本报讯】全国文代大会顷接获苏联苏维埃作曲家协会及苏著名作家法捷耶夫等的贺电，原电如下：致中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会：
    苏维埃作曲家热烈的祝贺自由中国的文学艺术工作者的代表大会。
    我们相信，文学艺术工作者在自由民主中国的建设事业中将给予宝贵的贡献。
    我们衷心希望你们，在创造有高度思想性的，服务于和平与进步事业的作品的努力中获得新的成功。
    　　　　　　　　　　苏联苏维埃作曲家协会　一九四九、七、七、致中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会：亲爱的朋友们：
    苏维埃作家热烈的祝贺自由中国的作家和艺术活动者的代表大会。
    你们在中国人民伟大的胜利日子会合了，这个胜利是在中国共产党及其领袖毛泽东的领导下实现的。
    文学和艺术及其活动者，作为人民的教育者，所起的作用是巨大而负责的。
    我们衷心的预祝你们，亲爱的朋友和同事，在团结一切中国民主作家和艺术活动者的事业上以及在创造紧密联系中国人民的生活与建设新中国的斗争的文学艺术作品上，获得完全的成功。
    法捷耶夫、萧洛霍夫、西蒙诺夫、吉洪诺夫、维什涅夫斯基、苏尔可夫、索佛洛诺夫、戈尔巴托夫、潘菲洛夫、列昂诺夫、考尔纳楚克、考席夫尼可夫。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九、七、七、
    【又讯】台湾民主自治同盟与中华全国民主青年联合总会先后电贺文代大会，台湾民主自治同盟代表台湾七百万的人民致贺，并称：“我们确信今后你们亦将在新民主主义建设的艰巨路途上，高高举起毛主席的大旗，正确、诚恳、而且热烈地指示给全国的建设斗士们应走的路线。”全国青联的贺电表示：“今后在新民主主义的建设工作里，我们相信在毛主席旗帜之下，中国进步的文艺工作者一定更能紧紧团结，在文化战线上为完成巨大的文化建设任务而斗争。”
