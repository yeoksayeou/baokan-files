Title: National Railway Workers' Provisional Representative Conference: Li Lisan Reports on Trade Union Work
Author:
Date: 1949-07-06
Page: 1

　　National Railway Workers' Provisional Representative Conference
  　Li Lisan Reports on Trade Union Work
    [Ping City News] The National Railway Workers' Provisional Representative Conference took a half-day break on the morning of the 3rd. In the afternoon of the 3rd and on the 4th (July 4th), the conference continued. Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, attended to report on trade union work. Comrade Lisan first asked the representatives to raise questions, and then comprehensively answered the questions raised by the representatives regarding trade union organization and tasks, how to run trade unions well, factory management committees, labor insurance and trade union welfare undertakings, and the relationship between industrial trade unions and local trade unions in detail. In the afternoon, all representatives divided into groups to discuss Minister Teng Lu's report.

### 全国铁路职工临代会上　李立三报告工会工作

1949-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国铁路职工临代会上
  　李立三报告工会工作
    【平市讯】中华全国铁路职工临时代表会议三日上午休会半日，三日下午及第四日（七月四日）继续开会，请全国总工会李立三副主席出席报告工会工作，立三同志首先让代表们提出问题，然后综合各代表所提出关于工会组织和任务、如何办好工会、工厂管理委员会、劳动保险与工会福利事业及产业工会与地方工会的关系等问题均作了详尽的解答，午后全体代表分组讨论滕吕部长报告。
