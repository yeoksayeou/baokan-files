Title: Luo Jing Trade Union Activists' Meeting Sends Respectful Greetings to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions: A Great Victory for the Chinese Working Class and Laboring People, Opening Up a Bright Future for All Countries Striving for Independence
Author:
Date: 1949-07-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 23rd: On July 15th, the Luo Jing Bucharest Trade Union Activists' Meeting passed a telegram of greetings to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Beijing:
    The trade union activists of Bucharest, the capital of Romania, warmly welcome the delegation of free Chinese trade unions among our laboring people at today's meeting. We have listened to the report of Comrade Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and hereby express our great admiration for the heroic struggle of the Chinese working class. Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party led by Comrade Mao Zedong, the Chinese working class has won successive victories in the struggle to liberate all of China from the hands of the criminal gang of Chiang Kai-shek, the tool of American and British imperialism. The great victory of the Chinese working class and laboring people is a victory for the forces of peace and socialism. This victory opens up a bright prospect for a better life and progress for all colonial countries fighting for independence. The Bucharest Trade Union Activists' Meeting expresses deep sympathy for the trade union organizations of the fighting Chinese working class. Your contribution to the liberation of the motherland in exchange for great sacrifices will become a brilliant achievement in rebuilding China's economic life on a new basis. We express our indignation at the steps taken by the Italian government, the running dog of American imperialism, to obstruct the Chinese delegation from attending the Second World Trade Union Congress. The will to strive for unity and the desire to defend peace are the banners of the entire working class. Despite the desperate attempts of American and British imperialists to undermine the unity of the world's workers and launch new wars, the world's working class will surely triumph. The meeting wholeheartedly congratulates the Chinese working class and its country on the glorious victories won, and wishes you new victories and final victory over the enemy. We wish you complete success in the struggle to strengthen the people's democratic regime and move towards socialist construction. We have been educated in the spirit of internationalism, and we are determined to establish strong ties with Chinese trade unions in order to strengthen our trade union organizations in the spirit of fraternal friendship. In this way, we will contribute to the struggle of the camp of peace and democracy, which is led by the great, powerful, victorious socialist country, the Soviet Union.
    Long live free China! Long live the heroic struggle of the Chinese nation against American and British aggressors! Long live the Chinese Communist Party! Long live the World Federation of Trade Unions, which defends peace and the unity of the world's trade unions! Long live the great Soviet Union, the fortress of peace and freedom for all countries! Long live Stalin, the leader and teacher of the world's working class!
    (Signed) Georghe Apostol, Chairman of the Romanian General Confederation of Labor (on behalf of the Bucharest Trade Union Activists' Meeting)

### 罗京职工会积极分子大会　电我全国总工会致敬  中国工人阶级和劳动人民的巨大胜利  给一切争取独立的国家开辟光明前途

1949-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　罗京职工会积极分子大会
  　电我全国总工会致敬
    中国工人阶级和劳动人民的巨大胜利
    给一切争取独立的国家开辟光明前途
    【新华社布拉格二十三日电】七月十五日，罗京布加勒斯特职工会积极分子大会，通过向中华全国总工会的致敬电，电文如下：
    北平中华全国总工会：
    罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特的职工会积极分子们，在今天的会议上热情地欢迎自由中国职工会代表团在我国劳动人民中间出现。我们听取了中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一同志的报告，特在此表示我们对于中国工人阶级的英雄战斗十分钦佩。在毛泽东同志所领导的中国共产党领导之下，中国工人阶级在从美英帝国主义的工具蒋介石罪恶匪帮手中解放全中国的斗争中，接连获得胜利。中国工人阶级与劳动人民的巨大胜利，正是和平与社会主义力量的胜利。这一胜利给正在为争取独立而奋战的一切殖民地国家，开辟了一个较好的生活与进步的光明远景。布加勒斯特的职工会积极分子会议，对于战斗的中国工人阶级的职工会组织，表示深厚地同情。你们以巨大牺牲换来的对于祖国解放的贡献，将成为在新的基础上重建中国经济生活的光辉业绩。我们对于阻挠中国代表团出席第二次世界职工会代表大会的美国帝国主义走卒意大利政府所采的步骤表示愤慨。争取统一的意志和保卫和平的愿望，是全体工人阶级的旗帜。尽管美英帝国主义者绝望地力图破坏工人全世界的统一并发动新战争，但是，全世界的工人阶级必将获得胜利。会议衷心地祝贺中国工人阶级及其国家所赢得的辉煌胜利，祝你们取得新的胜利，最后战胜敌人。祝你们在加强人民民主政权、走向社会主义建设的战斗中，获得完全的成功。我们在国际主义精神下受到了教育，我们确定地希望和中国职工会建立强有力的联系，以便在兄弟般的友谊精神之下，加强我们的职工会组织。这样，我们将对和平与民主阵营的斗争有所贡献，这个阵营是由伟大而强有力的胜利的社会主义国家苏联所领导的。
    自由中国万岁！中华民族反对美英侵略者的英雄斗争万岁！中国共产党万岁！保卫和平与职工会世界统一的世界工联万岁！和平与各国自由的堡垒——伟大的苏联万岁！世界工人阶级的领袖与导师斯大林万岁！
    罗马尼亚总工会主席乔治·阿波斯托尔（代表布加勒斯特职工会积极分子大会）（签字）
