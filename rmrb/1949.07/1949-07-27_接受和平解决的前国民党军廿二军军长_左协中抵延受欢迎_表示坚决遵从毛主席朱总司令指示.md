Title: Former Kuomintang Army 22nd Army Commander Zuo Xiezong, Who Accepted Peaceful Resolution, Arrives in Yan'an and Receives Warm Welcome; Expresses Firm Commitment to Abiding by Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu's Instructions
Author:
Date: 1949-07-27
Page: 1

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Beiping Broadcast】 Yan'an, 26th: Former Kuomintang 22nd Army Commander General Zuo Xiezong, who accepted the peaceful resolution, arrived in Yan'an recently and was welcomed by all sectors. General Zuo Xiezong expressed his gratitude for the warm reception from all sectors. He said: After the entire army received the reply telegram from Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu on July 11, they were very excited. From now on, we will resolutely follow the instructions, unite the entire army, participate in revolutionary work, and strive for the cause of people's liberation. He believes that after the four bandit armies of Hu were encircled and annihilated, the complete liberation of the Northwest is not far off.

### 接受和平解决的前国民党军廿二军军长　左协中抵延受欢迎  表示坚决遵从毛主席朱总司令指示

1949-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　接受和平解决的前国民党军廿二军军长
  　左协中抵延受欢迎
    表示坚决遵从毛主席朱总司令指示
    【北平新华广播电台广播】延安二十六日消息：接受和平解决方案的前国民党二十二军军长左协中将军，日前到达延安，受到各界的欢迎。左协中将军对于各界的热诚招待，表示感谢。他说：全军接到毛主席朱总司令七月十一日的复电以后，非常兴奋。今后将坚决遵从指示，团结全军，参加革命工作，为人民解放事业而努力。他认为胡匪四个军被围歼以后，大西北的全部解放，已经为期不远。
