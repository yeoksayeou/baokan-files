Title: Preparing to Convene the Northeast People's Representative Conference; Lin Feng and Others Elected as People's Representatives
Author:
Date: 1949-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 24th. Since the Northeast Administrative Committee issued a resolution on swiftly convening the Northeast People's Representative Conference on the 9th of last month, all provinces in Northeast China and various people's organizations in Northeast China have, under the auspices of their respective "Preparatory Committees for the Election of Representatives to the People's Representative Conference," elected official representatives according to the quota of representatives stipulated by the Northeast Administrative Committee before the 5th of this month. A total of 149 regional representatives, including Lin Feng, Gao Chongmin, Li Yunchang, Gu Dacun, and Che Xiangchen, were elected from the six provinces of Liaodong, Liaoxi, Jilin, Songjiang, Heilongjiang, and Rehe, as well as the four cities of Shenyang, Fushun, Anshan, and Benxi, and Lushun-Dalian. In addition, sixty worker representatives, thirty military representatives, thirty women's organization representatives, thirty cultural, educational, youth, and student representatives, ten representatives of staff members of Northeast Political Committee-level organizations, eighteen representatives of industrialists and businessmen, and sixteen representatives of minority nationalities were elected. The list of all 343 people's representatives has now been sent to the Northeast Political Committee. As all representatives have been elected as scheduled, the Northeast People's Representative Conference can be convened quickly.

### 筹开东北人民代表会议　林枫等当选人民代表

1949-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　筹开东北人民代表会议
  　林枫等当选人民代表
    【新华社沈阳二十四日电】自东北行政委员会上月九日发布迅速召开东北人民代表会议的决议后，东北各省及东北各人民团体均已在各自组成的“人民代表会议代表选举筹备会”主持下，于本月五日前按照东北行政委员会规定的代表名额选出正式代表。计辽东、辽西、吉林、松江、黑龙江、热河六省，沈阳、抚顺、鞍山、本溪四市及旅大共选出地区代表林枫、高崇民、李运昌、古大存、车向忱等一百四十九人，职工代表六十人、军队代表三十人、妇女团体代表三十人、文化、教育、青年、学生代表三十人，东北政委会一级机关职员代表十人、工商业家代表十八人、少数民族代表十六人。现全部人民代表三百四十三人的名单已送至东北政委会。由于全部代表已如期选出，东北人民代表会议将可迅速召开。
