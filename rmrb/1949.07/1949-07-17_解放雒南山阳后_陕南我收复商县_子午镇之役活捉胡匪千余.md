Title: After Liberating Luonan and Shanyang, Southern Shaanxi, We Recover Shang County; Over a Thousand Bandits Captured Alive in the Battle of Ziwu Town
Author:
Date: 1949-07-17
Page: 3

    Following the liberation of Luonan and Shanyang
    Southern Shaanxi, We Recover Shang County
    Over a Thousand Bandits Captured Alive in the Battle of Ziwu Town

    [Broadcast by the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Xi'an, 16th: After liberating the two county towns of Luonan and Shanyang, the Shaanxi Southern People's Liberation Army recovered Shang County town on the Xi'an to Jingziguan highway on the 12th. A part of the Kuomintang bandit army's 17th Division and a gang of local bandits entrenched in the county abandoned the city and fled.

    [Broadcast by the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Shaanxi Central Frontline, 16th: The People's Liberation Army's First Field Army annihilated the headquarters of the Hu bandit's 12th Division and its 34th Regiment in Ziwu Town, south of Xi'an, on the 11th of this month. According to preliminary statistics, the battle results are as follows: 1,195 enemy troops captured alive, three mortars seized, sixteen 60mm guns, sixty-three light and heavy machine guns, three hundred and twenty rifles, and a portion of other spoils of war.

### 解放雒南山阳后  陕南我收复商县  子午镇之役活捉胡匪千余

1949-07-17
第3版()
专栏：

    解放雒南山阳后
    陕南我收复商县
    子午镇之役活捉胡匪千余
    【北平新华广播电台广播】西安十六日消息：陕南人民解放军，在解放了雒南、山阳两座县城以后，十二日又收复了西安到荆紫关公路上的商县县城，盘踞在该县的国民党匪军十七师一部和土匪一股，弃城逃窜。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】陕中前线十六日消息：人民解放军第一野战军，本月十一日在西安以南子午镇歼灭胡匪十二师师部，和他所属三十四团，战斗中所获战果，据初步统计如下：活捉敌军一千一百九十五名，缴获迫击炮三门，六零炮十六门，轻重机关枪六十三挺，步枪三百二十支，和其他战利品一部。
