Title: Burmese Guerrillas Win Big Victory, Occupy Three Cities Including Myohaung
Author:
Date: 1949-07-07
Page: 3
　　
　　Burmese Guerrillas Win Big Victory
  　Occupy Three Cities Including Myohaung
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 6th] TASS Delhi, 4th: Indian newspapers recently reported that Arakan district Burmese guerrillas have recently achieved another major victory. After three days of fighting, the guerrillas occupied the cities of Ponnagyun and Myohaung (both places are very close to Akyab port). In the Upper Chindwin district, guerrillas occupied Mingin (70 miles from the Indo-Burmese border).

### 缅游击队大胜　占领苗洪等三城

1949-07-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　缅游击队大胜
  　占领苗洪等三城
    【新华社北平六日电】塔斯社德里四日讯：印度报纸最近报道：阿拉干区缅甸游击队，最近又获大胜。在三天战斗之后，游击队占领了邦纳吉云及苗洪两城（两地距阿恰布港很近）。在上更的宛区，游击队占领了明京（距印缅边境七十哩）。
