Title: British Communist Party Artists Group Cables Congratulations to Literary and Art Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-07-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 20th—The British Communist Party Artists Group cabled congratulations to the All-China Conference of Literary and Art Workers, the cable reads as follows:

　　The British Communist Party Artists Group extends its warmest congratulations to the All-China Conference of Literary and Art Workers in Beiping. Today, the eyes of all progressive elements are on China. The glorious victory of the People's Liberation Army is also a victory for the art, literature and civilization of your great people. Wishing the conference success.

　　Chairman Poulden
    Vice-Chairman Niven

### 英共艺术工作者小组　电贺文代大会

1949-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　英共艺术工作者小组
  　电贺文代大会
    【新华社布拉格二十日电】英国共产党艺术工作者小组电贺中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会，电文如下：
    英国共产党艺术工作者小组向北平中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会致最热烈的祝贺。今天一切进步分子的眼睛都看着中国。解放军的光荣胜利也是你们伟大人民的艺术、文学与文明的胜利。祝会议成功。　
　　主席　普尔敦
    副主席　尼文
