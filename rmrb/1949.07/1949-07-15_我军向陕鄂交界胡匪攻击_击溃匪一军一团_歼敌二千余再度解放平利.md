Title: Our Army Attacks Bandit Gangs on the Shaanxi-Hubei Border: One Bandit Army and One Regiment Crushed, Over 2,000 Enemies Eliminated, Pingli Liberated Again
Author:
Date: 1949-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, 14th: On the 10th, the People's Liberation Army launched an attack on the bandit gangs' strategically important Guanyazi positions between Zhuxi and Pingli on the Shaanxi-Hubei border, immediately capturing the entire area, crushing the entire 69th Army and the 472nd Regiment of the 158th Division of the bandit forces, killing, wounding and capturing over 2,000 officers and soldiers, including the commander of the 144th Division, Fu Shufeng. The Liberation Army re-liberated the city of Pingli the following day.

In this battle, the Liberation Army captured Fu Shufeng, commander of the 144th Division, Li Xueming, deputy commander of the 430th Regiment, Wei Huaming, political director, Liu Shenfeng, commander of the 403rd Regiment of the 135th Division, Xu Shaoxian, deputy commander, and 1,379 other officers and soldiers. Over 700 enemies were killed or wounded, including Li Ding (killed), commander of the 430th Regiment of the 144th Division. The army seized one chemical mortar, eight mortars, twenty 60mm mortars, twenty-eight heavy machine guns, seventy-five light machine guns, six hundred and thirteen long and short guns, forty-eight automatic rifles, ammunition, and other military supplies.

### 我军向陕鄂交界胡匪攻击  击溃匪一军一团　歼敌二千余再度解放平利

1949-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军向陕鄂交界胡匪攻击
    击溃匪一军一团
  　歼敌二千余再度解放平利
    【新华社西北前线十四日电】人民解放军十日向陕鄂交界竹溪、平利间胡匪关垭子天险阵地发起攻击，当即全部攻占该地，击溃胡匪六十九军全部及一百五十八师之四七二团，毙伤俘敌一四四师师长符树逢以下官兵二千余人。解放军并于次日再度解放平利城。
    在这一战斗中，解放军俘敌一四四师师长符树逢，四三○团副团长李学明，政治主任魏化明，一三五师四○三团团长刘申风，副团长许绍贤等以下官兵一千三百七十九人，毙伤敌一四四师四三○团团长李定（毙）等以下七百多人。缴获化学迫击炮一门，迫击炮八门，六零炮二十门，重机枪二十八挺，轻机枪七十五挺，长短枪六百一十三支，自动步枪四十八支，弹药及其他军用品一部。
