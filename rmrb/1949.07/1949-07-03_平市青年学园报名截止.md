Title: Ping City Youth Academy Registration Deadline
Author:
Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 1

　　Ping City Youth Academy Registration Deadline
    [Our Newspaper] The registration for the Youth Academy organized by the Beiping Municipal Summer Work Committee closed yesterday (the 2nd). The number of young people registered was 9,978. Among them, 1,497 were from the central district, 1,821 from the northeast district, 2,400 from the southwest district, 1,710 from the northwest district, and 2,550 from the southeast district.

### 平市青年学园报名截止

1949-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市青年学园报名截止
    【本报讯】北平市暑期工作委员会所举办的青年学园，昨（二）日报名截止。报名青年九九七八人。其中中心区一四九七人，东北区一八二一人，西南区二四○○人，西北区一七一○人，东南区二五五○人。
