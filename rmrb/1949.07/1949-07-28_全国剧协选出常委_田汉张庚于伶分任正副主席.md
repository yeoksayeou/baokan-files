Title: National Theater Association Elects Standing Committee; Tian Han, Zhang Geng, and Yu Ling Appointed Chairman and Vice-Chairmen

Author: (Qing)
Date: 1949-07-28
Page: 2

The National Drama Workers Association Elected Standing Committee
Tian Han, Zhang Geng, and Yu Ling Appointed Chairman and Vice-Chairmen

[This newspaper dispatch] The All-China Drama Workers Association held a plenary committee meeting at the Beijing Hotel yesterday (the 27th) at 3:00 p.m. More than 50 members attended. Chaired by Tian Han and Zhang Geng, the meeting first supplemented the formal member A Jia, who had been omitted during the previous ballot counting. Including the previously elected formal members, there were a total of 89 members (with six seats reserved for the liberated areas). Following this, Tian Han, Zhang Geng, Yu Ling, Song Zhi's, Ma Jianling, A Ying, Mei Lanfang, Chen Baichen, Cao Yu, Wang Dizi, A Jia, Xia Yan, Hong Shen, Zhou Xinfang, Guang Weiran, Sai Ke, Ouyang Yuqian, Ma Yanxiang, Ding Li, Xiong Foxi, Li Bozhao, Zhang Min, Zhang Zuoxiang, Cheng Yanqiu, and Cui Wei were elected as 25 standing committee members. Tian Han was elected as the chairman of the Standing Committee, and Zhang Geng and Yu Ling were elected as vice-chairmen. During the meeting, the entire body passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu. It also passed a motion to write a letter to thank Vice-Chairman Zhou for his consistent concern for the theatrical circles, and to have each delegation provide opinions on the performances of various cultural work troupes. Finally, it unanimously approved the request of the Shanghai Drama and Film Association to become a branch of the National Drama Association. The meeting adjourned at approximately 5:00 p.m.

### 全国剧协选出常委　田汉张庚于伶分任正副主席

1949-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国剧协选出常委
  　田汉张庚于伶分任正副主席
    【本报讯】中华全国戏剧工作者协会昨（二十七）日下午三时在北京饭店召开全体委员会，出席委员五十余人，由田汉、张庚主席，首先将前检票时遗漏了的正式委员阿甲增补，连上次选出之正式委员共八十九人（待解放区留六席）。接着选出了田汉、张庚、干伶、宋之的、马健翎、阿英、梅兰芳、陈白尘、曹禺、王地子、阿甲、夏衍、洪深、周信芳、光未然、塞克、欧阳予倩、马彦祥、丁里、熊佛西、李伯钊、章泯、张做祥、程砚秋、崔嵬等二十五名常务委员，并推选田汉为常委会主席，张庚、于伶为副主席。会中全体通过了向毛主席、朱总司令致敬电，并通过写信感谢周副主席对戏剧界的一贯关怀，及由各代表团提供各文工团演出之意见，最后一致通过上海戏剧电影协会为全国剧协分会的请求。于五时许散会。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（清）
