Title: Inter-city Remittance Between Beiping and Xi'an Now Available
Author:
Date: 1949-07-27
Page: 2

Economic News

　　Inter-city Remittance Between Beiping and Xi'an Now Available
    [This Newspaper's Report] The People's Bank of Beiping Branch has begun handling remittances between Beiping and Xi'an. Currently, only postal money orders are accepted, and exchange rates are posted daily. (Yu)

### 北平西安间汇兑已通

1949-07-27
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　北平西安间汇兑已通
    【本报讯】北平与西安间汇兑，人民银行北平分行已开始办理，目前仅承作票汇一种，汇率每日挂牌公布。　　　　　　　（宇）
