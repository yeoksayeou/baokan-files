Title: Indian "Pioneer" Newspaper Article Sternly Condemns the Pacific Pact
Author: 
Date: 1949-07-30
Page: 1
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 29th: TASS, Delhi, July 27th: The National Herald published an article by Indian journalist Sangaya, exposing the U.S. imperialists' proposed Pacific Pact as hostile to the vital interests of India and all other Asian countries. The article stated: The purpose of this Pacific Pact, instigated by the United States, is to obtain the vast manpower of Asia, attempting to acquire cannon fodder for the anti-Soviet war that the United States is preparing. The author strongly urged the people of India and Asia to give a crushing rebuff to this insidious intention of the American imperialist bloc, an intention to antagonize the Asian people against the Soviet Union, the "equal defender of all countries in the world." Sangaya also strongly condemned the traitorous behavior of the Indian reactionaries who support the American plan.

### 印先驱报著文　严斥太平洋公约

1949-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　印先驱报著文
  　严斥太平洋公约
    【新华社北平二十九日电】塔斯社德里二十七日讯：全国先驱报刊载印度记者桑加雅所撰的论文，揭露美帝国主义者拟议中的太平洋公约，是仇视印度与一切其它亚洲国家的切身利益的。该文声称：美国授意的这一太平洋公约的目的，在于获得亚洲的巨大人力，企图为美国正在准备的反苏战争取得炮灰。作者力促印度与亚洲人民给予美国帝国主义集团的这一阴险意图以粉碎性的还击，这种意图是使亚洲人民与“全世界各国平等的捍卫者”苏联相敌对。桑加雅同时强烈地抨击支持美国计划的印度反动派的卖国行为。
