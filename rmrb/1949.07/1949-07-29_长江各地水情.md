Title: Water Conditions in Various Parts of the Yangtze River
Author:
Date: 1949-07-29
Page: 2

　　Water Conditions in Various Parts of the Yangtze River
　　[North Ping Xinhua Broadcasting Station, Nanjing, 28th dispatch] The water levels of Yichang, Jiujiang, Wuhu, and Nanjing along the Yangtze River continued to decrease on the 27th; the water levels of Hankou and Zhenjiang rose. At 8:00 AM on the 27th, the water level in Yichang decreased by 0.53 meters compared to the 26th, Jiujiang decreased by 0.03 meters, Wuhu decreased by 0.07 meters, and Nanjing decreased by 0.15 meters. The water level in Hankou rose by 0.03 meters compared to the 26th, and Zhenjiang rose by 0.07 meters. In Nanchang, in the Ganjiang River basin, the water level at 6:00 AM on the 27th was 0.01 meters lower than on the 26th.

### 长江各地水情

1949-07-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　长江各地水情
    【北平新华广播电台南京二十八日消息】长江沿岸宜昌、九江、芜湖、南京四地，二十七号水位继续下降；汉口、镇江两地，水位上涨。二十七号上午八点钟，宜昌水位比二十六号下降了零点五三公尺，九江下降了零下零三公尺，芜湖下降了零点零七公尺，南京下降了零点一五公尺。汉口水位比二十六号涨了零点零三公尺，镇江涨了零点零七公尺。赣江流域南昌，二十七号上午六点钟的水位，比二十六号下落了零点零一公尺。
