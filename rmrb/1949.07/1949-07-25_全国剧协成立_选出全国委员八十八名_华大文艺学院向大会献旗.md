Title: National Drama Association Established; Eighty-Eight National Committee Members Elected; Literary and Art Institute of North China University Presents Banner to the Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-07-25
Page: 1

　　National Drama Association Established
  　Eighty-Eight National Committee Members Elected
  　Literary and Art Institute of North China University Presents Banner to the Congress
    [Report from this Newspaper] The All-China Dramatists Association held its inaugural meeting yesterday morning (the 24th). A total of 185 representatives attended. After Tian Han delivered the opening speech, Zhou Yang was invited to speak. He said: Drama is the most popular art form, and one of the most effective tools for mobilizing and educating the people. In the liberated areas, in rural areas, in factories, and in the People's Liberation Army, drama is widely and enthusiastically welcomed, becoming a truly mass drama movement. Dramatists must consolidate the existing mass base and further expand this base with the complete liberation of all of China. Drama should play a leading role in popularization work. Regarding the issue of improvement, Zhou Yang believes that improvement is not only about improving form, but mainly about ideological improvement. Literary and art workers in the liberated areas have begun to learn to portray workers, peasants, and soldiers, but they are not yet good at portraying the various personalities among the masses, especially praising and shaping new personalities and new characters among the masses. At the same time, while portraying the masses, they are not yet good at portraying leadership, that is, observing and depicting from the perspectives of both the masses and the leaders. Finally, he talked about the issue of unity in the drama world. He believes that the drama world must break down all sectarian prejudices. New dramatists must first closely cooperate with all those in the old drama world who are determined to reform, and jointly devote themselves to the cause of old drama reform, uniting and transforming the vast number of old artists. In addition, new and old dramatists must learn from each other, absorb each other's experiences, young dramatists must respect old dramatists, and old dramatists must cherish young dramatists and help them develop. After Zhou Yang's speech, Zhang Geng reported on the preparations for the Drama Association. The congress unanimously adopted the draft constitution of the All-China Dramatists Association and elected members of the National Committee of the Drama Association. The Literary and Art Institute of North China University presented a banner to the association. Hong Shen, Xiong Foxi, Ma Jianling, Guang Weiran, Song Zhi's, Zhang Zhixiang and others spoke successively, celebrating the establishment of the Drama Association. Finally, the dance team of North China University performed the Green Seed Dance and the Shaniko Dance to add to the excitement. (Qing)

### 全国剧协成立　选出全国委员八十八名　华大文艺学院向大会献旗

1949-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国剧协成立
  　选出全国委员八十八名
  　华大文艺学院向大会献旗
    【本报讯】中华全国戏剧工作者协会于昨（二十四）日上午举行成立大会，共到代表一百八十五人。田汉致开会词后，周扬被邀讲话。他说：戏剧是最富有群众性的一门艺术，是动员人民、教育人民最有效的工具之一，在解放区农村中、工厂中，在人民解放军部队中，戏剧受到广泛而热烈的欢迎，成为真正群众的戏剧运动。戏剧工作者必须巩固已有的群众基础，并随着全中国的完全解放而更加扩大这个基础。在普及工作上戏剧应起带头的作用。关于提高问题，周扬认为提高不只在讲求形式，主要是从思想上提高。解放区文艺工作者开始学会了表现工农兵，但还不善于表现群众中各种不同的个性，特别是表扬与塑造群众中新的个性，新的人格。同时表现了群众，又还不善于表现领导，即从群众与领导两方面的角度去观察和描写。最后，他谈到戏剧界团结问题，他认为戏剧界必须打破一切宗派门户之见，新戏剧工作者首先必须与旧剧界一切有志改革者密切合作，共同致力旧剧改革事业，团结与改造广大旧艺人。此外，新老戏剧工作者必须互相学习，互相吸取经验，年青戏剧工作者必须尊重老戏剧家，老戏剧家则必须十分爱护年青戏剧工作者，帮助他们发展。周扬讲话毕，张庚报告戏剧协会筹备经过，大会一致通过了中华全国戏剧工作者协会章程草案。并选出剧协全国委员会委员。华北大学文艺学院向该协会献旗，并由洪深、熊佛西、马健翎、光未然、宋之的、张致祥等先后讲话，庆祝戏剧协会成立，最后华大舞蹈队表演青苗舞、沙尼古舞助兴。（清）
