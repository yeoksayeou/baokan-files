Title: Telegram Congratulating the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-07-18
Page: 1

    Telegram Congratulating the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference
    [Ping City News] The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Social Science Workers sent a congratulatory telegram to the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference. The original telegram is as follows: Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference:
    Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, the Chinese revolution has won a basic victory. The convening of the New Political Consultative Conference and the establishment of the People's Democratic United Front Government will lay a solid foundation for China's great construction prospects. Chinese social science workers have long struggled for the revolution against imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucratic capitalism. In the future, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the People's Democratic United Government, they will unite and fully participate in the construction of the new China's political, economic, cultural, educational, and national defense undertakings. We hereby express our warm congratulations on the success already achieved by the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference, and our sincere support for the upcoming New Political Consultative Conference. We will strengthen our unity, learning, and work to welcome the establishment of the People's Democratic United Government.
      Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Social Science Workers, July 17th

### 电贺新政协筹备会

1949-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    电贺新政协筹备会
    【平市讯】中华全国社会科学工作者代表会议筹备会向新政治协商会议筹备会致贺电，原电如下：新政治协商会议筹备会：
    中国革命已经在中国共产党和毛主席领导之下获得了基本的胜利。新政治协商会议的召开与人民民主联合政会的成立，将为中国伟大的建设前途奠定巩固的基础。中国的社会科学工作者曾经长期地为反帝国主义、反封建主义、反官僚资本主义的革命而斗争，今后更将在毛主席和人民民主联合政府的领导之下团结一致，全力参与新中国的政治、经济、文教和国防的建设事业。我们特对于新政协筹备会已经达到的成功表示热烈的庆祝，对于即将召开的新政治协商会议表示忠诚的拥护，并将以加强我们的团结、学习与工作，来迎接人民民主联合政府的成立。
      中华全国社会科学工作者代表会议筹备会七月十七日
