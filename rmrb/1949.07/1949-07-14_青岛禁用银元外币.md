Title: Qingdao Bans Silver Yuan and Foreign Currency
Author:
Date: 1949-07-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 13th: The Qingdao Military Control Commission, in order to stabilize the market, ensure the people's livelihood, and develop production, issued a proclamation on the 1st of this month strictly prohibiting the pricing and circulation of gold, silver, and foreign currencies, as well as private buying and selling. This measure immediately received enthusiastic support from the broad masses of the people. A movement to reject the use of silver yuan has been launched in Qingdao. In recent days, workers and students have been conducting extensive street-level propaganda to reject the use of silver yuan and oppose speculation in gold and silver. Groups of citizens surrounded propaganda trucks, shouting slogans such as "Quickly ban silver yuan dealers!" People from all walks of life have sent letters to the Qingdao Jiaodong Daily, expressing their full support for the military control measures. Many citizens have begun to exchange silver yuan and foreign currencies for Renminbi at banks.

### 青岛禁用银元外币

1949-07-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　青岛禁用
  　银元外币
    【新华社济南十三日电】青岛市军管会为稳定市尝安定民生和发展生产起见，于本月一日布告严禁金银、外币计价行使或私自买卖。这一措施立刻获得广大人民的热烈拥护。拒用银元运动已在青市展开。连日工人、学生均在街头进行拒用银元和反对金银投机的扩大宣传。成群的市民包围着宣传卡车，高喊“快快取缔银元贩子！”等口号。各界人民纷纷投函青岛胶东日报，表示全力支持军管的措施。许多市民已开始将银元与外币向银行兑换人民币。
