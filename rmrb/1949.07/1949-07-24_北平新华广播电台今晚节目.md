Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-24
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program

    [This Dispatch] From 19:50 to 20:10 tonight, the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast a lecture program featuring Professor Liang Xi of Nanjing Central University speaking on "A Look at Nanjing: Past and Present." From 19:30 to 19:50, the Workers' Time segment, sponsored by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, will introduce the work of the Harbin Labor Bureau. From 22:20 to 22:50, the Arts and Literature program will feature the Shandong-Hebei-Henan Folk Art Joint Association's storytelling group performing the local Zhuizi ballad "Fiancée Encourages Husband to Join the Army."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，播送南京中央大学教授梁希讲：“南京的今昔观”。十九点三十分到十九点五十分职工时间，由中华全国总工会主办，介绍哈尔滨市劳动局的工作。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，冀鲁豫民间艺术联合会说唱典型组演唱地方坠子“未婚妻劝夫参军”。
