Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions' Newspaper "Worker's Daily" to be Published in Beiping on July 15th
Author:
Date: 1949-07-04
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 2nd. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions' newspaper, "Worker's Daily," has been decided to be published in Beiping on July 15th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has issued a notice stating, "In order to meet the needs of the development of the national workers' movement, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has decided to publish its own newspaper, 'Worker's Daily,' on July 15th. This is an important event in the history of the Chinese workers' movement. 'Worker's Daily' will guide the work, life, and study of employees, and strive to become a collective propagandist and organizer of the workers' movement. Based on the current conditions, 'Worker's Daily' will primarily target employees in industrial cities in North China, and focus on introducing the experiences of employees in North China to all parts of the country. At the same time, it is hoped that employee organizations in the Northeast, East China, Central China, Northwest, and other regions can systematically exchange their work experiences through 'Worker's Daily'. It is hoped that all regions, especially employee organizations in North China, as well as trade union cadres, trade union activists, labor heroes, model workers, and even every worker and employee, can take on the task of writing for 'Worker's Daily,' organizing correspondents, and subscribing to and promoting 'Worker's Daily.'"

### 全总机关报“工人日报”　七月十五在平出版

1949-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总机关报“工人日报”
  　七月十五在平出版
    【新华社北平二日电】中华全国总工会的机关报工人日报决定于七月十五日在北平出版。全国总工会为此发出通知称为“中华全国总工会为了适应全国工人运动发展的需要，决定于七月十五日出版自己的机关报——工人日报。这是中国工人运动历史上的一个重要事件。工人日报将指导职工的工作、生活和学习，并努力成为职工运动的集体宣传者和组织者。根据目前的条件，工人日报将首先以华北各工业城市的职工为主要对象，并着重将华北职工工作的经验介绍给全国各地，同时希望东北、华东、华中、西北等地的职工组织能将他们的工作经验，经过工人日报作有系统的交流。希望各地首先是华北职工组织和工会干部、工会积极分子、劳动英雄、模范工作者以至每个工人和职员，都能担负起为工人日报写稿和组织通讯员，订阅和推销工人日报的任务。”
