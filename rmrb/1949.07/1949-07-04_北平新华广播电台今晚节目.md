Title: Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
Author:
Date: 1949-07-04
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [Our Dispatch] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's speech program tonight from 19:50 to 20:10 will feature Mr. Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, broadcasting to the entire nation in commemoration of the "July 7th" Incident. During the women's hour from 19:30 to 19:50, "Only the Communist Party Truly Cares About Us" will be broadcast. From 22:20 to 22:50, the literary program will feature the joint choir of the Second and Third Literary Work Troupes of North China University singing the "Workers' Chorus."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，由中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深先生为纪念“七七”向全国广播。十九点三十分到十九点五十分妇女时间，播送“只有共产党真正关心咱”二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由华北大学第二、第三文艺工作团合唱“工人大合唱”。
