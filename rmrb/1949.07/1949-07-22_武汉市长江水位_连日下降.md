Title: Wuhan Yangtze River Water Level Declines Continuously
Author: 
Date: 1949-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 21st—The water level of the Yangtze River in Wuhan has been declining continuously for several days. It was 27.00 meters on the 15th, decreased to 26.97 meters on the 16th, and had dropped to 26.84 meters by the 19th. This year's highest water level was 27.09 meters, the fourth highest water level in the past 80 years. According to experienced people, as long as the rainfall does not increase sharply again, the expansion of the flood disaster can be avoided. Currently, various regions are continuing to strengthen flood prevention work to prevent the flood from rising again.

### 武汉市长江水位　连日下降

1949-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　武汉市长江水位　连日下降
    【新华社汉口二十一日电】武汉市长江水位连日下降，十五日为二七·○○公尺，十六日降为二六·九七公尺，十九日已降至二六·八四公尺。今年最高水位为二七·○九公尺，是八十年来第四次最高水位。据有经验人士说：今后只要雨量不再陡增，可免水灾扩大。现各地正继续加强防汛工作，以防洪水再涨。
