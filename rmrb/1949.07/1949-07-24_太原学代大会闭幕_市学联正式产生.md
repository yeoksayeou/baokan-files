Title: Taiyuan Student Congress Concludes; Taiyuan Student Federation Officially Established
Author:
Date: 1949-07-24
Page: 1

　　Taiyuan Student Congress Concludes
　　City Student Federation Officially Established
    [Taiyuan Electric] The first session of the Taiyuan Student Representative Conference successfully concluded on the 20th. At the meeting on the 13th, the guiding principles and tasks of the current student movement in Taiyuan were adopted, which are to strengthen learning, improve oneself a hundredfold, and cultivate talents to build a new democratic society. On the 19th, the charter of the Taiyuan Student Federation and the election method of the Federation's Executive Committee were approved. On the 20th, Niu Ye, Kang Wanru, and other ten people were elected as members of the Federation's Executive Committee, and the Taiyuan Student Federation was officially established. Telegrams were sent to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu to express respect, and telegrams were sent to the preparatory meeting of the New Political Consultative Conference and the North China Student Federation to congratulate them. The closing ceremony was held in the afternoon.

### 太原学代大会闭幕　市学联正式产生

1949-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　太原学代大会闭幕
  　市学联正式产生
    【太原电】太原市学生代表会首届会议，已于二十日胜利结束。十三日会议中，通过了当前太原市学生运动的方针与任务，是加强学习，百倍的提高自己，培养成为建设新民主主义社会的人才。十九日通过了太原市学生联合会章程及学联执委会选举法，二十日选出牛晔、康婉如等十人为学联执委，正式产生太原市学联，并分别致电毛主席、朱总司令致敬，电新政协筹备会及华北学联祝贺。下午举行闭幕典礼。
