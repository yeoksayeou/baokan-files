Title: People's Bank of Beiping Branch Universally Lowers Exchange Rates to Promote Material Exchange
Author:
Date: 1949-07-19
Page: 2

    Economic News

    People's Bank of Beiping Branch
    Universally Lowers Exchange Rates to Promote Material Exchange
    [This Newspaper's Report] In order to regulate finance in various regions and promote the exchange of materials between regions and between urban and rural areas, the People's Bank of Beiping Branch has readjusted exchange rates. Among the sixty-six exchange points operated by the bank's business department and its affiliated units, the exchange rates for all exchange points except Shanghai and other places have been universally lowered by 30% to 50%, effective from the 20th. The exchange points and newly set exchange rates are as follows: Mentougou, 1‰. Tianjin, Tanggu, 1.5‰. Zhuoxian, Gu'an, Dingxing, Yixian, Xushui, Baoding, 2‰. Zhangjiakou, Huailai, Xuanhua, Tangshan, Qinhuangdao, Changli, Leting, Fengrun, Zunhua, Yutian, Baodi, Qian'an, Luanxian, Guye, Cangxian, Bozhen, Jinan, Dingxian, Zhengding, Shijiazhuang, Wanxian, Shenze, Anguo, Hejian, Raoyang, Lixian, Gaoyang, Wangjiajing, Hengshui, Xinji, Dezhou, Huolu, Yangquan, Pingshan, Taiyuan, all 3‰. Linqing, Xuzhou, Xingtai, Handan, Nangong, all 4‰. Datong, Fengzhen, Jining, Yuxian, Yanggao, Xinxiang, Kaifeng, Zhengzhou, Heze, Luoyang, Linfen, Changzhi, all 5‰. Shanhaiguan, 6‰. Shanghai, Nanjing, Hankou, Hanyang, Wuchang, all to be posted daily. (Yu)

### 人民银行北平分行  普遍降低汇率促进物资交流

1949-07-19
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

    人民银行北平分行
    普遍降低汇率促进物资交流
    【本报讯】人民银行北平分行为调节各地金融，促进各区域间及城乡间物资交流，特重新调整汇率。该行营业部及所属各单位承做之六十六个汇兑点中，除上海等地外，其余各通汇点之汇率均普遍降低百分之三十至五十不等，自二十日起实行。各通汇点及新订汇率如下：门头沟千分之一。天津、塘沽千分之一点五。涿县、固安、定兴、易县、徐水、保定千分之二。张家口、怀来、宣化、唐山、秦皇岛、昌黎、乐亭、丰润、遵化、玉田、宝坻、迁安、滦县、古冶、沧县、泊镇、济南、定县、正定、石家庄、完县、深泽、安国、河间、饶阳、蠡县、高阳、王家井、衡水、辛集、德州、获鹿、阳泉、平山、太原、均千分之三。临清、徐州、邢台、邯郸、南宫均为千分之四。大同、丰镇、集宁、蔚县、阳高、新乡、开封、郑州、菏泽、洛阳、临汾、长治均为千分之五。山海关千分之六。上海、南京、汉口、汉阳、武昌均每日挂牌公布  （宇）
