Title: World Youth Festival Preparatory Committee Second Meeting Discusses Youth Festival Program
Author:
Date: 1949-07-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, July 17th: Budapest dispatch: The World Youth Festival Preparatory Committee held its second meeting here on the 14th to discuss the participation of youth from various countries in the Youth Festival and the program of the Youth Festival. In addition to representatives from the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Union of Students, representatives from youth organizations in the Soviet Union, China, Bulgaria, the United Kingdom, Czechoslovakia, France, Poland, Romania, Greece, Albania, India, South Africa, Venezuela, Hungary and other countries attended the meeting.

### 世界青年节筹委会二次会议讨论青年节程序

1949-07-18
第3版()
专栏：

    世界青年节
    筹委会二次会议讨论青年节程序
    【新华社北平十七日电】布达佩斯讯：世界青年节筹委会于十四日在此间举行第二次会议，讨论各国青年参加青年节与青年节的程序。出席该次会议者，除世界民主青年联盟与世界学联的代表之外，并有苏联、中国、保加利亚、英国、捷克斯洛代克、法国、波兰、罗马尼亚、希腊、阿尔巴尼亚、印度、南非、委内瑞拉、匈牙利等国青年团体的代表。
