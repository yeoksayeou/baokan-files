Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-11
Page: 1

Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
[This newspaper report] Tonight, from 19:45 to 20:30, the broadcast will rebroadcast Comrade Zhou Yang's recorded speech, "A New People's Literature and Art." From 19:30 to 19:45, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation will present a women's program, introducing recent incidents of unreasonable arrests of women by police in India and Argentina. From 21:00 to 21:15, the program will broadcast: "The Contributions of the People of Shandong to the Liberation War in the Past Three Years." From 21:15 to 21:30, International Affairs—Talking About the So-called Morality of the Gold Yuan Kingdom.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点四十五分到二十点三十分演讲节目，重播周扬同志“新的人民的文艺”录音。十九点三十分到十九点四十五分，由中华全国民主妇女联合会主办的妇女轮回节目，介绍最近印度和阿根延两个国家里警察无理逮捕妇女的事情。二十一点到二十一点十五分，播送——“三年来山东人民对解放战争的贡献”。二十一点十五分到二十一点三十分，国际时事——谈谈金圆王国的所谓道德。
