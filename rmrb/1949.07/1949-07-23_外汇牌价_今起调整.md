Title: Exchange Rates to be Adjusted Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1949-07-23
Page: 2

　　Economic News

　　Exchange Rates
  　To be Adjusted Starting Today
    [This Newspaper] The Bank of China's exchange rates will be revised starting today to US$1 = 2000 yuan (People's Currency, same below), £1 = 5100 yuan, and HK$1 = 300 yuan.

### 外汇牌价　今起调整

1949-07-23
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　外汇牌价
  　今起调整
    【本报讯】中国银行外汇牌价今日起改订为美钞一比二○○○元（人民券、下同），英镑一比五一○○元，港币一比三○○元。
