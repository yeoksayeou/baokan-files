Date: 1949-07-30

　　Traffic Briefs
    The Xi'an-Baoji section of the Longhai Railway was opened to traffic on the 20th.
    Shanghai's railway traffic was not affected by the typhoon. Direct trains between Shanghai and Beijing, as well as trains on the Beijing-Shanghai and Shanghai-Hangzhou lines, all departed on time on the evening of the 27th.
    The inventory work of the Wuhan Telecommunications Bureau, which was taken over, has been initially completed. Due to the participation of employees in the inventory work, as many as 379 kinds of materials inconsistent with the accounts and inventory were found.
    Workers at the Pukou Railway Machine Factory are renovating a standard locomotive to welcome the "August 1st" Army Day of the People's Liberation Army, and naming it "August 1st." Employees of the Hangzhou Car Depot Repair Department of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway and the Hangzhou Municipal Engineering Bureau Repair Shop are also rushing to repair the "August 1st" locomotive.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 29th)

### 交通简讯

1949-07-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　交通简讯
    陇海路西安、宝鸡段已于二十日通车。
    上海铁路交通未受台风影响，平沪直达列车、京沪、沪杭两路列车二十七日晚均准时开出。
    被接管的武汉电讯局的清点工作已初步完成。由于职工参加清点工作，查出各种与帐存库存不相符合的材料多达三百七十九种。
    浦口铁路机厂工人为了迎接“八一”人民解放军建军节，正装修一标准机车，命名为“八一”号。浙赣铁路杭州车房修理部与杭市工务局修车厂职工，也正赶修“八一”号机车。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社北平二十九日电）
