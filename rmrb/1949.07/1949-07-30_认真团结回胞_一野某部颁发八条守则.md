Title: Earnestly Unite with Hui Compatriots: A Certain Unit of the First Field Army Promulgates Eight Rules
Author:
Date: 1949-07-30
Page: 1

(Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station) Northwest Front, 28th: The People's Liberation Army, advancing towards Northwest University, is conscientiously implementing the policy of uniting with Hui compatriots. The political department of a certain corps of the First Field Army recently promulgated eight rules for uniting with Hui compatriots, requiring all commanders and fighters to earnestly implement the correct policy of uniting with domestic minority nationalities and jointly eliminating the remaining forces of the Kuomintang reactionaries in the operation to annihilate the Ma bandits. Recently, a certain division of the First Field Army, organized discussions by regiment, inviting Hui comrades among the commanders and fighters. At the meetings, Hui comrades vehemently denounced the brutal atrocities of the Ma Bufang and Ma Hongkui bandit gangs in oppressing the Hui people and all ethnic groups in the Northwest. They also pointed out that only by eliminating the Ma bandits can the broad masses of oppressed Hui compatriots be liberated. The Communist Party's policy of equality and friendship towards minority nationalities is the best policy for liberating Hui compatriots and all minority nationalities throughout the country.

### 认真团结回胞　一野某部颁发八条守则

1949-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　认真团结回胞
  　一野某部颁发八条守则
    【北平新华广播电台广播】西北前线二十八日消息：向西北大学进军的人民解放军，认真执行团结回族同胞的政策。第一野战军某兵团政治部队最近颁发团结回胞守则八条，要求全体指战员在歼灭马匪作战中，认真执行团结国内少数民族、共同肃清国民党反动派残余力的正确方针。第一野战军某师，最近以团为单位，分别召集指战员中的回民同志举行座谈，会上回民同志纷纷控诉马步芳、马鸿逵匪帮压榨西北回族、各族同胞的残酷暴行。并且指出：消灭了马匪，被压迫的广大回族同胞才能得到解放。共产党对少数民族的平等友好政策，是解放回族同胞和全国少数民族的最好的政策。
