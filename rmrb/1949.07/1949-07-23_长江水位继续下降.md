Title: Yangtze River Water Level Continues to Fall
Author:
Date: 1949-07-23
Page: 1

　　Yangtze River Water Level Continues to Fall
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping Broadcast] Nanjing, 22nd – Water levels at ports along the Yangtze River basin, with the exception of Zhenjiang, continued to fall on the 21st. The water levels in Hankou and Nanjing both fell by 0.03 meters on the 21st compared to the 20th, Jiujiang fell by 0.05 meters, and Wuhu fell by 0.04 meters. In addition, the water level in Nanchang in the Gan River basin also fell by 0.07 meters on the 21st compared to the 20th.

### 长江水位继续下降

1949-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　长江水位继续下降
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京二十二日消息：长江流域沿江各口岸水位，除镇江以外，二十一日继续下降，汉口、南京两地，二十一日水位，都比二十日下降了零点零三公尺，九江下降了零点零五公尺，芜湖下降了零点零四公尺。此外，赣江流域南昌二十一日水位，也比二十日下落了零点零七公尺。
