Title: Qian Junrui Succeeds as Deputy Director of the North China Higher Education Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-07-19
Page: 2

    North China Higher Education Committee Deputy Director
    Qian Junrui Takes Office
    [This newspaper reports] The North China People's Government issued an order on the 16th, stating briefly: Zhou Yang, Deputy Director of the North China Higher Education Committee of this government, has requested to resign from the position of Deputy Director of the North China Higher Education Committee due to holding multiple positions and being extremely busy. This is hereby approved. The vacant position will be filled by Qian Junrui, Standing Committee Member of the North China Higher Education Committee.

### 华北高教委会副主委钱俊瑞接任

1949-07-19
第2版()
专栏：

    华北高教委会副主委
    钱俊瑞接任
    【本报讯】华北人民政府于十六日发布命令略称：本府华北高等教育委员会副主任委员周扬因身兼数职，忙碌异常，呈请辞去华北高等教育委员会副主任委员职务，特予照准，遗职任命华北高等教育委员会常务委员钱俊瑞接充。
