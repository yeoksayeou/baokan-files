Title: Beiping, Tianjin, Hankou, Nanjing: American News Offices Ordered to Cease Illegal Activities

Author: 
Date: 1949-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 26th - The Military Control Committees of the People's Liberation Army in Beiping, Tianjin, Wuhan, and Nanjing have ordered the American news offices in the four cities of Beiping, Tianjin, Hankou, and Nanjing to cease operations in recent days, as the American news offices are a part of the US State Department and the People's Government has not yet established diplomatic relations with the United States.

### 北平天津汉口南京　停止美新闻处非法活动

1949-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平天津汉口南京
  　停止美新闻处非法活动
    【新华社北平二十六日电】人民解放军北平、天津、武汉、南京军事管制委员会以美国新闻处是美国务院组织之一部分，而人民政府尚未与美国建立外交关系，已分别于近日命令北平、天津、汉口、南京四地美国新闻处停止活动。
