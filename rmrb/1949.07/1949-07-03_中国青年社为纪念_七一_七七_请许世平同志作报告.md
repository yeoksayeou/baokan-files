Title: China Youth Society Commemorates "July 1st" and "July 7th" by Inviting Comrade Xu Shiping to Give a Report
Author:
Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 2

　　China Youth Society Commemorates "July 1st" and "July 7th"
  　Invites Comrade Xu Shiping to Give a Report
    [Ping City News] To commemorate "July 1st" and "July 7th," the China Youth Society has scheduled Comrade Xu Shiping, head of the Youth and Agriculture Department of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, to give a report on "The Youths Who Persisted in the Struggle Behind Enemy Lines During the War of Resistance" at the Xiehe Auditorium on Dongdan Santiao this afternoon (3rd) at 3:00 PM. Workers, students, and youths from all walks of life are welcome to attend.

### 中国青年社为纪念“七一”“七七”　请许世平同志作报告

1949-07-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国青年社为纪念“七一”“七七”
  　请许世平同志作报告
    【平市讯】中国青年社为纪念“七一”与“七七”，订于今日（三日）下午三时假东单三条协和礼堂请中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会青农部部长许世平同志报告“抗战中坚持敌后斗争的青年们”，欢迎工人学生及各界青年参加。
