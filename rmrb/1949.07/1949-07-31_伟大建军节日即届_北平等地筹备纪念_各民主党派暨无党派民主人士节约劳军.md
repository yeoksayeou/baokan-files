Title: Great Army Day Approaching, Beijing and Other Places Prepare for Commemoration; Democratic Parties and Non-Party Democrats Practice Thrift to Support the Army
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-07-31
Page: 1

　　Great Army Day Approaching, Beijing and Other Places Prepare for Commemoration
    Democratic Parties and Non-Party Democrats Practice Thrift to Support the Army
    [This newspaper dispatch] Beijing is enthusiastically preparing to commemorate "August 1st" Army Day. The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and 27 units including various democratic parties and organizations have formed a preparatory committee for the commemoration of "August 1st" Army Day. They will send representatives to participate in the military parade and flag awarding ceremony of the North China Military Region, and visit wounded and sick soldiers of the People's Liberation Army in various hospitals. On August 2nd, the municipal government will host a reception for the families of martyrs, soldiers, and veterans in Beijing. A certain unit of the Beijing Garrison held a preliminary inspection on the Dongdan parade ground on the 27th. The political department of the division decided to launch a wall newspaper exhibition and entertainment performance activities in the company units from August 1st to 4th, with the division as the unit. At the same time, in order to solemnly accept the military flag and military emblem, a vow has been formulated, expressing resolute support for the Military Commission's "June 15th" and the North China Military Region's "June 16th" instructions, and striving to build a completely regularized national defense force.
    [This newspaper dispatch] In order to celebrate the 22nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on "August 1st," Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Huang Yanpei (Deng Yunhe acting), Guo Moruo, Ma Xulun (absent due to illness), Peng Zemin, Shi Liang, Tan Pingshan, Cai Tingkai, Chen Qiyou and other leaders of ten democratic parties and non-party democratic individuals, including the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the Chinese Democratic League, the China Democratic National Construction Association, non-party democratic individuals, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, the China National Salvation Association, the San Min Chu I Comrades Association, the China Kuomintang Democratic Promotion Association, and the China Zhi Gong Party, held a symposium at the Beijing Hotel on the 28th of this month to discuss commemorating "August 1st" and launching a thrift-supporting-the-army movement. The meeting resolved: (1) To send a congratulatory telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. (2) To present a flag to the Chinese People's Liberation Army to pay tribute. (3) To organize a thrift-supporting-the-army movement committee, and appoint Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Guo Moruo, Peng Zemin, Tan Pingshan, Cai Tingkai and other seven people as members. And formulate specific measures to respond to the thrift movement. (4) The thrift-supporting-the-army movement committee will call on democratic individuals living in guest houses such as the Beijing Hotel to save three catties of millet per person per day from August 1st to 3rd, and donate one-half of their monthly pocket money for August to be used for army support expenses.
　　(Bai Sheng)
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th -- "August 1st" is approaching. Nanjing and Wuhan have both launched enthusiastic army support movements, and large-scale army support movements in Shanghai are continuing. The Nanjing Municipal Women's Federation is actively promoting the "letter-writing" army support movement. Workers from nine factories, including the China Agricultural Machinery Company and the Wireless Electric Factory, voluntarily donated materials and salaries to support the army. Students from fifteen universities and colleges, including Central University and the Military and Political University, have planned to save food and launch a 10,000-signature army support movement. Five organizations including the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League and clerks from seven major department stores including Central and Wing On are holding army support competitions. Staff members of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League will donate half of their July allowance to support the army. All members of the Nanjing Industrial and Commercial Economic Research Association donated one day's income to support the army. The Wing On Department Store decided to use all profits for two days from "August 1st" to support the army. In addition to mobilizing every woman to write letters of support, the preparatory committee of the Hankou Women's Federation is also organizing women's laundry teams to wash clothes for soldiers. Female employees of units such as the Pinghan Railway, the Post Office, and the Telecommunications Bureau are rushing to make condolence bags, and they are also mobilizing relatives and friends to participate in army support. All employees of the China Petroleum Company donated RMB 2.62 million and other large quantities of daily necessities for army support at the employees' congress on the 25th in just a few minutes. Citizens have set up tea stations on both sides of major roads in the city to entertain passing People's Liberation Army soldiers. The Shanghai army support movement has been going on for more than ten days. As of the 26th, the amount of money donated by people from all walks of life has reached RMB 70.4 million, and it is continuing enthusiastically.

### 伟大建军节日即届　北平等地筹备纪念  各民主党派暨无党派民主人士节约劳军
柏生
1949-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　伟大建军节日即届
  　北平等地筹备纪念
    各民主党派暨无党派民主人士节约劳军
    【本报讯】平市热烈筹备纪念“八一”建军节。北平中共市委与各民主党派及各机关团体共二十七单位组成纪念“八一”建军节筹备会，将派出代表参加华北军区阅兵授旗仪式，及慰问各医院解放军伤病员，八月二日将由市府招待平市烈、军属和荣属。平市警卫部队某部，二十七日于东单操场举行预检，师政治部决定八月一日至四日，以师为单位，展开连队墙报展览和文娱表演活动。同时，为严肃接受军旗军徽，已制定誓词，表示坚决拥护军委“六一五”及华北军区“六一六”训令，为建设成一支完全正规化的国防部队而努力。
    【本报讯】为了庆祝“八一”中国人民解放军建军二十二周年纪念，中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主同盟、民主建国会、无党派民主人士、中国民主促进会、中国农工民主党、中国人民救国会、三民主义同志联合会、中国国民党民主促进会、中国致公党等十个民主党派及无党派民主人士负责人李济深、沈钧儒、章伯钧、黄炎培（邓云鹤代）、郭沫若、马叙伦（因病缺席）、彭泽民、史良、谭平山、蔡廷＠、陈其尤等十人，于本月二十八日在北京饭店召开座谈会，讨论纪念“八一”展开节约劳军运动。会上决议：（一）向中国共产党中央委员会电贺。（二）向中国人民解放军献旗致敬。（三）组织节约劳军运动委员会，推定李济深、沈钧儒、章伯钧、郭沫若、彭泽民、谭平山、蔡廷＠等七人为委员。并拟定具体办法响应节约运动。（四）由节约劳军运动委员会号召北京饭店等招待所所住的民主人士从八月一日起至三日止每日每人伙食节省小米三斤，并将八月份零用月费捐献二分之一，拨作劳军费用。
　　（柏生）
    【新华社北平三十日电】“八一”即届。南京、武汉均已掀起热烈的劳军运动，上海大规模的劳军运动正在继续进行。南京市妇联正积极推动“一封信”劳军运动。中国农业机械公司、有线电厂等九个工厂工人自动捐献物资、薪金劳军，中央大学、军政大学等十五个大中学校学生皆已有计划地进行节食和展开万人签名劳军运动。青年团南京市工委会等五机关团体及中央、永安等七大商场店员都举行劳军竞赛。青年团南京市工委会工作人员将每人七月份津贴的一半捐出劳军。南京工商经济研究会全体会员捐献一日所得劳军。永安商场各商店决定自“八一”起以两天的全部盈利劳军。汉口市妇联筹委会除发动每个妇女写劳军信外，并正组织妇女洗衣队为战士洗衣。平汉铁路、邮政局、电讯局等单位女职员纷纷赶制慰问袋，她们并发动亲友参加劳军。中国石油公司全体职工在二十五日职工大会上数分钟内即献出人民币二百六十二万元及其他大批日用品劳军。市民于市内各大道两旁设立茶水站，招待过路解放军。上海劳军运动已进行十余天，至二十六日，各界人民献金已收到者达七千零四十万人民币，现正继续热烈展开中。
