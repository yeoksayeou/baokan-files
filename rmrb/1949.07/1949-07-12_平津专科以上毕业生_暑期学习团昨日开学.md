Title: College and University Graduates in the Beijing-Tianjin Area: Summer Study Group Opens Yesterday
Author: Jin Feng
Date: 1949-07-12
Page: 2
    The Beijing-Tianjin Area College and University Graduates Summer Study Group Opens Yesterday

    [This Newspaper] The Summer Study Group for College and University Graduates in the Beijing-Tianjin area held its opening ceremony yesterday (the 11th) in the auditorium of Tsinghua University. Zhang Xiruo, Deputy Director of the North China Higher Education Commission; Feng Naichao, Director of the Study Group; Chao Zhefu, Minister of Education of the North China People's Government; Yu Li, Vice President of the Supervisory Yuan; Deng Chumin, member of the Higher Education Commission; Chen Yuan, President of Fu Jen Catholic University, and others attended and spoke. Zhang Xiruo encouraged all students to study Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought diligently, learn to cultivate a work style that integrates with the masses, and earnestly practice in their work. Feng Naichao explained in detail the Study Group's guidelines, tasks, study content, and employment issues. Deng Chumin, Yu Li, Chao Zhefu, Chen Yuan, and others spoke successively, unanimously encouraging the students to put down their intellectual sense of superiority, change their old views, establish a revolutionary outlook on life, devote themselves to the people's cause, and serve the people wholeheartedly.
    [Another News Item] Graduates from universities and colleges in Beijing and Tianjin are enthusiastically participating in the Summer Study Group. Since the 8th, nearly two thousand people have registered despite the rain. The Study Group uses small groups as the unit for study and life. The study period is one month. Initially, the main focus will be on studying Chairman Mao's "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," and responsible comrades from the Party and government will be invited to give lectures at the school. The teaching policy adopts the principles of free thought and democratic discussion, advocating a humble learning attitude and obedience to the truth, so that students can master the key to combining theory with practice, establish a life outlook of serving the people, and be able to withstand tempering in practical work. (Jin Feng)

### 平津专科以上毕业生　暑期学习团昨日开学
金凤
1949-07-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　平津专科以上毕业生
  　暑期学习团昨日开学
    【本报讯】平津专科以上学校毕业生暑期学习团昨（十一）日在清华大学大礼堂举行开学典礼，华北高等教育委员会副主任委员张奚若，学习团主任委员冯乃超，华北人民政府教育部长晁哲甫，监察院副院长于力，高教会委员邓初民，辅仁大学校长陈垣等均出席讲话。张奚若勉励全体学员要努力学习马列主义和毛泽东思想，学习培养与群众结合的工作作风，认真地在工作中实践。冯乃超详细说明学习团的方针、任务、学习内容与就业问题。邓初民、于力、晁哲甫、陈垣等先后讲话，一致鼓励学员们要放下知识分子优越感，改变旧观点，建立革命人生观，投身到人民事业中去，全心全意为人民服务。
    【又讯】平津各大学及专科以上学校毕业生，踊跃参加暑期学习团。自八日起，冒雨报到者已近二千人。学习团以小组为学习、生活的单位。学习时间一个月。开始时主要学习毛主席的“论人民民主专政”，并将请党政负责同志来校讲演。教学方针采取自由思想、民主讨论的原则，提倡虚心学习、服从真理的态度，使学员能掌握理论与实践结合的钥匙，建立为人民服务的人生观，以便到实际工作中能经受锻炼。（金凤）
