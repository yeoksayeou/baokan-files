　　"Strive for Lasting Peace and People's Democracy"
  　Issues 34 and 35 are available.
    Xinhua Bookstore and New China Bookstore are distributors.
    The English and Russian versions of the organ of the Information Bureau of the Communist and Workers' Parties, "Strive for Lasting Peace and People's Democracy," Issues 34 and 35 (identical in content), have recently been sent to Beiping. This newspaper is the guiding publication of the international communist movement, a banner of internationalism, and a torch for the people's democratic forces worldwide in their struggle against imperialism and for lasting peace. Reading it allows one to understand how the forces of people's democracy, amidst the complex international situation, are calmly and firmly waging a heroic struggle for lasting peace and people's democracy. This newspaper is also the best reading material for studying and learning from the struggle experiences of communist parties in various countries. The newspaper is published semi-monthly, on the 1st and 15th of each month. The "May Day" issue is expected to arrive soon. All Xinhua Bookstores and New China Bookstores are distributors. Each copy is temporarily priced at 20 yuan in People's Currency.

### “争取持久和平与人民民主”　第三十四、五期运平  新华书店新中国书店代售

1949-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　“争取持久和平与人民民主”
  　第三十四、五期运平
    新华书店新中国书店代售
    共产党与工人党情报局的机关报“争取持久和平与人民民主”第三十四期和三十五期英文版俄文版两种（内容相同），最近已寄到北平。该报是国际共产主义运动的指导刊物，是国际主义的一面旗帜；是全世界人民民主力量反对帝国主义，争取持久和平的火炬。读了它可以了解在错综复杂的国际形势中，人民民主的力量是怎样沉着坚定地为争取持久和平与人民民主作着英勇的斗争。要想研究和学习各国共产党的斗争经验，该报也是最好的一种读物。该报每半月一期，逢一、逢十五日出版，预计“五一”号不久也可寄到，各地新华书店、新中国书局均有代售，每份暂售人民券二十元。
