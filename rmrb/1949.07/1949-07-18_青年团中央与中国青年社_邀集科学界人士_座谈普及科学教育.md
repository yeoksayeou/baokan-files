Title: Communist Youth League Central Committee and China Youth Press Invite Scientists to Discuss Popularizing Science Education
Author: Jin Feng
Date: 1949-07-18
Page: 2

    Communist Youth League Central Committee and China Youth Press
    Invite Scientists to Discuss Popularizing Science Education
    [Report from this newspaper] The Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and the "China Youth Press" invited scientists attending the preparatory meeting for the Chinese Science Conference on the evening of the 16th to discuss how to unite workers and peasants in construction, popularize science education, help young people study natural sciences, and publish a series of natural science pamphlets. Yuan Hanhqing, professor of the Chemistry Department of Peking University, Qian Weichang, professor of the Engineering Institute of Tsinghua University, Peng Qingzhao of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Yu Guangyuan of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, among others, spoke, all believing that to transform China from an agricultural country to an industrial country, it is necessary to popularize science education. The methods are to hold exhibitions, seminars, organize lectures, screen science films, organize trips, visits and internships, publish periodicals, compile and print series of books, and set up natural science clubs. The first step at present is to collect all published popular science readings, select and reprint them; natural science workers must also divide the labor and cooperate to translate and create new popular science series to provide "Chinese Youth" with scientific works. (Jin Feng)

### 青年团中央与中国青年社  邀集科学界人士  座谈普及科学教育
金凤
1949-07-18
第2版()
专栏：

    青年团中央与中国青年社
    邀集科学界人士
    座谈普及科学教育
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会宣传部与“中国青年社”于前（十六日）晚邀请出席中国科学会议筹备会的科学界人士，座谈如何团结工农建设，普及科学教育，帮助青年学习自然科学，和出版自然科学小丛书的问题。北大化学系教授袁翰青、清华工学院教授钱伟长、中华全国总工会彭庆昭、中共中央宣传部于光远等多人发言，都认为要使中国由农业国变为工业国，必须普及科学教育。方法是举办展览会、座谈会，组织讲演，放映科学电影，举办旅行、参观和实习，出版刊物，编印丛书，设置自然科学俱乐部，目前第一步是搜集所有已出版过的通俗科学读物，加以选印；自然科学工作者并须分工合作，翻译和创作新的通俗科学丛书，供给“中国青年”以科学作品。  （金凤）
