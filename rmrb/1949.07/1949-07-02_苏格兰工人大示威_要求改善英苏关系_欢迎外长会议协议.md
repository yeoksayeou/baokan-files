Title: Scottish Workers' Demonstration Demands Improvement of Anglo-Soviet Relations and Welcomes Foreign Ministers' Conference Agreement
Author:
Date: 1949-07-02
Page: 3

　　Scottish Workers' Demonstration
  　Demands Improvement of Anglo-Soviet Relations
  　Welcomes Foreign Ministers' Conference Agreement
    Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 1st – A Polish News Agency dispatch: Scottish workers recently held a large-scale demonstration in Glasgow (the largest industrial center in Scotland), demonstrating their desire for peace. The workers passed a resolution stating that improving Anglo-Soviet relations is a matter of fundamental importance to world peace, while the Labour government's policy of closely aligning with the American aggressive bloc is a threat to peace. The resolution welcomes the results of the Paris Foreign Ministers' Conference and calls on the Scottish people to fight for peace.

### 苏格兰工人大示威　要求改善英苏关系　欢迎外长会议协议

1949-07-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏格兰工人大示威
  　要求改善英苏关系
  　欢迎外长会议协议
    【新华社北平一日电】波兰通讯社讯：苏格兰工人新近在格拉斯哥（苏格兰最大的工业中心）举行大规模示威，表现了他们要求和平的意志。工人们通过决议说，改善英苏关系是对世界和平有根本重要性的问题，而工党政府与美国侵略集团密切结盟的政策，乃是对和平的威胁。决议欢迎巴黎外长会议的结果，并号召苏格兰人民为和平而斗争。
