Title: Xu Dabeng Appointed as Chief Military Representative of Kailuan Coal Mine
Author:
Date: 1949-07-15
Page: 2

　　Xu Dabeng
    Appointed as Chief Military Representative of Kailuan Coal Mine
    [This Newspaper] News: The North China People's Government and the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army issued an order on the 13th, appointing Xu Dabeng as the Chief Military Representative of the Kailuan Coal Mine. He will represent the North China People's Government and the North China Military Region in establishing close ties with the Kailuan Mining Bureau, supervising the production and sales operations of the Kailuan Coal Mine, overseeing the rational distribution of materials provided by the People's Government to Kailuan among the workers, and assisting the Kailuan Coal Mine in improving production methods, reducing production costs, and reforming unreasonable management systems, in order to achieve the goal of developing production.

### 徐达本任开滦煤矿总军事代表

1949-07-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　徐达本
    任开滦煤矿总军事代表
    【本报讯】华北人民政府与中国人民解放军华北军区十三日发布命令，派徐达本为开滦煤矿总军事代表，代表华北人民政府及华北军区与开滦矿务局建立密切联系，监督开滦煤矿生产、运销工作，监督人民政府援助开滦的物资在职工间的合理分配，协助开滦煤矿改进生产方法，降低生产成本，改革不合理的管理制度，以达发展生产之目的。
