Title: Manila Workers' Demonstration Protests Chiang Kai-shek's Visit to the Philippines, Denounces Quirino, Chiang, etc. as Lackeys of U.S. Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1949-07-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th. According to a France-Presse dispatch, a workers' delegation in Manila held a demonstration in front of the Philippine President's residence on the 11th, vehemently protesting Chiang Kai-shek's visit to the Philippines. The demonstrators held up various flags bearing slogans such as: "Quirino and Chiang Kai-shek are American Puppets," "Chiang Kai-shek is as Corrupt as Quirino," "Chiang Kai-shek, Quirino, Syngman Rhee, and Bảo Đại are lackeys of imperialism."

### 马尼拉工人大示威　抗议蒋贼访菲　痛斥季、蒋等是美帝走卒

1949-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　马尼拉工人大示威
  　抗议蒋贼访菲
  　痛斥季、蒋等是美帝走卒
    【新华社北平十二日电】据法新社电称，马尼拉的工人代表团十一日在菲总统寓邸门前举行示威，猛烈抗议蒋介石的访菲。示威者高举写着下述标语的各种旗帜：“季里诺和蒋介石是美国的傀儡”，“蒋介石象季里诺一样的腐败”，“蒋介石、季里诺、李承晚和保大是帝国主义的走卒。”
