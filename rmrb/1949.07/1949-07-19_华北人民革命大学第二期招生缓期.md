Title: North China People's Revolutionary University Second Enrollment Postponed
Author:
Date: 1949-07-19
Page: 1

    North China People's Revolutionary University Second Enrollment Postponed
    [This newspaper's news] It was previously reported that the North China People's Revolutionary University would enroll new students on the 20th of this month. According to the responsible comrade of the university, the second enrollment has been postponed due to reasons such as the first batch of students not having yet left the school.

### 华北人民革命大学第二期招生缓期

1949-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    华北人民革命大学第二期招生缓期
    【本报讯】前闻华北人民革命大学将于本月二十日招收新生，顷据该校负责同志谈，由于第一期学生尚未离校等原因，第二期招生决定缓期。
