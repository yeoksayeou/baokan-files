Title: National Film Association Established; Telegrams of Respect Sent to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the People's Liberation Army
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-07-27
Page: 1

　　National Film Association Established
  　Telegrams of Respect Sent to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the People's Liberation Army
    [Our Newspaper] The inaugural meeting of the All-China Film Art Workers Association was held yesterday morning (the 26th) at the Beijing Hotel. Following the opening address by Chairman Yang Hansheng, Mao Dun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, spoke, encouraging everyone to carry out film work effectively, reflect the great achievements of the people, especially workers, peasants, and soldiers, in revolutionary struggles and production construction through the art form of film. On the other hand, it is necessary to clear the reactionary film positions, study hard under Chairman Mao's direction for literature and art, complete the film construction tasks of New China, and record the growth of New China.
    Subsequently, the association's charter was discussed and approved, and the national committee members of the Film Association were elected, and proposals were discussed. Finally, the following temporary motions were passed: (1) To send a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu in the name of the conference; (2) To send a telegram to the frontline People's Liberation Army congratulating them on their victories; (3) To commemorate the three film photographers, Zhang Shaoke, Wang Jingan, and Yang Yinxuan, who sacrificed their lives in the final battle in the Northeast, a monument will be erected at the site of their martyrdom.
    [Another Dispatch] The Central Film Bureau hosted a banquet at the Beijing Hotel at 6:00 PM last night to entertain all the delegates of the Film Association and celebrate the establishment of the National Film Association.
　　　　　　　　　　　(Bai Sheng)

### 全国影协成立　电毛主席朱总司令及解放军致敬
柏生
1949-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国影协成立
  　电毛主席朱总司令及解放军致敬
    【本报讯】中华全国电影艺术工作者协会成立大会，于昨（二十六）日上午在北京饭店举行。在主席阳翰笙致开幕词之后，全国文联副主席茅盾讲话，他勉励大家好好开展电影工作，通过电影艺术形式，反映人民大众，特别是工农兵在革命斗争和生产建设中的伟大业迹。另一方面要肃清反动电影的阵地，在毛主席的文艺方向下，努力学习，完成新中国的电影建设任务，把新中国的成长记录下来。
    随后，讨论通过全国影协章程，选出影协全国委员和提案讨论。最后并通过临时动议：（一）以大会名义通电向毛主席朱总司令致敬；（二）通电向前线人民解放军祝捷；（三）为纪念在东北最后战役中牺牲的电影摄影师张少柯、王静安、杨荫萱三同志，在其殉难处，建立纪念碑。
    【又讯】中央电影管理局于昨晚六时在北京饭店设宴招待影协全体代表，并庆祝全国影协的成立。　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　（柏生）
