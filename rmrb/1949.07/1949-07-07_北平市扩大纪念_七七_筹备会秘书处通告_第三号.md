Title: Beiping City Expanded "July 7th" Memorial Preparatory Committee Secretariat Notice No. 3
Author:
Date: 1949-07-07
Page: 1

　　Beiping City Expanded "July 7th" Memorial Preparatory Committee Secretariat Notice
　　Third Notice

　　The city's expanded "July 7th" memorial will be held at 6:00 PM on July 7th at Tiananmen Square. It is hoped that all participating units will arrive on time. This is a special notice.
