Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Again Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-07-26
Page: 2
Economic News

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
  　Adjusted Again Starting Yesterday
    [Shanhaiguan News] The joint office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank in Shanhaiguan has adjusted the exchange rate to 1 RMB equaling 65 Northeast Yuan, effective July 25th.

### 山海关货币牌价　昨起再调整

1949-07-26
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　山海关货币牌价
  　昨起再调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处，自七月二十五日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券六十五元。
