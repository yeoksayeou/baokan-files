Title: Northeast Bank Loans Help Farmers with Summer Weeding

Author: 
Date: 1949-07-06
Page: 1

[Broadcast by the Xinhua Broadcasting Station in Beiping] Shenyang, 5th—The Northeast Bank is issuing loans to help farmers in new and disaster-stricken areas who are unable to carry out summer weeding due to a lack of food, fodder, and farm tools. The Northeast Bank's branches, sub-branches, and offices in Jilin, Liaoxi, Fushun, Zhengjiatun, and other places began issuing summer weeding loans in mid-June. By the end of June, a total of 4,320 tons of grain, 140 tons of bean cake fodder, 140 new-style weeding machines, and 630 million Northeast currency had been loaned out.

### 东北银行贷款帮助农民夏锄

1949-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北银行贷款帮助农民夏锄
    【北平新华广播电台广播】沈阳五日消息：东北银行发放贷款，帮助新区和灾区无力进行夏锄的农民解决口粮、马料、农具等困难。吉林、辽西、抚顺、郑家屯等地的东北银行分行、支行和办事处，从六月中旬起开始发放夏锄贷款，到六月底为止，一共贷出了粮食四千三百二十吨，马料豆饼一百四十吨，新式锄草机一百四十台，和东北币六万万三千万元。
