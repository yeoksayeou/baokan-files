Title: Jiaotong University Tangshan Institute of Technology Decides to Enroll Students in Beiping, Shanghai, Tangshan, and Hankou
Author: Chen Qi
Date: 1949-07-24
Page: 2

　　Jiaotong University Tangshan Institute of Technology
  　Decides to Enroll Students in Beiping, Shanghai, Tangshan, and Hankou
    [This Newspaper's Report] The Jiaotong University Tangshan Institute of Technology's Institute Affairs Committee has decided that this year it will enroll 520 new students, including 80 in the Civil Engineering Department, and 40 in each of the Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Architecture, Mining, and Metallurgy Departments. There will also be five specialized courses in Locomotive, Passenger & Freight Car, Signaling, Telecommunications, and Railway Engineering, with 40 students in each course. Examination centers will be set up in Beiping, Shanghai, Tangshan, and Hankou. The registration dates are from August 9th to 12th. The examination dates for Beiping and Tangshan are August 19th and 20th. Shanghai and Hankou are also planning to hold examinations at the same time. The school is also organizing a candidate service group, and students are enthusiastically signing up to participate. The group's introduction team has compiled "Introduction to Tangshan Institute," to help applicants choose departments and understand Tangshan Institute. It is scheduled to be published at the end of this month, ready to be sold to applicants during the enrollment period.
    (Chen Qi)

### 交大唐山工学院　决定在平沪唐汉招生
陈琦
1949-07-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　交大唐山工学院
  　决定在平沪唐汉招生
    【本报讯】中国交通大学唐山工学院经院务委员会决定：今年招收新生五百二十名，计土木系八十名，电机、机械、化工、建筑、采矿、冶金每系四十名，机车、客货车、号志、电讯、铁道工程五专修每科四十名。设北平、上海、唐山、汉口四考区，报名日期自八月九日至十二日，考试日期平、唐两地为八月十九、二十两日，上海、汉口亦拟同时招考。该校并组织考生服务团，报名参加之同学甚为踊跃，该团介绍组已编印了“唐院介绍”，帮助投考同学选系和认识唐院，定本月底出版，预备在招考时售与投考同学。
    （陈琦）
