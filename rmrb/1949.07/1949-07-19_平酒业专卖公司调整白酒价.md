Title: Ping Wine Monopoly Company Adjusts Baijiu Prices
Author:
Date: 1949-07-19
Page: 2

    Ping Wine Monopoly Company
    Adjusts Baijiu Prices
    [Ping City News] The Beiping Wine Monopoly Branch Company will adjust the price of baijiu (white alcohol), effective July 19th. The price is 450 yuan per jin (approximately 500g).

### 平酒业专卖公司调整白酒价

1949-07-19
第2版()
专栏：

    平酒业专卖公司
    调整白酒价
    【平市讯】北平酒业专卖分公司自七月十九日起调整白酒价格，每市斤售价四百五十元。
