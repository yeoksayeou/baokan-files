Title: Peking University, Tsinghua University, and Nankai University to Jointly Enroll Students
Author:
Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 1

【Ping Municipal News】 Peking University, Tsinghua University, and Nankai University will jointly enroll students this summer. There will be five test centers: Beijing, Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, and Shenyang. Registration will take place from August 1st to 5th, and examinations will begin on August 15th. The enrollment brochures for the three universities have been drafted and will be announced soon.

### 北大清华南开将联合招生

1949-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　北大清华南开将联合招生
    【平市讯】北大、清华、南开三大学本年暑期将联合招生，共设北平、天津、南京、上海、沈阳五个考区。八月一日至五日报名，八月十五日起考试。三校招生简章已拟定，日内即可公布。
