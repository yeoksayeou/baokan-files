Title: Zhang Ximan's Posthumous Poem
Author:
Date: 1949-07-12
Page: 4

　　Zhang Ximan's Posthumous Poem
    Thirty years of revolution for the people,
    Never speculated, never betrayed this heart;
    Regrettably in this new era,
    My life is taken by the demon of illness!
　　(Demon of illness refers to lung disease) (Attached image)
    Mr. Zhang Ximan's handwritten will

### 张西曼遗诗

1949-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　张西曼遗诗
    卅年革命为人民，
    绝未投机背此心；
    憾当今日新时代，
    却被么魔断我生！
　　（么魔指肺病）（附图片）
    张西曼先生亲笔遗嘱
