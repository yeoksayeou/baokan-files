Title: Working People of All Countries Mourn the Death of Dimitrov; All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Special Telegram of Condolence
Author:
Date: 1949-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 5th: Reports from various sources indicate that the working people of all countries are grieving the death of Dimitrov, the outstanding leader of the international working class.

　　In the Soviet Union, upon receiving the news, mourning flags were hung everywhere. On July 2nd, tens of thousands of Moscow workers automatically went to the factory squares after work to attend memorial services. After the meeting, they went to the Trade Union Building to pay their last respects to Dimitrov's remains. Many enterprises, institutions, and schools in Moscow held memorial services. On July 3rd and 4th, newspapers widely published Dimitrov's obituary, messages of condolence, and articles. Leningrad also held a memorial meeting attended by 15,000 people. Ukraine, Belarus, Azerbaijan, Moldova, the Donets Basin, the Urals, and many other places held separate memorial services.

　　In Milan, the delegates attending the Second World Federation of Trade Unions Congress immediately stood up to pay their respects on July 2nd when the chairman of the congress announced the death of Comrade Dimitrov.

　　In Poland, Bolesław Bierut, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party on July 2nd, stating: "Dimitrov's cause and creative ideas will forever live in the hearts of the working people around the world." The Polish government, trade unions, and other organizations sent telegrams of condolence. Polish newspapers published articles expressing the mourning of the Polish people.

　　In Czechoslovakia, on July 3rd, all newspapers published Dimitrov's obituary on the front page, as well as telegrams of condolence from the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party to the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and from Klement Gottwald and his wife to Dimitrov's wife, Rosa.

　　In Romania, newspapers published special articles of mourning on July 3rd. All newspapers added black borders and published Dimitrov's portrait, biography, and obituary, as well as telegrams of condolence.

　　In Austria, the Central Committee of the Austrian Communist Party immediately convened a memorial meeting after receiving the news of Dimitrov's death. The Central Committee of the Austrian Communist Party also sent a telegram of condolence to the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party.

　　The Communist Parties and working people of France and Denmark deeply mourned the death of Dimitrov. Newspapers in Finland, Egypt, and Pakistan reported the news of Dimitrov's death.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 5th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has sent a telegram to the Bulgarian National Trade Union, expressing condolences on the death of Dimitrov. The text of the telegram is as follows:
　　To Comrades of the Bulgarian National Trade Union:
　　We are shocked to learn of the death of our great workers' leader, Comrade Dimitrov, due to illness. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on behalf of all Chinese workers, expresses its deepest condolences. Comrade Dimitrov was the most loyal and outstanding fighter of our working class, and his death is a great loss to the working class worldwide! Throughout his life, he waged an unyielding struggle for the cause of the liberation of the working class, and made tremendous contributions to the development of the world workers' movement and the unity of the world's working class! His great achievements in the struggle for the liberation of the world working class will be immortal! His spirit of internationalism will always be our most glorious example! We send this telegram to express our condolences.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 5th

### 各国劳动人民　哀悼季米特洛夫逝世　中华全国总工会特电致唁

1949-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　各国劳动人民
  　哀悼季米特洛夫逝世
  　中华全国总工会特电致唁
    【新华社北平五日电】各方报道：世界各国劳动人民悲痛地追悼国际工人阶级卓越领袖季米特洛夫的逝世。
    在苏联，各地劳动人民得到消息后，到处悬起哀旗。七月二日，数万莫斯科工人在下工后，自动到工厂的广场参加追悼会，会后到职工会大厦最后瞻仰季米特洛夫遗容。莫斯科许多企业、机关、学校，都举行了追悼会。七月三日、四日，各报广泛刊登季米特洛夫的讣闻、悼念的消息与论文。列宁格勒也举行了有一万五千人参加的追悼大会。乌克兰、白俄罗斯、阿塞拜疆、摩达维亚、顿尼兹盆地、乌拉尔和其他许多地方都分别举行了追悼会。
    在米兰，出席世界工联第二届代表大会的代表们，七月二日听到大会主席宣布季米特洛夫同志逝世的消息，立即起立致哀。
    在波兰，统一工人党中央委员会主席贝鲁特七月二日致电保共中央称：季米特洛夫的事业和创造性的思想，将永远活在全世界劳动人民的心里。波兰政府、职工会等团体都发出唁电。波兰各报都著论表示波兰人民的哀悼。
    在捷克斯洛伐克，七月三日，各报头版都刊登季米特洛夫逝世的讣闻以及捷共中央致保共中央、哥特瓦尔德及其夫人致季米特洛夫夫人罗沙等唁电。
    在罗马尼亚，七月三日各报发表悼念的专文。各报都加上黑边刊登季米特洛夫的遗像、传略和讣闻及唁电。
    在奥地利，奥共中央委员会在接获季米特洛夫逝世的消息后，立即召开了追悼会。奥共中央并电唁保共中央。
    法国和丹麦的共产党和劳动人民都沉痛地哀悼季米特洛夫的逝世。芬兰、埃及、巴基斯坦的报纸都报道季米特洛夫逝世的消息。
    【新华社北平五日电】中华全国总工会顷致电保加利亚全国总工会，电唁季米特洛夫逝世，电文如下：保加利亚全国总工会诸同志：
    惊悉我们伟大的工人领袖季米特洛夫同志因病逝世。中华全国总工会代表全中国工人表示沉痛的哀悼。季米特洛夫同志是我们工人阶级最忠诚的优秀的战士，他的逝世实是全世界工人阶级的重大损失！他一生始终为工人阶级的解放事业进行百折不挠的斗争，对世界工人运动的发展与世界工人阶级的团结，尽了巨大的贡献！他为世界工人阶级解放斗争的伟大业迹，将永垂不朽！他的国际主义的精神永远成为我们最光辉的榜样！特电致唁。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国总工会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　七月五日
