Title: Celebration Gathering Held by the CPC Beiping Municipal Committee on the Eve of "July 1st"
Author: 
Date: 1949-07-01
Page: 2

　　“July 1st” Eve
  　The CPC Beiping Municipal Committee Holds Celebration Gathering
    [This Newspaper's Report] On the eve of the 28th anniversary of the Party's birth, the CPC Beiping Municipal Committee invited cadres from the Municipal Committee, Military Control Commission, and Municipal Government organs to a celebration "July 1st" dinner party. Leaders from the North China Bureau, the North China People's Government, and the North China Military Region, as well as responsible persons from the Beiping Municipal Branch of the China Democratic League, the Beiping Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, the September 3rd Society, the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the University Faculty Association, the Middle and Primary School Teachers' Union, the Beiping Municipal Student Federation, and organizations such as the Women's Federation, the Youth Federation, and the Communist Youth League, were invited to attend. Hosts and guests were extremely pleased.

### “七一”前夕　中共北平市委举行庆祝联欢

1949-07-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　“七一”前夕
  　中共北平市委举行庆祝联欢
    【本报讯】在党的诞生二十八周年纪念前夕，中共北平市委会邀请市委、军管会、市府机关干部举行庆祝“七一”聚餐联欢，并请华北局、华北人民政府、华北军区各领导同志与民盟北平市支部、国民党革命委员会北平市支部、九三学社、市职工总会、大学教职联、中小学教联、北平市学联及妇联、青联、青年团等团体负责人参加，宾主极为欢洽。
