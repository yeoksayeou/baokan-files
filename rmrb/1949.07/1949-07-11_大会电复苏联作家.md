Title: Telegram from the Congress in Reply to Soviet Writers

Author:
Date: 1949-07-11
Page: 2

To the Soviet Writers' Association, forwarded to: Fadeyev, Sholokhov, Simonov, Tikhonov, Vishnevsky, Surkov, Sofronov, Gorbatov, Panfilov, Leonov, Korniychuk, Kochetov:

　　We were extremely pleased and excited to receive your congratulatory telegram. The great victory of the Chinese revolution is the result of the long-term struggle of the Chinese people, and the forces of peace and democracy around the world have also given us great assistance. This victory has brought us all the superior conditions to develop literature and art. We are determined, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, to strive to create literature and art that serve the people, and to fight for the establishment of an independent, free, democratic, and happy New China. The literary and artistic workers of the Soviet Union have undergone long-term revolutionary training and possess rich working experience. We are willing to learn from you, stand with you, and strengthen unity with the people of the world to jointly strive for the comprehensive victory of peace and democracy throughout the world. We extend our brotherly respect to you.

　　National Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers, July 10th

### 大会电复苏联作家

1949-07-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　大会电复苏联作家
    苏联作家协会转：法捷耶夫、萧洛霍夫、西蒙诺夫、吉洪诺夫、维什涅夫斯基、苏尔可夫、索佛罗诺夫、戈尔巴托夫、潘菲洛夫、列昂诺夫、考尔纳楚克、考席夫尼可夫：
    接到你们的贺电，我们感到非常的愉快和兴奋。中国革命的伟大胜利是中国人民长期奋斗的结果，全世界的和平民主力量也给我们以很大的援助。这个胜利给我们带来了开展文学艺术的一切优越条件。我们决心在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导之下。努力创造为人民服务的文艺，为建立独立、自由、民主、幸福的新中国而奋斗。苏联的文学艺术工作者，已经经过了长期的革命＠炼，拥有丰富的工作经验，我们愿意向你们学习，和你们站在一起并和全世界的人民加紧团结，共同争取全世界的和平民主的全面胜利。谨以兄弟的情谊向你们致敬。
    中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会　七月十日
