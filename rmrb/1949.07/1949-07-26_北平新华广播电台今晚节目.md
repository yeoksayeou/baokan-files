Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-26
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [Our Dispatch] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast two articles during the youth time at 7:30 PM tonight: 1. What Youth League members should know about open Party membership; 2. The establishment of the League must be public. At 7:50 PM, Professor Hu Jingfu of Yenching University will give a lecture on "The Common Knowledge Women Should Have About Obstetrics and Gynecology." At 10:20 PM, the Northeast Lu Xun Academy of Arts and Literature Music Troupe will perform the Liaoning Drum ballad "The Explosion Hero Dong Cunrui".

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点三十分青年时间，播送两篇文章：一、青年团员对公开党应有的认识，二、建团必须公开。十九点五十分演讲节目，由燕京大学教授胡经甫先生讲“妇女对于妇产科应有的常识”。二十二点二十分文艺节目，由东北鲁迅文艺学院音乐工作团演唱辽宁大鼓“爆炸英雄董存瑞”。
