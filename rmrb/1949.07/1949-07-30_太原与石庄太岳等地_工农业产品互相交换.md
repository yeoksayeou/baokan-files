Title: Taiyuan, Shijiazhuang, Taiyue and other places exchange industrial and agricultural products

Author:
Date: 1949-07-30
Page: 2

　　Economic News
　　Taiyuan and Shijiazhuang, Taiyue and other places
  　Exchange of industrial and agricultural products
    [Taiyuan Electric] In order to regulate materials and stabilize prices, the Taiyuan Municipal Trading Company has begun to exchange materials with Shijiazhuang, Taiyue and other places. Earlier this month, the company signed a cotton-for-cloth contract with Shijiazhuang Trading Company. It was determined that the Taiyuan Municipal Trading Company would exchange 20,000 bolts of white cloth from Shijiazhuang Trading Company for 552,333 jin of raw cotton. The cotton has now begun to be shipped to Shijiazhuang. At the same time, the Taiyuan Municipal Trading Company also negotiated with the Taiyue Trading Company to exchange more than 1,800 bolts of cloth and 600 boxes of matches produced in Taiyuan, supplemented by more than 5.8 million yuan in RMB, for 545,000 jin of wheat, 100,000 jin of millet, 220,000 jin of corn, and 93,000 jin of soybean oil from the Taiyue Trading Company. Both parties have now begun to deliver the goods. Part of the cloth has been shipped, and more than 200,000 jin of grain has been shipped to Taiyuan. In addition, in order to ensure the grain supply in Taiyuan, the Municipal Trading Branch recently purchased 1 million jin of wheat in Shijiazhuang.

### 太原与石庄太岳等地　工农业产品互相交换

1949-07-30
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　太原与石庄太岳等地
  　工农业产品互相交换
    【太原电】太原市贸易公司为了调剂物资、平稳物价，已开始与石家庄、太岳等地交换物资。本月上旬，该公司与石庄贸易公司订立以棉换布合同。确定太原市贸易公司以原棉五十五万二千三百三十三斤，交换石家庄贸易公司白布二万匹，现棉花已开始运往石市。与此同时，太原市贸易公司又与太岳贸易公司商妥以太原所产的布一千八百余匹、火柴六百箱，辅以人民币五百八十余万元交换太岳贸易公司小麦五十四万五千斤，小米十万斤，玉茭二十二万斤，豆油九万三千斤。现双方已开始交货，布匹已运走一部，粮食已运到太原二十余万斤。此外为了保证太原市粮食供给，市贸易分司最近在石市购到小麦一百万斤。
