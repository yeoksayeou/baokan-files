Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 1

　　【This newspaper reports】Tonight's program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station, from 19:50 to 20:10, will feature a speech by Mr. Shen Junru, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, entitled "Commemorating 'July 7th' with the Principles of the Potsdam Agreement" (simultaneously rebroadcast by the Beiping People's Broadcasting Station). From 19:30 to 19:50, the Workers' Time will broadcast "Workers of the Whole Country Unite and Strive to Build an Industrialized New China." From 22:20 to 22:50, the Arts and Literature program will broadcast recordings of workers' songs from Lushun-Dalian, Northeast China.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，由中国民主同盟中央常务委员沈钧儒先生广播演讲，题目是：“以波茨坦协定原则纪念‘七七’”（北平人民广播电台同时转播）。十九点三十分到十九点五十分职工时间播送“全国职工团结起来为建设工业化的新中国而奋斗”。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，播送东北旅大工人歌曲录音。
