Title: Music Circles to Hold Meeting Commemorating the Twelfth Anniversary of the Death of Nie Er
Author:
Date: 1949-07-17
Page: 1

    Nie Er's Death Twelfth Anniversary
    Music Circles to Hold Meeting Commemorating
    [This newspaper's dispatch] July 17th marks the twelfth anniversary of the death of Nie Er, pioneer of new music. National delegates to the Literary and Art Congress and music circles in Peiping will jointly hold a commemoration meeting. The Northeast Cultural and Educational Team is also scheduled to hold a music evening at the Dahua Theater at 7:30 pm on the 17th. The program includes the "March of the Volunteers", "Ode to the Chinese Communist Party", and the opera "Achieving Merit." A special commemorative publication will also be released.

### 聂耳逝世十二周年音乐界将开会纪念

1949-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    聂耳逝世十二周年
    音乐界将开会纪念
    【本报讯】七月十七日为新音乐开路者聂耳逝世十二周年纪念，全国文代会音乐代表、平音乐界联合举行纪念会，东北文教队亦定于十七日晚七时半在大华戏院举行音乐晚会，节目有义勇军进行曲、中国共产党颂等及歌剧“立功”，并出纪念特刊。
