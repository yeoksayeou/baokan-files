Title: Remnant Bandits, Devoid of Humanity, Create Flood Disaster; Send Planes to Bomb Anhui River Dike Again, Strafe Anqing Riverside Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1949-07-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, 23rd—The Kuomintang bandit planes once again indiscriminately bombed the river dikes along the Yangtze River in Wangjiang, southern Anhui, and the flood victims suffering from the disaster. On the morning of the 18th, a Kuomintang Canadian Mosquito aircraft sneaked over the suburbs of Anqing City and strafed more than ten locations of flood victims along the river. On the afternoon of the same day, seven Kuomintang bombers sneaked into the area of ​​Sige Village in Hudong, Wangjiang County, and the Yangtze River dike in Dongliu County, killing four flood victims and injuring four crew members. The flood victims along the river all hate the Kuomintang bandit gang's crime of indiscriminately bombing river dikes, creating flood disasters, and being devoid of humanity.

### 残匪灭绝人性制造水灾　派机再炸皖南江堤扫射安庆沿江灾民

1949-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　残匪灭绝人性制造水灾
  　派机再炸皖南江堤扫射安庆沿江灾民
    【新华社合肥二十三日电】国民党匪机再度滥炸皖南望江一带沿江江堤及遭受水灾的灾民。十八日上午，匪加拿大蚊式机一架，窜至安庆市郊上空，扫射沿江十余处灾民。同日下午，又有匪轰炸机七架，窜望江县境湖东泗葛村附近及东流县境的江堤，共炸死灾民四人，伤船员四人。沿江灾民，对国民党匪帮此种滥炸江堤、制造水灾、灭绝人性的罪行，莫不切齿痛恨。
