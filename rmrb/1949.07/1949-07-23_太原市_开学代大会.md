Title: Taiyuan City Opens Student Representative Assembly
Author:
Date: 1949-07-23
Page: 1

　　Taiyuan City
　　Opens Student Representative Assembly
    [Taiyuan Electricity] The first student representative assembly in Taiyuan City after liberation grandly opened on the 16th of this month. One hundred student representatives participated in the assembly, representing 7,868 students from thirteen universities and middle schools across the city. To celebrate the opening of the assembly, various factories, government organizations, and groups presented many banners and congratulatory couplets. At the assembly, Chairman Zhao Zhongqi briefly and clearly explained the significance of the assembly. Afterwards, Lai Ruoyu, Secretary of the Taiyuan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, delivered a speech. After describing the great significance of the student movement in history, he particularly pointed out that the student movement must be combined with workers and peasants in order to exert greater strength. In studies, it is necessary to establish the idea of serving the workers and peasants.

### 太原市　开学代大会

1949-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　太原市
  　开学代大会
    【太原电】解放后的太原市学生首次代表大会，于本月十六日隆重揭幕。参加大会的学生代表一百人，代表全市十三个大、中学的七千八百六十八名同学。为庆祝大会的召开，各工厂、机关、团体赠送了许多锦旗及贺联。大会上，主席赵钟奇简短而明确的阐明大会的意义后，由中共太原市委书记赖若愚讲话，他在叙述了历史上学生运动的重大意义之后，特别指出学生运动必须和工农结合起来，才能发挥更大的力量。在学习中必须树立为工农服务的思想。
