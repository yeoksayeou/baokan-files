Title: Ping Trade Company to Export First Batch of Alpaca Wool in Exchange for Imported Kerosene
Author: Cheng Jie
Date: 1949-07-07
Page: 2
　　Economic News

　　Ping Trade Company
  　Exporting First Batch of Alpaca Wool to Exchange for Imported Kerosene

    [This newspaper reports] In order to meet the needs of the people, the Municipal Trade Company is striving to import kerosene in exchange for wool and camel hair. Today (the 6th), the Municipal Company reached a deal with an export merchant for the first time, involving 26,000 jin of wool and 12,000 jin of camel hair (products of Inner Mongolia, with a total value of 13.8 million yuan). After the wool is exported, it can be exchanged for kerosene to be used for lighting in rural areas. This trade will continue in the future.
　　　　　　　　　　　(Cheng Jie)

### 平贸易公司　输出首批驼羊毛将换取煤油入境
程捷
1949-07-07
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　平贸易公司
  　输出首批驼羊毛将换取煤油入境
    【本报讯】市贸易公司为了解决群众需要，以羊毛、驼绒争取煤油入境。市公司于今（六日）首次以羊毛两万六千斤、驼毛一万二千斤（为内蒙产品，共值一千三百八十万元），与出口商人达成交易。羊毛出境后，即可换回煤油，以供农村燃用。此项贸易今后将继续进行。　　
　　　　　　　　　　　（程捷）
