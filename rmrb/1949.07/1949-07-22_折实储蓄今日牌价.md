Title: Savings Deposits Linked to Real Value Today's Exchange Rate
Author: 
Date: 1949-07-22
Page: 2

Economic News

　　Savings Deposits Linked to Real Value Today's Exchange Rate
    The People's Bank of Peiping Branch Savings Department announced that the standard unit price for savings deposits linked to real value for today (July 22nd) is 453.38 yuan.
