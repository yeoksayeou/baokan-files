Title: Military Commission's Railway Department Takes Over Tangshan Jiaotong University
Author:
Date: 1949-07-17
Page: 2

    Military Commission's Railway Department
    Takes Over Tangshan Jiaotong University
    [Tangshan Electricity] The Railway Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission formally took over Tangshan Jiaotong University on the 13th, renaming it Tangshan Institute of Engineering of China Jiaotong University. The departments and programs of the former North China Jiaotong College belonging to the Institute of Engineering will also be merged into the institute. At 9:00 a.m. on the 13th, all teachers and students excitedly participated in the takeover ceremony. Comrade Gu Xi, the representative of the Railway Department, pointed out that the future educational policy is: 1. To implement the New Democratic education policy; 2. To combine intellectuals with workers and peasants; 3. To promote science, seek truth from facts, and break superstitions; 4. The new education should be combined with politics and serve politics. Tang Zhenxu, a member of the institute's council, said that under the corrupt rule of the Kuomintang, the school had failed to develop normally for more than 40 years; in the future, as a people's university, it will have a bright future.

### 军委铁道部接管唐山交大

1949-07-17
第2版()
专栏：

    军委铁道部
    接管唐山交大
    【唐山电】中国人民革命军事委员会铁道部，十三日正式接管唐山交通大学，定名为中国交通大学唐山工学院。原华北交通学院属于工学院的科系，也将并入该院之内。十三日上午九时，全体师生兴奋地参加接管典礼，铁道部接管代表顾稀同志指出今后的教育方针是：一、贯彻执行新民主主义的教育方针；二、知识分子与工农群众相结合；三、提倡科学，实事求是，破除迷信；四、新的教育应结合政治，为政治服务。该院院务委员唐振绪说，在国民党的腐败统治下，该校四十多年来始终未能得到正常的发展；今后成为人民的大学，将有光明的发展前途。
