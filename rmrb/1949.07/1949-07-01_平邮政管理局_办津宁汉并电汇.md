Title: Beiping Postal Administration Handles Telegraphic Transfers to Tianjin, Nanjing, Wuhan, and Taiyuan
Author:
Date: 1949-07-01
Page: 2

Economic News

　　Beiping Postal Administration Handles Telegraphic Transfers to Tianjin, Nanjing, Wuhan, and Taiyuan
    [This City News] The Beiping Postal Administration has now established telegraphic transfer services with Tianjin, Nanjing, Hankou, and Taiyuan. The remittance limit is temporarily set at 200,000 yuan per transaction. Telegraphic transfer services to other cities in the Liberated Areas will be gradually opened after negotiations are completed, for the convenience of the public. (Beiping Postal Administration Public Service Group)

### 平邮政管理局　办津宁汉并电汇
北平邮政管理局公众服务组
1949-07-01
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　平邮政管理局　办津宁汉并电汇
    【本市讯】北平邮政管理局现已与天津、南京、汉口、太原四地互通电汇，汇款限额每次暂定二十万元；至解放区其他各城市俟洽妥后亦将陆续开办电汇，以利公众。　（北平邮政管理局公众服务组）
