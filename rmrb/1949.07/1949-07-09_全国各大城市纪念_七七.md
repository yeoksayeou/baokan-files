Title: National Major Cities Commemorate "July 7th"

Author:

Date: 1949-07-09
Page: 1

　　National Major Cities Commemorate "July 7th"
    [Comprehensive Dispatch from This Newspaper] Party, government, military, and civilian circles in various cities across the country gathered on July 7th to commemorate the 12th anniversary of the "July 7th" Anti-Japanese War:
    　　　　Shanghai
    On the 6th, one million military personnel and civilians in Shanghai held the largest parade and demonstration ever seen in Shanghai. Starting at 4:00 PM that day, Generals Chen Yi, Rao Shushi, and Su Yu first inspected the troops in front of the former port headquarters at the end of North Sichuan Road. On the evening of the 7th, a grand gathering was held to commemorate the 12th anniversary of "July 7th" and celebrate the liberation of Shanghai. Over a thousand people attended, including General Chen Yi, General Rao Shushi, representatives of the New Political Consultative Conference Preparatory Committee Deng Yingchao, Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, Xu Guangping, and representatives from worker, youth, and women's mass organizations. Madame Sun Yat-sen, Soong Ching-ling, also attended. General Chen Yi, General Rao Shushi, and Ms. Soong Ching-ling spoke at the meeting successively. They pointed out that the victory of the "July 7th" Anti-Japanese War tells imperialists around the world that if anyone dares to attempt to invade and oppress us, they will certainly be defeated by the Chinese people. Zhu Junxin, a representative of the workers, called on Shanghai workers to strengthen unity under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, and together with people from all walks of life, overcome difficulties and build a new Shanghai. Mr. Chen Shutong explicitly pointed out that the Chinese Communist Party was the first to propose the words "Anti-Japanese." Mr. Huang Yanpei proposed paying tribute to the workers and peasants who contributed the most to the Anti-Japanese War and the anti-imperialist struggle, and paying tribute to the Chinese Communist Party, Chairman Mao, and the People's Liberation Army who led the Chinese people in their victorious struggle. The conference passed three telegrams to Chairman Mao, various democratic parties, various people's organizations, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De and all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army, and to the wounded and sick comrades who were gloriously injured in the revolutionary war.
    Nanjing
    To commemorate the 12th anniversary of "July 7th" and celebrate the liberation of major cities in China, Nanjing citizens enthusiastically held a grand parade of 300,000 people on the 7th. In the afternoon, a commemorative meeting was held. Mayor Liu Bocheng called on the citizens at the meeting to support the People's Liberation Army's advance towards the southwest and southeast, work hard in production, and complete the construction of a new democratic and new China.
    Shenyang
    On the morning of the 7th, 300,000 people from all walks of life in Shenyang held grand meetings in five districts to commemorate the 12th anniversary of "July 7th." Li Fuchun, Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Lin Feng, Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee, Wu Xiuquan, Chief of Staff of the Northeast Military Region, and Zhu Qiwen, Mayor of the Shenyang People's Government, spoke successively at the central venue, unanimously pointing out the great significance of commemorating "July 7th" this year. They also called on the people of Northeast China to unite closely with the people of all China and the democratic camp led by the Soviet Union to drive out American imperialist forces, swiftly sign a peace treaty with Japan with the participation of representatives from the New China, eliminate Japanese militarism, realize the democratization of Japan, and build a new China. The meeting passed telegrams of tribute to Marshal Stalin, to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Deputy Commander-in-Chief Peng, and all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army, and three telegrams to the New Political Consultative Conference Preparatory Committee, various democratic parties, various people's organizations, and compatriots from all walks of life. After the meeting, a grand parade and demonstration were held.
    Tianjin
    The commemorative meeting in Tianjin was held on the morning of the 7th, with over 2,100 representatives from all walks of life attending. Over 20,000 young workers, students, and soldiers gathered and celebrated that evening, and held a torch parade.
    Hangzhou
    The grand parade of 100,000 people in Hangzhou to commemorate "July 1st" and "July 7th" was held on the evening of the 6th. When the combat-hardened infantry, artillery, and motorized soldiers of the Zhejiang Military Region marched in formation through the streets, citizens rushed to give them flowers, and cheers were heard all along the way. Illuminated by the giant torches of the Railway Corps, the teams of various mass organizations, led by the Federation of Trade Unions, merged with the army and paraded together, the procession stretching for more than ten miles, with slogans and songs shaking the mountains and lakes.

### 全国各大城市纪念“七七”

1949-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国各大城市纪念“七七”
    【本报综合报导】全国各地党政军民各界均于七月七日集会纪念“七七”抗日战争十二周年：
    　　　　上海
    上海一百几十万军民，六日举行了上海前所未有的最大一次的游行示威。当日下午四时起，陈毅、饶漱石、粟裕三将军，首先在北四川路底旧港口司令部前检阅部队。七号晚上举行盛大集会，纪念“七七”十二周年和庆祝上海解放。到会的有陈毅将军、饶漱石将军，新政协筹备会代表邓颖超、黄炎培、陈叔通、许广平等，和工人、青年、妇女等群众团体代表一千多人。孙夫人宋庆龄也出席参加。陈毅将军、饶漱石将军、宋庆龄女士在会上相继讲话。他们指出：“七七”抗战胜利的事实，告诉了全世界帝国主义者，如果谁敢企图侵略和压迫我们，一定会被中国人民打败下去。职工代表朱俊欣号召：上海工人要在中国共产党和毛主席领导下，加紧团结，并且和各界人民一起，克服困难，建设新上海。陈叔通先生明确指出“抗日”两个字，是中国共产党首先提出来的。黄炎培先生提出向在抗日战争和反帝斗争中出力最多的工人、农民致敬，向领导中国人民进行胜利斗争的中国共产党毛主席和人民解放军致敬。大会通过了给毛主席、各民主党派、各人民团体，给人民解放军朱总司令和全体指战员和给革命战争中光荣负伤的伤病员同志的三个通电。
    南京
    南京市民，为了纪念“七七”十二周年和庆祝中国各大城市解放，七日狂热的举行三十万人的大游行。下午举行纪念大会，刘伯承市长在大会上号召市民拥护解放军向西南和东南进军，努力生产，完成新民主主义新中国的建设。
    沈阳
    沈阳市党政军民各界三十万人，七号上午分别在五个区隆重举行纪念“七七”十二周年大会，中共中央东北局副书记李富春、东北行政委员会林枫主席、东北军区伍修权参谋长、沈阳人民市政府朱其文市长，在中心会场先后讲话，一致指出今年纪念“七七”的伟大意义，并且号召全东北人民紧密的和全中国人民以及以苏联为首的民主阵营团结起来，为驱逐美国主义势力，在新中国代表参加下迅速签订对日和约，肃清日本军国主义，实现日本民主化和建设新中国而奋斗。会上通过向斯大林大元帅致敬，向毛主席、朱总司令、彭副总司令和人民解放军全体指战员致敬，给新政协筹备会各民主党派、各人民团体和各界同胞三个通电。大会结束后，举行了盛大的游行示威。
    天津
    天津的纪念大会在七日晨举行，到各界代表二千一百多人。青年工人、学生和战士两万多人，当晚集会庆祝并进行火炬大游行。
    杭州
    杭州十万人纪念“七一”和“七七”的大游行在六日晚举行，当浙江军区的久经战斗的步兵、炮兵、摩托兵列队经过大街时，市民争着给他们插花，沿途欢呼不绝。在铁路总队的巨大火炬照耀下，以职工总会为前导的各群众团体的队伍，和军队汇合起来，一同游行，队伍长达十余里，口号和歌声，震动山湖。
