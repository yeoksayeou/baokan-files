Title: 27th Anniversary of the Founding of the Japanese Communist Party Commemorated by Party Organizations at All Levels
Author:
Date: 1949-07-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, July 17th: According to a Reuters dispatch from Tokyo: July 15th was the 27th anniversary of the founding of the Japanese Communist Party. The JCP's national headquarters and local branches held commemorative ceremonies. On the same day, Shiga Yoshio, member of the JCP Political Bureau and member of the House of Representatives, published an article in the JCP's organ "Akahata" stating: "Our party is now stronger than ever before. The 27-year history of our party proves that our party can withstand the attacks of any reactionaries."

### 日共成立廿七周年  各级党组织均举行纪念

1949-07-18
第3版()
专栏：

    日共成立廿七周年
    各级党组织均举行纪念
    【新华社北平十七日电】据路透社东京消息：七月十五日为日本共产党成立二十七周年纪念日。日本共产党全国总部及各地支部举行纪念仪式。同日，日共政治局委员、众院议员志贺义雄在日共机关报“赤旗报”上发表论文称：“我党现在已空前强大。我党二十七年的历史，证明我党能够抵挡任何反动派的攻击。”
