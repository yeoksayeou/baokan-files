Title: Taiyuan Federation of Trade Unions to be Established on August 1st
Author:
Date: 1949-07-12
Page: 1

    Taiyuan Federation of Trade Unions
    To be Established on August 1st
    [Taiyuan Telegraph] The Taiyuan Office of the North China Federation of Trade Unions held its fourth standing committee meeting on June 28th and decided to convene the first workers' representative conference on August 1st to formally establish the Taiyuan Federation of Trade Unions.

### 太原职工总会将于八一产生

1949-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　太原职工总会
  　将于八一产生
    【太原电】华北职工总会太原办事处，于六月二十八日召开第四次常委会，决定于八月一日召开首届职工代表大会，正式产生太原职工总会。
