Title: North African Committee for Freedom Protests to Truman, Condemns Including Algeria in Aggression Pact
Author:
Date: 1949-07-12
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 11th: TASS, New York, 8th: The North African Committee for Freedom has issued a petition to Truman, condemning the arbitrary inclusion of Algeria in the North Atlantic Treaty by the United States. The petition declares: The French government suppresses the public opinion of Algeria. The people of Algeria paid a heavy price in the two world wars to pursue the promises of freedom and happiness to the colonies during the wars, but these sacrifices were in vain. The governments of some European colonies, despite talking about peace, still adopt a cruel policy of conquest and selfish exploitation of hundreds of millions of people in Asia and Africa. The North Atlantic Treaty unjustifiably supports the violent conquest of colonial peoples.

### 北非争自由委会向杜鲁门提抗议  谴责把阿尔及利亚列入侵略公约

1949-07-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　北非争自由委会
  　向杜鲁门提抗议
    谴责把阿尔及利亚列入侵略公约
    【新华社北平十一日电】塔斯社纽约八日讯：北非争取自由委员会，顷发表给杜鲁门的请愿书，谴责美国专横地将阿尔及利亚包括在北大西洋公约之内。该请愿书宣称：法国政府压抑阿尔及利亚的民意。阿尔及利亚的人民在两次大战中，付出了很大的代价，以追求大战期间答应给殖民地自由与幸福的诺言，但是，这些代价是白付了。欧洲一些殖民地的政府，尽管口谈和平，却仍然采用残酷地征服和自私地剥削亚非二洲千百万人民的对外政策。北大西洋公约毫无理由地支持对殖民地人民的强暴征服。
