Title: Art Exhibition Held by National Congress of Literary and Art Workers Opens
Author: Jin Feng
Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 2

　　The Art Exhibition Held by the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers Opens
    [Report from this newspaper] The art exhibition held by the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers officially opened yesterday (the 2nd) at the Art College. The exhibited works include art materials such as pictorials, window decorations, paper cuttings, cartoons, woodcuts, New Year pictures, comic strips, oil paintings, traditional Chinese paintings, peep shows, and sculptures. Music and drama materials include playbills, performance statistics, and stage models from drama troupes, cultural work troupes, and new Peking opera troupes in the Liberated Areas, the Kuomintang-controlled areas, and overseas. Literary materials include works and translations. Eighteen classrooms were arranged for the exhibition. Yesterday, on the first day, there were more than 1,300 visitors, including some delegates to the Congress of Literary and Art Workers, and mostly cultural and educational personnel, students, and office workers. The exhibition will last for half a month, from the 2nd to the 16th.
    (Jin Feng)

### 全国文代大会举办的艺术作品展览会开幕
金风
1949-07-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国文代大会举办的艺术作品展览会开幕
    【本报讯】中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会举办的艺术作品展览会昨（二日）在艺专正式揭幕，展览作品有美术资料，包括画报、窗花、剪纸、漫画、木刻、年画、连环画、油画、国画、洋片、雕塑等。音乐戏剧资料包括解放区国统区和海外的话剧团、文工团、新平剧团体的剧照、演出统计和舞台模型等。文学资料包括作品和翻译。共布置了十八个教室，昨日第一日参观者有一千三百余人，有一部分文代会代表，其余多为文教人员、学生和机关职员。展览日期将为半月，自二日起至十六日止。
    （金风）
