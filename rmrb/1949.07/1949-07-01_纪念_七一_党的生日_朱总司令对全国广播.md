Title: Commemorating the "July 1st" Anniversary of the Party's Founding: Commander-in-Chief Zhu's Broadcast to the Nation
Author:
Date: 1949-07-01
Page: 2

　　To commemorate the "July 1st" Anniversary of the Party's Founding
  　Commander-in-Chief Zhu's Broadcast to the Nation
    [Our Dispatch] Tonight, from 19:50 to 20:10, the Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station's program will feature Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army broadcasting to the nation in commemoration of the 28th anniversary of the Party's founding. From 20:45 to 21:30, Mao Zedong's essay "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," written in commemoration of the 28th anniversary of the Chinese Communist Party, will be broadcast. From 22:20 to 22:50, the cultural program will feature songs commemorating July 1st and other songs. The revolving program from 19:30 to 19:50 tonight is sponsored by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and will broadcast a comprehensive report entitled: "The Working Class Must Cherish the Property of the People's Motherland as They Cherish Their Own Lives." From 20:10 to 20:25, the music program will broadcast a medley of military songs from the Liberated Areas.

### 纪念“七一”党的生日　朱总司令对全国广播

1949-07-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　纪念“七一”党的生日
  　朱总司令对全国广播
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目是中国人民解放军朱德总司令为纪念党的二十八周年生日，对全国广播。二十点四十五分到二十一点三十分播送毛泽东主席为纪念中国共产党二十八周年而写的论文：“论人民民主专政”。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目：演唱纪念七一等歌曲。十九点三十分到十九点五十分轮回节目，今晚是由中华全国总工会主办的职工时间，播送一篇综合报导，题目是：“工人阶级要象爱护自己生命一样来爱护人民祖国的财产”。二十点十分到二十点二十五分音乐节目，播送解放区军歌联奏。
