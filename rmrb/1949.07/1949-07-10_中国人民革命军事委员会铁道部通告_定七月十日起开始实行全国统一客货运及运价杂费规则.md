Author:

Circular of the Railway Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission
    Implementing Unified National Passenger and Freight Transport and Fare Miscellaneous Fee Rules Starting July 10th

    The unified national passenger and freight transport rules and fare miscellaneous fee rules will be implemented starting July 10th, 1949. The main points are explained below for public information.

    A. Passenger Transport Relations:

    I. Railways to Pay Penalties to Passengers for Delayed Delivery of Luggage and Parcels

    1. For each day of delay in delivering luggage, a penalty of 2/30 of the fare will be paid.

    2. For each day of delay in delivering parcels, a penalty of 1/30 of the fare will be paid.

    II. The compensation standard for loss or damage to luggage is based on the market price of 14.4 square chi (Chinese feet) of white cloth (machine-woven) per kilogram as the maximum compensation amount.

    III. A tapering rate system (递远递减制) is implemented on all routes nationwide. The following examples compare the ticket price for 850 kilometers and the freight price for 300 kilograms of parcels with and without the tapering rate system:

    IV. Passengers without tickets, holding invalid tickets, or traveling in a car class or train not indicated on their ticket will be required to pay the fare for the traveled distance, plus a fine of 300 People's Yuan (人民币) if found in an ordinary passenger car, 600 People's Yuan if found in a second-class soft-seat car, and 1,200 People's Yuan if found in a first-class soft-seat car. If found on an express train, an additional express train fine of 230 People's Yuan will be levied in addition to the above fines.

    V. Between the Northeast and the North, limited to the Shenshan Line (沈山线) in the Northeast and the Beining Line (北宁线) in the North (i.e., between Ping and Shen), passengers purchasing tickets or consigning luggage within the customs area must use People's Yuan, and those outside the customs area must use Northeast Yuan.

    VI. Passenger tickets are divided into ordinary tickets, second-class soft-seat tickets, and first-class soft-seat tickets.

    VII. The ordinary ticket price is 4 yuan per person per kilometer. The second-class soft-seat ticket price is twice the ordinary ticket price, and the first-class soft-seat ticket price is four times the ordinary ticket price.

    VIII. Ordinary sleeper ticket prices are 640 People's Yuan per berth per night for the upper berth, 820 People's Yuan per berth per night for the middle berth, and 1,090 People's Yuan per berth per night for the lower berth.

    Second-class soft-seat sleeper ticket prices are calculated by adding one times the ordinary sleeper ticket price per berth per night. First-class soft-seat sleeper ticket prices are calculated by adding two times the ordinary sleeper ticket price per berth per night.

    IX. Express train ticket prices are calculated according to the following regulations, regardless of class:

### 中国人民革命军事委员会铁道部通告  定七月十日起开始实行全国统一客货运及运价杂费规则

1949-07-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国人民革命军事委员会铁道部通告
    定七月十日起开始实行全国统一客货运及运价杂费规则
    自公历一九四九年七月十日起实行全国统一的客货运规则及运价杂费规则兹将其要点说明如下，以便周知。
    甲、客运关系：
    一、行李包裹交付迟延铁路向客商支付罚款
    １、行李每交付迟延一日按运价的三十分之二支付罚款
    ２、包裹每交付迟延一日按运价的三十分之一支付罚款
    二、行李因损失或毁坏的赔偿标准每公斤按一四、四平方市尺白布（机器织）折合的市价为赔偿最高额
