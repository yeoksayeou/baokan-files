Title: Literary and Art Congress Performs Drama and Movies to Entertain All Delegates
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-07-20
Page: 1
Performances of drama and film at the Literary and Art Congress entertain all delegates.

[This newspaper report] The theatrical and film performance program of the Literary and Art Congress includes newly arranged performances: (1) Mei Lanfang's "Farewell My Concubine," will be performed at the Chang'an Grand Theater on the evening of the 25th to entertain all delegates. On the 24th, it will entertain various performing arts troupes. (2) On the 22nd at 2:00 PM, film and drama workers from the Southern First and Second Delegations will perform three short plays at the National Grand Theater to entertain all delegates. (3) Movies from the Kuomintang-controlled areas will be screened at the Majestic Theater from the 21st to the 28th, every day at 9:30 AM, to entertain the delegates. The films include "The True Story of Ah Q", "Hope in the World", "Night Inn", "The Spring River Flows East" (Parts 1 and 2), "Eight Thousand Li of Cloud and Moon", and "Reminiscences of Jiangnan."
(Bai Sheng)

### 文代大会演出戏剧电影招待全体代表
柏生
1949-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    文代大会演出戏剧电影
    招待全体代表
    【本报讯】文代大会的戏剧与电影的演出节目，新排的有：（一）梅兰芳“霸王别姬”，二十五日晚在长安大戏院演出招待全体代表。二十四日招待各演出文工团。（二）二十二日下午二时南方第一、第二代表团电影戏剧工作者在国民大戏院演出三个短剧招待全体代表。（三）国统区电影自二十一日至二十八日每日上午九时半在美琪影院演出，招待代表。有“祥林嫂”、“希望在人间”、“夜店”、“一江春水向东流”上下集、“八千里路云和月”、“忆江南”等。  （柏生）
