Title: USSR, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Finland Sign One-Year Delivery Agreement
Author:
Date: 1949-07-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th: TASS, Moscow: Following successful negotiations, the governments of the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Finland signed a one-year delivery agreement here on June 29th. According to these agreements, Finland will export prefabricated wooden houses, small boats, and other goods worth 100 million rubles to the Soviet Union; the Soviet Union will supply Poland with grain worth 80 million rubles, and Czechoslovakia with grain worth 20 million rubles, totaling 100 million rubles; Poland will import coal worth 80 million rubles to Finland, and Czechoslovakia will import sugar, machinery, and manufactured goods worth 20 million rubles to Finland. These agreements expand trade between the signatory countries on mutually beneficial terms and expand the bilateral agreements previously signed between these countries.

### 苏波捷芬四国　签订一年交货协定

1949-07-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏波捷芬四国
  　签订一年交货协定
    【新华社北平六日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克与芬兰等国政府，经过圆满的谈判，已于六月二十九日在此间签订了一年交货协定。按照这些协定，芬兰将向苏联输出价值一亿卢布的活动房屋木材、小船和其他物品；苏联将供给波兰价值八千万卢布的粮食，供给捷克斯洛伐克二千万卢布的粮食，共值一亿卢布；波兰将以价值八千万卢布的煤输入芬兰，捷克斯洛伐克将以价值二千万卢布的糖、机器与制成品输入芬兰。这些协定是在互利的条件下扩大签约国之间的贸易，并且扩大了以前这些国家之间所签订的双边协定。
