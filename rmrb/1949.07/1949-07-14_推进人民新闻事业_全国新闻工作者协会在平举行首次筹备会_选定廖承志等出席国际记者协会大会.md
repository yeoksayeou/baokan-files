Title: Promoting the People's Journalism Cause: All-China Journalists Association Holds First Preparatory Meeting in Beiping; Liao Chengzhi and Others Selected to Attend International Organization of Journalists Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, July 13th: The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association held its first plenary meeting in Beiping today (the 13th). The attending preparatory committee members included Wang Kangzhi, Bao Zhijing, Li Chunqing, Li Huang, Wu Lengxi, Meng Qiujiang, Lin Zhongyi, Hu Qiaomu, Hu Yuzhi, Xu Ying, Xu Maijin, Chen Kehan, Chen Hanbo, Pu Xixiu, Mei Yi, Zhang Panshi, Huan Xiang, Peng Zigang, Liao Chengzhi, Liao Mosha, Liu Zunqi, Sa Kongliao and other twenty-nine people. Guests included representatives from the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and the China Democratic League, as well as over thirty people from the press in Beiping, Tianjin, Shanghai, Shenyang, Xi'an, and other places.

　　The meeting began with an opening address by Chairman Hu Qiaomu. He pointed out that due to the victorious development of the People's Liberation War, national liberation is about to be realized soon. The conditions for establishing a national organization for people's journalists are now ripe. He said: Our preparatory committee's task is to prepare for the convening of the All-China Journalists Congress and establish the All-China Journalists Association, to promote the national people's journalism cause and serve the construction of a new democratic New China. After the speech, Hu Yuzhi, on behalf of the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee, reported on the process of the establishment of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association. The meeting then discussed the brief regulations of the preparatory committee and focused on discussing the scope of journalists, the tasks of the preparatory committee, and organizational issues. The meeting considered that to facilitate the progress of the preparatory work, the preparatory committee could establish branch associations in various places as needed. The meeting approved that the All-China Journalists Congress and the number of delegates would be 200, including representatives from newspaper staff, news agency staff, radio station staff, current affairs periodical staff, newsreel production staff, photojournalists, journalism education staff, and staff of news organizations at all levels of government, most of whom would be selected from the six major liberated areas (North China, Northeast China, East China, Northwest China, Central China, South China), the municipalities directly under Beiping and Tianjin, the municipalities directly under Shanghai and Nanjing, and the four field armies. Representatives from overseas areas, areas awaiting liberation, and other aspects would be invited by the preparatory committee to participate. The congress is tentatively scheduled to be held in Beiping in January 1950. During the meeting, a congratulatory telegram was received from the International Organization of Journalists, and the entire venue applauded warmly when the chairman read the telegram. The meeting unanimously approved applying to join the International Organization of Journalists and selected Liao Chengzhi, Chen Kehan, and Peng Zigang as the Chinese representatives to attend the International Organization of Journalists Congress to be held in September.

### 推进人民新闻事业　全国新闻工作者协会在平举行首次筹备会  选定廖承志等出席国际记者协会大会

1949-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　推进人民新闻事业
  　全国新闻工作者协会在平举行首次筹备会
    选定廖承志等出席国际记者协会大会
    【新华社北平十三日电】中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备会，于今（十三）日在平举行第一次全体会议。出席筹备委员王亢之、包之静、李纯青、李荒、吴冷西、孟秋江、林仲易、胡乔木、胡愈之、徐盈、徐迈进、陈克寒、陈翰伯、浦熙修、梅益、张磐石、宦乡、彭子冈、廖承志、廖沫沙、刘遵棋、萨空了等二十九人，来宾有中国国民党革命委员会及中国民主同盟的代表和北平、天津、上海、沈阳、西安等地的新闻界人士三十余人。
    会议首由主席胡乔木致开会词。他指出由于人民解放战争的胜利发展，全国解放很快就要实现。成立人民新闻工作者的全国机构的条件已经成熟。他说：我们筹备会的任务，就是筹备召开中华全国新闻工作者代表大会，成立中华全国新闻工作者协会，以推进全国的人民新闻事业，为新民主主义新中国的建设服务。词毕，胡愈之代表筹备会常委会报告中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备会产生的经过。会议接着讨论了筹备会简章，并着重地讨论了新闻工作者的范围、筹备委员会的任务和组织等问题。会议认为：为便利筹备工作的进行起见，筹备会可根据实际需要在各地建立分会。会议通过中华全国新闻工作者代表大会及代表名额为二百名，包括在报纸工作人员、通讯社工作人员、广播台工作人员、时事期刊工作人员、新闻电影著作人员、摄影记者、新闻教育工作人员、各级政府新闻机构工作人员等的代表，其中大部分将由六大解放区（华北、东北、华东、西北、华中、华南）、平津直属市、沪宁直属市以及四个野战军选出，而在海外区、待解放区及其他方面，则将由筹备会聘请代表若干人参加。大会召开时间暂定一九五○年一月，地点在北平。会议在进行中接到国际记者协会的贺电，全场于主席宣读贺电时热烈鼓掌。会议一致通过申请参加国际记者协会，并选定廖承志、陈克寒、鼓子冈三人为出席九月间举行的国际记者协会大会的中国代表。
