Title: Savings Deposit Linked to Real Value Today's Rate

Author: 
Date: 1949-07-24
Page: 2

Economic News

　　Savings Deposit Linked to Real Value Today's Rate
    The standard unit price for savings deposits linked to real value at the Savings Department of the People's Bank of Beiping Branch today (24th) is 504.71 yuan.
