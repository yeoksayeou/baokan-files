Title: Minister of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Union Issues Order Celebrating Air Force Day, Calls for Further Consolidating the Power of the Motherland's Air Force
Author:
Date: 1949-07-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 17th: Moscow Dispatch: Today is Soviet Air Force Day. The Minister of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Union, Marshal Vasilevsky, issued the following order:

    Comrades Pilots and Navigators, Gunners and Radio Operators, Mechanics and Engineers, Workers of the Aircraft Manufacturing Industry! Comrades Soldiers, Sergeants, Junior Officers, Senior Officers, and Generals of the Army and Navy!

    Today, the Soviet people and their armed forces are celebrating Soviet Air Force Day, and celebrating the new achievements of the Soviet Air Force in fulfilling the tasks put forward by our great leader and teacher, Comrade Stalin.

    On this national holiday—Soviet Air Force Day—I extend my respects and congratulations to you.

    I expect all personnel of the glorious Soviet Air Force and all workers of the aircraft manufacturing industry to achieve further success in consolidating the air power of our motherland.

    To celebrate Air Force Day, I order: Today, July 17, 1949, a twenty-gun salute will be fired in Moscow, the capital of our motherland, and in the capitals of the various Union Republics.

    Long live the Soviet Air Force! Long live the Soviet Armed Forces! Long live our powerful motherland! Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union! Long live our victorious inspirer and organizer—the great Stalin!

### 苏联武装部队部部长  颁发命令庆祝空军节  号召进一步巩固祖国空军威力

1949-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联武装部队部部长
    颁发命令庆祝空军节
    号召进一步巩固祖国空军威力
    【新华社北平十七日电】莫斯科讯：今天是苏联空军节。苏联武装部队部部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基特发布命令如下：
    飞行员和领航员、射击手和无线电服务员、机械士和工程师、飞机制造工业工人同志们！陆海军战士、陆海军士官、陆海军尉官、校官和将军同志们！
    今天，苏联人民和他们的武装部队，正在庆祝苏联空军节，并庆祝苏联空军在履行我们伟大的领袖和导师斯大林同志所提出的任务当中的新成就。
    当此全国性节日——苏联空军节之际，我谨向你们致敬并向你们致贺。
    我期望光辉的苏联空军全体人员和飞机制造工业的全体工人，在巩固我们祖国的空军威力中获得进一步的成功。
    为了庆祝空军节，我命令：今天，一九四九年七月十七日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科及各加盟共和国首都，各鸣礼炮二十响。
    苏联空军万岁！苏联武装部队万岁！我们强大的祖国万岁！苏联共产党万岁！我们胜利的鼓舞者与组织者——伟大的斯大林万岁！
