Title: Shanhaiguan Bank Office Currency Exchange Rate
Author:
Date: 1949-07-17
Page: 2

    Shanhaiguan Bank Office
    Currency Exchange Rate
    [Shanhaiguan News] The joint office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank in Shanhaiguan has changed its exchange rate from July 16th to one RMB yuan equals one hundred Northeast yuan.

### 山海关银行办事处货币兑换牌价

1949-07-17
第2版()
专栏：

    山海关银行办事处
    货币兑换牌价
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自七月十六日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券一百元。
