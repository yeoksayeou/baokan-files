Title: Stalin Receives British Ambassador
Author: 
Date: 1949-07-20
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th -- TASS reports: On July 18th, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Stalin, received the British Ambassador, Sir David Kelly. The Soviet Foreign Minister, Vyshinsky, was also present.

### 斯大林接见英大使

1949-07-20
第3版()
专栏：

    斯大林接见英大使
    【新华社北平十九日电】塔斯社报道：七月十八日，苏联部长会议主席斯大林，接见英大使大卫·凯莱爵士，苏外长维辛斯基亦在座。
