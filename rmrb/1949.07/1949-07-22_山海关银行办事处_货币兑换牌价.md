Title: Shanhaiguan Bank Office Currency Exchange Rates
Author:
Date: 1949-07-22
Page: 2

　　Economic News

　　Shanhaiguan Bank Office
  　Currency Exchange Rates
    [Shanhaiguan News] The joint office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank in Shanhaiguan has changed its exchange rate since July 21 to one RMB equaling seventy Northeast Yuan.

### 山海关银行办事处　货币兑换牌价

1949-07-22
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　山海关银行办事处
  　货币兑换牌价
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行，东北银行山海关联合办事处自七月二十一日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券七十元。
