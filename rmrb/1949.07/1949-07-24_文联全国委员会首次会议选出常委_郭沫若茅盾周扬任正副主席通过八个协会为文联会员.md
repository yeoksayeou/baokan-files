　　The First Meeting of the National Committee of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Elects Standing Committee Members
    Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, and Zhou Yang Appointed as Chairman and Vice-Chairmen; Eight Associations Approved as Members of the Federation
    [This newspaper's report] The National Committee of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, elected by the First Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers, held its first meeting at the Beijing Hotel yesterday (23rd) at 3:00 PM. Sixty-four members attended, chaired by Mao Dun. The meeting first elected Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, Ding Ling, Zheng Zhenduo, Xiao San, Shakov, Xia Yan, Tian Han, Ke Zhongping, Zhao Shuli, Ouyang Yuqian, Ma Sicong, Zhang Zhixiang, Yuan Muzhi, Xu Beihong, Yang Hansheng, Li Bozhao, Liu Zhiming, Hong Shen, and Cao Yu as the 21 members of the Standing Committee, and elected Guo Moruo as Chairman, and Mao Dun and Zhou Yang as Vice-Chairmen. The conference also approved the list of heads of various departments of the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles: Secretary-Generals: Shakov, Huang Yaomian, Zhou Weizhi; Liaison Department: Xiao San, Feng Naichao, Ye Qianyu; Editorial Department: Ding Ling, Cao Yu, He Qifang; Welfare Department: Zheng Zhenduo, Yang Hansheng, Jiang Feng; Guidance Department: Ke Zhongping, A Ying, Zhang Zhixiang. After listening to reports on the preparation and establishment of the eight associations for literature, drama, film, music, fine arts, dance, opera reform, and folk art reform, the conference exchanged views on issues such as the organization of each association and their interrelationships, and finally approved each of these associations as members of the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles. The meeting also decided to assign specific personnel to be responsible for preparing for the Northeast Tour Group and the Troops Tour Group, in order to meet the enthusiastic demands of literary and art workers, especially those in the newly liberated areas, to go to the old liberated areas and to the masses. (Ji, Lin)

### 文联全国委员会首次会议选出常委  郭沫若茅盾周扬任正副主席通过八个协会为文联会员
纪林
1949-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　文联全国委员会首次会议选出常委
    郭沫若茅盾周扬任正副主席通过八个协会为文联会员
    【本报讯】由中华全国文学艺术工作者第一次代表大会选出之中华全国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会，昨天（二十三）下午三时在北京饭店召开第一次会议。出席委员六十四人，由茅盾主席。会议首先选举郭沫若、茅盾、周扬、丁玲、郑振铎、萧三、沙可夫、夏衍、田汉、柯仲平、赵树理、欧阳予倩、马思聪、张致祥、袁牧之、徐悲鸿、阳翰笙、李伯钊、刘芝明、洪深、曹禺等二十一人为常务委员并推选出郭沫若任主席，茅盾、周扬任副主席，大会又通过了全国文联各部负责人名单，秘书长：沙可夫、黄药眠、周巍峙，联络部：萧三、冯乃超、叶浅予，编辑部：丁玲、曹禺、何其芳，福利部：郑振铎、阳翰笙、江丰。指导部：柯仲平、阿英、张致祥。大会听取文学、戏剧、电影、音乐、美术、舞蹈、戏曲改革、曲艺改革等八个协会的筹备及成立经过报告后，就各协组织及相互关系等问题交换了意见，最后通过各该协会为全国文联会员。会上并决定指定专人负责筹备东北参观团、部队参观团事宜，以满足文艺工作者，特别是新解放区文艺工作者到老解放区去、到群众中去的热烈要求。（纪、林）
