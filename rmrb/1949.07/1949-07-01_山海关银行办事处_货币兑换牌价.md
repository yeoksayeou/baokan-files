Title: Shanhaiguan Bank Office Currency Exchange Rates
Author:
Date: 1949-07-01
Page: 2

　　Shanhaiguan Bank Office
  　Currency Exchange Rates
    [Shanhaiguan News] The exchange rate of the Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank in Shanhaiguan on June 28th has been changed to 1 yuan of Renminbi for 150 yuan of Northeast currency or Great Wall currency.
    (Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank in Shanhaiguan)

### 山海关银行办事处　货币兑换牌价

1949-07-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关银行办事处
  　货币兑换牌价
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处六月二十八日牌价，改为人民币一元兑换东北币或长城币一百五十元整。
    （人民银行东北银行山海关联合办事处）
