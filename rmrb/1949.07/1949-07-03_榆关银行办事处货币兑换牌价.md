Title: Exchange Rates at the Yuguan Bank Office

Author:
Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 2

　　Exchange Rates at the Yuguan Bank Office
    [Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank has adjusted the exchange rate between Renminbi and Northeast Currency to 1:120 yuan, effective from July 3rd.

### 榆关银行办事处货币兑换牌价

1949-07-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　榆关银行办事处货币兑换牌价
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处，自七月三日起调整人民币与东北币兑换牌价为一比一二○元。
