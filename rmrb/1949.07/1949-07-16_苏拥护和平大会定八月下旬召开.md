Title: Soviet Peace Congress to be Held in Late August
Author:
Date: 1949-07-16
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th: Moscow dispatch: The Preparatory Committee of the Soviet Peace Congress recently held a meeting and decided to hold the Soviet Peace Congress in the Moscow Trade Union Hall from August 25th to 27th. Representatives from trade unions, women, youth and scientific organizations, writers' associations, cooperative federations, and other public organizations will attend the congress.

### 苏拥护和平大会定八月下旬召开

1949-07-16
第3版()
专栏：

    苏拥护和平大会
    定八月下旬召开
    【新华社北平十五日电】莫斯科讯：苏联拥护和平大会筹备委员会，最近举行会议，决定八月二十五日至二十七日在莫斯科职工会大厅举行苏联拥护和平大会。职工会、妇女、青年及科学团体、作家协会、合作社联合会及其他公众团体的代表，均将出席大会。
