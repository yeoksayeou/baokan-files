Title: Over Ten Thousand Jin of Inner Mongolian Camel Wool Being Transported to Zhangjiakou for Export
Author:
Date: 1949-07-05
Page: 2

[Chahar News] Since Tianjin adjusted its foreign exchange, making exports easier, the wool market has become more active. Prices are not high, and transactions are smooth. Zhangjiakou fur merchants have also sold a lot of the wool they had stored in Tianjin. Now, the Inner Mongolia office of the Chahar Province Fur Company has begun shipping goods to Zhangjiakou. Over ten thousand jin each of camel wool and coat wool have arrived in Zhangjiakou. After being baled in Zhangjiakou, they will be transported to Tianjin.

### 内蒙驼毛万余斤　运张准备出口

1949-07-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　内蒙驼毛万余斤
  　运张准备出口
    【察哈尔讯】自天津调整外汇，便于出口，绒毛市场转活跃，价格不高，成交顺利，张市皮毛商存津之绒毛也售出不少。现察省皮毛公司内蒙办事处开始向张垣启运，已有驼毛、套毛各万余斤抵张，在张扎包后运往天津。
