Title: Performing Arts Representatives Gather to Prepare for More Drum Song Creations
Author:
Date: 1949-07-13
Page: 2

    Performing Arts Representatives Gather
    Preparing to Create More Drum Songs
    [Ping City News] The performing arts representatives from various regions attending the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers, along with over sixty performers, gathered in Zhongshan Park on the 11th. Everyone unanimously agreed to create more popular works like drum songs to serve the broad masses of the people.

### 曲艺界代表集会准备多创作鼓词

1949-07-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　曲艺界代表集会
    准备多创作鼓词
    【平市讯】参加全国文代大会的各地曲艺界代表和演出的六十余艺人十一日在中山公园集会，大家一致同意以后多多创作鼓词等通俗作品，以此服务广大人民。
