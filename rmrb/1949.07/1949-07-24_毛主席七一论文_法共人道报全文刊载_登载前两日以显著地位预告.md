Title: Chairman Mao's July 1st Article Published in Full by the French Communist Party's L'Humanité; Announced with Prominent Placement Two Days Prior
Author:
Date: 1949-07-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 23rd: The French Communist Party's organ, "L'Humanité," published the full text of Chairman Mao's "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" and a photograph of Chairman Mao on the entirety of its third page on the 21st. For the two days prior, starting on the 19th, the newspaper had prominently announced on its first page, in the same column as news of the People's Liberation Army launching another offensive, that Chairman Mao's important work would be published.

### 毛主席七一论文　法共人道报全文刊载  登载前两日以显著地位预告

1949-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席七一论文
  　法共人道报全文刊载
    登载前两日以显著地位预告
    【新华社布拉格二十三日电】法共机关报“人道报”二十一日以其第三版的全部地位，刊载毛主席“论人民民主专政”的全文和毛主席的照片。在此前两天，从十九日起，该报即在其第一版，以显著地位，与人民解放军再度发动攻势的消息同栏，预告毛主席的重要著作将刊出。
