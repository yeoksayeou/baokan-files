Title: PLA Anniversary Approaching: Nanchang Prepares to Commemorate "August 1st"; Shenyang and Jinan to Hold Grand Military Parades
Author:
Date: 1949-07-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, July 24th: The anniversary of the birth of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, "August 1st," is approaching. Nanchang, Shenyang, Jinan and other places are actively preparing for the commemoration. People from all walks of life in Nanchang, the birthplace of the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army, the predecessor of the People's Liberation Army, established a preparatory committee for the "August 1st" Army Day commemoration on the 18th. It was decided that July 25th to 30th would be "August 1st" Propaganda Week, to carry out propaganda to citizens of all classes through various methods, and to organize lectures and various evening parties and symposiums, and to hire specialists to explain the history of "August 1st" and the People's Liberation Army. The Jiangxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party issued notices on the 15th and 18th successively, instructing all localities to hold grand mass rallies on "August 1st" to conduct extensive propaganda and education, and called on people from all walks of life and women to respond to the call of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, launch a letter-writing campaign, and organize the masses to offer condolences to the troops stationed in the area and the wounded in hospitals. The Political Department of the Northeast Military Region Headquarters of the People's Liberation Army decided to hold a military parade in Shenyang on "August 1st," and decided that in the commemoration activities in various places, it is necessary to link the current military construction tasks, implement the ideological education of building a regular and modern national defense force, in order to overcome the ideological deviations of lack of organization, discipline and personal plans in the troops. The garrisons in various places will hold military conferences on "August 1st" and invite local party and government leaders and representatives from all walks of life to participate. The Shandong Military Region of the People's Liberation Army also decided to hold a grand military review in Jinan on "August 1st" to check the achievements of the military and political education of the various troops, in order to learn from each other and better carry out future military training movements and build a modern and regular national defense force.

### 中国人民解放军诞辰即届　南昌筹备纪念“八一”  沈阳济南将举行隆重阅兵

1949-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国人民解放军诞辰即届
  　南昌筹备纪念“八一” 
    沈阳济南将举行隆重阅兵
    【新华社北平二十四日电】中国人民解放军诞生纪念日“八一”将届，南昌、沈阳、济南等地积极筹备纪念。人民解放军前身，中国工农红军诞生地的南昌市各界人民，已于十八日成立纪念“八一”建军筹备委员会，决定二十五日至三十日为“八一”宣传周，通过各种方式向各阶层市民进行宣传，并组织讲座及各种晚会、座谈会，聘请专人讲解“八一”和人民解放军的建军历史。中国共产党江西省委会，于十五、十八日先后发出通知，指示各地于“八一”召开盛大的群众大会，进行广泛的宣传教育，并号召各阶层人民及妇女们，响应全国民主妇联的号召，开展一封信运动，组织群众向驻地部队和医院的伤员进行慰问。人民解放军东北军区司令部政治部，决定“八一”在沈阳举行阅兵仪式，并决定各地在纪念活动中，要联系目前建军任务，贯彻建设正规化、现代化国防军的思想教育，以克服部队中无组织、无纪律及个人打算等思想偏向。各地驻军于“八一”将召开军人大会，并邀请当地党政负责同志及各界代表参加。人民解放军山东军区亦决定于“八一”在济南举行隆重的军事大检阅，检查各部队执行军政教育的成绩，以求互相学习，更好地开展今后的练兵运动，建设近代化、正规化的国防军。
