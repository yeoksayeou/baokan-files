Title: Telegram of Thanks to the International Organization of Journalists; Officially Requesting to Join the Organization
Author:
Date: 1949-07-14
Page: 1

　　Telegram of Thanks to the International Organization of Journalists
  　Officially Requesting to Join the Organization
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 13th] The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association sent a telegram in reply to the International Organization of Journalists, thanking them for their congratulations and formally requesting to join the organization. The text of the reply telegram is as follows: Mr. Heronek, Secretary-General of the International Organization of Journalists, Dr. Kranski, Executive Secretary:
    We deeply appreciate your congratulations on our meeting.
    Since its establishment, the International Organization of Journalists has been a force in combating all forms of Fascism and promoting the unity of people of all countries, a staunch defender of world peace, and a leader of progressive journalism. Our meeting today has unanimously resolved to request to join your organization. We will strive to unite progressive journalists throughout the country and work together with honest and peace-loving journalists from all countries to promote the world's progressive journalism and to defend lasting world peace.
  　　　Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association, July 13th

### 电谢国际记者协会　正式请求加入该会

1949-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　电谢国际记者协会
  　正式请求加入该会
    【新华社北平十三日电】中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备会第一次全体会议电复国际记者协会，感谢他们的祝贺，并正式请求加入该会，复电电文如下：国际记者协会秘书长赫罗涅克先生，执行秘书克兰斯基博士：
    深深感谢你们对于我们的会议的祝贺。
    国际新闻记者协会成立以来，一直是打击一切形式法西斯主义和增进各国人民团结的力量、世界和平的忠实保卫者、和进步新闻事业的领导者。我们今天的会议，已经一致决议，请求加入贵会。我们将努力团结全国的进步的新闻工作者，与各国的正直的和爱好和平的新闻工作者们，共同为推进世界进步新闻事业、为保卫世界持久和平而奋斗
  　　　中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备会七月十三日
