Title: Near Lao Cai, Northern Vietnam: Vietnamese People's Army Captures French Military Camp
Author:
Date: 1949-07-06
Page: 3

　　Viet Bac, near Lao Cai
　　Vietnamese People's Army Captures French Military Camp
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd] Reuters, Paris dispatch: The French High Command admitted on the 1st that the Vietnamese People's Army recently occupied the French military camp in the Follans area near Lao Cai in northern Vietnam. The French army was forced to flee after suffering considerable casualties. In Cochinchina, the Vietnamese army and the French army continue to fight fiercely.

### 越北老街附近　越南人民军攻占法军营

1949-07-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　越北老街附近
  　越南人民军攻占法军营
    【新华社北平三日电】据路透社巴黎讯：法国高级统帅部一日承认，越南人民军队最近占领越北老街附近佛兰地方的法军营地，法军于遭受相当伤亡后被迫逃窜。交趾支那方面，越军与法军继续激战。
