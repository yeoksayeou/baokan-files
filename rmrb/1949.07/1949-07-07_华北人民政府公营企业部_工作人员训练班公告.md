Title: Announcement of the Worker Training Class of the Public Enterprise Department of the North China People's Government
Author:
Date: 1949-07-07
Page: 1

　　North China People's Government Public Enterprise Department
  　Announcement of the Worker Training Class
    The examination papers for the technical and management personnel of this class have been graded. The preliminary list will be announced in this class, as well as in the People's Evening News on the 8th, the Beiping Liberation Daily, and the People's Daily and Tianjin Daily on the 9th. We hope that all applicants will pay attention.

### 华北人民政府公营企业部　工作人员训练班公告

1949-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府公营企业部
  　工作人员训练班公告
    本班技术及管理人员试卷业已评阅完毕，初榜除在本班公布外，并在八日人民晚报、北平解放报及九日人民日报与天津日报公布，希报考诸生注意。
