Title: Soong Ching Ling's Congratulatory Message
Author:
Date: 1949-07-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 1st – The full text of Soong Ching Ling's congratulatory message at the July 1st celebration in Shanghai on the 30th is as follows:

　　This is the greatest period in the lives of the Chinese people; their complete victory is at hand. Salutations to the people's victory!

　　This is the driving force for our motherland's construction and progress; our banner is "production," more production. Salutations to the people's power!

　　This is the new light on the earth; freedom is born, and its warmth and brilliance radiate to every dark corner enveloped by reactionary forces. Salutations to the people's freedom!

　　This high tide of victory is surging to every port; the people's movements in various countries are rising vigorously, combining our strength with theirs to strengthen this courageous struggle. Salutations to comrades in the democratic struggle around the world!

　　The strength of the warriors of this victory has grown; they are invincible in their heroism, unparalleled in their discipline, and their hearts are with the hearts of the common people. Salutations to the Chinese People's Liberation Army!

　　Welcome our leader—born in Shanghai, grew up in the mountains of Jiangxi, tempered into steel on the arduous road of the 25,000-li Long March, and matured in the soil of the countryside. Salutations to the Communist Party of China!

　　Yes, this is the greatest period—this is the milestone of the Chinese people's revolutionary struggle. We have broken free from the shackles of imperialism and colonial policies, we have eradicated feudalism, and the people are advancing towards new and more glorious heights. Salute! Long live the victory of the Chinese people's revolutionary struggle!

### 宋庆龄祝词

1949-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　宋庆龄祝词
    【新华社上海一日电】宋庆龄三十日在沪“七一”纪念会上的祝词全文如下：
    这是中国人民生活中的一个最伟大的时期，他们的完全胜利已在眼前。向人民的胜利致敬！
    这是我们祖国建设和前进的动力，我们的旗帜是“生产”，更多的生产。向人民的力量致敬！
    这是大地上的新光明，自由诞生了，它底温暖和光辉留传照耀到每一个为反动势力所笼罩的黑暗的角落。向人民的自由致敬！
    这是胜利的高潮荡漾到每一个口岸，各国的人民运动风起云涌，把我们的力量和他们的合在一起，加强这勇敢的战斗。向全世界民主斗争中的同志致敬！
    这一次胜利的战士们的力量增长了，他们的英勇无敌，他们的纪律无双，他们的心同老百姓的心在一起。向中国人民解放军致敬！
    欢迎我们的领袖——这诞生在上海，生长在江西丛山里，在二万五千里长征艰难因苦的路程上百炼成钢，在乡村的泥土里成熟的领袖。向中国共产党致敬！
    是的，这是一个最伟大的时期——这是中国人民革命斗争的里程碑。我们解脱了帝国主义和殖民地政策的束缚，我们铲除了封建主义，人民正走向新的更光耀的高峰。敬礼！中国人民革命斗争胜利万岁！
