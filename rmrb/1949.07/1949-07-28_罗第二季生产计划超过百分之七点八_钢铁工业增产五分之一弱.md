Title: Romania's Second Quarter Production Plan Exceeds by 7.8 Percent; Steel Industry Increases Production by Slightly Less Than One-Fifth
Author:
Date: 1949-07-28
Page: 3
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 27th: The Romanian News Agency reports that Romania's 1949 second quarter production plan has been exceeded by 7.8 percent. The mining industry exceeded the plan by 7.8 percent, the steel industry by 17.6 percent, the electric power industry by 0.8 percent, the chemical industry by 1.8 percent, the paper industry by 6.7 percent, the building materials industry by 8.6 percent, the timber industry by 16.3 percent, the textile industry by 0.1 percent, and the food industry by 13.6 percent.

### 罗第二季生产计划超过百分之七点八　钢铁工业增产五分之一弱

1949-07-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　罗第二季生产计划超过百分之七点八
  　钢铁工业增产五分之一弱
    【新华社北平二十七日电】罗马尼亚通讯社报道：罗马尼亚一九四九年第二季生产计划，已被超过百分之七点八。采矿工业超过计划百分之七点八，钢铁工业超过百分之一七点六，电力工业超过百分之○点八，化学工业超过百分之一点八，造纸工业超过百分之六点七，建筑器材工业超过百分之八点六，木材工业超过百分之一六点三，纺织工业超过百分之○点一，而食品工业则超过百分之一三点六。
