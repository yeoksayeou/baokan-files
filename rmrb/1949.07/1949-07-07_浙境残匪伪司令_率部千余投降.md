Title: Remnant Bandit Puppet Commander in Zhejiang Surrenders with Over a Thousand Troops
Author:
Date: 1949-07-07
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 6th: The commander of the Kuomintang remnant bandits' "Zhejiang Anti-Communist Second Headquarters" in Zhejiang Province surrendered to the Second Military Subdistrict of the Zhejiang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army on June 14th, leading 1,358 men. They brought with them 16 light machine guns, 3 rifle grenades, 260 rifles, 15 pistols, and 1 carbine and 1 Thompson submachine gun each.

### 浙境残匪伪司令　率部千余投降

1949-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　浙境残匪伪司令
  　率部千余投降
    【新华社杭州六日电】浙江境内的国民党残匪“浙江反共第二司令部”司令率领所部一千三百五十八人，于六月十四日向人民解放军浙江军区第二军分区投降，携来轻机枪十六挺，枪榴筒三个，步枪二六○支，短枪十五支，卡宾、汤姆枪各一支。
