Title: Commemorating the Twelfth Anniversary of the War of Resistance Against Japan, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Issues Slogans
Author:
Date: 1949-07-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 1st—The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has recently issued slogans commemorating the twelfth anniversary of the "July 7th" War of Resistance Against Japan. The slogans are as follows:

    I. Celebrate the great victory of the eight-year War of Resistance Against Japan!
    II. Celebrate the great victory of the three-year People's Liberation War!
    III. Celebrate the great victory of the liberation of Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Nanchang, Wuhan, Xi'an, Taiyuan, and Qingdao!
    IV. Salute the People's Liberation Army and other people's armed forces that defeated the Japanese invaders!
    V. Salute the People's Liberation Army that defeated Chiang Kai-shek!
    VI. Celebrate the establishment of the preparatory committee for the New Political Consultative Conference!
    VII. Support the prompt convening of the New Political Consultative Conference!
    VIII. Support the prompt establishment of a democratic coalition government!
    IX. Promptly conclude a peace treaty with Japan in accordance with the Potsdam Agreement!
    X. Achieve the demilitarization and democratization of Japan!
    XI. The people of China and Japan unite to oppose the long-term occupation of Japan by the United States!
    XII. Support the Sino-Soviet friendship alliance, and the joint defense of the Far East against aggressors by China and the Soviet Union!
    XIII. Support the world peace and democracy camp! Oppose the new war conspiracies of international imperialism!
    XIV. All imperialist aggressor forces withdraw from China!
    XV. All democratic parties, people's organizations, the working class, the peasant class, the petty bourgeoisie, the national bourgeoisie, minority nationalities, and overseas Chinese unite to consolidate the people's democratic united front!
    XVI. Resolutely, thoroughly, cleanly, and completely wipe out the remaining forces of the reactionaries!
    XVII. Fight to Taiwan, liberate Taiwanese compatriots, and capture Chiang Kai-shek alive!
    XVIII. Capture all the major war criminals alive!
    XIX. Welcome patriotic elements within the Kuomintang to stand on the side of the people!
    XX. Resolutely suppress the sabotage and disruption of reactionary elements!
    XXI. Raise revolutionary vigilance and eliminate hidden special agents and spies!
    XXII. Cherish our great motherland and protect all state property!
    XXIII. Male and female workers, staff members, technical experts, and patriotic industrialists, unite and strive to develop production and prosperity!
    XXIV. Male and female peasants, organize, improve agricultural technology, and strive to develop production and prosperity!
    XXV. Employees of the transportation sector, employees of the trade sector, and members of supply and marketing cooperatives, unite and strive to restore and develop urban and rural trade and domestic and foreign trade!
    XXVI. Teachers, students, scientists, writers, artists, journalists, and publishers, unite and strive to restore and develop the people's cultural and educational undertakings!
    XXVII. Communists and Youth League members, show your tireless initiative and spirit of learning on the political, military, economic, and cultural fronts!
    XXVIII. Build an independent, free, peaceful, unified, and prosperous New China!
    XXIX. Long live the People's Democratic Republic of China!

### 纪念抗日战争十二周年　中共中央发布口号

1949-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　纪念抗日战争十二周年
  　中共中央发布口号
    【新华社北平一日电】中国共产党中央委员会顷发布纪念“七七”抗日战争十二周年口号，口号如下：
    一、庆祝八年抗日战争的伟大胜利！
    二、庆祝三年人民解放战争的伟大胜利！
    三、庆祝解放上海、南京、杭州、南昌、武汉、西安、太原、青岛的伟大胜利！
    四、向战胜日本侵略者的人民解放军及其他人民武装力量致敬！
    五、向战胜蒋介石的人民解放军致敬！
    六、庆祝新政治协商会议筹备会成立！
    七、拥护迅速召开新政治协商会议！
    八、拥护迅速成立民主联合政府！
    九、迅速按照波茨坦协定，缔结对日和约！
    十、实现日本非军国主义化，实现日本民主化！
    十一、中日两国人民联合起来，反对美国长期占领日本！
    十二、拥护中苏友好同盟，中苏两国共同防御远东侵略者！
    十三、拥护世界和平民主阵营！反对国际帝国主义的新战争阴谋！
    十四、一切帝国主义侵略势力退出中国！
    十五、全国各民主党派、各人民团体、工人阶级、农民阶级、小资产阶级、民族资产阶级、少数民族、海外华侨团结起来，巩固人民民主统一战线！
    十六、坚决彻底干净全部地歼灭反动派的残余力量！
    十七、打到台湾去，解放台湾同胞，活捉蒋介石！
    十八、活捉一切首要的战争罪犯！
    十九、欢迎国民党内爱国分子站到人民方面来！
    二十、坚决镇压反动分子的破坏和捣乱！
    二一、提高革命警惕性，肃清暗藏的特务间谍！
    二二、爱护我们的伟大的祖国，保护一切国家财富！
    二三、男女工人们，职员们，技术专家们，爱国的产业家们，团结起来，为发展生产繁荣经济而奋斗！
    二四、男女农民们，组织起来，改进农业技术，为发展生产繁荣经济而奋斗！
    二五、交通部门的职工们，贸易部门的职工们，供销、运输合作社的社员们，团结起来，为恢复和发展城乡贸易和内外贸易而奋斗！
    二六、教师们，学生们，科学家们，著作家们，艺术家们，新闻工作者们，出版工作者们，团结起来，为恢复和发展人民文化教育事业而奋斗！
    二七、共产党员们和青年团员们，在政治的、军事的、经济的、文化的战线上，显出你们的不疲倦的积极性和学习精神！
    二八、建设独立的、自由的、和平的、统一的、富强的新中国！
    二九、中华人民民主共和国万岁！
