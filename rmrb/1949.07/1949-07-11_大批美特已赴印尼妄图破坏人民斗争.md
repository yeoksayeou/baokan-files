Title: Large numbers of U.S. Special Agents have gone to Indonesia plotting to undermine the people's struggle
Author:
Date: 1949-07-11
Page: 3

　　Large numbers of U.S. Special Agents have gone to Indonesia
  　Plotting to undermine the people's struggle
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 10th: According to a report by the Dutch News Agency on the 6th, a large number of official and non-official representatives of the United States have arrived in Indonesia. Under the instructions of the U.S. State Department, they are searching for materials related to the Indonesian Communist Party and democratic groups in order to launch a "resolute struggle" against them. The agency pointed out that fifteen American journalists are currently traveling around Indonesia to assist in collecting information about democratic groups in the country.

### 大批美特已赴印尼妄图破坏人民斗争

1949-07-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　大批美特已赴印尼
  　妄图破坏人民斗争
    【新华社北平十日电】据塔斯社六日引荷兰通讯社的消息称：大批美国官方和非官方的代表已到达印尼，他们奉美国务院的指令正在搜寻印尼的共产党及民主团体材料，以便发动对付他们的“坚决斗争”。该社指出：十五名美国记者目前正在周游印尼，以协助搜集关于该国民主团体的情报。
