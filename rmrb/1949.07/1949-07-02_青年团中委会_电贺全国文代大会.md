Title: Youth League Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to National Congress of Literary and Art Workers
Author:
Date: 1949-07-02
Page: 2
　　
　　Youth League Central Committee
　　Sends Congratulatory Message to National Congress of Literary and Art Workers
　　【Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 29th】The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League sent a congratulatory message to the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers, the message is as follows:
　　To all representatives of the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers:
　　Congratulations on the convening of this representative congress of Chinese literary and art workers. We believe that the congress will surely define the tasks of literary and art workers in the new democratic production and cultural and educational undertakings. We hope that the writers of the new China will go to factories, to the countryside, to the People's Liberation Army, and to the youth masses, to describe their various lives and struggles, in order to encourage and guide the younger generation to advance in a bright direction. In order to jointly cultivate new democratic literary and artistic youth workers, we hope to receive your guidance and assistance. We wish your congress success.
　　Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, June 29th

### 青年团中委会　电贺全国文代大会

1949-07-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　青年团中委会
  　电贺全国文代大会
    【新华社北平二九十日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会电贺中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会，电文如下：
    中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会全体代表们：
    庆贺中国文学艺术工作者召开这次代表大会。相信大会必将确定文学艺术工作者在新民主主义的生产事业和文化教育事业中的任务。我们希望新中国的作家们，到工厂去，到农村去，到人民解放军里去，到青年群众中间去，描写他们的各种生活和斗争，借以鼓舞与引导青年一代向着光明的方向前进。为了共同努力培植新民主主义的文学艺术青年工作者，我们希望多多地得到你们的指导和援助。敬祝你们的大会成功。
  　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会　六月二十九日
