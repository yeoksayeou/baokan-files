Title: Preparatory Meeting of the Chinese New History Research Association Established Yesterday in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-07-02
Page: 2

　　Preparatory Meeting of the Chinese New History Research Association Established Yesterday in Beiping

　　[This newspaper dispatch] The preparatory meeting of the Chinese New History Research Association, initiated by Wang Yechiu, Wang Chongmin, Wang Boxiang, Yin Da, Bai Shouyi, Xiang Da, Lü Zhenyu, Wu Yuzhang, Wu Han, Wu Ze, Song Yunbin, Du Shousu, Yu Jiaxi, Yu Zhaoxiang, Li Zegang, Zhou Gucheng, Zhou Yutong, Shang Yue, Jin Canran, Shao Xunzheng, Fan Wenlan, Hou Wailu, Ma Heng, Weng Dujian, Liang Sicheng, Rong Zhaozu, Tang Lan, Chen Yuan, Chen Jiakang, Chen Shu, Guo Moruo, Ji Wenfu, Zhang Yunbo, Hua Gang, Ye Dingyi, Ye Huosheng, Yang Shaoxuan, Yang Dongchun, Chu Tunan, Pei Wenzhong, Jian Bozan, Deng Chumin, Deng Yizhe, Li Jinxi, Zheng Zhenduo, Zheng Tianting, Qi Yanming, Qian Xingcun, Han Shou Xuan, Luo Changpei, and others, was formally established in Beiping yesterday (1st). The inaugural meeting was held at the Beijing Hotel yesterday afternoon at 3:30 PM. More than 30 of the association's initiators attended, including Guo Moruo, Fan Wenlan, and Deng Chumin. During the meeting, Guo Moruo, Fan Wenlan, Deng Chumin, Xiang Da, Chen Zhongfan and others spoke successively, unanimously expressing that historical workers across the country should unite and engage in the construction of the new historiography. The assembly unanimously passed the organizational rules of the preparatory committee and the provisional charter of the Chinese New History Research Association (full text of the charter attached). It was also decided to promptly prepare for convening a national representative conference of historical workers. The standing committee of the preparatory committee was elected, with Guo Moruo, Wu Yuzhang, Fan Wenlan, Deng Chumin, Chen Yuan, Hou Wailu, Jian Bozan, Xiang Da, Wu Han, Yang Shaoxuan, and Lü Zhenyu elected as members of the preparatory standing committee. The preparatory standing committee elected Guo Moruo as chairman, Wu Yuzhang and Fan Wenlan as vice-chairmen, and Hou Wailu and Yang Shaoxuan as secretaries, responsible for carrying out the preparatory matters for convening the national representative conference of historical workers.

### 中国新史学研究会筹备会昨在平成立

1949-07-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国新史学研究会筹备会昨在平成立
    【本报讯】中国新史学研究会由王冶秋、王重民、王伯祥、尹达、白寿彝、向达、吕振羽、吴玉章、吴晗、吴泽、宋云彬、杜守素、余嘉锡、余兆祥、李则刚、周谷城、周予同、尚钺、金灿然、邵循正、范文澜、侯外庐、马衡、翁独健、梁思成、容肇祖、唐兰、陈垣、陈家康、陈述、郭沫若、嵇文甫、张云波、华岗、叶丁易、叶蠖生、杨绍萱、杨东莼、楚图南、裴文中、翦伯赞、邓初民、邓以蛰、黎锦熙、郑振铎、郑天挺、齐燕铭、钱杏村、韩寿萱、罗常培等发起，昨（一）日在平正式成立筹备会。发起人会议于昨日下午三时半假北京饭店举行，到有该会发起人郭沫若、范文澜、邓初民等三十余人。会中，郭沫若、范文澜、邓初民、向达、陈中凡等均先后发言，一致表示全国历史工作者应团结起来，从事新史学的建设工作。大会全体通过了筹备会的组织规程和中国新史学研究会暂行简章（简章全文附后）。并决定迅速筹备召开全国历史工作者代表会议。选举了筹备会的常务委员会，选出郭沫若、吴玉章、范文澜、邓初民、陈垣、侯外庐、翦伯赞、向达、吴晗、杨绍萱、吕振羽等十一人为筹备常务委员会委员。筹备会常委会推选郭沫若任主席，吴玉章、范文澜任副主席，侯外庐、杨绍萱任秘书，负责进行召开全国历史工作者代表会议的筹备事宜。
