Title: Six Thousand Hungarian Workers Appointed to Senior Government Positions
Author:
Date: 1949-07-30
Page: 3

　　Six Thousand Hungarian Workers
  　Appointed to Senior Government Positions
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 29th: The Hungarian Workers' Party newspaper "Free People's Daily" (i.e., "Szabad Nép") reports that the number of workers recently assigned to leadership positions in government agencies, the military, and the courts is increasing. With the help and guidance of the Hungarian Workers' Party, six thousand workers have already taken on senior and mid-level positions in the government. The newspaper points out: "Workers are bringing vitality to various agencies and applying the experience they have gained in the workers' movement. Facts prove that workers are not only competent for the leadership positions they undertake in various departments, but also perform their tasks better than others."

### 匈六千名工人　任政府高级职位

1949-07-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　匈六千名工人
  　任政府高级职位
    【新华社北平二十九日电】匈牙利工人党机关报“自由人民报”（即“＠巴德·涅普”报）载称：最近被派到政府机关、军队和法院里担任领导职位的工人，其数目愈来愈多。在匈工人党的帮助与指导之下，已有六千名工人担任政府中高级的职位。该报指出：“工人们正给各机关带来朝气，并应用着他们在工人阶级运动中所获得的经验。事实证明：工人们不但能胜任他们在各个不同的部门里所承担的领导职务，而且能比别人更好地执行任务”。
