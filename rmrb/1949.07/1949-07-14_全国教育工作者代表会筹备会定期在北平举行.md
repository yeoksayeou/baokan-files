Title: Preparatory Meeting of the National Education Workers' Representative Conference to be Held Regularly in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-07-14
Page: 1

　　[Ping City News] The preparatory meeting of the All-China Education Workers' Representative Conference is scheduled to be held in Ping on the 22nd of this month. The initiators include 44 education workers from various aspects in Ping, including Dong Biwu, Lin Liru, Ye Shengtao, Chao Zhefu, Ma Xulun, Qian Junrui, and Sun Qimeng (list attached). Since June, the initiators have held five meetings, both full and partial, and after careful discussion and research, a draft of the preparatory meeting's charter and a method for allocating the number of preparatory committee members were decided. In the initiators' past meetings, it was unanimously pointed out that holding a national education workers' representative conference is to respond to Chairman Mao's call to "restore and develop the people's cultural and educational undertakings," unite all education workers, deeply study and resolutely implement the New Democracy education policy, so as to better serve the people. With the liberation of the whole country about to be realized, convening such a meeting has great significance. The list of initiators of the conference is as follows (surnames are arranged in order of stroke count): Ding Hua, Ding Haochuan, Fang Yuyan, Cheng Fangwu, Zhu Zhixian, Li Fusheng, Li Weihan, He Li, Du Junhui, Wu Han, Wu Hongmai, Qiu Chun, Lin Liru, Zhou Yang, Meng Zhaojiang, Liu ＠, Hu Xikui, Hao Renchu, Ma Xulun, Sun Wenshu, Sun Qimeng, Xu Shifeng, Xu Teli, Chao Zhefu, Zhang Zhirang, Zhang Zonglin, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Zhixiang, Tao Shufan, Lu Zhiwei, Chen Qiyuan, Tang Yongtong, Cheng Jinwu, Cheng Zhewen, Fu Binran, Dong Biwu, Dong Weichuan, Ye Qisun, Ye Shengtao, Liu Aifeng, Li Jinxi, Qian Junrui, Han Li, Xue Chengye.

### 全国教育工作者代表会筹备会定期在北平举行

1949-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国教育工作者代表会筹备会定期在北平举行
    【平市讯】中华全国教育工作者代表议会的筹备会，定于本月二十二日在平举行。发起筹备者包括在平各方面的教育工作者董必武、林砺儒、叶圣陶、晁哲甫、马叙伦、钱俊瑞、孙起孟等四十四人（名单附后）。自六月间起，发起人先后举行过全体的及部分的会议五次，经过仔细的商讨研究，决定了筹备会章程的草案，以及筹备委员名额分配的办法。在发起人历次会议中，一致明确的指出：举行一个全国性的教育工作者的代表会议，乃是为了响应毛主席“恢复和发展人民的文化教育事业”的号召，团结所有的教育工作者，深入研究并坚决执行新民主主义教育方针，以便更好的为人民服务。在全国的解放快要实现的今天，召开这样的会议，实具有重大的意义。该会发起人名单如下（姓氏以笔划为序：丁华、丁浩川、方与严、成仿吾、朱智贤、李复生、李维汉、何礼、杜君慧、吴晗、吴鸿迈、邱椿、林砺儒、周扬、孟昭江、柳＠、胡锡奎、郝人初、马叙伦、孙文淑、孙起孟、徐侍峰、徐特立、晁哲甫、张志让、张宗麟、张奚若、张致祥、陶淑范、陆志韦、陈其瑗、汤用彤、程今吾、程哲文、傅彬然、董必武、董渭川、叶企孙、叶圣陶、刘皑风、黎锦熙、钱俊瑞、韩黎、薛成业。
