Title: Youth Delegation Arrives in Harbin
Author:
Date: 1949-07-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 26th: The Chinese Youth Delegation, which will attend the Second World Democratic Youth Congress and the International Youth Day, arrived in Harbin City on the afternoon of the 24th. More than 3,000 representatives from various party and government organizations and mass groups went to the station to welcome them, and a welcome meeting was held in front of the station. After the delegation got off the train, they sang the "Liberation Army March" and marched into the venue in neat steps. The delegation will stay in Harbin for three days.

### 青年代表团抵哈尔滨

1949-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年代表团抵哈尔滨
    【新华社沈阳二十六日电】出席第二次世界民主青年代表大会和国际青年节的中国青年代表团，二十四日午后到达哈尔滨市。各党政机关和群众团体代表三千余人齐到车站欢迎，并在车站前举行欢迎会。代表团下车后，高唱“解放军进行曲”以整齐步伐走入会场。代表团将在哈尔滨停留三天。
