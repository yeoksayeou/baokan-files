Title: National Liberation Locomotive Begins Operation Last Night
Author: Shuzhi, Guang
Date: 1949-07-06
Page: 2

　　National Liberation Locomotive
  　Began Operation Last Night
    [Our Newspaper] The locomotive named "National Liberation," which was repaired by all the staff of the Beiping Locomotive Depot Inspection Section in commemoration of "July 1st," was successfully test-run on the 27th and 29th of last month. On July 3rd, the locomotive was jointly inspected by the Tianjin North Station, Tianjin East Station, Fengtai, Changxindian, Xizhimen and other locomotive depots. The results were good. It was specially used to operate Passenger Train No. 121 between Ping and Jin last night (the 5th). (Shuzhi, Guang)

### 全国解放号机车　昨晚开始行驶
树芝　光
1949-07-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国解放号机车
  　昨晚开始行驶
    【本报讯】北平机务段检车段全体职工为纪念“七一”所修复而命名为全国解放号的机车，自上月二十七、二十九日试车成功后，又于七月三日经天津北站、天津东站、丰台、长辛店、西直门等机务段，互相组织检验，效果良好。特于昨（五）日晚行驶平津间一二一次客车。（树芝、光）
