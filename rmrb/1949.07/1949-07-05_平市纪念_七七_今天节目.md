Ping Municipality's Program for Commemorating the "July 7th" Incident Today

[Ping Municipality News] Ping Municipality's expanded commemoration of the "July 7th" Incident and celebration of the New Political Consultative Conference Preparatory Committee will have the following program today (the 5th): (1) Lectures: At 4:00 PM, Yang Jingren, Ma Jian, and others will lecture at Northwest Middle School inside Guang'anmen; at 6:30 PM, Ye Jianying, Zhang Bojun, Xiao Ming, Liu Zijiu, and others will lecture at Tsinghua University, the Telecommunications Bureau, North China Electric Power Company, the People's Printing Factory, and Xiangshan Ciyouyuan; at 7:00 PM, Shi Liang will lecture at the YMCA. (2) Arts and Literature Broadcast: At 5:30 PM, Tan Fuying and Wang Yingqiu will broadcast the Peking Opera "Hitting the Fishing Boat to Kill."

### 平市纪念“七七”今天节目

1949-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市纪念“七七”今天节目
    【平市讯】平市扩大纪念“七七”与庆祝新政协筹备会，今天（五日）的节目计有：（一）讲演：下午四时由杨静仁、马坚等在广安门内西北中学讲演，下午六时半分由叶剑英、章伯钧、萧明、刘子久等在清华大学、电信局、华北电业公司、人民印制厂和香山慈幼院等处讲演，下午七时由史良在青年会讲演。（二）文艺广播：下午五时三十分在华声电台由谭富英、王迎秋播唱平剧“打渔杀家”。
