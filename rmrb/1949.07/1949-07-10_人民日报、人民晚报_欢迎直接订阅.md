　　People's Daily, People's Evening News
  　Welcome Direct Subscriptions
    The daily newspaper is priced at 600 yuan per copy per month, 20 yuan per copy for retail. The evening newspaper is priced at 300 yuan per copy per month, 10 yuan per copy for retail. Workers and students in Beijing Municipality who subscribe to the newspaper with a certificate of proof will receive a 30% discount. To subscribe by phone, please call (2) 4173, and delivery will be made the next day.
    　No. 154 Xuannei Avenue, Telephone 2-3543 Address　No. 117 Wangfujing Avenue, Telephone 5-0878
    　No. 286 Damochang, Southern District, Telephone 7-2657
