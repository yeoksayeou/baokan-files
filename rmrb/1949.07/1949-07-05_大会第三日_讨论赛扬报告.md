Title: Day Three of the Conference: Discussion of Saillant's Report
Author:
Date: 1949-07-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, July 4th Dispatch: TASS dispatch: The third day of the Second World Federation of Trade Unions Congress (the 1st) continued the discussion of the report of Saillant, the General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, in the morning and afternoon. Those who spoke in the discussion included leaders and representatives of the working people from the trade unions of Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, Argentina, Chile, Ceylon, the Netherlands, the Arab Workers' Congress of Israel, and the trade unions of the Free Territory of Trieste. They reported on the living conditions and struggles of the working people in their respective countries, expressed their deep gratitude to the World Federation of Trade Unions for its assistance to the trade union movement, and condemned the actions that split the international trade union unity.

　　Saillant made a summary of the discussion. Amidst applause, he suggested that the governments of all countries should remember that threats of imprisonment, retaliation, or execution will not force the World Federation of Trade Unions and its affiliated national trade union central organizations to abandon the struggle for the sacred cause of the working people.

　　The Congress has begun to discuss the following agenda item: "The general policy and struggle of the World Federation of Trade Unions for international trade union unity, defending peace and the rights of the people's democracy." Kuznetsov, the head of the Soviet delegation, made a report on this issue, which was met with prolonged and enthusiastic applause.

　　A total of 240 delegates attended the congress, representing 71,160,888 working people belonging to trade unions from 43 countries. In addition, 23 representatives from 10 countries attended the congress as observers.

### 大会第三日　讨论赛扬报告

1949-07-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　大会第三日
  　讨论赛扬报告
    【新华社北平四日电】塔斯社讯：世界工联第二届代表大会的第三天（一日），上下午继续讨论世界工联总书记赛扬的报告。在讨论中发言的，有罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、阿根廷、智利、锡兰、荷兰职工会、以色列的阿拉伯工人大会及的里雅斯特自由区的职工会领袖和劳动人民的代表。他们报告了他们本国劳动人民的生活和斗争情况，表示深深感谢世界工联对职工运动的帮助，并谴责分裂国际职工团结的行动。
    赛扬作了讨论的总结。他在掌声中，建议一切国家的政府要牢记着：监禁、报复或执刑的威胁，将强迫不了世界工联及其所属各全国性职工会中央机构放弃为劳动人民的神圣事业的斗争。
    大会已开始讨论如下一项程序：“世界工联争取国际职工会团结、保卫和平与人民民主权利的总政策与斗争”。苏联代表团团长库兹涅佐夫对此问题作报告，全场报以长时间的热烈鼓掌。
    出席大会的代表共二百四十人，他们代表四十三个国家的职工会所属七千一百六十万八千七百八十八名劳动人民。此外，尚有来自十个国家的二十三位代表以观察员资格列席大会。
