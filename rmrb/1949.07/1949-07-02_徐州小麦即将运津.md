Title: Xuzhou Wheat to be Shipped to Tianjin Soon
Author:
Date: 1949-07-02
Page: 2

　　Xuzhou Wheat to be Shipped to Tianjin Soon
    [Tianjin News] The Tianjin wholesale grain merchants' purchasing group to Xu(zhou) to purchase wheat had a satisfactory result in negotiating purchasing issues with the local trade company. A telegram has been received stating that over 100,000 catties of wheat have been purchased and will be shipped to Tianjin soon.

### 徐州小麦即将运津

1949-07-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　徐州小麦即将运津
    【天津讯】天津市批发粮商集体赴徐（州）购麦小组，与当地贸易公司接洽采购问题，结果圆满。顷得来电称：已购妥小麦十万余斤，即将运津。
