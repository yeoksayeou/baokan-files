Title: Revolutionary University Second Phase Enrollment: Quota Set at 5,000, Registration Begins on the 20th
Author:
Date: 1949-07-15
Page: 2

　　Revolutionary University Second Phase Enrollment
  　Quota Set at 5,000
  　Registration Begins on the 20th
    [Report from this newspaper] The over ten thousand students of the North China People's Revolutionary University are about to graduate soon. It is reported that in order to continue to cultivate a large number of revolutionary construction cadres, and to meet the needs of the current victorious development of the national revolution, the school has decided to enroll 5,000 new students in the second phase, and will accept applications from those who are interested in revolutionary construction work from the 20th of this month to the 10th of next month. The second phase enrollment guide of the school has been drafted and will be announced soon.

### 革大二期招生　名额定五千人　二十日起开始报名

1949-07-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　革大二期招生
  　名额定五千人
  　二十日起开始报名
    【本报讯】华北人民革命大学学生万余人于近日即将毕业，闻该校为继续大量培养革命建设干部，以适应目前全国革命胜利发展形势的需要，顷已决定招收第二期新生五千名，于本月二十日起至下月十日止接受有志于革命建设工作者报名投考。该校第二期招生简章已拟定，不日即可公布。
