Title: Dimitrov's Hearse Passes Through Romanian Border, Romanian Workers and Government Leaders Greet It
Author:
Date: 1949-07-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 7th: According to TASS, Dimitrov's hearse, returning from Moscow to Bulgaria, passed through the Romanian border on the 5th. A Bulgarian government delegation and Romanian workers and government leaders were at the station to greet it.
The Bulgarian Council of Ministers decided on the 4th that after Dimitrov's coffin is returned to Sofia, it will be placed in the meeting hall of the National Assembly to allow the people of the whole country to pay their last respects. In order to preserve Dimitrov's remains, a special mausoleum will be built on the square opposite the Council of Ministers building on September 9th. Dimitrov's remains will be moved to the mausoleum on July 10th.

### 季米特洛夫灵车过罗马尼亚国境　罗工人和政府领袖均往迎接

1949-07-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　季米特洛夫灵车过罗马尼亚国境
  　罗工人和政府领袖均往迎接
    【新华社北平七日电】据塔斯社讯：从莫斯科运返保加利亚的季米特洛夫灵车，五日经过罗马尼亚国境。保政府代表团及罗工人和政府领袖均到车站迎接。
    保加利亚部长会议四日决定：季米特洛夫灵柩运回索非亚后，将停在国民议会会议大厅。以便全国人民最后瞻仰致敬；为保存季米特洛夫遗体，将于九月九日在部长会议建筑物对面广场上建修特别的陵墓，季米特洛夫遗体将于七月十日送到陵墓。
