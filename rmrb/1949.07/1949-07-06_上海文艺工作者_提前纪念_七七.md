Title: Shanghai Literary and Art Workers Commemorate "July 7th" in Advance
Author:
Date: 1949-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 5th—Shanghai literary and art workers held a celebration rally in advance on the afternoon of the 4th to commemorate the "July 7th" Incident and celebrate the liberation. Over 3,000 people attended from more than 30 units including various troop and group cultural troupes, the Shanghai Military Control Commission's Literature and Art Department, the Shanghai Cultural Circles Association, the Shanghai Artists Association, the Shanghai Drama and Film Workers Association, the Shanghai Academy of Fine Arts, the Shanghai Conservatory of Music, and the Shanghai Drama Academy. This was an unprecedented gathering of Shanghai literary and art workers. After Chairman Huang Yuan delivered the opening speech, Vice Mayor Pan Hannian encouraged all literary and art workers in Shanghai in his speech to arm themselves with Mao Zedong Thought and to take Chairman Mao's speech at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art as the direction for literary and artistic work. Xia Yan emphasized in his speech: In the future, we must take on the glorious task of educating the masses to complete production and construction. Xu Guangping called on literary and art workers to serve the people's art. All dramas and art should promote the people's emotions. After speeches by Yu Ling, Ke Lan, and others, the Trade Union Cultural Troupe presented a flag to the conference. The conference passed three telegrams: one paying respect to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, one congratulating the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference, and one congratulating the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers. A parade was held after the meeting.

### 上海文艺工作者　提前纪念“七七”

1949-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海文艺工作者
  　提前纪念“七七”
    【新华社上海五日电】上海文艺工作者纪念“七七”庆祝解放大会在四日下午提前举行，到各部队各团体的文工团、上海市军管会文艺处、上海文化界协会、上海美术工作者协会、上海戏剧电影工作者协会、上海美专、音专、剧专等三十余单位，共三千余人。这是上海文艺工作者空前未有的大集会。大会主席黄源致开会词后，潘汉年副市长在讲话中鼓励上海一切文艺工作者要以毛泽东思想武装起来，以毛主席在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话作为文艺工作者的方向。夏衍在讲话中着重指出：今后，我们要担当起教育群众完成生产建设的光荣任务。许广平号召文艺工作者要为人民艺术服务。一切戏剧、艺术要发扬人民的感情。于伶、柯蓝等讲话后，总工会文工团向大会献旗，大会通过了向毛主席朱总司令致敬，向新政协筹备会庆贺，向全国文代大会致贺的电报三件。会后并举行游行。
