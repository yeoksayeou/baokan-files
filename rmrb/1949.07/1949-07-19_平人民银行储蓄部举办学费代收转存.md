Title: Ping People's Bank Savings Department Holds Tuition Fee Collection and Transfer Savings

Author: 
Date: 1949-07-19
Page: 2

    Ping People's Bank Savings Department
    Holds Tuition Fee Collection and Transfer Savings
    [This newspaper reported] The Savings Department of the People's Bank of Beiping Branch, in order to facilitate the collection of tuition fees by various schools in the city, and to facilitate the transfer to real-value savings deposits, so as to keep the collected tuition fees unaffected by price fluctuations, has been conducting the business of collecting tuition fees and transferring them to real-value savings deposits since the 13th of this month. In the past week, various schools have been coming to negotiate in an endless stream. As of yesterday (the 18th), Chunming, Pingmin, Zhonghua, Kongde and other schools have formally entrusted the department to collect and transfer savings; the Affiliated High School of the Normal University, Xiangshan Children's Welfare Institute, Chongde, Xiehua, and Minguo schools are in the process of applying. (Yu)

### 平人民银行储蓄部举办学费代收转存

1949-07-19
第2版()
专栏：

    平人民银行储蓄部
    举办学费代收转存
    【本报讯】人民银行北平分行储蓄部，为便利本市各学校收缴学费，并便于转存折实储蓄，以保持所收学费不受物价波动影响，特自本月十三日起举办代收学费转存折实储蓄业务，一周来各学校前往接洽者络绎不绝。截至昨日（十八日），已有春明、平民、中华、孔德等校正式委托该部代收转存；师大附中、香山慈幼院、崇德、协化、民国等校正办理申请手续中。      （宇）
