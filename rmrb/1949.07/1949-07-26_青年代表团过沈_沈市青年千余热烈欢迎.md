Title: Youth Delegation Passes Through Shenyang: Over a Thousand Shenyang Youth Enthusiastically Welcome Them

Author:
Date: 1949-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, July 25th: The Chinese delegation of 123 people, attending the Second World Democratic Youth Congress and the International Youth Day, passed through Shenyang on the evening of the 23rd on their way abroad. Over a thousand youth from all walks of life in Shenyang went to the station to welcome them. As the delegation's special train slowly entered the station, the welcomers continuously shouted slogans such as "Long Live the Solidarity of Democratic Youth Worldwide!" Xiao Hua, the deputy head of the delegation, addressed the welcomers, saying: The delegation will definitely bring the stories of the struggles of the youth in the Northeast and China to the whole world, and humbly learn from the Soviet Union's construction experience in order to build a new China. The delegation left Shenyang at 12 o'clock that night to continue its journey.

### 青年代表团过沈　沈市青年千余热烈欢迎

1949-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年代表团过沈
  　沈市青年千余热烈欢迎
    【新华社沈阳二十五日电】出席世界民主青年第二次代表大会和国际青年节的中国代表团一行一百二十三人，二十三日晚九时于出国途中经过沈阳。沈市各界青年一千余人，前往车站欢迎。当代表团的专车徐徐进入车站时，欢迎者不断高呼“全世界民主青年团结万岁”等口号。代表团副团长萧华向欢迎者致词，他说：代表团一定要把东北及中国青年的斗争事迹带到全世界去，并虚心学习苏联的建设经验，以便建设新中国。当晚十二时代表团离沈继续前行。
