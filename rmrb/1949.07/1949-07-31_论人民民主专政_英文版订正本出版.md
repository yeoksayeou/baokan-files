Title: "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" Revised English Edition Published
Author:
Date: 1949-07-31
Page: 1

　　"On the People's Democratic Dictatorship"
　　Revised English Edition Published
　　[Our Newspaper] The revised English edition of Chairman Mao's "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," printed by the Daily English News Agency, has been published. This book is bound together with Chairman Mao's speech at the preparatory meeting of the New Political Consultative Conference, and the price is 80 RMB per copy. Major bookstores in Peiping and Tianjin, as well as Guangming News Agency (telephone Peiping 3-1434, Tianjin 4-0723), are acting as sales agents. It is reported that the first edition of the book has been sold out, and a second edition is now being printed.

### “论人民民主专政”　英文版订正本出版

1949-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　“论人民民主专政”
  　英文版订正本出版
    【本报讯】每日英文电讯社印行之毛主席“论人民民主专政”英文版订正本业已出版，该书与毛主席在新政协筹备会上演词合订成册，每册售价人民币八十元。平津各大书局及光明报房（电话北平三——一四三四，天津四——○七二三）均有代售。闻该书初版业已售罄，现正再版中。
