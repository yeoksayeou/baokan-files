Title: Qingdao-Jinan Direct Passenger Train Service Opens, Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway Nanchang-Hangzhou Section Opens to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1949-07-17
Page: 1

    Qingdao-Jinan Direct Passenger Train Service Opens
    Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway Nanchang-Hangzhou Section Opens to Traffic
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Qingdao, 16th - With the full repair of the Jiaoji Railway, direct passenger train service between Qingdao and Jinan began on the 10th.
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Nanchang, 16th - The Nanchang to Hangzhou section of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway was fully opened to traffic on the 11th. The line is 635 kilometers long. The Liangjiadu Bridge south of Nanchang, due to the scale of the project, is not easily repaired for the time being, and will temporarily be connected by ferry.

### 青岛济南直达客车通车  浙赣路南昌杭州段通车

1949-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    青岛济南直达客车通车
    浙赣路南昌杭州段通车
    【北平新华广播电台广播】青岛十六日消息：胶济铁路全线修复以后，青岛、济南间的直达客车，已经在十日开始通车。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南昌十六日消息：浙赣铁路南昌到杭州段，已经在十一日全线通车。该线全长六百三十五公里，南昌以南梁家渡桥，因为工程较大，一时不易修复，暂时用摆渡衔接。
