Title: CCP Northwest Bureau and Northeast Bureau Hold Meetings to Celebrate the Party's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 2

　　【Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Second Dispatch】 The CCP Central Northwest Bureau and the CCP Central Northeast Bureau both convened conferences on the 1st to celebrate the 28th anniversary of the birth of the CCP. The CCP Central Northwest Bureau held a conference of party members and cadres in the morning, attended by more than a thousand people. Comrade Peng Dehuai, Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and First Secretary of the CCP Central Northwest Bureau, attended and delivered a speech. The CCP Central Northeast Bureau held a grand meeting in the afternoon to commemorate the 28th anniversary of the birth of the CCP, attended by more than 1,300 people. At the meeting, Comrade Li Fuchun gave a report, pointing out that the two major tasks of the Party in the current new situation are to thoroughly eliminate the remaining reactionary forces and to build a new China.

### 中共西北局和东北局　集会庆祝党的生日

1949-07-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　中共西北局和东北局
  　集会庆祝党的生日
    【北平新华广播电台二日消息】中共中央西北局和中共中央东北局，一日都召集了大会，庆祝中共诞生二十八周年。中共中央西北局召开的党员、干部大会，在一日上午举行，到会的有一千多人，中国人民解放军副总司令、中共中央西北局第一书记彭德怀同志，出席讲话。中共中央东北局，在一日下午召开盛大的中共诞生二十八周年纪念会，参加的有一千三百多人。会上，李富春同志报告，指出党在目前新形势下的两大任务，就是彻底肃清残余反动力量，和建设新中国。
