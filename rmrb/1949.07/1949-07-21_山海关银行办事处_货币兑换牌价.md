Title: Shanhaiguan Bank Office Currency Exchange Rate
Author:
Date: 1949-07-21
Page: 2

　　Shanhaiguan Bank Office
  　Currency Exchange Rate
    [Shanhaiguan News Dispatch] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank has changed its exchange rate since July 20th to one RMB (People's Currency) equaling eighty-five Northeast Yuan.

### 山海关银行办事处　货币兑换牌价

1949-07-21
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　山海关银行办事处
  　货币兑换牌价
    【山海关讯专电】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自七月二十日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券八十五元。
