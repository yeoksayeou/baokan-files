Title: Currency Exchange Rate of the Shanhai Pass Bank Office
Author: 
Date: 1949-07-15
Page: 2

　　Shanhaiguan Bank Office
  　Currency Exchange Rate
    [Shanhaiguan Dispatch] The joint office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank in Shanhaiguan has changed its exchange rate to one yuan of People's Currency for one hundred and ten yuan of Northeast Currency, effective from July 14th.

### 山海关银行办事处货币兑换牌价

1949-07-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关银行办事处
  　货币兑换牌价
    【山海关专电】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自七月十四日起，牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券一百一十元。
