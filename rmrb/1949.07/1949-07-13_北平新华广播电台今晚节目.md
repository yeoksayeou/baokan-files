Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-13
Page: 1

[This newspaper dispatch] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station tonight's program:

　　From 19:50 to 20:10, speech program: Ke Zhongping, head of the Northwest delegation attending the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers, will speak on: How to take our literary and art work a step further. From 19:30 to 19:50, youth program: Introducing the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth. From 22:20 to 22:50, arts program: Appreciation of Chinese music - Jiang Fengzhi's Nanhu solo.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，出席全国文学艺术工作者代表大会的西北代表团团长柯仲平讲：怎样把我们的文艺工作提高一步。十九点三十分到十九点五十分，青年节目，介绍中华全国民主青年联合总会。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，中乐欣赏——蒋风之南胡独奏。
