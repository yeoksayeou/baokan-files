Title: Cultural Congress Music Performance Open to the Public in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 2

　　【Our Newspaper Dispatches】The National Congress of Literary and Art Workers music performance will be held at the National Grand Theater for four consecutive days, on July 3, 4, 5, and 6. Except for the 6th, which is reserved for congress representatives, the other three days will be open to the public with tickets for sale.

　　This music performance is jointly presented by the Northeast, Northwest, and North China cultural troupes, factories, schools, song groups, and orchestras. The program includes orchestral ensemble, chorus, military band ensemble, Chinese music ensemble, and solo performances by experts. In terms of the nature of the music, there are marches, border folk songs, suites, choral pieces, and folk songs.

### 文代大会音乐演奏会在平公开演奏

1949-07-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　文代大会音乐演奏会在平公开演奏
    【本报讯】全国文学艺术工作者代表大会音乐演奏会，将在国民大戏院连续举行四天，日期为七月三、四、五、六日。其中除六日招待大会代表外，其余三天均公开售票。
    这一次的音乐演奏会，由东北、西北、华北各文工团、工厂、学校、歌团、乐队等联合演出，内容有管弦乐合奏、大合唱、军乐合奏、国乐合奏、专家的独奏独唱等。从乐曲的性质上说，有进行曲、边疆民谣、组曲、合唱曲、民歌等。
