Title: Paying Our Last Respects to the Great Leader of the Working People: Grand Funeral for Dimitrov Held in Sofia; Leaders of Fraternal Communist Parties All Attend to Pay Respects

Author:

Date: 1949-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th: TASS reports: Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, held the funeral of Dimitrov, the outstanding leader of the international working class, General Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Chairman of the National Committee of the Fatherland Front, on July 10th.

During the three days and three nights that Dimitrov's coffin lay in state in the Bulgarian Parliament Hall, 350,000 urban and rural working people of Bulgaria came to pay their respects, vowing before the coffin to be loyal to the cause of socialism and the cause of internationalism.

At noon on the 10th, members of the Politburo of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Chervenkov, Zhivkov, Yugov, Damianov, Poptomov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly Nechev, head of the Soviet government delegation Voroshilov, and Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Gorkin carefully carried Dimitrov's coffin out of the Parliament Hall.

The funeral procession slowly moved towards "September 9th" Square, where the temporary mausoleum was located. Following the coffin were: Dimitrov's wife, Rosa Dimitrova, and his children, members of the Politburo of the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Soviet government delegation headed by Voroshilov, delegations from Moscow, Leningrad, and Ukraine, and the leaders of the fraternal Communist Party delegations—General Secretary of the French Communist Party Thorez, Deputy General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party Longo, General Secretary of the British Communist Party Pollitt, Chairman of the German Socialist Unity Party Pieck, General Secretary of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Slánský, Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party Zambrowski, Secretary of the Romanian Workers' Party Luca, member of the Politburo of the Hungarian Workers' Party Gerő, member of the Politburo of the Greek Communist Party Partsalides, member of the Politburo of the Spanish Communist Party Uribe, etc. Following them were ministers of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers, representatives of diplomatic missions, secretaries of the National Committee of the Fatherland Front, members of the Presidium of the National Assembly, and prominent figures from the scientific, cultural, and artistic circles.

### 向劳动人民的伟大领袖致永别敬礼　保京隆重举行季米特洛夫葬仪  各兄弟共产党领袖均亲往送葬

1949-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　向劳动人民的伟大领袖致永别敬礼
  　保京隆重举行季米特洛夫葬仪
    各兄弟共产党领袖均亲往送葬
    【新华社北平十三日电】塔斯社报道：保加利亚首都索非亚，于七月十日举行国际工人阶级的卓越领袖、保加利亚共产党总书记、部长会议主席、祖国阵线全国委员会主席季米特洛夫的葬仪。
    在季米特洛夫灵柩停殡于保加利亚议会大厅的三天三夜期间，有三十五万保加利亚城乡劳动人民前往瞻仰，他们在灵前宣誓效忠于社会主义事业、国际主义事业。
    十日正午，保共中央政治局委员契尔文科夫、詹科夫、于哥夫、违米扬诺夫、波普托莫夫和国民议会主席团主席奈捷夫、苏联政府代表团团长伏罗希洛夫、苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金，谨慎地将季米特洛夫灵柩抬出议会大厅。
    出殡的行列慢慢地走向临时陵墓所在地的“九九”广场。走在灵柩后面的是：季米特洛夫的夫人罗沙·季米特洛娃和他的小孩、保加利亚共产党中央委员会政治局委员、以伏罗希洛夫为首的苏联政府代表团、莫斯科、列宁格勒和乌克兰的代表团、各兄弟共产党代表团的领袖——法共总书记多列士、意共副总书记隆哥、英共总书记波立特、德国统一社会党主席皮克、捷共总书记斯兰斯基、波统一工人党书记萨姆布劳斯基、罗工人党书记卢加、匈工人党政治局委员格罗、希共政治局委员巴萨利德斯、西共政治局委员乌里贝等。他们之后是保加利亚部长会议各部部长、外交使团代表、祖国阵线全国委员会各书记、国民议会主席团委员以及科学、文化与艺术界著名人士。
