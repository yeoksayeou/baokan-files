Title: Soviet Naval Academy to Open This Autumn
Author:
Date: 1949-07-21
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 20th -- Moscow Dispatch: The Soviet Naval Academy will open in Leningrad this autumn. The education period at the academy is two years. Those who have received higher education and have five years of work experience in the navy or in a leading position at the General Administration of the Northern Sea Route are eligible to become students of the Naval Academy.

### 苏海军学院  今秋开学

1949-07-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏海军学院
    今秋开学
    【新华社北平二十日电】莫斯科讯：苏联海军学院将于今秋在列宁格勒开学。该学院教育期限为两年。凡曾受高等教育并在海军中或在北海航线总管理处担任要职有五年工作经验者，得为海军学院学员。
