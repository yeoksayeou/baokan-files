Title: Dalian University Enrolling Students in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-07-12
Page: 2

　　Dalian University Enrolling Students in Beiping
    [This newspaper] Dalian University is about to hold entrance exams for first-year undergraduates in its Engineering and Medical Colleges for this academic year. The Beiping examination area will accept applications from July 18th to 20th, and the exams will be held on July 25th and 26th. The application location is at Peking University, and the exam location is at Normal University.

    This university has been under the direct leadership of the People's Government from its founding. It cultivates new-type technical cadres in accordance with the principles of New Democracy education. The university has taken over the equipment of the former South Manchuria Institute of Technology and Lushun Institute of Technology, and has also taken over the South Manchuria Railway Central Laboratory, Dalian Hygiene Research Institute, and Kwantung Hospital as teaching and research institutions. The first foreign language in the curriculum is Russian, making it the first university in the country to teach Russian.

### 大连大学在平招生

1949-07-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　大连大学在平招生
    【本报讯】大连大学即将招考本年度工、医学院本科一年级新生，北平考区七月十八日至二十日报名，七月二十五、六两日考试，报名地点在北大，考试地点在师大。
    该校从创校时起即完全在人民政府直接领导之下，依照新民主主义教育原则，培养新型技术干部。该校接收了过去南满工专、旅顺工大的设备，并接收满铁中央试验所、大连卫生研究所和关东医院，作为教学及研究机构。课程中第一外国语系俄语，为国内教授俄语之第一所大学。
