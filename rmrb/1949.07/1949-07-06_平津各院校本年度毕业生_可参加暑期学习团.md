Title: This Year's Graduates from Colleges and Universities in Beiping and Tianjin Can Participate in Summer Study Groups
Author:
Date: 1949-07-06
Page: 2

　　This Year's Graduates from Colleges and Universities in Beiping and Tianjin
　　Can Participate in Summer Study Groups
　　[Report from this newspaper] The North China Higher Education Commission issued a notice on the 5th stating: The commission has decided that all graduating students from Chaoyang College, China University, North China College and other colleges and universities in Beiping and Tianjin this year can participate in this year's summer study group for graduates of junior college and above (students who graduated in previous years are not allowed to enroll). If graduating students from the above colleges and universities this year voluntarily wish to participate, they should bring their credentials to apply to the commission at No. 66, North Chizi, Beiping, on the 8th and 9th of this month. After approval, they can join the study group.

### 平津各院校本年度毕业生　可参加暑期学习团

1949-07-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　平津各院校本年度毕业生
  　可参加暑期学习团
    【本报讯】华北高等教育委员会五日发出通告称：兹经本会决定，凡本年度朝阳学院、中国大学、华北学院诸平津院校毕业学生，可参加本届专科以上学校毕业学生暑期学习团（往年毕业者不得入学），以上各院校本年度毕业学生如自愿参加者，希于本月八、九两日、持证明文件至北平北池子六十六号本会申请，经核准后入团学习。
