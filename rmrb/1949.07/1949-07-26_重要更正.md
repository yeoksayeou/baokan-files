Title: Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1949-07-26
Page: 4

　　Important Correction
    In the "Soviet Studies" special issue of this newspaper on July 25th, fourth edition, the sentence "At this time, the proletarian dictatorship led by the proletariat and based on the worker-peasant alliance" in the article "The Struggle Between the Old and the New Under Soviet Social Conditions" on the seventeenth and eighteenth lines should be corrected to "At this time the proletarian dictatorship." We hereby make this correction.

### 重要更正

1949-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　重要更正
    本报七月二十五日第四版“苏联研究”专刊“苏维埃社会条件下的新旧斗争”一文第十七、十八行“这时候，无产阶级领导的，以工农联盟为基础的无产阶级专政，”一句，应改为“这时候无产阶级专政”，特此更正。
