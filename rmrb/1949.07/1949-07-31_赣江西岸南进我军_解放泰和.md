Title: Our Army Advances South on the West Bank of the Gan River; Taihe Liberated
Author:
Date: 1949-07-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Central China Front, 30th: The People's Liberation Army, advancing south on the west bank of the Gan River, liberated Taihe County, southwest of Ji'an, and strongholds such as Majiazhou and Baitujie, 40 li southwest of the city, on the 27th. The defending enemy's 23rd Army fled.

### 赣江西岸南进我军　解放泰和

1949-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　赣江西岸南进我军
  　解放泰和
    【新华社华中前线三十日电】赣江西岸向南挺进的人民解放军，二十七日解放吉安西南的泰和县城及城西南四十里的马家洲、白土街等据点。守敌二十三军逃窜。
