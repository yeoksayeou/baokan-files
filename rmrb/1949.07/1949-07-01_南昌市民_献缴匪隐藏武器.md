Title: Nanchang Citizens Voluntarily Surrender Hidden Weapons of Bandits
Author:
Date: 1949-07-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 1st—Citizens of Nanchang have voluntarily surrendered weapons and ammunition hidden by fleeing Kuomintang bandit troops. From the 13th to the 23rd of last month, in a period of ten days, they surrendered a total of 81 light and heavy machine guns, 8 submachine guns, 975 rifles, and a portion of ammunition.

### 南昌市民　献缴匪隐藏武器

1949-07-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　南昌市民
  　献缴匪隐藏武器
    【新华社南昌一日电】南昌市民献缴国民党匪军逃窜时隐藏的武器弹药。自上月十三日至二十三日十天内，共献缴出轻重机枪八十一挺，冲锋枪八支，步枪九百七十五支及弹药一部。
