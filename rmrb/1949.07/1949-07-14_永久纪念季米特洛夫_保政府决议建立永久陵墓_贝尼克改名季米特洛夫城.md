Title: Commemorating Dimitrov Forever: Bulgarian Government Decides to Build a Permanent Mausoleum; Bैनिक Renamed Dimitrov City
Author:
Date: 1949-07-14
Page: 3

　　Commemorating Dimitrov Forever
    Bulgarian Government Decides to Build a Permanent Mausoleum
  　Bैनिक Renamed Dimitrov City
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 13th: Sofia News: To commemorate Dimitrov permanently, the Bulgarian government has decided: To preserve Dimitrov's remains, a permanent mausoleum will be built on "September 9th" Square, where the temporary mausoleum is located. A Dimitrov monument will be erected in Sofia. The Dimitrov Medal will be established as Bulgaria's highest medal. Dimitrov's residence at 66 Oborishte Street will be converted into a museum. The complete works of Dimitrov will be published. The city of Bैनिक (southwest of Sofia) will be renamed Dimitrov City.

### 永久纪念季米特洛夫  保政府决议建立永久陵墓　贝尼克改名季米特洛夫城

1949-07-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　永久纪念季米特洛夫
    保政府决议建立永久陵墓
  　贝尼克改名季米特洛夫城
    【新华社北平十三日电】索非亚讯：保加利亚政府为永久纪念季米特洛夫，决议：为了保存季米特洛夫的遗体，即将在临时陵墓所在地的“九·九”广场建造永久陵墓。在索非亚建立季米特洛夫纪念碑。设立季米特洛夫勋章为保加利亚最高的奖章。将季米特洛夫在奥波陈斯开尔街的六十六号寓所改为博物馆。出版季米特洛夫全集。将贝尼克（索非亚西南）城改名为季米特洛夫城。
