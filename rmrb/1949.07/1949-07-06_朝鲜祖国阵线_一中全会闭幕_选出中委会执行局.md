Title: Korean Fatherland Front First Plenary Session Concludes, Elects Central Committee Executive Bureau
Author:
Date: 1949-07-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 5th: TASS, Pyongyang: The first plenary session of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Fatherland United Front has concluded here. The meeting elected a Central Committee Executive Bureau composed of 27 members, including Kim Il-sung, Kim Gu-bong, and Park Hon-yong; approved the Central Committee's organizational structure and leadership; and resolved to publish the newspaper "Fatherland Front."

### 朝鲜祖国阵线　一中全会闭幕　选出中委会执行局

1949-07-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜祖国阵线　一中全会闭幕
  　选出中委会执行局
    【新华社北平五日电】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主祖国联合阵线中央委员会第一次全会，已于此间闭幕。会议选出了由金日成、金＠奉、朴宪永等二十七人组成的中央委员会执行局，批准了中央委员会的机构及其领导，并决议发行机关报“祖国阵线”。
