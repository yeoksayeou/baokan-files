Title: Comrade Stalin Sends Telegram Congratulating Poland on Liberation Day; Leaders of Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Hungary, and Other Countries Also Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1949-07-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 23rd: According to the Polish Press Agency, Stalin, Shvernik, and the leaders of the governments of Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, and Hungary have all sent telegrams congratulating Poland on its National Day. Stalin's telegram to Polish Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz stated: "On the occasion of the anniversary of the liberation of the Polish nation and the fifth anniversary of Poland's rebirth, I extend friendly congratulations to you, Mr. Prime Minister, to the Polish government, and to the fraternal Polish nation, and I hope for the development of the new democratic Poland." Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky also sent telegrams to Polish President Bierut and Foreign Minister Modzelewski, respectively. President Gottwald of Czechoslovakia, Prime Minister Kolarov of Bulgaria, Deputy Prime Minister Rákosi of Hungary, and others sent telegrams to the Polish government on behalf of their respective peoples to express their congratulations.

### 斯大林同志　电贺波兰解放日　捷保匈等国领袖亦电祝贺

1949-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　斯大林同志
  　电贺波兰解放日
  　捷保匈等国领袖亦电祝贺
    【新华社北平二十三日电】据波兰通讯社报道：斯大林、斯维尔尼克和捷、保、匈政府领袖，均致电祝贺波兰国庆。斯大林致波总理西伦凯维兹的贺电称：“值此波兰民族解放日，波兰再生五周年之际，我仅向您，总理先生，向波兰政府和兄弟的波兰民族，致友谊的祝贺，并盼新民主的波兰获得发展。”苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席斯维尔尼克和苏外长维辛斯基，也分别电贺波兰总统贝鲁特和外长莫塞莱夫斯基。捷总统哥特瓦尔德、保总理科拉罗夫、匈副总理拉科西等，均代表本国人民致电波政府祝贺。
