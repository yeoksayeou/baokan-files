Title: Initiators of the Social Science Workers Representative Conference Gather Today to Unite National Social Science Workers and Dedicate Themselves to New Democratic Construction
Author:

　　Social Science Workers Representative Conference

　　Initiators Gather Today

    Unite National Social Science Workers

　　Dedicating Themselves to New Democratic Construction

    [Ping City News] The initiators' meeting of the Chinese Social Science Workers Representative Conference is scheduled to open at 3:00 PM today (the 14th) in Qin Zheng Hall, Zhongnanhai. The initiators include more than 230 social science workers from across the country in the fields of philosophy, economics, politics, law, and history. The meeting is scheduled to last from the 14th to the 17th. During this meeting, discussions will be held on the establishment of a preparatory committee for the Chinese Social Science Workers Representative Conference, so that the National Social Science Workers Representative Conference can be convened in the near future, a permanent organization can be established, national social science workers can be united, and they can dedicate themselves to the construction of China's New Democracy.

### 社会科学工作者代表会发起人今集会  团结全国社会科学工作者　致力新民主主义建设工作

1949-07-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　社会科学工作者代表会
  　发起人今集会
    团结全国社会科学工作者
  　致力新民主主义建设工作
    【平市讯】中国社会科学工作者代表会发起人会议，定于今（十四）日下午三时在中南海勤政殿开幕。发起人包括全国各地的哲学、经济、政治、法学、史学等部门的社会科学工作者共二百三十余人。会期预定自十四日至十七日。在此次会议中，将商讨成立中国社会科学工作者代表会议筹备会，俾能于最近期间召开全国社会科学工作者代表会议，成立永久性的组织，团结全国社会科学工作者，致力于中国新民主主义的建设。
