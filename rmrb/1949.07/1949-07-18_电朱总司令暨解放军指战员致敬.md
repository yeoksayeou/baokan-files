Title: Telegram of Respect to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De and the Commanders and Fighters of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1949-07-18
Page: 1

    【Ping City News】The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Social Science Workers Representative Conference sent a telegram of respect to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The original telegram is as follows: Respected Commander-in-Chief Zhu De and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:
    On the occasion of the founding of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Social Science Workers Representative Conference, we, on behalf of social scientists across the country, extend our highest revolutionary salute to you and all commanders and fighters. We thank you for your correct guidance and the heroic battles of all commanders and fighters, which defeated the counter-revolutionary armed forces headed by Chiang Kai-shek and have basically completed the liberation of the people of the whole country. From now on, we will have the possibility of engaging in Marxist-Leninist social science work in a free environment. We are determined to learn from your fighting spirit to arm ourselves with Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, so that our achievements on the ideological front can keep up with your great achievements on the military front, and so that we can work as effectively as you for the cause of national construction of New Democracy.
        The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Social Science Workers Representative Conference, July 17th

### 电朱总司令暨解放军指战员致敬

1949-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    电朱总司令暨解放军指战员致敬
    【平市讯】中华全国社会科学工作者代表会议筹备会向朱总司令暨中国人民解放军全体指战员致敬电，原电如下：敬爱的朱总司令暨中国人民解放军全体指战员：
    当此中华全国社会科学工作者代表会议筹备会宣告成立之日，我们谨代表全国社会科学界同人向你和全体指战员致最崇高的革命敬礼。我们感谢你的正确指导和全体指战员的英勇战斗，打败了以蒋介石为首的反革命武力，并已在基本上完成了全国人民的解放，从此我们就有了在自由环境下从事马列主义社会科学工作的可能。我们誓必学习你们的战斗精神来以马克思列宁主义和毛泽东思想武装我们自己，使我们在思想战线上的成就能够赶得上你们在军事战线上的伟大成就，使我们能够和你们同样有效地为新民主主义的国家建设事业而工作。
        中华全国社会科学工作者代表会议筹备会  七月十七日
