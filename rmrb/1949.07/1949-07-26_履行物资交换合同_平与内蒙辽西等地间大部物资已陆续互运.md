Title: Fulfillment of Materials Exchange Contract: Most Materials Between Beiping and Inner Mongolia, Western Liaoning, etc., Have Been Mutually Transported
Author:
Date: 1949-07-26
Page: 2

　　Fulfillment of Materials Exchange Contract
  　Most Materials Between Beiping and Inner Mongolia, Western Liaoning, etc., Have Been Mutually Transported
    [Our Newspaper] The materials exchanged between the Beiping Trading Company and Inner Mongolia, Western Liaoning, Jilin, and other external regions have been mutually transported in accordance with the provisions of the contract. As of the 25th of this month, the materials that the external regions should supply to Beiping include: two batches of Inner Mongolian wool, totaling more than 117,000 jin. The first batch of millet from Western Liaoning is 180,000 jin, and 28.8 cubic meters of wood. 120,000 jin of sugar from Jilin is being shipped continuously. The industrial products, etc., that Beiping should supply to the external regions have also been mostly shipped out. The goods supplied to Inner Mongolia have been completely delivered, and most of the materials to be supplied to Jilin and Western Liaoning have been paid, with only a few goods such as soap remaining.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Yu)

### 履行物资交换合同　平与内蒙辽西等地间大部物资已陆续互运

1949-07-26
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　履行物资交换合同
  　平与内蒙辽西等地间大部物资已陆续互运
    【本报讯】北平贸易公司与外区内蒙、辽西、吉林等地交换之物资，已依照合同规定陆续互运。截至本月二十五日，外区应供北平之物资有：内蒙羊毛两批，总计十一万七千余斤。辽西首批小米十八万斤，木材二八点八立方米。吉林砂糖十二万斤，正陆续装运中。至北平应供给外区之工业品等亦大部运出，供内蒙之货品已交清，应供吉林、辽西之物资已付出大部，所余仅香皂等少数货品。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（宇）
