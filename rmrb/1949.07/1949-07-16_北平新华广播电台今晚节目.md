Title: Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
Author:
Date: 1949-07-16
Page: 1

    Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This Newspaper News] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's lecture program from 19:50 to 20:10 tonight will broadcast the recording of dancer Dai Ailian's speech on dance work at the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers. From 19:30 to 19:50, the Youth Time program will introduce the Central Committee School of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. From 22:20 to 22:50, the entertainment program will feature Mr. Lian Kuoru of the Quyi Association telling the second part of the new Ping Shu "The Red Army Seizes the Wujiang River."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，播送舞蹈家戴爱莲在中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会上关于舞蹈工作的发言（录音）。十九点三十分到十九点五十分青年时间，介绍中国新民主主义青年团中央团校。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由曲艺公会连阔如先生说新评书“红军抢渡乌江”第二段。
