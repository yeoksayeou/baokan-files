Title: Ping City and Tianjin & Taiyuan Post Offices Interconnect High-Value Postal Money Orders; Ping City Post Office Revises Business Hours
Author:
Date: 1949-07-20
Page: 2

    Ping City and Tianjin & Taiyuan Post Offices
    Interconnect High-Value Postal Money Orders
    Ping City Post Office Revises Business Hours
    [Ping City News] Starting July 18th, the summer business hours for Ping City post offices will be changed to 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Sundays and holidays will remain 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM. Additionally, the Beiping Postal Administration will, from today, interconnect with the Taiyuan Post Office, Tianjin Post Office, and Tianjin Postal Money Order Bureau for high-value postal money orders. The limit for the former is 300,000 yuan, and for the latter two bureaus, it is 500,000 yuan.

### 平市与津并两邮局  互通高额信汇汇票  平市邮亭改订营业时间

1949-07-20
第2版()
专栏：

    平市与津并两邮局
    互通高额信汇汇票
    平市邮亭改订营业时间
    【平市讯】平市各邮亭夏季营业时间自七月十八日起，改为上午九时至十二时，下午三时至六时，星期日及例假日仍为下午三时至五时。又：北平邮政管理局自即日起与太原邮局、天津邮局及天津邮汇局互通高额信汇汇票，限额前者为三十万元，后两局为五十万元。
