Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Again Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-07-30
Page: 2
Economic News

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
  　Adjusted Again Starting Yesterday
    [Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank of China have changed the exchange rate to 1 RMB to 60 Northeast Yuan starting from July 29th.

### 山海关货币牌价　昨起再调整

1949-07-30
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　山海关货币牌价
  　昨起再调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自七月二十九日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券六十元。
