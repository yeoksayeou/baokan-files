Title: Changlu Salt Industry Company Adjusts Salt Prices
Author:
Date: 1949-07-19
Page: 2

    Changlu Salt Industry Company Adjusts Salt Prices
    [Ping City News] The Changlu Salt Industry Company has adjusted the price of edible salt. The price for cooperatives and government organizations is 23.5 yuan per jin for loose salt, and 24.5 yuan per jin for general merchants and civilians.

### 长芦盐业公司调整食盐售价

1949-07-19
第2版()
专栏：

    长芦盐业公司调整食盐售价
    【平市讯】长芦盐业公司调整食盐价格，对合作社及机关散盐每斤价二三·五元，一般商民每斤二四·五元。
