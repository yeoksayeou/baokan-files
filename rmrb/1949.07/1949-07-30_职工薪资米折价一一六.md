Title: Employee Salary Millet Conversion Price: 116
Author:
Date: 1949-07-30
Page: 2

Economic News

　　Employee Salary Millet Conversion Price: 116
　　[This Newspaper’s News] The employee salary millet conversion price for the latter half of July in Ping City, after assessment by the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions and other relevant departments, and approved by the North China People's Government, will be issued at a rate of 116 yuan per catty (approximately 500 grams).

### 职工薪资米折价一一六

1949-07-30
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　职工薪资米折价一一六
