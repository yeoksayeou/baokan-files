Title: North China People's Government Notice: Holiday for "July 7th"
Author:
Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 1

　　North China People's Government Notice: Holiday for "July 7th"
    To commemorate the twelfth anniversary of the historically significant "July 7th" Resistance War, it is hereby decided that all government agencies, organizations, factories, and schools will have a one-day holiday on July 7th to hold gatherings for publicity and celebrate enthusiastically. This notice is for
    all levels of government agencies, organizations, factories, and schools.
    North China People's Government, July 1st

### 华北人民政府通知　“七七”放假一天

1949-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府通知 　 “七七”放假一天
    为纪念具有伟大历史意义的“七七”抗战十二周年，兹决定于七月七日各机关、团体、工厂、学校均放假一日，集会宣传，热烈庆祝。右通知
    各级机关、团体、工厂、学校。
    华北人民政府　七月一日
