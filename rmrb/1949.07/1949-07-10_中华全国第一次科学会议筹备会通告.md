Title: Circular from the Preparatory Committee of the First National Science Conference of China
Author:
Date: 1949-07-10
Page: 1

　　Circular from the Preparatory Committee of the First National Science Conference of China
    In order to wait for the 49 members of the preparatory committee who are traveling from Nanjing and Shanghai to arrive in Beiping, the preparatory meeting is postponed to 9:00 AM on the 13th of this month at the Sino-French University in Donghuangchenggen. Preparatory committee members who have come to Beiping or are in Beiping can still report to the office of the committee at the European and American Students Association in Nanheyan before the 13th (no separate notice will be given).

### 中华全国第一次科学会议筹备会通告

1949-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国第一次科学会议筹备会通告
    兹为等候宁沪在旅行程中筹委四十九人到平起见，特将筹备大会展期至本月十三日上午九时开始在东皇城根中法大学举行，凡来平或在平之筹委，仍可于十三日前来南河沿欧美同学会内本会办公处报到（不另通知）。
