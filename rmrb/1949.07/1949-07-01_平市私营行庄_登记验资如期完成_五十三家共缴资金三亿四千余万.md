Title: Private Banks and Credit Institutions in Beiping Complete Registration and Capital Verification as Scheduled; 53 Institutions Pay a Total of 340 Million Yuan in Capital
Author:
Date: 1949-07-01
Page: 2

　　Economic News

　　Private Banks and Credit Institutions in Beiping
　　Registration and Capital Verification Complete as Scheduled
    53 Institutions Pay a Total of 340 Million Yuan in Capital
    [Reported by this newspaper] The registration and capital verification for private banks and credit institutions in the city, which began on May 20th by the Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China, was completed on schedule yesterday (30th). The institutions that have registered and fully paid their capital are: 17 banks, with a total verified cash payment of 180.06 million yuan; 36 credit institutions, with a total verified cash payment of 160.033766 million yuan. The total verified cash received is 340.093766 million yuan. The branch will immediately conduct a formal preliminary review and submit it to the Beiping Municipal People's Government for review in early July.
　　　　　　　　　
　　(Yu)

### 平市私营行庄　登记验资如期完成  五十三家共缴资金三亿四千余万

1949-07-01
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　平市私营行庄
  　登记验资如期完成
    五十三家共缴资金三亿四千余万
    【本报讯】人民银行北平分行于五月二十日开始办理之本市私营行庄申请登记及缴验资金事宜，已于昨（卅日）如期完成。已登记并全部缴验资金者计有：银行十七家共缴验现金一亿八千零六十万元。钱庄三十六家，共缴验现金一亿六千零三十三万七千六百六十元。总计收验现金三亿四千零九十三万七千六百六十元。该行于即日起进行正式之初步审查，于七月上旬陆续呈送北平市人民政府审核。　　　　　　　　　
　　（宇）
