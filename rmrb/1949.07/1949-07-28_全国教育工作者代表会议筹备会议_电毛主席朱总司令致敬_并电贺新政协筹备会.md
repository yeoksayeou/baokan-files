Title: Preparatory Meeting of the National Education Workers' Representative Conference Sends Respects to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Congratulates the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-07-28
Page: 1

Preparatory Meeting of the National Education Workers' Representative Conference
Sends Respects to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu
And Congratulates the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference

Respected Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu:
　　As the Chinese People's Liberation War is about to achieve nationwide victory, the preparatory meeting of our All-China Education Workers' Representative Conference opens in Beiping. We sincerely thank Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party for their correct leadership, and thank Commander-in-Chief Zhu for his wise command and all the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army for their heroic struggle. From now on, all education workers across the country, regardless of their ethnicity, region, or work position, are determined to unite closely under Chairman Mao's New Democratic cultural and educational policies, respond to Chairman Mao's great call to "restore and develop the people's cultural and educational undertakings," and serve the people wholeheartedly and diligently, to achieve the goal expected by Commander-in-Chief Zhu, which is: "to transform the old China, plagued by widespread illiteracy and backward education, into a new China with universal education and developed culture in culture." We extend our highest respect.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Preparatory Meeting of the First All-China Education Workers' Representative Conference
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 26

To the Gentlemen of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference:
　　The great victory of the Chinese People's Liberation War and the preparatory convening of the New Political Consultative Conference have opened a new era in Chinese history. From now on, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese people will devote their utmost efforts to the restoration and construction of the people's economy and the people's culture and education. Thanks to your call and assistance, education workers from all over the country and various departments are meeting in Beiping, preparing for the first National Education Workers' Representative Conference, to unite closely under Chairman Mao's New Democratic cultural and educational policies, strive to build a national, scientific, and mass education, and strive to establish and consolidate a new China of the people's democratic dictatorship. We firmly support the convening of the New Political Consultative Conference and the establishment of a democratic coalition government. We congratulate you on the success of your work and extend our highest respect.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Preparatory Meeting of the First All-China Education Workers' Representative Conference
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 26

### 全国教育工作者代表会议筹备会议　电毛主席朱总司令致敬  并电贺新政协筹备会

1949-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国教育工作者代表会议筹备会议
  　电毛主席朱总司令致敬
    并电贺新政协筹备会敬爱的毛主席、朱总司令：
    当中国人民解放战争即将全国胜利之时，我们中华全国教育工作者代表会议的筹备会议在北平开幕。我们衷心地感谢毛主席和中国共产党的正确领导，感谢朱总司令的英明指挥和中国人民解放军全体指战员的英勇奋战。今后，全国教育工作者，不问所属民族、所在地区和工作岗位的不同，矢志在毛主席的新民主主义的文化教育方针下，紧紧团结起来，响应毛主席“恢复和发展人民的文化教育事业”的伟大号召，忠诚勤劳地为人民大众服务，达到朱总司令所期望的目的，就是：“在文化上使文盲遍地、教育落后的旧中国，建设成为教育普及、文化发达的新中国”。谨致崇高的敬礼。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国第一次教育工作者代表会议筹备会议
　　　　　　　　　　　　　七月二十六日新政治协商会议筹备会诸先生：
    中国人民解放战争的伟大胜利，和新政治协商会议的筹备召开，开辟了中国历史的新时期，从此中国人民将在中国共产党的领导下，用最大力量，从事于人民经济与人民文化教育的恢复与建设。由于你们的号召和帮助，我们来自全国各地和各种部门的教育工作者正在北平开会。筹备第一次全国教育工作者的代表会议，在毛主席的新民主主义的文化教育方针下，紧紧团结起来，为建设民族的、科学的、大众的教育而努力，为建立与巩固人民民主专政的新中国而努力。我们坚决拥护新政协的召开，和民主联合政府的成立。我们祝贺你们工作的成功，并向你们致崇高的敬礼。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国第一次教育工作者代表会议筹备会议
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　七月二十六日
