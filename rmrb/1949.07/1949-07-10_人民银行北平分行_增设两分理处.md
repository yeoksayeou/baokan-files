Title: People's Bank of Beiping Branch Adds Two Sub-Branches
Author: Yu
Date: 1949-07-10
Page: 2

　　People's Bank of Beiping Branch
  　Adds Two Sub-Branches
    [This newspaper's report] To facilitate citizens and students, the People's Bank of Beiping Branch has specially established sub-branches at 53 Xizhimen Avenue and at Tsinghua University in Xiyuan. The Xizhimen Avenue sub-branch is scheduled to open on July 11th, handling the collection of grain payments, electricity, and telephone fees for trade companies, etc. The Tsinghua sub-branch opened on the 8th of this month, handling remittances, current deposits, inflation-proof savings, and school payment agency services. (Yu)

### 人民银行北平分行　增设两分理处
宇
1949-07-10
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　人民银行北平分行
  　增设两分理处
    【本报讯】人民银行北平分行为便利市民学生，特于西直门大街五十三号及西苑清华大学内各设分理处一所。西直门大街分理处定七月十一日开业，办理代收贸易公司粮款、电灯、电话费等业务。清华分理处已于本月八日开业，办理汇款、活期存款、折实储蓄及代理校款支付等业务。（宇）
