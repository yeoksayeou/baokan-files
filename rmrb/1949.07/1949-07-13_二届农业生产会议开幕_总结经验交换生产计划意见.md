Title: Second Agricultural Production Conference Opens: Summarizing Experiences and Exchanging Opinions on Production Plans
Author:
Date: 1949-07-13
Page: 1

　　Second Agricultural Production Conference Opens
  　Summarizing Experiences and Exchanging Opinions on Production Plans
    [Ping City News] The Second North China Agricultural Production Conference, convened by the Agricultural Department of the North China People's Government, opened yesterday (the 12th) in Ping City. Attendees included the directors of agricultural departments (provinces and cities) and section chiefs and department heads of the Agricultural Department, as well as responsible personnel from directly affiliated units, presided over by Vice Minister Zhang Chong. The conference will first summarize the agricultural production movement in the first half of this year, and then discuss and decide on various important issues in future agricultural production. Finally, preliminary exchange of opinions will be conducted on the issues of the 1950 agricultural production plan, in order to formulate a major production plan for the coming year. The conference is scheduled to last for six days.

### 二届农业生产会议开幕　总结经验交换生产计划意见

1949-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　二届农业生产会议开幕
  　总结经验交换生产计划意见
    【平市讯】华北人民政府农业部召开的第二届华北农业生产会议，已于昨日（十二日）在平开幕。到有各行署（省、市）农业处长及农业部处科长与各直属单位负责人员，由张冲副部长亲自主持。会议首先总结今年上半年的农业生产运动，进而研讨决定今后农业生产上的各种重要问题。最后并将就一九五○年农业生产计划诸问题，初步交换意见，以便拟订明年度的大生产计划。此次会议预定六天结束。
