Title: Building the School and Studying Simultaneously: Trainees at the People's Liberation Army Naval Academy Improve Ideological Understanding
Author:
Date: 1949-07-14
Page: 3

[Broadcast by the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Northeast, 13th dispatch: After more than a month of political study, the ideological understanding of the trainees at the People's Liberation Army Naval Academy has generally improved. The trainees are former officers and soldiers from the "Chongqing" warship, which revolted, and the "Lingfu" warship, who voluntarily came to the liberated areas to participate in the construction of the People's Navy. Since May, they have been building the school and studying diligently at the same time. In the process of studying, they have initially established the concept of serving the people.

### 一面建校一面学习　解放军海军学校　学员思想认识提高

1949-07-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　一面建校一面学习
  　解放军海军学校
  　学员思想认识提高
    【北平新华广播电台广播】东北十三日消息：人民解放军海军学校学员，经过一个多月的政治学习，思想认识已经普遍提高一步，该校学员，都是起义的“重庆号”，和自动来解放区参加人民海军建设的前国民党“灵甫号”官兵，他们从五月开始，一面建校一面努力学习。在学习过程中，初步建立了为人民服务的观点。
