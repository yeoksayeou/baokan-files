Title: Boxes in the Iron Wagon
Author: Ma Fanto
Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 4

　　Boxes in the Iron Wagon
　　Ma Fanto
　　The Shanghai-Nanjing-Hangzhou delegation's train
　　is heading from Shanghai to Beiping.
　　Among them is an iron wagon
　　that carries no people, only things.
　　Boxes of all sizes and shapes:
　　In tin boxes are film copies,
　　marked on the lid are "Lights of Ten Thousand Homes,"
　　"The Spring River Flows East,"
　　and "The White-Haired Girl."
　　The long tubes and flat boxes contain cartoons, pictures, newspaper clippings, and woodcuts.
　　The large and small iron boxes are opera costumes.
　　This box is being sent to the art school and China University,
　　that box is marked for the Organizing Committee of the Conference to receive.
　　Musical instruments, books, materials, paper plates...
　　Various literary and artistic works: novels, plays, poems, music,
　　weapons that have fought the Kuomintang's scissors, bayonets, and whips for five years.
　　In addition, there is a large copper drum in the corner,
　　ready to go to Beiping to participate in a grand ensemble
　　—the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers—
　　to beat out a booming sound.
　　The comrades of the guard squad
　　watch over these boxes day and night,
　　leaning on the boxes, their heads
　　swaying with the moving train,
　　Rumbling, rumbling, clatter, clatter...
　　The wheels fly quickly past
　　many liberated big cities,
　　Nanjing, Bengbu, Xuzhou, Jinan, Tianjin,
　　passing through the loess plains, corn and wheat fields,
　　passing through newly liberated areas, stations and villages leaving traces of war,
　　passing through the bustling markets of the old liberated areas, farm women and mule carts,
　　passing over the iron bridge destroyed by the fleeing bandits,
　　but our new bridge piers are already being built with clanging sounds...
　　The train speeds! The wheels roll!
　　Forward! Forward!
　　A literary and artistic force is advancing
　　to join forces with brotherly troops!

### 铁篷车里的箱笼
马凡陀
1949-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　铁篷车里的箱笼
    马凡陀
    沪宁杭代表团的列车
    从上海向北平进发。
    当中有一节铁篷车
    不载人，只装东西。
    大大小小方方圆圆的箱笼：
    马口铁匣子里是电影拷贝，
    盖上注明那是“万家灯火”，
    那是“一江春水向东流”，
    还有“祥林嫂”。
    长筒子扁匣子装的漫画、图片、报贴、木刻，
    大铁箱小铁箱是平剧的行头，
    这箱送去艺专，华大，
    那箱标明大会筹委会收。
    乐器、书籍、资料、纸版……
    各种文艺作品：小说、剧本、诗歌、音乐，
    五年来和反动派的剪刀、刺刀、皮鞭
    搏斗过来的武器。
    此外，角落里还有一面大铜鼓，
    准备到北平去参加一个大合奏
    ——全国文学艺术工作者代表大会——
    去敲出蓬蓬的巨声。
    警卫班同志们
    日夜看守着这些箱笼，
    靠在箱子上，他们的头
    随着行进的列车摆动，
    隆隆，隆隆，格达，格达……
    车轮飞快经过
    许多解放了的大城市，
    南京、蚌埠、徐州、济南、天津，
    经过黄土的原野，包谷小麦的田地，
    经过新解放区，留下战迹的车站、庄村，
    经过老解放区热闹的市集，农妇和骡车，
    经过被逃跑的匪军炸毁的铁桥，
    可是我们的新的桥座已经在叮当修造……
    列车飞奔！车轮飞滚！
    向前进！向前进！
    一枝文艺的部队正在进发
    要去和兄弟的队伍会师！
