Title: Zhangyuan People's Bank Develops Remittance Business
Author: Zhou Lin
Date: 1949-07-01
Page: 2

　　Zhangyuan People's Bank
  　Development of Remittance Business
    [Chahar News] The Zhangjiakou Municipal People's Bank branch is actively developing remittance services to facilitate material exchange and trade. The number of remittance points has now increased to thirty-three, including Beiping, Tianjin, Tangshan, Baoding, Shijiazhuang, Datong, Taiyuan, Yuci, Zhangbei, Anguo, Xuanhua, Yuxian, Yanggao, Lixian, Dingxing, Xushui, Cangzhou, Yixian, Fengzhen, Huailai, Zhuoxian, Hunyuan, Yang, Fuping, Tanggu, and other places. According to public demand, it is now preparing to open remittance services with Duolun, Jining, Qixiaying, Handan, Nangong, and Zhengding. (Zhou Lin)

### 张垣人民银行　汇兑业务发展
周林
1949-07-01
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　张垣人民银行
  　汇兑业务发展
    【察哈尔讯】张家口市人民银行分行，为交流物资，便利交易，积极举办汇兑业务。通汇点现已增到三十三处，计北平、天津、唐山、保定、石家庄、大同、太原、榆次、张北、安国、宣化、蔚县、阳高、蠡县、定兴、徐水、沧州、易县、丰镇、怀来、涿县、浑源、阳、阜平、塘沽等处。根据群众要求，现正准备和多伦、集宁、旗下营、邯郸、南宫、正定等处通汇。　（周林）
