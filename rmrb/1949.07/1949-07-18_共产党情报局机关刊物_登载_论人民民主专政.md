Title: Communist Information Bureau Organ Publishes "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship"
Author:
Date: 1949-07-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 17th: A dispatch from the Polish Press Agency: The just-published 15th issue of the Communist Information Bureau's organ "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy" contains Chairman Mao's "July 1st" essay, "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship." The majority of the issue's space is dedicated to articles commemorating the death of Dimitrov.

"For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy" will be changed from a bi-weekly to a weekly publication starting September 15th, published in Russian, French, English, German, and Romanian.

### 共产党情报局机关刊物  登载“论人民民主专政”

1949-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    共产党情报局机关刊物
    登载“论人民民主专政”
    【新华社北平十七日电】波兰通讯社消息：刚刚出版的共产党情报局机关刊“争取持久和平与人民民主”第十五期，登载了毛主席的“七一”论文“论人民民主专政”。该期大部分的篇幅，是刊登吊念季米特洛夫逝世的文章。
    “争取持久和平与人民民主”将自九月十五日起由双周刊改为周刊，以俄、法、英、德和罗马尼亚文出版。
