Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-25
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This newspaper dispatch] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's speech program from 19:50 to 20:10 tonight will broadcast a recording of the poet Xiao San speaking about "What I Saw and Heard While Visiting the Soviet Union." Women's program from 19:30 to 19:50: (1) Introduction to the Asian Women's Representative Conference to be held in China; (2) Introducing a conversation with an old woman, Shen. Literary program from 22:20 to 22:50, the North China Mass Drama Society will broadcast the Yangko opera "The Whole Family is Busy."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，播送诗人萧三讲：“游苏见闻”的录音。十九点三十分到十九点五十分妇女节目：（一）介绍将在中国召开的亚洲妇女代表会议；（二）介绍一位沈老太太的谈话。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由华北群众剧社播送秧歌剧“全家忙”。
