Title: Bulgarian National Assembly Holds Special Session, Kolarov Elected Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1949-07-23
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, July 22nd: Sofia - The Bulgarian National Assembly held a special session here on the 19th. Raiko Damyanov, Chairman of the National Assembly Committee, delivered a speech at the opening session, mourning Dimitrov. At the end of his speech, Damyanov proposed that the Assembly pay tribute to Dimitrov, the great son of the Bulgarian people. All members of the Assembly immediately stood in silence for one minute. On the 20th, the special session proceeded with the election of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers (i.e., Prime Minister). The Bulgarian Communist Party parliamentary group and the Bulgarian National Agrarian Union parliamentary group proposed that Kolarov, former Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs, be elected as Chairman of the Council of Ministers. All members of the Assembly immediately stood up and cheered for a long time, expressing their full support for this proposal. The proposal was then put to a vote and unanimously approved.

### 保国民议会举行特别会议　科拉罗夫当选总理

1949-07-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　保国民议会举行特别会议
  　科拉罗夫当选总理
    【新华社北平二十二日电】索非亚讯：保加利亚国民议会于十九日在此间举行特别会议。国民议会委员会主席拉伊科·达姆扬诺夫在开幕会议上致词，悼念季米特洛夫。在结束其演辞时，达姆扬诺夫提议议会向保加利亚人民伟大的儿子季米特洛夫致哀。全体议员当即肃立默哀一分钟。二十日，特别会议进行了部长会议主席（即总理）的选举。保加利亚共产党议会党团和保加利亚全国农民联盟议会党团建议，推选前部长会议副主席兼外长科拉罗夫为部长会议主席。全体议员一致立起，欢呼了好久，表示完全支持这一建议。这一建议随即付诸表决，得到全体一致的通过。
