Title: Soviet Union Foreign Cultural Association Hosts Foreign Writers
Author:
Date: 1949-07-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st: Moscow News: Denisov, Chairman of the Committee of the All-Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted foreign writers who came to the Soviet Union to participate in the 150th anniversary celebration of Pushkin's birth on June 28th. The foreign writer delegations in attendance included Xiao San from China, Li Jiying (transliteration) from Korea, as well as writers from Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Romania, England, Italy, the Netherlands, Finland, and Chile. The Soviet side was represented by Simonov and Tikhonov, Deputy Secretary-Generals of the Soviet Writers' Union, as well as writers, People's Artists, painters, workers in science, literature and art, members of the All-Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and representatives from the Soviet press.

### 苏联对外文协招待外国作家

1949-07-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联对外文协招待外国作家
    【新华社北平一日电】莫斯科讯：全苏对外文化协会委员会主席捷尼索夫，于六月二十八日招待到苏联参加普希金一百五十周年寿辰庆典的外国作家。出席的外国作家代表团，包括中国的萧三、朝鲜的李吉英（译音），以及捷、保、罗、英、意、荷、芬、智利的作家。苏联方面出席者有苏联作家协会副总书记西蒙诺夫和吉洪诺夫以及作家、人民艺术家、画家，和科学、文学与艺术工作者，全苏对外文化协会会员，苏联报界代表等。
