Title: Chairman Mao's July 1st Thesis Published in Full by Polish "People's Tribune" Newspaper, Accompanied by a Half-Length Portrait of Chairman Mao
Author: 
Date: 1949-07-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th: The Polish United Workers' Party organ, "People's Tribune" newspaper, published the full text of Chairman Mao's thesis "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" on July 10th, accompanied by a half-length portrait of Chairman Mao.

### 毛主席七一论文波“人民论坛”报全文刊载  并登有毛主席半身肖像

1949-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席七一论文
    波“人民论坛”报全文刊载
    并登有毛主席半身肖像
    【新华社布拉格十六日电】波兰统一工人党机关报“人民论坛”报，七月十日刊载毛主席“论人民民主专政”论文的全文，并登有毛主席的半身肖像。
