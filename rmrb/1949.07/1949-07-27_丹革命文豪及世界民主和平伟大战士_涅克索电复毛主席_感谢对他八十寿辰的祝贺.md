Title: Danish Revolutionary Writer and Great Warrior for World Democracy and Peace, Nexo Cables Chairman Mao, Thanking Him for His Eightieth Birthday Greetings
Author:
Date: 1949-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th. Danish famous writer Nexo cabled on the 24th of this month to thank Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China for his congratulations on his eightieth birthday. The cable said: "Thank you for your friendly congratulations. We are proud of our comrades in China. Martin Andri Nexo." It is reported that June 25th of this year was Nexo's eightieth birthday, and on June 24th, Chairman Mao sent a telegram to congratulate him, saying: "Respectfully wish Comrade Nexo, the Danish revolutionary writer and great warrior for the cause of world democracy and peace, a happy eightieth birthday."

### 丹革命文豪及世界民主和平伟大战士　涅克索电复毛主席  感谢对他八十寿辰的祝贺

1949-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　丹革命文豪及世界民主和平伟大战士
  　涅克索电复毛主席
    感谢对他八十寿辰的祝贺
    【新华社北平二十六日电】丹麦著名作家涅克索于本月二十四日电谢中共中央毛泽东主席对他八十寿辰的祝贺。电称：“谢谢你的友好的祝贺。我们因为我们在中国的同志而自豪。马丁·安德里·涅克索。”按本年六月二十五日是涅克索的八十岁生日，六月二十四日毛主席曾去电致贺称：“敬祝丹麦革命文豪及世界民主和平事业的伟大战士涅克索同志的八十寿辰。”
