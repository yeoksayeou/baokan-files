Title: New York Auto Workers' Strike Ends in Victory
Author:
Date: 1949-07-25
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th: Reports from various sources: The strike of 3,300 New York City bus workers belonging to the Transport Workers Union of the CIO ended victoriously on the 22nd. The strike began simultaneously on twenty-two bus routes at noon on the 14th of this month in opposition to the capitalists' dismissal of four mechanics. The capitalists have now agreed to reinstate the four mechanics.

### 纽约汽车工人罢工胜利结束

1949-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　纽约汽车工人罢工胜利结束
    【新华社北平二十四日电】各方报道：属于产联运输工人工会的纽约市公共汽车工人三千三百名的罢工，已于二十二日胜利结束。罢工于本月十四日中午在二十二条公共汽车路线上同时开始，以反对资方之解雇四名机械士。资方现已答应恢复四名机械士的职务。
