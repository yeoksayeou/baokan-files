Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-09
Page: 1

　　Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
    [This newspaper dispatch] Tonight from 19:50 to 20:10, Comrade Zhou Yang will give a lecture entitled "New People's Literature and Art." From 19:30 to 19:50, the youth program will broadcast "From Lifelessness to Vitality," which discusses the reform of the curriculum in the Department of Law at Peking University. From 22:20 to 22:50, the cultural program will broadcast recordings of songs by workers in Lüda.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，周扬同志讲：“新的人民的文艺”。十九点三十分到十九点五十分青年时间，播送“从死气沉沉到生气勃勃”，讲的是北京大学法律系课程改革的经过。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，播送旅大工人歌曲录音。
