Title: US Progressives Defend Democratic Rights to Hold Bill of Rights Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-07-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New York (TASS dispatch): Many prominent American progressives will hold a "Bill of Rights Conference" here on the 16th and 17th to defend democratic rights. Forman, the chairman of the conference, stated to the press that 564 American progressives have declared their support for the conference, including 119 professors from 84 universities and colleges, 88 ministers, 76 labor union leaders, 72 Black leaders, and many writers, artists, lawyers, and other professionals.

### 美进步人士保卫民主权利将举行人权法案会议

1949-07-18
第3版()
专栏：

    美进步人士保卫民主权利
    将举行人权法案会议
    【新华社北平十六日电】塔斯社纽约讯：美国许多著名进步分子，将于十六、十七两日在此举行“人权法案会议”，以保卫民主权利。该会主席福尔曼向报界发表声明称：已经有五百六十四名美国进步人士声明支持会议，其中包括八十四所大学和学院的一百一十九名教授，八十八个牧师，七十六名工会领袖，七十二名黑人领袖，及许多作家、艺术家、律师和其他自由职业者。
