Title: World Student Union Executive Committee Meeting

Author:

Date: 1949-07-01
Page: 3

　　World Student Union
  　Executive Committee Meeting
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th -- TASS, Rome, 28th: The World Student Union Executive Committee meeting has opened in Bologna. Williams, Secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, conveyed greetings to the attending delegates on behalf of the 56 million young people from 65 countries participating in the Federation. Grohman, Chairman of the World Student Union, delivered a report at the meeting.

### 世界学联　执委会开会

1949-07-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　世界学联
  　执委会开会
    【新华社北平三十日电】塔斯社罗马二十八日讯：世界学联执行委员会会议已于波伦亚揭幕。世界民主青年联盟书记威廉士，代表参加联盟的六十五个国家的五千六百万青年向与会代表致敬。世界学联主席格罗曼在会议上作了报告。
