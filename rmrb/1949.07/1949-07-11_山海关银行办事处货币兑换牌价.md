Title: Currency Exchange Rates at the Shanhai Pass Bank Office
Author: 
Date: 1949-07-11
Page: 2

　　Shanhai Pass Bank Office
  　Currency Exchange Rates
    [Shanhaiguan Dispatch] The joint office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank in Shanhaiguan has announced that, effective July 11th, the exchange rate will be one Renminbi yuan for one hundred and twenty-five Northeast yuan or Great Wall yuan.

### 山海关银行办事处货币兑换牌价

1949-07-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关银行办事处
  　货币兑换牌价
    【山海关专电】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自七月十一日起牌价，改为人民币一元兑换东北币或长城币一百二十五元整。
