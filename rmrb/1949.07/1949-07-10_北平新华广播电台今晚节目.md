Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-10
Page: 1

　　Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
    [This newspaper] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast a lecture program from 19:50 to 20:10 tonight. Artist Ye Qianyu will speak on "The Progressive Art Movement in the Kuomintang-Controlled Areas." From 19:30 to 19:50, the "Workers' Time" segment will introduce the "Worker's Daily." From 21:15 to 21:30, "International Current Events" will broadcast "Fighting Vietnam." From 22:20 to 22:50, the literary and art program will feature the new Peking opera "Four Persuasions," broadcast by the North China Peking Opera Theater.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，美术家叶浅予讲“国民党统治区的进步美术运动”。十九点三十分到十九点五十分职工时间，介绍“工人日报”。二十一点十五分到二十一点三十分国际时事，播送“战斗的越南”。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由华北平剧院播送新平剧“四劝”。
